Where is Lazarevskoye located? Black Sea, Lazarevskoye

There are such points on the map of Russian resorts, whose names are passed down from generation to generation. This happens, despite crises, economic shocks and the popularization of foreign holidays. Lazarevskoye is one of them. "Savages" and "cultural" tourists collect their bags and buy tickets in a southerly direction with a stop in this wonderful place. They know where Lazarevskoye is: only sixty-five kilometers from Sochi.

The history of the appearance of the village of Lazarevskoye





True, the resort was not always here. More than four thousand years ago, Circassians, jihis, kasogis lived here, who in those days did nothing but hunting. It took them a millennium to master agricultural work. And only in the fifteenth century did a great industry appear here - trade. At the place where Lazarevskoye is located, in 1839, during the military operations, the Russian fleet landed. A defensive fort was built here. Thanks to this, it was possible to repel the attacks of the British and Turks. At the head of our sailors was Admiral Lazarev, who, incidentally, was the discoverer of the mysterious Antarctica. His last name also gave the name to the future resort.

Crowds of vacationers began their pilgrimage to Lazarevskoye only in the second half of the twentieth century. Before, it was a quiet place where locals cultivated grapes, corn, tobacco, collected fruits, nuts and chestnuts.

After World War II, the construction of tourist camps, resorts and boarding houses began here.

How to get to the place by plane and train?

How to get to Lazarevsky? It all depends on the starting point of the route. The fastest option is a flight. There is no airport in the village itself. But the planes to Adler depart from many Russian cities.





Further from Adler to Lazarevsky in two and a half hours you can get on an electric train or passing passenger trains. A faster, more comfortable, but more expensive way is a taxi. A direct flight to the village can be reached by rail. There is a railway station, however, the train stop is limited to two to five minutes.

How to get to the place by private car?

For those who know exactly where Lazarevskoye is and prefers autotravel, there are no barriers at all. The roads are not bad, there are equipped parking lots with cafes and eateries. If you get together, the trip will not make a big gap in the budget. In addition, you can admire the richness of the natural environment along the way, and explore the distant surroundings during your vacation.

Housing Options

Most visitors are in the category who are not the first time visiting Lazarevskoye. The village has long adapted to the special resort rhythm and established the necessary infrastructure. For example, you can stay here not only in boarding houses or hotels. In the private sector there is always a comfortable inexpensive option for the "savages".





Everything is civilized, with amenities and quite affordable. They offer to rent not only a room, but you can also choose a separate house. By the way, for many local residents this is the main source of income, since there are no enterprises and industries in the resort.

Climate of the village

The climate in this area is very important for vacationers who prefer Lazarevskoye. The village, from May to November, in terms of weather is considered a real paradise. August is especially favorable. Sea temperature allows you to enjoy sea diving for almost six months. Piercing cold winds stop the massif of the Caucasus Range. Even in winter there is quite mild, comfortable weather.





Organized tourists, as a rule, upon arrival at the place, study the offers of local travel agencies and choose excursions. After all, the places where Lazarevskoye is located are famous for unique natural creations and historical monuments.
