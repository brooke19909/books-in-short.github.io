The most popular water park is Fantasy.

Fantasy water park has been open since 2009. You can find him in Maryino, at ul. Lublin, 100. It offers four swimming pools (for children and adults) and five slides. Here you can not only swim and enjoy water rides, but also sit in a cozy cafe that looks like a real pirate ship. Like the Kva-Kva water park (the largest water park in Moscow), Shine-style parties are also often held here.





A noteworthy fact is that Fantasy is part of a whole entertainment complex with a rollerdrome, bowling, billiards, karaoke, cafes and bars. A distinctive feature of the water complex is the division of its territory into 4 "continents": America, Europe, Antarctica and Africa. From one section to another you can get on a special inflatable raft.





The budget water park is Caribia

Water park "Caribia" (Zeleny Prospekt 10B) is notable for its unusual internal content. Some elements of the interior are made in the Roman style. There are 4 slides and 3 swimming pools for visitors, in addition, you can choose the type of entertainment suitable for yourself: slot machines, a solarium, a massage parlor, a 7D cinema and even laser paintball and a mini-football field. The ceiling of the water park is beautifully decorated in tropical style.





The water park regularly holds promotions and gives discounts on certain types of services, therefore the Caribia water park is considered one of the most affordable in the capital.
