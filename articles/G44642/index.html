We knit a scarf hood with knitting needles

In the cold winter, you always want to wrap yourself in something warm, fluffy. For this, a scarf and a hat will suit well. Fashion designers and designers offer fashionistas and everyone who appreciates practicality a convenient solution - this is a scarf-hood. This is an amazing combination of a warm scarf and a hood in one product. You don‚Äôt need to put on a few things at once, and you can throw off the cape-hood at any moment. Any fashionista will be able to knit a hood-scarf with knitting needles. This will require about a kilogram of warm soft wool and thick knitting needles of the sixth or seventh number.



Pattern for a scarf hood

To start manufacturing a scarf-hood should be with the development of patterns. Although experienced knitters can do without it. It will be necessary to knit two rectangles. One, the size of which is 22 by 76 cm, will be a hood, and the other - by the size of 42 by 150 cm - this will be the scarf itself. When the rectangles are connected, they will only have to connect.



Knitting pattern

You need to start knitting a scarf hood with knitting needles from a small rectangle. It is necessary to calculate the number of loops. To do this, put ten loops on the needles and knit a sample from several rows. Then measure the length of the sample. Next, as in mathematics, compose an equation from one unknown, and as a result you get the desired number of loops. For example, for our case: 10 loops is 8 cm, and you need 42 cm. Here is the equation - 10 p is 8 cm, X p is 42 cm. About 52 loops are obtained. It is more convenient to knit a simple pattern with a knitting scarf-hood. The scheme will be simple. Rows are indicated on the diagram and loops are indicated - either front (v) or wrong (-). 1st row: vvv- continue to the end of the row. 2 row: vvv- continue this way to the end. Knitting a hood-scarf with knitting needles is simple. Knit 52 loops on the knitting needles and knit the first 3-4 cm with an ordinary rubber band, that is, knit the front and back loops one by one. And in this sequence all the rows. When it is knitted about 4 cm with a simple rubber band, you will need to switch to English rubber band.



Knitting the main fabric

It will be warm and beautiful knitted scarf-hood knitting. The knitting pattern will help to figure out the pattern. Although it is simple. Since at first a simple elastic band was connected, it is very easy to switch to English. To do this, you need to knit the front loops as usual, but with the wrong loops you must do the following: make a crochet and simply throw the loop on the working knitting needle. And in the next row, the front loops need to be knit together with the yarn, and the back loops should be thrown back with the yarn. The entire scarf hood with knitting needles will be knitted with an English or convex elastic band. This pattern is suitable for this product and the fact that the knitting will turn out to be voluminous, the pattern is dense, and the product is very warm. When both rectangles are ready, it remains only to connect them. You just need to use a needle and thread - and a knitted scarf hood with knitting needles is ready. You need to sew with simple stitches, it is better to use the same woolen thread that knitted the product. The stitches should be done by the size of the front loops, this will help to hide the seam, and the product will look like a single whole. The ends of the scarf can be decorated with fringe, tassels, pompons, you just need to show imagination.
