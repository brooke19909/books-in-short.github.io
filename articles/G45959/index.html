Hotel Ocean Star Resort 4 * (Vietnam / Phan Thiet): photos, description, reviews of tourists

One of the most popular resort centers in Vietnam is Phan Thiet. It is here that you can fully enjoy the spacious beaches, the warm sea, gastronomic seafood specialties and magnificent views. The resort specialization is very flexible, not having a specific target audience of vacationers. He is ready to offer you a special, albeit a small list of attractions, delicious food, a wide selection of entertainments, hospitable and comfortable hotels. One of them is Ocean Star Resort 4 *.

Description





The hotel was founded in 2004, and the last restoration in it was carried out in 2013. The complex consists of three types of buildings: seven buildings on two floors, eleven bungalows and thirty-six one-story cottages. Guests have at their disposal a spacious private beach with a length of about 150 meters. The hotel is located on the first coastline and provides its guests with towels and sun loungers, parasols free of charge.

Restaurants and bars on site

The hotel, with its four stars, can offer tourists food on an all-inclusive basis, and only breakfast. In the first case, you need to make a distinction with a similar policy, for example, Turkish or Egyptian resorts. Sports and Entertainment

The complex offers guests one large pool with a jacuzzi and a children's section. Around him neatly arranged umbrellas and sunbeds - all that is needed for a relaxing holiday on the ocean, surrounded by the jungle.





On the spacious beach there is an activity for lovers of outdoor activities and water activities. All of them are literally within walking distance from the Ocean Star Resort 4 * hotel (Vietnam). Some are offered absolutely free of charge, for example, windsurfing, tennis, billiards, beach volleyball, while others will have to pay extra (motor and non-motorized sports at sea, water skiing, kiting, parasailing). For everyone who wants to break out of this paradise, car rental and numerous exciting excursions are available upon request.

Ocean Star Resort 4 * (Vietnam): location, sea and beach reviews





Tourists not only from Russia but also from all over the world who once visited this complex unanimously give the highest score possible for its location. The proximity (100 m) of the beach and the ocean, the wild jungle and privacy are evaluated exclusively as one of the main advantages. Some of the vacationers who traveled in the winter and spring months (from December to March), note the presence of waves, which makes swimming difficult. However, this state of affairs, as it turned out, is ideal for kitesurfing, especially since the hotel has a school with Russian-speaking specialists.

Ocean Star Resort 4 *: room and service reviews

Most living in rooms and villas, bungalows note their quality, decor and cleanliness on a rating of 3 and 3.5 on a five-point scale. We are talking mainly about not entirely new and noticeably dilapidated furniture upholstery, bedding and towels. Cleaning is carried out, according to tourists, regularly, but its quality does not quite match the declared level of the hotel. The staff is polite and smiling, but they do not speak Russian at all, and English is at the weakest level. All this complicates communication and, to some extent, mutual understanding between the administration and guests.





Over the weekend, according to the guests, a large number of local residents come to the hotel. Such a neighborhood is sometimes inconvenient, some note noise, but overall the atmosphere is friendly and calm.

Analyzing official information about the Ocean Star Resort 4 * hotel (Vietnam), reviews of its guests, we can conclude that there is some ambiguity and inconsistency. However, everything is quite fixable and smoothed out by other, more pleasant factors of rest at sea. In particular, the fact that tourists spend most of their time outside the walls of the hotel.

Who is the institution oriented to?

It is worth noting that the Ocean Star Resort 4 * hotel is decorated in the best local traditions and has a beautiful and interesting national color. A clean and well-groomed area allows you to spend more time in nature. The mixed national composition of the guests of the complex allows you to meet new people and learn something about a different culture.

Most tourists in their reviews note that the hotel does not have infrastructure for children, with the exception of a few swings on the territory and two offices in the pool. The sea is often hectic, the calm is characteristic in the early morning hours, which is good for water activities and to a lesser extent for swimming. Many note that on windy days, which are frequent, the wave covers an adult with his head. Therefore, the Ocean Star Resort 4 * hotel, reviews of which is mostly positive, is recommended for a romantic getaway for two, especially newlyweds, as well as those who are chasing a big wave. To a lesser extent, it is suitable for families with children and business people.
