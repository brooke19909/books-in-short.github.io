"Dream"





Many positive reviews were left about the Dream complex, located in the village of Olginskaya, on the left bank, ten kilometers from the metropolis. A small distance from the city allows tourists in just a quarter of an hour to be in the recreation center. The number of exciting activities offered here is amazing. After active entertainment, many people prefer to bathe and relax in the Russian bathhouse, available on the territory of the recreation center.
