Hotel Sun Fire Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya): description, photo, reviews of tourists

Choosing a place for an annual vacation or summer vacation is an important task, which should be approached thoroughly. Interesting excursions, mountain landscapes, clear waters of the Mediterranean and the picturesque nature, sometimes not touched by a human hand, is what combines the small resort of Alanya, which is widely known among tourists.

The popularity of local hotels is largely due to their affordable prices and Russian-speaking staff. The historical heritage of this city allows not only to enjoy clean air, but also to develop the horizons of both an adult and a small guest in these surroundings. Representatives of all ages will find something to their liking. The four-star Sun Fire Beach Hotel offers its guests comfortable accommodation and an unforgettable pastime all year round.

The distance from the nearest international airport is 140 km. Despite the considerable remoteness, vacationers note that overcoming this distance by bus after the flight is exciting and exciting, because the beauty of the surrounding valleys and the slopes of the Toros mountains are impressive.

These rooms differ in size, with a maximum of 35 square meters and a minimum of 22 square meters. Most of the rooms have direct or partial views of the sea. The interior has a modern design, which is emphasized by snow-white relief ceilings. The walls, in addition to numerous sconces, are hung with the works of painters, which gives the chambers harmony and poise. The floors are covered with light-colored ceramic tiles. Arrangement

Furniture set - standard, includes a double or single bed, desk, chair, coffee table, bedside tables and wardrobe. A large mirror visually increases the space and allows women to admire themselves. The split-system throughout the year is able to provide the necessary temperature conditions for each guest. LCD multi-channel TV has Russian-language channels, which allows you to brighten up the evenings by watching your favorite movie or news. The telephone helps staff instantly resolve tourist issues. Minibar is empty upon check-in. Use of the safe is not included in the price and is paid upon arrival. It is possible to install an extra bed in the form of a folding bed. A modern fire safety system is located in each apartment.

Wi-Fi is available 24 hours a day and does not require payment. The availability of unhindered communication with loved ones via the Internet is noted by many travelers in their positive reviews. Iron and ironing board are available upon request.

The bathroom has a modest design, equipped with a shower or bath, sink, mirror and hairdryer. There is a set of snow-white towels, the number of which meets international standards. Soap, shampoo and gel are updated daily.

Nutrition

Sun Fire Beach Hotel 4 * receives a positive plan for catering. Like most hotels in Turkey, the hotel offers its guests meals in the All Inclusive system. A five-time meal is held in the walls of the main restaurant in the form of a buffet, which implies an independent choice of the dish you like in an amount that can satisfy the feeling of hunger. Only late dinner is a fixed menu. According to the guests, the choice of various dishes is incredibly large: seafood, meat, salads, desserts and much more. Local chefs, creating taste and visual masterpieces, invest a piece of their soul. Extra payment is required for imported alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks.

The interior of the institution is modest. Ubiquitous spotlights and multi-level ceilings are harmoniously combined with marble floors and wooden furniture. The warm color scheme is conducive to a casual conversation and an increase in appetite.

In addition to the main institution for saturation, there is a pool bar, which during the day offers a wide range of drinks and light snacks.

Beach

The distance to the waters of the Mediterranean Sea is small, Sun Fire Beach Hotel 4 * (Kemer) is located on the first coastline. A distance of 30 m separates the guests from the snow-white sandy-pebble beach, the passage to which runs along the underpass. A distinctive feature of the beaches of Alanya is the presence of developed infrastructure: locker rooms, showers and bars are located everywhere. A gentle entry into the water is safe for bathing babies.

For guests of the hotel, deck chairs with umbrellas are provided for free use. They will help not only lovers of a natural tan, but also those who hide from the scorching rays of the sun, get a frantic pleasure from enjoying the sounds of the sea breeze and swimming in clear water. The rescue service takes care of the safety of swimmers, and the security service watches over the order and tranquility of spa residents. Towels are provided on a fee basis.

Coastal Activities

The coastal zone is not wide, however, on its open spaces there is a volleyball court, where lovers of outdoor activities gather daily. Communication and the struggle for team championship diversify the day of every tourist. In addition, the beach area has a wide range of water attractions: slides, scooters, windsurfing, as well as riding in circles and a banana.

Children's leisure

Sun Fire Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey) often receives positive reviews from grateful parents. The hotel complex is positioning itself as a place for a family vacation. This is confirmed by the guests who visited it. Pastime of young guests is an important task, which is solved by qualified personnel. For children there is a colorful playground in the fresh air, made of safe plastic. It includes ladders, transitions, swings and slides. Here, children can meet their peers, spend an endless supply of energy and breathe fresh air.

The presence of a children's room with many toys is a favorable place for those who want to leave the baby under the supervision of a professional nanny who monitors the order and organizes collective games of a different nature: sports, tasks for ingenuity and creative tournaments. The child will receive unforgettable emotions, pleasant memories and new skills thanks to the master classes.

A children's swimming pool is a favorite place for children, because not only their parents love to swim and dive.

Entertainment

Every tourist who decides to stay at this hotel will find something to his liking. Daily discos and animation programs will present a lot of positive and good mood. A professional team of artists, talented dancers and comedians will amuse and impress the audience. The evening ends, as a rule, with a disco, the entrance to which is paid extra. Using the services of a tour desk, you can go on a day to get acquainted with local attractions, of which there are countless in these parts.

health and beauty
