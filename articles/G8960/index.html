Guest house "Angelina" (Vityazevo, Anapa): address, description, reviews

For decades, the beautiful city of Anapa has been popular among fans of relaxing on the sea . Vityazevo is his neighboring village. There are beautiful beaches, well-developed infrastructure and many comfortable, small, home-like hotels, called guest houses. One of them is Angelina. Vityazevo will give you not only a bright festive fun of the southern resort village, but also quiet peace of mind if you choose this wonderful guest house for your stay.



Location

Regarding the convenience of location, ‚ÄúAngelina‚Äù (Vityazevo) has a double assessment. The address at this private hotel is such - Slavnaya street, building No. 22. This is a very quiet area away from many tourist business facilities, and the street, one might say, is one of the most extreme in the village. Behind it begins a sand dune. That is, the location of this hotel will seem ideal for lovers of silence and will not like at all those who expect enchantingly bright fun from their vacation. The village of Vityazevo from the geographical center of Anapa lies no more than 11 km. Therefore, you can always easily get to the airport . D. Station or any entertainment center. Another feature - the village lies on the spit between the Black Sea and the large estuary Vityazevsky. From "Angelina" to both approximately the same distance. Therefore, tourists have the opportunity to spend time on any of these two reservoirs.

How to get there

It is most convenient to travel by car to the Angelina Hotel. Vityazevo is a beautiful village, but its transport system is not very developed. In the summer, there are only 4 city bus and minibus routes. None of them reach Slavnaya Street, so guests of Angelina Guest House will have to get to the center either by taxi or on foot to the bus stops.

You can also arrive at Angelina by public transport from Vityazevo Airport, located just 4 km from the village of the same name. With w. Anapa railway station, you can get to Vityazevo by bus, and from there take a taxi to the guest house. You can also get to Angelina with a pre-ordered transfer. It is provided to Anapa's transportation hubs for free, and to the Tunnelnaya station for a fee. Also, the administration of the guest house, by agreement for a fee, provides a transfer to a pharmacy and some infrastructure facilities of the village.

Description

Mini hotel ‚ÄúAngelina‚Äù was built according to the original modern design. Vityazevo has such stylish beautiful buildings. The territory of the hotel is fenced, along the perimeter lighting lamps are installed. The area allows you to place a large swimming pool with a section for kids, a free (included in the price per room) parking lot, a playground for children, a gazebo. The hotel lobby is very spacious, bright, furnished with soft leather armchairs and sofas. On the ground floor there is a dining room, a mini club for children and a games room for adults. Reception works without days off and breaks. They provide services for ordering a taxi or transfer, washing and ironing guests, and calling a doctor. Also at the reception you can rent a baby stroller, iron and ironing board.



Rooms

To make a pleasant and comfortable stay in Vityazevo, Angelina offers its guests 65 rooms of different comforts. All have balconies. The view from the windows to the sea or the surroundings. Room categories:

  • "Junior Suite" designed for 2, 3 or 4 guests, plus 1 or 2 extra beds. Equipment - a classic set of furniture, including beds, bedside tables, wardrobe, chairs, table. Electrical equipment is represented by a refrigerator, TV, air conditioning, there is also a safe.
  • "Suite", designed for accommodation from 2 to 7 guests. The rooms of this category are larger and smaller, but all are two-room. Layout - bedroom, living room and hygiene room. Equipment - bedroom set, upholstered furniture, necessary electrical equipment.

The hygiene rooms of all categories of rooms have a washbasin, shower cubicle, toilet and hairdryer.

Each guest is provided with a set of bed linen (changes every 3 days) and a towel. Hygiene products, bathrobes and slippers are not provided here.

Nutrition

Tourists can relax without worries and hassle in the mini-hotel "Angelina" (Vityazevo), as there is a stylishly decorated dining room, where every day a variety of home-cooked food is offered. Guests can make their own menu for themselves. In addition to ready meals, there is the opportunity to buy some products.

The cost of food is not included in the price per room. Kitchens, where you can cook your own food yourself, in "Angelina" is not provided.



The hotel has a spacious gazebo with a set of furniture and barbecue. This allows everyone to cook their own kebabs or fresh fish, which is not a problem to buy in Vityazevo. In the village there are many cafes and dining rooms of different price categories, where they cook hearty and tasty. However, guests planning to eat in them may encounter some difficulties, for example, the large distance to food service outlets.

Service for children

Guest house "Angelina" is great for staying here with children of any age. The smallest clients are provided with cots and high chairs in the dining room. At the reception you can rent baby strollers. For the entertainment of the kids, Angelina has a beautiful children's room for games, and slides, swings and carousels are installed on the territory. There is also a children's section in the large adult pool. Animators are engaged in the day and evening with children.



Leisure for adults

Mostly for a relaxing family vacation, the hotel "Angelina" is suitable. Vityazevo has been a seaside resort for more than a year. Therefore, there are many objects for the entertainment of tourists. The most famous is the promenade "Paralia", which is translated from Greek, in fact, means the promenade. There are dozens of outlets, canteens, cafes, restaurants, an amusement park, excursion bureaus, and nearby there are a dolphinarium, a water park and a terrarium. But guests of "Angelina" to all these facilities need to go by taxi or by paid transfer. Therefore, in order to diversify the relaxation of its guests, daily animation is carried out in this private hotel for adults. On the territory of "Angelina" there is a pool in which a waterfall and a jacuzzi section are equipped. Around the pool in a large number of sunbeds and umbrellas. In brochures it is written that the water in the pool is heated, however, in practice this does not happen often. The hotel building has a games room with pool tables and air hockey. In addition, reception staff can book various excursions.

Sea

Noisy, fun, vibrant resort is Anapa. Vityazevo, although located very close by, has a more relaxed atmosphere and, most importantly, a cleaner sea. From Angelina Guesthouse, the beach is approximately 650 meters away, which must be overcome by crossing a sandy hill. The beach itself is also sandy with interspersed shells. The entrance to the sea is very smooth, the depth starts quite far from the water's edge. For children, the rest is incomparable. The sea in this part of Vityazevo is mostly calm and, starting in June, warm. Waves are rare, but then the water becomes less transparent, and a lot of algae nails to the shore. Of the entertainment on this beach, riding a banana, a catamaran, a tablet, water slides, and excursions to the sunken bulk carrier, completely hidden under water, are offered. This beach is also liked by many vacationers because there is no crowding of people here, even in peak season.



Mini-hotel "Angelina" (Vityazevo), reviews

About how the rest in this guest house went, the opinions of people are different. Someone was satisfied with everything and is going to come here again, while someone, on the contrary, does not recommend staying here. This is due to the features of the mini-hotel "Angelina", which is most suitable for those who want to spend a vacation away from the noise and crowding of the people, but in a beautiful environmentally friendly place. Benefits noted in the reviews:

  • clean comfortable rooms;
  • good infrastructure;
  • the availability of entertainment for children;
  • good food;
  • availability of free Internet on the territory;
  • great sea and beach.

Noted disadvantages:

  • remoteness from the center of the village and from all major infrastructure facilities, including shops and pharmacies;
  • interruptions in hot water;
  • not all staff are friendly and welcoming;
  • cold water in the pool.
