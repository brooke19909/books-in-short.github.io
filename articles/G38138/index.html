When starting a conversation about the Neo Belvista Hotel 3, it is imperative to pay attention to its location, the surrounding resort area.

Kemer, Goynuk

Kemer is a young resort area, which over the past two decades has transformed from a fishing village into an attractive resort. All this happened thanks to the investments of Western European capital in the development of tourism in Turkey.



Goynuk is a suburb of Kemer, ideal for a different type of vacation in the beach season: from May to October. This village is a prestigious resort area located 7 km from Kemer and 45 km from Antalya Airport.

Goynuk is famous for its clean pebble beaches, which have been awarded the Blue Flag, and the azure sea. The main attraction of this place is Goynuk Canyon. This gorge is at an altitude of 350 meters with a width of 6 meters and a length of 14 kilometers. Nearby there is a magnificent waterfall and cave with a length of almost 7 kilometers. It was there that the ancient Lycian Way once passed.

Neo Belvista Hotel 3 *

The local population is friendly and hardworking residents living in the mountainous part of the village. There are comfortable and fashionable hotels by the sea. Among the natural and man-made beauty, Neo Belvista Hotel 3 * is located. It is part of the Belvista hotel complex, along with other buildings of the highest category.



The hotel was recently built in 2012, but in 2014 it was renovated. The nondescript and compact exterior of the hotel building inside looks like a palace of marble and mirrors. The hotel is 87 spacious standard rooms ranging from 20 to 28 square meters. km, which can accommodate from 1 to 3 people.



Rooms

In each room for a good rest and comfortable stay there is:

  • balcony or terrace;
  • a bathroom combined with a bathroom;
  • air conditioning;
  • TV with cable or satellite TV (several Russian-language channels are available);
  • hairdryer and toiletries;
  • telephone;
  • safe and minibar at an additional cost;
  • bedding and towels.

Rooms are cleaned daily. Change of bed linen and towels - 2 times a week. For the convenience of guests there is a currency exchange office, parking, wireless Internet on site. Guests can use the pools of the Neo Belvista Hotel 3 * and all the amenities of the entire complex, even to the point that all restaurants and cafes can be visited.

Beach

The hotel's beach is about 750 meters away. This is a free public beach, with free parasols and sun loungers. However, guests of the Neo Belvista Hotel 3 * (reviews confirm this) can equally use the beach of the Larisa Vista Hotel. Quenns Park Beach, a 5-minute walk away, can be used for a fee.



Hotel concept

The all-inclusive concept is the policy of the Neo Belvista Hotel 3 * hotel. Turkey cares about the convenience of tourists. Meals: breakfast (from 7-00 to 9-30), lunch (from 12-30 to 14-00) and dinner (from 19-00 to 21-00) - "buffet". A wide range of fruits for dessert. All drinks of local production from 9-00 to 22-00 for free.

There is no animation in the hotel. However, you can attend the shows of the adjacent hotel, where young animators often hold funny parties and competitions.

For children

For children, the hotel has its own playroom. If necessary , babysitting services are available at an additional cost. The toddler restaurant has high chairs. If necessary, you can order a cot in your room. You can rent a stroller for a child for minimal money. Near the main pool there is a "paddling pool" - a children's pool with a depth of up to 40 cm.

Services

At Neo Belvista Hotel 3 * there is a bicycle rental service, which you can use to take a walk in the surroundings. Attention deserves the nearby hotel "Princess Elizabeth" 5 *, erected in the form of a huge ship. Near the Neo Belvista Hotel 3 * there is a local bazaar where you can make profitable purchases, be sure to bargain with sellers. If necessary, the hotel has a pharmacy, a grocery store and a tour desk.

Using the services of this office, you can purchase an inexpensive sightseeing tour of nearby and remote attractions. It is recommended to visit the Goynuk canyon with its waterfall and picturesque lemon groves. In Kemer, you should visit several nightclubs with discos.



Holidays at the Goynuk resort will strengthen vitality, give a boost of vigor for a long time, help restore health and soothe nerves. The beneficial effects of clean sea and air, warm sunshine and a light breeze will positively affect the health and mood of vacationers.

Vacation at Neo Belvista Hotel 3 * (Turkey) is a budget holiday with a lot of positive and positive emotions.
