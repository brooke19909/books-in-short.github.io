Ladybug from a helmet: we create a beautiful garden figure from improvised materials

Any summer cottage, courtyard, flower garden or playground can not only be decorated with purchased decorative figures, which are often quite expensive, but also make unique and beautiful things with your own hands.



The independent production of unique and interesting products is becoming an increasingly popular activity, as it allows you to create a real masterpiece from improvised materials and, in addition, enjoy the creative process itself. Having spent a minimum of material resources and having made only a little effort, you can transform your surrounding world, bring a little more brightness and colors to it. In this article, we would like to talk about how to make a beautiful and cheerful craft "ladybug" from a helmet. Yes, yes, it is from an ordinary construction helmet that we will make a real work of art! So, we proceed to an exciting creative work.

Ladybug from a helmet: a bright detail in your exterior

In order to make this simple, but cute craft, you need a minimum of supplies. Of course, the central element of all the work is the usual construction helmet. There are no special requirements for it, but it is desirable that its shape be round. It is better if it is without strong protrusions and a large visor. So the shape of the bug will be closer to reality.

Also, to complete this creative task of making a ladybug craft from a helmet, you will need different colors of paint: black, red and white. We advise you to use acrylic or for outdoor use. They will lie well on any surface and for a long time maintain the saturation and brightness of colors. Well and, of course, for applying paint you need brushes.

Ladybug from a helmet: stages of product design

The technology for making an interesting ladybug from a construction helmet is extremely simple. Even a child can easily cope with all operations, so the creation of this cheerful insect can be entrusted to your baby. Preparatory phase of work: it is necessary to take a helmet, clean it well from dust and dirt and allow to dry. After performing these operations, you can safely proceed to coloring. First give the body of the insect a bright red color, and then draw black spots on it. Now you can move on to applying color to the bug‚Äôs face: it should be black and the eyes should be white, so they will be more expressive. A ladybug from a construction helmet will become more elegant and welcoming if you draw her a smile, cilia and a thin mustache. That's all, your craft is almost ready! As you can see, making a ladybug from a helmet is quite simple and easy! After the paint has completely dried, you can place the product in the flower garden, attach it to a tree trunk or draw a large stump with it. If you wish, you can make the legs of your beauty from nails, fixing them in the body of the insect.

You can safely show imagination and make your craft brighter, for example, paint it in colors other than red and black. Bugs of yellow, orange, and violet tones look original and picturesque.
