How to make nylon flowers

Probably every person on the planet has his own hobby. It can be different: you can build birdhouses and draw with sand, you can do

crafts made of paper and fabric. And a lot of exciting activities, however, needlework is the most popular among women. This article will focus on the manufacture of nylon products, namely the creation of flowers.

Kapron flowers are a wonderful thing for decorating a room and a good reason to entertain yourself with something. They can be made for yourself, friends, acquaintances, and even for sale, because the finished material is very beautiful. Kapron itself is a universal thing: first you can wear tights, and when they wear out, make flowers out of them.

What you need to make flowers from kapron:

  • Kapron of red, yellow and green colors.
  • A wire of yellow, green, red colors with a diameter of 0.5 mm.
  • Nippers
  • Scissors.
  • 1 mm diameter wire for the foot
  • Sheet of cardboard and pencil.
  • Adhesive paper is green.

Draw on the cardboard the outline of the petal that you want for your flower. Now, in this form, it will be necessary to fold the wire in red and yellow.

For one flower you will need 6 petals (three red and three yellow) and two leaves. Now the frame can be covered with a capron of the same color as the wire. You can fix the capron with threads, but you can glue it. Petals need to be bent so that they look almost like real ones. First bend the yellow ones and twist them together to form a flower. Attach the red petals around the yellow ones. The first flower is ready. To form its leg, take a thicker wire and attach it to the bud. Wrap the wire with sticky paper, clinging to it green leaves. The first flower is ready! For this bouquet we need six more. You can try to combine other colors to make the bouquet bright. Kapron flowers are ready! They can be a good gift to loved ones and friends.

So that in future you get the most beautiful flowers from kapron, you need to train. And the material for training can be old flesh-colored nylon tights . Experienced craftsmen often acquire paints for fabric, they can paint flowers so that they can not be distinguished from real ones. Kapron for flowers will suit absolutely anyone. It can be multi-colored, with sequins, thick and thin. It all depends on your wishes.

Master Class. DIY kapron flowers





Take white nylon, wire and red fabric dye. Make a petal frame from the wire and tighten it with a capron.

Make five petals. Now the fabric needs to be decorated. On one petal, decorate only a little edge. The next two also have edges, but now apply more paint for a richer color. And paint the remaining three almost completely, leaving only small gaps. Now you need some cotton for stamens. Wind small pieces of fleece around the wire, decorate the cotton itself in black and yellow. Make three stamens and hold them together. Collect the whole flower and make a leg for it, as described in the previous version. The result will definitely please you!
