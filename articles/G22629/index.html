Sanya Marriott Yalong Bay Resort & Spa

Located in the south of the island, this luxurious luxury hotel is located on the coast. Having a huge area, the hotel has on its territory a golf course, swimming pools, which is perfect for tourists planning their holidays with their families. At check-in, a deposit of 500 RMB per night per room must be paid in cash, or this amount will be blocked on credit card. Everything will depend on the time spent in this place. Yalong Bay Mangrove Tree Resort

This hotel is located in the town of Sanya by the sea. The luxurious, contemporary oriental-themed rooms feature safes, minibars, flat-screen TVs and air conditioning. Bathrooms combine showers and bathtubs. The views from the window are simply amazing! Parking for guests with cars is free. The hotel is close to the train station (18 kilometers) and the airport (30 kilometers).

Entertainment is provided for every taste. Visitors can go canoeing, diving, water-skiing, renting bicycles and taking a ride in the surrounding area, quenching their curiosity. The hotel's restaurants are filled with Chinese, Korean, Thai dishes. The restaurant business is booming, because oriental cuisine is famous for its original dishes around the world. You can order whatever your heart desires. Dishes are prepared by professional chefs who are always ready to surprise guests with something refined and original. Also on the territory of the hotel are spa centers, various beauty salons, business centers, currency exchange points, conference rooms for various events. For children there is a special game club. To check into a hotel, a deposit of at least $ 200 is required. This is one of the best hotels in its class. Nearby is the main square with numerous fountains and architectural structures. In any weather, you can go there and independently explore a variety of buildings, and rest on the picturesque beaches with amazing views will be captured in your memory for a long time.

Resort Intime Sanya

This five-star hotel is located in the heart of the beautiful Dadonghai Bay with a total length of 6 km. This place is considered one of the most beautiful, so tourists who really appreciate the landscape tend to get here. There are many coconut fan palm trees, their own clean white sand beaches. There are not many people here, so the rest is ideal for lovers of peace, tranquility and silence. Vacationers can spread out in comfortable sun loungers and watch the beautiful views opening in front of them. The gentle southern sun gives the skin a wonderful shade. Coastal guard closely monitors the safety of vacationers. The hotel staff works day and night so that tourists feel fine, because the face of the hotel and the reputation of Hainan Island as a whole depend on the service. Another important and pleasant fact is that the sea is shallow and the bottom is shallow, which is an excellent option for relaxing with young children, because you can not be afraid of what will happen on the seabed.
