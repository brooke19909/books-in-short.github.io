Shepelevsky lighthouse - an interesting informal attraction of the Leningrad region

St. Petersburg is one of the most beautiful cities in Russia. In fact, there are much more attractions in the Northern capital and its environs than are described in official guidebooks. During a trip to the Leningrad Region, everyone can enjoy the interesting old building - Shepelevsky lighthouse. What is remarkable about this object and how to get to it on your own?

Photo and description of Shepelevsky lighthouse





Shepelevsky lighthouse is installed near the village of Shepelevo on the shores of the Gulf of Finland. A strategically important building was made in 1910 in France. Shepelevsky lighthouse is a round tower made of cast iron. Its total height is 38 meters. Like most similar structures, the lighthouse is horizontally painted with red and white stripes. The upper gallery is painted black, there is also a lantern. Lomonosov district, where the lighthouse is located, can rightfully be proud of such an unusual attraction. The construction not only celebrated its centenary, but also continues to be a strategic object. Today, like a hundred years ago, a lighthouse illuminates the way for ships. Every 16 seconds, the flashlight gives two long flashes. Depending on the direction of movement of the vessel, they may be visible in white or red light.

Interesting Facts





Shepelevsky lighthouse played an unusual and truly heroic role during the Great Patriotic War. The strategic object was the starting point of the Small Road of Life, passing in the winter season on the ice from Shepelevo to the islands of Seskar, Small and Lawnsaari. Today, near the lighthouse, you can observe another unusual building. This is a high-altitude radio tower, which is abandoned today. Some tourists climb it and enjoy a bewitching view. And this risk is justified, because the radio tower is approximately twice as high as the lighthouse. Of course, no one officially permitted such excursions. But such an excursion will give a lot of vivid impressions.

Shepelevsky lighthouse: how to get there by personal or public transport?





Getting to this unusual attraction is a snap. In St. Petersburg, minibuses to Shepelevo go from the metro stations Avtovo (route 402) and Parnas (route 401). You need to get to the final. Further on foot or on a local minibus you need to get to the monument to sailors. From it there is an asphalt road to the place from which the lighthouse is remarkably visible. By private car, the easiest way to get to the coordinates: 59.985736; 29.127121. The road is good until the lighthouse, you do not have to leave the car somewhere at a distance. Lomonosov district of the Leningrad region is rich in beautiful places and picturesque natural views.

Reviews of tourists about visiting Shepelevsky lighthouse





The lighthouse is one of the main symbols of marine romance. Many people living far from the coast have never seen such architectural structures with their own eyes. There are quite a lot of lighthouses in the Leningrad Region, and if possible, you should definitely visit at least a few of them. Shepelevsky lighthouse has an advantageous location. Getting to it is not difficult at all by personal or public transport. The road to this attraction will also enjoy beautiful views. Shepelevsky cape - the place where the lighthouse is installed, is itself quite picturesque. Here you can take great photos. Many tourists claim that it is especially pleasant to admire the lighthouse at sunset. Despite the growing popularity of informal attractions, there is never a large concentration of tourists. The territory beyond the perimeter of the military unit is available for visits. You can take a walk along the beach, climb an impromptu observation deck. This is a great place for a photo shoot, a romantic date, a family walk or travel with friends.
