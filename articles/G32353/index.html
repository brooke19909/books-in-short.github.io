Hurghada - for divers

Although the hotel complex has its own diving center, enthusiasts of exploring the coral world, for a change, seek to get the maximum experience in the best diving clubs in Hurghada, the capital of the Red Sea province, by diving directly from the boat that delivered them to the desired water area. Indeed, in addition to the coral reef located near Tia Heights Makadi Bay, which is quite compact, the main reefs near Hurghada are located further from the coastal zone. Crowded with populations of coral fish and various marine objects, they are located in the vicinity of Giftun Island, in a paid marine park. The most famous among them coral gardens are Shaab Ruhr Um Gamar, which is also called the encyclopedia of the Red Sea, and Gotha Abu Ramad.

However, the most interesting place experienced divers strive for in the waters of Hurghada is Karless Reef, which is also called the Moray House.

Divers staying at the hotel we are considering seek to visit the above reef gardens, painted in a wide range of colors: from bright pink to piercing blue (by the way, only living corals are painted).
