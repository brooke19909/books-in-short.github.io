Tunisia: North Africa resort climate

Many tourists believe that in African countries it is warm all year round, so if you go there in winter, you can swim in the sea and sunbathe on the beach. Unfortunately, Tunisia does not have such privileges. The climate in this country in the north is Mediterranean, and in the south - continental. The state has recently become the number one resort for European tourists, it is famous for its sun and beaches. Many travelers come to Tunisia in the winter, firstly, accommodation prices, and many products during this period are reduced, and secondly, this is the golden season for divers and surfers.



Those who do not like extreme heat are advised to visit Tunisia during the offseason. The climate here is most favorable from March to May and from September to the end of October. In the northern part of the country, the swimming season lasts from May to October, and then it begins to rain until January. Closer to the south, the weather is always hotter, but in summer the heat is tempered by sea breezes. True, people who cannot tolerate high humidity in the summer months are better off not coming to the Mediterranean Sea to Tunisia.

The climate is very good in the velvet season, which begins in September. During this period, most tourists go home, the sun is not so scorching, so relaxing here is a pleasure. The climate in Tunisia in August in the interior of the country is not the best to visit, because it is very hot, except for the area near the mountains of Tbes. You should come to the center of the country in spring and autumn, because if summer is very hot here, then winter is cold and snowy. If near the coast from December to February the temperature does not fall below +13 ¬∞ C, then in the interior regions there are often frosts.



The climate in Tunisia in November is most suitable for relaxing in the Sahara desert and southern oases, favorable weather persists until March. In winter, the nights here are very cold and the sirocco wind blows, which causes sandstorms in the interior. Depending on personal preferences and expectations from the rest, everyone should choose their own time for a trip to Tunisia. The climate in the north and south is favorable almost all year round, of course, swimming in the sea in winter will not work, but there are other interesting activities.

In the summer, tourists come to this African country to sunbathe, have fun, and swim in the sea. In winter, visitors are fewer and the heat does not bother, so you can safely explore all the interesting sights, admire the local landscapes, learn more about the history and cultural heritage of the country. Arriving in Tunisia during the winter months, you can save a lot, because during this period the prices for souvenirs, bl

in restaurants, travel packages and airline tickets. But for lovers of active water recreation, winter is considered to be the best season, the weather helps to provide conditions for surfing.

Whatever period you have to go to Tunisia, there is always something to do in this wonderful country. The rich cultural and architectural heritage can be appreciated in any weather, so you should not look at the temperature indicators, but rather not hesitate to pack your bags and go to meet new experiences.
