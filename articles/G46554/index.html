Smartphone "Asus Zenfon 5": reviews, specifications, settings, firmware

ASUS is one of the most recognized brands of mobile electronics manufacturers in Russia and in the world. The company supplies the market with high-quality, stylish and multi-functional devices. These include the Asus Zenfone 5 smartphone . What is remarkable about this device? What do its owners say about gaining experience using it?





Specifications

The first aspect in which we can explore Asus Zenfon 5 is the characteristics. Among the key:

  • Intel Atom Z2560 dual-core processor with a frequency of 1.6 GHz, equipped with PowerVR SGX544MP2 accelerator;
  • Android operating system in version 4.3 Jelly Bean, there is a ZenUI shell;
  • 5-inch display, resolution - 1280 by 720 pixels (type HD), manufactured using IPS technology, equipped with Gorilla Glass 3, displays 16 million colors;
  • RAM - 2 GB;
  • built-in flash memory of 16 GB, it is possible to install additional microSD modules;
  • Communications: USB 2.0 cable, Bluetooth in version 4, Wi-Fi;
  • battery capacity 2110 mAh.




At the disposal of the user, judging by the characteristics, a sufficiently productive and technological smartphone is provided. But do mobile gadget lovers share this view?

Features: user reviews

According to some users, the frequency of 1.6 GHz is not enough to perform modern tasks. However, in this case, the decisive role is played by another - the Atom Z2560 chip technology. The specificity of this chip lies in the ability to quickly process 3D graphics at a relatively low frequency. Thus, the processor in this case is a factor of high-performance smartphone.

Regarding the other characteristics, users generally rate them quite positively. And this is not surprising, because the device is equipped with an HD-display made using modern technology, quite competitive performance in RAM and flash memory, and also supports basic communication standards.

The general opinion of users about the characteristics of the device we have studied. Now consider the specifics of the design of the device.

Display

The Asus Zenfon 5 phone is equipped with a 5-inch display, which has a resolution of 720 by 1280 pixels, which corresponds to the HD standard. The phone screen is made using modern IPS technology. The display density is 294 ppi. Thus, the graininess of the screen is almost invisible.

The brightness of the display is estimated by experts and users as quite sufficient. Viewing angles are large. The display is comfortable to use even in the presence of glare. There is an oleophobic coating, due to which the surface of the screen is easy to clean. With the same comfort, you can view photos, videos, launch games and various applications on the display of the Asus Zenfon 5 smartphone.

The maximum brightness of the device display is 315 cd / sq.m. However, the actual comfort of using the device depends on the brightness level of the image. The contrast ratio of the screen is 755: 1, it can be considered quite typical for the corresponding hardware component, which is manufactured using IPS technology.

The smartphone screen, like the one installed on many other modern devices, has a multi-touch function and processes 5 simultaneous touches.

As a result, the load on the eyes of a person who reads an e-book using the Asus Zenfon 5 phone is significantly reduced. It is noteworthy that the phone can be used with gloves on. Setting up the Asus Zenfon 5 smartphone in terms of activating the corresponding option is very simple.
