Dreams La Romana Resort & SPA 5 * (Dominican Republic, Province of La Romana, La Romana): description with photo, overview of services, reviews of tourists

Dominican Republic is ideal for a relaxing holiday at sea. It is famous for its long beaches with soft white sand and a first-class level of service. Of course, rest on this island can not be called budget, so save on travel will not work. If you are looking for a respectable hotel, then pay attention to the beach resort Dreams La Romana Resort & Spa 5 * in the Dominican Republic. A detailed description will be presented in this article.

Where is the hotel situated?

This complex has a favorable location. It is built directly on the coast, so tourists can walk to the beach in 2-3 minutes. Hotel Dreams La Romana Resort & Spa 5 * in the Dominican Republic is located in the resort of Bayahibe near the city of La Romana. It is worth noting that it is completely surrounded by a tropical garden, so there are no walking promenades and other infrastructure facilities near it. This hotel assumes accommodation on an all-inclusive basis, so often tourists almost never leave its territory during the holidays. If desired, the center of La Romana can be reached by calling a paid taxi. There are no historical attractions near the complex either.

20 km from La Romana is a small airport. But it does not accept flights from Russia, so it is usually used only by local residents. Most often, tourists from Russia who come to rest at this hotel fly to Punta Cana International Airport. You can get to the resort from it in about 45 minutes. The easiest way to get to the hotel is by taxi. Also, if necessary, transfer payment can be included in advance in the price. Then a comfortable bus equipped with air conditioning will pick up tourists from the airport.

general information

The beach complex Dreams La Romana Resort & Spa 5 * can offer tourists a huge territory, most of which is occupied by a lush tropical garden. It is ideal for family and romantic, as well as outdoor activities at sea. But it is worth noting that this is a rather large and densely populated hotel. On its territory there are 10 three-story residential buildings. In total, the complex offers tourists 756 comfortable rooms. They are designed for both standard double and family accommodation. Therefore, with full occupancy, more than 1,500 people live in the hotel. Most often, tourists from Europe and the USA come here, so the administrators here speak excellent English. But there are no employees who speak Russian.

This hotel welcomes tourists with children of all ages, including infants. Children over 2 years old are charged separately. When registering, guests must present to the administrator not only an identity card with a photo, but also their bank card. The hotel rooms are strictly non-smoking. In addition, you cannot bring pets to the territory of the complex, as well as any unmanned aerial vehicles and your own water transport.

Room facilities

All rooms at the Dreams La Romana in the Dominican Republic are furnished with modern and comfortable furniture. Guests can stay in double or single beds, put their belongings in wardrobes or cabinets. The bathroom includes a hairdryer, make-up mirror, bath amenities and towels.

In the hotel rooms tourists can find the following equipment:

  • Plasma TV and a set of satellite TV channels connected to it;
  • individual air conditioning;
  • ironing facilities and iron;
  • Radio alarm
  • a minibar with free drinks - it is replenished daily with drinking water, beer and juice;
  • safe for storing documents and money;
  • wireless Internet.

Additional facilities for disabled guests are not available. Housekeeping and linen change every day.

Territory and service

On the vast territory of the Dreams La Romana 5 * hotel in the Dominican Republic, a developed infrastructure is created that allows tourists to relax comfortably in the highest class. The complex has a 24-hour front desk where you can exchange currency, arrange bike rental or purchase a tour ticket. For businessmen, the hotel has a business center with 9 conference rooms. The largest of them holds up to 515 delegates. In addition, several shops selling jewelry, cigars, and souvenirs are opened in the main building. Also, tourists can visit the beauty salon, laundry and hairdresser.

Beach and Pool

Hotel Dreams Dominicus La Romana in the Dominican Republic is a large beach complex with access to its own stretch of coast. All guests are provided with sun loungers, umbrellas that save from the sun, towels and mattresses. You can use them for free. For guests' convenience, a bar is located right by the sea, where you can order a drink, a cocktail or have a light snack and ice cream. There is also a huge water entertainment center on the beach. Tourists can:

  • windsurfing;
  • ride a catamaran, kayaks, canoes and motor boats;
  • attend diving and snorkeling lessons.

You can also sunbathe on the spacious terrace, equipped by the outdoor pools. There are 4 of them at the hotel. Moreover, 2 of them are intended only for guests staying in Preferred Club rooms.

Entertainment

The Dreams Dominicus La Romana 5 * complex in the Dominican Republic is ideal for an active or relaxing holiday. For any tourists there is entertainment for everyone. So, on the territory of the hotel almost every day evening discos and parties are held outdoors. In the evening, tourists can watch movies on the big screen right on the beach or go to the casino. But entrance to it is charged separately. Every day, hotel staff conduct classes to teach tourists how to dance merengue or Spanish. Day and evening, the complex has an English-speaking team of animators.

In addition, the hotel has a huge spa, but its visit is not included in the price. In this center, tourists can book a massage course, as well as wellness and beauty treatments. There is also a jacuzzi, sauna and steam room.

The hotel has excellent conditions for outdoor activities. Guests can:

  • play tennis (including table tennis), volleyball or darts;
  • sign up for aerobics lessons in water gymnastics;
  • Take a trip to the golf course
  • visit the gym;
  • rent a pleasure bike;
  • ride horses.

In addition, board games are offered free of charge to tourists. The garden has chess tables. In the evenings there is a billiard room.

Holidays with young children

Due to the picturesque nature and clear sea, the Dreams La Romana hotel is often chosen by tourists with small children for relaxation. When you make a voucher, you should immediately determine the date of arrival, because underage guests are not accepted here for some time. Otherwise, their placement order is standard. Children are provided with a separate crib in their room. All restaurants have high chairs where you can comfortably feed your little ones. For a fee, you can always call a nanny in your room.

An outdoor playground is on site. There is also a mini-club, where professional animators are involved in the organization of children's leisure. For convenience, several age groups are distinguished. Very small children (from 3 years old) and teenagers (up to 17 years old) can visit the mini-club.

Positive feedback about Dreams La Romana Resort & Spa 5 *

If you are looking for a comfortable option for your stay right on the beach in the Dominican Republic, then this hotel is perfect for you. This is exactly what tourists who have rested here earlier consider. In reviews, they almost always enthusiastically describe this place. Often guests return here several times, considering this hotel one of the best in the resort.

Why did they like this complex so much? In their opinion, it has many advantages:

  • a clean beach, where employees regularly clean garbage, and a calm sea, which is ideal for bathing young children;
  • a varied diet, the menu always has a lot of fresh seasonal fruits and natural juices from them;
  • a large landscaped area where you can walk in the shade of trees after relaxing on the beach;
  • excellent leisure organization, there is a team of animators that does not let tourists get bored;
  • there are many non-motorized boats on the beach, which you can swim on your own without an instructor;
  • spacious and bright rooms with new furniture and well-functioning appliances.

Negative reviews

Of course, there are no ideal hotels, therefore, Dreams Dominicus La Romana also has a number of drawbacks, which tourists mention in their reviews. However, they usually call them insignificant. Therefore, they did not spoil their impressions of the rest in this place. Nevertheless, it is worthwhile to familiarize yourself with the shortcomings so that upon arrival at the hotel you will not be upset because of overstated expectations. So, according to tourists, the management of the complex should take care of eliminating the following disadvantages of local service:

  • a large number of people living in the hotel, because of which there are always queues on the beach and in restaurants;
  • not the best quality cleaning, employees do not wipe the furniture from dust;
  • inattentive administrators, including in the bill for accommodation services and drinks that guests did not order;
  • some rooms and public areas need redecorating;
  • Most of the staff do not speak English well.

To summarize

Thus, the beach resort Dreams La Romana 5 * can be safely called one of the best in La Romana. This huge hotel is perfect for a relaxing, and for an active pastime at sea. Often, respectable tourists from Europe come here, which is also an indicator of quality. It can be recommended to both youth companies and couples in love, and families with small children.
