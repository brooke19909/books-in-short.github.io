Sea

For many tourists, an important advantage in choosing is that the By Dee Jomtien Hotel 3 * hotel is built a few steps from the sea and one of the best beaches in terms of cleanliness of Pattaya. However, it also loses significantly in terms of cleanliness and infrastructure, for example, the beaches of Greece or even our Black Sea coast. But the water here is warm all year, even in the rainy season. The beach, although located near the hotel, is urban, so everyone can relax here, but you need to pay for sunbeds and umbrellas. For traders of food, clothes, towels, souvenirs and anything else, the entrance here is absolutely free, so they defile along the coast all day and offer their goods.

But most tourists with the aim of a beach holiday in Pattaya go to the neighboring island of Koh Lan. From the hotel you need to go there first by tuk-tuk to the pier, then by boat, the ticket price for which can vary from 30 to 200 baht. In the latter case, upon arrival to the island, you are provided with a tuk-tuk free of charge, which takes customers to the best of the island beaches, and takes them back at the agreed time. Having bought a cheap ticket, tourists pay again for further travel. In addition, for 30 baht you will be transported for about an hour, and for 100, especially for 150, they will be delivered four times faster.
