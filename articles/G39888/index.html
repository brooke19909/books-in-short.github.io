Sanatoriums Druskininkai. The best sanatorium Druskininkai (reviews)

Since the eighteenth century, Lithuania has boasted its famous and ancient mud baths. Nowadays, any sanatorium in Druskininkai can offer balneological procedures to clients - this is the name of the wonderful area, which is located one hundred and twenty kilometers from Vilnius. It has everything - water that rejuvenates the body, and healing mud, and a wonderful climate for walking, and healing air for the lungs. The conditions, service and quality of the natural components that are used for treatment are similar to those that can be found in the best European resorts. But the prices here are much lower. Read about how you can relax and improve your health on this famous Lithuanian land and which sanatoriums are considered the best.



Resort Feature

First of all, Druskininkai is famous for its mineral waters. They treat diseases of the stomach and intestines, heart and joints, spine and skin, as well as female ailments. There are also rich peat-based mud deposits. They are used in procedures to treat the skeleton, muscle tissue, nerves, and the genitourinary system. You should know this when choosing a sanatorium for Druskininkai for yourself. This area is far from industrial zones. It is surrounded by pine forests, wild lakes where swans nest, sand dunes and meadows. A lot of healing paths have been laid along it. There is a professional health resort and many excellent resorts. They offer tourists and vacationers treatment courses, spa treatment programs, as well as beauty services. Even simple walks in the surroundings for a couple of weekends will allow you to regain strength, forget about stresses and crises and at least a little “emerge” from the everyday sea of ​​everyday routine. And if you know how to ride a bike, then you have a great vacation.



Egle

This hospital ranks first in the top reviews of Russian and foreign tourists. She rightfully bears the title of "the best sanatorium in Druskininkai." Here, vacationers can use the whole complex of pools filled with healing mineral water. Have your own pump room. The resort is located almost in a pine forest. He takes guests to rest and treatment all year round. All procedures and courses are developed individually by the specialists of the sanatorium. Local specialists will not only be interested in your diagnosis, but will also conduct their own research. Conditions have been created here for small patients. Egle Sanatorium (Druskininkai) is interesting in that its concept is based both on modern achievements of European science and on the traditions of treatment that developed in the Soviet era. In addition to wellness programs, he also offers special packages for people who want to lose weight, get rid of cellulite, and overcome stress. The rooms in the sanatorium are comfortable, meet all European standards. There is Internet access, satellite TV, a library with Russian books. This is the largest resort in Eastern Europe. His new wing, the Egle Plus, has suites.



Grand Spa Lietuva and Vilnius Sana

The next most popular spa resort is a huge health resort with a wellness center. It is located in the center of the town and has its own mineral spring. With it, several hotels and apartments are open for the convenience of vacationers. For its guests, this complex offers more than a hundred different procedures, according to the directions of treatment at the Druskininkai resort. “Belarus” and “Draugiste”

But this is not all the hospitals that Druskininkai is famous for. Sanatoriums “Belarus” and “Draugiste” also received a lot of praise. And this is not just statistics. “Belarus” is the most famous sanatorium in Lithuania. He is under "double citizenship." Belongs to the health resort of the Republic of Belarus, so the prices here are lower than in other resorts. It is located in the forest, famous not only for its source, but also for peat mud. It treats the stomach, intestines, joints, heart, nervous system. "Draugiste" stands on the banks of the Neman, in an old park. His patients live in four hotels nearby. His specialization is similar to the directions that are involved in Belarus. But the Draugist also treats the effects of ionizing radiation.



Dainava

This is a very small complex. Specialists in the treatment of nervous disorders, respiratory organs, gynecological problems work here. People who have had heart surgery come here to undergo rehabilitation. Near the "Dainava" are all the main attractions of Druskininkai, including the water park. So, despite its small size, it is a very popular and popular sanatorium.

Entertainment

If you come here in the summer, you can not only heal, but also have a good time. Every sanatorium in Druskininkai will offer you a first-class cultural program. Moreover, the resort at this time turns into a real tourist Mecca of Lithuania. A wonderful water park works here, where you can not only enjoy riding on slides of different heights and inclinations, but also take a steam bath in saunas, of which there are more than twenty. And there are many cafes, clubs and restaurants. Druskininkai invites for rest and lovers of quiet walks, and cheerful youth, and older people who want to maintain health. The opening of the holiday season and farewell to the summer is accompanied by interesting and fun holidays, which tourists do not recommend skipping.
