Terrace Hotel, Kabardinka: reviews

In the village of Kabardinka, one of the most attractive corners of the Krasnodar Territory, the Terrace Hotel is located . Kabardinka is a resort village located between Gelendzhik and Novorossiysk. The hotel was opened in 2014 and today is rightfully considered one of the best places to stay in the village.





Description

Hotel Terrace (Kabardinka) is located in the heart of the resort village. The hotel has a heated swimming pool on the roof, and a sunbathing area. From the roof, vacationers can enjoy beautiful views of the village and the sea. In the cozy courtyard for guests there is a gazebo with barbecue. The sea is almost nearby, the promenade and pebble beach are located at a distance of 500-600 meters from the hotel. It's about seven minutes on foot. For children, the hotel has a games room. Free parking is available for guests arriving on holiday by car. Near the hotel there is a market, pharmacy, supermarket "Magnet". Within walking distance are also a dining room, Sberbank and a bus station. If you want to see the sights, then you can walk to them. If tourists want to visit the "City of Kuban Masters", it is located one kilometer from the hotel. Nearby is also the church of St. Panteleimon. Active guests can rent bicycles. The distance to the airport of Gelendzhik is 12 kilometers. Check in at the hotel at 14.00, estimated time - 12.00. If you decide to come to the Terrace Hotel (Kabardinka), his address is : Russia, Krasnodar Territory, s. Kabardinka, Mira street, 33a.





Accommodation

The Terrace Hotel (Kabardinka) offers its guests a number of categories of rooms for relaxation.

  • The standard double room - its cost is from 1700 to 3500 rubles per day, depending on the season. The rooms are located on the ground floor of the inn.
  • Double room - the price of this room ranges from 1,500 to 5,000 rubles per day. The rooms are located on the second and third floors of the hotel.
  • Triple room - costs from 1800 to 6000 rubles per day, depending on the season.
  • Quadruple suite - price from 2100 to 6800 per room per night.
  • Family suite - its cost is from 2100 to 6800 rubles per day.

Breakfast is included. Children under 5 years old without extra bed are free of charge. Pets are not allowed. If you plan to book a room, then pay for two nights. Offers rooms of European standard hotel "Terrace" (Kabardinka). A photo of one of the rooms is given below.





Room Amenities

The Terrace Hotel (Kabardinka) offers its guests European-level amenities. Each room for vacationers provides:

  • large plasma TV (satellite TV);
  • air conditioning;
  • refrigerator;
  • a small safe for storing valuables and documents;
  • Private bathroom
  • towels;
  • hair dryer, slippers;
  • toiletries;
  • comfortable modern furniture;
  • hallway, mirror.

All rooms, except for standard double, have a private balcony. Also rooms in standard double rooms have a window only in the bathroom. On each floor for the convenience of guests there is a water cooler, as well as an ironing board and an iron. Room cleaning and towel change - daily. Once every five days, bed linen is changed. It is possible to place additional space. All rooms are non-smoking.

Mini-hotel "Terrace" in Kabardinka offers a number of additional services to vacationers for a more comfortable stay:

  • Air conditioning in public areas.
  • Free parking is provided (limited number of parking spaces).
  • Near the pool there are sun loungers and umbrellas.
  • At the request of guests, a taxi call is provided.

  • Bike rental is available.
  • The hotel has its own dining room.
  • For those who wish to cook their own equipped common kitchen.

  • Reception is open 24 hours a day.




Hotel "Terrace". sights

While on vacation in the Terrace mini-hotel, tourists have the opportunity to visit the attractions located in the village of Kabardinka:

  • The old park is one of the most popular and visited places in Kabardinka. The famous artist, sculptor and architect Alexander Alekseev took part in its creation . In the park you can get acquainted with the history and culture of various countries.
  • The unique wellness place of Kabardinka is the Kastalsky font. Its main attraction is the lake, about which many mysterious legends are composed. In addition, trout and sturgeon are found in large quantities in it, so there are always a lot of fishermen. There is a restaurant on the territory of the tourist complex, and its chefs can immediately cook the fish you catch here.
  • Not far from the market square in Kabardinka, another attraction recently appeared - the House upside down. The house is built so that its roof is on the ground. Going inside, you are standing on the ceiling. This is a very unusual building, which is definitely worth a visit.

Terrace Hotel (Kabardinka): positive reviews

Most vacationers here have the best impressions about the hotel:

  • Guests enjoyed the polite and friendly staff.
  • On the territory and in the hotel itself is calm, there is no noise.
  • The rooms are spacious, all amenities.
  • The rooms are clean, tidy, comfortable and cool.
  • The rooms are beautiful, comfortable, modern furniture.
  • Dinner can be heated in the microwave in the shared kitchen, if desired.
  • In the mornings, delicious cooked breakfasts are served. Guests especially enjoyed the cooked local fish. It is possible to order lunch and dinner.
  • There is an excellent pool on the roof, it helps out well when in the heat you do not want to go to a crowded beach.
  • Guests are especially grateful to the hotel staff for a well-organized mountain tour.
  • The helicopter tour over the coast was especially remembered.
  • Very convenient location of the hotel, near the market, shops, cafes.
  • Beautiful picturesque surroundings, there is something to see.
  • Many guests consider the value for money in the Kabardinka hotel to be excellent.




Negative reviews

The Terrace Hotel (Kabardinka) received negative reviews from tourists on some points.

  • Many guests believe that the beach is far to go, especially with young children.
  • Some are unhappy with the small selection of breakfast options.
  • Wi-Fi sometimes does not work well.
  • There are tourists who are unhappy that the roof terrace is closed in the evening. They wanted to admire the night views of the sea from above.
  • Complaints to the staff in terms of the fact that on the day of departure they asked to stay at the hotel until 13.00, but they were not allowed.
  • Guests also believe that there is clearly not enough refreshment bar near the pool.
  • Some vacationers believe that hotel prices are a bit overpriced.

Conclusion

Of course, one of the most modern and comfortable in the Gelendzhik district of the Krasnodar Territory is the Terrace Hotel (Kabardinka). How to get there . You can book a transfer at the hotel or come by taxi. Guests are always welcome here.
