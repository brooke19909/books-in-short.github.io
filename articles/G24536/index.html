How to make a truck out of Lego and what is the use of such an activity?

Lego is a wonderful tool for developing attention, perseverance, logical thinking and imagination of children. Lego military truck

If your child wonders how to make a Lego military truck, this is not a reason to get lost. It is enough to understand how military equipment can slightly differ from ordinary. Build from the parts of "Lego" the likeness of locators, guns or other tools and equip your creation with them - the military truck is ready!
