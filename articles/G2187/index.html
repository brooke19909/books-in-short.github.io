The cheapest hostel in Moscow: address, description of rooms, photos, reviews

For those who travel independently, the topic of finding cheap housing is relevant, in order to reduce costs for this cost item. The article will discuss what a hostel is and how to book places to stay in the cheapest hostels in Moscow.

What is a hostel?

The name Hostel is of English origin, and translates as a hostel. These were once, in fact, dormitories with cheap rooms in which students lived. Currently, the hostel is considered a comfortable, with a good reputation economy class hotel. The hostel has all the basic services. The main difference from rooms in ordinary hotels is the lack of a shower and toilet in the rooms for living. These amenities are located on the floor, as, in principle, this was in student dormitories.

The hostels have the opportunity to rent a room for one and two guests. To significantly save on accommodation, it is possible to rent a bed in a dormitory room designed for people of the same sex. Many travelers, sometimes, are looking for a room in the cheapest hostel in Moscow, not only for living, but also for the sake of an atmosphere of communication and new acquaintances with the same travelers as themselves.

Hostel Standards

In world practice, there are hostels of various standards, ranging from rooms without special amenities, where there are only beds, to boutique hostels where there are not so Spartan conditions. Services that are considered mandatory include a hot shower, clean bed and breakfast (not always, sometimes by appointment). Breakfast is included in the price. There is a laundry room, which, in addition to washing linens, serves, if necessary, customers.

There is a new type of housing - capsule hostel on the Arbat. Such a convenience has already been appreciated by many travelers in their reviews. What it is? These are designed sleeping places in dormitory rooms. They are somewhat similar to mini cabins in which neighboring snoring is not heard. Comfort is created through the organization of personal space. The capsule is convenient for rest, sleep, exercise and work. It has individual lighting, sockets and Wi-FI. And all this is blocked off by a dense curtain.

When looking for the cheapest hostel in Moscow for the long term, you should take into account the strict rules of accommodation that apply in it and the services that are provided in it:

  • regulation of check-in registration time and check-out time - estimated hour;
  • closing at night at a specific time (does not apply everywhere and not for everyone);
  • daily cleaning of all rooms;
  • Internet access and Wi-Fi;
  • DVD collection

Some hostels have lockers for storing things, lockers and safe boxes. More and more hostels organize co- working zones (Eng. Co-working - collaboration, work on the Internet), equipped with computers. Time requires having it in the arsenal of services.

In Moscow hostels, in contrast to European hostels, customers are given linen for a bed and towels. This is noted in their reviews by tourists. There is no additional charge, this is included in the price. The availability of breakfast in each hostel is negotiated. Either it is included in the payment for accommodation, and this is a cup of coffee with a sandwich, or not included, and for an additional fee, it can be prepared.

Rent hostel for family

Quite often, a search is made for the cheapest hostel in Moscow for a long period for a family. Typically, this option is looking for young people who come to Moscow for preparatory classes for admission to the university and for exams. It can be a tourist family who wants to go to historical places and visit the sights of the capital on their own. A hostel is the best option for this. All day goes on excursions, and the night - to relax. An option that involves renting a room for a predetermined period is the best suited for a family vacation.

How to book a room?

You can book a room in the hostel without leaving your home, or even while on the go, with a smartphone with the Internet. Now there is no such hostel and hotel that would not have its own website or email. That is, you can order a room through the search and booking form on the website indicating the date of arrival and the number of days of stay. You can make an order by e-mail, or by phone, if known.

The best option, of course, is the search for suitable housing on the sites of Moscow hostels. You have the opportunity to independently find a hostel in Moscow that is convenient for you, for example, inexpensively near the metro and rent it if your stay in the capital will be connected with travel by this type of transport.
