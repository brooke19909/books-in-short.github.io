Decor ideas for little gentlemen, or How to decorate boots for a boy

We make an application

For example, decor in the style of applique - how do you like this option? Suitable for your little man? To bring it to life, you will need a piece of old felt or felt. Option for those who know how to knit

Here is another idea. But this option is suitable only for those who know how to knit. There is no need for glue or other additional materials. Knitting needles and yarn are all you need. Find yarn that matches your felt boots. For children's shoes, you can use several bright colors. Take a measurement on the upper girth of the felt boots and dial the corresponding number of loops on the knitting needles. For this knitting, you can use two knitting needles or five, as you prefer. A plus of the latter option is the absence of a seam on the finished canvas.

Work order

So, we choose a suitable jacquard pattern (in the photo this is a combination of white and gray yarn) or embossed and we knit a fabric of the desired length. Then it is necessary to fix it on the inside of the felt boots and unscrew it to the front side. Everything - your unique boots are ready! Such will be very difficult not to notice.

Please note that this method of decorating children's boots is suitable for both boys and girls. It all depends on the color and pattern. How to decorate felt boots, what colors to use, what width to do the lapel - decide for yourself, focusing on your taste.

The simplest ideas for decor boots

But how to decorate boots for those who always have no time for these purposes.

The first option will require bright yarn and a thick needle. We thread a piece of yarn into a needle and along the front of the felt boots we make an embroidery that resembles lacing (large crosses). This will take you a maximum of 10 minutes. We leave the ends of the completed lacing more authentic. We fix pre-prepared brushes from the same yarn or pompoms on them. We knit on a bow and wear such boots with pleasure.

For the second option you will need a beautiful braid or lace, rhinestones and glue. Then it all depends on your imagination. The braid can be placed on the upper edge of the boots, and the rest of the surface is decorated with rhinestones. To do this with a glue gun is very convenient and does not take much time. But the result will definitely not go unnoticed.

Both of these simple decor methods will answer your question: "How to decorate felt boots for girls?"

And in order to adapt these options for men, you can simply change the color of yarn in the first option, and in the second you can replace the braid and rhinestones, for example, with decorative chains and jewelry in the military style (stars, anchors, buckles and so on).
