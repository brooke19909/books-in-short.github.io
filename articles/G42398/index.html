How to change the tariff for MegaFon? Simply!

MegaFon annually advertises the appearance of new tariffs. And, of course, their main difference from the previous ones is lower prices for communication services. So, the subscriber needs to know how to change the tariff for MegaFon in this case. And to do this is very easy and simple. In addition, this can be done in many ways. True, first you need to decide which tariff plan the client needs, and this is a little more complicated.

Tariff selection

For its subscribers, the company often develops far from one tariff, but a whole line. And this is not done at all in order to confuse someone. Obviously, the customers of the mobile operator are completely different. So, they also need various MegaFon tariff plans that satisfy their particular needs. That's why you should first carefully study all the offers of the operator and evaluate your preferences in communication services. And only after that decide how to change the tariff on MegaFon.

And how to do it? First you need to clarify which tariff is already connected. To do this, dial * 105 * 13 #, and the necessary information appears on the display. Now you can take a piece of plain paper and simply compare the prices for services at this tariff and the one you liked. Also, by ordering details for a month, you can see what most of the money is spent on. Someone mostly communicates within the network, while others have friends all over the country. Or, perhaps, the subscriber needs everything at once, but more.



Here for the latter provides MegaFon unlimited tariff "All inclusive". For a monthly fee, the client is provided with a package of minutes, SMS and Internet traffic. It remains only to select the desired package. And those who do not want to limit themselves in communicating with their loved ones from Ukraine, Kazakhstan or Belarus can take advantage of the ‚ÄúWarm welcome‚Äù tariff. There are offers for active Internet users, and for those who are not used to talking a lot.

Tariff change

Just now, after evaluating and choosing a really advantageous offer, it's time to find out how to change the tariff for MegaFon. Naturally, this can be done in different ways. The most commonly used USSD request is * 105 * 20 #. A menu will appear on the phone screen where you just need to select your favorite tariff plan and confirm its change.

Another, no less simple way is to use the Service Guide service. In its menu you need to select the section "Options, services and tariffs", and then "Change tariffs". Now it remains only to confirm your choice by clicking the "Order" button. Those who find it difficult to make a shift on their own can contact the company's contact center for help, leave a request on the site or come in person to the office.

Service Features

In any case, the service is provided free of charge, but it has its own characteristics. The new tariff plan will only become effective from the beginning of the next day, and if there is a prepaid minute or SMS traffic on the number, then only from the beginning of the next month. It is important to remember that if the connection is suspended on the tariff, you will not be able to return to it. In fact, it does not matter how to change the tariff at MegaFon, the main thing is not to be disappointed in your choice.
