Marina Plaza Resort Tala Bay 4 * (Jordan / Aqaba): photos and reviews of tourists

Marina Plaza Resort Tala Bay 4 * is a wonderful hotel built in a traditional Arabic style. It is relatively new since the hotel was opened in 2008. Over the years, the hotel has welcomed thousands of tourists who want to relax on the Red Sea, near the Gulf of Aqaba. And now, along with the popularity of the resort, the popularity of this hotel is growing.





Location

Located Marina Plaza Resort Tala Bay 4 * on the coast of Aqaba. You can reach the sea in just a couple of minutes. The beaches here are good, sandy. Interestingly, the coast of Aqaba extends 27 kilometers in length. Here the holiday season always reigns, because the air temperature even in winter does not fall below the 22 degree mark.

The beach at the hotel, by the way, is private. It is not very far from the city center, but nonetheless, guests here are provided with a free shuttle service to those places. Aqaba has its own King Hussein International Airport. It can also be reached quickly, in 15-20 minutes.

By the way, literally a stone's throw from the hotel there is a bus stop, several hypermarkets, shops, cafes and restaurants. From here you can also get to the underwater observatory, coral beach, Aqaba port and other local attractions. In general, the location is successful and advantageous in all plans.

What is available in the hotel?

Marina Plaza Resort Tala Bay 4 * provides its guests absolutely everything that they may need. On the territory there is an ATM, free car parking and Wi-Fi, a luggage storage room, two meeting rooms, a computer center, an office with a fax and photocopy machine, as well as a large conference room.

There is also a laundry with dry cleaning, ironing, a shared lounge, smoking areas, a souvenir shop, and a free newspaper, drinks and food delivery service. In one mini-market, which is in the hotel, you can buy only drinks and snacks. In the other - a wider choice. There is everything from household chemicals to canned goods. Therefore, when it becomes necessary to buy something, people may not even leave the hotel.

Reception at the hotel is open around the clock. You can go there for any question - be it the need to call a nanny or a doctor. Such an opportunity, by the way, is available. Needless to say, if the hotel has its own marina on a private beach.





Entertainment

In the territory of Marina Plaza Resort Tala Bay 4 * (Jordan), in addition to services that are designed to make the stay of guests as comfortable as possible, there are still different ways to have fun. Naturally, there is an outdoor pool and a sun terrace. In addition, the hotel provides professional equipment for water sports. When staying at the hotel, you should definitely try windsurfing or diving. You can rent a canoe, go water skiing. There is also everything you need for beach volleyball. And, of course, such trifles as darts and table tennis are also available here.

Fans of a more active lifestyle will definitely like the presence of a gym. After strength training, you can go to the SPA-center, which also has Marina Plaza Resort Tala Bay 4 * (Jordan). Here you can order cosmetic and wellness treatments. There are offices in which professionals serve couples in love. Many people like to come to the SPA together.

They do stone therapy, Swedish and Thai massages, aroma and hydrotherapy, reflexology. Customers can also visit the steam room, sauna or soak in the jacuzzi. However, a full list of services can be found on the spot. It is worth remembering only one thing - only adults are allowed into the SPA.
