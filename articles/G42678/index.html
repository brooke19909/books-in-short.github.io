Modifications

During mass production (1946-1958), the GAZ-M20 was released in several versions.

  1. The main prototype for the ‚ÄúVictory M20 DeAgostini‚Äù was the first modification (M20). The car received a heater, blowing windshield, new parabolic springs and an improved thermostat. Since 1950, the car has been equipped with a gearbox from a ZiM machine and a new liquid pump.
  2. The M20V version is the third generation of the modernized Victory (produced from 1955 to 1958). Among the features - a motor with a capacity of 52 "horses", a radio and an updated grille.
  3. M20A. the car went off the assembly line in the back of a fastback sedan, was focused on operation in a taxi service (1949-1958).
  4. M20B - convertible had reinforced safety arcs (1949-1953).
  5. GAZ-M20D (1956-1958). The vehicle was intended for the MGB, had a forced motor due to increased compression.
  6. M20G (M26). A high-speed version with a six-cylinder power plant with a capacity of 90 horsepower.
  7. M72. The modification is based on an army jeep with all-wheel drive chassis. Features - increased ground clearance, the presence of protective shields on the wheel arches, all-terrain protectors.
