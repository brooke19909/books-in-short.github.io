Simeiz, hotels: rating, reviews

Twenty kilometers from Yalta, between Alupka and Katsiveli, at the foot of the picturesque Mount Koshka, there is a quiet, cozy village Simeiz. Hotels, hotels, motels, as well as unique nature, mountains, cliffs, the purest turquoise sea, crystal clear air - there are a lot of advantages in the village. Admiring the picturesque landscapes of Simeiz is a great pleasure. Having been here once, as tourists say, I want to come here again and again.

Cozy welcoming village Simeiz: hotels, guest houses, inns

In Simeiz, pristine wildlife is very successfully combined with numerous hotels, villas, motels, rest houses with beautiful architecture and magnificent views of the sea and mountains. Their territories are surrounded by green parks, with unique southern plants.





If you wish, you can settle at the very sea and fall asleep to the sound of the surf. In the center of the village there is also a huge number of private mini-hotels and guest houses, where you will be welcomed as friendly people by the host family. For those who want to be alone with nature, listen to the birds singing and enjoy the smell of pine needles, at the very foot of Mount Koshka, in the extraordinary silence of a juniper grove, cozy cottages are for rent.





Based on the reviews and ratings of tourists about housing in the village of Simeiz, the hotels of which are numerous and diverse, we can single out the rating of the most popular holiday destinations.

Hotel "Ligo Morskaya", Simeiz. Reviews

The resort complex "Ligo Morskaya" is one of the first places in the rating of hotels in Simeiz. It is located in an old park, on the seashore.

Rooms of different classes are rented in the main building - an old beautiful building with magnificent architecture. There is also a guest house, which is located right by the sea.





The hotel is located a hundred meters from the beach. The complex has a cafe "Venice", parking, there is a billiard room.

According to the reviews of vacationers, it can be said that hotel customers note a beautiful territory, beautiful nature, but easy neglect. The guest house on the shore of a relatively new building, so everyone was satisfied, but the reviews about the rooms in the old building are mixed. Many recommend overhaul in the rooms and in the building, as well as improve the level of service.

One of the minuses is the remoteness of the hotel from the center of the village, 20-25 minutes you need to go to shops and cafes, but the advantage is its proximity to the sea.
