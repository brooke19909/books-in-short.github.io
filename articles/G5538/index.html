Brief information about the recreation center





Pension "Cold River" (Abkhazia, Gagra) is located on the slope of the Gagra Range, in a picturesque place. Around lies a pine forest, giving fresh clean air. The boarding house was built back in 1938. The last reconstruction was carried out in 2008. Then the buildings were repaired, furniture was replaced, air conditioners were installed everywhere.

Now "Cold River" is a modern resort where you can relax inexpensively and budget. The territory of the boarding house is not very large (less than 2 hectares), but is landscaped with beautiful southern plants. The resort is popular among tourists who come to Abkhazia on their own, in company or with families with children (from any age). A recreation center is open from May 1 to October 31.

Guesthouse





Vacationers are offered cozy and comfortable rooms in the buildings. The total number of rooms is 150 seats. The boarding house has a four-story, two-story and one-story residential building. The following room categories are available:

  • double standard (area 16 sq. m);
  • triple standard (total area - 18-20 sq. m);
  • triple two-room superior (area - 36 sq. m).

All apartments have the necessary furniture (single or double beds with orthopedic mattresses, bedside tables, wardrobes, tables and chairs, one extra bed in the form of a folding bed), as well as a TV and a refrigerator. In the superior room there is also a folding sofa. Almost everywhere air conditioners are installed. Where there are none, fans are provided. Each room has a balcony. The bathroom is equipped with a shower. Cold and hot water is supplied uninterrupted.

In total, the housing stock of the recreation center has fifty rooms, thirty of which are with sea views. Prices for accommodation in the boarding house "Cold River" (Abkhazia) start at 2880 rubles for knocking.

Services and services provided by vacationers





The cost of accommodation in the boarding house "Cold River" (Abkhazia) includes:

  • 24-hour front desk
  • the provision of medical care;
  • housekeeping, which is carried out every other day;
  • linen change once a week;
  • three meals a day;
  • rental of sunbeds and umbrellas for free;
  • guarded parking lot;
  • wireless internet (available only in cafes);
  • children playground;
  • Ironing room (located on each floor).

A safe and luggage storage are available at the reception.

For an additional fee, guests of the recreation center "Cold River" offer a transfer from a checkpoint or Gagra, rent a sun lounger on the beach, sightseeing programs in the surrounding area, ride horses, go fishing. You can also meet at the airport or at the railway station in Adler.

Also, the infrastructure of the boarding house "Cold River" (Abkhazia) includes:

  • a sauna;
  • bar;
  • library
  • a hairdresser;
  • a pharmacy;
  • sports area;
  • pool.

Near the guesthouse there are several small shops where you can buy food, fresh vegetables and fruits, various drinks, as well as souvenirs and other necessary items.

Leisure options at the boarding house and beyond





Most of the places for cultural and recreational activities are located in the center of Gagra. There are concentrated various shops, the city market, water park, bars, cafes, restaurants, discos, as well as the promenade. In the pension, vacationers also have something to do. For example, you can play table tennis or go for a swim in the round pool. Also, vacationers can fry their own fish or meat skewers on the grill. All necessary equipment and utensils are available.

Preferring open water should go to the local pebble beach. Moreover, it is located just 200 meters from the pension. Vacationers come down to the beach by elevator.

Near the boarding house there is a small cafe serving delicious dishes, among which there is barbecue. Noisy recreation (for example, water parks, discos and animations) is not nearby. Therefore, the "Cold River" is so loved by those who want to relax from the hustle and bustle of the city and enjoy nature, silence and tranquility.

The location of the recreation center

Pension Cold River is located 12 km from the city of Adler (Sochi) and 14 from Gagra (Abkhazia). You can get to the town center in just ten minutes by taxi. There is a shuttle bus from the train station or Adler Airport to the Psou checkpoint. There you need to transfer by taxi or bus, next to the village of Cold River.
