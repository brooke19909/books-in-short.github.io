How to crochet flowers: schemes, description, workshop

A fairly ancient and popular type of needlework is crocheting. With it, you can create very beautiful things. Women, and men, all over the world master this craft not only for crafts. It soothes. But in order to get things really not ordinary, many try to decorate them. And for this reason they want to know how to crochet flowers. At its core, these are small napkins. Therefore, to make them is not difficult. Now you will see this.

Legend

To master this needlework, you need to be able to read schemes and descriptions. Crocheted flowers are no exception. This craft is built on a specific alternation of air loops and posts.

An air loop is a loop that does not touch the previous row of the product in any way. She kind of hangs over him in the air. In diagrams, it is indicated by a dot or oval, and in the descriptions - in. P.

Columns are loops that connect to the previous row. The technique of their execution is as follows: hook with a. The item is inserted into one loop near below and a working thread is stretched through it. So on the instrument 2 loops are formed. If you stretch the working thread immediately through the one that remained on the hook, you get a half-column. If we separately knit them together, then we will just have a column (st. B / n. Or s. B / n.). In the diagrams, it is indicated by a vertical bar.

If there is one or more stripes across it, this means that you need to knit a column with one or more yarn over (st. N.). To do this, before you thread the tool into the previous row, you need to wind the working thread once or several times on it. Then these loops are knitted in pairs, and the column itself is obtained the higher, the more yarn was crocheted.

Small and simple flowers

So, a small flower crocheted, like most others, fits in a circle. The work should begin with the ring, which is obtained from the first loop in the work. Since the notation above is not quite typical, we will analyze it in more detail.

In the first row you need to knit six single crochet, in the second - two of the same columns are made in each of them. In the third row, you need to connect once or twice, then one column without a crochet. This will form a dense core.

Next, go to the petals. This will be the last row in the work. We make three air loops, three tbsp. n and a half-column. Then another half-column and seven double crochets. Repeat the last fragment four more times. The last knit half-column and two tbsp. n at the same point as the first three with a chain of air loops. Close the row with a half-column in the air loop.
