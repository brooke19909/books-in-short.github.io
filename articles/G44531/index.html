Description

Hurghada Grand Hotel impresses visitors with its picturesque grounds, decorated with intricate blue pools and well-groomed green gardens. In addition, tourists will be pleasantly surprised by the clean beach, which has a fairly significant length.

Hurghada Grand Hotel can be called a real oasis, or even a paradise. Advantageous location, high-quality service that meets the highest standards, as well as a vast park area in size make tourists come back here again and again.





Hotel location

One of the main advantages of the Hurghada Grand Hotel is its favorable location. It is located just 5 km from Hurghada International Airport and 10 km from the cultural and business center of the city. The hotel is located off the coast in a popular tourist area. Directly opposite the hotel there is a huge shopping and entertainment center called the Grand Mall, so there will certainly not be a shortage of entertainment for visitors to this resort.

Hotel infrastructure

Thanks to the developed and thought-out infrastructure, rest at the Grand Hotel Hurghada 4 * hotel becomes as comfortable as possible. Tourists have the opportunity to take advantage of:

  • two outdoor pools, as well as five located on the combined territory of a neighboring hotel;
  • reception working round the clock;

Tourists comments

In addition to positive feedback, tourists also leave some comments and complaints about the hotel:

  • monotonous dinners in one restaurant (because of which large lines accumulate);
  • old furniture in the rooms;
  • lack of heating in the pools;
  • lack of Russian-speaking animators in the kids club;
  • a large number of mosquitoes (room processing is carried out for an additional fee);
  • cockroaches and ants were noticed in the corridors of some buildings (due to their pestilence, the unpleasant smell of poison for insects was constantly present);
  • in some rooms the plumbing works intermittently;
  • the entry into the sea is strewn with small stones;
  • sightseeing boats park in the immediate vicinity of the beach, which does not affect the purity of the water in the best way (oil stains remain on the surface);
  • in the evening there are problems with the Internet;
  • noisy air conditioning;
  • from staff it is impossible to achieve quality service without leaving a tip.

Helpful information

Going on vacation to a particular hotel, it should be borne in mind that each of them has certain rules regarding accommodation and recreation. Many hotels instruct guests on arrival, but it is better to familiarize yourself with this information in advance. So, about the hotel Grand Hotel Hurghada (Egypt) you need to know the following points:

  • Check-in is after 14:00 local time. On the day of departure, all guests must leave their rooms and collect at the reception desk before 12:00;
  • if you do not need an extra bed when checking into a room with a child, then his stay will be absolutely free (the rule applies to kids up to 12 years old);
  • placing a child on an extra bed will cost half the cost of living an adult;
  • only one extra bed (child or adult) is allowed in the room;
  • check-in at the hotel with any kind of pet is not allowed;
  • be prepared for the fact that at the time of booking a room, management may block a certain amount of money in your card account until your arrival at the hotel;
  • if you want to use cable internet, you will have to pay ten dollars per hour for this service.
