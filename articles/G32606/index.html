The easiest ways to replenish Tele2 account with a bank card without commission

To remain without communication is a dubious pleasure. You can skip an important call, message or letter in your email inbox. Periodic monitoring and replenishment of the Tele2 account from a bank card without commission or in cash is a need for any subscriber.

One-time replenishment of Tele2 account with a credit card

This opportunity is provided by the mobile operator on its website. This requires a transition to the official resource of the company. Scrolling down the main page, you can see the section "Deposits and transfers".

There are 2 fields for entering data: in the first you need to register a phone number, in the second enter the desired amount. Be careful, the option "Bank card" should be selected in the header of the section. There is a limit on the amount for this method. The minimum payment can be made for 100 rubles. The maximum amount of a single transaction is 15,000 rubles.

By clicking on the "Refill" button, the correctness of the specified data is confirmed.

After that, you go to a new page where you need to enter information about the bank card. A check mark opposite "Save for future use" allows you to bind a card for the automatic use of details when re-paying through the service.

The received SMS message to the telephone connected to the plastic with the code serves to enter the password in the corresponding field of the window that appears.

The funds are credited instantly.

How to pay for Tele2 services from a mobile banking application?

The second method involves the use of an application installed on a smartphone or tablet.

The sequence of the procedure for replenishing the ‚ÄúTele2‚Äù account with a bank card of Sberbank is as follows:

  1. Download the application.
  2. Enter a password that protects access to the current account from third parties.
  3. Search for a section designed to pay for services of mobile operators.
  4. Select an operator, indicate a phone number, enter the amount of payment and select the card from which to be debited.

The balance is replenished in a matter of minutes, provided that there is no preventive work on the side of the bank.

Replenishment of Tele2 account from a bank card through an ATM

In the absence of access to a personal computer or laptop with the Internet and a negative balance of the mobile phone, you can use the services of an ATM.

The method is also popular with people who doubt the security of Internet payments.

To replenish the account, just insert the card into the card reader, enter the PIN code on the keyboard, select the section for payment for services.

Similarly indicate the number "Tele2" and enter the payment amount from the keyboard. Consent to the transaction is confirmed by pressing the "Enter" button on the screen or keyboard of the device.

If the ATM does not have problems with access to the Internet, crediting takes place instantly.

Payment via Internet Banking

This method involves authorization and access to your personal account on the website of the servicing bank. You can go through the procedure by specifying an identifier, as in the case of Sberbank, or an attached phone number and password.

The menu searches for the corresponding item. You can also enter in the search bar the name of the operator "Tele2", and it will be redirected to the appropriate section of payment. As in other cases, the telephone number is indicated, the personal account of which needs to be replenished. Check the minimum and maximum payment limit. Otherwise, the operation will be refused.

Updating the account status of the operator‚Äôs SIM card is very fast.

When using this method, do not forget to regularly update anti-virus programs that track not only threats in the system, but also detect hidden applications that are designed to steal data about details for entering your personal account.

Additional payment method with a bank card

If within walking distance is the salon of the mobile operator Tele2 or the networks of Svyaznoy, Euroset, you can contact the staff to replenish the balance. The staff will specify the phone number, payment amount and offer to pay by card or in cash.

In branded departments and subscriber points, payments are always accepted without commission. When paying through Svyaznoy or Euroset, first specify the conditions. They change periodically. They may charge a fee in any case or when paying only through the operator. In these stores, terminals are also installed through which most often payments are free. Bank cards are usually accepted directly at the cash desk of the store.

Payment through Yandex.Money

The payment system also provides the ability to top up a Tele2 account with a credit card. You can use the plastic of any bank, payment system or virtual wallet in the system. Without an account on Yandex, it will not be possible to pay for services. It is necessary to register or authorize in the service.

Payments of less than 5 thousand are made without commission. If the amount exceeds the specified limit, Yandex.Money will withdraw a 2% commission.

To conduct the transaction, the phone number and the desired amount are standardly indicated. At the second step, they choose where the funds will be debited from: a bank card or wallet.

The whole procedure takes no more than a few minutes. You can use the account on the site or through the mobile application installed on the device.

If you are in another country

Such subscribers of a mobile operator can use all methods of replenishing a Tele2 account from a bank card, except for conducting an operation through an ATM and company stores. When using a SIM card of a Russian company, it is necessary to clarify in advance questions regarding tariffing in international roaming for calls, incoming and outgoing SMS.

And do not forget, the use of auto payment can significantly save the risk of being left without a connection.
