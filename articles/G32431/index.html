The recreation center "Teremok" (Kichigino) - a country vacation in the forest

For a country vacation with friends or family, the Teremok recreation center is perfect. Kichigino is a picturesque village in the Uvelsky district of the Chelyabinsk region. In a relict pine forest, on the banks of the Uvelka river, you can have a great time away from prying eyes, because there are no more rest houses nearby.





Location Features

The ancient pine forest, where the recreation center "Teremok" in Kichigino is located, the photo of which is presented below, has earned the title of natural monument. Resting here, guests receive a portion of healing air. The calm atmosphere of the forest will help relieve fatigue and relax, and also activates the saturation of body cells with oxygen. Coniferous air helps suppress the activity of pathogens and replenish vitamin C reserves, increasing immunity.





The recreation center "Teremok" (Kichigino) will provide an opportunity to satisfy aesthetic tastes, because the panorama that opens up to the view from the steep bank of the Uvelka river is amazing. From a hill you can see how the river, meandering, rushes to the horizon, and the Kabanka river flows nearby, and together they head for the large South Ural reservoir.

Boat trips on a catamaran, boat or boat, which will provide recreation center "Teremok" (Kichigino), will help you approach nature.

Accommodation Features

The recreation center accepts guests all year round, so accommodation is provided here in the summer and in the winter. In winter, guests have at their disposal comfortable double, triple and quadruple rooms in a three-story building, and friendly companies can stay in cottages that can accommodate from ten to thirty people.





In summer, tourists living in wooden houses in the forest have the opportunity to feel like real foresters. On the territory of the complex there are three-, four- and six-seater wooden houses with a barbecue area and amenities on the street, thanks to which the recreation center "Teremok" in Kichigino got its name. Reviews about the rest in the forest are always good, thanks to the positive emotions from the surrounding nature and quality service.

  1. Building No. 1 and No. 2 are three-story capital buildings, in which there are 22 and 25 well-appointed rooms with a TV, a refrigerator, a dining corner and the necessary furniture, as well as a wardrobe or an entrance hall. On the ground floors there are cafes and places for relaxation.
  2. Small cottages - three two-story buildings for 10-12 people, which are equipped with four bedrooms (each), kitchens, toilets, showers, televisions, music centers, refrigerators, microwave ovens and electric stoves.
  3. Large cottage - a two-story building that can accommodate 20-30 people. The house is equipped with five bedrooms and a banquet room, in which a bar counter separates the kitchenette from the common area. Having a TV and a music center will help pass the time. Also, the cottage has three bathrooms and a shower.
  4. Wooden houses - panel buildings for three, four, six or eight places. They are equipped with the necessary furniture, and near the houses there are areas for relaxation and a picnic with barbecue, table and benches. Bathroom and shower - summer type, located on the street.
  5. Pergolas and verandas - suggest a day's stay for organizing celebrations or simple gatherings.

There is no charge for children under seven years old, and from 7 to 12 - only half the cost is paid.





The recreation center "Teremok" Kichigino is designed for outdoor recreation in the countryside and meals are not included in the rental price of residential premises. But guests can choose a comprehensive three meals a day in the dining room or order dishes from the daily menu.

For holidays and evenings, you can rent a banquet hall with a festively set table. The halls are designed to accommodate 35, 55 or 100 people. The service is always on top and is not satisfactory. Here you can celebrate any event in a pleasant atmosphere without undue pomp.

What is included in the price of the permit?

Arriving at the recreation center "Teremok", guests can not worry about the safety of their cars and use the free guarded parking lot with video surveillance for free. Also, at any time, guests can stay in the barbecue area and organize a barbecue or barbecue in nature.

Free access to children's and sports fields, beach loungers and a volleyball court on the beach, as well as ski and ice rink in the winter. This allows you to diversify the leisure of adults and to take children, which will make a suburban forest vacation not only useful, but also interesting.





At extra charge

In addition to food, the recreation center provides other paid services, such as:

  • visit to the bath;
  • rental of sports equipment: catamarans, boats, bicycles, fishing tackle, inflatable sledges (tubing).

Arriving at "Teremok" on vacation, everyone will be able to choose a lesson for their liking and financial opportunities.

sights

A kilometer from the recreation center, on the Tushkan Mountain, archaeologists recently worked who discovered numerous mounds serving as evidence that many millennia ago the Sarmatians lived on the South Ural lands. Therefore, the Chelyabinsk region and, in particular, the Uvelsky district can rightly be called the birthplace of this people, who became famous for their conquests. And some historians suggest that many knights of the round table, including the legendary King Arthur, came from a Sarmatian tribe.

An excursion to the excavation site is free of charge, accompanied by a guide. In addition to an interesting historical reference, guests who climbed the mounds have a magnificent view of the vast steppes and the valley river Uvelka. This unforgettable sight will leave a lasting impression.

How to get to the "Teremok"?

The best place for suburban outdoor recreation for urban residents will be "Teremok" - recreation center Kichigino. How to get there, you can see on the map. It is located in the Chelyabinsk region near the village of Kichigino.

Moving south from the city of Chelyabinsk along the Troitsky tract, after 75 km, guests get into the Uvelsky district. The recreation center "Teremok" is located in Kichigino. How to get there, tell any resident of this village. You need to drive along Mira Street to the end of the village, and then turn onto the forest road. And after one and a half kilometers the territory of the recreation center begins, the staff of which meets guests with village hospitality, which contributes to a good mood and the anticipation of a good vacation.
