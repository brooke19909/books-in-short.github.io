Location of the complex

Chusovaya camp site crossed the 80-year milestone back in 2014, but, despite its advanced age and the title of the oldest vacation spot in the Middle Urals, it has a modern material base, which is improved every year. The greatness of the Ural nature attracts vacationers who do not want to give up comfort. The complex is located on the banks of the river of the same name, on the old mountain of Dog Ribs, which you should climb by breaking 100 steps.





The base is based on the territory of the former tannery and, since 1934, has been hosting fans of outdoor activities, and during the Patriotic War there was a military hospital, the memories of which are in the museum. All-Union fame was a complex among lovers of rafting on mountain rivers. The previous name was given in honor of the nearby Kaurovskaya railway station. You can get to the camp site by car, moving along the Moscow highway from Yekaterinburg in the direction of Shalya, bypassing Revda (93 km) or Pervouralsk (83 km). Fans of railway transport will travel by train to the station Kaurovka. The road from Yekaterinburg will take only an hour and a half.

Well-groomed territory attracts vacationers, because in winter and summer there is something to do.

Summer rest

Given that the Chusovaya camp site in the Sverdlovsk Region is one of the oldest holiday destinations in the region, it also provides services to tourists all year round. In summer, everyone can enjoy the warm rays of the sun on their own beach, on the banks of a clear river.





Water rides, boat rides, rafts, catamarans, rafting on routes of different durations and difficulties diversify your stay. Biking or horse riding will give an unforgettable experience.
