How to control your TV from your phone: recommendations

In those days, when mobile gadgets were built on the old operating systems "Bada", "Symbian" and similar ones, many users, ask them the question: "Is it possible to control the TV from the phone?", Would turn a finger at the temple.

But with the advent of platforms such as Android, the latest iOS and Windows 10 Mobile, this procedure no longer seems fantastic. In terms of functionality, modern smartphones and tablets breathe powerful desktop devices into the back.

TV manufacturers also take this fact into account and contribute in every way to the development of this area in both hardware and software. general information

Almost every well-known TV manufacturer is trying to bring something new and original to their product. Yes, competing models have a lot in common, but there are a lot of branded features that others do not have. It is thanks to the latter that managing a smart TV from a phone is not so simple, because each brand has its own approach to this procedure, and universal solutions do not always work.

Nevertheless, the principle of operation of such a tandem is the same for everyone. There is a TV device, a smartphone, special software and wireless communications. This set allows you to control the TV through the phone.

For communication, use one of three modules:

  • Wi-Fi.
  • Bluetooth.
  • IR port

Manufacturers of mobile gadgets have almost completely abandoned the latest technology, because the first two completely level its effectiveness. But in some devices it is still in use and with its help you can also successfully control the TV through the phone. In this case, the effectiveness and functionality of the procedure is markedly reduced.

Software

As for the software, it can be divided into two main categories - these are universal and proprietary applications. Using your original software to control your TV from your phone is much easier. In addition, all available functionalities are fully disclosed and without any restrictions.

Universal applications also allow you to control the TV from your phone, but they do not always behave appropriately, if at all. Here we are talking mostly about TV devices from little-known brands or generally no-name factories. While almost every well-known manufacturer has its own software.

Proprietary software

If you have a branded device, then it is best to control the TV from your phone using special software, and not look for universal software. As for the operating system of the mobile gadget, all the programs described below are perfectly adapted for the Android and iOS platforms, and in some cases Windows Phone.

Manufacturers with their own software:

  • LG.
  • Samsung.
  • "Panasonic."
  • Philips.
  • Sony.

Consider the proprietary software of each brand in more detail.

LG

In order to control your LG TV from your phone, you need to go to the official website of the brand and in the software section download the branded LG TV Remote application. After installing the program, you will need to start scanning devices, while turning on your TV.

After searching, the program will list the supported devices, and if your TV is among them, it means the channel will work and you can fully control the TV from your Android, iOS or Windows Phone.

After synchronization, two windows should appear on your mobile gadget. At the bottom there will be an interface for controlling volume and channels, built by analogy with a keypad. In addition, with its help you can go to the main screen, take a screenshot, enable 3D effects and call up the application control panel.

At the top of the screen is a working area that provides access to all installed programs. The touch screen of the smartphone during operation mirrors your movements, where the pointer on the TV screen moves after your finger. With it, you can fully control the TV from the phone: play content, configure functionality with tools, and even play. In a word, we get not just a classic remote control, but a real complex for interacting with TV.

Samsung

In order to control the Samsung TV from your phone, you must also go to the official resource of the brand and download the Samsung TV & Remote program in the software section. The application interface, judging by the user reviews, is quite friendly, there are no problems with the toolkit.

The screen on the mobile gadget is divided into two parts. On one are the keys for selecting channels, the touch rocker for adjusting the volume level and the buttons for turning on the TV, as well as the transition to the navigation menu.

The second part of the screen is organized in such a way as to simplify the management of multimedia functions of the TV. Here we have a semblance of a joystick, by pressing the keys of which access to selected channels, content and other (customizable) elements is provided.

Users respond well to the application. It allows you to fully control the Samsung TV from the phone, but he has one fly in the ointment. This is an advertisement. In principle, it cannot be called aggressive, but this has not been noted for competing analogues.

So some particularly ardent opponents of advertising prefer third-party universal applications. You can control the TV through an Android phone or iOS. There is a version of the application for Windows Phone as well, but judging by the reviews, it is full of bugs and not very well adapted for this platform.

Philips

To work with Philips equipment using mobile gadgets, you will need a special Philips MeRemote program. You can find it on the official website of the manufacturer, in the software section.

At the first start, the application will offer to scan devices, and if any were found, it will present a list for synchronization. The program interface is simple and convenient. All the main tools are correctly divided into sections, so there is no need to wander around the menu.

On the main screen you can see a list of categories, by clicking on which the corresponding working area opens. Here you can interact with the gadget files, control the sound level, switch channels, open applications, etc.

One of the main advantages of the software from Philips is its speed and minimum system requirements for filling the mobile device. For the application to work correctly, even an ancient gadget with 512 MB of RAM supporting the Android platform is enough. The speed of the menus and tools is amazing. The delay in playing content after a click is less than a second of time. The SimplyShare brand technology used to achieve this performance is considered the best among other analogues.

Sony

To control Sony equipment using a smartphone or tablet, you need the TV Side View program. You can find it on the official resource of the brand in the "Files for download" section. Immediately after launch, the application scans nearby devices (the TV must be turned on).

After synchronization, access to the working areas of the program is opened. The interface design is made in dark and calm colors and divided into sections. Each category has its own working area with specific tools for it.

One table allows you to control navigation and switch channels, the other - controls the volume level, the third - is responsible for working with applications, the fourth - with files, etc. Dealing with the interface is not difficult. The application received competent Russian-language localization and an intelligently compiled help section.

It is also worth noting the well-developed voice control of the main tools. With it, you can not only switch channels and adjust sound, but also adjust brightness with contrast, open regular applications and even type text into a notebook.

The program is not particularly demanding on the system resources of a mobile gadget, but it needs at least 1 GB of RAM for correct operation. Otherwise, hangs and other lags may be observed. Advertising banners and pop-ups do not even smell here. But Sony never allowed itself to do this in its proprietary programs.
