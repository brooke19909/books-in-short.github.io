Golden Lotus Hotel Nha Trang 2 *: hotel reviews

Golden Lotus Hotel Nha Trang 2 * is a third-line hotel of the famous Vietnamese resort Nha Trang. This city is geographically related to the province of Han Hoa. A trans-Vietnamese highway passes through it. From the resort to the capital of the country - about a thousand kilometers.

The hotel itself is a tall and narrow ten-story building with a bright red-yellow facade. It takes only 5 minutes to go to the nearby beach (in Vietnam all beaches are communally owned). Some reasonable distance from the sea has its advantages: the rooms in the hotel complex are less noisy and more calm. The hotel complex enjoys a good reputation among tourists and travel agencies as a solid economy-class hotel.

Recommendations for tourists

However, for those who arrived at the hotel, some general recommendations taken by us from the reviews of Golden Lotus Hotel Nha Trang (Vietnam) guests are not out of place:

- Refrain from traveling during the rainy season (December to February).

- Have a sunblock with a protection factor of at least 30.

- When walking the streets, do not carry bags, cases, etc. It is better to have a backpack. Cunning Vietnamese thieves on the run, or even on a motorcycle, snatch everything that can be taken from their hands.

- For tourists who want to enjoy the nuances of Vietnamese cuisine, it is advisable to bring with them the usual medications that help with indigestion (activated charcoal, smecta, etc.).

In a hotel





In total, the hotel complex has exactly fifty rooms. They are divided into Standard, Superior, Deluxe, Twin, Family.

Rooms at Golden Lotus Hotel Nha Trang are furnished and renovated in a classic economy-style minimalist style. Before checking in, guests should make a deposit. There is a necessary set of furniture: beds, wardrobe, bedside tables, two chairs, a coffee table. The room provides appliances: air conditioning, hairdryer. A safety deposit box and a minibar are also provided for a fee.

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel.

Hotel infrastructure

By purchasing a tour and moving into the room, the guest can use the hotel infrastructure. Dining is provided by the Golden Lotus Hotel Nha Trang's restaurant, and hotel guests have the opportunity to choose between guest house, half board and breakfast. In addition to the restaurant, guests can have a meal in the hotel bar. At the boarding house, vacationers are provided with a number of related and additional services: use of the left-luggage office, dry cleaning, laundry, ironing, car and bicycle rental, tour desk.

Tourists often take scooters for rent for a few days to explore the surroundings of the resort.

Resort infrastructure

It is not for nothing that Golden Lotus Hotel Nha Trang guests prefer to buy a tour with breakfast only. The abundance of nearby inexpensive restaurants turns Nha Trang into a real gastronomic paradise.

Moreover, the closer to the sea, the more expensive the food. Therefore, we strongly recommend eating on the third or fourth line. So cheaper with a similar quality of food. On average, a Japanese restaurant check is $ 15, an Italian restaurant - $ 18, a dinner of freshly prepared seafood - $ 24.

Almost all the guests find their food options, and, we assure you, they rarely coincide. Nevertheless, starting from the reviews of guests, we can recommend, without embarking on long trips, to buy semi-finished exotic dishes, bring them to a hotel restaurant and ask them to cook individually. Thus, at a price of $ 10, guests can freely pickled pickled seafood of their choice: shrimp, squid, oysters, mussels, shells, crabs, all kinds of fish.

Cheap, tasty and satisfying cooked in the restaurant "Sailor", the owners of which are former Russians.

Shopping in Nha Trang

The shopping formula in Nha Trang comes down to the market dollar / dong rate, determined on the basis of this proportion: $ 100 is approximately equivalent to 2.1 million dong. The prices of goods at this resort are relatively low.

The guests of Golden Lotus Hotel Nha Trang 2 * are advised to buy clothes here: silk, leather, bamboo, cotton. The price here is three times cheaper than the one we are used to. A wonderful thing can be bought for $ 40-50. Leather products are generally cheap: a wallet - $ 10, a bag - $ 15!

Cheap souvenirs are sold at Cho Dam market. Moreover, one should bargain there, knocking down the price at least three times. Very beautiful wood products, all kinds of caskets. You can buy crocodile leather products relatively cheaply. (Fortunately, these animals are not uncommon here, and are even widely used as food).

Those who wish to make a more expensive purchase are advised to purchase pearl jewelry from jewelers.

It also makes sense to buy coffee and tea. They are quality and cheap in Vietnam. Taxis in Vietnam are inexpensive. Payment is made by meter. On average, the price per trip ranges from 1 to 5 US dollars.

Even cheaper you can travel around the city by bus 4 (ticket price - 7 thousand dongs).

Conclusion
