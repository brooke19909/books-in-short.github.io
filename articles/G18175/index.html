Upper, Small and Big Golovinsky Pond: description, recreation and fishing

Golovinsky ponds have a huge influx of people. There are many who want to relax near the house. Especially here, fishermen often come who do not have the opportunity to travel outside their community.

Each Golovinsky pond is in a fairly good environmental situation, except perhaps in the Upper. Some vacationers consider these reservoirs polluted, in fact, the water in them is transparent. Title

The name "Golovinsky Pond" comes from the village of Golovino, which was owned by the noble family of the same name. Ponds are located in the north-west of Moscow, in the same area, near the local cemetery. Golovinsky ponds are a system consisting of three water areas: the Big, the Upper and the Small. They were built in the XVIII century on the stream of the same name. The latter originates in the Khimki reservoir, passes through the Verkhny, Maly and Bolshoi ponds, then the channel flows into the tributary of Likhoborka. The reservoirs are quite full-flowing: a strong current is noticeable in the ponds.

Take a look at the story

Each Golovinsky pond has a very interesting history of formation, given that they are all combined into one system. Since the 16th century, the village often changed owners, and the most typical landowner estate appeared by the middle of the 19th century. She immediately became famous for her rare peach trees with delicious fruits. The last owner of the estate was a religious man and bequeathed his estate to the monastery. At the end of the century, a school and a hospital were opened with him, and during the war sisters nursed the wounded. In the early 1930s. religious institution closed. That was the story of the formation of the village of the same name, in which there are ponds.

Upper Golovinsky Pond

The Upper Pond is the smallest of the ponds. Its length is about 240 m, in width it stretches for 165 m, and its area is 3.4 hectares.

The pond is irregularly oval in shape, with banks overgrown with sharp sedge. The water is strewn with duckweed and mud, the pedestrian path is natural - in a word, a simple lake unsuitable for swimming, and even among the fishermen there are few lovers who try to catch something in the thickets. Here, most often there are tourists who like a calm and peaceful vacation on the shore of a reservoir.





Small pond

Small pond has irregular banks. Its length is 350 m, its width is about 125 m, its area is 3.8 ha. Connected to the Upper very picturesque stone channel. A lot of people are fishing on the shores of this pond - the reservoir is simply teeming with fish, fortunately, it is constantly being cleaned up. No less people come to him to sunbathe, swim, cook kebabs. Big Golovinsky Pond

The large pond differs from the first two in that the northern shore of the reservoir is reinforced with reinforced concrete slabs with a spillway. He is the largest. Its length is 500 m, its width is on average 270 m, its area is almost half the size of two other ponds, amounting to 7.5 hectares.

If you pass along the bridge lying over the channel of the Bolshoi and Maly ponds, you can admire the edge of the forest with old trees, many of which have reached the age of three hundred. A memorial plaque "Oaks" educates all interested. The trees were seen by the previous owners of Mikhalkovo - a village near Golovin. For those who wish to ride on a picturesque lake in water transport, the boat station in the southeast has hospitably opened its doors from 10:00 to 23:00. An hour of rental will cost five hundred rubles. A little further from the station is the official free beach. It has a comfortable sandy beach and a smooth approach. Nearby is a picnic area and an old estate of the XVIII century - "Mikhailovo".

This Golovinsky pond, reviews of which are only positive, is the most popular among other reservoirs. Flora is very rich. White willow, birch, maple, oak, larch grow here. The fauna of water bodies is represented by a variety of fish: pike, perch, crucian carp. Mallard nests along the shores.

You can get to the ponds by buses No. 123, No. 90. You need to get to the stop "Likhobory". You can also get to the Vodny Stadion metro station, which can be reached on foot in 15 minutes.





Pond fishing

Fishing is considered a kind of art. Every professional will agree that not everyone can do this.





For spinners, the Golovinsky Ponds, where fishing is constantly successful, are a place where you can show your skills. A festival takes place here every year. Competitions are held in one round, 3 hours are allotted for the whole process. As a catch, the jury accepts any kind of fish that is allowed to be caught. Most often come across perches, tench, crucian carp, scammers.

Few of the fishermen regret the time spent at the ponds. It is worth noting that the catch here is really amazing. No need to deny yourself the pleasure of visiting here.
