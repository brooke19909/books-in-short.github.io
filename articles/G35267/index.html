Hotels Turkey (Marmaris) 5 stars, all-inclusive: reviews, rating, photo

The city of Marmaris is a popular resort in Turkey, located in the European part of the country, on the border of the Mediterranean and Aegean Seas. He was assigned the status of a district center. It annually receives several million tourists from around the world.

The nearest airport is in Dalaman, at a distance of 95 kilometers from Marmaris, the airport in Antalya is at a distance of 320 kilometers.

Sights of Marmaris :

  1. The Old Castle is a museum called Fort Marmaris.
  2. Sculptures and monuments.
  3. Water parks.
  4. Street bars - a walking area, includes many different bars and restaurants for every taste.
  5. The promenade is the longest embankment in the whole country. On the promenade are: hotels, restaurants, cafes; on the other hand, there is a beach with a narrow coastline. The promenade is planted with eucalyptus trees and palm trees.

Features of rest





The sea in Marmaris is closed and surrounded by mountains, so the surface is smooth, the waves are insignificant and are infrequent. For lovers of shopping, there are many retail areas and a bazaar where it is possible to purchase goods with bargaining. Marmaris is considered a popular party resort, as there are many nightclubs and entertainment. To accommodate with comfort here is not difficult.
