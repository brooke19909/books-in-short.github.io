Grandmother's Park of Culture and Leisure in Moscow - we rest with the whole family! How to get to the Babushkinskiy park?

Finding a good place for a budget holiday with the whole family in the capital is not always easy. Not everyone can organize trips to nature; modern entertainment centers quickly get bored. I want something simple and comfortable, as in our childhood. Grandma's park offers a cheap and pleasant place to relax - a special place in modern Moscow. In a small area there are various activities for children and adults, but there are many quiet areas for walking. This is a favorite place for many residents of the region and Muscovites living for several blocks.

Historical reference



In the last years of the nineteenth century, a public park was allocated in the village of Losinoostrovsky. In the early years of its existence, this oasis of nature did not know much care, but even then the locals went for walks here. Seven years after the opening of the park, it was decided to improve it. The first tracks, sports and playgrounds, an open stage and much more. In 1955, the village of Losinoostrovsky was the city of Babushkin. The reconstruction was carried out everywhere - the streets were renamed, new houses and public buildings were built. In the spring, on the holiday of peace and labor, the Grandma‚Äôs Park of Culture and Rest re-opened after reconstruction. In less than ten years, the city of Babushkin will become part of Moscow and become one of its areas. The status of the park will also change, but the new name will remain with it.

Period of decline

Very often you can hear negative reviews about Babushkinsky Park. Their authors are most often people who have not been there for a long time. After the collapse of the USSR, like many other cultural objects of that time, the natural corner was really launched. Financing problems affected: old benches and rides began to break, but no new funds were allocated, bushes and trees were not trimmed, there were no interesting events. A whole generation of residents of the district, who were perplexedly looking at an abandoned square not far from their houses, had successfully grown up. The park really has a relatively small area. However, despite all these factors, in the troubled nineties of the previous century, his story did not end.

Green Moscow: Babushkinskiy park - revival



In 2005, a major reconstruction of the entire park area was carried out. The events related to the improvement took place not by accident in that year, because very soon the park should have turned 50 years old. The changes affected the appearance of the whole natural territory: new paths and benches were arranged, the lighting system was reconstructed, and landscape design was carried out. The infrastructure of the park was also noticeably updated. Today it is a modern open-air complex, which will be interesting to visit for adults and children of any age.
