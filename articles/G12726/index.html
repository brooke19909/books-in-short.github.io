MPM Kalina Garden 4 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria): hotel reviews

Sunny Beach in Bulgaria is popular among lovers of the Black Sea resorts. If a person wants to enjoy peace and quiet, then the southern or northern part of the coast will suit him, but if you want to spend time actively, then you should choose a hotel in the central part. MPM Kalina Garden 4 belongs to this category.





General view

This hotel complex is located in close proximity to the beach - only two hundred meters separates guests from the Black Sea. And this is not the only plus in the location of the hotel. Near it you can find anything you want - numerous shops, a supermarket and a public transport stop. Moreover, a water park is located very close by - just 800 meters from the hotel. And to the city of Nessebar, which is a real national treasure, you need to drive 10 minutes by car or minibus. Be sure to visit this place while staying at the resort. After all, this is a real miracle - a city that went under the water several centuries ago. By the way, the airport is not very far - a little more than 20 kilometers. So tourists can easily get to their temporary residence.

Outdoor activities

Guests of MPM Hotel Kalina Garden 4 can enjoy various on-site activities. There is a spacious swimming pool, not far from it there is a terrace where you can sunbathe and a cozy garden. Guests can also enjoy a delicious barbecue. It should be noted that there is a children's section in the pool, so people who come to rest with their child will have something to occupy the baby with. Every evening, the animation team conducts a variety of show programs and theme parties. And for young guests - special children's performances.





Activities for the soul

Guests staying at the MPM Kalina Garden 4 can also play billiards or go to the tennis court. There is even a mini golf course. For lovers of heat, an indoor swimming pool is open all year. The hotel even has a massage parlor. Its services will definitely appeal to people who like to relax with their whole body. For fans of uniform physical activity, a section of water aerobics was created. And for the youngest guests there is a playground, a children's room and a mini club. If you want something else, you can pay attention to local attractions. In these places there are many interesting architectural monuments, picturesque gardens and parks, as well as exhibitions and museums. Many tourists, by the way, come to MPM Hotel Kalina Garden 4 not to swim in the sea and sunbathe, but to thoroughly explore the local attractions and the rich cultural heritage of Bulgaria.





Facilities and service

All guests here are treated with great respect, and the staff is always ready to help in resolving certain issues. Workers are fluent in Russian and English, so there will be no problems with communication. Private parking is available on site, and a guest can be provided with a separate place, only you need to agree on this in advance (cost is 10 euros per day). It also offers to rent a car, if necessary. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the entire complex. A host of other services are also offered, including dry cleaning, laundry, currency exchange, a 24-hour front desk, and even a business center. It should be noted that the fact that there are even shops and souvenir shops. In general, everything that guests of the MPM Kalina Garden 4 hotel may need.





Bar and restaurant services

Many potential guests pre-study what the hotel can offer them in terms of food, since not all hotels offer breakfast and lunch. MPM Kalina Garden 4 (Bulgaria) mainly operates the buffet system in the main restaurant. There is also a snack bar where guests can order ice cream, pastries, a variety of snacks or fresh pizza from 10:00 to 18:00. They also offer evening treats (from 22:00 to 23:00). Alcoholic drinks can be ordered from 10 am to 23 pm. The lobby offers non-alcoholic cocktails and freshly squeezed juices. There is also a bar next to the pool. And besides, there are various cafes nearby, only few of the guests staying at Kalina Garden MPM need them.

Comments and reviews of tourists

Many people choose a country such as Bulgaria to spend their holidays. Hotel Kalina Garden is also often the choice of tourists. And I must say, he makes excuses. Guests say the hotel lives up to all expectations. The rooms are very comfortable, clean and spacious, the food is above all praise, everything is always tasty, fresh and original. In addition, very attentive waiters - as soon as the refreshments at the buffet were over, they immediately added more. Many argue that even taking breakfast alone can be completely satiated. Fans of entertainment note the attention of an unobtrusive, but interesting animation. And, of course, everyone liked the friendly and welcoming staff. The hotel receives positive reviews from guests of completely different age categories, and this can rightfully be considered one of the best recommendations.
