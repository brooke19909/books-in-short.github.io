Majestic Beach Comforts India (Varca): description, photo

There is an incredible place in Goa. Its name is Varka. Surprisingly, it is about this resort that you can read the largest number of positive reviews. This place has gained such popularity thanks to really high-quality service, modern hotels, a clean beach strip and a calm and quiet atmosphere.





Some will be bored here, because noisy clubs and party places can not be found, in return you can get rest for the soul and just enjoy the silence. The resort is worth visiting for those who are looking for a beautiful corner of unspoiled nature.

The beaches (cooking) fascinate with their state of peace. Therefore, most tourists do not find anything better than soaking up the sun.

Rest and leisure



No matter how beautiful the beach is, you still want to diversify your leisure time. The tourist will be offered a few differences, but they are also not too active. The first fun is watching dolphins. A person is put in a boat and taken to places where dolphins can be seen close by, they are even allowed to stroke them. In addition, you can observe not only the inhabitants of the seabed. In these places beautiful sunsets are unforgettably, and meeting him in a boat is pure romance.

Active entertainment

All large hotel complexes offer a range of services, the list of which includes extreme entertainment. Almost all of them are associated with water. You can try sailing, diving, boating in various boats and even go fishing.

To explore not only the sea beauty, tourists are offered specially organized walks on cycling. For a short period of time you can see all the surrounding attractions. A professional guide will help in all this.

There is a wonderful park of urban significance in Varca. There is a church on its territory, this is exactly the place that you need to see in the first place. But you should not stop for a long time at this park, in Goa there are many interesting objects.

To explore all the beauty and have a good rest, you need to stop somewhere. A good option would be Majestic Beach Comforts.

Hotel Description





This is a resort complex that has everything you need and more. The rooms have private bathrooms, relaxation areas, as well as the necessary equipment. The reception is open around the clock, so you can check in at any time suitable for the tourist. There is also the opportunity to take a bike for sightseeing. The hotel is conveniently located, from it you can easily get to all interesting places, and the airport itself is 20 kilometers away.

Service

  • daily press delivery directly to your room;
  • availability of a safe in the apartment;
  • special rooms for those who do not smoke;
  • availability of necessary equipment in the room (air conditioning, minibar, TV, hairdryer);
  • currency exchanger;
  • special luggage storage room;
  • a number of services to bring clothes in proper form (dry cleaning, laundress);
  • the hotel has several entertainment venues;
  • gym;
  • the hotel has its own access to the beach and sun loungers;
  • parking area is absolutely free;
  • On site there is an outdoor pool and relaxation areas.

Another great service a hotel can provide is shuttle service. This is very convenient, especially when a person arrives in a foreign country. To request this service, please contact the hotel prior to arrival.

In order to settle, you need to consider several rules. Firstly, you must have a passport with you. Secondly, the hotel requires a deposit. It is taken for insurance. For example, if a person breaks something or for other unforeseen situations. If such cases do not arise, then he will be returned upon eviction.
