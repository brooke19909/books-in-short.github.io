Rixos Premium Gocek 5 * (Turkey, Fethiye): description of rooms, service, reviews

The Turkish resort town of Fethiye is one of the most popular destinations for Russian tourists. At the same time, in the process of choosing a hotel, someone prefers complexes located near the main infrastructure (bars, restaurants, full-time clubs), and someone chooses secluded and remote corners on the coast, such as the small village of Gezhek, in which there is a five-star The luxury hotel Rixos Premium Gocek. The complex is surrounded by evergreen pine forests and mountains. If you give the place a characterization in a nutshell, then it is luxury and comfort.

Location of the resort





The hotel is located in the village of Gecek - a popular resort location. Of course, its main advantage is its geographical location. The village is located in a picturesque bay on the very shore of the sea, the surrounding slopes of the Tauride Mountains protect it from the wind. All this as a whole creates favorable conditions for yachting.

The resort complex Rixos Premium Gocek, which had the name Prefix Suites & Villas until the first of February 2017, is located on the first coastline, 700 meters from the center of the village, twenty kilometers from Dalaman Airport, 28 km from the center of Fethiye. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel is the shopping street Gezek - a colorful and authentic place where you can more than feel the atmosphere of the eastern bazaar. 20 km from the complex there is a water park with a wide selection of attractions and a bird sanctuary, as well as Kalis Beach, which is considered one of the most environmentally friendly in Turkey and picturesque (the panorama of the Toros Mountains opens over its entire length).

General description of the hotel

The hotel was built in 2014. Only adults and children over thirteen years old have the opportunity to relax in the complex, this is his policy. Pets are not allowed. The hotel offers its guests comfortable apartments of four categories, their total number is 192 rooms. Smoking is not allowed in the hotel rooms. Rooms are cleaned daily. Laundry, dry cleaning and ironing services are paid extra.

Restaurants and bars on site

There are enough places where you can enjoy food and drinks, including local production, on the hotel territory: five restaurants and three bars. The Rixos Premium Gocek Power System is an ultra all-inclusive program loved by many tourists. Those who do not want to be attached to time and place have the opportunity to choose only breakfasts.

Turquoise is its main establishment and serves a rich breakfast buffet for breakfast, lunch and dinner, as well as brunch on Sundays throughout the day.

Named after an ancient city in the Aegean, Daidala Restaurant offers its guests to try traditional Turkish cuisine and fresh seafood, enjoying magnificent views of the coast and the marina. It is open for guests from 19:00 to 22:00, admission is fifty Turkish liras, reservation is required.

People's is the only 24-hour restaurant at the Rixos Premium Gocek with a very versatile menu featuring a variety of world cuisines. It is located on the terrace. An ideal place to relax when twilight thickens over the sea and evening coolness descends.





Restaurant Azur is a secluded place on the coast where guests can have a hearty meal between salads, sandwiches, pizza, hamburgers, between lunch and dinner.

You can try various drinks in three bars of the hotel: Rixos Lounge, Nox Bar (overlooking the marina and an impressive assortment of cocktails), Nargile (it is located in the garden and surrounded by pine trees).

Rixos Premium Gocek Beach

The resort complex is located on the first coastline and has its own sandy beach called Secret Beach. All guests can reach the sea in just a few minutes. The entrance to the water is sand and gravel. The opening date of the bathing season depends on the weather, but, as a rule, this is the first of May.

Towels, sun loungers, umbrellas and mattresses are provided free of charge on the beach. All services are available from ten in the morning until six in the evening. From Secret Beach to the Village Port can be reached in 7 minutes by free shuttle (boat).

Hotel Facilities
