Booties for newborns crochet patterns for beginners are mini shoes, consisting of one part:

  1. First, a sole is formed. Dial a chain of 6 air loops. In addition, 2 more air loops are knitted to raise the pattern. Knit 5 crochet stitches. Universal booties sandals

The scheme of booties for beginners with a hook can be not only an option for the cold season, but also a summer product. Knit booties sandals algorithm:

  1. Form the sole according to the standard pattern. It is advisable to use thin knitted yarn. The upper part is easy to make from cotton yarn. When this element is completed, you need to fasten the thread and cut it.
