United Kingdom, Brighton: hotels. Hotel rating of Brighton

The picturesque city located in England on the south coast in East Sussex is Brighton. London is 80 kilometers from here, therefore, you can make a trip here at any time. The city is considered the most visited and popular sea resort of the country. In summer, tourists come here from all over the world. Hotels in London and Brighton are characterized by comfort and incomparable English charm.



Here you can enjoy the beautiful beaches, as well as explore the many historical sights. Luxury hotels are incredibly popular among wealthy ladies and gentlemen, accustomed to a vacation that is ideal in everything. Economy rooms allow you to accommodate, as well as taste all the delights of this resort for travelers with a small budget.

Brighton (Brighton) - a city whose beaches in the summer take a huge number of vacationers. The coast is buried in the sounds of music, flowers and greenery. Off the coast splashes the clear water of the strait. Hotels located in the center - this is a real European flavor. Not far from them are fashion boutiques, restaurants, shops for everyone's taste. In the evenings it is good to visit night clubs, pubs, bars. It is unlikely that it will be possible to book a hotel on the eve of departure during the season. This must be done in advance: the closer the time to vacation, the more difficult it is to find a cheap room.

8. Amherst Brighton

Considering hotels in London and Brighton, it is worth highlighting Amherst Brighton, which is located almost on the coast and only five minutes walk from the active center of Brighton. Guests can count on rooms with spacious bathrooms, a relaxed, welcoming atmosphere and excellent service. Whatever the purpose of your trip here, this hotel will delight you with its location. Guests can enjoy a delicious continental breakfast in the mornings , and a full English breakfast is available at an additional cost.



The hotel rooms are individually designed. Each is non-smoking, and each has its own bathroom. All rooms are well equipped. They have a variety of amenities, including a direct-dial telephone, LCD or widescreen TV, CD / DVD player, wired and wireless Internet, and plenty of snacks.

7. The Claremont

Elegant, stylish and comfortable rooms at The Claremont Hotel (Brighton, England) are complemented by discreet, unobtrusive and attentive service. At this place you will get a pleasant impression of your stay in this city.

The national travel agency Visit Britain gave the hotel 5 stars (the highest award) for hospitality. The complex also has a license for weddings and registration of civil marriages. Guests are invited to book a room with a private bathroom. All are located in the Victorian villa, which is surrounded by a wall and a garden. It is worth noting that a surprising combination of individual style with classic design elements was used in the interior of the rooms.

6. Brighton House

The old, although recently renovated, Brighton Hotel in Lonely Planet is referred to as a "luxury Regency hotel with amazing rooms." Its owners, Lucho and Kristin, are glad to all guests and are ready to give them a warm welcome. If you trust the British Automobile Association, "convenience and hospitality" are put in first place here. This place has clean, nice, comfortable rooms with their own bathrooms.



Hotel Brighton is located almost in the city center. Start your day with a rich breakfast buffet. It is prepared exclusively from organic ingredients. Then it's time to go to the beach. Preston Street, Metropol and Lanes are also steps away.

5. Drakes Hotel (Brighton, England)

Drakes Hotel offers you to book accommodation right on the promenade, with stunning views of the pier. It is worth noting that each of its unique rooms has air conditioning, a cocktail bar and satellite TV. The rooms also feature Vi Spring beds with cotton linens, velvet bedspreads and quilts with pillows stuffed with goose or duck down.

Some rooms have free-standing bathtubs and stunning Georgian-style windows. The restaurant offers its guests interesting new dishes from the chefs. In this place, they try to use exclusively seasonal local products in cooking. A lounge bar is open around the clock, and you can drink your favorite drink in it, admiring the sea from the window and sitting down behind a granite glossy bar counter. Free internet catches everywhere.

4. The Moreland

In a city like Brighton, hotels are available for every taste and budget. The Moreland, which is located in the very center, one minute from High Street (main street), offers rooms with a private bathroom. Just a couple of minutes to the city beach. The bright rooms are equipped with a TV. Extras include free toiletries, a hairdryer and terry towels.



The rooms have a full-length mirror and free Wi-Fi. Various cafes, restaurants and bars are in the surrounding area. In the morning, a hearty continental breakfast is served, which consists of cheeses, smoked mackerel, more than 12 varieties of cereal and cold cuts.

3. Thistle Brighton

Arriving in Brighton, hotels to your liking are not difficult to find. Speaking of Thistle Brighton, it is worth noting that it is 5 minutes from Brighton Pier and the Royal Pavilion. With stunning sea views, the hotel offers guests a restaurant, swimming pool and spa. Each room has an LCD TV and Wi-Fi. Slippers, cosmetics and bathrobes in the bathrooms await guests.

In addition, the Otium Health Club is open here, where you can go to the sauna and take a hot tub. You can also head to the well-equipped gym, then relax in the comfortable sun loungers. Brasserie serves classic British and European dishes. The excellent Atrium bar can also be visited: it is actually located in the heart of the hotel, in the atrium, under a glass domed roof. In this place fresh flowers and trees grow.

2. Atlantic Seafront

Domestic tourists are very interested in Brighton. Hotels here are located both along the coast and in the city center. Atlantic Seafront is located opposite the pier and close to bars, nightlife and restaurants. This hotel has free internet, and from the windows of the rooms you can look at the sea. In addition, some rooms have a digital TV with DVD.



An authentic English breakfast will be served each morning, and there are plenty of shops, bars and restaurants nearby. Lanes, the famous shopping spot, is a ten minute walk from here.

1. Hotel Seattle

Hotel guests are offered to book a room with stunning views of the marina and blue sea hotel Hotel Seattle (Brighton). The hotels located here often have rooms with such a spectacular view. But not everyone can offer such additional services as this one. Among them are rooms with unusual rain showers, a bar, a restaurant and completely free parking. Each room has access to the terrace, from where the sea is also clearly visible.



All bright and large rooms have their own bathroom. They are furnished with new furniture and also decorated with objects of art. Each also has large beds and desks. The restaurant with huge windows allows you to eat on the glazed veranda or outdoor terrace. At this place you can taste the English modern dishes that are created using quality local products. The hotel also has a bar where you can see the marina.
