Troika D Bank. Services and customer opinions

Troika D is a fairly young bank with a head office located in Moscow. Today, a financial institution is actively developing its retail business, however, servicing legal entities is still very important.

Bank certificate

The financial organization Troika D Bank CJSC was founded and registered by the regulator at the end of 2002 and was originally called Standard Bank CJSC. Serving corporate clients, as well as promoting investment programs, was chosen as the main line of business . At that time, the financial institution was a 100% subsidiary of Standard International Holdings (SIH).





In the first half of 2009, SIH and the Troika Dialog group entered into a strategic alliance agreement, as a result of which Troika received significant foreign investment, as well as a full stake in Standard Bank. About a year later, the credit institution changed its name and became known as Troika Dialog.

For several years, the organization was a member of the Troika Dialog financial group. So far, in 2011, the parent organization did not completely come under the control of Sberbank and was integrated into the company.

Initially, Sberbank planned to create, on the basis of the financial institution, one of the most powerful banks on the market, specializing in servicing large legal entities and providing investments. But the plans changed dramatically, and in 2013 the credit institution was sold to new owners. Around the same time, the name again changed to ‚ÄúTroika D‚Äù. The bank came under the control of new owners, including Yuri Zhukov.

To date, the financial institution is controlled by Vladimir Akayev through one of the Cypriot companies.

Deposits of Troika D Bank

Troika D Bank attracts deposits quite actively only recently, after the arrival of new owners who decided to reorient the business of the financial institution and direct additional efforts to the development of the retail sector.





Deposits offered by Troika D Bank have a wide variety of conditions. But they are of interest more to those who are used to leaving money in a credit institution for a relatively short time. You can place maximum free funds for 1 year. Interest in rubles can reach 13.5%, and in foreign currency 4.2%.

Some products for investors offered by Troika D Bank are not quite standard for the Russian market. For example, the Index deposit has a floating interest rate, which depends on the conditions offered by the TOP-10 participants of Russian banks in terms of the amount of funds raised from individuals.

Naturally, all deposits placed by individuals at Troika D Bank are insured by the state. Plus, customers are offered a variety of bonus programs.

Loans

Troika D entered the retail lending market quite recently. Nevertheless, both consumer and mortgage loans are offered today. Unlike most credit organizations, Troika D prefers an individual approach to each client.

It is enough to fill out an application on the website or in the office and employees will contact to discuss specific conditions. They are individually selected for each client and are not fixed. It is Troika D Bank that receives mostly positive feedback from borrowers.





Legal entities service

From the very beginning, the business of Troika D Bank was aimed at servicing and lending to corporate clients. This made it possible to assemble a team of professionals in a financial organization and provide services in accordance with the highest modern standards.

Legal entities can use loans at Troika D Bank, open a current account for current operations, and select a deposit. The bank is widely developing card products, offering corporate clients both salary projects and business cards.

The owner of organizations engaged in trade, acquiring services are available. For those whose business involves the supply of goods from abroad, currency control services are also provided . Troika D pays special attention to a variety of investment programs.





Customer Opinions

A financial institution focused on corporate clients should naturally meet all the highest requirements. Therefore, Troika D Bank receives mostly positive reviews, and in case of problem situations it always makes contact with the client and tries to solve the difficulties or malfunctions that have arisen.

Many private clients of the bank note the high professionalism of employees working in operating rooms. If necessary, they are always ready to provide the client with advice on all the products that Troika D offers.

Most clients also note the convenience of the location of offices, the polite attitude to each visitor and the work style of employees that meets the highest standards of banking services.
