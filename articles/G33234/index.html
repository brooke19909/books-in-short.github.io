Check-In / Check-Out

In accordance with the rules of the five-star Narcia Resort (Side) hotel, travelers are checked into the rooms they booked after two in the afternoon. However, this rule is not strict. So, the administration of Narcia Resort 5 *, reviews of tourists are confirmation of this, trying to accommodate those guests who are arriving earlier than the specified time as soon as possible. However, in the midst of the holiday season this may not be possible due to the full occupancy of the hotel. In this case, you have the opportunity to leave your things in storage in a special room and begin your vacation. After all, you will immediately be put on a bracelet upon arrival, and you can go for lunch, take a swim in the pool or the sea, and conveniently sit on the sun terrace or the beach.

As for the day of departure, the occupied room should be vacated before noon. If your plane departs only in the evening, and the hotel has a sufficient number of free rooms, then you can extend your stay for several hours at an additional cost. By the way, you can pay for your stay here both in cash and with plastic cards of such popular payment systems as Mastercard or Visa.





Accommodation for guests with children

Since the Narcia Resort Hotel 5 * (Side) is largely focused on family accommodation, they will be happy if you come to rest with children of any age. For the youngest tourists and their parents, the hotel has all the conditions for a comfortable, interesting and fun pastime.

Please note that if you place a child under 12 years old in existing bedding or a baby up to two years old in an extra bed, you will not have to pay extra for this. For placing a child or an adult on an extra bed, you will have to pay from 50% to 70% of the cost of living per person per night. Maximum in each room can be installed only one extra adult or cot. This service is available upon prior request. Therefore, if you want an extra cot or adult bed in your room, notify the hotel in advance and wait for confirmation.

Accommodation of tourists with animals

At this point, you should pay particular attention to those travelers who are accustomed to go on vacation, taking with them their four-legged pet. So, according to the rules of the Narcia Resort Hotel 5 *, the accommodation of guests with pets is strictly prohibited. Therefore, you will either have to choose another hotel to stay, or leave your pet at home for the rest.
