Short description





The recreation center "Venice" (Kamensk-Shakhtinsky), the photo of which you can see above, opened its doors only in August 2015. Therefore, everything is fresh and new.

On the territory of 20 thousand square meters. everything that allows you to enjoy your vacation has fit: comfortable rooms with comfortable furniture, an aquazone with clean water and water slides, a restaurant serving delicious dishes of fresh products, a tennis court and much more. Here everyone will find something to do.

List of Services

The price of accommodation at the recreation center "Venice" (Kamensk-Shakhtinsky) includes:

  • set breakfast from October to May;
  • placement of a child under 7 years old without a separate seat;
  • use of wireless internet;
  • use of satellite television;
  • housekeeping;
  • bed linen change;
  • use of the aquazone;
  • use of a children's playground;
  • the provision of medical services.




The cost of living varies depending on the category of rooms and may vary from time of year. Prices for accommodation start at 3,500 rubles per day (as of 2017).

For an additional fee on the territory of the recreation center "Venice" (Kamensk-Shakhtinsky) the following is offered:

  • provision of an extra bed;
  • secure parking;
  • food in the restaurant and bar;
  • set breakfast from June to September;
  • bicycles for rent;
  • playing billiards and table tennis;
  • visit to the sauna.

Number of rooms





Hotel "Venice" offers its guests to stay in one of 33 comfortable apartments. There are three categories of rooms available:

  • one-room double economy;
  • one-room four-seater standard family;
  • two-room or one-room double suite.

Each room has a double bed, bedside tables, a set of upholstered furniture (armchairs, sofa), coffee table, wardrobe, mirror, chairs, a TV stand, LCD TV. The bathroom in the apartment is combined, equipped with a shower. The bathroom also includes a hairdryer, shower set (soap, shower gel, shampoo), bathrobe and slippers. All rooms have air conditioning to maintain a comfortable microclimate.

The apartments are located in four small buildings. In total, the recreation center is designed for 66 people, excluding extra beds.

Catering





On the territory of the recreation center "Venice" (Kamensk-Shakhtinsky) works:

  • cozy resto-platform for 150 people;
  • luxurious banquet hall for various celebrations, designed for 300 guests;
  • bar.

The menu includes various snacks, salads, first courses, side dishes, pasta, meat and fish, pizza, dishes prepared on the grill, fresh vegetables for the season, as well as several types of delicious desserts. Separately worked out children's menu.

The bar has a large selection of beer, wine, liquor, whiskey, vodka, cognac, cocktails and soft drinks (coffee, tea, juices and so on). All orders are carried out in any corner of the recreation center: to the pool, to the rooms, to the sauna, billiards and so on.

Organization of leisure in the hotel complex





One of the main advantages of the recreation center "Venice" is its own aquazone. Around the pool there are sun loungers; there are two types of water slides. Nearby is a children's zone, where the depth is only 40 cm. It is noteworthy that cold weather is not an obstacle to water activities, since the swimming pools in Venice are heated.

Those who are tired of water treatments can visit the sauna. It is designed for holidays up to 10-15 people. If desired, meals and drinks can be delivered to the Finnish steam room from the restaurant of the hotel complex. In the sauna you can buy everything you need: brooms, shower sets, bathrobes, slippers.

Among the active entertainment at the recreation center "Venice" (Kamensk-Shakhtinsky) offer tennis and cycling. The tennis court is open during the warmer months from 10 a.m. to 10 p.m. The hourly fee includes the use of the site and the provision of the necessary game equipment. At the point of delivery of bicycles you will be provided with serviceable models of two-wheeled transport, where you can take exciting walks in the vicinity.

You can also relax in the billiards room. There are three gaming areas with professional tables and accessories, as well as a relaxation area with comfortable sofas and tables. It is possible to rent both one game zone and the entire hall.

Reviews about the recreation center "Venice" (Kamensk-Shakhtinsky)





In the short time that Venice exists, a sufficient number of vacationers have already managed to visit here. Mostly people come here for the weekend, and also to celebrate any holidays, family celebrations (weddings, birthdays) and corporate parties.

Guests of the recreation center "Venice" note the following points:

  • Good location. Near the recreation center is the Log Landscape Park, where you can walk and enjoy the beauties of nature.
  • Chic territory. Around the houses is a lawn and flower beds are broken. And between all this, the paths are paved with tiles.
  • It is possible, without stopping in the room, to enjoy the services provided in the hotel complex (swimming pool, sauna, restaurant).
  • Heated pool. Water even warms up to +25 degrees in winter.
  • Virtually no shadow.
  • The territory is clean and comfortable.
  • There is an ennobled children's playground with a whole game complex of slides, swings and other attractions. But it is not in the shade, so in hot weather it is not safe for children here.
  • Near the adult pool there is an outdoor shower where you can rinse after bathing.
  • The food is delicious in the restaurant, but you should expect your order a long time.
  • There is no way to go with your food, there are no refrigerators in the rooms.
  • The bar is open until 2am, so music is heard in the rooms.
  • Not all rooms are sterile clean. In some you can find stains on the mirror, dust and small debris.
  • The price of rest is quite reasonable.
  • Wireless internet works well throughout the recreation center.
  • There are quite a lot of people in the hot season.
  • From time to time, animators work around the pool.
  • The magnificent sauna works.
  • For children there are discounts on accommodation, entrance to the pool.
