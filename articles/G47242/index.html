Haier washing machines: description, features, error codes

Haier washing machines are manufactured by a Chinese company. In Russia, they are not as popular as Samsung, Electrolux, and Bosch. However, if you study the reviews of the owners, it becomes clear that these devices are worthy of attention. Almost all models are made in the original design, which is more suitable for expensive options. Technical specifications are also on top.

It is worth noting that Haier washing machines have a long warranty period (3 years). This is proof of quality and reliability. In the model line there are instances with large and low load. Devices differ in size. As a rule, the height of all is standard, but the depth can vary. There are also models that are equipped with a dryer. Most buyers claim that the washing machines of this brand work without breakdowns for 5-7 years.

disadvantages

Unfortunately, there are also comments on Haier products. Although the manufacturer is trying to correct them, the minuses still remain. The most important is factory marriage. Buyers claim that while still under warranty, the Haier washing machine may fail. The reviews of the owners, who were not lucky enough to encounter such a problem, assured that the service centers quickly enough respond to such calls.

Some models upset customers with the inability to choose the spin speed and additional rinse on their own. Unfortunately, the latter option is not provided in all Haier devices.

Many owners have noticed that sometimes the standard washing cycle is not enough for a good rinse. Also drew attention to the fact that spinning at maximum speed can lead to severe deformation of the fabric.



Haier washing machine errors

All Haier cars are equipped with a self-diagnosis system. Information is displayed using special codes.

ERR1 - the machine does not start (program failure).

ERR2 - violation of the drain system.

ERR3 and ERR4 - problems with heating water.

ERR5 - no water is supplied to the device.

ERR6 and ERR7 - module circuit failure.

ERR8 and ERR9 - failure of the water level control sensor.

ERR10 - Failure of the water system.

UNB - imbalance in the drum.

EUAR - the control panel has failed.

NO SALT - the detergent does not meet the requirements or is completely absent.
