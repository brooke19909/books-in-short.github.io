Attractions and entertainment Adler: photos and description, the most interesting places, entertaining facts and reviews of tourists

In southern Russia, near the border with Georgia, the city of Sochi is located. Its coast is washed by the Black Sea. This is a large resort city, known to millions of residents of the country, as well as abroad. Adler is a district of Sochi. It is located on the seafront between two rivers. Its length is about 17 kilometers along the sea. Adler can rightly be called a popular resort destination in Russia. Here, vacationers are provided not only the sea and beaches, but also the numerous attractions and entertainment of Adler in Sochi. It is worth paying attention to the features of this place.

A little bit about the history of Adler

The date of its foundation is 1869. Prior to this, there was a Russian fortification, founded in 1837, which lasted until 1854. Next to it was the Abkhazian settlement, known since the Middle Ages. Prior to joining the Russian Empire, these lands were owned by princes who traded with the Turks sailing here on ships. The Turks called this place Arta, which later became Adler. In 1910, a park was founded here, now called the Southern Cultures. And in 1961, Adler was included in the neighboring city of Sochi.

Olympic park and stadium

Among the main attractions and attractions of Adler, the Olympic Park and the stadium called Fisht are distinguished.

The Olympic Park is a complex of various buildings built for the Winter Olympic Games held here in 2014. Here were held competitions in hockey, figure skating, speed skating. After the games, they try to use this park as much as possible for all kinds of events. Its construction took about 6 years.

On the territory of the park there is a beautiful fountain with light music called "Olympic Flame Bowl". It is located on the main square of the park and is made in the image of the Russian folk tale of the Firebird, which here covers the pool with its wings. In the evenings, this place hosts a show using music and light. Many tourists and vacationers come here with great pleasure to see one of the attractions and attractions of Adler. Since the territory of the park is quite large, you can rent bicycles, rollers and even electric cars to quickly move around the park.

Another popular place on certain days is Fisht Stadium, located in the park. It hosted ceremonies in honor of the Olympic Games. After that, it was converted into a football stadium for the 2018 World Cup. It holds more than 40 thousand fans.

Car Attractions

Among the attractions and entertainment of the city of Adler, it is important to note the presence of a race track for Formula 1 racing. The Russian Grand Prix has been included in the list since 2014. All fans of car racing in the country come here for this grand prix. At other times, various types of competitions are held here, and on certain days, amateur riders can try their hand at their own car, having experienced the race track.

But this is not all that a motorist can see here. There are two museums in the park. The first museum is an exhibition of retro cars, as well as various things of the Soviet era. In it you can find many different exhibits, as well as car models, which drove prominent personalities of the Union.

The second is a museum of sports cars, or Formula 1. All racing enthusiasts can visit this place to explore the structure of the car, as well as enjoy the various sports cars that are presented here.

Rest near the sea and not only

For the winter games in Adler, a 7-km-long track was made along the promenade of the Imereti Bay. Today, both hiking tourists and cyclists walk with great pleasure on it. Fresh sea air, a gorgeous view of the sea, a beautiful beach along the path - all this leaves a good impression on vacationers walking. And if you set a goal to go all the distance, you can see all the Olympic facilities.

In addition to attractions and entertainment, a vacation in Adler also includes a beach. This is a great place to soak up the sun. Although the beach is pebble, in places you can see small sandy areas. In recent years, he has been landscaped. True, there is one drawback that prevents vacationers - the proximity of the railway.

For those who want a more active holiday with water, Adler has an amphibius water park. It occupies a relatively small territory, about 2 hectares, but accommodates enough attractions for children and adults. There are 5 pools, as well as several slides. The cost of rest in the water park is 1200 rubles.

Beautiful corners of nature

The nature of this region is considered delightful and very rich. Tourists who love excursions can enjoy the beauty of the Akhshtyr cave. It was discovered back in 1936. Its depth is about 160 meters. Since 1999, tourists have the opportunity to enter it and feel like explorers.

Another beautiful place is a waterfall called Deep Yar. This is one of the highest waterfalls in Sochi. Its height is 41 meters. Tourists are happy to go here with the help of a guide or according to signs placed everywhere. At the top of the waterfall, you can also visit the ruins of a Christian temple. Here are very beautiful views of the area.

Other attractions and activities of Adler

Everyone will agree that rest is considered inferior without visiting the market. In Adler, there is also such a place. Here tourists can buy delicious fruits and local delicacies grown here or brought from other places: Kuban, Abkhazia, Turkey. All kinds of spices attract buyers who come here.

On the territory of Adler is the shopping and entertainment center "Orange", where visitors can buy something to remember the trip, and relax, and just take a walk. The center consists of nine buildings, has a cafe, pool, observation tower, concert hall and much more. This town, where you can happily get lost, is one of Adler's attractions and entertainments both in winter and in summer.

The city also has a large two-story aquarium, which accommodates 30 aquariums. Here you can see a variety of tropical fish and the inhabitants of the seas and oceans. A feature of this building is a 44-meter-long tunnel, following which you can observe the life of the inhabitants of the aquarium.

Quite a few facts are known about the attractions and entertainments of Adler.
