What to see in Monaco

Most tourists who travel to this country, of course, primarily go to its capital, Monte Carlo. Of course, we all heard about the famous casino. Even if you are not a fan of gambling, you should take a look at its famous building. After all, it was built by the famous architect Charles Garnier - the one who is the author of the Paris Grand Opera. It surprises visitors with its luxurious decoration. It was here that episodes of several James Bond films were filmed. And right on the seashore there is a luxurious Japanese garden. This landscape park is good for walking at any time of the year. But the main attraction of the country, which is included in the mandatory program of any tour in Monaco, is the princely palace. He stands on top of a steep cliff. You can get there in two ways. Firstly, on a tourist train along a narrow track - this is paid. And secondly, on the elevator, which is laid inside the rock. This is already free for budget travelers. This is the real home of a noble family that rules the principality. Around noon, a colorful change of the guard takes place at the gate. If a flag flutters over the palace, then the prince is at home. And nearby there is a small museum where waxworks of all representatives of this dynasty - Grimaldi are exhibited.

Old city, museums and event tourism

Do not forget about the medieval history of the country. Tours in Monaco are impossible without a tour of the Old Town and the cathedral. Not far from it are the gardens of St. Martin. It offers marvelous panoramic views, especially of the surrounding cliffs, mountains and the Mediterranean Sea. And here is the famous Oceanographic Museum. It was he who for many years was led by the cult explorer of the deep sea Jacques-Yves Cousteau. A yellow bathyscaphe is installed in front of it, in which the scientist fell under water. The museum operates mainly in the summer. They come to Monaco to attend various interesting events. For example, in May, the Grand Prix of the famous Formula 1 races takes place here. And in February, a car rally called Monte Carlo is organized here.

Travel to paradise

Wherever you go on a tour to Monaco - from Moscow, Minsk or Kiev, to come here means to get to a blessed place. Despite the fact that passion and excitement are boiling here day and night, the country itself is very quiet and calm. Its inhabitants are dominated by the cult of traditional family values. Nobody is in a hurry here. In these places you will learn to enjoy life, every moment of it, you will drink a cup of coffee or a glass of wine in a restaurant for a couple of hours, and no one will push you. Behind any bend offers magnificent views. People dress simply but tastefully. Local cuisine will suit anyone. Tourists especially appreciate fruit tarts and beer with lemonade. The pavement everywhere is just incredibly smooth. Each house is surrounded by blooming gardens. Everywhere there are video cameras so that both locals and tourists can feel safe. In a word, a country ideal for a comfortable life. Not without reason are the rich so fond of resting here. But ordinary people can afford at least one eye to look at this paradise. Combined tours

To begin with, it is difficult to organize a trip to this country only. This is enough to see all the sights and even make a bet at the casino. Combined tours in Monaco are bus and air, they can pass through Italy, France and even Spain. Pros and Cons of Bus Tours

Although some trips with a visit to Monaco, as we have seen, include air travel, you will mostly have to move around inside the bus. Many have the question of how comfortable it is. Do tourists expect sleepless nights, after which they will not want any sights and beauties? The bus tour in Monaco, like any trip of this kind in Europe, has its pros and cons. Let's start with the positive.

Firstly, such a tour will be much cheaper than a trip by rail or air travel, and will allow you to explore several countries at once. Secondly, you can appreciate the national flavor and traditions of each state, into which you even get in transit.

However, quite often such trips involve a simply frantic rhythm, which is not suitable for everyone. If you like to relax and feel the taste of the country, it is better to choose a tour where you live in one place for several days, going from there on excursions included in your program or additional. Pay attention to the type and class of the bus. Cars should be with comfortable chairs, panoramic windows, designed for the fact that in a short time you have to cover long distances. And, of course, choose tours without night trips. Then you can sleep well at the hotel and with fresh energy go for new experiences.
