Beeline: "All for 150." Tariff Plan Description

Tariff plans with the included volume of services are very popular among customers of mobile operators. To deposit a certain amount at a time into the account and not to think about additional payments for a whole month is a really attractive offer. However, all tariff plans have their own characteristics, which cannot be ignored. Moreover, they should be introduced in advance. Indeed, many important points are hidden under the notorious "stars", implying unnecessary expenses.

What does the Beeline operator offer its subscribers for one hundred and fifty rubles a month? First of all, these are unlimited calls to the numbers of people who also use the SIM cards of this provider. In this case, we are talking about communication within their field. Calls are truly unlimited, without any restrictions. At the same time, communication with subscribers of other regions of the Beeline company is also carried out on fairly favorable and interesting conditions.





A daily package containing 100 minutes is provided. Within this limit, you can communicate for free with your friends and colleagues, relatives and friends. As soon as the installed volume is used up on the Beeline number (tariff ‚ÄúAll for 150‚Äù), all further calls (until the end of the day) will be charged. The cost of a minute of communication will be 5 rubles. It is a lot or a little - to judge only to subscribers. For comparison, I would like to clarify that when making long-distance calls to numbers of other operators, 10 rubles per minute are charged. When calling to numbers of other mobile operators within your region, the cost of a minute will be 1 ruble.

the Internet

It also offers its customers a package from Beeline All-for-150. The monthly fee (150 rubles per month) includes Internet traffic. A 1 GB package is provided for the entire billing period (per month). For some, this amount may show insignificant. In this case, you can activate additional options ("Highway", etc.).





It is also possible to use auto-renew speed. By the way, this service is activated by default on the tariff plan from Beeline (150 rubles a month). This means that as soon as you spend 1000 megabytes (during the billing period,) a package with an additional 200 MB for 20 rubles will be connected. The number of such connections is not limited. Thus, if you do not monitor the traffic, you can spend a significant amount. You can disable auto-renew by dialing 067 471 7780. To check the rest of the traffic - 06745.

It is also important to understand that if you turn off auto-renewal of speed and use the TP limit, then the speed limit will be set at 64 Kb / s. For the normal use of the Internet, this is not enough. However, if you wait until the end of the month, then it will be possible to reuse the provided TP traffic (1000 Mb).

TP Features

All tariffs have their own characteristics. Beeline (150 month) is no exception. I would like to highlight the following parameters:

  • Upon transition to TP 50 rubles will be debited . A one-time payment is charged from all subscribers when changing the tariff plan.
  • On the account at the time of the change of TP should be at least 100 rubles. Otherwise, the charge will not be made. By the way, this amount will not disappear from the account, its availability is simply necessary.
  • The daily fee is 5 rubles. For a month the amount is 150 rubles.
  • The service "Auto Renew Speed" is activated for all customers who switched to this TP or bought a new SIM card.
  • In order to optimize the TP and make it more profitable when calling to numbers of mobile operators and landlines in your region, you can connect the option for 50 rubles. According to its conditions, 100 minutes will be provided daily for calls to any numbers except Beeline.
