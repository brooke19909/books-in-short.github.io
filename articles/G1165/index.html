Cheap hotels of Kharkov: description

They will certainly be interested in those who first arrived in Kharkov, hotels. Inexpensive near the station you can stay in different hotels. We will consider them in our article. We also describe other cheap hotels that are perfect for the first time.

Hotel "Demetra" - apartments for those who want to economically settle

So, what are the hotels of Kharkov, cheap where you can rent a room? For example, an institution called "Demeter." Due to the high level of comfort, your stay here will be pleasant. At the same time, prices are quite low. For a day for an economy class number you need to give local 220 hryvnias, which corresponds to approximately 550 Russian rubles. You can also rent a room for three to four hours (for example, you need to wait until the train arrives this time). Then it will be necessary to pay only 120 hryvnia, that is, 300 rubles.





If you are interested in cheap hotels near the railway of Kharkov, then pay attention to the "Demeter". It is located near the South Station. You can get to the hotel from the railway by any tram. You will need to drive just one stop towards the center. You can also walk to the hotel. The journey takes about ten minutes.

Continuing to talk about inexpensive hotels in Kharkov, we describe this. The establishment is located 700 meters from the railway station of the city. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel, so every guest can easily access the Internet.

The hotel has a kitchen, which is fully equipped for self-cooking. For those arriving by car, there is parking. At the same time, there is no need to pay extra for the fact that the car is located there.

How to get to the hotel? The hotel is located a twelve-minute walk from Yuzhny Vokzal metro station.

The hotel is located near the metro station "Sports Palace". The hotel has rooms of different categories. The cost of living per day per person ranges from 350 rubles to 850, depending on the type of room.
