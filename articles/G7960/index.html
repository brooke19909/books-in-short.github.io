Hotel Cubanacan Tuxpan 4 * (Cuba / Varadero): photos and reviews of tourists

Cuba is a country that is especially loved by tourists. Tens of thousands of tourists from all over the world visit it annually. After all, this is a state with an interesting way, history and traditions. And nature generously rewarded the island, turning it into a real paradise. Do not forget that Cuba is perfect for tourists who want to spend the winter enjoying the warmth.

Naturally, tourist infrastructure is not developed throughout the country. Varadero is by far one of the largest and most popular resort cities - this is where the bulk of tourists flock. On the coast there are a large number of hotels of various categories. One of them is the hotel complex Cubanacan Tuxpan 4 *.

What does the hotel look like and is it possible to come here with a child? How long will the road to the beach take? What kind of food can you count on? How do people who have already managed to spend several days respond about the hotel? These issues should be addressed at the travel planning stage.

Hotel location features





The location of the hotel is an important point that employees first of all pay attention to. So where to look for the hotel Cubanacan Tuxpan 4 *? Varadero, one of the most beautiful Cuban resorts - it is here, almost in the very center of the city, that the hotel complex is located.

By the way, there are shops, cafes, restaurants and even a small shopping center nearby, so you will not feel divorced from public life. The distance to Varadero Airport is 27 km - in a few minutes convenient transport will already bring you to the hotel, which is very convenient. But the Havana airport is further away, namely 160 km. The distance to the Cuban capital is 152 km, so many travelers visit vibrant Havana during excursions around the island.

Cubanacan Tuxpan 4 *: description of the hotel and the territory

The hotel complex consists of two multi-storey buildings and occupies a fairly large territory. It should be said right away that the hotel was built in 1991, so you should not count on a unique design. Nevertheless, in 2012 a complete reconstruction was carried out here - tourists can count on high-quality repairs, clean rooms, a cozy atmosphere and working equipment.

In the courtyard there is a beautiful garden where you can hide from the scorching sun, walk along secluded paths, relax on terraces, play sports, swim in the pool, in a word, spend time with pleasure.

How many rooms are on site? Photo and description





The Cubanacan Tuxpan 4 * hotel complex is considered to be large - it offers 235 spacious rooms with balconies or private terraces. The rooms are decorated in a simple but pleasant style. You can count on a large bed, a spacious wardrobe, a table, chairs and other necessary furniture.

Household appliances are also available. You can shorten the time by watching satellite channels on TV. A modern safe with an electronic lock is mounted in the cabinet wall. There is a telephone, as well as a large roomy refrigerator. By the way, maids replenish supplies of drinking water daily. There is a good air conditioning system, which you just can not do without during the hot periods of the year. The rooms are cleaned every day, and not quite badly - this is confirmed by reviews of guests.

Food scheme for tourists





The issue of nutrition is extremely important for almost every traveler, because during your vacation you want to enjoy delicacies. What does the hotel Cubanacan Tuxpan 4 * offer? Guest reviews say that by and large there are no problems with food. The hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis, which means that you will find a full three meals a day.

Meals are organized in the form of a buffet - you yourself are free to choose the dishes that you like. The premise of the main restaurant is spacious and cozy, so you can have a good time here. The menu necessarily contains fresh fruits, vegetables, as well as meat, fish and seafood dishes. Guests are served drinks, however, coffee is prepared only in the local bar. By the way, here you can get light snacks during the day.

How far is the seashore?





For many tourists, Cuba is associated with picturesque beaches. And this is true - the country's coast is very beautiful. The hotel complex Cubanacan Tuxpan 4 * is located close to the beach. Just a few minutes - and you are already on an incredibly beautiful beach, covered with fine, white sand. Reviews indicate that the entrance to the sea is convenient and cleared of stones and algae. Cleaned ashore every day.

It is a municipal beach, so there is a lively atmosphere. For a small fee, you can rent a lounger and a comfortable mattress, hide from the sun under a wide umbrella.

Beach Travelers Entertainment

Fans of more active pastimes will not be bored. Nearby is a sports center where you can easily rent non-motorized water transport, including boats and catamarans. In addition, guests have the opportunity to go skiing, take a short boat trip, go windsurfing and sailing. By the way, experienced professionals will help you with this, who will conduct a small but effective training for very little money.

By the way, here tourists can attend diving courses (people who already have experience rent the necessary equipment here). Fans of fishing will not be disappointed either, because the underwater view is really rich here.

Hotel Cubanacan Tuxpan 4 * (Cuba, Varadero): description of the additional service





Of course, going on vacation, tourists rely on an interesting and comfortable stay. What can the hotel complex Cubanacan Tuxpan 4 * offer? Services provided to guests make life much easier, eliminating small household problems.

For example, you can exchange currency without leaving the hotel. A laundry room is constantly working here, so you will not have problems with clean clothes. There is no wireless Internet access (by the way, the Internet is a rarity in Cuba), but there are several computers in the lobby that can be used to access the network.

In case of health problems, a qualified doctor will help you. There is also a parking lot at the hotel and a place where you can rent a car or other means of transport for little money. Nearby is a small shopping center where you can buy food and some small things.

How do tourists spend their leisure time?





This hotel complex is more designed for beach holidays. Nevertheless, you can have a good time in the hotel area. In the courtyard there is a large pool where guests can swim and chill. Nearby on the terrace are sun loungers and umbrellas. There is also a bar with drinks and snacks.

There is another indoor pool with a heating system - this place is suitable for swimming in the evening or rainy weather. For a small fee, guests can use the sauna and relax in the jacuzzi. And there is also the opportunity to enroll in wellness massage courses.

There are several tennis courts on site that fans of this sport will appreciate. Spacious golf courses are located nearby - experienced players have a great time here, and beginners have the opportunity to receive instruction. Also popular with tourists are cycling, dance classes and horse riding lessons.

Do not forget about sightseeing holidays, because Cuba is a beautiful country with an unusual history. You can pick up and buy a sightseeing tour at the hotel or in any other tourist office of the city.

Family holidays at the hotel: are there any conditions for hanging out with a child?

For parents planning a trip with their child, comfort issues always come to the fore. So what can the Cubanacan Tuxpan 4 offer? The description posted on the official website indicates that the hotel complex is perfect for an interesting family vacation.

In terms of amenities, parents can expect an extra bed in the room (provided free of charge). In the buffet you can always find tasty and satisfying food for the baby. And if necessary, for a relatively reasonable fee, the baby can be left with the nanny for several hours.

As for entertainment, the children at the Cubanacan Tuxpan 4 * hotel are not bored. A special safe pool is equipped for them - the depth is shallow, and the water is always warm, so the guys can splash around and have fun in the company of new friends. There is a well-equipped playground for games with swings, as well as a mini club where teachers and animators are engaged with the guys.

What category of vacationers is the hotel suitable for?

Hotel Cubanacan Tuxpan 4 * is ideal for an interesting holiday on the shores of the picturesque Caribbean Sea. Varadero is a calm town, so you can be sure that your vacation will not be overshadowed by the noise of a big city.

The hotel complex is focused on family vacations - not only adult tourists, but also their kids spend interesting time here. In addition, youth companies often come here, because on the coast you can have a lot of vivid entertainment. Newlyweds often come here who want a romantic atmosphere and privacy.

How do travelers respond about the hotel?





A small conversation with a person who has already managed to spend several days in a particular hotel can sometimes be more informative than dozens of pages of official information. So what do travelers say about the Cubanacan Tuxpan 4 * hotel? Reviews of tourists by and large are positive.

The hotel complex is not new, but renovated, and the territory here is very beautiful - it is regularly cleaned and improved. The rooms are quite spacious, although not too bright. On the other hand, the presence of shade helps to cope with the summer heat. Some rooms have old appliances, but everything works, so tourists have no special complaints. Food at a decent level. Naturally, the guests who spent several weeks here note a certain uniformity of the menu, but the dishes are really tasty and fresh, they are served in sufficient quantities and no one is left hungry. By the way, excellent coffee is brewed in the bar, and you also have the opportunity to enjoy the taste of real Cuban rum.

The beach can be reached in a few minutes - daily walks to the coast can be really enjoyable. The staff at the hotel is very friendly and polite, some organizational problems and conflicts with the staff are extremely rare. Unfortunately, there are practically no Russian-speaking workers on the territory, but this will not be needed, since you can always explain.

Travelers recommend the Cubanacan Tuxpan 4 * hotel complex. Cuba is a great place to stay!
