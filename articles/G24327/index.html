Day of love, family and fidelity

Today, tourists know Murom largely thanks to Saints Peter and Fevronia, who have long become symbols of a happy family life. According to legend, Peter won the battle of a fiery beast, but the monster's blood got on his body, which caused terrible leprosy. No one could cure the disease.

Once a brave young man had a dream that Fevronia, the daughter of a flight attendant, could help him. The virgin in return asked to marry her, the future prince agreed. However, Fevronia envisioned such an outcome - the girl left one wound on her body, due to which the disease began to spread again. After the second attempt, the young married, but the boyars did not want to accept the princess.

Then Peter and Fevronia left their hometown. After their departure, the struggle for the throne began, in this troubled time, many people died due to the thirst for power. Nobles had to ask the princes for return. Until the end of their life, Peter and Fevronia were together and died at the same time and day.

Today, the relics of the saints are in the Holy Trinity Monastery. Since 2008, Russia has celebrated July 8 as Family Day, whose patrons are Peter and Fevronia. Husbands and wives come to Murom to ask for happiness and future offspring, and grooms and brides are married here. Guest house - rest in Russian

For more than twenty years in the field of Russian tourism such a concept as a guest house has been firmly entrenched. If you are going to Murom, hotels will welcome you warmly, but only one of the guest houses will feel at home.

This type of housing is no worse than traditional offers for tourists. Today, guest houses are especially popular on the Black Sea coast.
