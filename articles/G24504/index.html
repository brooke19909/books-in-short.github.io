Bullet ant

What insects eat insects? One of them is a bullet ant. It should be feared due to a strong bite resembling a wound. Hence the name of the insect. This ant is small, only two and a half centimeters in length. He arranges his house on trees. Before the attack, he screams piercingly, and then falls from a height on his victim.
