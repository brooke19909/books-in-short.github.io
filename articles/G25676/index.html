The recreation center "Infanta" (Inal Bay, Tuapse district): how to get, reviews

Summer is an amazing time, the integral part of which is good sunny weather, a good mood and a desire to go to the sea to sunbathe, enjoy plenty of fun and have a good time. Ahead of vacation or long-awaited vacation that you want to spend as varied and as interesting as possible. But where to go? How much will the package cost? Will there be something to see? Absolutely every tourist is asking these questions when planning a future trip.

If there is not so much free money as we would like, then you can consider a vacation in Russia, on the Black Sea coast, and choose a resort that is ideal for both interests and climate. One of the most visited resorts in Russia is Tuapse. Here you will find absolutely everything that is needed both for an active type of recreation, and for a calm, peaceful pastime. Tuapse is famous for its many beaches, the vibrant and incredibly beautiful promenade with diverse bars, restaurants, cafes and souvenir shops. Tourists can choose from excursions to help make their vacation unforgettable.





If you want to spend your vacation actively, the noise is not an obstacle and does not bother a large number of people, then in this case the hotel should be chosen closer to the waterfront. Then all the entertainment offered to tourists will be within walking distance.

If you want to relax calmly, enjoying the unusual nature away from the hustle and bustle, then you should consider the suburbs for relaxation.

For such a holiday, the Infanta tourist base is perfect. It is located in the Tuapse district, in the unusually beautiful Inal Bay between the resorts of Dzhubga and Arkhipo-Osipovka.





Short description

In Tuapse, Inal Bay is known to everyone as a small village consisting of numerous tourist centers. The place is especially popular among vacationers, because the beach is quite wide and long, almost 5 km long. The bottom is very flat and flat. Small pebbles do not cause absolutely any inconvenience, so it will be very comfortable to relax here with young children.

The village is surrounded by coniferous forest and mountains. It is famous for the presence of blue healing clay and springs. In this regard, the bay was given the status of a health resort.

Room Types

Accommodation at the Infanta recreation center in Inal Bay is an excellent choice for a summer vacation. Especially if you spend it in the "Infante". This recreation center is a complex of detached comfortable cottages. The choice of vacationers offers several accommodation options:

  • double suite;
  • double junior suite;
  • two-story junior suite made of timber;
  • standard house with a veranda.

Each cottage is equipped with a split system, so staying in it is always comfortable, regardless of the weather. Each house has a refrigerator, kettle, telephone, TV. If additional space is required, you can use either a cot or a folding sofa.





Landscape

The territory of the recreation center is incredibly beautiful, it seems that it is surrounded by greenery. An abundance of diverse trees and flowers fill the air with an exceptional aroma. Guests here have created all the comfortable conditions for relaxation. And the artificial waterfall pleases the eye and gives a romantic mood.

Entertainment

On the territory there is a large outdoor pool with sun loungers, a illuminated waterfall, a special area with barbecue facilities, a bathhouse, children's playgrounds, billiards, table tennis, karaoke, a cafe, a banquet hall for special occasions, and guarded parking.

At sea, if desired, it is possible to rent a boat or jet ski, ride catamarans, jet skis and other attractions.

And if there is a desire to go on interesting excursions, the Infanta recreation center will offer the most interesting options. Exit programs are regularly organized here that allow you to visit all the most important attractions. In addition, the list of proposals includes a boat trip, relaxation in the water park, excursions to Tuapse waterfalls.

Nutrition

There is a cafe on the territory of the base. Here you can taste incredibly delicious dishes. A special menu has been developed for young children, so you can not worry that the child will remain hungry.





The menu is quite diverse. It includes several options for hot dishes, cold dishes, a variety of drinks and desserts, so you can easily choose any dish that will appeal to both adults and children.

The recreation center "Infanta" is ideal for weddings, anniversaries and other significant events. The cafe can accommodate up to 150 people, the banquet room is designed for 30 seats. The original design of the halls, a specially created banquet menu, live music and an entertainment program will make any holiday unforgettable.

Recreation center "Infanta", Inal Bay: how to get

Direct buses go from Krasnodar here. Travel time will be about 2.5 hours. Goryachiy Klyuch also runs transport from the city, on which you can get to the recreation center.

Also a great advantage is the ability to book a transfer on a tourist base along the route Krasnodar - recreation center "Infanta", Inal Bay - Krasnodar. This service must be agreed with the administration by telephone in advance.





Reviews about the recreation center "Infanta" in the bay of Inal

Most of the tourists who visited here were satisfied. Tourists note that the recreation center itself is located in a picturesque place. The pluses include a well-groomed spacious area, on which there is where to walk. The base is perfect for relaxation. In summer, the weather in Inal Bay is almost always sunny, rains are extremely rare, and the water temperature is ideal to freshen up and get the most out of swimming.

Near the tourist base there are several souvenir shops and cafes where live music is played. All cottages are in fairly good condition; rooms are cleaned daily, which, of course, cannot but rejoice. The area is definitely clean. When planning a trip, you should know in advance about the rules of accommodation, food and room rates.

The recreation center "Infanta", the excellent weather in Inal Bay and the magnificent Black Sea will leave an indelible impression of your vacation!
