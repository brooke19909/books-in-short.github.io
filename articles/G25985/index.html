VTB Bank: customer reviews of loans, repayment terms, interest rates and refinancing

It will be interesting to get acquainted with customer feedback on loans from VTB Bank to everyone who is planning to apply for a loan in one of the domestic financial organizations in the near future. From this article you can find out what conditions and interest rates you can count in this bank, what needs to be done in order to arrange refinancing.

About the bank

Customer reviews of loans at VTB Bank are very different. Often it depends on what kind of loan visitors issued. Therefore, we will deal with all the options in detail.

VTB is a domestic commercial bank with a government stake of over sixty percent. It is considered the second largest country in terms of assets and takes first place in terms of authorized capital. The head office is located in Moscow, while the bank itself is officially registered in St. Petersburg.

It began its history in 1990 when, with the direct participation of the Ministry of Finance and the State Bank of the RSFSR, the Foreign Trade Bank was established. Initially, it appeared in the form of a closed joint-stock company in order to service the operations of Russia in the foreign economic market, as well as to assist the country's integration in various areas of the world economy.

Since 2002, the post of chairman of the bank's board has been held by Andrei Leonidovich Kostin.

Cash

According to customer reviews, a loan at VTB Bank is one of the most popular and sought after products. Any individual can issue this loan at a rate of 7.9 percent per annum. You can receive up to five million rubles for a period of up to seven years. It is noteworthy that the preliminary decision on the loan is made in the first quarter of an hour after filling out the application and providing all the necessary documents.

Under basic conditions, the interest rate with insurance, with a loan amount of one hundred to five hundred thousand rubles, will be 11.7% per annum, with an amount of five hundred thousand to five million rubles - 10.9% per annum. With the refusal of life, health and temporary disability insurance, the rate rises from 13 to 19.9 percent per year.

According to customer reviews, VTB Bank in Moscow now offers favorable options for loans on how to reduce interest rates to 7.9 percent per annum. To do this, you need to issue a special "Multicard", on which there is an interest-free period of 101 days. Then connect the option "Borrower" to it (this can be done by phone or in VTB-online). After that, you have to pay with this card and save on payments on loans.

In this case, your loan discount will depend on the amount of purchases, due to credit funds made on your card during the month. With an amount of five to fifteen thousand rubles, the discount will be 0.5%, with an amount of 15 to 75 thousand rubles - 1%, with an amount of 75 thousand rubles - 3%. Moreover, in the month of registration of the "Multicard" itself, you will immediately receive the maximum discount on the loan. Regardless of how much you made purchases on your card.

It is worth emphasizing that at the same time, the schedule of payments on the loan does not change, and after you pay by card, the payment and rate will be recalculated every month. If you have several loans in this bank, the rate will be reduced for the one for which there is a large debt. The difference compared to the payment schedule will be returned to your card before the end of next month, the maximum amount that can be expected is 15 thousand rubles.

The requirements for the borrower that the bank makes are the following: citizenship of the Russian Federation, official average monthly income in the amount of 15 thousand rubles per month, permanent registration in the region where the bank is present. Please note that you can get a loan in any region. The address of registration and your actual residence may not coincide.

In customer reviews of cash loans at VTB Bank, it was noted that obtaining a loan approval is not as difficult as it may seem to some for the first time. In the presence of necessary documents, as well as the absence of arrears to other financial organizations, a loan is drawn up without any problems.

Repayment conditions

Repayment of a loan at VTB Bank is carried out in equal monthly installments, they are also called annuity. If you wish, you can repay the loan ahead of schedule, and do it without a commission. The loan can be repaid in whole or in part. To do this, you will need to apply to VTB-Online with the appropriate application, choose the loan repayment terms - you either reduce the amount of the monthly payment, or the term. After this, it remains only to deposit the amount of the advance payment into your account.

There is also the opportunity to postpone or reduce the payment. The bank provides a service called "Credit Vacations." It is available when applying for a cash loan. In this case, you can skip any one planned payment every six months. You can use this service no earlier than six months after applying for a loan and no later than three months before the loan repayment ends. Your missed payment will be shifted to the next period, respectively, the loan repayment period will be extended by one month. Every user has the opportunity to connect this service for free. In customer reviews of cash loans at VTB Bank, it was noted that this is a convenient option, which is currently rarely seen anywhere.

There is also a feature "Preferential payment". It allows you to reduce the size of the first few payments (up to three) so that they consist only of the interest on the loan without repaying the principal amount. This service is also provided free of charge when applying for a loan.

Payment Methods

In the reviews of loans at VTB 24, the majority emphasizes that there are a large number of ways to repay the loan, so in this regard, problems almost never arise

One of the most convenient and common options is the design of the corresponding card. It will be issued upon conclusion of a cash loan agreement. After that, an account in your name may be opened at the bank.

If you already have a VTB bank card, you can repay the loan by depositing the required amount in cash at any ATM that has a function of receiving funds. If you need to replenish your account when paying off debt, this can also be done using an ATM.

For remote control of payment repayment, connect the VTB-Online service. It will be possible to do this even without visiting the department - in the mobile application or on the site. In VTB-Online you can, for example, set up auto payment. The service will allow you to transfer the necessary amount of funds to the account without your participation strictly according to the schedule.

You can also pay off monthly loan payments by making a transfer from card to card. In this case, you should consider that money can go up to three days, so do it in advance.

Among other ways of repaying loans are transfers from an account with another bank, at Russian Post branches, at payment terminals, through Beeline, Zolotaya Korona, Kari and many other service points. For example, you can put money on a loan in MTS or Euroset stores. In this case, you will have to pay a commission, remember that the term for crediting money to the account can be several days.

Refinancing

In their reviews of refinancing at VTB Bank, customers emphasize that this financial institution offers quite favorable conditions.

Moreover, the total loan amount should be from 100 thousand to five million rubles. For most, the loan term will be up to five years, and for corporate clients it can be extended up to seven years. Execute it without guarantors and collateral. Within the framework of refinancing, it is possible to combine up to six credit cards and loans into one common loan, even if they were issued in different credit organizations. After that, you will have a chance to get an additional loan for any purpose. There is an option of gratuitous transfer of funds to third-party credit organizations in order to pay off current and accumulated debts.

To apply for a loan, the borrower is subject to standard requirements. Loans that can be combined and refinanced should be in rubles. They can be either collateral or unsecured. For example, consumer loans, credit card debt, mortgages.

Before the expiration of the loan or credit card must be at least three months. You will have to pay off the monthly debt monthly in rubles. Also, in order to get approval for refinancing, you should have no loan arrears in the last six months, and also not have current arrears.

The organization does not refinance its loans. These are their conditions. This causes negative feedback on refinancing loans at VTB 24, as most other financial institutions provide this service.

Car loan

You can arrange several types of loans for a vehicle at VTB Bank at once. This can be a loan for a new car, motorcycle, used car from a dealer or individual.

Among the advantages of this product in reviews of customers about a car loan at VTB Bank, they note state subsidies for new cars, attractive lending rates, special programs with manufacturers, the possibility of acquiring a used car from an individual.

For example, by the end of this year, the specialty offer for new Super 5 cars is in force. You can borrow up to five million rubles with a down payment of 20 percent. To issue a loan under two documents, and for the first six months to receive a preferential rate of five percent.

Under the Freedom of Choice program, bank customers have the opportunity to purchase a used car from any individual. Down payment is not required. The loan rate will be from 9.9 percent per annum. The maximum amount is three million rubles, while the bank itself will conduct a comprehensive check of the seller and the car, it is possible to draw up a contract without hull insurance.

On favorable terms, you can become the owner of the domestic car "Lada". In the case of the acquisition of this brand, you will be asked to arrange a loan at a rate of twelve and a half percent, with an initial payment of ten percent. At the same time, you can get a car without concluding a CASCO agreement with the insurance company.

With the "Auto Privilege" program, an increased loan amount is available, as well as extended repayment periods. You can count on up to seven million rubles with an initial payment of twenty percent. The rate will be from 9.1% per annum.

When applying for a loan for motor vehicles, you can purchase any motor vehicles with a rate of 15.9%, and you can get a loan without hull insurance. In the case of purchasing a used car from an authorized dealer, a loan is issued for up to five years at a rate of 8.9% per annum.

Business Loans

Naturally, the bank works not only with private individuals, but also with business. There are separate advantageous offers for small, medium and large businesses.

According to VTB Bank customers, there are various loan programs for businesses. This can be revolving or investment loans, express loans, loans secured by acquired property, refinancing and leasing.

For example, in the case of express lending, you can issue up to five hundred thousand rubles for five years without collateral. With revolving lending, there is a service to cover the cash gap, that is, overdraft. In this case, you can get up to 150 million rubles for two years without collateral.

Lending for participation in an electronic auction involves an amount of up to 35 million rubles without collateral for a period of up to one year, which will be required to secure an application for participation in an electronic auction. Finally, to replenish working capital, it is possible to receive up to 150 million rubles for three years. In this case, you will definitely need a deposit.

Within the framework of investment lending programs, a loan can be issued on collateral (for its acquisition), on the security of acquired real estate, for example, the purchase of a warehouse, office, retail space or the development of your business. According to these proposals, entrepreneurs can receive up to 150 million rubles for a period of up to ten years.
