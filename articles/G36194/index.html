Beeline. Money service: card, mobile money and money transfers. How to activate the service and issue a Beeline.Money card?

Today there are a large number of different payment systems and currency operators, with which you can send money anywhere in the shortest possible time. This made financial turnover as free as possible.

If we talk about a mobile account, then replenishing it, as a rule, is not difficult - any payment terminal, bank or online currency website allows you to do this. The difficulty lies precisely in another - to withdraw money from a mobile account. Especially translate them into a freely convertible form (after all, actually transferring funds to another subscriber is quite easy).





One of the largest mobile operators in Russia has launched the Beeline.Money service of the same name. With its help, the user has a unique opportunity to pay bills, withdraw money from his account and transfer funds to other network participants as simple as possible! We will talk about how the system functions, as well as its advantages and disadvantages in this article.

general description

As you can guess from the very name of the option in question, the main purpose of the Beeline.Money service is money transfers. The operator made them possible in different directions, thanks to which the subscriber actually got access to the funds placed on his mobile account. Other operators did not do this before, so the service is really unique in its kind, and even somewhat innovative.

Thanks to her, your phone bill ceases to be "closed": the Beeline.Money service makes it a bit of a wallet that can be used if necessary. In the article we will describe in more detail on what conditions this can be done, and how funds can be exchanged and withdrawed.





Opportunities

The service makes it possible to transfer from a mobile account (Beeline) and to it using the Unistream payment service, as well as through a bank card in ATMs connected to the Beeline.Money service. In this case, a transfer to a card can be carried out without further withdrawing funds in cash. This is what the client does at his discretion.

As indicated on the mobile operator‚Äôs website, in this way, the subscriber can, for example, make transfers to his relatives or receive payment for the work done. In fact, Beeline mobile money can be equated with other electronic payment systems, because each subscriber is able to deposit and withdraw funds from his account.

Limitations





True, as in the case with any other payment system, Beeline has some limitations that you need to know about. Firstly, this is the amount that can be withdrawn using ATMs. A single payment cannot be more than 5 thousand rubles, while in a month the subscriber is allowed to withdraw from the system no more than 40 thousand. Even when withdrawing money through an ATM, it is necessary that 50 rubles or more remain on the mobile account.

Secondly, conditions are also imposed on which account the card should be. Beeline.Money makes it possible to withdraw funds from no more than 5 bank cards, provided that they were previously authorized and tied to the subscriber's account (we will discuss this later). Thus, making bulk payments will not work. Obviously, using such a mechanism as a method of conducting calculations is possible only in rare, emergency situations.

Thirdly, a restriction is also made on the number of subscribers who can transfer funds to one card (or account). So, through Beeline Money, money transfers can be carried out no more than from two cards within one month.

Service cost





As already noted, Beeline charges a fee for conducting operations to withdraw funds from the account. This is a normal procedure that all payment systems resort to. Its size is determined by where the transfer is sent. If the user selects a bank card as a means of payment, Beeline.Money charges 50 rubles, provided that the payment amount is between 50 and 1000. If the person is going to withdraw more than 1 thousand, but less than 14 thousand rubles, the commission will be equal 5.95% and 10 rubles per transaction.

For withdrawing money through the Unistream system, the conditions are more loyal. So, to receive funds in this way, it is enough to pay a fee of 2.99% of the total payment.

How to activate the service?

The advantage of the function is that it does not need to be connected purposefully. It is connected by default for all subscribers.
