Arbat

Among the hotels on the Arbat there is an institution with the same name. The hotel was opened in 1960. Later, it became part of the President Hotel complex. The Arbat Hotel is located in the very center of Moscow, in one of the quiet side streets of Stary Arbat. Accommodation here is convenient for those guests who want to enjoy all the charms of the capital and get to know better Russian culture. Not far from the institution are ancient buildings and government offices. Just a few steps away is the Foreign Ministry. In five minutes you can reach the metro stations Smolenskaya and Arbatskaya. In the immediate vicinity of the Arbat Hotel there are entertainment centers and restaurants, souvenir shops and cafes. Also nearby is the Smolensky Passage and museums - Herzen, Scriabin, Bely, Lunacharsky, Aksakovs. Very close is New and Old Arbat, the embankment of the Moscow River.

The advantage of the hotel is that it has its own courtyard with a garden. All guests receive breakfast, which is included in the price. All rooms are equipped with a working area, which may be needed for those guests who came to the capital to work. Luxury apartments have their own living area.

A daily breakfast is served in one of the restaurant's rooms. For events and business meetings, the hotel has a business center offering a wide range of services. High-quality internet is available throughout the hotel.

According to tourists, this hotel on Old Arbat in Moscow is worthy of close attention. It has a lot of advantages. Guests are impressed by the hotel's courtyard with a picturesque nineteenth-century Arbat style terrace. Instead of an afterword

As you can see, the choice of hotels at different levels in the city center is great. Here you can find institutions for every taste - from the most pathos and expensive to more democratic. Which institution to prefer, a matter of taste and needs. However, according to tourists, absolutely all hotels in the capital are always crowded. Therefore, it is better to book a room in advance. Relying on a happy occasion is not worth it.

When choosing a hotel, pay attention to its location and reviews. If you want to relax in silence, choose an institution located in quiet streets and courtyards. Do not rely on calm in the bustling central streets.

The choice of hotel should depend on your plans. An important factor is convenient transportation and the proximity of metro stations. Making transfers with bags in a stream of people is not very convenient. If you plan to see the sights of the center of the capital, these hotels will suit you. It is very important that the hotel is nearby. This will allow you not to waste time on the road and traffic jams. After all, you came to relax, and not spend time in transport.

In our article, we considered only a few hotels and apart-hotels. In fact, there are much more of them in the center of Moscow. Therefore, you can choose the right institution for every taste and budget.

If you plan to travel by car, check the availability of the entrance to the hotel and parking. Not all institutions can boast of the presence of parking and free spaces on it. This moment needs to be clarified in advance at the reception at the booking stage, since it is quite problematic to find a parking lot in the center in the future. In addition, the cost of parking is an additional cost for you.

The nuances exciting you need to be specified in advance. If you are not satisfied with one hotel for certain parameters, you can always find a more convenient option. A larger selection of hotels than in Moscow is probably difficult to find in any city in Russia. After all, the number of tourists and guests from other countries is steadily increasing every year, which means that new establishments regularly open.
