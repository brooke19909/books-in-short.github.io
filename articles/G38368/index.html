Embroidery kits Dimensions: description, reviews

For more than 30 years, the number one company in the world among manufacturers of products for creativity has been Dimenshins (Dimensions). The embroidery kits of this company rightfully deserve high confidence from customers. Dimeninshins is an American company, but China recently launched production of the same products under this name.

In the assortment of the company there are over a thousand types of products for embroidery with cross, stitch, tapestry. A wide selection of patterns and motifs, among which there are landscapes, still lifes, portraits, brought the company to the leaders in this industry.



Benefits of a Famous Company

Cross-stitch or Dimension embroidery kits are recognized worldwide. There are many significant advantages of this company over other manufacturers of embroidery kits:

  • High quality correlates well with reasonable prices (500-1000 p. For simple schemes and medium complexity).
  • Schemes, instructions are worked out perfectly, the threads are selected at the highest level.
  • The company carefully monitors, explores trends and tastes of customers, pays attention to the color palette, motifs for embroidery.
  • Unusual drawings, solid material, highlighting the most striking details.
  • Embroidery kits Dimensions are produced taking into account different ages, capabilities, skill levels.
  • Huge selection in different styles and themes.




Company Offers

Under several brands, Dimensions embroidery kits are available. Customer reviews indicate the popularity of the company's regular collection. For little needlewomen, children's kits are developed. Fans of embroidery paintings are invited to purchase a copy from the collection in glass frames (Daudreams).

Cross Stitch Kits Dimensions (Gold Collection) are especially impressive. A special brand is prepared for embroidery of pillowcases, tablecloths, aprons. The company also offers sets of felt, vintage products, coloring by numbers.

Main directions

What techniques offer needlewomen kits for embroidery Dimensios? For those who prefer to look at the diagram and independently calculate the crosses on the canvas, the Counted Cross sets are offered . These include canvas, cotton threads and pattern.

Needleworkers who prefer to embroider without canvas, immediately on the canvas, can purchase a set with a printed cross. The fabric in such collections is immediately indicated by the places for embroidery with the help of crosses. This collection is suitable for decorating children's blankets, pillows, ornaments.

Especially popular are tapestry collections. The necessary drawing is already immediately applied to the canvas, which is embroidered with threads of cotton, wool or acrylic.

A very effective stitch embroidery technique is also presented in the Dimenshins collection. For this work, a fabric sealed with silk is used. Most often, napkins, blouses, pillowcases are embroidered with satin stitch.

The company offers impressive embroidery kits in printed technology. A special tool is used for this, and the reverse side of the work is the front.

Felting embroidery lovers are offered sets for felt products. Most often, handbags, accessories, home decorations are decorated in a similar way. For those who do not want to bother with hard work for a long time, you can purchase sets of finished felt products .





Embroidery kits Dimensions (Gold Collection)

Design sets "Dimenshins" processed by artists from the initial drawing in a computer program. Designers then draw additional stitches and details where necessary. Skilled embroiderers experience the pattern, after which an improved image is prepared for release.

In the American complete set of sets, threads are assembled into knots, in Chinese collections they are simply arranged by numbers and distributed on cardboard organizers. You cannot buy threads of this company separately. Each set contains instructions for use.

There are a lot of beautiful and stylish embroidery designs in the Dimenschins gold collection. Here are some motives for creating a miracle on the canvas:

  • "Newborn kittens";
  • "Graceful Age";
  • "In harmony";
  • "Lady Chateau";
  • "Winter experience."

Stylish embroideries on the canvas always look fashionable and original. The collection contains many motifs on a Christian theme .





Design Dimenschins and its features

Dimensions embroidery kits are most often made with a cross and a half cross. But for special expressiveness of the paintings, they use a lot of additional stitches. The main feature of Dimenchins is the use of a backstitch (a special stitch made with a ‚Äúneedle back‚Äù).

Combination of threads is an important technique for company kits. The use of different amounts of yarn in the needle gives the picture volume and depth. Most often, the work is done in two stitches, but one stitch is used for extra stitches. Half crosses in "Dimenshins" are sewn in one direction. The instructions for the sets show where to start embroidering and how to sew the main stitches. In addition to the above embroidery techniques, French knots, eyelets, and coaching are also used.

First, needlewomen embroider crosses and half-crosses, and then additional stitches. Any interior with embroidered Dimensions products will become brighter and more expressive .
