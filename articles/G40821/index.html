How to get there

The hotel is located in the emirate of Ajman, on the shores of the Persian Gulf. It is about 20 kilometers from Sharjah. Approximately the same distance from the city of Dubai.

Ajman (Kempinski Hotel) can only be reached by taxi. After all, there is no bus service between this city and other areas of the country. The cheapest thing is to arrange the passage of four people at once.

You can still get on the tourist bus that goes to one of the Ajman hotels. But this does not always work. After all, such buses are most often filled to capacity. But most often, hotel guests book a transfer.

Territory and infrastructure

The former Kempinski hotel - 5 * Ajman Hotel - is located in a very picturesque place. Therefore, the architects tried to make his building as harmoniously as possible fit into the landscape.

It is located in the middle of a beautiful landscape park, resembling an oasis in the desert. All in palm trees, greenery and flowers. On the ground floor of the hotel building is a shopping arcade with a variety of shops. There is also a business center, a conference room and a room for weddings and banquets.

The area around the building is huge and very green. Many lawns, bridges, there is where to walk, especially with children.

Number of rooms

The Kempinski Hotel Ajman Resort is a fairly large hotel complex. It has 185 rooms. These are luxurious guest rooms. The main room categories are Superior and Suite. All of them are very spacious, bright and decorated in the style of Middle Eastern gloss.

The rooms have furnished balconies or terraces with views of the sea and landscaped garden. And of course, the higher the floor, the more beautiful the landscape from the rooms opens. All rooms have a kettle and capsule coffee machine. Sachets are replenished daily, as is drinking water.

There are slippers, bathrobes, and very good quality toiletries. Comfortable beds and great linens. There is a small refrigerator where you can store food.

They clean it twice a day, so that everything shines, they prepare the room for bedtime, and the curtains are closed. If you need an ironing board - they will bring it.

Service and entertainment

Of course, the reception is open 24 hours a day to check in and check in guests. And from the airport and back they are delivered in a limousine or BMW.

Kempinski Hotel Ajman is famous for its spa treatments. Therefore, tourists leave delighted reviews about the services of local massage therapists. You can order this type of wellness service at two spa centers or right on the beach. There you will be offered to take a steam bath in the hammam or relax in the jacuzzi. Spas are open until eleven in the evening.

There is a car rental desk at the hotel. There are many opportunities for hotel guests to go in for sports - there is a tennis court, a gym. In the morning you will be called for exercise, and in the afternoon - for aerobics. In the evenings, guests play billiards, bowling. There is even a night club. After dinner, a professional pianist plays in the lobby bar.

Very happy family with children. Kids are safe and not bored. The playground is gorgeous. The staff is very friendly. Workers are especially good with children.

There are Russian-speaking employees who will explain everything and show you, including where it is much more profitable to go shopping. The hotel bus carries free to the Dubai Mall in the evenings.

Sea, beach and pools

Just a hundred meters from the building of the Ajman Hotel (UAE / Ajman) is its own 400-meter sandy beach. There are many types of various water activities.

The distance between the sunbeds is very large, so that you do not have the impression that you are sunbathing in a crowd. Great sense of privacy.

The beach is very beautiful. The entrance to the sea is smooth, the sand is white. Every morning it is cleaned and thoroughly cleaned. You can take as many beach towels as you like, but change them at any time.

But it should be borne in mind that this is an open sea with waves, and not a lagoon. Therefore, there may be storms. And in winter - strong ebbs.

The huge outdoor pool is surrounded by palm trees, creating a dense shadow. Around him are sun loungers and deck chairs. There is a special pool for children. When you sunbathe near an artificial pond, you can hear the birds singing.

There are always free sunbeds - both at sea and by the pool. In winter, the latter, by the way, is heated. Therefore, if the weather is windy, most vacationers go there.

Along the beach line you can walk for a long time, go jogging. Each umbrella has a button to call the waiter, which will bring you an order.
