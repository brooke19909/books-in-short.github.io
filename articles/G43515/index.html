Location

From Denpasar International Airport to Hotel Nusa Dua Beach Hotel & Spa take only half an hour. And even if you book a collective transfer, because the bus with tourists first calls in Nusa Dua, and then leaves for Ubud, Jimbaran and Kuta. The resort itself is located in the south of Bali. The hotel we are describing is located almost in the center of Nusa Dua, surrounded by the same imposing and expensive "fives".

Without exception, tourists mention in the reviews the fact that you can walk through all the hotels of the resort without any obstacles. Want to lie on a lounger of someone else's beach? Yes please! Did you find evening entertainment or daytime animation at your hotel boring or just boring? Welcome to the disco in the neighboring hotel! Territory

Hotel Nusa Dua Beach Hotel & Spa is built among a lush tropical garden. The entrance is guarded by stone majestic gates. Passing through them, you will admire the view of the building, stylized as a palace. Its architecture traces the motives of the Middle East, and this is not surprising: they say that the hotel belongs to the Sultan of Brunei itself. From the gate, the path leads to the building along the ponds with blooming lotuses. They come down in cascades and are decorated with fountains in Balinese traditional motifs.

Many tourists mention the truly luxurious lobby, more like a throne room in the royal palace. Everywhere on the walls genuine objects of painting hang, figurines are visible, and the furniture is most likely antique. By the way, works of art made by local masters (batik, painting, sculptures) are also present in the rooms, and at any price category. This, according to tourists, gives an unforgettable experience of immersion in the national atmosphere of Bali. The hotel area is quite large. It fit 15 restaurants, bars and cafes, a spa, tennis court, a spacious swimming pool.

Room Categories

Monarchs and presidents of powers lived here in suites and villas, which will be discussed later. In the meantime, let's review the numbers for mere mortals. The premier room is almost twice as large as the superior, despite the fact that it is designed for two guests. We stopped at the hotel with the whole family? The Beach Hotel Nusa Dua has a family room for three and four people. These family rooms also feature beds for children.

If you do not want your children to sleep in a bunk bed, order the deluxe room for 3 (or 4) person categories. There are very spacious bedrooms with normal beds. As for filling the rooms, there is everything that is expected of the "five": air conditioning, TV, safe, minibar, hairdryer, bath and shower, bathrobes with slippers. Suites

Residents of higher categories of rooms in Nusa Dua Beach Hotel & Spa 5 * have certain privileges in comparison with other guests. But the apartments themselves are incomparably better. Club Palace room is located on the first floor of the building with direct access to the pool. A room with the same name as Club Palace Suite consists of a bedroom and a living room. The windows of the Agung Suite overlook the mountain of the same name. This chic room is located on the 4th floor and consists of two bedrooms and a living room. Nusa Dua Suite - two-tier. It also has two bedrooms and a living room, but it features a private pool and spacious verandas.

Royal Residence Villa is a two-story house with a private spacious pool, surrounded by its own garden. He stands right by the beach. If we compare the suites with regular numbers, it seems that their content is identical. The same air conditioners, televisions, etc. However, the whole point is in the details, isn't it? Luxurious furniture, premium bedding, precious trinkets decorating the rooms - all this will make your stay unforgettable.
