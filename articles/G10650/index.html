How can I make men's bracelets with my own hands?

Jewelry is always associated with feminine paraphernalia. However, this is not at all true. There are original and practical gizmos that will be enjoyed by representatives of the stronger sex. In addition, it is very easy to make, for example, men's bracelets with their own hands, they will become a wonderful gift for a brother, friend, beloved guy. Jewelry emphasize brutality, masculinity of the owner. Such materials as cords, ropes, leather are suitable for their creation. Let us consider in more detail several options, the most simple and interesting.

Watch cord bracelet



The thing intended for the man should be laconic, elegant and practical. Adhering to these principles, you can make a bracelet with your own hands, the male version of the jewelry is a beautiful watchband. Before starting work, you need to measure your wrist, measure the required length of the rope and secure it in the clasp-carabiner. After that, start weaving. To do this, you need to take the rope lying on the right, bring it under the base, then bring it up and skip it into the loop formed. Repeat all actions with the cord, which is located on the left. The result is a pattern of neat loops. At the end of the work, you need to cut the rope and process its ends with a lighter so that it does not crumble. Such men's bracelets, created with their own hands, will become a lifesaver in a campaign, and the owner of such a thing will be happy to wear it on his hand.


Shambhala bracelet

Shambhala woven bracelets are very popular. They are equally suitable for both women and men. They are created very easily, excitingly and simply. Men's bracelets, woven from waxed or leather cord with their own hands, will look very original. As decorative elements that we will braid, you can use anything. For example, metal beads, semiprecious stones, or even small parts, such as nuts. All these items are braided with a cord using a square macrame knot. These bracelets will organically look on your hand in combination with leather jewelry.





Leather bracelet

If you are considering how to make a men's bracelet so that your friend will like it, then pick up pieces of leather in your hands. This is the most suitable material for creating jewelry. For the manufacture of gizmos, strips of leather in black or brown are required. The easiest option is to flash them with threads, decorate with beads made of metal or wood. The clasp can be braided from threads using a flat macrame knot. The result is a model reminiscent of Shambhala. If your friend is a biker, then he will like other men's bracelets more, with your own hands you can create such a jewelry that more emphasizes the masculinity of the owner. To do this, you need to carve a wide blank of black or dark brown leather. Rivets or lacing are suitable as a fastener. Spikes will be appropriate, they are easy to attach with glue. Adhering to the tastes and preferences of the person to whom the gift is intended, on the bracelet you can depict a wolf, a dragon or Celtic runes. It all depends on the chosen model and your rich imagination.
