An unusually beautiful island is located in the Gulf of Thailand. They call him Phu Quoc. Map of Vietnam shows that it is the south of the country. The population of the island is more than one hundred thousand people. The nearest town to the island - Hatien - is located on the border with Cambodia.

Phu Quoc is located three degrees south of the Thai resort of Pattaya, and two degrees north of Phuket. The island has a somewhat elongated shape. Its length is 62 km, its width is from 3 to 28 km.





How to get to the island

Vietnam and its resorts are still not very popular with Russian tourists, although every year there are more and more people who want to spend their holidays here. The most famous among Russians visiting Vietnam is Phu Quoc. How to get to this exotic island from Russia? Simple enough, although the road will take a lot of time.

From Moscow, you will fly to Ho Chi Minh City by direct flight. Phu Quoc is fifty minutes of summer from this city.

Today, the tourism industry on the island began to develop rapidly. Naturally, passenger traffic increases to Fukuok every year. The airport (the only one on the island) can no longer cope with the loads. It was built in 1930, underwent some changes during the Vietnam War. At the beginning of operation, it had a 996-meter runway. After the liberation of Saigon (1975), the airport began to operate commercial flights. In 1983, the length of its runway was increased to 1496 meters.

The government has decided to build a new international airport. Its maximum throughput at the beginning of operation will be seven million passengers per year.

Many vacationers prefer to get to the island by sea. You can do this way from the cities of Rachza or Hatien. You will get to these cities from Saigon by bus or taxi in about eight hours. Then you will be offered to transfer to a boat, which will take you to the island in two hours.

We can say that the road to Phu Quoc (reviews of tourists indicate this) is quite tiring. This is especially true for those who decided to relax on the island with children.





Climate, weather

The island of Fukuok monsoon, subequatorial climate. Weather is determined by two factors. This is a dry season lasting almost the whole year (from November to September) and the rainy season (October) is unfavorable for recreation. You can relax here all year, but no doubt, it is better to exclude October. The rest of the months are very comfortable here.

All year the water temperature is between +28 and +30 degrees, and its average values ‚Äã‚Äãare +27 degrees. Naturally, these indicators may vary slightly in different years on the island of Phu Quoc. In August 2014, the air temperature ranged from +24 to +31 degrees, and the water temperature remained +28 degrees. As you can see, for the Russians, accustomed in recent years to the sweltering heat of the summer months, the weather is very comfortable on the island of Fukuok. Reviews of tourists say that the heat even at 30 degrees is almost not felt here.

There are several private clinics and a hospital on the island. If necessary, in each hotel you can call a doctor or take to his appointment. Many who have visited the island of Fukuok note a high level of medical care. In addition, they consider the cost of medical services very affordable. Beaches in Phu Quoc

This paradise has many attractions, but most of the tourists who visited Fukuok Island, their reviews (enthusiastic) are associated primarily with luxurious beaches. It is for the sake of relaxation that people from all over the world come here.

Fukuok is a clean, quiet and incredibly cozy and beautiful place. This is a very young, but rapidly developing resort. While there are no shopping malls, water parks, entertainment centers and zoos. Therefore, if you prefer an active holiday with a vibrant nightlife, this place is not for you.



Lovers of a relaxing holiday, privacy, boat trips and bright colors of pristine nature will feel very comfortable here.

However, even in the midst of the holiday season, there are not too many people here, which, of course, will appeal to lovers of a relaxing and secluded vacation.

Island Attractions: Pearl Farm

This company appeared in 1998. Today it is the most visited attraction in Fukuoka. Here you can watch for free how pearls grow and how they are processed. In the old years, these valuable pebbles were mined by professional divers. Today they are grown artificially in the waters of the Gulf of Thailand. Experts consider this place ideal for this procedure.



Natural pearls in these places are quite small, and do not always have an ideal shape. But the stones, grown artificially, are distinguished by their ideal shape, large size and beauty.

To obtain such specimens, particles cut out of shells of no more than four millimeters are placed in the body of an oyster. It takes from one and a half to two years to grow pearls with a size of 10-18 mm. It depends on the temperature of the water and some other conditions.

National park

This is a huge territory with an area of ‚Äã‚Äã31,400 hectares. Arriving here, you should first climb Mount Nuit. Its height is more than 500 meters. This is the highest point of the reserve. Most of the park is occupied by impenetrable rainforests. The temperature here is never lower than +25 degrees. High humidity at first may seem uncomfortable, but a little later this feeling disappears.

The most beautiful part of the park is in the north. Here, tourists fall into a real dense forest, without any trace of civilization. In the park you can see thousands of exotic plants that strike Europeans with their shapes, colors and subtle smells.

More than fifty species of mammals, including quite friendly primates, feel quite comfortable here. In addition, in the National Park you will have a unique opportunity to see a rare animal of a very touching kind - a sea cow (dugong).

You can come to the reserve by taxi. The fare is $ 5-10.



Kaodai Religion Temple

It was on the island of Phu Quoc that a new religion was born. The former governor of the island had a vision - the image of the Third Eye. This may surprise someone, but the most revered saints of this religion are Victor Hugo, Vladimir Lenin, Leo Tolstoy, Benito Mussolini, William Shakespeare. Today, this religion is preached by more than ten million Vietnamese. The basic dogma of this faith is the concept of the third salvation. According to the kaodaists, humanity has already been saved twice - the first time by Amitabha and Moses, the second time by Confucius and Jesus. Kaodoism preaches that this is the right path to the third salvation of mankind.

Tourists can get to the service in the temple, which is held not every day, but according to a special schedule. During it, the monks in snow-white clothes, with enchanting music, praise the huge eye. Then, the participants in the ceremony, accompanied by quiet and calm music, begin collective meditation.

The easiest way to arrive at the temple is by taxi. From anywhere in the island, the trip will cost you no more than $ 10.
