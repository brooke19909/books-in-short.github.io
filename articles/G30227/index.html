Hotel Marina Bay Sands in Singapore: description and reviews

Singapore is a mystery country to many. In just a few decades, it has turned from a backward colonial country into one of the most developed in the world, with the highest level of wealth. A lot of interesting and original stories, sometimes completely incomprehensible to us, tell about Singapore, its laws, state structure, life. Many tourists want to visit this country and see firsthand and see if all that is said about this state is true. Singapore (see photo in the article) is also attractive for its urban solutions. The metropolis, at first glance, resembles a city from the future. Once here, you feel like on another planet. There are many original and incomparable architectural structures. For example, the building of the Marina Bay Sands Hotel in Singapore is considered one of the masterpieces of world futuristic architecture. Of course, not everyone can afford to stay at this hotel, but nevertheless, the hotel never experiences a shortage of guests.



This mysterious Singapore ...

Singapore is a tiny state in Southeast Asia. It consists of many small islands located in the South China Sea, and is constantly lacking in area. The name of this policy-state comes from the Malay word “singa”, which means “lion”. In a word, Singapore is a southeastern “city of a lion” that could make a giant leap to a post-industrial level. Millions of tourists visit this country every year. Despite the fact that it is located in the middle of the sea, people most often come here, driven by curiosity, go for exotic, for new sensations. Indeed, in this country there is a lot of things, the description of which begins with the epithet “the most”, for example, “the largest zoo” or “the largest oceanarium”, etc. But the building of the Marina Bay Sands hotel in Singapore can be called one of the most original. For many tourists, acquaintance with a metropolis begins precisely from it, right from the borate of the aircraft during landing. The giant pool, towering above the city, leads many to the delight, but at the same time to bewilderment. The bottom view is also striking. The pool looks like a huge ark, resting on three huge columns. In a word, Singapore has a lot of interesting things that may surprise even the most sophisticated tourists.



How to get to Singapore

We want to make Muscovites happy, because Singapore Airlines flies from Singapore to the capital five times a week . The flight lasts about 10 hours. By the way, in the summer, the number of flights increases up to seven times a week. In addition, you can fly to Singapore by plane Emirates Airlines, that is, through Dubai (daily), Qatar Airways through the capital of Qatar, Doha (three times a week), Vietnam Airlines, China Eastern and Japan Airlines. For those who wish to purchase Marina Bay Sands tours (Singapore), you need to know that a visa is required to enter this country. However, in the case of a transit stay in the country of not more than 96 hours and the availability of air tickets to another state, you may not have a Singapore visa.

Hotel Marina Bay (Singapore): general description

So, you are going to the “lion city” in the middle of the South China Sea. Naturally, you want to stay in the most original, comfortable and high-rise hotel in the country. After all, it is very small, and therefore from any high-rise building a stunning view of the sea, islands and the beauty of the city itself opens. The five-star Marina Bay Sands hotel in Singapore is perhaps the most interesting not only in this country, but throughout the world. In addition, it is the most luxurious and expensive. It was built in 2010. The level of service at the hotel exceeds all expectations. As already noted, the hotel consists of three 55-story buildings. They are united by a common roof in the form of a giant ship. There is an extensive entertainment center-park with a wonderful pool at an altitude of 190 meters, a magnificent spa center, an entertainment complex, theaters, cinema and even a museum of modern art. About 20 restaurants are scattered throughout the hotel, there are bars, cafes, pizzerias, as well as the largest casino in the world. Inside the hotel there are canals with bridges through which gondolas float . This makes the hotel look like little Venice.



Location

Hotel Marina (Singapore) is located right on the bay, three kilometers from the business center of the country, Shenton Way. Changi Airport is only a 20-minute drive away. Directly next to the hotel are the Byfront metro station and the giant Singapore shopping center, which can be reached without even leaving the hotel.

Number of rooms

Marina Bay Hotel has more than 2500 luxurious rooms that are furnished with magnificent furniture sets and equipped with the latest household appliances and audio and video equipment. The hotel has rooms of the following categories: “Deluxe” (with an area of ​​39 sq. Meters and overlooking the sea), “Premier” (47 sq. Meters, with a view of the city), “Club room” (with an area of ​​62 sq. M. overlooking the business center), “Grand Club room” (79 sq. meters, with a city view), “Orchid Suite” (almost 100 sq. meters and overlooking residential buildings and the business center), “Bay Suites” (138 sq. meters), “Singapore suites” (area 142 sq. m), “Sands suite” (spacious suite with an area of ​​150 sq. m with a view of the urban areas), “Marina suites” (with an area of ​​200 meters in a square), Istana suite (213 sq. m), Straits suite (330 sq. m), “Mer online suites ”(387 sq. m.),“ Presidential suites ”(occupying an area of ​​529 sq. meters and looking at the gardens and city districts), Chairman suite (just giant rooms with an area of ​​629 sq. meters and with a panoramic view gardens and the city). By the way, the hotel has several rooms for the disabled. As you can see, such luxurious and spacious apartments are rarely seen even in a very prestigious five-star hotel in another country in the world. The minimum price for a daily stay at the hotel is 11-12 thousand rubles and can reach 100,000. In short, if you are looking for a budget vacation, then you better not go to Marina Bay Sands (Singapore). The prices here are very high, but the reception that the hotel guests receive is in all respects consistent with them. Among the thousands of reviews that can be found on various tourism forums, it is almost impossible to find a single negative. Everything is done here in order to please the guests.



Description of rooms

The furniture in all rooms is made of dark wood, the floors have carpets, there are no balconies in the rooms, but there are huge windows from floor to ceiling. All bathrooms are very spacious. They have showers, a jacuzzi, a full set of cosmetic and hygiene products. In addition to the Deluxe and Premier apartments, the remaining rooms have living rooms. In suites, vacationers are provided with a butler service. The presidential rooms have several bedrooms, an office, a gym, dressing rooms, a jacuzzi, a massage area, a piano, and an individual sauna. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, refrigerators, cable and satellite TV, flat-screen TVs, internet, safe, etc.



Nutrition

As already mentioned, at the Marina Bay Sands Hotel in Singapore there are not one or two, but more than 20 restaurants and other catering outlets. All of them are luxurious, with a wide range of dishes of cuisines of different nations. Some of the food and drinks you will not find anywhere else. After you arrive in Singapore, the Marina Bay Sands hotel will open its doors in front of you and appear in all its beauty as the best center for relaxation, entertainment and taste. Whatever dish you would like to try, you will find everything in one of the many restaurants of this luxury hotel. For example, at Cut American Steak House you can taste not only great steaks, but also appreciate a huge selection of expensive wines. But in the restaurant Sky on 57, which is located on the roof, you can spend a real romantic date. The hotel also has the Spanish restaurant Santi Santamaria, which serves popular Spanish dishes, such as paella, gazpacho, etc. And in the French restaurant Guy Savoy, a real French professional cook does wonders. His masterpieces can surprise even the most sophisticated gourmets. The same goes for the Japanese restaurants Waku Ghin and Hide Yamamoto, which are a real paradise for connoisseurs of culinary art. The hotel also has several Chinese restaurants. In the elite Italian cafe "B" you can enjoy Italian cuisine, while in Rasapura Masters you can taste the culinary delights of several cuisines at once: Singaporean, Filipino, Pacific and American. The hotel also houses the Marina Bay Sands Club Lounge with great rooftop city views. Breakfast is usually served here in the morning, and in the evening light dinners and wines. From here you can see all of Singapore. Hotel Marina Bay Sands, since it is one of the highest buildings in the city, provides guests with the opportunity to view the entire city, sea distances, gardens and parks. In the Sweet Spot cafe, which is located in the atrium of the hotel, you can enjoy coffee, tea, hot chocolate, and appreciate a huge assortment of desserts. The same is offered by Coffee Bean & Tea Leaf Beanstro, Java Detour, etc.



Infrastructure and Services

The hotel has parking, dry cleaning, doctor's services, concierge service, laundry service, many shops, direct access to a shopping center, currency exchange, a huge casino, etc. By the way, gambling is prohibited in Singapore, but the Marina Bay Sands hotel - A branch of an American corporation. It can not be visited by citizens of Iran, Cuba, North Korea, Syria and Sudan.

Sports and Entertainment

For sports enthusiasts, the hotel has a state-of-the-art gym, fitness center, Banyan Tree SPA center, a jacuzzi, beauty and massage rooms, a sauna, etc. This hotel is a great holiday island for visitors of all ages. Everyone here will find entertainment according to their interests and taste. In Marina Bay there are many interesting activities for children. You certainly won't be bored here.

Business services

Several times a year, this hotel hosts various large-scale events, conferences, forums, symposiums, etc. Over 250 conference rooms equipped with the latest audio and video equipment, as well as a business center, are equipped here.

Sky Park

As you already know, the Sky Park entertainment complex with a giant pool is located on the roof of the hotel. It consists of landscaped gardens, restaurants and cafes. The aforementioned spa is also located in the Sky Park.

Pool

Singapore hotels (photos of some of them can be seen in many tourist tourist brochures) basically have a superbly developed infrastructure and provide tourists with the highest quality service, however, what the Marina Hotel offers exceeds all expectations. What is the open-air swimming pool, 150 meters long. If you come to Singapore, the Marina Hotel at first glance will surprise you with its gigantic size. By the way, many tourists make their first impression of Singapore as an extraordinary country, when they notice a giant swimming pool located at an altitude of 57 floors.

Yes, and where can they be when the hotel staff, as soon as tourists are in the territory, begins to literally blow dust from them. After all, here they see real oriental hospitality.
