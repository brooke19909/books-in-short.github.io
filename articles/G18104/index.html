They excitedly ask Google: "How much to fly to Thailand?". The question is purely practical, but some hint of concern is guessed in it. Of course, those who asked him carefully looked at the map.

Holidays in Phuket

The unique resort potential and developed tourist infrastructure has the island of Phuket. Most tourists fly to the Land of Smiles solely because of the rest on its beaches. Their names - Patong (which bears the same name as the entire resort), Kata, Karon, Kata-Noi, Naihan - like gentle music, caress the ears of holiday-makers from different countries. Moreover, the "star" of the hotel for them does not play a decisive role. Most young tourists buy a tour to an inexpensive hotel complex, investing their money in excursions.

Climate

The tropical monsoon climate of the island determines the presence of dry (from November to April) and wet (from May to October) seasons. At the same time, the waters of the Andaman Sea are suitable for swimming year-round, and the air temperature is approximately the same throughout the year. In the hottest month - April - it does not rise above 28.5 ¬∞ C, and in the coldest - October - its value is 26.8 ¬∞ C.

In the dry, or high, season, the resort island is visited by a maximum of tourists. January is its peak: the sky is amazingly clear, the weather is beautiful, and prices are doubled. Hotels in Patong, and the Days Inn Patong Beach 3 among them, are filled almost to the eyeballs. Tours for this time should be bought in advance - for 3 months.

The rainy season in Phuket also has its ‚Äúpluses‚Äù: quite substantial discounts on the purchase of tours and lower prices for fruits. Moreover, the rains in Thailand are not so long, they usually go at night. Their duration is generally small - only 40-60 minutes. At the same time, the air temperature is in the range of 20-27 ¬∞ C, and the sea remains fabulously warm. Many people in temperate latitudes prefer to relax in Tae during the rainy season.

A feature of Patong resort is another climatic factor, which, perhaps, is not found in neo-oceanic resorts. This is the height of the waves. In June - August in the open water area they reach a height dangerous for bathers. At this time, tourists prefer to slide on them on the board rather than swim.

Beach vacation

Tourists of temperate latitudes, resting under the tropical sun of Thailand, are forced to observe safety measures - no one is safe from getting sunburn. To do this, they should not save on high-quality tanning creams, which are better to buy in the resort of Patong. The level of protection for local creams reaches the required SFF of 70, while drugs purchased at home reach only 30 units, which does not protect against burns. At the same time, the cream acquired in Patong should be applied 3-4 times a day to the body. What do you want - the tropics ...
