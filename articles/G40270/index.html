USB Headphones: Model Overview

USB-headphone for a computer has a number of undeniable advantages over the usual models at 3.5 mm (mini-jack). The latter work in tandem with an internal (in the system unit / laptop case) sound card and are limited by its capabilities. While USB-headphones have their own / built-in functionality and are connected on the principle of Plug and Play. In this case, looking for drivers or some other software is completely optional.

Another important feature of USB-headphones is the presence of a built-in amplifier with a digital analog converter. That is, the sound here is transmitted in a "digital", and this is already a kind of independent speaker system. The advantages of this format are quite obvious - this is the sound quality with volumes, and advanced functionality that can be controlled directly from the headphones, and ease of use in general. In addition, some headphones with a mini-USB port can easily connect to mobile gadgets, which again throws a stone into the garden of the usual 3.5-millimeter devices.

Such devices are primarily interesting to gamers and those who spend a lot of time at the computer. Compact and wireless USB-headphones with a microphone allow, for example, to conduct video conferences, presentations and not sit still, but to move around the room, showing on the board some graphs with calculations. Well, it is very important for gamers that all the basic settings, including sound ones, are always at hand. And this is exactly what the gaming USB headphones provide .

Today's market offers a wide range of devices of such a plan, and if professionals still somehow orient themselves in all this diversity, then it is very difficult for beginners to choose the suitable option. We will try to identify the best models of USB-headphones, which are distinguished by their high-quality component and a large number of positive reviews from users.
