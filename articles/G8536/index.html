Crocheting slippers is very simple. Baby and adult models for beginners

Slippers are such a homely, cozy, warm thing. They are worn by all, without exception: children, and adults, and boys, and girls. The lineup of this shoe is quite diverse. Which do you prefer? What is the main characteristic of such shoes for you? I want to note that the most comfortable and warm, of course, are knitted patterns. Crocheting slippers is a snap. There are several options that even novice craftswomen can easily perform. We will talk about them a little later. And now about what you need to make home-made shoes.

Necessary materials

Crocheting slippers is quite simple. The first thing you need is, of course, a hook. Its size depends on the yarn used - the thicker it is, the larger the number. Now about the threads. The most suitable option for winter models is considered to be natural wool yarn, but not 100%. In order for the slippers to serve as long as possible, only 50% of the wool should be part of it, and the remaining 50% are synthetic threads. For the summer version of home shoes, any cotton threads with various additives are suitable, the main thing is that they are not too thin. You may also need various accessories for decorating shoes. It can be buttons, lace, shoelaces, satin ribbons, decorative artificial flowers, beads and so on. The design of slippers will depend only on your imagination.





Children's slippers are very popular. Crocheted patterns for babies can be gentle and sophisticated, warm and fluffy, bright and funny. Any option made by the hands of mother or grandmother will surely appeal to your baby, which means that he will wear them with pleasure.

Crochet slippers. For beginners - the easiest way

We start knitting slippers with air loops (5) connected in a ring. Next, we tie the resulting ringlet row by row with columns with a crochet (as in the figure) until we come to the place of formation of the slot for the leg.



Leave two columns uncovered and continue knitting in the opposite direction. Having reached the left unclosed elements, we turn back. So until the very end, until we reach the desired size. Now you can connect the two parts of the backdrop. This is done with a hook or with a needle. Slipper is ready! The second is performed similarly to the first. In this embodiment, there is no difference between the right and left. To give slippers a finished look, you can tie them around the edge with yarn of contrasting color, insert a satin ribbon or sew on beads. Beautifully located in the center of the jewelry - knitted bows, flowers or buttons.

Slippers connected by the above method, but using a different pattern, such as lace, look very unusual. Try different options. Surely you will find for yourself the easiest and fastest way to crochet slippers.





Slippers from the remains of yarn

Another model of home shoes is made from individual fragments made with a hook. These slippers turn out to be very bright, since all available yarn residues are used. Finished square-shaped elements are sewn together in a certain order, as a result of which your feet warm warm and original slippers. The size of such a model depends on the size of the fragments.





Crochet slippers - what could be easier? And, you must admit, such cute little things will always be a welcome and unique gift.
