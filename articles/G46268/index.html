Non-state pension funds: ranking of reliable companies

Today, more and more people are investing in private pension funds (NPFs). This allows you to form a decent retirement pension. In addition, each company offers its own conditions. The rating of private pension funds will make the right choice. It will be discussed in the article.

NPF is a form of non-profit organizations working in the field of social security (except for old-age pensions). This activity is regulated by law, so any violations lead to liability.

Features of the NPF

These organizations have almost no differences from state PFs. Institutions typically performs:

  • Payment of pensions.
  • Accounting for money.
  • Investing in shares and stocks, bonds.
  • The accumulation of capital.




Commercial organizations may be included in one of the following groups:

  • Open or universal do not apply to the financial industry. Investor funds are assets.
  • Territorial supported by local authorities, work only in a specific region.
  • Corporate operate on the basis of the programs of the founders.
  • Captive work with the programs of the founders.

The reliability rating of private pension funds is indicated below. It is formed by savings, by the number of customers. Reviews about these companies are almost exclusively positive.
