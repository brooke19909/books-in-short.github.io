"Pines on the Volga", a camp site (Samara). Reviews and photos of tourists

Summer is a time of rest. Especially during the hot season, people are drawn to the bosom of nature, to dense green forests, where there is always coolness, and on clean sandy beaches you can sunbathe.

Corner of paradise

All this can be taken care of by a complex located on an island in the area of Mastryukovskie Lakes. There are "Pines on the Volga" - a camp site. Samara is just 25 km from this tourist paradise.



Thousands of travelers annually visit the place. Nature here has some incomprehensible magical effect on the human body. Most often, vacationers come to the base precisely for unique landscapes. The islet stands on the large and beautiful Volga River. From its shores, an amazing view of the Zhiguli mountains opens .

There are no highways on this piece of land. The air on the territory is very clean and healing. The land is covered with sand, so there is no dirt or dust.

In general, the unique microclimate that has arisen in this territory contributes to the healing of the body.

Convenient crossing

The Sosenki on Volga complex (hostel) takes care of its customers. Samara is located only a few kilometers from the establishment, so everyone can get to the point by car.

Getting to the base is very easy. Not far from the island is the town of Kurumoch. From this point it is very convenient to get to Lake Mastryukovo. Under the shores there is a paid parking lot. It should be noted that the reservation agrees on the preliminary reservation of a place for your iron horse. Boats are constantly plying to the camp site. Customers also like the fact that the crossing fee is carried out at the expense of the complex. Another plus of such a trip is that you do not have to search for your base on your own. After landing, workers meet the vacationers and immediately lead to the houses.

Spartan conditions

The complex can offer three types of housing, they differ in prices and conditions. The cheapest option is summer houses. They can freely accommodate from two to five persons. These rooms have remained since Soviet times, so there is a minimum of comfort. Private rooms have cold water sinks. Toilet and shower are on site. You can order a house with a veranda, which will fit all the things necessary for travel. On the open porch there is a table where you can have a nice dinner in the fresh air. For a fee, the administration provides refrigerators for hire. Large companies rent houses in two rooms.





The price of such huts ranges from 800 to 3000 rubles per day. The amount depends on the number of guests and the distance to the coast. It should be remembered that "Pines on the Volga" - a camp site. Samara and the inhabitants of the region are accustomed to Spartan holidays here, but guests from other cities can be frightened by such conditions.

Comfort rooms

There are also more expensive cottages on the territory. There are groups of four and six people. The house has a shower and toilet. There is also a kitchen corner with kitchenware, a fridge and a microwave. There is a TV and a large table in the lobby. The bedrooms are isolated. You can live in cottages not only in summer but also in winter. The houses in the cold season include heating. Prices range from 5,000 to 7,000 rubles.

There is another option for housing in the complex "Pines on the Volga" (Samara). You can order a vacation and a townhouse. The room is designed for two guests. Each has a shower and toilet. In front of the house there is an open veranda, whose area is about 10 m. There is also a small entrance hall where you can leave your things. In the rooms there are: a table, a TV and a kitchen corner with everything necessary for preparing dinner. In the bedroom there is a large bed for two and a chest of drawers with a wardrobe. The cost of living in such a house is from 2200 rubles per day.

Additional Information

Almost every room is equipped with a small kitchen. In the courtyard are gazebos, tables and barbecues. Therefore, cooking yourself is very simple. For those who want to relax from everyday worries, there is a dining room. There is always a varied menu. Workers immediately offer several dishes. They cook hearty and tasty. If you want something else, you can visit the store.

Tries to please the client "Pines on the Volga" (camp site) in everything. Samara is quite far away, so delicacies and exotic should be stocked up in advance in the city.



It should be noted that persons over 14 years of age can only be settled if they present documents. Children under 12 years old will not have to pay. They can sleep with their parents. If the kids want their own bed, then for a fee, the administration can ask to put a cot. Those looking for discounts should contact the management in June and August for information.

Entertainment for every taste

A great vacation awaits everyone who visits this mysterious corner of the earth. Very quiet and calm "Pines on the Volga" (camp site, Samara). Photos of guests are the best proof that the weekend flies here fabulously. Children can swim all day in the clear waters, dads can fish, and mothers can sunbathe under the warm sun. The administration conducts a variety of excursions for an additional fee.

For those who are accustomed to rest actively, rent sports equipment. There is also a bathhouse on site. You can play billiards, volleyball, table tennis. During the lunch break and heat, children play on the playground. For adults, a bar and cafe are constantly open. An ice rink is open during the winter season.





You can rent a jet ski, catamaran or boat.

The main advantages of a tourist base

On the territory of the institution there are animators who constantly deal with children on a professional level. Also, on weekends, they organize interesting and fun discos where you can meet your neighbors and make friends.

Many travelers know where the "Pines on the Volga" (camp site) are located. Samara (reviews indicate this) is a city whose residents are usually satisfied with the service and the condition of their housing, but tourists from other areas are more moody. Visitors leave various comments. To explain the dissatisfaction of customers who rested here is very simple. This complex is primarily designed to help people connect with nature, improve their health in the fresh air and enjoy simple living conditions.

Travel Impressions

But many tourists note that summer houses have long been in need of tremendous repairs. Visitors do not like the state of bedding. Guests say that the kits are not only old, but also full of holes, poorly washed. Therefore, in order to avoid unpleasant surprises during the rest, it is better to take towels and bed with you.

Some say that it is not happy with a cozy camp site. "Pines on the Volga" is a rather old complex, therefore, accordingly, some houses have already served their time. Many claim that the furniture is broken, refrigerators work very poorly. Some visitors complain about staff who have repeatedly shown bad manners.



But tourists who come to the island for the sake of beautiful nature and healing air are not afraid of such trifles. After all, they spend all the time not in rooms, but on the street, for interesting games and walks.
