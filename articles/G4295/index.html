Magnificent volumetric crochet rose: a master class for beginner needlewomen

If you want to decorate your clothes with a beautiful accessory, make a magnificent yellow flower (or any other color to your taste). A crochet rose is knitted quite simply, the main thing is to be able to "read" the scheme correctly and perform all the rows without errors. To make a craft, you will need to purchase yellow yarn (50% acrylic, 50% cotton) and a hook number 2.5. It is desirable that the knitting threads are not too tight. We will work according to the scheme below.





We will start crocheting the ‚ÄúRose‚Äù decorative element by creating four air loops and then connecting them into a ring using a connecting half-column. Next, we will perform one VP necessary for lifting, and knit twelve single crochet (RN) columns. We finish the first row with a connecting loop, and the second one we start with one VP. Next, we will carry out chains of three air loops, and between them knit one RLS each. Thus in the second row we will form six ‚Äúarches‚Äù.

Continuing the yellow rose



We start the third row, as usual, with one lifting air loop. Next, we will crochet the ‚Äúrose‚Äù crafts according to the following pattern: first, one single crochet is performed, and then one half-column in the same base loop. Next, three CHs are knitted in the next loop of the base. After that, one half-stick and one single crochet in the third loop of the air chain of the previous row are performed. By analogy, five more of these elements are knitted, rose petals. The row ends with a connecting half-column. Next, an air lift loop is performed. The fourth row is knitted by analogy with the second, only chains are executed not from three, but from five EPs. Between them knit on one sc. The fifth, sixth and seventh rows are created in the same way, the principle remains the same. Difficulties should not arise. As a result, you get a chic volumetric rose. Tell you how to make a charming "flat" rose





Sometimes, for decorating interior items or wardrobe items, voluminous flowers are not too suitable. In this case, a delicate flat rose (crochet) will help you gracefully decorate any product. The scheme of work for its manufacture is described below. To make such an elegant flower, you need a hook number 2.5, as well as cotton knitting threads of any color. We begin work by creating ninety-five air loops. We count thirteen VPs and close them with a connecting half-column in a circle. How to crochet a rose: we perform twenty-six double crochet columns in a ring. We make seventeen more of the same elements. In this case, four of them are no longer knit in the ring, but simply grabbing the chain. We skip six loops of the base, and in the seventh we perform one single crochet. So we get the first petal. By analogy, we carry out the following, as if "spinning" the bud around the core. Here we have got such a flat rose. You can vary the number of columns in the petals to make your flower look as realistic as possible.

Openwork decorative flower: crochet



If you want to perform a lush pink bud, take on the following knitting technique. It allows you to get a charming "stuffed" rose. Such an accessory will transform any outfit and make your look more romantic and attractive. For work you will need 100% cotton knitting threads, a suitable sized hook and needle with threads. You can choose the yarn of any shade you like: pink, coral, bright scarlet or milky white. We will perform the rosette using the combined method, that is, first we will tie a long ribbon, and then sew it, forming a beautiful bud.

Knitting technology roses

Let's start the implementation of our wonderful accessory with a set of chain of air loops. Their number depends on how many petals you want to complete. The more magnificent and voluminous you plan to tie the rose, the more VP will need to be done. After completing the initial chain, we skip four loops. In the fifth we knit four double crochet. We skip one loop and execute the next five CHs. Thus, we knit the entire row to the end of the chain. Then we collect three lifting VP and turn the work. We knit 4 RLS, after which we perform three VP and a connecting half-column. This pattern is repeated until the end of the second row. As a result, you should get a wavy spiral tape. When it is ready, you can cut the thread and proceed to design the roses in the bud. We turn the tape into a spiral and sew it on the back side. That's all, the charming accessory is ready. You can pin a pin from the wrong side and use it as an original brooch.
