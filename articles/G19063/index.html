Ambassador Plaza Hotel 4 * (Kemer): overview, features and reviews

When it comes time to vacation or vacation, many think about how to go on a trip and relax with benefits for body and soul. People dream of a beach with hot sand, a warm sea, beautiful landscapes, interesting walks in previously unfamiliar, picturesque places, exotic fruits and unusual dishes that you can try in the restaurant. After these wonderful days, tourists want to return home tanned, with vivid photographs and positive memories that would warm them on cold winter evenings.

As you know, one of the most popular and popular tourist destinations today is a vacation in Turkey. This country is characterized by a developed hotel business, there are many hotel chains. Of course, none of us wants something to overshadow the impressions of the rest. That is why the choice of hotel is very important for all travelers. The article will talk about one of the most famous hotels in Turkey.

Ambassador Plaza: location and territory

The hotel in question is located in the central part of the city of Kemer. It often happens that hotels in Turkey are located very close to each other. Ambassador Plaza is no exception. Very close to it is a five-star hotel.



The distance from Antalya Airport to the Ambassador Plaza is sixty kilometers. It is approximately one hour away. Fifty meters from the hotel there is a beach. The hotel has five buildings, each of which has four floors. These rooms are located very close to each other. The central building has a reception and a cafe.

Ambassador Plaza is a small hotel that is perfect for spending holidays with family or together with a loved one. The rooms in the hotel are cozy, the interior is decorated taking into account the requirements of tourists. A separate play area is reserved for children.

General information about the hotel

The Ambassador Plaza Hotel in Kemer has been around for many years. It was built in 1990. The hotel is part of the Ambassador Group Hotels chain. The quality of the infrastructure and service of the hotel can be judged by the awards that were awarded to him. The organization was awarded certificates for a high level of service and food, nature conservation, maintaining clean water near the beach area.

Ambassador Plaza 4 * is located a few meters from the sea coast. The beach does not consist of sand, but of pebbles. To protect your legs, it is advisable to bring special shoes with you. On the beach there is a bar where guests are offered cool drinks and snacks. Here you can also use a sun lounger for free, but it is best to take a seat before lunch, because then you can not have time. Towels can be brought from the hotel or rented on the beach for money.

Vacationers are pleased with the convenient location of the hotel. It is popular among couples, including the elderly, as well as among business people and youth companies. The tranquil atmosphere on the hotel's premises has a family holiday with children. The hotel stops citizens from different countries. For tourists from Russia, this hotel is also attractive because its staff speak Russian.

Hotel structure

The Ambassador Plaza Hotel has two hundred and forty guest rooms. One hundred twenty-four of them are non-smoking, if you smoke, you need to discuss this with the staff before checking in. Each room has air conditioning, bath and shower, hygiene products, bar, TV, coffee machine, luggage storage. Service in the rooms is carried out 24 hours a day, every day at certain hours they put things in order. At the hotel there is a swimming pool, two bars (one on the beach and one in the building), a room for business meetings.

It also offers guests:

  • gym;
  • gym;
  • bath and sauna;
  • table games;
  • hairdressing salon;
  • car rental;
  • first-aid post;
  • Spa;
  • water massage.

Rooms

All Ambassador Plaza rooms, regardless of category, have a standard range of amenities, for example:

  • air conditioning with heating and cooling functions;
  • luggage storage;
  • wardrobe;
  • satellite television;
  • shower and bath;
  • hair dryer;
  • sofa or chair;
  • balcony;
  • two phones.




There are several types of rooms in the hotel. The standard room is twenty-five square meters and has a balcony or terrace. The family room has two rooms (forty square meters each). It has four beds and a double sofa bed, balconies, a bathroom with shower. There are separate beds for children. The apartments offer views of the pool, sea, mountains or garden. Each room has a seating area.

The Ambassador Plaza Hotel in Kemer has facilities for people with special needs. This room is located on the ground floor of the hotel, it has a wider front door, a specially equipped bathroom, a terrace and a balcony.

Food

Catering in the hotel takes place according to the All Inclusive system. This involves a wide selection of dishes, both European and Oriental. Vacationers are provided with a variety of fruits, seafood, alcoholic beverages.

Those who wish can use the services of a buffet, order vegetarian or diet dishes. In one of the bars you can buy juice, beer, soda, cocktail and food (nuts, chips, chocolate). A variety of pastries, desserts and wines allows you to perfectly celebrate a birthday, wedding anniversary or any other celebration.

Entertainment

Vacationers in Ambassador Plaza 4 * (Kemer, Turkey) are offered a system of animation events for both adults and children. They include an entertainment program (weekly performances), yachting, sightseeing tours, swimming in the pool and water aerobics (including for children), walks outside the hotel (as it is located in the central part of Kemer, you can visit shops, clubs).



Children's animation is presented in sections (for example, in water aerobics), a playground for games, dance evenings for children. Professionals spend their leisure time with children, they organize classes of traditional dances, singing, drawing, as well as all kinds of game events and competitions. If you take your child with you on vacation, you can not worry: he certainly will not be bored. In addition, if you need to go away for a while, you can always use the services of a baby sitter.

What can I use for an additional fee?

Ambassador Plaza offers both free and paid services. You do not need to pay extra for a bath and sauna, a gym, a children's water park with attractions, wireless Internet (300 megabytes limit), a playground for games, board games, beach chairs and umbrellas, and a parking lot. An additional charge will be required for spa services, massage treatments, laundry, medical assistance, haircuts, a left-luggage office, photo shoot, fax and telephone facilities.

Change of linen and towels in the room is carried out three times a week or more often (optional). You do not need to pay extra for this. The hotel offers a paid excursion program. Judging by the reviews of tourists, these events are worth visiting, as they are interesting, and the guides know the history of their land well and can tell a lot about it.

As the attractions located near the hotel, you can list several parks (dinosaur park, Moonlight park with a beach, a theme park for nomads), as well as a picturesque and beautiful marina of the city. Thirteen kilometers from the hotel is the Phaselis Museum. For those who stayed at the hotel for the purpose of business trips and solving tasks related to professional activities, there is a business center. You can pay for the rental of the conference room, where you will hold negotiations, meetings, trainings, develop a project or discuss important issues with colleagues.

Wellness programs and caring procedures

Of course, everyone who comes to rest would like to relax both in body and soul. If you are going to attend an event at a hotel or outside, for example, going to a disco, a bar, a party or a restaurant, it is important to look well-groomed and attractive. In Ambassador Plaza Hotel 4 * in Kemer, wellness activities are provided. They include spa programs, water massages, skin and nail care, various types of showers and baths, a sauna and a bath. The hotel also offers the services of a gym (where there is a treadmill, a bicycle trainer, step aerobics facilities) and a fitness center (where you can train under the guidance of an instructor).

Advantages of the hotel

Ambassador Plaza Hotel 4 * in Turkey is very popular among tourists, including citizens of our country. A lot of reviews have been written about the hotel, and this can be seen if you start looking for information about the organization on the Internet. The positive characteristics of the Ambassador Plaza are usually listed as the good quality of service and courtesy of the hotel employees, the cleanliness of the rooms and their quality equipment (including technical), a pleasant atmosphere, and delicious food.

In general, we can say that this four-star hotel is fully consistent with its category. But the Ambassador Plaza, like any hotel, has its drawbacks.

disadvantages

Not all guests can say that they are completely satisfied with the service and infrastructure of the Turkish hotel. Some note the disadvantages of the institution. The negative location of the Ambassador Plaza Hotel 4 * in Kemer is the remote location of the hotel from the sea coast (you have to go around the building and cross the road to reach the beach). Pebbles instead of sand are not too familiar and convenient for tourists. Some vacationers do not like the fact that towels on the beach are not given out for free, if they do not want to spend extra money, they are forced to take a towel from their room.

Also, according to tourists, the hotel is not too developed a system of recreational activities. To visit them, you need to go to the city center. Not everyone can do this, because they care about their safety. However, if you are looking for a calm and measured rest, the question of animation is most likely not too important.
