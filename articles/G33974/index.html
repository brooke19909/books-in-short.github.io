Thailand, Khao Lak - a paradise on Earth

Attractive, colorful and very popular among Russian tourists is now the country of Thailand. Khao Lak is a resort that few have heard of, but everyone who got there now knows that paradise on Earth exists. Despite the fact that this small city is located in such a noisy and visited country, it is characterized by only silence, natural balance and cleanliness, in a word, everything that a modern person needs for complete relaxation. Therefore, if you are looking for a place where you can forget about the bustle, about problems and stresses, go to Thailand.



Khao Lak is located on the coast of the Andaman Sea, near Krabi. Therefore, you can get to the resort from this province by boat or ferry. However, most tourists prefer to fly to the international airport of Phuket and from there already follow directly to Khao Lak.

This region has a special climate that is not similar to that characteristic of most of the state of Thailand. Khao Lak is located in the zone of dry winds, which raise the local temperature to 35-37 degrees in the shade. Water in the sea warms up to 30 degrees. That is why the resort is ideal for families with children, because there is simply no chance to catch a cold in such a sea. It should also be noted that similar climatic features are typical for this region in the winter, and in the period from June to October it begins to rain, but the temperature does not decrease.



Like all of Thailand, Khao Lak is distinguished by its beautiful nature and clean air. It is in this region that snow-white wide beaches stretch for tens of kilometers, with palm trees visible on it. Immediately after the strip of sand begins lush tropical vegetation, which from individual islands smoothly passes into the dense jungle. However, getting lost in them is very difficult, since the island itself, on which the resort is located, is small. Cliffs that rise among tropical greenery and protrude into the sea, forming quiet azure harbors, complete the picture. It is for such natural beauties that many people go to Thailand.

In recent years, Khao Lak has become a center in which natural beauties and modern relaxing spa centers are combined. Also in the resort there are gyms, fitness centers and other facilities that tourists may need. Only four-star hotels are equipped with pools. But the salons where they carry out procedures of varying complexity and return health and youth to customers can boast all the hotels of Khao Lak. Thailand is a place that has all the conditions for a good rest, and you will return home a completely different person!





The Andaman Sea is famous for its high waves, therefore the resorts located on its coast have services where you can rent all the necessary equipment for entertainment on the water. Also, diving is practiced in this place, so tourists can see the virgin beauty of the underwater world. Here you can rent a bicycle and go on a trip to the local reserves, along the white beaches and picturesque green valleys. Indeed, it is from such natural wonders that the sights of the Khao Lak province are formed.

Thailand (reviews leave no doubt about the advantages of its resorts) is an amazing country, and if you are an avid traveler, but still have not visited it, then immediately correct this situation.
