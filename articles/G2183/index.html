DIY flower ribbon: a striking detail of your look

Flowers made of fabric are used for interior decoration and souvenirs, as well as for making jewelry. DIY handmade jewelry will perfectly complement the look and make it more feminine.

The secret of the popularity of copyright jewelry items is that the original beads or earrings are unique and created in a single copy. Jewelry made of fabrics is convenient to wear due to its low weight, and such jewelry looks spectacular and stylish.

To make a flower of ribbons with your own hands, first of all, you need to choose the right fabric and color scheme of the future product. For the manufacture of roses and massive compositions, satin and crepe satin are best suited, and chiffon and organza are used to create light and airy inflorescences.

A good idea would be to use tapes of different textures for optimal color solutions. It is important to remember that the general appearance of the finished flower depends on how successfully selected shades and types of fabrics are combined.

You can make a flower from ribbons with your own hands using various methods. The most popular are folding roses from fabric, assembling a ribbon sewn around the edges, making a flower from separately sewn petals. We will not need color schemes from ribbons, since the step-by-step instructions for the manufacture of these products are presented below. So let's get started.

To create flowers from satin ribbons, photos of which are presented below, materials were required:

  • a metal brooch decorated with rhinestones;
  • silver rhinestones;
  • silver organza;
  • dark gray satin ribbon;
  • Super glue.




From organza we cut rounded petals. The shape of the petals should not be perfectly round.





The edges of the petals must be melted with a candle, after which the petals will get a clear outline and a convex shape.









We lay several petals on top of each other, adding two sheets of satin ribbon to the base . The edges of the leaves also need to be melted over the candle.





We sew the central part of the flower with silver thread, glue a brooch in the center of the cup, in which we first remove the fastener. To make the flower look neat, when gluing, you need to use a colorless superglue that does not leave marks.

With the right approach, creating such a detail as a flower from ribbons with your own hands will not take much time, but it will require accuracy and patience.

A flower can be used as a brooch or to decorate a hairstyle, or you can make a necklace. To create a necklace you will need to make several additional parts. To do this, cut a smaller petal from the organza, melt its edges, glue rhinestones in the center of the cup. Glue four petals with rhinestones and a flower with a brooch to the cord, fix with a thread for reliability.





You can create a flower from ribbons with your own hands not only for your own use, but also for an original gift. A handmade gift will be liked and remembered by your loved ones. Present in the original packaging, decorated with interesting details, will appeal to everyone.
