Ski resorts in France: Tignes and other popular holiday destinations

Alpine skiing is becoming increasingly popular. Some people spend their winter holidays in Europe, where they can enjoy not only the sights, but also go skiing, snowboarding or sledding. Below is a list of ski resorts in France.

Tignes

This resort is one of the most popular in France. It is the second highest in Europe, and is located in the Rhone-Alpes region and 500 km from Paris. France Tignes ski resort is located near a small village. At first it was at an altitude of 1,500 meters, but was washed away with water. Then it was restored already at an altitude of 2,100 meters.

Now France's Tignes ski resort consists of several villages and is part of the famous Espace Killy. In this region, the total length of all the routes of the complexes is more than 300 km. France Tignes ski resort includes many ski slopes with excellent conditions for skiing.

There are also trails for beginners with free ski lifts. Also in the ski resort of Tignes in France, not only excellent conditions for sports, but also an entertainment program. There is a sports complex with a pool and saunas, an ice rink, a bowling alley and many restaurants, bars and discos. Tignes is a "green resort", so all cars must be placed in paid parking lots.

Val Thorens

Another French ski resort. It was opened at Christmas 1971 - then there were only a few lifts. And in 1972, the first ski school, sports club and tourism department were opened. Now in France, in the Val Thorens ski resort, one of the best lift systems.

There is the longest track for a sleigh ride - 6 km, a large snowpark and an ice hippodrome. Visitors are offered a wide selection of winter and summer activities. Val Thorens is a semi-pedestrian resort where you can get around by car. You can only park in certain places.

This resort has several hotels, two of which are five-star. And a large number of residences and club hotels form the basis for accommodating guests. It offers visitors a large number of restaurants, two of which have Michelin stars, bowling, bars, clubs, cafes, pastry shops and many other leisure options. Val Thorens is one of the largest ski resorts in France.

Les Arcs

This ski resort is nestled among forests and on steep rocky slopes above the Isere River Valley. This complex includes more than 100 tracks, the total length of which is 200 km. There are slopes and slopes for both beginners and professionals.

The peculiarity of the Les Arcs ski resort located in France is that there are hiking trails with a length of more than 25 km, which are very picturesque. Dog sledding is also available. Courchevel

Courchevel is one of the most famous ski resorts in France. It was opened in 1946, there was part of the competitions of the Winter Olympic Games. There is also a springboard for jumping. There are four stations in Courchevel, the most elite is at an altitude of 1,850 meters. This resort is ideal for beginners - there are smooth and wide tracks.

In ski schools a large staff of instructors who train everyone and are professionals. Also in Courchevel there are many hotels, restaurants, cafes and other entertainment for guests.
