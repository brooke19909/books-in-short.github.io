Necessary materials

To make bouquets of sweets with your own hands (see the steps in the article below), you need to prepare the following materials:

  • wrapping paper;
  • flower ribbon;
  • double sided tape;
  • toothpicks or skewers;
  • sticky flower ribbon;
  • capacity for a bouquet;
  • everything that can be used for decoration.

How to make a bouquet of sweets with your own hands?

Before collecting a bouquet, you need to make individual flowers. For this, blanks of a certain shape are cut out of wrapping paper. Each block will be wrapped in a fist and secured with a small piece of double-sided adhesive tape. A candy can be placed inside (preferably round or in the form of a truffle), which is attached to a wooden skewer (for example, a toothpick or skewer). After that, you need to once again fix the structure with tape. If desired, additional corrugated paper petals can be added.



Made small bouquets of sweets with your own hands can be decorated in different ways. For masking, it is worth using a decorative ribbon, which you can simply tie the stems into a single whole. You can also use some kind of container (a plastic form, a vase, a box, a flower pot, and so on), where the bouquet is fixed using special foam and double-sided tape. If desired, you can add decorative elements such as twigs, leaves, dried flowers or fresh flowers.

So, how to create do-it-yourself candy bouquets? A step-by-step explanation for your attention. Necessary items: sweets, colored paper, green cardboard, glue or double-sided tape, curly scissors.

Step-by-step instruction

Step 1.

From green cardboard, cut a circle with a diameter of 5-6 centimeters. Next, make a small cut from side to middle. After this, you need to combine the ends so that they form a cone. The resulting tip is cut off, since a candy attached to the leg will be inserted into the hole formed.





Step 2

Now we take double-sided colored paper and use the templates to cut out the petals from it, then fix them with glue on top of the conical candy wrapper. You can also use double-sided tape in green or brown.





Step 3

Using yellow paper, creates a heart-shaped flower around the candy that needs to gently and gently wrap the bud. At this stage, you need to be especially careful.





Step 4

Using a decorative tape, you need to fasten all the elements together.
