Bridge Family Resort 3 * (Sochi, Imereti resort): hotel description, services, reviews

Bridge Family Resort 3 * is a modern hotel, which is part of the Bridge Resort. This institution will provide a comfortable stay for each member of your family.





a brief description of

Bridge Family Resort 3 * opened in 2014. Guests are accommodated in one of the many buildings of the resort complex. The comfortable rooms feature an elegant modern interior and are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. The total territory of the complex covers 6 hectares.

In 2015, Bridge Family Resort 3 * was recognized as the best hotel for families. And this is not surprising. Its concept is designed so that all guests, regardless of age, can have a good time. For the smallest, there is a games room and numerous attractions, young people will be happy to take part in entertainment, and the older generation will be interested in wellness programs.

In an ecologically clean area (in the Imereti Lowland) is the resort Bridge Family Resort 3 *. Russia, Sochi, Adler, 45 Figurnaya Street, - the address where you will find this hotel. Privileges for regular guests

For regular guests, the hotel provides a system of bonuses and discounts. So, after the first visit, the guest is assigned the Silver status, which guarantees a 10% discount on subsequent room reservations.

If you have been staying at the hotel for 25 nights at one time or in total, then become the holder of the Gold status. Save 10% on reservations. You can take advantage of a discount of the same size in restaurants and a wellness center. Upon arrival you will receive a compliment in the form of a bottle of wine and a plate of fruit. One night stay will be free for you.

Platinum status is assigned to a guest after the 75th night. The discount increases to 15%. Among other privileges inherent in the Gold status, a meeting at the airport or train station and a transfer to the hotel will also be provided. You also have the opportunity to take 2 things to the laundry room for free.

Most of all privileges are given to owners of the Diamond status, which is assigned to a guest after 150 days of staying at the hotel. 20% off. All benefits of Platinum status are retained. Free wash 3 things.
