Negative reviews

So that your vacation is not spoiled by unpleasant trifles, prepare yourself in advance for possible negative moments that are characteristic of the Radisson Blu Ulysse Resort Thalasso Djerba. Reviews of tourists focus on the following facts:

  • The furniture and appliances in the rooms are very old. Everything needs repair and updating.
  • The balconies in the rooms are very small. They barely fit two chairs and a coffee table.
  • Both local and imported alcohol (except wine) is not of the best quality. Drinks are more or less tasty only in the form of cocktails.
  • After each order in the bar on the system "All inclusive" you need to sign in some check. This is very inconvenient, in addition, it seems that the administration controls the amount of alcohol consumed by each guest.
  • By 9:00, all sunbeds on the beach and pool are already occupied. But this is more the fault of the guests, who in advance take several places, regardless of whether they will use them.
  • Workers do not speak Russian (except for a couple of common words of greeting). English is also mediocre. Basically, it is necessary to explain in sign language.
  • The hotel's restaurants have a dress code, but for some reason, guests who violate it do not make any comments. It is unpleasant to see people in short shorts and slates.
