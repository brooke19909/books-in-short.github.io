Climate Sri Lanka

As already noted, the island is always warm, if not hot weather. The average temperature is +29 degrees, and water in the ocean - up to +27. However, as in any tropical country, there are two seasons here: dry and rainy. Precipitation falls mostly in the summer months. But from the end of October to the beginning of April, it is just perfect weather for a beach holiday. However, if you bought a tour to the Induruwa Beach Resort 3 * hotel in the rainy season, then do not worry too much, because precipitation mostly falls at night, and in the morning the weather is already fine. In addition, depending on the time of year, you need to choose among the resorts of the west and south (in winter) and east (in summer) coasts.

How to get to Sri Lanka from Russia?

There are no direct flights between the two capitals, Moscow and Colombo. However, in season you can fly here with a transfer in Male, the Emirates, Qatar, etc. The approximate flight time is about 14 hours (if you take into account the time for connecting). When you buy a tour at any of the country's hotels, you will be asked to pay a transfer from the airport to your destination. By the way, holiday packages to 5-star hotels include delivery, but a ticket to a hotel of a category lower, for example, to 3 * Induruwa Beach Resort 3 stars, this service is not included, and you need to pay extra for it. Induruwa

Induruwa is one of the best resorts in the west of the island, especially in terms of calm and measured relaxation. Here, tourists are waiting for incredible beauty of landscapes, wonderful white sand beaches, turquoise waters with an amazing underwater world, plus the opportunity to get acquainted with the life of the local rural population. Until recently, there was only a fishing village on the site of this resort, and today there are beautiful hotels, well-groomed, but not without pristine beaches. Nevertheless, families of fishermen continue to live in their modest dwellings and provide tourists with the opportunity to observe their lives. Moreover, this area is located in close proximity to the city of Colombo (64 kilometers), which means that it is not far from civilization. You can always go on a tour or shopping in the capital. Every year, the popularity of this resort in the eyes of Russian tourists is increasing, which is associated with the ideal ratio of "price-quality". Unlike many expensive tourist destinations, here you can relax with great comfort for a relatively low fee. So, for example, in 3 * Induruwa Beach Resort the cost of the tour is from 90,000 to 100,000 rubles per person for 11 nights, and this despite the fact that 25% of the total amount is the cost of the ticket.

Infrastructure and Services

Induruwa Beach Resort 3 * (Sri Lanka): reviews and ratings

Unfortunately, not all reviews about the Hotel Induruwa Beach Resort are positive. Tourists from Russia most often complain about the scarcity of breakfast. There can be no talk of any buffet. As for the service, then this is a complete order. Only some guests complain about the unceremoniousness of the staff, who right before their eyes begin to recount the tip. And everything else - the beach, entertainment, spa services, excursions - at the highest level. This is evidenced by the grateful reviews of tourists who visited this, albeit modest, but very nice hotel.
