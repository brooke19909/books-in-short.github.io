The resort complex Amara Wing Resort Comfort 5 * has 312 rooms with a total capacity of 724 people. You can choose one of the following accommodation options:

Additional Information

Going on vacation to Amara Wing Resort Comfort 5 * (Kemer), you should familiarize yourself with the rules of accommodation in this hotel. The following points can be considered the most significant:

  • check-in and eviction of guests is carried out, respectively, after 14:00 and until 12:00;
  • Children under 6 years old stay free of charge on existing beds, and children under 2 years old are provided with free special cots;
  • pets are not allowed in the hotel;
  • guests can use plastic cards to pay for services;
  • in all visits to the hotel smoking is strictly prohibited (except for places specially designated for this);
  • guests in beachwear and too open clothing are not allowed in restaurants;
  • when booking a room, guests are required to pay a certain deposit by credit card;
  • Registration and check-in is possible only after providing a valid photo ID.

General impression

Hotel Amara Wing Resort Comfort (Kemer) leaves a rather mixed impression. It is worth noting that in most cases the disappointment of tourists is due to the fact that the hotel clearly does not correspond to its five-star status. If it were the Quartet, there would be much less complaints. But the prices for accommodation and services are high.

What immediately bribes is the location. In the immediate vicinity there is a clean beach, picturesque mountains and the main street of Kemer. But the hotel has practically no territory. A small space is built up very densely, and therefore you will have nowhere to walk. If we talk about food, it is certainly of high quality, but, again, travelers expect much more from a hotel with 5 stars.

Why there are no complaints, it is to the service. All staff are very polite and do their best to please guests. At the same time, no one will look askance at you if you do not want to leave a tip. It is possible that this behavior of employees is caused by total control by senior management, whose representatives are always present at the hotel.
