How to get a loan with bad credit history: banks, ways, reviews

Banking organizations have become very demanding and picky. Now they do not give loans to people with a dubious reputation. All these data are in the credit history bureau and are stored for fifteen years. How to get a loan with a bad credit history? We understand.

Credit standing and bank approval

Before approving a loan, each bank must check the person who asks for it. When the credit history leaves much to be desired, banking organizations generally refuse without explaining the reasons. Why it happens? The fact is that a person with a negative credit history confirms his insecurity and windiness. For any lender, this is a risk. For this reason, client scoring is so important. Based on its results, a loan or loan request is approved or rejected. This applies to consumer loans, mortgages are considered slightly differently, and for clearer prospects you need to consult with specialists.

But still, even when applying for a non-mortgage loan, a spoiled credit history does not mean one hundred percent disapproval. After all, basically, banks pay attention only to malicious defaulters, who, due to non-payment, were summoned to court. Systematic minor delays are not a serious violation and in the future the creditor can count on a loan.

Where does the bank get information?

Fourteen years ago, namely on the thirtieth of December 2004, the federal law "On Credit Histories" was adopted in our country. According to this law, bureaus appeared that process and store information about all borrowers and, if necessary, provide this information to banking organizations.

Each bank has an agreement with some kind of credit history bureau. When a person comes to a financial institution and writes an application for a loan, be sure to check the status of loans already taken and repaid.

The important thing is that the bank cannot independently operate the data of the borrower without his consent. But this nuance is skillfully circumvented, indicating in the application for approval of the loan the consent point for processing personal data. If a person refuses to mark such an item, they may be denied a loan even at this stage.

We can say that not a single banking organization will approve a loan until it is convinced of the borrower's good faith and checks it through the credit history bureau.

Credit Opinion

You can get a loan with a bad credit history at the bank, or they may refuse. Each bank reacts differently to a bad credit history.

How to get a loan with bad credit history? Relatively simple if the bad story is due to systematic, but insignificant monthly delays. A financial institution will simply raise the interest rate or tighten the terms somehow. For example, they can reduce the amount of a loan or its repayment period.

How to get a loan with bad credit history, when non-compliance by the borrower with obligations is significant? Most likely in any way, due to the great risk to the bank. But everywhere there are different risk criteria, and if one institution is refused, then another can be approved. It is important to remember that if the story is badly damaged, then there is practically no chance of getting loans.

Extenuating circumstances

If you apply for a mortgage or a loan to purchase a car, then the chance for a positive answer is greater. Because a financial institution pledges real estate or a car, thereby reducing the risk of default on a loan.

Why the rating is down

Many people have a rough idea of how to get a loan with bad credit history , but not everyone knows why it goes bad.

There are credit history bureaus in which data on all borrowers are processed and stored. The degree of collaboration with the bureau is determined by the size of the organization. So, large banks have agreements with almost all bureaus. Private banks cooperate only with some BKIs, which may deprive some of the information. You can quickly get a loan with bad credit history in young banking organizations and financial institutions, where they give out express loans.

Rating Drop

  1. The borrower delays interest payments.
  2. Has late payments at the current time for no reason.
  3. The monthly loan payment exceeds half of the income.
  4. Credit issued as a result of fraudulent activities.
  5. A banking organization has registered a credit card with a borrower, but he does not know about it.
  6. Information about the closure of the loan was not transmitted to the bureau.
  7. The debt passed to the collection office, which for the most part do not notify the BKI.

It is clear that the credited can affect only the first three reasons, but what about the remaining four did not happen? Keep control over your credit history and, at the slightest change, contact the bureau.

Ways to change your credit history

After checking the history and finding there the reasons that the borrower is able to eliminate, they need to be eliminated. In a situation where the banking organization did not send data to the bureau, you need to contact the bank so that it corrects the error.

This is for unregulated reasons. What to do to the borrower himself to improve CI?

  1. Pay all arrears for the current moment.
  2. An ideal solution would be early repayment of the loan.
  3. Get refinancing loans with a bad credit history to reduce the amount of repayments.
  4. Get a small loan and pay it ahead of schedule or on time. Such an action helps to shape the story from scratch or improve the current one.
  5. You can quickly get a loan with bad credit history by taking any goods by installments.
  6. Get a mortgage or car loan (large amount) and a long term. Each application is considered separately, and there is an opportunity to change the decision of a bank employee in a positive direction. If it comes to small amounts, a computer program excludes a borrower with a bad reputation.

How to rehabilitate

Methods for closing a bad history of loans with other loans closed on time were considered by the borrower, but for some reason did not fit. What to do next and how to rehabilitate? There is another way to restore your reputation. Namely, evidence that the default on the loan was unintentional. As an example, long trips, flight delays, and treatment due to loss of working capacity are great. In the event of bankruptcy of an enterprise or delayed salary, a credit history can also be rehabilitated. To achieve restoration of a positive rating, the borrower only needs to confirm his words with documents.
