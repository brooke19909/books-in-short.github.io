Household refrigerated cabinets - food industry progress





Today, in almost every store you can see demo refrigerated cabinets that are designed for the sale and temporary storage of drinks in plastic and glass bottles. A wide variety of brands makes it difficult to choose a high-quality and reliable manufacturer of commercial equipment.

How is the refrigeration unit arranged?

Before purchasing a household refrigerator, you must first determine the place where it will be placed. For example, models with a lower compressor arrangement are more suitable for street trading. Such refrigerated cabinets are designed in such a way that they can easily be mounted special grilles that protect the housing from vandal destruction. The existing electromagnetic lock greatly facilitates the process of opening and closing doors, and also allows the seller to control them remotely from a store or kiosk. In winter, for continuous operation at low temperatures, a special reinforced compressor can be installed in refrigerated cabinets. In addition, such units are equipped with a combined temperature regime, the range of which can vary from -5 ¬∫ to +5 ¬∫, which is very important for products that need different degrees of cooling.





What is the popularity of refrigerated cabinets?

Fresh grocery products are the key to the success of any store. Even the smallest catering facility cannot do without special equipment . Refrigerated cabinets are popular in that they are versatile in use, and also have a large capacity with a fairly small size. In addition, such equipment is designed for long uninterrupted operation with sharp temperature changes, for example, in a hot shop or on the street. Unlike refrigerators, freezing units are designed for long-term storage of products, they can only be used indoors.

What refrigerated cabinets are there?





Depending on the built-in temperature regime, household refrigerated cabinets are divided into several types. The medium temperature unit with a working temperature of 0 to +8 degrees is designed for short-term storage of grocery goods, as a rule, it has a glass door. The freezer can cool to -30 ¬∫, but the combined refrigerator allows you to set the temperature from -20 to +8 degrees. In addition, commercial equipment differs in production technology and build quality, which completely depends on the manufacturer.

The most popular refrigeration cabinet today is Polyus, which has proven its reliability in long-term operation. The equipment of such brands as Polair, Inter, Ross, Technoholod, Ariada, Vestfrost and Cold is also popular. Multifunctional household refrigerators are designed not only for cooling and storing products, they also allow you to colorfully display a window and conduct a demonstration of goods in a large assortment.
