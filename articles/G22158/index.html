Funny master class: how to make a palm from a bottle

Plastic bottles are a very fertile material for all kinds of crafts, and generally suitable for the household. Of these, you can make flowerpots for indoor plants, organize a drip irrigation system, a stand for pencils and pens, lampshade for a lamp and much more. For example, an exotic palm tree in the yard.



Preparatory steps

First you need to learn how to make a palm tree from a bottle, and prepare auxiliary material. Firstly, the more empty plastic containers you have, the better. Most suitable bottles are brown, black and green, but you can use other colors as well. Secondly, you will need a strong wire: one segment is long, strong, which could be stuck in the ground, and three more more flexible, of different lengths. The first will become a rod for the trunk, and the rest will be useful for the crown. The next step in understanding how to make a palm tree out of a bottle is processing the plastic itself. Cut the brown bottles with respect to the bottom a little less than half. Just make curly cuts (with teeth) and bend out a little. This will create the effect of a scaly texture of the trunk. Cut green plastic along, without reaching the neck, then cut each half according to the type of palm leaves. Unscrew all the corks, except for the bottles that will be used last. Drill wire holes in these plugs.



We compose an exotic "plant"

Choose a suitable place in your garden or on your home playground. Insert the base pin deeper so that it is firmly fixed in the ground. Now string the barrel blanks one after another. If they are brown and black, you can alternate: this is good advice on how to make a palm tree out of a bottle more interesting and elegant. At the very end of the process, you should start attaching the wire to the crown. Bend the tip of the barrel into the loop with the help of pliers, insert the prepared segments into it in turn, spread them in different directions and also bend - you get a loop in the loop.

This is how the palm of the plastic bottles is made step by step, as we said. Let us clarify along the way: the barrel can be constructed in a different way. If you have a dry tree in the garden, cut its branches, leaving a few under the leaves, reduce the height to the desired size. Beat the barrel with the bottoms of the bottles - a raised surface to the outside. Use nails for fastening. They will be invisible, and an exotic beauty will be very natural. But back to our version. Now you need to string green blanks. Slip them upside down on the wire. Try to make sure that, as in the case of the barrel, the ends of the wire segments remain free.
