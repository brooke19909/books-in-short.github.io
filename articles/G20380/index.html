Xperia Kandelor Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya): description, service, reviews

Located about one hundred and twenty kilometers southeast of the famous Antalya, one of the most popular resorts in Alanya is often called the "pearl of the Turkish Riviera." This walled city dates back to the fourth century BC. There was a base of pirate frigates. It was through Alanya that all sea and land routes leading to Alexandria passed. Centuries later, Mark Anthony as a sign of his love for Cleopatra gave her this paradise.



general information

Today, Alanya has become a fairly popular center for youth recreation. The best hotels, pensions and hotel complexes are located on sandy beaches along the entire coast of the resort. The city has built a huge number of bars, restaurants and discos. During the summer tourist season, the influx of travelers increases the population of Alanya at least twice. Numerous hotels accept a huge number of people coming here from all over the world. The resort's all-inclusive tours are considered a visiting card. In general, Alanya is a youth and perhaps the most fun resort on the coast.

There are many really inexpensive and at the same time comfortable hotels located at some distance from the airport. Most of them were built in the nineties of the last century. Undoubtedly, they are periodically reconstructed and updated, but the resort is primarily intended for budget holidays. Therefore, those who plan to spend their summer holidays in Alanya and are looking for a good hotel at an affordable price should pay attention to the Xperia Kandelor Hotel 4 *, the description of which is presented in this article. From it it will be possible to learn more about this hotel complex, find out what is offered to tourists, and also understand what impression Russians had after staying in it.

Description

Xperia Kandelor Hotel 4 * is located in the center of Alanya, on the Turkish Riviera. It has an excellent location. Around this hotel are many ancient cultural monuments and entertainment venues. Here, history and natural beauty are woven together. The hotel is just two hundred meters from the famous Cleopatra Beach, across the street from it. Xperia Kandelor Hotel 4 * is part of the hotel chain Kleopatra Beach Hotels.



The entire infrastructure of one of the best tourist centers of this part of the Turkish coast is within walking distance from the hotel. The nearest international airport is in Antalya at a distance of one hundred and twenty kilometers. Therefore, the transfer from this air harbor to the hotel takes about two and a half hours. Xperia Kandelor Hotel 4 * was built in 1996. The last time a major update was carried out in it in 2015.

The territory of this hotel is relatively small, but completely landscaped. It has all the necessary infrastructure for a comfortable stay.

Xperia Kandelor Hotel 4 *: service

The infrastructure of this four-star hotel is fully consistent with its category. The registration desk is open without interruption, so if you have any questions, day or night, tourists can contact the duty administrator. As for the settlement, then in the Xperia Kandelor Hotel 4 * there are no bureaucratic delays. With the availability of rooms, even those arriving much earlier than planned will be able to quickly get the keys. The hotel does not have its own parking. Guests can park their cars outside its territory in a city parking lot. In addition, residents of the hotel can use the paid laundry, room service, and dry cleaning services. Free internet access is available in public areas.



If you feel unwell, guests can consult a doctor so that an experienced doctor provides the necessary assistance. It must be borne in mind that visiting a medical office is a paid service. In the administrative block there is also a rental point for vehicles, including bicycles, a tour office. Here you can also order a taxi, rent a safe and a cell in the left-luggage office.

The hotel also provides postal and concierge services, there is a photo salon, hairdresser, banquet hall.

This hotel does not have its own bathing area. It is public and two hundred meters distant from Xperia Kandelor Hotel 4 *. The beach is separated by a quiet road. It has a sand cover. Umbrellas and mattresses are issued free of charge. Water sports equipment can be rented on the beach.

Xperia Kandelor Hotel 4 *: reviews

Enough Russians have already visited this hotel. It should be said that the reviews of vacationers vary. Some were satisfied with the choice of hotel, others believe that it was not worth coming here. Many stay at the Xperia Kandelor Hotel because of the famous Cleopatra Beach.

There are no particular complaints about the location: all important infrastructures are within walking distance. There are many cafes and shops nearby.

The territory is quite small. The hotel is urban, so it is built on a small area. The rooms, although small, are very comfortable. Our compatriots consider the presence of disposable slippers to be a big plus. Another little surprise is the free filling of the minibar with drinking water and two bags of juice.

The food is excellent. So says the vast majority of Russians. This is noted not only by tourists, but also by representatives of travel agencies selling vouchers here. A huge variety of dishes, delicious cuisine. Many say that the food in this hotel is one of the best among Alanya hotels.

Another huge plus is the free sunbeds on the beach. Tourists have to pay for umbrellas and trestle beds along the entire coast of Alanya, but an exception has been made for the Kleopatra Beach Hotels hotel chain, which also includes Xperia.

Bottom line: many Russians expressed a desire to come here again.
