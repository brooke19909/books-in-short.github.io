Water Planet 5 - holidays on the Mediterranean coast

Description: Water Planet 5 is an entertainment hotel complex, which occupies a vast territory with an area of ​​62,415 square meters in the Okurcalar district. It is located on the Mediterranean coast. Around it are lush green forests. The hotel made sure that every vacationer here could find a suitable entertainment, feel the magical power of water. On its territory is the largest water park on the Mediterranean coast. It includes a large range of water activities, rides, slides, rafting, swimming pools with towers.



Water Planet 5 has a 9-story building, equipped with 7 elevators. There is another panoramic elevator that goes down to the beach. From the center of Alanya it is separated by a distance of 30 kilometers. And to get to Antalya International Airport, you need to overcome 90 kilometers. The hotel is a great place for families with children to spend their holidays, as well as for spending holidays with youth companies.

Rooms: The residential Background Water Planet 5 consists of 321 rooms. There are 282 standard, 30 suites, 9 large suites and 4 for the disabled. These are comfortable and cozy rooms decorated in a modern style. All rooms have air conditioning, safe. There is a minibar with soft drinks. There is satellite TV (there is a Russian channel), a telephone. The room has parquet flooring. There is a bathroom in every room. A hairdryer, ironing board and iron are provided. If necessary, one crib is installed. All rooms have a balcony. Some have sea views, some have mountains and garden views, and the rest have pool views.



Meals : Water Planet 5 provides its guests with all-inclusive meals in the main restaurant of the hotel, which is a modern air-conditioned room (220 people) with a terrace (320 people). In addition, there is a restaurant serving Turkish cuisine and a restaurant whose menu includes a huge number of seafood dishes. Vacationers here can visit one of them once every 7 days for free. In addition, guests can relax and unwind in 7 bars with a pleasant atmosphere, providing all kinds of drinks and a huge selection of light snacks.

Beaches: Water of the Planets has its own equipped beach with a 3-level platform. There are many recreational attractions. Vacationers are provided with a huge selection of entertainment, including diving, boating (allows you to appreciate the beauty of the surroundings), parachuting (get a good charge of adrenaline).



Tourist info: The hotel has 4 outdoor pools of various sizes, ranging from 200 square meters. meters and ending with 850 sq.m. The water park has 24 slides of varying difficulty. For sports lovers there is a tennis court and a football field. For those who come to combine leisure with work, 2 well-equipped conference rooms (60 sq.m and 210 sq.m) are provided.

Relaxing in the Water of the Planets, Turkey will be your paradise on earth. There is a huge amount of entertainment and opportunities for sports. There is an animation in Russian. The hotel is very busy, music plays all day, in the evening - live music. A visit to the amphitheater will help highlight the whole atmosphere of this holiday.

For those who, despite the rest, try not to leave the sport, there is a gym, darts, table tennis, beach volleyball, archery, aerobics. You can try your skills in billiards, minigolf or minisoccer. There is a sauna. A qualified masseur works. In the evening, all vacationers are waiting for an open disco.

Along with free services, there are a number of paid services on site. This is a car rental, where you can independently explore the beauty of the surrounding area, see the sights here and in neighboring cities. For those who wish to play tennis at night, when the hot sun does not burn, for an additional fee, the lighting on the tennis court and the necessary equipment are provided. Paid services include laundry, hairdresser, babysitting, photo services.



Reviews: The main advantage of the hotel is the placement on its territory of an excellent water park. In addition, there are many diverse entertainment, there is the opportunity for sports.

The rooms are cozy, decorated by modern designers.

The kitchen is excellent - for every taste.

The attendants are always ready to help vacationers, suggest and answer questions of interest. Reception is round-the-clock. There is also a safe where you can leave your valuables to keep.
