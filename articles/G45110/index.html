Description of the recreation center "Peresyp" in Kirillovka

Kirillovka is a Ukrainian beach resort on the shores of the Sea of ​​Azov in the Zaporozhye region, which in recent years has attracted more and more tourists. There are wide sandy beaches and a fairly deep sea. One of the regions of this resort village is Peresyp Spit. Recreation centers of Kirillovka and boarding houses stretch along the coast of this sandy strip from the center of the village to the ravine. From there, another Azov resort begins - Stepanovka Pervaya. At the very edge of the spit, not far from the ravine, there is a base of the same name - “Peresyp”. How to relax there and what conditions tourists offer - read the article below.



What is the spit Peresyp resort of Kirillovka

A long strip of beaches stretches to the north-east of the village. On one side is the Milk Estuary, and on the other, the Sea of ​​Azov. Unlike the neighboring Fedotova spit, there are not so many entertainments, restaurants and night clubs in Peresyp. Only at the very beginning of the beach, almost near the center of the village of Kirillovka, is the amusement park. There are also the only nightclubs on the spit - Rio and Galaxy.

Tourists come here for a relaxing holiday on the beach. They ride on minibuses either to the center of the village, or to the neighboring Fedotova spit. There they will find a water park (one of the largest in Ukraine), a dolphinarium, and much more.

Among the other resorts of Kirillovka, the Peresyp spit began to be built up. Recreation facilities here were mostly departmental; earlier, many children's camps worked on the spit. Be prepared for the fact that most of the housing offers here still have a certain post-Soviet spirit with the appropriate service. However, at the same time, new private hotels function on the spit, many of which are on the first line from the sea. Some are located by the estuary, across the road from the beach, but even then go to the treasured sea no more than 150 meters.



Recreation center “Peresyp” (Kirillovka) - territory and accommodation

This is a real resort, which is located in the most comfortable part of the spit. Housing is offered in two-story buildings on the seashore. Large well-groomed territory - fountains, trees, flowers, flower beds. On the base there are rooms of various types: apartments, studios, family, suites, economy. All of them are equipped with amenities and are built to European standards. All rooms have electric kettles and refrigerators, and you can rent a microwave from the property.

The apartments of the recreation center "Peresyp" are equipped with a kitchen, two bedrooms and a living room. In total, the tourist complex has 88 rooms that meet all the requirements of the world hotel nomenclature.



Recreation facilities

The recreation center is interesting in that it is the only place in Kirillovka where there is not only an all-inclusive system, but also adult and children's animation. You can order only breakfast, you can two or three meals a day.

The recreation center "Peresyp" in Kirillovka takes care of the health of its guests. Here you can work out on the simulators or on the outdoor sports ground. Wi-Fi is available throughout. For children, a modern playground has been equipped, where all safety measures have been followed, a maze has been built, a mini-club works.

The price includes medical care, beach equipment and entertainment. There is a large pool with two sections for children. The base offers various conditions for corporate leisure, seminars and conferences. You can rent rollers, bicycles, children's cars. The beach at the base has its own, there are sunshades, sunbeds, umbrellas, water activities: catamarans, "bananas", "buns". The beach is cleaned every day.

For a fee, guests can take the clothes to the laundry room and request a shuttle service. If you want to spend a romantic evening on the beach, then you will be covered with a separate table and perfectly served.



Reviews about the recreation center "Peresyp"

Many tourists, not without reason, believe that in this complex comfort is organized no worse than hotels in Egypt or Turkey, and at Ukrainian prices. The food is delicious, the products are fresh, the choice of dishes is diverse. The dining room plays nice music. Thanks to the animators, it’s not boring to relax here, but at the same time it’s quiet at night.

It is best to come here with children, on the basis for them all conditions are created. The employees of the recreation center "Peresyp" are responsible, friendly, professional.

There is a food delivery service to the beach and to the room. And if you couldn’t eat everything that was served for breakfast, lunch or dinner in one sitting, you can safely take the rest with you in disposable dishes - the staff will help you pack.
