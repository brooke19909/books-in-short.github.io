Why you should come to the Greek island of Kos

With its pleasant climate and many sandy beaches, Kos is always a popular choice for vacationers from all over the world. Most resorts are right next to the beach. The combination of diverse food, rich culture and great deals makes it the perfect choice for a family vacation.

Nearby is the International Institute of Hippocrates and the museum. They are both worthy of your attention.





The castle of the Knights of St. John the Baptist remains the main tourist attraction on. Kos. It is worth buying a ticket for 3 euros per person to visit this amazing place. It will be interesting to everyone, especially to a person who first gets acquainted with Greek history.

In addition to the above attractions of the island of Kos, any tourist visiting a resort for the first time cannot but be impressed by the archaeological sites that are located everywhere. Indeed, the city of Kos was once considered one of the most beautiful coastal centers of the ancient world. Many of the historical sites were found during excavations. Ancient greek architecture

So, the city of Kos is known for its historical heritage. These are about 30 large monuments and ancient sights. They are a few meters apart. The remains of the buildings that once adorned the island in antiquity consist of a huge amphitheater, gymnasium and the ancient Agora (or market). The most famous site found during excavations in the city of Kos is the area called ‚ÄúExcavations‚Äù. It is located just a 10-minute walk from the center of Kos. There are impressive colonnades, several magnificent mosaics, as well as ancient courtyards, Roman baths, the temple of Dionysus and the recently restored Roman amphitheater. The most impressive legacy is the ancient gymnastic zone, where 17 original roof supports were preserved. The base of the building, dating from the 3rd century BC, is decorated with a large mosaic depicting the battle between people and animals. The pearl in the crown is the Roman odeon, built in the II century AD, as a venue for public sessions of the Roman Senate, competitions. The festival of Asclepius was also held here, when musicians and poets from all over Greece and Asia gathered on the island of Kos.

The beach you dreamed about

Lambi Beach is located not far from the city of Kos, stretching north of the harbor to the northern edge of the island, along the cape. The purest Lambian sands are located along the eastern and northern shores and are filled with sunbeds for several kilometers. This beach is absolutely safe for everyone.

Hotels, taverns and bars that are on the beach attract many tourists. All lovers of water sports will like it here - there are water skiing, paragliding, surfing, etc. The beach has one of the largest surfing areas on the island of Kos.

Getting to Lambi beach is easy, there are many buses and taxis. There are bike paths for cycling enthusiasts.
