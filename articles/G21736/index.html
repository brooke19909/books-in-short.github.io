Hotel Beltur Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Kemer / Beldibi): photos and reviews of tourists

The Mediterranean coast of Turkey is one of the most popular tourist destinations. And it is here that the luxurious resort of Kemer is located. A few decades ago, Kemer was a simple fishing village, nothing attractive and not known outside the country.

Everything has changed rapidly since the first travelers were able to appreciate the beauty of the local nature and admire the many kilometers of sandy beaches. Now Kemer annually receives thousands of tourists from all countries who want to spend a vacation in this paradise.

Many believe that a trip to Kemer is a costly venture, but this is only partly true. There really are a lot of luxury hotels, accommodation in which is worth a lot, but in the neighborhood with them are huddled and hotels with more reasonable prices. At the same time, here you can also relax quite well. One of the representatives of this class can be called the Beltur Hotel 3 * hotel.

Description

This hotel is located in the picturesque place of Beldibi, which is located just 15 km from the city of Kemer. The hotel has a rank of 3 stars, but many sophisticated tourists claim the following. According to reviews, Beltur Hotel 3 * in Turkey (Kemer) claims to be on the same level with many 4-star hotels.

The total area occupied by the buildings and the private territory of the hotel is 6,000 square meters. m. The hotel consists of 5 buildings, each of which is a small 3-storey building (no elevators).





The year of foundation of the hotel is considered the 1986th, however, several years ago it was reconstructed, so the Beltur Hotel 3 * is ready to meet vacationers with fresh repairs, cleanliness and comfort.

Location

The hotel is quite well located:

  • The city of Kemer (a major tourist center of Turkey) is only 15 km away.
  • Antalya International Airport is 40 km away. It is at this airport that tourists arrive. At the airport you can take a taxi or take the bus.
  • From the hotel to the beach - about 100 m, so you can get to it in 5-7 minutes.

Hotel service

Anyone looking for a budget holiday option can safely choose this hotel. The cost of a ticket here is one of the lowest in Kemer, and the service is quite acceptable. The hotel is always happy to offer its guests the following services:

  • Reseption. This service is available around the clock.
  • Parking places. The hotel has a fairly large parking lot. You can use it absolutely free.
  • The Internet. Free Wi-Fi is available in the lobby.
  • Air conditioners. All public areas have climate control.
  • Dry cleaning and laundry services. There are no problems with clean clothes, however, this service is paid separately.
  • The medicine. There is a doctor in the hotel who provides appointments for a fee.
  • Safe. For storing valuables, vacationers can use the safe at the reception. The cost of this service is approximately $ 2 per day.

The hotel grounds are well thought out. There is a garden that is regularly looked after. In the shade of green trees you can relax from the summer heat. In the courtyard there are pools with equipped areas for relaxation.

The level of comfort in all of these rooms is exactly the same, so vacationers will be comfortable in any case.

Room Facilities

The rooms at the Kemer Beltur Hotel 3 * are quite spacious and bright, done in warm beige color. There is nothing superfluous, however, the installed furniture is enough to create coziness and attractive atmosphere. What facilities are provided in the rooms?

  • The floors are tiled.
  • Wet cleaning is provided every day.
  • Change of linen and towels. Linen is changed every 3 days (twice a week).
  • Air conditioning. Each room has a split system.
  • TV. The apartments are equipped with a TV and satellite TV. Among the proposed channels there are Russians.
  • Telephone. Vacationers can use the telephone for a fee.

Bathroom in the room. It offers a bath or shower, hairdryer. Upon arrival, guests are given toilet soap.

On the furnished balcony or terrace, which are adjacent to each room, you can breathe fresh air and enjoy the view from here. At the same time, the windows of some rooms overlook the sea, others - to the hotel courtyard.

The hotel accommodates guests on an all-inclusive basis. This means that vacationers may not care about where to find the nearest cafe to eat.





On the hotel territory there is:

  • main restaurant;
  • pool bar;
  • Front bar;
  • Terrace bar.

The main restaurant is designed in a laconic style, while it is quite cozy. In such an atmosphere, it‚Äôs nice not only to have lunch, but just to have a nice chat.

Entertainment

Staying at the Beltur Hotel 3 * does not seem boring at all. For the entertainment of vacationers, there are many possibilities.

  • Animation. The hotel has 3 animators who conduct daytime entertainment programs. Among them are Russian speakers. Such conditions are offered by no means in every 3 or even 4 * hotel, so Russian tourists will be quite comfortable. The animation team does not work here all year round, but only in the high season (from May 15 to October 31).
  • Disco in the evening. Paid entrance.
  • Billiards. The leisure room has several pool tables. Use of equipment is paid.
  • Table tennis. This service is also paid.




Pools

Usually a tour to Kemer (Beldibi) is chosen by those travelers who prefer a beach holiday. But even if a decision is made to spend several days at the hotel, there will be no disappointment. In the courtyard of the hotel there are immediately 2 adult pools and attractions:

  • Large pool. Its area reaches 150 square meters. m. The water in it is fresh, there is no heating, cleaning and cleaning are carried out regularly.
  • The pool is small. It is inferior in size to the main basin (area 90 sq. M).
  • Water attractions. There is a small water slide. It works on certain hours (before lunch - 11.00-12.00 and after lunch - 15.00-16.00).

The areas near the pools are paved with tiles, equipped with sun loungers and umbrellas. Towels are issued here for free. There is a bar.

health and beauty

In addition to lying under an umbrella and swimming in the Mediterranean Sea, rest at the Beltur Hotel 3 * hotel in Turkey is a great opportunity to take care of your appearance and health.

Vacationers can choose the option of entertainment according to their preferences, however, these services are paid:

  • a visit to the jacuzzi perfectly relaxes and improves mood;
  • sauna: the approximate cost of this service is $ 5;
  • hammam (Turkish bath) - a visit will cost about $ 20;
  • Massage - experienced masters will offer massage of individual zones or the whole body.




For children

In the Beltur Hotel 3 * quite often families with children stay. For their comfortable accommodation, all conditions are provided:

  • At the request of guests in the room you can install a cot.
  • The restaurant has children's dishes, there are highchairs for children.
  • There is a playground for outdoor games in the courtyard of the hotel.
  • Children's swimming pool. It has fresh water, heating is not provided.
  • Mini club. In a friendly company, children will have no time to be bored. The club expects children from 3 to 12 years old daily. Opening hours: before lunch - from 11.00 to 12.20 and after lunch - from 17.00 to 19.00.

Beach

Hotel Turkey Kemer Beltur Hotel 3 * is located at a distance of about 100 m from the sea, in connection with which it belongs to the 1st coastline. The beach here is private, sand and gravel. The hotel employees regularly clean and clean the coastal area, so there are no algae and garbage here. On the beach there are sun loungers, mattresses, umbrellas, so the rest here will be of high quality and comfortable.

Everyone can go to the city beaches, there are several of them. Entrance to them is free, and the territory is well equipped for recreation. Changing cabins, toilets, sun loungers and umbrellas allow you to relax with the whole family. There are a lot of green spaces in the district, so the landscapes are quite picturesque. There are small cafes and bars right on the beach. They serve fast food, snacks and drinks (alcoholic and non-alcoholic). Tourists are waiting for a huge amount of entertainment:

  • water skiing;
  • surfing;
  • scuba diving (diving);
  • riding on bananas;
  • yachting and boating.

The only negative is that in high season there are a large number of vacationers.





Those who love silence and solitude should go a little further from the city beaches. There are much fewer tourists here, the water is clean, but there is no beach equipment.

Beltur Hotel 3 *: reviews

What do tourists say about holidays here? Before buying permits for accommodation in a Beltur Hotel 3 * hotel in Turkey, reading reviews would be worthwhile. This will help to better organize the trip.





The following points were most often mentioned in the list of hotel shortcomings:

  • pebble beach, there are stones at the entrance to the water (this is somewhat inconvenient for vacationers with children);
  • lack of sun loungers during peak hours;
  • lack of choice of dishes in the restaurant.

However, not all tourists were dissatisfied. Many, on the contrary, speak out in favor of this hotel. In the list of benefits:

  • perfect cleanliness of the rooms;
  • proximity to the sea;
  • high-quality animation (a definite plus - Russian-speaking animators);
  • 3 star food
  • very low price.

Who should consider the rooms of Standard Beltur Hotel 3 * in Kemer as an option for accommodation? In general, Beltur Hotel is well suited for low-cost travelers, lovers of sightseeing tours and passive lying on the beach. As for food, there are many small cafes and restaurants in Beldibi and nearby Kemer where you can eat tight and tasty for a very small fee.
