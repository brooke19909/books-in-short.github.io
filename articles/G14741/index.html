Hotel "Passage" (Odessa): living conditions and guest reviews

Passage Hotel (Odessa) is located in the heart of the city, at the junction of Deribasovskaya and Preobrazhenskaya streets, in one of the most beautiful buildings. Most of the rooms face Deribasovskaya Street.

Passage Hotel (Odessa): address and interior

For tourists: the exact address of the hotel is Preobrazhenskaya street, 34.

The construction of the building that housed the Passage Hotel (Odessa) today was completed in 1886. This magnificent building with five dozen rooms is protected by the law of Ukraine, as it is an architectural monument of the 19th century.

Services provided around the clock

At any time of the day, both guests staying at the Passage for just a few hours and guests staying at the hotel can use:

  • laundry and dry cleaning services;
  • the help of hotel workers involved in ironing;
  • free internet (including Wi-Fi);
  • public parking services (in the event that there are no empty spaces in the hotel parking lot).

Fans of outdoor activities who do not want to be in the room all the time will have a great time communicating on the barbecue area owned by the hotel or in the covered gazebo.

The help of the attendants is guaranteed by the Passage Hotel (Odessa) to its guests, regardless of the time of day. In the same mode, 24 hours a day, there is a guest service department and a parking lot.



Accommodations

Registration of visitors wishing to stay at the Passage Hotel is carried out daily, from 13 to 14 hours.

The registration of guests leaving the hotel, the employees of the "Passage" produce daily up to 12 hours.

If the reservation is canceled, the penalties do not enter into force if the person who booked the hotel room canceled his reservation three weeks before the date of the proposed check-in.

Reviews of positive-minded guests of the Passage Hotel in Odessa

Judging by the reviews of tourists staying at the Passage during 2016, a vacation at this hotel is ideal for people who are in love with nature and are interested in architectural monuments, for whom such factors as an unrepaired room and the lack of a personal restroom are not priority.



Hotel "Passage", Odessa. Reviews of dissatisfied guests

The text below contains the opinions of guests of Odessa who visited the Passage Hotel. Reviews of tourists staying at the hotel can hardly be called positive. It is worth emphasizing that the rooms in question are among the cheapest rooms in the Passage.

Most of the guests expressed confidence that, despite the relative cheapness of the rooms, hotel employees could better care about the cleanliness of bedding and the rooms themselves.

The dissatisfaction of most customers is associated with disrespectful attitude on the part of the hotel administration and staff.

Some guests, whose comments were left (April 2013) on the portal dedicated to the level of service of Odessa hotels, expressed hope that the owners of the tourism business, of which the Passage hotel (Odessa) is a part, would pay attention and take measures to eliminate unsanitary conditions in the rooms , and also be able to find the funds necessary for a modest redecoration.



Some tourists claim that the Passage Hotel (Odessa, see photo above) reminded them of an illustration for a horror movie. Admiring the incomparable view that opens from the window of the room, the guests reproach the hotel owners for not observing basic hygiene rules in toilets and showers and recommend this hotel only to people with strong nerves. Also, visitors did not like the abundance of cobwebs and peeling plaster inside the building.

Some guests do not recommend this hotel to parents traveling with small children. The reason is the noise at night, the drafts and the lack of extra warm blankets.
