Knitting circular scarf: knitting pattern. Scarf snood

A scarf collar, and in a modern way - snood, gained its popularity a couple of decades ago. And he returned again, taking one of the first places among the winter accessories that are relevant this winter.

Which to choose

How to choose a circular scarf? Knit or crochet? We will consider knitting. What is meant by snud? This is an ordinary scarf connected in a ring. The easiest and fastest way to make it is to take your favorite scarf and sew the ends. You got a clamp.

Scarf collar has its own varieties. It can be in one revolution, in two and even in three. It also has a different width. It can be small, just to warm the neck in winter, and can be very large. This width is most often used to combine in one product both a scarf and a hood at once. These scarves are very practical. They warm on the street, and in the room they turn into an extravagant and voluminous scarf.

Also, a circular scarf with knitting needles can be complemented with such an accessory as a button. It diversifies the structure of your product, namely, you can not sew the edges, but fasten them on one large or several small buttons. It all depends on your taste and views.

The easiest way

Probably the easiest and fastest way to create a scarf-snood with knitting needles is to tie it with a front smooth surface. Let's look at an example.

  1. Dial the required number of loops (let it be 15) in order to see how our finished product will look.
  2. Knit the first row with facial loops. Do not forget to remove the first (edge) loop. This is necessary so that the edges are neat and delicate.
  3. Then turn over and knit the second row with purl stitches.
  4. We knit the third row again. Remember the edge loop.
  5. We knit the fourth row with the wrong ones.
  6. Having knitted twenty rows in this way, you will already see our facial surface.

Thus, the knitted circular scarf with knitting needles will have a very elastic shape and stretch well. Also gives your product a little volume. This knitting method can be applied to any yarn. Perfect for needlewomen who are just learning and want to make their own warming beauty for the winter.

Elastic scarf

The circular scarf with knitting needles will look original. Despite the complexity of the performance, knitting this gum is very simple.

  • We collect the required number of loops.
  • The first row is knitted as follows. We crochet and remove the first loop, the second we knit the front. Again yarn, remove the loop, knit the front. So to the end of the row.
  • The second row: the yarn with a removable loop is knitted on the wrong side, then we make the yarn and remove the next loop. Again we knit the crochet and the removable loop with the wrong ones. We make a crochet, remove the loop and again knit the crochet with the wrong loop. So to the end of the row.
  • The third row: the yarn with a removable loop is knitted with the facial for the back wall. We make a crochet, remove the loop and continue to knit further.
  • Thus, we knit to the desired result.

Seamless scarf

How to knit a scarf snood seamlessly? Circular knitting needles! On such knitting needles, as well as on ordinary, absolutely any collar can be connected. Whether it‚Äôs an openwork drawing or an English gum. But there are a few tricks to create the right and beautiful product.

When making loops and knitting the first rows, make sure that the loops do not twist. Everyone should lie flat and clear.

To mark the beginning of a row, mark it with a pin or colored thread.

A set of loops is done on a single needle, in a standard way.

When knitting on circular knitting needles, the removal of edge loops can not be done. Since knitting is done in a circle, this need disappears.

To prevent our wire from twisting during operation, lower it for several minutes in hot water. So it will become smooth, and will work much easier.

To simplify the task, the loops must be evenly spread along the entire length of the wire and the spokes themselves. Avoid finding loops in one place. This way you prevent spaces in the picture.

How to calculate the required number of loops

Many needlewomen at the beginning of their journey are faced with the problem of constantly dressing started. This is because the number of loops is initially incorrectly calculated. Even if you took a ready-made description and diagram, it may not necessarily turn out what is in the picture.



  • In order to calculate the number of loops you need, first measure the circumference of the head.
  • Then select the picture you like.
  • Knit a small piece with this pattern, ten centimeters wide and high. So you can understand how the finished product will look like.
  • In addition, count how many loops are in these 10 centimeters. Multiply them by the length you need. For example, the head circumference is 50 cm. In 10 cm there are 20 loops. So, for your product you need to dial 100 loops.
  • Also do not forget to include rapport in your calculations. Incorrect counting can bring down your drawing. Usually, with each scheme, the number of loops required for rapport is given.

How nice to close the loop

If you knit a scarf-clamp on ordinary knitting needles, then at the end the question will arise about how to make the seam less noticeable.



  • Option one. Just close the stitches and carefully sew the edges with a regular thread and needle. The color of the thread should be chosen to match the yarn.
  • The second option. The trick of the craftswomen. In order to make the seam thinner and invisible, do the following. Once you have finished the product and the last row is knitted, set aside the spoke with the product. At the beginning of your product, draw the same number of loops using a hook and a spare knitting needle. Then close all the stitches from the two knitting needles at the same time.
  • The third option. Use circular knitting needles. When working with them, no question about the seams will arise.
