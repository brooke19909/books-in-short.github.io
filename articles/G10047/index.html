Alexander Vasilievich Suvorov. Quotes and aphorisms

Suvorov, whose quotes have not lost their relevance in the modern world, is a hero of Russia. A brilliant military theorist, he served as the Generalissimo of both ground and naval forces.

Son of his father

Alexander's father, Vasily Ivanovich, was the godson of Tsar Peter. Grandfather, Ivan Grigorievich, served as a clerk, but the great emperor awarded Vasily the rank of general for services to his homeland. It was in such a family where military order was revered by his father, as well as fidelity to the sovereign, that the talented commander Suvorov grew up. The quotations belonging to Alexander precisely define some traits of his character.

  • "I am Russian! What a blessing!"
  • "An executioner can even be an executioner."
  • "Great adventures begin as usual."
  • "Always people have driven away true virtue."

The younger Suvorov, quotes about whose courage can be found in any historical chronicles, showed a firm desire to study military art.
