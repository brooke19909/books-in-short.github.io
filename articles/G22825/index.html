A bit about the hotel ...

Active tourists traveling around the world have long been known for such a resort as Sanya. Liking Resort Sanya 4 * is one of the many hotels built in the Hainan region. The hotel was built in Dadonghai Bay on the shores of the Great East Sea.



It is worth noting that the complex is located in one of the best areas of Sanya. Liking Resort Sanya 4 * is located just three kilometers from the city center and half an hour from the airport. Therefore, the road to the hotel will not be tiring for guests after the flight.

The hotel was opened in 2001 and since then annually hosts numerous tourists within its walls. In 2009, the institution was the last renovation.

Liking Resort Sanya 4 *: reviews

Speaking about the hotel, I want to refer to the reviews of tourists who rested in it. Indeed, it is thanks to the opinion of the guests that a more realistic picture can be formed. Liking Resort Sanya (ex. Landscape Beach) 4 * - a good complex located on the beach. The hotel is focused on a variety of categories of tourists, and therefore it will be comfortable for all guests of the resort to live here. The convenient location of the institution can be considered a big advantage, because far from every hotel can boast of what is located near the beach.



The complex consists of a number of buildings located in a beautiful green area. The complete set of all apartments is approximately the same. However, when choosing a room when booking, remember that some do not have a balcony, if this moment is important to you. According to tourists, the apartments are very spacious (with high ceilings) and well equipped. Of course, traces of scuffs on furniture are noticeable. Bathrobes and bedding are also far from new and need to be replaced. But as for the equipment in the room, it is all working and there are no problems with its operation.

It is worth noting that mosquitoes periodically appear in the rooms, so at night you should not leave windows and balcony doors open. Fumitox, which is provided for guests in the rooms, helps to cope with insects.

The bathroom has detergents, toothpaste, soap, gels, shampoos and toilet paper. All supplies are replenished by maids as needed. Three towels are provided for each guest, they are changed daily, but bed linen is available on request. On the whole, brochures are spread out in the rooms, asking you to change your clothes as little as possible. Such frugality will supposedly be rewarded with a souvenir. However, according to tourists, no surprises should be expected, this is just a successful advertising move.

As for cleaning at Liking Resort Sanya * (China), it is carried out regularly and at the same time it is of high quality, although not perfect. But overall the rooms are clean and comfortable. But the staff does not tip, even if they are left. And cash bonuses do not affect the quality of service. The hotel workers are attentive enough and are well disposed towards Russian tourists.

A little bit about the resort ...

The city resort of Sanya has excellent infrastructure, impeccable ecology and a wonderful tropical climate that allows you to relax here throughout the year. Sandy gently sloping beaches and the warmest sea attract couples with children here. The rich underwater world and clear water are ideal for diving enthusiasts. The resort has a wide selection of hotels of different levels, among which each tourist will be able to choose an acceptable option. Beach holidays are complemented by lush tropical nature, the beauty of which can be admired on excursions.

Dadonghai is the most popular resort among Russian tourists on Hainan Island. It is located near the city of Sanya and attracts supporters of outdoor activities. There are many entertainment venues, cafes and restaurants.

The beaches of the resort are located in the bay, their total length is about two kilometers. White sand, clear water and tropical trees are Dadonghai's business card. Even in the winter months, the temperature of sea water does not drop below twenty degrees.
