Do-it-yourself wood carving: how to prepare for work?

Do-it-yourself wood carving is a very interesting activity that will help not only to relax, but also allow you to decorate your home with new decorative elements. It should be noted that it is not difficult to learn this craft, a simple desire is enough. To begin with, you should choose the tool necessary for the work: a jamb knife, a penknife, a ruler, a simple pencil, wood, as well as a diagram or study guide.



Do-it-yourself wood carving should really please you, because otherwise, at the first failure, you quit this lesson. Knives should be sharp enough. If you like this craft, then later you will need to purchase a set of all kinds of chisels and incisors. You should also choose a suitable place for carving. This room should be cozy, quiet and well lit. In addition, it should not have any carpeting or other materials that can absorb dust, and from which it will be difficult to sweep the sawdust.

Do-it-yourself wood carving involves choosing the right material. For those people who are just learning this craft, you should train on soft woods: aspen, alder and linden. Beginners need to stock up on a decent amount of small workpieces. You also need to choose a specific technique for doing the work. Geometric carving is best for a beginner. The main technique of this technique is pruning and pricking.



Do-it-yourself carving involves observing a certain angle of the knife with respect to the surface of the wood. It should be 30-40 degrees.

Let us consider in more detail the method of cutting a simple triangle. First, note the middle of the figure. To do this, the knife must be kept strictly upright, and its end must enter the wood a few millimeters. From the made point, you need to make a pricking to the other vertices of the triangle. Now you can start cutting lines. If you hold the knife correctly, then you will break off a piece in the form of a pyramid.

Wood carving, for which it is not difficult to find sketches, provides for a gradual complication of the elements. Naturally, you need to do this only after you have certain skills and experience. This craft makes it possible to come up with your own style and bring it to life. Over time, you can not only process small blanks, but also make frames for paintings or mirrors, decorate furniture and other items, make souvenirs for friends.



Some elements can be decorated with through thread. But this requires some experience. Such wood carving for beginners is quite complicated and requires a lot of time. It should be noted that this craft fosters endurance, perseverance and patience. Naturally, you must be very careful when working with sharp tools. That's all the features of wood carving. Good luck
