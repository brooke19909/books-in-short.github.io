Reviews about Viva Ulaslar Hotel 4 *

Since today, when choosing a hotel, many travelers try, whenever possible, to study the comments of people who have already stayed in it, we offer you a generalized opinion of our compatriots about their vacation in Viva Ulaslar (Alanya, Turkey). It should be said right away that, as in the cases with other hotels, some of the reviews are positive, but still there are people who are unsatisfied with something. We offer to sort everything out in order.

As for the hotel itself and the rooms, there were no special complaints from our compatriots. Furniture and appliances in the rooms are not new, but everything is in good and working condition. The rooms are clean and nicely decorated. Cleaning was carried out efficiently and regularly. There were also no complaints about the frequency of changing linen and towels.

Very pleased guests with the location of the hotel. So, the distance to the beach is only a couple of hundred meters. In addition, near the hotel there are many shops and souvenir shops, as well as a bazaar with a large selection of products at very reasonable prices.

Several guests were unhappy that, having arrived at the hotel early in the morning, they settled only for dinner. However, most of the guests claim that a similar situation is possible only in the high season, when Viva Ulaslar is completely full. So, if the hotel had free rooms, then guests arriving much earlier than the checkout time (14:00) were settled almost immediately after arrival.

The Russians and the staff of Viva Ulaslar were pleasantly impressed. So, the hotel staff are very pleasant and friendly people and are always ready to help guests in any matter. Tourists who came to rest with children note the high professionalism of the animators who entertained the youngest travelers. According to them, the kids were delighted with them.

As for the beach, our compatriots were completely satisfied with it. It is located just two hundred meters. Sunbeds and umbrellas on it are comfortable and of high quality. The beach itself consists of sand and small pebbles. When entering the water there are no stones, so you can easily do without special shoes. The beach is regularly cleaned, so being on it is very comfortable.

Dining in the four-star Viva Ulaslar, according to most tourists, is consistent with the declared category of the hotel. The assortment of dishes, of course, is inferior to five-star hotels, but everything is very tasty and of high quality. Our compatriots were pleased that, in addition to chicken and meat, in the restaurant they were offered almost every day dishes of fish and seafood.

In general, summing up, we can say that the four-star Viva Ulaslar Hotel (Alania) fully met the expectations of travelers. According to most tourists, it is quite consistent with its category and value.
