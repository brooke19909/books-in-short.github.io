Appearance

The main task of such devices is to look and work where other smartphones cannot afford it. Our respondent fully complies with this requirement. Protection

The device is protected from moisture and dust according to IP68 class, that is, the device will feel fine and work at a depth of up to three meters, and without time limits. In addition, the smartphone is not afraid of falling onto bare asphalt from a height of human growth and a sharp change in temperature conditions.

Users in their reviews of the waterproof Blackview BV8000 Pro (IP68) are especially grateful to the designers for the lack of plugs while maintaining protective features. By introducing Capless USB technology, the manufacturer completely got rid of these ever-tearing and interfering elements.





The minijack for the headset also has no plugs. In this case, you should not be afraid of moisture: the technology has been thoroughly tested and field tested, so there is no reason for concern. Such solutions can often be found on A-group smartphones, such as the Galaxy S8 series from Samsung, the Xperia XZ from Sony, and the LG G6.

Screen

The device received an intelligent IPS-matrix, which can easily cope with high resolution at a resolution of 1920 by 1080 pixels (Full HD). An acceptable pixel density of 440 ppi allows you to give a good picture, as well as good contrast. The brightness margin is quite impressive, so on a sunny day the information is easily read from the screen.

In addition, viewing angles also pleased: the maximum angle for IPS-matrices allows you to quietly flip through photos or watch videos in the company of several like-minded people. In general, judging by the reviews on the Blackview BV8000 Pro (IP68), users are satisfied with the available screen capabilities. The results of a smartphone (by benchmarks and visually) are no worse than models from the premium segment, with the only difference being that the latter do not have such protection.

As mentioned above, the display received a reliable and impenetrable glass from Gorilla of the third series, coupled with a good oleophobic coating. In addition to the glass itself, there is still a factory film on top: it does not happen to be superfluous, and indeed there is none, but it provides protection. Moreover, the latter does not happen much.
