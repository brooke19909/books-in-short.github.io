Knitting summer blouses with knitting needles with a description (photo)

For real knitters, the end of summer should not be the reason to stop knitting summer blouses with knitting needles. With descriptions, diagrams, and inspirational photographs, the manufacture of such products can be continued year-round. Especially if the yarn is already bought, but work or household chores prevented in time from knitting something openwork and light.



Choose a model

This item is not useful for girls and women with a perfect figure, since they can afford to wear almost any wardrobe items. However, knitting summer blouses with knitting needles (with or without a description) can be a real problem for those who want to hide their shortcomings and emphasize their merits.

Openwork patterns are well suited for the manufacture of smooth, wide blouses. Wear a dark top as a lining. A silhouette will be visible through the holes of the canvas, but at the same time, all that is superfluous is hidden.

Do not wear transparent clothes without lining, as it almost always looks vulgar. The only exceptions are beach tunics designed for use with a swimsuit.

For owners of a beautiful bust, knitting summer blouses with knitting needles, with a description that provides a neckline , becomes an ideal option. Also, they can recommend models that are collected immediately below the chest and extended downward.

In the case when it is necessary to cover the shoulders and arms, you should pay attention to knitting summer blouses with knitting needles made of cotton with a deflated sleeve. A good example of such a product will be offered later.

Selection of yarn and knitting needles

Craftswomen with experience should not paint all the benefits of natural materials. For them, the obvious choice for summer products is cotton, viscose or linen.



Those who are just beginning to comprehend the wisdom of knitting art should know that acrylic and other artificial threads are suitable for knitting only elegant and festive items. Often these blouses become hot or hard. Acrylic can roll and stretch over time.

So all the same it is not necessary to save. It is better to immediately choose high-quality material and start knitting summer blouses with knitting needles (with a description of even very delicate patterns). Especially when it comes to clothing for children.



Knitting summer blouses with knitting needles made of cotton for overweight women

The photo shows a very successful and fairly simple model of an openwork blouse. It consists of two canvases, connected seamlessly with the sleeves, sewn along the sides and along the shoulder seams.



Such knitting of summer blouses with knitting needles for women does not differ in originality, but is considered one of the most practical techniques.

The pattern can be applied literally any. The main difficulty lies in the addition of loops for the sleeves, but in general the model knits quickly.

To get started, you need to tie a small fragment of the selected pattern and exactly the thread that you plan to apply to the product.

When the sample is ready, it should be washed and steamed with an iron. Now the canvas has taken its real density and can be measured. Depending on the composition and thickness of the yarn, as well as on the knitting technique, each craftswoman will get her own individual result. As a guide, we will take such a density: 22 loops per 10 cm and 18 rows per 10 cm.

Knitting summer blouses with knitting needles for overweight women (especially for such wide models) takes from 500 to 800 grams of yarn.

Product calculation

The diagram below shows an openwork pattern, as well as its rapport.



Rapport is the part of the ornament that repeats with a certain frequency. In this case, it is formed by six loops. When starting work, this fact should be taken into account.



To make a blouse for size 46, a cloth 49 cm wide is required. Therefore, you need to type:

49x22 / 10 = 108.

108-2 (edge) / 6 ( rapport loops ) = 17.6.

Round to a larger number and get 18 rapports. 18x6 + 2 = 110 loops.

After a set of loops, the first 2-3 cm should be knitted with a garter stitch.

Beveling for sleeves

Having knitted the necessary length of the part and approaching the armhole, you should calculate how many loops you need to add. This figure will depend on how long the knitter wishes to receive.

For the bevel in each front row, the following actions are performed:

  • Hem, double crochet.
  • Then they knit the fabric according to the pattern.
  • Double crochet before the last loop.

In the wrong row, the crochets are knitted and an expansion of the fabric is obtained. Gradually, new loops need to be involved in knitting a pattern.

Such additions are performed to a height of 3-4 cm (as indicated in the drawing with a pattern).

Then, to form the sleeve, the missing loops are gathered and a long fabric is knitted, which includes two sleeves and a part of the front or back. When collecting loops, you should remember about rapports and edge loops, otherwise the pattern may not converge.

The straps (garter stitch) on the sleeves can be knitted in the process of working on a wide fabric, that is, whole. A strapping made after stitching parts from loops typed on circular knitting needles will also look good.

Neck

To roll out had a beautiful round shape, you need to calculate it:

  • The width of the neck will be: 18 cm x 22 loops / 10 cm = 40 loops.
  • We will take 5 loops on each side to the bevel (total 10).

This means that you must first close the central 30 loops, and then cut one loop five times in each front row. Then knit exactly until the neck depth is 15 cm.

In the manufacture of a back part, such actions are optional. You can just make a flat top edge. Of course, if there is a neck, the model will look neater. In this case, its depth should be no more than 5 cm.

For strapping on circular knitting needles, all loops of the neck are gathered after stitching the parts. Using a scarf pattern will look organic, since it is already present in the product.

When making side seams, you need to leave small cuts. For a neat and finished look blouse, you can crochet these open edges.

Knitting summer blouses with knitting needles for women according to the described pattern allows craftswomen to quickly enrich their wardrobe with new products. Clothes with an ornament from braids or with a combined pattern look beautiful: braids and lace.
