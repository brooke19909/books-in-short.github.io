Hotel Royal Azur 5

Description. Royal Azur 5, located on the ecologically clean coast of Hurghada, in the Makadi Bay, is built on a fairly large landscaped area, surrounded by palm groves.



The hotel, part of the Swiss Inn hotel chain, was founded in 1998, and was completely renovated in 2011.

The hotel infrastructure includes a round-the-clock medical office, hairdresser, dry cleaning, luggage storage, currency exchange, laundry service and a conference room with all the necessary equipment. There are jewelry and perfume shops in the lobby, a shop selling papyrus, bookstores and souvenir stalls. The hotel has free parking, including indoor.



The 24-hour reception desk has a depository safe, a fax machine, here you can arrange a transfer, rent a vehicle, and order a taxi.

The hotel has a well-renovated banquet room, where a team of professionals helps to hold celebrations.

The distance to the nearest airport is 30 kilometers.

The rooms. Royal Azur 5 rooms (370 in total) are located in a three-story p-shaped building with two wings. There are also nine separate chalets built in a palm grove.

Room categories are deluxe, standard and superior. Each of them has a terrace or balcony, a standard set of furniture, a coffee table with armchairs, a large mirror, a TV that broadcasts satellite channels, including 2 Russians, a telephone with direct access, a minibar and individually controlled air conditioning.

In the combined bathrooms, in addition to the shower, bidet and washbasin, there is a hairdryer and everything necessary for hygiene.

Some rooms have hot drinks facilities. To access the Internet you need to pay extra. Upon request, you can get an ironing board, an iron, an alarm clock service.

The rooms are designed in bright and cheerful colors, uplifting. The floors in the hotel are tiled with small carpets.

Animals with a weight of not more than five kilograms are allowed.

Nutrition. Dining at Royal Azur 5 is offered according to the all-inclusive concept in the main restaurant (buffet). The menu alternates dishes from Mexican, European, Eastern and other cuisines.

Other restaurants - Fish Le Peshore, Italian El Rondo, Le Rivage and East El Mandra - work √† la carte.

There are also three bars: Tropicana (near the pool), a lobby where you can enjoy a delicious dessert, and a disco bar, which hosts entertainment programs.



Beach. Royal Azur 5 beach is private, with a sandy coating. You can reach the coral shallow through the breakwater. The entrance to the sea is uncomfortable in some places, so special shoes are required.

Additional Information. For children, Royal Azur offers babysitting services (upon request), a mini club, a playground for children, high chairs in the restaurant and a children's menu.

Adults can visit the gym, sauna, massage room, play billiards, mini-golf, table tennis, squash. Windsurfing and diving can be done with an instructor.

Digest. Royal Azur 5, reviews of which are only positive, in all respects corresponds to its category. Tourists praise the work of staff, catering and menus in restaurants; note the high quality of services. The hotel is suitable for families.
