The best resorts of Antalya

Antalya is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Turkey. It is located on the Mediterranean coast and attracts people from all over the world. The word "Antalya" is applicable both to a single city, and to the totality of famous resorts. It is about them that will be discussed in this article.

Antalya

You can not talk about the popular Turkish resorts and not to mention Antalya. This city is the largest on the Mediterranean coast. It is located 12 kilometers from the airport. At this resort you can find entertainment for every taste. Both outdoor enthusiasts and people who want to sunbathe on the beach can enjoy their stay in this city.





Antalya resorts are located in such a way that they are easily accessible. To get to the city of the same name, you need to take a plane, use water transport or make a trip by car. At 12 kilometers from the city is an international airport. True, direct access to Antalya by sea will not work. Will have to make a transplant and overcome the remaining path on another transport. The best hotel in Antalya is Barut Lara, which is part of the world-famous hotel chain Barut Hotels. You can get to it by bus and taxi. The status that almost all the resorts of Antalya have is 5 stars. All Inclusive is the most sought after concept, and the best hotels serve their customers using this system.

History

Antalya is a city with a long history. Currently, it is completely built up with modern buildings, it has a very well-developed infrastructure. However, this was not always the case.

The history of Antalya began in 159 BC, when the subjects of King Attalos 2, on the orders of the ruler, went in search of the most beautiful place on the planet. Then the city bore another name given to him in honor of the king - Attalia. In the 13th century AD, after the Seljuks took possession of the city, it became known as Adalia. It was changed to a name familiar to modern man in 1423, when Antalya became part of the Ottoman Empire. And only in 1923 the Republic of Turkey was founded here.

What to see in Antalya?

Antalya resorts attract tourists not only by the large number of beaches and shops. Arriving on the Mediterranean coast of Turkey, any traveler will discover the wonders of local architecture. The most rich in monuments of architecture is the resort of Antalya (Turkey). Since this settlement has a centuries-old history, the art of many civilizations has influenced its architecture. Greek, Ottoman, Byzantine and Seljuk buildings are still preserved on the territory of the city.





The resort city of Antalya in Turkey is divided into two equal parts. One of them, modern, attracts tourists with a developed infrastructure and an abundance of various shops. The second part, or the Old Town, fascinates with monuments of ancient architecture. This is where most of the old buildings, wooden houses are located. What are the most popular sights of the old city?

  • Kesik Minaret, or the Truncated Minaret. In ancient times, a temple was located in its place, later turned into the church of the Virgin Mary. In the 15th century, by order of the Ottoman prince Shehzade Korkut, a mosque was built here, and a minaret was erected next to it. In 1851, a terrible fire occurred, as a result of which the upper part of the minaret burned down. Therefore, he got his name.
  • The symbol of the city is the Yivli minaret, erected in 1230. Its height is 38 meters. This attraction can be seen from anywhere in Antalya. Eight half-columns of the tower are decorated with mosaics, which is why the minaret got its name. Translated, the word "Yivli" means "grooved."

Alanya

At 120 kilometers from the international airport is a popular resort called Alanya. This place is very popular among young people and couples. Here you can find fairly cheap hotels, as well as beautiful long beaches.

Antalya resorts are simultaneously cities. So, Alanya cannot be called a large settlement, but this does not prevent it from being popular. The city has picturesque orange and lemon gardens. Here, fortresses of the Seljuk era are still preserved. In the old town there are shops, fish restaurants, courtyards for tea parties and much more. All resorts of Antalya, including Alanya, have a developed infrastructure. This city has comfortable hotels, clubs, bars, shops and restaurants. Nightlife is centered on the outskirts.





Alanya has a large number of attractions. These are not only ancient fortresses that were already mentioned in the article, but also caves. The most popular is Damlatas. 15,000 years ago, people who suffered from a particular illness came to be treated in this grotto. The cave is the best suited for the treatment of asthma, as well as other respiratory diseases. Damlatash is not losing popularity at present.

Alanya can be classified as the "Best Resorts of Antalya" due to the local beaches. To the west of the city is a sandy beach called Cleopatra. Its length is three kilometers. On the opposite side, you can find the eight-kilometer beach of Alanya. On both beaches, tourists can enjoy water sports and choose entertainment for every taste. Relax after an active vacation at the Michell Hotel & Spa, which is the best in this city.

Belek

Belek is located 25 kilometers from the airport. This resort is ideal for outdoor enthusiasts. In autumn 1994, in September, the national golf club was opened, which is still one of the largest in the world. Belek regularly hosts golf tournaments.





Photos of the resorts of Antalya, including the hotel zone called Belek, are presented in the article. One of the world's largest five-star hotel projects has been implemented in this city. Since 1992, construction of comfortable or luxury hotels has been carried out here, the most prestigious of which is Maxx Royal Belek Golf & SPA 5 *. Those buildings that are currently appearing in the city are located in a pine forest.

Belek is a popular resort for people who love sports. So, everyone will be able to choose a lesson for everyone. Here you can ride a "banana" or water skiing, visit shady courts, and try yourself as a windsurfer. The hotels have polyglot animators that will not let you get bored.

Kemer

Young people and outdoor enthusiasts come to the resorts of Antalya. "5 stars" is a status that is not assigned to hotels just like that. Kemer has a large number of comfortable and luxury hotels. VIP rooms of the premium Marti Myra 5 * hotel are especially popular . Bars, restaurants, discos - all this attracts tourists to Kemer, located between the mountains and the sea. It gained popularity due to coniferous forests, orange groves, pebble beaches. Dreamers and romantics also frequent this resort. The fact is that this place is surrounded by secrets and mysteries.





Kemer has a large number of restaurants and cafes where you can drink real Turkish coffee. The beaches are sand and pebble, and they are absolutely free. Due to its location, this resort is also a prestigious yacht center. The local pier accommodates 180 yachts at once!

Kundu

This resort was built recently, but this did not prevent it from gaining great popularity. This place is best for families with children. Kundu is famous for its several luxurious hotel complexes. One of them exactly copies the Topkapi palace complex in Istanbul, and the other repeats the sights of Venice.





Kundu is attractive for its large sandy beaches, like other resorts in Antalya. There are luxury hotel complexes such as Aska Lara Resort & Spa, Baia Lara, Delphin Diva and others. Here you can choose entertainment to taste, as well as choose the best beach in order to enjoy the sea.

Side

Side is located 60 kilometers from the international airport. This resort is very popular with couples in love. According to legend, once Cleopatra and Mark Anthony arranged a romantic meeting here. It is in the Kaya, Sentido Perissia Hotel and Commodore Elite Suites & Spa hotels that couples most often stay in love or just make an appointment in restaurants. In addition, this place is the best suited for families with children. The name of the resort city is translated as "pomegranate", which, as you know, is a symbol of fertility.





Antalya is famous for many attractions. Side Resort is no exception. In this city, ancient buildings are located, for example, the ruins of the temple of the goddess Fortune and an amphitheater, in which 15 thousand spectators could simultaneously be. The modern city is full of various shops, restaurants, cafes and hotels. Here everyone can choose entertainment to taste, as well as enjoy the nature of Turkey.
