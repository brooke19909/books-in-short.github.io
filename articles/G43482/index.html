How to take screenshots on Samsung: learn to take screenshots

The capabilities of modern mobile devices never cease to amaze. Gadgets can perform different functions. With their help today you can go online, make a video call, watch a video on the network or exchange messages. Multimedia letters containing various kinds of screenshots are very popular. Next, we have to understand how to take screenshots on the Samsung. What kind of process is this? All the features of the process will be described below.

A screenshot is ...

The first step is to figure out which document is in question. What is called a screenshot?





The subject under study is a screenshot. Every modern computer user is familiar with screenshots of the desktop. For mobile devices, screenshot means the same thing. You can call it a kind of photograph of what is happening on the phone.

But how to take screenshots on the Samsung? Is it possible to somehow quickly realize an idea?

Ways to take screenshots

Yes. As practice shows, there are a huge variety of techniques that help solve the problem. What is it about?

To date, among the main ways to create screenshots, the following solutions are distinguished:

  • use of function buttons;
  • the use of a special stylus (SPen);
  • use of hardware settings;
  • work with third-party applications.

It should be noted right away that different keyboard shortcuts work on different Samsung models. Therefore, you have to study all kinds of options. Remembering them is not as difficult as it seems.





Stylus

How to take screenshots on the Samsung? The first way is to use a stylus called SPen. This method is not very common in practice, but it sometimes makes life easier.

So, to create a screenshot of any Samsung phone with a stylus, you need to:

  1. Open the desired pages on a mobile device.
  2. Press the SPen button.
  3. Double-tap the touch screen with the stylus.

If everything was done correctly, the owner of the gadget will see a message on the screen that the image was successfully saved. Now it can be opened, edited, sent in a message or deleted. Nothing difficult, incomprehensible or special!

Keyboard shortcut

Next, we proceed to a more interesting method. How to take a screenshot on Samsung Galaxy and any other models of mobile phones from Samsung? By using a variety of combinations of function buttons. Depending on the model of the smartphone and the installed operating system, certain techniques will help a person.

If you are wondering exactly how you can take screenshots on Samsung, it is recommended that you thoroughly study all the possible ways for Android operating systems. Most tips help you take a screenshot on Samsung. Most often, the following options are effective:

  1. Turn on the mobile phone. Press the button to turn off the smartphone, as well as on "Home". Keep the controls pressed until a distinctive click of the camera appears. The buttons must be pressed simultaneously.
  2. Open the desired page on your mobile phone. Press the "Turn off" and "Volume down" buttons. Such a solution is extremely rare on Samsung. Usually it is recommended for use on other phones with Android.
  3. After opening the document / page, press simultaneously on the "Home" and the "Back" button. Keep the controls pressed for a few seconds.
  4. Use the device shutdown button. In the functional menu, select the option "Screenshot". This method is extremely rare on Samsung.

Hardware features

If you do not want to play with buttons, you can go the other way. Today, Samsung allows you to control mobile phones using gestures. This option will help to create a screenshot. But how? How to take a screenshot?

On Samsung A3 and other Samsung models, you can use the following algorithm of actions:

  1. Enable gadget. Open its main menu after full download.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Carefully study the menu and select "Gesture Management" there.
  4. Visit the "Hand Movement" section.
  5. Select the line that says Palm swipe to capture.
  6. Open the document necessary for fixing on the display.
  7. Swipe your hand (palm) across the screen from left to right.

The actions taken will result in the user seeing the process of saving the resulting image. You can exit the settings and work with a screenshot.
