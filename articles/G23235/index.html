VTB phone insurance: features, insured event, "pitfalls", reviews

Today, for many, a telephone is not just a device for making calls and sending SMS messages. This is an organizer, a media player, a mobile bank, a full-fledged alternative to a bank card and a personal data storage. For us, a malfunction or a sudden loss of such a device will be very painful, taking into account the cost of the smartphone itself - many people manage to buy this equipment only on credit. Therefore, buyers, without hesitation, agree to the offer of a consultant to insure their gadget. Here we will tell you the whole truth about VTB phone insurance, an insured event, a statement about its occurrence.

Portable + Program

The product "VTB Insurance", specially designed for smartphones, laptops, tablets, cameras, cameras and other portable equipment, has a similar name - "Portable +". It can be purchased in stores of M.Video, Know-How, Euroset, Eldorado, Svyaznoy networks right away when you buy a brand new smartphone.





VTB phone insurance is quite attractive to many buyers. The company offers you compensation for damage in the following cases:

  • Mechanical damage to the device.
  • Theft, robbery, robbery, hooligan actions.
  • Drowning, flooding, destructive effects of other liquids.
  • Fire, explosion.
  • Write-off of large amounts of money from the number when the device is stolen.

What about an insured event? What you should know

VTB phone insurance: insured event

In order for you to compensate for the damage in case of any trouble that occurs, you need to act exclusively according to the plan proposed by the insurer:

  1. Do not try to fix it, fix the device yourself.
  2. Call the phone number specified in your contract and tell the operator in detail about everything that happened. False information will not be used to your advantage.
  3. Depending on what happened to the device, you need to report it to the appropriate authority no later than 24 hours after the incident: the internal affairs bodies, the fire service, emergency services, the Ministry of Emergencies, and the mobile operator.
  4. After that, you need to contact the warranty service center, where they will assess the damage caused to your gadget.
  5. Then you must provide the insurance company with a full package of documents: a copy of the insurance policy, warranty card, passport of the insured; the originals of the documentation confirming your purchase of the phone (cash and receipt receipt, slip, slip, account statement - the main thing is that the submitted document could accurately determine the name of the gadget, its cost and date of purchase), documents from the competent authorities, a statement about the occurrence of insurance a case with your signature, indicating the date of preparation and bank details; the conclusion of the service center, where the IMEL of the device or its service number is indicated, an accurate description of all the revealed damages, the cost of damage, a certification seal.
  6. After you provide VTB Insurance with all the documents, within 15 days the detected amount of damage will be transferred to the bank account indicated by you in the application.




As you can see, to get compensation for the insurance of VTB phones, you need to make a lot of effort.

Denial of compensation

The insurer may rightfully refuse to indemnify you for:

  • The firmware (software) was replaced on its own or not at the official service center, after which the gadget stopped working.
  • Battery malfunction - does not hold a charge.
  • Sticky buttons.
  • Poor touch screen response.
  • Malfunctioning speaker device.
  • Phone unrecognized SIM card.
  • Damage to the charging connector.
  • Restart or shut down the device yourself.
  • Inability to connect to the Internet and / or download applications.
