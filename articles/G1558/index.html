Where to spend your vacation in the Lipetsk region? Holiday Village: which one to choose?

With the approach of summer, many begin to look for suitable vacation spots in the Lipetsk region. Everyone seeks to choose a cozy corner in nature, where you can go for one day or for several weeks. Typically, the choice falls on a vacation in Zadonsk, Lipetsk region, since there are the largest number of tourist centers. This area is famous for its abundance of picturesque forests, tracts, ravines and Don open spaces. Local recreation centers offer not only to enjoy the pristine nature and its clean air, but also swim from the heart in clean reservoirs.

The recreation center "The Seagull"

If you are looking for a comfortable and affordable vacation in the Lipetsk region, the base "Seagull" may be a good option. It is located on the banks of the river. Don surrounded by a mixed forest. They offer to settle in a comfortable cottage for two or three places. On the territory of the base there are children's and sports grounds. Walking paths can lead you to the coffee bar. Also among the activities there are paintball and billiards.

"Blue light"

This complex is located 3 km from Zadonsk - on the banks of the river. Don. It welcomes guests all year round and at the same time accommodates 280 guests. The rooms can accommodate single people and families: from one to five vacationers. There are buildings in which it is good to live in the cold season, as well as summer houses.



The recreation center has a good infrastructure. There are several sports fields, a playground, a billiard room, and in winter there is a ski run. Its length reaches two kilometers. On the river bank there is a beach with umbrellas, awnings, catamarans, boats, boats. Available and fishing. Holidays in the Lipetsk region in the "Blue Light" can be diversified by horseback riding and hiking. There is a sauna and a bath. At the base there is a dining room for 80 visitors and a cozy cafe with a capacity of 120 seats. There are barbecue areas and barbecue facilities within the base.

It is worth noting that corporate events and leisure can be combined here. In the Lipetsk region, the base "Blue Light" is one of the most popular in terms of conferences, seminars, business meetings. There is a conference room where up to 100 people can freely sit. This place is equipped with air conditioning, new equipment. In addition, there is computer equipment. There is a connection and Wi-Fi. Since the camp site has a spacious bar that seats 120 people and a separate dining room, many people like to organize banquets and other events here. For example, weddings, corporate meetings, anniversaries are held here. Additionally, it is possible to order an entertainment program from the administration of the base.

The Quiet Don



This base is located in a secluded place among the mixed forest on the banks of the river. The property has fully equipped wooden houses with a kitchen. But you can eat in the local dining room, where homemade dishes are prepared. The base has a library, as well as a Russian bath. Vacationers can take a walk in the forest to pick berries or mushrooms. In addition, the base offers guests to visit a clean beach where you can soak up the sun and swim in the Don River. It organizes excursions to Zadonsk, as well as to the historical corners of the region. Nearby are male and female monasteries, where you can also go on a guided tour.

Russian Manor



Comfortable holiday homes in the Lipetsk region are not only on the banks of the river. Don. Near the Matyr reservoir in the arms of a pine forest is the "Russian Estate". This is a complex with its infrastructure and wooden houses for living. You can come here by your own transport and leave the car in a guarded free parking. This place is conveniently located - only 15 minutes from the city.

The base offers accommodation in one of the houses built of timber. Each room has a bathroom, TV, fridge, hot water. The camp site is equipped for a comfortable stay. There is a bathhouse, a pond, fishing, a playground for children, and a cafe are available. There is an opportunity to rent sports equipment. You can come to rest at any time of the year. An additional pleasant moment is that the furniture in the room and in the cafe is made in the same style of natural wood - especially for the "Russian Estate".

"Brigantine"



The Brigantine invites guests to rest in the Lipetsk Region all year round. The recreation center was built near the Matyr reservoir. Here you can get a boost of energy thanks to clean air, a beautiful beach and beautiful nature. The hostel is suitable for families with children. It is worth noting that there are many sanatoriums and recreation centers on the Matyr reservoir, but the Brigantine and the Russian Manor are considered the largest of them. They also have their own access to the beach. Given the price category, rest in the "Brigantine" will cost a little cheaper than in the "Russian Estate".
