About how to make a ring from a coin

If you are on this page, then you are interested in the question of how to make a ring out of a coin. Something like a virtual master class on this subject is presented to your attention. Everyone learns this differently. Someone is reading materials from foreign authors, someone is watching a video. The material presented here will become another source, enabling everyone to make a ring from an ordinary coin.

Choose a coin

Considering that people live in different corners of the earth, in which, accordingly, their coins go in circulation, we will not focus on any particular one. Use the one available in your country.

So how to make a ring out of a coin? First, you need to red-hot our monetary unit on fire. This will allow you to work with the material without making much effort. Only you should always remember one important detail. You can not go too far with heating, because otherwise the coin can distort its shape, which will lead to loss of visibility of some elements.





When the metal turns red and just starts to turn white, stop heating and quickly lower the coin into water, preferably cold. Just don‚Äôt be afraid of the hissing sound, this is normal. This is the first stage ends.

Drill

We continue to understand how to make a ring out of a coin. Now you need to carefully and without fanaticism drill a hole in the very center, which should not be too large, since this will entail the exit of too thin walls at the finished ring. This can be done on a special machine or using a drill.





When the hole is ready, take the bolt and put a coin on it through the freshly baked hole. Next, we take a pre-prepared plastic hammer (note that it must be precisely plastic, since the metal one will destroy all the elements) and we begin leisurely, evenly pull the ring down. Avoid strong impacts that could warp our product.

So we almost figured out how to make a ring out of a coin. When the material has become the size we need, we stop the stretch. Do not forget that it must be periodically turned over so that it does not take a conical shape. Now we only need to process the decoration.

Treatment

For the final finish, take sandpaper and carefully go through the entire plane. Then, with the help of a grinding paper wheel and goy paste, we will achieve the final and quite impressive effect. We hope you have learned how to make a ring out of a coin, and you can repeat it yourself at home.

Finally, let's list the equipment that we needed at the time of manufacture. Of course, this is a coin that we turn into a ring. We also used a crossbar, a plastic hammer to fit the future ring to the desired size, a drill for making holes, and sandpaper with a grinding wheel and goy paste to polish the finished product. If you learn how to make rings in this way, you will have a good opportunity to earn on the sale of such products.
