Knitted scarf - fashion accessory for the cold winter

A warm and practical scarf knitted from wool blend yarn will become an indispensable accessory in the cold season. Of course, this is a shirt shirt. Knitting or crocheting a knitted accessory will be a stylish detail of any image.





Say the word about the shirt-front

What is this type of scarf? In fact, this is a poncho cut off at shoulder level with a high, sometimes doubled neck. This form provides additional protection from the cold, not only the throat, but also the shoulders. By the way, this scarf model is perfect for both children and adults.



The shirt front, knitted with needles, can become not only the subject of a winter wardrobe. Depending on which thread is used, it can easily fulfill the role of a cape in the cool summer.





There are many types of such accessories. They depend on how the shirt shirt is made. With knitting needles, it can be connected both seamlessly and with decorative connectors. Such a division is not only aesthetic, but also practical, especially when it comes to a children's model.

In addition, the performance technique differs depending on who it is intended for. Female models are distinguished by the diversity of the use of patterns and decorative elements in the form of flowers. Knitting a shirt-front with knitting needles for men, as a rule, is limited by rigor: Scandinavian patterns, "bumps" and "braids" are the most common motifs for them.





Despite these subtleties, knitting such an accessory is a fascinating task, you just have to try.

How to knit a shirt front with knitting needles?

First of all, it is worth choosing a thread and knitting needles. Since the shirt-front rests on bare skin, it is better to give your preference to half-woolen threads. The product from them will gently envelop the neck. In addition, for the "feather test" it is worth choosing a melange thread, it will create its original pattern in the process of knitting. Circular knitting needles should be selected according to the recommendations given in the yarn passport.

There are two principles for knitting a shirt front. So, in the first option, you should start with knitting the neck, and the second suggests starting with the shoulder.

The technique of knitting from the neck. The number of loops, a multiple of 4., is dialed onto the knitting needles. It is quite simple to calculate the required amount: the neck volume in centimeters is calculated and divided by 10. After that, the resulting number is multiplied by the number of loops indicated in the yarn certificate for ten centimeters of the product.

The dialed loops are knitted with a simple or English rubber to the height of the neck. As a rule, it is 12-16 centimeters. Then all the loops are visually divided into four parts. At the beginning and at the end of each part from one front loop in each odd row, two are knitted. Thus, another thirty-six rows are executed, after which the loops are closed.





In order to avoid seams, you can use stocking knitting needles. A tied shirt shirt is best for adult models. But for children it is best to connect it with a seam, in the edges of which you can sew a zipper in order to facilitate the dressing process.

A shirt-scarf, knitted with needles or made of knitted material, will be a wonderful winter accessory. Especially if you master a variety of techniques for its implementation.
