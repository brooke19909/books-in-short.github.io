Where are the largest aquariums in the world?

The underwater world is striking in its colorful, diverse, originality. But unfortunately, not everyone who wants to get acquainted with the inhabitants of the seas and oceans can fall under the water. It was for this purpose that aquariums were created. With the help of modern technologies, people were able to create huge containers for keeping marine inhabitants. Thanks to this, everyone can enjoy turtles, sharks, stingrays, different types of fish in their immediate vicinity. The largest aquariums in the world are breathtaking and make you believe that a person is under water.



Almost every lover of the ocean can have fish at home and enjoy them for hours. But sometimes you want something more, for this the largest aquariums in the world are created to show visitors a more realistic picture of the underwater kingdom. One of the largest oceanariums is located in Beijing, but it is not real, but virtual. This is a miracle of technology, consisting of many LED screens, installed at a 25-meter height between two shopping centers. Such an amazing sight cannot be described in words.

Another place in the list of "The largest aquariums in the world" should be given to the cylindrical giant from Berlin, located at the Radisson Hotel. Its height is 25 m, the number of inhabitants exceeds 2.5 thousand. For a thorough inspection of all types of fish, an elevator is provided, moving on which visitors can enjoy the sea view.



If you are interested in the question of where is the largest aquarium with tropical inhabitants, then you should go to hot Africa, or rather to Durban. "Ushaka Marine World" is located in one of the most beautiful amusement parks. The aquarium itself consists of three dozen reservoirs of water, they are the inhabitants of the Indian Ocean. The giant from western Australia is striking in its grandeur. In the city of Hillaris, the AQWA aquarium is located, in which about 400 species of different marine inhabitants from this continent are concentrated.

Since Japan is surrounded by the ocean, it is understandable why this country has built such a huge oceanarium. In the list of "The largest aquariums in the world" "Okinawa Churaumi" takes second place. The volume of this giant is 7500 m 3 , a huge number of species of living creatures taken from the ocean are concentrated in it, including stingrays, sharks. Another huge aquarium is located in Atlanta (USA), its volume is 31,000 m 3 , about 100 thousand inhabitants of the seas and oceans live there, including stingrays, belugas, and whale sharks.



If you want to visit a fairy tale, then you should go to Dubai. In the shopping center there is an aquarium occupying 3 floors, its volume is 10 million liters. Everyone can enjoy the underwater world, walking along the transparent panel or through the tunnel, examining this aquarium at 270 degrees. The largest oceanarium in Moscow has been opened relatively recently, but it is still there and is happy to host guests. Of course, it can not be compared with the giants mentioned above, but do not forget that the city is not located near the sea or ocean, so it was difficult for the authorities to get underwater inhabitants and arrange premises for their comfortable living. Whatever the aquarium, small or large, it has a calming effect on a person, so you need to go to such establishments more often and enjoy the smooth and measured life of fish.
