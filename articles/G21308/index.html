Original party outfit - clown costume

Practically at every children's holiday there are joyful clowns bringing fun and always associated with funny tricks and comic situations. If at a fun party you want to be in the spotlight or to make the attention of guests focused on the child, then you should opt for a clown costume. It‚Äôs hard not to recognize a funny character thanks to bright clothes, an unusual headdress and an unforgettable nose. Sewing such an outfit will be discussed in the article.

What are the components of the outfit

A clown costume will be an excellent choice for both an adult and a child. It is suitable for both boys and girls. The main rule is bright colors that will make the image unforgettable.

If we talk about templates, you can easily take any picture you like from the pages of a magazine or book. And you can do everything intuitively, trusting your own creativity. It is important not to forget about the little things in the details that will serve as bright complementary parts of the clown costume.

Immediate parts that cannot be dispensed with

The outfit will not be complete without the following details:

  • A jumpsuit made of bright fabric that has sleeves or straps. However, it can be replaced with trousers with suspenders and a jacket. If you plan to create a female image, then you can limit yourself to a voluminous skirt and moose.
  • An adult can always complement the outfit with a bright tie and jacket. These items of clothing are ideal decorative elements.
  • Without special shoes, the clown costume would not be complete. It can be stylish boots or bright sports sneakers.
  • A wig with multi-colored hair is suitable for your head, which color everyone decides to give preference to.
  • Another important trifle is a false nose made of foam rubber, instead of which you can use makeup.
  • It is important not to forget about the headdress, cap or hat with bells.

Image selection

Not all clowns are funny and carefree, among them there are sad or even evil images. It all depends on the theme of the party. You can decide on the appearance with the help of books or some films to make an author's version of the costume. The main condition is to match the selected image to the age group and character traits.

How to make a costume for a child

Having considered some important little things, it‚Äôs worth moving on to creating a clown costume with your own hands. There is a place for things that are already safely forgotten. Pants made of bright fabric, to which straps or suspenders are sewn. Knee-high socks, t-shirts or sweaters can also be put here. It is easy to decorate additional items of clothing with the help of decorative buttons, and bright multi-colored shreds.

Of course, you need to think about additional accessories, which include:

  • headdress;
  • foam nose;
  • wig.

Shoes with rounded toes or bright sports shoes are ideal as shoes.

If you are planning to use shorts or breeches in a clown costume for a boy, then the different color socks that can be seen on clowns in the circus will serve as an ideal complement.

For the collar and cuffs, any improvised materials are suitable, it can be cardboard or paper. Makeup is a great tool to brighten your cheeks, paint a smile, and color your nose. The easiest makeup option will be to apply freckles and blush.

So, the questions with how to make a clown costume for a boy with his own hands should no longer remain.

Adult Costume

The option that will be presented below is perfect to make an outfit for a party for an adult. However, you can always use your own template.

To create a jumpsuit you will need bright fabrics, it is best to focus on contrasting shades. The clasp in this clown costume for an adult will be located on the back. After finishing sewing work, you need to think over jewelry, which can be played by large buttons, multi-colored patches, and several patch pockets. An interesting addition will be frill.

As shoes, boots from different pairs are suitable. In the form of decoration, you can use lush bows, which are easy to make from bright ribbons. With the help of baggy covers, any pair of shoes is perfectly masked.

How to make a hat

The perfect complement to the clown costume is a hat or a bowler hat, you can make them using improvised materials.

  1. On a thick sheet of paper, draw a semicircle and then cut it out.
  2. According to this template, a pattern is made from fabric with a margin of several centimeters at the edges.
  3. A tissue is adhered to the paper. The base is folded into a cone and secured with glue.
  4. It remains to attach the elastic band from below so that the headgear holds tightly.
  5. As an original addition, you can put a wide strip of fabric on the fringe, which is then glued to the cap. A pompom is made from sintepon or foam rubber and sheathed on top with a cloth.

Using the recommendations presented in the article, you can create an original clown costume and feel free to go on a holiday. A bright outfit is sure to be remembered by all participants of the event, not only adults, but also children will like it. And if a child comes in a clown costume for a holiday, then his attention is guaranteed, it is difficult to resist the sight of the original dress and not to devote proper time to the owner of the clown costume.

The article presented various costumes for both children and adults, and therefore there should not be any difficulties with sewing.
