Compliments for hotel guests

Hotel Laguna Vista Beach Resort 5 *, like other resort and hotel complexes, maintains a tradition of bonuses, surprises and compliments for its guests. In order to become a member of the loyalty program, just book a room or buy a tour package. So, the hotel receives the following compliments:

  • Upon arrival at the hotel, the newlyweds receive a bonus in the form of a room category upgrade (the service is available only if the hotel is not completely filled). There is also a large fruit basket. In addition, the newlyweds will enjoy a pleasant surprise in the form of decorating the room with petals of fresh flowers and figures from towels. The desire to participate in the bonus program must be reported at the stage of booking the tour, as well as during registration. Do not forget to present the marriage certificate to the administrator.
  • Tourists celebrating their birthday at the hotel receive a compliment in the form of a birthday cake. To receive a surprise, you must indicate your date of birth during registration, and also remind the administrator about it a couple of days before the solemn event.
  • Regular guests (these include tourists who have visited the hotel two or more times) when checking in receive a bonus in the form of a plate with exotic fruits. In addition, in the event that the hotel occupancy is incomplete, a room upgrade is provided.
