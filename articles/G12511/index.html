Cosmonaut costume with papier mache and bottles

If the child demanded that the parents wear an astronaut's costume for the New Year or other event, you should not immediately run to the store. A beautiful outfit can be made quickly and easily at home.





What is included in the equipment of the astronaut?

In order for a child to look like a real astronaut, you need to understand how the cosmonaut's suit differs, for example, from a diver's outfit.

In professional clothing of astronauts are always present:

  1. Helmet with a transparent visor.
  2. Shiny boots.
  3. The jumpsuit is white or orange.
  4. Jetpack.

What is the suit or material choice made of?

The clothes of the astronauts are made of durable and refractory composite materials. Children 's cosmonaut costume can be made from what is on the farm.

The materials that will help to make the outfit are as follows:

  • cardboard;
  • large plastic bottles;
  • felt or color cardboard;
  • old newspapers and an inflatable balloon;
  • glue, insulating tape;
  • rubber boots of the child;
  • a strip of fabric for the manufacture of straps - fasteners;
  • food foil.

Parents should not be confused by the penny cost of the necessary materials. If you perform the work carefully and with a soul, the result will please not only the child, but also those around him.

Cosmonaut Costume Design

Work sequence

So, the design is ready, all the materials are collected, it's time to start making the cosmonaut's costume with your own hands.

To make a space helmet from papier-m√¢ch√©, you need to inflate a balloon, tie the neck well and paste over it with old newspapers. In order for the product to be durable and keep in shape, 10-15 layers of newspaper and PVA glue are needed. The future helmet should stand 12 hours and dry. Then carefully cut the inspection hole with scissors.





Then you need paints and brushes, glue and a plastic bottle. The headpiece should be painted and attached with a transparent plastic visor.

Cosmonaut's shoes are very simple to make. You need to take rubber boots and gently wrap them with food foil. You can fix the result with silver or black electrical tape.





The most important stage of work is the manufacture of a jetpack. To do this, you need to take two large plastic bottles, they can be painted or pasted with electrical tape. You need to fasten the bottles together and stick to the cardboard backing. It is worth making holes in the corners in advance and inserting the straps into them so that you can put it on like a satchel.





Space rocket pack is ready! It can be decorated with "flame" exhausts and metal rivets made of felt or color cardboard.





Parents should involve a child in the creation of the costume. The joint work will bring a lot of joyful and unforgettable impressions, and the kid will be proud that he helped in the work on the space suit.
