How to Crochet Footprints

The crochet process is no less exciting than knitting. Using a hook to knit is even more convenient and easier. With it, it‚Äôs really possible to create the most intricate and unusual patterns, and products connected in this way are also very diverse. It can be sweaters, dresses, trousers, as well as soft toys, useful household trifles and everything you can imagine. To begin with, you can try to crochet the tracks, it is very simple and fast enough.





For knitting footprints of size 38, buy 100 grams of acrylic yarn. It is better not to use natural wool yarn, it will be quickly wiped and the product will not last you long. Having crocheted the tracks, you will be satisfied, because not everyone loves slippers , but any person will like the tracks. These slippers can be made in one evening, but if you are doing this for the first time, it may take a little longer. Having received the finished product, you will never forget how to crochet the tracks , and will use this method again.

Crochet footprint instructions

Type an air chain of 6 loops. Lock it in a ring with a connecting column.

First row. We knit carefully 3 loops aerial each other, they correspond to your first double crochet. We knit 11 double crochet, each time we introduce a hook into the ring. Close the row by connecting it in a column, introducing the hook into the air loop at the very beginning of the row.

Second row. We knit 3 air loops. Now we make an increase, in other words, we need to knit 1 crochet column into this loop of the base. We knit each subsequent loop, but we do this with 2 double crochet stitches.

Third row. We knit three air loops. Then we knit 2 double crochet - all this in the first

a loop. So you need to repeat ten times, and in the last loop knit 2 columns with the usual yarn. Close the circle, as usual, with a connecting column. So in the third row you get 36 columns.

The fourth row and all the rest can now be knitted without increases, we knit in three air stitches, 1 column each, with the crochet of the loop of the last row. Each time, close the row again with a connecting column.

Now we crochet the tracks with the help of the reverse and straight rows, tying our future sole.

Fifteenth row. It is necessary to knit only 3 loops, and then 21 columns with a simple crochet, but at the end of the 15th row we knit 3 loops again.

Now turn our knitting in the opposite direction and continue,

only in the opposite direction do a single crochet in each loop. You must repeat this procedure 7 times. Now you need to turn out our track, fold in half and knit 1 row of single crochet. Connect 2 halves and you get a seam on the heel. Tear the thread and twist it back to the front side. Crochet the tracks 1 with a row of posts. Knit another footprint similarly to the first.

If desired, crocheted footprints can be decorated with embroidery patterns. Tracks for girls and women can be decorated with knitted flowers of different colors. For boys and men, you can tie footprints of dark colors and without any decorations.
