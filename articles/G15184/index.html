The most expensive apartment in the world. Examples of luxury real estate and its description

Experts from international companies have analyzed the publicly available offers of the most elite living space on the planet. Where is she - the most expensive apartment in the world? Experts conducted a comparative analysis of the cost of 1 square. m in the largest cities on Earth. Where are the most expensive apartments? Of course, in Hong Kong. This city has for several years been a leader in total prices per square meter of housing.

The most expensive apartments in the world. Photos of apartments and penthouses

Below, the reader will be able to get acquainted with the elite housing of the planet. The indicated prices can vary within a thousand euros, both in plus and minus.

London, England)

The most expensive apartment in the UK - luxury apartments in London. The cost per square meter is about 72,060 euros. The area is 320 meters squared, the penthouse is estimated at 23 million euros.

The apartments are located in the center of the capital of Great Britain in the Hyde Park area. The house is modern built, inside the best designers made a magnificent repair. Today, this housing is the most expensive apartment in the world.



Monte Carlo (Monaco)

70 thousand euros - meter. Three bedroom luxury apartments located in the prestigious quarter of San Roman. The total area of ​​this penthouse is one hundred and forty meters squared. Chic repairs and great views of the sea.

Monaco is famous for having the most expensive real estate in Europe. The average prices in this principality range from 34-53 thousand euros per meter.

Hong Kong (autonomous city-state within the PRC)

Here, a meter of elite housing costs about 60 thousand euros. For this price you can buy one of 10 exclusive penthouses in an elite complex. The area of ​​each of them is 650 square meters. The building has a separate elevator, parking and a private garden. The complex is located in the prestigious Baek district. The price of the penthouse includes elite designer furniture, a roof terrace, a helipad, a gym with equipment and a magnificent pool. It offers panoramic views of the southern island, surrounded by the ocean.



Moscow Russian Federation)

Superb apartments in Brusov Lane. Of course, not the most expensive apartment in the world, but enough is close to this. Successfully competes with elite housing in other countries. A meter costs approximately 50 thousand euros, the area of ​​the apartment is 235 meters squared. The high cost is due to the original architectural solution and exclusive interior decoration by a popular designer.

Inside the apartment there is a huge living room, three bedrooms. Each of them has its own bathroom. The apartment is furnished by world famous brands.

Tokyo, Japan)

More recently, Tokyo was considered the most expensive city in the world. An apartment costs 16 million euros (approximately 40 thousand per square meter). One bedroom apartment in Minami Azabu quarter. The area of ​​the penthouse is four hundred and twelve square meters.

The house has the following premises: wardrobe, salon, bathroom, kitchen, bedroom. The walls are painted by Japanese artist Hiroshi Senjo. This is the most expensive apartment in the world, having one bedroom.

New York, USA)

Apartments in Central Park West. The total cost is approximately 61 million euros (42 thousand per square meter). These apartments are the most expensive in the United States.



Geneva, Switzerland)

Penthouse in the city center worth 12.5 million euros. The area of ​​the apartment is 400 square meters. m (31 thousand per 1 meter squared). The apartment has nine rooms: four bedrooms, two living rooms, a kitchen, a wardrobe and a dining room, and three more bathrooms.

Paris, France)

38 thousand euros per square meter - apartments with an area of ​​750 m. The total cost of the apartment is 23 million euros. The penthouse has a magnificent view of the city from the window, has a comfortable location, convenient access for cars and beautiful interior decoration. There is a separate kitchen for the chef, cozy guest rooms, several bedrooms with attached bathrooms, a games room, a private cinema and a wardrobe.

Dubai, UAE)

The famous Burj Khalifa skyscraper is similar in shape to a stalagmite. It offers apartments with an area of ​​208 meters in a square: two bedrooms adjacent to private bathrooms, a living room, a kitchen. Their cost is six million euros.



The penthouses are fully furnished, have marble flooring, their own sauna and jacuzzi.

Rome, Italy)

The most prestigious apartments in the capital of Italy have a cost of six million euros. Area - 617 square meters. m

These are the most expensive apartments at the moment. It is possible that in the near future the list will change, because the number of elite real estate around the world is constantly growing. In the largest centers of the planet: London, New York, Paris, Seoul and others - a huge number of houses with modern penthouses are built annually.
