"Oceanis Hotel". Crete. Description and feedback

In the town of Anissaras, not far from the village of Analipsis, there is a small but very cozy Oceanis Hotel (Crete). Clean and well-groomed landscaped grounds, comfortable rooms, a luxurious pool, quality service - what else is needed for an unforgettable vacation? This budget hotel will especially appeal to tourists seeking an active pastime, research and search for new experiences.

Description

"Oceanis Hotel" (Crete) is a modern four-story building, decorated in bright colors. The hotel is located 900 meters from the sea. Near the hotel, near the village of Analipsis, there are souvenir shops, bars and taverns. Just 4 km from the hotel there is the resort town of Hersonissos - the center of nightlife in Crete. Heraklion Airport is 25 km from Oceanis, and Rethymnon is 3 km away.

Residence

For those tourists who were attracted by its unique flavor of Greece (Crete), Hotel Oceanis is ready to offer 128 comfortable, high-quality rooms with views of the coastline. Standard apartments of 28 square meters can accommodate up to three people. They are equipped with a double bed (or two separate), a terrace, a shower, a hairdryer, a toilet, a telephone, air conditioning, television with music entertainment channels, a refrigerator, a bar and a safe. Special non-smoking rooms are available. Bed linen is changed three times in seven days. Cleaning is scheduled daily.

Nutrition

Oceanis Hotel (Crete) serves its guests according to the All Inclusive system, which includes dinner, breakfast and lunch in the hotel restaurant. Also, in bars, vacationers can order free local alcohol, coffee, tea. Tourists vacationing in "Oceanis", the service "room service" is available. The hotel has one restaurant serving Greek and international cuisine, and three bars serving drinks and light snacks.

Infrastructure and entertainment

Oceanis Hotel (Crete) has a large outdoor pool, a modern tennis court, a spacious conference room, an outdoor terrace and a business center. Also at the hotel there is a currency exchange, fax, Internet cafe, printer, copy machine, laundry, luggage storage, dry cleaning, cinema, parking, medical office, car rental, tour organization. Tourists can use the services of a porter, concierge, there is a shuttle service. At leisure, vacationers can play billiards, table and tennis, visit the games room.

For babies

For children there is a separate pool, cots in the room, a playground, a leisure club, and chairs in the restaurant. There is an experienced nanny.





Beach

The swimming area is located 800 meters from the hotel. On the beach there are umbrellas and sun loungers (for a fee). A small bus runs regularly from the hotel to the sea.

Crete. Hotel "Oceanis". Reviews

For its price category, this hotel offers decent service and accommodation. Neat rooms, beautiful grounds. Dishes in the restaurant are very tasty and hearty. An animation program is held once a week. It is not very convenient that the beach is far enough away. The rest of the tourists were satisfied with the rest.
