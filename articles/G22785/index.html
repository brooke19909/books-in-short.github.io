When is it worth going to Corfu?

At Blue Princess Beach Hotel Suites 4 *, guests are always welcome. But the climate of Corfu is not entirely conducive to winter holidays. The lion's share of the annual rainfall falls from November to January. But already in February spring comes to Corfu. During this period, the island literally blooms and smells. The heat begins at the end of April. But the deep Ionian Sea warms up slowly, and few decide to swim. The tourist season opens in mid-May in the part of the island that faces the mainland, since the strait is shallow. At the end of May, it becomes comfortable to swim on the rest of the Corfu coast. High tourist season lasts until the beginning of October. Storms that are becoming more frequent reduce the number of holidaymakers on the island, and with it prices. The most convenient period for excursions around the island is April and October. The weather is warm, sunny and without precipitation. If you are exclusively interested in beach vacations, then the summer months will suit you. However, with children it is best to go to Corfu in September. In early autumn, the sun is not so baking, and the sea warmed up best.

Where do guests stay?

Each of the one hundred and twenty rooms at the Blue Princess Beach Hotel Suites 4 * is individually decorated. And they all have either a balcony or (if located in the basement) a patio - a patio.
