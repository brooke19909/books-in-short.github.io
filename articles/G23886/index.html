Nokia 2700 - phone review

Several years ago, almost simultaneously, several interesting specimens from the Finnish manufacturer were released. These were devices from the Classic series: 6700, 6303, as well as the Nokia 2700 model, the characteristics of which we will consider today. Such phones have design characteristics, like the 6700. All of them are somewhat similar to each other. When you need to decide on a phone model that works clearly like a clock, you should be quite conservative. Two phones - 2700 and 5130 - are exactly the same. They are suitable for an adult audience, are completely similar in function, differ only in design.

Size, control components and design

Model Nokia 2700 weighs 85 g, size - 109.2 x 46 x 14 millimeters. All corners of the apparatus are smoothed. In the lower area, the device narrows, making it convenient to hold the phone in your hands. However, such a device is inconvenient to carry in a pocket in the front, because it is thick and heavy. The entire case consists of a matte plastic chrome shade.



The Nokia 2700 is presented only in black. A 2 megapixel camera is located on the back of the case. It is complemented by the inscription Nokia, which is framed by chrome plastic. The speaker grill is curved outward. Due to this, if you put the device on the table, the hole will not be blocked. On the upper part are the necessary connector for charging. And in order to connect a headset, there is a 3.5 mm output. The plug is covered with microUSB - input. The audio jack provides the ability to connect third-party headphones for listening to music. Due to its quality characteristics, the phone can replace an inexpensive player. The screen of this model is made of plastic, so it accumulates dirt and needs to be cleaned regularly. A hole for the earpiece is available at the top of the unit. The speaker has a high volume.

Screen specifications

The Nokia 2700 model has a display with a diagonal of 2 inches, and a resolution of 320x240 pixels. The screen provides a display of 262 thousand color shades. This type of display is also installed in the Nokia 2730 and 5130. For this price, it is very good. Viewing angles are ordinary. It is quite bright and outwardly attractive. The device has a modified platform S40 - 5. The advantage is that you can set the clock on the screen saver by clicking on the end call button.



Input Device Features

Nokia 2700 has a keyboard, divided into two modules, which are highlighted by a frame. All function and number keys are made of glossy black plastic. There is also a chrome backing. And it is very convenient in the presence of daylight, as the letters are clearly visible. The backlight of the entire keyboard cannot be turned off.



Battery quality

The Nokia 2700 phone comes with a BL-5C battery with a capacity of 1050 mAh. It gives a guarantee on the use of the device in talk mode for 6.4 hours, and in standby mode - 12 days. You can watch videos for no more than 4 hours, for shooting video resources the battery lasts for 1 hour. You can listen to music for no more than 12 hours.

Media, communications, software, reviews

The total memory is 64 MB. The first boot provides 8.5 MB on the device. In the memory of the device there are melodies, pictures of a standard sample. There is a slot for microSD memory cards (2GB). Model 2700 belong to 2G devices. There are 3 connection modes via microUSB. The device can work as a modem. Photos can be printed in MTP mode. There is a Bluetooth module (2.0 with EDR). The software platform in the 2700 has various modes of operation, games, screensavers, and themes. In the official documentation you will find all the tips for using the Nokia 2700 phone; instructions are included with the device.



And now we will see what the owners write about the device in the reviews. Among the strengths are the good correlation between price and quality, design, ergonomics of the case and keys, easy access to the battery, the presence of microSD, USB, 3.5 mm audio jack, Bluetooth, FM radio, battery capabilities. Users are pleased that there is no need to constantly clear the memory. Sound is recognized as high-quality and clean. The amazing ability of the phone to hold even a weak network signal is emphasized. It is noted that the device is resistant to external influences such as moisture, dirt and even concrete. The quality of the camera is also recognized as worthy (including night shooting) for its class.

Some reviews say that, unlike smartphones, this device is not afraid of freezes, crashes and unexpected reboots. The screen contrast also found a positive response from users. Often, among the strengths, excellent build quality is noted, which is typical for the models of this manufacturer. Among the shortcomings, users in reviews often mention a relatively long charging time, the lack of a USB cable in the kit. Some owners of the device lack the hardware photo and volume keys. The tactile sensation also doesn't appeal to everyone. There are complaints about the oversized size of the watch and the inability to change it.
