How to get to the hotel

It is not difficult to remember the address: Russia, Crimea, the city of Yalta, the village of Livadia, 95 Vinogradnaya Street. You can get to it in a variety of ways. If the path is not short, use the plane. Landing is carried out in the city of Simferopol, and then you can order a transfer, which will take you directly to the Livadia spa hotel. There is a more budget option - this is moving by train. You just need to get to the train station of Simferopol or Sevastopol. From both of these points, there is the possibility of a transfer to the hotel door, do not forget just to clarify in time where you need to be met.

Own car

If you travel by car, you need to get along the highway to the city of Simferopol, and then turn towards Yalta - to the village of Livadia.
