LCD "Admiral" (Krasnodar) - luxury housing in southern Russia

Several years ago, Krasnodar was a leader in the ranking of hundreds of the best cities in Russia. The Institute of Territorial Planning "Urbanika" recognized the capital of the Kuban in terms of quality of life as the best in the country. Maybe that's why people are drawn here so much and the city is being built up with a vengeance. The central region of Krasnodar has noticeably changed . One of his most beautiful new buildings is the Admiral residential complex.





Project

The complex includes five monolithic houses with different number of storeys: from 17 to 24. A total of 15 sections for 1,100 business-class apartments. The optimal area, its own infrastructure and good ergonomics, as well as the increased characteristics of sound and heat insulation, and transport accessibility make the Admiral LCD in Krasnodar (photo below) convenient for living and doing business.

Exterior finish gives a special appeal to buildings. It is made of modern high-quality materials using the technology of ventilated facades. Balconies and loggias in the complex are glazed with aluminum stained-glass windows.

Attractive conditions

Residential complex Admiral (Krasnodar) is located in the cultural and historical center of the city, next to the park, on Kubanskaya Street, which is considered one of the most attractive.

New buildings comfortably fit into the natural landscape of the coastal territory and the modern promenade.

The project was implemented as a single ensemble focused on the waters of the Kuban. The complex has a common courtyard with seating areas and ground and underground parking complexes. Separate play areas are allocated for young children, and sports grounds for those older (they are covered with a modern polymer composition). The first floors of the buildings are non-residential. They are planned outlets, catering points, offices, social services and various services.

The promenade is all pedestrian, with dedicated bike paths.

The territory of the Admiral residential complex in Krasnodar (reviews praise the originality of the layout and stunning views) is fenced and closed to strangers. Around the perimeter of the complex installed video surveillance systems. A professional operation system monitors all systems of the complex; there are concierges at the entrances.





After the start of sales, the grandiose Admiral Trade and Office Center will open in letter 5. It has many shops, restaurants, offices and a World Class fitness club.
