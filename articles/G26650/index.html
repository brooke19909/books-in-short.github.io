A great place to stay - Inal Bay: hostels, accommodation, prices

Who doesn‚Äôt dream of coming to rest in such a beautiful place as Inal Bay? The camp sites here are always crowded with people. The popularity of the Tuapse district is very easy to explain. First of all, tourists will find crystal clear sea water, well-groomed pebble beaches, and well-developed infrastructure.

Talking about this place, you can not keep silent about the deposits of blue clay. Thanks to her, special treatment procedures are carried out at many camp sites, and you can do them yourself. Near the cliff there is a small lake where you can meet people who use this gift of nature.

Any needs will satisfy the Inal Bay. The hostels offer all types of family, active and wellness holidays.

"Victoria"

The most popular in Inal Bay is the recreation center "Victoria". It occupies a very large territory (7 ha), overgrown with relict trees. The air here can be called unique: on the one hand - the sea, on the other - mountains. Tourists often take walks to spring water springs. Everyone who wants to enjoy the views of the Greater Caucasus Mountain Range is invited to go to the observation deck. It is located at an altitude of 700 meters.

On the territory of "Victoria" there is a dining room, a cafe. Guests arriving by car can use the parking lot. On clear sunny days, you can sunbathe on a clean beach (200 meters from it), plunge into the warm sea water, and play table tennis. For children, a playground with swings, stairs, sandboxes is organized at the base.

Inal Bay (camp sites operating on this territory offer various excursion programs) will leave a lot of unforgettable impressions, thanks to which you will want to come back every next year.



The Victoria base provides three types of rooms for living:

  • Norma are wooden cottages for 2 or 3 people. The rooms are equipped with necessary furniture, there is electricity, a refrigerator. The bathroom is next to the cottage.
  • Comfort - the most expensive rooms at the base. A day will cost at least 2000 rubles. Breakfast is included. The rooms are equipped with furniture, split system, there is a TV, which is connected to satellite TV, refrigerator. Each room has a bathroom and access to a terrace with armchairs and a table.
  • The cost of living in a standard room starts from 1155 rubles. As in the previous rooms, all the necessary furniture is installed here, there is a refrigerator, which is provided for two rooms. Showers are located next to the cottage in a common block.

Base of rest "Olympus"

On the shores of the Black Sea, or rather the Inal Bay, there is a beautiful base "Olympus". Visitors who rested in these places call it a little paradise. Here you can enjoy the picturesque surroundings, almost virgin nature, auspicious and clean air. Distance to the sea is about 350 meters.

Guests are offered to stay in economy-class houses for 2-4 people. You can also settle in comfortable cottages, the cost of which starts from 1190 rubles. And the economy option will cost 640 rubles. Here visitors are expected all the necessary furniture, amenities on the street. As for the cottages, they are much more comfortable. In the rooms, visitors will see new modern furniture, air conditioning, a bathroom and a TV. During relaxation, you can eat in the dining room.

In order to prevent guests from getting bored, the Olimp recreation center organized a special venue where you can play various board games (billiards, tennis). Sports equipment rental is available here. Those who cannot imagine their life without the Internet are encouraged to visit the Internet cafe. Children can play on equipped playgrounds. Everything on the territory of this base has a good rest.



"Oilman"

The Neftyanik camp site (Inal Bay, the second section) offers 4 two-story buildings for living. There are 35 rooms in total. You can rent both economy and superior apartments. Walking distance to the beach, promenade and mountain river.

The advantage of the Neftyanik base is its convenient location. Next to it is a dolphinarium and a water park, a market, grocery stores and a pharmacy. You can eat in the dining room: breakfast, lunch and dinner will be about 650 rubles per person. However, if you wish, you can prepare meals in the rooms in the equipped kitchens.



Infanta

In the village of Bzhid on the third site is the recreation center "Infanta". It offers vacationers a varied entertainment program. On the territory there is a sauna and a bathhouse, there is a swimming pool, barbecue areas, billiards, karaoke, tennis, sports and playgrounds. The territory is decorated with a beautiful decorative waterfall. Accommodation is possible in cottages of the category Standard, Junior Suite, Suite. Minimum room rate is about 1,500 rubles. Luxury cottages will have to pay at least 5000 rubles.



"Rainbow"

What will Inal Bay please tourists with? The camp sites that operate in this region are equipped in accordance with European standards. In their face value there are numbers of different price categories. For example, the recreation center "Rainbow" offers accommodation in a suite and junior suite. The cost starts from 600 rubles. The rooms are equipped with a bathroom, there is a shower, all the necessary furniture and electrical equipment (fan, air conditioning, kettle, TV, refrigerator, etc.). Interesting excursions are organized for visitors, a cafe is open.
