Rest on the left bank of the Don: recreation centers, reviews of tourists and prices

According to unofficial data, the left bank of the Don locals call Levberdon. This picturesque corner enjoys great success among Rostovites and visiting tourists. Picturesque landscape, clean air and well-groomed beaches - all these components make an unforgettable and relaxing holiday on the left bank of the Don. The recreation centers of Rostov are open year-round, so you can plan your vacation when convenient.





It is here, away from the busy city streets and highways, that you can truly relax, feel the taste of life and spend time in the circle of dear people. Every weekend, couples, large companies flock to this area, here they hold corporate weekends and romantic meetings. The left bank attracts everyone who wants to break out of the stuffy metropolis.

Features

The left bank of the Don belongs to the Kirovsky district of Rostov. Its length is about 30 km. The bulk of hotels, hotels, recreational facilities and nightclubs are located along the coast. What makes this place special and gives it advantages over other areas.

Levberdon was originally created as a tourist area with numerous restaurants of different styles and directions. The life here is special is significantly different from the right bank. This piece of land is filled with the atmosphere of the holiday, here you can have plenty of fun and relax. Wellness boarding houses and health resorts with picturesque natural resources are also built on its territory.





Tourists enthusiastically talk about the rest on the left bank of the Don. Recreation centers offer the best prices and quality service. Many customers believe that here you can really relax your soul and body. The laudatory words are addressed to the hotel staff: the friendly attitude and scruples of the staff cannot but rejoice.

Local authorities plan to develop and expand tourism, erect new modern recreational complexes and entertainment facilities. According to some reports, the entire business center of the city is planned to be transferred to this area. In the near future, the construction of a football stadium, retail space, water entertainment facilities and an equestrian center with an arena, stables, hotels, a children's park and a hippodrome will begin. To realize the desired projects in life, it remains only to calculate the estimate and find available funds. Soon you will see the updated left bank of the Don.

Chalet "Blue Wave"





All bases on the left bank of the Don are surrounded by amazing landscapes with rich flora and fauna. For example, a swimming pool, a SPA salon with cosmetic procedures, and three saunas appeared in the local area. The hotel has a restaurant, open from early morning until midnight. Here in a pleasant atmosphere you will be offered to try the specialties of Caucasian and European cuisine.

Levberdon complex





Are you dreaming of a fun, budget and memorable vacation? For this, rest on the left bank of the Don is suitable. Recreation facilities are reconstructed and equipped with basic conditions for living. One of them is Levberdon, which is located in a quiet place - in the village of Vertyachy. Base "Cool Don"





The hotel offers a safe, interesting and informative vacation on the left bank of the Don. The recreation center of Rostov is a beautiful nature, tranquility and complete bliss on the banks of the river. Hotel "Cool Don" is a real confirmation of this. The whole territory is guarded, so guests can not worry about their own safety and enjoy their stay in a boarding house.

There is a pier for boats and small yachts. Guests at this resort are fishing enthusiasts and environmental enthusiasts. For a comfortable pastime, the administration offers free use of barbecue facilities, sports equipment and playgrounds. A play area for children has been allocated.

Recreation facilities can be listed endlessly, there are an unthinkable number of them in this area, and at the same time for different wealth. The advantage of this area, as residents note, is the lack of tracks, noisy discos and the presence of fresh air. All of these aspects are extremely important for well-being and good mood.
