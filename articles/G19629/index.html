Tunisia hotels with a water park are waiting for you!

Here you can endlessly admire the unique nature: these are the enchanting beauty of mimosa, and magnolia, endless orange groves and fields of grapes ... And, of course, the golden sands of magnificent beaches! This vacation will be remembered by everyone without exception! Tunisia hotels with a water park, qualified and polite staff will certainly offer you high-quality service at the highest level. Tunisia hotels with a water park are very popular among tourists who come to rest with children, because they provide a rich animation program, and the slides located in the immediate vicinity and around the clock will not leave indifferent either the kids or their parents. The best Tunis hotels with a water park

Water slides in the recreation area are a fairly common occurrence in this country. However, Tunisia hotels with a water park do not offer a very large variety. You will find only 2-3 water slides, a large pool for relaxation and a small one for children. Keep in mind that the water park is present only with hotels with a level of 4-5 stars. In lower status you will not find this. So, here is our selection of the best hotels with water parks that Tunisia has given us.

Hotel "Marabu"





The water park on its territory is far from all the entertainments that you can find. This luxury hotel, located two kilometers from Sousse, has two tennis courts, restaurants, a Moorish cafe, saunas with a jacuzzi, outdoor and indoor pools that will heat the water according to your first requirement. Professional animation works for kids. There are also mini-pools, special clubs and playgrounds. So that adults can relax and devote time to themselves, the hotel offers qualified babysitting services that will not let your baby get bored.

"Prima Life Caravan"





This hotel is recommended for those who come for extreme experiences. It is located on the beach in the heart of Sousse. Tourists can enjoy diving, windsurfing and much more. There are also swimming pools, water slides, wonderful animation, spas, shops, restaurants, and all this is accompanied by impeccable service.

Magic Life Club Manar

This hotel is located on the coast of Hammamet, has its own fenced beach, which is equipped with bars and restaurants, working 24 hours a day. As usual in five-star hotels, everything is at the highest level: rooms, animation, food, entertainment and relaxation.
