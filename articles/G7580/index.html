Zoo in Izhevsk: photos, schedule, reviews

On the territory of the Kirov park in the center of Izhevsk, there is a zoo of Udmurtia. It is located on a hill in a picturesque place - on the banks of a pond. The zoo in Izhevsk is a favorite place for cognitive family vacations for residents of the city and its guests.

His visit will be an interesting journey into the animal world not only for children, but also for adults of all ages.

History

For a long time, the beautiful Kirov Park, once loved by many generations of residents, was neglected. The idea of ‚Äã‚Äãcreating a zoo in it arose from the president of the Udmurt Republic, Alexander Alexandrovich Volkov. The task was not an easy one, but, to the delight of the locals, it was brought to life. And on September 10, 2008, the zoo in Izhevsk officially opened. This event was a gift to residents on the anniversary of the city. Now it is a pride and an important socio-cultural part of the state infrastructure of the Udmurt Republic.

Zoo in Izhevsk: opening hours, contacts and schedule

Address of the zoological park: UR, Izhevsk, st. Kirova, 8.

You can get to it by tram: route number 1, 4, 7 or 10, the stop "Zoo".

Contact phone: (3412) 59-60-61.

Excursions can be ordered by number: (3412) 59-60-98.

On the official website of the zoological park it is indicated that at the discretion of the administration, slight adjustments to the mode of operation are possible, therefore, before a visit, especially from other cities, it is better to specify the hours of visits beforehand. Speeches of walruses named Enurmin, Eve and Nesseyka take place, depending on the season, at 11:00, 14:00 and 18:00.

Residents of the city and its guests can visit the zoo in Izhevsk every day, with the exception of Monday. The work schedule has different time intervals depending on the time of year:

  • summer period (starts from May 1) - from 9.00 to 21.00;
  • Autumn season - from 9.00 to 19.00;
  • winter months - from 9.00 to 16.00;
  • March and April - from 9.00 to 19.00.

You need to know that the ticket offices finish work 30 minutes before the closing of the zoological park.

Prices and benefits

The cost of an adult ticket is 150 rubles, and a child (considered the age of 5 to 14 years) is 50 rubles. For a tour of up to 20 people, the total price is 350 rubles. The zoo in Izhevsk provides preferential visits for these population groups:

  • a child under the age of five;
  • children with disabilities and persons accompanying them;
  • families with many children;
  • participants or invalids of the Second World War, as well as persons equated with them;
  • war veterans or disabled people of Chechnya, Afghanistan and other countries;
  • labor veterans of Russia and the Udmurt Republic;
  • rear workers ;
  • disabled people or veterans of the liquidation of a radiation accident at the Chernobyl nuclear power plant and at the Mayak Production Association;
  • invalids of I and II groups.

The privilege for free visits is provided if there is a document confirming the right to it: passports, certificates, birth certificates of the child.

Zoological Park Functions

The talisman and patron of the zoo is the wolf from the cartoon "Mowgli", a bronze sculpture of which can be seen when entering the zoo in Izhevsk. Tourists often take photos near him in order to capture his magnificent posture as a keepsake. It symbolizes a long and inseparable family life, where loyalty, devotion and care for each other reigns.



Izhevsk Zoological Park was created to perform such basic functions:

  • security activities to protect the animal world, save endangered species, breeding rare species;
  • scientific works on the study of certain animals, as well as natural conditions and their habitats;
  • education and upbringing of the young generation;
  • creation of a comfortable family cognitive recreation area.

The zoo in Izhevsk was able to fulfill all the planned tasks, despite the short time of its existence.

The inhabitants

Izhevsk Zoological Park has an area of ‚Äã‚Äãabout 16 hectares. It contains more than 300 representatives of the animal world. The zoo is home to many animals: Himalayan, polar and brown bears, leopard, Siberian lynx, panther, Arctic fox, fur seal, pony, camel, zebra, raccoon, Japanese macaque, chimpanzee, peacock, ostrich and others. Their list is constantly growing.



For a convenient trip to the natural world, the park is divided into thematic exhibitions. They are created according to the zoogeographic principle. Animals living nearby in a natural environment are settled close to each other. On the territory there are such zones:

  • "Far East".
  • "White North."
  • "Our taiga."
  • "Udmurt village."
  • "Pond".
  • "Country of the apes."

You can not only have fun, but also broaden your horizons by visiting the zoo in Izhevsk. Photos taken in the park will forever leave in memory the memories of his heroes and favorites.

The zoological park hosts very interesting performances of walruses and fur seals. It also has various exhibits dedicated to a specific topic, for example, the world of exotics.

One of the main characters is a teddy bear

A significant date in the zoological park is considered December 12, 2013. On this day, a couple of polar bears - Nord and Dumka - was born a baby. According to the director of the zoological institution Svetlana Malysheva, such an event occurred here for the first time. A bear couple has been living in the zoo since 2008, from the day it was opened. The good news about Dumka‚Äôs pregnancy was kept secret because of superstitions until the birth of the teddy bear. Zoologists did not excommunicate the cub from the dipper. He grew up with his parents in a den. Employees watched its development and upbringing using video surveillance. To the delight of the director and staff of the zoo, the mother recognized her baby. Everyone was very worried about this, because maternal instincts do not always remain in animals growing in captivity.

Choosing a name for the newborn has become an event in the city. Competition for nickname options for the baby was announced in April 2014. Prerequisite: the name must have the first letter "H", like the nickname of Father Nord. Many options were sent. On City Day, 06/12/2014, it was announced that the teddy bear in the Izhevsk zoo was nicknamed Nissan. Today, a healthy and active baby delights zoologists and visitors to the park.

Reviews

You can spend an exciting family walk by choosing a zoo in Izhevsk for this. Guest reviews of the park are only enthusiastic. All are pleasantly surprised by the cleanliness and comfort. Visitors are pleased with an informative trip at a low price. Most of all they like the spacious areas in which the inhabitants are located. They, unlike some other zoos, do not languish in tight cages, so visiting does not leave a hint of sadness.

In open-air cages there are ponds, ditches, huge stones, rocks, lawns, waterfalls, various plantings, fallen and growing trees. The conditions are as close as possible to the natural environment and have a lot of space, so the inhabitants do not feel in captivity. All animals are beautiful and well-groomed. Visitors note that the park is well-maintained, and the walk is comfortable. The zoo has a sufficient number of clean and free dry closets.

Cafe

A walk through the zoo requires a full day, so places for eating are important for any family. For a bite to eat, guests can check out the zoo. Izhevsk, as tourists say, has many decent establishments with affordable prices. Zoological Park is no exception. On its territory there are several cafes where you can eat with the whole family.

A varied menu, delicious dishes, the cordiality of the staff and reasonable prices will delight tourists. Most visitors are very pleased with both the taste and quality of the dinner, and its value. Those who prefer to eat their food brought for a walk can sit in the shade on a comfortable bench.

Conclusion

The zoo in Izhevsk is considered one of the most popular places in the Russian Federation and corresponds to the European level. It is located in a picturesque corner of nature. Thanks to the diligence of employees, the zoological park is a clean and well-groomed area for cognitive and fun family spending. The animal world is widely represented in it. Thematic zones help you quickly find the right inhabitants and make the walk comfortable. Huge enclosures allow their residents to feel free. Well-groomed animals stroll majestically and show themselves in full glory. This makes the walk not only informative, but also joyful.

Aviaries are separated from guests by wide moats with water or glass, so familiarity with the animal world is absolutely safe for visitors of any age.
