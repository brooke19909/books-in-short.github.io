Hotel "Breeze Spa" (Sochi): description, reviews

Sochi is the venue for the Olympic Games and just a beautiful resort city of the Krasnodar Territory, located on the Black Sea. Tourists choose it not only from Russia, but also from other countries. And for the vacation to be at the highest level, you must choose the right hotel. Breeze Spa (Sochi) is one of the most popular hotels. Having taken its place in the city center, it offers guests the highest level of service (as the administration claims) and the proximity of the sea. But is it really good there? And what do vacationers write in their reviews?





Location

The institution is located on the 2nd beach line, the distance from the sea is 300 meters. The place where the Sochi Breeze Spa (hotel) is located deserves special attention. Khostinsky district, where it is comfortably located, is the center of the city. A large number of attractions are concentrated here, the area is landscaped, clean and well-maintained. Staying in this part of Sochi, you can be sure that the vacation will turn out interesting and memorable.

Within walking distance are such attractive places for tourists as the circus, the arboretum, the Tree of Friendship garden museum, the central Sochi stadium, museums and the Winter Theater. A few kilometers away is Mount Akhun. The observation tower on it reveals a stunning landscape - the spurs of the Main Caucasian Range and the enchanting Black Sea.

In addition, near the hotel there are small shops and a large shopping center where you can purchase everything you need. In other words, this hotel and its environs provide conditions for a comfortable stay of tourists of any category.



Hotel Description

As in the area where the three-star hotel is located, it is also difficult to find signs of impurity in it. A beautifully decorated facade in a modern style invites residents and guests of Sochi to enjoy their vacation, make it unforgettable, relax your body and soul.

The rooms and common areas - cleanliness and comfort. Any groups can be accommodated here: young couples, groups of friends, families with and without children, and even if a person arrives alone, he will feel comfortable. Services are provided for business customers. The hotel can conduct seminars and arrange business meetings, if required by the situation.

Across the road there is a private equipped pebble beach. Umbrellas and sunbeds are provided free of charge, and water rides and beach equipment are available at an additional cost.



Room Types

The Breeze Spa Hotel (Sochi) has a total of 135 rooms. Among them:

  • standard - 83;
  • Superior comfort - 31;
  • two-room apartments - 12;
  • studio type - 3;
  • two-room suite - 1;
  • two-room suites - 4.

Description of rooms

Each of them must have a bathroom with shower or bathtub, telephone, hairdryer, TV and satellite TV, minibar, air conditioning. There is nothing extra in standard type rooms, except for two twin beds. The apartments are cozy, but quite simple in design.

Superior rooms include rooms with one double or two single beds. In addition to the above, they also have a radio. The decor is calm, perfect for relaxing.

Daily cost

Depending on the type of room, prices per person per day range from 3,500 to 12,000 rubles. A child under three years old stays free of charge if breakfast, lunch, dinner and a separate bed are not provided to him. The price includes meals. Type - at choice: two-time or on the All inclusive system ("all inclusive").

The price may be lower if the administration holds some kind of action. Children under 12 receive a 50% discount on food.

Infrastructure, entertainment

Guests are served meals in the Breeze restaurant. Vacationers are offered a variety of dishes exhibited at the buffet. In this institution you can also enjoy delicious European or Caucasian cuisine for a fee. In addition to the restaurant, there is a lobby bar with drinks.

On the territory - a large outdoor pool, open from May to October. Also, the Breeze Spa Hotel (Sochi) has its own equipped Lotus spa. It has everything that is necessary for pacification and complete relaxation:

  • classic, soapy, underwater and relaxing types of massage;
  • a whole complex of steam rooms, among which - a Russian bath, a Roman bath and a Finnish sauna;
  • fitness bar with healthy drinks;
  • gym;
  • spa programs for body and face care;
  • hydromassage pool and contrast fonts;
  • solarium;
  • pearl baths and healing showers;
  • attendant services;
  • a beauty parlor where you can order mesotherapy or even get piercings.




So that vacationers do not get bored, entertaining show programs and competitions have been developed that take place in the evening:

  • performance of KVN teams;
  • auctions;
  • club dances;
  • performance of vocalists and musicians;
  • guests vs animators;
  • "Mr. Breeze";
  • "Perfect match";
  • "Miss Sochi Breeze";
  • lotteries.

A disco is organized for toddlers and children under 12 years old. Animators work with them, and also from the morning until the evening the doors of the children's playroom are open.

Business Services

But not only for entertainment, there is the Breeze Spa Hotel (Sochi). It provides all the conditions for business customers. An individual approach is taken to each: a businessman can form his own conference package, which will include everything that is necessary for rest and work. For example, the following services may be provided:

  • food and accommodation;
  • coffee break;
  • excursion program;
  • rent of conference rooms and equipment there;
  • visiting the SPA-center and using its services;
  • animation and entertainment;
  • transport, driver.

Renting a conference room will cost about 800 rubles per hour. In total, 3 of them are equipped, each designed for 50 seats. They have:

  • marker boards;
  • demo screens;
  • chairs with music stands;
  • copier, printer, scanner;
  • fax machine;
  • a phone with the ability to make calls anywhere in the world, the Internet;
  • computer technology;
  • modern projectors.

In Sochi Breeze Spa (the hotel provides this service for an additional fee), each guest can rent a safe or a left-luggage office, use the services of a laundry, a workshop or sewing rooms, book an excursion and transfer.





Sochi Breeze Spa (hotel): reviews of vacationers

All people are different, and it is impossible to please every client. Therefore, even the most positive institution will have at least one negative review. G. Sochi, the Sochi Breeze Spa Hotel , its attendants, food and accommodation were not liked by everyone who rested there.

The first thing that frightened the guests was the repeated presentation of dishes on the buffet, there were even sour stewed fruit. The food is monotonous and tasteless, but they say about beer and wine that they were diluted with water. The bar has prohibitive prices for drinks, so many tourists are advised to buy the same juices at the nearest supermarket. The second is thin walls, and therefore everything that happens in the corridor and the next room is clearly audible. Third, an old and noisy air conditioner, from which air blows with an unpleasant odor. And finally, the fourth - unfriendly staff at the reception and in the restaurant.

But there are also positive points. Some tourists write that they are fed with dignity and delicious. Smiling and polite maids regularly do their job - wipe off dust and change bed sheets. Many mark the price in their reviews, calling it the best for Sochi and good in relation to quality. The pool is heated, near it, as in general on the territory of the hotel, is always clean. The location of the hotel also pleases guests, as there are many interesting places nearby.





Conclusion

Sochi Breeze Spa is a hotel whose photos evoke a desire to visit this place. A beautiful facade, a variety of interior styles, a lot of entertainment in the hotel and beyond make it a wonderful place to relax. The hotel is ideal for families with children and large groups of friends, for those who like to relax alone, and who came to the resort town for work. And although it can not yet be called a dream, but he is already close to it.
