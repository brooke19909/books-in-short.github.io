Kansk hotels: where to stay?

Kansk hotels, although few in number, are ready to offer guests excellent service and a different level of accommodation. Everyone has the opportunity to stay in low-cost hotels to save money. There are also more representative high category options for demanding customers. You can pick up an overnight stay by its location, closer to the right place.

Despite the category and stardom, all hotels in Kansk are ready to boast a good level of service. There is always clean, comfortable and friendly, all conditions are created to make guests comfortable.

"Cannes" - in the heart of the city





Cannes is a comfortable hotel located in the very center of the city. Located on the banks of the Caen River. Not all Kansk hotels boast such a level of comfort. Each room is equipped with:

  • mini-bar;
  • a safe;
  • telephone with the ability to call the city;
  • an electric kettle;
  • cosmetic accessories;
  • floor scales;
  • all the necessary accessories;
  • shower cabin;
  • plasma tv.

The hotel has several categories of rooms: economy (from 2,000 rubles); standard (from 2,500 rubles); Junior Suite (from 3,500 rubles); luxury (from 4,000 rubles) and apartments (from 5,000 rubles).

"Comfort" at a comfortable price

In the hotel complex "Comfort" guests are ready to settle in the following categories of rooms:

  • Single
  • Double rooms.
  • Suite

The hotel also has a cafe, a large banquet hall, a bathhouse. On the territory there is a warm parking lot. It can be used by owners of both cars and trucks.

Siberia is one of the oldest hotels in the city

In the very center of the city is one of the oldest hotels - Siberia. The rooms are comfortable and clean. They always follow the culture of service. After all, the main motto of the hotel is: "Creating the most comfortable conditions for guests to stay."





It offers visitors rooms of different levels:

  • Single
  • Double rooms.
  • The highest category.

"South" - a hotel in a picturesque place

"South" is a whole hotel complex. Not all Kansk hotels have such a wide range of services. The complex today includes:

  • Medical Center.
  • Sauna.
  • Canteen.
  • Beauty saloon.
  • Banqueting hall.
  • Hotel.

The hotel has deluxe rooms, junior suites and double standards.

The main advantage of the Yug Hotel is its location. It is located in a picturesque pine forest. It is only a quarter hour drive from the train station and the central part of the city.

All Kansk hotels are diverse. Each has its own zest. Somewhere the highest service, somewhere reasonable prices, some of the most convenient location. Each guest will decide for himself what is his priority.
