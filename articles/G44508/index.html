When is it better to go to Pattaya?

In principle, at any time of the year you can relax in Pattaya, here each period is favorable in its own way. The rainy season does not last too long. Due to the tropical climate, heavy rainfall is characteristic here, especially in September-October. But this can not spoil the rest and overshadow the experience, since it can rain rarely and not for long. Of course, it‚Äôs not very comfortable to rest in the rainy season. Due to the ebb from April to October, the sea goes far from the coast, and even muddy water becomes even dirtier. During such periods, it is dangerous to swim, because of the invisibility under water, you can easily injure yourself on a sea urchin or get a jellyfish burn.

The most favorable vacation in Pattaya is from November to June. The air temperature at this time is ideally comfortable for relaxing on sandy beaches and traveling to interesting places in Thailand. Water in this period leaves only at night, which practically does not interfere with rest.

If you nevertheless chose Pattaya, then rest on the islands will be simply unforgettable, especially on Koh Lana, and this is available to everyone. From June to September in Thailand itself is the rainy season. But, despite this, during this period it is very comfortable for Europeans - moderate temperature and humidity.
For those who want to see exotic places and relax on the clean beaches of the Gulf of Thailand, to taste delicious food, and all this for little money, then the most favorable period is from June to the end of December. Tours and accommodation prices are as low as possible and there are practically no tourists.
