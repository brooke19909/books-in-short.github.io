Best hostels in Astana: overview

Arriving in Astana, you must first take care of where you have to live a certain period. The city has a lot of options for accommodation, so there will be no problems with a lack of places. The main thing is to book a room a few weeks before arrival, so you probably will not be at the peak of the season. The hostels of Astana will be considered in the article, the traveler will only have to choose.

Americana

The hostel is located a few kilometers from the famous Baiterek monument. Guests can use the internet for free.

The bathroom is shared. Each room has kitchen facilities, but the room itself is not equipped. It is necessary to cook in the kitchen, which is located on the floor and is shared. You cannot come with animals, as in other hostels in Astana. The staff speaks both Russian and English. Parking is free.

To the airport - 12 km, to the notorious Palace of Independence, only 1 km, and the mosque from "Americana" was separated by 700 m.



Club Hostel

According to independent reviews on the Internet and surveys conducted in the hostel itself, it is safe to say that the part of the city in which the Club is located is the most popular. WiFi is free.

Each room has a wardrobe. The bathroom has a hairdryer. A washing machine, iron and ironing service are also available. If desired, food is ordered directly to the room. So almost all hostels in Astana work. The kitchen is shared, the lounge too. There is a left-luggage office where luggage of visitors develops.

The airport from the "Club" is only 15 minutes away, to the train station - exactly twice as much.



AliStar

The hostel is located 600 meters from Bayterek, which is so famous among people visiting this country. Of course, the internet is free.

Each room has a bathroom, in more expensive options there is also a living room. If necessary, the hostel provides a free shuttle service. No pets allowed. There is a playground. The staff speaks both Russian and English, so there will be no problems in understanding.

The airport is 12 km from here. The Exhibition Center is 700 meters from the hostel, while the mosque is 800 meters away.

Hostel Nomad

In this hostel, each guest will find entertainment for themselves. There are always welcome guests. While the settlement will take place, the administration will offer a cup of tea to the client. There is always someone to spend time with, just talk or make good and even useful contacts. Thanks to the pleasant interior and the number of rooms full, in each of them there is a state of coziness and comfort, which are inherent only in the hearth. All towels are new. A washing machine is also available.

Nomad Hostel is a place where you want to come back constantly. Arriving home from a vacation, a person will definitely mention this complex with a kind word. It has a nice interior, good service and a great atmosphere. Not all hostels in Astana are able to give such feelings. The administration does everything possible to leave the client satisfied.



Nur

The hostel is located in the center of Astana.

Each room has a kettle, air conditioning. Parking is private and free of charge. The bathroom where the shower is located is shared. Internet is free. Like the bathroom, the kitchen is also shared. You cannot come with animals. Heating is provided, there is a workable elevator. On the territory there are a couple of grocery stores where you can stint inexpensively. It is proposed to put luggage in a left-luggage office, a laundry is equipped. Hostel rooms are cleaned daily. Lockers where things are stored are locked, so there is no need to worry about their safety. Smoking is not allowed here. Children are also not accepted.

The airport is located 16 km from Nur.

Overnight

Hostel "Overnight" is located in the center of the capital of Kazakhstan. Due to the maximum proximity to the most popular attractions, you can see the left bank of the main city of the state without the help of public transport (and without spending money).

The rooms in the hostel are decorated in modern style, which is very popular with tourists. The staff here are friendly, responsible and will always come to the rescue. For each client an individual approach. Interesting people settle here, and those who come from abroad, which allows other clients to practice knowledge of foreign languages. The administration does everything to ensure that guests constantly return to the hostel.
