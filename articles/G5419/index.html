Location

Hotel Nissiana (Ayia Napa, Cyprus) is a modern three-star hotel, which is located just 150 meters from the famous Nissi Beach. It takes half an hour to reach the center of Ayia Napa resort, which is located on the southeast coast of the island of Cyprus.

Nissi Beach

It attracts thousands of tourists every year. Nissi, which in Greek means "small island", gives a new idea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe beauty of the landscape.

It is well sheltered from the wind and creates favorable conditions for all sports. Another unique feature of the beach is a smooth coastline with gradual descent and shallow crystal clear waters.

The beach offers a wide range of water sports. For example, water skiing, scuba diving, sailing. For those looking for a more secluded place, there is a beautiful bay, less than a 10-minute walk towards Makronissos Beach.

Many beach lovers know that the water temperature remains warm from April to November. Therefore, they prefer to travel in spring and autumn, when it is not hot and a quiet stay is possible. The beach has clean sand and water, without algae, sea urchins and jellyfish.



The European Union awarded all the beaches of Ayia Napa with the international Blue Flag Award for exemplary cleanliness and well-developed infrastructure that comply with EU standards.

Ayia Napa

This is a colorful, friendly and full of pleasant entertainment city. It received its name from the monastery founded by the Venetians, which is located in the center. At the moment, the monastery acts as a museum.

On the square in the city center, near the fountain, church and monastery, several times a week give free concerts in the open air. There you can see the national Cypriot dances.

In Ayia Napa, everyone can get acquainted with many institutions: cafes, bars, souvenir shops. At the resort, a stay in a recreational atmosphere and a friendly attitude to visitors by local residents make it possible to make new acquaintances or find an interesting campaign quite easily and naturally.

About Cyprus

The island is located in the Far Eastern borders of the Mediterranean Sea. It is known for its vibrant nightlife and excellent sandy beaches. It is famous for the bright sun all year round, unique cuisine, culture.

Travelers are attracted by cedar forests, valleys, and a clear turquoise sea. The true wealth and heritage of Cyprus are archaeological sites: Byzantine churches and palaces, mysterious monasteries hiding in the rocks, and architectural monuments.

In addition to the ancient Greeks, immigrants from Byzantium and Turkey lived here, and European knights influenced the history and urban planning of Cyprus. Cyprus, according to legend, is the island of the goddess of Greek mythology Aphrodite. She, an embodiment of beauty and sensual love, emerged from the sea foam just near the city of Paphos. Romantically in love couples often travel to the island, expecting that local beliefs and legends will help them find strong love.

WaterWorld Waterpark

Built in 1996, a large-scale construction throughout its existence has become famous thanks to a creative approach to equipment, quality service and reliability.

The theme of the water park is Greek mythology. An incredible number of slides will not leave anyone indifferent. On the territory of the water park there are cafes and bars where you can relax, as well as playgrounds equipped for kids.

Sea Grotto Caves

The atmosphere of mystery created by legends does not leave these places and attracts travelers who set off here on rented cars, taxis and bicycles.

There are also those who prefer sightseeing boat trips to these types of transportation.

Macronissos

This beach is famous for at least Nissi beach. It also has a number of family-friendly features: gently shelving, golden sand and a quiet pastime.

Rock Tombs of Macronissos

The complex is located near the beach. Nineteen tombs are carved in the limestone rock, and a sanctuary has been erected near them, where archaeological excavations are ongoing. Entrance to the complex is free.

Monastery

When tourists enter the monastery from the street, the first thing they feel is that the noise and bustle of the city remains behind the doors. Now inactive, but retaining the spirit of antiquity, permeated with tranquility and tranquility, he will be remembered for a long time by a traveler who has come here.

Not far from the building, a church was erected where you can pray and light a candle. Entrance to the monastery is free.

Museum of the sea

The museum is dedicated to marine life. The purpose of its creation is the desire to tell the visitor about the problems of preserving the marine world, its importance and impact on the history of the island.

The main exhibits of the museum are models of an ancient Greek ship, a papyrus boat. Several floors of the museum are filled with collections of stuffed turtles, fish and other marine animals, as well as fossils of extinct mollusks.

It is impossible to reflect in the review the whole variety of objects of flora and fauna of Cyprus, objects of art, united by marine themes that are collected in this museum. In addition to excursions, conferences, seminars and exhibitions are also held here.

Cape Greco

It takes an average of ten minutes to reach the magnificent cliff from the center of Ayia Napa. It is not surprising that the cape is read by one of the most beautiful places in Cyprus.

By the way, caves and grottoes formed in the rock (under the influence of sea waves). The cape, surrounded by clear waters, is popular with diving and spearfishing. Birds during a flight across the sea choose Cape Greco for relaxation!
