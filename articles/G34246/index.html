Guest houses in Adler by the sea: overview, description and reviews

Summer holiday season is coming soon. Many of our compatriots prefer to spend their holidays at sea, without leaving the country. For many years, the welcoming Krasnodar Territory has been accepting tourists not only from Russia, but also from Europe. Many people buy tours to expensive hotels and pensions, which are quite a lot on this part of the Black Sea coast. But there are those who prefer a cheaper vacation. They choose mini-hotels, guest houses. In Adler and, in general, throughout the region, every traveler can find accommodation for himself to taste and at a convenient price.



Rest in Adler

This resort, often called the "gateway to the Black Sea", is a suburb of Sochi. Relaxing here, you have the opportunity not only to swim in the sea or sunbathe on the beach, but also get acquainted with a rich excursion program. Judging by statistics, the most popular type of accommodation in the Krasnodar Territory is guest houses. In Adler, on the seashore in recent years, quite a lot of such objects have been built, offering both deluxe rooms and economy rooms. A great advantage of such a residence is their convenient location relative to the sea.

Where to stay?

Many tourists, choosing Sochi, Adler for a vacation, often face a dilemma: which accommodation option should I prefer? This is not a problem today. Comfortable luxury hotels are built around Adler; there are also hundreds of guest houses. This, of course, is not a hotel in the sense that was supposed before. Guest houses are small mansions or cottages in which from two to ten or twelve families can simultaneously live.

Staying at this mini-hotel you will have a great vacation. Having agreed with the owners, you can solve the problem with food. Many guest houses have small dining rooms where tourists can eat their own cooked food, and use the menu, which only has homemade food. The latter option, according to many, is very beneficial, since prices will be significantly lower than restaurant prices. At the same time, quality and taste are much better than catering dinners. And the rest becomes full, because you do not need to think what to buy for the preparation of breakfast or lunch.

Norm for a Russian tourist

Relaxing in the Caucasus, in Sochi and its suburbs, choosing such guest houses for living, has already become a very common type of vacation for our compatriots. Staying in a small cozy house by the sea, for example, in Adler, it turned out to many Russian tourists a more attractive option than living in a room on a high floor in a fashionable hotel. Here you can simply relax without the many conventions that are characteristic of upscale hotels. In such mini-hotels, according to many Russians with an average income level, you will spend your vacation not only much cheaper, but also calmer and even more interesting. This is especially true for those who go to sea with small children. Guest houses in Adler by the sea will appeal to all visitors.

Description

A significant part of the private sector hotels are located within a five or ten minute walk to the beach, and taking into account the even terrain it takes very little effort to walk to the coastline. The pricing policy in this town is the same as in any other resort: the closer the private guest houses of Adler, Sochi from the sea, the higher their cost. Of course, expensive rooms are also offered on the outskirts, but there are not so many of them. But such guest houses at the Adler resort have more spacious areas. They can offer a price, which includes food. In addition, the owners are trying in conditions of fierce competition in almost all respects to surpass their competitors located in the center or on the shore.
