What options of tariff plans with the included number of services are currently offered by the operator? How to choose the most suitable option for yourself?





The line "Megaphone. Unlimited"

For residents of the Moscow region, a whole range of tariff plans is offered, which already include packages of basic services of various sizes. These tariffs differ from each other in the number of available volumes of services and, as a result, in the size of the subscription fee. The service conditions are the same for each of the proposed options. When connected, the subscription fee is charged for the first 15 days and one second part of the monthly volume of services is available. After 15 days, the second part is debited and the remaining volume of services becomes available to the subscriber. Starting from the next billing period, the subscription fee for unlimited at MegaFon is deducted from the balance sheet once in full. The start date of the billing period is the day when the transition to TP was completed.

Tariff S

The line of Unlimited tariff plans at Megafon starts with the lowest tariff, which has a monthly fee of 300 rubles. With this payment, each month the client will receive:

  • 250 minutes for calls to all numbers in the home region;
  • 1000 megabytes Internet package
  • 200 text messages for sending to subscribers of your region.

Tariff M

This option will be more interesting for customers who prefer more active communication and use of the Internet. For a subscription fee of 500 rubles, the following volumes of data will be provided:

  • 500 minutes, which can be spent on calls to numbers, both your network and other operators;
  • 400 text messages designed to be sent to customers in your home region;
  • 3000 megabyte Internet package.

Tariff L

If the previous tariff plan is small and you need even more services, then you can look at TP ‚ÄúL‚Äù. According to its conditions, the client receives:

  • 1100 minutes for calls to all operators, including MegaFon to other regions;

Tariffs XL and VIP

If you are among the subscribers who are not used to limiting themselves to anything, we recommend that you pay attention to the tariffs XL and VIP. The first offers 2.5 thousand minutes, 2,000 messages and 10 gigabytes of traffic.

The VIP offer has a record number of services offered under the tariff plan:

  • 5,000 minutes and messages;
  • 20 gigabytes of internet.

The subscription fee for XL is 1400 rubles. It is written off from the balance sheet once a month in full. For the tariff, called "VIP", the payment is a larger amount - 2700 rubles.

For all tariff plans, except for the option with the minimum number of services (S), it is assumed that predefined limits can be used even outside the home region. When traveling in Russia, all available minutes in your region, messages and traffic can also be consumed at no extra charge.

It is also worth noting that within the included number of minutes, it means completely unlimited communication with Megafon subscribers in other regions. The only exception is tariff S, which allows you to communicate for free only with people who have a SIM card registered in the same region.

Please note that when counting minutes, calls to Megafon are also taken into account. Thus, if you take the M tariff, for example, then calls to Megafon (in your area and to other cities) also enter in 500 minutes. Only after the package has been used up, it will still be possible to make calls within the network for free.
