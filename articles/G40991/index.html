Marina Grove in Gelendzhik: a brief description, photo

There is one small village in the southern regions, located near Kabardinka and Gelendzhik, at the foot of the Markotkh mountain range. This place is quite far from the sea coast, but its history is very interesting.

The article allows you to get acquainted with some information about Maryina Grove (Gelendzhik).

Village Maryina Roscha

This settlement belongs to the Kabarda rural district, which is part of the municipality of Gelendzhik (resort city). Maryina Roshcha is the closest suburb of Gelendzhik, located almost on the border with the northern part of the city, at the very foot of the Markotkh Range.

The distance from Maryina Grove to the city of Gelendzhik (center) is 5 km, and the city of Novorossiysk is 25 km. The village itself has no access to the coast of the sea. The best beaches are located in Blue Bay and Gelendzhik. The population as of 2010 is more than 1700 inhabitants. Near the village passes the federal highway M4, thanks to which there is a regular transport connection of Maryina Grove with Gelendzhik, Novorossiysk and Kabardinka.

The infrastructure of the village is represented by schools, kindergartens, shops and a first-aid post.

Legend

About Maryina Grove (Gelendzhik) there is an amazing legend. Such a beautiful name of the village is associated with one story about the girl Mary. During the Caucasian War, a duelist exiled from the capital served in Kabardinka. He was followed in these parts by his young wife named Marya. Her husband, a brave warrior, was once mortally wounded by the highlanders, and his loyal horse was brought to the Russians by his faithful horse.

Climate and nature

Living in your own home in Maryina Roscha, Gelendzhik, Kabardinka is very profitable and good. The climate in these places is pleasant, Mediterranean. Early spring, hot summers, mild winters. The average summer temperature is + 25C. Winter weather is characterized by some instability. It is accompanied by precipitation (rain and sleet). Autumn can last until January.

In the village there is Lake Makrygino, which is a favorite place for tourists. Fish and crayfish are found in the pond.

Rich Maryina Grove and flora. Around the village there are pine forests, hornbeams, oaks, beech trees, lindens, dogwood, wild pears, hawthorn bushes and many other species. The wildlife is also rich. Wild goats, wild boars, deer and other mammals live here.
