How to tie matching straps on a sundress? Options - from simple to openwork

What should be the straps on the sundress? Different. For the summer they can be made thin. On autumn clothes, they may very well be very wide. Again, choosing straps for sundresses, it is necessary to evaluate the entire product. They should create a harmonious ensemble.

Simple Straps

To get started, you need to dial a chain of three loops. Then the length of the row will be increased by two loops. They will be the width of the strap on the sundress. If you want to make it wider, then the number of loops in the chain will have to be increased.

First row: three aerials for lifting, on three loops of the chain, tie a double crochet and two more two times, so that in total there are five such columns.

The second and each subsequent row: again 3 loops, then 5 double crochets. The last row of straps must be performed in such a way as to make the reduction two times, that is, to do two double crochet with one vertex.

To straps on the sundress became more elegant, they can be tied with a fan pattern. It is formed by five double crochets connected from one base. You need to alternate them with connecting posts.





The easiest webbing

For such a strap, you will need to immediately dial the chain of the desired length. Then, on it, execute three rows of half-columns without a crochet. If the yarn is very thin, and you want to make the strap wide, then the number of rows is supposed to increase. For a thicker strap, you can replace the half-columns without a crochet with the same elements, only with a crochet.

To make them better combined with a sundress, you can tie them along the outer edge with an element from the main pattern.

Large fan pattern option

For such a strap, you will need to dial the chain so that it is immediately the right length. It should be borne in mind that the pattern repeat is 6 loops. The first row of the pattern is formed by connecting posts.

In the second row, you need to connect the arches of three loops, which must be attached to each third element of the previous row with a connecting column.

The third row of the pattern: two lifting loops, 9 double crochets ("large fan") in the first arch, connecting - to the top of the second, continue to knit such elements to the end of the row.

Repeat this pattern on the second side of the strap. On a sarafan they need to be fastened with the same yarn. The straps are simply sewn in the right places.





Lace strap

To knit it, you just need to dial the initial chain. But not simple, but double. The beginning of her knitting is a knot that is at a great distance from the end. Because both of its ends will be involved in the work. Then tie one air loop.

Make a yarn with a working thread, hold the hook under the free end and pick up the working thread. Pull it through everything that is on the hook. That is, the main working thread is always picked up and stretched, and the free end rises and attaches to the chain.





Crochet openwork straps option for sundress or top

It will require a chain of 7 loops. In the first row you need to make 3 loops for lifting, and then in the same base - a double crochet; in the 4th loop of the chain make three double crochets; in the 7th, tie two of the same columns. The second row and all the others are an exact repetition of the first.
