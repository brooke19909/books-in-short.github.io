Hotel Caesar (Turkey): there is everything for a good rest

Hotel Caesar (Turkey) was founded in 1995, it operates on an all-inclusive basis. The territory of this beautiful place occupies about 200,000 square meters. The hotel is located in a picturesque location on the Mediterranean coast and is surrounded by a pine forest. Belek village is 5 km away, and the international airport is 30 km away. Bright nature, warm sea, numerous events, the highest class service - all this is offered by Turkey, the Caesar Hotel. Reviews of tourists after a vacation in this complex do not allow to doubt the magnificence of service, service and the desire to visit it more than once.



What are hotel rooms?

Everything is designed for any requirements and tastes. Hotel Caesar (Turkey) cordially offers to choose a room in the main building of the complex or to settle in a bungalow or villa. Whatever you stay at, comfort and a magnificent view from the window are provided to you. Each room has a private bathroom, satellite TV, telephone, air conditioning, safe, minibar. And if you choose a villa or a bungalow, then you can still relax in the jacuzzi.



What is in the hotel?

Hotel Caesar (Turkey) offers its guests all the best for an unforgettable vacation. The complex has six bars, five restaurants, tennis courts, water slides. If you have arrived not only to relax, but also to work, then at your service are conference rooms that can accommodate up to 300 people. Three pools, a beauty salon, spa, doctor's consultation, laundry - all this is provided by the Caesar Hotel (Turkey).



Dining at the hotel complex

As mentioned above, vacationers can either relax in the bar or try delicious dishes of both local and European cuisine in one of the restaurants. They are open six days a week, but tables need to be reserved in advance. Meals in the main restaurant are built on an all-inclusive basis. This system provides the guests of the complex with the first and second breakfast buffet, lunch, snacks and two dinners. In addition to the standard there is a children's and diet menu.



Entertainment

The Caesar Hotel (Turkey) is an ideal place to relax, because a team of professional animators works here throughout the season. They organize leisure activities for adults and children on the beach and near the pools. If you enjoy outdoor activities, then be sure to visit the entertainment and dance costume shows, an open disco right on the beach. Or spend an evening in the amphitheater, which is built in the antique style. During the day, fun competitions and competitions on land and on water are held. Do you want to attend dance or water gymnastics courses? Not a problem! Would you like to play soccer, darts, volleyball? Not a question at all! The hotel administrators did not forget about their little visitors. On the territory of the complex there is a mini-club consisting of several fabulous houses: a mini-cinema, a place for games, a children's bedroom, a restaurant, a separate amphitheater. Professional educators deal with children, conduct all kinds of contests, developing games, competitions. On the territory there is a playground with carousels and swings, a small water slide, a swimming pool and many other places where children will be busy with useful and interesting things.



Only the most positive emotions arouse in Turkey. Hotel Caesar, the photo of which you can see here, will remain in your memory forever, and you will want to return to this fairy tale more than once.
