Hotel Viking Star SPA 5 * (Turkey, Kemer): description, photos, prices and reviews of tourists

If you, like millions of other residents of our country, plan to spend your holidays in Turkey and are looking for a comfortable hotel at a reasonable price, then pay attention to Viking Star SPA 5 *, located in Kemer. We suggest that you get to know this hotel more closely and find out what awaits guests here.



Location

The five-star Viking Star SPA Hotel is located in the very center of the largest resort city of Turkey - Kemer, on the second coastline. In walking distance for guests there is everything you need: shops, banks, restaurants, clubs, etc. The distance to the sea is 800 meters. The nearest airport is in Antalya. The distance to it is 55 kilometers. Thus, upon arrival on the way to the hotel, it will take you a little over an hour.

Since the hotel is located at some distance from the coast, shuttles running on this route all day are delivered to the beach. So tourists will not have to walk to the sea.



The cost of living in a hotel

According to our compatriots, the price for accommodation in this hotel is quite a budget for a five-star category. So, a seven-day stay here at the height of the holiday season, which falls on July-August, will cost you from 70 thousand rubles for a single room, from 93 thousand rubles for double apartments and from 126 thousand rubles for a family room. The indicated prices assume all-inclusive meals.

Viking Star SPA 5 *: traveler reviews from Russia

Today, many tourists, before deciding on a hotel choice in a particular country, want to collect as much information about it as possible. A large role in this is played by the reviews of people who have already had occasion to stop at the place of interest. In this regard, we decided to make your task a little easier and give generalized comments by our compatriots regarding their vacation at the Turkish five-star Viking Star SPA hotel. Looking ahead, we note that most tourists really liked the hotel. They were satisfied and do not regret the money spent. But we will understand in more detail.



The hotel itself and its territory

Most of the tourists were satisfied with the rooms provided. So, according to them, the apartments are quite spacious, clean, with fresh repairs. The furniture is not very new, but of high quality. The same goes for technology. Everything is functioning properly in the bathroom. Cleaning, change of towels and linen, replenishment of toiletries is carried out daily. In this regard, the guests had no complaints. If you leave a tip, the maids will twist the towels of swans and other figures. Please note that water in the minibar is only available on the day of arrival. In the future, it can be dialed from the cooler. Seasoned travelers are advised not to leave food in the room. The hotel has a lot of ants, and they certainly will not fail to drop in to visit you if there is anything to profit from. As for the Internet, then Wi-Fi, according to the guests, is in the hotel everywhere: both in the building and on the street.

The hotel area seemed to our compatriots small. However, there is enough space for everyone here.

Tourists leave a lot of positive comments regarding the location of Viking Star SPA 5 *. So, according to our compatriots, the hotel is located in the city center. Therefore, all infrastructure facilities for its guests will be within walking distance. So, in the vicinity of the hotel there are large clothing and shoe stores, supermarkets, souvenir shops, banks, restaurants, bars, etc.



Nutrition Comments

In terms of food, most of the guests of Viking Star SPA 5 * (Kemer) were not disappointed. So, according to them, the choice has always been a lot of perfectly prepared dishes from meat, poultry, seafood. Also constantly available was a mass of side dishes, fresh fruits and delicious desserts. In the evenings, grilled dishes were prepared. So nobody will be hungry here.

However, some of our compatriots mention one drawback in their comments. It concerns lunch boxes, which you can ask to prepare at the reception if you go on an excursion early and do not have time to have breakfast. So, according to the guests, they put very little food in them. Therefore, experienced travelers recommend starting in the evening to grab rolls from the restaurant, sausages, cheese and other products that can complement breakfast.
