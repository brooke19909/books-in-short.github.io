"Tashla" ski resort (Orenburg region): description

In the Orenburg region there are many beautiful places. People love to go outdoors not only in the warm season, but also in winter. At this time, many sports are available. Skiing and skiing from the mountains always bring a lot of positive emotions. Tashla ski resort (Orenburg region) invites its visitors to have a great time. Here you can enjoy nature and air, as well as go down the mountain. Great experiences will be provided.

General information

A village called Tashla has its own name for a reason. Translated from the Turkic language, it means "rocky". Here you can see mountains and hills that are great for skiing. Lovers of winter sports often come here. The recreation center "Tashla" (Orenburg region) is also suitable for beginners, as the conditions for training are the most optimal. Small differences in altitude will allow beginners to learn to ski and snowboard. If necessary, it is available to seek help from instructors who work in the complex. There is a special lift for visitors. It is equally convenient for both adults and the younger generation.

Tashla ski resort (Orenburg region) is well known not only in the region, but throughout the country. For the guests prepared excellent service and comfortable conditions. All the necessary equipment can be rented directly in the complex.

The place is often called "Orenburg Switzerland", as it can compete with well-known resorts. Here you can not only admire the local beauties, but also engage in many sports. The ski run is six hundred meters long. Guests can go tubing, skiing, ice skating, and use the sauna. The dining room and cafe are open for the possibility of food during the holidays. The place is also great for teaching children winter sports.

Address

Ski complex "Tashla" is located on Lenin Street, building - 5. Not far from the center is the Timashevs park and the estate of the same name. They can be a guideline for searching.

How to get there

There are several ways to get to the sports complex. If visitors travel by car, they can leave the car in a dedicated parking lot. It is also accessible from Orenburg. For this you need to ride intercity buses. Suitable "Orenburg-Gorodki" or "Orenburg-Tugustemir." Both routes make a stop in the village. From there you can go to the ski base "Tashla" (Orenburg region).

Additional Information

In the sports complex for visitors there are lots of options for a great pastime. In addition to skiing, you can play badminton, volleyball, soccer and tennis. For an active lifestyle, there is a special platform with good coverage. Athletes can use a treadmill two hundred meters long. In addition, you can always go to work out in the gym. In winter, an ice rink is poured at the stadium for visitors. In the indoor center, guests can play billiards and many board games.

Tashla ski resort (Orenburg region) is suitable for holidays on weekends and holidays. A hotel is open here, so you can stay overnight for the required number of days. The hotel can immediately accommodate about two hundred people. There are rooms for both two and four persons. They have all the necessary conditions. Guests can choose a room with a bathroom, a TV and a shower. There are also economy options. They have amenities on the floor. If desired, it is available to rent a superior room. Guests' cars can be parked with security and video surveillance. Visitors note a low cost of living.
