Water & Amusement Parks in Anapa

The Black Sea coast is the most common place for summer holidays in Russia. From the southern coast of Taman to Adler, dozens of resort towns and villages are located. Anapa is one of the most visited resorts in Russia.

The history of Anapa is rich and interesting. For hundreds of years, the territory of the city was inhabited by various peoples. With each new ruler, culture also changed, which left unique historical and cultural monuments. Archaeological Museum "Gorgippia" is located on the embankment of Anapa. It is a monument of the ancient period and represents the excavation of the ancient city of Gorgippia, once prosperous and known for its crafts. The monuments of eventful Anapa history are also the Russian Gate, the lighthouse, the crypt of Heroon. Anapa's modern monuments are also interesting: a monument to a vacationer, "White Hat", a floral clock. Monuments to outstanding figures of science and culture - A. S. Pushkin, V. A. Budzinsky (the founder of the resort), A. S. Korytin (the creator of the seaplane) - are in the city called Anapa. Water Parks Anapa

Places where you can have great fun with friends or the whole family are water parks. Amusement park "Sunny Island" is located just near the huge water park "Tiki-Tak." He can offer about ten different pools, including for the smallest, more than twenty water slides and rafting. "Rainy Season" is a fun little island for kids.



Directly near the coastline, in the center of the resort town, there is the Golden Beach amusement park on the water. This water park is the most popular among residents and guests of the city, due to its convenient location and the presence on its territory of a large number of places for entertainment. For young visitors there is a whole complex "Treasure Island", where there are many slides, bridges and passages for fidgets.
