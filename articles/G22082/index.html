Shopping center "Cosmoport", Samara: address, photos and reviews

The Cosmoport shopping center in Samara is one of the most beloved places of citizens. It is here that adults and children will find refuge. There are a lot of good stores that sell absolutely everything. Also at Cosmoport there is an entertainment center and a good movie theater. In between shopping, guests of the shopping and entertainment center can have a bite to eat in the establishments, which are also numerous there.



general information

The shopping center "Cosmoport" is located in Samara at the address: st. Dybenko, 30. It is in the very center of the city, which allows you to get to it without much effort, even using public transport.

Cosmoport is a shopping and entertainment center that spans 14 hectares of city square. Such a large scale of the premises allows you to place more than 180 different shops, about 15 restaurants and cafes in the complex, a huge entertainment area for children and an incredible movie theater, in the halls of which citizens gather every day to watch the latest news from domestic and foreign cinema.

The Cosmoport shopping center (Samara), despite its enormous size, was not built for long (one might even say, in record time). In just one year, the building of a huge shopping center was elevated on an empty territory, which was immediately filled with places for recreation and shops.

All institutions and shops in the Cosmoport shopping center (Samara) are located on three floors (first, second and basement).



The shops

The shopping and entertainment center has a huge number of stores, many of which sell original products from world famous manufacturers.

On the ground floor of the building, there were 9 pharmacies and stores at once that sell goods to maintain health and beauty: Focus Optic, Doctor Lens, Okvision Optics, Implosion, Melody of Health, Zdorovit, First Samara Optics "," US Media "," Luxurious Vision ". There is also a store that sells pet products - Beethoven. It is located on the ground floor.

On the territory of the Cosmoport shopping center in Samara there are four huge hypermarkets, which are also located on the ground floor of the center. These are Media Market, a hypermarket of electronic and household appliances, AUCHAN hypermarket, Leroy Merlin, a store for home and repair goods, as well as Sportmaster, a sports goods hypermarket.



Jewelry and accessories here can be purchased at Gala Collection ("Gala Collection"), Tiara & Shampoo ("Tiara & Shampuu"), Zifa ("Zifa"), "Snow Queen". There are also 14 shops on the ground floor where you can buy perfumes and cosmetics for the whole family: B&B (B & B), NYX (NUX), Divage ("Divage"), Organic Shop ("Organic Shop"), profCOSMO ("ProfCOSMO"), TONY MOLY ("Tony Moli"), Yves Rocher ("Yves Rocher"), "Zdorovit", "L'Etoile", "On Health.ru", "Hairdressing shop", "Rive Gauche" , "Constellation of Beauty."

The shopping center has 10 branded cellular stores, 9 stores where you can buy gifts and souvenirs, 13 stores with goods for repair and leisure, 7 stores that sell books, games, and hobby items. There are also 11 stores selling electronics and household appliances.



As for clothing and shoe stores, a considerable number of representatives of well-known brands rent a place in the Cosmoport shopping center. So, here you can find shops of such manufacturers as Calvin Cleine ("Kelvin Kline"), Colin's ("Collins"), Bershka ("Bershka"), Black Star Wear, Lagerfeld ("Lagerfeld"), Pierre Cardin ("Pierre Cardin "), Modis (" Modis "), a fur boutique" The Snow Queen ", and some others.



Cafes, restaurants

In the Cosmoport shopping center (Samara) there are many places where you can have a tasty and satisfying snack - these are 13 cafes and 3 restaurants.

On the ground floor of the shopping and entertainment complex you can find a cafe of the famous French brand Cinnabon, drop in Coffee Like, or go to the famous McDonald's. Also for fast food lovers, there are KFC cafes and BurgerKing. In addition, there are other places: 33 Penguins, Starbucks, YES! Pizza, TashirPizza, SushiPort.



As for restaurants, there are only three of them. One of them is the restaurant of Italian cuisine "Perchini", the second serves Japanese and Italian cuisine, and it is called "TOSE", and the third is a cozy coffee house called CARRIE ("Carrie"). All three establishments are located on the second floor of the complex.

Services

Right on the territory of the Cosmoport shopping center there is an opportunity to do manicure in one of the studios that provide such services: Queen B Nail (Queen Bee Nail), Lena Lenina's manicure studio and Lyubov Ionova's manicure studio. You can also make eyebrow tattoos in Brow Art Studios ("Brow Art") or pierce your ears in a specialized salon, which is located on the ground floor.

In the same shopping center there are several items for the repair of household appliances and electronics, a jewelry workshop and dry cleaning "Terem". On the second floor there is an atelier where everyone can order tailoring according to individual standards.

Cinema "Kinomost"

One of the places favorite by the townspeople is the Kinomost cinema in the Cosmoport shopping center in Samara. It is located on the second floor of the complex and occupies a large area. It is here that every visitor has a very wide choice of a viewing platform for cinema novelties, since the cinema consists of nine halls, which can accommodate 1340 people at a time. Each hall is equipped with the best sound systems and high-quality film projection equipment, which allows you to fully enjoy watching movies at the cinema in the Cosmoport shopping center (Samara).



Children's entertainment center "Vacation"

For the youngest guests in the shopping and entertainment center there is a children's amusement park, which is called the "Vacation". It is also located on the second floor and occupies a large area. On the territory of this park there are many attractions that are designed for children of different ages, various entertaining events are constantly held with the participation of kids, events and master classes. On the territory of the park it is possible to carry out various children's events, for the organization of which the Vacation administration takes with great pleasure.

In between entertainment, guests and their parents can go to the children's cafe, which is located in the park. The reviews of those who visited this shopping center are mostly positive. Many say that everything can be bought without leaving the building and without rushing anywhere.

How to get there

As mentioned above, the address where you can find the Cosmoport shopping center is Samara, ul. Dybenko, 30.

This point of the city is located in the center, at the intersection of several transport routes. That is why it is very convenient to get here both by public transport, and your own car or taxi. By the way, for customers of the shopping and entertainment center near the building there is free large parking.

As for the access by public transport, you can get here by bus to the Dybenko stop by routes 66, 129, 45, 46, 22, by trolleybus No. 15 to the Avtomagazin stop or by trams 2, 14, 23 to the same stop .

In the event that the visitor arrives at the shopping center in his own car, then he needs to know that the entrance to the parking lot is from the streets of the Soviet Army and Antonov-Evseenko.
