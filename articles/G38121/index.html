Water planet

Water Planet - one of the largest water parks in Turkey, it is only 35 kilometers away from Side towards Alanya. This is a unique place with the best entertainment of the Turkish Riviera. The water park is located on the seafront, surrounded by lush vegetation. Here you will find the most amazing water attractions for every taste and age: a pool area for the whole family, a slide with four parallel lanes for adults, a children's zone with water activities, a kamikaze hill - for adrenaline lovers.
