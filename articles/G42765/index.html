What is a loan?

The transfer of value from one owner to another for temporary use is called a loan. This is a kind of system of economic relations, allowing on special conditions to use the financial assistance of an individual or legal entity. Today, many financial institutions offer loan programs . It is possible to take money in cash or purchase goods on credit. In this case, the bank transfers a certain amount immediately to the seller‚Äôs account, and the buyer pays the debt to the financial institution.



Loan agreements today are executed on various conditions. The interest rate as well as the maturity are taken into account in the first place. As a rule, the smaller the amount indicated in the contract, the greater the overpayment will result. To apply for simple consumer loans, the client needs to provide a minimum package of documents: a passport, as well as an identification code. If a contract is drawn up for the purchase of real estate or a vehicle, additionally financial institutions are asked to provide a statement of income, as well as information about the place of work. In some cases, you must come to the bank with a surety. Credits can be issued by any adult citizen.
