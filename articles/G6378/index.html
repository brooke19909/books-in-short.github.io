Waterpark "Aquarius", Nikolaev - an active vacation for the whole family. Photos and reviews

"Aquarius" (Nikolaev) is a wonderful water park in which everyone can enjoy a fun and unforgettable experience: both a child and an adult.

Description

This complex is located in the southern part of the city. Here you can do many water sports, have a great time. Waterpark "Aquarius" is an institution with a good reputation, which has managed to establish itself as a well-equipped recreation area.



The first guests came here back in 1997. Since that time, work is constantly being done to improve the available slides and improve the quality of service. Nikolaev water park is an excellent choice for those who do not want to depend on natural vagaries, air temperature in the street and other factors that a person can not control.

Thanks to the attraction, thrills can be obtained by flying down the hill, spraying water jets in all directions, even when snow is blowing on the street. Who said that only summer time is suitable for water activities?

Features

Attention deserves the attraction "Water slide". It is twisted into a spiral. Rising to a height, people slide down it, gaining speed and eventually flying into the pool. Typically, such a descent causes an adrenaline rush and thrills.

There is also a children's pool, where you can give your child to go swimming. If your child is not yet five years old, classes for him can be completely free. There are separate rates for adults and athletes who also practice in local halls. Benefits are provided for pensioners, students, orphans and anti-terrorist operation participants.
