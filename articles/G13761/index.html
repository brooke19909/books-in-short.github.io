Waterpark of Magnitogorsk "Waterfall of Miracles": photos and reviews

Not so long ago there was an ordinary average basin on this place, vividly reminiscent of the Soviet past of the country. Not much time has passed since the start of repair work, and the ultramodern water park of Magnitogorsk has grown. He has no competitors and cannot be, since this is the only water-entertainment center in the entire Southern Urals.



History of occurrence

The water park of Magnitogorsk was born in the projects of its inventors more than 10 years ago. Many foreign experts who already had extensive experience in such construction participated in its implementation. Russian architects and builders also played an important role in the birth of this giant. As a result, this wonderful complex was born. The water park of Magnitogorsk was created specifically to provide recreational and recreational services at affordable prices. Moreover, their range is incredibly high, both adults and children will find classes here. Disabled access to the complex is provided.

Imagine that you live in the tropics, and not in the capricious climate of the Southern Urals. Regardless of the vagaries of nature and the time of year, you can enjoy a beach holiday, without spending time and money on a trip out of town. The entertainment complex is significantly different from the boring riverbank. Here you can experience the excitement and severity of sensations, improve health and undergo useful procedures. Equipped sports fields and gyms will help improve your athletic performance. Thus, the Waterfall Wonderland water park (Magnitogorsk) is a place where you will always find a favorable microclimate, friendly staff and emotional comfort.

Contraindications

They are indicated on the signs of the water park, but we recall again. People who are intoxicated are not allowed on the rides . Children and pregnant women can ride only from small, gentle slides. If you have serious diseases of the cardiovascular system, then such loads can be harmful.

The water park staff constantly monitors the performance of devices. The optimum air temperature is maintained at +30 degrees, the water in the pools is not less than +28. Humidity is not more than 56%.

Children's town water park

The Dolphin pool consists of three bathtubs and can simultaneously accommodate up to 60 children. Here are excellent conditions for kids: underfloor heating, air temperature not lower than +32 degrees and a huge number of inflatable toys. This contributes to the rapid development of swimming skills. On the basis of this complex are working groups "mother and child" for babies from 4 months. Instructors conduct hardening and learn to swim. Of particular importance are these classes for children with disabilities or small patients with impaired posture. Here, too, there are slides and attractions, which are much smaller than adults, but still very interesting.

The children's area often becomes the venue for fun water discos and noisy competitions, here you can celebrate the birthday of the child, he will remember such a celebration for a long time. Sports sections work here on a separate schedule, but you can just relax with your child at any time.
