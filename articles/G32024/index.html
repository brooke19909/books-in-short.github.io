General information about the navigator for mushroom picker

Modern technologies allow a person to greatly simplify their life. So, even for mushroom pickers there is a gadget. No matter how loud such a phrase may sound, but it is true. The mushroom picker is a real device that tracks the current position of a person and shows him the direction of movement.

What is it



The mushroom picker is a GPS device. Its main purpose is to indicate the current location of the gadget. Most often, this is a very compact equipment with a small screen and minimal power consumption. Unlike automobile devices of a similar purpose, it should have a number of characteristics.

How to choose

In the market you can find many devices for this purpose. But it should be clearly understood that the navigator for the mushroom picker will be used in certain conditions. And this leaves an imprint on its design and operational characteristics.

Reception quality

This characteristic is one of the key. The accuracy of determining the current coordinates depends on it. And in dense forests it is very difficult to break through a signal to satellites through the crowns of trees.

Working hours

An equally important indicator that should be considered when choosing a device is the battery life. Few people go in search of mushrooms for only half an hour. In addition, various force majeure circumstances may arise along the way. And this means that the time spent in the forest can be extended. And in this case, turning off the navigator due to low battery can lead to undesirable consequences.





Water resistant

Of great importance is this indicator. In no case should you take a navigator for a mushroom picker without moisture protection. After all, even a small drop of dew when it gets inside the device is able to disable it. And what can we say about rain, which can suddenly begin at any moment.

Tagging

This parameter is not fundamental. It can be classified as useful options. The ability to label is not present on all devices. This option is useful because on the map you can independently indicate a mushroom place, which can be returned at the next visit to the forest.

Download bitmaps

This characteristic should also be attributed to the optional category. It is useful in that you can download satellite raster images to the navigator, from which it will be clear where is which forest, where is the clearing. This will allow you to navigate on the spot and continue the path in the right direction.





Cost

On a mushroom picker navigator, the price depends on many factors. This is the operational characteristics of the product, and the eminence of the manufacturer. The most popular are models from Garmin, Magellan and Bushnell. These companies have long been engaged in the production of navigation tourist equipment and have established themselves only from the best sides. The minimum will have to pay about 3,500 rubles for a budget navigator for mushroom pickers.

Owner reviews

Owners of such devices with full confidence speak not only about the usefulness of this device, but also about its necessity. Firstly, they note the ease of orientation on the ground using the navigator. Secondly, the device gives them a sense of confidence in their safety, as you can always return to their starting point. Thirdly, all mushroom pickers talk about the ease of use of equipment and its unpretentiousness.
