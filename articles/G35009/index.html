Nokia 222 phone: reviews

Microsoft, which acquired the Nokia brand, continues to market devices related to classic phones. Among the most famous models of devices of the corresponding type in the Russian market is the Nokia 222. What are its features? What are the main competitive advantages of the device?





Device Overview

The device in question has been supplied to the Russian market since September 2015. The device is presented in 2 versions - classic and Dual SIM. A remarkable fact can be noted: Microsoft, which at that time acquired Nokia‚Äôs assets, continued to use the name of this Finnish brand in models of classic phones, while the American corporation began to release smartphones on its behalf.

The device is made using the classic form factor, has a color display and a standard keyboard unit. Phone Nokia 222 - reviews can confirm this, - characterized by compactness, relatively light weight, fairly capacious battery. It has a built-in FM radio. The phone is controlled by a branded operating system, which is supplemented by a large number of pre-installed programs.





The device is available in black and white color versions with a glossy case. In this case, in the second case, the device is often referred to as Nokia 222 Rome. Reviews about the design of the device can be quite positive. The phone looks very stylish and is suitable for users with a variety of clothing preferences. For formal suits, Nokia 222 Black is suitable (user reviews can confirm this). The Rome version will look great with casual wear.





Device specifications

Consider the main characteristics of the device Nokia 222, reviews of which are found on many thematic online portals. This device has:

- support for communication standards GSM 900, 1800, 1900, 850;

- monoblock case with a standard telephone keypad;

- support for 2 SIM-cards - in the corresponding version;

- a screen classified as TFT with a diagonal of 2.4 inches, a resolution of 240 by 320 pixels, an indicator of the number of pixels per inch of 167 PPI;

- a camera with a resolution of 2 MP, which also allows you to record videos with a resolution of 320 by 240 pixels and a maximum frequency of 15 frames / sec;

- built-in FM-radio;

- Dictaphone;

- headphone jack;

- support for communication interfaces Bluetooth, micro-USB, WAP, GPRS, EDGE;

- slot for connecting additional micro-SD memory cards with a maximum capacity of 32 GB;

- a battery capacity of 1100 mAh, which ensures the operation of the device in standby mode for about 504 hours.

These characteristics generally correspond to those that characterize a typical device in the corresponding price segment. But the phone in question has a number of undeniable advantages. To understand what they are, we study its characteristics in more detail.





Software

We noted above that the phone operates on the basis of a branded operating system. It's about the Nokia Series 30+ OS. This OS allows you to install applications such as, for example, the Opera browser in a version adapted for use on relevant devices, Facebook, Twitter, and many other popular solutions.

Display

As we noted above, the device is equipped with a fairly miniature display with a diagonal of 2.4 inches. At the same time, the device allows you to display colors in QVGA mode, which is quite an impressive indicator for classic phones. On the screen of the Nokia 222 device - user reviews can confirm this - it is quite possible to view in sufficient detail the contents of multimedia files created using the device‚Äôs camera or downloaded to the phone using the available communication interfaces.

Battery

The battery capacity of the phone is 1100 mAh, which allows, if you follow the data provided by the manufacturer, use it without recharging in standby mode for about 504 hours. Regarding talk time, the battery lasts about 20 hours. If you listen to music on the phone, then the battery resources will be enough, in turn, for about 50 hours.

Actually, the powerful battery that the Nokia 222 phone is equipped with - user reviews can testify to this, is one of its key competitive advantages. In principle, few modern devices in the corresponding category have a comparable resource. Even for those users who are used to using modern mobile applications, the battery capabilities of the phone in question can come in handy in many situations. Therefore, to acquire an appropriate device only because of the presence of a powerful battery in it is a completely reasonable solution.

Camera

The device has a rather modest camera by modern standards, which has a resolution of 2 MP. In principle, the phone in question is not designed to perform the multimedia functions that characterize the work of modern smartphones and tablets, which are equipped with significantly more powerful cameras. But, using the one that is installed in the Nokia 222 DS device (reviews can confirm this), the user will be able to take a quick photo, for example, of any important text - for example, an announcement in the subway or at a bus stop, and at the same time its text will be completely readable.





If necessary, you can record a short video in the quality that is sufficient to certify certain circumstances significant for the phone user - for example, the timely appearance of an appointment by appointment. From this point of view, the camera makes it possible to solve important tasks of the user.

Communications

The device in question does not support 3G and 4G standards for connecting to the Internet, but allows you to use various network resources using the WAP and GPRS protocols. The bandwidth of the respective channels is enough to, for example, read or send a message by e-mail, upload or download a small file - for example, represented by a text document or a photograph with a low resolution.

If necessary, the device can be connected to third-party devices via USB- interface or Bluetooth in version 3.0. These standards are quite universal and allow you to exchange files between your phone and a wide range of other multimedia devices.



Among those features of the device that are especially appealing to users and experts are support for connecting microSD memory cards. Thanks to this option, the user is able to quickly save data generated using the multimedia tools of the phone on a reliable medium. This option can be useful when traveling when the Internet and other devices may not be available for this purpose. A capacious battery is also useful here, which will allow for autonomous operation of the device even during long journeys.

Reviews

Let us consider in more detail what users write about their experience in utilizing the capabilities of the Nokia 222. The user reviews can be considered the most powerful aspects of the device:

- a powerful battery;

- support for many modern communication technologies;

- the presence of a large number of useful applications;

- universality;

- reliability;

- ease of use in terms of enabling both hardware and software functions.

There are few obvious flaws in the phone in terms of its consideration as a device sold in the corresponding market segment. These are perhaps the least powerful camera - some offers in the same price segment in this sense can be considered as more competitive, and also maybe not the most technologically advanced display. However, classic phones - user and expert reviews can confirm this - are less designed to take advantage of camera and display capabilities. Among the most popular functions of devices in this segment is a capacious battery, support for basic communication technologies. From this point of view, the Nokia 222 mobile phone - reviews can confirm this - can be attributed to the most competitive solutions.

Summary

So, Nokia 222 is a classic device that combines both traditional and modern technologies. It can be called a fairly universal device: it has support for a wide range of modern communication standards, allows you to use popular mobile applications, create and view various types of multimedia content.





Most likely, the main competitive advantage of the device is a powerful battery. It allows you to provide autonomous operation of the device while traveling or, if necessary, to conduct a long conversation at a time when it is not possible to recharge the device. Phone Nokia 222 Dual SIM - reviews can confirm this - it is compact, easy to use, and functions reliably. Thus, it can be considered a very competitive product in the modern Russian and world market of communication devices.
