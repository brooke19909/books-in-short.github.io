How to return life insurance for a car loan: procedure, necessary documents and calculation of the amount of payments

Credit organizations that seek to get the most out of risk often require the client to receive a life insurance policy.

Buyers have the right not to conclude an agreement on unfavorable conditions for them. Moreover, they are not required to sign an agreement on the provision of any insurance services. You just need to inform the manager that this option is not of interest. And you need to do this even at the stage of paperwork, but after a positive decision from the bank is received.

How can I refuse a registered policy?

In the event that the agreement was nevertheless signed or the clause on the policy was included in the loan agreement by default, is it possible to return life insurance for a car loan? It should be noted that in no case should you delay the termination of such an agreement. The chances of getting your money back every day will be less.

You need to contact the insurers directly. To do this, they write an application for the return of life insurance for a car loan, collect the necessary papers and wait for an answer. If the company does not meet, then you can go to Rospotrebnadzor, RSA, the Central Bank or to the court.

It is important to note that if more than six months have passed since the conclusion of the agreement, then the insurers may refuse to satisfy the client‚Äôs claim, referring to the fact that the financial resources were spent on all kinds of administrative expenses.

With an insurance premium of more than fifty thousand rubles, you can request a report on how exactly the funds were spent. If the cost of the policy was included in the loan amount, you still have to contact the bank. After parting with insurers, it will be necessary to revise the terms of the agreement in terms of recalculating the loan amount and the size of monthly payments.

Procedure

How to return life insurance for a car loan? The procedure in this case will be as follows:

  • Draw up a written claim to the insurance organization and to the bank. It must indicate the reasons why a person wishes to refuse this service.
  • Within ten days after the submission of the corresponding complaint, the banking organization together with the insurer must give an answer. In practice, there are many cases in which lenders made concessions to their customers, offering to settle everything in peace.
  • If they do not agree to concessions, then they will have to go to court with a lawsuit. You should not hope for a very quick and at the same time positive outcome, since any large company has its own legal department that deals with such issues.

During consideration by the court all circumstances of the case will be established. In the event that the bank actually exceeded its authority and forced the client to conclude an unnecessary agreement at an overestimated rate (insurance was included in the loan amount), a decision may be made regarding the return of life insurance on a car loan. True, this is not always the case, since it is usually difficult to prove that a person did not know about unfavorable conditions for himself. Typically, all items are prescribed in documents under which borrowers put their signature, often without reading them. Only then they learn that a fairly large amount is added to the cost of the car, which will also be charged interest.

Thus, repaying funds for life insurance on car loans can be quite problematic, and borrowers will have a lengthy lawsuit. Even if such a thing gives a positive outcome, further problems may still arise. For example, banks may increase the interest rate on the loan for the remaining period, if such an item was provided in the contract. As a result, borrowers will have to pay more, and the benefits of the process will not be so significant. When the increase in insurance is not provided unilaterally, then the credit institution may terminate the contract.

Amount calculation

It is worth paying attention to the fact that customers have only five calendar days from the date of signing the agreement to return life insurance for a car loan. It was such a term that was legalized in June 2016, and borrowers who do not want to use certain imposed services should fit into it. If a citizen succeeds during this period, then the insurer is obliged to return the paid sum to him, and quickly enough, namely within ten working days. Basically, this money is transferred to a loan.

But what can be done if this deadline has already passed? Then you need to carefully read the terms of your contract with the insurance company, see how it is terminated and find out whether the payment can be returned.

Most often, this is possible only with the condition of early settlements by the buyer with a credit institution. To return life insurance for early repayment of a car loan, you must provide a certificate of lack of debt. They take her at the bank. After receiving it, the client also contacts the insurer or the financial institution in order to receive part of their money.

Required documents

So, as already noted, in case of missing deadlines, the return of life insurance for a car loan is allowed only if the loan is repaid ahead of schedule. At the same time, it‚Äôs worth trying to return at least some of your contribution. For this, they turn to an insurance company, and not to a banking organization. The bottom line is that you need to write an application, submit documents and wait for an answer.

Together with the application, the company should provide:

  • Client's passport along with a copy of the car loan agreement.
  • A policy with a certificate taken from the bank on the full repayment of existing debt.
  • Receipt of payment of insurance premium.
  • Details of the account where you want to return the funds.

Under the terms of the agreement, it may be prescribed that insurance is not returned in case of premature settlement with the bank, then the client, unfortunately, will not receive any money back.

How to return life insurance for a car loan at VTB?

In insurance practice, there is a concept of a cooling period during which customers can refuse the company‚Äôs services and return their funds if there was no corresponding event. This measure is considered very beneficial for borrowers. Now consider how to return life insurance for a car loan at VTB.

So, termination of contracts during the cooling periods is as follows. After receiving a loan, customers collect a specific set of documents. For example, a passport along with an insurance contract, receipt of payment of a premium, and if the amount is included in the cost of the loan, then a loan agreement with a repayment schedule. Among other things, you will need the details of the account where the funds will be returned.

In addition, you need to prepare an application for exclusion of the client from the number of participants in the voluntary insurance program. Its form is not established by law. The form can be taken from the appropriate organization or found on the Internet. The application must indicate the name of the applicant along with passport details, the name of the creditor, a link to the contract, as well as the insured risks. An application shall be accompanied by an inventory of all documents.

He is served in the company with which the contract was concluded. It must be written in duplicate, on one of which the employee of the organization puts the registration number and date. It is allowed to send this paper with notification by registered mail through the Russian Post. The terms of consideration are ten days from the date of registration. At the end of this period, funds must be transferred at the specified details. In the event that the financial transfer is delayed without explanation, the applicants have the right to file a demand for return to the judicial authority.

Life Insurance Benefits

It is worth noting that this service is beneficial to customers. Moreover, it is fair to say for all three parties (customers, a bank, a company issuing a policy). What is the advantage?

For the company itself, it is about receiving bonus payments, the amount of which is calculated individually or depends on many different factors (objects / subjects of insurance, gender, age, lifestyle, work, etc.), or it is fixed and is formed by multiplying the contribution by certain odds (usually around two percent). In banks, as a rule, the second option is used.

For a financial institution, risk minimization with guaranteed return of funds due to the agreement is noted, and an increase in the size of the loan is observed (therefore, an increase in interest income). In addition, receiving a commission from the company is not excluded.

For a client in the event of an insurance situation and there is no opportunity to return money to the bank, the borrower will not become a debtor, since the insurance office will directly return the money for it. A solid financial compensation can not only repay the debt, but also provide material borrowers for a sufficiently long period.
