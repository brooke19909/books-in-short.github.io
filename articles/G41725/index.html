Phone Nokia 3120: specifications, analogs and reviews

Everyone looking at a photo of Nokia 3120 Classic starts nostalgic. The devices of the Finnish manufacturer can be safely called a legend. For the first time, a model with such an index appeared in 2004. She replaced the not-so-popular Nokia 3100 phone. Why was it not in demand? The reason is the overpriced. Looking at the successor, you can see that the manufacturer has radically changed its approach by releasing a budget copy. And this decision was definitely true! The Nokia 3120 quickly won the hearts of customers. The target audience was mainly composed of young people. People in adulthood rarely paid attention to this model. But this did not affect sales volumes too much. Considering that the selected index is easy to remember, the company decided to continue the line by adding the prefix Classic to the well-known name. It is the characteristics and features of this device that will be considered in the article.



Packaging and equipment

Nokia 3120 Classic - a typical "budget", designed to make calls. The phone index is a direct confirmation of this. Naturally, belonging to a certain segment was clearly reflected in the packaging. For the design of the box were chosen nice colors. All are warm shades of green. On the front panel arcuate lines are used. There is also a photo of the phone. The name of the model is presented under it.

Greater requirements for a set of equipment should not be presented. The reason for this is the same low price. In addition to the device and the battery, the user is offered a small bonus in the form of a monaural headset. She is a wired type. Equipped with a control button with which you can receive calls. Earphones are plastic, foam tabs are absent. Also included is a Nokia 3120 Classic charger. It is quite large. Unfortunately, although a USB port is provided, the buyer will not find the cable in the box.

Design

Particular attention of buyers was attracted by the Nokia 3120 Classic. It is completely spared from sharp corners. All lines are smooth, the lower end is arched. The phone weighs only 85 g, which is an indisputable advantage. Also, you can not ignore the dimensions. They are optimally selected by the developers - 111.3 √ó 45.4 √ó 13.4 mm. The surface of the case is of two types: on the front side - glossy, on the rear - matte. According to users, this solution is quite practical. All Nokia 3120 Classic phones are decorated in three colors. At first glance, there seems to be nothing special in the design, but the device immediately catches the eye.

During use, the owners do not feel discomfort. Due to its light weight, it is convenient to hold it in your hand when talking. There are no problems wearing either. It fits everywhere - even in pockets, even in a bag.

The back cover looks impressive, mainly due to the original design of the space near the lens. It is placed in a transparent plastic plate that rises slightly above the body. The camera window itself is located in the upper part, and in the bottom - the brand name and resolution of the optical matrix. On the left you can see the flash. Above it is a hole for the speaker. Under the cover, the user will find a battery. The Nokia 3120 Classic works with one SIM card, so right below the battery is a slot for installing it. But the slot for the memory card is brought to the end. To replace the drive, you only need to remove the cover.

The front panel of the phone is designed typically for push-button monoblocks. The upper part is occupied by the screen, the lower part is the keyboard. Controls

The lateral sides of the phone can be safely called non-functional. In addition to the volume control key, which is displayed on the right side, there is nothing more. The rocker is comfortable. Due to the fact that it protrudes significantly beyond the line of the body, it is easy to press it, and it is not even necessary to find it with the eyes.

The power button Nokia 3120 Classic is located typically for devices of this brand. By briefly clicking on it, the user will be able to quickly access the profile menu. Unfortunately, a standalone key to activate the camera is not provided.

At the bottom, the manufacturer has located almost all the important elements. This is a connector for connecting audio devices that are equipped with a 2.5 mm plug, microUSB port. The latter is covered by a plug on silicone mounts. On its front side there is a special designation. At the same end, the manufacturer brought out a connector for connecting a device for charging the phone.

Also at the bottom, closer to the back cover, you can see the hole for attaching the strap. The owners claim that threading it is a matter of one second. You will need to remove the cover and just throw the rope on a plastic hook. Then close the back of the case so that a characteristic click sounds.

Nokia 3120 Classic: display specifications

In comparison with its predecessors, the phone received a pretty good display. In its manufacture, the developers used a TFT-type matrix. The diagonal, of course, is small - only 2 inches. The maximum permissible resolution with which a picture is displayed is 320 √ó 240 pixels. The color rendering ability is worthy of respect. Few button phones (apart from modern ones) can display up to 16 million colors. On the Nokia 3120 Classic, any picture will look realistic, rich and bright. The clarity is a little lacking, but given the year of manufacture of the phone, the display clearly refers to the strengths.

High contrast in sunny weather from the TFT-matrix should not be expected. The plastic with which the screen is protected greatly glare, which leads to a low level of readability. The picture looks like black and white.

Font change is available in user settings. In total, up to 11 lines are placed on the screen: 8 of them are text, the rest are official.



Keyboard

The Nokia 3120 Classic is equipped with a mechanical keyboard. It consists of a digital unit and a control panel. Right below the screen are two soft keys that are used to work with the phone menu. Below them, the reset and receive buttons are symmetrically displayed. In the center there is a 5-way joystick. Conditional arrows are made in the form of a chrome frame.

Large keys are used for the digital block. All of them are rectangular in shape with the exception of "*" and "#". They are practically not divided among themselves - the distance is literally 1 mm. In the horizontal row - 4 keys, in the vertical - 3. For those who prefer to work blindly with the keyboard, there are two special droplets on the ‚Äú5‚Äù. The surface of the buttons is glossy, flat. Their move is pretty soft. The response is clearly felt. When pressed, a characteristic click is heard. The digital unit has a white backlight, making it convenient to use the phone in any light.



Performance and memory

The Nokia 3120 Classic is a simple way to make calls. These devices do not require a processor. There were no user comments on performance. The device works the same as other similar devices.

A very small volume of native memory storage - only 30 MB. About 5 MB is already taken by the installed software. To download files, the user only needs 25 MB. However, the possibilities of the Finnish device are not limited to this. The developers have provided for the use of microSD flash drives. Officially, the manufacturer claims that the phone can operate with memory cards, the volume of which does not exceed 4 GB.

Dismantling Nokia 3120 Classic

In most cases, when the owner does not use a case, dust, small particles of sand and other dirt get clogged in the phone case. This can lead to a malfunction over time. In order to prevent damage, which will require a paid repair, it is necessary to carry out cleaning. Dismantling the device does not require special knowledge, so you can save by working on your own. To do this, you need a tool. Necessarily screwdrivers and a thin stiff iron blade, such as a scalpel. If you act carefully, you can even replace it with a regular plastic card. It is very convenient to open special fasteners with such an object.

So, let's start the disassembly:

  1. Remove the back cover.
  2. Remove the battery, external drive and SIM card.
  3. Carefully remove the front panel, given the presence of plastic mounts. The latches must be opened very carefully, as they break easily.
  4. Having removed the front panel, access to the internal "filling" is opened. The board rests on the screws. There are 6 of them. Having unscrewed the four upper ones, you can remove the metal plate under which the connector is located.
  5. Having unfastened the connector, we remove the display.

After these simple manipulations, you need to wipe the dust and collect the device in the reverse order.
