Xiaomi Redmi 4X 16GB: specifications, comparison with analogs and reviews

Many users are familiar with Xiaomi technology. It is famous for its quality and low price. The article will focus on the well-known device Xiaomi Redmi 4X 16Gb. Specifications are described below. The device is sold in two versions: 16 and 32 GB. They are no different from each other, except directly the volume of internal storage.





Specifications

The smartphone is made of plastic and metal. It works on Android 6.0.1, the MIUI shell is installed on top. Supports work with two SIM cards. The processor received eight cores. RAM reaches 2/3 GB. Among the interfaces, you need to notice the headset port at 3.5 mm, as well as the charging connector. Capacitive display. The diagonal is 5 inches. The resolution was 720 by 1,280. The camera has a flash, autofocus. Its resolution is 13 megapixels + 5 megapixels. It is possible to work with some navigation systems. Also installed proximity sensors and lighting. There is a radio and a compass. The battery is non-removable type, its capacity is 4 100 mAh. Type - lithium polymer. The device weighs only 146 g. For more details reviews and specifications of Xiaomi Redmi 4X 16Gb are described below.



Equipment

A phone is sold along with a charger and cable for connecting to a computer. In addition, there is an injector of SIM cards. It is made of metal. Of course, documentation and warranty are provided.



Design

The phone is not much different from other devices in the Xiaomi Redmi line. The design has calm notes, it does not look very attractive. Sold in three different colors: black, gold and pink.

Designers tried as much as possible to create such an appearance that would allow the device to be universal. The frames are thin, the body has contours. Glass type 2.5D. In the case there are metal parts. Such a set is standard, there are no bright accents at all.

Many owners note that the developers have overdone it. They created an uninteresting device that does not have any distinctive features. As a comparison, you can use Meizu and its products. They are very similar to each other, but due to the design of the camera and some details, such as the keys under the display, the phones look more recognizable and interesting.

Arrangement of elements

All elements are located as standard. On the front side there is a display. It also has a speaker and a front camera. There are sensors right there, however, they are camouflaged and difficult to find right away.

Under the display there are touch keys that are responsible for the next set of commands "Back", "Home" and "Task Manager". There is no backlight, which does not please buyers. Indeed, in the dark, it is impossible to make out the buttons. Previously, they used reflective paint in such phones, but now they have refused to use it. Under the Home button there is an indicator of missed events.

There is a special tray on the left side of the device. One is designed for a "micro" type SIM card, and the second is formed under a "micro" or "nano". On the right side you can see the volume rocker, as well as turning on the device. In this, the characteristics of Xiaomi Redmi 4X 16Gb are excellent.

At the top there is an infrared port, a hole that is a microphone and a headphone jack. The lower edge has speaker and microphone grilles. Between them you can see the port for USB.

The back of the device is made of metal. At the bottom and top there are plastic inserts. In the left corner there is a camera and flash. Fingerprint scanner is located a little lower, then comes the manufacturer‚Äôs logo, which is fully connected to the black background.

Due to the fact that the phone does not have sharp edges, it is easy to hold in your hand. Thanks to this, all the buttons on the side faces and the fingerprint scanner are exactly under the fingers. The assembly is of excellent quality: there are no creaks or backlashes. The keys are tight, the scanner is fast, no errors are observed.





Display

The device works with a screen of 5 inches. This feature of Xiaomi Redmi 4X 16Gb is liked by many. The resolution was 1,280 by 720 pixels. Created using OGS technology. In other words, there is no air gap. Given that this model is budget, the presence of backlighting, a deep black color and the complete absence of impurities in a white shade is simply a luxury. The viewing angles are excellent. However, with a strong deviation of the screen, slight distortion is still noticeable. FullHD resolution is not available, however, the cost does not allow us to consider this a drawback.

When exposed to sunlight, the brightness levels will suffice, however, end-to-end. The minimum indicator will allow you to successfully use the device in the dark. These characteristics of Xiaomi Redmi 4X 2 16Gb please buyers.

Allowed to change the temperature of the screen. This is done in the phone settings. However, many owners say that this is unnecessary. The standard version suits almost everyone.

The device simultaneously recognizes up to 10 touches. There is an oleophobic coating.

Camera

The device pleases its owners with a 13 megapixel camera. PDAF mode supported.

The matrices are not particularly brilliant in quality, however, as for an inexpensive device, everything is fine. About such characteristics Xiaomi Redmi 4X 16Gb is written in the reviews. The owners note that if you look at the camera as a whole, then there are no critical flaws. Autofocus is fast, accurate. Sound

The sound is good, the playback is clear. These characteristics in Xiaomi Redmi Note 4X 16Gb / 3Gb are normal, but there are no features. Some owners note that sometimes low frequencies are lacking in music. There is a built-in equalizer and sound optimization mode. The latter can be adjusted for certain models of headphones. The headset does not come with the kit, so you will have to purchase it separately.

Wireless interfaces

The phone has wireless technology modules. It's about bluetooth, Wi-Fi. There is no NFC, however, there is an infrared port. Supported communication standards 3G, 4G and others. Their quality is amazing, there are no distortions and failures.

The phone works with three navigation systems. It's about GPS, GLONASS. The Chinese BeiDou system is also supported. Communication with satellites is established quickly, the signal is not lost.

Performance

The Xiaomi Redmi 4X 16Gb Gold device did not receive any performance characteristics beyond the special ones. They are similar to their previous Redmi 4 modification. It will be difficult to play heavy games with the maximum graphics set, however, the processor will have enough power to perform normal functions.

The basis of the device was a chipset operating on 8 cores. Each frequency is 1.4 GHz. RAM received 2/3 GB. Depends on modification.



Offline work

Battery life is considered a powerful indicator for this device. Of course, these are not some records, but the indicators are good. Battery power - 4 thousand mAh. The work environment is optimized and the screen resolution consumes little.

The manufacturer claims that the device can last about 36 hours during conversations, 15 hours in the video playback mode, 13 hours even playing heavy games, 18 days with the fourth generation mobile network turned on, and 15 hours with navigation activated.

What do the owners say about this? They use the smartphone Xiaomi Redmi 4X 16Gb Black, the characteristics of which please many, at full brightness. When watching high-quality videos (720 r), the phone lasts 12 hours, in a browser a person can "sit" for no more than 9 hours, and play games - 7 hours.

In what conditions does the device show itself perfectly? If you use it in average mode during the day and at maximum in the evening, then the phone will exceed all expectations. At the same time, you should not wake up the phone every 15 minutes, otherwise the results will noticeably worsen. The standby mode is optimized, so the battery charge is slower.

Owners use their phone like this (based on reviews): about 10 minutes of calls, an hour of browsing sites and reading with average brightness, 1.5 hours of listening to tracks with the display turned off, 30 minutes. on social networks, regular chatting and viewing mail, creating ten photos, 20 min. to use cards. At the same time, mail, Twitter and Telegram are working on the background. In this mode, the device shows the balance of 60% of the charged battery 15 hours after disconnecting from the network. At the same time, the screen is turned off on average for about 4 hours.

If you wish, you can draw out better characteristics. It is only necessary to activate the energy saving mode, as well as use the utility from the manufacturer, which detects and turns off applications that work in the background and require a lot of energy.

The only minus in the characteristics of the smartphone Xiaomi Redmi 4X 16Gb is the lack of a quick charge option.
