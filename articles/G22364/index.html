Sonesta Pharaoh Beach Resort 5 * (Egypt, Hurghada): hotel description, reviews of tourists

Many tourists, planning another vacation, prefer Egypt. There are many reasons for this: the magnificent sea, wonderful beaches, excellent climatic conditions and many incredibly beautiful places. A lot of hotels hospitably open their doors for vacationers, one of which is the Sonesta Pharaoh Beach Resort 5 *. In this beautiful place they are able to create the perfect conditions for guests to spend their vacations.

Hotel location

In the resort city of Hurghada, on the Red Sea coast, the magnificent Sonesta Pharaoh Beach Resort 5 * hotel is spread. This is a wonderful place, which against the backdrop of the desert looks like a wonderful oasis with luxurious palm trees and lots of greenery. You can get to it in a matter of minutes, since the hotel is only 15 km from the international airport. You can get there by bus or book a transfer, which will cost a little more.



The resort town center is only 18 km, so many hotel guests can quickly get there by bus and enjoy walks along the streets of this fabulous place. Hurghada in November was simply flooded with tourists, because it was at this time that the heat subsided a bit, and you can walk around enough. However, if you forget about sunscreen, you can still burn in the sun.

Territory

The Sonesta Pharaoh Beach Resort 5 * hotel has a very large area - 21,000 square meters. m. For the convenience of vacationers, electric cars ply here, which can deliver to any corner of the territory or to the beach. Despite its huge size, the territory is always cleaned. Here they carefully look after lawns and decorative trees, which always look neatly trimmed. There are many flower beds everywhere, the territory is simply buried in the beauty of wonderful flowers. The main element that gives a touch of special flavor to gardens is palm trees. All walkways in the hotel are tiled, so you can comfortably stroll through the enchanting gardens.



There are two large pools near the Sonesta Pharaoh Beach Resort Hotel. One of them is indoor; in winter, water is heated in it. There is also a children's pool with various small slides. According to many mothers, their babies frolic with great pleasure in the pool, not wanting to go to the sea.

Near the pools there are umbrellas and sun loungers. They are provided in sufficient quantities, and even at the height of the season, all guests can comfortably settle down without experiencing inconvenience. Towels are also provided by the pool.

The Sonesta Pharaoh Beach Resort 5 * hotel (Hurghada) offers guests the most various services that will make the rest comfortable and unforgettable. Reception here works around the clock. All complaints and complaints from vacationers are considered urgently.

When negotiating or other events, the hotel staff helps with organizational issues.

Shopping enthusiasts can visit the local shopping center, where you can buy various souvenirs or necessary things. The Sonesta Pharaoh Beach Resort 5 * hotel (Hurghada) has an exchange office where you can exchange money for local or other currency.



Vacationers can also use the laundry or dry cleaning services. Washing and cleaning of clothes is carried out here for a fee. An experienced doctor also works at the hotel, who, if necessary, can consult or provide first aid. Medical services at the hotel are provided only for money.

Beach vacation

A special delight among vacationers at the Sonesta Pharaoh Beach Resort 5 * hotel (Egypt, Hurghada) is caused by the beach. This is a very neat and well-groomed place with a length of about 380 meters. The beach belongs to the hotel and is located at a distance of 100 meters from it. Vacationers can walk to it or drive an electric car.

There are always plenty of sun loungers and parasols on the beach. In addition, you can always use special fences that protect from the wind. After bathing, a shower is offered. Towels are issued immediately.

The entrance to the sea is very comfortable because the beach is sandy. There is a shallow depth near the shore, which allows parents not to worry about the safety of their children. Swimmers get to deeper places on two pontoons. You can change clothes on them and leave your things while you are in the water.



Near the beach is a coral reef where you can see many marine life. Fish swim even in shallow water, but this is rare. You can admire the representatives of marine fauna after low tide, when crabs and ophiocomas do not have time to hide in the depths of the waters.

Rest (Egypt, Hurghada): prices

Given the excellent living conditions, it can be noted that the cost of the tour is relatively low. A stay of one person for seven days at the Sonesta Pharaoh Beach Resort 5 * hotel will cost about 550 dollars.

The cost of the tour largely depends on how far away the city of departure and Hurghada are on the map. The greater the distance between these cities, the more expensive the tour will be. The cost also depends on the chosen power system.
