Can crochet practical slippers?

In homes where guests often come, the question periodically arises of where to get the required number of slippers. There is an easy way to solve this problem - crochet slippers from yarn. A clothesline is also suitable.





You may doubt whether knitted slippers will be strong enough and socks? To make the product practical, it is enough to choose the right material for the job. First of all, you need to take a thick synthetic or blended yarn of tight torsion. If you have leftover threads but are not sure of their quality, add a second, cotton or viscose to the main thread, and crochet slippers in two threads.

Today we crochet slippers in two ways: from the toe and from the sole.





The first version of knitting is suitable for beginner needlewomen, it is extremely simple and requires the ability to knit double crochet. We start work with a chain of 5-7 loops closed in a ring. A new row: two lifting loops, then we knit 2 double crochets from each loop. Next row: double crochet. We alternate rows until a slipper of the desired size is obtained, usually 5-6 cm, then we knit in a circle the same amount without adding. Sock toe is ready, we expand the work and knit 2/3 of the circumference with double crochets, expand the work, knit a new row. Thus, the necessary length of the slipper is tied, after which the seam on the heel is connected. The edge of the resulting product can be tied in columns or "crustacean step" to maintain its shape and decorate slippers. When worn, the heel will stretch somewhat and gently sit on the leg.





The second option, crocheted slippers on the sole, is a little more complicated and requires certain skills. First we knit the sole: we collect a chain of loops about 15-18 cm long (2/3 of the foot length) and knit an oval in columns, adding loops for rounding at the ends. The finished sole will be slightly larger than the size of the legs. Next, we make 2 lifting loops and go to the design of the top of the slipper. Knitting goes in a circle with double crochet, evenly tapering in front. To do this, knit 2 columns and close with one loop. The back of the slipper is knitted without decrease, forming a heel.

When the product reaches the desired height, close the hinges and tie the edge with a "walk step". To get slippers with lapels, at a height of 10 cm, start knitting the shaft with a turn from the center, about 5 cm. Bend the edge of the slipper, fix it with a thread or decorative buttons.

If you know how to knit a chain and posts, try knitting slippers from an old rope without using a hook. Tie a loop at the end of the rope and pull the loop through it with your fingers, followed by the next. Soon you will get a chain equal to the length of the foot, preferably a little longer. Knit the sole like a regular cloth, pulling the rope through the loops with your fingers, just do not tighten the loops too much. When the width of the sole is sufficient, you can, without cutting the rope, knit a toe slipper or just a horizontal bar, as on slippers. Such flip flops can be washed in the machine after each reception of guests and stored hanging by a loop.

Now you know how to crochet slippers with or without crocheting, and decorate them depending on your taste and imagination. Have a good work!
