Runaway alarm clock: instructions, reviews

The peak of brain activity and human activity in general occurs in the morning. There are people who get up without any problems. But sometimes each of us is faced with an irresistible desire to sleep longer. In order to be in time, you need to wake up in time. Special devices help with this. One of them, namely the runaway alarm, will be discussed in this article.

Description

The dimensions of the device are small, only 12.5x8.5x9 cm. The weight is 350 g. The alarm clock is powered by several AAA batteries. There are four of them. They are inserted into a special compartment, which is closed with a small bolt. The device is made of high quality plastic, and the wheels are covered with rubber. The alarm clock is available in several colors. On the expanses of the network you can find a device made in white, black, blue, orange or pink. Rubber in any case will be white. The wheels are massive. They ride without problems not only on the laminate, but also on the carpets.





On the upper side of the alarm clock there are several small round buttons that allow you to adjust the time, select a mode, and also set the hours and minutes. Under them is a large key with the inscription "Snooze", with which the alarm goes off. On one side is a small LCD display, and two more buttons above it. An alarm clock looks like a small smiling robot. Below is a removable cover that hides four batteries. The device is equipped with instructions that detail the purpose of each button.

Positive traits

An alarm clock running off in the morning has several advantages over other devices. Firstly, this mechanism can be called "smart." Having encountered an obstacle in his path, he tries to overcome it and is looking for ways to go around him. Finding an alarm clock that escapes and hides becomes more difficult, because the device is constantly changing its trajectory.

Secondly, the mechanism is protected from damage. It is designed in such a way that without problems it can fall from a height of up to one meter. Even if he was left on a nightstand or bedside table for the night, he would still begin to run away and hide, before jumping off a hill. Despite the fact that the manufacturer guarantees high durability of the device, do not drop it from a height of more than 60 cm. This will prevent the wheels from breaking.
