Lemon Tree Amarante Beach Resort 4 * (India, Goa): photos and reviews of tourists

Is it possible among the cold and snow to regain the feeling of a carefree summer? Of course. Many avid travelers know this and therefore are already beginning to look through the offers of tour operators in order to choose for themselves a place where you can relax in the New Year holidays. Quite often, the choice of our compatriots falls on Goa. This Indian state captivates the hearts of tourists with its snow-white beaches, affordable democratic hotels and great opportunities for an excellent holiday away from the city bustle.

Experienced travelers know that in India it is not necessary to look for an expensive five-star hotel. Hotel complexes of a lower level no less welcome tourists and are always ready to provide them with quality service. In reviews of Goa hotels, Lemon Tree Amarante Beach Resort 4 * always looks as advantageous as possible in comparison with other complexes of this state. Here guests from around the world and locals like to stay here, so guests will never be bored at the hotel.

Today we will tell readers about Lemon Tree Amarante Beach Resort 4 * and give an assessment of this hotel complex, focusing on the reviews of our compatriots who have been here.





Hotel Description

Photo Lemon Tree Amarante Beach Resort 4 * usually at first sight amaze tourists with its originality and some extraordinary beauty. And this is understandable, because it was built in the Portuguese style, as evidenced not only by the architecture of the building, but also by the painting, which plays interestingly against the background of colored stained-glass windows.

Guests evaluate the design of the hotel complex for the highest score, because it immediately evokes thoughts of relaxation and something sunny. Many write that the hotel is fully consistent with the spirit of Goa itself (India).

Lemon Tree Amarante Beach Resort 4 * was built eleven years ago. Therefore, it cannot claim the title of new, but tourists believe that the number of rooms looks quite worthy. In addition, five years ago the hotel was updated, repair work affected the entire territory of the complex.

If you want to spend time more fun, then go outside. There are a huge number of cafes, restaurants and entertainment venues where young people gather in the evenings.

In general, the territory of Lemon Tree Amarante Beach Resort 4 * looks very well-groomed and pleasant to the eyes. Many green spaces have been planted here, for which hardworking staff is watching. Therefore, a walk in the shade of flowering shrubs brings great pleasure to all guests, without exception.





Location

Lemon Tree Amarante Beach Resort 4 * (India) is located in the north of Goa. From here it is quite convenient to go on cruises and on water excursions. Their choice in travel agencies is quite wide.

The hotel is located forty kilometers from the airport. Experienced tourists are advised to take an individual transfer. So you will get to the right place much faster.

It is noteworthy that the entire Lemon Tree hotel chain has a convenient location relative to the main infrastructure of the resort. Therefore, the hotel described by us positions itself as a place suitable for family vacations.

Beach area

Nearest beach to Lemon Tree Amarante Beach Resort 4 * - Candolim. It is considered one of the best in the district and is characterized by snow-white sand. The beach area is quite well equipped, but if desired, vacationers may not rent sunbeds. They have the opportunity to soak up the hot sand absolutely free by spreading towels.

The hotel is located on the first coastline. Lemon Tree Amarante Beach Resort 4 * and Candolim are separated by about two hundred and fifty meters. It's nice that they pass through the hotel. Vacationers need to go to a small gate, and they instantly find themselves on the shore. This is suitable for families with children who do not like to go to the sea on a busy road, especially considering the fact that traffic rules in India are a bit weird.

If you want to visit another beach, then go to the Bagu. It is eight kilometers from the hotel and can please with an abundance of entertainment and noisy parties in coastal clubs.





A few words about infrastructure

Lemon Tree Amarante Beach Resort 4 * refers to small hotels with less than a hundred rooms. Therefore, its infrastructure is rather modest, which is noted by all tourists in their reviews.

In one of the sections we mentioned that most of the guests gather near the pool. It has a shallow depth: in the middle of an adult, the water level will be about the chest. The disadvantage of this recreation area is the lack of sunbeds. This is especially noticeable when on a weekend the Indians' families settle in. They begin to occupy the sun loungers literally from six in the morning, and lovers to soak in bed will definitely remain out of work.

The hotel has one restaurant and bar. If desired, guests can spend time at the spa. His services are paid extra, as well as the work of the business center. He, however, is empty most of the day.

The front desk has great functionality. There is a luggage room, currency exchange and tour desk. Reception staff are always very friendly, but understand absolutely nothing in Russian. They also do not speak English perfectly, but judging by the reviews of Lemon Tree Amarante Beach Resort 4 *, this does not complicate the understanding too much. Everything can be explained using simple gestures.

The hotel is equipped with parking, which is highly appreciated by tourists who love independent trips on rented vehicles.

Lemon Tree Amarante Beach Resort 4 *: services

Almost all vacationers note that the services provided at the hotel are consistent with four stars. However, some believe that their level could be improved. However, this is a purely personal opinion of a certain group of guests.

Wi-Fi is excellent on site and in rooms. But do not forget that you can connect to it only one device in the apartment. Moreover, the password is individual for each number.

Guests can take the opportunity to send dirty clothes to the laundry or dry cleaning. Ironing service is also available.

Vacationers give a good assessment of massage. This service is also paid, but hotel guests write that they provide it at the highest level.

The complex offers car rental. Of course, the choice of transport here is not too wide, but, in principle, you can always find something suitable.

The rooms are cleaned once every two days, while the maids themselves determine the quality criteria of their activities. Sometimes, as tourists write, all towels or bathrobes disappear from the apartment. The next day they can be returned in a completely different quantity. But such trifles are usually resolved rather quickly and cannot spoil the rest of the positive-minded guests.





Hotel compliments

The hotel complex offers two types of complements for guests:

  • for birthday;
  • newlyweds.

If when booking your room you will inform about the name day, then the hotel will give you a magnificent cake on this holiday.

Newlyweds also receive a cake from the administration. But do not forget to inform the hotel about your event.

The contingent of the hotel complex

This nuance in the reviews is noted by almost all tourists. When booking a room in this hotel, do not forget that there are always quite a lot of local residents. As you know, Indians are a very, very noisy nation. Therefore, from the very early morning until late at night you will have to listen to laughter, screaming, screeching and singing.

If you are ready for this state of affairs, then feel free to go to Lemon Tree Amarante Beach Resort 4 *. Otherwise, you better look for another hotel complex, where it will be much quieter and calmer.





Nutrition

The hotel offers its guests a variety of dining options. Most often, tourists choose only breakfast, because there are quite a few cafes and restaurants around the hotel. The main feature of the food in the restaurant of the complex is the focus on local flavor. Most of the dishes are full of spices, which is not suitable for every tourist. In addition, many of them are vegetarian, this is also not like vacationers. For breakfast, several types of quite European dishes are served, such as scrambled eggs, rolls, sausages and so on.

It is noteworthy that the restaurant at the Lemon Tree Amarante Beach Resort 4 * hotel is one. However, it is divided into several different zones, which are separate institutions:

  • Citrus Cafe. This is where the breakfast buffet is served. He works no more than eighteen hours a day.
  • Palmeria Grill. Grilled dishes are prepared in this part.
  • Mango Bar. This institution is seasonal.
  • Pool Bar. The bar serves drinks and light snacks.

In general, guests are satisfied with the work of the restaurant and bar. In the reviews, they note that no one will remain hungry at the hotel, although a wide variety of dishes should not be expected.





Brief description of the number of rooms

The hotel has sixty rooms. Those apartments located on the ground floor have their own access to the garden. Room types are few in number, there are only two:

  • Plaza. Three can accommodate here, because the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe apartment is twenty-five square meters.
  • Pavillion. Room size is identical to the previous one, but it is divided into a bedroom and a living room. Only three guests can check in here.

It is nice that the hotel has several rooms for people with disabilities.

Each air-conditioned room has a tea / coffee maker and satellite TV. However, among the nine hundred channels it is impossible to find a single Russian. This upsets many tourists.

Daily maids bring drinking water to the room. One and a half liters of liquid is put on each guest, sometimes it is issued even in excess.

Some vacationers complained in reviews that the technique does not always look new and works well. But overall they were satisfied with the condition of the rooms.





Cost of tours to the hotel

The hotel complex in Goa belongs to the middle price category. But with the approach of the peak of the tourist season in India, the cost of tours begins to increase.

A voucher with a flight from Moscow for two weeks will cost vacationers one hundred and fifty thousand rubles. This price includes breakfast for two people. If ten days of rest are enough for you, then the price of the tour will drop to ninety thousand rubles.

Negative reviews of tourists about Lemon Tree Amarante Beach Resort 4 *

Naturally, vacationers evaluate the hotel differently. Many liked it, while others, on the contrary, found a huge number of minuses in it. We will talk about them in this section:

  • a certain category of tourists believes that the hotel as a whole does not live up to expectations;
  • the kitchen is absolutely not suitable for the nutrition of Europeans and young children, it uses spices in large quantities;
  • tasteless desserts;
  • poor sound insulation;
  • old air conditioners that are very noisy during operation;
  • unsuccessful views from the window;
  • not always clean linens in the rooms.

Of course, to some, these shortcomings may seem insignificant. However, for other tourists, they will be decisive when choosing a hotel.

Positive comments

Despite the abundance of negative comments, most tourists highly value their stay at this hotel. They distinguish the following advantages of the hotel complex:

  • friendly and friendly staff;
  • convenient access to the beach;
  • interesting design;
  • beautiful and well-groomed territory;
  • comfortable bed;
  • stable internet;
  • Hotel cleanliness;
  • an individual approach to each guest;
  • great location.

Judging by the reviews, this hotel complex will suit different categories of vacationers. It will be comfortable for young people, families with children and couples in love. Holidays in this hotel will be remembered for a long time by those who appreciate the opportunity to spend a couple of weeks in the winter under the hot sun in excellent conditions.
