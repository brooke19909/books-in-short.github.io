How it all started

The era of digital television for Russia began in 2010, when television channels in DVB format began to be launched. The country will be fully covered by 2015. But already in many regions TV operates in digital quality. Such changes are dictated by the fact that there is a transition from analog broadcasting to new technologies. Digital television is ubiquitous, which provides better signal transmission and is less costly.





The pros of digital television

The advantages that a new generation of television possesses guarantee the viewer a high-quality picture without distortion and clear sound even with a small signal level. Thanks to the use of the MPEG4 image compression format, it becomes possible to transmit a larger number of television channels and not depend on the antenna, which is located on a high roof. High-quality broadcasting (even on the ground floor) will be provided by a digital set-top box or receiver. It can be connected to absolutely any TV that has a DMV connector. Therefore, this service is now available not only for owners of city apartments, but also in the country. To make the fairy tale come true, various digital terrestrial television receivers or, as they are also called, set-top boxes or receivers are produced. They are connected as additional equipment, in addition to the antenna. They can be built-in to a TV or a separate device.





Technology development

Began broadcasting in DVB-T format. But the evolution of television does not stand still. More advanced news have appeared. The modern DVB-T2 standard was widely distributed in Europe, and then was developed in Russia. So far, both types of digital signal transmission operate in parallel, but gradually the second completely replaces the first. The image is transmitted in compressed MPEG4 format, in a wide resolution of six hundred twenty-five lines and supports Dolby AC-3 sound. Signal transmission is not affected by weather conditions. Connecting a TV receiver is quite simple. A layman can also cope with this task. But first, get acquainted with the devices that offer stores and Internet sites.
