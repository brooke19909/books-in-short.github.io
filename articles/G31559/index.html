Hotel Blue Sea Le Tivoli 4 * (Agadir, Morocco): overview, description, rooms and reviews

The North African state of Morocco has recently been a fairly popular holiday destination for tourists. The best resorts of the country are located along the coast of the Atlantic Ocean. The number of Russian tourists who decided to spend their vacation here is also more frequent, which is not surprising. After all, there are good hotels here where you can relax and pay little money for this. One of such hotel complexes is Blue Sea Le Tivoli 4 *. And here it is worth telling in more detail.





Location

The hotel complex is located in the large city of Agadir. And not far from the center - you can walk to it in 10 minutes. And to go to the airport for long, because he has his own Agadir, called Al Massira.

But the most important thing for tourists is its direct proximity to the sea. The beach is at a distance of 250 meters from Blue Sea Le Tivoli 4 *.

In addition, various attractions are concentrated nearby. For example, the Amazigh Museum of Cultural Heritage is located within a 3-4-minute walk, and a little further - the house of active activity (club), as well as Suk al-Had (the largest shopping area in all of Morocco), saturated with local color.

In general, you can get anywhere from the hotel. Indeed, 200 meters from the building there is a stop of the route 11-22-97-98. And back, respectively, you can also get on the bus, saving on a taxi.

What is in the hotel?

Blue Sea Le Tivoli is a 4-star complex, and this already suggests that on its territory there is everything a tourist needs. Starting from Wi-Fi and ending with parking (however, parking is located next to the hotel).

In the lobby, guests can see a 24-hour front desk, a concierge providing their services, a left-luggage office, an ATM, as well as a tour desk and currency exchange.

Also on the territory of the complex there is a small but modern conference room with a business center working with it, a mini market, several shops, as well as car rental, a beauty salon and a hairdresser. In general, all that may come in handy.

About entertainment

Of course, many people go to Blue Sea Le Tivoli 4 * to enjoy a beach holiday on the sea, which is within easy reach. But in the hotel you can do something else. For example, take a swim in the outdoor pool or sunbathe on the terrace.

The hotel also has its own SPA center with several treatment and massage rooms. There guests can enjoy various services, a full list of which is better to familiarize with upon arrival. But body and face treatments, massages, as well as a hammam and sauna are especially popular.

Also in Blue Sea Le Tivoli 4 * (Morocco, Agadir) there are such standard entertainments as table tennis and a full court, darts, and also a solarium. A playground was organized for children at the hotel, and a separate mini-club was indoors. So no one will be bored - neither adults nor kids.

Guests about leisure

Tourists who visited the Blue Sea Le Tivoli 4 * (Morocco, Agadir) leave good reviews. They say that the private beach that the hotel has is quite spacious and clean (they keep order here), but in high season it can be crowded. But there are free sun loungers and umbrellas.

And the complex itself has a large and green area, well-groomed and clean. The pool may be small, but deep (more than two meters).

And also, as guests assure, an excellent team of animators works here. They do their best to cheer and occupy guests. They arrange shows, performances, invite you to classes in yoga, water aerobics, darts, tennis, badminton, water polo. Sometimes they even organize master classes in Arabic. And this is only in the afternoon. In the evening there is much more interesting - they organize dances, competitions, karaoke. And for children there is a separate program. So no one will be sad here - professional staff simply will not allow this.

2-seater standards

Now, special attention should be paid to the apartments that the Blue Sea Le Tivoli 4 * hotel offers its potential guests.

The most inexpensive option is the standard, designed for two guests. These rooms are 31 square meters. m. Inside there are two single beds, tightly adjacent to each other. The standard is decorated in a bright style, which can not but rejoice. Inside there is air conditioning with the ability to connect heating, a plasma TV, a seating area, and a bathroom with all necessary accessories.

The average price of a weekly stay in this room is 20-25 thousand rubles. (with breakfast) for two people. If you take half board, the cost will increase to 30 thousand rubles. Would you like "all inclusive"? Then it will be necessary to pay about 40-45 thousand rubles.

But you need to remember that the price is not final. There are often discounts here, especially outside the holiday season. If you manage to book a room at such a good time, you will be able to save about 30% of the initial cost.

Recommendations

People who have been to the Blue Sea Le Tivoli 4 * leave different reviews, but many still share recommendations that may be useful to those tourists who only intend to go on vacation.

Firstly, if you want a more quiet and relaxing holiday, then you should go to Agadir from the end of May to the last days of June. This is the time of Ramadan. During this period (from May 27 to June 25, to be more precise), an incredibly calm atmosphere reigns here.

They also give tips on choosing a room. A good option are apartments located in the third building. It is quite quiet there, but nevertheless it is located in the center of the complex. If you want complete peace of mind, you should ask for a room in the building located to the left of the pool, behind the administrative building.





What else do guests say?

In general, this hotel will be a good option for those travelers who are planning a budget vacation, but nevertheless want it to leave an unforgettable experience. The hotel is decent and well maintained, but for such a low price you can forgive the shortcomings, if they are located. However, when something does not suit you, you just need to contact the reception, and the problem will be fixed. Additionally, they will clean the apartments, change the burned out light bulb, bring fresh towels, insert new batteries into the remote control from the air conditioner. And they will do it as quickly as possible.

The rooms in the early days are quite quiet, the only sounds that come in the morning are the cries of gulls and the singing of birds. Sometimes you hear the sound of the ocean.

And yet, while staying here on vacation, it is worth going at least for one excursion. To do this, you need to contact the reception - the hotel is in contact with a good local licensed company that provides its services at reasonable prices. The most popular destination is an excursion to Legzira, a luxurious, beautiful beach, untouched by civilization, with soft, reddish sand and clean, cool water. Only there you need to wear comfortable shoes - the descent is difficult.

In general, the Blue Sea Le Tivoli hotel (Agadir, Morocco) will definitely leave a positive impression if you want to pay a little money for a decent level of comfort.
