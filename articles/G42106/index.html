Satellite receiver - a guide in the world of television

The world of television is expanding and popularizing every day. Opens more channels, launches new television projects, improves the quality of transmission of picture and sound. The general availability of television guarantees him huge prevalence. The number of apartments in which there is no TV, probably, can be counted on the fingers. Every year, the demands of man are growing, the bar rises. 10-12 channels at the moment means an acute shortage of choice, while a few years ago it was considered an incredibly large number. The satellite receiver is a solution to this problem, it is designed to significantly increase the number of TV channels and improve the quality of data transfer. This article is entirely devoted to satellite receivers and their capabilities and features.

Most people mistakenly believe that the more expensive the equipment, the steeper and better it is (it works, but not always). The satellite receiver is a clear confirmation of this. Yes, the most expensive receiver will include a dozen more different functions, but often they are unnecessary in the daily operation of the device. Any such device is selected individually for the user and his desires. But the first thing you need to pay attention to is the channel coding system (DRE, Conax, Viaccess, Irdeto, etc.).

Key points



  1. Absolutely any satellite receiver allows you to watch channels with free and free FTA access (means Free To Air). If the user does not have to pay a monthly fee for using the receiver's additional features and watch paid channels, then the best option in this case is the FTA satellite receiver, the cheapest and most common at the moment.
  2. Significantly more expensive is the second option, giving access to the most popular encoding - Viaccess. Highlights: there is the beloved NTV-Plus and several access card slots. That is, you can connect the system to several satellites at once and watch several paid providers.
  3. There are other receivers that open different encodings. But this option is much less common.
  4. There is a satellite receiver that opens all kinds of encodings. The price, accordingly, will be much higher. In expensive models, as a rule, there are many additional functions, such as a slot for a hard disk, the presence of a modulator, USB inputs, etc.
  5. Satellite receivers hd - a breakthrough in the quality and clarity of the transmitted image. Such devices allow you to watch your favorite channels in FullHD format, which is very pleasing to the eye. For example, Tricolor satellite receivers are a great way to enjoy the fullness and brightness of an image. To do this, you just need to purchase a package called "maximum HD" containing a set of high-quality channels.

One way or another, but the satellite receiver is a great addition to any TV, opening up a huge world of TV channels and programs to users. The choice of it again depends on the requirements of the user.
