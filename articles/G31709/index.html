Hotel "Prestige", Kursk: guest reviews

Hotel "Prestige" (Kursk) is a beautiful multi-storey building located in the central part of the city. All conditions are provided here so that guests feel comfortable and at ease.

Short hotel description

The Prestige Hotel (Kursk) looks quite elegant and modern, which fully reflects its internal content. Noble elegance can be seen in the design of both rooms and other rooms of the hotel. It is worth noting that all apartments are equipped with air conditioning, TVs, minibars and other amenities. Thus, here you can feel no less comfortable than at home.

Huge leisure opportunities are offered to guests by the Prestige Hotel. Kursk is visited mainly for business purposes, and after a hard working day you definitely need to relax. The pool with hydromassage, a sauna, and also a spa will certainly help you with this. In addition, you can enjoy coffee and desserts in the relaxed atmosphere of the lobby bar. In the evening, live piano music is played here.

Hotel "Prestige" (Kursk) invites you to enjoy real culinary masterpieces that are served in the restaurant. The main emphasis here is on Russian cuisine. The price also includes breakfast, which includes a wide variety of hearty and delicious dishes.





Hotel Location

The Prestige Hotel (Kursk) is very popular among tourists and business people. The address of this establishment: 13. Endovischenskaya Street. This is the very center of the city, which is approximately 10 minutes from the famous Red Square. Also nearby is a landmark such as Znamensky Cathedral. Nearby are many cafes, shops and entertainment venues. To get to Kursk Airport, you have to overcome about 10 kilometers.

Luxury apartments

If you are accustomed to luxury and comfort, then you will definitely like the Prestige Hotel in Kursk. Luxury apartments are elegantly decorated in a classic low-key style. Here you are presented with two spacious bright rooms, from whose windows an incredibly beautiful view of the main street of the city opens. The living room has soft sofas and a TV. Great attention is paid to the decor (crystal, paintings, etc.). As for the bedroom, it is equipped with an incredibly comfortable orthopedic king size bed.





Studio apartment

Studio apartments are one of the most popular options offered by the Prestige Hotel (Kursk). The photo shows that the rooms are quite spacious, and through the large windows they get a lot of light. The room is decorated in restrained shades, which allows you to relax and have a good rest. You have the opportunity to choose the option with large or one and a half beds. Such apartments are quite often chosen by business people, because a full-fledged working corner is equipped here. In addition, here is perhaps the most powerful wireless Internet signal.





Standard Apartment

Standard room is the most economical accommodation option that the hotel can offer. It has all the necessary amenities, as in the apartments of the previous category. Price reduction becomes possible due to more modest design and smaller area. Here, a rather restrained and unobtrusive color scheme, which creates an especially warm and cozy atmosphere. It is also worth noting the presence of a modern shower with the possibility of hydromassage.

Negative reviews

Despite the many positive comments, the Prestige Hotel in Kursk is also characterized by some negative aspects. Reviews of tourists note the following:

General impression

The Prestige Hotel is simply ideal for those travelers who are accustomed to elegance and luxury. The rooms and all other rooms are decorated in a restrained classic style, and the warm colors create a special atmosphere of coziness. Particular attention is paid to decorative elements. In general, the external impression of this institution is quite positive.

The main advantage of the hotel can be considered its location. This is the central part of the city, where, in addition to shops, cafes and business centers, there are also many attractions that attract tourists. It is also worth paying attention to the fact that the price includes a fairly substantial tasty breakfast.

This hotel is just perfect for those who want to get to know both the city itself and its history as closely as possible. It is also a great accommodation option for business people. Here you can fully relax in order to gain energy for new discoveries.
