What is the "Riviera" - everyone knows

Many people are at work daily from morning to evening. This is very tiring. And of course, when they take a vacation, they begin to wonder where to go on vacation. Someone goes to foreign countries, someone goes to visit another city. Well, someone spends their entire vacation in the country, enjoying it.

But there are places where you can relax at an affordable price. One of these places is the Riviera recreation center (Kazan). A large number of holidaymakers gather here annually.

Have you long wanted to know what the Riviera is? We consider further in more detail.

What is the Riviera?

In the beautiful city of Kazan there is a unique entertainment complex called the Riviera. It is located near the picturesque Kazanka River. Thousands of people visit the complex annually.



The city of Kazan is famous for its good-natured and hospitable residents. Therefore, it is not surprising that the Riviera has very good service and a lot of inexpensive and attractive services.

The complex consists of a four-star hotel, a large water park, a beach with outdoor pools.

The hotel has two hundred and ten cozy and colorfully decorated rooms from economy to luxury and presidential rooms. All rooms are equipped with a TV, a wardrobe, a bathroom, a desk, a safe, and a minibar. Depending on the category of the room and the number of residents, there are one or two beds and upholstered furniture. For business people there are special conference rooms and meeting rooms.

Waterpark Riviera"

The water park consists of ten attractions. There are safe ones, but there are also extreme ones.

What is the "Riviera", even small children know. Indeed, a lot of interesting and spectacular attractions are open for them here. They can independently, without adults, ride the slides.

For guests staying at the hotel, access to the water park is free. For the rest, the prices are also quite reasonable.

Impossible to forget the Riviera

Near the entrance to the hotel "Riviera" there is a small zoo, which will also delight young children.

A chic restaurant in the hotel, a cinema will not leave anyone indifferent.



If you know what the "Riviera" is, then you understand that it is difficult to forget this place. Everyone who has ever been here is sure to come back to celebrate the holidays or just relax.
