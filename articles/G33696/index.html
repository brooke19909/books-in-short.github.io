Sochi, Hotel "Rainbow": address, choice of room, ease of booking, quality of service, additional services and reviews of visitors and customers

If you are looking for options where to stay in Sochi, the Rainbow Hotel will surely interest you. A modern comfortable complex is located in the very center of the resort. The territory is surrounded by relict trees, which creates a picturesque view and a cozy atmosphere.

Promotional Offers

The hotel "Rainbow" in Sochi has a number of promotional offers.

  • When booking a room for more than 10 days, one-way transfer is provided free of charge.

You can get information about the relevance of promotional offers from the administrator of the Raduzhny Hotel in Sochi. The phone number is on the official website of the institution.

Rules of residence

Guests at Raduzhny Hotel are required to abide by certain rules of accommodation. Here are the main ones:

  • Upon check-in, the guest is required to present an identity card and make an advance payment for the entire period of residence.
  • Check-in is after 14:00, and checkout time is noon.
  • Bed linen is replaced on the third day after check-in, and towels - daily.
  • It is forbidden to transfer room keys to strangers, as well as leave visitors in the room without the presence of a guest.
  • It is forbidden to use any heating devices, except those that are in the room.
  • Non-smoking rooms. The fine is 2500 rubles.
  • It is forbidden to eat food or drinks in the lobby, corridors and other public areas.

Reservation Features

If you want to book a room in the hotel in question, you should know the following:

  • In the summer season, booking involves an advance payment of 30% of the cost of living for the period.
  • If canceled up to 30 days before the expected date of arrival or more, prepayment is refunded in full. Otherwise, a penalty of 100% prepayment is imposed on the client.
  • In case of non-guaranteed reservation (without prepayment) the application is canceled if the guest has not confirmed his arrival the day before the expected date before 14:00.

Negative reviews

If you want to evaluate in advance the living conditions at the Raduzhny Hotel in Sochi, photos and information on the official website will not give you objective information. It can only be found in the reviews. Travelers focus on the following disadvantages of the hotel:

  • breakfasts are held in a neighboring sanatorium, and very little time is allocated for them (only 1 hour);
  • the promised daily towel replacement is not carried out;
  • in the bathroom there is a distinct smell of sewage;
  • the staff is quite indifferent to the guests;
  • high prices for accommodation and related services;
  • in the attic rooms are incredibly crowded, the closet does not open, as the bed interferes;
  • dim lighting in the room (you need more lighting fixtures or more powerful bulbs);
  • small windows in the rooms (because of this, little light and fresh air penetrate into the room);
  • bed linen is not changed without complaint about the reception, although they must do it every three days;
  • not very conscientious cleaning - basically it consists in the removal of garbage;
  • the real state of the rooms differs significantly from the photos on the official website (for the worse);
  • the beach closest to the hotel is paid (500 rubles per person);
  • the rooms have many minor breakdowns that cause discomfort during the rest;
  • in bad weather, the TV shows with strong noise - there are stripes on the screen, and the sound hisses a lot;
  • lack of sound insulation between the rooms - everything that happens through the wall is clearly audible;
  • in the rooms located on the ground floor, you can find ants and other insects;
  • the hotel does not have its own cafe or at least a lobby bar, which is extremely inconvenient;
  • in the wardrobe there is some unpleasant musty smell, which instantly soaks the clothes;
  • the rooms are very small (the two guests are completely unable to turn around);
  • upon eviction, the maids very carefully check the number, which is unpleasant enough for the guest;
  • the bathroom is very damp and already has traces of mold (apparently, this is due to poorly functioning ventilation);
  • the hotel is located on a hill, so getting to it is physically difficult.
