How to tame finances: Sberbank deposits for senior citizens

Today, more and more elderly people abandon the habit of keeping their savings at home, use piggy banks and turn to the services of banks. It is profitable and convenient, since interest is accrued on the deposited amount.



Features of the work of Sberbank

One of the most reliable was and remains Sberbank of Russia. Deposits for pensioners offered by this financial institution are distinguished by high social standards.

Not so long ago, Sberbank announced a new line of investment proposals. Residents of the country who are already 55-60 years old can use these services. It is worth mentioning another gift that Sberbank made - deposits with senior pensioners.

In order to open an account, you need to have two documents with you: a passport and a pension certificate. In addition, it is allowed to draw up a power of attorney for a relative. In this case, he will be able to freely manage your money. Interest is paid monthly, but may also be added to the amount deposited. Experts say that Sberbank deposits for pensioners will bring more income if you open an account for a period of at least 3 years.

As you can see, the largest financial institution in Russia again takes care of the poor.
