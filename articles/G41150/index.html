Bow from ribbons for discharge from the hospital. How to make a do-it-yourself bow bow

When a child is born with you or your loved ones, not only the baby himself, but also his parents begin a new life. The baby is protected from prying eyes, but at the same time it is dressed up in colorful diapers. A beautiful handmade bow made for discharge becomes an indispensable attribute of the first children's wardrobe.

To make this wonderful accessory for a newborn, first of all, do preliminary preparation for work. Gather all the necessary tools and materials, only after that get down to business.

What is needed for this?

You will need about four meters of a satin ribbon of delicate pastel color (pink, blue, beige, etc.). Its width should be two and a half centimeters. Prepare another strip of this material with the same parameters. But it should be darker than the previous one.





To decorate a bow from ribbons for discharge, use colored or white rhinestones. Also, for the decor you will need a light openwork lace, which will go well with the main material.

In order to trim the edges of the parts, you need a candle or lighter. And to fasten the elements together, use a strong glue or a needle with a thread (a thin fishing line can also be useful for this work).

Getting to work

We begin to make a bow for discharge with our own hands, it will consist of several layers. We form the bottom row of light petals. To do this, cut the light tape into pieces of 20 centimeters each.

The next tier must be made of a darker satin. So that the tips of the petals that lie beneath it look out.





To make a beautiful bow for discharge, you need to make sure that its details are arranged symmetrically to each other. To achieve this result, begin work on each layer with pairwise gluing (or stitching) of cuts at right angles.

After that, bend one edge of the strip, which is located below. Glue its tip in the central part. Then do this operation with the remaining edges. You will have two separate parts. Fasten them together so that you get a neat cross.

Correct fixation

Now glue the two finished identical shapes so that none of their loops overlap completely with the others. Using the same principle, make the next tier, which will be darker in color. Depending on which fixing method you choose (stitching or gluing), the bow from the ribbons on the statement will have a certain shape.

Attach the top layer of crafts to the bottom. At the same time, make sure that their centers overlap perfectly. Thus, you can achieve the same length of edges that overlap each other. That is, the loops from the bottom layer will protrude at the same distance from under the top in any part of the craft.

Extra emphasis

To make an original bow for discharge, the master class recommends making one more tier of it from openwork lace or light, woven braid. This delicate and airy decor element will be in the center of the overall composition. To work on it, you will need about 16 centimeters of this material.





Find the middle of the finished cut. Then fix one edge in the middle of the workpiece. Bend the strip in half and fix the second side in the same place. It is necessary to fix in such a way that as a result the lace forms a figure eight. Securely fix the formed part on a previously made base.

Continue to decorate

A bow for statement, created with your own hands, can be additionally decorated in the central part. For example, if it is intended for a boy, use an asterisk made of fabric for this purpose. A delicate flower is perfect for a girl.





To make it, cut from the tape 6 strips of 9 centimeters each. Each of them must be doubled so that its halves form a right angle. Then connect the ends together - and get a beautiful finished petal.

To give parts of the flower extra volume and greater relief, collect each of them at the base. After that, elongated tubercles and depressions will appear on the surface.

Solder the bottom of the part with a candle or lighter. Thus, you will make the petals more durable, they will not delaminate. Sew each of them at the base so that the parts hold their shape better.

Now connect the finished elements to each other. Cut a small circle out of the atlas. It will be the core of the future flower and will help you cover the place of fixation of the petals at their base. When the blossoming bud is ready, attach it in the center of the bow made earlier.

We place accents

To make a bow for discharge (you can make a masterpiece with your own hands) became even more festive and bright, you can use rhinestones. Glue the shiny stones to the edges of the craft and in its center.

If the crystals are small, use a damp toothpick to push them onto the fabric. You need to attach it very carefully so as not to smear the glue, which then will be difficult to remove from the fabric without spoiling its appearance.

Rhinestones can be the same color as the main material of which the craft is made, or white. If you have enough patience, you can lay out a congratulatory inscription on the ribbons, which a little later attach to the product.

Working with ponytails

Before that, either cut them obliquely, or make a notch in the form of triangles with the help of scissors in them.

Make two small bows from the darker satin. For each of them you will need one strip for the loops and a shorter one for the central part. Put the ends of the tape on each arc, press them to the middle of the cut and sew.

In this case, for fixing, it is necessary to use threads, not glue, since they will not stop you from making beautiful folds on the parts. Before you tie a bow for discharge, fix on these auxiliary parts the central strips that will cover the place of their junction with the ponytails.

In the end

Glue them on the ends of the previously prepared tape. Do not place them near the edge, but back off by about a dozen centimeters. This is where your work on this wonderful craft ends.





Now you know how to make a bow for discharge. In ancient times, it was believed that any things or accessories made by hand could protect the baby from the evil eye. Therefore, your creation will become not only an excellent decoration, but also a good amulet for a child.
