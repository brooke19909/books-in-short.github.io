Ares Hotel Kemer 3 * (Turkey / Kemer): traveler reviews and hotel description

Fans of leisure in Turkey can choose a resort for every taste: the number and variety of hotels will allow even the most demanding tourist to relax in comfort. If Kemer is your favorite vacation spot for you, and you do not claim to have a luxurious vacation with excesses, then it is better to stay at Ares Hotel Kemer 3 *. In this article, we will tell you what this hotel is like, what kind of holidaymakers leave reviews about it.





Where is?

The location of the hotel is very convenient - in the very center of Kemer, to be precise - 100 meters from the main street. From neighboring resorts Ares Hotel Kemer 3 * is also nearby: transport can reach Antalya in half an hour (the resort is located only 35 kilometers from the hotel). Accordingly, the nearest airport, which is also located in Antalya, is approximately 50 km, which is very convenient for tourists.

Hotel Description

Ares Hotel Kemer 3 * (Turkey) is located near the coast of the Mediterranean Sea, the main beach is only 500 meters, the water is 1000 meters. The hotel building was erected back in 1994, but the most recent restoration took place quite recently - in 2013. Therefore, vacationers are waiting for fresh rooms and updated hotel infrastructure.

The territory is small, but all you need to relax is: sun loungers, umbrellas, etc. There is a road between the hotel and the beach, along which you can walk on warm evenings and breathe in the fresh sea air.





Beach description

The hotel is located near a 300-meter public beach. Despite the relatively small size of the latter, there is enough space for everyone. Pebble beach on the coast. As practice shows, tourists most often choose beaches with white or yellow sand, but there are those who recognize only pebbles.

The city beach has a sufficient number of sun loungers, mattresses and umbrellas. Unfortunately, vacationers will have to pay extra for amenities. The same goes for towels. But the cost of renting a sunbed is small - only a few euros per day.





Description of rooms

Ares Hotel Kemer 3 (Kemer / Turkey) has a little over 100 rooms. The rooms are mainly of the "standard" class, that is, their area does not exceed 30 square meters. m. In each of them a standard set of furniture: bed, bedside table. There is air conditioning, shower, washbasin, toilet and necessary accessories: paper, towels, etc. Each room has a TV with satellite TV and a telephone. For Russian tourists, a Russian channel will be a pleasant surprise. If you need a hairdryer, you can pick it up at the reception upon request, by the way, absolutely free. The same goes for the safe. The windows of all rooms overlook the hotel with a swimming pool, there is a balcony.





Linen in the rooms is changed several times a week, which is quite acceptable for a three-star hotel. Cleaning takes place 3-4 times, if there is a need for additional service, then for a few euros the maid will visit you.

Hotel infrastructure

Ares Hotel Kemer 3 * (standard-class) has its own pool. Unfortunately, there is no bar directly by the water. However, there is an indoor restaurant on site and a bar inside. You can sit in the restaurant from morning (10.30) until late in the evening, it is open until 22.30. As for the bar, it offers a large number of soft drinks and alcoholic drinks, there are vodka, gin, red and white wines. Near the hotel there is a terrace where you can comfortably spend time relaxing from the pool and the sea.

The hotel area is small, but orange trees delight the eye of tourists with greenery and shadow. Dining at the hotel is standard - an all-inclusive buffet.

Tourists who need Internet access can use it for free in their rooms and for a fee on site.





Entertainment

With the amusement of the Ares Hotel Kemer 3 * hotel: an outdoor pool for adults, sun loungers, sun loungers, mattresses and umbrellas. Usually tourists come here who value calm and measured rest. Those who simply need entertainment and discos until the morning go to neighboring resorts or to other hotels. Due to its convenient location, it is easy to get from this hotel to any place in Kemer. For many tourists there was enough swimming pool and restaurant, to get other impressions, they went outside the hotel. In the center of Kemer there are many shops and cafes, a bazaar is open, in general, there is something to see.

Children's holiday

For tourists who plan to relax here with children, I want to say right away that there are no special entertainments and services for young guests. All that you can count on in Ares Hotel Kemer 3 * is a swimming pool for the smallest. Animators, children's clubs or playgrounds are not here.





Cost of rest

Before talking about the cost of rest, It should be noted that in Kemer there are several hotels Ares - Hotel, Club and Siti.

Briefly about each. Club Ares Kemer is a traditional hotel with several floors and 110 rooms. Unlike Ares Hotel, there are several suites. The service includes round-the-clock service, free wake-up service. There is a playground, tennis court, massage room, sauna and jacuzzi. The beach offers water sports and diving. By the way, it is located near Kemer, just 1 km from the center.

Hotel Ares Siti is slightly smaller than the previous ones, it has a territory of 1680 square meters. m., 73 rooms, two buildings in 3 and 4 floors. Compared to Hotel, the hotel has several advantages: ceramic flooring in rooms, a snack bar on site. The hotel also does not have its own beach, but there are several interesting places to visit nearby - the Yeryuk Museum and the port with yachts.

The cost of rest at Ares hotels is approximately at the same level, since they all fit into the category of three stars. For example, 7 nights at Ares Hotel cost about 12,000-14,000 rubles. A tour in Ares Siti will cost tourists an average of 25000-28000 rubles, in Ares Club - 14000-16000 rubles.

Recommendations of tourists

Before you go on a trip and book a ticket, you need to carefully study the information provided about the hotel, take an interest in the impression of the trip with friends. Reading guest reviews is the best way to select the right hotel or resort. First of all, tourists recommend choosing a hotel that suits you according to your financial situation. It makes no sense to go where all the additional services are paid, and you will not be able to cover these costs. Three-star hotels are suitable for unpretentious vacationers who do not need luxury and scope. Ares Hotel is one of those hotels.





Ares Hotel Kemer 3 *: reviews

For the most part, the impressions about staying at Ares Hotel are not very positive. Every second vacationer gives the hotel a rating of 3 on a five-point scale. Many complain that the shower and toilet are combined and there is no separate shower, that is, when you take a shower, the entire toilet is in the water. Someone got a broken toilet or washbasin. But these are isolated cases. This problem can always be solved by asking the hotel administration for a different number. Someone is embarrassed by old furniture, but it is naive to expect luxury apartments from a three-star hotel.

According to some vacationers, they got a room with a very small area - 6 square meters. m. Unfortunately, this fact takes place. Most often this is due to the fact that tourists simply do not specify information regarding the room, and then write negative reviews.

Despite the recent renovation, the walls of the hotel have remained the same. And according to vacationers, there is no sound insulation between the rooms. Some tourists, especially those who rested there with children, complained about the constant loud music playing in the hotel, which prevented the children from sleeping, the noise and the slamming of doors. In addition, due to the low cost of recreation, it was not always possible to meet there cultural and intelligent tourists who were tempted by the all-inclusive program (we are talking about the excessive use of alcoholic beverages). Unfortunately, such guests are often found in inexpensive three-star hotels and spoil the impression of a vacation to others.

Many are dissatisfied with the food at the hotel. According to most, the portions in the restaurant are small and meager. According to vacationers, the staff simply saves on food, and hotel guests have to have lunch and dinner in the city.

Among the positive aspects of the hotel Ares Hotel Kemer 3 * (Kemer center) are noted for the courtesy and friendliness of the staff, clean rooms and quality cleaning. Also, many highlighted the rapid settlement, proper operation of the air conditioner, which is very important in the heat. The proximity of the sea and urban establishments was to many's liking.

Analyzing the reviews left by guests, the hotel can be described as a place for unpretentious tourists. Everyone knows that you need to pay for quality service and a variety of services, and trips to Ares Hotel are cheap. Therefore, when choosing a hotel you should always understand that a three-star hotel will not provide you with animation programs, wine at a meeting and a free set of services. If you are ready to sleep on old beds, be content with a pool near the hotel and a city beach, then Ares Hotel is what you need.
