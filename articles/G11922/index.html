"Owl" - recreation center for lovers of active leisure

To have a fun and unforgettable corporate event, holiday, vacation or weekend, it is not necessary to go far. To date, there are many holiday homes that are located in suburban areas. These institutions are suitable for a relaxing pastime, and for celebrations, and for sports activities.

"Owl" - a recreation center in the Sverdlovsk region

This establishment is located near Yekaterinburg. It is located in the middle of a coniferous forest. This tourist base is known for its good infrastructure, high quality services, developed leisure system.

The staff of the institution, who worked on its construction, did not specifically change the natural landscapes in order to preserve the beauty and charm of the forest. A large selection of entertainment, the possibility of playing sports in both winter and summer, a playground for games and halls for celebrations make this organization popular. The recreation center "Owl" is located in the village of Kashino, Sverdlovsk Region, thirty kilometers from Yekaterinburg.

Guest Accommodation

The guests stay in the campsite in country houses.

Guests are accommodated in cottages of the following types:

  1. House for twenty people. It has five bedrooms, a kitchen, a bathroom, an attic, a billiards room, and a TV. Near the cottage is a gazebo for relaxing and cooking kebabs. The house also has a living room with a fireplace.
  2. Cottage for fifteen people. It has three bedrooms, a sauna, two bathrooms, a shower, a TV.
  3. House for six people. It has two bedrooms, a living room, a bathroom and a shower, satellite TV.

Holidays

Many guests celebrate solemn events at the recreation center "Owl" in Kashino. The staff is ready to assist the newlyweds in conducting the wedding. The bride and groom will not have to worry about such nuances as a photo shoot, an entertainment program, music and light, a choice of dishes, decoration of the hall and table setting.



Employees of the recreation center will assume all these responsibilities.

They can also assist in organizing corporate parties for those who would like to go out with colleagues to nature. For these cases, guests of the camp site are provided with gazebos, equipment for barbecue.

Those who wish can try themselves as cooks and take cooking courses with real professionals. The recreation center "Owl" is also suitable for those who plan to celebrate a birthday, including a children's one.

Entertainment

At the camp site there are many opportunities for interesting pastime for couples with children and groups of friends. Leisure includes a sauna, which can accommodate twelve people, cycling, skate rental. Active leisure also includes skiing, skiing, a water park, and motorbike rental. A very popular service is the provision of gazebos for events. This allows guests to relax comfortably in the open air even in cold weather. The arbors are always warm, there is all the necessary equipment for the preparation of barbecue.

Guest opinions

The recreation center "Owl" enjoys a fairly good reputation among vacationers. Guests talk about such advantages of the organization as reasonable prices, good work of the water park, polite and attentive staff. Many people appreciate the leisure system, the opportunity to engage in various sports and spend time with children.

Most guests are satisfied with the quality of service and the cleanliness of the rooms, but there are some flaws in the organization‚Äôs work. Also, some were disappointed by the lack of insect protection on the windows, since in the summer it is very inconvenient.
