Location

In a beautiful place with unique nature and landscapes is a resort complex Barut Kemer Resort 5 *. Turkey, Kemer, Ataturk Boulevard, 24 - the address where you can find this institution. It is about half a kilometer from the central part of the city. Antalya International Airport is 55 km away.

Hotel rooms

Barut Kemer 5 * offers travelers to stay in comfortable apartments with modern amenities and original design. The following accommodation options are available to guests:

  • Standard offers king-size beds or twin beds. The third guest can sit on an extra bed. The room is very bright due to the huge window, which occupies almost the entire wall. From the balcony you can enjoy panoramic views of the garden, as well as accommodation with side sea views.
  • Deluxe is an apartment consisting of a living room and a bedroom. Convenient and rational layout of the room allows you to place in it everything you need for a comfortable stay. The calm color scheme has bright accents.
  • Superior is a huge bedroom that can accommodate three guests. The room has a double and one and a half beds. It is worth noting the presence of a working area, as well as a cozy corner for relaxation.
  • Family rooms of the hotel occupy two floors at once. The first level is an adult bedroom. It is equipped with a king size bed, and also adjoins the bathroom with a bathtub. The second floor is the attic. It has a children's bedroom and a bathroom with shower.
  • The royal apartments are decorated in elegant black and white with silver accents. The main bedroom is combined with the living room through decorative zoning. There is also an extra room with a pair of twin beds. The room has two bathrooms.

Catering establishments

  • The fast food restaurant is especially popular with children. It serves hamburgers, pizza, gozleme cakes, pastries and much more.
  • Safran is a unique establishment in which two cultures intersect. Here you can get acquainted with the culinary traditions of Lebanon and the Mediterranean countries.
  • Ocean is a seafood restaurant. Cozy design and delicious food create a warm, homely atmosphere.
  • Kumsal is an open-air snack restaurant located near the sea coast. Here you can not only refresh yourself, but also admire the beautiful views.
  • The lobby bar invites you to enjoy quality drinks and light snacks in a quiet and relaxing atmosphere. It is worth noting the elegant and unobtrusive design of this institution.
