A bit of history

The city, which later became the best Soviet health resort, has an ancient history. It was founded by a Greek merchant back in the 2nd century BC. However, as a resort, he declared himself only at the beginning of the 20th century. And this is the great merit of a relative of Nicholas II - Prince of Oldenburg. Locals still cherish the memory of this man. It becomes clear that one has only to visit the city.

Old and New Gagra

Conventionally, the city can be divided into 2 parts. Their border runs along the river Tsykherva. Old areas attract with coziness and intimacy. This is the historic city center, striking in its picturesqueness and architecture of the beginning of the century.



New Gagra is a modern center, the development of which was carried out in Soviet times. This is where you need to go shopping, as the most famous supermarkets and shopping centers of the city are located in these areas. There are many restaurants, cafes, water parks and other entertainment venues.

An important nuance for tourists planning to visit the city of Gagra: motels and resorts are located mainly in the Old part. But private mini-hotels and small hotels in New Gagra.

Gagra: resorts and other places of temporary stay

Going on vacation to a foreign city, any tourist wonders about temporary residence. Gagra in this regard provides a wide variety of options. Arriving here, any tourist can count on:

  • At the hotel. For example, Lulu is a private institution that belongs to the economy class. Alex Beach Hotel, on the contrary, is suitable for those who like discreet luxury.
  • On boarding houses and sanatoriums. Accommodation in such institutions allows you not only to relax, but also to improve your health. Among the most famous options are Colchis, Cote d'Azur, Ilios.




In addition, you can rent a cottage or apartment. However, it is worth remembering that in Gagra there is no hot water in the private sector in the summer. In hotels and resorts, it usually happens all the time.

It is advisable to buy a ticket to the sanatorium in advance, especially if you decide to go to Gagra in the high season. Otherwise, many resorts (for example, "Nart", "Amra") will be inaccessible. Some of them can be accessed without a pre-purchased ticket.

Prices in Gagra

Many tourists who visited Gagra recognize its merits: nature, cuisine, hospitality, the sea. But almost all of them, as a minus of their stay at the resort, call its prices.

In fact this is not true. In peak season, the cost of living in hotels and resorts increases. However, at other times, prices here are quite affordable.

The most expensive month is August. Accommodation will cost 1,500 rubles per day. Quite a high cost in July - 800 rubles. But in June and September you can relax for 600 rubles. The cheapest option is to go to Gagra at the beginning of the beach season. In May, prices have not yet risen, and a hotel room will cost only 400 rubles.
