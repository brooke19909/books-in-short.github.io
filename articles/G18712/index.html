DIY coffee beans: ideas and workshops

Handmade masters use any natural or waste materials in their work. They did not ignore the smooth coffee beans. Mostly dark brown roasted grains are used, although green coffee is sometimes used to create alternating color elements.

Do-it-yourself crafts from coffee beans are easy to perform. You can buy the most inexpensive coffee, the main thing is that the grains are the same roasting and size, then the crafts will look aesthetically pleasing. Products designed in this way can be very different. These are souvenirs, framing mirrors and photo frames, decorating vases and wall panels. If you have artistic skills, then a beautiful picture can be made from coffee beans.

In the article, we present a master class of crafts made from coffee beans with detailed explanations of the work. Each sample proposed to the reader is accompanied by a photograph in which the master will see the final result of the work.

Christmas beauty

To preserve the world's forest reserves, it is now customary to create Christmas tree crafts. They are made of satin ribbons and tinsel, quilling strips and plastic bottles. Let's look at the option of making crafts from coffee beans with our own hands in the conical shape of a Christmas tree.

As a basis, take a dense cardboard of any color. For strength, its edges can be fastened with a clerical stapler. If foam of the appropriate size is available, then you can use it. For gluing with small grains, thick PVA glue is used.

Do it yourself craft from coffee beans in two layers. The first settles down a flat part of grain for the best coupling. The second layer is glued with the reverse side so that longitudinal recesses of the grain are visible.

When the cone became completely brown, it remains to decorate it. To do this, use any elements: plastic beads, Christmas tinsel, beads and polystyrene balls, small satin bows and snowflakes made of plastic. The lower part of the craft of coffee beans with their own hands tied with a layer of cotton wool or synthetic winterizer. Such a Christmas tree can be put on a shelf, and on a festive table.

Valentine's card for a loved one

On Valentine's Day, it is customary to give your soulmate a small heart-shaped gift. But you can buy it corny in the store and write a poem, or you can surprise your partner by creating such a spectacular heart from coffee beans with your own hands. Such a souvenir will make it clear to your beloved how you tried for him, show your creativity and desire to try for a guy.

Believe that he will not throw such a valentine into the closet and will not forget right there. He will hang him in a prominent place and will boast to all his comrades what kind of creative girlfriend he has.

A strong cardboard is taken as the basis of the heart. It is glued on both sides with burlap on PVA glue. A hemp rope is placed around the perimeter of the craft. A loop is pulled out of the recess of the upper part, so that you can hang a craft from coffee beans on a hook.

The orange zest, star anise, and a couple of cinnamon sticks connected by a hemp natural rope are used as decor. For contrast, you can place a twisted thread or a piece of light tissue on one half of the heart. Coffee grains are glued in one layer with the top down.

The work is completely natural and environmentally friendly. This craft can be hung both in the kitchen and in the living room. It will be interesting to look at the do-it-yourself panel made of coffee beans in this style.

A piece of interior

Such a spectacular mill can be made both for decorating a home interior and for presenting handmade works at any exhibition. They make such a souvenir from coffee beans with their own hands only from natural materials. A glass jar and a cardboard base glued with burlap are taken as the basis.

The jar is wrapped in medium thickness with a white rope. The lower and upper edges are best fixed with a glue gun. The remaining surface of the can is lubricated with thick PVA. The mill roof is made of a cone twisted from a sheet of cardboard.

Coffee beans are glued around the base with the round side up. You can place a few grains along the front door. Also, two bags with grains are attached to the base. Mill blades are made of wooden sticks, connecting the details with a hemp rope.

This element is attached to the roof before gluing coffee beans. When everything is installed, the roof begins to decorate, placing the grains tightly to each other, with the rounded side up. At the top of the cone, one grain is attached in an upright position.

The doors to the mill are made of straw. The straws are ironed so that they become flat, and glued them side by side. Cross jumpers are glued over parallel to each other.

Decoration of frames for paintings and photographs

Do-it-yourself frame of coffee beans with your own hands. You can stick the photo on thick cardboard and stick coffee grains along the perimeter a couple of centimeters wide. Even such a primitive design will already look interesting. But if you want to really create a unique thing, then carefully consider the frame decorated with coffee beans in the photo in the article.

Used elements of varying degrees of roasting, as light green coffee, and darker and completely roasted to brown. Grains are glued according to the pattern. These are vertically arranged elements, and circles, and squares. In some places of the frame, alternating grains or strips of different colors are used.

You can paint individual elements with golden acrylic paint from a spray can, creating brilliant accent points in the work.

Bottle decoration

It is advisable to use glass beer or wine bottles to do the work; containers of an unconventional shape can be selected. In addition to coffee beans, hemp natural thin rope, linen fabric, cotton lace, wooden buttons or beads are used in the decoration. All materials are absolutely environmentally friendly.

You can use the design option given in the article in your article, but having an artistic taste and experience of this type of work, you can easily come up with your own way of decorating. In addition, you can use any natural materials: cereals and cork, ropes of different thicknesses and colors, twigs and small sea pebbles.

If these decor items are simple elements of the interior of a room or kitchen, then you can close the hole with lace, a piece of linen cloth or burlap. Well, if you decide to create a vase for fresh flowers or put dried plants or branches there, then the hole is left open.

Candlestick Decoration

Thick brown candles can also be decorated with brown coffee beans. The shape of the product does not matter. It can be square, round, and cylindrical. It is best to stick the grains not with glue, as it will release toxic fumes when heated.

It will be most convenient to fuse small details into the paraffin of candles. How to do this, we will consider further.

Grain fusion

You need to prepare an additional candle in order to get paraffin. It is rubbed into a separate enameled bowl and melted over low heat. Then a candle is lowered into the hot liquid and its side part is enveloped. Until the paraffin has hardened, they attach the coffee beans, pushing them inward.

If once is not enough, then you can repeat the procedure again. Gluing can be done in stages, dipping a candle in a layer of molten paraffin or smearing a separate part of it.

DIY vase made from coffee beans

The craft is made in two stages. The first step for the foundation is to pick up a cup. It can be with or without a pen. In order for the vase to be voluminous, it is necessary to make a soft litter. To do this, attach sponjiks on the entire base to the PVA glue. Then the soft pillow is tightly tied with a hemp rope, the surface of the winding is again lubricated with thick PVA glue.

Only then grains of coffee are attached on the entire surface. You can have them in two layers, laying on the second any patterns or ornament. Additionally, the surface of the vase is covered with acrylic varnish. It dries quickly, does not have an unpleasant odor, work can be done in the room or in the kitchen. After opening with varnish, the surface becomes bright and shiny.

If the craft has a handle, then you can attach a flower made of satin ribbons to it, or simply tie a lush bow. You can decorate a vase by laying hemp rope in layers or by making a strip of natural fabrics or threads. Burlap or linen will look beautiful.

Coffee Bean Topiary

With your own hands you can make a beautiful and spectacular craft. This is a decorative tree that can serve as an interior item in an office room or in the living room of an apartment. The "trunk" of the tree may be flat, but may have a tortuous shape. The upper part of the crown is most often round in shape, but there are exceptions. For example, on Valentine's Day, you can create such a beautiful heart-shaped craft for your soulmate.

The basis for gluing coffee beans is represented by a foam ball, from which a heart shape was chiselled. For work, you need hemp thread, PVA glue, a curved stick, a flower pot, coffee grains, lace, a satin ribbon of brown and beige colors. For decoration, the star anise star is useful. You can use other materials of natural origin.

Completing of the work

To make coffee bean topiary with your own hands, you first need to take a foam base and wrap it tightly with a rope or thick thread, spreading the surface with PVA glue. Its ends at the beginning and at the end of the winding can be fastened with a glue gun for reliability.

Then a stick is wrapped in a similar way, acting as a trunk. Having spread the threads at the base of the heart, you need to firmly insert the rod into the hole formed. You can squeeze a little glue from the gun onto the edge of the stick. Then work continues on decorating the flower pot. It is wrapped around the outside with a rope over the entire surface. It is attached in a similar way.

In order for the stick to hold firmly in the pot, you need to pour in the gypsum solution inside and, until it hardens, insert the stick by sticking it deeply. When the mixture has frozen, you need to fill the top of the pot with coffee grains. You can add more additional details. Then the pot is decorated with lace from natural threads. A bow made of satin ribbons is tied at the base of the stick.
