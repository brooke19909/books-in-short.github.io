Hotel Sefikbey Hotel 3 *, Kemer, Turkey: photos, reviews of tourists

Kemer is a beautiful Turkish resort. It is surrounded by pine forests, behind which peaks overgrown with forbs rise. The coastal zone in this part of the country is mostly pebble. But there are sandy beaches. They are artificial. They are concrete platforms recessed into the sea. Their surface is covered with clean, bright yellow sand.

Kemer has everything for a fun and memorable vacation. In the center of the village there are discos and night clubs. Closer to the outskirts, which are occupied by family hotels, travel agencies, inexpensive canteens, playgrounds and grocery supermarkets are concentrated. Housing in the city for every taste and budget. There are fashionable hotel complexes and affordable private hotels.

Traveling around Kemer is best by minibus. The fare is inexpensive, about fifty rubles. If you wish, you can call a taxi. At night, a double rate applies. In the evening, the cars are busy. They have to wait about thirty minutes. Tickets for concerts and parties are bought at local ticket offices and at hotel receptions.

Optimal choice

The hotel complex Sefikbey Hotel 3 * is located in the heart of the resort village of Kemer. This is a small private hotel that focuses on accommodating tourists with children. Four-story residential buildings are not equipped with elevators. The rise is carried out by stairs. The yard is small, but cozy and tidy. From the international airport to Sefikbey Hotel 3 * less than sixty kilometers.

Working conditions

The hotel does not accept single men. There are no special rooms equipped for people with disabilities. Pets are not allowed for travelers. There are no large apartments. The staff at the reception desk of Sefikbey Hotel 3 * will check in after 14:00. It is recommended to leave the room on the day of departure until 12:00.

General information

The hotel complex was erected in 2004. Four years ago, renovation of residential and public areas took place. The area occupied by the hotel exceeds 3,500 square meters. At the disposal of travelers are two four-story residential buildings. The number of rooms of the three-star Sefikbey Hotel (photos are presented in the article) is sixty rooms of various levels.

Transport accessibility

Near the hotel runs a large highway. Ataturk Boulevard connects Kemer with the federal highway leading to the international airport. The hotel is on the second line. To the sea about five hundred meters. To get to the beach, you need to cross the highway.

Near Sefikbey Hotel 3 * in Kemer there are a lot of eateries and cafes. Opposite is the Kemer Shopping Center shopping mall. It has a grocery supermarket. There is also the Rota Ataturk store. To his right is a pharmacy kiosk. A stone's throw from the hotel is a kebab and ice cream shop.

A five-minute walk from the hotel farmers market. Its counters are bursting with fresh fruits and vegetables, meat and dairy products. Sefikbey Hotel 3 * (Turkey) is located in an area with developed infrastructure.

Rooms

All rooms in the hotel belong to the Standard category. Their area is 20 square meters. The apartments have private balconies. They are equipped with air conditioning and TVs. The rooms have electronic safes. Their use is not included in the price at Sefikbey Hotel 3 * in Kemer (Turkey). The price of the service of storing things in a safe is 70 rubles per day.

The bathroom has an electric hairdryer. Maids regularly update their hygiene supplies and change towels. A shower cabin is installed. The room has a telephone. It serves to communicate with the reception. The rest of the calls are paid. Reviews of Sefikbey Hotel 3 * say that the rooms do not have refrigerators. Internet connection is very slow.

The apartments have sea or mountain views. It is not possible to indicate the location of the room in advance. A room is provided upon arrival. For the possibility of choosing a suite you need to pay extra. There are no fixed rates. In high season, the price of the service is higher.

Standard rooms have ceramic tiles. Judging by the reviews of Sefikbey Hotel 3 * (in Turkey), they do not clean the rooms very carefully. The bedroom has one double or two single beds. A cot is provided as an extra guest. Bed linen is changed twice a week.

Beach

On the seashore hotel residents walk. You can use the bus if you wish. Transfer to the beach is paid. The bus runs four times a day. The coast is covered with small pebbles. In some places there are sandy islands.

Towels are not provided on the beach. For chaise lounges and umbrellas it is necessary to fork out. The standard rate is 100 rubles. The entrance to the sea is smooth but rocky. According to reviews, there is no animation at Sefikbey Hotel 3 * in Kemer (Turkey).

Gastronomy

Dining at the hotel is based on the all-inclusive principle. The system starts at 10:00 and ends at 22:00. The price includes basic local meals and drinks. For breakfast, hotel guests are invited at 07:30. The meal ends at 09:30.

Dine at the hotel from 12:30 to 14:00. Dinner starts at 19:30 and lasts until 21:30. The restaurant's menu has an abundance of salads from vegetables, deli meats, side dishes, cereals and soups. Dietary food is not provided.

Infrastructure

The reception in the lobby is open 24 hours a day. Hotel staff accepts cash and bank cards of Visa and MasterCard systems. Car parking is located opposite the main entrance to the hotel. She is free. It is not necessary to reserve a place for the car in advance.

There is a high-speed internet connection in the aquazone and lobby. It is paid. The tariff is 120 rubles per day.

Water activities

An outdoor pool is located in the courtyard of the hotel complex. Its bowl has a standard rectangular shape with metal stairs on the sides. The pool area is 63 m¬≤. It operates from 08:30 to 19:00. Around it there are sun loungers with soft mattresses, umbrellas and tables.

There is a children's aquazone. It is very small and is located next to the adult pool. Hotel visitors praised the opportunity to relax by the water and at the same time watch children playing.
