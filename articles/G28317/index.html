Lake White. Ryazan region and its pearl

The nature of the Meshchera region is sometimes amazing. Dense forests, a variety of flora and fauna, clean water. One of the pearls of the Meschera can be called Lake White (Ryazan Oblast). 22 kilometers from the city of Spas-Klepiki (150 km from the capital), this reservoir of karst origin is located. Millennia ago, water paved its way in the mountains, forming secret caves. Over time, they collapsed, and in their place a huge pit of water appeared. The reservoir in the Klepikovsky district of the Ryazan region is unusually deep and clean, like all the lakes formed in this way. There are many of them in any territory. Their names are traditional for all areas. In almost any region you can find the Holy, Light or Lake White. Ryazan region boasts one of the most picturesque of these sources.





Forests on all sides border the water surface. The depth of the lake is 52.5 meters. Although there are places where measurements show 101 meters. It is thanks to this depth that it freezes the very last in this region. Its area is more than 33 hectares, the coastline stretches for 2.2 km. The flora and fauna of the reservoir is diverse and unusual. For example, a midwort - a plant that has been preserved since the ice age - is a witness to a long-gone era. It can be found only in three areas: Ryazan, Vladimir and Moscow - at the bottom of karst lakes. But as for the fauna, Lake Beloe is also rich in it (Ryazan region). Fishing here will be very, very exciting. Of course, in the summer it will be difficult to catch anything at a depth, but fishing near the shore can be very plentiful: pike, tench, ide, roach, perch. True, a large number of vacationers may well violate the peace and quiet necessary for fruitful fishing. And when the ice begins, lovers of winter fishing will feel completely in their element. Foam beer lovers in the season can please themselves with freshly caught crayfish.





Vacationers can be found here at any time of the year. Moreover, there may be several options for spending time.
