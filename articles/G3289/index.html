The proletarians are the strength of the popular movement.

At all times and periods of history, the main driving force behind revolutions was the student body and the proletariat. The first was driven by an inquiring mind, maximalism and a desire for change. The proletariat believed that the main reason for their troubles was the state, which oppresses the common people.

The meaning of the word "proletariat"

It is generally accepted that the proletarians are people who united and overthrew the tsar during the period of revolutionary events in Russia in 1917. It's true. However, the history of this word is older than most people think.





The word "proletariat" appeared during the time of the Great French Bourgeois Revolution. It was introduced by Simond de Sismondi. He noted that the proletarians are a group of people who do not have the necessary amount of means for a decent existence. They live one day and do not think at all about what will happen tomorrow.

Later in Western Europe they began to consider all people who belong to the working class and sell their labor power as proletarians.

Proletarians in Russia

The greatest scale of the movement of the proletarians was noted in Russia in the period 1917-1920. This was the period when Marxist-Leninist theory influenced the public consciousness and activity of people .

Karl Marx in his book The Principles of Communism noted that the proletarians are that social class of the population that lives off of the sale of their own labor and does not have capital that can be used.





Over time, small industrialists, artisans and merchants began to join the proletarians. It is believed that the class of the proletariat is precisely that class that is always opposed to the bourgeois. Karl Marx wrote that for the triumph of justice in the state, the working class must become a hegemon and establish a "dictatorship of the proletariat." The bourgeoisie were to be replaced by the proletarians. Their main goal is to build a communist society, first in Russia, and then throughout the world.

World Ambition

The revolutionary events of 1917-1918 ended successfully for the protesters. The monarchy was sent to the dustbin of history. The new leadership and people faced the task of building communism in the near future. Initially, it was planned to create a communist society in Russia itself, and then throughout the world. Leadership has set a minimum plan: to build communism around the world for ten years. Moreover, it was even planned to cancel the history, which was before 1917, and start the countdown again.
