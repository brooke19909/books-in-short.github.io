How to distinguish the original charger for the iPhone?

Apple products are loved by many. Active young people and other admirers of the works of Steve Jobs are ready to give a lot of money every year for the purchase of updated versions of gadgets. However, in addition to buying the device directly, device owners are forced to purchase the necessary accessories. This market is developed no less, and grows even more actively. More and more companies produce and sell for sale numerous cables, chargers and other necessary trifles, without which the use of the gadget is impossible. Most of the products come from China, with appropriate quality.

The original charger for the iPhone comes with the device. But often the owner of the gadget thinks about buying an additional accessory in order to charge the smartphone at work or in the car. For such a case, there is a car charger for the iPhone. It connects to the cigarette lighter and, depending on the model, can charge one or two devices. The accessory can be supplied with a cord.





In the event that you need to purchase an additional charger for the iPhone, you must carefully approach the choice of accessory. The fact is that non-original cables do not have a special chip that provides a secure connection to a smartphone. Thus, a non-original charger for iPhone 4 can cause malfunctions of the gadget and lead to costly repairs. Given the considerable cost of Apple's smartphones and tablets, it is worth considering the unreasonableness of such savings.





How to distinguish the original charger for the iPhone from a fake? It should be noted right away that not all copies of the original are so bad. Many accessory manufacturers are directly certified by Apple. In this case, you can be sure of the quality of the goods.

However, most of the market is occupied with copies of originals from China. Such accessories are easy to distinguish in appearance. The original charger for the iPhone looks neat and made very high quality. It must necessarily contain information about the place of production.

Fake chargers usually look less neat. Often they have more weight compared to the originals. Also on fake information about the place of production may be unclear or completely absent.

First of all, when choosing an accessory, you should focus on matching things to Apple's classic design. It is this parameter that manufacturers withstand in every detail. The original charger for the iPhone will always be made of quality material, the wire will be flexible and reliable. The presence of any additional components will immediately tell you that the device is made in China. Often, fakes look very eloquent, and finding them will not be difficult, even for lay people.



Buying an aftermarket charger from an Apple-certified manufacturer can be less vigilant. Charging can be done in bright colors or have some other inconsistencies with the original. The main thing is that there is a necessary chip inside.
