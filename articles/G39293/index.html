Nha Trang Beach Hotel (Vietnam / Nha Trang): reviews and photos

Nha Trang (spelled Nha Trang), Vietnam, not in vain bears the title of the country's beach capital. This is one of the most popular holiday destinations for tourists from all over the world. Many kilometers of sandy beaches stretch along the turquoise sea, forming bays and coves. Looking at the photos of this resort, one involuntarily begins to doubt: is this not a photoshop? No, beautiful islands, magnificent coral reefs and picturesque bays are a reality. But what about the resort of Nha Trang with tourist infrastructure? Judging by the reviews of vacationers, it is also at its best. Many restaurants and cafes in Nha Trang will satisfy any hunger. Gourmets have the opportunity to taste both swallow nest soup and exotic seafood. Nha Trang is suitable for any category of tourists. Fans of an imposing beach holiday make up the lion's share of all holidaymakers. But lovers of vibrant nightlife will also not get bored in Nha Trang. Those who cannot live without surfing or diving will find what they are looking for in the azure waters of the bay, which occupies an honorable twenty-ninth line in the ranking of the most beautiful bays in the world. There will be something to see in Nha Trang and lovers of excursions. And those who care about their health can correct it in the mud baths and thermal waters of Thubpa. But where to settle and which hotel to choose? In this article we will look at a hotel called Nha Trang Beach Hotel. We built its description solely on the reviews of vacationers.



How to get to Nha Trang

Unfortunately, direct flights from Moscow to this resort in Central Vietnam depart only once a week. From the metropolitan Domodedovo, they are operated by Vietnam Airlines. For independent tourists, this can be of some difficulty. But the same airline company with enviable frequency sends flights to Nha Trang from Ho Chi Minh City and the capital. So getting to the resort with a change may be the best option. For example, from Ho Chi Minh, an hour will fly here, and the trip will cost, according to reviews, only twenty-eight dollars. In addition, during the high tourist season (winter-spring), a whole squadron of charters departing from various cities of Russia lands at Nha Trang Airport. The resort recently acquired its own hub: until 2001, Camran was a military air base. This airport is located thirty kilometers from the city. You can get to the resort from the airport by taxi (fifteen dollars) or by bus ($ 2). According to reviews, Nha Trang occupies an advantageous position almost in the middle of Vietnam, stretched from north to south. A railway line runs through the city, connecting Hanoi with Ho Chi Minh City. You can also get to Nha Trang from the two main cities of Vietnam by bus. The journey from Ho Chi Minh City will take seven hours. The station is located in the northern part of the city.



When to Go to Nha Trang

Best in the cold season. When dank and damp weather reigns in Russia, Vietnam (Nha Trang) will meet you with the sun and will envelop you in warmth. You can relax here all year round. After all, unlike North Vietnam, there is no winter in Nha Trang. Water temperature does not fall below +24 degrees, and air temperature - +22. According to reviews, in the summer the sea does not bring the desired coolness. The heat reaches its peak - +32 degrees. The best time to visit Central Vietnam, tourists call March-April. The temperature of the air and sea is then the most comfortable. Although most charter flights from Russia begin to arrive in the winter. Since September, the weather begins to deteriorate: Vietnam (Nha Trang) covers the rainy season. It lasts until December. In May and June, despite the dryness of the air and the low probability of precipitation, winds blow, which cause unrest at sea. However, if you are a keen surfer, you need to go to Central Vietnam at the beginning of summer. But you should not trust bikes about the horrors of the rainy season. Precipitation is plentiful, but short-term. You will certainly get your share of the sun and tan. And at a lower price. Tours in Nha Trang require an average traveler of 25 thousand rubles. However, prices largely depend on the season, length of stay and hotel category.

Which number to choose

Guest rooms at Nha Trang Beach Hotel are divided into Superior, Deluxe City View, VIP City View and VIP Sea View categories. As the name implies, a view of the sea opens only from the last, most expensive class of rooms. They are spacious and adjacent to the terrace. Those traveling to Nha Trang on holiday packages are usually settled in deluxes. They are smaller, and the windows often overlook the corridor, ventilation shaft or construction site. And in the rooms of the superior there are no windows at all, or they go to the construction site. Room equipment also depends on the category. Excellent soft and spacious beds in VIPs and deluxe. Vietnam, Nha Trang: prices

When traveling in Asia, you need to know two rules. First: buy where locals shop. And the second rule: feel free to bargain. Prices for the same product may vary significantly. For example, carved wooden figurines in a local supermarket stand, translated into Russian rubles, about a hundred thousand. Stuffed alligators are popular among wealthy tourists (from thirty to three hundred thousand rubles). In principle, Nha Trang is a resort spoiled by visitors, it can hardly be called a budget. Jewelry and branded electronics are sold at higher prices than in Russia. Reviews are advised to go shopping at Cho Dam Market. It all depends on your bargaining ability. You can bring down the price to half the previously announced. Tourists usually bring home summer clothes, tea, coffee, fruits. The popularity of local medicinal drugs is not declining. Python fat and rubbing with cobra venom, coconut oil and the classic Vietnamese "star" are sold without a prescription in any pharmacy. Beach ammunition rental on local coasts is paid. Day use of the sunbed will cost one hundred and twenty rubles. Food (as, indeed, everywhere) is very dependent on the class of the institution. Let's just say: in a cafe, a tight meal together will cost 200-250 thousand dong. But seafood prices are biting: they ask for eight hundred thousand for lobsters, and three hundred and fifty thousand dongs for a kilo of shrimp. Excursions from street operators cost from twelve (sightseeing in Nha Trang) to twenty-five dollars (to the Northern Islands or to Yangbai waterfall).
