Blue Bay of the Krasnodar Territory: photos, description, weather. Rest in the Blue Bay

There are a lot of places in Russia where you can enjoy peace and quiet surrounded by fabulous landscapes, as well as splashing in the clear sea. And more and more tourists come to this corner to take a break from household chores and the endless city bustle.

The place, which will be described in the article, is considered ideal for relaxation both for couples in love and youth companies, and for families with children.

The Blue Bay (Gelendzhik) of the Krasnodar Territory is clear blue sea water, a beautiful pebble beach, amazingly fresh air, wild fairy-tale nature and much more.





Location

Blue Bay is a secluded microdistrict of the resort town of Gelendzhik, which is gifted by nature with wonderful mountain air, magnificent bays and clear sea.

The Blue Bay itself is a small resort village located 10 kilometers from the central part of Gelendzhik. A good road leads here.

Due to its unique geographical location, this village is a real oasis. On the one hand, there is a beautiful resort town full of entertainment, and on the other, a wonderful bay with its wildlife.

In the village of Blue Bay of the Krasnodar Territory there is everything for guests, for their unforgettable vacation. It is good for those tourists who are tired of worldly fuss, city noise. Life in this picturesque place seemed to freeze, and here you can truly feel the peace of mind and peace. Moreover, separation from civilization is not felt at all here - minibuses and buses constantly run along the magnificent highway (Blue Bay - Gelendzhik).

In this resort district there are many hotels and hotels at affordable prices. Numerous shopping malls and cafes are located along the promenade, where you can buy interesting local souvenirs and have a decent meal.

Due to its unique location, in the Blue Bay of the Krasnodar Territory, it is easy to combine relaxing on the beach with a lot of exciting excursions. Positive emotions can be obtained on a trip along the gorge of the Pshada River, to its dolmens and waterfalls. These are very beautiful places.

On the conditions of rest in the Blue Bay

Rest in the village (food, rental housing and other services) is cheaper than in Gelendzhik, due to the high competition of a large number of hotels and remoteness from the resort center.

The inhabitants of Gelendzhik bathe here , since the sea is much cleaner, especially at the height of the season (July - August) than in the Gelendzhik Bay. Ideal for couples and honeymooners to relax in the Blue Bay (Krasnodar Territory). Reviews about it for the most part are very decent, and even there are enthusiastic ones among them. Almost everyone is struck by the crystal clear sea with a turquoise hue and a pebble beach, on which it is comfortable to walk even barefoot. Most likely, water purity is connected with this moment. Vacationers also note that there are enough locker rooms and showers on the beach. The hotels there are almost all the same, but it is better to choose them in the northern part of the village due to the closer location to the equipped beach.

The only drawback noted by tourists is the presence of traffic jams on the highway.





sights
