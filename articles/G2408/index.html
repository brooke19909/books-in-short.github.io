LCD "Millennium": description, prices, reviews

Yekaterinburg is a Ural city nestled among forests, mountains, rivers and lakes. To get to it from the capital of the state, it is necessary to overcome two thousand kilometers, which will take several hours of flight by plane or more than one day of travel by train. But, despite such remoteness from the capital, Yekaterinburg is a large industrial city in which for the past eleven years there has been a constant population growth. In this regard, it is very promising for developers who are not tired of erecting new residential complexes. One of these is the LCD Millennium, a large-scale project of one of the largest developers in Yekaterinburg.

Location of the residential complex

Choosing a plot of land for the development of his project, the developer - Corporation "Your House" LLC - did not lose. LCD "Millennium" in Yekaterinburg is very well located in the Leninsky district in the south-west of the city. This area is considered the most popular and promising because of the developed infrastructure, in terms of environmental conditions and transport interchange. In walking distance there is a park named after the 50th anniversary of the Komsomol, which makes life in the complex particularly attractive because of the ability of its residents to stroll here in the evenings and on weekends, breathe fresh air.

Apartment prices

The price in the LCD Millennium per square meter of living space starts at 62 thousand rubles. Reviews about the complex

Reviews about LCD "Millennium" are from buyers, both positive and negative. Negative are related to the quality of construction. Many buyers of apartments are unhappy with the zoning of residential premises, which is carried out by the developer. Also, many did not like the exterior finish of residential buildings and its quality.

As for the positive reviews, they are associated with the arrangement of the courtyard, equipped with playgrounds, sports grounds and recreation areas. Also, buyers of apartments are pleased with the developed infrastructure, which gives additional comfort to life in the residential complex "Millennium".

External infrastructure

The location of the residential complex in one of the districts of the city guarantees its residents the use of all its rich and developed infrastructure. Near the complex there are kindergartens, comprehensive schools, shops and supermarkets. There is also a university of physical education, a children's city hospital, and for adults - a regional clinical hospital.

Internal infrastructure

The developer of the residential complex has provided a sufficiently developed internal infrastructure so that the residents, returning home after a busy day and on weekends, can walk around the territory, relax, watching the children play at playgrounds and sports grounds. All of them were built or will be built in the courtyard of the residential complex "Millennium".

In addition, it will be convenient for owners of their own vehicles to park their cars in the underground parking. And arriving guests will be able to park in guest ground parking.

Ecological situation

If we talk about the environmental situation of this particular district of the city, the developer made the right choice, choosing it to build his project. There are no industrial enterprises. And within walking distance there is a huge park of the 50th anniversary of the Komsomol, where it is so good to take a walk in the evening, admire the pond, breathe fresh air. The location of the complex in such an ecologically beautiful area increases its perspective and makes it very attractive for buyers.
