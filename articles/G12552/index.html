Hotel Imperial Palace Hotel 4 *, Turkey - overview, description and reviews of tourists

Deciding on a place for a long-awaited trip, many opt for Turkey, which is rich in diverse resorts from secluded deserted corners to the notorious centers of night festivities. Here, every tourist will find something to their liking: get a long-awaited dark-skinned tan or visit a number of attractions, enjoy active entertainment or admire the beauties of local landscapes.

One of the unique and popular among tourists is the city of Kemer, which combines diverse entertainment. No less varied here is the proposed housing for the night. The four-star Imperial Palace Hotel annually attracts a large number of vacationers who do not skimp on positive reviews about it: high-quality service, chic apartments, a spacious swimming area and nutritious dinners - something that guests are incredibly happy with.





general characteristics

The Imperial Palace Hotel 4 * (Kemer, Beldibi) was built in 2004, and during its existence has undergone several renovations, the last of which was held in 2015. The main building is represented by a five-story building, which in its design resembles a snow-white castle. In the courtyard there is a swimming area and a mini water park.

A compact area of ​​3,500 square meters is dotted with exotic shrubs, bright flower beds and tropical palm trees. The cleanliness of the alley and relaxation areas is maintained by daily cleaning.

The distance to the airport of international importance is 40 km, moving from which does not tire new guests. Check-in with pets is not possible. Smoking is not allowed in the rooms. Disabled persons can be accommodated in specially equipped rooms. Being in the first coastline led to proximity to the waters of the Mediterranean.





Accommodation

The room fund of the Imperial Palace Hotel 4 * (Beldibi) is represented by 75 one-room apartments, which are classified as Standard. The total area of ​​the rooms is 23 squares. The interior has classic essays, while combining with modern design. High ceilings and panoramic windows visually expand the available space. Each guest is happy with access to their own terrace or balcony. They are equipped with wicker furniture and allow you to see the delightful sea distances or the peaks of the Toros Mountains.

The floors are laminate. The selected color scheme of light tones makes the room comfortable for living. One of the walls has a patterned wallpaper that brings originality and gloss to the finish. Room lighting is provided by numerous lamps, evenly distributed throughout the room.

Tidying up the daily cleanliness of the maids. For owners of a good morning sleep, the “wake-up call” service is provided. Room service requires additional charges.





Equipment

The furniture is made of high quality wood and consists of a bed, bedside tables, wardrobe, stool and tabletop. Individual air conditioning maintains the required temperature. A TV with a Russian-language channel will brighten up the evenings spent in the apartments and allow you to keep abreast of the latest news. The telephone provides communication with the front desk. Refill the minibar with soft drinks is possible for a fee. When populated, it is filled with drinking water. Wireless internet is free.

The bathroom has amazing sophistication. There is a bathroom with a marble countertop under the sink, shower and hairdryer. There are all necessary personal hygiene products and a mirror. The number of towels complies with international standards.

Nutrition

Meals are served in buffet style. Everyone wants to choose one of the proposed ones and puts them on his own plate. This method is convenient for guests and hotel employees, so it has gained unprecedented popularity. As the guests note in their positive reviews, the interior of the main restaurant is bright, cozy and friendly. White furniture, multi-level ceilings have a good meal and good mood.

The variety of dishes served is great: meat, fish, vegetables, fruits, desserts and much more. High-level chefs create culinary masterpieces that can satisfy any gastronomic needs. There is an indoor hall for 150 people and a summer terrace for 200 people. Diet menu is free.

There is a pool bar, on the beach, in the lobby. According to the all-inclusive concept, the consumption of all drinks, with the exception of imported alcohol, Turkish coffee and fresh freshes, is free of charge.





Aquazone

In order to maintain the body in good shape and improve the health of the body, an open-air swimming pool is open all day for swimming. Its area is 350 square meters. It is filled with fresh water and is not equipped with a heating system. Has a gradual increase in depth. For a comfortable rest and air baths, it is surrounded by sun loungers with umbrellas.

A children's pool is located in the immediate vicinity. Splashing, swimming and jumping into the water amuse the kids, giving them genuine emotions of happiness and enthusiasm.

The presence of a mini-water park, working in accordance with the established schedule, in the morning and afternoon, is to the liking of all age groups of vacationers. Three bright slides of different modifications bring everyone positive emotions and good mood.

Every day, water aerobics training is held here. Easy exercises are to the liking of everyone who bathes, as they help increase immunity and improve body shape.





Beach

The distance from the hotel Imperial Palace Hotel 4 * (Turkey) to the private beach is 50 meters. Thanks to this, everyone who wants to get a delicious dark-skinned tan or spend a romantic evening on the beach can easily get to him at any time of the day. The coastal zone is strewn with pebbles, as well as the entrance to the water. There are beach umbrellas and sunbeds with mattresses that allow you to take sun baths with a high level of comfort.

The waters of the Mediterranean Sea in these environs have unprecedented transparency, and the underwater world can be called truly unique. Thanks to this, diving and snorkeling are widespread. Diving into the deep expanses will acquaint vacationers with exclusive marine animals, plants and fish.

In addition, banana, water scooters and skiing are in demand.





Children's leisure

Imperial Palace Hotel 4 * (Kemer) reviews from tourists who came to rest with their children receive mostly positive. Parents note that pastime of little guests is thought out for trifles. Children aged 3 to 10 years are given the opportunity to spend their free time in the children's mini-club. Here you can go through cooking classes, draw and play board games. Watching cartoons with peers is pleasant for every child.

There is an outdoor playground with slides, swings, ladders and labyrinths.

Highchairs are provided for use in the restaurant. A crib is available upon request. There is a possibility of renting strollers, pots. Each parent can take advantage of the services of a nanny, which will take the kid’s leisure time with exciting activities.





Entertainment

Imperial Palace Hotel 4 * reviews are extremely rare. The majority of responses are positive. Many tourists praise leisure activities. Here everyone can find something to their liking. A variety of leisure activities is available at the fitness center. The simulators located here, steppers and treadmills will help you lose extra pounds and find attractive body shapes. Experienced instructors are always ready to help, who will draw up a schedule of favorable loads on the body.

Table tennis and darts are free. A game of billiards is paid extra. Particularly impressive is the evening animation. Amazing show programs, pool water performances, national dances and much more are included in the program of the event.

A visit to the sauna, steam room and hammam will heal every guest, give a new charge of strength and vitality to the body. Massage from professionals in your field will give you an unprecedented feeling of relaxation and peace.

Additional services

The hotel also receives a lot of warm feedback for the provided additional services. There are not so many of them here as in five-star hotels, but there is everything for a full pastime. Additional services include laundry and dry cleaning, which are paid upon delivery. The hotel has a currency exchange, grocery stores and souvenir stalls. Car parking is free. Doctor services on request.
