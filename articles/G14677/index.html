Merhaba Hotel 3 * (Alanya): description and reviews

The main advantage of the Turkish resort of Alanya is that it is remote from the cold currents of the Mediterranean Sea and protected from the northern winds by picturesque mountains. The weather here in season is just gorgeous. This is what attracts thousands of beach lovers here. To rent a house in Alanya, of course, is not too difficult. For example, inexpensive rooms for tourists at this resort are offered by the Merhaba Hotel 3 * hotel. Reviews about this hotel on the Web are mixed, but you can stay here for a while, according to vacationers.

Where is the hotel located

This complex takes tourists directly to Alanya itself. The city center is only 1 km from the hotel. This complex was built near the Mediterranean Sea. Its guests can reach the beach in just a few minutes. Nearby the Merhaba Hotel 3 * (Alanya) are located, for example, interesting sights such as the Tersane shipyard, Karain Magarasy caves, Kyzylkule tower, etc.





Unfortunately, the Merhaba complex is quite far from the airport. Upon arrival, its guests have a very long time to get to this hotel, at least 2.5 hours, since the distance from the airport to the hotel is as much as 130 km. This many vacationers, of course, are attributed to the minuses of the location of the complex.

Hotel Features

This hotel was built a very long time ago - in 1965. But of course, over the entire existence of the Merhaba Hotel 3 * (Alanya City Center ) , cosmetic repairs and large-scale reconstructions have been repeatedly carried out here.

Accepts guests of Merhaba in two four-story buildings. Also on site there is one bungalow built on 2 floors. The total area occupied by this recreation center is 8 thousand m 2 . That is, the hotel is quite large.

What is not allowed in the hotel

Of course, when staying at this hotel, its guests must abide by certain rules. For example, pets are not allowed in the rooms of this complex. This concerns service dogs as well. Of course, residents are not allowed to smoke in the apartments rented at the hotel.

Additional services

One of the hotel's rooms is specially adapted for people with disabilities. Guests of this hotel can access the upper floors by elevators.





Appearance

The complex looks like Merhaba Hotel 3 * (Alanya), according to vacationers, very attractive. Its buildings are built in a light modern style. The facades of the hotel buildings are twined with greenery, which gives them a spectacular resort "southern" look. Beautiful tropical trees are planted in the courtyard of the hotel. Such a situation, according to vacationers, immediately sets up the tourists who arrived to the "vacation" mood.

Who resides

Most British, Germans and Poles stay at this hotel. Usually there are few Russian tourists here. But sometimes there is an influx of domestic vacationers or local residents.

Merhaba Hotel 3 * (Alanya): room reviews

The rooms for rent at the Merhaba Hotel 3 * deserve, as already mentioned, the mixed reviews of the guests. In various ratings on the sites of tour operators vacationers usually give this complex 2-4 points out of 5 possible.

The advantages of rooms rented at this hotel are primarily their cleanliness. Maids are cleaned here, judging by the reviews, quite often. And the bed linen in this hotel, as vacationers note, is usually quite clean. His maids change about once every three days. In particular, the workers of this hotel try to fulfill their duties well if the guests leave them a small tip on the bed. If the maids suddenly forget to change the linen in the room, tenants should contact the administrators. In this case, the problem is likely to be resolved quickly enough.

The main minus of the rooms of this hotel, guests consider not particularly comfortable beds and mattresses. In addition, air conditioning at this hotel is often set so that the cold air from them goes directly to the bed. But guests, fortunately, can easily solve this problem by adjusting the flow.





Also a serious drawback of the hotel Merhaba Hotel 3 * (Alanya), vacationers consider the increased humidity in the showers. The plumbing in the rooms of this complex, unfortunately, can leak, and quite strongly.

Hotel service

Since the Merhaba Hotel 3 * (in Alanya City Center) is a three - star hotel, guests, of course, can use some amenities and, in fact, on its territory . Of course, this complex also has an outdoor fresh water pool. It works from 8 am to 6 pm. For a fee, residents can call a doctor in the room or wash and iron clothes in the laundry room.

The disadvantage of this hotel can be considered primarily that there is no spa. Many three-star hotels in Turkey provide such a service to their guests.

One of the features of the Merhaba Hotel is that it has a fairly large conference room. At the same time it can accommodate up to 80 people. The following services are also provided on the territory of the complex:

  • Volleyball Court;

  • table tennis.





Some tourists, including domestic ones, come to this hotel with children. For younger guests in the Merhaba , a playground is equipped in the courtyard. There are also high chairs in the hotel restaurant. Unfortunately, there is no mini-club in this complex. There is also no stroller rental point in the hotel. Babysitting services for vacationers are also not provided. However, with all this, the paddling pool in this hotel is still equipped.

Merhaba Hotel 3 * (Alanya City Center): service reviews

Regarding the infrastructure of the complex Merhaba Hotel 3 * on the Web, unfortunately, there are not too good reviews. There is practically nothing for tourists to do on the territory of this hotel. Many Turkish three-star hotels provide their guests with much more amenities than Merhaba. For example, animations on the territory of this complex are never performed at all. As already mentioned, there is no spa here.

Service in the hotel Merhaba Hotel 3 * ( Turkey, Alanya) , judging by the reviews, guests of Turkey are not provided with very high quality. However, in this regard, the complex, of course, has certain advantages. The main advantage of the hotel tourists consider almost complete silence on its territory. In the courtyard, neither the sounds of public transport, nor the loud music of any clubs are heard.

In terms of silence and tranquility, the Merhaba hotel wins much against many other more expensive local hotels. In hotels in Alanya, even pools can often be located almost on the roadway. And tourists bathing in them are forced to breathe exhaust fumes. The pool of the Merhaba Hotel 3 * (Alanya, Alanya Center) is located deep in the courtyard, surrounded by tropical greenery. And although it has small dimensions, swimming in it, according to vacationers, is quite convenient and pleasant.





The guests of Merhaba also include the presence of elevators in the buildings. Such equipment is not available in every three-star Turkish hotel. Unfortunately, there are not enough sun loungers by the pool at this hotel, not for all of its guests. To use a sun lounger, tourists should occupy it here early in the morning.

The opinion of tourists about the hotel staff

The staff of this complex is praised by many guests. According to travelers, the staff at this hotel are outgoing, friendly, and helpful people. Most of the employees of the Merhaba Hotel 3 * (Alanya), however, cannot speak either Russian or English. So, tourists who choose this hotel to stay, most likely, will have to use a program-translator to communicate with its staff.

Dining at the hotel: reviews

If desired, in the complex Merhaba Hotel 3 * you can rent rooms with breakfast, lunch and dinner. The hotel's dining room, like its rooms, has earned different reviews from tourists. Some vacationers believe that for a budget hotel the food here is quite tolerable. Others suggest that the hotel food is tasteless. The portions in this hotel, judging by the reviews, are served quite large. But at the same time, almost no meat dishes are offered to guests. Even the sausage in the hotel restaurant is served to residents only soy.

For breakfast at this hotel, guests can take fried eggs, cheeses, sausages, milk, cottage cheese, cereals. From drinks in the morning, residents are offered coffee or very brewed tea. For lunch and dinner, tourists at this hotel eat soups, chicken, vegetables, watermelons. Also, vacationers can take not very tasty cookies in the dining room at this time.

The hotel's beach is located between two plates. The entrance to the sea is quite convenient. However, many tourists recommend travelers relaxing at the Merhaba Hotel to be careful on the beach. During waves, swimmers here are sometimes referred to as plates, because of which they can get injured.
