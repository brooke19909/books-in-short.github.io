EuroStar Jomtien Beach Hotel & Spa 3 *: hotel reviews

If you are looking for an inexpensive, but comfortable accommodation option in Pattaya (Thailand), on the seashore, then the EuroStar Jomtien Beach Hotel & Spa 3 * hotel may suit you perfectly.



General information, location

The hotel in question is located 100 meters from Jomtien Beach, in Pattaya (Thailand). The city center is easily accessible from here by tuk-tuk or rented motor scooter. Bangkok Airport is 170 kilometers away. So the journey to the hotel will take several hours.

EuroStar Jomtien Beach was built in 2008. It is a seven-story building. He has practically no own territory. The hotel's housing stock is represented by 55 superior and deluxe rooms. Almost all apartments have direct or partial sea views.

There is a swimming pool on the third floor of the hotel with an adjacent sun terrace. The hotel also has a restaurant serving Thai and European dishes, a bar and a spa. Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel.



EuroStar Jomtien Beach Hotel & Spa 3 *: traveler reviews

Since a vacation hotel for tourists becomes literally the second home, it is very important not to make a mistake in choosing it. Indeed, even if you consider yourself to be an unassuming person, then you probably want to live in comfortable conditions, have cafes, bars, shops and, of course, a beach if you are talking about relaxing on the sea. Therefore, travelers on the eve of their holidays, sparing no effort and time, collect information about the hotels of the place where they plan to go. Considerable help in this difficult matter, according to many tourists, is provided by studying the reviews of those who have already stayed in a particular hotel. After all, former guests try to share useful information based on their own experience. This allows you to better understand if a particular hotel is right for you.

Today we offer to find out what comments our compatriots were awarded with EuroStar Jomtien Beach Hotel & Spa 3 * (Pattaya, Thailand). We note right away that most tourists from Russia and other CIS countries were very pleased with their choice. This is indicated by the rating of the hotel, based on the ratings of its guests. It can be called very high, because it is 4.5 points out of the maximum possible five.



Housing stock

The guests were quite satisfied with the rooms offered by him in the hotel in question. According to them, recently made repairs are noticeable here, and the apartments themselves are quite spacious, bright, comfortable. They have everything you need for a comfortable stay. So, the apartment has comfortable furniture (beds, bedside tables, wardrobe, armchairs, desk), a bathroom with a shower, air conditioning, a TV with several Russian-language channels, a telephone, a refrigerator with a minibar, an electronic safe (installed in the closet). Please note that balconies are only available in deluxe rooms. On them you will find a table, a couple of chairs and a clothes dryer. There is also no electric kettle in the superior apartment. But you can always get warm water in the cooler on the first floor, near the reception. Tourists who have commented on the EuroStar Jomtien Beach Hotel & Spa report that the rooms do not have a hairdryer by default. But you can always, if necessary, take it for the entire period of residence at the reception. No additional deposit will be required from you. A big plus, according to many tourists, is the fact that there is access to wireless Internet throughout the hotel (including apartments). Wi-Fi is free, and the connection speed, according to travelers, is very good.



Cleaning service

Our compatriots respond extremely positively to the work of the EuroStar Jomtien Beach maids. So, the rooms were cleaned on a daily basis. The maids towels were updated several times a week. Bed linen is changed every 6-7 days. Also, some guests note that sometimes upon returning to the room they found amusing figures of towels and flower petals on the beds.

As for the rest of the staff of EuroStar Jomtien Beach Hotel & Spa 3 * (Thailand, Pattaya), then vacationers were also satisfied with them. So, according to tourists, the hotel staff is friendly, polite and attentive to guests. You can always contact the receptionist with any question or request.



Population

In accordance with the internal rules of the hotel, registration of newly arrived guests here is carried out after two in the afternoon. However, in fact, as our compatriots note in their reviews, in most cases those who arrived at the hotel earlier were placed in the rooms almost immediately. It depends on whether the previous tenants vacated their apartments, and on whether they had already managed to clean them and prepare them for moving in. Also keep in mind that you will be asked to make a deposit of $ 100 upon arrival. On the day of your departure, a hotel employee will inspect your room, and if everything is in order, then the deposit amount will be fully refunded. If you used the minibar, you can also pay when you hand over the keys. In general, on the day of departure, the room should be vacated before noon. If your departure for the airport is planned later, then for an additional fee you can extend your stay at the hotel. But many tourists preferred to leave the room, leave things for storage in the luggage room and continue to relax until the time of departure from the hotel.



Nutrition

When booking accommodation at EuroStar Jomtien Beach, you can choose the option without meals or with breakfast included in the price. Tourists who settled on the second option, according to them, did not regret their decision at all. Breakfast is served in the buffet restaurant on the ground floor. According to most tourists, they are quite diverse and are designed mainly for Europeans. So, in the morning you can eat omelet or scrambled eggs, ham, sausages, noodles, stewed vegetables, rice, pancakes, toasts with jam or honey, waffles, fresh vegetables and fruits, juice, tea and coffee. If you did not pay for breakfast, you can still visit the restaurant in the morning. A meal will cost you about 200 baht per person. Travelers can also have lunch and dinner in the hotel restaurant for a fee. But most travelers preferred to eat in the many cafes in the neighborhood and in the center of Pattaya.

Sea

A big plus of EuroStar Jomtien Beach Hotel & Spa 3 * is its proximity to the beach. You can reach it in just a couple of minutes. The beach is municipal. You will have to pay about 50 baht for renting a sunbed and umbrella per day. The beach is sandy. According to compatriots, it is regularly cleaned, but the sea still often brings garbage to the shore. The water here is opaque. This is due to the fact that the bottom near the coast is muddy. Our compatriots, according to them, combined rest on the beach near the hotel with trips to other beaches.
