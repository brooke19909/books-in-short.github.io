Karadag Dolphinarium: description and reviews of tourists

Not far from the village of Kurortny in the Crimea is the Karadag Dolphinarium - one of the largest and most interesting on the peninsula. The modern entertainment complex is based on the basis of a research center. How much are tickets to performances today and how to get to this dolphinarium yourself?

A modern dolphinarium with an interesting history



Near the village of Kurortny the biostation was founded in 1901 by the outstanding scientist T. I. Vyazemsky. For 90 years, the scientific association has been studying the nature of these places. The main attraction here is the Kara-Dag volcano. The diversity of natural resources and landscapes makes these places incredibly interesting for research. There was also a dolphinarium at the biostation, where scientists could observe the behavior of fur seals and dolphins. After the collapse of the USSR, the biostation was on the verge of closure. It was then, in 1991, that the Karadag Dolphinarium was officially opened to the public. Today there are two showrooms. The dolphinarium is open all year, and during the active tourist season, performances are held daily.

A dolphin show that everyone will love!



The Karadag Dolphinarium delights its guests with performances of dolphins and fur seals. Marine artists masterfully twist somersaults in the air, jump through rings, play with balls and other objects. Did you know that dolphins can sing and draw? In the program of submissions of the Kagadag dolphinarium, you can also see such numbers. After the end of the performance, everyone can take colorful photos as a keepsake and chat with the artists. Real seawater flows into the dolphinarium pools, thanks to which dolphins and fur seals always feel comfortable and full of strength. During a visit to the dolphinarium, guests can learn many new and interesting facts about the life of marine artists and their behavior in their natural habitat. There is also a marine aquarium in the scientific and entertainment complex, which you can visit before the show or immediately after it. In memory of an interesting excursion, guests can purchase themed souvenirs and even an exclusive drawing drawn by a dolphin. Every year, tourists from all over the Crimea come to the Kurortnoye village to swim with the local dolphinarium artists. The dolphin therapy service is very popular. Swimming with dolphins gives positive emotions, and also has a beneficial effect on the human body.

Opening hours and ticket prices

From June to September, the Karadag Dolphinarium delights guests with two performances every day except Monday. You can watch dolphin shows at 11:00 and 15:00. The duration of one performance is about an hour. On days when there are many people who want to see performances, additional sessions are organized. The cost of tickets is: adult - from 300 rubles, children - from 150 rubles. Karadag dolphinarium prices are very low. In many coastal cities, the cost of tickets to a similar dolphin show is much higher. Dolphin therapy sessions are by appointment. In the active tourist season, swimming with dolphins is possible daily, by prior arrangement. Videography of the presentation is paid, it must be paid for when purchasing a ticket.

Exact address and directions



Dolphinarium is located in a very picturesque place, near the extinct volcano Kara-Dag. The exact address of the scientific and entertainment center: Crimea, the city of Feodosia, Kurortnoye village, Beregovoy lane 11. You can get here from Feodosia or Koktebel by bus. Every day, from the main bus stations of these cities depart routes that follow to the village of Kurortnoye. For tourists who love maximum comfort, local travel agencies offer trips to the dolphinarium with a shuttle service and additional excursions.

Real Dolphinarium Reviews



The most popular dolphin show is among tourists with children. All the kids really like the show. Throughout the performance, a friendly laugh and applause are heard in the hall. Sea animals make no less impression on adolescents and adults. The Karadag Dolphinarium has positive reviews and due to the low cost of tickets for submissions. Tourists who visit during their vacation several performances in various entertainment complexes say that it is in the resort village of the highest quality show at a low cost. The dolphinarium itself is also popular with guests. It is always clean and comfortable, the auditorium pleases with comfortable seats. What is especially pleasant, what is happening in the pool and on the stage is clearly visible even from the last rows. A trip to the dolphinarium is a great way to spend your vacation.
