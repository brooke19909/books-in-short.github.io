Waterpark "Fairy Tale" in Omsk - the best place to relax

A wonderful hotel complex will welcome every guest with Russian hospitality, offer the best rooms with coziness and comfort. A magical place, the highlight of the whole complex is the water kingdom of the fairy tale water park in Omsk.

The benefits of relaxing in the "Tale"

At any time of the year, the water kingdom awaits its visitors. It is warm and cozy in the winter cold. In summer, you can hide from the heat, plunging into the water surface of the pool, giving vigor and pleasure. Winter or summer - it does not matter - visitors will receive an incredible positive and surge of energy. After all, water is life. What is there only! Four water slides, hydromassage area, swimming paths. And for young visitors a magical pool with warm fabulous water. Laughter and joy are heard everywhere, only here the children will feel the delight and enjoyment of dangling in the water.

Entertainment for adults

For adults, there are plenty of water treatments that give you great well-being and health benefits. Finnish sauna, Russian bath, hammam. Miracle, not a "Tale"! The sauna will warm with its gentle steam, birch brooms will expel negativity and ailments. A Russian bath will offer a good steam room, after which it is best to drink a cup of hot tea with raspberries. In cold weather it is good to spend leisure time for the benefit of the whole family. Rest for adults will give pleasant moments. Service is at a high level. Restaurant, banquet hall, cafes and bars. Fans of rolling balls can do this in the billiard room. Intense sports games of volleyball, basketball and football are held on specially equipped venues. Residents of Omsk know the "Tale" well. Every person needs a break from the city bustle and everyday problems. Coming here is easy. Chernuchye is close to Omsk, everyone can afford to spend time no worse than abroad.

Children's World Magic

Waterpark in Omsk "Fairy Tale" - this is one of the favorite places for entertainment kids. There is a playground, an entertainment room. Qualified instructors will look after the children. A variety of games will bring a lot of pleasure to young visitors.
