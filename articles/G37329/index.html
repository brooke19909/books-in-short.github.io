Sanatorium "Livadia". Livadia - sea tour

Livadia is a small picturesque village on the southern Crimean coast. 3 kilometers southwest of Yalta, on the eastern slopes of Mogabi, the snow-white Livadia Palace rises. This palace complex is the main attraction of the resort village. Today sanatoriums, Crimean health resorts work here , the unique Livadia sanatorium is open for visitors.

Livadia - resort





In the 60s of the 19th century, the village of Livadia was acquired by the royal house of the Romanovs. Of course, soon these places became favorite for the rest of the whole family. A magnificent palace in the style of the Italian Renaissance was built as a master's house in a picturesque meadow almost near the sea. Nearby were built estates, houses of aristocracy and noble families. Once upon a time there was a wonderful mountain forest, then a beautiful park was set up on forty hectares of land, and for 150 years now it has been delighting everyone with its beauty. A bit of history


Livadia architectural ensemble is a unique historical and cultural monument, an achievement of architectural and park art. Designed by architect Krasnov. In 1925, after the revolution, the entrance to the territory of the former royal house became free, in 1931 a health dispensary began to work here. It was in the Great Hall that the Yalta Conference of the anti-Hitler Union took place in the last months of the Second World War, here it was decided to create the UN and the "Declaration on the Liberation of Europe." At the time of the conference, the palace became the seat of US President Roosevelt.

Infrastructure

The infrastructure of the sanatorium includes: fashionable restaurants with a wide range of gourmet dishes from various cuisines and Crimean wines, a bar, secure parking, sports grounds, courts, a gym, a sauna, a hall for seminars and other events. There is satellite TV, a club, a rental point, a library, a post office, shops, road ticket offices, an indoor pool (on the territory of the Black Sea sanatorium).

Services

The food in the sanatorium is three meals a day, various diet options are taken into account, during the meal guests are served by waiters. In a separate closed room of the restaurant "Royal cuisine" - improved food, at an additional cost.

Excursions to Yalta, to the famous promenade, to local attractions, a botanical park and a zoo, boat trips are organized.





Treatment

Sanatorium "Livadia" is the only sanatorium on the entire coast where rehabilitation and recovery is carried out after acute myocardial infarction. It has a special unit for recovery and rehabilitation.

Also, in the sanatorium they treat hypertension, ischemic heart disease. The medical profile of the institution is respiratory, nervous and cardiovascular diseases, post-infarction rehabilitation. There is a strong diagnostic base, laboratory diagnostics, X-ray, ultrasound, diagnostics by the Voll method are carried out . Take an ophthalmologist, dentist and ENT doctors.

On the basis of the sanatorium there is a hydropathic center, where oxygen, pearl, herbal baths in sea water, and hydromassage are held. Thanks to the climate, clean and fresh air, sea bathing, massages, herbal medicine, diet food and physiotherapy exercises, effective results in treatment are achieved.
