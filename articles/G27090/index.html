Holidays in Poland on the Baltic Sea: reviews and photos

Holidays in Poland, which is located in Central Europe and washed from the north by the Baltic Sea, have recently been popular with tourists from all over the world. This is due, first of all, to the relatively affordable prices in the country in relation to other European resorts, as well as to the availability of modern resort infrastructure, unique natural landscapes, a long sandy coastline of well-groomed beaches. We will tell you about the most popular holiday destinations in Poland by the sea in an article.

Kolobrzeg

Kolobrzeg is one of the largest resort cities, which is successfully located in the central region of the Baltic coast near the Parsent River. Kolobrzeg has a rich history. In the Middle Ages, a fortress stood at this place, and the famous salt mines, many of which are located in the immediate vicinity of the city, have been widely known since the 7th century. Thus, thanks to the export of salt, Kolobrzeg began to flourish and develop.

The construction of the first sanatorium in Kolobrzeg began at the beginning of the XIX century. It was a comfortable resting place, which was designed for 1000 people. But, unfortunately, during the war years the city was almost completely destroyed, and its restoration became possible only in the second half of the 20th century. It was then that the global construction and reconstruction of Kolobrzeg began, which allowed him to receive the title of a resort of the highest category.

Kolobrzeg attracts tourists from all over the world with its mud baths and balneotherapy, as well as a wide line of beach sand zone and the azure sea. The main focus of this resort place is the treatment of a wide range of diseases.

Kolobrzeg is famous for its beaches, whose length is about 12 kilometers. In addition, the beach area has excellent infrastructure in the form of sun loungers, showers, umbrellas, and rental equipment for sports equipment. There are also cafes, bars and restaurants where you can comfortably relax and rejuvenate.

According to numerous traveler reviews, a summer vacation in Poland (Kolobrzeg) is ideal for families with children, as well as all lovers of the temperate climate.

Krynica Morska

Krynica Morska is a small resort town located on the Baltic Spit. It is known for its vast oak and pine forests, dunes and sandy beach area, whose width reaches 50 meters.

Reviews about holidays in Poland (Krynica Morska) are characterized by a positive focus on local fishing and the extraordinary development of active sports.

The most popular attraction of the city is the lighthouse, which was built in the second half of the XIX century. Among the other attractions of this small town are villas and pensions that were built in the XIX-XX centuries.

Especially popular with tourists is the observation deck, which is located on the Camel Hump dune, which offers a beautiful panorama of the entire Baltic spit. The spa town of Krynica Morska is ideal for a secluded family holiday on the Baltic Sea in Poland.

Miedzyzdroje

This is a small town, which is located on the small island of Wolin, or rather - in its northern part. The spa town is known for its sources of mineral waters, which have a therapeutic effect on the respiratory tract.

Miedzyzdroje is a resort town with a rich history. The first settlement on this territory appeared in the second half of the XII century. And in the first half of the 19th century, the city became a resort, namely, salt baths were installed, guest houses, entertainment venues and restaurants were opened.

According to numerous reviews of tourists about holidays in Poland, it has been established that the city of Miedzyzdroje is famous for the mildest climate on the entire coast of the Baltic Sea.

It is worth noting that, like any port city, Miedzyzdroje is famous for seafood and fish, which you can buy from local fishermen when they return to the shore with a catch. Beer made at local breweries is also particularly popular.

The most famous attraction of the city is Volinsky National Park. It is an unspoiled natural corner. Here you can make a promenade and enjoy the unique natural landscapes, as well as numerous representatives of the local flora and fauna.

Swinoujscie

winoujcie is a port city located in the northwest of the Baltic coast. It is known for its health park, which was founded in the 19th century, as well as for its long and wide beach area. Sanatoriums and boarding houses of the city specialize in the treatment of numerous diseases.

On the territory of the city there is a mass of therapeutic mud and exits of mineral springs. This is a real health resort in Poland. Tourists from all over the world come here to improve their health.

The city's beach has a record length, which reaches 13 kilometers. It is equipped with everything necessary, and restaurants and cafes are nearby in ancient buildings.

Among the attractions of this small town deserves special attention the lighthouse, which was built in the second half of the XIX century at the mouth of the river. The pigs. The height of the structure reaches 68 meters and it can be seen from anywhere in the city.

It is worth noting that in the past Swinoujscie played the role of a fishing port. Therefore, the Maritime Museum occupies an important place in the city. It features fishing tackle, tools and maps, as well as local specimens of flora and fauna.

Historically, the city was surrounded by a chain of forts, some of which have survived to the present. As planned by the city administration, historical expositions are located there, concerts and temporary exhibitions are held.

Sopot

The most famous holiday resort in Poland is the city of Sopot. It is located between the major cities - Gdansk and Gdynia. Sopot has a well-equipped resort infrastructure. There are golf courses and minigolf, water parks, bike paths, the length of which reaches tens of kilometers, and tennis courts.

Sopot beach is equipped with comfortable bathing, which overlook the sea. Within walking distance from the beach area are restaurants, cafes and nightclubs.

The most famous tourist attraction is the well-known "Dancing House". It is located on the main street - Heroes of Monte Cassino. Inside the house there is a restaurant, slot machines and shops.

Also recommended for visiting are the city and archaeological museum, the State Gallery of Art and the Triad Gallery of Modern Art.

Ustka

In the central part of the Baltic coast is the resort town of Ustka. This small town used to be a fishing village, but quickly gained fame thanks to the reviews of tourists about holidays in Poland.

A beautiful sandy beach with a gentle slope stretches along the entire city. It is equipped with a variety of children's attractions, rental equipment for outdoor activities, sunbeds and umbrellas.

Ustka is also famous for balneotherapy and mud baths, which will combine a beach holiday with a healthy body.

The city is equipped with tennis courts, swimming pools, mini-golf courses, bicycle paths and horse riding facilities. Ustka is distinguished by well-groomed shady squares and parks with conveniently located comfortable places for recreation.

Among the attractions, it is recommended to visit the beautifully preserved historical part of the city. You will surely be conquered by well-groomed streets, houses and churches, buried in flower beds and greenery.

Yastarnya

Jastarnia is a small and elite resort town. It is located on the Hel Peninsula and differs from the rest by the presence there, mainly of expensive five-star hotels. This place is ideal for a secluded family holiday with children. The resort town has all the conditions for windsurfing and fishing. Yastarnya is a young resort, but it is developing rapidly. In the future, it is planned to further equip the beach area and urban infrastructure.
