Why can a bear be called a unique animal?

Bear - in every sense a unique animal. Outwardly, he is awkward, but in fact fast and swift. The speed of his run can be compared with the speed of a horse. With the blow of his paw, he can kill a person without any problems. Nowadays, even a grizzly bear (which is far from the biggest bear in history) causes real horror among hunters. They do not risk hunting him without taking an automatic weapon with them. No wonder the Indians at one time equated the killing of a bear with the killing of the leader of an enemy tribe. It is the leader, and not the best warrior. Interestingly, the same cave bear was much larger than a grizzly, but it was successfully hunted by Neanderthals for several thousand years. In the ancient caves in which these people lived, many skulls of these huge bears are found. Neanderthals did not have automatic weapons.
