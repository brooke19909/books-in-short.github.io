Turkey, Belek. "Papillon Belleville" (hotel): reviews, description, photo

A fairly young Turkish resort town of Belek is located in the province of Antalya. Every year this place is becoming increasingly popular, although the infrastructure suitable for vacationers began to develop here only in 1984. Today, this city is the most elite of all the Mediterranean resorts in Turkey.

If you want to get into a five-star hotel, located almost in a nature reserve, and which will be much better than similar hotels of this category in other parts of the country, then definitely choose Belek. Papillon Belleville is one of the hotels of the popular Turkish Papillon chain, which is perfect for a luxurious family vacation.





Location of the Hotel Papillon Belleville

To get to the hotel, you need to go from Antalya Airport to the east. Only about 35 km of path, and you will find the gate to this wonderful green garden called "Papillon Belleville" (Belek). Just 4 km from the hotel there is the small town of Kadriye, which, if desired, you can visit and diversify your vacation in this way.

Where hotel guests can live

It offers vacationers - 519 rooms of various categories. They are located both in a detached four-story building, and in a bungalow complex.





In the main building, guests arriving at the Papillon Belleville hotel (Belek) can choose their standard room or royal suite. Bungalow rooms are standard club rooms. Several rooms located both in the main building and in detached houses are specially equipped for people with disabilities so that they can enjoy all the delights of relaxation.

Cleaning and linen and towels are carried out daily, so tourists are always surrounded by cleanliness and fresh aromas. All kinds of shampoos, shower gels and other personal care products change as necessary.

Thanks to modern TVs and cable television in the rooms, guests can enjoy watching their favorite movies or shows. Tourists can always be in touch and calmly visit their favorite sites if they went to rest in Belek. Papillon Belleville is a hotel in all rooms of which there is access to free high-speed wireless Internet.

Beach Hotel Papillon Belleville

The warm Mediterranean Sea is, of course, an incomparable pearl that Turkey (Belek) gives to all its tourists . Papillon Belleville is one of the hotels in this warm country, which has a wonderful private beach.

This is a wonderful sandy strip 250 meters long with a pleasant and gentle entry into the water. A distinctive feature of the beach owned by the Papillon Belleville hotel (Belek) is that it has been awarded a special award - the international Blue Flag certificate. The choice of a place to get it is carried out by environmental specialists. Its presence at the hotel's beach is a guarantee that this coastal area meets all international standards.





There is a special area for non-smoking guests on the beach. There is also a zone exclusively for the adult population, which includes all vacationers from 12 years old.

For a comfortable stay near the sea there are sun loungers with mattresses and umbrellas, beach towels are issued. If you suddenly want to have a bite to eat, you can do it in the Beach Bar Bar: a variety of drinks, with and without alcohol, and a light snack bar are at your service.

Reviews and impressions of tourists

Hotel "Papillon Belleville" (Belek), whose photos I want to review again and again, is very popular among vacationers and has many positive reviews.

Of course, the negative aspects of relaxation are also found, but they are much smaller. For example, some are unhappy with the uniformity of dishes served in restaurants and the lack of a large selection of fruits at the very end of the tourist season (starting in the second half of October). According to many tourists reviews, the number of rooms of the main building is quite old and definitely needs repair. Therefore, quite often, people who visited this hotel are advised to choose rooms located in the bungalow.





But the people who had a rest in "Papillon Belleville" (Belek), leave comments on the hotel territory more than positive. The huge number of pines and eucalyptus trees is especially delightful; there are even fruit trees, such as tangerines, pomegranates and lemons. The contact zoo especially delights children, of course, but their parents also watch the pets with delight.

About the quality of the beach, which has the hotel "Papillon Belleville" (Belek), tourists' reviews are also positive. Smooth entry into the water is great for children, especially the smallest. Also, our compatriots note that the employees of the complex often clean the beach, so there is never garbage on it.

Most of the tourists who stayed at this hotel write in their comments that children's animation is best organized here. Some note that adult show rooms are a little boring and more suitable for people of retirement age. At the same time, every review about animation for children is a real delight from what they saw.
