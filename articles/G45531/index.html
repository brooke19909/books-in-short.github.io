Several reasons to visit Irkutsk and Krasnoyarsk. How to get from one city to another?

Irkutsk and Krasnoyarsk are two stunning cities located on the territory of the Russian Federation. You can talk about their sights endlessly. But still, it’s worth at least once in your life to come and see these great places.

Several reasons to visit Irkutsk

What do we know about Irkutsk? This is the same city, which is located on the banks of the most legendary Angara River. Naturally, in this Siberian region the most beautiful natural sources, recreation facilities and the embankment. However, there are several more reasons to visit this wonderful place:





  • Go to the "130 quarter". This is the most popular attraction, which is located right in the city center. In this historical place there are several wooden buildings that have been preserved since the beginning of the 18th century.
  • You should take a ride on the railway, passing in the middle of the mountains and the river. The view from the window will be incredibly mesmerizing.
  • The whole family needs to go to nerpinaria. Several times a month, the organizers of the entertainment complex create a show. It should be visited by both adults and children.
  • In Irkutsk there are several temples with a unique design.
  • Those who want to learn more about the history of this region should visit the Museum of the Decembrists.

It’s not necessary at all, having arrived in the Siberian region, to organize any kind of cultural and entertainment program. Just walk along the main streets and squares of this city. Each building here deserves special attention.

Several reasons to visit Krasnoyarsk

Krasnoyarsk is the sunniest and most beautiful region in Russia. Tourists from all countries come here to try juicy fruits, bask in the sun and enjoy the picturesque nature. In addition, there are several more places that a traveler should look into:





  • The cinema house, which was built in 1910, is still preserved here. People can see how this art genre looked like more than a thousand years ago.
  • The Vinogradovsky bridge, having a unique design, is built across the Yenisei River. It is an architectural landmark in this city.
  • Incredibly beautiful view, especially at night, has the Theater Square.
  • It is worth approaching the local temples, cathedrals and churches that have an old-fashioned design.
  • It is mandatory to go to a cafe in order to enjoy mixed Russian cuisine.

To a greater extent, this city will appeal to tourists with children, a huge number of entertainment facilities are open for them, allowing to brighten up leisure.





Two unique cities - Irkutsk and Krasnoyarsk. What unites them among themselves? These are historical sights, a location on the river bank, and the most beautiful natural springs. Moreover, each of them has its own characteristics. It is recommended to study all the interesting places of each of them. Fortunately, there are several types of vehicles that allow you to move from one area to another.

Car

Each experienced driver is likely to want to cover the distance from Irkutsk to Krasnoyarsk by car. And he may have such an opportunity. The total distance is 1062 kilometers. That is, you should be prepared for the fact that you will have to spend about 13 hours on moving in one direction.





Unfortunately, there are no large cities between these two points, that is, planning a place for an overnight stay or a vacation will be quite difficult. Gas stations and other necessary facilities for a motorist are extremely rare. The route running between these two settlements is hardly satisfactory. Often there are pits, bumps and other irregularities. Thus, this is a very convenient, but not the safest and easiest option, it is recommended to give preference to public transport.

Bus

To overcome significantly the distance from Irkutsk to Krasnoyarsk is extremely difficult. There is no direct flight in this direction. You can try to find several alternative routes with transfers, but such a tiring way is unlikely to suit anyone.

A train





The most comfortable option to move from Irkutsk to Krasnoyarsk is a train. The train leaves the passenger station twice a day daily. The total travel time on such a vehicle will be about 20 hours. It is preferable to set off on an evening flight, as during the period of sleep time will fly by quickly. The approximate cost of one ticket will be from 2000 to 4500 rubles. It is recommended to purchase them at the box office.

Airplane

The fastest way to get from Irkutsk to Krasnoyarsk is by plane. The total travel time will be 1 hour 10 minutes. A direct flight is operated by IrAero once every two days. Departure is approximately carried out at 7:40, however this time can sometimes change. The approximate cost of one ticket is 6,500 rubles.

When planning to cover the distance from Irkutsk to Krasnoyarsk on any vehicle, it is worth remembering the time difference, which is exactly one hour.
