Briefly about rest in Paphos

The city of Paphos is the ancient capital of the Greek island of Cyprus (now this status is assigned to the only major city of the island, Nicossia). This island, since the XVIII century, is one of the classic European resorts. The status of the resort is associated with ancient legend and history. (Cyprus is famous for the legend of the birth of the beauty goddess Aphrodite, the apostle Paul preached here and the famous philosopher Cicero lived.) And, perhaps, that is why the name of the adjective ‚Äúpathos‚Äù appeared in many languages. If we add to the above the enchanting beauty of some of the most picturesque beaches in the world and the European level of resort business, we will get a brief description of the charms of Paphos.





Here, on the enchanting beauty of the Mediterranean coast, are built not only marble villas of millionaires, reminiscent of the estates of the Roman patricians, but also a chain of hotels. Among the latter, there are also quite budget ones, providing a decent and comfortable stay.

Hotel location

In addition, the hotel complex is a convenient platform for various excursions, beach holidays. Paphos is a city interesting for tourists not only as a resort.
