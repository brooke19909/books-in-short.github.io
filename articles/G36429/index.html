The activities of Modulbank: reviews

For small businesses, the difficulty is to organize financial work, as large banks refuse to work with beginners because of the large waste of time and small revenue. Now more and more often entrepreneurs turn to specialized financial institutions that deal specifically with small business issues. The Modulbank organization, reviews of which can be heard in the business environment at various forums and congresses, is an excellent opportunity for those who are just starting development. What services are provided by the bank, and where can I get them?

What is Modulbank?

In order to become a sought-after financial institution and have only positive feedback, Modulbank put a lot of effort into studying the needs of small business entrepreneurs.





A loan at a low rate, most of the operations via the Internet, a minimum package of documents for concluding contracts, opening a current account as quickly as possible, conducting currency control, transferring unclaimed amounts to a deposit - this is exactly what modern businessmen need. And all this can be obtained from this financial company.

Services provided by Modulbank and reviews about them

One of the most important opportunities provided by a financial institution is bookkeeping. Moreover, for those entrepreneurs who are on a simplified tax system, it will be free. Those businessmen who have already taken advantage of this do not hide their enthusiasm, because how much time is freed up for the main activity.

In order to make contact with specialists, it is enough to have an Internet connection. If you need any act or sales receipt, the accountant will notify the client, and that, in turn, can send a copy or photograph of the necessary document. For entrepreneurs on a different taxation system and legal entities, it is necessary to conclude a paid contract.





In addition, specialists can conduct personnel affairs, suggesting how much vacation pay or sick leave should be paid to employees. For beginners, this is especially difficult. Modulbank customers appreciated the service and left the most positive feedback.

Another important service is currency control. Banking employees independently monitor international transactions, prepare documents, the signing of which will take no more than an hour. Thus, the necessary amount will reach the entrepreneur on the same day the transaction has passed, and businessmen consider this a huge advantage over large banks.

Possibility to open a current account

A checking account is the main requisite of each company, even if it is still very small. The institution under consideration allows us to open such a props in five minutes, sitting at the computer even at home. To do this, you need to register on the bank's website and fill out a data sheet to obtain the necessary documentation. With an agreement, an entrepreneur can leave the manager at the most convenient time for the client.

The advantages of such a transaction include:

  1. Financial payments are carried out almost around the clock, which means that partners even in different time zones will receive transfers.
  2. Modulbank charges interest on cash balances on the account.
  3. The ability to transfer unclaimed amounts to a deposit account at 7% per annum.
