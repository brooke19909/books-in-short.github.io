Pension Azovsky

Crimea 2011 pension Azovsky opened its eighth season. In 2003, a group of Moscow investors acquired a pioneer camp and transformed it into a great place for families. New buildings with comfort class rooms were built, and all other buildings were overhauled. The rooms have all the necessary amenities, hot and cold water.

Vouchers are sold first-hand, they are without intermediaries, which allows vacationers to save money. Pension Azovsky occupies a very impressive area - six and a half hectares and is under constant protection, which gives children the opportunity to frolic in plenty in nature. The infrastructure is thought out in a boarding house to the smallest detail. There are cafes and bars on site, where discos are organized in the evening. A new water park works for children, playgrounds and a games room are equipped, a trampoline is installed.

Organizing teachers deal with children, conduct various contests and competitions with them, and organize exhibitions. For adults, new tennis courts have been built that meet all international standards, there is a football field, as well as basketball and volleyball courts. You can use all this for free. Those who wish can take fascinating tours of the Crimea. You can visit the water parks in Koktebel and Sudak, or make a trip to the Karadag dolphinarium and admire the trained dolphins who can make funny numbers and tricks.

For children, two small slides are installed, from which they ride with pleasure. The water in the pool is heated and there is no need to worry that the children will become cold and sick. But the most important thing is that this beautiful pool can be used for free.

Pension Azovsky continues to create the most comfortable conditions for vacationers, which are located in one- and two-story cottages. The rooms are small but have everything you need. The suites are equipped with air conditioning, refrigerator, TV, safe, furniture. It should be noted that the mattresses on the beds are orthopedic. There is also a bathroom with a washbasin and shower, where uninterrupted supply of hot and cold water is provided.

At the same time, the boarding house can accommodate 420 people. Comfort rooms for two to three people are located in the new buildings. They have ceiling fans and plasma TVs. Standard and Standard Plus rooms are also available. In standard rooms, water is heated from sunlight in special tanks. If you wish, you can rent a refrigerator and use the storage box, which is located in the administration. For young children there is a dairy kitchen, you can replace the usual bed with a nursery, order a pot and small high chairs.

The price includes three meals a day with elements of the buffet. For kids who have not yet switched to the common table, you can cook in household rooms with electric stoves and microwave ovens. In the pension there is a first-aid post.

Pension Azovsky provides vacationers with all the conditions for a comfortable stay, not only with the whole family, but also for those who want to relax in peace and quiet. A summer vacation in the Crimea, the Azov boarding house will make it truly full and interesting, add strength and health for a whole year, and give children an unforgettable experience for life.
