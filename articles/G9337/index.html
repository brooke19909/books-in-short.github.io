Beautiful maple leaf flower

Every autumn, numerous bright ‚Äúfeathers‚Äù of trees fall to the ground, creating fertile soil for new shoots and giving birth to a slight sadness in the hearts of people by the gone warm days. But foreign craftswomen came up with how to make a flower from maple leaves , which can be from orange-yellow to bright red. Making such a bouquet from natural material does not take much time, and it can be stored until next fall and even longer.



Preparatory stage

To make flowers from maple leaves, you need to pick up the right material. To do this, you should go to the nearest park or alley where this beautiful tree grows . Leaves should be chosen as bright as possible and preferably in different shapes. Already dried specimens are not suitable, you may have to pick them from the tree. If the collection takes place in late autumn, there is no need to be afraid to damage the tree - it is already sleeping, the main thing is not to damage the branches. One magnificent flower of maple leaves will require a tiny specimen of any color, two or three large red, orange or yellow, and the sepals are made of the most green natural material.



homework

So that the flower from maple leaves does not crack and turn out bright, the manufacturing process should not be delayed. Natural materials quickly dry in the apartment and become unusable after five to six hours in the room. Pick up green threads of a natural shade or floral ribbon, sharp scissors and leaves sorted by size and color. They take one element, carefully bend it through the finger and begin to twist to get the core of the future bud. The main rule is that the base should not fall apart, you can lightly fix it with thread or make a couple of skeins with tape tape. Then take a large sheet and twist in the same way, gently pressing at the base to make a suitable shape. Gradually increasing with new elements, the rose is adjusted to the desired size and a sepal is made from a green blank. A flower from maple leaves is ready when the base is tightly wrapped with the last detail, and everything is fixed with threads or floral tape.

Making the composition

Having checked all your windings for strength, so that the flower does not fall apart during operation, you can make a bouquet. For a beautiful composition, you need to prepare from three to seven buds of different sizes, thin branches, to which all the elements are wound, and additional leaves. One bouquet can be created in just one or two hours - it can be an excellent gift for a loved one or a souvenir at a bohemian party. From the remaining materials it is easy to make applications, flowers from leaves to decorate a mini vase, or an album with a herbarium in a vintage style.



To ensure that the bouquet does not deteriorate longer and has a festive appearance, the craftswomen recommend sprinkling it with ordinary hairspray (or with sparkles). This process will be exciting not only for adults - any child can easily do such a job, and a walk in the fresh air to collect material will be useful for the whole family. Compositions can be supplemented with branches of hazel, mountain ash, acorns and small chestnuts. Such a gift will be nice to get a grandmother, child or colleague with good taste.
