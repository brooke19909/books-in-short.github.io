Payment systems: rating, comparison, reviews

Payments and transfers on the World Wide Web have become the norm for a good half of Internet users. In addition, almost all services of this plan have their own or third-party cards. Payment systems are gaining momentum due to the fact that you can pay for the service or cash out through the usual plastic cards.

There are a lot of such services in the vastness of the Web, and every year there are even more. And if experienced users are more or less oriented in all this variety, then beginners simply shrug their hands and study the ratings of payment systems on the Internet in the hope of choosing their best option. The choice is further complicated by the fact that everyone has their own needs and preferences. And if one service is good for the first user, then it is not a fact that it will meet the requirements of the second and third. So there is something to deal with.

Below you will find the rating of electronic payment systems, which includes the most popular and well-established services. All the products described below can be found on the official resources of the developers, so there should be no problems with testing. The rating of payment systems is based on the calculation of expert opinions (financial web magazines, independent analytical companies, etc.), as well as the responses of ordinary users.

Difficulty choosing

As mentioned above, there are a lot of similar services, both from domestic developers and foreign ones. For a clearer picture, we denote the ratings of payment systems in the world and specifically for Russia.

Both the former and the latter have their own specific (territorial) advantages and disadvantages, which must be taken into account. But first things first. Let's start with Russian services and continue with foreign ones.

"Yandex money"

Many people call Yandex.Money a national payment system. In principle, the way it is. The service was made by Russians, for Russians and is used mostly by Russian-speaking users. The system appeared much later than WebMoney, but immediately found its fans. Moreover, there is a reason to love her.

If you try to switch from WebMoney to Yandex Money, then the functionality of the latter service will seem simple, understandable and as friendly as possible. Instead of a local client, a web interface is used here, where everything is competently categorized and no tedious searches, as well as learning instructions, are threatened. Judging by user reviews, the service is perfectly adapted to Russian realities. Here you will find literally everything you need: paying fines in the traffic police, utilities, paying off loans, contributions to municipal organizations and much more.

"Kiwi"

Qiwi is one of the oldest and most trusted payment systems. Judging by user reviews, it is mostly used by beginners. The service, like Yandex.Money, does not have a local client, you need to manage it through the web interface.

The latter is simple, understandable and should not cause serious problems with development. The service reliably protects the user's data and finances from hacking, but still the percentage of illegal seizure of accounts is slightly higher than the similar indicators of Yandex.Money and WebMoney.

Users in their reviews also note that the system is very synergistic with commercial resources and without any problems allows you to set up payment acceptance or payment on your website. Unlike the two previous services, there is no identification as such.

For normal operation, that is, with bank cards and ordinary payments, you do not even need to send copies of your passport. It is enough to enter data in a special field. But for more serious operations (large amounts, connecting an online store, etc.) meticulous identification is still needed.
