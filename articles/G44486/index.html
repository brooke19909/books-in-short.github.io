The village of Dzhemete. Holiday Village near the sea

The suburb of Anapa Dzhemete, located five kilometers from the city, stretches along the coastline right up to Vityazevo. The small village of Dzhemete is a very attractive place for many tourists who want to relax on the clean Black Sea coast. There are no more than 1000 permanent residents in it, but in seasonal time the village is crowded with vacationers.

Beach holidays in Dzhemete





The name of the village from the Adyghe language is translated as "gold placer", and this is not an accident. The fact is that the sands of Anapa resemble gold. Dzhemete is located directly along the coastal zone, so the maximum distance to the beaches is only a few tens of meters. These are beautiful beaches that have no equal on the entire Black Sea coast. Moreover, these beaches have rightfully earned the title of the best in all of Europe. These places are especially suitable for families with children, as the bottom of the beaches of Dzhemete is flat and flat.

Initially, the village was built to strengthen. In 1828, in order to strengthen the path to Anapa and protect it from the attack of the Circassians, small buildings were erected, which later turned into residential settlements.

How to spend time in Dzhemete

The resort village is famous not only for its amazing beaches. Frequent guests of Dzhemeta are tourists who prefer a wellness and spa vacation. There are many boarding houses that provide medical and preventive care. It is here that the soil is rich in healing mud with a unique composition, mineral waters, saturated with hydrogen sulfide. Here you can take advantage of all the delights of the best doctor - nature. The purest sea breeze, warm healing sand and sunny baths are the best medicines. On the beach you can rent a sun lounger, umbrella or snorkeling equipment.

Dzhemete has a developed infrastructure. There is everything for a good rest. The village has many cozy restaurants, cafes, bars, shops and markets. Vacationers can choose for themselves entertainment for every taste. Here you can go diving, ride catamarans, water skiing, jet skis. Fans of extreme sports can soar above the sea by parachute or hang glider.





In the evening you can dance until you drop at an incendiary disco. The recreation center "Alenka"

In the very center of the village of Dzhemete is a beautiful recreation center "Alenka". A few steps away is the market. The infrastructure of this region is especially developed. Vacationers can settle in 2- or 3-storey buildings with cozy comfortable rooms. The territory of the recreation center is filled with gazebos, tables for relaxation. There is also a huge playground with numerous swings and places for children's games. Just five minutes away is a beautiful beach. Dzhemete Water Park is 500 meters away . The recreation center by the sea is surrounded by grocery stores and cafes. Nearby is a stop.

How to get to Dzhemete

You can get from Anapa to the village by minibus or taxi, which runs every 5 minutes. Anapa can be reached by plane, train or bus. By train, you can get to Novorossiysk (Tunnel station, the penultimate station), Anapa or Krasnodar. Regular buses run from Krasnodar to Anapa. A trip takes an average of 3 hours. You can also get there by your own car (the roads of the Krasnodar Territory are considered the best in all of Russia).
