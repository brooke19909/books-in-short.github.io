General impression

The Gloria hotel chain (Turkey) is widely known and is associated with quality service and a vibrant vacation. It is worth noting that the hotel is thought out to the smallest detail so that guests can fully relax. Spacious elegant rooms, huge pools, a private beach - all this immerses tourists in a carefree atmosphere. The restaurant has very tasty food, as well as the possibility of forming an individual diet menu. Well, of course, it is worth noting that the hotel has many Russian-speaking employees.

Despite the fact that Gloria (hotel-resort, Turkey) makes a rather positive impression, there were some shortcomings. The main one is the constant rise in prices from season to season. At the same time, the quality of services does not get better. Very little attention is paid to beach care. As a result, sharp stones, trash and algae. The constant noise from bars and other establishments does not contribute to a quiet and relaxing holiday, day or night.
