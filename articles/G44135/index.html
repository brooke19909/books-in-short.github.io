How to get a Yandex-wallet. Brief instruction

In recent years, all kinds of electronic wallets have become very popular. And this is no accident. After all, it is much more convenient to pay for a purchase while sitting at home in front of a computer. The most popular are payment systems such as Yandex and WebMoney. Electronic wallets are almost impossible to crack. But the most reliable in this regard remains Yandex. This explains its popularity among users. However, the question often arises of how to get a Yandex-wallet and how to use it correctly.

How to register and create a wallet in the Yandex payment system





To have a Yandex wallet, you need to go through a simple registration. You can use the electronic system through the web interface. Until 2011, a special program was used, which was installed on a computer. However, recently it has been recommended that all money transfers be carried out precisely through this interface.

Login

So how to get a wallet? Yandex.Money (the instructions for registering in the system will be clear to everyone) is such a simple service that even a novice and an inexperienced user can cope with it. First of all, you need to create an account on the Yandex portal. Simply register mail in the system. The password and login you received can be used in the future to create accounts in other services.

If you already have an Yandex account, log in using your username and password. There are situations when the input data for the account has been lost. The creators have provided for such an option. You can enter the site using social networks. This is much simpler, since you can get a wallet much faster. The authorization buttons through social networks can be seen at the very bottom of the form.

Account opening

Once you have logged in, you can begin to create a wallet.

To do this, you need to log in to the Yandex.Money service by entering your username and password or through a social network. In the window that opens, in the most visible place there is a large button "Open Account".

Should I indicate a phone number





At the very first step of registration, you will be asked to indicate your mobile phone number and, of course, enter captcha. Do not miss this opportunity, as
It is impossible to open an Yandex electronic wallet without additional confirmation. For money transfers, they often require a one-time password, which usually comes in the form of SMS. In addition, thanks to the binding
mobile phone, if necessary, quickly and easily restore access to your account. This is required when hacking an account or losing a password.

Registration without number

If you do not want to indicate your number, then you should click on the link "I do not have a phone." As a result, you will be transferred to the second step of opening an account. Here, in addition to the payment password, you will have to specify the recovery code, since it is simply unrealistic to start a new Yandex-wallet without additional security measures. You create your own recovery code. If you nevertheless indicated a phone number, then you will simply receive a code in the form of SMS, which you indicate for further registration.

Reusable and one-time payment passwords





On the next page, you will need to enter an invented payment password, which is usually transmitted using an encrypted channel when working with a wallet. This does not allow him to be intercepted during operations with electronic money. In the future, it‚Äôs still better to activate a one-time password, which is automatically sent to the phone number you specified, since it is easy to get a Yandex-wallet, and protecting it from hacking is a little more difficult.

Security

The security of an electronic wallet worries every user. Yandex provides the following function. If you forget to close your account, then after 15 minutes access to it will be automatically interrupted. This protects your money from theft. No one can take advantage of your forgetfulness.

After registration

So, how to get a Yandex-wallet, figured out. Now the question is how to work with it. After registration and clicking on the link "Open account" you will be redirected to the service page. Here in the column on the left you will find the number of your new wallet. On the same page you will find useful information on how to withdraw, transfer, replenish, spend electronic money and attach a bank card.

Settings





To get started, you need to perform a few simple steps, since getting a Yandex-wallet is easy enough, but you also need to configure it. First, click on the "Account Management" link. The Yandex.Money system is anonymous. But this affects the capabilities of the electronic wallet. You can expand them by clicking on the "Change Status" link. Here you will learn about the available options and how to connect them correctly. For more comfortable work, it is necessary to provide the administration with your passport data. After that, you will have extended withdrawal limits, the ability to transfer funds, as well as making payments. This, of course, may not be necessary for everyone. But, in order to secure your wallet, you should connect a function such as a one-time payment password. This will increase system security. To do this, just click on the link "Go to a one-time password", after that you will open several options for safe work with the Yandex.Money service. The easiest option is to receive a key in the form of SMS. The rest is a bit more complicated.
