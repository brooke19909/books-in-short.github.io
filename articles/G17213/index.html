Nexus 5x Smartphone Review

If you do not closely monitor the smartphone market, then you probably are not aware that Nexus mobile devices are developed by various companies in partnership with the famous Google corporation. In most cases, these smartphones are designed by the Android developer. These devices have the most various modifications and characteristics.

Today we will be testing the Nexus 5X. A review of the device is also included in this material. All Nexus smartphones regularly receive updates to the latest versions of operating systems. In addition, they are considered to be one of the best in terms of characteristics and external parameters.

Development



Google annually selects the most popular and successful manufacturers capable of creating new devices from the Nexus line. It is also possible that one developer can release not one device, but several at once. A similar situation, for example, happened with LG, which in just one year managed to create several Nexus devices at once, including 5X. He will be discussed today in this article.

Appearance



So, before us is the Google Nexus 5X. We begin the review with the case material, as well as the external parameters. If you pay attention to the design of Nexus, then you can immediately understand that manufacturers are not so keen on this aspect, and in the end the devices are quite simple. Naturally, the logic of a large Google corporation is immediately obvious: it wants smartphones from the Nexus range to have a universal and unique appearance that many will like and will not cause complaints from users. However, many manufacturers of these devices manage to fulfill the wishes of a large corporation. In particular, this can be said about the LG Nexus 5X. Phone overview will allow you to get acquainted with the presented model, as well as determine for yourself whether to buy it or not. In fact, in the first place, everything will depend on your wishes and preferences. We can safely say that the device is unique in its kind.

Classic



Turning your attention to the front of the device, you can see that the screen with a diagonal of 5.5 inches is covered with an additional protective glass Gorilla Glass 3. Also, this display has an oleophobic coating. On the front of the smartphone are two speakers. They allow you to get high-quality sound from the Nexus 5X. The rear view provides an opportunity to notice a serious flaw. The fact is that the large camera module stands out significantly. The same can be said about the fingerprint scanner and, of course, the Nexus logo. It looks impossible to determine how this device differs from other similar smartphones, as manufacturers seriously decided not to think about the design. If you want to purchase a mobile device with an unusual appearance, as well as body shapes, then you probably will not even notice this option.

Protection

A few words about the strength of the Nexus 5X. A review of the case allows us to say that the front of the device is completely covered with Gorilla Glass, and the sides are made of matte plastic. The back of the smartphone is also made of plastic. It is incredibly pleasant to the touch. Also on the back there is a special soft-touch effect, it is very convenient. Thanks to him, the phone is less polluted and practically does not slip out of his hands.

Let us turn our attention to the size of the Nexus 5X. A review of the dimensions made it possible to prove that, despite the appearance, the device turned out to be quite light and compact. The code name of this smartphone that was used in the development is Bullhead. Model numbers are H791 and H790. The phone was released in 2015. This model is the first device that was equipped with the Marshmallow operating system - Android 6.0. The smartphone is presented in black, white and blue colors.
