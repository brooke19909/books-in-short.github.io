Knitted cardigan in the style of Chloe: description and manufacture

Differences Cardigans Chloe

In order to knit a cardigan in the style of Chloe, you must first understand what a cardigan in the style of Chloe looks like. The brand has existed for a long time and the main target audience of the brand is similar to fans of the Dior brand.





The main distinguishing feature of the girl in the Chloe style is femininity and lightness. Models of clothes are airy and fancy. Moreover, all models are absolutely applicable to real life. Simplicity and lightness of the silhouette, the absence of heavy and bulky elements. Everything creates a feeling of tenderness and softness, the same is manifested in the color scheme.

Colors can be powdery, as if muffled, and can be juicy, but not provocative. All this should emphasize the amazing femininity of shoppers. With the arrival of designer Claire Veit Keller into the Chloe fashion house, the models have largely acquired non-conformism and deliberate negligence, continuing to combine them with light silhouettes of a simple cut. In order to create a knitted cardigan in the style of Chloe knitting needles themselves need. Choose a small diameter to resemble the latest collections. One of the new fashion trends is the red color and its shades. Therefore, the choice of yarn should be approached with all responsibility. We recommend the color "Grenadine" (a shade of red).

But if red is not to your liking, you can choose the equally trendy color "Port" (Tawny Port) or the noble "Butter" (Butterum), referring to the nude palette and perfectly combined with any color. Wool is the best fit, but acrylic will look no less exquisite. We start knitting a cardigan in the style of Chloe with our own hands.





The knitted cardigan has a chest circumference of 34 0.5, 37 0.5, 44 0.5, 47 0.5. For example, let's analyze the smallest one. The pattern is called an elastic band; it is knitted by a set of odd. number of stitches.

For a cardigan, you need to connect six rows of straight lines. The final eight rows should be repeated twice, so that 303 loops remain. Persons. a row fits with a decrease, out. also knit with a decrease. Next, we knit 22 rows directly. The final 24 rows should be repeated two more times so that 279 stitches remain. The front row fits with the diminution, the wrong side as well. 271 stitches remain. Next, repeat eleven straight rows.

Knit on out. side installation row. It is necessary to knit 17 stitches and mark with a marker, 51 stitches to mark with a marker, tie to a marker and remove, knit to the end of the row.

In order to associate a series with a decrease in faces. the side will need: to the marker face crossed - knit two loops twice. Then knit up to two loops to the marker, then two faces. crossed loops - each two times. Reduce four loops.

To knit a row with a decrease in out. side will need: to the wrong side of the marker crossed - two stitches to knit twice. Then knit up to two loops to the marker, then two out. crossed loops - twice. Reduce four loops.

The next six rows fit straight. Then follow the faces. and out. rows with a decrease of 4 loops. There will be 255 stitches. Running braid at the waist. This is followed by 26 rows of lines, the remainder - 239 stitches.

Chloe-style knit cardigan is divided into a shelf and a back.

For the right shelf, we knit one row of facial. We knit an armhole: at the very beginning of Izn. row three loops close once, after three loops two more times. You should get 47 stitches. Next, 27 rows must be tied directly and finished with the front ones. In order to form the shoulders, the beginning of the back row is 14 stitches, which are closed once, after another 13 stitches once. There are 19 stitches left. Remove everything on the loop holder.

The left shelf is symmetrical in the opposite direction.

For armholes, 3 loops should be closed at the beginning of the next two rows. At the beginning of the next four - 3 loops. The remainder is 95.

After the front part, two purl stitches are knitted together until the end of the row. From the inside, the two front loops are knitted together to the end of the row. Then we knit 26 straight rows.

Knitted knitting Chloe cardigan is almost ready. There was a collar, sleeves and a zipper. Sleeves are knitted by dialing 51 loops and alternating out. and individuals. rows with decreases. For the collar, 18 loops of each of the shelves are knitted up. It is also worth doing with a sleeve. This cardigan is executed on a lightning. Zipper is sewn on both sides of the shelves.
