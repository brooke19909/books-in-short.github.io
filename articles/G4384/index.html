Phuket is very popular among Russian tourists. It is really very good to relax here in season. And one of the most visited resorts of this island by domestic travelers is Patong. The tourist infrastructure of this town is developed simply superbly. To rent here is inexpensive and at the same time quite comfortable housing will not be absolutely no difficulty.

Lamai Inn Hotel: Location Features

With this three-star hotel in Phuket, many Russian tour operators work. This small hotel is located directly in Patong Beach . Tourists praise the Lamai Inn 3 * for its convenient location. Near this complex there are literally all the necessary elements of a city infrastructure for a pleasant stay. Just a 10-minute walk away, for example, is the most popular Patong resort street - Bangla Road.





Successfully located this hotel, according to most tourists, and in relation to the beach. Guests of this hotel can walk to the famous Patong in just 5 minutes with a calm step.

General description of the complex

It is believed that the hotels of Lamai Inn 3 * in Phuket , the photos of which are presented on the page, are just perfect for tourists who prefer to spend most of their time at the resort on the beach or in clubs. The service at this hotel is unobtrusive for vacationers. The complex is considered simple and inexpensive. However, in order to simply spend the night, in the opinion of most tourists, it fits really perfectly.

They accept guests at this hotel in a neat three-story building, built in traditional Thai style. The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day. Tourists who arrive at the hotel at night will be accommodated immediately upon arrival.

The property offers an airport transfer service to Phuket guests. Of course, it may be especially relevant for vacationers arriving on the island without the help of tour operators and independently booking hotel rooms. Hotel Lamai Inn Hotel 3 * is located, unfortunately, quite far from the nearest airport - 37 km. Therefore, to get to this hotel without a transfer for such vacationers would certainly be somewhat difficult.

How to feed at the hotel?

About food in the hotel Lamai Inn 3 * tourists leave exclusively positive reviews. You can rent rooms at this hotel with or without breakfast. Tour operators usually provide their customers with a choice in this regard.

Most tourists are advised to purchase packages at this hotel with breakfast. The food here is really good. In most hotels in Thailand, vacationers who rent rooms without meals prefer to visit a cafe in the city. In Lamai Inn 3 * , even such guests go to the hotel's dining room. They only need to pay 150 baht for breakfast.

For those guests who rented hotel rooms with meals, experienced tourists recommend not to be late for meals in the dining room. Breakfast here lasts strictly until 10 a.m. Those residents who came to the dining room after this time will have to pay the same 150 baht for the choice of dishes.

For a fee in the dining room of this hotel you can take additional dishes. Breakfast here consists of sausages, scrambled eggs, ham, toasts with butter, etc. Served in the hotel's dining room are mainly dishes of European or Thai cuisine. Moreover, they are almost always fresh, hot and tasty.
