Hotel Pondok Sari Bungalow Resort 3 * (Bali, Indonesia): description, service, reviews

Bali is the most developed Indonesian resort. It attracts many tourists with pristine landscapes, impressive volcanoes, ancient temples and tropical forests. Not so long ago, our compatriots chose Bali as a seaside resort. In our article we want to talk about one of the island's hotels - Pondok Sari Bungalow Resort, located in the city of Kuta.

A bit about the hotel

Pondok Sari Bungalow Resort is a budget hotel in the center of Kuta. The hotel is only ten minutes drive from the airport, and in the immediate vicinity are numerous supermarkets, cafes and restaurants. The complex is located opposite Pemuteran Beach.





The location of the hotel allows you to quickly reach all the key places that may be of interest to tourists. The complex itself consists of two- and three-story buildings located in a small but green area.

All apartments of the hotel are equipped with minibars, telephones, safes, televisions, refrigerators, air conditioning, bathrooms, hairdryers and kettles. Each room also has its own terrace with a table and chairs.

Dining at the hotel

Pondok Sari Bungalow Resort rates include breakfast only. They are served in the form of a buffet. The range of food is not very diverse. As a rule, the same dishes are served daily: eggs, scrambled eggs, rolls, tea, rice, salads, fruits. The choice is small, but it‚Äôs difficult to stay hungry. The rest of the time you can eat in any cafe that you liked.

Despite the apparent simplicity of local food establishments, they cook quite tasty. Moreover, experienced tourists note an interesting trend. The farther the cafe is from the ocean, the cheaper the food costs. Moreover, the quality of the dishes offered is no different. And since the Pondok Sari Bungalow Resort (Bali) is not located on the first line or even on the second, the cost of food in the nearest establishments is more than acceptable. You can choose your favorite cafe after visiting several of them. In principle, the menu in them is not too different, get ready for the fact that you have to eat exclusively local food.

Pondok Sari Bungalow Resort: service and infrastructure

As we have already mentioned, the hotel has a very small, but at the same time beautiful and green area. There is a beautiful pool, a fountain with fish, a relaxation area equipped with sunbeds and umbrellas. The reception of the hotel employs an attentive and smiling staff who are always ready to help in resolving any issue.

You can rent a bike at the hotel for more convenient transportation around the resort (rental price is 500,000 rupees), and the rental price is much lower than in other locations. Knowledgeable tourists strongly recommend taking this type of transport as a means of transportation, otherwise you will have to walk a lot.





At the reception you will be able to navigate in a variety of sightseeing trips. Guests can enjoy massages at the hotel.

Beach vacation

The main reasons that are currently very popular holiday in Bali are prices and beautiful beaches. By the way, Kuta is perhaps the only place on the entire coast where the waves break not on coral reefs, but on golden sand. The tides are almost not felt. The entire coastline of the resort is incredibly beautiful and in demand by tourists. From morning till night, surfers ride here, and in the evenings not only vacationers, but also locals come to enjoy the beautiful sunsets.

The coast is very wide, sandy with gentle slopes into the ocean, which makes the rumbles of the waves beautiful and long. Stronger waves are observed in the very center of the beach. Every tourist will find here entertainment to their liking. Numerous cafes, boutiques, and surf schools were built along the coast.





Hotel Pondok Sari Bungalow Resort 3 * (Indonesia) is quite far from the coast. According to tourists, the road to the ocean is 20-30 minutes leisurely step. On the way there are numerous shops selling souvenirs, so the road is not very boring. Of course, not all vacationers like this remoteness of the hotel from the coast, but this nuance is fully compensated by the rather low cost of living in a hotel.

Experienced tourists recommend renting bikes, this can be done right at the hotel reception. Owning such a vehicle will greatly simplify the movement of the resort. You can quickly get to the beach, as well as visit the modest sights of Kuta.

A bit about Bali

Why recently, in terms of beach holidays, a holiday in Bali has been popular? Prices for tours offered by operators in this area are incredibly attractive for tourists. It is the low cost of travel that is a decisive factor for many vacationers. Moreover, Bali is an interesting country in terms of nature, exotic, temples and attractions. There is always something to see. The country is able to surprise even those people who travel a lot in South Asia.

Undoubtedly, all Asian countries have something in common, but at the same time, each is unique in its own way. In this regard, the island of Bali is a real storehouse of a foreign culture, which you certainly want to know at least a little. Having decided to visit the island, prepare in advance for the fact that part of the time you just need to devote to trips and attractions. A bounty-style beach vacation is a great prospect, but you need to look at the local exotic too.





It is worth noting that the most developed tourist towns on the island are Seminyak and Kuta. In general, these two resorts are very similar to each other: the ocean, beaches, parties, bars and restaurants. However, Kuta is more attractive to young people, thanks to affordable prices. The cost of a standard room per day is approximately 1678 rubles, there are also promotional offers. Often tourists buy a tour. Accommodation in a Superior double room for 7 nights will cost about 100,000 rubles. The price includes breakfast.

The resort has also been chosen by numerous surf lovers. The local coast is an ideal place for those who want to conquer the wave. For this reason, the district has an incredible number of schools and rental points for surfing equipment.

Pondok Sari Bungalow Resort: reviews

Summing up the conversation about the hotel, I want to turn to the reviews of tourists who managed to visit it. What are your holidaymakers' experiences with Pondok Sari Bungalow Resort? The description of the hotel, given by us earlier, gives only an approximate idea of ‚Äã‚Äãthe hotel, and real reviews of tourists will help to make a real picture.





It is worth noting that the reviews about the hotel are quite controversial. Some tourists are satisfied with living in it, especially considering the budget cost of apartments, while others, on the contrary, express a lot of discontent. And now about all the nuances in order.

The first thing you should pay attention to holidaymakers: the hotel is significantly removed from the coast. If for someone this moment is very important, then you should look for another option.

And now about the numbers. Hotel apartments are not very diverse. They are decorated with national-style wooden furniture. All interior items are not new, but in good condition. In principle, the apartments have everything that an unpretentious tourist needs. In the closet you can even find an iron and an ironing board, which is surprising for a hotel of this category. Rooms also include tumble dryers and umbrellas. For Bali, heavy rains are normal. The island has a fairly high humidity, so it is extremely difficult to dry things. Yes, and in some apartments you can stumble on mold.

All rooms have air conditioning, which are working properly, which is important at high temperatures. The rest of the apartment is rather modest. According to tourists, some rooms do not have windows, which is quite inconvenient. In addition, it is worth knowing that all the apartments of the hotel are quite modest in size, there are no spacious rooms.

According to tourists, the cleaning of apartments is superficial. Maids arrive daily at nine in the morning. Until this time, it is advisable to leave the room, otherwise you run the risk of simply being left without cleaning. Guests have many complaints about linen. Towels are very old and extremely washed, although they are changed regularly, subsequent ones are no better than the previous ones. Bed linen has long been in need of replacement.





If you plan to actively relax and devote time not only to a beach holiday, but also to sightseeing of local attractions, then the hotel is quite suitable for a budget holiday.

The hotel has a fairly small but beautifully decorated and green area with a pool and a fountain. Nearby is equipped a recreation area with good sunbeds.

General impression of the hotel
