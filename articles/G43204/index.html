Festival-Fair "Diagonal Alley" in St. Petersburg: one of the most magical events in the Northern capital

At the end of the summer of 2016, a truly magical festival was held in St. Petersburg. For three days, a real "Slanting Alley" worked for wizards and sorceresses from all over the country. This name is known to everyone who is familiar with the story of Harry Potter. In the Northern capital, a qualitative reconstruction of the magical space described in the books about the adventures of the boy wizard was prepared.

The magic slanting alley exists!





The organizer of the most fabulous event of the year in the northern capital was the production center of BANTEEVA GROUP. Oblique Lane or Alley is the place described in Harry Potter books.

In his work, the author talks about the fact that in the very center of London there is a small shopping district for wizards, which Muggles cannot get into. Shops located on Diagon Alley offer a variety of magic items, including magic wands, books, and stationery for studying at Hogwarts. For the festival, the territory of the loft project "Floors" was chosen.

Oblique Alley is a festival fair designed for visitors of any age. What is noteworthy, the event aroused interest not only among Harry Potter fans. Many visitors to the fair did not know too much about magical reality before visiting it. But having visited such a magical place, everyone wanted to learn as much as possible about the world of a young wizard.

Festival-Fair "Diagonal Alley" (St. Petersburg) in detail



Everyone could attend the magical event. Entrance to the territory of the festival space was carried out by special tickets. The cost of attending the event for adults (people over 21 years old) was 500 rubles, for students (12 years old-21 years old) - 300 rubles, for children (8-12 years old) - 100 rubles.

Children under 7 years old, accompanied by adults, could go to the fair for free. Tickets could be purchased in advance at the box office or directly at the entrance to the festival. Shopping in magic stores, cafes and restaurants was paid separately. The festival "Slanting Alley" was an incredible success and gathered a lot of enthusiastic reviews.

The organizers plan to repeat the event, and the possibility of holding the fair on a regular basis is being discussed. Follow the news and try not to miss the most magical event in the cultural life of the Northern capital!
