The location of the complex is very convenient, which certainly pleases tourists. The main attraction of the city - Kusadasi fortress - is just a stone's throw away, and just 2.2 kilometers to the ladies' beach. As for the nearest airport, it is located 53 kilometers from the hotel.

Richmond ephesus

The hotel complex Richmond Ephesus is distinguished by impeccable cleanliness and quality, for which it was awarded the Blue Flag, installed on its own beach. Its other positive qualities are its convenient location (only 15 minutes drive to Ephesus and the center of Kusadasi). The cozy rooms, in addition to a TV and air conditioning, have a safe, a private bathroom, a hairdryer and free toiletries.

The hotel has indoor and outdoor pools, a water park, a hammam, a gym, playgrounds for various games, free parking for cars and an original store where you can buy good, fine wine. In addition, Beach, Sunset and Amfi Tiyatro bars provide a variety of drinks throughout the day.

Kustur Club Holiday Village

Private beach, outdoor pools, water slides, steam bath and much more - all this you will find in the 5 star hotel (all inclusive) in Kusadasi Kustur Club Holiday Village. Among the other advantages of the establishment are the opportunity to visit the fitness center, sauna and tennis court. A mini-club is open for children at certain times: from 10 a.m. to noon and from 14:30 to 17:30.

The hotel has three restaurants with a varied menu. If you wish, you can visit the pancake room located in the Kustur club. There are also 5 hotel bars.

Kustur Club Holiday Village is just 5 kilometers from the city center.

To register upon arrival at the hotel, you will need the same credit card that you used for booking. If you used someone else's credit card, you need a scanned copy of it and a power of attorney from the card holder. If you do not submit these documents, you will have to pay the room fee again, and the amount paid for the first time will be returned to the card that you used first. Pay special attention to this when you are planning on the road.

Prices from 4 to 7 thousand rubles.
