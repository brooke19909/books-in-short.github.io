Hotel location

Eurasia Boutique Hotel 3 * - This is a five-story building, built in 2008 in the center of the Thai resort of Pattaya, on the third street. From the Suvarnabhumi International Airport, the road will be about two hours (a hundred and fifty kilometers). Ordering an individual transfer to the hotel will save you a long way. All passengers will be delivered to their place of residence, which will significantly reduce travel time.

To the famous busy place - Walkin Street - from Eurasia Boutique Hotel 3 * You can walk for fifteen minutes. It is convenient to buy products at the Festival shopping center, which is also within walking distance. Nearby are many shops, private cafes, eateries. Therefore, the hotel is perfect for those who like active nightlife, as well as for business trips.

Souvenirs from memory: caution will not hurt

Thailand, whose Asian exotic attracts thousands of tourists every year, is rich in souvenir products.



In memory of the trip, tourists bring souvenirs and products made from materials of Thai origin. Before exporting anything from the country, consult about the consequences with competent persons or representatives of the operator, with the guide.

Sea shells, mollusks of any kind, stuffed representatives of the fauna, and ivory products are prohibited for export from the country. It is strictly forbidden to export soil, seeds, plants.

The death penalty is the export of narcotic drugs. You can not suspect the appointment and composition of some ointments, medicines purchased privately.



It should be prudent, since the law in relation to such facts is very severe and it will be quite difficult to solve the problems.
