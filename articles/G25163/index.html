Abrau Durso, boarding house "Star": location, description, holidays with children

Pension Zvezdniy is located near the sea, surrounded by forests of the Abrau Nature Reserve and 5 kilometers from the famous village of Abrau-Dyurso, located on the Black Sea coast of the Caucasus. The nature here is amazing. Three elements came together here: sea, mountains and forest. Pension "Star" in Abrau-Durso will help you to relax from the hustle and bustle, restore strength and give an incredible boost of vigor.





Location

In the Krasnodar Territory there are several boarding houses bearing the name "Star". But the best of them is the one that is located in Abrau-Durso. Pension "Star" is located in a unique area, which is called "Maly Liman tract." This secluded place is located five kilometers from the village of Abrau-Durso and a wonderful lake called Abrau. The sea is just two hundred meters from it, so you can walk to the beach in a few minutes.

A unique climate combining sea and forest air, infused with the smell of juniper trees and cypresses, will bring undoubted health benefits. The Caucasus Mountains, covered with forest, protect the area from the wind and create comfortable conditions for relaxation. A clean finely pebble beach makes swimming safe and comfortable.





Pension "Star"

The health resort can take up to 200 vacationers, the services of which provide comfortable rooms for every taste: from budget to luxury. But regardless of the budget, vacationers are allocated comfortable rooms equipped with air conditioning, TV, shower and toilet.

Pension Zvezdny (Abrau-Durso) has equipped sports fields and a gym, which allow training camps for sports teams. For lovers of outdoor activities there is the opportunity to ride around the boarding house. There are bicycles for this. Volleyball and basketball courts, as well as a tennis court, are always at the service of lovers of these sports.

Breakfast is included in the price of the ticket, and a three-meal meal complex is provided for an additional fee. According to vacationers, the cooks of the boarding house cook deliciously. Do you want to eat on your own? Each room has an equipped kitchen where you can prepare lunch or dinner. In addition, the hotel has a bar where you can have fun with friends.

A special pride of the Zvezdniy boarding house (Abrau-Durso) is a pump room with mineralized and clean water, which is served in the rooms. If you come to rest on your own transport, you can use the guarded parking lot for a car. And the entire territory of the institution is protected, so the rest can safely be called safe.
