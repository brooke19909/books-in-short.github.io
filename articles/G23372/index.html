Rest in the village of Volna, Krasnodar Territory

How many of us have not heard of the resorts of the Krasnodar Territory? Big black sea cities are heard - Sochi, Anapa, Gelendzhik. But many people want to take a break from the noisy streets, and then they choose quiet small villages on the seashore.

One of these villages is Volna in the Krasnodar Territory.





Location and transport

On the Taman Peninsula, rich in beautiful places, is the village of Volna. The nearest administrative center is the city of Temryuk, Temryuk district of Krasnodar Territory.

The airport of Anapa is located closest to Wave - 70 kilometers. From Anapa you have to go by bus or by car. The bus to Volna in the Krasnodar Territory runs only twice a day, morning and evening.

Getting to the village is quite far and long, but it provides vacationers a really relaxing holiday, far from the hustle and bustle of a lot of people, characteristic of the resorts of the Kuban.

There is no public transport in the village itself, but this is not a problem, because the Wave is easy to get around on foot in a relatively short time.

Rest in the village of Volna, Krasnodar Territory

The village is focused on tourism, so it has affordable housing for any wallet. You can choose the private sector, mini-hotels or recreation centers. Due to the high coast, tourists in these places like to set up campgrounds.





The village is very well maintained. In it you will not find anything remotely resembling garbage.

The beaches in this resort place are sandy and have their own characteristics:

  • Since the village is located on a natural hill, you have to go down to the sea along narrow winding paths. It will be quite difficult for people in years and young children to get to the sea.
  • The strip of the beach is narrow; at the same time many vacationers cannot be on it.
  • The sand on the beach is golden white and fairly clean. The waves bring interesting seashells to the shore, which children and lovers of marine crafts like to collect.
  • Dolphins love the sea near Volna in the Krasnodar Territory, and they can often be seen from the shore.
  • The entrance to the sea is gentle, but then goes down quite steeply. Families with young children should be careful not to let their children swim alone.
  • The beach in this area is mostly wild, without the usual umbrellas and sunbeds.

The bathing season begins in May, by this time the water is already warming up enough for water procedures. The air temperature is also very comfortable, because the Taman Peninsula is one of the warmest places in Russia.





Entertainment and infrastructure of the village

Near the Waves there are several diving centers. As in most resort places in Taman, there is pure clear water here, in its depth you can see many ships and even submarines that have sunk in the Great Patriotic War.

Thanks to the surrounding steppe and a high bank up to 20 meters high, a light breeze constantly blows in this area, which contributes to the development of paragliding.

Fishing enthusiasts specially come to the Volna village of the Krasnodar Territory and nearby villages to pursue their favorite hobby - a lot of fish that prefer clean water live on this sea site.

The settlement has several cafes, markets and a grocery store. In the souvenir shop you can buy crafts from local shells.

Clubs and restaurants can be reached from here for a long time and are inconvenient, if you are planning nightly entertainment, it is better to look for a larger village or city to spend your holidays.

Summary

Choosing where to relax, you need to carefully study all the reviews. In the Wave of the Krasnodar Territory will appeal to those who prefer a relaxing holiday, far from the hustle and bustle of large companies. But for those traveling to the sea with small children, getting to the beach will be problematic. People with mobility difficulties should also choose a place to relax with a gentle slope to the water.
