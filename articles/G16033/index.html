Yurga hotels in the Kemerovo region: description and photo

The city of Yurga with a population of about 82 thousand people is located in the north-west of the Kemerovo region on the Tom River, and is a large industrial center of the region. The city-forming enterprise is Yurginsky Machine-Building Plant LLC. And also on the territory of the city 15 plants and factories of various orientations are successfully operating.

In Yurg, there are a large number of shopping centers and shops. There are also cultural facilities, parks, cinemas, clubs and sports facilities. In this article we have selected the 5 best hotels of Yurga. Accommodation in them will be comfortable both during a tourist trip, and during a visit to the city with working questions.

Hotel complex "12 feet"

One of the best hotel options in the city of Yurga is the 12-foot hotel and entertainment complex, which is located a 5-minute walk from the Tom River at ul. Leningradskaya, house 1.

Hotel room "12 feet"

The hotel consists of 5 types of rooms:

  • two-room "Suite" worth 3,300 rubles;
  • Junior Suite, which will cost guests 2,800 rubles;
  • Double room - 2 300 rubles;
  • double room - 2 600 rubles;
  • single - 1,300 rubles.

The price includes a good breakfast, which is highly appreciated by hotel guests. The apartments include a private bathroom with amenities, a flat-screen satellite TV, a dressing room, a fridge and a kettle, free Wi-Fi.

The hotel-entertainment complex also has a night club where you can relax in a fun company, a kids club, billiards, a cinema, a bar and a sauna where you can relax after a busy day. For hotel guests the cost of a sauna is 600 rubles per hour.

The 12ft Hotel is especially popular with couples who praise the level of comfort and service.

"Siberia"

Hotel Yurga "Siberia" is located at: Kemerovo region, Yurga, st. Victory 15. She is an excellent accommodation option when visiting the city.

For accommodation, guests are provided with single and double rooms, which have everything necessary for a comfortable stay, including bath amenities and a TV.

"Viburnum"

The hotel of apartment type "Kalina" is considered one of the most popular among hotels of Yurga. It has 20 apartments of different levels of comfort. Economy studios are equipped with a TV, fridge, microwave, dishes, a double sofa or bed. Bed linen and towels are provided free of charge.

Prices for these apartments start at 600 rubles per night. The cost may vary depending on the days of the week. Standard one-room apartments will cost 1200 rubles on a weekday and 1,500 on Friday and Saturday. The apartment usually has 2 beds for 3 or 4 people, an LCD TV with cable TV, a fridge, a stove and a microwave, dishes and a kettle. Some apartments have a washing machine, iron and ironing board.

One-room (1,400 rubles on weekdays; 1,800 rubles on Friday and Saturday) and one-bedroom apartments from 2,000 rubles per day have a refrigerator, a flat-screen TV with cable channels, a refrigerator, a microwave oven, a washing machine and Internet access.

Luxury apartments are equipped with large 2X2 meter beds, plasma panels, a large bathroom and many others, which may be necessary for a comfortable stay, even for a long time. You can find all the information about the accommodation facility in Yurg and the phones of apartment-type hotels on the company's website.

Mini-hotel "Tamerlan"

Another great accommodation option is Tamerlan. This place is a family wellness center as well as a hotel in Jurga. The center is located at: st. Mashinostroiteley, house 53-b / 1. The small number of rooms of the hotel is divided into 2 types: a double room at the cost of 1 700 rubles per night and a studio for 2 200 rubles.

All rooms are quite spacious, have good soundproofing, a balcony with city views, a flat-screen cable TV, a refrigerator, a microwave, a kettle, a table with chairs and a private bathroom. Guests have the opportunity to order a continental breakfast for 150 rubles.

On the territory of "Tamerlane" you can also have fun and usefully spend time. At your service: a jacuzzi, a swimming pool, Turkish and Russian baths, a Finnish sauna, billiards, water aerobics, a beauty salon, a fitness studio and a kids club. Free high-speed internet is available on site.

Hotel and entertainment complex "Citadel"

Among the hotels of Yurga, special attention should be paid to the Citadel hotel and entertainment complex, which is located at 121 A Moskovskaya Street. Hotel guests note the comfort and cleanliness of the rooms, polite staff.

It offers 10 rooms of different levels of comfort. The cost of a 1-room apartment is 1,200 rubles per day; 2-room apartment will cost 2,000 rubles; a semi-suite will cost 2,200 rubles, while a Lux will cost 2,700 rubles a day. The price includes a delicious breakfast. On the territory of the GRK there is also a night club, a restaurant, a sauna with a jacuzzi, a pool and a Turkish bath. You can call a taxi at reception. Internet throughout the Citadel is free

Hotel "Russia"

And the last in the list of the best hotels in Yurga is Russia, which is located in the very center of the city. The number of rooms has 22 comfortable and cozy rooms.

The hotel guests are especially noted for the polite staff and quiet atmosphere. The cost of a room per day is from 2 100 rubles, depending on the type. The apartments come with a private bathroom, fridge, hairdryer, cable TV and free wi-fi.

On-site facilities include a cafe, a gym, a bathhouse, a sauna with a pool and karaoke, a laundry service, a business center and a left-luggage office. Also for car owners the hotel offers a guarded parking lot. Near the hotel there are various shops, a market, banks, cinemas, a sports complex, an urban recreation area. Hotel address: st. Volgogradskaya, house 2.
