Ibis Phuket Patong Hotel 3 * (Thailand, Patong, Phuket): description, services, reviews

It is no secret that one of the most popular holiday destinations in Thailand is the island of Phuket. This place can amuse any traveler with bright colors of nature, mild climate and high quality service. By the way, the most crowded is Patong Bay. Here on the shore there are a huge number of hotels. One of them is the Ibis Phuket Patong Hotel.

Proper planning is the key to a really good vacation. Therefore, before choosing a hotel, any traveler tries to get the maximum amount of information. Are shops, cafes and the beach located close to the hotel? Are there any conditions for having fun with the whole family? Can a guest count on good food, comfort and coziness?

Location and its features





As you know, when choosing a stopping place, you should find out about its location. The hotel complex Ibis Phuket Patong 3 * has a rather favorable location. It has already been mentioned that it is located in Patong Bay. The distance to the world famous beach is about 500 m. A convenient path leads to the shore, therefore, daily walks for tourists are usually not a burden. In addition, you can use public transport and hotel transfers, you just need to ask in advance about their driving schedule.

About 20 km is the main Phuket - a kind of center of the island with a lot of attractions and a huge number of tourists who come here every year. But the road from the airport will take no more than 20-30 minutes (by the way, the hotel offers a transfer service).

Brief information about hotel infrastructure

The Ibis Phuket Patong is a medium sized complex. The territory here is small, but beautiful, green and guarded around the clock. Its area is 5 thousand square meters. In the center is a large five-story building, built in a modern style. By the way, the hotel was opened not so long ago, in 2008. They have already managed to carry out reconstruction, so the building will please with a beautiful design and fresh repair.

The hotel has elevators, which is convenient. By the way, the ground floor is reserved for public use - this is the reception desk, restaurant and bar, as well as a spacious lounge with upholstered furniture and coffee tables where you can relax or wait for check-in.

Room categories offered by the hotel

Ibis Phuket Patong Hotel offers its customers check-in in one of the 258 rooms. For the most part, these are small but very comfortable standard rooms with two single or one king size bed. There are also the so-called Hollywood rooms - their area is slightly larger, and the design is more original.

In addition, Family Suites is at your service - spacious rooms designed for 3-4 people. They are great for people traveling with children, as well as for large companies. Of course, such rooms are larger, consisting of a living room and a sleeping area.

Hotel Ibis Phuket Patong 3 * (Thailand, Patong, Phuket): room description





Of course, when planning to spend several days at the hotel, it is worth asking about the conditions of accommodation. Former guests speak well of this place. The rooms are nicely furnished and quite spacious. There is a private furnished balcony where you can relax in the fresh air.

Of course, at your service and modern appliances. In particular, you can create pleasant temperature conditions in the room using a powerful air conditioning system. On TV, guests watch satellite channels (there is even one Russian). There is also a telephone, as well as an electronic safe. Upon request, guests are provided with an iron and other ironing facilities. In the minibar daily replenish the supply of drinking water (all other drinks for an additional fee). There is also an eclectic kettle, coffee, tea and utensils necessary for their preparation.

The bathroom is small, but with modern appliances. And hygiene standards are observed here - cleaning is carried out every day and includes the replacement of towels. Toiletries and a convenient stationary hairdryer are provided.

What kind of food do guests offer?





Accommodation in the hotel room Ibis Phuket Patong 3 * includes daily breakfast. They pass in the form of a buffet in the spacious room of a hotel restaurant. Guests say that you can eat here in a relaxed atmosphere. Although the selection of dishes is small, you can always choose something tasty. There are no queues for food, and freshly prepared food. By the way, guests note the presence of various fresh exotic fruits.

You can have dinner here in the restaurant. However, in the evenings, dishes are prepared to order of visitors, and the bill must be paid separately. On the other hand, the prices here are normal, so many guests prefer to eat at the hotel.

In any case, food problems do not arise, because this is a resort area - within walking distance of a huge number of cafes and restaurants for every taste and budget. You can even buy food in supermarkets, and on the beach there is an opportunity to have a good snack.

Entertainment and amenities by the sea





Of course, picturesque beaches and fun holidays by the sea - this is what tourists are waiting for when arriving on about. Phuket Ibis Phuket Patong Hotel is located approximately 500 meters from Patong Beach. This section of the coast is considered one of the most popular - thousands of tourists relax here. You can get to the sea on foot, and the road does not take much time.

This is a public beach, so quite often it is crowded. Nevertheless, the coastal area is clean and well maintained. It is cleaned several times a day, and the entrance to the sea is regularly cleared. Children feel great here, because there is a shallow area especially for bathing the smallest tourists. For a small fee, you can rent a sunbed, an umbrella and even eat. By the way, large beach towels are provided at the hotel.

Fans of more active recreation will also not be bored. Along the coast there are water sports centers where you can rent water transport (boat, catamaran), windsurf board and scuba diving equipment. Beginners can quickly complete training courses by working with experienced instructors.

Do they offer an additional service?





Comfortable rooms and excellent food are what you need for a good stay. Nevertheless, Ibis Phuket Patong Hotel provides its guests with some bonuses. To begin with, it is worth saying that the reception is open around the clock, which is very convenient. Here you will exchange currency for local bills at a favorable rate. It is possible to rent a safe from the administrator (provides maximum security). Luggage storage is also available. For a small fee, guests enjoy access to the Internet, and the speed here is quite good.

The hotel has a business center where you can send a fax. There is the necessary computer and office equipment, as well as a small post office. Near the hotel building is a parking lot, which guests use for free. By the way, both in a hotel and in a city, you can rent a car - many tourists do just that, because having your own transport opens up new opportunities for exploring the city.

How to spend your free time at the hotel?





Ibis Phuket Patong Hotel is most often used only as an overnight stay - travelers spend most of their time on the seashore. Nevertheless, you can always relax in the hotel.

There is a small freshwater pool in the courtyard for guests. Around are loungers with mattresses and umbrellas (by the way, they are always enough here, even at maximum occupancy). On the terrace, guests often have fun and get to know each other.

There is also a massage parlor at your service. After all, Thai massage is famous all over the world - it needs to be tested on yourself at least once. By the way, massage therapists work all day, sometimes even until late in the evening.

Entertainment can always be found outside the Ibis Phuket Patong Hotel. Thailand is a country with amazing culture and history, interesting traditions and stunning architectural and natural attractions. The hotel will help you to choose and purchase an interesting excursion, or just advise interesting places in the vicinity.

Vacation with a child: what can the hotel offer?

Today, people are increasingly taking their children with them on a trip. Of course, in this case, tourists are interested in comfort issues. What can Ibis Phuket Patong Hotel count on?

In fact, the hotel is suitable for a family vacation. For children there are discount systems. An extra bed is immediately delivered to the room. Also at the disposal of tourists and special family rooms. In the restaurant you can always find something useful and tasty for a child of almost any age.

There is a special pool in the courtyard for the kids - the water here always warms up well. Here kids love to splash around under the supervision of their parents. For a small fee, you can use the services of a professional experienced nanny - the prices at the hotel are not too high. On the territory of the city you can find a lot of entertainment for children and their parents.

Hotel Ibis Phuket Patong Hotel 4 *: guest reviews





A little communication with people who have already managed to spend several days at the hotel can sometimes provide a lot of valuable information. So what do they say about the ibis Phuket Patong 3 * hotel?

Almost all the reviews left by tourists are positive. The hotel is small but really cozy. The rooms are freshly renovated, new appliances and functional equipment in the bathroom. By the way, there are no problems with hot water.

The food at the hotel is delicious, and there are several good restaurants nearby where you can eat for little money. One has only to say that the staff at the hotel do not speak Russian. On the other hand, this does not prevent them from being polite and quickly solving problems that arise.

Another huge plus is the low cost of living, especially if you correlate it with the level of service. Here you can spend a truly relaxing and comfortable stay, spending a lot less money.
