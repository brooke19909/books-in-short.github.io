LCD "Green Island": information and reviews

Moscow is a huge metropolis, spreading widely in different directions and already beyond the Moscow Ring Road. The population of the agglomeration exceeds 15 million people, and all of them need housing. The tendency to sell existing housing in the capital and move beyond the Moscow Ring Road is becoming more fashionable . Thus, you can immediately get a double benefit: to get a newer housing, and save money. LCD "Green Island", located in Kotelniki, offers just such an option for moving.





A bit about Kotelniki

In the south-east of Moscow, adjacent to the Moscow Ring Road, the cities of Lyubertsy and Dzerzhinsky, the city of Kotelniki is located. This year its population exceeds 44 thousand people, and over the past 12 years, it continues to grow steadily. The matter is not limited to natural population growth. To a greater extent, this is the merit of developers who choose the land of this city for the construction of their facilities.





The city of Kotelniki is an industrial city; it operates enterprises for asphalt-concrete production, mining and processing of molding materials, and the manufacture of metal structures. There are organizations in the food, electrical and chemical industries. There are also many retailers represented by such large hypermarkets as Auchan, Stockmann, Ikea.

The last metro station of the same name enters Kotelniki. Thus, residents of the city can get to Moscow not by train or bus, but immediately by metro.





Location of the complex: how to get

LCD "Green Island" is located in Kotelniki, microdistrict South. It is at a distance of about 1.6 kilometers from the Moscow Ring Road. And a little more than 3 kilometers from the Kotelniki metro station. If you get your own car, you need to go to Mega Belaya Dacha on Novoegoryevskoye Shosse and turn right onto Zheleznodorozhnaya Street almost immediately, which will lead to the residential complex.

Also, bus number 474 goes from Kotelniki metro station to Yuzhny microdistrict. But this is not the last way to get home. By suburban train, you can get to the Yanichkino station, then walk along Zheleznodorozhnaya and turn onto Asphalt Street. The distance from the complex to the station is less than a kilometer, which can be overcome in about 20 minutes.





Description of the complex

What is the “Green Island” residential complex being built (Kotelniki)? These are several buildings of different storeys (from 12 to 17 floors), united by a common courtyard. Buildings are constructed using monolithic-brick technology using the most modern and high-quality building materials.

Each building is equipped with passenger and freight elevators, lowering residents to the underground parking, which is also provided for by the project. It will be three-story and located under the house, courtyard. As for parking spaces, for almost every owner of their own car, there is one. 410 separate places for cars will be available in the first building of the LCD “Zeleny Ostrov” parking lot. Building 2 will provide 684 parking spaces, 440 in Building 3. Parking spaces are separated from neighboring ones by a mesh fence. At each level, 4 fire compartments are equipped with elevators for transporting fire departments.





Apartments in the LCD "Green Island"

Building 3 is being built seven-section, it will have 415 apartments. His project does not provide for non-residential premises. In other buildings, a different number of apartments is also planned, and their first floors will be designed to accommodate non-residential premises of social infrastructure. The ceiling height of commercial premises is 3 meters.

The total number of apartments in the complex is expected to be 1370 of different sizes and layouts: one-room, two-room and three-room. Ceiling Height 2.76 meters.





One-room apartments provide more than 46 square meters, two-room apartments - from 65 - and three-room apartments - 93-118 square meters. The cost of one square meter of living space in the complex is just over 80 thousand rubles. Traditionally, the most expensive apartments are one-room and two-room. The cost per square meter in a three-room apartment is already much lower and amounts to approximately 64 thousand rubles. Thus, on average, the smallest apartment will cost about 3.9 million rubles. You can buy them in various ways, both in cash and when using mortgage lending provided by several banks.





Ecological situation

ZhK Zeleniy Ostrov (Kotelniki) is located in the middle between the Tomilinsky forest park, which allows residents of the complex to breathe fresh air, on the one hand, and many of the above industrial enterprises and the railway, on the other. The cement plant is located just 700 meters from the complex, which cannot but affect the ecology. But a huge green array smooths out all the negative impression. And nearby ponds with specially equipped recreation areas and beaches only confirm that the location of the complex is very good in terms of environmental conditions.

Internal infrastructure

What does the developer of the complex considered in the article offer to the services of customers? In the courtyard will be equipped with playgrounds for young residents of the complex. Adults will have the opportunity to relax in specially equipped recreation areas. For outdoor activities and sports, sports grounds are equipped. Guests of the residents of the complex arriving in their own cars will be able to park them in guest parking lots. The developer did not stop at this and included landscaping, landscaping, and also lawn sowing in the project. The whole courtyard area will be well lit in the evening by street lamps.

External infrastructure

LCD "Green Island" is located in the city of Kotelniki. Therefore, the services of its residents are not only what the developer directly offers, but also the entire nearby infrastructure of the city. In walking distance from the complex is an educational school, two kindergartens, a music school. The Pyaterochka supermarket, branches of Sberbank of Russia, and cooking are located in the complex itself. Nearby there is a city clinic, dental center, hospital. Thus, all the objects of social infrastructure that are necessary for a normal comfortable life are available.

Reviews of customers and residents about the complex

At present, not all the housing of ZhK Zeleniy Ostrov housing complex have been commissioned and settled by the developer. But those who already live here can make up their minds about their purchase of residential real estate. Negative reviews of residents relate to a greater extent to the location of the residential complex and the lack of a normal access to it. For those who own a car, to get out of the neighborhood and get to the nearest metro or straight to their work is not any there. For those who are not a happy owner of a car, there is a real inconvenience in transport accessibility. To get to the same metro station "Kotelniki", you need to travel some distance by taxi. Sometimes, especially during peak hours, this is almost impossible.

Also referred to LCD "Green Island" reviews of dissatisfied residents and regarding the condition of access roads. Upon the acquisition of apartments by the developer, he was promised the repair of existing roads and the construction of new convenient access roads. However, so far this promise has not been fulfilled.

Among the advantages, many note the relatively low cost per square meter of living space. Also a plus is the presence of a well-developed infrastructure due to the fact that the complex is located within the city limits of Kotelnikov. This is especially good for those who have small children.

Residential complex in Tver

LCD "Green Island" (Tver) is a residential complex of the same name, but only in another city. It is located almost in the center of Tver, having very convenient transport accessibility, well-developed infrastructure around.



Apartments in the complex are rented out in fine finish, which allows its customers to significantly save on repairs. And for all its merits, the price of living space per square meter is not so high.
