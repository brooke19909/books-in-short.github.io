Clown Smoking Fluid: Flavors

In their classical sense, cigarettes were replaced by electronic cigarettes or vapes. The relative cheapness and reduction of harm to health has led to an increase in the number of people who like to "steam". Liquids for electronic cigarettes attract, first of all, with their huge variety of taste and aromatic palettes.

Liquid for cigarettes CLOWN

The world famous American manufacturer Bad Drip has released a new brand of vaping liquids - Clown. The company has established itself in the market as a manufacturer of liquids with a unique unique aroma and excellent quality.

According to legend, the whole line of tastes was invented by a crazy clown who escaped from a psychiatric hospital. Clown Fluid has a vibrant packaging design that embodies the original name. The bubbles depict colorful, but crazy and even frightening clowns. You cannot confuse this packaging with any other.





Line of flavors

Mixes of liquids imitate the tastes of the most popular American sweets. It is worth trying them at least once, and you will not stop. Clown e-cigarette smoking fluid comes in five distinctive and memorable flavors:

  • Clown Splitz.
  • Clown Sweet Tooth.
  • Clown Twisty.
  • Clown Laffy.
  • Clown Pennywise.




Splitz is a combination of delicate vanilla ice cream and ripe banana. The mixture allows you to plunge into the dream: to visit the island, lie in a hammock under banana palm trees with mouth-watering ice cream in your hands.

Sweet Tooth is Clown's updated premium fluid. Electronic cigarettes with such a taste will give pleasure and tranquility. The aroma of fresh raspberries and the sweetness of cotton candy will take you to a carefree childhood, a time of sweets and entertainment.

Twisty is a vibrant mix of berries and fruits with milk yogurt. It is not clear what is more in this combination - ripped summer berries or yogurt. Taste and smell intertwine, creating a picture of sunny summer.

Laffy combines ripe sweet grapes with the taste of blueberry toffee. This kind of combination of tastes makes the mixture extremely attractive.

Pennywise is a fairly sweet liquid that combines the taste of fragrant strawberries and juicy watermelon. The notes of peach in the aftertaste give even more originality. This summer mix will blow your mood. The feeling of sunny summer is provided to you.

If you want to bring a festive mood into your monotonous everyday life, then Clown liquid is just what you need. A wide range of tastes, excellent quality and convenient packaging will undoubtedly please the buyer. If we talk about the price, then 30 ml of liquid will cost 1100-1500 rubles (depending on the city and the store).

Benefits

The main feature of this series of liquids is that it belongs to the premium class. High quality will please electronic cigarette lovers.

Key benefits of Clown fluid:

  • The naturalness of the components, which makes the use of the mixture safe.
  • A fairly wide and original range of taste and smell.
  • The ratio of glycerol to propylene glycol is 65:35. This means that the liquid can be poured into any device for soaring. Thick, saturated steam is guaranteed.




When choosing any liquid, you should always consider that only a high-quality mixture will provide a pleasant and comfortable process of vaporizing electronic cigarettes. After all, aroma and taste is the most important thing in exhaling steam.

Characterization of Clown fluids

Clown fluid is made up of natural ingredients. The composition of the mixture includes purified water, vegetable glycerin, flavorings identical to natural, and food grade propylene glycol.

The Clown line is represented by a liquid with three types of nicotine content - it is 0 mg, that is, nicotine-free, 3 and 6 mg. All liquid is packaged in glass strong bottles of 30 ml.





BAD DRIP is very concerned about the image and, of course, about its fans. The manufacturer has developed a package that is equipped with a special pipette for convenient refueling of electronic cigarettes. Such an addition is not found in every manufacturer of smoking fluids. In addition, the bottles are protected from children, although the mixture should not be left in places accessible to children. Another nice addition is a special system for controlling the first autopsy.

Before buying a liquid, make sure that you do not have contraindications to "soaring". The use of liquid for electronic cigarettes is prohibited for all minors, pregnant and lactating mothers, as well as people who have intolerance to any components.
