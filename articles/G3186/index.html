If you come to Yaroslavl, do not forget to visit the dolphinarium!

Dolphins are credited with magical features. They cause delight not only in children, many generations worship them. According to the legend of ancient times, these creatures were once pirates. They attacked the ship of Dionysus, son of Zeus, the god of fun. They chained him in chains, and he took and turned first into a bear, then into a tiger. And slipped out of the strong fetters. The pirates got scared and jumped into the depths of the sea themselves. And there they turned into handsome dolphins, so that with their existence and great ability to atone for the misconduct of a past life. They began to be called "people of the sea."

Amazing warm-blooded creatures easily come in contact with people, even while on the high seas. They are obedient and malleable if a person has established a close relationship with him and is sincerely friendly. Not surprisingly, the audience is always in line for performances with dolphins. Circus on the water - a haven for aquatic inhabitants. Dolphinarium is one of such popular entertainment venues. The first of them was created in the late thirties of the last century in the United States. And in the post-war years, a dolphinarium was opened in Crimea, in which there was no general access. Here, marine mammals were taught the art of fighting. In addition, there are dolphinariums and in the territory of ordinary industrial cities. Now we will tell you about such an institution.

We are going to Yaroslavl: Dolphinarium is the place to visit

Now let's talk about this wonderful institution. Consider its features and structure. So, Yaroslavl ... The dolphinarium of this city is considered one of the largest. It has been hosting visitors for five years now. The main show program is of great interest to both city guests and local residents. Therefore, another attraction can rightly be considered a dolphinarium in Yaroslavl. The price of a ticket ranges from four hundred and fifty to six hundred and fifty rubles. Here you can not only enjoy the performance of sea circus performers.





On the territory of the dolphinarium is a small hotel complex with comfortable rooms for vacationers. In addition, there are different options for catering. You can have a tasty and satisfying snack in a cozy cafe or in a restaurant. In the wellness center, visitors can undergo a number of special programs. Dolphinarium in Yaroslavl offers anti-stress courses. The ticket price for ten sessions is about forty-five thousand rubles. The benefits of communication and close contact with these mammals have already been confirmed by numerous studies. Dolphins relieve stress and fatigue, give a good mood.

Amazing creatures

On the territory of this dolphinarium is the "Studio of living tropical butterflies." Incredibly beautiful, vibrant exotic winged creatures are gathered from all over the world. Riding

So, you want to visit the dolphinarium (Yaroslavl), how to get there we will now tell you. If you will be getting from the station, you can take bus number 104.





It follows from the Yaroslavl Bus Station station to Dubki. It is in this village that the Dolphinarium is located. Travel time - no more than 30 minutes.
