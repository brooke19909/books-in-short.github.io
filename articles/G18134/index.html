Museum of happiness: photos and reviews of tourists

What is happiness? Each person has their own answer to this question. In Novosibirsk today, everyone can visit a very unusual and incredibly positive museum of happiness. The exhibition, dedicated to one of the most pleasant and controversial human feelings, includes amulets and positive characters from around the world, aphorisms and proverbs, as well as other happy objects.

The history of the first Museum of happiness in Russia



Spouses Irina Nekrasova and Yuri Dremin - musicians from Novosibirsk, quite famous in their city. These creative people have always considered themselves happy enough and were able to enjoy the little things. Even more, a married couple likes to share their own happiness and positive emotions with people around them. Today, Irina and Yuri admit that their museum of happiness appeared literally "by itself." The first exhibits were souvenirs given to the couple during their trips and purchased by them personally. When the collection became large enough and interesting, the idea arose to show it to the public. The official foundation date of the museum is 2009. The first years the exposition did not have a permanent residence permit and was mobile. The creators gently dubbed their collection the “Museum of Happiness in a Suitcase”. The happy collection has been exhibited many times in various cities of Russia. Since 2013, the exposition has gained permanent residence; today it is housed in a historic building recognized as an architectural monument of the early twentieth century in Novosibirsk.

Novosibirsk Museum of Happiness: photos and description





To date, the museum's collection consists of more than 500 exhibits. What is curious - the exposition is constantly updated. Everyone can bring something of their own to the Museum of Happiness in Novosibirsk. Owners of the collection rarely refuse gifts, provided that they are positive and bear joy. One of the most interesting exhibits is a stylized flag / map of the world, drawn by American artist Carroll Hiltner. Instead of the usual contours of the continents, this painting depicts the happy faces of children. In 2012-2013, the flag traveled around the world, during which representatives of 40 countries of the world signed autographs, including politicians and well-known public figures.

The Museum of Happiness in Novosibirsk boasts a large collection of figurines and images of guardian angels. There was a place in the collection for 300 lucky public transport tickets . During the tour, museum guests can see the charms and symbols of happiness of various peoples of the world. These are magical signs and items such as horseshoes and folk dolls. Many exhibits are handcrafted and unique in their kind. Museum owners also collect a collection of aphorisms, folk sayings and jokes about happiness.

Happiness is for everyone! Museum social activities



In the Novosibirsk Museum of Happiness, you can not only visit the tour. It regularly hosts meetings with interesting people, workshops on various needlework techniques and other events. Owners of a happy collection as creative people actively support a variety of cultural events and projects. From time to time, the museum hosts exhibitions and fairs of masters. Irina Nekrasova and Yuri Dremin regularly organize charity celebrations and meetings in nursing homes, boarding schools, rehabilitation centers, and health camps. The organization also conducts the so-called happiness lessons in educational institutions of Novosibirsk. The Museum of Happiness is always happy to make new friends and helpers. If you want to know more about the organization’s social activities, contact the museum’s management directly.

How to get on an excursion to the Museum of Happiness?





The Novosibirsk Museum of Happiness is a private organization. You can view the collection by appointment only. To book an excursion, call + 7-905-934-93-57. You can visit the museum as part of groups of 5 to 12 people. Children under 5 years old can watch the exposition for free. The cost of tickets for adults is 500 rubles, for children over 5 years old - 300 rubles. Discounts are provided to tour organizers for groups of 10 people or more. You can clarify the details by phone when recording.

Where is happiness in Novosibirsk?

The exact address of the museum: Novosibirsk city, 1905 goda street, house 13. The exact coordinates of this place: 55.040023; 82.895546. The nearest public transport stop is Bank (down Lenin Street). You can get to it on urban transport routes: 13, 32, 48, 53. The nearest metro station is Ploshchad Gagarina-Mikhailovskogo. You can easily find the Museum of Happiness, even if you first appeared in this area of ​​Novosibirsk. The exposition is housed in an old two-story house of pre-revolutionary construction. The museum has a souvenir shop where you can buy pieces of happiness and take them with you.

There is happiness in Moscow! Photozone Museum on Old Arbat





In order to get your dose of happiness in the museum, you do not have to go to Siberia. Not so long ago, a happy exhibition was opened in the capital of Russia. The Moscow Museum of Happiness on the Arbat is a unique space consisting of photo zones. Unique interiors have been created here with an abundance of props for photo shoots. The plots are the happiest - everyone's favorite holidays and fairy tales. Each visitor to the museum can be photographed in a Cinderella’s carriage, in a “real” tank, against the background of a car tied up with a gift ribbon, and in other interesting locations. There is also a festive table awaiting the birthday party, and New Year's zones with Santa Claus, as well as simply beautiful natural “landscapes” and interiors. The creators of the exposition recommend that visitors come at least together and do not forget the cameras. Happy Museum in Moscow is ideal for wedding and creative photo shoots. What is especially nice - all the props are made quite high quality and realistic. Visitors need only a good mood and a desire to pose in front of the lens.

Information for tourists who want to visit the Museum of Happiness in Moscow

Where is Moscow happiness located? The exact address of the unusual exhibition of photolocation: Old Arbat, 22. The Museum of Happiness in Moscow is open daily without lunch and weekends, from 11.30 to 23.30. You do not need to make an appointment to visit - tickets can be purchased at the entrance. The cost of visiting is 300 rubles. The purchase of an entrance ticket makes it possible to conduct an unlimited time photo session in the locations of the museum. All exhibits and props can be touched.

The main idea of ​​Russian museums of happiness





Have you been happy today? It is with this question that organizers of happiness museums meet their guests. The modern rhythm of life in big cities, a lot of stress and problems do not allow many of us to notice pleasant trifles. But happiness is so simple in reality. This is a delicious tea, chocolate bar, an interesting book, a purr of a cat and a sincere smile of a child. If you feel sad or just want to see something new and unusual, be sure to visit the Museum of Happiness. The activities of this organization are not reduced to the scientific study of a discipline. The Museum of Happiness collects and gives emotions to everyone. Come here for a great mood!
