Retro style photo shoot. Photo shoot styles for girls

Most likely, many girls dreamed of realizing themselves in a certain role, arranging a memorable professional photo shoot and getting the long-awaited photos. However, venturing to this step, they often ask themselves the question: "In what style to make a photo shoot?"

Qualitatively captured moments by a professional photographer will delight you throughout the rest of your life, because this is a piece of your story, and it will tell someone. Therefore, it is important to choose a good photographer who can choose the most suitable shooting style for you.

However, if you wish, you yourself can determine the role for yourself by studying the most interesting styles for a photo shoot in the studio.





Erotic style photo shoot

If you are ready to bare in front of the lens, then this option is for you. As a result, you will get extravagant and exciting images of your naked body. The degree of nakedness can be varied, as a result of which you can try yourself in any context: romantic, flirting or outright passionate.

60s photoshoots

Inspired by the images of the famous 60s, any girl will want to try on bright dresses in purple, emerald and red colors, try out rich makeup with flirty arrows and false eyelashes, make a huge comb on her head and style her hair in the style of Bridget Bardot. Photoshoots in the style of the 60s will certainly appeal to you. Feel a little rebel!





Retro style photo shoot

The memorable images of Sophia Loren, Marilyn Monroe, Marlene Dietrich are eternal standards of beauty, style and elegance - to this day they excite the minds of modern fashionable girls.

Preparing for a photo shoot

To recreate the retro style in modern conditions is not as easy as it seemed. Digital equipment can transmit many effects that were previously inaccessible to black and white photography. Therefore, professionals use black and white materials with low sensitivity when working with flashbacks.

To add color, use dull shades with the least number of midtones. Of course, to obtain high-quality photo materials, it is important to recreate the retro atmosphere. Interior items and decorative elements should be made "antique", in the style of a past era. Of course, this also applies to creating the image of the model: clothes, makeup and hairstyle must match the environment.





Image creation

A retro-style photo shoot involves painstaking work on makeup, in which the most striking emphasis is on the expressiveness of the eyes against a background of light, even pale skin, underlined by a bright blush.

The image is complemented by small and thin eyebrows and neatly outlined pencil lips. To ensure a light tone, an appropriate tonal agent is applied to the skin of the face, cheekbones are outlined with blush of a reddish or peach color.

The most difficult to perform is the outline of the eyes so that they acquire maximum size and expressiveness. The classic make-up scheme is as follows: first, eyelids are drawn with a black pencil so that the outer corner of the eye is emphasized, that is, an arrow is drawn. After that, shadows are applied over the pencil and shaded. Then the makeup is again adjusted with a pencil. In the end, the eyelashes are tinted, here it is necessary to achieve their length and volume using the appropriate mascara.





Retrograde

If a retro style photo shoot is planned, it means that you need to make a hairstyle in the form of wave-like curls with a straight or oblique parting. Length is not a problem. Such hairstyles are done on both long and short hair, and the size of the waves can vary as desired from small to large. The presence of curls gives the face an extraordinary seductiveness and flirty.

So, the retro look has already been created, then you need to decide on the mood and emotions. Drawing up an image is the main and integral part of a photo shoot, which differs from ordinary shooting in its artistic direction. Ready-made photos are able to express all the mystery of your nature, they can take their rightful place in the interior of your home or workplace.

Retro fashion does not fade away

This style is quite often chosen by modern girls for photo shoots. A retro-style photo shoot is able to reveal the full depth of the image, concentrating all attention on the mood of the model and the expression of her eyes. Through such photographs, it seems, you can feel the emotional mood and personal impressions of the master who created the pictures.





Rock and Military Styles

If you draw inspiration in the modern world, then "musical" styles and military are very popular, implying extensive freedom of action.

Masters inspired by the varieties of rock, experimenting, create ever new images of models. Here you can experience all the multifaceted feelings: a little recklessness, naturalness, relaxedness and freedom, after which, having received your photos, you will recall with delight the moments experienced in the studio. Styles of photo shoots for girls are diverse, so why not try yourself in one of the images.

Important points

If you plan to capture yourself in a military style, then it should be borne in mind that due to the specifics of the image, this role may not be suitable for everyone.

For example, if you want to pose with a weapon, then it would be nice to ask how to hold it in your hands. This fairly common mistake is very noticeable in most photos, which otherwise may turn out to be flawless.

There is nothing impossible in creating this or that style, the main thing is desire. Therefore, guided by the advice of an experienced photographer, feel complete emancipation in front of the camera and try to get used to the role as much as possible, then everything will certainly work out.

If you still doubt your abilities and the appropriateness of the whole event, then know that famous models and actors have also come a long and hard way to achieve their goals, and that they are not born actors.
