Major City - the leader of the Russian automobile business

Major City is a company that is a leader in the automotive business of Russia and the largest dealer center in Europe. The company began its work twenty years ago as an official dealer of Jeep and Chrysler automobiles. Today, Major Auto Holding is considered a branched structure from dealerships of several dozen well-known brands of European, Japanese and American production, the range of which is constantly expanding. The company annually opens new centers and strives to improve the infrastructure of existing offices.

The company is currently a dealer of forty automobile brands. In the capital of Russia, the company represents ninety branches, and seven branches have already been opened in St. Petersburg. Its employees are constantly engaged in training and improve their skills in the training center and very competently and responsibly carry out their work.
