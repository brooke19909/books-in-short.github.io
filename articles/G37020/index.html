Sberbank Platinum debit card - reviews, review, description and conditions

Today, Sberbank is the largest financial institution, which prefers to contact a large number of citizens of the Russian Federation. The bank has a large number of credit programs, the ability to open accounts and much more. The most favorable conditions are the Platinum card from Sberbank, reviews of which are mostly positive. However, before you talk about receiving it in more detail, it is worth considering the differences in some categories of plastic cash carriers.





Different types of cards

Before contacting a credit institution for the opening of plastic, you should decide in advance what type it will be. Today there are debit and credit cards. In the first case, the client simply opens an account with a financial institution, and after receiving funds in it, he can withdraw them at any ATM. If a person orders a credit card, then in this case he receives a certain amount of financial resources directly from the bank itself, which he can use and gradually repay the debt. If we talk about platinum plastic, then it happens in both categories. However, most often, bank customers draw up a debit card of Sberbank Visa Platinum. She will be very useful in many situations.

Cost of services

According to reviews, the platinum debit card from Sberbank is not issued to everyone. However, even if the client became its lucky owner, it is worth considering tariff rates.

First of all, you will need to pay 4900 rubles for card service. This cost includes not only 1 year of service, but also all subsequent use of plastic. If the card is reissued, there is no need to pay extra.

Also, many will be pleased that when withdrawing currency from ruble accounts, no additional expenses are expected. The only exception are situations when money is transferred by a third party through another territorial bank.





If you receive money through the cash desk of Sberbank itself (provided that the daily limit has not been exceeded), then the commission will not be calculated. In situations where a client of a financial institution wants to withdraw a larger amount, a commission of 0.5% of the amount that exceeded the limit is withheld. If funds must be withdrawn through another financial institution, then the conditions will be the same.

Also, in reviews of the VISA Platinum debit card of Sberbank, many draw attention to the fact that when using a bank machine of another financial institution, you need to be prepared for 1% of the total amount of money being deducted. When using cards on trading floors, no commission is provided.

Limits

According to reviews, the Visa Platinum card from Sberbank is issued with certain restrictions. In this case, you can withdraw no more than 500 thousand rubles per day and not more than 5 million per month. There are no limits on the payment of certain goods or services.

If a citizen of the Russian Federation wants to withdraw cash without using a card at the cash desk of another bank, then in this case there is a limit of 50 thousand rubles. Among the paid services, it is worth highlighting the receipt of statements for the last 10 operations performed on accounts. In this case, it will be enough to pay 15 p.





The same cost awaits those who want to receive data on the current spending limit. However, in this case, you will have to pay this commission only in the situation if an ATM of another bank was used. Subject to an unacceptable overdraft, the commission is 40% per annum.
