Hotel Location

The hotel is located in the heart of the Protaras resort . Hotel Description

The five-star hotel belongs to the large Tsokkos Hotels chain and was opened in 2000. After 12 years, the hotel underwent repairs, after which the rooms and public areas became even more comfortable. The hotel is a building with an unusual layout, in which the balconies of the rooms seem to hang over the hall, climbing plants go down. The exterior of this hotel blends perfectly with the colorful surroundings, and dark accents play a key role in the interior.

In front of the hotel there is a huge territory with a swimming pool and green spaces, smoothly turning into a beach, and this splendor ends with the charming turquoise-blue smooth surface of the Mediterranean Sea. Constantinos The Great 5 *: number of rooms

The hotel has 153 rooms, which overlook the garden, pool, beach and sea. All of them are comfortable to stay and are decorated in an elegant modern style. Each has its own balcony with a table and chairs, where you can spend breakfast or dinner.

The interior of the rooms is clearly thought out, they are furnished only with high-quality furniture, using which guests of the Constantinos The Great 5 * (Cyprus, Protaras) will receive exclusively pleasure.

  • TV and satellite channels.

The rooms are designed for two people. In addition, another berth for a child (crib) or adult can be installed.

Conditions for children

Constantinos The Great 5 * Hotel (Cyprus) opens its doors to families with children and invites young guests to spend time as fun and eventful as their parents. There is a children's playground in the fresh air, and in bad weather, kids can create in a specially equipped room, where there is everything for drawing and other activities. In the fresh air - a separate pool with a mushroom-waterfall. Animators with an interesting program entertain children day and night.

In the restaurant you can ask for a special menu for the child, and in the room there is the possibility of installing a cot. If necessary, parents can use the services of a nanny - the hotel staff speaks several languages, including Russian.
