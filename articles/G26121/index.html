Flora and fauna

A large number of conifers contribute to clean air. From almost all sides, the sandy strip of the lake is surrounded by coniferous forests. Most of them are pines. Almost entirely covered with pine trees and the island of Barsuchiy. Due to the fact that Lake Otradnoye is surrounded by mixed and coniferous forests that grow along the entire perimeter of the coastline, foxes and deer, wolves and wild boars, moose and bears, ferrets and hares live here.

The local world is also rich in feathered representatives. Ornithologists count almost 280 of their species. In the swamps, wild geese and ducks are noticed. Capercaillie, hazel grouse and black grouse are often found. The flora of these places is vast and diverse - at least thirty species grow only medicinal plants. The forests surrounding the lake are rich in mushroom and berry places. Some of the species of local vegetation are listed in the Red Book.



Otradnoye Lake: fishing

In Otradnoye a huge number of fish species: from roach and perch to zander, pike and trout. Fans of winter fishing are waiting for whitefish, ruff, burbot and perch. In the spring, roach and scavenger are most caught.

Fishing lovers can easily reach the lake. If you walk on foot, then from the station "Sukhodolye" to the reservoir 2 kilometers. There are also country roads that can be reached by car. Near the lake runs Priozerskoe highway. In order to get to its coastline, you need to turn right at the Gromovo station and move to the village of Yablonovka. Also, the path can be laid through the station "Otradnoe".
