Caring for a washing machine: the main rules for using appliances

In modern life, a washing machine is by no means the last. This is due to the fact that every day there is a need for washing clothes, especially if there is a small child in the house. It is important to properly care for the automatic washing machine. It depends on how long the device lasts. If everything is done correctly, then the equipment will not fail too quickly.

Terms of use

Before you start using the washing machine, you need to read the instructions for it. You should pay attention to all the recommendations and useful information. Below we look at the nuances of caring for a washing machine, which is installed separately or in the headset in the kitchen.

Many housewives are wondering how to properly care for the equipment. However, you should first understand the installation and connection of the device.

Installation and Connection Rules

The device must be placed away from heating appliances and any flammable objects. Also, the machine should not stand where the ambient temperature drops below zero degrees. The floor must be firm and even, without any pits and indentations. If you do not follow this rule, the washing machine will vibrate, make noise. This leads to quick wear of the device. The unit should be installed in a place where little dust accumulates. In addition, the room should be dry. The machine should not be affected by direct sunlight and water. From moisture, the device quickly fails.

Rules for using the device

The durability of a household appliance item primarily depends on the attitude of its owner. Here are the basic rules that you must follow in caring for a washing machine:

  • If the water hose suddenly jumps off, turn off the power immediately.
  • Wash bedding, carpets and other such things is prohibited. After all, the materials from which they are created are not intended for processing in a washing machine.
  • If the washing machine is turned off, you need to close the tap.
  • When washing things that are stained with cream, lotion or oil, the rubber seal is deformed.
  • In the care of the washing machine, you can also include a thorough inspection of things. Her clothes should be checked so that there are no hard objects in her pockets.
  • If the water is turned off, then, preparing for the next wash, you should open the faucet as carefully as possible.
  • Laundry should be laid so that the door does not pinch it.
  • If there is dirt on the drum or the inner walls, you need to disconnect the machine from the power supply and wipe the problem areas with a damp cloth. Otherwise, rubber and plastic may lose color, corrosion will appear.
  • The drain pump filter should be cleaned periodically.

In addition, water needs to be softened. To do this, use supplements in the form of "Kalgon" or any other. Due to hard water, the water heater may break.

Nuances

How to properly care for a washing machine? Rinse it with special tools will not be enough. From how the consumer cleans the drum and maintains cleanliness in it, the operational life is completely dependent. After each cycle, disconnect the device from the power supply. Before cleaning and maintenance, too. Children should be prohibited from approaching the technique. If they want to try to clean the machine, then they must be watched for.

How to clean the filter?

Caring for the Indesit or Atlant automatic washing machine, it is imperative to monitor the condition of the filter. It must be opened. To do this, use a key or a coin. Next, the cap is removed. In this case, the water should drain gently. If there is garbage in the filter, then you need to delete it. After that, wash the part with soapy water. Next, you need to install the cover back into place.

External surface cleaning

It doesn‚Äôt matter if the Atlant automatic washing machine or another company is maintained, in any case, the front, top and side panels should be washed regularly. This is done with a soft cloth. Detergents should be household, non-abrasive. After you need to wipe the surface dry. Water must not enter the control unit.

Washing the powder compartment

Using a special lever, you need to remove the device. Then make a soap solution and rinse the compartment with it. Using a toothbrush, it is necessary to process niches. This is how you need to take care of the washing machine (LG, Samsung, etc.). Next, install the compartment back.

Strainer Care

The following procedure is carried out once a year. Turn off the water supply and disconnect the hose. To prevent water from escaping, the tap must be closed with a rag. There is a strainer at the end of the hose. It should be pulled out and washed until completely cleaned. After this, the hose should be reattached.

Rubber cuff wash

It is necessary to bend the edges of the gum and wipe its inner area with a sponge. The item must be rinsed in white or a chlorine-based product. Next, wipe the outer part. After that, you need to start the rinse.

Drum care

Many housewives use citric acid to avoid excessive formation of plaque on the drum and other parts of the device. Caring for a washing machine with this tool is very simple. It will be enough to take 200 g of the substance and put it in the drum. Next, start the wash at 60 degrees.

Additional points

If during washing clothes alcohol, kerosene, benzene or any other explosives got into the washing machine, then the equipment is unlikely to work for a long time. This leads to the fact that a short circuit, fire may occur. In addition, cleaning products with strong acids are also prohibited.

It is important when caring for a machine not only to clean it regularly, but also to follow all the rules of use. In addition, the ‚Äúwashing machine‚Äù must be constantly descaled. Thanks to this approach, the life of the heating element will increase.

Water quality

The quality of the water completely affects the operation of the washing machine. If it is tough, scum will appear. Such water has a bad effect on the state of the heating element. If urgent measures are not taken, then it will break down quickly enough. In order to get rid of hard water, you need to use some means.

Popular remedies are various manufacturing additives. However, some of them are not cheap. If you consider that you need to use them constantly, then it is better to use simpler methods. One such remedy is citric acid. She can clean not only the drum, but also to descale. Pour it into the dispenser and start washing at maximum temperature.

There are also additional softeners. These filters can be bought at specialized stores. They are expensive, but they purify the water not only for the washing machine, but also for the entire apartment.

Additional tips

So that there is no unpleasant odor from the machine, you should put dirty laundry in it only immediately before washing. Putting it there on an ongoing basis is not recommended. To maximize the quality of washing, you need to separate colored items from white. Wash them separately separately. Use only special powders. There are many of these in stores. If the owner leaves for a long time, then the washing machine must be de-energized and shut off the water. So you can avoid unpleasant situations until a person is at home.
