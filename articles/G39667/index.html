Cinnamon Dhonveli Maldives 4 * (Huraa, Maldives): photos and reviews of tourists

Maldives are fabulous islands that are visited by thousands of tourists every year. This is an incredibly beautiful place where you can live almost on the water, while enjoying the privacy and high quality service.

One of the very popular places for spending holidays is considered the hotel complex Cinnamon Dhonveli Maldives 4 *. Naturally, when planning a vacation, each person tries to get the maximum of really reliable information about the chosen stopping place. So where is the hotel located and is it easy to get to? Is it possible to count on a fun and active holiday on the beach? What level of nutrition should I expect? What kind of reviews do tourists leave about this place?

Hotel location features





Of course, an important point is the location of the hotel, especially when it comes to islands. Where is the hotel Cinnamon Dhonveli Maldives 4 * located? Male, namely the northern part of the atoll, the island of Donveli - here, in the middle of a palm grove and the vast ocean, is the hotel complex.

It is worth saying right away that this island is quite small, and its entire territory is occupied by the hotel. The nearest international airport is Male, 13 km from the property. Naturally, tourists are provided with a comfortable transfer, namely a boat - in 20 minutes you can easily reach the hotel territory by water.

What is the territory of the hotel Cinnamon Dhonveli Maldives 4 *? Description





As already mentioned, the hotel occupies a secluded island. Its area is small, but everything is perfectly furnished - well-thought-out infrastructure, lush gardens and excellent design delight the eyes of any, even the most demanding tourist.

Hotel Cinnamon Dhonveli Maldives 4 * consists of small bungalows. Some of them are lost in the middle of a tropical garden, while others are located in a semicircle in the water, on special stilts. On the island itself there are pools, playgrounds for sports and recreation, in short, everything you need for a pleasant and comfortable pastime.

Regardless of where exactly your room will be located, you can fully count on comfort and coziness. All rooms are spacious with large windows and bright carpets on the floor. By the way, they are arranged in an interesting island style that many travelers like.

Of course, the room has all the amenities, including household appliances. Guests can watch TV, use the electronic safe and telephone. By the way, room service is available round the clock, which is convenient. The air conditioning system is modern, with a simple and clear control system. A refrigerated minibar is available, but it is filled only on request, and delivered drinks are not included in the price.

The bathroom is equipped with working modern plumbing. Depending on the category of the room, you can use a shower, bathtub and even a jacuzzi. In some rooms, an additional shower and toilet are located in the courtyard, in the open air.

They come to the rooms to clean daily. During cleaning, maids also change towels, replenish stocks of shampoos, soaps and other hygiene items.

Meals for guests





What kind of food can a hotel guest expect at Cinnamon Dhonveli Maldives 4 *? The Maldives are still islands, which means that in most cases they have to eat on the territory of their hotel, as in this case. Naturally, boats and other water transport run between the islands around the clock, but it is not always convenient to make a trip to visit a restaurant.

That is why for tourists planning to spend their holidays on a secluded small island, food is a very important point. Hotel Cinnamon Dhonveli Maldives 4 * provides its guests with full three meals a day. You can eat at the main restaurant - here guests organize a buffet. Reviews say that the dishes here are very tasty, and there are always several varieties of meat and fish, seafood and, of course, fresh exotic vegetables on the menu. In the evenings, themed parties are organized for guests where you can treat yourself to traditional food from different countries.

Restaurants for hotel guests

On the territory of the hotel complex Cinnamon Dhonveli Maldives 4 * there are several catering establishments. Some of them can be visited for free, while in others you need to pay each bill.





For example, you can have a great time at Raalhu Restaurant, which serves delicious international cuisine. At least once you should pay a visit to Rehendhi - there is a rich selection of traditional Maldivian food. Also, guests love to spend time at Maakana Cafe and Raiyvilaa Bar - here they will offer you main dishes, light snacks, drinks (including alcoholic ones), as well as coffee, tea and delicious desserts.

Beach facilities and water activities





Since the hotel is located on a small island, the beach, of course, is here private, private. Near the bungalow there is a kind of "wild" sections of the coast where you can swim, but employees do not recommend doing this, because there are no lifeguards nearby. There is also a descent into the water from the water bungalows, but only experienced people bathe here, since the current is too strong.

There is a fully equipped, spacious beach, where guests are invited. The entrance to the sea is cleared, a rescue team is constantly working, and the shore is cleaned several times a day. Guests can use sun loungers and umbrellas for free, there are even special water loungers with which you can relax on the water. By the way, on the shore you can get a fresh towel at any time.

Of course, one of the most striking entertainments of travelers is scuba diving. Even without diving, swimming with a mask and fins, you can admire the bright tropical fish. Unfortunately, the local reef was recently destroyed by the tsunami - nature here is only beginning to recover. Nevertheless, the diving school offers excursions with immersion in the most interesting areas. Here you can get training and get a diver certificate. Of course, you can ride on water transport, engage in windsurfing and sailing, play volleyball on the beach, in a word, have fun.
