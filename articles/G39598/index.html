Trust the leader, or how to open an account with Sberbank

Tell me how to open an account with Sberbank? This is one of the most popular issues that occupy a considerable number of Russian citizens. The fact is that the level of trust in this financial institution remains traditionally high. Even despite the fact that the percentage on deposits, as well as the speed of service, sometimes leave much to be desired.



In addition, do not forget that the branch network of the institution is represented by more than 20 thousand branches, as well as ATMs, the number of which is about 30 thousand. Accordingly, finding a bank representative, as well as a place where you can withdraw money, is not a problem at all.

It should be noted that people who are interested in how to open an account with Sberbank increasingly prefer not the usual for many passbooks, but much more convenient plastic cards. By the way, the leadership of this financial organization some time ago raised the issue of the abolition of customer savings books . But so far this question remains unresolved.

So, how to open an account with Sberbank by establishing a plastic card? There are two options. The first (and fastest) is the execution of the prepaid Momentum. His client receives on the day of contacting one of the branches. The advantages of this product include the speed of opening, the absence of the need to pay for services, and gaining access to various banking services. But, unfortunately, there are also disadvantages. Firstly, the daily limit on settlements is 100 thousand rubles. Secondly, you can use the card only in the Russian Federation. Thirdly, withdrawal and replenishment operations can only be carried out at ATMs of a given financial institution. Although given their number, it is not so difficult to do. But, as you know, the situations are different.

The second option is the opening of a map of international standard. Here the "plastic" will have to wait a bit. In addition, you will have to pay for annual maintenance. As in the first case, the client gets access to many interesting banking services.
