Hotel Arthurs: address. How to get there

Where is this place located? Hotel "Arthurs" (Moscow region) is located twenty kilometers from the Moscow Ring Road, in the Mytishchi district, in the village of Larevo, ownership 3, building 1.

To get to it by car, you should turn from the Moscow Ring Road towards Dmitrovskoye Shosse and then go twenty-two kilometers to the sign "Larevo Village". After driving straight for 300 m, turn right - and after one kilometer you are at your destination.

If you plan to travel by public transport, you should catch an electric train at Savelovsky station in the directions of Dubna, Iksha, Savelovo, Dmitrov and get off at Katuar station. From the station you can call a taxi to the hotel.
