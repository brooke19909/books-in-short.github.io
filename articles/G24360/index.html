How to make a pompom from a napkin? Step by step instructions, interesting ideas

Fluffy pompons are easy to make from paper napkins. They can decorate the festive table, laying on each plate or decorating cutlery, decorate the room for a children's holiday. Now often use paper pompons for the design of stores. In the article we will tell you in detail how to make a pompom with different edges from a napkin, give detailed instructions on the manufacture of such elements of room decoration.

Necessary materials

First of all, you need to think about the size and color of future pompons. If you need to make a large number of them, you need to understand that they must be harmoniously inscribed in the interior of the room so that the decorations do not look clumsy. It is better to take gentle and pastel colors. If you decorate a group room of a kindergarten or a school class with children for the New Year, then you can take winter shades - blue, white, pale blue.





Napkins can be taken in different sizes, then pompons will turn out different not only in color but also in size. To hang them somewhere, you need to prepare a wire or a thin, durable cotton thread, matched to the color of the paper. Before you make a pompom from a napkin, you need to prepare a ruler and sharp scissors, if you have curly scissors, then you can use them.

Step-by-step instruction

How to make pompoms from napkins? This is not difficult. You can try to work with children. First you need to unfold the napkin. It is advisable to make several layers, especially if you want to create a magnificent pompom. If you stack a pack of multi-colored napkins, then the finished product will be multi-colored. Then you need to collapse it with an accordion. The strips should be the same.





Then you need to determine the center of the accordion and tightly tie the middle with wire or a dense thread. Then the edges are processed. Next, we will tell you how to make a pompom with different ends from a napkin.

Edging

There are many options for the edges of the pompom. They can be cut into "noodles", then the pompom will look like a chrysanthemum. If you round off the edges with curly scissors or just cut off the harmonically folded edges in a semicircle, then there will be a pompom with round ‚Äúpetals‚Äù. You can offer another option, how to make pompoms from paper napkins.





To do this, it is necessary to cut the sharp layers of paper firmly pressed together with an accordion and cut into sharp corners. Then the product will be with pointed "petals", as in the first photo in our article. It must also be remembered that the more layers the folded accordion has, the more magnificent the pompom will be.

Next stage

Next, we will tell you how to make pompoms from napkins with your own hands. After the edges of the folded napkins are cut and the middle is firmly connected, you can begin to unfold the layers. You need to do this alternately, very gently and carefully.





Fingers need to stroke each "petal". Since the fold line of the paper runs along the center point, you need to distribute the layers so that voids in the middle of the pompom are not visible. They tie the pompom to the central part on a long thin thread or silver New Year's rain and hang it on the ceiling or on the room's lights. They should not be suspended too close to electrical appliances, do not forget about safety measures. After all, paper napkins are flammable material. And lamps with prolonged use heat up and can even create "wind" and rocking from side to side for light pompons.

Room decoration options

It‚Äôs not enough to know how to make a pompom out of a napkin; you also need to be able to arrange them beautifully. Often, when decorating rooms, garlands of pompons of various sizes are used, in decreasing or, conversely, increasing. First, the largest ball is tied to a thread, backing off a few centimeters so that they do not touch, a smaller ball is tied to the center point and at the end is the smallest pompom. Colors also vary, alternating light and brighter shades.





You can tie the balls at different heights into a bunch on beautiful silver ribbons, which are tied with a lush bow at the top. Another option that is more suitable for rectangular rooms or school classes is, after pulling the fishing line from corner to corner, hang the pompons at different levels on the diagonal of the room.

The choice is always yours. After reading the article, you can make pompoms from paper napkins in many ways. You can also use corrugated tissue paper for large balls from the stationery store.
