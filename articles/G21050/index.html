Sheraton Rhodes Resort 5 * (Rhodes, Greece): hotel description and reviews of tourists

The Greek Sheraton Rhodes Resort 5 * hotel , which belongs to the Sheraton Hotels & Resorts hotel line, is a resort complex for a high-class holiday. It is located in an area called Ixia, on the Aegean Sea. If you want to feel at home and at the same time plunge into the luxury of a warm Greek island, then this hotel will surely appeal to you. Moreover, he collected a sufficient number of good responses from guests who visited there. Set amidst a beautiful landscape park, literally a few tens of meters from the famous beaches of Ixia, this hotel is perfect for lovers of traveling around the Mediterranean, families with children, lovers and honeymooners. This is a good opportunity to experience Greece, in which there really is "everything."



Near the sea ...

Sheraton Rhodes Resort 5 * is on the first line. It consists of a white multi-level building with elevators. It is perfectly inscribed in the relief of the mountain. From the center of the famous Rhodes, famous for medieval architecture and the fortresses of European knights, it is only four kilometers away. Charter and regular flights from Moscow and (in season) St. Petersburg, as well as from the mainland, arrive at the island's international airport. From there, as a rule, a shuttle service delivers travelers to the hotel, especially since traveling from Diagoras is about fifteen kilometers. Since the island is very close to Bodrum, this picturesque Turkish resort is often visible from the hotel rooms and from the veranda of the main restaurant.



Infrastructure

Since Sheraton Rhodes Resort 5 * is a whole complex, as its name speaks of, it has a large and beautiful garden, a terrace and a mini market so that guests have a place to walk, relax and buy souvenirs and beach items. Guests can park their car here if they come with their own transport. The hotel even has a ballroom and dance hall. For those who organize various seminars and conferences at this hotel, they offer fourteen halls of various capacities, as well as a business center. An on-site ATM machine and a tourist office are located on site, where you can also get information about the hotel, its services and nearby attractions. Tourists call the hotel grounds magnificent. Everything is carefully looked after, everywhere is clean, there is nothing unpainted, shabby or shabby. A lot of flowers, rare plants and trees. The hotel lobby is furnished in Art Nouveau style, there is a piano and many nooks to sit.

Facilities at Sheraton Rhodes

The lobby and reception are open 24 hours a day. Day and night the hotel is guarded by a special service. Porters help carry things. You can also store things at check-in. Wash and clean clothes, get a haircut and do styling and manicure, call a doctor if necessary, send a fax, make photocopies of documents, change money - all this is available at the hotel. By the way, the car rental office also works here, because which Greek islands do without travel by car! As in many resort hotels of this chain, the hotel has a fitness center, as well as massage therapists, you can take a steam bath in the sauna and improve your health with various thalassotherapy procedures. Greek cuisine in the Rhodes "five"

The Sheraton Hotel in Ixia is famous for its gastronomic diversity. It operates on a "boarding house" system, although only breakfasts can be borrowed here. Therefore, drinks for lunch and dinner must be paid. The main restaurant "Castelliania" has a veranda, so guests can have a meal in the air, enjoying the breeze and looking at the sea. Here they only have breakfast and dinner. The sunsets are especially beautiful in this restaurant, but then you need to come to the evening meal early, otherwise all the tables in the part where they are better seen will be occupied. They serve mainly Greek dishes, and there are also special offers for vegetarians.



And the restaurant, open only at lunchtime - Mediterranean - is located in the garden, near the main pool. Here you can savor various Mediterranean goodies. You can also have dinner in the Italian restaurant Llonda. Two bars with drinks, cocktails and snacks - one at the lower pool, and the second in the lobby - open until one in the morning. The food is extremely tasty, great choice, all the freshest. For breakfast, meat and fish cuts are served, among other things, for dinner there are many types of meat, including lamb. A lot of different sweets, ice cream and fruits. Meals will be long, but you will not regret even once.

Pool, sea and other pleasures of life

The beach at Sheraton Rhodes Resort is private and is right behind the garden. Aliens are not allowed here. There are several pools - three in the open air, and one under the roof. They are at different levels, and in one there is a jacuzzi. Sun loungers by the pool should not be occupied with towels. The administration periodically removes them. Most of the guests, as tourists write, mainly accumulate in the pools. At sea, they are much smaller, so there are enough sunbeds for everyone.
