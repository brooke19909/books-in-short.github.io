Excursions

It is important to note that Club Palm Bay 4 * is also involved in organizing excursions. Sri Lanka, northwestern side., Marawila - a place full of different animals. This was already discussed at the beginning. And one of the most popular excursions is sending to a bird sanctuary, located 45 minutes from the complex. It was declared the first wetland in 1991 and, moreover, a Ramsar landmark. 264 species of plants and almost the same number of terrestrial fauna and birds.

Many more like trips to Kalpitiya, because there you can watch spotted dolphins, bottlenose dolphins and huge whales. If you want to look at these creations, it is better to buy tours from November to April on the island of Sri Lanka. Prices are lower, and the likelihood of seeing more marine animals is higher.

Half an hour from the hotel there is a picturesque beach with reefs, where all lovers of diving and snorkeling go - after all, there is a rich underwater world with tropical fish and other creatures that can be seen closer.

Excursions to Wilpattu National Park, founded in 1938, are also organized. It is simply huge, and Asian elephants, gubachi bears, buffaloes, and also leopards, ibises, pelicans and many other animals live on its territory.

In general, it is not surprising why tours to Sri Lanka, the prices of which are quite high, are bought up at the moment. After all, there really is something to do and what to see!

What you should know

Check-in at 12:00. Guests are evicted from morning until 11:00. In the hotel you can live with children of any age. If guests come with a child whose age is less than 2 years, then for his stay you will not have to pay. But he will have to sleep on the beds in the room. However, you can inform in advance, even when filling out the application, that the trip is planned with the baby, and in this case, a crib will be installed in the apartment.

If the child is older, then for his stay will have to pay (50% of the cost per person). But he must be younger than 12. If the child is 13, then he is already considered an adult guest, and the full price is paid for him.

Need extra beds? It is necessary to make a request to the hotel. In advance, before departure. Administration must send confirmation.
