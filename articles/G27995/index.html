Hotel Solemar Hotel 3 * (Rhodes, Greece): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Greece is considered one of the most sought after tourist countries. Travelers from all over the world are attracted not only by its ancient culture, but also by a quality beach holiday. Low cost of living is also a decisive factor when choosing a place to stay. Wanting to save money, tourists pay attention to the Solemar Beach Hotel 3 * hotel (Rhodes). What can he offer his guests?

Hotel location and nearby attractions

A multifaceted vacation can offer tourists Greece (Rhodes). Solemar Hotel 3 * is located on the most popular island of the country. There are ancient monuments, and medieval cities with narrow streets, and snow-white beaches, and well-developed infrastructure. A bus stop is 100 meters from the hotel.

Numerous shops, souvenir shops and restaurants can be found nearby. You can get to the complex from Diagoras Airport, which is 12 km from the village. Travel time will be no more than half an hour. Arriving at the Solemar Beach Hotel 3 * hotel, you should definitely visit the following attractions of the island:

  • Acropolis of Ialysos (3 km);
  • Rodini Park (4 km);
  • Acropolis of Rhodes (4.5 km);
  • Temple of Apollo (4.7 km);
  • the old part of the city of Rhodes (5.5 km);
  • Palace of the Grand Masters (6 km);
  • water park of Rhodes (6.5 km).

Brief information about the hotel

The hotel was built in 1970 during the mass development of the resort village of Ixia. On its territory is a lush garden consisting of fruit and olive trees, flowering shrubs. In total, the complex has 102 rooms. All of them are located in the five-story main building, which is built in a Mediterranean style. This place is well suited for couples and older people who prefer a quiet vacation. Hotel Solemar Hotel (Ixia) 3 * Standard (Rhodes) allows children with any age. Pets are strictly prohibited.





The complex is considered an inexpensive place to relax. The minimum price for a double standard room is about 1500 rubles per night. It will cost a little more in July and August, which are considered the peak of the tourist season on the island. The most expensive apartments will cost tourists 5,000 rubles. It is worth remembering that check-in to the rooms starts here at 13:00. You will have to leave early in the morning before noon. The hotel does not have Russian-speaking staff, so it is recommended that you familiarize yourself with the basic rules of the English language before traveling, or at least take a phrase book with you.

Housing stock

Solemar Hotel 3 * (Rhodes) is considered a small hotel, which has 102 rooms. They are designed for small families of two to three people. The last repair in them was made in 2007. The rooms do not differ in exquisite style, but they have everything you need for a comfortable stay. Each room has its own small balcony, on which there is a table and several chairs. It offers views of the Aegean Sea or the mountains strewn with lush vegetation. The floor is tiled. All apartments are cleaned daily. The only exception is Sunday.





Tourists can choose one of the following rooms:

What facilities are there in the rooms?

Numerous amenities in the rooms are an indicator of the high quality of service. All tourists who settled in the Solemar Hotel 3 * complex can use the following equipment:

  • Individual air conditioning, through which the rooms maintain a comfortable temperature for sleeping. You can use it from June to September.
  • TV old model. Satellite channels are connected to it, among which there are several Russians.
  • Radio. It broadcasts only Greek radio stations, so it is only suitable for listening to music.
  • A small refrigerator where you can store food and drinks.
  • Wardrobe and dressing room.
  • Clothes dryer and ironing facilities.
  • Bathroom amenities and hairdryer.
  • Direct telephone. With it, you can contact the hotel, order room service. For a fee, employees can call you in the morning and wake you up.
  • Internet is wireless. It is paid separately.
  • Change of linen and towels regularly, 3 times a week.




Local infrastructure

The service provided by the hotel also greatly affects the rest. Solemar Hotel 3 * has a fairly developed network of infrastructure facilities, which includes both paid and free services. We list the main ones:

  • Quick check-in or check-out service. It is suitable for those who do not like to wait long for their settlement, standing in queues.
  • Currency exchange point. At any time, tourists can exchange rubles for euros at the most favorable rate.
  • Public parking located next to the hotel. Here, tourists can park their car for free or rent a local car.
  • Wi-Fi is distributed throughout the hotel. You can connect it at the reception. The cost of the service is 3 euros per day.
  • Fax and photocopy located at the reception. For a fee, everyone can use them.
  • Safe box for storing important documents, jewelry and money.




How to entertain yourself in the complex?

A good rest is not only comfortable rooms and quality service, but also the entertainment that the complex offers its guests. Tourists staying at Solemar Hotel 3 * (Rhodes) leave positive reviews. In them, they note the good organization of leisure. For its guests, the hotel can offer the following types of entertainment:

  • An outdoor pool that only works in fine weather. Tourists can take free sun loungers and sun umbrellas and sunbathe on the terrace.
  • Water entertainment center on the city beach. Here you can rent windsurfing equipment, ride motor boats, catamarans or canoes.
  • Playground for playing beach volleyball.
  • Internet cafe. For an additional fee, guests can use the high-speed connection, play computer games.
  • Shared lounge area where you can watch TV.
  • Paid room for playing billiards and darts.
  • Bicycles for rent.
  • Aerobics and water gymnastics classes are regularly held on the pool terrace for everyone.
  • Equipment for karaoke. The hotel also organizes singing contests.
  • Table tennis.




Food system, restaurants and bars in the complex

Tourists staying at the Solemar Hotel 3 * hotel are offered all-inclusive meals. It includes breakfast, lunch and dinner, as well as desserts and light snacks. During meals, concentrated juices, mineral water, hot and cold drinks are served. Alcohol lovers will appreciate free draft beer, Greek wines and other local spirits. European cuisine is served for breakfast: scrambled eggs, scrambled eggs, sandwiches, granola, cereals, fruits, sliced ‚Äã‚Äãsausages and cheese. For lunch and dinner, tourists are invited to try Greek and Mediterranean cuisine, fish and seafood dishes. The all-inclusive system is open until 23:30. After this time, all drinks and food are paid separately.





The following catering establishments work for hotel guests:

  • Central restaurant. All major meals are organized here. A shared buffet is available for visitors. It accommodates about 200 people.
  • Lobby bar. Tourists can have a meal by ordering hot drinks, sweets, light snacks, ice cream, as well as wines, beer, cocktails, juices and fruits.
  • Snack bar. It serves fresh pastries, tea, coffee, milk, fruits and desserts.

Conditions for children

Solemar Hotel 3 * is suitable for a relaxing holiday with young children. They are given a substantial discount on accommodation in the complex. A cot can be delivered to the room for a fee. If parents need to leave, then they can call a paid nanny who will sit with the child. She works around the clock. For convenience, the central restaurant is equipped with high chairs for feeding.

The hotel offers children a standard entertainment experience. They have a shallow freshwater pool. A mini club has been organized for children over four years old. Experienced animators conduct educational lessons and fun games. You can relax outdoors by sending your child to the playground equipped with modern carousels.

Guests staying at Solemar Hotel 3 * leave different reviews. They appreciated the following advantages of the hotel:

  • Tasteless beer. It seems to be diluted with water.
  • A small shower that is not suitable for full people. The drain is completely clogged, so the water leaves for a very long time and spills onto the floor.
  • To go far to the beach, you have to get around the road and several local cafes.
  • Very noisy if the windows overlook the road.
  • I come across old towels with sewn holes.
  • There are very few fruits on the menu. For lunch and dinner, they give some apples, oranges, and sometimes a few slices of watermelon.

Is it worth it to relax in this place?

After reviewing the description and reviews of this hotel, we can say that it is suitable for a budget holiday. Families with young children, elderly people and tourists who are looking for a place for a relaxing vacation should come here. For youth companies, by contrast, this place may seem too boring.
