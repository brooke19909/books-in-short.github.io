Atlanta Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Alanya) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

As you know, Alanya has long attracted fans to spend summer vacations on the coast of Turkey. This is not surprising, because the beautiful nature and warm clear sea here are perfectly combined with hotels of different stars and a variety of entertainment. Today we offer a closer look at one of the hotels in Alanya - the four-star Atlanta Hotel. We will find out that this hotel complex offers its guests how much the cost of living in its walls will cost, as well as find out what impression our compatriots left after relaxing in this hotel.





Atlanta Hotel (Turkey): where is it located?

The hotel is very comfortable on the beach. It is located on the first coastline and has its own beach. The hotel complex is located just fourteen kilometers from Alanya. The nearest village - the village of Kargicak - is just 300 meters away. Also nearby is one of the most popular settlements in Alanya. So, the distance - the village of Mahmutlar (Mahmutlar) - Atlanta Hotel is only five kilometers. In order to get to the hotel from Antalya Airport, you have to overcome 136 kilometers. Thus, the journey from the air harbor will take you about two hours.

Within walking distance from Atlanta Hotel 4 * (Turkey), there is a large shopping center and a picturesque forest. The nearest hospital is thirteen kilometers away. The police station can be reached in just five minutes by car. An important factor is that no construction work is being carried out near the hotel, so the rest will be calm and peaceful.





What is the Atlanta Hotel (Antalya)?

Atlanta Hotel opened its doors to guests for the first time in 1990. In 2002, the hotel complex was completely renovated. The hotel itself consists of one six-story concrete building, built in the Art Nouveau style. It is equipped with an elevator and includes 70 standard rooms.

The staff at Atlanta Hotel (Alanya) speaks both English and Russian. Therefore, you will not have any problems in communication. We accept both cash (US dollars and Euros) and plastic cards of the Visa and Mastercard systems . According to the rules of the hotel, for an additional fee, guests traveling with their pets are allowed in its territory. Atlanta Hotel accepts tourists from April to November.

In general, the hotel positions itself as a comfortable and cozy place for a relaxing family vacation and is designed for middle-aged and older guests.

Number of rooms

Atlanta Hotel 4 * offers its guests to stay in one of 70 rooms, ten of which are designed for non-smoking guests. All rooms are standard and have sea views. Rooms are 25 square meters. There is a bathroom, hairdryer, individual air conditioning, telephone, TV, radio and balcony. You can also use the minibar and safe for a fee. On the floor - carpet.

Rooms are cleaned on a daily basis. Bed linen is changed once every two days.





Nutrition

Meals at Atlanta Hotel are organized on an all-inclusive basis. The main restaurant serves a buffet breakfast with Turkish and international dishes, as well as a variety of fruits and desserts for breakfast, lunch and dinner. The main restaurant is divided into two sections: closed (capacity 120 people) and open (capacity 140 people). Throughout the day, guests can enjoy snacks, pastries and tea or coffee. All Turkish-made drinks (both non-alcoholic and alcoholic) are provided to guests free of charge until eleven o'clock in the evening. You can order imported drinks at the bar, but you will have to pay for them separately.

Entertainment

Atlanta Hotel has a spacious (100 square meters) outdoor saltwater pool. There is also a children's pool with a slide. The pool has a sun terrace with sun loungers and parasols.

Fans of outdoor activities on site can do fitness, play mini-golf, tennis and table tennis, volleyball. There is also a sauna. There is a diving center on the beach. In the evenings, the hotel hosts a disco.

Sea beach

Atlanta Hotel is located on the beachfront and has its own private beach. Its length is one hundred meters. The beach is sandy, but there are stones when entering the water, so care must be taken not to damage the legs. The beach is equipped with showers and changing cabins. Hotel guests can use sun loungers, mattresses and umbrellas for free, but you will have to pay extra for a beach towel .





Infrastructure

Hotel "Atlanta" in Alanya has everything you need for an excellent vacation and a comfortable stay infrastructure. So, the hotel reception is open around the clock. Therefore, if you plan to check in at an inopportune time, or if you have any problem that needs to be addressed immediately, you can always contact the duty administrator. Here you can call a taxi in the direction of Mahmutlar - Atlanta Hotel.

Also at the hotel you can take advantage of laundry and dry cleaning services, call a doctor if necessary, and rent a car. At the reception you can leave valuables in the safe (this service is paid). On-site parking is available, the use of which is free for guests. Also in public areas of the hotel you can use free Wi-Fi.

Cost of living

Rest at this hotel can be classified as budget. So, a seven-day vacation for two in Atlanta 4 * with a flight from Moscow will cost about $ 800.

Atlanta Hotel reviews

When choosing a hotel in a particular country, a lot of people are guided by the reviews of vacationers who have already been here. In this regard, we decided to bring to your attention the generalized comments of our compatriots who stayed during their trip to Turkey at the Atlanta Hotel (Alanya).

In general, most tourists rated Atlanta Hotel 4 * (Turkey) as an acceptable place to spend a relaxing and peaceful vacation on the beach. The location of the hotel made a considerable impression on vacationers. So, she is at the very end of a string of hotels. Thanks to this, the water in the sea is much cleaner here than in other places. In addition, "Atlanta 4 *" is located on a hill surrounded by picturesque cliffs, and from the windows and balconies of absolutely all rooms a magnificent view of the sea and the surrounding area opens. Also, tourists really enjoyed swimming here with masks, because near the coast you can find many different marine animals.

As for the beach, many tourists note discomfort when entering the sea due to the presence of large stones at the bottom. Therefore, enter the water carefully. If you come to rest with children, stones can be a real problem. In this case, experienced tourists recommend not going to the hotel‚Äôs own beach, but to the municipal beach located a couple of hundred meters from it. The water there is not so clean, but the entrance to the sea is sandy and gentle.





Extremely positive emotions are caused by the hotel pool. It is filled not with standard chlorinated, but with real sea water. At the same time, swimming in it is allowed even at night.

As for the rooms, they do not cause any particular complaints. Despite the fact that the furniture and appliances in them are quite old, everything is very clean and comfortable. Cleaning is carried out carefully and regularly. True, some guests complained about the frequent lack of hot water.

The greatest number of complaints from tourists was caused by food at the Atlanta Hotel. So, the vast majority of vacationers found the local food very meager and for the most part tasteless. Many tourists would not recommend coming here with children, as it will be quite difficult to feed the kids here.

As for the hotel staff, there are practically no complaints. The vast majority of tourists noted the friendliness and helpfulness of the staff. Opinions of vacationers were divided about the animation, which, by the way, in Atlanta 4 * is not at all. Someone after previous trips to Turkey got used to the fact that they are entertained by animators from morning to night, while someone liked that there is an opportunity to spend time in peace and quiet.

In a word, if Alanya is chosen as the destination for your vacation, Atlanta Hotel will become a good economical option for not very demanding people who do not care about the animation in the hotel.





Alanya Attractions

The main attraction of this popular resort in Turkey is the Ich-Kale fortress, located on top of a hill towering above the city. Its walls stretch for six and a half kilometers. There are no less than 140 watchtowers. On the territory of the fortress today you can observe the ruins of a medieval castle, as well as ancient churches, baths and other buildings. Also inside the fortress there are a number of residential villas that were built here relatively recently - in the 19th century. In general, Ich-Kale is a real open-air museum, accessible to all who wish.

In Alanya, there are other attractions, among which you can distinguish the mosque built on the orders of the Sultan Aladdin Keikubat, the mausoleum of Aksebe Turbesi and the Tersane shipyard. All these structures date back to the 13th century.

In Alanya, there are also very interesting museums. One of them is the ethnographic museum located in the Kyzyl-Kul tower, as well as the archaeological museum, which presents the rarest floor mosaics created by the hands of ancient masters in the second century BC. By the way, the Kyzyl-Kul tower is one of the main attractions of the city. She is even depicted on the flag of Alanya. The tower is an octagonal structure made of red brick, the height of which is 33 meters and the diameter is 29 meters.
