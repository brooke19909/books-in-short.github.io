"IPhone 5" - replacement touchscreen. Apple Service Center

The iPhone5 mobile phone is considered one of the perfect gadgets of our time. Apple engineers have created a high-quality and functional smartphone that has been working smoothly for a long time. But careless users can drop the phone by breaking the screen. If the touchscreen on the iPhone 5 does not work, then its replacement is usually required. This is described in the article.

What is a touchscreen?

A touchscreen is a touch screen that allows you to use your fingers to control the operation of the device. Since this part of the gadget is used most often, it is on it that various breakdowns appear.





Touch screen replacement is considered a popular service in service centers. Typically, a breakdown is associated with mechanical damage - falls, bumps, kinks, bays. The state of the gadget depends on the accuracy of use.

Main problems

Typically, replacing the touchscreen on the "iPhone 5" is performed if the following problems are observed:

  1. On the surface there are cracks, chips and other imperfections.
  2. There is no sensor reaction to pressing.
  3. Color distortion from the main screen is distorted. Color spots may be observed.
  4. The touchscreen works partially.




If there are such signs, you need to contact a specialized center where the masters will perform a replacement. If the original part is installed, then after repair the device will last a long time. That is why it is important to contact Apple centers.

Replacement Stages

Replacing the touchscreen on the "iPhone 5" is carried out in several stages:

  1. It is necessary to turn off the gadget, pull out the battery. You also need to unscrew the 2 screws located on the bottom end of the device. Use the suction cup to remove the front panel with the screen.
  2. The plate, which is in a special set, serves to open the latch along the perimeter of the gap between the glass and the body.
  3. The cables used to connect the screen module and the motherboard must be disconnected. This must be done carefully, since there is a risk of damage, because the cables are fragile.
  4. The front panel is presented in the form of a module. It must be disassembled, remove the front camera holder. It is necessary to unscrew the screws that secure the Home button and the 4 screws of the screen module.

Such instructions are used when performing home repairs and in a service center. But only in the second case will it be possible to do the job efficiently.

Apple Service Center

Replacing the touchscreen with "iPhone 5" requires skills and experience, therefore it is preferable not to carry out repairs yourself, but to entrust this to professionals. It is advisable to contact the Apple Service Center, where specialists cope with various problems. Such centers are located in every major city.





First, the masters perform equipment diagnostics to determine the causes of the breakdown and the complexity of the repair. For replacement, original parts are used, which eliminates the possibility of repeated failure. Moreover, the work is performed in a short time. After repair, a warranty is provided.

Difficulties

Replacing the touchscreen on the "iPhone 5" can be complicated by some nuances:

  1. There may be damaged loops. Usually this happens when the broken glass is removed improperly, especially during independent work.
  2. Some use the wrong glue. The glass must be fixed to a special composition, otherwise the screen will be damaged even with minor impacts. If you order work in the center, then this will be avoided.
  3. Before work, the old glue is completely removed, since only then can new glass be qualitatively fixed.

The update has some features. The screen consists of an LCD monitor, a protective glass and a sensor. These parts are glued together with a special composition with which only masters can work. Terms of use

To not require repair iPhone 5, you must carefully handle it. The device must not come into contact with water, since many elements become unusable due to moisture. High humidity is harmful to the gadget, so you should not take it to the bath or bath.

Do not use the equipment in snow or rain. When talking, it is better to use a headset. For the screen, you need to apply a high-quality protective film, which will eliminate glare and make the surface matte. The device must be worn in a reliable case. The manufacturer produces original accessories for different versions.





The iPhone does not need to be carried in your pocket or bag along with hard objects - keys, coins. After all, the appearance of scratches cannot be avoided with them. It should be remembered that in some parts of the technique dust, dirt and even cosmetics are clogged. To charge, you need to use the original power supplies and cables.

Thus, the safety of the gadget depends on the user. If he takes care of the device, then repairs will not be required often. And if you still need to eliminate the drawback, you need to replace the screen in the Apple Service Center.
