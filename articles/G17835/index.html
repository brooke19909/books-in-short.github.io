How to make a rosette troll costume?

Your daughter loves a cartoon about trolls and now wants to look like a Rosette on all mornings? Support the baby. After all, only in childhood do girls know how to value a fairy tale and want to live in it. Making a troll costume with your own hands is very simple. Interesting? Read the details below.

Party dress



The troll costume is very simple to make. It is based on a blue dress. Of course, in the cartoon, Rozochka wears a casual outfit, but it's better to choose something more solemn for a matinee. For example, you can buy or sew a satin dress with a light chiffon wrap. At the neck you need to make a pad. It should be made from the same fabric from which the dress is sewn. The overlay will stand out not with material, but with color. The shape of this decorative element is a drop, only its narrow end does not converge at one point. Decorate the overlay with flowers, beads and buttons. At the bottom of the dress, you also need to make an applique. Three pink drops should be cut and a trefoil should be made from them.

You can make a wig yourself. A troll costume would be inferior without him. For this purpose, you can use artificial strands of hair, which should be pre-painted in pink, or take thick woolen threads. For the manufacture we need a mesh hat. We tie threads to it or stick artificial strands. For volume, insert a hair roller in the center of the hairstyle. We collect artificial hair on the crown and tie it with an elastic band. If desired, you can make a troll bang. At the very end, insert the rim into the hairstyle. It can even be glued for reliability. We decorate the rim with the same colors as the dress.

Suit for baby



Some girls, even for New Year's performances, want to go in the form of a Rosette. The troll costume is easy to make, so it will be easy for mom to satisfy the desire of the child. The base will be pink pants and a turtleneck. They should wear a blue dress. Sew his business half an hour. The dress pattern is a rectangle, with a neckline under the arms and a round neck. Since Rosa had a floral ornament along the hem, it needs to be repeated. You can even make the whole bottom different from the dress color, for example, green. As a decorative element, a tourniquet should be used. He will hide the seam, and the pattern from it is quite simple to lay out. A decorative patch should be sewn to one of the shoulders, in the form of a rectangle, with a rounded edge.

The troll costume should be complemented with a bright wig. It can be made from fur. To produce less headgear, the frame can be made from newspapers. Pasting them with fur is a simple task. The bottom of the hairstyle should be decorated with a blue ribbon with flowers sewn to it.
