Water park in Achinsk - envy of the entire Krasnoyarsk Territory

Summer is in full swing. The sun burns mercilessly, and everyone dreams of being near the sea, on the banks of a river or lake. And in extreme cases - in the pool, and better in the pool of the water park. That's right, the water park in Achinsk is now called the brand new, not so long ago open center for water entertainment, designed for families, amateur and professional fitness classes.

A tour of the recent past

In the fifties of the twentieth century, near the small former merchant city of Achinsk, the construction of a huge giant plant, the Achinsk alumina refinery, began. Together with the plant, in less than 20 years, microdistricts of modern Achinsk have grown, which provided housing for 180 thousand new settlers. The young city needed an entertainment infrastructure. By the end of the sixties, when the Palace of Culture, which still holds city festivals and concerts, and a sports stadium for 11,000 people, had already built a network of cinemas in Achinsk. Each major district of Achinsk had its own cinema: Siberia (the main cinema of the network), Yunost, cinema clubs Komsomolets and Stroitel and a specialized children's cinema Orlenok.

In the nineties of the twentieth century, with the collapse of the Soviet Union, the entire cinema network of the country was in a deep economic crisis, and the halls in Achinsk were empty. The rooms of cinema clubs eventually occupied gyms. In the former main cinema "Siberia" the youth center of the same name was located, but the building of "Youth" was empty for a very long time. Cinema on water attractions in Achinsk "waved without looking"

A little more than two years and about 150 million rubles (according to media reports) were spent on the re-equipment of the building.

In the spacious room of the Achinsk water park (in the former cinema), a large pool and water attractions are very conveniently located. In addition, there is still a fitness room with fitness equipment, a sauna, comfortable locker rooms and showers.

Twenty years ago, families came here for movie shows. The premiere days when film actors and directors came to creative meetings were a whole event for the townspeople.

Today, the Forma aquacenter in Achinsk is also gathered by families and companies, but in order to have a good time near the water. Achinsk water park is ready to receive guests on weekdays from 9 am to 9 pm, on weekends (Saturday, Sunday) - from 10 am to 10 pm. In the halls, police officers and medical personnel are constantly on duty. Occupations with professional trainers in the pool and gym are possible.
