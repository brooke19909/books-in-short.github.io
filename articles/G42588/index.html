Laguna Beach Alya Resort & Spa 5 *: description, infrastructure, reviews

In this article, we will talk about the Turkish hotel Laguna Beach Alya Resort & Spa 5 *.





Turkey annually attracts many tourists from around the world. There are many hotels and hotels aimed at different people. There are youth, family hotels, you can find both a budget option and a luxury hotel. Weather conditions allow you to enjoy swimming in the warm sea from May to November.

Laguna Beach is a modern complex located in a picturesque place in a small village - Karaburun. The village is located on the azure coast of the Mediterranean Sea and is considered one of the least explored places on the peninsula. Sea water here is especially clear and clean, so fans of beach holidays, beautiful landscapes and the rich sea world love to come here.

About the complex

"Laguna" opened last year, so everything here is new, modern and comfortable. It is located on the eastern slope of the ancient settlement Justinianopolis. The hotel staff and management have created excellent conditions for a good holiday: a lot of entertainment, a well-equipped beach, bars and restaurants, a pool with an aqua park, a SPA center, and fascinating leisure for children.

Remoteness from the main objects

From the hotel complex Laguna Beach Alya Resort & Spa Hotel 5 * to the airport of Antalya and a half hours drive to the air harbor Gazipasa - an hour. From the hotel to the beach about 500 meters (2nd line). Alanya city center is at a distance of 35 kilometers from the complex.

Where to go? What to see?

By itself, the Laguna Beach Alya Resort Spa 5 * hotel resembles a large cruise ship, in your free time you can stroll through the well-groomed green area and enjoy the sea air.

The coastline along the hotel is rich in many restaurants and bars where you can spend your free time enjoying the sea beauties and delicious food.

In Alanya you can visit the city fortress, stalactite caves, "Adam Atajagi", the Red Tower, the Byzantine chapel.

If desired, hotel guests can rent a car or bicycle, ride around the village. See the magnificent scenery, the ruins of the ancient city of Justiniano, where today archaeological excavations are carried out.





What guests say about Laguna Beach Alya Resort Spa 5 *

Reviews about the complex are mostly positive. His discovery happened recently, but he has already managed to prove himself on the good side.

Tourists who spent their holidays in the “Lagoon”, first of all, note the modern style of the hotel, new furniture, beautiful interior, clean rooms. At first glance, the hotel pleases with its neatness, cleanliness, and service. Judging by the reviews, the hotel staff is very polite, well-mannered, spotlessly clean and attentive. Maids provide quality daily room cleaning.

As for the beach, it is about 10 minutes in calm steps. There is a bus that periodically runs from the hotel to the beach. The entrance to the sea is good, the water is clean, the beach itself, according to vacationers, is large enough, there is enough space for everyone.

A lot of positive feedback about the restaurant Laguna Beach. Vacationers express gratitude to both the cooks and the waiters. The service here is fantastic, the cuisine is very tasty, a wide selection of dishes, the food is aimed at residents of different countries. There are several bars in which smiling bartenders prepare delicious and beautiful cocktails for both adults and children.

How much is a ticket

The cost of a week-long rest at Laguna Beach Alya Resort & Spa 5 * for two adults starts from 36 thousand rubles (departure from Moscow). For a family with two children a vacation lasting 10 days will cost about 75 thousand. The package price includes flight, transfer, accommodation, meals.

Price without a flight starts at 10 thousand per person. The hotel is considered a budget option. The cost is 100% consistent with quality.
