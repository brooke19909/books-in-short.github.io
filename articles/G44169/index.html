Excursions - to guests of Turkey

According to tourists, one of the most popular excursions in Turkey is a visit to the thermal springs of Pamukkale and the city cut out in the rocks - Cappadocia. These excursions are quite long, it takes 2 days. For their implementation, travel agent services are required.



The mineral waters of Pamukkale formed a natural cascade of snow-white pools. Here, the ancient Romans recovered. According to legend, here Queen Cleopatra herself took healing baths.

Cappadocia is famous for its underground rocky cities with complex architecture, water supply, sewage.

Excursions on your own. Alanya

The most creative tourists organize excursions on their own using their English vocabulary and advice from local residents. A bus runs from Avsallar to Alanya every 10 minutes. It takes about 1 hour to get to Alanya Bus Station. The fare is 4.5 lira per person. Here at the station, the exchange rate of $ for lira is more profitable than in the exchangers of Avsallar.

From here we recommend taking a bus number 4 to the Ich-Kale fortress. He walks in an hour, the ticket price is 2 lira. The road to the fortress takes about half an hour, the ticket price is 2 lira. You will pay 10 lira per person for entering the fortress. Then we recommend that, going down the mountain, walk about 3 km towards the salt cave (tourist attraction) and the famous Cleopatra Beach.
