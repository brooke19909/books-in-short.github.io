Rhodes - a miracle island washed by two seas

This piece of land, which has the shape of an ellipse, is cradled by waves of the Aegean Sea on the eastern side and the Mediterranean on the western side. Afandau - a picturesque town on the coast of the Aegean

As noted above, the Golf View 3 * hotel is located near the village of Afandou, which is located 19 km from the town of the same name in the center of the island. An ancient history is associated with its name (Afandou - invisible). Once the pirates of the Aegean Sea chose this town and often began to visit here and rob the locals. And then the townspeople decided to move deep into the island, in a place that would be hidden from the eyes of the pirates. The sea robbers were very surprised when they once discovered that there were no more cities in the area, but did not dare to go in search of it and left the inhabitants alone. In Afandou you can find real Greek flavor: winding old streets, low houses in traditional Greek architecture, rural markets with many handmade rugs. Sometimes it even seems that civilization has not touched these places. However, the most valuable in these parts is the hospitality of local residents. Golf View 3 * Afandou will welcome guests with warmth and hospitality. They will be able to see the most interesting architectural monuments belonging to the Byzantine era: the Church of the Virgin Mary near the central square of the town, the Panagia Katholiki church (Church of Our Lady), decorated with frescoes of the 17th and 18th centuries. The village also has a museum of folk art with an exhibition of works by local artists. Tourists can also try various dishes of national cuisine, visiting the many taverns, also decorated in the traditional Greek style.



Beaches Afandou

The coastline of Afandou is over 3 kilometers long. However, there are not many beaches. The main part consists of bizarre cliffs and mysterious caves. Locals know the passages to them, and if you wish, you can negotiate with them and take a short tour. The water off the coast is crystal clear, and the beaches are quiet and calm. You can go diving or windsurfing. There is also plenty of water fun. Some Afandou beaches have been awarded the blue flag by the World Environmental Organization. By the way, not far from the coastline in the immediate vicinity of the Golf View 3 * hotel is the only golf complex on the island. Here, at the height of the tourist season, various music shows and other social events are organized.
