Districts of Rostov-on-Don: a brief description

By her decree, the customs, which stood at the mouth of the Temernik River, were rebuilt into a fortress, calling it Rostov. Due to its convenient location and foreign merchants, the economic level has grown rapidly here.

In 1863, the districts of Rostov-on-Don became subordinate to the Region of the Don Army. However, already in 1920, Soviet power was established in the territory. After that, industry began to appear in the city. In 1927 the largest factory was built - Rostselmash. A little later in the city there will be a chemical enterprise and metalworking industry. Such a rapid growth of industry led to the fact that Rostov began to quickly turn into a large center, which, of course, contributed to its economic development. At present, large areas of Rostov-on-Don are Kirovsky, Voroshilovsky, Zheleznodorozhny, Leninsky and others, which we will describe in this article.



Kirovsky district

The central district of Rostov-on-Don is Kirovsky. This is the historical, cultural, financial and business center of the city. All administrative authorities, large trade and financial institutions are located here. If we compare the rest of Rostov-on-Don with Kirovsky, then the latter is the oldest, since its appearance coincides with the foundation of the city itself. The remains of the fortress, which was still being built by Dmitry Rostovsky, clearly testify to this .

Central street - Sedova. The first industrial enterprise in Rostov appeared in 1857, in parallel with it attempts were made to build the first water supply in the city. In the Kirov region, there are 10 secondary schools, 18 preschool institutions and 5 higher education institutions. There are many places for cultural recreation in the area. These are the regional philharmonic society, the cinema house, the Rostov and Burevestnik cinemas, the Puppet Theater, etc. At present, the population of the district is 64.7 thousand people.

Voroshilovsky district of Rostov-on-Don

Voroshilovsky district is the most populated part of the city. The current population is about 216 thousand people. Large industrial plants, institutions of the military-defense complex, and more than 600 small business enterprises are located and successfully operate here.



Zheleznodorozhny District

The foundation of the district falls on 1937. The population here is about 102 thousand people. Today on its territory there are about 2700 enterprises of large, medium and small businesses. It is considered one of the most beautiful, if you compare all the districts of the city of Rostov-on-Don.

Within the territory of Zheleznodorozhny, on the right bank of the Don, there is a great place to relax - Kumzhenskaya grove. Also here is the largest in the country botanical garden and Park them. Sobino.

Leninsky district

Leninsky was formed in 1920. Previously, it was called Temernitsky, but was later renamed. The population is about 80 thousand people. Leninsky district is the cultural center of Rostov. Here are parks and palaces of culture, the Educational Music Center. Nazaretova.



Oktyabrsky District

October was founded in 1937. The population is about 167 thousand people. Not only 3 universities, but also 6 research institutes are located on the local territory. There are also many entertainment venues, one of which is the Rostov Zoo. It is considered the largest of all that are in Russia. At the zoo, the "Young Zoologist" club operates.

Pervomaisky district

The foundation of the district falls on 1936. The number of residents varies within 183 thousand people. Today, about 2 thousand enterprises operate in the district. There is also a suburban railway station and Rostov airport. In Pervomaisk concentrated about 50 educational institutions, including 2 universities and 20 schools.
