Atlas Hotel Vityazevo: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Hotel Atlas (Vityazevo) is the embodiment of modern architecture and classic interiors. The hotel offers a quality vacation for the whole family for affordable money. We suggest that you consider the option of living in Atlas in more detail.

Description of the hotel complex





Atlas Hotel (Vityazevo, Anapa) is a new four-story hotel complex, which opened its doors in 2013. The entire interior of the hotel is made in a modern style with classic notes. The rooms are large and bright.

Entering the hotel lobby, you will immediately plunge into a special atmosphere. After all, it has an original design that displays the name of the hotel complex. The ceiling is voluminous, decorated with a picture of an atlas with maps, framed in a round gilded frame.

The favorable location of the Atlas hotel provides it with a constantly large number of guests. The hotel complex is located near major entertainment venues, as well as a seven-minute walk to the sea coast.

Hotel Rooms





Atlas Hotel (Vityazevo) offers its guests the following room categories:

  • triple standard;
  • double and triple junior suite;
  • five-bed suite from two rooms;
  • five-bed studio with two rooms.

Each room of the Atlas Hotel contains a set of furniture necessary for a comfortable stay: single and double beds with orthopedic mattresses and hypoallergenic pillows, bedside tables, a desk with a chair and / or a dressing table with a pouffe, wardrobe. Of electrical appliances, there is a plasma TV and a mini fridge. The rooms are also equipped with a safe.

There are 44 rooms. The climate can be maintained using air conditioning. Each room has its own bathroom with a full bath and a balcony. It has a table and chairs.

The cost of living in the Atlas hotel as of July-August 2016 began from 2100 rubles. For children under three years of age, no fee will be charged, and for children from three to ten, a discount of ten percent of the cost of the main place is provided.

Services provided by the Atlas Hotel staff





Atlas is a hotel in Vityazevo, where the following services are included in the price:

  • parking;
  • transfer from the airport, from the bus station, Anapa railway station ;
  • wireless internet (Wi-Fi);
  • heated pool;
  • playground;
  • use of ironing board and iron (available on each floor);
  • storage of expensive items in the safe at the administrator;
  • use of an electric kettle, which is located at the reception;
  • high chair in the dining room;
  • daily housekeeping;
  • linen change every three days.

For an additional fee, Atlas Hotel staff is ready to offer you (prices are as of June-August 2016):

  • transfer from the railway station Tunnel and back for 1000 rubles;
  • transfer from / to Krasnodar for 3500 rubles;
  • laundry service (about 200 rubles per bookmark).

Catering





A separate item is food. The Atlas Hotel has a dining room where you can order both a comprehensive three meals a day, and separately breakfast, lunch or dinner. The menu consists of homemade dishes: hot meat and fish dishes, a variety of salads and snacks, many desserts and fresh pastries, drinks.

Atlas Hotel also has a bar and cafe. They close at 23:30.

A few negative impressions





Nobody had completely bad impressions about the rest in the Atlas hotel. But here are a few points that, according to vacationers, can be fixed:

  • Parking is free only for those who have pre-booked their vacation at the hotel.
  • Some rooms on the ground floor overlook trash cans and car parking. They are also quite noisy.
  • The cabinet is not big enough. If resting in the room more than three people, then the hangers may not be enough.
  • Animation for children is extremely rare. In other hotels, such entertainment was once every couple of days.
  • There were no mosquito nets on the windows and doors.
  • The dining room may not calculate the order correctly.
  • The soundproofing in the rooms of the Atlas Hotel is not at the highest level. You can hear not only the noise from the street, which is very busy, but sometimes even from the neighboring rooms and from the corridor.

Location of the Atlas Hotel





The exact address of the place where the Atlas Hotel is located: Vityazevo resort, 13 Alexandriisky proezd.

Nearby are a variety of entertainment: water park, dolphinarium, Paralia, an amusement park, as well as many catering establishments and bars. Atlas Hotel (Vityazevo) is located next to the Central Beach. But in fifteen minutes there is one more.

The distance to Anapa from the Atlas Hotel is 11 kilometers, and to Novorossiysk - 50 kilometers. Anapa Airport is seven kilometers away.
