We are going to rest in Astrakhan. "Alpine Village" - a great place to relax with the whole family

Russia is a large state in which there are wonderful places to relax. For those who would like to spend their holidays in a unique place and get an unforgettable experience, it is proposed to visit the city of Astrakhan. More precisely, we will talk about the village Verkhnelebyazhye. There is a wonderful recreation center. It is located on the Volga River, more precisely, in its delta. This is the fishing base "Alpine Village" (Astrakhan). It opened not so long ago and operates year-round. The complex itself is a fishing complex, but at the same time, the base is simply an excellent place for a successful and relaxing pastime for the family.



Living conditions

Someone, and the Russian fishermen should be well aware of this place. Vacationers always have at their disposal excellent buildings, the rooms of which, without exception, are equipped with both refrigerators and bathrooms. But the most important thing is the presence of air conditioning in every room. Under such conditions, who does not want to come to rest in Astrakhan ?!

"Alpine Village" offers rooms of different price categories. For example, anyone can stay in an exclusive suite, whose main advantage is the presence of a jacuzzi. Vacationers are offered one-story and two-story houses with double and quadruple rooms. Terraces are protected by mosquito nets, so spending evenings in the fresh air will be quite comfortable. Also, in order to maximize the level of convenience, sofas, armchairs and tables are installed here.

Location Benefits

Convenient location is another advantage of this complex. Just 46 km is Astrakhan. "Alpine Village" is located far from the noisy highway, in a quiet corner of nature - the Volga River Delta. Vacationers can be sure of clean air.



Infrastructure

Describing this recreation center, it is impossible not to mention the developed infrastructure. On the territory there is an excellent cafe in which tourists are invited to eat. There is also a pretty good restaurant where, if the guest wishes, his own catch can be cooked. The halls in it are large, so you can arrange banquets.

At the disposal of any tourist there is always a bathhouse with billiards, which can be a great addition to relaxation, as well as great entertainment. After a bright, intense pastime, a vacation in such a beautiful place as Astrakhan will be remembered for a long time. "Alpine Village" offers its guests a parking zone. It is very convenient for those who come here in a personal car.

Neighborhood

Almost the entire territory of the base is surrounded by a beautiful park. Different trees grow in this zone. Each vacationer, of course, will be satisfied with this park, having discovered a large number of fauna specimens, all kinds of birch trees and a large number of relic plants can become an object for admiring . A wonderful and calm atmosphere of unity with nature will help everyone to temporarily abandon the work of everyday busy life.
