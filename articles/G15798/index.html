Smartphone Xiaomi Redmi 4A 16GB: specifications, comparison with analogs and reviews

After the release of Xiaomi Redmi 4 and its "PRO" version was announced, all buyers forgot about the Redmi 4A model. This is in vain. The device has a small cost, but good features. The assembly is beautiful, the display is excellent, performance is also on par.

Before reading the review, you should immediately say about the main feature of the phone Xiaomi Redmi 4A 16Gb. Its characteristic will be described later. It's about cost. In China, it does not exceed $ 75. If we talk about the Russian Federation, it does not rise above the mark of 5 thousand rubles.



Unpack

For a long time, budget phones, like their expensive "brothers", do not have an interesting configuration. The described model is delivered in a cardboard box. It has a very small size. There is not much space, but everything fits perfectly into the container. The whole set weighs no more than 325 grams. This feature of Xiaomi Redmi 4A 16Gb pleases.

Inside you can find a cable for charging and connecting to a computer, a power supply. The quick charge function is not integrated. If you buy in foreign stores on the Internet, then you can also get an adapter to the outlet in the kit.

Features

Many Chinese companies are creating low-cost phones with a metal case, as well as necessarily embed fingerprint scanners in their models. All this, of course, pleases, but the quality of implementation leaves much to be desired. Is this the case with the described model?

Xiaomi manufacturer did differently. He created a plastic case, refused a fingerprint scanner. Thanks to such savings, a more or less powerful chipset was installed. A quality screen is also installed, and the assembly satisfies even the most critical user.

The external characteristics of Xiaomi Redmi 4A 16Gb (Rose and Gold - two color options) are excellent. With the fact that there is a pink and gold phone, while its front part is white. Only the color of the rear panel changes.

You can understand that the phone is made of plastic only after touching it. Also gives out the weight of the smartphone. However, this is not scary, since the phone was created in good faith.

The manufacturer is clearly not going to use glass such as 2.5D. The described device received the usual flat panel. Its brand and manufacturer are not advertised. Oleophobic type coating is available, and it is of high quality. On the screen you can notice the presence of light sensors, distances, there is also a speakerphone and a camera.

Unfortunately, there are problems with the sensors of the device. At one point, all sensors may turn off. For example, many buyers complain that their display does not turn off during a call when presented to the ear, the auto brightness option that is turned on does not work, and the accelerometer does not rotate the picture to a horizontal position. Buyers report that in 90% of cases, this can be cured by rolling back to factory settings. But at the same time, even though the screen backlight works, it's still bad, it is often late and incorrectly adjusts the brightness. In this, the characteristics of Xiaomi Redmi 4A 16Gb buyers are not encouraging.



Design

On the bottom of the front panel are touch buttons. They do not have a backlight, so in the dark you have to rely on your intuition. If desired, in the settings you can change the key assignment.

Installed regular MicroUSB. Unfortunately, the manufacturer refused the USB Type-C connector. Otherwise, it would have been necessary to use other components, and, accordingly, the cost of the device would be higher. OTG mode works.

The phone also has the following characteristics: Xiaomi Redmi 4A 16Gb Gold has an infrared port and a headphone jack. In this matter, the manufacturer does not change its traditions.

There is an interesting pattern on the back cover. The mirror logo of the manufacturer is also applied. This is done so that the device does not seem boring. The idea was 100% successful.

Near the bottom of the device you can find a speaker. It is external, used for hands-free calling and listening to music. Buyers note that it is quite impressive - expectations were much worse. The volume is average, the speaker has decent quality. Due to the speaker on the back, when the phone is on it, the sound becomes muffled. This is a minus, for some quite critical.

The owners say that in the model the most pleasant feature is the ratio of the size of the device, the screen and its thickness. The phone is convenient to use.
