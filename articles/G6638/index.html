Crystal Springs Beach Hotel 4 *: reviews and photos

From time to time, every person wants to take a break from the monotony of working everyday life. And a vacation by the sea is ideal for travelers of different ages and categories. Quite often, Cyprus becomes the stopping place for tourists. This is an excellent choice not only for the beach, but also for excursion vacations.

Of course, on the coast of Cyprus there are a huge number of hotels. One of them is the modern resort Crystal Springs Beach Hotel. Travelers naturally try to find as much information as possible about this place. Where to look for a hotel and what living conditions to rely on? Is it suitable for families with a child? How tourists respond about the hotel?

Where is the hotel complex located?





Crystal Springs Beach Hotel 4 * is a cozy place located near the seashore in the territory of the famous Cypriot resort town of Protaras. The hotel is surrounded on all sides by a garden and a picturesque park, where it is always pleasant to walk, enjoy the exotic beauty and the much-desired coolness.

The distance to the city center is five kilometers - it is quite easy to get here by taxi or city bus. By the way, in ten minutes you can get to another interesting town - Paralimni. The nearest international airport is located in the city of Larnaca, the distance to it is about 50 kilometers. On the other hand, guests are usually provided with a convenient transfer, so you should not worry about the road. Crystal Springs Beach Hotel is a great place for a relaxing beach holiday. Different categories of travelers stay here, including families with children.

Hotel Crystal Springs Beach Hotel (Protaras): description and photos of rooms





The hotel has 145 rooms. Of these, 111 are standard double rooms. In addition, there are 14 spacious family rooms, as well as 20 combined, which are suitable for relaxing large groups. The furnishings of the rooms are modern, there is the necessary amount of furniture, as well as access to a private balcony.

Tourists can count on a set of household appliances, which consists of a TV (you can watch some Russian channels), an air conditioning system, a radio, a telephone and a small safe. There is also a minibar. Guests receive a sufficient amount of purified drinking water, and you need to pay for other drinks.

The bathroom will also appreciate modern equipment. You can take a shower at any time. There is also a toilet, hairdryer, wall mirror, hygiene items and a set of clean towels.

What kind of food can tourists expect?





The guests of the Crystal Springs Beach Hotel 4 * hotel complex are free to choose the food scheme convenient for them. The price includes daily breakfast. The Salamina Restaurant is constantly open on site. In the morning, guests can enjoy a buffet. Reviews indicate that the menu is quite diverse. Guests are offered salads and light snacks, fresh fruits, homemade cakes, drinks, in a word, traditional breakfast dishes. In addition, the restaurant can have dinner or lunch for an additional fee (if the all-inclusive system has not been paid).

Poolside is the Yialos Bar. Here guests can get a chilled drink, light snacks, as well as a large number of dishes prepared on the grill. There is another bar in the lobby - Tefkros, where you can relax in a pleasant atmosphere, order a strong drink and have fun in the company of friends. Do not forget that in the city there are many cafes, restaurants and eateries where you can eat.

Where is the beach located? Water recreation





Of course, for the most part, travelers go to Cyprus with the aim of having fun by the sea. What can the Crystal Springs Beach Hotel 4 * offer? Protaras is famous for its picturesque beaches. One of them is at the hotel‚Äôs disposal and can be reached in a few minutes. This is a private stretch of coast, so you can count on some privacy and comfort. The beach is covered with soft sand, and the entrance to the sea is cleared. On the other hand, special rubber shoes will not hurt. There is a small lagoon where children like to dabble.

Fans of more active recreation will also not be disappointed. It is easy to rent a pedal boat or boat on the beach. In addition, tourists can enjoy boat and water skiing, sailing and scuba diving. Naturally, these are paid entertainments, but their cost is quite justified.

The list of additional services for guests





Guests at Crystal Springs Beach Hotel can expect some additional services. For example, a currency exchange office is constantly working here. Laundry facilities are also available. In case of deterioration of well-being or health problems, you can count on the qualified help of a specialist (paid service).

The hotel is sometimes used for various events, including business (seminars, meetings, conferences) and ceremonial (birthdays, weddings). Therefore, there is a spacious conference room for 120 people.

How do tourists spend their free time? Entertainment and leisure





Naturally, most travelers come to Cyprus in order to have fun on the seashore. Nevertheless, you can find yourself an interesting lesson at the Crystal Springs Beach Hotel. In the courtyard there is a small but clean pool, which, by the way, is surrounded by picturesque thickets of exotic plants. Nearby there is a terrace with sun loungers and parasols. This is the center of the hotel‚Äôs public life - tourists here swim, have fun, relax and make new friends.

There is also a large SPA center, the services of which are paid separately. Here you can sign up for various anti-aging, cosmetic and wellness treatments, visit a sauna or a spacious bath, use the services of professional masseurs. The entrance to the gym is also constantly open.

In the evenings, various themed evenings are regularly organized in the restaurant or on the terrace near it, dedicated to the cuisine and culture of a particular country. By the way, the most popular are folklore performances, thanks to which travelers can learn more about Cypriot traditions.

Children's leisure on site





Many tourists are going on a trip with their children. Are there any conditions necessary for this at the Crystal Springs Beach Hotel? Cyprus is a great place for families. And this hotel provides the necessary amenities. In particular, extra beds are delivered to the room, while the restaurant offers special meals to small guests.

As for entertainment, your child will definitely find something to his liking. For example, children can use a special shallow pool with clean, warm water. There is a games room with a huge number of toys for children of different ages. And the guys have a good time outdoors with a swing. The hotel also has several professional nannies who are happy to look after the child for a small fee.
