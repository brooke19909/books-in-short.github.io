Marina 3 * (Cyprus / Ayia Napa): description of the hotel and services, guest reviews, photos

One of the most magnificent holidays, which will be remembered for a lifetime, can be spent on the wonderful ancient island in the Marina 3 * hotel. Cyprus is famous for its many hotel complexes suitable for family vacations, and Marina is no exception. An excellent location on the coast of a beautiful bay with picturesque nature, a bewitching atmosphere, interesting and fascinating excursions, decent service, personal service - all this makes this hotel quite popular with many tourists.

Island of the Gods - Cyprus

The resort areas of fabulously beautiful Cyprus attract thousands of tourists. The gentle rays of the warming Mediterranean, clean white beaches, turquoise waves with rumbling waves, well-developed infrastructure and ancient historical monuments - all this can be obtained and seen while relaxing on a fabulous island. Traveling in this colorful country will allow tourists to make interesting discoveries for themselves, learn a lot of new and interesting things, becoming a participant in an informative excursion program. Hotel complexes will help to make the rest more complete, among which there are quiet, located in modest villages, and funny and noisy, located in the central areas of cities. Many of the hotels offer a range of services for children. A huge variety of hotels famous for the beautiful city of Ayia Napa, located on the fabulous coast. Among them is the hotel Marina 3 *. It is suitable for an exquisite and romantic vacation with your soulmate. In the shade of exotic relict alleys, each of the vacationers will be able to enjoy indescribable pleasure by tasting local dishes and feeling the refined aroma of local wines. And quiet romantic evenings to the sounds of mesmerizing music will make your stay in this place unique. In addition, only on this island you can completely escape from the urban everyday life, the bustling bustle of megacities and immerse yourself in the fairy-tale world of this ancient people, get to know the locals, their identity and culture. Numerous sights of the city can complement the rest.

Rooms

For its vacationers, the Marina 3 * hotel (Cyprus) provides 131 cozy rooms. All are located in a four-story building. Most rooms have great sea views. But those that overlook the city have an equally attractive view, since the hotel building is located at the highest point, and all the sights and beauty of the village are visible in the palm of your hand.

The type of rooms is determined by the category of the hotel, that is, its number of stars (3 * Marina). Standard-rooms of the hotel are equipped with all necessary equipment. They are divided into single with sea view (10), double with sea view (90), triple single rooms (4), single rooms with a view of the hotel courtyard (27). Each has its own air conditioning, which will allow you to individually control the temperature in the room. There is a satellite or cable TV. Using your phone, you can order this or that service in your room. Each room has a minibar, which is filled for an additional payment with drinks chosen directly by the guests of the room. For storage of valuables and documents there is a safe (for a fee). The bathroom has a shower or a small bathtub, a hairdryer, a make-up mirror, bath towels and necessary toiletries. One extra bed is available upon prior request.

The rooms have a kettle and cups, which makes it possible to enjoy a cup of aromatic tea or coffee directly in the room. There is a refrigerator (1 euro per day).

All rooms are decorated in light colors, making the room brighter and warmer. The floors in the rooms are carpeted. Each has access to the balcony.

Nutrition

For its guests, the hotel Marina 3 * (Cyprus) offers various types of food: breakfast, full board, half board. Breakfast is served in the hotel restaurant as a buffet with a wide selection of light and nutritious dishes. There are dishes for vegetarians, for those who need diet food. A summer outdoor terrace, modern sophisticated furnishings and wonderful views of the sea expanse will allow you to enjoy a refined meal. Upon prior reservation, breakfast can be served directly in the room.

Beach and port

Many hotel complexes do not have their own beach, including Marina. The beach of 3-star hotels is basically a city beach located near the hotel. During their stay, guests can soak up the Municipal Beach, just 350 meters away. This is a sandy coastline with free admission. It is superbly equipped and provides a range of entertainment for both adults and small vacationers. In addition, for an additional fee on the beach, you can try your hand at various sea sports. The entrance to the sea is gentle, which attracts families with small children here.

Umbrellas and sun loungers located on the beach, vacationers will be able to use for an additional fee, while at the hotel by the pool they can use this equipment for free.

No less popular place for vacationers is the port. It is located in a small enclosed bay and resembles a quiet harbor, where small pleasure boats and fishing boats are lined up in a row. The berth is small in size, which does not allow the mooring of large ships. The largest ships that can be seen here are elegant snow-white yachts offering exciting excursions. Especially popular with vacationers is the "Black Pearl".

Territory, infrastructure

The territory of the Marina 3 * hotel is rich in green spaces and numerous buildings. The Botanical Park attracts vacationers of all ages - here you can take refuge in the shade of thick trees, spend a romantic evening and just take a walk, enjoying the sea air. The hotel has a small outdoor pool with a hot tub filled with fresh water. For those who came not only to relax, but also to work, a conference room for up to 30 people is provided. The hotel has a mini market where you can buy a small souvenir, which in the future will always remind you of a wonderful vacation. Parking is available.

Representatives of the female half of vacationers who seek to maintain their originality and attractiveness will be able to use the services of a beauty salon. Guests are offered a wide range of services: tonic and relaxing massage, solarium, sauna, jacuzzi, hairdresser.

Additional services

For its vacationers Marina Hotel 3 * provides a number of additional services. So, hotel guests can always use the services of dry cleaning or laundry. In order to explore the city and its surroundings, there is a car rental service. The hotel has a doctor. To store your valuables, you can use the safe installed at the reception, there is a currency exchange office.

Entertainment and sports

The wonderful hotel Marina 3 * provides its guests with a rich entertainment program, which even slightly exceeds the level of service of a hotel with a three-star category. An animated presentation or a comedy film, watched in the cinema, will always help to cheer you up. Those who love peace and quiet are always waiting for the library reading room. Guests who prefer a more active holiday can diversify their vacation by playing tennis, basketball or volleyball, darts. There is a free gym that allows you to keep fit even during leisure. Along with free entertainment, there are those that can only be obtained at an additional cost. Among them are billiards, tennis court, archery. In the evening, guests can enjoy magnificent live music in the style of Cypriot nights or enjoy the colorful show. Quite often themed evenings are held, the most popular of which are national folklore with bewitching music that carries the unique flavor of the local people. At the end of the evening, all vacationers are waiting for a small disco

For children

How to get there

In order to get to Marina 3 *, you need to fly to Larnaca Airport, and from there it is only a 45-minute drive to Ayia Napa, where the hotel is located at: POBox 30056, 5340 Ayia Napa, Cyprus.

Reviews

A memorable vacation, bright colorful moments, excellent climatic conditions and the gentle emerald sea can be obtained at the economical Marina 3 * hotel. Reviews about him are mostly positive. Among the main advantages of this hotel complex, tourists note the colorful landscapes that open from the hotel. Friendly staff who know Russian are also not ignored.

A lot of positive reviews about a varied diet, about developed infrastructure. Near the hotel there are many taverns that do not go unnoticed by tourists. There are no complaints about the rooms. All of them are filled with home comfort and excellent decor. Rooms are cleaned every day. There is a separate ironing room where you can iron your things for free. We can not say about the proximity of the bus stop, including intercity buses. Many vacationers mention cognitive sightseeing trips organized by hotel staff. Well, perhaps the most important thing is the affordable cost of the rooms.

Along with positive characteristics, there are also those that note the minuses of the hotel. Despite the magnificent municipal beach, which is distinguished by cleanliness, guests note the absence of their own beach. The disadvantages include the distance from the airport, which makes it difficult to travel to the hotel. Many guests complain that although the Internet is in public places, it is expensive enough for an economical hotel. In addition, there are guests who complain of poor soundproofing in the rooms. But lovers of a great economical vacation, such little things can not overshadow the vacation. There are so many positive emotions that, against the general background, such trifles are simply invisible to many.

According to most vacationers in Marina 3 *, reviews and photos of the hotel can not fully convey all the sensations received during the holidays. In order to feel its whole atmosphere and appreciate the surrounding beauty, you need to stay at this hotel at least once.
