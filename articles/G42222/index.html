Sun Connect Zorbas Village 4 *: hotel services, accommodation options and guest reviews

Crete is a wonderful place where many would like to spend their holidays. For some it is only in dreams, while others are not averse to translating the idea into reality in the near future. But before that you need to choose a hotel. And a great option would be Sun Connect Zorbas Village 4 *.





general information

This 4-star hotel is located in Hersonissos, the northern city of Crete, between the Aegean and the Mediterranean Sea. And to be more precise, in a cozy resort town of Anissaras. The best part is that you have to go a maximum of two minutes to the sea from the hotel. The nearest airport is also nearby - in Heraklion (this is about 25 kilometers distance).

In Sun Connect Zorbas Village 4 * there is everything that should be in a good hotel. And even your own small water park, which is very loved by guests. There are several swimming pools, a SPA center with a sauna and massage services, a tennis court, and billiards. Wi-Fi is free throughout. There is a private parking, car rental, laundry, 24-hour reception. And the employees, by the way, speak not only Greek, but also English, so no one should have communication problems.

Sun Connect Zorbas Village 4 * (Anissaras), as mentioned above, serves its guests on an all-inclusive basis. Well, it is worth noting that this service is fully justified.

The cost of tours to this place is not high, but the guests receive decent service. Take, for example, an institution called Main Restaurant Ariadne. This is the main restaurant, which is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner. A wide selection of food and drinks is provided, and themed evenings are regularly held there.

What do the guests say?

Finally, a few words about what reviews people who have already visited in the hotel leave it. Well, the first thing they do is note the cost of the tours. And in a good plan! Accommodation prices can not be called high in principle, but if you take a ticket, and even a burning one, it will be one and a half times cheaper.

Reviews are generally positive. Guests are happy to talk about the beautiful and well-groomed territory of the hotel - everything is beautiful, green, palms and flowers delight our eyes. There is always a lot of fruit in the restaurant. Juicy melons and watermelons, apples, nectarines with peaches, plums, oranges, kiwi. A special treat is Greek yogurts, feta cheese, local honey and vegetables in different forms.

The beach is spacious and clean, the order on it is constantly monitored. Sun loungers and umbrellas are free, you can find a free seat, but not at the very high season. However, many just lay a towel and lie on it.

The cleaning, by the way, is very high quality. And the guests are pleased that the maids put the bungalows in order when the guests are in another place (at breakfast, for example, or on the beach). Towels and linen are also changed regularly.

In general, this is a good hotel, which is worth staying if tourists dream of a quiet and relaxing holiday in the heavenly part of Greece.
