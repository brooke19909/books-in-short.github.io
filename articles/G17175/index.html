Dolphinarium on Krestovsky Island

Between Novorossiysk and Anapa on the Black Sea coast there is a small urban-type settlement Utrish, in which the dolphinarium is located.

A little about this marvelous place

In 1984, a scientific marine station was opened in Utrish, which is engaged in the study of marine mammals. It was created at the initiative of the Institute of Ecology and Evolution named after A. N. Severtsov of the Russian Academy and has a certain number of branches in the cities of the Russian Federation and an office in Moscow. Dolphinarium on Krestovsky Island was opened on August 27, 1998.





Until 2000, it was located in the basin of the Maritime Institute. Now located in the Spartak pool. Dolphinarium on Krestovsky is the only one in the town. Therefore, he quickly captured the love of not only Petersburgers, but also guests of the city.

The audience has the opportunity to enjoy the beauty, grace, ingenuity and dexterity of mammals, listen to the singing of snow-white whales, and see the performance of the walrus.

Artists and their tricks

Dolphins are remarkable animals; they enjoy the tremendous attention, reverence and love of most viewers. They have a well-developed mind and outstanding capabilities, which are shown in the submissions. Dolphins do different tricks: dive and then jump high out of the water, spin in the air, sing and even dance. Representations include fur seals, belugas, bottlenose dolphins of the Black Sea, as well as sea lions.

In the presentation program in various combinations take part:
- snow-white belugas;
- northern lions;
- Pacific walruses;
- Dolphins of the Black Sea.

Dolphinarium on Krestovsky invites everyone to their performances. Here you can see how artists sing, dance, outline, make the highest jumps and interesting tricks. The program lasts about an hour. Allowed free shooting of performances from their place.

Bathing

Apart from various shows whose program is alternately updated and changed, the dolphinarium on Krestovsky invites you to swim with dolphins.
Such an activity enthralls even the most severe adult. Swimming is performed in small groups of people only under the supervision of a trainer and trainer. Friendly and funny dolphins will ride you on yourself and perform a couple of tricks in the pool in your presence.

Dolphinarium (Krestovsky Island). What additional services does the institution provide?

Dolphinarium also runs dolphin therapy courses for children and adults with depression and psychological disorders. Scientists have proved that communication with these wonderful animals calms a person and improves his mood.

Dolphinarium on Krestovsky also offers additional services.
Here you can visit the gift shop. On sale are: trinkets, magnets, photos of artists, toys and badges, figures of dolphins in different designs, balloons, a disk with an already recorded performance program. You can still get a picture that dolphins painted before your eyes.

You can have a wonderful time in a comfortable cafe. You can enjoy desserts, drinks and hot dishes at your choice. The establishment has air conditioning, as well as free Wi-Fi. It is possible to order group dinners.

In the dolphinarium at the entrance is a buffet. For kids and their parents they sell popcorn, ice cream, smoothies and cotton candy.

Photos for memory

After the performance, you can take pictures with dolphins on the platforms. Photographic equipment of the dolphinarium (the photo is printed and given to you for an hour after the end of the presentation). Prices range from 300 to 700 rubles - depending on the type and size of the picture. Claims for the quality of photos are accepted only for an hour. The price for shooting your photo equipment is 300 rubles per photo.

Feeding dolphinarium artists is strictly prohibited! There is no wardrobe in the establishment - in the hall you are in outer clothing.

Dolphinarium on Krestovsky: price

Depending on the sector, adult tickets cost 300-800 rubles, and children's tickets cost 100-500. For holidays and vacations (also applies to Saturday and Sunday), prices are higher: 400-1000 rubles - an adult ticket, from 200 to 700 rubles - children.
