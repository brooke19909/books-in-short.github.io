Firm Gorenie: manufacturing country, product reviews

Gorenie was founded in Slovenia, and the name comes from the village of the same name. Today it is a well-known manufacturer of household appliances, whose headquarters is located in Velenje. The company began work with the release of agricultural equipment. Today it is one of the ten largest manufacturers of household appliances in Europe. 1.65 million units of large household appliances are delivered to the markets annually. 95% is exported.

About the manufacturer





Gorenie (manufacturing country - Slovenia) was founded in 1950. In 1968, the experimental production of refrigerators with compressors began. In 1980 the company entered the American market. Gorenie has won many awards for ease of use, design and attention to the environment.

In 2017, Gorenie refrigerators (manufacturing country - Slovenia) received the prestigious award in the Innovation category.
