How to install Samsung Pay on the phone

Today, the world of technology does not stand still, but is progressing day by day. Every day the modern trend is gaining momentum in the form of a service that is based on mobile payments. This service helps a person make purchases without using either credit cards or cash. Indeed, today many people use smartphones, and they will allow you not to wear anything else. You just need to master this information. This article will talk about how to install Samsung Pay.





History Information

August 20, 2015 is considered to be the birthday of this program, because it was on this day that Samsung Pay service began to operate in South Korea. In the same year, but in September, US residents learned how to install Samsung Pay on a phone. They began to use the application in the territory of their country. A year later, many other countries, including Russia, learned how to install Samsung Pay. But as for Russia, here you can use this application only on certain phone models.





Devices that can support Samsung Pay

The manufacturers of the new technology used certain types of technologies throughout the system, for example Magnetic Secure Transmission. This fact means that every owner of Samsung phones using this program can pay with it in a store of absolutely any nature.





What is needed to make using this server simple

First of all, in order to understand how to install Samsung Pay on Galaxy, you need to make sure that the bank you use uses this type of system. After all, if the bank does not use it, then there is no point in installing anything.

It is worth noting that today the system we are considering in the Russian Federation uses about ten banks of the largest type, which are leaders in this business. You can find out more about which banks are connected to this system on the official website of the application. But it is worth noting that if you did not find your own there, do not despair. The system is becoming more relevant, and every day new banks sign a deal with this company.

In the event that you have verified everything and you have the right type of phone, you can safely proceed to registration. Before installing Samsung Pay on S7, you need to make sure that it has the latest firmware version. You need to download this application, then activate it on your device. Experts recommend that you set a password on it in the form of a fingerprint. You can also use a specific PIN in addition. This is necessary so that no one will use your system and your money.





Further in this application you need to register your card. It is worth noting that the use of registration of 10 cards is provided, but not more. To register, you need to scan the card, as well as enter its number. The number must be entered manually, as well as its code.

Next, you get acquainted with the services of the service, find out what it offers you, then you need to confirm the registration in the form of a password. This password, in addition to being one-time, will also come to your smartphone via SMS. Further, the system requires your signature, on the screen with your finger you draw it. A signature is necessary so that the cashier in a particular case can compare the signature on the check and in the system.

That's it, your registration has been successfully passed! You have mastered the information on how to install Samsung Pay, and your smartphone is now an excellent and convenient tool for settlements in stores.





How does this system work

How to install Samsung Pay, we figured out, now it remains to understand how to use it. In this case, there is nothing complicated, you just need to bring your smartphone to the terminal.

It is worth noting a rather important fact, it consists in the fact that this application works not only on specially equipped terminals, but also on ordinary ones that have a magnetic strip. As a rule, cashiers do not know about this innovation and can come up with many reasons to prevent you from using this system. But this application exists and shows its work. Soon everyone will know about him.

You need to know that at the checkout there are no signs about the receipt of such a means of payment. The terminal itself recognizes your smartphone and perceives it as a card, after which you just need to enter the code and pay in such a way without the participation of the card and cash.

Safety of this innovation

The process of obtaining information on how to install Samsung Pay, we went through. But is this service safe? Today in Russia there are quite a few smartphones that could accept this program. In addition, these are smartphones of one company.

Undoubtedly, the security issue of such an innovation is of concern to many consumers today, but Samsung experts assure that all payments are protected quite reliably and provide three reasons:

  1. When making a payment, the system does not use your card number, but a specific code that you know.
  2. Each purchase can not do without entering a password on your part or a fingerprint.
  3. In order for the payment to pass, the system uses special cryptograms. These cryptograms are directly responsible for the security of the smartphone and payment.

Consumer benefits
