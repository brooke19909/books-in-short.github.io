Hotel Kompas 3 * (Albena, Bulgaria): photos and reviews of tourists

An excellent family vacation will please travelers Kompas 3 * . Albena, (Bulgaria) is a popular European resort, which pleases tourists with a warm sea and affordable prices.





Location

A 6-minute walk from the sea, in a quiet park area is a cozy hotel Kompas 8 4. Bulgaria, Albena, 3 line, 9620 - the address of this resort. It is 40 km from Varna International Airport. Very close (300 meters) is a large stadium. Also, a huge number of attractions are available to guests of Kompas 3 *. Albena (Bulgaria) will delight you with such tourist sites:

  • water park (1 km);
  • Baltata (2 km);
  • Queen Mary's Palace (7 km);
  • golf course (8 km);
  • Golden Sands (10 km);
  • Aladzha Monastery (12 km).




Number of rooms

For guests in Hotel Kompas 3 * 169 comfortable rooms are provided. You can stay in apartments of the following categories:

  • Single rooms are chosen by independent travelers. They are quite compact and concise. They include a bed, a nightstand and a desk. The bathroom is equipped with a shower.
  • The apartments are suitable for those travelers who are accustomed to home comfort. There is a pair of one and a half beds (can be shifted), as well as folding upholstered furniture. This room features its own kitchen and dining area.
  • Double standard room can be equipped with a large or a pair of single beds. A workplace and a small corner for relaxation are provided. You can enjoy beautiful views from the furnished balcony.




Room Amenities

The guest rooms of Kompas 3 * Hotel (Albena, Bulgaria) are equipped with a minimum set of necessary amenities. In your apartment you can use the following options:

  • individually controlled air conditioning system;
  • bathroom with shower, washbasin and bath amenities;
  • landline phone for reception calls;
  • satellite television;
  • furnished balcony.

Infrastructure facilities

Developed infrastructure Hotel Kompas 3 * boasts. Bulgaria (Albena) is a modern resort, to which this institution is trying to comply. For this purpose, the following facilities are provided for guests:

  • own garden with walking paths and benches;
  • catering establishments;
  • wireless Internet in public areas (free 1 Mb);
  • paid public parking in the immediate vicinity of the hotel;
  • ATM for withdrawing cash from plastic cards;
  • luggage room for guests luggage;
  • currency exchange point;
  • reseption;
  • Internet corner in the lobby area;
  • ironing room.




Service

Many high-quality services are provided by Kompas 3 * guests. Albena (Bulgaria) will please you with this service:

  • transfer to the international airport;

Catering establishments

Delicious and satisfying meals will provide travelers Kompas 3 * Albena. Here you can visit the following places:

  • The main restaurant is a cozy dining room. Thanks to the glass walls, a lot of sunlight penetrates into the room, which creates a pleasant and light atmosphere. There is also an outdoor terrace surrounded by lots of greenery.
  • "Dorostor" is a luxurious restaurant with sea views, which invites you to get acquainted with the national Bulgarian cuisine. The concept is supported by a thematic interior, and there is also an international menu.
  • The pool bar provides guests with local drinks. Bartenders will prepare for you cocktails according to the original recipe. There is also the opportunity to eat salads and snacks.




All Inclusive Concept

Your vacation in Kompas 3 * resort will become full and carefree. Bulgaria gives you the opportunity to relax on all your favorite All Inclusive system. It includes the following items:

  • three meals a day in the main restaurant;
  • three times a week, dinner is held outdoors in a barbecue format;
  • from 11:30 to 16:30 in the bar you can eat light salads, pizza, fruit and ice cream, as well as enjoy refreshing drinks;
  • During the day, guests can enjoy local alcoholic and refreshing drinks;
  • Guests can take part in Bulgarian theme nights several times a week.

The all-inclusive concept is valid at the hotel until 23:00. After the specified time, you need to pay extra for drinks and food.

additional information

Before traveling, it will be useful for guests to familiarize themselves with the rules of accommodation at the selected hotel. So, speaking about this institution, it is worth paying attention to the following information:

  • at 14:00 and 12:00, respectively, check-in and eviction of guests;
  • Children under 2 years old are provided with free cots;
  • Pets are allowed at the hotel upon prior request and at an additional cost;
  • there is an opportunity to pay for received services in non-cash form;
  • birthday people receive a gift in the form of a dessert;
  • newlyweds who have presented a marriage certificate are entitled to a romantic room decoration, a bottle of champagne or a plate of fruit (at the discretion of the administration).




Positive reviews

A lot of positive moments and vivid memories gives travelers Kompas 3 * (Bulgaria). Reviews note the following advantages of this resort:

  • quite convenient location relative to urban infrastructure;
  • the hotel is in a quiet place, where there are no noisy clubs and entertainment venues;
  • good enough food (there are a lot of dishes, they are delicious, cooked at home);
  • if any products at the distribution in the dining room run out, their stocks are immediately replenished;
  • a good children's pool with a comfortable temperature and a lot of inflatable toys;
  • despite the fact that not far from the sea, those who wish can take advantage of the free shuttle service;
  • the food in the hotel is formed mainly from "healthy" food;
  • the staff is quite sociable and pleasant;
  • cleaning is done only when guests are not in the room (if you want to relax in the room, no one will knock and break in);
  • the door is opened using a modern magnetic card;
  • the restaurant's menu has a lot of fresh fruit from local farms;
  • Each room has a cozy furnished balcony with a beautiful view;
  • there are many coniferous trees around, which makes the air clean and fragrant;
  • mostly immigrants from the CIS countries rest in the hotel, and therefore you can always find an interesting company;
  • The main restaurant also has an outdoor terrace.




Negative reviews

Unfortunately, this hotel is not without significant drawbacks. So, from the negative reviews you can get the following information:

  • the upholstery of sofas in the lobby is very shabby, and in some places there are even holes through which foam rubber is visible;
  • many tourists go around the hotel (including the restaurant) in bathing suits alone, and the staff do not make any comments to them about this;
  • there is not a single ramp on the hotel territory that would contribute to a comfortable rest for the disabled, as well as guests with young children who are carried in wheelchairs;
  • only one elevator, next to which queues constantly gather;
  • there is practically no animation, and therefore it is rather boring on the territory (and especially in the evenings);
  • despite the fact that wireless internet is declared, in fact it practically does not work (and if a signal is present, then it is almost impossible to connect to it);
  • outdated interior (reminiscent of an old Soviet sanatorium);
  • there is practically no infrastructure for walking and entertainment around the hotel;
  • constant interruptions with hot water (especially in the evenings, when all guests want to use the shower);
  • parasols and sun loungers on the beach are available only at an additional cost;
  • the administration is reluctant to solve the problems that guests have during their stay;
  • poor-quality coffee in the restaurant;
  • instead of juices, guests are served concentrated powder drinks;
  • the rooms are very dirty, and the maids will not try, even if you leave them a tip;
  • in the apartments located on the ground floor, you can see cockroaches;
  • employees carry out harassment of insects in a hotel without warning the guests about this, which can be hazardous to health;
  • the hotel needs major repairs;
  • little entertainment for children (only a pool with slides and inflatable toys deserves attention);
  • to the sea you need to go down the stairs, and going up will be problematic (especially for the elderly and sick people);
  • towels are changed only if they are very wet and dirty;
  • information from reception staff has to be literally pulled out by ticks (and no information booklets are provided in the rooms);
  • old TVs with poor image quality;
  • pool too deep (people who swim poorly would like smaller areas).

Compass is a great budget vacation option at one of the most popular resorts in Bulgaria. It is worth noting that the institution is quite old, which leads to low prices, in addition, the hotel is removed from the sea. But this can not be clearly considered a disadvantage. On the slope of the mountain, where the Compass is located, there are many coniferous trees that give off a pleasant aroma and create a cool shade.
