Principle of operation

As you can see, fitness bracelets have a lot of advantages. How do they work?

Outside, manufacturers make such gadgets such that they give the owner a minimum of discomfort. The inside of the fitness bracelet is a small electronic board with various sensors attached to it. All elements are enclosed in a plastic, often waterproof case.

Accelerometer

One of the sensors located in the fitness bracelet is an element in the form of two small electric boards with a counterweight between them and the electric discharge. At a time when a person is at rest, the accelerometer is not working. But from the moment when the owner of the fitness bracelet is physically active, the counterweight, which is located in the middle between the boards, begins to contact them. This allows the gadget to create a spatial picture of ongoing movements.

Those who are wondering what is the best fitness bracelet to buy for themselves should look into the gadget's instructions. In some modern models of such devices, triaxial accelerometers are installed. Unlike standard sensors, they are able to track the movement of its owner and its acceleration relative to three coordinate axes at once.

Heart rate monitor

It is very important for a person to monitor the frequency of his heartbeat. This allows you to most effectively apply physical activity. By controlling our pulse zones, we are also able to significantly improve the body's response to the manifestation of activity. For example, if the main goal of morning runs is losing weight, then the heart should beat with an approximate frequency of 130 beats per minute. With a slower or faster heart rate, fat burning will be ineffective.

The principle of operation of such a sensor is based on reading the readings of two electrodes. With their help, the potential difference during heart beats is recorded. The received data, as well as during the operation of the accelerometer, is received wirelessly by the smartphone.

Which fitness bracelet with heart rate monitor is better? The following parameters directly affect the accuracy of the readings of this gadget:

  • good contact with the skin (the bracelet should fit snugly on it, that is, have a curved shape and small size);
  • device height (it is desirable that the sensor stands out a little from the bracelet and is not buried in it);
  • the ability to rotate the heart rate monitor to the back of the wrist (this parameter is important for obtaining the most accurate readings).

Pressure measurement

Which fitness bracelet is best for older people and hypertensives? Judging by the feedback from users, the smart gadget is most suitable for them, allowing you to measure not only the pulse, but also blood pressure. In this case, the acquisition will not only be an attractive sports accessory. Fitness bracelet with pressure measurement is also a medical device that will allow you to monitor the state of the body in real mode. The use of such devices is also recommended for athletes and those who seek a healthy lifestyle. You can apply it in the gym, conducting cardio workouts. In this case, a fitness bracelet with pressure measurement will help to control the intensity of the received physical loads, adjusting them based on the capabilities and needs of the body.

Can we talk about the accuracy of the readings of the tonometers installed on such gadgets? If we consider the principle of operation of fitness bracelets, then their measurement of pressure is somewhat different from that which takes place in classical medical devices. Smart device sensors detect the speed of the pulse wave. In this case, the heartbeat frequency is measured, and the received information is subject to analysis. It corresponds to the truth in 80% of cases. At the same time, there is also a certain error of results, ranging from 10 to 15 millimeters of mercury. Thus, the accuracy of the obtained pressure data using a fitness bracelet is lower than with the same measurements performed using a medical device. But all this is fully compensated by the ease of use and the ability to check pressure without outside help.
