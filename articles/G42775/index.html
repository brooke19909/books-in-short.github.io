The simplest product

If you don‚Äôt have much time, but still want to create an interesting craft, this option is suitable. Because it is performed simply and quickly, but it turns out very beautifully.

Necessary materials:

  • flower pot;
  • small pebbles, beads or shells;
  • small brush with stiff bristles;

Working process:

  1. To make the original flower pot with your own hands, you just need to glue the surface of the prepared flower pot with glue.

  3. Leave the craft to dry for 5-6 hours.

For wine cork collectors

A rather unusual look is a self-made flower pot, the photo of which is presented below.

  • wooden corks from wine ;

  • awl;

Working process:

  1. To create such a miracle, you should make holes in the middle of all traffic jams with an awl in advance.

  3. On finished "kebabs" pour glue on the beginning and the end. This is required so that the plugs do not slip and break the structure.

Very original and somewhat extravagant and noble look vases for flowers that look like they are made of fabric. Although in reality this is only half true.

The most difficult thing that can cause a lot of questions for the fair sex is how to make a cement mortar. Therefore, we will begin our step-by-step instruction with an explanation of this point.

Stage Two

Working process:

  1. In reality, there is no difficulty in this matter. You just need to mix water and cement in equal proportions. Then mix everything thoroughly until smooth.
  2. The product must be started immediately, because otherwise the solution may freeze. And we won‚Äôt do anything with him.
  3. So, we take the prepared jar and put it in the place where it will not interfere and can then dry dry.

Cache-pot "Marine option"

Another very simple idea. It requires a minimum of time. But the product looks quite worthy.

Working process:

  4. Dry the finished craft for six hours.
