Hotel Ambassador 3 * (Golden Sands, Bulgaria): photos and reviews of tourists

Bulgaria attracts tourists with an inexpensive but quality vacation. Resorts annually host a huge number of tourists from different countries. Golden Sands has been considered the most popular of them for many years. Here along the coast you can find first-class hotels, and modest "triples". Tourist complex Ambassador 3 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) is a budget hotel, so it is often chosen by thrifty people. What can he offer them?

Location and General Information

The construction of the hotel was completed back in 1968, when the resort was actively built up. Initially, it was used as a vacation spot for the political elite of Bulgaria. After the fall of the socialist regime, the hotel was renovated and rebuilt, equipped for the needs of ordinary tourists. Now every summer she hosts a lot of travelers. The last renovation of all premises here was completed in 2002. The complex has 190 rooms, which are located in a four-story building. The hotel accepts tourists with young children. You can come here with pets, but their accommodation is paid separately. In this case, before issuing a voucher, you need to get an individual permission from the administration.





Hotel Ambassador 3 * (Golden Sands) is built on the second beach line, but the distance to the sea is only 50 meters. It is located in the southern part of the resort. Next to it is the Riviera National Park. Near the hotel, tourists can also find many shops, fruit stalls and souvenir shops. Varna is the nearest major city, which is located 19 km from the resort. The international airport is 25 km away. Taxis can be reached in just 10 minutes. Transfer by tourist bus will take more time, but it will cost much cheaper.

Number of rooms and its equipment

The rooms in this budget hotel Ambassador 3 * Standard (Golden Sands) are simple and small in size. Do not count on modern repairs or luxury in decoration. Standard apartments are designed for two adults, to which two children can be additionally shared. Room size is 19 square meters. m. Most of them are equipped with their own small balcony. The windows overlook either the sea coast, or the garden and the parking lot. Employees clean the rooms every day, complement cosmetic and toiletries. They also change linen and towels several times a week.





The equipment of the hotel rooms Ambassador 3 * (Golden Sands) is also quite modest. Opposite the bed is usually an old TV with a large kinescope. Cable TV with Russian channels is connected to it. For a fee, you can use the phone or connect wireless Internet. Guests can adjust the temperature in the room using an individual air conditioner. A minibar can also be delivered to the room upon request, but it will not be included in the price. The bathroom has a set of cosmetics, while a hairdryer is issued at the reception.

Hotel facilities

The standard service is offered to its guests by the Ambassador 3 * hotel (Golden Sands). Reviews of tourists note the presence of free car parking, which is undoubtedly an advantage for travelers traveling around the country in their own car. Parking is guarded around the clock. The reception is also open day and night. At any time, tourists can exchange currency here, book an excursion or rent a car. An airport shuttle is available for a fee. The reception also has a safe and luggage storage.





The hotel has its own small shop selling souvenirs, food, tobacco and alcohol. There is also a hairdresser. For an additional fee, the administrator can call a doctor. Each reception is paid separately. Tourists can always take dirty clothes to the laundry.

Leisure options

A huge selection of leisure options offers tourists the Golden Sands resort . Ambassador 3 * also prepared its own entertainment program for guests. The complex has a spacious outdoor pool. Nearby is a sunbathing area. On a public beach for a fee you can rent equipment for water sports or play volleyball with other tourists. The hotel also has an indoor pool with mineral water.





The pride of the hotel is its own spa center , which offers a large selection of wellness programs. There are courses of balneotherapy (mineral water treatment), aromatherapy , paraffin therapy, thalassotherapy (treatment with sea water and algae), as well as mud therapy and anti-stress programs. Tourists can also pay for massage, visit the sauna and jacuzzi. For outdoor activities, the hotel has a gym, equipment for playing table tennis and billiards. You can also rent a bicycle, play chess and volleyball.

Catering

When making a tour, guests can choose and pay for one of the three food systems. Hotel Ambassador 3 * (Golden Sands) offers the following concepts:

  • breakfast only;
  • half board (breakfast and dinner);
  • all inclusive.




The main restaurant of the hotel serves breakfast, lunch and dinner every day. Meals are held on a strict schedule. In between, tourists can eat cookies, sandwiches with tea or coffee. Chefs prepare dishes of both European and Bulgarian cuisine. The all-inclusive system also includes locally-produced soft drinks and spirits. Their guests can take in the pool bar. They are poured into plastic glasses. After 22:30 all drinks are served only for a fee. The cafe in the lobby offers tourists not only drinks and snacks , but also desserts, fresh pastries, ice cream and fruits.

Conditions for relaxing with children

For tourists who want to have a budget vacation with children, the Ambassador 3 * hotel (Golden Sands) will be a great option for a vacation. Children under two years stay here for free. In addition, parents can request a cradle for the baby in the room. The restaurant always has baby chairs available for free. For a fee, chefs can develop a special children's menu. Also, tourists at any time can call a nanny in the room and leave the child with her.





There are no children's or adult animations at the hotel, so parents must take care of organizing the entertainment themselves. Near the adult pool is a small "paddling pool" for kids. On the grass nearby is a playground equipped with swings, a slide and a sandbox.

Positive opinions

It is always useful to read reviews of other guests before issuing a ticket. Many shortcomings, as a rule, are not indicated in the description of the hotel. Information about it may also be outdated. Therefore, opinions help tourists to find out the real situation. Hotel Ambassador 3 * (Golden Sands, Bulgaria) gets mixed reviews . The guests noted the following advantages:

  • A huge balcony in the room, on which it is quite possible to sunbathe, dine.
  • Good location. Very close to the sea and a walking street. Nearby there is a seafood restaurant, a bus stop and shops.
  • There are no lines in the hotel restaurant, and dining tables and chairs are enough for all tourists.
  • The staff quickly solves the problems. If you did not like the number, then it will be replaced.
  • The hotel is quite quiet, so you can sleep with the windows open all night. Usually adults and elderly Europeans stop here, so drunk people almost never meet.




Negative opinions

Unfortunately, some tourists were not satisfied with the rest at the Ambassador hotel (Bulgaria, Golden Sands). Reviews often describe the shortcomings of the hotel, which spoiled their impression of a vacation. We list the main disadvantages of living here:

  • The rooms are in terrible condition. There is dust everywhere, trash from former guests. Tourists who settle in rooms often have to tidy up and change their bedding on their own.
  • Air conditioning is not working. It produces warm rather than chilled air, which also has a rather unpleasant odor of dampness.
  • Monotonous breakfasts. The buffet is not replenished. The food is taken apart very quickly, so if you are late for an appointment, you can stay hungry.
  • The mineral pool is in need of repair. The water in it is cold, around the dirt and dust, pieces of broken tiles are lying around. Accordingly, swimming in it is simply impossible.
  • The hotel area is heavily polluted. Garbage, plastic glasses and cigarette butts are not cleaned. The lawn is also not looked after.
  • The staff can be rude to guests. Many tourists note that the administration scornfully treats tourists from Russia.

conclusions

We can say that the Ambassador 3 * hotel (Golden Sands), the photo of which can be seen in this article, is suitable for an extremely budget holiday. Often guests recommend choosing another hotel.
