Grand Hotel Plaza 4 *, Egypt, Hurghada: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Combining an advantageous location on one of the private beaches, Grand Plaza Hotel 4 * (Egypt, Hurghada) offers all its visitors, without exception, hospitality of the East and affordable luxury, where a wide selection of facilities for recreation and entertainment is complemented by first-class service that can satisfy even the most moody and demanding guests. You will feel like at home, just add to this comfortable, clean, cozy and well-equipped rooms with balconies, which offer magnificent views of the Red Sea or a large swimming pool.

Hotel Location





It is worth noting that the Grand Plaza Hotel 4 * (Hurghada, Egypt) is widely known and popular not only among Russian vacationers, but also among guests from all over the world. One of the priority factors when choosing a place of residence is its location. This hotel is located directly on the Red Sea coast, on the territory of a private beach, which is important. This provides some privacy and a small number of people.

The airport is located close enough to the hotel (only 5 km), so the trip will be short and pleasant. You can independently explore the city itself, since the center of Hurghada is only 10 km away, and therefore evening walks and interesting excursions are provided to everyone. Fans of shopping will also like this place, because only a 15-20 minute drive from the hotel is one of the largest shopping centers in Egypt, Grand Mall.

Hurghada, hotel Grand Plaza Hotel 4 *: description





The building was rebuilt in a classical style and consists of three floors, there is an elevator. In total, the hotel has 230 comfortable rooms:

  • standard (with garden or sea view);
  • family (windows overlook the green zone);
  • Suite
  • for people with disabilities.

All rooms of Grand Hotel Plaza 4 * are characterized by a beautiful interior, executed in bright colors, contributing to relaxation and a first-class rest. Each of them has its own bathroom or shower, hairdryer, toiletries. For guests' convenience, a telephone with international line access, a safe and air conditioning are installed. The apartments also have a TV with two channels broadcasting in Russian, a minibar with free mineral water (one bottle daily, and two on the day of arrival). From the balcony or terrace (depending on the type of room), guests enjoy views of the Red Sea coast, so many choose Grand Plaza Hotel (1 line) 4 *. Hurghada (Egypt), despite the proximity of the desert, will please you with an abundance of greenery. The garden at the hotel can be called a truly paradise, there are many romantic fountains and exotic plants.

Restaurants on site





There are three restaurants on site. The main one is on the second floor and is called La Palma. The stylishly decorated room is spread out under a transparent dome and panoramic windows over the wall with views of the hotel, the garden and the beach. It will offer you to taste both traditional and national oriental dishes, as well as European gastronomic delights.

The second restaurant in the Grand Hotel Plaza 4 * is called Catamaran and is located right next to the open-air pool (pictured), which adds to it a certain charm and charm. An ideal place for dinner in a romantic setting, when dusk falls on the city, and the hot sun sets over the horizon.

On the second floor next to the pool you can also visit the Italian restaurant Casa Mia. Its tables are also located in the open air, and this allows guests to enjoy the evening coolness in a pleasant relaxing atmosphere.

Supply system

Basically, the Grand Plaza Hotel 4 * (Egypt, Hurghada), whose rating is high among mainly domestic tourists, offers its customers an all-inclusive system. This leads to a higher tariff rate for the rooms, but do not forget that it includes not only food, but also drinks in the bars and restaurants of the hotel (some restrictions are allowed). What is meant by this term? Everything - this is breakfast, including late (second), lunch and dinner according to the familiar buffet system and local drinks. The All Inclusive complex may be different: luxurious or somewhat more modest.

Entertainment and sports

Beach and tourist vacations without entertainment are simply unthinkable, and they are more accessible, the better. The hotel offers its many guests free exercise in regular and water aerobics in the company of professional trainers, spend time on the tennis court, play water polo, darts or volleyball on the spacious beach, and also go windsurfing (on the day thirty minutes), provided that you There is a special certificate confirming the availability of training and experience.

For an additional fee, you can visit a relaxing massage, a jacuzzi and a sauna, a daily disco in the territory of the Hotel Grand Plaza Resort 4 * (admission is free, you will have to pay for drinks), billiards, ride on a banana, catamaran or canoe.

Free entry and no time limit for training in the gym, which is an indisputable advantage, because inactive rest and a rich buffet should somehow be compensated.

The hotel has two spacious pools (one in the winter with heating), equipped for children and people with disabilities. On the contrary, at the Hotel Grand Plaza Resort, you can ride for free on the water slides.

And of course, friendly and cheerful animators work for the guests, ready to make your stay even more eventful, and in the evenings live music and special evening shows are organized.

Nearby Attractions

No matter how pleasant the passive rest is, at some point each of the hotel guests will want to go beyond it and see what is located around this wonderful place. Be sure, it will pleasantly surprise you. For convenience, you can rent a car or bike.

Not far from the Grand Plaza Hotel (ex. Grand Azur) 4 * is Sindbad Club Aqua Park, which is part of the hotel of the same name. It is equipped at the highest level with a huge swimming pool in the center and two towers, an artificial beach and imitation of waves. The advantage is that for safety reasons, the children's area is separated from the adult. The entry price is quite affordable, given that it includes drinks with snacks and is $ 25.

It is also worth paying attention to the aquapark Titanic, located next to the hotel of the same name. This is truly a huge entertainment complex, considered one of the best in Egypt. It offers visitors about 20 different attractions for every taste and age.

Large aquarium in Hurghada

It is located near the airport, approximately 9-10 km from the hotel. Not too close for a walk, however, it is quite accessible if you have a rented car or buy a sightseeing tour and it is somewhat more complicated with public transport. However, it's worth it. The aquarium is unique, it is the first not only in Egypt but throughout the world to assemble an extensive collection of fauna and flora of the Red Sea.

The complex includes spacious aquarium tunnels with fresh and sea water, as well as an open-air zoo and swimming pools. The whole territory is divided into certain thematic zones, exotic inhabitants can be seen not only live, but also watch documentaries on a large 3D screen. A walk along a suspension bridge, a small maze or cave will be fascinating and unusual.

Who is the hotel recommended?





First of all, relying on guest reviews and owners' recommendations, it is worth noting that Grand Hotel Plaza 4 * is the best for family and romantic getaways. All rooms are designed for children, and if necessary you will be provided with an extra cot or chair for free.

For those who are going to go on a honeymoon or just spend time away from everyone, the hotel offers special evenings and romantic programs (dinner on the beach or in the gazebo on the garden accompanied by live music). A large territory and a variety of entertainments allow everyone to find something of their own and not get bored.

Unfortunately, the hotel does not offer its guests pet-friendly services.
