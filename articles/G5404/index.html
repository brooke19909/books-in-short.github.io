Sea "Seagull" - Cigal Hurghada Hotel on the Red Sea

Ancient Egypt has long attracted the attention of not only researchers and historians. The development of tourism in this country was due to the huge flow of people coming. Ancient civilization and historical sights attracted some here, others - the hot sun and the gentle warm sea. But for those and for others it was necessary to create favorable conditions for staying in the country. Despite the fact that in Egypt itself the level of the economy is not high enough, the Egyptian resort areas are distinguished by developed infrastructure and excellent quality of service.

Dining at the hotel will delight even the most demanding tourists. The buffet in the Cleopatra restaurant offers a huge selection of dishes. Although the hotel area is not very large, but there is always enough entertainment. Animations with contests throughout the day and evening shows will fit perfectly into your daily routine. A Turkish bath, a jacuzzi and a sauna will help you relax.



For children, there are also entertainment that will fill with a variety of impressions all day. A playground, a shallow pool and a mini club will not let the kids get bored and allow their parents to calmly go about their business.

The beach is not far away, as the SeGal Hurghada hotel is located on the first coastline. Like the pool, the beach provides free sun loungers and umbrellas. Beach towels are also issued absolutely free.



The Red Sea is attractive not only for bathers. You have the prospect of learning to dive, windsurf, go fishing on a boat and ride a catamaran, banana and water skiing.



If you do not want to break away from work and during your vacation, then specifically for business people, the Cigal Hurghada Hotel has provided access to the World Wide Web in an Internet cafe and two conference rooms.



The hotel is perfect for those who come actively and have fun. Young people will be delighted with local entertainment, both on site and outside the hotel. A night disco and good music will make you have fun from the heart.

In historical places you can ride with excursions from your operator. Visit the famous Luxor Temple, ancient pyramids, museums and mosques.

The Cigal Hurghada Hotel has been open for tourists since 2000 and its staff is well trained in the Russian language. So the language barrier will not be a problem on vacation, and you can solve all problems with the staff without resorting to the services of guides.

The season in Hurghada lasts all year round. But the best months to stay in Egypt are October, November and May.
