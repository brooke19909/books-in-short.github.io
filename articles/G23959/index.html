Hera Park Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Side) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Side is one of the most popular and lively resorts in the Republic of Turkey. In addition to a developed tourist infrastructure and a wide hotel base, it is also famous for its title as an open-air museum. After all, a lot of historical and cultural attractions of Turkey are concentrated here. If you want to have a great rest in Side and are looking for a comfortable, but inexpensive hotel to stay, pay attention to Hera Park Hotel 4 *. Next, we will introduce you to this hotel in more detail, and also find out what impression the tourists from our country have left here.





Where is

The hotel in question has a very good location. So, from the city center of Side it is only one kilometer away. Thus, all the entertainment and infrastructure of a major tourist center are within walking distance for hotel guests. The distance to Manavgat is five kilometers. The nearest airport to the hotel is in Antalya, 65 kilometers from Side. Thus, after arriving in this air harbor, the road to the Gera Park Hotel will take you just over an hour. And along the way, you can not only enjoy the local scenery from the windows of a car or bus, but also listen to the entertaining story of the guide about the features of this region (in case the transfer will be arranged for you by a tour operator). The hotel in question also has its own magnificent sandy beach. It is only 150 meters away. There are both sun loungers and parasols, as well as equipment for water activities.

Hotel Policies

In accordance with the internal rules of Hera Park Hotel 4 * (Turkey), check-in for arriving guests begins at two in the afternoon. If you arrive at the hotel earlier, if you have free rooms, they will try to check in immediately. On the day of departure, the occupied room should be vacated before noon. You can pay for accommodation and use of additional services at the hotel both in cash and with plastic cards.





Accommodation for family guests

The four-star Hera Park Hotel allows guests with children of all ages. At the same time, babies under two years old will be provided with a playpen, and older children will have an extra bed. Please note that there is no charge for children under seven years old on an extra bed. For older children you will have to pay 50% of the amount of one person per day. Each room of the hotel can only have one extra or a cot. If you need such a service, you should notify the hotel administration in advance, and then wait for confirmation from it.

Accommodation for guests traveling with pets

If you are used to traveling abroad in the company of your four-legged pet, be sure to pay attention to this item. So, in accordance with the rules of Hera Park Hotel 4 *, pets are not allowed on site. Therefore, you just have to choose another hotel, more loyal to tourists with animals, or leave your favorite home for the duration of your vacation.





Number of rooms

As already mentioned, the hotel in question has 211 rooms located in an eight-story building equipped with elevators. The housing stock here is represented by standard type apartments. You can choose a room for double or triple occupancy. There is a balcony. Carpeted flooring is in the rooms . Rooms are cleaned on a daily basis. Bed linen and towels are changed three times a week. In addition, all hotel guests have the opportunity at any time to order a room service.

Nutrition

The restaurant of the Hera Park Hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis. Three times a day, guests of the hotel will enjoy a buffet with a variety of dishes, desserts and snacks of both national and international cuisine. In addition, during the day you can grab a bite to eat at the snack bar and enjoy a drink in the bars.





Beach vacation

Only 150 meters from the hotel there is a beach belonging to Hera Park Hotel 4 *. The beaches in Side are sandy, with a gentle entry into the water. Thanks to this, it will be very comfortable for tourists arriving on vacation with children. The hotel's own beach has sun loungers and parasols, as well as equipment for water sports.

Entertainment and infrastructure

Hera Park Hotel 4 * has at its disposal all the necessary infrastructure for a comfortable stay and excellent relaxation. So, the reception here is open around the clock, and administrators speak not only Turkish and English, but also Russian. Therefore, at any time you can contact the reception for help. The hotel also provides the following services: room service, transfer, car rental, tour desk services, currency exchange, dry cleaning and laundry, fax and photocopying. On the territory of "Gera Park" there is a large outdoor pool with water slides, as well as an indoor pool and a tank designed for safe bathing of children. There is also a sun terrace with sun loungers, parasols and a bar. In addition, hotel guests have the opportunity to visit a beauty salon, hammam, sauna, massage, play table tennis, billiards, darts, do water sports on the beach.





Cost of living

As for the price of accommodation in the hotel in question, it is very attractive. So, a seven-day stay in the midst of the holiday season in July and August will cost you from 41 thousand rubles for a double room and from 47 thousand rubles for a triple room.
