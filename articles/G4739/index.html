Hotel "Beshtau", Pyatigorsk: description of rooms, service, reviews

The famous Pyatigorsk is a resort of federal significance, which is called a real natural museum of mineral waters. It was in this city in the Stavropol Territory that the history of domestic balneology was laid: in 1863, the first Russian balneological society was organized here.

The medical base of the resort consists of more than forty sources, differing in their chemical composition and temperature of healing water. The tangible therapeutic effect for which people from all over Russia come here all year round is provided by the influence of the foothill climate, water procedures and the special treatment method used here, which provides for dosed physical activities (walking, climbing in mountainous areas on certain marked routes).
