Guest House Ilmu Vieno

In order for a vacation in Karelia to succeed, you need only three things: safety, comfort and, of course, adventure. You will find all this in the guest house Ilmu Vieno, which is located in the village of Vidana. This recreation center is in a very convenient location. If the child is still too small, and you are afraid to leave for the wilderness, then Ilmu Vieno is what you need. From here to Petrozavodsk is only twenty-five kilometers. Right behind the house you will see a large field and forest. So here urban comfort, rural exoticism and unforgettable Karelian nature are successfully combined here . Hospitable hosts will lodge you in a cozy cottage, around - well-groomed large area where you can arrange picnics. Lunches in nature will be liked not only by your children, but also by yourself. The forest is just a few minutes walk. So your child will not get tired at all and will gladly help to collect mushrooms and berries, which are abundant here. If you want to see local sights, then the owners of the estate will arrange an excursion around the area: Kizhi island, Kivach waterfall, and Martial waters. Not far from the recreation center there is a private zoo, a visit to which will give the kids a lot of fun.





The recreation center in Medvezhyegorsk

An unforgettable vacation can be arranged on the shores of Lake Onega, at the recreation center in Medvezhyegorsk. Here you can swim in the crystal clear water and sunbathe on the sand dunes that stretch around the reservoir. Nearby is a forest in which you and your children can engage in "quiet hunting": there are a lot of cranberries and lingonberries. And fishing enthusiasts can meet the dawn with a fishing rod in their hands. Does the soul require outdoor activities? Try river rafting. Rafting in Karelia with children is a very fun, unforgettable adventure. Experienced instructors will do everything to make the trip safe. But there is an age limit. On such a trip, children who have reached the age of ten are taken. And, of course, the route will be the easiest. More experienced tourists can offer more difficult routes. Fans of terrestrial extreme sports also have something to do - exciting trips on quads will not leave anyone indifferent. You can also visit a rope amusement park called Sherwood. Rest in Karelia with a child will be truly unforgettable if you go to a recreation center in Medvezhyegorsk.
