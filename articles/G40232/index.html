BBK Technique: LED TVs. Reviews and specifications

BBK, whose televisions are known worldwide, has recently released a new line - LED models. Thanks to the beautiful appearance and impeccable bright details, the TV will fit into any design of the room. Under the BBK brand, Vanilla TVs are available in different edging colors (it is made in the shade of champagne), Mandarina is characterized by a predominance of orange, and for Avokado - green.
