How to sew a hare? Material selection, pattern, work procedure

Many needlewomen who have small children are concerned about how to sew a hare. This article introduces several sewing techniques. Let's talk about which fabrics are best used for making a soft toy with your own hands. A detailed workshop on tailoring toys from fur, as well as the technology for making an eared friend from a sock, will be presented here.





Materials and Tools

We recommend that you keep all items on hand before starting work. They are simply necessary for work.

Cardboard or polyethylene for patterns. The most convenient material for patterns is dense polyethylene. Through it, the material is perfectly visible, and it is impossible to make a mistake in the location of the pattern. This is especially important for fabrics with a pattern. Patterns made of polyethylene do not tear and do not wrinkle. You can store them in a file for documents. If you do not have the opportunity to purchase such polyethylene, then replace it with cardboard.

Marker for marking the material. The best choice is a disappearing marker. As the alcohol evaporates, the image disappears. You don‚Äôt need to wash anything up and worry that the marks will spoil the appearance of the product over which you piled so much. The only negative of the disappearing marker is the high price. Yet it is a professional tool. If you are not sure what you will sew in the future, then you can do with a fine or graphite pencil.

Professional tools: roller knife, ruler, rug. At first, it‚Äôs enough for you to have a school ruler, a centimeter tape and ordinary scissors in the arsenal (you can use stationery). If you want to sew soft toys in the future, you can purchase professional seamstress tools:

  • tailor scissors;
  • zigzag scissors (so that the edges do not crumble);
  • roller knife (for quick cutting of circles and ovals).

Thread

It is important that they are of high quality: strong and do not fade. Since we will be sewing a stuffed toy, when stuffing, the seams will stretch. Seams should be strong, but elastic. To sew a hare, get durable polyester threads for machine stitching.

How to sew a hare and how to fill it

A soft toy will need to be filled with something. Consider the main types of fillers. We will conditionally divide them into two groups: natural and synthetic. Take care of this in advance.

Natural fillers

Vata. The most affordable and cheapest type of filler. In addition, it is completely natural, and nothing bad will happen if the child even swallows a small part. A huge minus is that the cotton material absorbs moisture. Over time, mold may appear in the toy, which is completely unacceptable for children's things.

Significant disadvantages you can find when stuffing. Experienced craftswomen are well aware of them, but for beginner needlewomen they can ruin the mood.

It is hard to stuff a toy with a complicated construction with cotton wool: elbow bends, inaccessible and bottlenecks. After washing, a thing stuffed with cotton wool looks awful, natural material gets lumpy. It will not be possible to fluff it inside the toy - you will have to rip the product.

Wool. This is a good kind of filler for a toy sewn from dense dark fabric or knitwear. Through the thin light fabric, all the bumps on the body of a soft friend will be visible. Many needlewomen for this purpose use the remains of woolen threads. Fluff them and stuff products.

High-quality, but not expensive filler - sliver . This is peeled sheep wool. A huge minus is that it is sold in rolls. Acquiring it to make one toy is unprofitable. Buy a sliver if you want to start producing expensive and high-quality products on stream.

Working with sliver is not easy. First you need to fluff the material. You need to fill the toy slowly, gently pushing the filler with a stick. Do not wash the product. Can be dry cleaned or frozen for disinfection in the freezer. You can put the toy in a bag with soda, shake it well, remove the product and vacuum it.

Herbs. If you want to sew a toy for sleeping, then a grass filler will be a great choice. You can collect them yourself or buy in a pharmacy.

Synthetic fillers

Sintepuh. With this filler you can achieve a perfectly flat surface. It is very light and soft. The material was invented specifically for stuffing toys, so it has practically no drawbacks, and it's easy to work with.

Synthetic winterizer. Inexpensive filler, which can be found in any needlework store, it is sold in rolls. Before work, you need to tear it with your hands. The material is not suitable for filling tilde dolls with thin legs and handles. Puppet girls will have ‚Äúcellulite‚Äù from this material.

Before you sew a hare, you need to choose a harmonious combination of fabric colors. If you use multiple colors, you can use the color wheel. Try not to combine cold and warm tones. Focus on how ‚Äútasty‚Äù the combination looks. Remember that a properly selected palette should be pleasing to the eye and cause emotions and a desire to touch a thing.

How to sew a hare of fur

The most ‚Äúmimic‚Äù toys are obtained from faux fur. The bunny is very easy to manufacture. Here we will take a step-by-step look at how to sew a hare. The master class is designed for beginners.

For work you will need:

  • fabric (faux fur);
  • a small piece of white fleece;
  • beads for eyes;
  • brown paint (pastel chalk);
  • cardboard for a pattern;
  • threads.

Redraw the hare pattern, which is presented in the photo below. Cut out the details from the fabric, grind them, leaving a hole for stuffing. Make cuts in rounded areas. Turn out the toy.





From white or cream fleece, make a circle for the face. Sew the spout with black thread. From the ribbon, make a scarf for the toy. Decorate it with an artificial flower.





Tint the eyes, nose, paws, ears with brown paint. So we figured out how to sew a hare. This soft toy will become your kid‚Äôs best friend!
