A water park in Tyumen has opened

Tyumen is one of the major industrial centers of Western Siberia, located far from the most promising place for a beach holiday. To many, the water park in Tyumen, and even under the open sky, seemed to the company rather absurd and commercially doubtful. Nevertheless, this happened, despite all the forecasts of local skeptics.

Water park in Tyumen opened

This extraordinary event happened last summer. The first water amusement park in the city opened in the territory belonging to the Urartu hotel and restaurant complex, on the shore of Lake Van located in the city .



It consists of five slides of different sizes and designs, three of which are designed for children of different ages, and two large are designed for adult visitors. The water park in Tyumen is located in the open air, and Tyumen, as you know, is a northern city, and there are not so many hot days in the year here. And therefore, the time of its functioning is limited to the summer months, and most of the year these five slides are doomed to play the role of abstract sculptures, made in the style of constructivism. Nevertheless, the summer months in Tyumen are quite hot, and now residents have the opportunity of such an atypical vacation for an industrial city as a visit to the water park. In the recent past, it was impossible to dream of such a thing, wealthy citizens saw water parks only on vacation in Turkey or in Egypt. In addition to attractions, a number of necessary works were done at the water park to equip the shore and deepen the reservoir. The depth here now reaches two and a half meters, which makes it safe to descend from the highest hill.


Water park in Tyumen. Prices and working hours

There is a water amusement park in the summer months without weekends and holidays. The cost of an hour stay in the aquazone is 250 rubles for adults, 200 rubles for children and 100 rubles for pensioners. On Saturday - 50 rubles more. Preschool children are free. The water park in Tyumen is open from 11 am to 9 pm on weekdays and on Sunday. And on Saturday - from 10 am to 10 pm.

This is just the beginning.

The first experience of creating a water park in Tyumen should be recognized as successful. Reviews about him in the city are very positive. Locals liked the first water park in Tyumen. A photo in memory of such an unusual beach holiday will delight people throughout the long Siberian winter.

The next natural step should be the creation in the city of a full-fledged water park under the roof, capable of receiving visitors all year round without interruption. Surely there are investors who will invest in such a project. After all, it is very promising, a water entertainment complex will quickly pay back the money invested in its creation. Very rich people live in Tyumen, and they want to relax in comfort without regard to the season and weather. But, when planning your visit here, you should remember that you will also have to pay for entering the beach area - 150 rubles for adults and 100 rubles for children and senior citizens.
