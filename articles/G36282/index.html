How to make a 3D picture with your own hands?



Picturesque paintings attract the eye not only when they are painted with oil on canvas. No less admirable are the products that have come out of the hands of needlewomen. The volumetric picture made of various improvised materials will be very effective. Suitable paper, fabric, leather, beads, ribbons.

Picture on the wall

On the wall of the house you can create a huge canvas, a sense of texture will give a layered masterpiece. Initially, the surface is cleaned of whitewash, wallpaper, paint. The image itself is putty putty. PVA glue and water-based paints are added to it. Each layer, with a thickness of not more than 1 cm, is applied gradually. It will take a week to create such a picture, but the result will amaze you and your friends.

Paper painting

A real miracle also comes out of the paper, using the application technique. Layered shapes are cut out. Lay them on top of each other. It is better to start the process by choosing an interesting picture. Print it in several copies (ten to twelve are enough). Our three-dimensional picture will depict a fabulous creature, for example a small fairy in flowers. It is advisable that copies be made on thick paper. Then you need to insert one picture into the frame. From the rest, cut out the figure of the main character and other necessary elements. From the laminate substrate, make similar parts, but smaller in size than the originals. Now the most important thing begins. A three-dimensional picture is created by gluing the cut out elements to the picture in the frame, and it is necessary to go from large to small. In some cases, you can do without a substrate. In conclusion, it is necessary to paint the sides of each glued layer and then in several steps cover the painting with acrylic varnish.

Beadwork

Embroidery is distinguished by the type of stitches - stitch or cross, by the use of materials - from ribbons or beads. If you are well acquainted with cross-stitching, then mastering the way to make three-dimensional patterns from beads will not be difficult for you. Even the patterns of the drawings and the canvas are the same. Having fixed the thread on the fabric, you can begin to create a picture. The needle must be removed from the lower left corner, pass the bead and enter into the upper right corner. Continue on. To move to a new row, the needle through the wrong side must be entered on the front side, pass it into the upper right corner of the cage and sew the beads, following from top to bottom. It will turn out just a wonderful three-dimensional picture, if you choose the scheme when choosing the color of beads. For work, ready-made embroidery kits or a picture created by yourself are suitable. It is enough to find a suitable image and break it into squares using the Paint program.





You can get acquainted with another interesting way, how to make a three-dimensional picture with the help of wire and beads. For its manufacture, the French weaving method or mosaic is useful. As a result, bright butterflies flutter on the sheet of cardboard inserted into the frame and beautiful flowers bloom. Special magazines or tips and recommendations of masters on various thematic resources will tell you more about the various types of creativity and needlework.
