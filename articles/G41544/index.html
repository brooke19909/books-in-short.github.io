Ancient Russia: culture and its features

Ancient Russia, whose culture was a vivid phenomenon in the development of the country, was famous for its beautiful monuments of architecture and literary creations. What influenced its development? How did the concept of the world change? All this needs to be understood.



Ancient Russia: culture and its features before the adoption of Christianity and after

As you know, the ancient state was subordinate to the pagan religion, as a result of which we can talk about several characteristic features of that society. Firstly, oral folk art prevailed. It was then that the epics, songs and tales began to arise. People passed from generation to generation the most important information that has survived to this day. Secondly, wooden architecture was developed. Then in Russia there were no stone buildings, but after the whole world there were strong wooden temples and huts. Thirdly, there were no written sources. Yes, before the adoption of the new faith, such monuments of art were not on the territory of our country. Fourth, there were a lot of pagan gods. Features of the culture of Ancient Russia after the adoption of Christianity have changed a lot:

  • book business appeared and great literary works began to appear actively;
  • stone architecture began to develop (the most striking example is St. Sophia Cathedral in Kiev, which was built in honor of the defeat of the Pechenegs);
  • new, non-existent, art forms appeared - fine, as well as jewelry.
