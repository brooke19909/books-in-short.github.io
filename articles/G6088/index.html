Hotel Pavlina Beach Hotel 4 * (Niforeika, Greece): overview, rooms and reviews

Peloponnese is one of the most popular areas of Greece. Every year, you can see crowds of tourists - compatriots who decided to combine a vacation on the beach with interesting excursions around the country with an amazing history. Of course, there are many hotels on the coast, and the traveler always has plenty to choose from. A good place, chosen by tourists, is the Pavlina Beach Hotel. But of course, before booking or buying a tour, you should find out some information about the selected hotel.

Brief information about the territory and location of the hotel





Of course, in the first place it is worth asking about the location. Where to look for the hotel complex Pavlina Beach Hotel 4 *? Peloponnese, the picturesque resort village of Niforeika - it is here, on a hill, just a few meters from the beach that tourists spend their holidays.

It is worth saying that the place is quite convenient, since it is easy to get from here not only to the beach, but also to the most interesting cities from a historical point of view. Travelers often travel to Delphi, Mycenae, Olympus and even Athens. You can do this yourself or purchase a guided tour. By the way, the nearest airport is nearby - it is located in the city of Araxos, at a distance of about 10 km.

The hotel area itself is very beautiful - there are small two-story buildings here, between which walking paths are laid. Greenery pleases the eye almost everywhere - there are palm trees, bright flower beds, neat lawns, shrubs, in a word, all in order to enjoy the beauty of nature and silence.

Hotel complex Hotel Pavlina Beach (Peloponnese): description and photos of rooms





This is a medium-sized hotel with 93 rooms. Naturally, tourists can choose a category suitable for them, however, for the most part they still offer standard rooms designed for 1, 2 or 3 people. There are more comfortable suites and even separate bungalows with their own veranda, which are "lost" in the middle of the garden.

Furniture in the rooms was changed not so long ago, and the equipment here is modern. You can be sure that during the holidays you will use the air conditioning system, as well as the radio, telephone and TV (there are English and local channels). The rooms have a small refrigerator. Upon request, an electric kettle is also delivered to the room.

The bathroom will please with cleanliness, as well as the constant availability of water. You can take a shower, and use a hairdryer and various toiletries. Towels are changed daily.

Meals: bars and restaurants in the hotel





On the territory of the Pavlina Beach Hotel 4 * there is a wonderful restaurant that pleases its guests with a relaxed atmosphere, good service and, of course, delicious dishes of international, Greek and Mediterranean cuisine. In addition, the tavern, decorated in a bright, national style, is constantly working. In the lobby of the hotel there is a bar where many guests relax in the evenings. And by the pool there is a small cafe where, sitting on the terrace with bamboo furniture, you can slowly sip a chilled cocktail.

As for the scheme, here, each guest can choose how and when to eat at the hotel. You can pay three meals a day at the time of booking, you can only order breakfast, or you can eat in hotel restaurants, each time paying the bill.

Relax on the beach: leisure and amenities on the beach





As already mentioned, the cozy Pavlina Beach Hotel is located on a hill almost by the sea. You can go down to the shore using a special staircase. The beach is covered with sand and pebbles, so it is worth taking special shoes with you. The entrance to the sea is cleared, and cleaned ashore daily. By the way, the coast is rocky, and you can hide from the heat in the shadow of the rocks.

Of course, tourists can use sun loungers, mattresses and sun umbrellas. If you wish, you can rent a boat, ride a water bike or take a romantic boat trip.

Additional service

Despite the remoteness from the city, Pavlina Beach Hotel can offer an additional service that will make your vacation much more comfortable. Here, most tourists travel by rented vehicles, which can be left in the hotel underground parking.

You can rent a small safe from the administrator - here your things will be in complete safety, as there is constant video surveillance behind the counter. By the way, the service costs a little. You can use free internet in the lobby, but you already have to pay for access to the World Wide Web in your room. A laundry service, as well as a shop where various souvenirs and household items are available to guests, offer their services on site.

By the way, there is also a computer corner, where at the same time you can use some office equipment, as well as send (or receive) a fax.

Hotel Pavlina Beach Hotel 4 * (Greece): entertainment





Of course, most tourists arrive at the hotel to relax on the beach. Nevertheless, you can make your leisure time more diverse at the hotel. Especially for relaxing in the courtyard, a swimming pool with clean cool water is equipped, next to which you can relax while lying on a comfortable sun lounger.

At your service - a games room, as well as a good collection of films. You can have fun playing billiards or ping pong. And for the tourists created a platform for basketball and volleyball. Fans of tennis will also be pleased, as the hotel has its own court. And of course, do not forget about excursion vacations, because only a few hours away are ancient cities, where civilization originated many hundreds of years ago.

Are there any additional services for the child?





As already mentioned, the hotel has rooms of different capacities, so families with children feel quite comfortable. The restaurant's menu is varied - you can pick up food for the child, and they cook deliciously here.

If we talk about entertainment, then little tourists have a great time on the beach. And on the territory of the hotel there is a pool of small depth, where kids love to splash around and learn to swim. At the disposal of children - and a spacious outdoor area. There are swings, slides, sandpits, sports equipment and other attractions that will provide your child with fun.

What do tourists say? Reviews and opinions of former guests

What do former guests think about the hotel complex Hotel Pavlina Beach? People leave positive reviews. Immediately it is worth saying that this hotel is ideal for a relaxing beach holiday - lovers of peace and unity with nature will be delighted. But fans of beach fun and nightclubs may be slightly disappointed, as parties are rarely held here, and there are not so many discos nearby.

The rooms are standard - there is the necessary furniture and appliances. By the way, they are really kept clean. By the way, the staff here are very welcoming and do everything to make hotel guests feel comfortable.

The beach is located close to the hotel, and the descent to the coast is not so scary - the steps are comfortable and a wonderful seascape opens from the hill, which just asks for a picture. Travelers are satisfied with their stay here, which is why they recommend the Pavlina Beach Hotel, because comfort and quality of service are at a high level, but accommodation prices are affordable.
