Hotel Kata Sea Breeze Resort 3 *, Phuket, Thailand: overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

One of the most attractive beaches of Phuket in Thailand is Kata Beach. He is very quiet and calm. Only clear sea, bright sun, white sand and endless relaxation. It seems that time here is slowing down, and magnificent sunsets and sunrises are endless in space.

Between the golden sands of Kata beach and the emerald green of the tropics of the inland hills, the Kata Sea Breeze Resort 3 * hotel offers its guests an excellent combination of quality of service and price.

Location





The location of the hotel is one of its main advantages. It is located on the southwest coast of the island, an hour's drive from the international airport. The second coastline, the very center of Kata Beach - and only 350 meters to the coast. You can easily reach the nearby beaches, Patong 16 km. This is a very picturesque place from the point of view of nature, and at the same time, all the guests are in the thick of things: night markets, many souvenir shops, cafes and restaurants.

Sports and Entertainment

Having an impressive territory, the Kata Sea Breeze Resort 3 * hotel (Phuket) skillfully placed on it four whole outdoor pools of various shapes and sizes. Towels for guests are provided absolutely free of charge, and if necessary, you can take them with you to the beach. In the hotel itself, you can visit the spacious fitness center with exercise equipment or have fun in the jacuzzi.

Separately, it should be noted that the Kata Sea Breeze Resort 3 * hotel always has a playroom with air conditioning for the youngest visitors, playgrounds on the territory, a special area in the pool, and an adapted menu, which is of considerable importance, given the severity and spice of traditional thai food. health and beauty





For an extra fee, you can visit the spa and treat yourself to a variety of luxurious treatments, or book a masseuse in your room or pool. One of the most common procedures is peeling of the skin of the legs with the help of small fish. Be sure to try this method, but not on the street, but in reliable institutions. In the hotel this service is also available.

Kata Sea Breeze Resort 3 *: location reviews

If you believe the reviews of tourists, the best thing that the hotel described in our review can boast of is its excellent location. One of the best beaches in Kata is literally within walking distance. It is public, for this reason sunbeds, mattresses and umbrellas must be paid. Travelers who visited it unanimously claim that the place is magnificent: both in cleanliness and silence, and in natural beauty. They emphasize the proximity of numerous restaurants with cuisine specializing in seafood, bars and the market. Many people recommend not sitting still, but for a small fee, ride in a tuk-tuk (a local form of transport) to Patong Beach, where nightlife is most active.





Kata Sea Breeze Resort 3 * (Phuket): room and service reviews

After analyzing the reviews of tourists, we can safely say that the hotel completely and fully justifies its three stars. The territory, according to the guests, is very spacious, shady and very well-groomed. Particularly pleased with the rooms on the ground floor, which have their own access to the pool. In general, those who lived in the hotel note the courtesy and cordiality of staff who are always ready to meet the needs of customers. The rooms, according to our compatriots, are clean, sound. They are simple but cozy, albeit no frills, but they have everything you need for a comfortable stay, cleaning is carried out in good faith and daily.

Guests note minor problems with wireless internet. In the Kata Sea Breeze Resort 3 * hotel it is paid - you can just connect to it only in the lobby, but the signal is very weak. The reviews also mention breakfast. They are not particularly diverse, but very tasty, and you can eat here at every corner.





The hotel is recommended for couples with children, the elderly and those who come to Thailand on business, as it is guaranteed to have a good time here.
