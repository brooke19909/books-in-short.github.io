Nevsky Prospect is one of the most popular and famous places in the world, lovingly sung by poets and prose writers. Best cafes on Nevsky Prospekt (St. Petersburg)

What are the advantages of institutions located in this place? Among the positive aspects, the following can be noted: convenient location; the availability of popular attractions of the city; free Internet; courtesy of employees; A wide variety of menus and a pleasant atmosphere. We offer you information about the most popular cafes on Nevsky Prospekt (St. Petersburg). Among the establishments that are very popular with local residents and visitors, include the following:

  • "Singer". This place has a leisurely rest with a cup of coffee or tea. From the huge, full-floor windows of the institution, beautiful views of the Kazan Cathedral and Nevsky Prospect open. For visitors arrange entertainment programs. You can order a banquet, the capacity of the hall - up to eighty people. An account per person will be from a thousand rubles. The menu offers dishes of Russian and European cuisine.
  • Cafe "Flowerbed". Amazingly kind and homely atmosphere make this place one of the most popular on Nevsky Prospekt. Among the advantages of the institution are delicious author's cuisine, attentive attitude to each client. Also, various handicraft master classes are often held here. The menu of this establishment deserves special attention. Familiar dishes are presented here in the most unexpected ways. Caesar salad is prepared with chicken rolls, and dorado fish is served with red wine and grape sauce.
  • LaCheletta is one of the most popular Italian cafes in St. Petersburg. Its address is the embankment of the Fontanka River, 30. The most popular dishes of Italian cuisine are pizza, lasagna, pasta, risotto. Here you can taste the incredibly delicious ice cream made from whole milk with the addition of fresh fruits and nuts. What do visitors say about this place? Amazingly nice place with affordable prices, fast service and excellent food.
  • "Rasputin" - a cafe on Nevsky Prospekt (St. Petersburg). The photo will be presented later. The institution is located on the embankment of the Moika River, 37. It is definitely worth a visit if you are walking along Palace Square and its environs. Once in this place, you decide that you are transported to the 19th century. Here you will be offered the most delicious dishes of Russian cuisine: classic dumplings; pancakes with different fillings; homemade sausages and much more. There are also banquet options on the menu. Near the cafe there is a convenient parking. The average check at the institution is from 600 rubles.
