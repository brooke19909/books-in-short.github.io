How to make luminous shoelaces with silicone?

You can make authentic luminous shoelaces in the following way. For this you need to purchase:



  • Silicone tube 2 meters long. The diameter of this tube should fit freely into the lace hole on your sneakers or sneakers.
  • Liquid silicone (we will fill the tube with it).
  • Four LEDs (two per lace).
  • Coin batteries (4 pcs.).

Getting to work

Before making luminous shoelaces, divide the silicone tube into two parts one meter in length. This will be our future shoelaces. Now we fill both tubes with liquid silicone in turn. From both ends, you need to make a hole for the LEDs with a drill or a similar method, for example, with a sharp metal stick. Note that if you use the wand, you will have to make a little more effort. After that, immediately insert the LED into the hole with the bulb inside. Do the same with the second end of the future lace. Now the LED wiring needs to be treated with glue, we attach the batteries to it. Those who already know how to make luminous shoelaces themselves can try to slightly modify the product and add small switches to the tubes that will overlap the wiring contacts with the batteries and, thus, save battery. Ready-made laces need to be passed through the holes of your sneakers, and the batteries should be carefully hidden.





How to make luminous shoelaces in an alternative way?

This method is fundamentally different from the previous one. There is no silicone tube and LEDs. You will need ordinary laces and fluorescent phosphoricidal fabric dye (25 grams is enough). Apply paint to the laces, then put them in the oven for a few minutes. Now your shoes will glow, and you do not need to mess around with liquid silicone and batteries. Good luck
