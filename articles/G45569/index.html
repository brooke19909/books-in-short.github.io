Hotel Zdravets 4 * (Bulgaria / Golden Sands) - reviews, photos

If you want to relax in one of the best resorts in Bulgaria, then definitely go to Golden Sands. It is located on the territory of the national park of the same name in the vicinity of Varna. Thanks to the combination of sea and mountain air, a healthy microclimate is formed here, which perfectly contributes to relaxation and restoration of strength. In addition, Golden Sands are famous for their sandy beaches, equipped with all the necessary infrastructure. In total, the resort has about seventy hotels of various categories. We want to introduce you to a four-star hotel of economy class called Zdravets 4 *. Next, we will find out what awaits the guests here, and also we will figure out whether our compatriots liked to relax in this hotel.





Location

The hotel is located almost in the heart of the Golden Sands resort. Thus, the entire infrastructure of this city (shops, shopping centers, souvenir shops, restaurants, bars, nightclubs, etc.) is located within walking distance for guests. The distance from the hotel to the beach is only 150 meters. Therefore, in just a couple of minutes you can walk to the warm sea. Very close to the airport. It is located in Varna and is 25 kilometers from the Zdravets hotel. So upon arrival you can reach the hotel in just 20 minutes.

Golden Sands, Bulgaria - Zdravets 4 *: photos and description

This hotel consists of one six-story building equipped with an elevator, accommodating 105 rooms (standard and mezzanines). The hotel has a beautiful garden, a large open-air pool, bars, a restaurant, as well as a sauna. In addition, guests can use Wi-Fi, exchange currency, book a tour, rent a car, leave valuables in a safe. Hotel Zdravets 4 * is equally well suited for families with children and seniors, as well as for youth.





Hotel Policies

As in many other hotels in the world, Zdravets has a checkout time. So, the arrival of guests arriving from two in the afternoon. On the day of departure, the occupied apartments should be vacated before noon. You can pay for accommodation and using additional services here either with a plastic card or in cash.

Number of rooms

As already mentioned, the four-star Zdravets Hotel (Golden Sands, Bulgaria) offers everyone to stay in one of 150 rooms located in a six-story building. Most of them are standard, but there are also several mezzanines, which are two-story apartments. Each room has its own bathroom, air conditioning, TV, telephone, mini fridge. Also, along with beds, folding sofas are installed in the rooms. Rooms are cleaned every day. Maids renew linen and towels twice a week.





Nutrition

Guests of Zdravets 4 * can choose the power scheme depending on their preferences. So, the hotel offers guests either breakfast only or all-inclusive meals. Meals are held in the hotel's own restaurant and are organized in buffet style. Here you will be offered excellently prepared dishes of both national Bulgarian and European cuisine. In addition, the hotel has several bars where you can enjoy your favorite drinks.

Zdravets 4 * (Golden Sands, Bulgaria): Traveler Reviews from Russia

Since today almost all tourists in the process of planning a trip abroad try to carefully examine the comments of people who have already visited this or that hotel and stayed in a particular hotel, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with the opinion of our compatriots who vacationed this summer at the Zdravets hotel . Running a little ahead, we note that this hotel basically left the guests a pleasant experience. So, according to them, it represents the optimal combination of the price of accommodation and the quality of services provided and living conditions. But we learn more about everything.





Comments of tourists about the number of rooms and the location of the hotel

As follows from the stories of our compatriots, Zdravets 4 * is a standard economy class hotel. This hotel, like many other Bulgarian hotels of this type, is somewhat reminiscent of a Soviet boarding house. The rooms here are spacious, bright, comfortable and clean. The atmosphere in them is minimal, but there is everything you need for a comfortable stay. For example, the bathroom has a hairdryer, so you don‚Äôt need to take it with you from home. Pleased with the presence of a small refrigerator in the apartment and a working air conditioner. Also, our compatriots were satisfied with sound insulation. So, even those guests, whose windows overlooked the pool, where, as a rule, it is quite noisy both during the day and in the evening, with the balcony door closed, they could easily rest. As for cleaning, there were no complaints about the maintenance personnel in this regard. According to tourists, the maids daily cleaned the rooms, and towels and bed linen were changed every 3-4 days.

Our compatriots also found the location of Zdravets 4 * very successful. According to them, the hotel is located close to the center of Golden Sands, so all the entertainment and infrastructure of this resort city are within walking distance. It is also convenient to travel from here to other cities in Bulgaria. Literally one and a half hundred meters from the hotel is the beach. By the way, tourists liked the local beaches very much for their fine sand, cleanliness and excellent infrastructure. Experienced travelers recall that almost all the beaches in Bulgaria, and Golden Sands as well, are municipal. Therefore, for the rental of sunbeds and umbrellas you will need to pay extra.





Reviews of our compatriots about food and infrastructure of the Zdravets hotel

As for food, many tourists were quite pleased with both the variety of menus and the quality of food. True, sometimes some dishes nevertheless became boring, but this fact did not spoil the general impression. By the way, experienced travelers recommend booking only breakfast when booking a room at the Zdravets 4 * hotel (Golden Sands). This will allow you to have the opportunity to try various dishes of national cuisine in the numerous restaurants and taverns located in the vicinity of the hotel. Moreover, the prices here will seem quite affordable to you, and the portion sizes will pleasantly surprise you.

Judging by the reviews, almost all tourists enjoyed spending time by the pool. So, according to them, the hotel has a fairly large and clean adult, as well as children's pools. In addition, there you will also find a bar where you can order your favorite drink. By the way, another bar is located in the lobby of the hotel.

Travelers also note the availability of Wi-Fi at the hotel. It can be used free of charge in the hotel lobby. In addition, for an additional fee, you can access the Internet from your room.





Impressions of guests about the hotel staff

Our compatriots recall the employees of Zdravets 4 * (Bulgaria) mostly with very kind words. So, in their opinion, the staff at the hotel are very pleasant, friendly, hospitable people who are always ready to help. Also, tourists had a good impression about the staff of the tour desk located here. They were always ready to give more than comprehensive answers to all questions regarding interesting places and trips.





Conclusion

Summing up, we note that almost all the tourists who visited the four-star Zdravets hotel were very pleased with their choice. In their opinion, this is a great place for a relaxing family vacation for those who want to save on accommodation, but not at the expense of convenience and comfort. Our compatriots considered the price of hotel accommodation to be more than appropriate for the conditions and service.
