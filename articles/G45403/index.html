Gerbera from foamiran - a step-by-step master class

Artificial flowers are in fashion today. But to decorate your home, you do not have to buy lush bouquets. You can make them yourself. In this article we will tell you how to make a gerbera from foamiran.

Blanks

How to create a gerbera from foamiran? A step-by-step master class will help even a novice needlewoman to make a magnificent flower.

The first step is to draw a pattern and prepare all the materials for the job. What might be needed? Foamiran, iron, scissors and a stick for legs. We begin to manufacture gerbera. From foamiran, two long strips should be cut. One of them should be burgundy, the other pink.
Now you should cut both strips in turn. How to do it? Visually mark the center and make cuts along one edge to it. We try to make sure that they are all uniform.

Picking a flower

The next action is picking the center of the flower. How to make a gerbera from foamiran so that it does not differ from the original? You need to try to assemble the product carefully, without rushing. Before you start making the middle of the flower, you should paint the wand. If you use a regular wooden skewer as a leg, which is worth taking for this purpose, then it is easy to paint it with acrylic paint.

Dip the stick in superglue and start wrapping a foamiran on it as shown in the picture. But to attach the tip of the material to the middle of the color you need not with glue, but with a heated iron. We bring the tip of the material to it, and when it begins to melt, just attach it to the center. Now you need to create a second row of pink stripes of petals.





The sequence of actions will be almost the same, only the petals should be slightly bent. This is easy to do with an iron. We heat the incised edge of the foamiran strip a little and bend the petals with your fingers, giving them some disheveledness. Then this strip needs to be wound already on the finished center.

Create the petals

At this stage, the gerbera from foamiran should acquire petals. Cut them to a pattern. Moreover, a lot of workpieces should be done so that at least two rows are enough. And better - three.





We attach the finished petals to the center. To do this, we heat the tip of the workpiece on an iron and glue it to the lower part of the middle. Work needs to be done in rows. First, make the outer ring of petals. Then glue the second row of parts in a checkerboard pattern.





Well, the final stage is the third ring of petals, which visually make the flower more magnificent.





The final stage

The final step in the manufacture of gerbera from foamiran will be its detailed revision. First of all, you need to make a stub-stalk that will hide the inside of the flower from prying eyes. Cut a circle from green material and make a zigzag on its edges. Now this part needs to be heated with an iron, stretched through the entire skewer and attached to the base, covering the place where the petals were attached. If you wish, you can make the stalk even wider by wrapping a thin strip of foamer on it. This is necessary not only for beauty, but also to give the construction stability.





When everything is ready, it remains to make leaves. We cut them out of green material, and if you have a special stamp, then you can use it to apply it to the vein sheet. If there is no such device, the veins should be painted with acrylic. Foameran is easily tinted, so there should not be any problems. Using an iron, we attach the leaves to the leg. In order to hold them firmly, not only the base, but also one third of the green blank should be glued to the stem.

Gerbera is ready. To collect a bouquet, by analogy, you can make another 4-8 flowers. Moreover, multi-colored gerberas look beautiful. Of course, blue from red will be problematic, so it is desirable that the base is white or beige.
