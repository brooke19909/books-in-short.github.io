Five-star hotel Majestic Mirage Park (Kemer / Turkey): detailed description of services





If you are traveling abroad with a child, then the requirements for the hotel should be overstated. Among the abundance of tourist complexes it is difficult to make the right and unmistakable choice. We bring to your attention the budget hotel "Majestic Mirage Park" (Kemer) with five stars, conveniently located off the coast of the Mediterranean Sea. It is here that nobody and nothing will interfere with your vacation with children.

Near the hotel towers the picturesque Taurus Mountains, which protect the resort area from the winds. The warm subtropical climate allows tourists to enjoy a fabulous vacation year-round. The complex can be reached from Antalya Airport (20 minutes). Near the hotel there is a rather rich infrastructure, equipped with shopping pavilions and entertainment venues.





There is a bus stop opposite the building, from where public transport runs to the center of Kemer and Antalya. If desired, guests can go to these resort areas and have fun in the famous water parks. Pension "Majestic Mirage Park" (Kemer) attracts a spacious territory with a beautiful tropical garden, where pomegranate, orange trees and palm trees grow.

The stupefying aroma of fruit stands acts as an aphrodisiac and immediately improves mood. The hotel territory looks like a small village with its own river, a picturesque pond with live fish, a zoo and interesting infrastructure. The administration carefully treats young guests and tries to make their stay safe, rich and colorful.

Number of rooms





The Majestic Mirage Park Hotel (Kemer) consists of 2 bright 5-storey buildings and a number of 2-storey cottages. There are more than five hundred rooms of different class in the hotel. Absolutely all rooms with panoramic windows, open terraces with stunning views of the blue sea or the flowering garden with pools. In 2011, the rooms were completely renovated and furnished with new furniture.

For a pleasant stay, the rooms have a flat-screen TV with Russian channels. They have air conditioning, a telephone and Wi-Fi. There is also a free safe for valuables. Upon check-in, a filled bar with refreshing juices awaits guests. Cleanliness and comfort are guaranteed. Scheduled cleaning is carried out regularly.





Nutrition

The five-star Majestic Mirage Park complex (Kemer) operates according to the European all-inclusive food concept. Meals are served in the main restaurant with outdoor terrace. There is a diet and children's menu. On the tables you can always see a variety of cereals (in the morning), an abundance of fruits, vegetables and meat dishes cooked on the grill.

Guests can grab a bite to eat in one of the 6 bars, where light snacks, Turkish cakes, baked potatoes and burgers are prepared. In addition, all establishments serve coffee, cocktails and beer. A la carte restaurants invite you to dine and spend time in a pleasant setting . There are four of them in total (fish, Italian, Turkish, Latin American). Each has a Russian-speaking staff who will help you choose the dishes according to your taste preferences.

Hotel Description

Impeccable service and excellent animation are praised by almost all tourists. Animation shows deserve special attention and respect. Every day, a professional group of artists organizes dance evenings, concert programs and theatrical performances. Jokes, contests, interesting scenes with draws and prizes cheer up and fill with positive.





Quality leisure is organized for children. Young guests enjoy studying in a development club with a friendly teacher, allowing parents to relax a bit. Two outdoor pools with water slides and a playground with equipment were built.

For sports and wellness, there is a fitness center, hard and quartz tennis courts, aerobics, bowling, a billiard room and futsal. There is a library, currency exchange, car rental, a medical office, a beauty salon. For business travelers - 15 conference rooms of various sizes. The list of services is very rich.

Thanks to a favorable climate, picturesque landscapes and various entertainments sunny Turkey attracts Russian citizens. Majestic Mirage Park (Kemer) is one of the few hotels that ideally combines attractive pricing and quality of service.
