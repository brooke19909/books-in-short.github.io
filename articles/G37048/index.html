What to bring from Crimea as a gift: ideas, tips and reviews. What can be brought from Crimea as a souvenir?

Rarely is someone who does not like to visit amazing and certainly interesting places during their holidays. And to get there something for memory is a sacred thing at all, and you need to approach this thoroughly in order to get the original little things that carry the spirit of that locality. And of course, the sunny peninsula of Crimea, which hospitably welcomes guests, deserves the closest attention both for its sights and unique souvenirs. Moreover, it combines a warm sea, picturesque mountains, wide steppes and karst caves. This provides ample opportunities for cultural and outdoor activities. And if we recall the possibilities of the peninsula in the field of treatment: phyto-, water- and mud therapy, which are in no way inferior to the best world resorts!





And what souvenirs can I bring from the Crimea? Their list is very extensive: starting from magnets with the image of species and ending with therapeutic mud and herbal balms.

Wood products

It is known that juniper is one of the symbols of the Crimean peninsula, and therefore on the trading shelves you will find many products from this evergreen tree. The air here is saturated with the smells of juniper, and, according to tourists who have already visited Crimea, even an ordinary flat saw will become an inexpensive, but valuable memorabilia that can be used as a hot stand. A huge plus of this piece of wood is that it is durable in itself and also retains its smell for a long time. And if at first it pleases with a pleasant aroma without outside influence, then after about a year - only after exposure to heat.

And yet, what to bring from the Crimea? An urgent issue, even when you decide to buy a souvenir made of juniper wood. The choice here is wide, and anyone will find something to their taste.





Many praise combs from this tree, claiming that they are useful for hair and scalp, and all thanks to their bactericidal properties. And what a variety of juniper jewelry is presented by local craftsmen: earrings and rings, original beads and bracelets, as well as unusual but no less cute pendants, of amazing beauty and sophistication.

Tableware

And how many dishes and household items - eyes run up! Therefore, when you think about what to bring from Crimea as a gift to a business lady, you will find a lot of useful things. So, for example, you can buy juniper spatulas for mixing food or a spoon - indispensable things in the kitchen. Or present a wooden box where it can put your favorite and pleasant little things or jewelry. And if you want to please the car enthusiast, then get massage covers for the seats of his favorite car.

Other wooden gizmos

But wooden products are not limited to things from juniper, you can choose souvenirs from other species that are common on the peninsula. It can be objects from beech, ash, pine or plum. They look no worse than junipers. But, unfortunately, they do not have the same amazing aroma.

Wines and infusions





Another symbol of the Crimean peninsula is wine and infusions. According to people who have been here, this is a great option for what you can bring from Crimea as a gift. Moreover, it is believed that many varieties have healing properties, and even taste is not at all in doubt.

Moreover, it is worth noting that it is most reasonable to purchase them in company stores or factory cellars. Especially if you decide to present the drink as a gift. For such cases, there are wines in branded gift bottles that are made in the form of various beautiful figures. Well, or the usual container has paper packaging, where various interesting things are often printed, such as the legend of the origin of Crimean wines or the features of a particular drink.





In this case, what wine to bring from the Crimea? Note that due to climatic conditions, grapes here become more sugary. Because the wine from it is sweet and quite strong. There is plenty to choose from: muscat, madeira or sherry. In addition, if you want to present it in honor of any significant date or celebration, you can order wine of a certain year from the factory.

What to bring from Crimea as a gift? Pay attention to the Bakhchisarai herbal balms, because many spoke of them as delicious drinks. Fragrant Herbal

Incredible in its variety of souvenirs from the Crimea. What to bring a lover to mourn for? The answer is simple - a collection of Crimean herbs. Such tea, prepared in accordance with old recipes, will become an assistant in restoring strength, fighting colds and maintaining immunity. Essential oils





Still doubting what souvenirs to bring from Crimea, pay a little your attention to essential oils. Such a gift will be not only pleasant, but also useful, because it can be used for aromatherapy or taking a bath, as well as during inhalation. Moreover, when buying oils, you will receive advice from the seller, and something else about their application can be read both on the label and on the package.

It is important to remember that essential oils are sold in small glass jars, on which there must be appropriate labels. In Crimea, such enterprises as the Crimean Rose plant, the Alushta essential oil state farm plant, and the Crimea-Aromat plant are engaged in their production. And in order to avoid collision with fakes, make purchases in souvenir shops or cosmetics stores. The reason for this is the storage conditions of the oil: it retains its properties and does not deteriorate at a temperature of +15 degrees. From which it follows that the goods, which lay on a table in the sun, are probably already unusable.

And therefore, deciding what to bring from the Crimea, pay attention to the breadth of the offered assortment.
