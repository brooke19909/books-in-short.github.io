Gelendzhik: holidays with children. Reviews and photos

Holidays in the city of Gelendzhik with children is a great choice. The resort infrastructure is such that it will appeal not only to adult tourists, but also to the smallest travelers. In Gelendzhik, you can stay in the private sector, or you can book a room in a hotel or rest house, choosing the one that will appeal to you more.



Rest in the sanatorium

You can gain strength and improve the health of the whole family in a sanatorium. Gelendzhik (which is very comfortable for families with children) offers several health resorts: Arkhipo-Osipovka, Pearl of the Sea, Red Talka, Friendship, and Solar.

City Hotels Overview

In the city of Gelendzhik, a vacation with children will turn out not just good, but first of all interesting. For children more than for adults, you need a quality kitchen and special entertainment. Some hotels make sure that little tourists are comfortable and fun with them.

Hotel "Victoria"

Located just 50 meters from the sea. This is a fairly comfortable hotel with rooms of different classes and equipment. It offers free Wi-Fi, spa treatments and a pool, as well as many activities. The hotel is located on Lunacharsky Street (Gelendzhik). Holidays with children (2014) will cost 3,000 rubles per day (standard room) and 7,000 rubles - a suite. Extra bed for children under 12 years old - free of charge. Every day, the hotel organizes any entertainment for kids and adults. A colorful playground is located on site. For an additional fee, you can take the child to a special room with a tutor. For the smallest, strollers and cribs are rented here.



Guest House "Bay of Fortune"

The year-round guest house offers a quiet, relaxing holiday with your family. Free hotel guests can swim in the pool, use the Internet. Nearby there is a cafe of Russian, European and Caucasian cuisines, a parking lot, a bar with many soft drinks. The guest house is located in a very picturesque place - Thin cape in the city itself with the name Gelendzhik. A vacation with young children here will turn out unforgettable thanks to the animators, a trampoline, a pool for kids and a very beautiful design of the hotel, which is literally buried in flowers. Room rates - from 2000 rubles. Each has a bathroom and all amenities.

Hotel "Orlitsa"

This hotel accepts children over three years old. Kids up to five years old live here for free. The hotel is equipped with all the necessary things to relax. It is located in a part of the city with developed infrastructure. The hotel has a swimming pool, sun loungers and umbrellas, barbecue facilities, a playground for children and much more.

Gelendzhik, holidays with children: photos, opinions, reviews

The city has many beaches. Resting with young children, it is impossible to visit each of them. Therefore, it is best to choose a city of sand and pebble beach. Do not worry about water and coastal qualities. Clean beaches and the sea - the advantage of the resort of Gelendzhik. Holidays with children over 10 years old will be much more interesting. You can rent bicycles and ride around this relatively small city, exploring the sights and staying at local parks and cafes. Kids like to dig in the sand and build figures. Tourists confirm that almost every Gelendzhik hotel is suitable for families with children: there are playgrounds, rooms, pools and a lot of entertainment.



Entertainment

At the resorts of the city (and we remember that this is Gelendzhik), relaxing with children (the reviews of those who have already visited here, confirm this) will turn out to be fascinating due to the many parks, water parks, water attractions and similar entertainments in the city.

Park "City of Fairy Tales"

This square for parents with children opened in 2014. The visitors are immediately greeted by the heroes of their favorite cartoons and fairy tales: "Prostokvashino", "Well, wait a moment", "Little Red Riding Hood". The whole park is replete with flower beds of various shapes and colors, and in the center is a fountain.

"Safari Park"

This is a giant rehab center for animals. On its territory there is a zoo, arboretum, museums, an exhibition of butterflies, the aquatic world. Here you can not only take a walk, but also take a ride on the cable car - the longest in Gelendzhik. Chair road, without cabins. Therefore, the journey through it is very exciting. The length of the road is 1.6 kilometers.

Clothing should be taken care of, sometimes the temperature difference in individual areas reaches 10 degrees. Therefore, in cool weather, you need to dress warmer. Cloaks, rugs are provided for rent. Among other entertainments, there are caves in the park that are equipped for tourists. The length of the Bear Cave is 200 meters. The first grotto housed an underground lake with a waterfall. In the second one there are many beautiful butterflies, here you can observe the process of their development from a larva to a beautiful insect. Bats live in the third grotto.

It will be interesting not only for children, but also for adults. There are many animals in the zoo, some of them can be stroked and fed.

At the very top of the Markotkh Range, Jurassic Park is located. Here you can see the full-size models of ancient giant animals. From the observation deck offers a beautiful view of the beauty of Gelendzhik: sea, mountains, hills, capes.

Of course, Safari Park can be called one of the most significant attractions of the city with such a wonderful name Gelendzhik. Holidays with children will greatly benefit from visiting this place. Those who have been here recommend arriving early so that you can spend the whole day in the park.

Arkhipo-Osipov Dolphinarium

The most intelligent mammals are always especially pleasing to small viewers. Representations in this dolphinarium show since 2006. It is located in the open air and operates seasonally: from May to September. Experienced trainers deal with animals. Spectators can not only admire the tricks performed by dolphins, but also stroke them.



Oceanarium

The oceanarium allows you to plunge into the beautiful water world and get acquainted with its inhabitants, without putting on a diving suit. Area - 400 square meters. It is divided into two zones. The first presents freshwater animals. In the second - the inhabitants of the seas and oceans. Everything is thought out here: design, music, video. And the guides will share fascinating stories about animals. The oceanarium is located in the very center of the city, whose name is Gelendzhik.



Vacation with children in the amusement park

It presents more than 20 swings and attractions for children of all ages. On site there is a cinema, a mini zoo and a haunted castle. The park is painted in the brightest colors, planted with flowers, a walk along it will give a lot of positive emotions and impressions.

This is not the whole list of hotels and entertainment in Gelendzhik. In this city, you can relax with the whole family, including with young children.
