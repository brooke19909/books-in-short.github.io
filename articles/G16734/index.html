OTP credit card: reviews, terms of use, is it worth opening

Credit cards are in demand among Russians, since they allow you to pay with the issuer's funds without interest within the grace period. The bank with the Hungarian roots of OTP is one of the leaders of the Russian Federation in issuing credit cards. OTP credit card reviews will allow you to evaluate the prospects of using the product.

Types of OTP credit cards

In 2019, the bank offers 3 types of credit cards to its customers:

  1. "Big cashback."
  2. "Lightning".
  3. "Clear map."

Customers can receive any of the credit cards on the same day. OTP Bank credit card conditions vary depending on the type of product. The borrower is allowed to have no more than 1 OTP credit card.

Big cashback card review

The product about which the most reviews were left (from OTP credit cards) on the popular Banki.ru service is the Big Cashback card. OTP Bank credit card terms with cashback for purchases:

  • the maximum limit is 1 million rubles;
  • up to 7% cashback for purchases in selected categories;
  • 1% cashback on all other purchases using the OTP card;
  • service without commission;
  • free issue;
  • the opportunity to get a credit card on the day of application.

The size of the cashback for purchases is limited: no more than 36,000 rubles per year. The product belongs to the premium category. The cardholder can evaluate the capabilities of the Mastercard World customer support program.

The bank charges interest on the balance: if the client has more than 10,000 rubles, it is possible to receive up to 4% per annum. It is allowed to carry out expenditures on an OTP Bank credit card in the amount of up to 100,000 rubles. per day and 700 000 p. per month.

The interest rate is not the lowest in the country - from 24.9% per annum. But the owners have up to 55 days of the grace period during which it is possible to spend OTP Bank funds and not pay interest for use.

Reviews on OTP credit card with cashback

Most negative reviews of OTP loan products are related to the Big Cashback card. But the negative clients is not associated with the conditions, but with the possibility of obtaining a credit card.

The largest number of complaints is related to the following point: when sending an application for a credit card of OTP Bank, it turned out that the customers could fill in all the data. After processing the questionnaire, the borrowers received SMS with preliminary approval and an available limit on the card. But at the office or at the point of issue of the card, managers refused to receive a credit card for various reasons, for example, due to insufficient solvency or unreliability.

Under the terms, OTP credit card reviews are positive: the bank does not cheat on charging cashback for purchases and allows owners to choose the category with maximum bonuses on their own. Not all users like the work of Internet banking, but it does not spoil the impression of the chosen credit card.

According to reviews, an OTP credit card with a cashback can be issued with only a Russian passport. Credit cards are issued to citizens with constant registration and stable income.

Credit card activation: conditions, features

OTP credit cards are not automatically activated. This condition allows you to protect funds on a credit card from intruders. Card activation is possible by phone.

How to activate an OTP bank credit card:

  • call support (you can find the phone number on the website);
  • introduce oneself, give passport details, loan agreement number and card information;
  • invent and set a PIN code;
  • confirm the operation.

Without setting the PIN code, the client will not be able to complete transactions in excess of 1,000 rubles and withdraw cash. Purchases from 1 ruble to 1000 are possible without entering a PIN code - using contactless technology.

The credit card is activated within 24 hours from the moment of contacting the bank support service. The call is free, the line is open around the clock.

Setting a PIN code for OTP cards

If a client has already activated a credit card, but has not set a PIN code on it, he can do this by a toll free number. When setting the code, all credit card details should be named. and information from the passport of the Russian Federation.

The code will be installed after checking and confirming the information. If the client has forgotten his PIN, he should also call support and follow the instructions of the manager.
