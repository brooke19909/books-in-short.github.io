Base "Grandfather's Farm" in the Astrakhan region: description, services and reviews

Choosing a place to relax with your family, you sort through various fishing bases. "Grandfather's Farm" is a place that will be interesting to both those who visit it for the first time, and already experienced fishermen. It is better for hunters to come when the hunting season has already started. And just vacationers can swim and enjoy beautiful views of nature year-round.





About base

The base "Grandfather's Farm" is located in the Astrakhan region. To get to it, you need to remember three coordinates: drive 170 km above the city of Astrakhan and 250 km below the hero city of Volgograd, and then postpone 21 km to the city of Kharabali, and now you can see an island measuring 80 √ó 20 km, replete with numerous ilmeni (lowlands, in which water accumulates from the spill of rivers) and eriks - channels through which water from rivers flows into the lowlands. It is from them after the spring flood that you can catch a large number of the most diverse fish.

The recreation center "Grandfather's Farm" in the second half of the summer gives you the opportunity to please yourself with a ram, lump, roach and scavenger when fishing from the shore. And do not go far, within 50 meters from the rented house there is access to a reservoir.

Opposite the base is the island of Chub, famous for its asps. And how not to be a chub, when underwater pits are located nearby, in some places up to 30 meters deep?

Where to live at the base?

Grandfather Farm offers three accommodation options for guests:

1. The rooms are double. Equipped with a standard set of furniture and household appliances (refrigerator, air conditioning). There is a capacious freezer in which it is proposed to store the caught catch.

2. Cottages. Built on the first coastline, can accommodate up to 6 people. But in order to live there, it is necessary to call at least 4 vacationers at the same time. The cottage consists of three rooms, two of which have 6 beds (3 in each), and the remaining room is reserved for a common room. A standard set of satellite TV, a 300-liter freezer, a small set of kitchen utensils - this is what the cottage is furnished with.

3. A house from a log house. Lined from specially selected logs. It consists of one room, which can accommodate 3 people, but rents at least two. It boasts a summer terrace. The situation is no different from that in the cottage.

Children under the age of 6 can live in any category of houses for free.

In all the houses offered, hot and cold water is supplied, which passes through several degrees of purification, and is further further disinfected with ultraviolet light.





Service Prices

Rent a three-room cottage will cost 1600 rubles. per day per person, but it is necessary to remove at least four (and in August and September this figure rises to 5). Additional accommodation is free of charge.

Rent a log house will be in 1450 rubles. per day per person, but with the condition that all 3 places will be occupied.

If only two are settled, the price may not change, but for this it will be necessary to pay one of the boats for the entire rest.

Double rooms can be rented for 2100 rubles per room.

What is fed at the base?

Base "Grandfather's Farm" feeds its guests with dishes prepared according to recipes of Russian cuisine. Three meals a day, baking of own production is served every evening. Tea can be enjoyed at the buffet. Sweet tea is served with tea.

The pride of the cook is a unique triple ear and pilaf of a fat tail sheep, cooked in the traditional way - at the stake. These dishes are included in the daily menu, they are prepared once a week and you do not need to pay extra for them. Based on the preferences of the guests, the chef can prepare the selected dish from caught game or fish.

Guests can choose how many times to eat food per day and, accordingly, just pay for it. But according to the reviews of guests there, it is clear that almost everyone eats 3 times a day.

The cost of three meals a day is 850 rubles. For children under 6 years of age, payment for food is made with a 50% discount.

Who can be caught on land?

Base "Grandfather's Farm" offers to hunt on an island located nearby.

Last year, in the Astrakhan region, the hunting season for waterfowl was opened at the end of September. It was then that it was possible to begin the legal shooting of swamp-meadow and waterfowl inhabitants. And in November it was already possible to begin hunting for steppe, field and fur animals. On the last day of the year, hunting for the first group of animals was stopped, and a couple of months later, on February 29, the hunting season was officially closed.

From waterfowl it was possible to shoot for several varieties of ducks: gray, mallard, diving and other species.

From the steppe species of birds you can shoot at the goose, sandpiper, coot, coot, pheasant and woodcock. In October, it is possible to shoot waterfowl up to 20 animals a day, and steppe ones - up to 5. A hare is issued on vouchers (a hare lives like this and does not know that he will die on a voucher!). Wolves and jackals can be shot without restrictions.

Six years ago there was a hunt, during which 25 wolves were caught. Eight hunters participated in it, each with a gun. But after the top, which was soon tamed, got to the base staff, the wolves were not shot.

To take part in the hunt, you must send a copy of your hunting ticket in advance. The price of waterfowl will be 800 rubles.
