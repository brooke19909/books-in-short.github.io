Hotel Allamanda Laguna Phuket 4 * (Thailand / Phuket): photos and reviews of tourists

For many tourists, Thailand is a favorite vacation spot. What is easy to understand is that it has a wonderful climate, good beaches, exotic food, and reasonable prices for accommodation. But where is the best place to stay? This question interests many people who decide to go on vacation. Well, Allamanda Laguna Phuket 4 * will be a good option.





general information

There is a hotel complex in Phuket. More specifically, on the beach of Bang Tao. The latter extends for seven kilometers. And in the south ends with a small cape, from the hill which offers amazing views of the local landscapes. Casuarines grow along the entire length of the beach. Bang Tao is an ideal place to sunbathe and swim in silence. Here, compared with the famous beaches of Thailand, peace and quiet reigns.

By the way, you can walk to the sea from the hotel in 5-7 minutes. The airport from Allamanda Laguna Phuket 4 * is also nearby - about 20-30 minutes drive. Anyway, the complex has a good location. Laguna Phuket Golf Club is 2 minutes away, Tesco Lotus Supermarket and Bliss Beach Club are 20 minutes away, and Bang Tao Temple is 5 minutes away by public transport from the resort.

Service

In Allamanda Laguna Phuket 4 * there is everything you need for the comfort of visitors. Wi-Fi throughout the hotel (including in the rooms) is free of charge, as well as private parking. For an extra fee, a shuttle service can be offered to tourists. However, there is also a car rental point here, so you can rent a car and not depend on transport.

The hotel provides concierge services and a 24-hour reception (express check-in is possible), there is a safe, a tour desk and luggage storage. In addition to the above, there is also a dry cleaning service with a laundry, where guests can also iron clothes.

It also offers babysitting services at an additional cost. There is even a conference room equipped with modern technology. It is designed for 100 people. And finally, it is worth noting the fact that the hotel has rooms that are fully equipped for guests with disabilities.





Leisure

In Allamanda Laguna Phuket 4 * you can not only live comfortably, but also have fun. There are two outdoor pools and a sun terrace (with sun loungers, towels and umbrellas), opportunities for kayaking, a nearby golf course, as well as bike rental and a small SPA center. They make various types of massages (Thai, reflexive, relaxing), and they also provide many other popular face and body treatments.

And also in Best Western Allamanda Laguna Phuket 4 * regularly conduct various interesting events. For example, a festival in honor of Songkran - Thai New Year.

Nutrition

According to people who stayed at the Allamanda Laguna Phuket Hotel 4 *, the food there is excellent. There is no restaurant in the hotel itself, so breakfast is organized for guests in a cafe located opposite the hotel. The choice is not bad, the most important thing is that all the food is fresh and not spicy (which many people worry about when going to Thailand). Guests are offered sausages, ham, several types of fruits and rolls, rice, vegetables (tomatoes, cucumbers, peppers, herbs), beans, fried bacon, noodles, cereals and granola, and also hot dishes - omelettes and fried eggs. However, they can cook eggs here as the guest wants - at will. Still served stew, fried potatoes, seafood and much more. Water, milk, tea, coffee, juices are also abundant.

About accommodation

In Allamanda Laguna Phuket 4 * (Phuket) there are rooms of several categories. The budget option is a one-bedroom apartment. Their area is 82 square meters. m. Inside, in a bright and spacious bedroom, there is a large bed for two people. There is also a separate living room, a full open-plan kitchen, and a dining area. And another room has access to a private spacious balcony with views of the surroundings and the golf course.

If we talk about amenities, we can say with confidence: the apartment has everything you need for a comfortable stay. There is a TV with satellite channels, an iron with ironing facilities, air conditioning, and the kitchen has a microwave, refrigerator, cutlery, stove, electric kettle and much more. The bathroom is equipped with a shower, toilet, sink and hairdryer. Each guest is also provided with individual cosmetic sets.

And now about the price. For two people a weekly stay in such a room will cost only 22-25 tons. And if you need breakfast, you will need to pay only 5-6 tons.

About staff

People who have been here say that the hotel has nice and friendly staff. The only negative is that no one speaks Russian. Only in Thai or English, therefore it is better to take a phrasebook or dictionary with you on the road.

Check-in at the hotel starts at 14:00. But if the guests arrived earlier, then there will be nothing wrong. There are two scenarios. They can be given a temporary number so that they wash themselves, put themselves in order and rest. Then, when the apartments ordered by tourists are released from previous residents, they will be relocated. And the second option - guests will be accommodated immediately if the room that they issued is already prepared for living.

Cleaning, by the way, at the hotel daily. Maids constantly bring clean bottled water, tea and coffee sets to their rooms, and also replenish hygiene supplies. Linen and towel changes are also daily.





Helpful information

This hotel allows children of any age. Moreover, if the guests arrived to rest with a baby whose age does not exceed four years, you will not have to pay extra for his stay. But you will have to stay in the places available in the room. Fortunately, the apartments are equipped not only with beds, but also with a sofa in the living room.

If the child is not even two years old, then a crib will be allocated for him. It will also be free. But if someone more adult (over four years old) comes with guests, then for the installation of an extra full-sized bed and accommodation, you will have to pay 800 TNV per day (at the current rate, this is about 1,400 rubles). But at most in one room only one berth can be provided. And if the installation of an extra bed is necessary, then the administration must be informed in advance. The guest will have to send a confirmation.

The hotel also has connecting rooms. If potential guests need just such apartments, then on this occasion, you must also inform in the application or contact the administration directly.

You should also be aware that when applying for a reservation, you will need to provide your credit card details. The property accepts Visa, Master Card, American Express and JCB. The card must be provided upon arrival, along with an identity card.

To arrange a transfer, you must contact the hotel at least three days before departure. It will be necessary to report the details of your flight, as well as discuss other relevant nuances. A transfer costs 1,500 TNV (approximately 2700 rubles).
