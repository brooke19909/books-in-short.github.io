Hotel East Sea Resort Paradise 4 * (Pattaya): description, photos and reviews

If you are looking for a comfortable hotel in Pattaya (Thailand) near the beach and the city center, then the East Sea Resort Paradise 4 * can be a great option.



Description

The four-star hotel is located 4 kilometers from the center of Pattaya and 300 meters from the sea in an area with a well-developed tourist infrastructure. Utapao Airport is 43 kilometers away, and Bangkok Air Harbor is 110 kilometers away.

The hotel was built in 2008. It consists of 40 one-story bungalows. The total area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe hotel is just over 20 thousand square meters. Each room has high-quality comfortable furniture, TV, fridge with minibar, bathroom, air conditioning, hairdryer and telephone. The hotel has a large swimming pool and a sun terrace.



Hotel East Sea Resort Paradise 4 * (Pattaya): traveler reviews

Since the hotel becomes a kind of second home for the traveler during a trip abroad, it is very important not to make a mistake with her choice. After all, if you will not be happy with your hotel in many ways, then your vacation experience will not be so rosy. Experienced tourists advise at the stage of choosing a place to stay to study as many reviews of real people who have already stayed in a particular hotel. After all, from them you can gather reliable information about the pros and cons of a particular hotel. Today we invite you to get acquainted with the opinions of travelers about the East Sea Resort Paradise 4 * hotel. Immediately, we note that basically its guests were satisfied with their choice. According to them, the hotel offers pleasant accommodation at an affordable price.



Territory, location

A great advantage of the East Sea Paradise Hotel, according to travelers, is its location. So, from here on a tuk-tuk you can get to the center of Pattaya in just a few minutes. If you want to Walking Street, you can take a walk on foot. This will take you about an hour. Due to the fact that the hotel is located in a quiet street, nothing will disturb your vacation. Literally a couple of minutes walk from the hotel there are several shops where you can buy everything you need. The surrounding area also has a variety of cafes, tour desks and car rental facilities.

As for the territory of East Sea Resort Paradise 4 * (photos of the hotel can be seen in this article), then, according to the guests, it is not very big, but green and well-groomed. There are many beautiful trees, there is a large swimming pool with a sun terrace and comfortable sunbeds.

Housing stock

All rooms at the hotel are located in small bungalows. According to travelers, the apartments are quite spacious, clean and comfortable. You will not find much luxury here, but all the furniture and appliances are of high quality and in good working condition. A big plus, according to tourists, is the presence of virtually free Wi-Fi access throughout the entire hotel.



Service

Special complaints about the quality of service at East Sea Resort Paradise 4 * guest reviews do not contain. True, some tourists complained that sometimes the cleaning of their rooms was not very thorough. However, there is always the opportunity, in case of dissatisfaction, to contact the reception and ask, for example, to clean the floor, replenish supplies of personal hygiene products or replace towels. In general, the staff of East Sea Resort Paradise are very friendly, welcoming and attentive. All employees are always ready to help you in resolving any issue.

Nutrition

This item, according to many of our compatriots, is, as they say, a weak link in the hotel in question. So, the cost of living here includes only breakfast. They are not held in buffet format, as is customary in most hotels in Thailand. In the morning you will be offered scrambled eggs, scrambled eggs or boiled eggs, sausages, toasts and pancakes. Moreover, the menu is the same every day. In this regard, few of the guests had breakfast daily at the hotel. Many preferred in the morning either to eat food purchased at the mashinakh (mobile stalls), or a snack in one of the nearby cafes.

Beach

According to tourists, from East Sea Resort to the nearest beach can be reached on foot in about seven minutes. Here you can rent sunbeds and umbrellas. True, the bottom near the shore is quite rocky, so you can injure your legs. Our compatriots advise going a little to the left to the wild beach. Sun loungers are not available for rent here, but swimming is quite comfortable. In general, experienced travelers warn that Pattaya is not the place where you should count on an unforgettable beach vacation. Swimming here vacationers mainly go to nearby islands.
