Hotel Lou Lou a Beach Resort 3 * (United Arab Emirates, Sharjah): photo, description and reviews of travelers

The Gulf Coast is a place that is popular with tourists. Moreover, the UAE is a country that is developing rapidly and has a lot to offer tourists. On one of the secluded beaches is a cozy hotel complex Lou Lou a Beach Resort 3 *. So what conditions can be expected?





Where is the hotel? Short description

Naturally, the traveler is primarily interested in the question of where to look for the Lou Lou a Beach Resort 3 * hotel. Sharjah is a vibrant tourist town located just a 30-minute drive from Dubai. It has its own airport, which can be reached in 15 minutes. By the way, the hotel was built on the seashore, next to the blue waves of the bay. The town center with its markets and entertainment is about 3 km away.

Hotel Lou Lou a Beach Resort 3 * is a large building, which was built in 1993. By the way, the last restoration was carried out in 2008. After it was followed by several cosmetic repairs, so today the hotel meets all standards.





Hotel Lou Lou a Beach Resort 3 * (Sharjah): what do the rooms look like?

By the standards of the country, the hotel is considered small, as it consists of 135 rooms - there are standard rooms, as well as superior rooms with spacious balconies and breathtaking sea views.

All rooms are spacious, bright, with large windows. Some have a fairly spacious terrace or balcony. Naturally, every traveler can count on the availability of modern technology, including a telephone, radio and a convenient, powerful air conditioning system. In the evenings, you can relax watching your favorite satellite channels. There is a minibar, but its filling is paid.

The room has a modernly equipped bathroom with shower or bathtub (in some rooms). There is a toilet, a large mirror, a washbasin. Guests are also provided with hygiene products and clean, large towels.





Beach holiday

Naturally, many travelers choose the UAE as their vacation destination precisely because of the presence of picturesque beaches. Hotel Lou Lou a Beach Resort 3 * owns its own stretch of coast, to which its territory directly opens. The entrance to the sea here is convenient and cleared, but it is still worthwhile to get suitable shoes. Sun beds and umbrellas are free to use. But other water activities, including the use of water transport, are paid, but the prices are relatively affordable.

Food, entertainment and additional services

First, pay attention to the issue of nutrition. Accommodation at the hotel Lou Lou a Beach Resort 3 * includes free breakfast. The hotel also has a 24-hour restaurant where you can eat for a reasonable fee. The lounge bar offers a wide selection of drinks, but it is worth saying right away that alcohol is prohibited here.

Naturally, guests are offered a number of additional services. For example, here you can exchange currency at any convenient time (the rate, according to reviews, is not bad). You can deposit some things with the administrator. There is also a conference room for 700 people and a fairly modern business center. UAE, Lou Lou a Beach Resort 3 *: what do tourists say?

The opinion of travelers who have already managed to visit a particular place is a storehouse of useful information. So what they say about the hotel Lou Lou a Beach Resort 3 *? The reviews are mostly positive, as the quality of accommodation justifies the cost. The rooms are cozy and comfortable, you can count on high-quality daily cleaning. The proximity and privacy of the beach is also a very positive moment. The food is tasty, although sometimes monotonous. The hotel staff is friendly and sociable, always ready to help and suggest something. The hotel is perfect for tourists who prefer a relaxing beach holiday.
