Movenpick Jumeirah Beach 5 * (UAE, Dubai, Jumeirah): description, service, reviews

The UAE is one of the most popular destinations for beach lovers . In our article we want to talk about one of the five-star complexes of the famous Dubai - Movenpick Jumeirah Beach 5 *.

A bit about the hotel ...

Modern Movenpick Jumeirah Beach 5 * is located in the complex "Jumeirah Beach", which offers to visit the most interesting entertainment venues. Convenient location in the city center near the coast is one of the many advantages of the establishment. Near the hotel there is a beach shopping center, where the doors of restaurants and shops are open for visitors.

The hotel complex is located very close to the beaches of the Persian Gulf. The hotel offers luxurious apartments with sea views and excellent infrastructure.



From Dubai Airport, you can get to Movenpick Jumeirah Beach 5 * in just thirty minutes. The location of the hotel in all respects is very convenient and advantageous. Going beyond its walls, you find yourself in the very center of a large modern city. And if guests of other hotels organize trips to the famous Dubai Mall, then guests of Movenpick Jumeriah Beach 5 * can go on foot to the shopping center located next to the hotel at any time.

Beach vacation

Movenpick Hotel Jumeirah Beach 5 * is located in close proximity to the beach. The road to the coast takes no more than three to five minutes. Jumeirah Beach is one of the best stretches of the coast of Dubai. It stretched for a distance of two kilometers along the famous promenade "Wok". The beach is free, but it is equipped with showers, toilets and changing rooms. A lot of playgrounds, sections with street exercise machines are equipped on its territory, there are also green lawns and a treadmill. It is worth noting that the beach is incredibly popular among tourists and locals. There are always a lot of people here.



The popularity of this stretch of coast is explained by the fact that there is no need to pay entry fees, and the infrastructure is no worse than on private beaches. Local patrols pay close attention to safety, which is important for tourists. There are also rescue stations where real rescuers are on duty around the clock.

Holidays in November

Weather in the UAE in November favors holidaymakers. It is this month that the largest number of tourists is observed. And this is due to the fact that the heat is gradually subsiding. The weather in the UAE in November is becoming more favorable for all kinds of events: sporting events, tournaments, championships, festivals and tourist excursions. True, in Dubai, air temperature is higher than in other emirates. On average, there is + 30-32 degrees. Even at night, the readings of thermometers do not fall below the mark of + 17-18 degrees. Therefore, in the evenings you can take exciting walks along the coast of the Persian Gulf. The new promenade is literally created to have a good time here.

November is considered a comfortable time to relax in the Emirates. If before this there is heat, then with the onset of autumn, the situation changes slightly. Resting in November, you can see local attractions. The weather is favorable not only for beach vacations, but also for active travel and shopping.
