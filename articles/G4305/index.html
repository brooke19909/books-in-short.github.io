DIY do-it-yourself winter crafts for kindergarten and school made of natural material (photo)

Parents, immediately connecting all their imagination and imagination, begin to invent something original and very effective, so that everyone likes it. And the baby will not experience any special feelings, because he did not do winter crafts in kindergarten with his own hands. And though she is very beautiful, but he did not participate in its manufacture.

The main thing is participation

Psychologists believe that the child should prepare the crafts themselves, and parents can help him in something. And this will affect its development, self-esteem.

New Year is a magical holiday that all children are waiting for. Do not count how many different crafts you can prepare with your child to participate in a winter school or kindergarten exhibition! The child will be happy to join the creative process. It will be very interesting for him. Children love to sculpt, stick and paint something. And here are a few options for how to make winter crafts yourself. A photo of the finished work in the article is for clarity. Based on the description of the creative process, you can make your own corrections and changes.



Snowman from a plastic plate

No need for a kindergarten child to offer to make too complicated winter crafts with their own hands. He can‚Äôt do it on his own, and it‚Äôs completely uninteresting to watch how mom masters and glues something. Offer to try to make such a cute snowman, which can then be hung on a Christmas tree.

It‚Äôs better to get creative while playing. First, you need to interest the child by asking which fairy-tale character you can fashion from snow, and then suggest making it from improvised materials. A 4-5 year old baby with her mother‚Äôs help will be able to make a winter do-it-yourself craft from an ordinary plastic plate, paper and paints.

On the table in front of the child should lay out everything that is needed in the process. He must independently determine what part to work with at each stage.

So, take a deep plastic plate. A snowman's face will be made of it. It should be in a circle to make incisions of 1-1.5 cm.

Next up is work with colored paper. Adults should help the child draw on a sheet of appropriate color a carrot, several circles for the mouth, a beautiful hat and jewelry for the toy. Eyes can also be made of paper, but purchased ones, those designed for applications and fabric toys, will look prettier.

Invite your child to try to cut out some detail. Adapting to scissors is not so easy to small handles. But carving is a great method for developing fine motor skills. In the end, if the baby fails, adults will always help.

When all the details are prepared, you can begin to design a snowman. First, the child glues the eyes one by one, then with the help of PVA glue - a nose from a carrot and a mouth from black circles. To make the snowman look elegant and festive, you should stick a hat on his head and decorate it to your taste. Glossy paper, foil, beads and rhinestones can be used. It remains to attach a rope, by which it will be possible to hang a snowman on a Christmas tree.

This type of winter crafts (do it yourself) for the kindergarten are very suitable. The child does everything almost independently, and in the creative process, motor skills, imagination, and thinking also develop.



The game of designers. Family crafts for kindergarten - sleigh and snowman

Since the teacher gave the task to the competition to make winter crafts in the kindergarten with her own hands, it is necessary to fulfill it. And best of all, by connecting the whole family to creativity, play designers.

Each participant will have his own task. Of course, the child will not only be busy in the process, but will also head the position of the head of the family design group. Let the kid try to reveal his organizational abilities and feel like a real leader. This will increase his self-esteem and affect the formation of character.

Creative process

Participants receive a task from the leader. Mom will take care of a snowman. From a synthetic winterizer with the help of a needle and thread, two balls should be made for the body of a winter fairy-tale hero. From threads, multi-colored fabric and decorative wire wrapped in fluffy yarn, make him hands, headphones, pompons on the body and carrots. After that you need to sew all the details of the snowman figure with a thread, finish the decorative elements, and glue the eyes. Mom can be congratulated - she did her job.

The kid, although a creative director who monitors how to make winter crafts with his own hands for the kindergarten, must also take part. His task is to decorate ice cream sticks with multi-colored paints. For a baby 3-4 years old, such a task is within its power. If the child is older, you can entrust him with the cutting of small snowflakes from snow-white napkins and their subsequent gluing on a sled. Then you need to glue the ice cream sticks so that you get a sled, attach a rope to them, and also firmly seat the snowman so that he does not accidentally fall at the crucial moment.





Magic snowflake

Sometimes, it can be difficult to figure out what kind of children's DIY crafts (winter) can be made. It‚Äôs worth connecting a fantasy. From pasta and vermicelli of different shapes, it‚Äôs really possible to make funny pendants on a Christmas tree in the form of snowflakes or angels. Children of 5-6 years old will cope with such a craft. But adults cannot be left unattended.

First you need to pick up pasta of various shapes and lay out an ornament of them on the table. If you liked the picture, you can begin to work. The child should be invited to first paint each detail in a specific color with acrylic paint. It will turn out to be very beautiful snowflake of gold or silver color. Cotton pad angel

Offer the child 5-6 years old from cotton pads to make a Christmas toy in the shape of an angel. Do-it-yourself winter crafts of this type are also suitable for school. You can come up with a whole composition!

The principle of how to turn cotton pads into angels is very simple. The disk is folded in a triangle and fixed with glue. A large bead sits on glue from above - this will be the head. The nimbus is made of golden filament tow or wire. First attach a loop to the bead, after which it will be possible to hang the ornament on the Christmas tree. The wings are also cut out of a cotton pad, and then spread out and stick to the body. Craft is ready!

Christmas toy from knitted socks

And such an idea for mothers who are ready to donate a pair of knitted socks for children's creativity or know how to work with knitting needles. Try to make winter crafts (with your own hands) with your child in the form of decorative Christmas balls. For needlewomen who know how to knit, create decor for such a New Year‚Äôs toy - it‚Äôs an hour. But there are mothers for whom this type of needlework is unfamiliar. How to be in this case?

First, pick up a sock with a beautiful winter ornament and a suitable Christmas ball in diameter. Then you need to cut off the upper part of the sock, process the edges so that they do not bloom, put on a knitted cylinder on a Christmas tree toy and fix it. The decor, like the craft, is ready!



Doll snowman

And this type of winter crafts for the garden with your own hands is very simple to do. They are suitable for a school exhibition. Moreover, according to this principle, it is possible to make various winter fairy-tale characters: Santa Claus, his granddaughter, various animals.

Even the smallest children (three to four years old) can handle the creative process. Mom will have to help a little so that the craft has an aesthetic and finished look.

After the winter crafts made with your own hands are ready, the figures can be left in the kindergarten. They are useful in order, for example, to show kids a New Year's fairy tale with the participation of such dolls.

How to make a puppet snowman out of a sock? Step-by-step instruction

You will need scissors, a plain white sock, a synthetic winterizer, buttons, several beads, a strong thread with a needle, a colored rag of fabric.

First you need to cut off the toe with the heel from the top of the toe. Sew one edge to make a pouch. Fill it with a padding polyester. Next, you need to divide the bag into two parts, tying it with a strong thread, so that the head and body of the snowman are obtained.

After you need to sew the beads on the face. It will be eyes and nose. From colored matter, it is necessary to cut a scarf and wrap the neck of the figure with it. You can make a bow out of the same fabric if, as conceived by the snowman, a girl.

From the remaining cut off part of the sock, you can make a hat and put it on the head of a homemade toy. It remains to sew a few buttons to the body. Puppet snowman is ready. So that he would not be bored, he should make him a girlfriend or friend from another sock for the company.





"Winter House" - craft composition

When thinking about what to do winter crafts with your own hands for school, you should consider the age of the student. Agree, if a fifth grader brings an applique or a decorated Christmas tree toy, it will not work to impress and fully demonstrate his creative talents, it is very simple. What if you create a whole composition on a winter theme? To build a house and a courtyard with a wicker fence, to decorate everything with artificial snow, to mold a snowman? It will turn out very original. Such winter crafts for the garden, made with your own hands, can also be brought to the exhibition. Of course, everyone will understand that most of the work was done by an adult. On the other hand, the composition is very beautiful and worthy of praise.



How and what is the composition "Winter House" made of

Make it easy if you have patience. The house, courtyard and wicker fence are built from rods made of newspaper tubes painted with stain. All parts are glued to silicone glue. There are many options for making a snowman. It is possible from cotton wool, pompons, threads, socks with a synthetic winterizer. Cotton wool and polystyrene balls will replace snow.

Such winter crafts always look spectacular. With your own hands, you can make parts and complement the composition from natural material . For example, in the courtyard of a winter home, you can install a miniature basket filled with acorns or mountain ash. From chestnuts or nutshells it is possible to make animals (dog, hedgehog).

Here are some beautiful and original DIY winter crafts that you can make. The creative process is always interesting. And when the results of one's labor are visible, special sensations come from looking at the work.
