Parador Beach Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Alanya): description, service, reviews

The southern town of Alanya is one of the most inexpensive resorts in Turkey. To rent here comfortable rooms at a low cost, those who wish to spend a vacation by the sea will not make much work. Most Russian tourists in Alanya stay in three-star budget hotels. Such hotels offer their guests quite decent accommodation and good service. For example, many domestic sea lovers rent rooms in Turkey in Alanya at the Parador Beach Hotel 3 * . Travelers at this specialized forum of tour operators respond quite well to this hotel.

Location

This hotel, according to many tourists, was once built in a very convenient location. It is located directly in the city of Alanya, right on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. The road from the Parador Beach Hotel 3 * (Turkey) to the coast takes no more than 2-3 minutes. At the same time, residents of the complex can reach the city center in just 30 minutes on foot.





This hotel is very well located in terms of views from the windows of the rooms. From the balconies of some rooms rented at this hotel, its guests can admire one of the most interesting sights of Alanya - the ancient citadel on the hill. This ancient fortress, among other things, is also very beautifully illuminated in the evenings.

Russian vacationers who have rented 3 * rooms at Parador Beach Hotel usually arrive in Alanya to Gazipasa Airport. You can get from here to the hotel in just 15-20 minutes. Sometimes domestic travelers who decide to relax in Turkey with accommodation at this hotel first fly to Antalya Airport. This transport air hub is located about 130 km from Alanya. The road from here on a tour operator's transfer bus to the hotel will, of course, take much more time - about 2.5 hours.

What is a hotel

Hosts Alanya guests Parador Beach Hotel 3 * (Turkey) in one stylish neat multi-story building. Despite the fact that this hotel is located directly in Alanya, and therefore it belongs to the type of city, it has its own territory. Judging by the reviews, the courtyard of this complex looks very neat and attractive. Its decoration is in harmony with the design of the hotel itself and contributes to creating a holiday mood in the south for guests.

The check-in time at this hotel, unlike many other Turkish ones, is 12:00 h. In addition to cash, cards are also accepted for various services at this hotel. With pets, tourists are not accommodated in this hotel. Smoking is prohibited throughout the hotel.
