Pimrada Hotel (Phuket, Patong): reviews and photos of tourists

If you plan to spend a vacation on the island of Phuket (Thailand), you are accustomed to outdoor activities and do not want to spend much on a hotel, then the three-star Pimrada Hotel (Patong) can be a very suitable option for you.



Description, location and photo

Pimrada Hotel is located in the heart of Patong, a 10-minute walk from Bangla Road and Jang Ceylon Shopping Center. The hotel is located in an area with well-developed infrastructure - bars, restaurants, clubs, shops, supermarkets, spas, markets, travel agencies, etc. As for the nearest beach, you can walk to it in 15 minutes. Phuket Airport is 50 kilometers from the hotel.

Pimrada Hotel (Patong) 3 * is a small city hotel. He has no own territory, as well as a swimming pool. The housing stock is represented by 32 standard and deluxe rooms. All apartments have comfortable furniture, a flat-screen TV with 50 cable channels, a refrigerator with a minibar, air conditioning, an electric kettle and tea set, a safe, a bathroom. Please note that windows and balconies are not available in all room categories. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel. Rooms are cleaned and towels are changed daily. Beach towels are also provided free of charge . They must be taken in the morning and returned in the evening.

There is a cafe on the ground floor of Pimrada Hotel for tourists, where you can taste both Thai and European dishes. Also, guests can rent a motorbike, use the laundry and dry cleaning services, exchange currency, book an interesting tour. In addition, for the convenience of guests, the hotel has an Internet cafe. The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day. Therefore, at any time, you can contact the administrator with a request or question.



Pimrada Hotel: traveler reviews

Even if you consider yourself an unassuming person and do not plan to spend a lot of time while traveling abroad at a hotel, you will surely still want to live in a comfortable room with comfortable furniture. Not to be mistaken in choosing a hotel will help you study the reviews of those people who have already stayed in the place you are interested in. After all, they can share their opinions on the real advantages and disadvantages of a particular hotel, based on their own experience. Today we invite you to find out what impressions our compatriots had on their stay at Pimrada Hotel in Phuket. Immediately, we note that the vast majority of tourists in their choice were not disappointed. A confirmation of this is the rating of the hotel, based on the ratings of its guests. So, it is 4.5 of the maximum possible five points.



Location

As travelers note in their reviews, the location of Pimrada Hotel 3 * is unlikely to completely suit those who do not want to go to the nearest beach for more than five minutes. So, according to tourists, the hotel is really far from the sea. On the way to the nearest Patong Beach, guests take about 15-20 minutes. However, the path passes by the Jung Ceylon shopping center and Bangla Road, where you can buy something, have a snack, have a look at a bar, etc. There is also a market not far from the hotel where you can buy fresh fruits and seafood. In the vicinity of Pimrada there are also massage parlors, supermarkets, souvenir shops, scooter and car rental points and much more. As our compatriots note, if you are accustomed to outdoor activities, then this hotel is perfect for you.



Housing stock

Most of the guests in general were satisfied with the 3 * rooms offered by Pimrada Hotel. However, many tourists pay attention to the fact that the standard type apartments do not have windows. For some, this may be a plus (no noise from the street is heard, saving), but for someone this situation is not at all suitable. Therefore, consider this fact in advance so as not to experience disappointment during your vacation. Moreover, you are unlikely to be able to change the standard deluxe room even with a surcharge already in place, since the hotel is in great demand among tourists, and there are almost never any free apartments here.

In general, according to vacationers, the rooms in Pimrada are quite spacious, bright, with fresh repairs, new furniture and modern appliances. Everything works without interruption. And if some kind of breakdown happens, then after a message at the reception, hotel staff will quickly fix all the malfunctions.



Nutrition

When booking a room in the hotel in question, you can immediately pay for breakfast for the entire stay. However, judging by the reviews, many tourists prefer the option that does not provide any food. If you still chose breakfast, then they will be held in the hotel cafe in the buffet format. The choice of dishes here, of course, cannot be called very diverse, but in order to eat in the morning this is quite enough. So, for breakfast you will be offered fried eggs, stewed vegetables, pasta, rice, bacon, sausages, nuggets, fresh fruits and vegetables, cereals, toasts, tea and coffee.

During the day, you can also eat in the hotel's cafe for an additional fee or in the many cafes in the neighborhood. According to our compatriots, here they cook very tasty almost everywhere, and the prices are quite reasonable. Also, many enjoy visiting the local market, where you can buy fresh seafood, which you will immediately cook for a very small surcharge. Experienced tourists are advised not to bypass the shingles (mobile food stalls).

Sea

As already noted, at least a quarter of an hour will have to walk to the nearest Patong Beach from Pimrada Hotel (Phuket). Moreover, the sea in this part of Phuket, according to many travelers, is not very clean. Therefore, most tourists prefer to visit other beaches of the island (Kata, Karon, Paradise and others). You can get to them by taxi or tuk-tuk. However, this is not cheap. Therefore, many vacationers prefer to rent a motobike. In this case, you will receive complete freedom of movement in Phuket and will be able to independently visit all its beaches and attractions, while saving a lot of money.
