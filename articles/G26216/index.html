Dar El Olf 4 * (Tunisia / Hammamet): description and reviews

Holidays in Tunisia attract many. These are chic white beaches, and the tender waters of the Mediterranean Sea, and thalassotherapy, and a rich excursion program, and comfortable hotels of various categories, among which 4-star hotels, for example Dar El Olf 4 * Hammamet, are still the most popular.



Features of rest in Tunisia

Despite the fact that this country is located on the African continent, there is no sweltering heat, at the same time it is warm and comfortable throughout the year. In a word, here is a real Eden for beach lovers. In the winter months here, as well as throughout the Mediterranean, it is hardly possible to swim in the sea, however, walking around the local sights, sunbathing in the gentle winter sun, breathing in the fresh sea air will even work out very well. The most favorable months for thalassotherapy classes and "lazy" relaxation on the beach are September-November, that is, the so-called velvet season. The shores of Tunisia are especially fond of fans of diving, as well as yachting. Tunisia has a great many small resort towns, which until recently were fishing villages. In this article we want to tell you a little about the Hammamet resort and the actual hotel Dar El Olf 4 * (Tunisia).

In view of all this, Hammamet is deservedly considered the tourist capital of Tunisia.



How to get there

From our capital to Tunisia daily scheduled flights. In addition to the capital, there is also a completely new Enfida, as well as Monastir airports. You can get here only on a charter flight, and even then only in the high season. Estimated flight time is 4 hours. If you bought a ticket to the Dar El Olf 4 * hotel, then buy a ticket to one of these cities. There should be no significant difference. From everywhere, the hotel is not so far away. If you need to immediately pay for a transfer service when making a tour, then you may not even think about the way from the airport to it, everything will be smooth and quick.

Hotel Description Dar El Olf 4 *

This hotel is quite fresh, and it is located in the presentable resort of Yasmine Hammamet. Nearby there are golf courses as well as a marina. The four-story building is in the traditional Moorish style, but not on the first line. Nevertheless, from here to the sea is only three hundred meters. The hotel has a chic spa area, whose area is 480 square meters. m

Number of rooms

In total, the hotel has 174 rooms of three categories (standard double, standard single and family). All of them, regardless of type, are equipped with air conditioning, balconies, satellite TV and Wi-Fi. The area of ‚Äã‚Äãa standard single room is 30 m 2 . It is furnished with a wide semi-double bed, sofa, desk, wardrobe, equipped with ironing facilities. There is access to adjoining rooms. Balcony and windows overlook the pool and garden. The bathroom is equipped with a bath, it has bath and toiletries. The standard double room at the Dar El Olf 4 * hotel can be of two types: either with one double bed or with two single beds. Size and all amenities - as in a single room. The family is almost twice as spacious as the previous ones. It is designed to accommodate 4 persons. There is one family bed and two single beds. The rooms overlook the garden and the sea. The premises are cleaned daily, and linen is changed only once every 7 days. This rule often causes tourists dissatisfaction with the Dar El Olf 4 * hotel. Reviews of many vacationers, even if they are positive, contain dissatisfied notes precisely with the infrequent change of bedding.



Infrastructure

The hotel has a restaurant, bar, cafe, lobby, shops and souvenir stalls, elevator. In front of the building is a swimming pool with a sunbathing area (with umbrellas and sun loungers). As already noted, the hotel has a magnificent spa center where clients can undergo balneological procedures, mud therapy, steam baths, etc. In addition, there is a beauty salon and a sports club. To entertain tourists in the evenings there is a disco on the dance floor, as well as animation. There is a games room with board games, a bowling alley, a volleyball court, table and tennis and much more.

Nutrition

This hotel does not operate on an all-inclusive basis. Tourists can choose
BB (breakfast only) or NB (half board). Le Vert et Jaune Restaurant serves international dishes during meals. And throughout the day, you can satisfy your hunger snacks in the snack bar or in the outdoor cafe.



Beach

The hotel is located at a distance of 300-350 meters from the seashore. At the private beach, guests can use the inventory for free. There is also a bar and cafe. There is a net for beach volleyball, as well as many water activities (for a fee).

Dar El Olf 4 * (Tunisia): reviews of former guests

Of course, the opinions that can be found about this hotel are subjective. However, most of them are friendly. Of course, there is dissatisfaction with some features of the service. For example, the fact that linen and towels are changed less often than we would like. And some are not satisfied with the taste of too spicy dishes. And the rest of the guests are satisfied with both the service and the internal structure of the Dar El Olf 4 * hotel.
