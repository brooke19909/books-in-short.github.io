What to see in Moscow for tourists

Moscow is a large city with a rich history and beautiful architecture, known for its centuries-old history, established traditions and unique Russian culture. Probably, every person has a desire to visit the capital of Russia at least once, but then the question arises before him: "What to see in Moscow?"





In order to capture the most interesting in your memory, it is important to foresee in advance what places in Moscow are worth seeing. Therefore, now you are given the opportunity to get acquainted with the main attractions of the capital.

If you are interested in what to see in Moscow, here are some of the main attractions of the city.

1. The Kremlin is the main political, spiritual, architectural and historical part of the city. Also there is the residence of the President of Russia. On the territory of the Kremlin you can see and visit the Armory, which is part of the Grand Palace of the Kremlin, the Diamond Fund of Russia, where you can get acquainted with a unique historical collection of jewelry, the Historical Museum. From Manezhnaya Square offers a beautiful view of St. Basil's Cathedral. And there you can stroll through the Taynitsky Garden and look inside the Assumption, Arkhangelsk and Annunciation churches.





2. The famous Red Square is located near the Kremlin. Walking along it, you can visit the mausoleum of Lenin, and you can go to the eternal flame and watch the changing of the guard. Also on Red Square you can visit St. Basil's Cathedral.

3. The Tretyakov Gallery - the largest collection of unique original paintings by different artists. There you can stroll through the spacious halls and plunge into the world of painting. The gallery often hosts various exhibitions.

4. Vorobyovy Gory - this is what you need to see in Moscow, because it offers an excellent view of most of the city. The observation deck is one of the favorite places of many visitors and residents of the city. From here you can also see the building of Moscow State University, which is known for its unique architecture. Moscow University has a high 12-ton calm, and the total height of the building is 240 m.

5. Kolomenskoye is a historical museum-reserve, which is known for its architecture and ancient history. Once it was the king‚Äôs estate - back in the 14th century. Then, in the 17th century, they built a large wooden palace and planted a garden. And now you can get into the estate through the gates with a tower clock and enjoy the historical architecture.

This is not all that is considered necessary in Moscow to see. In the capital you can visit the Novodevichy Convent, which was built in the 16th century and is still operating. It is also worth going to the Kuskovo estate, which is also called the Moscow Versailles. You will be amazed by the architecture of the residence, but most of all you will admire the collection of glass and ceramics from different countries from antiquity to modernity.

But, if you are going on a family trip, then you probably thought about what to see for children in Moscow. Regular museum excursions may seem boring to them. Therefore, you should go on a voyage along the river on a yacht. Such a trip will surely cause delight among children, and at the same time, it will be possible to see the capital from the other side - from the side of the river. It offers a completely different, no less impressive view of the city. To ensure that children are not bored, special events for children are held on the yacht, in addition, you can have excellent lunch there with excellent dishes.





What else is interesting to see in Moscow? Take a walk in the Gorky park, because it is one of the most famous and largest parks in the capital. On the alleys of the park you can find many recreational activities, auctions and complexes. And if you came to Moscow in the winter, then the Gorky Park has the largest ice rink in the city.

One day is not enough to get acquainted with all the sights of the capital. And now you already know what you can see in Moscow. You will stroll through ancient places with a rich history, amazing architecture and interesting traditions, and your memory will capture for a long time the most interesting moments of your trip.
