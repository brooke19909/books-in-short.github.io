The best vacation for the whole family - the water park "Rainbow" (Cherepovets)

Most recently, the first water park in the Vologda Oblast, the Raduzhny water park, opened in Cherepovets.

General information about the water park





This is a kind of large entertainment center, which consists of two zones. The main area consists of a large swimming pool, a small outdoor pool and a pool designed for children.

Waterpark "Rainbow" (Cherepovets), offers all its guests not only have a good time, riding down the slides, but also the opportunity to relax in the jacuzzi, discover the hammam, soak up the hydromassage waterfalls. Residents of the city are very fond of this water park for its salt room, which is located in the zone of increased comfort. Here you can improve your health and enjoy the imitation of the sea breeze.

For young children, the water park "Rainbow" (Cherepovets) provides the opportunity to spend time in the children's playroom. Children and adult visitors are constantly monitored by experienced lifeguards. Thanks to this, the management of the water park can guarantee each of its guests a pleasant and safe pastime.

Water disinfection

Waterpark "Rainbow" (Cherepovets) is famous for its water treatment system, which is the most modern in the Vologda region. It is represented by four completely independent pools.

Pools are cleaned with chlorination and ultraviolet radiation. The latter method is no longer used in any basin of the Vologda Oblast.

Cost of rest





Everyone who wants to relax properly, it is best to start by viewing the official website, where there is complete information about all the services that the Raduzhny water park (Cherepovets) offers its visitors. Prices on this site are presented in several categories, so you need to pay special attention to them.

A distinctive feature of this entertainment center is the opportunity to spend a children's birthday here. Especially for such a holiday, there are animators in the water park who are ready to entertain the child and his guests for an amount from one and a half to four thousand rubles, depending on the time and the chosen program.
For little vacationers, in the water park there is an akvanyan who will look after children for 350 rubles per hour. And when purchasing a subscription, the cost will be only 250 rubles, which will significantly save money.
Waterpark "Rainbow" (Cherepovets) offers special conditions for students. So, a swim in two and a half hours for a group of ten people will cost children only 230 rubles per person. However, this promotion is valid only on weekdays.
Prices in the water park vary greatly depending on which zone the vacationer plans to spend time in. If he wants to use the jacuzzi, breathe in the salt cave or visit the sauna, he will have to pay extra for providing a comfort zone.

On the site you can also track information about promotions that are constantly changing.

Reviews of the water park "Rainbow"





On many Vologda sites you can see useful information about how good the water park "Rainbow" (Cherepovets). Reviews about this water park are of great importance for city guests who are considering where to spend their free time.

Guests of the city who have already visited the water park "Rainbow" (Cherepovets), praise this recreation center, as here you can not only freely ride from the slides, but also relax your body and soul.

Of course, there are also negative reviews that are completely drowned among the many positive reviews. Moreover, most of the positive reviews were left by guests from the regional capital who come to Cherepovets as a family for an unforgettable vacation.
