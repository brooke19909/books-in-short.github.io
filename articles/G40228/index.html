Description of the resorts of Greece. Unforgettable vacation

What do we know about Greece? Ancient architectural monuments, the Mediterranean and Aegean, Olympus, scenic spots, clean air, islands. Each stone here tells its own story, all cities differ from each other in their unique uniqueness.





Kastoria

Why do many people choose a trip to Greece as a holiday? Because there is a lot of sun, resorts, beaches, mild climate and diverse programs for tourists: both sightseeing and entertaining. If you are interested in this country, first read the description of the resorts of Greece, each of which is good in its own way. Let's start with Kastoria - a city located on the shore of a mountain lake Orestiada. It attracts tourists with clean mountain air and pristine silence. Paralia Katerini

The description of the resorts of Greece should definitely include the village of Paralia Katerinis, a complete antidote to Kastoria. This is a place of fun, discos, a riot of youth and large companies. Types of outdoor activities are very diverse: from climbing the famous Olympus with excursions to Greek cities to visiting monasteries in the rocks. This monastic complex Meteora ("soaring in the air") is called the eighth wonder of the world. For sports enthusiasts, there is a water park, and golf, basketball, and tennis courts.





Halkidiki

A complete description of the resorts of Greece is impossible without islands and peninsulas. One of the latter is Halkidiki, a picturesque place with beaches and bays. Most of all, couples love the resort, as there is clear sea water and wonderful air. It is considered the most environmentally friendly mass tourism destination in the world.

Chania, Mykonos, Rhodes and Corfu

Undoubtedly, the best resorts in Greece are islands. These include the famous Corfu with sandy beaches and cozy bays. Chania - among the clean and wide beaches of the island there is one very remarkable, with pink sand. In the south of the Aegean Sea is Mykonos, one of the most fashionable Greek resorts. It should be noted the island of Rhodes, washed immediately by two seas (Aegean and Mediterranean) . It is developing rapidly and is becoming increasingly popular among vacationers. Luxury hotels and various entertainment venues are concentrated here. Swim on about. Rhodes can, starting in May, ends the season in October.

Crete

And, of course, the description of the resorts of Greece should include its largest island - Crete. Its shore is a series of bays with beaches of sand and pebbles. The climate of the island is considered tropical, that is, dry and hot, which is very attractive for most tourists.





Kos island

The resorts of Greece are diverse and beautiful, the description of which can be continued for hours. Kos Island is one of the most successful holiday destinations among Europeans due to the abundance of greenery and flowers, palm trees and sandy beaches. If you want to try local cuisine, you should definitely go to one of the many restaurants located here, at the same time, and admire the panorama that opens. Here you will be offered a boat or boat trip, you can go windsurfing. Beginners of sailing will be taught the basics at school, and for professionals there is a windsurfing center.

Visit Greece - and your vacation will turn into a fabulous and unforgettable trip!
