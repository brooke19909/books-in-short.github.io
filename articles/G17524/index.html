A great place to relax - Kaluga region. Holiday Village: Which one to choose?

Kaluga Region (Russian Federation) is very often visited by tourists from all over the country. Choosing a place to stay is hard enough. The article will present good options that will not greatly affect the pocket of vacationers and help to spend a vacation in a good mood.

Golitsyno

Golitsyno is located in an ecologically clean area and is an eco club. Its exact location is the village of Semenovka (Kaluga region). The recreation center is 30 km away from the center. The territory of the complex itself stretches over 44 hectares. Many plants have been planted here, so there is no problem with landscaping. The infrastructure of the base is also well developed.

Golitsyno will be a great solution if you need to spend a relaxing and memorable vacation. You can come here both with your family and in complete solitude.





"Dove"

A lot of guests receive the Kaluga region. The recreation center "Dove" is a cozy place that visitors are somewhat reminiscent of a Russian village. Residents are lodged in a wooden house, made in the national style. The first floor has a veranda. There are also sleeping rooms (there are three of them). The set of furniture that is located in the living area may vary depending on the requirements of the guests. Here, as a standard, there are a table, chairs, bedside tables, tables, etc.

You can rent a house either completely or individually. Children under 5 can stay for free, and children from 5 to 10 are subject to a small discount.

Meals are available at an additional cost. The menu is dominated by dishes of Russian cuisine. All food products that are used in the cooking process are homemade.





"Forest"

The recreation center "Forest" (Kaluga region) is a complex on 682 hectares. On the territory there are numerous meadows, forests and more than 10 lakes. Civilization is far from this base, which captivates many tourists. Plunging into the atmosphere of silence and nature, you can forget about the pressing problems for a long time.

The recreation center offers a huge list of entertainment. Thanks to this, people will find vacation more intense and enjoyable. Qualified specialists work with guests, which will allow organizing all events competently.

Lesnoye is a base of 96 rooms. There are suites, and standards, and junior suites. All rooms are spacious and bright.

Meals are provided in the restaurant, where 300 people can eat at the same time. Chefs here prepare not only national dishes, but also Italian, French, Caucasian and others.

Next to it is a natural park. The territory itself is as clean as possible, there is healing air. You can relax here at any time of the year, without exception. Ugra

Due to the incredibly beautiful nature, the Ugra recreation center constantly hosts many guests. Kaluga region has long been popular with tourists. The complex is located on the coast of the river of the same name. If guests appear in a large company, then they are offered to settle in a cottage; in the case when a small family arrives, the administration settles them in hotel rooms.

All accommodations are designed for 2 people. From furniture here you can find chairs, cabinets, bedside tables, dressers. Shower and toilet are in each room. During the cold season, heating is provided. There is always hot water.

Meals are provided in the cafe-bar, located in the recreation center. You can cook yourself in the kitchens, which are in the rooms.





Pine River

The country hotel is located on a beautiful river, the "house" of which is the Kaluga region. The recreation center provides a wide range of services, ranging from entertainment to wellness treatments. The staff is more than hospitable, nature delights new guests, and the rooms smell pleasantly of needles.

Visitors are lodged in cottages, which have two floors each. They can accommodate no more than 40 people at a time. If necessary, the administration can provide a cot.

"Pines"

Located next to the Oka River (Kaluga Region) recreation center "Sosny". Here you can spend a vacation with both family and colleagues. Weddings, corporate parties, graduations, banquets, other holidays - all this is organized on the territory of this complex. There are many gazebos, benches. There is a cafe, a bathhouse, halls. The zoo works, in it you can find a rabbit, a pig, a raccoon. Guests are accommodated in cottage type hotels.
