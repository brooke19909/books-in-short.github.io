Seamelia Beach Resort Hotel & Spa 5 * (Turkey, Side): description, service, reviews

Seamelia Beach Resort Hotel & Spa 5 * is a luxury resort hotel located on a private sandy beach in the Evrenseki district, which is located about 5 minutes drive from Side and 50 kilometers from Antalya Airport. Many tourists come here, as this hotel offers 5-star service at fairly low prices for this level. However, the complex has many advantages, and it is worth briefly telling about all of them.

With their description, perhaps, it is worth starting the story about Seamelia Beach Resort Hotel & Spa 5 *. This complex has everything - from free high-speed Wi-Fi to parking.

In Seamelia Beach Resort Hotel & Spa 5 * you can spend your time interestingly. There are two large outdoor pools with terraces and one indoor (heated), Turkish bath and sauna, fitness center, water sports facilities, a night club with a DJ.
