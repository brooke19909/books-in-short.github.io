Hair dryer for curling: an overview of the best models, specifications, reviews

Every day, almost every girl resorts to using a hairdryer. Drying and curling their hair is certainly very convenient, but it is worth remembering that some models are not intended for daily use. This article presents the best hair dryers for curling hair from a variety of brands.

Criterias of choice

It is worth noting that the purchase of a hair dryer for curling requires some knowledge. So, first of all, the buyer should pay attention to the power of the selected product. The optimum value of this indicator is considered to be power from 1,400 to 1,800 watts. There are models with higher power. Their use can lead to severe hair overdrying.

Choosing a hair dryer, do not forget about its functions. The third most important criterion is considered such an indicator as weight. There is an opinion that the heavier the hair dryer for curling, the better the styling result. It is also important to pay attention to the body material of the device. If it is made of heat-resistant plastic, then the device can be bought without fear. If not, then the buyer may not count on the long service life of such a hairdryer with a hair curler.

And finally, it is recommended to pay attention to the number of attached nozzles. As a rule, a curling iron-hair dryer should have at least two nozzles. But it is better to choose the one that has more of them. This will expand the range of possible styling.

Hair Dryer Tips

For those who want to create stylish curls with their own hands, there are a few tips. The first - classic - involves the use of curlers. It is best to wind wet hair on them, fixed with mousse. Then the hair should be dried with a hairdryer and the curlers removed. Curls are ready.

As for the second tip, beauty bloggers decided to use a plastic bottle to implement it. Curling hair with a hairdryer and a bottle is carried out according to the following principle:

  1. A plastic container cuts off the neck and cuts a vertical hole on the side. The hole should hold the neck of the hair dryer.
  2. A strand of hair is pushed into the bottle. A hairdryer is inserted into the hole.
  3. The hair dryer turns on for 10 seconds.
  4. A lock is taken out of the bottle.

In order for the hair curling with the help of a bottle and a hairdryer to be as effective as possible, it is recommended to coat the hair with special fixators. The result from this procedure amazes absolutely everyone.
