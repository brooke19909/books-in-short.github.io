A wonderful place - a zoo in Krasnodar

Do you want to come on an excursion to Krasnodar? Safari Zoo is definitely worth a visit. This is truly a wonderful place. The zoo in Krasnodar began its existence very unusual. The idea about its creation came to the head of the director after he was presented with the green-winged parrot Ara for his birthday . It was he who became the first pet of this institution.

The zoo in Krasnodar opened its doors in 2006. He appeared in the district CHP in the park "Sunny Island". The territory of the entire park is divided into animal habitats, so it becomes clear to young visitors in what climate this or that animal lives in nature.

Features

The zoo in Krasnodar is truly unique. On its territory collected exotic animals from around the world, most of which are listed in the Red Book.

This marvelous park spreads over ten hectares of forest-style land. A lot of work has been done to equip this place. On the territory you can see fancy buildings and marvelous flower beds. Contented and well-groomed animals live in the park.





What could be more fun than watching animals? Each animal has its own enclosure. Special care is provided to each tenant. Park staff love their wards, devotedly work for the benefit and prosperity of this marvelous place.

Animals

Now in the park there are such animals as lambs, kangaroos, striped hyenas, lemurs, fur seals, peacocks, camels, giraffes, gibbons and hippos.

By the way, the latter lives very well here. This worthy beast feels good in the park.

There are even pinnipeds - two walruses Lucy and Dusya. They are trained, so you can see the performance with their participation. In the nearby pool there are another artists performing - seals, plastic chairs are opposite them, so that tourists could sit down while watching this performance.

You can, of course, see predators in the park: a lion, a white tiger, a black panther and a Syrian bear.

Separately, I want to talk about peacocks. At the beginning of the foundation, the director of the park was independently engaged in the cultivation of small peacocks.





Everything would be fine, but the chicks fed on ant eggs, so I had to keep an anthill on the site and feed the babies. At first, the peacocks consumed eggs, and after three days they also began to eat ants. Due to a lack of food, the chicks may die. Fortunately, at that time it was possible to save the chicks, now these amazingly beautiful birds walk freely around the park.

Well-groomed animals of the park annually delight both employees and visitors with an addition in families. For example, in early April of this year, a striped hyena was born here . Such animals reproduce very poorly in captivity, so the appearance of the beast brought a lot of worries, but, of course, joy.

Zoo in Krasnodar: ticket prices and opening hours

The zoo is open from 9am to 9pm. The cost of an adult ticket is 450 rubles, and a child ticket is 350 rubles (children under 14 years old). Admission is free for babies up to two years. A guide can be used at the park. The cost is 500 rubles.





On the territory of the safari park, you can take pictures and video, but you need to pay extra for this.

There are pleasant discounts for groups - an adult who accompanies a team of ten children visits the park completely free of charge.

Little conclusion

In this safari park you can realize your cherished dreams by seeing the most exotic animals of the world. Therefore, if you are thinking whether to go to this place, then, of course, choose the first option. Believe me, you will not regret it! Safari Park and its inhabitants are waiting for you!
