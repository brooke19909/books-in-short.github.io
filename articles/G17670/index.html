Hotel "Sputnik" (Moscow): address, route, photos and reviews of tourists

Built in 1967, the hotel was originally intended to accommodate the functionaries of the All-Union Central Council of Trade Unions and their foreign colleagues. Orientation to the placement of the official public has identified fairly high requirements for safety and comfort. After a change in the political system, the hotel became available to ordinary citizens. Over two decades, a special contingent of customers has formed. For many businessmen who come to the capital on business issues, the Sputnik Hotel (Moscow) is the temporary headquarters. Leninsky Prospekt provides a unique opportunity to quickly reach by car the most important state institutions.

Hotel Description

Sputnik Hotel (Moscow) is a sixteen-story building of a typical Soviet project. Number of rooms - 349 for 532 beds. Service level: 3 stars. In 2006, the premises were repaired and completely refitted. The interior is restrained, no frills, but very stylish. In general, the democratic atmosphere of the hotel meets the requirements of sophisticated guests. The hotel does not have its own recreational area. Children under seven years are free of charge without extra bed. Checkout time - 12 00 .

Location

The hotel is located in the Gagarinsky district on the main highway of the capital, south of the center, 4.5 km from Red Square. Built in the green part of the city near the territory of the park "Neskuchny Sad" and not far from the Park named after Gorky. The distance from Vnukovo Airport is 23 km, from Sheremetyevo-2 - 31 km.

Walking distance to Moscow Business School, Moscow State University, Luzhniki Sports Complex. Due to the proximity to the famous business centers, shopping malls, historical sites, the hotel is very convenient for both business people and tourists.
