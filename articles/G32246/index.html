How to get there

As you probably already understood, it is most convenient to go to the water entertainment town from Krasnodar. Even before the opening of the water park "Niagara" (Adygea), the leadership made sure that the townspeople could easily get to them. And therefore, a free shuttle ring route was launched, running from the Mega supermarket along Gagarin, and making stops at Yablonovka, Magnit, Sberbank, and Lukoil gas station. The first bus leaves at 9:30, and the last from the Niagara water park at 20:30. Shuttles travel at intervals of fifteen to twenty minutes. Those who want to come to the Niagara water park with their own transport will also not experience any problems. Turgenevskoe highway is a well-known city highway. Attractions of the water park "Niagara" in Adygea

Photos taken by visitors indicate that the territory of this entertainment town is huge. At the entrance there is a map of the complex so that visitors do not wander for a long time, but go immediately to the desired attractions. The territory of the water park is divided into three zones: adult, children and relaxation. The latter is a great alternative to sea beaches. Here you can sunbathe on sun loungers under umbrellas, swim in the pools and bask under the jets. In the children's area there are shallow pools, low and safe slides for the kids and a playground. Well, those who are already above 1 m 20 cm are waiting for breathtaking slides. The most popular in the water park Niagara (Adygea) are Volna, Tabagan, Pigtail and Multislide. Rugs are provided for descents. What is pleasantly striking is the fact that many employees of the water park monitor the safety of visitors. Before entering every adult slide there is a stand where the descent height, slope, depth of the pool when diving, permitted and prohibited skiing positions are indicated.

Leisure infrastructure

In the water park "Niagara" (Adygea) everything is done for the greatest comfort of visitors. You can sunbathe in the relax zone. For those who want to calmly swim, a separate pool is equipped. There are spacious locker rooms where lockers are locked. The reviews noted that the showers are clean and tidy. At the entrance you are given a card on which you can put any amount of money. You can pay with it all over the territory of the Niagara water park, in a cafe and restaurant, buy tanning cream, a rubber ring or a bathing suit in a store. Unused funds will be returned to you at the exit.

Schedule and prices

Water Park "Niagara" (Adygea) is in the open. Therefore, it only works from the beginning of June until mid-September. Doors for the first visitors opens at 10:00. But at night, the water park turns into a dance floor with foam parties and discos. The fun ends only at 3:00. Prices vary depending on the days of the week and the duration of the visit to the entertainment center. If you take a ticket for the whole day (until 20:00), then this will cost an adult 1400 rubles and 1200 rubles. to kid. If the first or second shift, then the price will be 800 and 700 rubles. respectively. The water park practices the "golden watch" - from 17:00 to close. Entrance visitors will cost 600 and 400 rubles for evening visitors. If you want to give yourself a birthday present, bring your passport with you. Admission is free for birthday parties.
