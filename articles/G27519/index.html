Camp "Pearl of Mologi", Cherepovets: reviews, how to get there

Children's recreation camp "Pearl of Mologi" is located in the Cherepovets district of the Vologda region. Here you can spend your summer vacation. The camp is located at the mouth of two rivers - Mologi and Starorusskaya. This is a beautiful, picturesque place. Nearby are the Rybinsk Reservoir and the Darwin Nature Reserve. Children live in comfortable buildings in a round-the-clock guarded area.

Camp description

Camp "Pearl of Mologi" used to be called the "Sail". This children's health institution is dedicated to the marine theme. Its territory, located at the mouth of two rivers, is called the harbor town. The buildings for living here are called schooners, and the troops are called crews.





Camp "Pearl of Mologi" is located in a coniferous forest. The following facilities are located on its territory:

  • housing for children;
  • bath house;
  • yacht Club;
  • canteen;
  • medical building;
  • a place for a bonfire;
  • football field;
  • tennis court;
  • gym;
  • basketball court;
  • Hall for celebrations;
  • astronomical observatory;
  • library;
  • billiard room;
  • cinema hall.

The camp has its own sandy beach on the banks of the Mologa River with facilities for water activities.





Rest is designed for children and adolescents aged 7 to 15 years. During each shift (lasting 21 days), the Pearl of Mologi camp accommodates up to 170 children.

Accommodations

Children live in comfortable two-story buildings. The rooms are designed for 4-5 people. Each child has a bed, a table and a bedside table. In addition, on each floor there are amenities:

  • washbasin;
  • restroom;
  • sink for washing feet;
  • shower cabins.

Hot and cold water is supplied around the clock. In the buildings there is a common hall for holding detachments, luggage storage rooms, rooms for counselors and educators, there are fountains with clean drinking water. Maids regularly clean buildings. In front of the house there are sports and playgrounds.





Nutrition

Five meals a day are organized at the Pearl of Mologa camp. The menu is varied and satisfying. The diet includes fish, meat, dairy dishes. Children are regularly served vegetables and fruits, there is a bakery where fresh pastries are prepared.

A duty squad is setting the table, and waiters are also working in the dining room. All conditions for a varied and healthy diet are created here.

Entertainment

Here you can play sports, for this the camp has a football and volleyball court, tennis courts, a gym, a billiard room. In a yacht club, children under the supervision of adults can ride a catamaran.

For children, quizzes, contests, show programs, concerts, role-playing games are organized. And also classes are held on modeling, beadwork, knitting, dancing, and fine art.





Medical service

Medical care is carried out in a separate building. Helping children is around the clock. Qualified staff works. Here, they will provide assistance to the child in case of accidents and colds.

If necessary, children can undergo treatment in a medical building. There is a physiotherapy room where methods such as herbal medicine, climatotherapy, aromatherapy, hydromassage baths, and oxygen cocktail treatment are used.

How to get a ticket to the camp?

To get a ticket, you need the following medical certificates:

  • health resort card;
  • certificate of epidemic well-being;
  • copy of medical policy.

When sending a child to the camp, it is necessary to warn counselors and educators about existing chronic diseases and taking the necessary medicines. Vouchers can be purchased from tour operators Cherepovets and Vologda. The cost of one shift of rest in 2017 ranged from 26,000 to 28,000 rubles.





Directions

On parental day, moms and dads can come to the children. They are interested in the question of how to get to the "Pearls of Mologa".

The address of the recreation camp: Vologda Oblast, Cherepovets District, rural settlement Nikolo-Ramenskoye, the village of Veshnyaki (the camp is 1 km from the village).

From Cherepovets to the camp can be reached by bus, which goes to the village of Kharlamovskaya. He passes by the village of Veshnyaki. Travel time is about 2 hours. From Veshnyakov to the camp you need to walk about 1 km. However, the bus runs only on certain days of the week (Thursday, Saturday and Sunday), 2 times a day.

How to get to the camp "Pearl of Mologi" from Cherepovets by car? This is the most convenient way. From the city you need to go along the Kirillovsky highway, then turn onto the A114 highway, and after passing 62 km, turn left. After 10 km there will be a village Veshnyaki. The journey will take about 1.5 hours.

Children are transported from Cherepovets and Vologda to the camp and back with special comfortable buses.
