Restaurant "Old Castle": fun the old fashioned way

Restaurant "Old Castle" is always an open door in time, which hospitably transfers in an instant to the medieval world of fun feasts, excellent food and an unforgettable vacation. Once upon a time, people who lived in the castle district hid behind the walls of the fortress in troubled years when the enemy attacked. Now, when no one besieges the city, breaks through the walls, the “Old Castle” performs other - peace-loving, but also very important functions: it gives you a good mood, helps you enjoy your vacation and distracts from exhausting and hard working days, brightens up a life in which Unfortunately, places for the holiday for some reason are becoming less and less.





Podolsky "Old Castle"

Connoisseurs of antiquity and romantic times, when knights in iron armor performed feats for the gentle look of a lady of heart, can always immerse themselves in this atmosphere of the past by visiting the Old Castle restaurant (Podolsk). This is a respectable place in which the past and present miraculously intersect. Sitting at a wooden table in the cozy and luxurious atmosphere of the feudal lord’s castle, it’s easy to imagine yourself and your friends as a chivalrous company resting after heavy battles with your beloved. The interior has at its disposal: tiled mosaic floors, the likeness of which can still be seen now by visiting European castles; narrow stained-glass windows, wooden furniture, simple in shape and can even be said to be clumsy made using the simplest carpentry tools, as if delivered straight from the Middle Ages.

What can I say: inside the building you can really feel the spirit of antiquity. And from the outside it looks like a real stronghold, able to withstand more than one attack of a militant enemy. There is a highlight, especially loved by children, which the Old Castle restaurant has in common with the palace - it is a fountain-waterfall. The murmuring water descending down the stone cascades is a very remarkable and artistically designed means of relaxation.





Premises and surroundings

The restaurant "Old Castle" is located in Podolsk on Kirova street, 84. There is a banquet hall in which 70 invited people can freely celebrate the event, and a room for VIP visitors for 10 people. In summer, verandas open with gazebos perched close to the fountain. There are own parking spaces. The restaurant is ready to offer wines of Georgian and European producers of famous brands, all kinds of strong drinks.

During the festival there is the opportunity to watch broadcasts of football and hockey matches, biathlon and tennis competitions.

Menu

The menu consists of Georgian and European dishes. Caucasian dishes are in special demand. One can understand the mystery and exquisite taste of the cuisine of beautiful Georgia, praised by poets, by tasting khinkali and chashuli on kets from tender mutton, young pork, and veal. The institution is also famous for fish dishes, desserts ... The average price for services per person is 1000-1500 rubles.

The Old Castle restaurant is ready to offer services from Monday to Thursday from 12:00 to midnight, from Friday to Saturday - opening at 12:00, closing at 02:00.





Ivanovo: "Old Castle"

Equally original in the design and just as hospitable is another institution with the sign "Old Castle" (Ivanovo). This restaurant is popular among both the local population and among visitors. In Russia, Ivanovo is called the city of brides, which means that modern knights are simply obliged to come here in order to finally find their beautiful darling. A romantic evening spent in the "Old Castle" will be unforgettable.

Menu

The menu is composed of dishes of European and Russian cuisine. There are original dishes, the names of which seem to have come from the pages of medieval chronicles: roast "Richard", for example, or salad "Cardinal".

Room

Inside the "Old Castle" - three rooms. For large festivities a banquet hall for 35 people is offered, for fans of billiards there is a bar for 15 people, there is also a VIP room, this is a first-class room for 6 people.

It works seven days a week. Located on the street. Lezhnevskaya, 155. Opens its doors on weekdays at 11.00, closes at 24.00. On holidays, the opening is an hour later.

It is possible to use free Wi-Fi.





Siberian "namesake"

Restaurant "Old Castle" (Tomsk) is happy to open its gates to let in tired travelers, feed and cheer, give an opportunity to relax and get a charge of vivacity and positive emotions.

There is a hall for business meetings, a gallery hall, the function of which is to host large companies. The karaoke room with the song title “Nightingale” works for the joy of music lovers.

Menu

In the menu list, visitors will find familiar and unknown, but equally appetizing and delicious dishes of Caucasian, Russian, European cuisine.

Guests and residents of the city can find this place, the medieval surroundings of which simply amazes with its style and unusualness, at the following address: Tomsk, st. Gagarina, 11 / a.

The institution is open daily from 12.00 to 3.00.
