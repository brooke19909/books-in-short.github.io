Should I go to Milan in November?

Should I go to Milan in November? This question is of interest to many compatriots. In our article we will find the answer to it. We will also consider the main entertainment and excursions during this period.

Weather

In November, Milan comes in a real autumn with showers, fogs, although there are good days too. This month is the wettest of the year. It is known that a quarter of annual precipitation falls here in late autumn.





Note that in Milan in early November it is still quite warm, the air temperature is about +15 degrees. The end of this month is characterized by a lower temperature (no more than +10). Sometimes during this period there may even be frosts. There were years when it was possible to observe snowfalls in Milan in November. As you can see, the weather is very diverse at this time of the year. Therefore, it is worth preparing well for a trip to Milan in November. It is imperative to bring an umbrella, waterproof shoes, scarves, hats, as well as warm jackets.

Despite the charm of the weather, do not despair, because in Milan in November there is still good weather. Moreover, even on cloudy days, the sun peeks out, which immediately changes the mood for the better.

What to do in November?

This month in Milan you can visit museums, restaurants, cafes, opera and football. Of course, in this place you can go shopping. The only thing that can overshadow the rest is the dull weather.

Tours and activities in the Italian city

An ideal month for attending indoor events is November. During this period, tourists can go to the theater. You can also visit one of the exhibitions. In addition, tourists, of course, are waiting for restaurants and shops.

Quite popular museums are the Brera or Ambrosian galleries. The canvases of famous Italian masters are presented here. Also, tourists should visit the Leonardo da Vinci Museum of Science and Technology and museums of archeology.





Tourists should visit the opera La Scala. Tickets there should be purchased in advance.

The Duomo is a special place in Milan. It is here that tourists come who just got into the city.

The most vivid impressions will be the visit to Milan Cathedral. It has one interesting hidden option that not everyone knows about. There is an elevator in the cathedral itself. It delivers tourists to the roof, so they can see the city from a bird's eye view.

Those who come to Milan cannot spend their vacations without shopping. The most iconic place in the city is Quadrilatero D'oro. This is a whole block. There are shops of the most famous designer brands, as well as an atelier. True, this quarter is not a very cheap place.





Those who enjoy walking can wander through the streets of Milan. It is likely that there you will find interesting stores with attractive prices.

Festivals in Milan in mid-autumn

Throughout the year, concerts by various artists take place in Milan. Even world-famous stars come here. The churches performed concerts of organ music. Popular DJs can be seen at local discos.

Bright festivals take place in Milan. The most popular is the Milan Jazz Festival. It takes place annually, starting from the first week of the month. The festival ends at the end of November. At this event, European and Italian performers of different genres with world names are performing.

What are the prices for vacation?

Milan is attractive to our compatriots even in late autumn. Despite the fact that the November weather is terrible for Italians, for Russians it seems quite acceptable.

Yet the flow of tourists in Milan in November is clearly declining. This contributes to the fact that prices for tours, hotels and flights will be lower.

Milan in November. Guest reviews





Those who visited this place in November talk about a wonderful vacation. Many were very lucky with the weather. Most tourists have a good time here in the fall, sightseeing.

Little conclusion

As you may have noticed, in November the weather in Milan is usually not very good, but there are still warm days. The bulk of tourists choose this city for the November holidays in order to bring something interesting in a monotonous autumn. After reading the article, you can now decide for yourself whether you should go to Milan in November or whether you would be better off from such an idea.
