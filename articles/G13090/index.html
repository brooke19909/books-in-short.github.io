Agoy Village: recreation, climate, accommodation options and reviews of tourists

Agoy is a small village on the Black Sea coast, which is part of the Tuapse region. This settlement received its name in honor of the river on which it is located. The village is famous for its cozy atmosphere, favorable microclimate and comfortable conditions for accommodating vacationers. If you want to spend a vacation in a picturesque place and enjoy the lack of fuss - go to Agoy. Rest in this village will live up to all expectations and will be remembered for a long time.





Agoy: general information

The name of this settlement, according to experts, means: "located in the heart of the mountains" (approximate translation from Adyghe). Indeed, the settlement is located in the valley of the Agoysky pass, in the immediate vicinity of Tuapse. The village was built at the mouth of the river of the same name. According to historical documents, this settlement has been leading its modern history since 1915. Until recently, the village was considered "wild" and was popular only among tourists who choose extreme travel away from civilization. Today Agoy offers a quite comfortable vacation, there are shops, cafes and quite modern hotels, you can easily rent a room with local residents. A lot of tourists come here in the holiday season, but despite this fact, the situation in the village is not at all the same as in the large resort cities.





Climate and nature

Holidays in Agoy will not only allow you to sunbathe and swim enough in the sea, but also significantly improve your well-being. The microclimate of this area is considered beneficial and healing. Do not forget that the village is located in the mountains. In its vicinity you can see a variety of landscapes - these are steep cliffs, green meadows and forests, beaches. The air here is saturated with the smells of coniferous trees and fragrant herbs, according to many vacationers, they breathe easier, and from habit they even feel dizzy. Agoy is located in the subtropical climate zone. A completely modern resort village is surrounded by virgin forests and meadows. Here you can see more than 1000 species of wild plants, many of which are listed in the Red Book, including 30 varieties of various orchids. In the forests live exotic animals for many residents of our country: raccoons, foxes, Caucasian cats, wolves, roe deer. However, do not worry - wild animals rarely visit the village itself.

What to do during the holidays?

From the green valley, the village gradually descends to the Black Sea coast. The beaches near Agoy are different - there are rocky, there are covered with sand and small pebbles. The sea bottom is shallow and flat, respectively, here you can safely swim with young children and teach them how to swim. From afar, the water seems bright azure, and nearby it is completely clear. Storms and high waves in this part of the coast are a rarity. On comfortable beaches, tourists will be offered traditional entertainment - from parachuting and fishing on the high seas to quad biking. The village of Agoy offers a fun holiday - there are enough cafes and bars where you can have a nice dinner with barbecue during the day and dance to popular hits in the evenings. Excursions to local natural attractions are also offered, the most interesting of them: Kiseleva rock and waterfalls (there are 33 of them in the area). Private accommodation

Many tourists, going on vacation to the Krasnodar Territory, prefer to look for accommodation on the spot, focusing on the signs "room for rent". You can use this method of finding housing in the event that it will be Agoy. Rest with accommodation in the private sector has its advantages. Many owners offer rental housing with meals for a nominal cost, while others just treat their guests with fruits and vegetables from the garden, homemade wines and preserves. Agree, this is a really good bonus that can make your stay more enjoyable. Agoy, the private sector, is mainly old-built houses with 1-2 floors. But there are also modern cozy cottages. If you wish, you can book accommodation in the village via the Internet before the trip. In this case, ask to describe in detail all the conditions, check the availability / quality of the bathroom and the exact cost per day.





Accommodation in hotels and guest houses

In the entire Krasnodar Territory, tourism is one of the main sources of income for the local population. Therefore, hotels and pensions are being built today in all settlements located on the coast. No exception and Agoy. Here today more than 20 hotels, mini-hotels and recreation centers are ready to receive tourists. Each tourist will find a suitable option according to their preferences and vacation budget. The village of tourists will be pleased with reasonable prices: from 350 rubles per day per person. If you have a list of specific accommodation requirements, book your accommodation in advance. Carefully read the information about the location of the hotel, the size and furnishings of the room, as well as the conditions of comfort. You can look for housing in this segment even after arriving in Agoy. Recreation facilities here are rarely crowded, and you can find 1-2 rooms even in the midst of the season.





Reviews of tourists about holidays in Agoy

What do travelers who have already managed to visit this village say? It is worth noting that lovers of noisy parties and a variety of bright events are really bored here after large resort towns. But fans of a quiet contemplative holiday or those who go on vacation with young children will surely like Aga. Rest here is relaxing and allows you to gain strength for many months to come.
