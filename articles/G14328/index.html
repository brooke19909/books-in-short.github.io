Water parks in Novosibirsk - an addictive leisure

Water parks first appeared in the 1940s. Today, America is the leader in terms of the number of water complexes, and Japan has the largest water park in the world - the Ocean Dome, which simultaneously serves 10,000 people.

Of course,   water parks in Novosibirsk are many times smaller. However, the impressions they give to their visitors are priceless.



Water parks in Novosibirsk are not only tons of water and all kinds of attractions, they are still unbridled fun, adrenaline and a supply of good mood for the future.

Water parks firmly hold a leading position in the world of entertainment and have long become a part of the active life of citizens. Where, if not in places like water parks, in Novosibirsk you can plunge into the abyss of tropical waters in the middle of the high cold season with bitter frosts and appreciate a comfortable contrast; ride along the winding hills, flopping on a grand scale into a captivating whirlpool, and, emerging, again search for the unconquered peaks. It is in the water kingdom that a pleasant amusing activity is combined with a healthy event.



One of the most popular water parks in the city is Leader Land. The water park is located on Republican Street, 12/1, building 2. The children's water park is equipped with slides, fountains, and also provides swimming training services. The water park has a pool with geysers, Russian baths, a Finnish steam room, as well as a cafe and a sandy beach. "Leader Land" is in the open air, so in winter its entire territory is flooded with water and turns into a skating rink.

The next water park is in the possession of the Borvikha Hotel. The water park at the Borvikha Hotel is a great place to relax with friends or family. The water park has geysers, waterfalls, a jacuzzi and counter currents. After relaxing in this water park, you will get a lot of positive emotions and a portion of unforgettable memories.

People who have recently moved to a Siberian city often wonder about the place where the water park in Novosibirsk is located. In addition to the complexes already mentioned, the city has a Miami Lux water park, where in addition to slides and pools, a sauna and massage are also available for guests. The created conditions are perfect for both family vacations and friendly company.



Not so long ago it became known that in the city it is planned to build a water park with a health complex, which will be the largest in Russia. The new water park in Novosibirsk, whose address will be in the Ob River, near the Dimitrovsky Bridge, should become an ornament on the left bank of the river. It is assumed that there will be a 25-meter pool with various depths, a large pool with an artificial wave, a children's pool with attractions, slides, fountains, as well as other water infrastructure. On the territory it is planned to place various cafes, restaurants, tennis courts, gyms and much more.

A visit to the water park is an active and exciting vacation, to which Siberians have already joined. And the code became known that another entertainment complex will soon appear, which will complement the list of objects called "Water Parks in Novosibirsk", the pride of the indigenous people for their city has increased even more!
