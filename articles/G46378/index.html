What is a botanical garden

The park is broken for the sake of a pleasant atmosphere, a kind of green oasis of nature among the gray concrete jungle of urban society. And, although at first glance it might seem that the purpose of the botanical garden is the same, its role is much more thoughtful and serious.

The Botanical Garden of the Ural Branch of the Russian Academy of Sciences Yekaterinburg (the Ural Branch of the Russian Academy of Sciences of the city ‚Äã‚Äãof Yekaterinburg), like other similar objects, was founded primarily for research purposes. Its aesthetic appeal is in no way disputed, but it is only secondary in priority.



What to do in the botanical garden

Botanical Garden (Yekaterinburg) was founded in 1936. His primary task was to study in the field of possible acclimatization of various flora representatives in the given climatic zone. In addition, a lot of attention in the work of this department is paid to the study of local plant species.

Thanks to such studies, programs are being developed to preserve and increase the diversity of plants, which can take root at fairly low average annual temperatures. The interdependence of various organisms and the possibilities of enrichment and restoration of endangered species are also being studied.

Current state of affairs

Currently, the Botanical Garden (Yekaterinburg) has six modern laboratories that are equipped with the appropriate equipment. A collection of different species and varieties of plants reaches 4 thousand. Some of them grow in greenhouses, where they maintain the necessary microclimate.



What to see

But if you are not a professor in biology, the Botanical Garden (Yekaterinburg) is more interested in you, as a beautiful living museum. And this interest is not only deserved, but also highly praised.

The first greenhouse features a rich collection of flowering plants. Among them, it is especially worth highlighting a diverse palette of orchids. These exotic plants do not cease to amaze fans with their unusual shapes and a wide range of colors.
