What are the most popular countries for tourism?

If you believe the data of the World Tourism Organization, more than a billion people annually visit foreign countries. Where do most modern tourists and travelers go, what are the most popular countries for tourism? We will not take into account those people who come to other countries for only one or several days, we are interested in those who spent at least a week there.

France

The most popular countries for tourism in Europe is headed by France. Paris, in particular, is the city most visited by foreign tourists; it is often called the most romantic place on earth. It is here, on the top of the Eiffel Tower, that a huge number of offers of hand and heart have been made, it is in Paris that the newlyweds come. In a word, everything here is shrouded in an atmosphere of love and romance.





Of course, not all tourists who come to France are lovers, there really is something to see, something to be surprised. A huge cultural heritage, amazing national cuisine, fine wine, chic nature - this is what attracts tourists from different parts of the world. If you want to spend a few days at sea, feel free to go to the Cote d'Azur - the most fashionable resorts and luxury hotels are located here.

Spain

This country will satisfy the taste preferences of both lovers of beach holidays and lovers of attractions that are part of the world cultural heritage. A comfortable climate, a host of cultural and historical sights, excellent Mediterranean cuisine and the goodwill of the locals are what many tourists celebrate.





Be sure to visit Barcelona - one of the most beautiful cities not only in Europe, but also in the world.

China

USA

Surely every second inhabitant of the world would like to visit the United States. If you are looking for the most popular countries for tourism, you will surely find America in each ranking. For over a century, the country of great opportunities has been attracting not only emigrants, but also tourists. Fans of entertainment, megacities beckon New York, Las Vegas, Chicago. In turn, lovers of local beauty will find here for themselves a lot of interesting things. How do you like the Grand Canyon or, say, Yellowstone National Park?





The standard of living in America is impressive, and some tourists after their trip really begin to think about moving. That is why the United States tightened visa requirements, especially for Russians.

Great Britain

It is difficult to call England a classic holiday destination, however, the most popular countries for tourism include it in their list. The country certainly does not lack tourists.





Of course, most tourists go to London, offering attractions for every taste: from art to traditional football. But there are those travelers who visit the remote corners of the country where you can admire stunning landscapes.

Turkey

The most popular tourism countries, or rather the list of already listed, must be supplemented by Turkey. This is a favorite resort for a beach holiday among Russians and not only. You are guaranteed wonderful weather, warm and gentle sea, gorgeous beaches with a wide coastline.





If you want to get to know Turkey better, feel free to go to Istanbul - a city with a centuries-old history and a huge cultural heritage connecting Europe and Asia.
