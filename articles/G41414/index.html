Ski resorts of Andorra, their specifics, recommendations of specialists

The Principality of Andorra has two main sources of replenishment of the state treasury: 1) duty-free trade and 2) ski tourism. And if the first thrives year-round, then the second - only from December to May. But the ski resorts of Andorra at that time bring huge income. Indeed, almost 80% of the territory of the dwarf state is lifts, slopes, hotels. All this ski structure is not only not inferior, but to a certain extent even surpasses the famous alpine resorts.



There are only five valleys in the principality, where skiers, snowboarders, and luge enthusiasts ride. Three of them are connected by a single system of tow ropes, saddles and cable lifts. The ski resorts of Andorra are two hundred and eighty kilometers of pistes of varying degrees of difficulty.





It is also one hundred and fifty lifts combined into one system. Their total capacity is 140,000 people per hour. Naturally, all routes are protected by fences, protection from avalanches, marked, marked with distances and pointers. Each station has ski schools for both adults and children, both for beginners and for those who wish to improve their level. Each resort has its own kindergartens, where you can enroll your baby so that with a clear conscience go to conquer the slopes. Restaurants, equipment and ammunition rental points, sports shops will let you not lack anything.
