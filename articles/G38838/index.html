White dragonfly



Such an image is suitable for an adult girl, for example, for a schoolgirl. How to make a dragonfly costume for a girl with your own hands? The basis of the image will be a long white dress. On it you can sew a translucent top layer, for example, a hand-painted chiffon. Transparent fabric will add sophistication to the look, and the pattern will make even an ordinary dress unique. White tights and long leggings will help to complement the image of a dragonfly. It remains to make a hat and wings. To make a headgear, you will need two plastic cans.
