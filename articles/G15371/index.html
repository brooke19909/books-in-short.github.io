Guest House "Nadezhda", Kabardinka: services and reviews of tourists

Not far from Gelendzhik, in a valley protected from all sides by mountains, is the village of Kabardinka. Here in the holiday season, thousands of tourists come to the sea.

Resort Kabardinka

Kabardinka is a large village, its population is more than seven thousand people. At this place, the climatic conditions are unique and are ideal for a comfortable stay. Nature itself is conducive to the successful functioning of a large number of resorts offering treatment for many diseases. In Kabardinka there are children's health camps to which children and adolescents from all over Russia come to rest and improve. Modern Kabardinka is a resort place in which infrastructure is actively developing, there is an active construction of private cafes, clubs, hotels and guest houses.

Guest House "Nadezhda" (Kabardinka)

Especially popular among the guests of the resort are guest house services. From year to year, there are more offers to settle in them in Kabardinka, there is fierce competition among such institutions, so the living conditions in them are constantly improving. In 2014, the guest house "Nadezhda" was commissioned. Kabardinka, st.Kashtanovaya, 16 - its location. Vacationers can get here from airports and railway stations of large southern cities - Gelendzhik, Anapa, Krasnodar and Novorossiysk - by public transport.

Guest House "Nadezhda" (Kabardinka) offers the organization of a meeting of tourists and a paid transfer, a preliminary reservation of which will provide a quick and comfortable trip to the place. For guests with private cars, free parking is provided on site. Nearby are the Kabardinka embankment and the pebble city beach, grocery stores, cafes and restaurants.



Accommodation and guest services

Guest House Nadezhda (Kabardinka) is a three-story building with balconies. The guests are offered standard-class rooms. There are 18. here. Accommodation from two to five guests is possible.

Each room has its own bathroom with shower. Plumbing equipment in it is new, an uninterrupted supply of hot water is provided. The furniture in the guest rooms is functional and provides comfort. The beds in the rooms can be single or double, there are wardrobes, tables and chairs. Beautiful curtains and bedspreads create comfort.



For additional comfort of guests:

  • refrigerator;
  • air conditioning;
  • twenty-channel digital television;
  • free wifi access.

Housekeeping and linen change regularly - every five days. Guests can take their laundry to the laundry for an extra fee or use the washing machine on their own. There is an ironing room on the floor.



Guest House Nadezhda (Kabardinka) offers guests to use the food in a local cafe. But there is also a common kitchen, equipped with all necessary appliances and utensils. Here guests can cook for themselves. The owners of the guest house offer homemade fresh bread. On the territory there is a recreation area with barbecue and barbecue.

The owners of the guest house organize excursions. Guests can bring pets upon prior arrangement.

Guest reviews about accommodation

Pleasant living conditions, care and comfort are provided by the guest house "Nadezhda" (Kabardinka). Reviews are only positive. Guests are surrounded by constant care, any requests and wishes are fulfilled quickly and efficiently. Tourists are ready to return here in the next holiday season and have a good rest, often they recommend this place to their friends and acquaintances.
