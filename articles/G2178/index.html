LCD "Krylov", Yekaterinburg: reviews, address, photo

Yekaterinburg is a city with one and a half million citizens, dynamically developing both as an industrial and cultural center of the Trans-Urals. It is not surprising that housing construction is proceeding very rapidly here. So, at the end of the year 2016, two large projects were immediately commissioned in the city, one of which was the Krylov residential complex, or, as it was called in the old fashion, Krylov. This complex surrendered in two stages, and each became an exclusive. Its slender tall buildings, made in white and blue tones, and in the evening lit by dozens of backlighting lights, still attract attention from afar. Residents of the complex can boast not only its design and decoration, but also their own swimming pool, beauty salon, children's club.

Location

In the most elite place of the city built LCD "Krylov". Yekaterinburg is steadily developing and expanding, rising to an international level. In the general plan of its improvement there is a clause on the demolition of old buildings that are of neither architectural nor historical value, and in their place the construction of modern residential buildings, parks, shopping centers, children's institutions. The Krylov housing complex, which began to be built opposite the Central Stadium, on the site between Tatishchev, Klyuchevskaya, Pirogov and Melnikov, also fits perfectly with this item. To get a place for the construction site, the developer had to demolish several old houses. Now this area has become more relevant to the modern level and more presentable. Located LCD "Krylov" in the city center. Here, within a radius of one kilometer, there are kindergartens, schools, a stadium, supermarkets and shops, restaurants, cafes, clinics, bank branches, a university, a medical college, and a research institute.



There are also no problems with transport. The stops near the new complex have not yet been approved, the metro is also far away (the nearest station, Ploshchad 1905 goda, 1700 meters), but within a radius of 400 meters there are bus stops, minibuses, trolleybuses. In total, about 20 routes run here, so you can easily get to anywhere in Yekaterinburg.

Developer

LCD "Krylov" is a project of the largest in the Urals, the company "Sinara Development". According to the structural organization, it is a divisional holding company, which is part of the Sinara group. The areas of activity of the holding are full management of construction projects in the region, from the stage of their design to the implementation stage. On the first two floors of the complex, according to the plan, they had to build a children's club, a beauty salon, several shops and offices. Nearby planned to build a fitness center. Residential complex "Krylov-2"

Next to the first back in 2015, they began to build the second house of the Krylov residential complex . It was supposed to consist of five sections, also of different heights, but in this case at 15, 18 and 30 floors. The style of the buildings is ultramodern; concrete and glass prevail in the exterior design, which successfully distinguishes the Krylov housing complex. The photo captured facades made exclusively of glass panels with a mirror effect. They give the complex a somewhat formal and at the same time compositionally finished look. In general, the buildings of the second stage continue and complement the architectural ensemble of the first stage, but here the designers made some distinctive details. So, a bright illumination has been arranged along the contours of the structures, which very effectively decorates the buildings at night. The second highlight is the outdoor panoramic elevators, using which residents can enjoy the surroundings. They will be placed on each section. The project provides for special panels so that the movement of elevators does not interfere with the owners of apartments in the immediate vicinity of the walls of which they are mounted. In addition, internal elevators are left.
