Leonardo Privilege Eilat 4 * (Ex. Golden Tulip Privilege): reviews, photos

Leonardo Privilege Eilat 4 * is a comfortable hotel on the shores of the warm Red Sea. Thanks to the popular and beloved all-inclusive system, you can leave your worries and enjoy an unforgettable vacation.

Hotel Leonardo Privilege Eilat 4 *: characteristic

A full rest must be not only bright, but also carefree. These are the conditions that the Leonardo Privilege Eilat 4 * Resort Hotel promises its guests. In this hotel people of absolutely any age and hobbies can have a great time. There is also a large clean pool, around which there are many comfortable sun loungers, a sports hall, and a steam sauna, as well as a huge playground where young travelers can have fun. It is worth noting that mainly this hotel is preferred for family holidays, because the situation here is quite calm and friendly.





At the Leonardo Privilege Eilat 4 * hotel, great attention is paid to guest rooms, because it is with them that you can make up one or another impression of the institution as a whole. The design of the apartments is modern, concise, restrained, but at the same time very original and bright. The rooms provide a host of amenities without which a modern traveler simply cannot imagine his life. It is important that, without exception, all apartments have access to the balcony, and therefore you will always have the opportunity to breathe fresh air while admiring the surrounding views.

Due to the fact that the 4 * Leonardo Privilege Eilat resort hotel (ex. Golden Tulip Privilege) has a favorite all-inclusive system, you will be absolutely free from worries about delicious and nutritious food. Arriving three times a day in the main restaurant of the hotel, you will meet a beautifully served buffet with a huge variety of dishes. During the day, you can grab a bite to eat and drink your favorite drinks at the bars, one of which is located right by the pool. In the evening, fresh fruits and dairy products will be available to you.

Resort Location

4 * Leonardo Privilege Eilat Resort (ex. Golden Tulip Privilege) is located in the north of Eilat next to a large clean beach. In this area, a huge number of entertainment venues, shops, restaurants and walking spots are concentrated. The local airport is just a 5-minute drive from this hotel. But if you need a large international airport terminal, then you have to overcome as many as 354 kilometers. To get to the capital of Israel, you will need to drive almost 400 km.

Hotel Guest Apartments

In resort Leonardo Privilege Eilat 4 * (Israel, Eilat) you will find 247 modern comfortable rooms, which are divided into the following categories:

  • Standard rooms include both double and triple occupancy. They are one-room, but quite spacious and bright. In addition to beds, it also has a desk and soft chairs. On the balcony there is a towel dryer, as well as plastic chairs where you can enjoy the blue pool.
  • Junior Suite is a spacious one-room apartment with a large double bed and a huge soft sofa. Since it is laid out, it can serve as an additional berth. Quite often, this option of accommodation is chosen by small families with children.




  • Family Suite - a huge two-room suite, which is designed to accommodate large families or companies. Due to the fact that in each bedroom, in addition to beds, there are also folding sofas, up to 6 people can comfortably live here. The main feature of the apartments can be considered that each of the rooms is equipped with a separate bathroom. But the balcony is provided only in one of the bedrooms.
  • 8 rooms from the general fund are provided for guests with disabilities. Apartments have wider doorways, as well as the presence of handrails in all rooms.
  • If you do not want to overpay for a suite, but relax in a large company, you can book a couple of standard apartments that will be interconnected by interior doors.

The decoration of the rooms of the Leonardo Privilege Eilat 4 * hotel deserves special attention. Photos show us an original bright design.

Facilities at the hotel apartments

Hotel Leonardo Privilege Eilat 4 * offers its guests to use the following amenities that are provided in each room:

  • The bathroom is adjacent, equipped with a large bathtub, as well as an electric hairdryer.
  • The apartments have individual air conditioning, which you can configure for both cooling and heating to create a comfortable microclimate.
  • In the bathroom, in addition to towels, there are also bathrobes and slippers, as well as a set of cosmetics for the shower.
  • A fixed-line telephone gives you the opportunity to contact the administration at any time of the day, as well as make international calls (you can also use a service such as an "alarm clock", which means a call from the reception at the time you need).




  • So you can always make hot tea or coffee, the room has an electric kettle and kitchenware.
  • In the minibar you will always find refreshing drinks or alcohol, as well as a small selection of snacks as an appetizer.
  • To be calm about the safety of valuables, leaving the room, be sure to put them in a safe.
  • All apartments have a small table that you can use as a work or cosmetic.
  • A large flat-screen TV broadcasts a solid set of cable channels.

Resort Infrastructure

The tourist infrastructure of the resort Leonardo Privilege Eilat 4 * (Eilat) can be described as follows:

  • Those who can not forget about work even on vacation will certainly be pleased with the presence of a business center with computer equipment, as well as several conference rooms for seminars and business negotiations.
  • The lobby has a spacious living room where visitors can meet and chat with each other, sitting at a large table.
  • If you have a festive event planned, then you do not have to worry about finding a suitable restaurant, because the hotel can provide you with a spacious banquet room for rent.




  • In the large flowered garden you can sit on a bench or in the gazebo, sheltered from the sweltering heat.
  • In bars, one of which is located near the pool, you can try unusual cocktails, as well as a light salad.
  • A buffet is served three times a day in the restaurant, and the rest of the time you will be able to order from the menu.
  • The entire territory and premises of the hotel are covered by a signal of free wireless Internet.
  • In the immediate vicinity of the hotel is a large free public parking area.
  • The reception is open around the clock with complaints and complaints from hotel guests (currency can be exchanged here).
  • With the help of an ATM located in the lobby, you can withdraw cash from your plastic cards.
  • You can always leave your luggage in the left-luggage office.
  • If your clothes are dirty or wrinkled, then this problem will help you solve the laundry.
  • The elevator will help you to comfortably and effortlessly reach the upper floors.
  • The hotel has a small chapel.
  • For souvenirs, buy souvenirs in a small shop.

Sports and Entertainment

In the resort Leonardo Privilege Eilat 4 * (Israel) you can always have fun and bright time thanks to the following features:

  • The epicenter of the resort life of this hotel can be considered its huge and clean swimming pool, next to which there are many sun loungers, as well as umbrellas to protect from the sun.
  • Sauna is one of the most popular services, because it has a beneficial effect on the health of the body as a whole and on the balance of the nervous system.
  • Modern equipment located in the gym will help you keep fit in shape, and thanks to large mirrors you can continuously monitor the results of your workouts.




  • The SPA center will help you maintain beauty and youth, a massage parlor will be an excellent option for those who have health problems of the musculoskeletal system.
  • For children, a playroom is provided, as well as a huge playground with attractions located in the open air.

Useful information for tourists

If you have chosen Leonardo Privilege Eilat 4 * Resort (Eilat, Israel) for your vacation , then first study some information regarding accommodation in this institution:

  • Upon arrival at the hotel, check-in is possible no earlier than at three in the afternoon (the exception is when there are available rooms - then a certain fee is charged for this service).
  • Check out time is 11 a.m. Until this time, you must have time to leave your room and hand over the keys to the administrator (for an additional fee, it is possible to extend the time you spend in the apartment).
  • Cots for babies are available for $ 12 per day (if the child sleeps in existing beds, then the cost of his stay remains unchanged).
  • Extra beds or cots are not available, as instead of them there is upholstered furniture.
  • It is strictly forbidden to come with pets, otherwise your reservation may be canceled.
  • You can not bring a large amount of cash with you, because the hotel has a cashless payment system.
  • If you arrive at the hotel on Saturday or the day of a religious holiday, check-in will be done only after sunset.
  • Access to the spa is subject to a surcharge.

Leonardo Privilege Eilat 4 * (Eilat): reviews of satisfied tourists

The description of hotels in brochures or on specialized sites does not always contain objective information, and therefore it is better to get acquainted with the opinions of tourists. As for the resort Leonardo Privilege Eilat 4 *, reviews about it are quite positive and are accompanied by the following comments:

  • The staff is very attentive to the guests, and especially to those who come with small children (without any additional requests, immediately after checking in they bring baths, playpens and other items necessary for the kids).
  • Despite the fact that all-inclusive meals are provided in the restaurant, you can well order lunch in your room.
  • The food in the dining room is very tasty and varied (especially pleasing is the large selection of desserts that are served in the evening).




  • For dinner, they offer delicious local wine.
  • Not a single complaint or request to the hotel staff remains without an answer and attention.
  • In order to get acquainted with local culinary traditions, it is not at all necessary to go to expensive restaurants, as national dishes are served at the buffet.
  • If you or your child is not suitable for food that is served according to the All Inclusive system, then you can agree with the cook about a special menu.
  • Very nice public beach with clean and soft sand, as well as incredibly comfortable entry into the sea.

Negative hotel reviews

It is important to know not only positive, but also negative moments of relaxation in Leonardo Privilege Eilat 4 *. Reviews about this institution contain a number of negative comments, the most significant of which can be considered the following:

  • The interior of the hotel is already quite "tired" and shabby, and therefore the repair will not interfere.
  • The floor in the room is covered with carpet, which is completely covered with spots. Despite the fact that they clean the apartment every day, it does not get any cleaner (the only positive point is the daily change of bed linen).
  • Frozen convenience foods predominate on the buffet menu.
  • The same dishes are served every day for breakfast, and since their variety is not very large, they become boring in a couple of days.
  • The furniture in the rooms is very old and scratched.
  • The actual design of the hotel is much more modest than in the photo on the Internet.
  • Even if you arrived at the hotel with huge, heavy suitcases, you should not expect someone to help you get them to the room.
  • The restaurant is very cramped, and therefore there is little fresh air and huge lines at the distribution.




  • In all the variety of dishes served in the restaurant, there are no dietary ones, which creates great difficulties in providing food for children.
  • To get to the sea, you have to cross a busy highway.
  • Despite the fact that the hotel is focused on a quiet family vacation, it is always very crowded and noisy (this is due to the specifics of local tourists).
  • All hotel rooms, including rooms, are thoroughly saturated with the smell of tobacco smoke.
  • At night, the noise of the ventilation system makes it very difficult to sleep.

General impression of the hotel

Mixed feelings evoke relaxation in the resort Leonardo Privilege Eilat 4 * (Eilat). Reviews about this place mark exceptional courtesy and courtesy of the staff in relation to the guests. The restaurant also serves a delicious buffet three times a day. Its menu is significantly different from the standard, because here the emphasis is on the local cuisine. In addition, there is a huge amount of living space and light in the rooms, as well as a private balcony. Perhaps this is all the positive points.

The specificity of this hotel is that, according to the preferential system, a huge number of local people are settled here, which is characterized by very strange standards of behavior. They smoke in restaurants, rooms, lobbies, scatter trash, shout - and no one makes any comments about this. Therefore, if you are planning a vacation in this institution, check with the administration in advance whether such rides are planned for the period of your intended stay. It is also worth noting that the hotel just needs repairs, because all the furniture is old, creaking and very worn.
