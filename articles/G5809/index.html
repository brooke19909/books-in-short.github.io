The water park in Ulyanovsk has opened and is waiting for guests!

The ancient Russian city of Ulyanovsk is located on the banks of the great Volga River, currently about 500 thousand people live and work here. The city has many entertainment venues, including the Ulet water park.

The water park in Ulyanovsk was opened on January 1, 2016. It was a kind of gift to all local residents and guests of Ulyanovsk from the city administration. In the first days after the opening of this entertainment center, thousands of people visited it. At the moment, the water park is one of the most favorite places of entertainment for citizens and tourists. Moreover, it is worth noting that the prices in the Ulyanovsk water park are not too high, so this type of vacation is affordable for most residents. That is why there are always a lot of people here, including couples with kids.

Where is located

At the moment, not all visiting visitors to Ulyanovsk yet know where the water park is located in the city. This is understandable, because the water park in Ulyanovsk was opened not so long ago. Therefore, even some locals have not yet been in this center of health and entertainment. To find the water park, which received the specific name "Fly away", can be quite simple. After all, it is located in one of the most beautiful places in the city, namely in the Alexander Park. The park is known to literally every resident of Ulyanovsk, as it itself is a natural landmark of the city. The exact address of the water park: Alexandrovskaya street, building 60 V. The institution is open all day from 9 am to 9 pm.

How to get to the water park?

Getting to the park is also quite easy, as it is located in the city center. If you do not have a car, you can use public transport, namely minibus No. 55. Get to the stop located on Mozhaiskaya Street, after which literally 300 meters will remain to the entertainment center. Few people know that the water park of Ulyanovsk, the opening date, which was known to the citizens long before this event, is visited by hundreds of people every day.

The water park in Ulyanovsk, which opened in the winter of 2016, was originally planned as an institution with two levels. Inside, the designers designed the room in the form of a pirate ship. Here are the tentacles of a giant kraken, which encircle the columns and penetrate the giant stones. The staff working in the park constantly monitors the internal temperature of air and water.
