Pasta Easter Egg: a master class

The lightest and most beloved religious holiday is Easter. With enthusiastic trepidation, the housewives start a dough for fragrant butter cakes. Children are interested in painting eggs painted in rainbow colors. An atmosphere of reverence and pacification fills every house in which they expect a bright resurrection.

To create the appropriate mood, not only the basic Easter attributes help, but also do-it-yourself crafts. Original and unusual can look like the most ordinary objects in every house, in the hands of a person with a rich imagination. After reading this article, you will learn how to make a very interesting craft - an Easter egg from pasta.





DIY materials

To make a stunning element of decor at home, you will need such materials:

  • Pasta. For a more effective result, it is better to use pasta of various shapes: spirals, spaghetti, wheels with membranes, scallops, shells, and straws are suitable.

  • PVA glue. To create a base, it is better to use ordinary PVA glue, and to decorate the product, you can use a glue gun or more durable glue.

  • Balloon. Using a ball, you can make an Easter egg from pasta of any size, depending on personal preference.

  • Spray paint. Paint in the spray can be needed to achieve a better result. Most of all, for such an craft, gold or silver is suitable.

  • Glass deep plate. An enameled or plastic bowl can be used. It is needed for mixing working ingredients.

  • Cardboard. From thick paper, a stand for a finished egg is made.





Having prepared the necessary set of materials, you should figure out how the craft will look. An Easter egg from pasta should be the embodiment of one’s own imagination, all households can be involved in its manufacture so that everyone puts a particle of his soul into the decor element.

Foundation process

So, let's begin to describe how to create an Easter egg from pasta. A master class on its manufacture will help you understand all the details. To start working in prepared dishes, mix PVA glue with pasta, which will serve as the basis of the egg. Balloon, inflated to the desired size, gradually pasted over with pasta, leaving a small hole through which you can subsequently get the bursted balloon.





For convenience, the work should be performed in several stages, so that the elements have time to stick to the rubber base. The future Easter egg from pasta is completely pasted over with figured products in one layer. For this process, it is better to use wheels with membranes inside. This will give the finished product an openwork and delicate look.

When it is completely dry, the ball can be pierced with a needle, and then pulled through the hole left. The base from which the Easter egg is made from pasta is ready. You can proceed to the next stage of work.

Decorative elements





The long-awaited moment comes - you can proceed to the decorative design of the craft. Carefully, in order not to damage the fragile base, various flour products should be gradually applied to the pasta egg from pasta . You can make flowers from shells by connecting sharp edges at one point. For gluing decorative elements it is convenient to use a glue gun.

At the top of a pasta egg in the form of spirals, you can make a cross. You should also beautifully decorate the hole through which the ball reached. Pasta glued close to each other in the form of bows or stars will look perfect on the finished product. The hole itself can be beautifully decorated with spaghetti, intertwining them with each other in the form of a wicker. Further details that will decorate an Easter egg made from pasta and a ball may reflect personal preferences.

Decorative egg stand

To make a stand for a decorative egg, a sheet of thick cardboard should be folded in the form of a bag with a wide hole from the bottom. The outer diameter of the paper ring should be identical to the diameter of the product. From the second sheet of cardboard it is necessary to make another product in exact accordance with the dimensions of the first. The edges can be fastened with glue.

Two paper blanks need to be interconnected so that it looks like an hourglass. The sides of the "bags" with a smaller diameter should be adjacent to each other, and the wider sockets form the leg of the stand and the bed under the Easter egg from pasta. The master class on decorating the stand involves following certain rules:

  • The general theme of the decor of the stand should correspond to the theme of the finished product. Decor elements should be the same types of pasta that decorate the egg.

  • The edges of the stand should be decorated using the same technique as the hole. Then the finished decorative element will be harmoniously included in the overall ensemble.

  • It is better to stick pasta with a glue gun. If it is not, you can use glue such as "moment".

Staining step

Finished and dried parts should be painted with special paint. Masters performing such interior details recommend the use of aerosol. A brush can leave unwanted smudges and stains. When staining, care should be taken to protect the respiratory system. You can wear a gauze bandage or respirator.





The product must be placed on a surface previously covered with a newspaper. Spray paint evenly at a distance of 20-30 cm from parts. The egg and stand should be painted over twice to prevent skipping. When finished, allow the parts to dry as expected. It is advisable to glue an Easter pasta egg to the stand. This will prevent accidental fall and give the structure extra strength.

Additional decorative elements





If desired, additional decorations can be put in the hollow space of the egg. You can make them from the same pasta or thick paper. What the internal element will be depends on the preferences of the wizard. A small chapel made of pasta and painted with a different color paint looks spectacular. If the egg itself is made in gold, the silver interior decor will harmoniously look.

Afterword

A self-made egg will be a wonderful addition to the interior. The love, soul and imagination invested in it will allow you to admire the work done not only to relatives, but also to all coming guests on a bright Easter holiday. After reading the article, you can easily make an Easter egg from pasta. Photos that complement the text are a great visual example of what can happen.
