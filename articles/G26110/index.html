Chalet "Orbit": description, reviews

Country holidays are increasingly attracting residents of megacities. Almost every family wants to escape from the hustle and bustle with nature. Fresh air and new experiences provide an opportunity to gain strength for future workdays. Children here are free to run and play throughout the complex, in contrast to the bustling streets of the city.

Where is it located and how does it work?

The Orbita camp site is located in the Samara region near the village of Shigony. It is surrounded by a pine forest, a river flows near it. Guests at any time of the year will be able to find entertainment and actively spend their leisure time.





The recreation center is open throughout the year. The rooms have a heating system, and tourists feel comfortable there even in the most severe frost. Here around the clock there is a guard that monitors the order and does not let strangers here.

Description of the complex

The Orbita camp site occupies a large territory. It contains: 7 cottages, a hotel complex, a place for barbecue, parking, security building. All houses are thoroughly built, outwardly beautiful and neat.

The area is pretty well maintained:

  • sidewalks are laid;
  • lighting installed;
  • places for rest organized;
  • broken flower beds.




Cleaning and garbage collection takes place daily.

Accommodations

Guests can rent a room in a hotel complex or rent a detached cottage. Everywhere there are comfortable conditions for relaxing after an active day.

  • The VIP house has 3 bedrooms, a kitchen, a living room, a private pool and a sauna. The interior is made in a classic style with a predominance of calm tones. The rooms have single and double beds. The kitchen has all the necessary appliances. In the room with a pool, a relaxation area is organized.
  • Luxury cottage is designed to accommodate several families or a large company. The house has two separate entrances. Each half of the cottage has three bedrooms and a kitchen. All rooms are equipped with necessary furniture and appliances. The house has its own pool and sauna.
  • Cottage "Cozy" is designed to accommodate several families. Two entrances to the house. Each half has 2 bedrooms, a living room and a kitchen.
  • One-room rooms in the hotel complex are equipped with double beds, TV, soft chairs, shower and toilet. The design is made in a classic style.
  • Two-room rooms have a living room, a corridor and a bedroom with a large bed and air conditioning. It has its own toilet and shower.




Rooms are cleaned daily. Bed linen is changed once a week.

Entertainment

The Orbita camp site offers its guests to spend active and interesting weekends, holidays or vacations. Here, various entertainments are thought out that can be used at any time of the year.

For younger visitors, there is an outdoor playground and a games room in the hotel complex. A sports field with a modern coating for playing football, basketball and volleyball is organized on site. It is lit in the evenings.

The hotel complex has an 18-meter pool and sauna. Also in this building there is a billiard room with three large tables and all the necessary accessories.





The complex has two bowling alleys throughout the year. There is a seating area nearby. Here you can order food and drinks from the restaurant.

The Orbita camp site in Shigonsky district has a bike rental and other sports equipment rental center. In winter you can rent skis, sledges and skates.

On the territory of the recreation center there is a large gazebo for picnics with all necessary equipment. It can be rented daily.

Additional services

The Orbita camp site in Shigonsky district offers to use the restaurant menu. Here you can fully eat 3 times a day. And also at the request of guests, the cook prepares fragrant kebabs in the fresh air.

The complex has two outdoor pools with a broken relaxation area around them. One is for adults and the other is for children. The water in them is constantly cleaned, and the bottom is cleaned daily with special devices. At Orbita camp site (Shigony) there is a private beach on the river bank.

The restaurant has the opportunity to hold a banquet for any occasion. During the New Year holidays, an extensive entertainment program is held with the participation of Santa Claus and Snow Maiden.

Chalet "Orbit": reviews

Regarding the work of the complex, you can find a variety of comments. As for the arrangement of the territory, tourists do not have any comments, and almost everyone is satisfied with the living conditions in both the cottages and the hotel complex.

Several negative reviews can be found about the restaurant. Guests note that it looks more like an average dining room and does not have a specific mode of operation. The food here is estimated by vacationers at "4".

Regarding the friendliness of the staff and the service, there are practically no comments. In winter, tourists are unhappy that the water in the indoor pool is quite cold.





Many vacationers are upset that you can use the sports ground only for an additional fee at the Orbita camp site. The prices for its rent are small (400-500 rubles per hour) for the company, but for one family they are quite tangible.

In general, the guests are satisfied with the rest in the complex and hope that the administration will take into account the comments of customers and try to correct all the nuances so that the rest becomes even more comfortable and memorable.
