Flat and voluminous cereal crafts on the theme of "Autumn"

Usually at the beginning of the school year in kindergartens and schools conduct thematic classes and competitions dedicated to the September theme. If you need to make something unusual, pay attention to crafts from cereals on the theme of "Autumn". From materials that are definitely at home, it is easy to create real masterpieces.



What is required

To do crafts from cereals on the theme "Autumn", prepare the following:

  • Colored cardboard for the base.
  • Templates, stencils, samples (leaves, mushrooms).
  • PVA glue.
  • Different types of cereals, both processed and not (buckwheat, semolina, barley, green and ground peas, white and red beans, lentils). Also suitable are sunflower seeds, zucchini, pumpkins, watermelons.

In short, the choice of material is limited to what you have available. When a craft needs to be done urgently, with imagination, and there is no time to go to the store, you can find a suitable option from improvised materials.

The benefits of creativity

Crafts made from cereals on the theme of "Autumn" or any other, dedicated to the seasons or holidays, are useful for children. In the process, the child gets acquainted with new material for creativity, studies interesting information about the world around him. In addition, he develops fine motor skills, sorting out details, feeling the embossed surface of an already completed product, receives a massage of his palms, fingers, which is very important and useful.

Cereals on the topic of "Autumn" step by step

Consider the general sequence of work without resorting to a specific plot. You can make mushrooms, landscape, hedgehog or a bouquet of autumn leaves or flowers. Depending on the complexity of the selected composition, some work steps will require more time.





So, a panel of cereals is performed as follows:

  • Take a sheet of colored cardboard for the background. Everything will be glued on it.
  • Draw based on the contours of the image that you will lay out. You can use stencils or samples from books.
  • Work sequentially: spread glue on that part of the panel where you will apply the groats.
  • Lay out the picture if the elements are large (beans, beans), or fill in the selected area (decoy). Do so all the intended details. The panel can be done with partial or full filling.
  • Autumn theme allows you to create panels of cereals, while maintaining their natural shade.
  • If you want to make some elements brighter, paint them with acrylic paints, for example.
  • Prepare additional decor, if necessary, and paste it in the appropriate places (autumn leaves of paper, flowers, butterflies).
  • Place the panel in a frame under or without glass to preserve the surface texture.

Volumetric products

Children's cereal crafts on the theme of "Autumn" can be made in the form of three-dimensional compositions, for example, a house standing among autumn foliage, mushrooms, a basket with a crop or flowers. Such an object is created using the same technology as the panel. The difference is that here the cereals will not stick on a flat surface, but on the details of the volumetric frame.
