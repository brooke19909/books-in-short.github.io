Maya Melissa Garden 4 * (Turkey / Belek): photos, prices, description

The journey from Antalya Airport to the Maya Melissa Garden 4 * hotel takes only half an hour. They accommodate guests quickly enough. The hotel complex is positioning itself on the market as a family- type hotel . It opened in 2008, and its last reconstruction took place in 2013. Based on its distance from the sea, it is classified as a 3rd-line hotel. The distance from it to the beach is about one and a half kilometers. However, this slight inconvenience is successfully overcome due to well-built logistics. Hourly vacationers drive to the beach bus.





Territory and architecture of the hotel

The hotel area - 20 thousand m 2 - is quite sufficient for an active family vacation. There are three (!) Swimming pools for adults and two for children. Sunbeds enough for everyone at any time of the day. (Not all hotel complexes can boast of the last circumstance.) From the point of view of architecture, the building of the Maya Melissa Garden 4 * hotel itself is of interest. Despite the standard wall material - reinforced concrete, the designers have maintained the modern modern style. The original six-story building with three facades (one of which is the main one) has an individual characteristic exterior. However, designers have not only worked out the exterior, but also the functionality of the rooms. And yet, the culmination of its architecture is the main facade. The original color scheme (decorative frontal plane with emerald tint) individualizes the building and makes it recognizable.

Hotel rooms





Maya Melissa Garden 4 * hotel offers its guests 210 rooms. Among them, the most so-called standard (195 rooms). Their area is approximately 26 m 2 . The remaining 15 rooms are family, two-room. Rooms are cleaned daily by hotel staff. Towels and bed linen are changed twice a week.

Standard room with a balcony and a bathroom. It is furnished with two beds (double and single), a table, several chairs. From household appliances in the rooms there is a refrigerator, air conditioning, TV. There is a small kitchen room with the ability to make tea, coffee, warm food. It also has a small dining table with chairs. Paid in their room, spa guests use a safe and minibar. The rooms are quite compact, but not small. Moreover, the family bathroom is equipped with a jacuzzi.

A pleasant moment for guests of the Maya Melissa Garden 4 * hotel is the interior design of their rooms, which is made really professionally. The rooms are well-repaired and the floor is decorated with a stylish laminate that imitates beech. Designer furniture. This is clearly felt in how proportionately it is selected relative to the living space of the hotel rooms.

By the way, we point out a drawback: hotel guests note the unstable operation of hotel Wi-Fi. The latter circumstance is associated with a twofold overload of its capacity for servicing users (in fact, 700, instead of the optimal number - 300).

Excursions





Of course, having arrived on the land of Turkey, it would be extremely frivolous to spend the whole vacation under an umbrella by the pool, overly carried away by passive rest. Tourists who think that the opportunity to go to historical relics will be offered at an economical price are deeply mistaken. It should be noted that interesting, entertaining, exciting excursions come at a price in Turkey. Moreover, hotel managers who actively offer them from hotels (this is a common practice) openly speculate with them, earning 100% of the actual cost of the excursion on the trust of their guests.

Therefore, many tourists from the very beginning plan their budget, sharing the funds that will be paid for the tour, and the money for which tours will be bought. It is possible to pre-purchase them on the Internet. You should choose the site of a travel agency serving tourists in the area where the hotel is located, and order for yourself those who are interested.

Typically, tourists who are guests of the Star and Crescent Land, plan several excursions to historical relics and (or) natural phenomena. The rich history of Asia Minor (as Turkey was once called) and its unique nature are indeed of particular interest to travelers.

Sometimes nature and history merge here together, as happened in Pamukkale (Cotton Castle). The cascade of unique travertine snow-white mini-pools is filled with healing mineral water. It is noteworthy that this ancient object was converted and adapted as a dispensary by the ancient Romans. They chained a geyser in stone and arranged in the largest pool, named after Cleopatra in terms. Unfortunately, the earthquake was ruthless to marble columns and porticoes. However, modern tourists can still take warm mineral baths here and see the fragments of ancient columns.

Nature and man-made architecture merged together in Cappadocia, glorious both for its unique rocks and underground churches of the first Christians. Uniquely preserved Ich-Kale fortress will introduce you to the history of Byzantium.

It will be interesting for children to visit the discovery park, water park. Well, adults will be happy to visit the eastern bazaar of Alanya and the famous jewelry shops.
