Smartphone Huawei Y6 Pro: reviews, specifications

Modern mobile gadgets always delight with a stylish design, high-quality and large screen, good performance and support for other high-tech devices. Nevertheless, there is one very significant point that manufacturers either forget about or specifically assign almost the last role to it - this is the battery life.





Engineers and designers of companies are trying to make new models of smartphones thinner, more powerful, with large screens at Full HD-resolution, with support for a huge range of applications, in general, more beautiful and more expensive. This statement is refuted by the hero of today's review - a stylish and very capable gadget from the middle price range, distinguished by a capacious battery and good technical indicators. This is Huawei Y6 Pro. Characteristics, hardware and communication capabilities, as well as the opinions of specialists, together with the reviews of ordinary smartphone owners will be discussed later in the review. On the shelves of many stores, the model is presented as Enjoy 5, Honor 5X Play, Honor 4C Pro and Holly 2+. With its impressive weight of 160 grams, the device has the appropriate dimensions: 143.1 x 71.8 x 9.7 mm, which fits well with decency for five-inch gadgets. At the moment, official dealers have two versions of colors: Huawei Y6 Pro Gold (golden) and Gray (gray).

Screen

The five-inch model has a basic resolution of 1280 by 720 pixels, which even today, in search of "more, higher and more powerful", in this class of devices is considered a decent feature. The density of dots per inch is also a good indicator - 294 ppi. And all this works on a completely sensible IPS-matrix, with proper saturation and contrast.





The brightness adjustment range has a good variation in the entire Huawei Y6 Pro series. The review of corners is also up to the mark. You can easily watch photos or videos in the company of one or two friends. Moreover, viewing angles are equally good both horizontally and vertically for all Huawei Y6 Pro models. Hardware platform

For the speed of the smartphone, the Mediatek MT6735P series platform runs on four cores with a clock frequency of 1.3 GHz (ARM "Cortex-A53"). For video streams, the built-in chip from the Mali T720 MP2 series at 600 MHz is responsible. In general, the capacities for most daily tasks are more than enough for the Huawei Y6 Pro models.

The review showed that the gadget is equipped with two gigabytes of RAM and 16 GB of internal memory. Moreover, the user of them remains approximately 10 GB. The device supports all modern micro-SD-drives up to 128 GB, and therefore, owners of Huawei Y6 Pro should not have problems with free space. The case from the brand (company) is equipped with special pockets, so you can store all your memory cards and SIM cards in one place.

Communications

The model provides for work with two micro-SIM cards. They work alternately, that is, when one SIM card is active, the other switches to offline mode. The gadget feels great in the core networks of all well-known mobile operators and supports 2G, 3G and 4G technologies.





Support for popular wireless protocols is also available: Wi-Fi at 802.11 b / g / n and Bluetooth version four. Some owners in their reviews complain that the Huawei Y6 Pro mobile phone was not equipped with NFC technology, but for this price, the available features are more than attractive.

In addition to the standard set of communications, the model supports domestic GLONASS and imported GPS / A-GPS. The gadget can easily be used as a navigator, coupled with standard Google Map mapping.

Offline work

Two things are especially attractive in the Huawei Y6 Pro - the price and battery life. The available 4000 mAh battery allows the phone to "live" in normal mode for about two days. Even if you load it, as they say, to its fullest, charging will still be enough for almost a day.

More specifically, at full screen brightness, playing video in Full HD consumes the battery for almost eleven hours, and this is an excellent indicator for models in this segment.

Autonomy can be significantly prolonged if you turn on special energy-saving modes and disable especially "gluttonous" third-party applications. Moreover, the gadget takes on the optimization of the software and the entire device as a whole, competently reminding and connecting the currently required mode (custom function).

Separately, it is worth mentioning another feature of the model. Y6 Pro can act as an external battery powering other gadgets, and this, as a rule, is the prerogative of expensive devices from the category "price exceeded 30 thousand rubles." Users in their reviews appreciated this feature, putting the highest rating in the autonomy section.
