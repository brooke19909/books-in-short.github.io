Syzran hotels: addresses, description, photo

The ancient Russian city of Syzran is located in the Samara region. It is a developed administrative center of the Syzran region. This wonderful ancient city is located on the banks of the Saratov reservoir. The population is 185.3 thousand people.

From the history of the city

The fortress on the Volga was founded by the governor Grigory Kozlovsky by decree of Peter Alekseevich in 1683. The main architectural symbol of the city is the white tower of the Kremlin. There were five such towers once - four of them are wooden, and one, twenty-seven meters high, is made of stone. This is the Spasskaya Tower. At first it was the gateway, and then it was rebuilt into the belfry of the Cathedral of the Nativity of Christ.



Of particular historical value to the building is the fact that the Syzran Kremlin, reduced to two buildings, is the only one on the banks of the Volga between Astrakhan and Kazan.

Syzran today

Today the city ranks third in the region in terms of industrial capacity. Heavy, food, light industry are developed in Syzran, construction is underway, transport and small business are developing.

Today, both tourists and travelers who want to see the ancient sights of the city come to Syzran. For them there will be no problems with accommodation in hotels. Comfortable Syzran hotels are located in almost all areas of the city. They offer not only comfortable rooms where you can relax after a busy day, but also conference rooms where you can meet with business partners, celebrate the celebration in banquet rooms, visit restaurants and cafes.
