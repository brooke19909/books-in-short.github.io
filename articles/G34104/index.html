Rest in the village of Veselovka: recreation center, living conditions, entertainment

The recreation center "Rainbow" is one of the best located in this region. However, the article will discuss not only her, but also about other options, such as Gems, Friendship, and Uralochka.





It should be noted that in the village itself there are few places where you can relax if you do not take into account camping. However, they are all in demand and really deserve praise in both maintenance and cleanliness.

Veselovka (recreation center "Rainbow") is surrounded by unique nature. This is a small village where the population reaches a peak in the summer. At the same time, many vacationers prefer to rent a house, rather than rent a room at the camp site. On the one hand - cheaper, but on the other - this option does not provide an entertainment program.

"Rainbow"

For a better pastime in this area, you must come to the village of Veselovka. The recreation center "Rainbow" is located 300 meters from the coastline. Guests can use the sauna, pool, dining room. The latter offers three meals a day.





Guests can be accommodated both in standard rooms and in separate houses. The administration allows children of any age to enter. Especially for them equipped playground. Each room has air conditioning. Equipped with a car park for those who arrived in their own car. Meals are included in the price. It is carried out using the technology of the "buffet", which cannot but please the guests. The center is only 3 km. The recreation center works from the beginning of summer until the beginning of autumn. No pets allowed.

"Gems"

"Gems" are located on the Taman Peninsula. Next to this recreation center is the Black Sea. At the same time, this complex is able to accept only 200 people. The recreation center "Gems" (Veselovka) on its territory has a dining room, a dance room, a sports ground. All rooms are quite comfortable and cozy. The beach with all amenities is located 200 meters from the base. Work "Gems" from May to October.

In the center of Taman there is a branch of the recreation center. It takes tourists all year round. Residents are housed in a three-story house, where there are only 60 places. The rooms are designed for 2, 3 and 4 people. They are quite spacious and comfortable. They have the latest furniture and appliances. A TV and fridge are included. On the territory of "Gems" there is a bathhouse, a billiard room, hockey, a parking lot, as well as a playground.

"Friendship"

In Temryuk district, the best village is Veselovka. The recreation center "Friendship" is located 200 meters from the Black Sea coast. The complex is designed for 75 people. The base is perfect for those who want to come with family. The rooms have all the amenities, as well as household appliances.





The dining room is working, there is an animator who will brighten up an ordinary evening on the territory of this base. Literally one step away from the living area there is a cafe, shops (grocery and wine), as well as a barbecue area.

The recreation center "Friendship" in Veselovka is located 500 meters from Solenoe Lake. It has healing properties. Nearby are the historical and cultural centers of the area. They attract most of the tourists.

"Uralochka"

Tourists often come to the village of Veselovka. The recreation center "Uralochka" stands on the Black Sea coast. 200 meters from the settlement there is a beach with all the necessary equipment for a pleasant time.

The recreation center offers its guests rooms that are distinguished by excellent comfort, there is a sports and dance floor. A buffet and a dining room are also located on the territory, where 120 people can simultaneously sit.





The central equipped beach is much further than the usual coastline. To the last distance is only 10 meters. Children will not be bored by specially trained people (animators) who constantly conduct recreational activities.

For adults, excursions to crocodile and ostrich farms, museums, Abrau-Durso. Anapa, which is located very close, has a planetarium, parks, dolphinariums and water parks. The latter are most often visited by children. Minimum room rate - 600 rubles.
