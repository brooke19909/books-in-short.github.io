Hotel Albatros Jungle Aqua Park (Egypt / Hurghada): overview, features and reviews of tourists

The flow of tourists coming to Egypt is not decreasing. And this is not surprising: there are excellent climatic conditions, low prices for accommodation in hotels, quite good service. People who prefer a measured beach vacation come here, so they buy all-inclusive tours, those attracted by the Red Sea with its many coral reefs, as well as travelers who combine relaxation with sightseeing. And there are a lot of them in Egypt, as you know.

The Russians have long been chosen by local resorts, including Hurghada, which is considered the oldest tourist center. It is located on the west coast of the Red Sea, five hundred kilometers from the capital. It is believed that it was from this city that tourism began to develop in Egypt , which became so popular among our compatriots . Vacationers mostly come to Hurghada with their children. This is due to the fact that local hotels offer a fairly developed infrastructure for small tourists. In addition, on this part of the coast, children can safely swim in the sea, and their parents can not worry that children will injure their feet on corals.

Overview

For those who come to Egypt not only to swim and sunbathe on the beach, but to see local attractions, Hurghada is perfect. From this resort you can easily get to Luxor, visit the Giza Valley and see the famous pyramids. Transport accessibility of Hurghada, many tourists call one of the advantages when choosing a destination for recreation. Well, and, of course, it is important that this Egyptian resort has a well-developed tourist infrastructure. The city has a lot of small shops and supermarkets, there are night clubs, water parks, restaurants, cafes, hookahs. Almost all the beaches in Hurghada are artificially deepened lagoons. Many large hotels offer divers to dive into their own coral reefs.

In terms of price factor, the resort of Hurghada is considered quite democratic. In this city, tourists will be able to find any accommodation options for themselves, based on their own wallet and taste. Location

Jungle Aqua Park Resort is just ten kilometers from Hurghada Airport. This undoubted plus in their reviews is noted by many tourists. This is of great importance when choosing a accommodation option for those tourists who come to rest with young children. Kids, as a rule, do not tolerate transfer after a flight, so proximity to the airport undoubtedly plays one of the decisive roles in choosing a hotel. Albatros Jungle Aqua Park 4 * is located fifteen kilometers from the center of Hurghada. It is thirty-one kilometers from Soma Bay, and thirty-seven kilometers from El Gouna.

Infrastructure

This relatively new hotel in 2009 received its first visitors. It has a fairly large territory, which is completely landscaped: neatly trimmed lawns, flowering trees, many palm trees and evergreen shrubs are everywhere.

Jungle Aqua Park Resort has a highly developed infrastructure for its category. Guests are greeted by receptionists in a spacious lobby where it is always cool. Friendly staff promptly issues room keys to visitors, answers any questions. The reception is open around the clock. Here you can also order a taxi, purchase an excursion tour, and return valuables to the left luggage office.

The receptionist will also call a doctor if necessary, arrange a transfer. A car rental service is available in the lobby. The hotel infrastructure includes a laundry, a currency exchange office, and a bank branch. Guests can use a beauty salon, receive money from an ATM, purchase the necessary goods in one of the stores operating on the territory of Albatros Jungle Aqua Park (4 *).

Nutrition

Like the vast majority of Egyptian hotels, the Albatros Jungle Aqua Park hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis. 5 restaurants, 9 bars, cafes and a fast food restaurant on its territory are waiting for visitors. Throughout the day, guests can get snacks and drinks, coffee and tea. Guests can enjoy German, Italian and Mediterranean dishes upon prior reservation. In one of the restaurants, food is prepared right in front of visitors.

Meals at the hotel are organized on a self-service basis - buffet. The menu, judging by the reviews, is quite diverse: cereals, egg dishes, several types of sausages, cheese, jams, etc. are served for breakfast. The choice is large. In general, food is one of the advantages that Jungle Aqua Park 4 * (Hurghada) is famous for. Reviews that food is not enough and that it is monotonous are not entirely fair. There is always delicious pastries on the trays, many desserts, including jelly, cakes and pastries. Fruits are served on a seasonal basis, so not every race you can see watermelons or, for example, persimmons.

Beach

Hotel Jungle Aqua Park (Egypt) is located on the second line from the sea. But this does not bother vacationers in the least, since a bus runs to the beach every ten to fifteen minutes. The distance to the sea is one kilometer, you need to go only five minutes. Many tourists go to the beach in the morning, and spend the afternoon in the water park. The sea in this part of the Red Sea is warm and clean. The beach is sandy, entry into the water is comfortable, smooth. The beach has all the necessary infrastructure - toilets, showers, a bar. Sun loungers and umbrellas for hotel guests are free of charge, towels can be taken from the room.

The beach is a sand lagoon, at a depth of fifteen meters live amazing corals.

Services for the smallest

Tour operators Albatros Jungle Aqua Park 4 * is offered as an excellent option for a family vacation. Many come here with children who really like to spend all day in the water park - in the hotel it is simply huge. For its young customers, Albatros Jungle Aqua Park 4 * offers fifteen sections in unheated pools, slides, a playground. Parents can get an extra rollaway bed for them. The restaurant has free chairs for easy feeding.

In general, children are the most welcome guests of this hotel. Numerous entertainment programs are organized for them. In addition to water activities, a club operates in the hotel, in which children play, participate in competitions, etc., under the supervision of young animators.

Free entertainment

The hallmark of this hotel is, of course, its water park - the largest in Hurghada. It consists of thirty-two outdoor pools, two of which are heated, and thirty-five slides. But in addition to the water park in Albatros Jungle Aqua Park 4 * provides a lot of entertainment. On its territory there is a well-equipped gym, mini-golf and football grounds. Those who wish can do water aerobics in the pools in the mornings, play bocce or darts. The beach has a net for beach volleyball. In the evenings, show programs are organized, and live music is played in many bars.

Paid entertainment and services

Those who prefer an active vacation are advised to visit the spa, sauna and Turkish hammam. Judging by the reviews, the hotel has an inexpensive massage room, the prices of which are significantly lower than in the city. Fans of water activities can go scuba diving, as well as diving or kitesurfing. There are special centers on the beach where you can not only rent equipment, but also pay for the services of a licensed instructor.

Additional Information

Many interesting entertainments await tourists outside the hotel. On the main street of the resort there are many entertainment venues. In addition, in the lobby you can buy sightseeing tours to local attractions, go to Hurghada to visit the aquarium. Many Russians liked a boat trip and a bathyscaphe dive, which is filmed on video.

The hotel offers its guests several compliments: birthday, wedding anniversary, and newlyweds. Regular customers get a discount.

Prices

Today, quite a lot of Russians have already visited Albatros Jungle Aqua Park. The prices in this hotel depend on the season and the room category.
