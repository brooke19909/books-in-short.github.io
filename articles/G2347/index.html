Hotel "Kraft", St. Petersburg: photos and reviews of tourists

If you want to get to know the cultural capital closer, pay attention to such an accommodation option as Kraft. St. Petersburg will be in full view due to its convenient location. And high standards of service will satisfy even the most demanding travelers.





Institution History

One of the most popular accommodation options is Kraft (St. Petersburg). Description of the hotel should begin with the history of the building in which it is located. This is the so-called Nikolaev house, which was built in the XVIII century. The building has a specific triangular shape, characteristic of the then classicism.

At the beginning of the twentieth century, this building became a bookstore, which was opened by the famous businessman and educator Sytin. In 1917, the shop underwent nationalization and subsequent liquidation. The latest history of the Nikolayev house began in 2001, when it was included in the list of objects of historical and cultural heritage. And by 2015, restoration and finishing work had been completed, transforming the building into a modern Kraft hotel.

Location

A favorable position is characterized by Kraft (hotel, St. Petersburg). The address of this institution is Sadovaya street, 25. The hotel is located in the central part of the northern capital, literally 2 km from the Moscow railway station and 12 km from Pulkovo airport. This is an ideal place for those who want to get to know the city better, because in the immediate vicinity there are many attractions. So, you will overcome the path to Nevsky Prospekt in less than 10 minutes. One and a half kilometers from the hotel is St. Isaac's Cathedral, and a kilometer - Palace Square. The hotel is a little more than 1 km from the Hermitage and the Mariinsky Theater.

Kraft Hotel (St. Petersburg)

How to get there for a traveler? To the left of Pulkovo Airport there is a bus stop with bus number 39. Drive to Moskovskaya metro station, where through the underpass you need to go to Sennaya Ploshchad station, where the hotel is located.

If you arrive at the railway station, you need to go to Nevsky Prospekt and go to the Ploshchad Vosstaniya metro station. There is a public transport stop nearby . You need to take any bus and get to the intersection of Nevsky Prospekt and Sadovaya Street. Next, turn to Muchnaya Lane, from the side of which the hotel entrance is located.

Additional Information

Going to the Kraft Hotel (St. Petersburg), guests should familiarize themselves with some rules of accommodation in this institution, namely:

  • Check-in is possible after 14:00, and guests must leave the room on the day of departure before noon;
  • Children under 6 years old stay in rooms for free if they do not need extra beds (children under 2 years old are provided with special cots);
  • pets are not allowed in the hotel;
  • the administration accepts credit cards;
  • guests who are not citizens of the Russian Federation are charged a registration fee of 150 rubles (this rule does not apply to citizens of the Republic of Belarus and Kazakhstan);
  • The administration reserves the right to block a certain amount of funds on the guest's credit card as collateral.
