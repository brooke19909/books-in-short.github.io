When to go to Sochi

The resort of Russia No. 1 accepts vacationers all year round. After the Olympics in 2014, the tourist infrastructure for mountain skiers improved. Krasnaya Polyana stood out as an almost independent resort. There are many sports facilities and, of course, well-equipped slopes for descents. Those who come here to improve their health and recover from ailments are not interested in the weather and the temperature of the water in the sea, but in the specialization of the health resorts. But these two categories of vacationers, in general terms, know where to relax in Sochi. In the summer, the resort simply overwhelms the influx of holidaymakers who are eager to taste the beach pleasures. The lion's share comes in July and the first half of August. But knowledgeable people advise not to succumb to the general hype and go to Sochi in September. The velvet season in the Caucasus will delight not only with mild warmth, but also beaches not filled to the eyeballs, and - importantly! - reasonable prices.

Where to stay cheap in Sochi

The local population of the resort totals only four hundred thousand inhabitants, while every year three million tourists rest here. Therefore, we say frankly: it is difficult to find budget housing in Sochi. Beach vacations are a costly affair for visitors and beneficial for locals who try to squeeze as much as possible in three months of the season so that there is something to exist for the rest of the year. Therefore, be prepared to face difficulties in finding affordable housing. There is no single answer to the question of where it is inexpensive to stay in Sochi. The same room or hotel room can cost differently. Therefore, the main rule in the search for budget housing is to negotiate without intermediaries. Early booking can also reduce the cost of renting a roof over your head. What to choose - a boarding house, a recreation center, a hotel, a guest house or an apartment (or even a room) with private individuals - you decide. The main thing is to decide what is important for you: amenities, price, proximity to the sea?



Where to stay in Sochi inexpensively: private sector

Apartments and rooms have been and remain the most affordable housing for budget travelers. Convenience is another matter. Do not expect to find budget housing with a European-style renovation and new furniture. For two hundred and fifty rubles, you will most likely be handed over a converted garage with a toilet in the courtyard and an outdoor shower. In the process of finding cheap housing from private owners, you need to pay attention to two patterns. First: the farther from the center of Sochi, the more the price drops. Therefore, it is worth taking an interest in the Lazarevsky, Adlersky and Khostinsky areas. If in the center of the apartment there are about three thousand rubles a day, then in Khost or Adler you can find for 600. But these satellite resorts have their own center. If you are interested in where to stay inexpensively in Sochi, then you need to look for a place away from the sea. For example, in Adler, this is the district of Blue Dali, the streets of Farmers, Verkhneizvestinskaya and everything that is beyond Lenin Street. There you can find a very decent housing and for little money (300 rubles). Another thing is how you will daily overcome a high angle of inclination, climbing from the sea up.



Guest houses

These are small family-run hotels with several rooms. The amenities in these guest houses are completely different. Sometimes there is one toilet and shower for several rooms, and sometimes a bathroom is in the room, along with a refrigerator, air conditioning, TV. In terms of food, guest houses often work like hotels: either order food from them, or dine somewhere on the side. But you can find those where they rent mini-apartments - a room with a small kitchen. Such guest houses are the best option for those who are looking for where it is better to relax in Sochi. Reviews are again advised to pay attention to Adler. Prices range from 250 to 600 rubles per person per day. And this is at the peak of the season (July-August)! Choosing a guest house to stay in Sochi, you should also contact the owners directly, bypassing the intermediaries. Unlike private owners hiding from the tax, such family hotels are officially registered and often have their own page on the Internet.

Hostels

Students and other young people not burdened by children are also often looking for where to stay cheap in Sochi. This category of vacationers chooses hostels. A distinctive feature of such housing is that the rooms are designed for a large number of residents - from four guests. The rooms are often equipped with bunk beds. But in such hostel hotels there are kitchens, which is important for those who want to save on food. Hostels are located in prestigious places. So, VeryHostel is located in the very center of Small Sochi, near the Riviera, the singing fountains and the Church of the Archangel Michael. You can also recommend "Garage" and "Hostel Olympic." The latter is located five kilometers from the central district of Sochi. But, along with the kitchen, vacationers can use the washing machine, and the rooms have air conditioning.

Which district of Sochi to choose

The resort, as we recall, is very stretched along the coast. And this means that the sea is enough for everyone. Another thing is that the beaches are not always well-maintained. And if you decide where to relax with children in Sochi, you need to keep this in mind. Pebble beaches, especially with large stones, are not always useful for the legs of a child. In some places, the depth begins a couple of meters from the shore. In the Central District of Sochi, the beach is landscaped, but its congestion (especially during the peak season) nullifies all the joy of relaxation. With children it is better to stay in Loo, Adler, Dagomys. On the other hand, almost all the entertainment for young holidaymakers is located in the Central District. In the park "Riviera" there is an aquarium "Secrets of the sea." You should not miss the opportunity to visit the arboretum with children. In addition to the rich flora, there is a small zoo where a child can take part in feeding ostriches. There are two dolphinariums in Sochi. In one of them penguins participate in the show. And near the seaport there is a water park (Mayak beach). The minus of the Central District is overpriced compared to other parts of the city. Lovers of wild beaches choose Hosta.
