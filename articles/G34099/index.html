Kavros Beach 3 * (Greece / Crete) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Crete is not only the largest island of Greece, but also the most popular resort region of this country. Every year, tens of thousands of travelers come here, attracted by magnificent sandy beaches, excellent infrastructure, as well as the widest selection of hotels and entertainment for every taste and budget. Crete will appeal to both young people and family tourists who want to have a great rest on the seashore. If you are thinking about spending your vacation on this island, but want to find a comfortable hotel at an affordable price, then consider the Kavros Beach 3 * hotel. Next, we propose to learn more about what is offered to the guests here, and also find out whether our compatriots who have already stayed in it were satisfied with their choice.

Where is

The three-star Kavros Beach Hotel is conveniently located on the seashore. Nearby are the tourist villages of Kavros and Georgioupolis. The distance to them from the hotel is about three kilometers. Just seven kilometers from the hotel is one of the most famous Cretan natural attractions - freshwater lake Kournas. In antiquity, the city of Corion was located here, and the reservoir itself was called Korisya.

In the vicinity of the hotel there is all the necessary entertainment infrastructure: taverns, bars, shops, souvenir shops, etc. 40 km from Kavros Beach 3 * is a large port city of Chania. If you are one of the tourists who come to Greece for the most part for a beach holiday, you will be pleased with the fact that the Kavros Beach Hotel is located on the very shore and has its own beach. It is large, clean, sandy, with a gentle entry into the water. You can comfortably sit on the free sun loungers.





How to get there

The nearest airport to the hotel is in the capital of Crete - the city of Heraklion. The distance between it and Kavros Beach Resort 3 * is 95 km. Thus, upon arrival at the air harbor, your journey to the hotel will take about two hours. If you travel using the services of a travel agency, you will most likely be provided with a transfer from the airport. In this case, you will be delivered to the hotel by a comfortable bus, and along the way the guide will entertain you with a fascinating story about Greece in general and Crete in particular. Therefore, time on the road will fly by. If you travel on your own, then you can order a transfer directly to the hotel or get to the place of accommodation by bus. If during your trip to Greece you plan to visit not only Crete, then you can move around by sea. In this case, your route will run through the port city of Chania.





Crete, Greece, Kavros Beach 3 *: description and photo

This hotel was built in 1999. In 2006, a large-scale reconstruction was carried out here. Sometimes this hotel can be found under the name Kavros Beach Yiassou 3 *, since it consists of two bungalow complexes - Kavros Beach itself, as well as Yassou Criti. They are located parallel to each other on the very shore of the sea. In total, this hotel complex includes two hundred rooms: standard and suites. They are small but comfortable, equipped with everything necessary for living. In addition to a great beach holiday, guests are invited to spend time by the pool, eat in a restaurant, enjoy a drink at the bar, visit the gym, play volleyball, tennis or billiards, have fun at a disco, and take part in fun contests and events organized by animators. Hotel Kavros Beach 3 *, reviews of tourists confirm that, is the best solution both for family people with children and for youth companies who want a budget holiday in Crete.

Posting Rules

As in the vast majority of other hotels around the world, the three-star Kavros Beach has a set checkout time. So, check-in of arriving guests is made in the afternoon. However, if you arrive at the hotel much earlier, then if you have available rooms, they will try to check in immediately. If there are no free rooms (this often happens at the very peak of the tourist season in July and August), you can leave your things in the luggage compartment and begin to relax. After your room is prepared, you can check in. As for the day of departure, you should vacate your room no later than 10 a.m. In this case, it is necessary to return the room key and pay for the entire period of stay. You can pay in Kavros Beach 3 * both in cash and with a plastic card.

restaurants and bars

Dining at the Kavros Beach 3 * hotel (Crete) is organized on an all-inclusive basis. Three times a day, an experienced chef serves a buffet in the hotel restaurant. Here you can taste both familiar and traditional Greek dishes. In addition, guests can enjoy delicious snacks, desserts, fresh vegetables and fruits. During the day, guests are invited to enjoy coffee with freshly baked pastries. In addition to the restaurant in Kavros Beach, there are two bars: by the pool and on the beach. They have a wide selection of drinks for every taste.





Sea and beach

As already mentioned, the three-star Kavros Beach Hotel has its own spacious beach. It is sandy, equipped with everything you need for a great vacation. There is also a bar on the beach where you can always refresh yourself with a drink. Please note that the hotel does not provide beach towels. Therefore, you can either bring them with you, or buy them in one of the shops next door to the hotel.

Entertainment

Kavros Beach Hotel has a large freshwater pool. Next to it is also equipped with a children's pool and a spacious sun terrace, where you can comfortably sit and relax. There is also a bar with a wide selection of drinks.

Fans of outdoor activities can play tennis, ping pong, volleyball, billiards, visit the gym. At the height of the holiday season, animators work at the hotel, and in the evenings there are entertainment events, shows and discos.

In addition, tourists who have already visited Crete, strongly recommend that you do not spend all the time in the walls of the hotel, but be sure to go on trips around the island. After all, there is a mass of interesting sights.





Infrastructure

The Kavros Beach 3 * hotel has all the necessary infrastructure for a comfortable stay and pastime. So, the reception desk is open around the clock. In the lobby of the hotel you can use the Internet via Wi-Fi. Turning to the reception, you can rent a car, book a tour of interest, as well as get background information.

The cost of living in a hotel

As for the price of accommodation in Kavros Beach, it generally corresponds to the category of the hotel. So, a seven-day stay in a standard room will cost from 30 thousand rubles.
