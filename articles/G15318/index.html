Pattaya in November: weather, reviews of tourists, tips for vacationers

Tourists like to visit exotic countries not only in the summer, but also at other times of the year. Should I go to Pattaya in November? Of course. This is an amazing place in which it is pleasant to relax regardless of the season. Pattaya in November will give you many interesting opportunities and exciting experiences.





Great place to stay

Pattaya is one of the wonderful cities of Thailand. It is located in the southeastern part of this exotic country. More than one hundred thousand inhabitants live here, but thanks to tourists who come here from all over the world during the year, this figure is much larger. Sometimes it reaches several million vacationers. Such a number of people come because there are the best beaches in all of Thailand, many nightclubs, bars, restaurants. Other sights of the city also deserve close attention of tourists.

Weather in Pattaya in November

If you come here in the fall, you will not regret it at all. In Pattaya, most precipitation falls from March to November. The rest of the time they are insignificant. Rains sometimes occur, but, as a rule, not for long, after a bad weather, the warm sun always shines. The temperature in the second autumn month here is 29-30 degrees Celsius. The most optimal for sunbathing and swimming in the sea. The water in it is very warm and pleasant. The weather in Pattaya in November is quite comfortable, so you can safely come here at this time. You can not only enjoy the pleasant climatic conditions, but also visit many interesting places. Read more about this.





Tours and activities

A huge variety of activities is waiting for you in Thailand. Pattaya in November offers interesting excursions and entertainment not only for adults, but also for children. We invite readers to familiarize themselves with the most popular:

  1. Visit to the Nong Nooch Tropical Garden . You will certainly love this place. Here is a collection of the most beautiful tropical plants from around the globe.
  2. International Fireworks Festival. At the end of November in Pattaya, you can become a member of an amazing sight. Pyrotechnics come here from different parts of the world to demonstrate the beauty of their art. Amazingly beautiful salute lasts for an hour. The action is accompanied by pleasant music. It is impossible to remain indifferent to such beauty, and the mood remains upbeat for a long time. It should be noted that at the festival you can hear the performances of famous singers and musicians, as well as buy souvenir products.
  3. Tourist beaches, which are considered the cleanest and most comfortable on the entire coast of the Gulf of Thailand.
  4. Water park "Ramayana". Despite the fact that it was opened in 2016, it is always crowded here. Adults and children with great pleasure ride on various slides, feed elephants (which are located in a separate enclosure), relax in cafes, bars and restaurants.
  5. Zoo Khao Kheo. Its main feature: animals are not in cages, but freely move around the territory. Some can even be stroked and fed. Fortunately, predators are behind bars. The territory of the zoo is huge, and there are about eight thousand different animals. You can move around on transport or on elephants.
  6. Harbor Mall Pattaya. Your children will be delighted if you come here with them. Thailand's largest entertainment center has a wide variety of gaming areas. Here you can play slot machines, slide down the slides, jump on trampolines, rock climbing and much more.   7. Oceanarium. It strikes with a variety of underwater inhabitants. For the convenience of visitors, there is a division into several zones, each of which is responsible for a certain part of the world.




Pattaya in November

If you are still thinking about whether to go to this beautiful city in the fall, we suggest that you once again pay attention to all the advantages of such a holiday.

  • the rainy season ends and the rainfall is very small;
  • there is no big influx of tourists who like to come here in the summer and winter months;
  • hotel prices and excursions have not yet begun to rise;
  • You can see and take part in a wonderful holiday - the International Fireworks Festival;
  • without queues and crowds you can admire the main attractions of the city;
  • at this time, prices for tickets are reduced, as well as more favorable offers from employees of travel agencies.




Holiday Tips

If you decide to spend a week or two in Pattaya in November, then the following information will definitely come in handy:

  1. Pay attention to the safety of your belongings. Especially valuable should be left in the safe with the hotel administrator or in your room. You should not take expensive jewelry and things with you on a trip.
  2. Be sure to bring sunscreen, as well as the necessary medications, for a vacation in Pattaya in November.
  3. Remember that smoking is only allowed in strictly designated areas.
