How to fund Yandex money account, as well as registration, withdrawal and transfer

Before you start your Yandex-wallet, you need to register in this system, one of the largest Internet companies in the Russian network, offering its users a huge number of different services.





The first step of registration requires entering your data, such as first name, last name and login in the system. So, having registered in the system, click on the menu tab under the item "Create Yandex account". To create a Yandex wallet, enter the following in the fields provided:

  • payment password;
  • code number;
  • e-mail;
  • your birthday

After a message appears stating that the registration of the wallet was successful, you will need to go to the "Start using Yandex money" tab. Now you will learn how to fund your Yandex money account, how to withdraw money and pay bills, issue a bank card for this payment system or link an existing one.

How to fund Yandex money account

You can perform this operation:

  • with a bank card ;
  • In cash;
  • electronic currency;
  • prepaid cards.

In order to learn how to fund Yandex money, you need to select the appropriate item in the menu. Then, following the link, just follow the instructions.

How to withdraw funds

Users of the system are concerned about the question of not only how to replenish the Yandex money account, but also how to withdraw it from there. There are several ways:

  • Transfer to your bank card.
  • To a bank account.
  • Through a translation system.
  • ATM withdrawal.

For instant withdrawal of funds, you must use the issued bank card from Yandex money or attach the cards of banks such as Alfa Bank and Otkritie Bank to the account.

In order to be able to withdraw cash from Yandex-cards, go through verification to confirm your identity.

Identification can be done in the following ways:

  • visit the office of the company;
  • send a notarized letter;
  • make a payment through the CONTACT system, Euroset;
  • using an existing Otkritie bank card.

However, it is possible to pay for purchases in stores without identification. The commission in this case is zero. Yandex payment system card has all the advantages and benefits, like similar cards of any banks. It is plastic, belongs to the MasterCard system, is fully functional. The card is valid for three years.





How to exchange Yandex money for webmoney

This direction is rather complicated, and not all exchange offices offer such a service. However, there are services that allow such a procedure. For this, it is necessary, using any monitoring of exchange points, to select those that work in this direction. In this operation, they act as participants in the process of a difficult operation. You transfer funds from your Yandex account to them, and they, in turn, transfer the necessary amount from your WebMoney wallet to yours. In fact, there is no direct translation between the two systems.
