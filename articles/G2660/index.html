How to add to the blacklist in MTS? How to disable or enable the MTS blacklist

Mobile communication is a great achievement in the field of human communication. It is unlikely that there will be a person who completely abandons the opportunity to communicate with their loved ones, relatives, colleagues through mobile communications.



What is the "Black List"

But sometimes in life there are situations when mobile communication from an assistant and a friend turns into a bothersome, unpleasant, annoying element of life. It also happens that communication is undesirable only with some familiar (or unknown) people.

Or maybe a person wants only a certain group of people to reach him. Avoid unwanted phone contacts will help a service called the "Black List". It is provided just for these purposes. How to add ignored numbers to the black list in MTS and other telecom operators, it is useful to know for each subscriber. Moreover, the modern rhythm of life leaves a person not so much free time that he does not want to spend on unwanted communication.

Blacklists come in many types. You can add to them:

- all incoming;

- all outgoing;

- while staying in roaming;

- outgoing international;

- outgoing international, except for calls directed to the "home" country;

- without restrictions (the subscriber can not call, and he does not receive calls).

When connecting a service, a person selects the type of blacklist he needs. And this solves some of his problems.

How to activate the service

There are several ways you can use to activate the blacklist service. Let's consider them in more detail.



The search for connectivity usually begins with the subscriber learning the instructions for the model of his mobile phone. If there is such a service in the device options, then the connection occurs without the participation of a telecom operator. Typically, the procedure takes a matter of seconds.

If there is no option in the phone, then you can install a special application program. After its installation, the creation of a list of banned subscribers will be possible.

Several of the following ways to activate the service will help answer the question of how to add to the black list in MTS.



The next installation option can be called independent, but the telecom operator is directly involved in this procedure. You need to dial the sequence * 111 * 442 # (call) on your mobile phone, after which you will receive instructions that the subscriber must follow. In case of its error-free actions, the service is connected immediately.

You can activate this offer by sending an SMS with the text 442 * 1 to number 111.

You can use the Internet assistant to activate the call barring service. To find it, first you need to go to the MTS official website. You can configure the service from your personal account, into which the user enters after authorization.

How to disable this offer

Disabling the Black List service (MTS) is possible in the same ways as connecting it. Only the combination of signs for the call and SMS will change:

- 442 * 2 - SMS text message for number 111.

The bans activated by the Black List service can be canceled all at once.
