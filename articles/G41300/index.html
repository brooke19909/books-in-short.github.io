Selfie shop. How to choose a selfie stick?

Selfie stick is not only fashionable, but also really necessary device. Such a device has many other names: a stick for a phone, a selfie stick, a telescopic monopod, an extension cord for a selfie. In the modern world, this device is popular with both young people and older people. How to choose a selfie stick? The answer to this question can be found in the article below.

What is a selfie stick?

A telescopic monopod is used so that you can take your photo at any time without asking outsiders for help. This device allows you to take pictures at an unusual angle. Thanks to the stick for the phone, you can take selfie at the same time with all the friends in the company. Also, when using this device, beautiful panoramic photos are obtained.





Most monopod models are equipped with a camera control button, which is built into the handle of the device. This greatly facilitates the process of creating photos.

Time magazine considered the selfie stick one of the twenty-five best inventions of 2014. Statistics say that at least 25% of US residents posted on social networks selfies made with this device.

How to choose a selfie stick for your phone? A detailed answer to this question will be given below. And now it's time to learn how to use the telescopic monopod correctly.

How to use a selfie stick ?

To use the selfie monopod correctly, you need to take a smartphone, fix it on the end of the stick using a special mount and connect your mobile device using the Bluetooth function. Next, expand the tripod, choose the best angle and take a picture.

In some monopod models, communication between them and the smartphone is not via Bluetooth, but through a special cord. This wire just needs to be inserted into the headphone jack, after which the device is ready for use.

How to choose a selfie stick for iPhone and other smartphones? First of all, you should find out what types of monopods exist.

Types of Selfie Sticks

There are four main types of monopods:

  1. Selfie stick with a bluetooth button on a tripod. Such a device works through bluetooth. The button that activates the camera is located directly on the handle of the device. Such a monopod needs periodic recharging of the battery built into it.
  2. Selfie stick with a separate remote control button. This device works via Bluetooth. A monopod with a remote control will appeal to snowboarders, diving enthusiasts, skiers and fishermen, that is, people who take pictures in conditions where there is the possibility of exposure to moisture on the camera.
  3. Selfie stick with a cord and a button on a tripod. This monopod model communicates with the smartphone not via Bluetooth, but via wire. Simply plug this cord into the headphone jack on your mobile device. Such a device does not require charging, because the power is supplied by wire from the smartphone. When using this model of selfie stick, there is a chance of damage to the cord.
  4. Selfie stick without buttons. Such a device works like a regular tripod. In order to take a photo, you need to set a timer on your smartphone. This is the cheapest selfie stick.

How to choose a monopod for a mobile device? Below is a description of the main criteria that you need to pay attention to. The first one is appearance.

The look of a selfie stick

How to choose a selfie stick? First of all, you should pay attention to its appearance. The device must be made of high quality materials. Plastic should not look cheap. No burrs are allowed on the device. All surfaces must be well treated. The accuracy of the seams is another point to pay attention to.





The base of the monopod on which to mount the smartphone should be made of durable metal. This suggests that you have a high-quality selfie stick.

How to choose a device that will hold the smartphone securely? To do this, you need to pay attention to the locking mechanism of the mobile device.

Smartphone mount and adjustment

On most models of selfie sticks, mounts are installed that allow you to adjust their width for reliable fixation of the smartphone and giving it a horizontal position. A special rubberized clip holds the mobile device.

Some models are equipped with a locking adjustment system that allows you to rotate the camera 180 degrees. Thanks to this, the panoramic angle becomes even wider.



If you have a lightweight smartphone, then the mount can be anything. But if you take photos using heavier mobile devices, the fixation must necessarily be done against torsion.

How to choose a selfie stick if you need to install a camera or camcorder on it? In this case, you need to pay attention to the screw with which the telescopic devices are fixed. Each selfie stick has a metric or inch thread mount. In the first case, only a smartphone can be fixed on the device. But if the monopod is equipped with an inch thread, then it will be possible to mount both a telephone and a video camera or a camera on it.

How to choose a selfie stick and not make a mistake with the size? You need to decide what length of the device you need.

Selfie Stick Length

Telescopic monopods come in various lengths. The average figure for this parameter is 23 centimeters when folded. The longer the device, the more mass it has.





For those who use a selfie stick very often, a lightweight and compact device with a length of seventy centimeters to one meter is more suitable.

If you rarely use a device such as a selfie stick, what is the length of the device? In this case, it is recommended to purchase a monopod no shorter than 105 centimeters. This will allow you to capture the largest angle of panoramic shooting.

There are also mini monopods, which are famous for their unique compactness. They easily fit in a backpack, pocket or wallet. True, they are not cheap.

Conclusion

A telescopic monopod is a device that you need to create your photos. A smartphone is mounted on such a device. The modern range of monopods is wide and varied. How to choose a selfie stick? First you need to decide which of the four main types of these devices described in the article you need. Next, you need to pay attention to such tripod parameters as its appearance, smartphone mount mechanism and length.
