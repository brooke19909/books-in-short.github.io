Big Terenkul Lake

The recreation center "Sphere" is located on the shore of the cleanest lake Big Terenkul, which is located in the ecologically clean Chebarkul resort zone. Its area is more than 2 square kilometers. This lake has a tectonic origin and is fed by many springs. But near the shore its depth is small, so that the water manages to warm up. But on the territory of the recreation center, all conditions for a comfortable stay on the shore are created: a sandy beach, a gentle slope into the water, a fenced area, pontoons.

The shores of the lake are covered with coniferous forest. It is surrounded by mountainous terrain. Therefore, the air in the vicinity is clean and healing. The lake is rich in fish. Here, in addition to common carp, bream and silver carp, pike, grass carp, and ripus are found everywhere.

Accommodation and prices

The recreation center "Sphere" provides rooms of various levels of comfort. These are 2, 3, 4, 5 and 6 bed rooms of economy or luxury. Everyone can choose the option of living at their own expense. The cheapest double room can be rented for 2500 rubles. Moreover, the price includes a full dinner in the restaurant, the room has a TV, refrigerator and a bathroom.

Superior rooms also have satellite TV, shower, refrigerator, kettle. Some units include a kitchen with a dining area and stovetop. In total, there are more than two dozen houses of different sizes on the territory of the recreation center.

Young guests of the recreation center will not be bored. On the territory of the children's game club "Magic Country". Here experienced teachers will look after a child of any age. Rest on the basis of "Sphere" is comfortable and safe. There is a first-aid post and babysitting services are provided .
