Hotel Brestol in Moscow and its services

Hotel Brestol in Moscow provides its guests with twenty rooms of various price levels. The cost ranges from 600 to 2000 rubles per hour.

Hotel equipment meets modern requirements. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, plasma TVs and multimedia centers that support DVD and MP3.

In the living room there are devices for serving a "light" table. If necessary, hotel guests can order food delivery from the hotel restaurant to the room.

Almost half of the rooms have a jacuzzi. All bathrooms are equipped with stylish expensive fixtures. A hairdryer, two bath towels, soap, shampoo and shower gel are provided . Those who wish can additionally order a bathrobe (200 rubles) and terry slippers (100 rubles), disposable slippers (50 rubles) and additional terry towels (50 rubles).

Booking and payment procedure

If you read the reviews about the Brestol Hotel in Moscow, it becomes obvious that it is easiest to reserve a room by telephone. Subject to availability, it can be booked one hour before the estimated time of arrival. If the guest does not arrive at the appointed time, the reservation is automatically canceled.

When the hotel does not have rooms available, a reservation is made in agreement with the hotel administrator. At the same time, a service is provided to notify the client by phone about the possibility of occupying the apartment.

Guests can expect to be released in the hotel lobby. In this case, they will be offered snacks and drinks from the hotel restaurant.

Payment is made in cash in rubles. All necessary documents confirming payments (cash receipts for all types of services) are provided by the hotel.

Hotel Brestol in Moscow provides rooms for rent only for adults. When placing an order, the guest must present an identity document (passport, driver‚Äôs license or military ID).

The minimum period for which you can reserve a room is one hour (for rooms priced above 1,100 rubles per hour). For rooms worth 8,000 rubles, this time is two hours, and for rooms worth 6,000 rubles, three. With daily reservation of rooms a substantial discount is provided (over 40%).

When releasing a room, a twenty-minute unpaid delay is allowed. Exceeding this period leads to the need to pay an additional hour of residence.
