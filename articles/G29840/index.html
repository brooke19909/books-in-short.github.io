Resort Rethymnon

The city meets the needs of all categories of vacationers. Here (since this is Greece after all), there is everything: wide sandy beaches with a gently sloping bottom for bathing children, secluded coves, as if created for lovers, pacifying rural landscapes on the outskirts, fun and shopping in the city itself. The historical center is more like an open-air museum than a settlement, and a tourist eager for excursions will find here a rich Byzantine and Venetian heritage, Ottoman mosques and fountains. If you are in love with Crete, then you can not find a better place for deployment than Rethymnon. From here you can easily make radial trips around the island. Olympic Suites Hotel Apartments is located just two kilometers from the center of Rethymnon. Walking along the beach to the city is a pleasure. And two hundred meters from the entrance to the hotel there is a bus stop. So trips to Crete are available even for those who do not rent a car. Rethymnon can compete with Chania, the second favorite resort for Russians regarding the convenience of location. When to go to Rethymnon

This question cannot be answered unequivocally. If you want to explore the sights of Crete in a comfortable environment - not under the scorching sun and not among the crowds of tourists - any season except summer is suitable for you. Winter is very mild (January temperature is about +14 degrees), but rainy. Spring meets tourists with summer heat, but the deep Mediterranean Sea warms up for a long time. What to see in Rethymnon

Despite the comfort of the rooms and the cleanliness of the pools, you can not limit yourself to 24-hour sitting at the Olympic Suites Hotel Apartments 4 *. Reviews recommend overcoming two kilometers (on foot or by bus) and see the sights of Rethymnon. First of all, you need to visit Fortezza - a fortress that was built by the Venetians in 1573. The lighthouse, still towering above the port, dates back to the same era. On Platanov Square you can admire the Rimodi Fountain. In Rethymno there are a lot of evening entertainment - bars, taverns, discos.
