Slavyanski 3 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria): hotel description, services, reviews

Inexpensive and high-quality vacation offers for tourists Bulgaria. The country is ideal for a budget holiday. Compatriots love her for the clear sea, sandy beaches and decent service. The entire Black Sea coast is built up with tourist complexes of various categories. There are also chic five-star hotels, and four-star hotels are more modest for tourists. Tourists who want to save money often stop at the huge Slavyanski ("Slavic") 3 * complex (Sunny Beach). Is this hotel suitable for a budget holiday?

The nuances of rest in Bulgaria

The tourist season in Bulgaria traditionally begins in early May. In winter, beach hotels do not work here, because the weather at this time is cold and is not suitable for swimming. The season ends with the advent of autumn, namely at the end of September. Most tourists come to rest in July or August. Those who do not like crowded beaches and intense heat can visit the country in early September. The sea temperature will be lower than in the summer months, but it is still suitable for swimming during the day.

In addition to a beach holiday, this country is famous for health resorts offering a variety of wellness treatments. These include mud wraps, treatment with sea and mineral water. Water lovers should visit the numerous water parks of the country. Bulgaria can offer tourists an interesting excursion program. Many ancient and Byzantine monuments have been preserved here. And the country is famous for its picturesque nature. Connoisseurs of beautiful landscapes will surely like the Rila and Pirin national reserves.

Hotel location

The tourist complex Slavyanski Hotel 3 * is located directly on the beach of the Black Sea coast of Bulgaria. To reach the sea, tourists need to cover a distance of only 50 meters. The beach and the hotel are separated by a small pedestrian road, along which guests love to stroll in the evenings. The hotel is part of the famous Sunny Beach resort, which is considered the most environmentally friendly vacation spot in Bulgaria. The sea here is not too deep, so here you can relax with the children. The resort has many playgrounds and pools for them. Children's discos and competitions are constantly held. And 10 km from here recently opened a large water park.





You can get to the Slavyanski 3 * hotel (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) from Burgas Airport, which is 28 km from here. Paid transfers are organized to the resort, and tourists can also order a taxi or rent a car. From the hotel guests can easily get to the center of Sunny Beach, the distance to which is only 2 km. It is worth taking the time and visiting the city of Nessebar, which is one of the oldest cities in Europe. It can be reached by bus. Travel time will be about half an hour.

General information about the complex

Hotel "Slavic" (Bulgaria) stands out among well-groomed and small hotels located on the coast. Tourists who came here are met by a tall building of impressive size, built in an urban style. The only building of the complex offers 250 comfortable rooms, including family apartments, bridal suites, and non-smoking rooms for travelers. It was built in 1973, when there was a massive development of the resort. Amazing panorama opens from the last, twelfth floor of the building. Tourists can enjoy stunning views of the coast or the mountain range located nearby.





The complex allows children of all ages. You can come here with small animals, whose weight does not exceed 3 kg. Prices for accommodation here are very low. For example, the minimum cost for a standard double room is approximately 1,500 rubles. Thus, apart from paying for additional services, a week-long rest here will cost tourists about 10,500 rubles for two. Check-in starts at 14:00, and you will have to leave the hotel early in the morning. Eviction takes place from 8:00 to noon. We accept Visa and MasterCard.

Room Amenities

All rooms are equipped with the necessary amenities for a comfortable stay. So, everywhere there is a separate bathroom. Should I go here on vacation?

If you are looking for an inexpensive hotel for relaxation and do not really care about the quality of service, then the Slavyanski 3 * complex (Sunny Beach) will be your ideal option. The price for a room here is quite consistent with the quality of the services provided, but you should not hope for a luxurious reception. This place is designed for a quiet holiday away from the hustle and bustle, so lovers of vibrant nightlife should choose another hotel. "Slavic" is perfect for couples with young children, as well as for older people.
