Sperlonga Resort in Italy

In today's world, fewer and less ideal places from an environmental point of view remain. And so I want to plunge into the clear sea water, feel warm clean sand under my feet. Such a rare and charming place is the town of Sperlonga in Italy.

Location Features

Sperlonga is located in the province of Latina in the central part of the country. The town is located on the slopes of the cliff of Monte Magno on the picturesque coast of the Tyrrhenian Sea. This coast of Italy is called the Riviera di Ulisse or the Riviera of Odyssey.

History tour

The coast of the Tyrrhenian Sea in Italy is considered one of the most beautiful. The fabulous white town of Sperlonga is a quiet and comfortable place, ideal for a relaxing holiday. But that was not always the case. In ancient times, a small fishing village was repeatedly subjected to destructive raids by pirates. Due to the constant threat of attack, local residents, taking advantage of the terrain, preferred to equip their homes in caves and grottoes, covering the rocky coast in abundance.





Residents even surrounded the city with a fortress wall. The town also received its name from the Latin word spelunca, which means "cave, grotto." During the time of the Roman Empire, a wonderful coastal area was liked by the emperor Tiberius. He built a luxury villa here, which is still one of the main attractions of Sperlonga. Later, many conquerors fought for Sperlonga. The city was repeatedly destroyed almost completely, but always revived again. Calmness and silence came to Sperlonga only in 1861, when the city became part of a united Italy. And tourism came to the town only in the middle of the 20th century.

Climate and ecology

The Tyrrhenian coast of Italy is known worldwide for the purity of coastal waters and beaches. It even earned the Blue Flag from the European Foundation for Environmental Education for its high environmental performance. Sperlonga in Italy is famous for the most clear days in a year. The comfortable Mediterranean climate allows you to open the holiday season in April, and the last tourists leave the town in late autumn.





In summer, air temperatures rarely exceed 30 degrees Celsius. The temperature of sea water is about 25 degrees. Guests of the city note the cleanliness of not only the beaches and the sea, but also the city streets, hotels, restaurants and other places of recreation.

Hotels and entertainment

For tourists around the world, one of the most beloved countries is Italy. Sperlonga, in turn, is among the Italian resorts, where a place to stay must be booked long before the start of the vacation. The small size of the city and ideal conditions for relaxation provide a full house for the entire holiday season. In Sperlonga you can rent a separate apartment or room, or you can book a villa. City hotels belong to the category of 2, 3, 4 stars. Basically, these are small-sized hotels with all the necessary infrastructure for a comfortable stay. A distinctive feature of Sperlonga hotels is its proximity to the beach. Almost from anywhere to the sea can be reached in five minutes on foot. People go to Sperlonga mainly for a relaxing beach holiday and beautiful natural views. It is a pleasure to spend time on the Tyrrhenian Sea, and then sit in a restaurant in the open air, try Mediterranean dishes prepared according to authentic recipes.





You can admire the landscapes from one of the many Sperlonga viewing platforms or in the evening visit the popular Fortino disco. And you can go to Cassino to the water park, which will live up to the wildest expectations of lovers of active entertainment.

sights

Sperlonga sights in Italy include, first of all, unusual architecture, which was formed thanks to numerous caves and grottoes. One of the most famous is the cave of Tiberius. It is also interesting to see the ruins of the ancient Roman villa of Tiberius, the grotto of Ninfeo, the ancient church of Santa Maria, located in the upper old part of the city. Excursions to the nearby towns of the Lazio region are aesthetic pleasure. This is the port of Gaet, and the old city of Fondi, and the temple of Jupiter Anhur in Terracina. In the central part of Italy, historical monuments are found literally at every step. Due to the location of the spa town, relaxing in Sperlonga, you can easily make wonderful excursions to Rome, Naples, Capri, Pompeii.
