How to make a grenade from paper - the main ways

Since ancient times, paper has attracted many people who are not indifferent to needlework. Working with her develops logic, thinking, and opens various skills in a person. Of particular interest are crafts on military subjects. All these pistols, helicopters are admired by many fans of this topic. Fans of this art form are always busy looking for something new and unusual. Our article will be able to interest such people, since it will discuss how to make a grenade out of paper at home. It turns out that there are many technologies for making such crafts. In this case, you can use a lot of things that gather dust in a closet and do not find a use for themselves. How to make a grenade out of paper? This article will tell you some interesting ideas and help you build this craft out of ordinary materials.

The first technology for making paper grenades

So, how to make a grenade out of paper, the outline of which would be quite simple? To prepare the grenade body, prepare a cardboard base in the shape of a cylinder. We proceed as follows. The height should be calculated so that it matches the height of the firecrackers. Then a figure is cut out of the cardboard, which is folded into a cylinder. The edges should overlap by one centimeter. Then they must be glued together. Triangular protrusions also stick together one to one. The first bottom should be further strengthened with a cardboard circle, planted on glue.

Preparing a charge for a paper grenade





To prepare a charge and a fuse, matches are taken and they are attached to a firecracker on an adhesive tape. In this case, the matches should touch each other's heads, and only one of them should be in contact with the part of the firecracker, which is subject to ignition.

Now you can proceed to the preparation of the trigger and checks. To do this, a strip is cut out of a plastic bottle, the width of which should be approximately five millimeters. One end of this strip is fastened in a loop-shaped stapler. It will come in handy when you pull the check. A brown stripe is also cut out of a matchbox and glued to the second end of a previously prepared strip of plastic. The same end should be wound to the firecracker so that the cardboard strip from the matchbox is in close contact with the first match.

The result of the preparation of the grenade





At the last stage of the preparation of our grenade, we return to its already prepared bottom. In the center of the bottom, you will need to make a hole in the shape of a rectangle to output a plastic loop. After that, glue is applied from the end of the firecracker near the check, the check is passed through a rectangular hole in the bottom of the grenade. The case is filled with peas, the second end of the firecracker is sealed. Then the grenade is closed with a second bottom with glue. Here is such a simple technology that shows how to make a grenade out of paper without any special conditions. However, this method is not unique.
