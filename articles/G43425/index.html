Pearl of China - Dalian, hotels of the city - a priceless setting of the resort

Recently, China has attracted more and more attention. This is facilitated not only by the ancient history of the country, but also by certain climatic conditions that contribute to improving health, various treatment methods that have been tested not even for years - millennia. It is no coincidence that many tourists flock here in the hope of improving their health.



One of the best resorts in China is considered to be Dalian. Hotels are amazing. You can choose a suitable hotel, which will provide for everything that a person may need on vacation. The rooms here are equipped with necessary amenities, many hotels (Dalian) have wireless internet. It is very convenient for travelers.



Those who come to China to improve their health will be interested in the fact that the Dalian spa provides a variety of treatments. A tourist can buy a ticket to a sanatorium and undergo a comprehensive examination. Traditional Chinese medicine is practiced here. Those who wish can undergo treatment according to European methods. Acupuncture is very popular. It is not necessary to go to a sanatorium to take such a course. Good specialists can be found in other places. You can come to Dalian, whose hotels will welcome any traveler with pleasure, and start acupuncture sessions at the nearest medical center.

Of course, in addition to treatment, the traveler will want to do something else, for example, get acquainted with the sights of the city of Dalian. Its hotels are adjacent to beautiful squares. The highlight of the city is Zhongshan Square. There are many financial and government institutions. In Dalian there are many interesting tea restaurants, beautiful flower shops.

Those who fall into the western part of the city will be interested to look at the largest square in Asia. It is called Xinhai Square. Just half a kilometer from it is the exhibition center, which is rightfully considered one of the main, most interesting sights of the city.

Guests of China will be interested to look at the most interesting exhibits presented at the Dalian Museum of Natural History. Hotels usually have attendants who will tell you what else interesting this particular guest can be seen in this city.

It is fascinating in Dalian that the city is completely different at different times of the day. In the afternoon, it is a vibrant business center. At night, various lights and bulbs are lit here, illuminating the streets and squares with enchanting colors. Jets of many fountains soar into the sky.

Drowning in greenery, sparkling with fantastic lights, delighting with its original architecture, Dalian is rightfully loved by tourists who seek to get here at least once more.
