Sights of the island of Taiwan: the capital of Taiwan - Taipei

Taiwan on a map (Republic of China) is a small island nation located near mainland China. It attracts tourists with its beautiful nature, wavy plains, streaming mountain rivers, mysterious architecture and the color of oriental culture. Consider the most famous sights of the state of Taiwan.





Capital of taiwan

Taipei - the largest city in the state, is officially considered the capital of the Republic of China . The wonderful climate and rich history make this place attractive for tourists.

Serious changes that have occurred in China in recent years in the field of culture have not affected the island. Today, Taipei is a unique combination of Japanese, Chinese and European architecture. Thanks to this, more and more tourists annually dream of visiting this country.

Taiwan Island Attractions

The capital of Taiwan is the place where every traveler must visit. The city meets its guests with a lot of attractions that demonstrate not only the history, but also the modern life of the island.

National Museum

The National Museum is world famous because it contains the richest collection of various works of Chinese art. Visitors will not be able to remain indifferent, contemplating the most magnificent products from bronze, jade and jasper, gorgeous paintings by the great Chinese masters, the amazing beauty of the fan and much more.

Chen Museum

Another rich collection of Chinese art is on display at Chen's small private museum. Here you can admire the oldest products from porcelain, ceramics and stone.

Ling Antai Farm

Here you can familiarize yourself with the history of Chinese architecture. This ancient building was built in 1783. A visit to this building is also of interest because tourists have a unique opportunity to find out how ordinary peasants lived in the old days.





National Theater

Many of the island's attractions help guests better understand the traditions of Chinese culture and the history of the state of Taiwan. The capital of Taiwan is Taipei, meanwhile still a young city. Therefore, even in such facilities as the national theater, there is everything necessary for conducting modern performances. Here you can go on tour with the world's best ballet companies, singers, as well as see performances of the Japanese theater or the Beijing Opera.

Entertainment Center World Yesterday

Having visited different cities of Taiwan, each tourist will have the opportunity to compare the greatness of classics and the charm of modern times. Of course, there are large shopping centers on the island. All guests of the state of Taiwan should definitely visit here. The capital of Taiwan - Taipei is proud of the modern entertainment complex World Yesterday. Its peculiarity is that during the game, children get acquainted with the traditions of Chinese culture, its history and mythology. A visit to such a center will be not only very fun, but also very informative.





Chiang Kai-shek Memorial Complex

Those who came to the island to get acquainted with its history should definitely visit here. Chiang Kai-shek is the first president of Taiwan. He did a lot of good by rebuilding the country after the war devastation. Around the complex is a beautiful park where you can relax, contemplating the grandeur of Taiwanese architecture and the splendor of nature.
