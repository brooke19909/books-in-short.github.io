Is material assistance taxed: legal regulation and laws

Workers are sometimes paid various types of benefits. But is material assistance taxed? The answer here will be in the affirmative. Providing their employees with a certain amount of financial support, employers should take into account that personal income tax can be withheld from this payment. But it is also important to know that there are types of financial assistance from which tax is not withheld. What is it? What conditions does the tax law introduce in the current year? This is what we will understand in the article.

Main issues

Is material assistance taxable? It depends, first of all, on two conditions. It is important for the employer to know the answers to the questions:

  • What category of citizens is this kind of help needed?
  • What amounts of financial support are tax deductible?

Material support from all other incomes of citizens is distinguished by the following characteristics:

  • It does not depend on the activities of the worker.
  • It is independent of the performance of the employing organization.
  • It is not affected by the cyclical nature of work.

In Russia, the grounds for receiving financial assistance can be divided into two groups - target and general.

Key Terms

Before you specifically determine whether material assistance is taxed, we give the definitions of the main concepts that appear in the article:

  • Material assistance is a cash payment to a citizen who is in dire need of such financial support. These funds are allocated by the employer to a specific employee. What could it be? Cash support from the union to working students. Financial assistance for a serious illness of an employee. Benefit in case of death of a family member of an employee. Material assistance here is also distinguished by another special characteristic: it is a lump sum payment.
  • Personal income tax. Definition - in the decoding of the abbreviation. These are taxes on personal income. Accordingly, financial assistance is a kind of income of a citizen. Financial compensation that is issued by organizations (including the employer) is the income from which it is necessary to pay personal income tax.

But there are some exceptions. Is material assistance taxed? Not if its amount does not exceed 4000 rubles. With that financial support that is more tangible than this value, personal income tax must be paid according to the law of the Russian Federation.

Who is issued?

Is material assistance taxed? We determined that yes, but not in all cases. For example, if this is a payment of less than 4000 rubles, then you do not have to pay tax.

But this is not the only exceptional condition. There are categories of citizens whose material support should not be subject to personal income tax. First of all, this:

  • Persons who have become victims of a terrorist takeover, attack, attack.
  • Citizens affected by natural disasters as a result of an accident.

In addition, financial support will be exempted from taxation in the following cases:

  • Payment of damage in case of certain harm to health.
  • Cases when a citizen must incur unexpected cash expenses. The reasons here are different - from the wedding and the birth of children to the funeral of close relatives.
  • Additional material support for large and low-income families.

It is important to determine that in such situations, the law does not force the employer to help his employee financially. This decision is up to the employer. And if he determines that financial assistance is needed, it is paid.

Registration of material support from the state

Is holiday material assistance taxed? Yes, if the value of this payment exceeds 4 thousand rubles.

Now we will focus on the execution of financial support not on behalf of the employer, but on behalf of the state. Here you also need to start the business by writing a statement. Only on its basis will the issue of providing the citizen with material state support begin to be dealt with. When preparing a document, you must pay attention to the following:

  • Unambiguously and clearly state the reason why you need to receive this type of financial support.
  • Attach documents that certify the fact that you need material assistance to the application. For example, certificates confirming that a situation really had a place to be.

With all the prepared documents, you should then come to one of these government agencies:

  • The department of social protection of the population of your locality.

Please note that in this case, financial assistance will be one-time, rather than periodic support. This applies to benefits from both the employer and the state.

Benefit Decision

At the workplace, a citizen passes his application to the employer. The latter gets acquainted with the contents of the document, the attached references. Based on this, he can decide on the appointment of financial assistance - issue an order to issue a certain amount of money.

If the application was submitted to the social protection authorities or the Pension Fund, you should wait for the decision of the state structure within 7 working days. At this time, a commission will be created, which will issue a verdict on whether it is necessary for this citizen to appoint financial support.

If the state body made a positive decision, the applicant shall be notified of this. He can then receive help from the state in the manner indicated by him in the application:

  • Withdraw this amount from your bank account (it must be indicated in the document).
  • Get money in cash at a savings bank.

Material assistance is a type of support for citizens on behalf of the employer or the state in special life circumstances.
