LCD "Olkhovsky Park" (Yekaterinburg): description, reviews

Yekaterinburg is a huge metropolis, the capital of the Urals and the center of industry. Currently, the city is being actively built up; excellent residential complexes appear in well-maintained areas of the city with excellent ecology. Residential complex "Olkhovskiy Park" is a bright representative of modern, meeting all the requirements of the residents of the projects. It is on it that we will focus on this material.





about the project

Residential complex "Olkhovskiy Park" (Yekaterinburg) is a project of a comfort-class residential neighborhood built in the very center of the city along the Olkhovka River. The project provides for the construction of five buildings with variable number of storeys. The first floors of the houses will be reserved for commercial infrastructure: shops, bank branches, cafes and much more. Spacious parking is provided for car owners.

Location

Location - perhaps the main advantage of the LCD Olkhovsky Park. The residential complex is located almost in the very center of the city, on the embankment of the Olkhovka River. On the one hand, residents of the complex have access to the infrastructure of the Zarechny district, calm and restless, on the other hand, proximity to the city center. Indeed, all the attractions, the largest parks and squares are within walking distance from new buildings.





Thanks to the good location, residents of the LCD Olkhovskiy Park will be able to enjoy panoramic views of the city center, all its attractions, and during the festivities directly from the windows of the apartments enjoy fireworks and fireworks.

Apartments, layouts

A variety of planning decisions is what all customers have noted. The developer really tried to take into account all the wishes of modern interest holders. The choice of spacious apartments from 1 to 3 rooms. Rational planning allows you to use every square meter of area and provide comfort even in a small "odnushka". Apartments with a spacious kitchen-living room and isolated bedrooms are very popular - an excellent solution for both newlyweds and large friendly families.

Finish

All apartments in the residential complex "Olkhovsky Park" are for rent with a turnkey finish. Thus, immediately after the delivery of the house, happy owners can move into their cozy nest. Finishing is represented by a laminate of the highest class of strength on the floor, washable wallpaper on the walls, paint on the ceiling, entrance and interior doors, two-chamber double-glazed windows, European-made plumbing, electrical wiring. In addition, satellite TV and high-speed internet are provided in each quarter.

Infrastructure

The ground floors of the houses are occupied by a store, bank branches, cafes, and beauty salons. Within walking distance from new buildings is SK "Dynamo", a clinic, kindergartens and comprehensive schools. With a calm step, you can stroll to the cultural and historical center of the city, go down to the Olkhovka River and visit the well-maintained park.





Closed courtyards, isolated from cars, provide the highest level of security. In the courtyards there are modern playgrounds and sports grounds, walking areas and leisure sectors for residents of different ages.

Terms of purchase

Apartments in the residential complex "Olkhovsky Park" are sold as part of the equity program. Accordingly, the transaction eliminates the possibility of double resale and guarantees a full refund in case of failure to fulfill contractual obligations by the developer. Mortgages are possible.
