Hotel Mulia Resort Nusa Dua Bali 5 * (Indonesia): reviews

Bali is a place that many travelers associate with a real paradise. Miles of white beaches, tropical jungle, exotic culture and, of course, high-class service - what could be better for a vacation? This island is becoming a gathering place for tourists from all over the world.

Of course, the most popular resorts are located on the coast of Bali. And experienced travelers often choose the small but fabulously beautiful town of Nusa Dua, staying at the Mulia Resort Nusa Dua Bali. According to reviews, this is a luxurious hotel complex that gives guests a lot of positive emotions and pleasant memories.

But there is a flip side to the coin: living in the territory of the Mulia Resort Nusa Dua Bali 5 * costs not so little. That is why tourists are interested in any information about the hotel. What actually is this place and how do former guests speak about it? What type of vacation is the hotel suitable for? Where is the beach located? Answers to these questions will be useful to every traveler.

Location Description

It's no secret that the location is an extremely important characteristic of a hotel, because every traveler wants to have everything at hand. Mulia Resort Nusa Dua Bali is located on one of the most picturesque shores of the island of Bali, in the luxurious resort of Nusa Dua. The city center with all its attractions, restaurants and shops is a 5-minute drive away.

Ngurah Rai International Airport is located just 16 km from the Mulia Resort Nusa Dua Bali. The tours offered by the agencies already include a convenient transfer to the hotel, although you can use public transport or a taxi. In any case, the road will take no more than 30 minutes.

Room Categories

What do the rooms at the Mulia Resort Nusa Dua Bali look like? The photo gallery on the official website of the hotel, as well as the pictures left by the guests themselves, indicate that the rooms are luxurious. By the way, at your service rooms of the following categories:

  • Mulia Grander is a spacious room of 57 square meters located in secluded bungalows. It offers a spacious room with an elegant design, from which you can go directly to your own large terrace - the sea view is amazing. By the way, this is where newlyweds prefer to stay.
  • The Royal Suite, whose area is 83 square meters, is divided into a bedroom and a living room. The terrace is furnished with the same luxury - elegant wicker furniture, a table, sofas with soft pillows, which are so nice to relax on while enjoying views of the garden and the beach.
  • Premier Suite - large rooms, consisting of several bedrooms, living room, kitchen (area is 140 sq. M). Here, tourists can enjoy the incredible design and wonders of modern technology.
  • The Ducal Suite is a room (140 sq. M.) Located on the top floor of the main building. Large windows, a huge terrace, a dome-shaped ceiling, luxurious furniture and sophisticated design - all this will please even the most fastidious tourist.

Hotel Mulia Resort Nusa Dua Bali: photo and description of living rooms





To begin with, it is worth saying that all the rooms on the hotel territory are spacious, and the rooms are decorated in different ways - you simply cannot find rooms with the same design. The furniture here is expensive and new - at your service are large beds, wardrobes, sofas, tables, soft chairs, in a word, everything that you may need during your vacation.

Nobody complains about the technology. The rooms have televisions with large screens (you can watch satellite channels), telephones, air conditioners, portable and ceiling fans. You can use the safe. There is a minibar with a cooling system, although only drinking water is delivered free of charge (brought to the room twice a day).

The bathroom is spacious and clean, the plumbing is new. Tourists are provided with a hairdryer, terry bathrobes and slippers, soft towels, hygiene and beauty products. Maids clean here every day, and they do their job well.

Catering for tourists





Every traveler is interested in the issue of nutrition, because tasteless or poor-quality food can ruin all the impressions of the rest. Tourists who have already managed to visit the Mulia Resort Nusa Dua Bali, say that in the local restaurant you can always have a tasty meal. When paying for accommodation, you yourself can choose a convenient scheme and order, for example, only breakfast or full three meals a day.

The main restaurant stands out for its luxurious design. The buffet always serves dishes of European, Mediterranean, Asian, Japanese and Chinese cuisine. You can enjoy fresh exotic fruits and fresh vegetables in plenty. There is a separate table with a special menu for children, and vegetarians can always choose something tasty and healthy. Lunches and dinners are more varied - often guests are served meat and fish cooked on the grill, or even roast a whole pig on a spit.

There are several bars at the hotel, where visitors are offered a huge selection of cocktails, wines and stronger drinks. It is worth considering that alcohol is paid separately. By the way, surprisingly delicious coffee is prepared in local cafes.

Where is the beach located?





As already mentioned, the courtyard of the Mulia Resort Nusa Dua Bali overlooks the sea. Tourists can relax by the picturesque bay, splashing in warm water. The beach is completely covered with soft white sand. According to reviews, during the day the hotel employees constantly clean the beach - here it is really very clean and safe.

Comfortable sun loungers with mattresses and umbrellas are arranged in even rows on the beach. Here you can easily get a wide clean towel. There are also spacious showers, where at any time you can rinse off with sea salt.

The guests also respond well to the work of the beach bar. Polite employees are always ready to offer a chilled drink or an exotic cocktail. By the way, delicious home-made ice cream is also served here.

Water recreation and other entertainment

Tourists who have settled in the hotel are provided with all kinds of entertainment. You can easily rent a boat or pedal boat at the hotel. Many travelers simply love to snorkel, because the waters here are clean and clear - nothing prevents you from enjoying the underwater scenery.

It offers travelers - water skiing, boats, bananas and even romantic boat trips. And on the beach itself is equipped with a convenient volleyball court, covered with special sand.

Description of additional service





Of course, in such a luxurious hotel you can count on additional services. For example, laundry and dry cleaning are available for guests - the cost is quite affordable. Guests can use the parking lot, and on the territory of the hotel rent a suitable transport without any problems. By the way, employees will call a taxi upon request or arrange a limousine ride.

The hotel often becomes the venue for wedding ceremonies. Professionals are involved in the organization, there is even a photographer and florist here.

This place is also suitable for business meetings or business vacations - a large conference room, the necessary equipment for performances, and various office equipment are at the service of travelers. Internet is available in the territory, but access to it is paid separately.

Services for families with children

The hotel complex Mulia Resort Nusa Dua Bali is suitable for families. There is something to do not only for adult tourists, but also for their children. A spacious pool is equipped for the children, the water in which always warms up well. Your child can also have fun on the playground, which is surrounded by a garden - there are swings, slides, a sandbox and other attractions that children like.

For a small fee, the child can be left with the nanny. The hotel has a mini club with a huge game room, but its visit is not included in the price - the service is paid separately.

Leisure of residents: what to do in your free time?





According to the guests, the hotel territory can be inspected for weeks, admiring the luxury and sophisticated design. But you will have the opportunity to spend time more actively. For example, guests can use a large communal pool around which there is a solarium terrace with sun loungers and parasols. By the way, animators often work here, organizing various entertainment events. There are several private small pools that are suitable for tourists who want to spend time in silence.

If you wish, you can sign up for beauty and wellness treatments at the local spa, which, by the way, is popular because guests from other hotels often come here. Many guests recommend using the services of massage therapists - real professionals work here. You can relax in the jacuzzi. There is a gym where you can find not only sports equipment, but also experienced instructors.

Throughout the day, animators arrange various entertainment events for guests. And in the evenings there is a disco, where many tourists have a great time, enjoying music and dancing.

Reviews and recommendations of former guests





What do former guests say about staying at the Mulia Resort Nusa Dua Bali? Booking, like other travel sites, judging by the reviews, confirms that almost all travelers are satisfied with their stay here.

Of course, the buildings and the entire hotel area are very beautiful - for some tourists it even seems pompous. Nevertheless, everything here is admirable, ranging from successful landscape solutions, ending with picturesque sea views and a variety of architecture. The rooms are spacious and clean (upon request, maids look into the rooms several times a day). There are no problems with fresh towels, hygiene products and drinking water - everything is delivered daily.

Food and animation are also at their best - the restaurant always has a huge selection of dishes, and they only serve guests fresh and tasty food. At the same time, the animators are actively working to diversify the leisure of guests, to organize interesting entertainments for them. By the way, all the staff at the hotel are nice, pleasant in communication, hospitable - it is always pleasant to communicate with them.

Tourists unanimously recommend the hotel complex Mulia Resort Nusa Dua Bali. Indonesia, in particular the island of Bali, is a suitable place for an ideal vacation that will definitely be remembered for a lifetime.
