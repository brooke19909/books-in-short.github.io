Hotel in Pushkin, Leningrad Region. Hotels and mini-hotels of Pushkin

The city of Pushkin attracts tourists with a variety of historical sights, which have become an integral symbol of our country. Booking any hotel in the city will be useful for people who do not want to lose a single minute walking along Tsarskoye Selo. He entered the UNESCO list. The city of Pushkin is considered to be a scientific, educational, and military-industrial center.



Its hotels are located near interesting places. Among the main attractions of the complex, the Catherine Palace is usually distinguished , where tourists want to get in order to see the restored amber room stolen in the war and the architecture of the halls.

In the Catherine Park there is an opportunity to walk along the many alleys, to see the Turkish Bath, the Hermitage pavilion, the grotto, from the Granite Terrace - a panorama of the park. In the halls of the Alexander Palace there are various household items of the tsar dynasty. In Alexander Park there is a Chinese bridge made of pink granite, a Chinese village, the ruins of the Chinese Theater and the Dragon Bridge. Another interesting attraction is the Lyceum Memorial Museum. Near the palace and Catherine's Park there is a registry office.

There are few hotels in Pushkin, therefore, it is worth worrying in advance about the place of residence, planning a visit to this city.
