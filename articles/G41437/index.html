Sugar Palm Place 2 *: hotel description and reviews of tourists

Holidays in Pattaya (Thailand) are in demand by a large number of tourists, especially in the autumn-winter period, due to the clear advantages over other travel options.

Holidays in exotic Thailand give everything: an abundance of fruits, a warm sea of ‚Äã‚Äãemerald color, sandy beaches, lush flora and fauna, a variety of impressions and types of entertainment. Pattaya is distinguished by moderation of prices for accommodation and a wealth of choice of leisure options, such as at the Sugar Palm Place Hotel (Pattaya).

Hotel location

A small but comfortable hotel, which is ten minutes from Dogtan Beach (less than one kilometer), is known for its convenient location. It is not far from it to get to the famous Jomtien Beach, and to the center of Pattaya. Nearby is the water park of the Park Pattaya hotel, a night market, a lot of shops, cafes and shops.

The third coastline, on which Sugar Palm Place 2 * is located, is not a problem for those who need an intense vacation in Pattaya, who are interested in diving into the fun and variety of Thai relaxation. However, according to vacationers, it is perfect for families with children. The availability of the water park will only enhance the children's experience of relaxation, as evidenced by the reviews.

Sugar Palm Place 2 *: room description

In this economical and chamber hotel there are only forty-eight rooms of the Standard and Superior categories, each with a view of the park or the street. Standard rooms have an area of ‚Äã‚Äãthirty-five square meters.



The room has a wide double bed, a wardrobe, a trilage, a refrigerator with a replenished minibar, air conditioning, a TV with Russian broadcasting. All rooms have a balcony.

The rooms of the Sugar Palm Place 2 * Superior category hotel have a seating area, they also have a view of the garden or a busy street. They are more spacious, their area is more than fifty square meters. All rooms have a balcony, amenities. In the room: double bed, folding sofa, trilage, fridge with minibar (paid top-up), air conditioning. Rooms include everything you need for a comfortable stay for hotel guests.





Services

The hotel rooms are cleaned daily. Linen is changed once every three days. For vacationers at the Sugar Palm Place 2 * hotel It offers the use of wireless Internet (Wi-Fi zone), free safe deposit box at the reception (reception). On request, household appliances are provided: iron, ironing board, hairdryer.

At the hotel, upon check-in, a deposit of one thousand baht (national Thai currency) is charged, which can be purchased at the exchange office across the street from the hotel. The hotel has a Russian-speaking staff. Upon request, services are provided for an additional payment (transfer, laundry, international communication, doctor's services).

Nutrition

Southeast Asia attracts not only with its natural exoticism, Thai cuisine is no less interesting and peculiar . The abundance of spices enhances the taste experience of dishes. In addition, spices play a specific role in disinfection.





At Sugar Palm Place 2 * breakfast is not included in the price, as is usual in other hotels. But there is no urgent need for this. The abundance of restaurants and cafes near the hotel helps to solve the problem of satisfying the appetite.

The hotel also took care of those who prefer to order meals in the room. The restaurant of the Sugar Palm Place 2 * hotel can offer a wide variety of European, Thai and Russian dishes, with room service.

You should definitely try Thai fruits, freshly squeezed juices. Exotic and world-famous variants of insect dishes are decided not to be tasted by all travelers. Seafood and grilled vegetables are traditional Thai food.

Entertainment

According to tourists, remoteness from the coast does not create problems, since the beach in Pattaya is usually not very actively used, preferring swimming in the sea on the islands. Shipping to the islands is around 100 baht.





More attracted by tourists vacationing in Pattaya, night clubs, shows, hookah and gaming establishments. A wide selection of excursion programs allows you to see all the exotic of Asia. A colorful transvestite show and panoramic historical plots, feeding crocodiles and elephant trekking, massages and spas will leave no hesitation for choosing when planning further trips in the autumn-winter period.

On the territory of the hotel Sugar Palm Place 2 * (Pattaya) for children there is a trampoline, the proximity of the water park creates excellent conditions for the possibility of a family holiday with children.

Excursions to the botanical garden, acquaintance with the species of tropical butterflies, a trip to the tiger zoo, a walk along the Kwai River will be interesting .

Reviews of tourists about holidays in Pattaya

Holidays in Pattaya are remarkable for their democratic nature, as the regulars of this vacation option note. It does not require compliance with the dress code, the minimum luggage - bathing accessories, shorts and T-shirts - will be quite enough for a trip to the hospitable Tai.

Hotels of level 2-3 * are quite suitable for a short stay, since the main rest takes place on the seashore or in nightclubs and sightseeing tours. No exception and hotel Sugar Palm Place. Reviews tourists about him they say that this small hotel corresponds to the level of service of a strong three-star hotel or more, the hotel rating is more than four points out of five possible.

It should be noted that the certification system that tourists are guided by when choosing a hotel is of European origin. And in Southeast Asia, the level of "stars" is assigned by the operator, so it may differ in the interpretations of different suppliers of leisure services.
