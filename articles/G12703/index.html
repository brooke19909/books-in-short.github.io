Hotel "Rus", Oryol: description and reviews of tourists

The history of Oryol, one of the most beautiful cities in Russia, dates back to 1566, when, by decree of Tsar Ivan IV, it was founded to protect the borders of the country from raids by the Tatars. Since then, the city has gone the way of rapid development and serious trials, and by the middle of the 18th century it turned into one of the largest Russian centers of the grain trade. In the XX century, the Eagle was awarded the title of "city of the first salute" and "city of military glory." Today, the regional administrative center attracts visitors with its unique attractions. Thanks to its countless temples, monuments, green squares, the Eagle is famous among tourists as a pleasant place to relax.





Many hotels of various categories have been built to accommodate guests in the city. Many travelers who visited the regional center left their rather mixed reviews about the Rus Hotel (Oryol).

All equipment, although old, but in working condition, guests say. Wi-Fi in the hotel catches exclusively in the lobby, tourists are sharing, you can use the Internet in a cafe on the ground floor.

The authors of posts on social networks call the hotel a vivid representative of the remnants of the Soviet past. The disadvantages of the hotel, the guests believe that the building is quite old, the repair here was made, in their opinion, during the Soviet era.
