What is the difference between 2 din radio and analogs of type 1 din?

The first distinguishing feature between these two counterparts is the visual difference in size. Dimensions vary in height. If the width and length of both versions are 17.8 and 18 centimeters, respectively, then the height of the Chinese 2 DIN radio tape recorders is 10 cm. For category 1 DIN, this figure is half as much.

It is worth noting that the automobile systems in question can be installed in an additional niche provided by manufacturers of some brands of cars. This is due to the fact that the standard socket is not always suitable in size.

Another difference between the first and second modifications is the screen size. Version 1 DIN is equipped with a display from two to seven inches. Chinese 2 DIN radio tape recorders have a screen of at least 6-7 inches with adjustable tilt angle and additional options.

Installation

To begin with, we dismantle the old car radio, remove the standard frame, providing the subsequent possibility of mounting a new model. If you wish, you can use improvised means, for example, a profile for the installation of drywall sheets. They will serve as a new framework.

Before you start connecting an advanced Chinese radio, you must connect and check the corresponding connector and antenna, as well as unscrew the transportation fasteners from the DVD drive (at the top of the device), otherwise the disks will not go to the working part of the audio system.

Then the entire installation is installed directly in the console. As a rule, the fixation is quite rigid due to the fastening at three points. Work on replacing the 2 DIN car radio is impossible without an adapter. You can buy a ready-made version or make this device yourself.

In the latter case, it is necessary to make control measurements, cut off the cross-beam, fill it with thermal adhesive solution, sand and paint.





Final work

Repair of the Chinese 2 DIN radio is best entrusted to professionals. But its do-it-yourself installation ends with the following operations:

  • The panel is adjusted neatly in accordance with the dimensions of the installed product. It may be an old numbering base.
  • If desired and necessary, you can cut a niche for parking sensors.
  • Its installation consists in connecting the working parts, releasing the monitor from the case, soldering the screen with thermal glue from the back of the adapter.
  • Then the frame is installed in a regular place. The work can be considered finished, it remains to configure a new audio system.
