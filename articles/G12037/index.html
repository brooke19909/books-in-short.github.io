Beaches in Lazarevsky: beauty and comfort

The beaches in Lazarevsky are mostly pebble, they consist of flat pebbles polished by water. The length of the beach strip is about 7 kilometers. Its width ranges from 20 to 50 meters.





How to get there

It is very convenient to go to the beaches, since there is no sharp descent to the sea in the highlands, the area near the coastal strip is shallow, so to get to the sea you just need to walk for a few minutes along the asphalt paths, in the shade of greenery. Throughout the beaches in Lazarevsky, and there are a lot of them, there are plank coverings on which it is convenient to walk barefoot, because pebbles are very hot from the sun at the height of the season.

Cleanliness and review

The beaches of the Lazarevsky district are the cleanest on the entire Black Sea coast of Russia. They do not meet pits, elevations or a sharp descent into the water. Also, there are no breakwaters throughout the Lazarevsky region, which is certainly good, since only the Black Sea is open to your eyes, not clouded by these large, heavy and unsympathetic structures. Relaxing on the beaches of the Lazarevsky district is a pleasure!





Service

The infrastructure of the resort village of Lazarevskoye is well developed. For your comfort and convenience, beach equipment rental centers are offered - sunbeds, beach umbrellas, jet skis, catamarans, jet skis, and jet skis. You can go sea fishing, ride a "banana" and "cheesecakes", make a unique flight over the sea surface by parachute or in a "flying satchel". Also at your service are sightseeing helicopter rides or horseback riding along the coast, various attractions, boat trips on a yacht.

Cleanliness and order on the beaches in Lazarevsky are supported by daily cleaning, carried out by specialized contractors several times during the day. This is the key to your health and guarantee the purity of sea water. This is precisely the reason why scuba diving enthusiasts come to this resort village. All conditions for them are created here.

The beaches in Lazarevsky are not only free municipal. There are also clean wild beaches , remote from the bustle of people and roads. In addition, there are several nudist beaches where lovers of such a holiday can calmly sunbathe naked, not shy and not afraid of anything. In addition, there are beaches belonging to the sanatoriums, some of them can be accessed not only by the rate, but also by paying an entrance, the cost of which is acceptable.





In the village of Lazarevskoye, it is difficult to find a sandy beach, since the seabed is mostly pebble, and there is little sand there. But there are beaches on which, along with pebbles, there is a considerable amount of sand. This is a kind of sandy islets. These include: the beach of the sanatorium "Lazarevskoye", the beach of the sanatorium "Odyssey" (the best, most well-maintained and comfortable piece of the coast in this resort village).

Since the coastline is wide, the beaches in Lazarevskoy have many different cafes, bars, pizzerias, restaurants, which have the most varied menus, a wide selection of drinks, ice cream and snacks. You can choose a place according to your financial situation. After all, there is nothing better than having breakfast or dinner in a restaurant in the fresh air, admiring the sea and feeling the blow of a salty breeze. It's fine! Near the beaches there are many parks where you can hide from the sun in the shade of exotic green alleys, sit on cozy benches, stand under the spray of fountains, and breathe in useful sea air!

The beaches in Lazarevsky are very convenient, comfortable, beautiful, so if you have not yet decided where to go, the answer is one: "Choose Lazarevsky!" You will not regret your choice!
