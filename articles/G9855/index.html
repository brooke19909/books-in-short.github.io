Hotel Asrin Beach (Turkey). Description and feedback

The small economical hotel Asrin Beach (Turkey) is located in a quiet area away from the bustle of the city. It is an ideal place for a relaxing holiday. In a modernly designed hotel occupies a small territory, surrounded by lush green lush exotic vegetation.





Description

The Asrin Beach Hotel (Turkey), built in 1999, was renovated in 2004. The windows of the rooms of the six-story building overlook the sea. The area occupied by the complex is 7600 m 2 . The hotel is 17 km from the tourist resort of Alanya in the Incekum area.

Rooms

Asrin Beach Hotel (Turkey) is ready to offer 188 comfortable apartments of standard and family types to accommodate tourists. Room size averages 23 m 2 . All of them are equipped with modern comfortable furniture, a balcony, a minibar, individual air conditioning, a bathroom, a safe, television with satellite channels, a toilet, a telephone, a hairdryer. Cleaning is done every day, linen is changed twice a week. The hotel has specially equipped rooms for the disabled, designed to accommodate three adults.

For those tourists who are attracted by Turkey with its amazing color from year to year, the Asrin Beach Hotel has created all the conditions for a comfortable and carefree holiday. Nutrition

Asrin Beach Hotel (Turkey) operates on an all-inclusive basis. In addition, two restaurants and three bars are open daily for holidaymakers. In these establishments in a quiet relaxed atmosphere you can enjoy excellent dishes of traditional or European cuisine. The bars offer a variety of non-alcoholic and alcoholic soft drinks.

Entertainment

For tourists there is a sauna, gym, massage room, hammam, jacuzzi. Asrin Beach guests can play table tennis, beach volleyball, darts, billiards. A fun animation program is held daily. On the beach, tourists can enjoy various water sports. For kids, a separate pool with a water slide and a children's playground are provided.





Beach

It is across the road 140 meters from the main building. The beach is clean, sandy, has a gentle slope into the water. On the shore there are umbrellas, mattresses, sun loungers, towels, as well as a bar with various drinks.
