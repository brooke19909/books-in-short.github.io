Holidays in the city of Protaras

Protaras is a famous city located in Cyprus. Many tourists decide to spend their vacation in this place. Protaras is literally an hour's drive from Larnaca International Airport, which is located in the southeastern part of Cyprus. This is a fairly compact, but very picturesque resort town, delighting tourists with a beautiful bay with crystal clear water, a small island located near the coast and cozy fine sandy beaches. And, of course, one cannot fail to note the entertainment offered to the guests of this city. Restaurants, bars, cafes, amazing exotic food, delicacies, nightlife - all this creates an amazing aura for visitors. No wonder these places were marked in 2005 with the Blue Flag of the European Union. But for a good rest there are few picturesque landscapes pleasing to the eye. Service and quality of service are also important, and, of course, the place of temporary residence. If you want to come to this city, then you should pay attention to the hotel Odessa Hotel 4 *.
