Hotel-park "Vasilievsky" (Samara). Park-hotel "Vasilievsky": prices, reviews

Hotel-Park "Vasilyevsky" is an elite, suburban complex that will delight every visitor with a cozy atmosphere and cordiality of staff. In fact, this is a comfortable hotel, around which there is a huge park. A wonderful recreation area is organized here, where you can spend holidays and corporate parties, relax with your family, and also stop during a business trip.



Where is

It will be especially useful for visitors to find out where the Vasilievsky Park Hotel is. Bezenchuksky district of the Samara region, northwest of the village of Vladimirovka. This countryside complex offers an excellent getaway away from the busy city streets. If you are coming from Samara, then you need to turn left on the P226 highway on the ring, before entering Chapaevsk. This is the road to Volgograd. Now you need to move directly to Volgograd to the village of Bezenchuk. You need to get to Novo-Sadovaya street and go directly to the Vasilievsky Hotel-Park. If you travel by rail, then you will have 20 minutes to drive from Bezenchuk railway station. It will be convenient to take a taxi.

Infrastructure

The first thing that is important for tourists is the hotel. Park-Hotel Vasilievsky offers its guests accommodation in log houses - from standard to VIP category with a private pool. The choice of rooms for accommodation is quite large. Guests are offered 12 rooms of the category "comfort". On weekdays, the cost of staying in it is 2800 rubles. per day, on weekends - 5200. There are 4 rooms of the category "comfort" for people with disabilities.

In the presence of 20 rooms of the category "standard". They are the most economical, 2500/4500 per day. Ten junior suites at a price of 5200/8900 and, finally, 4 suites - 10400/18000 rubles. Prices are not the lowest, but the level of service is fully consistent with the stated prices. The Vasilievsky camp site (park hotel) is a vacation spot for VIP-class representatives, so you can always count on the decent behavior of other guests.



Entertainment

In addition to rooms for accommodation, there are 3 sports fields with professional coverage. You can arrange competitions in football, basketball, volleyball, tennis. Usually, most guests join such events. The restaurant complex will also not leave you indifferent. This is a huge banquet room for 150 people, a wine cellar for 30 seats, a hookah zone for 10 people. Here you can taste exquisite wines, complementing them with a wonderful day.

Separately, it is worth noting the picnic area. Here you can gather as a whole company and have fun breathing in delicious aromas. The hotel staff will take all the trouble to kindle and maintain coal, for a fee they can fulfill the duties of a barbecue.

Number of rooms

Today we are talking about the Park-Hotel Vasilievsky, Samara Region. Let's take a closer look at what the declared numbers are.

  • The category "standard" is a one-room cozy room, modernly and tastefully furnished. There is one double bed, plasma TV, minibar, hairdryer and air conditioning. The price includes breakfast according to the "buffet" system, parking services, the provision of a safe for valuables.
  • The comfort category is also a single room with a double bed. The room has a small kitchen area with a sink, you can make yourself tea, there is a microwave, refrigerator, plasma panel and minibar. Breakfast is also included, and rooms for people with disabilities have handrails.
  • The Junior Suite category is a comfortably furnished living room and two separate bedrooms. The room has a plasma panel and the rest is a set of household appliances and furniture. The price includes breakfast.
  • The category "luxury" is a two-story log house. The first floor is a cozy living room, kitchen and meeting room. On the second floor are three modern bedrooms. The house has two bathrooms. A bathrobe and slippers are provided, as well as tea and coffee making facilities.

The entire hotel is non-smoking, only specially equipped places can be used for this. Hotel Park-Hotel Vasilievsky (Samara) gives you the opportunity to have a great rest from the bustle of the world. All staff will be happy to serve and fulfill any request. Territory

We have not talked about nature at all. No wonder this place is called the park. The territory is very well-groomed, beautiful paths are laid, between which lawns are broken, and trees form wonderful oases of coolness. Everywhere is very clean and beautiful. There are alleys of mighty oaks, islets of birches and slender rows of fluffy firs. Such a strict pretentiousness of park alleys is a little broken by a stunning lake, which children love. There is not much entertainment here, you can sunbathe on sun loungers or splash around in the water. There is a catamaran rental service, so you can ride with the whole family. It is better to leave the lake before evening, because when the sun leaves, mosquitoes attack. There are not too many, but unpleasant.



Children's entertainment

We have already considered what adult tourists can do, and what can you think of for kids? And the Park-Hotel Vasilievsky (Samara) took care of this. The reviews of vacationers confirm that the children most of all did not want to leave home. This hotel is called the perfect place for families with kids. There are several outdoor playgrounds with a special, safe cover and trampolines. The hotel has a closed children's room where you can climb the maze, play in the dry pool with balls, draw or watch cartoons.

In order to combine the leisure of adults and children, special slot machines work. You can fight in air hockey, racing or shooting games. In the fresh air, dads with children ride bicycles or arrange kart rides. Shared love is, of course, a pool. If you do not want to go to the lake, you can swim right here. For the guys, their own paddling pool is equipped, but the big one is much more interesting, with all kinds of lights, geysers, fountains. For adults, there are 3 types of baths and a spa center. This complex works up to 22 hours, so the second half of the day is perfect for water procedures.
