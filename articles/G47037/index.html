Krasnodar Territory, all-inclusive hotels. The best hotels of the Krasnodar Territory

Those who plan to spend a vacation on the sea coast, improve their health and gain vitality are well suited for holidays in the Krasnodar Territory. Hotels here have a variety of price categories and are ready to accept tourists of any income. This is one of the richest and most beautiful Russian regions. A feature of relaxation in the resorts of the region is not only a great opportunity to bask in the sun somewhere in Sochi or Yeysk, splashing around in the sea water, but also get acquainted with many historical monuments.

Anapa, Tuapse, Gelendzhik ... These resorts offer many kilometers of beaches, interesting places for hiking, exciting diving and the pristine beauty of the surrounding nature. Rest here is combined with wellness and medical procedures, which use water from mineral springs and, of course, healing mud, for which many people come to the Krasnodar Territory.

All Inclusive Hotels

The all inclusive concept at our resorts is a relatively young concept. The owners of the domestic hotel business have adopted it from their foreign colleagues. However, Russians are well aware of this service, so they appreciate all-inclusive hotels when they come to Krasnodar Krai. The coastal hotels of the region work mainly on this concept.

Tourists are attracted by the fact that the whole range of services offered by the hotel can be obtained for a single, pre-set price. This means that residents can spend the entire vacation, paying for it at the very beginning, in a travel company, and no longer spend almost nothing during their entire vacation on room, food and entertainment.

This option, according to many, contributes to cost savings and makes it possible to spend a good time by the sea for a relatively small fee. True, at the same time, for those who come to Krasnodar Krai, all-inclusive hotels provide only standard services, as elsewhere in the world, offering additional entertainment for a fee. But many all inclusive package is quite happy.

Upscale Hotels





Like many resorts, in a region such as the Krasnodar Territory, all-inclusive hotels comprise the lion's share of the housing stock. Hotels working on this concept have their own "starryness", the category of which plays a decisive role. The higher the status of a particular institution, the more services are meant by the concept of all inclusive.

The best hotels in the Krasnodar Territory working under such a program provide free massage services, secure parking, an unlimited number of local drinks in bars, use of the pool, etc. Most of these hotels at their own expense arrange transfers from the airport or railway station. But this whole package is available if tourists choose expensive ones - the best hotels in the Krasnodar Territory, such as Deauville and Almira in Anapa, Prometheus Club in Lazarevsky (Sochi) and many others.

Five star apartments

In the region there are a lot of hotels operating under the all-inclusive program - from a low level to luxury. Each tourist who wants to feel all the charms of a sea holiday with a full package of paid services, chooses where to stay, depending on the vacation budget.



Hotels and hotels of the Krasnodar Territory from the category of "5 stars" are usually large hotel complexes. They offer a large selection of basic and additional amenities and services. The staff in many of them is specially trained for maximum helpfulness and restraint.

Often five-star hotels on the coast of the Krasnodar Territory have several bars and restaurants, shops and even night clubs in their infrastructure. The arrangement on the territory of SPA centers is widespread. Mandatory attribute are pools.

Room Requirements

Apartments in the best hotels in the region necessarily have spacious (at least twenty-five square meters) rooms, sometimes a bedroom and a living room, depending on the degree of comfort. The rooms should have a bath and shower, toilet (sometimes even two), bidet. The closet must have bathrobes and slippers.
A berth, as a rule, is represented by a double bed of the double version or two single beds. Change of linen in five-star hotels must occur every day.

In the Krasnodar region there are hotels of such well-known chains as Kempinski Grand (Gelendzhik), Pullman Sochi Center and Radisson Blu Paradise in Sochi, as well as Grand Hotel Valentina in Anapa.



Top best hotels

Many of them are located in Sochi, where the Olympics have recently been held. The rating of hotels is based on the reviews of vacationers and taking into account the views of expert professionals in the tourism sector. Since Sochi is a resort city, a great advantage for hotels is their proximity to the sea, the cleanliness of the nearest beach, and the presence of outdoor pools on the territory.

One of the best is Hyatt Regency Sochi, located in the central area, within walking distance of many local attractions and establishments. The hotel has a rich infrastructure, there are restaurants, an outdoor pool and one hundred ninety-eight rooms of various categories.

Another hotel - Pullman Sochi Center - offers a wellness center located on the sixteenth floor, Fit & Spa Lounge, sauna, hammam, massage services and relaxation therapy. Inns

There are especially many of them in Adler. Almost all mini-hotels of the Krasnodar Territory are perfect for a relaxing and leisurely vacation. They are mainly located within walking distance from the sea, often even on the first line. Such hotels are distinguished by a special atmosphere of home comfort. A small number of their rooms limits the number of guests, so pre-booking for tourists is often very important.

For example, Adler mini-hotels usually have a maximum of twenty rooms, have a small kitchen and a reception desk. Many of them are the Kristina, Irena, Bliss House, Camilla, etc. - are within walking distance from the main entertainment facilities of the resort. Residents can get to the aquarium and water park in a matter of minutes. Around such mini-hotels there are always many small shops, cafes and restaurants.

Prices

Finally





On the coast of the Krasnodar Territory, many hotels have been built with sandy, pebble or artificial beaches with aerosolaria, showers, rental boats and catamarans. Arriving at the resorts of this paradise, many, judging by the reviews, get the most out of their holidays, enjoying the beautiful natural corners of the Black Sea coast.
