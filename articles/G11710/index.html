Photosession in the field: what ideas can be implemented?

Over the past few years, a photo shoot in the field has gained great popularity. It can take place in summer or autumn. But, according to professional photographers, there is nothing strange about this. The field is a great place with which you can discover any storyline. Everything else, there are no people there. Accordingly, the model will not be disturbed by the views and opinions of passers-by cast on it. Due to this, she will be able to fully immerse herself in a certain image, completely liberate herself.

Some important nuances





At first glance, a photo shoot in the field may not be successful. However, this deserted place has all the necessary attributes in order to be able to create landscape and portrait photography. The main thing is to choose the right plot, make a choice in favor of the optimal angle and start creating. When choosing a place from which to shoot, you should make sure that nothing interferes with capturing the image of the model.

In order for the photo shoot in the field to be perfect, you should use a wide-angle lens, the focal length of which does not exceed 28 mm. Long-focus glasses with a distance of 100 mm are also allowed. Using them, you can squeeze the perspective, reduce the gaps between the objects (spikelets of wheat, for example) to a minimum.

The airiness and lightness inherent in photographs

Through the use of a wide-angle lens, a photo session in the field can be quite good. A photograph in which a small figure of a person will be depicted will only emphasize the beauty of nature, give a feeling of freedom and lightness. Most popular ideas

A photo session in the field in the summer should depict the model as freely as possible. There should be a feeling that a person was able to escape from the captivity of the stone jungle and get into nature, while receiving a huge charge of vivacity. You can use a fairly popular story in which the girl will run from one part of the frame to another, holding a shawl in her hand, fluttering in the wind.

Parsing ideas for a photo shoot in the field, one should highlight a fairly popular plot. It will cause associations of wheat open spaces. This is a photograph showing a languid girl lying on meadow grass or on crumpled wheat with her eyes closed. To create such a frame, you should use additional attributes. To prevent black spots of the earth, you must use straw or freshly cut hay. To everything else, there should be a hill next to which the photographer will shoot the girl.

Optimal time for photo shoots

A field photo session is best done in the early morning or evening. At this time, the sun is low enough above the horizon. Due to this arrangement, the photographer can get a unique opportunity to create a great photo. A girl's photo shoot in the field may turn out to be slightly sad. For this, the model should be depicted, going into the distance.

Family and wedding photo frames





The field can be used for photographing a wedding. To do this, choose flower meadows, which will not happen any work. Such places will be filled with bright colors and saturated colors, which will help make the photo more beautiful. Many couples arrange a photo shoot in the field right during the wedding itself. To do this, pick up balloons, umbrellas. You can create soap bubbles during the shooting process. All these details will be in perfect harmony with the vast expanses of the fields.

In the field, you can arrange a photo shoot for the whole family. The wind, which is almost always present in the fields, will give the photos lightness and airiness. In addition, the frames obtained will look advantageous for the simple reason that the endless expanses of nature will be assigned the role of the second plan. The focus should be on models. Movement plays an important role

Great attention during the shooting process should be given to how the models move. In the fields, a plot is very often used in which a person runs or walks. This is due to the fact that due to this, you can reveal the free nature of the model, unlimited in its behavior. For portraiture , the use of posture while sitting and lying is also characteristic. Insanely beautiful can be a photograph in which only the face is visible, and the body is hidden by grass.





Conclusion

In this review, some particularly popular ideas for a field photo shoot were considered. If you show all your imagination and approach the matter responsibly, then the photos can turn out beautiful.
