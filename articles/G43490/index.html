HSM Canarios Park 3 * (Mallorca): room description, service, reviews

Plan your vacation carefully. It depends on what emotions you have to return home with. In this case, you need to pay attention to all the little things. It is imperative to choose the right hotel. For different categories of vacationers, a different approach to organizing recreation is suitable.

One of the good options for spending your holidays can be a three-star hotel in Mallorca HSM Canarios Park. The rest here will be provided to visitors, will be described later.

Location

HSM Canarios Park 3 * is located in Spain, on the island of Mallorca. It is part of the Balearic Islands archipelago. This resort is located on the east coast of the island. This is the center of the tourist area called Calas de Mallorca. This corner of the island is known for its picturesque nature.



The hotel is located near the beautiful bay, which is covered by picturesque cliffs. This part of the island is famous for its clear waters. Here you can plunge headlong into a wonderful vacation surrounded by the beauties of the Mediterranean. Romantic, measured rest here will bring a lot of positive emotions.

The hotel address can be found in all tourist guides. Tours are regularly organized from Moscow to Mallorca . Hotel address: Spain, Calas de Mallorca, C. Antena, 23.

How to get there

You can get from Moscow to Mallorca by plane. This is one of the most convenient ways to travel. You can book a place in the hotel on your own or using the special offers of travel agencies. The second option is considered preferable. Rest in this case will be organized correctly and harmoniously.



The airport is located 70 km from the resort. A bus stop is provided here. By public transport you can get from the airport to the city. The stop is 100 meters from the hotel. It is very convenient for those who decide to travel on their own.

The nearest town is Calas de Mallorca. It is at a distance of 1.5 km. From here you can reach the hotel by car. Almost all tours to Mallorca from Moscow provide for the delivery of tourists to the place of recreation.

general description

Hotel HSM Canarios Park 3 * was built in 1970. The last restoration of the rooms was carried out in 2016. This is a stylish, cozy resort located in close proximity to the sea (400 m). Here 334 rooms of various categories are rented. Smoking is prohibited on site.



This is one of the best options for the ratio of quality and price. The presented tourist complex is the best in Kalas. The resort has a swimming pool. You can go to the equipped beach number 7 in 10 minutes walk from the hotel.

The tourist complex is comfortable for adults, youth and families with children. The style of organizing a holiday is described as beach, calm and romantic. Nearby there are shopping centers, attractions, shops and other resort infrastructure. You can head to the bus stop to explore the surroundings.

Beach vacation

Many vacationers come to the resort for the beach measured relaxation. All conditions are created for this. HSM Canarios Park Beach is a beautiful sandy coast. It is close to the hotel.
