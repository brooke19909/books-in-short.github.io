Special offers for customers and employees

The bank has special offers that can be used by regular customers and employees of this organization. In particular, this is a partnership loan.

You can use it if you meet one or more of these conditions:

  • have a good credit history;
  • you have a salary card in the bank "Orange";
  • are employees of a company that is located at the cash settlement services in this financial organization;
  • Work in a company that is in the top 400 according to the rating agency Expert RA;
  • You have a deposit in the bank "Orange", opened more than a month ago;
  • own a credit card of this financial institution.

Under the terms of the partnership, you can count on the amount from 50 thousand to two million rubles. The loan term is from 13 months to five years, the rate of 18 percent per annum.

You can reduce the rate by 1 point if you have a good credit history, insure yourself, get a loan in the form of bank transfer to an account opened with a bank, transfer your salaries to a bank card, hold the position of a middle or senior manager. As a result, the rate can be reduced to 13 percent if all these points are followed.

To obtain this and other loans, it is necessary that the borrower be a citizen of the Russian Federation with permanent registration in the region where the bank is present, that is, in St. Petersburg or Moscow. At the time of granting a loan, its minimum age should be 23 years, and at the time of loan maturity - 65 years. Work experience at the current place of work - at least three months, and general - at least one year.

The bank is ready to provide customers and employees with the fastest possible financial assistance under the Standard overdraft program. Such additional financing of a card account is another option to a salary card. It can be provided in case of lack of funds on your card. In this case, the bank adds the missing amount. The overdraft is repaid automatically as soon as you put the money back on the card.

The credit limit of the card account is set individually; it is no more than two net monthly income minus payments on current loans. Moreover, it cannot be less than 15 thousand and more than 300 thousand rubles. The maximum credit limit is one year from the date of granting. The extension is carried out automatically, without providing additional documents on current conditions. The interest rate on the loan in rubles will be 26%, in foreign currency - 20%.

Overdraft "Salary" is provided for holders of salary cards of the bank "Orange". In this case, the credit limit will be no more than 85% of net income per month, the rate - 25%.

Car loan

You can find quite a lot of customer reviews about car loans at Orange Bank. The financial institution has developed several proposals for the purchase of a car or motorcycle. To buy used and new cars, you can use the "BuyBack" car loan. His secret is in deferring the main debt, so that the monthly payment can be half as much as usual.

During the entire loan term, you have to pay only part of the cost of the car and interest on the loan, which will be about a third of the main debt. The balance is paid at the end of the loan term when you decide to keep the car for yourself. Otherwise, you can return the car to the salon at a residual price and leave for a new one. Either use this offer again, or extend the loan for a new term, or sell the car yourself, pay the balance, while also earning.

The loan is issued without down payment in the amount of one hundred thousand to 2.8 million rubles. Duration - from 2 to 6 years. The rate of 12.9% per annum when purchasing a new car, 16.9% - when buying a used car.

Car loan "Real car enthusiast" is issued in cash for the purchase of a used car. At the same time, it is possible to complete a deal with an individual without intermediaries in the form of a car dealership. Money is paid in cash, so the seller does not have to open a bank account. At the same time, specialists of a financial organization will check the legal purity of the car you are purchasing, help you prepare the necessary documents, and provide a comfortable platform for negotiations.

The loan is provided without down payment. The amount and term of the loan are the same as in the previous version, the rate is 16.9%.

Finally, the True Rider loan is a special lending program for those who want to buy an ATV, motorcycle, snowmobile or water equipment. The loan amount is from one hundred thousand to two million rubles, the loan term is five years, by agreement, it can be increased by one more year. In the presence of collateral, the rate is 19%, without collateral 20.5%.

Credit card

It is possible to get one of the most demanded banking products in the country at this financial institution - a credit card. Orange Bank offers a grace period of 65 days.

65 days is the period during which the client can use credit funds without paying any interest. This is the most convenient financial product that allows you to quickly borrow money from the bank on favorable terms. The only thing you should be absolutely financially disciplined person.

When applying for this card, you get a credit limit of up to one million rubles, which you can use for 65 days for free. Its validity is two years. In case of non-payment of debts after the expiration of the grace period, the loan rate will be 28%.
