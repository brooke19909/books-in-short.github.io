Hotel "Elizar-Hotel", St. Petersburg: address, reviews

Elizar Hotel is an incredibly beautiful and bright establishment that will give you a high level of comfort and an unforgettable vacation experience.





Brief description of the hotel

A distinctive feature of such an institution as Elizar Hotel is its location in a historic building of the 19th century, which was restored in accordance with modern ideas about comfort and luxury. It provides free Wi-Fi, a parking area, a steam sauna and many more services. It is also worth noting the work of the tour desk, where you can organize fascinating study tours.

Elizar-Hotel is a relatively small establishment, which has about 40 rooms. All of them are characterized by varying degrees of comfort, as well as the availability of all necessary amenities. It is worth noting that paying for the apartment, you are guaranteed to get a rich continental breakfast. You can also stipulate the provision of an extra bed.

If you decide to stay in a place like Elizar Hotel, you should definitely visit its restaurant. It focuses on fresh meat dishes. You will also have the opportunity to sing karaoke. If you do not want to go down to the restaurant, or are planning a romantic dinner, the waiters will deliver your order directly to the apartment.





Hotel Location

This hotel is characterized by a rather convenient location, which will please not only tourists, but also business people. Here are located not only famous cultural sites, but also industrial facilities. A few meters away is the Lomonosovskaya metro station (a free shuttle is provided to it), from where you can quickly get to anywhere in the city.

Hotel Rooms

Hotel "Elizar-Hotel" can offer you the following accommodation options:

  • The rooms of the category "Economy" suggest double occupancy in a room of 12 square meters. m. There is a minimum set of necessary amenities, including its own combined bathroom.
  • Standard apartments can accommodate 2, 3 and 4 people. Their area is slightly larger than the previous category. The windows offer beautiful natural landscapes.
  • Superior apartments are characterized by a fairly impressive area, as well as a wide range of amenities. There is a separate sitting area, represented by a folding soft sofa.
  • Luxury apartments are a studio in which a bedroom and a living room are combined in one space. Also a feature can be considered the presence of a spacious furnished balcony. The bathroom is equipped with a bath.




The amenities provided in the apartment

Elizar-Hotel 3 * offers you to use the following amenities in your apartment:

  • landline telephone, which is provided for internal communication with hotel services, as well as making long-distance calls;
  • widescreen modern TV broadcasts many satellite channels;
  • so that in summer you do not suffer from heat, a fan is provided in the room;
  • thanks to the presence of a refrigerator, you can make a small supply of products to be able to eat at any time;
  • the bathroom has a built-in hairdryer, thanks to which you can quickly dry your hair or do styling;
  • shower or bathtub, depending on the category of room.




Hotel Infrastructure

St. Petersburg hotels are characterized by cordiality and hospitality, which is expressed in developed infrastructure and quality service. So, Elizar-Hotel can offer its guests the following:

  • secure parking with video surveillance, the use of which is included in the price of the room;
  • if you have too bulky or valuable luggage, then you can leave it in a storage room;
  • there is a safe at the reception , the cell of which can be rented by any guest;
  • Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel, access to which is included in the price;
  • if necessary, the reception staff will call a taxi for you at the appointed time;
  • hotel staff provide guests with every assistance in booking and purchasing tickets for air and rail transport;
  • Babies under three years old can be provided with special cots;
  • if necessary, you can be provided with a transfer to anywhere in the city;
  • a travel agency organizes interesting excursions accompanied by a professional guide;
  • equipped with a barbecue area;
  • in the restaurant you can not only eat deliciously, but also sing karaoke;
  • after a hard day you can take a steam bath in the sauna;
  • There is an indoor pool that is open year-round.




Useful information for tourists

The Elizar Hotel has a number of rules that govern the accommodation and leisure of tourists:

  • check-in of guests starts after 13:00, but the administrator needs to check out on the day of departure and hand over the keys before noon (the conditions can be changed individually, subject to availability of rooms and after making an additional payment);
  • children using existing beds stay at the hotel for 300 rubles per day;
  • an extra bed will cost you 1350 rubles per night;
  • guests have the opportunity to come with their pets, for which an additional fee is charged (depending on the size of the pet);
  • the hotel has bank terminals with which you can pay for the services received in cashless form;
  • parking spaces are provided not only for cars, but also for bulky cars.




Positive reviews

Many tourists choose "Elizar-Hotel" (St. Petersburg) for their accommodation . Among the positive reviews, the following comments should be especially noted:

  • very polite, helpful and outgoing staff at the reception;
  • there is an iron on each floor;
  • a very convenient location that will appeal to both tourists and business people;
  • delicious and hearty breakfasts are served every morning in the restaurant;
  • towels are replaced daily (they are always clean and fresh);
  • very beautiful and well-groomed area with a small decorative fountain;
  • thanks to the presence of an additional heater, the room will be warm even in the coldest weather.




Negative reviews

"Elizar-Hotel" (St. Petersburg) gives the guests a number of negative impressions:

  • the quality of the cleaning in the room is just disgusting - dust and rubbish everywhere
  • quite often there are incidents when the rooms do not change bed linen after previous guests;
  • sockets are very poorly fixed, which not only causes inconvenience, but is also unsafe;
  • if you book a room with a double bed, then most likely you will be offered to accommodate 2 shifted;
  • if in other hotels room cleaning is an obvious duty of the maids, then here you have to literally demand it;
  • very few channels are broadcast by a satellite dish;
  • very hard mattresses on the beds;
  • the rooms are just terribly audible both from the street and from neighboring rooms;
  • due to the lack of mosquito nets in the rooms there are always a lot of mosquitoes;
  • the cafe is full of unsanitary conditions, as well as an unpleasant smell (apparently, hoods do not work well);
  • despite the fact that smoking is prohibited in the hotel, there is a smell of cigarettes in the corridors;
  • the bathroom is incredibly cramped;
  • the same thing is served for breakfast every day.

General impression

Elizar-Hotel is a rather interesting establishment, which occupies a restored historical building. As a result, the atmosphere of antiquity was preserved, as well as a feeling of complete comfort. Nevertheless, employees are not very careful about the number of rooms. this is manifested in the disgusting quality of cleaning, and it is especially unpleasant to detect stale bedding when checking in.

The main advantage of the hotel is its convenient location near cultural attractions, as well as business facilities. In addition, an absolutely free shuttle to the nearest metro station is provided in the mornings. But many do not like the fact that the hotel is located in the courtyards of multi-storey buildings. Nevertheless, many prefer to use this territory in case of lack of space in a private secure parking.

Thus, it can be said that Elizar Hotel is a great option for unpretentious tourists. But if you are used to paying attention to little things, then this option will not suit you.
