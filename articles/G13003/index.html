Hotel "Metropol", Moscow: address, photo, owner, history, reviews

The Metropol Hotel (Moscow) is the decoration of the capital and one of the main buildings of Theater Square, which is historically valuable for the city. Today this hotel is one of the most famous in the capital. He celebrated his 110th birthday and is proud of his story!

Hotel History

Surprisingly, in the very place where the Metropol Hotel (Moscow) is now located, the hotel was previously located. The baths worked under him, and this place was very popular among the inhabitants of the city. At the end of the 19th century, the hotel was sold. In its place, according to the plan, they had to create a new hotel complex that would meet the cultural level of Moscow.

The only owner of the Metropol Hotel in Moscow at that time was S.I. Mammoths. He chose the project of the architect V. Valcot, after which the construction began. Alas, Mamontov was not destined to implement this brilliant idea, he was convicted in the embezzlement case and soon lost his fortune. The new owners rebuilt the old complex, making a number of adjustments to the Valcot project. The opening took place in 1905.





The hotel at that time was luxury. Here, guests were offered such wonders as a telephone and refrigerators, and all rooms were spacious and each decorated in its own unique style. Soon, a cinema was opened at the hotel, which only further surprised the guests of the city.

During the formation of the Soviet regime, the hotel did not work for its intended purpose. Here was the Second House of Soviets. That was until 1930, after which the complex again became a high-level hotel.

The architectural value of the complex

Moscow today is not just the capital, but also the historical center of Russia. Each building is a value. This also applies to such a building as the Metropol (hotel, Moscow). The history of the complex began in the era when modern was in fashion. Its spirit is felt especially in the exterior of the building.

The paintings inside were made by such famous masters as Vasnetsov and Korovin. The interior decoration is characterized by different styles, as was originally planned by Mamontov. The whole complex appears to the visitor as a historically valuable architectural ensemble. Majolica panels occupy a special place in it. Among them - the work "Princess Dreams", made by Vrubel and now transferred to the Tretyakov Gallery. Previously, any passer-by could admire this work of art on the facade.



Also a great advantage of the hotel is its location. Directly on the Theater Square, a few steps from the Kremlin, stands the Metropol Hotel (Moscow). Her address: Teatralny proezd, house 2.

Halls of the complex

The glory of the building was brought not only by the stylistic decoration and modern equipment of that time. Hotel "Metropol" (Moscow) is a variety of halls. What do they look like today and what are they for?



In total, there are nineteen different halls, large and small, for guests. They are used for all kinds of events. These are banquets, presentations, meetings and so on. The employees of the complex assure that their approach in working with small groups of 15 people or a large number of guests of several hundred will not leave anyone indifferent.

Famous guests

The hotel saw many high-level guests. At one time, Alexander Kuprin and the great composer Sergei Prokofiev lived here. Also, Mao Zedong, Marlene Dietrich, Pierre Cardin, Michael Jackson, Julio and Enrique Iglesias, Placido Domingo and others stayed in the rooms.

In 1991 the hotel was again restored and opened in its original form. He was also evaluated by a special commission, which assigned him a well-deserved rating of five stars.

Visitors reviews

The fact is that the Metropol Hotel (Moscow), whose address is already a historical value, cannot, in principle, have only positive reviews. Yes, having read on various sites what clients write, you can come to the conclusion that in most cases this is praise. It is for this reason that I would like to pay special attention to what the guests liked the most, and to what did not like it.

Positive reviews:

  • Of course, absolutely all visitors note the excellent location of the hotel. In principle, this is his calling card. The balcony offers a gorgeous view of the Bolshoi Theater. Who does not dream about it?
  • Also, a positive mark deserves both the external and internal decoration of the hotel. Those who come to Moscow to shop are always advised to settle in the area. Here is an abundance of shops of various price categories.
  • Customers also appreciated the setting of breakfast and dinner. The sounds of the harp during morning coffee were remembered by many. This is perhaps the most vivid impression of the hotel. Breakfast includes gourmet dishes and caviar.
  • Valued "Metropol" and Muscovites. Halls are often rented here for various events. The quality of service is always kept at a high level.




There are also negative reviews. Which sphere they relate to is described below.

Negative feedback:

  • Some guests noted the complexity of the entrance to the complex. This is not surprising and applies not only to rush hours. Very often, when driving in personal vehicles, you can encounter such a problem.
  • Also, guests who visited the hotel in the winter months wrote that the room was somewhat cold. There was also a wish that the floors in the bathrooms be warm.
  • The Metropol Hotel (Moscow), the photo of which is presented in our article, according to some users, is already in need of repair. Recall that the restoration was last carried out in the 80s. Otherwise, the guests have no complaints about anything.
