Today, no one can be surprised by the presence of a mobile phone, but quite recently, some 15 years ago, this type of communication was not just a luxury; only a few could afford it. The development of mobile communications is moving forward by leaps and bounds. Almost every month, a global brand offers its latest in mobile communications. Everyone already knows how to use it, but not everyone knows that the concept of IMEI is closely connected with the concept of a mobile phone. What is this term? In this article we will consider its significance.



So IMEI - what is it? IMEI is an abbreviation, it means a unique number of a mobile device. It is decrypted as follows - International Mobile Equipment Identifier. It consists of 15 digits, the producer country is encrypted in the first two digits, the model code of the device is encoded in the next 6, another six digits is the individual number of this particular phone, the last 15 is the spare identifier.
