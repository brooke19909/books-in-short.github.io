The best hotels of Venice: photos and reviews of tourists

Venice is a city in Italy, which is its symbol and decoration at the same time. Venice consists of a group of small islands separated by canals and connected by bridges. The islands are located in a closed bay between the rivers Rho and Piave. Venice is a World Heritage Site.

The whole city is incredibly beautiful. It is almost impossible to get acquainted with it in a few days. You need to come to Venice for at least a week and leisurely explore local attractions, wander through the narrow streets, ride gondolas and feed the pigeons in the central square.
