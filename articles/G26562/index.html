Perhaps one of the main innovations is the touch panel. "Sony" decided that the yard is no longer the nineties. The era of "Dandy" and "Mega Drive" has sunk into oblivion. It's time to move on. Ergonomics

Sony Dualshock 4 retained the same settings as the fellow. Therefore, if you are literally rooted in Dualshock 3, then you will not have to relearn. Dualshock 4 has a built-in vibrodrive, accelerometer and gyroscope. All this is necessary during the game, the use of such technologies brings new sensations. Now immersed in the game world has become much easier, and to perform various actions through the touch screen is much more pleasant than clicking on the buttons.

Everyone knows that playing races, fighting games, football using the keyboard is another perversion. The guys from Sony decided to take pity on PC gamers.
