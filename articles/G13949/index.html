Electrobroom "Karcher". Customer reviews

Cleanliness in the house is not only beauty and order, but also ensuring a healthy atmosphere in which there is no place for dust and bacteria. Therefore, it is so important to choose the right household appliances for cleaning the apartment. The German company "Karcher" is widely known for its products designed for use in car washes and in everyday life. But the Karcher electric broom, which deserves the best reviews, deserves special attention.





This device is intended for dry cleaning. In fact, it is the middle link between a vacuum cleaner and an ordinary broom. Externally, the device resembles a mop: a long handle and a nozzle in the form of a standard brush. A garbage container is placed inside it, which is also an advantage, because there is no need to sweep the rubbish in a heap, as happens when cleaning with an ordinary broom. The capacity of the waste bin is 0.5 l. Due to its light weight (two kilograms), the unit is very mobile and easy to operate, thanks to this the Karcher electric broom is very convenient to use for both adults and children. It can also be used for educational purposes, so that the child learns how to help with housework.
