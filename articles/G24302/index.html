"Aurora Club" (recreation center): photos, reviews

Finding a good place for a vacation or a country walk for any resident of St. Petersburg is not a problem. There are wonderful tourist complexes that offer their guests a wide variety of services. The choice of a particular hotel or health resort depends only on the preferences of vacationers themselves and on their financial capabilities. For those who want to spend an unforgettable vacation near beautiful landscapes and magnificent lakes, enjoy all sorts of entertainment and forget about the bustle of the city for a while, Aurora Club is an excellent base for recreation, where couples with children can spend time and friendly companies.

Location of the recreation center

Aurora Club is not very far from the Northern capital of Russia. Only some 70 kilometers from the city towards Vyborg - and anyone can be in this wonderful place next to a pine forest and clear lakes.





In order to well imagine the location, it is better to know the exact address of the complex. Not far from St. Petersburg is located the Aurora Club (recreation center): Vyborg district of the Leningrad region, the city of Zelenogorsk, the village of Polyany. This is the so-called Semiazerye district. Here, very close to the recreation center are several reservoirs of different sizes. The Aurora Club itself is located on the shores of Lakes Long and Horseshoe. And a little further there are two more large freshwater sources - Polyanskoye and Gladyshevskoye.

How to get there

Coming to this place is very simple. To do this, you can use both personal transport and public. Aurora Club is a recreation center that can be reached from St. Petersburg from Finland Station. To do this, you need to get to Zelenogorsk by train, and from it to the Avrorovets stop by buses No. 401 or 483. You can also use the K-827 minibus, which leaves from the Grazhdansky Prospekt metro station to the village of Polyany. If vacationers are more comfortable with a personal car, then they will need to drive along the Primorsky highway. Then, after the bridge, turn right near the Polyana sign. Continue along the A-125 highway until you see a turn to the base.

Aurora Club: description and photo

Surely all the guests of the recreation center will be interested to know that in the Soviet years this place was a stunning health camp for children called "Aurorovec". Over the years, namely in 1999, over the vast territory of the complex of 17 hectares, they began to make a complete transformation of the infrastructure.





Instead of camp buildings there are now two comfortable hotels, an apart-hotel and several detached townhouses. The magnificent nature, beautiful lakes and magnificent pine trees give this place a certain charm and attractiveness, which vacationers do not stop talking about in their reviews. Aurora Club is located in a very picturesque place. The recreation center, the photo of which is presented in the article, looks truly fabulous. And largely thanks to the surrounding nature, new guests do not stop coming here.

It has a beautiful beach, its own stable, an excellent restaurant, a bar and a cafe. Sports grounds located on the territory of the recreation center have round-the-clock lighting. And it is quite convenient for those who prefer to play after sunset.

Housing stock

Due to the wide variety of accommodation options, any guest who comes here will be satisfied. Aurora Club offers a large selection of rooms - a recreation center (Vyborgsky district). On the shore of Lake Long there is an Odyssey apart hotel, positioned as a luxury complex. It can comfortably accommodate in studios, suites and suites. There are luxurious penthouses with a private sauna. All rooms at the aparthotel are designed for a maximum of 4 guests. They have kitchens, the necessary furniture, appliances and utensils, a heated floor and a balcony, which offers a beautiful view of one of the two reservoirs.





In the immediate vicinity of the forest stands the Shell Hotel, which has standard one-room rooms with everything you need. They are designed for two guests. In the Reef complex, which is located almost opposite the Shell building, guests can stay in double, triple and quadruple rooms. In both hotels, only single beds are available for sleeping.

On the territory in different places built and townhouses, which are perfect for relaxing large companies. There are houses called "Cape", "Breeze" and "Lagoon", designed for 4-6 people. They are two story. On the first floor there is a kitchen with a living room, on the second - there are two bedrooms. Townhouses "Poseidon" and "Neptune" also consist of two floors. But they still have a spacious terrace and a basement, where the sauna is arranged.





What to do for the guests of the recreation center?

Aurora Club is a recreation center in which no one will be bored. In addition to a beach holiday, which has local lakes, you can ride catamarans and boats, go fishing and even go diving. You can explore the surroundings on horseback from the local stable or ride on bicycles and ATVs with a breeze. Sports enthusiasts can enjoy billiards, minigolf, paintball or football. In winter, you can rent skates or skis. Little vacationers will be able to have a great time at the kids club, where they will be welcomed by kind and cheerful animators and many diverse activities every day.





The conference room located on site will allow you to hold important working meetings or training. Another Aurora Club is a recreation center where you can perfectly celebrate a holiday in the Nautilus restaurant. Club staff will help with the design and organization of the event, and tired guests can then be accommodated in hotel rooms. Especially valuable is the fact that the recreation center operates throughout the year. Therefore, no matter what month the event is planned, it can definitely be arranged here.

What do guests think?

Very different reviews about the recreation center. The Aurora Club made a mixed impression on its guests. There are people who were satisfied with everything. And they were particularly impressed by the local nature. Others scold the rather old furnishings in the rooms and the lack of professionalism of the staff.





Some say that living here is not worth the money they ask for it. Sin and food at a local cafe. She, according to some vacationers, is not very tasty and quite monotonous. But a large number of various activities that can be found here, everyone likes.
