Hotel Queens Park Goynuk, Turkey, Kemer. Reviews and photos of tourists

The favorite vacation spot of many people, including our compatriots, is Turkey. A short flight to this country, a wide selection of hotels from very budget places to very exclusive options - all this makes Turkey very attractive for any vacationer. Hotel reservations can easily be made not only with the help of tour operators, but also via the Internet. The second method is very convenient nowadays, when almost everyone has access to the World Wide Web. A popular place in Turkey is the city of Kemer, where many different hotel complexes are concentrated. One of the accommodation options for a good rest can be the Queen's Park Resort Goynuk 5 * hotel.

General information, photo

The Queens Park Goynuk 5 * Hotel was built a long time ago, back in 1994. The name by which this resting place is known now is not original. Someone could hear about this hotel earlier, only he was called first Joy Grand Kemer 5 *, then - Justiniano Wish Grand Kemer 5 *.





However, in 2010 this complex was acquired by Queen's Park chain, and the hotel received its new name, under which Turkish guests now know it.

Location

The Queens Park Goynuk Hotel is located in a small village called Goynuk, on the shores of the warm Mediterranean Sea. Just 7 kilometers away is the center of a rather famous city - Kemer. The distance to the Antalya International Airport from the hotel is approximately 50 km, so that having arrived in Turkey, guests can quickly reach their vacation destination.

Common territory

A fairly large area is occupied in the city of Kemer by the Queens Park Goynuk hotel, along with all its developed infrastructure. Its territory covers 60 thousand square meters. There is everything to ensure that the vacation was exciting and interesting, allowing you to both relax and get a charge of vivacity, which is enough for long working days. On this large territory there is a lot of greenery, which makes it possible in the heat to easily find a secluded place in a cool shade.





It offers - two outdoor and one indoor pool, two water slides, its own beach, tennis courts, playgrounds for sports games, ping pong tables, an amphitheater, restaurants and bars, a fitness club, a conference room, a children's playground, SPA-center.

Where guests are accommodated

Vacationers at Queen's Park Resort Goynuk (Turkey, Kemer) 5 * are offered accommodation in the main building, which was renovated in 2010, as well as in detached bungalows, renovated in 2011. Since not many years have passed since the last renovation , guests can count on the beautiful decoration of the hotel.

The main building is a four-story building. It has 195 rooms of Hotel Room and Hotel Family Room categories.





A little further from the main hotel building is a complex of cozy bungalows, each of which has 2 or 3 floors. They are surrounded by greenery and are great for those who want to ensure a more secluded and relaxing holiday. In these detached houses are Club Room and Club Family Room in the amount of 280 pieces.

Between the two parts of the Queens Park Goynuk hotel there is a special passage built above the ground. This allows guests to safely move from the main building to the complex from the bungalow and back.

From 8 a.m. to 2 a.m., all guests can use the hotel's restaurants and bars without restriction. How to have fun on vacation

Everyone who booked hotels in Turkey and then came on vacation to this country will spend a lot of time on the beach, sunbathing on comfortable sun loungers and swimming in the gentle waters of the Mediterranean Sea. At the Queens Park Goynuk hotel, you can diversify your holiday with all kinds of sports games, water activities, daytime and evening performances.





For tourists who lead an active lifestyle, we offer all kinds of activities to help keep ourselves in shape and during the holidays. On the tennis court or at the ping-pong table you can wave your rackets. Fans of volleyball or football can put together a team and throw the ball for fun. You can also play darts or billiards. You can even rent a bike and ride around the hotel with a breeze.

Various water activities are available. At the disposal of guests - any non-motorized water transport, windsurfing and diving.

For those who want to take care of their body and relax, the hotel has a spa center with massage, gym, sauna, hammam and jacuzzi.

How to have fun

In Turkey, there are many places where you can go on a guided tour and usefully spend your free time. For example, at a distance of about 20 km from the Queens Park Hotel Goynuk in the direction of the town of Tekirova are the ruins of ancient Phaselis. Majestic coniferous trees surround them, so this informative walk will not cause inconvenience on a very hot day. On the contrary, you can hide from the heat in the shade of trees, examining the remains of the ancient city, and then also cool off in clear water on the local beach.





For little tourists

If Turkey was chosen for the long-awaited family vacation, the Queens Park Goynuk hotel will be a good place where you could stay. After all, there are good conditions created for recreation not only for adults, but also for children.

Young tourists can enjoy not only the warm waters of the Mediterranean Sea and games on the sandy and pebble beach of the hotel, but also use the special services that the Queens Park Goynuk hotel provides specifically for young tourists. At their disposal is a children's playground where they can frolic throughout the day. There is also a mini club on site for guests aged 4 to 11 years. It works daily from 10am to 5pm. There is a children's pool where kids can have a great swim for fun if you don't want to run to the sea.





Upon request, a cot and a pot are provided for free to guests in their room. There is also the opportunity to rent a stroller for your baby, if suddenly the guests could not bring their own to rest. This is very convenient, because not everyone will be able to take children's transport on the plane.

Animators often arrange various children's performances, and at a special disco for young tourists, children can show their dancing skills and successes.

The restaurants provide highchairs and a special menu, thanks to which parents can tasty and healthy feed their child. Here you can also use a blender and a heater for baby food.

General impressions of tourists

When choosing a place for a future vacation, tourists rely not only on the description of hotels in Turkey on their official websites and information from travel agencies, but also on the impressions of other vacationers. Reviews of the Queens Park Goynuk hotel will make it the most complete impression.





As always, our compatriots evaluate the hotel both positively and negatively, but most agree that this place to stay does not match its stars.

Advantages of Queen's Park Resort Goynuk

Almost all reviews praise the large and spacious Queen's Park Resort Goynuk. It is very green, planted with a lot of flowers. A variety of trees also did not leave our compatriots indifferent. Among them are palm trees and fruit trees. Tourists also like the hotel‚Äôs excellent location overlooking the thick green mountains.

According to the reviews of our compatriots, the hotel has a very good children's mini club. Here, not only watching cartoons with children and drawing, but every day they come up with new topics for games, competitions, master crafts.





They respond positively to the animators and entertainment shows that they host. Programs for children, organized at the highest level, are especially noted.

Disadvantages of relaxing at the hotel

In their reviews, many vacationers write that the rooms leave much to be desired. Despite the fact that the last repair was carried out relatively recently, all rooms require updating. The furniture is simple, in some places there are ragged pieces of walls. Also, guests are unhappy with the cleaning of the rooms: many write that it is carried out irregularly.

Some tourists who visited the Queens Park Goynuk hotel are unhappy with the rather monotonous dishes in restaurants and a small selection of drinks.





Not satisfied, our compatriots were left and the service staff as a whole. Some write that the waiters in restaurants serve slowly, and last but not least they approach Russians. Reception staff rarely respond to requests and comments, probably waiting for some kind of reward for their services.

Of course, any positive feedback about this hotel is definitely the opposite. There are tourists who were delighted with the food and staff. But most guests believe that this hotel does not reach the declared five-star level.
