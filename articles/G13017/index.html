The history of children's summer recreation camps in Russia dates back to the 19th century. In those days, on the territory of the present RF there were special children's health-improving colonies in which the children of wealthy parents had a rest. Today it has already become a good tradition in the summer to send a child to the camp. Among the many options, parents are most interested in the summer recreation camp Dzerzhinets.

Most popular: Dzerzhinets, camp near Izhevsk

One of the most sought after is a camp located in a picturesque area near the city of Izhevsk (7th km of Yakshur-Bodinsky tr., 22, Izhevsk). The children's camp has been open since 1987. Total area: 11 ha. It accommodates 415 guests, designed to accommodate guests aged 7 to 14 years. Children are placed in two comfortable landscaped brick buildings with amenities on the floor, as well as in one bedroom building with private facilities in the rooms. The rooms accommodate from 4 to 5 people.





The camp provides medical care, provides round the clock security. Pedagogical staff for working in the camp are formed according to the results of competitive selection, for selected specialists it is obligatory to take preparatory courses "School counselor."
