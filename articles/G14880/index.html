Location

In the heart of the picturesque resort town of Turkler, Senza The Inn Resort Spa 5 * is located. It is only 15 km from the center of lively Alanya and 100 km from its airport. Gazipasa International Airport is 60 km away.





Senza The Inn Resort Spa 5 *: room description

The hotel management ensures that guests stay in the most comfortable conditions. So, you can settle in rooms of the following categories:

  • Standard 24 sqm apartment m is a spacious bedroom with a large bed. Opposite there is a cosmetic table with a mirror. There is also a small workplace. From the balcony you can enjoy the mountains or sea views. Four rooms of this category are equipped specifically for a comfortable stay for the disabled.
  • Duplex is a luxurious two-story room, which is often chosen for family accommodation. The lower level is a small living room, as well as a bedroom with twin beds. Also downstairs is the bathroom. On the second floor there is another bedroom with a large bed.
  • Family room - these are two bedrooms separated by a sliding interior door. One of them has a large bed, a cosmetic table and access to the balcony. The second is equipped with twin beds and a soft folding sofa. Each room has a private bathroom.
  • Royal Suite with sea view consists of three spacious rooms - a living room and two bedrooms. There is one large and three single beds. There is also a couch and a comfortable sofa for extra seating. The room is equipped with two combined bathrooms.

It is worth paying attention to the equipment of rooms Senza The Inn Resort Spa 5 *. The photo shows a restrained interior style with a predominance of classic elements.





Room Amenities

The Senza The Inn Resort Spa 5 * hotel is not only beautiful and fun, but also comfortable. So that you can fully relax in your apartment, they provide such modern amenities:

  • the front door is opened using an electronic key, which is also an activator of the supply of electricity in the room;
  • there is a private balcony equipped with comfortable furniture;
  • thanks to the radio, your stay in the room will be accompanied by a pleasant listening to local music;

  • a minibar with your favorite alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks;
  • air conditioning system; wireless internet signal;
  • landline telephone for communication with the reception and calls abroad (in the bathroom - emergency call handset).

Hotel Restaurants

The management of the Senza The Inn Resort Spa 5 * hotel took care of a full and tasty food for guests. Turkey, like any other country, is characterized by unusual cuisine. To enjoy local and international dishes, visit the following restaurants:

  • The main restaurant invites guests to enjoy breakfast, lunch and dinner, during which buffet meals are served. Both Turkish and international cuisine are presented here. Guests can sit in a spacious roomy room or in the fresh air.
  • The Mediterranean restaurant specializes in fish dishes. Fresh seafood prepared according to a wide variety of recipes. There is also a large selection of vegetable dishes with spicy dressings.
  • A Turkish restaurant will introduce you to the culinary traditions of the state from Ottoman to the present. In addition, the menu presents dishes that correspond to different regions of the country. The atmosphere is maintained thanks to the theme design and national music.
  • The Italian restaurant-pizzeria is liked not only by adults, but also by children. Lasagna, pasta and other Italian dishes are also served here. There is an extensive wine list.
  • For lovers of spicy dishes and fiery music, there is a Mexican restaurant. Here you can try the most delicious dishes of Latin America, dance or listen to the live performance of the singers.

Supply system

In this hotel with special awe are to ensure that guests are well-fed and satisfied. For this purpose, the following power system has been developed:

  • from 07:00 in the main restaurant breakfast begins;
  • at 10:00 a brunch is served for those who like to sleep longer or have already managed to get hungry;
  • at 11:00 in the garden, as well as on the beach, Turkish gozleme cakes begin to be prepared;
  • at half past twelve in the main restaurant lunch is held;
  • from 15:00 guests are treated to ice cream;
  • at 16:00 in the lobby bar, guests can enjoy coffee with cakes or traditional Turkish sweets;
  • at 19:00 dinner is held;
  • From 22:15 until the early hours of the night, the lobby bar serves nightly snacks.




Hotel Bars

The bars of the Senza The Inn Resort Spa 5 * hotel are responsible for providing guests with quality drinks and light snacks:

  • Relax bar will give you the opportunity to relax, hiding from the hustle and bustle. A cozy atmosphere, a calm color scheme, as well as quiet music complement the atmosphere. It serves both spirits and natural herbal teas.
  • The lobby bar is the main establishment of the hotel, where life is always seething. During the day, you can have a friendly conversation or business negotiations, and in the evenings - listen to live performance of musicians.
  • Guests can enjoy cold beer and soft drinks at the beach bar. For lunch, you can eat fast food.
  • The pool bar serves cocktails. You can try drinks from the bartender or create them yourself.
  • The Irish Pub is the perfect place for beer and football lovers. Here are regularly watched sports matches.
  • Connoisseurs of nightlife will appreciate the disco club. You can enjoy strong drinks and incendiary dances here until late at night.
  • Hookah is equipped in the open air. The establishment is a cozy tent with soft sofas.

Helpful information

It is worth knowing some rules of accommodation at the Senza The Inn Resort Spa 5 * hotel (Turkler). The following points can be considered the most significant:

  • reservation of a la art restaurant is required;
  • Guests under the age of 18 are not served alcoholic beverages;
  • The hotel reserves the right to make changes to the concept of the tour without prior notice to guests;
  • check-in and eviction of guests is carried out, respectively, after 14:00 and until 12:00 (early registration and extension of stay is possible at an additional cost);
  • in case of loss of a beach towel or card, on the basis of which it was issued, a penalty of 10 euros will be charged from the guest;
  • to contact the reception, on the keyboard of the phone installed in the room, you need to press 0;
  • both the login and password for using wireless Internet are the guest room number.

  • Wireless internet is tolerable only in the lobby area.
