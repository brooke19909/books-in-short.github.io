Alana Nha Trang Beach Hotel 4 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): room description, service, reviews

Tours to Vietnam from Moscow and other cities of Russia have recently become increasingly popular among domestic tourists. And no wonder. You can relax in Vietnam - the country of the gentle sea, picturesque nature and ancient legends - no worse than in Turkey or Egypt. In this state there are many attractions, ancient monuments, entertainment venues and, of course, comfortable hotels. For example, the four-star Alana Nha Trang Beach Hotel offers tourists, including domestic, comfortable rooms and excellent service at a not too high cost.

Where is the hotel and how to get to it

Alana Nha Trang Beach Hotel 4 * is located, the reviews of which tourists are simply magnificent, in Nha Trang, one of the most famous resorts in Vietnam, the capital of Khanh Hoa province. To get to this city is best, of course, by plane. This is currently the fastest and cheapest way. And it is it that is offered to domestic holidaymakers by tour operators. You can fly from Moscow to Vietnam in about 10 hours. Airplane tickets cost about 25-30 thousand rubles. But, of course, it is better to get a ticket from a tour operator. In this case, 30-35 thousand rubles. it will cost both flight and hotel accommodation.





The hotel itself is located in the European quarter of about 40 km. from Cam Ranh Airport. The easiest way to get to it is by ordering an individual transfer. Such a service is provided at the hotel.

People with aerophobia can also get to Nha Trang by train. But in this case you have to go at least a week. Yes, and the train ride will cost more than the flight. A transfer service is also available from the Nachang Railway Station to the hotel. In this case, you have to go very short. The distance from the railway station to the hotel is only 26 km.

General description of the hotel

The Nha-Trang Hotel is a multi-story building. This hotel is brand new and in perfect condition. Its interior is decorated in high-tech style. It is located near the coastline and is intended exclusively for non-smoking guests. In total, Alana Nha Trang Beach Hotel offers tourists 108 comfortable rooms. The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day. Upon arrival, guests will be charged a deposit of $ 100. The checkout time is considered to be 14 hours (departure - at 12 hours).

Number of rooms

If desired, in the Alana Nha Trang Beach Hotel 4 * hotel, you can rent both expensive apartments and a cheaper room. The hotel offers its guests rooms of the following categories:

  • Superior Room (26 m2).

  • Executive Suite (42 m2).

  • Deluxe Ocean View (47 m2).

  • President Suite (27 m2).

  • The Club and The Club Family (65 m2).





For the convenience of tourists, the rooms include:

  • telephone and TV;

  • work desk and comfortable bed;

  • air conditioning and safe.

If desired, guests can also use the minibar. This room also has coffee and tea making facilities. Each bathroom at Alana Nha Trang Beach Hotel is en suite. It has everything you need (including a hairdryer). Extras include slippers and a bathrobe.

Hotel infrastructure

Thus, according to many tourists, this hotel is quite comfortable for living - Alana Nha Trang Beach Hotel. The description of the numbers given above confirms this. But of course, on the territory of the hotel, vacationers have the opportunity to use a variety of amenities. For example, if you wish, you can rent a rather large conference room in this hotel. Various business events are often held here. Many tourists rent cars in Vietnam in order to be able to see as many attractions as possible. Alana Nha Trang Beach also offers free parking. True, judging by the reviews of tourists, free places on it are not always available. Various collective entertainment events at this hotel can be held in a large banquet hall. This room is well suited for organizing weddings, anniversaries, birthdays, etc.





Hotel service

This hotel has earned very good reviews from vacationers, including the fact that free WiFi is available throughout its territory. You can use the Internet here even in the rooms. In many other hotels, this service is purchased separately. And it is quite expensive.

Tourists have the opportunity to wash their own dirty things directly at the hotel. To do this, the hotel has a large laundry room. Also, if you wish, you can not bother and just hand over things to dry cleaning. This service is also provided to guests. Dimensional items, if necessary, can be left in the storage room. In addition to this, the hotel provides guests with a service such as assistance in acquiring the most interesting excursions.

Things to do in Alana Nha Trang Beach Hotel

Tourists have the opportunity to spend time very pleasantly at this hotel. For this, here are equipped:

  • fresh water pool;

Deck chairs, for example, all six pieces are installed here. In addition, after 12 noon, the sun leaves this part of the building. Is it worth it to go with children

For younger guests, the Alana Nha Trang Beach Hotel 4 * also provides a number of services. Very often here, for example, various kinds of entertainment events are organized for children. But some tourists complain that getting to her with a child in her arms (via a tiled staircase) is not very convenient.

Alana Nha Trang Beach Hotel: restaurant, dining room and cafe

At the hotel, among other things, the Cinnamon Restaurant also operates. Reviews of tourists this institution has earned good. It serves local, Asian, European cuisine. In total, the restaurant is able to accommodate up to 250 guests at a time. The hotel also has a small cozy cafe Alana. Here, among other things, you can order good freshly brewed coffee.

It operates on site and a beer bar. It is located on the roof, next to the pool. The prices for drinks here, according to many guests, are quite adequate. Breakfast is included in the room rate. Tourists have the opportunity to come to eat from 6.30 to 10.30. In the dining room, among other things, there are also highchairs, which, of course, adds to the hotel attractiveness in the eyes of vacationers.

Hotel beach

To the sea from Alana Nha Trang Beach Hotel guests have to get no more than 2 minutes. The beach at this hotel has its own, private. Outsiders are not allowed here. Hotel guests have the opportunity to use sun loungers for free. However, they are issued, unfortunately, only for a few hours. But if you wish, you can pay a small amount for a deck chair and use it all day.

The beach itself, belonging to the hotel, judging by the reviews of tourists, is constantly being cleaned. The coastline and the sea are very clean. The beach is covered with soft sand and walking on it, as well as sunbathing here, is very convenient. In the immediate vicinity of the beach there is a rather popular night club in the city.





Reviews of the hotel's beach at vacationers have earned good. However, many tourists recommend that beginners go a little further - beyond the park. Here is another very clean, comfortable and at the same time almost completely deserted long sandy beach. The water in the sea near the hotel is always warm. Storms in this part of Vietnam almost never happen. In any case, in the morning the sea near the hotel is usually calm. After lunch, most often begins a little excitement (quite tolerable).

sights

Tours to Vietnam from Moscow are becoming more attractive, including because in this state there is simply a huge number of places worthy of visiting vacationers. To get information about them, guests of the Alana Nha Trang Beach Hotel have the opportunity directly on its territory.

Attractions in a city like Nha Trang there is just a huge variety. For example, vacationers can go:

  • to the Tyam towers of Panagar;

  • at Vinpearl Amusement Park;

  • to the Baho waterfalls;

  • to the oceanographic museum, etc.

The Taman towers of Panagar are Buddhist and Hindu temples built in the 7th-12th centuries. ad. They still operate as religious objects, despite the fact that Vietnam is a socialist republic. The inhabitants of this country consider the towers to be the strongest spiritual place and conduct rituals (sometimes even with sacrifices) that are not very clear for a European person.

Fantastic amusement park Winperl is located on the island of Hong Che, completely covered by rainforests. Its total area is 200 thousand m 2 . Here, tourists will find a huge number of different water activities and extreme fun. The park territory is divided into several zones:

  • amusement park;

  • shopping area;

  • aquapark;

  • amphitheater;

  • indoor play area.

Baho Waterfalls are located about 17 km from the city, near a small Vietnamese village. The path to them lies through the jungle and piles of huge boulders. You can come to this beautiful place both with a guided tour and by taxi. The entrance ticket costs 45 rubles.





The National Oceanographic Museum of Vietnam is located in the city of Nha Trang, near the Nha Trang Hotel. Vietnam is a tropical country, the ocean is warm, and therefore many unusual fish and aquatic animals live in it. In the Nha Trang Aquarium, tourists have the opportunity to look at sharks, stingrays, moray eels, etc. You can watch fish and sea animals in the museum not through the glass, as in other similar places, but directly live. Sea inhabitants swim in this oceanarium in huge pools.
