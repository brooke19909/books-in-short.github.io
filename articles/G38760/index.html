Hotel Royal Boutique Hotel 4 * (Greece, Corfu, Kanoni): description, services, reviews

The presentable, recently restored Royal Boutique Hotel 4 * complex is very popular among tourists. Love it for many reasons. And all of them should be listed.





Briefly about the hotel

Royal Boutique Hotel 4 * is located near the city of Kerkyra, which is the capital of the island of Corfu, one of the largest in Greece. This place is called Kanoni. It is generally believed that it is here that the best views of the beautiful sunsets open. Even in Kanoni you can see amazing rare birds that usually rest on the beach shores during the time of migration.

The beach is a short walk from the hotel. At 600 meters from the hotel is Mouse Island - one of the most interesting sights. There is also the Vlacherna Monastery, which you should also look at.

The location at the hotel is good. In 5-10 minutes you can drive to the center of Kerkyra. Fortunately, there is a bus stop near the hotel. What is available in the hotel?

Royal Boutique Hotel 4 * is a hotel complex with exclusive service. So the hotel has all the services that guests may need. Free Wi-Fi is available, as well as free parking. Concierge, babysitting and express check-in / check-out are available.

On site you can find a camera for injuring things and a tour desk. There is a currency exchange, 24-hour reception, dry cleaning with laundry, and also an office where you can send / receive a fax and photocopy documents.

The hotel has several shops, a car rental service, a souvenir stall, and a room service with drinks. If desired, guests can order apartments with increased soundproofing or non-smoking rooms. There are also bridal suites.

It should also be noted that the staff working at Royal Boutique Hotel 4 * is multilingual. The staff speaks Greek, English, Italian, German, French and Arabic. And although this is not indicated in the descriptions, many speak Russian as well. Many have mastered this language due to the large influx of guests from Russia recently.





Entertainment

Royal Boutique Hotel 4 * (Corfu) receives extremely positive reviews . Largely precisely because there are all sorts of entertainment available. Although, as all knowledgeable people know, boutique hotels are famous for their service and level of service, and not for well-organized leisure. However, this hotel is a kind of exception.

It has an outdoor pool, a beautiful garden and a sun terrace. There is the opportunity to go horse riding, cycling, diving, play billiards or golf. You can also rent a canoe. There is also a massage room on site.

Even for children there are conditions - a small and shallow pool with sea water.

In the Royal Boutique Hotel 4 * (Corfu) there are several good, stylish establishments in which guests can satisfy their hunger. There are two options - it is only breakfast or half board (with dinner). Serve refreshments in the main restaurant. There are also several bars. One is by the pool and the other is the lobby. And there is also a cafe-bar. In addition to these establishments, guests can visit the √† la carte restaurant, which serves excellent Italian and Mediterranean cuisine.

2-bed apartments

Royal Boutique Hotel 4 * (Corfu) has rooms of different categories. The most budgetary and most popular are the standards designed for 2 guests. Their area is 22 square meters. m. Inside there are two single beds, shifted into one large bed. The stylish apartments open onto a private furnished balcony. There is good air conditioning, a small safe, a minibar and a plasma TV with satellite channels. There is also a bathroom with a bath, shower, toilet and hygiene products. Slippers, towels and bathrobes are provided.

A week in such apartments (with breakfast) will cost two for only 22 tons. For three people, the cost will increase to 35 tons. And despite the fact that these are prices acceptable for Corfu, there are good discounts here. Especially out of season.

Guest Comments

People vacationing at the Royal Boutique Hotel 4 * (Corfu) leave good reviews. Most guests claim that the hotel meets the stated description. Everything in it is at a decent level.

The polite and hospitable staff note special attention. Employees are always ready to help with advice and resolve any issues that may arise. Gels, shampoos, soap - everything is regularly replenished as soon as the maid sees that her stock is out of stock. Daily cleaning, good (dusting, mopping), linen changed every few days, towels - on request.

Also, if the guests have a late flight in the opposite direction, they should notify the administration in advance. It is advisable immediately after purchasing a ticket. In this case, administrators will extend the reservation for several hours, of course, free of charge. Guests are also advised to reserve a room in the main building (the one further from the pool).

If you pay attention to the reviews left about Royal Boutique Hotel 4 * (Corfu), you will notice: many recommend booking apartments in advance. And that really will be the right decision. The hotel is popular, many want to spend their holidays here. Therefore, in the midst of the season, there may simply not be any free dates and rooms left. Out of season, the situation is better: both in the resort it is quieter and the hotel is more spacious. Therefore, if possible, it is better to go to the Corfu Royal Boutique Hotel 4 * in the fall. In October, the temperature of water and air in these places is about 20 degrees.

As a conclusion, it is worth saying that Royal Boutique is a great option for people who want to stay in a good hotel with a decent level of service and at the same time pay a small amount of money. Many tourists have already confirmed that the decision to live here is justified.
