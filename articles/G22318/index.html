A bit of history

This famous ski resort (Chulkovo) is located in the historical parts of Borovsky Kurgan. There are mountains, the height of which reaches about two hundred meters.

The mention of this place in the documents dates back to the eleventh century, once there was an ancient Russian settlement. During excavations, archaeologists discovered ancient gizmos belonging to our descendants who lived in the second century BC. Walking around the surroundings, you can feel the spirit of antiquity, catch the mystical breath of earthly being.

Weather

The weather conditions of the "Chulkovsky Carpathians" are no different from Moscow. In winter, a temperate cold climate, snow usually falls in November, and melts in March. The lack of precipitation is not a problem; there is a snowmaking system in the complex. On average, the temperature is minus four degrees - this is quite comfortable for practicing skiing.

Price policy

How much does a vacation cost? Adults from Monday to Friday will pay thirty rubles for using the lift, and on Saturday and Sunday - fifty rubles. For a child, the cost is twenty rubles on any day. If necessary, you can take a subscription for an unlimited number of upgrades. On weekdays, it will cost five hundred rubles, and on weekends - seven hundred. Children under seven years old ride for free.



The cost of renting ski equipment for adults: skis, poles, boots - six hundred rubles per hour, the same set for a snowboard will cost you, equipment for a child costs four hundred and twenty rubles per hour.
