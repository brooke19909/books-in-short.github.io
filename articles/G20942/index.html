LG Nexus 5: features and reviews

Generally speaking, a smart enough chipset is worth noting in a smartphone, which allows it to function successfully. The camera the device has is also good. If you give an assessment on a five-point system, then it is close to 5. The device was originally supplied in two standard color solutions, but their circle may expand, given that there are now devices of this model that are already red. On the market you can find a version of the phone with 32 GB of internal memory.

As you know, a distinctive feature of the Nexus range is their price. This model can be purchased for 15 thousand rubles, this is a minimum. And, of course, the highlight of the program was software called Google Android. And the latest, latest version.

Equipment

The Nexus 5 delivery set, the characteristics of which will be given in this article, is rather modest. It includes the smartphone itself, a charger, as well as a USB cable for it and a clip with which you can change the SIM cards in the device.

Design





The smartphone design can hardly boast. The previous device of the model range looks much more advantageous against its background. The thing is that it used glass that surrounded the smartphone on both sides. In the Nexus 5 model, the design characteristic suggests that plastic was used in the manufacture of this device. Above it is covered with soft touch material. It is velvety to the touch.

The case has a rectangular shape. The ends located above are slightly protruding. The edges of the device are rounded, which allows it not to dig into the palm of the hand. The back surface is quite flat and even. Rounding is observed only at the edges of the faces themselves.

If we talk about color solutions, then the option with red or white color will be the most advantageous. It is better to refrain from acquiring a model made in black, as it looks primitive. If you are not interested in the design, and you just need a powerful filling, then you can ignore the color.

Dimensions





In all three planes, the length of the smartphone is 137 mm, the width is 69 mm, and the thickness is 8.59 mm. With such metric data, the weight of the smartphone is 130 grams.

The relatively small thickness along with the rounded edges allows the device to lie comfortably and securely in the hand, it does not slip in the palm of the hand. Perhaps, according to these characteristics, only the Galaxy S4 can compare with the Nexus 5. Although even it is not so convenient, since it belongs to glossy phones, which means it slides in the hand.

Fingerprints that quickly cover the back cover will clearly not please Nexus 5 users. The feature suggests that the black model has fingerprints as a key flaw. Although getting rid of them on the back of the device is easy enough, but their constant accumulation can really annoy purity lovers.

Strength

The surface of the display was subject to oleophobic coating, due to which fingerprints from the touch screen are removed quite simply. In addition, the coating is quite durable. It is difficult to leave a scratch on it unintentionally.

On all sides





The finger on the display slides evenly and smoothly. That, in fact, speaks of comfort when using it. The device case has a non-separable type. It is assembled for glory. You will not notice any creaks, backlashes. He also will not emit a crunch if the apparatus is twisted.

As mentioned earlier, the camera module is not quite standard. It protrudes above the surface of the back cover, but even so, operation has shown that it is difficult to spoil the lens glass. At the top of the front panel is a speaker. The design of the Nexus 5, the characteristic of which deserves at least 3 points, does not shine with originality, but it can still boast of practicality.

The proximity sensor, located next to the front camera, works stably, but sometimes it still allows itself liberties and gets lost. But to say a bad word about the light sensor will not work. The backlight level due to its work is regulated not only smoothly, but also precisely.

On the opposite flank are the volume buttons. The manufacturer made a statement that the buttons are made of ceramic materials, which is very helpful.

In addition to the power button on the right side is a tray for SIM cards. It can be removed with an ordinary needle, not only through the use of specialized injectors. At the bottom you can see the input type MicroUSB, and to the right of it is a microphone. The upper edge accommodates another microphone, as well as a jack reserved for a wired headset, standard 3.5 mm. On the back cover next to the camera is an LED flash.

Here is such a design has the LG Nexus 5. Its characteristics indicate that the phone, in principle, is quite convenient and comfortable to use, fits securely in your hand. However, originality in terms of design would clearly not hurt him.

Display

This is the threshold after which using the device with one hand is difficult. The threshold was not crossed by the company, and this makes the use of a smartphone more convenient.

In general, there are better and worse models in the smartphone market in terms of color rendering. Our smartphone does not play the main, but not the last role.

Nutrition

The battery belongs to the lithium-polymer type, it is non-removable. The capacity is in the region of 2300 mAh. Watch a video in high quality (of course, if at maximum brightness) it will turn out no more than 5 hours. At the same time, you need to use headphones to output sound. Music can be continuously played for 35 hours, at high brightness the games will last 3 hours, and with bright backlight you can use the Internet for 6 hours.

Google Nexus 5 Specifications





LG Nexus 5 is equipped with a Qualcomm family chipset. This time it's the Snapdragon 800. 4 cores that work at 2.26 GHz provide good performance. Adreno 330 is used as a graphics accelerator.

Google Nexus 5 specs: memory





The amount of RAM is 2 GB. For personal use, more than 1 GB is available, usually 1.3-1.5. Depending on the version of the smartphone, either 16 GB of long-term memory or 32 GB may appear inside it. Nexus 5 phone, the characteristics of the filling of which were described earlier, does not imply the installation of additional memory drives.

The question of the relevance of the device is currently not yet quenched. The most active users can pass by the model, since the battery of the device is not the most powerful, it will not be enough for a long time.
