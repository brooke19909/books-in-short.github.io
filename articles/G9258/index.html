Car loan in Sberbank: percentage, terms and reviews

A new car is the ultimate dream of most motorists, but the cost of such a vehicle is becoming a key problem on the way to realizing desires.

As a rule, at such moments the only way out is lending. The most profitable option for those who want to buy a car are special banking products - car loans. The first question that arises for the future owner of the vehicle is which bank to contact. In this article, we consider the conditions of a car loan at Sberbank. This financial institution is most in demand among borrowers.

Where to get a car loan?

Why is Sberbank addressing the vast majority of borrowers? First of all, because it is an old time-tested bank that has been operating for several decades. In addition, more than fifty percent of Sberbank shares are under the control of the Central Bank of Russia, which cannot but inspire confidence in potential customers.

Car loan concept

The purchase of a vehicle on credit involves the issuance of borrowed funds for specific purposes. In this case, to buy a car. In this case, the guarantee of repayment of the issued loan is the pledge of the vehicle.

Thus, the full owner of the car, the borrower becomes only after full settlement with the financial institution for the amount of the principal debt and interest stipulated by the contract.

A car loan in Sberbank can be issued not only for the purchase of a new vehicle, but also for a used car.

Loans to Sberbank

Currently, Sberbank's customer service includes an extensive range of loan services with convenient credit conditions. In many ways, this financial institution is in great demand.

As for special programs on car loans, Sberbank terminated such programs in connection with the crisis situation in the country since the end of 2014. Therefore, citizens who wish to purchase a new or used vehicle can use the means of a consumer loan.

Consumer loan for the purchase of a car

To receive funds for the purchase of a vehicle, you can use one of the following products:

  1. Unsecured consumer credit, provided for any needs of the borrower. The maximum loan amount for a loan is 3 million rubles for a period of three to sixty months at a rate of 12.9 to 19.9 percent.
  2. Consumer loan for any needs of the borrower with the involvement of guarantors. The maximum loan amount is 5 million rubles for a period of up to five years inclusive at a rate of 12.9 percent.

An important condition for the provision of such a loan is as follows: only a citizen of the Russian Federation aged 21 to 75 years can act as a guarantor. Moreover, the upper age barrier should not come before the payment of the last installment on the loan. The surety must have a steady income and a good credit history.

Loan interest rates

When using consumer credit funds to purchase a new car, the interest rate will depend on whether the borrower is a client of Sberbank, namely: does he have a salary account with a credit institution.

Accordingly, loans are provided to Sberbank's salary clients on more favorable terms:

  • for customers receiving a pension or wages to an account opened with Sberbank, the loan rate varies from 12.9 to 19.9 percent;
  • for other citizens, the interest rate set by the bank will be in the range from 13.9 to 19.9 percent.

Judging by the feedback of borrowers, preferential lending conditions plus a maximum loan amount of five million rubles allow you to purchase any model of passenger car both domestic and foreign manufacturers at a rate of 12.9 to 19.9 percent per annum.

In comparison with other credit organizations offering car loans, a consumer loan in Sberbank with such rates can be considered as the most advantageous offer.

  4. Possibility of paying off debt up to five years.

Order of registration

To obtain a loan under one of the selected car loan programs at Sberbank for individuals, the borrower must perform the following actions:

  1. Fill out the application form and prepare documents according to the list established by the bank.
  2. To apply with documents to a branch of Sberbank or a car salon cooperating with a credit institution.
  3. Get approval for a loan application.
  4. Select and arrange a vehicle at the dealership (at the dealer, manufacturer or its official representative).
  5. To issue an insurance policy with any company that provides auto insurance services against possible risks, including theft, damage and other types of vehicle loss. (The list of insurance companies is available on the credit institution's website).
  6. Sign a pledge agreement with the bank.

After the actions taken by the borrower, the bank will transfer the loan funds directly to the seller‚Äôs account, and, if necessary, to the insurance company. Borrower Requirements

The basic requirements for potential borrowers for car loans are not much different from the requirements for issuing consumer loans, and includes such conditions:

  • Citizenship of Russia.
  • Age from 21 to 75 years (at the time of payment of the last installment) and up to 65 years if the borrower can not confirm the income.
  • Work experience of at least six months at the current place of work (for salary clients and pensioners receiving payments to the bank account - at least three months) and at least one year of seniority for the last five years, except for pensioners clients of Sberbank.
  • No co-borrowers required.

The list of documents required by a financial institution on car loans is somewhat wider and in addition to the standard set includes: a copy of the title deed, a document confirming payment of the purchase, an agreement with the seller.
