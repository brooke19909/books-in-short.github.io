Embroidery pattern for Easter. We decorate the house for the main spring holiday

Easter is one of the most important Christian holidays. It symbolizes the resurrection of Jesus Christ. Traditionally celebrated after Lent. There are many symbolic meanings of the holiday - this is renewal, and divine light, and the triumph of life. There are traditional elements that accompany this day: colored eggs, Easter cakes. The house is decorated with toys, pendants, festive textiles.

How to decorate a house for Easter?

For Easter, there are European and Russian traditions. Although, over time, the merging of cultures is manifested in their unification. Embroidery patterns for Easter can have both figures of Easter rabbits and Orthodox symbols: crosses, pussy-willow branches, Easter cakes, Easter. Combining these motives is a common message. Such jewelry should be bright, clear. They can be a little childish - simple, with bright colors, naive.

Traditionally, this holiday is associated with conversation after Lent. This is a great occasion to decorate napkins and tablecloths, towels and towels with thread floss and needles.

In needlework stores, you can purchase a plastic canvas for embroidery. It can be cut as you wish. In this way, for example, cup holders in the shape of an Easter rabbit or a brightly colored egg can be made.

Using soluble canvas, it is convenient to transfer embroidery to clothing items or finished textiles. You can update the look of sofa cushions by embellishing them with the theme of the Resurrection or create a festive mood by transferring these stories to a sweater, shirt or dress.
