"Fast money": reviews of employees and customers

The brand created by Small Credit Shop LLC in Ulyanovsk in 2008 is now known to many residents of the Russian Federation. A year after its founding, the organization set about developing new cities and towns. Since 2011, the company "Bystrodengi" has the status of MFIs. The popularity of this financial agent continues to gain momentum. Consider all the main points associated with the activities of "Quick Money". Feedback from customers and employees about this microloan organization is highly controversial.





The work of the operators who receive clients at the offices of MFIs is evaluated by visitors first of all, and not always positively.

Operating room of the company: reviews with a plus sign

  • Customers enjoy a homely atmosphere in many departments of this organization.
  • Regular visitors of the company are satisfied with the attitude of the employees and the quality of the services provided.

Private individuals who do not have a relationship with the staff give negative feedback about the company "Fast Money".

Cash and foreign exchange transactions: reviews with a minus sign

  • Individuals making currency exchange operations at the bank complain that as a result of the slowness and incompetence of the operating room workers, money is converted for a very long time. During this time, due to changes in the exchange rate, customers lose quite large sums of money.
  • There are cases when office employees refused to serve clients with poor eyesight, in particular, those who forgot glasses at home.

Loan operations: reviews with a plus sign

Issuance of credit resources: reviews with a minus sign
