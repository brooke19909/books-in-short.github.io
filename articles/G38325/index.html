"Gorky Park, Kazan" - this is the name of the Central Park of Culture and Arts named after In order to make it clear to A. M. Gorky, this is precisely about the Kazan landmark, in contrast to the world-famous Moscow one. After all, Gorky Park in Moscow, like Luxembourg Park in Paris, and Hyde Park in London, is a kind of visiting card of the capital.

Analogue to the capital





But Kazan also had the right to call its green spaces that way, because Alexei Peshkov, having arrived here at the age of 16 with a dream of entering Kazan University, lived here for 4 years - from 1884 to 1888. Naturally, he spent a lot of time in urban squares separated by the Arsk field and bore the names of Russian and German Switzerland. The field, in ancient times located near the Kazan Kremlin, got its name in honor of the tract running along it from the Tatar city of Arsk to Kazan.

History Battlefield

It is known not only by the fact that now the forest plantings surrounding it are almost united and have a common name - Gorky Park. Kazan is famous for its opposition to Moscow princes. It was here, on the Arskoye field, that a battle took place between the inhabitants of the city - Kazan - and the troops of Ivan the Terrible in 1552, which ended in the capture of Kazan. Here, on the Arskoye field, the troops of Emelyan Pugachev were also grouped, as a result of which in July (10-11) 1774 the Krasnoufim fortress was taken and a victory was won near Kengur. Therefore, these places in 1833 were visited by A. S. Pushkin, who wrote his "History of the Pugachevsky Revolt."

Park location

As a holiday, every evening the youth of the cities sought to parks in the country and, of course, to Gorky Park (Kazan). Everyone knew how to get there, or rather, get to it - the Vakhitovsky district, where the park is located, is the historical center and the main urban territorial and administrative unit of Kazan. Everything is nearby, within walking distance of the Kazan Kremlin.





Ershova Street borders the southern part of the park, Vishnevsky - the east, Podluzhna - the north, and the western edge of the park is limited to the Suvorov School. Since the formation of the Republic of Tatarstan, a construction boom has begun in its capital and other cities.

They were not just taken out, they were thrown out, some lay for a long time in the surrounding ravines.
