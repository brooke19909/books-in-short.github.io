Night club "Jagger" (St. Petersburg): reviews

Club "Jagger" is one of the most beloved places of residents and guests of the city of St. Petersburg. The institution offers customers excellent cuisine and drinks from its own brewery. However, the main reason why visitors come here is live concerts of rock legends.

Exclusive music

Unique energy reigns in the club. The atmosphere of this restaurant is fun and a lot of positive emotions created by invited musicians. On a spacious concert venue perform the stars of the world stage. Everyone can enjoy their performance. The hall is equipped with sound and lighting technology innovations that make the performance more high-quality. Not only famous artists sing in the club, but also little-known groups with a great future.





It should be noted that musicians work exclusively "live". The main style is the tunes of the last century. In particular: blues, rock and roll, jazz, disco, funk and rock.

The Jagger Club selects the musicians very carefully. Address of one of the most famous institutions of St. Petersburg: pl. Constitution, 2. Relatively close to two metro stations.

The restaurant has its own site, which is constantly updated. It is there that you can learn about the numerous performances and concerts of your favorite musicians.

Tasty food

However, not all customers come to the club because of the performances of the artists. Many are attracted to the rich restaurant menu. The complex has an excellent cuisine, the dishes of which are praised by all, without exception. You can try here Mexican, European and American delicacies. Talented cooks are responsible for the quality of food.

Jagger Club offers its customers a bite to eat both main courses and exclusive soups. Especially often, visitors order branded sausages and snacks for a foamy drink. The institution offers three types of beer, the price of which fluctuates around 200 rubles per glass. You can also enjoy delicious desserts.

However, visitors claim that it is best to dine here and order a business lunch. Such a complex is very satisfying, tasty and inexpensive, regular customers share. It should be noted that every day the dishes change.

In addition, on weekends and holidays, the administration offers various promotions and discounts both on the kitchen menu and on the bar card.

Popular place

In addition to all of the above, some visitors are not happy with the location. The metro is quite far (1.5-2 kilometers from the station).
