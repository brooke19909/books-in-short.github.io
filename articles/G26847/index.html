Hotel Karon Phunaka Resort Spa Karon Thailand, Phuket: description, photos and reviews

Along Karon Beach, which is one of the longest in Phuket, there are many tourist complexes. Some of them are very popular. These include the Karon Phunaka Resort & Spa. Karon is a picturesque place that is popular among visitors. And many people who decide to relax here stay at the mentioned hotel. And now it‚Äôs worth telling about it in detail.





Location

From this it is worth starting the story about Karon Phunaka Resort & Spa (Karon). The complex is located in a beautiful location on a mountain called Naka, and due to its location on a hill from any point of the hotel, an amazing panoramic view of the ocean and picturesque natural landscapes opens.

30 kilometers from the complex is Phuket International Airport. There is also Krabi International Airport, but you have to go more than 50 km to it.

It is also worth noting that the beach here is not located in close proximity. But this is not a big problem for tourists, as the hotel provides a free regular shuttle service to it. Walking to Karon Beach can be reached in about 15-20 minutes.

It is also useful for tourists to know that the complex is located near popular attractions. In a few minutes you can reach the famous Karon Night Market and Suwan Khiri Ket Temple. Also nearby is the inaccessible but picturesque Freedom Beach, Big Buddha, Plaza Kata shopping center, shooting range and many other interesting places.

But in general, the hotel stands apart, which will appeal to lovers of silence. And if you want noisy fun, you can take a taxi or tuk-tuk, and in 15 minutes drive to Patong Beach, located in the western part of Phuket. It is the most popular and noisy on the island. There, everyone will definitely find entertainment to their taste.

Infrastructure

Karon Phunaka Resort & Spa (Karon) has everything that a modern tourist needs - from free high-speed Internet to parking.

There is a sun terrace and several pools, one of which is panoramic. The hotel also has its own SPA center, which has a sauna, a large modern fitness room with new exercise machines, several massage and treatment rooms, which offer a wide range of cosmetic services - from body wraps and scrubs to peeling and facial masks. Everyone can get acquainted with the complete list individually, or after arriving at the hotel. If you want to visit the SPA, you need to sign up for a session in advance, for which you can contact the reception. Which, by the way, works around the clock.

While at the Karon Phunaka Resort & Spa (Karon), you can still do squash, racquetball, golf or windsurfing (on the beach). By the way, in the evenings there is a karaoke show, the opportunity to participate in which is provided to everyone.





Service

This topic should also be noted with attention, telling about the Karon Phunaka Resort & Spa 4 * hotel. The registration desk in the complex is open around the clock, there is a currency exchange, a tour desk and a luggage storage room. In addition, all visitors are entitled to use the services of a concierge.

It is important to note that the hotel can order breakfast directly to your room. Even guests, if they need it, are offered a special diet menu. Packed lunches on the road and fresh newspapers are also arranged.

Karon Phunaka Resort & Spa 4 * cares about the comfort of guests, so for those who come with children, babysitting services are available, which will sit with the child while adults are resting.

The hotel has laundry and dry cleaning services where guests wash, clean and iron clothes. There you can also hand over your pants for pressing.

There is a need to make a copy machine, print something, or send / receive a fax? Not a problem, because the complex has a small business center where you can complete all of the above.

I would also like to note that in Karon Phunaka Resort & Spa 4 * (Phuket, Karon) there are car rental and transfer services. The provision of the latter must be agreed with the administration in advance.

restaurants and bars

A few words should also be said about them. And first of all, it is worth noting the attention of the institution known as Phunaka Restaurant. This chic restaurant with panoramic ocean views serves breakfast, lunch and dinner. There is a diet and children's menu.

According to the guests who were here, breakfasts in the restaurant of the complex Karon Phunaka Resort & Spa 4 * (Phuket, Karon) are good, standard for Thailand. They serve fresh vegetables, lettuce, sausage and cheese slices, fruits (tangerines, bananas, pineapples, papaya, watermelons, etc.). There is also a large assortment of pastries, hot dishes (national specialty - spicy chicken noodles), fried bacon, pancakes, cereals, eggs in various forms and drinks.

Tourists recommend having dinner here in local taverns with a large selection of exotic dishes. Others advise to go to a nearby market, where at low prices you can buy a lot of all kinds of goodies.

It is also worth noting that the hotel has two bars. One is aimed at connoisseurs of alcohol and drinks, and the other is designed for snack lovers.





About apartments

Hotel Karon Phunaka Resort & Spa 4 * (Thailand, Phuket) has 86 rooms. All of them are divided into three categories - 2-seater "standards", "deluxe", as well as family apartments designed for three people. All of them should be briefly described.

The area of ‚Äã‚Äã"standards" is 36 square meters. They are spacious, cosily decorated rooms with a private bathroom and a balcony with panoramic views of the rainforest and the Andaman Sea. There are two options for "standards." Some have a large king-size bed, while others have two standard beds. It is worth knowing that in rooms of any type there is also a folding sofa.

They are also equipped with a widescreen satellite TV, telephone, air conditioning, ironing facilities, seating area, dressing room, two toilets, a fridge, a minibar and an electric kettle. In addition to the above, guests are provided with a hairdryer, free hygiene products, bathrobes and slippers.

What about the price? A week of living in such a room at the height of the summer season will cost two guests about 30 thousand rubles. This price includes VAT (7%), city tax (1%) and service charge (10%). If you want breakfast included in the price, you will need to pay about 5-7 thousand rubles.

Now about the "Deluxe". Accommodation in a room of this category will cost about 42 thousand rubles. (with breakfast). Conditions and amenities are similar to the above. Of the features - the presence of a jacuzzi, cable channels and an alarm clock.

But the family apartments of the Karon Phunaka Resort & Spa 4 * hotel have more differences from "standards" and "deluxe". They are decorated in a different style, their area is 39 square meters. m., and inside there is a large king-size bed and a 1-bedroom. And family rooms can be adjoining (suitable for people who come to rest in a large company). The cost of a weekly stay for three people with breakfast will be approximately 50 thousand rubles.

Naturally, the indicated prices are just an example. Accommodation can be cheaper. If you want to save, then you should go on vacation in spring, winter or autumn. Better yet, book your apartment in advance. For tourists planning their trip well in advance, good discounts open up.





Helpful information

Studying the description of the hotel Karon Phunaka Resort & Spa 4 *, you need to pay attention to organizational issues. Check-in here starts at 14:00, and guests are evicted before 12:00. It is important to remember this, since flights will have to be selected as appropriate as possible for a given time.

The complex allows children (they have limited access to the SPA-salon). If there are more guests than sleeping in the room, they will be provided with extra beds. However, they must inform the property in advance of arrival that they are needed. The provision of this service must be confirmed. It is also worth remembering that additional accommodation costs 1540 TNV per night (at the current rate it is about 2600 rubles).

By the way, when making a remote reservation, you will need to provide your credit card details. Karon Phunaka Resort & Spa 4 * (Thailand, Phuket) accepts "Visa", "Master Card" and "American Express". It is very important to provide personal card details (and not borrowed from anyone), because upon arrival you will need to present it at the reception along with your main document (passport). F. I. O. must match, otherwise there is a risk of being denied settlement, to which the administration has the full right.

Criticism of guests

Many people who stayed at the Karon Phunaka Resort & Spa 4 * hotel, the photo of which is provided above, leave reviews about the complex, helping to understand what advantages and disadvantages it has.

The most controversial point is its location. As already mentioned at the very beginning, the hotel is located on a hill, at some distance from the beach. Many consider this a serious flaw. And active people with physical fitness are a virtue. The slope along which you have to go to the beach and back is really very steep. Therefore, many recommend taking with them comfortable, non-slip shoes.

However, this location also has advantages. In addition to additional physical exertion, guests get the opportunity to live in a quiet place (the situation on the hotel grounds is really peaceful) and enjoy the atmosphere of a cozy village surrounded by tropical vegetation.

By the way, for people who do not want to strain, but who want to relax here, there is a way out. There is a moped rental service right in front of the hotel. It costs 200 baht per day (about 340 rubles), and you will also need to present a copy of your passport. Many tourists advise renting a moped, because it is not only convenient to get to the beach, but you can also travel around the island. And independent travel across Phuket, as people assure, is even more interesting than some sights.

Most guests are impressed by the welcome. Guests are welcomed with cooling drinks and fruits in a wicker basket. If people arrived earlier than 14:00, but at the same time the hotel has available rooms of the reserved category, then they will not have to wait.

In general, the staff is characterized as attentive and friendly, which respects the guests.

By the way, many more pay attention to the territory of the hotel. It is very clean and well maintained - so much so that you can walk on it barefoot. General impressions

As you can already understand, tourists about Karon Phunaka Resort & Spa leave mostly positive reviews. And they often contain useful information that helps potential hotel guests more accurately imagine the place to which they are sent. Often it refers to the description of the rooms.

The Internet, for example, in apartments is everywhere. In the literal sense of the word. Even the toilet has a wire to which you can connect.

The beds are large and new with countless pillows. The hygiene room has both a bathtub and a shower at the same time. Plumbing, by the way, sparkles with cleanliness, which pleases the most demanding guests.

Instead of air conditioning, the apartments have a comfortable and functional climate system.

If we talk about negative nuances, then the presence of insects can be attributed to them. But this is the specificity of the local climate. People for whom this moment is a problem should capture several fumigators on the road. Another typical Thai feature is the lack of sidewalks for pedestrians. Therefore, having come here, you have to get used to the crazy movement.

Hotel focus

Karon Phunaka Resort & Spa 4 * (Phuket) is an ideal place for people who dream of a quiet and secluded vacation. Many tourists who came to this complex and did not familiarize themselves with the concept of the hotel in advance even called it boring in their reviews. And there is really little entertainment here. After all, this is a SPA hotel, designed for lovers of relaxation. Here, even on the territory of the panoramic pool, you can only sunbathe and swim. Drinking, smoking and eating is prohibited. By the way, that is why there are always very few people there.

Many say this is a hotel for romantics. And it's hard to argue with this statement. Romanticism is emphasized by the appropriate stylization of the apartments, spacious balconies, the absence of noisy "neighbor" hotels, and picturesque surroundings. It is especially atmospheric here in the low season, when very few people rest. And the cost of living is lower in such periods, which can not be attributed to the pluses.

In general, this hotel is recommended to go to tourists who want relaxation and emotional relaxation. Here such a contingent stops. Very rarely, this complex is chosen by older people and those who have small children. All because of the not very convenient location, which was mentioned earlier.
