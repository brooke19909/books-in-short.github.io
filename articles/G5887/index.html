Hotel "Marty La Perla" (Marmaris): description of rooms, service, reviews. Marti La Perla 4 *

Hotel "Marty la Perla" (Marmaris) is actually located not in the city itself, but not far from it, in a small settlement called Icmeler. This resort is located 8 km from Marmaris in the province of Mugla. The location of the settlement is very good. At this point in the Aegean is a small bay. Icmeler itself is surrounded on three sides by a pine forest. The air is clean, nature is simply mesmerizing. Locals advise you to go for a walk in the mountains, because from the top you have an unforgettable view of the narrow bay, small green islands in the middle of the sea blue, a dense forest.

In the center of all this beauty, the Marti La Perla Hotel (Marmaris) was built on the coast. Opposite it are two picturesque rocky islands. You can rent a boat and ride around them. Those who love fresh air and the surrounding nature, rest in such a place will leave an indelible impression. From large and noisy cities, it is best to choose just such small villages as Icmeler for relaxation. Both the length of its streets and the width are approximately 1.5 km.

From the coast you can see the port of Marmaris with many yachts and ships. The city received its name in honor of the marble mined here in antiquity. Now it is a famous resort and center for sailing. Guests at the Marty La Perla Hotel (Marmaris) can go there for shopping, on interesting excursions, in the evenings, having fun in local restaurants and bars. But first, let's look carefully at what this hotel is.

Resort Features

The first thing that distinguishes the Marty La Perla Hotel in Marmaris is its beautiful view from the window. Some rooms look at the sea bay, while others look at the greenery of the park area and the pine forest. Even when buying a trip to the resort, you can pre-select a room with a view you like.

I would like to note the cleanliness of the beaches. Throughout the territory of Icmeler stretches a sandy beach of an unusual dark color. This beach is awarded the Blue Flag of UNESCO for the cleanliness of the coast.

Another feature of this resort is the permission for all settlers to use the services of the Marti Resort Hotel located in the neighborhood.

All rooms are non-smoking. Some sites describe the hotel as an adult-only place. Children under 15 are not allowed. But there are sites where there is no such information, but on the contrary, they write what kind of entertainment for children there is in the hotel. To confirm this, you need to check with the travel agent when booking.

Additional services

At Marty La Perla in Marmaris, service is always top notch. Housekeeping is provided daily. The hotel has a new environmental principle for changing linen and washing towels. According to tourists' opinions, in order for the room to change linen or bathrobes, you need to submit an application to the registration desk. This is done in order to save water and prevent clogging of the water supply system with detergent waste. Laundry and dry cleaning services are available at the complex, where you can wash personal items for a fee.

Visitors can get well in the spa with a full range of services, do massage, but the fee is paid independently. But to play billiards, tennis on the outdoor courts, exercise on the simulators in the fitness club is free.

If necessary, you can order a transfer from the airport and back. And if tourists arrived by car, then free parking is provided.

Money can be exchanged directly on site. It is allowed to use the services of a concierge, a messenger.

On the territory there is a hairdresser, a beauty salon, a Turkish steam room and a Finnish sauna, a games room.

Not far from the front desk there is a luggage storage room and a safe where you can store money and valuables.

Marty La Perla (Marmaris): entertainment

The main attraction at this resort, of course, is sunbathing and swimming in the warm waters of the Aegean. By the way, in the city of Marmaris there are two seas: the Mediterranean and the Aegean. Weather

According to travelers, this is a blessed land, the weather in Marmaris pleases all year round. The best time is called June, July and September. Sunny days, air temperature 27-29 degrees. It can rain once a month. Water in the sea is 25 degrees.

In winter, the coldest nights are considered at a temperature of +10 degrees. This is January and February. In the afternoon - + 15-17 degrees. But the water is warm even in winter. Below 17 degrees does not fall. If you wish, you can swim even in February. For our tourists, such water will be just right, it will only refresh after a feast in the evening.
