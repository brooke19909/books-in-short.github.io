It is not necessary to be able to knit complex patterns with knitting needles - braids will decorate the product

Of course, we all, both adults and children, love to dress up. And the original clothes are especially attractive. Things sewn or knitted with their own hands have always been in fashion, as they are unique and inimitable. Needlework helps to create your own image, style. Therefore, knitwear is so highly valued, especially now, in the age of modern technology. Patterns made with knitting needles or crochet allow you to make products very diverse.





Braiding Technique

One of the original and fairly simple patterns in knitting is a braid. It is enough to be able to perform the front and back loops to get the desired ornament. The whole secret is in throwing a group of loops in different directions and combinations.

How to make woven patterns with knitting needles, consider the example of knitting a double pigtail. The selected motif consists of 16 loops. The wrong side fits in the design, that is, over the front loops - front, over the wrong - wrong.





Legend: and. p. - purl loops, l. p. - front loops.

The first, second, third rows - 2 and. p., 12 l. p. 2 and. P.

The fourth row - 2 and. p., remove three loops on the auxiliary knitting needle and leave at work, 3 l. p., knit the stitches with the front from the auxiliary knitting needle, remove the three stitches on the auxiliary knitting needle and leave it to work, 3 l. p., knit loops face with auxiliary knitting needles, 2 and. P.

Fifth, sixth, seventh rows - like the first.

The eighth row is like the fourth.

Continuing weaving, repeat 1-4 rows. As a result, you will get an upward-directed pigtail, standing out of two located side by side. If you change the principle of transferring additional loops in 4 and similar rows, then the braid will be directed from top to bottom.





Patterns made with knitting needles using various weaves are widely used in such products as rugs, pillows, caps, scarves, sweaters, pullovers. A combination of different ornaments is possible, which will give the product an elegant look.

The use of different colors when knitting

Another way to decorate the product can be a combination of threads of several colors. Take for knitting yarn of two or three contrasting colors that combine with each other. The easiest option for color knitting is to alternate rows, more difficult is to follow the pattern according to the scheme. Even a small finish will make the product more elegant. Two-tone patterns with knitting needles delight the eye with originality and colorfulness.



Extra yarn for knitting is also used to make jacquard patterns with knitting needles. The principle of this technique is that with the help of a yarn of a different color, an ornament is knitted on the front surface, while there are waist ties on the wrong side. The structure of the material must be necessarily the same, that is, have the same thickness and quality. Multicolor can be obtained even if the yarn in its structure is a color that transforms one into another. Any patterns with knitting needles will be appropriate here.

Even novice craftswomen will be able to put into practice simple tips for creating unique knitwear. It all depends on desire and imagination. Knitted clothes are modern, beautiful, fashionable. At the same time, it is always comfortable in it, because you feel the warmth of the hands that created the thing for their loved ones and relatives. Therefore, do-it-yourself models will provide you with a great mood and joy.
