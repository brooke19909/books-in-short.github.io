Canon IXUS 170 digital camera: description, specifications, reviews

Lines of entry-level cameras in recent years have advanced significantly in the quality of technical filling and functionality. Today, an inexperienced amateur can enter the world of photography with an inexpensive device that provides good shooting capabilities. Such proposals appear in the lineup of large manufacturers, but not every development is successful in the market. An example of a very promising device is demonstrated by the relatively recent Canon IXUS 170 model, an overview of which is presented below.

Model Overview





The camera appeared on the shelves of photo salons in a company with two other state employees in the 160 and 165 series. Together, this trio was to form the core of the proposal for novice users. I must say that the devices received more than worthy stuffing and at the same time they have good ergonomics. Obviously, the Canon IXUS 170 is the most developed member of the family, with an image sensor, a powerful processor and a high resolution of 20 megapixels. If we conduct a broader comparison with competitors from other manufacturers, the device will also benefit from optical stabilization.

As for the design, the manufacturer did not provide for any special delights. The traditional form factor with the usual placement of controls is used. The color scheme is also standard - in addition to the main version of the Canon IXUS 170 Black, versions in blue and silver shades are also provided. In general, the camera looks stylish and bright, but does not indulge in originality. The stake is made on minimalism, which, however, cannot be called a minus.

Positive camera reviews

The owners of the device appreciate it for performance, functionality and ease of use. This is a very good combination for representatives of the budget class, to which the Canon IXUS 170 nevertheless belongs with a stretch. Reviews also emphasize the availability of reference materials in the form of message messages with which inexperienced users quickly delve into the intricacies of settings. All this is supported by the speed of the model, provided by a good processor.





With regard to video shooting, there are also a lot of positive feedback. The compact unit allows you to shoot in HD, using a wide range of different effects. As owners of the Canon IXUS 170 note, the developers successfully transferred many options from the shooting modes to video shooting. However, in this part of the operation of the camera has its own nuances.

Negative reviews

Claims mainly relate to the average level of received photo and video materials. Having a rich optional, the model can not compete with representatives of a higher class in terms of the characteristics of photographs. In particular, many note the presence of noise and blur. However, not all functions really improve the quality of shooting. An exception is the Canon IXUS 170 optical stabilizer. Reviews also indicate poor video quality. Despite the high resolution, there are defects typical for most cameras in mobile devices. There is also criticism of the organization of the menu. The manufacturer performed it according to the standard scheme and you can get used to it, but it takes a long time to get to some options. This problem is exacerbated when you need to take pictures online.

Conclusion





The model is interesting because in it for a small price you can get a wide range of features, some of which, until recently, were present only in premium digital cameras. Another thing is that the Canon IXUS 170 camera is not able to provide a result that would correspond to a given level of performance. Suffice it to say that many budget versions with less extensive settings and without optical stabilization can produce pictures with better detail. Nevertheless, many of the shortcomings are compensated by good ergonomics, nice appearance and small size of the model. Therefore, in the role of a compact digital camera for everyday photos without special quality requirements, this solution justifies itself. Especially when you consider the democratic price tag.
