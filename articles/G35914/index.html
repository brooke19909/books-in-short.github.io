Umm Al Quwain Beach Hotel 4 * - luxury hotel in the Emirates

Umm Al Quwain Beach Hotel 4 * is located in a place little known to tourists in the AOE, in one of the smallest of the 7 emirates - Umm al-Kuwain.

Despite its modest size, it is interesting in that it is a kind of anti-Dubai, because the pace of life here is very measured and calm. There is not a single large-scale shopping and entertainment center on the territory of the entire emirate, there are only 7 hotels, three and four stars, there are not five of them. Prices are higher than in other emirates or even in Dubai or Sharjah, so there are not many people who want to relax here.

Airport and transport

Getting to the Umm Al Quwain Beach Hotel 4 * hotel will not be difficult, since the road from the airport in Dubai will take no more than 40 minutes. Formally, there is an airport on the territory of Umm al-Kuwain, but only members of the local flying club can use it. There is a skeleton of an airplane, which is considered a local attraction. Public transport is not provided, there are only two free destinations that travel on schedule to Dubai and the water park. You can get around by taxi, which, incidentally, is cheaper than in Dubai.

The center of the emirate is filled with administrative, office premises, but on the outskirts there are many monuments and historical places.

Climate at the resort

A subtropical climate prevails in all the emirates, with a large number of sunny days and low rainfall. On average, the air temperature in winter is +20 degrees, water at the same time is not lower than +17 degrees. In summer, the temperature can rise to +45 degrees, and water can reach almost 40.

There are separate rooms for smokers and non-smokers. For each tourist staying at the Umm Al Quwain Beach Hotel 4 * in the UAE, free parking for a car is available.

Pools and beach

The private open-air pool in Umm Al Quwain Beach Hotel 4 * is always filled with clean water. In the cool season, the water is heated. A private beach is fenced, there are always free sunbeds and umbrellas. The beach is very well maintained and spacious, staff serves in this area. To the right of the beach are boat moorings, on the left side the beach goes to infinity.

During their stay, tourists can enjoy massages and beauty treatments at the spa and relax in the sauna.

Entertainment

The emirate is primarily known for its fishing, but this is not the only entertainment here. There are golf courses, tennis, paintball, horse riding. There is also an incredibly large water park, to which the hotel is 20 km away, there is a free shuttle service.

The district has a large number of parks, reserves, there is even an artificial volcano. Art lovers can visit museums, exhibitions, many ancient buildings, mosques, which will plunge into the historical past of the area. Fans of Arab exoticism can attend camel racing, it is very unusual and exciting.

Tourists reviews

Hotel rating by tourists average 8 out of 10. Most reviews of Umm Al Quwain Beach Hotel 4 * are positive, but there are also unpleasant situations. The hotel is especially praised by guests who were relaxing with children, according to them, this is a very comfortable place to travel with a child, cots can be placed in rooms, breakfasts for children are also provided, there are small slides next to the pool. Arabs are very fond of kids, take care of their good mood and are always ready to play with them if they have free time.

Minus consider old furniture and a modest buffet. Absolutely all visitors are very impressed by nature, on the territory, except for palm trees, many different trees, bushes, birds.
