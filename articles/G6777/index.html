Novotroitsk hotels: description, addresses

Novotroitsk is one of the cities of the Orenburg region, which is located on the banks of the Ural River. Tourists have something to see and where to go. Peter and Paul Cathedral, Museum of Local Lore, a large number of monuments and entertainment venues. City guests can stay in hotels. About the best we will provide information below.

Hotel "Metallurg" (Novotroitsk)

It offers vacationers about eighty rooms. If desired, you can order both single and double. Each room is equipped with upholstered furniture, a bed, a wardrobe, a TV. On the wall hangs a TV that will not let you get bored. The room has a bathroom and toilet. Among the advantages of this place, it should be noted the availability of personal hygiene items, convenient parking in front of the hotel, free Wi-Fi, friendly service. Reception is open 24 hours a day. Here you can not only sleep and relax, but also have a tasty meal in the restaurant, as well as undergo a number of cosmetic procedures. Including: hair removal, facial cleansing, eyebrow correction and others.

Address: Sovetskaya, 20.

"Classic"

Among the hotels of Novotroitsk it is very popular. After all, here you will be provided with a pleasant and quality vacation. Cozy rooms include everything you need so that customers can forget about all their daily activities and problems. There is also a sauna, pool, karaoke, guarded parking lot. Payment can be made in cash or by bank transfer.

The institution is located on Makarenko Street, 20 V.

"Home hotel"

Very comfortable and decent place. There are few rooms, but each one has air conditioning, a refrigerator and other household items. The hotel operates around the clock. Her address is very easy to remember - Komarova street, 9.

"Magnolia"

We continue to review the best hotels in Novotroitsk. Friendly staff and comfortable rooms are always waiting for you at Magnolia. There is a parking zone near the hotel, which is very convenient for people who travel by personal vehicle. Rooms are cleaned daily. The hotel address is Korolenko street, 124.

"Jasper"

It is located in the very center of the city. Customers who stayed here leave only positive reviews. It is a quiet, comfortable place. The rooms are spacious and clean. Luxury, of course, is not observed here, but the hotel "Jasper" is quite suitable for living and recreation. This place is located on Lenin Avenue, 24.

"Comfort"

Here you can inexpensively rent a private room or a bed in general. The hotel operates around the clock. Her address is 18 Leninsky Komsomol Street.
