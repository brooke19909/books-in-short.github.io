Review Asus K501LX

In the line of laptops from Asus, the devil himself will break his leg. The company produces many series for various tasks. For example, the ROG line is designed for games, N can boast of its multimedia, X is a budget line, and ZenBook is a classic ultrabook. In this article, we will consider the K line, or rather, the recently released Asus K501LX (you can read the reviews about the laptop below). Want to know more about this device? Welcome to this article!

Appearance





Typically, Asus design elements wander from one line to another. It is for this reason that it is quite difficult to distinguish one series of laptops from another. This rule is true for Asus K501LX. The laptop is very much like its brothers. Quite restrained and at the same time stylish appearance. Clear and even edges of the case, giving the device additional rigor. Extra elements that could distract the user from work are absent. Among the features you can notice the metal cover. It protects the laptop from various damage and scratches.

Ergonomics

Using an Asus K501LX laptop is a real pleasure. In terms of ergonomics, the laptop deserves the highest praise. The dimensions of the device are quite modest. The laptop is thin and light. This, in turn, allows you to use the gadget everywhere. For example, carrying it with you every day to study or work will not be difficult. Also pleased with the ability to adjust the viewing angle. The display can be rejected at a pretty decent angle. Therefore, with one easy movement of the hand, you can adjust the PC for an adult or a child.





Mixed feelings are caused by the stiffness of the hinge, which constantly pulls the body base. On the one hand, this protects the laptop from unintentional opening while traveling. On the other hand, it is simply impossible to open it with one hand. It is also worth noting that the case very quickly collects fingerprints. Fortunately, this problem is not global enough to be considered a serious flaw. In addition, prints are well cleaned with an ordinary cloth.

Input Devices

The device uses an island-type keyboard. That is, all buttons are located at a small distance from each other. This feature allows you to reduce the likelihood of mixics to zero. The keys themselves are made of quality material, no discomfort when touching is felt. In addition, the quality mechanism pleases. The keys bounce off rather sharply after pressing, which positively affects the speed of text input. There is no backlight, but this can hardly be called a significant minus.

The touchpad is also up to par. It is quite large and supports not only two-, but also three-finger gestures. Due to this, the level of ergonomics of the device is significantly increased. In addition, the touchpad is characterized by increased sensitivity. You can be sure that not a single touch will be ignored.





Performance

The first thing worth noting is that the laptop is sold in several variations that differ in technical characteristics. On board the Asus K501LX there is a processor from the notorious Intel company with the marking Core i7 or i5 (it all depends on the configuration). This is a pretty good component, which is famous for its high and, no less important, stable performance. As for the video adapter, the laptop has a discrete card from Nvidia called the GeForce GT950M. This is a fairly powerful video chip that contains 2 or 4 gigabytes of its own memory (again, depending on the configuration).

In general, the graphics card and graphics chip interact perfectly with each other, forming a fairly productive tandem. Surely this is a merit not only of the hardware, but also of the software. To improve the performance of the device, a lot of modern technologies were involved. As a result, the laptop can easily cope with such simple tasks as watching HD-quality videos, surfing the Internet, listening to music, etc. Moreover, the laptop is even able to run modern games on medium graphics settings. At the same time, stable 50-60 FPS are maintained.





Cooling system

As you know, the weak side of any laptop is the cooling system. Fortunately, the Asus K501LX is okay with this. The laptop has two fans that prevent extreme heat. Therefore, you can forget about such a terrible word as "throttling". Damage to components due to critical temperatures certainly does not threaten the laptop. It is worth noting that the coolers work quite quietly. And this is an undoubted advantage of the device.

Asus K501LX: reviews

The public responds about the new brainchild of Asus very positively. Many people like the design of the device, others are delighted with the high performance of the gadget. Perhaps the main drawback of the Asus K501LX 90NB08Q1 is its rather high price. But, as you know, you have to pay for quality.
