How to make an assassin costume?

An assassin costume is the object of dreams of any teenage gamer or cosplayer. But let's take it in order: who are the assassins? Initially, historically, assassins were called eastern warriors who honed their killing skills from childhood.

They did not belong to the army and were mercenaries of the highest class, whom not every ruler could afford to hire.

Assassin's clothes consisted of a long, but fixed on different parts of the body spacious hoodie. This allowed them to move stealthily, silently and quickly, without constraining their movements. But the assassin costume became much more popular after the release of the game Assassins Creed with its legendary characters: Altair, Ezio and Connor. Each of them had its own, individual style, based on which, one can identify the main criteria. Altair costume - oriental note, lightness; Ezio Auditore - the pomp and pretentiousness characteristic of that era; Connor - maximum practicality as well as efficiency.
