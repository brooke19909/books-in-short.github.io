Automatic washing machine: how to choose

At the current time, a washing machine is no longer a luxury, but an urgent need. Now the market offers a lot of diverse models that can satisfy the taste and preferences of different consumers. It is worth saying that a washing machine is an aggregate that is problematic to choose. It is worth using certain recommendations to select the very option.



Washing machine: subtleties of choice

First of all, you should decide where you put it, on which its dimensions will depend. In this case, it is important to consider the amount of laundry that is supposed to be loaded into it. Two options: built-in unit, or freestanding. The depth of the machine depends on the loading capabilities.

You will need to determine the type of load in the next step. It is a frontal and vertical version. Machines with vertical loading are distinguished by their more modest dimensions, as well as the ability to load in the washing process, which can sometimes be very convenient. The front loading method is convenient in the sense that the surface of the machine can be used for any economic purpose. The drawback here is often called the mandatory presence of a space for an opening hatch. All built-in models are frontal only. A mini-washing machine is a convenient option for those who do not need to wash too much.



Further, you are required to determine the appropriate class of washing, spinning, as well as energy consumption. The effectiveness of washing completely depends on the class of washing. The best indicators are considered to be classes A and B. The energy consumption class is another parameter that affects the choice. Here we have in mind the dependence of the energy expended on the amount of linen loaded. The spin class indicates how wet the laundry will be at the exit. Here, classes A and B are also considered the best.



A washing machine is usually purchased with a mandatory attention to functionality. Units equipped with a dryer are usually distinguished by their high cost, however, they are indispensable for those homes where there is no way to hang out the laundry. Modern models are equipped with many features and programs. The most expensive ones are characterized by the presence of 20 washing programs. Each buyer needs to independently determine how important a particular washing program is.

It is important to pay attention to the spin speed. This parameter indicates how dry you will be getting clothes from the machine. It is important to note that different types of fabrics require different spin speeds. The ability of a machine to operate at significant speeds affects cost. The volume of the drum is another important indicator. For a small family, 4.5-5 kilograms will be enough, for large ones - 7-9. It is important to pay attention to the manufacturer, the most popular at the moment are models from Indesit, Electrolux and LG.

If we talk about choosing such a complex unit as a washing machine, it is worth noting that this should not be difficult, since you can always turn to consultants in the hardware store for help.
