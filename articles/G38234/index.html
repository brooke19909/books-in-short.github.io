Dedicated to thrill lovers

Blue hole. This is an underwater cave, which is located on the coast of Belize among coral reefs (Central America). It is a funnel 120 meters deep and 305 wide. The cave arch collapsed and was flooded when the sea level rose. The blue hole became known to the world after the telecasts of the researcher Jacques-Yves Cousteau. It was he who became the pioneer of this amazing natural phenomenon. The underwater world of the cave is very diverse, which attracts divers. Feeling as if you are in a cartoon about the mermaid Ariel.

Underground rivers and lakes are undoubtedly the most unusual places. There are many such people on the planet. And, of course, Lake Melissano in Greece deserves special attention. This is a cave and a lake at the same time. Clean as tears, water combined with virgin thickets and unusual stones - what could be more beautiful? The only thing missing is the fairytale nymphs and elves.

The underground river Puerto Princess in the Philippines attracts and fascinates. The largest underground river in the world. Its length reaches eight kilometers. Puerto Princess is one of the wonders of nature. The area around the underground reservoir, as well as the reservoir itself, are declared a national park. You can get into the cave directly from the open sea, where the river flows. Maybe this is the most mysterious place on Earth?





Hamilton Lake in the USA is also of interest. It is simultaneously a ground and underground water body. A very long time ago, Indian tribes lived on the territory around the lake , the most severe of which are Apaches. Here you can hear a lot of different legends and legends. They will not leave anyone indifferent. The lake and the area around it are considered a nature reserve.

If we talk about natural reservoirs, we should mention the Pamukkale basins in Turkey. A place that will leave its imprint on the tourist‚Äôs memory for a long time. The pools are large hills filled with natural healing waters. Millions of tourists visit the country, only to see this most mysterious place on Earth. Mysterious places of the planet

How many interesting and mysterious nature hides! It seems that there is not enough life to go around the Earth and see everything with my own eyes. But, on the other hand, there would be time and desire. After all, nothing is impossible!





So, where are they - the mysterious places of the planet? Photos of natural wonders can be found on the pages of various encyclopedias. It is enough to look through one of these books and decide on the travel route. You should definitely see the Giants Road in Ireland, the Troll Language in Norway, canyons in the USA, Spotted Lakes in Canada, and much, much more.

The Road of the Giants. It is laid out on the coast of Ireland from stone columns. Many legends and legends exist on her account. The most common legend: one Finn built the road to fight the sea monster. In fact, the trail was formed due to volcanic eruptions. The area where it is located is a nature reserve.





Antelope Canyon in the USA. America is rich in canyons, but the most amazing is Antelope Canyon. In the photo he looks like a human creation. After all, how can nature fashion such lines and shapes? Can! Winds and rainwater for a long time carved out and washed sand caves. It is difficult to name all the shades of the canyon. In the morning he is alone, in the evening another. And the best play of colors is in the afternoon, in the sunlight. The canyon is located on the lands of the Navajo tribe. And to see the most mysterious place on Earth, you need to pay the Indians for crossing their land.

Spotty Lake in Canada. The shape of the lake resembles a honeycomb, each cell of which is filled with water of a different color. This is because the reservoir is saturated with various minerals, which color the water. In summer, water evaporates, leaving beneficial minerals on the surface.

Stone Forest in China. These are amazing stones resembling small mountains. At one time there was a sea that washed soft rocks, leaving stone trunks. The forest is located in Yunnan and is very popular among tourists. It has narrow paths and passages that are better not to wander alone - you can get lost. Similar formations exist in Russia on the banks of the Lena River (Yakutia).





The pyramids of Yoganuni in Japan. The world knows many pyramids, but the Japanese were discovered recently, only 20 years ago. The peculiarity of the buildings is that they are under water. According to scientists, the age of the pyramids is ten thousand years. There are no clear answers who and how built this artifact. However, terraces, trenches, various lines and inscriptions testify to a certain, perhaps still unknown, civilization that was flooded by the sea.

Loving tourists should definitely visit the Tunnel of lovers. It is located in the Rivne region in Ukraine. An abandoned railway, it would seem, should have a frightening, mystical appearance. But with this tunnel the opposite is true! In summer, the railway section is overgrown with greenery on all sides and takes the form of a tunnel. Many lovers and newlyweds hurry here for a photo shoot. The tunnel is very similar to the path from the fairy tale "Alice in Wonderland." So you are waiting for the Cheshire Cat to appear ...

A bit of horror stories. Ghost towns

In addition to stunning and mysterious natural corners, our planet is full of terrible and unpleasant places that chill the soul with their mystical secrets. As they say, horror films "rest" in comparison with them. Thrill seekers - just right!

In the category of ‚Äúmost abnormal places‚Äù the first place can be legally given to Ukraine and its ghost town of Pripyat. Empty streets, playgrounds and lonely houses seemed to be specially created as scenery for a creepy film. Pripyat is the youngest ghost town. At the time of the disaster, he was only 16 years old. There is a similar town in Russia - Kadychkan (Magadan). It dates back to 1943, when high-quality coal deposits were discovered at the site of the future settlement. Since then, a small village has been laid. However, after a mine explosion in 1996, life in the city became impossible due to the increased level of radiation. People stunted before our eyes. Oradour-sur-Glan - a French village, whose inhabitants were shot by German invaders during the Second World War. 642 people were killed in one day, of which nearly 500 were women and children. The city itself was burned. Not far from the place of the tragedy, a new city with the same name was built, and the old Oradour-sur-Glan recognized as a historical monument.

There is also in America its own ghost town - Detroit (Michigan). The formerly thriving city now looks like a ghost: ruined houses and schools inspire fear and horror. The most impressive was once the largest railway station in the world. Detroit is a great place to shoot mystical films. In abandoned churches, dolls testifying to satanic rites are scattered everywhere.

Ghost towns are the most unusual places on the planet. They make you think about the consequences of human actions and reconsider your attitude to nature.

Roads of death

Our land is braided by roads and highways. There are good highways and not so. And there are trails, the stay on which inexplicably gives rise to fear.

For example, the Death Road in Bolivia. The scariest road in the world! She claimed thousands of lives. The road runs high in the mountains and stretches for 70 km. On one side are cliffs dotted with crosses and monuments, and on the other hand, a cliff and precipice. And although the construction of a safe path has begun, they still use this road. Eyewitnesses more than once noticed strange white shadows, flickering either in front or behind the car. Probably the souls of the dead on this trail, which can not find rest in any way ...

An unusual road is in the mountains of China. This is not just a track, but a tunnel road. Periodically, the route seems to enter the mountain itself, where on the one hand there are rocks, and on the other there are such windows over a precipice. They say that if the driver looks out that window, he will immediately fall down ...

There is a road of death in Russia. This is the name of the Lyubertsy-Lytkarino highway. This is a narrow winding highway on which drivers drive with extreme caution. Strange silhouettes were seen by more than one traveler. Rumor has it that in these places there are ancient burials. Perhaps restless souls seek answers to their eternal questions. The whole road is dotted with monuments, crosses and flowers.

And by and large, all the roads of Russia in one way or another can be called the death paths. Either the drivers are such, or the highways, but the Russian Federation is among the first countries in road accidents.

What do the caves of Russia hide?

Caves are the most mysterious places of the planet, which from ancient times have caused increased interest in people. Different beliefs and legends exist about these natural creations.

For example, Sablinsky caves in the Leningrad region took more than one human life. They prefer to be silent about this place. You cannot read about it in the newspaper and you will not hear on the radio. The secret zone is covered in secrets that no one has yet been able to solve. Previously, fugitives and prisoners hid in caves. Probably, they could not find a way out of them, and now their souls are doomed to eternal search.

Many horror stories are connected with the Kashlukat cave in the north of Russia. It is popularly known as the cave of the Black Devil. It is not surprising, because its grottoes also have mystical names - Skeleton, Obscurantism ... On the walls of the cave you can see the ancient spells of shamans, who for a long time held their rites here. They say that those who managed to survive after going to the cave go crazy over time.





The most abnormal and mysterious places of Russia are enclosed in mountain ranges. One of these is the Mountain of the Dead, or the Dyatlov Pass. Many people learned about this place from the movie of the same name. This is not just an invention of the director, it is a real mountain, and it is located in the north of the Urals. Back in 1959, a group of young tourists tried to conquer it. However, all members of the group died under mysterious circumstances. Rescuers looking for tourists found only a tent torn from the inside. What force made people leave the place of overnight in severe frost? What scared the band members so much? There are still no clear answers to these questions. Locals consider this place damned.

These are far from all the anomalous places of Russia. Many secrets are hidden in the water bodies of the country.

Ivachevsky lake (Volgograd region). The reservoir fascinates with its beauty and mystery. However, the picturesque area not only pacifies, but also catches up some fear.
