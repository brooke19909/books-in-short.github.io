A complete list of cities in the Samara region

Samara region is located in the southeast of the European part of the Russian Federation, located in the middle reaches of the Volga River. It stretched 335 km from north to south and 315 km from west to east. It is the fifth largest region of the Volga region. The list of cities in the Samara region is not very large. In total, it includes 11 settlements of this category. The largest (millionaire) is Samara, the smallest is Neftegorsk.



An exhaustive list of cities in the Samara region with a brief description is presented in the article.

A complete list of settlements with the status of "city" in this administrative unit of the Russian Federation

So, a complete list of cities in the Samara region in alphabetical order looks like this:

  1. Zhigulevsk. A famous attraction of the city is the Volga bend Samarskaya Luka.
  2. Kinel, near which there is a mournful monument in honor of the mother, from whom the Second World War took all the sons.
  3. Neftegorsk lies in the center of oil fields.
  4. Novokuybyshevsk. The city pays great attention to sports.
  5. Oktyabrsk lies on the banks of the Saratov reservoir.
  6. Otradny, whose surroundings are rich in gas and oil.
  7. Pohvistnevo was transformed into a city in 1947.
  8. Samara is the center of the economic, scientific and cultural life of the Volga region.
  9. Syzran is known for the Kashpir oil shale deposit.
  10. Tolyatti is a major automotive center.
  11. Chapaevsk is a city of military prowess and labor glory.

Population
