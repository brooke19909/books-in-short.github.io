Rest in Saki, Crimea: beaches, description of hotels, entertainment, photos

Saki is one of the oldest mud and mud bath resorts in Crimea, where hundreds of thousands of tourists come annually who want to have a good rest and improve their health. And after each such rest in Saki in the Crimea, they return home with a huge number of the most pleasant memories, refreshed, rested and full of strength and energy.

Description of the city of Saki

The city of Saki is an excellent Crimean resort where you can improve your health thanks to healing mud. It is located on the West Bank of Crimea just a few kilometers from the sea. The population of the city is about 40 thousand people, and every year several hundred thousand more tourists come there who want to relax, escape from the gray everyday life and improve their health. They choose to rest in the Crimea in Saki "Surf" or "Tangier" (private recreation centers), "Northern Lights" or "Yurmino" (sanatoriums), "Golden" or Lennara (hotels). Or maybe any other sanatorium, hotel or recreation center, which in the city has a huge amount. Moreover, in this resort town you can not only improve your health, but also have a good rest in the arboretum park and water park, see some interesting sights, enjoy sunbathing on velvety beaches and taste delicious and healthy water from mineral springs.

sights

It does not matter if you will rest in Crimea in Saki in the guest house of the Priboy recreation center or in any other place, you should definitely take the time to go outside this territory and see the sights of Sak that deserve the closest attention of tourists. The first step is to go to the salty Saki Lake, whose healing mud can cure a huge number of diseases. You also need to look into the local history museum, where you can get acquainted with the history and life of this resort town. Then you can visit the museum and the settlement of Kara-Tobe, where you can see many interesting archaeological finds, whose age reaches a couple of thousand years. Then you can go to Saki city park, arboretum, which affects its greenery and multicolored. And finally, you can look at the St. Elias Church, which recently celebrated the centenary of its existence, where you can admire its decoration and feel peace of mind.

Beaches

If you decide to enjoy your vacation in Saki in the Crimea, then it is most important for you to improve your health. This means that most of the time it is better to spend on the beaches by the sea and near lakes, where you can fully saturate your body with useful substances, taking sun and mud baths, as well as enjoy the cleanest air.

There are few beaches in this city, especially since it does not adjoin the sea coast, but if you take a little bus or car ride, you can easily reach them. The most important beach of Sak is the city beach, which is recognized as a spacious and well-equipped with many cozy cafes where you can have a bite between meals and sunbathing. True, there are always a lot of people here, so for privacy lovers, it is better to choose some wild beach in the vicinity of the city instead. The recreation center "Surf" in the Crimea in Saki

The most popular holiday destination in Saki is the Priboy recreation center, which is located only 60 meters from the sea and 900 meters from the Saki Salt Lake. This recreation center covers a huge territory, on which there are many guest houses and mini-hotels, so there is definitely enough space for everyone here. In addition, this place has a powerful infrastructure, since on its territory, in addition to houses for settlement, there are several mini markets and shops with everything necessary for an excellent beach holiday and not only massage parlors, grocery stores, 10 cafes, 20 bars, various attractions for adults and children, a karaoke bar and a place where stunning discos are held. The prices at the recreation center "Surf" in Saki in the Crimea in 2018 are quite affordable and amount to 1,100-4,000 rubles per night in a room, depending on the month and comfort of living.

Guest houses

But there is, in addition to the recreation center "Surf", in Crimea in Saki guest houses that differ from each other in price category, infrastructure and remoteness from the sea or lakes.

  1. Guest House "House by the Sea" is located on Morskaya Street in Building 4B, 800 meters from the sea, which can be overcome in 10 minutes. Here you can rent a room or a whole house for a large company.
  2. Guesthouse "Juniper" will delight tourists with a magnificent green courtyard, strewn with many roses and juniper. It is located on Flower Street in house 31 and allows you to rent a room at a price of 600-800 rubles per person per day.

Holiday Homes

There are also very comfortable and cozy holiday resorts and hotels in Saki in Crimea, which offer tourists a huge range of services.

  1. Hotel Villa Magic is located at a distance of 300 meters from the sea and allows you to not only stay in a comfortable economy and standard room, but also enjoy a visit to the sauna, bar, bathhouse and gym, as well as take a child in the children's room.
  2. Hotel Lennara is located right on the seafront and allows you to get an average level of comfort, as well as enjoy a visit to the sauna, bathhouse, bar and cafe, rent beach or sports equipment and take the child, entrusting him with the animator.
  3. Hotel Tangier is located right on the seafront and allows not only to enjoy your vacation, but also to improve your health. On its territory you can also visit a disco, bar, bathhouse, sauna, put the car in the parking lot and exchange currency if you arrived in Saki from abroad.

Sanatoriums

An excellent wellness stay in Saki in the Crimea can be obtained in the sanatoriums of this resort town, which are available here for every taste and in any price category.

  1. The sanatorium "Poltava-Crimea" has an extensive medical profile and excellent infrastructure, since there are an indoor pool, a sauna, a bathhouse, a children's room, a gym, a hall for film screenings and concerts, a disco and a parking lot on its territory.
  2. The Burdenko sanatorium is focused on the treatment of the nervous system, neurology, musculoskeletal system and musculoskeletal system. On its territory there is a separate pond, outdoor sports grounds, tennis court, sauna, bathhouse, bar and cafe. You can also visit a beautician there.
  3. Sanatorium "Northern Lights" is most focused on the treatment of ENT diseases. Here you can get a full board of 4-5 meals a day, as well as visit a cafe, a bar, a hall for concerts and film screenings, a children's room and a sports ground.
