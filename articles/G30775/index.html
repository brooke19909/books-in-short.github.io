The Nai Harn Phuket 5 *: hotel description, photos and reviews

The west coast of Thailand's largest island - Phuket - is entirely occupied by resorts. All of them, starting with Patong, are very noisy. Therefore, tourists are of the opinion that for peace and quiet you should go to small islands such as Phi Phi, Koh Phangan or Koh Tao. But few people know that the southern coast of Phuket is a paradise of nature. Do not think that all the beautiful beaches are concentrated exclusively on the western side of the island.

In the south, there is Nay Harn impeccable in every way. This is exactly the place that a tourist looking for solitude and tranquility in the lap of tropical nature needs. Nai Harn beach is still little "inhabited" and spoiled by civilization. Only a few hotels have been built here, and today we will tell you about one of them. Meet: The Nai Harn Phuket 5 *. Photos of the territory, rooms and the beach, a description of the hotel and reviews of tourists about it can be found in this article.

Hotel History

If you start looking on the Internet for information about The Nai Harn Phuket 5 * hotel to read its description and reviews of tourists, then most likely you will not find much. And all because the hotel recently changed the sign. The first to start exploring the Nai Horn coast were sailors. The bay was convenient for mooring ships, and a little further into the sea strong winds blew. Somewhere in the mid-80s of the last century, a yacht club opened here. Not all athletes wanted to spend the night on board their ships, and to go to Patong or Phuket Town was far away.

Therefore, in 1986 the hotel opened. It was called the ‚ÄúRoyal Phuket Yacht Club‚Äù and existed under this sign until 2014. Gradually, neighbors appeared at the hotel, for example, the chic and new Sunsuri Nai Harn Phuket 5 *. In the reviews of 2013-14, one can often read that this hotel was considered the best on the coast. And the Royal Yacht Club is outdated - both physically and mentally. Therefore, it closed with the end of the tourist season of 2014. About 20 months was a complete reconstruction. The new hotel, under the guise of The Nai Harn Phuket 5 *, received its first visitors in early 2016.

Location

The southern coast of the island can be called quiet and calm, but it is still not a wilderness or a separation from the civilized world. There are a couple of shops, a fairly large selection of cafes. At lunchtime, the beach is visited by mackerel (traders of hot dishes, soups and salads). The two-kilometer Nai Horn beach itself stretches a long strip along a rocky cape. At its top is a Buddhist temple. On one side of the cape is the hotel Phuket Nai Harn, Sunsuri Phuket 5 * (Thailand) - on the other. In the middle, away from the sea, there is another hotel - All Seasons Nye Harn. And it's all.

At one edge of the bay there is still a pier for snow-white beautiful yachts. Phuket Airport will have to drive 55 kilometers. And if you miss civilization and want to walk along the meander Bangla Road in Patong, then you will have to overcome 25 km. Thus, it can be stated that the location of the hotel will appeal to families with children, people looking for a secluded and measured rest. Young people who travel to Phuket for fun, it is better to look for housing on the west coast of the island.

The Nai Harn Phuket 5 *: description of the beach and pool

A beautiful wide strip of pure white sand stretched for two kilometers. Part of Nai Harn Beach is reserved for hotel guests. Umbrellas and sunbeds on it are free for them. Beach towels are provided at the hotel. On the coast there is a free rental point for snorkelling equipment, you can also go kayaking or catamaran. A little further is the surf school, where for a fee you can take lessons from a professional instructor.

The pool at the hotel guests liked. It is also made with panoramic views of the bay, and swimming in it is a pleasure. The pool has a children's section and a relaxation area with shallow water. The solarium terrace is lined with sunbeds and umbrellas. The pool is always quiet and there are few people, since most tourists prefer to swim in the sea.

Services

The Nai Harn Phuket 5 * hotel (Thailand) has a spa center. It also has pools. Various types of massage, sauna, hammam are available. Those who love sports can attend free yoga classes or work out on simulators, rent a bike, play volleyball or soccer. Wedding ceremonies, celebrations or business meetings can be arranged upon request. For the latter, the hotel has several conference rooms. A shuttle service to the airport is available for a fee.

Reviews

The vast majority of vacationers enjoyed a stay at The Nai Harn Phuket 5 *. Reviews of tourists are like laudatory odes.
