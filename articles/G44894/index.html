The Irbis TX 22 tablet does not turn on - what should I do?

Any mobile device may fail over time. In order to understand why the tablet of this model does not turn on, you need to deal with the most common reasons.

  1. The battery is out of order. The first step is to check the charge level. If this is not the case, then it is worth inspecting the battery - if it is swollen.
  2. Memory card. Often, device health issues are caused by an external drive. In order to check this version, the tablet is simply turned on without it.
  3. Moisture In this situation, it is not recommended to turn on the gadget on your own, it is better to immediately contact a service center.
  4. Software. If the system files are violated, the tablet may not turn on. In this situation, you should try to reset the settings. If this does not help, you should seek the help of specialists.

It is important to understand that without certain knowledge, it is by no means recommended to disassemble the tablet yourself, as this can lead to a breakdown that cannot be repaired.
