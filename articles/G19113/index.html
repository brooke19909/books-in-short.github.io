Knitting needles from mohair: schemes for beginners

The stoles with mohair knitting needles are very beautiful and sophisticated. They lie lightly on their shoulders, combined with any clothing - a dress with a deep neckline, turtlenecks and even t-shirts. They perfectly warm their owner, at the same time transforming her image, helping him to become even brighter. Now, when long autumn and then winter days and nights are ahead, it's time to take up their knitting. Schemes you will find later in the article.

Palantine "Tenderness"

This stole with mohair knitting is easy to knit, even beginners will master the work. Picking up tools, they will be able to go from the birth of the first timid loop to the assembly of an amazing product that can be presented as a gift to mom, sister and even the boss so that it is more poloyal. Each woman will appreciate such a present and will be grateful to the craftswoman.





It is worth saying that the stole with brushes will be much more elegant. There is nothing difficult or supernatural in their creation. It is enough to take a small ball of the same yarn from which they were knitted, and a wide cardboard box (for example, from under the powder). Wrap threads on the product, trying to keep them evenly and slightly tight. Then crop them on one side (this is important!). Then take the hook and attach with it the threads to the scarf in a slender row.

Openwork palatine with brushes

The next model of stole with mohair knitting needles is perfect for strict business girls. For example, Mohair Delicate Nako, Angora De Luxe from Yarnart or Angora Gold Alize, they are well suited for creating a product. In addition, they are cheaper. Just the thing for beginners!

How to care for a knitted stole from mohair?

After you looked at the patterns of the stole from mohair with knitting needles and even created your first amazing product on them, try not to forget to look after it:

  1. In no case do not clean them dry, since the mohair is a thin thread, quickly wears out and deteriorates, the stole can lose its beautiful appearance. Wash it better if necessary with special products in warm water.
  2. After washing, the product is strictly forbidden to wring out - it can be deformed. To dry, lay it on a table or other smooth surface somewhere in a dark place, not far from the battery.
  3. The mohair stole must be combed to give the pile the right direction.
  4. To prevent mohair from slipping, the product should sometimes be soaked for 2-5 minutes in a solution of 10 liters of water, 2 tbsp. tablespoons of vodka, 3 tbsp. tablespoons of ammonia and 1 tbsp. tablespoons of turpentine.

All these rules will help preserve the beauty of the knitted stole. Knitting mohair for beginners is not so difficult to knit. As you can see, there are schemes, and they are very simple. Create with pleasure!
