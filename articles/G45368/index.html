Hotel "Bohemia", Anapa: overview, description, rooms and reviews of tourists

Of course, it is difficult to lure a modern tourist to Russian resorts. As soon as it became possible to travel the world, contemplate the beautiful and enjoy the excellent service, the Russians took advantage of it. More and more lovers of beach vacations can be seen in Turkey, Tunisia, some enjoy exotic in India and Thailand. But far from always an expensive vacation can afford the Russians. Moreover, some cannot leave the country or cannot draw up the necessary documents. But this is not a reason to deny yourself a vacation. In Russia, there are many resorts both on the Azov and the Black and Caspian Sea, but many have simply forgotten about them. Indeed, up to a certain point, the tourism industry in our country did not develop, all that could be obtained from leisure was the sun, sea and beach. Currently, the situation has changed radically: resort towns and resort areas are being actively built up, many excellent hotels are appearing, service is being improved, infrastructure is being developed, beaches are being equipped - all this creates excellent conditions for relaxing in the country. Tourists do not need to issue a passport, and due to the advantageous offers of airlines and tour operators, they manage to save on their sea voyages. All lovers of beach holidays, especially families with young children, will like to relax in Anapa. It is here that we propose to go.





About the resort

Anapa - the famous resort of the Krasnodar Territory, ideal for a beach holiday. Anapa is increasingly choosing families with young children. Warm, fine sand, a gentle entry into the water, a shallow depth of sea water even at a distance of 10-15 meters from the shore, excellent infrastructure and plenty of entertainment for children are what all modern parents will appreciate. Of course, relaxation and its quality largely depend on living conditions. Anapa offers to stay in a luxury five-star hotel or settle in the private sector away from city noise. We want to bring to the attention of potential tourists and vacationers the Bohemia Hotel (Anapa), which received flattering reviews from vacationers. So what is his secret to success? Can a three-star hotel compete with foreign options?

About hotel

Hotel "Bohemia" (Anapa) is a great place to relax for all those who want to fully enjoy a beach holiday. Comfortable rooms, first-class service, polite and friendly staff - all this will create the necessary conditions for relaxation.

A good location is what almost all reviews of the Bohemia Hotel (Anapa) say. Indeed, for the resorts of the Krasnodar Territory, being on the first coastline, that is, literally on the coast, is a rarity. At the same time, the hotel is located near the center of the resort town, so all vacationers will be able to take advantage of the developed infrastructure. Within walking distance are the largest shops, a bus station, a central market, a call center, as well as local attractions and entertainment.

The description provided by the hotel administration and tourist portals emphasizes the possibility of checking into one of the 18 comfortable rooms. Yes, the hotel is small, but many tourists note calm and silence, which is absolutely necessary on vacation. Check-in takes place within 10-15 minutes, even if tourists arrive somewhat earlier than the scheduled time.

The price includes breakfast buffet. They are held in the hotel restaurant. Breakfast does not differ in variety, usually porridge, meat and cheese slices, cottage cheese, pancakes, cheesecakes, as well as tea and aromatic coffee. Nobody will certainly remain hungry. Service

Hotel "Bohemia" 3 * (Anapa) offers vacationers a good service. Rooms are cleaned daily, towels and bed linen are changed as needed. Hotel guests note the quality of cleaning: all surfaces are wiped, the room is ventilated, plumbing is cleaned, beds are made. That is why the rooms are always clean, fresh and comfortable. The staff is cleaned during the absence of vacationers, so nothing will disturb their peace and measured rest.

Many potential vacationers who are just considering the Bohemia Hotel (Anapa) are interested in the issue of Internet access on its territory. Unfortunately, a wireless high-speed internet access point exists only in the hotel restaurant.

At the hotel reception, all vacationers will be able to get acquainted with the excursion program, get a detailed map of the resort, and also use the shuttle to the station or airport. For an additional fee, tourists can also use the laundry services. If you read the reviews of real vacationers, in almost all of them you can find a mention of the courtesy and responsiveness of the hotel staff. Entertainment and sports

There is no entertainment on site, but everything that tourists need is literally within walking distance. Be sure to visit the local water park, cinema center. Fans of nightlife will surely appreciate the best clubs on the coast, visit a beach disco.

Sea and beach

The nearest sandy beach is just 200 meters away. A wide coastline, fine sand, a gentle entry into the water and a clear sea - this is what all vacationers who choose the Bohemia Hotel (Anapa) can count on for their stay. The beach is equipped with sunbeds and umbrellas, you can rent them for an additional fee. Here, on the beach, there is enough entertainment for children and adults, rental of water sports and sports equipment is open. In season, a volleyball net is pulled on the beach and spectacular competitions are held between vacationers.





Prices

Of course, all tourists are interested in what pricing policy the Anapa resort adheres to. Hotel "Bohemia", the price of accommodation in which depends primarily on the category of rooms, quite affordable. The cost of a daily stay in a standard room starts at 1300 rubles, a double room with a balcony will cost a little more - at 2200 rubles. Accommodation in a suite starts at 3,000 rubles. For an additional fee, hotel staff will decorate the room with champagne, rose petals and candles - most often this design is ordered by the newlyweds.

To summarize

So, the 3 * Bohemia Hotel in Anapa is a great place to relax. Occupying an advantageous position, the hotel can offer comfortable rooms and excellent service. If you are just going to rest and are in the process of choosing the best option, pay attention to this hotel. Enjoy your stay!
