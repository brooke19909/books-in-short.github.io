Tectonic shift

The definition of this phenomenon has been given for a long time and precisely: it is a break of a single solid continental plate located under the earth's crust. What threatens humanity with tectonic plate faults? The scenario is this: one, even a small fault, will cover the planet with a chain reaction. The melted glaciers will release the plates from pressure with their enormous mass, the earth's crust will rise, ocean water will rush into the depths of the faults. Magma under the bark is hot - about one thousand two hundred degrees Celsius. Steam with basalt dust and gas will be with great force and everywhere will be thrown out of the ground. Showers will begin - unprecedented, akin to the flood. Volcanoes will wake up - all to one. After that, indescribable tsunamis will sweep away everything from the face of the planet. There is enough time for the whole alignment from the beginning of the fault to the eruptions of volcanoes, you can even run away if you find where.
