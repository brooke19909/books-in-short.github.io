Guest house "Rainbow", Kabardinka: photos and reviews. Holidays on the Black Sea

The village of Kabardinka is one of the most popular resorts of the Black Sea coast. This small village is located on the very shore, between the cities of Gelendzhik and Novorossiysk. The secret of the demand for this resort lies primarily in its fairly well-developed tourist infrastructure. For example, inexpensive and quite tolerable housing in the village can be rented very easily. Many hotels offer good service at this resort to tourists, including the Raduga guest house. Kabardinka - the village, among other things, is also very well-groomed. Therefore, it's really nice to relax here.

general description

The guest house "Rainbow" (Kabardinka) is a three-story building of rather interesting architecture with a large spiral staircase, twined with vines. The hotel courtyard is tiled and landscaped, and therefore looks quite attractive. In addition to swimming pools, on-site parking is available for a fee.





The hotel rooms are for non-smokers only. Pets are not allowed in the hotel. In addition to the main floors, the hotel building has an attic. Comfortable rooms are also available here. The attic also has a large sun terrace. From it, among other things, there are also truly wonderful views of the sea and the city.

Room facilities

Equipped all the hotel rooms are very good. In triple and quadruple standards, as well as in the apartments there are showers. In addition to beds, standard rooms include cushions, tables and chairs, wardrobes, refrigerators and a TV. All rooms are air-conditioned. Most rooms have balconies. Of course, there are mirrors in the hotel rooms. If necessary, additional rooms can be booked in the hotel rooms.
