Hotel Djerba Holiday Beach (Tunisia / Djerba): photos and reviews of tourists

Djerba Holiday Beach is a hotel complex on the Tunisian island of Djerba in a resort area near the town of Midoun. To the city itself - about twelve kilometers. This is the southeastern part of the island. The hotel stands directly on the sea and can safely position itself as a beach. The hotel belongs to the LTI network with German management. Therefore, these three letters used to even appear in the name of the hotel. The LTI line is known worldwide for its high standards of service. It includes only five and four star hotels. But each of them has an individual style and features inherent in the country where it is built. No exception and "Djerba Holiday Beach." Reviews of tourists about this hotel we will consider below.



Djerba, Midoun

The island on which Djerba Holiday Beach (Tunisia) is located is the warmest resort in the country. It is always several degrees hotter here. Therefore, the season on it lasts a little longer. The island is famous for its excellent beaches with fine and white sand, like flour, as well as good quality hotels. French tourists love these places, and domestic travelers say: on Djerba it is immediately clear that Tunisia was a European colony. Midoun is the second most important tourist area on the island after Houmt Souk. Like any similar resort, it is famous for its beautiful hotels, promenade, water activities, bars, nightclubs - in a word, well-developed infrastructure. True, the city by whose name it was named represents a sharp contrast with the coastal areas, and here you can really see the real oriental flavor of Tunisia. Travelers are advised to visit not only local mosques and markets, but the Lal Khadriya historical museum, and the animalia crocodile farm.



How to get there

The airport closest to the Djerba Holiday Beach hotel, from where you can fly from Moscow or other Russian cities by regular flights, is in Monastir. This is an international hub named after Habib Bourguiba. The local Zarsis airport serves mainly domestic flights. Charters from European countries often fly there. Russian airlines also launch flights with organized groups there in season. Yes, and go from there to the hotel only twenty-five minutes to thirty, so you will not spend much time on the transfer. Near the hotel there are several shops, casinos and bars.

Rooms

All rooms at the Djerba Holiday Beach Hotel have balconies, individual air conditioning. Room types are different - there are standards, family rooms and suites. From many windows you can see the sea. Tourists claim that the rooms with garden and pool views are also very good. Each room has a plasma TV with satellite or cable channels. There are two Russian programs. The rooms are clean, comfortable, the beds are comfortable, and leather upholstery is at the head. There is a sofa. In the corridor there is a large closet, with shelves and many hangers. In the shower there is a real bathroom, hairdryer, large mirrors. Everywhere is spacious and comfortable. There are safes in the rooms, but they are paid and without combination locks (locked with a key). By the way, if you open the windows, the air conditioning in the room automatically stops working. A refrigerator is available for a fee. Clean and change towels daily. There are chairs with tables on the balconies, but no clothesline. If you leave a tip, twist the swans from the towels.

Rest and treatment at sea

The hotel has its own beach. Go there for about three minutes around the hotel, and then through a small road where almost no one drives. Umbrellas, sun loungers, sun loungers and other accessories of sea recreation can be used for free. The entrance to the water is very gentle. If you want depth, you need to swim away. On the beach there is a nice fine sand of white color. He does not repent even in the heat. Here you can not only indulge in relaxation and "seal" rest, but also engage in water sports, in particular, ride a sea scooter. Sometimes algae takes to the beach, but they are immediately removed. But mostly the water is clear. Beach towels are issued on bail - ten dinars. At the same time, tourists receive two receipts, blue and white. The first must be kept until the end of the vacation, because without it they do not return the deposit. And on a white receipt, towels are being changed. On the shore there is a bar that distributes free Internet, which, according to tourists, works better than on the territory of the hotel. In the LTI Djerba Holiday Beach hotel itself there are three swimming pools - a large one, in the air, for adults, a children's paddling pool and an indoor spa. There, by the way, you can take a course of the famous Tunisian thalassotherapy. Tourists leave very good reviews about spa specialists. The main artificial pond is very beautiful, surrounded by palm trees. Around him there are many sun loungers, and in the case of waves on the sea or other bad weather, he will always help out tourists who like to swim.
