Beautiful handmade bead beads: description, schemes

DIY jewelry has not gone out of fashion for several years. Bright, stylish and light bead beads - this is an original decoration for the summer. To make them will require several hours of work. You can make them with your own hands, even without the skills of beadwork. Try to replenish your collection of jewelry with a stylish accessory.

Materials for work

Regardless of which version of the beads will be made, beads will be needed for work. You can choose any size that is more suitable for the conceived product.

Also, for some models you will need beads, chipped stones, bugles. All this can be found in needlework stores or take advantage of supplies from the needlework box. To make a bead you will need a nylon thread or fishing line.

To complete the work, you need accessories: locks, clamps, fasteners. They are also available at needlework stores. If it is not possible to purchase ready-made ones, you can be smart and resourceful, and close the fastening places with what is available: ribbons, pieces of leather or take accessories from your old jewelry.

Easiest option

An option that even a child can cope with is beadwork made of beads and beads. You can take both colorful material and one shade. Beads are strung on the thread, stones or beads are periodically added in a chaotic manner. It all depends on the imagination and desire of the needlewoman.

If the bead beads are selected from the same material and the same beads, then you need to think through the frequency is low, and follow the pattern.

To make the product beautiful, you need to make several tiers. Each should be slightly longer than the previous one by an equal number of centimeters. The shortest row should be slightly larger than the circumference of the neck. Their number depends on the wishes. Next, you need to connect the ends of each tier with one node and fix the lock. Hide the tails of the fishing line under the beads and secure. You can do without a lock at all, making long beads closed in a ring. In this case, you need to string at least 1 m beads.

Air

Very impressive and original look beads-air. To make them, you will need beads of different sizes and shades, as well as beads. The remains of beads available in the house can go for this decoration. For work, you will need a fishing line and a hook No. 1.5-2.

The length of the product can be made any as desired, but not less than 50 cm. The product, consisting of 30-50 rows, looks beautiful. The set is conducted on one continuous line. The length of the strung beads can be calculated by the formula: 1 m. Low = 2.1 m. Air filament. To get 40 rows, you will need about 9.5 m. Low and 20 m. Fishing line.

If the beads are of different colors and caliber, then it is better to mix in one container. String in the order in which they come across the thread. So that the work does not get confused, you need to immediately reel the fishing line into a ball.

When all the beads are dialed, you can begin to knit the beads from the beads for beginners. To retreat from the edge of 15 cm and make the first loop, knit 8-10 air loops. Then move the bead and loop, interweaving the beads. Alternate a loop with a bead with an empty loop. And so on until the end. At the end, again knit a chain of fishing line. To prevent a long thread from tangling, it should be wound into a skein.

From a thick cardboard cut a rectangle whose length is equal to the length of the future product (50 cm). Using a safety pin, fasten the beginning of the chain to the edge of the workpiece. Wrap in dense, even rows to the end of the chain. Secure the last loop with a pin.

Pass a piece of fishing line of 20 cm into the needle, pass it under all rows on one side of the workpiece, without passing a single thread. Tighten and tie tightly. Attach the lock. On the other hand, do the same. Fasten the ends of the fishing line and cut it off. This is how you can make bead beads simply and quickly. It takes a small amount of time.

Woven necklace

Beadwork made of beads looks very beautiful with their own hands. The master class is given below. Make them not difficult. String on nylon string 1 m long beads. Such threads will need 10 pieces.

Fold the prepared threads together and tie exactly in the middle. Fix the center with a needle on a wooden surface, tablecloth or foam sheet. Divide into three parts and braid the usual three-row braid. Connect the ends together and secure with a knot. Attach a clasp or ribbon to tie.

You can weave a five-row braid, then you get a completely different look. And you can tie one of the flat knots of macrame - a sort of marine knot made of beads.

In this case, it is better to use one color of the material. This necklace looks very stylish, you can make a jewelry for any item from the wardrobe.

Layered beads

Multilayer beaded beads look very richly. First you need to decide on the length and size of the future jewelry. The best option is 30-50 threads. A larger number will be heavy in the sock, and a smaller one will not look so spectacular.

For a 50-cm necklace 50 cm long, you will need 25 m of kapron thread. She lays down better than the fishing line. String the beads with a needle. The assembly of the product is similar to the assembly of "air" on a cardboard rectangle. The ends are also pulled together and the lock is attached.

To assemble such beads, you can make a special board. Nail the hook at one end, and screw another hook or screw at the desired distance. Winding bead thread between these stands. Beads, like the Egyptian Queen Cleopatra, are ready.

Beadwork

Needleworkers who own the basics of beadwork can afford lace beads. Even the simplest schemes look original with the right selection of materials.

You can try to make a simple chain of rhombuses. Measure the line at least a meter long. String 4 beads, stretch the opposite end of the fishing line through the last to make a ring. To put 2 beads on one end of the fishing line, 1 bead on the other. Thread the thread into the second bead. It turns out another link in the chain. So continue to the end of the row to the desired length. If you want to make several rows of bead beads with your own hands, the diagram of which is described, then you need to continue as well, only instead of picking one bead you need to thread the thread into the protruding bead of the previous row. Beads with pendants

Simple handmade bead and bead beads look good in combination with pendants. String a row on a fishing line of the desired length, close it into a ring. You can weave a chain of rhombuses or use another simple pattern. The pendant can be taken ready-made or made from improvised materials.

You can make a brush from small beads. For a product 6 cm long you need to string 12 cm low per thread. Make at least 15 such segments. The ends to hide. Fold all the threads together, align the edges. Put a bead of 1 cm in diameter or a dense ball of cotton under the center of the bead bundle and secure it with a fishing line under the bead, grasping it from all sides. So the brush turns out. It remains to attach it to the beads with the help of a bead ring or special accessories.

Long Pendant Beads

Beads with pendants look stylish. Their manufacture requires some skill and accuracy. Such products are made according to the size of the neck circumference. Weave a chain or string a row of beads on a thread. Fasten the lock. Find the center of the necklace and attach the thread. String the beads and beads for the pendant. You can combine different shapes and colors of the material for originality. At the end, unfold the needle and thread it through the entire suspension, skip only the most extreme bead. Thread the needle through one or more base beads. The frequency of weaving depends on the ideas of the craftswoman. It can be done through each bead for a dense pattern or after several, then the suspension will be rarer. Repeat to the other side from the center.

In this option, you can make a beautiful pattern. Here you need to strictly adhere to the scheme, gaining the right amount of beads of each color. As a basis, you can take patterns for cross-stitch. In this case, each cell corresponds to beads. No need to choose large drawings.

You can make bead beads with your own hands in different ways. And for this, it is not necessary to be a guru in needlework. You just need to stock up on materials for work and patience. Such decorations are ideal for hot summer days. In addition, homemade beads are original and unique.
