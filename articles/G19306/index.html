How to knit footprints: methods and tips

With the onset of cold weather, many people think about how to warm their feet for themselves and their loved ones. In this regard, knitters often think about how to knit footprints. This is a universal item: at home they can easily replace slippers, and if you put them on in winter shoes, no frost will be scary. Tracks are knitted very quickly - even not too experienced knitters will create a pair of lovely slippers over the evening. With the right choice of model and yarn, such shoes are suitable not only for women, but also for men and children.

Knitting footprints



There are many options for knitting footprints. The appearance and shape of them depends on the devices used. There are two main ways to knit footprints with knitting needles.

For beginner needlewomen, you can advise the option of knitting with two knitting needles. This method is the easiest to execute, but the tracks will be with a seam on the heel. In this case, knitting begins with the upper front and side parts, that is, the size of the set of rows should correspond to the circumference of the legs. To understand how many loops to dial, it is best to pre-knit the sample and calculate the density of knitting. Any pattern can be chosen - in accordance with the tastes and skills of the knitter.





In addition, the tracks can be crocheted. Today you can find quite a lot of information on how to knit footprints. For beginners, there are a large number of workshops. In general terms, there are two main options for crocheting footprints. The second option is how to knit footprints - round on four knitting needles. In this case, knitting is similar to working on socks. The only difference is the short top.

At the first, the sole is made first. To do this, round the chain of air loops knit columns without a crochet. On turns, three loops are added. Next, the top is knitted - a straight canvas with decreases at the beginning of each row. At the end of the work, the parts are connected with single crochet columns.

In the second option, the tracks are seamless. The sole is knitted, as in the first case. Further, the increases cease, due to which a rise is formed and the upper part of the track is tied.

Tips for Knitters

If you have certain skills, you can make the tracks more original. For example, taking the remains of yarn, which usually accumulate at needlewomen, you can knit striped slippers. It is important to ensure that the strips are symmetrical. In addition, you can knit traces of melange yarn.

Ready-made slippers can be decorated with pompons, knitted flowers, embroidery, and beads.





To prevent stretching of the upper edge, it is crocheted with a "crustacean step" or single crochet. The edge looks original if knit the crochet columns alternately in the first, second and third row with a contrasting thread. It is important not to allow the thread to be pulled together when tying the finish, otherwise the traces will be difficult to put on.

To provide additional strength, the heel, and preferably the entire sole, should be tied in two threads. Thanks to this, slippers will last longer.

If you wish, after a short workout you can knit original and beautiful tracks. Many needlewomen are looking for new options for how to knit footprints. Slippers in the form of sneakers look interesting, for example.
