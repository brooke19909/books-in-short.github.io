"Golden Vine" (Gelendzhik, Herzen St., 5): description, photo, guest reviews

One of the most popular places of pilgrimage for Russians seeking to relax in their homeland is Gelendzhik. Here near the sea there are guest houses, cottages and mini-hotels that can satisfy all tastes and wishes. At the same time, the conditions created in these private institutions are by no means inferior to the hotel service in terms of comfort. One of the most popular accommodation options is the guest house "Golden Vine" (Gelendzhik, Herzen St. 5).





About the resort

Tourists are always interested in future conditions of rest. Gelendzhik resort is located on the Black Sea coast, stretching for 100 km from Arkhipo-Osipovka to Kabardinka. The healing climate of Gelendzhik smoothly transitions from subtropical to temperate. In this he resembles the Crimean. The special charm of the resort, according to many vacationers, is the abundance of greenery on its territory - 87% of the area is occupied by forests.

In Gelendzhik, you can spend your holidays with maximum comfort, because for a low price the most interesting entertainments are available here: boating, a jet ski or a catamaran, a hike to Mount Markhot.





Where to stay at this resort? Numerous reviews recommend accommodation in the guest house "Golden Vine" (Gelendzhik) on the street. Herzen, 5. There are all conditions for a hassle-free quality vacation.

Guest Impressions
