Elysium 5 *. Holidays in Cyprus. Hotel Elysium, Cyprus - photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Those who appreciate the comfortable conditions of rest, those who are very scrupulous about the level of service and want to be surrounded only by beautiful and high-quality items both in everyday life and during their holidays, prefer to stay exclusively in deluxe hotels, over which signs are proud five stars glow. This category also includes Elysium (Cyprus), which is considered one of the best hotels in Paphos.



Distinctive features of 5 * hotels

According to the criteria of the world classification of hotels and hotel complexes, 5 * seaside hotels should be located on the first beach line and have their own beach. They must have the following infrastructure:

  • gym and spa (with sauna and massage room);
  • pools (it is desirable, both open, and indoor, and children's, and adult);
  • lobby and snack bar;
  • several chic restaurants with a large selection of dishes not only local but also European cuisine;
  • a large sports area, including tennis courts, golf, basketball, volleyball, indoor soccer, etc.

In addition, many five-star hotels belong to the global hotel brands, and they usually order household items (bathrobes, slippers, hygiene products, etc.) to famous brands. Hotel Elysium (Cyprus) belongs to the global hotel chain Great Hotels Of The World. The administration orders the household items to the Italian brand Bulgari.

Hotel "Elysium 5 *"

On Paphos - the most elite Cypriot resort - the best five-star hotels of the island are concentrated. However, the hotel complex Elysium 5 * can be considered the most advanced and, as a consequence, the most expensive of them. Although, you must admit, not always a high price speaks about quality. However, when it comes to this gigantic hotel complex, hardly anyone will doubt the quality of its services.



general characteristics

In this fashionable hotel, tourists will find a combination of style and luxury, comfort and reliability, excellent service and a helpful attitude, care and hospitality. It offers both comfortable apartments and chic villas. The days spent in this hotel remain in the memory of each of the vacationers for a long time. Especially like here the newlyweds who spend their honeymoon in the walls of the Elysium 5 * hotel. After all, this attitude of tourists is special here. For example, upon crossing the threshold of their chic room, the newlyweds immediately see a beautifully made bed, a bottle of champagne and a vase of fruit and chocolate, candles, etc. Caring staff tries to turn every day of their stay at the hotel into a real holiday. By the way, you can also organize a wedding here. At the same time, the administration of Elysium Hotel 5 * will take care of all the arrangements for holding this celebration.



Location

This hotel complex is located on the first beach line of Paphos, 30 kilometers from the city airport, where you can fly by direct regular or charter flight from Moscow and other major cities of Russia. The hotel administration meets its guests right at the airport terminal and delivers luxury cars to the Elysium hotel. Paphos is an elite resort, and therefore such an attitude towards the guests here is in the order of things. In addition, this ancient city is the center of various historical monuments. So, not far from the hotel (25 km) are the Tombs of the Greek kings - one of the main attractions of the city.

Of the modern facilities near the complex are various shops, bank branches and shopping centers. Paphos is a small town. The main population of the city totals no more than 40 thousand people. Nevertheless, the infrastructure is well developed here, and the city authorities made sure that the guests of the city, and they mainly belong to the category of wealthy people (VIP), did not experience any inconvenience in Paphos. The administration of the Elysium 5 * hotel, in turn, is taking all steps to provide its guests with the highest quality service.



Number of rooms

It should be noted right away that the hotel consists only of suites that have balconies with sea views. However, there are rooms that are classified as superior. These are luxurious royal apartments, as well as luxury villas with private gardens and pools. All 250 rooms of the Elysium Hotel are adapted for families with children. The interior uses high-quality natural materials, elegant decor elements that give the premises a luxurious look. The cost of living in a hotel, depending on the category of the room, varies from 10,000 to 15,000 thousand rubles per day. True, expensive? That is the reality. Holidays in Cyprus (prices for accommodation and hotel services) are very expensive. However, this does not affect the island's popularity as the best beach resort in the whole Mediterranean.

Entertainment

Hotel Elysium is mainly designed for a respectable and quiet holiday. Therefore, on its territory you can hardly find a lot of noisy entertainment. Of course, in the evenings there is a disco and animation is arranged, but those tourists who cannot imagine their vacation without loud fun later complain about it in their reviews. The situation is aggravated by the fact that this hotel is located far from the city center, where you can have fun. But there is a wonderful library.





Sports enthusiasts can work out in the gym, play tennis, do aerobics, etc. And the spa center operating at the hotel will provide everyone with a full range of spa and beauty treatments. As for children's entertainment, there are not so many of them - a club and a pool. Therefore, kids here are often uninteresting and bored. This can also be read in the reviews of dissatisfied tourists from Russia, who unanimously reiterate that this hotel, and Paphos itself, is not at all suitable for children

Beaches

The beach of the Elysium Hotel is a weak link in this luxury hotel. It is very tiny and uncomfortable, so most vacationers prefer to use the luxurious pool. However, the beach is well equipped, and therefore those who can not imagine a vacation without the sea may well, having closed their eyes not some inconvenience, use it. There are sun loungers, umbrellas, a bridge for safe entry into the sea, various water activities: scooter, diving, water skiing, banana, parachute, catamaran, etc.



Conclusion

Rest in a five-star hotel Elysium 5 * will give tourists a lot of positive emotions and impressions. However, people with an active position, as well as families with children, are better off choosing another hotel that is more suitable for this.
