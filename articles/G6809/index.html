Koni Village Hotel 3 * (Greece, Stalis)

Crete is a Greek island located at the crossroads of three parts of the world: Europe, Asia and Africa. Here, vacationers enjoy pristine golden beaches, excursions, comfort and excellent service.

The island has many diverse hotels. More budget option - Koni Village Hotel 3 *. Stalis is the nearest beach from the hotel, which is located in the resort village of Stalida.

Address and Location

The hotel is located at: Main Road, 70007, Stalis, Greece. From the hotel to the center walk about 1 km. There is also the famous Malia Strip. The nearest airport is Heraklion Nikos Kazantzakis Airport, 24 km from Koni Village Hotel 3 *. There is another airport - Sitia Public Airoport, but it is located 60.3 km from the hotel.

From the closest attractions, vacationers can visit: the Likhnostatis folk art museum, an aquarium, an aqua park, a golf club, a dinosaur park, a saltwater aquarium, and much more.

Terms of Accommodation

Vacationers can safely stop by the Koni Village Hotel 3 * in Greece on Crete starting at 14:00. Check-out is mandatory until 12:00, as maids must clean the room by the arrival of the following guests. Prepayment and cancellation policies vary by room.

Guests can come to rest with children of any age. A child under 2 years old has the right to free accommodation. A crib is provided, but at the request of guests. All children under 6 years old can stay for free on extra beds. However, only one extra bed is allowed per room. Unfortunately, pets are strictly prohibited by the hotel.

At the reception, only three types of payment cards are accepted:

  1. American Express
  2. Visa
  3. MasterCard.

At the entrance to the hotel you must provide exactly the card that was used at the time of booking.

Description of rooms

The guests are offered comfortable, clean and modern apartments, which have everything necessary for living. The hotel has a variety of rooms:

  1. Single room (28 sq. M.) With a single bed.
  2. Double room measuring 28 square meters. m, with one or two beds. Vacationers can choose for themselves.
  3. 28 sqm Double Room m. It has one large bed and one single. These rooms are for two adults and one child.
  4. Three types of family-type rooms. Their area is 42 square meters. m. The difference between the rooms is in the number and size of the beds.

As a rule, guests are given rooms with a balcony and air conditioning.

In addition, amenities include: electric kettle, mini fridge, satellite TV, bathtub (shower) with toiletries, safe, telephone, radio, work desk, seating area, bathrobe, slippers. If there is a lack of an item, you can always call the reception and order.

Prices

Of course, a single room is the cheapest and is designed for one person. Its cost per day is about 3000 rubles. Breakfast is included in this price.

For double rooms, the cost varies from 4200 to 6200 rubles. The price depends on the location of the apartment and the number of beds.

The cost of family rooms varies from 4,500 to 8,500 rubles. Breakfast is included in this price. The bonus of these rooms is that no prepayment is required at time of booking. You can pay on the spot on the day of arrival.

Nutrition

The hotel Koni Village Hotel 3 * in Greece has a restaurant serving Greek and Mediterranean dishes. In addition to choosing the menu, there is also a buffet, which is very convenient for vacationers. If the guests are not attracted to the kitchen in the hotel, you can always find something suitable outside. Indeed, in the district there are a large number of restaurants that cook a variety of dishes:

  • European
  • Italian
  • Russians
  • British
  • international;
  • Mexican
  • Chinese
  • Indian
  • Asian

In addition, the restaurants serve unusual alcoholic cocktails, real coffee, a variety of overseas teas, etc.

Territory

Guests can relax on one of the terraces or in the garden. For sports enthusiasts, there are: a swimming pool, tennis court, billiards and darts. Bicycles can also be rented, but they are charged separately. For young guests, the hotel has a games room and a playground.

A large outdoor pool or massage parlor will help you to relax and relax.

The reception offers a wide variety of services, but some of them are paid separately and are not included in the price.

Service

As for the service, the Koni Village Hotel Apartments 3 * has a concierge who can help solve almost any issue related to accommodation.

Maids clean the rooms every two days. Bed linen and towels are changed. However, if guests wish, the room will be cleaned daily, but the hotel must be notified in advance about this service.

In addition, the hotel has the following services:

  • transfer from and to the airport;
  • vending machine with food;
  • car rental;
  • laundry.

The listed services are paid separately.

The staff speaks several languages: German, Greek, English, French, Russian. Therefore, vacationers from Russia can easily communicate with the staff.

Free Wi-Fi and private parking are available on site (reservation is not needed). There is also secure but paid parking.

Vacation with children

Of course, many guests come to rest with children. Unfortunately no babysitting services are available at this hotel. But there is a small children's pool, a playroom for toddlers and a playground located near the terrace. Parents can relax and watch the baby.

Outside the hotel there is also a lot of entertainment where children will be interested. This is a variety of sites, water park, oceanarium, dolphinarium, zoo and more. However, you have to go to the center, which is not very far from the hotel.
