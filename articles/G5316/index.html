Island countries - awesome vacations all year round

Finally, the long-awaited spring has come. You can no longer be afraid of evil frosts and smile at the gentle rays of the sun. However, even on the coldest days, you can truly enjoy warm days somewhere on the coast.

The island countries surrounded by azure waters of oceans, seas and straits on all sides have gained immense popularity. Most of them boast stable mild and hot weather all year round. It is this factor that has the main influence when choosing a resort by tourists for an excellent holiday.





Fantastic island countries, spread out in the pirate waters of the Caribbean , are happy to give a piece of their magnificence and natural harmony to a traveler yearning for the dullness and dampness of a big city. These countries include Cuba, Barbados, Jamaica, Bahamas, Antigua, Barbuda and others. The stunning nature, animal and plant diversity presented at each of these resorts, the magnificent romantic atmosphere and the bright sun reflected in the clear waters of the sea - what else is needed for an excellent and memorable vacation?

The island countries, which include Cyprus and the Maldives, will allow you to enjoy a great vacation for every taste. Moreover, these resorts are similar to each other only on one principle - they are excellent for relaxing at any time of the year. Otherwise, there are a huge number of differences between them. Cyprus offers tourists not only lazy entertainment on the golden or black volcanic sand - in winter you can enjoy plenty of skiing and snowboarding on the island. The Maldives - the atolls of the Indian Ocean - are excellent for those who want to spend an unforgettable vacation, enjoying the beauty of the spicy waters that accumulate in the lagoons of each island of the archipelago.





The island countries of the world include more than 45 states. Of these, the largest number is concentrated in Oceania and Asia. The most famous resorts are Fiji, Taiwan and the already mentioned above Cyprus and the Maldives. These countries will not disappoint travelers in winter or summer.

Fiji is an archipelago whose name resembles a bright, juicy and ripe fruit flowing out of juice. The most interesting thing is that under such a sweet name hides a turbulent cannibal past. Here passengers were afraid to land even accidentally wandering ships, because the result could be their eating by local barbarian tribes.

As if trying to make amends for his unpleasant past, now Fiji, like many other island countries, is a haven of peace and comfort for the world, offering travelers a wonderful vacation. Lovers of serenity and stunning silence flock here, like streams.





The island countries of Europe are represented by only five states, but everyone knows their names - Great Britain, Iceland, Ireland, Malta and Denmark.
