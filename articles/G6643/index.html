Hotels in Yerevan: addresses, descriptions, reviews and photos

Yerevan - the capital of the Republic of Armenia - is one of the oldest capitals in the world. According to the cuneiform inscription, which was left by the king of Argishti II, Yerevan (Erebuni) was founded in 782 BC. e., 30 years before the creation of Romulus and Remus, "the eternal city of the world" - Rome. The ruins of the ancient city-fortress are located in the southern part of the capital on a high hill towering above modern quarters. Yerevan, and Armenia as a whole, even in Soviet times attracted the attention of tourists. The infrastructure here is well developed, there are many restaurants, museums, souvenir shops. Guest reviews suggest that everyone can choose the option that matches the wallet and preferences. Hotels in Yerevan offer a more organized form of relaxation.
