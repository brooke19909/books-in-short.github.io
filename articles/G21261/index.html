Cut and sew capes

The easiest of dog clothes to make is a cloak. Sewn from raincoat fabric, it will protect the animal from rain. In cooler weather, warmer clothes for the toy terrier will be required. With your own hands, wrap patterns can be done quite simply. Having built the drawing by the standards, the craftswoman imposes it on a fabric folded in half so that the upper straight pattern is directed along the fold line of the material.
