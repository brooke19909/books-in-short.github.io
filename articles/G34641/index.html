Hotels in Stavropol: rating, addresses, room selection, quality of service, additional services, reviews of visitors and customers

Many people know that Stavropol is a "city of the cross", what is so remarkable about this southern corner of Russia? Spacious squares with historical monuments, sculptures and walk alleys will tell you the answer. Since 2013, Stavropol three times took first place in the all-Russian competition for the title of the most comfortable settlement among the capitals. The monument of the guardian angel holding the cross in the center of Alexander Square, Kazan Cathedral, the spring of Seraphim of Sarov, the Fortress Wall, the Tiflis Gate, the cultural life of the townspeople, beautiful fountains, green areas in and around the city - everyone will find something interesting here.

sights

Leisure during your stay can be devoted to exploring many places, starting with parks, for example, Victory Park, where you can have fun on the rides and just walk along the alleys. The Eternal Glory Memorial is striking in its grandeur. Alexandrovskaya and Cathedral Square, Fortress Mountain and the German Bridge, built back in Tsarist times, may interest tourists. The outskirts of the city have healing mud lakes (Petrovsky and Krasnogvardeisky districts).

Cultural life

Academic Drama Theater is located on Lenin Avenue. Many who have visited this beautiful cultural center admire the high professionalism of the actors and their mesmerizing game. The theater is highly regarded outside its city and goes on tour, for example, in the capital. On 135 Dzerzhinsky Street, you will find the oldest state museum of local lore, transformed into a historical-cultural and natural-landscape museum-reserve, striking in its size and significant collections, representing not only the region, but also the entire North Caucasus.
