Zhuravlevka, Kharkov - a place for active summer holidays

In one of the largest megacities of Ukraine there is one wonderful place where you can enjoy nature and have fun in the air without leaving the city limits - this is Zhuravlyovka beach (Kharkov). There is everything you need for a wonderful holiday: sports grounds, a children's town and much more. Locals also like to come here in order to gather a large group of friends on the river bank and cook kebabs. Therefore, this district, located in the city center, is considered one of the most popular places among Kharkiv for spending their summer active leisure. We learn a lot of interesting things about this beach from the article below.

Description

A very picturesque and cozy place called "Crane". Kharkov can rightfully be proud of it. It is located by the river near a pine forest and has two beaches, but only one of them is paid, and the second does not. On the free one there is absolutely no infrastructure for a pleasant pastime, but only in the bathing season a nurse and a rescue team are on duty daily.



Therefore, all citizens prefer to visit the paid beach, where the entertainment complex "Mill" (Zhuravlevka, Kharkov) is located. The photos taken in the territory where this recreation area is located show that there is a shower, toilet, storage rooms, inflatable slides by the river, a trampoline, a children's town and much more for a varied and interesting holiday.

Many visiting tourists like Zhuravlevka beach (Kharkov). Locals are also delighted with him. They love to come with all families. Parents have the opportunity to relax, while their child rides on a swing or has fun with animators working in the complex. In addition, here you can celebrate any special occasion, anniversary or wedding in a cozy cafe or in the gazebos that can accommodate up to eighty people. Guests coming here from other cities may not have to worry about where to stay. A comfortable hotel was built near all the attractions and attractions. It works around the clock and has luxury apartments.

Beach vacation

Zhuravlevka beach (Kharkov), due to its location, is becoming a very popular place, especially in the summer. Here you can have a great time by the river and enjoy absolutely safe swimming. On the spacious territory of Zhuravlyovka beach, which Kharkov has at its disposal, there is also a large inflatable slide designed for an adult audience, a diving board for diving, and spacious grounds for playing tennis, volleyball or football. Comfortable leisure activities are also facilitated by umbrellas, deck chairs, benches located on the shore. It is possible to connect to free Wi-Fi. All these amenities and entertainment can be used absolutely for free, since the fee is taken only for entrance to the beach in the amount of 40 hryvnias (about 100 rubles) per person.





Water activities

But this is far from everything that the hydropark on Zhuravlevka (Kharkov) can please its visitors with. There is still a boat station, which operates until nightfall. The entrance fee to the beach already includes a walk on a hydropeda or boat for an hour. During such a trip along the river, life jackets are issued to children and adults . There is also a lifebuoy in the arsenal, making this event absolutely safe. Such boat trips will allow you to retire from the hustle and bustle of the world and spend a little time in peace and quiet, enjoying the scenery of the surrounding nature.





Other leisure activities

In addition, you can have a picnic with your friends or family, having arrived at the entertainment water complex on the basis of Zhuravlevka beach (Kharkov). Pergolas located on the territory of the hydropark are considered a great place, according to the townspeople. Here you can get together in a fun company and cook kebab together. They are all made of high quality material and are also equipped with comfortable furniture.

All arbors are equipped with a package of charcoal, a set of tablecloths for single use, barbecue. There are also sockets, so you can turn on the tape recorder and make your holiday more fun. Rental of gazebos also includes access to all the entertainment located in the hydropark, and the use of a place for a car in a spacious parking lot.





What else can you do?

But a boat trip can be considered a real hit this year. It can be ordered by coming to the Zhuravlyovka entertainment complex. Kharkov, a paid beach on the territory of which is equipped with many attractions, invites lovers of board games. After all, this place gives vacationers the opportunity to use backgammon, chess and checkers for free. In addition, every weekend various kinds of master classes, children's entertainment events, and outdoor training are held at the hydropark. Here is the only rope park in the city above the water.

Where to eat

After such active games in the fresh air, there is no doubt a desire to have a bite. On the territory of Zhuravlyovka, for this occasion, there is a cozy cafe where customers can enjoy refreshments, all kinds of snacks and delicious desserts. This establishment has sun loungers. Therefore, visitors have a unique opportunity to lie in a pleasant cool, watching the water surface of the river.

Those who wish to eat more densely, it is better to head to the restaurant "Mill", located on the same beach. It consists of two halls and a porch, made in the medieval style and reminiscent of the refectory of hunters. Here you can also organize any event, celebrate a birthday or spend a romantic dinner with your soulmate.





Guest reviews

Due to such an abundance of entertainment and relatively low prices, the paid Zhuravlevka beach is very popular. Not only locals, but also guests of the city like to come to this hydropark. Therefore, in the summer it is crowded here, especially on weekends. People like that the territory is always clean and tidy, as well as many arbors are built. Vacationers who come here with the whole family are very pleased with this hydropark. Here, they will always find something to do, both children and their parents. Such a large selection of swings, slides and trampolines will be able to occupy any kid for a long time, while adults will enjoy relaxing on the beach and swimming in the river.

How to get there

To get to this beach is not difficult at all, since Zhuravlyovka is located in the historical district of Kharkov, that is, practically in the center of the city. The exact address is as follows: 2A Heroes of Labor Street. You can get there by metro or by tram number 16, number 26, number 16a, plying to the stop "Recreation Area".



Thanks to the magnificent Zhuravlevka beach, people always know what to do in Kharkov in the summer. This hydropark has the opportunity to have fun without leaving the city limits and get a lot of positive emotions, pleasant experiences, as well as get rid of stress and fatigue.
