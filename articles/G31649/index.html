Hotel Description

In St. Petersburg there are quite a lot of hotel complexes, among them you can always choose the right one for any wallet. But it is natural that every tourist wants to settle in a place where he will receive a high level of service for little money. This approach is the concept of the Galact Hotel in St. Petersburg. Here, each guest is treated with love and attention, and any problems are solved in the blink of an eye. Many guests note with great surprise how caring and friendly the staff at the hotel are. After all, this is usually what the Russian service sector lacks.

The Galakt Hotel, or Galakt Hotel, as it is also called, has only two stars. But in fact, guests receive a service that meets the service of a good three-star hotel complexes. And some guests claim that according to certain characteristics they would even assign four stars to the hotel.

If you read the description of the Galact Hotel (St. Petersburg) in the tourist catalog or on the website on the Internet, you can find out that it belongs to the economy class and is suitable for budget tourists. The hotel has several awards, and the recommendations of regular customers form its rating. If we take a ten-point scale as the basis, then Galact usually gets at least eight points, which is very good for a hotel with only two stars.

The Galact Hotel has been operating in St. Petersburg for about twenty years, and every year it improves the level of service and updates the equipment of the room stock. At the moment, guests are accommodated in 78 rooms of various categories. Not so long ago, guests got the opportunity to live in apartments that are decorated as a small cozy apartment with a dedicated kitchen area.





Address

The address of the Galakt Hotel (St. Petersburg) can be easily found in any directory. The hotel is located on Red Dawn Boulevard, at number eight. This street belongs to the Nevsky district and is located at some distance from the city center.

Location

Despite the fact that the Galact Hotel in St. Petersburg is not in the center, its guests believe that it has a rather convenient location. Particularly pleasing to city guests is the fact that there is a metro station nearby. Therefore, getting to any point in St. Petersburg is not difficult.

The nearest metro station is "Lomonosovskaya", it is necessary to go to it no more than ten minutes. Proletarskaya is located a little further, and about three kilometers from the hotel there is Obukhovo station.

When you go on a trip to a large city, of which St. Petersburg is, it is worthwhile to orient yourself perfectly in the distance that separates your hotel from infrastructure facilities important for a traveler. For example, the Galakt Hotel (St. Petersburg) is located nine kilometers from the Moscow railway station. By public transport you will cover this distance in just fifteen minutes. Tourists will have to take a taxi about thirty minutes to the airport, and only five minutes to the River Station.

Hotel Facilities

Galakta guests describe with great enthusiasm how wide the range of services is provided to guests. It is noteworthy that the cost of living in some rooms includes breakfast. And for those tourists who plan an early departure from the hotel for an excursion, the cooks will collect a lunch box so that the guests do not remain hungry.

The hotel has a tour desk, where you will find an interesting program. Sometimes the price of accommodation for the action (they are regularly held at the hotel) includes a bus city tour of the city.

It is nice that Galact treats its guests with great care, so they can use the domestic room. Here, guests have the opportunity to heat food in the microwave, wash and iron things, and pour water from the cooler. Of all the listed services, only washing and drying are paid.

At the reception, Galact hotel guests in St. Petersburg have very friendly receptionists. They are ready to answer any questions, help solve problems, as well as copy documents for a small surcharge, write information to disk or other media, or send an e-mail at your request.

For customers who came to St. Petersburg on business, the hotel has a meeting room. The average rental price is three hundred rubles. When renting for three hours or more, the price decreases to two hundred rubles.

A doctor works at Galakta, and attentive employees are ready to organize a banquet or other event for their guests according to the list of clients' wishes.

All hotel guests have access to the Internet, can use a guarded parking lot for vehicles and aromatic lamps, which create in the rooms just an unusual atmosphere.

Number of rooms

Standard

Rooms of this category do not differ in large sizes. They occupy only sixteen square meters and are divided into three types:

  • single;
  • double with two separate beds;
  • double with a large bed for two guests.

The room is equipped with a shower, a bathroom, a fridge, a kettle and a TV. There is also a safe, which can be used for an additional fee. The cost of living starts from two thousand rubles a day.

Comfort

These rooms are a bit more spacious than the previous ones - eighteen squares. They are also divided into single and double, but equipped much better than the standard. Each air-conditioned room has a plasma TV with satellite channels. The room has a fridge, kettle and telephone. The shower room pleases guests with warm floors, here you can find guest toiletries and a set of towels. It is nice that guests can use the tea set, which the maids replenish daily.

The cost of living in such a comfortable room ranges from three thousand rubles per day.





Wedding number

In such apartments for newlyweds the most comfortable conditions are created. The rooms of this category are divided into two groups:

  • sixteen square meter standard;
  • comfort of eighteen square meters.

The newlyweds will receive a compliment from the hotel and the opportunity to order a banquet at a discount. For a night in a wedding room, guests will have to pay about four thousand rubles.

Tourist numbers

Rooms up to eighteen squares are designed to accommodate a group of tourists. Their distinguishing feature is the lack of a shower in the room, however, guests receive an individual bathroom and sink. There is a shower room on each floor of the hotel. Living in such a room is the most economical option - about two thousand rubles a day.
