Hersonissos Village Hotel Bungalows 4 *: reviews, rating, photo

Greece is considered one of the best, most pleasant and beautiful places to relax. Each island in this state has a special atmosphere and gives tourists the opportunity to visit paradise while remaining on the earth. In this beautiful part of the world there are many hotels, among which you can choose an institution depending on the budget. One of the great options is the Hersonissos Village Hotel Bungalows 4 *. Photos, ratings and other necessary information about the hotel - in this article.

Hotel Location

This hotel is located in Hersonissos, which belongs to the prefecture of Heraklion (Crete, Greece). The distance to the airport is 25 km, and to the beach - 400 m. The city center is about a kilometer, there are shopping centers, pharmacies, catering places and many other establishments.





Description of Hersonissos Village Hotel Bungalows 4 *

The hotel is located on a hill, so that from its entire territory, as well as from the balcony and from the windows of the rooms, a picturesque view of the garden, the sea and the surrounding area opens. The hotel complex consists of one main building and detached bungalows. The territory, including all buildings (their interior and exterior), is decorated in the traditional style of a Cretan village. The hotel was opened in 1989, in 2007 a complete reconstruction was carried out, and in 2013 all rooms were updated.

Hersonissos Village Hotel Bungalows 4 * (Greece, Hersonissos) belongs to the HERSONISSOS HOTEL GROUP hotel group. This is an ideal hotel for spending time, which will leave warm feelings in the soul of vacationers. It will be no less pleasant for tourists to find out that it belongs to economy-class establishments, although it has four stars.





Rooms in the hotel complex: description and their equipment

The hotel has 160 rooms.

  • Standard single.

The approximate cost is 3900 rubles per person per day. There is a balcony and access to the patio, refrigerator, telephone, safe and air conditioning, TV with satellite channels. The floor is tiled, marble. In the bathroom there is a bathtub with shower, toilet, washbasin and hairdryer.

  • Standard double with 1 or 2 beds.

The daily rate for this room at the Hersonissos Village Hotel Bungalows 4 * is approximately 4,600 rubles. This category features elegantly decorated, air-conditioned rooms with balconies overlooking the landscaped garden. It includes satellite TV, a refrigerator and a safe, a fully equipped bathroom with all necessary accessories.

  • Standard Double or Twin Room with Sea View.

The cost per room is about 5000 rubles. The only difference from the previous version is a direct view of the sea.

  • Family.

A day in a room of this category will cost about 6300 rubles per person. They are equipped in the same way as the previous options, only in addition there are 4 single beds - for a large family or group of friends.

  • Standard double with 1 or 2 beds and extra beds.

The approximate price is 4600 rubles. The room has everything you need. It can accommodate parents with a child, and there is also an extra bed for guests. Total: 2 single beds, 1 double, and 2 sofa beds. There are the same rooms, only with sea views, which is why the price is slightly higher - 5000 rubles.





Hotel Food System

In Hersonissos Village Hotel Bungalows 4 * (Crete, Heraklion), All Inclusive accommodation is possible. Meals are served in buffet style according to the following schedule:

  • 7.15-9.45 - breakfast in the central restaurant;
  • 12.30-14.00 - lunch in the snack bar;
  • 18.30-21.00 - dinner in the central restaurant.

Also at these establishments at other times you can order food and drinks at an additional cost. There is a pool bar with a variety of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, snacks (10.00-18.00), and here you can also order amazing coffee and delicious pastries (16.00-17.00). The cuisine in the restaurant is Mediterranean. Please note that the dress code is valid.
