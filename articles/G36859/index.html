Plan

The basic rules of investing begin with a plan. It is very important to have a detailed plan, which will contain all future steps to achieve the goal. Planning is very important, as it indicates a specific goal, which should be moved in the first place. Moreover, in this way the big task is divided into several small and quite achievable ones. At the same time, W. Buffett emphasized that the plan should be presented in writing in an ideal form. You need to understand that it is unlikely to follow it exactly, but it is important to adhere to the general provisions.

Principles

The investment rules of Jeremy Miller are based on important principles, which we will discuss below. It is very important to understand the value of investing in your education and knowledge, as this alone is the guarantee of your stable success. Remember that it is not always good to invest in the most profitable enterprise. Sometimes it's better to invest in something that you really like, but it brings a little less income. Thus, you yourself will increase your profit, as you will know your product absolutely and admire it as a buyer. This principle is confirmed by personal examples of W. Buffett.

The next important principle is that you need to get rid of what you do not understand. This means that if you are not an expert in this, it is better to sell your shares and find something to your liking. Another important principle is that the investor must first flee the sinking ship. You should not hope that the situation will improve dramatically, as this happens very rarely. It‚Äôs better to immediately sell the ‚Äúbad‚Äù assets in order to save not only your money, but also your nerves.



Remember the price. This is a very important factor that many people forget. If stock prices are high, then it‚Äôs better not to risk it and buy something cheaper, right? But this is the wrong choice. Remember that if the stock is expensive, then the company has a prosperous period that it can share with you. Do not be greedy, it is better to analyze the situation and consult with an expert if necessary.

The next important principle is based on the fact that you need to invest not only in domestic but also international companies. So you go to a new level. In addition to increasing your income, you will gain invaluable experience and will be able to create reserve capital in case there is a sharp decline in investment in your country. Remember that local companies are always at greater risk than international giants, which means that their level of reliability is much higher. Large companies go bankrupt much less often, since too much depends on them, and such a situation is completely unprofitable for a huge number of people.

Remember the winners and look for them. The fact is that every industry and company experiences a period of decline and boom. That is why it is very important to look for those who are at their best and invest in them. Do not forget about the diverse development and invest in what is at the rise of its popularity.

It is very important to use analytics and fresh calculations. Of course, this does not guarantee absolute success, but it will allow you to stand out from the group of uneducated investors and weed out most of the offers. However, analytics does not mean solely collecting information regarding the manual and your product. It involves viewing the news, studying schedules and calculations, checking financial reports.

Warren Buffett notes that many novice investors make a similar mistake and invest only based on short-term events. However, decision-making on this principle very often leads to failure. Do not look at upcoming events, analyze the entire history of the company.

The last important principle that the talented investor speaks of is that it is worth having patience, and perhaps this is one of the most difficult tasks. The fact is that many investors buy shares and immediately try to sell them at maximum cost, thus making a profit in the shortest possible time. However, W. Buffett advises quite different. He conducts a thorough study of the company in which he wants to invest. Sometimes it takes more than six months, but then, the investor does not plan to sell the shares, as they will be profitable for at least the next 10 years.

What can be recommended to beginners? Firstly, you should create a personal investment portfolio - a certain amount of money that you will use for investing and capital turnover. The next rule of investing is that you need to see the goal and not spray on the little things. They will become available to you when you achieve the main thing.



It is very important to constantly develop, as only this will allow you to get a decent profit. Not wanting to take a step forward, you only make yourself worse. Explore new trends, try different things, learn to choose. Do not keep your active in only one place; remember about diversification. Try to recoup your investment. Remember that there will never be a 100% guarantee, so invest in yourself to develop and learn more useful information.
