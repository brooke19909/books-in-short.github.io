What is noteworthy is the Proletarskaya metro station

The metro station "Proletarskaya" has been known to Muscovites and guests of the capital since 1967. On the last day before the onset of this year, it was put into operation as part of the launch site of the Zhdanov-Krasnopresnenskaya line. She got her name in accordance with the name of the Moscow region and, unlike the latter, retains it to this day. The Proletarskaya metro station is located between the Taganskaya-radial and Volgogradsky Prospekt stations. And since 1997, it has been part of the interchange hub connecting it with the Lublin-Dmitrov line.



Metro station "Proletarskaya": engineering features

The only remarkable point in the engineering solution of the station is the fact that it has as many as three underground vestibules. Two are located in the end parts of the main hall, and the third appeared later, after the Proletarskaya metro station became a transfer station. Station architecture

In architectural terms, it does not represent anything significant. It is based on a project that has become standard. The vault is supported by two rows of columns of forty pieces each.
