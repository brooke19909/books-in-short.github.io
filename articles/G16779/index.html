What changes have occurred in the land tenure system? Rights and obligations of landowners

Land tenure provides for the presence of an object or a person transferred to him in inherited lifetime possession. The rights and obligations of such entities are well reflected in the legislation of the Russian Federation over the past few decades. What changes have occurred in the land tenure system is briefly described below.





Inversion of landowner rights

The rights of landowners were first formulated in the 20th century. During this period, there was no private property on the territory. What changes have occurred in the land tenure system is well shown by a comparison of the editions of the corresponding code of the Russian Federation over the past few decades.

The rights of landowners in relation to land have not changed. It is allowed to be used only for its intended purpose, without harming the ecology and the environment. You can use at your discretion common minerals, water resources that are located on the territory of the facility.

Construction of buildings is allowed only taking into account architectural, fire and other standards. Landowners have more rights to use their plots, to receive profits and income from the sale of agricultural products. On the territory of the land relations facility , any cadastral, land management and land reclamation works are allowed.





Landlord Responsibilities

In the process of using territories, landowners have a range of responsibilities. They gained distribution in the XXI century, which is fixed by relevant legislation. Compared to the past decade, environmental protection is now of great importance. More land reclamation activities are being held.

It is forbidden to allow pollution, land depletion, destruction of soil fertility sites. The legislation provides for the protection of geodetic, boundary and other special signs. Much attention is paid to architectural, sanitary-hygienic and other standards in the construction of buildings. These are the changes that took place in the land tenure system in the 21st century.

Legal activity

Any legal or natural person who wishes to receive land in a lifetime inheritable possession must know for what purposes it can be provided. Legal activity is fixed by the relevant legislation. Landowners in XXI received more freedom to exercise their rights. They can transfer the land by inheritance, leave on bail, lease at their discretion. The territory may be withdrawn only in accordance with existing legislation.

The obligations of landowners have become more regulated, which is also enshrined in applicable law. On the territory of the plots, more cadastral works and measures on on-farm arrangements have been carried out. Their goal is the improvement of land, the approximation of their condition to the prospects of the economy. These are the changes that have taken place in the land tenure system over several decades.





Prospects for land tenure inversion

In the future, the inversion of the land tenure system will be developed by conducting measures for on-farm and inter-farm arrangements, cadastre and land reclamation in order to eliminate the shortcomings of plots, the formation of new farms. A study will be carried out of the territories of settlements, the restoration of endangered villages. Planned and work to increase the profitability, competitiveness and profitability of sites. As we have already seen, all the rights and obligations of the owners of territories are well stated in all editions of the Land Code of the Russian Federation. What changes have occurred in the land tenure over several decades, reflect the current regulations.
