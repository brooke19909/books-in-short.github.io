We choose hotels in Egypt for families with children

Many tourists choose Egypt because of low prices, a short flight and the opportunity to relax in this country all year round. When it is cold in Turkey, it often rains, in this African state not only the sun shines, but you can also swim. But in the former Ottoman Empire, almost any resort hotel has an infrastructure for small guests. Egypt in this regard is somewhat behind its competitor in the tourism market. What to do? First of all, for those traveling to Egypt (vacation with children), hotels need to be chosen wisely. The right hotel is the key to a good rest.



So what to do? Of course, a budget vacation does not mean bad. However, when you travel with children, especially young children, you should not save on a hotel. If funds are running out for you, the best place to travel to Egypt is Hurghada. Hotels, the prices for which are lower than, for example, in Sharm El Sheikh, often offer good services for their young guests. Even if you pay more for a five or four star hotel, you will save on the entertainment of your child. Since such hotels necessarily have a mini-club with Russian-language animation, a zoo, a water park or special show programs for children.

What hotels in Egypt for families with children are famous for such services? In Hurghada, this is "Albatros Jungle Aqua Park 4 *", in the territory of which is located "Jungle Aquapark". Entrance to it is free for hotel guests. A similar water entertainment complex is also available in the Titanic Aqua Park complex, but it is recommended to go there with older children, since a tidal wave pool, high slides and kayaking will add adrenaline to an adult visitor, but are dangerous for children.





The child does not play alone in the sea. Therefore, so that he rests from his parents, and you from him, you should choose such hotels in Egypt for families with children, in which there is a special animation for them. Not every hotel has one. Therefore, it is worth looking at the feedback of those who came here, if only to make an impression. In five-star resort hotels, as a rule, there is not only an excellent close-knit team of animators, but also mini-clubs, moreover, with a breakdown by age groups. The beach is an important component of relaxation by the sea. Even if you see a beautiful sandy strip in the photograph of the hotel, this does not mean that sunset in the sea will be such.
