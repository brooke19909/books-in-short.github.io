The recreation center "Sandbox" in Fokino: reviews

On the amazingly beautiful shore of Rudnev Bay is the tourist base "Sandbox". This place to relax is very different from such zones in that it perfectly combines natural landscapes, pleasant silence and all the benefits of modern civilization. Here, every vacationer will find entertainment pleasing to his soul.

Recreation center in the Primorsky Territory

Infrequently in the Primorsky Territory you can find a place for relaxation at this level. Sometimes tourists do not want to travel far and leave their home country in order to take a break. The recreation center "Sandbox" in Fokino is a great place both for a quiet pastime with the whole family with children, and for relaxation with a fun company of friends. An ideal place for tourists is located near cities such as Vladivostok and Khabarovsk. To be more precise, then on the 99th kilometer of the Vladivostok - Nakhodka highway.





The Sandbox will present a pleasant break from everyday affairs at any time of the year. According to the classification of starfish, the zone has two stars. In addition to relaxation, on the territory of the recreation center "Sandbox" in the Primorsky Territory, you can take advantage of offers of boat trips on a yacht and a boat, as well as excursions in Primorye.

How to get to the recreation center in Fokino?

To get to the recreation center "Sandbox" in Fokino, you can use two options. The first option is to take public transport, namely, bus number 506. This route leaves from Vladivostok. Once you get to the city of Fokino, take a taxi that will take you to the final point.





The second option is to use your personal transport and navigator. Your task is to get to 99 kilometers along the Vladivostok - Nakhodka highway, and then turn right and continue to follow the signs.

Accommodation and prices

Four options are available for living at a recreation center in the Primorsky Territory: 2- and 3-storey cottages, 4-bed summer houses and rooms in the hotel complex.

Two-story cottages offer 2-room rooms on each floor. Each room has a separate entrance. The rooms are well equipped with double beds, a chest of drawers and other furniture. In the cottages there is a kitchen with all the necessary kitchen set and utensils. The bathroom has a shower, toilet and wash basin with hot and cold water. In addition, there is a living room. It has two folding sofas and armchairs, a TV and a coffee table. There are four such cottages in the recreation area. For a more comfortable stay, the area adjacent to the cottages is equipped with a barbecue area.

Three-story cottages are designed for 12 and 7 people. In addition to bedrooms, a living room, a bathroom and a kitchen, here you can find a sauna with a steam room on the ground floor.





At the recreation center in Fokino, there are 25 four-seater houses for relaxation. Each house is equipped with one double and two single beds, a table, chairs, a refrigerator and a clothes hanger. Each house has a barbecue area.

The hotel complex offers regular double rooms, enlarged double rooms and double 2-room suites.

Ordinary double rooms (there are only three of them) include two single beds, a TV, a fridge, a fan, a minibar and a bathroom. The "enlarged" double rooms differ from the usual ones only in that they include 1.5-sleeping beds.

2-seater two-room suites are represented by a bedroom with a double bed and necessary furniture, as well as a living room with upholstered furniture, a minibar and a TV.

In total, seven rooms are available in the hotel complex. On the territory of the base there are places for camping.

The cost varies from the choice of room or hotel house. On average, the price varies from 500 to 3600 rubles per person per day.

Food in the Sandbox

For guests of the recreation center "Sandbox" in Fokino there is a cafe and a dining room. The price of a comprehensive meal for three meals is an average of 800 rubles per person. If you choose accommodation in a cottage, then you can cook in your own kitchen. Cooking in the hotel complex and 4-bed houses is prohibited.

Leisure and recreation at the tourist base in Fokino

This recreation center is an ideal place for lovers to relax in nature and stay in the circle of some conditions of civilization. Since the place is located on the coast of Rudnev, guests are offered beach vacations and swimming in the shallow water of their own beach in Rudnev Bay. In addition to swimming, the base has playgrounds for children with swings and gazebos. Sports and volleyball courts are available for adults.





For those tourists who want a more energetic vacation, such additional services as excursions around the Primorsky Territory and boat trips on yachts are provided. Here, each guest will find entertainment.

Reviews about the recreation center "Sandbox"

A large number of reviews of the Sandbox can be found on the Internet. Vacationers who visited the recreation center "Sandbox" in Fokino, were satisfied with the service and a great time. Guests note the unusual tandem of the amazing nature of the Primorsky Territory and the presence of modern amenities of civilization. Accommodation available for tourists provides a choice between all possible options. Value for money and quality have been noted more than once.





Interesting spending time will appeal to both people with families and children, and the company of cheerful friends. In addition, the territory is perfect for corporate events and various seminars. Also, guests of the recreation center talk about a pleasant stay here, regardless of the season and weather.
