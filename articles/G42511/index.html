Comfort, perfect service, oriental hospitality - the best hotels of Marmaris are waiting for you!

Marmaris is considered the most elegant and the most European of all resorts of the Turkish Riviera. It is located in the largest natural harbor in the whole world, in the place where the Aegean Sea meets the Mediterranean. The coastline of Marmaris is truly beautiful - picturesque bays, well-groomed beaches. The nature surrounding the town is beautiful: hills, cliffs, mountain ranges immersed in the greenery of pine groves and exotic trees. The city itself is famous for its embankment with a length of 4 kilometers. Along it are the best hotels in Marmaris, which are built in a wide variety of architectural styles. Here you can find hotels in the Oriental style, reminiscent of the palaces from the "One Thousand and One Nights", and fashionable hotel buildings, built in the form of giant steamboats. There are also those that resemble European castles in their architecture. However, the best hotels in Marmaris, in whatever style they were built, at first glance leave an impression of respectability and comfort. And most importantly, for a vacation in Marmaris, you can choose a decent hotel with very affordable prices. For those who prefer to relax on the highest level, we present a list that includes the best 5 * Marmaris hotels. For your information, this rating is constantly changing. This is due to the fact that the number of hotels in the resort is growing every year.

The approximate cost of living in hotels of this category is from 2500 to 4500 rubles. They all work according to the world-famous All Inclusive system, which means that almost all services on the hotel territory are included in the tour price. By the way, this system was developed in Turkey. Naturally, all tourists, without exception, are flattered by such a precautionary attitude towards their person. Once in the territory of a Turkish hotel, a vacationer forgets about his bank cards and wallet. Despite the fact that he already paid in full for everything, having bought a tour to a five-star hotel, it seems to him that here everything is done for him for free, since you do not have to pay in cash for this.

Food in hotels 5 *

The best hotels in Marmaris pay special attention to the nutrition of customers. Almost all hotels have "buffet" for breakfast, lunch, afternoon snack, dinner, etc. The variety of dishes offered to tourists is so great that they do not have time to taste each for all the days of their vacation. Many hotels have special children's tables, the menu of which is drawn up by pediatricians, as well as a special menu for allergy sufferers. For those who want to taste something special, there are a la carte restaurants in the hotel complexes where you can order dishes of cuisines of different nations. Some hotels have Japanese sushi bars or Italian restaurants.

Services and entertainments provided by the best hotels of Marmaris



All 5-star hotels in this resort have fitness centers as well as spa facilities. Vacationers are provided with massage services, Finnish and Turkish baths, a gym and aerobics, several pools. Moreover, for children there are special pool-paddling pools with small slides. For younger tourists, children's rooms, playgrounds and even babysitting services are also provided. And for their parents - tennis courts, football and volleyball fields, water activities, discos, animation, etc. All of the above can also be found in hotels in the class below. The best 4-star hotels in Marmaris, such as Marti La Perla or Sunrise, are not inferior to five-star hotels. Therefore, before buying tours, carefully read the reviews of tourists who have already visited this or that hotel complex. Maybe you will understand that there is no need to buy expensive tickets, if you can just as easily relax in hotels a little less.
