Restaurant "Villaggio" in Moscow: description, services.

Moscow is one of the most visited cities in Russia. Every year, many tourists from different cities and countries of the world relax here. One of the most visited places in the capital can be called Vorontsov Park. Both locals and visitors love to walk here. After a pleasant time in the park, vacationers usually look for a suitable place where you can eat deliciously. Today we‚Äôll talk about one of these places.





In this article, we will consider the Italian restaurant " Villaggio", located in the southwestern part of Moscow, in the immediate vicinity of the famous Vorontsov Park, where tourists, newlyweds and couples in love often walk and arrange beautiful photo sessions.

Of the advantages of this institution, many guests highlight a bright and beautiful interior, the presence of a room for children, parking and, of course, excellent cuisine.

Description

The name of the restaurant "Villaggio" translated from Italian means "village", "small courtyard". Thanks to the hard work of the designers, visitors to this wonderful establishment really feel like in a cozy Italian restaurant. The atmosphere of leisureliness is complemented by subdued light, quiet background music. The restaurant itself is very welcoming, calm and comfortable. The menu contains the classic positions of the cuisines of Italy, Europe, Georgia. For children special dishes are provided.





Restaurant halls

The restaurant contains three spacious rooms, the interior of which is made in a Mediterranean style. Each zone is very bright and pleasant, with a lot of beautiful interior items.

  • The main hall in the restaurant "Villaggio" is able to accommodate up to 150 people. It has a music stage, a spacious dance floor, modern sound equipment, plasma panels hang on the walls, in the presence of a piano.
  • VIP zone is designed for 25 people. It is decorated in bright colors, in a romantic style.
  • The fireplace room accepts from 30 to 45 people. It is decorated with a large beautiful fireplace.

For young customers of the restaurant, the building has a specially designated children's room. In the warm season, visitors can dine outside on the beautiful veranda.

Kitchen

Restaurant "Villaggio" positions itself as an institution based on Italian cuisine. But besides the classic Italian dishes (pizza, pasta, risotto) here you can taste the delicious dishes of Georgian cuisine, many of which are cooked on an open fire. The professional team of cooks ‚ÄúVillaggio‚Äù manages to impress with exquisite dishes of even the most sophisticated gourmets. For children, the restaurant has a special children's menu.

Location

The institution is located in the South-West district of the Russian capital opposite the Vorontsov park.

The exact address of the restaurant Villaggio: Vorontsov ponds, p. 11.

The nearest stations of the Moscow metro: Yugo-Zapadnaya, Kaluzhskaya, Novye Cheryomushki.





Anyone who wants to find out about the availability of places in a restaurant or reserve a table can do this for free by contacting the manager of the institution by phone or by sending a request in the form available on the website Villaggio-rest.ru.

Information for those wishing to have a banquet in the restaurant

The cost of a banquet menu in the restaurant "Villaggio" starts from 3 thousand rubles per person. In order for the whole institution to be closed for the sake of your celebration, it is necessary to arrange personally with the administrator.

Reservation of halls is carried out after making 50% of the total amount of the order, 10% of the cost of the banquet is for maintenance.

The restaurant allows you to bring strong alcohol with you.

Opening hours of " Villaggio" - from 12:00 to 00:00.

The restaurant accepts applications for weddings, anniversaries, corporate parties. Here they will help you develop a menu, recommend good artists, hosts and musicians, and also decorate any of the halls according to your taste and desire.





Additional Information

Among the additional services of the Villaggio restaurant in Moscow, customers are offered wireless Internet, the opportunity to sing karaoke, and the area for the celebration by animators. On certain days, live music is played for guests.

In addition, there is free parking on site for up to 50 cars and designed specifically for guests arriving in their own car.

The average bill is 1.5 thousand rubles.

On weekdays, all meals during lunch have a 20% discount.

Conclusion

Restaurant "Villaggio" on Vorontsov Ponds is a great option for connoisseurs of delicious food, comfort and beauty. Any holiday organized in this institution will certainly be excellent!
