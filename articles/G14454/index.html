Junior Suites: what is it?

Some of us have often come across such a phrase as Junior Suites. What it is? You need to know that this is one of the categories of rooms in hotels. Every year, the popularity of foreign countries is growing, and therefore various innovations are introduced in hotels that attract a large number of tourists. To date, there are many varieties of hotel rooms. Surely, any of us, looking at the proposed rooms of a foreign hotel, met such a variety as the Junior Suites. What is this number and what are its conditions?

What is Junior Suites

Junior Suites are a superior room. Most often, this number is a fairly spacious room. The berth in it can be fenced off from the rest of the space. In general, such a room resembles a room of a category called De Luxe. The difference between the two is that the Junior Suites have an improved layout. For example, a bed in the afternoon can be converted into a living room. Many people choose this category of rooms because of the very comfortable conditions. After all, it has everything you need.

What is in the room

In a room called Junior Suites there are some things without which the rest would not seem so comfortable. Firstly, the room has a room with a toilet and shower. It is usually beautifully designed, and some hotels even have bathtubs. Secondly, there is air conditioning, which is extremely important for any vacationer, especially in hot countries. The air conditioner must be of good quality and always with a remote control, as constantly getting up from the sofa to turn it on or off will be uncomfortable for the vacationer. Thirdly, the room has a refrigerator, it can be small in size. This is convenient, since you can put products bought on vacation there. Fourth, the room has a TV, most often it is a plasma panel suspended on the wall. Fifth, of course, a minibar, because on vacation every tourist can afford to drink a glass of champagne or wine in the evening. And, of course, a comfortable double bed of excellent quality. Indeed, most often couples or newlyweds come to rest.

Room Interior

As for the interior of the room, it is quite interesting and luxurious. The ceilings in these rooms are perfectly flat, and can be suspended. Wallpaper is mostly plain or have some discreet patterns, sometimes with sparkles. The beds in them are usually wide, but not high, made from noble species of wood. On the beds you can always see expensive bedding and soft pillows, of which there are several. An expensive parquet or linoleum is laid on the floor, sometimes it is covered with carpets. The curtains in the rooms are quite dense and to the floor. In addition to all this, the interior may contain any other details, such as floor lamps or paintings. And the windows in these rooms are quite large, making the room very bright during the day. In some hotels you can find windows to the floor, which is very fashionable now.



In general, these rooms have everything that will make your stay unforgettable and comfortable. After all, a hotel room is one of the details that form one or another impression of a vacation abroad. Remember, you can not save on your own vacation. Otherwise, for you it will not be rest, but sheer torment.
