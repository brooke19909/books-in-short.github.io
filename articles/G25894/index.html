The best TVs for game consoles

Many people love game consoles, and this is not surprising, because they provide an opportunity to plunge into the unreal world. But to really feel involved in the gameplay, you need to TVs for game consoles were not just good, but the best. What models are suitable for these purposes?





main parameters

We offer an overview of the most popular parameters from which you should build on, choosing a TV for a game console.

  • Input delay . This parameter is the most important, it talks about the time it takes to transmit a signal to the device screen. The lower the input delay, the better. When choosing TVs for game consoles, pay attention that this parameter is less than 30 ms, otherwise the picture on the screen will appear later than your actions.
  • Response time . It talks about the speed with which the matrix of the TV changes the color of the pixel. The shorter the response time, the better for the gamer. Ideally, devices with response times of less than 5 ms are suitable for game consoles.
  • Screen options . To play it was interesting, choose TVs that support FullHD format. As for the size of the screen, they should be related to the size of the room and the distance from the screen to the players.
  • Viewing angle . This characteristic plays an important role, as it depends on it whether the colors will be distorted during the game. Models are best suited for consoles in which you can evaluate image quality from different angles.

How to choose?

The market for game consoles is represented by numerous brands, so you can buy models for both children and adults. To begin with, let's say that game consoles are devices with unique technical characteristics, which are provided by the presence of a processor, RAM, hard drive and video processor. Today, the market offers numerous game consoles for TV. How to choose and what to pay attention to?

Of course, many come from the brand name, which may be familiar from childhood. But still, it is worthwhile to understand for whom the toy is purchased - for adults or children. In any case, the models should have a good and understandable joystick. In the basic configuration, consoles are supplemented by two joysticks, so you can play two players at once. The second important point is the connection methods. There are two of them: the most common, but not the highest quality in terms of image - RCA and HDMI, capable of transmitting high-resolution video. Now consider the most popular brands of set-top boxes for TV.
