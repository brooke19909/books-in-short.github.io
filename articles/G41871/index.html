Hype: what is it? Hyp Pitfalls

Hype is an online project that promises investors high interest rates (from 8-15% per month to 1% per day or more) on deposits.



Hype: what is it? Each similar project begins with a beautiful legend. It may be some kind of offline business: ‚ÄúOur company is the largest developer in the city of N., and we urgently need additional investments‚Äù or ‚ÄúWe have our own casino, and working capital needs to be increased to increase profitability.‚Äù However, more often as a legend appear online projects. The most common option: "Our team of professional traders is able to turn the invested funds into your personal Eldorado." Sometimes, to prove their "decency", customers are paid interest for a period. But projects do not provide any actual evidence of successful work. That‚Äôs why every investor makes such a hyip ( hype ) investment at his own risk.

There are fairly well-known projects that have left their mark on history: Gamma, StabilityFX, VladimirFX. And although some worked for several years and brought their investors a certain income, everyone has one end.

Essence



Hype: what is it? At the heart of any such project is a financial pyramid. All interest payments to customers are made at the expense of incoming funds from other customers who were reckless to make a contribution later. And according to the law of the genre, any financial pyramid sooner or later falls.

Types of HYIPs

  1. Low yield. This species is the most long-playing. The project offers investors a low percentage of return - 7-15% per month - and therefore is able to exist for quite some time.
  2. Hype with medium and high profitability. These types of projects seek to "get rich" investors and offer from 20% per month or more. Their lifespan is short. Medium-income ones can last several months, while in highly profitable ones, the life path ends in a week. And the majority of participants know about the fraudulent nature of such financial pyramids.

How to organize

To create and implement Hype, you need one, maximum two people. Everything is much simpler than it might seem at first glance. Hypov scripts are present on the Internet and are not so expensive. They are ready and set up. You only need to replace the interface, upload it to the server, connect to electronic payment systems and you can start it.



And you do not need to have superintelligence. The network of such projects is darkness and darkness. And if opening and launching is a fairly easy matter, then promoting and attracting investors is a much more laborious undertaking. In order for people to take their hard-earned money into the project, it takes a lot of work. Specialized forums and sites can help, the main task of which is hype monitoring. The forums are home to a huge number of "referralists" who will give the most comprehensive answer to the question: "Hype - what is it?" and others like that, will show screenshots of "successful" payouts. The task of such agents is to attract new investors. And this is done quite simply. The agent in his signature on the forum indicates a link to the project that needed promotion. As soon as someone clicks the link and signs up, the referral gets his percentage right there.

There is nothing strange in the referral program itself. It is used by many online projects (online games, online stores, exchanges, hosting).

The exact scheme can be represented in this way. The organizer (admin) launches his project and recruits referrals so that they promote the resource on popular forums. New investors come, many of whom already have experience working with Hyps. From each contribution, the organizer leaves a certain percentage for himself, a part is transferred to the referral through an affiliate program. The rest of the electronic money supply remains in the accounts so that any of the depositors can withdraw their funds on demand.



As soon as the acceleration period has passed, investors begin to withdraw profits and deposits, because they know that the holiday will not last long. And as soon as the influx of fresh funds will not compensate for the outflow, the admin stops paying and takes everything. Such spontaneous "bankruptcy" got its name - "scam". As a result, investors receive less money than they could if the project paid until the end.

Who is in profit

As a result, three categories of participants benefit: organizer, active referrals, experienced investors. The losers are newcomers who believe that they invested in a serious investment program or, having burned themselves once, imagined themselves to be experienced investors.

Conclusion

For most investors, investments in Hype projects are not profitable. It will not work to earn large amounts, but the risks are high. And most importantly - a lot of time is wasted.
