Oasi Azzurra Village 3 * (Italy, Sicily): hotel description, service, reviews

Italy is a fabulous country, famous for its hospitality, unique atmosphere, colorful landscapes and, of course, incomparable cuisine. And many tourists, considering options for a vacation, choose this particular state.

If we talk about relaxing on an Italian beach, then perhaps one of the most popular places is Sicily - an island shrouded in ancient legends. And often travelers stop in the resort town of Messina. It is here that the hotel complex Oasi Azzurra Village is located.

Of course, when going on a trip, it is important to find out all the details. What does the hotel offer a tourist? How far do you have to go to the beach? Is there any children's animation here? Will there be a power problem? Answers to these questions are important to many wanderers.

Location: where is the hotel located?





To begin with, it is worth considering the issue of location. Where to find the Oasi Azzurra Village hotel? San Saba (San Saba), a picturesque fishing village on the shores of the Tyrrhenian Sea - is the perfect place for a relaxing holiday and complete immersion in the mysterious atmosphere of the island. Here, travelers can enjoy views of the cliffs and the Aeolian Islands.

By the way, the distance to the city center is only 6 km - here you can find large shopping centers, night clubs and other entertainment venues. 16 km away is the main train station in Messina, from where you can easily get to almost anywhere in Sicily. The famous Taorming Resort is about 60 km away. By the way, the hotel offers a free shuttle to / from the airport, which is important for many tourists.

General information about the hotel complex

The Oasi Azzurra Village 3 * hotel complex is located almost on the beach - the distance to the beach is no more than 10 m. The hotel area is quite large and, as former guests say, very beautiful - guests can live surrounded by a lush Mediterranean garden.

The hotel itself is a complex of three three-story buildings interconnected. By the way, in Oasi Azzurra Village travelers have been welcomed since 1980. Hotel buildings are updated almost every year, so you can count on all the blessings of modernity with a calm soul.

Room categories: what do travelers offer?





The Oasi Azzurra Village hotel complex is not so big - it consists of 60 apartments of various categories. Travelers can choose a suitable room.

  • Mono Sea View - spacious apartments for 2-3 people. It has a bedroom, a small but private kitchen, a work area with a comfortable table, and an outdoor terrace.
  • Bilo Sea View - here there is a separate bedroom, a living room with a folding sofa, as well as a kitchen with the necessary appliances. The area is 40 square meters, which is designed for 4-5 people.
  • Trilo Sea View - The total area is 50 square meters. There are two spacious bedrooms with terraces, a kitchen and a living room. The room is suitable for a large company or family (6-7 people).

Additional information about living rooms





Former guests say that living on the territory of Oasi Azzurra Village 3 * can be very comfortable. It offers spacious, clean rooms with private balconies and modern furniture (including a work desk).

Of course, the rooms are equipped with modern technology - there is a TV, small safe, telephone. In the cold season, central heating is available. Of course, in the kitchen you will find all the necessary utensils and equipment for cooking (hob, fridge, microwave, electric kettle).

The bathroom will please with cleanliness and new plumbing. You can take a shower at any time. Guests are provided with a hair dryer, a basic set of hygiene products, clean towels. Tourists note that the rooms are really kept clean - maids visit every day.

Holidays by the sea: beach and water activities





As already mentioned, Oasi Azzurra Village is located on the beach - you can reach the beach in a few moments. The coast here is covered with sand and small pebbles, so tourists are advised to acquire appropriate shoes.

This is a private area where only hotel guests rest - you can count on a quiet pastime. Guests enjoy free use of sun loungers and parasols. For an extra fee, you can rent a catamaran, jet ski or boat. Many tourists prefer to spend time windsurfing or snorkeling.

Hotel complex Oasi Azzurra Village: service

This is a small three-star hotel, which nevertheless offers its guests some additional services. To begin with, it is worth saying that there is free WiFi in public areas - this is important for almost every modern traveler. By the way, in many Sicilian hotels you need to pay for Internet connection.

Laundry facilities are located on site, and a medical aid post and post office are located nearby. For a small fee, guests rent cars or bicycles, which gives them the opportunity to explore the island on their own. By the way, cycling in the surroundings can be a real adventure - the scenery here is simply amazing, so you should take the time to walk.

Oasi Azzurra Village Hotel (Sicily): Guest Leisure





Of course, tourists for the most part come here, planning to spend the lion's share of time on the beach. But there are other attractions on site. For example, guests can refresh themselves by the pool at any time. Water is regularly changed here, and around there is a terrace with comfortable sunbeds and garden furniture. Water aerobics classes are held in the pool in the mornings - you can attend them for free.

By the way, you will not be disappointed if you always and everywhere strive to maintain good physical shape. A small sports hall with the necessary equipment for training is open year round. You can pre-order the services of a massage therapist. During the high season, sports are constantly held here - you can attend yoga, zumba, and Pilates classes.

There is a games room and a theater on the territory of which various shows and performances are regularly held. In the summer months, guests are entertained by a team of fun Italian animators who know a lot about fun. By the way, fans of night fun will also not be disappointed - discos are held in the open air on the seashore.

Do not discount tours, because Sicily is an island of amazing beauty. By the way, for guests often organize hiking trips. For a small fee you can make a real voyage to the nearby islands. And there are plenty of historical sights in the vicinity. A Russian-speaking guide works on the territory of the hotel, and there are enough tour agencies in the city.

Vacation with a child: additional information





Is this hotel suitable for families with children? Of course, yes, because families with children often stay at the hotel. As already mentioned, spacious apartments are ideal for parents with kids. An undoubted advantage is the availability of their own cuisine, because not all children are suitable for food offered by Italian restaurants.

You can be sure that your child will have fun, because in the courtyard there is a special pool with warm water, in which the guys just love to splash around. It is worth taking the child to the playground - here he can not only play, but also get acquainted with peers from different parts of the world.

Children's animation on site is also available. Throughout the day, experienced guys look after the guys, who fully take care of the organization of leisure for small tourists. Here the kids go in for sports, participate in fun competitions, do art, in a word, have fun from the heart.

It will never be superfluous to ask the opinion of former guests about a particular hotel. What do Oasi Azzurra Village Hotel think? Reviews about this place are extremely positive.
