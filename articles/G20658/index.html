Camp in Anapa "Pearl of Russia". Description, reviews

This article will discuss a wonderful children's sanatorium. It is located in the picturesque town of the Krasnodar Territory and is rightfully considered one of the largest in Russia.

The Pearl of Russia camp in Anapa accepts children from seven to fourteen years of age all year round for treatment. There is a sports complex "Antei" in the territory, a school is working. A library is open for children, a newspaper is published, and children's television and radio studios are broadcast. There is a college on the basis of the sanatorium, which trains specialists in the field of resort business.

Conditions of stay

Children who have come to rest are accommodated in four or six-bed rooms with loggias. By age principle, detachments of thirty children each are formed. They are supervised by educators. Near each building there are sports and bonfire sites.





Nutrition

The Pearl of Russia camp in Anapa provides five-day meals for young guests. The menu includes dishes of vegetables, meat. The diet also includes fruits and homemade cakes. Children eat in the spacious dining rooms that are available in each building.

Leisure

Separately, dwell on the organization of children's free time. An extensive program has been developed for them, providing for various forms of relaxation. Much attention is paid to the development of leadership skills among children. For this, a system of children's self-government functions, a president, a parliament are elected. The complex runs its own monetary unit - levasik.

Great opportunities are available for sports. Every morning, charging is carried out, and then during the day the children frolic in football, beach games, have the opportunity to engage in table tennis. Spartakiad and "Small Olympic Games" are held every shift.





In addition to sports, various fairs, music festivals, and competitions are organized for children. The tradition of the sanatorium is to conduct evening meetings. Sitting by candlelight, the children discuss the past day and make plans for the next, and also share their thoughts with each other.
