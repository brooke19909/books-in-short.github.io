A typical central processor for entry-level devices is the Alcatel One Touch Pop Star 5022D. The characteristics of its hardware are really modest. The silicon crystal of this CPU itself is produced by a process technology with norms of 28 nm. Of course, this is not the advanced technology for the production of semiconductor chips, but due to its use it turns out to reduce the cost of the CPU. And this, in turn, in the niche of budget devices comes to the fore. This central processor perfectly copes with the simplest and most common tasks - reading literature, playing audio recordings or videos, surfing various Internet resources. Even the most simple and average in terms of requirements for the hardware resources of the gadget toys in this case will definitely go. Smartphone Alcatel One Touch Pop Star 5022D just can not cope with the most complex and demanding 3D games. Its performance in this case will certainly not be enough. In reality, this gadget will have to be charged daily, with rare exceptions. The best solution to this problem is to buy an external additional battery.
