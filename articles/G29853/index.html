Instant Speed: Essence and Concept

Even a primary school student knows how to determine the speed of moving an object in a straight line: it is enough to divide the distance traveled by the time that was spent on such movement. However, it is worth remembering that the result obtained in this way allows you to judge the average value. If an object moves unevenly, then at certain parts of its path, the speed of movement can vary markedly. Therefore, sometimes such a value as instantaneous speed is required. It allows you to judge the speed of movement of a material point at any moment of movement.
