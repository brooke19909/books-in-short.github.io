Sights of Nakhodka: photo and description

The sights of Nakhodka are one of the main reasons forcing people to come to this city located in the Primorsky Territory. Temples, museums, monuments, natural beauties - in this village, every traveler will find something to see. Where is it worth starting to get acquainted with this amazing city, what sights of it you definitely need to see?

Sights of the Find: where to start

Of course, tourists will not be able to learn all the secrets that this village hides in a few days. Therefore, it is worth choosing the most famous sights of Nakhodka for exploring the city. For example, you can start by visiting the Temple of the Kazan Icon of the Mother of God. This building makes an indelible impression primarily due to its size, the height of the temple is more than 40 meters. The temple is crowned with nine golden domes, which effectively shimmer in the sun in fine weather.





The Temple of the Kazan Icon of the Mother of God is able to receive more than 1800 visitors in its walls at the same time. The most memorable is the special sound of the bells, which is associated with their impressive dimensions. It is known that the height of the largest of them is six meters. It also impresses with a diameter of over four meters. It is not surprising that the temple is remembered first of all, listing the most striking sights of Nakhodka.

The construction of this religious building was carried out since 1997, the dedication ceremony was held only in 2010 due to funding problems. In the temple there are several icons with an unusual history, for example, one of them lay at the bottom of the well for several decades.

Museum and Exhibition Center

Museums are another reason why the city of Nakhodka attracts tourists, the sights of which are described in this article. The Museum and Exhibition Center is a place that cannot be missed, becoming a guest of this village. The museum has been hosting visitors since 1981, in the 90s it gained independence, having separated from the Primorsky State Museum.

It offers visitors more than 32 thousand interesting exhibits. Here you can find old photographs and books telling about the history of the city. People who are attracted to numismatics and archeology will enjoy visiting the museum, fans of rare printed publications will find a lot of interesting things here. Also among the exhibits are many paintings written by foreign and Russian artists. Of course, there are also objects of folk art.





Gleb Travin Travel Museum

Gleb Travin Travel Museum is another place worth a look. It is impossible not to mention, listing the most striking sights of Nakhodka, Primorsky Territory. The museum is dedicated to the brave traveler Travin, who covered about 45 thousand kilometers by bicycle, spending three years on it. Travin visited Central Russia, Siberia, the Far East, visited the Transcaucasian and Ukrainian lands, the Central Asian republics.





The museum presents evidence of a feat accomplished by a brave cyclist, exhibits related to this journey.

Memorial "Mourning Mother"

The memorial "Mourning Mother" is also impossible to ignore, listing the sights of Nakhodka, photos of which can be seen in this article. This monument has been considered a symbol of the city for several years. It was erected in 1979 in memory of the terrible tragedy that happened in 1965.





It was then that the unfortunate Boxitorg team found their death in the Bering Sea. Of the 25 sailors, only one man managed to escape. The height of the majestic sculpture is seven meters. The gaze of a woman holding a child in her arms is directed towards the bay. Of course, the names of all the victims to whom the memorial is dedicated are carved.
