Rivers of the Sverdlovsk Region: Ufa, Tura, Sosva, Iset

The rivers of the Sverdlovsk region several centuries ago were famous for a large number of fish. However, with the construction of the dam, the population of species began to decline sharply. The first dam, located on the Iset reservoir, caused the death of many representatives. Unfortunately, the installation of dams touched almost all (even mountain) rivers, so the number of fish inhabiting other streams is reduced to this day. The rarest species of vertebrates, such as grayling, taimen, nelma, are found only in Tavda and its large tributaries. The remaining rivers of the Sverdlovsk region are not impressed by the species diversity.

Main rivers

The subject of the state, the center of which is Yekaterinburg, includes more than 50 water flows. All of them differ in size, nature of the flow, parameters, etc. But among them, nevertheless, one can distinguish leaders by their length. Large rivers of the Sverdlovsk region: Iset, Sosva, Ufa and some others.

Fishing

Fishing in these places is good. The bottom of the rivers is very unstable, the rises are constantly replaced by depressions. And this allows you to choose fishing places and "sort" them according to the desired species. Also, the rivers of the Sverdlovsk region allow you to travel by boat, so you can get into the new backwater area. In such bays, a large number of waterfowl are often found.

In the upper part, the coast is mostly covered with cliffs and forests. There are destroyed dams, parts of which must be destroyed.

Sosva

Sosva flows only in the Sverdlovsk region, the right tributary of the Tavda River. The length is also more than 600 km. Like other rivers of the Sverdlovsk region, it is one of those where fishing is allowed. In the upper reaches of the stream there is the Denezhkin Stone Reserve Fund. Belongs to the Kara Sea basin.



Ufa

The river belongs to the Volga. It is the right and largest sleeve of the Belaya River. Ufa flows through the Sverdlovsk region, captures the Chelyabinsk region and Bashkiria (named after its capital). Its total length is almost 1000 km.



Tour

One of the most popular rivers and the second longest is the Tura River (Sverdlovsk Region). Fishing, which often ends with unprecedented success, is not the only reason for the popularity of this stream. It is known for its history and the beauty of landscapes. Since the Tour is one of the largest rivers in the region, it is navigable. As you know, it was from here that the chieftain Yermak began the conquest of the Siberian Khanate. Also along the river was the famous Babinovskaya (Gosudareva) road, which lost its due significance after the construction of the Siberian-Moscow highway. In the 19th century, deposits of gold and platinum were discovered on the tributary of the Tura. Then a village was built on this place. And the gold rush began. Even now there are travelers who are trying to find a piece of gold on the banks of the river.



Brief Description of Tours

The tour is located in the Sverdlovsk and Tyumen regions and is a left tributary of the Tobol River. The length of the river is 1030 km, which allows rafting along the forest. Upward from the mouth, at a distance of 635 km, the river is navigable. Vessels are most often engaged in fishing, as the waters are full of various species of fish. Cities such as Tyumen, Yekaterinburg, Turinsk and Nizhnyaya Tura are located on the Ture River. The river to Verkhoturye is mountainous, often with rocky cliffs and slopes covered with forests. Fauna and flora

Unfortunately, plants and animals of the Tura River of the Sverdlovsk Region are not impressive with huge populations and a variety of species.

Closer to Tyumen, the number of fish is increasing, and most sites on the banks are rented by amateurs. Locals often use nets for fishing. For example, zander lives in the Tyumen region, and pike and burbot live in the Turin settlement. Also in the river you can catch perch, chebak, bream and ruff.

For lovers of natural attractions, the river banks will be interesting due to botanical monuments, many plants on limestone rocks, swamps and Mount Shaitan.



Entertainment

For those who prefer a mobile holiday, rafting on the river, with numerous picturesque parking lots, will be fascinating. Nature along the banks of the river attracts attention with picturesque views and wonderful landscapes, it will be easy to choose a suitable place to relax. Travelers who come to these places are offered the following options: parking near the Andronovo village on a beautiful beach, a green cape near Turinsky Sloboda, beautiful banks on both sides of the river near the Red Yar. And if you follow the Great Siberian Way, the route may take about 5 days. Its length is approximately 100 km. During this rafting, you can see three reservoirs, the Verkhoturskaya Hydroelectric Power Station, enjoy beautiful views of nature, meet the friendly inhabitants of the surrounding villages, and also go fishing.

The medicine

On the banks of the Tura River there are numerous medical sanatoriums, such as Obukhovsky, one of the oldest centers built at the end of the 19th century, White Stone, Kurya and many others. All of them specialize in the use of mineral water and healing mud. Yar village has hot springs containing iodine and bromine salts. Thus, tourists can not only enjoy the views of the surrounding nature, but also improve their health.
