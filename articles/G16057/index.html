The best hotels of Kirov (photo). Kirov hotels with hourly rate

Kirov is one of the most beautiful cities in Russia, while its residents are famous for hospitality and friendliness. You can go here for a relaxing holiday, as well as to satisfy your own curiosity. In the city there is something to "profit" for lovers of painting and architecture, lovers of literature.



For fans of a quiet pastime there are rivers and ponds, on the banks of which you can go fishing, looking at the clouds in reflection; forests in which you can breathe fresh air, richly saturated with the smell of berries, needles and mushrooms, walking slowly.

In a city such as Kirov, hotels, hostels and hotels are presented in a huge variety. This article will look at the best of them.

Hotel "Lux"

Considering the hotels of Kirov, photos of which are presented in this article, you can start with the hotel "Lux". It is located in the historical part of the city, near the bus and train stations. Nearby is the city hall, medical academy, forestry college, Vyatka Humanitarian State University, Central Market, regional clinical hospital.

During the walk you will have the opportunity to visit the largest shopping centers in Kirov : Kvadrat, Yubileiny, Rosinka, Evropeisky, TsUM, in addition, various entertainment centers, restaurants and bars of the city, Internet cafes.

Hotel Sputnik

This hotel is located in a quiet part of the city, surrounded by lush greenery. It is worth noting that outdoor enthusiasts will be pleased that the establishment is located next to the track and the ski jump, therefore, hotel guests can spend time in peace and quiet, as well as devote themselves to sports. This opportunity is not provided by all hotels in the city of Kirov.



The hotel has 77 rooms of different categories. This makes it possible to choose a room to your liking. On the territory of the institution there is a guarded parking lot, a sauna and a gym. For guests who come here for a business purpose, the hotel offers an equipped conference room for up to 40 people. Moreover, for all guests of the establishment free internet is available throughout.

Hotel "Iskozh"

Hotel "Iskozh" is located in the park part of the city, near the administrative center, where there are various hotels in Kirov. Hourly pay here is available to everyone who does not need to settle for a day or longer. Near the hotel there is a large shopping center City and a sports complex Soyuz.

Here you can connect to the Internet via Wi-Fi, in addition, provide wellness services.

When considering hotels in Kirov, in the center, it is worth noting the new comfortable Classic Hotel. It represents the best ratio of quality and price among similar institutions. Business people are offered a meeting room and a modern conference room.

Hotel Alpha

Considering the best hotels in Kirov, this hotel should be noted. This is a modern, brand new business hotel. The level of room equipment and service will pleasantly surprise any demanding guest. The institution is distinguished by a loyal price policy.
