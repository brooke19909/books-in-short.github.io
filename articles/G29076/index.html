Goa North: Reggae Beaches

The most favorite place for tourists in this Indian resort is, of course, North Goa. The beaches here were once chosen by European and American hippies, who were attracted here by solitude, proximity to nature and the ability to live in the simplicity of morals. Although since then a lot of water has flowed, including salty sea, and the resort has become a prestige

A great place to relax, having acquired hotels, restaurants and all kinds of entertainment, life here is still inexpensive. Tasty and cheap to eat, admiring the surf, and to budget in a good hotel - these are the chips that Goa North boasts.

The beaches of this resort, on the one hand, are similar to each other, and on the other, each of them has its own specifics. They are gray in color, since the sand on which vacationers are happy to sunbathe is of volcanic origin. Their combined length is about thirty kilometers. North Goa, whose beaches are often separated by rather high and sharp cliffs, abounds with picturesque lagoons, where you can enjoy life without interference. Therefore, esoteric lovers, seekers of a pure spirit and other people who are tired of the bustle of modern life still come here.

The coast of this resort begins from the famous Fort Tiracol.

This beach is practically deserted, and by its state one can judge how the local places looked many years ago. But not one such shore is in the resort of Goa North.
