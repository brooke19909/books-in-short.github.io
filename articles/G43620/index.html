Camp site "Assembly", Saratov: description, reviews

If you want to have an active and interesting rest, you will be invited to visit the Assembly camp located in the village of Shumeyka, Engelsky District, Saratov Region. Saratov is located at a distance of 20 kilometers from the tourist complex, so here you can stay away from the bustle of the city.





Description

The recreation centers of the Saratov region are mostly located, of course, on the banks of the Volga. The tourist complex "Assembly" is not an exception, which is located on the banks of the Volga surrounded by wonderful nature. It occupies a huge landscaped area on which the hotel buildings and guest houses for living are located at the service of tourists. Quiet and peaceful countryside atmosphere and high quality service will please the most demanding guest. Here you will be offered a wide variety of leisure activities.

  • The assembly camp (Saratov) has dance halls and karaoke rooms, its own cinema, as well as a billiards room.
  • In summer, in hot weather, at your service are swimming pools and sandy beaches on the banks of the Volga. In winter, winter sports are prepared for you. A choice of skates, skis and a snowmobile.
  • In good warm weather, vacationers can ride on the Volga on a yacht or boat.
  • For lovers of steam bath works.
  • And for children, the recreation center "Assembly" has prepared various attractions and playgrounds. For younger guests, a whole entertainment system has been developed here.




Accommodation

For a comfortable stay, the Assembly Camp (Saratov) offers a number of interesting options.

  • The guest house "House of Bears" - designed for 15 guests, the cost of living is from 15,000 rubles per day.
  • Guest house "Courchevel" - accommodation for 30 people is possible, the cost is from 20 thousand rubles.
  • Guest house "Santa Barbara" - designed for 8 people, accommodation costs from 15,000 rubles per day. Perhaps an additional resettlement of up to 7 people.
  • Hotel "Peter" - can accommodate 2 guests, the cost of living per day from 2000 rubles.
  • Hotel "Hops" - designed for 4 guests, accommodation costs from 4,500 rubles per day.
  • Guest house "Murom" - can accommodate 15 guests, the cost of living in it is from 15,000 rubles a day.

Meals are not included in the price of accommodation in guest houses and hotels. Smoking is prohibited throughout. Check-in is at 14:00, check-out is at 12:00.





Additional accommodation conditions

In addition to the main places for vacationers, it is possible to install additional ones.

  • If an additional adult or older children is accommodated, an additional fee of 500 rubles per person per night will be charged.
  • Only one bed can be added per room.
  • If children under 6 years are accommodated in existing beds, then their accommodation in the camp site is free. Cots are not available in the rooms.
  • All additional services are paid separately during the stay.
  • Pets are allowed upon prior request. This service is likely to be paid.
