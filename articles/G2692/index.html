Bowling in Elektrostal on Mira street

Bowling is still one of the most popular and affordable entertainment. Both children and adults love this sports game. However, for a good rest, just a game is not always enough, many prefer to combine an active game with the opportunity to have a good time at the table. Not all organizations offering bowling as entertainment have the ability to combine these preferences of people.

Bowling in Elektrostal is presented in the Corsair entertainment center, where in addition to the game, visitors can organize a feast or just have a bite to eat in the restaurant located in this entertainment center.

Bowling in the entertainment center

Bowling "Corsair" in Elektrostal is located on Mira street, house 27a. This is a fairly large entertainment complex in which visitors can play on 4 tracks in different halls.

All equipment for the game is modern. The design of gaming zones will pleasantly surprise vacationers. The backlight and vibrant colors create a good mood.

Bowling lounges at the Corsair Entertainment Center are open daily. Opening hours are the same. The visit is open from 12:00 to 00:00 at night. But, at the same time, the administration has formed three time intervals, the cost of playing in which is significantly different.

The most inexpensive game time is Monday through Thursday until 17:00, and the most expensive on Friday and Saturday from 21:00 in the evening.

A restaurant

Bowling in Elektrostal is not only a game, there is a restaurant with the same name in Corsair. An interesting place is that there are sports broadcasts, parties. But this does not mean that the restaurant looks like a sports bar.

The interior is made in wood, you can sit on benches with soft pillows. Of course, there is a marine theme in the design.

The restaurant hall, in fact, is a continuation of the bowling zone. From here you can watch the game of your team. The tracks also have tables where you can rest while waiting for your turn in the game.

The complex has two menu options: restaurant and bowling menus. All dishes are copyrighted, only the variety of the menu differs. The restaurant is more detailed and complex.

The entertainment center does not forget about young visitors, it is planned to open a children's room in the near future. In the bowling center, both adults and children will have an interesting time.
