Beldibi

This beach resort in the historical Turkish province of Lycia, where the Endam Hotel 4 * is located, is famous for the sea and mountains. It is located halfway between Kemer and Antalya. The Taurus Mountains in this place come very close to the sea, because they are picturesque here, like nowhere else. Directly below them is the village, and across the highway - its resort extension. It consists of several streets parallel to the sea. In addition to hotels, cafes and shops, you can hardly find anything here. The coast is built up with large and small hotels, which mainly stretch along the main street - Ataturk Avenue. Kemer and Antalya are usually reached from here by a minibus number twenty-eight. The sights here are mostly natural. Tourists highly recommend coastal parks that stretch along the sea along the road towards Antalya.



Territory how to get that nearby

Endam Hotel 4 * is located sixteen kilometers from the center of Kemer and twenty-five from Antalya. Go to the airport even further. You have to overcome about forty kilometers. If you are going by transfer, then consider yourself lucky. This hotel belongs to those where they come first, and pick up the last. On the other hand, the relatively close location to Antalya and Kemer allows you to go there simply by regular bus or dolmush minibus. The hotel consists of one main building. Nearby is Beldibi's city disco called Fly. The not too large size of the hotel attracts people who cannot stand the hustle and bustle of large crowds.



Number of rooms

The Endam Hotel 4 * has about eighty rooms where tourists are accommodated. Almost all of them are standard rooms. The interior is European. The rooms, according to tourists, are very cozy and comfortable. Everywhere there is a new and functional furniture - wardrobe, trellis, in each room - balconies. The rooms are air-conditioned (split system), equipped with a good bathroom with shower and hairdryer. Plumbing is new. Hot water around the clock. Every day, maids put on toiletries. It is possible to order a special bed for a child. The rooms are clean, all working. The hotel buildings are located so that the upper rooms (starting from the second floor) offer excellent views of both the mountains and the sea. Wet cleaning is done every other day, very carefully. Towels are changed three times a week. The linen is snow-white.



Nutrition

The Endam Hotel 4 * (Kemer) operates according to the "all inclusive" system in the main restaurant - very cozy, as tourists say. It has a pleasant atmosphere and very helpful waiters. There are high chairs for children. There is always a choice of dishes. For breakfast, eggs in various forms, omelets, cheese and sausage slices, jams and honey, delicious buns. Coffee from the vending machine. Juices in bars are packaged, not powder. Constantly there is chicken, shawarma, barbecue. Often given lamb, beef, fish. Of the side dishes - rice, pasta, french fries, vegetables in different forms. Meat is grilled every night on the grill. Of the fruits, tourists note watermelons, melons, figs and grapes. Also good are cakes from your own oven (with and without filling). They are called gesleme. A variety of soups - both for lunch and dinner. They even cook borsch. Services and entertainment

In the hotel Endam Hotel 4 * in the evenings there is a disco club. At the reception there is a paid safe, where you can put money and valuables. There is an exchange office. There is a billiard room. Bike rental is available. They also have access to the spa with a good sauna and hammam. There is a playground on the territory, equipped according to all safety rules. Some administrators speak Russian. There are practically no cases of theft in the hotel, and besides, there are video cameras on all floors. So for safety, tourists put the hotel a solid "five". In the lobby of the hotel you can use free Wi-Fi. The owners of the hotel are well managed with staff, always wondering if tourists need anything.



Sea

Pebble (like almost everywhere in Kemer) beach is a hundred meters from the Endam Hotel 4 * (Turkey). Go there for two minutes. The sea is very clean, the water is clear. But the depth is great and there are often waves. So for very young children, swimming on this beach may not be suitable. Tourists recommend rubber shoes or ‚Äúcoral‚Äù slippers for a comfortable entry into the water. But at the same time, the hotel staff tried to clear the shore of large boulders. Therefore, the beach "Endama" is considered the best in all Beldibi. There are free sunbeds and deck chairs that belong to the Endam Hotel 4 * (Beldibi). From the hotel you can also go to the "wild" coast. There, the entrance to the water is sandy, but you can run into a sea urchin. In addition, the hotel has a large outdoor pool, as well as a children's pool. Around him are also loungers and deck chairs. The pool has a water slide.



Tours in the area

If you came to Endam Hotel 4 * (Kemer, Beldibi) with the help of a tour operator, then, of course, you will be offered all kinds of excursions. These are Pamukkale, and the Lycian Worlds, and others, even more distant places. Of course, Turkey is a country worth seeing, but is it worth it to go so far, especially if your vacation is a week or ten days. And these trips are quite tiring. Is it not better to pay attention to local attractions. Living in Beldibi, you can easily explore Kemer and Antalya on your own, simply by using public transport. In the small town of Goynuk, which is two to three kilometers from the hotel, you can sit in colorful taverns or go on foot on a short trip to the mountains, visit the picturesque canyons and waterfalls. After all, the Lycian Way, known in antiquity, began from here. And if you are a lover of ancient antiquities, then with two buses within an hour you can easily get to the famous Phaselis. These are the ruins of the ancient Greek city, which are located directly on the sea, just east of Kemer. Here you can wander for hours, walk in the coniferous forest and swim in the sea, right at the ancient monuments, as well as sunbathe on the small pebble beach.

Endam Hotel 4 * - Reviews

Some tourists, knowing that once this hotel was a "three rubles", at first were afraid to go here. But then they noted with satisfaction in their reviews that the hotel did not disappoint them. Great cooks, great staff. Employees make sure that tourists are comfortable. Nobody is rude to you and does not beg. It is very quiet and peaceful. The hotel guarantees a real measured rest. There is no animation, but many consider this a plus. But at the disco by the bar by the pool, you can have fun and dance at home. The situation in the hotel is very cozy, family. The owners of the hotel are ready to help everyone and solve any problem. Take a break from the bustle, look around, admire the stunning mountains, swim in the amazing sea - this is where tourists come here. No wonder some come here not only annually, but even several times in one season.
