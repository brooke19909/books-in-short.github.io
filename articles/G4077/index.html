Holidays in Rimini, Italy: photos, reviews of tourists

Holidays in Rimini is popular among various tourists from around the world. Even Italians themselves often visit this resort. To be fully prepared for the trip, travelers should read this material. We will provide basic information about the town, as well as reviews from people who have already visited it.

Total information

Holidays in Rimini are popular because it is a resort town with a fifteen-kilometer beach area and a variety of attractions. Every year, travelers from all over the world come here to have fun.

It is located on the east coast and is one of the most popular places in the Adriatic. The settlement was founded by the Romans back in 268 BC. Now you can fly here by plane or get on land by means of transport with transfers.

There are no problems with moving around the resort. You can use buses and trams for up to two euros or rent a car directly at the hotel. An alternative is to call a taxi, but you will have to pay 15 to 20 currency units for the trip.

Beaches near the city

Holidays in Rimini mean a long enjoyment of the excellent climate and sunny weather on the beach. The advantage of the resort is that you can relax equally well on the entire coastline. It is divided into certain sections, which are separate beaches. Their total number is 150 and only two - under numbers 1 and 100 - are free. On them, sand and water are not as clean as in paid places.

Payment is made for a sunbed with an umbrella, and you can simply go in for a swim without making the necessary amount at the entrance. If you take the complete set, the price will be 16 euros, and a set of two positions can be taken only four units more expensive. The locker room key is also included.

The employees control the free use of the beach, and therefore it is not possible to relax with your towel here. The infrastructure on the coastal strip is well developed. You can come here with children, because the sea near the beach is shallow and they can have fun with water.

In Rimini, often on the beach you can see water rides and all kinds of slides.

Local kitchen

Holidays in Rimini in Italy, as in any other place, will be incomplete if a person does not taste the delights of local cuisine. The resort is part of the Emilia-Romagna region, which has the glory of the country's gastronomic center. It is here that different varieties of Parmesan, Parma ham, Bologna sausages and a variety of sauces are produced. This feature was reflected in the kitchen of local catering establishments. There is a pasta factory in the area around Rimini. Because of this, in the menu of every cafe or restaurant you can order this dish of all kinds with any sauce.

Lunch, on average, will take 12 euros, and if you take a comprehensive set, then 18. This also includes a portion of wine in volume 0.25 liters. For lovers of a snack on the go, the doors of local fast food restaurants are open. Here you can eat pizza, pita bread with toppings, paninos and other dishes.

Restaurants can be divided into a separate category. They are open to people who are willing to give 150-200 euros for dinner. The cuisine here is the most diverse, there are even Russian, Indian and Japanese dishes.

Start sightseeing

In Rimini, spending your holidays with children is a great place for sightseeing. Their number is not so large, but several places are simply a must.

The first of these is the Tiberius Bridge, which was nicknamed the Devil for its incredible strength. The construction was erected in 14 BC, and, according to legend, the lord of darkness himself helped people to create it. The true reason for its strength lies in the excellent wooden piles and a mixture of volcanic ash, which replaced concrete.

The arch of the emperor Augustus can compete with this attraction in popularity and prescription. It was built about two thousand years ago. An older arch cannot be found in all of Italy with its rich history. Near the building, tourist life is always in full swing because of its beauty. Once it was part of the city wall with a gate. Now in this place travelers are invited to plunge into the era of Roman conquests and world fame of the once powerful empire.

Two historical places

Photos from a vacation in Rimini can replenish the collection of any traveler, if you visit the main attractions in the city. The Malatesta Temple is one of them, because it was erected in the XII century. Its design is exemplary in the style of Franciscan Gothic. Travelers can see the building in an already redesigned form as conceived by the greatest Italian master Leon Alberti. Not all decisions of the architect were implemented, but even without this, the architectural monument looks attractive.

Only on this square you can see the old trading counters, built of stone and many other attractive buildings.

Different exhibitions

If you come to Rimini, you can take a unique opportunity to see all of Italy in miniature form. To do this, just visit the special theme park. It is called "Italy in Miniature" and demonstrates 270 architectural structures with an approximate location on the peninsula. All of them have historical significance and attract tourists from all over the world to view. Copies are made with special attention to details and exactly 25 times smaller than the original structures.

The park is already ending its fifth decade, and it is an interesting alternative for those who cannot afford a full trip around the country. Another famous venue is the exhibition center called Rimini Fiera. It is famous for the fact that business meetings are held here for entrepreneurs who wish to invest money in the tourism business or other industries in the resort. Tourists are advised to immediately go to the market sector of the building, which presents a variety of useful things for relaxation.
