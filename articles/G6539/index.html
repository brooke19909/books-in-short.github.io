Sharm Plaza ex Crowne Plaza Resort - waking fairy tale

On the Red Sea coast lies a magnificent hotel with a five-star category - Sharm Plaza ex Crowne Plaza Resort. The first reception of vacationers here took place in 2006. Since then thousands of tourists have been resting here. Its total area is 120,000 sq.m. In 2012, major repairs were made. From its territory offers a unique view of the vast expanses of the sea. He is preferred by the category of vacationers who love silence, calm and measured.



8 kilometers from Sharm Plaza ex Crowne Plaza Resort is the city airport. And at a distance of 15 kilometers is the center of Naama Bay. The hotel consists of a complex of buildings, including the main two-story building and 6 three-story bungalows. The housing stock is 547 rooms, including 366 standard ones. There are non-smoking rooms. The hotel also thought about people with disabilities. There are also connecting rooms. In addition, there are such as family rooms, superior, junior suite. Pets are not allowed.

All rooms of Sharm Plaza ex Crowne Plaza Resort equipped with individual air conditioning. There is a bath, hairdryer. An extra rollaway bed is available (upon reservation). The superb surrounding landscapes can be seen from the balcony or terrace.



So that guests do not get bored during the holidays, a number of various entertainments are offered. Free activities include a gym, table football, aerobics, futsal, water polo, aqua aerobics, darts, and a disco. Those who do not have enough of the above activities, you can use paid. So, you can visit the sauna, steam room or jacuzzi, play billiards, get a massage by a qualified specialist, visit the diving center. For those who want to explore the nearby cities and surroundings, you can use a service such as car rental. For health and beauty, there is a SPA salon.



To your guests Sharm Grand Plaza Resort offers such types of food as AI, SUPER AI. From 10 a.m. to 11 p.m., concentrated juices are provided to vacationers. This list is complemented by beer, soft drinks, rum, vodka, gin, brandy, whiskey. Local wine, coffee and tea can continue it. From 11 a.m. and from 4 p.m. snacks are offered throughout the hour in bars located by the pool and on the beach. The hotel has three restaurants and 5 bars. Children's menus can be ordered at the children's restaurant.

Sharm Plaza ex Crowne Plaza Resort It has its own beach, located 100 meters. Here you can see beautiful corals. Special shoes are recommended to avoid inconvenience. There is a shower, lifeguards work.

Those who prefer fresh bathing are invited to use one of the 5 outdoor pools, 2 of which are heated. This allows you to enjoy swimming even in cool weather. In addition, there is an Olympic pool, which is heated throughout the year.

For those who come to this paradise not only to relax, but also to work, a well-equipped conference room is available (for a fee), the capacity of which is up to 550 people. Among other services Sharm Plaza Resort 5 - free parking, various shops, currency exchange, a bus to the city (for a fee), taxi. The hotel did not forget about such a category of vacationers as children. They also have a swimming pool and a playground for games.

Reception is open around the clock. The staff is multilingual. Wheelchair rental is available for people with disabilities. On the ground floor of the main building there is a cinema and a library. At the reception there is a safe for storing valuables of vacationers during their stay at the hotel.
