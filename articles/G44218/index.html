Krasnodar, Inal Bay: review. Inal Bay on the map

Pleasant with its beauties Krasnodar. Inal Bay is one of the paradise places of this region. Almost all buildings in the bay are recreation centers (there are about 100 of them). They are located mainly along the road. Often tourists on recreation centers leave a positive review. Inal Bay is 130 km from Krasnodar and 60 km from Tuapse. At the same time, about 10 thousand people can rest here. The wonderful climate and affordable prices attract a large number of people who want to enjoy the beauty of local landscapes and the smell of the sea.





Also, the bay is famous for blue clay. Various spas use it in body healing procedures. The healing properties of this miracle of nature are known to many people who had previously rested in the bay, and those who have never been here. They go to this place to take advantage of the gift of nature and improve their health.

How to get there

Any way to the bay leads through the city of Krasnodar. Inal is located in the Tuapse district. You can get there by train, having reached Tuapse. You will have to travel 29 hours from Moscow, 43 hours from St. Petersburg, 84 hours from Novosibirsk, 68 from Yekaterinburg, and 12 from Rostov-on-Don. The Tuapse-Gelendzhik bus runs from the railway station in Tuapse, which will take you to the bay Inal. If you fly by plane, then there is only one road - the airport of Krasnodar. There is a Krasnodar-Sochi bus from the airport, followed by a transfer in the village of Dzhubga to the Dzhubga-Gelendzhik flight. And on it you can already reach your destination. There are also routes that can be accessed directly to Inal Bay, usually passing through Krasnodar. You can get there by car, then you need a map. Inal Bay is clearly visible on the map.





From the history

The story goes that Inal Bay was named after one of the preachers of the Christian religion, who lived on the Black Sea coast. No information about the righteous has been preserved. Only the name of the bay and the legend of its existence.

According to another version, this place was named after the prince. The event is dedicated to the reconciliation of the tribes. According to legend, 2 tribes lived on the banks of the Inal. The daughter of the ruler of one of them and the son of the leader of the other were in love and met secretly. Once such a meeting ended tragically. The young man was captured and decided to be executed. But a loving girl managed to dissuade her father from this crime. The daughters of the prince managed to reconcile both tribes. Since then, the bay began to be called Inal.

Another legend says that, after a truce was concluded between the two warring tribes, the settlement of Bzhid appeared, located near the bay. An elder during the celebration of the end of civil strife was broken glass. He ordered that a village be built on this site.

Which of the legends is closest to the truth is difficult to determine today. This is also important. Maybe this is what allowed her to flourish and become a favorite vacation spot for many tourists. And beautiful legends only ennoble the Inal Bay and give it mystery.

Weather and climatic conditions

Tourists, filled with impressions after relaxing in the bay, can leave only positive feedback. Inal Bay is pleased with its weather conditions, which correspond to the geographical location. The climate here is Mediterranean, humid subtropical, which is very beneficial for vegetation, so it blooms richly. Here you can meet various trees, greens.





You can begin to relax in Inal Bay in May (approximately from the 20th day) and continue until September (through the 25th). This period is characterized by the most favorable weather: almost always there are sunny days, the air temperature reaches 24 degrees. Rains are rare in May, June, July, August. A rare summer warm rain will only please, refresh the air. This is the best time for swimming, as the water temperature is kept at +17 degrees and above. Showers begin in autumn (November-March).

Climatic conditions in Inal Bay are optimal for recreation. Air, humidity, wind, rain, sun, clouds, temperature - the most resort resort.

Holidays in Inal Bay

Located near the city of Krasnodar, Inal Bay pleases everyone with its beauties. Weather, wonderful climate, plentiful vegetation, sea ... There are no industrial enterprises around. Blue clay, part of the deposits of which is located directly in the sea, is considered to be therapeutic, with anti-aging properties. Vacationers take mud baths at the foot of the mountain in a small pond. Then they wash away the mud in the sea, enhancing the healing effect. Because of such procedures, seawater takes on a bluish tint.
