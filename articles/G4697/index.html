How did the iris bracelets come about?

In the recent past, baubles were woven from various materials, such as threads, beads, etc. Nowadays, one very interesting thing has appeared on store shelves, namely irises. They are bright and very original bracelets made of multi-colored rubber bands.





The products were invented by Chin Chong, a former crash test engineer at Nissan based in Michigan (USA). He has two daughters. They loved to weave gum bracelets, and once, playing with them, Chin Chong wanted to do something original to impress the daughters. But suddenly I realized that it was hard for him because of the thumbs: small rubber bands did not want to weave together.

Do it yourself bracelet

You can buy a fun and cheap toy at any handicraft store - a rainbow loom. It will turn weaving bracelets into an entertaining hobby for middle and older children, as the device allows you to create cute and original accessories for yourself and friends.





There are various patterns of weaving bracelets from gum. Most of them can be weaved with their own hands, among which I would very much like to mention an interesting fishtail bracelet.

It is made of small rubber bands:

  • For starters, we take three small gum, preferably different colors.
  • One elastic band is twisted by an eight and put on the index and middle fingers.
  • We put on the two remaining rubber bands without twisting.
  • First, one end of the lower gum rises through the finger, then the second.
  • After that, another elastic is put on and the ends of the lower one again rise.
  • When the desired bracelet size is reached, two rubber bands are lifted through the fingers, and the latter is removed and associated with any burst.

Here in a simple way stylish bracelets are made of rubber bands. Weaving such accessories is an activity that brings children pride in their ability to needlework. After all, do-it-yourself bracelets not only form a collection of beautiful accessories, but also help to develop an aesthetic perception.





Tips for Beginners

  • It may seem to you that forever spent on weaving a bracelet with your own hands. Do not despair, and the result will exceed all expectations.
  • Do not fill the entire length of the machine if you have a small wrist.
