Borovoe: hotels, motels, recreation

People go to Burabai not only to have a good rest, but also to enjoy the heady mountain air and swim in the clear transparent waters of the local lakes.





A lot of sanatoriums and health resorts have been built here, annually opening the doors for holidaymakers of any social status and with a different budget. On Lake Borovoye, hotels and motels delight with their diversity; local recreational facilities offer relaxation for every taste.
