Andalucia Beach 4 * (Bulgaria, Elenite): hotel description, reviews

Recently, holidays in Bulgaria have been gaining more and more popularity among residents of Russia. It will cost many times cheaper than trips to Turkey, and also will have a number of advantages. In Bulgaria, prices are much lower compared to popular resorts, and the service is adequate.

One of the great resorts is Elenite, which is a complex of hotels and beaches near Nessebar. Hotel Andalucia Beach 4 * is part of this complex and is gaining more and more popularity among Russian tourists.





Rooms

The hotel was built in 2007, the rooms are divided into two categories: with windows to the park area and with windows to the sea. The rooms are designed for two people, plus one child. Covering - a carpet. All norm have access to the balcony. The beds in the room are single, plus a folding chair-bed. Each room has a TV with local cable TV, hairdryer, air conditioning. Towels are changed and rooms are cleaned daily; bed linen is changed twice a week.

A cot for a small child is available on request. The room has a safe, which can be used for a fee. Animals are allowed in weight of not more than 5 kg.

Tourists note with positive feedback the level of cleanliness in the rooms. Cleaning is done on time; you do not need to remind you to change linen or towels either.

A 4-star hotel Andalucia Beach is not suitable for a romantic pastime, but couples with children can deliver a lot of pleasant feelings.





Infrastructure

The hotel is equipped with everything that you may need on vacation: there is an Internet cafe, car rental, a currency exchange center. For hotel guests there is free parking, but the number of places on it is small. The Andalucia Beach Hotel is fully equipped. It has everything you need: a SPA center, a hairdresser and shops. The lobby bar features free Wi-Fi. Payment is accepted by Visa and Master Card. The nearest town can be reached in 15 minutes by bus.

Location

The hotel is part of the resort complex "Elenite", conveniently located on the beach, ten kilometers from the resort of Sunny Beach. A few kilometers away is Nessebar, one of the oldest cities in Europe, and 50 km away is Burgas - a large trading city with an interesting architectural ensemble. Nearby is the reserve reserve Kamchia, at the mouth of the river of the same name. According to tourists, the place has a pleasant, clean air, mild climate. Water temperature in summer is from 18 degrees to 26. You can also swim in September - this month the average water temperature reaches 23 degrees Celsius.





Beach

The beach is a ten minute walk from the hotel, mostly sandy, in some places there are stones. According to tourists, the water is clean and warm, but sometimes the flow nails algae. On all beaches of the resort in the daytime lifeguards are on duty. There are sun loungers and umbrellas on the beach, you can use them for free, as well as many souvenir shops and snack bars in the beach area. The Atlantis Water Park is 300 meters from the complex, and the use of swimming pools and slides is free for Adalusia customers. The water park also has a bar and spa services.





Sports and Services

On the territory of the Andalucia Beach 4 * complex (Elenite) there are a large number of establishments providing leisure for both adults and children. Free aerobics and aqua aerobics, gym. Outdoor enthusiasts can play table tennis, volleyball or throw darts. Paid services include massage, SPA treatments, Turkish bath, sauna and jacuzzi.

For children there is a separate pool, animator services are provided. There is a leisure club for children from 4 to 12 years old. For the smallest you can order a nanny.

For the safety of guests' health, there is a medical center where it is possible to receive qualified medical assistance at any time of the day.





Entertainment

On the territory of the resort "Elenite" is a shopping center that will definitely appeal to fans of shopping abroad. There is an equestrian center for children and adults, and horseback riding along the coast is regularly carried out. For kids there are a large number of specially equipped playgrounds. There are both daytime and evening entertainment programs for adults and kids, with animation, music and dancing. There are sports clubs: surfing, archery, billiards, trampoline. You can always rent pedal boats on the beach.





Nutrition

Meals are provided through the All Inclusive system. On the territory of the Andalucia Beach 4 * complex (Bulgaria) there are two restaurants, a lobby bar, points for a quick snack with hot dogs and french fries. Nearby there is a pastry shop for lovers of sweets. The diet includes cereals, soups, salads. Breakfast includes cereal, bread, cheese and sausages. The drinks on the menu include tea, coffee, soft drinks and beer. Strong drinks are sold only in the lobby bar.





What to see

According to vacationers, it will be very interesting to visit the historical center of old Nessebar, as well as its archaeological museum. The tour will introduce you to the stories of the ancient Thracian settlement that once existed on this site. Fans of antiquity will also like the ruins of the village of Nessebar. Also in the vicinity of Burgas it is possible to see the remains of the world's oldest Orthodox churches: the Hagia Sophia, the Church of St. John and the Basilica of the Blessed Virgin Mary. On tours of ancient temples you can learn about how Orthodoxy originated and developed in the early Middle Ages. Also in Burgas it is imperative to visit the current church of St. Blasius, St. Stephen and the Church of the Virgin. The Bulgarian churches and temples in their architecture belong to the Sofia type and are saturated with the spirit of history.





The benefits of a holiday in Bulgaria

As mentioned earlier, the advantage of a holiday in Bulgaria is the most optimal price-quality ratio. Here you will get the maximum level of comfort and service for relatively little money. Food is inexpensive but of high quality. The food is always freshly prepared and amazingly tasty. There will also be no problems with the language barrier: Bulgarian is largely in tune with the Russian and Ukrainian languages.

The local population is more friendly towards tourists than residents of popular resorts in Turkey and Egypt. Bulgaria is inferior in popularity to Turkey and Montenegro, which means that you can see the sights without crowds of tourists.

The political situation in the region is calm, and you can relax without worrying about your own safety and the safety of your family and friends.

The Elenite region is characterized by an abundance of coniferous vegetation, an unusual combination of mountains, forests and sand dunes. The clear sea and the absence of the usual city bustle will also be remembered.





What problems may arise

Andalucia Beach 4 * receives negative reviews, however, like every hotel. In the hot season, when the influx of holidaymakers is large, there may not be enough sun loungers on the beach. According to the guests, due to the high load, coffee machines in the restaurant and bar at the hotel often break down, as well as in the dining room there are not enough free tables.

In the rooms on the carpet, spots and traces from previous guests sometimes remain.

Sometimes with a large influx of tourists there are difficulties with cleaning, for example in the dining room.

About a third of vacationers complain about too cold water in the pool, although everyone else considers its temperature pleasant and invigorating.

The disadvantages of the Andalucia Beach 4 * hotel include late check-in - only at the end of lunch, that is, at 14-15 hours, which is not always convenient, especially after an air flight. But it is worth noting that a similar condition is present in almost every hotel, and it is not worth talking about it as a negative. It is better to check this moment with your tour operator when booking and choose the most optimal departure time.

Caution is worth walking and swimming in the evenings: due to the proximity of the mountains in the evening, the temperature drops rapidly, and if the average daily temperature is +28 ... + 30 degrees in the summer months, then in the evening it drops to +14 ... + 16. In order not to freeze in the evening, you need to take warm clothes with you.

Sometimes there may be technical difficulties with paying with a card, however, such problems are quite easily resolved. In general, rest at the Andalucia Beach 4 * hotel (Bulgaria) is a rest according to European quality standards. The pricing policy of the country and the low influx of tourists will allow you to get a comfortable unforgettable vacation for the whole family, a lot of pleasant impressions, and at the same time do not cause serious damage to the budget.
