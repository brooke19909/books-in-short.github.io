Vietnam, hotel Vinh Suong Seaside 3 *: description, services, photos and reviews of tourists

Vietnam is an amazing place on earth, the inhabitants of which have managed to turn a country living below the poverty line into a coveted tourist home. Now there are equipped beaches everywhere, hotels have been rebuilt, shops are working, and many tourists come here not only across the southern sea, but also with the aim of getting acquainted with the sights, as well as with the difficult and dramatic history of this country.

Location





200 km from Ho Chi Minh City, on the first coastline of Mui Ne, ten minutes from Fantien, a cozy small hotel Vinh Suong Seaside 3 * ("Vin Suong Seaside") is located.

Hotel Description

This is a cozy hotel complex, consisting of two buildings, several bungalows and villas, which is surrounded by coconut trees and immersed in orchids grown in a greenhouse. Hotel Vinh Suong Seaside 3 * operates on BB system, that is, it offers its guests accommodation and breakfast. If we talk about the average price of a burning tour for a weekly stay of one person in a double room, then it is approximately 30 thousand rubles. In addition to accommodation, the price includes airfare and transfer. If we talk about standard prices for accommodation in each of the categories of Vinh Suong Seaside 3 * rooms, then they differ from the amount mentioned above, and you can find them below. It is useful for anyone who still doubts the safety of a visit to this Asian country to know that the hotel described in the article is under round-the-clock protection, so that all the relevant requirements here are fully implemented.

Hotel facilities

Before going to the beach, everyone is advised to visit the spa. This is necessary to prepare the skin for sunbathing. You just have to peel the skin of the whole body, and the tan will lie flat. Favorite procedures - a jacuzzi, sauna, massage - are provided to everyone every day, and the fair sex will surely please the masters of the beauty salon.

For guests of Vinh Suong Seaside 3 * who want to cool off on a hot day and relax by the cool water, there are two outdoor pools.

For guests who prefer an active lifestyle, Vinh Suong Seaside Hotel offers a game of tennis and beach volleyball, while water lovers can enjoy water sports.

You can rent a moped, car or bicycle and ride around the territory and beyond.



For adherents of a leisurely intellectual game, tables for playing billiards are offered, where everyone can demonstrate and hone their skills.

Everyone who needs laundry services can use them for a fee.

Lovers of fun and loud singing are invited to visit karaoke.

If you do not have time to exchange dollars for Vietnamese dongs, the hotel has a currency exchange at an acceptable rate. One US dollar equals 21,632 Vietnamese Dongs (VNDs).

Fans of literature and fresh newspapers with magazines can visit the library, where there are not only interesting books, but also a binder of the press on any subject.

The Vinh Suong Seaside 3 * hotel has the organization and holding of various seminars and conferences, the guests are provided with an equipped business room that can seat up to 150 people at a time.

Tourists reviews

Tourists who visit the hotel always express very different opinions, and our hotel was not an exceptional case. The reviews were different, and often they depended on which building the hotel guests lived in.



Those who were accommodated in the buildings noted that one of them lacked balconies, and this turned out to be inconvenient in terms of drying clothes and towels for some guests who did not read the guide very carefully. But the serviceability of all television channels was unanimously noted by all (the hotel is equipped mainly with LG TVs). Some rooms do not have safes - it is proposed to store things at the reception, this point should also be taken into account when checking into the hotel.

Of course, if you are going to visit Vinh Suong Seaside 3 * (Vietnam), hotel reviews will help to make the first invisible impression of the place, but you need to focus not only on them. Check with the tour operator all questions of interest so that then the vacation does not turn out to be spoiled. By the way, not a single case of theft or loss of documents or valuables in the entire history of the hotel was recorded.
There were no complaints about the cleanliness of the rooms - every day the staff carried out a good cleaning, and linen and towels were changed once every three days. Guests noted a not too large hotel area, but it was clean and well maintained. Tourists living in a bungalow with a terrace were delighted. The excellent condition of furniture and textiles (linen and towels) was noted.

As for the breakfasts served in the hotel complex, the not so great variety of dishes was compensated by their wonderful taste. The hotel chefs were very praised. But in general, tourists are advised to eat all at once in restaurants near the hotel: the cost of dishes compared to Russian counterparts is simply ridiculous - a dinner for two can cost as little as 300 rubles! The dishes are fresh, unusual for Russians, but tasty. By the way, shops with souvenirs and alcohol are found at every step.

Many guests were very pleased with the free 24-hour Wi-Fi connection throughout the hotel, as well as the service - very thorough and respectful.

Party-goers will not like the hotel, if only because of its location - the city of Phan Thiet does not have fun and youth recreation. It is ideal to spend a relaxing vacation with young children, without making unnecessary requests. The hotel has everything you need, but no frills. For everyone who wants to travel, there is the opportunity to consult a guide on how to get to local attractions. The minus of the hotel guests considered lack of knowledge of the Russian language and poor knowledge of English.

conclusions

Tourists who come to any hotel usually expect attention, participation from the staff, and also want to expect to receive at least the minimum amenities for their stay. The hotel described in the article has all this. Despite the three-star category, the budget hotel Vinh Suong Seaside 3 * leaves quite a good impression. Reviews about him are mostly positive. Indeed, the hotel grounds are beautiful and well-groomed, all without exception, guests were pleased with the clean beach and sea, as well as delicious breakfasts at the hotel. Finally, the hotel guests were grateful to the staff for their attentiveness, friendliness and help in everything.
