LCD "Height", St. Petersburg: description, layout, developer and reviews

St. Petersburg is an unusually beautiful city, but the panorama of many of its streets is spoiled by buildings that are already 50-70 years old. They do not represent any artistic or historical value, therefore, in terms of urban development, there is a clause on their demolition and the construction of modern complexes in the vacated place. LCD "Height" - one of these. It was built on the site of an old hostel, which had long exhausted its design and design capacity. The new complex has become a decoration of the street and the entire area. Residents of its middle and upper floors will be able to admire the bewitching views of the city, which will be in full view of them. We offer a detailed overview of all the advantages and disadvantages of this facility.

LCD "Height" is being erected in St. Petersburg, in the Nevsky district, along the street Tsimbalina. The building is assigned number 25. Nearby are the streets of Babushkina, Sedova and Dudko, a little further - Railway Avenue. Prospect Obukhovskaya defense and the Neva embankment from here - a little more than 1000 meters. There are no other housing complexes near the residential complex "Vysota", there are only low buildings, industrial buildings and all kinds of offices around. The railway is far from the complex, but literally 50 meters there is a dead end used by the marshalling yard. It is also close at hand, only 900 meters away.

Ecology

This question is perhaps the most "painful" for the developer and future residents of the residential complex "Vysota". St. Petersburg is one of the three leaders in the most polluted industrial and automobile emissions of Russian cities. Petersburgers know that the Nevsky district is considered the most industrial in the city. There is a porcelain factory, there are two breweries, a chemical company "Reagent". They are so environmentally unfavorable that exclusion zones are created near each. In the soils of the Nevsky district, in excess of norms, cobalt, lead and other harmful substances were found. In fact, the residential complex under consideration is surrounded on all sides by the industrial zone, which is its significant drawback. Apartment owners will have to put up with the negative impact on the health of all industries in the area. The sorting station, from which noise is constantly heard, also contributes to environmental degradation.

A pleasant bonus for residents will be: Babushkina Park, which is only 450 meters away, the embankment with Krupskaya Garden, a small Dudko Garden and Ivanovsky Quarry. Before him, just 600 meters away.



Who is building

The LCD "Height" the developer is young, but diligent. The company is called Okhta Park Development. She entered the construction market in 2014 and has already managed to perfectly prove herself in the construction of sports facilities for the Sochi Olympics. This company also commissioned social facilities, such as a recreation center and a hotel complex, built by it in certain regions of the country. Residential complex "Height" - her first experience of this kind of construction work. The company began to build it in 2015, and already in the II quarter of 2017, delivery is scheduled. The complex is being built in one turn. At the moment, the construction of the building and the construction of the boiler room are completed, now internal work is underway.

LCD "Height" (St. Petersburg): reviews

This complex was designed to update the panorama of the Nevsky district.
