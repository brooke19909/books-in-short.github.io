The resort village of Abrau-Durso: hotels, hostels, guest houses

A small resort village located on the Black Sea coast, named after the Durso River. Its source is in a small lake, which is closed by high mountains. This river flows through a valley surrounded by two mountain ranges. Hotels and hotels were built in the valley (closer to the sea), and grape plantations stretched in its depths. It is from these world-famous berries that the champagne beloved by many is produced - Abrau Durso.

How attractive is the village?

Abrau-Durso is not one village. It consists of two settlements - Abrau and Durso, located next to each other (a distance of seven kilometers). But you have to overcome this path along a picturesque mountain road. Therefore, from Abrau it is not too convenient to get to the sea. Here tourists stop for whom privacy, beauty of nature, peace are important. If you dream of a luxurious beach vacation, we recommend that you go to Durso.



Abrau-Durso: hotels

This wonderful village has been popular since ancient times. Today, Russian tourists enjoy visiting Abrau-Durso. The hotels of this picturesque place may well satisfy the requirements of the most demanding guests.

Some of the vacationers stay in luxury hotels, where all the details for a comfortable stay are thought out. In recent years, small and comfortable private hotels in Abrau-Durso have become very popular. In this article we will present you the most popular hotels, guest houses.

Hotel Imperial (Abrau-Durso)

This magnificent hotel can be called the most popular in the village. Exquisite rooms, excellent service and unique views of the lake - all this awaits you in the Imperial. The unique Champagne-SPA (spa complex) will amaze you with special rituals based on the healing properties of grapes, it will allow you to completely disconnect from everyday problems, and relax your body and soul.



Hotel "Imperial" is decorated in the style of Provence. This provides vacationers a comfortable stay and creates a romantic atmosphere.

The price of each room includes:

  • breakfast;
  • gym;
  • thermal zone;
  • multi-channel TV;
  • outdoor parking.

Hotel Durso

It is not surprising that hotels with a pool coming to Abrau-Durso choose with great pleasure. This choice allows you to relax here not only in the beach season, but also at any time of the year. These institutions include the hotel "Durso", which was built eighty meters from the sea.



Accommodation

The hotel is distinguished by rooms of different levels of comfort.

Standard

The rooms have two single beds and a double wardrobe. All rooms are equipped with modern split systems, televisions, DVD players, stereo systems, refrigerators with minibars, internal telephones, bathrooms with showers. The windows offer beautiful views of the mountain slopes, which cover the relict thickets of juniper.

Junior Suite

These rooms have a double bed, a built-in wardrobe, a chest of drawers, a perfume table with an ottoman, a rattan coffee table, two armchairs, a TV, a split system, a music center, internal phones, a DVD player, a refrigerator with a minibar.

Suite (two-room)

The room has a double and two single beds, wardrobes (in each room), a table for perfumes, a chest of drawers and an ottoman, folding upholstered furniture and a coffee table. All rooms have a DVD player, TV, fridge and minibar, stereo system. You can enjoy picturesque sea views from the windows.

Hotel services:

  • parking;
  • a restaurant;
  • pool;
  • bar;
  • organization of excursions;
  • gazebo;
  • Internet (free).

Guest house "Asteria"

If you do not like a large concentration of holidaymakers, then in Abrau-Durso hotels you can choose small ones. These include the guest house "Asteria". It offers guests an outdoor pool, free parking on site, Wi-Fi. All rooms include a flat-screen TV. Some rooms overlook the garden. Guests have at their disposal a fully equipped bathroom.



At the front desk, guests are happy to see around the clock.

Augustine (mini-hotel)

Today, many Russians are happy to spend their holidays in Abrau-Durso. The hotels of the village are well equipped for a relaxing family vacation. "Augustine" is located seven kilometers from the coast. Wi-Fi (free) and private parking are available.

The rooms of this hotel are two-level. They are equipped with a TV, split system, electric kettle and fridge. Bathrooms offer hair dryers and complimentary toiletries.

If you wish, you can use the additional service for delivering drinks and food to your room, or prepare your own dinner on the grill and enjoy it outdoors.

Lake Abrau is five hundred meters from the hotel. Fans of attractions can visit the Church of St. Xenia of St. Petersburg, which is located nearby. Guests can enjoy fishing, water skiing or cycling.

Hotel Laguna

Do not worry that you will run into housing problems in Abrau-Durso. Resort hotels open their doors to guests throughout the year. Hotel Laguna offers twenty rooms of various categories at reasonable prices. It has everything you need - satellite TV, a bathroom, a minibar and much more.



A shuttle service can be arranged here (at an additional cost). Five minutes from the hotel is Lake Abrau. It offers vacationers an inexpensive cafe that offers Russian dishes. Outdoor enthusiasts have the opportunity to play billiards or to rent a bicycle. Fans of sightseeing holidays will not be bored either. The hotel offers interesting trips, boat trips and exciting fishing.

Number of rooms
