Japanese knitting

If you like to knit or crochet, then you will definitely like Japanese knitting. The technique of this type of needlework will not be much different from the ordinary, but things related in this way look very original and

interesting. Perhaps this is due to the fact that the culture of Japan is very vibrant, expressive and original, and it is reflected not only in art, but also in ordinary knitting with knitting needles and crochet.

Magazines containing Japanese crochet patterns, issued respectively in Japan, are also popular in Russia. In these magazines you can find a lot of new and interesting things for yourself. And knowledge of the language for this is completely optional.

Hats, toys, clothes, various home accessories - all this can be connected using the Japanese knitting technique.
