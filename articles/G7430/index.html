Bouquet of tulips

Such a tender and sweet bouquet on March 8 (it is very easy to make it with your own hands) in the form of tulips is a wonderful idea, because these flowers are one of the first to appear in spring. Tulips are made using plain cotton fabric and wire.

You will need a little patience and care. What do you need for work:

  • candy;
  • pieces of fabric of white, red and pink flowers (size 10 by 10 centimeters);
  • a piece of green felt - for sheets;
  • green tape;
  • scissors.

Execution technique

For those who want to make work easier, there is the opportunity to create a sweet bouquet of sweets in literally an hour.

  • Wrap the candy in a cloth so that you get a tulip bud.
  • From the bottom, stick the skewer and tie the bud with tape, wrap the skewer to the end.
  • Cut green leaves from felt and with the same tape attach them to the base of the bud.
  • Make the required number of flowers from fabrics of different colors.
  • Gather the finished flowers in a bouquet and tie it with a satin ribbon.

Your first sweet bouquet is ready. True, simple enough?

Let's start the process of creating a beautiful and sweet gift. Before making a sweet bouquet of sweets with your own hands, decide on its design. What sweets you need will directly depend on this.

The progress is as follows:

Create an original sweet bouquet: master class

To make the necessary bouquet you need:

  • chocolates - from 7 pieces;
  • bright corrugated paper;
  • a thin ribbon five millimeters wide;
  • green wire;
  • nippers;
  • scissors.

Step-by-step guide

Do you want to learn how to make sweet bouquets with your own hands? For beginners, candy florists will not be difficult to make this gift. From the proposed workshop you will learn how to make a beautiful and delicious bouquet.

The phased implementation is as follows:

  • First cut the tape into seven short sections of ten centimeters. Then, with wire cutters, divide the wire into short pieces, cutting ten centimeters.
  • Cut several rectangles 18 by 12 centimeters from the corrugated ribbon (the same amount as the buds are planned). From the obtained rectangular piece of tape, cut a rectangle with one sloping side with scissors.




  • Fold both pieces of paper into each other. Then put your candy on top of the sheet in the center.
  • Twisting is done along with wire and candy. Next, wrap the top of the wire with green tissue paper.
  • Wrap corrugated paper with tape, but do not tighten it! Take the scissors and curl the ends of the tape. Then fluff and form a magnificent flower bud from them. After lay out the resulting flowers so that it resembles the arrangement of gladiolus flowers.
  • First, fold the three flowers and wind them together on one wooden skewer, using narrow green paper. You can also insert decorative artificial grass for the beauty of the bouquet. And so do with each flower.
  • You should get a magnificent bouquet. All flowers should be stacked together, with the center in a straight line.
  • Next, lay your bouquet on a decorative packing wrapper and tie it with a beautiful wide ribbon.

As you can see, creating a beautiful and sweet bouquet with your own hands is quite simple. The main thing is to express a desire and apply a little imagination.
