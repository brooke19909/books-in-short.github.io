Recreation facilities in Makhachkala by the sea: photos and reviews of tourists

In everyday life, we often hear a lot of reviews about such popular resorts as Antalya and Alanya, Egypt and Vietnam, and completely forget about sunny Dagestan, where nature itself has prepared everything necessary for an amazing vacation. Therefore, today we want to talk about recreation centers in Makhachkala. What can surprise you with this balneo-mud seaside resort? First of all, it is worth noting the stunning warm climate and favorable geomagnetic environment, which will positively affect meteosensitive people. Mineral waters and mud of local salt lakes do not go unnoticed by tourists.

The Caucasus is the most famous Russian health resort, there are as many unique resorts as there are nowhere else. Recreation facilities in Makhachkala are located in one of the picturesque places of the Caucasus, which has the most healthy factors. These are the sea and mountains, healing mineral springs, clean air.



Base "Dolphin"

It is located on the shores of the Caspian Sea, just eight kilometers from Makhachkala. The base itself is located in a fenced area, which is decorated in a completely home-like manner, like a cozy landscaped courtyard. There is enough space for car parking, reading and socializing. The sea is located only 70 meters from the building. Equipped sandy beach and calls into its hot embrace. If your goal is quiet, comfortable and not too expensive recreation centers in Makhachkala, Dolphin is exactly what you are looking for.



Number of rooms

To accommodate guests there is a whole complex of practically separate houses (despite the apparent autonomy, they are a single building), each of which has its own individual features and characteristics. Recreation facilities in Makhachkala have good conditions, and this is no exception. The rooms are very comfortable, with all amenities, in each room there is a TV and a fridge, air conditioning, hot and cold water. It offers guests a spa and wellness center. Judging by the reviews of tourists, this recreation center is a great place for a family vacation. A stunning beach, many excursion routes, developed infrastructure, a huge number of opportunities for a great vacation.



Cost of rest

This is one of the most important factors by which recreation centers in Makhachkala are selected. Dolphin is a place suitable for budget travelers. Double junior suite will cost you only 1000 rubles per day. A double superior room with air conditioning costs 1800 rubles, and a quadruple - 2500. For 3000 rubles you can rent a five-bed cottage. Judging by the reviews of tourists, all rooms are clean and comfortable.

The recreation center "Anji"

Next, I would like to tell the reader about the Anji Hotel. The recreation center in Makhachkala should be bright and sunny, just like this friendly land, and is fully suitable for these requirements. It functions actively from May 1 to October, that is, throughout the summer season. Another name for the hotel is Arena. Adults and children have a great time here, so do not hesitate to choose this option for a family trip, as well as for fun corporate parties. First of all, the guests, of course, are interested in the warm Caspian Sea and the golden sand, only after a few days tourists begin to be interested in other entertainments, and there are not so few of them here. It has a modern bar and sauna, a huge pool with sun loungers, and a tour desk.



Accommodation for tourists

The recreation centers in Makhachkala differ in the level of comfort of the apartments that they have prepared for their guests. At the recreation center "Anji", rest is somewhat more expensive, however, the rooms look much more interesting. In addition, you can choose one of three options: standard, luxury or VIP. The latter are especially good, with panoramic windows and a stylish interior. Standard room rates start at 3,000 rubles, while VIP rates start at 5,000 rubles per day. Tourists emphasize that the level of service here is much higher than at other bases, however, payment is not available to everyone.

Hotel "Jami"

We continue to consider recreation facilities in Makhachkala. Jami is one of the most modern complexes, which is great for you to spend your vacation here. Three spacious two-story buildings are presented to your attention, each of which consists of 18 superior rooms with balconies and loggias. The territory of the recreation center pleasantly surprises with its well-groomed. Across the territory, neat walking paths with lamp lighting, the houses themselves, like toys, are bright and elegant.



Infrastructure

Like other recreation centers in Makhachkala, Jami offers tourists many opportunities for entertainment and relaxation. It features a huge gym and volleyball court, the most common sports equipment. A massage room and a medical center are constantly working on site. Every day for those who wish, meetings are held with the masters of the arts of Dagestan. In the evening, guests are invited to a fun disco. The base has its own beach, fully equipped, with sun loungers and changing rooms, as well as showers. Judging by the reviews, the rest here is always bright and interesting, especially noteworthy is that it is very interesting for children. They with great pleasure spend time on bright, elegant playgrounds, on attractions.
