Hotels of Yevpatoriya. Evpatoria, Crimea: rest, hotels

Tourists from all over the world come to the Crimea to have a great vacation, bask in the gentle sun, soak up the sandy beach, swim in the waters of the Black Sea. However, not one vacation is complete without a search for a place to live. You can visit the numerous hotels in Yevpatoriya, which have proved themselves only from the best side. Close to the sea location, many additional services in hotels - all this makes this vacation attractive for travelers. This article will describe several hotels.





Empire Hotel

Every tourist can look into the hotel "Empire" (Evpatoria, city center). The restaurant of this hotel complex will delight guests with delicious dishes of European cuisine. The hotel's medical center offers wellness services, massages, beauty treatments, a sauna and a large indoor pool.



The hotel complex "Empire" is a three-star. It is a 5-minute walk from the coast. The Imperia Hotel (Yevpatoriya) is located in a popular resort area, therefore it is surrounded by many shops, cafes, restaurants, pharmacies and other institutions. Registration at the hotel is as comfortable as possible for tourists.

Due to its proximity, travelers can walk from the hotel in a few minutes to the Arboretum, Gogol Square, Frunze Park. The latter, by the way, is the Central Park of Recreation and Culture.

Dolphinarium is 300 meters away. At 1 km from the "Empire" is the local Disneyland. To always stay in touch with friends and relatives, to share their impressions with them, travelers can take advantage of free Wi-Fi access.
