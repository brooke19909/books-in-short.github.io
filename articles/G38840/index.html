Electric grill: types, advantages

How nice to go with your family out of town, cook kebabs or grilled chicken. Well, when there is such an opportunity. And if she is not? What about those who want to cook something similar in their apartment? In this case, an electric grill will come to the rescue. Surely you immediately imagined a massive device that can be seen in restaurants or cafes. In fact, modern technology has made it possible to create quite compact grills that can be easily placed even in the smallest kitchen. But first things first.



If you have a burning desire to purchase a device that allows you to enjoy a delicious chicken or grilled fish, then pay attention to the types and possibilities of such devices. Immediately put aside stationary electric grills, as they are large and designed for maximum use in a country house. An electric grill, the so-called portable, is your option. It is with its help that you can cook meat and fish without using vegetable oil.

The cheapest option, and at the moment one of the most popular, is a one-sided electric grill. The principle of its operation is that the products are laid out on a grate, under which there is a water tray. The heating surface may be different, depending on the model - metal, glass-ceramic, stone. The most unpretentious is a metal surface, since it will cost less, and will take care of it

easier. Glass ceramics are afraid of cold water and clean worse. Difficulties in washing will arise in the case of non-stick coating, as it is easy to scratch it. The electric one-sided grill is easy to operate, but the minus is that the products are baked on one side, so they will have to be turned over periodically.

At home, you can use a double-sided grill for chickens electric. Its difference is that the heating surface is located not only from below, but also above, and in some models on the sides. Such models are more powerful, cook faster and more efficiently.

Well, pay attention to non-contact electric grills. In this case, for example, chicken or meat is mounted on a skewer (skewer), which constantly rotates during cooking. Thanks to such a device at home, it‚Äôs easy

You can even cook kebabs. The number of skewers depends on the model, in some of them several at once. Skewers can be located both vertically and horizontally. The non-contact electric grill is large, costs an order of magnitude more expensive, but cooks tastier and faster thanks to its considerable power. In addition, during the cooking process there is no need to turn the products over, and you can monitor everything through the glass door.

This is how an electric grill happens. Reviews about him are quite positive, they say that chicken, meat and fish are very juicy and fragrant. Many models are compact, so even in a small kitchen there is a place for them. Of course, during the purchase you should pay attention not only to power, but also to size. After all, the larger the family, the larger the device itself should be.
