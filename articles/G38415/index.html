Where and when is it better to go on vacation to Tunisia, what time of year?

Every year, Russians ask themselves: where to spend a vacation. And often, in their search for a suitable place, the hot country of Tunisia arises. When is it better to go on vacation to the Tunisian coast, what to do there and which resort to choose? We will tell you about all the features of vacation in this country.





Geographical position

In the northeast of the African continent is the Republic of Tunisia or, as it is popularly called, Tunisia. When is it better to go on vacation to this country? It depends on the purpose of the trip. The state is located on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea and has a very long history of human settlement. Therefore, it can offer tourists a varied vacation.

Tunisia borders with Libya and Algeria, on the water it is adjacent to Italy. The northern part of the country occupied the spurs of the African mountain system Atlas, the coastal territories are characterized by a flat landscape. In the south of the state is located the famous Sahara desert. And the middle of the country is a series of salty picturesque lakes.

The capital of the state is the city of Tunisia, this is the largest settlement. Besides him, here you can visit about 15 more large cities. The coastal part of the country is most populated; the most attractive cities for tourists are located here.





Climate

In the ratings of countries beloved by Russians for beach holidays, Tunisia is not the last place. When is it better to go to rest in this state? This question can be answered if we evaluate the local climate. The country is located immediately in two natural areas: the Mediterranean subtropical and tropical desert. The weather in the state is influenced by two main factors: the Mediterranean Sea, which softens and moistens the air along the coast, and the Sahara desert, which in the summer contributes to a significant increase in temperature.

Tourists mainly come to the coast, where the mild Mediterranean climate dominates with long hot summers and short rainy winters. In the summer, the thermometer rises on average from 22 to 32 degrees, and in winter - it stays in the region of 7-15 degrees of heat.





Best season

The main question for those who are going to go to Tunisia: when is it better to go on vacation? It depends on why the tourist is going to this interesting country. For those who are eager to swim and sunbathe, but not much "fry" in the sun, the best time is from April to June and September to early October. In July-August there is quite a lot of heat up to plus 40-45. But the sea breeze greatly facilitates the existence in such heat. Therefore, these months are attributed to the "high" tourist season, which affects prices, which increase by 30-40%.

But for those who want to get acquainted with the culture and history of this ancient country, March-April and October-November are perfect. The rainiest months are January-February, and traditionally at this time the flow of tourists is reduced to the lowest levels. This instantly affects the prices of hotels and food, so those who want to see Tunisia and save a lot of money come here during this period. Therefore, a definite answer to the question: in Tunisia, what is the best time of the year for a vacation, which is not. It all depends on the needs and opportunities.

Therefore, it is important to decide, going to Tunisia, where it is better to go on vacation - to small non-tourist cities or untwisted resort places.

Resorts

Reflecting on which part of Tunisia it is better to go to rest, it is again worth evaluating your desires and interests. The most famous and popular resorts of the country are Sousse, Monastir, Mahdia, Hammamet. The latter is the most prestigious and fashionable vacation spot. Only Djerba island can compete with him in a luxurious vacation, where the best luxury hotels are located. Prices in these places are above average, but the service is excellent.

Sousse is a gathering place for young people, there are many nightclubs, pubs, restaurants and open-air discos, the largest and most famous of which is Voga-Voga. A universal resort can be called Hammamet, which harmoniously combines the possibilities for a relaxing, and for youth recreation.

Monastir can be called calm and quite economical, but you need to be prepared for the fact that the tourist infrastructure here has long been in need of modernization. The capital of the country is a real eastern metropolis, therefore it is better to come here for a tour, but not to stay for long, since it is difficult to relax here.

One of the youngest and most developing resorts is El Kantaoui, where you can find fairly modern hotels and a good infrastructure for tourists, but the prices here can not be called low.





sights

Tunisia leads its documented history from the 9th century BC. e., when the city of Carthage was formed on the site of a modern country. And today, the remains of this ancient city are the most important attraction of the country.

The second most important place that tourists need to see is the Sahara desert. Also, having arrived in Tunisia, you need to visit the blue-white city of Sidi Bou Said, which allows you to see the traditional way of life of Tunisians.

Interesting sights also include the UNESCO-protected Colosseum in El Jem, the Sidi Ukba Mosque in Kairouan, the city of Berbers Matmath.





Things to do

For many tourists, Tunisia becomes a synonym for the beach. Rest, the time of the year for which is limited in cold Russia, of course, in this country is the main entertainment. However, in Tunisia, there is something to do without it.

The second most popular pastime, after the beach, is an excursion to the Sahara. It is worth seeing this famous place, you do not need to think that it is only endless sands. As part of the traditional two-day tour, tourists ride camels and ATVs, visit the homes of the Bedouins, see the sights: Mount Tembain, Fort Tisawar, oases, sample local dishes, and enjoy magnificent sunsets and sunrises.

Another attraction for tourists is the great opportunity for thalassotherapy. Along the coast of the country, medicinal algae grows, which help improve the condition of the body and skin. In Tunisia, you can find chic spa centers and small beauty parlors with reasonable prices, but in every place the services will be of decent quality.

Also, tourists are offered to visit crocodile farms, go diving and fishing, take a trip on a yacht. In addition, there are numerous clubs, cafes, discos, water parks for vacationers.





Vacation with children

There are many reasons to visit Tunisia. When is the best time to travel with your child to this wonderful country? For this, time with a mild warm temperature regime is perfect - this is April-June, September-October.

With children, it is better to choose calmer cities, for example, Sousse, Hammamet, Tabarka, Nabeul. In each of them you can find zoos, water parks, amusement parks, playgrounds, museums and entertainment centers. For families with children, Tunisia is quite safe and comfortable, you just have to take the usual safety measures, for example, wash your hands, do not drink tap water, do not get involved in spicy street food.





Tourists reviews

More and more Russians recently visit Tunisia. When is it better to go to rest? Reviews say that the country is beautiful at almost any time of the year, except for cloudy and rainy January and February, although there is something to do in these months too.

Tourists note in their stories the excellent centers of thalassotherapy, fascinating travels in the Sahara and a rich excursion program. Also, vacationers talk about the excellent beaches of the country with fine white sand and a convenient gentle slope to the water.

You can also read in the reviews that additional security measures must be observed in the country, as fraudsters and thieves are often found here, and the rest of the country is friendly and safe.
