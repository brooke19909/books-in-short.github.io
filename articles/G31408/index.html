Recreation facilities on the Russian island: tourist recommendations

Recreation facilities on the Russian island are in constant demand among the residents of Primorye, since it is here that you can get high-quality service for relatively little money. A large number of tourist organizations allow you not to spend money on the first available object, but to make a thoughtful and right choice.



Where is Russian island located?

The Russian island is located in the Sea of ​​Japan, while the distance between it and the southern part of Vladivostok is 800 meters. The capital of Primorye itself is located on the Muravyov-Amursky peninsula, recreation centers on the Russian island are separated from it by a small strait, which is called the Eastern Bosphorus. The geographical object is washed by the Ussuri and Amur bays.

The total area of ​​the island is almost 98 square kilometers, about 5300 inhabitants live on it. The local bay, called Novik, stretches from the northwestern part of the island to the southeast and divides it into two parts. The narrow part, located in the northeast, is sometimes called the Saperny peninsula.

Why exactly there?

Despite the fact that in the Primorsky Territory there are a huge number of recreational facilities, recreation centers on the Russian island are extremely popular. Their most important advantage is low prices for the services provided. For just a few thousand rubles, you can rent a house for several days with meals.

Another important factor is the close distance from the regional capital and transport accessibility. After the construction of the bridge in 2013, the number of vacationers here has grown significantly, since now the island can be easily reached with your own car and public transport, which runs here with enviable regularity.



"White Swan"

The recreation center on the Russian island "White Swan" is a dispensary where anyone can put their own health in order. It is here that several medical departments function at once, where they treat with mud, sea water, physiotherapeutic procedures, exercise therapy, massage, etc. All applied procedures were created on the basis of programs developed by research institutes specializing in rehabilitation therapy.

Vacationers here are offered rooms "economy", "standard", "comfort" and "comfort +". All of them are located in capital buildings and wooden houses (the latter are intended only for living in the summer). Vacationers can purchase a ticket for 10 days, check-in for 2-3 days will have to be discussed with the administration of the institution. With all this, the cost of living can vary from 1800 to 4700 rubles, depending on the chosen house and a range of services.

What should not be confused with?

The recreation center on Lake Kaldy - “Russian Island” - is located in the Chelyabinsk region, however, this does not prevent vacationers from trying to find a similar institution in Primorye. In this region there are enough other tourist sites specializing in traditional Russian holidays. Some institutions allow you to visit your territory without buying a ticket, which is very welcomed by the guests of the island.

Here you can not be afraid of insects - the owners of the bases annually in several stages conduct anti-mite treatment of the territory. The Russian island welcomes guests all year round, in summer you can enjoy beach vacations and fishing, and in winter - ice skating, sledding and skiing. Constantly working Russian baths, cafes, playgrounds and bars. If you wish, you can organize various events, local event-teams will be happy to satisfy any of your whims.



Reviews about Russian island

The best place in Primorye, where a recreation center that is convenient and cost-effective can be located, is Russky Ostrov. Reviews of local travel agencies are mostly positive. The guests of the island as a positive moment especially note the possibility of relaxing in the local bases without acquiring rooms and vouchers.

Some visitors complain about too high prices, but against the background of all the other tourist bases of Primorye this seems like an unnecessary demand. And, of course, we must not forget about the free opportunity to enjoy magnificent landscapes that look especially impressive in the winter season.



"American"

The recreation center "American" (Russky Island) consists of several wooden houses and cottages that surround four comfortable houses. Each room has beds, TV, fridge, all necessary furniture. Bed linen is provided, which is periodically replaced. In the summer months, barbecue facilities, benches and tables are specially installed near small houses. Most of the cottages are located a few meters from the sea.

If we talk about the cost of living, in this case everything will depend on whether the recreation center "American" is free. Russky Island at the peak of the season is constantly filled with tourists, and it is difficult to find a free place here. Cheap houses are bought up quickly, the cost of living in them is 2500-3000 rubles per day. More expensive rooms can be purchased for an amount of 3.5 to 4 thousand rubles per day. Meals are not included in the price, however there is a cafe that is ready to feed the guests of the base at reasonable prices.

"Renaissance"

The base "Renaissance" (Russky Island, Vladivostok) is another tourist organization offering a high-class vacation to everyone. The institution offers to use summer houses, baths, gazebos, cafes, cottages and other infrastructure at very attractive prices. A large number of rooms of different classes are constantly replenished, the administration is expanding the number of additional services.

The cost of renting a room for a day here is 3-6 thousand rubles, visiting the bath - 1-3 thousand rubles per hour. When holding a buffet or other celebrations, the service of one guest is from 1.8 to 3 thousand rubles. On October 5, 2015, the base was closed for reconstruction, it is planned that it will again work on April 29, 2016.



"Surf"

In search of a quality holiday, you should definitely pay attention to the Russian island. Base "Surf" - one of the local tourist agencies, which operates only in the summer. Despite this, it is quite difficult to book summer houses here, as they are located on the shores of the strait and offer wonderful views.

One of the main advantages of the base is that vacationers get all the necessary appliances for cooking - plates, pots, pans, kettles, and there is also a gas stove in every house. If you don’t want to cook on your own, you can visit the local dining room with fairly reasonable prices. Electricity is supplied without interruption, and spring water is delivered in bottled containers.



"Panorama"

Recreation facilities on the Russian island, although not numerous, but allow their guests to relax and enjoy pastime away from civilization. "Panorama" offers vacationers to use sports equipment rental, sightseeing tours, as well as a wide range of additional services.

Here you can move into one of four one-story buildings, consisting of several lounges. In most of them you can put an extra (third) bed, of course, at an additional cost. Meals are not included in the price, it must be paid separately. You can get to this base with the help of a ferry that runs between the island and Vladivostok daily.



"White Swan"

Recreation facilities on the Russian island mainly work only in the warm season, this also applies to the institution "White Swan". This base appeared here relatively recently, but is already very popular with local tourists. Vacationers are offered accommodation in comfortable double rooms with all necessary amenities. Each room has a TV, fridge and loggia.

The high quality of the services provided and the comfort offered can seriously hit travelers. In particular, renting an apartment here can cost 5-12 thousand rubles. There are more advantageous offers. So, standard rooms can be rented for a day for 4-6 thousand rubles. The exact cost of living is best checked with the administration of the base, since it can vary both up and down.
