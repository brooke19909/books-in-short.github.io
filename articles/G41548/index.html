Phone "Nokia Lumia 530": reviews, specifications

Nokia Lumia phones are among the most popular in Russia. Fans of mobile electronics from the Russian Federation respect the Finnish (recently - de jure American - after a well-known transaction with Microsoft) brand for the consistently high build quality of devices, functionality, performance, excellent design. To what extent do these features characterize the Nokia Lumia 530 smartphone?

Specifications

Let's consider the main characteristics that the Nokia Lumia 530 Dual SIM mobile phone has.





The device is controlled by the Windows Phone OS version 8.1.

The phone has a processor with 4 cores and a clock frequency of 1200 MHz.

The RAM of the smartphone is 512 MB.

The built-in flash memory is 4 GB, it is possible to install additional microSD modules up to 128 GB.

The device supports 2 SIM cards.

The diagonal of the phone‚Äôs display is 4 inches, the resolution is 854 by 480 pixels, the density is 245 pixels per inch. The sensor is a capacitive type, with support for Multitouch.

Camera - 5 MP, video can be shot at a speed of 30 frames / sec.

Support for communication standards: Bluetooth in version 4.0, Wi-Fi, 3G, DLNA, USB, 3.5 mm audio. Possible synchronization with PC. There is support for GPS, A-GPS, GLONASS.

Battery capacity: 1430 mAh.

Model Features

What features is characterized by Nokia Lumia 530? Feedback from users and specialists in this regard is very different. Many focus on an exceptionally attractive combination of price and device functionality. The cost of a smartphone gives reason to talk about it as one of the most competitive in the relevant aspect on the market.





According to some users and experts, the phone in question has something special that compares favorably with other budget models: an approach to design. In this sense, the most affordable devices from Nokia are not much different from premium models. The Lumia line is characterized by a noticeable dissimilarity of individual devices in terms of hardware, but the appearance of all devices is uniform and equally attractive.

Another interesting feature of the Nokia Lumiya 530 phone (reviews by many experts confirm this) is that it unifies with flagship models in terms of software as well. The interface structure of the Windows Phone 8.1 OS installed in the device is almost the same as in the premium devices of the Lumia line from Microsoft.

Construction and appearance

We begin a detailed study of the phone with its appearance. As some experts note, the design of the device in question is to some extent similar to that developed for the Lumia 510 smartphone (despite the fact that the appearance of the devices is uniform throughout the entire lineup). The material of the phone case is of excellent quality plastic. The controls of the smartphone are standard and their location implies a very high comfort of using the device. If you remove the back cover from the phone, you will see slots for additional memory cards and SIM cards, as well as the device‚Äôs battery.





Owners do not record any backlashes, obvious creaks during the operation of the smartphone. Convenience of use and high build quality are the key, according to many users, characteristics of the appearance and design of the Nokia Lumiya 530. The reviews of many experts in the field of mobile gadgets are generally close to this assessment. From this point of view, the device in question compares favorably with many budget models of smartphones.

Screen

The device‚Äôs display has the same diagonal as the Lumia 520 phone, however, the resolution is slightly different - 854 by 840 pixels (800 by 480 in the 520 model). Such a difference, according to some experts, is due to the desire of designers to provide playback on screen video in 16: 9 format. Experts attribute the fact that the pixel density in the phone is higher than in the indicated analogue: the Lumia 520 screen is characterized by an indicator of 235 ppi, the model in question is 246 ppi.

At the same time, the display that is present on the Nokia Lumiya 530 phone (reviews by many enthusiasts of mobile gadgets reflect a not entirely positive assessment of this feature) is made using relatively outdated TN technology. While the standard solution, even for many budget devices, is an IPS-type matrix. The screen, made by TN technology, in general, has good performance characteristics - in terms of support for the Multitouch option, for example, however, it has small viewing angles. In addition, in bright lighting, glare appears on the TN-type display. The developers of this smartphone model, in addition, did not begin to equip the device screen with an oleophobic coating - which, in principle, can also be considered a standard solution even in the segment of budget devices. This feature predetermines tangible difficulties with the removal of various contaminants from the display, as well as fingerprints.





The phone does not have a function to automatically adjust the screen brightness. But this is not related to the quality of the corresponding hardware component - the smartphone simply does not have a light sensor installed . However, according to customer reviews of Nokia Lumia 530, the absence of this option predetermines a minimum of inconvenience. In addition, as smartphone users note, you can always change the brightness of the device‚Äôs screen manually - by choosing its low, medium or high level (but these options are not fixed, you can use the smooth way to determine the optimal screen brightness).

The display of the smartphone allows, thanks to Multitouch technology, to process up to 5 simultaneous finger touches. Experts, as well as device users, rate the quality of the corresponding function as high.

Camera

The Nokia Lumiya 530 phone (reviews of some users are also not very positive in this part) has only one camera located at the back of the device. The lack of frontal is criticized by fans of "selfie" and talking on Skype. And this despite the fact that on many competing models - 2 cameras. Also, many users complain that the corresponding hardware component is not equipped with either a flash or autofocus. However, the camera has a good resolution - 5 MP. The quality of the images under adequate environmental conditions is characterized by users and specialists as quite decent. And this despite the fact that much more pronounced portions of criticism in terms of image quality sometimes go to premium models of smartphones. By the way, not all the flagships of the leaders in the market of mobile gadgets are equipped with the same autofocus function.

Like other devices of the Lumia line, the device in question is characterized by excellent sound reproduction quality, and in all modes - through the speaker or when using headphones. Iron and performance

The phone is equipped with a powerful Snapdragon 200 processor with 4 cores. The chip runs on a Cortex-A7 architecture. Its clock frequency is 1.2 GHz. The processor is also equipped with a built-in Adreno 302 graphics processing module, which, experts say, is optimized for working with office software, video files, as well as for launching undemanding games. The amount of RAM is 512 MB. It may seem that this is a bit, since many competing budget solutions have an indicator of 1 GB or more. However, the specifics of the Windows Phone OS is such that it does not need a lot of RAM for its functioning. The specified amount of RAM is enough to run popular applications and games.





Test results

What about the actual performance of the Nokia Lumia 530? The reviews of the owners of the device, as well as the opinions of experts characterize this aspect as a whole very positively. In the MultiBench tests, the device showed results that are also comparable to the numbers for the Lumia 630.
