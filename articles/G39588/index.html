Guest house "Sosnovy Bor", Pitsunda: reviews of tourists

Pitsunda is an amazing resort in Abkhazia, which was extremely popular in Soviet times. To date, this legendary city has not lost its relevance and is in demand among Russian tourists.

Pitsunda - the pearl of Abkhazia

Pitsunda is located at a distance of about forty kilometers from the Abkhaz-Russian border. The uniqueness of the resort is that almost all along the coast of Pitsunda there is a coniferous grove with relict pines. Forest in some places grows right on the beach. The air is saturated with the aroma of pine and sea, has healing properties.





Exotic nature, a unique climate, the cleanest sea and gorgeous beaches are what vacationers come to this wonderful place for. Abkhazia is a picturesque and proud country with its own flavor, as if it was created specifically for recreation. However, the political situation has done its job. The resort infrastructure of Pitsunda is somewhat neglected. Perhaps that is why rest here is quite economical. There are no new hotels of the European level in Pitsunda, the number of vacationers is quite moderate. They are located mainly in private households, guest houses and mini-pensions.

Guest House Sosnovy Bor (Abkhazia, Pitsunda)

When planning their vacation in Abkhazia, many tourists prefer quiet, picturesque places to relax, which give peace and peace of mind. One of these places is the village of Ldzaa (Pitsunda).

Sosnovy Bor is a guest house located in this cozy village. The recreation center is located on the second line from the beach, fifty meters from the pine forest. Homeliness, modest prices for vacations and decent living conditions await vacationers in this beautiful place.





Sosnovy Bor Guest House (Pitsunda) is a small resort complex that consists of a main two-story building and one-story cottages. Near each cottage there is a resting place, a green meadow, alleys and benches. The territory is very neat, there are mowed lawns and flower beds everywhere, small interesting palm trees and shrubs very decorate the yard.

Accommodation for vacationers

Guest house "Sosnovy Bor" (Pitsunda) offers vacationers twenty-five rooms of economy and standard categories. The hotel is assigned a category of two stars, so the conditions here are appropriate.

Economy rooms of the guest house "Sosnovy Bor" 2 * (Pitsunda) - these are separate small wooden houses from a log house, designed to accommodate two or three people. The rooms do not have amenities, bathrooms are on site. The appearance of these rooms, judging by the photographs presented, is decent and very attractive, in the so-called forest style. Inside, everything is lined with wood, the furniture is pretty decent: beds, bedside tables, a chest of drawers with a mirror. Each room has air conditioning and a TV on the wall. At the entrance there is a cozy porch with a table and stools.





Standard rooms in the Sosnovy Bor guest house (Pitsunda) are located in detached single-story houses, as well as in a two-story building.

Log houses with amenities have a larger area than houses with economy rooms. Basically they are designed for three or four people. Shower and toilet in the room. There is no kitchen. Inside the walls are sheathed in wood. The furniture looks very beautiful: beds, bedside tables, an armchair and a wardrobe. The house has a refrigerator, air conditioning and TV. To relax at the entrance there is a veranda with a table and chairs. There are one-room rooms, and there are more expensive ones - two-room ones. The second room is a living room with a large sofa, a coffee table and armchairs.





Standard rooms (double and triple) are also located in a two-story hotel-type cottage. The furniture looks pretty high quality: beds, bedside tables, wardrobe. There is a TV and air conditioning. Shower and toilet in the room. On the ground floor, each room has access to its terrace, on the second floor there is a balcony.

Catering

On the territory of the guest house "Sosnovy Bor" (Pitsunda) there is an equipped summer kitchen for those vacationers who want to cook their own meals. The kitchen is an open summer house with a kitchenette, two refrigerators, a TV. A gas stove, microwave, kettle and enough dishes are all for common use. In front of the kitchen there is a large platform with tables and benches.

For those who do not want to cook on their own, there is a dining room next to the guest house, where you can buy set breakfasts, lunches and dinners.

Hotel infrastructure

Wide pebble beaches, a majestic pine forest and a unique climate are the main advantages of the Pitsunda resort. Sosnovy Bor is a guest house that gives its vacationers the opportunity to enjoy all these charms as much as possible. The air here is healing, saturated with coniferous volatile and iodine.





The hotel has 24-hour parking, a children's playground and a barbecue area. Nearby there are shops, a beauty salon, a bar, a dining room.

The center of Pitsunda is only three kilometers from the guest house.

Tourists reviews

What are the real features of the rest in the guest house Sosnovy Bor (Pitsunda)? Reviews of tourists will help to understand this and draw conclusions about the positive and negative sides of this place.

There are a lot of reviews about Sosnovy Bor, both good and not very good. This speaks, first of all, about the popularity of this hotel, the photos of which are very pretty and attractive and, apparently, really attract tourists.

Tourists who understand that the level of the Sosnovy Bor Hotel is only two stars are mostly satisfied with their vacation. They note the simplicity and hospitality of the owner, hotel guests feel his care and attention to customers. Vacationers from economy rooms are satisfied with the sufficient availability of bathrooms and their cleanliness. There are no complaints about the kitchen, there are a lot of dishes, there are no problems with cooking. Tourists write that they rest in this place for several years in a row, and this is also an indicator.





There are also negative reviews full of negativity. They are completely opposite to the previous ones, and it seems that they are written about different places. They say about the carelessness of the administration, about various breakdowns of household appliances, about the poor condition of the rooms, mold and dirt. Some are unhappy with the reservation, others with a beach, others with a lack of good internet.

It is worth saying that there are more sincere good reviews about the hotel than bad ones, but the latter are nevertheless alarming.

Pros and Cons of the Hotel

Based on such conflicting reviews, we will try to draw conclusions about the pros and cons of the guest house.

Pros:

  • beautiful nature around the hotel;
  • unique climate;
  • proximity to the sea;
  • the presence of a playground;
  • spacious, well-kept yard;
  • green spaces in the territory;
  • peace and quiet;
  • simplicity and hospitality of the owners.

Minuses:

  • lack of service;
  • deterioration of some numbers;
  • lack of food at the hotel.

For any vacation, mood and good company are very important. A vacation in an economy room without amenities may be the best if dear people are nearby and have a good mood, while around there is magnificent nature and a warm gentle sea.

If you want to spend your vacation in silence, surrounded by elegant pine trees on the banks of a clean bay, then you should visit this beautiful place - Abkhazia, Pitsunda, guest house "Sosnovy Bor".
