Briefly about Side

The city is a popular resort area in the Turkish Mediterranean and is located between no less famous and favorite domestic tourists Alanya and Antalya. Despite the modest territory, in different directions from the center of Side stretched stripes of hotels and beaches. In addition, nearby villages are often included in the resort area.





The formation of the cuisine of this part of Turkey was largely influenced by the traditions of the Mediterranean. Therefore, here you can taste an incredible amount of seafood and different types of fish.

The main destination in Side is a beach holiday. However, the area attracts another category of guests - fans of excursion tourism, since the resort has an incredible number of ruins of ancient structures. No wonder, because the city was founded by the ancient Greeks. Evidence of their stay can be called a small cape, on which fragments of walls, an amphitheater, temples, baths, a market square and much more have been preserved.

Getting to Side is easy. The nearest airport is Antalya. Air traffic connects it with many Russian cities. Travel time is 3.5 hours, and the ticket price is from $ 250. You can find cheaper options, but the flight will be long (5.5-18 hours) due to transfers in Istanbul and Ankara.

From Antalya to Side is only an hour away by car. If you drive on your own, then at the exit from the terminals you can use the services of taxi drivers who are on duty there around the clock. On average, a trip to the Royal Dragon Hotel will cost 270-300 Turkish liras. In order to save money, you should take a walk to the bus station located near the airport. From here ply dolmushi - shuttle buses. The cost of the trip in this case will be from 20 to 30 Turkish liras.





And those who are not in a hurry or are afraid of flights can choose other ways of travel:

  1. By bus. Travel time will be more than three days. There are many options for the route - through Georgia, Bulgaria and other countries. For example, leaving Moscow, you can get to Batumi in 48 hours, then transfer by bus to Antalya (another 26 hours). Then drive to Side by dolmush or taxi to Royal Dragon Hotel 5 *.
  2. On a ferryboat. Twice a week, high-speed vessels depart from Sochi to Trabzon. Cruise time - 4.5 hours. Then you have to take a bus to Antalya, and then to Side.
