The city of Pitsunda is located in a beautiful place in Abkhazia. Thousands of tourists annually come to these parts to enjoy nature, silence, breathe in the fresh sea air. The central place here is Cypress Alley, it is not difficult to book a cottage, you can do this in advance.





Abkhazia Pitsunda

The city of Pitsunda was founded in the distant VI century BC by merchants from the ancient Greek Miletus. Pitius - the city was named after relict pines, which grow only in the Colchis Valley (in this area). In the 1st century, Christianity reigned in Abkhazia, and Pitsunda was the center from where the teachings of Christ spread throughout the Caucasus. The Pitsunda Church of the Assumption of the Mother of God of the X-XI century is still preserved, it is still operating, it receives hundreds of pilgrims and tourists. It hosts services, concerts of organ music.

In the last century, in the 60s, the Soviet government decided to equip a resort area in Pitsunda. The purpose of its existence is not only treatment, sanatorium and resort services, but also just the relaxation of Soviet citizens on the sea coast. The city has been built many hotels, chic restaurants, equipped beaches on the sea coast. In the Soviet Union, the resorts of Abkhazia were considered the most prestigious and in demand. Today, in Pitsunda, the doors of hotels and resorts for vacationers are also open. The Cypress Alley, where cozy private hotels are located, is waiting for its guests. Everyone who rested in local cottages responds with warmth and love about the days spent here.

Resort Climate





The climate in the resort of Pitsunda is subtropical. There is no cold winter here. The average winter temperature is 3-5 degrees above zero. Spring and autumn are warm enough: 15-20 degrees. Summer is hot - 27-35 degrees above zero. Precipitation most often occurs in winter (rain). Year-round warm and mild climate allows you to constantly bloom and bear fruit to various plants. So already in February-March, mimosa blooms, and on New Year's Eve everyone is waiting for Abkhazian sweet tangerines, which no overseas fruit can compare with.

You can relax in Pitsunda all year round. Those who gather here should take into account that the rainiest month is January, and August is the hottest. Humidity reaches an average of 85 percent. Despite all these factors, high in the mountains of the Caucasus there are glaciers that never melt. Vacationers leave wonderful photos under palm trees where snow-capped peaks can be seen in the background.

Tourism development

Pitsunda is a great place to spend a vacation. In these paradises, people do not just relax. The proximity of the sea, mountain air, the smell of pine needles and fir resin, the gentle sun - all this allows you to get such a charge of vivacity, energy, which will be enough for the entire upcoming working period. Those who wish to improve their health also go to these parts. Mud, hydrogen sulfide baths, mineral springs help many get rid of long-standing ailments or relieve chronic diseases.

Of course, Abkhazia attracts tourists with its amazing nature. The cypress alley in Pitsunda is the highlight of the city, and everyone who has already been here is dreaming of coming back here. Sea water on local beaches is considered the cleanest on the entire Black Sea coast.

Local authorities are striving to develop tourism in every possible way; more and more mini-hotels are being built in the private sector. Small cozy cottages are more popular. It is in such villages as Cypress Alley that people feel their unity with nature. Enjoy your stay.
