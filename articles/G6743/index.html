Lenovo A916: owner reviews, specifications

A stylish phablet with excellent technical specifications and a flawless design is the Lenovo A916. Reviews about it, its features, pros and cons of this device will be further examined in detail.

Phablet positioning

Initially, this device was a mid-range solution. In November 2014, its technical specifications and cost were fully consistent with this particular market segment. But now, two years after the start of sales, this gadget has already sunk into the niche of entry-level devices. The result is a very interesting solution, which is based on a full-fledged 8-core CPU and is equipped with a large display whose diagonal is five and a half inches.





Therefore, Lenovo A916 is of most interest to those who need a large-sized device with good hardware.

Equipment

The bundle for this phablet is as follows:

  • The smartphone itself.
  • 2500 mAh battery.
  • Standard interface cord.
  • Charging adapter.
  • Silicone protective case.
  • Warranty card.
  • User's manual.
  • Front protective film.

This list lacks a stereo headset and memory card. They will have to be bought separately.

Design

This phablet has a typical design, as for devices of this manufacturer. The front panel displays a touch screen. The lower part of the front panel is occupied by three control buttons. Above the screen, a speaker, a front camera hole and touch elements are displayed. The control buttons are on the right edge of the smartphone. Wired ports are displayed at the top: audio port and micro-USB. The back cover of the device is made of plastic, which in appearance resembles leather. Here is the main camera, its dual backlight and a loud speaker (hidden behind the net).





There are two color options for the case of this phablet: black and white. More widespread Lenovo A916 Black. Reviews indicate its great practicality. But the white version is much less common, and mostly girls and women prefer it.

Touch screen and its features

One of the key benefits is the touch screen in the Lenovo A916. Characteristics (reviews indicate this nuance) he, of course, not outstanding, but still very, very good. First of all, you need to highlight the diagonal, which in this case is five and a half inches. And the screen resolution is 1280x720. More specifically, the picture in this case is displayed in 720p or, as it is also called, HD-quality.





The matrix of the screen is made by the most popular and common technology at the moment - "IPS". The same nuance significantly improves the energy efficiency parameters of this gadget. As a result, the display quality on this device is really excellent, which leaves all possible competitors far behind.

Cameras

Another nice point is the main camera in the Lenovo A916. Customer reviews highlight her very, very high-quality photos and videos. This is not surprising: it is based on a 13 megapixel sensor. At the software level, it implements such important technologies as autofocus, geometries, digital zoom and touch focus. All this allows you to get high-quality images in almost any situation.

With a lack of lighting, two LEDs are turned on, and this allows you to get good photos even with a minimum level of illumination. This camera can record video in 1080p format. At the same time, frames will be updated 30 times per second. The front camera is based on a more modest sensor - only 2 megapixels. This is enough to make video calls using such a device. But the "selfie" he will be of medium quality.

And what is inside of us?

A fairly productive filling in the Lenovo A916. Reviews, in the first place, highlight the MT6592M central processor. It includes 8 cores "Cortex A7". In the highest load mode, they can accelerate to 1.4 GHz. The minimum frequency is 300 MHz. Also, if you do not use any computing module in the process of processing information, it is automatically turned off.





Almost any software on the Android platform will run without problems on this CPU. The only exception to this is the new 64-bit programs. This limitation is due to the fact that the MT6592M processor can process only 32 bits of information per cycle. To process the graphic image displayed on the touch screen, this gadget is equipped with a Mali-450MP4 graphics card. The amount of RAM in this device is 1 GB, and the capacity of the integrated drive is 8 GB. There is also a slot for installing an external 32 GB memory card.

Battery

2500 mAh - this is exactly the battery capacity in Lenovo A916. Reviews indicate that in the most austerity mode, you can stretch a maximum of 2 days. If, on average, you use a phablet, then this value will already be reduced to a day. Well, when you start a resource-intensive toy, the battery life on one charge will generally decrease to 12-13 hours.





Given the large diagonal of the touch screen and 8-core CPU (albeit energy efficient), these are excellent indicators. In this case, autonomy can be increased only by connecting an external battery to the gadget.

Software component

Under the control of the widespread, but far from fresh version 4.4 of the Android OS, the Lenovo A916 mobile phone functions. Reviews also highlight a redesigned interface that is more reminiscent of iOS. This is not surprising, because Vayb YuA, the Lenovo branded shell, is installed on top of the system software. It is immediately worth noting that this device has been sold for a long time. Updates for him were not received and in the future they are no longer expected. Therefore, the new user will have to be content with exactly the software that is already available.

But what about the owners?

Most users are satisfied with the Lenovo A916. Owner reviews highlight a far from fresh version of system software. But there's nothing to be done about it. But, in principle, most programs on this version of the OS run and function perfectly.





But the advantages of this phablet are much greater:

  • Large and high-quality display.
  • There is a productive processor solution.
  • A good degree of autonomy.
  • Flawless main camera.

Summary

A very tempting entry-level phablet is the Lenovo A916. Reviews mark its excellent technical and program parameters, and its price is a modest 110 dollars. In any case, none of the direct competitors of this gadget have such a combination of price and parameters. Therefore, if you need a large-sized smartphone with a good filling, then there is essentially no alternative to the Lenovo A916.
