Montenegro resorts: description and prices

Montenegro is a hospitable country that can become a great place to relax and an alternative to many popular tourist destinations. The magnificent nature, amazing climate, clean beaches and amazing mountain ranges will allow you to relax as it should. You can restore strength for a long time by visiting the resorts of Montenegro. The description of these places shows that everyone will find something to their liking here. In Montenegro, you can relax with your whole family, spend a romantic trip or have fun with friends.

At any time of the year it is not difficult to find several reasons to visit the resorts of Montenegro. Description of opportunities for recreation amazes with the diversity of even the most sophisticated travelers. Ski resorts, health resorts with healing springs, a rich excursion program, the proximity of Europe and amazing local cuisine attract thousands of tourists from around the world.



Herceg Novi is one of the most famous and popular places known as the resorts of Montenegro. The description of this city is worth starting with a story about beautiful beaches and gardens and the ancient Sea Fortress. The gentle sea and clean mountain air, combined with excellent local cuisine, will allow you to enjoy your vacation and restore strength. Numerous tourists return from year to year to this fabulous place called Montenegro. Resorts, prices for vacations, hospitality of local residents and a huge number of other advantages allow you to combine an interesting pastime and enjoy excellent living conditions.

Not far from Herceg Novi is the island of St. Stephen - a popular resort of Montenegro among celebrities. This island looks like an elite boutique hotel, which has everything for a wonderful holiday of world stars. There are unusual pink beaches, upscale hotels, yacht clubs and spa resorts.





In neighboring Igalo, everyone can improve their health and regain their strength at the mud springs, which are known as the best resorts in Montenegro. The season here lasts about 5 months, during which the beautiful beaches of the Adriatic Sea allow you to relax your body and soul.

For lovers of history and an active excursion program, the city of Kator is perfect. This place begins its history since the Middle Ages and is special among all the resorts of Montenegro. Amazing Kator with the remains of medieval architecture attracts many tourists. In the summer, there are many interesting festivals in the vicinity.

It is worth paying attention to the main city of the country - Podgorica, choosing the resorts of Montenegro. Description of ancient monuments and beautiful architecture attracts many tourists. You can start exploring the city by walking along the old narrow streets, gathering at local restaurants with amazing cuisine and exploring local attractions. You should definitely visit the ruins of the Turkish fortress, ancient monasteries and other interesting architectural monuments.



All outdoor enthusiasts will have something to do in the ski resorts throughout the year. Well-equipped trails are ready to receive tourists until April and offer numerous location options at hotels and guest houses at affordable prices.

Montenegro is beautiful at any time of the year, and holidays in this country will always be remembered as one of the best in life!
