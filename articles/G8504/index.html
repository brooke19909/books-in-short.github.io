CTP insurance without additional insurance

MTPL policy must be mandatory for all motorists. But it turns out to be quite difficult to acquire. On the forums, user complaints about insurers appear daily. The latter refuse to sell the policy, citing the lack of forms, and offer to issue any other service. But the inspectors say that there are no problems with the documents, the reluctance to conclude contracts is illegal. So, how and where can I get CTP without additional insurance?

Situation

Since 2003, driving without compulsory motor third-party liability insurance is prohibited in Russia. The penalty for breaking the rules is 800 rubles. For a long time there were no problems with car insurance. On a call, the agent was ready to come to any place in the city. But a year ago, the situation has changed. Companies began to refuse to issue CTP, citing the lack of blanks or long waiting lines. Although the problems were still earlier. FAS, Rospotrebnadzor, RSA and other regional governments have begun checking the activities of organizations, but there is no result. MTPL insurance without life insurance or other services is practically not issued. Drivers have to either overpay a few thousand rubles for the policy, or write a statement about the refusal of the service and wait another 30 days for an answer. Most choose the first option. In those insurance companies (IC) that draw up documents without additional services, queues of several dozen people a day. And you can buy a "clean" policy only at the office, and not through an agent. But experts say that there are other ways out of the situation.

Depending on the region and the IC, the ‚Äúclean‚Äù OSAGO will cost 4-6 thousand rubles. Plus additional services. It should be remembered that for the inhabitants of rural areas and many small cities, increased coefficients are used. Therefore, the cost of the policy can reach up to 10 thousand rubles. This situation does not suit all drivers. It turns out that driving without OSAGO insurance is fraught with a fine. But the policy itself increased in price by 2-3 times. What to do in this case?

Renew policy in advance

Experts advise car owners to contact insurance companies (IC) no later than 2-3 months before the expiration of the previous contract. According to Art. 445 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, the organization is obliged to draw up a document within 30 days after receiving the application. The changes that have come into force prohibit companies from refusing to provide services without real good reason. If this happens, it is necessary to write a statement to the main office of the company with a request to issue CTP without additional insurance. If after 3-5 days there is no answer, you can go to court. But this is an extreme measure.





Action algorithm

Choose an insurance company. View on the website on the Internet if there are any representative offices in your city. The application will be accepted by mail, but you will have to go to the office for the policy.

Prepare a package of documents:

  • Vehicle Registration Data (TS).
  • Statement in duplicate. The sample can be downloaded from the company website.
  • Driver's license.
  • Diagnostic card. It can be issued at the service station or immediately in the UK, but for a double fee.
  • The document proving the identity of the insured, or a certificate of state registration of the legal entity (if the application is executed at the office).

These papers with the inventory must be sent to the company by registered letter with delivery confirmation and wait another month for an answer. Usually the result comes earlier. The countdown begins on the day of receipt of the notice of delivery of the parcel.

You can personally go to the UK and write an application for compulsory motor third-party liability insurance without additional insurance. But it will take more time and nerves. In addition, it is necessary to make sure that the number, date and signature of the person in charge are stamped on the application.



The letter that will arrive by mail will most likely have a refusal for some made-up reason, as well as an offer to drop into the office and find out all the nuances on the spot. Employees of the branch for a "clean" CTP will advise you to apply in a month. However, your application should already be recorded in the database. To confirm your words, grab a notification from the mail and the response of the UK from the main office. Further actions depend on how events develop. Perhaps employees will be asked to undergo inspection before issuing a policy. By law, they really have the right to do so. The client will be assigned the time and place of the meeting. It is very important that these actions are recorded on paper in the form of an official invitation letter. Next, you need to arrive at the appointed time and place. Most likely, the client will immediately receive CTP insurance without additional services. The tariff is calculated individually for each vehicle, but in general, 3-4 thousand rubles. You can keep fit.

Transit insurance

Situations in life are different. Very often, drivers buy cars in other areas, and then transport them to their hometown. But if you buy a vehicle that has been deregistered, then 10 days are left to complete the formalities (passing the TO and receiving the policy). Firstly, a short time. Secondly, a person may not want to change SK. You can, of course, at your own peril and risk, slowly get to your hometown. But you need to be prepared to pay a fine. Without OSAGO insurance, it was dangerous to drive earlier also because state registration numbers were removed from the car. Their restoration cost much more than a policy. Changes in the legislation that came into force last year abolished this punishment and increased the fine from 500 to 800 rubles.

Especially for such cases, temporary insurance for compulsory motor third-party liability insurance has been developed without inspection. It is valid for 20 days, and costs about 500-1000 rubles. This time is enough to get to your hometown and do the paperwork of normal documents.





Riding without CTP insurance

According to statistics, in Russia every fifth driver does not draw up a ‚Äúcitizen‚Äù or forgets to extend its validity. As a result, law-abiding citizens suffer. If compensation problems arise, they have to wait several months. But lawyers suggest how you can speed up the process of receiving payments if the culprit of an accident without compulsory motor third-party liability insurance.

First you need to make sure that the absence of the policy is recorded in the certificate from the scene. You should also clarify the address of registration and residence of the culprit of the accident. You can get information from the traffic police who draw up certificates. Without this data, sending a subpoena will be very difficult. An independent examination report will also be required. It is desirable that the culprit of the accident be present during this process.

Then, a pre-trial claim is drawn up, in which the route is indicated, the circumstances of the accident, the total amount of damage caused is indicated. In addition to estimates, this figure can include the costs of evacuating and storing a car, conducting an examination, and moral damage.




As a rule, after drawing up the paper, the culprit agrees to compensate all the costs in full and not to bring the case to court. Otherwise, he will also have to pay the state fee and the services of a lawyer. MTPL insurance without life insurance will cost less than legal costs. So it is better to spend time on the policy than to compensate for all costs due to accidents.

DSAGO as a way out

Voluntary motor third party liability insurance can really help a person out of an accident. But before acquiring a policy, it is worth soberly assessing the likelihood of a particular situation.

The real average CTP payment is 15 thousand rubles. Serious accidents are rare. Experienced drivers should be enough. Experts recommend purchasing DSAGO only for beginners. As additional services to the policy, it is proposed:

  • payment of compensation excluding depreciation;
  • the services of an emergency commissioner (in practice, he rarely visits the scene of an accident);
  • free car evacuation, if ordered through the UK;
  • legal support (valid only in theory).

These additional services are in fact rarely needed. The cost of the policy varies between 0.2-0.3% of the amount of coverage. It is calculated in the same way as OSAGO.

No client will like it when they impose services that are not of interest to him. But SK has its own rating of popular products. And if any of them in this region becomes unprofitable, then agents, abusing the trust of customers, try to impose it.
