The Yelken Mandalinci Spa - Wellness Hotel, located in a small village called Turgutreis, is no exception.

general information

This spa hotel is eighteen kilometers from the center of Bodrum. It was built in the last century - in 1990. But it was constantly being renovated. The last major overhaul at the Yelken Mandalinci Hotel Spa Hotel was completed three years ago. It has a small territory of six thousand square meters. It is very beautifully landscaped. But the hotel looks especially attractive at night, when numerous lights turn on, illuminating not only cozy paths, but also tall palm trees. There is a separate pool area with gazebos and a bar. A walking path leads to the port. Here lured fish swim, which vacationers spoiled with bread. There are many restaurants and shopping malls along the promenade. The hotel is fifty-three kilometers from the airport

Infrastructure

Yelken Mandalinci Hotel Spa is a four star hotel. He has a fairly developed infrastructure, which includes car rental, a currency exchange, a hairdresser. There are two small shops on the territory.

The reception is open around the clock. Here you can receive a fax or photocopy documents. There is also a safe box and a left-luggage office.

The hotel provides ironing and laundry services. On the ground floor there is a meeting room. You can order a taxi from the receptionist, buy a plane ticket or arrange a return transfer. There are wheelchair accessible tracks on site.

Housing stock



The Yelken Mandalinci Hotel Spa building is a story building. It has a total of one hundred twenty-five standard and family rooms. Before each - furnished balconies, sitting on which you can admire the sea panoramas or the garden.

Amenities include a safe and a minibar for a fee. There is also a digital TV, a refrigerator with free bottled water on the day of arrival. The entire Yelken Mandalinci Hotel is air-conditioned. The rooms have split systems with manual control. The furniture is new, there is a work desk, a large makeup mirror, beds with orthopedic mattresses.

The combined bathrooms have showers, a hairdryer, and all hygiene items that are updated daily. The apartments are also cleaned with the same frequency. Bed sheets with towels are replaced every other day. Both buildings have elevators.

The hotel has wheelchair accessible rooms that have bathrooms for wheelchair users. Free WiFi is available throughout.

Nutrition

Yelken Mandalinci Hotel Spa 4 * offers its customers rates that include only accommodation or the all-inclusive concept, which, in addition to the room rate, includes food and drinks, but only on the hotel territory and with some restrictions. From alcohol only local alcohol is given at no extra charge.

The on-site restaurant is a buffet room, the main specialization of which is international cuisine, which are offered to residents for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

The hotel charges an additional fee for elite or imported drinks, as well as for room service. There are two bars on the territory - a lounge and a pool. On the roof of the main building, there are two restaurants on the menu - fish and Italian.

rules

Check-in time at Yelken Mandalinci Hotel Spa is fourteen hours, however, arriving outside of school hours are served as promptly as those who arrived on time. Departure is scheduled exactly at noon. Upon receipt of the number, the administration requires a deposit: in cash or by credit card. In addition, photo identification is required.

According to the rules of the hotel, in order to receive a massage course, you need to make a reservation, and, before arrival. For the safe in the rooms you need to pay an extra two euros per day. Pets and guide dogs are not allowed. An additional cot is available upon request.

Entertainment and the beach

The sea is only one hundred and fifty meters. Beach loungers, umbrellas - all this is free. On the territory of the Yelken Mandalinci Hotel (Bodrum) there are two pools - an adult and a children's. There is also a sauna, massage room, spa baths. In addition, there is a well-known fitness center in the region.

Leading an active life, tourists can go kayaking, parasailing, and water skiing.



Reviews

Those who rested in this hotel, returning home, be sure to share their impressions. Their reviews are very useful for those who are just going to visit the Yelken Mandalinci Hotel Spa.

The location of the hotel for almost all tourists is marked with an "excellent" rating. The territory is estimated at the same amount. Many are also happy with the rooms. The hotel staff is always attentive, but for some it was difficult to communicate with him, because the employees do not speak Russian, but they understand English very well.



The food provided by residents is also completely satisfied with the Russians. A variety of dishes, an abundance of fruits - all this allows you not to leave the restaurant hungry. The hotel attracts not only couples, but also demanding elderly travelers who prefer average comfort on a budget option.

Many good words have been said about the Italian restaurant located on the roof of the main building, especially about the unusual dishes offered in it.
