What to see in Cheboksary

If you come to Cheboksary for a few days, be sure to check out the sights of the city. Here are the most popular objects among tourists:

  • Naberezhnye Cheboksary (Istoricheskaya, Sovetskaya, Teatralnaya, Pedestrian Bridge, Moskovsky Prospect, "Road to the Temple") is a favorite vacation spot of citizens. City celebrations are often held here.
  • Monument Mother Patroness (Herzen Street, 40) - the highest monument of the city (46 m with a pedestal). The construction symbolizes blessing, getting rid of problems and preserving culture. The monument was erected in 2003 at the expense of citizens.
  • Holy Trinity Monastery (Konstantin Ivanov Street, 1) is one of the oldest monasteries in Chuvashia and Russia as a whole. It was erected in 1566 by order of Ivan the Terrible in order to spread the Orthodox faith.
  • Vedensky Cathedral (21 Konstantin Ivanov Street) is another old church, the oldest building in Chuvashia. It was built by order of Ivan the Terrible in 1555. The first wooden version was completely destroyed during the fire. The building was later restored in stone.
  • The Church of the Vladimir Icon of the Mother of God (16 Sespel Street) is a church that burned down in the 17th century. An absolutely untouched icon was found in the ashes. Until 2013, there were ruins in its place. And only recently it was decided to restore, the new temple is called the ancient icon preserved after the fire.
  • The Botanical Garden (Yakovleva Avenue, 31) was founded in 1978 on the banks of the Kukshumki River. More than 750 species of trees, more than 700 types of flowers and more than 300 types of medicinal plants grow here.
  • The Chapaev Square and Museum (51-59, 46A Lenin Street) is the only Chapaev square in Russia, broken down on the site of its small homeland (the village of Budaiki), a landscaped area of ‚Äã‚Äã6 hectares. . The museum was opened in 1974.
  • Beer Museum (6 Kupts Efremov Boulevard) - has been operating since 1997. Here are collected many interesting exhibits regarding brewing, you can listen to informative lectures. There is a pub on site.
