Mari Kristin Beach Hotel (Greece / Crete): photos and reviews of tourists

A large seaport, modern resorts and a treasury of legendary civilization - all this is the sunny Greek island of Crete. The combination of excellent infrastructure, Mediterranean climate, vibrant local nature and unique historical sites makes this place an ideal option for entertainment and relaxation. Anyone who is just planning to spend their vacation days here should listen to the many positive reviews about Mari Kristin Beach Hotel 3 *, a budget hotel located a stone's throw from the sea coast.





About hotel

Hotel Mari Kristin Beach Hotel 3 * (Greece), reviews of which can often be found on Russian-speaking forums for tourists, is located in the small coastal village of Hersonissos, 25 km from the main island city of Heraklion.

The four-story hotel building was erected in 1984. After a major renovation in 2014, the Mari Kristin Beach Hotel offers 65 comfortable rooms for living and relaxation.

Check-in and check-in at the hotel is usual for European hotels 14-00 hours, check-out time is up to 12-00 hours. Hotel Mari Kristin Beach Hotel 3 * (photo of rooms, see the article below) has facilities for families with children and for people with disabilities.

Beach, pool

Crete is famous for its sandy - pebble beaches with clean and warm sea water. Entrance to the city beaches is usually free. Even in the "hot" tourist months there is no need to take up seats in advance or to book sunbeds and umbrellas - all vacationers are freely accommodated in the coastal zone. The three main Cretan beaches, including the famous beach and Star Beach water park, are deservedly marked with a blue flag.

Mari Kristin Beach Hotel is located on the first coastline, so the road to a small hotel beach takes only a few minutes. The beach, as noted by former hotel guests, is pretty good, equipped with sunbeds. The coastal water is clean, but some tourists did not like the entry into the water. Here, as on all Cretan beaches, you can rent equipment for water sports and entertainment.

The pool at the Mari Kristin Beach Hotel is quite an unusual geometric shape. Former hotel guests often mention that the water is fresh, chlorinated and fairly clean. Near the pool there is a small area equipped with comfortable sun loungers and umbrellas.

  • Family - 30 m¬≤, two connecting rooms.

Each room has access to a terrace or balcony, there are separate rooms for non-smoking guests. The price includes daily cleaning, towel change, hygiene items. Bed linen is changed at least 2 times a week.

The rooms in the hotel are equipped with double or single beds, there is a bathroom (toilet, bath or shower), refrigerator, telephone, TV (satellite dish), hairdryer, electric kettle (optional). For an additional payment: air conditioning, safe.

Important! This room can accommodate a pet. Pet accommodation is free, but only by prior arrangement when booking a room.

For tourists who want to have fun, the main advantage of staying at the Mari Kristin Beach Hotel will be a good location - a fifteen minute walk from the city center. Excursions, sights

What attractions can I see at Mari Kristin Beach Hotel? Greece and the island of Crete, like an open-air museum, provide an opportunity to see the architectural monuments of ancient history. The exposition of local museums includes unique archaeological artifacts discovered during excavations in the places of ancient Greek cities. In Hersonissos itself there are not so many interesting historical objects, in comparison with the Greek cities located on the mainland. But it is in the city that the main and most famous Cretan attraction is located - Knossos Palace, under which the labyrinth of the Minotaur is located. In addition, tourists will be interested to see the remains of ancient stone theaters, the Roman seaport or the ancient Christian basilica.

No less interesting will be excursions to the capital city of Heraklion and other villages of Crete, as well as boat trips to small local islets. Guests at the Mari Kristin Beach Hotel can choose any sightseeing tour in a small agency located opposite the hotel or explore the island on their own, traveling by rented transport.
