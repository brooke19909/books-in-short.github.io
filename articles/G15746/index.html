How can I transfer money to a Sberbank card from my phone?

Today we will try to find out how you can transfer money to a Sberbank card from your phone. Is it even possible to do this? Every modern client of Sberbank will be able to understand such issues. After all, the implementation of money transfers from this financial institution is one of the most popular services. What you need to know about phone transactions?



Chances of success

Is it always possible to transfer part of the funds to the Sberbank card from the phone? And is there, in principle, such a function?

Yes, transactions of the mentioned type are possible. But not always. Only customers whose cards have no more than 16 digits per room can work with mobile transfers. Otherwise, a person will not succeed.

Methods of transferring funds

Transferring money from your phone to a Sberbank card is not difficult. The main thing is to know how to act in a particular case.

In general, the following scenarios are allowed:

  • transactions through the sites of telecom operators;
  • transfer via USSD request;
  • sending an SMS command to implement the task.

Next, we will consider all of these options in more detail. In fact, even a child will be able to perform a number of simple steps.



MegaFon and translations

How to transfer one or another amount of money from a phone to a Sberbank card? It all depends on the specific mobile operator. Consider the most common and large firms.

In the case of MegaFon, it will be necessary to implement the task as follows:

  1. Open megafon.ru website.
  2. Log in to your personal account.
  3. Select the "Money Transfer" function.
  4. Click on the line "To a bank card."
  5. Indicate the phone number from which funds will be debited.
  6. Enter the operation confirmation code. He will come in an SMS message.
  7. Prescribe the number (16 digits) of the bank card where you want to transfer funds.
  8. Confirm the operation.

Done! In a few minutes, the money will be transferred to the specified bank account. This technique is not used so often. It requires certain costs. It's about commission. It amounts to 7.35% and fixedly 95 rubles if the citizen decided to transfer no more than 5,000 rubles to the Sberbank card from the phone. Otherwise, the surcharge to interest will be 295 rubles.

Beeline and translations

But this is only the beginning. How to transfer funds from a phone to a Sberbank card ? The next operator in line is Beeline.



The order of operations is as follows:

  1. Go to the mobile operator page in the browser.
  2. Log in to the service.
  3. Click on the item "Pay from phone account".
  4. Select "Money Transfer".
  5. Indicate the payment system. In our case, this is Sberbank.
  6. Enter the bank card number, the phone number of the sender, as well as the amount due to be credited.
  7. Click on the "OK" button.

Done! Now we know how to transfer a certain amount of money to a Sberbank card from a Beeline phone. The commission in this case is also charged, but it is less than that of MegaFon - only 5.95% and 10 rubles extra. The transfer will be completed within 5 days. A transaction usually takes several hours to process.

MTS to help

Next in line is another large company providing mobile access - MTS. Many residents of the Russian Federation work with this operator.

Transferring money to a Sberbank card via a phone with a MTS SIM card is not difficult. Here comes the "Internet Assistant." It is also called the "Personal Account" of the subscriber.



The manual on transferring funds from phone to card is as follows:

  1. Go to mts.ru.
  2. Log in to your personal account.
  3. Go to the "Payment Management" item.
  4. Open "Money transfers" in the "Easy payment" tab.
  5. Click on the line "Transfer to a bank card."
  6. Enter the data requested by the system. Usually this is the number from which you will have to charge money, as well as the amount and number of the destination card.
  7. Put a mark near the item "From the MTS phone account".
  8. Click on the "Next" button.
  9. Confirm the process.

As in the previous case, finances will be transferred within 5 days from the date of sending the request. The commission for such transfers at "MTS" is not the highest - only 4% of the payment amount.

Tele2 and bank plastic

How to transfer this or that amount of money to a Sberbank card via Tele2? To crank up the business, you must do the following:

  1. Open in tele2.ru browser.
  2. Click on the line "Payments and transfers".
  3. Select the command "To a bank card."
  4. Indicate the beneficiary bank. In our case, this is Sberbank.
  5. Enter the phone number of the sender and the card number of the recipient.
  6. Prescribe the amount due for transfer.
  7. Enter the transaction confirmation code in the designated field.

The commission "Tele2" for the operation under study is 5.75%. Money can be transferred to the indicated account up to 5 working days, but this is a huge rarity. As a rule, funds are transferred to the card in a few hours.



SMS to help

Money can be transferred to the Sberbank card from the phone using SMS requests. Each mobile operator has them. The subscriber just needs to form a message and send it to one or another short number.

It is recommended to pay attention to the following queries:

  • Card_number of the card_month and year of validity_amount, sending to number 3116 - "MegaFon";
  • Card_number plastic_sum, sending to 7878 - Beeline;
  • the previously proposed message form sent by number 159 - "Tele2".

That's all. Only on this, the possible ways of transferring money do not end there. There are some pretty interesting tricks.
