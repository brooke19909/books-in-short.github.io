Learning to knit booties

Knitting booties for beginners is worth trying after the first knitted scarf. Ease of execution, small size and a minimum of time spent, and as a result - excellent mastery of the technique of making crochets, adding and decreasing loops. And most importantly, you will learn how to knit charming warm booties for the baby. We start knitting booties with needles.

For booties, you need 25-30 grams of yarn, preferably wool or cotton. Since you are just learning to knit, take booties uncomplicated, but at the same time quite beautiful.





We start knitting booties from the middle of the sole. We knit 40 loops on knitting needles and knit 2 rows with facial loops (pattern - garter stitch). From the third row, add 4 loops through the row to expand the sole. To do this, after the edge we knit 2 persons., Raise and twist the broach between the loops (so that there is no hole in the product), we knit it front. The following loops are added in the middle of knitting (between 19 and 21 loops) and at the end, before 36 loops. In the same way, we knit the fifth, seventh and ninth rows, in the middle between each addition, each row becomes 2 more loops. As a result, we get 56 loops.

How to knit booties: toe

We knit a toe with "English rubber band". 1 row elastic band - 1x1, 2 row: hem, * wrong side is removed without knitting, while the working thread is put on the removed loop, it turns out like a double thread *. Repeat from * to * to the end of the row. In the third row, the front (double) loops are knitted with one front, purl according to the pattern of the second row. With an elastic band we knit only 16 rows.

Next, we reduce the loops to form the toe. The first row: hem, 17 English viscous, * purl, 3 loops of one (remove 1 loop unbound, the working knitting needle is introduced from left to right in 2 loops, knit them 1 front and put on the removed front loop) *. From * to * we knit 4 more times, 1 wrong side, 17 with English rubber band, hem. Second row: over the English gum we knit the English gum. Over the shortened loops we knit all the wrong loops. Third row: edge, 17 with an English elastic band, 2 loops of one front, 7 front, 2 loops of one front, 17 with an English elastic band, edge.

The fourth, sixth and eighth rows are knitted similarly to the second; the fifth and seventh rows are knitted similarly to the third. In height, knit booties with an English elastic band with 12 more rows and for the side 7 rows with front stitch. We knit the second foam in the same way. Sew on the front side with a knitted invisible seam. All booties are ready! Decorate at will.





How to crochet booties for a boy.

For work you will need:

- hook corresponding to the yarn;

- yarn wool or cotton, 2 or 3 colors;

- shoelaces.

We knit the sole of the booties, according to the scheme. In the last row, we have 60 single crochet posts along the edge of the insole, cut the thread and carefully hide it.



We start tying from the center of the middle of the insole columns without crochets, introducing hooks into the penultimate row. The middle of the insole is found by folding it in half along.

2,3 and 4 rows are knitted without additions with crochets (with a thread of a different color, we knit 3 rows). We break the thread.

In the 5th row, we count 16 loops and from 17 we begin to knit a toe according to the pattern. We break the thread.

From the place indicated by the red arrow in the photo, we begin to knit the sides of the booties.

1 row. Using a contrasting thread, we knit 4 lifting stitches, 28 single crochet stitches.

2 row. 4 lifting loops, 29 single crochet stitches.

In the third row: 4 lifting stitches, 2 single crochet stitches, 1 air loop (lace hole), skip 1 stitch of the bottom row, continue knitting with single crochet stitches, knit the last 3 stitches in the same way as the starting stitches.





4th row knit with crochets without passes.

The fifth and seventh rows are knitted similarly to the third.

The sixth and eighth rows are knitted similarly to the 4th.





We knit a booty tongue on the middle part with a thread that knitted the side. 9 rows high, 10 single crochets wide. We make the rounding of the tongue - 2 single crochet into a common top, 6 single crochet, again into a common vertex of 2 single crochet. We break the thread, carefully hide the end.

The edge of the side and tongue is tied with a contrasting thread "crustacean step". We knit laces from the air loops or use a satin ribbon. So warm, soft and beautiful booties-shoes are ready.
