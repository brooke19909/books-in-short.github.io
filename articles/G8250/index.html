Chocolate Museum

What will be interesting to the sweet tooth who came to the city of Pokrov? Its sights are very diverse. More recently, a chocolate museum was created in Pokrov.





This is due to the fact that one large German company opened a factory in the city. Her chocolate makes up a third of all products that go to stores in Russia. In addition to exhibits telling the story of this sweetness, here you can see a fountain of hot chocolate and a cocoa tree. But this is not all the surprises about the favorite delicacy of many. Near the museum is a monument to chocolate. The bronze fairy holds in her hands a bar of chocolate that has been wiped to a shine. Since from the moment the monument was opened, the belief has spread that if you rub it, then life will be sweet.

Sights. Cover

Not far from the city is the famous Vvedensky island monastery. In the distant 1708, monks of the Anthony Desert Sergey and Timofey built wooden cells and a chapel on the island of Vyatsky, now Vvedensky, lake.



Over time, other monks began to settle here, together they erected the Church of the Introduction of the Blessed Virgin Mary. A little later, the stone church of St. Nicholas the Wonderworker was built. The construction was led by the monk Sergei, who had been consecrated in hieromonk shortly before.

What other attractions were built then (Veil)? Near the gates of the monastery was built a wooden church of the Prophet Elijah. Of course, the island on which the Orthodox shrines were built became a place of pilgrimage. For those who wanted to offer prayers to the Lord, a monastery hotel and a dining room were built, the stay in which was free. But laymen could be in them no more than three days.

After the communists came to power, the monastery, like other religious buildings, suffered a bitter fate. The cathedral was not destroyed, but its inhabitants were dispersed, and the premises were transferred to a children's labor colony. In the nineties of the last century, the buildings were again transferred to the Orthodox Church. And if before the revolution there was a working monastery, then in our time it has become a monastery for women. Its territory is striking in its abundance of picturesque places. This is a wooden bridge, and well-kept flower beds in the garden, beautiful descents to the water. In addition, travelers will undoubtedly be pleased to contemplate the refined and austere beauty of the restored monastery.

In addition, travelers will be amazed by the luxurious nature around the Black and White lakes. Mighty pine trees frame their shores. And on Lake Black there are healing mud.

Holy Protection Church

What else is interesting Pokrov? Sights, photos of which are presented in the article, captivate tourists with their history and appearance. For example, in Pokrov itself there is an ancient shrine, a temple, near which several centuries ago a settlement first formed, and then a city. This is the Holy Protection Church, which first belonged to the same Anthony desert, and was wooden. Then they built a beautiful building with gilded domes.



During the years of Soviet rule, domes and crosses were cut from the church. Then the service in it was prohibited, and the premises were given for storage. More than twenty years ago, the temple was returned to believers and restored.

Other attractions: description

Describing the sights of Pokrov (Vladimir region), we will talk about the building, which houses the city administration. It is also one of the monuments of architecture. Built in the mid-nineteenth century, the two-story building is red, with large windows, especially on the second floor. Windows and doors framed by white building materials stand out luxuriously against the bright background of the wall.

What other interesting sights? Pokrov is proud of the city's maternity hospital. Its architecture is very curious. The building was built as a hospital during the time of Napoleon's invasion. Then in its place at the end of the nineteenth century the Zemsky hospital building was erected . And now this is the birthplace of young citizens of the Intercession. The whole building has the shape of the letter "P", above the main entrance are two original narrow turrets. The windows in this two-story building are narrow and tall.

Not far from Pokrov, only twenty kilometers away, there is a memorial monument at the site of the death of Yuri Gagarin and Vladimir Seregin. Here is a museum dedicated to the memory of two pilots. In the forest, at the crash site, a sixteen-meter stele was installed.
