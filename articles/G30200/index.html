Village Dolzhanskaya - rest, reviews. Yeisk, Dolzhanskaya, Krasnodar Territory

Holidays on the coast have always been popular with travelers. After all, what could be better than warm sea waves and the gentle sun? And today, many tourists are interested in questions about what the village of Dolzhanskaya represents. Rest, reviews about this place and living conditions - all this will be the subject of our conversation today.

In fact, this is an amazing place with a unique climate and natural conditions, as well as a rich history and a wide selection of entertainment. That is why every year hundreds and thousands of tourists flock here from all over the country and neighboring countries.





Where is the village Dolzhanskaya?

Of course, travelers are primarily interested in questions about where this cozy town is located. And in order to enjoy all the delights of a beach holiday, you will need to go to the Krasnodar Territory. Dolzhanskaya stanitsa is located near the base of the Long Spit and is part of a nature reserve. This is actually an amazing place. Despite the fact that the population of the town is only 8 thousand people, it has something to offer its guests. In particular, sandy and shell beaches, which stretch for 20 kilometers, will delight any, even the most fastidious traveler.

A brief history of the area

If you are looking for an interesting vacation destination with a rich history, then Yeisk is perfect for you. Dolzhanskaya village was founded by the Don and Zaporozhye Cossacks. After Russia occupied the territory of the Zaporizhzhya Sich in 1775, immigrants from there began to move to the lower reaches of the Kuban River. Of course, by that time small Cossack settlements of Staroderevyankovsky and Staroshcherbinovsky smoking areas already existed here.



Dolzhanskaya village was founded in 1848. By the way, at first she was called Long - in honor of the scythe, which is located nearby. The modern name stuck a little later. By the way, settlements began to form below, closer to the sea, since it was easiest to dig wells here. And along the spit were fishing artels.

Today, the village of Dolzhanskaya (Yeisk district) is a popular resort place that is in demand among those who want to relax on the beach.

Description of climate and environmental conditions

This territory is characterized by a temperate continental climate, which is an undoubted advantage. The air temperature here rarely exceeds 25 degrees. Even in August, when it gets really hot, the air is softened by the soft sea breeze. That is why it is easy to acclimatize here even to those people who are accustomed to a colder climate.

Another advantage is the coast of the Sea of ​​Azov. Spacious beaches of pure soft sand or shells await you here. Descent to the sea is convenient, and even children can have a great time in shallow water. By the way, the water here heats up pretty quickly. In addition, the reserve is covered with forests and parks - there is even a zone planted with pine trees, thanks to which even the air here has healing properties. If you are interested in what the village of Dolzhanskaya is, recreation, reviews of tourists, then first of all you should know that this place is just perfect for wellness. After all, near the coast there are many medical centers and resorts.





Yeisk. Dolzhanskaya stanitsa and conditions of rest

This place is intended primarily for a quiet family vacation. However, tourists who crave entertainment will also not be disappointed. There are markets where you can buy interesting souvenirs. You can also play billiards or visit one of the tennis courts.

There are various water attractions, including large water slides, which will appeal to both adults and children. You can visit the riding school, where experienced teachers work with students. Fishing enthusiasts will not be bored. Of course, there is also a rich excursion program.

Active sports

Of course, water sports are extremely popular on the territory of Dolzhanskaya stanitsa. Here you can ride a catamaran, take a short boat trip, try out water skiing or even take part in a beach volleyball competition.



Moreover, the bay is an almost perfect place for windsurfing. Wave lovers from all over the country come here. In the spring, competitions are held here every year under the name "Dolzhanskaya Spit Race". And in the fall they organize the All-Russian festival for surfers "Dolzhansky Marathon". By the way, if you are new to this sport, then you should not worry - here you can take some lessons from real masters, as well as rent or buy all the necessary equipment.

Accommodations

Of course, most tourists are primarily interested in questions about where to stay and what living conditions await them in this town. In fact, the choice is wide enough - everyone can find a suitable place. Naturally, near the beach there is a tent city for people who like to completely merge with nature during their holidays.



But this is not all that the village of Dolzhanskaya can offer. The private sector is also quite developed. Indeed, at almost every step, tourists are offered to rent a room, a cottage or even a whole house. Payment in most cases is daily. The price, of course, depends on the proximity to the sea and the availability of certain amenities.

The most popular recreation centers

Many tourists are primarily interested in questions about whether there is a suitable recreation center on the coast. Dolzhanskaya stanitsa naturally offers its guests decent living conditions. For example, the center "Kristina", which is located at the end of the spit and overlooks the Taganrog Bay , is considered a good place . Along the coast there are small houses for vacationers that go directly to the beach from small cockleshells.

Here tourists are offered rooms of various categories, including rooms with a high degree of comfort, an individual bathroom and air conditioning. In addition, each room has the necessary set of furniture and a spacious refrigerator, which is very convenient.

There is another recreation center. Dolzhanskaya stanitsa offers its guests check-in at the Rainbow complex, which is also located on the side of the Taganrog Bay, right on the beach. The base offers more than 80 rooms in the main building or separate cozy bungalows.



Naturally, there are other, no less comfortable options. For example, near the beach there are many small guest houses, as well as recreation centers "Windrose", "On Kalinina", "Elite", "Cossack coast", etc. Each traveler will find a suitable place for himself.

Treatment and rehabilitation in the village

Of course, there is much more that Dolzhanskaya Spit can offer . Recreation facilities here are famous for their hospitality and comfort. But there are places intended for recovery. For example, the sanatorium-boarding house “Severyanka” is quite popular, where guests are offered comfortable rooms, delicious food and a lot of entertainment. And especially for children in the summer, the Salyut camp opens its doors.



What is used to make travelers healthier? Of course, the mild climate and sea water have a positive effect on health. Not everyone knows that on the territory of the village there is also a large Dolzhanskoe lake, the mud of which is famous for its healing properties, because it consists of sulphurous iron, carbonates, magnesium, calcium and sulfates. In health resorts, patients are offered various applications, as well as mud baths, which are used to treat diseases of the joints, muscles, bones, skin and even the cardiovascular system.
