How to make paper turntables yourself?

How to please your baby? Buy another Kinder Surprise or just candy? While the child is small, the family budget is constantly experiencing shocks in the form of acquiring new shoes and outerwear. These purchases are sometimes made even a couple of times a season. Therefore, money, as usual, is not enough. However, even in this situation, you can please the little one with a new toy. We will tell you how to make turntables from various materials. This extremely simple to manufacture, but colorful thing will give the child a huge amount of positive emotions.





How to make turntables: materials

You can use any materials at hand to create a new toy. Cardboard, colored paper, plastic bottles, or durable and thick enough polyethylene that can hold a shape will do. You will also need a small, well-rounded stick for a pen, carnations, acrylic paints, brushes, and Moment glue. You can use a beautiful holographic gift wrapper. You will also need a simple pencil for drawing, a ruler and scissors.

How to make turntables? Process description



Even those who do not often do crafts can make such a toy. The process does not last long, and if you attract children to it, then the time will fly by fun and quietly! Let's start with the pattern. Draw on a sheet of paper in a cage the estimated size of the turntable. If it will be made for walking along the street, it is better to make it out of plastic. A five-liter water bottle is perfect. Draw a circle, and then cut as shown in the diagram. If you invite the baby to paint it with acrylics or gouache, then there will be no limit to the joy of the little designer. However, it is first necessary to slightly warm up the plastic above the flame of a lighter or candle, giving it a characteristic shape for the product. The cooled and painted spinner should be pierced with a hot nail in the center. And then, choosing a nail or self-tapping screw of a smaller diameter, fix the product on a stick. It should not be fixed tightly, since in this case the air will not be able to rotate the toy. That's all! Now you know how to make turntables from a plastic bottle. The child is satisfied, and after the end of the summer season, the toy can be fixed in the garden. She will perfectly scare away birds.



And how to make a turntable out of paper? It's as easy as that

In order for the pinwheel to turn out beautiful and moderately durable, you need to take colored paper. It is desirable that it be not only bilateral, but also dense. If you have only the standard option, then you can strengthen it by pre-gluing it with several layers of ordinary office paper, and sticking color on both sides. After this masterpiece has dried, you can begin to draw the future shape of the toy on paper. Then carefully cut and bend the sharp edge to the center. Pin it with a pin. So do the rest of the petals. And now, with the help of a clove, we expand the mounting hole and nail the pinwheel to a previously prepared stick. Done! If you are a fan of origami from paper, the pinwheel will require a more serious approach and careful implementation of instructions.
