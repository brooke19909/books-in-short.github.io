Vizant 750st DVR-Radar: review, specifications and owner reviews

In modern society, it is difficult to imagine a motorist who would not install a video fixation tool on the windshield. The article will discuss what it is for, as well as the Vizant 750st gadget.

The need for surveillance

It so happened historically that drivers are used to play safe in case of unforeseen circumstances. Due to the global congestion of megacities with cars, the likelihood of accidents has increased. Moreover, there are frequent cases when the culprit manages to escape from the scene, or the situation is such that it is not clear who is right and who is to blame.

For this, Vizant 750st DVRs (or some other model) are mounted on the windshield, capable of recording everything that happens, providing the driver with a reliable evidence base.

Thanks to the use of video surveillance, fraudulent actions on the roads that began in the mid-90s, when criminals deliberately provoked an accident in order to blackmail the driver and demand large amounts of money, were completely eliminated.

Now this can only be seen in the movies. And the surveillance cameras installed on almost every pillar will also help recreate the details of the collision, if the recorder has not photographed everything.

Description

The Vizant 750st DVR with anti-radar function interested drivers not only with its design, but also with its capabilities. The fact is that the gadget is designed as a rear-view mirror and in fact it is. Therefore, it is simply mounted instead of its less functional "progenitor", eliminating the need to install any additional devices on the windshield.

Vizant 750st perfectly functions as a radar detector, accurately recognizing the signals of modern speed detection systems. Works with radiation ranges such as K, Ultra K, Ka, X and Ultra X.

The gadget provides for the adjustment of sensitivity levels (there are three in total), which allows you to adjust the operation of the product in case of false positives. Notification occurs through sound notification and light indication.

There is one camera that records HD video (1280 √ó 720) at a frequency of 30 frames per second. The output image is clear, thanks to a viewing angle of 120 ¬∞, it is possible to capture all four lanes, including road signs and license plates.

The ability to view recordings right at the scene of an accident provides a 4.3-inch display.

What is missing?

As a rule, drivers choose for themselves ‚Äúfriend and assistant‚Äù for one purpose: to fix everything that happens around. This requires the presence of WDR-technology, due to which high-quality recording is carried out at any time of the day, based on the principle of balancing the image intensity: the darkest places are lightened, and the brightest ones are darkened.

It is WDR that allows for high-quality shooting in the dark with low street lighting. The Vizant 750st does not state such a function instruction, which is an annoying omission.

In addition, the G-sensor, another name for which is the shock sensor, plays an important role. The module captures the moment of a sharp increase in the load on the vehicle and the moment of decline in speed. At the same time, video recording is automatically turned on, the file is placed in a special area on the memory card that is protected from erasure.

If someone crashed into a car in a parking lot, the automatic shooting starts again. Therefore, the absence of such a useful module as an electronic accelerometer significantly reduces the likelihood of recording the culprit of a traffic accident on video.
