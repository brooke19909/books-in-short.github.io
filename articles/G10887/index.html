Is it worth it to go to Bialystok and what is profitable to drive from there?

The city of Bialystok is located in the eastern part of Poland, close to the Belarusian border. Every year thousands of tourists from different countries flock here. However, not all of them go to see the numerous architectural monuments, ancient churches and palaces. So, for the citizens of Belarus, the city has become a shopping center thanks to the largest market in this part of the country and many shops. From this article you will learn many interesting facts about Bialystok and what is profitable to carry from there.



general information

Bialystok (Poland) is known for the fact that in its markets you can find any product. Here they buy high-quality food, clothing that surprises with high quality, household appliances, as well as souvenirs and gifts for every taste. In addition, tourists are attracted by numerous shops and supermarkets scattered throughout the city. For those who are not coming here for the first time, one gets the impression that during trips to shopping centers they lose the language barrier. Of course, there may be slight difficulties with the translation, but everyone can understand the general information. Numerous promotions inspire vacationers with which shop windows and store advertisements are especially inspiring. And markets and private shops attract thrifty tourists by the fact that with their owners you can bargain and receive significant discounts.



Bialystok Markets

Those who travel to Poland for shopping are often interested in the question of why you need to visit Bialystok and what is profitable to bring from there. To answer it, we will tell you about the two largest markets of this city.

  • The cavalry market can conditionally be divided into two parts. The first is the Hetman Clothing Bazaar, or Getman Market. The second is the bazaar on the Cavalry, or the Old Market, which amazes visitors with its size. On the territory of 8.5 hectares there are more than 1400 private shops and stores. Tourists usually buy shoes, clothes, jewelry and fashion accessories here. Prices for the same groups of goods can differ dramatically from each other. That is why everyone will be able to get what they want according to their taste and without too much emptying their wallet.
  • Fasty food night market will surprise you with a huge amount of goods provided by industrial and agricultural enterprises. On a vast territory (53 thousand square kilometers) there are about 130 modern spacious pavilions offering buyers the fruits of labor of local farms.




Shopping Bialystok

To save tourists from having to roam the vast expanses of the local market in search of necessary goods, the doors of many shops are hospitably open. All of them provide comfortable shopping conditions and demonstrate a high level of service. What goods are famous for Bialystok and what is profitable to carry from there:

  • Clothes and shoes. In large chain stores, promotions are often held that allow you to buy goods at attractive prices.
  • Sporting goods. The largest center is Decathlon, where you can find equipment for almost all sports.
  • Jewelry. Bialystok is considered an ideal place where you can buy similar products for all occasions at fairly modest prices.
  • Food and related products. You can buy at any supermarket. Many buy tea, chocolate, wine and other alcoholic drinks for the future.

Travel Tips

If you want to know what Bialystok is famous for and what is profitable to carry from there, carefully prepare for the trip:

  • Outline a shopping plan by reviewing the sites of the stores you need.
  • Sign up for the newsletter of the right companies and find out about the ongoing promotions in advance.
  • Check the directions to the selected stores.
  • Compare the prices of Bialystok goods in advance with the average prices in Poland.
