Kirillovka, Sea of ​​Azov: rest. Kirillovka, Fedotova spit - recreation center

The Sea of ​​Azov attracts tourists with its velvety waves, fairly clean beaches, and most importantly, peace and quiet. It is not as crowded as on the Black Sea coast, but no less beautiful. One of the resorts of the Sea of ​​Azov is Kirillovka.



A little about the resort of Kirillovka (Sea of ​​Azov)

This is an urban village. It is located in Ukraine (south of the Zaporizhzhya region). It is very popular with tourists, since 1999 it has been awarded the title of a resort of national importance. There are a number of landscape gardening and other man-made natural monuments.

The resort is famous for its entertainment. You won’t be bored. At the same time, you can spend a quiet family vacation here, depending on the recreation center and its location.

To more accurately navigate the expanses of this resort, you should know that on its territory there are two braids - Peresyp and Fedotova spit. Holidays in any part of Kirillovka will turn out to be of high quality due to well-developed infrastructure.

Kirillovka (Fedotova spit). Recreation facilities. Amore

The recreation center offers a wide selection of rooms, various in equipment and cost. In the courtyard there is a fountain, a pond with fish, benches and gazebos - everything to ensure a comfortable stay. By the way, the area of ​​the village of Kirillovka Fedotova Spit (a recreation center is located here) is known for its dolphinarium and water park. From base "Amore" to them at hand.

Gulf Stream

The recreation center consists of several cottages. They offer beautiful views of the expanses of the Sea of ​​Azov. Additional services include training in surfing and renting fishing equipment. The beach of the recreation center is equipped with sun loungers and umbrellas.

"Orchid"

Here the rest will be very economical. In the kitchen on the ground floor, people can cook for themselves. In the center of the courtyard is an outdoor pool. On the territory there are children's swings, barbecue facilities and gazebos.



"Texas"

Elite hotel complex with suites and junior suites. Three meals a day are organized for its residents. Texas also includes an entertainment center. The entertainment show includes striptease, dancing, video disc. Active contestants are awarded prizes.

"Berth"

A recreation center, next to which an old ship really stopped. The landscape design of this base can only be envied. Those who wish can rent detached cottages. Luxury and standard rooms are also available. You can eat in the local cafe, which serves exclusively home-cooked food. Self-cooking is also possible in the shared kitchen.





This is not a complete list of boarding houses, recreation centers and hotels on the territory of Fedotova Spit. There is housing for every taste and budget. One of the most popular bases has the same name as the area. So, the place where the whole family can come to rest is Kirillovka, Fedotova spit.

The recreation center "Fedotova spit"

She is already good at her location. A magnificent landscape opens from her yard - the shore of the Sea of ​​Azov. The total area of ​​the base is three hectares. Despite the rather arid climate and the scorching sun, the territory is well landscaped. There are bushes, trees, and lush flower beds. For those who come by car, there is a parking lot.

Infrastructure

The infrastructure of the recreation center is well developed. At the disposal of its guests is a large grocery store, a dance floor, a playground for children, a computer room. In general, this resort has everything you need at your fingertips - this is what Kirillovka offers. Fedotova Kosa is a recreation center where you can rent catamarans, boats and various sports equipment for entertainment.



Family holidays at this recreation center will also work out well. Every day a teacher works for children here, organizing cultural events.

The beach, assigned to Fedotova Spit, is equipped with awnings, deck chairs and locker rooms.

Accommodation

As for the rooms, the hotel has two types. The first is economy class numbers. They have cold water, a kitchen with a fridge and a bathroom. These rooms are designed for 2 or 4 guests. The second type is luxury rooms. They are air-conditioned. The kitchen has a fridge. There are no problems with the bathroom - hot and cold water supply. In the evenings, vacationers can watch TV.



Nutrition

For those vacationers who want to save some money on food, kitchens function in every house of the recreation center. The kitchen has an electric stove, kettle, cold water, refrigerator and necessary utensils. If vacationers do not want to cook, then they have at their disposal a dining room. She is clean, comfortable. The menu has special diet dishes, dishes for children. Only fresh products are used in cooking, a lot of vegetables grown in the southern regions of Ukraine.

You can also have lunch and dinner in the summer cafe, where pleasant music is played. Here you can not only eat deliciously, but also enjoy a relaxing break with a glass of wine.

Prices

If you prefer an economical vacation, then the most acceptable option is the Fedotova Spit recreation center. Room prices depend on the house and the quality of the room itself. The minimum cost is 50 hryvnias per day per person. Meals are not included in the price. You can call at the base at any time. For reference: one hryvnia is about three rubles.

Entertainment near the recreation center

Perfect for those who love a calm, but at the same time rich holiday Kirillovka (Fedotova spit). The recreation center of the same name is located within walking distance from all the entertainment of the village.

On the beach you can book a ride on a jet ski. Cost - from 20 hryvnia per 1 minute. The instructor details the rules for using this vehicle. For those who do not feel self-confidence, skating with an instructor is offered. You can choose a sport mode when the driving speed will be very high. A ride on a jet ski can bring a lot of positive emotions!



For those who prefer to admire the beauty of nature at low speeds - catamarans for hire. Cost - from 15 hryvnias for half an hour of skiing. From the open sea it’s good to observe the life of the village of Kirillovka, from the side it looks very beautiful. In some areas, at a distance of 50 meters from the sea, you can safely stand in the water.

For extreme sports, beach businessmen offer a number of entertainment such as "banana", "pills". All these watery things cool up and cheer up. Near the entertainment center "Texas" is a Ferris wheel. A walk on it will allow you to look at the expanses of the sea and the village of Kirillovka from a bird's eye view. These landscapes look especially beautiful at night.

Another extreme beach attraction is parachuting. A powerful speed boat pulls a parachute along. Extremes like birds soar above the sea waters, and after a three-minute walk they are returned back to shore. This entertainment is for real daredevils.

At the very beginning of the Fedotova Spit district there are two clubs - Texas and Storm. These are typical youth nightlife venues where you can have fun, dance to the sounds of modern music.

The nature of the Sea of ​​Azov

In the village of Kirillovka sandy beaches. The maximum sea depth in the area is 14 meters, and the average depth is 8 meters.

In July, the temperature here rarely drops below 25 degrees. The average temperature is 26-28. Even in the evening it is very warm here - 22-24. Thus, in July there is the most comfortable stay. Kirillovka, Fedotova spit, recreation center - everything is buried in greenery. In the village there are many flower beds, plantings.

The underwater world of the Sea of ​​Azov is very diverse. It is inhabited by many representatives of flora and fauna. As part of the water, 92 chemical elements were found that positively affect human health.

For people who also want to improve their health during the holidays, the resort of Kirillovka is great. Recreation facilities are represented in large numbers.
