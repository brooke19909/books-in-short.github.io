Hotel CHC Galini Sea View 5 * (Crete, Greece): overview, description, rooms and reviews

In 2007, on the largest Greek island, the CHC Galini Sea View 5 * hotel was opened. This is a fashionable complex, the main "highlight" of which is the undoubted comfort and modest prices for accommodation. They can be considered acceptable, even if the guest decided to order the all-inclusive service, which is also here. However, about this hotel you can tell a lot of interesting things. A better start with general information.





Location

In the western part of Crete, there is such a large city as Chania. This is not only an interesting place from a historical point of view, but also a resort popular in our time. The town of Agia Marina-Nea Kidon belongs to the prefecture of Chania. This is where the CHC Galini Sea View 5 * is located. This complex is located on a hill, so you need to go down to the sea. But still, the path will take a little strength and only two minutes.

At a distance of 4-7 kilometers are several interesting sights. For example, the church of Agios Dimitrios, the Franciscan monastery of St. Fragkiskos, the municipal garden, the golden beach, the archaeological museum of Chania and much more. By the way, the airport is also nearby. It is located 10 kilometers from Chania.

But the most important thing is Daratso beach, which is the highlight of the resort and the most beautiful in the whole bay.

For leisure

In CHC Galini Sea View 5 * created excellent conditions not only for relaxation, but also for entertainment. There is an outdoor pool of 310 square meters. m. Indoor is located in the wellness center and works seasonally.

The Beach Wing building is 200 meters from the main building of the hotel. The hotel has an elevator that can be used to reach the level of the main road.

You can also engage in various sports. For example, scuba diving, go water skiing or motorbikes. Still here, parachuting and sailing are well developed. It is only necessary to contact the reception, if you want any of the above.

CHC Galini Sea View also has an electronic games room, a TV corner and a billiard room. There is a gym, a bath with a sauna, several massage rooms and a SPA center where you can devote time to pedicures, manicures and other face and body treatments.





About nutrition

The fashionable CHC Galini Sea View 5 * hotel has several establishments where guests can taste gourmet cuisine.

The main restaurant offers amazing panoramic views of the bay. There is an institution on the third floor. The spacious, stylishly decorated restaurant can accommodate 500 people. And here there is never a crowd sensation. Here, according to the buffet system serves international and Greek cuisine. And hot dishes are cooked right at the guests, organizing whole culinary shows.

There is a snack bar near the pool. There you can order juices, exotic cocktails, alcoholic beverages, grill, barbecue and all sorts of other snacks. There is also a main bar. It offers panoramic views of the bay, as well as from the restaurant. It works only in the evening. This establishment offers a large selection of alcoholic beverages, coffee, tea, etc.

And also CHC Galini Sea View 5 * (Crete) has two a la carte restaurants. In one you can taste the delights of Italian cuisine, while in the other you can enjoy Chinese.
