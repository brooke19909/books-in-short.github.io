Skating rink (Chistye Prudy): address and mode of operation

Every winter, people expect not only the New Year, but also the opportunity to have a good time in the fresh air. Many sports are especially fun. Ice skating equally attracts citizens of all ages. The skating rink on Chistye Prudy collects quite a large number of visitors every year. They like the pleasant atmosphere as well as the location of the place to stay. Historical data indicate that people came to ride ponds on Chistoprudny Boulevard in the last century.

general information

The skating rink on Chistye Prudy begins to work in December and stops operating in approximately March. Here visitors are waiting for natural ice and a pleasant pastime. This skating rink is located right on the ponds. Entrance here is free, so visitors do not need to spend extra money on tickets. Under good weather conditions, the resting place is open from Monday to Sunday around the clock.

Many guests come here from different parts of the city to go ice skating. Here lovers often meet, family vacations, teens come for skating together. On the territory there are two fields especially for skate lovers. In addition, on Chistye Prudy you can play hockey, as there is a separate field for it. Also, for entertainment, food points, snow slides are open. There are places near the skating rink where it is convenient to change shoes. In the absence of personal skates, you can contact the rental point to take them for the duration of the session.

By New Year's holidays, the territory is decorated with various installations. Many users share a photo of the skating rink on Chistye Prudy on the Internet to show it to other people. Where is the ice rink

The popular place got its name for a reason. The skating rink is located right on the ponds with beautiful views. You can find it quite simply, as well as get to your destination. The skating rink on Chistye Prudy is located on Chistoprudny Boulevard, Building 1. There are several ways to get to it:

  • Metro - Chistye Prudy station, as well as Turgenevskaya or Sretensky Boulevard.
  • Trams 3 and 39 to the metro station Chistye Prudy.
  • Bus 3 to the same stop. Or buses number m3 and t25 to the stop "Pokrovsky gate".

On the tram, you can make a pleasant trip along Chistoprudny Boulevard, passing near the skating rink.
