Description of an interesting establishment with sea animals





A mobile dolphinarium visited Irkutsk. Irkutsk and its residents gladly received the news of this. A dolphinarium mobile tent was installed near the Fortuna shopping center on Lower Embankment. Dolphinarium in Irkutsk is equipped with the latest technology. That is why he is not afraid of the cold, he can run smoothly at any time of the year in all climatic zones of our country.

The dolphinarium in Irkutsk is part of the traveling troupe of Moscow, which constantly travels around the country on tour and shows its indelible impression of a water show. Dolphins, fur seals take part in it, and in some cases walruses and even beluga whales are involved in the performance.





Residents, as well as guests of the city, fell in love and visit the dolphinarium in Irkutsk with pleasure and joy. Reviews about him are usually enthusiastic. Indeed, both adults and children like to watch marine animals. Here are some positive points noted in the reviews by those who have already managed to visit the mobile dolphinarium in Irkutsk:

- he began his work during the period of severe frosts, but the ideal microclimate was created indoors for both animals and visitors with a comfortable air temperature of 24 degrees Celsius and slightly elevated humidity;

- the performance is not prolonged, its duration is less than an hour, which is optimal and allows children and adults to get a lot of pleasure from the show of marine animals, not getting tired at all;

- The program of the performance is very diverse, artists with tails, fins and fins communicate with the audience. They also dance beautifully to the music and perform amazing acrobatic stunts that captivate visitors.

Dolphinarium Opening Hours

The program consists of performances by Black Sea dolphins and fur seals. To get to the performance, you need to purchase entrance tickets for a trip to the dolphinarium in Irkutsk. The ticket office hours are organized in such a way that anyone can buy entrance tickets to the show at a convenient time for him. They work daily, from ten in the morning until seven in the evening. You can always find out about the availability of tickets for a specific day at the ticket office and the possibilities of organizing a group visit to the presentation by phone.

Dolphin shows

The performance lasts approximately 45 minutes. The cost of admission tickets varies depending on the location of seats in the hall. The hall seats about 550-650 people. Daily performances begin at eighteen, and on weekends two more additional shows are added to them - at twelve and fifteen hours. Children under three years of age can watch the performance for free on the basis of the birth certificate provided by the accompanying adults or an entry in the passport of one of the parents. But for children older than three years, the price of tickets is the same as for adults.

Additional services for visitors

In dolphinariums you can chat with dolphins, swimming with them in the pool. This service is paid. Therefore, if you have such a desire, you need to buy a ticket at the box office for such an exciting adventure. The minimum time for providing this service is ten minutes.

Little conclusion

Now you know what a dolphinarium in Irkutsk is. Despite the fact that it is mobile, the performances are interesting there, and the conditions for the audience and actors are comfortable.
