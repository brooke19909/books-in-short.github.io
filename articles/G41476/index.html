Hotel Sea Breeze Village 3 * (Goa, India): description and photos

Want to spend an unforgettable vacation in Goa (India)? Are you looking for a budget hotel, but with comfortable and equipped with all the necessary rooms? Planning to live near the beach? In this case, Sea Breeze Village 3 * may suit you perfectly. About what is offered to guests here, and we will talk further.



Location

The 3-star Sea Breeze Hotel is located in Goa, in Calangute, 500 meters from the beach of the same name. In the vicinity of the hotel there are a lot of cafes, shakes, shops, supermarkets, tour agencies, a clothing and grocery market. So tourists will always find where to go and what to do. The nearest airport is Dabolim. It is 46 kilometers away from the hotel in question. So upon arrival, your journey to the hotel will take no more than an hour.



Description

Sea Breeze Village 3 * (Goa) was opened in 2007. It is a small hotel with a beautiful, well-groomed and green area. The housing stock of the hotel is represented by 56 rooms. They are located in several two-story buildings. The hotel has an area of ‚Äã‚Äã3200 square meters. m

The hotel has two outdoor pools (for adults and for children), a restaurant, a bar, and laundry. For an additional fee, guests can be provided with a doctor. Tourists can also rent bicycles.

Sea Breeze Village is perfect for active travelers who are not used to spending a lot of time at the hotel, but prefer to visit the beach, explore the surroundings and go on excursions.



Housing stock

As mentioned above, in total the hotel in question has 56 rooms. All of them belong to the standard category. Rooms are 25 square meters. A maximum of three guests can be accommodated here. The rooms are located in three two-story buildings. Many of our compatriots who have visited the Sea Breeze Village 3 * (India, Goa) advise, if possible, to settle in rooms whose windows do not open onto the pool. The fact is that, according to them, sometimes other tourists make noise around the pool all night long, interfering with the rest of the other guests. Therefore, in order to protect yourself from such trouble, it is better to immediately take care of the room in which loud sounds will not reach you.

As for the hotel apartments, they are, according to tourists, clean, bright and pleasantly decorated. It has everything you need for living: a TV with cable channels, comfortable furniture, a refrigerator (no minibar), air conditioning, ceiling fan, bathroom with shower, balcony or terrace. Throughout the hotel guests are provided with free Wi-Fi access. True, as our compatriots note, the Internet does not ‚Äúcatch‚Äù well in all rooms. But in general, there were no particular complaints about this. As for the storage of documents and valuables, there are no safes in the apartments. However, you can use the special cells at the reception. But you should keep in mind that for this you must have your padlock. Many tourists also got out of the situation by hiding valuables in suitcases equipped with padlocks with keys.

Service, staff

Rooms were cleaned every day. Towels were renewed several times a week. But bedding here, according to tourists, change infrequently. However, for most guests this did not cause any particular complaints.

The guests also had no complaints about the rest of the staff. So, the hotel staff has always tried to help tourists if necessary. In addition, they were always friendly and welcoming. Please note that few people speak Russian here. However, even knowing the basics of the English language, you can communicate without problems.



Nutrition

The cost of living in Sea Breeze Village includes breakfast only. They pass in a hotel cafe. Tourists who first vacationed in Goa, breakfasts seemed scarce. However, experienced travelers claim that they are quite standard for Indian economy class hotels. So, for breakfast you will be offered scrambled eggs or omelet, potatoes with vegetables or meat, toasts with jam, tea, coffee, juice. In general, according to vacationers, tourists have no difficulties with food in Goa. After all, there is a mass of cafes where you can eat cheaply.

Beach vacation

The advantages of this hotel, many tourists attribute its relative proximity to the sea. So, the beach can be reached in about 7-10 minutes. The beach here is large, fine sand. There are a lot of people here. Therefore, experienced travelers are advised to explore other beaches of the island. You can do this by using the services of a taxi or by renting a motorbike.

By the way, on many beaches there is a mass of so-called shakes, where you can either have a full lunch, or have a snack or treat yourself to a drink. In almost all of them, customers enjoy free Wi-Fi, as well as sun loungers with umbrellas, where you can spend the whole day.

Opinion of tourists

Judging by the words of our compatriots who left 3 * reviews about Sea Breeze Village, they were generally satisfied with the hotel. A confirmation of this is his rating, which is 3.9 points out of five maximum possible.
