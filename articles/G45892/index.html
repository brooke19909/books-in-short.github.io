Zaraysk hotels: description

Zaraysk is located 145 km from Moscow. This city attracts tourists with its interesting sights. It also has many operating enterprises and people come here on business trips. Therefore, hotels with different levels of comfort and room rates are located in the city.

Shopping and hotel complex

  • Suite

The rooms are bright and modern renovated, comfortable furniture is located. Each room has a TV and air conditioning. They also have a clean and comfortable bathroom. Free parking is available at the building. You can book a room at any time of the day. Settlement is also made without time limits.





Dining at the hotel is not provided. At the reception there is a minibar with hot drinks. On the lower floors are trading floors with all the necessary goods and products. There are cozy cafes nearby.
