What are photo collages and how to create an original DIY gift

Bulky photo albums or general collective photographs that evoke boredom are a thing of the past. Today you can create an interesting, expressive and compact souvenir, in which the most memorable events of life will be collected.

Collage is a combination of images

Answering the question about what is photo collages, we can say that this is a wonderful opportunity to make your favorite pictures interesting on one sheet. A collage of photos is an unusual, beautiful and memorable gift for relatives and friends, work colleagues, classmates.

You can compose photographs on various topics: wedding, family tree, anniversary or birthday of a child, landscape and many others. It all depends on your preference. You can also choose an unusual form of such work.

Manufacturing tips

If you want to get a flawlessly executed collage, you should contact a specialist designer. Usually they resort to their help in those cases when you need a photo collage, the photo of which is processed in the appropriate programs and selected in form and content. Workshops and laboratories for the manufacture of such souvenirs perform them using a variety of effects: volumetric images, blurry pictures, tinting, masking and many others.

However, you can create a photo collage at home, showing imagination and creativity. First you need to determine its theme, which will determine the shape and color scheme of this memorable subject.

You can use various computer applications for processing digital photos, choosing interesting effects.



Distribute the images by size and importance: in the center it is better to place the most important photo for you, and place the rest of the images around.

Frames on such collages can also be decorated with pieces of photographs.

Puzzles collected from parts of photographs look original.

Theme of photo collages

Pets are a special occasion to create a photo collage. Photos of your favorite cats and dogs are not always in a prominent place. Therefore, collecting them on one poster is a great idea for animal lovers. By the way, you can combine all the pictures in a shape that resembles your pet in silhouette.

Pictures of nature are always popular. You can create a landscape photo collage. A wonderful solution is to arrange a calendar with photos of different seasons: autumn, winter, spring and summer.

Various family or corporate celebrations captured in the photo will look great if they are beautifully distributed in one collage. Such propagated posters will be original gifts for guests attending an anniversary or wedding.

From the history of occurrence

Photo collage as an art originated in the 19th century. Daguerreotype masters used combination printing to enhance and decorate pictures. In the 50-60s of the XX century, this trend flourished thanks to the work of American photographers - Welsmann and Michaels, the pioneers of photomontage. In recent years, photo collage has become the most popular way to design advertising, theater and movie posters. It is used to design books and magazines.

What are photo collages today? This is an opportunity to create a unique souvenir, to beautifully arrange various advertising and social booklets, to draw attention to burning problems.
