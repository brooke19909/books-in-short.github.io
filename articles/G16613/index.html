Lake Lembolovskoe on the Karelian Isthmus

To get here, you should move from St. Petersburg to the north in the direction of the Karelian Isthmus. Lembolovskoe Lake is located in the Vsevolozhsk District of the Leningrad Region. Its length from north to south exceeds 10 kilometers, and its width varies between 2-3 kilometers. The coast is largely rugged. The nearest settlement is the village of Vaskelovo, located on the eastern side.

Lembolovskoe Lake as an option for a Sunday holiday



This is a great place to relax in the nature from city hustle and bustle in a shorter or longer period of time. This is the most economical and low-budget vacation option, which does not mean that such a choice is not justified. The nature of the middle band has a rather large recreational potential, which is not yet fully utilized. If there is a desire to make sure of this, then the path lies on Lake Lembolovo. A map of the Leningrad Region indicates that the road here from the northern capital will not take much time. The access to the lake is quite convenient throughout the journey. But in the absence of vehicles, you can also get here by train from the Finland Station, you should get off on the 54th kilometer platform.

Lembolovskoe Lake is surrounded by quite picturesque hills covered with dense coniferous and deciduous vegetation. Among other things, hazelnut grows on its shores , the crop of which can be harvested at the end of summer. The shores are sandy and comfortable for swimming. Lake Lembolovskoye has a shallow depth, and in the summer months the water in it warms up well throughout the volume. This pond is well placed for a simple beach holiday, and for fishing. This is due, not least, to its hydrological regime. The water in the lake is flowing, five small rivers and streams flow into it, and only one flows out. This circumstance prevents waterlogging of the reservoir.

Fishing on Lembolovsky Lake

This is one of the main reasons to be here. The fish in the lake has not yet been transferred, despite the fact that they are caught very intensively, and in recent years the fish stocks of the lake are clearly decreasing. Best bream, pike, zander and perch are caught. On the eastern shore of the reservoir is the village of Vaskelovo, and on the southern - the military unit. Therefore, fishing is best to go to the western and eastern shores of the lake. You can catch both from the shore and from the boat. In winter, ice fishing is practiced on Lake Lembolovsky.



Lembolovskoe lake. Holiday Village "Lenenergoremont"

This vacation option is most suitable for those who are not ready to part for at least some time with the benefits of civilization. A decent level of comfort, and everything necessary for a pleasant pastime can be found on the old, but well-preserved recreation center from Soviet times. Her small houses are staggered on a secluded cape, a peninsula extending deep into the body of water. There is a boat pier, a bathhouse, sports grounds with equipment and a traditional open-air place, where it is customary to grill kebabs. Guest houses can accept tourists even in winter.
