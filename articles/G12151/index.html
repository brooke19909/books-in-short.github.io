Knitted poncho with knitting needles: beautiful, warm, stylish

Knitted poncho is a convenient, practical and beautiful piece of clothing. This thing is universal, it is worn by little girls, young girls, women of middle and old age. In addition, the poncho will be to the face and "slender", and ladies with curvaceous. This garment advantageously hides all the flaws of the figure. All these facts indicate that in the wardrobe of every representative of the fair sex a knitted poncho must be made, with knitting needles. Where to get such a little thing? Handicrafts can be bought in specialized stores or ordered directly from the masters. Those women who have at least basic knitting skills can do the poncho with their own hands. Read our article to the end and see for yourself. Here you will find information on how to make knitted poncho knitting needles (with description).



What could it be?

This hand-knitted garment can be worn at any time of the year. An openwork poncho is perfect for cool summer evenings. It is knitted with needles from a thin cotton or silk thread using yarn patterns. Such a product is more like a light pelerine.





A warm winter poncho is knitted from wool. Products from mohair and angora also turn out fluffy. Today it is very fashionable to wear such products, made with bulk patterns of thick yarn. To work on them use thick knitting needles. Knit a poncho with knitting needles of a wide variety of patterns. Beginners can do it in hosiery, garter stitch or elastic. Experienced needlewomen use all kinds of combinations of patterns in creating such a product: braids, plaits, openwork tracks and more.

How not to make a mistake with the choice?

In order for the knitted poncho to have an aesthetic appearance and not lose it after the first wash, it is important to be able to choose the right materials for its manufacture. When you come to buy yarn, carefully read the information on the label. It indicates which number of knitting needles is suitable for working with this type of thread. That's just such a tool and get it. Also pay attention to the thickness of the thread and its composition. If the materials and tools are selected correctly, you can be sure that your knitted work will look beautiful and neat.

How to make a poncho with knitting needles?



From the following description, you will learn how to knit the simplest model of such a product of a square shape. On knitting needles with a long fishing line, dial 180 loops and knit directly until the work height reaches 40 centimeters. Find the middle of the row and close the center 26 stitches. Next, continue to knit the product in two parts: first one side for 14 centimeters, and then the other. After that, connect the hinges again into a single product, gaining an additional 26 loops in the center. Do the work again with a straight blade over 40 centimeters. When the total height of the product reaches 54 cm, close the hinges. As a result, you get a square with a hole in the middle. Fill the neck with several rows of 1x1 gum. If you want a poncho with a high collar, then trim the height of 15-20 centimeters. On the bottom of the product, you can make a fringe by tying pieces of yarn to the loops of the first row.

That's just the knitting poncho knitting. Just a couple of evenings - and in your wardrobe a new beautiful and original piece of clothing will settle.
