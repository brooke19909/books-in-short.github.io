What can be made from champagne cork: interior design ideas

Cork is a unique material for creating various crafts. It easily crumbles, pierced, cut, glued and painted. Crafts made from this material can become interior decoration, everyday objects or unique souvenirs and jewelry. So why neglect such good material? Let's use it to create a new masterpiece. You ask a question - what can be made from cork from champagne - useful and interesting? For example, make a rug in the bathroom . To do this, you will need from 200 to 400 traffic jams. The quantity depends on what size you want to make the product. You will need an old rubber mat, knife and waterproof glue.

How to make an original bath mat

Cut the cork in half. Take the backing and start sticking corks on it.

You can arrange them both vertically and horizontally. When all the corks are glued, take a knife and cut off the excess backing. Of course, to make such a project a reality, you will have to empty more than one bottle of champagne. Seek help from relatives and friends to gather the right amount of material.

And what else can be made from cork from champagne? For example, a cutting board, lampshade, ottoman, hot coasters, casket, original panels, candlesticks and much more.

We also suggest making a chair from champagne cork. Such a decorative element will fit harmoniously into the interior of your miniature flower garden.

Use pliers to cut the bottom of the basket, which connects the four twisted wires. Pull it out and straighten it. The wire is strong enough, be careful. Then twist the back of the chair out of it. Next, remove the aluminum plug from the twisted wire . She will be a seater. Four twisted wires will become legs for a chair. Connect them to the back.
