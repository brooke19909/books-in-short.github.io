How to make a penguin costume for a baby with your own hands

Need to make a penguin costume for a child? Do it yourself with your own hands. If you have absolutely no experience in needlework and sewing, take the most basic option based on ready-made clothes with a minimum of independent work. Advanced craftswomen will be able to please the baby with a real masterpiece. By the way, the child will be happy to help you if you organize the joint creative process correctly.





Where to begin

Before making the outfit, of course, you need to take measurements, but in order to create a children's penguin costume , you should first decide what you will make it from and how much time you have left. If it is very small, you should not complicate your work, but it makes sense to come up with the simplest option for implementation.

The basic principle of creating a penguin costume is a successful combination of white and black colors with the addition of characteristic details (beak, paws, eyes, wings). For beauty, some complement the outfit with a butterfly (original and ‚Äúofficial‚Äù). Both boys and girls can play penguins. A female version may be required, for example, for dancing in kindergarten. Thus, first of all, it is worth analyzing what ready-made clothes you already have in the right color, and with a limited amount of time, you can easily make an outfit based on existing things.





New Year penguin costume: ideas and options

You have several possible ways to work. The choice depends on the material available and how much money and time you are willing to spend on creating an outfit. And, of course, your sewing experience matters. So, you can act in the following ways:

  • make an outfit of available things in suitable colors;
  • completely sew a penguin costume for the child with your own hands according to the finished pattern;
  • create separate details for the pattern or without it and combine them with existing clothing.

The first option is the easiest. It is suitable if the child suddenly remembered in the evening that tomorrow he needs to be a penguin at school. The second method of work, you can say the opposite, it will be appropriate when you have enough time before the holiday or performance and you can sew. For beginners who want and have the opportunity to do something more original than in the first case, you can advise a combined option.





What is required

If you consider that you are still sewing a New Year's penguin costume, you need to prepare the following:

  • a list of parts with measurements taken from the child or ready-made templates of the appropriate size;
  • paper for the pattern;
  • a pencil, ruler, eraser, or printer to print a workpiece downloaded from the Internet;
  • scissors;
  • the fabric is white, black, and also a little yellow or red for the beak and paws;
  • pins
  • a piece of chalk;
  • thread with a needle;
  • sewing machine.

As you can see, the set is standard.

How to make a hat

If you look at any penguin costume (the photo can be found above or below in the text), you will see that in each version there is a hat that imitates the head of a bird with a beak and eyes. In complex products, it is performed as a unit with a jacket or cloak, that is, in the form of a hood. It is sewn both from a combination of details in black and white, and only from black fabric.





If you need to make a children's penguin costume in one day, it makes sense to take a ready-made black hat, and it is better to have a tight-fitting shape (for example, a bathing suit) and sew on top your eyes and beak. If you have enough time, sew a hat from scratch. It is easy to make it from two semicircles stitched along an arc or from parts resembling petals (of 4 or 6 pieces). Use the same jewelry.

As for the manufacture of the beak, it is made both flat, in the form of a triangle, and a volumetric cone. In the second case, you need a little filler, for example, holofiber or shred remnants. Eyes, by the way, can also be slightly convex. Then you need to not just stitch the black circle on the white and on the cap, but pre-fill the parts with the same stuff for stuffing. In a word, there are many options for simple execution. If you want more creativity and realism, use a ready-made template you like.

DIY penguin costume for a child (the simplest option)

The most elementary outfit for a girl is easy to make from improvised means, or rather, available clothing. It is enough to take black tights or leggings, red or yellow socks (they will play the role of paws), as well as a white and black turtleneck, t-shirt. Ideal if you have a white vest. It is enough to put it on a turtleneck and the suit is ready. You can find an unnecessary t-shirt and cut off her sleeves. Wearing a black turtleneck or sweater, it will look quite acceptable. The same penguin carnival costume, only with black shorts, and preferably pants, is easy to make for a boy. To complete the composition is a butterfly. You will definitely need to make a hat, which, as already mentioned, can also not be sewn from scratch, but use a suitable blank.

Penguin costume (medium difficulty)

If you did not find your favorite pattern or prefer to make them yourself, it is worth deciding what details your outfit will consist of.





Carnival costume of a penguin, in addition to a hat, can be composed of the following options for details:

White:

  • panties;
  • shirts;
  • overalls.

Blacks:

  • vests;
  • tailcoat;
  • jacket;
  • capes with wings;
  • large collar with a hood.

Choose those items of clothing that you can sew without difficulty in constructing patterns. If everything is done correctly and accurately, you will get a magnificent penguin costume. The photographs presented in the text clearly demonstrate the possibility of implementing simple and quick ideas. To make an original and stylish carnival outfit, you need to be able to fantasize and come up with easy ways to solve the problem. Being a professional dressmaker is not necessary at all.

So, you have learned how to make a penguin costume for a child with your own hands. This is not difficult. Choose any option you like. Consider the amount of free time you have and the level of complexity of the model.
