Sanatorium "Semenovskoe": description, travel, prices and reviews

Sanatorium "Semenovskoye" is located in the Stupinsky district in the south of the Moscow region. The health resort is located away from busy roads, in a forest on the banks of the Lopasnya River. This is one of the picturesque corners of the Moscow region. The sanatorium is owned by the Federal Security Service of Russia. It is intended for the treatment and rehabilitation of employees of this organization. However, not only FSB workers can spend their vacations here, they also sell commercial trips for vacationers. Therefore, many people are interested in information about living conditions and treatment, as well as about prices at the Semenovskoye sanatorium.

From the history of the health resort

The sanatorium is located on the territory of the old estate "Otrada-Semenovskoye". Her main house has survived to the present in a dilapidated state and looks abandoned. The personal park has been preserved. The health resort is located on the site of former outbuildings. In 1977, new buildings for the sanatorium were erected on the estate, and since then Semenovskoye has been open to vacationers. Here you can spend a vacation, as well as undergo treatment and rehabilitation.

Address and Directions

The sanatorium of the FSB "Semenovskoye" in the Moscow region is located at: Stupinsky district, Semenovskoye village, Shkolnaya street, 1. The distance to the capital is about 78 km.

To get to the health resort by public transport, you need to take an electric train from the Paveletsky station to Mikhnevo station. Then transfer to bus 48, which follows to the village of New Life. You need to get off at the Semenovskoye stop. The journey from Moscow to the sanatorium takes about 1 hour 40 minutes.

Number of rooms and prices

The sanatorium "Semenovskoye" has single and double rooms. They are equipped with a loggia, refrigerator and TV. There are superior rooms (suites), consisting of three rooms. They are designed for 1-2 people. Each room has the necessary set of dishes.

Prices in the sanatorium of the FSB "Semenovskoye" (Moscow region) depend on the degree of comfort of the room, on the season, as well as on the duration of treatment and rest. The price includes food, accommodation and therapeutic procedures. You can find out the exact prices by calling the resort.

Four meals a day. It is carried out on a custom menu. The vacationer is offered several options for dishes, and he chooses the most suitable of them.

Services and infrastructure

The health resort specializes in spa and rehabilitation treatment of vacationers with pathologies of the heart and blood vessels, musculoskeletal and central nervous systems, as well as digestive organs.

In the sanatorium "Semenovskoye" there is an office for functional diagnostics, where you can undergo an examination of the cardiovascular system. There is a clinical laboratory where a general and biochemical blood test is done.

You can get advice from doctors of the following specialties:

  • otolaryngologist;
  • ophthalmologist;
  • gynecologist;
  • neuropsychiatrist.

There is a physiotherapy department. Here, procedures such as massage, manual therapy, inhalation, acupuncture, and magnetic laser treatment are performed. The department has modern devices.

On the territory of the sanatorium there is a source of mineral water. Its use is useful in diseases of the gastrointestinal tract. A photo of the pavilion with a mineral spring can be seen below.

There is a herbal bar serving herbal drinks. The hydrotherapy unit has a Sharko shower, a sauna, and therapeutic baths.

The sanatorium is well organized leisure travelers. There is a cinema hall, a swimming pool, regularly organized excursions to interesting places near Moscow. There is the opportunity to exercise in the sports and gym.

The health resort buildings are located near the Lopasnya River. The resort has its own beach with a boat station.

Here you can relax and undergo treatment in the winter, as the health resort accepts vacationers throughout the year.

Reviews about the sanatorium

On the Web you can find many positive reviews about the resort. Vacationers write about the attentive and responsive attitude of doctors and nursing staff of the health resort. People are satisfied with the living conditions, the rooms have everything you need. The food in the dining room of the sanatorium is varied and delicious.

There are also negative reviews. They relate mainly to living and living conditions in the sanatorium. It is not always possible to stay in single rooms, they are often busy. In the summer, sometimes there are interruptions in water supply, because of this, Charcot's showers and therapeutic baths are closed to the public.
