Knitted skirt (crochet): scheme

Of all things, the skirt is crocheted most easily and quickly . The scheme is usually an alternation of several patterns, due to which original products are obtained. Beginners are better to start with children's things. Firstly, they fit faster. Secondly, in case of error, they can be dissolved and reconnected. Third, crochet experience is gained.

How to knit children's skirts?

The hook allows you to knit the product in parts or in whole. Girls like fluffy skirts. Using openwork patterns, get shuttlecocks. Some novice craftswomen do not understand how to create tiers in a product. Let's consider in more detail how a three-tiered skirt for a girl is crocheted.

The scheme consists of three patterns:

  • the first pattern includes double crochets (indicated in the diagrams by CH);
  • the second picture is represented by a filet grid;
  • the third - with an openwork pattern.

In terms of waist, knit crochet in a circle. Literally after ten to fifteen centimeters, knit a filet net (CH, two air loops (VP), CH), adding loops. Three shuttlecocks are then attached to the net, so the length of the skirt depends on its size.

Next, from the second row of the fillet grid, you begin to knit any openwork pattern that is used for collars, napkins, bedspreads. You start the second tier from the middle of the fillet net, and knit the third shuttlecock in its last row. After creating the tiers, return to the belt again and tie it first with arches, then fill them with double crochets.

Crochet motif skirts: schemes, description

Products from motifs are convenient in that they knit individual elements faster. But when assembling the whole product, beginner needlewomen may have difficulties. Therefore, it is better for beginners to choose rectangular or square patterns, and leave round, floral patterns to professionals. Before work, you need to do some preparatory steps.



  1. Take measurements.
  2. Draw a pattern.
  3. Connect the motive, measure its size in centimeters.
  4. Correlate the size of the motive with the pattern (you can schematically measure the location of the motives, then you will see how much "shortage" or bust "
  5. Make adjustments, that is, with a small shortage, scatter centimeters for tying all the motives in several rows. When "busting" or modify the pattern (but then it should be repeated throughout the product), or also scatter centimeters on other motives.

You can alternate motifs with sirloin pattern or columns. Firstly, on simple patterns it is easier and inconspicuous to increase or decrease the loops, and then attach the motives. Once all the calculations have been completed, it remains to crochet the skirt. Patterns of openwork and floral patterns are given below.

Beginner Tips

For winter options, choose thick threads and the same hook, then even a skirt made of crochet posts will contact you quickly. For summer options, cotton and a thin hook (number 1.5-3) are suitable. The easiest option is to tie a filet net, and sew individual floral elements on it.

Another option is to use ready-made stripes of patterns. For example, maple leaves, squares with flowers. Such strips go from fifteen centimeters, so with these patterns you can start and finish the product. Such patterns can be horizontal or vertical rectangular stripes, wedges.

Fancy patterns allows you to create crochet. Patterns of skirts can be made of maple leaves, represented by a yellow-orange-brown gamut. The top of the skirt is knitted from dark yarn, and the bottom is laid out with bright autumn leaves. Since the motives are sewn onto each other, the skirt is opaque (do not use thick wool, as the product will be too voluminous and heavy).

Brief Summary

The hook allows you to knit products in a whole circle, details, parts, wedges, motifs. So that the clothes do not shine through, choose a dense pattern. Under the openwork skirt, you need to sew a lining or use tiers. For beginners, it is better to start working with children's products or with ready-made schemes, and with the advent of experience, you can combine the patterns yourself.
