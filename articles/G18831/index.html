Hotel Koh Chang Resort & Spa 3 * (Thailand / Phuket): photos and reviews of tourists

Thailand is an exotic country that attracts tourists with its unique nature, mild climate and incredibly interesting culture. Over the past few years, many new hotel complexes have opened on the coast that can offer the traveler a welcoming meeting and real home comfort. One of them is a small hotel called Koh Chang Resort Spa 3 *. What is this place and what conditions can tourists expect? This question is interesting to many readers.

Description and photo of the hotel. Location





The hotel complex Koh Chang Kacha Resort Spa 3 * is located in a picturesque area, near the sea coast, in the west of Phuket. The journey from Pattaya to the hotel takes about four hours by bus. Chang Island International Airport is approximately 35 km away.

The hotel territory is relatively small (its area is about 8 thousand square meters), but it is beautifully furnished. Here you can stroll in the shade of palm trees and firs, enjoying the beauty of nature, mountain views and fresh air. The hotel consists of three large buildings and several small bungalows that are suitable for lovers of a quiet secluded holiday. This place was opened in 1989, and the last thorough repair was carried out in 2013.

Regardless of which category you choose, you will find a cozy, clean room with a homely, relaxed atmosphere. Guests can relax in comfortable beds. There is a necessary set of furniture and household appliances. The summer air conditioning will help to cope with the summer heat. There is a TV, so you can spend your free time watching your favorite satellite channels. You can also use the phone. There is a minibar, but they fill it only with drinking water - all other drinks in the room are delivered only at an additional cost.

The bathroom has a shower. Some rooms have a bathtub and a private jacuzzi. A hairdryer is provided free of charge upon request. They clean here daily, simultaneously changing towels and replenishing supplies of hygiene products.

Catering for tourists





Hotel Koh Chang Kacha Resort Spa 3 * operates according to the BB scheme - this means that guests are offered free breakfasts (bed & breakfast). Buffet lunches are served in the hotel restaurant every morning. The reviews confirm that the menu is good, there is a selection of fresh fruits and pastries. You will be offered aromatic tea.

For lunch and dinner, you can in the same restaurant. However, in the daytime and in the evening there are menus and dishes are paid separately. It offers masterpieces of Thai and international cuisine. Near the pool there is a bar where, for a small fee, you can order a chilled drink or a delicious exotic cocktail.

In the vicinity there are shops where you can buy products at low prices. In addition, there are enough cafes, bars and restaurants where you can find food for every taste. In short, nutrition is unlikely to be a problem.

How close is the beach? Holidays by the sea





Arriving on the island of Phuket, tourists primarily rely on an interesting vacation on the beach. What can the hotel offer Koh Chang Resort Spa 3 *? The beach is about 300 meters away - you can reach it in 15 minutes. The coast is clean, covered with sand, the entrance to the sea is convenient and gradual. There is a pier on which you can walk.

The waters here are warm and calm. Guests enjoy free sun loungers and parasols. A clean beach towel is available at the hotel . By the way, the coast is calm, there are few tourists, and therefore it is an ideal place for a relaxing holiday in the lap of nature.

There is an opportunity to do water sports. For a small fee, you can rent a catamaran, boat or canoe. Travelers also enjoy windsurfing and sailing. There is also a club for divers - here you can consult a professional, take training courses, rent the necessary equipment, and also get a boat trip to the most interesting places on the coast.

Additional service





Despite its relatively modest size, the Koh Chang Resort Spa 3 * hotel can still offer its guests some services. For example, on its territory there is a small parking lot. You can quickly rent a transport in the city, even the hotel staff themselves will help you with this. By the way, the most popular form of transport here is a scooter, which is much faster and easier to explore the surroundings.

Also, guests can take a safe at the reception - this ensures the safety of things. The hotel has internet access. There is even a small conference room, which often becomes the venue for business events and various holidays.

Description of entertainment at the hotel

The hotel is designed for a peaceful, leisurely vacation. Nevertheless, you can have fun here. At the entrance there is a large pool, next to which there are sun loungers with soft, comfortable mattresses. This is the center of public life at the hotel - guests relax, sunbathe, swim and socialize with each other. By the way, a shallow pool is located nearby, in which children splash. There is also an outdoor jacuzzi.





The hotel has a good wellness center. Guests can visit the bathhouse, sauna, relax in the jacuzzi with an excellent hydromassage system. It also offers cosmetic and anti-aging treatments, as well as a real Thai massage. All this costs are not too expensive, especially considering the good quality, and therefore is popular among guests.

Naturally, those who regret may be interested in excursions at the hotel staff. Here, as well as in the city, you can buy many interesting tours that will allow you to explore the surroundings, get to know the local culture and traditions.

Hotel Koh Chang Resort Spa 3 *: reviews

Many travelers are interested in the opinions of people who have already managed to spend some time in this place. Reviews about the hotel Koh Chang Resort Spa 3 * are mostly positive. The hotel is small but beautiful. It is always calm, there is no fuss, noise, nothing that could overshadow your vacation. The food seems a little monotonous to some, but the food is delicious. A huge plus is the presence of your own, not too crowded beach. This hotel is suitable for travelers who prefer a quiet holiday on the beautiful shore. And the cost of living here is more than affordable. This makes the hotel so popular among holidaymakers.
