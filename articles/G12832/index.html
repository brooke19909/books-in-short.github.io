Moscow - Taganrog: attractions, route description, traveler reviews

Moscow and Taganrog are very interesting cities of Russia. This is due to historical facts, a huge number of attractions and a friendly attitude to visitors. It is worth at least once in your life to visit these outstanding places and to appreciate their beauty with your own eyes. What are they famous for? It is worth exploring this issue in more detail!

Short guide to Moscow

The opinion of the provincial capital of our country is different. For some it is a magnificent city ​​with a rich history. Many travelers consider it a center of mass entertainment. And for most people, it is an ideal place to earn money, study at an educational institution, or regulate trade relations.

The first place to visit in the capital is its “heart” - the Kremlin on Red Square. About 5 million tourists arrive on its territory annually. This attraction is often compared with the visiting card of this city. The second place in popularity is the Sparrow Hills, their beauty simply cannot but bewitch. The Kolomenskoye museum reserve is also included in the program of a tourist tour in Moscow, here tourists can discover new moments about the history of their country.





The capital can be proud of a huge number of entertainment centers for young people, children and the elderly.

Excellent city on the shores of the Sea of ​​Azov

Taganrog is located right on the shore of the Gulf of the Sea of ​​Azov. Needless to say, how attractive is the nature in this place? Favorite hobby of local residents is fishing, hunting and water sports.





In order to feel the greatness of this place, it is not at all necessary to go somewhere. Just walk along the streets of the city and enjoy the attractive view of the architectural buildings: houses, churches, museums. Many of them have retained their appearance since the pre-war era.

It is mandatory to visit the house-museum of Durov, Pushkinskaya embankment, the house of Tchaikovsky - all these objects will remain in human memory for a long time.

Route Features

Moscow and Taganrog are united by many factors. It is recommended that you independently make a comparative description by visiting each object.





You can get from one point to another in various ways, the route itself has several features:

  • In these settlements there are all kinds of points for public transport: stations and airports.
  • The approximate distance along the highway is 1100 kilometers, in the air the distance is noticeably reduced.
  • There is no time difference in Moscow and Taganrog, it is very convenient for travelers.

For the road to be fast, safe and comfortable, it is important to take care of choosing the most suitable vehicle in advance.

Possible ways to overcome the distance

There are five main modes of transport that allow you to overcome this distance:

  • Own car. Two cities are united by the famous M4 highway. It is famous for the lack of bumpy places, bright markings and a wide strip. Signs are installed along the entire road; it is almost impossible to stray from the route. The total travel time is approximately 13 hours. On the way you can find many gas stations, grocery stores and cafes.
  • It is impossible to fly by plane from Moscow to Taganrog by direct flight. Will have to additionally make a transplant in the city of Rostov-on-Don. The entire flight takes only 2 hours 30 minutes, including a short stop.




  • There are several possible options for overcoming this distance by train: direct flights and transfers. Depending on the chosen flight, the entire journey may take from 16 to 24 hours. Basically, departures are formed at night.




  • If there is no way to overcome the distance Moscow - Taganrog by car, then a taxi will be an excellent alternative to this method of transportation. It is better to use the services of official companies than private traders. In this case, you can save money spent three times.
  • The most tiresome way to get around is by bus. This is due to the fact that more than 11 hours will have to sit in one place. Departure takes place daily in the early morning.

What type of vehicle is the best? Each traveler will answer differently to this question. You should make your choice, relying on your own preferences.

Reviews

If you pay attention to the reviews on overcoming the distance Moscow - Taganrog, you can see that many tourists like how the transport connection between these two points is established. Near each railway, bus station and airport there are stops from which public transport departs in a different direction every 15 minutes. The road along the highway is very picturesque, driving along it, you can discover many new places worthy of admiration.

Moscow and Taganrog are two wonderful cities that will leave a lot of positive emotions for visitors. There are several ways to overcome the distance between them. Public transport leaves in this direction often, so there will be no problems with buying a ticket for it.
