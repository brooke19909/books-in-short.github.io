Hotel Palazzo Versace Dubai (UAE, Dubai): description, reviews, photos

Having decided to spend an unforgettable vacation with friends or a long-awaited family vacation, many tourists opt for the United Arab Emirates. Once having visited these lands, they will forever remain subjugated by the amazing beauties and unique culture of the local population. Hospitality, responsiveness and kindness - that is what blows in the air of this state. Luxurious hotels, a mild subtropical climate, a number of outstanding attractions and the versatility of the entertainment offered are to the liking of every guest of these colorful lands.

The tourist season begins in September and ends in May. The summer period is very sultry. Despite the ubiquity of air conditioners, the air temperature reaches about + 40 ° C. However, the five-star Palazzo Versace Dubai hotel offers its guests comfortable living conditions and high-quality service throughout the year.





General characteristics of the institution

Hotel Palazzo Versace Dubai 5 * Deluxe (UAE, Dubai) is a nine-story building, the doors of which were opened to welcome the first guests in 2014. With its graceful architecture, it resembles an Italian palace of the 16th century. The majestic strict entrance, high white ceilings, unique landscaped gardens and elegant furniture from Italy create the feeling of being in a real castle. The new arrivals immediately catch the eye of the legendary Medusa by Pietra di Fiume and the Greek style of decoration.

Pets are not allowed. Smoking is prohibited throughout: both in the rooms and beyond. At check-in, a deposit of about 500 AED per night is charged using credit cards or cash. Disabled people are welcome.

Location

The annual influx of tourists to the Palazzo Versace Dubai is due to its favorable location. The distance to the international airport is only 9 km, which allows those arriving to be in their apartments in a matter of minutes. Dubai Metro Station is within walking distance. The city center and the legendary shopping center are about 10 minutes away.





Number of rooms

The total number of rooms for living is 384 units, among which 169 are residences with different numbers of bedrooms. The classic interior with a predominance of light pastel colors creates an atmosphere of grandeur and grace. The floors of the rooms are covered with parquet, the bathroom and the kitchen with stone mosaic. There is access to a balcony. The penetration of sunlight is realized through panoramic windows, the view from which may vary: blue bay or urban infrastructure. Chambers options:

  1. Sibling.
  2. Two-level.
  3. Penthouse

Room sizes range from 50 to 1200 square meters.

Home improvement

The Palazzo Versace Dubai 5 * De Luxe hotel, the description of which is presented to the reader’s attention in the article, is equipped with all the necessary devices and equipment that would allow guests to feel comfort, coziness and tranquility. Each apartment is equipped with an intercom with a video screen at the front door. High Speed ​​Wi-Fi - Free. The main area - the living room - is spacious, contains a high-resolution LED-screen and satellite TV, which allows you to diversify the evenings spent within the personal apartments. The kitchen area is equipped with high-quality fittings and numerous electrical appliances, including a built-in oven with gas stove, microwave and dishwasher. There is a dinner table for six people, a set of rare dishes, sets for making tea and coffee. Bottled water is free.

The sleeping area has a double bed, a spacious wardrobe, bedside tables and a TV. Bed linen - high quality, harmoniously combined with the existing interior.

The bathroom is equipped with a jacuzzi with massagers, a shower and a set of personal hygiene products. There is a separate room with a washing machine, ironing board, iron and dryer. Slippers and bathrobes are provided.

Housekeeping is carried out daily by conscientious maids. There were no complaints about cleanliness and order in the reviews of guests about Palazzo Versace Dubai 5 *. They claim that everything just sparkles with cleanliness.





Satisfying hunger

According to guests, even a slight feeling of hunger in the walls of the Palazzo Versace Dubai is simply impossible. Eight elegant restaurants with cuisines from different countries welcome guests, giving an amazing gastronomic pleasure and enjoyment with the help of the surrounding interior and upholstered furniture. Among them: Vanitas - accepts guests over the age of five and operates on a custom-made menu. Preference is given to Italian cuisine. The total capacity is 106 seats, 36 of which are located on the outdoor terrace. Advance booking required.

Many guests prefer the Mosaico restaurant: marble floors, majestic Greek columns and mirrored walls leave a lasting impression on visitors. The hall is complemented by green plants. The panoramic windows overlook Dubai Creek. It can accommodate 140 people at the same time. Dress code required. There is no need to reserve a table.

The indescribable atmosphere of comfort and tranquility attracts guests to the Enigma restaurant, which delights guests with Turkish cuisine. Reviews of tourists about this institution are extremely positive. Limitations - 12 years old. Reservation required.

Not only residents, but also guests of the city visit Q's lounge bar, where frequent concerts of live music are held, and bartenders delight everyone with their unique cocktails.

Professional and professional chefs work in these and other restaurants of the hotel, who put a bit of soul and a portion of good mood in each dish. Their masterpieces are able to surprise everyone who decided to have lunch or dinner at these establishments.





Aquazone

On the territory of the hotel complex Palazzo Versace Dubai 5 *, the photo of which you have the opportunity to see in the article, three pools are equipped: Central, West lagoon and East. To visit them you need to purchase a subscription. The price includes the use of numerous sun loungers, which are surrounded by a swimming area, towel and soft drinks. Their consumption is necessary in the days of sultry heat in the vast expanses of Dubai. The validity period of the subscription is from 9 am to 7 pm. Any other drinks and meals at the pool bar require an additional charge. All pools are large and spacious. Numerous leafy palm trees give the aquazone a tropical touch.

A children's pool will delight kids with the opportunity to splash around in the open. Nearby is a sand zone with sandboxes, buckets and other toys that children will do in the fresh air.





Children's leisure

Palazzo Versace Dubai receives positive reviews very often from tourists who come on holiday with their children. They argue that the pastime of young children is thought out to the smallest detail. In addition to the swimming pool, children can spend their free time in the children's playroom. A variety of toys, cars, educational centers will amuse the kids. Under the guidance of a qualified educator who monitors order every second, they play games, participate in competitions and make creative crafts. Master classes are a favorite pastime of many children, because they not only develop, but also allow you to achieve a specific result. Between drawing and walking, children can watch cartoons in the company of their peers.

In the fresh air there is a game complex with many diverse slides, safe swings and spacious mazes. Here, little guests will spend all their energy before an evening sleep.

If parents prefer to retire or enjoy a romantic dinner, babysitting services are provided.

Sport and beauty

The doors of the fitness center are open to every guest. Here you can throw off a couple of extra pounds, find beautiful shapes and maintain muscle tone. And most importantly - to heal your body with regular exercise. A variety of equipment is available to everyone: fitness equipment, treadmills, barbells, dumbbells and much more. Experienced instructors will help in compiling a favorable volume of loads.

The spa has age restrictions and is only accessible to adults. A number of anti-aging and wellness treatments, relaxing massage to the sounds of calm music and barely flickering candles are available here.

Healthy sauna, bathhouse and hammam will please with their service and the opportunity to spend time with friends.





Entertainment

In walking distance from the territory of Palazzo Versace Dubai 5 * (UAE) is a golf course, water park. Here you can rent bicycles for a walk, ride a horse or go to discover the local beauty and sights. You can visit the quiet and peaceful library. Having chosen the book he likes, the reader goes on a journey through the pages of his favorite novel.

Additional services

In order that vacationers do not need anything, there is an extensive list of additional services, the main of which is a transfer. Experienced drivers will deliver guests from the airport and back at any time convenient for them. On the territory there is a secure parking, ATM, currency exchange, souvenir shops and grocery stores.

For those who wish to learn more about local attractions, a tour desk is open. Those who prefer to travel only in the company of friends or family can use the car rental service. A doctor's call is paid extra. Laundry and dry cleaning services are available around the clock.

Press, food and drink delivery to the room is carried out by responsible employees promptly upon request. An instant in solving problems, according to tourists, is one of the main advantages of the Palazzo Versace Dubai. A hairdresser and a beauty salon are happy to give a new look and hairstyle to each client.

For those who want to combine a pleasant relaxation with useful work, there are three conference rooms of various modifications. They are equipped with advanced technologies and have a unique interior design that facilitates the adoption of important decisions and matches the format of business meetings.

Rooms are cleaned daily by professional and experienced maids. Reception is open 24 hours. There is luggage storage space.





Wedding program

Beautiful views of the Dubai Gulf and a spacious park area attract couples in love with the opportunity to organize an unforgettable wedding ceremony here. The unique atmosphere will complement the image of the charming princess and her prince, and this important event in the life of the newlyweds will become unforgettable. A celebration hall with exclusive chandeliers and a delightful interior, cutlery and dishes from the Versace Home collection, interesting color schemes of comfortable furnishings will allow you to fully enjoy this event. The capacity of the halls is from 10 to 600 people. A compliment to the hotel for the newly baked family is the additional decoration of the apartments and the provision of sweets in the form of sweets.





Hotel highlights

Many guests note in their positive reviews about Palazzo Versace Dubai 5 * the extraordinary zest of this establishment, one of which is the presence in each room of free samplers of Versace perfume. An amazing design solution impresses not only adults, but also kids. Gloss and wealth encourage travelers to come back here again and again. And the footage captured by professional photographers will remind you of the summer adventure at the Palazzo Versace Dubai for many more years.
