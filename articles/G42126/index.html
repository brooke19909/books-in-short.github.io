Hotel Location

First you need to find out exactly where the Al Khoory Executive Hotel is located. We have already said that it is located in the UAE. Let's talk more about this country.

The United Arab Emirates is a federal state located in the Middle East. The UAE is located in the southwestern part of Asia. It is washed by the waters of the Persian and Oman gulfs. It is worth noting that a visa is required in the UAE. To make it, you have to spend about 6,000 rubles. Al Khoory Executive Hotel is located in the emirate of Dubai, which is located on the Arabian Peninsula in the southern Persian Gulf. The economy is based on the oil industry, port business and tourism.

The climate here is quite hot and dry. The temperature in Dubai never drops below +6 degrees. In summer it is about +40 degrees. In winter, the weather becomes more comfortable, because the columns of the thermometers do not rise above +26 degrees.

Al Khoory Executive Hotel is centrally located in Dubai, a 15-minute drive from the international airport.

Hotel Rooms

Al Khoory Executive is a small hotel by Arab standards. It has only 208 comfortable rooms of different categories:

  • "Standard" double. It is decorated in orange and white. Most of the rooms are occupied by a bed. There is a TV on the opposite wall, next to which is a small table. The room has a bathroom and a balcony with a wonderful view. The cost of this accommodation option per day is 4400 rubles.
  • The deluxe double. It is decorated in yellow and white. There is a wall-mounted TV in front of the large bed, a small table next to it. It also has a sofa, air conditioning and a minibar. The bathroom has a shower or bath. This room has a balcony with amazing views. The price per room per day is about 6000 rubles.
  • Executive. The main difference is that instead of the standard balcony there is a large terrace with comfortable armchairs and a table. The room rate is 6500 rubles.
  • Suite This room is designed for 2 guests. The layout includes a bedroom, living room, bath. a room and a small entrance hall. The room is decorated in yellow and green tones. The bedroom has a bed and a small armchair. The living room has a TV and sofa. The cost of the room is 8000 rubles.

What sights are nearby

Many tourists, choosing a hotel, wish that it was located not very far from the center and main attractions. What is next to the Al Khoory Executive Hotel 3 * in Dubai? We offer a short list of interesting objects:

Negative hotel reviews

As elsewhere, this hotel has flaws. In negative reviews about Al Khoory Executive Hotel 3 *, tourists note the following shortcomings:

  • Breakfast is very monotonous. Many say that the products are fresh, of normal quality, but there is absolutely no variety.
  • No face towels.
  • No transfers to shopping centers.
  • No clothes dryers. The rooms have nowhere to dry things.
  • Staff may forget to replenish the minibar. To prevent this from happening, you need to put an empty tray in a prominent place.
  • Far to the sea. For some 20 minutes, walking is too much. This is especially true if the heat is +40 degrees.
