Checking the balance of MTS. Ways

Many people need to always be in touch. Zero balance on the phone can be an extremely unpleasant surprise and disrupt all plans.

To prevent this from happening, a timely check of the balance of MTS, Megafon, Beeline or any other telecom operator is necessary, especially since it is not difficult to do so. How this happens, we will consider in this article the example of an MTS operator, the balance of which is checked in several ways.

USSD balance request

The essence of this method is to dial a specific alphanumeric combination on the phone. Mobile assistant



Checking the balance of MTS is possible using the Mobile Assistant service. All the required information can be heard "live". To do this, call the number "111", and then follow the recommendations of the autoinformer. To save time, you can also use quick access combinations:

111-2 - find out the balance;

111-13 - find out about credited payments;

111-3 - request the service "Promised payment".

After each combination, you must also press the call key.
