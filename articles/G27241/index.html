Air conditioners Pioneer: manufacturer, reviews

Air conditioning systems have long ceased to be a luxury. Today they are the natural means of climate control in country houses and city apartments.

Manufacturer Information





This belief is actively supported by manufacturers of HVAC equipment, among which Pioneer should be highlighted. Since 2000 , under this brand, high-quality devices have been produced that help to effectively regulate the microclimate. The company relies on reliability and durability, as well as a wide range of functions.

In the market you can find split-systems and air conditioners in a wide variety, but Pioneer offers equipment related to economy and business classes. Installations can be semi-industrial.

After reviewing the history of the company, you will understand that it was one of the first to make climate technology available to a wide range of users. During its existence, the brand has gone through many stages of development. Today it offers a wide range for the commercial and domestic sectors.

Model Reviews: KFR25BW





This equipment option costs 14,800 rubles. and is a wall split system that works in several modes. Among them are the following:

  • ventilation
  • drainage of air;
  • maintaining temperature;
  • night mode.

After reading reviews of the Pioneer air conditioner, you can understand that it works not only for cooling, but also for heating. The cooling power is 2650 watts, heating - 2700 watts. When cooling and heating, the power consumption is 825 W and 748 W, respectively.

You can control the work from the remote control. This feature is very popular with consumers. The design provides for the presence of an anion generator. The split system belongs to the A-class of energy efficiency. When the indoor unit is operating, noise is generated up to 30 dB. According to users, this value is comfortable in order to install equipment in a residential apartment.

The maximum air flow, according to consumers, is quite high and equivalent to 7.5 m 3 per minute. Equipment has no ventilation mode.

Additional modes are:

  • ventilation;

If you want to use the ventilation mode, you should know that it is activated without heating and cooling. Consumers also like the fact that this version of the device has a dehumidification mode. If necessary, you can use the on and off timer.

The design uses refrigerant type R 410A. If desired, you can adjust the fan speed. Consumers are given the opportunity to change the direction of the air flow, use the memory function settings, as well as a system that eliminates the formation of ice.

Design and antibacterial filters





As for the design, it is, according to consumers, attractive. The case has a streamlined shape and meets modern trends. Parts of the indoor unit and panel are made of high quality safe plastic. User's manual





In order to extend the life of the air conditioner, use it according to the instructions. For example, equipment should not be placed in direct sunlight. Its outlet and air inlets should not be blocked, as this will contribute to reduced performance and may cause the system to stop.

Instructions for air conditioners Pioneer supplied. You should familiarize yourself with it before installing the equipment. From it you can find out that during operation of the device the doors and windows in the room must be closed, which will increase the efficiency of the device and reduce energy consumption.

If the air filter becomes dirty, performance will decrease, so wash this part of the appliance from time to time. It is not recommended to lay objects on top of the outdoor unit and stand on the surface to avoid damage or falling of the device.

Error Codes

Pioneer air conditioners have a self-diagnosis system that allows you to identify problems that occur during operation. The user will be able to find out about them by the codes displayed on the display. If you see the designation E0, and with it the yellow diode lights up, then this will indicate a violation of the function of the discharge temperature sensor. When the green diode lights up and you see error E2, it will indicate a violation of the function of the evaporator temperature sensor.

If the voltage supply is interrupted, the air conditioner will display error E8 on the display. If there is no connection between the external and internal modules, you will see the symbol EP on the display. By examining the error codes for Pioneer air conditioners, you can understand what problems are occurring in the operation of the device. For example, if the unit starts up incorrectly, you will learn about it by the letter P0. If the mains voltage is too high, the air conditioner signals this by displaying P3.

Finally

Pioneer air conditioners became true pioneers and appeared in private homes when split systems from other manufacturers had not even entered the market. Today, such equipment can be called indispensable not only in private homes, but also in offices, as well as in public places.

These devices have all the standard functions and additional options that make operation more comfortable. For example, the Anti-FUNGUS function provides effective drying of the indoor unit before turning off the air conditioner. This prevents the formation of unpleasant odors and molds inside the case.
