Location

Resort Adalya Elite Lara Hotel 5 * is located in Antalya, on Tessisler Street, 416-1. This place is in close proximity to the beach. The distance from the international airport is 5 km. Resting here, be sure to visit the following tourist sites:

  • shopping center "Platinum Premium Mall" (1 km);
  • Butterfly Park (4 km);
  • Lara Beach (8 km);
  • golf club (9 km);
  • Theme Park of Legends (10 km);
  • Hadrian's Gate (17 km);
  • Kesik minaret (17 km);
  • clock tower (17 km);
  • Old Town (18 km).

Description of rooms

To accommodate guests in the hotel Adalya Elite Lara 5 * provides 525 rooms. Each one is elegantly decorated and equipped with necessary amenities. The following categories of apartments are available for accommodation:

  • Standard room can be equipped with one large or two twin beds. You can enjoy garden, sea, or city views from the windows. The room also has a desk and soft armchairs for relaxation. The balcony has plastic furniture.
  • For family accommodation, two-room rooms are provided. The parent bedroom has a double bed and a desk. It has access to a balcony. In addition to a pair of single beds, the children's room also has a folding sofa. Each of the rooms has its own bathroom with a bath or shower.
  • The suite is a large studio room, which combines both a bedroom and a living room. The number is divided into functional zones by a decorative partition. Thanks to the rotating design of the TV stand, you can watch your favorite channels while lying on the bed or sitting on the couch.
  • Deluxe Suite consists of two rooms. The large living room is equipped with upholstered furniture and a large dining table. In addition to a large bed, the bedroom has a cozy sitting area. The bathroom is equipped with a jacuzzi.
  • Some rooms are reserved for people with disabilities. They are characterized by the presence of safety handrails, wide doorways, as well as the convenient location of sockets and switches.

Negative reviews

The Adalya Elite Lara 5 * hotel is not without its drawbacks. Reviews allow us to talk about such negative points:

  • a small beach on which constant crowds and a total lack of sunbeds;
  • tasteless cocktails in bars;
  • towels in the bathroom smell strongly of laundry soap;
  • too modest contents of the minibar;
  • Wireless Internet does not work well both on the street and in rooms;
  • there are few showers on the beach, which is why queues line up near them;
  • there are no cotton pads and ear sticks in the bathroom;
  • there are few toys in the children's playroom;
  • the hotel is removed from the center of Antalya (you have to travel about half an hour by taxi or bus);
  • no bathrobes (they are brought only after contacting the reception).
