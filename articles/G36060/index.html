DIY satin ribbon brooch : the necessary materials and tools

A rose is a beautiful flower that cannot but inspire the manufacture of various jewelry. It provides a huge field for imagination, and jewelry always looks stunning and in a new way.

To make a rose brooch, you need to prepare:

  • satin ribbon about 5 cm wide;
  • a candle and a lighter;
  • glue moment (can be replaced with hot glue);
  • pin mount;
  • scissors, a needle, threads under the color of the tape.

Rose Brooch Making Instructions

Creating a beautiful brooch with your own hands from a satin ribbon in the shape of a rose, the first thing you need to do is make petals. The ribbon should be cut into equal squares and cut corners, creating circles from them. The number of parts for all will be different, with the necessary number it will be possible to determine during the course of work.



The edges of future petals must be treated with fire so that they do not crumble. You need to bring the ribbon to the candle in a special way: from the side right at the base of the flame, otherwise the atlas will be smoky.

Then the tips of the petals must be burned from one side. To the flame at a distance of about 1.5 cm, you need to bring the edge that goes along the tape. The part must be deformed, the more kinks there are, the more beautiful the final result will be.

When the petals are ready, proceed to making the middle of the rose. One of the round parts is flashed with thread in a circle, tightened, stuffed with filler and sewn down. After that, the finished ball is sheathed with other petals inside out.

When several sewn parts form the middle, the petals begin to be sewn with the front side inward. At the same time, they can be slightly pulled together at the base. The size and splendor of the future brooch will depend on the number of petals.

In this way, you can make several roses of different sizes, group them into one composition and stick to the cardboard base, after gluing it with a satin ribbon. You can complement the composition with leaves of green satin. A mount is glued to the resulting decoration, and the brooch is ready.

DIY satin ribbon frill brooch

Clothes decorated with frill always look elegant and refined. However, modern fashion does not imply the use of large collars, but neat and stylish jabot brooches.

You can make a fashion accessory yourself, for this you will need not only satin ribbons, lace, beads, glue, a brooch pin are useful.



The first step is to cut the lace into 6 strips, each of which is shorter by a few centimeters from the previous one. In general, it is necessary to choose the length, starting from the desired size of the future decoration.

From a satin ribbon 15 cm long it is necessary to make the basis of the future frill. To do this, cut the ribbon on one side in the form of a semicircle and process it with fire.

Next, stripes of lace must be picked up, collecting them on a thread. The longest piece of ruffle is glued along the edge of the tape, then all the others are glued in descending order.

Next, from the satin ribbon, you need to make two bows: one slightly larger than the other. They need to be placed on top of each other, tied with a ribbon and fixed with glue. The resulting bow is glued to the ruffles, a pin is glued on the back side, the place of attachment is also sealed with a satin ribbon. A brooch made of satin ribbon with your own hands is made, if desired, the middle can be decorated with beads, beads or other decorative elements.

How to make a kanzashi brooch

Today, Kansashi technique is very popular and in demand in our country. The basis of this art is the ability to fold petals of various shapes from pieces of fabric. The master collects all the petals from silk into one composition, using tweezers, and glues it to the base.



Not everyone can make a kanzashi brooch from satin ribbons with their own hands . Inexperienced craftswomen should start with the simplest jewelry. The complexity of the product is increased by adding the required number of petals. Volumetric flowers are ideal for creating brooches and jewelry for hair.
