How to put money on your phone with Sberbank: all ways

Every third Russian citizen prefers to pay for services through Sberbank. The most popular transactions are addressed to suppliers of housing and communal services, the Internet and mobile operators. Sberbank customers can replenish the balance of a mobile without a commission within 1-2 minutes. There are several options how to put money on the phone with Sberbank.

Payment methods for mobile communications through Sberbank

Clients can replenish the cellular balance not only with a card, but also in cash. Funds are credited almost instantly.

How to transfer money to a phone from Sberbank?

  1. With the help of SMS-information service "Mobile Bank".
  2. In self-service devices.
  3. Via the online bank Sberbank Online.
  4. In a mobile application for smartphones.
  5. Using the service "Auto payment".

Paying your phone through the Mobile Bank

To use this option, the client must have a bank card with an active SMS information service. It is allowed to pay for cellular communication if a free package of services - "Economical" is connected. Sberbank does not charge a fee when using the service to pay a mobile phone bill.

How to put money on the phone from Sberbank at 900?

  1. Send a message with the recharge amount to 900. For example, "200".
  2. After sending to the client in a response notification, a code will be received to confirm the operation. It should be entered and sent again SMS.
  3. Check the operation by requesting a mobile balance.

If the client has several Sberbank cards and wants to send funds from a specific account, SMS to number 900 should be supplemented. In the first message, a space after the replenishment amount must indicate the last 4 digits of the plastic carrier. Example: "200 1234", where 1234 is the end of the bank card number.

Sberbank allows you to quickly replenish the balance of a loved one using USSD codes: send notifications to 900. For the operation, the requirements are the same: a bank card with an active Mobile Bank.

How to transfer money from Sberbank via phone to another person?

  1. In the message to the number 900 it is necessary to indicate the recipient's phone number without the number "8" and the amount of replenishment through a space. Example: "9102008777 270".
  2. Confirm the operation by sending the code in the response message.

Money will be debited from one of the cards tied to the number. There is no commission for replenishing the balance of a phone that is not connected to a Sberbank card.

Mobile payment in Sberbank terminals

In self-service devices, a commission is not charged when paying with a bank card. Clients can deposit money in cash, but in this case, the bank will charge 5% for transferring money to the phone from Sberbank. The self-service area is open around the clock.

How to put money on the phone from Sberbank in the terminal using a card?

  1. Insert the payment tool into the device.
  2. Enter code.
  3. Go to the "Payments and Transfers" section.
  4. Select the "Mobile Communications" function.
  5. Enter the phone number without a figure eight.
  6. Collect replenishment amount.
  7. Check and confirm the operation.
  8. Pick up a card.

When paying, a check is issued. It contains details of the operation: phone number, mobile operator and the amount paid.

To pay in cash:

  • select the "Top up mobile" tab;
  • determine the operator or immediately dial a phone number;
  • enter the amount of replenishment;
  • check conditions, size of commission;
  • deposit money into the terminal and confirm the payment.

When paying in cash, remember that Sberbank ATMs do not give change. The client can transfer the rest of the amount to another mobile number or pay utilities, the Internet, send to charity.

Top-up your cell phone balance at Sberbank Online

Internet banking allows you to pay bills around the clock. How to put money on your phone with Sberbank Online? This can be done in 2 ways: through the "Quick Payment" function or the "Transfers and Payments" tab.

How to transfer using a quick payment service:

  1. Go to "Quick payment" by clicking on the icon with the image of the smartphone.
  2. Enter the phone number, amount and debit card.
  3. Confirm with code 900.
  4. View payment status. A Done icon means the operation completed successfully.

The second option allows you to deposit cash by phone number in the general menu of payments "Sberbank online". Here's how to make a payment:

  • Click on "Transfers and Payments";
  • select "Mobile Communications";
  • go to the operator search or click on the payment "Mobile communications, payment of any phone number";
  • enter data: phone, amount, debiting account;
  • check and confirm the transfer;
  • view status.

Activation of automatic recharge service

"Auto payment" of Sberbank allows you to forget about the need to deposit funds to the account of a mobile operator. Money will be transferred automatically in the amount set by the client.

The minimum payment is 50 rubles. Cash is debited from the card after an account balance equal to or less than the specified amount when connected, for example, 30 rubles.

Payment is available for a fixed amount with a frequency of write-offs once a week, month, quarter or year. The service is activated in Sberbank terminals or online banking.

How to activate "Auto Payment" (for example, "Sberbank online")?

  1. In the menu "My auto payments" click on "Connect auto payment".
  2. Select "Mobile Communications".
  3. Find an operator.
  4. Fill in the data: amount, write-off account, frequency, type of service, date of next payment, name.
  5. Check conditions and confirm.

No service fee is charged. If there is no money on the card, the funds are not debited, the service is suspended.

Translation in a mobile application

The mobile version allows you to make transactions in the Internet bank, which is always at hand. You can transfer money from Sberbank to a phone number in a mobile application according to the instructions similar to payment through Payments and Transfers in the desktop version of Sberbank Online.

The difference between payments is the lack of confirmation: the version of the Internet bank for smartphones does not require entering a code when paying.
