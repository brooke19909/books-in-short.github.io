Pontos Hotel (Vityazevo, Anapa): description, services, reviews

The Russian Black Sea coast, despite the large number of foreign resorts, is very popular with tourists every year. Someone comes here solely for fundamental reasons, for someone such a vacation is much more profitable than traveling abroad, and someone loves Russian resorts for their unique nature and unchanging Russian flavor.

Some resorts in the Krasnodar Territory have been favorite vacation spots for decades, and some of them have gained popularity relatively recently. Located near a city such as Anapa, the village of Vityazevo is a resort that gained its fame no more than 15 years ago.

Today it is a great place with developed infrastructure and numerous guest houses and small hotels where you can stay throughout the summer. The Pontos Hotel (Vityazevo) is just one of them. It can be safely chosen by families with children, and groups of friends, and couples in love of all ages.

We are going to rest in Vityazevo

It is definitely worth a trip to Vityazevo. After all, in summer, guests will find sunny weather, a very long and wide sandy beach with small dunes, the gentle and warm sea and wonderful air, which happens only on the Black Sea coast.

Tourists traveling here may not be worried about housing problems. Every year the number of guest houses located near the sea is growing here, so the resort can easily receive a very large number of guests. There are hotels in Vityazevo for every taste and budget: there are simple and inexpensive accommodation options, and there are entire hotels with luxurious decoration and good service, the cost of living in which is much higher. Some tourists prefer the private sector or stay in local pensions and holiday homes. Children's camps are also organized here, so do not be surprised at the crowds of children who can be seen on the local beach.

The resort itself has a very unique flavor. Once the village of Vityazevo was founded by the Greeks, and their descendants can be found here today. It is thanks to them that everything here is saturated with the European spirit, and in many aspects of the resort's life, Greek notes can be traced. Many of the hotels built here resemble Greek architecture. Even strolling along the main street called Paralia, leading to the sea, tourists probably feel clearly not in the Russian resort.

In the village, everything is aimed at maintaining tourist activity: hotels, numerous cafes, canteens and restaurants, various shops with beach accessories, souvenirs and various other stuff and, of course, wine shops.

What activities are available for tourists?

The guests of the village will definitely not be bored. What entertainment is not here! They can be found both for adults and for very young tourists, for young people and for older people.

Want to have fun on the beach? All kinds of water sports are at your disposal, as well as high inflatable slides and trampolines installed right on the beach. Want to ride with the breeze and see the surroundings? Feel free to get behind the wheel of the ATV and go on an exciting walk. Want to get plenty of dancing? Then you will find nightclubs and incendiary discos working here in the dark.

At the local dolphinarium you can enjoy fascinating performances of wonderful dolphins, fur seals and other marine life. Nobody will be indifferent to such a pastime! If there are few water entertainments on the shore, you can safely go to a local water park: some of its slides can drive even the most desperate daredevils crazy. And in the amusement park, guests of all ages will enjoy fascinating carousels, all kinds of shooting ranges with prizes and even a Ferris wheel, from which you can enjoy views of Vityazevo.

We stay at the Pontos Hotel

Very often, when choosing a hotel for a vacation, your eyes just run up from the variety of possible options. This is especially true for those who are planning to visit the Krasnodar Territory, who are waiting for Anapa, Vityazevo. The Pontos Hotel will be an excellent option for all those who value good service, comfortable accommodation and original design solutions.

The hotel owes its name to the sea, on the coast of which it is located. After all, once it was affectionately called the "hospitable sea", and in ancient Greek it sounded like "euxinos pontos". For the first time, this hotel took vacationers in the summer of 2009, and more precisely in July. Then the hotel "Pontos" (Vityazevo) consisted of only one building. And after 5 years, the hotel room stock increased - another building appeared.

The hotel area is not very large, however, like all others in this village. Here, tourists will not find hotels with chic spacious areas where you can walk at least all day. Local guest houses and hotels have fairly small adjacent spaces, but this does not prevent them from having everything necessary for the convenience of guests.

The Pontos Hotel (Anapa) boasts beautifully furnished rooms, two large outdoor pools and one small. Near them are sun loungers for relaxation. The hotel also boasts its own parking lot for vacationers and a playground for children's games. There is also a wonderful courtyard with gazebos, so at any moment you can just sit on the territory and relax from daily affairs.

How to quickly get to Pontos

To arrive at the Pontos Hotel (Vityazevo), you must first get to Anapa. This can be done either by plane or train. From the airport and from the railway station of the resort town, you should get to the bus station by shuttle bus or bus. Here you will need to transfer to a fixed-route taxi with the number 134, 128 or 114 and drive directly to the Pontos Hotel. You can also take a taxi or book a transfer directly from the hotel.

Offered rooms

For their relaxation, guests who arrived at the Pontos Hotel (Anapa, Vityazevo village) can choose one of the comfortable rooms located in two buildings. In their design, the designers used five colors: burgundy, beige, green, chocolate and blue. So when choosing a room, you can focus on your favorite color.

Guests are offered three main categories of rooms: standard, studio and suites. Each of them is equipped with necessary furniture, a TV, a safe, a refrigerator, air conditioning, a telephone and a balcony with comfortable chairs.

The smallest in size are the standard rooms. Their area is 16-18 square meters, for sleeping in them there is one double bed or two single beds, and there is also an additional sofa bed or chair bed. Thus, up to 4 guests can stay in this category. The bathroom has a shower.

Cost of living in Pontos

The cost of rooms and additional hotel services is different, depending on when the guests plan to visit the Pontos Hotel in Vityazevo. Prices for accommodation in the summer in a standard room start from 2500 rubles. per day in June and reach 3900 rubles. in August. The cost of extra bed in these rooms starts from 625 rubles.

A studio number in early June will cost 2900 rubles, and in the highest period - 4400 rubles. Extra bed costs 725 rubles. and more.

The most expensive rooms are suites. Accommodation in them will cost 4200 rubles. in early summer and at 6300 rubles. in the highest season. For an extra seat, you will need to give 1050 rubles. or more (depending on the season and age of the guest).

The price includes the use of swimming pools, wireless Internet on site, parking and some entertainment. For an additional fee of 200 rubles. per day in the room you can order a cot.

Catering

For an additional fee, guests who come to rest at the Pontos Hotel (Vityazevo) can enjoy delicious dishes that are offered in the local Gamma restaurant according to the buffet system. For breakfast and lunch you will need to pay 400 rubles. per day for one person, for a full board - 800 rubles. per day. The morning meal is from 8 to 10 in the morning, lunch is from 13 to 15, dinner is served from 18 to 20 in the evening. Guests can also enjoy a variety of dishes on the menu at this restaurant.

There is also a summer bar in Pontos called BQ, where anyone can try freshly grilled dishes. The basement bar serves a variety of drinks and alcoholic cocktails, as well as delicious dishes from the restaurant's menu. Fans of pizza and colorful Japanese dishes can visit the Grand Cafe, also located on site.

Services for an unforgettable vacation

Hotel Pontos will surely appeal to guests with children. Indeed, for young vacationers, excellent conditions are created here, thanks to which they will not even want to leave the hotel. Firstly, there is an excellent children's playground. Secondly, an entire children's playroom has been organized, in which the teacher looks after the children. It works only in the summer from 10 am to 8 pm. Often animated entertainment programs are arranged for children and their parents, so dying from boredom will not work here.

In the modern gym, all sports fans can continue their workouts, and game fans will gladly join the competitions in table tennis, billiards, chess and backgammon.

If necessary, you can use a hairdryer, laundry and ironing services. And for lovers of baths, the hotel has an infrared and Finnish saunas, which can be used for 800 rubles. at one o'clock. Shuttle services and a tour desk are also available.

For those who rest with pets

The hotel will also be appreciated by tourists who cannot imagine their trip without a pet. Not many hotels provide this opportunity to vacationers. For only 100 rubles. per day, guests can lodge their favorite dog or cat in their room. In this case, certain rules should be observed, for example, you should not take your pet to the pool or catering places on the hotel territory. The animal will have to walk outside the hotel, and you should not allow it to be on beds or other furniture. For violation of the introduced rules, a fine of 1,500 rubles will be charged from the owners.

Meeting place

The Pontos Hotel is also suitable for a business meeting. There is a rather roomy conference room with a capacity of 100 seats. It is equipped with the latest technology: there is a video projector with a screen, an LCD TV, microphones, a special board, speakers with an amplifier, a webcam and Internet access. This space can be used even by those who do not live in the hotel. And if after the event guests plan to stay at the hotel, then they will be given a discount on room rental.

How the hotel cares about the safety of guests

Very carefully, the owners of the hotel make sure that there are no unpleasant incidents. Round-the-clock security of guests at the hotel provides a modern fire alarm and video surveillance cameras installed in different places of the hotel. This is undoubtedly one of the advantages of Pontos, because any tourist wants to relax without any troubles.

Did the hotel like holidaymakers?

Guests who have already visited the Pontos Hotel (Vityazevo), write reviews about it mostly positive. Despite the rather high cost of staying here, many say that the hotel is worth the money due to the decoration of the rooms and the service provided.

Tourists vacationing here with children are delighted with the local animation. The guests are also very pleased with the food of the hotel restaurant and bars: everything is tasty and cooks quite quickly. Tourists are also satisfied with the staff who work here, who are happy to fulfill all the additional requests of the guests and help in solving problems.

Of course, there are those who are not satisfied with remoteness from the sea. You can find closer housing in the village, but it is unlikely that a couple of minutes will seriously solve this problem. Someone swears at the mess in the rooms, and at the small territory of the hotel, while others do not say anything bad about these aspects.
