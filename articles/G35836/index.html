Application Tips

This appliance can only be used indoors. It should not be placed in containers filled with liquid. If the device is connected to the network, then leaving it unattended is prohibited. Also, do not service the device in the same condition. If the cord is damaged, replace it immediately. This is done by specialists, and not manually by the owner. Do not use the device if it fell from a height or was deformed due to any impact.

The device must be stored in a closed room; liquids must not be allowed into it. Switching off and moving the device while holding the cable is prohibited. The cord should be placed far from surfaces that may become hot. Do not touch the plug and the appliance with wet hands. No debris or any other objects, including hair, should get into the openings of the steam cleaner or any moving parts. Fingers in them are also prohibited.
