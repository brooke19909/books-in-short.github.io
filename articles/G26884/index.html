"Krasnaya Gorka" in Podolsk - the ideal solution for the acquisition of residential real estate

At a distance of only 15 km from the Moscow Ring Road and 36 km from the center of the capital, Podolsk is located, which is its second largest satellite city. The northern part of Podolsk is in contact with Moscow, which makes it especially attractive for those who want to live in the immediate vicinity of the capital, but who do not have the means to purchase residential real estate directly in the metropolis.





Location of the residential complex

As already mentioned, such close contact between cities makes Podolsk especially attractive for developers. More and more residential complexes are being built, which provide housing to everyone, in accordance with their tastes and economic opportunities. One of the large-scale development projects is the Krasnaya Gorka residential complex in Podolsk.

Housings of the residential complex are located on the streets of Kolkhoznaya and Sadovaya. This area is about 2 kilometers from the city center, which is very convenient in terms of transport accessibility. The collective farm street is connected to the Warsaw highway, through which you can get directly to the capital both in a diverse public and private car. Travel time from Krasnaya Gorka in Podolsk to the nearest metro will not take more than half an hour.

Internal and external infrastructure

Since the residential complex consists of many residential buildings, the total number of apartments in which exceeds one thousand, the developer provided for the construction of a comprehensive school, a kindergarten, a supermarket, pharmacies and other social infrastructure in his project. Many of them will be located on the first floors of buildings, in non-residential premises specially designed for this purpose. In the future, it is planned to build a sports center and retail and office complexes.

In addition, the residential complex "Krasnaya Gorka" (Podolsk) is located so that its residents can use the city infrastructure. Until all these social facilities have been put into practice, residents of the residential complex can use schools, kindergartens, Yerino sanatorium and sanatorium with excellent recreation facilities, and the Podillya stadium.
