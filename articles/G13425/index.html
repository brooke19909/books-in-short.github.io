BBK TVs: reviews. Models, specifications, features

Choosing a TV is not so simple and requires maximum attention from the buyer. After all, it often happens that a user at home understands that he bought a "pig in a poke." This is because some of them focus only on value, not understanding what is in front of them for the model and what to expect from it.

Manufacturer

Today we look at BBK TVs, reviews about them and their parameters. BBK Electronics LTD is a Chinese company that sells consumer electronics worldwide. The manufacturer is popular in the CIS countries and at home. Europe is less likely to acquire BBK products.



In general, BBK is only a division of the corporation. There is a whole group of companies since 1995. Specifically, under the name BBK, products fall into Russia, Ukraine, Moldova, Belarus and Kazakhstan. Electronics comes to Europe and the USA under the name OPPO.

Now the corporation produces televisions, DVD equipment, audio equipment, tablet computers, microwave ovens, lamps, etc.

Diversity

Now on the market there are many interesting solutions from the company. Reviews of BBK TVs are often mixed. This is caused by a lack of confidence in the Chinese manufacturer, and not always high-quality assembly materials.

But in general, the company sells TVs of different diagonals. There are both small 22-inch models and 55-inch giants. Users can use excellent devices with the Android operating system, the functions of a ‚Äúsmart TV‚Äù and even sound panels.



Giant

The case is made of plastic. Regarding new-fangled models, this one has got noticeable frames. But the stand according to the latest fashion is small legs that seem fragile at first glance, but in fact are stable and strong. You can hang the TV on the wall, there are all the necessary mounts.

The remote control is functional, with a bunch of buttons, but everything is clear on it. So even if you first pick up the remote control of this model, you will be able to find at least the power button.

Despite this diagonal, the screen resolution is small - 1920 x 1080. In general, HD-quality will be enough, but fastidious users may notice a lack of detail. Sophisticated

Reviews
