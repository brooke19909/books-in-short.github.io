Pension "Arcadia" (Koblevo) - services, rooms, reviews

"Arcadia" (Koblevo) is one of the best boarding houses of the Ukrainian resort located in the Nikolaev area. It is quite popular among vacationers both domestically and among tourists from neighboring countries, especially Russia, Belarus and Moldova. This recreation center works according to the concept of "family pension". It enjoys well-deserved fame both among tourists with children and large companies. The boarding house operates seasonally, from early June to the first half of September.



Koblevo

This resort has been known since the days of the Soviet Union. But even now, the small village of Koblevo consists exclusively of hundreds of boarding houses and recreation centers, which stretch along the sea for six kilometers. There is virtually no private sector. Pension "Arcadia" (Koblevo) is located here very well - on the one hand, on the seashore, and on the other - in the middle of a very developed resort infrastructure. After all, there is a water park, a whole city of attractions, and various bars, clubs, restaurants, karaoke. In the evenings, discos and concerts are held. How to get to the boarding house, territory

You can independently reach the base both from Odessa and from Nikolaev. Buses to Koblevo depart from both cities. Moreover, this resort is located just halfway from Odessa to Nikolaev. You need to go out at the bus station. But it is located far from the area with pensions and recreation facilities. However, from there every half an hour a bus leaves, which carries tourists. He stops right next to the boarding house, on demand. The Arcadia complex (Koblevo) has a large green area. There is a lot of shade, benches, cozy corners for relaxation. On the territory there is a parking, and in the lobby of the main building there are shops and a large conference room. It is often rented by various organizations for conferences. Near the boarding house there is a market, pharmacy, call center, as well as an exchange office.



Rooms and board

Base "Arcadia" (Koblevo) consists of three buildings with different numbers of floors. Most of the rooms in the boarding house are two-room, designed for vacationers in an amount of 2 to 4 people. There are also very spacious rooms for guests. For example, three-room or two-level rooms. All rooms for vacationers have a TV, refrigerator, wardrobe with enough space and hangers. All rooms are air-conditioned, modern, comfortable. Showers with good plumbing, hot water is served around the clock. The rooms are tastefully decorated. "Arcadia" (Koblevo) provides food for tourists. There is a two-story air-conditioned cafe with several rooms. It is open from eight in the morning until eleven in the evening. The menu is excellent, a lot of meat, fish, five kinds of side dishes, undiluted juices, dairy products of local production. Tourists especially praise the local pastries - pancakes, rolls, bagels - as well as delicious brewed coffee. If you want to cook yourself, you are provided with excellent cuisine. In addition, there are many inexpensive restaurants nearby.



Beach facilities

"Arcadia" (Koblevo) provides vacationers with the whole range of services that modern tourists require. On the second floor of the cafe there is a free Wi-Fi. At your request, a multicooker, an electric kettle will be provided in the room. At the reception you can take a hairdryer and iron. The administrator has a safe for valuables. On the territory of the base there are other services and entertainment for vacationers of all ages. Library and billiard tables, the opportunity to play volleyball and tennis. There is a playground for children. And upon request, you will heat the sauna. You can come here with pets, but first you need to agree with the administration. The pension is located just a five-minute walk from the beach. From each of its buildings to the sea no more than one hundred meters. Umbrellas and beach mats can be rented at the base.

Reviews

Many came here on vacation. Koblevo (Arcadia, in particular), is associated by most tourists with a good level of service, friendly staff and good food. The rooms in the pension are spacious, comfortable, even luxurious. Lovely dining room. Helpful maids who cleaned the room, sometimes twice a day. Settled quickly and without problems. The service staff is very polite, unlike many other neighboring bases. Beautiful area with flowers and gazebos. The sea is very close.
