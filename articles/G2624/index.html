Section 3. Budget options: campsites, hostels, monasteries





You can often find out about the rating of the best hotels in Greece from those who are already fortunate enough to visit this state. Experienced travelers in most cases are happy to share information, their observations and thoughts.

However, not everyone knows that camping at this resort is also widespread. Such special venues are open from April to October. Of course, in this case we are not talking about the rating of hotels in Greece, but in fact it will be possible to save.

Accommodation in a camping house for one night will cost a tourist 5-7 euros, a tent costs even less. The location of such parking is always indicated on tourist maps.

Hostels in this country are not as common as campsites. Five youth hostels work in Athens during the year, two are open in Santorini and one each in Ios, Paros and Corfu. All hostels are members of the International Hostel Federation of Athens.

True, such institutions operate only in the spring-summer period. Usually the cost of an overnight stay is 7-9 euros (per person).

Some Greek monasteries also host travelers. This applies mainly to monasteries on the mainland, and on the islands they are closed for living. Here you should adhere to certain rules - observe the decency in clothing and daily routines. Cohabitation of men and women from among tourists in monasteries is prohibited.
