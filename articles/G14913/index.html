Waterpark (Kiev): prices

Dream Island will delight visitors with the following special rates:

  1. Ticket "Birthday". It costs 1 UAH. It operates throughout the day and gives a discount (15%) on the menu of bars and restaurants located in this territory.
  2. "Day of Neptune." Active only on Tuesdays and allows you to have fun in the water park from morning to evening.
  3. "Family". Will cost 200 UAH. one adult with a child not older than 12 years old. On a weekday at this rate you need to get to the water park in Kiev no later than 14 hours. A parent with two children will pay 300 UAH.
  4. "School". It costs 60 UAH. for two hours with a baby up to 12 years old from 10.00 to 16.00. Free passes one person, accompanying five children. The tariff is valid if there is a group of 10 students.
  5. There is a student rate. It is valid upon presentation of the necessary document and is purchased for 100 UAH. for three hours daily (excluding Tuesday and weekends).
