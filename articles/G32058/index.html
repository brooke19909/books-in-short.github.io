Halkidiki: hotels, holidays, reviews

Halkidiki is one of the most popular and ecologically clean corners of the Mediterranean. Tourists will find here everything necessary for a good rest: gentle, transparent and warm sea, cliffs overgrown with pine trees, peace and tranquility. Well, of course, if you get bored, you can always plunge into the world of fun entertainment, shopping and interesting trips around the peninsula. And if you have already decided which type of vacation you like, it's time to choose a hotel in Halkidiki that would be convenient for you. And it depends not only on your tour operator, but also on what you are going to this peninsula for. Now we will figure out where it is better to settle down so that the rest leaves only positive impressions.

Choosing a hotel for your holidays in Halkidiki

This peninsula consists of three resort areas. Athos, Kassandra and Sithonia - everywhere there are beautiful beaches, amazing landscapes, as well as accommodation for tourists. Halkidiki hotels can take guests to rest almost all year round.

Choosing a hotel, you can consider different options: budget and luxury, near the sea and at a distance from the beach, without food and ‚Äúall inclusive‚Äù, only for adults and for families. It all depends on your preference. But no matter what hotel you choose, the cleanest beach, the azure sea, the air filled with the scent of pine trees and excellent service will be provided to you.

The famous Sani chain is very good in this regard. It unites not just hotels, but chic five-star complexes operating on the ‚Äúultra all inclusive‚Äù system. Halkidiki hotels of this line are located in the territory of this reserve. Often in such hotels themed gastronomic evenings are organized, where the best chefs of the country compete.

Halkidiki: 4 star hotels

Such hotels are mainly located in the Kassandra area. It is here that the majority of tourists come to rest on the peninsula of Halkidiki (Greece). 4 star hotels can, of course, be found on Sithonia and Mount Athos. Among Russian-speaking travelers, Pallini Beach on Kassandra is most popular. The level of service and comfort in this hotel is not much inferior to five-star luxury complexes. It offers spacious rooms with a huge area (over 40 meters), bungalows right on the beach, a summer cinema, good windsurfing and water ski instructors.

Near the town of Uranopolis is a good "four" - "Aristotels Holiday Resort". Its tourists appreciate not only the opportunity to choose a duplex room, studio or apartment with a kitchen, but also because of its proximity to various Orthodox shrines. In addition, this hotel is famous for its spa, and the fact that it is the only hotel in Halkidiki with a water park. And if you want to get acquainted with the traditional culture of Greece, welcome to Hanioti Grandhotel, where they regularly organize evenings of national music and dance.

Are there any hotels in Halkidiki with their own beach?

The coast on the peninsula has a Blue Flag for its cleanliness. Therefore, the condition of most of the local beaches can be considered ideal. Many hotels have their own access to the sea. Their beaches are sandy or pebble. They are equipped with toilets, changing rooms, showers, special areas for children, sun loungers, umbrellas and sunbeds. The beaches are cleaned by special staff.

According to tourists, the best private coast at the hotels "Pallini Beach", "Grekotel Pella", "Annamaria Resort" on Kassandra, as well as "Antemus", "Blue Dolphin" and "Danai" in Sithonia. Vacationers also say that the hotels of the Sani and Porto Carras chain are also not inferior to the above.
