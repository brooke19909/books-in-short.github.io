Magnificent Rome hotels welcome guests from all over the world

Italy is a magnificent country, a visit to which cannot but be remembered. This state provides great opportunities for relaxation - skiing in the Italian Alps, visiting the fun Venetian carnival, tasting the most delicious traditional dishes and wines in the Italian provinces, walking through Milan's fashionable boutiques and swimming in the azure waters of the Adriatic Sea. But the most interesting way to spend a vacation in Italy is to visit the "eternal city", located on seven hills, the great Rome.

Purpose of the trip

In Rome, tourists will always find an exciting pastime. This city is full of historical and cultural monuments, evidence of the highest level of development of the most ancient civilization. Rome is one of the most beautiful cities in Europe. At first glance, it amazes its guests with the incredible contrast of its old streets and modern architecture. Not far from each other there are cozy cafes decorated in a historical style, and modern business centers, ancient ruins from the times of the Great Roman Empire and fashion boutiques, as well as numerous hotels in Rome. It is not surprising that many tourists who come to the capital of Italy once, certainly come back here, because, as the saying goes, "all roads lead to Rome."

This amazing city hosts many excursions: from the ancient Colosseum to the ruins of the Temple of Venus, from the heart of this ancient city at the ruined building of the Roman Forum to the Pantheon, the great temple of all the gods. The best way to get to know Rome is to take long and leisurely walks, which may include visits to some of the attractions - the Basilica of Emilia, the famous Thermae, Palatine Hill and the temple of one of the founders of the heart of Italy - Romulus.

Believe me, this trip will not be limited to weekends - the capital of the country is so large and diverse that it will take several weeks to get to know it.
