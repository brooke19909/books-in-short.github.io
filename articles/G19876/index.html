Sirius Deluxe Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Alanya): hotel description, services, reviews

The Turkish city of Alanya is one of the most famous resorts of the Mediterranean Sea. It is located on a picturesque cape at the foot of the Toros Mountains (altitude 250 m), 110 km from Antalya Airport. The resort is famous for its mild climate, magnificent sandy beaches, streamlined service and historical monuments that amaze the imagination. Tourists from all over the world strive here to admire the wonderful gardens that grow on the Taurus mountain range or the stalactite caves of the Devil, frightening their name. A significant part of all the attractions of Turkey is located in Alanya.

The resort infrastructure is developing rapidly: new bars, discos, and hotels are opening. This resort is very popular with young people, but, according to reviews, it is great for lovers of a relaxing family vacation.

As in any other tourist center, in Alanya there are a large number of hotels of all types.





Sirius Deluxe Hotel 5 * is one of the best institutions of its level. Tours to this hotel are a great choice for those who like to relax in comfort.

For those who are looking for an inexpensive and comfortable option for a vacation, the authors of numerous reviews recommend purchasing a ticket to the Sirius Deluxe Hotel 5 *, which can be an excellent choice to solve the problem of where to spend leisure time.
