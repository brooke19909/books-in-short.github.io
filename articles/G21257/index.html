Karolina Hotel 4 * (Bulgaria / Sunny Beach): reviews and photos of tourists

Travelers call the Karolina Hotel 4 * an oasis of calm and at the same time rich rest. This option is suitable for both family and romantic getaways.





Location

In the eastern part of the popular European resort is Karolina Hotel 4 *. Bulgaria, Sunny Beach, 8240 - the address of this resort. It is just a 6-minute walk from the well-maintained public beach. Burgas International Airport is 20 km away.

A lot of attractions can please travelers Sunny Beach. The following tourist sites are of the greatest interest:

  • sports stadium (100 m);
  • night club (500 m);

Accommodation

Sunny Beach is a popular destination for sea holidays. Guests can stay in rooms of the following categories:

  • Standard rooms - mean double occupancy on one large or two twin beds. It also has a desk, wardrobe and large mirror. There is a private furnished balcony. Most of the rooms are interconnected, which is a great option for families.
  • A studio is a combination of a bedroom and a living room within one living space. In addition to beds, the room has upholstered furniture, which is used not only for relaxation, but also for additional accommodation.
  • The apartment is a spacious two-room suite. The bedroom is equipped with beds, a soft sofa and a wardrobe. As for the living room, it is combined with the kitchen area. The balcony offers luxurious pool views.

The rooms in the Karolina Hotel 4 * are decorated in Baroque style. Nevertheless, the situation cannot be called pretentious. The interior is adapted in accordance with modern trends.

General impression

Hotel "Carolina" 4 * is a fairly new institution, which has already earned popularity among tourists.
