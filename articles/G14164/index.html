Gorky Park, Nha Trang: description, mode of operation, reviews

Many will probably think that this same Nha Trang is located somewhere in Russia or in the territory of the former Soviet Union, since it has Gorky Park in it. In fact, this is the name of the Vietnamese city. And Gorky Park itself, as the tourists who have been there, say, is a real outlet for the Russians. Being in a foreign country, you can literally feel at home. So, Gorky Park (Nha Trang) - a full description, colorful photographs and reviews of compatriots.





Location of Nha Trang

Vietnam is not a popular tourist destination for Russians. But, apparently, the situation has been gradually starting to change lately. Nha Trang is one of the popular resorts in Vietnam, which has earned the love of guests of the country for two reasons: a good location and focused work on the development of a settlement in the tourism sector.

Nha Trang is located on the central coast of Vietnam. Its main street (Changfu) is a six-kilometer boulevard that stretches along the sea. Nha Trang is located 450 km north of Ho Chi Minh City and 1200 km south of Hanoi.

Where to look for Central Park in Nha Trang?

Gorky Park (and the Central Park of Nha Trang, as the complex is also called) is located on that very main street - Changfu Boulevard. So, when a person comes to this city, one way or another, he sees an interesting building in the form of a lotus, painted in pink. From here, facing the sea, you need to go to the right side. You can also ask the Vietnamese for directions or take a taxi. By the way, you can get on a cycle rickshaw - locals are happy to help tourists find Central Park.
