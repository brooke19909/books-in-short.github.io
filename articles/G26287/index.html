Simple and very beautiful paper planes

More and more people are resorting to needlework. Today you can do anything with your own hands, and it will look better than in a store window. Origami is popular - this is an unusually interesting work, which also develops a person. One of the easiest crafts are paper planes. Probably, everyone in his life made paper crafts. In children, airplanes and ships are considered the most favorite toys, and to make them with your own hands will be for the child only in joy.



Paper airplanes are made in five stages. For this you need a sheet and a pencil. It is best to do the craft together with the children, which will contribute to their development, and on the part of the parents - the manifestation of love and attention. Cardboard is sometimes used to make an amazing airplane. Models from such material are a little more difficult to do, but this way it will remain for a longer period. Paper airplanes can be decorated with paints or just use colored paper. You can make such a souvenir using the origami technique, or you can use more complex tricks, which will give a completely unique and beautiful result.



Making an airplane out of paper is actually a snap. To start, so that he can fly, you need to work with the bow of the craft, namely to strengthen it. We take a sheet of paper and fold it in half. We bend the corners on one side to the middle (to make a nose, the plane will look like an open envelope). Next, draw a triangle with a pencil, and bend all the excess paper in the opposite direction. After this, a sharp triangle should turn out, it is the sharp part that needs to be bent a little for the soft flight of the figure. Paper airplanes turn out to be uneven and may have errors during flight if the corners, wings are incorrectly bent or you overdo it, for example, bending the right wing more than the left. Everything should be symmetrical. At the last stage, you need to fold the resulting plane in half and spread your wings. That's actually what origami planes are made of.



Having studied the basic algorithm, you can then try to make the craft more complicated. Indeed, in fact, there are a lot of types of airplanes, and they differ only in the number of folds and various paper operations. There are various models, colors and techniques for making crafts. They can turn out with patterns or in a strip, with a blunt nose or triangular. And you can also, having originally folded your wings, get a picture or composition on the "roof" of the airplane.

Fortunately, now it is full of a variety of videos, instructions, books in which you can find anything you like, and with pleasure learn yourself and show how it is done to your child. From a sheet of paper you can create a real work of art. If you want to learn how to make origami (and this is actually very beautiful), then it is better to purchase special collections in which each step is described in detail, and even with pictures. Starting with an airplane, you can come to a wonderful vase of paper flowers. And for the child it will be interesting to make animals, flowers and other figures.
