What is zoom?

Zoom is the ability of the lens to change its own focal length in order to adjust the scale of the photographed object. The presented property of the lens allows you to zoom in or, on the contrary, to zoom out the image without moving the camera in any way. To resize the object in the photo inside the lens, a kind of lens shift is performed. The presented permutation is responsible for zooming.

Varieties of zoom

What is optical zoom? The concept implies a rearrangement of lenses in the lens. In contrast to optical zoom, there is still digital. What does digital zoom mean? This is a change in the scale of the scene, but without any involvement of optics. We can say that this is a simple image frame and its increase digitally.

Multiplicity and zoom are one and the same?

In budget amateur photographic equipment, zoom is a multiplicity. For example, a fourfold zoom indicates that the camera can change its own focal length exactly four times. In professional equipment, the magnification is not indicated, but only the minimum and maximum marks of the focal length are noted. Nowadays, amateur cameras often offer the user a larger zoom than in comparison with professional photographic equipment.

What are the pros and cons of optical zoom?

Optical zoom is vital for people who are going to take beautiful, clear photographs, take photos at long distances, and possibly print their own pictures in large format. It is worth noting that the operation of the optical zoom of the lens is an indistinguishable operation of the optical zoom of the camera. What does this mean? The zoom zooms in several times, and the exposure of the frame and its focal length are modified, with each click of the zoom control button. Now there are good cameras with twentyfold zoom, allowing without loss of quality to photograph the paws of a fly or something else.

Benefits of Optical Zoom:

  1. Excellent sharpness, good color reproduction even with a large increase in the frame, small details are always visible, there is no blur at all.
  2. The resolution of the photos does not change when approaching, and this is extremely important for the subsequent printing of pictures in high quality.
  3. All photos are obtained as 300 dpi. This is enough to create an excellent calendar, a huge banner-banner or even a poster. The quality of the photo does not change at the same time.

Negative points:

  1. Techniques that have good optical zoom are expensive. Many manufacturers position cameras such as professional equipment, and this suggests that it is problematic to purchase such a camera in a regular store.
  2. Optical zoom does not turn on separately from digital, and this complicates the work with equipment.
