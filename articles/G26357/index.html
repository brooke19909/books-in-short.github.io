Park Hotel "Retur", St. Petersburg: description and reviews

Nowadays, people travel a lot. And those who go to another city or abroad on a business trip or for the purpose of relaxation, need to stop somewhere. The most modern option for tourists is a hotel. Nowadays, there are many types of hotels. And one of them is a park hotel. This is the kind of hotel where you can relax. The park hotel is characterized by a well-equipped area, it is usually located next to the forest, on the coast of a natural or artificial reservoir, in a beautiful area, next to gazebos and flowers.

We will talk about one of the hotels of this type in this article.

Park Hotel in St. Petersburg: location, characteristics

The hotel is located in the construction of the 18th century, on the coast of the Gulf of Finland. The building has high ceilings and large rooms. The facility was equipped as a hotel in 2000 and made it convenient for vacationers to live.

Park Hotel is located at: Resort District of St. Petersburg, Sestroretsk, 28 Bolshaya Kupalnaya Street.

Who is the hotel suitable for?

Due to the democratic and calm atmosphere and the variety of recreational activities, the St. Petersburg Park Hotel "Retur" is suitable for both leisure and leisure time. Here, youth companies, couples, and families with children rest. In the hotel you can escape from the city noise and routine everyday life and spend time in a relaxed atmosphere, enjoying communication with family and friends. The hotel rooms are decorated with fresh flowers, from the windows of the rooms you can see a beautiful panorama of the Gulf of Finland.

In addition, the park hotel has several halls for celebrations, which is why holidays are often held here among colleagues, relatives and friends. Those who come to organize business meetings, trainings and seminars can rent a specially equipped conference room.

If you can‚Äôt imagine your life without your pet, the hotel staff has good news for you. You can take your pet with you (for an additional fee from 200 to 500 rubles, depending on its size). If you need to leave for a while, the hotel staff will look after and take care of the animal.

Accommodation in rooms

Park Hotel "Retur" has 32 rooms of average comfort. All rooms have TV, bath, toilet, shower, sink. The rooms are cleaned, bed linen is regularly changed.

As for the categories of rooms in the hotel, they are all divided into three types:

  1. Standard rooms (there are 24 of them in the hotel). These are rooms with a classic interior, each of which has a double bed with a bedside table, a TV, a wardrobe and a shower.
  2. Junior Suite (only one room of this category) - a room decorated according to an individual design model, with a double bed, TV, wardrobe, bath and shower. From the window opens a picturesque picture of the coast.
  3. Suite (there are four rooms of this type in the hotel) - a room with a chic bed and a wardrobe, as well as a hot tub, has an elegant design, great for honeymooners.

The rooms of the Retur Park Hotel cost from three to nineteen thousand rubles per day. Price includes buffet breakfast.

Country houses in a hotel complex





If you wish, you can stay not in the room, but in the cottage. There are two types of country houses in the hotel:

  1. Standard cottage (a country house with two bedrooms for a quiet or active pastime).
  2. Luxury cottage (a room with a large hall and kitchen, as well as a bedroom, suitable for families with children or for companies). This country house features a wide double bed, sofa and TV.

The cottages can accommodate families and groups of up to four people. The cost of living is from seven to eleven thousand rubles a day.

Nutrition

Retur Park Hotel customers in St. Petersburg can use the services of a restaurant that is open 24 hours a day and is designed for seventy people. In the institution you can try European and Oriental dishes, original delicacies prepared by the cook, as well as national Russian food. In summer weather, you can eat on the veranda, enjoying the sound of the fountain and beautiful flowers and greenery. In addition, the hotel has two bars where you will be offered hot and cold drinks, alcohol.



Breakfast at the hotel is included in the price and usually leaves a good impression on visitors. The choice of buffet dishes is varied. There is almost everything for every taste: sausages, fried eggs, salad, omelet, oatmeal, sandwiches with cheese and sausage.

Hotel Services

Park Hotel "Retur" offers various types of recreation: active and relaxing, both for the body and for the soul. The hotel has two pools (outdoor and indoor), a sauna, baths (Finnish and Russian), massage treatments. For fans of board games, a special room is equipped, and sports enthusiasts can rent equipment, visit the gym, and go jogging. You can also go horse riding or improve your health (use physiotherapy, acupuncture, therapeutic baths or hydromassage). Near the hotel there is a beach where you are invited to sunbathe in armchairs. The hotel also has a library and wireless internet. For children there is a playground where they can play, as well as a games room indoors and a petting zoo. So the hotel offers recreational opportunities for people of all ages and preferences.

Collective leisure

If you spend time in a big company, Park Hotel Retour will do everything to make your weekend interesting and memorable. Near the hotel there is a specially equipped area for a picnic, volleyball and tennis. You can go on an excursion or go sailing. In winter you can rent sleds, skis or other sports equipment. You can also ride horses, fry barbecue on the grill. The Park Hotel is great for celebrations. For this, two specially equipped rooms are located on the territory of the hotel.

Solemn events in the park hotel

At the hotel you can organize and hold any festive event (graduation or corporate evening, birthday, wedding celebration, wedding anniversary, and so on).

To this end, the Retur Park Hotel provides two rooms:

  1. Banquet Hall Chalet is a room with a classic interior. The room has many flowers and candles, light furniture, a crystal chandelier that complements the luxurious festive atmosphere. There is a dance floor, as well as a veranda with beautiful views of the coast. The room is designed for sixty people.
  2. The summer panoramic tent is designed for one hundred and sixty customers, it provides for the celebration of outdoors. The interior is designed according to the requirements of the organizers of the celebration and in accordance with its theme. Music and lighting will complement the festive atmosphere and make the holiday truly fabulous.

For those who celebrate a birthday at the hotel, gifts are provided (unlimited use of a hookah, accommodation in a cottage for a day). The birthday man can bring his own alcoholic drinks for the holiday. Dishes for the celebration can be selected from the restaurant menu. As a bonus, a birthday person is given a walk or photo shoot with a horse.

Hotel Opinions

Park Hotel Retour receives different reviews: both positive and negative. Among the advantages of the organization are the courtesy and friendly attitude of the staff, the beautiful surroundings, the proximity of the hotel to the beach and the bay, wonderful views, delicious food, the ability to easily get to the building and go home.

Many were satisfied with the way they celebrated and how they had a rest with friends and relatives. Despite the fact that the Retur Park Hotel has all these advantages, reviews about it are also negative. Some guests did not like the interior and furnishings of the rooms (in their opinion, the rooms were not clean enough, the linens were poorly washed). Some of the customers are dissatisfied with the quality of the zoo and game room. Also, the buffet service is not very satisfying: there is often not enough food, few hot drinks, and you can‚Äôt have breakfast and coffee in the restaurant, as it opens only during the day.
