The most popular hotels in Tver

The Russian ancient city of Tver is located on both sides of the Volga. There are a huge number of different architectural monuments that play a significant role in the development of national history. The richest collections of museums, as well as the cultural life of the city attract many foreign and Russian tourists.





Tver is one of the largest scientific, cultural and industrial centers of our country. In economics, it is famous for the chemical industry, mechanical engineering, printing and textile industry. In addition, it is a "green" city: groves, parks, boulevards, squares, green spaces along the streets, there are botanical gardens.

In this article, we will consider the most popular hotels in Tver so that it will be easier for you to make a choice when planning a trip to this beautiful city.
