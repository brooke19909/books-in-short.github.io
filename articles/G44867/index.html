Hotel Luna 4 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands): photos and reviews of tourists

Without a doubt, Bulgaria is a popular holiday destination. On the western coast of the Black Sea there are many hotels that offer travelers a vacation at affordable prices. There are hotels for family, youth, and recreation. Tourists often choose the Luna 4 * hotel (Golden Sands, Bulgaria) for their vacation. But is it worth staying at this hotel?

Holidays in Golden Sands: to whom and when should you go here on vacation?

Golden Sands is the most popular and densely populated resort in Bulgaria. Tourism began to develop here recently, so the hotels built have not yet had time to grow very old. For travelers are offered a variety of bars and restaurants specializing in Bulgarian cuisine, sports grounds, balneological centers and mud clinics. The resort is located on a hill, so for families with children it is better to choose hotels located on the first beach line. For example, the tourist complex Luna 4 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands).





The holiday season begins here in mid-June, when the air temperature warms up to 25 degrees. The best time to relax is July. This month, the weather is hot here, and the sea warms up the most. It is worth considering that the heat near the sea is easier to carry than in the city, because the coast is blown by light breezes. It is worth visiting Golden Sands in early September, when most tourists left the resort. Warm weather lasts until early October.

Hotel location and nearby attractions

Hotel Luna (Bulgaria) is located 500 meters from the central part of the famous Golden Sands resort. The road to shops, souvenir shops, restaurants and cafes from here takes no more than 10-15 minutes. The complex is located on the first beach line. The public beach is less than 50 meters away. To get to it, tourists need to walk along the promenade that separates the hotel from the sea.





You can get to the complex from the airport of Varna, which is 25 km from Golden Sands. An average taxi ride takes less than 20 minutes. Also in the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are many attractions that are worth visiting, once in this part of Bulgaria. The most interesting are:

  • mooring for yachts (600 meters);
  • golf course (900 meters);
  • Party Street (1 km);
  • Amusement park "Aquapolis" (2 km);
  • Aladzha Monastery (3.5 km);
  • Baltata Nature Reserve (7 km);
  • water park of Albena (8 km).

Housing stock

In the building of the Luna 4 * hotel (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) there are 265 rooms. Each one has a private balcony with a dining table and several chairs. The rooms are a quality repair. The walls are painted in light pastel shades, and the carpet is on the floor. More than half of the rooms have windows facing the sea. Special non-smoking apartments are available. Rooms are cleaned every day, and linen and towels are changed every 2 days.

The following room types are offered for hotel guests:

  • Standard room with park or sea view. Its area is 22 square meters. m. The room has two separate beds and an additional folding chair. Maximum occupancy is 3 adults.
  • 29 sqm Family Room m. One-room apartment in which there is one double bed and a sofa bed. At the same time 4 people can live. Rooms overlook the sea or the park.
  • Apartments. Their area is 45 square meters. m. These are spacious rooms with a separate bedroom and living room. 3 adults and 1 child can live here.

Room features

The well-being of apartments is an important factor influencing leisure. After all, it is here that tourists return in the evening to relax after a busy and hard day. For a quality stay, each room of the Luna 4 * hotel (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) is equipped with a whole range of amenities that make rest here comfortable and cozy.





All rooms have the following equipment:

  • A modern flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Available viewing Russian-language channels. The size of the TV may vary significantly depending on the cost of the room.
  • Individual air conditioning. Tourists can adjust the temperature in the room using a special remote control.
  • Telephone for making international and local calls, as well as for communication with the administration. On a call, for an additional fee, tourists can order delivery of newspapers, drinks and food directly to the room.
  • Paid internet.
  • Minibar, which can be delivered to your room upon request. Its content is not included in the price and is paid separately.
  • Bathroom with shower, washbasin, makeup mirror and hairdryer. The hotel provides all the necessary toiletries and towels.

Children's rest in the Luna hotel

"Luna" is a hotel (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) that takes care of even the smallest guests. The complex offers free accommodation for children under 12 years old who will sleep without occupying a separate bed. You can book an extra bed for a child or a baby cradle at the time of booking. There are also restaurant chairs for guests, and a stroller can be rented for a fee.





But entertainment for children is not so much. The indoor pool has a shallow heated compartment for them. Children's animation works. The hotel does not have a playground and a mini club, so parents themselves will have to take care of organizing leisure activities for the child.
