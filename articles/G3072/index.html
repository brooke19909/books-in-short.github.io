Service management: how to disable "SMS-dialogue" on "Beeline"?

Favorable communication via SMS is available to all Beeline customers. The operator provides a number of services that allow a decent save. What service does Beeline offer us? "SMS-dialogue" is a rather demanded service of the operator. The target group of users are people who often communicate with specific subscribers by sending text messages.





This service is provided for a fee and can be managed by the client. About how you can connect, how to disable the "SMS-dialogue" on "Beeline", as well as the cost of this service will be described below.

Service Description

Due to the variety of options and additional services that the black-and-yellow operator offers, not all network customers know what this service is ("Beeline"). "SMS dialogue" - what is it? Under this name is hidden nothing more than preferential communication via SMS with one specific subscriber. You can choose an interlocutor from among Beeline customers. Features of use

The Beeline service, which is discussed in the current article, has a number of features:

  • Within one day, fifty text messages are provided for each of the participants in the conversation, which they can send.

  • The monthly fee is written off both from the number of the "owner" and from the SIM card of the person he called (the "participant").
