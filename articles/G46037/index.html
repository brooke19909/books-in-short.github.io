Short description of the Tobol Hotel in Tobolsk

If you are looking for a good place to stay in Tobolsk, we recommend that you pay attention to the hotel complex, which we will describe later in our article. We will introduce you to this object in more detail, present a list and the cost of the services provided. In addition, this review gives an objective assessment of the Tobol Hotel based on feedback from its customers.

Location

The hotel and entertainment complex "Tobol" is located in the central part of the city. A seven-minute walk from it is a church and the main attraction of this place is the Kremlin. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel are the shops "Red and White" and "Magnet".

The exact address of the complex: Tyumen region, the city of Tobolsk, Oktyabrskaya street, 20. Opening hours: around the clock.

Description

"New Tobol" is a comfortable and modern hotel with a huge selection of services. Here guests will find a high level of service, new rooms, a cozy and relaxed atmosphere. The interior of the hotel is made in a classic elegant style. The whole area is clean and beautiful.

Residence

The number of rooms of the Tobol Hotel (Tobolsk) is presented in various categories. In total, guests are offered 38 comfortable, furnished rooms, each of which has a TV, air conditioning, a balcony, a shower, toiletries.

We give the cost of rooms per night with breakfast:

  • standard economy - 2500 rubles;
  • double comfortable room - 3 000 rubles;
  • Junior Suite - 6 thousand rubles;
  • two-room suite equipped with the "smart home" system and European furniture - 12 thousand;
  • three-room luxury suite - 18 000 rubles.

The hotel accepts cash and bank transfer, payment by cards.

For business people

Each year, the described Tobol Hotel hosts various large companies and holds business meetings and seminars on its territory. The hotel's conference room meets all modern standards and is equipped with the necessary equipment. Clients in their reviews have repeatedly noted the individual approach of the staff in servicing and the high level of service.

Reviews for the Tobol Hotel in Tobolsk

Judging by the guests, the complex is a very good place for relaxation and accommodation. The main feature is its unique location in the historic city center, comfortable and clean rooms equipped with modern and new furniture, as well as friendly and polite staff.

In addition, during your stay at the hotel there is a great opportunity to have fun and have a good time. Hotel guests speak positively about the work of its restaurant and entertainment venues.

As for breakfast, the reviews are also good. Meals are organized on the basis of the "buffet". The dishes are standard: porridge, omelet, cottage cheese, sausages, vegetables. Everything is satisfying and tasty.

Of the minuses, customers note uncomfortable bathrooms, poor pressure and prolonged heating of water. Some noticed the smell of sewage in the rooms.

In general, the Tobol Hotel in Tobolsk is a pretty good option for a reasonable price. Here the price corresponds to the quality.
