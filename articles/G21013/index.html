Crafts from waste material for kindergarten. Tips for Carer, Mom and Dad

In all children's institutions, one of the most important topics of classes is the topic of respect for the environment. The younger generation is taught not only to protect nature, but also to rationally use its gifts. One of the directions in such classes is the topic of reusing various things. Kids learn from junk material to make a variety of crafts. Often parents are attracted to this process, offering to participate in exhibitions and competitions of such products. And then mothers and fathers begin to think about what is so original and beautiful to do. To help you, we present the article "Crafts from waste material for kindergarten", which describes wonderful ideas of such products. It will take quite a bit of time to complete them. Your children will be quite capable of making them with their own hands, but under your guidance. Since scissors and glue are used to create them.



Egg container

The carton that sells the eggs can turn into wonderful products. The simplest thing you can create from it is a caterpillar or centipede. How to make a craft out of junk material of this kind? Follow the instructions provided, and you will succeed.

  1. Cut one row of cells completely from the container. As a rule, there are five of them. Pierce each cell with a needle or awl on both sides. The paws of the insect will be attached to these holes.
  2. Cut the fluffy decorative wire into pieces (5 pcs.) Of 10-15 centimeters and pull it through the holes. Bend each foot slightly down to make the product more stable. You can use ordinary wire by wrapping it first with yarn or tape.
  3. Paint the whole product with gouache or watercolors.
  4. Glue the eyes with glue. It can be cardboard circles, buttons, sequins.
  5. Perform horns on the wire head.

In the same way, you can make crafts from waste material for kindergarten (from cardboard packaging for eggs) in the form of spiders. Only here, only one cell from the container is used and three pairs of paws are inserted into it.

Juice container



Original handicrafts from waste material, and most importantly, very useful, can be obtained from empty cardboard packaging for drinks. We suggest that you carry out bird feeders with your child .

On the box, draw windows from two or three sides, not reaching the bottom of 5-6 centimeters. Leave one wall closed so that the wind does not blow draft food for the birds. Cut out all the holes. Attach a loop to the top of the box. All that remains is to hang the feeder on a tree, sprinkle grain and wait for guests to sparrows and tits.

Plastic bottle



Thinking about the option of doing crafts from waste material for kindergarten, think about a PET container. Very easily and quickly, together with the child, you can make a pencil stand out of the bottle. Cut off the top of the bottle and then use only the bottom. Wrap it with colored paper, secure it with tape. Seal the sharp top with colored tape or tape. Decorate the pencil box with applique, stickers or decorative ornaments (bows, sequins, lace).

For storage of daughter's hairpins and rubber bands from two bottles, you can make a casket. To do this, cut off the lower parts of them by 10 centimeters. Connect them together, sewn on one and the second half of the zipper. Before doing this, pierce the holes in the plastic blank with a hot awl. The product closes well and it is very convenient to store small accessories in it.

Crafts from waste material for kindergarten can be made from other things: disposable tableware, napkins, various packaging. Look at those items that you are prepared to send in the trash bag. Show your imagination, maybe you can make an interesting little thing out of them? Inspiration to you!
