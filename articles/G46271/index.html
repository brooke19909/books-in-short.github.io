"Tropical island"

How to make a cake from sweets? If you are giving a present to a child or a creative adult, then you can give your creation the shape of a tropical island. Making such a cake will be easy. You need to make a box out of cardboard. If you do not want to do this, you can take a ready-made foundation. Now we glue the box on the outside with "grass" made of corrugated paper. Fill the base with sweets. In the corners we fall asleep sweets in golden candy wrappers, and in the middle we lay out a wavy line of sweets in a blue wrapper. Now you need to make a palm tree. For its manufacture we will use sweets in gold candy wrappers. We spread the sweets around and wrap the row with tape. We lay out the second tier in a checkerboard pattern relative to the first. Again we fix the result with tape. We continue to repeat these steps until the palm tree rises at least 20 cm. Now you have to make the leaves. You can use live leaves or make them out of colored paper. As coconuts on a palm tree, you can hang candies in a gold wrapper.

Barbie Cake

Want to make a gift for a girl? Above you can see a photo of a candy cake. With your own hands, such a product will be very simple to make. Better stick the sweets in waves or semicircles.

Cake in kindergarten

Is your child's birthday soon? Then it's time to think about making a cake from juice and sweets. Such a treat will look beautiful on the festive table, and most importantly, unlike a regular cake, you will not need to think about how to divide yummy into pieces. Make three round boxes of cardboard and fill them with polystyrene or newspapers. Fix the blanks on top of each other in ascending order. Now you will need to glue the ends of the boxes with corrugated paper. To make the cake look festive, you can make each tier a different color. Glue juice boxes to the end of the first row. Decorate the second tier with cookies or pastries in the package. But on the third row, stick candy, candy bars or small chocolates. In the upper box, you can pour lollipops, or any other caramel. Each tier can be bandaged with colorful ribbons.
