Hotel Vile Oliva 4 * (Petrovac, Montenegro): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Montenegro is a country that tourists love for its warm climate, the stunning architecture of coastal cities and the relatively low cost of vacation. People come here for active excursion vacations, and just to spend time at the sea, to sunbathe and gain strength. There are many tourist complexes on the Adriatic coast, but among them the hotel Vile Oliva 4 * (Montenegro, Petrovac) is especially popular. What attracts travelers to it?

Hotel location

Petrovac is famous for its calm atmosphere, ideal for families. The small beach is surrounded by numerous olive and pine groves. It is here that the Vile Oliva 4 * hotel (Petrovac) is located. It is located just 50 meters from the sea, which will take you less than two minutes to go. The complex and the city beach are separated by a small road. Tourists love to walk on it in the evening. A bus stop is 100 meters away. The central part of Petrovac is 150 meters away. There, travelers will find many Mediterranean restaurants, bars and shops.

The nearest airport is Tivat Airport, 35 km from the property. The road to it will take about half an hour. 17 km from Petrovac is the city of Budva, which is considered the main tourist center of the country. Be sure to go there for history buffs. Budva was founded almost 2500 years ago. Often it is called the oldest settlement on the Adriatic coast.

Basic information and cost of living

The modern hotel Vile Oliva Hotel 4 * (Montenegro, Petrovac) is a complex of small cottages located on the territory of a cozy olive grove. It opened its doors to tourists in 2008. The last renovation of the rooms was completed in 2012. The main building is a snow-white two-story building where the reception is located. But most tourists like the villas - three-story cottages scattered throughout the hotel.





As in many other hotels, check-in here begins at 14:00 and lasts until midnight. The cost of living in a standard double room is approximately 4,500 rubles per night. The most expensive apartments will cost tourists 10,000 rubles, but they are also designed to populate a larger number of people. In total, the hotel offers travelers 180 comfortable rooms. Allowed children with any age. You can pay for services with Visa and MasterCard credit cards. The hotel has a Russian-speaking staff. Leave the rooms after the rest is necessary from 6:00 to noon.

Hotel Residential Fund

Hotel Vile Oliva Petrovac 4 * (Montenegro) can provide comfortable and affordable accommodation. The rooms have a private bathroom and a small balcony. The design of the apartments is classic. It is dominated by black and white colors. Some rooms have a side sea view, but most of them overlook a park or mountains. In addition to beds, each room has wardrobes, bedside tables, a desk and a chair. The bathroom is equipped with a shower, washbasin and a large mirror, which is suitable for shaving or applying makeup.





For its guests, the hotel has prepared the following types of rooms:

  • Standard (20 sq. M.). Designed to accommodate two adults and a child. It has no sea view.
  • Superior (24 sq. M.). Equipped with a french bed. It is possible to place an extra bed. Accommodation of a family of three is supposed. There are several rooms with sea view.
  • Superior apartment (48 sq. M.). They consist of a separate bedroom, living room and bathroom. There are two balconies, each of which faces the sea. Designed to populate three adults and one child.
  • Two-storey apartments (35 sq. M.). On the ground floor there is a living room with a sofa bed and a bathroom, and on the second floor there is a bedroom and a balcony.

Room Amenities

For a comfortable stay, each room of the Vile Oliva 4 * hotel (Petrovac, Montenegro) was equipped with all necessary equipment. Guests can evaluate the quality of service using the following amenities:

  • Air conditioning that cools rooms on hot days.
  • TV is a modern model. A lot of satellite channels, including Russian, are connected.
  • Bath amenities, towels, bathrobes and a hairdryer.
  • Telephone for round-the-clock communication with the hotel. And also on it you can order room service, as well as the delivery of drinks and food. For a fee, international calls are made.
  • A safe for storing valuables and documents.
  • Minibar for drinks. Its filling is paid separately.
  • The apartments are additionally equipped with a kitchenette. There is a refrigerator, microwave, stove for cooking. An electric kettle and kitchenware are also provided.
  • Daily maid service. Towels and bed linen are changed several times a week.

Service and Facilities

A good rest is not only equipped rooms, but also competent work of infrastructure facilities. The Vile Oliva complex (Petrovac, Montenegro) offers tourists a developed system of services. His guests can take advantage of the following:

  • Luggage storage located at the reception. In it, tourists can leave large bags so as not to occupy a place in the room.
  • Reception that works around the clock. Here you can ask a question to the hotel staff or solve a problem. An express check-in or check-out service is also available for an extra amount.
  • Conference room for 120 people. It is possible to use fax or photocopy.
  • Car parking and garages. Paid car rental is possible. This is especially convenient if you intend to travel around Montenegro yourself.
  • Own shop on site. You can buy newspapers and magazines, drinks and snacks, fruits and souvenirs.
  • Facilities for travelers with disabilities. All stairs have ramps, and the hotel buildings have spacious elevators.
  • Laundry that provides paid cleaning, drying and ironing services.
  • Internet in the lobby of the main building. His connection is paid.
  • Currency exchange point.




All about nutrition in the complex

The hotel Vile Oliva 4 * (Petrovac, Montenegro) offers two types of food systems for guests: half board (breakfast and lunch) and all-inclusive. The last option includes basic meals, afternoon tea and an unlimited number of local drinks. A main restaurant is open daily for hotel guests, serving Balkan and Mediterranean dishes. A shared buffet is available for visitors. There is also an outdoor terrace where you can dine surrounded by ancient olive trees. Drinks are available at the beach bar. It serves refreshing cocktails, including alcoholic drinks, ice cream, light snacks and desserts. A similar bar is located on the poolside terrace. Free drinks are only available until 23:00.

Should I come here with children?

The atmosphere of the resort town is perfect for families with children. Hotel Vile Oliva 4 * (Petrovac, Montenegro) is no exception. A cradle is provided for a child in the room upon request, and a good discount is provided for his stay. The main restaurant is equipped with high chairs for easy feeding.
There is a children's pool for kids. It is shallow, and its area is only 9 square meters. m. For children from two to eight years, a mini-club. Children can also play in the outdoor playground.





Guest positive opinions

Tourists often read the opinions of other guests in order to understand whether the complex is suitable for recreation or not. Hotel Vile Oliva 4 * (Montenegro, Petrovac) receives mostly good reviews . The guests appreciated the following advantages:

  • Good hotel location. Close to the beach and promenade, which is nice to walk in the evenings. Here, performances are held every day, artists work.
  • Large area on which olive trees grow. The hotel prides itself on cooking in the restaurant with the addition of oil made from olives grown in the garden.
  • The rooms are gradually being restored. Only two buildings with an old repair remained in the complex.
  • Very tasty food, although not too varied.
  • Mostly pensioners rest here, so the hotel is quite quiet and calm.
  • Friendly staff who made a small gift goodbye. The employees are responsive and always smiling.
  • Nice pool with warm water that doesn't smell like bleach.




Hotel criticism

This is not to say that the Vile Oliva Petrovac 4 * hotel receives only positive reviews. There are enough negative opinions. Usually guests of the complex criticize him for the following disadvantages:

  • The rooms have a very low level of lighting. Even if you turn on all the lamps, the rooms will be dark.
  • The tap water runs a little warm, not hot.
  • The safe in the room did not work. At the reception they refused to solve this problem.
  • Room cleaning is not always high quality. The dust is poorly cleaned, not every day they take out the garbage.
  • Lifts often break.
  • Poor soundproofing in the rooms. You can hear neighbors and passing cars, and people relaxing by the pool.
  • There are not enough tables in the restaurant for dinner. You have to stand for quite a while and wait until the place is free to eat.

Draw conclusions

Having learned the basic information about the hotel Vile Oliva (Petrovac) 4 *, reviews, prices for accommodation, we can conclude that it is suitable for families with small children, the elderly, newlyweds and just couples in love. Quiet surroundings contribute to a relaxing holiday. Accommodation here is not cheap, but the price is consistent with the quality of the service provided. It is worthwhile to clarify that for youth companies, relaxation here may seem boring. They better find a hotel in Budva, which is designed for outdoor activities, and not in Petrovac.
