How to turn on the flashlight on the iPhone 5S: tips and tricks

Today we have to figure out how to turn on the flashlight on the iPhone 5S. Is there this option on the studied smartphone? Where is she located? Answers to all these questions will be presented later. In fact, there is a flashlight on almost every modern mobile phone. But is there an option for "apple" devices?

Description

In fact, there is a flashlight on iPhones. It is located on the rear panel, near the camera lens. The flashlight works like a flash. But he can also perform the lighting function. The main thing is to know how to turn on the flashlight on the iPhone 5S.

Quick turn on

Now let's talk about how to activate this option. How to quickly turn on the flashlight on the "iPhone 5S"? The first option is to use the Control Center. With it, you can turn on and off many options.

To activate the flashlight, you must:

  • Turn on the mobile phone.
  • Make a wipe at the bottom of the screen.
  • Find the flashlight icon and click on it. It is located on the left side of the appeared box.




That's all. After that, the flash will light up and will function as a regular flashlight. But that is not all. How to turn on the flashlight on the "iPhone 5S" when making a call?
