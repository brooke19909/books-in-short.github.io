Megaminx: how to collect an unusual cube?

The inexplicable world of the Rubik's Cube draws a large number of people. Increasingly, new types of this entertaining puzzle appear, for example, Megaminx. How to assemble it to enjoy the process?



What is Megaminx?

The dodecahedron puzzle was invented based on the classic Rubik's Cube. Collecting it is somewhat more difficult.

What is interesting about Megaminx? How to assemble it, you can understand after analyzing the algorithm. A feature is that the faces can be rotated clockwise and counterclockwise 180 degrees.

A fairly complex assembly process provides a six-color version of the puzzle that is not immediately understood.

Secrets of building a cube

How to collect "Megaminx"? For beginners, there are manuals and instructions that make this task easier. The puzzle is assembled according to the algorithms created for the Rubik's Cube, but the process is different in that Megaminx has more moving elements.

The puzzle helps to develop intelligence and mindfulness. The toy will be interesting to all ages. By training the assembly skill, you will improve the speed of thinking. The puzzle is believed to help maintain strength of mind and clarity of mind for years to come.



Professionals know how to assemble a Rubik's Cube. Megaminx has already won the attention of a large number of fans who organize competitions. They are happy to share their secrets and stories related to these unusual inventions.

Interestingly, the record build time is only 33.17 seconds! Every fan of such toys can try to assemble them at speed.

Types of cubes "Megaminx"

Even puzzles require new and more complex developments. Fans of Megaminx note several varieties that differ in complexity and design:

  • Gigaminx;
  • Teraminx;
  • Petaminx
  • Yotaminx.

They differ from each other in the number of layers. The more of them, the harder the puzzle to assemble.

Benefits

Puzzle "Megaminx" has become interesting to everyone. The toy is made of high-quality plastic, the average weight of about 150 g.

Both children and adults like it. Their benefits are immediately visible. The puzzle develops logic, thinking and fine motor skills. Great for restless kids.



Speedcubing

Young people came up with a movement that brought together fans of the Rubik dice assembly for speed. Championships are held where everyone can demonstrate their skills. The movement rallied puzzle lovers of different ages.

The assortment of types of Rubik's Cube is quite rich. Improvements are made by fans of Megaminx. How to assemble a quick and easy puzzle, they tell with great pleasure. This allows you to make the championships more interesting and attract more people. The surge in the popularity of original puzzles occurred in 1996, and has not subsided to this day.

Assembly tips

Experts advise starting with a classic cube, and later switching to more complex versions, for example, Megaminx. How to assemble it, it will become clear after a few successful trials.

If it seems to you that the method is too slow, thoroughly study the rules written for the Rubik's Cube. Having understood the essence and formula, it is not difficult to assemble a puzzle.
