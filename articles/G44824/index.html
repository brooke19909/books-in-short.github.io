Kaissa Beach Hotel 3 * (Gouves)

The modern aparthotel has a great location on the Mediterranean coast, on one of the beaches of Crete. Located in a popular resort village, surrounded by beautiful gardens. The hotel rooms offer beautiful views of the mountains and the sea. All rooms are bright and comfortable, equipped with comfortable furniture, have a modern design (decoration is done in the style traditional for Greece). The last renovation of buildings and rooms took place in 2007.

Location

The 3-star Kaissa Beach Hotel is located in the famous Cretan resort town of Kato Gouves. The distance from the hotel to the center of the village is about 600 meters, to the airport - 16 kilometers, to Heraklion - about 18 kilometers. Within walking distance from the complex are many shops, cafes and taverns.

Beach

Two hundred meters from the Kaissa Beach Hotel 3 * there is an urban sand and pebble beach. An undead road runs between the beach and the hotel. Sun loungers and parasols at the beach are available at an additional cost.

Hotel Impressions

Reviews about the tourist complex Kaissa Beach Hotel 3 * can be found quite controversial, but mostly people say that this is a good option for their money.

The rooms are ordinary, bright, quite spacious, there is a small kitchen with a minimum set of dishes. In the reviews there are complaints about non-working stoves and the lack of a TV and hairdryer. Also, according to some hotel guests, there are insects in the rooms. Cleanliness in the rooms on the "C grade", cleaning is not every day, the insulation is poor.

Also, guests of the complex do not like the lack of a reception desk and Russian-speaking staff. Judging by the reviews, the food is satisfactory, the portions are large, but it takes a long time to wait for the order, and not everything can be tasty.

We should also say about the sea. Many travelers choose this hotel because of its close proximity to the beach. The sea is across the road, but from some reviews it turns out that the beach is not equipped. To a decent beach where you can swim comfortably, you need to go about five minutes. There, in particular, they bathe with children due to the lack of waves, but the boat parking is located. And you have to go to a good beach with clean sand for about fifteen minutes. On all beaches there is no toilet and shower, no changing booths.





For entertainment, a Greek evening is held at the hotel once a week, but the entrance costs 12 euros.

In general terms about the hotel Kaissa Beach Hotel 3 * we can say that this is an average budget hotel for economical and undemanding tourists. Suitable for a relaxing holiday company or family with children. But, going there, you need to be prepared for a long walk to the normal beach, average room and not very tasty food.
