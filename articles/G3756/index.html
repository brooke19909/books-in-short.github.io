DIY Organza Flowers for Beginners

Organza flowers are beautiful, stylish and modern. From them you can create compositions for wedding decor, beautiful table setting or decorating hairstyles. We offer you to learn how to make organza flowers with your own hands. For beginners, the step-by-step instructions below are easy. They will allow you to quickly learn the basics of working with organza, so that in the future you can start creating more complex compositions.

What is required

To make flowers from organza you need to have at hand:

  • organza cut;
  • a needle;
  • sharp scissors;
  • threads of a suitable color;
  • cardboard templates;
  • matches or a lighter;
  • a candle;
  • decorative elements (beads, rhinestones, beads, etc.).

How to make an organza flower

Beginners should not immediately take on the creation of complex options. In order to make the simplest flower of organza, you need:

  • draw patterns on the cardboard in the form of 5-6 circles, each of which is 3 mm smaller than the previous one;
  • apply patterns to organza;
  • circle with a pencil;
  • cut out;
  • light a candle;
  • gently process the edges of the circles above the candle, holding them at right angles to the flame;
  • to impose circles on each other;
  • flash the petals with a thread;
  • to fix;
  • sew 2-3 beads in the middle of the organza flower or glue a few beads using a glue gun.

To make the product more attractive, you can cut the petals from the fabric of several similar shades. At the same time, it is better to make the largest circles from organza of a more saturated color, and gradually switch to paler ones.

Roses

The Queen of Flowers will be more difficult to make. For this:

  • circles of different sizes are cut from organza;
  • with sharp scissors make 5 neat cuts on each circle, not reaching the middle;
  • singe the edges of all the blanks above the candle so that they bend inward;
  • stack circles with already finished edges on top of each other in order from larger to smaller;
  • fix them in the middle with a thread.

Decorate the middle of the resulting rose to your taste with rhinestones or beads.

How to create organza flowers with your own hands without using a candle

Carefully processing the edges of the workpieces so that they do not turn black, is often quite difficult. Usually this is due to the poor quality of the candles. In this case, the problem can be easily solved by replacing it.

In addition, in stores you can find a special organza for making flowers and use to create floral arrangements. It does not pour in, which greatly facilitates the work. If such fabric is not sold in your city, there is a way to create organza flowers without using a candle.

Step-by-step instruction

A beautiful, neat flower will turn out if:

  • cut 5 circles from a regular organza with a diameter of 11 cm and the same number of such blanks with a diameter of 13 cm;
  • fold them gently in the shape of a semicircle;
  • Sew manually with a thread of a suitable color along the semicircular edge, retreating 5-6 mm;
  • tighten and tighten the thread so that the workpiece takes the form of a petal;
  • collect the petals of the same size in a ring so that you get 2 blanks in the shape of a flower;
  • sew the blanks with thread;
  • sew a large bead in the middle of the flower or stick a large strassik.

Such a flower can be fixed with a glue gun on an elastic band. Then you will get a beautiful decoration for the hairstyle.

Making a red poppy. What will be needed for manufacturing

Such a brooch flower will decorate a jacket, dress or women's hat. In order to make it, you will need:

  • scarlet organza;
  • felt;
  • candle (matches or lighter);
  • scissors;
  • pin for brooch;
  • needle with thread;
  • thick cardboard;
  • beads for decoration.

Manufacturing procedure

Like other organza flowers, poppies begin to craft by creating patterns. They represent 3 circles with a diameter of 10, 9 and 8 cm.

Further:

  • 4 blanks of different diameters are cut from organza;
  • each fold 2 times;
  • cut the folds, not reaching 1 cm to the end;
  • holding the workpiece by the uncut tip, round the corners;
  • singing petals over a candle;
  • move them apart and process the area between the petals above the flame.

Assembly

In order to facilitate this process, you need a dense cardboard, or rather, a cover from a shoe box. Thread a thread into the needle and insert it with the ear down, so that the needle sticks out like a stake. Further:

  • string the petals on the needle, starting with a larger one;
  • straighten the workpiece, giving the flower a beautiful shape;
  • when all the petals are strung on the needle, stretch the needle, stitch the middle of the flower several times and fix it;
  • decorate the middle of the flower with beads;
  • a circle is cut out of felt a little more than the length of a medium-sized English pin;
  • make small cuts;
  • insert a pin;
  • fix it on the flower with glue.

Volumetric organza and satin rose

To make such a decorative product you will need:

  • white satin and organza;
  • cardboard templates in the form of circles of different sizes for making petals;
  • scissors;
  • a needle with a thread of a suitable color;
  • candle;
  • big bead.

Operating procedure

Making a beautiful snow-white flower for a wedding decor is carried out as follows:

  • Cut out parts from organza and satin using prepared templates;
  • singe parts along the edges above the candle flame;
  • pick a flower, placing under each petal of the same organza, but from the atlas;
  • stitch all layers;
  • decorate the middle with a large white bead.

Kanzashi flower

Such an ornament will look very beautiful on the hair. To make it, you need to take:

  • a piece of organza in the form of a long ribbon 1.2 cm wide;
  • a bead;
  • needle with thread;
  • glue.

The work is performed in the following sequence:

  • 6 strips of 10 cm long are cut;
  • first fold in half in length, then in width and again in length;
  • the resulting petals are sequentially put on a needle;
  • pull a thread through them;
  • pull it in a circle;
  • fix;
  • spread the flower in a circle;
  • form petals, giving volume;
  • fasten in the middle a bead.

Now you know how to make organza flowers with your own hands. The master classes presented above will help to create compositions and decor items with which you can please your friends, sisters, daughters, decorate your home or just cheer yourself up.
