Hotels in Makhachkala: photos and reviews

Makhachkala is a city on the shores of the Caspian Sea, is the capital of the Republic of Dagestan and the largest settlement in the North Caucasus. The multinational population is about six hundred thousand people. The modern name Makhachkala received in honor of the revolutionary Makhach Dakhadayev in 1921. Now it is a large city in which the resort business is developing rapidly. Makhachkala hotels are being built to all international standards. They can satisfy the needs of any tourist, along with the resorts of Turkey and Egypt. Golden sand beaches and rapidly developing infrastructure attract more and more guests on the Caspian coast.

A bit of history

Historically, at different times, armies of the Huns, Persians, Arabs passed along the Caspian coast. This "Dagestan corridor", through the village of Tarki, passed the caravan route to Derbent and all of East Asia. The first information about this dates back to the VIII century. Near the village, on the site of modern Makhachkala, Russian troops laid down the Petrovskaya fortress. And subsequently an artificial harbor and port were built. Petrovsk received rapid development at the end of the 19th century. Were built: the railway, a large paper mill, oil refinery, brewery and cooperage.



Makhachkala hotels on the federal highway E119

The President Hotel (Makhachkala, Avtomobilistov per. 7) is located near the highway in a picturesque foothill corner. The hotel fund consists of 18 rooms of different comforts. There are both standard rooms and luxury apartments. The restaurant has a fine selection of dishes, in the morning a rich breakfast is included, included in the room rate. There is free parking.

The Alhambra Hotel (Makhachkala, Engels St., 1zh) is located on the highway near the trolleybus ring and the zoo. The rooms are equipped with everything you need, including toiletries, bathrobes and slippers. Guests can relax in the spa and wellness center with massage services. And also take a steam bath in the sauna or visit the Turkish hammam. In the restaurant you can not only have breakfast, lunch and dinner, another brunch and afternoon tea are organized.

According to the residents, the hotels near the E119 highway are a wonderful vacation that takes place away from the bustle of the city, surrounded by magnificent nature and clean mountain air.



Hotels near the railway station and port

The Lux Hotel (Makhachkala, Daniyalova St., 1) is located two blocks from the railway station. On the ground floor there is a small conference room, a snack bar and a restaurant, as well as a spa center with a sauna and massage services. In the cozy courtyard you can relax in the shade of the garden and use the barbecue area. Free parking (upon reservation), pets are allowed.

Hotel "Caspian" (Makhachkala, Buinakskogo str., 8) is located in the city center near the port, not far from the hotel city garden and beach. Standard rooms are equipped with everything necessary, have a fresh repair and good plumbing. There is free parking, a restaurant, pets are allowed (no extra charge).

Guests note that the Makhachkala hotels near the port are very conveniently located, as this is the city center with convenient transportation. It is also worth considering a moderate fee and good service. Hotels are well suited for short stays and business trips.

Resort Reviews

Vacationers agree that Makhachkala has its own unique flavor. The Caspian Sea, the beauty of the mountains and clean air do not leave tourists indifferent. Makhachkala hotels provide services for both discerning and budget guests. Hospitality and benevolence of local residents are noted. The chaotic building of the city is considered by some as a minus, while others consider it a feature that adds its charm. Opinions were also shared about the arrangement of many beaches. One lacks comfort, others are touched by virgin landscapes. Curious tourists are pleased with the opportunity of cultural leisure. The city is interesting for its museums, theaters and parks. National cuisine has a separate list of praise. Only for her sake are many ready to give up their usual vacation in Turkey and Egypt.
