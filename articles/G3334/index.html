Le Soleil Bella Vista Hotel (Tunisia, Monastir, Skanes): photos and reviews of tourists

In recent years, travel agents have noted an increased demand for holidays in Tunisia. For a long time, our compatriots were scared by the unstable political situation inside this country, but by now it has stabilized as much as possible, and Russian tourists have begun to actively buy up tours in this direction. Four-star hotel complexes are in great demand, which can provide guests with a high level of comfort.

One of these hotels is Le Soleil Bella Vista Hotel, and it was he who received a lot of positive reviews from tourists in the current holiday season. Interest in this hotel complex is very high, so today we decided to describe it in detail. We will give readers a brief description of Le Soleil Bella Vista Resort Hotel, talk about its infrastructure and features. In some sections, we will rely on the feedback of our compatriots who this year had a rest in this hotel complex.



Le Soleil Bella Vista Hotel: description

This hotel is considered quite new, because it was commissioned in the early nineties of the last century. During construction, very serious requirements were made to the design, and as a result, a magnificent complex appeared in front of the guests, successfully combining the Moorish traditional style with modern technologies. Oriental flavor is read in the appearance of the hotel and in the design of rooms, and the beautiful green area creates a certain atmosphere of bliss and luxury.

I would like to add that the territory of Le Soleil Bella Vista Resort Hotel looks very well-groomed and conducive to relaxation, and the palm grove spread around the hotel is a real highlight of the whole complex.

Recent repairs were carried out at the hotel about six years ago. Tourists often note that the number of rooms looks quite decent, and describe the hotel in bright colors.

Le Soleil Bella Vista Hotel has two buildings in which five hundred and two rooms are equipped. They can simultaneously accommodate a little more than one thousand people. Guests are offered a choice of four room categories.

Based on the reviews of Le Soleil Bella Vista Resort Hotel, we can say that the hotel complex is well suited for a relaxing and measured stay. Here, children and those who want to completely disconnect from the hustle and bustle of everyday life feel great.

Beach of the hotel complex

Le Soleil Bella Vista Resort Hotel 4 * has a very beautiful beach, which is praised by almost all tourists. Many consider it a real advantage of this hotel complex.

The beach area is a wide strip of golden sand, which is regularly cleaned by hotel staff. There are three rows of sun loungers, so they are enough for all guests.

The entrance to the water is gentle, and the sea in this part of the coast is rather shallow, therefore it is suitable for bathing children and those who do not feel very confident on the water.

It is noteworthy that the hotel itself and the beach area are carefully guarded. If outsiders can still enter the hotel grounds, citing the need to visit the store, then the coast is not available to them. Tourists will not be annoyed by local merchants, who often spoil the rest at other resorts.

The only minus of the beach area, our compatriots consider the abundance of jellyfish in the water. In the summer months they are especially active and sting bathers rather painfully. Even in adults, redness occurred, which later transformed into bruises. With the onset of September, jellyfish swim to the shore less and less and are no longer interested in those who are in the water.



Infrastructure

Photos of Le Soleil Bella Vista Hotel are quite beautiful, but it‚Äôs extremely difficult to determine how developed the infrastructure for vacationers is. Therefore, we will try to fill this gap and give readers a description of everything that awaits them at the hotel.

Tourists spend a lot of time by the pool with an area of ‚Äã‚Äãalmost six hundred squares. It has a spacious recreation area, which is almost always occupied by vacationers of Arab origin. And in the evenings our compatriots gather here, having fun until the morning.

Hotel guests can use two conference rooms. Both of them are quite small, accommodate thirty and forty people. But here is everything you need to successfully conduct a business meeting or a festive event.

The territory has eight bars and restaurants, whose staff will not allow guests to stay hungry. The food here is very tasty, our compatriots especially praise the national cuisine and sweets.

At the 24-hour reception there are no employees who speak Russian. However, the simplest requests are understood by both the front desk and the maids. The receptionist is at the reception from the earliest morning to evening, he promptly responds to any requests and comments, so do not be shy to contact this friendly person.

The hotel has laundry facilities and a beauty salon. At the reception, guests can exchange currency, place valuables in a safe or rent a transport, for the storage of which a spacious parking lot is equipped.

Check In Rules

The rules at this hotel are standard, but you must know them before booking Le Soleil Bella Vista Resort Hotel. For example, check-in occurs after two in the afternoon. Many of our compatriots write in reviews that they were settled without much persuasion much earlier than the specified time. But often they tried to give the keys to the apartments easier than those that were paid. When an error was indicated, the situation was instantly corrected.

It is necessary to check out of the room before noon, by this time guests must pay all bills and hand over the keys to the front door.

Paid and free entertainment

Each of the hotel guests has the opportunity to play mini-golf or football for free. On the beach you can get a catamaran, and for those who have a license, windsurfing is available. A few words about nutrition

The hotel complex operates on an all-inclusive basis. This is another reason why people here are happy to go on vacation with the kids. The main restaurant Le Carnavale accommodates seven hundred guests, it operates according to the following schedule:

  • from seven to ten in the morning breakfast;
  • brunch lasts until eleven in the morning;
  • dinner begins at thirteen hours and lasts two hours;
  • for dinner, the first guests come at seven in the evening, the meal ends at half past ten in the evening;
  • from eleven o'clock in the evening until midnight, late dinner lasts.

The hotel has several a la carte restaurants:

  • La Fiesta. In this Italian restaurant you can have dinner for free once during your vacation, but only by appointment (dinner starts at seven in the evening and ends at ten in the evening). The institution is designed for fifty people.
  • Mediterrano. Here you can taste delicious Mediterranean cuisine with an abundance of seafood. The restaurant has a lounge and an outdoor terrace. The hall can accommodate one hundred people, under the starry sky there are enough places for only thirty hotel guests.
  • Neptune Pub. This roomy establishment will accommodate one hundred people. But in the open air there are even more tables that can accommodate up to four hundred guests.

Bars have their own work schedule:

  • The Kiosk. The cafe opens immediately after breakfast and offers guests a large selection of snacks.
  • Piano bar. This poolside bar opens its doors at six in the evening. The institution works until midnight.
  • Cafe Maure. Here, all guests can try the hookah, the schedule of the bar is identical to the previous one.
  • El Mahfel. The bar is located in a disco and serves dancers. It opens at half past ten in the evening and closes at two in the morning.

I would like to add that in all a la carte restaurants it is strictly forbidden to come in shorts.
