Aquila Atlantis Hotel 5 *, (Crete, Heraklion): hotel description, reviews

Aquila Atlantis Hotel is the most luxurious hotel in Heraklion. It is in a very convenient location, the staff of the complex is polite and attentive, and the rooms are furnished and equipped with everything necessary for comfort. Therefore, people from all over the world come here. However, everything should be told in more detail.





Location

Many people, choosing a hotel in which they will stop upon arrival, are guided by the location of the complex. Aquila Atlantis Hotel is located in the heart of Heraklion, just a minute's walk from the local archaeological museum, which is a tourist attraction. It is also worth noting that the windows of the rooms overlook the historic port and the Venetian medieval fortress.

The airport, by the way, is located five kilometers from the hotel. This is a significant plus, since upon arrival at the resort you will not need two more painful hours to get to the hotel. There is also a bus stop just 400 meters away. Shopping center is also within walking distance. In general, since this is the center of the city, it can be reached without any difficulties to any object of interest to a tourist.

By the way, the complex was built a long time ago, but in 2013 it was decided to carry out its full reconstruction. And after that, the hotel became rightfully considered the best and most modern hotel on the island of Crete.
