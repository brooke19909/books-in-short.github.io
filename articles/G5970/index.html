Resort "Adam and Eve", Turkey: features, rooms and reviews

A trip to hot Turkey always combines a multidirectional vacation. This country attracts with its rich history and amazingly clear waters of the Mediterranean Sea. Here, every traveler, regardless of his age, can have fun and enjoy the long-awaited vacation. Interesting excursions, picturesque nature, equipped beaches and hotels for every taste and pocket are what attract tourists here. A favorable climate for healing and healing waters are inalienable advantages of the resort towns of this land, one of which is Belek.

Belek and its positive aspects

This city has become famous among vacationers from around the world due to its eucalyptus and pine forests. The season opens in early May and lasts until mid-November. In winter, the flow of guests is not particularly reduced. Golf lovers come here, and indoor pools save the situation.

If you have become the proud owner of a tour to Turkey, the Adam and Eve Hotel is pleased to meet every guest in Belek. Staying here, everyone can feel as if in a paradise on the outskirts of the planet, and the regulars of this institution do not skimp on positive reviews about him. The leading tour operator "Tui" often offers to consider the hotel "Adam and Eve" in Turkey as an option. The hotel complex truly has five stars, offering its guests exquisite apartments, high-class service and a full range of various entertainments.

general characteristics

Hotel Adam & Eva (Belek, Turkey) was erected and opened its doors to accommodate the first guests in 2006. The spacious territory of 100 thousand square meters is a lost oasis, surrounded by lush vegetation and picturesque forests. The desire to return here again certainly appears with every vacationer who has been here at least once. Over the years of its existence, the hotel has been subject to numerous repairs and improvements. The last global renovation took place in 2014.

The well-groomed park area includes numerous pools, places for relaxation and paved alleys, which are covered in places with red paths. The main building is represented by a six-story building equipped with elevators. It is surrounded by numerous two-story snow-white villas. Infrastructure includes a mini-water park, a nightclub and areas of all kinds of entertainment.

Location

One of the main advantages of this institution is its location. The beach is less than 50 meters away. The absence of carriageways makes it possible to overcome it with ease. Transfer to the nearest airport will take about 40 minutes. A short trip after the flight will appeal to travelers, as noted in their many reviews of the Adam and Eve Hotel (Turkey). A well-developed transport system allows you to reach Antalya within 45 hours, which is located 45 km from the hotel.

There are many hotels near Adam and Eve (Turkey) and all of them are also good for accommodation. This suggests that the city is busy. In general, as tourists note, the land of Turkey is rich in attractions, many of which are concentrated in the Belek district. It is possible to go on a tour to the ancient city of Perge, which will certainly surprise tourists with the ruins that have survived to this day. Keprylu Canyon National Park is a picturesque place, walking along which you can enjoy the unique flora in full. However, many people come to these parts in order to visit the Zeytin Tash cave. It surprises with the beauty of underground formations hanging overhead, and is open to visitors by absolutely everyone.

Settlement Features

According to the internal rules of the Adam and Eve Hotel (Turkey, Belek), check-in with pets is unacceptable. Smoking is not allowed in public places - there are specially equipped areas for this. There are apartments for people with reduced mobility. Like all visitors, this category of tourists will enjoy their holidays.

Check-in time is standard here - 14:00. Leave the apartment before 12:00. Quick check-in and late check-out are available. The staff has a high level of professionalism and speaks various languages, so the language barrier is leveled. When paying at restaurants and other services, it is possible to use bank cards.

It should be noted that this hotel complex accommodates guests exclusively at the age of 16 years. That is why on tourist sites the price for the hotel "Adam and Eve" (Turkey) is indicated for two adults, without children.

Supply system

In accordance with the current ultra all-inclusive system, five meals a day are served in the form of a buffet in the hall of the main restaurant, the atmosphere of which is conducive to eating and friendly conversation. You can taste delicious snacks, ice cream and Turkish cakes in the institution Regular Snack (bistro), which is located near the central pool. Ebruli Confectionery will indulge its visitors with wonderful desserts.

On the territory of the hotel complex there are more than five narrowly targeted restaurants that operate on a custom-made menu. It requires prior reservation of the table and compliance with the dress code. You can quench your thirst in one of the seven bars located in the lobby, by the pool, on the beach and in the courtyard of the Adam & Eva hotel complex.

Beach

The location on the first coastline allows guests to easily reach the beach, the length of which is 300 meters. On the golden sands are comfortable sun loungers with soft red mattresses. For those who want to hide from the sun, you can sit under umbrellas and awnings. Lease of tents is paid upon. There is a towel dispenser and a small bar. A wooden pier allows you to be in the midst of sea waves and enjoy the most beautiful views lying in a deck chair. In the evening, discos are held here. In order for the time spent here to be eventful and varied, you can play volleyball, ride a water scooter or go for a walk on the open sea.

health and beauty

For those who care about their appearance even at the resort, the doors of a fitness center equipped with numerous fitness equipment, dumbbells and other equipment are open. Professional instructors will help in the implementation of training, evenly distributing the load on the body. The territory of the SPA center occupies 5500 square meters and includes a Finnish sauna, a steam room, a shock pool and a hammam. Wellness treatments for face and body skin will give an unprecedented pleasure and relaxation to every visitor. In the massage room, where talented masseurs work, you can replenish the supply of strength and give relaxation to every cell of the body.

Entertainment

The range of the proposed pastime within the walls of the Adam & Eve Hotel 5 * resort is surprisingly diverse. Here you can play billiards or bowling, compete for the championship in table tennis or darts. One of the main attractions is diving. Acquaintance with the underwater world of these surroundings impresses both the beginner and the experienced diver.

Four synthetic tennis courts are open for play. Here you can use the services of an instructor, which are paid extra. Rental of balls and rackets is included in the price of tickets. Indulge in a platform for playing soccer, squash and mini-golf. The evening ends with a delightful show of a talented team of artists, which involves absolutely every tourist in the fun process.

Additional services

To meet the maximum possible list of guests needs, a list of additional paid and free services has been created. There are 11 conference rooms of various configurations that allow you to implement a business event in different formats. Transfer, doctor's call and laundry are paid upon delivery. Car parking does not require prior reservation and is protected. The reception is open 24 hours a day. Next to it are a tour desk and a currency exchange. Professional photographers, whose services are paid separately, will help capture unforgettable days of relaxation.

If you still decide to visit Belek, then you should know that the price of tickets to the Adam and Eva hotel (Turkey) is not quite affordable - from 70,000 rubles for 7 days of stay (2 adults without children). But, as many tourists convince, it's worth it. They say they will be happy to come to the hotel complex again.
