Stadium "Locomotive" (Simferopol): description, history, photo

The multisport stadium Lokomotiv (Simferopol) is a republican sports complex. It is used mainly for matches with the participation of TSK (local football club). True football fans know the place where the Lokomotiv stadium (Simferopol) is located. Address: st. Pushkin, house number 46, Simferopol, 95011.





Description

The Lokomotiv Stadium (Simferopol) was built in 1967 and reconstructed in 2004. He was considered one of the best in Ukraine, where the matches of the USSR team were held.

Parameters

The Lokomotiv stadium (Simferopol) is distinguished by the following characteristics:

  • Capacity: 19 979 seats.
  • Field dimensions: 104.6 mx 67.5 m.
  • Lighting: 1600 lux.
  • Coating material: natural grass.

History

Lokomotiv Stadium Simferopol was built in 1863. The decision to build it was made after Kiev officials, in view of the lack of necessary sports infrastructure, stopped the transition to Class A from the predecessor of Tavria - Simferopol Avangard.

A vast area near the former headquarters of the Tauride Military District was chosen as the territory for construction. On holidays, military demonstrations and parades were usually held here. On ordinary days, lovers played soccer.

The parameters of the area made it possible to place on it the whole complex of necessary infrastructure. In many ways, the convenient landscape facilitated the task for the builders: the central stand of Lokomotiv was erected on a hill. In April 1967, the first match was held at the new stadium.
