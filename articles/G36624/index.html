Yekaterinburg, recreation center "Sunny Island": features of rest and rooms

Everyone who wants to relax and recharge with positive energy is waiting for “Sunny Island” (recreation center). Yekaterinburg is only a few tens of kilometers from here. Easy and comfortable to get to this place everyone can.

The road to the institution

The territory of the institution is located in a clean pine forest, whose ecology contributes to the healing of customers. Nearby is the picturesque Mostovka River. However, many city dwellers come here for the singing of birds and the unique healing aroma of pine needles.

The base offers a lot of entertainment. Rest will be interesting and useful both in summer and in winter.



The complex looks like a particle of paradise. Fully lives up to its name "Sunny Island" (recreation center). Yekaterinburg (how to get there, prompt signs) is located 35 km from the institution.

Complex address: Verkhnepyshminsky district, s. Mostovsky. The course must be kept on the city of Verkhnyaya Pyshma, then go towards the village of Baltym. From this point you should go to the village of Mostovsky. On the left side of the road there will be signs indicating the road to the base.

Guests are accommodated in three buildings. The rooms have modern bathrooms. There are televisions, refrigerators, there are internal phones. Luxury rooms are equipped with a hydromassage bathtub. The furniture in the rooms is new. The rooms have workplaces.

"Sunny Island" is a recreation center in Yekaterinburg, which occupies one of the first places (according to visitors' reviews). Guests enjoy good service and affordable prices.

Comfort and democracy

Here you can live in economy rooms, where the bathroom and toilet are in the block, and in family rooms, where the administration puts extra cots. There is a special offer from the institution for the newlyweds. Their bedroom is festively decorated with flowers and candles. The administration provides all this (as well as the room itself) to the newly made couple for free.

This complex is one of the best institutions that Ekaterinburg can offer. The recreation center "Sunny Island" offers maximum privacy for large companies. You can also live in the cottage. A wooden two-story house stands away from the center. Here they are not afraid to break the silence and have fun until the morning. The house has four bedrooms, the rooms are furnished with everything necessary. Bathrooms and showers meet all requirements. In the spacious kitchen you can cook yourself. There are necessary utensils, a roomy refrigerator and a high-quality microwave. The hall has a TV, music center and karaoke. The cottage accommodates up to 15 people. For convenience, there is a barbecue near the house. It should be noted that free and fast Internet is constantly working.





Sincere meetings

On the territory is a building intended only for entertainment. Here you can hold banquets. The total number of seats is up to 40 guests. The kitchen is attached to the room. The cafe bar immediately accommodates up to 70 people.

The institution professionally organizes meetings of a wide variety of companies. Come here to relax as company employees for corporate parties and seminars, and families for weddings and children's parties. In the halls there is equipment for photo and video presentations. Special conference rooms accommodate up to 150 people. A lot of visitors decide to spend New Year's holidays in this institution. Weddings are especially beautiful here. Newlyweds usually book an outdoor ceremony in a fabulous nature.

“Sunny Island” (recreation center, Yekaterinburg) has very positive reviews. In particular, visitors are impressed with extra. entertainment programs. Everyone is relaxing here.

The administration is preparing a special show for the holidays. Concerts, competitions, discos will not let anyone get bored.





Hot entertainment

Guests note that the summer in this complex is beautiful. Improve its additional offers. There are many sports fields in the complex. Visitors can play soccer, volleyball. The fields for these games are different. Guests can even arrange championships among themselves.

There are paintball halls. These rooms are decorated using military symbols. Visitors get the impression that they were in a virtual game. For kids, a separate, completely safe children's playground is equipped.

The whole Yekaterinburg is hiding here from the hot sun. The recreation center "Sunny Island" knows what to interest customers. For guests who want to learn something new and wish to raise the level of adrenaline, cable courses work. In order to better view the entire territory, the administration offers to rent a bike or roller.





Entertainment for the lazy

For supporters of calmer fun, there are tennis and billiard tables on site. You can play cards, dominoes and other intellectual games.

You can relax and unwind on the banks of the river, taking sun baths. It always plays beautiful unobtrusive music. On the beach there is another playground in the sand for mini-football.

It should not be forgotten that, first of all, “Sunny Island” is a recreation center. Yekaterinburg and residents of other cities come here for soulfulness and peace. In the evening, guests usually sit in gazebos. Some fry kebab, others communicate with friends.

You can relax, restore strength and relax in a Russian bath or sauna (Finnish and infrared). An additional service for fashionistas is the Sharko shower.





Frost is not a hindrance!

Visitors are not discouraged here in the winter. Not too much entertainment from November to March can offer Yekaterinburg (recreation center "Sunny Island"). If you first agree with the employees of the establishment, then snowmobiles and ATVs will be prepared for your arrival.

For ski enthusiasts, a piste for 5 and 10 km opens. Guests enjoy rolling between snowy trees and full chest breathing in the fresh frosty air. Equipment can be rented.

Annually pour a high and long slide. Here they give sledges, "buns" and skates. In the cold season, an ice rink is opened. The skating rink (indoor) is well lit and equipped with light music. Beautiful and rhythmic songs are constantly played in the room. You can order a site for playing hockey.

There is one more reason why almost the whole of Yekaterinburg wants to come here in winter: the recreation center “Sunny Island” is located on the banks of the river. In the cold season, you can successfully go fishing here. No less popular are walks in a snowy forest, a game of snowballs and gatherings around a fire.
