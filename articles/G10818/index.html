Zmeykovsky waterfalls (Sochi): excursions to picturesque places

A famous attraction of the Khostinsky district is Zmeykovsky waterfalls (Sochi). Not everyone knows how to get to this wonderful place, so we will describe the detailed routes a little later.

These cascades are located on the territory of the Sochi National Park. They got their name thanks to Mount Zmeyka, where the Dikarka River flows. It is in its streams that high, from 3 to 10 meters, waterfalls are born.



Short description

The route along these natural wonders is located in a green forest, where many rare plants grow - boxwood, laurel cherry. The river is constantly changing throughout the way and can be either perfectly straight or curl at right angles. The bed itself is made up of stony reaches, as well as rocky shores. But its main feature is Zmeykovsky waterfalls.

Zmeykovsky waterfalls have one feature - numerous springs. Thanks to them, the river is fueled, and water cascades never dry up. It is worth mentioning that at the end of the route there is a natural key with very clean water, which you can pour into bottles and drink right there.

Throughout the journey, you can find small pavilions and cafes where they offer refreshments and continue their journey through the waterfalls. And, of course, no one forbids to go off the trail and wander through the woods far from groups of tourists, have a picnic or photograph the views of the surroundings.



Voucher purchase

A variety of companies provide numerous excursions, the main purpose of which is to visit Zmeykovsky waterfalls (Sochi). You can buy tickets for the miracle of nature in the summer season at the ticket offices of tour operators, and in winter at the train station, Platanova Alley or at the Riviera park.

If you decide not to use the services of travel agencies, then it will not be difficult to get to the waterfalls yourself by car or public transport. In this case, you will have to pay only for entry.



Zmeykovsky waterfalls (Sochi): how to get to the place on your own?

In order to get to the waterfalls by car from the city of Sochi, you will need to go to Kurortny Prospekt, and then head towards Adler. At the bridge to the Matsesta River, you should turn and move to Old Matsesta. Then you need to turn onto Chekmeneva street and drive through the village of Kraevsko-Armyanskoe. Not far behind it will be a sign "Zmeykovskie waterfalls (Sochi)." Near it, you need to turn left, and there along the concrete road go to the bridge over the river. Drive along it for several kilometers, and there will be a rolled primer, which leads directly to the gates of the Sochi National Park.

To get to the Zmeykovsky waterfalls without your own transport, you need to take bus number 120, get to the stop of the same name, cross the track, then go down the concrete road. When you see the bridge, turn left and go straight to the ticket office of Sochi National Park.



Excursions in Sochi: reviews of tourists

People from all over Russia come to Sochi to see this attraction. Tourists celebrate the incredible beauty of the forest and the river, as well as fresh air and clear spring water. Interestingly, most of the people who have been there recommend that you go the route yourself, without the help of guides and guides.

To make an excursion to the Zmeykovsky Falls, no special preparation and equipment is required, so people of absolutely any age can come to Sochi themselves and see this natural wonder with their own eyes, as well as enjoy spring water and relax. The approximate time of the complete passage of the entire tourist route is about one and a half hours. Zmeykovsky waterfalls (Sochi) will give an unforgettable experience, after which you will want to come here again.
