An extremely fashionable women's accessory today is a scarf crocheted from woolen yarn - for the cold season - or from cotton threads - for summer. About how you can make some models yourself, is described in this article.



Openwork scarf crocheted for spring and autumn

It is easy to make such a multifunctional accessory from thick yarn, which will serve as a luxurious addition to the outfit and warm it in the cool time. Such a scarf is crocheted according to the proposed scheme from the bottom corner up. The dots in the drawing indicate the air loops, simple segments - single crochet, the lines drawn with perpendiculars are double crochet. If the segments come from one point, it means that several columns are knitted from one, and if the segments are joined together at the top, then several columns are knitted into one. Crocheted a lace scarf pretty quickly, looks gorgeous.



Kerchief "Colorful squares"

Each craftswoman, fond of knitting, has a whole collection of small multi-colored glomeruli. These are the remains of yarn from various models. There is an option to use them, making a bright, original and very beautiful scarf. This crocheted knitted scarf consists of separately made squares, which are then sewn together with one another. It should be remembered that several triangles will be needed on the slice, because it itself has the shape of a triangle. There are a great many options for making squares. Here is the easiest one.

  • 1 row: a ring of five air loops.
  • The 2nd row does not fit from the loops, but from the middle of the ring. Three columns with two double crochets, five stitches (per angle) - the algorithm is repeated 4 times.
  • We knit the 3rd row in a different color, starting from the corner. Three columns with two crochets, five air loops, 3 columns knit from one corner. Next, the algorithm is repeated for the remaining three angles.
  • For 4 rows, use another color again. How to knit corners, the master already knows.
