Hellhound deck in Clash Royale.

There are many ways to conquer the ladder. Even if you have only "Hellhound" from the legendary cards. Today we will consider a couple of decks using this unit, which can be used at high levels of arenas.

Description

Before the first deck with the Hellhound will be considered, let's examine the characteristics and operation of this card. What positive can be noted in it?





  • A huge supply of health (4380 on the first level).
  • Fast activation.
  • After death, spawns Hell Puppies of the 5th level.
  • You can get a card already at the 4th arena.
  • Flies.

The disadvantages of this card are much more:

  • Cost of 7 Elixir.
  • Slow speed of movement.
  • Low damage.
  • Flies (melee units will ignore it and will beat on your attacking troops).
  • Attack on buildings only.

The last point may cause controversy, but if the enemy releases his troops behind the Hound, it can go further to the tower, while your attacking units will fight with the landing. In general, the deck with the Hellhound as the basis will be very weak, but if you have little choice, then let's consider the options.
