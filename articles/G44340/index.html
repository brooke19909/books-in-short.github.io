Tablet repair in the service

Tablet computers are appreciated by many users for their virtues. They are mobile, easy to operate and easy to operate. But, like any other technique, these devices tend to break. And this leads to the fact that it is required to carry out tablet repairs.

Common breakdowns

  1. The tablet does not turn on. This breakdown occurs due to the failure of any battery. It can be a battery, charger, connector or controller. But there may also be other reasons, among which there are often problems with the processor and cooling system.
  2. Broken screen. With this combination of circumstances, repair (tablets insured against this, is no longer available) is inevitable.
  3. Inoperability of communication channels. One of the main advantages of tablets is the ability to access the network. Data can be transferred via Wi-Fi or 3G / 4G, if the gadget is equipped with the corresponding modems.
  4. Software issues. A similar malfunction is expressed in the form of a spontaneous reboot, slow operation, crashes or errors. The reason for this behavior may be incorrect software operation, virus infection, problems of RAM or hard disk. Self-diagnosis is difficult due to the large number of possible reasons.
  5. Other malfunctions. This category of breakdowns may include the lack of image, sound or other features optionally provided for this model.

Cost of services in service centers

For tablet repairs, prices depend on many factors. This is a type of malfunction, and the complexity of the work, and the price of the components required to restore the functionality of the device. One cannot answer this question so simply. Moreover, in each case, preliminary diagnostics are required, which is performed free of charge in most service centers.

One of the most common problems is the problem with charging the device. After the diagnosis, a conclusion will be made on the reasons for such functioning of the equipment. If the battery is faulty, then replacing it will cost from 600 rubles, soldering the connector costs about 500 rubles, and replacing the power controller is already about 1200 units of Russian currency. It should be remembered that you will have to pay for components separately.



Another malfunction experienced by service center specialists is expressed in the form of broken screens. Repair of tablets with such damage is estimated at 900 rubles. Repair Chinese tablets

Separately, it is worth highlighting this particular category of gadgets. Not all companies providing services for the repair of equipment, are taken to eliminate damage to Chinese tablets. This is due to several factors. Firstly, cheap device models have poor build quality. This means that such a device is quite unreliable. Secondly, it is problematic to find spare parts for them.
