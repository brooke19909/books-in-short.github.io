Sharm El Sheikh, Coral Hills Resort 4 *: hotel overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

One of the best places for sea holidays can be considered the Egyptian city of Sharm El Sheikh. Coral Hills Resort 4 * is located in an incredibly picturesque place.





Brief description of the hotel

If you like the hot sun and the azure sea, then the ideal place to spend your vacation is Sharm El Sheikh. Coral Hills Resort 4 * is characterized not only by a good location, but also by an incredibly high-quality service, which involves providing many services to tourists.

In order for you to be able to fully enjoy the beautiful views that Sharm el-Sheikh is so rich in, Coral Hills Resort 4 * offers you to stay in rooms with huge windows. In addition, you will certainly be surprised at the beauty and practicality of the design, as well as the quality of service of the apartments.

For those who love vibrant entertainment, one of the priority areas is Sharm El Sheikh. Coral Hills Resort 4 * will provide you with many opportunities to fill your leisure with active and fun activities.





Hotel location

Resort Hotel Coral Hills Resort Sharm El-Sheikh 4 * (Sharm El Sheikh) is located on the highest point of the city - Um El Sid Hill - directly above the sheer and picturesque cliffs. Also, after making a 20-minute journey by taxi or bus, you can find yourself in Naama Bay, famous among travelers. The road from the airport to the hotel will seem to you quick and easy, because it takes only 20 minutes of your time.

Hotel Rooms

Hotel Coral Hills Resort 4 * (Egypt, Sharm-El-Sheikh) presents 156 guest rooms to the attention of tourists, two thirds of which can be attributed to the standard category. This is just a small bedroom with a king size bed or two single beds. If we talk about family rooms, they are somewhat more spacious and consist of two rooms. 2 bedrooms are connected by a door.

What is provided in the rooms

In all apartments of the hotel you can take advantage of the following amenities:





  • Standard rooms have a shower, while family rooms have a large bathtub;
  • a large package of cable channels (including several Russian ones) will be available for viewing thanks to a widescreen flat TV;
  • each room has an alarm clock (but if you do not trust the work of equipment and electronics, then you can order a similar service by contacting the reception staff);
  • so that you can independently regulate the air temperature in your apartments, they have a modern air conditioning;
  • due to the fact that the room has a safe, you can be absolutely calm for the safety of your money, documents and valuables during your absence;
  • the phone will allow you to contact the reception staff with a question or complaint at any time, as well as make a call abroad;
  • You can enjoy the scenery or hang your laundry for drying on a small but very cozy balcony (if your room is located on the ground floor, you will be provided with a spacious, furnished terrace).

The infrastructure of the hotel complex

In order for travelers not to feel the remoteness of the resort from the city center, the Coral Hills Resort 4 * hotel (4 * resort, Sharm El Sheikh) has developed a full-fledged tourist infrastructure, which includes the following objects:

  • Reception desk is the heart of the hotel, where check-in and check-in is carried out, as well as all issues and problems that arise;
  • the hotel has a parking zone, which meets all the requirements and standards of convenience and safety;
  • several times a week the doctor takes in the hotel, and in case of urgent need he can be called at any time;
  • without leaving the hotel, you can make a currency exchange, as well as cash money from a plastic card using a bank machine;
  • for independent sightseeing, you can rent a car from a large fleet owned by the hotel;
  • there is a center on the beach providing services for lovers of diving and other marine activities;
  • restaurants are where the best local chefs prepare dishes from the freshest products;
  • drinks and a huge selection of snacks are offered to tourists by hotel bars.




Positive reviews

All the advantages of the Coral Hills Resort (Sharm el-Sheikh) hotel in Sharm el-Sheikh are reflected in the positive reviews left by vacationers on specialized resources. The most common comments are:

  • upon arrival at the hotel, guests are immediately invited to have a meal in the restaurant;
  • the rooms are clean enough and all their equipment is in good condition;
  • the apartments have a good set of furniture and appliances;
  • daily a thorough cleaning is carried out in the rooms, during which supplies of hygiene items are constantly replenished;
  • excellent food with a wide range of dishes and fresh products;
  • hotel staff do not disregard birthday people, delighting them with a festive cake, as well as original room decoration;
  • there is a transfer to the sea;
  • beautiful pool with hydromassage and a waterfall;
  • for Egyptian hotels in this hotel is pretty good alcohol.




Claims and comments

All shortcomings and gaps in terms of service were reflected in negative reviews and comments from tourists:

  • the staff does not understand Russian (but is fluent in English);
  • the buses that take tourists to the beach and back are too small, so there is always not enough space in them.
