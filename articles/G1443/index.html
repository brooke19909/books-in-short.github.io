Interesting places

The city of Lloret de Mar has a very ancient history. It is here that you can admire the ancient commemorative finds dating back another 250 BC. e. For all such significant exhibits, the amazing Verdaguer Cultural Center was built. For curious kids and adults who admire the marine nature, there is a maritime museum nearby. Entrance to all establishments is paid, the average ticket price is about 4 euros per adult.

The sea boulevard is perfect for hiking, here you can explore interesting places, get acquainted with the famous copy of the Canale fountain. The original of this architectural work can be seen in Barcelona. The reconstruction of the copy was established with the help of state representatives back in 1968.

The local symbol of Lloret de Mar is the Bronze sculpture of Donna Mirener, it is also customary to call it the "Fisherman's Wife". It can be seen from afar, as it is located on a cliff at the end of the local beach. This is an amazing statue of a woman who looks into the distance, allegedly escorting her sailor to the path.

Another significant destination in Lloret de Mar is the Beach Castle. A few decades ago, a certain Narsis Plaha lived here. Work on the construction of the building began in 1935, according to the project of the famous architect Isidor Boche, but the construction was interrupted for some period due to the civil war, and the castle was completed only after 5 years.

Places for rest

Not far from the center is the water entertainment center Water World. This is the most popular place among tourists, especially during the hot season. This center has a huge number of water high-speed slides, large pools, children's and adult attractions. There is even the opportunity to play mini-golf. Parents who come to rest with their children may not have to worry much about them: there is a specially equipped children's corner for the kids, animators and rescuers look after the visitors.
