Heading to Budva

Budva is the historical center of a mysterious country with the beautiful name Montenegro. Last-minute tours from Minsk, which you can purchase by a lucky chance (anything can happen in life) will help you make a trip that you will definitely remember for a long time.

The town of Budva was founded almost 2.5 millennia ago and is perhaps the most ancient settlement on the Adriatic coast. Therefore, fans of antiquity and historical cultural sights will not be bored. In addition to educational excursions, in Budva there are many places where you can also have a good rest and have fun. Numerous restaurants and cafes, attracting the aromatic smells of delicious food and tart wine, casinos and hippodromes, sports fields and tennis courts - all this, of course, attracts crowds of tourists. Budva is a paradise for lovers of cognitive and outdoor activities.





Or maybe we fly to Becici?

Have you ever heard about the paradise of Becici? This is a place where you are sure to get an exceptional and memorable vacation. Montenegro (from Minsk by airliner it takes only two hours to fly there) will even seem to you to be the dearest and closest country.

What is interesting about this unknown land with such a beautiful name?

I must say that by choosing the village of Becici for living in the hospitable Montenegro, you can "kill two birds with one stone." The first is that the place is located only three kilometers from the famous Budva. Thus, you can visit all its places of interest and gambling establishments. By the way, you can get there by unhurried steps along the seaside boulevard or on a small "road train" plying between the settlements. And secondly, settle in a quieter and quieter corner, without even moving far from the center of civilization.





Becici is famous for its small pebble beach, which extends for almost three kilometers. In a competition held in Paris in 1936, he even won the Grand Prix as the best place on the coast in all of Europe. Water skiing is very popular among vacationers, the installation for which is located in the western part of this beach. The largest hotels of the Budva Riviera, restaurants, numerous cafes and bars - all this is adequately presented here for vacationers.

Another well-known and beloved holiday-maker's place of residence during the beach season is the small settlement Rafailovici, also very close to Budva.

Petrovac. Have you flown?

Are you a supporter of a cozy family and romantic getaway? There is a very successful route for you - Petrovac (Montenegro). Departure from Minsk - at any time at the request of the customer. And again, if you pay attention to the distance to the same Budva - the center of historical culture - it will be only 17 kilometers. But Petrovac itself differs from the named corners of this country in its natural marvelous beauty. Here you can not only bask under the gentle rays of the southern sun, have fun on water rides and admire amazing landscapes, but also go on a guided tour on two historical islands - Katic and Holy Week, in the Museum of Roman Mosaic, where you can feel the spirit of ancestors who lived in the IŒôI century BC.
