How to make bubo from yarn: types, description, photo

Round fluffy pompons are a traditional decoration for many knitwear. They are attached to the top of the cap, to the ends of scarves, to mittens, sweaters, pullovers and other items of clothing. Especially popular balabons made of thread are used by knitters who are engaged in the manufacture of children's products and crafts. Knowing how to make bubo from yarn, you can not only decorate classic products, but also craft soft toys, sofa cushions, rugs, bedspreads or other elements of interior decor.



Bubon Formation Methods

Almost all methods of manufacturing pompons consist in repeatedly winding threads on some basis with the subsequent cutting of the formed loops. Based on what should be the volume of the finished balabon, its size and color, the craftswoman can choose one of several options:

  • The formation of a large fluffy pompom.
  • Making a medium-sized and small jabber.
  • Simultaneous creation of several identical elements.
  • Crochet a pompom.

A simple method of making bubo from yarn

This method should be considered first of all, since it can be safely called universal. For work, you need such materials:

  • Cardboard.
  • Sharp scissors.
  • Selected yarn.
  • Needle with a wide eye.

As a basis for winding threads, two circles of thick cardboard will be used. Their diameter should exceed the planned size of the finished product by 20-30%. It should be noted that the shape of the circles must be correct, otherwise you will have to spend a lot of time and material to align the shortcomings of the pompom.

Inside each large circle, draw and then cut a hole. It should be placed exactly in the center of the workpiece.

How to make a bubo with your own hands: the main technique

Then you can proceed to the main stage: combining the blanks and winding threads on them. To do this, use a crochet hook or needle.

Some experienced craftswomen recommend cutting cardboard blanks from the outer edge to the central hole before making a bubo from yarn. Due to this, it is possible to form coils from threads directly from the skein without cutting off individual fragments.

Continue winding until yarn layers close the center hole. As a rule, for an element of medium size (eight centimeters) you will need about 20 grams of material. This is a mandatory measure to create a truly voluminous and magnificent bubo, as in the photo below.



Shutdown

When enough yarn is wound on the rings, it should be carefully cut, inserting a scissor blade between the two warp discs. No need to immediately open the rings. It is only necessary to slightly spread them apart and intercept with a strong thread all layers of threads that are between them. The pompom is almost ready, you only need to shake it well and cut it, setting the desired shape.

That's the whole technology that describes how to make a bubo from threads.

Create smaller products

If you need to make small pompoms, working with cardboard circles will be inconvenient. Alternative methods exist:

However, this is a fairly quick way to make bubo from yarn.

2.To get pompons of a very small size, winding the threads should be on the teeth of the fork. Its shape is very convenient for intercepting and fixing wound layers.

To do this, a strong thread, selected as a tie, is threaded between the teeth, pulled and tied to a knot. After cutting, such pompons are very fluffy and have a fairly high density.

Mass production

In cases where you need several pompons of the same color and size, it makes sense to apply the conveyor technology of their production. To do this, you need to wind a sufficient number of layers of yarn between strong vertical supports (often the legs of a table or chair cope with this role).

Next, the resulting strands are bandaged in several places with strong ties. Cut one long strand into several short ones so that the cut falls exactly in the middle between the two closest ties. Ready-made small pompons need to be cut and fluffed with your fingers.

Thinking about how to crochet a bubo, you can use the scheme for making a round part of an amigurumi toy. The finished sphere is stuffed with synthetic winterizer or other light material.



Possessing the skills of how to make a bubo from threads, any craftswoman will be able to create a very creative decor that will be an adornment of her house or a great present to a close friend.
