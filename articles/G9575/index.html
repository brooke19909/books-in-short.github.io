Train 463rd Samara - Adler: reviews, route and features

Residents of our country, including Samara, very often spend summer vacations on the Black Sea - in Lazarevsky, Loo, Sochi, Adler. Getting to these resort towns is the easiest and cheapest way, of course, by train. In the summer, there are many "southern" trains on the country's railways. Residents of the Middle Volga region who decide to go to the Black Sea often take a ticket, for example, on the train 463rd Samara - Adler. There are different reviews about this composition on the network - both good and not very good.

Description of composition

Most often, tourists who decide to spend their holidays in the resorts of the Black Sea coast of the Russian Federation, of course, purchase tickets for reserved seats in order to save money. But some people prefer to travel with great comfort - in a compartment. In summer, train No. 463 is composed of both varieties of wagons.





Not so long ago, the Russian government, together with the leadership of Russian Railways, decided to stop the production of old- class reserved cars . At first, they wanted to gradually replace them only with two-story ones, completely stopping the assembly of ordinary ones. However, this decision was later quashed. Today, the country produces both double-decker and simple reserved seats, wagons modified and adapted to modern requirements. The latter in our country so far, unfortunately, are collected in not too large numbers.

The train number 463 may include both new and old reserved seats. At the same time, passengers themselves are not provided with information about which of them sold tickets. The price of travel documents for old and new cars is no different. Meanwhile, travel conditions may vary significantly.

Departure / Arrival

Departing from Samara Station, train no. 463 at 16:55 Moscow time. On the route to his destination, it is 1 day 21 hours. In total, the train route ( 463rd) Samara - Adler includes 41 stations .





The final train arrives at 14:22, on the second day of the trip. In Adler, his maintenance is carried out , after which he goes back to Samara. In this case, the train is assigned the number 464 . The approximate distance in a straight line between the starting and ending stations on the route of this train is 1418 km.

Ticket price

There are travel documents for the train 463rd Samara - Adler, reviews about the journey in which (and not too good ones) many passengers leave online, not too cheap. Recently, the prices for the passage of Russian Railways, according to many citizens of Russia, are too high . And in particular this applies to the summer time of the year. It was during this period that the largest influx of passengers of the railway transport was observed.

The cost of traveling on the 463rd / 464th Samara-Adler train depends , of course, primarily on the time spent on the way (that is, on the distance between the departure and arrival stations). Also, the type of wagon affects the price of the travel document. In a compartment, the trip will cost much more.

So what exactly is the ticket for the 463rd Samara-Adler train? The price of travel documents to a reserved seat car of this train in the summer of 2016 was approximately 2,700 rubles . A trip in the compartment of this train could be bought for 4700 rubles, that is, almost twice as expensive. What will be the fare in the composition in the summer of 2017 and in subsequent years, of course, is still unknown. Most likely, travelers will need to pay more.





When is the best time to take a ticket?

So, the prices for train No. 463 Samara - Adler can be considered quite high . The cost of travel in this train depends , however, not only on the type of car and travel time, but also on exactly when the passenger acquires the travel document. Experienced travelers are advised to buy tickets for this train as early as possible - for 40-45 days. In this case, the passenger will most likely be able to get even a small discount on travel. Also, ordering tickets early, the traveler has the opportunity to choose the most convenient place for himself in the car.

If a passenger does not take care of purchasing a travel document in advance, it may happen that there will not be any available seats on the train. Of course, in this case, the tourist will be offered to purchase a ticket for a later date. However, this option for passengers traveling to the Black Sea may not always be acceptable. After all, hotel rooms in resorts are usually reserved in advance. Therefore, the lack of seats on the train for a certain number can lead, among other things, to financial losses.

Meanwhile, in some cases, such an unpleasant situation can be avoided by slow passengers quite easily. Additional cars are attached to train No. 463, usually at the very last moment. Therefore, tickets for this train at the box office can suddenly appear in a day or two from the time of departure. In any case, it is necessary to check the possibility of acquiring a ticket on the day of departure by leisurely vacationers.

Future passengers of train No. 463 should not neglect the recommendations given above . Even a month before the day of departure of tickets at the box office for this train may already be very small. The "Soviet South" among Russians has recently become in fact a most popular holiday destination.

Reviews about the service in old reserved seats and coupe

Waiting for special conveniences on the road to holidaymakers who bought tickets for the 463rd Samara - Adler train, reviews of old wagons of which are mostly only bad in the network , of course, is not worth it.
