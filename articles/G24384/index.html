Hotel Cleopatra Luxury Resort 5 *, Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh: overview, description, features and reviews

It's no secret that for our compatriots Egypt is perhaps the most popular tourist destination along with Turkey. The Russians are especially fond of the mysterious and fashionable Sharm el-Sheikh, whose name already sounds something east-intriguing and luxurious.

But rest, even in the most heavenly place, can be spoiled by poor quality staff and a poor hotel. Therefore, the choice of housing should be approached more than responsibly. What is necessary for a quality and comfortable stay? The issue is purely individual, but in general terms it can be said that this should be the perfect combination of price, quality, location and the required infrastructure. One of the popular hotels among compatriots is Cleopatra Luxury Resort Sharm el Sheikh 5 *, we will talk about it and a little about its analogue in Hurghada.





Hotel location

Hotel guests can use the shuttle to her daily upon prior request.

One of the main advantages of Cleopatra Luxury Resort 5 * is its location on the first line of the coast, so its guests can get to the comfortable and clean private beach of the Red Sea in the Sinai Peninsula in a few minutes. The hotel has an impressive territory of 100,000 sq.m. with well-developed infrastructure.

Hotel Description

The entire hotel complex consists of the main building and separately located clusters, a total of 324 rooms, as well as a spacious pool, diving and fitness center, a large conference room, 4 bars, 4 restaurants, a night club, a tennis court and a children's play area. All rooms and apartments are decorated in a modern style, equipped with the most appliances. The color background is comfortable and unobtrusive: white-blue textile and wooden trim, which gives the atmosphere of nobility and charm.

Restaurants on site

The main establishment is The Market Restaurant & Terrace. He is ready to offer hotel guests not only a sumptuous dinner, but also all-inclusive breakfasts and dinners. The buffet and the abundance of dishes not only of national oriental cuisine, but also of European ones, will allow you to enjoy your meal. Breakfast is served on the terrace right on the open-air beach.





The hotel can also often be found under the name Cleopatra Luxury Resort Collection 5 *. This is due to its complex architectural solution and a large number of objects on the territory. Visiting the sea and not tasting its gifts would be a big mistake, so you should definitely pay attention to the second restaurant - Red Sea Wharf. Delicious dishes made from the freshest seafood will delight even the most sophisticated gourmet. And the Fellini restaurant, as the name implies, offers visitors Italian cuisine. You need to keep in mind that both of them work a la carte, that is, for an additional fee.

Excellent meat and vegetables will be served by the Beach Grill restaurant located on the territory of the hotel overlooking the waters of the Red Sea. In addition to the main dishes, they offer tea, coffee and delicious sweets.

All restaurants have a special children's menu.

Hotel Bars

There are many places for a relaxed and relaxing stay in the hotel, including four bars. Each of them is special and has indescribable color and charm. So, near the pool you can enjoy refreshments and cocktails, as they say, without departing from the ticket office in Central Park or in Villa, where everything else is served exquisite ice cream of various varieties and types. And in the Pilli Pilli Pub, in an exceptional atmosphere, you can play billiards and try a real oriental hookah - a multifunctional and interesting place.





End the evening in the Cleopatra Luxury Resort Collection 5 * at a disco in a nightclub, enjoying modern music and dancing. Or you can enjoy sunset and local drinks or foreign drinks (for a fee) in the Piano Lounge - this is a nice lobby bar with a spacious terrace and views of the coast.

Sports and Entertainment

No matter how comfortable and relaxed exclusively the beach passive rest, any tourist will sooner or later want something interesting and new. And in the case when there is no desire to leave the hotel, you can turn to the services provided by the Cleopatra Luxury Resort 5 * themselves.

The most important place on vacation is the sea and the pool. With the first, everything is clear - the hotel is located on the first line and the beach is within walking distance. There will be no problem with pools either, especially since there are two of them. Umbrellas, sunbeds and towels near them are provided absolutely free, although they are not always free, so you need to take care and leave the room a little earlier. Pools Cleopatra Luxury Resort Sharm 5 * impress with their size: open - 900 square meters. m, with heated water - 1600 square meters. There is enough space for all guests, and for children there is a special department.

Here, every day and in the company of professional instructors, you can do water gymnastics (aqua aerobics), which has a beneficial effect on the whole body and especially the musculoskeletal system. For those who can not imagine life without sports, there is a free spacious fitness center.





But for water activities, diving courses and dives will have to pay extra. However, for children snorkeling, that is, diving with a mask, is free, as well as equipment rental for this.

health and beauty

The hotel is not in vain called the Cleopatra Luxury Resort 5 *: in addition to entertainment, it also offers services to maintain health and beauty. Women will especially enjoy their own spa. Massage (regular and water) is a good addition to a sauna or a real Turkish hammam.

Leisure and sightseeing programs

Many tourists who visited this hotel respond positively to the work of its animators and note rich entertainment programs in the evenings. There is plenty of space for this: our own amphitheater, Waves Club Privee (night club). Numerous entertainment programs will not let vacationers get bored. Fans of a combination of beach and sightseeing holidays also have something to offer. Domestic and local tour operators will be happy to take you on a wide variety of excursions. Including the historical and cultural center of Egypt - Cairo and Alexandria, the majestic pyramids and the Sphinx, Luxor and the monastery of St. Catherine, Jerusalem and Jordan.

Guest reviews on location and infrastructure

I would like to note right away that Cleopatra Luxury Resort 5 * (Sharm El Sheikh) has a generally positive reputation among tourists, but you can often find criticism against it. Hotel guests who have been there on vacation unanimously note its excellent location (near the sea and the airport), which is perhaps the most important plus. Particularly emphasize the cleanliness and comfort of the beach. The entry into the sea is quite small, and you will not be able to see the marine inhabitants easily like that, but everything is calm and there is a pontoon from which you can plunge into the water without any problems.





Guests of Cleopatra Luxury Resort 5 * have different reviews on the quality of service and rooms, on average rating it at 3-4 on a scale of 5 points. Despite the declared luxury, there are shortcomings and shortcomings. So, according to some guests, the hotel seems to be unfinished: here and there there are some imperfections - the tile falls off, the tap leaks, the furniture is ragged, etc. But in general they do not spoil the overall impression.

Well appreciated the work of animators and numerous recreational activities that do not allow you to get bored. The reviews also positively note the large territory of the hotel and its well-groomed, as well as spacious pools for every taste. As some say, throughout the rest you can not even go to other places.

About food and restaurants

Hotel guests note the good quality of food offered on an all-inclusive basis. It is tasty, fresh, not monotonous, but without any frills. You should definitely try the fried eggs for breakfast, which is prepared right in front of your eyes and with the ingredients that you prefer to choose. And also amazing (judging by the reviews) pastries and sweets. Gourmets are recommended for a fee to visit the restaurant, which serves seafood and an Italian cafe with excellent pizza.

Summing up and based on the reviews, vacationing at Cleopatra Luxury Resort Sharm 5 *, you can safely evaluate the hotel for a solid four, however, you have to doubt the rest of the VIP class declared by tour operators and owners of the complex.
