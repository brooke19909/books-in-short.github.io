How to make paper airplanes? Walkthrough

Origami is a great art, but nothing is simpler than a paper airplane. On this page you will find a detailed explanation of how to make paper airplanes that can fly long and high.



You need:

  1. A sheet of paper of standard size. It is better if it is dense, but thinner than cardboard.
  2. Stapler or glue.

If you plant the paper model correctly, you will see aerobatics! It is not surprising that for many children and adults the most interesting game is a paper airplane.

Stage 1

Fold a sheet of paper in half along, like a book, and open it. You should form a fold line in the center.



Stage 2

Gently fold the opposite top corners so that they align as shown in the picture.



Stage 3

Fold the resulting triangle with the tip down and align its top with the fold line so that it looks like a tall envelope.



Stage 4

This step of the lesson on how to make paper airplanes is one of the most difficult. Fold a pair of upper corners to the central axis of symmetry about one centimeter above the tip of the triangle.



Stage 5

Wrap the top of the triangle over the bent corners. Smooth the fold lines.



Stage 6

Fold the future airplane along the very first fold line as on the sample.



Stage 7

Fold the wings so that the height of the body of the paper airplane is equal to one centimeter.



Stage 8

Fold the flanks of both wings to the height of one centimeter in the same way as shown in the photo.

Step 9

Finally, the moment came to the final part of the lesson on how to make paper airplanes. Just glue the body of the aircraft or fasten the fuselage with a stapler so that it retains its desired shape during the flight.



Step 10

Proceed to the most pleasant - tests!

Advice:

  1. The main thing now is the correct launch. For the model to make a dead loop in the air , point the glider nose up and push it forward with force. The airplane will cheerfully outline an invisible circle and further it will become simple to plan. Two corners, bent along the flanks of the wings, did their job!
  2. It is convenient to launch such an airplane design from an elevation, then it will be leveled and longer plan down in the air stream.
  3. One corner of the wing can be left unchanged, and the second bent down. After such a transformation, your test plane will be able to get into a tailspin! A stream of oncoming air will inevitably pick it up and, like a spinner, spin it around the axis of symmetry.

Now you are no longer faced with the question of how to make paper airplanes. The plane you have made is a real conqueror of hearts of all generations and peoples! However, most likely, this paper airplane model is not your last.
