Circus of Kiev "Kobzov"

The Kiev circus called "Kobzov" was founded by a famous artist in Ukraine - Nikolay Kobzov. This is one of the country's brightest big tops. He gives his ideas in a collapsible tent.



general information

Circus "Kobzov" (Kiev) has already presented its show program in many cities of Ukraine. At the same time, he managed to win the respect of such foreign experts in the field of circus art as Dirk Quick, Urs Pils, Fabio Montico, Istvan Christoph and Genis Matabos.

The scale of the big top impress even experienced specialists in this matter. It is designed for 1300 seats. The height of the mobile tent is twenty-two meters, and the diameter is forty-four. To ensure smooth operation, 95 people were involved. Three hundred-ton equipment moves around the cities and towns a caravan of 35 wagons, 89 trucks and buses. Kyiv Circus "Kobzov" is equipped with professional-grade lighting equipment. In addition, he boasts a powerful Dolby Surround sound system, comfortable armchairs and modern bathrooms. This circus tent (Kiev, Troyeshchina and Borshchagovka) differs from other similar projects by a team of professional actors from different European countries, the best directors, a huge number of interesting exhibitions, an abundance of animals, amazing show ballet and unexpected surprises from the organizers.

Viva

The Kobzov Circus (Kiev) is famous for its main program called Vivat. This show is striking in its scale. The best Ukrainian and foreign choreographers and directors were invited to create it. In addition, each number is complemented by a memorable performance of a show ballet. Costumes of artists are very bright and expensive. Viewers think that they got to a real carnival. We list what awaits those who want to plunge into this magical world:

- performances of brave equilibrists;

- show of acrobats;

- attraction with bears and programs with many other animals;

- numbers with tigers and lions from E. Farina - the world star of Italian dress-up;

- incredible choreographic performances;

- a show created by the champion of Ukraine on a bicycle trial;

- Clockwork clowns that can make anyone laugh.



Team members

Circus of Kiev "Kobzov" exists thanks to a close-knit team of professionals in their field. These selfless people spend most of their lives in a big top. The opportunity to see relatives is extremely rare. It is not at all surprising that many are looking for a mate within the team, and not in the "big world".

It is worth noting that artists have to face a variety of difficulties in the process of moving to their next destination. Already on the spot, they should install everything, prepare the big top for the arrival of the audience, conduct exhausting hours of training and give people a holiday, despite the enormous fatigue.



Circus of Kiev "Kobzov" has an excellent troupe. It is formed including from world stars. Among them, Tatyana Belova - a balancer, who has repeatedly received prizes at international festivals, Vladislav Chernysh - two-time champion of Ukraine in cycling trials, as well as many others.

Senior Pet

Emmanuel Farina is the jewel of the Vivat program. This Italian trainer gained worldwide fame not only because he became a multiple winner of various festivals. Farina gave the Pope an unusual gift - a lion cub. Benedict XVI, of course, accepted the gift, blessed the animal and asked the circus artists to look after him properly. Since then, the miracle beast has been the talisman of Kobzov.

The performances of the charismatic Italian are particularly popular. Emmanuel does not get tired of promoting love for animals. Each of his numbers is unpredictable, because the artist loves improvisation.

Something for adults

Madness is a provocative show representing a new trend in circus art. It makes one wonder, be enthusiastic and recharge with genuine emotions. Madness Circus is a show that delights in the soul and horror in the eyes.

The project is based on the deepest meaning: humanity has become indifferent, lowered its hands and lost its spirituality. In a constant pursuit of material values, natural wealth gradually and irrevocably disappears. Today we do not find time to consider the world around us. Thanks to the artists, beasts, magicians and show ballet, the audience is involved in a unique atmosphere of passion, crazy energy and happiness.





Multiland

The smallest viewers also expect a surprise from the organizers of the show. At the entrance to the tent they are greeted by funny life-size puppets. In order to play with the children, the ninja turtles, Masha and the Bear, the squirrel from the Ice Age, the Winx fairies, the Bob Sponge and the characters from the cartoon "Madagascar" came off the TV screens. Everyone can take pictures and play with their beloved hero (no fee will be charged).

Zoo, terrarium

Visitors to big tops have the opportunity to familiarize themselves with how they contain different animals. The local zoo has become home to eighty species of animals. As for the terrarium, it presents thirty species of reptiles.



Conclusion

Want to visit an unusual big top? Come to Kiev! The circus (address - Heroes of Cosmos St., 4 or Mayakovsky Ave. 52) in the capital of Ukraine will help to get a lot of new experiences and have fun from the heart.
