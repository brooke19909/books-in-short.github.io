How to make a bow out of a ribbon correctly?

If balls are used to decorate large-scale celebrations, then ribbons are still used as decorations for small banquet rooms, apartments and houses. This is due to the fact that it is almost impossible to make any large decoration from a ribbon, because the material is delicate and is not able to maintain shape without support. Only in the case of using the frame can a large tape be made. In other cases, designers prefer to work with ribbons of small sizes, because it is easy, fast and very simple. Therefore, today we‚Äôll talk about how to make a bow out of a satin ribbon, with which you can decorate something in the house or banquet hall.



Many women like to sew dresses, blouses and tops. Instructions on how to make a bow out of a ribbon may come in handy. After all, such a bow will be a wonderful decoration, for example, for a new fashionable summer outfit. In any case, such tips are quite valuable, because you not only save money on buying bows, but you can also make them in accordance with your personal preferences. In addition, advice on how to make a bow out of a ribbon can be useful in making gift wrapping. We buy gifts regularly. And in order for your present to be the most beautiful and unusual, you need to make the original packaging. You can also pack a gift in the store. But why spend money if, with the help of tips on how to make a bow out of a ribbon, you can decorate the wrapper yourself?


So let's get started. We make a bow from the ribbon in accordance with the specific case for which you will need it. Materials must be selected appropriate. Consider the simplest options - satin (for attaching to fabric) and paper (for decorating gift wrapping) ribbons. The technologies are identical, only it is necessary to remember that if you work with fabric, you may also need pins, and if with paper - ordinary PVA glue.

You can make a bow in several ways:

  1. A bow with two "ears". This is the most common bow with which you can decorate both gift wrapping and dress. To do this, make two "ears" in the tape. Then we tie them together and tie a bow. For sure, every student knows about this method, because there is nothing complicated in it. Next, we straighten our bow so that the "ears" become magnificent.
  2. Lush bow. This is already a more complicated way of making a bow out of a ribbon. But do not despair. This bow is most suitable for gift wrapping, you probably saw it. To make it, attach the center of the ribbon to the paper, and then start folding it in different directions to make loops. We fix them in the position that we prefer - some make a very magnificent bow, others attach corners to the end of the paper. When making such a bow, you need to remember that the wrong side of the ribbon should be inside. Then your bow will turn out beautiful, glossy and will give the gift the most elegant look.
