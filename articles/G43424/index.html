Laura Beach 4 *. Paphos Hotel Cyprus

Cyprus deservedly received the status of one of the best holiday destinations in the world. The main feature for the Russian tourist, who puts this resort one step above the others, is a simplified visa regime. It is this parameter that helps travelers quickly get to the fabulous island. Of course, the priority and important is the choice of hotel for your stay. So, in the territory of the popular resort of Cyprus - Paphos - there is a huge number of hotels of all categories of services. One of them is Laura Beach 4.

Hotel description: This establishment is located on the golden coast, washed by the azure waters of the Mediterranean Sea. Even though the hotel is located in the center of the island's resort life, which is in full swing in Paphos, the atmosphere of Laura Beach 4 is filled with calm and silence. The location of the hotel is very good - a few kilometers away are the attractions of the resort, a huge number of clubs, discos, restaurants and cafes. A distinctive feature of the hotel is the recent restoration, bringing the institution closer to the rank of the most comfortable and cozy hotels on the island.





Rooms: After the reconstruction, Laura Beach 4 has increased the area of ​​residence, and today the buildings consist of 309 rooms. Most of them are standard rooms that can accommodate a maximum of three adults. Fantastic sea or mountain views can be enjoyed from the balcony of this room. The minibar located in the apartments will quench your thirst with soft drinks, and climate control will create comfortable conditions for relaxation even in the most ruthless heat or cold. A comfortable and equipped bathroom will help to remove fatigue. A TV with satellite channels will entertain visitors with interesting programs.

In addition to standard rooms, Laura Beach 4 has bungalows. These comfortable apartments have garden or sea views. The living area of ​​the house - the living room and bedroom - are separated from each other by a partition. In addition to bungalows, presidential suites, executive suites and studios are available.





Meals: “All Inclusive” - the main restaurant Laura Beach 4 is based on this system . Cyprus, like any other high-class resort, offers this system in almost every hotel on the island. This principle implies that from morning to late evening, an unlimited choice of food, snacks and drinks (both alcoholic and non-alcoholic) is available to visitors.





Beach: Of course, the main attraction that attracts a huge number of holidaymakers to Cyprus are the stunning beaches washed by the gentle waters of the Mediterranean Sea. It is this coastline that is located in the immediate vicinity of the Laura Beach 4. The hotel’s beach strewn with golden sand is equipped with the attributes necessary for a comfortable stay: sun loungers, umbrellas and towels are provided for each guest.





Additional information: The hotel offers a variety of entertainment for its clients, which include lazy relaxation at the pool's edge, active sports (tennis, water polo), chess, animation for children and adults, and much more. Those who wish can use the services of a diving instructor to immerse themselves in the colorful underwater world of the Mediterranean Sea.

Reviews: Like any institution, this hotel has both fans and ardent opponents. Monotonous food is what visitors of Laura Beach 4 are mostly unhappy with. Customer reviews vary on average at 4.3 and 5 points. But the main characteristic that unites all positive odes is the calmness and the atmosphere of the hotel, which is favorable for relaxation.
