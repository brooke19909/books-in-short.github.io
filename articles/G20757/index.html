Hotel Deeden Pattaya Resort: reviews, descriptions, photos

Do you consider yourself a lowly tourist? Looking for a budget hotel in Pattaya (Thailand)? Are you afraid of the relative distance from the city center and the beach? Then be sure to consider the Deeden Pattaya Resort 3 * as an option for your stay.



Location

Diden Resort is located in South Pattaya. The city center can be reached in a quarter-hour by tuk-tuk, taxi or motobike. In the vicinity of the hotel there are cafes, bars, shops, supermarkets, exchange offices, grocery and clothing markets and much more. The distance to the nearest beach is 3 kilometers, you can get here for free using the shuttle service provided by the hotel.



Deeden Pattaya Resort 3 * (Pattaya): photos and description

Diden Resort was built in 2009. It is decorated in a traditional Thai style. Two years ago there was a partial renovation. The hotel consists of several buildings, accommodating 104 rooms of the categories of standard, superior and deluxe. The hotel also has a swimming pool, sun terrace, bar, cafe, parking, 24-hour reception, luggage room.

Territory

As our compatriots note, Deeden Pattaya Resort 3 *, the photo of which can be seen in this article, compares favorably with hotels in the center of Pattaya in that it has, albeit a small, but its own green area. Location

This item is perhaps the most hotly discussed by tourists who visited the "Diden Pattaya Resort". As already mentioned, the hotel is located on the very outskirts of the city at a sufficient distance from the center. Therefore, you have to get to the main entertainment places either by tuk-tuk with transfers, or by taxi. Walking turns pretty far. However, this location of the hotel largely determines the cost of living in it. After all, accommodation in the same hotel, but in the center of Pattaya, would cost an order of magnitude higher. In addition, many experienced travelers are advised to rent a motorbike. This will allow you not only to save on tuk-tuk and taxis, but also be able to go around all the surrounding beaches and visit the sights.



Nutrition

The cost of living in Diden Pattaya Resort includes breakfast only. They pass in a hotel cafe in a buffet format. In general, almost all guests were very pleased with their quality. So, in the morning you can eat fried eggs, scrambled eggs or boiled eggs, sausages, sausage, salad, Thai soup, fruits, vegetables, toast, juice, tea and coffee. Breakfast starts at 7:30. If you plan to go on an excursion early in the morning, then inform the reception in advance. You will have breakfast with you. Service, staff

As for the hotel employees, the guests were quite satisfied with them. Despite the fact that few here understand not only in Russian, but also in English, the employees always without problems understood what tourists want from them, and fulfilled their requests and wishes. And it was always done with a smile.

Did not cause any complaints and the cleaning of the rooms. True, guests pay attention that it is held here on demand, and not on schedule. So, if you want your number to be cleaned, then put a sign on the door. The maid will also bring fresh towels and change bed linen.



Check in

Check-in at the hotel in question is usually carried out immediately upon arrival. Therefore, even if you arrive at Diden Pattaya Resort early in the morning, you will most likely receive your room keys in a few minutes without having to wait for the check-out time. However, please note that upon check-in you will be asked to leave a deposit of 500 baht. Upon departure, it will be returned to you in full.

Sea

Since Diden Pattaya Resort is located relatively off the coast, guests are offered a free shuttle to Jomtien Beach. This service can be used from 9 to 17 hours, and a travel schedule is available at the reception. Jomtien Beach is a municipal beach. For the rental of sunbeds and umbrellas, you must pay. But you can always stay on a free section of the beach, laying a mat brought with you. By the way, towels can be taken for free at the reception. Many experienced tourists are advised to not only be limited to Jomtien and other beaches of Pattaya, but to visit the islands. Here the water is much thicker. You can get here by ferry departing from the pier of Pattaya.
