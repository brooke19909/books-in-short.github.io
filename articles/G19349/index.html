Borovets (ski resort, Bulgaria): reviews

Many winter sports enthusiasts are well acquainted with the Bulgarian town called Borovets. The ski resort located here is considered one of the best in the country. If you have not decided where to spend your Christmas holidays, we recommend reading our article. Perhaps it is Borovets (Bulgaria) that will become your home for some wonderful January days.

Winter resorts in Bulgaria

Every year, thousands of Russian tourists flock to this small hospitable country to spend their legal holidays by the sea. Summer days are filled with bliss, hot sun and fun activities. But eventful life does not stop in the country in the winter. Lovers of outdoor activities, professional athletes and vacationers with children are welcome in the cold months in cozy Bulgaria (ski resorts). Prices for services and accommodation here are much more affordable than in other European countries. Adventurous contemporaries have transformed the legacy of the Soviet past into comfortable, modern hotels, beautiful tracks and wonderful places for families.



Bulgaria, ski resort Borovets

If you are looking for a cozy place for a winter holiday, then perhaps you should look here. Picturesque landscapes, hospitality of local residents and a relaxed atmosphere will be remembered for a long time. The mild climate of Bulgarian nature will not disappoint fans of skiing, and a variety of routes will allow each tourist to choose entertainment at their discretion. Borovets (ski resort) welcomes guests from November to April and provides them with all the conditions for a good rest. Beginners can use the services of a professional trainer or instructor, parents can take their kids to kindergarten in the snow, and professionals can experience the most difficult tracks.



Town

Tourists are waiting for numerous hotels. Borovets offers quite comfortable hotels with varying degrees of "stardom". Each of them is famous for its high level of service, many have swimming pools, gyms, gyms for fitness and saunas. Also, tourists can settle in apartments or small cottage complexes. If you book in advance, then you can find pretty good deals - from 1500 rubles per day. Before choosing a hotel, you should carefully examine the area. Please note that some of them will take quite a while to get to the place of skiing. Here you can see many entertainment venues in which vacationers can spend the evening after a ski trip. Tourists prefer to dine not in hotels, but in local mehans (restaurants) and cafes. The taste of dishes of national cuisine, impressive portion sizes, excellent wines and low prices remain in the memory of Russian gourmets for a long time. In their free time from skiing, guests of the resort enjoy sledding and snowmobiling, visit the local ice rink and enjoy the surroundings as part of a panoramic helicopter tour. Borovets (ski resort) in the past was the hunting ground of the Bulgarian rulers, but now anyone can enjoy the fresh mountain air and the beauty of the surrounding nature. Knowledge of the history of this town gives a special piquancy to staying in it and significantly raises the self-esteem of vacationers. Do not forget that this resort is the largest in the oldest in the country. No wonder it was Borovets who hosted the Alpine International Ski Cup several times.



Trails

Three ski areas of the Borovets resort (Bulgaria) are united by a common ski lift system, which can be reached by free buses from all remote points of the town. The total length of the ski slopes is about 40 km; cross-country skiing trails are separately equipped. On each of these routes there is always a sign indicating the level of difficulty, so tourists can easily navigate and choose their own route. Skating in Sitnyakovo is suitable for beginners and intermediate enthusiasts, training for beginners is also provided here. Second-hand athletes will be interested in the second zone - the Hawk. It is considered the best, and it is on its tracks that competitions are held in the main ski disciplines. In Markujik, mostly intermediate and high level skiers ride. Professionals can try their hand at sites for slalom and for giant slalom. Good conditions are created for those who prefer cross-country skiing, snowboarding and ski jumping.

Tourists reviews

Russians usually remember with pleasure the holidays organized by hospitable Bulgaria. Ski resorts, prices and quality of services are rated quite high by them. Of course, it is difficult for this small country to compete with the rich European neighbors, but the prices for tickets and accommodation are quite cheap. Tourists note that the lack of a language barrier helps them to feel at home. Russian-speaking instructors can easily teach the intricacies of skiing for adults and children, and staff in hotels and restaurants can easily solve the difficulties. Most tourists agree that it is impossible for a person who has visited here at least once to forget the beauty of this region. Clean mountain air, evergreen forests, well-equipped ski slopes and the friendliness of the local population are just a few of the reasons why every guest wants to come back here again and again.
