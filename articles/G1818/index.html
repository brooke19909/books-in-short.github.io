Where to go in Tambov with friends and family?

The small but beautiful city of Tambov, located on the territory of the Russian Federation, is known for a large number of different attractions. However, this settlement is remarkable not only for cultural, but also for entertaining places, a visit to which will be interesting for people of any age. Let's look at the most interesting places in the city.

Extreme Center for Kids "Wings"

Where to go with children in Tambov? In the extreme center "Wings". Its vast territory includes several zones at once, each of which differs from the others. So, in one part there is a trampoline zone with parapets, designed for various tricks, in the second there is a climbing wall, in the third - an area with tatami, which includes inflatable attractions.





There is a fourth zone. This is a trampoline area with an obstacle course. Also in the center there is a separate recreation area. There, parents can sit while their children frolic and tumble.

Zoo them. G. R. Derzhavina

Where can I go with a child in Tambov? Of course, to the zoo. Probably a rare child who would not like animals. Moreover, not ordinary but absolutely unknown animals live in the zoo.





Representatives of predators, herbivores, as well as all kinds of birds, reptiles and monkeys live here. A trip to this place will be not only interesting for children and their parents, but will also be a useful lesson for kids.

Entertainment facility for children "Giraffe"

Entertainment centers, including a huge variety of games, will always remain one of the most popular places among children. This is not surprising, because in such an institution the child is able to spend the whole day discovering more and more hobbies. In the "Giraffe" installed more than 40 different video games, simulators and similar devices.

Entertainment center "Festival Park"

In this place there is an opportunity to relax not only for children but also for adults. Therefore, it is recommended to visit the institution together. After all, a common hobby unites family members, makes them closer to each other.

Embankment of the Tsna River

Where to go with a girl in Tambov? For example, on the embankment of the Tsna River. A magnificent view and a pleasant atmosphere can make even an ordinary walk a romantic date. Moreover, on the embankment there are a lot of various restaurants and cafes where you can relax and eat.

Cinemas of the city

Where to go in Tambov in the evening with your beloved? If a girl is a fan of new movies, then she should definitely be invited to the movies. In addition, several cinemas were opened in Tambov at once. In each of them show the latest news of films and cartoons. For example, very popular institutions are Cinema Star Rio and Kinomax-Watercolor.

Kartingdom

An interesting lesson, which is driving on maps (racing cars), will give any girl an unforgettable experience. Great speed and the spirit of competition will help lovers to have fun.

Entertainment center "Cube"





Where to go for fun in Tambov with friends? To the center called "Cube." In this establishment, a company of friends can compete in a game of bowling, fight in billiards or an American pool. Also in the center is a cozy cafe. Therefore, a pleasant pastime is simply guaranteed.

Karaoke club Time Club

Where to go with Tambov for those who like to sing? At the karaoke club Time Club. Singing lovers should definitely visit this place. After all, there are two halls in it at once, one of which is designed for a large company, and the other for a small one. This club has very high-quality equipment, as well as a large projector.

Paintball Club Factory

Here is the largest paintball field in the city, as well as a relaxation area. At the same time, the organizers offer the participants not only to choose a venue for the competition, but also to choose additional equipment that is issued there.

Laser tag club "Voynushka"

Where to go with Tambov with friends? All fans of science fiction are invited to visit the incredible laser tag. This place is essentially reminiscent of paintball. Only here in this game, participants are given special weapons that shoot safe lasers.

Atrium Nightclub

Incredible modern music, a comfortable VIP zone, as well as a large dance floor are waiting for new visitors to the club every day. Unusual interior and delicious dishes will help make the evening unforgettable.

Museums of the city





Where to go with Tambov for a cultural holiday? In the Tambov Regional Museum of Local Lore. This place regularly hosts exhibitions of various subjects. Therefore, it will be interesting to return to it again.

Also go to the motor vehicle museum. Interesting excursions and fascinating stories of guides await all tourists. Museum "Spring" will appeal to history buffs. After all, it is in it that they will be able to fully tell about the city and its past.





State Drama Theater

The theater has a varied program and the most famous performances that you can even go to with children, because it is very important to teach kids from an early age to culture.

In addition, in Tambov there are a lot of various sights and monuments. If travelers came to explore the city, then it is unlikely that they will have one day to get acquainted with them. Therefore, it is worth arriving at least three days, or better, five.
