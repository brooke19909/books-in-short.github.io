The best hotels of Kostroma: photos and reviews

In our article we want to discuss the best hotels of Kostroma. In this beautiful city there are quite a lot of places where guests of the city can stay, and it does not matter on what issues they came. Kostroma hotels are so diverse that they will satisfy the needs of not only tourists, but also business people.

Hotel Azimut

Azimut is a business hotel. Kostroma is a rather large city, and therefore every year it is visited by many guests, including many business people. The hotel complex is a good option for such people to live, as it has many opportunities for meetings, conferences, trainings, seminars and business meetings. A team of professionals works at the Azimut Hotel (Kostroma), which will help you perfectly prepare for an event of any level.

Number of rooms at the Azimut Hotel

The Azimut Hotel (Kostroma) has rooms of different levels:

  1. Double standard - comfortable apartments with shower, air conditioning and satellite TV.
  2. Superior has a larger area than a standard room.
  3. Standard Bungalow is a two-room apartment, made in a classic style. These rooms are located in separate cottages, they are ideal for accommodating not only families, but also friendly companies.
  4. Superior Bungalow - two-room apartments also located in cottages. These rooms have a bedroom, living room, dining area and extra beds.
  5. Bungalow apartments are rooms with a private sauna, summer terrace, living room, located in brick cottages.

Nutrition

Breakfast at the hotel is served in buffet style, and you can also order the best dishes of European and Russian cuisine in a specialized restaurant decorated in the original Russian style.



In addition, guests of the hotel can visit the BirStadt. The beer restaurant has an original interior and a large selection of different beers and dishes. In the banquet room "Azimut", made in a solemn classic style, wedding and other special events are held.

Guest reviews of the Azimut Hotel

The hotel is located on the outskirts of the city, and therefore more convenient for those traveling by car. The guests of the complex note good rooms in the hotel, very nice and polite staff, tasty and plentiful breakfasts in the Russian style.

Aristocrat (spa hotel)

The famous "Aristocrat" is a spa hotel. Kostroma boasts many beautiful hotel complexes, but Aristocrat is a premium hotel that has truly luxurious apartments and well-groomed territory. An institution of this level dispels the standard understanding of the hotel. Each guest here can feel like in a homely atmosphere of warmth and comfort.



Kostroma hotels are very diverse, but the Grand Hotel stands out among them with style, comfort and luxury. The interiors of the complex combine classic elements with modeling and forging. Hotel facilities

Grand Hotel (Kostroma) can offer its guests accommodation in the most luxurious apartments. The wonderful location of the complex immerses guests in the atmosphere of forests. While staying at the hotel, guests can enjoy the birds singing, forgetting about the usual noise of cars. Rooms can be booked directly on the hotel website, which is convenient and practical.

The complex has a private pool. Local staff serves guests quickly and efficiently, demonstrating knowledge of their business.

The hotel administration provides its guests with the opportunity not only to have a great time surrounded by tranquility and luxury, but also to organize and conduct business events of various levels (conferences, seminars, meetings). To do this, there is a conference room that can accommodate up to one hundred people.



The hotel's spa area welcomes visitors daily. Here, hotel guests can enjoy swimming in the outdoor and indoor pools, visit the hammam, jacuzzi and Finnish sauna, and massage treatments.

In addition, the hotel is preparing and conducting weddings. For such events in the arsenal of the hotel there is a wedding restaurant, made in the style of luxury with stucco gold decor and mirrors. The huge bright room amazes with its beauty and, as much as possible, is suitable for celebrations.

The hotel provides a service such as a "daily visit." In fact, this is a one-day subscription, which allows guests to spend time in the spa area, walk around the territory, have lunch (buffet), visit the gym, children's room.

Hotel Cruise

Hotel Cruise (Kostroma) offers its guests well-equipped rooms in a modern style of minimalism and comfort. The complex has apartments of the class: luxury, standard, comfort, standard with terrace.

The suites are equipped with a large double bed, have a spacious living room, which allows you to relax comfortably. The bathroom has a large bath. Rooms are 46 square meters.

The standard rooms are more modest in size (22 square meters).

Comfort apartments are equipped with panoramic views of the city landscape. First-class rooms are decorated in light colors, creating a cozy, but at the same time elegant atmosphere.

The hotel also has standard rooms with a terrace.



Guests of the Cruise can visit the eponymous restaurant with panoramic views of the Volga River. The restaurant chef offers visitors the best author's dishes. The hotel building has a unique location, thanks to which the windows of the restaurant offer impressive views of the historic part of Kostroma and the bridge over the river.

The hotel has its own free parking, rental of sports equipment, tour service. Guests of the complex can book a transfer to anywhere in the city.

Guest reviews "Cruise"

Volga

Hotel Volga (Kostroma) is a worthy place for guests of the city. The modern three-star hotel is located in the very center of Kostroma, which is very convenient for tourists. Public transport is located very close to the hotel, and the main city streets are within walking distance.



The number of rooms in the complex will satisfy even the most demanding guest. It offers one hundred fifty rooms of various categories, each of which is equipped with cable TV, wireless Internet and telephone.

Restaurant "Volga" is a complex designed for 250 people. In addition, there is a Russian hall for 150 people. On the roof of the restaurant is a summer terrace with 150 seats. The restaurant chef prepares delicious dishes for visitors. The high level of service in the restaurant, reasonable prices and a pleasant atmosphere, made this place popular not only among guests, it is also actively visited by residents of Kostroma.

The hotel administration provides services for organizing exhibitions, meetings, meetings, offering several conference rooms of various capacities, which have all the most necessary modern equipment.

Hotel guests note the high service and friendliness of the staff. Rooms with a beautiful view of the Volga are especially delighted. As for food, the restaurant serves very tasty dishes at very reasonable prices, breakfasts are organized as a "buffet". For guests of Kostroma, the Volga Hotel can be a good place to stay.

Hotel Golden Ring

The Golden Ring Hotel (Kostroma) was built on the banks of the Volga in 2006. It is designed for those people who value not only comfort, but also personalized service. The guests of the establishment can enjoy the views from the windows of their rooms, walk along the Volga embankment and at the same time are close to local attractions.



The hotel offers its guests 33 three good rooms of different categories (from standards to VIP apartments). The hotel has its own restaurant offering the best dishes of European cuisine.

Tourists who visited the Golden Ring Hotel speak very warmly of him. The hotel is really very beautiful and comfortable, but without undue pathos. It is located on the one hand in the center of the city, and on the other, on a quiet street away from the hustle and bustle, which makes it more attractive. The rooms are new and clean, they have refrigerators, minibars, bathrobes, slippers. In the hotel restaurant they cook very tasty and satisfying, at the same time they are pleasantly pleased with the prices; they are quite acceptable here, unlike many hotel establishments. The guests especially praise the staff of the Golden Ring. Polite and friendly employees always try to answer all questions and help.
