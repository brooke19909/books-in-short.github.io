How to make a Dunno costume

The time before the New Year is a series of pleasant troubles associated with the upcoming holiday. Buying gifts for loved ones and friends, pondering the menu, and, of course, children's matinees in kindergartens and schools. And you always want the child to look no worse than the others, which means it's time to think about a fancy dress. If you do not want to spend extra money on an outfit, then you can always do it yourself. With a bit of patience and skill, you get an outfit that you can never distinguish from a purchase.

What to choose

Among a huge number of different fairy-tale characters, it is always difficult to make your choice in favor of this or that beloved hero. This article will talk about the costume of Dunno. Everyone will probably remember such an ambiguous hero of the famous writer Nosov, as well as his memorable clothes.

Easiest option

For tailoring the original costume, you can use those elements of clothing that the child already has. An orange shirt is perfect, which, however, can be replaced with a t-shirt. Pants or shorts should be yellow. To the outfit is fully consistent with the description, you must not forget to take care of the suspenders, they must also be yellow.

To complete the image, there is not enough green tie and the same socks.

As an ideal material, felt can be used that holds its shape well. When sewing a hat, you need to remember that the edges must be hard. Therefore, it is worth using a cardboard frame. Also, an unnecessary hat, which has large fields, will perfectly serve as the basis. It is enough to sheathe the costume detail with a blue cloth.

What other options are there

For tailoring a Dunno suit, any plain fabrics of bright colors are suitable. It is completely optional to look for a pattern of a shirt and pants, you can sew according to the model of those things that a child already has.

When sewing details of a New Year's costume, it is worth considering that the cut should be free, so that all things look a little larger than they should actually be. This method will allow the child to look more like a fairy-tale hero.

As an additional detail, you can buy a yellow wig, it was of that color that Dunno's hair was. Or sew on the bottom of the hat pieces of yellow fabric that will mimic the hair.

So, as you can see from the article, making a Dunno costume with your own hands is not as difficult as it seems.
