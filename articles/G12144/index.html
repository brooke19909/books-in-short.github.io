Children's photographer Karina Kil

Every day, billions of photographs are taken in the world. Since 2000, when the first phone with a built-in camera appeared, people began to shoot much more often. A huge number of people have a desire to become photographers. However, this profession is not as easy as it might seem. There are few real professionals. The individual style and originality of photographs is especially appreciated. The article is dedicated to Karina Kiel - a talented and wonderful person, a professional in her field.

Biography of photographer Karina Kiel

Karina Kiel is a family and children's photo artist. She lives in Russia, in Sochi. She is the mother of two sons. Children are what inspire her to be creative. Karina loves to create fabulous shots and completely immerse herself in a creative atmosphere. Sometimes it seems that she has unlimited imagination, and this can easily be seen in any of her works.

What inspired the photographer Karina Kiel to do her work professionally?

She came to the photograph through her sons. Every mother wants children to have good and high-quality pictures. So Karina had a desire to capture indescribable children's emotions, to keep as a memory the moments of growing up guys. Since this all started.

Master classes

In order to become a professional in this matter, you need to constantly learn something new and update knowledge. That is why the master classes of masters remain very popular today. Karina successfully conducts large-scale educational classes in the production of children's photography, as well as online courses on image processing. Moreover, she often conducts broadcasts in her social networks, where she answers questions and tells how to improve her skills. She also hosted online meetings to process her students' images.

Photographer Karina Kiel travels the world very often and holds master classes and shoots in different parts of the world.

As long as you are interesting to yourself, while you are internally filled, while you have something to share with people, you will be interesting.

Karina advises never to stop there, move on and develop skills. If you work on yourself every day, then everything will work out.
