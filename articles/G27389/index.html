Water parks in the Krasnodar Territory. The best water park (Krasnodar Territory)

Today, many people prefer to spend hot summer days in water parks. They are built in large and small cities, at resorts and in places far from the sea.



According to experts of these institutions, the best of them are concentrated in the Krasnodar Territory. Today we will try to make a short trip to the most famous and popular water parks in this region.

"Golden Bay"

If you want to visit the largest water park in the Krasnodar Territory, then you should go to Gelendzhik. Here you can see the largest building of this type.

The Golden Bay complex began to receive guests in 2004. Every year it is developing rapidly, winning more and more fans. The construction is one of the five largest water parks in Europe. Its area is more than 15 hectares. It has 70 descents of different heights, 49 slides, 17 pools, 10 original attractions. On the territory there is a huge restaurant, pizzeria, cafe. People who first came here are struck by the unique Golden Bay construction - an aquapark (Krasnodar Territory), built in the style of mixing countries of the world and eras.



Fans of extreme relaxation will appreciate the Kamikaze hill. True, not everyone dares to go down from it - the height of the structure is 25 meters! Here you can play water polo or beach volleyball, swim in one of the two pools (one of them has an area of ‚Äã‚Äã900 sq. M). There are pools with a circular and longitudinal wave. For younger guests there are children's slides, pools and attractions.

Hippopotamus (Gelendzhik)

One hundred meters from the sea is another popular water park. Krasnodar Territory is famous for its entertainment complexes, which are deservedly popular.

Hippopotamus works from the beginning of May until the end of October. It offers vacationers - three pools, seventeen slides. Five slides and a shallow pool were built for children. In addition, you can leave your child in a children's town under the supervision of an experienced trainer-instructor. Kids are entertained by funny clowns. A special menu has been developed for young vacationers in the Safari children's cafe.





Adults are invited to the Hummingbird beer bar. Here you can taste original cocktails or beer, of which there are more than 20 varieties. If you are hungry, you can satisfy your hunger at the Hungry Hippo or Limpopo cafe. In this water park (the only one in the Krasnodar Territory), non-cash payment for services is carried out.

AquaLoo

By the number of entertainment complexes Gelendzhik is not inferior to Sochi. Here you can also choose a good water park. Krasnodar Territory is proud of its largest resort. Here, a lot of work is being done to improve the quality of leisure for citizens and guests from all over our country.

The AquaLoo complex includes a sanatorium, a boarding house and a luxurious water park. This is the only building in the city, open to visitors all year. There are two zones: summer and winter. Water temperature +28 degrees. In pools for children, it is higher: +30 degrees. In the off-season in the open area, the water is heated.

At AquaLoo, all guests undergo mandatory medical supervision. 4 times a day, a water analysis is performed. There are 10 slides of different lengths. The longest of them is the Space Bot (145 m). A little shorter pipe "Rafting" (110 m), which descend on rubber circles.

In the central part of the territory an excellent children's town is built. In the shallow pool of children, various slides await.

Waterpark Lermontovo (Krasnodar Territory)

This is a famous resort village, the main attraction of which are the mountains and the sea. Here you can admire the magnificent nature - mountain valleys covered with relict forests, caves and waterfalls.

In the center of the village is a magnificent water park "Chernomor". It is located in the hotel of the same name. This is not a very large complex, but both adults and children can have a good rest.

The main attractions of the Chernomor are, of course, a variety of slides.



"Olympia"

In the suburbs of Anapa, in the village of Vityazevo, in 2012 a water park was opened. The Krasnodar Territory has such a number of entertainment centers that, perhaps, this event has already surprised few. And completely in vain. This is one of the few thematic water parks in Russia. It is decorated in the Greek style. You are given a unique opportunity to visit Ancient Greece. You will be able to watch the competitions of the ancient Olympians, see the amazing heroes of myths and legends.

Olympia has several independent zones. These are the Poseidon pool, the Hades and Zeus complexes, the Espizium children's town.

"Equator"

Today we are considering water parks in the Krasnodar Territory, photos of which are presented in this article. It would probably be unfair not to talk about such a structure located in Krasnodar itself.

Adult visitors can ride from nine slides of different heights. The most popular of them is Wave. It creates the effect of free fall from a considerable height - 10 meters. At rafts you can go down the Black Hole - a closed pipe. It has a length of 100 meters, equipped with lighting effects.



Kids with pleasure master the slides "Children's Tobogan" (4 m), "Zero Gravity" and "Rainbow".

All summer in this water park entertaining events are held - corporate parties, graduation parties, and even weddings. In the evening, you can take part in a foam party or a disco.
