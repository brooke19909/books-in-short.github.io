"Signalman" recreation center: overview, location, contacts and reviews of vacationers

The nature of the Leningrad region is beautiful and diverse. There are many places where you can relax from the bustle of the city. Built in this region and a huge number of convenient recreation facilities. One of these complexes is the Signalman. This recreation center offers its guests, judging by the reviews, a pretty good service.

Location Features

This complex is located on the Karelian Isthmus, not far from the village of Petrovsky. Its buildings were built on the shores of a picturesque lake, next to a wide sandy beach. From all sides the base is surrounded by a dense pine forest. The distance from the Svyazist complex to St. Petersburg is only about 70 km. You can get here by car in just an hour and a half.





You can, of course, get to this complex by train. She leaves at 16:23 from Finland Station. You should get off the train at the Sosnovo station. The "Signalman" base itself provides its customers with a shuttle service. The bus of the complex departs from the station square at 18:20. You can find him in the parking lot at the stop of route number 1. There is a shuttle bus to the base daily.

The exact address of this complex is as follows: recreation center "Signalman", Priozersky district, s. Petrovskoe. There are no big cities or factories near this tourist center.

Initially, the Svyazist recreation center belonged to the St. Petersburg Telephone Network (later Rostelecom). Mostly the employees of this organization went on vacation here on preferential vouchers. Subsequently, the company sold the base. Therefore, today anyone can get here.

Number of rooms

Residents of the Leningrad Region who decide to relax at this camp site can rent here a standard class room designed for 2-4 people. All rooms for rent in the buildings of the Svyazist complex are equipped with:

  • Phones

  • TVs

  • beds;

  • tables with chairs;

  • bedside tables and wardrobes.





Some rooms also have soft corners. All rooms are decorated in light colors. In two-story buildings, a toilet and shower are located on the floor. In the large body, the bathrooms in each room are individual.

"Signalman" is a recreation center where you can rent not only ordinary apartments, but also two VIP-class rooms. In addition to the bedroom, these rooms include a hotel and a kitchen with a microwave.

"Signalman", recreation center: reviews of tourists about the rooms

Opinion about the rooms of the Svyazist complex was relatively good for tourists. Of course, there are no special amenities for guests here. However, the base rooms look nevertheless quite clean and comfortable. According to most residents, you can relax in the rooms of the complex very well. The soundproofing in the rooms rented out on the basis of the rooms is just excellent, and their decoration is quite new and pretty.

Complex infrastructure

The rooms on this base are thus provided for the simplest. However, there are a lot of conveniences, in fact, in the territory of the Svyazist complex. If desired, guests of the base can, for example, swim in the 25-meter pool. There is also a large sports hall on site. Here you can play football, basketball or volleyball.

" Signalman" - a recreation center, offering its guests, among other things, a visit to one of the three Finnish saunas. Each of these baths is designed for 12 people. The saunas have relaxation rooms and a jacuzzi. Also in the complex there are Turkish baths, designed for swimming 24 people at a time.

The guests of the complex have the opportunity to keep fit in the gym. In addition, you can visit the solarium at the base. For children, a computer room and a games room are equipped in one of the buildings. Sports equipment rental is also working in this complex. In winter, ski runs are made in the surrounding forests. Tourists can park their car in the free parking lot.





Reviews of the infrastructure of the complex

About the rooms of the "Signalman" base, her former guests speak rather restrainedly. Actually the complex itself and the service provided in it, most tourists very much praise. The nature surrounding the base is really just magnificent. And the air here is crystal clear.

The pool and saunas in this complex, according to its former guests, are actually very comfortable. True, you can visit them for free only once a day for 40 minutes. But in order to relax and rest, this time is enough for most guests of the base.

Many tourists also relate the advantages of the complex to the fact that there is a place on the upper platform designed for grilling kebabs.

Beach complex

" Signalman" - a recreation center , as already mentioned, located on the shores of Lake Petrovsky. The places here are actually very beautiful. The water in the lake itself is quite clean. Therefore, most of the guests of the base in the summer visit not only the pool, but also the local sandy beach. In addition to changing cabins on the lake, next to the base there are also playgrounds. There is also a bath complex on the beach. The places of the lake, safe for acceptance of water procedures, are marked with special signs.
