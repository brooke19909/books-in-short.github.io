Description of the boarding house "Pestovo" in Mytishchi district

In the review below, we will focus on a spiritual place of rest located in Mytishchi district - Pestovo boarding house. This holiday home appeared in the village of the same name in the distant 1937 and was originally open to actors of the Moscow Art Theater and their families. In the 90s, the sanatorium was transferred to the Moscow administration, and after that it became the property of the Russian Central Bank.

About boarding house

Pestovo is located in an incredibly beautiful and quiet place. Here everyone relaxes in solitude with nature, walks through the landscape park, turning into a forest, breathes fresh air and enjoys the birds singing. Despite the fact that the organization does not have an official website and it is extremely difficult to find information about it, local residents and many Muscovites know this place well and come here with pleasure to relax. The sanatorium is a picturesque architectural ensemble consisting of a main building and two outbuildings. The guesthouse is open all year and is happy to meet guests at any time.

Location

The exact address of the pension "Pestovo": Mytishchi district, the village of Pestovo, Berezovaya Alley, ownership number 6 (Moscow region).

It should be noted that the sanatorium is located on the territory of the strategic reservoir near the dam.

basic information

All guests of the Pestovo boarding house are offered comfortable and bright rooms. The rooms - freshly renovated, clean and tidy, new linens. There are all necessary furniture for a comfortable stay, toilet, shower, hairdryer.

Near the boarding house there is a store where you can purchase food and necessary household items.

In the sanatorium there is always something to do, no one will be bored. On the territory there are several sports fields, a cinema hall, places for recreation, games and entertainment. In the medical building for an additional fee, you can go through many procedures: a capsule for sleeping, a solarium, various saunas.

On the territory of the pension there is a spacious dining room, a tea room, an excellent bar where you can have a tasty meal and quench your thirst with various drinks

Vacation with children

Many guests of the sanatorium, who were lucky enough to rest with children on its territory, were delighted with the holidays. There are excellent conditions for children. There is a wonderful games room, modern sports fields, various sections, a children's menu.

Placement of children is allowed from the age of four.

Reviews

Judging by numerous reviews, the Pestovo boarding house is a great place to relax for people of all age categories. Here couples with children, elderly people, youth and all those who are tired of the hustle and bustle of big cities have a rest.

The institution has very good staff, attentive, kind, helpful. The service is wonderful, the cleaning is decent, the response is instant on any request.

The territory, according to vacationers, is huge, guarded, kept in perfect order. The nature is amazing - around the forest, silence, a clean reservoir. Around the birds, squirrels with hands eat. The place is very sincere and amazing.

A lot of good reviews about food at Pestovo. The dishes are very tasty and satisfying, you can take something to choose from.

The rooms also earned a lot of positive reviews. The guests claim that the rooms are comfortable, clean, there is everything you need.

Conclusion

Summing up, we can say that the Pestovo boarding house is a place for spiritual relaxation. If you are looking for a sanatorium for a quiet and relaxing pastime, where you can calm down, relax and enjoy nature and breathe fresh air, then you are here. Many guests of the boarding house say that they would love to return there again.
