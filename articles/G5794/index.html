Hotel "Villa Sova", Abkhazia: description, features, rooms, location and reviews

Many tourists, tired of the daily noise and crowded streets of big cities, are looking for a relaxing and comfortable vacation somewhere in nature. And one of such quiet and comfortable places on the sea coast can be the picturesque resort of the Republic of Abkhazia - New Athos.

New Athos Resort

Due to some remoteness from the border with the Russian Federation, the number of holidaymakers here is much less than in many resorts in Abkhazia. The tourist infrastructure in New Athos is just developing. There are no huge shops and hypermarkets, numerous annoying entertainment on the beach, already a long time familiar city bustle. But in abundance there are natural pebble beaches, clear water in the sea, located within walking distance of architectural and religious attractions and unique landscapes with amazing vegetation, waterfalls and small ponds.

But in order not to spoil your vacation with unpleasant trifles, it is very important not to make a mistake with the choice of your place of residence. Many Abkhazian hotels were built in Soviet times, and at the moment they need to improve the condition of their housing stock and the level of services provided. They should not be considered for placement.

First of all, you need to pay attention to new hotels and mini-hotels, which in recent years have appeared in resorts in connection with the active development of the tourism industry. Here tourists are offered comfortable rooms, quality food, wonderful service. A worthy option for a good rest can be the hotel "Villa Owl."





Short hotel description

The unusual forms of the four-story building of the Hotel Villa Owl (Abkhazia, Athos), the colorful appearance of its facade attract the eyes of many vacationers. For the first time, the hotel met its tourists in May 2015, offering them at their disposal forty one-room and two-room comfortable rooms, from whose windows breathtaking views of the sea and mountains open.





On the ground floor of the hotel there is a cozy cafe with panoramic windows on the sea and a hand-painted interior. Here, guests are always waiting for delicious breakfasts, lunches and dinners on a custom-made menu.

The hotel has a small but very well-groomed area, which is guarded around the clock. Entrance to it is carried out using a magnetic key issued to all residents of the hotel.

Location

The spectacular Hotel Villa Sova is located on the first line in the central part of Novy Afon, 5 minutes by car from the main attractions of the resort. The exact address of the hotel: Republic of Abkhazia, New Athos, 30 Sukhumi highway.

You can get to the hotel from the Russian Federation as follows:

  1. From Sochi Airport or Adler Train Station, proceed by public transport or taxi (about 15 kilometers) to the border with Abkhazia (Psou River).
  2. Walk through border posts on foot.
  3. On the territory of Abkhazia by public transport or private taxi (about 100 kilometers) drive to New Athos.

Nutrition

When buying a tour to the Villa Sova hotel (Abkhazia), guests do not need to worry about high-quality and nutritious food during their holidays. Breakfast is already included in the price of the package; a comprehensive lunch or dinner is available at an additional cost in the dining room on the ground floor of the hotel. The cafe operates on the principle of "custom menu".

Beach

Hotel "Villa Owl" (New Athos) is located on the first coastline. Only a few meters separate the hotel building from the equipped pebble beach. To get to the beach, just go down the stairs of the hotel.





Entrance to the beach is free, rental of beach equipment (sun umbrellas and sun loungers) is available at an additional cost. But this is not a compulsory service, you can perfectly sit on your own beach rug at the very edge of the sea.

Additional amenities

To ensure that guests stay at Villa Sova hotel was pleasant and comfortable, the hotel staff is ready to offer a number of additional services that are not automatically included in the price of the travel package, but are purchased exclusively at will and are paid separately.

  • Late check-out. If the guest wishes to check out later than the checkout time set at the hotel, he pays for the full day.
  • Meeting guests at the railway station or at the airport.
  • Excursion service in Abkhazia in comfortable cars.
  • Laundry and ironing of personal belongings of guests.
  • Installation of an extra bed is strictly by agreement with the hotel. Children under the age of five are free of charge when using existing beds and without meals.
  • Guarded parking for personal vehicles.
  • Pets up to 5 kilograms free of charge at the hotel.
  • Access to free Wi-Fi on site.
