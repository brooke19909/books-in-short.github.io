How to enable the Find iPhone feature: step-by-step instructions

Find My iPhone is a great innovation that Apple introduced to users since June 2010. Now this service is integrated with iCloud. This is an amazing feature that can help those who may have left their iPhone in the wrong place. It is also useful for those who are afraid that they will not be able to prevent attackers from gaining access to personal information.

What it is?

Find My iPhone helps a lot to determine where an iPhone can be when it is not near you. Thanks to satellite images and cartography, he will show where your smartphone is located, provided that the device has a signal and is turned on.

Where is the Find iPhone feature? It is accessible from any Internet-connected computer, tablet or portable device thanks to the iCloud website. How does Find My iPhone work?

How to make sure the Find iPhone feature is on? Firstly, the Find My iPhone service only works if your iPhone is turned on and has a signal. If it is turned off and does not transmit data, the service will not work. However, you will always see if the phone is turned on. The service will also not work if your iPhone has been switched to airplane mode.

Each user needs to focus on when and how the Find iPhone feature works. Enabling this option is simple. If your iPhone is missing or lost, you can use your computer to open the icloud website. Log in to your icloud account. On the screen, select your iPhone, and after that you can find it, play a sound, put it into lost mode, or delete all the data on it remotely.

If you enable the Find iPhone feature, its four great features will be activated:

How to use Find My iPhone to find your phone?

Sign in to iCloud using the account you used when setting up Find My iPhone. This is probably your Apple ID or iTunes account.

Click Find iPhone in the iCloud Web Tools section. The Find My iPhone service immediately starts trying to find all the devices on which it is turned on. You will see messages on the screen when the application is turned on.

When your device is found, a window will appear in the right corner of your web browser. It lets you know how much battery is left in your phone, and offers several options.

Click Play Sound. This is the first option, because sending sound to the device is better when you think you have lost it nearby and want to find it faster. It can also be useful if you think that someone nearby got your smartphone, but denies it.

You can also click Lost Mode. This allows you to remotely lock the device screen and set a password (even if you have not previously set it). This prevents the use of the device or access to your personal data.

As soon as you click the Lost Mode button, enter the password you want to use. If you already have this setting, it will automatically be used. You can also enter a phone number where the person who discovered the phone can contact you (this is optional). You also have the option to write a message that appears on the device‚Äôs screen.
