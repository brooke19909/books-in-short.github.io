Hotel The Jomtien Twelve 3 * (Pattaya, Thailand): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Thailand is a country that is ideal for tourists who prefer a quality vacation with good service. Snow-white beaches will appeal to lovers of a relaxing pastime, and young people will appreciate the stormy parties that are held throughout the country. For history buffs, there are many ancient Buddhist monuments and temples in Thailand. Choosing a place for a budget holiday, you should pay attention to The Jomtien Twelve 3 * hotel (Thailand, Pattaya). What is this hotel like?

Basic hotel information

The small hotel offers tourists 60 rooms, which are designed for two people. It was built in 2005 in an area not far from the beach. Travelers from all over the world stop here, but most of all tourists are from Asia. The Jomtien Twelve Hotel is also preferred by locals. In addition to their native language, the staff speaks English. Therefore, Russian-speaking tourists who do not know the basic knowledge of this language, it will be difficult to understand the hotel administrator.





It is worth knowing that the hotel is strictly prohibited accommodation with animals of any size. Extra beds are not available even for a fee. Tourists will be able to enter their room no earlier than 14:00 local time. By the way, the time difference with Moscow here is +4 hours. At the end of the vacation, you must leave the hotel no later than 12:00. The hottest season in Thailand is considered the period from March to May. Nevertheless, tourists come here all year round, because even in the winter months the temperature in the country rarely drops below 20 degrees.

Where is the hotel situated?

Hotel The Jomtien Twelve Hotel 3 * (Pattaya) boasts a favorable location. It is located in the southern part of the resort, in the area of ‚Äã‚ÄãJomtien Beach. Unlike the northern part of Pattaya, it is suitable for a relaxing holiday. There are practically no noisy discos in the open air, loud nightclubs and bars. The hotel and the municipal beach are separated by a small road. The path to the sea will take only a couple of minutes. Pattaya Pedestrian Street, where you can buy inexpensive things and souvenirs, is 5 km from the hotel. It is also worth visiting the local aquarium, 2 km away from the complex. You can get around the resort by taxi or tuk-tuk (small buses with an open top).





You can get to the hotel from the international capital airport of Bangkok. Jomtien is approximately 125 km away. Travel time is about two to three hours by bus. Taxis can be reached faster, but the cost of the trip will be much higher. Also, when making a tour, a paid transfer from the airport and back is organized.

Number of rooms

The Jomtien Twelve 3 * Hotel (Pattaya) has 60 comfortable rooms, each of which is suitable for a small family or a young couple. All rooms have modern high-quality repairs. The design of the rooms is classic with national elements. As a decor, photographs are used, inserted into wooden frames, small indoor plants. All apartments must have their own bathroom, which is equipped with a shower and a water heater. There is also a small balcony in the room, which usually houses a dining table and chairs. The windows overlook the sea or the city.

  • Standard . It is designed to accommodate two people who will sleep in one double bed. The total area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe room is 20 square meters. The apartments located on the ground floor have a private terrace with garden views.
  • Superior . It is more spacious than a standard room, and its area is 25 square meters. There are several adjacent apartments.
  • Deluxe Spacious room is 30 square meters. There is one double bed, as well as a balcony with ocean views.
  • Plus . These apartments are located on the top floors of the hotel and have city views. Their area is 40 square meters. m

Room features

Apart from furniture, all rooms at Jomtien Twelve Hotel (Pattaya) have many amenities that make living here as comfortable as possible. All guests can take advantage of the following amenities:

  • A flat-screen TV with satellite channels, including several Russian speakers, is available.
  • Kitchenette and small fridge. There is a set for making tea or coffee, a microwave. An electric kettle is also available in the room upon request.
  • Minibar with free drinking water. The remaining drinks are available at an additional cost.
  • Wireless internet is not included in the price.
  • Air conditioning in some rooms. With it, the room maintains a temperature that is comfortable for sleeping.
  • Free toiletries in the bathroom. The set includes shower gel, shampoo and soap. Bathrobes and towels are also provided.
  • A safe in which guests can store valuables, money and documents.
  • A telephone is available to contact the hotel for room service. Guests can ask to call the room in the morning and wake them up.
  • Ironing board.
  • Regular housekeeping, as well as change of towels and bed linen.




Types of food, restaurants and bars

Most tourists who come to Thailand prefer to eat not in a hotel, but in restaurants and cafes specializing in local cuisine. Therefore, The Jomtien Twelve Hotel offers only basic meals. There is no all inclusive system. When making the tour, travelers can only pay for breakfast. The hotel has a small restaurant serving international cuisine, where guests can enjoy a daily buffet. It includes a standard set of dishes: sandwiches, sliced ‚Äã‚Äãham and cheese, eggs, scrambled eggs, rice, as well as fresh fruits and vegetables. Upon request, a special children's menu can be prepared for the child.





There are no other catering establishments on the territory of the hotel, but there are many cafes, restaurants and bars nearby that offer tasty and hearty dinners at affordable prices.

Infrastructure and entertainment

The Jomtien Twelve Hotel 3 * hotel does not offer guests a wide selection of entertainment or services, but it has everything you need for a comfortable stay. So, all guests can connect to themselves a free "Wi-Fi" by requesting a password at the reception. There is an additional safe, as well as a luggage room for storing large bags and luggage. They are convenient to leave at the reception, so as not to take up much space in the room. At the hotel you can order and pay for a transfer to the airport, choose a tour of the sights of Thailand. There is free public parking nearby where any tourist can park their car. It is possible to rent a car to travel around Pattaya on your own.





For guests of the hotel there is a laundry room where you can hand over clothes. Her laundry is included in the price. There is also its own business center, which provides several rooms for events for business travelers, as well as a meeting room. You can relax after a walk in the city in the massage room. Guests can use the fitness room or relax in the hot tub. The hotel also has its own terrace, surrounded by a lush garden where you can stroll in the afternoon or evening.

Relax in a hotel with children

Unfortunately, The Jomtien Twelve 3 * hotel (Thailand, Pattaya) does not provide suitable conditions for tourists with children. Coming here with a small child, it is worth considering that the hotel has no cradles for babies. But the main restaurant is equipped with high chairs for feeding. If necessary, children can leave the nanny, who offers their services for an hourly fee. Parents will have to do leisure activities for their children on their own. On the territory of the hotel there is no mini-club, no children's animation, no playground, no pool.

Advantages of the hotel

Before buying a trip to Thailand, you should definitely read the opinions of tourists about hotels. The Jomtien Twelve Hotel has positive and negative reviews. First, we list the main advantages of relaxing in this place:

  • Clean and comfortable rooms that are consistent with the cost of living. All rooms are carefully cleaned daily.
  • Good breakfasts. The hotel provides about 6 dishes to choose from, while you can take as much as you want.
  • Good location. Walking to the beach in less than a minute, and the center can be reached in half an hour.
  • The Internet works without interruptions, and its speed is good, so you can even watch movies and listen to music.
  • A clean beach with few local people. It is not crowded, and the sea is very warm.




Hotel Disadvantages

The Jomtien Twelve Hotel is not an ideal place to relax. According to tourists, it has serious shortcomings that can overshadow a vacation in Thailand. Consider the most significant of them:

  • Rooms that do not have a balcony or terrace have nowhere to dry clothes. Even in the bathroom there are no special hangers or ropes.
  • Moody staff. Administrators work slowly, no one smiles. They do not understand English well.
  • Near the road it is very noisy, so you should take rooms with access to the sea. Old air conditioners are also very noisy.
  • The closets in the rooms smell of mold and dampness.
  • Dark rooms that overlook neighboring buildings.
  • In the rooms ants crawl.
  • Towels are issued stained in blood.

Conclusion

Thus, we can conclude that The Jomtien Twelve hotel will be a good option for travelers who want to save money. For a small fee, it provides ample amenities. It is worth recommending this hotel to tourists who prefer not to sit in the hotel, but to organize their leisure activities on their own. People looking for a relaxing beach holiday can be bored here. Families with small children should still choose a different hotel. This place is more targeted at youth companies.
