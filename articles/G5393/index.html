Description of a simple way to make origami hearts

How to make a pleasant surprise for your soulmate, gently recalling feelings? You can make, for example, a small paper heart and decorate them with gift wrapping. Or just give a large product by signing it and using instead of a postcard. Perhaps the most popular are rolled up crafts. Let's figure out how to make origami. Hearts can be different in size, shape and folding method. Below is the simplest option. Necessary materials

The described method, how to make origami of the heart, will allow you to create it in the form of a flat souvenir, while the wrong side will be different from the front. On the back you will see bends and turns, so it is more advisable to use a heart to decorate flat objects. Therefore, paper can be used not very thick, painted only on one side. You can take for work and more dense material, for example, colored cardboard. But in this case, it will be necessary to somewhat simplify the work at the first stage of folding. Also for work you will need scissors, a ruler, a simple pencil. Glue, adhesive tape (scotch tape) will be necessary to secure an already finished heart. You can also combine two products into one, which will allow you to use this craft for hanging.





The first stage is preparatory

Now you will learn how to make origami hearts. Start with the preparatory work.

  1. On a piece of colored paper, draw a square-shaped pattern. To do this, measure the width and set aside the same length. Draw a line perpendicular to the long sides, and then cut off the excess paper with scissors.
  2. Fold the square in half with the front red side up for the outline of the midline, then turn it back.
  3. Rotate the workpiece 90 ¬∞ around its axis. Fold it in half to get a two-layer template. The bend should be located on the upper horizontal line.

The workpiece is ready for further work.





The second stage - folding the heart

Now you can proceed directly to the manufacture.

  1. Make two bends on a two-layer template according to the scheme.
  2. Closer to the middle line of the workpiece, perform two more inversions (see figure).
  3. Then fold the paper in the form of an ‚Äúaccordion‚Äù along the lines received.
  4. Rotate the layout 180 ¬∞ around its axis, with the front side away from you.
  5. Pull the protruding plane by the edge down.
  6. Flatten and press the top, forming a corner recess characteristic of the heart.
  7. Overlap the two lower corners, reducing them to the midline towards each other. Press and smooth the bend lines for sharper contours.
  8. Shape the shape of the heart. To do this, wrap down the upper upper corners by a small amount so that the product is proportional.
  9. Scroll the heart back around its axis, with its face up.

So, we looked at the simplest way to make an origami heart. But there are many other modeling methods. Let's talk a little about creating crafts by cutting and pasting in separate places.





Other craft making options

Of course, any origami ‚ÄúHeart‚Äù looks very impressive. There are other, quite simple options for making a love souvenir. You can, for example, create volume from a flat sheet by easily making small hearts for hanging.



  1. Cut out several blanks in the shape of a heart from paper (the approximate size of the square is 5 x 5 cm).
  2. Then make small cuts on them in the midline in a downward direction (1.5-2 cm).
  3. Fold the loose parts over the slot under the paper. Smooth a ruler over the top of these lines for clarity.
  4. Apply a small amount of glue to the curved allowances, and then join both sides together. The middle of the heart will bend at an angle, creating a volumetric effect.
  5. Glue on the inside of the ribbon for hanging. You can also fix such a heart on a small stick.
