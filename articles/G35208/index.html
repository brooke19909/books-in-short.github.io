Pension "Pine"

Considering the boarding houses of Nebug, it is necessary to mention "Pine". This is the center of rehabilitation and restorative medicine of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of Russia, which is located on the Tuapse coast of the Black Sea, in the village of Sosnovy.

In this place, an ecologically clean zone and a special microclimate is observed. Flora near the boarding house "Pine" is truly unique. It counts more than 50 varieties of rare specimens. In addition, in the territory this diversity is unusually decorated in green flowerbeds, lawns, stairs, arbors, bridges and streetlights and creates a single architectural and park composition. At the same time, the natural appearance of the relief is preserved.
