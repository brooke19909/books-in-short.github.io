Credit "Corn" - what is it?

The "Corn" plastic card is a product of the famous MasterCard payment system and allows you to conveniently and cheaply pay for purchases of goods in stores, fuel at gas stations, restaurant services and various places of recreation. It can be paid at any place where plastic cards are accepted. Credit "Corn" is very convenient in that it can be used both anywhere in the world and on the Internet.





If you urgently need money to buy household appliances or just for everyday needs, you can always take advantage of a quick consumer loan. To do this, you need to contact one of the salons of the Euroset network, which in the Russian Federation has more than two and a half thousand. Credit "Corn" is usually issued in 30-40 minutes without a guarantee or collateral. For its registration you will need only a valid passport of the Russian Federation and a ready-made plastic card. After receiving a loan, you can withdraw it in cash at any ATM, or you can pay with a credit card and get pleasant additional bonuses.

Features of this service





"Corn" - a card on which credit allows you to pay bills in any store or cafe that has the possibility of cashless payments. Moreover, the cardholder receives a bonus in the form of 1% of all funds spent by him back to the card account. In addition, the company provides users with a convenient online account with which you can pay for many everyday services, such as utility bills, the Internet and many others. For all this, the holder will also be awarded bonus points to the card account.

Credit "Corn" has certain conditions that are established by the Bank "Renaissance Credit":

  • the maximum amount is 300 thousand rubles;

  • grace period - 55 calendar days;

  • the interest rate provided by the Kukuruza loan is individual for each client and varies between 24.9% and 79.9%;

  • minimum debt payment - 5% of its total amount;

  • fines for violation of the loan program maintenance regime - 750 rubles;

  • the total cost of the loan, expressed in interest rate - from 0% to 117% per annum;

  • The payment period lasts 25 days;

  • when repaying a loan through a third party, an additional commission may be taken.
