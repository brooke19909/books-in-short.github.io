Screen

From the case and buttons, we smoothly proceed to the main control element of the smartphone - its display. The Sony Xperia Tipo has a 3.2-inch screen. Battery

Hardy device, based on the capacity of its battery, is also difficult to call. A battery with a capacity of 1,500 mAh was installed on the phone. For a more sophisticated device, of course, this would be enough for a day of work. But, given that our phone has a simple screen and a minimum of processor performance, as the reviews show, it can easily last up to two days on a single charge.

Memory

With the amount of internal memory on this device, everything is "very bad." Initially, the phone provides 2.9 GB of shared memory, of which the user can use only 2.2 GB, since the rest is designed for system files.

Also, given the support for 3D games on this smartphone, you can download several entertaining applications on it and, thus, turn it into a game center. There are a lot of variations - and with 32 GB of memory they can all become a reality.

conclusions

Yes, as you can observe for yourself by reading our review, the phone has a lot of shortcomings. It is weak in terms of the characteristics of the camera, processor, graphics engine. Even the Tipo case device may seem inconvenient and illogical to someone.

On the other hand, he has a lot of advantages. The first is a long working life on a single charge, which is rarely seen on budget Android phones (the Sony Xperia Tipo manual tells us about 2 days of active work). The second is its compact size (despite the thickness of the case, in general, the device takes up little space - due to this it is convenient to always keep it at hand in any situation). Thirdly, the presence of 2 SIM cards, which makes the smartphone an indispensable communication assistant. Plus, you can talk about such features as the universal Android platform, which can become the basis for installing any other software; the ability to expand the memory card (then the phone can be used as a portable multimedia station). If we weigh, in this way, the pros and cons, our "modest" Xperia Tipo begins to seem not so weak a device. On the contrary, it is possible that the manufacturer specifically developed this model in order to occupy a certain niche of budget and low-performance gadgets in the hope of conquering this segment of the market.





Whether they succeeded or not is difficult to say. The model is not as popular as some flagships; most likely, many users have not heard of this modification at all. However, judging by the reviews, there is a demand for the device - which means that the Xperia Tipo also has its own audience happy with the work of the gadget.
