Hotel "Starfish" (Olginka / Tuapse district): characteristics, services, reviews, photos

Surely every second resident of our country not only heard about the picturesque village of Olginka, but also repeatedly spent his vacation there. On the compact territory of this village in Tuapse district, medical sanatoriums, dispensaries, and boarding houses offering professional therapy have been built. Despite the small size of the village, the tourism industry is quite well developed here. At the Black Sea resort there are children's entertainment facilities, areas for the adult population, and more specifically for youth (nightclubs, restaurants, disco bars), have been landscaped.

Well-known artists regularly come here, music festivals and concert programs are held. In the daytime, guests will find the azure sea with clear water, well-groomed long beaches, romantic sunsets. Since the Soviet years, the resort has remained popular among Russian residents. And all thanks to the affordable cost of living in comfortable complexes. One of these is the Starfish Hotel (Olginka). Today's publication provides introductory information about this hotel.

Coastline





There is no separate beach at the Sea Star Hotel (Olginka), but 30 meters stretches the municipal coast, strewn with rolled pebbles. The plot is cleaned daily, so it is always very clean and comfortable here. Descent into the water is convenient, safe for children.
