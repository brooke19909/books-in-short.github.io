Mini paradise for travelers

A small island at the junction of the Pacific and Indian oceans is popular with tourists because of its unusual climate - here from June to October there is heat, and from November to March - humidity and tropical thunderstorms. Experienced travelers know how to get to Bali cheaply, which is why they usually relax here 2-3 times a year, since it is quite difficult to find such an atmosphere of relaxation and high-quality service.

Some of those who once visited here even decide to move, which is why the island's population over the past 100 years has increased by a quarter and currently stands at about 4.2 million people. A large number of hotels are located here, music and religious holidays are constantly held, sculpture and painting museums are open, so everyone can find entertainment to their liking.
