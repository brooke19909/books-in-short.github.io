New building of the residential complex "Tikhiy Don", Rostov-on-Don: overview, developer, layout and reviews

Rostov-on-Don is a large southern city, whose inhabitants no less than others need comfortable living conditions. Over the past few years, the city has rebuilt, not only new residential neighborhoods have appeared, but also full-fledged residential neighborhoods with their own infrastructure. Speaking about which projects modern residents of the city give their preference, it is necessary to focus on those that provide a high level of comfort: spacious apartments of improved layout in the area with the developed infrastructure, excellent transport accessibility and good ecology. LCD "Quiet Don" (Rostov-on-Don) - a large-scale project, quite popular in the city. As part of this material, we will conduct an objective review of it and reveal the secret of popularity among local residents.





about the project

LCD "Tikhiy Don" (Rostov-on-Don) is a premium residential complex located in the city center. From the windows it offers a picturesque view of the main attraction - the Don River. The project merged all ideas about beauty and functionality, which only confirms numerous reviews.

The builder "Vertoldevelopment" faced a difficult task - in the very center, the heart of the city, to create a perfect world where everything would be provided for the most comfortable life. The complex is being built in 4 stages: the first building has already been commissioned and populated, the commissioning of the last facility is scheduled for the end of 2019.

Location

Many people compare the project with the LCD Quiet Yard (Rostov-on-Don), but if you draw a parallel, the difference is obvious. Even if you look at the photo presentation of each project, you can see the difference. But in order to finally convince you, the words should be supported by real facts. So, first of all, a great place was chosen for the construction of the facility. On the street of Nizhny Novgorod, in close proximity to all infrastructure, a business center and the best attractions of the city, the Tikhiy Don residential complex (Rostov) is being built. Reviews of the first buyers who managed to visit the facility even at the stage of laying the foundation, admired. Many were conquered by the place of development, the opportunity to live in the cultural and historical center of the city, every day enjoying the beautiful view from the windows.





It is important that the construction of the facility is carried out in accordance with and in full coordination with the urban development and improvement project, and is monitored by local authorities. Today, the new residential complex Tikhiy Don (Rostov-on-Don) is the most prestigious in the city.

Transport accessibility

The complex is well located and relative to transport accessibility. Having a personal car, you can easily get to anywhere in the city. There are also public transport stops near the complex, providing the necessary mobility to those who do not have a private car.





By the way, the problem of insufficient number of parking spaces has been solved with the help of underground parking. Moreover, in the short term, the construction of its own multi-level heated parking with the right to acquire parking spaces in the property.

Architecture

Architecture and technology are also what distinguishes the Tikhiy Don LCD from the Tikhiy Yard LCD (Rostov-on-Don). The reviews confirm that the use of advanced technologies and innovations, Quiet Don is a clear leader, there are no such residential development projects in the city.

Exquisite design finishes using expensive materials in all common areas - from entrance groups to the parking lot and the surrounding area. All entrances are equipped with high-speed silent elevators, a 24-hour video surveillance system and a concierge room.

Infrastructure

LCD "Quiet Don" in Rostov-on-Don boasts excellent infrastructure. All that is needed for a full and vibrant life in the city center is located on its own guarded territory: a kindergarten, a fitness center, shops, service industry organizations, restaurants and cafes. The developer paid great attention to the development of sports, equipping the territory with modern playgrounds and sports grounds for both children and adults, there will be a playground for basketball and futsal.

Given the fact that the project is being submitted in several stages, while its first tenants have to experience some inconvenience. A global problem for the city is the lack of places in kindergartens. So, the project provides for the use of the area on one of the first floors of buildings for a preschool educational institution, which should solve the problem, at least for residents of elite new buildings. In the meantime, all residents can freely use the infrastructure of the district: within walking distance there is a school, a clinic, grocery stores and non-food items, shopping and entertainment centers, theaters and city parks - there will certainly not be any problems with organizing leisure activities.





Beautification

Many believe that life in the city center, accompanied by endless noise, bustle, cannot be comfortable and measured. But LCD "Quiet Don" (Rostov-on-Don) is a pleasant exception. On its territory, excellent walking areas and recreation areas are equipped, where even in the hot sultry summer you can feel the pleasant coolness and freshness of the river. In order to spend time in nature, breathe air, it is not necessary to leave the complex. Residents of the first commissioned house do not cease to leave their enthusiastic reviews, urging everyone to pay special attention to the complex, while there are still free apartments.

Apartments, layouts

If you decide to stay on the LCD "Tikhiy Don" in Rostov-on-Don, the apartments will also not disappoint you. The choice of customers offered the studio, one-, two-, three- and even four-room options. The apartments are for rent with rough finish, but all the minimum necessary work has already been done: hot and cold water has been supplied, heating radiators, metering devices are installed.





The complex has 718 apartments designed for buyers with different needs. The apartments are represented by a variety of planning decisions, but they are all quite bright and spacious, which provides comfort to every modern resident of the metropolis. Here you will find small but cozy studios, full-fledged two- and three-room apartments with a large kitchen-living room, a hall, isolated rooms and an additional bathroom, as well as huge four-room apartments, ideal for a large and friendly family. A gift from the developer - free-planning apartments, providing a huge flight for imagination and bold design experiments.
