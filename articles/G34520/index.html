How many stamps to stick on an envelope: calculation principle

How many stamps to stick on an envelope in a particular case depends on the direction and cost of shipping. The type of envelope also influences the final figure. If it is marked, then the costs will be lower.

Cost

To understand how many brands to stick on the envelope, you need to decide on the tariffs. For example, the cost of a simple letter can be 19 rubles, while a registered one will cost 37 rubles. These numbers are subject to change every year, therefore information needs to be updated.

Cost is important to know because stamps are a sign of payment for postal services related to shipping. They are used to send regular letters, small packages and written correspondence.

A simple calculation will help determine how many stamps are glued on envelopes in Russia: their total face value should cover the shipping cost. You can find out the exact numbers by looking at the current postal rates.

That is, stamps are used to pay for mail services. Moreover, the use of this method allows you to save money. In cash, the amount will be slightly larger.

In order not to look for information on tariffs and not to manually calculate how many brands to stick on an envelope, it makes sense to use an online calculator. This method allows you to quickly and accurately determine the final cost of the service.

Marking

Envelopes are used to send letters. And they are divided into two types:

  • Marked. On the envelope there is a stamp with the displayed letter (letter) A, B or D.
  • Unmarked. There is no stamp on the envelope.

If the letter B stands, then in the vast majority of cases, the need to calculate how many brands to stick on the envelope disappears. In the case of other letters, such a scheme is also possible, but it is necessary to clarify. In the calculator, such features will also be taken into account.

Total

How many stamps you need to stick depends on the final cost of the letter. You can reduce it by purchasing a labeled envelope.
