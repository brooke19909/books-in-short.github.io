Infrastructure

Sanatorium "Small Salt" is a large complex of various buildings. The main building is allotted for comfortable rooms for vacationers. Everyone can choose a room to their taste and financial capabilities. Economy rooms are located in a separate four-story building, on the 3rd and 4th floors. In the same building on the ground floor there is a banquet hall for 100 people, and on the second floor there are cozy dining rooms.

The treatment department is located in a separate one-story building. The same building is reserved for the health complex. It has a Finnish sauna and a hot tub.

In such a picturesque corner, there cannot be no place for romance. Proof of this is the boat station, from which you can take an unforgettable walk along the Solonice River. Treatment

Sanatorium "Small salts" of the Yaroslavl region offers therapeutic measures aimed at combating the following diseases:

  • diabetes mellitus (rehabilitation of patients);
  • diseases of the musculoskeletal system;
  • diseases of the cardiovascular system;
  • joint ailments;
  • diseases of the respiratory tract and lungs;
  • digestive diseases;
  • treatment of children with cerebral palsy, as well as prone to frequent diseases.




The sanatorium offers a wide range of spa treatments aimed at healing and relaxing the body. Thanks to the means that are used, the mood improves and the person's well-being improves markedly. In addition, they have a beneficial effect on the skin. Such procedures include: miostimulation, massages, a solarium, Finnish and infrared saunas, a pool with hydromassage, and a cedar mini-sauna. There is also a gym on the territory of the complex, and in the vicinity there is a salt cave and terrain track 300-300 meters long.

Leisure

Much attention is paid in the sanatorium to leisure of residents, so no one will be bored. It regularly organizes entertainment shows, dance evenings, and performances by famous artists. Members of the older generation may like song nights under the button accordion.

If you do not strive for noisy fun, pay attention to the schedule of literary evenings, film screenings and creative meetings. The sanatorium library contains more than five thousand books of various genres, so you are sure to pick up a volume for yourself to pass the evening. There is also the opportunity to play chess or checkers, to participate in a table tennis or billiards tournament, in the summer in volleyball competitions, and in the winter in the ski relay. Bicycles, rollers, fishing rods, skis, ice-boats, sledges, scooters - all this can be rented by every vacationer for a small fee.

In addition, the Small Salt (sanatorium, Yaroslavl) offers excursions to the village of Vyatskoye, the city of Kostroma and the city of Yaroslavl.

Nutrition

For food uses the "Menu-order" system of the sanatorium "Small salts". Reviews say that you can choose dishes for every taste. In addition, greens and vitamin salads are additionally served. Meals for more visitors are three meals a day, including a second dinner. But for patients with diabetes, an afternoon snack is also provided, and a menu for them is developed separately, but without an order.





For kids there is also a special menu (also without order), which is agreed with Rospotrebnadzor. Children are fed 4 times. If parents wish, then for a fee you can transfer the child to the menu by order.

Vacationers who have already been here say that the food in the sanatorium is very tasty. Chefs who work in Small Salt care for the good taste and quality of each dish. And if for the period of your stay in the sanatorium any special occasion has occurred, then you can mark it right here - in a specially equipped banquet room. For such events, a menu consisting of specialties is provided.

How to get there

How to get to the sanatorium "Small salts"? The official website of the institution (www.msoli.ru) offers to book a room directly on the Internet, without wasting time. You can get here by public transport or by your own car. In the first case, you need to take a train to Yaroslavl, then transfer by bus to the village of Nekrasovskoye or to Kostroma and get off at the Malye Soly stop. The bus of the sanatorium will pick you up from here, but first you need to arrange with the administration. If you will drive your own car, then you need to move to s. Small Salt in the direction from Yaroslavl to Kostroma, then turn right. After five kilometers you will see a sanatorium.
