Ski resorts are popular all over the world, and Russian lovers of outdoor activities are lucky that they do not need to go abroad to have a good rest, doing their favorite sport.

The village of Krasnaya Polyana is rapidly gaining popularity due to its unique location. Everything is within reach here - the airport is only 40 km, the Black Sea coast is 39 km, Sochi is 70 km and the mountains are around. Hotels in Krasnaya Polyana provide comfort and relaxation for every taste and budget.

Krasnaya Polyana

The named village is located in a historical place, as evidenced by numerous ancient dolmens and the remains of dilapidated fortresses. Since ancient times, Circassian families lived here, engaged in gardening, agriculture and beekeeping. On the other hand, they often hunted and robbery.

For 47 years, a war was fought over the territory of the Caucasus between tsarist Russia, Turkey and Europe. It was in Krasnaya Polyana that this war was destined to end in 1864. The village founded in this place was initially empty, since the Circassians left these places, but in 1878 the Greeks settled it, giving it the name Krasnaya Polyana.

Today, the best ski resort in Russia is located here , and hotels in Krasnaya Polyana welcome guests all year round.



The mild climate, beautiful mountains, the proximity of the sea and professional ski slopes of different difficulty levels - all this together brings this resort to the world level, while maintaining affordable Russian prices.
