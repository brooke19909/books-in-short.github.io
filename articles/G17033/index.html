Simple and fast: do-it-yourself cool pillow

Making a fun pillow with your own hands is easier than it seems at first glance. It will take only a little skill, patience and source material. You can use this product yourself, or you can give it to a friend in honor of some holiday.

Materials

To make the pillow comfortable and pleasant, it is important to choose the right material for the bed and filler. It should be soft, gentle, not necessarily scratchy. For a breastplate, flannel, fleece, cotton knitwear, satin are best in this case. Synthetic fabrics are more wear-resistant, do not wrinkle, are easily erased. Less - they can become quite electrified. Natural material is more environmentally friendly, but it wrinkles easily, and a self-made cool pillow will quickly lose its original beautiful appearance.

For a decorative product, you can use such decorations as buttons, beads, beads, sequins. If you plan to use the pillow for its intended purpose and put it under your head, you should refuse solid, spiky decor items. In this case, use soft fabric appliqu√©s and embroidery.

It is recommended to use holofiber or thick foam as a filler.

Pig pillow

Consider the technology of making a gift for the upcoming New Year. The easiest way to make a cool pillow-pillow with your own hands is to put embroidery on the finished product.

The pattern in this case will be as simple as possible. Take a large sheet of paper, even a regular newspaper will do. Draw on it an even circle of the right size - this is the body of the future pig. Then next depict another one, two times smaller than the first - this is a five-cent. Next, draw two more small circles - these are the eyes. Now draw a triangle - these are ears, two pieces of such details will be required. To make the pillow look funnier, eyes can be made in different sizes.

Cut out the patterns, gently transfer to the fabric and sew with frequent stitches over the edge. Sew two pink buttons on the patch and two black buttons on the eyes. Now it remains to embroider the tail - and you're done!

Another simple pillow

This item is also suitable as a comic gift.

DIY cool pillow pattern is very simple. To make it, you will need a piece of soft fleece of pink color, a small piece of fabric of a lighter shade, black threads of embroidery for embroidery.

First you need to cut the fabric. From the fleece, cut two squares measuring 30 by 30 or 40 by 40 cm. From the same fabric, cut four equilateral triangles with a side length of 7 cm. Making the pattern for a fun pillow with your own hands is finished. Everything is simple.

Fold the cut triangles in pairs with their faces inward and sew on both sides. This will be the pig‚Äôs ears. Turn and straighten.

Fold both squares together with the front side inwards, place the ears in the corners and pin them with pins. Then sew around the perimeter, leaving a small clearance on one side. Turn to the front side.

From a light pink fabric, cut an even circle with a diameter of 5-7 cm and sew it into the center of the square. This is a patch. Then thread the eyes and nostrils. Fill the pillow with holofiber or foam rubber, and then sew in the left clearance.
