City of Risan (Montenegro). Sights, beaches, how to get

The city of Risan (Montenegro) is one of the oldest settlements. It is located in the Bay of Kotor. In the IV century BC, it was the capital of the Illyrian state, therefore, maritime business and trade flourished in it. It was during this period that the first mention of the city falls, which is confirmed by historical documents. Archaeological excavations have revealed many interesting things. It turns out that in ancient times the city of Risan (Montenegro) was powerful. There were numerous buildings of beautiful design.



Today

Now this town is not very densely populated, about 2,000 people live in it. But when the holiday season begins, a huge number of tourists from different parts of Europe come here.

If you enjoy a calm and moderate stay, we recommend that you visit Montenegro. Risan is characterized by a leisurely, serene and measured life. One gets the feeling that the people living here are in no hurry. The local population is distinguished by friendly disposition and hospitality. If you need help, you can always count on it. Recently, the tourism industry has begun to develop rapidly in this region.





How to get to Risan?

Montenegro is a small European country located in the western part of the Balkan Peninsula. On the southwestern side, its territory is washed by the waters of the Adriatic Sea. It is on its coast that the city of Risan is located. The village is surrounded by mountains, which is why there is a very warm climate. You can get to Risan from the city of Tivat, the duration of the trip will be about an hour. The closest are the settlements of Kotor (20 minutes drive) and Herceg Novi (40 minutes).





Holidays by the sea

In Risan there is a large sand and pebble beach. It is conditionally divided into 3 parts. Hotel Teuta is located on the coast and the center of the beach belongs to this establishment. Two other parts are at the disposal of the city municipality. Risana beach (Montenegro) is ideally clean, as here they carefully monitor the order. On the shore there are various cafes, playgrounds. It features showers, changing rooms and toilets. The waters of the Adriatic Sea are crystal clear, which means that here is an ideal place for snorkeling.





What is worth seeing in Risan?

Since the city has a long history, there is something to see. To get more information about Risan, you should visit the tourist center, which is located in the central part of the village. Here you will be told where you can stay or have a tasty meal, what excursions are conducted and what you definitely need to see in these places.

The villa of the once famous Roman patriarch is considered one of the most interesting sights of Risan. In Montenegro, there are many architectural monuments dating back to the era of Roman rulers. Among this historical heritage, an ancient villa has been preserved to this day. But the most valuable is the mosaic made on the floor. Before visitors opens a panel on which Gibnos (the god of sleep), lay on his bed.





If you are interested in objects of the Middle Ages, be sure to visit the marble castle, located near the beach. The entire interior is made of blue marble. Particular attention should be paid to sculptures made of Greek stone. You can stay in the castle for the night, for this there are special rooms for tourists.

In the city of Risan, a family estate, which belonged to the Russian count Ivelich, was also preserved.





Health Benefits

The climate of these places is quite mild, and the air has healing properties, so many people come here not only to relax, but also to improve their health. The location of the city of Risan (Montenegro) is unique. On the one hand, it is washed by the waters of the Adriatic Sea, on the other, it is surrounded by mountainous areas, in which coniferous forests predominate. The air, saturated with the sea breeze and the aroma of pine needles, is truly healing. It has a beneficial effect on the functioning of the respiratory system.

The treatment center "Vaso Chukovich" invites patients with orthopedic problems.





Purchases

Every tourist wants to bring some souvenir from the trip in memory of the past vacation. What and where can I buy in Risan? The best place to shop is the local market. Here you can purchase various products, vegetables and fruits. Also on the market you can buy all kinds of souvenirs, household items or clothes. The choice is yours.

Risan (Montenegro): reviews

Those who have visited Risan at least once leave reviews only more positively. Despite the fact that the town is very small, there are many interesting places, amazing nature, pristine beach and friendly hosts. Come to Risan - you will definitely not regret the time spent here.
