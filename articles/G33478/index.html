Pliska 2 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria): hotel description, services, reviews

Budget vacation offers for its tourists Bulgaria. There are many complexes, accommodation in which will be very cheap. One of them is the Pliska 2 * hotel (Bulgaria), which is located in the central part of Sunny Beach resort. In addition to the price, it attracts travelers with a good quality of service.

Briefly about the main thing: a description of the body

The hotel was completed in 1985. Unlike large tourist complexes, it almost does not have its own territory. On it is a four-story hotel building, built in the classical style. It is not distinguished by luxurious decoration, but it looks very neat. At night, the external light turns on. Tourists come here every summer. Hotel Pliska 2 * (Sunny Beach) accepts tourists from May to September. In winter, the building undergoes repair work. The last complete renovation of all premises was completed in 2006.





As in other European countries, settlement here begins no earlier than 14:00 local time. All children are welcome at the hotel. You can also come here with small animals whose weight does not exceed 3 kg. Before issuing a voucher, it is necessary to notify the hotel manager of the type, breed, weight of the pet, as well as vaccinations. In addition, additional costs will apply for his stay. After the end of the vacation you need to leave your apartment before 12:00. Payment for accommodation and additional services at the hotel is possible only in cash or by bank transfer.

Hotel location

The hotel has a prime location. The beach is municipal. It is just 50 meters from the complex. The road to it will take only 2-3 minutes. Near the hotel there is also a night club "Lazur", which hosts loud night discos. Such a location will be beneficial for young people, and families with children, by contrast, may suffer from loud noise. Another club is 500 meters away - Grand. Not far from the complex there is a promenade, where there are many restaurants, cafes, retail outlets, fruit stalls, souvenirs and clothes. A local amusement park is 800 meters away. The bus station is 700 meters away.

The nearest airport is located near Burgas, 35 km from Sunny Beach. To get to it, tourists will have to pay a transfer, hire a taxi or rent a car. On average, the trip to the hotel takes a little more than 1 hour. The city center can be reached by bus or on foot. It is only 300 meters from the complex. 3 km from Sunny Beach is the ancient Nessebar, which is worth visiting for lovers of historical attractions.

The number of rooms of the hotel and its amenities

Hotel "Pliska 2 *" has 110 small apartments. Their interior is not distinguished by luxury or sophistication. All rooms are standard. They are designed to accommodate two adults and a child. A toiletry kit is also available for guests.





The description of the hotel room will be incomplete without mentioning the amenities provided by the hotel. So, in the rooms there is a small TV of the old model, which broadcasts several satellite channels. There is also an individual air conditioning in the room, which cools the room in hot weather. For an additional fee, tourists can use the electronic safe, which is designed to store money, documents and valuables. You can contact the manager using the phone. You can also make international calls, but you will have to pay for them separately. Cleaners daily cleaned the rooms. Towels are changed every two days, and bed linen is changed once a week.

Local infrastructure

Despite the fact that the Pliska 2 * hotel (Sunny Beach) is considered a two-star complex, it offers its guests a developed network of infrastructure facilities. For an additional fee, guests can connect wireless internet to their devices. The daily fee is about 10 leva. Near the hotel there is a public car park, where you can leave your car at any time. Parking is free.





Reception at the hotel is open day and night. Here, tourists can rent a car, exchange currency or use fax and photocopying services. Express check-in or check-out is available for a fee. The building has separate smoking areas. A laundry service is available on site, where clothes and shoes are washed, dried and ironed for you. Coming here, it is worth knowing that the hotel does not have Russian-speaking staff. The administrator can communicate with you in English and Bulgarian.

Food options, restaurants and bars

The hotel does not have an all-inclusive system. But tourists can pay half board, which includes breakfast and dinner. They are served in the main hotel restaurant. Dining tables and chairs are located in the spacious hall, which seats more than 130 visitors. Weather permitting, an outdoor terrace is available. All meals are served at the buffet. Also on site is a bar where you can buy soft drinks, including spirits, order cocktails, desserts or ice cream. For a fee, hotel guests may be asked to deliver food directly to their room.

Rest in a hotel with children

For young guests, the Pliska 2 * hotel (Sunny Beach) has equipped a small playground on its territory. Here is a plastic slide, several swings and a sandbox. Children can take part in recreational activities that take place in the neighboring tourist complex. A cradle for babies is provided free of charge upon request of parents. Also, children under 2 years old are not paid. In the hotel restaurant, guests can take special high chairs for feeding.





Guest reviews: benefits

The guests of the Pliska 2 * hotel (Sunny Beach) are satisfied with the rest. They believe that the cost of living is fully consistent with local service. In their reviews, they describe many advantages. We list the main ones:

  • The rooms have new furniture. In the shower, plumbing works flawlessly. Faucets do not leak, and water leaves quickly.
  • Friendly staff who do their job efficiently. At the same time, employees do not require guests to tip for their services.
  • The hotel does not have its own pool, but tourists can go to the neighboring tourist complex and swim there.
  • The restaurant serves huge portions of dinner for dinner, which anyone can eat.
  • The hotel area is very well maintained and tidy. The building itself is also quite clean. There is no dust either on the windows or on the furniture.
  • Great location. Within walking distance is both the sea and the central part of the resort.
  • There are many nightclubs in the area, but loud music and noise at night do not interfere with sleep.

Should I stay in the complex?

If you are looking for a place for a budget holiday, then the Pliska 2 * complex (Sunny Beach) will be an excellent option. The main advantage of the hotel is its favorable location. You can stay here for youth companies and families with children. The hotel justifies the status of a two-star complex. In addition, some guests note that the rest here is much better than in some three-star hotels in Bulgaria.
