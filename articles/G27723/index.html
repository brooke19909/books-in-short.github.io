How to check a car for a deposit in banks: reliable ways, recommendations and reviews

Quite often, citizens buy a car on credit. However, under the terms of the loan agreement, they do not have the right to sell it until debt obligations are fully repaid . But unscrupulous individuals have developed a whole scheme to circumvent this rule. They resell the car to people who do not suspect that the car is in an encumbrance. How to check a car for a deposit in banks will be described in this article.

Owner restrictions

As mentioned above, for those who buy a car with a loan, certain restrictions apply. For example, a car is pledged to a bank when a sales contract is still being drawn up. That is, even at the time of purchase of the vehicle, the future car owner no longer has the right to completely dispose of it. Or in another option, when a certain amount of money is required, and the bank is ready to issue it against the security of the machine as a guarantee of return on invested funds. Naturally, the bank takes the registration certificate for the car and keeps it at home until the borrower repays the last payment.



In fact, the lack of a technical passport is not the reason that prevents the resale of vehicles. The car owner makes a copy of the TCP and can, without the above document, sell the car to another person. In addition, there is no single database of all pledged cars. It would seem that this problem could be solved if banks were more responsible for maintaining such a base. However, in practice it turns out that it does not matter for them who to pick up the car from in case of non-payment of debt. Thus, in order not to become a victim of a fraudster, you need to know how to check a car for a deposit in banks.

How to check a car for a deposit in banks

If you consider it from a theoretical point of view, you can contact all existing banks and request information from them on the availability of a pledge of a particular car. Naturally, in practice this is simply not feasible. No bank will give such information, because it does not have the right to do so. Everything related to customers is confidential.

If you are lucky, you can find a car in the database of banks and dealerships. But, unfortunately, not all credit organizations send their reports there. That is why information of this kind is limited and is not a reliable confirmation of the fact of encumbrance. So how to check a car, car, car for a deposit? There is an answer to this question. For example, you can see the insurance policy "Casco". If a bank is indicated in the ‚Äúbeneficiary‚Äù column, this is evidence that the car was purchased on credit. Trying to protect themselves from all sides, banks oblige their borrowers to insure vehicles so that in the event of an insured event, the company will cover them with all the damages. Thus, even the presence of a "Casco" policy at the seller should already alert. Due to the high cost of this insurance, ordinary drivers rarely purchase it.

You can contact the credit bureau and make a request to the seller. If in his history of credit payments there is a record of a car loan, then this will indicate that it is better to look for another purchase option.





Who could be the victim of scammers - victim reviews

In order to enjoy driving the purchased car and not worry about the bailiffs coming, you must carefully study all the documents and make some inquiries. What to do if the purchased car is pledged?

If, nevertheless, such an unpleasant incident happened to the buyer and it turned out that now his car is in an encumbrance with a credit organization, then there is a way out of this situation. You can sue and claim damages from an unscrupulous seller. However, most often, such sellers try to get rid of the car precisely because of the inability to pay the debt. That is why the court, having decided to recover the debt from the previous owner, can maximize its recognition as insolvent, that is, bankrupt. In this case, this does not make it easier for the bank or the injured party. The latter will repay the debt for the previous owner, if he has such an opportunity, and the bank may even lose its profit. Thus, knowing how to check the car for a deposit, you can avoid such unpleasant moments.
