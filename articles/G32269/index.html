Do-it-yourself iPhone 5 display replacement: step-by-step instructions

Before starting the repair process, prepare the workplace. It is desirable to cover the smooth surface of the table with a dense fabric in order to reduce the sliding effect. Good lighting and the sound of your favorite composition are ideal helpers in such a crucial business as replacing the display on your iPhone 5 with your own hands.

Step # 1: Release the frame of the screen module





  • Remove the two end screws that are located at the bottom of the chassis (at the edges of the system connector).
  • Install the suction cup remover on the protective glass (as close as possible to the Home button).
  • With some effort, as they say, without fanaticism, pull the ring fixture mounted on the front of the device, a tool.
  • Gently push the credit card inward, between the bottom of the case frame and the glass.
  • After a barely noticeable separation line appears, reinforce the suction cup retraction, while using auxiliary movements, pry off the outgoing glass with a plastic card.




Attention! It is at this point that replacing the display on the iPhone 5s often fails. And with rather deplorable consequences, since at the time of removal the unlucky repairman tears the connecting cable of the fingerprint sensor, which is mounted in the Home button.

  • So, if your device has an ambiguous addition in the form of the letter "S", then with a careful movement, unfasten the biosensor connector from the system board.
  • Smoothly move the lower part of the released screen module upwards, while the upper edge of the glass should remain in place (place the display vertically in relation to the horizontally placed telephone).

Step # 2: Removing the ‚Äúvisual‚Äù block

Replacing the display on the iPhone 5c (Russian-language interpretation of the S-modification) is done according to the same scenario - ‚Äúdisassembly / assembly‚Äù, as in the case with the previous fifth version of the device.

  • Unscrew the four screws of the protective cover (upper right corner), under which there are contact pads for connecting the cables of the module to be removed.
  • Then, with careful movements, disconnect the three connecting connectors from the motherboard of the mobile device.
  • Dismantling can be considered complete!

Step # 3: Reverse Process - Installing a New Part

  • First of all, transfer the speaker and its connecting cable to the new module, on which the light sensor and front camera are also installed .
  • Position the metal frame and the dismantled Home button (in the case of the iPhone 5s, the flex cable for the Home button).




  • Connect the connecting elements of the video block to the corresponding connectors.
  • Install a protective shield from above and fix it with the same four bolts.
  • Insert the frame into the frame, and the positioning process should begin from top to bottom.
  • Fasten the screen module with the end screws from the bottom.
