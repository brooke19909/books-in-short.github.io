Resting actively! Water Park Hotels Greece

In principle, not only avid travelers know that Greece is one of the most popular tourist countries. Every year, it receives millions of guests who come to the very heart of the Balkans in order to touch the ancient culture. But today, tourists are interested in not only world-class attractions, but also other entertainments, such as water rides. And popular wisdom, which says that in this country is everything, applies to them, too. Here the best hotels in Greece with a water park can offer their guests an unforgettable vacation. There are really many such attractions in the Hellenic country, it happens that there are several of them at one resort.





And usually hotels in Greece with a water park have 4 or 5 stars, so tourists will find a high level of service, good food and comfortable accommodation. And this includes a hotel complex called "Mitsis Family Village", which is located in the southwest of the island of Kos, in the town of Kardamena. This hotel has 219 rooms, including apartments, family rooms and private villas that are decorated in a traditional Greek style. All are equipped with air conditioning, comfortable furniture and modern appliances. And on the territory of the hotel there are several pools with sea and fresh water. There are also sports fields with free equipment. The water park itself is also located on the territory of this hotel, it has a separate area intended for children. There are low slides and a shallow pool.
