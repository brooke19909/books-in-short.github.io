A brief history of the resort

The village was founded at the beginning of the 19th century by the Dukhobor communities. In the middle of the century they were evicted to the Caucasus, and instead of them, peasants from neighboring provinces were settled.

Since the 1920s, the settlement has been developing as a medical resort. The peak of tourist development falls on the 60-80s of the XX century. It was during this period that the area was built up with rest houses, boarding houses and bases, beaches were built and infrastructure was developed.

In 1999, the village acquired the status of a state resort. Now it is a village with a 15-kilometer resort area. On its territory are located about 360 recreation centers and boarding houses and about 20 children's recreation complexes, which are located on the braids and in the town itself.
