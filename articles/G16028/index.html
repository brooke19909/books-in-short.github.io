Bulgaria, Sunny Beach, Royal Palace Helena Park 5 * - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Sunny Beach is one of the most popular resort places in Bulgaria. Tourists are waiting for the developed infrastructure, a wide selection of hotels, beautiful nature, excellent beaches and the warm Black Sea. According to many travelers, Royal Palace Helena Park 5 * is one of the best hotels in this resort. We will talk today about what the hotel liked the tourists so much for today.





Description and location

Royal Palace is part of the Royal Helena hotel complex, located on the Bulgarian Black Sea coast and represented by the Royal Palace Helena Park 5 * and Helena Sands 5 * hotels , as well as a number of villas. Royal Palace Helena Park 5 Housing Facility

In total, this hotel has 236 standard apartments and deluxe rooms. All of them have their own bathroom with bath or shower and hairdryer, TV (there are Russian channels), telephone, safe, refrigerator, individual air conditioning and a balcony. Rooms also include Wi-Fi. For a fee, you can use the room service. Apartments are cleaned and towels are changed every day. The maids renew their bed linen once every 2 days.

Prices

Royal Palace Helena Park 5 * (Bulgaria): reviews of tourists from the Russian Federation

Since today, most tourists planning a trip to a particular hotel make their choice, largely based on the impressions of other travelers who have already stayed here, we bring to your attention the generalized comments of our compatriots about their vacation in Royal Palace Helena Park . First of all, we note that many tourists were more than satisfied with this hotel. In addition, according to them, this hotel is one of the best in the entire resort of Sunny Beach. But we will understand in more detail.

Impressions of travelers about the hotel, housing stock and territory

First of all, many guests really liked the hotel building and its territory. So, the residential building is very stylish and elegantly decorated, inside everywhere there are paintings and sculptures. The territory, although small, is superbly planned and very well-groomed. Here you will find a lot of fountains, sculptures, benches, hanging swings , etc. There is nowhere to go for a walk, but this problem can be easily solved by going beyond the boundaries of Royal Palace Helena Park 5 *. So, there is a promenade that stretches along the coast. In addition, many tourists were very pleased with the constant presence of guards at the hotel.

As for the hotel rooms, they seemed to our compatriots very spacious and equipped with everything necessary. According to the guests, in most rooms Wi-Fi catches well. Guests liked the new comfortable furniture and modern appliances. So you will not find any noisy air conditioners and humming refrigerators. Room cleaning is regular and very high quality. In addition, if you leave a tip, the maids will construct funny figures from sheets and towels on your beds. In addition, shampoo, soap, shower gel, etc. are replenished daily and towels are updated. Bed linen is changed every two days.





Comments from travelers about the location and staff of the hotel

Exceptionally positive impressions were left by tourists from the location of Royal Palace Helena Park 5 *. The reviews of people who have been here are unambiguous: the hotel is located on the edge of Sunny Beach resort, which makes it rather quiet here. So no noise from discos and nightclubs will prevent you from sleeping. According to tourists, many even rested with open windows. Another advantage is the presence of a stop opposite the hotel, from where you can take a bus to Nessebar and other settlements.

As for the staff of the Royal Palace Helena Park, there were no particular complaints from the guests. Rather, on the contrary, many tourists say that the hotel staff is always very polite, smiling, friendly, attentive and willing to help. In addition, almost everyone here speaks good Russian, so you will not have any communication problems.





Guest reviews about meals

Almost all of our compatriots who visited the Royal Palace Helena Park 5 * (Bulgaria) very well respond to the level of food in this hotel. So, according to them, guests are offered a large selection of well-prepared dishes. At the same time, the buffet always had hot dishes from both meat and fish and seafood. You can also try cold snacks and salads (often they add seafood). Many guests liked the local cheeses and cold cuts very much . In addition, there was always plenty of fresh fruits and berries at guests' disposal. For lunch and dinner in the main restaurant you can get several types of ice cream. A big plus for family tourists was the fact that the menu always had cereals (semolina, buckwheat, rice), delicious soups and much more. So there was always something to feed the children. The only minor drawback was considered by some to be not very tasty coffee from vending machines in the restaurant. However, for an additional fee, you always have the opportunity to order good coffee.

In addition to the main restaurant, you can have lunch and a snack during the day on the platform near the pool. But the choice of dishes is not so wide. However, themed dinners (Mediterranean, Bulgarian and barbecue) are held here three times a week. Experienced travelers recommend visiting them.





The opinion of guests about a beach holiday

Tourists resting in the Royal Palace Helena Park 5 * (Sunny Beach), claim that the hotel's own beach is really only 50 meters away. Free sunbeds are almost always there. However, loungers on the first line, as a rule, guests occupy in the morning. The beach is sandy. At the entrance to the sea there is a pebble strip one or two meters wide, then there is again a sandy bottom. There is a beach area with paid sunbeds. Some tourists preferred to relax on it, since there are fewer people and a completely sandy entry into the water. As for beach towels, you will find them in your room upon check-in. You can change them for clean during the day in a special kiosk near the pool.





Impressions of our compatriots about animation and entertainment at the hotel

As for the animation in Royal Palace Helena Park 5 *, then in general it seemed rather weak to most hotel guests. Water aerobics takes place here during the day and a couple of competitions are organized near the pool, and in the evenings the animators hold shows, but they do not collect special spectators and participants. Also in the evenings there is karaoke and a disco. There is also animation for the little ones: a games room during the day and a mini disco in the evenings.

On site you can play volleyball or have a good time by the pool. There is also a bar with a large selection of drinks. Also, guests are advised to use the free coupons for visiting the bath at the spa center offered to you upon check-in. According to them, there are several types of excellent paired for every taste and relaxation zone. A massage service is available at an extra cost.
