Hotel "Samara", Lazarevskoye: photos and reviews

The modern rhythm of life exhausts people so much, takes all our strength and vitality, that each of us simply needs a rest. Each person has a rest in his own way. Someone likes outdoor activities, someone prefers to sit in the most comfortable home atmosphere in front of the TV, in this position and spend his vacation.

But only the sea can provide an opportunity to be distracted, truly relax and clear your thoughts of everything negative and evil. That is why so many tourists and vacationers leave at the first opportunity on the coast. Of course, currently overseas resorts are very popular, some tourists go to the best Asian countries for the sake of exotics.

But in the pursuit of luxury and impeccable service, we completely forgot that the sea is in our country. In order to bask in the sun and swim in warm sea water, it is not necessary to cross the border. Rest in the Krasnodar Territory is a budget alternative to fashionable overseas resorts.

It is foolish to count on excellent service, luxurious rooms and landscaped grounds. But if you take the choice of hotel more carefully and responsibly, for little money you can have a great rest in Russia. This option will delight those who, for certain reasons, cannot leave the country. We offer to pay attention to the hotel "Samara" (Lazarevskoye). Reviews about him are few, but they are enough to objectively evaluate it. Moreover, Lazarevskoye is considered an excellent resort with a developed infrastructure.

Rooms

Hotel "Samara House" (Lazarevskoye) takes care of the comfort of living of all its guests. The hotel offers single, double, triple and even quadruple rooms with necessary amenities. Each room has a private bathroom, a set of functional furniture, as well as air conditioning, which provides coolness and freshness in the suffocating heat on the street, and a TV with satellite channels that organizes leisure travelers.

The hotel has two suites that will satisfy the requirements of even the most demanding vacationers. The furniture in the rooms is represented by comfortable beds, a wardrobe, bedside tables. The furniture is not new, but quite solid and comfortable to use.

If you choose a two-room number, you will see a set of a sofa and comfortable armchairs in it. All rooms have access to a shared balcony, some of them are facing the sea, so vacationers can enjoy stunning views.

Now, with regard to the state of plumbing - it is clear that it is not new, but it works properly. At the height of the season, problems with the supply of hot water are possible, but they are temporary. If you believe the reviews of tourists, the equipment is working properly, in particular, the air conditioner cools the air in the room to a predetermined temperature in a matter of moments.





Territory

Surely you imagine a rather comfortable and relaxed vacation in Lazarevsky. Hotel "Samara" really can offer you this. The hotel area is small, but well-maintained. Vacationers note a good garden, broken in the courtyard, a beautiful artificial pond laid out with natural stone, as well as a great outdoor pool with umbrellas and sun loungers.

It's no secret that the weather in the resorts of the Krasnodar Territory is very unpredictable, and so the pool will become a worthy alternative to sea water and help organize leisure activities in case the sea is not affectionate and supportive to vacationers.

Small on-site parking is provided for parking of hotel guests. Your transport will be guarded around the clock, so there is not the slightest cause for concern.

Nutrition

Unfortunately, there is no restaurant on the territory of the hotel, so all vacationers will have to eat in local dining rooms, cafes, maybe restaurants. Sea and beach

If you choose the Samara Hotel (Lazarevskoye) for your holiday on the Black Sea, you will be able to meet sunrises and see off sunsets every day on the coast. The sea is just around the corner, only 5-7 minutes walk. All tourists will find a gorgeous pebble beach with a gentle entry into the water and developed infrastructure. On the beach for an additional fee, you can rent a sunbed and umbrella, which protects from scorching sunlight. Here, on the beach, you can rent sports equipment, do water sports, which you have long dreamed about. Due to the developed infrastructure, the cleanliness of the beach, families with small children often relax here.





To summarize

Hotel "Samara-House" in Lazarevsky is an excellent option for relaxing both youth and families with small children. It offers comfortable rooms, good service and access to the resort infrastructure. If you are looking for an inexpensive but decent option for your stay during your vacation on the Black Sea, take a closer look at this hotel.
