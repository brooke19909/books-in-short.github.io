Calculation of the cost of products

The produced goods should, on the one hand, justify all costs incurred on it, and on the other hand, bring the desired income. How to calculate the objective price of your product?

The cost of a thing is established taking into account the following criteria:

  • the average market price of similar products;
  • the cost of yarn;
  • aggregate costs of raw materials and delivery, multiplied by the coefficient of complexity of work;
  • time spent.

Determining the cost of the product, you will need to take into account the color of the yarn. So, if it is dark, then working with it is more difficult due to the fact that it quickly causes eye fatigue.

It is worth noting that a single formula for determining the cost of a product tied by a master manually does not exist. Each needlewoman has the right to independently set the price.
