Crochet summer tops - unique and versatile clothing

In the hot season, absolutely everyone - adults and children, try to wear clothes that maximize the body's ability to breathe and cover it as little as possible. Today, knitted clothing is almost the most fashionable direction. Creating crochet summer tops is in great demand. Each item that you have connected and replenished your wardrobe is unique! Such clothes will become your favorite because they are comfortable, practical and very beautiful.

Top - clothes that do not have sleeves. This is a generic name for a T-shirt, blouse, underwear or swimsuit. It is possible to create it from any type of yarn. Aerial clothing crocheted with an openwork pattern is the best choice on a hot day.

This thing can be either integrally knitted or from individual motives - round, square or indefinite shape. You can tie many different flowers and leaves, and then combine them into a single canvas.

The most beautiful dresses, shorts, T-shirts for the summer season, our charming kids can be bought in stores. And you can pick up a crochet hook, the brightest yarn, love and tenderness - and even knit stunning unique things.

Let's create clothes for a little girl that her girlfriends in the sandbox will certainly not have.

So, summer crochet top, description:

We will need:

  • Yarn with a natural composition for knitting summer things of different colors, matching each other in shades, for example, red, orange, yellow, green or green, blue, blue, purple;
  • Knitting hook No. 2 or No. 3:
  • 2 beautiful small buttons.

Let's start knitting from the top in the middle of the front. We collect 6 air loops. We close them in a ring. We tie seven crochet columns, introducing the hook into the ring. We turn the knitting. 3 air loops for lifting, in each column of the previous row - 2 double crochets, in total there will be 14. In the next row we knit 27 double crochets and so on.

We continue knitting, making sure that the fabric remains flat. The color of the threads is changed through an equal distance (4-7 rows). We knit in this way to the length of the topic we need. We tie the bottom edge with small fans, and the top with single crochet columns.

We try on a T-shirt for a child and determine the length of the straps. We will knit them with yarn of the color from which we started work. We collect a chain of air loops equal to the length of the strap + 10 loops (this is the length of the buttonhole for the button). We knit with crochets, starting with the eleventh from the hook of the air loop. We need to knit 2 such strips. We sew them in the right places, on the back we fix the buttons, and our wonderful bright summer top is crocheted.
