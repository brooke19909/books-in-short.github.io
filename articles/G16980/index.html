Hotel Gangehi Island Resort 5 *, Maldives: description, service, reviews

Maldives is a real paradise for travelers seeking complete relaxation and peace. 1190 small islands surrounded by the cleanest sea and coral reefs are perfect for scuba diving. However, rest here can hardly be called budget. Most often there are five-star hotels, one of which is the Gangehi Island Resort 5 * complex. Its location, number of rooms, services and reviews will be discussed in this article.

Gangehi Island Resort Hotel: general information and location

The tourist complex is located on the northern edge of the small atoll Ari. Its dimensions are only 200 meters wide and 150 meters long. There is an impressive coral ledge near the island, so this hotel is perfect for diving or snorkeling . The nearest airport is Khuhule Airport, in Male, 83 km from Ari. You can get to the hotel by seaplane. The trip takes only half an hour. But you should know that planes fly only from 06:00 to 16:00, so if you arrive later than this time, you will have to arrange an overnight stay in Male at your own expense. The hotel can arrange a shuttle service upon request. A direct Moscow-Maldives flight will take about 9 hours.





This welcoming hotel was opened in 2008. It works all year round, taking tourists even on New Year and Christmas holidays. Minimum stay in the complex is 5 days. You can come here with small children, for them the staff of Gangehi Island Resort provide an extra bed. But with pets check-in is prohibited even for a fee. Registration of tourists takes place at the reception from 14:00 to 20:00 local time. You need to leave the hotel before noon. You can pay for all additional services only with a credit card.

Number of rooms and its equipment

The complex has prepared for its guests 45 rooms, which are located in small cozy bungalows. Most of them are built directly on the water, so the beach can be reached in 1-2 minutes. Some villas are located on the coast. They are surrounded by a lush tropical garden. We list the main types of rooms that Gangehi Island Resort (Maldives) offers:

  • Club Rooms - These apartments are located deep in the island in the main hotel building. The most budget rooms that do not differ in large area and exquisite decoration. Their area is 34 square meters. m. They are designed for 2 adults and 1 child.
  • Garden Villa - spacious bungalows located at a distance from the beach. Surrounded by lush tropical vegetation. Area - 64 square meters. m. Hold up to 3 adults. This room has a double bed.
  • Beach Villa - bungalows located on the coast. The configuration does not differ from Garden Villa, however, they have direct access to the beach and ocean views.
  • Overwater Villa - villas built on the water. There is a private terrace with sun loungers. Area - 76 square meters. m. Allowed 3 adults and 1 child.
  • Overwater Deluxe Villa - improved villas, from the windows of which you can watch sunrise or sunset. Area - 102 square meters m. 4 adults can live here.




Each room is equipped with individual air conditioning, a fridge and a minibar for storing drinks. A coffee machine and tea set are available on request for a fee. The bathroom has a hairdryer and bathrobe for each guest. Free wifi works . Guestrooms feature plasma televisions with local cable television, safes, telephones and fans.

Local infrastructure

Since the territory of the Gangehi Island Resort hotel is significantly removed from the benefits of civilization, there are all the infrastructure facilities necessary for a comfortable stay. In the main building of the complex there is an Internet cafe where guests can get high-speed access to the network. The island has its own store, which sells beachwear and shoes, souvenirs and gifts, as well as jewelry. You can relax and unwind in the wellness center, which offers massage services and Ayurvedic treatments. True, you can visit it only for a fee.





At the reception and in the bar there is free Wi-Fi , but its speed is only suitable for viewing social networks. Guests can also relax in the air-conditioned living room with TVs, or in the library. Paid laundry staff will take care of your belongings. In addition, if necessary, you can ask the administrator to call a doctor on the island.

Entertainment

Entertainment in the Gangehi Island Resort 5 * complex is focused on a relaxing beach holiday. On their own beach, tourists can use sun loungers and parasols for free. You can also go diving or snorkelling. In addition, the hotel has an experienced marine biologist who will accompany the swimming with an interesting story about the inhabitants of the house reef. Tourists can use the gym for free. Several times a week, instructors conduct group classes in yoga and water gymnastics. On the beach you can also ride a canoe, play volleyball or go fishing. In the evening, animators conduct an entertainment program and discos. Guests can play a variety of board games at the bar.





Suggested meals

Since Gangehi Island Resort Maldives is located at a distance from the main island, the hotel completely takes care of the entire catering. When you make a voucher, you can only buy breakfast, pay full board or an all-inclusive system.

You can eat fully in the restaurant, located in the main building. A daily buffet is served here, serving international and Asian dishes. Upon request, all guests can enjoy a special diet menu. If you want to try something special, then for a fee you can visit the restaurant, open on the terrace overlooking the ocean. Grilled fish and other seafood are cooked here. For a bite to eat, guests can check out the snack bar . Soft drinks and spirits, cocktails are served in the bar.





Gangehi Island Resort: positive reviews

Before choosing a hotel and buying a ticket, experienced tourists usually seek feedback on their proposed vacation destination. Unlike advertising brochures, they truthfully describe not only the advantages of the hotel, but also its shortcomings. Hotels in the Maldives tend to have an excellent reputation because they provide top class service. The complex described by us was no exception. Its guests note the following advantages of relaxing in this place:

  • Absolute cleanliness throughout the island. The rooms are cleaned 2 times a day, bath and beach towels are regularly updated.
  • Friendly staff who will be happy to help you solve problems. But it is worth noting that the administrators here do not speak Russian.
  • Varied and always fresh buffet meals at Gangehi Island Resort. The menu always has a lot of fruits, from which you can prepare fresh and squeezed juices.
  • Huge portions at the √† la carte restaurant. You can save money and safely take one dish for two.
  • If there are free rooms at check-in, tourists are offered to stay in superior apartments for free.




Negative hotel reviews

It is worth noting that negative feedback about the Gangehi Island Resort is written quite rarely. As a rule, they describe minor flaws that are unlikely to greatly spoil the impression of rest. We list the main disadvantages of the hotel that tourists have identified:

  • The staff will arrange a very expensive shuttle service. You can get to the island on your own much cheaper.
  • You cannot take food and drinks out of the restaurant.
  • Unpleasant smell in the bathroom in the rooms.
  • Wi-Fi works intermittently, and its speed leaves much to be desired.




Draw conclusions

We can say that this hotel is distinguished by high-quality service, well-organized food and a wide selection of entertainment, so it fully justifies the cost of living. The only minus of traveling to this place is usually called the long flight Moscow-Maldives, which takes a lot of time. Most often diving enthusiasts, young couples come here, but tourists with children are almost never here. Often wedding ceremonies are celebrated on the island. The hotel is perfect for an active and relaxing beach holiday.
