Hotel Karteros Hotel 3 * (Crete): description, reviews and photos

Do you dream to visit Greece? Carefully plan your vacation, plan a route and think about what you would like to see first of all and in which hotel to be located. For example, Karteros Hotel 3 is an excellent solution for those who are interested in cultural activities and sightseeing trips.

A bit of history

The Greek island of Crete is one of the favorite vacation spots of tourists from different countries. Resorts of various levels are located throughout its territory, there are many attractions.

Of course, the first thing to do is find out what famous places, temples and palaces are in the district. And only after that choose a decent hotel. Those who like a quiet, secluded family vacation, we recommend choosing the area Karteros (Crete). It is very beautiful, a lot of greenery, there are many popular excursion routes nearby.

The city of Heraklion is the capital of the island. According to historians, about 2.5 thousand years ago there were already settlements here. In confirmation of this, it is worth visiting the ancient palace of Knossos, which is one of the most ancient buildings indicating that there was a civilization. In the old part of Heraklion you can visit the church of St. Mark, visit the Venetian fortress, Loggia and admire the Morozini fountain. Also in the modern capital are open restaurants, bars and discos. If you want to relax, be sure to use this information. Only 6 km from the city center is the modest but very cozy Minos Village Karteros Hotel 3. It is often chosen by those tourists who do not want to spend too much on expensive accommodation: it is important for them to get acquainted with the history of Crete, to visit the sights.

Short hotel description

Hotel located a few kilometers from the central airport, on the seafront, in the picturesque town of Karteros. Sometimes you can find the old name of the hotel - Minos Bay Karteros. The innkeeper named Minos called the "brainchild" in his honor. But more recently, the hotel began to be called in a new way. Now it is called Minos Village Karteros Hotel.



Tourists are pleased to note that the territory is clean, spacious and well-groomed, there is a swimming pool surrounded by a magnificent dense garden. The hotel itself consists of several two-story buildings built in the traditional Greek style - in compliance with clear geometric shapes, without frills. It is cozy, homely, comfortable and warm. Near the entrance there is a terrace with tables. The front door opens onto a narrow small street where there are practically no cars. A very convenient option for parking a car. This is especially true for those tourists who rent a car, and return to the hotel only for the night.

Room Reservations and General Policies

Check-in at the hotel lasts from 14.00 to 23.00. Official check-out of guests is from 7.00 to 12.00. I would like to note that the reception is open around the clock. If you cannot check out on time, then the hotel staff will always meet you and can extend the procedure for a couple of hours. Pets are allowed, the weight of which does not exceed 5 kg.

At Karteros Hotel 3 The system of remote room reservation is working. Moreover, they do not require any additional funds, payment for accommodation is carried out at check-out. The administration pre-blocks funds on a credit card. If there is a need to cancel the reservation, it is best to do this no later than a day before arrival at the hotel. Please note that conditions may vary for different rooms. Credit cards Visa, MasterCard, American Express or cash are accepted.

The owner of the hotel has some rules, such as a ban on the use of their drinks near the pool and during meals. There is also a strictly designated time for breakfast, lunch and dinner - the rule is not violated.

Services requiring additional payment

Minos Village Karteros Hotel 3 offers car hire or motorcycle rental. A safe at the reception desk is a great option for storing documents and personal valuables. You can take a taxi, arrange a transfer.

Vacationers can order food in the room, rent a banquet hall. Laundry and dry cleaning services are provided, as well as ironing. The nanny works.

Services included in the price

  • Bicycle rental and free parking.
  • Currency exchange.
  • A visit to the gym.
  • Animation: Greek style BBQ dinner (once a week).
  • Outdoor pool (open from April to October).
  • Wireless Internet.
  • Table tennis.
  • Billiards.
  • Playground for games.
  • Luggage storage.

Which number to choose?

Minos Village Karteros Hotel It offers 20 standard rooms and 40 apartments. There are air conditioners, a heating system is installed. The rooms have access to the balcony. It has a TV with satellite channels and a fridge.

In the bathroom - shower and hairdryer, bath accessories. Sleeping places - 1 double bed or 2 single. There are cabinets with hangers and drawers.

The apartment has 2 rooms. The first has 2 single beds, a kitchenette (with hobs, an electric kettle and kitchen utensils), the second has 1 double bed.

There is a refrigerator, a telephone, a TV and a safe. The apartments have a beautiful bathroom, and there is also a shower.

The rooms are cleaned every other day, they take out the garbage and wash the floors. Bed linen - 2 times a week. Issue 3 towels, change them 1 time in 7 days.

Food options

Tourists staying at Minos Village Karteros, They offer half-board meals, that is, breakfast from 8.00 to 10.00 and dinner from 19.30 to 21.00. Additional drinks are served during dinner, such as wine (red, white and pink), beer, there is Cola, sprite and soda. From 16.00 to 17.00 you can order tea or coffee with pastries, eat ice cream.



Breakfast is a bit monotonous, so the dishes quickly get boring. However, for many tourists the assortment is not satisfactory: ham and cheese, Greek salad and pizza, tea, coffee, juice, milk and delicious homemade cakes. Yogurt, fruit and eggs are rarely served for breakfast. For dinner, the menu is more extensive. They offer meat and fish, vegetables and fruits, everything is delicious. You can order eggplant with feta, kebabs, potato and meat casseroles, side dishes, original salads. There are several Cretan dishes. Dessert is not provided.

On the territory of Karteros Hotel 3 There is a bar and a restaurant. The menu here is extensive, dominated by European and Greek cuisine. If one of the visitors follows a special diet, they will certainly be offered dishes suitable for his nutrition system. The restaurant has 2 rooms: open (near the pool) and indoor.

Entertainment for adults and children

At Karteros Hotel There is a multi-level pool, divided into several sections with different depths.

Adults and children can swim here. Sun loungers are installed near the pool (free), you can ask for an umbrella (they do not require additional payment). As for entertainment on site, you can advise to visit the billiard room or play ping pong. Children spend time in the playground. The hotel is convenient in that it is located near the capital, where you can take a bus (stop nearby) or a rented car.

Be sure to walk towards Heraklion. On the way there are taverns, shops, and a pastry shop where they offer delicious pastries. But the most interesting thing is that nearby there is a stable where you can ride horses.

Beach vacation

The distance to the beach does not exceed 150 m. The coastal territory borders on other resort areas. The closest "neighbor" of Karteros Hotel is Amnisos, a popular destination for beach holidays for many tourists. The seashore is covered mainly with fine golden sand with pebble inclusions. Umbrellas and sunbeds are available at an additional cost.

The road leading to the beach is located to the left of the hotel. In the season until 17.30 on the coast there is always a lifeguard on duty. The entrance to the sea is sand and pebble, but sometimes stones come across. Depth increases gradually, you can swim wherever you like. It will not be very comfortable for parents with children. The sea is often hectic, with high waves, which is why swimming with kids is problematic.

Depending on the height of the waves, duty officers hang out a green, orange or red flag. So vacationers can independently assess the degree of danger to themselves.

Sightseeing vacation

Sightseeing tickets are best purchased on Heraklion's seafront. For example, a trip to Santorini will cost 100 euros (for tour operators it is much more expensive). You can independently go by car towards the southern coast, visit the Gulf of Elounda and Lake Kournas. If you choose a walk in Heraklion, then you are lucky enough to see a beautiful church in the rock. An interesting excursion should be added to a visit to the Cretan evening and the Butari winery.

If you rent a car, then in a couple of days you can visit Knossos and the fortress in Rethymnon on your own, see the cave of Zeus (which, according to legend, was born on Crete) and visit the oceanarium.



Tired of endless walks along the coast, drop by a cozy Greek cafe where you will be treated to amazing coffee. There are many traditional taverns on the waterfront. Be sure to order a lunch or dinner in the Greek style, feel the hospitality and kindness of the locals.
