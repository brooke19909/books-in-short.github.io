Non-standard types of insurance in the Russian Federation

Life insurance, health, property insurance against various risks has long entered our lives, has become part of everyday life. In this article we will deal with the main types of insurance and select from their number the most specific, non-standard, unusual categories.

The main types of insurance in Russia

First of all, insurance contracts are divided into 2 types:

  • Mandatory . The law compels such insurance, and ignoring its requirements leads to appropriate penalties. This category includes compulsory motor third-party liability insurance, insurance for passengers, compulsory medical insurance.
  • Voluntary An insurance contract is concluded solely at the request of a citizen.

What types of insurance are there? It can also be individual or group, and payments on it - one-time or as annuity. By determining the amount of compensation, there is a proportional compensation (any agreed part of the damage) and the first risk system (all damage is paid within the agreed insurance amount).





According to Chapter 48 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation, types of insurance in the Russian Federation are divided into 4 categories:

  • property;
  • personal
  • responsibility;
  • entrepreneurial risks;
  • in light of recent trends, specific ones are also singled out.

Briefly consider all the voiced types.

Property insurance

Three types of insurance can be attributed to this category:

  • real estate (including engineering structures inside the insured premises);
  • vehicles (both in the case of complete destruction, and in the event of damage);
  • cargo (during transportation, unloading, storage).




Personal insurance

One of the most common and comprehensive types of insurance in Russia. It refers to:

  • life insurance (recently, accumulative life insurance has also been included here);
  • accident insurance (injury, illness, deprivation of an organ or part of the body, disability, death);
  • medical insurance (compulsory medical insurance - mandatory; voluntary medical insurance - voluntary);
  • minors insurance;
  • pensioners insurance (compensation is available only upon reaching retirement age) - compulsory and voluntary;
  • Passenger insurance of various modes of transport;
  • employee insurance.




Liability Insurance

This category is very multifaceted. We list a number of insurance that it includes:

  • civil liability for causing any harm to third parties;
  • responsibility for the quality of manufactured goods;
  • liability for defaults to the creditor;
  • occupational liability - for harming the employee to customers, consumers;
  • civil liability of organizations, enterprises that constitute a source of increased danger;
  • civil liability of various carriers - urban, railway, air, water transport, metro, etc .;
  • CTP.




Business insurance

This includes insurance:

  • financial guarantees;
  • loss or damage to property of the enterprise;
  • investment;
  • risk of default;
  • deposits;
  • export credit.




Non-Standard Risk Insurance

Non-standard types of insurance are quite interesting:

  • insurance of construction activities - the responsibility of developers;
  • agricultural;
  • international (for access to medical services of world clinics, as well as for citizens who are often abroad);
  • collective (insurance of several persons as a whole);
  • title (risk of loss of ownership by court order);
  • insurance of risks associated with financial investments;
  • political risks (the influence of factors related to the policy of a state on the transaction).
