Tachai Island - a paradise in the Andaman Sea

The island of Tachai is known in a tourist environment due to the virgin purity of its sand. If you get here, you will have every chance to be convinced of this on your own experience.

About paragraph

Tachai island (Thailand) is located at some distance from the islands of the Similan group. However, he is still ranked among her by a 1998 decree.

Tachai Island is the territory where it was possible to preserve the purity and innocence of nature. Man did not have time to significantly change anything here. There is an opportunity to stroll between the tropical thickets, from which a blissful fragrance emanates, to admire their splendor and multicolor. Being on the ocean, you will see in front of you a bright blue water, characterized by crystal clearness. You can soak up the white sand without debris. This is exactly what nature thought the island of Tachai. Fortunately, now we can touch this magnificence.

An attractive spot for divers is the local coral reef. It can be called one of the most magnificent in the world. In a great variety in the local waters live fish characteristic of the tropics. In addition to them, turtles, underwater slopes and different types of sharks chose this environment for themselves.





Special zone

Tachai Island is included in the heritage of a national park called Mu Ko Similan. Thanks to this, access to it has become more limited. Tourists are allowed here without obstacles from November to April. To restore the nature of this beautiful place, the rest of the time they give her rest and stop contact with tourists. Whatever you say, human touches leave their mark.

The Andaman Sea, where this item is located, is an excellent environment for the development of wildlife. There is a distance of 20 kilometers to the archipelago. Such entertainment, a way of relaxation, like an excursion to the island of Tachai, became available only about two years ago. So, they have not yet managed to discredit nature, and the administration is making every effort to continue to do so. Once here, you forget completely about the existence of the world outside. Reason is immersed in silence, and the soul experiences true grace.





What awaits a tourist

Tours here include spending time on beautiful beaches that are over 700 meters long. Thanks to an experienced guide, you can get into the very depths of the island, where colonies of the so-called chicken crabs live, whose livelihoods can be looked at.

Together with the group you will climb the mountain, where there is a viewing platform. You have to spend only 7 minutes climbing. There is also a swing for photos of couples in love. The water here is very clean. Through it you can clearly see the different tones of blue.

They also carry out such an interesting procedure as snorkeling, which implies swimming under the water surface. You will be given a pipe and a mask. Thus, you will see the turtles living at the bottom, as well as the stunning landscape of this area. There are all the wonderful details that can be seen in resort points such as the Red Sea.

Where and how much to buy a ticket?

If you have enough enthusiasm to visit Tachai (island), how to get there becomes one of the main issues that interest many tourists who have not been there. And really few people managed to visit this paradise because of the short duration of its existence. There is nothing difficult to visit.

Many travel agencies Khao Laki and Phuket are engaged in the implementation of trips there. To do this, you need to fly directly to one of these cities, where you will be included in the excursion group. Such a trip can cost about 2000 baht (at least one person). This price includes a wonderful vacation on the sea coast, snorkeling, a walk in the forest full of tropical plants. Tours, including diving, are easy to get. They can cost 4,500 baht per person.
