"Frigate", recreation center in Orekhovo: description, reviews

Picturesque lake, pristine nature, mushrooms and berries in a pine forest - all this is offered to you by Fregat, a recreation center in Orekhovo, fifty kilometers from the Northern capital. Residents of St. Petersburg are happy to come here for the weekend. It is also an ideal place for a rural vacation. The health resort welcomes guests all year round.

Description

The camp site is located 3.5 kilometers from the Orekhovo railway station. This place is great for lovers of an active lifestyle. Fans of volleyball and basketball can compete in the indoor gymnasium, there are tables for playing table tennis and billiards, gym services are offered. It is pleasant to swim in clear lake water, and sunbathing can be taken on a floating pier and shore flooring. On the territory there is a dining room, mostly vacationers cook on their own. Corporate events are held at specially equipped venues. Arbours for picnics are equipped, fans of cooking barbecue in nature can rent a barbecue. Guest Accommodation

Frigate, a recreation center in Orekhovo, offers guests several options for accommodation.

  • In two-story cottages of superior comfort at the same time can accommodate from 2 to 12 people. Each has two units with a separate entrance. The rooms in the cottages are double and triple.
  • The one-story cottage of superior comfort, designed to accommodate from 4 to 9 people, also has two blocks with a separate entrance. The rooms in the cottage are double and triple.
  • In two-story standard cottages at the same time can accommodate up to 14 people. The rooms are double and triple. Tourists living in such houses can use the bathroom, washbasins and showers located in a separate heated building.

Additional services

In order to make your stay at the camp site more diverse and eventful, various services are provided here.

  • Guests can enjoy their time in the fireplace room with billiards.
  • The services of a Russian wood bath with the possibility of acquiring brooms and other bath accessories are provided, there is a relaxation room with a music center, a TV and a samovar.
  • You can rent a boat, bike, other sports equipment.
  • Banquet facilities are available.
  • There are places for cooking barbecue.
  • At the recreation center in Orekhovo, there are outdoor volleyball and mini-football courts, an indoor sports hall for tourists.
  • At the camp site "Frigate" equipped with a platform for team building in nature.
  • The administration will help you with corporate events and holidays.
