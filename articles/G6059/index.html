Pension "South" of Adler and Crimea: features of the number of rooms, food, entertainment

Pension Yuzhny (Adler) consists of two parts. These are “South” and “South-2”. The second object is located closer to the sea than the first. In addition, it was built on the eve of the Olympics, so its condition is much better, and comfort is at a higher level.

Infrastructure

Pension occupies an eight-story building. A medical center is attached to it. There is also a cinema and concert hall. The ground floor is the place where the restaurant, sauna, rental is located. The entrance to the indoor pool and the games room are located here. Minus the ground floor is the location of the technical rooms, karaoke hall and disco.





They offer to rent everything you need for entertainment:

  1. Bicycles
  2. Sticks for walking.
  3. Mini-golf clubs.
  4. Tennis rackets and more.

At the reception there is a Sberbank terminal and an ATM. In addition, the institution has a Turkish and Finnish baths, laundry, bowling room, manufactured goods and souvenir shops.

Anyone presenting a spa card can attend the prescribed procedures.

Territory arrangement

Pension "South" Adler occupies a large territory, which everywhere has lights, including near the pool and fountains. From a heated outdoor pool, a slowly flowing river leads to a children's pool. In addition, on the territory there are:

  1. Playground.
  2. Mini golf course.
  3. Several tennis courts.

Room Features

In the dormitory building, rooms of different categories are presented:

  1. VIP Suite. Consists of 2 rooms, designed to accommodate 4 people. Pension Yuzhny (Adler) provides only one room at this level.
  2. Studio Suite. The institution provides six one-room rooms of this type, designed to accommodate four visitors.
  3. Suite In the pension there are seven two-room 4-bed rooms of this class.
  4. The standard is improved. This category is 6 double one-room numbers.
  5. Standard 3-seater. Consists of one room. The institution has 48 rooms of this class.
  6. Double standard. It occupies 1 room. There are 105 rooms of this class.


In addition to a large bed and a TV, each room has a built-in wardrobe. Thanks to the backlight, it is easy to find the necessary things in it even for those who arrive at night or early in the morning. Slippers, bathrobes, clothes hanger are available in all rooms. Guests of luxury rooms can use refrigerators with transparent doors, built-in air conditioning, which can be controlled by a conventional remote control, as well as safes.

The bathrooms have underfloor heating, sliding doors. Rattan furniture is installed on the balconies - a table and two chairs.

Nutrition

How does the “Southern” Adler boarding house offer food? Since it is located in the south, where almost all year round there are fresh vegetables and fruits, they account for 40% of the diet. Various salads and juices are served both for breakfast and for lunch and dinner.





Waiters serve porridge, sour cream, cottage cheese, Ossetian pies, pancakes, meatballs, meat, fish, cheeses, sliced ​​and many other dishes. They are always helpful and attentive. Breakfast consists of a large amount of food, lunch and dinner are much more modest.

Entertainment

A vacation without entertainment can hardly be called a complete rest. The Yuzhny boarding house (Adler) offers its customers a variety of entertainment programs. The reviews of those who rested here indicate that the entertainment in the pension is worthy of attention:

  1. Evening karaoke and discos.
  2. Entertainment programs on various topics.
  3. Boat trips.
  4. Jet skiing, catamaran.
  5. Umbrellas, sun loungers, mattresses are offered on the beach for vacationers from the pension.
  6. Various organized excursions - to the Olympic venues and to the Riviera park (Sochi), to museums, to Krasnaya Polyana.

It is impossible to list everything that the Yuzhny-2 boarding house (Adler) offers for vacationers. Contact facility: st. Lenin, house number 233.

Pension in Crimea

In the territory of Crimea there is a similar institution with the same name. Pension "South" of Crimea is the best institution for family holidays in the western part of the peninsula. First of all, it is known for its successful location: the boarding house is located in the park, which was designed by specialists of the Nikitsky Garden.





Here guests are welcomed according to the high standards of Crimean hospitality - cordially and with due respect. Funny contests, funny games, show programs, in which the guests of the institution take part under the guidance of a team of professionals, are always noisy, fun and not boring here.

Three meals a day boarding house operates on a buffet system, taking into account the small guests. Since the place where the Yuzhny boarding house is located is the Crimea, you can always try one of the national drinks - wine, go for a tasting at the winery or any other object of interest for the organized excursion.
