Guest House "Cranes" (Dzhemete): overview, description and reviews of tourists

You can fully relax with the whole family by choosing the Cranes Guest House. Dzhemete will please you with beautiful nature, warm sea and developed resort infrastructure.





a brief description of

In a quiet resort area is located the guest house "Cranes". Dzhemete is one of the most popular destinations for domestic tourism. This institution is open from May to October. Hotel Policies

Choosing the guest house "Cranes" (Dzhemete) for accommodation, guests are obligated to abide by the rules of accommodation. It is worth paying attention to the following points:

  • Check-in starts after 14:00, and check-out is before noon;
  • for registration, guests are required to provide an identity card, as well as a birth certificate for each of the minor children;
  • If you evict from the hotel earlier than the declared time, the prepaid amount will be charged as a fine;
  • Extraordinary replacement of towels and bed linen is subject to an additional charge;
  • starting from the third day of stay, rooms are cleaned every other day;
  • after 23:00, guests are required to remain silent on site;
  • in case of damage to the property of the hotel, the guest agrees to compensate the full price;
  • after 23:00 it is forbidden to bring guests to the hotel;
  • rooms cannot use electrical appliances;
  • It is forbidden to leave strangers in the room, as well as give them keys;
  • You can not bring food and drinks purchased in other places to the dining room;
  • Without the permission of the administration, dishes and cutlery cannot be taken out of the restaurant.
