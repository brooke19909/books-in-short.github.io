Hotel "Volga" (Ulyanovsk) - a modern hotel in a classical style

Hotel Volga (Ulyanovsk) is located in the historical and cultural part of the city. Most of the hotel rooms, which are located on Goncharova Street, overlook the river of the same name. The hotel is very well located for guests who can, while living in it, comfortably spend business days and participate in the cultural life of the city. Also, the Volga Hotel (Ulyanovsk) is quite close to the airport and the railway station.

The hotel was built during the Soviet Union and is a classic option for accommodating city guests who arrived in Ulyanovsk with business or cultural intentions.





The Volga Hotel (Ulyanovsk) has a developed infrastructure that includes cafes, souvenir shops, ATMs of various banks, a hairdresser, a conference room, cash desks of the railway station and airports, and a sauna. It also offers free Wi-Fi throughout.

Business Services

If the purpose of the visit to Ulyanovsk is a business trip, the hotel is ready to offer conference hall services. The hall with a capacity of 20 seats is located in the Volga Hotel (Ulyanovsk), address: ul. Goncharova, 3/38 . Hall rental can be provided to both the hotel guest and outside customers. The conference room is equipped with everything necessary for seminars, round tables, mini-conferences and other collective events. The hotel is also pleased to offer a meeting room designed for a company of no more than 8 people.





At the request of the customer, the hotel staff organizes coffee breaks or mini-banquets for participants in collective events with full service and an interesting buffet menu.





Other services

The hotel is pleased to offer guests delivery (transfer) from the airport or railway station and back in a comfortable car at any convenient time. The service is provided around the clock upon reservation.

There is also a tour desk at the Volga Hotel (Ulyanovsk), which will offer interesting tours of the city and its environs with visits to the main attractions.

Promotions and special offers

Hotel Volga (Ulyanovsk) provides its customers with a wide discount system for different categories of guests:

  • a 10% discount is given to newlywed couples, groups when placing more than 10 people, as well as when placing any number of people for a period of more than 10 days;
  • 20% discount is provided to applicants entering secondary and higher educational institutions of Ulyanovsk, as well as participants of the Second World War, liquidation of the consequences of the Chernobyl nuclear power plant, and disabled people of the 1st group.
