Hotel Prima Life Imperial Park (Tunisia, Hammamet)

Tunisia today is one of the popular Mediterranean tourist destinations for residents of both Eastern and Western Europe. This country is located in the north of the African continent and is washed by the waters of the most gentle of the seas - the Mediterranean. The coast of Tunisia is rich in picturesque bays, picturesque palm and orange groves, peerless sandy beaches and other beauties. Thanks to all this, many thousands of tourists go here every year for a lot of positive emotions. Indeed, in addition to water activities and beach relaxation, Tunisia also offers an informative vacation: excursions to local historical and natural attractions, including desert safaris. By the way, it is on the territory of this state that the ruins of ancient Carthage are located. Tunisia's most popular resorts are Sousse, Monastir, Mahdia and Hammamet. Here I would like to dwell on the latter, since it is here that Prima Life Imperial Park 4 * is located - a magnificent four-star hotel, which is part of the Prima Life chain.





Hammamet - the most comfortable of Tunisian resorts

This town, which is considered the most tourist in this African country, was once a favorite vacation spot of the British Prime Minister - Lord Winston Churchill. And this, of course, is the best advertisement for the resort. After all, this famous Englishman was distinguished by excellent taste, and the fact that he preferred to relax in Hammamet speaks only in favor of this town. In addition to him, the resort was favored by the French novelist Guy de Maupassant, as well as the magnificent Oscar Ould. The advantages of this place are wonderful natural parks, olive and jasmine gardens, the aroma of which extends over many kilometers. There are also professional thalassotherapy centers (sea treatment), modern and superbly equipped water parks and hotel complexes, built in close proximity to each other. A prime example is the Prima Life Imperial Park Aqua Life Yasmine Hammamet Aquapark 4 *.





Yasmine Hammamet

6 kilometers from the resort is the most comfortable tourist area, not only in the country, but throughout North Africa. This is Yasmin-Hammamet. Already from the name it becomes clear that the resort is immersed in jasmine gardens, and if you add to them orange and lemon plantations that grow along the entire coast of the country, even a person with a weak sense of smell can feel the fragrance of these plants several kilometers from the coast. It hovers over the entire resort, turning it into a truly paradise, which Tunisia is proud of. Reviews of tourists (Hammamet often becomes the subject of discussions on various portals) about the coast are almost always positive, even more so - enthusiastic. As for the hotels, we must admit that the service in them is inferior to Turkish and even Egyptian ... But the prices here are quite affordable, which makes holidays in Tunisia accessible to people whose income level is below average.





Hotel Area

The resort begins with the yacht port Marina and stretches along the entire coast of the Hammamet Bay to the Medina - the central part of the eponymous resort city. The zone has excellent infrastructure, everything is subordinated to the needs of tourists who rest here throughout the beach season, that is, from April to the end of November, although July and August are still the most popular months to relax. Wide sandy beaches and turquoise clear water in the sea, warmed up by the southern sun to 25-28 degrees, are especially attractive for tourists. Hotels in this tourist area are mainly located not on the water itself, but on the other side of the boardwalk, and their guests have to make short and very pleasant walks to the private beaches belonging to the hotels. So, Prima Life Imperial Park 4 * is located just 150 meters from the sea shore.





How to get to Hammamet?

There are direct regular flights from Moscow to Monastir and the capital of Tunisia all year round, but planes fly to the airport of Enfid only during the beach season, or rather, in the summer months. If you purchased a ticket to the hotel Prima Life Imperial Park (Tunisia / Hammamet), then it is most convenient, of course, to fly to Enfid, since it is located 48 kilometers from this resort, while you need to travel 65 kilometers to the Tunisian airport, and to Monastirsky - 85. Whatever city you fly to, you need to get to Hammamet-Yasmin by road: on minibuses, long-distance buses or taxis. If tourists have paid in advance for the transfer to the hotel, then they will be met at the airport by a representative of the tour operator or the hotel complex. Depending on the number of new arrivals, a trip to the hotel will take place either by minibus or by bus.
