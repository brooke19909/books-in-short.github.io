Hotel "Iren" (Pitsunda, Abkhazia): description, service, reviews

In Abkhazia there are many resorts that have long been loved by the residents of Russia and neighboring countries. Pitsunda is one of them. Many tourists come here because the mild climate reigns here and in summer there are the maximum number of sunny days. The coast of Pitsunda is washed by the clear Black Sea, the temperature of which is the highest during the holiday season.

Hotels in Abkhazia are very popular in hot weather. One of them - "Irene" - tourists especially praise in their reviews and comments left on social networks and on special sites.



Location and General Information

The hotel is located near the city center and near the coastline - from its territory to the sea is only 200 meters.

Hotel "Iren" was built in 2011, and from that time on, hundreds of tourists who are accustomed to good living conditions and high level of service annually relax on its territory.

Near the hotel there are several famous attractions that guests are trying to visit. Among them - the temple of the Pitsunda Mother of God, as well as Lake Inkit, to which fishing enthusiasts go for the next catch - it is rich in fish resources like no other reservoir.

Rooms

The rooms of the Iren Hotel in Pitsunda (Abkhazia) are located in two modern six-story buildings, which are conveniently located among fruit trees growing in the recreation area.

The attention of the guests are presented apartments of two different categories: "standard" and "luxury". They differ from each other in the degree of comfort and the availability of certain amenities.

"Standard"

Standard rooms are designed to accommodate two people. They are decorated in a contemporary style and furnished with comfortable furniture. Guests can stay in double beds and put things in a separate closet, specially designed for this purpose. They also have desks and chairs. Each room of this class has a plasma TV with the ability to view satellite channels, as well as a refrigerator. Guests can use the private bath and toilet, which are provided in each "standard". All apartments have a balcony with beautiful natural landscapes.

Room price per day - about 4000 rubles (depending on the month of the visit). This price usually includes food.



"Suite"

Hotel "Iren" can also offer guests rooms of a high level of comfort - "luxury". Such apartments consist of two rooms, but just like Standard, are designed for two people. One of the rooms is a bedroom. It has a large double bed, bedside tables, a separate place where you can put personal items, as well as a TV. In the living area there is a furniture set, consisting of soft armchairs and one sofa, a coffee table, as well as a desk and chair. There is also a kettle, microwave and refrigerator - this is enough to prepare a small number of simple dishes, as noted by the hotel guests.



These apartments have a modern air conditioning system, which allows you to maintain optimal air temperature in any weather outside the window. There is an individual shower room with a bathroom and a sink.

The cost of living in this room will be approximately 8,000 rubles per day. This price also includes comprehensive meals.

Conditions for children

On the territory of the Iren Hotel in Pitsunda you can relax with young children, as there are some conditions for them. First of all, the hotel‚Äôs rules indicate that if children come with guests, they provide an extra bed for them, and free of charge. In this place for recreation there are no age restrictions for babies, so you can come even with babies.

The hotel has a well-equipped children's playground where kids can actively relax and make new friends among their peers.

Nutrition

As in many hotels in Abkhazia, Iren already includes three meals a day in the room price, which is provided during breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Abkhaz cuisine, which consists mainly of vegetables and fish, is served in the local dining room. As the guests themselves note, in the food that is served here, the taste and aroma of herbs are clearly audible. Guests are treated to local home-made wine.

The guests are also presented with a summer cafe, in which vacationers can eat, but only on a fee basis.



Beach

At a distance of 200 meters from the Iren Hotel there is a sandy beach, which is public, but guests of this particular recreation area are its frequent guests. It is well equipped for a pleasant pastime; here you can rent sun loungers, umbrellas and air mattresses. On the territory there are several showers, changing rooms and toilets.

In order to get to the beach, hotel guests must walk along a small stretch of road that runs between tall old pines.



Additional services

On the territory of the Iren Hotel in Pitsunda there is an opportunity to rent a bicycle, where you can go on a trip to the countryside or to the sights of the resort. For all guests who come to relax in their own car, there is a guarded parking lot, one place on which costs 50 rubles per day.

On the territory of "Iren" (Pitsunda) there is a small tour desk, which is very popular among guests. Groups of tourists gather here who wish to visit specific places and go on a trip around the area.

Throughout the hotel there is a free access point to the Internet (Wi-Fi). This allows guests to stay in touch with family and friends throughout their stay in Iren (Pitsunda).

Laundry facilities are available on site for guests for a fee.



Reviews

In their reviews of "Irene" (Pitsunda), guests often share their impressions gained during the holiday. Many of them write that a pleasant and relaxed atmosphere reigns in the complex. They also note good service at local catering establishments. The guests say that they prepare very tasty dishes that have rich taste.

As noted in the reviews of vacationers, the beach, which is located near the hotel, is very clean and comfortable. A beautiful view of the sea and the surrounding nature opens from its shore.



Hotel address

Hotel "Iren" is located in Abkhazia, in the Gagra district, at the address: Pitsunda, Agrba street, 2.

Before the visit, pre-book a hotel room. This can be done by telephone at the number indicated on its official website, or by filling out a special online form, which is posted there.
