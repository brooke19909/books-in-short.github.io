Where in Abkhazia it is better to relax with children. Tips & Tricks

If you are thinking about where in Abkhazia it is better to relax with children, first make sure if it makes sense to go to this amazing country with young offspring. Abkhazia is a very hospitable country, at least today, and it is worth worrying about the safety of children here no more than at any other resort. However, there are nuances that you need to pay attention to when choosing where in Abkhazia it is better to relax with children.



Firstly, if you are counting on a short week's rest, then it is better not to take young heirs to this country with you at all. The thing is that this Black Sea territory is characterized by a very long acclimatization. As a rule, during the first ten (!) Days of rest, children will not be able to fully enjoy the features of the healing mountain-sea climate. All this time, their body adapts to the unusual environment. Therefore, if you are taking a child to Abkhazia for the purpose of his recovery, count on at least a monthly vacation.

Secondly, Abkhazia is a country where the weather even in the hottest summer months (July-August) can change very sharply. For example, in the morning there was heat, and not a cloud in the sky, and by noon - no more than +15 Celsius, a sharp wind and rain. In addition, nights in the mountains are even cold in summer. Therefore, choosing where it is better to relax with children in Abkhazia, in any case, you need to take care of clothes and shoes in case of different weather.



Thirdly, if you are planning a vacation in Abkhazia with a child, you should pay special attention to his nutrition. Most likely, he will not be able to eat with you in local cafes and restaurants. The fact is that the dishes of the Abkhaz cuisine are very tasty, but because of the increased sharpness and fat content, they are in no way suitable for the diet of our children. Therefore, deciding where in Abkhazia it is better to relax with children, choose either boarding houses designed for Russian tourists, where you can eat our usual food, or rely on your own culinary abilities, renting housing in the private sector.

If the above recommendations do not scare you, you can safely go on a journey at the end of which you will find a unique country - Abkhazia. Where to relax with your child, you can decide both at home and upon arrival. The last option for those who prefer a holiday in the private sector. In this case

You can cook delicious food yourself using fresh products that you can buy inexpensively from hospitable hosts. And do not think that living in a private house somewhere in the mountains will be boring for children. On the contrary, the life of the local population is unique, your offspring will have an unforgettable experience of communicating with wildlife.

For those who prefer a more civilized vacation, it makes sense to stay in one of the many guest houses scattered along the Black Sea coast. Here you will find clean pebble beaches and the gentle Black Sea. When deciding where it is better to relax in Abkhazia, do not forget that this country can give not only a high-quality beach vacation, but also an exciting excursion program - there are a lot of historical sights. In addition, in Abkhazia today there are 170 healing springs, the waters of which are not inferior in their properties to world-famous, such as Borjomi and Narzan. So in this country you can not only enjoy sunbathing and swimming in the sea, but also get acquainted with an interesting culture, as well as improve your health.
