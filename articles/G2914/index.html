Villaggio Agrumeto 3 * (Calabria, Italy): hotel description and reviews of tourists

For many years, summer tours to Italy have been very popular among tourists from Russia and other countries of the world. Moreover, every year more and more new places are added to the list of visited resorts. So, more recently, Capo Vaticano (Calabria) was added to it, and with it new hotels. Among them, one of the most popular is Villaggio Agrumeto 3 *, Calabria region.

Calabria: geography and climate

Calabria is a region located in the southern part of the Apennine Peninsula. Its western shores are washed by the Tyrrhenian Sea, and the eastern - by the Ionian. Calabria consists of 5 provinces - these are Reggio Calabria, Cosenza, Catanzaro, Crotone and Vibo Valentia. It is on the territory of the latter that Cape Capo Vaticano is located, characterized by beautiful beaches and crystal clear water. 2-3 km from the cape is the village of Ricadi, near which is the Villaggio Hotel Agrumeto 3 *. Most tourists stay here only to be able to enjoy plenty of beach vacations and magnificent views of the cliffs, steppes, bays and cliffs.





Pleasant holidaymakers in Capo Vaticano and the mild climate prevailing here. These places are characterized by moderately cold winters and hot summers. How to get there

At a distance of 1 km from the nearest resort center is the hotel Villaggio Agrumeto 3 *. Lamezia Terme - the international airport where tourists from all over the world usually arrive, is located 68 km from the hotel. There are several ways to get from the airport to the hotel - take a taxi directly on arrival, arrange a transfer in advance, or go to Capo Vaticano by rail.





It is worth noting that the latter is the most budgetary option, however, first you will need to get from the airport to the train station using the local bus. And then you just need to take a ticket and, after waiting for the train, following the route Lamezia - Terme - Reggio Calabria, get to the station of Ricadi. From the station to the Hotel Villaggio Agrumeto 3 * walk no more than 15 minutes. By the way, thanks to the proximity of the station, you can diversify your stay in Capo Vaticano with trips to other cities in the region, many of which are famous for their rich historical past.

Entertainment and sports

Villaggio Hotel Agrumeto (Calabria) provides all the conditions for a pleasant and eventful pastime. The hotel has two outdoor pools (one for adults, the second for the youngest vacationers), two volleyball courts - on the beach and next to the hotel, aerobics classes, a tennis court, however, equipment for the game and lessons are paid. There is also a TV room where every guest at Villaggio Agrumeto can relax and watch an interesting movie. The hotel also offers bike or car rental. In the evenings, tourists are entertained by professional animators, however, all events are conducted exclusively in Italian, which often upsets tourists from Russia. Although, on the other hand, this is a great way to practice learning a new language.





At a distance of 100 meters is a luxurious sandy beach, umbrellas and sun loungers are free of charge for hotel guests. But here, besides swimming in the purest sea waters and sunbathing in the sun, you will find many pleasant entertainments. This is water skiing, canoeing, and pedalo. There is also a diving center on the beach, where everyone will be taught diving and will spend their first acquaintance with the flora and fauna of the Tyrrhenian Sea.

Local Attractions

If you want to diversify your vacation and leave the Villaggio Agrumeto 3 * hotel for a while, then you will have plenty of opportunities for this. So, literally 10 km from Ricadi is the resort town of Tropea, which is located on a sandstone rock surrounded by the bays of Joya Tauro and Santa Eufemia. The city has many historical and architectural attractions that were erected in the XVI century.





Another city worthy of attention is Soverato. Its main attractions are the churches of St. Anthony of Padua and Madonna del Rosario. In addition, a trip to the island of Sicily will not take much time from here .
