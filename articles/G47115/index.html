Hotel Lindos Village 5 * (Greece / Rhodes): traveler reviews

Recently, Greece has been very popular among tourists. Many do not mind coming here on vacation. And absolutely all people in advance are looking for a hotel in which they will live during their stay in a foreign country. Many people opt for Lindos Village 5 *, which is right, because this hotel complex is perfect for relaxation for a large number of reasons.





About hotel

Lindos Village 5 * is located on the island of Rhodes. And to be more precise, in the small town of Lindos, which was founded in the 10th century BC! Although it is not known exactly when this town was built. There is even a version that Lindos is 27 centuries old. Now only a few hundred local citizens live here, and all other people are numerous visitors who want to sunbathe on the beaches, swim in the sea and get acquainted with the culture and attractions of Greece.

Now about the hotel itself. He is not young, the complex was built in 1970. But a complete update was carried out recently, in 2015. So the hotel is modern, with comfortable rooms decorated in a pleasant style. By the way, until September 1, 2012, this complex was called PGS Steps of Lindos Village 5 *.

Location

The hotel is located on a hill from which you can see stunning panoramic views of local beauty. This hotel is also adjacent to the complex called Lindos Royal. Both hotels stipulate that guests have the right to use certain services of each of them.

The hotel has a good location. 50 kilometers from the airport and 45 from the center of Rhodes, the capital of the island. And the bus stop, by the way, from which minibuses depart to any place, is only 600 meters from the hotel.

The sea can also be reached in a few minutes. But due to the fact that numerous steps and descents lead to the beach, the hotel is not recommended for people with disabilities. For the rest, everyone here can comfortably relax.





Leisure

In Lindos Village 5 * there are a lot of all kinds of entertainment. Fans of an active, sporting lifestyle will definitely enjoy the presence of a football field and tennis court, as well as a swimming pool. More guests can play beach volleyball or engage in water sports. For example, water skiing or "banana". Diving classes, by the way, are also available. You can also take time to paragliding. In general, you will not be bored here. In the evening, by the way, evening and afternoon entertainment shows are held.

And still, being in Lindos Village 5 * (Rhodes), you should definitely visit the local SPA center. There you can enjoy a Turkish bath, sauna, massage, jacuzzi, as well as numerous health and beauty treatments. The complex also has a fitness center with all the necessary equipment for a good workout.

By the way, the beach, despite the fact that it is located below, belongs to the hotel. Entrance to its territory, awnings and sunbeds are free.
