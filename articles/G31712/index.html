Recreation center "Osinovets lighthouse"

In the Vsevolozhsk district, right on the shore of Lake Ladoga, there is a cozy and interesting place for a comfortable stay - the Osinovets lighthouse base. The area is well-groomed, there are convenient paths and a drive for cars, green areas covered with a lawn, flowerbeds are laid out and interesting bushes grow. Around extends amazing nature - forest and pond.

List of services provided and their cost

Base "Osinovets lighthouse" has everything you need for a comfortable stay. Here guests will find:

  • comfortable rooms;
  • wood bath;
  • beach;
  • a sports ground;
  • gazebo with barbecue;
  • cafe, banquet hall and summer tent;
  • feast for yachts and boats;
  • guarded parking;
  • wireless Internet.




The price includes accommodation, breakfast, use of wireless Internet and parking. Room prices start at 3,500 thousand rubles. You can order a bath for 1,500 rubles per hour. Renting a full bath complex will cost 25,000 per day. Brooms, sheets, towels and other accessories are provided for a fee.

All prices are as of the summer of 2017 and are subject to change.

Number of rooms

Stay at the recreation center offer a two-story hotel. It is small but very comfortable. There are only four rooms on the floors. Each has a double bed and one extra bed in the form of a folding sofa. Also in the apartment there is a chest of drawers, bedside tables, TV. Bathroom with combined toilet and shower.



There is also a large cottage at the Osinovetsky Lighthouse recreation center, which has three VIP rooms on the ground floor and one on the second. The apartment has a double bed and other necessary furniture, fireplace, TV. The bathroom is combined, it is equipped with a shower. The room on the second floor has access to a balcony.

All apartments offer wonderful views of Lake Ladoga and the surrounding nature.

Catering

There is a spacious cafe on the territory of the Osinovetsky lighthouse recreation center for up to 80 guests. It serves dishes of Russian and European cuisines, as well as delicious smoked fish from Ladoga and various options of barbecue. Additionally, there are two venues for organizing various celebrations (weddings, birthdays, anniversaries, and so on):

  • a huge summer tent for 150 guests;
  • Banquet hall in the bath complex accommodates up to 30 people.




Holiday Options

The recreation center "Osinovets lighthouse" was created primarily for a comfortable stay on the shore of the reservoir with the ability to steam. A wood sauna is designed for six to eight people. It has a separate exit to the lake, and you can rent it for two hours or a whole day.

There is a cafe in the base serving delicious dishes. But for those who want to cook their own barbecue, there is a gazebo with barbecue facilities. It seats up to fifteen people.

Fans of active entertainment will love the equipped football field and a place for playing volleyball. Sometimes the lake pleases with big waves. This means that you can go windsurfing. If you do not have your own equipment, then you have the opportunity to rent it right on the spot. Do not want to conquer the waves? Just swim in clear water. The slope of the coast is gentle, and the bottom of the reservoir is sand and gravel. Depth does not begin immediately, but gradually.

If you prefer calm relaxation options, then you can just soak up the sun on a well-kept sandy beach. And if you want to go fishing, then on the base they offer traditional Russian fishing with kebabs on fishing vessels. Around the "Osinovets lighthouse" there is a pine forest, in which it is so pleasant to walk along the paths.

It is good to rest here not only in summer but also in winter. It is no longer possible to swim and play sports games in the open air, but other forms of entertainment remain available: a bathhouse, barbecue, winter fishing, walks in the vicinity.

Nearby is the main attraction of the area, after which the base is named. A walk to the lighthouse will be another form of entertainment. Access is closed because the attraction is still active. But nearby are picturesque ruined.

How to get to the Osinovets lighthouse?

The recreation center is located at: Leningrad region, Vsevolozhsk district, Osinovets village, shore of Lake Ladoga, highway Life Road, 308. It is approximately 35-40 km from St. Petersburg. There is a base near the lighthouse, so finding it is not difficult.



You can get here by car. The roads here are good, without potholes. It is necessary to leave St. Petersburg on the Road of Life, which leads towards Vsevolozhsk. Directly along it, you will reach the Osinovetsky Lighthouse base. Also, trains from Finland Station go here. You should leave at the station "Ladoga Lake". Then you need to go back about 1,500 meters.

Another unusual option is to sail on a yacht or boat. Watercraft can be left on a specially equipped berth.
