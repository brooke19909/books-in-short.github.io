Sanatorium "Blue Issyk-Kul". Kyrgyzstan, the sanatorium "Blue Issyk-Kul" - photos, prices

Issyk-Kul Lake is located between two ridges of the Northern Tien Shan. This place is located in the north-east of the country. Issyk-Kul is translated as "hot lake". The fact is that it does not freeze in winter. This is due to the high salt content in the water.



General information

Issyk-Kul Lake is located at an altitude of about 1600 meters above sea level. It takes the 2nd place in transparency after Lake Baikal. Issyk-Kul is also included in the list of seven deepest lakes of the whole world.

Interesting Facts

A huge number of traditions is compiled about the lake. For example, legends about flooded cities, treasures, relics of the Apostle Matthew and Tamerlan. Some of them are really based on reliable facts. This is evidenced by the discovery of the remains of the ancient city at the bottom of the lake.

Climatic features

Not only ancient legends and geographical aspects annually attract crowds of tourists from all over the CIS. Many seek here for the sake of relaxation in a temperate mountain-sea climate. Few can resist the prospect of a long-awaited vacation among the soft breezes, the smell of needles and unusually clean air. Staying here already brings pleasure.



Main advantages

Many people know about Issyk-Kul Lake . It is famous for the healing properties of air and water. Each year, the popularity of the Kyrgyz reservoir is only increasing. Currently, holidays on a high mountain lake at the peak of popularity. Everyone who has ever visited these places is struck by the beauty of Issyk-Kul. The misty outlines of the mountain ranges, the refreshing light breeze, cool water and golden sand beaches leave no one indifferent. Why just admire the horizon line that merges with the blue lake.

Healing factors

You can relax here with the whole family. This place is ideal for improving health. The water of the lake has amazing properties. It is used to treat many ailments. In the lake is healing mud mud. It is used as a remedy. For many kilometers there are beaches. Basically they are practically free. Therefore, each guest will be able to find a great place to relax and swim.

Features of accommodation

There are many establishments on the coast where vacationers stop. Guests can choose the perfect option for every taste and budget. Those who do not appreciate pathos rest can stay in inexpensive boarding houses. Fans of active sports are sure to find something to their liking. Guests can use steamboats, scooters, trampolines and even parachute jumps. Fans of spending time on the beach will also be satisfied. They will be able to lie on soft warm sand and take time to contemplate the water surface. The tourism sector on the lake is developing rapidly. The quality of service is increasing continuously. All conditions have been created for guests so that they can relax and have fun. A full range of related services is provided. Issyk-Kul has many places where guests can stay. There are both traditional pensions and motels, as well as exclusive cottages. Vacationers can also stay in guest houses in the private sector. Pensions are located mainly in the northern part of the coast. They have a fairly developed infrastructure. This is their main difference from institutions that are located on the south coast. On the territory of the lake there are many boarding houses. According to some reports, there are already more than one and a half hundred.
