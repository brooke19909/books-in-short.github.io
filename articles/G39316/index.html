Popular hotels in Elabuga

The small city of Elabuga, located in Tatarstan, will be interesting to architecture and history lovers, as well as business travelers. There are modern, well-equipped hotels with the highest level of service and spacious apartments. In order not to worry about accommodation, it is best to book Yelabuga hotels in advance. Gor.ka is located in the north of the city. It has a sauna, and free internet is available. All rooms at this Yelabuga hotel have a refrigerator and a TV with many channels. The bathroom comes with a hairdryer and shower.



There is a 24-hour front desk, grocery delivery services. A variety of cafes and restaurants are located nearby. This Yelabuga hotel offers free parking. The train station is 24 kilometers away.
