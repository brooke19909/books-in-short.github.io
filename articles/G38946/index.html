How to get there

It was said above that you can get from Korolev from Moscow by train. However, an electric train is not the only way. Buses run from the capital to the science city. Departure point - metro "VDNH", buses stand next to the hotel "Cosmos". Remember or write down numbers: 392, 499, 565. Approximate travel time - 45 minutes, excluding traffic jams. The ticket price is a little over one hundred rubles.

For those who do not want to go by train or by bus, the route Moscow - Podlipki Dachnie can be taken by taxi. The issue price is from four to six thousand rubles. Travel time is about an hour.

Have you decided to go from Moscow to Podlipok Dachnykh by train? The cheapest and fastest option. Travel time is about 35-40 minutes. One-way fares are sixty-one rubles.

If you wanted to visit Bolshevo or Lesnaya Polyany, then buses and minibuses run from the Podlipki Dachny station. You can get to Bolshevo by buses No. 1, 2, 12, 13, 15. Bus number 31 runs from Podlipok to Lesnie Polyany.
