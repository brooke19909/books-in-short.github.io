Hotel "Constantinople", Vityazevo: description of rooms, service, reviews

Holidays by the sea is always a joy for both adults and children. Hotel "Constantinople" in Vityazevo offers comfortable accommodation and high quality service for its guests. There are all conditions for a rich and fun holiday.

Hotel "Constantinople" in Vityazevo: address and how it works

The complex was built in 2009. The hotel is located in the village of Vityazevo. This village is located 10 km from Anapa (Vityazevo village, Roz street, 5). An excellent infrastructure has been created here, which is not inferior to the city.

Getting here is not difficult. The guest house is approximately 8 km from the airport and Anapa Train Station. The administration of the complex provides a free shuttle service from these transportation junctions by prior arrangement.





The hotel welcomes guests year-round. Room reservation can be made by phone or through the work of online services. The guest house consists of several buildings that are not connected by transitions.

Complex features and service

The territory of the guest house is landscaped and landscaped as much as possible. Cleanliness is always maintained here, garbage is taken out several times a day. According to reviews, the hotel "Constantinople" in Vityazevo enjoy spending evenings on the open terrace.

There are tables and comfortable chairs. In the daytime, large umbrellas protect the holidaymakers from the scorching rays of the sun. Roommates often have fun on the terrace in the evenings in the evenings, and in the future such an acquaintance will develop into a strong friendship.

Around the pool are comfortable sun loungers to relax after an active swim. Here guests love to take sunbaths. In the evening, the entire territory of the complex, including the pool, is illuminated with special lighting.





At the reception, managers work round-the-clock, who are ready at any minute to help resolve the issue with vacationers. This lounge has soft sofas and armchairs. On the coffee tables a fresh press appears every morning.

Rooms are cleaned every other day. Bed linen and towels are changed every 3 days. The rooms have round-the-clock hot and cold water. In buildings on each floor there is an ironing board with an iron.

On the territory there is a sauna and a guarded parking lot for vacationers who arrived by personal car. To facilitate the life of guests, there is a small laundry room at the Konstantinopol Hotel (Vityazevo). The service and maintenance in it is excellent, you can quickly wash clothes for an additional fee.

Hotel "Constantinople" in Vityazevo: room description

All rooms in the hotel have a good level of comfort. Visitors will be able to rent one of 46 rooms in each building:

  • double room with one large bed in block B - decorated in a classic style, has a balcony, large dressing table, sofa, plasma TV, bathroom and shower, air conditioning;
  • triple room in block B - a large bed and a sofa bed, TV, split system, bathroom and shower, wardrobe;
  • family room - the most spacious room, 2 rooms, has one double bed and a folding sofa, there is a flat TV, a balcony, a bathroom, a bathroom, an air conditioner, a safe, a hairdryer, a large wardrobe;
  • room for 4 people in block - 27 m 2 , 2 rooms, two double beds, a bathroom, refrigerator, air conditioning, TV, wardrobe, internet;
  • 3-bed standard in building A - has one double bed and a folding armchair or three single beds, TV, a bathroom and shower, air conditioning, telephone, wardrobe, some rooms have a balcony;
  • 4-bed standard in block A - 4 single beds or a double bed and a folding sofa, TV, air conditioning, bathroom and shower, wardrobe;
  • 2-bed standard in block A - double bed or two single beds, TV, air conditioning, bathroom and shower, balcony;
  • Suite in building A - two rooms, a double bed and folding sofas, a bathroom and a toilet, a Jacuzzi, a split system, a wardrobe, TV, the Internet, a hairdryer, a refrigerator, a safe, a telephone.




All vacationers have the opportunity to use free Wi-Fi throughout the complex.

Accommodation Features

Hotel "Constantinople" in Vityazevo has several rules for guests, which must be observed when checking in and during rest:

  • children under 3 years old are accommodated free of charge without a separate seat; a cot with sides can be installed without payment;
  • tenants with children of any age are accepted;
  • in some rooms it is possible to provide an extra bed on a cot for an extra charge;
  • visitors with animals do not settle in the hotel;
  • meals are not included in the price, you can eat in any other institution.

These points make it possible to increase comfort and make the stay more pleasant for guests of the boarding house.

Recreation areas

The beach with all amenities is 500 meters from the hotel. Guests spend no more than 10 minutes to reach him from the Constantinople Hotel in Vityazevo. The beach belongs to the city and is quite spacious.

The guest house has its own outdoor pool. It is divided into several zones depending on the depth. Fresh water is poured into it, which is purified in a multi-level filtration system. The adult unit has hydromassage.





Hotel staff also perform manual cleaning with special vacuum cleaners daily in the morning. On cool days, the pool includes heating for a comfortable swimming holidaymakers.

A special coating is laid around, which does not slip when water gets on it. Therefore, guests cannot be injured in this recreation area.

Children's area

For younger visitors, the hotel has separate areas for entertainment. There is a playground on site. The pool has a special area with minimal depth.





Throughout the warm period of the year, animators work at the hotel, who conduct entertaining events and games with children. The building has an equipped room with toys. Here, children can spend their time in the cold season and evening.

In the dining room, children are provided with special high chairs for children up to three years old. At the request of parents, a stroller can be rented for 150 rubles per day.

Nutrition

On the territory of the complex in building A there is a dining room. Here you can order full 3 meals a day for the entire period of rest. It is also possible option to pay only lunch or dinner, or the acquisition of dishes upon receipt of food.

The menu includes nutritious dishes with mandatory preparation for each serving of meat and fish products. For small visitors, cereals are offered for breakfast.

For cooking, only fresh products with quality certificates are used at the Konstantinopol Hotel (Vityazevo). The food here is varied and contains daily large portions of seasonal fruits and vegetables.

Entertainment in the guesthouse and beyond

During the holiday season, animators work at the hotel. They conduct various show programs for both adults and children. Various activities take place daily in the evenings. Even the most serious vacationers take part in them with pleasure. After all, people come to rest in order to relax and forget about all domestic problems.





Near the hotel there are various places for recreation and entertainment:

  • two amusement parks;
  • bistro and cafe;
  • restaurants
  • water attractions;
  • night discos;
  • karaoke clubs.

The village of Vityazevo has always been famous for its well-groomed and green territory and a large number of places for leisure travelers.

Tourists reviews

An impressive number of reviews on the Constantinople Hotel in Vityazevo and its work have been collected on various Internet sites. Most of them are positive.

The guests of the boarding house note the excellent work of the maids and girls at the reception. The canteen staff has minor complaints about hostility. Also, vacationers are unhappy with the lack of prices for dishes. Because of this, each time it is necessary to tear off the workers of the dining room from the distribution of dishes.

Tourists are happy with the cleanliness of the pool and the children's relaxation area. In some reviews, you can see complaints about broken toys in the room for children in the buildings.

Tourists note that in the immediate vicinity there is a small shop selling fresh fruits and vegetables. Also on the positive side indicate the presence of an ATM near the hotel.

Outdoor enthusiasts have the opportunity to rent bicycles at the hotel. Tourists note that they are in good technical condition and walks on them are excellent.





Parents of young children are happy with the opportunity to rent a stroller. They also find it very convenient to provide high chairs in the dining room. The hotel has a small cafe where, according to customer reviews, cook delicious kebabs and serve a variety of quality wine.

The complex has gazebos where you can relax with a group of friends or family for a delicious dinner. Guests note that in the evenings it is usually quiet, the noise from entertainment venues does not interfere with sleep.

There are negative reviews about the presence of dust on tables on balconies and terraces. This can be attributed to the fact that they are in the open air and staff cannot clean every hour or two.
