How to make a money tree from beads with your own hands?

A do-it-yourself money tree from beads can be made quite quickly and easily. To do this, you just need to stock up on some patience, the necessary materials and show creative imagination.

Many refuse to make a money tree out of beads with their own hands just because they do not know how to weave various figures from small beads with holes. However, in this creative endeavor it is absolutely not necessary to master such a technique perfectly. This is due to the fact that beads are needed only for simple and easy creation of leaflets, and the trunk and branches themselves will be made of wire and gypsum.

How to make a money tree from beads with your own hands?

Necessary tools and materials for work:

  • a small decorative pot - one piece;
  • thick copper wire - two to three meters;
  • gypsum - two hundred grams;
  • PVA glue - one hundred milliliters;
  • thin copper wire - three to four meters;
  • green beads or Chinese coins - several packages (for leaflets);
  • wire cutters - for wire separation;
  • golden or brown spray paint - one spray can;
  • dishes for diluting the sticky mass.


Step-by-step instructions for creating a trunk

Do-it-yourself money tree made of beads (more precisely, its frame) should be made of thick copper wire. It is worth noting that the size and direction of growth of such a plant you should choose at your own discretion. However, the more branches there are on the tree, the thicker and more beautiful your self-created souvenir will turn out. Thus, having built the base of the wire, it should immediately be installed in a small decorative pot and fixed using special glue.

In order to create this mass, in a separate bowl it is necessary to mix PVA glue and building gypsum in equal proportions . It is worth noting that such a mixture hardens instantly, so it is recommended to use it extremely quickly for its intended purpose.

After the frame is installed in the pot, you should proceed to its design. For this, the newly diluted adhesive mass must be glued to the wire so that in the end it is similar to a tree trunk.



Money tree made of beads. With our own hands we create beautiful and bright leaves

While the gypsum barrel hardens, you can start creating the crown. To do this, you need to use a thin copper wire, on which you want to string green beads and twist it into a leaf shape. The more there are such blanks, the thicker the tree will turn out.

How to make a money tree with your own hands from coins purchased in a store?

Such a souvenir is obtained beautiful and unusual not only from beads, but also from Chinese coins, which can be purchased at stores selling Feng Shui symbols. They are twisted on a thin copper wire in the same way as beads.



The final step in creating a money tree

Prepared leaflets of beads or coins should be fixed on the trunk, and then tightly covered with foil and painted the tree in golden or brownish color using a spray paint. After that, it is recommended to leave the souvenir for a couple of days for complete drying.
