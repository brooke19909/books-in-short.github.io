Hotels in Yuryev-Polsky: description and reviews

Many tourists come to the ancient Russian city of Yuryev-Polsky to admire the Mikhailo-Arkhangelsk monastery, St. George's Cathedral, take a walk along the quiet streets. I would like to finish the day full of impressions in a comfortable hotel. Yuryev-Polsky hotels offer their guests the best conditions in modern comfortable rooms.

Hotel "Yurievskaya"

The hotel is located in the historic center of the ancient city. It is only a few steps from here to the historical and architectural museum. From the windows you can admire the Mikhailo-Arkhangelsk monastery, the shopping arcade of a provincial town. At the Yuryevskaya Hotel Yuryev-Polsky you can order a bus ticket, use the services of a guide. Car owners are given free parking in an unguarded parking lot, and there is free access to wireless Internet. The hotel has 14 rooms. To accommodate guests, the following options are offered:

  • standard room with one double or two single beds - 2 800 rubles per day;
  • Junior suite with one double or two single beds - 3,200 rubles per day.

At this hotel in Yuryev-Polsky, room rates are inclusive of a continental breakfast. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, TV, telephone, electric kettle. Superior rooms have a refrigerator. The hotel is located at 1 Sovetskaya Square.

Votchina Country Club Country House

Votchina Hotel is located in a large private house within walking distance of Lake Kuchka. At a distance of two kilometers from the country house is Lake Forest. Guests have access to a well-equipped kitchen with a fridge, washing machine and microwave. Ironing facilities are provided. There is a picnic area in the fresh air. Guests can also relax on the terrace. The shared living area has a dining area. The house has a large flat-screen TV, sofa. It offers free parking and WI-FI. Bike rental is available. The area is popular for fishing. The whole house is for rent, designed to accommodate five guests. The rooms have wooden floors, heating, air conditioning. Pets are allowed here. The cost of living is 4,000 rubles per day. The hotel is located in Yuryev-Polsky at the address: p. Kalman, house 11.
