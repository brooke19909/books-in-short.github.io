Hotel An Vista Hotel 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): photos and reviews of tourists

Going on vacation to Vietnam, pay attention to such an option as An Vista Hotel 3 * . The Asian flavor and European standards of service are organically combined here .





a brief description of

If you settle in the high-rise An Vista Hotel 3 * hotel , Vietnam will be in front of you at a glance. This is a relatively new resort facility, which at the beginning of 2016 underwent a major overhaul. It has everything you need for a good rest: comfortable rooms, a cozy restaurant, a bar with a large assortment of drinks and much more. It is worth noting the skilled work of the staff, which will help to solve not only issues related to accommodation, but also will advise on sightseeing tours and city infrastructure.





Location

An Vista Hotel 3 * is located in the coastal area. Vietnam, Nha Trang, 96/12 Chang Fu Street - hotel address. This is one of the most popular places in the city, where a huge number of shopping and entertainment venues, as well as catering points are concentrated. 300 meters from the hotel there is a large night market, where at affordable prices you can buy a lot of local goods. At 100 m there is a public beach. To get to the international airport, you need to overcome 35 km, and to the train station - only 3 km.

Vietnam is one of the most interesting destinations for travelers. The following tourist sites are closest to the An Vista Hotel 3 * hotel:

  • water park (1 km);
  • diving center (1 km);
  • yacht club (1 km);
  • main city square (2 km);
  • Tram Huong Tower (2 km);
  • Dam Market (3 km);

Accommodation

About 99 comfortable rooms are provided for guests of An Vista Hotel 3 * . Nha Trang will welcome you with apartments of the following categories:

  • The standard can be equipped with a double or a pair of twin beds. The room is decorated in bright colors, which visually makes it more spacious. You can enjoy mountain views from the balcony.
  • Superior room differs from the previous category in a more spacious area. In addition, there is a seating area with soft chairs, a coffee table and a floor lamp. City views open out of the windows.
  • Deluxe consists of two functional areas. The bedroom is equipped with large or single beds. Behind the decorative partition there is a folding sofa (can be used to accommodate an additional guest), as well as a coffee table. It offers mountain views.
  • Suite is a spacious two-room apartment, consisting of a full bedroom and living room, separated by a sliding door. The windows and the balcony offer a magnificent view of the ocean. And even in the bathroom, near the washbasin, there is a window through which you can admire beautiful landscapes.
  • This hotel has special rooms for honeymooners. The centerpiece is a huge four-poster bed. The bathroom is equipped with a jacuzzi. Guests will also be greeted with a romantic decoration using candles and flower petals.

In a single interior solution all the rooms of An Vista Hotel 3 * are made . The photo shows a fashionable white color, which harmoniously combines with bright accents of chocolate and gold colors.

Helpful information

It will be useful for travelers to familiarize themselves with some rules of accommodation at the An Vista Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang) hotel. Pay attention to the following points:

  • check-in of newly arrived guests, as well as their registration is from 14:00 to midnight (for an additional fee, quick check-in is possible);
  • You need to leave your room on the day of departure from 6:00 to noon (hourly billing is provided for late eviction);
  • when booking a room, the guest is required to make an advance payment by credit card;
  • a prerequisite for registration at this hotel is the availability of an identity card with a current photo;
  • one child under 5 years old can stay in the room for free (without extra bed);
  • an extra bed for children will cost you $ 10, and for adults - $ 15 per night;
  • only one extra guest is allowed in each room;
  • self-registration is possible only from the age of 18;
  • quadrocopters cannot be used without special permission from the hotel;
  • due to recent events at the hotel, the use of Galaxy Note 7 devices is prohibited;
  • plastic cards are accepted;
  • newlyweds receive a compliment in the form of fruits and a bottle of champagne;
  • pets are not allowed (this rule also applies to service dogs and guide dogs).

Hotel An Vista Hotel 3 *: negative reviews

Sometimes unpleasant little things can seriously spoil the overall impression of relaxation. That is why travelers should carefully read the comments about the hotel An Vista Hotel 3 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang). Reviews allow us to talk about the following disadvantages of this hotel:

  • maids do not do wet cleaning (just wipe the dust and take out the garbage); the rooms are quite cramped;
  • beach towels of an unpleasant gray color, stained (it is better to bring them with you or buy on the spot);
  • to visit the beach you need vouchers, but they do not give them out upon arrival (you need to constantly remind the administration about this);
  • prices for excursions in the hotel bureau are 2 times higher than the cost of similar tours in local agencies;
  • fruit is rarely found at breakfast (and if it is, it is mostly green);
  • bed linen is not changed throughout the rest, if you do not require this from the administration;
  • in the corridors and rooms you can find lizards and insects;
  • for two guests they are given 1 disposable tube with shampoo (but toothbrushes are updated daily);
  • periodically there are problems with the reservation, because of which you run the risk of spending the whole day or night in the lobby or go in search of another hotel;
  • You may be asked to pay for the contents of the minibar, which you did not even touch (therefore, it is recommended that you be in the room during the check by the maids and administrators);
  • near the hotel, an aerodrome is under construction, the building of which will block a beautiful view of the mountains from some of the rooms;
  • the hotel is located on a narrow street that tourist buses cannot call in, so you have to walk some distance with your suitcases;
  • if you do not get an excursion from the hotel Russian-speaking guide, then you can not count on help on any issues;
  • uncomfortable stairs in the hotel (steps are very high and narrow).
