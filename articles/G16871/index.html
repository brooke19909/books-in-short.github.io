Smartphone Explay Tornado: model reviews and reviews

The Russian brand Explay is among the leaders in the mobile device market in the Russian Federation. The company produces inexpensive high-performance and functional devices. Among them is the Explay Tornado smartphone. What are its features? What interesting can be said about his brother - the Explay Tornado tablet?

Key features of the smartphone

The main feature that characterizes the smartphone Explay Tornado, reviews of which are distributed on a huge number of portals of lovers of mobile electronics - support for 3 SIM-cards.





But also other features of the device also deserve attention. If only because Explay is known as a supplier of devices that successfully combine functionality and a reasonable price.

Device specifications

Explore the key features of the Explay Tornado smartphone. Reviews of many users regarding them are extremely positive in terms of comparison with the democratic price of the device.

The device is equipped with a fairly large screen - 4.5 inches. Display resolution - 854 by 480 pixels. Its structure uses the high-tech IPS matrix.

The MediaTek 6582 processor installed in the smartphone has 4 cores and operates at a clock frequency of 1.2 GHz.

The phone supports communications in 2G and 3G standards - and on all three SIM-cards. Using the device, you can also connect to Wi-Fi networks, GPS. There is support for Bluetooth.

The amount of RAM installed in the device is 512 MB.

The amount of internal flash memory is 4 GB.

There are two cameras. The main one has a resolution of 5 megapixels, front - 0.3.

Battery capacity: 1550 mAh.

The dimensions of the device are as follows: length - 65.6 mm, width - 32 mm, thickness - 9.2 mm. The device weighs 141 grams.

The device is available in several colors - white, black, blue, red, and green. For example, the Explay Tornado White modification is very popular in Russia. Reviews regarding its design are very positive.





White color makes the phone suitable both for young people and for people who, by virtue of their profession or activity, such as entrepreneurs, wear clothes in a conservative style. Distributed in stores in the Russian Federation is also a modification of the black device - Explay Tornado Black. The reviews regarding her design are also quite positive.





This model will look great, for example, against the background of a classic suit. But no matter what color the phone is, whether it is a stylish White model or a strict Explay Tornado Black smartphone, user reviews are unanimous in one thing: the devices are quite in line with modern trends in the design of mobile devices. It is noted that the build quality and materials in all color modifications are the highest.

Appearance

What does the device look like? Appearance Explay Tornado (reviews of many users confirm this) - modern, stylish, moderately conservative. The device in this point of view is suitable for people with a wide variety of preferences in the field of fashion and style, as we noted above. On the back of its panel there are quite unusual patterns, stylized under the skin.

The camera is not located in the center of the device, but in the upper left corner. It is equipped with a rather spectacular flash of the LED type. At the bottom of the case is a speaker covered with a delicate perforated damper. On the right side of the device are the power button of the device, as well as a key to control the sound volume. There are no items on the left side of the device. At the top of the device is a slot for connecting a microUSB wire, as well as a headphone jack. Below is a microphone.

The display of the device is covered with a strong protective glass. Above it is a speaker, an additional camera, as well as light and proximity sensors . Here is a signal LED. Under the screen are the standard touch buttons - go to the menu, "home" and "return". Screen - with support for multi-touch (up to 5 clicks at a time).

To install a SIM card in the device, you need to remove the back cover and remove the battery. Each of the three SIM cards is installed in a separate way. It can be noted that the formats of the slots for the 1st and 2nd cards are standard, for the 3rd - only micro SIM.

Actually about performance. Let's consider this aspect in more detail. How impressive is it, based on the hardware characteristics of the smartphone? And what do users say about this?

Performance

To what extent can the Explay Tornado be considered productive? The reviews of users who tested it using games and various applications generally characterize the device as quite suitable for launching common games and programs. Due to the fact that the device has a relatively small amount of RAM - only 512 MB, demanding games, unfortunately, will slow down slightly.





However, a powerful processor with a frequency of 1.3 GHz and 4 cores, as well as an integrated module for processing graphics such as Mali400MP, largely compensates for the lack of RAM.

Offline work

As we noted above, the battery capacity in the smartphone is 1550 mAh. By the standards of the modern market this, I must say, is not enough. What do experts say about this, who tested the Explay Tornado smartphone for battery life? Expert reviews, as well as users, generally characterize the device as very average regarding the function in question, which is not surprising, based on battery capacity indicators. The batteries last for about one day with fairly intensive use of the device.

It can be noted at the same time that battery life for many smartphone users is far from the most important criterion. And therefore, if one device has more attractive performance characteristics than a similar one with the same cost and with a battery capacity higher, then the first one will be most likely selected. Surely, many mobile electronics enthusiasts who wanted to buy Explay Tornado did the same. Perhaps it turned out to be preferable for them to have a device with a processor in 4 cores and 1.3 GHz, an excellent display and a modest battery, than a gadget with not the most powerful hardware, but a capacious battery.

In addition, not every similar smartphone can support on all three SIM-cards - like Explay Tornado - 3G. The reviews regarding this remarkable property of the phone are the most positive.





A tool with a characteristic that is very important in a number of cases falls into the hands of the user, for example, if on a trip you need to urgently switch from one operator to another with a cheap tariff option for accessing the Internet.

User reviews

Design, as we noted above, the owners of the device praise, and this applies to the phone in all its color solutions. Using the device, as many mobile electronics enthusiasts believe, is convenient. In many ways, this is facilitated by a functional screen with multitouch support.

Opinion regarding the device among Russian users is generally positive. Undoubtedly, the Russian brand Explay has managed to offer the market a competitive, high-performance, stylish and sought-after device. And for an adequate price - about 4900 rubles.

Older brother

The smartphone we are considering has a brother - the Explay Tornado tablet, reviews of which are also popular on the thematic portals of mobile electronics enthusiasts. What are the features of this device? Consider its key characteristics, and also compare them with the corresponding parameters of the Explay Tornado smartphone. Of course, the comparison will be very conditional - since the devices belong to different classes. But we are focusing precisely on possible similarities.

Explay Tornado Tablet Features

The display size of the device is 7 inches, the resolution is 1024 by 768 inches. The type of matrix is ‚Äã‚ÄãIPS. It is clear that in terms of display size, the tablet will be larger. Similarly, the screen supports the multi-touch function. Technologically, the displays on the smartphone and tablet are very close.

The processor installed on the tablet is MediaTek MT8312. Unlike the chip that the Explay Tornado smartphone is equipped with, it has 2 cores. The clock frequency of both processors is the same. However, the MT8312 microchip has been adapted - specifically for the tablet. In this sense, the corresponding hardware components, like the devices themselves, are of different classes.

Both devices support connecting to a computer via microUSB, working with wireless networks, and communicating via Bluetooth. There is GPS support.

On the tablet, you can install 2 SIM-cards of a usual format, and it, like a smartphone, supports Internet connection via 3G.

Well, despite the fact that the devices belong to different classes, their technical characteristics are quite close and indicate that the devices are designed to solve approximately the same tasks: access the Internet from any available channel, whether WiFi or 3G, launch undemanding games and applications, amateur photography.
