Pattaya Emerald: Hotel Emerald by Sofi 3 *

Description. The first thing that catches your eye when you arrive at Emerald by Sofi Pattaya 3 * is the wonderful location of the hotel. Transport, slowly moving along noisy streets among a myriad of shops, suddenly turns to one side, drives 100 meters, and vacationers find themselves in a quiet, calm and very picturesque street. The contrast with the main street is simply amazing.

Emerald by Sofi 3 * fully matches its name

july It really looks like a bright emerald, and the hostess is actually called Sofia.

Tourists of all ages stay at the hotel: spouses without children and with them, small youth companies, elderly people, in short, all who are not afraid of the concept of ‚Äúeconomy class vacation‚Äù.

The second, but not the last advantage of Emerald by Sofi 3 * is transport accessibility. Once you leave the hotel, walk 100 meters and raise your hand, a jeep will immediately stop nearby, which will take you where you need to. The fare is 10 baht (read: rubles) per person, regardless of distance.

The rooms. Emerald by Sofi 3 * is 36 rooms of different comforts located on different floors of a five-story building. Standard rooms are no different from those located around the world. They have balconies, bathrooms with hairdryers, toilets, refrigerators, air conditioners. A nice detail: the rooms have microwaves, so you can cook or reheat food right in the room. Pleased with a free safe and WI-FI.

The rooms with higher comfort are more spacious, but otherwise they differ little from the "standards".



Cleaning is carried out every other day, linen is also not changed daily, but the cleanliness of the hotel fully meets the requirements of economy class.

It should be noted that Emerald by Sofi 3 * was opened in 2011, therefore both the furniture and the sanitary ware are in good condition.

Beach. The sandy beach of Jomtien is located opposite the hotel, it is not more than 5 minutes walk. It is equipped, like all beaches. Umbrellas and chaise lounges are paid (about 30 baht for the whole day). It is better to swim in the morning: in the evening the ebb begins, and the sea recedes.

Nutrition. All who rested in Emerald by Sofi 3 * indicate that the menu in the hotel is pretty monotonous, but no one was left hungry. The type of food is best negotiated when buying a tour.

The hotel has a restaurant and bar.

Information for vacationers. The hotel infrastructure is even slightly better than the economy class suggests. There is a good pool, bar, great live music, karaoke, a spa room. Parents can hire a babysitter.



A Russian-speaking representative is always present at the hotel, so there is no problem with understanding.

A hundred meters from the hotel there is a street with many inexpensive shops, snack bars, massage parlors.

Reviews Everyone who is used to relaxing in three-star hotels is quite satisfied with the service. Guests note the excellent work of Russian tour operators present at the hotel. The rooms are not cleaned daily, but diligently.

Tourists like that upon arrival at the hotel they are given SIM cards with an inexpensive rate for talking with Russia and local calls.

The cuisine, despite the fact that the dishes are repeated day after day, is quite decent: the hostess of the hotel takes into account the habits of foreign tourists, so even the national food here is tasty and not too exotic. Both European and local Thai dishes are of high quality.
