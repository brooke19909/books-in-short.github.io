Hotel Kavros Garden 3 * (Crete): description, reviews and photos

One of the most picturesque resort towns of Crete is the village of Georgioupolis. It perfectly combines the charm of a small fishing village and developed tourist infrastructure. If you want to spend an unforgettable vacation in a calm and picturesque place of Crete and at the same time stay in a comfortable economy class hotel, we suggest you pay attention to the Kavros Garden Hotel 3 *. Today we will get to know this hotel more closely, and also find out the opinion of our compatriots who have already visited here in the summer.





Where is

The three-star Kavros Garden Hotel is located three kilometers from the village of Georgioupolis. The city of Rethymno is 16 kilometers away and the port city of Chania is 40 kilometers away. You can get to these settlements by bus (a bus stop is 200 meters from the hotel). The nearest international airport is 96 kilometers from the hotel and is located in the city of Heraklion. Thus, after arrival, the journey to your hotel will take about two hours. Kavros Garden has its own magnificent beach. It is located a couple of hundred meters from the hotel, so you can walk to it in just a few minutes. By the way, if you are planning a trip to Crete on your own, without the help of a tour operator, you can order a transfer from the airport directly at the hotel. It will cost you less than a taxi.





Kavros Garden Hotel 3 * (Crete): description and photo

This hotel is perfect for both family and youth recreation. It consists of several buildings, surrounded by beautiful vegetation. Guests are invited to stay in one of the 100 comfortable rooms of standard and family type. Kavros Garden not only has its own magnificent sandy beach, on its territory there are also two outdoor pools, a tennis court, sports grounds, a gym, a restaurant and two bars.

Posting Rules

In accordance with the internal rules of Kavros Garden 3 * (Crete), guests are checked into rooms after two in the afternoon. However, if you arrive at the hotel earlier, then if you have free rooms, you will be accommodated immediately. On the day of departure, you must leave the room before noon.





Number of rooms

As already mentioned, the three-star Kavros Garden Hotel has 100 rooms located in several small buildings. The housing stock is represented by two types of rooms: standard and family. All rooms have air conditioning, a private bathroom with a bathtub and a hairdryer, a TV with Russian channels, a mini fridge, and free Wi-Fi. Some rooms also have a kitchenette. Rooms are cleaned every day (excluding Sunday). Maids change towels and bedding several times a week.

Nutrition

Hotel Kavros Garden 3 * offers its guests a choice of food according to two schemes: "half board" and "all inclusive". In the first case, you will be offered breakfast and dinner, and in the second - three meals a day. A buffet is served in the hotel restaurant and includes both local and international dishes.





Beach vacation

As already mentioned, the Kavros Garden 3 * hotel (island of Crete) has its own beach. It is only a couple of hundred meters from the hotel. The beach here is sandy and very clean. There is a narrow pebble strip when entering the water. Also, the entrance to the water is gentle, so the beach is great for families with children. On the beach you can use sun loungers and parasols for free. In addition, there is equipment for water sports (for an additional fee).

Entertainment

As already mentioned, for guests of Kavros Garden 3 * on the territory of the hotel are equipped with two outdoor pools. Near them there are spacious sun terraces with comfortable sun loungers and shady umbrellas. There is also a bar with a wide selection of different drinks. During the day, guests of the hotel will be offered animators to play volleyball, water polo or do water aerobics. In addition, on site you can play tennis, ping pong or billiards.





Infrastructure

Hotel Kavros Garden 3 *, reviews of tourists confirm that, it has everything you need for a comfortable stay. So, the reception here works round the clock, and all hotel staff are always happy to answer questions and help guests. The hotel also has an internet room and a TV lounge. Throughout the hotel you can use free Wi-Fi. The hotel has its own parking, so if you plan to rent a car, you will not have problems with parking. In addition, Kavros Garden offers guests a shuttle service to and from the airport.

For children

The three-star Kavros Garden Hotel also took care of the leisure and comfort of the youngest travelers. So, for children there are special shallow sections in the pools. In addition, there is animation for the smallest.
