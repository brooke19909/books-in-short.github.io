The village of Lazurnoe (Crimea): conditions, housing, reviews of tourists

Each corner of the Crimean peninsula is unique and worthy of the attention of tourists. Some prefer developed resorts with a huge selection of entertainment, while others seek to retire to nature and enjoy their holidays in silence. Each of the guests of the Crimea will find for themselves that very ideal place for a real good rest.

Lazurnoe Village

Just lovers of quiet relaxation and extraordinary nature should pay attention to the modest village of Lazurnoye, which is located just a few kilometers from Alushta.

The village of Lazurnoe (Crimea) is located at the foot of the picturesque Mount Kastel. In Soviet times, it began to build a large hotel complex in it, but until today the construction has not been completed. The resort infrastructure in Lazurny is poorly developed. There is no entertainment in this place, the beaches are wild and undeveloped, but there is always room for accommodation.





The cleanest sea and sparsely populated beaches are the main attractions of the village of Lazurnoe (Crimea). Those who wish to have fun can visit Alushta: only about half an hour by bus - and you are in the very center of the city, and there is a lot of entertainment there.

The beaches in the village are pebble. Minimum beach activities in high season are still available. Near the sea there are several tents with drinks, pasties and ice cream.

The surroundings of the village of Lazurnoe (Crimea) are very picturesque. Not far from the center there is a small lake, higher in the mountains there is a small rivulet. A variety of vegetation, a combination of mountain and sea air create excellent conditions for relaxation and treatment.

Camping "Cote d'Azur"

Crimea is not only a modern service and comfortable beaches. Among tourists there are lovers of wild relaxation, and it was they who chose the coast of the village of Lazurnoe. Near the sea for the "savages" organized camping "Cote d'Azur."

In the summer, camping enthusiasts can stay in a comfortable place near the village of Lazurnoe. The tent camp can simultaneously accommodate more than a hundred people vacationers.





Tents are rented out here or tourists can put their own.

Each tent has lighting; there are wooden flooring and soft mattresses.

On the campsite there were built showers and toilets, washbasins, garbage cans, drinking water is transported.

Fans of wild vacations do not ignore this place, so in the season there are a lot of people here, especially on weekends.

Housing in the village of Lazurnoe

The private sector of Lazurny offers tourists lodging in small guest houses and mini-pensions. There is housing for any wallet, so if your budget is limited, then it is better not to find a place.

The guest houses of the village are quite modern and comfortable, but they have one feature - almost all of them are located on a hill, and in order to reach the sea, you will have to overcome a number of steps.

Part of the boarding houses are located at the foot of the Kastel mountain, among them is the Lazurny boarding house (Crimea), which has a green area, cozy gazebos in the yard. Rest here allows you to enjoy all the charms of the Crimea - mountain landscapes, sea landscapes, beautiful clean air and peace.





Among the guest houses of Cote d'Azur, a modern manor that also offers rooms is striking. It is located on the mountain itself and is built on three levels, which are connected by chic stairs. Silence reigns in the district, only the singing of birds violates and complements this idyll. Fresh air, mountain springs and extraordinary natural beauties are the village of Lazurnoe (Crimea).

Reviews about the rest in the village

Of course, this kind of vacation is not for everybody, that's why reviews about Lazurny are contradictory. Those tourists who were not ready for the features of the village expressed their disappointment with the lack of the usual resort infrastructure. However, all vacationers are happy with nature.

Beaches are the sights of the village of Lazurnoe (Crimea). Then, when there is no place to drop a leaf in Alushta, in this wild place deserted shores, pure water.





Many tourists were dissatisfied with the great descent to the sea, however, these are the features of this mountain village. In general, rest in Lazurne is exclusively for an amateur. Yes, there is chic air, silence, peace, beautiful nature, but the lack of infrastructure for many tourists is a significant drawback.
