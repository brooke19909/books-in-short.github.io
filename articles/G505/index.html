New Year tree made of paper. With our own hands we make a decorative tree

Creative people try for any occasion to prepare gifts for relatives and friends with their own hands. A paper Christmas tree is a wonderful New Year present. Such a product will certainly serve as a beautiful decoration of the interior of the house. How to make a handmade herringbone? You will find the answer to this question in the materials of this article. We have selected the most interesting ideas for you. Study them and translate them into reality.



Origami from paper "Christmas tree"

For work, prepare the following materials:

  • color double-sided paper;
  • scissors;
  • plasticine;
  • wooden stick;
  • decor (sequins, beads, buttons, bows);
  • wrapping material (mesh, paper, fabric);
  • glue;
  • compass;
  • tape.

Description of the process of making crafts "Christmas tree" from paper.

  1. On a piece of paper, draw a circle, cut it along the contour.
  2. Divide the resulting part into seven identical parts and put marks. From the center to each point, draw straight lines.
  3. Fold the product on the principle of a fan, performing folds precisely in stripes.
  4. Glue the tip of the wooden stick to the center of the paper blank from the wrong side. Next, attach the product joints to the skewer, forming a Christmas tree figure. The sides of the product also stick together. It turns out a Christmas tree, inside of which a stick-trunk is glued.
  5. Form a cube from plasticine and insert the tip of the craft barrel into it.
  6. Wrap the bottom of the product with wrapping paper, net or cloth, tie a ribbon at the base of the barrel.
  7. Decorate the figure with decorative elements: beads, sequins, bows.

Decorative Christmas tree made of paper. We put in the paper

Old magazines with color pages can make a bright and exclusive Christmas tree. For its manufacture, the following materials will be needed:

  • wooden block;
  • pin (metal, plastic, wooden);
  • Magazines
  • thick cardboard;
  • scissors;
  • ruler;
  • pencil;
  • hole puncher;
  • big bead.

You will learn how to make a Christmas tree out of paper from the step-by-step instructions.

  1. Fix the pin (you can take a thick wire) in the bar.
  2. From cardboard, make square patterns of different sizes.
  3. Circle the patterns on the magazine sheets and cut the blanks (20-25 pieces of various sizes). If you have curly scissors, then do this with them. Squares will turn out with beautiful wavy or jagged edges.
  4. From thick cardboard, make gaskets measuring 3x3 centimeters. There should be a lot of them, about a hundred pieces. Punch a hole in the center of each such part with a hole punch.
  5. Starting with the largest workpieces, string the squares onto the pin. Wearing 5-6 parts, insert the gasket. It will serve as a separator of paper elements. Thus string all the details cut from magazines, arranging them from large to smallest.
  6. At the top of the tree, glue the bead (to the tip of the pin). For convenience of fastening, you can make a hole in it with a hot awl, pour glue into it and put on the tip. A decorative Christmas tree made of paper is ready.

Here are such simple ways to make beautiful New Year trees from available materials, we told you in this article. Use them, and literally in one evening you will make exclusive gifts for loved ones with your own hands.
