The best ski resorts: "Tuutari Park", St. Petersburg

Only 25 kilometers from St. Petersburg, if you leave from the metro station "Moskovskaya", and the traveler arrives at "Tuutari Park" (St. Petersburg). This is the real splendor of skiing - bright, interesting, active and fun. Any guest can choose something for themselves: there are cheesecakes for children, simple skis for beginners, serious slopes for professional snowboarders. There are lifts, cafes and restaurants, playgrounds. The resort is ready to welcome guests in winter and summer.





Fun in the park

Visiting Tuutari Park (St. Petersburg), guests can play:

- volleyball;

- basketball;

- football;

- streetball.

You can ride:

- horses;

- snowmobiles;

- cheesecakes;

- cross-country or skiing.

There are excellent opportunities for swimming and paintball, snowboarding, paragliders await.

The possibilities are endless

“Tuutari Park” (St. Petersburg) today is a recreation center equipped in accordance with European standards. The infrastructure is developed here, which guarantees comfort for all guests. The resort works not only as a destination for family vacations and sports, but also as a multifunctional platform, allowing the organization of various events.

The park organizes events throughout the year, both in winter and in summer. Arriving guests can participate or watch what is happening.





How much it costs?

Of course, a potential visitor to the resort is interested in what prices are in Tuutari Park (St. Petersburg). Here is the approximate cost of the service. It may vary. The price is determined by the administration of the resort, on weekends a vacation costs slightly more:

- the lift will cost 400 rubles per hour;

- children's lift costs 200 r / hour;

- An additional discount on the ISIC card allows you to use the lift for 350 r / hour;

- season ticket for the season with the right of transfer for an adult costs 20 thousand, for a child - 12 thousand rubles;

- An evening subscription that allows visiting the park on weekdays until six in the evening will cost 12,000 rubles;

- A subscription to a family of two parents and one child costs 44 thousand.

Equipment rental

Rarely do guests visit Tuutari Park (St. Petersburg) with everything they need. This is not a problem, because equipment can be rented. Estimated price tag in rubles:

- snowboard - from 200;

- snowboard boots - from 150;

- children's snowboard - from 150;

- sticks - from 50;

- points - from 100;

Tuutari Park is a ski resort equipped with a skating area. For adults, access to the skating rink will cost 150 rubles, and up to 12 years old it will cost only 100. Skates are rented out without time limits, and it will cost 250 or 350 rubles, which depends on the age of the visitor.





Unique location

The resort was opened in a region rich in healing waters and mountain slopes near the Northern capital of Russia. Under “Tuutari Park” 25 hectares of land were allocated near Mount Kirchhoff. Incidentally, the beauty of local landscapes was once appreciated by Peter the Great.

The emperor built himself a small hunting lodge in the area and had fun skiing and shooting game. That is how the first person in the state who became a true legend of Russia spent his leisure time. During World War II, the historic building was destroyed, but the image of a hunting lodge has survived to this day in local legends. Seeing where it was located with your own eyes - isn’t this a real pleasure for someone who cares about Russian history? In addition to the important historical background, the region attracts tourists with its crystal clear air, luxurious silence of wooded mountains and rich mineral springs, warm even in crackling frost.

However, no matter how ancient and rich the history of the area is, Tuutari Park itself is a completely modern resort, equipped in accordance with current standards and designed for the discerning client. It is equipped with the latest technology, it is developing and improving over the years.
