Water park "Plaza" in Blagoveshchensk: description, address, mode of operation

The water park "Plaza" in Blagoveshchensk is a favorite vacation spot for all citizens. Here, visitors not only spend time in pools and slides, but also play sports. The water park conducts various events and promotions. In cozy cafes you can have a snack and relax after an active holiday.

Where is

The official address of the water park "Plaza" in Blagoveshchensk: st. Factory 154/1. Near the center there is a small parking lot for personal transport of visitors.

Guests can familiarize themselves with the opening hours of the Plaza water park in Blagoveshchensk: from Tuesday to Sunday from 10:00 to 22:00. On Monday, visitors are expected from 13:00 to 20:00.

The Plaza water park in Blagoveshchensk has several pools. Each has multi-level filters that purify water. Timely processing is carried out here to protect vacationers from becoming infected with various infectious diseases.

Steam rooms and games

The wellness center has an area where children can relax from water activities and have fun. Trampolines, a labyrinth with soft balls, slot machines and small attractions are installed in the game rooms.

In this area, there are places for creativity and master classes. Professional educators and animators work in the play area. Here, customers often have parties for their children's birthday with quests and a festive table.

There are several steam rooms in the center. They are equipped in accordance with all requirements and standards. In the Russian steam room humidity reaches 100%. It is allowed to use brooms and bath procedures.

In a Turkish steam room, the conditions are somewhat milder. Staying here helps to remove toxins from the body, improve metabolism and normalize the functioning of the cardiovascular system.

Infrared steam rooms are distinguished by the presence of special equipment that forms rays. They give the opportunity to warm the body as much as possible. These procedures are very beneficial for the body.

other services

In the aquapark "Plaza" in Blagoveshchensk, there is a gym. It has the most modern sports equipment. Experienced trainers can work with clients at will and for an additional fee.

There is a cafe-bar on site. The menu consists of dishes of Russian traditional cuisine. It has a special section for children. It includes favorite dishes of kids.

The cafe has a good range of soft drinks, milkshakes, homemade soft drinks. In it you can organize any celebration.

The leisure center has a swimming school. In it, professional trainers conduct group and individual classes for children from 5 to 15 years.

The center also conducts classes in water aerobics. They invite everyone to the adult residents of the city, including pregnant women. There are several massage chairs in the center where guests can relax.

During warmer months, an outdoor terrace is available. Near it there is an outdoor pool, sun loungers and a small garden.

Reviews

There are a lot of various comments about the work of this entertainment complex. Mostly reviews are written in a positive way. Guests note that the locker rooms are always clean, there are enough lockers for all visitors.

Vacationers are pleased with the opportunity to visit the salt cave, the microclimate of which helps to strengthen the respiratory system and general immunity. Residents of the city are happy to attend classes in water aerobics. They are also encouraged by the possibility of teaching children to swim under the supervision of experienced instructors.

According to customer reviews, the cafe has a pretty good menu, but the prices are somewhat overpriced. It serves delicious milkshakes.

Several negative comments were left about the quality of the dressing room. There are several complaints that they do not provide a coat hanger, and sometimes expensive top things disappear. Therefore, guests of the complex do not recommend going here in mink coats.

There are reviews that at the checkout staff does not always have information about ongoing promotions. Therefore, clients should remind them of this and in case of conflict, contact the administrators of the Plaza water park in Blagoveshchensk, whose photos are presented in the article.
