The best insurance company in OSAGO: list, features and reviews

The problem of choosing an insurance company is faced by both beginners and people who in the past had negative experience in purchasing a policy. Most often this is due to the unreasonable expectation of payments. Therefore, it is important to know how to choose the right company. It is important to understand how to take into account not only subjective factors, but also real financial indicators. Read more about how to determine which insurance company is better for CTP and any other type of insurance, read on.

Problem

Annually, 42 million car owners acquire CTP insurance and another 5 million - CASCO. After amendments to the legislation were made in 2014, customers seek only their insurer for damages. The amount of payment depends on the policy of the company. How to understand which insurance company is better to issue compulsory motor third-party liability insurance?





Search for information

A good CTP insurance company is a reliable company that can quickly fulfill its obligations . Even the most unfavorable insurer should be able to make payments and not go bankrupt.

The complexity of the choice is compounded by the number of companies operating in the market. How to choose from hundreds of organizations which insurance company is better for compulsory motor third-party liability insurance? You can, of course, conduct your own analysis of open information posted on official websites, forums, blogs and social networks. It will not be superfluous to know the opinion of the company's former customers. Do not forget about the annual ratings of insurers.

Collection of information

If we are talking about a small or little-known company, then the first thing is to check whether it has a license to conduct business. Only companies with the appropriate license can provide insurance services. In Russia there are more than 400 of them, and each of them has dozens of agents. The register of insurers is presented on the Bank of Russia website in the financial market section. It is better not to contact insurers registered in another country. More trust deserve organizations operating over 10 years in the market.





Payout Amount

The spread in damages is less than it was before. When calculating the cost of repairs, all organizations must apply the methodology approved by the Bank of Russia. Differences can only be in the timing of payments. This is how the situation looks in theory. In practice, in 2014, insurers raised 150 billion rubles, and transferred only 90.3 billion to the victims, while according to the law, 77% of the collected funds should be paid to victims. The explanation is simple. Firstly, in 2014 there was no unified methodology for determining the cost of repairs. Secondly, at the time of purchase of the policy, funds are paid to the cashier. An insured event may occur at any time in the future. Therefore, most of the collected premiums go to the reserve.

Financial stability

The ability of an organization to fulfill its obligations in any economic situation is reflected in financial indicators. It is especially important to analyze reporting when it comes to long-term cooperation. Even a good OSAGO insurance company may have problems with payment. What should I pay attention to?

The authorized capital must exceed the minimum established (120 million).

The growth of the organization‚Äôs assets reflects the positive dynamics of development, especially if it is carried out at the expense of the collected premiums. A good CTP insurance company has a large amount of free cash.

The number of concluded agreements must coincide with financial capabilities.

Insurance reserves most often do not exceed inflation. But their decline in dynamics is a bad trend.





Too high a payout level should also alert potential customers. Indeed, in essence, the organization‚Äôs earnings are collected bonuses. How can a company exist if it has paid customers more money than it raised?

Large companies transfer to other organizations up to 50% of the insurance portfolio.

That's how a good insurance company (CTP) works.

Reviews

Each case is individual. Even after analyzing the feedback on the forums, it is difficult to determine which organization is the best insurance company in compulsory motor liability insurance. All customers face the same problems:

  • long consideration of the application (it may take a month to verify the documents);
  • underestimated amount of payments (each insurance reconciles all calculations of appraisers with a reference to the SAR, the received value is adjusted for the return of the amount of VAT, and the rest should be paid to the client).

Most affected are customers who purchased an insurance policy in Crimea. Not all insurance companies have representative offices there. Therefore, customers have to return for payments to Rosgosstrakh.





Insurance rate

Low prices should alert potential customers. Dumping is done by small or completely new companies that are trying to attract customers. The strategy is relevant only at the first stage of work. Over time, it threatens bankruptcy. Chasing low prices when concluding a long-term contract makes no sense.

The amount of payment upon the occurrence of an insured event is calculated based on the tariff multiplied by the amount of coverage selected by the client. The resulting value is adjusted for correction factors. In the case of compulsory motor third-party liability insurance, this may be the age of the driver, place of residence, method of storing the car, driving experience, etc. The more inexperienced the driver and the older the car, the more risks the company will have to cover. This in turn will affect the size of the contribution. A penny policy cannot cover all the risks. The insurer will not fulfill the promise. The client will lose funds and insurance coverage.

The presence of bonuses and loyalty programs, on the contrary, has a positive effect on the image of the company. A large company can reduce prices for regular customers.





Ratings

In the ranking, companies are ranked in five categories:

  • highly reliable - class A;
  • satisfactory level of risk - class B;
  • low level of reliability - class C;
  • bankruptcy - class D;
  • revocation of license - class E.

The best companies are further divided into three groups: reliable, very reliable and extremely reliable.

The agency does not evaluate work for free. Therefore, not all companies are included in the rating. At the same time, the absence of a company on the list does not mean that it cannot be trusted. People‚Äôs ratings are quantitative, and agencies are qualitative. To derive a more or less adequate assessment, one should rely on data from several sources for one long period.

Assessment scale "EXPERT RA"

External factors:

  • volume, dynamics of changes in assets, equity, capital, insurance premium;
  • position of the organization on the market (participation in associations, unions, reputation, brand, relations with supervisory authorities and specialization);
  • corporate governance (owners' potential, risk management, organization structure, infrastructure, activity transparency);
  • geography (the presence of a developed branch network).

Insurance business:

  • insurance portfolio ( risk diversification , profit by type of insurance);
  • customer base (the presence of constant counterparties, the proportion of terminated contracts, methods of distribution of policies, reinsurance partners);
  • reinsurance activities (reliability, effectiveness and diversification of protection, risk reinsurance, the ratio of the amount of payments to own funds).

Financial indicators are analyzed for:

  • liquidity and solvency;
  • profitability and loss ratio;
  • investment policy.
