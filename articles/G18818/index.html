Overview of push-button phones with "WhatsApp"

The so-called button phones is getting smaller. Although they are considered obsolete and functionally limited devices, however, today among us there are those who prefer to use them. Moreover, now there is the opportunity to purchase a phone in which the button keyboard is adjacent to the touch screen, which gives it a number of advantages over standard devices. In this article we will help those who wish to purchase a push-button phone with Vatsap support.

About Vatsap

This is the name of the application that enables people to exchange instant text messages. Using it, you can also send photos, videos, audio recordings. The application was made free in 2016. Today, every seventh inhabitant of the Earth uses it.

About Phones

It is easy to understand that the Vatsap application of push-button phones will not work correctly on all devices of this type. Only the following are suitable from them:

  • running on operating systems "Windows", "Android" or iOS
  • having large amounts of RAM;
  • equipped with a camera that is built into the "body" of the phone;
  • connecting to the internet.

Today, push-button phones are being created, on which Vatsap is already installed. That is, these phones do not need to download this application on their own, but you can immediately use it to receive and transmit text data, etc.

Suitable phone models

It is time to talk about those phone models that either already have the Vatsap application in their functionality or have the ability to install it there. We list some of them.

Elari safephone

This device is equipped with a touch screen (its diagonal is 2.4 inches), despite the fact that it is push-button. The developers supplied this phone with two cameras - front and rear, as well as a red SOS button. The device is one of those that support 3G.

Sonim XP6

This device has 8 GB of internal and 1 GB of RAM. He also has a touch screen as well as the above analogue. The phone supports 4G, is equipped with a 4800 mAh battery.

RugGear RG310

Another device that you should pay attention to those who want to buy a push-button phone with the Vatsap application. The device operates on the Android OS, version 4.2. The phone with two SIM cards works with 3G technology. Having received this device for use, it will also be possible to use WI-FI and GPS technologies. The RugGear RG310 has two cameras.

Nokia Asha 300 Red

This device, which began selling in the fall of 2011, has a button keyboard and touch screen. On this phone, with a Li-lon type battery and a plastic case, a 4.0 megapixel camera is installed. The phone is equipped with 128 MB of internal memory. The device has a slot for a memory card.

In this article, we talked about only a few push-button phones on which it is possible to install the Vatsap application. In fact, there are many more. So you should not have problems with choosing the right device.
