What is the age of the earth?

The minds of many scientists and ordinary people have long been haunted by the question: "What is the age of the Earth?" At the moment, the most popular way to determine the age of fossil finds is radiocarbon. But even this most reliable method has significant errors. Recently, scientists have realized that many factors affect the rate of radioactive decay , so it's impossible to take it as a constant. There are also many other theories that are allowed, but they need to be evaluated only together with other facts related to the theory of creation and evolution.
