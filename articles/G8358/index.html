The recreation center "Shrub": rest in a pine forest

In the summer, almost everyone wants to relax in nature and improve their health. Residents of the Nizhny Novgorod region can be helped by the Kustorka camp site (Pavlovsky district). Here, surrounded by a pine forest, there are all conditions for a full-fledged wellness stay.

Situation

A huge advantage of this base is its location. It occupies the territory near the lake. There is a sandy beach, which allows you to refresh yourself in cool water and even sit with a fishing rod. But this is not the only body of water in the area. Nearby there are several lakes, near which you can walk and get a portion of new sensations. And 12 km Oka flows. You can highlight the day and diversify your leisure.



Also, the local pine forest fills the air with the aroma of needles, so if you have experienced overstrain or stress, you need to come here to restore emotional balance and emotional strength. These forests are truly rich in mushrooms. Even if you have never before been fond of quiet hunting, it will be difficult to resist here, watching your neighbors carry whole baskets.

The Kustorka recreation center accepts guests not only in the summer. Vacationers in the cool season can choose a room in one of two brick buildings. Here you can hold an important event or organize a corporate holiday.

Number of rooms

Kustorka (Nizhny Novgorod Region) invites its guests to choose a room for their own stay. The recreation center has two buildings, they have economy rooms with private facilities and suites with all amenities. Any option is designed for 2, 3 and 4 places. Also, if you are relaxing with a company, you can rent a separate house. There are wooden cottages and panel houses.



The room rate includes three meals a day. For children, depending on their age, certain discounts apply.

Food at the camp site

Arriving on vacation, no one wants to spend days at the stove in the kitchen, so the Kustorka recreation center offers its guests three meals a day in the dining room. The daily menu is always varied. So that no one has any problems with digestion, the range includes traditional Russian dishes. Only experienced chefs work here, who produce tasty and satisfying dishes.

The dining room has a pleasant atmosphere, wooden furniture. Through the windows, the room is filled with coniferous aroma, and the surface of the water opens up to the eye . If the weather is bad, the room becomes cozy from a lighted fire. The staff also tries to be friendly and helpful. All these conditions contribute to a good appetite and good mood.

Infrastructure and leisure



"Kustorka" is a recreation center (Nizhny Novgorod region) with developed infrastructure. In addition to the dining room, there is a bar. Finnish and real Russian baths and a pool are also equipped. There are tables for billiards and tennis. Sports fields are available for guests, and a games room is for children.

On the base there are rental points for sports equipment, boats, catamarans. You can go hiking or cycling. There is a video showroom where you can watch movies.

The romance of nature can be supplemented by reading books, they can be easily taken at the local library.

If you prefer active entertainment, the Kustorka recreation center offers paintball. There are special platforms for this, and if you have not played this funny game before, local instructors will help you get comfortable.

Fishing on the lake



Another way to enjoy your time is fishing. The Kustorka staff will tell you all the features of local fishing. If you arrived without gear, there is an opportunity to rent them. So that a successful catch is not wasted, the assistant chefs will help cook the ear, soot or salted fish.

Reviews about the camp site

Picturesque places and good conditions for relaxation attract many guests who have been returning here for more than one year. The recreation center "Kustorka" has become a favorite place not only for residents of the Nizhny Novgorod region, but also for Muscovites. Almost every guest likes the service at the camp site, although some features were noticed. For example, reviews show that the canteens follow the traditions of Soviet catering. This means that the menu has meat dishes, pasta, borscht, cereals, but very few vegetables and fruits. For many children and men, such nutrition is only a joy, but women understand that in the summer there should be more healthy food.

Also, some advise you to order good rooms in the spring, for example in April. The fact is that the base is very popular, and there are not as many rooms with all amenities as we would like.



Another point noted by vacationers was the lake, which seems a bit swampy. Although this landscape looks picturesque, some do not dare to swim in a pond, and swim only in boats. But if you take a walk to Pioneer Lake, which is a few kilometers from the base, you can swim in a clean but cool place. On weekends, there are many vacationers on Pioneer Lake.
