"Pomegranate Bay". Rest and treatment in the Sverdlovsk region

Sanatorium "Pomegranate Bay" is a wonderful medical and health complex, which is located in the Sverdlovsk region. At the same time, it is far from urban areas and highways. The complex is located in a forested area, where there is always fresh air and you can enjoy wonderful birdsong for days on end. It is cool in the heat, while you can improve your health with the help of air nourished with pine needles. In season, the forest has a lot of different berries and mushrooms. But if a person does not understand the grades, then it is better not to collect anything. Nearby is a pond with a comfortably equipped beach, where there are sun loungers and umbrellas. The complex itself provides its guests with clean and comfortable rooms, as well as the services of professionally trained staff.

Room Service





The institution offers rooms of different categories. There are budget options, as well as premium apartments. Despite this, each room is very comfortable and equipped with everything necessary. From household appliances there is a refrigerator, TV, electric kettle. There is also a separate bathroom with a hairdryer, a set of mini-cosmetics, towels and bathrobes. Depending on the category of the room, the bathroom is equipped with a bathtub, jacuzzi or shower. All plumbing and furniture is new, so do not worry about the fact that it will fail.

Food in the health-improving complex "Pomegranate Bay"




The dining room is on the ground floor. There are always delicious smells, it is framed in a cozy home style. This is done in order to complete the comfort of guests. The dining room is designed for 180 seats. You can always choose your favorite table. It is very interesting that they offer different types of food. You can eat 3, 4 or 5 times a day. The menu is compiled individually, based on the needs of the guest. The daily diet has everything you need, healthy and wholesome food predominates. This, of course, is fresh vegetables and fruits, and at any time of the year. Porridge, light soups, boiled meat and fish, also on the table there are sour-milk products and home-made cheese. If the guest wants to adjust his figure, then the dietitian will be happy to create an individual menu. And in the dining room they will prepare everything you need. If the client wants to enjoy sweets or other "harmful" products, then there is a bar where you can have a snack or a tasty cocktail.

Wellness treatments

Doctors of this institution treat a variety of diseases, such as diseases of the blood supply system, disorders of the nervous system, various respiratory diseases, as well as digestive tract disorders. The treatment is carried out by professional doctors who take advanced training courses and constantly improve their knowledge.

Base "Pomegranate Bay" offers its visitors an interesting program called "Mother and Child." This is a very pleasant and interesting way of healing the body of a mother together with children. Throughout the course, a person is invited to drink clean water, consume environmentally friendly products, take walks in the fresh air, as well as water procedures.

In addition, a huge number of therapies are offered, for example, mud therapy, hydrotherapy. There is also a huge list of massage types offered, for example, acupressure, vacuum-roller and many others. There is a special salt chamber and a sound room. And this is not the whole list of services provided. All the necessary equipment is available, and, of course, professional doctors. The complex boasts its own spa, where you can relax and forget about all the problems.





Before taking any procedures, the guest is examined, he must go through a therapist and a number of narrow specialists. A pediatrician works for children. After the examination, complex treatment can already be prescribed. If the guest does not want to be examined, then he is offered neutral procedures and good service.

Entertainment and additional services





The administration of the complex took care of all aspects for a good rest. For leisure, they suggest spending time in a huge library where you can pick up a book for every taste. A variety of excursions are arranged. In the evening, a cultural program with animators and live music is organized. There are also sports activities such as volleyball, a gym and much more. There is a children's room for children, as well as an outdoor playground.

Sanatorium "Pomegranate Bay". Prices

Rest and recreation in this complex is available to everyone. Compared to overseas resorts, prices are quite low, and the level of service is not inferior in quality.

A single room costs about 1800 rubles per person per day, a double room costs 1600 rubles, a double suite costs 2300 rubles, a single room costs 3800 rubles.

You can pay for accommodation with treatment. In this case, another 500-1000 rubles must be added to the base cost, depending on the number of days.

Accommodation under the program "Mother and Child" without treatment costs from 2000 rubles. up to 4000 rubles., with treatment - from 3500 rubles. up to 5000 rub.
