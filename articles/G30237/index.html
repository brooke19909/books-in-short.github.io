What material is best for making cosmetics

A fashionable crocheted cosmetic bag will become functional only when the yarn and design are chosen correctly. The functionality of the product will also depend on the quality of the thread. If the yarn is too light, then the makeup bag will not last long.

The material must meet several conditions:

  1. Yarn should be easy to wash. To do this, it is advisable to use synthetic threads.
  2. It is advisable to use yarn in dark colors, as the product will often get dirty from cosmetics.
  3. So that the product does not appear spools and loops do not get stuck, it is worth choosing yarn, which is a retinue of two threads.

Other preferences are determined by the features of the type of future product and the desires of the needlewoman herself. Some craftsmen make threads themselves from old dimensionless tights or garbage bags.

Arrangement of a homemade cosmetic bag

To make the product meet all the needs of the user, it is better to make a cosmetic bag yourself. The easiest way is to tie the product. Usually, a crocheted cosmetic bag is trimmed and decorated with your own hands. The main finish implies the presence of such elements:

  • User-friendly clasp. Most often used lightning, button or button.
  • On the "case" can be placed pockets for storing small hairpins, rubber bands, cotton buds.
  • If the model implies the presence of a cover, then it is necessary to consider the mechanism of its fastening and fixing.
  • In addition to the functional arrangement, it is worth paying attention to decoration. You can decorate a cosmetic bag with any elements and in any quantity.

If the bag for cosmetics will be used only at home, then you can attach legs to it or make a stand. When the accessory is used to carry cosmetics in a bag, then too complicated arrangement is not required.

Crochet Round Cosmetic Bag

Crocheting a round cosmetic bag is quite simple. In this case, a product of this form will become a real decoration of the toilet stoic. An interesting shape and spaciousness make the product for storing cosmetics a favorite in comparison with other options. The only drawback is that it is inconvenient to transport this accessory.

The principle of manufacturing a round cosmetic bag:

  1. Link a chain of 5 air loops (VP). Close the chain into the ring.
  2. Knit the first row with single crochet columns (RLS).
  3. The second row also knit sc. In each column of the previous row, knit 2 sc.
  4. In each subsequent row, add 8 RLS evenly.
  5. When the circle will satisfy its size, you can begin to manufacture the walls of the cosmetic bag.
  6. Without cutting the thread, you need to turn the work and start knitting through a row from the edge.
  7. In the process of manufacturing the walls of the cosmetic bag, no additions or decreases are necessary.
  8. Upon completion of knitting the boards, we proceed to the manufacture of the cover.
  9. The lid is knitted in the same way as the bottom of the product. Then the element is fixed with a zipper or buttons.

DIY oval cosmetic bag

The principle of making an oval cosmetic bag is the same as a round one. But there is a difference in knitting patterns, which sets such a shape for the product.

The instruction of the oval-shaped crochet cosmetic bag is quite simple if you first learn the principle of creating a round analogue:

  1. Link a chain of 5 VIs.
  2. We turn the work and knit RLS in each VP. It turns out a strip.
  3. Next, circular knitting is done - the strip is tied.
  4. In each subsequent round, it is worth adding 2 sc. So, in each subsequent round 4 additional RLS appear.

Then the walls fit and, if necessary, the lid. The principle of decoration is determined by the needlewoman herself.

How to make a flat mini cosmetic bag with your own hands: for beginners

The simplest option for self-production is a flat crochet cosmetic bag (instructions for beginners are presented below). In this case, the product has a rectangular or square shape, which simplifies the knitting pattern.

The sequence of actions will be as follows:

  1. Link a chain of 15 VPs. The number of loops determines the size of the product - this is the length of the finished purse for cosmetics.
  2. Next, you need to knit the fabric using any pattern. For beginners, the ideal option would be double crochet (CCH), which are knitted in each column of the previous row without additions or decreases.
  3. To determine the desired height of the cosmetic bag, you need to periodically fold the canvas in half. When the size will be completely satisfied, you just need to sew two halves of the fabric on the sides.
  4. In order for the cosmetic bag to close, you can sew in a zipper.

Crochet cosmetic bag

A cosmetic bag that has the shape of a bag and has the same functionality will look very impressive and original. A detailed and understandable description of a cosmetic bag crocheted in the shape of a bag will help simplify the work and create a real masterpiece. To do this, you can use this option:

  1. First dial 5 VP. Close the chain in a ring, since the base of the bag will be knitted according to the principle of a circle.
  2. The first row to knit sc. Then, in each subsequent row, do 8 uniform additions.
  3. When the circle reaches the required size, you need to finish the work by fastening the thread.
  4. Next, a strip of the desired width is knitted. It should be of such a length that allows you to connect the two parts of the finished circle.
  5. Fold a circle in half. Now, between the halves, on the one side, a previously connected strip is sewn.
  6. On the other hand, zipper is sewn.

To make the product visually look like a bag, you can sew on pens from any type of material.
