Zouara Hotel 3 (Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt) is an ideal place to relax

Description: the beach complex Zouara Hotel 3, built in 2002 in the most popular, environmentally friendly and beautiful Egyptian resort of Sharm El Sheikh, in the picturesque bay of El Nabq, 10 km from the airport and 1 km from the sandy beach, give you an unforgettable vacation and a sea of ‚Äã‚Äãpleasant experiences. On the well-groomed and ennobled territory elegant three-story buildings are located, in which spacious and beautifully decorated rooms are located.



It offers tourists a variety of entertainment, sports facilities, water sports and much more. Thanks to the developed infrastructure, attentive attitude of employees and impeccable service, you will feel at ease, relaxed and comfortable. And the lack of nearby transport hubs and noisy discos will contribute to a calm and quiet pastime.

The Zouara Hotel can be recommended to families with children, youth and scuba diving fans , since it is here that you can freely observe the life of marine life.

Number of rooms. The hotel consists of 175 rooms. Simple and comfortable rooms are equipped with everything necessary for a quality stay. Mandatory attributes are central air conditioning, a dedicated bathroom in the rooms, a hairdryer, a color television with satellite TV and a direct dial telephone. There is also a minibar, but it is filled for a fee.



This room has a spacious balcony with views of the courtyard. From the upper floors you can see the blue sea and the panorama of the city. For guests provided round-the-clock service - for a fee. The price includes daily cleaning. Bed linen is changed at least once a week. A cot is available on request for your children.

Nutrition. Zouara Hotel 3 is an all-inclusive property. Tables are served in the restaurant as a buffet from 10 am to 10 pm. Free drinks of different strengths of local production are offered (wine, rum, gin, beer, vodka, whiskey, tea, coffee). Light wines are served at lunch and dinner.

You can taste desserts, taste alcoholic cocktails and freshly squeezed juices in the lobby bar. And in the hotel restaurant for guests, professional chefs prepare various delicacies and international dishes.

Beach. A sandy and pebble equipped beach is located 1 km from Zouara Hotel 3. Every half an hour a comfortable bus runs to it. All services (sun loungers, mattresses, umbrellas, towels) are offered for free.



For entertainment, sports fields, catamarans, motorbikes, windsurfing and diving are offered here. There is a free bar with refreshing drinks. Walking along the beach is recommended in special rubber shoes, as there are large stones. Entrance to the sea is unsafe for children, so it is better to take small inflatable pools with you.

Additional Information. The Zouara Hotel 3 complex provides a variety of services. For example, for children there is a small round pool, a playground with swings and a sandbox. Adults will also not have to be bored: there is a gym, table tennis, aerobics, water aerobics in the outdoor pool, a billiard room, darts, horse riding, futsal.

Visit the steam room or sauna: here you can relieve stress and cleanse the skin, and then experienced massage therapists will make you a wellness massage. You can relax and unwind in the jacuzzi, and in the evening go to the amphitheater or internet cafe. Once every seven days, a concert group arrives at the hotel and holds funny performances.

For business travelers, a conference room with 150 seats has been allocated. At the hotel you can always take advantage of the shuttle service to the city, taxi, laundry / dry cleaning and parking. There is also a medical office, an exchange office, a network of souvenir shops and a beauty salon.

Digest. The budget guesthouse Zouara Hotel 3 has significantly improved the quality of service in recent years. Today it is a hotel with a developed tourist destination, offering tourists good living conditions and multifaceted leisure. This is one of the few three-star complexes operating on an all-inclusive system.

Most tourists were satisfied with their stay at Zouara Hotel 3. Vacation reviews were mostly positive. Everyone liked the comfortable and clean rooms, the professionalism of the staff and the delicious food. At an affordable price a lot of entertainment, entertainment and sports services are available to vacationers.
