The mini-hotel was opened in 2012. The building of this inexpensive hotel in Podolsk (prices for accommodation here are very affordable - from 2000 rubles per day) was erected from logs - environmentally friendly material.

"Jungle"

This is a hotel complex, which is located in the Podolsky district. It is located in a forested area between pines and birches, in the territory of the club "Aivengo". In this place you can enjoy the beauty of nature near Moscow and silence.



The complex offers 6 cozy cottages, of which two are equipped with a private pool with a sauna, as well as 63 comfortable rooms. In the territory of this Podolsk hotel there is a cafe-bar where you can taste delicious dishes from the chef, hold a business meeting or meet friends.

The hotel offers accommodation in 34 rooms of various categories.
