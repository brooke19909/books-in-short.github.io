"Achilleion Park", Kabardinka. Photos and reviews of vacationers

The small village of Kabardinka, located within the boundaries of Gelendzhik, is located on the shore of the Tsemess bay. From the north, it is surrounded by spurs of a mountain range. The area in which Kabardinka is located is considered one of the most comfortable in terms of climatic conditions on the entire Black Sea coast of the Krasnodar Territory.





Rest in Kabardinka

A region with low humidity, the largest possible number of sunny days, a wonderful sea, a long swimming season - all this became the reason that pioneer camps, motels and boarding houses were built here in the Soviet years.

Nowadays, many modern private mini-hotels and large hotels have appeared. Kabardinka, the hotels of which are overwhelmingly located almost in its center, began to actively develop tourist infrastructure. The road to the sea takes only five to ten minutes walk along the city streets.

More and more Russians come here year after year. Many liked Kabardinka. Hotels of the village by tour operators are positioned as suitable for both family and romantic holidays. Tourists who choose this destination for relaxation, can find an option for every taste and prosperity - from inexpensive mini-hotels to luxury apartments.

One of the hotels that has recently gained great popularity among Russians is Achilleion Park (Kabardinka - its location).

general information

This relatively new boutique hotel is located in the suburb of Gelendzhik, in the picturesque resort village. It is managed by a Greek company, whose management is fully consistent with high European standards of guest service. Achilleion Park Hotel (Kabardinka) is open year round. It is also suitable for people with disabilities who can fully relax on its territory.





"Achilleion Park" (Kabardinka) is a boutique hotel, and this status can be seen in everything: the six-story building is designed in a classical style, it is decorated with an atrium and an elegant hall. The hotel is perfect for almost any vacation: it is designed for couples with children, young people and business people. Its distinguishing feature is isolation. Achilleion Park (Kabardinka) is located away from other hotels in this resort, which provides its guests with an atmosphere of exceptional privacy.

Its territory borders on the one hand with colorful houses, and on the other - with an evergreen forest and bearing a cool and fresh mountain river. The wide beach is half a kilometer from the hotel. Nearby are many of the resort's attractions and leisure facilities.

The distance from the hotel to the railway station located in Novorossiysk is 21.1 km, and the airport in Gelendzhik is 15.2 km.





Infrastructure

The elegant hall of the Achilleion Park Hotel (Kabardinka), designed in the best traditions of European design, is lined with luxurious upholstered furniture and decorated with a stylish mini-waterfall with illumination and a decorated aquarium in which goldfish swim. In order to allow guests to spend their leisure time comfortably, a large plasma TV hangs on the wall. The atmosphere of the hall is conducive to a calm measured conversation or to business negotiations taking place over a cup of coffee. The hotel is air-conditioned, everywhere you can connect to wireless Internet.

Those who come to rest in their own vehicle are invited to use the guarded parking lot with a large number of parking spaces. In general, the special atmosphere of such a resort as Gelendzhik, as well as the bewitching surrounding nature, combined with the wide range of services that Achilleion Park (Kabardinka) offers, leave tourists with an unforgettable experience of staying at this hotel. Young people can also have a nice rest here, as numerous entertainment venues for almost every taste are located literally within walking distance - on the embankment of the village.





Considerable attention is paid to the hotel and those guests who combine vacation with work. The presence of an equipped conference room and a spacious restaurant, where you can hold not only gala dinners, but also business negotiations, as well as experienced hotel staff, contribute to a rich and productive business stay.
