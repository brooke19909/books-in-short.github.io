Additional Smart Internet or how to add traffic to MTS subscribers?

Not all unlimited Internet users who are MTS subscribers are within the limit of the tariff plan. As a rule, traffic ends some time before the new billing period. At the same time, some subscribers simply stop using the Internet until a new volume of traffic is provided. But most of them use additional Smart Internet and a number of turbo buttons, which allow to prolong the use of the Internet at a good speed. What are the conditions for connecting additional traffic provided for on some Smart tariff plans? Is it possible to refuse to automatically increase its volume?





Optional Smart Internet - what is it?

The MTS operator provided for the possibility of exhausting the limit of Internet traffic by subscribers before the start of a new billing period and developed the auto-renewal functionality. An additional Internet package is automatically connected as soon as the main volume of megabytes ends according to the tariff plan. It‚Äôs impossible to force a similar service. Automatic activation will occur if the number of the ban is not set and the traffic included in the monthly fee has been completely spent by the client. The option is controlled directly by the subscriber. If you wish, you can simply refuse it.

Additional terms

If an additional package was spent, and the gigabyte volume at the main tariff was not added (that is, a new billing period did not come), then the second such package is activated . At the same time, no more than fifteen such options are allowed per month. Automatically connection occurs only if the amount necessary for connection is present on the client‚Äôs balance.

Also, potential users of this option and those who already use it should be aware that you should not save on additional Internet traffic. In particular, this applies to cases when it is connected one or two days before the next billing period and the provision of the next volume of traffic. This is due to the fact that the balance of the additional package is not carried forward to the next month and all balances are canceled.





The cost of additional Internet

Additional Smart Internet connection is not free. On a number of tariff plans, its cost is 150 rubles. In this case, the package size is one gigabyte. The tariff plans that use these conditions include:

  • TP "Smart Non-Stop";
  • TP "Smart Plus";
  • TP "Smart 092016";
  • TP "Smart Top".

On all other tariffs of the Smart line, the Internet package, which is additionally connected, has a volume of 500 megabytes. Its cost is 75 rubles.
How to connect an additional Smart Internet to the MTS number?

As mentioned earlier, on Smart tariff plans, additional traffic is activated automatically, after the end of the main package according to the tariff plan. The subscriber can refuse such a prolongation and establish an appropriate ban. You can also monitor the status of an additional Internet package, namely the remaining megabytes, via the Internet. To do this, just register on the official resource on the page of your personal web account. For ease of viewing, it is recommended to use a mobile application. Additional Smart Internet will be displayed in the list of activated options and the rest will be displayed on it.





Is it possible to disable the automatic activation of additional Internet?

To turn off the automatic renewal of traffic and, as a result, unplanned write-offs of funds from the balance, it is enough to dial the request on the mobile device: * 111 * 936 #. In response to it, the operator will receive a notification from the operator. After the ban is set, Internet access after the exhaustion of traffic will be possible only when the turbo buttons are activated. You can refuse the ban and restore automatic renewal of traffic by typing the same request.





Conclusion

The additional Smart Internet service at MTS is available on the Smart line tariff plans. Whether to use it or not, the subscriber decides. At any time, you can refuse to automatically activate it whenever the limit on the base tariff comes to an end. You can also increase the number of gigabytes by connecting turbo buttons of various sizes. Unlike the additional Internet, they can be connected not only when the remainder of the main traffic is zero, but also combined with each other (for example, activate a 1 gigabyte turbo button together with a 5 gigabyte package). By the way, the traffic that remains on the connected turbo button is also not transferred to the new month. When a new period begins, all megabytes on the additional Internet and turbo buttons burn out.
