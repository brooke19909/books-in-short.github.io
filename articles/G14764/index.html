Waterpark "Volzhsky Dali", Saratov - photos and reviews

The Volzhsky Dali sanatorium (Saratov) is located on the banks of the legendary Volga River, only fifteen kilometers from the city. The health-improving complex occupies twenty-three hectares of forest land, the base buildings are surrounded by oak forests and stand on the hills, which in the form of a horseshoe surround the buildings. The cleanest air of the forest is stupefying, it draws from the river with freshness and coolness, the body feels pleasant humidity.

Sanatorium: reviews

Tourists who have already been here assure that Volzhsky Dali (Saratov) is a modern health complex, which includes buildings for accommodation with economy and luxury rooms. Inside each building, all the rooms are decorated by specialists, on the walls you can see paintings by famous artists of Saratov. The whole complex will surprise with sophistication and beauty. On the camp site are located: a gym, saunas, baths and much more - each vacationer will find something to his liking.

Entertainment: reviews

Tourists assure that the Volzhsky Dali water park (Saratov) is a great opportunity to relax and unwind from the bustle of the city, to recover from difficult everyday life.

For several years now, a treatment center with water attractions has been operating in the camp site. The building was built in accordance with modern standards and technologies, according to a special project, thanks to which the ventilation and air conditioning system fully supplies healing air, which penetrates the physiotherapy center. Room temperature and humidity are controlled and remain optimal, despite the weather conditions on the street.

The water park is a great attraction, which was created by more than one master. The bowls of the pools are mosaic tiled and illuminated by lamps, fountains in the form of rain divide the water canvas into zones. Tourists say that thanks to the new and powerful treatment facilities, the water at the rides is always clean and clear. There are two Jacuzzi lagoons in the water park that will plunge you into the world of pleasure. Also, visitors will appreciate the underwater volcano, steep slides for adults who love extreme sports. And, of course, it also has rides for young visitors to the Volzhsky Dali water park (Saratov). It offers entertainment for every taste.



The center is equipped with hydromassage baths, which are located directly in the pool at different levels. In addition, there are also showers in which there are ordinary showers and special, equipped with various nozzles, which supplies water from different angles.

For fans of the bath there are two swimming pools for cooling, there is also a separate water bowl for kids up to five years old. In the period from September to May, the water park gives you the opportunity to swim at any time.

In addition to rest, this is a great opportunity to restore your health in case of problems with the musculoskeletal system. A special trainer in physiotherapy exercises will teach you the necessary exercises. Water attractions can be visited not only by the guests of the camp site, but also those who wish to have a good time with their family or friends on the weekend.
