Hotel Lotus Resort 3 *, India, Goa: overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

In this short article we will discuss the hotel Lotus Beach Resort Goa 3 *, reviews about it, its advantages, disadvantages and much more. Ready? Then let's go!

Hotel location

Lotus Resort 3 * is located in Benaulim, in the southern Indian state of Goa. Benaulim is a clean, well-groomed, densely planted village on the shores of the Arabian Sea. At the same time, Benaulim is the name of a beach in South Goa.



The distance to Benaulim beach is about 250 meters, so hotel visitors will need 5 minutes to reach the beach on foot.

The nearest town, Margao, is 5 km away. Margao has the nearest train station.

The capital of Goa is the charming city of Panaji, located 30 km from the Lotus Beach Resort 3 * hotel (Goa). You can get there by bus, for which you need to pay 12 rupees.

Panaji has many cultures of the world. Here you can find a number of beautiful architectural complexes (both historical and modern). Some of the best Indian restaurants are also located in Panaji.

Dabolim Airport is 25 kilometers from the hotel. It accepts both charter and regular international flights operated by various airlines. In addition, you can get to Dabolim, and then to Benaulim, with a change at the Indian airports of Delhi and Mumbai, as well as at airports in Middle Eastern countries, such as Dubai or Doha.

Rooms

The hotel, which belongs to the Lotus Resorts hotel chain, has 48 rooms of various categories (standard, deluxe and suite) located in a beautiful landscaped garden, and offers its visitors so much sought after privacy and comfort along with many amenities.





Bright spacious and clean rooms of Lotus Resort 3 * (South Goa) are ideal for guests to stay and relax, providing a combination of luxury and comfort. The rooms are beautifully decorated, equipped with comfortable double beds and are modernly equipped with all the amenities that make your stay comfortable: air conditioning, refrigerator, cable (satellite) TV.

In addition, the hotel offers a coffee maker, kettle, safe, hairdryer. Each room includes a bathroom, shower.

Guests of all categories of rooms receive a complimentary breakfast during their stay at Lotus Resort 3 *.

Infrastructure, services

The hotel has an outdoor pool, which allows guests to relax during any time of the day.

If desired, clients can have a wonderful time playing table tennis, and their children will find exciting entertainment in the children's play area.

Hotel guests can rent a car. Free parking is available during your stay.

Also at the hotel you will find services such as currency exchange, tour bookings at the tour desk located on site, laundry, concierge, luggage storage.

Lotus Resort 3 * will be happy to offer its guests seminar rooms that can accommodate up to 50 people, 24-hour reception service. Hotel visitors can book a transfer to or from the airport.

Restaurant Sea Wave





Continental and Indian dishes are served on site at Sea Wave Restaurant. It is impossible to spend your holidays here without those delicious dishes that you will be offered in this restaurant.

Your taste buds will feel these dishes even before the plate appears on the table. The hotel boasts a restaurant serving dishes that serve as a gourmet haven. Do not forget to order Tikka chicken dish and Mutter mushroom - this restaurant specializes.

Here, all guests of Lotus Resort 3 * are served every morning breakfast.

Sea Shell Bar

In the perfect atmosphere of this bar you will be offered the best drinks, among which are liquors, cocktails, including non-alcoholic ones. They are sure to cheer you up. At the bar, guests can order snacks to their taste and choice. This is one of the best bars in the Benaulim area.

Sightseeing: Benaulim Beach

Benaulim Beach in South Goa is a place where tourists will find a sense of peace and enjoy a relaxing and comfortable stay. This snow-white sandy beach with a small number of people will allow you to relax and enjoy the surrounding beauty, as well as enjoy the dolphins, which are here swimming quite close to the shore.

The beach extends for about 2 km. It is clean, but there is practically no local population.

Another feature of this beach is the sea bottom is flat and very clean, without shells, without vegetation.

In the bars and restaurants located on the beach, you can taste the dishes, the choice of which is quite wide, at affordable prices.

For those who prefer an active vacation, there are also all the possibilities: you can rent a boat, scooter, bicycle, buggy and arrange yourself fascinating walks both by sea and by land.

According to the guest reviews of Lotus Beach Resort 3 *, Benaulim beach is the most beautiful in Goa, it is endless, and the sea is always different: it is either calm, or breaking into each other in the form of splashes of champagne waves, or huge ridges that can cover with your head.

On the beach you will find souvenir shops, grocery supermarkets.

In the evening you can witness a stunning sunset: the sun sets in the sea and changes the color of the waves, sand, turning them into gold.

In Benaulim, in addition to the wonderful beach, you can visit the Church of St. John the Baptist.





Colva

Colva is the largest and most spectacular beach of South Goa. A beach of white sand, outlined along its coast with coconut palms, stretches for 25 km.

It is 6 km from the resort Lotus Beach Resort 3 *.

Varca Beach

Varca Beach is located about 2 km from Benaulim. It is one of Goa's most popular tourist attractions. It is impossible not to love Varca with its beauty. Here you will find many shakes offering all kinds of snacks, souvenir shops, salons where Indians will make a wonderful relaxing massage.
