The Charm Resort Phuket 4 * (Thailand, Phuket): description, service, reviews

If you want to relax on the beach, while immersed in a completely different culture, then most likely the traveler will decide to go to Thailand. The tourism industry is developing very fast here, every year new hotels and entertainment complexes open. Moreover, tours to Thailand from Moscow or other cities are not so expensive.

It's no secret that for a rich beach holiday it is better to go to the island of Phuket, where the picturesque beaches and the unique culture of local residents are perfectly combined with the comfort of the modern world. There are a lot of hotels here, but often tourists prefer to spend their holidays on the territory of The Charm Resort Phuket 4 * hotel. This place is famous for its excellent service and good living conditions. But before the trip it is worth collecting the maximum of useful information.

Where to look for a hotel? Location Features





When choosing a stopping place, experienced tourists take into account the location. The Charm Resort Phuket 4 * hotel is located in a busy area, next to Patong Beach, which, by the way, is considered one of the most popular holiday destinations in Thailand. Bangla Road, the famous entertainment street with all the cafes, nightclubs and other entertainment, is just a 15-minute walk away.

By the way, there is a bus stop nearby, where you can see the schedule of public buses. Phuket International Airport is about 35 km away. In any case, the road after arrival does not take much time, and tourists are most often provided with a transfer.

Hotel The Charm Resort Phuket 4 *: description of the territory





This is a relatively large hotel complex, which consists of several four-story buildings. The hotel is brand new since it was opened in 2014. The buildings are created and decorated in a modern style - marble floors, spacious waiting rooms, technical equipment, an elevator system and air conditioning create truly comfortable conditions.

The hotel area is large - there are terraces, a huge pool and even your own garden. The whole yard is decorated with greenery and flower beds, the state of which is regularly monitored by employees. Former guests note that it is very clean.

Hotel Room Categories

The Charm Resort Phuket 4 * hotel is quite large. At your service - 246 rooms of the following categories:

  • Spacious Deluxe Rooms (some have direct pool access).
  • Junior Suites, which feature elegant furnishings and large king-size beds (some also have direct access to the pool).
  • Charm Suites with exquisite design and large windows.
  • Family rooms with extra bed. There are two-room rooms with one and two bathrooms, which are ideal for a large family or a group of friends.

Do they provide meals?





On the territory of The Charm Resort Phuket 4 * hotel there is a cafe where guests are offered breakfast. The room here is spacious, with new furniture and an outdoor terrace. Breakfast is served as a buffet. In the reviews, tourists note that the menu is more than varied - visitors are offered toasts, fresh pastries, pancakes, jam, snacks and salads, fresh vegetables, meat, fish and some traditional Thai and Asian dishes. In a word, everyone can have breakfast here, regardless of culinary preferences.

There is a bar by the pool where guests are offered alcoholic drinks, cocktails, desserts and light snacks. The prices here are moderate, therefore there are always enough visitors. By the way, just a few steps away are cafes and restaurants for every taste, whether it is a cozy half-empty diner or a luxurious rooftop restaurant. Prices, like, in fact, everywhere in Thailand, are pleasantly pleasing.

Holidays on the coast: what to count on?





Holidays by the sea - the desire of many travelers. What does the Charm Resort Phuket 4 * hotel offer? Patong Beach is just 100 meters away. This is a public and widely known place, so there are always many tourists here.

Nevertheless, travelers respond positively about this place - the sand is very clean, the beach is cleaned constantly, and the waves do not rise so often, which, however, does not interfere with fun for fans of surfing and sailing.

You will have the opportunity to rent a sunbed or an umbrella, ride a boat or a catamaran, take a boat trip, get a lot of vivid impressions from riding a speed boat or water skiing. There are bars and cafes on the shore where you can eat while enjoying the incredible scenery. Windsurfing and scuba diving instructors also work here. Tourists often organize volleyball competitions, as well as parties and shows. Nobody has to miss the beach.

Basic services on site





Hotel guests are provided with everything necessary for a cloudless, pleasant pastime. There is always one of the employees at the reception, and if possible, guests will be populated even earlier than specified in the official rules.

The administrator, upon request, will provide you with his own safe deposit box, which is located in the video surveillance zone. There is a luggage storage room, and the money at the hotel can be exchanged. Laundry and dry cleaning services are available. In the courtyard there is free parking, and in the hotel (or in the city) you can rent a vehicle for a small fee, whether it be a bicycle or a car.

It is worth saying that in one of the buildings there is a large banquet hall. Sometimes it is used for conferences and seminars, but often the territory of the hotel becomes the wedding venue for Thais or foreign tourists. Workers are happy to organize holiday events, and quite successfully deal with this.

Leisure of tourists: what to do in the hotel area?

Travelers come to Patong, wanting to spend days on the beach, and in the evenings to relax in one of the establishments of Bangla Road. Most tourists need a hotel only for a comfortable overnight stay. But if necessary, you can have a good time on its territory. A huge swimming pool with clean fresh water crosses the entire courtyard. Solarium terraces with comfortable sunbeds and mattresses are also equipped here - guests sunbathe, swim, have fun and make new acquaintances.





At any time, guests of the hotel can use the sauna or relax in the jacuzzi, enjoying the fresh air and tropical landscapes. There is also a fitness club. Group classes are held here only if there are a sufficient number of people who want to, but in any case you can work out in the gym, and for a small fee you also get the advice of an experienced instructor.

Do not forget that Phuket is a true paradise island with a huge number of attractions. Excursion agencies work in the hotel and next to it, where each tourist can pick up an interesting tour personally for himself.

Are there any services for children?

Children need rest and new experiences no less than their parents, not to mention the healing sea air. Thailand, Phuket, Patong Beach - a good place for a family vacation. The city has enough attractions and entertainments that will be interesting to children, and the guys have a great time on the beach, splashing in warm, clear water.

As for the amenities at The Charm Resort Phuket 4 * hotel, there are spacious family rooms. The rooms are clean, the territory is spacious enough for children's games, and in the restaurant you can pick up simple and healthy dishes for the child. But there are no special entertainments for children here, alas.

Reviews and impressions of tourists

In preparation for the trip, it is important to find out not only official information, but also the opinions of tourists who have already managed to visit the hotel The Charm Resort Phuket 4 *. People leave positive reviews. The hotel really boasts a modern design and a high level of comfort. The rooms have the necessary equipment, and the furniture is new. Cleanliness in the rooms (and in all buildings) is maintained - the maids are cleaned daily.

Guests are also praised by the local restaurant - the dishes here are fresh and tasty, and there is always a choice of food. The location, by the way, is also convenient, because in a few minutes you can reach a cozy cafe, beach, a large market and a shopping center. Tourists leave a special thanks to the staff - they are always smiling and attentive, help solve any problem and really make you feel welcome. In addition, tours to Thailand from Moscow are not so expensive.
