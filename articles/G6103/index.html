How on Beeline to check subscriptions and disable them?

How to check the subscriptions on Beeline? This question is faced by people for various reasons: some customers began to lose money from the account, others only recently purchased a SIM card and want to check for paid options, etc. In order to find out if there are any additional services involving a monthly fee, you can use one of the following methods.





How to check subscriptions on Beeline yourself?

The subscriber can receive data on which options and subscriptions are activated on the number in any way convenient for him:

  1. Through the Internet, through the "My Account".
  2. By phone.
  3. By launching a special application for mobile devices.
  4. Through the autoinformation system.

In addition, there is always the option to contact the customer service (by number 0611) and check with specialists about the availability of additional services, options or subscriptions.





How to check Beeline subscriptions from your phone by entering a request?

One of the most common options that can help the user get the necessary data is to enter a USSD request. The advantage of this method is the fact that the subscriber, along with the name of the additional services, will be sent information on their disconnection.

In response to the request * 110 * 09 #, which you need to enter a mobile phone, an SMS message will be sent to him in a few minutes. In it you can familiarize yourself with the list of subscriptions and find out how they are deactivated. There is no universal team that allows you to immediately refuse all paid services and options. And this means that disabling each subscription is carried out individually.





How will the internet help?

How to check connected Beeline subscriptions via the World Wide Web. Through the Internet, you can get information on the number in two ways:

  • Through the "Personal Account" of the subscriber. It is available on the official website of the Beeline company. For authorization, you must specify the phone number and temporary password that will be sent to it.
  • Through the mobile app. You can download it through a specialized store with applications for devices (Playmarket, AppStore).

Receiving information through the voice menu

Another option for clarifying information on subscriptions connected to the number is the voice menu (AVR). Having dialed the customer service number from the phone regarding which you need to receive data, you need to follow the prompts of the autoinformation system. other methods

You can check paid Beeline subscriptions by ordering the "Account Detailing" service. The document will show all the expenses of the subscriber for the specified period, including debiting the fee for existing subscriptions and services. You can use this method if the client notices the regular, unreasonable disappearance of money from the account. It should be remembered that detailing is provided on a paid basis. Its cost will depend on the length of the period for which the necessary data.

Conclusion

In order not to be tormented by the question of how to check subscriptions on Beeline, you should carefully pay attention to various offers of services and options. Most of these newsletters are activated by the subscriber independently: knowingly or accidentally. Be that as it may, it is necessary to carefully consider the issue of connecting additional options and clarify their cost.

Some of these subscriptions may be free for a certain period, after which a monthly fee will appear. Similar recommendations can be given to subscribers of other mobile operators. After all, they also offer a number of different newsletters, as on Beeline. You can also check subscriptions through the customer service, using the Internet or the appropriate commands.
