How to photograph a beginner photographer with a DSLR?





If you decide to start your career as a photographer right away with a professional camera, then be prepared to meet a number of difficulties and obstacles in your path.

The first thing worth mentioning is that the camera itself will not give you the perfect picture in terms of composition and lighting. Therefore, in order to get good shots, try to follow a few rules.

Horizon rules

The SLR camera is your window into the real world, a semblance of your view and understanding of the world. Make sure that the horizon is not cluttered in your shots. Fashion skewed space has long gone out of fashion. Look at the street - you see all the objects directly, all the streets are located exclusively horizontally, and the columns - vertically. It should also be in your picture, if it's hard for you, then in the viewfinder, focus on straight lines, this will help you a lot at the very beginning of your journey.





Also emphasize the rule of the golden ratio. Mentally divide your horizons into 9 identical rectangles (by glancing at three vertical and three horizontal lines). After that, select the extreme points of the rectangle located in the center. Done? Excellent! The thing is that these four points (conditionally, of course) are for our eyes the most favorable and convenient for perception. Therefore, when you take pictures, pay more attention to them, this will help you a lot.

Manual settings on the SLR camera

SLR cameras are primarily distinguished by the fact that they provide the owner with the opportunity to fully build their future picture, starting from the light and ending with the focal point.

If you have never worked with SLR cameras in person before, we strongly advise you to start by reading as many tutorials and watching video tutorials as possible. At the same time, pay more attention to such details as:
- aperture;

- exposure;

- focusing;

- zoom.

All these values ‚Äã‚Äãcan be adjusted even in the simplest and most inexpensive SLR camera; the size of these parameters is one of the main components of the price of photographic equipment.

Composition and proper frame construction





In the end, I want to talk about how to properly configure the parameters of your camera - that's not all. How to photograph with a SLR camera depends on the correct construction of the frame. To understand this issue well, read about the types of composition (closed, open, and so on).
