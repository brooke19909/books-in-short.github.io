Hotel Royal Court Hotel (Prague / Czech Republic): photos and reviews of tourists

Our article will be useful to those who are planning a trip to Prague in the near future and are looking for a suitable hotel where they can stay. Of course, any tourist is interested in finding a good hotel for relaxation. We suggest considering as one of the options Hotel Royal Court Hotel (Prague), located in the heart of the New Town.

A bit about the hotel

Royal Court Hotel is a 3-star hotel that can become a real home for you during your stay in beautiful Prague. The hotel has a rather convenient location not only in terms of proximity to the main interesting places of the city, but also in terms of transport interchange. Just a five-minute walk from the complex is a metro station. A walk to the famous Wenceslas Square takes no more than fifteen minutes. Ruzyne International Airport, which attracts all tourists, is 16 kilometers from the hotel. Hotel Royal Court Hotel (Prague) is located in the former Royal Court of the 19th century.

Number of rooms

Hotel Royal Court Hotel (Prague) offers its guests accommodation in 45 comfortable rooms. The complex has two categories of apartments: superior and standard.

Standard rooms have one large double bed or two single beds. Guests planning to stay at the hotel with a baby up to two years old can notify the hotel in advance by filling out a special document on the website, and then a cot is provided for the child free of charge when settling.

Absolutely in all apartments, guests can use a modern hydromassage shower, safe, hairdryer, plasma TV. The hotel has satellite TV. The rooms have air conditioning and heating, an electric kettle.

Superior apartments are superior, more spacious rooms, which have a spacious comfortable bathroom instead of a shower (slippers and a bathrobe).



In a separate building next to the hotel there are apartments designed for groups of tourists, couples and entrepreneurs. There are studio and family rooms, in which there is not only a bedroom, but also a living room. In addition, these apartments are also equipped with a modern bathroom and kitchen. The studio rooms have a large double bed and a sofa bed as an extra bed. It is also possible to order extra beds, but for their use you need to pay 10 euros per day.

The family apartments can accommodate six guests. These rooms have a double bed and a sofa in the living room. In addition, extra beds can be accommodated in the living room.

All apartments have wireless internet access, which is included in the price. The hotel accepts guests with pets, but a preliminary request must be made. For a stay at the hotel for one pet a fee of 10 euros per day will be charged.

Dining at the hotel

In the Royal Court Hotel 4 * (Czech Republic, Prague) in the morning guests of the complex receive breakfast in the restaurant, the cost of which is included in the stay. The buffet serves a variety of European dishes. The restaurant welcomes guests for breakfast from seven in the morning until half past ten.



For lunch and dinner, guests can order any menu items. Due to its convenient location, guests can also visit the many cafes and restaurants located nearby. As we already mentioned, some categories of rooms are equipped with their own kitchens, which gives tourists the opportunity to cook their own meals. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are several large supermarkets where you can purchase everything you need.

Hotel infrastructure

Check-in at the Royal Court Hotel 4 * (Czech Republic, Prague) takes place at two in the afternoon, but guests must leave before twelve in the morning. All hotel staff are fluent in English, communication in Russian is also possible.

The hotel has parking spaces on the street and in the garage. If you plan to visit Prague by car, please make a request in advance at the hotel so that a place is reserved for your car. In this case, you must provide information about the brand of the machine and its parameters. All this must be indicated in a special form on the hotel website. Parking is charged at EUR 15 per day.



The hotel does not have its own car rental point, but at the reception, tourists are provided with information on where you can rent a car nearby. There is also a travel agency that can offer guests all kinds of sightseeing trips around the Czech capital.

Spa

The Royal Court Hotel 4 * (Prague) has its own spa, decorated in Egyptian style with a predominance of brown and golden tones. Here you can visit different types of massage (cleansing, regenerating, aroma massage), relax in the relaxation area with a cup of tea, surrounded by many candles. In addition, tourists can visit the Finnish sauna and jacuzzi. To visit the spa, you must pre-register at the reception.

Masseuses from Thailand work at the salon. In addition to body massage, they can offer their visitors foot massage by using biologically active points. The hallmark of the institution are aromatherapy sessions conducted using a special bag filled with herbs.
