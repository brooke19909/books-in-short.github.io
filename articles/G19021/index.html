Iriver player: instruction and reviews

Iriver models in the compact audio player segment have always occupied a separate niche. While most competing manufacturers strictly adhered to certain standards of the modern gadget, South Korean specialists mastered the new ergonomics and design possibilities, often deviating from established rules. Actually, to this day such a principle of experimental development brings success in the market, and equally bright disappointment among fans - depending on the model. One way or another, but there are invariable principles by which the Iriver player is developed in almost all families. This is the desire to achieve wide functionality, energy efficiency and high-quality sound. Of course, only the user who is well acquainted with the nuances of its operation can fully use the player‚Äôs pledged resources.





Device controls

Among the main elements due to the operation of players of this brand, one can distinguish a headphone jack, a loop for a cord, a connector for a charger, a port for line-in, etc. Of particular note is the presence of the USB port, which acquired the device in recent versions. This is a multifunctional means of communication with the gadget through which you can charge it and exchange information. Also on the outside of the case, the Iriver player is provided with a Reset button, which allows you to reset the settings. In the latest modifications, there is the possibility of integrating memory modules of modern formats up to Micro SD. For direct control of the sound characteristics, volume keys are provided, and the use of a digital media player is usually realized through the touch panel, although in the oldest versions these tasks are solved by mechanical buttons.

Charger Instructions





In most modern versions of Iriver, recharging is implemented through the mentioned USB channel, although there are exceptions. In all cases, the player itself is provided with an internal battery, which can be completely restored in 3-4 hours, depending on capacity and model. It is important to note that when connected to a PC in standby mode, energy will not be filled. In order for the Iriver player to start charging, computer activity is required. It should also be borne in mind that models with USB in addition are equipped with modern energy-saving technology. This means that by programming the operation of the device through a timer, it is possible to optimize the waste of battery power. In practice, this is done, for example, by setting the model to turn off the backlight after a few seconds or minutes. Of course, music playback does not turn off, although the interval of the player itself can be configured in a similar way.

Iriver Software Instructions





Again, in later versions of the player, the company provided the possibility of ergonomic use of communication between the gadget and the computer. It is implemented using the Iriver plus 4 system, which is a software package with the basic functions of a media manager. First of all, the user must initialize the program and start its installation. Next, the music files present in the player will be automatically read. If necessary, they will be sent to the manager‚Äôs library each time they connect to the computer. As the instruction notes, the Iriver player in this part is conveniently controlled by the Transfer button, which allows you to move files between systems. In addition, you can fill the library through third-party sources, for example, by extracting tracks in CD-ROMs.

Additional functionality management





The use of auxiliary capabilities is carried out through standard player controls. The user may be offered different functions depending on the modification. So, to start working with an additional option, the settings window is usually displayed, and then a specific function is corrected. In particular, familiarization modes or a playback mode can be controlled. As for the settings itself, the Iriver player in this way allows you to control the equalizer parameters, adjust the sound characteristics, operate with stereo effects, etc.

Video instruction

The ability to display visual information is basic for many models of the line. Usually, through the main menu, the user can select a list of available clips and give a command to play one of them. The standard controls of the digital player are also enumerated between lists and folders. An example of such an interface is the Iriver E300 player, in which a multi-level list system is conveniently organized. By the way, the maximum video playback time in this model with a liquid crystal screen is 8 hours.





Positive model reviews

It is worth noting that positive reviews about Iriver products are often interrelated with the model belonging to a particular segment. In this case, the rule applies - the closer the version to the premium level, the better it manifests itself in the hands of users. One of the reference models of this type is the Iriver S100 player, which is praised for its high sound quality, reliable assembly, ergonomics of the interface and attractive design. But again, this is far from the most affordable option and the price tag. This also includes reviews of the E300 model, which has successfully combined both new technologies and previous branded developments.

Negative reviews

A reverse example of the attitude of users to the products of this company are reviews on the T7 modification. Although the model was conceived as a simple budget option for a convenient and versatile player, users did not appreciate the efforts of the developers. Hoarse sound, modest equipment, low battery power - and this is only part of the responses that often receive budget Iriver players. Reviews also indicate a lack of pronounced designer shades that are present in higher-level devices. However, some claims apply to almost all representatives of the line - for example, the low quality of the complete headphones and the locking system, requiring a double press of a button with a pause delay, can be called.





Conclusion

With all the shortcomings, the manufacturer stubbornly competes on the same platform with Sony, Philips, Cowon and other giants of the market. An example of a successful development of the company is the Iriver E300 player. The instructions for a small device are segmented into several sections, a considerable part of which is an enumeration of functions. This document clearly shows how great the technological potential of modern Iriver devices is. This applies to ergonomics, and to the sound quality, which in many ways allows South Korean specialists to be in demand in this segment. Of course, not every device displays the best quality products, but it is important to consider the price balance. Still, the same flagship players are often cheaper than analogues from other major manufacturers.
