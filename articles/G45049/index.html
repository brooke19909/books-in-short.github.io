Popular beaches of Nizhny Novgorod

If you are planning a vacation to Nizhny Novgorod, it would be nice to plan your leisure time in advance. At the same time, it is important not only to decide where you will spend the evenings, but also to organize a day's rest, for example, to find out which beaches of Nizhny Novgorod deserve attention. So, for example, out of 20 officially open bathing places in 2013, only 6 of them were equipped with showers. But many are interested just to take a tour of the beaches, because some of them are located on the Volga and Oka rivers , and the rest are on the lakes.

Officially, all the beaches of Nizhny Novgorod open on June 1, from that day they are in full readiness and can accept all vacationers.
