DIY polymer clay necklace

The ability to make a necklace with your own hands is always a creative, interesting and very exciting process. Such jewelry will emphasize the individuality of its mistress much more than what was bought in the store. Needlework involved girls and women of many generations. However, today to create a wide variety of jewelry, you can even use the latest technological progress. More and more active for decorative elements, including jewelry, apply a material called "polymer clay". In the article, we will consider what it is and how to make a necklace from it.





Polymer clay

This substance is similar to the clay that everyone is used to seeing, and some use it. It is sold in handicraft stores in different colors. Everything is molded from it.

After molding, the resulting product is baked in the oven. It is usually enough to hold it for 20 to 25 minutes at a temperature of 110 degrees. But on each package, manufacturers write the exact instructions that should be followed. They may differ slightly from each other.

After heat treatment, the product hardens, does not stick to hands, does not stain anything and does not change shape anymore. Very often jewelry is made of polymer clay. Here the field for imagination is unlimited.

Miracles from polymer clay

Such a necklace, made with your own hands, can imitate, for example, natural stones, beads or bones. In addition to the form, various color schemes with polymer clay are possible. Moreover, you can use both the finished color of the material, and add various shades, midtones, overflows and more. To create the desired color, paints specially designed for working with clay are used. However, many needlewomen use ordinary oil paints, acrylic and others. Even eye shadow sometimes enters the creation process. And all this plastic polymer clay transfers easily, acquiring the desired shade. Below we consider an example of how to create one beautiful necklace, schemes to which will be useful in the future in the manufacture of roses.





What is necessary for work

For the necklace you will need the following consumables and tools:

  1. Polymer clay of 3 colors (white, brown and beige).
  2. Liquid polymer clay with a brush.
  3. The stack at the end of which is the ball.
  4. Acrylic rolling pin.
  5. Needle.
  6. Special blade.
  7. Cutter (flower shape).
  8. Rondels and beads.
  9. Fishing line.
  10. Extension cord and lock.
  11. Pins.
  12. A wire no more than 1.5 millimeters in diameter.
  13. Scissors.
  14. Round pliers.
  15. Quick-drying glue.

Having prepared all the materials, you can begin to work.





Master class: do-it-yourself necklace

Clay of brown and beige shade is taken. Triangles are formed from both pieces and connected so that a rectangle is formed. In this case, beige is taken twice as much as brown. Clay is rolled out with an acrylic rolling pin, each time folding the layer in half. Gradually, an increasingly smooth transition from one color to another will turn out.

Having received the desired shade, the oval layer is cut transversely into many strips of approximately the same width (from 2 to 4 centimeters). After that, the strips are stacked. In this case, the second plate is placed one centimeter forward, and the third one a centimeter back and then in the desired order.

At the resulting pile, the ends are cut to give an even shape. This is most conveniently done with a special blade. A cylinder is carefully formed from the stack with his hands. Then it is cut with a blade into small plates. They will form petals. Moreover, the thinner the plate, the smaller the diameter of the future petal. Slices stretch and thin with fingers first. Then the corrugated blade can get the serrated edges of the future petal. And after that, laying on the palm of your hand, with the help of the stack it is pulled, giving the desired deepening.

First, the bottom row of five pieces is formed. The beige color should be at the tip of the petal, and brown at its base. The remaining rows form an overlap.

Our handmade necklace will consist of five roses: three large and two smaller ones. The center of the flowers is smeared with plastic. Then, having formed from clay several white balls with a diameter of 3 to 5 millimeters, pour them directly into the core.





Necklace assembly

On a wire, on one side of which a loop is made using round-nose pliers, beads, rondels, then roses and then beads with rondels are cooked alternately. After that, the ends are fixed. For reliability, you can also saturate the right place with glue. On one side and the other, a pin with a hat is inserted, the remaining shaft is removed with pliers and a loop is twisted.

In addition, it is desirable to additionally fix the flowers with a satin ribbon. The loops from the threads are connected to the loops of the wire frame. At the end of the work, a lock with an extension cord is attached.

That's it, the necklace of beads and roses made of polymer clay is done.
