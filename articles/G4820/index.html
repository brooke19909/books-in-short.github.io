How to make a wig for a doll without high costs

Jointed dolls (BJD) have the ability to change parts of the body, including wigs. Thanks to this, you can make your original image. But this hobby is not the cheapest. The cost of materials is high, but you can always find an inexpensive alternative to this. From our article you will learn how to make a doll wig with your own hands and inexpensively.





Materials for a wig

To make a wig, you will need the following materials:

  • silk tape or a piece of curtain fabric;
  • thread and needle;
  • bag or cling film;
  • scissors;
  • pencil;
  • brush;
  • PVA glue;
  • cotton fabric.

A meter of tress for doll wigs costs from 150 rubles. If you have a Barbie-sized doll, then this is not a problem. But if your doll is more than 70 cm tall and the head of the toy is large, you will need a lot of curls, which means there will be serious costs.

Instead, you can use the tape - loosen it and straighten it, or gently curl the strings, then you will get beautiful strands. In addition, curtain fabric can be dissolved. It is shiny and smooth, making the wig beautiful.

There are many options on how to make a wig at home for a doll without high costs. Holders for curtains will be used in this article . They are woven from smooth threads, and it is easy and quick to dissolve them.

Preparation for work

Wrap the top of the doll with cling film or bag. Better film, as it will sit tight and will not slip. Try to pull it properly on your head so that there are no wrinkles.





Prepare a bowl or bucket of water to wash the glue from the brush and hands. When creating a wig at each stage, your hands must be clean. This is a painstaking job. Any negligence can affect the quality of the wig and its appearance. How to make a wig is easy to understand, the main thing is to learn how to make it carefully.

Making a wig cap

Apply glue to the head with a brush.





Wrap it in flaps of fabric in a circle. Accept the edges.





Put another piece of fabric on top, cut its edges so that it is convenient to press them to the head.





After you have well nibbled the pieces of fabric to the doll‚Äôs head, make sure that they will not puff and peel off. Now wait for it to dry.





After the hat has dried, draw a pencil outline of its edges and cut. The contour should extend above the forehead, above the ears and be slightly above the neck. What should eventually turn out, you can see in the photo in the article.





Preparation of strands (tresses)

Now start dissolving the material that you use into strings and curls. Advice on how to make a wig so that it does not cause inconvenience: use a large durable needle or a small hook.





After the strands were unraveled, you need to start to connect them into tress. Just take one long thread and begin to tie strands to it.





Comb the tresses with a comb with rare cloves. If it is difficult to comb, moisten your hair with water and even apply a balm.





Straighten the strands using a regular iron, but it‚Äôs more convenient to do it with a curling iron, and it will turn out better. And if you want to curl them, then dampen them, wrap the handles on the rod and pass them with an iron or curling iron, or place them near the battery and wait for drying. However, the temperature from the curling iron or iron will be more effective.

Sewing tresses to a hat

Strands can also be attached with glue. But if you are not sure of your accuracy, it is better not to use it, otherwise all the locks will be dirty with it, and it will be almost impossible to clean them. In addition, preparing tresses is the most painstaking work. It will be a shame if everything goes down the drain.

Mark with a pencil on the cap circles, focusing on which you will fasten the strands. Do this if you are doing it for the first time. Otherwise, it will turn out crooked, and you will not understand: is it possible to fix it and how. Making a wig is not so simple in practice, but the process is easy to remember.





Fasten the knot of thread at one of the edges of the cap in front. Make sure the knot is strong enough. Start sewing one tress in a circle until you reach the opposite edge. Do not make the thread too long to be enough for the entire work phase. In any case, in the process she will get tangled in her hair. Unnecessary nodules appear. Many puppeteers note that making doll wigs requires a lot of patience and great accuracy.





Here is a hat completely lined with strands. But you have not yet fully learned how to make a wig. He at this stage does not look attractive enough, and there is no parting.





Take another piece of tress. The thread to which the strands were attached will be parted. Place it on the head of the toy as you like and sew it on. Guide the needle precisely along the parting. Better do the point seams.

Trim your hair at the end of the job. If they stick out, moisten them, rinse with plastic wrap and rubber bands and leave overnight. After that, they will be more obedient.





How to make a wig yourself: a few more tips

If you want to make a wig of an unusual shape, it is better to prepare a sketch in advance and think over the course of work. To make an unusual wig from the material that was proposed in this article will not work, especially if it is small in size. Therefore, separately make braids, buns, jewelry and stick or sew them to the wig. It is better not to use hot glue, as it stretches strings that can get stuck in strands. Cleaning it without damaging the wig will be extremely difficult.
