disadvantages

However, in spite of all the advantages of a vacation at Drakino, the park hotel has some minor flaws in terms of organizing a vacation.

  • Insects. In the event that you are going to visit the resort in the summer, be prepared for a large number of mosquitoes. Since the settlement is located in a pine forest, and visitors are offered many activities organized outdoors, you should stock up on insect repellents.
  • Additional paid services. Access to some services is only available at an additional cost. So, you can use several types of bathtubs, massages and a gym.




Climate

Due to the fact that the location of the recreation center is Serpukhov district, the Drakino park has become an ideal place for vacations. The climate is temperate continental, which indicates comfortable temperatures throughout all seasons. Warm, non-hot summers and ordinary cold winters make vacations especially attractive: tourists will not have to suffer from sudden changes in temperature, as well as bad weather conditions.

Unfortunately, the Serpukhov district cannot boast of a very clean environment. Of course, compared to large cities, the air here is much cleaner and ideal for restoring health. However, in Serpukhov built about 10 chemical factories operating in an active mode.

Unlike the city center, in the vicinity of this factor is not felt much. It is known that the only nature reserve in the Moscow region was built in the region.

Calculating the weather, you should focus on the Moscow forecast - changes in the suburbs will be minimal.





sights

In the event that you do not have enough events organized in the park, you can easily visit the interesting places that the city of Serpukhov can offer guests. Drakino (Park Hotel) is just a 10-minute drive from the city, so you will quickly get to the village.

The park is located near the city, which is a huge plus for the tourist. Among the attractions there are many churches, temples and cathedrals, some of them are of federal importance. In addition to buildings of this type, the city is famous for its ancient and original architecture, which has historical value. On small streets you will see many monuments dedicated to various historical events and people.

Nevertheless, one of the most striking sights is the Prioksko-Terrasny Nature Reserve, the only one in the entire Moscow region. The forest islet has almost 1000 plant species. Despite its small area, 142 species of birds and 57 species of mammals live in it.





Holidays in different seasons

Depending on the time of year, the Drakino park, which is only positive reviews, offers its visitors various types of outdoor activities.

  • Summer. In addition to the rope town and some other camps with active events, the Drakino park offers an equipped beach on the banks of the Oka. There you can not only enjoy the warm waters of the river, but also for an additional fee ride some attractions: a catamaran, slides and boats. The beach itself is well equipped for swimming: there are changing cabins, showers, awnings, special places for barbecue. Fishing enthusiasts are also lucky: they will be able to enjoy fishing in specially equipped places for this.
  • Autumn, spring. Thanks to comfortable temperature conditions, these seasons are perfect for outdoor activities. Children are offered a rope town, camps. Adults can visit horse clubs and ride horses, ride ATVs and go-karts. You can also include gameplay in your leisure time: paintball is perfect for this.
  • Winter. Unlike many competitors, the Drakino park is receiving more and more positive reviews. This is not surprising, because the park staff are ready to receive visitors at any time of the year! That is why in winter for tourists, special sledding, snowmobiling, and a music skating rink were organized.

Cost

An important issue is the cost of the trip. It is worth noting that this vacation belongs to the category of budget, so a lot of money for the organization will not go away. Much depends on the cost of the ticket to the suburbs.

Drakino Park, the price of accommodation in which depends on the number of included services, offers several packages for visitors.

  • "Festive". The resort often organizes various discounts and promotions depending on the upcoming holiday. So, when booking rooms in advance, you will receive a discount of 5 to 20%. In addition, during the holidays there are more events and concerts.
  • "Normal". With two meals a night, the room will cost from 4 to 6 thousand, depending on the number of people and the type of room.
  • "All inclusive". When you purchase this package of services, you are given the opportunity of three meals a day on the buffet system, visiting all the procedures and services of the spa hotel, as well as almost all attractions and clubs. The extra charge includes only small services: paintball and pony rides, snowmobiles and ATVs.

In addition, the type of room affects the cost. The average cost of living in a single room (for one night) is 6,000 rubles, for a double room is 8,000. Apartment rooms cost from 13 thousand.

In each of the rooms you can use a TV, a bathroom, a refrigerator.

Drakino Park, which is only positive reviews, makes the most advantageous offers for its guests!

  • Charge all electronic devices and put them in a nearby pocket. Quick access to you should be a phone, documents, money and tickets.
  • Check the weather for the near future and choose the most comfortable clothes. Do not forget that you have to spend a lot of time in the fresh air - clothes should be as comfortable as possible.
  • Prepare a rest program in advance. Despite the fact that various events are held in the park every day, you still have the opportunity to visit the nearby city and get acquainted with its attractions. Examine the information about them, make a list of interesting places and follow it.
  • Calculate your budget carefully. You should not spend all the money in the early days: you should be familiar with the local shops and the goods presented in them. By visiting several of them and comparing prices, you will choose the most appropriate purchase option. You can also take advantage of the many services provided in the park.
  • Book rooms. Even if there are still free rooms at the time of departure, it is worth warning and booking a room in advance. And in the case of early booking you can provide a discount. This is especially true closer to various holidays and major events.
