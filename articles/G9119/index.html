Fetal Doppler - an indispensable tool for every expectant mother



Fetal doppler is a special medical device that is specifically designed for use in the field of gynecology and obstetrics with the goal of an objective and constant assessment of the general condition of the fetus. This device can be used both in maternity hospitals, antenatal clinics and specialized clinics, as well as at home. In this case, a fetal doppler will allow you to hear the fetal heartbeat, starting from the twelfth week of his life. In addition, it will provide an opportunity in real time to monitor the condition of the unborn child and mother, registering all the necessary indicators and professionally assessing the functional capabilities of the respiratory system.

Features of the doppler device

Outwardly, a fetal doppler, reviews of which are only the most positive, is usually a small-sized device equipped with several sensors and an ultrasonic probe. The configuration of the device can be very different, and the range of the device‚Äôs functionality will depend on it.





To date, on sale you can find both very simple models designed to record only the fetal heartbeat, as well as rather sophisticated multifunctional devices that can record a wide variety of parameters that reflect the state of vital activity of a woman and a child. For example, a fetal doppler can capture blood oxygenation, respiratory rate, blood pressure, and heart muscle contractions. In this case, the obtained data can be displayed on the screen in the so-called "real time" mode or in the form of averaged values. Separately, it should be said about the distinguishing feature of the latest generation of devices, which consists in their compatibility with digital systems and allowing you to listen to and record ripple on a removable digital media.

Product Benefits

Among the main advantages of such a device as a fetal doppler, first of all, it should be noted its convenience and ease of use. Most models run on standard batteries, which allows you to take the necessary readings under any conditions. This makes it possible to effectively prevent a wide variety of pathologies.

In addition, modern high-quality models allow you to capture incoming pulses with the highest possible accuracy, thereby increasing the guarantee of the safety of the health of the child and mother. It is also worth mentioning the possibility of controlling the speaker volume and the presence of a recording function, the duration of which can be about twenty minutes. Among other things, it should be noted a high degree of safety of such devices for both the future baby and mother.

Doppler Functionality

As for the functional characteristics of this type of medical device, experts primarily talk about its high sensitivity and the ability to receive a signal without interference. In addition, one cannot fail to note the simplicity of operation, the compactness of the device and its lightness. Also in the list of functional characteristics, you can include a separate speaker that performs the function of protecting the sensor, and a cable for connecting to a computer, which quite often comes with the device.

Major manufacturing companies

You can buy this device today in almost any company selling professional medical equipment. Among the main manufacturers at present, the companies Bestman and Bistos can be distinguished. In addition, he has a high level of sensitivity, allowing you to conduct the necessary monitoring from the first week of pregnancy.
