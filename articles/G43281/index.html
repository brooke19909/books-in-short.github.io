Sights of Kirovo-Chepetsk: list, description and interesting facts

Kirovo-Chepetsk is a small provincial town, the administrative center of the Kirov region with a population of just over 70,000 people. If you visit Kirov as part of a business trip, come to visit friends or relatives, be sure to go to Kirovo-Chepetsk, wander the streets of the city and visit its main iconic places. The best sights of Kirovo-Chepetsk - this is what we will talk about in this material. We will consider only the most interesting places and monuments that are of particular value to the residents of the city.





Stella at the entrance to the city

What does Kirovo-Chepetsk start for all guests? Of course, from the stella decorating the entrance to the city from Kirov. The main attractions always start from this place. Stella appeared here in 1990 and today is a full-fledged symbol of the city. The construction, 20 meters high, is complemented by a pair holding a shield with chemical signs and formulas. The authors of the project decided to show all guests of the city that the city is the birthplace of young and promising chemists.

Many people call this attraction of Kirovo-Chepetsk the patroness of the city, its main protector, many legends and beliefs are associated with this place.





Temple on Vyatka Embankment

The large red brick temple, founded in 1989, is a matter of pride for all local residents of Kirovo-Chepetsk. The sights of the city begin with this iconic and very popular place. For the construction of the temple, a picturesque place was chosen, surrounded by greenery. From the hill on which the temple is located, a breathtaking, according to many, breathtaking view of the Vyatka River opens .

In the early 90s, a Sunday school was opened at the temple, which, like the temple itself, is still operating today.

Church of Grace

Surely you are also interested in very unusual sights of Kirovo-Chepetsk. Description of the Church The grace of evangelical Christians-Baptists distinguishes it from the entire architectural ensemble of the city. Features of beliefs cardinally distinguish this temple from all that are in Kirovo-Chepetsk and other cities of the country. The building is in the best traditions of postmodernity: red brick, huge windows of a fanciful shape.





The building of the factory "Absolute"

It would seem that what else could Kirovo-Chepetsk offer its guests? The history of the city, attractions closely related to it, of course, interest tourists. But what if not industrial enterprises can get to know the city and the way of life in it more closely? Be sure to visit another quite interesting place in the city - the building of the Absolut meat processing plant. The building belongs to the late Gothic style, it is strikingly different from all objects of urban architecture.

The building is remote from the city center, get to it long enough, but you can rest assured: it's worth it. The building is very interesting, amazing photos are taken against its background. In addition, the factory operates a company store where all guests and tourists can buy delicious sausages - an excellent souvenir.

Chicken Leg House

Do you think that only such a house exists in a fairy tale? Then be sure to visit the main and most famous sights of Kirovo-Chepetsk. Their description and reviews will definitely send you to an amazing place that locals have long called the house on chicken legs. It is located at the entrance to the city, immediately behind the stella, so you definitely will not pass by.





For architects, the project was experimental, they decided to install a multi-storey building on supports, which gave it such a striking name. The house is attractive not only for its appearance, but also for its internal layout. This is a full-fledged house with two-story residential apartments - today the only one in Kirovo-Chepetsk. The abundance of windows, the presence of an unpopulated technical floor, two elevators and only one staircase - you see, a rather interesting and certainly non-standard set for a non-modern building. This house is the pride of the city, a vivid example of Soviet construction, the work of young and promising architects.

The Eternal Flame Memorial

Surely, such a memorial is in every city of our vast country, but it is simply impossible to delete it from the list of Kirovo-Chepetsk sights. Structurally, the memorial is no different from those that are installed in other cities of the country. Another thing is noteworthy: not so long ago, in 2006, a memorial sign was installed near it. This is a bright memory in honor of the participants in the liquidation consequences of the Chernobyl accident (recall, the accident at the station occurred in 1986). And here is what is especially interesting: the city authorities were categorically against the installation of the memorial sign, and then the residents of the city jointly installed it secretly on the night of April 25-26, 2006.

Military equipment on Kirova Avenue

After the Great Patriotic War and military operations in Afghanistan, military monuments appeared in many cities of the country. This town of the Kirov region was no exception . The sights of Kirovo-Chepetsk, the list of which is not so great, are presented and BMP. The monument greets guests of the city right at the intersection of Kirov Avenue and Gorky Street. This place was chosen in 2007 for the erection of a monument in honor of all those who died during the hostilities. On granite slabs adjacent to military equipment, the names of 14 residents of Kirovo-Chepetsk, who were directly involved in the military operation in Afghanistan, are engraved. Several times a year, it is in this place that meetings and rallies are held.





House of Culture "Friendship"

In the Soviet years, houses of culture in the cities of our country were erected according to a standard design, which is why it is hardly possible to surprise anyone with architectural delights. Nevertheless, the Druzhba recreation center, together with the square in front of it, has long passed into the category of the most beloved and most visited sights of Kirovo-Chepetsk. Family couples come for a walk here, lovers and, of course, city guests walk.

Columns of a white shade stand out effectively against the background of a burgundy, even wine shade, selected for the general background - this is the highlight of the Palace of Culture and the bold decision of the city's architects. Reporting concerts of local ensembles take place in this building, and celebrities and stars come here with their tours.

Recreation area "Karkino"

The sights of Kirovo-Chepetsk are represented not only by architectural objects. You should understand what a picturesque place the city is, and the places here are really amazing. If you come here for a few days, be sure to visit the Karkino recreation area. It can be safely attributed to the sights of Kirovo-Chepetsk.





It is located outside the city, literally immersed in greenery and creates all the necessary conditions for a complete outdoor recreation: a sanatorium, small houses for one or two families, beaches, a real Russian bathhouse, a swimming pool, a sports ground and a place for barbecue. Artificial ponds with crystal clear water, numerous waterfalls and unique trees - this is what allows you to forget about everything in the world. If you come to the city in winter, do not refuse to visit here. In winter, a full-fledged ski resort operates here. If you have long dreamed of skiing or snowboarding, take this great opportunity.

Oryol Lake

And here is another wonderful and picturesque place, a landmark of Kirovo-Chepetsk. The picturesque reservoir is considered a natural monument, is under state protection. Around the lake are picturesque dense forests and impenetrable swamps, so you should be extremely careful when walking around its surroundings. Small islands located in the middle of an oval lake add mystery to it. On the northwest side there is a small but very popular beach area in the summer. But do not forget that the lake is under state protection; therefore, before visiting it, you should familiarize yourself with a set of certain rules.





To summarize

As part of this material, we highlighted the most interesting and significant for local residents, the main attractions of Kirovo-Chepetsk. Among them there are architectural monuments of the Soviet period and the present, as well as picturesque places around the city. If you are traveling here, be sure to visit at least some of the attractions described, because this will allow you to get acquainted with the culture and history of the city, to understand its way of life. It is really worth it, you can not even doubt it.
