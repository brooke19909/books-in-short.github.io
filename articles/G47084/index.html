Hotel "Severnaya", St. Petersburg. Hotel reviews

St. Petersburg is rightfully considered the second capital of the Russian Federation, as well as a city with a rich historical heritage and a developed tourism industry. Many people mistakenly believe that rest in this unique city is expensive. The lack of financial opportunities is not a reason to put off your vacation in the long box. In St. Petersburg there are tourist complexes, hotels and hostels for a budget holiday.

Available for any citizen with different material incomes is the Severnaya Hotel (St. Petersburg), which has a convenient location and offers a lot of opportunities for active, business, sightseeing and educational activities. Hundreds of guests come here every year, and not only from Russia. The main contingent is business travelers, young people who prefer visiting local attractions for the money they save.

For example, to see firsthand Peterhof, the famous Hermitage and the glorified white nights. And also take a walk along the promenade, watch the drawbridges, go to museums and exhibitions. Vivid and pleasant impressions The northern capital leaves a lot. So, let's move on to the description of one of the most popular budget hotels in the city.

Object Features





Departmental hotel "Severnaya" (St. Petersburg), the photo of which is presented in the article, was conveniently located in the building of the former Medical Academy. This is a fairly comfortable hotel at an affordable cost with a cozy housing. The best overnight stay for an economical tourist is not to be found. Here you will find privacy, an atmosphere of peace and, most importantly, you can freely walk around the city center.

You can find it in a beautiful historical area called Shuvalovo-Ozerki, the development of which began in the 70s. Upon arrival, you must present a passport or other document proving your identity. It is possible to book your room in advance. For those who wish, a meeting is organized at the station or at the airport. They will pick you up in a comfortable and modern car and take you to the door of the hotel.

It will not be difficult to get to the complex on your own: the metro stations Ozerki, Prospekt Prosvescheniya and Civil Prospect are nearby. The Severnaya Hotel (St. Petersburg) is well located in relation to the historical sites of the city, such as Palace and St. Isaac's Square, Bronze Horseman, Promenade des Anglais. Within walking distance are concentrated large shopping galleries, entertainment centers, a water park and many other institutions that will be of interest to guests of the capital.

Where to stay?





The Severnaya Hotel (St. Petersburg) is located in a modern nine-story building, equipped with apartments for 1010 people. All rooms with partial amenities (toilet and shower - shared on the floor). The rooms include accommodation for up to three people. Rooms with or without a TV are available. On some floors, there are kitchen areas with household appliances and a set of dishes.

All residential premises have been redecorated and equipped with modern furniture. Each room has a loggia with panoramic views of the city center. There are beds, a small wardrobe with hangers, bedside tables, a TV (not all), chairs and hygiene items. There are no failures with water supply and heating. Thoroughly cleaned regularly.

Supply system





Hotel Severnaya (St. Petersburg) provides full hot breakfasts in a cozy cafeteria for an additional fee. According to the guests, the diet is quite balanced, the dishes are varied, they are distinguished by freshness and pleasant taste. You can also eat at any other institution within the city. There are a huge number of inexpensive bars, canteens and restaurants nearby with the hotel building.

How to get to the complex, which is located in St. Petersburg?

Digest: opinions of tourists

After reading the many reviews of people on the network who have already repeatedly stayed at this hotel, you can understand: the main audience of the guests was satisfied with the living conditions and quality of service. Almost everyone liked the territorial location - the Severnaya Hotel (St. Petersburg) was located close to the central attractions. Reviews in a positive way were said about the number of rooms.
