What is an air conditioner without an outdoor unit

An air conditioner without an outdoor unit is also called mobile. It does not require special skills for installation and is installed in a convenient place for you. The device is equipped with a durable folding handle and rollers to make it easy to move from place to place

It is noteworthy that in most cases, air conditioners without an outdoor unit, the price of which is in the middle price segment (about 10 thousand rubles), are used in houses, apartments, small offices, etc. As you can see, this type of device is ideal for installation in rooms on the facades of which it is impossible to install an external unit.





Device advantages

  1. You do not have to carry out any installation work outside the house. You will be able to maintain the exterior appearance of the building, since only two small decorative nets that can be painted in any color are required to be installed on the facade.
  2. The ability to place all the components of the system in a single unit without causing damage to the functionality.
  3. Since environmentally friendly refrigerants are used in the manufacture of air conditioners, you will take care of your environment.
  4. An air conditioner without an outdoor unit shows high energy efficiency and saves electricity.

When installing such a device, it is important to take into account the fact that warm air is removed from the air conditioner with a corrugated hose-duct into an open window, window or door. Usually, this hose is included in your kit.

Minuses

It is important to note that the disadvantages of such devices are very, very few. So, an air conditioner without an outdoor unit operates in small areas - not more than thirty square meters, and it also works quite noisily - 40-50 dB.
