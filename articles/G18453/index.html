Rest wild on the Volga. Rest by the river

Not everyone can afford foreign travel. And today, many Russians decide to spend their holidays in picturesque places in the middle zone of our country, away from the hustle and bustle of megacities. One option is a savage vacation on the Volga. In the territory through which it flows, there are many forests, rivers and lakes. In addition, to go there just a couple of hours from the Moscow Ring Road.



In summer, on the banks of the Volga River, you can notice a large number of campers. These are the so-called "savages." This holiday has its advantages. So, you can independently determine the route, depending on your desires and the state of the wallet.

This article will discuss the theme of wild beaches on the Volga River. Moreover, it is relevant in the holiday season.

Kalyazin beaches

Extremely beautiful vacation spots on the Volga. Savage, let's say, you can go to the Kalyazinsky district of the Tver region. It is 190 km from Moscow. A couple of hours away, and nature will show you all its wealth. Forest or sandy beaches of the Volga with their clean air will be remembered by vacationers for a long time. Every year, more and more tourists rest on Kalyazin beaches, some of whom are selected for only a few days, and there are those who prefer a one-day vacation. It is not surprising that in the summer the beaches turn into real campsites.



How to choose a suitable place

In the case of choosing a vacation on Kalyazinsky beaches, it is advisable to study in advance the scheme of travel from Moscow by car.

You also need to take into account the congestion of the shores, as at the weekend it is very difficult to find a free place in the sun. For quick orientation on the ground you need to have a map of the entire area with you. It is characterized by many small rivers, in which the water is the cleanest. People who constantly choose for themselves a vacation as a savage on the Volga already know such places. Those who go there for the first time need to spend some time to find a suitable place to relax.

It should be borne in mind that not all beaches have a convenient asphalt road. But, as a rule, country roads by the beginning of the season are often leveled by local transport, so traveling through them will not create difficulties.



Sandy beaches

On small tributaries, beaches with sand are very difficult to find. Many of them are located in large villages. One of these beaches is located in Porechye - a rural center in Kalyazin. Such places are good for those who decide to take a sunbath at the weekend.

Grassy beaches

Those who prefer to go to the Volga with a tent, opt for grassy beaches. The grass on them is not very tall, not more than 10 cm. This is due to the influx of a large number of tourists who regularly trample it.

Forest shore

You can find the forest shore by turning right after Porechye and driving along the river. There are a lot of vacationers who prefer to rest as a savage on the Volga. They come here for a few days with their families. On such shores it is convenient to dry clothes by pulling ropes among the trees. In addition, the forest serves as protection in windy conditions.

Before going on a vacation by the river, you need to study the weather forecast, and then decide on the warmest time for swimming and barbecue.



What to take with you on vacation

It must be remembered that in the evenings, vacationers face such nuisance as mosquitoes and midges, and in the heat - horseflies. Therefore, it is necessary to take care of acquiring reliable protection - a spray or cream from insects.

In order to safely walk along the bottom of the river, it is better to bring comfortable sandals with you. This will protect your feet from cuts, because sometimes vacationers throw various rubbish directly into the river, including bottles and cans.

It is advisable that the sandals are securely attached to the feet and not fall off when swimming.

In order to protect your feet from grass and pebbles, you need to take light slippers. They are comfortable shoes after bathing.

What else do you need to take on vacation as a savage on the Volga? Disinfectants and disinfectants. The cheapest and most effective means are diamond greens and hydrogen peroxide. It is better, of course, that they are not useful, but all the same there will not be any excess medicine, because the probability of getting injured on vacation is quite high.

In the summer it is very difficult to maintain the freshness of the products. There is no electricity on wild beaches, which means that perishable foods like meat and milk are best avoided. The optimal amount of edible is as much as you can eat in a day. All products will be available in the area. Moreover, there are many convenience stores. But it should be noted that alcoholic beverages are sold only until 22 hours.



Since vacationers spend a lot of time in the sun, one must not forget the sunblock and protection against burns. But in any case, you should follow the rules of being in the sun in order to bring a beautiful tan from rest, and not skin peeling off from a sunburn.

sights

The main attraction of the city is the flooded bell tower. You can also visit the museum of local lore. For lovers of river walks there is a yacht club. Luxurious yachts will attract the attention of every vacationer.

You can also rent boats. Walking on a boat or jet ski will bring vacationers a lot of positive emotions and memories.

In the fight for cleanliness

In order for the nature of the Volga to please many for many years to come, you need to take care of garbage disposal in advance. To do this, it is better to take garbage bags with you. Indeed, nobody cleans the Kalyazin beaches, and a lot of garbage accumulates during the season. By decision of the district authorities, about a hundred garbage containers were installed at the roadsides. Therefore, at the end of the rest, it is necessary to collect all the garbage that is available, and even that which was left by other vacationers, and take it to a roadside container. From there, he will then be taken out by special services. The cleanliness of the beach should first of all be watched by the vacationers themselves, because, as a rule, one wants to return to such places of rest again and again.



Recreation facilities on the banks of the Volga

1. The recreation center "Eremin Mountain". It is located 400 km from Moscow, in the Valdai district of the Novgorod region on the shores of Lake Yar-Lambo. It is located near the famous Valdai Lake. Those who like fishing on the Volga come to the base, who want to ride a boat, go for a swim, fry kebabs on specially equipped platforms.

For holidaymakers, excursions to Valday, Seliger, and Novgorod are available.

The territory of the base is occupied by: 9 wooden cottages, a cafe, a pier, two baths, a sports complex, a swimming pool. There is a grassy beach with a sandy entrance.

2. Pension "Northern Lights". It is located 400 km from Moscow in the Valdai district of the Novgorod region, on the shores of Lake Dinner, not far from Lake Valdai.

Vacationers can visit the Temple of Sergei Radonezh, located on the shores of Lake Valdai. In the pension / complex there is a sandy beach, a sauna, little houses in the forest. For fishing lovers all conditions are provided.

3. Shale mine in the Ulyanovsk region. Located 30 km from Ulyanovsk. In order to reach it, it is necessary to turn in front of the Undors towards the settlement of Shale mine, drive to the village and there, in front of the store, turn left. This place is suitable for those who choose an inexpensive vacation on the Volga.

There is an excellent relaxation area. It is located on the banks of the Volga, which in this place is simply indescribable. This is the perfect place to relax savage.

The coast is sandy, and the beach is pebble, with a muddy bottom. The beach is wild, so it is better not to neglect safety measures and take a walk in shoes, as there is a chance of being injured by fragments from bottles.

4. The recreation center "Lily of the Valley". Located on the banks of the Volga, in pine forests, 50 km from the city of Cheboksary.

On the territory of the base there is a cafe, a volleyball court, a beach, boat rental, barbecue facilities, first-aid kits, free but not guarded parking. Rest with children is possible from any age.



5. Fishing and hunting base "Volga-Delta". It is located 100 km from Astrakhan, near the Caspian Sea. On the base there are all conditions for a comfortable stay. In addition, everyone who is interested in fishing on the Volga (he rests as a savage or just came to sit with a fishing rod on the shore), all the necessary conditions are created here.

In conclusion, I want to note that the Volga has a variety of natural resources and historical values. Every Russian should feel proud of how rich the nature of his native country is. And this feeling will surely overwhelm everyone who visited a vacation spot on the Volga!
