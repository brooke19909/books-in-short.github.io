Hovima Santa Maria Aparthotel 3 * (Spain / Canary Islands Tenerife): description, service, reviews. Holidays in Spain

The island of Tenerife is the most popular resort where tourist life in full swing in the summer. Of course, for the convenience of vacationers there are many entertainment venues, cafes, restaurants and markets. There are also comfortable hotels in Tenerife. For example, tourists, including Russian, can rent rooms at the Hovima Santa Maria Aparthotel 3 *.

Where is the hotel located

This complex is located on about. Tenerife (Canary Islands) in the city of Costa Adeje. The nearest airport is just 17 km away. That is, tourists can book the places booked here in literally 20 minutes. The hotel is only 1 km from the center of Costa Adeje (Spain).





The undoubted advantages of this hotel tourists include its proximity to the sea. To get to the beach, hotel guests need to overcome only about 300 meters.

General description of the hotel

Hovima Santa Maria Aparthotel 3 * is quite old. It was built in the middle of the last century. However, this does not mean that the decoration and equipment in the rooms rented at this hotel are old and uncomfortable. Of course, the hotel periodically underwent renovations. The last time the repair in the complex was done in 2010

Hosted by Costa Adeje, this Hovima Hoteles hotel is housed in a seven-story building with original, modern architecture. The territory of the hotel has its own. It is believed that this hotel is best suited for family travelers who come to Adeje for a vacation with their children.





Spain in March, including the island of Tenerife, is an unusually romantic place. Nature at this time of year begins to come to life, and the rains practically stop. This month is considered the beginning of the holiday season in the country. The latter lasts until mid-autumn. But, of course, tourists can come to the Hovima Santa Maria in the winter. This hotel is open all year.

Number of rooms

Tenerife guests have the opportunity to rent one of 402 rooms in the Hovima Santa Maria Aparthotel 3 *. The hotel mainly rents economy rooms, standard rooms and apartments. By categories, the rooms of the complex are evenly distributed across all floors. Four rooms in the hotel are equipped for people with disabilities. In the rented rooms, guests can use:

  • telephone

  • TV

  • fridge and kettle;

  • a set of dishes;

  • safe.





The television in the hotel is satellite. Guests are required to pay a fee for using the safe. Of course, shower rooms are equipped in all rooms of this hotel. Towels in the bathroom residents can use for free. This hotel has earned good reviews from vacationers for the availability of spacious balconies.

The ability to rent low-cost housing - that's why, among other things, vacation in Spain is very popular. The prices for renting rooms in local hotels are about the same as in Goa and in Thailand. For 100 thousand p. You can rent a good double room for 10 days. The Hovima Santa Maria hotel is no exception in this regard. The infrastructure in the hotel Hovima Santa Maria 3 *, according to most of its former guests, is developed relatively well. On the territory of the complex, its residents can use:

Of course, guests of the hotel and in the pool can swim. Spain in March, June, September - a vacation spot suitable just perfect. The weather on Tenerife is excellent these months. As already mentioned, many tourists come to the Hovima Santa Maria Aparthotel 3 * with children. For small guests there are many amenities. For children, the hotel has a games room and a playground. Infrastructure Reviews

The service provided to guests at the Hovima Santa Maria Aparthotel is considered by many tourists to be quite worthy. Hotel staff praises most of its former guests. The hotel staff is speaking, unfortunately, only in English. However, one girl - the administrator Natasha - still understands the Russian language. The courtyard of the hotel, most of the guests considers quite beautiful. The kindergarten is really there, but very small. In fact, this is just a designer planting of several trees and flower beds. Dining at the hotel

It is possible to rent rooms in the Hovima Santa Maria Aparthotel 3 * hotel with breakfast or a guest house. The food at the hotel, according to the residents, is not bad. The portions at the local restaurant are always large. So guests are never hungry. Regarding the variety of menus, the hotel restaurant also earned good reviews from tourists. The cooks of the restaurant are quite tasty. The only thing - some former residents of the hotel complain that the dishes in the local restaurant are served cold.

Hotel beach

The Hovima Santa Maria Aparthotel 3 * is located on the first coastline. To get to the sea, its guests can literally 10 minutes. Unfortunately, this complex does not have a private beach. Therefore, its tenants visit the public. You can lie for free on your bedding on this beach. For the use of showers, toilets and sun loungers, a small amount is charged.

Some hotel guests also visit the beach in Los Gigantes with black sand. It is located quite far from the hotel. You need to go here by car for about 30 minutes. There is also a very beautiful Playa del Duc beach in a 15-minute walk from the complex. The coastline here is unusually picturesque. The color of the water in the sea, according to many tourists, is simply indescribable.
