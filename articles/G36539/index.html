Service "Help out"

Any MTS subscriber, if there is a question, how to call, if there is no money on the phone, can use this service. "Promised payment"

This service is especially relevant at the moment when the balance on the phone very soon becomes zero or has already become, and this raises the question of how to make a call if there is no money on the phone. To activate the service, you need to dial the following combination on your phone: * 111 * 32 # and the call key.

"Top up my account"
