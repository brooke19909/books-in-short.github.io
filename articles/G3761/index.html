Varieties of Shawls

Today, a crocheted lace shawl is no longer a wonder. And even before the 17th century, Europe did not know about such an accessory. It was used mainly in the East.

It is generally accepted that a shawl is a huge half-board. It looks like an ordinary scarf, only has impressive dimensions.

Such accessories come in many forms: woven and crocheted or knitting. There are even options from macrame or specific lace. The long side of the shawl often exceeds 1.5 meters. This allows a woman to wrap herself in her completely.



Some people call the shawl a wide scarf, which has another name - the stole. In order not to make a welter in this matter, we will focus on the fact that openwork shawls with crochets with patterns are also rectangular. In addition, an equilateral rectangle is a square, and therefore a large scarf.

Simple and beautiful lace pattern

Every woman wants an openwork shawl in her closet. Crochet (the scheme of work mastered by many craftsmen), such a product can be built in a couple of days. To do this, you can use the simplest pattern.

We collect the number of air loops equal to the length of the side of the future square, and we knit the first row according to the scheme: 2 air loops, 2 double crochets through 2 loops, 2 w / n, 2 double crochets in a row, 2 air loops.





In the second row we knit 7 stitches and 4 double crochets over a group of 4 stitches.

The third row is the most interesting. It is knitted from double crochet posts. A group of columns with a common point knits 2 rows at once. This is the vertical pattern report. We knit it until the fabric becomes square or the length that we need to make a crocheted openwork shawl. This scheme is ideal for beginners.

Fan shawl from the base

Before that, we looked at crocheting an openwork shawl, the patterns of which were built either from the corner or square. Now let's pay attention to a more traditional way of making this accessory.

The diagram clearly shows that knitting begins with a set of air loops. In the first row, arches from the same military style with crochet columns are knitted. But starting from the second row, the main pattern is built using double crochet.



In each row, the number of loops decreases. Due to this, the canvas gradually narrows and eventually comes to a triangular shape.

This pattern also requires fringe. You can make it not only with the help of brushes, as in the previous version, but also by knitting large arches from air loops at the edges with small gaps between them. This will give an interesting fringe that will beautifully hang from the shoulders of a woman wearing this shawl.

Semicircular model

For some reason, it is generally accepted that a crocheted openwork shawl, the scheme and description of which is the subject of this article, should be only triangular. But some craftswomen have long developed patterns of semicircular products. And they look no worse than those that have a corner.

To tie this shawl, you must have an average level of skill, since there are many transitions from one patterned pattern to another. At the same time, there are no complex loops and columns in the scheme, which guarantees the success of almost every craftswoman who has a sufficient level of attention.





The presence of sharp angles in the pattern does not foresee fringe shawl decoration. This adds to it a certain charm that real fashionistas will appreciate.
