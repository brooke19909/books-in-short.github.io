Learning to make a flower from colored paper with your own hands (four ways)

Colored paper is the most common material for creativity. Even in kindergarten, each of us received the first skills to work with her, performing simple applications. It is possible to make unusually beautiful voluminous crafts from it: garlands, wreaths, origami figures , voluminous flowers. It is the latter that our article is devoted to. Today we will learn how to make flowers from colored paper. You can also view photos of such products here. If you were impressed by such beauty, then get down to business.



Pion

You can make a lush flower from colored paper with your own hands in just a couple of minutes. For work you will need:

  • a sheet of double-sided paper of any color;
  • scissors;
  • glue;
  • stapler.

Fold the paper with an accordion. Cut the edges in a semicircle. Secure the structure in the center with a stapler. Next, fold the workpiece in half, tie the bend place with thread so that the product does not fall apart. Use your fingers to gently spread each layer of paper, forming petals. Chamomile

This product is quick and easy to make, and looks simply charming. Try to make it and see for yourself. For work, prepare: yellow cardboard, landscape white sheet, scissors and glue. How to make a camomile - a flower from colored paper with your own hands? Read about it in the instructions.

On two yellow cardboards, draw two identical circles and cut them out. From white paper, cut strips 2 cm wide (10-12 pieces). Glue each tip to the tip without bending the part in the center. It turns out the petals in the form of loops. Glue all these elements along the edge of the yellow circle, one next to the other. Attach the second round part from above. Everything, a voluminous flower from colored paper in the form of daisies is ready.





rose flower

For the manufacture of this version of the product you will need double-sided colored paper, PVA glue, scissors. On a sheet, draw a circle of the desired diameter and cut it. The larger its size, the larger the resulting flower. Next, cut the part in a spiral, starting from the edge and moving towards the center. The result is a blank in the form of a serpentine. Lubricate the ‚Äútail‚Äù of the strip with glue and wind the spiral in a circle, pressing one edge with your fingers, and letting the second twist freely. Form thus a flower. As you twist, add glue along the entire length of the strip to the bottom edge. Rose - flower made of colored paper with your own hands.

Tulip

This product is made using the origami technique in just a minute. Fold a square sheet of paper in half diagonally. It turns out a double triangle. Lay it in front of you right angle up. Fold the right corner up, smooth the fold with your fingers. Similarly, follow the right side. It will turn out a product in the form of a tulip with three double petals. To prevent the parts from unbending, fix them with glue. Here is a flower made of colored paper with your own hands in the form of a cute tulip.
