Knitting pattern of front and back loops. Samples and schemes

People who want to learn how to knit, first of all learn how to knit stitches, knit stitches, and make stitches. But even with such simple tricks, you can knit beautiful relief patterns. A huge number of them were invented. Suitable patterns from the front and back loops with knitting needles for any kind of thread. Products made of thick yarn will be voluminous, from thin - elegant.

The main thing in knitting such products is to carefully consider the loops and act according to the scheme. Each knitting pattern is depicted graphically using legend. Each cell is one loop. If the cell is white, then the loop is facial. If a black circle is drawn in the cage, then the loop needs to be knitted inside out. Depending on the characters drawn, the front and back loops alternate in the product. Consider several patterns and patterns of simple patterns for beginner knitters.

Three samples with schemes

The first sample consists mainly of facial loops that look like wavy vertical lines on the finished product. After a set of loops, the first row is knitted according to the scheme. After the edge, look at the conventional signs: 3 persons., 1 out., 3 persons., 1 out., 1 persons., 1 out. Then the pattern repeats. The second row is the back of the product. Knits as loops look. In the third row, the pattern is already changing. It is necessary to carefully monitor the location of the black circles so as not to go astray, otherwise the pattern immediately deforms.





This is the biggest difficulty in all patterns of front and back loops with knitting needles. It requires attention and concentration, however, as in all complex patterns. However, if the pattern is simple and several elements are knitted, then you can already visually understand that the pattern is broken. If this happens, do not worry. This is easy to fix. You need to gently pull one knitting needle and pull the thread to weave one row. Then back you need to insert the needle in order in each loop. Then to continue back the pattern of the front and back loops with knitting needles is correct.

Children's plaid

The relief pattern from the alternation of loops for a baby blanket into a stroller looks very nice. The thread must be chosen not prickly, so that the product does not irritate the delicate skin of the baby. Knitting begins with 6 rows of garter stitch, that is, all of the front and on the top and back of the fabric.





Next, we look at a sample of the pattern from the front and back loops with knitting needles and begin to knit the pattern itself: 9 purl and 3 front. On the back, loops are knitted as they look. After 4 rows, the pattern shifts by three loops. Front strips will be staggered. The first and last 6 loops are knitted with a garter stitch. The last six rows are knitted in the same way. Then the plaid will turn out with a frame.

Male pattern

Such simple patterns with front and back loops according to the scheme will look beautiful on sweaters for boys and men. This is a strict set of horizontal and vertical rectangles. Alternation changes: 5 wrong and 2 front loops. The reverse side is knitted as usual for such patterns, that is, as the loops look. After knitting four rows, the pattern changes to the opposite. Where there were facial - now purl, and vice versa.





It is better to take a simple smooth yarn for such a pattern.

Rhombuses

To correctly knit diamonds, you need to draw them on the diagram. The row consists of an odd number of loops. Rhombuses can be made from both front and back loops. In the first embodiment, they will be convex, in the second case - recessed into the canvas.
