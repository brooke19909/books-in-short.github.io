Portable Sven speakers. Short overview and description

Sven has long been known in the vast market. The products of this brand are very popular among many users. They are especially interested in speaker systems, because for relatively little money, it is possible to get very high-quality sound. But today we will not talk about computer acoustics, but about Sven portable speakers, which have recently been in great demand. So let's get started.

Portable speaker SVEN PS-50

The first model to start with is the Sven PS 50. The speaker is the youngest of the three presented, so you probably won‚Äôt have to expect supercharacteristics or megaphone from it, although it may be different.





Appearance

Frankly speaking, the column looks like an amateur. The design is very reminiscent of cheap Chinese radio from the 90s or early 2000s. On the front of the column is 1 speaker covered with a perforated mesh. To the right of it is a small display on which various information and control buttons are displayed. They are made according to the type of those that were in ancient cassette or CD players.





At the bottom you can find the connector for a memory card, on the right side there is a USB connector for conventional flash drives and a microUSB port for a charger. There are only 2 3.5 mm jacks on top - one for headphones, the other for wired connection. Well, the back cover is most of the battery cover.





Build quality is above average, case material is glossy plastic. In total, all this creates the impression of cheapness, and the gloss also gets dirty very quickly with fingerprints.

Specifications

If we talk about the technical characteristics of the Sven PS 50, then everything is extremely simple. The total power of the speaker is 3 watts. There is only one speaker and its size is 40 mm. The frequency range is from 90 to 20,000 Hz. Of the interesting features, it is worth noting the presence of a display, support for memory cards and standard flash drives. As for the battery, its capacity is 800 mAh and a full charge lasts about 5 hours.





Sound

The portable Sven PS50 speaker sounds nice enough, which is generally unexpected. There are low frequencies that are not very well designed, but nonetheless. The middle is well audible, and, of course, high frequencies. The volume margin is decent, but only up to 65-70%. If you increase it above these marks, you get a mess. Wheezing and extraneous sounds are not observed.

Reviews and price

Reviews of the Sven PS 50 portable speaker show that this model has several drawbacks, which include a weak battery, the lack of a bluetooth module, a glossy case and the lack of a menu for navigating tracks.





You can buy Sven PS 50 for about 550-800 rubles, which is not so much.

SVEN PS-250BL

The next portable speaker on the list is the Sven PS-250BL. This model is already more expensive than the previous one, but its characteristics are much better. Other materials are also used here and the look is more attractive.

Appearance and characteristics of the model

The Sven PS-250BL portable speaker looks very much like a boombox, only small in size. Extendable carrying handle is very attractive, which is very convenient. Case materials - this is a nice matte plastic. Gloss is only on the side ends - not scary. Front and rear of the column is a metal mesh with perforation. Behind her, traditionally hiding speakers.





Governing bodies are placed on a separate strip. Of the interesting, it is worth noting the presence of microphones and a button for answering a call. A set of connectors is standard: USB, microUSB, AUX and 3.5 mm. Connectors hide behind a rubber stopper.

Of the characteristics, it is worth noting a power of 10 W, the presence of a bluetooth module, a frequency range from 100 Hz to 22 kHz, a 2200 mAh battery that allows you to listen to music for up to 10 hours, although the manufacturer assures 20 hours and compact size.





Sound quality

Almost no complaints about the sound. The bottoms are audible, but again poorly designed. There is a good middle and high frequencies are very audible. The volume margin is also decent, but after 80% of the speakers begin to turn the sound into a mess. There are no rattles, squeaks and other noises.

Reviews and price

If you look at the reviews about this model, then it has several disadvantages, but not too critical. All of them are connected mainly with appearance and design, so listing them does not make much sense. Otherwise, users are happy with everything.





As for the cost, the Sven PS 250 can be bought from 1700 to 2700 rubles. The spread is large, but, fortunately, the column is sold everywhere and you can choose the most suitable price for yourself.

SVEN PS-460

The latest model and the oldest of the presented. The appearance of the speaker is somewhat reminiscent of JBL, only with a carrying handle integrated in the housing. By the way, a pen is needed here, since the column weighs almost 2 kg.





All connectors are located on the back, including the power switch. In front there is a small display, which is very convenient and controls in the form of small buttons. There is even a separate equalizer button.

Case materials are presented in the form of a good matte plastic with a rubberized coating and perforated metal mesh. Build quality is good.





Sound

Now you can move on to the sound quality of the Sven PS-460 portable speaker. In general, the sound is very similar to the previous models, but a little better. The situation with frequencies is absolutely the same for everyone - there are not enough low and middle ones. High frequencies are heard very well. There is a margin of volume, but at the maximum level, there is a "mess". You can try to adjust the equalizer, but this is unlikely to help.

Reviews and cost

As reviews of the Sven 460 portable speaker show, this model has a slightly weak battery, a poorly fixed AUX connector on the rear panel, and a weak antenna for receiving radio signals. In other moments, users have no complaints.





As for the cost, you can buy this model, on average, for 2000-2500 rubles, although there are options as more expensive, and vice versa.
