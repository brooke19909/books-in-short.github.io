Pension "Anapa Breeze", Anapa: photos and reviews of tourists

Holidays in Russia can be as comfortable and interesting as abroad. Anapa is a city where residents from different regions of the country strive to get. It has a wonderful mild climate and warm sea. In the boarding house " Anapsky Breeze " all conditions for an exciting and comfortable stay are created.

Where is

The complex is located in the Krasnodar Territory. Anapa Breeze Hotel is 2 km from the center of Anapa. The exact address of the hotel: Pionersky Prospekt, 20a / 15. You can get here by public transport or book a shuttle service at an additional cost.





The hotel is located 4 km from the railway station and 12 km from the airport. The beaches are in the immediate vicinity of the Anapa Breeze boarding house. The hotel is located on the second line from the sea.

Accommodation

The number of rooms is represented by three categories of rooms. They are equipped with everything necessary and have a good comfort class. The standard room for 2 and 3 people is equipped with:

  • wardrobe;
  • dressing table;
  • 2 bedside tables;
  • beds (2 or 3);
  • folding armchair;
  • soft puff;
  • TV
  • a refrigerator;
  • telephone
  • air conditioning;
  • an electric kettle and a minimum set of dishes.

Each room has a bathroom and shower with warm water at any time of the day. The hall provides furniture for storing outerwear and shoes.





The family room consists of two rooms. The bedroom has two beds, which, if necessary, can be combined into one large. Near them are neat nightstands. In the same room there are two chairs that can easily be transformed into a full-fledged berth for one person.

In the living room there is a large folding sofa. The room has a modern slide wall and chairs. Both rooms have televisions. The family room has a minimum set of necessary equipment:

  • refrigerator;
  • split system;
  • Electric kettle;
  • hair dryer.

Each bedroom has access to a spacious balcony.

Economy rooms have double or single beds. They have nightstands and a wardrobe. A bathroom and shower are in every room of this class.





Of household appliances, there is a refrigerator, a TV and a fan. The rooms on the upper floors have small balconies. The economy room has a special room for cooking. A cooler with cold and hot water, a microwave oven, tables and chairs are installed here. There is no extra charge for using the room.

Also in this building is equipped with a small children's corner with colorful toys and board games.
