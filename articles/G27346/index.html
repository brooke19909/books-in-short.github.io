Chinese restaurant "Heron", Vladivostok

This restaurant opened two years ago and has already managed to win the recognition of the residents of Vladivostok.

Heron: atmosphere

Guests who enter the charming courtyard of this establishment immediately find themselves in modern China. Outside and inside, the Tsaplya restaurant (Vladivostok) is made in true Chinese style. Visitors enjoy a warm and pleasant atmosphere. By the way, a heron is a sacred and proud bird, as the Chinese say. Therefore, the restaurant has several artificial birds that give exclusivity to the institution.





Kitchen

The Tsaplya restaurant (Vladivostok) is proud of the skill of its professional Chinese chef Wu Yulong, who knows how to surprise any guest. For the preparation of gourmet dishes, he uses the oldest recipes and high-quality products. Traditional food is served in a modernized, modern serving. But the chef does not stand still, he studies Cantonese, Beijing, Chandon and Sichuan cuisines to impress gourmets with originality and non-standard.

Also, visitors can enjoy seafood dishes, the menu has almost thirty of their varieties, various cold snacks and delicate desserts. On weekdays, from 12:00 to 16:00, lunch discounts apply.

Dishes from the chef and the cost of one serving are listed below:

  • "Tsap-la" - 750 p.
  • Lamb leg - 1750 p.
  • Shark fin soup - 1550 p.
  • Trepang with leek - 1990 p.
  • Peking duck - 2050 p.

In the alcoholic menu you can find any drink - from beer to strong whiskey.





The Tsaplya restaurant (Vladivostok) consists of three halls:

  • Banquet for 150 people. It can hold various celebrations, such as birthday, weddings, alumni meetings and the like.
  • VIP room for 10 people. It is designed for business meetings and corporate parties. In general, for those who need privacy, so that outsiders do not interfere with negotiations or celebrations in their circle.
  • Hall with a dance floor. Here you can not only eat and drink with friends, but also dance to incendiary tracks from professional DJs and enjoy the sounds of live jazz music.




If you want to have dinner or spend an evening in the Tsaplya restaurant, you need to book a table in advance, because this restaurant is in demand among residents and visitors of the city, it is always crowded there.

Restaurant Tsaplya (Vladivostok): visitor reviews

Everyone who visited this place, put a solid four and wish growth and improvement. Celebrate quality and quick service. They also say that in the whole city one cannot find better Chinese cuisine.

The Tsaplya restaurant (Vladivostok) is also popular among tourists. Those who can not afford to travel to China come here to enjoy the traditional cuisine and atmosphere of this country.
