Hotel Adalya Artside Hotel 5 *, Turkey, Side: photos, tours, reviews

Side is one of the brightest and most visited regions of Turkey, in the hotels of which every year agencies sell a huge number of tickets. One of the most popular places to stay is the Adalya Artside Hotel 5 * hotel, reviews of which can be found on the Internet, and all of them are usually positive.

On the territory of Adalya Artside Hotel 5 * quite often various kinds of reconstructions and renovations are carried out, one of the last major was carried out in 2012 - just then the interior of the apartment was updated and some changes were made in terms of technical equipment of the buildings.

Throughout the territory there are four five-story buildings, as well as a large aquazone. In addition, it features several outdoor sports fields and a large beautiful garden that pleases travelers throughout the resort period.



Location

Located Adalya Artside Hotel 5 * in Side. In the vicinity of it there are a huge number of attractions that many travelers seek to visit, and in the vicinity you can observe the beautiful nature.

From here, the central part of the region can be reached by breaking 10 kilometers. Many guests do this with a rented car. You can also use the services of a taxi or local public transport.

With regard to the proximity of the hotel to the airport, it is located at a distance of 62 kilometers from the location of the air traffic hub in Antalya.



Rooms

Adalya Artside Hotel 5 * belongs to the category of large places for recreation, as it has a large number of rooms, consisting of 417 apartments. All of them are evenly distributed over four five-tier buildings. Depending on the number of places for guests and the conditions provided, the apartments are divided into two types: standard and family.

Each room features a modern furniture set and a new interior, created during one of the latest renovations. They have good air conditioning, with which you can create the proper temperature conditions in the apartment, regardless of what is happening on the street.

There are also modern bathrooms, combined with bathrooms. Here everything is made in one style, there is a considerable amount of decor, mirrors, and there are also several cabinets where bathrobes, towels and soap accessories are neatly folded.

Standard

Standard category rooms, of which there are only 274 in the Adalya Artside Hotel 5 *, are divided into two types within the group, depending on which view opens from the window: Land View and Sea View. All of them are located on a small area (29-32 sq. M.) And are represented by one rather spacious room, decorated in bright colors.

Among the furniture there are without fail two beds: single and double. In some rooms, one single is replaced by a two-story place, which allows you to distribute not three, but four guests in the apartment. There is also a small place to store clothes and small items, in addition, a pair of nightstands is provided. In the area, which is near the large window, there is a small sofa with small coffee tables made of durable glass. Tourists talk about the pleasant tea parties that can be held here.

As for the technical equipment of these apartments, they have a medium-sized plasma TV, a minibar, and a safe. In addition, the room has a landline telephone connected to the international line, but its use is paid.



Family

Very often, in tourist reviews about Adalya Artside Hotel 5 * (Turkey / Side), one can come across the opinion that this place is perfect for relaxing with all the family members. Such opinions are due to the presence of a number of conditions for this category of guests. One of them is the availability of a separate type of room, which is designed to accommodate up to four guests with the possibility of dividing the living space.

Adalya Artside Hotel 5 * provides a category of rooms, each of which is 57 square meters. m, it is divided into two rooms, each of which is a fairly comfortable bedroom.

Each bedroom has a large double bed, a place to store the necessary things, as well as a working area in the form of a table and chair. Near the window in one room there is a pair of armchairs and a small table - here you can drink tea or coffee, prepared using a special substation. The rooms also have large pendant mirrors and plasma TVs that broadcast several Russian-language channels.

Family-type apartments in the bathroom have a children's sink and a pot, the presence of which is considered by guests from the positive side.

Entertainment

Tourists often admit that they do not have to be bored while staying at the Adalya Artside Hotel 5 *. The reviews of 2017 say that recently, traditional entertainment programs were introduced here, which are held in the daytime, starting in the morning. Most often, they are represented by sports competitions held in the open air or in the aquatic zone.

Traditionally, in the evenings, Adalya Artside Hotel 5 * (Turkey) hosts discos that can continue throughout the night. Often DJs play them, performing beloved by many world hits. You can attend such events for free.



Conditions for children

For the category of the smallest guests, the hotel has several conditions due to which the stay of the kids becomes not only comfortable and fun, but also safe. Among them - an outdoor pool in an open area, as well as a large children's area, on which there are several swings and labyrinths for their active pastime.

In their reviews, parents often talk about the pleasant possibility of free cots in apartments. Only many recommend advising in advance of its need, as the number of beds is limited.

In the evening, a disco is held for the kids, and during the day they can visit a special club where babysitters and animators can spend time with them.



Nutrition

Often in reviews of Adalya Artside Hotel 5 * (Side), tourists share their impressions of food in this hotel. They say that every day you can visit five restaurants that serve dishes of different cuisines of the world. Among them, one that offers Italian food and seafood is very popular. Travelers note the freshness of all products used by cooks, as well as the originality of the presentation of finished culinary art. The administration of the hotel warns every tourist that reservation of tables is required before a visit to the restaurant.

The hotel also has six bars, the entrance to one of which (disco) is paid, it works throughout the night. Here, customers are offered an extensive menu, which presents the most original and nutritious snacks, as well as desserts. In addition, each of them offers a special set of cocktails and standard drinks, which will certainly appeal to even the most spoiled gourmets.



Infrastructure

Staying in this place for rest, all tourists for a set fee can use the additional services that are provided at the request of the guest. Some of the most popular among them are those that turn out to be a beauty salon. Here, according to vacationers, only professional masters of makeup, manicure, as well as hairdressers work. In addition, the state has several cosmetologists who offer the possibility of applying masks, peeling, body wraps, etc.

Often guests use laundry and dry cleaning services. On the ground floor of the hotel there is a medical office where a local doctor can provide qualified assistance, it is also paid.

If desired, guests can rent a car from the range presented by the hotel. By vehicle you can take a short trip around the area.



Aquazone

This site in the hotel attracts special attention from guests. It is quite large in size and is a harmonious combination of a large sun terrace, three pools, a small water park, as well as a bar.

On the sun terrace there is a huge number of sun loungers that tourists can borrow for free. There are large beach umbrellas that protect them from the harmful effects of sunlight.

In the central part of the aquazone are pools that operate without a built-in heating function. If desired, vacationers can have fun on the water slides, which are a water park. It has three slopes for adults and three for children.

Sports and spa

For fans of working on their bodies in the building of one of the buildings there is a large gym in which several units are represented. Tourists love to visit this place, which is reflected in their reviews.

In addition to the hall there are several outdoor areas designed for sports games. There are bowling and billiards rooms in the buildings. On the beach there is a small volleyball court, which is incredibly popular among guests.

Fans of hot relaxation can visit the hammam and sauna located in the thermal part of the hotel spa. In the same area there is an indoor pool, which becomes especially visited in the winter, when the water in the aquazone located on the street does not warm up enough.

For a fee, everyone can visit the large massage room, where everything is arranged so that guests can be in a state of complete relaxation. Professional massage therapists can please tourists with special relaxing and therapeutic techniques.



Prices

The cost of the tour in Adalya Artside Hotel 5 * is on average about 65,000 rubles per person for two. This price traditionally includes flight and transfer costs. In addition, medical insurance required for crossing the border with Turkey is paid for this.

Having been here, travelers recommend visiting this hotel to their relatives and many acquaintances, showing photos taken at Adalya Artside Hotel 5 * filled with bright colors and particles of eternal summer.
