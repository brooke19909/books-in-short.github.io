Hotel Kata Lucky Villa Pool Access Kata Phuket, Thailand: reviews

Many of our compatriots come to rest on the Thai island of Phuket. And of course, Russian tourists would like to know what good hotels are available in this popular resort. Good reviews of guests have earned many hotels in Phuket. And one of the best, according to Russian vacationers, is the Kata Lucky Villa Pool Access Kata. For some tour operators, this hotel is marked as a three-star, while others have a four-star hotel.

Location reviews

Excellent opinion among domestic vacationers about this hotel has developed primarily due to its fairly convenient location. This hotel is located in the central part of the popular Kata Bay. From the airport, vacationers who have booked rooms here will have to travel about an hour by the tour operator's transfer bus. To the capital of the island, the city of Phuket, from the hotel about 25 minutes by shuttle bus. The popular Patong Resort is just a 15-minute drive away.





This hotel has earned good reviews from tourists, including for its relatively close location to the ocean. The Kata Lucky Villa Pool Access 3 * complex is located on the second coastline. The distance from its gate to the nearest beach is only about 500 meters. That is, the residents of the hotel can walk to the sea in about 20 minutes.

Many domestic vacationers prefer to book rooms in this hotel on their own. Such guests usually book a transfer from the airport here.

The advantages of this complex, tourists include, including distance from the road. Built hotel in a quiet side street. Therefore, in the mornings, hotel guests have the opportunity to listen not to the roar of motorbike motors, but to the singing of tropical birds.

Description of the complex

Phuket welcomes the Kata Lucky Villa Pool Access Kata Hotel in several one-story buildings and villas. The hotel territory, according to its guests, looks simply magnificent. The hotel courtyard is literally surrounded by tropical greenery. The complex has a lot of neat lawns and flower beds with southern luxurious ornamental plants. All paths in the hotel are covered with beautiful paving tiles. Therefore, you can safely stumble around the complex even in the evening or at night.





The reception, as in most other Phuket facilities, is open 24 hours a day at Kata Lucky Villa Pool Access Kata. Unfortunately, you cannot live with cats and dogs in this complex. Most tourists believe that this center is best for a relaxing holiday with children. However, young people, as well as elderly vacationers, will certainly feel quite comfortable here.

The rooms at this hotel are decorated in a modern hi-tech style. Most tourists find this design quite enjoyable. The villas in the hotel are built in traditional Thai style. Each of these houses has a small veranda equipped with tables and tables. Here, tourists can optionally sit in the evening with a glass of beer or a cup of tea.

Rented rooms

In total, this hotel offers tourists 95 well-equipped rooms. If you wish, you can rent standard rooms, a deluxe villa or pool access with access to the pool. In all rooms rented tourists can use:

  • air conditioners;

  • TVs

  • refrigerators;

  • Phones

  • safes.





The mini-sets for making tea or coffee are also provided for guests at this hotel. The hotel's showers include hairdryers, shampoo and soap. Walking around the rooms barefoot or in the shales, guests will not have to. The rooms also include slippers and bathrobes. In rainy weather, guests can use umbrellas for free.

Of course, there are balconies (terraces on the ground floor) in the rooms of this hotel. Since the complex is located quite far from the coast, beautiful views of the ocean from the windows of its rooms, of course, do not open. However, forest-covered mountains can be seen very well from the balconies of some rooms. The furniture in the rooms includes wide beds, sofas, wardrobes, desks.

Guest reviews about the service

The infrastructure of the hotel Kata Lucky Villa Pool Access 4 * (Phuket), most of its residents according to their development, consider it quite appropriate for four stars. The staff at this hotel are quite polite. The pool at the hotel is not particularly large, but swimming is quite comfortable. It has a depth of about 1.2 meters. This, of course, is not particularly much. However, the water in the pool of the complex is always quite clean and does not smell like bleach. Everything else, when bathing in it, hotel guests can admire the simply magnificent mountain scenery.

Really like the residents of the hotel and the massage sessions held on its territory. Visit at least one of them advises most vacationers.

Tourists have a good opinion about this hotel for one more reason. Unfortunately, hurricanes quite often happen on Kata. At the same time, electricity is disconnected throughout the resort. In such cases, residents of the Kata Lucky Villa Pool Access hotel, reviews of which are actually very good, do not experience any inconvenience at all. The fact is that the hotel, among other things, has its own generator. Therefore, the light in the complex is turned on literally 10 minutes after it disappears in Kata itself. With electricity, therefore, residents of this hotel never have problems. The same goes for hot water. She is always at the hotel.

Tourists consider some of the drawbacks of the center to be that sometimes noisy Chinese people come here. Moreover, they usually come to the hotel in large companies at night. However, the Chinese make noise most often only in the yard, and in their rooms they are almost inaudible. In addition, the inhabitants of the Celestial Empire leave the hotel in most cases very quickly (they rest for only 2-3 days).

Hotel dining reviews

Actually the hall of this institution itself is considered by many tourists to be quite clean and tidy. The staff in the restaurant, according to the former residents of the hotel, is quite polite and quick.

In addition to the restaurant, there is also a small cafe on the hotel grounds. It is located next to the pool and earned just excellent reviews from residents. According to the opinion of many vacationers, they cook in this restaurant even better than in the hotel restaurant.

Kata Lucky Villa Pool Access 3 *: beach reviews

The location of the complex relative to the sea, many tourists, as already mentioned, consider it quite convenient. But older people can get to the beach in the heat for 20 minutes, of course, it can be difficult. In addition, you need to go to the sea from the hotel directly along the roadway. Therefore, at the Kata Lucky Villa Pool Access Kata, among other things, there is also a free shuttle service to the coast.

Actually Kata beach itself is considered by most tourists to be the best in Phuket. The sand on the coastline here is clean, white and very soft. The water in the sea at this place is usually completely clear and clean. Free loungers for guests of the Kata Lucky Villa Pool Access Kata, however, like most others in Phuket, is not provided. You need to pay for their rent separately. But the coastline on Kata itself is very wide. And therefore, despite the rather large number of vacationers, there is always a lot of free space.

The water in the sea on Kata beach is very warm in season. And therefore, swimming here is quite comfortable not only for adults, but also for children.

What you should know about

Unfortunately, there are often very large waves in this place of the island on the sea, out of season. At this time, tourists should adequately calculate their strength and not go into the water too far. Lifeguards are constantly working on Kata beach. However, risking vacationers is still, of course, not worth it. Most of the hotel guests, when excited by the sea, prefer to swim in the pool.

sights

An employee at the tour desk at Kata Lucky Villa Pool Access Kata is always ready to help guests plan their trips around Phuket. Of course, there are not so many interesting sights on this island, as, for example, in the same Greece or Italy. However, there is still something to see here.

Many hotel residents, for example, buy excursions to the very beautiful Similan Islands. This tropical archipelago is one of Thailand's largest national parks. It consists of 11 islands, some of which are open for tourists to visit. They bring Phuket guests to the islands by boat.





Most tour operators offer hotel guests, of course, a free sightseeing tour. Vacationers who agree to it can inspect a jewelry factory, a latex factory, visit a fruit market, etc.

Useful advice

Many tourists rent a car or bike in Thailand. With such equipment, of course, you can see a lot more attractions. At the same time, such independent excursions will cost, of course, much cheaper than organized by tour operators.
