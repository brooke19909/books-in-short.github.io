Ski resort "Spas-Kamenka": description, address, reviews

Do you want to have a good rest in Russia? The Spas-Kamenka ski resort is a good opportunity for everyone to have a good time both in winter and in summer. Now - more!

Affordable entertainment throughout Russia

Sometimes you look out the window and dream about visiting a beautiful place, relaxing and playing sports. I want to soak in a warm and comfortable bed, when outside the window there is snow and frost. Immediately in the head there appear images of mountains shrouded in a lush snow blanket.



Skiing, snowboarding and many other sports activities have become available throughout Russia. Now you can relax in any ski area in a huge country.

Oh, great Russia! What is fraught with this large and beautiful country? Everyone will find something to their liking here. Professionals, amateurs, new ones - everyone can find their own sport for themselves and engage in it. There is no better place to practice winter sports than Russia. Numerous tours will not let bored adults or children. It is difficult to imagine a more communicative place for life, development and work.

Ski resort "Spas-Kamenka"

This is a truly unique place in Russia. If you want to have a good time in a beautiful place, that is, the Spas-Kamenka ski resort, which offers you to find a lot of sports activities and relax with friends and family.

How is this resort complex different from others? The answer is simple: it is located in an ecologically clean area. It is famous for the fact that it is a village with cottages that are rented by visitors. Like the Alps, it is just as clean and beautiful.



The air here is transparent, and it seems that you are in a dream. This is how beautiful the Spas-Kamenka ski resort is. In summer, vacationers can enjoy the fresh air and breathtaking views. Also in the summer you can go on a picnic or hiking with friends or family in the mountains. Well, winter is fraught with many sports entertainment. The village of Spas-Kamenka turns into a ski resort and invites snow lovers to visit. Everyone will like it here!

Snowpark

Snow park "Spas-Kamenka" is located in the rented village. This is a great place to go skiing or snowboarding. And at the foot of the mountain you can rent a cottage for housing, which is specially equipped in the Alpine style. You can also rent a gazebo, which already has a barbecue. The best part about this gazebo is that it is located on the lake. The park offers non-skiers to hire an instructor, and for lovers an excellent track for tubing, snowboarding or skiing. Not everyone goes on vacation with their equipment, so the complex offers to rent it. Having visited the Snow Park "Spas-Kamenka" once, you will definitely want to return there. You will be satisfied!

Relaxation

Ski holidays in "Spas-Kamenka" an opportunity to have a good time. The resort has at least 3 slopes, each of which is from 100 to 300 meters long. There is also a tubing track that is 200 meters away. Well, the length of the slopes reaches 40 m. By the way, there are really long ski slopes. This fully meets the needs of fans of extreme sports.



The sports complex took care of everything. There is a special map on which the tracks for professionals, amateurs and, of course, students are indicated. The latter can go skating school right there while relaxing at the resort. In order to rent equipment, you need to find a specially prepared office for this. Here, everyone can choose for themselves special equipment, skis and other necessary things. Want to get an unforgettable ski vacation? Then to you here!

Reviews

"Spas-Kamenka" reviews are mostly positive. Ask where you can relax with your family? The answer is simple: at the Spas-Kamenka resort. Dozens of people who visited here were delighted with the service, not to mention the beautiful nature and clean fresh air.



Many people who come here do not want to leave this amazing place, they believe that this is an ideal environment for living. People sadly leave this area, promising themselves to be sure to return here. One cannot help but love the purity of this complex. So say everyone who has been here at least once.

The guests of the complex warmly talk about how their children had fun riding in a sleigh. Young lovers recall how pleasant it was to go down together on a snowboard from one of the slopes. And most men say that fishing in a transparent lake is just a pleasure, because even fish can be seen there. They are happy to take their gear and sit with fishing rods on the shore.

Of course, how can tourists not like the barbecue and barbecue in the gazebo? Women, the hostesses, turn their attention most often to the situation in the cottages, they say that the interior is amazing. Inside you can find a fireplace, large spacious bright rooms, comfortable furniture.

Location

Getting to the Spas-Kamenka complex (Dmitrovsky district) is easy. It is located near Moscow, along the Dmitrov highway, 26 kilometers from the Moscow Ring Road. Ski resort "Spas-Kamenka" is also a sports complex. From the city of Moscow, it can be reached quickly and easily. For this, a person living in the city who decided to visit these places will spend only half an hour on the road. It is very convenient for Muscovites, as they value their precious time. Address: Dmitrovsky district, der. Spas-Kamenka, st. Embankment, d. 2.



If you decide to go, then do not put off. There are not many vacations, as you know. And if life is stormy, it takes place in Moscow, then there is nothing to think about. Urgently need to go to rest in these beautiful places. One has only to decide, and the road will take almost no time. Muscovites can forever forget about long trips and unbearable bustle on the roads. Half an hour - and here it is, a treasured and beautiful place.

About the village

The territory of the village occupied not less than 60 hectares and includes a huge clean lake and a ski resort. As for infrastructure, it is quite rich. Advantages of infrastructure - fitness center, cafe, diving club, restaurant, paintball club, playgrounds.

You can‚Äôt forget about fishing spots, an ice rink, a kindergarten, a hotel and even a petting zoo. All this is specially built for a comfortable pastime of vacationers. As for the cottages that can be rented, they are all in the same style. You can rent a house, for example, on weekends with friends, or on vacation with your family.

About 51 cottages were built for 6 or 8 people. At least 12 double rooms and one triple are offered to guests. Arriving by car can not worry, since there is an open guarded parking.

Many lovers of European cuisine are simply obliged to try it in a cafe, which is located in the administrative building. It works from 11:00 to 23:00 Moscow time. Vacationers who prefer to eat in a closed place can visit the restaurant. He is also in the administrative building. Restaurant opening hours are the same as the cafe.



For the convenience of tourists, domestic services were specially created, they include ironing and laundry. Well, those who prefer to wash, clean, iron and do other household chores on their own can rent an iron, an electric kettle, a refrigerator, a fan and other electrical appliances.

Speaking of amenities, it is worth noting that Internet services are provided almost everywhere, and they are free. From entertainment for lovely ladies there is karaoke, but for their men - billiards. Those who arrived for a long time and used to keep themselves in good shape can visit the fitness club.

Beach

Spas-Kamenka is not just a winter resort, it is also a summer recreation area. Summer is not inferior to winter in the number of visits to this place. It was for the summer hot days on the lake equipped beaches, gazebos, deck chairs and much more.

The lake attracts not only lovers of swimming, but also avid fishermen to go fishing, for example. Entrance to the beach is free, but if you want to use sunbeds, you will have to pay for such a pleasure.

Total

Nobody argues that there are a million places for rest on the planet. Of course, it is difficult to believe that the young resort "Spas-Kamenka" can be something more attractive than everyone else. But strangely enough, this is a piece of paradise, which is located near the bustling city of Moscow.





Do you have a desire to take friends and take a couple of summer days away from work and business on vacation at Spas-Kamenka, enjoy coziness and comfort, barbecue and a cool lake? This is not only a breath of fresh air, but also a drop of dew in the early morning that basks in the sun. So clean and beautiful this resort. In addition, it is multifunctional, as it works both in winter and in summer.

By the way, in the winter everyone wants to break away from their adventure. Go where the adrenaline boils. In winter, you need to enjoy every snowflake, have plenty of rest and ski. And again the Savior-Kamenka comes to the rescue.

Those who have already been here will never forget these wonderful places. They will definitely return here in order to breathe clean mountain air again. Well, for those who have gathered or are going to, but are postponing, for some unknown reason, a vacation trip to the Spass-Kamenka ski resort, we will give advice: do not delay!
