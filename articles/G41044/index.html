How can I spend my vacation? Cherepovets: recreation

In Cherepovets there are several places intended for active pastime, each of them has a different purpose. Some bases are designed for recreation with the whole family, while others are suitable only for anglers or hunters.

Base "Upper Fish"





The fishing base "Upper Rybinka" provides an opportunity for all fishermen and hunters to get a good rest. Cherepovets has a recreation center , equipped according to modern European requirements. In addition, this resort provides everyone with the opportunity to hold corporate and family events.

The tourist base is located twenty kilometers from Cherepovets, in a separate, protected area. Convenience lies in the fact that it is located on the shore of the Rybinsk reservoir, that is, everyone who wishes can wander along the shore here with his fishing rod or spinning.

The base consists of small wooden cottages where you can relax not only with your soul, but also with your body, because there is a bath complex and a huge number of sports grounds on the territory.

The recreation center "Sosnovka"

Holidays in Cherepovets can also be well spent at the Sosnovka base. Here you can relax not only with your friends or colleagues, but also with your whole family. Sosnovka offers all its guests the opportunity to hold corporate parties, weddings and any other holiday events. For convenience, the leaders of the recreation center provide all their visitors with the opportunity to order a professional presenter or DJ for their holiday. Recreation centers Cherepovets, as a rule, are located several tens of kilometers from the city.

Karpovo: family vacation

Cherepovets recreation centers provides those where you can relax with your family and usefully spend the weekend. The recreation center "Karpovo" is known among locals for its winter entertainments, rental of sports equipment and a sauna. Athletes and other guests come here from all over the region. Four guest houses are designed for eighty-six people.

"Karpovo" is used for a variety of mass sports events, as well as corporate events and family holidays.

The recreation center "Roshchino"





"Roshchino" is a relatively large base on which three hundred and sixty vacationers can simultaneously be accommodated. This resort also compares favorably with everyone else by the ability to purchase vouchers immediately at the base, without the need to travel around the city and look for points of sale.

Guests of the city are delighted with what Cherepovets offers them to spend their holidays. Recreation facilities located in the district are almost completely buried in the forests, so here you can enjoy breathing fresh air and enjoy hiking as much as possible.

All kinds of discos and celebrations are often held at Roshchino, and thanks to its proximity to the city, anyone can enjoy them. For those who are tired of hanging out in the air, the Roshchino base invites its vacationers to spend time in the fitness zone, gym or compete in a game of table tennis.

Holiday Village "Gorodishche"





Most locals also like to spend their time to good use and see what kind of vacation Cherepovets can offer them. Recreation facilities located in close proximity to the city are the best option in this case. The Gorodishche is located just eight kilometers from the city, on the picturesque shore of the Rybinsk Reservoir. Here you can not only hunt or engage in fishing, but also fry kebabs, play tennis and plenty of karaoke.
