What to bring from Paris: tourist guide

Paris is the most romantic capital in the world. Culinary Mecca, the center of world fashion and just the birthplace of the Eiffel Tower, very many people like this city. Going to visit the French, one cannot help but think: what can be brought from Paris? Indeed, without memorable purchases, any trip will become a little boring. In addition, relatives are always waiting for at least a modest, but still a gift from another country. Here is a detailed instruction on what souvenirs to bring from Paris, the magnificent capital of France.

Gourmet Souvenirs





Of course, in a country so famous for its culinary achievements, they are almost obsessed with food. So one of the simplest and most obvious tips about what to bring from Paris will be some kind of delicacy. Even inexpensive wine or cheese here are of enviable quality. Do you like more original solutions? Buy chocolate or cider. Great pastry and wonderful pasta cakes will also be great. The best and most delicious are made in the world famous Laduree confectionery. There they come in all possible colors and tastes, so your favorite sweet tooth will definitely not be disappointed after presenting such a souvenir.





In search of an answer to the question of what to bring from Paris, go to a large department store in the district of De Fans. There you can immediately buy everything that you would like.
In addition to sweets and alcohol, you can buy a can of Maille mustard. Choose its classic or Provencal variety, which is distinguished by red color. Or maybe you would like the blackcurrant option more? The brand's brand store is located at Madeleine Metro Station. Finally, champagne. Try to buy some small bottles to have a tasting at home. What is no way to remember a great vacation?

Cosmetics & Perfumes

Any woman who is not indifferent to aromas or decorative cosmetics will not think for a moment about what to bring from Paris. Of course, the French perfume! Elite high-quality brands are more affordable here. As a small perfume souvenir, you can buy a traditional soap from Provence. Its magical aroma and lavender extract make such a souvenir a great gift. The sophisticated fashionista will be pleased with the Hermes branded soap. Unusual aromas and high quality from a world-famous manufacturer of luxury goods will hardly disappoint anyone.
