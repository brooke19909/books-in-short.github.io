Crocheted sunflower: step-by-step knitting instructions, photo

Crocheted flowers are incomparably beautiful and naturalistic. They act as decorative interior items, serve as decoration of wardrobe details and simply delight our eyes. If you want to learn how to create knitted flowers yourself, this article is for you. In it, we will tell you how to easily and simply crochet a bright sunflower. Using our detailed descriptions with photos, even beginners will be able to make an interesting application with their own hands, spending a little time and materials.

The first stage: we buy the necessary and get to work

To create a crocheted sunflower, you will need to prepare the following materials and tools:

  • thin (180 m by 50 g) cotton threads of three colors: yellow, brown and green;
  • hook number 2;
  • scissors.

We start knitting a flower from the core:

  1. Take a brown thread and create an amigurumi ring. We make 3 air loops (VP) and 15 columns with one double crochet (C1H).
  2. We close the row with a connecting loop (SP).
  3. The second row of the sunflower core starts, like the first, with 3 VP and 1 1 (in the same base loop).
  4. Next, in each column of the base we knit 2 C1H.

  6. We close a number of joint ventures.

  8. The row is completed by a connecting loop (in the third loop of the initial base chain).
  9. Thanks to the additions, we count 48 columns. We fix the thread and cut it off.

Second stage: we knit lush petals

After making the core of the flower, we proceed to work on its petals:

  • We take the yellow thread and attach it to the workpiece. We carry out 14 VP and, skipping one loop of the base, we attach the chain to the core with the help of the joint venture.
  • Again we knit out 14 air loops and make the second petal, backing off 1 loop of the base.
  • According to this scheme, we work until the end of the row. As a result, we get 24 petals.

In the next row we do the strapping of the resulting arches.

The first and all other petals will be designed as follows. We take turns in the arch:

  • 2 single crochet;
  • 2 half crochets;
  • 2 single crochet
  • 2 double crochet posts
  • 2 single crochet
  • 2 half columns with one crochet;
  • 2 single crochet.

Between the petals in the loop of the base we perform a connecting half-column.

In the last row we do the petal binding. To do this, we carry out connecting half-columns in all base loops, and in the center of the petals we make a picot of three VPs, forming a sharp edge. We fix the thread, cut it off.

Congratulations, half the work is done! Our bright crocheted sunflower takes on recognizable shapes.

The third stage: knitting the stem and leaves

To make a crocheted sunflower stalk, take a green thread. We collect the desired number of air loops (we have 51 VP). Next, we tie the resulting chain on both sides with connecting half-columns. Set the workpiece aside (without cutting the thread) and proceed to knitting the leaves.

We will execute them using a simple scheme:

  1. First, we collect 15 air loops.
  2. In the second loop from the hook we make 1 single crochet.
  3. The next one is another.
  4. Next, we knit 2 half crochets, 2 double crochets, 2 double crochets, 2 single crochets, 2 half crochets and 2 single crochets.
  5. At the end of the row we knit two VP.
  6. We turn the workpiece and make the exact same strapping on the other side of the initial chain.

Fourth stage: assembly of the application

  • The finished leaf is processed along the edge with half crochets without a crochet.
  • From three air loops we make a leaf stalk. Do not forget to tie the stalk with half crochets without a crochet.
  • We attach the leaf to the stem using a connecting loop.
  • The second leaf is made by analogy with the first.
  • Attach it to the stem from the other side.
  • We tie the blank with another row of half-columns without a crochet.
  • We attach the stem to the flower, fasten and cut the thread.

Ideas for using the "sunflower" motif in the interior: crochet napkins

Beautiful crocheted sunflowers perfectly refresh any interior, making it cozier and more elegant. Unusual hot coasters, tablecloths, cushions and comfortable rugs with floral motifs, made by yourself, will radiate warmth and love and will become the main decorations of your home.

If you want to add bright accents to the interior of your living room or dining area, we suggest you knit an interesting sunflower napkin.

We will use a simple scheme. It should not cause difficulties for those who are just mastering crochet.

The sunflower napkin will be made of yarn of two colors, black and yellow, with a density of 280 m per 100 g. A hook No. 3 or No. 3,5 will suit it. You will also need scissors.

Knitting the center part of the napkin

Having prepared the necessary tools, we begin work.

  1. We take the black thread and make 6 VP, connecting them into a ring using a joint venture.
  2. In the second row, we knit three lifting stitches and in the same base loop a lush column consisting of two double crochets.
  3. Next, we make * 3 VP and a magnificent column of three double crochet *. The pattern * - * is repeated until the end of the row.
  4. We knit the third row using the pattern: 3 double crochet, 2 VP, 3 double crochet, 1 VP.
  5. In the fourth row, we alternate groups of three columns with one crochet and two air loops. At the end, we fix the thread and cut it off.
  6. For knitting the fifth row, we attach a yellow thread to the workpiece. In an arch of two loops of the previous row, we knit a single crochet, a picot of 4 VP and again a single crochet. Next, we perform an arch of 5 air loops.
  7. We work according to this scheme until the end of the row.
