Laminate and parquet floor cleaning

Many people wonder what to do with cleaning surfaces made of wood. Can Karcher SC 1020 (steam cleaner) be used in this case? Reviews of housewives who regularly use this device, confirm its versatility and harmlessness. However, there are some nuances of such cleaning.

In the kit there is a special nozzle for cleaning the floor, it is also suitable for the laminate. However, during the process, a strong steam stream must be avoided, otherwise the wooden surface may swell and deteriorate. To clean the waxed floor and parquet, covered with special mastic, a steam cleaner can not be used.

What kind of water to fill?

When using the steam cleaner for the first time, remember that detergents must not be added to the tank. The ideal liquid for its normal operation is plain water, and even better distilled. It does not contain impurities, therefore it does not form scale in the boiler. If tap water is used, the tank will eventually have to be cleaned.





In no case should you add perfumes, rainwater or other liquids to the steam cleaner. This may result in damage to the device or contamination of its internal parts.
