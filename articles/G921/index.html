Materials and Tools

Before learning how to pack a box in gift paper, decide on the set of necessary devices. You will need the following:

  • wrapping paper (thin, thick, corrugated, crepe, embossed, mother-of-pearl, decorative);
  • ruler or meter;
  • scissors;
  • double sided tape;
  • decorative elements (ribbons, bows, flowers, beads, thematic, for example, Christmas decorations).




In fact, nothing special is needed. It is important to choose the right combination of paper and decor. Do not overdo it. Everything should look harmonious and neat.

Material calculation

The range of decorative paper in which you can wrap gifts is huge now. Some options are cheap, others are much more expensive. In order not to overpay or, conversely, not to be in a situation where the purchased material was not enough, it is important to understand how to determine the right amount of paper to pack the box. This is done with the help of simple measurements and simple mathematical calculations.

If you have a round or oval box, proceed as follows:

  • attach a tailor's meter or ordinary thread (tape) to the side surface of the box and measure its length (in fact, a circle or oval);
  • measure the height of the box and lid;
  • determine the diameter of the base (cover);
  • proceed with the calculations: you will need a strip the length of the circumference of the lid and the height of the box taking into account the allowances (no more than 2 cm on each side, depending on the size of the box) and two squares with a side equal to the diameter of the circle of the lid, also taking into account the allowances;
  • fold the resulting dimensions.

If you have a square or rectangular box, proceed as follows:

  • measure the width of the box and the dimensions of the side parts;
  • determine the length of the entire surface, that is, sum the dimensions of all the faces along which you will wrap the box with paper;
  • perform the calculations: add the double height of the side edges to the width of the box - this will be the width of the required sheet; add to the size determined in step No. 2 the allowances for gluing - the length of the sheet was also calculated.

As you can see, the calculations are simple. If this math is still not very clear to you, just grab some newsprint, wrap the box around and, when unfolded, measure the material used.

Square box packing

If you figured out the calculation of the amount of paper and trained on the newspaper, then understanding how to pack a box in gift paper is not difficult. In fact, first you wrap the box around the entire central part of the surface so that the same supply of paper remains on both sides, and then carefully wrap the side elements. First, work with the top edge, then with the side edges. Last of all, double-fold the hem. Everything is very simple.





How to wrap a round shape

To pack the box in gift paper, you need to cut out several separate parts from the sheet, and then glue them. So, work as follows.

  1. Cut a strip for the side surface, taking into account the allowance for gluing and bending from the top and bottom (the calculation of the dimensions was given above).
  2. Cut two circles for the bottom and cover (bottom with stock, cover without).
  3. Strip the height of the lid with a bend allowance.
  4. Glue the circle to the bottom of the box, securing the curved allowances to the side.
  5. Glue the side strip by folding the upper allowance inside the open box.
  6. Fix the circle of the cover to the surface by folding the allowances down onto the side.
  7. Glue the side strip, also hiding the excess inside the cover.
  8. Decorate the box.
