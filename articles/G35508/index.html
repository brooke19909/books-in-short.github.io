Brateevsky cascade park - a green recreation area with a unique landscape

Today it is a well-maintained natural recreation area with an interesting landscape and several viewing platforms. What kind of entertainment does the park offer its guests? How to get to it?

The history of the creation of the recreation area



In the 1980s, active construction of residential districts in Maryino was carried out. On the site of the modern Brateevsky park then there was an unattractive wasteland, on which sand was actively mined. As a result of the excavation of a significant amount of soil, a gulf formed here, which existed until the beginning of the 2000s.

During the construction of the Lefortovo tunnel , it was decided to improve the Brateevskaya embankment. They brought soil that was released during the work. The bay was filled up, an original relief was formed with an abundance of terraces and hills. It was decided to turn this place into Brateevsky cascade park. The grand opening of the recreation area took place in 2006.

The first trees were planted on the territory, stairs with ramps, tiled walkways, lanterns and urns appeared. Quite quickly, the recreation area began to be popular among residents of the nearest housing estates. However, the park did not receive enough care. Quickly, the recreation area began to litter and lost its original picturesque appearance.

Improvement of Brateevsky park in 2016



In 2006, the cascade park on Brateevskaya Embankment survived its rebirth. As part of landscaping the green areas of the capital, stairs and paths were repaired here, street lamps were replaced, flowerbeds were destroyed. Brateevsky cascade park has become more beautiful and well-groomed. There are new benches, sports and playgrounds. Today it is a full-fledged family recreation area.

The total park area is 38.9 hectares. For walking, a convenient network of paths has been created. All stairs are equipped with safety ramps.

Leisure opportunities



The well-maintained Brateevskaya embankment is a favorite place for walks of young mothers with children of all ages. On neat paths it is convenient to walk with a stroller. Toddlers on their feet really like children's towns and playgrounds. Many students enjoy exercising with simulators or play soccer on the field with special grass.

Adults can join the sport and an active lifestyle in Brateevsky park. This recreation area is attractive due to its original landscape. From different elevated points of the park, you can take a completely new look at the surroundings. Beautification of the park continues; very soon cozy cafes and new attractions should appear here.

Interesting events

One of the highlights of 2016 was the international festival of fireworks. Brateevsky cascade park was chosen as one of the venues for this colorful performance. Recognized masters of pyrotechnics from various countries competed among themselves in their art. Unbelievably beautiful fireworks exploded in the evening Moscow sky, flowers bloomed and the most unexpected figures appeared.

During the event, many guests visited Brateevsky Cascade Park. A fireworks festival is planned for this year. It is possible that you can watch the pyrotechnic show again on Brateevskaya embankment.

Due to its unique landscape, this park resembles a giant open-air amphitheater. And this means that many spectators can enjoy the sight in the night sky without interfering with each other.

Where is the main entrance to the park?



You can get to the recreation area by personal or public transport. You should navigate to Borisovskie Prudy Street , it is on it that the main passage to the Brateevsky Cascade Park is located. Entrance to the recreation area is free and free. The building closest to the gates of the park has the address: Borisov Ponds 10. You can use it to travel on the navigator in your personal car.

If you decide to go by public transport, the easiest way is to use the subway. You need to get to the station "Borisovo", not far from it is Brateevsky cascade park. How to get to the recreation area after exiting the subway? Everything is very simple, you need to walk on the side of the Borisov Ponds. Soon you will see the entrance to the park.
