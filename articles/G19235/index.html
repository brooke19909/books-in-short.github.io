Description





The balneological resort of St. Constantine and Elena is the most beloved corner of tourists and guests of Bulgaria. The Sirius Beach Hotel 4 * is rightfully considered to be one of the components of the resort area and tourism industry of Bulgaria. The hotel complex, built in 2003, is comfortable, with modern buildings, service according to European standards. Everything is available here for lovers of a relaxing and cozy holiday away from the city and the noisy bustle.

Accommodation




Sirius Beach Hotel 4 * (St. Konstantin and Elena) is a large eight-story building with apartments that are different in their comfort and equipment. The number of rooms consists of 150 apartments. The rooms have a bed, telephone, satellite TV, air conditioning, minibar, in-room safe and a balcony with a view. The building has 2 elevators. The hotel complex operates on the concept of All Inclusive (all inclusive).

Service

Rooms are cleaned daily, towels are also changed every day, bed linen is changed twice a week or at the request of vacationers. Wi-Fi access is free. The reception is open 24 hours a day, you can order a taxi and ask to wake up, if necessary. The hotel staff is very cultural and welcoming. You can contact for any reason.

The price includes the use of outdoor pools and inside the complex, the ability to use an umbrella and deck chair with a mattress and a towel.

Nutrition





Meals at the hotel are carried out as a buffet, breakfast - 07: 30-10: 00 - includes fruits, cheeses and sausages, various bakery and confectionery products. You can also have breakfast in the pool, the bar is open from 10:00 to 11:00, and lunch from 12: 30-14: 30. International cuisine includes fresh salads and main dishes. In the snack bar on the beach from 13:00 to 15:30 you can try pizza, salads, fruits. After lunch from 16: 00-17: 00 lovers tea, coffee with cookies, dinner from 18: 30-21: 00 international cuisine with a vegetarian menu.

Themed evenings are held several times a month. Tea, coffee, mineral sparkling and still water, beer and various cocktails are offered. For those who wish, you can taste Bulgarian alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks.

Entertainment and sports




The entertainment complex for children is represented by playgrounds and attractions, a team of animators will offer various entertainment programs, children's theme nights, dance lessons and games on the beach, and, of course, a disco.
For lovers of sports and outdoor activities, the Sirius Beach 4 * hotel complex (Bulgaria) has a tennis court, fitness and aerobics rooms with an instructor, and sports grounds for volleyball and basketball.

There is a conference room for vacationers, for car owners there is a free 24-hour secure parking, beauty and spa salons, cafes and restaurants. Pets are not allowed in the hotel. For convenience of access to the hotel, you can order a transfer.
