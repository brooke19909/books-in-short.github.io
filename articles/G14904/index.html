What is collecting?

Collecting coins is a very exciting activity that can interest anyone. Over time, their value increases, especially if they are rare. In order to start collecting, you must first find rare coins, but this is not so simple. For example, going through your wallet, certainly not everyone will be able to find at least one such instance.





The collection of money found should be stored very carefully, that is, you need to purchase a special album with plastic sheets, which are divided into pockets. This is done so that the coins do not touch each other and are not rubbed. With the help of transparent sheets, the collection will be viewed very easily, and it can also be shown to friends without having to remove the coins once again.

Coins in the album must be accompanied by information, the following data must be indicated there:

  1. A type.
  2. Sizes.
  3. Design parties.
  4. Mint.
  5. Year of coinage.
  6. Alloy.

Materials needed by collectors

Many collectors consider numismatics not just a hobby, but an investment. Currently, coins are minted not from precious metals, as before, but from cheap alloys. Before you start making your own collection album, you should get the following materials:

  1. Felt tablecloth - it is needed in order to lay out coins on the table.
  2. Tweezers with rubber tips - you will need it for the accurate handling of valuable items.
  3. A bright lamp with white light - it will allow you to consider everything to the smallest detail.
  4. Album for coins - about it was written a little higher.
  5. Cotton gloves are pure white.
  6. Two loops are needed in order to carefully consider everything.
  7. Card catalog - it can be maintained simply in an Excel document, this is necessary in order to keep track of all its valuable copies.
  8. Several transparent cases - needed for transporting coins.
