How to organize a vacation in Karkaralinsk in nature?

How to organize a quality vacation in Karkaraly? The best solution here is to visit the local tourist complexes, which are concentrated in the district of the city. We will talk about them in the presented material.

"Miner"



Shakhtar Rest House (Karkaralinsk) is located in the middle of a picturesque forest in the immediate vicinity of the cleanest mountain lake. Near the recreation area there is a modern ski complex and cable car.

The guests of the institution have the opportunity to stay in one of the suites, junior suites or standard rooms, which are located in 3- and 4-storey buildings. Here, the clients of the holiday home are provided with full three meals a day.

In this place, the best conditions for sports enthusiasts are created. The complex has football, tennis and volleyball courts. For children, play areas with carousels, sandboxes, and all kinds of ladders are equipped.

The most curious clients of the holiday home have the opportunity to go on a fascinating tour accompanied by a guide. Employees of the institution will happily lead to the mountain lake Shaitankol, covered with numerous legends, show the caves in which primitive people lived.

Tas Bulak



To organize a vacation in Karkaralinsk with benefit, it is enough to visit the Tas Bulak complex. It is located in the middle of a birch-pine forest. There are several small mountain lakes nearby.

On the territory of the recreation area there are several buildings for accommodating visitors. Each of them is in close proximity to lakes and beaches, cafes, saunas, and restaurants.

What kind of entertainment does the Tas Bulak complex offer customers? For leisure activities, an outdoor pool, Russian steam rooms, a billiard room, and a shooting gallery are available here. Boats, catamarans, rollers and bicycles are rented on site.

The smallest visitors to the complex are available playgrounds, children's rooms. Entertainment and care for the kids is provided by experienced animators and nannies.

"Three caves"



Overlooking Karkaralinsk, recreation areas of the city, it is worth noting the Three Caves complex, which is located on the territory of the national park. The institution is surrounded by dense forests. Nearby are the rocks, in which there are caves of primitive people. Visitors to the recreation area are offered walking tours, visiting the sights of the region by car.

Clients of the establishment are available cozy wooden houses designed for 4 guests. How much does it cost to stay in the Three Caves complex, Karkaralinsk? Holiday homes offer accommodation prices from 10,000 rubles per person.

Zhartas



A good vacation in Karkaraly can be organized on the Zhartassky reservoir. Fans of fishing and hiking in the picturesque area love to come here.

Visitors to the recreation center "Zhartas" have the opportunity to comfortably accommodate in cozy residential buildings, which are designed for 400 people. Vacationers are offered four meals a day. Here you can not only relax, but also undergo treatment. On the territory of the complex are diagnostic rooms. Visitors are offered herbal medicine, acupuncture, inhalation, mud baths, massage treatments.

Zhosaly

The Zhosaly Recreation Area is known for its healing mineral waters. Those who come here need treatment for diseases of the digestive and nervous systems, respiratory organs, and the female and male genitalia.

Holidays in Karkaralinsk, in the sanatorium "Zhosaly", looks ideal for lovers of tourism. Nearby there is a lake filled with the same healing waters, where you can enjoy swimming, get a good tan.

As for entertainment, there are sports fields in the recreation area. Mushroom pickers and berry pickers have the opportunity to get out into the forest. There are also cozy cafes on the territory that allow you to enjoy your vacation in a relaxed atmosphere.

"Glade"

Continuing to survey Karkaralinsk, recreation areas of the region, it is worth noting such a popular place as "Glade". Visitors are offered accommodation in two-level wooden houses located in a picturesque area, not far from the mountain ranges. There is a covered porch, water, light. Near each house there is a barbecue and a place for a fire.

Near the recreation area "Glade" there is a reserve in which you can see deer, bison, deer in a natural habitat. Not far from the complex there is a museum where stuffed animals and unique animals that inhabit the Karkaraly district are represented.

Finally

So we examined the best recreation areas of the Karkaraly region. Naturally, we did not select all the places that are available for visiting. However, it is these objects that seem to be the best solution for organizing a quality outdoor recreation.
