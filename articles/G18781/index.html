Dubai safari: tour selection, car and guns, excursions, wild animals and unusual hunting

Mysterious and mysterious, a little frightening and bewitching with its beauty - the desert is one of the main attractions of the United Arab Emirates. If you have not been lucky enough to visit the kingdom of sand, be extremely careful - it is very easy to fall in love with him at first sight. Dubai safari is a bright and memorable entertainment for tourists. To visit the UAE and not go on this exciting journey - perhaps this can be considered a crime.

You are ready?

It is worth mentioning right away that a safari in Dubai is an entertainment exclusively for those who prefer an active holiday and cannot sit in a stuffy hotel room. Gold sand dunes await tourists from all over the world. Only here you can feel the adrenaline rush, breaking dunes on a comfortable and maneuverable SUV. Particular pleasure will be given in the evening in the desert, when in the rays of the setting sun you can look around at the sandy silk waves.

It is not surprising that travelers bring thousands of vivid photographs from the Dubai Safari tour. As part of the excursion program, tourists will be able to feel like locals, try on traditional costumes, ride a camel, and also enjoy all the charms of the life of nomads. And in the evening, at sunset, the master will skillfully decorate the body with exquisite mehendi patterns.

What to wear?

At first glance, it may seem that this issue is not appropriate or, at least, not so important as to pay priority attention to it. But, as practice shows, it is the wardrobe of an unprepared tourist that causes considerable discomfort during a safari in Dubai.

Many tour operators specializing in trips to the Emirates, offer to make an exciting journey through the desert. However, not many of them give recommendations for choosing clothes.

Tourists should remember that they will have to spend the whole day in the desert (some excursion programs are designed for the whole day), so when choosing clothes, you should focus on the variability of weather conditions in the desert. So, for example, in the morning and at night it can be quite cold, and in the afternoon there is a high probability of getting sunburn due to high air temperature.

Therefore, you should take a warm tracksuit and a headdress with you in case of gusty wind. During the day you should put on spacious and bright clothes. It is important to close the body as much as possible to protect it from the harmful effects of sunlight. As for shoes, lightweight, breathable, but completely enclosed sneakers or sneakers are the best option.

Briefly about travel

Dubai safaris are entertainment for strong and active tourists. It all starts with the fact that travelers are picked up from a comfortable hotel and taken to the nearest desert, located outside the city limits. The road will take about 60 minutes. After that, tourists change to jeeps specially prepared for trips along sand dunes. Typically, this is a Toyota Land Cruiser with a powerful inner frame (for protection against upheavals).

Arabs do not like measured and calm trips in the sand. They overcome the Golden Mountains assertively and extremely. For this reason, the car must be durable, reliable and hardy. Endurance and a healthy spine will be needed by the extremals themselves.

A walk through the dunes is not a test for tourists pampered by blissful idleness. This is a real trip for those who are tired of standard sightseeing programs with architectural and historical sights. A walk through the desert is quite extreme - many experience emotions comparable to those experienced during the Paris-Dakar rally. Therefore, people with a weak vestibular apparatus should not rely on an extremely pleasant experience. Unfortunately, motion sickness is one of the most common problems among tourists pampered by the sun. Also, pregnant women should not go to the desert - this can adversely affect their health. A desert jeep safari in Dubai is not just a sand trip. This is a real surge of adrenaline and a lot of impressions. Experienced travelers recommend that you do not overeat before the trip, as well as stop drinking strong drinks.

Helpful information

Jeep safaris are organized every day. You can find out the time of group gathering from the hotel administrator. As a rule, the duration of the tour is 24 hours. There are excursions that last 9 hours. In this case, tourists will spend the night at the hotel. But, as practice and numerous reviews of excursionists show, it is better to give preference to a daily trip, since it is a night spent in the desert that will give unforgettable emotions and vivid impressions.

The price includes:

  • Soft drinks.

For a fee, tourists will be offered low-alcohol drinks of local production, a camel ride, dressing in traditional costumes and much more.

Safari Park

Opened recently - in mid-December 2017. Almost 150 million dirhams were spent on its creation. The administration of the largest leisure center plans to populate more than 5 thousand animals by 2020.

The zoo is open daily from 9 am to 5 pm. The cost of tickets is different:

  • Without safari: 50 AED (adult ticket) and 20 units (children).
  • With a safari, the cost of visiting will be slightly higher: 85 dirhams for adult tourists and 30 dirhams for children.
  • Safari separately: for adults 50, for children - 20 units.

Dubai Safari Park consists of several areas: Safari Village, Arabian and Asian Village, picnic and hiking area, as well as African Village.

Safari Park Tour

First, tourists enter the Arab village - they are transported in small trains with open windows to the habitats of wild animals. Here you can meet a married couple of wolves, business ostriches, horned ungulates, etc. Gazelles lie right on the road, preventing tourists from following the route.

Then the excursionists enter the Asian village - here you have to move on foot. Here is the Theater. Tourists will be able to watch a couple of flashy gibbons, Himalayan bears and crocodiles.

Most of all expectations from the Safari zone - tourists will be able to ride on buses to the habitats of unique animals. Giraffes and lions, tigers and zebras, hippos and other species - a meeting with such representatives of the animal world will remain in the memory of tourists for a long time.
