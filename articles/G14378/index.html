Sun Princess 3 * (Marmaris, Turkey): hotel description and reviews of tourists

Residents of both Russia and other former Union republics have long been chosen by one of the most popular Turkish resorts - Marmaris. The reason is quite simple: a healthy climate and a clean warm sea here are perfectly complemented by a wide hotel base, which allows you to find accommodation in accordance with your tastes and financial capabilities, as well as a variety of entertainment for tourists of all ages. So, if the destination of your trip to Turkey is Marmaris, the hotel "San Princess 3 *" will be a good option for a budget holiday. We offer you to get to know this hotel better by finding out what it offers to its guests.





Where is the hotel located?

This hotel has a very favorable location - just a couple of kilometers from the center of Marmaris and three kilometers from the resort village of Icmeler. The hotel is located on the shores of the Aegean Sea. You will have to walk only 130 meters to the beach. From the nearest international airport, located in the city of Dalaman, the hotel Sun Princess 3 * is removed at a distance of 90 kilometers. So upon arrival at the air harbor you can reach the hotel in one and a half to two hours. By the way, for the convenience of guests, the hotel offers them a shuttle service to / from the airport for an additional fee. The need to inform the hotel in advance about the need for this service.

Sun Princess is a comfortable three-star hotel, perfect for families with children, as well as for fun youth companies. From the windows of the hotel you will have a magnificent view of the picturesque coast of the Aegean Sea, worthy of the artist‚Äôs brush. The hospitable and polite hotel staff will always be happy to help you with all your questions: organizes excursions, advises the best shops, bars, nightclubs, etc. If you want to go on an independent trip to the sights of Marmaris or other regions of Turkey, then the hotel in Sun Princess 3 * at your service there is a car rental. In a word, all the conditions for a comfortable stay of guests are created here.

Hotel Policies

In accordance with the internal rules of this three-star hotel, check-in of new arrivals takes place after 14:00. If you arrive at Sun Princess earlier, and your room has already been vacated by the previous guests and prepared by the hotel staff, then you will be put into it immediately. If the room has not yet been cleaned, you have the opportunity to leave your luggage at the reception and go for a snack, take a dip in the pool or take a walk in the surroundings. As soon as your number is ready, you will be immediately settled in it. As for departure, it is necessary to vacate the room before noon. At the same time, payment should be made for accommodation, as well as the use of additional services. If you have paid in advance for your stay in Sun Princess 3 * with a tour operator, then you will only have to pay extra for services not included in the price if you resorted to them. We accept cash as well as plastic cards of Visa and Mastercard systems .





Since a significant part of the guests of this hotel are family people, children of any age are allowed here. If you plan to put extra beds in your room for children, you should discuss this with the property in advance. Perhaps this service will be provided on a paid basis. As for people who prefer to spend their holidays without parting with their four-legged pets, then, in accordance with the rules of the Sun Princess 3 *, accommodation of such guests in the hotel is not allowed. Therefore, if you still want to go to Marmaris in the company of your pet, then you should look for another hotel.

Number of rooms

Hotel Sun Princess 3 * (Marmaris) offers tourists accommodation in one of 98 comfortable rooms, which are represented by standard rooms and suites. Disabled rooms are also available. The apartments in the hotel are clean, spacious and equipped with everything necessary for living. Also note that smoking is prohibited in all rooms. Each room has a balcony with wonderful views of the Aegean Sea or the surrounding landscape, a bathroom with a shower or bath, hairdryer and toiletries, cable TV, a minibar, air conditioning and a telephone. On the floor - ceramic coating. You can also use the safe for an extra charge. Rooms are cleaned daily, and bed linen and towels are changed three times a week. In addition, you can order a room service for a fee.

The hotel has several bars: in the lobby, near the pool, as well as an English pub. In the first, you can relax in a cozy atmosphere with a cup of tea, aromatic coffee or a glass of wine. Pool

On the territory of the Sun Princess Hotel 3 * there is a large outdoor pool. Near it there is a sun terrace with sun loungers and umbrellas, the use of which is free. Relaxing by the pool, you can enjoy refreshing and alcoholic drinks in the bar located here.

Sea beach

Despite the fact that the hotel is located on the second coastline, it has its own beach, the distance to which is only a hundred meters. Here, the services of guests of the Sun Princess Hotel 3 * (Marmaris) are provided with free sunbeds and umbrellas. The beach is sand and pebble. The entrance to the sea is gentle, without stones, which is very convenient for families who come on vacation with small children. There are plenty of water activities on the beach. So, here you can go canoeing, catamaran, water skiing, windsurfing, etc. The beach itself is clean, and the water is warm and pleasant.

The youngest guests of the hotel are not deprived of attention. So, for them there is a children's pool, as well as a mini club, and in the evenings mini-discos are held. Infrastructure

Hotel Sun Princess 3 * has the necessary infrastructure for a comfortable stay for guests. So, the reception desk is open twenty-four hours a day. Also, such a service can be very useful if you checked out of the hotel at noon on the day of departure, and your plane leaves only in the evening. Thus, you can leave things in the locker and continue to have fun.

Also on the territory of Sun Princess 3 * there are shops where you can buy souvenirs or things necessary for relaxation, a hairdresser, laundry and dry cleaning. In addition, if you want to rent a car or book a particular tour, this can also be done at the hotel. Sun Princess public areas have free Wi-Fi. In addition, the hotel has a medical office where an experienced doctor is always ready to assist you in case of illness.

The hotel also offers banquets, celebrations and business meetings. If you need to use a fax or printer, you can contact the reception.

Sun Princess 3 *: tourist reviews

It's no secret that today, most travelers, when choosing a hotel in a particular country, pay a lot of attention to studying the comments of people who have already been here. Therefore, we offer you a summary of the three-star hotel Marmaris "Sun Princess".





It should be noted right away that the reviews about this hotel are very controversial. Someone was completely satisfied with their stay here, while someone was literally outraged and insisted that he would never return here again. We will deal in order.

First of all, the vast majority of tourists were very disappointed with the fact that the hotel does not have its own beach, although it is indicated in the brochures of all tour operators that there is one at a distance of one hundred meters. However, in fact, no beach "Sun Princess" does not have. Therefore, going to the sea, you can settle down on a municipal beach or the beaches belonging to neighboring hotels. As a rule, you will have to pay extra for renting sun loungers and umbrellas.

As for the hotel itself and the rooms, then, according to many guests, the repair here was clearly carried out for a long time. So, the walls are ragged in some places, the furniture in the rooms is not new, sometimes some tourists leaked taps or there were problems with the air conditioner. Otherwise, the rooms in Sun Princess Marmaris 3 * turned out to be quite good: they are quite spacious, clean, and the balcony offers a pleasant view of the mountains, neighboring hotels or the sea. As for cleaning, here the opinions of tourists differ. So, someone claims that cleaning and changing linen and towels was carried out regularly. Other guests assure that similar procedures in their room were carried out no more than once a week.





Another controversial issue was the food in the hotel. Some tourists found the menu monotonous, the dishes are not very tasty, and the fruits and salads are weathered. According to them, it is necessary to come for lunch and dinner at the very opening of the restaurant, otherwise you may simply not have enough main meat dishes. They were also very unhappy with the fact that for the food mentioned above, which the cooks laid out in plates of guests in portions, they had to stand in long lines. Another part of the vacationers found the menu quite acceptable for a three-star hotel.

Perhaps the overwhelming majority of our compatriots considered its location to be the main indisputable advantage of the Sun Princess Hotel. So, the sea can be reached in just five minutes, nearby there are a lot of restaurants, shops, souvenir shops and tour agencies. Also, the hotel daily organized a free shuttle service to the famous street of bars in Marmaris, which all lovers of nightlife are simply obligated to visit.
