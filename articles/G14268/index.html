"Dzhubga", water park - a guide for tourists

The Tuapse district has the largest water park in the region called Dzhubga. Its territory allows you to simultaneously receive up to two thousand visitors. There are 14 different sizes of water slides on it. Unique water rides are available. Dzhubga is a water park for which a Turkish design and construction firm created all this splendor.



Now we will conduct a short tour of the most popular water attractions.

  1. The flying hill “UFIL Fling Bords”, consisting of a large number of steep bends, unexpected ascents and descents. It contributes to the release of excessive adrenaline and is very high for fans of extreme descents.
  2. Larger Tsunami funnel. On it you need to slide from a height of ten meters on a special boat, while the movement is somewhat reminiscent of the swing of a pendulum of an ancient clock. It’s just breathtaking on this swing, and this attraction is rightfully one of the most difficult in the water park. Admission to it is allowed only to adults, several rescuers are immediately watching the work. Dzhubga is a water park that cares about the safety of its visitors.
  3. Water slide "Big Hall" with a diameter of two meters and a length of 112 meters. Here the visitor moves through a black tunnel, glowing with all the colors of the rainbow. This creates a great effect, still adding a little thrill. The Big Hall, like the Tsunami, is intended for the most determined and desperate. You especially realize this when after 30 meters you reach a speed of 70 km / h.
  4. Attraction "Kamikaze", which is the most high-speed slide.
  5. Two calmer and softer waterslides - “Rafting slide” and “Body slide”. They offer vacationers calmer turns.
  6. “Dzhubga”, an aqua park in the Tuapse district, also attracts with the “Space Boat” water attraction, which combines two slides - “Space Hole” and “Black Hole”. The design is unique in that it uses two or single inflatable boats on this slide. The descent from the top begins calmly, then continues in boats, and ends with a dive into a trap or pool.
  7. Visitors get the most vivid impressions by taking a ride on the Royal Cobra waterslide, which is quite an extreme attraction and is a new leap in the entertainment industry.

"Dzhubga" - a water park, which took care of entertainment for children. For them, children's pools and special children's slides are equipped .



But this is far from all that the Dzhubga water park boasts. Photos taken from a bird's eye view show us the four pools available here. The largest of them has a length of 0.5 km and is called the “Lazy River". Waves are artificially generated in one of the pools, and a small storm or wave height can be ordered from the operator.

The water park has taken all measures to comply with all requirements for clean water and its hygiene. Constantly functioning filters with the necessary components. Near the establishment there is a free car park, which can accommodate up to 500 cars. Inside there is a pizzeria and a cocktail bar.

Opening hours of "Dzhubgy" - from 10:00 to 24:00.
