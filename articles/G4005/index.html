How to make a little unit for a year with your own hands from napkins

The most popular figures in the form of numbers are created from corrugated, tissue paper or ordinary colored napkins. Before making a large unit, you need to prepare the necessary materials:

  • cardboard or expanded polystyrene;
  • marker;
  • centimeter;
  • scissors;
  • thin galvanized or floristic wire;
  • paper napkins;
  • masking tape;
  • stapler;
  • glue gun.

A large cardboard box is suitable for creating a product: a volume unit will be formed from it. From a sheet of polystyrene foam, you can cut out a flat figure and decorate it using the same technique.

Cut the blank from cardboard:

How to make paper flowers

Now it‚Äôs time to prepare a decor for a napkin. If you are using tissue or corrugated paper, take 2-3 rolls. Napkins will need several packs, depending on how voluminous you make the flowers.

  1. Take napkins and fold several times. The more layers, the more fluffy the bud will be.
  2. Cut a circle with a diameter of 2.5-3 cm. The size of the flowers can be increased, but you should focus on the width of the figure so that the blanks can be placed in even rows, but this is not necessary.
  3. Fasten the circles from the napkins in the center with a stapler. If you use expanded polystyrene instead of cardboard, fix the center of the buds not with a stapler, but with a thin floral wire. Its edges are twisted on the back side and serve to fix the flower on the number.
  4. If desired, cut each layer, shortening it by 2-3 mm, so that the flower turns out smooth.
  5. Start pulling the edges of each napkin layer toward the center, one after the other, moving in a circle. It should be a fluffy bud. Make the required number of colors, focusing on the dimensions of the cardboard blank.

It remains to glue the buds to the base. You can use different shades of napkins and combine them to create different patterns. Now you know how to make a little unit for a year with your own hands with flowers from napkins. But there are several more options for the design of such crafts.

Scrapbooking

As one of the ways to decorate the workpiece, you can offer decorating with flowers used for scrapbooking. Buying such an ornament in a store will be too expensive, therefore it is better to make it yourself using ordinary wet wipes. They must be dried before use.

For work you will need:

  • a stencil of cardboard in the form of a flower with five petals;
  • scissors;
  • dried wet wipes;
  • glue gun;
  • candle;
  • artificial stamens;
  • paint.

First, cut out several blanks for the petals using a stencil. Using stencils of different sizes, you can make flowers very similar to natural ones. No need to try to make the edges perfectly even - so the buds will look more natural. Then dilute the paint in a small container, and, having achieved the desired color, lower the blanks into it one after another, wring them out and completely dry. Use a hairdryer to speed up drying. Before assembly, scorch the edges of the petals above the candle to bend them to the center. Use the center of the flame so as not to burn the edges and spoil the flowers.



Assembling flowers and product design

When all the petals are processed, cut a hole in the central part of each blank and, after collecting 2-3 blanks together, place the stamens in the center and spread them to the sides. Make the right amount of flowers. It is not necessary to glue the cardboard base completely. If you prime the blank and cover it with white paint, you can make a composition of flowers for scrapbooking in the center, and the rest of the design in the same technique, but with other objects. Use brightly colored paper, unusual cotton fabric, wood and metal elements, ribbons and beads.
