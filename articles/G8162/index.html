TVs, laptops, tablets, smartphones DEXP: reviews

The Russian company "Dexp" is located in the city of Vladivostok and was founded by a group of engineers. Initially, the company was engaged exclusively in the assembly of personal computers. The first Dexp laptops the world saw in 2000. They differed in high performance and prices were reasonable.

However, great competition in the market constantly forced the company "Dexp" to improve the quality of its goods. Soon tablets were released that worked on quad-core processors. Also today there is a large selection of smartphones. In addition, it should be noted that the development of personal computers "Dexp" continues.





Features smartphones "Dexp"

Many DEXP smartphones get good reviews due to their compactness. Their cases are made of plastic, and this should be taken into account. The touch panels are quite comfortable, and you can control the device with great comfort. Smartphones work on two cards. In order to save energy, the model can be put into standby mode.

The screen resolution of smartphones is on average 1280 by 720 pixels. Installed camcorders allow you to shoot in good quality. Additionally, you can take photos even in the dark. According to the software, smartphones of the DEXP Ixion series receive positive reviews. All the main functions in the devices are present.





Reviews about the smartphone "Dexp Ixion 5"

These smartphones DEXP reviews have the most diverse. Many buyers praised this model for its design. It turned out to be quite compact and weighs a little. As a result, you can use it with great comfort. On the functional side, this model has both obvious advantages and certain disadvantages.

First of all, it should be noted a convenient calendar that allows you to take notes and control your daily affairs. DEXP Ixion-5 has bad reviews only because of software. It is very difficult to work with some office programs. This is due to the fact that the amount of RAM is too small.





Reviews about the phone "Dexp Ixion XL"

The indicated DEXP phones get positive reviews for their high graphics performance. The interface is pretty nice, and the video device reproduces in good quality. However, DEXP phones receive bad reviews from many consumers. They are usually associated with a weak camera. It is not suitable for recording video, and this should be taken into account.

The graphics accelerator in this model has a series of "Mali 400". The amount of RAM is enough to work with many programs at a decent speed. There are no problems with games on this smartphone either. As a result, we can say that this model is more successful than the previous modification.

TV line

TVs DEXP reviews are diverse, and if we talk about the new line, they are produced, as a rule, in silver and black colors. By design, the models are quite different, and if necessary, you can always find the right configuration. Screen resolution is on average at 1920 by 1080 pixels.

Thus, the picture is pretty beautiful, and you can watch dynamic scenes with great pleasure. About DEXP TVs, negative reviews are most often seen due to a weak speaker system. There may also be some problems setting up TVs. By default, the brightness of many models is overpriced. Of the benefits should be noted support for Smart TV.





Consumer opinion on the TV "Dexp LED 50A8000"

The reviews released by DEXP TVs deserve good thanks to their tuner. All major television formats it supports. Additionally, the user is able to connect this model to a personal computer at home without any problems. The input signal formats are supported by a wide variety. The user can change the screen settings.

The system reproduces all the main graphic files. The sound power of this model is at the level of 20 V. In turn, there is no subwoofer, and this is a minus. Codecs this TV supports many, as well as audio formats. On the rear panel, an antenna input and a composite connector are available. Additionally, there are headphone outputs.

Reviews about the TV "Dexp LED 42A8000"

The reviews made by DEXP TVs get mostly good. Some buyers appreciated this model for an interesting design. Additionally, the TV has a colorful backlight that gives it elegance. Of the shortcomings, it is worth noting the complicated channel setting. In some cases, the system comes into conflict with the tuner. The brightness parameter in this model is 300 cd per square meter. m. The diagonal of the screen on the TV is 106 cm. In turn, the resolution is at around 1920 by 1080 pixels. In general, this TV for home fits very well and provides a lot of opportunities for the user.





New tablets

In most cases, DEXP tablets deserve good reviews. Operating systems are installed on the device "Android" version 4.4. They are designed for quad-core processors. As a result, many models have 1 GB of RAM. For many programs this is enough. However, the performance of the device is sometimes not happy.

Support for Wi-Fi and bluetooth in all models is available. Video processor manufacturer provides a series of "Mali 400". Touch screens are capacitive and break quite rarely. DEXP tablets clearly deserve bad reviews for weak cases that do not tolerate even the slightest blows.

What do they say about the Dexp Ursus 7MV tablet?

This model is perfect for office workers. Its performance is good due to the powerful processor. At the same time, you can play games on the tablet. The touch screen is set quite high quality and responds perfectly to all touches. In addition, it should be noted the multifunctionality of the device. First of all, it is important to say about the various sensors that are installed in the device.

Additionally, there is a function of automatic screen orientation. You can connect the device to a personal computer through a special connector. There are also headphone outs and a variety of headset. The battery in this model holds generally good energy. The battery capacity is exactly 2500 mAh.





What is interesting tablet "Dexp Ursus 10MV"?

This model is capable of boasting a high clock speed. The amount of RAM she has is 1 GB. The operating system is installed "Android" version 4.4. The memory card device supports up to 32 Hz. The screen resolution of this tablet is 1024 by 600 pixels. The video processor manufacturer provides a series of "Mali 400". The films are capable of reproducing films in high quality. The speaker and microphone on the tablet are built-in. Of the shortcomings, a weak camera should be noted. There are also certain problems with its configuration. In terms of functionality, this model does not stand out among other devices.

Company laptops

DEXP laptops receive the most diverse reviews. According to experts, many models are distinguished by a unique matrix manufacturing technology. The screen size of laptops is 15 inches, and the resolution is at 1366 by 768 pixels. The cover of the screens is glossy. There is no support for stereoscopic images.

Hard drives in devices have a memory capacity of 1000 GB. In turn, the drive configurations presented by the manufacturer are very diverse. Of the advantages, it should also be noted powerful batteries. Additionally, there are many headset jacks. In general, Dexp laptops are suitable for office workers.

Reviews about the laptop "Dexp Mars E108"

Mars E108 DEXP - laptop, reviews earned positive. Many buyers choose this model for its simplicity. The performance of this modification is good due to the high-quality Intel processor. He maintains the maximum frequency during operation at around 24000 MHz. The volume of the first level cache is exactly 1000 Kb. The type of network connector is provided by P45. The battery capacity is 4300 mAh. Headphone outputs on the side panel are available. The network adapter is installed built-in type, and its speed is 1000 MB per second.

Notebook Achilles G103

The DEXP-branded laptop reviews are pretty good. This model will appeal not only to office workers, but also to gamers. Her graphics settings are quite acceptable for this. Also on the laptop you can watch movies in good quality. The processor in the device has a series of "Intel Cor 7".

The parameter for automatic frequency increase is at the level of 6100 Kb. The graphics accelerator in the device uses a discrete type. The video chip in the laptop is installed in the Vidia class. In dimensions, this model is very compact and weighs only 2.3 kg. The batteries in this model are rated at 4300 mAh. Additionally, there are cells for six batteries. The network connector in the device is P45.





Summarizing

Given all of the above, we can definitely say that Dexp laptops deserve the attention of buyers. In turn, smartphones are not able to surprise people with something new. Thus, it is best for the consumer to give preference to more well-known brands.

If we talk about tablets, then the situation is ambiguous. Many models have good characteristics, but the owners note serious shortcomings. As a result, it is probably not worth recommending Deksp tablets to all people, as well as TVs.
