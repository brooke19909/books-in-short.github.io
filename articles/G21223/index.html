Design features

We will analyze the characteristics of the Redmi 3S phone later, but for now let's turn to the appearance of the device. When you look at it, you can absolutely say that we have a product from Xiaomi. Over the years, the company has developed a certain style. Smartphone hardware

So, take a look at the filling of our hero. The device has a 64-bit eight-core processor from Qualcomm with a clock frequency of 1.4 gigahertz. Now consider the amount of internal memory. Here, things are exactly the same as with RAM. There are versions with 16 and 32 gigabytes. Naturally, it is better to purchase 32 gigabytes. Hybrid slots are a real disease of modern manufacturers. But nothing can be done here - such a trend. Other features of the hardware include LTE support, an infrared port, a very good GPS module, and quite high-speed Wi-Fi and Bluetooth transmitters. Screen specifications

What will please the display of new items? The device has a five-inch IPS-screen, created using technology One Glass Solution. This approach allows you to achieve realistic colors, but when replacing the glass will have to change the entire screen, which significantly affects the affordability. But that is not the point. The screen resolution is 1280 by 720 pixels, which corresponds to the HD standard. This permission is already becoming standard for budget gadgets. Despite this, the pixel density is quite adequate. It is not possible to consider the individual elements of the matrix. The brightness margin is quite comfortable. We continue to consider Xiaomi Redmi 3S 32GB and the characteristics of its screen. The most obvious advantage of this display is its almost accurate color reproduction. The screen is covered with the latest Corning Gorilla Glass protective glass. There is also an anti-reflective coating and a special grease-repellent (oleophobic) layer. Main photomodule

So, what can the main camera Xiaomi Redmi 3S 32GB offer us? Its characteristics are far from flagship, but for the budget model it is quite appropriate. But overall, the quality of the pictures is acceptable. The standard camera application provides only automatic mode. No pampering with white balance and ISO. Clean machine. In addition to its main task (photographing), the main photomodule can write Full HD video at a frame rate of 30 per second. Quality is standard for a budget lens.
