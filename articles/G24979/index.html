Balitaya Resort Naklua 12 3 * (Thailand / Pattaya): photos and reviews of tourists

Today, Thailand is rightfully considered one of the most popular tourist countries. Chic resorts, breathtaking beauty of nature, rich culture and genuine hospitality are what every traveler will like. Quite popular among Russian tourists is the hotel Balitaya Resort Naklua 12 3 *, which is located in the picturesque Pattaya. And today, many people going on a trip are interested in the question of what this hotel has to offer. Are there any conditions for living with children? How far is the beach? What services does the hotel offer? This information will be useful to the reader.

Where is the hotel situated?

Of course, travelers are primarily interested in the question of where to find the Balitaya Resort Naklua 12 3 * hotel on the map. This cozy hotel is located in the northern part of Pattaya, at Naklua 12, just 200 meters from the city center - it can be easily reached even on foot. The area is calm, remote from the hustle and bustle of the city, and protected from noise, but, on the other hand, is located in close proximity to markets and interesting sights.





The international airport is at a distance of 119 km. This, of course, is far away, but the hotel always organizes a shuttle service for its guests, which will greatly simplify your trip.

Short general description of the hotel





What is the hotel complex Balitaya Resort Naklua 12 3 *? Thailand is a great place for any type of vacation, so every hotel offers a warm welcome and excellent living conditions.

The hotel is brand new - it was built in 2013, so it is equipped with modern technology. It consists of two buildings, decorated in a simple but elegant style with elements of traditional Thai decorations. On the floors there are rooms, catering establishments, as well as a swimming pool, a wellness center - in short, everything that is needed for a truly comfortable stay.

What rooms offer tourists?

The hotel complex consists of 81 deluxe and superior rooms. All rooms are spacious and bright, furnished with comfortable, new furniture, including a bed, tables, chairs, wardrobes and small nightstands. Naturally, there is an individual toilet and a bathroom where you can take a shower. Guests are provided with a selection of quality hygiene products, including soap, shampoos, shower gels, etc. Towels are changed every day during cleaning.

Naturally, the rooms also have a complete set of necessary household appliances. In particular, you can use a TV, hairdryer, mini fridge. There is also a free safe with an electric lock and a set for making drinks (electric kettle, mugs, tea, coffee).

Each room has a wireless Internet connection - the code can be obtained at the reception. By the way, this service is completely free.

Nutrition scheme in the territory of Balitaya Resort Naklua 12 3 * (Pattaya)





Of course, good nutrition is one of the important moments of relaxation. Travelers booking a room at the Balitaya Resort Naklua 12 3 * hotel can choose one or three full meals a day. In the main restaurant for travelers, buffet spreads are served , where everyone can choose their own delicious dishes. Naturally, here you will find an abundance of fresh, exotic fruits, as well as soups, meat and fish dishes, delicious cold drinks, coffee, etc.

This three-star hotel also offers tourists to visit two restaurants. The Main Restaurant offers a huge selection of traditional European dishes, while The Thai Restaurant serves a selection of the most popular and sophisticated Thai dishes. In the lobby of the hotel there is a small but cozy Cat's Eye bar, where you can order coffee, light snacks, soft and alcoholic drinks, as well as exotic cocktails.

In addition, next to the hotel there are several restaurants and cafes where you can dine at a very affordable price. And if you wish, there is always the opportunity to visit the local market, which is located just a few minutes away - here you can replenish fresh fruit and enjoy traditional Thai dishes.

Beach holidays for travelers





Hotel Balitaya Resort Naklua 12 is located on the second coastline - the distance to the beach is about 500 meters. Naturally, you can get there on foot or using public transport, but for the residents of the hotel there is a free shuttle service that will quickly take you to the seashore.

The beach here is public, open. Of course, you can use local umbrellas, sun loungers and beach towels, but at an additional cost. If you are interested in more active types of recreation, then here you can find something to your liking.

For example, you can play beach volleyball, ride a boat, banana, boat or water ski. These services are paid separately. Near the beach there are many cozy cafes where for a small price you can have a snack while enjoying breathtaking views of the sea.

Are there any conditions for relaxing with children?

Today, many parents travel the world with their children. Is Thailand (Pattaya) suitable for this purpose? Rest with a child here, of course, is possible. And this hotel offers some additional amenities that will make your vacation more comfortable.

For example, upon request, a baby cot will be delivered to your room - this service is completely free. And in the restaurant caring waiters will certainly offer a highchair for feeding a small guest. A qualified nanny works at the hotel territory - for a small fee you can leave your baby with her for several hours. And in the rooftop pool there is a special children's section where children can splash around and enjoy the sun's rays in complete safety, under the supervision of staff and parents.
