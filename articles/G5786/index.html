The best recreation center in Udmurtia on Kama: prices, reviews

Russia occupies a huge area, covers hundreds and thousands of cities. Among the many settlements there are real tourist pearls. Therefore, before looking for a vacation abroad, you can get acquainted with the places that your own country offers. For example, the Udmurt Republic, on the territory of which the Kama River flows . Features of nature, picturesque views, quiet walks along the shore - all this will seem fascinating, give new impressions and vivid emotions. By the way, it is worth noting that here it will be interesting for both lovers of beach holidays and active tourists who are fond of hunting and fishing.

In this article, we consider recreation centers on the Kama (Udmurtia): description, prices, location.

Kama

This river is considered the main tributary of the Volga. However, some argue that it is the latter that flows into Kama, and not vice versa. So it is huge and full of water, has a lot of tributaries.



A river originates in the village of Kuliga, where there are four streams that are sources. At the very beginning, the channel flows quite windingly, and becomes full-flowing lower, in the place where Vishera flows. There are supporters that this particular river should be the main one in Russia, but historically everything turned out differently.

In the lowlands, Kama spills over vast distances, forms sleeves. In some places, the width of the river reaches one kilometer.

One of the amazing and picturesque places opens where the Kama flows into the Volga - Tanaev floodplain meadows.

A variety of fish inhabit the waters, which attract lovers to sit here with a fishing rod. For example, crucian carp, sturgeon, bream, pike perch, sterlet, pike, catfish.

But not only a quiet and relaxing holiday offers this area. In the upper reaches of the river, where the water is faster, noisier, tourists gather for rafting and active spending time.

In addition, there is more than one recreation center. In Udmurtia you can really have a good rest and get acquainted with the diversity of nature of your native country.

"Pearl"

Seventy kilometers from Izhevsk, surrounded by a beautiful pine forest, is a cozy and modern place for tourists. "Pearl" is a recreation center in Udmurtia, which opened after a break a year ago. Today it is distinguished by comfortable housing and beautiful buildings.

A distinctive feature of this place is that the base is rented entirely to one company or family. Here you can hold a wedding, birthday, corporate party.

A huge house made in the form of a log house is provided for living. The team can accommodate up to fourteen people. There are five double rooms and one for two to four people. However, if you do not need to spend the night, then up to forty vacationers can comfortably accommodate at the base during the day.

There is no own dining room here, so they bring everything they need with them. On the one hand, this is not very convenient, on the other - if you do not like catering, then this is what you need.

If we talk about prices, then you need to consider that you get a territory where only your family or company will rest. Therefore, you have to pay 10,000 rubles per day. This is for fourteen people, for each next plus 300.

"Ryabinushka"

If you combine the concepts of the first and second options considered, you get a place that is perfect for relaxation both in a narrow circle of close people and a large team. Such options are offered by this recreation center (Udmurtia).

Ryabinushka is located twelve kilometers from Sarapul and twenty minutes from the ski resort. For housing, six houses with four and six double bedrooms are provided. In addition, each room is equipped with its own bathroom, shower with hot and cold water. Of household appliances - a refrigerator, TV, microwave, kettle.

The houses have a kitchen, which allows you to cook full lunches, breakfasts and dinners, since the base does not have its own dining room.

There are small houses for three to four people. Differ in that the toilet is on the street.

In addition to houses, tourists and vacationers can stay in private tents. There are special platforms for this.

It is a comfortable and attractive recreation center (Udmurtia). Prices per day - from 400 to 1000 rubles per person. But it is worth considering that basically whole houses or rooms are rented, the cost of which is from 2000 to 9600 per day.

Hotel "Gorlitsa"

Spend time on the banks of the Kama can be interesting and fun. It offers a variety of accommodation options. One of them is the Gorlitsa hotel complex. It can be considered an ideal place for a family with a small child: specially equipped play areas, environmentally friendly materials used in construction.

Cottages are offered on two floors. There is a kitchen equipped with everything necessary, a lounge, a modern bathroom and shower, bedrooms. If desired, you can visit the restaurant, which offers a menu consisting of dishes of different national cuisines.

The number of rooms includes standard and junior suites. On the territory there are various gazebos, a beautiful pond with carps, a children's town.

The daily cost is from 1900 rubles.





Club Hotel "Rainbow"

You can relax in the summer and in the winter in the comfortable rooms that Rainbow offers its guests. This is a club hotel with standard and luxury options. With the onset of cold weather, a comfortable atmosphere is maintained due to autonomous heating. The rooms on the ground floors are equipped with underfloor heating.

Own artesian well allows you to enjoy clean and safe water.

All rooms are equipped with modern furniture, it is possible to install extra beds.

It offers guests a spacious restaurant with a bar and dance floor. A great selection of dishes will please the most sophisticated gourmets.

As you can see, there are a variety of recreation centers in Udmurtia. Prices per day in the "Rainbow" start at 1300 rubles, so it can not be called one of the cheapest. However, it is characterized by high comfort and pleasant atmosphere.
