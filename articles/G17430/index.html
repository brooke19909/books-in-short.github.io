Types of Props



You can experiment with clothes, shoes, create costume images on the theme of the wedding, using the style of past years. Pictures of children



The next event after the wedding is the birthday of the child. Prepare the details for a children's photo shoot is quite realistic at home. In the pictures it is important to emphasize the charm of the baby, his first steps, a smile. Therefore, the background and things around it must be selected appropriate colors: pale pink, white, cream. Attributes in the photos will tell you how much you love your child. Near it you can put soft toys, cake, butterflies, ribbons, flowers. For pictures of older children, you can use costumes, for example, pirates, superheroes, princesses and other characters.

Specialist advice



When taking pictures of kids, it is worth considering the appropriate props for a photo shoot, such as toys, books. Equally interesting photographs can be taken both at home and in nature, especially if the weather is warm and sunny. It should be remembered that children do not pose and will not be able to stay in one place for a long time. But this is not so important. The most important thing is their immediacy, and only a good photographer will be able to convey all the children's naturalness of movements, facial expressions. Getting started, you need to play with the child.

How to make your own design

Props for a photo shoot to do on their own is quite easy. You can create three-dimensional letters for wedding photos, a suit for baby pictures, a crown. We will dwell on the latter in more detail. In order to get a beautiful golden rim, you need the following material. Wire, scissors, five gold leaves, paper, hair band. You will also need a thermal gun. First, you need to cut out the leaves of paper. You get a stencil eight centimeters long. It must be attached to the gold sheet and cut along the contour of the finished figure. Blanks can be obtained in different sizes, shapes, but be sure to make two larger than all, their length will be ten centimeters. We must wait until the thermal gun heats up, and with it, fix everything on the wire from the wrong side of the rim.

So that decor elements do not interfere with the ears, they must be mounted at a distance of eight centimeters from the edge on both sides. It is necessary to work carefully so that the gluing place is not noticeable. During the manufacture of the accessory, you must periodically try on the rim. In conclusion, you can add the product with beads. Thus, a wonderful props for a photo shoot comes out, and it takes a little time to create it. There is no need to purchase a finished item or rent it in order to make a beautiful photograph.
