A brief overview of the shopping center "Komsomoll" in Yekaterinburg

Entertainment

In addition to shopping, you can also have fun in the building of this shopping center. This opportunity is never missed by either children or adults. The visitors ‚Äôfavorite place at the Komsomoll shopping center (Yekaterinburg) is the RolikS cinema, where hundreds of guests visit every day. In addition to the children's area and the cinema in the Komsomall shopping center in Yekaterinburg, many visitors are entertained by the Five Stars family recreation club, where the bowling alley is located.

Catering establishments

During entertainment or active shopping, many visitors to the shopping center experience a feeling of hunger, which can be very easily satisfied by visiting catering establishments, including cafes and restaurants. Among them, those that offer fast service are especially popular: Juice Master, Sweet Cafe, Blinoff, and Mr. Burger Gourmet fans can stop by the Italian restaurant Nico Pizza, Della Mio, or Din Hall. Comedy Home Pub is also very popular, where during the whole time the establishments show commercials with the participation of Comedy Club residents





Mode of operation

Shopping and entertainment complex is open every day from 10 am to 10 pm. However, this mode applies exclusively to shops, as places for entertainment and catering have their own schedule. In particular, the RolikS cinema opens half an hour before the start of the entire complex, and the last show in it ends at one in the morning. Bowling opens at noon and works until 10 pm (Friday and Saturday - until six in the morning). As for the Karusel hypermarket, it can be visited until 11 p.m. on any day of the week.
