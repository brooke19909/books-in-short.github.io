Metal detector frame: setup, installation, instructions and reviews

A metal detector frame is very common today - at train stations, shops and clubs. Such devices work by creating an electromagnetic field, when in the case of the appearance of metal objects it is distorted. Stationary models, which are also called arched, are considered more perfect and reliable in operation.





What is the essence

The metal detector is placed in the aisle area or immediately after the door, or in a special fenced area. Power is supplied by alternating current. The installation features of these devices include a flat surface that does not emit vibrations and distance from any metal products. In general, the main way to protect crowded spaces is precisely a metal detector frame. The instruction of each model prescribes attention to a number of trifles regarding configuration and proper installation. All stationary models have a general principle of operation, and differences are expressed only in technical characteristics:

  • scale step size when setting sensitivity;
  • the possibility of using several detectors on the same network;
  • the possibility of operation in an external environment;
  • design.

Design Features

A metal detector is a device that detects and classifies a metal. The wire lies inside the transmission frame, and electric current moves along it - it creates an electromagnetic field. If some metal object is near the frame, the magnetic field will begin to change, as a result of which an electric current will be produced. He will create a magnetic field that will move in the opposite direction to the main field of the transmitter, and metal will be detected. The external metal detector frame also has a coil that neutralizes the effect of the transmitting coil as much as possible - it is called a receiver. When choosing this equipment, it is important to consider the quality of discrimination, that is, the detector must recognize and classify detected objects.

How does it work

How do metal detector frames work? In fact, the principle is simple: a radio signal is sent from one wall of the frame to another, which is then returned. But radio waves cannot pass through the metal, so when they collide with metal products, the reflected signal will return much faster. When the reflection time of the sent signal decreases, the device immediately triggers.





In this situation, the frames will respond to any metal products - from rivets on clothes to zippers on a bag. Setting the metal detector frame allows you to reduce the sensitivity of the device, which at the same time does not affect its effectiveness. It turns out that all large metal objects that will sweep through the frame will be immediately detected.

Is there any harm

Today, it is increasingly being said that a metal detector frame is harmful to human health. Experts say that the degree of harmfulness depends only on the principle of operation of a particular device. Most frames record changes in the magnetic field if metallic objects are introduced into it. When passing through the framework, an electromagnetic field penetrates us, but it is of low intensity. It turns out that the metal detector frame cannot be harmful.
