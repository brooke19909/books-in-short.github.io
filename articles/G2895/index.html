For a good rest, people often choose comfortable objects of ecotourism. Such preferences are easily explained to the urban dweller. During the day we are constantly accompanied by: bustle, working moments and traffic jams. Therefore, for a relaxing vacation or weekend, it is important to isolate yourself from annoying factors and accumulated worries. And where, if not in nature, you can bring your emotional state back to normal.

There are many unique tourist corners in Russia. One of these is the recreation center "Russian Hunt" (Penza). This is an amazing complex in a forest zone, bordering a picturesque pond. It operates year-round, is distinguished by high service and hospitality. Here, in a quiet and cozy atmosphere in the bosom of wildlife and the expanse of water, you can fully spend weekends and holidays.

A little about the complex





Suburban recreation "Russian Hunt" (Penza) has rightfully won the glory of the best tourist center in Russia. A mixed forest, an ecologically clean area, a hilly plain and magnificent nature - all this will give an unforgettable experience of a vacation. This place perfectly combines modernity and Russian traditions.

The hostel is constantly expanding the list of entertainment and active services. The complex is happy to offer its guests an extensive exciting weekend program with costumed events, dance evenings and children's parties. Here you can have fun celebrating any celebration: wedding, name day, corporate parties, anniversaries.

The well-groomed area is suitable for hiking, cycling and horse riding. Cozy gazebos with barbecue facilities and a view of the duck yard are built near the pond. There is an artificial waterfall, stone blocks and a beautiful bridge has been built that runs across the pond.

Number of rooms





Do you want to wake up to the murmur of water and birdsong? Then come to the complex "Russian Hunt" (Penza). For guests built wooden cottages and a small hotel consisting of 11 rooms. All residential buildings are decorated in the style of a Russian estate, combining modern engineering systems and natural materials.

Regardless of the selected category, you will be offered the whole range of necessary amenities: telephone, central communications, satellite TV, bathroom, air conditioning. If you wish, you can rent a VIP house near a reservoir and enjoy privacy. In the presence of a sauna, a swimming pool, a spacious living room with a fireplace in the improved cottage of the Russian Hunting camp (Penza).

Accommodation prices are affordable. So, for example, a VIP-house for two people will cost 5000 rubles. (day) including breakfast. Single room on weekends - from 2000 p., On weekdays - from 1300 p. You can rent a cottage for 12 hours, then the cost will be 50% less.

Active leisure





One of the most exciting activities in the Russian Hunting complex (Penza) is an off-road ride on ATVs and snowmobiles under the guidance of an experienced instructor. The route passes through picturesque forests and along the river Sura. Such a journey will not leave anyone indifferent and will be remembered for unforgettable and vivid moments.

Another no less interesting form of entertainment is the shooting range. For these purposes, there are two shooting ranges with artificial targets. A wide selection of weapons is provided: crossbows, slingshots, bows, air guns and rifles. An introductory briefing will be held for you and they will show you in detail how to shoot.

For conservative tourists - volleyball, badminton, paintball, billiards and ping pong games. At the rental point, you can rent bicycles or roller skates and ride in the surrounding area. In winter, skis and sleds are issued. All sports equipment and equipment - paid.

Business service





Increasingly, business travelers choose country hotels as the venue for their presentations and meetings, as fresh air and a relaxed atmosphere help them tune in to the right mood. The recreation center professionally organizes any corporate event, regardless of the number of people.

The order price starts from 1800 r. If desired, you will be served a buffet table and provide an assistant. Business guests will have a large conference room, banquet facilities, tea room and seasonal outdoor pools.

Wedding organization





Each couple in love wants to have their wedding bright and original. The complex "Russian Hunt" (Penza) not only professionally organize a celebration, but also provide a comfortable cottage for newlyweds, help creatively decorate the room and deliciously set the table. They will also develop a thematic program based on your wishes and arrange an enchanting disco.

Additional services
