Cyprus Legendary: Aphrodite's Bath - A Place Full of Charm

There are many mythological traditions associated with charming Cyprus. According to legend, there lived great heroes who left their mark on history, and the gods of ancient Greece. Sights of the island attract those who dream of real adventures and are eager to learn something new.

Picturesque grotto

The place where the goddess of love and beauty bathed is one of the most beautiful places that the island of Cyprus is famous for the whole world.

The bathhouse of Aphrodite is a secluded grotto covered with dense vegetation, where the charming wife of Hephaestus took baths. It was here, in the twilight, among the fragrant flowers and to the sounds of the quiet singing of birds, she washed herself in a natural spring, surrounded on all sides by thickets. The daily ritual of bathing with cold water gave the goddess strength and made her even more beautiful. It is believed that life-giving moisture erased all wrinkles and retained the female beauty of Aphrodite for several centuries.

Aphrodite's Bath (Cyprus): how to get there?

The official English name for the source is Baths of Aphrodite. Public transport does not go here, and getting by car is very easy. First you need to get to Polis, from there turn north and get to the village of Latchi on the B7 highway. The main thing is to look at the signs with the name of the bath.
