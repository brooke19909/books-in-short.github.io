How to clean the silicone case from yellowness: features and recommendations

The phone is a rather fragile item. Especially when it comes to modern smartphones. Therefore, for their savings, it was decided to use special covers. They can be made from various objects, including plastic and silicone. Over time, this accessory becomes dirty and requires maintenance. To clean it, care must be taken not to cause any damage.

Cleaning rules

How to clean the silicone case? This case has its advantages and disadvantages. Such an accessory looks attractive, provides protection to the device. The disadvantages include the fact that this cover absorbs dirt.

When cleaning, you should know that this material is very gentle and soft in its structure, so you should adhere to certain rules. How to clean the silicone case and not damage it will be discussed below.





When carrying out this procedure, you can not use:

  1. Abrasive materials, such as sandpaper. Under their influence, the cover will come in an unsightly state.
  2. Metal-coated sponges will also ruin the silicone case.
  3. Brushes that have stiff bristles.
  4. Items that may pierce silicone, such as needles or scissors.
  5. Cleaning products that include bleach.

As for sharp objects, they are not recommended for use, because they can pierce the silicone. If this happens, then dirt will enter the resulting cracks. As for chlorine, it causes a yellow pigment in silicone. Therefore, if you use such a tool when cleaning the product, it will turn yellow. Then it will look unattractive. In addition, from bleaching, silicone will also become cloudy.

It‚Äôs best to clean this case with such simple products as water and regular soap. It would be better if you take liquid soap or shampoo. A suitable dishwashing detergent is also suitable.

Soap

How to clean a silicone case using soap? In order to prepare a solution, you need to take warm water. Add liquid soap to it and shake it to make foam.





After that, the silicone case should be placed in water and left there for half an hour or an hour. The residence time of the cover in water depends on the degree of contamination. The dirtier it is, the longer it should be left in the water. After soaking, it is recommended to take a soft sponge or a rag. Then it‚Äôs worth cleaning places that are very dirty. Then rinse the cover under running water.

If after this cleaning procedure there are any dirt or stains, then the cleaning process can be repeated. After washing the cover, wipe it with a dry cloth. This is necessary so that the residue from dried drops or stains are not visible on it.





You should know that silicone cases are better to wash often. Since this material has the property of absorption. If any pollution is not cleaned for a long time, then getting rid of the stain will be problematic. In the case when the pollution is negligible, you can wipe the cover with a damp cloth. You can also use ordinary household soap.

Advice

How to clean the silicone case from complex dirt? In the case when washing the cover with soap and water did not bring results, you should go to other cleaning methods. They are more cardinal. But you should know that the silicone case is quite susceptible to chemical elements. Therefore, in case of damage, it will become impossible to use it for its intended purpose. It is especially problematic to get rid of traces such as a pen or felt-tip pen. Traces of exposure to food products such as coffee or tea are also not amenable to removal. Stains from clothing are not displayed. They can be made lighter. At the same time, it is completely impossible to withdraw them.





How to clear the yellowness of the silicone case? In stores, special cleaning products for silicone cases are presented. Their price is quite high. Given the fact that the cover itself has a rather low cost, it would be better to use improvised means for cleaning it than to buy special tools.

So how to clean the silicone case from yellowness? Below is a list of recommendations that will help restore the appearance of the accessory. After you can continue to use it for its intended purpose.

Stain removers

How to clean silicone phone case using bleach? As mentioned above, do not use household chemicals that contain bleach. And stain removers, which do not include this component, are suitable for cleaning the cover.





Such products include Vanish and Boss bleach. There are two ways to clean the cover using the above stain removers. Namely, prepare a solution with the addition of bleach and place a cover in it for a certain time. Or apply the product directly on a dirty surface and clean it with a small brush or sponge. You can use a toothbrush.

Soda

How to clean a transparent silicone case using soda? An effective remedy is ordinary baking soda. To prepare a solution using it, you need to take a small amount of this substance and add a little water. With this slurry, you can begin to clean the cover. Do not rub heavily, as this can damage the product. All actions should be carried out with soft movements.

Nail polish remover

How to clean a silicone case from dirt using nail polish remover? Another famous cleaning agent is nail polish remover. Acetone-free product should be used. A test is recommended first. To do this, take a small amount of liquid. Then apply with a cotton swab to the cover. Next, you should look at the reaction of silicone. If everything is in order, then you can continue to carry out cleaning activities. This method should not be done on silicone products that are painted in one color or another. Since the paint will peel off, the cover will lose its attractive appearance.

Toothpaste

Toothpaste is also an excellent cleanser. In order to carry out cleaning measures using this product, it will be enough to squeeze a small amount of paste onto the cover. Then clean the product with a toothbrush or sponge.





Then the silicone case should be washed well under running water. This must be done so that there are no stains left. Use toothpaste to clean the white covers. Colored objects can be damaged by toothpaste. You can also use paste to clean transparent cases.

Alcohol-containing solutions

How to clean the silicone case from the handle? In order to remove traces, it is necessary to use a solution that contains alcohol. You can also use two cleaning methods here. Namely, soak the cover in a solution that contains alcohol, or wipe it with a cloth that is moistened with alcohol. After soaking, rinse the cover in water. After washing, wipe it with a dry cloth.

It is worth knowing that denim leaves marks on silicone. Therefore, it is better to refuse contact of the cover with such material.
