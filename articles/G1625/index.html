Phuket - the most pathos resort of Thailand

The islands of Phuket and Samui attract tourists mainly with high-quality, beautiful beaches, a rich spa. Phuket is the largest island in Thailand, but at the same time the most developed tourist destination. Phuket has a very beautiful nature, a lot of beaches in the Bounty style, beautiful coral reefs, and well-developed infrastructure. On the west coast there are many pathos hotels, quite expensive, which belong to the most famous world hotel chains.





The most popular form of transport in Phuket among tourists is motorcycle taxi. On it you can travel inexpensively (40-50 THB) for short distances. A classic taxi costs ~ 600 THB per hour. For independent trips for short distances, tourists often rent scooters. They are inexpensive, do not consume a lot of gasoline and, in conditions of heavy traffic on the roads of crowded resort towns, behave better than other modes of transport. Also, rental of boats and boats for cruises and trips to small picturesque islands and cliffs, which managed to light up in more than a dozen box office films, is in demand on the islands.

How to choose a hotel in Phuket?

When choosing a resort hotel in Phuket, you should pay attention to your own trip goals. The fact is that Phuket is a rather diverse resort. Family travelers and leisurely travelers should avoid the Patong area whenever possible. This is a very active and noisy corner, it is also called "Little Pattaya." This is a paradise for shoppers and party-goers. Fans of a relaxing and quality holiday should pay attention to hotels on the west coast of Phuket. Also in the south of the island there is a mountain range, and in general the area in Phuket is quite hilly. Add to this the precarious state of some highways, the generally bad internet and the ridiculously tragic situation with driving rules and traditions. All these factors do not greatly contribute to the popularization of the island among fans of visiting attractions and traveling on rented cars. So it is advisable for such tourists to settle close to the sights they seek to visit. Given the size of the island, this is not at all difficult.

Hotel Baumanburi 3 * (Phuket, Patong) has a very good location. It is located in the southern part of Patong resort, on the first coastline, just 400 m from the beach.

Patong Beach

Hotel Baumanburi 3 * (Phuket) is very well located just 400 meters from Patong Beach. It is a public, urban beach. The south coast of Patong is a very popular place among young, cheerful and unassuming tourists. It is definitely not suitable for lovers of a secluded and quality sea holiday. This is the patrimony of lovers of active pastime, beach sports, fun. Patong Beach is very well equipped, has a developed infrastructure (cafes, supermarkets, cabins, toilets, parking lots, lifeguard stations, water ski rental, boards and scooters). But on the other hand, the beach line is almost always crowded, the sea near the coast is unclear, the coast is not very clean, but very noisy. In addition, the entire infrastructure on the beach is paid: sunbeds, umbrellas, mattresses need to be rented additionally.





For those who want more serene sea holidays, you should spend 15 minutes on a trip from the Baumanburi Hotel 3 * (Phuket) to Karon Beach. On the banks of the Karon there are many expensive chain hotels. This is the most luxurious and pathos beach in Phuket, it is flat, comfortable and very picturesque. The coast is covered with quartz sand; it differs in structure from usual. Such sand does not heat up much even in direct sunlight, but crunches underfoot like fresh snow. Karon Beach is also municipal, everyone can relax on it for free, but sun loungers, umbrellas and other amenities need to be rented for an additional fee.
