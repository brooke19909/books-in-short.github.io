Guest House "Singing in the Blackthorn", Anapa: description, rooms and reviews of tourists

Thousands of Russians in the summer seek to go to the sea in order to have a wonderful rest and at the same time improve their health. A reasonable alternative to Turkey, Egypt and other famous resorts is our Black Sea coast. Tourism is developing at a tremendous pace, which means that every year the service improves, new pensions and hotels appear.

One of such wonderful places where you can have a good vacation, many tourists consider the guest house “Singing in Blackthorn”. Anapa is a beautiful resort town, which is visited by more than a million tourists during the summer season. Many of them strive to find a quiet, cozy place so that it would be comfortable and safe to live with children there and at the same time it would not be very expensive to pay for it. It is to all these wishes that the “Singing in Blackthorn” guest house in Anapa fully meets the overview of which we offer in this article.



Location

One of the main principles by which almost all tourists choose a hotel in any resort is its location relative to the main infrastructure. For some people it is important that there is a transport interchange nearby, for others - the beach, for others - entertainment centers.

“Singing in Blackthorn” (Anapa) is a guest house that is more suitable for those who like walking, and for those who want to relax in a quiet corner, next to which there are shops, transport stops and any tourist services.

“Singing in the blackthorn” is just such a place. It is located in the cottage area of ​​Anapa, on Ternovaya Street, building No. 12. It is not very far from its center. You can walk there in no more than 15 minutes. The same goes for one of the major markets of the city. But the coast will have to go more than half an hour. Close enough to the guest house is only a bus stop (it is about 100 meters away), several cafes and shops. But on the other hand, tourists who have chosen “Singing in a Blackthorn” can enjoy relative peace and silence during their entire vacation, which can be quite difficult to find in high season.

How to get there

Easy enough to get to the guest house “Singing in Blackthorn”. Anapa has an airport in its infrastructure, where planes fly from all major Russian settlements.



From there to the mini-hotel it is most convenient to go by transfer. It needs to be ordered in advance. The service costs 500 rubles during the day and 600 rubles at night.

You can also come to Anapa by train. From the railway station to “Singing in Blackthorn” you can also order a shuttle service that costs 450 rubles during the day and 550 rubles at night.

Car enthusiasts can come to the guest house in a private car. Here they are waiting for free parking in front of the gate and paid (100 rubles per day) on the territory. The staff of “Singing in the Blackthorn” are ready to provide a transfer to their vacationers even in Krasnodar. And from the airport, and from the railway station it will cost 4000 rubles.

Description

“Singing in Blackthorn” (Anapa) is a guest house located almost in the center of the city, in one of its sleeping areas. Therefore, the hotel territory is small, and all infrastructure facilities are located very compactly. There are three residential buildings, a cozy patio with a chic playground, a spacious pool, a guarded parking lot and a beautiful rose garden.

Many tourists note in their reviews that the whole area is very well-groomed, clean and tidy. There is also a corner for sports games, tennis tables, a spacious gazebo, and the building has a library. The central building has a reception desk. Here you can use the safe, laundry room (for a fee), take a hairdryer, as well as an ironing board and iron. Also at the reception you can book a tour, call a doctor, rent a baby carriage, video equipment for a fee, and order tickets.



Rooms in building number 1

The guest house “Singing in Blackthorn” (Anapa) offers its guests excellent living conditions. Reviews of tourists about his room stock are only favorable. The guest house itself is small. In the three buildings available here (each in 3 floors) there are a total of 20 rooms. Their windows overlook the pool or the city. Each building has rooms of different categories, differing in size, equipment and design.

Building number 1 was built very first, but it is in excellent condition. Room categories are as follows:

- Economy for 2 or 3 guests. Equipment - a minimum set of high-quality furniture (beds, a table, a wardrobe, bedside tables), a small but well-functioning refrigerator, an old model TV, a properly functioning air conditioner. Shower, toilet are located on the floor.



- Standard for 3 or 4 people. The economy rooms differ in that the hygiene room is in the room. It is equipped with a washbasin, toilet and shower.

Rooms in building number 2

The private hotel “Singing in Blackthorn” can offer its guests very simple and fairly inexpensive living conditions in building No. 2. Room categories are as follows:

- Economy, designed for 1 guest. Equipment - a bed, a table, a wardrobe, a fan, a small refrigerator, a TV and a washbasin. All other amenities are located on the floor.

- Economy for 2 people. The equipment is the same as in a single room, and all the amenities here are on the block.

- Standard for 2 or 3 people. Equipment - a set of furniture, air conditioning, small refrigerator, armchair-bed, TV. These rooms have a hygiene room with a toilet, shower and washbasin.

Rooms in building number 3

This is the newest building, which received its first guests in 2015. Everything is done here according to European standards, because this building is the most popular. The rooms in this building are of the following categories:

- Economy improved, designed for 1 person. Equipment - good new furniture, air conditioning, modern TV, compact fridge, armchair-bed, electric kettle. Facilities are located on the floor.

- Junior suite for 2 people. These numbers are with a loggia and without. They are equipped with modern furniture and electrical appliances, equipped with a spacious hygiene room.



Nutrition

Rest for the most different tastes is offered by the guest house “Singing in Blackthorn”. Anapa is a southern city where you can see an abundance of fruits and vegetables almost all year round. Guests can purchase these and other products on the market, in the Magnit supermarket located near Ternovaya Street, or in small shops and prepare their own meals. For this purpose, the inn has a kitchen equipped with an electric stove, sink and microwave. All culinary specialists can use dishes, pots, pans for free here. Those who do not want to stand by the stove during their holidays can order breakfast, lunch and dinner from the hostess of the hotel for a fee. She cooks very tasty and varied, every day she offers something new. In addition, you can eat in the many cafes, restaurants and eateries of Anapa.

Leisure

A calm and at the same time varied vacation is provided by the guest house “Singing in Blackthorn”. Anapa is a very interesting town, proud of its attractions. You can see them by buying tours at the reception of the hotel. Transport drives off directly from the gates of “Thornies”, and returns here. Tours are carried out in such places:

- ostrich farm;

- mud volcano;

- winery;

- dolphinarium;

- dolmens;

- waterfalls;

- holy sources of the Caucasus;

- Taman;

- Novorossiysk;

- Sukko and Su-Psekh.

On-site guests can spend time near the pool, which has umbrellas and sun loungers. For sports lovers, tennis tables are set up in a separate corner of the courtyard. For the youngest guests in the hotel a wonderful children's animation is held.



Sea

Many tourists love beach vacations in Anapa. Mini-hotel "Singing in a blackthorn" in relation to the sea is not very convenient. From it to the sandy and pebble beaches go at least 30 minutes. Handouts report that the road lies through a children's park, but it takes only one part of the path, and the other will have to be overcome through the city streets. This is perhaps the most important inconvenience that hotel guests will have to face. On the beaches closest to it, the sea is clean, entry into the water is convenient for both kids and adults. On the beaches you can take umbrellas and sunbeds for a fee.

Guest House "Singing in a Blackthorn" in Anapa: reviews, prices

This private hotel is open from the first of May to the last of October. Room prices depend not only on their category, but also on the month for which they are rented. So, least of all, namely, only 300 rubles a day per person, in May you will have to pay for an economy room, 350 rubles for a standard, and 400 rubles for a junior suite.

In July, the same categories of rooms will cost respectively 500, 550 and 600 rubles, and in September-October - 350, 400 and 500 rubles. Such prices were in the 2016th year. What will be in the 2017th, you need to specify when booking. People who have been staying at the Singing in Blackthorn guesthouse for more than one year have commented that this is one of the best places to stay in Anapa for very reasonable money.



Hotel “Singing in Blackthorn” (Anapa, Russia): reviews

Regarding what services and what services are provided in this private hotel, the opinions of tourists are different. Most people like “Singing in Blackthorn” - a cute cozy corner of Anapa, where the atmosphere is always friendly, almost homely. The advantages noted in the reviews:

- low prices;

- the ability to order a transfer;

- comfortable rooms;

- high-quality cleaning;

- tasty food;

- animation for kids;

- interesting excursions;

- the presence of a pool.

But there are people who did not like this guest house. The shortcomings noted by them:

- far from the sea;

- not very close to the center and the market;

- not enough dishes in the kitchen;

- poorly working electric stove and microwave;

- limited pool time.
