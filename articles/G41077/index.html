TT Hotels Hydros Club 4 * (Turkey, Kemer): description, service, reviews

TT Hotels Hydros Club has all the necessary conditions for a comfortable stay and a diverse stay. The tourist complex has a favorable location. It is located in one of the most picturesque Turkish resorts in Kemer. Pure water, wonderful fresh air - these are exactly the conditions that a person needs to improve his own health. And a great addition to all natural wealth is a high level of service. It is these components that make TT Hotels Hydros so incredibly popular among tourists from all over the world.

General Description





On the territory of the entire hotel complex there is unlimited Internet, the speed of which allows you to perform absolutely any operation. Each client at any time can chat with family or solve business issues, so tourists stay in touch even on vacation. The administration takes care of each of its guests. Each room has everything you need for a perfect holiday. There is a TV, a small bar, a small kitchen is equipped, air conditioning. The room is furnished with very comfortable and modern furniture. The bathroom is also equipped with everything you need, there are bath accessories and sets of mini-cosmetics.

Each room has a balcony. It is quite spacious here, so you can comfortably sit down, enjoy the local horizons and just breathe fresh air for a pleasant conversation with family or friends. Catering is organized in restaurants, of which there are a sufficient number. The cooks of each restaurant offer a wonderful and very tasty menu. Vacationers can enjoy authentic dishes or try something classic. For guests who have arrived in their own car, a parking zone is equipped, so this problem will not cause inconvenience.

Service

On the territory of the complex TT Hotels Hydros (Turkey, Kemer) there is a wonderful spa offering a wide range of services that will help the guest to enjoy his vacation and feel complete relaxation. In addition, you can visit the sauna, jacuzzi and much more. Particular attention is drawn to massage sessions. And all of the above is just a small part of the services that are offered within the walls of this salon. For those people who are used to watching their figure, a fitness center and a wonderful gym work at the base. Here, professional trainers will give recommendations on how to properly monitor your figure and health. But if guests decide to completely relax, then you can spend your time playing mini-golf, table tennis or billiards.

Location

Hotel TT Hotels Hydros (Turkey, Kemer) is incredibly comfortable. It was erected at one of the most beautiful resorts. Clear water, fresh sea breezes, decent living conditions and excellent service - all this will please even the most fastidious guest. All these conditions are the very formula for the success of TT Hotels Hydros HV 1, which helps to attract more and more tourists every year.





A special highlight of this institution is its location. It is located in one of the quietest places on the coast. City landscapes surround it on one side, and quiet wildlife on the other. It is this area that gives travelers the opportunity to get to know the real Turkish atmosphere. Relaxing here, guests will feel the atmosphere of real hospitality and peace. Here peace and a certain harmony reigns, which adds to the vacation an even greater atmosphere of relaxation. Staying on the beach can be alternated with visiting all kinds of excursions, which are quite a lot. Thanks to this, you can learn new facts and expand your horizons quite well. The hotel was built in 1988, restored several times, the last reconstruction was in 2012.

The hotel complex consists of several buildings in which the apartments are located. The hotel accommodation fund is 341 rooms, each of which, without exception, is comfortably furnished and has all the conditions for a comfortable stay. Unfortunately, the hotel cannot accommodate your pets. Therefore, before you go on vacation, you need to take care of your home friend.

Accommodation





The room fund of the institution represents several types of apartments:

- Family Rooms. The room has everything you need. Two bedrooms and two bathrooms, so you can comfortably accommodate the whole family.

- Standard Rooms. The room accommodates a maximum of two people.

- Club Family Rooms. The maximum number of guests in this room is 4. The room has one bathroom and two bedrooms.

- Club Standard Rooms. The maximum number of guests is 3 people.

Room features

Each hotel room is cozy in its own way, has all the necessary furniture and can provide comfort to every guest. Each apartment has a bathroom with a shower, washbasin and other amenities. The toilet room includes a hairdryer, terry slippers and a bathrobe, as well as a set of cosmetics. To make the guest feel as comfortable as possible, the room has an air conditioning system, a TV, a telephone. There is a safe to keep calm for your expensive items. Each room has a balcony, where you can comfortably accommodate. Cleaning takes place every six days out of seven. Bed linen is changed three times a week. Towels are changed daily. Service TT Hotels Hydros, according to guests, deserves a high mark. The staff is friendly and helpful.

Infrastructure

The hotel area is ennobled, each client will be able to find some activity to taste. There are 3 bars, where you can always drink some delicious cocktails or arrange a light snack. There are also 2 outdoor pools, near which there is a relaxation area with sun loungers and umbrellas. Especially heat-loving can visit the indoor pool, where the water is always heated. There are several water slides for children, so they definitely will not be bored. Wi-Fi is working throughout the institution, you can always get access to the Internet. If the guest arrived without his own computer or tablet, then you can visit the Internet cafe, but this service is included in the range of paid offers. The complex has a currency exchange office. You can visit numerous shops, buy souvenirs or some new things in your own wardrobe. For business guests, there is a conference room for meetings or seminars. There is a parking zone for guests on their own car, where vehicles will always be safe. For everyone who monitors their appearance, there is a beauty salon, as well as the services of a doctor. There is a laundry room where you can tidy your own clothes at any time. Those who are accustomed to travel by car, but come from distant places, you can always rent any car, this will help the hotel administration.

The administration of the institution is very kind to young guests, so a number of services are provided for them. Children should enjoy the rest, and parents need help. Free services

The administration of the institution offers its customers a wide selection of free services that will help you to have fun and relax. Minigolf at TT Hotels Hydros is another favorite pastime for most guests.

Range of paid services
