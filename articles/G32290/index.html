Knitted swimwear: patterns, description, choice of yarn

Swimwear, knitted or crocheted, came into fashion a very long time, but they became popular recently. And the point is not at all that there were no craftswomen capable of creating them. Those who are thinking about how to knit a swimsuit, it is recommended to start with the following: purchase supplies. The best threads are those that contain elastane. Having rummaged in handicraft shops, you will find many types. Also on sale there are many magazines with patterns for knitting swimsuits.

Additional materials for knitting and assembling a swimsuit

  • Hat or linen gum. Tie it with the threads from which the swimsuit is tied. The gum has an uneven thickness, take the one that is thinner. These straps will more reliably support the bodice.
  • The vein is made of silicone. With her we will tie a swimsuit around the edge.
  • Inserts. You can not sew them, but if you have a small breast or you are not happy with its shape, then you need to use it.
  • Decorative elements for decoration.

The choice of yarn for knitting

Threads containing elastane retain their shape perfectly and are pleasant to the touch. They do not need to be stretched in the process of creativity. We knit as from plain yarn. Here are a few of them:

  • Cotton-stretch, producer "Kamteks". From a medium-thick thread, you will get both wonderful openwork swimwear and a flat cloth. It is very pleasant to the touch, often children's clothes are knitted from it.
  • Alize Lara- she is a little thicker. Also suitable for lace.
  • El-cotton is very similar to Lara.
  • Sofist by YarnArt.

So what yarn to knit a swimsuit? Decide for yourself! Just keep in mind that all threads containing elastane sit down during washing. To avoid unpleasant surprises on the beach, before knitting a swimsuit directly, tie a sample and wet it. Be sure to tie the edge of the finished product created from such threads with an elastic band!
