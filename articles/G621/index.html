Sberbank, "Economical" package of a mobile bank: reviews

"Mobile Bank" (tariff "Economical" and "Full") allows you to receive current data on the status of the current account and transactions with the card, make a variety of payments, and also, if necessary, block the card. In addition, you can repay loans and receive news from the Sberbank-online system.
