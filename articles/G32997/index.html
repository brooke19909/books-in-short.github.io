Cotton ear plugs

How to make earplugs from wool with your own hands? This option comes to mind first. After all, the named material is always in every house. Special cotton balls are also suitable, with which this procedure will be even easier to do. It does not matter if the cotton wool is sterile, as it is coated with polyethylene before use.

Two small pieces of it are needed, which will correspond to the coin in size. Circles are formed from them, so that it is more convenient to place the product in the auricle. Polyethylene is necessary so that small wool fibers do not remain in the ear after application, as this will lead to contamination. The cotton wool needs to be wrinkled tightly and wrapped with polyethylene, but a small oblong piece should be left ‚Äúfree" so that you can easily remove the earplugs.

After the option is tested, it is necessary to wrap the product with thread or elastic so that the cotton wool does not fall out of polyethylene. It is recommended to use such earplugs for a maximum of a week, since cotton wool quickly gets dirty and loses its shape. Wearing it too long will cause bacteria to grow in the ear.

Do you have old headphones?

If the old, broken headphones are at home, then the person is already halfway to find out what you can make ear plugs with your own hands. The optimal basis will be vacuum devices.

First, remove the silicone tips from the headphones using scissors or a knife. Sometimes, they can simply be pulled and removed. Then in the tip of the accessory you need to make a small hole that will be identical in diameter to the base of the silicone inserts. The hole is made with a pen without paste, or with tweezers. The base of nozzles is pushed into the resulting cavity. It is important that everything holds tight and snug against each other.

Such earplugs will last more than one year. They have the disadvantage that they do not completely muffle all the noise. A person ceases to react so sharply to them, but he still hears. Therefore, if the hearing is very sharp, and the mind is susceptible to volume fluctuations, then this option will not work.

Special sets

Having learned how to make earplugs with your own hands at home, some will change their mind about creating them. In this case, there are special kits for creating ear plugs that make the process much easier. The composition of such devices, as a rule, includes two bases, which are in different containers or bags. The material of each type must be divided in half, and then mix one with the other.

The resulting mass must be well crushed to get a homogeneous consistency. After the tool is inserted into the ear, where it should take the appropriate shape. It is enough to hold for 10 minutes, and then you can already take it out if necessary or leave it.

From toilet paper

Few people today know how to make earplugs yourself from toilet paper, and this is an effective way to muffle all the surrounding sounds. You need to take two small pieces of paper and roll them until you get a round shape. After they are moistened with water and squeezed so that they are slightly moist. This procedure is necessary, as dry pieces will remain in the ear canal and cause irritation and inflammation.

The funds received will be placed in the ear. If the size fits, then it makes no sense to add or remove pieces. The main rule in this method is that use is limited to once. Repeated use is strictly prohibited, as it causes the development of infections in the auricles.

Operation tips

Knowing how to make earplugs with your own hands, do not limit yourself to this information only. There are certain rules that are very important when using any ear plugs:

  1. Before starting the procedure, wash your hands thoroughly. This is best done with special antibacterial agents.
  2. During the placement of the ear plug, you need to place them at the beginning of the auditory canal.
  3. Keep materials and ready-made ear plugs, if their composition allows, in a clean place, protected from dust and dirt.
  4. To create funds, you can use all of these materials. But, of course, the best way is real earplugs or kits for constructing them. This is the safest way that does not require effort.

If, nevertheless, there is a need to make an adaptation on your own, you need to approach the procedure with all responsibility. Indeed, the health of the ears depends on the quality of the finished products.
