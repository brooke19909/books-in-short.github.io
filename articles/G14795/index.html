Where to go with children in November to relax: tips and reviews

Holidays in November are very popular among Russians. Many strive for a warm ocean, choose exotic tours, but someone likes to travel around Europe at this time. Today in a short time you can find yourself anywhere in the world, you just need to contact the operator and choose the right tour. If you are thinking about where to go with children in November, to relax with the whole family, then the recommendations presented below will help you make a choice.





November - time for relaxing with children

November is not the best time in Russia. Dullness, dampness, and somewhere already the frosts are depressing, drive into depression. It was at this time that Russian holidays and school holidays were held among children. All parents have a question: where to go with their children in November, where else can you catch the sun's rays, bask in the sun? Well, for some, the weather is not so important, many schoolchildren go on various excursions to Russian cities, in the most amazing places in the autumn holidays, to learn more about the history of their homeland, to see the sights, and it's just nice to spend time with their classmates . It is clear, after all, that when children travel together, they become more friendly. Joint trips of parents with children, a class teacher make the team more united. The choice of tours is huge both for personal trips and for groups, both in hot countries and in Russian cities.

Choose exotic countries. Why is this direction? The main thing is the sea and heat

All mummies who think about where to go with a child in early November, all experienced travelers are advised to go only to hot exotic countries. At this time, it is simply necessary to take the baby from our gray autumn to the sea. Extend the warm period, this will significantly improve health, prevent various colds, which at this time are already in full swing around Russia. In Thailand, for example, the warm high season is just beginning, hundreds of summer beach lovers flock here . In India, it is best to choose Goa, your child will enjoy running barefoot along the beach. The sand here is either bright yellow, or, conversely, white, almost like snow. If the child is small, it is best to stay in the southern resorts for as long as possible, extend the feeling of summer. Well, if you go to rest only during the autumn holidays, then pick up tours with a short flight, so that the road takes as little time as possible.





Thailand

For everyone who plans a vacation in November with a child, where to go is perhaps the first question. One of the popular destinations is Thailand. You can get acquainted with many tours and choose the most suitable for you. A lot of excursions, entertainment, it will immediately become clear that it will not be possible to cover everything, so decide on which of the islands you will go to. Phuket will delight the unexplored jungle, impenetrable forests. Everyone who has been here is advised to go on such a trip with the children. Enchanted forests will plunge them into the world of fairy tales and adventures. Children will remember their trips to unknown places for a long time. Fans of holidays and festivals are advised to stay in Pattaya. Here more lively entertainment - the village of elephants, a crocodile farm, the folk village of Glush Nooch. The richest excursion programs are implemented in Bangkok. A relaxing holiday is preferable on Koh Samui.

Vietnam

Vietnam has long recovered from the war almost 45 years ago, and today it has turned into a wonderful resort. The weather here is excellent all year round, so for those who decide where to go with a small child in November, our advice is Vietnam. Better to choose the southern regions of Vietnam. This is Nha Trang, Phan Thiet or Mui Ne, during this period the air temperature warms up to +32, and water to +25. Your child can run on the white sandy beach for a long time. Tourists who are here are advised to visit the wonderful Winperl Amusement Park with their children. You can spend time here from morning to evening. And, of course, it will be useful for everyone to try Vietnamese cuisine, famous for its spices, seafood and exotic fruits. Excursions to Halong Bay, to Ho Chi Minh City, to pearl farms, as well as to nature reserves are popular in Vietnam.

Maldives





If your vacation falls in the fall, and you want to relax with your family, you do not need to think for long about where to go with a baby in November. Choose the Maldives. It is in the fall that prices for tours are reduced, which allows many to go to these heavenly places. At this time, a wonderful period begins. The monsoon season ends, warm, dry weather sets. Air +30, water + 28 degrees. In the Maldives, people just relax, enjoy the sun, sea, exotic nature, fruits. Peace and quiet, the beaches are semi-desert. There are no noisy festivals, entertainment. Truly paradise a peaceful corner. Well, if someone wants to have fun, then you can go to the capital Male. Just on November 11, Republic Day is celebrated here with noisy fireworks and parades.

UAE

It is not easy to decide where to go with your child in November, but if you want to plunge into the warm summers, get a lot of pleasure from entertainment, go to the United Arab Emirates. At this time, wonderful weather reigns here. The heat is not so annoying, during the day the temperature rises to +30 degrees. Water in the sea is warm +28 degrees. The most attractive places for fun can be found in Dubai - dozens of amusement parks, water parks, dolphinarium, aquarium, oceanarium. Parents who have been here with their children are advised to visit the following places:

  • Safa Park. You can drive cars all day here, but not thoughtlessly. It is required to comply with traffic rules. Useful and informative for children.
  • Dubai Zoo. The territory is quite compact, so the children will not get tired when examining animals. In total, there are 1,500 animals, and very rare species are found.
  • Wonderland Park. A huge amusement park, consisting of three parts. You can spend here all day. Interesting for children of any age.
  • Wild Wadi Water Park. This is perhaps the most extreme water park in Dubai. 24 water slides interconnected reproduce the Wadi River. You can reach speeds of up to 80 km / h. The most expensive water park has clean pools, a unique treatment system, the water is heated to +28 degrees.

Hainan. China





When deciding on a family council the question of where to go with a child to relax in November, but not just lie on the beach, but see something informative and interesting, choose China, namely Hainan. Everyone who has been here during this period leaves only rave reviews. This tropical island is located in the very south of China, it is compared with Hawaii. By climatic conditions, they are really similar, as they are located at the same latitude. In October, the rainy season ends and a wonderful warm season sets in. In Hainan, you need to pay attention to the sights.

  • Butterfly Gorge. Children will be thrilled. Hundreds of exotic insects fly freely in the reserve park. Rare species from South America, Africa and Southeast Asia were brought here.
  • Park "End of the World". On the sandy beach there are many stone blocks, each of which represents some unusual figure. Very impressive sight.
  • Volcano Ma An. A sleeping volcano will have to climb the mountain along the steps hollowed out in real magma. Climbing up, you will find yourself on the observation deck, which around the perimeter bypasses the crater of the volcano. Fantastic sensations when viewed.
  • Nanshan is a Buddhist center. On the territory there are many temples of Buddha. The main statue is the golden thousand-armed Guanyin Buddha, all studded with precious stones.

Egypt





If you are considering budget options and are thinking where to go for a vacation with your child in November, go to Egypt. Many Russian families with children gather here for the autumn holidays. Rest will not seem boring to you. In Europe, the beach season at this time has already ended, and in Egypt there is still heat, the air warms up to +32, water - up to +28 degrees. It is better to book hotels in advance, since in November Arabs celebrate Ramadan. It may even happen that at the last moment there will be no free places in hotels. For families with children, it is best to choose Hurghada here. The beaches are sandy, the sea is warm, clean. Because of the wealth of the underwater world, the Red Sea is often called the "marine aquarium." It will be interesting to visit children on excursions to ancient monuments. Plunge into the atmosphere of the millennium past. It will add zest to relaxation and visiting a real oriental bazaar, be sure from shopping you will get real pleasure. Bright colors, the smell of stupefying seasonings, exotic dishes do not leave anyone indifferent.

If you find yourself in Egypt, you should definitely visit the ancient monuments of the era of the Pharaohs. In Cairo, for thousands of years, stands the famous figure of the Sphinx, the pyramid. Admire the beauty of the mosques of Sultan Hassan, Muhammad Ali, Al Rafai. Visit the National Museum, there you will see many amazing exhibits, a guide with interesting stories will lead you through all the halls.

GOA





Many people fly to Goa from Russia with children and, if possible, stay here all winter. Warm, mild climate allows you to forget about the fierce cold and for the whole winter to get rid of colds. The best season is October-November. Air temperature holds up to +32 degrees. Until January, nights can be a little cooler, you should take a windbreaker with you. However, it is not necessary to carry heavy luggage with you, you can buy everything you need here at inexpensive bazaars. The most attractive is the Anjun flea market. It takes several kilometers, stretches along the coast. You can buy almost everything here. Prices are actually penny. Merchants offer bedspreads, hats, scarves, handmade vests, a variety of figures of elephants, ashtrays, jewelry.

Goa can be called an atypical Indian state. They profess Christianity, which is still left by the Portuguese. In Panaji you can visit the most beautiful Catholic churches - St. Catherine‚Äôs Cathedral, the Church of the Immaculate Conception. Margao has a beautiful church of the Holy Spirit. Plantation trips are very popular in Goa. Children will be interested to see how various spices grow: cloves, coriander, cinnamon, caraway seeds, as well as exotic mango, papaya, and pineapple fruits.





Where to go with children in November in Russia?

During the autumn holidays, in many Russian schools, according to tradition, teachers and parents collect their children and take them out on tours of Russian cities in an organized manner. Our country is huge, it has something to see. Some also travel to friendly countries of the near abroad. What are the most popular destinations? Where to go with children on vacation in November? Here are the tips of experienced travelers, they offer the most popular and interesting destinations.

Saint Petersburg Everyone knows the generally accepted excursion places of St. Petersburg. I want to note that in addition to them, you can visit the theater with children or go to the circus. Unusual museums include the Water Museum. Excursions here are very interesting and informative. There is also a Cat Museum, a Toy Museum, a Puppet Museum.

Kaliningrad It is always attractive for its originality: ancient architecture, German streets on the outskirts of cities, fortresses. If you get to the Friedland Gate Museum of History and Local Lore , you can virtually stroll along the streets of old Koenigsberg. Worth a visit to the Museum of Military Equipment and Fortification. Of interest here are the Museum of the World Ocean, as well as the Amber Museum.

Traditional destinations during the autumn holidays, of course, are considered to be late on the Golden Ring. The cities nearest to Moscow are famous for their architectural ancient monuments and rich history. These are Suzdal, Tula, Vladimir, Sergiev Posad, Yaroslavl.

In the near abroad it is best to go with children to Minsk. A culture close to us, hospitable residents will make the trip unforgettable. But there is something to see in the Belarusian Republic. In addition to standard excursions to historical places and museums, you can go with children to the local zoo, not only our usual, but also exotic species of animals live here. For boys, it will be interesting to visit the Aviation Museum. The atmosphere here is unusual. You can not just admire the technique and listen to fascinating stories. It is allowed to try on suits of pilots and even sit on airplanes. The Oceanography Center operates in Minsk with interesting lectures. You can go to the water park "Swan" or to the entertainment centers "Cosmo", "Discovery", "Titan", "Tsargrad".
