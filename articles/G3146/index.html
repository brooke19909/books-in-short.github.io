How to disable trust payment on Beeline: basic methods

If the money on the phone balance is close to zero, but you can’t top up it, you can use the “Trust payment” service. The Beeline company provides the subscriber with an amount for a while, and then deducts it. But sometimes such a service is not needed. How to disable "Trust payment" on Beeline? There are several easy ways to do this.





Shutdown methods

To solve the problem of how to disable the "Trust payment" service, Beeline offers several options:

  1. Dial the number 0611, and then follow the prompts of the operator, so that you can connect or disconnect any service.
  2. Press * 141 * 0 #, which allows you to put a ban on connecting the service.
  3. Visit your personal account through the official website, where in the services section you can disable the "Trust payment".
  4. An unmistakable option is to visit the sales office, where specialists will help you solve the problem, but then you need to take your passport with you.

If someone else has access to the phone, then you can put a ban on the service so that there is not even an accidental connection to the service. You can disable the "Trust payment" of the home Internet. Beeline for this offers to visit your personal account, where the client can perform all necessary operations.





Disconnection via USSD-commands or personal account

Using short numbers to disable services is very convenient. After sending them, a message about disabling the service. You can use them repeatedly.

And how to disable “Trust payment” via the Internet on Beeline, we will tell you more. To do this, you must visit the operator’s official website, and then go to your personal account. You can access it by the number * 110 * 9 #. After that, the subscriber will have access to various services and options.

So, in order not to use the money in debt, you must visit the section "Connected Services". Each service has a “Disable” button. And after a while a message will be received about the completed operation. You can enter your personal account at any time of the day if there is Internet.

Office visit

One of the convenient ways is to visit the office, which is now very much in every city in the country. To solve the problem, just call the number and provide a passport. The operator will tell you how to disable the "Trust payment" on Beeline.

In the office, in addition, you can take booklets with numbers connecting and disconnecting services. Just a few keystrokes on the phone keys, and the desired service will always make communication more comfortable.

Disabling automatic service

For subscribers who constantly forget about replenishing their phone accounts, the operator has opened the Auto Payment service. It differs from the banking service, which offers automatic replenishment of the balance when it is reduced to a certain amount.

The service from Beeline involves the use of funds, like a trust payment service. The subscriber is automatically credited with money if the phone balance is less than 50 rubles. Amount and commission are determined by different factors.

Amortization

So, how to disable “Trust payment” on Beeline, you already know. To do this, just dial: * 141 * 10 #.

And to pay off the debt in a situation when you had to use the service, you just need to replenish the balance of the phone in the usual way. This can be done through the terminal, with a bank card or using an electronic wallet. It is enough to credit the required amount, since a commission is still charged. The operator deducts funds automatically, so no additional steps are required.





By the way, so that there is always money on the balance, you can connect auto payment from Sberbank. Then the funds will be received upon reaching a certain amount.

We repeat that subscribers should not have a question about how to disable “Trust payment” on Beeline, since it is very simple to do this. In addition to this service, customers are provided with other services that provide a comfortable connection.
