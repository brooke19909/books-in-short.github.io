Turning on the Mobile Bank

Everyone can find out the balance of a Sberbank card via SMS. But first, he will have to connect an option called "Mobile Bank". With its help, you can turn the phone into a convenient organizer to control your bank account.

At the moment, the connection of the service is as follows:

  1. Through an ATM. It is enough to find the appropriate service in ATM from Sberbank, and then follow the instructions on the screen.
  2. By phone call. The client needs to call 8 800 55 55 50 and wait for the operator to respond. After that, it is required to announce your intentions and name the requested data.
  3. Online. For this reception, the Sberbank Online service is used. After authorization in the system, the line "Mobile Bank" will be in the "Personal Account" of the user. If you click it and select "Enable", and then follow the prompts, you can activate the option in a few minutes.
  4. By contacting any branch of Sberbank. You need to have a passport and a phone with you. A citizen writes an application for connection and waits for a notification about the completion of the procedure.

Perhaps this is all. Now you can easily find out the balance of the Sberbank card via SMS.

SMS requests and invoice

Let's start with this option. It does not require any special knowledge and skills. A citizen just needs to write a message and send it to number 900. After the actions have been taken, a letter will be sent in response with the amount of money on the plastic attached to the number.
