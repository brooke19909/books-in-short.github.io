Hotel "Villa Plaza" (Lazarevskoye): recreation, photos and reviews of tourists

The picturesque village of Lazarevskoye is the famous sunny resort on the Black Sea, located an hour away (by train) from the center of Sochi. This place is notable for its favorable subtropical climate, excellent ecology and reasonable cost. Unlike Sochi, here you can fully relax without spending fabulous money on hotel accommodation. In addition, the village is calm, there is no fuss and constant crowding inherent in the metropolis.

However, the resort cannot be called estranged from civilization; many recreational facilities, clubs and restaurants have been built on the territory. Year-round for tourists, doors are open in the best pensions and hotels. The hotel complex "Villa Plaza" (Lazarevskoye) invites you to comfortably stay. A magnificent recreational institution right on the sea coast is surrounded by a flowering tropical park and filled with the atmosphere of a holiday.

general information

The creamy five-story building of the family -run Villa Plaza Hotel (Lazarevskoye), the photo of which can be seen in the article, rises beautifully above the promenade. The complex occupies high positions in the rating of hotels of this resort. All vacationers are greeted with a friendly smile and hospitality, surrounded by attention and care.





Crystal cleanliness and comfortable surroundings, magnificent landscapes and an abundance of entertainment events - all this will not leave indifferent the most demanding customers. Walking distance to the coastline, local attractions and public transport.

The hotel is designed for a diverse audience, regardless of status and material wealth. Conditions and service are carried out at a high level. The administration strictly monitors the cleanliness, order and safety of the territory - video surveillance is installed around the perimeter, there is a security point.

Number of rooms

For a comfortable and relaxed stay in the hotel "Villa Plaza" (Lazarevskoye) there are 62 rooms of different classes: economy, standard and luxury. A loggia is attached to each room, from which a breathtaking panorama of the village and the azure sea opens . The rooms are spacious, well renovated, furnished with necessary furniture and equipped with technical equipment. In the economy rooms, the bath and toilet are located on the common floor.





Helpful staff will provide an extra cot upon request. The superior apartments have a split system, a bar with drinks (for a fee) and a washing machine, satellite TV and a set of kitchen utensils. Cleaning and replacement of the bed are provided (on schedule).

Supply system

Guests have at their disposal a large dining room with a kitchen set and the necessary household equipment for self-cooking. There is a refrigerator, table, barbecue, barbecue. If desired, guests can pay for food and enjoy healthy, balanced and varied food.





It operates in the cafeteria with an outdoor terrace, which serves delicious dishes of local, European, international cuisine. The family-run Hotel Villa Plaza (Lazarevskoye) regularly receives rave reviews from satisfied guests. They liked the quality of the food, the table setting and the friendly service.

Coastline

The resort village Lazarevskoye is favorably distinguished by its wide and clean coast. Hotel "Villa Plaza" is located near the municipal pebble-sand beach, with a convenient and even descent into the water. The road to it will not take more than 5-7 minutes on a convenient staircase with wooden steps and handrails.

You can rent a sun lounger and a canopy. Tourists will be offered a variety of leisure activities: jet skis, windsurfing, parasailing, diving, ATVs. On the shore, a grid is stretched for active games. Boat trips on a boat and boat for a fee.

Internal infrastructure





After arriving at the Villa Plaza Hotel (Lazarevskoye) in your own car, you will be provided with a completely free parking space in the protected area. There is a souvenir kiosk and currency exchange office in the courtyard. It offers guests - rental cars, shoe shine, dry cleaning / laundry, ironing and ticket sales. Businessmen will be able to freely hold important symposia and presentations in the conference room. High-speed Internet is provided free of charge, for a fee - fax, landline telephone and photocopy.

Leisure activities

The complex "Villa Plaza" (Lazarevskoye) has a travel agency with a huge selection of excursion routes at an affordable cost. In the courtyard there are two pools: an adult and a children's. For the young guests there is a play area with slides and carousels.





Women will be delighted with the beauty salon (massage, solarium, bathhouse, hairdresser, manicure / pedicure, sauna). At any time it is allowed to visit the gym and play sports. In the evening, live music is played in the courtyard of the hotel, everyone around enjoys a pleasant atmosphere and dancing. You will not be sad.

Afterword

With eastern hospitality, the non-resident inhabitants of Lazarevskoye meet the sunny village. "Villa Plaza" reviews from tourists are constantly receiving positive. Pleased with pricing and the proximity of the beach. You can easily get to all the tourist facilities of the Greater Sochi (Krasnaya Polyana, Olympic Park). Almost all guests are happy to return here again. After all, modernity, comfort and European quality are combined here.
