Reviews about the rest in Kabardinka: overview, features and description

Kabardinka is usually called the settlement of the Black Sea coast, located between Novorossiysk and Gelendzhik. It is difficult to call Kabardinka the most popular resort on the coast, most often it is preferred by older couples, as well as families with children who prefer a relaxing and peaceful vacation. Vacationers will probably notice groups of children and adolescents, and all because camps have continued to exist here since Soviet times, many of which are popular.

Climate

Of course, reviews about holidays in Kabardinka are what you need to build on when choosing a resort. It is important to consider everything: the geography of the area, climate features, average daily air temperature, as well as sea water. Kabardinka is famous for its mild, dry climate; the season lasts from mid-May until the end of October.

Resort Features

The resort is literally immersed in greenery, a lot of coniferous trees that fill the air with an amazing and enchanting aroma. Many people call Kabardinka a health resort. If you want to strengthen immunity, get rid of respiratory diseases, you definitely should come here at least for a couple of weeks.

When to go to Kabardinka?

Ideal for relaxing in Kabardinka early June and mid-September. At this time there are not many vacationers, the air temperature is comfortable for living, the sea is warm and gentle, the likelihood of a storm is minimal. In autumn there are so many fruits and vegetables that tourists stock up on vitamins and bring juicy and ripe fruits home.

How to get there

If you are planning an independent trip, it would be nice to know in advance how to get to the resort from Novorossiysk or Gelendzhik - the nearest major cities. If you want to organize an unforgettable vacation in Kabardinka, reviews, tourist tips will surely help you.

So, as already noted, Kabardinka is located between Novorossiysk and Gelendzhik, you can get here in several ways: by plane, train and bus. There is another option that has recently been chosen by an increasing number of tourists - in a private car. In this case, it is necessary to move along the M4 "Don" highway, from where there will be an exit to Kabardinka. Even if this is the first such a long trip for you and your family, you will never get lost, especially nowadays there are a lot of gadgets that will help in organizing navigation. The duration of your trip, of course, depends on what the starting point of the route is.

Beaches

Kabardinka beaches are equipped with everything necessary for a good rest. Sports equipment rentals, all kinds of water activities, souvenir shops and local sweets are all within walking distance. Prices, surprising tourists, reasonable.





The beaches themselves are pebble, in some places tourists can rent sunbeds for a small fee, as well as umbrellas hiding from direct sunlight. The territory of the village is regularly cleaned, so the beaches can be called quite clean and well-groomed.

Leisure

Unfortunately, in the vicinity of Kabardinka there are very few attractions. Most of them are associated with natural resources. Locals love to offer tourists excursions to the lotus valley. The tour itself is fascinating and interesting. But you should prepare yourself for the fact that lotuses do not bloom all year round, so the probability of being deceived is quite high.
