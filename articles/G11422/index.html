The best hotels in Magnitogorsk: description and reviews of tourists

Magnitogorsk is the second largest city in the Chelyabinsk region. This is the largest world center of ferrous metallurgy. It is also the cultural center of the South Urals. Both of these factors explain the large flow of people who want to come to Magnitogorsk. Hotels will provide a warm welcome and comfortable accommodation.

Hotel Aurora

If you are looking for accommodation in the city center, look at the Aurora Hotel, located at 122/1 Karl Marx Avenue. In the hotel you will have the following options:

  • accommodation in rooms of different categories of comfort;
  • free wifi;
  • Free private parking
  • the ability to stay in a room with pets (on prior request and at an additional cost);
  • restaurant and bar;
  • packed lunches;
  • buffet breakfast or room service;
  • sauna;
  • equipped smoking areas;
  • transfer organization;
  • round-the-clock registration service;
  • safe;
  • conference hall;
  • banqueting hall;
  • laundry.

Reviews

About the Aurora Hotel, guests leave a number of positive reviews:

  • good restaurant with tasty food and reasonable prices;
  • friendly and friendly staff;
  • comfortable orthopedic mattresses on beds;
  • on each floor there are coolers with drinking water;
  • good sound insulation (neither conversations of neighbors, nor music from the restaurant, nor noise from the street are heard);
  • beautiful city views open from the windows of the rooms;
  • Convenient location relative to the railway station;
  • new and well-washed bedding.

You should also pay attention to such negative comments:

  • uncomfortable atmosphere in the rooms;
  • poorly attached towel hooks in the bathroom;
  • the rooms need redecorating (special attention should be paid to the worn dirty carpet);
  • there is not enough space in the bathroom to accommodate bath and cosmetic accessories;
  • narrow curtains do not completely cover the windows.

Hotel "Panorama"

Panorama is a cozy mini-hotel in Magnitogorsk. The address of the institution is Lenin Avenue, 129. This is the very center of the city next to the Metallurg ice arena. The mini-hotel is located on the 16th floor of a residential building. From here very beautiful city panoramas open. It is worth noting the following advantages of this accommodation option:

  • 7 comfortable rooms that can accommodate a total of 20 people;
  • a coworking zone where you can work at a computer, hold a business meeting or relax by the fireplace;
  • extended continental breakfast is included in the room rate;
  • at early departure lunch boxes are provided;
  • shared kitchen with a full range of appliances, appliances and utensils for self-cooking;
  • laundry and ironing facilities;
  • parking with video surveillance;
  • free wireless internet access;
  • round-the-clock registration and check-in service;
  • free hot drinks.

Reviews for "Panorama"

Positive feedback about this hotel in Magnitogorsk :

  • very polite, friendly, helpful and qualified staff;
  • perfectly clean linens and towels;
  • cozy and stylish atmosphere in the rooms;
  • excellent orthopedic beds;
  • a huge TV screen in the room;
  • beautiful panoramic views can be seen from the windows and from the balconies of the rooms;
  • excellent shared kitchen, equipped with everything necessary to cook food on your own (you can save a lot on food in the cafe).

It is also worth paying attention to the study of negative comments:

  • there is no carpet in the common lobby, so noise is heard at every step (especially if a woman in high heels walks past the room);
  • high prices for accommodation;
  • poor location on the outskirts of the city (a long drive to the center, airport and train station);
  • constant interruptions in the wireless Internet;
  • the mini-hotel is located in an apartment building.
