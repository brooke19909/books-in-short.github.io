Park-hotel "Premiere": description, services, reviews

Many residents of big cities today choose to relax in park hotels. Such hotel complexes are located in suburban areas and are designed so that people can change the situation and get distracted from the daily affairs and the bustle of megalopolises.

Park Hotel Premiere: Features

This hotel complex is located near the city of Tula. This place is considered one of the most beautiful corners of nature. Therefore, many choose it as a recreation area to distract from the noise and routine of the big city. Here you can spend time with parents and children. The park hotel is also great for friendly gatherings, corporate events and business meetings.

The hotel complex is located in the middle of a coniferous forest near the coast of the river.

Guests are given the opportunity to stay in cottages. On the territory of the park-hotel "Premiere" in Tula there are eleven comfortable country houses on two floors.

The guests of the establishment can be sure that during the rest they will not have to be bored. After all, the hotel employees have provided for them an entertainment program and various types of active leisure.

At the entrance to the hotel complex, visitors can use the parking lot, which is guarded around the clock.

The Premiere Park Hotel is located in the Tula Region in the village of Egnyshevka, Aleksin District.

Residence

The guests are accommodated in country houses, consisting of two parts, each of which is designed to accommodate four people.

There are also rooms for six and eight people with one entrance, which is located on the veranda.

The houses consist of two tiers, on the first floor there is a corridor, a bathroom, a bathroom and a shower, a kitchen, a refrigerator, a stove, dishes, a sofa and a TV.

On the second floor there is a bedroom, a wardrobe and a bedside table. On the 2nd tier there is also a balcony.

Near the houses there is a barbecue, and those who went out of town to fry kebabs will not have problems with their preparation. Also near the cottages there are comfortable gazebos where you can relax.

Food and organization of holidays

For guests of the park-hotel "Premiere" a cafe is provided, which consists of several rooms. The menu includes a large number of copyright dishes, and each visitor can always choose what he likes. The hotel also provides services for various events: weddings, birthday parties, corporate parties.

The employees of the hotel complex are ready to organize a bright and unforgettable holiday in accordance with the wishes of their customers. The entertainment program will not let guests get bored and will not leave them indifferent.

Leisure

The hotel guests do not have a question about what they can do during their holidays. After all, the hotel complex provides a leisure system. It includes sports, swimming pool and sauna.

During the day you can use these services for free.

However, in order to visit the sauna by a large company, you will have to pay extra. Those who wish to practice winter sports can rent skis; fishing enthusiasts can use special equipment. Guided tours of museums and other historical sites are also available. Many families with children like to relax in this hotel complex, because the child will always have something to do here - there is a playground and a room for games.

For friendly gatherings, the staff of the Premiere Park Hotel can provide a large gazebo. It is always warm in it, because it is heated. The gazebo has all the necessary equipment for the preparation of barbecue.

If the street is very cold, an event in a friendly company is best organized in the banquet hall, which is located near the cafe. It, like the gazebo, can be booked.

Guest opinions

Reviews about the Premiere Park Hotel are very diverse, and they reflect both the strengths and weaknesses of the establishment. Among the advantages of the hotel complex, guests call beautiful nature, the picturesque coast of the river, fresh coniferous air. Guests also enjoy the flexible check-in system and the friendly and attentive attitude of the hotel staff. They say that it is a quiet, peaceful place, which is great for sharing with children. Also, guests are pleased with the availability of good dishes in the rooms, barbecue and gazebos for cooking barbecue and a comfortable pastime in a friendly company.

Many guests claim that in general, the Premiere Park Hotel is worth the money. However, the institution has its drawbacks. For example, some visitors say that they did not like the lack of a grocery store near the recreation area. There are guests who believe that the rooms in the houses are in need of repair, and the furniture is in need of replacement.

Also, some guests are unhappy that the TV, toilet and sofa are only on the ground floor.
