Bar "Hands Up" in Moscow: address, photo, reviews

The Hands Up Bar in Moscow is an institution that has recently opened and has become very popular. It is widely known among those who come from the 90s and is a fan of the group of the same name. As visitors themselves note, in such an institution you can always visit with friends, family and even work colleagues. A rather pleasant and sincere atmosphere prevails here, which is conducive to friendly communication.

The famous singer Sergey Zhukov is the owner of the bar "Hands Up" (Moscow). Griboedova - the embankment in St. Petersburg - is another place where there is an institution of this network. In the Northern capital of Russia, the brand created by Zhukov is no less popular than in Moscow.



Location

This restaurant-club is located in the metro station "Kropotkinskaya" on Lenivka street, located in the Khamovniki district. Not far from this place is the famous Museum of Chocolate, as well as the gallery of Ilya Glazunov. Guests of the bar can easily reach it using public transport (metro, bus) or taxi. For those arriving by car, free parking is organized near the entrance to the institution, it is constantly monitored.

The main hall is presented in brown. The walls are decorated with dark upholstery, which goes well with square wooden tables and leather ottomans. One of them has a large mirror in a golden frame. Here is a modern karaoke system, perfect for playing your favorite music - many guests prefer to arrange small solo concerts in the bar "Hands Up" (Moscow).



Kitchen

The menu of the institution includes dishes of Japanese, Russian, Italian and European cuisine. According to the management, the bar has a team of professional chefs who can truly surprise visitors with the tasteful qualities of the food served.

The menu here is presented in very bright colors, there are several comics on its pages, the participants of which are famous performers of the 90s: groups "Ivanushki", "TATU", "Strelka", Sasha Zverev, Glukoza, "Na-Na" and Bari Alibasov, as well as many others. Such an original representation of him, undoubtedly, attracts the attention of guests.

On the pages of the list of dishes there are several options for appetizers (herring with potatoes and herbs, pickles and marinades, "Mimosa", roasted vegetables, cold cuts with mustard and horseradish) and simple salads ("Egg under mayonnaise", "Stolichniy", " Caesar "," Greek "," Hands Up "). A separate group in the menu are beer snacks (chicken wings in honey and soy sauce, croutons with dryers, "Beer plate").

For the first, two varieties of soups are offered (meat hodgepodge, chicken broth with vermicelli), and for the hot - several fish and meat dishes (mackerel, chicken cutlets, beef stroganoff). If you wish, you can taste dishes from the grill, visitors can taste the barbecue, which is prepared from pork and chicken.

Italian cuisine in the bar "Hands Up" (Moscow) is represented by three types of pasta (with shrimp, carbonara, bolognese).

Visitors celebrate the original desserts served at the bar. All of them are also from the 90s. Tiramisu is especially popular here, as well as ryazhenka with poppy seeds. Both children and adults are fans of ice cream, which is served in several types, with different additives.

Bar card

The Hands Up Bar (Moscow) offers an excellent selection of alcoholic beverages. Among them are vodka, several types of whiskey, cognac, tequila, brandy, rum, liquors, gin, cider and bottled beer, which is especially popular among guests. Several types of wine and champagne are also offered.

Among the soft drinks there are several options for soft drinks and fruit drinks. In addition, guests love to order compote and freshly squeezed juice. Among families who relax in a bar with children, milkshakes are widely in demand.



Entertainment

Every Friday and Saturday in the bar of Sergey Zhukov's "Hands Up" show programs are organized. Often, as part of their conduct, the public is entertained by famous DJs, vocalists, as well as musicians. A poster with a schedule of upcoming events can always be seen on the institution's official website in a special section.

The bar can be a wonderful venue for any kind of event. Often, birthday parties are celebrated within its walls, wedding banquets are organized, as well as receptions. A small celebration can be held in the karaoke room - the evening will definitely become unforgettable!



Stocks

For regular visitors and certain categories of people, the institution regularly makes a variety of discounts and offers bonuses. So, everyone who celebrates his birthday in the bar "Hands Up" (Moscow), can receive a discount of 50 percent on the entire amount of the order. Such an offer is valid only if a preliminary reservation has been made. If you organize a bachelor party or a bachelorette party in a karaoke room, you will also receive a discount in this amount on the whole account. Many reviews of the bar "Hands Up" (Moscow) say that on this occasion it is possible to organize a stripper.

The institution has a unique offer called "Major". It implies an all-inclusive service, and the cost is 24,900 rubles. This promotion applies to all menus and bar cards.

The bar has two popular offers. One of them involves the purchase of the "18 to me already" promotional package, which includes a liter of juice, 0.5 liters of strong alcohol, cold cuts of meat or fruit, as well as a free karaoke song. The cost of such an offer is 2500 rubles. When ordering a specialty cocktail "Alyoshka" also relies on the possibility of one free song.

Prices

The pricing policy in the club is quite acceptable, especially when you consider that it is located in the central part of Moscow. The average bill per person in this place is about 1000-1200 rubles. Guests note that for fairly low prices you can have a great time here, sing your favorite hit in karaoke, and also taste deliciously cooked food.

Opening hours and address

The Hands Up Bar in Moscow is located on Lenivka Street 3. You can visit it any day of the week from nine in the morning until midnight. Before the visit, especially if it is planned for the evening, it is advisable to reserve a table. This is done by the phone number, which is indicated on its main website or on the official website of the institution.





In general, you can find a considerable number of reviews about this restaurant. They often say that they cook great food here, about interesting show programs, and also about the wonderful atmosphere of fun that reigns every evening in the walls of the Hands Up bar (Moscow). The photos taken during the parties perfectly reflect the positive mood of the guests who visited the institution, as well as the general atmosphere. Come and you will not regret it!
