Rostov-on-Don hotels: overview, description, rating, reviews

Rostov-on-Don is the largest city in the south of Russia. It is interesting from the point of view of tourism, and also provides ample opportunities for science and business. It is not surprising that a huge number of people rush to this place. Rostov hotels will provide you with a welcoming meeting, a comfortable stay and a lot of pleasant experiences.

Hotel Marins Park

If you are accustomed to comfort, pay attention to the Marins Park Hotel. Rostov, Budennogo street, 39 - the address of this institution. This is the central part of the city, just 600 meters from the train station. Also nearby is the central city market and the Cathedral of the Nativity. Guests are available with the following options for accommodation:

  • Standard - a spacious bright room. You can choose between a large double bed or a pair of single beds. The room has everything you need for a comfortable stay - a bathroom, a desk, a large mirror and a wardrobe. The cost of living is from 2700 rubles.
  • Superior standard. It features an increased area and good equipment. In addition to the large bed, there is a small soft sofa for relaxation and additional accommodation, a coffee table and a full-fledged workplace. The bathroom is equipped with a large bath. The cost of living is from 3300 rubles.
  • Suite - a luxurious two-room suite decorated in a classic style. Spacious bedroom in bright colors decorated with reproductions of paintings. The white walls of the living room are organically combined with dark brown furniture. The cost of living is 4300 rubles.
  • Apartments - a huge room, which in terms of comfort and functionality is not inferior to a full-fledged apartment. The peculiarity is the presence of a kitchen area with a dining table, a breakfast bar and all the necessary appliances. The cost of living is 11500 rubles.
