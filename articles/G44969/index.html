Land characteristics

It is worth noting that such mountain landscapes are completely uncharacteristic of the Russian Plain. Due to the special climate, the Vorgola Valley has become a refuge for many rare plant species that are more characteristic of the Caucasus and the Alps. Here, for example, one can find mountain ferns (Robert's rodent, worm and hairy bone), high floristic diversity (more than 457 types of higher plants), rare plant species (Podolian shivereka, Alaunsky cotoneaster, pulled peppermint, fluffy currants, etc. ) These tracts are of great value, so they were taken under protection. In particular, they are part of the Galichya Gora Nature Reserve.

The unearthly splendor of the rocks

Vorgolsky rocks (Lipetsk region) are located on both banks of the river at an altitude of 40-50 meters. The rocky shores stretch upstream several kilometers from the village of Nizhny Vorgol, forming a picturesque canyon-like valley, which is surrounded by greenery in warm time. The legendary Vorgol caves are also located here. The smallest rock cave ‚ÄúSmoked Stone‚Äù, which is cut by a crack, is considered the largest. According to legend, this "scar" at the cliff appeared from the blow of the heroic sword. By the way, the Vorgolsky rocks are fraught with many more unsolved secrets. This region has preserved the traces of such great conquerors as Tamerlan, Batu, Mamai and others.

At the foot of this stone formation there is an entrance to the cave. Many old-timers say that the labyrinths inside the cave stretch for 3-5 kilometers, and several half-filled passages lead into its deep dungeon.



Raven Stone

This rock is located on the right bank of the Vorgol River, its area is 9.5 hectares. Visually, it seems that the mountain is covered with huge slabs of limestone. From top to bottom, it is cut by a gorge, at the bottom of which there is an entrance to the cave. Inside it there is an 8-meter corridor that leads deep into the cliffs. It constantly narrows and on the 40th meter completely turns into a narrow mink.

Worgol Valley

The height of individual rocks in the valley reaches 20-25 meters - these are Fox, Big Kichy, Ravens and Smoked stones. Vorgolsky rocks are a shelter for 7 species of fern, lichens, oak groves and lime trees. At the foot of the cliffs are splendid meadows.

The world of fauna here is also quite diverse. In this region there is a brazhnik butterfly, as well as several species of bumblebees: reddish, steppe and moss, gray. Wild bees live here - the Tartar halikta and the Andren mountain, their area is the Caucasus, the Urals, Western Europe. There are different types of vertebrates.



How to get from Moscow by car

As already mentioned, the unique Vorgolsky rocks are located in the Lipetsk region 10 km from the regional center of Yelets. To get to these amazing places from Moscow, you first need to go to Voronezh (360 km) along the M4 highway. You get to Yelets, and after the traffic police post you need to drive about 1.5 km and turn right onto the "Key of Life". Further, the road will lie through the railway lines, and after moving you need to go along the asphalt, after 1 km there will be a sign N. Vorgol-Dernovka. This is what you need in Dernivka (that is, to the right). You can get to the village of Dernivka on an asphalt road. We move the power station, which will be on the left, and drive into the first left after it.

After entering the village, you will see a shop on the right and a stop on the left, then drive along the asphalt to the fork and run into the second bus stop. From here we turn left onto a dirt road. We fall into a wide field and drive straight along the same primer. After about a kilometer you will see a right turn. It leads to a small career, and to the right of it there will be a platform above the rocks. Having traveled a little more than a kilometer along the riverbank, you will run into a dead end. This will be the final destination of your destination.

Climbing festival

Climbing fans have long been chosen by the Vorgol mountains. It even hosts the annual festival organized by the Russian Climbing Federation, the authorities of the Lipetsk Region, and several other organizations. According to the conditions of the competition, participants are offered 40 routes with varying degrees of difficulty. Extremes must ensure their safety on their own. Winners are awarded a diploma and valuable prizes from the organizers of the competition. During the festival, participants live in tents, cook dinner together and sing with a guitar.

A wonder among the plains

According to many travelers, the Vorgolsky Mountains are not only a very picturesque area, where many relic plants and animals have been preserved, but also a place with a unique energy. In this area, people begin to feel peace, tranquility and an incredible charge of vivacity. To see the mesmerizing panorama from the top of the rocks, to visit the karst caves, where there are traces of the culture of the ancient Slavic peoples, is simply an indescribable pleasure. These mountainous formations are like an oasis among endless plains, steppes and meadows. It seems like they are sprouting from the ground right in front of you.



However, to see firsthand these amazing landscapes, you first need to come to Yelets (the Vorgolsky rocks are only 10 km from this district center), and from it you will have to get directly to the mountains themselves.
