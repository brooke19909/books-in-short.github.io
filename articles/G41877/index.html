Filippos Hotel 3 (Thessaloniki, Chalcedon): traveler reviews

Thessaloniki is rightfully considered the main historical center of Greece. This ancient city was built a long time ago - in ancient times. Today, among other things, it is also one of the most popular among tourists around the world resort. Inexpensive comfortable apartments, for example, vacationers can rent in Thessaloniki without any problems. Russian tour operators work with many hotels in this city and its environs. And at the same time, the Filippos Hotel 3 * is rightfully considered one of the most convenient hotels in Thessaloniki among domestic vacationers .

How to find a complex

This budget hotel is located not in Thessaloniki itself, but in the suburbs nearby - in the picturesque Greek village of Chalcedon. Domestic holidaymakers arrive in Greece, of course, mainly by plane. The closest to the hotel Filippos Hotel 3 * and the airport is located in Thessaloniki. The distance from it to Chalcedon is about 30 km. Tour operator shuttle buses usually travel to the hotel from the airport in about 1.5 hours. You can also get to Chalkidon from Thessaloniki by taxi. Goes in this direction and low-cost public transport. The bus station is in the immediate vicinity of the hotel. You can walk from it to the hotel in just 3-5 minutes.





In relation to the beach, the Filippos Hotel 3 * complex is also very conveniently located. Built hotel on the first coastline. This means that the road to the beach at the hotel guests can take no more than 5-7 minutes.

What you should know about

Descriptions of the Filippos Hotel 3 * hotel on the websites of tour operators on the network are usually given corresponding to reality. But the future guests of this hotel should still be more attentive. The fact is that in Greece there is another hotel with the same name. It is located on the island of Thassos, 4 km from the port of Prinu. This complex is also assigned 3 stars. Guest reviews Hotel Filippos Hotel 3 * (Thassos) served well. In the ratings on the sites of tour operators, vacationers give him 4-5 points out of 5. In general, this is also a good hotel. But to the complex located in Chalcedon, he, of course, has nothing to do.

Hotel Description

Hotel Filippos accepts guests in one two-story building of beautiful modern architecture. The hotel courtyard is equipped in such a way that residents are as comfortable as possible. On the territory of the hotel there are flowerbeds and exotic southern trees planted everywhere. The courtyard of the hotel is lined with paving tiles of a pleasant light pink hue (the color of the facades of the building).

This hotel was built a long time ago. However, the furniture and household appliances in it are not too old. The fact is that several years ago a large-scale renovation was carried out in the hotel.





The reception at the Filippos Hotel 3 * complex (Thessaloniki, Chalcedon) is open 24 hours a day. The hotel also provides rooms for disabled guests. Smoking is not allowed in the rented rooms at this hotel. However, for the convenience of guests, the complex provides specially equipped places for this .

Hotel Rooms

All rooms for rent at the hotel are of the standard class. For the convenience of guests, the rooms of the Filippos Hotel 3 * include:

  • TV;

  • air conditioning.

Also, residents have the opportunity to use bedside tables, tables and cabinets. The hotel also has a minibar. In one of the offices of the closet there is a safe. The key to it should be taken at the reception.

Bathrooms in this hotel, as in any other three-star hotels, are standard. Showers are not provided in them. Installed in the bathrooms pallets with watering cans closed with a curtain. During washing, water from such structures can pour from the pan to the floor.
