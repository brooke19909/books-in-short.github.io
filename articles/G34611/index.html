Miraggio Thermal SPA Resort 5 * (Greece, Halkidiki): traveler reviews

Miraggio Thermal SPA Resort 5 * is an excellent hotel complex located in a picturesque location. Its highlight is the presence of its own SPA-center and direct proximity to the sea. However, there are more interesting features of this hotel. And about all of them it is worth briefly telling.





Location

Miraggio Thermal SPA Resort 5 * is located in Halkidiki. This place is a recognized pearl of Northern Greece. Halkidiki is famous for its pristine beaches, most of which are awarded the EU Blue Flag. And there is incredibly beautiful nature. Halkidiki is an evergreen paradise in Greece.

The hotel is located here. And to be more precise, on the beach in a village called Paliouri. The place is very successful - after all, in 15-20 minutes you can reach the villages of Hanioti and Pefkohori, where life is in full swing. True, there is one drawback to this place. To the airport far - go more than 100 kilometers. Ho, in principle, this is an insignificant drawback, because guests do not need to go there twice a day. Moreover, you can always use the transfer.

And yet, this complex is completely new. After all, its opening took place in May 2016, just a couple of months ago. And it has already become popular in advance, because this hotel belongs to the Med Sea Health group. Not surprisingly, a few days after the opening of the hotel there were no empty rooms. Indeed, many people want to relax in Greece in the summer, and then a new hotel has opened, which means an additional option has appeared. And very good.

Leisure

In Miraggio Thermal SPA Resort 5 * you will not get bored. Of course, the vast majority of tourists who come here spend all their free time on the beach. However, there are many other options with which you can brighten up your leisure time.

On the territory there are several pools, one of which with sea water, and the other with a gorgeous view of the beach. There is also a sun terrace. Fans of a sports lifestyle will definitely like a fitness center with a variety of fitness equipment and a health club. There are also two outdoor tennis courts and a marina (with yachts, of course, where you can ride).

Bicycle rental is also available and motor boat rides can be booked. In general, water sports are very popular here. And the hotel provides guests with all the equipment necessary for diving, water skiing and sailing. In the end, if none of this attracts you, you can just take the mask with the pipe and dive. And for an additional fee, guests get access to the water park.





For body and soul

In Miraggio Thermal SPA Resort 5 *, you can do many more. The hotel has a yoga instructor who conducts lessons in his profile for those who wish. And there is also a full-fledged SPA center. There, guests can order cosmetic and wellness treatments, undergo a personalized consultation, take a steam bath in the sauna, soak in the jacuzzi or simply go for a Swedish massage. Even in SPA, aroma and hydrotherapy, reflexology and thalassotherapy are carried out. They also do deep muscle massage and stone therapy (the use of hot stones). And there are also procedures using hot and mineral springs. Guests can also enjoy mud baths.

You can also enjoy the procedures in the hydrotherapy bath and in the massage shower. This is one of the most beloved activities in the SPA for many guests who have already managed to visit here.

In the center there are several treatment rooms in which guests who come as a couple are served. Many lovers like to go for a massage together and enjoy the services together. It is really very romantic.

Nutrition

In Miraggio Thermal SPA Resort 5 * (Deluxe), as in any other self-respecting establishment, there are establishments in which guests can eat and drink well.

Take, for example, a restaurant called Kritamo Main. It serves its customers buffet style. They offer dinners, lunches and breakfasts. There is also the Portofino restaurant, which serves Italian dishes. But it is open only for dinner.

A there is still an institution called Toroneo, located on the beach. It serves seafood as well as Greek dishes. Open facility for lunch and dinner. It is popular with guests. For this reason, the administration decided to introduce a condition - those people who want to have their meal here must pre-book a table. To do this, go to the reception.

And another institution is the Oasis Pool. There you can have lunch and dinner. Ho reservations are also required. The main specialization of this establishment is Greek cuisine.





Service

In Miraggio Thermal SPA Resort 5 * (Deluxe) there is everything that can be necessary for the comfort of guests. There is free Wi-Fi available throughout the hotel and parking is available near the hotel. Concierge and babysitting services are available. By the way, people who come with children may not worry that they will be bored. Especially for them there is a playground and a play area indoors - with board games, puzzles, books, music and films.

The complex has an ATM, a luggage room, a currency exchange and a 24-hour reception. Dry cleaning and laundry services are available. Ironing service is available.

There is also a business center, a conference room and an office with a photocopy and fax machine. In addition, there is a minimarket and shops, a shared lounge, shuttle and car rental services, a souvenir stall and a small church. Beauty salon, hairdresser, food delivery, facilities for people with disabilities - all this is also inside.

About booking and accommodation

This is also a pretty important topic regarding the Miraggio Thermal SPA Resort 5 * (Deluxe).
