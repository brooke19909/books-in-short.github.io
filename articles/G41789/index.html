Models of skirts for women with a protruding belly with patterns, photo

For a long time, fashion existed only for slender women. Today, finally, models of skirts for women with a protruding belly have appeared.

Secrets of the right choice of skirt style

Stylish, visually reducing figure flaws outfits for the full became a real breakthrough in the fashion industry.





Models of skirts for women with a protruding belly look luxuriously, photos of which are presented in the article. And all because couturiers know some secrets that every reader of this article can now use.

The right size skirt is the number one secret

The main mistake of many women with magnificent forms is the choice of too loose, baggy clothes. Such models, although they hide ugly folds on the body, visually increase the completeness even more.

The second mistake is the choice of too narrow robes. It does look vulgar at all. After all, a tight-fitting outfit exposes even a tiny fold at the waist.





Skirts for women with a protruding belly should be exactly the size that suits the lady. This is the main secret of the visual harmony of a beautiful woman with magnificent forms.

High Waist and Wide Belt - Secret Number Two

Some people believe that skirts for women with a stomach are absolutely contraindicated. This is a wrong judgment. Just when choosing a style, it is important to determine where to make the waist (forgive the reader for the phrase from the joke).





And so it is. Indeed, when choosing a model of skirts for women with a protruding belly, it makes no sense to emphasize this part of the figure. It is best to make the belt wide and raise it slightly above the waist. And on the line where the stomach begins to protrude, stitch the main details of the skirt. This will be slightly higher than it should be in the classic version.

This is the second secret that makes the models of skirts for women with a protruding belly comfortable and simply chic.

Maxi skirts - the secret of harmony number three

Not without reason in the past women wore long dresses, up to their ankles. And their skirts were magnificent, adjacent to the body only at the waist or under the chest. Such clothes even made it possible to hide the pregnancy in the second half.





So modern couturiers turned their eyes to the past. And today, retro models of skirts for women with a protruding belly have come into fashion - long, lush, luxurious.

Maxi outfits visually increase growth. And the lady, of course, seems thinner and slimmer.

Additional details on top of the skirt - secret number four

You can cover your tummy with a drape, basques, wide ruffles and ruffles. Such skirt models are very suitable for women with a protruding belly and narrow hips. It is important not to overdo it with these details.





But mostly mature ladies prefer to cover the upper part of the skirt with a blouse with a frill or a jacket.

The splendor of the bottom of the skirt - the secret of harmony number five

There is a secret for those who do not want to draw attention to their wide hips. The fact is that the main details of the skirts, gathered in small folds on the belt, create unnecessary volume in this case. Therefore, ladies with wide hips should not make too fluffy a skirt immediately from the waist.

Young ladies in pleated skirts look beautiful. It is best to stitch them to the line of the widest volume at the waist.

But you can get by with just an extended skirt. It is not difficult to build a pattern for such a style if there is a straight silhouette pattern. The drawing shows how to make a pattern yourself.





On the pattern from the tops of the darts, draw vertical lines to the bottom. If desired, you can make another line, without tucking, then the skirt will be more magnificent from below.

Next, the pattern along the lines is cut to the tops of the darts. If the pattern was made on paper or polyethylene, then the darts can be stabbed with pins. If a piece of cardboard, they are cut out.

Combining the received parts from above, the bottom smoothly connect with one line.

A mature woman and a young girl in skirts of styles half-sun, sun, year look very presentable. And you can sew such models at home with your own hands.

Shaped sun-flared and half-sun: patterns of skirts

For overweight women with a stomach, this model should be quite long. To make a pattern, you will need only two measurements: the length of the product and the volume of the waist.





The drawing clearly shows that at first the product length plus some measure ‚Äúa‚Äù is laid aside. Therefore, we first calculate it by the formula: the waist circumference should be divided by a constant value for all sizes - 6.28. This is the doubled number pi, which is obtained from the formula for finding the radius along the circumference.

Now we add the obtained value with the length of the product and draw a semicircle. From the same central point, we form a second inner semicircle. So the pattern for the flared sun skirt is ready.

Such a model can be sewn as a whole-cut one (if the width of the fabric allows), and with a seam. In the latter case, the cutting of the fabric is carried out as shown in the drawing.

To build a half-skirt pattern, you need the same measurements. Only the value of a is calculated a little differently. After all, the half-sun consists of one part, therefore, the inner semicircle is equal to the length of the waist circumference. And the entire circumference will be equal to 2 waist circumference. This means that the radius "a" will be twice as large as when cutting the flared sun. This value is found by the following formula: the waist circumference is divided by 3.14, where the divider is a constant number.

Some novice tailors may have a question: how to draw such large circles? After all, you cannot even find such a compass in the house! Experts advise using a string. It replaces the compass step.

To do this, a pencil is attached to the end of the string (if we draw a pattern on paper) or a tailor's felt-tip pen, crayon, a piece of dry soap (if we build the drawing immediately on the fabric). Then, the desired radius is measured from the writing instrument. This place is fixed in the center of future circles. Now, pulling the rope, rotate a pencil or felt-tip pen around the axis, at the same time marking a circle shape on fabric or paper.

You can use a second hand for this purpose. If the radius is greater than the reach of the hands, then you should ask someone else to help.

Skirt pattern year

This is another of the most suitable styles for ladies with a tummy. Its meaning is that the skirt remains adjacent from above, but below the hips, at the knees or in the calf area it becomes lush. Each dressmaker herself decides where the expansion should be. The skirt as a result has the shape of an open tulip. It looks very impressive.

Even a novice dressmaker can sew such models of skirts for women with a protruding belly. There are no problems with patterns for a year.





  1. You just need to take the usual pattern along which the details of the straight skirt are cut - the front and back.
  2. On both patterns from the center, mark the third part in width and draw a vertical line. Then you should transfer the tuck to this auxiliary line.
  3. Now we cut the patterns along the line just drawn, the tucks go away, and the cut in this place bends.
  4. We build triangles that will be folds a year. To do this, you need to calculate its height - measure from the point where the fold begins to the bottom of the product. In the drawing, it is equal to 35 cm. We make an arbitrary base - the splendor of the bottom of the skirt will depend on it. You can do it, as the fashion designer advises, - 10 cm. We lift the vertices at the base of the triangle upwards by 3.5 cm. Now we connect these points with a smooth line passing through the middle of the base.
  5. The resulting triangle is cut in height.
  6. On the parts obtained from 2/3 of the halves of the front and rear, on both sides we attach the halves of the triangles with right angles so that their bottom coincides. Triangles are folds year.
  7. For narrow details (1/3 of the pattern), a triangle is drawn on only one side (not at the center line).
  8. The final version of the pattern: we circle the received parts, comparing the line at the point of attachment of the triangle top to the main part.
  9. Two asymmetric parts with two triangles and one part with one triangle should be cut. But before cutting a part with one triangle, the fabric folds in the direction of the shared thread. The pattern should be superimposed with a straight line to the fold. Only 6 parts should be obtained.
  10. Do not forget to make allowances for seams!

There is an option not to make a specially continuous pattern for wedges, but to cut them separately. They are easily sewn between the details of the skirt. Some designers prefer to make them from a different color of fabric.
