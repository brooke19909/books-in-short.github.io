What does BIN Insurance offer?

CASCO - insurance that allows you to cover the costs of the owner of the vehicle upon the occurrence of an insurance event for risk "Damage" and "Hijacking".

Currently, BIN Insurance is ready to offer several insurance options under the CASCO program.

  1. Classic.
  2. Comfort.

Who is the product of the BIN Insurance company focused on? Employee reviews indicate that the Classic program is perfect for adults and experienced drivers, and Comfort for those citizens who have just received a license.

Classic program

Under this program, settlement upon the occurrence of an insured event is carried out only if there are documents from the traffic police. Thus, the client who has taken advantage of this program must record each insured event with the competent authorities. This restriction allows you to significantly save on the size of the insurance premium and issue a policy at a good price.

Comfort program

Under this program, the policyholder has the right 2 times during the entire term of the contract to declare (regardless of the amount of damage) about the insured event without providing documents from the competent authorities.

Regardless of the insurance program chosen, the client, when applying for the policy, makes the decision on reimbursement of the insurance payment by calculation or repair at the station. This right of choice distinguishes the insurance company BIN Insurance. Customer reviews indicate that almost all insurance structures today stopped paying in cash.

Which program to use is up to you. One thing remains unchanged: the insurance company BIN Insurance takes into account the wishes of customers and offers only the best conditions.
