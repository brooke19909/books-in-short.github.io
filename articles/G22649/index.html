Hotels in Kolomna (Moscow Oblast): overview, prices and reviews

Kolomna is one of the oldest cities of the Moscow Region, which has existed since the 12th century. The city has always been an important industrial center and transport hub of Russia. There are not many attractions to surprise visitors, but each of them has its own historical value. In the city of 420 monuments of regional and federal significance, the Assumption Cathedral, the Kolomna Kremlin, the Church of St. John the Baptist, the houses of merchants Meshchaninov and Shevlyagin are distinguished.



Hotels in Kolomna are of various categories. You can immediately highlight the hotel of the same name, in which there are four types of rooms: classic luxury, standard, grand suite and business suite. Each of them is equipped with necessary amenities for a comfortable stay. In addition, individual groups are offered individual meals, although this is possible only after agreement with the administration of the institution.

Considering the hotels in Kolomna, it is worth noting that the 40th Meridian is considered one of the best accommodation options. This is a hotel complex, which is located on the banks of the river. Oka and has only positive reviews from guests. This is the most suitable hotel for a romantic and family vacation, as well as for business people. The territory offers guests leisurely walks, and the restaurant can please you with delicious dishes and stunning views of the river. In addition, pets are allowed here.

Hotels in Kolomna allow you to comfortably stay for several nights. The main number of hotels is provided to guests with wi-fi (and without it, accommodation can now be hard labor), parking, breakfast included in the price, services for the purchase of railway and air tickets, etc.

In this article we will consider the 5 most popular hotels in Kolomna.

Kolomna is suitable for celebrations, business, private and corporate events. There are 5 conference rooms suitable for events for up to 250 guests.
