Uninhabited island: how to survive and not panic

Probably, everyone had a desire to visit an uninhabited island, where everywhere there are white sandy beaches, azure water and palm trees. However, sometimes there are situations when in such a tropical resort the goal is the same - to survive. There may be many situations why this happened, but there is only one result. Therefore, there are several rules that will help you best get out of the situation.

Do not panic

First, you should assess the situation, looking around.

Quite often in such cases, people may have amnesia. Therefore, you should calm down, think and remember what happened, what events preceded this. It is also important at what time of day you are there. If it is day and the sun is directly above you, it is worth exploring a desert island and finding a shadow. In addition, you should not be very active in behaving: screaming, running, as this can attract the attention of animals, among which there may be predators.

Try to locate

Take a look around if there is a pond nearby. Sometimes people get to an uninhabited island from the air. Therefore, your fall can happen right in the forest. In this case, you should try to find a pond. It can be a river, ocean, sea.

Food search



Sooner or later, anyone who lands on an uninhabited island will want to drink and eat. Therefore, it is advisable to find food in advance. Here they can become berries and plants. You may also see familiar fruits, such as coconuts and bananas. However, if you meet a fruit that is not known to you, then it is better not to eat it. Remember, it can be poisonous. Fresh water

You need to look around and look for a source of fresh water. If he is found, you can consider yourself lucky. However, it is worth remembering that many unknown microorganisms may not be able to survive at all costs. An uninhabited island can bring unpleasant surprises in this case, such as poisoning or dysbiosis. Therefore, water must be boiled. If there is no reservoir, then it will have to collect dew and rain. To do this, use large leaves. Of course, this method will not allow quenching thirst, but it will not let die either.
