Hotel Varca Palms Beach Resort 3 * (India, Goa). Hotel reviews

Varca Palms Beach Resort 3 * - a modern and comfortable hotel complex with budget rates. Here you can enjoy the untouched tropical nature, soak up the beach, and get acquainted with the peculiarities of the original local culture.





Hotel Description

Hotel Varca Palms Beach Resort 3 * is an amazing combination of untouched tropical nature with modern and comfortable apartments, which creates simply ideal conditions for living and relaxing travelers. It is especially worth noting the fact that the decoration of the rooms is dominated by wooden elements. Thanks to this, even while in the room, you can feel the unity with nature.

Relaxing in Varca Palms Beach Resort 3 *, you will have many opportunities for entertainment. So, the most interesting excursions will be prepared for you by the employees of the tourist office. And if you are inclined to independently explore a new area, then for convenience you can rent a bicycle.

Be sure to get acquainted with Indian cuisine in the restaurant of the hotel Varca Palms Beach Resort 3 *. Here, local chefs observe all traditions, which makes their dishes original and original. Be sure to check out the bar to taste the original cocktails.





Where is the hotel

Varca Le Palms Beach Resort 3 * is located in an amazing place among tropical palm trees and exotic plants on the Varca beach of the same name. This is the southern part of Goa. Margao Railway Station is 10 km from this resort. If you prefer to travel by plane, then, getting to the airport, you will overcome the path in 45 minutes.

Guest apartments

For a comfortable stay, guests of Varca Le Palms Beach Resort 3 * are invited to check in the following apartments:

  • Superior is the largest room category. They are located in cozy buildings, the architecture of which is made in accordance with local traditions. The room itself is a bedroom with a small seating area.
  • Garden View is a detached compact villas that hide from prying eyes in the greenery of the garden. Here, in addition to the bedroom, there is a small entrance hall, as well as a cozy living room area with soft armchairs, a coffee table and a floor lamp. There is also a small private terrace for gatherings and admiring nature.
  • Deluxe are spacious two-room numbers. The bedroom is equipped with a huge double bed. The living room is combined with a small dining room. The main feature of these apartments is a direct access to the pool through their own terrace.




In the rooms

In each room of the Varca Palms Beach Resort 3 * hotel (India, Varca) you will find the following set of necessary amenities:

  • bathroom with toilet and shower;
  • air conditioning, working from the general ventilation system of the hotel (the exception is only detached villas, where an individual split system is installed);
  • in the event that the air temperature supplied by the air conditioner does not suit you, it is possible to use a ceiling fan;
  • to provide leisure while in the room there is a TV connected to the cable operator;
  • the phone gives you the opportunity to contact loved ones, as well as resolve issues with the reception and other hotel services;
  • each room has a minibar, but the use of this service is paid (many tourists prefer to use it as a refrigerator).

Hotel Services

The resort and hotel complex Varca Palms Beach Resort 3 * (India, Varka), has developed the following range of services for its guests:

  • You can swim in the shade of exotic palm trees using the clean and well-kept outdoor pool with disinfected fresh water;
  • You can play tennis on the modern court;
  • Sauna services are in great demand, where you can completely relax and restore vital energy;
  • in the spa, you should definitely undergo cosmetic and relaxation procedures developed according to the Ayurvedic technique traditional for India;
  • in the wellness center you can use the services of a qualified doctor or a specialist in alternative medicine;
  • be sure to visit the hotel restaurant to learn all the intricacies of Indian cuisine;
  • Have a colorful cocktail or your favorite drink in the cozy bar;
  • You can have a snack during the day in the diner, where the most popular dishes typical for fast-food establishments are presented;
  • near the reception there is an Internet club where you can use stationary computers connected to the cable network;
  • upon reservation, food from the restaurant will be delivered directly to your room;
  • with all your questions or problems regarding accommodation, you can contact the reception, which is open 24 hours a day (here you can make a currency exchange);
  • book an interesting and fascinating tour at the tourist office;
  • in order that your things do not take up too much space in the room, hand them in to the luggage storage room;
  • if necessary, a qualified nanny will look after your children;
  • wash and iron your clothes in the laundry room;
  • business negotiations or seminars can be organized in the conference room;
  • You can be provided with a special decorated room for a banquet;
  • at the reception, guests are invited to use the safe;
  • The resort complex has several shops.




Helpful information

Going to the hotel Varca Le Palms Beach Resort 3 *, it is useful to get acquainted with the following information:

  • When planning a trip and calculating your time, please note that check-in and check-out are at noon;
  • children under the age of 5 years stay free of charge, provided that they do not require the installation of an extra bed;
  • an extra bed will cost 1,500 rupees per night for both children and adults;
  • Please note that the apartment cannot accommodate more than one extra bed;
  • When planning your vacation for holidays, you should be prepared for the fact that the price of a gala dinner will include the price of a gala dinner;
  • in no case bring pets or other animals to the hotel - this is strictly prohibited;
  • newlyweds, those who have a birthday, as well as guests celebrating their wedding anniversary, are entitled to receive a nice compliment in the form of a cake (fruits are also provided for a birthday);
  • You have the opportunity to pay for services through a plastic card through the terminal.




Positive reviews

It is important to know the opinions of tourists before a trip to Varca Palms Beach Resort 3 *. The reviews reflect the following positive aspects of living in this hotel complex:

  • if guests arrive at the hotel before noon, they are invited to go for breakfast;
  • there is no additional charge for using the safe at the reception;
  • free Wi-Fi is available in the lobby area;
  • fruit is offered for breakfast every day;
  • large green area;
  • the travel agency organizes very interesting and informative excursions.




Negative reviews

Reviews about this resort contain the following negative points:

  • Despite the fact that the estimated time is set for noon, in fact, settlement occurs much later;
  • very old furniture, everything needs repair in the room;
  • the hotel has a lot of insects, and especially in the dining room;
  • the hotel rooms have very thin walls, which is why they have a high level of noise;
  • tap water is almost cold;
  • neither the employees nor the hotel administration pay attention to the behavior of local vacationers;
  • almost no cleaning is done in the room (the floors can periodically sweep up).
