Midia Grand Resort 3 *: hotel description

The advantages of Bulgaria include the lack of a language barrier, the possibility of independent movement around the country. Excellent cuisine, the color of old Bulgaria, its folklore, ethnic identity - sufficient conditions to make the vacation unforgettable. Accommodation in a three-star hotel on the beach provides a good opportunity to combine a beach holiday and excursion programs.





Hotel Description Midia Grand Resort 3 *

The apartment complex is located on the first coastline in the ancient Bulgarian city of Aheloy. The historic open-air museum town of Nessebar can be reached in five minutes. A transfer from the international airport in the city of Burgas takes you to the hotel in less than half an hour.

By bus for one lion you can get from Midia Grand Resort 3 * (Aheloy) to Sunny Beach. This is the busiest resort on the southern coast of Bulgaria. There are many entertainment centers, supermarkets, wholesale stores, boutiques and amusement parks.

Accommodation





Guests of the Midia Grand Resort 3 * complex (Bulgaria) are offered a choice of several accommodation options. One and two bedroom apartments, double rooms and a panoramic apartment. All rooms have a sea view. Each has adjustable air conditioning, cable TV, high-speed Internet, a fridge with a minibar, replenished for an additional charge.

  1. One bedroom apartment. The room has two beds, which can be divided to accommodate TWIN. In the living room there is a sofa, a bathroom with shower.
  2. Two bedroom apartment. In this category of rooms at the Midia Grand Resort 3 * hotel is convenient for families with children. In each of the two bedrooms there are two beds, in the living room there is a folding sofa and the possibility of placing on an extra bed. The room has its own terrace with sea views. Family or company can accommodate up to 5 people.
  3. Double Room. The room has two beds for the main places. You can install a chair-bed or a sofa bed on request, depending on the size of the room. Convenient for the economical stay of families of three people.
  4. Panoramic apartments. These rooms have a large open balcony and direct sea views. There are two beds in the bedroom; it is possible to install a sofa or armchair upon request.

Hotel infrastructure and service

All information about services, prices and answers to questions can be obtained at the reception, which offers round-the-clock guest service. On the territory of the hotel Midia Grand Resort 3 * There is a beauty salon (massage services), minimarket, souvenir shops, a conference room. The complex has its own parking and car rental service, currency exchange, laundry.





For sports and maintaining good shape, you can visit the fitness center, sports grounds (volleyball, soccer, tennis). You can choose from board games, billiards. On the territory there are two swimming pools with a relaxation area, free sun loungers and umbrellas.

The animation team works in the daytime and evening. Children under two years stay free in the room. For them, the hotel has a specially equipped playground, a section in the pool, a games room.

Nutrition

On the territory of the Midia Grand Resort 3 * complex there is the main Midia restaurant with indoor halls and open spaces on the terraces. You can eat according to the "buffet" concept or order a menu for breakfast, lunch, dinner. Traditional Bulgarian and European dishes are offered.

The lobby bar in the reception area provides a good selection of coffee and strong drinks. The pool bar offers cocktails, refreshing drinks.





Tours and activities

Bulgaria is interesting in terms of excursions. If you have an open Schengen visa, you can set aside a couple of days to visit Europe. A Bulgarian visa does not give such an opportunity. Ticket purchase is available in Burgas. If you relax in Aheloy, Midia Grand Resort 3 * hotel, Nessebar should be visited more than once. This is the nearest sightseeing object that will open the whole flavor of Bulgarian culture.

  • Nessebar. This is a peninsula that is connected to the mainland by a thin isthmus. The open-air museum city provides an opportunity to immerse yourself in the everyday life of old Bulgaria, taste seafood dishes in the tavern , wander through the ruins of ancient fortresses, visit churches and numerous souvenir shops.
