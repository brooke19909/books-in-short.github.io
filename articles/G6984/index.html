Belarus hotels: rating, review, description and reviews. Hotel "Belarus", Minsk: overview and guest reviews

Belarus is famous for its abundance of clean rivers and lakes, endless forests, unique reserves, national parks.

A huge number of tourists come here to get in touch with nature, rich historical heritage, traditions. Belarus’s hotels of various categories have opened their doors for them all over the country, from elite to budget ones.

The capital, the city of Minsk, has concentrated a lot of hotel facilities in the center and surrounding areas.

President Hotel 5 *

Elite hotel complex consists of:

  • 154 rooms from standard to apartments;
  • 2 restaurants;
  • spa / wellness center;
  • indoor pool;
  • saunas, baths;
  • a beauty salon;
  • travel agencies;
  • billiard room;
  • ATMs
  • car rental, bicycles;
  • parking.

Dry cleaning, laundry, ironing, and shoe shine services are provided. Use of the Internet, a fitness room is free. There is a transfer to / from the airport.



The rooms are decorated in an elegant classic style. Air conditioning, TV, radio, telephone, minibar fill the room with comfort. The attached bathrooms are equipped with toiletries, towels, a bathrobe with slippers and a hairdryer.

Reviews

A lot of positive feedback confirms the high status of the institution - great, chic, comfortable, tasty!

The general sense of unsatisfactory certification of the service can be expressed by the words: “There is nothing perfect,” - expensive breakfasts, pillows with synthetic fillings, a wall was visible in the room window, little entertainment, they provided an unbearable transfer machine.

Hotel Minsk 4 *

The hotel complex is a leader among 4-star analogues. The residential building is included in the list of architectural monuments.

For the settlement are offered 1-room rooms with a spectacular well-groomed interior for 1-2 persons, 2-3-room suites and superior apartments with a fireplace, a presidential room with a dining and working area, 2 bedrooms, a sauna.



Guests can use satellite LCD-TV, minibar, safe, long-distance telephone, hairdryer. There is always tea / coffee and appropriate utensils. Free Wi-Fi, fitness, parking.

A large package of services is provided:

  • visa support;
  • currency exchange, ATMs;
  • rental of business offices;
  • transfer to all transport nodes;
  • spa center, beauty salon (massage, beauty treatments, hairdresser);
  • hammam, Finnish steam bath, jacuzzi;
  • casino.

For guests are open restaurants with national and European cuisine, buffet. There is a lobby bar in the lobby.

Reviews

Most tourists had a positive impression:

  • Impeccable cleanliness of the room, bed linen;
  • in the bathrooms - slippers, bathrobes, cosmetics;
  • polite Russian-speaking staff;
  • Great central location.

Demanding customers found flaws:

  • limited selection of dishes (but the cuisine is excellent);
  • was too hot;
  • insufficiently lit room;
  • interiors with a touch of the Soviet era.

Hotel "Belarus" (Minsk) 3 *

The 22-story building rises above the river. Svisloch (city center). More than 500 rooms are classified as deluxe, apartments, business class.

The rooms have shower / WC, refrigerator, cable TV, telephone, Wi-Fi, mini-bar.



Despite 3 stars, the Belarus (Minsk) hotel has an infrastructure that is not much inferior to status institutions:

  • 3 restaurant halls, grill / snack bars;
  • sauna, swimming pools, gym, tennis court;
  • night club, disco, casino;
  • business center;
  • currency exchange, safes;
  • transfer to / from the airport, parking, car rental;
  • Souvenir shop;
  • hairdresser, laundry, dry cleaning.

Reviews

Most of the guests highly appreciate the Belarus Hotel. The reviews concerned the successful design and cleanliness of the rooms, the friendliness of the staff, good food.

But there are claims that:

  • it is impossible to reliably close the door from the inside;
  • from the movement of a person sleeping at night in the corridor, a light-on sensor is activated;
  • low quality internet connection, pipipax;
  • far metro, kiosks with SIM cards, affordable cafes.

East Time Hotel 2 *

"East Time" refers to the budget category of hotels. Its location far from the center, next to the Vostochny bus station, at first glance seems like a drawback. However, having saved on a rent for accommodation, tourists can make bus trips around the cities every day both for sightseeing and shopping. Municipal transport regularly goes to the center - the final stop is nearby.

Clean comfortable 1-3-bed rooms with bathrooms are furnished with new furniture, there is a TV, Internet. The 24-hour front desk will provide a kettle and a hairdryer. Buffet breakfast included. As the best hotels in Belarus, East Time provides free Wi-Fi.



Reviews

Former guests celebrate the attention of the staff, comfortable beds, fresh linen, delicious food.

The motivation for dissatisfaction was the following facts:

  • Wi-Fi is caught only at the bottom;
  • the muzzle from the windows;
  • did not like the coffee.

Park Hotel Zamkovy 4 *

Belarus hotels in numerous cultural centers of the country (Vitebsk, Grodno, Gomel, Brest, etc.) sometimes surpass the capital's charm and chic.

In Gomel, the park-hotel of the Zamkovy luxury class has recently opened. Its architecture combines the style of Old Slavonic castles and modern trends in the decoration of buildings. This feature can be traced in exquisite interior interiors, landscape design. The hotel territory is part of the famous Gomel Park over the river. Sozh.

42 rooms are equipped according to international standards:

  • a restaurant with a banquet hall, a 24-hour bar;
  • billiards, sauna, parking;
  • conference room for 50 people.

Beautiful surroundings, unobtrusive attention of the staff have to hold business meetings, weddings, romantic vacations. This is confirmed by the feedback from visitors.



Hotel "Belarus", Krasnaya Polyana

There is a popular hotel facility outside the country. Spa Hotel Belarus (Krasnaya Polyana, Sochi) is located on a slope, it resembles a large mountain chalet.

Here is a good base for a good rest:

  • indoor and outdoor pools;
  • tennis court, billiards;
  • sauna, bathhouse, solarium, massage room;
  • ski equipment rental.

The rooms - TVs, air conditioning, free internet. Attached bathrooms include showers, hygiene kits, and hair dryers. Restaurants offer a menu and buffet, snack bar - light snacks.

Hotel "Belarus" (Krasnaya Polyana) was opened in 2014 and has already taken a lot of vacationers for recovery.

Reviews

Guests with rare exceptions are unanimous that the kitchen could be tastier and more varied, the scatter of spa treatments throughout the complex is uncomfortable, and TV catches few channels.

But everyone set a high score for fresh air, beautiful mountain views, a large park, and fascinating skiing.

Belarusian hotels use the world practice of online booking, discounts on rent and all kinds of promotions.
