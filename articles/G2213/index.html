Travel to Israel. Herzliya - fashionable resort

In the south-west of Asia, there is a beautiful state - Israel. Herzliya is a resort town. It is located in the southern part of the Sharon Valley. Its location is very favorable, since it is only 12 km from the largest city of Israel (Tel Aviv). In time, the road will take only 15-20 minutes. Herzliya's main advantage is the Mediterranean Sea. The coast is well developed infrastructure. The beaches are equipped in accordance with European standards. Those who want to spend their holidays unforgettably need to choose Israel.

Herzliya, although a small city, but very expensive. This must be taken into account when planning a vacation. The area on which the settlement has grown is about 24 square meters. km And the population has almost reached the mark of 100 thousand people. Herzliya is part of the Gush Dan agglomeration.



A bit of history

The city began to be built on the abandoned land of the Sharon Valley. In the first place, Herzliya was to become a major agricultural center. The fertile soils of the valley and the favorable climate of the area contributed to this. The first settlers were American Jews: only 7 people. The year of the founding of the city of Herzliya - 1924. And he received the name in honor of a Jewish politician. His name is Theodor Herzl. In 1960, Herzliya was officially given the status of a city. On the emblem are 7 stars, which symbolize the first seven settlers.

Population

The proximity to Tel Aviv and the Mediterranean coast very quickly contributed to the increase in population in Herzliya. In the 1960s, the number of inhabitants increased to 35 thousand people, and in 2010 it began to double this number. In recent years, the population level is approaching the mark of 100 thousand.



Characteristic

After the war of independence, the state began to gradually develop. Now all over the world Israel is recognized as the best tourist and medical research center. Herzliya is a city that played an important role in this. The development of tourism was facilitated by the location on the Mediterranean coast. The city has a typical Mediterranean climate. There is a long sunny summer and mild short winters. The average temperature in the warm season varies between +30 ¬∞ C. In winter, it does not fall below +20 ¬∞ C.

Currently, the city consists of two regions: the resort area and the prestigious Herzliya Pituach district. The tourist site is the territory of the Mediterranean coast. Here you will find chic city hotels, villas, yacht clubs and everything you need for a rich vacation.



Hotels and hotel complexes

Hotels are very popular among tourists in the city. Herzliya (Israel), as mentioned above, is a fashionable resort where various high-level services are provided to guests. Of course, such comfort will cost a lot. But the impressions received will fully pay off all the costs incurred. So, let's look at where you can stay while relaxing in this beautiful city:

  • Orchid Okeanos Suites. A luxury hotel with all necessary amenities. The architecture of the building is such that from all sides there are wonderful views of the sea thanks to panoramic windows.
  • Luxury Sea. The hotel is located directly on the beach. It offers visitors everything you need. The building is surrounded by numerous restaurants and bars. Near the hotel there is a yacht club where you can rent a boat.
  • Michelle Suites. The hotel complex is located in a residential building. This hotel is significantly different from the tall buildings of the city. But it also provides all kinds of services for visitors, ranging from free parking and a huge pool. Each room has a balcony or terrace. And the beach is 20 meters from the hotel.

Herzliya Pituach

In the Herzliya Pituach area, the local population lives. According to statistics, most of the richest residents of Israel live here. On this territory beautiful villas and country houses have been erected. It is also worth noting that this area is an information and medical center of the city.

All the inhabitants of the planet know that some of the best doctors in the world work in a country like Israel. Herzliya often receives foreigners who come here for treatment. The local medical center will not only provide qualified medical assistance, but also offer the most comfortable conditions for the treatment period. Specialization of the institution: the most complex cardiovascular diseases, inflammation of the skin and problems of the musculoskeletal system. In addition, the Herzliya Pituach area is distinguished by the development of information technology. Also in the city there are electronic, textile and food industries, precision processing.



sights

Currently, the city of Herzliya (Israel) is actively being built up with new residential complexes, scientific and technological offices. Huge budget funds are spent on infrastructure development. Among the attractions you can highlight the Apollonia National Historical Park, the Sydney Ali Mosque, and the Tel Michal Archaeological Site. Not far from the city there are two more national parks: Yarkon and Tel Afek and Herzliya. They organize tourist excursions. Here you can meet representatives of local flora and fauna, see the beauty of picturesque nature, admire the lakes, ponds. On the territory of the parks there are pedestrian and bicycle paths, picnic places.
