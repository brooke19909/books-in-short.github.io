Zoo, Chelyabinsk: work schedule, ticket price, address and photo

Have you decided to visit Chelyabinsk? The zoo, the photo of which you see in our article, is a very good option for spending holidays not only for children, but also for adults.

Each parent is interested in the question of where to go with the child, where to spend time with the benefit for the development of the baby. Each city has a set of such places. The larger the village, the more options you can find.





How to spend time with a child in a big city? There is a large selection of various children's activities. You can visit the theater, cafe or take a walk in the park, there are also labyrinth rooms in entertainment centers.

In addition to theaters, parks and shopping centers, a good place for a joint holiday with children is the zoo. Chelyabinsk is no exception. This city has a modern large animal park, which offers not only to look at the animals, but also to touch some of them.

The institution can become for young people a place for wonderful romantic dates. At least this is unusual. Such a walk will especially appeal to you on a hot summer day.

The cost of a ticket to the zoo is low, for adults and children from 12 years old - one hundred rubles, for children from 5 to 12 years old - fifty rubles, kids under five are free of charge.





In the zoo of the city of Chelyabinsk, you can see many species of primates, predators, amphibians and birds. Many animals came here from other zoos in the world. In addition to entertainment, the organization is conducting scientific work.

A visit to the zoo is not only a walk in the fresh air. This is an acquaintance with wild and domestic animals, a study of their lifestyle. You can not only take a walk around the park, but also attend developmental classes on topics of interest to your child. It is interesting that such lessons are completely free, students pay only the entrance ticket to the zoo. And this cannot but rejoice: nowadays, less and less you can find quality free services for children.

Activities for children. Bagira Club

Those children who are interested in biology are waiting for the Bagira club of young biologists at the zoo. Young animals choose an animal, observe it, and record all information in diaries. This activity is of great importance so that the child can prove himself in biology classes, reading reports, telling classmates about his notes.

The zoo (Chelyabinsk) has long been famous for high-quality, interesting work with children.

Contact zoo

As you know, communication with animals has a good effect on health. The dog, sitting at the bedside of the sick owner, did a lot for his recovery, almost as much as the doctors who treated him. For children who love animals, there is a petting zoo in the park.





Everyone loves to touch kitties and puppies. But where else can you see little decorative hedgehogs, little ducks, chickens and piglets? Sure, only here! This department is located in the zoo, but a ticket there is purchased separately. The proceeds are mainly spent on animal feed and on their maintenance. How much is the entrance to the contact zoo (Chelyabinsk)? The ticket price is only 30 rubles, kids under one and a half years pass for free. Please note that the contact zoo is open during the warmer months, from May to September, from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. (Friday, Saturday and Sunday - an hour later), the rest of the time - on Friday, weekends and holidays from 11 a.m. to 4 p.m. if the air temperature is not lower than plus 10 (this happens in the Urals in October and April).

By the way, on the territory of this zoo ride a pony. Of course, such walks are not carried out every day. You can ride a pony from Friday through Sunday. Opening hours - from 11:00 to 18:30. There is also lunch, the duration of which is exactly 60 minutes (from 13:00 to 14:00).

It is always pleasant not only to visit the zoo, but also to learn a lot of new and interesting things.

Zoo news. Exemplary feeding of animals

This magical place, as always, surprises its visitors, especially in the summer. For example, in July of this year, an entertaining experiment for children was held. Children love to chew! It is especially good that professionals observed this process. They directed children's energy in the right direction.

Recently, zoo was born in the zoo. A contest for the best names for these funny tiny kittens has been announced.





It happens that people plan to come to the park while feeding animals, but the daily regimen for different animals is different. This problem was solved by the zoo. Chelyabinsk in this case was the first among other cities to organize an indicative feeding of animals. This means that you can come at a certain time and see how the animals eat. The event takes place on Fridays at exactly 13.00.





Spectators do not get bored: quizzes are held with them, they are told interesting information about the animals that participate in the indicative feeding.

Office hours

Choose a time convenient for you to visit the zoo (Chelyabinsk). Opening hours: in the summer - from 10 to 20 hours, the ticket office - until 19 hours. In winter, the institution works from 10 to 18 hours, the ticket office - until 17 hours, since during this period the demand for this type of service decreases slightly. Winter is traditionally considered the period from October 15 to April 15.

Zoo address

This information is simply necessary for those who decided to visit the Chelyabinsk Zoo. The address of the institution is 191 Truda Street. This animal world, where sincere people work, where they know how to deal with children, is always waiting for guests in its vast spaces.

Now you know that in Chelyabinsk there is an amazing place - a zoo on the territory of which will be interesting to both children and adults. Come here more often, give pleasure and pleasant emotions to loved ones, dear people.
