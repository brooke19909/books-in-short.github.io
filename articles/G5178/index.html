Weather

The Logoisk ski complex is located north of Minsk and opens its season in late November or early December, with the establishment of stable cold weather. The time for active skiing at the end of March is ending.

At the ski resort in Logoisk there are no sharp temperature drops, strong winds and frosts. Winter here is usually mild, snowy. The snow cover is dense and durable, its thickness reaches 30-45 cm.
