Balakovo Hotels: choose the best!

Balakovo is a Russian city located in the Saratov region. It is located only 161 km from Saratov on the picturesque left bank of the Volga River. What is this town famous for and what hotels of Balakovo can tourists choose?

1762 is the year when Empress Catherine II issued an official manifesto allowing the Old Believers to return to their homeland. With this manifesto, the history of the city began. Settlers who returned from abroad, and laid the first stone of the town.

Hotels of Balakovo: "Aragvi"

The ideal place to relax in Balakovo is the Aragvi complex. Here you can not only get enough sleep, but also have fun mentally. At the disposal of customers are Finnish and Russian baths, VIP rooms, a cozy area where each client can fry the barbecue himself. Fresh air and fresh beer simply cannot but please the soul! The variety of cuisine of the local cafe can surprise any true gourmet.





The client is also pleased with the opportunity to pay by credit card (prices from 2400 rubles per night), close proximity to the city center (a little over four kilometers). Balakovo hotels are able to welcome dear guests with hospitality!

Guest house "Zarechny"

Located in the very center of the city, the Zarechny guest house is rightfully called the best hotel in Balakovo. Silence and clean air, a beautiful view created by the hands of skilled landscape designers and nature itself, will delight customers at home. But despite the pristine appearance, the service is at its best. Visitors will be offered everything that a modern person needs: a phone, a computer, free Wi-Fi. For guests arriving in Balakovo in a personal vehicle, a comfortable and secure parking is provided. Restaurant "Zarechny" offers the best dishes of Russian and European cuisine. There is a banquet hall for 32 people, where you can celebrate a birthday or corporate party. The interior of each room is restrained and elegant, executed in light pastel colors, the view from the window is pleasant. A hot and tasty breakfast is included in the price. It also has a sauna and an excellent pool. Rest in "Zarechny" is good at any time of the year!
