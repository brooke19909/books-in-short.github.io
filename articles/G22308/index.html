Therefore, you should take information from the reviews of vacationers. But you should also be critical of them. After all, the concept of quality rest is subjective. Give someone some fun partying, while another wants peace and quiet. The same can be said about the territory of the hotel, its rooms, beach, pool, food, animation ... almost everything. And what is the hotel "Alara Star 5 *" (Turkey)? We will talk about this in our article. No advertising - just a thoughtful analysis of the reviews of tourists.





This hotel (as, indeed, the entire region of the Anatolian Riviera) is loved by visitors from Russia. This fact has its advantages. Closest to the hotel among the cities of the Republic of Turkey is Alanya. The Alara Star Hotel (5 stars) is only twenty-five kilometers away. It so happens that Alara Star guests are among the last to arrive at the vacation spot. Transfer from the airport to the hotel often takes about three hours. But, comforted reviews, the road is easily overcome. Often stops are made at the cafe, and the guide interestingly talks about Turkey and the sights floating outside the window. You can also get in another way, from various international airports of the country. Buses are well developed in Turkey. Turkey, Alanya: Hotel Alara Star 5 *

In fact, this is not even a hotel, but a complex. On the top of the rocky peninsula there are two hotels: the five-star Alara Star and the budget three with the same name - Alara Hotel. Since the owner of the hotel complex is single, visitors can use the entire infrastructure: beaches, pools, bars. Animation and entertainment are also common. This, of course, is a greater bonus for Treshka guests than for those who have chosen the 5-star Alara Star Hotel for their holidays. Alara Star (Turkey) consists of one six-story building with an elevator. Eighty percent of the rooms in this building have direct or side sea views.





Alas, the reviews warn that at the height of the season it may happen that you will be accommodated in those rooms whose balconies "look" towards the mainland.
