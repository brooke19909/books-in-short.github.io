How much is a diagonal of 7 inches? How to convert to centimeters?

So we could determine the diagonal of the screen by eye and decide for ourselves whether it is a lot or a little. The easiest way to figure it out is to convert inches to centimeters. Connoisseurs know that one inch equals 2.54 centimeters. Based on this, it is easy to calculate how much it is - a diagonal of 7 inches. It will be equal to 17.78 centimeters. Take an ordinary ruler and see what kind of segment it will be. TV diagonal





If you are trying to pick up a TV, then one of the important parameters is its length between two opposite edges - lower and upper. This is the diagonal of the TV. Modern models are available with different diagonals. There are very large televisions with a diagonal of 50 or more inches. But as for the seven inches, this is extremely small for the TV. Perhaps there are no such models with such a miniature diagonal. However, the market has special TVs for cars or for traveling, but such devices have been popular for a long time. Now they are replaced by tablets.
