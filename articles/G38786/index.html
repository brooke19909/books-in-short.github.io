How to sew a do-it-yourself sun skirt on an elastic band

This article contains tips on how to sew a sun skirt on an elastic band. This element of the wardrobe is popular for more than a dozen years among women of different ages. With the help of such a skirt, you can emphasize the beautiful line of the hips, slender legs or, conversely, hide the wide hips behind the flowing fabric. If you like beautiful things, then you just need to get this element of clothing.

Sewing a skirt on an elastic band is very easy!

The main thing is to choose a beautiful fabric, on which the appearance of the product will depend. Either it will easily fall down your hips, or it will be hard and cumbersome to sit on you. So pay attention to the selection of material. Also get an elastic band of medium width, threads to match the fabric. It is advisable to wash and iron the material before use. After all the preparations, you can start cutting.





How to sew a sun skirt on an elastic band without a pattern?

It is not so difficult to do this without a pre-prepared drawing. The main thing is to correctly and accurately apply subsequent measurements to the fabric. First of all, we measure the circumference of the hips. A piece of fabric is folded in half. We will postpone measurements on the fabric from the corner. We measure the radius equal to the circumference of the hips + 5 centimeters for allowances. Divide the obtained value by 6. Seamlessly draw a circle line with a given radius. Then we calculate the following measurement: the required length of the product + first radius. We also set this value aside from the corner and draw a circle line with a second radius. So we came directly to the question of how to sew a sun skirt on an elastic band. From the folded fabric we cut two identical parts. We do not need fabric from the corner to the first line. We fold these two elements with their faces inward and sew along the sides. Iron the seam and process the material on the overlock. After that, we prepare a belt, the sizes of which are equal to the circumference of the hips with allowances for the seam, the height of the belt is at your discretion, about 5-7 centimeters. Fold it in half and stitch along the lower edge, while bending the fabric inward. Iron with iron so that the seam is not on the front side. Under the belt we attach the bent upper edge of the skirt. The product will be ready after processing the hem.





What to wear with a sun skirt?

It looks great not only with tight-fitting T-shirts and blouses, but also with lightweight straight-cut jackets. Almost everything fits this skirt. In terms of shoes, you can wear both high-heeled shoes or platform shoes and low-heeled ballet flats.





How to sew a sun skirt on the floor?

Today, long skirts, dresses, sundresses have become very fashionable. Yes, and not everyone suits short clothes, so many began to wonder how to sew a sun skirt on an elastic band. But to do this is just as easy, you just need to stock up on a lot of material. And for such a length, it is better to sew a wide belt, which will make your figure even more slim. If your lines are perfect, then the elastic can be left a small width. The product looks great, the belt and base of which are made of different materials that complement each other.
