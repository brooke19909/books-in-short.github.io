Salted dough lamb: how to make a similar product?

Earlier, the development of children implies a joint knowledge of the new and the transformation of the information obtained by the method of play and labor. A great example of a child's adaptation to existing concepts is crafts. In a playful and completely relaxed atmosphere, the child perceives the shape of objects, their meaning and functionality. In order for the child, having fun, to perceive the animal world, one can make a lamb from salt dough with it.





Such miniatures can be perfectly adapted to the company of Christmas toys, as well as used in the design or holiday addition of the room. Giving the animals obtained with the help of paint liveliness and energy, they can be safely used as objects for counting while teaching children. The main rule for creating any crafts remains fantasy and goodwill, then any objects acquire a crushing charge of positive and warmth.

A lamb from salted dough, the photo of which you see in our article, will turn out beautifully for young children. Such animals are prepared from a mass that does not contain harmful or chemical additives. Due to the suppleness and convenient structure, the test bases perfectly affect the baby's fine motor skills, do not irritate the skin and do not cause him bewilderment or fear.

Sheep, lamb from salted dough

Any parental initiative or desire should be discussed with the child: the more benevolently the idea is presented, the more calmly it will be accepted.





If the offspring agreed, you can safely proceed to the preparation of high-quality salt dough. This requires the following:

  • a glass of good sifted flour;
  • 125-130 ml of pure tepid water;
  • 120 g of fine salt;
  • 2 tbsp. tablespoons of not very liquid PVA glue.

Manufacture

To mold a lamb from salt dough, it is necessary to properly knead the working base. The first step is to mix all the dry ingredients - it is better to use a small whisk, so the process will go easier. Then comes the moment for the fluid. Glue with a thin stream must be poured into water and mix well. Only the finished mixture can be poured into the dry part. In order to get elastic dough in the future, you need to slowly knead it. Then the finished product must be wrapped in polyethylene and sent to the refrigerator for 30-35 minutes.





The creative stage can be carried out with the little one together - the lamb shape should be chosen together, so the child will not feel like an outcast. Modern masters often publish stencils or forms for their fans, but you can also draw a future toy yourself, endowing it with some features. A scientist lamb from salt dough or a sheep architect (artist) will look fun. The moment of discussion of subtleties will provide the foundation for the development of imagination. You can reinforce the efforts of the child with cartoonish examples.

To get streamlined figures, you need to roll out the base and try to create a uniform thickness throughout the layer of dough (so the legs or head will not differ from the rest of the body). You should not stop only on the creation of one beast, if the offspring has gone into a rage, you can develop activities to a small zoo.

Carefully drawn (printed) mold must be applied carefully to the test (not allowing them to completely stick together) and cut along the contour. When the mold is free from dough, you can supplement the composition with ears of residues, curls of wool and a tail (you can create using the garlic).

You can complement the composition with meadow herbs, flowers or a wreath.

The last manufacturing stage





In order for the lamb from the salt dough to dry thoroughly, it can be placed in the summer on the open balcony for several days or baked in a home oven. The temperature should be set to a minimum so that small parts do not fall off, and the figure itself does not crack. After the oven, you can give the products a good rest and lower the temperature. That's practically ready and lamb from salt dough. Coloring can be started the next day, so the base will not get wet and will keep its shape and color well.

A toy

In order for the lamb from salt dough to serve as a Christmas tree decoration, he needs to make a loop. You can choose to match the actual colors on the figure, but you can not drive yourself into any frames and diversify the models with juicy details.

Some mothers advise creating a lamb in detail, complementing the toy with unusual parts: the legs can be made of twine, and the legs can be left out of dough or replaced with colorful beads. Another option is to create a wreath on the head of the animal from pieces of tissue. Also, the sheep can be woven a necklace of beads or hang a mini bag on the foot, thus marking fashionistas in the bestial company. To photograph the result, you can paint the product with acrylics or using a spray can, so the animals will not be empty pieces of dough, but full-fledged characters.
