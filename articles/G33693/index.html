Galilee, Israel: photos and descriptions of attractions, tours, interesting facts and reviews of tourists

Traveling to Galilee and Israel has always attracted everyone who had the desire to visit the biblical lands, to see nature, as well as architectural monuments and the life of local residents. For centuries, representatives from different countries and cultures have come here. Why is Galileo attractive in Israel? What are its attractions? To find out the answers to these questions, you need to go on a trip to this interesting place on our planet.

Bit of history

Galilee, located in the north of modern Israel, near Libya, was first inhabited by the ancient Canaanite people. After that, its territory was owned by many other nations and powers - Egypt, Israel, Assyria, Babylon. After the capture of Babylon, the mido-Persians of Galileo became the possession of Persia. Two centuries later, Alexander the Great conquered it, making it a Greek colony. And in the 60s of the first century BC, Rome began to own it. In a certain period, Galileo was devastated and began to be inhabited only after 70 AD. In the seventh century, it was annexed to one of the provinces of the Caliphate. Today, Galilee is part of modern Israel. It is divided into Upper Galilee and Lower. From the history it is clear that this land has experienced many events.

Natural scenic spots

Galilee has a beautiful nature, which can attract all connoisseurs and lovers. Beautiful landscapes, attractive sea, majestic mountains - this is just a little that a traveler can see when going on an excursion or hiking. So, what picturesque corners of nature are here in the Galilee in Israel?

Firstly, there are several mountains in Galilee that attract nature lovers. For example, Mount Tabor, which can be perfectly seen from the side of Nazareth. Its height reaches about 600 meters. This mountain attracts all tourists due to its rich biblical history. Another mountain is Arbel, whose height is more than 180 meters. From its top, a bewitching view of the entire Galilee opens, and a mountain spring flows from its rocks. Another mountain is Mount Overthrow. Its highest point is about 400 meters. At its top there is a platform for travelers from which you can see Nazareth and the surrounding area.

Secondly, the Lake of Galilee, which is called the sea. Its shores are considered a popular resort area. Here you can swim, relax with tents and enjoy charming views of the surrounding nature. Children can also have fun in the water rides and in the water park.

Thirdly, national parks and reserves. For example, Banias Park is a wonderful place for tourists. Here is the beginning of the Jordan River, which is formed from a small stream flowing to the Banias Waterfall, after which it becomes a river. There are also several caves in this park.

Monuments of history and architecture

On the territory of Galilee, many archaeological excavations are carried out. Traces of Roman colonization have long been found in the Zipori National Park. A Roman villa, a theater and a synagogue were excavated here. Also interesting places for excursions are the remains of the Belvoir fortress, which impresses with its size, the ancient buildings in Capernaum that carry visitors in ancient times, and Megiddo Park. It is located in the foothills at an altitude of 60 meters. In biblical times, Megiddo played an important strategic role, and trade routes passed near him. The mountain and foothills were the scene of hostilities of the past.

A wonderful place that has preserved the ancient era is Skifopolis. In its place were found an amphitheater and city streets that attract tourists. Several cities, including Acre, Cana, Nazareth, Capernaum, also preserved many ancient monuments in the form of houses, churches and temples, statues, ruins of ancient buildings and much more.

In the description of the Galilee of Israel, one must also add one place that delights visitors and remains in their memory. These are Bahai Gardens. A wonderful and cozy corner for relaxation attracts everyone. There is no such unique landscape anywhere. Therefore, everyone who comes to Haifa is eager to get here.

Another monument of history and architecture is the Nimrod fortress. It is located at an altitude of 800 meters. Many lovers of antiquity come to its ruins. Here they can walk along the secret corridors of the fortress and see massive towers.

Other sights of Galilee

For hikers, the Nahal Snir Nature Reserve is a great place to relax. If you visit this place in spring, you can get into a beautiful flowering garden full of pleasant aromas. Tourists are provided with three hiking trails. One of the highlights of this place is a long deep stream that adorns the local nature.

A visited place in the Galilee in Israel is a winery bearing the name of the city - Katzrin. This factory is famous for its fine wines. Here, tourists can follow the entire process of making this favorite drink.
