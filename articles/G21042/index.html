Sewing Details

A simple instruction on how to sew a T-shirt for a doll will help you make a new thing for a toy fashionista.

We will prepare the necessary materials:

  • pattern;
  • fabric, flaps;
  • crayon;
  • scissors;
  • pins
  • threads and needles.

A step-by-step instruction describes how to sew a T-shirt for a Barbie doll:

  1. Iron the rags so that there are no wrinkles or clamps.
  2. Attach the pattern to the wrong side of the fabric, secure with a pin and draw around the pattern with chalk.
  3. Without removing the pins, carefully cut the details of the t-shirt.
  4. If you plan to make a certain decor on the front of the t-shirt, it is better to do this before embroidering the elements: embroidery, beading, drawing.
  5. With a needle and thread we sweep the details of the clothes.
  6. If the fabric crumbles, it is better to hem the edges of the neck, the bottom of the top, and the sleeve.
  7. We sew a T-shirt on a typewriter or sew it by hand from the wrong side. We turn on the front side.
  8. Sew on the back with Velcro.
