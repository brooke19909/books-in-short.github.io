Vacuum cleaner Philips FC 9174: description, specifications, reviews

Manufacturers of modern vacuum cleaners offer ever new technological solutions that facilitate the cleaning process. Radical changes in the segment were made by the concept of bagless vacuum cleaners, which are equipped with aquafilters. At the same time, classic models do not lose popularity. This class represents the Philips FC 9174, endowed with a traditional design and high power. Despite belonging to a family of models with a typical form factor, this version received many original technologies.

Model Overview

Philips in this case relied on a combination of performance and functionality. The unit turned out to be quite massive, so it is difficult to attribute it to successful creations in terms of ergonomics. In the basic version, the Philips FC 9174/01 vacuum cleaner with a dust collector is equipped with a wide range of modern cleaning tools. The basis of this set is a turbo brush that successfully copes with dust and hair. Actually, due to the high suction capacity, there are practically no problems with the quality of cleaning.





Understanding the weaknesses of all powerful vacuum cleaners, the manufacturer sought to eliminate their impact on the operating process as much as possible. In particular, sound insulation was implemented well, although comparison with compact versions with a power of up to 1,500 W is out of the question. The physical control of the Philips FC 9174 case is facilitated by convenient handles in the design and rubber wheels. These add-ons make it easy to handle the unit, whose weight is more than 6 kg.

Specifications

If small low-power vacuum cleaners attract the consumer with their maneuverability and compact size, then options from the top link benefit from the power potential. It cannot be said that this model demonstrates out-of-the-box performance indicators, but a competently implemented design with functional organs allows optimal spending of the implemented potential.





So, the technical specifications of the Philips FC 9174 are as follows:

  • The force of the air flow is 47 l / s.
  • Input power level - 2,200 watts.
  • Soundproofing - 78 dB.
  • Suction power potential - 500 watts.
  • The vacuum pressure is 34 kPa.
  • The capacity of the dust bag is 4 liters.
  • The weight of the vacuum cleaner is 6.3 kg.
  • Cleaning radius - 10 m.
  • Cable length - 7 m.

Vacuum cleaner features

The model is a classic solution for a vacuum cleaner with a separate dust collector. At the same time, the developers provided the device with technological and productive nozzles from the TriActive family. The differences of this device include three-time action in the cleaning process. In particular, the Philips FC 9174 vacuum cleaner can simultaneously capture large debris through a large front opening, collect dirt and dust in hard-to-reach places, and also clean furniture surfaces.





This means that during cleaning, the user does not have to change nozzles depending on the specific working conditions - TriActive devices can cope with almost all the tasks of household cleaning. Also, the vacuum cleaner is characterized by high environmental friendliness and sanitary-hygienic effect. In addition to getting rid of dust and debris, it provides easy air cleaning, eliminating allergens.

Accessories for the Philips FC 9174 model

The model is equipped with dust collectors and special filters, thanks to which in many ways it is possible to achieve high cleaning performance. S-bags have a high-strength structure and can hold up to 4 liters. Actually, this accessory determined the considerable dimensions of the device, thanks to which the operator can work longer without replacing the dust collector. In addition, the Philips FC 9174 is equipped with HEPA AirSeal filters. The mentioned function of air purification in the room is realized precisely due to these filters. The system absorbs air, treats it, and then releases it without any dust particles.





HEPA filters have various modifications, so for each case it is worth using an element with suitable characteristics.

Positive feedback about the model

Owners emphasize the high cleaning efficiency in almost all modes. The suction capacity of increased power ensures the removal of dust and debris in a matter of moments. The reliability of the case with accessories is also noted. The combination of plastic and metal components, as noted by users, has been successfully completed from a design point of view. In general, many emphasize the stylistic advantages that a Philips FC 9174 vacuum cleaner has.

Reviews regarding ease of use are also found, but not so often. In this regard, we can mention a rather large radius of action and a good layout of the working bodies. It is worth noting that the developers were able to neutralize many of the shortcomings inherent in traditional models with dust collectors. This, first of all, refers to a well-thought-out filtration system, which not only delays the accumulated litter, but also provides a passive air washing in the room.

Negative reviews

Most of the negative feedback is addressed to ergonomics. This is understandable, since the unit with a capacity of 2,200 W and with a 4-liter dust collector simply cannot but cause inconvenience in operation because of its size.





I must say that this model is not the largest of the high-power segment. But there are also disadvantages in the form of problems with the motor. If immediately after cleaning, the Philips FC 9174 vacuum cleaner ceases to turn on, then it is more likely to be a consequence of overheating. It is a repeated refusal to work most often associated with such phenomena. But this only occurs after working with large areas for a long time.

Conclusion

In recent years, in the segment of household appliances, there has been a tendency to move away from familiar and outdated concepts. But far from always replacing traditional solutions with modern ones gives good results. At least, the efficiency of the classic design of the bag vacuum cleaner is confirmed by the Philips FC 9174. The reviews note that it is able to compete with the best representatives of models with water filters. This applies to both power and functionality with the flexibility of cleaning.





Not so unambiguous comparison in terms of sanitary and hygienic advantages. Of course, the introduction of modern HEPA filters increases the level of bacteriological protection, but competitive lines are constantly evolving in this direction. Moreover, Philips has borrowed air purification products from vacuum cleaners with aquafilters, combining them with traditional dust collectors. The result was a hybrid in which previous technologies were preserved, and modern developments were successfully implemented.
