DIY garbage bag dresses: instructions, photos

Designers around the world surprise us every day with their inventions. A variety of materials are used. At the same time, classic fabric is often used last. Many have already managed to see in the photo clothes made from raw meat or packages made from chips. And the latest fashion trend is clothing from garbage bags. Today, every representative of the fair sex can feel like a real designer. The original cellophane dress can be made at home.

Where to wear a garbage bag dress

Such an outfit, of course, can hardly be called everyday. Yes, and it is not very practical. The cellophane from which the clothes will be made cannot be called hygienic and environmentally friendly. However, each girl can make a dress out of garbage bags for a fashion show. Rather, it is an option for entertainment. If you exploit such clothing for no more than a few hours, it certainly will not bring harm.



New Year's carnival is another reason for the original outfit. With the help of garbage bags, you can create a stunning dress that will surely amaze others. And if you show a little imagination, then in general it will be difficult to guess that the clothes are made of such a piquant material. In addition, dresses from garbage bags do not require special skills and high financial costs when creating them.

What will be required for work?

To make a beautiful outfit, first of all, be patient. After all, a masterpiece does not always work the first time. It is desirable that the product is made by one person. After all, everyone has their own thoughts, ideas that are difficult to implement at the same time.



If we make dresses from garbage bags without using other consumables, then sewing accessories may not be needed. Individual parts will be joined with glue or double-sided tape. An ordinary wooden clothespin will also come to the rescue. It will need to be used for preliminary fastening of individual elements. And, of course, not without scissors. With their help, we will cut out the details of the dress.

In order to create a sophisticated model with tucks and assemblies, you should have special sewing skills. Here you can not do without a needle and thread. But in no case can stitching parts on a sewing machine. Cellophane is a delicate material. In the process, you can simply break it.

Minimalism style

A miniature dress made of garbage bags will look great. A photo of a girl in such a dress will surely amaze others. For the manufacture of the model can be used only one package. Particular attention should be paid to the strength of the starting material. A sturdy trash bag with a capacity of more than one hundred liters is ideal.



The main tool for making the outfit will be scissors. It is necessary to make three holes - for the head and hands. Here, in fact, the dress is done. You can decorate the product with a belt of contrasting cellophane or flowers made also from garbage bags. On such an outfit will be spent a minimum of time and effort.

The main emphasis is on contrast

Really bright model can be made of garbage bags that differ in color, structure and shape. Today, supermarkets have a huge number of garbage bags. The more varied the choice, the better it will be possible to express your imagination. The essence of the method is layering various packages on top of each other. Thus, you can get a lush rustling dress. In addition, such an outfit will have an original color. After all, individual elements can differ significantly from each other.



A fluffy dress is made quite simply. As a basis you will need elastic. Pre-prepared strips cut from garbage bags will be tied on it. The top of the dress can be made on the same principle as in the previous model. That is, in a dense bag, holes for the head and hands are cut. You can also make a corsage. But this option is performed on the model immediately before the release. Parts are fastened with double-sided tape.

Airy dress

Another interesting option for making dresses from garbage bags is to fill the feed with air. To implement such an idea, you need a foundation. It can be an old dress or a knitted t-shirt. Garbage bags are pre-filled with air and tied to a knot. It turns out "balloons." Small packages are best suited. Further, the elements are alternately attached to the bottom of a previously prepared base.



It will take a lot of time to create an original dress. Each "ball" must be tightly fixed. You can use a small thin needle. It is worthwhile to ensure that she does not shatter the package, otherwise all the work of inflating will go down the drain. An outfit of inflated trash bags is the least practical. You can only stand in it. Squat in such a dress will not succeed.

Hook to help

Cellophane is actively used not only in sewing, but also in knitting. Using an ordinary hook, you can make a truly original dress from garbage bags. A photo of such a product can attract many. Indeed, in the picture it will be difficult to recognize the source material. From the side it seems that the outfit is made of ordinary yarn.

In order to tie dresses from garbage bags, you will need hook number 2 and a huge number of garbage bags. It is advisable to stock up on raw material in advance. If it is not enough, it will be difficult to find the right color option.

A dress from garbage bags is knitted very quickly. The instruction is to knit the columns in a circle. It is important to take measurements correctly in advance. After all, cellophane can not be called elastic material.
