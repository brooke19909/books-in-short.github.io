Hotel 4 * Sharm Cliff Resort (Egypt / Sharm El Sheikh): photos and reviews of tourists

Today we will talk about one very popular hotel in Egypt, its positive reviews, advantages, disadvantages, attractions and much more. If you are looking for the perfect place to stay, be sure to come to the Sharm Cliff Resort. Everyone will like it here!

In the center of the resort of Sharm El Sheikh, twenty kilometers from the airport (which has international status) and not far from the vibrant Naama Bay area, which has long been chosen by tourists, there is a 4 * Sharm Cliff Resort. This place abounds not only with hotels, but also with various shops and small shops where you can buy souvenirs for memory of yourself and loved ones, as well as taste local exotic dishes and fruits.

Sea and places for swimming

Most often, vacationers who come to the resort of Sharm El Sheikh are interested in local beaches near the Sharm Cliff Resort (4 stars). The charm of the hotel lies in the presence of two beaches nearby at once.

The first is the cozy little Wind beach ("Wind"), just a five-minute walk from the hotel. The sea is shallow, and there are not too many corals at the bottom. It is suitable for lovers of a quiet relaxing holiday. There is both pleasant music and water activities.

Hadaba (‚ÄúHadaba"), another more popular among swimming and outdoor activities, is located a little further - five kilometers away. You can walk to the hotel from the hotel by going down the stairs to the shore, or take a special bus directly from the hotel. The sea here is deeper, and the abundance of different marine life and corals is such that other resorts in Egypt can envy .



Fans of diving and snorkeling have loved this place for a long time due to the beauty and brightness of its underwater world and clear water. The only thing worth remembering is that on this beach (this applies to any other beach in the Sharm El Sheikh resort), you need to wear special shoes because of the risk of cutting your feet on numerous corals. This warning applies to all who came to rest in Egypt, the Sharm Cliff Resort 4 * hotel is no exception. Believe me, everyone will like it here!

Territory and number of rooms

On a plot of land one and a half square kilometers (on a hill) are 2- and 3-storey buildings 4 * Sharm Cliff Resort. There are standard rooms for two people, in addition, single and family (consisting of two bedrooms).

In each room available:

  • a bathroom with a shower to freshen up after a walk under the scorching Egyptian sun;
  • air conditioning is another important component of a comfortable holiday in the south. The main thing is not to overdo it and not to get too cold, having returned from the heat to a cool room;
  • terrace (in the rooms on the ground floor) or a balcony (in the rooms on the second and third floors). It is so pleasant to sit in the open air in the evening, admiring the views of the city opening from a height, drinking drinks or just reading an interesting book;
  • for the benefits of civilization, each room has satellite TV, as well as a telephone, Wi-Fi access (you can use it for free in the hotel lobby), a hairdryer, a small safe for storing valuables and documents, and a minibar.




In addition, there are several retail outlets in the hotel lobby where you can cheaply buy souvenirs for memory and accessories for a beach holiday: from sunblock to snorkeling equipment and beach slippers.

Excursions

Many tourists, arriving at the resort of Sharm el-Sheikh, do not want to spend all their time at the nearest beaches, restaurants or clubs. And scuba diving is just what will help to make your vacation bright and truly unforgettable.





If you decide to visit Sharm Cliff Resort 4 *, reviews from 2015-2016 will help in choosing the best place for this lesson. For example, you can go on an excursion to a national park called Abu Galum.

After traveling for about two hours in jeeps, tourists find themselves on an amazing golden beach, where clear water gives you the opportunity to admire the diversity of marine life. There are colorful corals and vibrant tropical fish. You can have lunch after swimming with fish. Fresh and melting in the mouth, it is cooked directly with tourists. And then, after riding about a month and a half on horseback, you can immerse yourself in one of the most popular Egyptian places for diving - the Blue Hole (aka Black Hole).

If the tourist does not crave long trips and diving, but wants to enjoy fish and coral reefs, then he can safely go to the island called Tiran, which is so close to Sharm El Sheikh that it is even visible from the coast. Although it will not be possible to land directly on it, a comfortable yacht brings tourists quite close. If you rent snorkeling equipment, you can take a look at the world of the local coral reefs. This place attracts tourists with its beauty and variety of underwater life.

Guests of the 4 * Sharm Cliff Resort, who wish to visit world-famous sights in addition to the surroundings of Sharm El Sheikh during their holidays, have the opportunity to go on 1- and 2-day sightseeing tours to Cairo (where, among other things, you can see the legendary pyramids Giza), Israel or Jordan.
