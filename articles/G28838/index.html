Resorts in Turkey - the perfect place to relax

Resorts in Turkey occupy a leading position in the world in terms of attendance. They are washed by the waters of warm seas, there is a mild climate, amazing landscapes and unique historical monuments.





In climatic conditions, Turkish resorts have small differences, which depend on their location. So, the warmest is the Mediterranean coast, where the subtropical climate prevails with hot, dry summers and warm, humid winters. Also warm weather will please the west of Turkey with the gentle waters of the Aegean Sea. The beach season has the longest duration: from April to the end of October. The northern part of the country with the Black Sea coasts is cooler and rainy. But in the mountainous regions of the eastern part there is a big contrast in temperatures: winter is cold and with heavy rainfall.

The resorts of Turkey are buried in colorful natural landscapes and mountains, chic parks, unique waterfalls and unique sources are located here. One of the famous natural phenomena is Pamukkale - a spring rich in calcium, which created bizarre white cascades here. And such a region as Cappadocia is one of the most beautiful places in the world, it is famous for its mysterious natural formations. Turkey has a long history, ancient architectural structures, amazing palaces and mosques. An excellent assistant for traveling to interesting places will be a map of Turkey, whose resorts are scattered throughout the coastline.





Every year, millions of vacationers come to the Turkish coast , service is constantly being improved and supplemented in the country, which puts Turkey in one of the first places among the countries most loved by tourists. The resorts of Turkey 2013 opened many new hotels, with chic rooms, pools and thoughtful infrastructure.

The warmest region is Alanya, which is surrounded by the Toros Mountains and citrus groves. In this picturesque place, the holiday season lasts until late autumn. Alanya is perfect for families with children.

Belek is famous for its chic five-star hotels, eucalyptus groves and pine forests, ancient monuments and many entertainments.

The pearl of the southwestern coast of the country is Marmaris. Located on the peninsula and washed by the Mediterranean and Aegean Seas, this resort attracts beautiful golden beaches, magnificent natural landscapes and a vivid contrast between the ancient and modern architectural structures of the city.





In the national cuisine, it is worth noting the unique Turkish sweets. The most common desserts are baklava, sorbet, Turkish delight and many others. And among the dishes the main place is meat, the unique recipes of which strike even the most spoiled gourmets.

All resorts in Turkey have comfortable hotel complexes, cozy hotels, restaurants and a huge range of entertainment such as diving, jeep safari, rafting. Turkey is an ideal place to spend a vacation, it is suitable for lovers of a secluded and relaxing holiday, and for active tourists.
