Hotel Description





Hotel "Crystal" (Adler) is a 3-star comfortable hotel located in a six-story fully renovated building. Among the advantages of the hotel it is worth noting the following: excellent location - close to the sea and in the very center of Adler resort, bright and spacious rooms with all necessary amenities, a wide range of delicious dishes in a cozy cafe, adult and children's pools, secure free parking, free wireless internet , a barbecue area and a wide range of services.

In addition, the hotel has a wonderful territory surrounded by greenery: vacationers will be pleased to walk along it, looking at fountains and exotic plants. In the evenings, the lights turn on and the hotel looks especially beautiful.

Who should relax here? Those who are not used to giving up comfort even on vacation, but do not want to spend a fabulous amount on hotel accommodation. Families with children and couples who love outdoor activities or combining swimming in a clean and warm sea with visiting sights and entertainment places.

Location

Hotel Crystal (Adler), the photo of which you see below, is located just a hundred meters from the beach and in the city center. Adler International Airport is only 6 kilometers from the hotel, and five kilometers from the train station.





Within walking distance are attractions such as Amphibius water park, an amusement park, Russia's largest oceanarium, dolphinarium and others. On the waterfront where the hotel is located, there are many shops, restaurants, bars, night clubs and other entertainment venues.

Beach

The hotel is located near the urban pebble equipped beach. The sea here is clean, with a smooth entrance, which will be especially convenient for tourists who come to the resort with children. There are sunbeds, umbrellas, changing cabins, a rescue station and a medical center.

For those who love outdoor activities, there are many water activities on the beach: jet skis, catamarans, boat trips on a yacht or boat, banana rides, parachuting and much more.

Number of rooms

Kristall Mini-Hotel (Adler) offers fifty rooms to accommodate its guests. They can accommodate from one to four people. You can choose from two categories: one-room and two-room "Standard" and two-room suite. Rooms are cleaned every day.





In each room you will find all the necessary amenities: bed, bedside tables, wardrobe, telephone, desk, TV, minibar and air conditioning. All rooms have a balcony with stunning sea views. The interior is decorated in a restrained and refined style. In two-room rooms there is a bedroom and a living room in which there is comfortable upholstered furniture. The suites have a DVD player and tea set.

In each room there is a combined bathroom with shower and sink, as well as cosmetic and hygiene items and disposable slippers.





Nutrition

The hotel has a cafe with a beautiful interior, which offers a wide range of delicious dishes. The price does not include meals, but it can be ordered on site for an additional fee.

In addition, the hotel has a bar where you can enjoy light snacks or refreshments, or maybe just chat over a cup of coffee with friends outdoors.





If you want to try dishes of national or other cuisines, you need to go only a few dozen steps to get to the promenade. There you will see a large number of shops, cafes, bars and restaurants where you can dine for every taste and budget.
