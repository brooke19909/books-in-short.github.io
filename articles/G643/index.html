Beautiful name - Red Pond

The beautiful name Red Pond has at least five water reservoirs and several rural settlements located in the vast expanses of our homeland. So, in 28 kilometers from the city of Moscow there is a small but comfortable village Krasnye Prudy. Cozy cottages are located far away from a dusty and noisy highway, and you can get to them by a quality asphalt road.



The main task of the designers of this village was to develop such a planning solution that would make it possible to use the magnificent landscape created by nature itself to the maximum and fit Krasnye Prudy into it so that everything harmoniously harmonizes and naturally blends. And, we can say that they did a pretty good job of their task.

The village covers an area of ​​20 hectares, which houses houses for every taste and budget. Along the perimeter, Krasnye Prudy is fenced, and at the entrance they have a checkpoint with a security service, which ensures reliable security for all residents. Also on the territory there is an administrative complex and parking for arriving guests.

A real pearl can be called a stunning cascade of reservoirs, which is located in the village of Red Ponds. Fishing in this place promises to be excellent, because there are a lot of very different fish.



On the picturesque shore of the purest ponds, you can not only comfortably sit with a fishing rod and go fishing from the heart, but also enjoy the beauty of the local nature. In addition, there are many recreation centers nearby, among which you can even find fishing ones.

Fans of homemade pickles will be able to walk around the local forest in which they will find not only a variety of berries, but also mushrooms. In addition, here you can have a picnic in the open air, breathe in the entrancing air and even go on a small hike with the children.



The village has the same name with the famous reservoir of the city of Moscow, which was once called in the ancient times - the Red Pond. In the surviving chronicles of the distant 1423, he is known as the Great. It was located between the current Yaroslavl station and Verkhnyaya Krasnoselskaya street. Then the Red Pond occupied a large area equal to 23 hectares. Over time, they began to use it for draining various waste and sewage, and as a result, the area of ​​the reservoir decreased to 9 hectares. In 1910, the Red Pond was buried - there was no trace of it.

The eponymous settlement appeared relatively recently, but has already gained great popularity among lovers of outdoor activities and a healthy lifestyle. These places will certainly appeal to those who are tired of the daily bustle of the big city, the noise and dust of highways and the gassed air of modern megalopolises. A special expanse here for children. A large open space for games, clean lawns and the absence of nearby busy tracks - all this will definitely be appreciated by people with a child.



Probably, the village of Krasnye Prudy can be safely called the place in which dreams come true of a comfortable suburban home amidst picturesque natural landscapes, fresh, clean air, absolute peace and quiet!
