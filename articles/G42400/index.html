The Whisper Hotel 4 * (Thailand, Pattaya): description, service, reviews

Thailand's annual vacation is growing in popularity. One of the most popular resorts is Pattaya. The reason for this is a non-hot climate, clean air, sea breeze and a wide range of various entertainments. The sandy shore and clear waters of the Gulf of Thailand attract travelers to these parts, and the four-star hotel complex The Whisper Hotel offers tourists comfortable accommodation and an unforgettable pastime.





general characteristics

The Whisper Hotel 4 * hotel, the description of which will be presented to your attention in the article, is completely new, it first received guests in 2017. It is represented by an eight-story building, on the roof of which is a swimming pool. The main building is equipped with elevators. The nearest international airport is 31 km away. Such a small distance leads to the absence of long journeys after tiring flights. Settlement is carried out no earlier than 14:00, but leave the rooms must be no later than noon.

The staff speaks Thai and English. Pets are not allowed in the rooms. There are rooms for the disabled. Within walking distance are shopping centers and recreation areas. The rules prescribe the collection of bail from guests, however, as previously experienced, this does not happen. It is possible to install an extra bed for a fee. No cots. One child under the age of three years without extra bed is free of charge.





Accommodation and interior

Guests are accommodated in comfortable apartments, the total number of which is 91 units. All of them belong to the category of Standard. They have a furnished balcony. Offering views from the windows - the expanses of a busy city.

The rooms at The Whisper Hotel 4 * (Thailand) are designed with all modern trends in mind: original lighting, ceramic flooring that creates the illusion of self-leveling floors, spotlights and spaciousness - all together provide a comfortable stay. The color scheme is represented by pastel calm tones in combination with bright elements of bedding or furniture.

The Whisper Hotel 4 * (Pattaya) receives positive reviews for the coziness and comfort of the rooms. Vacationers note the daily order and cleanliness of the rooms, which maids induce during the absence of guests in the rooms. There is a food delivery service, however, it requires additional fees.





Conditions and equipment

According to numerous positive reviews, there is everything for a carefree stay and overnight. The room is equipped with individual air conditioning. There is a telephone, multi-channel TV, electric kettle and refrigerator. The minibar is replenished daily with drinking water. The latest fire safety systems are present. Internet is available throughout and is included in the price.

The furniture set is made of quality wood and includes beds, bedside tables, a wardrobe with a mirror door, a tabletop and a chair.

The bathroom contains showers with massage devices, a sink, a mirror, a hairdryer, a set of personal hygiene products and snow-white towels. Here you can also find disposable slippers and bathrobes.

Entertainment and leisure

A fitness center equipped with cardiovascular equipment, treadmills, steppers and other equipment will help to realize the goals of those who wish to increase their immunity and maintain beautiful body shape. Qualified instructors will help draw up a program of gentle loads on the body and conduct aerobics classes.

The purpose of the holidays is often to improve the body. It is possible to realize it with the help of useful activities, which include swimming. Normalization of blood circulation, restoration of metabolic processes, strengthening of the musculoskeletal system, elimination of extra pounds and rejuvenation - this is what a person gains when he is and is actively moving in a pool or sea.

The Whisper Hotel has an outdoor pool surrounded by sun loungers with soft mattresses and umbrellas for those who do not want to touch the gentle sunshine of Pattaya. The presence of a bar made it possible to fully relax while sunbathing. There is a children's aquazone.





Beach

Every traveler will be awarded a dark tan, because visiting the beach is easy to implement, thanks to its good location. A 10-minute walk separates guests from the Cote d'Azur, hot sands and unforgettable emotions. The water surface of these areas is shallow. This favorably affects the bathing of babies and the peace of parents: their child is played aground, without a threat to life.

To ensure that the time spent here does not seem dull and boring, travelers are invited to take advantage and visit the water attractions. These include: jet skis, scooters, banana and parachute rides, boat trips to the open sea, and scuba diving.





Additional services

For the convenience of guests, the reception is open around the clock. Smoking is possible in specially equipped places. A currency exchange office is located near the hotel complex. Parking spaces are provided for anyone arriving by car. The hotel has a grocery and souvenir shops where tourists can purchase essential goods and gifts for friends.

The front desk staff can help solve dry cleaning and laundry issues. Luggage storage is free. The computer center will allow you to diversify your leisure time by playing popular games or talking with family.

Transfer is an important service sold for a fee. Experienced drivers will safely deliver guests to or from the airport.

For conferences, meetings or business meetings, a business center, called a conference room, is perfect. Strict design and warm arrangement sets up for cooperation and solving important issues.

The Whisper Hotel 4 * (Pattaya): reviews

The Internet, despite the still short-lived existence of the hotel, is full of positive reviews about it. The guests love the nutritious meals that take place in the hotel restaurant. However, only breakfast is free. The lack of crowds of Chinese positively affects the mood of tourists. The possibility of wireless Internet access throughout the area has a beneficial effect on the overall impression of the hotel.

Many guests in their reviews note that The Whisper Hotel 4 * service offers a fairly high level. Tourists recommend the hotel to their friends and acquaintances.
