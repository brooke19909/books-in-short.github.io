Hotel L'ancora Beach 4 * (Turkey / Kemer): photos and reviews of tourists

The Russians have long liked Kemer. This corner of the Turkish coast is very beautiful. Surrounded by mountains, Kemer is immersed in the greenery of dense plantations - parks and forests. The azure bay, clean beaches, a special microclimate, a long swimming season - this is not the whole list of those reasons why our compatriots choose this particular direction.



Rest in Kemer

Tourists come to this resort all year round. And although the high season, starting in April, ends at the end of October, nonetheless the hotels here are always crowded. Vacationers come here to breathe fresh air, spend time at the pools that are in every hotel complex, see the sights and just enjoy the beautiful nature.

There are many hotels in this resort - for every taste and prosperity. Those who prefer a budget vacation can choose accommodation on the second line. Kemer is considered a universal resort: here with equal success both young people, accustomed to active pastime, and elderly or couples with children will have a good rest. Most hotels in the city have a very developed infrastructure, allowing you to spend a carefree and comfortable vacation. L'ancora Beach Hotel 4 * is no exception .

Today L'ancora Beach Hotel 4 * is a completely refurbished four-star hotel, offering tourists a wide variety of services and entertainment.



He has a very good location. The hotel is located in the center of Kemer on the first line. Nearby is the promenade, which has many shops, bars, restaurants and clubs. Antalya is forty-five miles away, and the nearest international airport is fifty-five kilometers away.

Infrastructure

L'ancora Beach 4 * (Kemer), being a four-star hotel, offers a fairly wide range of services. Guests can spend their full rest here using them.

There is free parking on site, reservation of places on which is not required in advance. On the first floor of the main building, tourists can exchange currency, use an ATM, hand over things to the laundry or dry cleaning. If necessary, you can take the advice of a doctor (visiting the office is paid).

The registration desk serves customers around the clock. Fax and photocopying machines are also installed here. Those who prefer to explore the local historical monuments on their own, it will be convenient to rent a car.



There, in the main building, there is a hairdresser. There are modern elevators. You can also arrange a transfer at the registrar, as well as reserve a deposit box safe. Those who arrive at the hotel outside the check-in hours, if available, will receive it immediately, otherwise they can wait in the cozy lobby with a large TV.

For conferences in L'ancora Beach 4 * there is a large business hall that can seat up to sixty people at a time. There is also a reading room and a TV room.

Housing stock

In L'ancora Beach Hotel (4 *, Kemer) one hundred thirty-four rooms of the following categories: economy, standard and family (2 + 2). They are equipped in a four-story building with an elevator. In total, the hotel can simultaneously accommodate two hundred and seventy people. After major renovation, the rooms are equipped with new furniture and high-quality plumbing. The rooms as well as the public areas are air-conditioned. The room has a split system with manual control, there is a TV, paid safe and minibar, telephone.

The combined bathrooms have a shower, hairdryer and slippers. After each cleaning, the hygiene items are updated. Bed and towel change, according to the rules, carried out three times a week. On the floor and in the rooms and in the bathroom there is a non-slip ceramic coating. Some rooms have small balconies with plastic furniture.



Nutrition

L'ancora Beach Hotel (4 *, Turkey, Kemer) operates on an all-inclusive basis. Tourists are invited to breakfast, lunch and dinner according to the schedule set by the administration, which they will receive in printed form on the day of their arrival. The price also includes the receipt of additional snacks, alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks of local production from ten in the morning to eleven in the evening.

The hotel has one main restaurant. There are also three bars, including on the beach and poolside. For an additional fee, you can get some types of imported drinks, freshly squeezed juices. At the beach bar, snacks, cocktails and juices are not included in the price, they must be paid on site.
Near the hotel, within walking distance, there are several inexpensive restaurants and cafes.

Sea and beach

L'ancora Beach Hotel (4 *, Kemer) has its own swimming area. Since it is part of the city beach, there is a fairly developed infrastructure: toilets, showers and changing cabins, as well as a wide range of water activities. Residents of the hotel and holding the hotel bracelet can use sun loungers, trestle beds and umbrellas for free. Towels can be obtained by leaving a deposit at the registration desk.



The coating on the beach is pebble, so there is a need for special rubber slippers, especially for children. Those who have not brought bathing shoes with them can purchase them in stores located at the hotel. The entrance to the water is comfortable gentle.

Since Kemer is located in the bay, there are no big waves and winds here. The swimming season begins in April, the peak occurs in the July and August months.

Services for children

L'ancora Beach Hotel 4 *, positioned by tour operators as a family, annually receives a large number of small guests. For children, chairs are provided in the restaurant for convenience of feeding, on request you can get a special menu by making a request at the registration desk, as well as a crib in the room.

In addition, a shallow paddling pool for adults is provided for babies, as well as a playground. Funny animators entertain the kids, while older children can attend the club. If the parents have urgent matters, they can invite a Russian-speaking nanny (the service is paid).



Health and beauty

Today, leading a healthy lifestyle has become a prerequisite for many. Therefore, the hotel provides a massage room for guests, beauty treatments. The free services include a fitness and spa center, sauna, hammam, as well as a jacuzzi and a sun terrace.

Sports and Entertainment

Since not only couples, but also young people have a rest in Kemer, the resort has a rather extensive infrastructure, provided specifically for this category of tourists - night clubs, discos. L'ancora Beach (4 *) is no exception. Reviews of many Russians begin with how they had fun at their hotel. And this is not surprising: every night there are noisy discos, there is great animation. Nearby is a large water park. Using a paid transfer to Antalya, young people can spend the evening in the famous entertainment venues of this big city.

Many tourists also go in for sports on vacation. For this, the hotel provides gym equipment in a large and bright gym, and also offers aerobics classes, which are held in the pool in the morning.

Additional Information

On the territory of L'ancora Beach 4 * there is a large and clean pool with free mattresses, sunbeds and umbrellas. On the city beach, vacationers can ride a catamaran and scooter, take advantage of many other paid water activities.

Reviews

Russians have long come to this hotel. Most of them, upon arrival, share their impressions. Judging by the estimates of many tourists who stayed at this hotel, they liked it. The staff mostly understands Russian, and some even speak. Cleaning is done in good faith. Every day on the beds, funny animals molded from sheets flaunt, which raises the mood of tourists.

Russians rated the food at the top five. The menu is always different dishes: a lot of meat, fish, fresh fruits and vegetables, salads are always offered. Many good words have been said about the work of the restaurant chef, as well as about the woman who baked delicious flat cakes with cheese and potato filling every afternoon.

Tourists liked the location of the hotel - next to the promenade. All important facilities are within walking distance, including a pharmacy, and inexpensive stores where you can buy not only gifts and souvenirs, but also clothes for a low price.

Many tourists remember the musicians who come to the hotel three times a week, and especially the incendiary dance of the waiters who performed the national Turkish dance

As for the rooms, some of our compatriots were somewhat annoyed by their small area - eighteen square meters. But there are no complaints about the decor and design. Some did not like the fact that in the evenings in the rooms there was the sound of a disco, which prevented children from sleeping. And the rest I liked the hotel, many are even going to come back here again.
