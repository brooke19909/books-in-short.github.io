Review 6303i Nokia Classic. Specifications

Nokia has long since given up its leading position to more advanced and change-ready firms. However, old models are still relevant and cause genuine interest. One of such devices is 6303i. What will interest the user in this device?

Design





A glance at the 6303i Nokia Classic is enough to indulge in memories. The design of the phone returns the user to those days when the appearance of smartphones had personality and differences. A small candy bar weighing only 96 grams is made in the usual Nokia style.

The device is made of ordinary plastic, but with metal inserts. Accordingly, despite its miniature size, the device weighs decently. The use of metal allowed us to make the device even more reliable. It is almost impossible to detect gaps and creaks in the phone.

At the bottom of the device, the manufacturer placed a microphone, a charging socket, a USB connector, as well as a headset input. Not without interesting solutions. The USB jack is covered by a plate. This solution allows you to reduce the accumulation of dust and dirt in such a problem for the phone. The manufacturer placed the volume control on the right side of the device, and the power button - at the top of the mobile phone.

On the front of the device, as in all monoblocks, controls and dial buttons are placed. The screen, unfortunately, is rather small, since most of the surface is occupied by keys. Although for a regular mobile phone, a miniature display is not a problem. Above the screen of the device is a speaker.

Behind the Nokia 6303i Classic was a camera, flash and speaker. All elements are located in the upper part of the case, which is quite reasonable. When working with the phone, the user will not close the speaker.

In general, the appearance of the phone released in 2010 turned out to be in the spirit of Nokia. Elegance and simplicity - this is how you can describe the impression of the cell.

Hardware

Model 6303i is a regular cell phone, which means that the "filling" is fully consistent with the simplicity of the device.
