Mortgage in St. Petersburg: banks, terms and conditions of registration

Today, not every person living in the cultural capital can afford to buy their own housing. Of course, making such a large sum of money is almost impossible for an ordinary office worker or builder. That is why the mortgage in St. Petersburg is in great demand among citizens.

By and large, this program is a loan that is given to people to buy real estate. Although the debt repayment period may be more than 20 years, the interest rate on the debt is usually almost half the cost of the apartment. In addition, many organizations providing mortgage services in St. Petersburg require a down payment. Such amounts are also very difficult to collect in a short time. That is why, before taking a loan, it is recommended not only to study the real estate market, but also the services of banks.

The term for which a loan is granted is usually 25 years.

Military lending

In 2005, the government launched the Military Mortgage Program in St. Petersburg and other cities. According to the innovation, the state provides people serving in the army with living space not in kind, but in money. This means that a soldier can receive a subsidy in the amount of 1 million rubles and spend it on down payments on a mortgage or purchase low-cost housing for this amount.





In addition, the Military Mortgage in St. Petersburg involves the allocation of funds in the amount of 2.5 million rubles to repay loans. These cases are considered in private. The average amount of subsidies issued by the state is about 3-4 million rubles. If a soldier wants to purchase housing at a higher cost, then he pays the rest on his own.

However, it is worth considering the fact that this subsidy contains a number of amendments and exceptions. Accordingly, not every person serving in the army can receive substantial financial assistance.
