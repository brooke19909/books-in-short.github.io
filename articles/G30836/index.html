Hotel Possidi Holidays 5 * (Halkidiki, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

Halkidiki is one of the main resort areas of mainland Greece. Tourists from all over the world come here all year round. And in the summer there are especially many travelers. And everyone wants to settle in some good hotel by the sea. Well, exactly such a place is Possidi Holidays 5 *.





Briefly about the hotel

The hotel was built in 1992. Many are embarrassed by her ‚Äúage‚Äù, however, the complete reconstruction took place in 2011, so there is no need to worry about the condition of the hotel - everything inside is new and modern.

Possidi Holidays 5 * is located right on the seashore, at arm's length from your own beach, in the resort village of Possidi. It is located on the Kassandra Peninsula. This village is a small settlement surrounded by picturesque pine groves. And just nearby was the ancient city of Mendi. The first settlers appeared there in the 12th century BC - excavations have shown this. In general, the sights from the hotel are also nearby.

The hotel itself is surrounded by pine and olive trees. The territory in which it is located is estimated at 33,000 square meters. m! In general, the location is extremely good. Silence and solitude - the perfect atmosphere for a relaxing holiday. The only small minus is the Macedonia Airport is far away, 105 kilometers from the village.





Entertainment

Being in Possidi Holidays 5 *, you can brighten up your leisure time in various ways. It has an outdoor swimming pool and a sun terrace with sun loungers. Guests are also offered a variety of professional equipment for water sports. For example, water skiing, canoeing and catamarans.

The hotel also has a small SPA-center - with a hammam, jacuzzi, massage room, sauna and beauty salon.

Lovers of an active lifestyle will definitely like the availability of bicycle rental and a good gym on site. There are two more tennis courts (with lighting and special coverage). You can also play volleyball on the beach.

By the way, you can reach the sea in one minute. The beach is sandy, clean, which is not surprising - because it is private. There is a bar, deck chairs, umbrellas and towels.

Even the children whom the parents took with them on vacation will not be bored. For them, Possidi Holidays Resort 5 * has a games room and a separate playground.

Service

This hotel has everything for guests. Free Wi-Fi and parking, 24-hour reception (individual registration of guests is possible), luggage storage, currency exchange, laundry, ironing, conference room, office with photocopy and fax - this is just a small list of services provided.

Also in Possidi Holidays Resort 5 * there is a common lounge, transfer and food delivery services with drinks to the apartments, car rental and even shops. And guests, if they wish, can ask to provide them with a family room or suite for the newlyweds. By the way, there are no problems with communication. After all, the staff speaks not only English, Greek and German, but also Russian.





Nutrition

In Possidi Holidays 5 * (Halkidiki), as in any other 5-star hotel, there are several good places where guests can satisfy their hunger or thirst. There is a vitamin bar located near the pool. Fresh juices are served there. In the main restaurant, in addition to breakfast, lunch and dinner, themed evenings of Mediterranean and Italian cuisine are regularly organized. They also serve barbecue. Many guests pay special attention to the tavern with the beautiful name Magnolia, where they cook excellent Greek dishes. And the main bar boasts a large assortment of alcohol, cocktails and live music every night. A special highlight of the atmosphere adds a fireplace.

In general, no one is left hungry. The guests who were here say that even if you arrive closer to the night, when dinner is already over, the staff will set the table in the room. A wide selection of dishes. There are barbecue, fish, meat, fruits, desserts, vegetables, side dishes, sweets. There are no queues in bars and restaurants, and the buffet is replenished until the meal is over. This level of service can not but rejoice.

Family apartments

Many people with children decide to go on vacation with them. Well, for this category of tourists, the Possidi Holidays Resort Suites 5 * have separate rooms called family rooms. Their area is 33 square meters. m. This space is divided into a bedroom, a balcony and a bathroom. Inside, there are three wide single beds and one folding sofa. Maximum occupancy for this room is four people. 2 adults and 2 children. But children are considered guests whose age has not reached 12 years. Anyone older is considered an adult.

Amenities - all the same as in the previously listed rooms. There is no capacity for extra beds in this apartment. The view, by the way, can open either to the garden or to the sea. If the moment is fundamental, then it should be clarified even at the time of booking.

85 000 rubles - this is how much a weekly stay in a family apartment with half board will cost. But many, coming here, take a full board. In this case, you will have to pay 20,000 rubles more.

Reservation

The Possidi Holidays Resort 5 * hotel, the photo of which is provided above, is very popular. Which, in principle, is not surprising. So, that is why it is better to reserve an apartment here long before the intended trip. Early booking is the best way out.

Check-in starts at 14:00, but for guests, individual registration is available. Only this needs to be agreed. If people arrived earlier, but the room is ready for them, then there will be no problems with checking in.

The hotel, as mentioned, is allowed to stay with children. For babies whose age does not exceed two years, free cribs are provided. True, it is advisable to inform that it is necessary in advance. So that the administration has time to prepare everything.

By the way, when making an early booking, you will need to provide your credit card details (as a guarantee of arrival). The hotel accepts Visa and Master Card.





Vacationers

People who have been to Possidi Holidays 5 * (Greece, Halkidiki, Possidi) are happy to share their impressions regarding their vacation here. The first comments are always about the location and the hotel itself. Everyone says that the hotel looks like a large and beautiful villa, surrounded by lush vegetation. The sea here is good, clean, and the sunset is convenient. There are also deck chairs with umbrellas.

The staff is attentive and polite, but sometimes tired, as workers work seven days a week (this applies to the high season). In the store, located in the hotel, fairly reasonable prices. Many are there who buy home gifts - oil, wine, cheeses, etc.

Guests are advised to rent bicycles and go to Olympus, a beautiful and interesting place with a picturesque waterfall on top of the mountain.

Another plus is that there is rarely a show. Some consider this a minus, as there are people who like animation. But the plus is that the territory is always quiet. So basically people who lack this peace come here.
