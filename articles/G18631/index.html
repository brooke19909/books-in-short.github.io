Beginning of work

In order to fully reveal the potential of the drone and get maximum pleasure, you need to connect any mobile gadget with a screen to your control panel - a smartphone or tablet. Of course, you can do without them, but why then buy a professional version, when you can fly blindly and on the standard?





The drone synergizes with mobile devices both on the Android platform and on iOS. Before proceeding directly to the flights, they will need to install proprietary software (can be found on the official DJI website).

After installing special software, the setup wizard carefully guides you through all the necessary points before starting the flight. After that, the picture from the camera of the quadcopter DJI Phantom 3 Professional should appear on the screen of the mobile gadget. Even a novice in this business will get used to the management: the hints are universal, and the functionality itself is utterly simple. Nevertheless, it is better to take the first steps on earth, and not immediately embark on a heavenly adventure.

Flying

The characteristics of the DJI Phantom 3 Professional quadrocopter allow it to move away from the pilot by no more than two kilometers, with a vertical ceiling of 500 meters. The last parameter defaults to a hundred meters, but in the settings you can remove the restriction.

It should be noted right away that information about places where it is forbidden to fly is sewn into the ‚Äúbrains‚Äù of the device. The latter include some state, military and other institutions, as well as airports. The data is constantly updated for a specific flight area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe drone, so do not be surprised that your quadrocopter suddenly refuses to visit some "unfriendly" place for it.





Climbing speed is about 5 meters per second, landing - only 3 m / s, and the maximum flight speed - 16 m / s. The existing characteristics of DJI Phantom 3 Professional allow you to work wonders in the sky, while competing counterparts have much more modest capabilities (in this price category).

Of the minuses, one can note a short flight range in cities with a population of over one million, because the frequency ranges are jammed and, for example, in the center of Moscow the drone will not fly further than 500 meters, while in Samara it calmly overcomes the threshold of almost two kilometers. Accordingly, to shoot videos and photos on DJI Phantom 3 Professional in megacities at a great distance also fails.

Standalone time

The drone and the control panel are equipped with intelligent rechargeable batteries, that is, the battery has a dynamic indicator of the charge level and perfectly keeps its rating. And if the last indicator is absolutely unimportant for smartphones, then for such a technique as a quadrocopter - this is the most important moment, because a voltage loss is fraught with a fall of the device.





DJI Phantom 3 Professional received on board a battery of 4480 mAh (15.2 V). Some revisions are equipped with a more capacious 6000 mAh battery, so here is how lucky. There is a spare battery in the package, so there should not be any serious problems with autonomy.

In good and calm weather, the drone will calmly hold out in the sky for about half an hour. If you fly it in the autumn, that is, when the temperature is close to zero and with gusts of wind, then for more than 15 minutes one battery will not be enough.

The battery is fully recharged in about an hour. It is easy to calculate that for continuous flights you need three batteries, so fans of non-stop entertainment will have to buy another battery.

In general, if we compare the battery life of the drone with competing counterparts, then our respondent is doing well. The model does not differ in long flights, but the pleasure of the latter (in half an hour of time) can be obtained in abundance.
