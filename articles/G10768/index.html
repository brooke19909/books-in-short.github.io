What documents are needed?

To get to the hotel ‚ÄúJoekvara‚Äù (4 * hotel, Gagra, Abkhazia), reviews of which can be found both positive and negative, you must first cross the border. So, what documents do Russian citizens need to take with them on a trip?

An adult should have a tourist voucher and a civil or foreign passport with him. In addition, children under 14 years old are required to take a birth certificate, which should have a citizenship note. At the same time, a child over 14 years old must have the following documents with him: a passport (civil or foreign), a birth certificate with the same citizenship mark and a medical insurance policy, necessary in case of accidents.

A citizen of Russia who is not yet 18 years old, traveling without parents, must provide the border services with a notarized power of attorney to leave Russia for Abkhazia, signed by both parents. If you are also not 18 years old, and you plan to go to the Zheoquara Hotel, where you have already purchased tours with one of your parents, then note that in this case there is no need to make a power of attorney for your departure to another country.

In addition, it is worth noting that you will not be able to cross the border with temporary ID cards, and residents of neighboring countries and the former CIS can cross the border with Abkhazia only if they have a foreign passport and migration card.
