How to knit a fashionable sweater with knitting needles?

Knitting is a very interesting and creative process. However, it requires certain knowledge and skills. Without them, the novice master will be very difficult to cope with the work. Therefore, we further propose to study the technology for making fashionable sweaters with knitting needles.

Training

Experienced craftswomen advise first of all to determine the model of the conceived product. Sweaters are mostly worn in the cold season. But this does not oblige the needlewoman to supplement her work with a stand-up collar. There are sweaters with a rounded neckline, decorated with a simple elastic band. However, the most popular are knitted fashionable sweaters, in which the goal line is practically not expressed. They are carried out the easiest, but they look very impressive and original.

Having thought over the style, we proceed to the choice of the pattern. There are no and cannot be strict recommendations on this issue. Although traditionally sweaters are decorated with braids, plaits or elastic. Moreover, if you knit with unusual yarn, you can do all the work in hosiery or garter stitches.

Patterns

When choosing a pattern for your ideas, you should not try to find the option that will cause difficulties in execution. And if the reader is just beginning to learn the basics of knitting, it is better to completely tie the whole work in hosiery or garter stitch.

The first option involves knitting facial loops on the front side, and the wrong ones on the wrong side. Moreover, it is important to note that, starting to knit a sweater with this pattern from the beginning, it will be possible to decorate the lower edge with an interesting twist. The second option is considered the simplest. It involves knitting facial loops on both sides of the fabric. Yarn

In order not to make a mistake when buying knitting threads, you should be guided by the pattern that will be used to tie the desired product. Experienced craftswomen advise using plain yarn for knitting complex patterns. Therefore, novice needlewomen often choose it to make a fashionable sweater, in which braids and plaits are provided.

If the studied wardrobe item will be made into one of simple patterns (hosiery or shawl knitting, embossed patterns), it is better to purchase interesting knitting threads. These include gradient yarn, patchwork, "fluffy", one that spontaneously folds into various patterns and many other types. Experienced beginners are advised to choose melange yarn. It will allow you not to get confused in the process of knitting and create a product with an unusual color scheme.

Knitting needles

It will be possible to knit a really effective model of a fashionable sweater with knitting needles, if you prepare good knitting needles. A similar title can be awarded to those made of metal. But not too soft. Otherwise, they will bend at work and cause a lot of inconvenience. Readers who knit too loose loops can use wooden tools. But in any case, it is better to use ring knitting needles for knitting sweaters. But for hoses, hosiery is more suitable. We select the size of the tools taking into account the yarn. To make sweaters, it is better to use those that are equal to the thickness of the thread. Having approved the most suitable knitting needles, the craftswoman needs to carefully study their appearance. Tools should be well polished, have a smooth tip. If this is not the case, it is wiser to consider others.

Merki

To make a fashionable knitting sweater, it is extremely important to measure the person to whom it is intended. Only in this case you can make a convenient thing. Therefore, we take a centimeter tape and determine the following parameters:

  • chest girth;
  • product length;
  • armhole height;
  • forearm girth;
  • sleeves length;
  • neck circumference;
  • distance from the base of the neck to the tip of the shoulder.

Then we prepare a sample of the selected pattern - a square of 10 x 10 centimeters. We count the number of loops and rows, divide by ten. The first value is multiplied by the girth of the chest, the girth of the forearm, the girth of the neck and the distance from the base of the neck to the tip of the shoulder. The second - for the length of the product, the height of the armhole and the length of the sleeves. All new parameters are adjusted taking into account the pattern repeat. Then we fix it, because on them we will knit a fashionable sweater with knitting needles. For youth, he will, or for a more advanced age, decide the needlewoman. The rules for taking measurements are valid in any case.

Technology

Knitting a sweater is very simple:

  1. We collect loops for the girth of the chest.
  2. We knit, moving in a circle, to the level of the armhole.
  3. Separate the front and back and knit each part separately.
  4. Sew on the shoulder seams.
  5. In the armhole, we collect loops for knitting sleeves and knit to the desired length.
