Lampedusa Island: tours, reviews, photos, hotels. How to get there

Lampedusa Island is a paradise. Everything has been created here for an unforgettable vacation - a clear azure sea, white sand, bright sunshine and beautiful hotels.

History of Lampedusa

According to scientists, the island of Lampedusa existed in the Bronze Age. This is proved by archaeological finds. It is established that for a long time Berber pirates lived here. Later, the island became the property of the kingdom of Sicily. Then the island was owned by representatives of the Habsburg clan, and even later - the Tomasi family. They resold the island to the rulers of Naples. Not many people know that Russian Emperor Pavel I was interested in this island .



After the British took possession of the island, the development of agriculture on this land began. Soils were cultivated, the local population began to grow grapes.

origin of name

During the Middle Ages, the island got its name, which meant the lights that lit the hermits, thus helping to navigate the sailors.

Island Description

The island of Lampedusa, the photo of which you see in this article, is of volcanic origin. It is located near Sicily (205 km), is part of the Pelagic Islands. Its area is only 20.2 km. It is inhabited by fishermen and divers for sponges. Agriculture practically does not develop due to lack of fresh water.



Climatic conditions

The climate on the island is moderately subtropical, very favorable for recreation. It is warm and sunny all year. The tourist season lasts from early April to late October.

The sea on the island is great. It is filled with a huge variety of fish. This has always been the main source of livelihood for the inhabitants of the island. Thanks to the beauty of the deep sea, the Lampedusa Sea is a paradise for diving enthusiasts.

Attractions Lampedusa

They are concentrated in the only town of the same name on the island - Lampedusa. It's nice to walk along its old streets, admire the neat, like toy houses. In addition, the island of Lampedusa is famous for its picturesque lagoons.

In the southwest of Lampedusa are the famous Rabbit Island and Rabbit Beach. Many tourists consider it one of the 10 most beautiful beaches in the world.



Rabbit Island is a protected area, National Park. Here they lay eggs of a turtle called caretta. The birth of their babies and the transfer of babies to the sea occurs in late August and early September.

Lampedusa Island has many caves, bays, indentations, rocks, which makes it very popular among those who love secluded places. When the wind blows from the south, you can rent a boat to visit the North side of the island of Lampedusa, where there are gorges and caves.

This is a great place for a measured week's rest at sea. If you want to visit the island of Lampedusa, you can buy tours at almost all travel agencies in our country.

Flats

Renting an apartment on the island can be a good vacation solution. This is a convenient and not very expensive option. Many apartments on the island are rented through agencies, but can also be rented from private individuals. You can find a beautiful and functional apartment with magnificent sea views at a very reasonable price.



Recreation and entertainment

Divers and lovers of yachting enjoy visiting the island of Lampedusa. Holidays for this category of tourists are just wonderful. Recall that the most picturesque lagoons are located here. You can also rent a bicycle or scooter and go on a trip around the area. You can play volleyball at the beach.



Transport

You can move around the island by car, which you will be offered to rent. You can also take a public bus. If desired, a taxi can be called from the hotel.



What to bring from the island?

Usually these are small, but pretty souvenirs - shell jewelry, various beach accessories - pareos, shoes, swimwear. Many tourists bring excellent wine, Italian cheeses, sweets.

How to get there

If you are interested in the island of Lampedusa, we will tell you how to get there. There is an airport on the island. It provides several seasonal flights. The largest load on the airport falls on the summer months.

You can get to this airport from Rome, Milan, Catania, Palermo, Bologna. Therefore, you need to arrive in one of these cities and from there go on a direct flight to the island.

Lampedusa Island: reviews

Today, many of our compatriots have visited this picturesque island. According to those who have been there, the island of Lampedusa is an ideal place for those who want to relax from the bustle of the city and enjoy nature. Here diving and fishing fans feel great. For fishing you can rent a boat.

Fans of nightlife will not like it here. The nightlife on the island is not very active. Some tourists complain about the high cost of tickets and accommodation. Nevertheless, the flow of tourists to this island is increasing every year.
