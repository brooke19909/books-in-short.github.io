Mordovia: attractions and recreation

Before you go abroad, you should think about whether you want to visit interesting places in your country? For example, the Republic of Mordovia, the sights of which are described below, can be a great place for cultural recreation and enjoying nature.

Culture of Mordovia

The expositions of the Mordovia Regional Museum of Local Lore (it also has nine regional branches), the Mordovian Republican Museum of Fine Arts (this institution has three branches), the Temnikovsky Museum of History and Local Lore, and the Museum of Military and Labor Feat and are considered modern large museum complexes in the republic. many others.

The beginning of the last century brought worldwide fame to the local sculptor Stepan Dmitrievich Erzya, who became famous thanks to his work. He owes his pseudonym to the name of one of the Mordovian nationalities. The value of his work is recognized by experts from many countries, and is also widely known in the republic itself. They try to promote his creations, because in them he emphasized the peculiarities of local culture.





Saransk

The Saransk fortress was built in the middle of the XVII century, where the rivers called Insar and Saranka merge, from which the name of the city later came. She survived the era of peasant wars, was besieged and was taken by Stepan Razin and his troops. Already in the 18th century, detachments of Emelyan Pugachev entered the city, whom the population greeted with great honor.

If you have some free time to get acquainted with the sights of Saransk, you should try to look at least at the building of the Nativity of the Virgin Monastery, which also contains the grave of Admiral Ushakov.





Temnikov

The city of Temnikov is considered to be the oldest in the Republic of Mordovia. Sights, photos of which you can see below, include the building of the famous museum of local lore, as well as many old buildings that convey the atmosphere of past centuries. In addition, in Temnikovo there is a church of the Assumption of the Blessed Virgin Mary. Also here you can see the exposition of the house-museum of the composer L.I. Voinova.





Mordovian Reserve

The territory of the Mordovian State Nature Reserve is located in Temnikovsky District (Republic of Mordovia). The sights listed in this article cannot be illuminated without it. The reserve is located in the vicinity of the village of Pashty. Here you can also see the exposition of the Museum of Nature, which includes a large number of samples of the plant and animal life of the region, as well as photographs. In the reserve, a significant area is occupied by woodland. There are also some protected species of animals, including deer, beavers, black storks, etc.
