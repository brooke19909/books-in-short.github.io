Anapa, boarding house "Black Sea": address, description and reviews of tourists

The Black Sea boarding house (Russia, Anapa) offers its guests a year-round vacation. Here you can spend not only a summer vacation, but also come on a winter vacation.

This place is ready to welcome guests at any time of the year. This situation makes it so popular among numerous tourists. The building is new, so is the building. The boarding house was founded in 2006, and the building was specially built for a tourist complex.

Location





The Black Sea pension (Anapa) is located very well. The complex is located in a small but very cozy village Vityazevo. There is a boarding house on the shore of a warm estuary. The surroundings are very picturesque, the air is fresh, and the water in the estuary is at a comfortable temperature. Everything you need for a good rest and recovery is here. Not far from the place of residence there is an entertainment center where you can watch a movie, play bowling, go to the rink or just have dinner. At 500 meters from the pension there is a beach with all necessary conditions. The beach area is cleaned daily.

Description of the complex

The best choice for a wellness holiday in the vastness of his homeland is undoubtedly Anapa. Pension "Black Sea" is a whole complex of several buildings. There are two residential buildings and the administration building. The whole territory is landscaped, perennial pines grow, which provides fresh and healthy air.

Accommodation





The Black Sea boarding house 2 * (Anapa, Vityazevo) offers its guests rooms of different categories. Therefore, you can choose apartments here on any pocket, regardless of social status. There is an opportunity to settle in both a budget room and more luxurious apartments. Regardless of the category of room, each room has a balcony, a comfortable bathroom, there is a safe and a refrigerator, in hot weather you can use a fan. Superior rooms have air conditioning instead of a fan, and some apartments have several.

Number of rooms

The area is 13 square meters. m. There is a balcony on which there is a dryer for clothes and towels. The furniture is all new and comfortable. The furniture set includes a double bed, wardrobe, bedside tables and a chair with a table. The room has air conditioning, which is installed in accordance with all safety regulations. There is also a set of necessary utensils, a safe for storing valuables and a refrigerator with a TV. In the bathroom there is the necessary set of towels and cosmetics. Cleaning takes place daily.

The bathroom has all the necessary toiletries and towels with bathrobes. And, of course, there is air conditioning and other necessary equipment.

  • Standard room - 2-seater.

Its area is 12 square meters. m. There is a balcony. All the necessary set of comfortable furniture. Double blood or two single can stand. This issue is resolved with guests at their request.   • Standard room - 3-seater.

This room is 15 square meters. m. There is a balcony. A necessary set of furniture, namely 3 single beds, bedside tables, a table with a chair and a wardrobe with hangers. There is a TV with a fridge, a set of dishes, a safe. The room has a bathroom with all toiletries.

For children under 3 years old, only the community fee is paid. Accommodation is free. But for food you have to pay extra or you can refuse it. Extra beds for children are not delivered to the room, so the child will have to sleep with parents or in an adult bed.

Nutrition





Fairytale relaxation, warm sea, healing air and gentle sun - all this is amazing Anapa. Pension "Black Sea" offers food, which is included in the price. Guests can have breakfast, lunch and dinner in the dining room, or order room service. There are elements of a buffet, the administration of the boarding house is constantly working on the menu and trying to improve it.

Territory improvement





A huge amount of manpower and resources was invested in landscaping. However, as in all other similar complexes, which Anapa is famous for. Pension "Black Sea" provides the most comfortable conditions for a good rest and recovery. On the territory there is a beautiful modern pool, its area is large. Therefore, if a guest does not want to swim in the estuary, then he can spend time near the pool. The water in it is fingerprinted, there are sun loungers and umbrellas. All conditions for aerotherapy are created here. There is a special platform for such a procedure, sea air and needles have a beneficial effect on human health.

How to spend leisure time?

In addition to passive rest and relaxation, you can find more active entertainment. There is a beautiful tennis court, and there are tables for table tennis. You can try your hand at volleyball or basketball, visit the gym or play badminton. All these activities are free and are included in the price. All the necessary equipment for sports activities can be rented at the reception. For a more passive and relaxing holiday, you have the opportunity to visit the sauna, bathhouse. There is also a cosmetology office and a massage studio on the territory, where professional staff will provide all the necessary services in the field of cosmetology. There is a beauty salon on site, where wonderful stylists work to help you choose a beautiful hairstyle or makeup.

Infrastructure



Not only for recreation, vacationers go to Vityazevo. Anapa can also offer a wonderful holiday for children. Pension "Black Sea" takes care of the little guests. Parents can try good coffee or have a tasty snack in a small cafe located on the territory of the complex. There is also a bar where you can try all kinds of cocktails.

A small town has been created for children in the form of a playground with all kinds of mazes, houses and swings. This is an active and interesting vacation, moreover, here children can find new friends. In order for parents not to worry, the playground is in the natural shade of trees, so it won‚Äôt be hot.

For guests who come by private car, parking is available. Using it belongs to the list of free services. Conveniently, the parking is guarded around the clock, so worry about your car is not worth it.

Excursions

Even cultural entertainment is available to those who have chosen the city of Anapa for their holidays (Vityazevo, Pension Chernomorsky). Responses of tourists indicate that the administration of the complex thought out not only leisure activities within the boarding house, but also made sure that guests could comfortably spend time outside it. The administration organizes excursions for its guests. This is a very convenient service, because you can book the necessary tour directly at the reception. Payment is made upon. Here you can relax not only physically, but also culturally. Campaigns to the theater or cinema are organized. In a word, the cultural program is thought out very well.

Important information

When checking into a boarding house, you must have a package with all documents. For adults, a passport is required. If there are children with adults, then having a birth certificate is simply necessary, otherwise some problems may arise later. Getting to the guesthouse is not difficult. This can be done even by bus, in this direction several minibuses go from the station.

What do tourists say about room stock?

The boarding house provides for its guests rooms of completely different categories. Everyone can choose an option for themselves in the necessary price category and level of comfort. There are apartments of the category "standard" and rooms "superior". Regardless of the room category, the room has everything you need. The rooms are decorated in a classic style and have a calm color palette. Therefore, nothing will irritate the eye here, the atmosphere is soft and pleasant. They are fed quite a variety of dishes. The food is great for children. The food is tasty and quality cooked. For a change, you can visit the cafe in the complex.

General impressions





Pension "Black Sea" is perfect for a relaxing family vacation with children. The rooms here are spacious and comfortable. The furniture is new, household appliances are working properly.

A large number of needles grow on a vast territory, which has a beneficial effect on the health of the body. Children can have fun at the specially equipped playground.

The path to the sea is 30 minutes. The road along the picturesque alleys does not tire at all, but rather charges with new forces.

Vityazevo is an ideal choice for those who care about their health and are not used to overspend.
