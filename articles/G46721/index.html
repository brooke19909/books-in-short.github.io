Usually brought from Turkey:

  • Turkey has always been famous for its handmade carpets. If you believe the reviews of guests of the Sunland Beach Hotel 4 *, then bargaining with the seller, you can buy a real work of art for less than 5,000 rubles.
  • It is better to choose jewelry and jewelry in Kemer, as there is more choice and the price can be found suitable.   • Furs and leather products are a visiting card of Turkey, which is famous for its fashionable products. Their guests at the Sunland Beach Hotel 4 * are also advised to buy in specialized stores, and not in the market, where you can run into a fake.
  • Handmade tea sets and pottery are also popular with travelers.

For sweets, coffee, fruits and olive oil, you should come to the market in Kemer on Monday. It works only one day a week, but all the fruits and vegetables on it are the freshest. This is noted in their reviews by guests of the Sunland Beach Hotel 4 *.

How to relax with children

Many travelers traveling for the first time on holiday to Turkey are interested in how comfortable it will be with children. In this regard, Sunland Beach Hotel 4 * will suit them as well as possible. Toddlers are provided with cots in rooms and chairs in the restaurant.

So that parents can devote more time to themselves, there is a kids club on site. Children are expected there from 10 am to 12:00 or from three in the afternoon until five in the evening. Fun and educational games, various workshops and a playground will not let them get bored.

In a special children's pool, kids under the supervision of their parents will be able to learn how to swim. Well, the rest is waiting for the largest in Europe and the only "Dinopark" in the country. All its "inhabitants" are made in full size and look very similar to the ancient reptiles, and some of the dinosaurs can move and make frightening sounds.

Here, every child can feel like a hero from the movie "Dinotopia" or an archaeologist from "The Lost World" by Conan Doyle. For children, a section is allocated in which the skeleton of a dinosaur is "buried". Under the guidance of a specialist, kids learn with the help of brushes and scrapers to discover secrets hidden in the ground.



All the parents who visited this place with their children say that they got an amazing experience of traveling together with their child along paths, followed by another giant predator or dinosaur waiting for them in the thickets and among the trees. Given that the park occupies 30,000 m 2 , there is something to see. In addition, children can play and swim in the pool located on its territory.

No less interesting for kids will be visiting the caves in which dolphins live, you can enjoy their performance in the dolphinarium of the Moonlight Park.

The Turks did not spare the territory to create an ecopark, for which 40,000 m 2 were allocated. Over 350,000 reptiles and amphibians live here. Many of the presented species are on the verge of extinction, the ecopark has become their home of salvation, where conditions are created that are close to their natural habitat.

As guests of the hotel note in their reviews, you can safely take children here, as they certainly will not be bored.
