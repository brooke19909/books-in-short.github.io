Negative reviews

Unfortunately, even in five-star hotels, travelers are faced with negative aspects and shortcomings. Sentido Blue Sea Beach 5 * (Crete) is no exception. Guest reviews contain the following information:

  • already at the booking stage there is a mess with payment (although it must be made upon arrival, a large amount is blocked on the credit card when making a reservation);
  • even if you agreed in advance with the hotel about early check-in, this does not mean that you will be able to check into your room immediately upon arrival;
  • for most guests, the complete surprise is the fact that only those guests who have a rest on the all-inclusive system can use the beach for free, and everyone else must pay an entrance fee of 4 euros;
  • on the road to the sea you need to cross a busy highway;
  • there are no amenities on the beach like showers or changing rooms;
  • quite often, guests are accommodated in rooms that do not correspond to the reserved category (do not be shy to raise this issue and make a complaint);
  • the hotel has no elevators or ramps;
  • the descent to the beach is very steep and, as a result, traumatic;
  • a very small pool in which there is not enough space if all guests decide to swim in it;
  • the restaurants are very slow waiters, who are also not very welcoming with guests;
  • in the dining room the same music is constantly spinning in a circle;
  • despite the fact that the hotel is located almost on the seashore, fish dishes in the buffet menu are practically absent;
  • due to shower problems and the absence of a special partition, water flows throughout the bathroom;
  • the entire bathroom is covered with black mold and other types of fungus;
  • bath towels are old, washed, and also have holes;
  • bedding is changed very rarely;
  • for beach towels you have to pay a deposit of 10 euros, which can be collected only the next day;
  • rather cramped rooms with modest equipment;
  • on the territory, as well as in all areas of the hotel, there are a lot of mosquitoes that they do not fight with;
  • on the door to the bathroom there is no lock, no hook, or anything else that can be locked;
  • no matter how many people occupy the room, only one key is given per room.
