Rest in the ski resort "Eagle Mountain"

If you do not want to be bored by the window on cold winter evenings, you can go to the Eagle Mountain ski resort, which was equipped in Toksovo. This modern complex has a developed infrastructure. It is located in a forested area on a picturesque lake shore. Also, many choose this place to stay, because it is close enough to the city.

Resort Facilities

Eagle Mountain in Toksovo works and hosts guests around the clock. In addition to skiing, various other services are provided here. For example, if you need to organize your holiday, you can contact the administrator for help. Here you can also rent a room in which it is convenient to conduct seminars or trainings.



Visitor Accommodation

You can come to these places overnight. The Eagle Mountain ski resort accommodates its guests in the park. There are cottages with 8 beds with three bedrooms and 4 beds with one bedroom. In cozy houses there is all modern household equipment that vacationers may need. After active fun on the snowy slopes, it is pleasant to return to the cottage, where there is a sauna and shower. You can also prepare a delicious meal using the fully equipped kitchen with a fridge. Each cottage is equipped with a TV. The period of settlement in any of the houses - from one day or longer.



Winter leisure at the resort

"Eagle Mountain" has three tracks. But usually they arrange skiers of various levels. The first track is intended for those who first started skiing. The second is suitable for those who want more serious descents, but are not yet ready for extreme sports. The last track is the most difficult and made so that it will appeal to experienced athletes.

Those who want to ride a cheesecake use a separate descent.

All routes with which Eagle Mountain is equipped do not stand out for their particular duration or for too steep slopes and elevations. Basically, they are intended so that you can study, train and improve your skills a little. That is, the park is equipped to enjoy the simple skiing.

The slopes are always in good snow condition thanks to special equipment. Even if the temperature is only -3 outside, the meter remains the thickness of snow drifts.



In order to climb the slope, there are two modern and safe lifts, as well as a separate children's lift. Their throughput is 1200 people / hour. They are also equipped with an automated access control system.

In the park there is a rental point for snowmobiles, cheesecakes, cross-country skiing and other sports equipment.

Types of entertainment on the slopes

Eagle Mountain offers the following mountain activities:

  1. Skiing. For this, there are three slopes of varying complexity.
  2. A slope for fans of the new school and for snowboarding. To do this, there is a main track and a small snow park.
  3. Family riding on cheesecakes, for which there is its own track.
  4. For children riding a gentle slope with a separate lift.




If necessary, you can ask an instructor for yourself or for your child.

Summer vacation at the resort

Even in summer you can come to the resort in Toksovo. "Eagle Mountain" in warm time offers to visit a sports complex, which has a volleyball and basketball court. There is also a darts, a football field, a mini-golf course, table tennis, an ATV track, and a paintball field. On the lake near the beach there is a station where you can rent a catamaran, boat or kayak. Also near the water there is a summer atmospheric cafe. In addition, the beach itself has a pleasant rest.
