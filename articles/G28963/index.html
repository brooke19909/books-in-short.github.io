Season in Bali. Bali: weather. Bali: holiday season

Bali is perhaps the most popular Indonesian resort. Thousands of tourists from all over the world come here every year. Pristine landscapes, boundless snow-white beaches, amazingly beautiful nature, the unique culture of the local population - all this attracts travelers here. The tourist season in Bali is open year round.

general information



Bali is one of the islands of the Indonesian archipelago. The size of the island is relatively small. Its area is less than 6000 km¬≤. The nature here is amazing. On the territory you can find many types of rare plants. Banana and fig groves, huge centuries-old trees, tropical jungle - all here are extraordinary. There are a lot of monkeys in Bali. Often you can see them in human settlements. Some locals tamed them and keep them as pets. Balinese are a very friendly people. They honor their traditions and customs, try to strictly abide by them. This should be considered for tourists visiting this resort. Respect for the local order is one of the main conditions for staying on the island.

Climate in Bali

This region has an equatorial monsoon climate . And this means that the change of seasons is not very noticeable here. Most of the time in Bali is very hot. Air temperature often exceeds +30 Àö, and water - +26 Àö. In lowlands and highlands it can be slightly cooler. But on the coast and in the resort area, as a rule, it is warm. The nature of the weather is measured only by the amount of precipitation. From April to October it is dry and sunny. From November to April, showers pour. But this is only a conditional division. Indeed, in the summer long rains can suddenly suddenly begin, and in winter, warm and sunny weather can be established.

Rains in Bali



As mentioned above, depending on the strength of the wind and the amount of precipitation in this region, two time periods can be distinguished. This is the so-called dry season and it is time for heavy rains on the island. What are they characterized by? The rainy season in Bali is usually from November to March. Showers here are short-lived and they go mainly at night. By morning, often everything dries up. After all, it is very hot here. However, eyewitnesses say that the rainiest months in this area are January and February. Rainfall can be very long during this time. Often they go all day. But the prices for permits during this period fall sharply, which cannot but please our compatriots. Thus, in the rainy season in Bali, you can significantly save on vacation. Many tourists come here in December-January to spend New Year holidays here.

Dry season in bali

When is the best time to relax in this region? Of course in the summer. The dry season in Bali is characterized by minimal rainfall and less hot weather. It lasts about six months: from June to October. This is the most favorable time to visit this island. A truly unforgettable vacation in Bali can be. In July, as a rule, the largest concentration of tourists is observed here. This, in turn, affects the increase in the cost of air tickets, various services and accommodation in local hotels. Why is this region so attractive to travelers from around the world at this time? Plus, the dry season is not only in the absence of rain, but also a strong gusty wind, which is important for surfing. In addition, the period from June to October is the time for many national holidays on the Indonesian island.

Local holidays and festivals



The tourist season in Bali is open almost all year round. True, those wishing to get to the local holiday will have to guess with time. Balinese live on their own calendar. Among the local holidays, the following can be distinguished:

  • Galungan. It lasts 10 days. And it is celebrated every 210 days in a year. Religious holiday. It is believed that on this day the gods visit the island so that good conquers evil. The streets are decorated with penjors. Numerous ceremonies take place during the celebration days.
  • Day of silence Nyepi. This is the local New Year. It takes place in March. On this day it is forbidden to work, have fun and make noise. The whole island is immersed in silence.
  • Wesak Festival. Passes in Borobudur for 4 days.

Since Bali is a tourist and sightseeing region, many festivals are focused on tourists coming here. During the celebration, travelers can get acquainted with the art of dancing and ancient rites. So, in September, a 6-day literature festival takes place in Ubud. And in Denpasar in early June, the Pesta Kesenian Bali Arts Festival kicks off.

Surfing season



Where can I have a great time doing sports? Of course in Bali. The holiday season is open year-round. Thousands of surfers from around the world seek to visit the island. The only thing you need to pay attention to the tourist who wants to "tame" the rapid wave is that in some period of time some places intended for this sport may be unsuitable. For example, during the rainy season, surfpots in the south-west of the island often do not function due to low demand from the population and tourists. And in itself this time is not the best period for gliding along the waves. In the rainy season in Bali, the beaches become dirty and the waves of the ocean are muddy. Great places for surfing during this period: Keramas, Serangan, Lembeng, Padang Galak and others. In the dry season, it is better to visit spots on the southwest coast, those that did not work in winter.

Beach season

In Bali, you can find many magnificent sandy beaches of all shades: from golden and white to dark brown (volcanic origin). The water here is always warm. Its average temperature is +26 ... + 28 ¬∞ C. Holidays in Bali in May, perhaps, can be considered one of the most comfortable weather conditions. But lovers to lie on a sunny beach in a stormy period should not go here. Indeed, in addition to heavy rains at this time, severe storms are not rare on the coast. The water in the ocean therefore becomes muddy, "dirty." Algae and garbage nails to the shores. Speaking of swimming here, it is worth mentioning such a thing as the ebbs and flows. They occur every day throughout the year, only at different times. Throughout the summer, the weather on the island is beautiful. Holidays in Bali in August will delight you with gentle warm waves, bright sunshine and a light refreshing breeze.

A 2-week vacation in Bali is not enough to inspect all of them. The holiday season lasts all year. But, as a rule, tourists prefer the summer months of the dry season for excursions. Wedding in bali



Hundreds of lovers annually choose this region for the wedding ceremony. It should be noted that island legislation does not officially register unions of Catholics and Christians. But the symbolic wedding ceremony in this paradise will give unforgettable moments to all newly made wives and husbands. Typically, the peak season is in the summer months. There are many who want to combine a honeymoon and a holiday in Bali. In June, the largest number of marriage ceremonies are held here. However, to make it turn out the way you want, you should take care of this event in advance. 6-7 months before the date of the ceremony, you need to submit an application to the organizer and pay for the service.
