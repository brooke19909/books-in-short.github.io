Moscow, "Buninsky Alleys" - new buildings from the builder. Description, layout and reviews

Modern Muscovites have the opportunity to purchase an apartment in almost any area of ​​the city. Developers do not cease to please with their fantastic offers, thinking through everything literally to the smallest detail. Of course, real estate in Moscow for many is impermissible luxury. That is why New Moscow, a district in the direction of the Kaluga and Warsaw Highways, is currently very popular. It is in this direction that the capital will develop. By purchasing real estate here, each buyer acquires not just an apartment, but a Moscow residence permit and the privileges of living in the capital. We offer you to pay attention to the area "Buninsky alleys". New buildings are presented in a sufficient variety, meet all the requirements of comfort and safety.

As part of this material, we will talk about the best projects from the largest developers. Reviews of real residents, experts' assessment will allow making the most objective review of each presented option.





About the area

What makes the area of ​​the metro station “Buninskaya Alley” so attractive? New buildings from the developer - a great offer for those who are thinking about buying a property. Currently, the district is being actively built up, all the necessary infrastructure facilities are appearing here: kindergartens, schools, clinics, shopping and entertainment centers, fitness clubs and much more. Currently, the Kaluga highway is at the stage of reorganization: in the near future, new transport interchanges will appear here that will reduce traffic density and eliminate traffic jams. And, of course, today the new buildings of Moscow ("Buninskaya Alley") are located in an ecologically clean area, in the immediate vicinity are green parks and squares with ponds and lakes. In addition, it is quite quiet and calm, which residents of vain Moscow will appreciate.

LCD "Buninsky meadows"

Residential complex “Buninsky meadows” is a bright representative of a new residential quarter, located near the metro station “Buninsky alley”. New buildings are presented by Peak Group. From the metro station to the complex is only 12 minutes away, so all residents will have access to infrastructure and can easily reach anywhere in the capital. The complex is surrounded by several parks and ponds, the place is eco-friendly and picturesque. The construction of houses involves the phased construction of buildings and all necessary infrastructure. As the complex grows, new kindergartens, schools, parking lots and recreation areas will appear here. The project provides for the construction of 4 schools, 5 kindergartens, clinics, FOKa. A 10-minute drive from the complex is the Mega shopping center, where you can make the necessary purchases, visit the cinema and have fun with the whole family.





Price policy

The economy-class residential complex is a great option for those families who dream of having their own separate housing. The cost per square meter starts at 80,000 rubles. All apartments are fully finished, just bring furniture.

LCD "White Nights"

And here is another great LCD ("Buninsky Alley"). New buildings are becoming more modern, high-quality, thoughtful. A101 Group managed to positively establish itself in the construction world, having implemented several successful projects, and now the turn has reached the New Moscow. The houses are being built using monolithic technology, which guarantees their reliability and durability.

The apartments are represented by a variety of planning solutions, they are rented out with rough finish, but all the necessary work has been done: cold and hot water, electricity, plumbing installed.





Price policy

New buildings in the Buninsky Alleys district are currently available to many. Moreover, you can save a substantial part of your budget if you purchase an apartment at the construction stage. LCD "White Nights" refers to comfort-class housing. But despite this, the cost per square meter starts at 74,000 rubles.

LCD "Buninsky"

If you value comfort, transport accessibility and well-developed infrastructure, you will surely like the Buninsky (Buninsky Alley) residential complex. The developer is the Peak Group. On his account there are many complexes in New Moscow. All of them are united by a common concept: an ecologically clean area, developed infrastructure, spacious apartments with decoration. The complex is located on the territory of New Moscow, only 6 km from the Moscow Ring Road, not far from South Butovo. The metro station "Buninskaya Alleya" is only 10-15 minutes walk at a calm pace. The project provides economy class buildings of 20 floors each. All of them are equipped with high-speed silent elevators, ground parking, landscaped courtyards and playgrounds.





About pricing

Peak Group is trying to provide as many Muscovites with spacious apartments as possible, therefore, it offers affordable housing. An improved one-room apartment with a turnkey finish can be purchased at a price of 115,000 rubles. per square meter of area.

Microdistrict "New Butovo"

If you are interested in new buildings near the metro station "Buninskaya Alleya", pay attention to the new residential neighborhood, located just 10 minutes from this station. This is a large-scale project, presented by 15 houses built using monolithic-brick technology. Buyers will be able to purchase one-, two-, three- and four-room comfort class apartments ranging from 25 to 100 square meters. Currently, most of the houses have already been built and populated. A kindergarten for 375 places, a shopping and entertainment center with shops, a cinema and an amusement park have already been opened. In the near future, another kindergarten, a comprehensive school and a polyclinic will appear on the territory of the microdistrict. Car owners will be able to take advantage of ground parking, even acquire ownership of a parking space, saving themselves once and for all from parking problems.





Price policy

The cost of apartments in the microdistrict starts at 85,000 rubles per square meter. Interesting planning decisions are the main feature of the project.

LCD "Prima Park"

New buildings near the metro station "Buninskaya Alley" are represented by another excellent complex - "Prima Park". On the territory of New Moscow, in the direction of the Warsaw highway, just 7 km from the Moscow Ring Road there is a new complex. The Buninskaya Alley metro station is just a 15-minute walk away. The complex of the European level has attracted the attention of many, promises to be one of the most interesting.

The project provides for the construction of eight houses with economy class apartments. It is also planned to erect a shopping and entertainment complex next to residential real estate, the necessary objects of social infrastructure, a kindergarten and schools. The builder (EnCo Invest) paid great attention to the development of sports, so he thought out landscaping, building playgrounds and sports grounds, as well as recreation areas where you can have a great time with the whole family. For car owners and guests of the complex, underground and ground parking are provided.





Price policy

Buyers will be able to purchase one-, two- and three-room apartments of improved layout at a price of 79,500 rubles per square meter.

LCD "Legend 18"

Enjoying clean air, walking along winding paths, living in the economically developed area of ​​New Moscow - all these dreams can become an accessible reality. And all thanks to the new buildings of "Buninsky Alley".

LCD "Legend 18" - a project presented by the concern "Krost". The originality of the concept and architectural solution allowed to give the new building such a big name. She will appear near the Garden Park Edalgo and will become part of it. The concept of a "vertical city" formed the basis for construction. The first floors of the house will be reserved for parking and infrastructure.





All buyers will be able to purchase one-, two- and three-room apartments of improved layout. The apartments are for rent with rough finish, some of them have a dressing room and pantry for organizing a convenient storage system.

How to get

Currently, the developer offers to purchase housing with interest-free installments. To receive it, you need to pay 10% of the price of the apartment, an installment plan for a period of 12 months is given. The business class residential complex will receive the attention of those who count on comfortable living conditions. The cost per square meter of living space starts from 124 000 rubles. For this money, each buyer will receive an apartment built using modern technologies and unique materials, as well as access to all objects of developed infrastructure.

To summarize

If you want to live in an area with a developed infrastructure, enjoy the comfort of life of a modern Muscovite, pay attention to the area of ​​the Buninskaya Alley metro station. New buildings here impress with their diversity, they are represented by experienced developers, on the account of which there are many successfully completed projects. Stunning ecology, access to all the facilities of the capital's infrastructure, convenient transportation, all this is noted by residents of New Moscow and those who just decided to purchase an apartment here.

Among the options presented, everyone will find for themselves an excellent complex to their liking, be able to satisfy their taste preferences and meet the planned budget.
