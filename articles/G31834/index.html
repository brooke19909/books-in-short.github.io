Thanks to my grandfather for the victory: Victory Day applications

Victory Day is a great holiday in memory of how the destructive war, destroying everything and everyone in its path, was stopped. Applications for Victory Day should not be of a military nature, but rather, to express the end of the war. The main symbols of victory are, first of all, the St. George ribbon, white doves, and the main attributes of memory are eternal flame and carnations. And the red star is a symbol of the army, which won a great victory.

Materials

The application on the theme "Victory Day" can be made of different materials. This is paper, and plasticine, and even fabric. You can even combine these materials to get an interesting application. A sheet of dense cardboard of a light shade, for example blue - the color of a clear sky, is well suited for the substrate.





Paper

It will be good to make cloves out of paper. They will look more beautiful on applications for Victory Day , if you make them voluminous.

It is necessary to prepare: colored paper, scissors, glue.

To make the stem, you need to cut a long wide strip of green paper, roll it into a tube and glue it so that the paper does not unfold. The finished stalk needs to be crushed a little and glued to the substrate. Next, cut a leaf from green paper and glue it on a substrate to the stalk. On top of the stem you need to glue another piece of green paper to make a thickening - preparation for the flower petals.

It remains to make the flower itself. Cut it out of red paper. It will be necessary to cut at least nine isosceles triangles. Roll each of them along the long side and glue - the resulting petals will look like a cone. You can also flatten them and make a few cuts on top to make it look like a fringe, like on clove flowers.

The first 5 petals are fan-glued to the prepared top of the stem. The remaining petals are also glued with a fan, but already on top of the first row. The flower is ready! To apply for Victory Day, you can make several flowers and place them according to the type of bouquet.

Plasticine

From plasticine, you can portray fire.

Necessary to prepare: plasticine, stick.

First you need to roll the flagella from plasticine in yellow, red and orange. The fire is bright in the center and darker at the edges. Place the flagella of plasticine in the same way - yellow more in the center, then orange and red. Press the flagella to the cardboard.

Now, with a stick or a toothpick, draw along the plasticine from bottom to top so that uneven, shallow grooves are obtained. It should look like torn flames. The eternal flame for applying to Victory Day can be decorated in the base - a star - or made like a torch.

the cloth

The fabric is associated with softness and warmth. If made from the fabric of a white dove - this will add tenderness to the application. White felt is best suited.

It is necessary to prepare: white felt fabric, pins, pencil, dove image on paper, scissors, glue.

To save time on drawing, a picture of a pigeon can simply be printed. Now you need to cut out the pigeon according to the elements, separately the trunk and wings. Attach these elements to the fabric, carefully pin them with pins so that they do not accidentally slide out, and outline. And again cut the pigeon, now from the fabric.





We paste the prepared elements from the fabric onto the substrate. Beak and eyes can be made of paper. Glue the eyes directly on top of the fabric. In order to make the outline of the pigeon look more distinct, and the wings do not merge with the body, you need to carefully circle everything with a gray pencil.

Conclusion





May 9 - Victory Day. The application on this topic should be bright, carry the joy of life, while reflecting the gratitude and pride. It can be very difficult to put such a meaning into the craft, but if you try, everything will work out!
