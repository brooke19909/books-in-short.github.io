Hotel At Home Boutique Hotel 3 * (Phuket, Thailand): description and photos

If you are looking for an inexpensive but comfortable hotel in Phuket (Thailand) with a good location near the beach and the main tourist attractions, then we suggest you consider At Home Boutique Hotel 3 *. We will find out further what awaits guests here.



Location

According to travelers, the hotel in question can confidently boast a very successful location. So, it is located almost in the very center of the city. However, its windows do not overlook the busy road, so that nothing will interfere with the rest of the guests. Bangla Road, a popular tourist attraction, can be reached in just 7-10 minutes. The journey to the Jungle Ceylon Shopping Center will take no more than five minutes. Also in the vicinity of the hotel there are a lot of cafes, bars, car rental and organization of excursions, massage parlors, markets, souvenir shops, shops, etc. The hotel is also located near Patong Beach. So, our compatriots, vacationing here, argue that the road to the sea took them no more than a quarter of an hour and did not cause any inconvenience. Along the way, you will meet a lot of outlets and cafes where you can have a snack, drink freshly squeezed juice or a shake and buy something.



General information, photo

At Home Boutique Hotel 3 * (Phuket, Thailand) was built in 2012. This is a regular city hotel without its own territory. It consists of one six-story building, equipped with a lift for the convenience of guests. Housing stock is represented by 51 numbers. The hotel has a 24-hour front desk, parking, luggage storage, currency exchange, Internet cafes, a spacious lounge with comfortable sofas and armchairs, a restaurant, a bar, and a rental car.

Our compatriots, having left 3 reviews about At Home Boutique Hotel, claim that the hotel is fully consistent with its price and category. According to them, it is a very good option for those who want a budget and comfort to relax in Phuket. A confirmation of this is the hotel rating based on travelers' ratings. He makes 4.3 points out of the maximum possible five.



Cleaning service

The hotel guests, according to them, were quite satisfied with the quality of the work of the maids. So, tourists note that cleaning of their rooms was carried out regularly and always at a time convenient for guests. Towels and bed linen are also updated several times a week. As necessary, maids replenish supplies of personal hygiene products (soap, shampoo, shower gel, etc.).

Our compatriots also had no complaints about the rest of the staff at Home Home Boutique. So, all the employees here are friendly, friendly, quick, polite and always happy to help or answer any question. Communicate here, according to tourists, have mainly in English.

As for the settlement, then, in accordance with the internal rules of the hotel, it is made after two in the afternoon. However, as tourists from different countries note in their reviews, if you arrive at the hotel earlier than the indicated time, and there will be available rooms, then the keys will be issued to you immediately, without condemning you to waiting. On the day of departure, the apartment must be vacated before 13:00. If your departure to the airport is planned at a later time, then you can either extend the stay for an additional fee, or hand over the room keys, leave things in the luggage storage room and continue to relax.



Number of rooms

As already mentioned, there are 51 rooms at the At Home Boutique Hotel 3 * (Phuket, Patong). All are deluxe. The apartments here, according to the guests, are spacious, clean and comfortable. They have everything you need for a comfortable stay: comfortable furniture, split air conditioning, TV with cable channels (including in Russian), minibar (you can use the content for a fee), safe, telephone, hairdryer, bathroom with shower, electric kettle and tea set; balcony with plastic chairs and table. Flooring - ceramic tiles. A big plus, according to most travelers, is the availability of Wi-Fi access in all apartments and in public areas of the hotel. Wi-Fi here can be used absolutely free.

Please note that some guests are advised to check into rooms with windows facing the street whenever possible. Otherwise, the view from the window will be reduced to a nearby hotel.



Nutrition

The price of the hotel in question includes breakfast only. According to tourists, they cannot be called particularly diverse, however, in order to eat in the morning, this is quite enough. So, here you will be offered omelet or fried eggs, side dish, salad, bacon, sausages, pastries, juice, tea or coffee. During the day, you can also eat in the hotel restaurant.
