The best hostel in Irkutsk

Many people come to the city of Irkutsk not only to relax, but also on business trips. Therefore, you need to know in which hostels you can inexpensively, but comfortably relax. The article will describe the best places. Their rooms are always clean and have all the necessary equipment.

Montana

Hostel "Montana" in Irkutsk is quite popular. A day you need to pay about 400 rubles per room. The hotel not only provides quality service to guests, but also provides a huge number of additional services. You can always order a transfer. The reception is open 24 hours a day. In the room fund there are several rooms that differ in price. Thanks to this, any guest with any budget will be able to find the best option for himself. You can live in a shared room or in an individual room with 2 beds. It is possible to rent barbecue facilities. There is also a shared kitchen. You can park your car in the parking lot. A children's room is equipped for children.

The reviews about the hostel in Irkutsk write that the staff is friendly. The room is nice, it is comfortable to live in. The kitchen is also equipped with the necessary equipment. All rooms are clean, cleaned in them every day. There is no need to pay extra for this service.

"Bravo"

Inexpensive hostel "Bravo" in Irkutsk will cost living in 400 rubles per place per day. The advantage of this establishment is that it is located near the airport. For guests internet access. The rooms have a TV. The bathroom is shared. You can order a transfer. There is a common living room, kitchen, luggage room, travel desk, safe. The kitchen is shared. Free parking is available for cars. A gift shop is located next to the hotel.

There are rooms of various types, including for families. All are soundproofed. Smoking is allowed only at a designated area. In the common living room, the TV has children's channels. The staff speaks Russian, German, English.

Residents report that the information of the administration of this hostel in Irkutsk that the rooms are isolated and no sounds are heard is a lie. Many complain that any sneeze is heard. The rest of the hotel is only praised. It is great for those with morning flights, as the airport is nearby. There are vending machines with food.

"Lotus"

This hostel is located in Irkutsk on Baikalskaya, house 14b. Room rate - 730 rubles. This establishment is located in the city center. There are numbers on any wallet: from budget to luxury. The hotels offer breakfast with foreign dishes, as well as a guesthouse of Russian cuisine. There is free internet, parking, guarded around the clock, city maps, transfer. The hotel can also arrange a city tour.

In reviews of the institution, many say that this hostel in Irkutsk is one of the best. Parking space costs 150 rubles. The kitchen has an electric kettle. There is a ski area nearby. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning and heating. You can order delivery of food or drinks. For free, the administrator can wake the tenant, call him a taxi, and also deliver the necessary correspondence. If necessary, you can use the services of ironing, laundry. In addition, guests note that for such a small price - this hostel is luxurious. The workers are polite. The rooms, although small, but comfortable.

Bast-basket

This hostel is located in Irkutsk in the center, also on Baikalskaya Street. The minimum daily cost of living is 500 rubles. Free internet is available on site, and parking is also free. You can come with pets. The registration desk works constantly. The airport is just 4 km away.

There is a shared kitchen with a kettle, a fridge and all the necessary accessories. The rooms have a safe, air conditioning, heating. With regards to food, that is, vending machines with food. The bar is open. Packed lunches are available. Room service is available.

The staff speaks Russian, English and Chinese.

In the reviews about the hostel in Irkutsk they write what is cozy in the restaurant. The rooms are well decorated, equipped with everything necessary. In addition, the hotel is quiet. Pets are really accommodated, but, as a rule, the administration agrees only on not very large pets.

"Autograph"

This hotel is also inexpensive - you will have to pay about 400 rubles per day. The number of rooms is extensive. There are hostel and family rooms. All have a small kitchen. It has a kettle and microwave. Amenities are not individual, located on the floor. Meals are not included in the price. You will have to cook it yourself in the shared kitchen.

An equipped ski area is located next to the hotel. If you need a tourist to be met at the airport, you can order a transfer for an additional fee.

The hotel has a ski storage room. You can come with pets. The staff also speaks English. No smoking on site. The reception desk is open all the time. Cleaning is done every day. Internet connection.

City

An excellent hostel that takes from 500 rubles per day. The hotel has internet access. Guests have access to a shared kitchen and bathroom. They have everything you need for washing and cooking. There is also a bar on site.

Free parking space is provided. For accommodation with pets you do not have to pay extra. The hotel has ironing, housekeeping, and laundry services. Guests can watch TV or play board games in the shared lounge. Luggage storage facilities are available.

The hotel reviews write that it is clean and comfortable. The staff is friendly. The location of the hostel itself is also called a plus.

Rus-Irkutsk

This hostel in Irkutsk will cost 350 rubles a day. The hotel is located in the city center. The location is as convenient as possible, because from here you can choose absolutely any route for the tour. The rooms have beds, lockers, keys, sockets, lamps. Guests have access to a shared kitchen where you can prepare your own meals. On the territory of the institution is open sports and reading rooms, there is free internet.

Not far from the hostel is a quarter in which there are many cafes and restaurants, shops and shopping centers. In the reviews they write that guests are sometimes noisy, the administration rarely reacts to this. There are no complaints about other nuances of the hotel.

Summary

The article describes the best hostels in the city. They are clean and always comfortable. The administration will be happy to answer all questions, populate and fulfill the request.

It should be noted that the Hostel Quest also exists in Irkutsk. For many tourists this is misleading. The fact is that this institution is not a hotel, it is a special quest room where you can play with friends.
