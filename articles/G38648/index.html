Hotel The Mangrove Panwa Phuket Resort 5 * (Phuket, Thailand): description and photos

Planning to visit Thailand? Are you looking for a comfortable hotel with comfortable rooms and excellent service at an affordable price? Are you not bothered by the need to get to the beach and city center by taxi or rented motorbike? Then the The Mangrove Panwa Phuket Resort 5 * hotel can be for you a very good option.



Location

This hotel is located in Phuket (Thailand), 36 kilometers from the international airport. So it will take you less than an hour to get to the hotel. The city center can be reached by taxi or bike in 10 minutes. The nearest Panwa Beach is a five minute walk. However, it is of little use for swimming, so tourists go to Ao Yon or other beaches in Phuket. In this regard, experienced guests are advised to rent a car or scooter to be mobile and to be able to visit all the places of interest. By the way, a free shuttle service is organized several times a day from the hotel to Ao Yon Beach and to the city center. But here you have to go back already yourself.

Guests can also use the laundry facilities and book an excursion to a place of interest.

In general, judging by the reviews of our compatriots, the Mangrove Panwa Phuket Resort is ideal for those tourists who wish to get away from city noise and relax while enjoying the sea, nature and excellent unobtrusive service. However, some travelers do not recommend coming here for people of age and families with small children due to the fact that the hotel area is multi-level, so you often have to overcome the steps. In addition, all the facilities of the tourist infrastructure of Phuket, guests will have to get by taxi or scooter, which for some categories of vacationers can be of some difficulty.



Housing stock

As already mentioned, The Mangrove Panwa Phuket Resort 5 * hotel has at its disposal 32 rooms located in the main building and 16 villas. All residential buildings are located in a picturesque and very well-groomed area. So from your windows you can enjoy the tropical garden. By the way, a number of apartments have a magnificent view of the sea. The hotel rooms are presented in the following categories: studio deluxe two-bedroom (can accommodate up to four guests), studio villa with sea view (designed the same as all subsequent ones, for two), open-plan villa with views garden, studio deluxe single.

The apartments, according to our compatriots, are equipped with everything that you may need during your stay. So, the rooms have comfortable high-quality wooden furniture. Especially the guests praised the large comfortable beds, on which, according to them, you can sleep well. Also in the apartments there is a flat-screen TV (cable channels), electronic safe, telephone, air conditioning, bathroom, hairdryer, balcony or terrace. Some rooms are also equipped with a jacuzzi. In addition, the apartments have a kitchenette with a fridge, microwave, electric kettle and kitchenware. According to travelers, it fits perfectly into the design of the spacious rooms. A big plus also, according to hotel guests, is the fact that there is free internet access both in the apartments and throughout the territory. So you can always be in touch with your friends and family, staying at home.

Guests always have the opportunity to order room service for an additional fee.



Cleaning service

Tourists vacationing during their holidays at this hotel had no complaints about the work of the maids, judging by their comments. So, according to the guests, the cleaning of their rooms was carried out very efficiently - every day or on demand. Maids regularly updated towels, bedclothes, brought bottled drinking water, replenished supplies of tea set and personal hygiene products.

As for the rest of the staff, then, according to the guests, all the staff at the hotel are very welcoming, smiling and always happy to help.



Nutrition

Our compatriots who left 5 * reviews about The Mangrove Panwa Phuket Resort hotel, according to them, were very pleased with the work of the local restaurant. So, since breakfast is included in the price, in the morning almost all guests ate at the hotel. According to tourists, the food here is very tasty and satisfying. Throughout the day, guests can also eat in the hotel restaurant for an additional fee. A big plus, according to our compatriots, is the fact that many of the dishes on the menu are adapted to the taste habits of travelers.
