Legie 3 *, Prague: reviews and description

Prague is a city in the Czech Republic that attracts a large number of tourists from all over the world. There are a huge number of historical monuments, and on the old streets you can see unique architectural structures erected several centuries ago.

Every year, the capital of the Czech Republic is visited by thousands of tourists. All of them are forced to look for a place suitable for living during their stay in the city. Many travelers prefer renting rooms at the Legie 3 * hotel in Prague. Reviews about this place can be found in sufficient quantities on specialized tourist resources, as well as on personal pages of travelers.

general information

The hotel has a category of "three stars", but despite this it provides excellent conditions for living and recreation. It was built in 1930 and is currently located in an old building that has eight tiers. It periodically carried out various kinds of renovations, one of the last restorations was made in 2016. During the repair work, the interior of the hotel was changed, modern furniture was installed, and the technical equipment was improved in the apartments.



Location

Reviews of Legie 3 * (Prague) often mention its excellent location. Tourists on the positive side note that the building itself is located in the very center of the capital, within walking distance from the main attractions. Not far from here is the famous Charles Bridge and the Puppet Theater, which many travelers who appreciate art also seek to visit.

Next to the hotel are concentrated business centers, as well as office buildings, which together represent a large business quarter. That is why quite often Legie 3 * lodgers become tourists whose trip is connected with resolving business issues - such people value access to important infrastructure and the availability of comfortable conditions for relaxation - all this is available in this Prague hotel.

In reviews of tourists about Legie 3 * it is also mentioned that transport links are located within walking distance - this is also very convenient, especially for those travelers who do not have a personal car, but who want to travel around the city. The airport is a 15-minute drive, and the nearest metro station is a five-minute walk. Tourists in their reviews of the Legie 3 * hotel in Prague note this fact as a positive point.

Rooms

The hotel has a small number of rooms - it has only 55 apartments. All of them are presented in three categories: standard, family and luxury. For some time now, each of them has been modernized, after which the furniture equipment has been updated. Now, if desired, guests can take the opportunity to adjust the desired temperature using an individual air conditioner, which is available in each room.

All apartments have an attached bathroom to the main square. Everything is also arranged in a modern way in it: there is a new plumbing, represented by a bathing place, a bathroom and a sink. There is a large mirror as a decorative object, and there is also a place to store towels, bathrobes and slippers. In addition, each tourist at the time of arrival receives a personal set of bath accessories.

Family apartments

Most tourists are inclined to believe that this place is more suitable for youth recreation - this is often stated in their reviews. It also provides accommodation for tourists with children.

Suites

The rooms of this category present to the attention of tourists the conditions for superior living. In reviews of Legie 3 * (Prague), the features of such rooms are often mentioned, travelers highlight their rich decoration and homely atmosphere.

Service

Hotel guests are provided with excellent service, which they often write about in their reviews of Legie 3 * (Prague). An overview of additional features provides various services. Among them, the most popular is the possibility of renting cars that are in the hotel fund. They can be provided by the day, with the possibility of allocating free parking space for cars. Many travelers recommend that you definitely use this service and take a rental car to explore local attractions and the most interesting architectural objects.

Also, often guests prefer to use the possibility of paid parking. The hotel notes that a car parking space is only available with prior reservation.

For an extra fee, guests can take part in excursions organized by professional guides. Many guests prefer to book an individual tour, but collective trips to the sights of Prague are often organized.

For business

People who visit the Czech Republic as part of a business visit often become Legie 3 * guests (Prague). In the reviews, they share their impressions from their stay in this place. Here, they said, an excellent conference room was organized in which it is possible to conduct various kinds of business events.

The hall is equipped with lighting and sound equipment, which is able to make any presentation more vivid and vibrant. Invited guests can sit in comfortable chairs at wooden tables. If necessary, visitors can be provided with office supplies that can be used.

The hall can be rented hourly, on the reservation of a certain time.

Additional features

Hotel guests can always seek advice of any nature from the administration, who are around the clock at the reception. If necessary, they can assist in calling a taxi or make a wake-up call at the time set by the tourist.

On the ground floor of the building, not far from this counter, there is a small shop selling various souvenirs with the symbols of the country and the main sights of Prague. In reviews of Legie 3 * and in the description of the hotel it is often said that here you can also hand over your luggage to the safe in a safe - it is located behind the counter. Storage is carried out on a fee basis, is charged daily.

Nutrition

In reviews of Legie 3 * (Prague), it is often said about delicious food, which is provided for guests. Each morning, guests staying on a package tour are invited to a restaurant located on the ground floor. Here, a buffet is served for them, which serves excellently prepared by talented chefs dishes in the style of Russian and European cuisines.

As for the interior of the institution, a large number of classic details prevail in it. Many pieces of furniture are made of light wood. Visitors can quite comfortably sit in comfortable chairs with soft seats, at tables covered with white tablecloths.

If desired, vacationers can order room service - this is carried out at an additional cost.
