Games of our childhood: paper doll for cutting with clothes

Modern mothers and grandmothers who have crossed the 40-year frontier and the older generation will surely tell their daughters and granddaughters about one of the most beloved toys of their childhood - paper dolls, which could be changed into a variety of outfits. There are, of course, such sets for cutting and now. And if you did not find them in smart modern supermarkets - it does not matter! Homemade doll



If you were not able to get at least one copy of the toy, the trouble is fixable. After all, without even having special inclinations in the visual arts, you can draw such a silhouette doll. You just need to practice a bit. The same applies to the wardrobe. Come up with styles and models, create, feel like a famous couturier! A doll for cutting with clothes is for this purpose intended. Attach it to a blank sheet, circle the contours with a simple pencil. Make magnificent sleeves, flared skirts, pick up silhouettes interesting to you.
