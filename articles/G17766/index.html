SPA services

Ample opportunities for recreation and restoration offers its guests Hedef Resort & SPA Hotel. The beach, animation program and much more will fill your leisure time. And if you want to completely relax, go to the SPA salon, where you can go through the following procedures:

  • Swedish massage - manipulations using valuable vegetable and essential oils. It is aimed at general muscle and emotional relaxation. The duration of the procedure is 1 hour.
  • Sports massage is performed before or immediately after visiting the gym. It can be aimed both at stimulating muscle activity, and at relieving stress.
  • Aromatherapy massage is carried out using valuable esters. It is performed at a slow pace with a low pressure intensity. The duration of the procedure is 1 hour.
  • Thai massage involves alternating compressive and stretching movements. In total, the master can use up to 60 techniques in one session. Despite some pain, in the end you will feel complete relaxation and a surge of energy. The duration of the procedure is from one and a half to four hours.
  • Reflexology is the effect on biologically active points located on the feet. By acting on a particular zone, the master exerts a therapeutic effect on a specific organ. The duration of the procedure is half an hour.
  • Deep massage is performed with a high intensity of pressing and is aimed at removing internal blocks and eliminating tension. This will allow you to get rid of the pain and stiffness of the joints. The duration of the procedure is 1 hour.

Additional Information

If you are planning a vacation at the Hedef Resort & SPA Hotel, check out some additional information about his work. Here are some points to consider:

  • Check-in of newly arrived guests starts after 14:00. Early check-in is available at an additional cost.
  • On the day of departure you need to leave the room before noon. For each hour spent in the apartment after checkout time, a fee is charged.
  • The rooms cannot accommodate extra guests.
  • Pets are not allowed.
  • Guests can pay for services received in cashless form.
  • Self-registration is only possible from the age of 18.
