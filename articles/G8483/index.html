Sanatoriums and pensions in Gagra: photos and reviews

In Soviet times, Gagra was one of the most popular resorts in Abkhazia. Beautiful nature, clear blue sea, gentle southern sun - nothing has changed in this hospitable city. Gagra resorts and pensions today are also welcoming their guests.

Sanatorium "Amra"

The location of the sanatorium "Amra" is Old Gagra. The health resort has its own arboretum. It has many alleys and paths for walks, cozy places that have a relaxing holiday. The main three-story building, impressive in its architecture, is designed to accommodate vacationers. At the same time, 254 guests can live here. Now the first building is completely reconstructed. In May 2018, it is planned to open it under the new name Amra Park-Hotel & Spa. There is another small building.

The price includes inhalations, physiotherapy, classes in the gym, meals as desired (breakfast only, full board, without meals). A spa complex is open for guests, sports grounds and a cafe are equipped. On your own pebble-sandy beach there are sun loungers, parasols, showers and changing cabins. The health resort also offers library services, a tour desk, and has its own cinema. If you want to relax on the territory of Old Gagra in a boarding house or sanatorium, then the best option would be Park Hotel Amra Park-Hotel & Spa.

Pension "Caucasus"

Recreational boarding house "Caucasus" is located in a five-story typical building. Around the hotel is a picturesque park area. 30 meters from it has its own equipped pebble beach. Sun beds and umbrellas can be rented. Vacationers are provided with three meals a day, which is included in the price. Tourists eat in the dining room, there is also a cafe, from which stunning landscapes open. There is a disco in the evenings. Accommodation in the boarding house "Caucasus" (Gagra) is offered both in economy rooms with a shared shower on the floor, and in deluxe rooms. A visit to the hydropathic and organization of excursions are provided for a fee. The cost of excursions ranges from 350 to 1500 rubles.

Sanatorium "Moscow"

In Abkhazia in Gagra, boarding houses and sanatoriums will offer you relaxation for every taste. A good choice would be the sanatorium "Moscow". Here vacationers are accommodated:

  • in the six-story main building in single and double rooms of standard, junior and deluxe categories;
  • in the climate pavilion, a one-story building just five meters from the sea, each room has a balcony, partial amenities.

Three meals a day are served in the dining room according to the order-menu system. On the territory there is a sports ground, a bar, a cinema hall, a library, discos are held in the evenings. Private pebble beach is located at a distance of 80 meters from the buildings. Since the health resort is located on a hill, an elevator works to access the beach. The resort is surrounded by a picturesque park.

Sanatorium "Sana"

Rest in the guesthouses of Gagra is beautiful, but if you need to heal, then you can stop your choice at the sanatorium "Sana". The sea and the sand and pebble beach are 500 meters from the sanatorium. Around the health resort there is a beautiful palm grove, many exotic plants, paths for terrenkur are equipped. The sanatorium has its own balneological department, inhalation, biochemical and clinical laboratories, massage and physiotherapy rooms. The number of rooms is represented by 75 rooms with balconies with outdoor furniture. Sana is the only sanatorium in Gagra with its own hydropathic center.

Pension "Sunny"

Like most Gagra resorts, Solnechny has its own equipped beach, on which there are sunbeds, umbrellas, showers. Sun loungers are provided free of charge. The boarding house is located in a high-rise building surrounded by a pine forest on the picturesque slope of the Gagra Range. Great sea views are available from all rooms. Each has a large balcony. The room system is block - in one block there are two rooms with a shared shower and a bathroom. The holiday home has a large park area for walking. A small river with crystal clear water flows through the territory. The rooms have free WIFI.

Sanatoriums and pensions Gagra. Tourists reviews

Rest in Abkhazia always leaves a lot of impressions. Many tourists share about them in their reviews.

Given the fact that the Amra sanatorium reopens after reconstruction, it is now difficult to objectively evaluate many reviews. But there are a number of points why tourists choose this particular sanatorium. It is a clean and warm sea, a spacious beach, an incredibly beautiful park, and the whole nature around. Amazing air: it smells of eucalyptus and boxwood. The staff is attentive. As one of Amra's vacationers wrote, a land resembling paradise.

Pension "Caucasus" is popular with tourists for its location on the first line, there is a private beach, clear sea, warm water. Pleasantly surprised by professional staff and daily cleaning: the rooms are always clean. In the evenings, live music is played. There is a cafe. Shops within walking distance.

Guests of the sanatorium "Moscow" consider the sea and nature to be magnificent. The building and the park look very beautiful on the side of the mountain. Given the power of accommodation is inexpensive. The balcony offers stunning views. Plumbing and furniture are old. According to vacationers, the rooms need a complete overhaul, and the park needs restoration.

As for the boarding house "Energetik", vacationers like on its territory a magnificent tropical park with alleys, a wonderful beach and clear sea. Great location near the market. Unfortunately, very old rooms, elevator work poorly. Many note the lack of communication among staff.

Tourists speak of the sanatorium Sana, as well as other health resorts of Gagra, as majestic Soviet buildings that need serious reconstruction. A big plus is the presence of a balcony with outdoor furniture, which offers beautiful views. In the mornings, morning exercises are carried out for vacationers. Of the minuses - the beach is very far away.
