Talk about the cost

You already know how to call the MTS operator, but you need to know about one interesting point. Oddly enough, the fact that the call is made for free does not mean that you will not have to pay anything. If the service that you ask the operator for can be made by you through the self-service terminal, then you will pay 10 rubles for it. This does not apply to simple consultations, but this includes, for example, connecting additional options. Other solutions to problems





Instead of calling the operator, you yourself can solve many problems without resorting to his help. There are tons of options for this. You can always choose the most convenient. We manage SMS services

This is a free resource available to anyone at any place where he has a telephone connection. Upon this request, you will receive a complete list of services that you can get using this method. If you sent an application to change the tariff plan, and this is impossible for technical reasons, you will receive a response: "This request cannot be fulfilled." However, nothing will be deducted from your balance sheet. You can also find all service codes on the operator‚Äôs website. This method is available even to a child, and you can use it, for example, on a trip.
