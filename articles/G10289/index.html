Jomtien Orchid 3 *: hotel description

Description. Jomtien Orchid 3 Hotel is located in Pattaya (Thailand), the third line from the sea. This is an eight-story building, which was erected in 1992. The last repair was carried out in 2010. Thanks to the convenient location of the hotel, any tourist can get to the city center without problems using a taxi ("tuk-tuk"). In a short period of time, you can reach the main shopping centers, for example, Central Festival Pattaya, or visit the famous Walking street. Walking along the streets of the southern part of Pattaya, where the hotel is located, you can find a lot of souvenir pavilions, various cafes and minimarkets. Jomtien Orchid 3 is great for an economical family vacation. Bangkok Airport is 150 km, Pattaya is 40 km from the hotel.

Additional services for vacationers.
