Hotel location

The resort complex Sentido Vasia Resort & Spa 5 * is located in the cozy fishing village of Sissi, which attracts tourists with silence and beautiful nature. Near the hotel there are many shops, catering establishments and other infrastructure facilities. Public transport is also nearby. Heraklion International Airport is 39 km away.

  • Superior is a spacious room of 25 square meters. m. In addition to a large bed, there is also a folding sofa, which serves to relax or accommodate additional guests. A workplace is also provided. From the balcony you can enjoy the pool.

Everything for a comfortable stay is in the rooms of the Sentido Vasia Resort & Spa 5 * hotel. Greece is a welcoming resort where they care about the welfare of travelers. The rooms include the following amenities:

High-quality service is one of the main features of the Sentido Vasia Resort & Spa 5 * hotel. The following services are provided here:

  • free wireless internet in the lobby area;

Entertainment

The Cretan Sea is a great place to relax. However, in addition to being on the beach, guests also need entertainment. This hotel provides the following facilities for organizing leisure activities for guests:

  • 7 outdoor pools of various sizes with lots of opportunities for entertainment and relaxation;
  • SPA salon with a children's therapeutic zone;
  • open amphitheater, which hosts evening shows and costume shows;
  • daily sports animation, which includes morning exercises, aerobics in the pool, as well as yoga and dance classes;
  • football field with natural grass lawn;
  • after dinner discos are held, and several times a week - theme parties.




Restaurants

You will have no shortage of delicious food and quality drinks if you opt for the Sentido Vasia Resort & Spa 5 * hotel. Crete is famous not only for nature and culture, but also for culinary traditions. You can get acquainted with the cuisine of this area, as well as around the world in the following institutions:

  • Dionysos is the main restaurant of the hotel. It is open daily for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Here you can enjoy local and international dishes served in buffet style.
  • Pelagos is a cozy tavern in the open air. It serves traditional Cretan dishes. The peculiarity of the establishment is that you can have dinner while admiring the sea.
  • La Trattoria is a luxurious Italian restaurant that boasts a rich menu and an extensive wine list. It is possible to dine in the cozy lounge or on the outdoor terrace.
  • Gourmet Restaurant is located on the outdoor terrace overlooking the sea. Guests can sit on cozy wicker sofas with soft pillows. The chef prepares exclusive gourmet cuisine.




Bars

An unlimited number of drinks and light snacks awaits you in the bars of the resort hotel Sentido Vasia Resort & Spa 5 * (Sissi). Guests can visit the following facilities:

  • The lobby bar is luxurious in light colors and has a relaxed atmosphere. Here you can conduct business negotiations, a romantic date or have a good time with friends.
  • The game bar is an atmospheric establishment with a pleasant interior. In addition to the bar, there are billiard tables, electronic machines and plasma panels for watching sports matches.
  • The pool bar is open from 10:00 to 18:00. Here, in addition to refreshing drinks and alcohol, guests are also served snacks and fast food.
  • The tropical bar is a great place for lovers of fresh fruits and original cocktails. The facility is located next to the pool in the cool shade of trees.
  • Aquarium is a trendy night club where discos and theme parties are held. Here you can also enjoy the music of professional DJs.

Positive reviews

Many travelers visiting Crete choose the resort Sentido Vasia Resort & Spa 5 * for relaxation. Reviews note the following advantages of this institution:

  • three times a week a free shuttle to the nearest city is provided;
  • if guests arrive earlier than the check-in time, employees do everything possible to reduce the waiting time;
  • on the day of arrival, guests will expect fruit, wine, and a free minibar in the room;
  • maids replenish daily supplies of tea and coffee bags, even if the previous ones have not been used up yet;
  • pleased with the presence of an electric kettle and utensils in the room;
  • tasty and high-quality food, similar to homemade (but without much variety);
  • all day guests are offered delicious ice cream (you can take it in unlimited quantities).

Negative reviews

It is also worth paying attention to the negative aspects that you may encounter at Sentido Vasia Resort & Spa 5 *. Crete is visited by a large number of tourists, and therefore reviews about this hotel are quite numerous. In them you can find negative comments of the following content:

  • Despite the fact that the hotel was renovated relatively recently, the situation in it is very shabby;
  • there is a lot of garbage right outside the hotel (employees could clean up in the adjacent area);
  • the bathroom smells very damp, a lot of mold;
  • the restaurant has almost no food for young children;
  • Considering that the hotel has few tourists from the CIS countries, the employees do not speak Russian (animation is only in English);
  • queues are constantly lined up at the distribution in the restaurant (especially a lot of people gather for fresh juices).

When planning a trip to Crete, many dream of relaxing in a 5 * hotel. Sentido Vasia Resort & Spa (Sissi) is a cozy hotel located in the middle of beautiful nature. Despite the presence of some shortcomings, in general, the institution makes the most positive impression. It offers quality service and a cozy atmosphere. A delicious meal and a warm sea will complement the picture of relaxation.
