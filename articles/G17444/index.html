Garcia Resort & Spa 5 * (Turkey / Fethiye): service overview, prices, reviews

Turkey is famous for a large number of various places where you can relax both in the lap of nature and beaches, as well as in urban conditions, satisfying your gastronomic appetite. If with the second option everything is quite simple, then a lot of debate and controversy erupts over which city's beach is better. One of the places where you can relax is a town called Oludeniz, which is several tens of kilometers from Fethiye. As in any resort town, there is a large number of hotels. Among them can be distinguished hotel Garcia Resort Spa, which has five stars.



Hotel location

Garcia Resort Spa Oludeniz 5 * is located in a small Turkish town called Oludeniz. This town is not isolated, but is under the jurisdiction of a larger resort town - Fethiye. Of course, the location of this hotel cannot but please visitors. At the request of people who have stopped at this hotel, you can go to the stunning white sand beach, which is one of the best in the world. Also nearby is the Oludeniz National Park. Elements of untouched nature have been preserved in this place, which allows the average person to feel all the splendor of our planet in just a few tens of minutes. If your plans include the purchase of any fish products, then a couple of blocks from the Gracia Resort Spa is the city‚Äôs fish market, where you can buy all kinds of fish at a good price.



How to get to the hotel

This hotel is located in Turkey, which means that the most important stage of the trip is arrival in the country. You can do this by flying from your city to the capital of Turkey, the city of Istanbul. Neither Oludeniz nor Fethiye has its own airport, and the nearest one is located in neighboring Dalaman. The cost of a ticket to this city is about 100 US dollars in both directions per person. From Dalaman, there are two ways to get to the coveted Fethiye Garcia Resort Spa. The first is the most expensive and easiest - to take a taxi and go straight to Oludeniz. At the same time, you will spend no more than one and a half hours on a trip. The second way is cheaper and more complicated. From Dalaman you can take buses to Fethiye, and already from this resort city - to Oludeniz and Gracia Resort Spa, in particular, local taxis and minibuses leave several times per hour. The road may seem long and tiring, but if you want to get real pleasure from the rest, then it is worth doing.

The facade of the building and the view from the windows of the rooms

The hotel was built relatively recently, and the reason for this was a noticeable increase in the flow of tourists to Oludeniz. The building itself is made in bright and warm colors. Its facade is completely white, and the roofs are covered with brown tiles. Each hotel room faces the south side of the hotel, allowing you to see the stunning nature and Oludeniz Bay. If you look at the Gracia Resort Spa from afar, it may seem like it is not a hotel building, but a ship filled with a large number of passengers interested.



Hotel Conditions

This hotel can be classified as the "Best Fethiye Hotels". Once here, you can forget about home life, which probably already bored everyone. The main advantage of the hotel is that it can easily serve its visitors, food and drinks are delivered directly to the room. In addition to accommodation, you can highlight the possibility of transfer from anywhere in the city to the hotel, as well as the possibility of transportation directly from the airport to the hotel as additional conditions. If you do not have time or forgot to exchange currency in your hometown, the hotel can provide an exchanger service and change money in accordance with the exchange rate. As a pleasant addition to all hotel services, one can highlight the possibility of using a left-luggage office for your expensive and important things. If you have a small child who needs endless attention, then you can use the nanny service that works at the hotel Garcia Resort Spa 5 *. Marmaris, Fethiye, Belek - the administration of hotels in these cities always tries to make the vacationer the most pleasure from staying in them.

Sports and recreation

Booking a room at the five-star Garcia Resort Spa, you can not only relax in comfortable rooms, but also take advantage of various services that provide the opportunity to play various sports games. Fans of board games will be able to test their skills in billiards, darts or table tennis. For those people who prefer more water and wellness treatments, there is a water park, spa and sauna. People who want to maintain their physical fitness or even improve it will be able to take advantage of the opportunity to visit the fitness room, which is located on site. In addition, there is always the possibility of sending straight to Oludeniz beach to experience the charm of the Mediterranean Sea. Favorable is the fact that this beach is located just a couple of kilometers from the hotel, and in order to get to it, you will need no more than half an hour leisurely walk.



Food & Beverage

The hotel has enough facilities to eat well. If you do not want to leave your own room in order to have breakfast or lunch, you can always order the dish you are interested in. Hotel staff will try to quickly fulfill the order. If there is no need for breakfast in your room, you can head down to the restaurant and eat at the buffet. A buffet is served every morning. In the evening, you can have dinner at the same restaurant, but if you are a lover of alcoholic beverages, then you can go to the bar. In addition, there is always the opportunity to eat somewhere outside the hotel. For example, in Oludeniz, small restaurants are very popular where they cook fish dishes. There is also the opportunity to dine with fish dishes at a special floating restaurant that will give your evening a little spark of romanticism.



Hotel rooms

The hotel has all the amenities to attract the largest number of visitors. At Garcia Resort Spa you can order one of 151 rooms, each of which has air conditioning and free Wi-Fi. At the visitor's choice there are three room options: standard with a minimum number of bonuses, deluxe and luxury. Having ordered a place in the Garcia Resort Spa 5 * Standard, you can enter a comfortable room with a balcony that faces south and provides the opportunity to contemplate all the stunning nature of the town of Oludeniz and its beach. In addition, each room has a large LCD TV with the ability to watch cable TV. The diagonal of each TV is 32 inches. Depending on your living conditions, you can use the free safe-deposit box services to store the most important things that you brought with you. There is also the possibility of using a minibar, which is in every room of the hotel. The rooms of the hotel also provide the presence of combined bathtubs and showers, you can use hair dryers, which are in each room. Housekeeping is carried out every day during the absence of guests on site.



Hotel Room Rates

Hotel room rates are very diplomatic for five star hotels. If you are interested in the standard room Garcia Resort Spa 5 * (Fethiye), the reviews of which are good enough, you will need to pay an amount of $ 235 for a double room with one bed. A deluxe room for two will cost a little more and will cost the visitor $ 246. If you decide to go to Oludeniz not two, but three together, then there is the opportunity to settle in a standard room for three people. In this case, you will need to pay an amount of $ 350 for the room. Suite is the most expensive option at the Garcia Resort Spa. The cost of one such room is a little more than 400 US dollars. Where to go for a walk

Of course, few visitors will want to spend all their time relaxing in the Garcia Resort Spa. Despite the fact that the town of Oludeniz is not large in size, there are many interesting things to see and visit. One of them is, undoubtedly, the resort's beach, which was included in the list of ten best and safest beaches around the world according to Time. Another entertainment that you can entertain yourself is diving. Having sunk to the depths and reaching the bottom of the Mediterranean Sea, you can see all the beauty of the underwater world that envelops the glorious town. If sea adventures do not inspire you, then you can ride in a balloon. And in October there is an opportunity to get to the famous air show. People who do not like extreme sports can be offered a walk in the valley of butterflies. Such an excursion can cost $ 15.



Hotel reviews

Like any other place in Turkey, you can hear a lot of positive and negative reviews from visitors to the five-star Garcia Resort Spa. Among the positive aspects, the work of the personnel stands out, who works at a very high level, trying as soon as possible to check in the visitor in accordance with all his requirements and preferences. Food and drinks are also highlighted as a positive feature. Visitors acknowledge that the minibar was not always full on time, but the quality of the food never caused any complaints. The main disadvantage noted by tourists is the remoteness of the beach from the hotel. According to them, it is not at a distance of 3 kilometers, but a little more. But the solution can be a car rental.

In general, every reader can draw their conclusions about the hotel Garcia Resort Spa 5 * (Turkey). Guest reviews about the hotel are subjective and can hardly be considered undeniably true. Therefore, decide for yourself whether to go to this hotel or not.
