Guest House "Manor Kutler" (Sokolinoe, Crimea): description, reviews

Spend a vacation surrounded by the majestic Crimean mountains, enjoy the crystal clear water of mountain lakes and waterfalls, retire in ancient caves, go fishing away from everyday city bustle, for example, in the estate of Kutler in Sokolin. What could be better for people spending a whole year at work in a dusty stuffy city, surrounded by skyscrapers and cars?



To Crimea today

Holidays in the Crimea for many are associated with crowds of people on the beaches and transport problems. Indeed, direct trains do not go to Crimea at the moment, but the ferry crossing the Kerch Strait will certainly become part of an exciting journey, especially for children. A comfortable ferry room with comfortable soft sofas, a bar and huge windows that offer magnificent views of the Crimean sea expanses will present a romantic hour of approaching the mysterious land. Moreover, now without problems it is possible to purchase single tickets Moscow - Crimea, including travel by train, bus and ferry.

There are options

But as for the large number of people who reached out to Crimea after its accession to Russia, this can be avoided. Crimea is not only the sea. If you prefer a quiet secluded vacation away from noisy companies and large cities, you will like a place called Sokolinoye in Crimea. The sea is far enough from it, but you can also visit the Yalta Beach if you wish. Fortunately, the prices for holidays in Crimea are not the most serious among Russian sea resorts.



In this article we will take a closer look at one of the most popular hotels in the village of Sokolinoye. Mountains, lakes, waterfalls, canyons, horseback riding, hospitable hosts, home cooking and much more awaits you in a wonderful place called "Kutler Manor".

Guest House "Manor Kutler"

The family-run guest house "Kutler Manor" is located on the edge of the forest, at the foot of Ai-Petri Mountain in the picturesque Belbek Valley. This place, remote from large settlements, is perfect for a quiet family vacation on the outskirts of the village of Sokolinoye in the Bakhchisarai district.

This place is accessible to many, but only those who want to enjoy the genuine beauty of the Crimean mountains and relax in silence, away from city noise, visit it. Due to its location in the Crimea, Sokolinoye can please guests with the splendor of untouched nature, rich history and enchanting mysticism.



The estate is 34 km from Yalta beaches. Nearby major cities - Sevastopol and Simferopol, Bakhchisaray, Yalta. Simferopol Airport is approximately 70 km away.

Comfort and hospitality

The hotel has a real friendly atmosphere, there are no casual guests, everyone who came here once will become a long-awaited guest and friend of the estate owners. It offers comfortable rooms with chic mountain views, free Wi-Fi and air conditioning. The hotel offers many excursions to nearby attractions for all tastes.

Near the Kutler Hotel in Sokolin there is a deciduous forest. Walking along the forest paths, you can come across the old orchards. Near the estate there is a mountain lake, on the shore of which you can just have a good time or go fishing in the Crimean mountains.

Excursions, sights

The guests of the estate are offered a variety of excursions, among which everyone will find suitable in duration and comfort. Near the hotel there is a beautiful Silver Stream waterfall and the famous youth baths in the Grand Canyon.

In the Belbek Valley, in which the hotel is located, begins the Grand Crimean Canyon. In its central part is the Blue Lake, which is famous for its clear water and beautiful scenery. You can swim in it - the water in the lake is +10 degrees.

It is noteworthy that the Grand Crimean Canyon was formed almost 2 million years ago in the limestone layers of the soil under the influence of water erosion. It originates 5 kilometers from the village of Sokolinoe. Its depth in some places reaches 320 m, and its length is about 3.5 km.

17 km of mountain road lead to the top of Ai-Petri Mountain. Breathtaking serpentine and stunning views will make the trip to the mountain easy and interesting.

Hippotherapy and air baths

One of the services of the estate is horse riding through the ancient Crimean forests to the nearest attractions. For example, to Syuyrensky fortress and monastery Chelter-Koba with a unique yew grove. Of the natural attractions, it is worth noting the Kokkozka River, which originates in Sokolin, and the small grotto of Danilich-Koba.



Of the historical sights, the hunting house of Prince Yusupov is most popular. It was built in 1908 at the request of the prince's mother. And you can get to the Grand Canyon along the Yusupov trail, it is easy and comfortable to go along it, it is difficult to get lost, as there are special marks throughout the trail.

Eagle Flight and Boar Pass

Another interesting place that the estate workers advise to visit is the Eagle Flight plateau. The plateau got such a telling name because it was chosen by these graceful predators. Getting to the plateau from the village of Sokolinoe is very easy, they are only 2 km away.

You can enjoy the picturesque views and the mountain serpentine after breaking the Bicske pass. Its other name is Kabaniy Pass, opinions about the origin of this name differ. Some argue that wild boars live in these places, in whose honor the pass was named, and someone compares a mountain road with a pig's tail. In the old days, caravans, merchants, and merchants traveling from the Principality of Theodoro to the Baydar Valley and back used this road.

To the east of the village is the mystical mountain range Boyko, which travelers called the Crimean Shambhala for the strange things that are happening here. It is more difficult to get to it than to the rest of the sights, the road leading to the mountain runs through the Grand Canyon, and therefore only submits to avid tourists. But not very prepared travelers can visit the Karagol canyon: 5 km along an easy-to-walk trail, and you will find yourself in a completely different world with a fantastic landscape.



A large military base is located on Mount Beden-Kir, whose buildings resemble an observatory rather like eggs of a huge bird, which made a nest in a quiet comfortable place. These modern buildings successfully fit into the landscape and dilute the natural beauty, they should certainly be seen by connoisseurs of modern architecture.
