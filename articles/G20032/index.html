Location

Hotel "Asteria" (the city of St. Petersburg) is located near Nevsky Prospekt, in the historical center, on Fontanka River Embankment Street . Leaving the hotel, you immediately immerse yourself in the era of St. Petersburg of the tsarist times - nearby are the houses of the Olenins, Pashkovs, the Imperial House of Law and the Sheremetev Palace.

Walking to Nevsky Prospekt, where there are many famous sights of the city, will take you no more than ten minutes. A little further there are the Admiralty, Palace Square and the Hermitage - places that must be visited for each guest of St. Petersburg.

For those who want to plunge into the theatrical life of the city, it will be convenient that the legendary Alexandrinsky and Mariinsky theaters are nearby, as well as the equally stunning Tovstonogov Bolshoi Theater.

Number of rooms

Hotel "Asteria" (St. Petersburg) offers you seventy-two comfortable rooms, decorated in a unique and refined style of this city. All of them are divided into seven categories: single and double "standard", superior rooms, "luxury", as well as three Suites - Apraksin, Fontanka and Love.





The view from the windows of the rooms can be of three options - to the embankment of the Fontanka River, to the courtyard, and for those who settle on the upper floors - to the breathtaking panorama of St. Petersburg.

In each room, guests are offered a bed, a table, a wardrobe, a TV, cable TV, a seating area, a radio alarm clock, a telephone, a minibar and air conditioning - all that is needed for a comfortable stay. For those who want to always stay in touch, free high speed internet is available.

All rooms have a private bathroom with a shower, bath or Jacuzzi, hairdryer, towels, slippers and hygiene items.

Nutrition

"Asteria" is a hotel (St. Petersburg), which will delight guests with delicious dishes. On the ground floor there is a cozy restaurant with magnificent views of the river and the historic city center. The restaurant is divided into two zones: White and Guest rooms.

In the White Hall are celebrations: weddings, receptions, banquets. Guests are given a great opportunity to celebrate their holiday in one of the most delightful places of the city, made in the style of the Petrine era.





Guests can have breakfast in the Guest Room in the morning. Breakfast is buffet style, but you can order food with you. It is also possible to include lunch and dinner in the restaurant. Here you will taste dishes of European and Russian cuisines, skillfully prepared by the chef, and drinks selected from the cocktail and wine lists. The restaurant administration also remembers the little guests - therefore, a special menu is available for children, and if desired, children's parties can be organized with professional animators.

The hotel also has a bar and buffet serving drinks and light snacks.

Hotel "Asteria" (St. Petersburg) - how to get

The hotel is located at 71 Fontanka River Embankment. You can get to it by reaching the Sennaya or Pushkinskaya metro stations. In the first case, the road to the hotel will take no more than seven minutes, in the second - about ten. The metro at Moskovsky Station is just two stops from the Pushkinskaya metro station.

If you have too many things and want to get to the hotel with comfort, you can pre-order a transfer. Please note that this service is paid.

Terms of accommodation

Hotel "Asteria" (St. Petersburg), the photo of which you see below, follows the rules adopted in European hotels: check-in is at 14:00, check-out is at 12:00. Room reservation is made on the official website of the hotel or on intermediary sites. You can pay there by bank transfer or upon arrival at the reception. The hotel accepts both cash and Visa and Master Card.





Cots are provided free of charge for children, and adults can also be accommodated in an extra bed. The cost of this service will be one thousand one hundred rubles. One room can accommodate no more than one extra bed.

All rooms are non-smoking, as smoking is prohibited on site. The building has smoke detectors. Small pets are allowed, but prior arrangement with the hotel is required. If you cancel your reservation no later than two days before the expected date of arrival, no fee will be charged. If you decide not to arrive at the hotel later, a penalty will be charged in the amount of the total cost of the stay. You should pay attention to the individual booking rules in each case.
