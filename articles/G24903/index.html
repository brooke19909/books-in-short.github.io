Mouline baubles for beginners - not as hard as it sounds!



When you want something bright and unusual, different hand-made ideas come to the rescue. How many beautiful things you can do with your own hands! For example, baubles made of mouline thread for beginners are an excellent option even for those who do not have the slightest skills in the art of weaving bracelets. It will take you just a few minutes to master the technology, and with it you can already create real masterpieces.

How to weave baubles from a floss for beginners?

Bracelets made of threads were born a very long time ago, and became most popular during the heyday of the hippies. And this is not surprising: weaving them is very simple, you can do it both at home and on the street.

Baubles made of mouline thread for beginners seem to be something complicated, however, the only thing that needs to be mastered is the way to knot correctly. But first you need to tie all the threads together. Here is the procedure:

  1. Choose any three colors.
  2. Each will need two threads (their length should be about a meter).
  3. Putting them together and stepping back from the edge of 10 cm, tie a strong knot.
  4. Use tape to attach a loose 10-centimeter tip to a table or any other surface.


So, the first stage is over. Now you are finally convinced how easy it is to weave baubles out of a floss. For beginners we explain in great detail. Next stage:

  1. Separate 2 threads of the same color from the rest.
  2. First make sure that they are not twisted together.
  3. Now take the right thread in your left hand, and the left thread in your right. At the same time, the hands should be crossed among themselves (the right one is on top).
  4. Now put the left thread over the right one so that a small space forms between them. Strictly follow the instructions, and then baubles made of floss (for beginners) will quickly obey you, and in the future you will be able to weave them with your eyes closed.
  5. Next, thread the same thread from the bottom up into the resulting space.
  6. Take it by the tip and begin to pull it up from you. As you may have noticed, all this time the thread held by the left hand remains motionless and stretched. It is important to ensure that the nodule is correct.
  7. Have you done? And now, without letting go of the thread from your left hand, braid it again - just like the previous time.

You can be congratulated: you have mastered the basics of fenkoplentva! It is such double knots that are the basis of all such bracelets. And this concerns not only oblique, but also more complex direct weaving.

What to do next? Your following actions:

  1. The thread you hold in your left hand can be released. Take the next one, a different color, and double braid it. Use the same thread as the last time.
