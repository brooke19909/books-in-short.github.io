Picturesque places

Borovoe Lake is one of the most famous sources from the group of reservoirs, united by one name. This complex is located in the eastern part of the Kokchetav Upland. There is clear and clean water: the bottom is visible even at great depths. Lakes, surrounded by pine and birch green areas, purify the air and create certain climatic conditions.

Offers for vacationers

Rocky mountains, clean air, pine forests and pristine lakes are the criteria that will help anyone who comes here to relax and gain strength. For active pastime, horseback riding, catamarans, hiking with climbing elements to local peaks are offered. For others, it will be more interesting to lie down on the beach during the day, swimming and sunbathing, and in the evening to sit in pleasant company by the fire.

The programs of some resorts cover not only medical procedures, but also horseback riding and skiing in winter. One of the important advantages is the fact that thanks to coniferous forests there are absolutely no mosquitoes on the territory. Steppes, ponds and forests - this is what visitors can see when they visit the territory where Borovoe Lake is located.





Infrastructure

A visit to the lake can be either spontaneous or pre-thought out. Thanks to the developed infrastructure, everyone can find a place that suits their tastes and requirements. On the territory of the lakes there are many private houses offering rental for tourists, there are many hotels and resorts. But the number of places available for accommodation will depend on the season. Enjoy the views, breathe fresh air or improve your health - that's what people come to Lake Borovoye for. Reviews about this place are only the most positive.

The healing properties of the reservoir

The history of the establishment of sanatoriums begins at the beginning of the XX century, when in 1910 the first kumysolechnik was opened. In the following years, many travelers came to Borovoe Lake in order to breathe fresh air and take sun baths. It is also known that the water and clay of the local salt pond Maybalyk have healing properties.

The composition of the water includes many useful elements, so some resorts located near this lake offer a similar service. For example, the Shchuchinsky sanatorium works in a similar way, which was reopened after the Great Patriotic War. The recreation center is popular not only among local residents, but is also popular with foreign tourists. The treatment program includes the use of koumiss - mare‚Äôs milk, which, combined with a calm and measured life in the paradise of Borovoe Lake, has a beneficial effect on the treatment of diseases of the nervous system and heart.





Acquaintance with wildlife

The government of Kazakhstan does not leave without due attention the preservation of pristine nature and in every way protects Lake Borovoye from pollution. For example, in the woods you can meet roaming roe deer, as well as enjoy cranberries and cranberries. And for those who were not lucky enough to meet animals in their native environment, most hotels and resorts on their territory have small zoological gardens in which wild animals live. In them you can see wild boars, deer, llamas, roe deers and eagles. And in order to get acquainted with the world of animals living in the local territory, you can visit the Museum of Nature, located in the village.

Fishing

An interesting vacation will be for avid fishing rod lovers. The water here is clean, and crayfish live in the lake, which indicates a wonderful ecology. In reservoirs there are a huge number of different types of fish, namely crucian carp, pike, perch, carps and carp. In order to enjoy beautiful fishing, people from all over the world come to the pond.





How to get to Lake Borovoe?

There are several ways to get to the reservoir:

  • By bus from Omsk. Transport runs daily, takes about 11 hours in time.
  • By train from Yekaterinburg. Route: Sverdlovsk-Alma-Ata. Rides through Schuchinsk. After that you can transfer by bus or taxi and get to the nearest sanatorium.
  • By private car. In this case, there may be delays at customs due to regular buses.
  • By train with a change. First you get to Petropavlovsk, then to Schuchinsk. Then you can take a taxi or get to the desired place by bus.

The simplest and less time-consuming method is to purchase a ticket through a travel agency. The only thing a traveler will have to face is the waste of time in choosing the right option. You will need to look through all the methods offered by the company and select the suitable one. Thus, you can visit Borovoe Lake without any problems.
