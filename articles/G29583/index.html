Standard procedure

Prior to the release of iPhone 7, all Apple mobile devices had two buttons: Home and Power. The instructions below will help you hard reset the iPhone 6 and older devices. The user will need to simultaneously press the power and power buttons, holding them for 10 seconds.

The user will see a slider with the words "Turn off", which must be flicked. After turning off the device, wait a couple of minutes and turn it on again. As a result of a hard reboot of the iPhone, the user will eliminate most of the failures that will never make themselves felt. This method will be effective even if the device does not respond to pressing hardware buttons.

How to perform a hard reset of "iPhone 7"?

Due to the functional change in the capabilities of the Home button, Hard Reset is performed differently. In this device, the Home key has ceased to be mechanical. Therefore, the above method with holding the "Home" button will be useless.

To perform a hard reset, you will need to hold down the Volume Down and Power buttons. The keys should be held until the screen goes blank and the Apple logo appears . As you can see, the manipulation is almost the same as in older models of mobile devices.

Restart for iPhone 8

The company has introduced various functional, technical and conceptual changes to the new line of smartphones. Developers are changing the usual options for access to a hard reset of "iPhone 8".

Owners of new smartphones can forget about the usual retention of buttons. The user should press and slightly hold his finger on the volume up button. At the same time, you need to hold the volume down button and the Power key. After the familiar logo appears, the buttons can be released.

Not all users manage to carry out this procedure the first time. However, mastery in this matter will come with experience, so do not worry. It is not recommended to depart from the indicated sequence of actions. Otherwise, the smartphone does not recognize the signal and is activated in standard mode.

How to reboot without a lock button?

Any mechanical damage that occurs as a result of a fall can lead to damage to the device. As a result, the user is at a loss in how to hard reset the iPhone if the hardware lock button is out of order.

You can use the Assistive Touch function. To do this, you need to go to the "Settings" section and tap on the "Basic" item. Then click on the "Universal Access" section and activate the Assistive Touch option. A soft button will appear on the screen, by which you should tap and select the "Device" item. The system will open a menu in which you need to click on the "Screen lock" item . After that, you can turn off the phone.

Additional way

The user can wait until the battery is completely discharged. This option involves hard resetting the iPhone without using hardware buttons. The gadget owner does not need to do anything other than sit and wait for the battery to fully discharge and the smartphone will turn off.

In order to speed up the process, you can turn on any video or program. Thus, the battery can drain in just a couple of hours. Then, the user simply needs to connect the mobile device to the charger. The method is not widespread, however, it has revenge to be.
