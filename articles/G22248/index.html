How to sew the sides in a crib with your own hands

To protect the newborn baby from many problems that may arise during sleep, you need to properly equip the berth of the crumbs. The best option will be a high-quality mattress and the presence of sides on the walls of the cradle. If a good mattress with orthopedic characteristics can only be purchased, then the second element of bedding is easy to sew on your own. There are many options thanks to which you can understand how to sew the sides qualitatively and correctly. It is worth paying attention to the selection of quality materials and the correct design of the product.

What are the sides in a crib for? What characteristics should they have?

The sides for the crib of a newborn baby is not a decoration, but a necessity. This type of protection ensures the safety of the baby during sleep - the crumbs will not be able to hit the solid walls of furniture. Additionally, soft bedding elements protect against drafts or hypothermia. The decorative function is also not excluded.

Before stitching the sides, it is worth asking about what the elements should be:

  • Covers should be made of high-quality fabric, which will not fade, roll or divide into fibers during operation.
  • The filler must not be toxic. Natural materials are not welcome, as they can cause allergic reactions.
  • Elements should be soft, appropriate size, securely attached to the walls of the crib.

There are other criteria for assessing the quality of the sides, determined by individual preferences and requirements. It is worth checking that all elements are well fixed, otherwise the child will be able to tear or pull a trifle in his mouth.

Each option has its own characteristics regarding the design and arrangement. The more complicated the pattern, the more interesting and elegant the whole structure will look. Additional decorative elements and inserts can become a relevant addition even to the simplest product design.

Features of the choice of material for the manufacture of the cover, the inner part and fasteners

The health of the crumbs depends on the quality of the raw materials, so when buying materials it is worth paying attention to some points. As separate components, it is necessary to consider a filler, a cover for the sides and fasteners.

  • A good filler is foam rubber. It keeps its shape well, does not get off during operation and washing. But it can retain excess moisture, which can cause the development of pathogenic microflora.
  • Hollofiber will be an excellent alternative to synthetic winterizer and especially cotton wool. Soft, does not stray, is not the basis for the development of bacteria, is easy to care for. Over time, it can stray into breasts, which will have to be separated manually.
  • More expensive alternatives to holofiber are: periotek, holkon, polyester. Each option has exceptionally positive characteristics, one of which is hypoallergenic.

Covers should be made from natural fabrics with a soft base. You can use high-quality synthetic fiber fabrics by all criteria. The best way to fix it is ties that connect the elements together and with the walls of the cradle. Only after selecting good materials to do the job, you can decide how to sew the sides safe and practical in terms of use for a certain type of crib.

How to start work in the manufacture of sides

To get started, you need to study the instructions for the manufacture of this device to limit the walls of the crib. The algorithm for manufacturing the sides is quite simple. You can sew the sides to the crib with your own hands, if you follow several steps:

  1. Prepare suitable tools and materials.
  2. Take measurements from the crib, create a sketch sketch.
  3. Create patterns and pattern for the element.
  4. Basting, and then sew the pattern.
  5. If necessary, decorate.
  6. Wash and stroke.

Each stage takes time to complete, then the product will be neat. Pay special attention to calculations that relate to parameters. It is worth considering that a lot of fabric goes to the hem of the edges.

What materials may be needed to make the simplest sides

The simplest in design are flat sides. One piece completely covers one wall of the cradle, so the production takes a minimum of time. For the manufacture it is worth preparing the following materials:

  • It is preferable for this type of structure to choose foam rubber as a filler.
  • The fabric from which the covers will be made. The amount depends on the parameters of the crib.
  • Tight ribbons that will become ties. It is worth preparing about 10 meters.
  • Sewing threads that match the color of the fabric covers.

It is necessary to prepare tools that are useful in the work. This set is standard for the manufacture of any type of skirting. You will need scissors, a sewing needle, a measuring tape, and chalk. In addition, it is worth taking an iron and preparing a place of work, removing excess objects from the surface of the table.

Features of the manufacture of items to soften the walls of furniture

When the tools and materials are prepared, the measurements are taken from the cradle and the sketch is prepared, you can begin to work.

The algorithm for how to sew the sides in the crib for newborns:

  1. Using the measuring tape, transfer the wall parameters to the fabric. Use the chalk to draw the borders of each element.
  2. Scissors cut the fabric. Cut the same parts out of foam. The dimensions of the filler should be 5-7 cm smaller than the tissue segments.
  3. Bend the edges by 2-3 cm and iron, fixing the position of the hem. Bend the edges again by 2-3 cm and iron the edges again.
  4. Fasten smooth edges with a basting seam so that they do not separate during the final stitching process.
  5. Rate the resulting product. You can correct irregularities or other errors.
  6. Prepare tapes. Cut the material into strips 25-35 cm long, process the edges.
  7. Sew the sides to the crib with your own hands according to the pattern. You can use a sewing machine. Fix the tapes in the corners.

It is enough to wash and iron the finished product. In the manufacturing process, it is necessary to make periodic fittings in order to understand whether the parameters of the sides correspond exactly to the values ‚Äã‚Äãof the walls.

Aspects of making pillow fences at home

Sewing the sides for a newborn‚Äôs crib with their own hands in the form of small pillows is as simple as flat products. The principle of manufacturing pillows:

  1. Make patterns for pillows. Each detail should be a square. It is necessary to make an equal number of parts from each type of fabric.
  2. Tighten edges and smooth. Perform the same action again.
  3. Make bastings by flashing the edges.
  4. Cut strips from the fabric and stitch them to form ribbons. Thanks to these elements, the pillows will be attached to the battens of the walls.
  5. Sew parts on three sides on a sewing machine. Sew textile strips into the corners.
  6. Insert filler into the workpiece, distributing it evenly over the entire plane. Sew the fourth side.

The product is ready to use. You can tie each pillow to the rods, or you can fix a strip of fabric by pulling it between the corners of the walls. All pillows will be tied to this element.
