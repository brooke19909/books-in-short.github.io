Cretan Malia Park Resort (Greece, Crete): overview, description and reviews

Cretan Malia Park is a hotel that is very popular with visitors. People from different countries go on vacation here. Russia, of course, is no exception. Well, you should tell in detail about the advantages of this complex.





Location

Cretan Malia Park is located in the resort town, which is located in the northernmost part of the Cretan island. And he is called Malia. The city is located 30 kilometers from Heraklion. There is the nearest airport. About the same distance from Malia is Hersonissos. This is where people come who want to plunge headlong into the vibrant nightlife of Crete. Malia itself was once the town of the Minoan ancient state. And now it is a popular resort.

The hotel is located 1.5 kilometers from its center. Right on the beach. Which, by the way, has been awarded the EU Blue Flag for more than ten years in a row.

The hotel itself can be seen from afar. The whole complex seems to be surrounded by greenery. By the way, this is one of the most pleasant "highlights" of the hotel - its park area. The hotel itself was built in 1989, but since then it has undergone two repairs. The latter ended a few years ago. Therefore, in the apartments and in the hotel itself, everything looks very beautiful and modern.

In Cretan Malia Park, in addition to the pool, which is in the territory of each hotel, there are conditions for various entertainments.
