Hotel Pefkos Garden Hotel 3 * (Pefkos, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

Greece is an amazing country that attracts tourists with its clear sea, sandy beaches and a huge number of cultural monuments. At the same time, rest here is relatively inexpensive. People who want to save money usually settle in the Pefkos Garden complex (Rhodes). It offers comfortable accommodation in a picturesque location at low prices. But is it worth choosing it as a place to relax?

General Hotel Information

The construction of the complex was completed in 1993. It consists of several white two-story buildings decorated with arches. All are located in a lush garden, in which there are numerous trees, shrubs and flowers. Hotel Pefkos Garden Hotel 3 * offers 93 modest but comfortable rooms for tourists. Settlement in them begins no earlier than 14:00 and lasts until midnight. The hotel can accommodate children of all ages. Moreover, adolescents under 16 are given good discounts on vacation. Tourists can also bring animals with them. But it is worth notifying the hotel in advance. Eviction starts at 12:00. For a small fee, you can extend your stay for several hours if the complex has free rooms.





The hotel staff speaks many languages, including English and Russian. We accept Visa, MasterCard and Maestro credit cards. The last restoration of all available rooms in the hotel was carried out in 2005. Also in 2010, a second floor was completed at the hotel near some of the buildings, where several new rooms appeared.

Location of the complex and nearby attractions

The hotel is located near the center of the resort village of Pefkos, in whose honor it was named. A few decades ago, only a fishing village was built here, and now this place is considered one of the best for relaxing on the island of Rhodes. Hotel Pefkos Garden Hotel 3 * (Greece) is approximately 45 km from the island's capital. It takes about an hour to get to the city by car. The nearest town, Lindos, is only 3.5 km away. The property is located 250 meters from the sandy beach. The sand here is not perfectly clean, sometimes small stones and pebbles are found. Coming to rest here, you should definitely visit the following attractions:

  • St. Paul's Bay (3.5 km);
  • the old streets and acropolis of Lindos (3.7 km);
  • Church of the Virgin (3.7 km);
  • Vlycha beach (3.8 km).

It is also worth highlighting a day on a trip to the city of Rhodes. Here are the largest architectural monuments on the island. It is worth visiting the Palace of the Grand Masters, Cape Prasonisi, Strongili Island and the ancient antique acropolis.

Housing stock

The hotel offers tourists modest but comfortable rooms. Most of them are designed for a small family of two to three people. The classic design is combined with exquisite decor elements that create an atmosphere of home comfort. The walls are painted in pastel colors and the floor is tiled with ceramic. The furniture is made of light wood. Linen and curtains, on the contrary, are sewn from bright fabrics that will give guests a charge of positive mood in the morning. All apartments in Pefkos Garden Hotel 3 * (Greece, Rhodes) have a private balcony or terrace. The rooms have beds, a desk, several chairs, a large mirror, wardrobes and bedside tables. The room is decorated with fresh flowers growing in the garden, and fruit vases.





The complex has the following types of apartments:

  • Standart room. It consists of a room and a bathroom. There are two berths. An extra rollaway bed can be delivered upon request. The area is 17 square meters. m. Windows and a balcony overlook the sea, garden or pool.
  • This room has bunk beds. It differs from previous apartments only in the presence of an extra bunk bed. The room can accommodate 3-4 people at a time.

Each room is cleaned daily by the hotel. Towels are changed several times a week in the bathroom. Bed linen is changed less often, on average, once every five days.

More about room equipment

Any guest staying at the Pefkos Garden Hotel 3 * (Rhodes) will be able to enjoy a cozy and comfortable atmosphere in the room. It is achieved through the following amenities:

  • Individual air conditioning that maintains the optimum temperature for sleeping even in the hottest weather. They are charged a separate fee (7 euros per day).
  • Safe for storing documents, money, mobile phones and other things. Provided for a fee.
  • A flat-screen TV with satellite channels. Among them there are several Russians.
  • Telephone. The receptionist can also wake up tourists in the morning by calling the room.
  • A small refrigerator where you can leave food, fruits or drinks.
  • The rooms provide paid Wi-Fi.
  • Bathroom with shower, washbasin, slippers, bathrobes and toiletries. A hairdryer can be delivered to the room upon request.

Food concept, restaurants and bars.

When making a voucher, tourists can choose one of several food options that the Pefkos Garden Hotel 3 * (Rhodes) offers. Guest reviews are controversial. Many of them note that the food here is monotonous, so they quickly get bored. Guests can choose the all-inclusive system or half board, which provides only breakfast and dinner. The menu includes European and Greek cuisine. For tourists there are several catering establishments in the hotel:

  • Central restaurant. All the main meals are held here. Meals are served at the buffet. For lunch and dinner, guests are offered local wine, beer, soft drinks.
  • Snack bar located by the pool. Here tourists can have a bite to eat, ordering a sandwich, ice cream, fresh pastries. Served with mineral water, juices, as well as instant coffee and tea.




Hotel facilities

High quality service is achieved through a developed network of infrastructure facilities. For tourists who stayed in one of the buildings of Pefkos Garden Hotel 3 *, thoughtful service is provided. It includes the following services:

  • A pay phone located in the complex. With it, you can make calls to other countries. You can use it for an additional fee.
  • Free wifi in all public areas. There is a paid internet corner providing high-speed access.
  • Reception in the main building, working around the clock. The administrator will help to solve the problems at any time of the day or night.
  • A common living room with a TV, where tourists can wait for their check-in or just relax.
  • Small orthodox church. Religious guests of Pefkos Garden Hotel 3 * (Rhodes) can visit it for free at any time.
  • Express check-in upon check-in or check-out. Available after paying a small amount to the hotel administrator.
  • Private parking. Car rental available.
  • Currency exchange.
  • Paid safe at reception.
  • Fax and photocopy services.
  • Laundry, where for a small fee the staff will clean, wash and glance the guests' clothes and shoes.




Entertainment program for vacationers

Leisure organizations pay no less attention than the services provided. For guests of the hotel Pefkos Garden Hotel 3 * a good selection of entertainment is offered. Tourists can relax by visiting the following entertainment sites:

  • Two outdoor fresh water pools. They work from the beginning of June until the end of September. There is a sunbathing area nearby. Guests can take umbrellas and sun loungers for free.
  • Private beach 250 meters from the hotel. Vacationers can play volleyball and water sports.
  • Beauty salon where wellness and beauty treatments for face and body are performed. There is also a massage room for tourists. All services are provided at an additional cost.
  • Equipment for playing darts.
  • Karaoke. Travelers can not only perform songs for pleasure, but also participate in a karaoke contest with other hotel guests.
  • Entertainment programs for adults and children. Events are regularly held to introduce tourists to Greek culture.
  • Hall for computer games.
  • Playground and equipment for table tennis.
  • Billiard room.




Organization of children's leisure

A huge and quiet garden provides for a relaxing holiday with children. Hotel Pefkos Garden Hotel 3 * offers good conditions for their stay. Kids under two years old can relax for free. In addition, they will be given a baby cradle. It is only necessary to make a request about its necessity when issuing a ticket. If a child under 12 years old will be accommodated in existing bedding, a discount will also be provided. The central restaurant for children has special chairs for feeding.

Entertainment at the hotel is not too diverse, but include everything you need for a good rest. For children there is their own shallow pool, in which they will swim under the supervision of experienced lifeguards. There is also a playground in the fresh air with slides, swings, a sandbox and a trampoline. A mini-club also offers its services, which can be attended by children from 4 to 12 years old. And for the baby you can call the nanny.





Average cost of living

The cost of rest in the tourist complex Pefkos Garden Hotel 3 * depends on the time of vacation. The package will be more expensive if you go here at the height of the summer season. It is much cheaper to stay here in early June or September. The weather at this time is warm, but the heat is gone. And in the sea you can swim only during the day. The cheapest standard rooms will cost tourists an average of 2500 rubles per night (2016 prices). Thus, a week-long stay at the hotel will cost 17,500 rubles for two. A slightly more expensive package will be issued, which includes an all-inclusive power system.

The price of more spacious rooms is much higher. The maximum cost of living in the best apartments of the complex will cost about 7000 rubles per night. For a week of rest, tourists will have to part with 50,000 rubles. Given that such a room accommodates four people, the average tour will cost about 12,500 rubles per person.

What did tourists like about the hotel?

Unfortunately, it cannot be said that travelers staying at the Pefkos Garden Hotel 3 * (Rhodes) complex leave only positive reviews. In their opinion, the hotel has many pros and cons. First, we list the main advantages:

  • The hotel rooms offer beautiful views: sea, pine park and mountains.
  • The area is very clean and well maintained. The air smells of pine trees growing nearby.
  • Good location. Very close to the beach, as well as a bus stop.
  • Tasty and satisfying breakfasts and dinners, but not too much choice of dishes.
  • The clear blue sea with a convenient entrance. Not small, well suited for swimming adults.




What did tourists dislike about the hotel?

Hotel Pefkos Garden Hotel 3 * reviews describing its shortcomings, gets quite often. Tourists note the following disadvantages of the hotel:

  • The rooms need repair and replacement of furniture. The bathroom is equipped with a tight shower. It will be difficult for people with a tight physique to wash themselves. Hot water is not always in the evenings.
  • The Internet only works at the reception. But even here its speed is very low.
  • Many flies and mosquitoes. Ants crawl in the rooms, sometimes cockroaches are also found.
  • There are very few fruits. They are given in batches. For example, a slice of watermelon, a few peaches or oranges.
  • The cleaning is very mediocre. The maids only brush off the beds and wipe the floor. Towels and bed linen are washed, sometimes they have sewn holes and stains.
  • Near the bar it is very noisy in the evenings.
  • Inadequate hostess of the hotel, which can easily cheat guests, scream at them. Solving problems with her is very difficult.
  • There are no elevators in the buildings, but there are quite a few stairs everywhere. It is not recommended to rest here for people with sore feet.

Conclusion

This is not to say that the hotel will be your ideal place to relax. Reviews of the Pefkos Garden Hotel 3 * are often negative, and some tourists are advised not to stay here. But the complex is suitable for those people who are looking for a very inexpensive vacation in Greece, for couples with children, as well as older people. But still, if you have extra money, then you should choose another hotel.
