Crete Hotels Open Doors

Crete is the largest and most southern Greek island, outlines similar to a flying dragon. Its length is 257 kilometers and its width is from thirteen to sixty kilometers. Relaxing on the island is like falling into a fairy tale, the cultures and climatic conditions of the countries of Asia, Africa and Europe are so intricately interwoven here, since Crete is located at the busiest crossroads of these three parts of the world.

A feature of Crete is that in spite of the large attendance, nature has been preserved here in its original form. In the North of the island there are a long strip of wide sandy beaches that are washed by a crystal-clear sea.

Some beaches are awarded with blue flags - symbols of cleanliness. Thanks to the warm currents, the swimming season begins here much earlier than in Turkey, which is located in the neighborhood. The western part of the island is buried in lush vegetation, here is a small lake and mountain rivers flow. If you add to all of the above even the hospitality and hospitality of local residents, you get the perfect place for a spa holiday.

Crete, whose hotels are distinguished by the highest level of service, are located on the resort island in the hundreds. Thousands of tourists come here on vacation annually, but the island is especially popular among Russians.
The reality in Crete is intertwined with mythology in such a way that it is impossible to understand which event is historical and which is mythological. Many legends and myths of ancient Greece are associated with the island of Crete. For tourists, Greece is interesting for its rich ancient culture. Crete hotels which almost all are built in the Greek style, not only allows you to feel the Greek atmosphere, but even feel like some kind of mythological character.
