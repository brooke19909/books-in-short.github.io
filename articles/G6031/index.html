Florence hotels: list, rating, room reservation, services, pricing, tips and reviews of visitors

Florence is a truly marvelous corner, which will be interesting for many Russians to visit. There are a lot of attractions, as well as simply beautiful places. Many travelers, choosing a tour to Florence, are often lost in determining the place to stay. That is why we will further consider which hotels in Florence (Italy) are the most popular and where the highest quality service is offered.

Palazzo niccolini al duomo

One of the most popular hotels in Florence is the Palazzo Niccolini al Duomo. This place to stay offers tourists excellent conditions of the luxury level. Speaking about these conditions, it should be noted that the average rating for this hotel on almost all tourist portals is 4.8-5 points out of five. You need to understand that this rating is assigned based on reviews left by the travelers themselves.

This hotel offers tourists interesting offers related to accommodation. Palazzo Niccolini al Duomo only has furniture made from high quality materials, as well as air conditioning and Wi-Fi. In addition to all this, the rooms offer excellent technical equipment in the form of TVs, corners for making hot drinks and a minibar.

All guests who arrive in Florence are delivered to their location by special transport allocated by the hotel as part of the transfer service.

Guests who relax here note the excellent location of the hotel, as well as the magnificent views of the Duomo from its windows.

Considering the pricing policy of Palazzo Niccolini al Duomo, it should be noted that the six-day rental of a hotel room is a little less than 40,000 rubles.

Martelli 6 Suite and apartments

According to travelers from different parts of Russia, Martelli 6 Suite and apartments is one of the best hotels in Florence. The rating of this hotel is usually more than 9 points out of 10 available (data from TopHotels, Booking).

The complex is located in the central part of the city, next to the world-famous Cathedral of Santa Maria del Fiore. Most of the apartments in this hotel‚Äôs room offer stunning views. In addition to the main furniture set, the apartments include air conditioning, a small refrigerator, and a plasma TV.

Many experienced tourists recommend this hotel for those who prefer to combine their stay with a trip to interesting places and delicious food. It is around Martelli 6 Suite and apartments that there are a lot of interesting sights, as well as restaurants and cafes.

The cost of daily rent of a room in the hotel in question is 7600 rubles, which most guests consider to be quite acceptable payment for the decent service that is performed here, as well as for comfortable living conditions.

San frediano mansion

Many tourists choose San Frediano Mansion only because this Florence hotel is located in the city center, among a huge number of attractions, as well as surrounded by entertainment venues and restaurants serving delicious food.

The special attention of tourists is drawn to the design of rooms in the hotel. Their interior is presented in a classic style, there are wrought iron beds, decorative fireplaces, lacquered furniture, as well as a real abundance of expensive and high-quality textiles. In addition, while staying here, guests can enjoy the wonderful technical equipment of the apartments, some of which have air conditioning, a plasma TV and a minibar with a safe.

The level of service in this place speaks for itself of what ratings the hotel has on various portals. For example, on the website Booking San Frediano Mansion, the complex was rated by tourists as 9 out of 10, which is the best indicator of the status of a place to stay.

Often, couples who are planning a romantic trip to Florence are recommended to make a trip here.

La corte dei principlesi

In the ranking of the best hotels in Florence (in the city center), the fourth place is occupied by the hotel with the romantic name La Corte Dei Principi. It is located within the historic city center, which provides easy access to the main attractions of the region. The peculiarity of this hotel is also that it is located in an old building, which is located next to the fortress of Fortezza da Basso.

All rooms are presented in the categories of comfort, standard and family. They are remarkably equipped from a technical point of view. There is also a set of furniture necessary for a comfortable stay of tourists of different ages.

La Corte Dei Principi provides pets in its apartments, but this must be done by prior arrangement.

The building has a luxurious restaurant, where every morning La Corte Dei Principi serves a buffet spread of Mediterranean and European dishes.

If we talk about the cost of living in the hotel in question, it should be noted that the daily rental of apartments is a little less than 6000 rubles. The rating of this place on various portals is 8-8.3 points out of 10, which is a pretty decent indicator.

Hotel ariele

The hotel in the Florence area - Hotel Ariele is very popular among tourists. It is here, judging by the reviews, it offers high-quality service, excellent service, as well as a luxurious interior in the apartments. It should be noted that this place has a high rating assigned as a result of consideration of reviews of tourists vacationing here. So, on the world-famous Booking portal, accommodation at Hotel Ariele was rated 8.2 out of 10.

Hotel Ariele is located in the center of a huge number of attractions. Here in the district there are also many restaurants, bars and cafes that will be interesting to visit for many gourmets.

As for the design of the rooms, it is made in a classic style, with the addition of expensive elements in the form of noble soft fabrics, lacquered wood and crystal lamps. The apartments at Hotel Ariele are equipped with excellent technical equipment, in which guests can use air conditioning, a minibar, a safe, a telephone and watch TV with satellite channels at any time.

The hotel in Florence in question has a very beautiful garden in its courtyard - it offers views from most of the rooms. Provided the weather is fine, a buffet can be served for guests in this garden. Tourists who rested in this place often note the picturesqueness of the territory that surrounds the hotel.

You can book a hotel in Florence (Italy) online by visiting the website of any travel operator that collaborates with Hotel Ariele. This can also be done directly on the hotel website. As for the price of accommodation, on average, the daily rental of apartments costs about 3300 rubles.

La porta del paradiso

Among the hotels in the center of Florence, La Porta del Paradiso is a must-see place, a place where representatives of all generations can adequately relax, enjoying the surrounding beauties. Not far from its location is the Basilica of Santa Maria Novella, as well as the Lower Fortress.

As for the rooms that are offered to tourists, they are presented in a single category - standard. It should be noted that in their equipment there is everything that is necessary for a comfortable stay. The apartments are equipped with private bathrooms with all amenities.

Not far from the location of the hotel in question are the most important transportation hubs, as well as places where you can have fun and good time at any time of the day.

The daily cost of living in a comfortable room is about 8300-8500 rubles. For this price, guests can take advantage of the opportunity of free access to the Internet, as well as all the amenities of the service provided, which also includes providing guests with a daily breakfast in their room.

Hotel St. James

Hotel St. James is a vacation spot that, in accordance with international standards, has a two-star status, but in fact offers a wonderful vacation in comfortable apartments - that's what the tourists who have been here say.

Hotel St. James is a hotel in Florence that is close to some very interesting city attractions. For those who are going to be here even for a short time, experienced tourists recommend visiting the Museum of San Marco, the church, the Gallery of Academicians. In addition, travelers claim that people who are interested in acquiring interesting goods and souvenirs will definitely be interested in visiting the Saint-Lorenzo street market, which is also within walking distance of the hotel in question.

As for the peculiarities of tourist accommodation in the hotel in Florence under consideration, you can come here with your pets. For guests' cars, parking is offered here, which is under 24-hour security, as well as multilingual staff who speak 4 languages. All apartments that are offered to accommodate guests have excellent decoration, as well as an elegant interior. Access to the Internet here is completely free.

As for the ratings for accommodation in the hotel in question, Florence (Italy), Hotel St. James has marks of 8-8.2 points out of the maximum 10. If we talk about the cost of living, it is about 7200 rubles per day per room.

Hotel arno bellariva

For a joint vacation together, a hotel called Hotel Arno Bellariva is often chosen, which is located in Florence in a very picturesque place - on the banks of the Arno River.

All apartments presented in this best hotel in Florence are decorated in a classic style with original decor and interior decoration. Each room has a large number of textiles, as well as modern furniture made of quality material, among which wood predominates.

The apartments of this hotel are equipped with bathrooms, which offer not only plumbing, but also bath accessories, necessary to maintain cleanliness.

Hotel Arno Bellariva provides breakfast and dinner delivery to your room. Pets are allowed here, for which, in some cases, additional charges may apply. As for the ordinary cost of renting an apartment, it is, on average, about 6,000 rubles per day.

Hotel aida

Very good, comfortable accommodation offers Hotel Aida, which can be found a considerable amount of positive reviews. Travelers who stayed in it talk about comfortable living conditions, as well as the great service at this hotel. It should be noted that the positive reviews about this place to stay are well supported by the ratings given by the tourists themselves at various tourist portals. So, on Booking this place for rest and accommodation was rated by guests exactly 8 out of 10, which is considered an excellent indicator.

Speaking about the accommodation of guests, it should be mentioned that it is made in very spacious and comfortable rooms, which contain modern furniture and appliances, as well as a bathroom with a shower installed in it. Among the general conditions, the hotel also offers a number of rooms that are fully equipped to accommodate guests with disabilities.

The cost of living in the apartments of this hotel in Florence (Italy) is relatively low - it is only about 4000-4500 rubles per day. This, according to travelers, is quite acceptable, especially considering all the conditions that are offered to vacationers.

Sleep florence

The rooms, which are real apartments, ideal for a whole family, offer tourists the best hotel in Florence - Sleep Florence. Here, in each room, there is not only a sleeping and relaxing area, combined with a place intended for watching TV. In addition to all this, there is a small kitchen connected to the dining room.

All guests of this hotel can use the Internet access, which is provided here for free, as well as air conditioning, satellite TV and parking for cars.

Sleep Florence is a hotel in Florence with smoking rooms, however, non-smoking guests also have a hotel group of apartments in which they can feel as comfortable as possible.

The cost of living in the room of the hotel in question is about 4,500 rubles per day - this price, according to most guests of Florence, is very favorable, due to the high level of room service. The hotel has high ratings on various tourist sites - as a rule, their indicator does not fall below 8 points out of 10, which is pretty good.
