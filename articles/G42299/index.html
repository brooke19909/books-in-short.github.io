Rethymno

Half an hour by bus from the hotel Amnissos Hotel Rethymno 3 * - and you are already in a beautiful medieval town. Amnissos Hotel 3 * (Crete) has more than eighty rooms for guests. Of these, twenty-four rooms are in the main building, and the rest are in the bungalow.
