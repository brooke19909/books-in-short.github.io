Baywatch Resort 4 * (India / Goa): photos and reviews of tourists

Today, the focus of our article will be the Baywatch Resort 4 * hotel. What is the focus of this hotel? How far is it from the sea? And what is nearby, in the resort of Colva and even further in South Goa? We learn all this from the reviews of tourists who visited the hotel. Travelers from the homeland at this hotel are few. Whether you consider this a huge plus or a disadvantage of the hotel complex is up to you. But those who rested at the hotel did not complain about room decoration, food, infrastructure and animation. Although they pointed to certain specifics of the hotel. Which one? Read about it below.



Location specifics

Where is Baywatch Resort 4 * located? South Goa is just a part of the state of India. But how she does not look like her northern sister! Goa was discovered as a hippie party place in the seventies of the last century. Gatherings to the trance music, parties on the verge of breaking the law (when it is not clear which grass is burning), huts under thatched roofs with a minimum of amenities, cows on the beach - all this is now left. But only in North Goa. In the southern part settled quite decent people with money. Luxurious hotels stretch along the coast with a well-kept beach. Their guests are cherished and cared for in Ayurvedic salons and spas. Do you want to see "real India"? Nothing is simpler: north of the mouth of the Zouari River, which is the symbolic border between the two parts of Goa, you will find it in abundance. But do not be afraid that in the south you will not see the real luxurious Indian nature. Under the nose of vacationers, as evidenced by the reviews, there is a real reserve. Here you can get acquainted with wildlife.



Colva specificity

Colva - this is the name of the largest and oldest resort on which the Baywatch Resort 4 * hotel is located. Goa (here we are talking about the southern part of the state) is a vacation destination for wealthy retirees from Western Europe. But Colva has its own specifics. It is the most fun resort in South Goa. There are even night rave parties, remotely resembling those that are arranged nightly in the north of the state. European youth loves to rest in Colva. It is she who brings revitalization and drive to a sleepy, cozy city. On weekends, Europeans are joined by residents of the county capital, the city of Margao. Wealthy Indians rent luxurious colonial-style villas on the beach. Another specificity of Colva (and the whole state of Goa) is the spread of Catholicism among the local population. The fact is that until recently this area was under the protectorate of Portugal. The colonialists left behind the Mediterranean buildings, and the missionaries left the elaborate Baroque churches.

Accommodations

Three two-story buildings of the Baywatch Resort 4 * hotel accommodate seventy-six Deluxe rooms. According to tourists, these are spacious rooms with a balcony. The filling of the room as a whole corresponds to the stardom of the hotel. It has silent air conditioning, a safe, a minibar and a TV with satellite channels.
