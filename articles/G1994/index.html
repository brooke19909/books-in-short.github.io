How to make crafts for giving from waste material?

Each time, using the discarded material for our crafts, we thereby help clean the planet. Make them easy, but they turn out to be very beautiful. The clear leaders here are the tires along with plastic bottles. If you look carefully around, you will find a huge amount of application for these seemingly unnecessary things.



The easiest way to use crafts from waste material for giving. There they can find the widest application. Flower beds arranged from discarded tires, children's toys from the same material, various paths from corks or bottles - a lot that you can make, you just have to put your hands on it. The wheels for cars that have already served their due term find here a second holiday life.

And what kind of crafts from waste material for children are obtained if two materials are combined. For example, in order for the sun to appear on the playground, you need to dig one-third of the tire. Fasten the plastic caps on top so that then screw the bottles upside down. This simple procedure must be done with all the covers. When the bottles are installed, proceed to painting. Yellow or orange color will do. What is not the rising sun? Both beautiful and comfortable to climb.
