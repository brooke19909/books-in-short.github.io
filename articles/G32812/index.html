Tropitel Naama Bay Hotel 5 *: photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Egypt, despite the political upheaval, does not cease to be the most popular budget tourist destination for winter holidays at sea. Two resorts of the country - both Hurghada and Sharm el-Sheikh - compete among themselves in attracting vacationers. Over the past twenty years, these cities have turned into a multi-kilometer chain of beaches, built up with luxurious and affordable hotels. They tirelessly "grow" with new resort villages. Today we present for those who have already visited this wonderful country, and for those who are planning to come to Egypt for the first time, the Tropitel Naama Bay Hotel. The photos of this green oasis among the sultry Sinai desert are simply impressive. But what is the reality? What is a hotel like? Its pros and cons will be discussed below. For analysis, we took not only official information about the hotel, but also studied the reviews of travelers staying in it.



Sharm el Sheikh - the pearl of the Sinai Peninsula

This resort is located right on the Red Sea coast, away from the main part of Egypt. This is not Africa, but the Sinai Peninsula of Asia. Sharm el-Sheikh (Sharm-el-Sheikh) is famous for its unique microclimate, which makes winter holidays possible. Mountains close the resort from the cold winds from the north, one of which, Mount Moses, is a shrine for believers of the three world religions.

Nature endowed the coast of Sharm el-Sheikh with unimaginable beauty wealth - coral reefs, so the northern coast of the Red Sea is considered a mecca for divers. And those who do not know how to dive, can also see the colorful inhabitants of the reefs. In some places, corals and tropical fish can be found near the coast and get to them by swimming with a snorkel and mask. If you compare with other places of recreation, for example, with the same Hurghada, then Sharm el-Sheikh is considered a youth resort. It is always noisy here, there are a lot of discos, bars and night clubs in the city.

General description of the hotel

For lovers of chain hotels, we inform that the institution belongs to the leading line of Tropitel Hotels & Resorts, which has many hotels around the world. Tropitel Naama Bay was built in 2002. In fact, this is a large hotel complex, a real small town for a paradise holiday. Its territory is so large that special electric cars ply on it, spreading lazy guests from restaurants or the main building to remote bungalows. If someone does not want to walk around the territory, he can ride on it. Officially, the hotel complex is divided into two zones: the main building (pools and other infrastructure near the main building) and the Resort (park area with bungalows).

Territory

If you look at the Tropitel Naama Bay 5 * hotel from a bird's eye view, you can see that it resembles a flower. Number of rooms

The Tropitel Naama Bay Hotel 5 * hotel has 390 rooms for guests. The lion's share of them (namely 321) are standard double rooms. Suites and four villas are available. Even the standard room has a small balcony. All rooms are equipped with powerful air conditioning, which allows you to receive hotel guests during the hot Egyptian summer. In the bungalow on the ground floor instead of balconies - terraces. Standard rooms include a hairdryer and toiletries in the bathroom, cable TV, a spacious safe and a minibar. The floor is covered with ceramic tiles, which is very appropriate in the heat. They clean the rooms every day, linen is changed at your request. For a fee, you can directly go out of the room to the World Wide Web or Skype via a wireless network.

Beach and Pools

Tropitel Naama Bay Hotel 5 * is a second-line hotel. It is located across the road (promenade) from the coast of the sea. However, the hotel has its own beach. It's not big but very comfortable. Coverage is sand without stones and corals, there is also a pontoon for quick descent into the water, since the coast here is very shallow and you have to go far. The beach is convenient for young children. There is a fresh shower, deck chairs with mattresses, umbrellas.

You can go water sports, take windsurfing lessons or take a diving course. If you forgot your snorkeling mask and snorkel at home, this ammunition can be rented on the beach.

From buildings to the sea - 200-800 meters. On the territory of the hotel itself there are four pools: the main (or large) in the form of a lagoon, Olympic for sports swimming, a mini-water park with slides and Resort Pool. Of these, the first three are heated in winter. There is a bar near the main pool during daylight hours. Sunbeds and umbrellas on the sun terrace are also free for visitors, as well as on the beach.

Services for children

Tropitel Naama Bay 5 * is trying to earn fame as a family-friendly hotel in Sharm El Sheikh. The hotel service has a lot of little things that are designed to make the stay of kids and their parents enjoyable. Three out of four pools (except the Olympic) have separate heated sections for children. There is a games room with toys and an outdoor playground. For kids, children's animators work. If parents want to be alone, they can hire a skilled nanny or baby sitter for a fee. The restaurants have high chairs for feeding babies and a special children's menu. By agreement with the property, a baby cot is available for free in a room for a child under two years of age.
