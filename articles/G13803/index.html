Empuriabrava, Spain - Spanish Venice: description, reviews

Many people have a question about whether Empuriabrava really built in Spain is an imitation of the world famous Italian Venice. Of course not! They simply draw a parallel between these amazingly beautiful cities as built on the water. But each of them has its own atmosphere. Empuriabrava is a young city and a popular resort on the Costa Brava in Spain. Yachtsmen like to come here. We will get acquainted with this extremely unusual city with canals and boats along with roads and cars closer.

Building a city on the water

The construction of Empuriabrava began in the 60s of the last century. This was a special project, which provided for each land plot, its own pier and access to the sea. The city developed as a yacht port. Fans of outdoor activities - yachtsmen. And they love this city because of the climate with frequent winds. For their owners, collective mooring places for personal boats and boats have been organized. There are a lot of such berths, and they are distributed throughout the city. City architecture

Houses in the city were built gradually. Construction depended on the income level of land owners. The difference in styles and design of buildings is striking. Along with luxurious villas built in the classic Mediterranean style, with lawns, statues and palm trees, you can observe the pier built up with typical small houses. There are also apartment buildings in the city. Mainly dominated by two- and three-story buildings. It is much more profitable to buy an apartment in Spanish Venice than to maintain a villa.





In the city there are hotels for tourists coming to rest. Some travelers rent a pitchfork for vacation. Each visitor has his own abilities. Boats and boats are necessary for their owners to move along the canals of the city, serving both streets and alleys. There are also several bridges over the canals.

City location

Empuriabrava in Spain is considered the largest city on the water, as it has a length of artificial canals of the order of forty kilometers. It is located in the Bay of Roses of the Mediterranean Sea. The administrative center of this region is 65 kilometers from Empuriabrava. Nearby, in the city of Figueres, there is also the nearest railway station. It is only 15 kilometers away. The local population varies in the region of 8000 people. During the tourist season, with the arrival of those who want to relax in this water paradise, this figure grows to 75,000 people. Lovers of outdoor activities, yachtsmen come here. After all, Empuriabrava is one of the largest yacht ports in the world. So, from an unknown place Empuriabrava turned into a real bohemian center of tourism.

Types of rest

The climatic features of Empuriabrava make it ideal for outdoor activities. Sunny days prevail in the year. There are about three hundred of them. Tourists come to go water skiing, yachting, kayaking and jet skiing. You can catch a fish practically without leaving home or go fishing on the high seas.

Empuriabrava is a real paradise for windsurfers. And not far from the city is one of the largest centers in Europe for parachuting. There is an opportunity to test yourself in this sport by paying the rent of a small plane. This pleasure is not cheap. In addition, you can fly in an ultralight pilot-controlled aircraft, fly in a balloon or take a pair jump.



A separate holiday article in Empuriabrava is the nightlife of the city. Bars and restaurants, discos and evening walks on lighted canals - all for tourists. For walks, you can rent a yacht or pay a short trip by boat.

One day tourism

Tourists in the city, as mentioned above, a lot. A certain part of them are travelers and groups visiting the day. The city is interesting to them for its man-made channels. By the way, there is a help desk here that provides information on any topic related to the city, there are maps, printed materials and books. Here they give information about where you can find a rental point for pleasure boats.

Typically, tourists rent a boat and ride along the canals. The route of such a journey is approximately 30 kilometers. Boat trips are allowed on a specific circular route. Swimming in private areas is prohibited. Usually, for such travelers, the color that they observe on their journey through Empuriabrava is quite enough. Their feedback is that the city is very calm, with a measured life and beautiful villas along the canals. A walk usually works in a peaceful manner, and time spent on water distracts from the frantic rhythm of city life.



For tourists in the city, an hourly boat rental service is provided. There are several rental points in the city, but the tariffs are almost the same for everyone. Each of them has boats of its own specific color. The demand for water transport is almost year-round.

Resort beaches

Many tourists choose for their beach holidays a pleasant climate and the gentle sea in the coastal cities of Spain. These include the youngest resort town of Empuriabrava. The beaches here are very large. The length of the strip of La Rubin beach is 1.8 kilometers and the width is 40 meters. Camping sites with relatively low prices for vacations are located along its entire coast. On the beach area there are bars and cafes, a center for windsurfing and kiting, toilets, changing rooms.

The second beach of Empuriabrava is 1.3 kilometers long and 60 meters wide. There are cafes and bars, rental points of umbrellas and deck chairs. Tourists especially like that the large size of the beaches creates the effect of free space, regardless of the number of vacationers. Enough space for everyone. The entrance to the bay at this beach is gentle. You can swim for children. The entire coastal zone of urban beaches is considered the best on the Costa Brava and has more than once held the honorary award - the Blue Flag. There is also the Small beach of Empuriabrava. It is actually small in comparison with the previous ones.

Weather in Spain in October

The location of the resort town of Empuriabrava in a picturesque place where the sea is adjacent to the majestic Pyrenees forms a peculiar mild, but wayward climate. You can relax in this place all year long. Snow does not fall in winter, spring in these parts is early, summer is not too hot, autumn is mild. Nevertheless, travelers and locals say that it is wayward about the climate. This is due to constantly blowing winds. It is in October in Spain, as tourists say, that the winds create difficulties for a beach holiday. At this time, it is best to go windsurfing, yachting or wakeboarding.

Wonderful relaxation and entertainment for every taste await tourists in a city called Empuriabrava. Spain is a country where tourism and historical heritage are combined.
