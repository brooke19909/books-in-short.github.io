What services can I track mail through?

Arrival at the sorting center

Arrival at the pick-up point

When the purchased product has passed all stages of transportation, it gets to the post office closest to the buyer. Within a few days, mail employees write a notice of receipt and bring it to the recipient in the mailbox. If the addressee does not arrive within a week, a repeated notification is issued. A parcel that has been without demand for a month is sent back.

If a person was tracking a parcel through online services and saw that it was in place, he may not wait for a notification, but come to the post office with the number of the item and, having named it and showing his passport, get a box with the purchased goods.





If he missed all the notifications and did not follow the status for a long time, then when he tries to understand where the package is, he can see the status of "export from the country of departure" again, but now this country will be Russia, which means that the purchase has gone back. Only dialogue with the seller will help here, he can stop the return shipment or send the goods again. But not all sellers agree to this, therefore, if you are waiting for a parcel from abroad, do not rely on notifications from the mail, but check the location of the goods yourself.
