How to choose a tablet with a good camera and battery (reviews)

The range of modern mobile devices is so wide that making a choice, in fact, is not so simple. There are many models that differ in price, manufacturer, technical specifications. Even making a choice between a metal case and a classic "plastic" is not so simple.

In this article, we will talk about how to focus on two very important criteria - battery capacity and camera. In order to make it easier for the reader to navigate this topic, along the way we will offer examples of various devices.

The foundation of any choice





To begin with, we note that the task of any user is to choose the most affordable tablet with a good camera, low battery consumption and high performance. It is logical that each of us, acquiring a mobile device of any type, is inclined to expect better indicators from it. But this does not happen.

Tablet battery

Let's start with the battery, because it determines the duration of the device. Many buyers, however, underestimate such a parameter as battery capacity, simply not giving it any importance. And in vain.





Since the importance of a capacious battery for your mobile device, you will understand in the first days of working with it. Perhaps the priority for you is to buy a tablet with a good camera; but in the case of a small battery capacity, after a couple of hours of filming, the device will sit down and you will have no choice but to put it aside and wait until it becomes possible to connect to the network. Believe me, this is very annoying.

Therefore, you need to try to find a model that will have a more capacious battery that can hold a charge for longer. However, it should be understood that the duration of the device depends on two criteria - charge and battery capacity. If the latter has small dimensions (which is what manufacturers strive for, making the tablets minimal in thickness), respectively, it can not show a large capacity.

Thus, there must be a balance between how much space the battery takes and how long it can provide the gadget.

We select the battery for the screen





The battery capacity that we already mentioned is measured in milliampere-hours. In a letter, this value is indicated as "mAh". You can see it in the technical specifications of any tablet or phone. The higher the specified indicator, the longer the gadget can work without additional charge.

However, there is another factor - the level of charge consumption. It, in turn, depends on the capabilities of the device. If, say, a simple keyboard phone has a battery of 700mAh for a week, then for a 7-inch tablet a battery of 3500 mAh is a day of work.

There is such an installation: pick up the battery should be the size of the display. Let's say for 7 inches 3500 mAh and more - this is normal; for 8 - 4200 mAh, 9- and 10-inch tablets will work perfectly with 6 and 7 thousandth batteries. Recall, this is about ensuring that your gadget is enough for at least a day of work.

When choosing devices, do not forget that a tablet with a good camera and battery will cost a lot. True, it will be much more comfortable to use it.

Charge rate

According to user reviews, you can observe such a problem on budget- class devices , for example, the cheapest Lenovo, some models of Asus, Samsung and other companies. Camera for video and flash

When looking for a device for photographing and shooting videos, do not forget about the flash. As the reviews show, working with it on the tablet is much more convenient, and photos in low light turn out to be better. The flash is not only on expensive and medium-priced gadgets (iPad, Samsung Note, Samsung Tab), but also on inexpensive tablets (Nomi, Assistant, Impression, Ainol, MYTAB and others). So picking up a computer with lighting for pictures is not so difficult.





Another issue is cameras for video. Here you need to look not at the number of megapixels, but at the video resolution. A good indicator is the resolution of 720p, excellent - 1080p (HD - quality). So, for example, removes Asus Eee Transformer or the same Apple iPad.
