The Cyprus resort of Protaras has recently become a favorite vacation spot for many Russian tourists. Here, away from the bustle of the city, life becomes calm and measured, and time flows unnoticed. Protaras favorably distinguishes itself from other resorts of Cyprus in a leisurely atmosphere. This small town in the southeast of the island is located just one hour from Larnaca Airport. Today one cannot even believe that not so long ago there was a deserted coast covered with shrubs and fig groves. Thanks to the rapid development of infrastructure, in the last decade, modern Protaras resembles Las Vegas rather than a Greek settlement.





The golden sandy beaches of Protaras stretch for several kilometers, and miniature bays are surrounded by massive cliffs that protect the beaches from the wind. The swimming season here lasts from April to the very end of October. The resort boasts a mild Mediterranean climate and good weather almost all year round. Temperature in summer (average) +30 degrees, in spring, at the beginning of the beach season, +25. Protaras offers guests well-equipped beach lines, excellent service, many water attractions, as well as hotels of different levels. Of course, first of all, the resort is chosen by families with children and lovers of measured relaxation at sea, however, entertainment lovers will not be bored here. In the afternoon, life in the town is quiet and measured, but by the evening the resort comes to life. There are many restaurants, colorful night clubs and discos, from where in the evenings, along with modern rhythms, romantic melodies of buzuki are heard, and in the center of the town there is a beautiful ‚Äúsinging‚Äù fountain. Open-air restaurants are very popular with locals and visitors alike.





Attracted by Protaras and numerous divers from around the world, this is one of the most interesting places to study the underwater life of the Mediterranean Sea. The coastline here is very indented, while diving with a scuba diving and mask you can see unusual underwater caves and rocks.

Beaches and hotels of Protaras

A small resort town famous for its beaches, they are rightly considered a landmark of Protaras, the best, well-maintained and clean in Cyprus. Many of them are marked with the Blue Flag. In addition, in the summer the Mediterranean Sea is calm, and therefore even the sandy beaches of the village boast clear water. Each of them has a convenient entry into the sea. The most famous is the beach in Fig Three Bay, in the center of the resort village, it is named because of the fig trees growing here. Konnos Bay is considered the best for a secluded holiday, for visiting with children a small Scoutari Beach is only 50 meters long, and Louma Beach is a larger beach (400 meters), but the latter is a few kilometers from the shops and taverns of the center of Protaras. For diving enthusiasts, Pernera Beach is the place to go. This beach offers not only relaxation by the sea, but also service in any of the five restaurants located on its territory. Not only professionals, but also beginners can dive here, there are instructors. However, the beach is not suitable for families with children because of the rather steep bottom.

Protaras is ready to offer guests of the island a lot of comfortable options for accommodation - from relatively budget to luxury. Those who are going on vacation to Cyprus (Protaras), reviews of tourists about hotels will be useful to study. After all, as you know, the main thing that determines the choice of a hotel is the ratio of price and quality. From this point of view, four-star hotels are of interest. These are very comfortable complexes located right on the coast (maximum 7 minutes walk), with optimal prices. Most of the beaches in the village are municipal, but 4-star hotels have their own beaches. Tourists visiting Cyprus (Protaras) 4 * hotels can choose based on prices and their own preferences. However, it should be remembered that the prices here vary depending on the season, the comfort of the room and the location of the hotel relative to the center of the resort town. So, on average, room prices in hotels of this category start from 15 thousand rubles per night.
