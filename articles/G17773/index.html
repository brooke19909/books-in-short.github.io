Kleopatra Arsi Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Alanya): description of rooms, service, reviews

The need for a change of scenery, getting unforgettable vivid impressions and new emotions attract us far beyond the horizon. Therefore, as soon as the opportunity arises, pack your suitcases and go to the Mediterranean Sea!





Sandy shore, clear water, entertainment at every turn. Around the mass of historical attractions that have absorbed the breath of centuries.

Alanya

This small town located in the south of Turkey, 150 km from the province of Antalya, was founded by the ancient Greeks in the 3rd century BC and in ancient times was a powerful pirate center that could withstand the Roman Empire. The status of the resort was previously received by the agricultural Alanya in the 1950s. Today it is one of the largest tourist centers in the world, welcoming guests from all over the world every day.

Comfortable accommodation offers many Turkish hotels. Noteworthy is Kleopatra Arsi 3. The hotel infrastructure has everything you need for a comfortable stay. That is why the coastal complex is so popular among tourists.

General information. Description of rooms

Kleopatra Arsi 3 is a hotel complex located 150 meters from the famous Cleopatra beach. According to legend, Mark Anthony brought thousands of tons of Egyptian sand here on ships, and then gave this part of the coast to his beloved. Therefore, this beach with perfect composition sand, similar to which was found only in one place - in the north of Africa - is so popular among vacationers.

For your information

The reception is open around the clock. The staff speaks four languages: Turkish, German, English, Russian. Upon check-in, an identification document is required (with photo). Check-in time is from 14:00. Pets are not allowed. The minimum age threshold for self-registration is 18 years. Settlement - at 12:00. Payment can be made in cash or by credit card Visa and MasterCard.



For a fee, you can use the safety deposit box at the reception. Luggage storage is also available.

In case of a change in the reservation date or cancellation for 2 days, no fee will be charged. If the reservation was canceled later than the specified time or the guest did not call at all, the penalty in favor of the hotel will be the full cost of the reservation.

In the sun

The swimming season in Alanya lasts from April to October, but spring and autumn are considered the best time to relax. It is at this time that there is still no intense heat, and the sea water is already well warmed up. The beach at Kleopatra Arsi Hotel 3 is its own, constantly maintained clean. Located 80 meters from the residential building. The hotel does not provide beach towels. Umbrellas, mattresses, chaise lounges are paid.

Those wishing to relax on site can be accommodated by the pool. Sun beds and umbrellas are provided free of charge.

How to get there

Cleopatra Arsi Hotel is 122 km from Antalya International Airport and 40 km from Gazipasa Airport. You can get here by ordering a transfer at the hotel or on comfortable tour operator buses. Travel time is about 1.5 hours.

The hotel is conveniently located for public transport.





sights

A regular pastime in the hotel may prefer to get acquainted with the surrounding attractions. It is informative, interesting and memorable for a long time.

Waterpark Alanya . Great place for fun. Kleopatra Arsi 3 is located 1.3 km from it. As in any water park in the world, there are queues for attractions. But what is the insignificant waiting time in comparison with unforgettable emotions! The water park is equipped with 24 slides, including Kamikaze, Black Hole. Four swimming pools (one of them with artificial waves), waterfalls, fountains, a jacuzzi, an amusement area for children, parachuting or bungee jumping - a lot of entertainment for every taste. You can satisfy your hunger in one of the restaurants and cafes; you can enjoy sweets at the local pastry shop.

Damlatas cave . Located in the center of Alanya, 100 meters from the sea. Known for its healing properties due to the presence of natural minerals. The fundamental factors that have a beneficial effect on health are the natural ionization of the air and the high content of carbon dioxide. Behind the scenes is called the "cave of wet stones."



Dim Chai Valley . This is a must visit place. Beautiful nature, waterfalls, mountain rivers. A floating restaurant with tables on pontoon rafts and cabins for relaxing on stilts is especially popular among tourists. The main dish on the menu is fantastically delicious fish cooked in various variations.

Red tower . It is a 20-minute walk from the hotel. It is a symbol of Alanya, imprinted on the flag of the city. An outstanding example of defensive medieval architecture. It was built in the 13th century in order to protect the city from pirate raids. Full restoration was carried out in the 20th century. Currently, there is an ethnographic museum.

Many will be impressed by the trip to the ancient docks of the shipyard. Their depth inside is 40 meters, which allows you to hide several ships from prying eyes.

Hotel guests are offered a variety of excursions. In street agencies, their cost is cheaper than that of tour operators. The nearest office, Minister Tours, is across the street from the hotel.
