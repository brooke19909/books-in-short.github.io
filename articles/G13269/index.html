Sultan Beach, Kemer: a great option for a fun holiday

Kemer is a resort town located in the Turkish Mediterranean. More than 60 kilometers separate it from the capital of Turkey - the well-known Antalya. The Taurus Mountains rise above Kemer. The local microclimate differs significantly from the climate of the same Antalya, despite the proximity of these cities. Precipitation (both in winter and summer) is less than in the Turkish capital. In the summer it is quite hot and sunny, most of the year in Kemer there is excellent weather. Tourists in Kemer are always waiting, they are welcome all year round. From a wide variety of all kinds of hotels, my eyes run up. These are private pensions and expensive five-star hotels. If you are determined to visit Turkey, we suggest you stay in one of the best four-star hotels called Sultan Beach. Kemer is pleased to welcome you!



Location

Sultan's Beach is located away from busy cities. It is 65 kilometers from Antalya. It is located near Kemer, in the village of Camyuva. The distance from the beach is about 100 meters.

Building

The building of the Sultan Beach Hotel (Kemer) has a unique design, it was built in the Ottoman style. The size of the complex is small, but it is very cozy. The hotel has two buildings, each of which has 4 floors, elevators work. The hall with a grand dome of 14 meters high amazes tourists!



The territory of the hotel "Sultan Beach"

Kemer offers its guests quality relaxation and a high level of service. At the hotel you can find everything you need: a main restaurant and an additional one that offers exclusively a fish menu (for a fee), in addition, the hotel has three more bars. You can swim in the large outdoor pool, but if you do not want to sunbathe, there is also an indoor room. Here you can use the services of a doctor, laundry, hairdresser and conference room, which can accommodate 100 people at once.

Rooms

The hotel has 138 rooms, most of which are economy rooms. The hotel has only 8 two-room suites and one presidential class room. Half of the rooms have a gorgeous view of the sea. There are apartments for the disabled. The standard room is designed only for three people, and the suite can accommodate no more than 4. In each room you can count on a shower, hairdryer, TV with Russian channels, air conditioning and a telephone. Please note that a minibar and safety deposit box require an additional fee. The hotel always has daily housekeeping, and bed linen is changed three times a week.
