How to pack a flower or bouquet with your own hands?

Flowers - a universal gift for any occasion. Not a single celebration is complete without a bouquet, whether it is an anniversary or a wedding, a professional holiday or International Women's Day. Flowers for the composition are selected taking into account preferences and depending on the celebrated date. The presentable appearance of the composition depends not only on the components, but also on how to pack the flower or bouquet.



Types of packaging materials

The most common way to pack a bouquet is to wrap the flowers in a wrapping film. Roses, carnations and chrysanthemums are wrapped in a thin film, decorated with an ornament, or transparent. Fasten the film with beautiful lush folds using tape and a stapler. Thanks to this simple technology, even from three modest flowers you can create a real masterpiece. How to pack a flower or bouquet? You need to rewind the stems with scotch tape at the base of the bouquet to mimic the extra volume, and add palm branches and gypsophila to the inflorescences.





Bouquets are popular for the decoration of which a floral grid is used. Packaging made of this material ensures the safety of the bouquet during transportation, since the mesh is a fairly strong bending frame. These qualities of the material allow you to create volumetric compositions.

How to pack a flower or a bouquet of delicate inflorescences? The classic version is the use of a wide satin ribbon encircling the stems in 2-3 girths or completely draping the lower part of the composition. Of the fabrics used are thin organza, non-woven, sometimes lace in combination with thick paper of a contrasting color.

Corrugated paper gives the flower composition a special lightness and fragility, but it is very easy to accidentally spoil it.





Dense leaves and greens are used as a wrapper - the flowers look organically, the bouquet can be immediately placed in a vase with water, without violating the arrangement.

How to pack flowers: master class

The type of material for decoration is selected depending on what flowers are used. For pink lilies, take double-sided wrapping paper. The square, the side of which is twice as long as the bouquet, is folded in half with the pattern down, we place the flowers so that the stems remain below the paper.

Carefully wrap the flowers on both sides, bandage with a pink ribbon.


A beautiful and neat bouquet is ready!





How to pack a flower in an original way?

From simple materials at hand you can make an unusual work of floristic art, if you get creative. To decorate white roses we use burlap, rope and wooden buttons - we get original bouquets for brides!



An interesting combination, isn't it?





Florists note that the real trend of recent times is the minimalism and naturalness of materials for decorating flowers. Plain paper used for packaging parcels, in combination with ribbons and organza, emphasizes the beauty and freshness of garden and wildflowers.



Everyone can beautifully pack flowers with their own hands , since the creative process requires only accuracy and sincerity, and your imagination will complete the rest!
