The village of Kuchugury of the Krasnodar Territory is a wild corner of the Russian south

A joke is popular among Russians, the meaning of which is that in the summer warm months 50% of the country's population moves to the Krasnodar Territory. Indeed, in large cities on the south coast in high season, prices are high, and sometimes there are too many vacationers. It is difficult to believe that in such a popular holiday destination there are still sparsely populated and truly wild places. Meanwhile, the village of Kuchugury of the Krasnodar Territory is one of them.

Climatic and natural features



Vacationers will enjoy the village with a pleasant climate, clean and sparsely populated beaches, as well as a calm sea. In Kuchugury, the weather is almost always good and the sun shines brightly. Today, this resort is considered health-improving. There are several mud springs on the coast, but the most popular and famous among them is the mud volcano Plevak (Cape Pekla). In the vicinity of the village there are several large resorts and modern spa hotels. Kuchugury of the Krasnodar Territory is a great place to relax for tourists of all ages. The infrastructure of the village is under development, but today there is everything for a comfortable stay and a variety of entertainment.

What to do on vacation: entertainment and excursions

Many tourists going on vacation to the Krasnodar Territory choose the Kuchugury village primarily because of the excellent conditions for a relaxing beach holiday. Where is the village of Kuchugury of the Krasnodar Territory?



All tourists, who are going to visit this settlement for the first time, have difficulty in drawing up a trip route. The village of Kuchugury is located 30-35 kilometers from the city of Temryuk, the nearest settlements are Golubitskaya and Peresyp. There are various ways to get to this resort. From Anapa (75 km) - by rail or by taxi. It is also possible on any form of transport to Temryuk, and then on a scheduled bus. If you go on vacation by train, get tickets to one of the stations: Krymsk, Tunnel or Slavyansk-on-Kubani, from all these platforms shuttle buses go to Kuchugury. Do not be afraid of the possible difficulties of the road, remember: rest in a quiet, calm and incredibly beautiful place is worthy of some effort. Remember or write down for yourself the address of the end point of the route: Krasnodar Territory, Temryuk District, Kuchugury - and then you will definitely be able to get there and not get lost.
