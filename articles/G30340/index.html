Location

Absolutely all reviews of tourists who visited the Baan Karonburi Resort (Phuket), noted its convenient location. This hotel is located on the Andaman Sea, almost the Karon Beach, the most wonderful in Phuket. It is only necessary to cross the road to step on clean white and yellow sand. On the other hand, the hotel is very close to restaurants, cafes, bars, shops, shops, massage parlors and other objects of the Thai tourism industry. Description

Hotel Baan Karonburi Resort occupies a compact territory, on three sides sandwiched by other hotels of different stars. On the fourth side there is a road along which a rivulet hurries to the sea. This water artery among tourists causes mainly complaints, since it interferes with enjoying the sea during rains. In a small area available to the hotel, the designers harmoniously arranged two four-story buildings, two swimming pools, a bar, a gazebo with massage couches, a parking lot. All this was decorated with subtropical evergreen vegetation, flowers, laid neat paths. The result was a very beautiful and functional area.

In the main building of the hotel is the reception, which serves tourists around the clock. The cozy lobby has tables, soft leather sofas and armchairs, a TV and Wi-Fi. Also in the lobby there is a shop where popular Thai goods are offered. At the reception you can hand over things to the left-luggage office, call a doctor, order a taxi, rent a vehicle, exchange currency, and buy excursions.





Rooms

One hundred spacious cozy rooms are approximately equally divided between the two buildings of the Baan Karonburi Resort. Reviews of tourists about living conditions here are quite satisfactory. Balconies are available in every room, even located on the ground floors. Views from the windows can be on the sea, pool or on neighboring hotels. The design of the rooms is close to chamberliness. Everything is executed in beige and brown tones, distinguished by sophistication and rigor. The highlight is the transparent door-wall to the hygiene room.

Sea

It is advisable to plan a vacation in Phuket from November to the end of April, although sometimes in March it can be windy and rainy. The air temperature during the day is always above +30 degrees Celsius, and the waters off the coast of the Andaman Sea are in the range +26 ... +28 o C. Baan Karonburi Resort is located, as noted above, near the best Karon beach in the city. Here is a paradise for children, since the sand is clean, shallow entry into the water, but the depth begins close to the coast. A feature of the beach is the lack of free sunbeds. You can rest here only in the sand, laying a hotel beach towel and installing a hotel umbrella (available in each room). Sun loungers are only paid (200 baht). Entertainment here for every taste - water rides, bars, cafes, but also a secluded corner is not difficult to find, since the length of the sandy coastline is quite large.





Leisure

Baan Karonburi Resort offers two swimming pools for its guests. One of them, narrow and long, is located along the hull. Sunbeds here are wooden, with mattresses and towels. The second artificial pond is equipped with a jacuzzi. His form is more free. Next to it is a bar. Sports lovers can use the free gym. There are no sports fields on site.

Animation as such is also absent; tourists entertain themselves on their own. Due to the location of the hotel, it‚Äôs not difficult. For 400 baht, you can go to Patong on a tuk-tuk, where until the morning life is in full swing, and entertainment is an endless stream. About the same to the beach, bay and at the same time the port of Chalong, from where you can go to the islands by boat. In Phuket Town, which is also easily accessible from the hotel by tuk tuk, temples, parks and architecture are of great interest. Tourists can visit all interesting places on their own or by buying sightseeing tours. In addition to the above, butterfly gardens and orchids, farm turtles, snakes and pearls, a shell museum, Monkey Mountain, a tiger park, an aquarium, a zoo and many other unique places in Phuket are popular.





Additional Information

Baan Karonburi Resort is perfect for tourists of all categories: those who travel to Thailand for a relaxing holiday at sea, and those who want to plunge into the vibrant nightlife. Parents with children are also comfortable here. The hotel has a swimming pool for kids, and cots are provided in the rooms. Not far from the hotel, literally within walking distance, the Dino Park is located, which children will certainly enjoy. Children under 2 years old stay free of charge.

Check in here runs from 14:00 local time.

Check out should be carried out before 12:00, but, according to tourists, hotel staff do not forbid tourists to use the pool, bar and towels without bracelets.
