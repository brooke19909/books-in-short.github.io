Decor. Credit card without annual maintenance

Credit cards have become quite a convenient tool for managing money. They are rightfully considered the type of loan that is easy to obtain and convenient to repay. Despite the wide selection, the most popular is a credit card without annual service, which will be discussed later in the article.

Main advantages

Trying to keep regular customers and attract new ones, many banks are developing new conditions for servicing credit cards. The main advantage is the interest-free period for using cash. It is different for all banks, but this possibility gives the borrower the right not to think about financial problems for some time.





A credit card without a fee for annual servicing is becoming more and more relevant, although when concluding an agreement, a bank client needs to be especially careful.

Firstly, such a card may have a high interest rate outside the interest-free period. Secondly, free service can last only the first year after receiving a card. And thirdly, there may be a high percentage for cash withdrawals, conversions, etc.

Features

A credit card without annual service, unlike the others, can have a minimal set of options. In other words, it is given to familiarize yourself with the bank. Most likely, after the end of the validity period, such a card will be transferred to annual maintenance with a wide range of options.





A card without a service is often an advertising company of a bank to attract new customers, so you should figure out how beneficial it is to use it:

  • The average annual servicing of a credit card is from 750 rubles. up to 3,000 rubles, depending on the type of card and the set of options, therefore, before concluding an agreement, a bank client needs to find out for himself whether the card will be used constantly or is it a fallback option.
  • If the card will be used constantly, especially for cashless purchases abroad, you should pay attention to the size of the conversion. After all, if the card is without a service, then most likely the bank will take sky-high interest for this option, then the meaning of free service disappears. The client of the bank will lose money on other operations with the card.
  • Clients who constantly work with cards can be issued a credit card without annual service with a full range of options (usually these are ‚Äúgold‚Äù cards for privileged customers).

Decor

You can apply for a credit card without an annual service when you personally apply to a financial institution, or through the official website of the bank, leaving an online application. For registration, you only need a passport, but in this case, the limit on cash will be small.

If you want to receive a large amount, you need to be prepared to submit a full package of documents:

  • passport of the Russian Federation;
  • certificate in form 2 of personal income tax confirming income;
  • additional identity documents;
  • a copy of the work book;
  • other documents requested by the bank.

If a person has a salary card of a certain bank, then it is better to make a request for a credit card in the same place. There is an option that the interest rate in this case will be less, and annual service will be absent.

Cash withdrawal

The credit card is designed to conduct cashless payments. If the borrower intends to withdraw cash, he must be ready to pay a commission in the amount of 3% to 7%, and also lose an interest-free period. The use of cash withdrawn from a credit card is fraught with a huge overpayment and a large commission.





There are banks that offer credit card withdrawals. But such a service is more accessible to privileged clients, participants of salary projects and employees of the bank offering this option.
