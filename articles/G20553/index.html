Iberotel Makadi Oasis Club - a beautiful hotel complex for family holidays in Egypt

This hotel is the largest in the vicinity and will provide you with an unforgettable vacation in Egypt.

Sea, Makadi Beach, Pools

The coast of Makadi Bay is known for its coral reefs. To admire all this beauty of the Red Sea, it is enough to walk a couple of hundred meters (it depends on which building you live in) along a shady palm alley, cross the road, and you find yourself on a hotel beach. It is equipped: there are many umbrellas, on the sunbeds - mattresses. But if you want to stay comfortably right next to the water, it is recommended to arrive early. In addition, there are showers, toilets, everything is cleaned. Since the reefs are nearby, special slippers are highly recommended for swimming. Although the water is clear and the bottom is very good, stones sometimes come across. But the advantages of the local coast include the fact that the beaches of other hotels are not closed. You can safely walk and choose your favorite place for swimming. In addition, the ebbs on a hotel beach are almost invisible.

In addition, the hotel has five pools. One of them is the so-called "family". In winter, two out of three adults warm up. Children's pools are always warm. Makadi Makadi has a huge water park with fifty slides. Admission is free for hotel guests. Before him, you need to go by bus "tuff-taf", which runs every quarter of an hour. It is open, and children really like to ride it "with the breeze."
