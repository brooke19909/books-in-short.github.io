Great vacation for little money at Life Syedra Princess Hotel 4 *

If you decide to stay in Turkey, but there are no material opportunities, you should pay attention to the prestigious, modern and budget hotel Life Syedra Princess Hotel 4 *, which can in some sense compete with many five-star hotels. Location

Mahmutlar is a small village on the shores of the Turkish Riviera. It is there that the warmest sea off the coast of Turkey and low prices for vacations. This compensates for a certain distance from the airport, entertainment centers and large retail outlets. Mahmutlar is the most distant resort of the Antalya coast.

Tourists who purposefully save money, but who want to spend their vacation days comfortably, are sent to this place purposefully. Mahmutlar - a resort designed for any contingent of vacationers. It is comfortable for couples with and without children, the elderly and youth. The lack of a developed entertainment industry is compensated by the proximity of the city of Alanya, where there are lots of nightclubs, entertainment centers, playgrounds for children, water parks and restaurants. For a couple of dollars, you can drive from Mahmutlar to the center of Alanya within 15-20 minutes.

Sights of the village

Relaxing in Life Syedra Princess Hotel 4 *, you can get acquainted with all the sights of the village. There is a beautiful park where it is nice to wander at sunset, relaxing in the shade of trees. Near the park are the ancient cities of Laertes and Siedra. Walking through the ruins, you can touch the story. On the territory of these ancient settlements there are buildings of well-preserved mosques and caravanserais.

While in Mahmutlar, you can set aside a couple of days for visiting distant sights using a rented car or the services of an excursion bureau. The most interesting will be visits to the Ich-Kale fortress in Alanya, the mausoleum of the Sultan of Turbesi, the Tersane shipyard, caves with colorful stalactites and stalagmites. For fishing enthusiasts, Mahmutlar organizes recreational trips along the Dimchay River, where you can go fishing for trout. Nearby is the Dimchay Cave with a salt lake inside. A visit to this cave will leave an unforgettable experience. Active tourists often climb the mountains surrounding the village. There is clean air, aromas of needles and flowers, springs and mountain rivers with crystal clear water.





Our compatriots in Mahmutlar

Mahmutlar is a Russian-speaking resort. A significant part of the property built in the village belongs to Russian businessmen. Therefore, there are many of our compatriots there. Even the Mahmutlar market is like a Russian bazaar. If you wish, you can visit the major shopping centers in Alanya - "Alanium", "Metro" and "Kipa".

Most hotels in the resort village are located on the first coastline, including the Life Syedra Princess Hotel. Only a four-lane highway separates the hotel from the sea. The second line is shops, restaurants, cafes, travel agencies, real estate agencies, hairdressers and boutiques. On the third line are houses and complexes, grocery stores.

Life Syedra Princess Hotel 4 * was built in 1990. Its total area is almost 5000 square meters. meters. Over the entire period of existence, several updates, restorations and renovations were carried out. Now it is a huge modern six-story building.
