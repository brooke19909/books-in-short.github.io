Where is the park located

When viewing photos of these places there is a feeling that these are illustrations for a children's fairy tale. Here is a huge mountain, only the one in which Ali Baba found the treasures. At the foot lies a fabulously beautiful valley, in which, like mushrooms in a clearing, houses are located that are not similar to each other. Weren't the seven dwarfs trying? In fact, the "Shtykovsky Ponds" (Primorsky Territory) is the valley of the Artemovka River. It is about an hour drive from Vladivostok. The territory of the recreation center is more than 5 hectares - this is where expanse. All important holidays are brightly and colorfully celebrated, and for this special weekend tours are organized.



Shtykovskie Prudy, Primorsky Krai: how to get there

There are two options: contact a travel company or go on your own. The tour operator will solve all organizational issues, you just have to enjoy your vacation. But you can get together on a trip in your own car and relax with your closest people. Base "Shtykovsky Ponds" (Primorsky Territory) will win your heart. You need to leave Vladivostok on the highway to Nakhodka. You will have to drive to the village of Shtykovo, and after two kilometers turn left. You will not be able to miss a turn, as there will be a pointer there. Now you need to follow right before turning to the base, and it is indicated by a pointer with the name of the park. The distance from Vladivostok is about 80 km, travel time takes 1-2 hours.

Shtykovsky Ponds (Primorsky Territory) is not only a favorite vacation spot for residents of Vladivostok. Khabarovsk residents are also happy to rest here, despite the fact that they have to spend much more time on the road, because the distance from Khabarovsk is 690 km.



Cost of rest

It will depend on the selected set of services. Entrance to the base is paid - 150 rubles on weekends and 100 on weekdays. For children under 14 years old, only 50 rubles. This is a day stay without accommodation. If you want to arrange a vacation for a day or more, it is difficult to find a better place than Shtykovsky Ponds (Primorsky Territory). Reservations can be made in advance through the official website. So you will be sure that cozy housing is prepared for you. Rent of a double house starts from 1800 rubles per day on weekdays and 2200 on weekends. The largest house for 14 people costs 17,500 and 21,000 rubles, respectively. You will be provided with all bedding, and near the houses there are equipped pavilions and picnic areas, the use of which is included in the rental price.

Arriving for one day, you can easily rent a gazebo where you can have a good and fun time in order to forever remember such a Primorsky Territory. Shtykovsky Ponds offer their guests a large selection of cozy places. Outdoor picnics (table, benches + barbecue) in winter and summer cost 500 rubles per day. Teremok (gazebo with electricity) - 300 rubles per hour in the winter and 1000 per day in the summer. A large gazebo by the lake for 25 people with electricity will cost 250 rubles per hour. There are so-called teplushki here. This is a mansion with tables, benches and a wood-burning stove or electric heater. Nearby there is a barbecue. A heating box for 15 people costs 500 rubles per hour in the winter and 1000 per day in the summer.





Entertainment

In summer it is a world of sun, greenery, cozy arbors and endless enjoyment. For children, special playgrounds with cartoon characters, labyrinths, rope towns are equipped here . The audience of all ages will enjoy playing on volleyball, football and basketball courts. In addition, on a vast territory, you can enjoy riding bicycles, rollers and electric cars, and make horse riding.

Separately, it is worth mentioning about various creative studios. They will be interesting to everyone, but especially to children. Leaving them with the instructor, you can relax for several hours. This is a pottery workshop in which, together with the master, you can create your first vase in a real circle. Felting workshop - here you can learn how to make wool, if not felt boots, then at least beautiful crafts. Fusing studio is a world of modern plastic and bright details. It‚Äôs easy to create a beautiful hairpin or beads in an hour. Soap making workshop - the world of aromatherapy.

In winter, ski and skate rentals open; a ski complex has recently been launched. The descent, however, is not too steep, but suitable for beginners and children. For lovers of steam bath and sauna.
