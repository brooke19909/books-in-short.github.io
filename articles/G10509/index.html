Round

This is the easiest way to make a flower from balls with your own hands. In its implementation, we need tape and scissors, as well as the base on which this craft will be made. It can be either a wall, a window or a table. In general, everything where there is a plane suitable for placing such an ornament. At the first stage, all the balls are filled with air using a pump or manually. We start with the petals. They should be located around the circumference. To do this, it is enough to make the petals not from one row, but from two or even three.





2 sausages

In this case, the flower from the balls with their own hands is made from two oblong blanks. One of them must be necessarily green (after all, the stem!). On one side, approximately 1/3 is measured. At this point, it twists in the form of a figure of eight, through the center of which a stem passes. Everything is attached using tape. On this basis of our crafts is ready. Next, take the second sausage. We bend it strictly in the middle. Then divide into three equal parts, twist and collect them together. You should get 6 sausages, assembled together. Two places of their collection are connected to each other. Everything is attached to each other using tape, then in a similar way to the stem, which was made in advance. Now our flower made of balls, made with our own hands, is ready, we can already use it at our discretion: or give it to the child so that he uses it as a toy; or give a wife and make her a pleasant surprise.





Summary

Any scheme of a flower from balls given in this article is simple and affordable. Everyone can easily do this craft. There is nothing complicated about it.
