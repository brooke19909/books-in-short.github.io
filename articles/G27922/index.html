Hotel Grand Plaza Resort Sharm 5 * (Egypt / Sharm El Sheikh): overview, description and reviews

Grand Plaza Resort Sharm 5 * belong to the best hotel complexes of Sharm El Sheikh. The hotel in Egypt is built on the Red Sea coast, the territory is about 14 thousand square meters. Here you can see an unusually beautiful area with palm trees, exotic bright flowers, lagoons of an unusual shape, as well as many swimming pools and several buildings in which the guests of the complex who decide to visit Egypt live. Sharm Grand Plaza Resort 5 * hotel offers a comfortable stay in modern rooms, here you can enjoy the sea views, engage in active sports. Friendly staff will be happy to help you with any questions.

Description of a five star hotel

Location Grand Plaza Resort Sharm 5 * - Nabq Bay (Egypt). Reviews about the hotel are in most cases only positive. High-quality service, comfortable conditions, beautiful local landscapes and plenty of entertainment - all this can be seen by reading the comments of visitors.





The hotel was built 10 years ago (2006), once reconstructed. Available for booking about 500 rooms, including standard type (about 100), superior and deluxe. There are suites and family rooms.

Grand Plaza Resort 5 * Sharm El Sheikh is located on the sea coast in the Nabq Bay area, which is not far from the Old Town (several kilometers). The airport is very close: only 8 km. The hotel attracts not only adults relaxing alone or with companies, but also couples with children. For the child there is a large playground, a pool and a club with all kinds of entertainment. You can charge a separate crib in the room and use the services of a Russian-speaking nanny.

Each room has all the necessary conditions to make the guest feel comfortable: modern furniture, cable TV with several Russian channels, a bath and shower, a minibar with cool drinks, a refrigerator and a split system. You can choose a balcony / terrace with views of the garden, the sea or the pool on site. The rooms are serviced around the clock (extra charge). Almost all are designed for 4 people. The family room and suite requires 2 beds and sofas. On the spacious terrace you can relax in the evening after a hot day. Cleaning is carried out 7 days a week, Wi-Fi can be used for a fee.

What is remarkable about the hotel

In the Grand Plaza Resort Sharm 5 * you can visit a medical and massage room, a modern beauty salon, a wellness center, a hammam. Visitors can take advantage of laundry, shuttle service, dry cleaning, as well as parking and an Internet cafe. VIP services available. The territory includes a bank branch and an inexpensive store. Near the main building there are 5 swimming pools with free sun loungers and parasols.





For those who appreciate outdoor activities during the holidays, diving, tennis with bright lights at night, as well as badminton, football and darts are provided. There is a gym, a room with board games. Professional animators work daily.

Dining at the hotel is All Inclusive. In addition, there are several restaurants (5) where you can try the menu of cuisines of Italy, the East and international dishes. Snacks and cold drinks can be taken in the lobby, bars located by the pools and on your own beach.
