How to make Pokemon from paper: schemes will help

The art of origami is so varied and interesting that even anime fans decided to try making funny figures that look like famous Pokemon heroes. And I must say that they did it.

Children are very fond of origami. Paper Pok√©mon will look like real ones and will be useful as bookmarks in a book. For example, there is a corner bookmark scheme in the form of a smiling Pikachu. The task seems difficult. And really, how to make a Pokemon from paper? Collecting them is pretty hard for a newbie in origami, as many schemes are confusing.

To understand the subtleties of the assembly will not be possible immediately. But parents or older comrades who already know the rules for making the simplest figurines of a dog or penguin can help this child.

The simplest assembly scheme for Pikachu

For the image of Pikachu, many variations of schemes have already been created. There are complex assemblies and lighter ones that can be done in a few minutes. But let us dwell on the most famous scheme created by Robert Lang.

In order to implement this model, you need to carefully understand the scheme. It is necessary to collect the initial form called "Fish". This blank is known to everyone who is familiar with the basic elements. So, do the following:

  • We bend the rhombus, or "fish", in half. We bend the long lower triangle at an angle. Bend the remaining small triangles 2 more times. Then we unfold and do the same with the long triangle a second time, but arching it the other way. It will be a tail. We assemble it, as shown in the diagram.
  • Turn the workpiece over. We make a double bend in the middle. It will separate the legs from the top and will be the basis for Pikachu's arms. The second triangle is the base of the body and head. You need to bend it in half. Its top should fall exactly in the center of the double bend.




  • On the sides we make blanks for making hands in the form of folded pockets.
  • Open the triangles above and make a large pocket. We bend it twice in half, and you can already distinguish the outline of the familiar Pokemon. It is necessary to shape the ears, hands and bend the paper legs so that the figure "was able" to stand.




The specified algorithm is shown in the pictures, so if you want, you can figure out how to make a Pokemon from paper using schemes.





Of course, it is advisable to draw with a pencil the eyes, nose and mouth shape of the cartoon character. It is good if the student independently creates such a miracle as paper Pokemon. Schemes will certainly help in this. It is important to understand that for a child to create objects with his own hands is very useful - in this way the brain develops.

How to make Pokemon from paper - modular shapes

Some heroes of the animated cartoon were created voluminous. They are more beautiful and interesting, but consist of so many small details - modules. These build options take a lot of time, and, of course, not everyone has the patience to put them together. For example, for the manufacture of Charmander you will need more than 400 modules only for the creation of the body, not counting the tail, head and legs.

But if the child is really interested in how to make a Pokemon from paper, which will be voluminous at the same time, then everything will surely work out. You just need to find a step-by-step guide and do everything according to the instructions.
