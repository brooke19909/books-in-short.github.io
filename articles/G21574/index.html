Sony Xperia V: reviews. Sony Xperia V: smartphone. Sony Xperia V: phone

Smartphones are a real symbol of modernity, and many can no longer imagine life without them. About a year and a half ago, the Sony Xperia V came out, which at that time quickly became the flagship model. What kind of reviews did he deserve ? Sony Xperia V is one of those smartphones that has been stably under the Christmas trees for two years in a row. This fact alone speaks volumes.

Screen

This component is most often awarded praise. Everything is fine with the phone: 4.3 inches, resolution 1280 x 720, which at that time was considered a very good indicator, and even today the smartphone does not look like an anachronism. Users really like the Sensor on lens option, thanks to which the display has significantly increased viewing angles. Mobile Bravia Engine 2 technology, which was perfected in this model, makes the picture even more realistic. Many buyers say that this option was the reason for the purchase, since none of the competitors had such a high-quality image.

Moisture protection

More precisely, protection not only from moisture, but also from dust. This option has earned numerous accolades. Sony Xperia V is the gadget that you can safely take to the beach with you. Users note that with it you can not be afraid to get in the rain; the phone may lie a couple of hours in the sand, but dust will not get inside the case. If your smartphone gets dirty, you can easily wash it right under running water.

LTE



Sony Xperia V is the first model from a well-known Japanese company that fully supports work in Russian LTE networks. It can function in the following ranges: I, III, V, VII and XX. Please note that in any case, you will need a special SIM card. Many buyers swore for a long time with technical support, not having the slightest idea about this nuance.

Design

This item also earned good reviews. Sony Xperia V was not without reason a hit of sales. Even today, buyers often pay attention to its beauty. The concave lid looks fresh and unusual, distinguishing it from the pile of the same type of flat "bricks" that have flooded the world market today. In addition, seasoned gadget lovers note that the release of this model was the milestone from which Sony completely abandoned the buttons on the front panel: since then they completely moved directly to the display.

However, reducing the number of buttons does not always cause delight of buyers. Alas, due to the disappearance of the mechanical camera button, you now have to use the application icon on the display, as a result of which it is quite possible not to have time to make a successful shot. The connectors here are also not enough - microUSB and a 3.5 mm jack for headphones. Actually, this smartphone Sony Xperia V is different from contemporaries, who often also have an HDMI port.



Of course, they are all protected by plugs, which many also do not like: over time, they become loose, and girls complain that because of their density they sometimes break nails. However, to each his own. If you are constantly in nature, then these features are by no means called disadvantages.

A powerful aluminum edge around the perimeter of the case not only adds a certain charm to the smartphone, but also makes it much stronger. In any case, it was she who deserved excellent reviews: the Sony Xperia V may fall from a decent height, but there will not be a single crack or deep scratch on the case.
