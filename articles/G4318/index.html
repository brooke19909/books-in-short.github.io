Aviators Park in St. Petersburg. Camping

In St. Petersburg there is an ideal place where you can have a great time - this is the Aviators Park. It is suitable for a relaxing pastime, and for outdoor activities. Many residents of the city and its environs come here to spend time with friends on a picnic or to have fun with the whole family. Especially if the weather is fine on the street and the sun is shining brightly, then there is no better place for such walks in the whole Leningrad region.

Appearance story

In the territory where the Aviators Park is located today, the main airfield of Leningrad was previously located. At the beginning of the twentieth century, this base was used to test new aviation equipment and various airplanes. There were also trained and prepared for the work of aircraft mechanics and pilots.





This place was once of great importance for Russian aviation, and many Soviet records were set here. But in the forties, after the surrounding area began to be built up with new buildings, this airfield was closed. And later its territory turned into a resting place for local residents.

Then, in the sixties, it was decided to build an Aviator park here, which has a very interesting layout. The runway of the former airfield turned into Novo-Izmailovsky Avenue.

In 1968, in this cultural place, it was decided to erect a monument to military pilots having a height of twelve meters.

Description of the park

This area consists of many winding and bizarre paths and alleys, and its area is more than thirty hectares. In the heart of this cultural place, its main attraction is located - a pond, on the banks of which the famous monument is located. Thus, this monument is visible from any corner of the park.

The whole area is planted with deciduous trees, but there is also a small alley on which only spruce trees grow.

Aviators Park is always full of vacationers, as here you can practice any kind of sport, take walks to improve your health or simply spend your leisure time in nature and breathe fresh air.





Active and fun pastime.

In St. Petersburg, there are a considerable number of park areas, but this one is most suitable for cycling. There is a pair of paved paths that are 750 meters long. Also in this place it will be convenient to ride on skateboards and scooters.

Aviators Park (St. Petersburg) is also ideal for jogging and Finnish walking, as its perimeter is two and a half kilometers.

There is also a volleyball court and a basketball court with rings, which are especially popular among young people. Football lovers will also find something to do in the park, they will be able to drive the ball on a football pitch equipped with a goal.

In addition to many places where you can organize outdoor activities, the park even has the opportunity to do fitness or yoga in group classes, which are held there weekly.





What else can you do?

It is officially forbidden to fry kebabs and make bonfires in the park zone in order to avoid pollution of the surrounding area and burning of trees. But this does not mean that there is no way to arrange a vacation in nature with delicious goodies, and for this it is not necessary to cook something in the Aviators park.

You can simply take a picnic basket there , after putting a lot of different and tasty sandwiches in it, and then with your family or friends go to a wonderful pond to enjoy the atmosphere of peace and tranquility prevailing there.

The main alley of the park is especially good for walking mothers with prams, and older children can be taught rollerblading and cycling, as this road has perfectly flat asphalt. In one secluded place of this territory there is even a cozy children's corner, where a trampoline is periodically installed.

The Aviators Park pond is popular with avid fishermen who fish here only for the sake of sports interest, since there are not a lot of fish here.





Park Features

This cultural place of St. Petersburg is located near the metro station "Victory Park", so it is very convenient to get there. It is not scary to walk here even in the evenings, since the main avenue has beautiful lighting, and everything is visible as if in the afternoon.

In the daytime, you can hide from the bright sun in the shade of trees and sit on the benches that are located under them. In general, the territory of the Aviator Park is clean and well-groomed, so many citizens here like to spend time.

Guest reviews

This place among many local residents of St. Petersburg is associated with the childhood years when they came here for a walk with their parents. Now, being already adults, many citizens, according to the old tradition, visit this park with their children. Almost all the inhabitants of the city like this territory, since there is no pathos here, as in other modern parks, and there is an atmosphere of peace, silence and complete unity with nature.





The pond is somewhat similar to a forest lake, and on its shore you can have a great time, regardless of the time of year. In the spring, for example, you can have a picnic or barbecue, and in the summer sunbathe with the whole family.

People who often visit it also like the fact that there is one small but cozy cafe in the park. You can have a tasty and inexpensive meal in it if you get hungry during your vacation.

Those who are fortunate enough to live near this place are very pleased that they can constantly admire its nature from its windows, the look of which perfectly removes all the accumulated stress. And in the morning to do jogs that improve well-being and charge positive energy for the whole day.

Interesting Facts

In 2005, the construction of a tennis school was planned at Aviators Park, but for this it was necessary to cut down a large number of trees growing on its territory.

The locals did not particularly like this, and they staged a protest rally, thanks to which they managed to stop cutting trees and keep the Aviators park safe.

How to get to the park?

This place is located in the Leningrad region, in the city of St. Petersburg, in its Moscow region. The closest metro station is Victory Park, from which the distance to the park zone is about one kilometer. It can be easily overcome with an unhurried and walking step along Basseinaya Street.

If you plan a trip to the park by car, then you can drive there along Novoizmaylovsky Prospekt, which you should call from Kuznetsovskaya Street. In the territory of this place there is a parking for up to fifty places, so leaving the car under supervision will not be difficult.





Any resident of St. Petersburg, of course, knows where the Aviators Park is located, the address will look like this: 196128, the city of St. Petersburg, Kuznetsovskaya Street.

This green corner of one of the districts of the city for all the time of existence is very fond of all residents. They visit him in order to relax there with the whole family or with friends and have a great time in nature.
