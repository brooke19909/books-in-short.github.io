Today we would like to introduce you to one of the four-star hotels of this resort called Tsokkos Paradise Village. The number of rooms of the hotel complex

As mentioned above, Tsokkos Paradise Village Hotel (Ayia Napa, Cyprus) has 136 rooms located in small cottages. The hotel complex is represented by single apartments and cozy studios. The rooms are simply furnished, but bright, stylish and modern. Each room has a bathroom with bath amenities, air conditioning, TV (there are also channels with Russian language broadcasting), minibar, telephone, kitchenette and balcony. On the floor - carpet. Nutrition

When reserving a room in Tsokkos Paradise Village (Cat. A) 4 * (Cyprus), you can choose the power system: half board or all-inclusive. You can have breakfast, lunch and dinner in the main restaurant of the hotel. Here you will be offered both familiar dishes and delights of local cuisine. Beach and sea

As already mentioned, despite the fact that the Tsokkos Paradise Village (Cat. A) 4 * hotel is located almost on the coast, it does not have its own beach. This fact gives tourists the opportunity to choose a place to stay to your taste. After all, Ayia Napa is famous for some of the best beaches in all of Cyprus! By the way, many of them are marked with a special UNESCO sign in the form of a blue flag, symbolizing their environmental cleanliness. The most popular local recreational areas are Nissi Beach with incendiary parties and DJs, a calm and respectable family holiday, Makronissos Beach, and the picturesque Grecian Bay. On all beaches, travelers are offered comfortable loungers, sun umbrellas, as well as plenty of opportunities for water entertainment and sports.

Pool

The hotel complex Tsokkos Paradise Village (Ayia Napa) has a large open-air pool with crystal clear fresh water. Next to it is a sun terrace with comfortable sun loungers and parasols, as well as a bar with a wide selection of drinks and snacks. In addition, the hotel also has an indoor pool, as well as a tank designed for safe bathing of the youngest guests.





Entertainment at the hotel

In addition to swimming in the pool and sunbathing on the sun terrace, the Tsokkos Paradise Village Hotel also has a gym, sauna and jacuzzi. In addition, guests have the opportunity to play tennis, ping pong, billiards, darts.

Throughout the day, guests of Tsokkos Paradise Village (Cyprus) are entertained by animators offering to do aerobics, aqua aerobics, play fun games and take part in funny contests. In the evenings, the hotel hosts vibrant shows and discos.

The hotel took care of the leisure of the youngest travelers. So, for them there is a special pool, a playground, as well as a mini club. In the evening, the children will be happy to dance in a mini-disco.

Hotel reviews by Russians

Since many travelers, when choosing a hotel, are often guided by the impression of people who have already stayed in it, we will give generalized comments from our compatriots regarding their stay at Tsokkos Paradise Village. In general, it can be noted that for the most part Russians were satisfied with their stay at this hotel.

As for the rooms themselves, our tourists found them quite spacious, bright, clean and well furnished. The furniture here is not new, but it is in very good condition. A controversial issue is always nutrition. As for this hotel, most of the guests were satisfied. True, breakfast seemed to our compatriots a little monotonous, but the quality and taste of the food did not cause any complaints.
