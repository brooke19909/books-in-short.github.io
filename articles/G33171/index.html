Hotel Fishermen's Harbor Urban Resort 5 * (Thailand / Phuket): photos and reviews of tourists

Today, many people choose such an exotic and interesting country as Thailand as their vacation destination. It is beautiful, picturesque, there are numerous beaches that tourists love so much. But in order to rest was a success, this is not enough. Need a good hotel. Many decide to choose a 5-star option. Well, one of the best in this category is Fishermen's Harbor Urban Resort 5 *.





Briefly about the hotel

This complex was built and opened in 2010. Not so long ago, in 2015, they made repairs here. And the hotel was renamed - before it was known under a different name. Fishermen's Harbor Urban Resort 5 * is a popular hotel complex for many reasons. And one of the main ones is its location.

The hotel is located near Patong Beach, which is the most popular in Phuket. You can reach it in 5-7 minutes. About 3.5 kilometers away is another famous Thai beach - Karon Beach.

Phuket Airport, by the way, is also relatively close. It can be reached in 30-40 minutes. And in the immediate vicinity of the hotel there is a Phuket Simon cabaret, Patong pier, Banana Walk shopping center and many other interesting places.

Service list

Fishermen's Harbor Urban Resort 5 *, like every self-respecting hotel, provides guests with everything that can be necessary. It offers free Wi-Fi and private parking, a luggage storage room, a tour desk and a currency exchange service. The reception is open 24/7, so individual check-out and check-in are possible here.

Some people want to come to rest with children and are wondering if the hotel has conditions for them. Well, here for the kids organized a play area indoors. You can also call a nanny who will sit with the child while the parents are resting.

In addition to the above, in Fishermen's Harbor Urban Resort 5 * there is a laundry with dry cleaning, a conference room, a business center, transfer services, smoking areas, delivery of magazines, food and drinks directly to the apartments, as well as amenities for guests with disabilities. As you can see, the list is impressive. Needless to say, there are even provided VIP-services. By the way, the staff here, in addition to Thai, also speaks Chinese and English.





Entertainment

Of course, many tourists go to Thailand in order to get acquainted with the local culture, explore the sights and enjoy a beach holiday. But, in principle, in Fishermen's Harbor Urban Resort 5 * you can have fun without leaving the hotel.

There are two pools - one small and the other large, with salt water. There is also a terrace where guests can sunbathe.

The hotel also has its own SPA center. There are treatment and massage rooms, as well as rooms where sessions are held for those people who came to the SPA together, as a couple. Here you can try a lot. For example, massage (Thai or deep, muscle), body wraps, facial treatments (both cosmetic and therapeutic), aromatherapy or reflexology. You can also enjoy the jacuzzi here. And, by the way, there is an excellent gym with modern fitness equipment necessary for the organization of full-fledged physical activity.

And yet, of course, there are such standard entertainment as darts and table tennis.

Nutrition

I would like to say a few words about this topic. In Fishermen's Harbor Urban Resort Patong 5 * there is an excellent restaurant and two bars - one ordinary, and the second with snacks and soft drinks. The third is near the pool.

The guests who were here say that breakfast is delicious, albeit without frills. As, however, in all other hotels. There are hot dishes (omelets and scrambled eggs), warm fresh pastries, freshly squeezed juices, fruits in variety. Good coffee in large quantities - it is poured by the waiter, you just have to call him to the table. Anyway, the drinks here are high-quality and undiluted, which pleases.

If you want variety, you can go to the night market. The hotel is very well located, it is nearby. And there you can not only buy fruit and other food at modest prices, but also order dinner (seafood is especially popular here), which will be prepared immediately at customers' disposal.

Helpful information

Fishermen's Harbor Urban Resort 5 *, photos of which are provided above, is a very popular hotel. Therefore, in order to reserve exactly the number that you want, and not choose from the remaining ones, it is better to deal with the issue of reservation in advance. There are only 390 apartments, but the hotel is rarely free.

Settlement starts at 14:00, and ends at noon. Here you can live with children. If people came with a child who is not two years old, then he will not have to pay extra. In addition, the administration will provide a cot.

A child up to 12 years old can stay for free. But if he is over two years old, you will have to sleep on one of those beds that are in the room. Need an extra bed? It will be installed, but then for a day from above you will have to pay extra 500 TNV (about 890 rubles). If a person over 12 years old has arrived with guests, then for his accommodation and an extra bed, it will be necessary to add 1,000 TNV (approximately 1,780 rubles) per day from above.

And that another bed is needed, the administration should be warned in advance. Guests must be sent confirmation of the Fishermen's Harbor Urban Resort 5 * hotel providing this service.
