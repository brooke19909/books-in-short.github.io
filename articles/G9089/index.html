Knitting lessons: pearl elastic with knitting needles. Ways of implementation and useful recommendations

The pattern connected by knitting by the method of alternating the front and back loops forming the stripes is called an elastic band. Such a canvas is dense and elastic. A product made with a similar pattern holds its shape well. There are a lot of varieties of such a pattern: simple elastic, English, hollow, pearl. The technology for performing the latter is discussed in this article.

How to knit pearl elastic with knitting needles, we read in the following description. On the needles we dial an odd number of loops.

  • 1st row: 1 edge loop is removed without knitting, 1 loop is front, 1 is seamy until the end of the row.
  • 2nd row: edge, remove the back loop , while making a crochet, we knit the 1 front loop with the front loop. This alternation of loops is performed until the end of the row.
  • 3rd row: hem, 1 loop with a crochet we knit the front of the front wall, 1 purl is seamy. In this way we draw up the row to the end.
  • 4th row: repeat the pattern from the second row.

Description of knitting pearl gum in a circle

When performing seamless products in circular rows, the design technology of the patterns changes somewhat. And all because with this method of knitting there are no wrong rows. Work is performed only on the front side of the product. The following instructions describe how to knit (pearl elastic) in a circle.

  • 1st row (auxiliary): alternating one front and one wrong loops.
  • 2nd row: the front loop is performed on the front, the crochet, the wrong side is removed without knitting. So we design the pattern to the end of the row.
  • 3rd row: the front is knitted to the front, the wrong side with the yarn is made the wrong side.

Further, the "pearl elastic" pattern with knitting needles is performed again from the second row.

Pattern application

This figure is used to design sleeve sleeves, neckline, bottom hem. They also use it in the creation of such accessories as scarves, hats, hoods. Useful tips when designing products with patent rubber

  1. If you plan to use this particular pattern for cuffs or necks, then for knitting the main fabric, select a pattern with a suitable structure in volume and density.
  2. The knitted "pearl gum" pattern with knitting needles (diagram and photo confirming this) forms a corrugated canvas. Therefore, the product made by him will add you a few centimeters in volume. Consider this fact when doing work.
  3. The clothes connected by such a pattern cannot be hung up after washing, as the fabric can stretch out. Dry the product should be laid out on a horizontal surface.
