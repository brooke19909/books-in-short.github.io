Jokingly and seriously: how to make a water bomb

Remember the "man in his prime" who was always ready to be mischievous, play a fool, fool around? He even made "the biggest and loudest" plop "in the world!" Well of course this is Carlson! Himself a "gunsmith"

Let's discuss how to make a water bomb, for the first time the easiest. What do you need for this? A plastic bag made from milk, kefir or sour cream. Pour water into it. Clamp the hole with a clothespin. Climb higher - to the balcony of the 5-6th floor, for example. Lift the bag over your head, wait for someone to pass under you. Calculated that the flight path will correspond to the movement? Then unclench your hands, and let your victim already think about how to make a water bomb.

Sponge, start over!



For creative and original-minded people (namely, these are our children) it will not be difficult to come up with something funny almost from the air. And even more so, it is not a problem for them to solve the problem to which our article is devoted. For example, do you know how to make a water bomb from ordinary sponges for bathing or washing dishes? But even a 5-6 year old kid, imagine, he knows! What is needed for this? A few sponges (at least five) to connect in a bunch, strung on a harsh thread. Gather water in a basin and put the workpiece into it. And then follow the instructions outlined in the paragraph above. That is, also rise to a higher floor, at least to the roof. Basin, of course, taking with him. Wait for the passerby and drop your weapon on him, just do not squeeze. The more fluid is absorbed into it, the stronger the ‚Äúplop‚Äù will be. Here's how to make a water bomb from the most elementary improvised means!

Paper Throwing Shells



And now something more interesting, more original. Imagine that it is possible to make projectiles from paper. Yes, yes, from it, and fill with liquid, and throw at the alleged or obvious enemy. To do this, origami art will come to your aid. A water bomb is made of paper according to a certain pattern. The sheet is taken square in shape and bent diagonally on both sides. Then, bending the corners and fixing them, you get a ball hollow inside. Fill it with water and quickly use as directed! Otherwise, the paper will get wet, and the effect of the projectile will turn against you. But, if you have time, you can admire with what "bang" the bomb will burst! In a word, it will be fun!

Exploding balls



And finally, water bombs from balls. This is already elementary. Take ordinary rubber balls, preferably round. Substitute them under the tap and fill with water, of course, cold. Try to have so much fluid that the ball does not stretch too much. Otherwise, he simply breaks, not having time to complete his mission. After collecting the required amount, tie a hole with a thread, as if you had done with an inflated ball. Instead of balls, ordinary fingertips or even thin medical rubber gloves will do. The scheme is still the same: filled, tied up, aimed, fired. And enjoy the effect of an exploding bomb!
