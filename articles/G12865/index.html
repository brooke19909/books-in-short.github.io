Base "Long Lake" (Moscow region): description and reviews

Moscow region is famous for its luxurious coniferous and deciduous forests, clear lakes and sources of mineral waters. There are a lot of convenient recreation centers near the capital, and one of the most popular with tourists is the Long Lake.

Location Features

The complex "Long Lake" (Moscow region) is located in the middle of the mixed forest of Dmitrovsky district. By the variety of landscapes, the area around the base can be called one of the most interesting in the Moscow region. The territory of this region consists of two pronounced zones - low and elevated. Guests of the base can admire the picturesque surroundings not only from its territory. If desired, vacationers can visit the hilly area near the village of Rakovo - the highest in the Moscow Region (228 m above sea level).

The base is located on Lake Dolgoe, stretched from the northwest to the southeast and connected by a picturesque channel with about. Round. The nearest settlements to the base are the village of Rybaki and Ozeretskoye. The rest house belongs to the Ministry of the Interior.

Center Infrastructure

Active recreation is organized for tourists on the territory of the Long Lake complex (Moscow region): in the winter, vacationers can go ice-skating or skiing, in summer guests take horseback rides around the base. The car can be parked for free.

Of course, guests of the base can swim in the lake if desired. It is located 50 m from the buildings of the complex. The beach is clean, sandy, equipped.

Pure water and a large number of fish is famous for Dolgoe Lake (Moscow Region). Fishing in this pond will certainly be fun. You can rent the necessary equipment directly at the base.

In one of the buildings of the complex is equipped with a large conference room that can accommodate up to 120 people at a time. If necessary, this premises can be rented, for example, for a training, briefing or meeting of the board of shareholders.

Guests who come to rest at this base can not worry about the safety of their children or their own. The territory of the complex is guarded around the clock. This base works year-round without holidays and weekends. Pets are not allowed on the property.





Dining room complex

"Long Lake" (Moscow region) is a recreation center, offering its guests rooms including a boarding house. The dining room on the territory of the Long Lake complex belongs to the base. The administration of the center rents it out for receptions and banquets. On the territory of the base are equipped gazebos with barbecue facilities.

Treatment

The Long Lake base (Moscow region) is oriented towards the usual quiet rest surrounded by nature. However, some tourists come here in order to heal a bit.

Dmitrovsky district is an ecologically clean place. The air here is not polluted by any industrial emissions or car exhausts. In the Dolgoy itself, which belongs to the group of glacial lakes, contains a little radon, so swimming in it is very good for health. A small medical center, of course, operates on the territory of this base.

Guest reviews

The impressions of the guests about the Long Lake complex exist both good and not very good. Most guests find the rooms quite comfortable. The furniture in the rooms is comfortable, in good condition, household appliances are working properly. Of course, there are not very good reviews about rooms in the base buildings. They are connected primarily with the fact that repairs in some rooms were carried out for a very long time.

Not too flattering reviews are sometimes received by the staff of the Long Lake base. Unfortunately, the employees of the complex sometimes treat their guests not too warmly.

The disadvantage of this center, some guests consider the rather high cost of living.





Dolgoe Lake itself (Moscow region), the tourist reviews deserved just excellent. The water in it is really clean and is always warm in the summer. Swimming in the lake on the beach of the complex is convenient not only for adults, but also for children.

How to get there

The base is located, as already mentioned, about 25 km from the Moscow Ring Road. Vacationers usually come here in their own cars. The easiest way to get to the complex from Moscow is along the Leningradskoye Shosse. Follow this road to the village of Black Dirt, then turn right and move through the village of Myshetskoe to Lake Krugloye. Drive along this reservoir to the village of Rybaki (following the sign "Zarya"). Then turn left and go all the way.

You can also get to the base "Long Lake" (Moscow region) and Dmitrov highway. In this case, you need to turn to Lobnya near the village of Krasnaya Gorka. On Rogachevskoe highway you should go to the village of Ozeretskoye, and then turn towards the village of Rybaki.





If necessary, you can get to the Long Lake base by public transport. In this case, in Moscow, you first need to get to Savelovsky Station and take the train to Lobny. In the village you will need to wait for the bus going to the Long Lake holiday home.
