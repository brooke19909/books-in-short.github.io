Hotel Sentido Graceland Khao Lak Resort Spa 5 * Thailand, Phang Nga: photos and reviews of tourists

A little over ten years ago, a hotel was opened in one of the most popular resorts in Thailand , which became known as the Sentido Graceland Khao Lak Resort Spa. The 5-star complex gained popularity quite quickly. And he still uses it. Tens of thousands of tourists come here every year. Why does everyone like this hotel so much? Well, it's worth talking about this in more detail.





Location

Sentido Graceland Khao Lak Resort Spa is located in a place called Khao Lak. Initially, it was a small cozy village located on the coast of the bay of the same name. But, when the tourism industry began to develop, then nearby settlements were also referred to Khao Lak. So the resort has increased significantly in size.

The airport is 65 kilometers from the hotel. Just like Phuket. And if visitors to this popular resort can have a good time immersed in the nightlife of Thailand, then Khao Lak is an ideal place to relax in a quiet and calm atmosphere and enjoy magnificent landscapes. And yet this resort is famous for its amazingly clean beaches with white sand and clear sea water.

Largely due to its location, this hotel has become famous. Sentido Graceland Khao Lak Resort Spa has a private beach area that is within easy reach. From here, you can drive to the city center and Khao Lak Lam Rou National Park in 10 minutes.

Facilities and service

Sentido Graceland Khao Lak Resort Spa, like any other self-respecting hotel, has everything that can make guests relax as comfortable and relaxed as possible.

There is free high-speed Wi-Fi, private parking, concierge, 24-hour reception, separate luggage storage, laundry with dry cleaning, business center, tour desk and conference room. In addition, guests can get food and drinks directly to their rooms. People who come to rest with children are invited to sit.

For body and soul

Sentido Graceland Khao Lak Resort Spa 5 * also, as the name implies, has its own spa center. There is a hammam, a bath and a sauna, as well as a jacuzzi and several massage rooms. Sukko is translated as "harmony of mind and body." And every guest staying here must definitely visit this spa center. Indeed, it is not for nothing that Sukko are the best SPA-complexes in Asia.

Here you can enjoy Thai massage, body wraps, beauty treatments, steam room, aromatherapy, reflexology and much more.
