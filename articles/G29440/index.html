Activation iphone 4s

If you first bought a world-famous smartphone with a bitten apple on the back, then you may have questions about what Iphone activation is . Indeed, in the products of competing brands HTC, Samsung, etc., activation is not applied. But that's the whole Apple, guys know how to create difficulties for their users out of the blue.

So, the topic of this article is how to activate iphone 4s, what steps you need to go through to successfully complete this procedure and what you need to do to get an Apple ID (Apple account).

The material is suitable for verification with any phone running iOS 5 (it defaults to 4S).

First, prepare a micro sim.

Since iPhone activation is possible only through the Internet, you need to worry about finding a Wi-Fi zone, or connect using the cable that is included with the computer, (I hope it is clear that it must be connected to the World Wide Web). Then download iTunes.

If the phone is connected to the network, carefully read the instructions that appear on the screen and follow them. First you should choose the country and interface language (I advise you to choose Russia and Russian).

The next question will be: Allow me to determine the location of the phone (useful if you plan to use maps and navigation). Here you can either allow or reject the offer, in any case later, in the phone menu, you can change this parameter.

All the following menus and questions can be passed, or you can skip, they are not required. The main activation of iphone 4s was successful. But in order to feel the full power of the iPhone 4S, I recommend that you go through the remaining points to the end.

So, we reconnect to the Internet via Wi-Fi or GPRS, log in with your ID if you have one, or create a new one (it's free) and enable or disable Siri (voice assistant).
