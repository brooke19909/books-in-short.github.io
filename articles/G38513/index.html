Bank "DeltaCredit", mortgage: reviews, conditions, interest rate

If you believe the reviews, a mortgage from DeltaCredit is one of the most profitable and convenient ways to purchase your own home or improve your living conditions. This is a large financial organization that offers a loan for the purchase of an apartment or its share, a private house at reduced rates. Advising DeltaCredit customers, bank managers focus on profitable mortgage products and the rules of paperwork. Each person who plans to take on long-term contractual obligations must adequately understand and evaluate their capabilities not only at the moment, but also for the future years.

Mortgage loans

This is the only financial institution in Russia that specializes exclusively in mortgage lending. The company offers potential borrowers the most comfortable conditions for housing loans and a variety of programs. The lender offers:

  • relatively low interest rates (from 8.25%);
  • comfortable conditions for young families and holders of family capital;

  • personal account, where in online mode the user will be able to view information on current payments, balances, schedules, etc.

This year began for this financial organization with major changes. Against the background of a decrease in the nationwide key rate, the DeltaCredit mortgage in 2018 also underwent a number of changes. To date, the bank offers to issue a loan at 8.25% per annum. At a favorable rate, you can get a loan for the construction of a private house, the purchase of an apartment in the secondary market or in a new building, as well as the purchase of a garage, apartments, land, etc.

This bank can conclude an agreement on refinancing a mortgage paid on less suitable terms. According to reviews, a mortgage in DeltaCredit is most often executed on the security of own, existing property.

How the rate is calculated: discounts and allowances

To get a loan on the most comfortable conditions, the consumer must meet the requirements that the bank makes to borrowers. Despite the fact that this financial institution sometimes provides a mortgage without a down payment, DeltaCredit Bank has priority in customers who are able to pay immediately at least 5% of the loan body. Moreover, the need to make the first payment and its size is determined by a number of factors:

  • type of housing purchased;
  • the presence of real estate owned by the borrower;
  • experience at the last place of work;
  • income over the past six months, etc.

If the borrower plans to make maternity capital as an initial contribution. In this case, the payment should not exceed 15% of the total cost of the loan facility. The maximum mortgage term can reach 25 years.

The reason for the increase in commissions and allowances may be:

  • inability to confirm official income, provide a certificate from the employer;
  • providing data on earnings in a banking form;
  • individual entrepreneurship;
  • refusal of the borrower from life insurance, health.

Many customers in their reviews complain that the favorable low rate on billboards and the company's official website do not always correspond to the numbers of the real contract.

Who can become a borrower?

The mortgage terms at DeltaCredit cannot be called unhindered, but nevertheless this bank is very popular among borrowers due to the loyalty and variety of loan programs. The persons applying for a mortgage loan are subject to a number of general requirements:

  • age limits (the borrower must be at least 21 years old and not older than 65 years);
  • the presence of official experience, confirmed by a work book or contract;
  • actual solvency;
  • citizenship of the Russian Federation.

It is worth noting that the term "age limit" means not only the age at which the borrower has the right to participate in the transaction, but also to complete the payment of the loan. For guarantors and co-borrowers, who are often relatives and close family members of the client, similar requirements are presented. In order to obtain a more favorable mortgage rate, DeltaCredit allows the applicant to attract no more than three guarantors.

In the predominant number of cases, an initial payment is required, the amount of which is established depending on the estimated value of the acquired real estate, the type of financing and the applicable loan scheme. According to the terms of the bank, any citizen who has expressed a desire to participate in the payment of mortgage payments is entitled. Sometimes the bank makes concessions and approves the application for a loan without confirmation of labor activity, the presence of a permanent registration in the region of real estate acquisition.

Special offers from the bank

In addition to the general conditions, DeltaCredit offers potential borrowers to participate in promotional programs and take advantage of peculiar benefits. These include:

  • The so-called mortgage holidays - the period during which the payment under the contract can be reduced by almost half. According to reviews of DeltaCredit, the bank allows you to pay a mortgage under such a scheme within 1-3 years.
  • Making a loan according to the passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation. To consider the application, the client will need to provide a copy of the passport and fill out a special form. The decision is made within three business days.
  • No down payment. Only those borrowers who already own real estate and are ready to provide it as collateral can purchase housing without start-up capital. The annual rate will be determined by the amount of the down payment, and the loan amount cannot exceed 80% of the value of the collateral.

What difficulties arise during refinancing?

Selling mortgage products to many banks is the primary source of income. Clients using credit services are forced to pay interest to financial institutions. Thus, borrowers reward the bank for a loan and many years of service. Moreover, in some cases, customers can count on refinancing a mortgage.

DeltaCredit offers borrowers to conclude new agreements on more convenient terms. Refinancing helps not only attract customers to the bank, but also make housing loans more affordable. This service implies a special procedure for re-registering a mortgage, during which some obligations are replaced by others. Thanks to refinancing, clients have the opportunity to close the debt on an existing loan with new credit funds issued on more favorable terms.

When deciding on re-lending, the client must take into account all the nuances and understand how appropriate it is to conclude a new contract. DeltaCredit, approving the application for refinancing the existing debt, agrees to serve the client and to carry out early repayment of his debt to another financial institution. Based on the agreement, as a rule, a more loyal interest rate is set.

To document the contract, the client must provide documents confirming ownership of the mortgage property. The bank considers the application for refinancing individually, the decision depends on a number of factors:

What is needed for refinancing

First of all, the borrower must provide:

  • passport;

  • a statement of the absence of debt to the previous creditor bank;

A client who needs a new loan to pay off old debt needs to fill out an application form. The borrower will again have to go through all the verification and registration procedures that he went through when registering a mortgage. The bank consultant will prepare a list of documents to be prepared by the applicant. After the client collects the necessary package of securities and transfers it to DeltaCredit, a decision will be made within 5 days to approve or refuse to grant a loan for the following reasons:

DeltaCredit, like any other bank, will not want to contribute to the loss of its own profit.
