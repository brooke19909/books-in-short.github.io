Favorite hobby, or What can I collect?





Collecting today is rightfully recognized as one of the most popular and entertaining hobbies in the whole world. It should be noted that, in essence, this hobby is not thoughtless gathering of any homogeneous objects, but implies a conscious study of the history of the appearance and subtleties of the modern existence of collection items, the identification of a certain classification of things. The high degree of development of modern technologies and the Internet provide ample opportunities to search for the most diverse and very rare items, so it‚Äôs much easier to start collecting anything today than, for example, twenty years ago.



Where to begin?

First you need to decide that you are really interested in how much time and money you are willing to spend on this fascinating hobby. And only then try to decide what you can collect exactly in your case. Because when you start collecting something, it is very undesirable for you to suddenly lose interest in everything halfway through. I want this hobby to become a thing of a lifetime, so that the items collected are in sufficient quantities, because the activities of these collectors are sometimes truly grandiose in scope, many of them have already taken pride of place in the Guinness Book of Records. The question of what can be collected is at a certain moment able to arise before everyone, since both young and old are interested in this hobby.



This hobby is a kind of game that fills our free time, this is undisguised pleasure received from shifting, browsing and admiring our treasures, this is an anxious expectation of a new instance that will replenish the collection. In fact, you can collect anything you want - from match labels and bottle caps to antiques, luxury cars, yachts and helicopters. There are completely unusual collections of passports, ballpoint pens, transport tickets, adhesive plasters, movie cameras, handcuffs, calculators and even toilet lids.

Collection cost

When deciding what to collect, it is necessary to be guided not only by personal preferences, but also by financial capabilities. So, for wealthy people, the question of what can be collected does not have a certain framework; they can stop their choice on anything. As a rule, these are old books, paintings, weapons, antiques, expensive wines and cigars. Some exhibits reach a value of tens, or even hundreds of thousands of dollars. However, collections consisting of simpler things can be no less interesting and original: unusual bottles, badges, corks, butterflies, natural stones, candy or ice cream wrappers.



Collecting Rules

The collection of objects of one particular type, as a rule, has its own name, its own rules. Do not think that you can collect stamps by simply holding them in a drawer on a table, in a heap, without systematizing, this is not a collection, this is rubbish. These philatelists follow all storage rules, they will never take the stamp with their bare hands, for this there are special tweezers. Copies are stored in special albums, and the time and place of acquisition of each can be found in special registers of the collection.

It is easier for adults to decide on the choice of collectibles, our tastes and preferences are already formed. However, if we want to instill this hobby in our child, we must definitely help him, suggest options, and what to stop on, let him decide. The girl can collect porcelain figurines, shells, miniature dolls, soft toys, buttons, butterflies, jewelry. Boys will be interested in model cars and planes, soldiers, stamps, music CDs, natural stones, stickers, bottle caps or key chains.
