Design, dimensions and controls

So, before us is Honor 7. The review should begin with the appearance of the device. The smartphone received a classic design, a rectangular shape, smoothed corners, as well as a large screen, which occupies almost the entire space of the front panel - 76.7%. The device looks decent. There are no distracting elements, such as decorative inserts or inscriptions, on the front surface. The front camera and its flash were made equal in size and positioned at the same distance from the speaker. Dimensions successful - 143 x 71 x 8.5 mm. The device is compact. He is not too thin, but he cannot be called fat either. Weight - 157 grams. The device is convenient to hold in your hands, it does not slip. The front side is protected by Corning Gorilla Glass 3. An oleophobic coating is present. It is very effective. The prints remain, however, they are easily erased. The finger glides easily on the work surface. Around the perimeter there is a thin edging, which is made of special glossy plastic. The back side is partially made of aluminum. The corners are polished, the material is rough. The manufacturer claims that the lid has a ceramic coating. Bottom and top there are a couple of plastic inserts. They have antennas. Thus, the problem of blocking the signal by hand is solved. If the quality of the connection deteriorates, the smartphone activates the antenna, which provides better reception. Additional energy is not wasted during this. The inserts have a relief pattern.





The following colors are available for sale : silver metallic and gray graphite. The case does not disassemble. The battery is built-in. The assembly is excellent. Above, in the center of the frontal part, there is a speech speaker. Its volume is high. The interlocutor is heard perfectly. The tone is pleasant: there are low and high frequencies. On the left is the front camera, light and proximity sensors. On the right is a single-section LED flashlight for highlighting when shooting on the front camera.

The developers did not forget about the indicator of missed events. The bottom side sheltered the microphone, speaker and microUSB connector. Above there is a 3.5 mm audio output, as well as an IR transmitter. There was also a place for a second microphone. The power button and volume key are placed on the right. On the left panel there is another mechanical button that serves to activate various applications, supports double, short or long press. A little higher is a metal slot in which a pair of nano-SIM cards is placed. A microSD is installed nearby. On the back there is a camera eye that extends a few millimeters above the body. Here is an LED flash, as well as a fingerprint scanner, which has a square shape and smoothly bent corners. The convex inscription Honor is also provided. The sensor is capable of recognizing a fingerprint that is applied at an arbitrary angle. The minimum speed for unlocking a smartphone is - 0.5 seconds. Implemented a self-learning algorithm. The frequency of unlocking the smartphone speeds up its recognition. The sensor can be turned into a button for taking photos, turning off the alarm or receiving a call.

Display

So we found out what Honor 7 looks like. We continue the review with a detailed description of the screen. Its diagonal is 5.2 inches. Furthermore, the physical size is 65 x 115 mm. The top frame is 12.5 mm, the bottom is 15 mm, and on the sides is 3.5 mm. There is a fairly effective anti-reflective coating. Display Resolution - FullHD - 1080 x 1920 pixels. The pixel density is 424 per inch, with an aspect ratio of 16: 9. IPS matrix has no air gap. The maximum brightness is 380 cd / m2 (white color). Contrast Ratio - 860: 1.





Battery

Let's see how long the battery life of the Honor 7 model can be. An overview of the battery is given below. The device uses a non-removable lithium-ion battery, its capacity is 3100 mAh. According to the manufacturer, the Huawei Honor 7 smartphone is able to work 30 hours under load or 60 hours in light mode. In practice, the device can function independently for about two days during traditional use. The battery density is 600 W * h / l. Thanks to SmartPower 3.0 technology, it is possible to save up to 30 percent of energy through software and hardware optimization.





Communication opportunities

The Honor 7 phone works on second and third generation networks, and also supports 4G CAT 6 - 300 Mbps. There is an infrared transmitter and Wi-Fi standard ac. The device also has Bluetooth 4.1, USB 2.0, GPS and even GLONASS.

Memory

The device has 3 GB of RAM. The built-in flash memory here is 16 gigabytes. To store data and applications, 10 GB is provided. The smartphone is equipped with two camera modules: Sony IMX230 at 20 MP and 8 MP.
