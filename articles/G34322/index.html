Hotel "Agapinor Hotel 3" (Cyprus, Paphos): location, description and reviews of tourists

Of course, holidays on the island of Cyprus are very popular. Tourists from all over the world flock here to enjoy a wonderful beach holiday and high-class service. And the city of Paphos offers its guests an exciting journey into the ancient world, shrouded in riddles and myths.

Today, many travelers are interested in Agapinor Hotel 3. After all, this hotel complex is famous for its hospitality and comfort. Where is the hotel located? Hotel Agapinor Hotel 3: location





The island of Cyprus is known worldwide for its unique nature, mild climate and numerous beaches. Agapinor Hotel 3 is located in the legendary city of Paphos, which is located in the southwestern part of the island.

The location of the hotel is its undoubted advantage. After all, the hotel complex was built almost in the center of the resort of Paphos, or rather between the Old and New parts of the city. The hotel is located on a rocky hill, so it offers breathtaking views of the Mediterranean Sea and high cliffs. And the mountain air brings coolness.

The nearest airport is approximately 11 kilometers from the hotel complex. And in a few minutes you can reach the center of the Old Town with all its markets, historical sights and, of course, modern entertainment.

Short description of the hotel and infrastructure





It is worth noting immediately that this hotel complex is considered relatively small. Its area is only 158 square meters. The main building consists of five floors, which accommodate rooms for vacationers. The building has two elevators.

Despite its modest size, the hotel area is well equipped - there are swimming pools, a terrace with sea and rock views, and places to relax. And, of course, everything is decorated with exotic plants and huge palm trees.

Who is suitable for rest at the hotel?

Of course, travelers are primarily interested in questions about whether the conditions of rest at Agapinor Hotel 3 are suitable for them. Paphos is a hospitable city that offers its guests a lot of entertainment. Both youth companies and elderly people come here.

The hotel is perfect for a relaxing beach holiday with children, as even the smallest travelers have excellent conditions here. Nevertheless, fans of noisy parties will also not be bored, because the city center can be reached in a few minutes - here you will find luxurious restaurants, bars, discos and clubs. In addition, near the hotel there is a large market and a shopping center that will appeal to all shopping lovers.

Accommodation conditions: description of rooms





Hotel Agapinor Hotel 3 has at its disposal a large five-story building, which houses rooms for tourists. In total, the hotel has 73 rooms. There are both standard single rooms and superior rooms, as well as large rooms for families.

Each room has a small private balcony with a plastic table and chairs, where you can meet the dawn, enjoying breathtaking views of the sea or watching life in the hotel.

Naturally, the rooms are equipped with the necessary amount of furniture and household appliances. Here you will find a large-screen TV with satellite channels. And also a telephone, a radio and a modern air conditioning system. The spacious bathroom has a shower, washbasin, hairdryer, and a set of clean towels and personal hygiene products. An ironing board and iron can be delivered to the room upon request. For a small fee, you can rent a safe for storing personal belongings.

The scheme of food on site





Of course, Agapinor Hotel 3 offers its guests a good meal. Buffets are served in the main hotel restaurant called Artemis. The hotel complex boasts a rich menu - here you will find local and international dishes, including fish, seafood, fresh fruits and vegetables, as well as light snacks and delicious desserts. By the way, theme nights are organized twice a week at the restaurant. And there is also a specially designed menu for children and vegetarians, which is also important.

Hotel guests are also invited to visit the local Agapinor bar, which offers a huge selection of drinks, cocktails and light snacks. Moreover, themed parties are also held here . Sometimes at night, discos or karaoke evenings are organized, where each guest can demonstrate their singing talents.

There is another bar by the pool where residents can enjoy refreshing drinks. You can have a good time in the local coffee shop. Tables are located on the outdoor terrace next to the pool.
