Secrets of arranging small-sized apartments





Can a small housing be comfortable? It all depends on the number of people living in the apartment, their everyday habits and needs. If desired, even on a minimum area, you can place all the necessary zones. Look at the smallest apartments in the world, their layouts vary, but the general design rules remain unchanged. Visually increase the volume of the room using light colors and glossy surfaces. The most suitable interior styles for decorating small apartments are minimalism and hi-tech. Refuse unnecessary decor in the interior.

It is also useful to regularly audit personal items and get rid of excess. This simple rule, combined with regular cleaning of the room, will get rid of the feeling of clutter and crowding.

Furniture transformers are manufactured today in many factories. And these are not only sofa beds that are familiar to us, but also modern partitions, folding tabletops and built-in berths. Such solutions will create the most comfortable living conditions even on a limited area.

Do not forget about the principle of vertical zoning. An attic bed will be an excellent alternative to a traditional sleeping place, if you want to make a podium, do not forget to create storage boxes in it. All these simple solutions perfectly save space and only increase the functionality of the room.
