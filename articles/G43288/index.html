Barry Boutique 5 * (China, Hainan): description of the hotel, reviews

Hainan is rightfully considered the best resort in China. It has everything you need for a first-class holiday: clean beaches, healing springs, developed tourist and urban infrastructure. The country's traditions attract tourists no less. So, unforgettable wedding and funeral ceremonies are held on the island, which are worth visiting for all travelers who have come here. Fans of a comfortable stay are increasingly choosing the Barry Boutique 5 * hotel. What attracts tourists to it?

Where is the hotel?

The hotel is located on about. Hainan at Dadonghai Bay Sanya Resort . It is built on a busy street in the city. Nearby there are many shops where tourists can buy not only souvenirs and food, but also clothes, shoes, jewelry at very low prices. At 250 meters there is a large grocery supermarket and cafe. The Chinese market is 3 km away. Private beach area is 300 meters away. It is separated by a highway. You can get to it on foot or take the free bus provided by the hotel.





The nearest international airport is 18 km from Barry Boutique Hotel 5 * (China, Sanya ). It takes about an hour to get to the hotel by bus. You can get a little faster by taxi, but the cost of the trip will increase significantly. The distance to the central part of the city is approximately 5 km. Sanya International Trade Center is 2 km away. A wax museum is 100 meters away . Deer Turned Head Natural Park is 2.5 km away on a high mountain, which can be seen from the hotel.

Brief information about the hotel

Barry Boutique 5 * can be called a modern hotel, because it was opened in 2012. Located in a high-rise building, it almost does not have its own territory. The building, built in a modern Asian style, has 33 floors. They accommodate almost 400 comfortable rooms. The complex is aimed at businessmen who come to China for work, as well as tourists who plan to spend most of their time outside the hotel. Settling with animals of any size is strictly prohibited. Children under 12 years old are free of charge, provided that they will sleep in the existing beds.





When registering at the hotel, you will be required to make a deposit, the amount of which is approximately $ 300. It must be returned after the end of the vacation, if the furniture and equipment in the room remain in good condition. Check-in here starts from 15:00 local time. The hotel has Russian-speaking staff, but most of the employees speak English and Chinese. You need to free rooms on the day of departure until noon. If you have free apartments, you can extend your stay for several hours at an additional cost.

Number of rooms

The hotel offers its guests 388 rooms. Most of them are designed for two people and a child. But there are also spacious apartments, which can simultaneously accommodate four adults and a child. The windows of the Barry Boutique 5 * Hotel (Hainan) overlook the city. The rooms located on the upper floors will delight their owner with an amazing sea view or mountain landscape. The interior of the apartment is decorated in a classic style. The walls are painted in soothing colors and ceramic tiles are laid on the floor. Some of the premises are equipped with a small balcony. There is also a bathroom equipped with a toilet, washbasin and shower. True, in some rooms it is separated from the main room by a thick curtain, and not by an interior door.





Guests are offered daily housekeeping . If desired, they can refuse it by hanging a warning sign on the door. Bed linen and towels are changed 2 times a week. If the laundry is dirty before this time, then you can crumple and put it on the floor so that the thing is replaced during cleaning. When checking into the rooms, tourists are invited to choose rooms with a shared double bed or separate beds.

Detailed equipment description

Each hotel room is equipped with the maximum number of necessary amenities to ensure guests a comfortable stay. Justifying its five-star status, the Barry Boutique Hotel 5 * hotel (China) equipped all rooms with modern flat-screen TVs. They are connected to cable TV, which broadcasts TV channels in Russian and English. If the weather is hot, tourists can use individual air conditioning. On cold days, on the contrary, the hotel staff includes heating in the building. Guests can leave their things in the dressing room and bedside tables. The rooms also have ironing boards and irons, an electronic safe, a pay phone and a minibar.

The bathroom is equipped not only with free toiletries (shampoo, conditioner, shower gel and soap), but bathrobes, slippers and a hair cap. Free towels and a hairdryer are provided. A large mirror hangs above the washbasin, with which you can shave or apply makeup. Some rooms are additionally equipped with a small kitchen. There is a minimal set of dishes for making tea or coffee, as well as an electric kettle.

Dining at the hotel

Depending on their own needs, guests of the Barry Boutique 5 * hotel can choose one of the following catering concepts. Most often, guests only pay for breakfast. This is a convenient option for those who spend most of their time outside the hotel. Optionally, you can also choose half board or full board. Accommodation without meals is possible.





There is a restaurant serving European cuisine on the sixth floor. This is where all breakfasts, lunches and dinners take place. A buffet is served for visitors. Free vegetarian meals are available. At dinner, tourists are served √† la carte. On the ninth floor there is a spacious banquet hall, from the windows of which a beautiful view of the sea opens. All kinds of events are held here, including European weddings and parties. There is also a bar and a wine room on site, where guests can enjoy refreshing drinks, including alcoholic drinks.

Infrastructure

The local infrastructure will appeal to all lovers of a comfortable stay in the highest class. The territory of the Barry Boutique 5 * hotel has a large business center, which hosts various conferences, presentations and business meetings. For an additional fee, hotel staff can take care of all organizational issues and table design. The center offers four conference rooms that can accommodate from 30 to 300 delegates. All rooms are equipped with modern computer equipment, including projectors, flipcharts and interactive screens. Vacationers can also hire experienced secretaries and translators.





Wi-Fi is free throughout the hotel . There is a 24-hour front desk with a currency exchange, luggage storage and electronic safe. Here you can book a shuttle to the airport or rent a car. Tourists can also use the services of the porter. Laundry and dry cleaning for an additional fee not only wash, but also iron your clothes. You can wait for your check-in or just relax in the common hall, equipped with leather sofas and a TV. It is worth noting that there is no parking on site. The hotel is equipped with modern elevators that will quickly deliver guests to even the very last floors.

Leisure activities

There are few entertainments in the hotel, so it is unlikely to suit those tourists who expect to spend most of the time in the hotel. It is open daily from 8:00 to 23:00. For a fee, guests can use the spa and sauna. There is also a small sun terrace, which houses free sun loungers and parasols.





The hotel has its own private beach stretch of 150 meters. There is a water entertainment center for tourists. For example, vacationers can snorkel. The hotel has a fitness center equipped with modern fitness equipment. The hotel also offers a billiards room, bicycle rental and hiking.

Conditions for children

Children staying at the Barry Boutique 5 * hotel receive a good discount on vacation. As mentioned above, children under 12 years old can stay at the hotel for free if they sleep on existing beds. An extra bed is available in the room for a fee. For babies a cradle is provided. In the restaurant, visitors can use the high chairs for feeding. In addition, children whose height does not exceed 1.2 meters can eat in the restaurant for free. For tall children, a 50% discount is offered. Younger guests of the restaurant can also entertain themselves in a specially equipped children's corner. There is also a mini club on site where parents can leave their children for a while. Animators entertain them by conducting sports and training events. True, it is worth considering that employees do not speak Russian well.

Positive opinions of guests

Tourists like to stay at the Barry Boutique 5 * hotel. They usually leave good reviews about him. According to guests, the complex stands out among other hotels with the following advantages:

  • The hotel serves good breakfasts. They include not only the standard European set (sandwiches, pastries, sausage and cheese slices, cereals), but also Chinese dishes.
  • Upon arrival, guests are given a beach bag with towels.
  • Clean, tidy rooms. The plumbing in the bathroom is new and works perfectly. The rooms smell of freshness.
  • Nice staff, although many of the employees hardly speak Russian or even English.
  • Good hotel location. Nearby there are shops, cafes, restaurants. The beach is within walking distance.
  • The rooms are regularly changed toiletries, replenished tea set.




Negative opinions of guests

But not all guests were satisfied with the time spent here. Many liked the stay, but they still noticed some of the shortcomings of the Barry Boutique Hotel (5 stars), which would be nice to fix. In their comments, they describe the following disadvantages of the hotel:

  • Elevators can not cope with the load. Sometimes you have to wait a long time to get to your floor.
  • Most of the hotel staff speaks only Chinese. Due to misunderstanding, a large number of problems arise.
  • Breakfast ends very quickly. If you come to the restaurant after 10:00, then you can completely stay hungry.
  • The street is very noisy. Even tightly closed windows do not save. The hum of cars, the screams of people and loud music are constantly heard.
  • Limited swimming area. The sea in this place is very shallow, therefore it is suitable only for bathing children.
  • Sometimes there are jellyfish in the water that can sting painfully.

General conclusions

If you decide to relax on about. Hainan, you can safely choose this hotel for your stay. It offers a developed infrastructure, modern functional rooms, a small entertainment program, as well as tasty and varied food. You can recommend the hotel to businessmen, families with children, youth companies and adult couples who prefer a respectable vacation. It is also suitable for tourists who return to the hotel only for the night to relax and sleep.
