Shekvetili Village (Georgia): Features of Rest

The village of Shekvetili in Georgia is considered one of the most picturesque places where there is every opportunity for a relaxing holiday. It is famous for its colorful pine forests and magnetic sands. If you are tired of the hustle and bustle of busy resorts and want to spend your holidays in seclusion and silence, you can safely go on vacation to Georgia, to Shekvetili. Reviews of tourists about this place are always enthusiastic. So what is this village so good for? Now we will tell.



Description

Shekvetili is located in the administrative district of Guria, in the resort region of Ozurgeti, between the city of Kobuleti and the village of Ureki, on the Black Sea. Indeed, when you come to rest in Georgia, in Shekvetili, and walk among the green alleys, neat houses, thickets of Pitsunda pines, it seems that everything here is made to order - the village seemed to have left the picture of a fairy tale book. That is why Shekvetili at first sight falls in love with travelers.

Historical reference

The history of the village dates back to the 17th century. Then at this place was a trading post in Western Georgia. At the beginning of the 19th century, the Russian army placed a border post here. During the Crimean War there was a fortified garrison, where about two thousand soldiers served.



In 1853, five thousand Turkish paratroopers attacked the garrison, many defenders of the fortification died. The rest, and there were not more than thirty of them, were able to escape into the forest and escape. After that, the garrison was abandoned for a long time, and then gradually began to take root in people. In the 20th century, a cozy village arose with well-groomed beaches and beautiful Georgian nature. Now in a quiet, cozy place it already does not remind of aggressive battles and wars.

Beaches

More than three kilometers stretches the coastline of Shekvetili. The Georgian village is known for healing magnetic sand, which has a beneficial effect on the body. The fact is that the local sand contains a lot of magnetite, which, when heated and in contact with human skin, can improve blood circulation and enhance metabolism. Tourists resting on the beaches of Shekvetili note a number of features, namely: the dark color of sand; low occupancy; Shallow depth (you can swim even during strong waves); gentle entry into the water; the presence of coniferous forest, which saturates the air with medicinal substances, thirty meters from the coast.

Weather

A favorable climate is another advantage of Shekvetili. Georgia generally has a mild climate, but here it is unique and inimitable due to the mixing of sea air and air descending from the Bakhmaro mountain range, which in combination with pine forest coniferous fluids gives the effect of oxygen enrichment with a large amount of ozone. People who visited the village say that they seemed to have both a rest on the sea and in the mountains at the same time.



The tourist season opens here in May, when the air warms up to +27 degrees, and the water temperature reaches +17 degrees. Summer in Shekvetili is hot, with rare rainfall and a large number of sunny days. In July-August, the water temperature reaches 25 degrees.

What to see in Shekvetili

The Georgian village has no iconic sights, but there are a lot of interesting things in the district. For example, the amusement park "Tsitsinatela" in the neighboring town of Kobuleti. The park is designed for visitors of all ages: there are extreme attractions and quiet fun for the whole family.

You can also go to the village of Shemokmedi, which is located eight kilometers from Ozurgeti, and visit the monastery, on the territory of which there are a domed church, a bell tower, and the Divine Basilica. Many travelers go here to see the oldest icon of the Transfiguration of God in Georgia.



Shekvetili (Georgia): reviews

Tourists who visited this village, as a rule, remain satisfied. All celebrate beautiful beaches, warm climate, relaxing holiday and healing magnetic sands. Thanks to these factors, families with children especially like to relax in Shekvetili. But there are those who do not really like here. Some travelers complain of increased humidity and remoteness from civilization. Others are unhappy with the lack of discos and nightclubs.

Tourists who are interested in visiting nightly entertainment venues should choose other Georgian resorts as their vacation destination. As already mentioned, Shekvetili goes precisely for a secluded, quiet vacation. Have a nice trip!
