Turkey has long been one of the most popular tourist destinations for citizens of the Russian Federation. Of course, most of them want to relax in Antalya, since this area is considered the most developed in terms of infrastructure. However, other Mediterranean resorts on the Turkish coast are also perfectly prepared to meet tourists from all over the world. In addition, in cities located some distance from the international airport, for example, in Alanya, the prices are relatively low. Do not believe? Judge for yourself. Here you can relax in a five-star hotel at the prices of a budget three-star. For example, choosing the 5 * Insula Resort & Spa hotel, located on the coast of Alanya, you will need to pay about 15,000 rubles (per week) for your stay. This is surprisingly small! In Antalya, a hotel of this category will cost twice as much.





Cost of vacation in Turkey

For many Russians, this southern country is associated with cheap service. However, turning to a travel agency, they understand that such information is not entirely true. And that in Turkey, vacation can be both budgetary and very expensive. It all depends on the class of the resort in which the selected hotel is located. In Antalya, which is the capital and air gate of the Turkish Riviera, the cost of living in five-star hotels is much higher than in remote areas, for example, in Alanya. So, in the aforementioned Insula Resort Spa 5 * (Turkey), as already noted, a weekly tour costs about 350 dollars, while in Antalya rooms in a deluxe hotel cost from 700 dollars a week and more. The price is also affected by the price of the transfer and the number of excursions included in the package. On average, sightseeing tours cost $ 200-300 more. In a word, those tourists who want to get the highest level of service, but want to save money, can choose the resort of Alanya. Of course, you can relax in an expensive resort by choosing good 4 * hotels in Turkey . However, why stay in a four-star hotel when you can book a 5 * hotel at the same price, but in Alanya? In addition, this city is interesting in terms of cognitive relaxation. There are many attractions here. Tours at the Insula Resort Spa 5 * hotel usually include a rich excursion program, which makes them popular in the tourist market.





Alanya: walled city

This resort is 125 km from Antalya International Airport. It is popular among young people. There are many entertainment venues: discos, nightclubs, bars, etc. Because of this, it is considered the noisiest of the Turkish resorts. The hallmark of this town is a fortress dating from the 4th century BC. In addition to hotels of various categories, campsites and pensions are scattered throughout the coast. The disadvantage of the coast is that there is sparse vegetation. However, the hotel administration, in order to correct this "flaw", is intensely engaged in landscaping its territories. So, for example, around the building of the Insula Resort Spa 5 * there are many tubs with palm trees and other tropical plants, and well-groomed lawns and flower beds are pleasing to the eye. The coast of Alanya is divided into several areas: Avsallar, Mahmutlar, Konkaly, and others. In the last of the above, the aforementioned hotel is located.

In addition, many four-star holiday homes have undergone reconstruction, which significantly increased their degree of comfort. As a result, some of them added one more to their 4 stars. For example, the Royal Vikingen Resort & Spa 4 *, built in 1990, began to be called Insula Resort Spa 5 * since 2013. After renaming and upgrading the service, the hotel has improved significantly. As for the village of Konakli in general, it offers tourists a lot of entertainment, small pebble and sometimes sandy beaches, the opportunity to engage in water sports, as well as attractions for adults and children, and much more. By the way, the swimming season here begins in May and closes at the end of September. In the high season, the average air temperature in these parts is +27 ¬∞ C.

General Description of Insula Resort

After that, the hotel from a four-star hotel turned into an institution with 5 * status and became known as Insula Resort Spa 5 *. It is considered to be a spa hotel in its type and includes a well-equipped beauty and relaxation center. The total territory of "Insula" is about 20,000 square meters. meters. Location

So, Insula is located on the first beach line in the village of Konakli, 120 km from Antalya and 12 km from Alanya. The village center is just a couple of minutes walk. Number of rooms

The hotel has a total of 293 rooms, of which only 44 have sea views. 197 are standard (13-20 sq. Meters), 95 are one-room and two-room family.
