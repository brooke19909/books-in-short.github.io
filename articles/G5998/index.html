Hotel "Kebur Palace" (Moscow): address, description of rooms, service, reviews

Moscow is one of the most visited cities in Russia. Of course, tourists always come here - both from abroad and compatriots. During a vacation or business trip, I want to stay comfortably, but it's hard to choose something from the impressive variety of hotels that is observed in Moscow.

Now it is worth to pay attention to the hotel "Kebur Palace". This is a 4-star hotel located near the Kremlin and Red Square. Among the guests of the city, it is popular, and it should be explained why.

Location

Hotel "Kebur Palace" is located on 32 Ostozhenka Street, building 1. It is difficult not to notice this beautiful building of extraordinary architecture.

Walking distance from the hotel is the Central House of Scientists of the Russian Academy of Sciences, Krymskaya Embankment, a monument to Peter I, the Tretyakov Gallery, Cathedral of Christ the Savior, Center for Photography named after Lumiere brothers, etc.

At a distance of 1.5-2 kilometers is the Kremlin, Red Square, Arbat, GUM, as well as other well-known metropolitan attractions.

Service

At this 4-star hotel, the service is top notch. Here is the list of services provided:

  • Outdoor terrace.
  • Indoor swimming pool.
  • Solarium.
  • Sauna.
  • Billiard room.
  • 24-hour reception desk.
  • Free Wi-Fi in the entire hotel.
  • Parking zone (in Moscow its presence is really extremely important and relevant).
  • Transfer to the airport and back.
  • Luggage storage.
  • Laundry and ironing. Shoe shine is also provided.
  • Hall for various events.
  • Small business office.
  • Room service.

There is one more nuance that should be noted when talking about this hotel in Moscow: there is also public parking nearby.

Here are just some of the procedures you can try here:

A restaurant

On the territory of the hotel "Kebur Palace" in Moscow there is a chic institution that deserves special attention. It is called the Tiflis Courtyard. He is often chosen for weddings and other events. In the huge banquet hall, designed for 300 people, there is a stage where professional musicians perform, delighting guests with live performance.

First-class service and excellent cuisine are what guests are offered in this establishment. And the hotel is always ready to provide assistance in organizing events.

If we talk about the direction, the institution specializes in dishes of European, Russian and Georgian cuisines. Here you can also smoke a hookah and watch live broadcasts of important matches.

Terms of Accommodation

A person who has decided to stay at the Kebur Palace Hotel (the address is mentioned at the beginning) should know some organizational issues. They can be distinguished in the following list:

  • Check-in starts at 14:00.
  • Departure ends at 12:00.
  • Children are welcome at the hotel. There is no need to pay extra for a child under 6 years old. If the children are under 2 years old, then they are provided with a free cradle. For full-sized extra beds you need to pay 1500 rubles per day.
  • If an additional service is needed, then you need to send a corresponding request to the hotel in advance.
  • When booking, you must provide your personal credit card details. The hotel accepts five options: Union Pay, American Express, Maestro, Master Card and Visa.

By the way, the hotel "Kebur Palace 4 *" provides guests with official accounts for reporting. Only that such a document needs to be received by a guest needs to be clarified in advance.

Cost

It should be noted that the hotel "Kebur Palace" is not a budget option. A day in the most inexpensive room (in a standard single) will cost about 10-12 thousand rubles. This price also includes breakfast, VAT and other charges.

The most expensive option is a two-story suite. A day in these apartments will cost 45,000 rubles for two people.

It should be noted that these prices are approximate. They may vary, but not significantly.

However, if you check into the hotel directly, and not pre-booked through any third-party service, it will turn out to be much cheaper. So, for example, prices for daily accommodation in a small single room start at 5100 r. And in a two-story suite - from 30 000 p.

Exclusive Offers

Guests who choose to stay at the 4-star Kebur Palace Hotel are offered unique promotions. Among them:

  • 10% discount at the Tiflis Yard Restaurant. To receive it, you need to present a key card from your apartment.
  • Early booking. If you reserve an apartment by contacting the property directly, you can get a lot of pleasant bonuses: unlimited access to the sauna and pool, breakfast in the restaurant, a reserved parking space. But the most important thing is the favorable price, which is fixed in advance.
  • Special offer offer. When making a reservation, a 10 percent discount is directly provided. But the minimum stay is 2 days.
  • Promotion "Long Stay". It is relevant for people who want to stay at the hotel for more than 7 days. There are also discounts calculated automatically when making a reservation.

If you want to learn more about a particular offer, you can contact the property directly. Friendly administrators will surely answer questions of a potential guest.
