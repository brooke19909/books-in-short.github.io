Leila Behti: French star success story

Most French people know who Leila Behti is. Photos of this beauty now and then appear on the covers of glossy magazines, revealing to fans new secrets from the actress's personal life. But for a Russian, this girl is still a real mystery, as our media rarely publish news about her.





Leila Behti: an early dream of the scene

The future actress was born into a family of immigrants from Algeria. It happened on March 6, 1984 in the small town of Issy-les-Moulineaux, not far from Ile de France. In addition to Leila herself, her parents raised three more children. At the same time, the girl was the youngest in the family, and therefore received the most attention.

Behti Leila had dreamed about the big stage since high school. It was then that she began to look for announcements of castings for stellar projects and television shows, simultaneously checking how she corresponded to the requirements set forth in them. However, at an early age, she did not dare to test.





Education

To begin with, the school in which Leyla Behti studied was with a theatrical bias. This helped her learn the basics of acting before she came of age. At the same time, speaking on the school stage, the girl fully realized the fact that in the future she sees herself only as an actress.

Therefore, immediately after school, Leila enters the theater courses for Stefan Gild. Here she undergoes six-month training, which allows her to get closer to her dream. However, for complete triumph, she needs to graduate from Berenger Basti's art studio. After all, the name of this school allows young actresses to receive a significant bonus when passing star castings.

True, the cost of training in this institution was simply prohibitive. At that time, the Behti family did not possess such funds, and therefore Leila decided to take care of her future herself. To do this, she often had to run away from school in order to catch the next part-time job.
