The Three Corners Royal Star Beach Resort: description and reviews of tourists

The Three Corners Royal Star Beach Resort is a seaside resort located in the heart of Hurghada. Here you can spend time calmly and serenely, enjoying excellent service, a warm sun and transparent waves.





Hotel Feature

The Three Corners Royal Star Beach Resort is a luxury beachfront hotel that allows you to enjoy the sea right from your room. In addition to swimming in the Red Sea, there is also a clean freshwater pool. Also an indisputable advantage is the ability to use free wireless Internet in the lobby and other public areas.

The Three Corners Royal Star Beach Resort will certainly delight you with its comfortable rooms with an original and colorful design. An important fact can also be considered that a set of amenities will allow you to feel absolute comfort and a warm homely atmosphere. Each room has individual air conditioning and a modern TV. In addition, you will certainly enjoy sitting on a cozy balcony, enjoying picturesque views.

The Three Corners Royal Star Beach Resort is a beachfront resort with many facilities and amenities. For all questions you are interested in, you can consult the reception staff. A team of professional animators has prepared many exciting games and sporting events for you. You can also enjoy the treatments provided by the spa and visit the beautiful themed restaurants, as well as cozy bars.





Resort Location

The indisputable advantage of the resort and hotel complex Three Corners Royal Star Beach Resort 4 * is that it is located on the very shore of the Red Sea, on a beautiful private beach. We can say that this is the central part of such an exotic city as Hurghada, where you can visit many cafes, shops and entertainment venues. From here it is quite convenient to get to the international airport, because it is located only 9 km away.





Resort Resort Guest Apartments

The Three Corners Royal Star Beach Resort offers 212 comfortable rooms. They are divided into the following categories:

  • There are 186 standard rooms. They are a small room in which there are two one and a half orthopedic beds for guests.
  • Family rooms are spacious rooms that are twice the size of a standard apartment. There is a large double bed for adults, as well as a pair of single for children.
  • Since this hotel is often chosen by their newlyweds for their honeymoon, several special rooms are provided for them. They have a romantic d√©cor and are equipped with a jacuzzi bathtub.
  • Since this hotel does not provide extra beds in rooms, non-standard apartments with triple occupancy were provided.
  • The best suites of this hotel are considered to be classified as "Suite". There are two rooms, one of which acts as a bedroom, and the second as a living room.




What is provided in the apartment

In all rooms of the hotel you will be provided with the following amenities:

  • the bathroom may have a shower or a bath with a jacuzzi (and in addition there is a hairdryer, bath accessories and a set of disposable cosmetics);
  • thanks to the individual air conditioning system, your room will not feel discomfort from the Egyptian heat;
  • to make you feel completely safe, all apartments are equipped with a fire alarm with smoke detectors;
  • the room has a small refrigerator, which can also act as a minibar;
  • a large package of satellite channels that you can watch using a widescreen modern TV can fill your leisure time;
  • you can call abroad or contact the administration using a landline telephone;
  • You can not worry about the safety of your belongings, because the apartments have an electronic safe;
  • on the balcony or terrace there is garden furniture, as well as a rope for drying clothes.

Positive comments

The Three Corners Royal Star Beach Resort, reviews about which our compatriots write on specialized resources, leaves tourists with a very positive impression:

  • very good menu - all products are fresh, the dishes are very tasty and also varied;
  • upon arrival at the hotel, all guests are treated with freshly squeezed juice as a compliment;
  • There are many night clubs near the hotel with a good entertainment program;
  • the hotel is quite quiet, and in the evenings pretty good concerts are held;
  • the pool is very clean and well maintained, and around it there are many sun loungers and umbrellas.




Negative reviews

Many tourists stay at he Three Corners Royal Star Beach Resort (Hurghada). Reviews, in addition to positive impressions, also contain some comments:

  • reception staff may knowingly put you in a bad room to require a large surcharge for relocation;
  • air conditioners are very old and practically do not cool the air in the room;
  • very boring and sluggish animation (we can say that it is practically not in the hotel);
  • even despite the presence of a safe with a combination lock, money and valuables constantly disappear from the rooms (therefore it is better to carry everything with you);
  • the hotel area is very small, and therefore there is practically no place for walking;
  • Despite the fact that the beach near the hotel is private, there are always many boats of local fishermen on it, and therefore swimming is very inconvenient;
  • room cleaning is mediocre enough, but if you leave a tip, the situation can be significantly improved;
  • the alcohol in the hotel is just disgusting (the only thing you can drink is local wine).

Hotel Three Corners Royal Star Beach Resort 4 * makes a pretty good impression. Despite minor flaws, it provides guests with a sufficiently high-quality service and provides a high level of comfort. It is important that the sea is located very close, and there is also a well-kept and clean pool. The hotel also takes care of the nutrition of its guests. All dishes are not only healthy and nutritious, but also very original and tasty.
