Hotel Alejandria Hsm Alejandria 3 * (Spain, Mallorca): photos and reviews

The island of Mallorca is certainly one of the most popular resorts in Spain. The rest here is mainly Germans, Swiss and French. But for some time now, Russian tourists began to explore the island. Reviews about him as a resort are exceptionally good. There are a lot of inexpensive and at the same time quite comfortable hotels in Mallorca. For example, many Russian operators conclude contracts with the Alejandria Hsm Alejandria 3 * hotel .

Location of the complex

This modern hotel was built in close proximity to the sea (300 m). Hotel guests need to go to the beach no more than 5-7 minutes. Location of the hotel Alejandria Hsm Alejandria 3 * most tourists find it very convenient. Right next to it is a bus stop for buses to Palma de Mallorca.





The airport is located just 8 km from the hotel. It takes no longer than 30 minutes to get from the hotel by shuttle bus to the hotel. The resort town of Playa de Palma, which is closest to the complex, offers Mallorca guests a wide variety of activities. There are nightclubs, discos and leisure parks.

Description of the center

The hotel Alejandria Hsm Alejandria 3 * was built in 1970. Of course, this is quite a long time ago. However, the furniture and decoration in this hotel is still not too old. The fact is that in 2010 a partial reconstruction was carried out here.

Alejandria Hsm Alejandria 3 * hotel welcomes guests of the island in one five-story building of a modern building, the main decoration of which is light metal parapets of loggias.

The rules for living in this complex are almost the same as in any other hotel in Spain. When registering a room, guests must pay a city tax (1.1 Euro per night). Only children under 16 years of age are exempted from it. Some services at this hotel are provided for a fee. In addition to cash, the hotel accepts credit cards (Visa, MasterCard).

Hotel rental: description

By the standards of Spain, the hotel Hsm Alejandria Hotel 3 * (Mallorca, Spain) - q remains large. Tourists are offered 221 comfortable rooms. If desired, guests can rent at the hotel:

  • one of 212 rooms standard double room;

  • single room.

Standard rooms are equipped with two single beds. These rooms can accommodate 2-3 people. All rooms for rent in the complex have:

  • TV;

  • telephone;

  • safe.





Each room, of course, is equipped with individual showers. Here, guests are invited, among other things, and a hairdryer. Guests can use the safe in the hotel rooms only for a fee. Television at the hotel is satellite. For an additional fee, hotel residents can receive a Wi-Fi password.

Room Reviews

The opinion of the rooms rented out at the Alejandria ex Hsm 3 * hotel (Mallorca), the photos of the interiors of which are presented on the page, turned out to be quite good among tourists. Most guests find them quite comfortable. The main disadvantage of hotel rooms, according to most vacationers, is the lack of air conditioning and ventilation. During the holiday season, it is very stuffy in the rooms rented in the hotel, as well as in the hall and corridors. If desired, guests can rent a fan at the reception. But it will be quite expensive. In addition, there are a limited number of fans in the hotel, and they are usually not enough for all guests.

The furniture in the hotel rooms, judging by the reviews of tourists, is set quite comfortable. The maids clean the rooms quite often. Guests have no special complaints about the hotel staff. The only thing, many tourists note the fact that maids very rarely wash floors in rooms. Meanwhile, on the tile, any dirt becomes very noticeable. But in any case, the maids sweep the beach sand off the floor quite carefully.





The bathrooms in this hotel, according to many guests, are quite comfortable. The same can be said about the balconies in the hotel. The loggias here are quite spacious. The only thing is, the railing of the hotel balconies is not too convenient for drying clothes. Therefore, tourists who have booked rooms at this hotel should definitely take a rope with clothespins from home.

Hotel infrastructure

The service at this hotel is provided for tourists with a standard, characteristic for almost all three-star hotels in Spain. In the Alejandria ex Hsm 3 * hotel (Mallorca), residents can use:

  • seasonal outdoor pool;

  • luggage storage room;

  • car rental

  • a terrace;

  • currency exchange office;

  • elevator.





The hotel also has a small TV room and a games room. For the use of the Internet in the hotel rooms, as already mentioned, guests must pay. On the territory of Wi-Fi, residents are free to use. The access code is indicated on the reception plate (behind the column). For children in the hotel there are special chairs in the restaurant. Parents can also order a crib in the room.

Entertainment at the hotel

Are arranged in the Alejandria Hsm Alejandria Hotel 3 *, of course, and animation. Live music is played here several times a week and free evening shows are organized. During the day, guests of the hotel can visit the billiard room if they wish.

Infrastructure Reviews

The service at the Alejandria ex Hsm 3 * hotel , according to many residents, is not bad. The pool at the hotel is quite comfortable. In addition, it has a small children's area. Free wifi at this hotel is not too fast. You can use it mainly only at night and early in the morning. The hotel staff is polite. Some hotel staff speak Russian. English is spoken by all employees of the complex.





This hotel is mainly settled by German youth. Unfortunately, such guests behave very noisily - they get drunk, sing songs, throw bottles and even plastic furniture from balconies. This, of course, most tourists consider the main disadvantage of the hotel. However, according to many Russian vacationers, sober Germans are quite polite and sociable people. In any case, you can get used to them quickly enough.

Dining at the hotel

Of course, like in any other Spanish three-star complex, the Alejandria Hsm Alejandria 3 * hotel has its own restaurant. Rooms can be rented with breakfast or guest house. The hotel reviews are good or bad. Most tourists consider the hotel‚Äôs dining room a plus, above all, the variety of dishes served. However, according to many guests, the hotel cook does not cook well. Rice in the dining room may be undercooked, and omelette may be salted. But the pastries in the hotel restaurant are almost always delicious. A lot of this hotel serves fresh fruit.

There is an Italian corner in the dining room. Here, if desired, you can order, for example, several types of spaghetti, lasagna, pizza, etc. With half board, residents can only take drinks at a restaurant for a fee.





In addition to the dining room, of course, there is also a bar in the hotel. It is located next to the pool. Here, if you wish, you can buy some traditional or original cocktail.

Alejandria Hsm Alejandria 3 *: beach reviews

The sea is not too far from the hotel. Unfortunately, this hotel does not have a private beach . The guests of the complex visit the municipal. On the coast in this place of Mallorca alternate equipped and non-equipped beaches. If desired, residents of the complex can relax on anyone. On equipped beaches, guests of the island, among other things, are offered sun loungers and umbrellas. Renting them is not too expensive. Unfortunately, there are no locker rooms and toilets on the beaches in this place.

The sea opposite the hotel, judging by the reviews of tourists, is almost always transparent and calm. Small waves here usually rise only in the late afternoon. The entrance to the sea in this place is very gentle. Vacationers have to walk a long belt in the water in order to get to the depths. For children, this, of course, is very convenient. But adult tourists are better off choosing a beach on the coast some distance from the hotel. For example, the guests of the complex praise the coastline opposite the center of Spain.

City infrastructure and attractions

Of course, guests of the Alejandria Hsm Alejandria 3 * hotel can fully use the amenities provided by Mallorca to their guests. Near the hotel there is, for example, a large supermarket. Here, if you wish, you can purchase products, beach accessories, electronics, etc.





Experienced residents of the complex recommend that beginners must visit Palma de Mallorca. You can get to this city in about an hour. You should take bus number 23. The stop is located near the Mercadonna supermarket.

And there are a lot of various attractions in Playa de Palma. For example, tourists can visit Mallorca Cathedral, Portopi Lighthouse, Almudaina Palace, etc. in this city .
