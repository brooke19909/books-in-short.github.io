Invite

If you are looking for a great place with a pleasant homely atmosphere, we recommend that you choose this option. A stunning level of service provides comfort and coziness to hotel guests throughout their stay. Among the advantages of the invite hotel are its proximity to the Maryino metro station and excellent rooms at affordable prices.

Apartments of 4 types: standard (price from 3,000 p.), Comfort (3,500 p. Per day), junior suite (from 4,500) and luxury (price of 6,000 rubles). Do not forget that it is better to book a room in advance: there may not be any free places.

Another bonus for the guests of Invite is a cafe, with the best choice of dishes among the hotels of Maryino. Do not hesitate, you will definitely like it here! Hotel address: Novomaryinskaya, 12/12, building 1.

"Sofia"

A good option for an inexpensive hotel near the Maryino metro station (photos are presented in the article). It is suitable for those who want to stay in a room with their pet. The highly professional hotel staff will always help to solve any unusual situation.

The reception is open around the clock. In addition, the hotel is equipped with convenient parking, a laundry service and a small cafe with delicious homemade pies. The rooms are always clean and tidy. This is closely monitored by a team of neat maids. You can choose an accommodation option from the category:

  • Standart room;
  • double with 1 or 2 beds;
  • Junior Suite

The cost of living per day starts from 4,000 rubles. All rooms are equipped with necessary furniture and modern appliances. Junior Suite has a large TV and minibar. Hotel Sofia is located at 39 Belorechenskaya Street. You need to know about the availability of rooms in advance. There is cash and bank transfer.

Rendezvous

A popular hotel in Maryino, which is very popular among guests of the capital. Cozy rooms made in high-tech style, decorated with unusual elements of decor and wonderful flowers. The pleasant color of the walls and upholstered furniture give comfort and coziness. Each room has a bathroom equipped with everything you need. Air conditioning, a TV and a telephone are also available, which can be used to contact the reception at any time.

The number of rooms includes: standard, junior suite, luxury and premium. Prices for Moscow are quite affordable. From 3,500 rubles per day. You can also rent a room for a few hours (there is an hourly fee). Another of the advantages of this hotel in Maryino is its parking, equipped with video surveillance and an excellent restaurant, with a large selection of delicious and quality food. Located "Rendezvous" at the address: Porechnaya street, 13 building 1.

"Diadem"

Arriving in Moscow, you can also use the services of apart-hotels. In Maryino, the best is Diadem. It is a comfortable one-bedroom apartment located on the 17th floor of the Business House business complex. The windows offer a gorgeous view of the capital. The apartments are equipped with the most comfortable furniture and appliances, there are dishwashers, televisions and microwave ovens. The walls are decorated with magnificent paintings and small fashionable lamps.

Apartments of two categories: deluxe and business class. The cost is from 3 800 rubles per night. Children under 9 years old can stay for free upon presentation of a birth certificate. The friendly hotel staff always carefully monitors the cleanliness and ensures the comfort of visitors. Diadem is located at 6. Bratislavskaya Street. Near the hotel there is everything for a comfortable stay: pharmacies, convenience stores, sports clubs and wonderful parks. This is a great option for people who come on a business trip.

You can stay in standard, deluxe or VIP. The cost of 2,200 rubles. These are the lowest prices in the Maryino metro hotels (the address will be indicated below). For residents of the hotel, convenient parking and a 24-hour laundry service. The staff of Bratislava is very friendly and always ready to help. Address: Porechnaya street, 31 building 1.
