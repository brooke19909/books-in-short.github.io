Guest house "Valentina", Lazarevskoye: description and reviews

The Lazarevskoye microdistrict is one of the largest resort and recreation centers not only in Sochi, but also on the entire Black Sea coast. It is protected from the cold air masses by the Caucasus Mountains. Therefore, the climate here is especially mild and vacation in Lazarevsky is not overshadowed by anything. Prices in the village vary. You can find both very expensive hotels and budget places with good service. One of these is Valentina Guest House. It will be discussed in our review. You can also find out how to get to Lazarevsky.

Hotel Description





Guest house "Valentina" (Lazarevskoye) is located at the beginning of the village of Lazarevskoye on the main street. Despite this, it is quite quiet and comfortable here. To the sea to go only five minutes. The seashore begins immediately after descending a small staircase. After all, the location of the hotel is the first coastline.

In the guest house "Valentina" you can not worry about the fact that there will be many people around. The hotel has a fairly small number of rooms, so this is the place that is ideal for relaxing from the bustle of the city. You can come here even with small children.

What does the guesthouse look like?





Guest house "Valentina" (Lazarevskoye) is a fairly large complex, which consists of three floors. The number of rooms is twelve rooms, which are designed for two or four people. Each room is equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. The furniture set consists of single and double beds, bedside tables, a wardrobe, a dressing table with a high chair, a dining table and chairs. Among the electrical household appliances in the room there is a TV and a refrigerator.

Each has an individual bathroom and is equipped with a shower. The climate in the rooms is regulated by air conditioning.

The cost of living in the guest house "Valentina"





If you want to have a good vacation for a reasonable price, then your vacation in Lazarevsky is the best suited for you. Prices for accommodation in the guest house "Valentina" start from 1600 rubles in the month of July-August. The minimum period for which you can stay at the hotel is three days.

You can come to the hotel at any time of the year, because even in the off-season guest house "Valentina" does not close. Prices for accommodation in different periods will vary slightly. So, starting in September, the cost of living will be from 1000 rubles.

Children under six years old without a separate bed.

List of services provided





The staff of Valentina Guest House is ready to offer the following services, which are already included in the price:

  • parking lot, which can accommodate even bulky transport, the total number of places is eight;
  • transfer from the railway station of the village of Lazarevskoye;
  • daily floor cleaning;
  • linen change once every five days;
  • organization of one excursion;
  • use of simulators;
  • playground;
  • first aid kit and doctor's call;
  • provision of sewing accessories (threads and needles);
  • Ironing board with iron on each floor;
  • expedited check-in and check-out;
  • order a taxi to the guest house "Valentina".

Services for an additional fee:

  • transfer from Sochi or back;
  • organization of additional excursions;
  • Private guarded parking for 150 rubles per day.

Catering





There is no dining area on the territory of Valentina Guest House. But next to the hotel there is a cafe serving Armenian and Russian cuisine. Here you can order a comprehensive three meals a day. The price for breakfast, lunch and dinner is approximately 440 rubles. The cost may vary slightly, but guests of the guest house receive a discount of twenty percent.

There is also a grocery store a couple of minutes walk from the guest house. This is in case you want to cook something yourself in one of the hotel's kitchens. By the way, the catering facilities in the guest house are one per floor. Therefore, there is no need to languish in line to the stove. Each kitchen has cooking utensils.

Leisure activities outside the guest house





Victory Street is central. Walking along it towards the center, you can see a lot of various entertainment venues: cafes and restaurants, bars and clubs, shops and souvenir shops.

You can also book various excursions or diving in crystal clear water, visit an amusement park, visit dolphinariums or water parks, and arrange walks on ecological routes along the untouched places of the Sochi National Park.

On the beach itself there are also a lot of types of entertainment. There is the opportunity to ride a jet ski, a banana, a catamaran, windsurf, parachute and so on. There is also a pier for pleasure boats and yachts. A variety of children's attractions are provided for children.
