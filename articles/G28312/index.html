On the eve of the holidays: what gifts can you make yourself

Going to celebrate this or that celebration, we are faced with the problem: what would it be like to present, interesting, unusual, really joyful and pleasant? Let's try to offer some ideas that you can take into service.

Creative in action



So, what gifts can you make yourself? If you can sew, knit, embroider, then the simplest and best thing is a new thing. Elegant dress, blouse, skirt, shorts - a great option for a child. Openwork jacket or vest, shawl, stole, shawl - for an adult daughter (or mother), sister, girlfriend. A hat, a scarf, socks and gloves - these are what gifts you can make with your own hands for a man (teenage boy). For good friends, tie a bedspread on a bed or chair, a luxurious tablecloth. Handwork has always been highly valued. And it will be all the more pleasant for a person to receive such a present, the more you put the warmth of your soul into it. For children, parents can make dollhouses - from plywood or cardboard boxes. Daughters will be delighted, believe me! And if you live in a private house, a perfect example of what kind of gifts you can do with your own hands will be a children's swing - then your dads and older brothers should be too smart and smart. Boys can please mothers, aunts, grandmothers, older sisters with wood crafts. Of course, if they know how to carpentry, locksmith. Jigsaws, nail files and other tools are put into business - and here mom joyfully holds in her hands a new cutting board, rolling pin or even a picture made with a burning device.


Congratulatory video

From what gifts you can make yourself, an interesting option is a video. It is suitable for almost any celebration - family or personal. What is the point: in almost every family there is now a computer, and not one, as well as office equipment - scanners, printers. In operating systems, special video editors are built into standard programs. If we talk about XP, then there is Movie Maker. For example, if New Year's holidays are on the nose, and you have not decided which gifts to make with your own hands, make a film about your loved ones. From family albums, select the most interesting pictures, scan to a computer. Come up with interesting signatures, congratulations. On the Internet, find colorful postcards, pictures. And of course, musical accompaniment. Arrange all the materials in the desired order and mount your roller. Show it on New Year's Eve: there will be no limit to the joys of home!

Tasty surprise



Considering gift ideas that almost everyone can make with their own hands, one cannot forget about culinary masterpieces. And first of all about sweets. Cakes, muffins, pastries - but you never know how you can please relatives and friends, having in stock three or two of their signature recipes! However, salads, special sandwiches, and other gastronomic delights are also suitable. Even ice cream, having the necessary ingredients, is not very difficult to prepare at home. It is important that everyone will like such surprises! Naturally, the dishes should be decorated diligently, with chic. And here you will find useful carving skills. Check out the various manuals on how to cut vegetables and fruits in an original way and decorate cooked food with them. And show your art in all its glory.

Happy holidays and nice presents!
