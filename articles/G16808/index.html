Nokia Lumia 635: reviews. Smartphone Nokia Lumia 635: specifications, price

At a time when there was a fierce struggle for leadership between Apple and Samsung in the mobile device market, another strong player - Nokia, acquired by Microsoft IT giant Microsoft, took quite a solid position in the market. So, a new line of Lumia smartphones, controlled by the Windows operating system, was introduced. In particular, a year ago the Nokia Lumia 635 model was launched onto the market, which we will review in this article.

Lumia smartphones in the mobile market

To begin with, it should be noted that Lumia smartphones have been on the market for a long time, and for all this time they have already managed to take good positions, having won the trust of customers. These phones have a number of undeniable advantages even over the seemingly indisputable leaders of Samsung and Apple (we will return to this comparison more than once).





Firstly, Lumia is controlled by Windows Phone, which already provides some alternative to those who are tired of Android and iOS. Secondly, Lumia have a nice design, and this is a clear plus in the event that a person doubts which model he wants to purchase. Thirdly, Nokia is famous for its cameras, which can be attributed to the arguments for the purchase of the 635th model. Finally, fourthly, the Lumia smartphone will cost less than Apple and Samsung, but in quality, it will leave competitors far behind.

Design and housing

As already noted, the appearance of Nokia devices is on top. Despite the fact that the case is made of glossy plastic, on which fingerprints remain, the phone has a proprietary bright design designed using smooth angles. Thus, the phone looks like a toy. This, in fact, attracts attention.

On the whole, on the Nokia Lumia 635 (the overview clearly demonstrates this) the layout of the navigation keys is standard: they are located on the side of the phone. The mobile device lies easily in the hand, it is quite pleasant to the touch, although it can give glare in the sun. The disadvantage of such a beautiful coating on the case is its susceptibility to scratches: they will be clearly visible on a glossy surface.

Graphic characteristics of the Lumia 635

Characteristics of graphics issued by the smartphone can also be called another strength of Lumia. The results. Pros and cons of the device

To summarize our review of a mobile device. The first thing that pleases the Nokia Lumia 635 is the price. Yes, the device can be confidently called affordable, so it will suit both a business, busy person who appreciates the style and wide functionality of the phone, and a school student who simply likes this model. Regarding the appearance, one can also note the Nokia-branded glossy gloss of the model, which can also be seen on the cover of the iPhone 5C. Visually, it seems that the smartphone is covered with some kind of plastic shell. In general, it looks pretty attractive. Even the 635th model has a good camera that allows you to take large-size, high-quality images, and, of course, the non-standard Windows Phone 8.1 operating system. You can even talk about it separately. This is a universal tool that is highly optimized for a variety of tasks. WP shell literally "flies"; Accompanied by visual special effects, it can be called both functional and pleasant to work with. Lumia also has a good screen.





Now about the shortcomings. You can start again from the display. Although the color rendering in general and the pictures in particular on the 635 model are on top, the disadvantage is the too glossy texture of the surface on which the user's fingerprints are clearly visible. The same can be said about the smartphone case, on which they are also clearly visible. The second negative point in working with the phone is the lack of a front camera, which could serve as an excellent tool for creating selfie pictures.

The third nuance of the Nokia Lumia 635 (the instruction, by the way, does not mention it) is the lack of the ability to control the smartphone while the hands are in gloves. This is a significant drawback for countries where winters are characterized as long and cold.

You can also note the absence of metal inserts in the phone: it is entirely made of plastic. Of course, this does not make the Nokia Lumia 635 practical. The reviews confirm this.

Finally, someone may also find the Windows Phone platform uncomfortable. In order to handle it, you need a habit, which, of course, owners of devices on Android and iOS do not have. However, do not rush to be disappointed if you first bought yourself a Lumia. It may well turn out that he is not quite good.
