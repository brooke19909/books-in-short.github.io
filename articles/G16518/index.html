Lara is a great place to relax

Lara is a beautiful place to spend a relaxing holiday. Fans of nightlife can easily get to Antalya, where life is in full swing, people are bustling.

Lara is famous for its beautiful scenery and clean beaches. In the eastern part of the town flows the picturesque river Duden, which forms the Lower Duden waterfall. It is located near the famous beaches, stretching for 12 kilometers to Belek itself. A powerful stream of water falls from a high cliff directly into the sea. You can watch this spectacle for hours. And upstream is Upper Duden. The streams of a mountain river can be observed from one of the cafes in the town. Popular with tourists and locals, the restaurant on the Tunektep hill is a rotating observation deck at an altitude of 800 meters. In the restaurant you can enjoy alternately magnificent views of the sea and the mountains, taste fresh fish dishes and excellent local wine. For romantics, Lara has Karaalioglu Park - a grove of orange trees and palm trees, where you can hide from the heat and enjoy the sunset in the evenings.
