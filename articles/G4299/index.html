Hotel Dodeca Sea Resort 5 * (Rhodes, Greece): reviews, photos

Love and favor of travelers have earned the resort hotel Dodeca Sea Resort 5 * . Here tourists are expected by beautiful nature, modern architecture and quality service.





a brief description of

In a picturesque resort village on the sea coast, the Dodeca Sea Resort 5 * hotel is located . The hotel was opened in 2000, and already in 2013 was overhauled. Thus, the interior and technical equipment are in line with the latest trends. Given the developed infrastructure of the hotel, both lovers of a relaxing holiday and fans of active pastime will like it here.





Location

In the cozy tourist village of Lalisu, the resort complex Dodeca Sea Resort 5 * is located. Greece, Rhodes, 14 Ferenikis Street - the address where you can find this institution upon arrival. It is 1 km from the picturesque town of Trianda, as well as 8 km from the international airport. To reach the beach, you will have to overcome no more than 50 meters.

Number of rooms

Dodeca Sea Resort 5 * hotel has more than 200 rooms. The following categories of apartments are available for guests:

  • Standard rooms can accommodate from 2 to 5 people. In addition to large or twin beds, the apartments also have folding sofas. There is a small dining table and kitchen area. There are plastic chairs on the balcony.
  • Superior are superior rooms that have been completely refurbished. The windows overlook the pool. Two rooms have beds, as well as folding furniture. There is also a dining area.
  • Junior is a spacious studio that combines the functions of a bedroom, living room, as well as a small kitchen. Functional areas are separated by decorative partitions, as well as a bar counter. Leaving on the veranda, you get to your own pool.
  • Suite is a luxurious room with panoramic sea views. The bedroom has a large king size bed. In the living room there is a couch and a coffee table. Both rooms have a TV. There is also an additional guest bedroom with a separate toilet.
  • Studio is the best accommodation option in this hotel. The main advantages are a direct view of the sea, as well as the availability of its own access to the pool. The room is equipped with a large bed, a seating area, as well as a bar. The bathroom includes a jacuzzi.




Room Amenities

Everything for a comfortable stay of guests is provided in the resort complex Dodeca Sea Resort 5 *. Greece will delight you with beauty and hospitality, and this hotel will provide the opportunity to use the following amenities in the rooms:

  • combined bathroom with bath or shower, as well as an electric hairdryer, hygienic cosmetics and a set of bath accessories;
  • a safe with an electronic combination lock, the use of which is possible for an additional fee;
  • TV with cable or satellite channels (several Russian-language programs are provided);
  • landline telephone for reception calls and international calls (the latter service is paid);
  • a small refrigerator for storing food;
  • Private balcony with comfortable garden furniture.




Infrastructure

In order for the rest to be full and comfortable, it is important to have access to a large number of infrastructure facilities. So, in the Dodeca Sea Resort 5 * hotel you will have the opportunity to use the following options:

  • a left-luggage office where guests can check in their luggage while waiting for check-in or before leaving;
  • the staff of the 24-hour reception desk will accept your complaint at any time of the day or give advice on a matter of interest to you;
  • restaurants where you can enjoy delicious international dishes, as well as order a comprehensive meal;
  • a bar with a huge assortment of drinks, salads and snacks;
  • private car parking with 24 hour security;
  • wireless internet signal in public areas;
  • a beauty salon where you can make a manicure, male and female haircuts, as well as cosmetic procedures;
  • currency exchange point;
  • a medical office where you can get an express examination, as well as get first aid.




Guest Services

Attentive attitude to the guests of the resort complex Dodeca Sea Resort 5 * is manifested in a wide range of services. The following services will be available to you at this hotel:

  • delivery of orders from the restaurant and bar to the room;
  • the formation of an individual diet or children's menu;
  • organization of holidays and business negotiations;
  • wedding manager services;
  • rental cars and other vehicles;
  • safe rental at the administrator;
  • washing guest clothes in the laundry room;
  • round-the-clock currency exchange.

Vacation with children

For the rest of the whole family, such an accommodation option as Dodeca Sea Resort 5 * is perfect. Rhodes is a great resort for children. Here they expect a mild climate and many attractions. This hotel also provides the following opportunities for young tourists:

  • children's animation program;
  • a pool with water slides and inflatable toys (there is the possibility of heating);
  • a playground with attractions (a safe cover is provided);
  • a mini-club where kids will have an interesting and fun time, being involved in the entertainment program from animators;

  • Friendly reception staff greet guests with a sincere smile, and are always ready to help during their stay;
  • spacious area, which is carefully looked after;
  • convenient location relative to the sea;
  • gentle entry into the sea without sharp stones (small pebbles mixed with sand);
  • two large pools on the territory;
  • tasty and varied food prepared in a homely manner;
  • a lot of desserts and fresh fruit in the restaurant menu;
  • good gym with modern fitness equipment;
  • the restaurant menu has a large assortment of traditional Greek dishes;
  • delicious cocktails are served at the poolside bar;
  • There is a public transport stop near the hotel, which greatly facilitates movement around the island;
  • clean beach with all the necessary infrastructure for a quality vacation;
  • every evening after dinner, a disco;
  • At the reception there are Russian-speaking managers;
  • quick placement without unnecessary delays filling a large number of papers;
  • a lot of entertainment for children;
  • in some of the rooms there is a kitchen area with household appliances and utensils;
  • many sunbeds both near the pool and on the beach;

  • You can rent a car for a symbolic price.




Negative reviews

It is worth noting a number of disadvantages that characterize the hotel Dodeca Sea Resort Ex Fo 5 *. Traveler reviews speak of the following negative points:

  • unlike most five-star hotels, where the use of the safe in the room is free, you have to pay extra for this service;
  • maids come to clean either early in the morning, when guests are still sleeping, or after dinner, which is very inconvenient;
  • both in the daytime and in the evening, the pool is too noisy and crowded (almost all animation is concentrated here);
  • the waiters in the restaurant are too slow;
  • there are a lot of mosquitoes in the room that even a fumigator cannot handle;
  • smokers everywhere in the hotel, because of which the smell of tobacco smoke is felt in the rooms and on the street (this is despite the fact that smoking is prohibited by the hotel‚Äôs rules);
  • nearby the hotel there are sewage from a nearby establishment, and therefore there is often an unpleasant smell on the territory;
  • in the rooms located on the ground floor the walls are covered with mold;
  • part of the rooms was not renovated (it is worthwhile to clarify this moment in advance to avoid unpleasant surprises);
  • in the dining room there are a lot of flies that the staff does not fight with;
  • in order to take beach towels, you need to make a cash deposit;
  • rooms of improved categories are not very different from standard ones, and therefore overpayment is not always justified;
  • Until June 15 (until the "hot season" has begun), most infrastructure facilities and entertainment do not function.

General impression

Many travelers choose this hotel for relaxation, placing high hopes on its five-star status. However, they are not always justified. Unfortunately, the service and general atmosphere of this hotel is significantly different from similar establishments in Turkey and Egypt. Nevertheless, guests are provided with quite comfortable accommodation and quality food. Also, you can not ignore the convenient location of the hotel. A luxurious beach, public transport, and the resort's main promenade are all nearby.
