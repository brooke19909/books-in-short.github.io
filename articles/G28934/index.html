Fraud or business

In fact, mobile operators, and in particular, MTS, impose paid services on us. From the point of view of the lawyers of the company - no. Where to learn about subscriptions

Before you turn off MTS paid services, you need to know exactly which of them are tied to a specific SIM card. To do this, send an SMS message to the short number 8111 with the number 1, if you need to get a list of paid services, and with the number 0, if you need a list of free subscriptions. When sending an empty message or SMS with any other text, a list of all additional applications and options is sent to the number.

This is not the only, but the fastest and most convenient method.

Ways to disable paid services

There are several ways to disable paid MTS services:

1. Direct appeal to the operator. To do this, call 0890 (the call is free) and wait for an answer, and then ask to unsubscribe from all paid services. Cons of the method: long waiting time, the need to double-check operator actions. Pros: you can unsubscribe without leaving your home or office.

2. Through USSD commands. To do this, dial the following characters: "* 111 * - a special code to cancel a specific service - lattice - call." Pros: fast, convenient, reliable. Cons: you must first find out a special code.



3. Contacting the MTS salon. This method is ideal for people who are not friends with modern electronics. The essence of the method: the client describes his problems, and the consultant solves them on the spot. Pros: no need to figure out what and how to do it yourself. Cons: you have to look for the MTS salon, and the consultant can not only dissuade existing subscriptions from being turned off, but also impose a couple of additional ones, naturally, not free.

4. Shutdown through the electronic terminal. Pros: you can unsubscribe from services on the way to the store, since there are terminals in almost every store or shopping pavilion. Cons: sorting out for the first time can be difficult; accordingly, more time is wasted.

5. Through the official website of MTS. In order to access your personal account, you need to enter a phone number and password. Pros: convenient, fast, a lot of information. Cons: for those who do not have the Internet, it will be difficult to use this service.

6. Disconnect via SMS. Each service has its own short number. Pros: fast, convenient. Cons: you need to know a special number.
