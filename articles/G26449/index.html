"Sokolsky Manor" in the Altai Mountains: reviews

For true lovers of outdoor recreation, away from the bustle of the city and polluted recreation, the Sokolsky Manor will be a great place. A big plus is the presence of favorable accommodation conditions with all amenities. Choosing this place to relax, you can be sure that you will have a good rest and at the same time you will not get tired of outdoor recreation. The "Sokolsky Manor" is located in the Altai Mountains. Clean and fresh air, amazing scenery and lots of entertainment - all this awaits you when you visit this place.

Modern holidays in the Altai Mountains

The estate located on the right bank of the Katun River is a wonderful place to relax both with the whole family and in a cheerful company of friends. The tourist base presents all modern technologies in terms of design and equipment. There is also a beautiful artificial lake.





Both adults and children will find entertainment to their liking both on the territory of the base and beyond. To the choice of visitors are presented different rooms and services. It can accommodate 65 people at a time.

All created favorable conditions contribute to a comfortable and safe rest for all guests. The staff of the Sokolsky Manor is always ready to help and do everything possible for a good holiday.

How to get to the tourist base?

In order to get to the "Sokolsky Manor" in Chemal, you can use two options. The first option is available for travelers regardless of their financial situation. You need to use the bus from Novosibirsk, Barnaul or Biysk to Chemal. Then exit in the village of Askat and go to the left bank of the Katun River, using the bridge. After that, contact the estate by phone and you will be met.

The second option will be available to owners of personal vehicles. To get to the base, take your navigator with you. Your task is to get to the village of Elekmonar by car, cross the bridge and turn right towards the Rublevka Altai, then follow the signs. There is a free secure parking on site.

Accommodation and prices

Accommodation in the Sokolsky Estate in the Altai Mountains is available in double and quadruple rooms of standard and standard + classes. In addition, for adults and children under 14 years of age, it is possible to purchase additional beds in rooms.

Standard rooms are equipped as follows: a double bed, two nightstands, a TV with satellite TV, a rocking chair, a heater and a bathroom with a sink. Each room has its own veranda. Standard + rooms are not much different from regular standard rooms. They are furnished the same way, only in addition they have a shower.





For companies of four in the Sokolsky Manor there are four-bed rooms. The furniture in these rooms includes two double beds, bedside tables, a wardrobe, a bathroom with a washbasin, a rocking chair, a heater and a TV. On the veranda, vacationers can relax and enjoy a tea party. There are 4-bed rooms category plus. There are two rooms with a double bed and a sofa bed. The rest of the furniture is the same as in a regular quadruple room. An addition to the bathroom will be a shower.

Prices for accommodation in the tourist base "Rublevka" Altai vary depending on the selected room. On average, the cost is from 2000 rubles to 5000 rubles per day per room. In addition, you can pay extra bed for an adult at a price of 800 rubles, and for a child under the age of 14 at a price of 500 rubles per day. Children under five years old without a separate bed stay free.

Meals at the Manor

Meals are not available on site. At the water park there are cafes and restaurants where you can have a delicious breakfast, lunch and dinner. For those who wish, there is a kitchen equipped with all electrical appliances, where you can cook your own meals. Near the kitchen there is a small banquet room.

There is a barbecue area near the kitchen.





Leisure and recreation at the Sokolsky Manor

Vacationers can use the following services:

  • Large and small pools. The size of the large pool is 13 * 6 meters, and the small 3 * 3 meters.
  • Board games: checkers, chess, lotto.
  • Children's trampoline in the public domain.
  • Playground with various swings, slides and so on.
  • Tennis, volleyball and basketball courts.
  • Sauna with steam room and relaxation room for 8 people.
  • Bicycle and ATV rental.
  • Guided tours of the Altai Mountains: on foot, horseback, water and automobile.

Reviews about "Sokolsky Manor"

A lot of positive reviews about the tourist base "Sokolsky Estate" indicate a high level of service. Guests of the base note the friendly attitude of workers and staff. The estate is a great place for any vacation: with family and children, as well as with colleagues or friends. Accommodation in mini-houses is very convenient and comfortable.





The environment around is very clean and the air too. Here guests will find peace of mind from everyday life and city bustle. At the same time, tourists can return to civilization every time they want it. It is very convenient that cafes, a water park and restaurants are located on the estate and there is no need to go far. For those who want to relax and learn more about the world, there is the opportunity to use the tour service. The guide will tell you all about the surrounding area and local attractions. You can also plunge into the world of extreme sports and ride in Altai Mountains on ATVs with the whole company.
