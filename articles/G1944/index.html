How to spend “Thank you” points from Sberbank: program conditions, bonuses, accumulation and calculation of points

Long accumulated bonuses and now do not know where to spend "Thank you" points from Sberbank? Or just want to register in the program, but do not know how to do it. We will tell you the rules of registration in the considered program “Thank you from Sberbank”, as well as how to accumulate and spend points.

How to get involved?

In order to participate in the program “Thank you from Sberbank”, you need to register. The registration process takes no more than a couple of minutes. The program is available to every bank card holder from Sberbank. Moreover, if there are several cards, the bonus account is combined.

In order to register in the program through a Sberbank ATM, you need to insert a card into the terminal and enter your personal PIN code. In the main menu that appears on the screen, select the "Bonus program", and then enter the mobile phone number. Thus, consent to join the program will be confirmed. An SMS message with a password will be sent to the phone, which will allow you to contact the bank’s contact center for bonuses.

In order to register with the Sberbank Online Internet Banking, you must first enter your personal account (enter your username and password), select the “Thank you from Sberbank” section, then enter your phone number and email address. In the end, you must confirm your agreement with the program rules. A password message will also be sent to the mobile phone.

In order to register through the Mobile Bank, you must send a message with the text “Thank you” to Sberbank's short number - 900. Also, the message should indicate the last four digits on the front side of the bank card with a space. In response, you will receive a message with a code that also needs to be sent again to 900. Registration is completed.

Points under the program will be accumulated when paying for purchases by credit card. Full conditions for accumulation and bonus program levels can be found on the bank's website. Now the question arises: "How to spend already earned Thank you points from Sberbank?" We will figure out how and where to do it.

How to use bonuses?

One accumulated point is equal to one ruble. You can spend them in offline stores, in online stores, on Travel and Impression sites from Sberbank, on the Online Market.

Also, using points, you can purchase various coupons that allow you to get a discount in a particular store, or get a discount of up to 99% on a service, for example, pay for a trip to GettTaxi. We will consider some offers below, however, the full list can be found on the bonus program website or clarified in the call center.

How to find out the balance?

Naturally, you can find him in your personal account of Sberbank Online Internet Banking. Points are indicated in the "Thanks from Sberbank" section. You can also check the balance of bonuses on the card at any ATM of Sberbank. Information is located in the Bonus Program section.

Also, the balance can be found in the Sberbank mobile application. In addition, a special “Thank you” application from Sberbank PJSC was released, in which you can check not only the account balance, but also the existing shares. In addition, you can find out the number of points by sending an SMS message with the word "Thank you" to the short number 900. In the SMS message, it is also important to indicate the last four digits located on the front of the card.
