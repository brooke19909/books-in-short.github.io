Hotel Banana (Turkey / Alanya): rating, features and reviews of tourists

Among the many places interesting for a long-awaited vacation or family summer vacation, Turkey is very popular among travelers. This is an incredibly interesting and colorful country with vibrant city life and a measured rural routine. It combines nature untouched by human hand with surviving remnants of ancient civilization, wild beaches and touristy bars, mountains and plains.

The resort town of Alanya is a storehouse not only of the cleanest shores and clear waters of the Mediterranean, but also a treasury of various entertainments, where every vacationer will find something to his liking. Every year a large number of representatives from different parts of the world come here. The four-star Banana Hotel offers its guests to stay in one of three buildings off the coast. As experienced, the main advantages of this institution are a welcoming reception, polite staff and incredibly tasty dinners.

general characteristics

A distinctive feature of the Banana Hotel (Turkey, Alanya) is a very long path of existence and development. The first guests crossed the threshold of the main building in 1969. It is represented by an eight-story building, equipped with elevators for the comfortable movement of guests.

In 2004, two additional two-story cottages were built ten meters from the Mediterranean Sea. For almost half a century, the hotel complex has undergone a number of transformations, the last of which is in 2010.

On the territory, whose total area is 8.500 square meters, there are swimming pools, sports fields and many green spaces. The aroma of fragrant herbs, an orange grove and spreading palm trees, according to vacationers, create a picture of a paradise.

Location

The nearest airport is 135 km away. It is located in the city of Antalya. A two-hour trip after the flight, as new arrivals claim in their reviews, is not at all tiring. In order to get to the center of Alanya, travelers need to overcome a distance of 2 km.

Accommodation Features

According to the internal rules of the Banana Hotel 4 *, pets are not allowed in the apartment. It is strictly forbidden to smoke in the rooms. For people with disabilities, special rooms are equipped. The staff is universal, speaks not only the native language, but also Russian and English. Payments are accepted in cash or by cards of international payment systems. If necessary, extra beds are placed in the room.

Accommodation

In total, Hotel Banana (Alanya, Turkey) offers 221 rooms for checking in, among which the following categories are distinguished:

  1. Standard.
  2. Family
  3. Studio
  4. Lake rooms.
  5. Suites.

Their main distinguishing feature is the different dimensions and the views that open from the window. The smallest rooms of the Standard class are 20 square meters, while the largest are 50 square meters. The largest apartments are two-room, but there is no adjacent door.

The hotel complex Hotel Banana can be classified as economical hotels. The interior is notable for its simplicity and lack of pretentiousness. However, according to experienced, it is incredibly cozy and calm here. Light walls and ceiling visually expand a small space. There is access to a balcony. In the dark, floor lamps and wall lights illuminate the room. The floors are covered with ceramic tiles in warm colors.

Maids are engaged in daily cleaning. Neat and attentive, they easily restore complete order. According to many guests of the hotel complex, everything sparkles with cleanliness. Linen is changed twice a week. Awakening of guests at the appointed time is carried out by bona fide reception staff. In general, all the staff at the hotel take care of the comfortable stay of each of their guests. This allows you to feel comfortable at home.

Degree of comfort

The furniture is primitive, and includes a double bed, armchairs, a wooden wardrobe, a desk with a built-in minibar, a luggage stand and chairs. For the convenience of tourists, bedside tables are located at the beds. The mirror is located above the countertop and allows you to admire the daily tan.

The rooms at Happy Banana Hotel are equipped with modern fire fighting systems and split air conditioning. Creating a comfortable temperature regime is the key to true relaxation and enjoyment. The TV is multi-channel, it is possible to view Russian-language channels. Phone - helps to instantly resolve emerging issues through communication with the reception. Minibar at check-in is empty. Use of the safe is paid extra.

The bathroom in the rooms of the hotel complex Hotel Banana 4 * (Turkey, Alanya) is equipped with a shower or bath, sink, hairdryer and mirror. White towels are updated daily. Personal hygiene products are represented by soap, shower gel and shampoo.

Supply system

According to the current All-Inclusive concept, guests are invited to taste the masterpieces of local culinary, served in the form of a buffet. Each independently determines the choice of dishes and puts it on a plate in the required volume. Meals are held within the walls of the main restaurant, the interior of which has a classic design. It is complemented by exquisite table setting and comfortable furniture. There is an outdoor terrace.

In addition to this institution, there are two restaurants using the A'la Carte system. To visit them, you need to pre-book a table and observe the dress code. The presence of four bars allows you to quench your thirst almost anywhere in the hotel complex.

All dining areas have high chairs. The main restaurant has a diet and children's menu. Imported alcohol, freshly squeezed juices and drinks in glass bottles are subject to additional charges.

Beach

Tourists spend the lion's share of time during their long-awaited stay in Turkey, diving and swimming in the waters of the Mediterranean. The infrastructure of this zone is at a decent level. Own pebble and sandy beach on the expanses of the municipal coast is filled with numerous sun loungers with soft bedding. Listening to the sea light breeze, sunbathing under the rays of the scorching sun - calms the body, and restores strength. For those who want to hide from the sultry heat in the shady space, there are umbrellas.

On the beach area there are showers, changing rooms and a security service. Towels are provided for a fee.

In order to diversify beach leisure, there is an equipped volleyball field and a number of water activities, among which, according to vacationers, the most popular are banana riding, snorkeling and boat trips. The ability to plunge into the deep spaces and learn the underwater world of amazing inhabitants and beautiful nature, to the liking of many travelers.

Aquazone

The Banana Hotel (Alanya) has four swimming areas and a children's pool. All of them do not have a heating system. Water is fresh and warms up under the sun. The mini-water park delights not only children, but also adults: at any age it is pleasant to go down and get a lot of pleasant sensations from riding on water slides. Regular exercises with water aerobics and playing water polo maintain muscle tone and increase the mood of all participants.

Children's leisure

Not only adults, but also children in the walls of the hotel complex Banana Hotel 4 * (Turkey) will receive a positive charge of energy and an unforgettable experience. To ensure that their day is not monotonous and dull, a children's mini-club functions in the daytime. Its design is incredibly bright and colorful. Numerous toys, cars, tracks, dishes and dolls will allow each kid to find something interesting for him. In addition, here you can create a picture masterpiece or just decorate. A professional team of animators every second raises the mood for each baby, not only with its fabulous images, but also with fun active games.

Walking in the fresh air is useful at any age. For children in the open air there is a game complex made of high-quality safe plastic. Carousels, slides and passages will delight kids with their diversity.

Babysitting service available. In this case, the parents will be calm for the safety of their child and enjoy the long-awaited vacation in full, while the baby will develop and be filled with positive emotions.

Entertainment in the hotel complex

For lovers of outdoor activities, the doors of the gym are open. Numerous exercise machines, dumbbells and other equipment are located here, which allows not only to gain strength, but also to lose a couple of extra pounds. Playing table tennis and darts does not require additional charges. Evenings end with vivid shows from a team of animators who amuse and amuse each viewer of their talent. After - a disco, entrance to which until 24:00 - is free. There is a mini golf and soccer field. Turkish bath and sauna will give true pleasure to every lover of high temperatures. Massage procedures will allow everyone to experience a frantic sense of relaxation. New strength and good spirits are guaranteed to everyone.

Additional services

You can easily reach your destination with the help of experienced drivers. Transfer is not included in the price and is paid upon arrival. A doctor's call is possible if necessary. Ironing and laundry services are available around the clock. Club Hotel Banana has grocery stores and souvenir stalls. The acquisition of a memorable gift, as guests noted, is an important ritual for most tourists.

To create a new image, the doors of a hairdressing salon are open, professional stylists of which will help realize the most unimaginable fantasies of clients.

Creative photographer services are available to every traveler. Unique vacation shots captured in the pictures will remind you of the past days the whole coming year.

The reception is open 24 hours a day. A currency exchange is located nearby. Parking spaces are available without prior reservation. For those who want to get to know the local surroundings with comfort, you can rent a car.
