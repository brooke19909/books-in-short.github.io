Le Meridien Dahab Resort, Egypt, Dahab: holidays, traveler reviews

If you plan to spend your vacation in Dahab (Egypt) and are looking for a comfortable hotel with excellent service, then consider the five-star Le Meridien Dahab Resort as a suitable option.



Location

This hotel is located in Dahab. It is 90 kilometers from Sharm El Sheikh Airport. The hotel complex was built on the very shore of the Red Sea. Here is the Gulf of Aqaba.

This hotel was opened in 2007. It is a complex of one-story buildings. The residential fund Le Meridien Dahab Resort 5 * (Dahab) is represented by 182 rooms in the following categories: superior, deluxe and deluxe. Non-smoking and disabled apartments available.

Le Meridien Dahab Resort: reviews of tourists from the Russian Federation

Many modern travelers pay great attention to the process of choosing and booking a hotel in a particular country in the world. They try to collect as much information as possible about the hotel of interest to them, in order to make the most complete and close to reality idea of ‚Äã‚Äãwhat they will expect on vacation. So, today many are no longer satisfied with the exclusively official description of the hotel and the advice of managers of travel agencies. Travelers try to get acquainted with as many comments as possible of people who have happened to visit this or that hotel. In this regard, we bring to your attention the generalized reviews of our compatriots who recently rested in the five-star Le Meridian Dahab Resort. Just note that most of the comments are positive. According to tourists, the hotel is well worth the money spent. But we learn about everything in more detail.



Hotel itself

In general, almost all the guests were satisfied with the apartments offered by him at Le Meridien Dahab Resort. So, according to them, the rooms here are very spacious, clean, comfortable, with a fresh repair and a nice design. Furniture and appliances are modern, all in excellent condition. In addition, a number of guests in their comments noted very comfortable beds, on which you can sleep well. Tourists also liked that the bathroom has not only a shower, but also a full bath. Some travelers praise great hair dryers. Therefore, you do not need to bring this device from home with you. As for cleaning, in general, there were no special complaints about this. So, according to our compatriots, sometimes the maids forgot to change a dirty towel or take out the trash. However, it was worth contacting the administrator at the reception, as the problem was solved almost instantly. A minor minus was considered by some guests as the absence of free internet in the rooms and in the whole of the hotel. The cost of this service cannot be called low. Free Wi-Fi is available only in the lobby. However, the connection speed here, as noted by travelers, is extremely low.



Territory

As for the territory of the Le Meridian Dahab Resort hotel, judging by the comments of our compatriots, the tourists were very pleased with it. So, according to them, it is very beautiful here. In addition, the territory of the hotel complex is quite large. There are many places for walking and relaxing holidays.

Nutrition

As for food at the Le Meridian Dahab Resort hotel, here the opinions of most tourists coincided. So, most travelers as a whole were satisfied with it. According to them, the buffet restaurant has always had a fairly large selection of dishes from beef, fish, chicken, seafood, salads, snacks, fresh fruits and vegetables. In addition, you could always taste delicious desserts. However, as our compatriots note, the administration and the hotel chef should think about how to diversify the menu for every day. So, even having plenty to choose from, a few days later tourists noted that they were tired of eating the same thing all the time. However, it is certainly impossible to stay hungry here.

Tourists had no complaints about drinks at this hotel. But some guests complained that at dinner the waiters sometimes did not have time to bring them the ordered drink on time. As a result, guests had to eat dry food. The opportunity to take a drink here is not provided for by the internal rules of Le Meridien Dahab Resort.

In addition, our compatriots advise, if possible, to come for lunch and dinner at the opening of the restaurant. The fact is that if you come closer to the end of the meal, then you may not get some dishes.



Sea

Judging by the reviews of travelers, the beach vacation offered by the Le Meridian Dahab Resort hotel has its own characteristics. So, first of all, it is worth paying attention to the fact that you need to go down to the sea along a large number of steps. Accordingly, on the way back they will have to overcome them in the opposite direction. Many tourists, especially people of advanced age, this feature can frighten. However, as our compatriots note, it is not worth worrying about this. The fact is that you can always call the reception and ask for a transfer to the beach. In this case, a small open-top car will be waiting for you at your case, which will take you to the sea and then take you back.

This hotel does not have a sandy beach. You can swim here from the pontoon and the pier, going into the water along the ladder. At the bottom there are a lot of corals and sea urchins, so it is strongly recommended to get special rubber shoes.

Aqaba Bay, according to many travelers, cannot boast of such luxurious coral reefs, which can be found, for example, off the coast of Sharm El Sheikh. However, you can watch the underwater inhabitants here. To do this, it is enough to ‚Äúarm‚Äù with a snorkeling mask and sail a little to the side of the pier.

As for the hotel's beach itself, it is quite large. There are many sun loungers and parasols, there is a bar.



Entertainment

Many travelers come to Dahab to enjoy water sports such as kitesurfing, windsurfing and so on. Indeed, in this area the wind almost constantly blows, which creates ideal conditions for such entertainments. Near the hotel there is an artificial lagoon. There is a surf school where, if necessary, professionals will give you several lessons, as well as offer to rent all the necessary equipment.

In addition, there is a diving center next door to Le Meridian Dahab Resort. Here you can also rent the necessary equipment and dive with an instructor.

As for entertainment on site, you cannot call them particularly diverse. After all, the hotel is designed for a relaxed and relaxing holiday. Therefore, you will not find noisy animations here. However, if you wish, you can always attend classes in water aerobics, yoga, play volleyball, tennis or billiards.
