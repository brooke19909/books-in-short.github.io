Hotel Golden Line 4 * (Golden Sands, Bulgaria): photos and reviews of tourists

Our article will interest those tourists who are planning a vacation in Bulgaria, which has not lost popularity for many years among our compatriots. Whatever the purpose of your trip - a romantic trip, family vacation or business, you will appreciate the new Golden Line 4 * complex (Golden Sands).

A bit about the hotel ...

Having chosen the Golden Line 4 * hotel (Golden Sands), you can always be sure of the quality of service and a decent level of services provided. Golden Sands resort has long been loved by our citizens as a holiday destination. For many years, Bulgaria has been a leader in popularity lists and still does not lose ground. The new Golden Line 4 * complex (Golden Sands) has become another gem of the famous resort.



The hotel is built on the coast, a hundred meters from the beach and seventy meters from the main promenade of the resort. Tourists will not be difficult to get to the complex, because Varna Airport is only 25 kilometers away. There is a bus stop near the hotel.

Golden Line 4 * (Golden Sands) is a seven-story building built in a park area.

Number of rooms

Golden Line ApartHotel 4 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) offers its guests 79 rooms of different categories:

  1. A studio is a great option for a group of friends or families. Apartments of this category are very spacious, but at the same time very functional. Bright rooms seem to be filled with sea air. The apartment has two single beds, wicker furniture, a wardrobe, a coffee table and a chest of drawers, a TV. Each room is equipped with its own kitchenette, dining table with chairs, electric stove, extractor hood, sink, electric kettle, refrigerator, kitchen and tableware. The bathroom includes a hairdryer and toiletries.
  2. One bedroom apartment. Rooms of this category are designed for secluded relaxation with maximum comfort. They are decorated in noble pastel colors, which gives a feeling of lightness and comfort. The apartments may have a view of the sea, pool, city or hotel park. The rooms have a bedroom and a living room with a sofa. In addition, there is a kitchen area, a large built-in wardrobe between the bedroom and the living room.
  3. Two bedroom apartment with a wonderful view of the pool or the sea. These rooms are suitable for families with children. The larger bedroom has a double bed, and the smaller has two single beds. Spacious kitchen area allows you to cook with comfort. Each room has its own air conditioning. The apartment has two bathrooms with cosmetics and a hairdryer. The large bedroom is equipped with armchairs, a coffee table, and a dining table.
  4. Three-bedroom apartments can be a good option for large families or friendly companies. These rooms are located on the upper floors of the building and offer stunning panoramic views. They are very spacious and each of the bedrooms has its own air conditioning. The apartment has two spacious balconies with wicker furniture. In the hallway there is a wardrobe, in the larger bedroom there is a chest of drawers, a wardrobe, bedside tables. The presence of the kitchen area makes the stay in the hotel even more comfortable.

SPA-center

Services for vacationers

Golden Line 4 * (Bulgaria, Golden Sands) provides its guests with free Wi-Fi speeds of up to 2 Mbps. If there is a need for more speed, then the service is provided for a fee (one euro per day). The hotel is one of the few complexes that accepts guests with animals. The only condition is pre-registration and coordination with the administration. For the stay of a pet you need to pay extra five euros per day. The hotel has its own parking, which can accommodate up to seventy cars.

A shuttle service is available at the property. Depending on what you need, it can be a transfer business, economy or VIP (the cost of the service from Varna airport is 30 euros, from Burgas - 150 euros).



The hotel has shops where you can buy souvenirs for relatives and friends, Bulgarian wines. In addition, the hotel has an English teacher who will help to increase the level of knowledge of the language (the cost of an hour of classes is 20 euros).

Children's holiday

What else is interesting Golden Line 4 * (Golden Sands)? The description of the hotel would be incomplete if you do not recall the services that are provided for the youngest guests. Since the complex is focused on family vacations, caring for children's leisure is quite logical. The hotel has a children's playroom and babysitting services. In addition, for children there is a separate pool with a shallow depth. It is located near an adult reservoir, which makes it possible to keep the situation under control. The children's pool uses thermal heated water, in addition, it is equipped with children's toys. The hotel has a playground with slides, swings and all the necessary attributes. The games room is located on the ground floor and is equipped with furniture and games. The hotel's restaurants have a special menu for children, and the spa has even developed chocolate massage sessions for young tourists.

Additional services

Hotel Golden Line 4 * (Golden Sands, Bulgaria) has one of the largest territories in the resort. The building of the complex is surrounded by more than a hectare of green space. The entire hotel is at the service of vacationers.



The complex has at its disposal several banquet rooms, so you can hold celebrations here. The rich interiors of the restaurants and the excellent banquet menu will make any event unforgettable. The hotel staff helps organize high-level celebrations.

In addition, the aparthotel has a conference room where you can conduct business meetings and negotiations. The hall is able to accommodate 120 guests, it is equipped with all necessary equipment.
