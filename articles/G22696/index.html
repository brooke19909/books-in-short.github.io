Buyuk Anadolu Didim Resort 5 * - hotel on the Aegean Sea. Description and feedback

Buyuk Anadolu Didim Resort 5 * is an excellent five-star resort complex. It is located on the very shore of the Aegean Sea, in the territory of the village of Aidan. It is about six kilometers from the town of Didim, famous for its ancient historical sights. The nearest airport to the hotel is in Bodrum. Before him, about eighty-two kilometers. The largest cities near the hotel are Kusadasi and Ephesus. The distance to them is seventy and eighty kilometers, respectively. The hotel has a private beach, well-equipped rooms, a spa, where excellent professionals work. Food at the hotel is beyond praise, and tourists leave mostly commendable comments on its services and entertainment. In the article we will get acquainted with this hotel and try to consider its main attractive features.



Didim

This town itself is not a resort, but rather a tour. If you are staying in Turkey on the coast of the Aegean, then you are sure to come here to at least see two things. This is the main local beach Altinkum (Golden Sands) and a huge temple of Apollo, built in the eighth century BC. Its ruins neither in size nor in antiquity are inferior to other famous structures of antiquity that are nearby. For example, the temple of Artemis of Ephesus. At the same time, the local sanctuary of Apollo is in much better condition. Here you can also see the ruins of the stadium where the Holy Games were held. This temple in importance and preservation can only be compared with the Delphic Oracle in Greece.

Rooms & Facilities

The Buyuk Anadolu Didim Resort 5 * hotel has a rather large area, about twenty square kilometers. She is beautiful, with views of the sea from everywhere. Alleys are planted with citrus and pomegranates. There is a small bird farm. The hotel is medium in size and accommodates two hundred and seventy-eight rooms. The most chic and sophisticated - four junior suites (area forty-two meters), twelve suites with living rooms.

Special guests are offered two Royal rooms. This is a luxury apartment, each of which is almost one hundred square meters. Standard rooms more than two hundred and fifty. They are large and spacious. All rooms are air-conditioned, the bathrooms have excellent plumbing and hairdryers. There is Internet access via Wi-Fi. One of the rooms is specially equipped for people who find it difficult to move independently. There are several shops on the ground floor in the lobby. The hotel offers free parking. A beautiful fitness center, jacuzzi, hammam, sauna - all these benefits are also included. There are good sports equipment. On the territory there are tennis courts, football and volleyball courts. Animation works - during the day of games and contests, gymnastics and dancing, and in the evening - shows and concerts. There is a good playground for children's games that meets all standards. She stands under a shady canopy. Near it is an olive grove where you can walk.

Nutrition

Hotel Buyuk Anadolu Didim Resort 5 *, like almost all Turkish "fives", works for tourists on the "all inclusive" system. The main restaurant specializes in Turkish cuisine. It is large, designed to receive three hundred and fifty people at a time, which excludes long lines at "peak hours". Two bars - in the lobby and by the pool - offer snacks and drinks. On the beach there is also an institution with cocktails. Tourists write about excellent food at the Buyuk Anadolu Didim Resort 5 * hotel. Reviews paint us the following picture. Sea, pools, beach

As mentioned above, the Buyuk Anadolu Didim Resort 5 * hotel has a private coast. The beach here is sandy, equipped. The sea is warm, clean, the entrance is shallow. Diving enthusiasts will have to swim for buoys. Beach towels are issued against a security deposit. There are always free sunbeds with umbrellas, very clean.

There are also two swimming pools. One of them is a mini water park. There are three slides that work twice a day on a schedule. Children really like to swim here, and the pools are always clean. A bridge is thrown over one of them.
