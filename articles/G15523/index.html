DIY embroidery on T-shirts: interesting ideas with photos, execution technology and patterns

In our wardrobe there are always things that can be decorated or remade. Still good things, spoiled with a speck in a prominent place that does not wash. Jeans or trousers worn on the knee. T-shirts and t-shirts bought at the sale. Perhaps the time has come to sort out your closet with clothes?

Perhaps, in the process of analyzing the rubble you will come across things that make you want to pick up sewing accessories and do decoration. And we offer you a selection of embroidery on T-shirts with their own hands.

What do you need for work?

  1. Actually, a t-shirt. It is desirable that the thing be monophonic. Then do-it-yourself embroidery on the T-shirt will stand out and look organically.
  2. A sheet of paper and a pencil. They are needed for transferring circuits. Choose your favorite pattern, enlarge to the desired size. Attach the paper to the computer screen and circle the pattern. Even better if you have a printer at home.
  3. Disappearing or washable marker. It is necessary for marking the template on the fabric.
  4. Threads. Traditionally, floss is used for embroidery. You can experiment with the types of threads. Try adding lurex to the floss or embroider the patch with one shiny thread. Now in the assortment of stores there are lurex of different colors. Also try to add thin and thick knitting yarns in places.
  5. Scissors.
  6. Hoop. Necessarily required. Otherwise, the fabric after embroidery will be ‚Äúknitted", that is, gathered in folds.
  7. Different size beads, beads, sequins, rhinestones. Any template can be decorated with these elements. Pick combinations to your taste, don't be afraid to experiment. Then at the exit you will get an original designer embroidery of a T-shirt with your own hands.
  8. Needles. It is advisable to choose comfortable needles with a long flat eye. Threading will be easier.

Do-it-yourself stitch embroidery on a t-shirt

The technique of this embroidery is simple and complex at the same time. Used seam pl called "forward needle", uncomplicated in execution. Craftswomen recommend clearly transferring the contours of the pattern to the fabric in order to confidently make the next stitches. The surface smooths the picture with a dense layer, the thread lies in one direction. Sometimes the outline of the pattern is embroidered separately.

The stitches are divided into oblique and straight. Straight stitches should go clearly in the direction of the thread of the embroidered fabric. The smooth surface is characterized by such a technique as a smooth surface with flooring. To do this, the outline of the pattern is performed with the usual stitch, the whole element is filled with it. After this, the thread, which should be thicker than the main thread, covers the entire pattern. When using this technique, the embroidery will look voluminous and convex.

And if you try cross-stitch?

Very popular technique. Known since time immemorial, she has long won the respect of millions of needlewomen. Countless patterns and elements - and just one kind of stitch. It is carried out by floss thread. Do-it-yourself cross-stitch embroidery on a T-shirt opens up great opportunities.

You can embroider individual small items.

And you can embroider a whole picture. These seals are embroidered with a cross, and to give the work volume and realism, the edges are trimmed with long stitches. They imitate wool. Very beautiful and interesting idea.

For this technique, the main thing is to master a simple stitch and correctly transfer the pattern to the fabric. Recently, programs for creating circuits have appeared. There are both paid and free. The main charm is that you can make a diagram according to any picture or photo. Then print it and embroider.

Thus, with the help of such applications, you can even embroider a photo of your family on the fabric. Or create an original gift - a T-shirt with an image of a birthday person. Such a present will not go unnoticed.

Another idea is to combine embroidery and applique work

A very beautiful T-shirt turned out.

For this work, it will be necessary to stock up pieces of fabric. Chiffon or organza will do. Circles of different sizes are cut from the fabric. The edges must be treated with a lighter or a candle. This is done quickly. You can not scorch the fabric with the edge of the flame, as it smokes.

Then a flower is gathered from circles of different sizes and sewn onto a T-shirt. The center of the flower is filled with beads and beads. The stems are embroidered.

Conclusion

In conclusion, I would like to mention some nuances.

  1. If you will decorate things from knitwear or any other elastic fabric, special needles will be required. An ordinary needle is not suitable for this purpose.
  2. To transfer patterns to fabric, use tracing paper. Copy the picture to it. Attach tracing paper to the fabric. At the end of work, the paper easily comes off and leaves no residue.

Now you know how to make embroidery on a T-shirt with your own hands. We wish you good luck and creative success!
