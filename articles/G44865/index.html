Robot vacuum cleaner: reviews, 5 best models, rating, names and functions

The modern woman has less and less free time for a thorough cleaning of the house. The only salvation in this case are specialized devices that help greatly facilitate the process of maintaining cleanliness in the house. A good example of this is specialized robotic vacuum cleaners. They automatically clean the room. If you are not sure which equipment is best to buy, then it is worthwhile to study in detail the reviews of the 5 best models of robotic vacuum cleaners.

Criteria for choosing a robot vacuum cleaner

Any person who wants to purchase a modern device must understand the criteria for his choice, as well as focus on the ratio of "price / quality". If we talk about the company, then there are no special criteria. Now many manufacturers from China are creating not only high-quality, but also not inferior in terms of value to European analogs devices. In addition, there are vacuum cleaners of Asian brands, which are included in the group of premium appliances and are considered the most high-quality and effective when cleaning the house.

Autonomy

Due to the fact that such devices operate on a special battery, the buyer should pay special attention to their autonomy. For an apartment with three rooms, you can purchase a robot vacuum cleaner, designed for two hours of work. If the device works less than the necessary time, then it will not be able to completely remove the apartment and it will have to be replaced with a new one.

Dust collector capacity

Another important factor when choosing a technique is the capacity of the dust bag. The containers for collecting garbage in an automatic type vacuum cleaner do not differ in their large size, for this reason it will depend on the volume of the dust collector how often you will have to clean the equipment.

Route building

The built-in function of automatically building a route around the apartment is the next criterion. All devices work autonomously, that is, a person does not exert any efforts during their work. For this reason, the robot vacuum cleaner needs to somehow navigate the house. Especially the route building function is important for cleaning an apartment with several rooms. Models from the budget class for the first time remember the whole apartment, and after that they already work along the previously laid route, which helps to quickly and efficiently clean the entire apartment.

Such devices can be controlled through a specialized control panel or using the remote control. The second method is considered more comfortable to use, since a person can, even without getting up from the couch, return the vacuum cleaner to its original place or install a program of re-passage to a certain place in the house that seems poorly cleaned.

Instrument size

The size of the device will affect which interior items in the apartment the vacuum cleaner will be able to call in and which ones will drive through. Another quite important factor is the fact that many modern models of robot vacuum cleaners are equipped with special wheels that can lower or rise, that is, they have peculiar shock absorbers. Using this function, the device can easily overcome obstacles in its path (for example, low objects and wires). In technology from the budget class there is no such function, so it just gets stuck in place.

Brushes and sensors

Also, special attention should be paid to the number of built-in brushes and sensors. There are such models of robotic vacuum cleaners that have only one main brush. Such a model is considered to be the best option, since the device cannot drive close to the wall and collect garbage in the immediate vicinity of it. The same can be said about the corners of the room. They spin and throw all the dirt on the main brush.

For orientation in space, special infrared sensors are built into the vacuum cleaner, which automatically detect the obstacle in front and help to go around them in time. In addition, such a sensor prevents severe contamination of the device. In some devices, there is a sensor that takes into account the height of the object in front. This will prevent the vacuum cleaner from getting stuck under the cabinet or other furniture in the room.

Functional Robot Vacuum Cleaner

In vacuum cleaners from an expensive category, an automatic cleaning function is built in. The principle of its operation is as follows: the charging base empties the container in the vacuum cleaner, which allows the device to continue cleaning. After cleaning, the owner of the equipment only needs to remove rubbish from the charger from time to time.

There are situations when in a room you need to delimit a certain space that a vacuum cleaner should not call into. This can be achieved with a simple magnetic tape, which is glued to the floor. For ease of use, some devices have a special program that allows you to configure it to work on the days of the week or a special timer.

The built-in parts of the robot vacuum cleaner may break down over time. Especially often this happens with batteries and filters. For this reason, when purchasing a device, it is important to remember that after a year or more, its parts may have to be replaced with new ones, which means that one-day firms will not work.

Particular attention should be paid to the following factors:

  • dust collector volume;
  • Battery capacity;
  • the presence of the function of scanning the room and remembering the route;
  • control from the remote control or electronic panel;
  • the presence on the market of parts suitable for this model of vacuum cleaner;
  • other additional functions in the device.

Conclusion

Which robot vacuum cleaner is better? To begin with, it is important to decide what budget the user is willing to spend on the purchase of a new technological device for cleaning the house. It is also important to evaluate the required battery life of the device. If the apartment has three rooms, it is best to purchase a vacuum cleaner that can work for several hours. The suction power of dust and dirt will directly depend on the power of the device. For lightly loaded floor coverings, a vacuum cleaner with a low power - up to 60 watts is well suited. Reviews of the 5 best robot vacuum cleaner models recommend paying special attention to the Philips and Kitfort brands.

The size of the device will directly depend on its patency. The more compact the device, the greater the likelihood that it will be able to independently reach the most inaccessible places.
