The best hotel by the sea in Sochi. Selection tips, reviews of tourists

Sochi is one of the most popular Russian resort cities. Rest here is suitable for people who like the active life of the city, as well as its rich variety of cafes, restaurants, entertainment venues and clubs. On the beaches there is a wide selection of water attractions - jet skis, "bananas", "bungee", etc. It must be borne in mind that a hotel by the sea in Sochi will be expensive. A standard double room can cost up to 5,000 rubles per season. per day per person. Many of the hotels often have their own private beaches, equipped and well-groomed.



A paid (or closed) beach area can also be assigned to orphanages, health centers and sanatoriums. You can find a hotel by the sea in Sochi, located next to open public beaches, rather dirty and crowded, near which all the main entertainment venues are concentrated.

When considering hotels with a pool in Sochi by the sea, it is worth noting the Marins Park Hotel. This is one of the largest hotels in the city. Here guests are offered more than 400 rooms. People arriving by car will not have problems with their device - the hotel provides parking.
