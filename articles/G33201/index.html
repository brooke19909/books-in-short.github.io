The second reason may lie in an unstable source of customer income. This refers to potential borrowers who do not work anywhere officially. That is, it can be builders and contractors performing design work, freelancers, artists who have irregular fees, etc.

Reason Four: The Tainted Story

Perhaps the most important point that lenders pay attention to is their impeccable credit reputation. That is, the borrower is obliged to comply with all the rules prescribed in the loan agreement, not to violate the terms of the monthly payment, to deposit money exclusively in a strictly specified sequence and amount.

And, accordingly, if the borrower has violated the rules established by the bank, then his credit history will be damaged. And this means that it is rare for any lender to agree to deal with an unscrupulous client.
