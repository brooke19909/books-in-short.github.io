Attractions around Selyatino

Having chosen the Rus Hotel for your relaxation, you can get acquainted with the most interesting sights located in the vicinity of Selyatino.

  • Visit the Petrovskoye-Alabino estate (four kilometers from the village), luxurious in the past estate. And although now it is not in the best condition, this unique ensemble deserves attention.
  • Take time to visit the Afineevo estate, located ten kilometers from Selyatino. Now, only the Church of St. John the Baptist, the fountain, the foundation of the estate and the path paved with marble have survived from the rich lordly possession.
  • The Sidorovskoye estate, located eleven kilometers from the village, has an interesting history. It was famous for its picturesque ponds and magnificent parks. Many ponds are no longer today, but in the surviving park, tourists can admire linden, larch, Siberian fir.
  • Twelve kilometers south of the village of Selyatino, you can visit the Trinity-Odigitrievskaya desert, founded in 1826. The women's monastery, which was located on its territory, was given the status of a monastery. Since March 2007, the Trinity-Odigitrievskaya Desert again acts as a women's Orthodox monastery.
