How to connect, use and how to disable "Auto payment" ("Sberbank")?

This article will provide information on the concept of "Auto Payment", talk about its advantages, settings, and connection. What is it for? How to disable "Auto Payment" ("Sberbank")? Examples will be given.

What is "Auto Payment" and how does it work?

"Auto payment" is one of the types of operations designed to improve service. Auto payments are a good way to replenish a mobile phone account without commissions automatically from a card opened in Sberbank.

Is it available to everyone? Owners of corporate cards and Sbercards are not able to connect this service. In this case, you can do this. If you do not have an international card or it is the property of a third-party bank, contact the nearest branch (namely, Sberbank).

Car payments are not only for paying for cellular communications, but also for settlements with utilities. It also provides for the repayment of credit debt (including third-party banks), the transfer of contributions to the FIU. But more often than not, the population uses "Auto Payment" to pay for telephone bills.

The holder of a registered international card of Sberbank sets a minimum threshold for the amount on the balance of the phone. And if the limit approaches the minimum value, funds are automatically transferred to the specified account of the subscriber (in the established amount). When the transfer is completed - the SMS notification about the operation is received to the owner of the phone.

Payment for communication services of any operators can be made from one card, but you can only connect the Auto Payment offer to one of the numbers to which a specific account is linked.

Auto Payment service settings

The holder of an account with Sberbank and a cell phone can set certain settings. Several offer options allow each client to choose the option that suits him best.

For example, write-offs may not be made upon reaching a certain threshold, but every month, week or day in a certain amount. You can set a maximum limit - limit the transfer from card to phone. The banking threshold is 30,000 rubles. The minimum transfer amount set by a financial institution is 30 rubles.

The advantages of automatic payments

One of the advantages is the speed of replenishing the balance of the phone and the inability to go into the negative. The service is triggered wherever the client is. Automatic operations are mandatory reflected in the statement of the current (personal) account. You can make changes to offer settings at any time. This service is completely free.

The scheme for connecting the service "Auto Payment" from "Sberbank"

To forget about constant replenishment of the balance of the phone, you only need to submit an application for connecting the proposal in the department of a financial institution. There is another option, for example, if you are a client of Sberbank, then using the Mobile Credit Organization you can activate the Auto Payment service. Its activation functions are available in ATMs and bank terminals. If the service is available to the subscriber, then a text message arrives at his phone number within one and a half hours (but no more than 24). If the notification goes unnoticed, then the client agrees that the service is connected and will be constantly executed. If someone is denied by the bank in this offer, then a notification (SMS) will also be sent indicating the reason for the refusal.
