Hotel Grand Hotel Palace (Greece / Thessaloniki): photos and reviews of tourists from Russia

The luxurious 5-star Grand Hotel Palace, located in Thessaloniki, enjoys an extremely good location. Only a 15-minute drive separates it from the airport, and within a radius of 2-3 kilometers are concentrated such interesting sights as the Palace of the Emperor Galerius, Democracy Square, the Basilica of St. Demetrius, and much more.

However, a good location is not the only plus of this complex. A lot can be said about him, but now I would like to dwell on the most interesting features.





general information

So, the Grand Hotel Palace is the largest 5-star conference hotel in Thessaloniki, existing since 2004. Once in it, it is impossible not to feel the aristocratic atmosphere and not to feel creative contrasts in harmony with warmth, hospitality and tranquility.

The complex, located just 2 kilometers from the city center, is impossible not to notice. This is a beautiful 5-storey building, made in the classical style, successfully fits into the surrounding landscape. The neoclassical style will attract everyone's attention - pediments, columns, marble, wrought iron, and inside - balusters, natural wood, skillful carving, sophisticated draperies, sparkling chandeliers ... you need to see for yourself.

Business structure

As already mentioned, the Grand Hotel Palace is a hotel of a special orientation. Therefore, it should be noted that there are two large modern conference centers on its territory, the total capacity of which is 3,300 people.

They, in turn, have eight spacious rooms with all the necessary equipment for official events. But that is not all. There are also two meeting rooms, two banquet rooms and a business center.





Entertainment

Despite the fact that the Grand Hotel Palace, located in Thessaloniki, is a conference hotel, it is possible to brighten up your leisure time quite well.

The first thing that deserves attention is a wellness fitness center with an indoor heated pool, constantly cleansed with ozone. On its territory there is an excellent gym, sauna and steam room.

There is also a spa, where guests are offered a wide range of different types of massage and treatments that harmoniously combine therapeutic techniques and the secrets of modern cosmetology. Naturally, masters use only natural products.

Wedding organization

This is another activity of the Grand Hotel Palace. Highly qualified, experienced specialists annually arrange hundreds of different weddings - modern, classic, custom and private.

The hotel itself has everything you need to ensure that one of the most important events in life goes perfectly. In any of the banquet rooms can accommodate up to 1,000 people, so even a wedding with a grand scale will be held with a bang.

The chef will develop a special creative menu taking into account the wishes of future newlyweds, and the master pastry chef will bake a luxurious designer cake. The hall and the venue will be decorated with stunning floral arrangements. However, everything else will also fall on the shoulders of the organizers - starting with the installation of lighting, ending with the selection of music.

When ordering a wedding package of services, the newlyweds receive:

  • Accommodation in a bridal suite with late check-out.
  • VIP reception - a greeting in the form of champagne, a basket of fruits, honey and nuts.
  • "Honeymooners breakfast" right in the room.
  • Wedding cake and champagne.
  • Tasting dishes to design a festive menu.
  • Photo / video shooting.

This, of course, is only a small part of the standard package. You can develop your own! The desire of the guests at the Grand Hotel Palace is above all. But for this, of course, you will need to contact the administration directly, and long before the planned celebration.

Accommodation options

Now we can say a few words about the rooms that the hotel has. So here are the categories here:

  • Deluxe, 25 square meters. m. Bed options: either one 2-bedroom or two standard. All deluxe apartments are elegantly decorated with wooden furniture, air conditioning, a minibar, plasma TV and a balcony.   • Executive Room, 28 sq. M. m. The choice of beds is similar, conditions too. VIP privileges are available: access to the Prive business center, covered parking space, compliment from the hotel (wine, fruits, sweets). Week of residence - 65-70 tons

Grand Palace in Thailand

It will be useful to get distracted and pay attention to a hotel of the same chain located in Pattaya. And this is the Grand Jomtien Palace Hotel. Three star, really.

It is located a minute walk from the sandy beach. On its territory there is a large spa, an open-air bath, several pools, a sauna, a gym, as well as a coffee shop and restaurant.

Compared to the Greek complex, the Grand Jomtien Palace Hotel in Pattaya offers extremely low prices for accommodation. In addition, there are discounts! In winter, a week in an improved double room for two people costs only 17-18 tons! And in a junior suite - less than 40 tons. True, at the height of the holiday season, prices rise, but this phenomenon is found in resorts everywhere.
