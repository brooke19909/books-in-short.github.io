Hotel Millennium Resort Patong 5 *, Thailand, Phuket: reviews

Holidays in Thailand have long been loved by travelers from around the globe. The world-wide popularity is ensured by dense tropical forests, as if descended from the pages of glossy magazines, affordable five-star hotels with good service, comfortable weather for most of the year, an abundance of exotic dishes and fruits, the opportunity to swim in the Indian Ocean and get to know the local fauna.

The five-star Millennium Resort Patong, built in 2007, is located in the south of Thailand on the island of Phuket. The favorable location of the hotel and quality service made it a popular and favorite vacation spot for tourists from all over the world.





Hotel location

Millennium Resort Patong ( Phuket ) receives positive reviews for a convenient location. Located next to Bangla Road's famous night street, the hotel is just a five-minute walk from the beautiful scenic Patong Beach. Translated from Thai, the word "patong" literally means "banana forest." It is in this area that the main entertainment and shopping centers of Phuket are concentrated , life is seething here.

The hotel consists of two buildings - Beechside and Lakeside. One of them is located in the midst of a dynamic life, the other is located somewhat away from this bustle, which makes the hotel suitable for both active tourists and a relaxing family vacation.

Hotel Exterior

The hotel is located on the second line of Patong Beach. The Millennium Resort Patong offers breathtaking tropical landscapes. The airport is a 40-minute drive from the hotel, a shuttle service is provided, and just a couple of minutes walk is a large shopping center where you can go shopping or a restaurant.

The hotel also has two of its restaurants, where they are always excellently prepared and welcomed by visitors.

In addition, guests can relax near the beautiful pools of the Millennium Resort Patong, where there are always free sun loungers, use the hotel's fitness room or visit the spa, and guests arriving with their car can leave it in free parking.





Interior Millennium Resort Patong

The hotel rooms are spacious and bright with comfortable luxurious furniture, with large panoramic windows through which a breathtaking view. Guests note the friendly hotel staff and unobtrusive quality timely service. The rooms are always comfortable and clean, there is free internet access, the availability of toiletries and bath accessories and a bar are regularly updated. Despite the proximity of a crowded street, a night bazaar and an entertainment center, the hotel has excellent soundproofing and street noise does not hurt to enjoy your vacation.

Hotel kitchen

Guests of Millennium Resort Patong 5 * (Thailand) invariably remain delighted with the dishes served in the hotel restaurants. The cooks are true masters of their craft, they use only high-quality products, create a unique game of tastes and take care of a beautiful aesthetic presentation. Most of the hotel restaurant tables are located on the street. Breakfast in the fresh air under the rustle of palm leaves, in which a warm tropical wind is tangled ... What could be better?

There is also a room delivery service when you want maximum privacy and peace.





Services provided to guests

The hotel administration is important to maintain a high standard of service. Therefore, they try to make the rest of the guests as comfortable as possible. To ensure that guests do not worry about anything and are not distracted by the everyday routine, the hotel provides a wide range of services: shoe shine, ironing, laundry, skilled babysitters who will look after children, currency exchange, dry cleaning, newspaper delivery ... At the reception you can order tour or rent a car and explore the sights on your own. Valuables can be handed over to the hotel safe and not worry about their safety. You need to enjoy life in Phuket, and Millennium Resort Patong understands it!

For guests with disabilities, special rooms are equipped where the entire furniture set and plumbing are maximally adapted to special needs.

Is the hotel suitable for families with children?

At first glance, it might seem that Millennium Resort Patong is a hotel for active party-goers only. But it is not so. Reviews of those who came to rest with children are no less enthusiastic than the responses of individual tourists or youth companies. The hotel always welcomes the smallest guests. Attractions nearby

Near the hotel is Patong Pier, which offers a charming view of the stormy waters of the ocean. The pier can be reached in less than half an hour on foot, while exploring the colorful streets of the city.

One and a half kilometers from the hotel complex is Simon Cabaret. Opened in 1991, now this cabaret is considered the most spectacular in Phuket and attracts many curious spectators.

In the southern part of the island you can see the majestic beautiful temple complex of Wat Chalong. Temple buildings are striking in their elegance and richness of decoration. The territory of the complex is well-groomed, beautiful, with green spaces. The atmosphere that reigns at Wat Chalong contributes to peace. From the hotel to the temple can be reached by car, or by ordering an excursion to the travel agency at the hotel.

Phuket FantaSea Park is 7 kilometers from the hotel . This is a real Phuket legend! A fantastic amusement park, bright, as if covered with a myriad of confetti, it will be interesting to both adults and children. On the territory there is a theater, a zoo, a terrarium, shops ... In the evening, guests of the park will enjoy an unforgettable Thai-style show, based on the ancient local epic about the prince!
