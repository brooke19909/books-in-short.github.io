Hotel Venice Beach 3 * (Tunisia, Djerba Island): description, service, reviews

In search of a new tourist destination for a vacation with the whole family, you should pay attention to Tunisia, or rather, its island part - the island of Djerba. The flow of travelers here does not dry out all year round, and many tourists return here more than once.

There is no particular secret to such popularity. Vacationers are attracted by the mild climate, sandy beaches, thalassotherapy resorts and wonderful landscapes, very reminiscent of the Caribbean. In addition, the velvet season here lasts quite a while, and the cost of trips is acceptable (compared to many European resorts).



A lot of hotels have been built on Djerba, one of them is the 3 star Hotel Venice Beach.

Hotel location

This hotel is located on the island of Djerba in the Midoun tourist area. At a distance of 18 km from the hotel is the largest city of the island and its capital - Houmt Souk. Its population is 64 thousand people. Tourism infrastructure is well developed here, so vacationers will be able to feel as comfortable as possible.

Hotel Venice Beach is 25 km from Djerba Zarziz Airport. Upon arrival (if transfer is not provided), the tourist area can be reached by public transport or by taxi.

The beach is at a distance of about 300 meters, so you can get there on foot in 5-10 minutes.

General description of Hotel Venice Beach 3 *

The hotel was built in 1993, but at that time it was called differently - Venice Beach Djerba Midoun. After major repairs in 2011, the hotel was renamed. Hotel Venice Beach 3 * is made in the classical Moorish style and is a two-story bungalow and a three-story building. The hotel has 159 rooms.

The surrounding area is well equipped. Everywhere trimmed lawns, well-groomed trees and shrubs. There are places to relax where you can sit in silence and enjoy the wonderful view.





Hotel facilities

Considering that the hotel has 3 * status, the guests are provided with the most comfortable living conditions. Reception offers:

  • 24-hour reception;

  • luggage storage for guests;

  • safe at the reception (fee for this service is charged separately);

  • rent a car (paid separately);

  • transfer (at the request of guests);

  • housekeeping every day;

  • laundry service;

  • Internet at the hotel (paid service);

  • use of umbrellas and sun loungers by the pool.

The entire hotel staff speaks several languages, so there will be no particular difficulties in this regard.

Hotel rooms

The hotel offers to accommodate:

  • standard rooms;

  • family rooms.





Regardless of which room guests choose, it will be clean, bright and spacious. The apartments provide everything that may be required to ensure the comfort of residents:

  • TV with satellite TV;

  • telephone;

  • a bathroom (there is a bath or shower);

  • terrace or balcony (the space is equipped with a place to relax, where you can just sit with a cup of coffee and enjoy the surroundings);

  • air conditioning.

Entertainment at the hotel

Most tourists arriving in Tunisia at Hotel Venice Beach 3 * spend a lot of time on the golden beaches of the island or go on excursions. Those who decided to stay at the hotel will not be bored either.

The hotel has a sufficient amount of entertainment:

  • indoor pool;

  • outdoor pool;

  • entertainment program (day and evening) - animators;

  • video games and slot machines;

  • bowling;

  • a pool table.

For those who want to always be in shape and not relax even on vacation, there are many opportunities for sports:

  • well-equipped gym;

  • tennis court;

  • table tennis;

  • mini golf course;

  • universal platform for basketball and volleyball.





health and beauty

SPA procedures and beauty industry services will help you enjoy your stay in this paradise. Tourists can choose:

  • Access to the spa (facial and body treatments are offered here);

  • massage room;

  • a sauna;

  • jacuzzi;

  • hammam (Turkish bath);

  • beauty salon (with hairdresser, nail service).

All these services are available to hotel residents at an additional cost.

Nutrition

Hotel Hotel Venice Beach 3 * offers all-inclusive accommodation. On the territory there are:

  • a restaurant;

  • main bar;

  • pool bar;

  • american bar.

Meals in the main restaurant are offered as a buffet. Opening hours:

  • from 06:00 to 10:00 - breakfast;

  • from 12:30 to 14:00 - lunch;

  • from 19:00 to 21:00 - dinner.





Children's and vegetarian cuisine is served free of charge.

In the bars there are:

  • alcoholic beverages (this is vodka, gin, beer, whiskey, local wine);

  • soft drinks (soda, coffee, tea, water, concentrated juices).

Drinks must be paid separately.

Hotel infrastructure for children

On Djerba, Hotel Venice Beach 3 * is considered a family hotel, so children of any age can stay on site. Infrastructure and services are well thought out, so parents do not have to think about what to do with their children.

The hotel has:

  • Children's swimming pool;

  • playground;

  • mini-club for children from 4 to 12 years old;

  • babysitting services (they need to be paid separately);

  • the restaurant provides highchairs;

  • in the room at the request of guests, you can install a cot;

  • the restaurant has dishes for children (children's menu).

Beach

Beach holidays are the main attraction on the island of Djerba, since there are not so many attractions here as on the mainland of Tunisia. The entire coast is divided into separate beaches, most of which relate to a particular hotel. The beach of Hotel Venice Beach is a well-maintained coastal space with fine white sand. Here, tourists can use umbrellas and sun loungers.

This beach is perfect for a quiet family vacation, including with children. For the smallest vacationers on Djerba there are excellent conditions: a gentle descent into the water and a non-rocky bottom. The coastal area is regularly cleaned, so there is no garbage and algae on the shore.

There are city beaches in the district, however, they are located at some distance and are less adapted to a comfortable rest. On the beach, Hotel Venice Beach 3 * you can not only sunbathe and enjoy a cool cocktail, but also have fun. Fine sand is perfect for active beach games on it. There are also water slides, yachts (you can rent) and other water activities.





Additional Information

Among other things, there are several facts that may be useful to visitors.

  1. In the hotel you can relax with children of any age.

  2. Pets are not allowed in the hotel.

  3. The local currency is the Tunisian dinar, but here they are willing to accept euros and dollars.

  4. Paid both in cash and with a plastic card MasterCard, Visa.

  5. The velvet season lasts from May to October.

Hotel Venice Beach 3 *: reviews

Every year, Hotel Venice Beach 3 * Hotel hosts hundreds of tourists who come to Tunisia to get to know this colorful country and bask in the warm sunshine. Most of those who leave feedback are completely satisfied with their stay on the island of Djerba and, in particular, the level of service at this hotel. Most often, the following details are noted:

  • daily room cleaning;

  • always tasty fresh food;

  • diverse cuisine (a large selection of meat, side dishes, always fresh pastries, lots of fruits);

  • good animation;

  • Well-trained and very helpful staff.

Many tourists say that they would love to come back here again and feel free to recommend the hotel to other travelers.

There are also those guests who were not completely satisfied with their stay at the hotel, in the list of the most common reasons:

  • poor staff knowledge of the Russian language (mainly communication takes place in French and English);

  • lack of sun loungers and umbrellas on the beach;

  • slow Internet.

Hotel Venice Beach, located on the island of Djerba in Tunisia, is a family-friendly hotel, perfect for those who are primarily looking for the opportunity to enjoy a beach holiday in a quiet place. The cost of accommodation is quite affordable given the all-inclusive basis. At the same time, the hotel has a status of only 3 stars, which means it is suitable for those who do not have high requirements for living conditions.
