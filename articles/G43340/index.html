Beaches Taman: description, photo, reviews

The uniqueness of the geographical location of the Taman Peninsula makes it an attractive holiday destination for tourists. But there are so many diverse beaches that it is not easy to choose the right place. Most often, tourists come to the village of the same name with the region, although the peninsula has many other excellent villages and beaches. To choose the best beaches of the village of Taman or the entire peninsula, it is worth reading our article in which we will talk about the features of recreation in this region.





Geographical position

In the south of the Krasnodar Territory there is an amazing place - the Taman Peninsula. It is washed by the waters of two oceans: Black and Azov, as well as the Kerch Strait. The flat terrain, the long coastline with a large number of convenient bays, a favorable climate make Taman a very convenient place for a beach holiday. The beaches of Taman are more than 200 km long. High, picturesque, steep banks alternate with gentle, comfortable approaches to the water. The peninsula is distinguished by diverse vegetation and beautiful landscapes. The main source of fresh water in the region is the Kuban River and its tributaries. A feature of the Taman Peninsula is a large number of estuaries and mud springs. The latter have a therapeutic effect on humans, so relaxation can be combined with recovery.





Climate

The beaches of Taman are in the zone of temperate marine climate. The weather is due to the influence of two such different seas - the Black and Azov - and the mountains' insecurity from the influence of the continent. Taman has a large number of sunny days, there are more of them than in Sochi or Anapa. The summer season begins in May and ends in October. The average summer temperature is +24 degrees. The main difference between the climate of Taman is a long, warm and comfortable autumn. That is why the best grapes in Russia are grown here. Winter in the region is short, with strong winds and rains, on average the temperature at this time of year is about 2 degrees Celsius. Spring on Taman is early and dry. Flowering of plants begins in March-April, and in May it is already possible to sunbathe and swim in shallow water.

Central beach

The name was given to the region by the village of Taman. The central beach of this village is the oldest equipped place to relax.
