How to sign up for tax online: tips and tricks

The tax service in Russia begins to receive citizens not only in the order of a "live" line, but also by appointment. This is very convenient for those who are used to saving their time. Today you have to understand how to enroll in tax through the Internet. Is it possible to realize an idea at all? What should every citizen know about this process? Understanding all this is not as difficult as it might seem at first glance.



Is there any chance

Can I sign up for tax through the Internet? Moscow or any other city is not so important. Throughout Russia, the same rules apply regarding the issue under study.

Fortunately, today the entry to the Federal Tax Service using the World Wide Web is possible. To translate ideas into reality, there is a special service. With its help, it is proposed to make an appointment with the tax authority of any region of Russia.

General rules

What should each taxpayer learn about before using the appropriate service? There are a number of rules to keep in mind.

How to sign up for tax through the Internet? To do this, you will have to remember that:

  1. Recording is carried out only for the next 14 days. If you are lucky, you can book time for the next day.
  2. It is allowed to issue several reservations. The number of entries proposed to be implemented is 3 visits on the same issue. This means that with the help of the Internet, they record in the tax only 3 times in 2 weeks. But for several reasons, you can come and make reservations on the same day.
  3. You can be late no more than 10 minutes.
  4. If the employee who conducts the reception is busy, the citizen is guaranteed reception within half an hour from the time of recording the consultation.
  5. It is important that the information used when working on the Internet, and real data about the citizen match.
  6. It is offered to receive at visit only that service concerning which the appointment was made. Therefore, it is recommended to book several windows in advance on various issues.

Having remembered all this, a citizen is able to easily get a consultation with the tax inspectorate. There is nothing difficult or strange in the rules.



Recording process

But how to sign up for tax through the Internet? To do this, you need to visit the official page of the Federal Tax Service of the Russian Federation (nalog.ru). It is here that there is a specialized recording service.

A step-by-step instruction for translating ideas into reality is approximately as follows:

  1. Go to the nalog.ru page and look for "Appointment Record" there. For faster implementation of the idea, it is proposed to visit the website order.nalog.ru/details.
  2. Examine appointment information. About it has already been said earlier.
  3. Agree to the requirements and rules. To do this, at the bottom of the page, click on "I agree."
  4. Fill in all the fields that appear on the site. It's about user data. At the end of the process, click on "Next".
  5. To indicate information about the reason for visiting the tax service, as well as about the time of the visit.
  6. Confirm the reservation of a particular date.

That's all. Citizen will be issued an electronic coupon. It is either printed at home or in the tax office in a special terminal.
