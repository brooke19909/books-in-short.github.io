Jaz Mirabel Park - Nabq Bay

The chic Jaz Mirabel Park, which is part of the Mirabel Resort complex, along with two other hotels, is built on the Red Sea coast. Tuscan style in the architecture of the buildings in harmony with the surrounding landscapes.



Description

The territory on which the complex is located is simply amazing in its size - 120 thousand square meters. meters, fully landscaped and thought out to the smallest detail.

The hotel infrastructure includes shops, laundry, luggage storage, a small conference room, disco, currency exchange, lobby, medical room, shopping center, beauty salon. It is impossible to simply list the entire range of services that are offered to vacationers: it is swimming in pools, and sports, and music.

You can just walk along the alleys among the beautiful lawns with flowers or relax under the branchy palm trees. The 24-hour reception desk has a safe, a rental car, rental equipment for swimming. Here you can arrange a transfer, use the services of a tour desk.



The nearest airport is ten kilometers away. Sharm El Sheikh is twenty minutes away by car.

Rooms

The Jaz Mirabel Park housing stock in three-story buildings consists of 196 Superior and Superior family categories with pool views. Some have a separate sleeping area with sliding doors.



The rooms have comfortable new furniture, a minibar and a safe. If necessary, an iron and ironing board are available on request. All rooms are connected to the air conditioning system.

Combined bathrooms have hygiene products, a hair dryer and bathrobes.

In front of the rooms are small balconies with comfortable thatched furniture, where you can enjoy the surrounding panorama in the evenings. On the floor - ceramic tiles.

Nutrition

Jaz Mirabel Park offers an all-inclusive meal plan. This means breakfast, lunch and dinner from seven in the morning until midnight in a buffet style . Serving food at the main restaurant "Al Flooka". All bars offer free local drinks and snacks. Three other restaurants - Italian La Piazza, Chinese Zen and Oriental Tarbouch - are √† la carte.





Jaz Mirabel Park has three bars. They are located on the beach, in the lobby and by the pool.

Beach

Jaz Mirabel Park has its own beach, which is twenty minutes walk. Entrance to the water is very convenient for children, there is also a pontoon. Everything you need for bathing and sunbathing - towels, umbrellas and trestle beds - is free for hotel guests.

Five hundred meters from the coast there are excellent coral underwater places, very popular with divers.

Additional Information

Children Jaz Mirabel Park 5 * offers a children's section in the pool, a small slide, a playground, a club, the use of high chairs in restaurants.

The beach offers a wide range of water activities, including snorkeling, diving, water polo. Equipment can be rented at the beach or at the reception.

While the kids are playing, their parents can swim in one of the two large pools, go down from the water slides, sit in the Internet cafe, visit the fitness center and massage room, sauna or hammam. Fans of sports games will enjoy playing football, tennis, basketball.

For the use of billiard tables and tennis courts in the evening, you need to pay extra.



The hotel has a tour desk that organizes a tour in any direction.
