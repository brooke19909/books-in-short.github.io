Sanatorium Anapa "Pearl of Russia": description and conditions

On Pionersky Prospekt, ten kilometers from the center of the southern city, there is a year-round cycle complex - the Anapa sanatorium “Pearl of Russia”. It corresponds to its name - it is a whole children's town, with its well-developed infrastructure for children's recreation and entertainment system.

The sanatorium is designed for organized recreation and recreation of children. Their stay in this complex meets not only the goal of preserving and strengthening their health using natural factors. Here, a system of children's recreation is thought out in such a way that a favorable climate becomes a natural background for the full spending of vacation time.

Description of the complex. Anapa: “The Pearl of Russia”





The sanatorium, whose photo is decorated with prospectuses about children's vacations in the Krasnodar Territory, is a recognized children's resort of Russian scale, an analogue of which cannot be found. Everything breathes care of children here - parks, beaches with children's playgrounds, a system of health centers. Every year, thousands of children from all over the world take a rest here.

Anapa Sanatorium “Pearl of Russia” is a complex of four buildings: “Solnechny”, “Sokol”, “Primorsky”, “Cosmos”. The complex also has well-equipped cottages.

The complex organizes children's vacations according to the concept of a children's recreation camp. Check-in is carried out according to a shift schedule. Accommodation in dormitories on the territory of the sanatorium camp takes place in living rooms, designed for up to six people. There are toilet and shower rooms next to the living rooms. In the cottages amenities are located in the living rooms. Children with complicated forms of the disease are accommodated in double, triple rooms. Professionally trained personnel work with them - pedagogical and medical personnel.

Sanatorium Services





Known for its comprehensive services Anapa. "Pearl of Russia" - a sanatorium, whose photo eloquently speaks of the conditions for children's relaxation - carries out rehabilitation of children on the basis of a modernly equipped medical and diagnostic complex with a hospital department. For guests there is a sports complex "Antey", a pump room with mineral water. The health resort has a wide profile for the treatment and prevention of diseases.

During school hours, classes are held in the classrooms of the resort college school. V. A. Budzinsky. The price includes full five meals a day, wellness, cultural and recreational activities. Upon arrival of organized groups of children, a transfer is provided.

In the territory occupied by the sanatorium of Anapa “Pearl of Russia”, there is everything necessary for leisure and recreation of children: game rooms and sports grounds, clubs, a swimming pool, a hundred meters from the buildings - equipped beach. This is the hotel’s own sandy beach, equipped with sanitary conditions, preventive measures for the safe stay of children near the water.





Recreation and entertainment

Rest of children is organized according to the game leisure program "Sea Brotherhood". They are organized into units, taking into account their wishes and age characteristics. Work with children is carried out by a team of animator counselors with special professional training.

Depending on the interests and inclinations of children, they can spend their free time in a creative, sporting, entertaining form. For this, in a city such as Anapa, the children's pearl sanatorium "Pearl of Russia" offers:

  • creative workshops in circles and sections;
  • discos;
  • children's karaoke bar;
  • Watching movies;
  • Friendship Festivals;
  • sports and entertainment events;
  • work in the press center;
  • Junior TV and Radio Studio.

Documents required for check-in and check-in

To arrange a ticket and check into the Anapa sanatorium “Pearl of Russia”, you will need to prepare documents according to the list:

  • An extract from a medical card about a child’s health.
  • Certificate of vaccination.
  • Certificate of presence / absence of epidemiological contacts.
  • Insurance policy.
  • Birth certificate.

You can get to the resort by rail to the station. "Tunnel" or by air to the airport of Anapa.

In case of the arrival of an organized group of children from fifteen people, a transfer to the sanatorium in Anapa “Pearl of Russia” is provided for free. Private transfer service is possible upon prior request for an additional payment. Transport stop - “Pearl of Russia”.
