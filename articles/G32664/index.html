Hotel Villa Bonita (Budva Montenegro): photos and reviews of tourists

Montenegro is an unusual country, which is surprising for almost all tourists, without exception. Why is it so unique? This can be talked about endlessly. Many of our compatriots are struck by the combination in the local cities of some kind of poverty and simplicity, reminiscent of Soviet times, with European accuracy and restraint. Such a mix causes a great desire to get to know Montenegro closer, especially since its resorts are really worthy to relax on them with the whole family. A particularly beautiful place is the spa town of Budva. Its residents are proud of their history, ancient monuments of architecture and the magnificent sea coast. The advantages of Budva can also include low prices for hotels, hotels and dinners in restaurants. This makes the rest accessible to the average average Russian family. Among the abundance of hotel complexes of the resort, everyone can choose something special and inexpensive. Today we will tell you about the Villa Bonita Hotel . This cozy place will surely appeal to those who dream of a measured rest surrounded by historical monuments with a touch of salty sea spray.





Hotel Description

If you prefer huge multi-story hotels, then Villa Bonita in Budva is not your option. But those who know how to appreciate the hospitality of the hosts who personally meet each guest, the delicious home-style cuisine and cozy atmosphere, will come from this hotel complex in real delight.

Villa Bonita fully lives up to its name; it is a small four-story villa with thirteen rooms. The hotel is designed in a traditional style combining simplicity, soothing colors and modern amenities. Many of the guests in their reviews note that they would love to return here more than once.

Hotel Villa Bonita (Montenegro / Budva) was built in the late nineties, but the last renovation was carried out recently - just five years ago. This is evident in the new furniture, excellent textiles and flawless technology.

The hotel complex Villa Bonita belongs to the "three rubles", that is, it has three stars. However, his guests note that this is one of the best "triples" in the resort. Therefore, you will not be able to book a room just before the trip, in the high season the apartments are booked for five to six months. And this indicates the huge popularity of the hotel.

If you focus on the reviews of tourists, then we can say that Villa Bonita 3 * is an excellent option for a budget tourist. In addition, it is recommended for families and is perfect for young people who plan to spend only nights in the hotel.





The location of the hotel complex

Since Montenegro is a relatively small country, it is difficult to find resorts and hotels located at a decent distance from the airport. Therefore, Villa Bonita 3 * in Budva has a very convenient location relative to the city center and other resort areas.

If you have landed in Podgorica, then you will have to drive about sixty-five kilometers to the hotel, but from Tivat, where there is also an international airport, you will need to overcome only twenty-seven kilometers.

It is nice that Villa Bonita is located a little distance from the old district of Budva, considered one of the most important attractions of the city. Tourists arrive here in 10-15 minutes, because the distance is not more than one kilometer.

If you want to explore the entire resort, you can use public transport. Two hundred meters from Villa Bonita 3 * (Montenegro) is a bus stop. Therefore, it will not be difficult for you to get to the center of Budva or the nearby coastal villages, where you can see a lot of interesting things and try national dishes.





Hotel beach

It is worth noting that Villa Bonita is located on the second coastline. Hotel guests will have to cross the road to get to the beach. It belongs to the category of sand and gravel and belongs to the municipality.

A total of five hundred meters separating the hotel complex and the beach area, you can walk in seven minutes. In addition, the road near the hotel is not very busy and does not cause its guests any problems.

The Slovenska Plazha beach stretches for more than one and a half kilometers. There are relaxation areas with sunbeds and umbrellas. Please note that they are only available for a fee.

Infrastructure

Since Villa Bonita 3 * (Montenegro) in Budva belongs to small family hotels, there are not too many infrastructure facilities on its territory. However, it is worth noting that most hotels in Montenegro have a limited territory and are not too actively involved in the development of infrastructure. In the country, the tourism industry is only beginning to gain momentum, so the local hotel complexes are significantly different from the similar ones, for example, in Turkey or Egypt.

You can always rent a car at the hotel. It is very convenient to move around the resort and its environs, so many tourists are happy to take this opportunity. In order not to worry about the safety of the car, hotel guests leave it in free parking. It is equipped with a small number of seats, but problems associated with their shortage never arise.

If necessary, guests can use the laundry facilities. For a small surcharge included in the bill for accommodation, your items will be washed and ironed while you enjoy swimming in the sea or sunbathing on the beach.

The hotel has one cozy restaurant twined with ivy and grapes. It is very pleasant to spend time with a glass of wine or a delicious homemade dessert prepared by the caring hands of a local chef.





Facilities and service

Our compatriots leave reviews about Villa Bonita very good. Everyone notes the simple and homely atmosphere created by the owners of the hotel. They always greet their guests with refreshments and are interested in all the nuances of their relaxation, they are ready to help or suggest something at any moment. Often the owner of a hotel complex absolutely unselfishly carries guests to local beaches and attractions.

The hotel catches Wi-Fi perfectly, so you can freely use your laptop and tablet as you wish. Rooms are cleaned daily; it includes a mandatory change of towels, which is very positively appreciated by tourists from Russia.

Air conditioning is installed throughout the hotel, so it is very comfortable to be in it even in the hottest weather.

Hotel stay with children

Villa Bonita in Montenegro is happy to host families with children of any age. Since the rooms in the hotel complex are quite spacious, an extra bed is quietly included. It is better to warn the hotel owners in advance about such a need in order to avoid misunderstandings when checking in.

Unfortunately, there are absolutely no entertainments for children at Villa Bonita. Here you will not find playrooms and specially equipped playgrounds, so make sure to take toys from your home for your baby. Otherwise, you will have serious problems in order to organize the leisure of the child.

There is no special children's menu in the restaurant. But the cooks will always be happy to prepare something special at your request, there is absolutely no problem with this. The owners of the hotel are always trying to please their young and adult guests.





Nutrition

Since there is only one restaurant in the hotel, all the meals of the guests are held in it. Villa Bonita offers the possibility of booking rooms with breakfast included in the price or with two meals a day. You can choose any option at your discretion.

Tourists enthusiastically describe the dinners at the hotel. They always consist of several dishes on the menu, and large portions do not leave a single chance to leave the restaurant hungry. If you do not want to look for a suitable cafe in Budva, you can have breakfast, lunch and dinner at the hotel. A delicious three-course lunch will cost you no more than six euros. Many of our compatriots claim that they do not feed anywhere cheaper and tastier at the resort.





Number of rooms

The small family-run hotel has only thirteen rooms spread over four floors. Keep in mind that the ground floor apartments do not have windows, but otherwise they are quite comfortable and have everything you need for guests.

Each room in the hotel is equipped with a kitchenette, where there is a stove, kettle and all household utensils that you may need when cooking. The apartments are air-conditioned, a TV with several Russian channels, a balcony or terrace (second to fourth floor). The floors in all rooms are covered with parquet board, which makes the room even more comfortable.

The bathroom has a shower and a set of towels per guest. Please note that beach towels are not provided at the hotel. Therefore, for trips to the sea you will have to purchase them yourself in the shops of Budva.

The cost of rest at the hotel

Montenegro does not apply to countries where vacationing will become a ruin for the family budget. Therefore, many of our compatriots book hotels on their own and purchase tickets. However, it is still better to fly to Montenegro on a tour package.

On average, the cost of a week-long stay at Villa Bonita for two with a flight from Moscow will cost you sixty thousand rubles. This price includes airfare and breakfast. If you plan to take half board, then in this case the ticket will be more expensive by about seven thousand rubles.

Some tourists claim that if you wish, you can buy a tour to this hotel for fifty thousand rubles. It is just necessary to follow the offers of tour operators and be ready to pay almost instantly.
