Didim Beach Resort Aqua 5 * (Didim, Turkey): description, service, reviews

The Aegean coast of Turkey is a great place to relax, because tourists will find here not only the notorious sea and beach, but also monuments of ancient culture. One should not forget: prices for tours to Turkey are much cheaper than to Europe. In other words, here you can relax with chic for a much smaller amount.

Tourists will be able to find on the coast and small cozy hotels, and large hotels such as Didim Beach Resort Aqua. This institution has a rank of 5 stars, and, according to tourists, fully justifies it.

Description

Didim Beach Resort Aqua 5 * is a fairly large hotel that can accommodate up to 1000 guests. The private territory of the hotel is 19,000 square meters. km, which houses the hotel, swimming pools, parking, a beautiful garden and much more. The hotel building consists of 2 buildings, each of which has 8 floors.



Didim Beach Resort Aqua is a relatively new hotel, the year of its foundation is considered to be 2001. The last renovation in the buildings was carried out in 2007. This establishment has a good reputation, which was able to provide high-quality hotel services, an excellent beach and a lot of entertainment in the vicinity.

Where is it located?

Hotel Didim Beach Resort Aqua 5 * has a very convenient location. It was built in a quiet corner on the very shore of the Aegean Sea, surrounded by palm trees.

  • The small town of Didim is only 1 km from the hotel.
  • At the same distance (1 km) is the picturesque town of Altinkum.
  • Bodrum Airport is 90 km away.

Service at the hotel Didim Beach Resort Aqua Elegance Thalasso 5 *

Those who are looking for a comfortable hotel with an all-inclusive accommodation system should definitely take a closer look at Beach Resort Aqua 5 *. Everything is provided for a comfortable stay of tourists, and well-trained staff is always friendly and helpful. What is offered at the hotel?



  • Reseption. The reception is open every day, seven days a week, 24 hours a day.
  • Luggage storage. You can use this service immediately after registration - the camera is at the reception.
  • Parking place. Car parking is located in the courtyard of the hotel (in a private area). This service is completely free.
  • Security. The issue of security in a hotel is taken especially seriously.
  • Porter services. Vacationers do not have to carry heavy suitcases to their room. The porter (porter) will quickly cope with this task.
  • ATM. You can use it around the clock.
  • Elevator.
  • Laundry and dry cleaning services. Work is carried out quickly and efficiently, but this service is paid.
  • The Internet. Wi-Fi is free in the lobby.

For business travelers, the hotel offers a spacious well-equipped conference room.

Room Facilities

The hotel rooms are relatively small, but this does not prevent them from being comfortable and homely.



Each room has a tiled private bathroom. There is a toilet, bath or shower, washbasin, hairdryer.

  • Safe. You can use the safe to store valuables for a small fee.
  • TV with satellite television. There will be channels in Russian.
  • Mini bar. Free in the minibar only bottled water. All other drinks need to be paid separately.
  • The flooring in the buildings is different. The Elegance has carpet and the Resort has laminate.
  • Air conditioning system. Each room has a split system.
  • Telephone. Use of the telephone is offered free of charge.
  • A coffee maker or kettle for making coffee at any time.
  • Furnished balcony. It is not available in all rooms.

Nutrition

The food system is represented by several institutions at once. Among them:

  • 3 restaurants (2 of which a la cart);
  • 5 bars.

The main restaurant offers all-inclusive service and serves visitors buffet style. Daily guests are welcome here for breakfast, lunch and dinner.

The snack bar serves snacks and freshly baked pastries from 12:00 to 16:00.

Sport cafe, Dolphin Bar And Pool Bar are open until 24 hours, here you can try local drinks, both soft and strong.

Opening hours of the beach bar - from 10 to 18 hours.

In the disco-bar you can spend time until 02:00.

Free activities and sports

It is unlikely that vacationers can get bored here, even if they decide not to leave the hotel at all. Didim Beach Resort Aqua 5 * has excellent infrastructure.



  • Fitness Centre. A well-equipped gym opens its doors daily for those who want to keep their bodies fit.
  • Sauna or Turkish bath. Everyone chooses which option to choose, however, in any case, time will fly by unnoticed.
  • Table tennis. Tables are located in the entertainment area.
  • Darts.
  • A good playground for outdoor games (this is volleyball and basketball).
  • Animation. During the day and night, the hotel hosts entertainment programs.
  • Night disco in one of the bars.
  • Indoor heated pool.
  • 2 outdoor pools (one of them is equipped with 8 water slides).

Entertainment and sports for a fee

Those who find the free entertainment service at Didim Beach Resort Aqua 5 * not enough will be advised to use other leisure options.

  • Massage. Massage rooms are tastefully equipped, and sessions are conducted by experienced and very skilled craftsmen.
  • Tennis court. Equipment rental will have to be paid. Moreover, this is a great opportunity to learn how to play tennis - the coach works with beginners.
  • Bowling room.
  • Billiards. Paid rental equipment.

Fun for kids

The smallest travelers will also be able to have fun, because no less interesting things have been prepared for them:

  • children's pool (shallow depth, which makes it as safe as possible);
  • water slide;
  • mini-club with games and entertainment (service for children aged 4-12 years);
  • animation (entertainment during the day).

In addition, parents can always use an extra baby bed in the room and high chairs in the restaurant.

Beach

The hotel is located about 300 meters from the coast. The beach here is clean sand, so it is a pleasure to be here. It offers tourists sun loungers, mattresses, umbrellas.

The Aegean coast is ideal not only for a quiet beach tour, Didim Beach Resort Aqua 5 * will offer a lot of entertainment for active tourists:

  • diving (an instructor works here);
  • riding a banana;
  • surfing (equipment can be rented on the beach);
  • canoe ride.

Didim Beach Resort Aqua 5 *: reviews

It is worth noting that there are not so many reviews from Russian-speaking tourists about this hotel. As it becomes clear from the stories of the guests themselves, this hotel is not particularly popular among our compatriots. Mostly Europeans and Turks themselves rest there. However, most leave praise.



Service. Hotel Didim Beach Resort Aqua Elegance Thalasso 5 * is decorated quite stylishly and pleasantly, tourists note the increased attention of staff and administration. Help where necessary. The hotel does not speak Russian at all.

The food is tasty and quite varied. In addition to the main meals, barbecue and snacks with fresh pastries are regularly arranged.

Animation. This service is often remembered, as it is really very high quality. The animation team conducts a new program every day, organizes contests and competitions.

Of the negative aspects, it is worth remembering only that it will take about 1.5 hours to travel from the airport to the hotel. This is a bit boring, especially on the way there is absolutely nothing interesting.

Small hotel area. With maximum occupancy, crowding is felt.

In general, the Didim Beach Resort Aqua 5 * hotel makes a good impression and (apparently) fully confirms its status as a 5-star hotel. It is worth going to active travelers, lovers of a quiet beach holiday and lovers of a good excursion.
