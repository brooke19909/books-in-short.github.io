Lenovo K3 Note Smartphone: review, specifications and reviews

Lenovo does not get tired of pleasing us with budget models whose quality is becoming ever higher. So, the developer of Android devices introduced the world to a new device at an affordable price. This is a smartphone Lenovo K3 Note, a review of which will be the basis of this article. Its cost in the online store of the company is only $ 150, while the functionality and capabilities of the model went far beyond this price segment.

What is this smartphone, what technical characteristics does it have, finally, what kind of reviews can be found on the Internet - all this you will learn from our article.

Positioning

The model is really offered to customers as a budget, more affordable version of a productive and powerful smartphone from a Chinese developer. However, there is nothing wrong with the fact that the country of origin of the model is China. Build quality, stability and the level of components themselves indicate that the device is worthy of us. A brief overview of the characteristics, taking into account the price of Lenovo K3 Note White, makes it possible to note a really attractive ratio of quality and price of the device.





Equipment

The model is offered in the original box from a simple, matte with yellowness cardboard. It indicates the name of the manufacturer and a brief information in Chinese. There are no official deliveries of the phone to Russia, so no one will adapt such details.

Opening the box with the Lenovo K3 Note K50, you can see the device itself, instructions (written in hieroglyphs), as well as a set of adapter and cord for connecting the smartphone to a PC. No other accessories or additional options were found with the device.

Design and Build

The body design of the device does not contain anything original, but is presented in a somewhat unusual form for Lenovo. So, the entire back part, as well as the side faces of the smartphone, are united by one plastic cover. The front part is a protective glass under which the display hides. Thus, the apparatus consists, like a sandwich, of two layers stacked together. Although this does not prevent the phone case from being monolithic and tightly knocked down.





The texture of the mentioned cover is also quite pleasant to the touch - it is matte, due to which the phone lies comfortably in the hand. Three combinations will be on sale - yellow, black and white. And in general, all dimensions of Lenovo K3 Note (the review proves it once again) are selected as accurately as possible and beneficial to the user. Thus, the smartphone is really easy to use.

The navigation buttons located on the sides of the device are attached directly to the back cover. Having removed the latter, it will not be possible to fully control the model. The device has two cameras. The resolution of one is 13 megapixels, the other is 5 megapixels. There are no flashes, which could illuminate the photographing area somewhat, the device has a special software option that gives the pictures brighter tones.
