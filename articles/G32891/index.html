Grand MetroPark Bay Hotel 4 * (China, Sanya): reviews, photos

Sanya is located in the south of Hainan Island (China). The city is rightfully considered one of the most beautiful places in the Country of China, and therefore often becomes the choice of couples and honeymooners for a honeymoon. Here the bright sun, clear water, diverse fauna and flora, the jungle, there are even hot springs. In addition, Sanya has a very rich ethnic culture, many attractions, as well as various places for a pleasant pastime.

To accommodate everyone who wants to spend their holidays here, a lot of hotels have been built in Sanya. One of the popular options in the city is Grand MetroPark Bay Hotel 4 *. What the hotel is and what kind of living conditions the administration offers tourists will be discussed in this article.

Hotel location, transport accessibility and nearby attractions





Grand MetroPark Bay Hotel is in close proximity to Phoenix Island, where the international airport is located. If necessary, you can use the shuttle service. It also offers free access to Haitang Bay and Dadonghai Tourist Area. Seats must be reserved in advance.

The city has well-developed transport links. It is also possible to rent a car to drive around Sanya on your own with the aim of exploring the city. For example, nearby Grand MetroPark Bay Hotel 4 * there are such attractions:

  • Luhuitou Park;
  • Dadonghai;
  • Town Square;
  • Yalong Bay (it has a rainforest park).

Exact Address: No. 133 Nabianhai Road, Sanya, Hainan, 572000, China.

Description and necessary information





The hotel has everything necessary for an excellent holiday, whether the trip is business or its purpose is to get to know the city. The four-star hotel was opened in 2013 and renovated in 2016.

It is a twenty-story building, the exterior and interior of which are made in oriental style. Each element is well thought out from a design point of view, thanks to which tourists feel comfortable both in rooms and in common areas.

Registration of guests is carried out around the clock, there is the possibility of early check-in. You must have an identity document with you. The minimum age for self-registration is 18 years. Departure is at noon. When booking a room, the hotel requires prepayment.

Supply system





Every day, guests are given the opportunity to have a buffet breakfast. The service is not included in the price and is paid extra. At other times, guests of the Grand MetroPark Bay Hotel 4 * (Dadonghai) can have a meal in one of the two restaurants. Phuket Island's chef invites you to enjoy the unique taste of seafood barbecue, while Victoria culinary offers delicious local, Cantonese and Sichuan dishes. There is a bar and a snack bar with a variety of snacks. Breakfast, meals and drinks can be delivered from the restaurant to your room.

The hotel has a tour desk, which offers tourists an unforgettable trip to Sanya. Grand MetroPark Bay Hotel 4 * has a large outdoor pool, around which there are sun loungers with umbrellas with the ability to do water sports and a fitness center for those who follow their figure. Living conditions for children

If you believe the reviews, the Grand MetroPark Bay Hotel 4 * (Sanya) does not have cots, although it is allowed to stay with a child of any age. For an additional fee, you can use the services of a children's club, where professional animators work. In addition, there is a playground for games and a small pool. Independent living of children is prohibited (up to 18 years old only accompanied by an adult).

Cost of living





Prices for hotel accommodation are significantly different from the season, type of room, length of stay and some other factors. For example, in the fall season the cost of a trip for two for 8 nights can range from 50,000 to 60,000 rubles. There are also cheaper and more expensive options. But the positive reviews of tourists about the Grand MetroPark Bay Hotel 4 * (Sanya) make it clear that the price is more than justified, since the hotel is very good. About the opinion of vacationers will be written a little lower.

For the entire stay, a fee of 300 CNY is mandatory. If there is such a need, an extra bed for sleeping is organized in the room. The cost of a folding bed is 200 CNY (about 1,700 rubles) per day. Other possible additional payments are recommended to be specified directly at the hotel.
