Kirovograd Hotels: Overview

Throughout the history of its existence, the city was called: Fortress of St. Elizabeth, Elizavetgrad, in Soviet times - Kirovograd, now the city is called Kropyvnytsky.

The city is located in the central part of Ukraine on both banks of the Ingul River. At this place at different times were the sites of the Cimmerians, Scythians and Sarmatians. Excavations of numerous mounds around the city date the first settlements to the Middle Paleolithic era. In the middle and end of the XVIII century, the central location and intersection of several trade routes led to the construction of the fortress of St. Elizabeth on the banks of the Ingul River and several Cossack settlements.

Golden time of the city

An economic impetus to the development of the city was given by the permission to hold four fairs, which merchants gathered on all sides. In the middle and end of the XIX century, the industrial development of Elizavetgrad (Kirovograd) began. At this moment, they began to implement the general development plan: they built a large bridge over Ingul, numerous stone houses, laid new boulevards and parks, then the first inns and hotels of Kirovograd appeared. The stimulus for the development of the city was the construction of the Kharkov-Odessa railway through Elizavetgrad. This is connected with the construction of several industrial enterprises, the emergence of a telephone exchange and telegraph, electric tram, water supply and other benefits of civilization.





Kirovograd Hotels

Hotels of Kirovograd in the historical city center:

  • Hotel "Tourist" (ul Ushakova, 1a) has 163 rooms of different comforts. It has a parking lot, restaurant, hairdresser and laundry.
  • Hotel complex "Europe" (st. Karl Marx, 13). A large hotel in the business center of the city with extensive infrastructure, which includes a restaurant and a night cafe, a fitness center and a beauty salon, a billiard room and a gazebo with barbecue facilities.
  • The Kiev Hotel (Karl Marx St., 50) is a six-story hotel with improved planning rooms, there is a cafe-bar, and some of the premises are leased for offices.

These are old hotels in Kirovograd, reviews of which are very different. Usually everyone is satisfied with the price for a short stay, location and a good breakfast, included in the price of the room. However, it is worth noting that the majority of those living in them felt themselves in the deep Soviet past.





Modern hotels

After 2010, modern hotels in Kirovograd were built.

  • Among them stands out the Reikartz Hotel (1d Chernovola St.), in which 62 modernly equipped rooms. At the disposal of visitors there is a parking lot, a conference room, and a breakfast buffet is included in the restaurant each morning, which is included in the price of the room. According to visitors' reviews, the Reikartz Hotel is always a high level of service.
  • The private hotel "Maria" (Komarova str., 33/1) offers 23 rooms of different comforts, the cafe serves delicious homemade dishes, and you can relax in the sauna with pool.
  • The Elizavetgrad Hotel (Soborna St., 1a) has 17 rooms. It offers a fitness center, billiards club, a 24-hour supermarket and a cafe. The hotel receives excellent reviews. Good interior, friendly staff, cleanliness and high level of services are noted.
  • Hotel "Aeropol" (Poltavskaya St., 20) can offer 17 small but comfortable rooms. Located near the airport. Which is very convenient for early departures or for transit passengers. It features a conference room, a restaurant and a sauna. Guest reviews about the hotel are positive. Many consider it one of the best and, arriving in the city, stay in it.

Country hotels in Kirovograd

The photo cannot convey all the beauty of the picturesque place on the river bank, where the Eagle's Nest hotel and restaurant complex is located (the village of Subbottsy 24 kilometers from the city).

Eagle's Nest is a modern hotel that has seven rooms and is ideal for families. In addition to the standard range of services, a good restaurant and bar, there is a playground with sun loungers near the outdoor pool, and there is also a Russian and Turkish bath with the services of a bathhouse attendant and masseur. In this complex you can have a good wedding and celebrations, business meetings and presentations. Fishing and boat trips are organized. According to visitors' reviews, this hotel is really a quiet and cozy corner in the lap of nature, where you can comfortably relax from the bustle of the city.



Country hotels in Kirovograd are also represented by two mini-hotels, favorably located on the outskirts of the city at the intersection of republican routes, which is very convenient for travelers and drivers of transit vehicles.
