Black Sea coast - the best vacation



Dreaming of a good rest? The Black Sea coast is waiting for you! The beaches, the stunning nature of its beauty, many places for recreation, entertainment and amendment of their own health. Learn more about the Russian tourist paradise!

The Black Sea coast of the Krasnodar Territory is one of the most visited resort places in our country. The mild climate, combined with the heterogeneous landscape from the mountains of the Caucasus and the flat terrain, create a corner of amazing beauty and picturesqueness that does not leave indifferent many tourists. The swimming season in the Black Sea region lasts from late May to October, winters are mild and warm, and therefore you can relax on the Black Sea all year round. In summer, resorts offer tourists to visit water parks, beaches, gardens, water sports, which attracts the exciting Black Sea.



In addition, the Black Sea coast is rich in attractions. You can get to the times preceding the ice age by visiting the Tisosamshitovaya grove located on the slope of the ancient mountains with its rare plants and animals kept in enclosures. Fans of exotic nature will also be pleasantly surprised to visit the Sochi Arboretum, Russia's largest park of subtropical plants, or the Riviera Park, founded back in the late 19th century. Connoisseurs of sour fruit will appreciate the Citrus Garden of Friendship. Connoisseurs of ancient Greek myths should definitely visit the Vorontsov caves, a labyrinth hidden in the rock and created by nature itself. Another "evidence" of the long-standing presence of the gods of Olympus in the Black Sea is the Prometheus cliff hanging over the waters of the Agura River 125 meters above the ground. According to legend, the angry Zeus chained Prometheus who stole the fire precisely to this rock. The Black Sea coast is also noteworthy for other wonders. Here you will find amazing archaeological sites - ancient dolmens. These are huge closed boxes-tombs, made of stones 3 - 3,5 thousand years ago. And do not forget to visit Stalin's dacha - a unique historical museum that keeps the secrets of the greatest leader of the times of the USSR.



Rest in the offseason differs from the summer season with the proposed activities, and the cost of living at this time is much lower. You can rest and at the same time improve your health in numerous boarding houses and sanatoriums that provide their clients with medical supervision and various healing procedures.

The Black Sea coast is famous for its most popular resorts, such as Sochi, Anapa and Gelendzhik. Other major resort cities will help you find out the map of the Black Sea coast.

Sochi is one of the warmest cities in the Black Sea region. Rich in entertainment centers, concert venues, hotels and sports clubs, Sochi is ideal for lovers of active nightlife.

Anapa, located on the other border of the Black Sea coast, is colder than Sochi, but it is in no way inferior to it in the number of entertainments, children's health camps, numerous boarding houses, and therefore deserves the title of the Black Sea health resort and resort for the whole family.

Gelendzhik is an ideal resort for lovers of measured relaxation. Walking on yachts, wild dolphins, pristine nature, natural beaches - all for big cities tired of the hustle and bustle.
