Rest in Kemer



Despite the fact that in its territory this town is noticeably inferior to many other Turkish resorts, its tourist infrastructure is developing so rapidly that the number of visitors here increases every year.

The resort has wide pebble beaches. The swimming season here lasts from May to the end of October.

Homer mentioned about Kemer in his Iliad. Nearby are the ruins of two ancient cities, so many of the tourists combine sea vacations, including on the famous Moonlight beach, in the citrus gardens of which are cool and shaded, with exploring the surrounding attractions.

Kemer Hotels

Resort hotels are divided into two types. In some of them, adherents of a quiet vacation stop, in others, on the contrary, energetic young people who like the closeness of entertainment. The only exception is the town of Tekirova in Kemer, on the beach of which there are small pebbles and where tourists often go with their young children.



The predominant part of the hotels of this resort has a vast territory and is located on the first sea line. They all work year-round. That is why Turkey is so attractive to many.

Sunland Beach Resort 3

This three-star hotel, built in the former fishing village of Camyuva, is seven kilometers from the center of Kemer. There are many shops and small cafes nearby.

It was built in 2002, when the resort was just developing. Since then, the hotel has been redecorated more than once . The last major renovation with the replacement of plumbing and furniture was carried out two years ago. And now, despite its low category, the hotel offers its guests comfortable and convenient rooms, which many hotels with a greater "star" do not have.

Territory

The area on which the Sunland Beach Resort 3 (Kemer) is located is six thousand square meters. It is designed as a huge garden, in the shade of large trees of which vacationers can breathe fresh air, admiring the sea and the surrounding Taurus Mountains. The hotel has many paths that illuminate numerous lanterns in the evenings.



Convenient signs are installed everywhere indicating the main infrastructures, so that you can‚Äôt get lost among the greenery.

There are many palm trees and fruit trees in the garden. A brook passes through the territory, ending with a small cascade and a fountain.

Infrastructure

Despite the fact that the Sunland Beach Resort is a 3-star, it nonetheless offers its customers a fairly wide range of quality services. He has a guarded parking lot that does not need to be reserved in advance. There is also the possibility of parking large tourist shuttles.

On the stone-walled territory of the hotel there are also several small shops, a souvenir kiosk. On the ground floor in a single building of the hotel there is a currency exchange office, there is the possibility of storing things in a special chamber, concierge and porter services.



A 24-hour reception desk has a depository safe, a photocopier is installed. Here, guests of Sunland Beach Resort 3 can book a shuttle to the airport, which is sixty-five kilometers away, as well as call a taxi. Many tourists rent a vehicle, including a bicycle, to explore Kemer's surroundings on their own. Those who prefer guided tours can book a sightseeing tour of this corner of Turkey, accompanied by a Russian-speaking professional. The hotel also has a room for first aid, laundry and ATMs.

Housing stock

Located seven kilometers from Kemer, Sunland Beach Resort 3 is a three-story building with ninety-six rooms for a maximum of two adults plus two small ones. At the same time, about two hundred and ten tourists can stay at the hotel.

Although the area of ‚Äã‚Äãstandard rooms is small, it is quite sufficient for a comfortable stay and a great holiday on the beach.
In front of all rooms there are small furnished balconies overlooking the pool with slides or in the chic garden. The rooms are equipped with split air conditioners with manual control, TVs (there is one Russian channel), telephone, minibar.

Bathrooms in the hotel are combined, there is a shower. Tourists can use a hair dryer. Accessories in the bathroom are updated daily, towels are changed with the same regularity. After a day, bed linen is updated.

The hotel does not have rooms for disabled guests, check-in with pets, even the smallest, needs to be discussed with the administration in advance.

The cost of living in Sunland Beach Resort 3, the photo of which is quite suitable for relaxing in it, can be attributed to the budget category of rest. Hotel Policies

Like every other, Sunland Beach Resort 3 has its own rules, originally established by management. Upon departure, for example, vacationers must vacate the room they occupy before twelve o'clock in the afternoon. At the same time, tourists finally pay for those additional services that were not paid in advance.

For convenience, many tourists do not carry cash, so they pay with plastic cards of payment systems, which are also valid in this hotel. But in order to avoid misunderstandings, it is more correct to discuss all payment options in advance with the administration.

Nutrition

Everyone who comes to Sunland Beach Resort 3 is offered an all-inclusive meal plan. This system consists of Swedish-style tables set up in a restaurant with a wide variety of dishes. Visitors are warned about the meal time in advance, upon check-in, giving them a note on the schedule.

There are also two bars - on the beach and by the pool. Here, hotel guests can eat or quench their thirst.

Sea



Vacationers in their reviews call the beach at Sunland Beach Resort 3 excellent. Indeed, the sea in this part of the Mediterranean is crystal clear and always warm. You can reach the beach in three minutes by crossing the motorway at the passage under it. There is a bar on the beach, and racks, trestle beds and umbrellas can be obtained without extra charge. Here you can play volleyball, use the toilet and shower. The entrance to the water is gentle, convenient for children. There is also a pier from which it is convenient to dive.

The beach is not sandy, but pebble, so it is better to have special slippers with you.

Entertainment

In addition to two swimming pools with slides - an adult and a children‚Äôs, around which sun loungers and umbrellas are placed - Sunland Beach Resort 3 offers a Turkish bath, massage room, gym, TV lounge, billiard room. Those who love sports will enjoy playing table tennis or darts, doing aerobics, chess.

Many tourists in their reviews especially note the interesting animation by the pool and on the beach. Every fifteen days, the hotel hosts the famous "Turkish Night". On the beach, tourists can go water skiing and canoeing, parachute jump, rent a jet ski for a fee .

For children



Sunland Beach Resort 3 offers a small pool for its guests, around which specially trained personnel are constantly on duty, a playground, arranged in the shade of tall palm trees. In the restaurant, parents can request high chairs and special menus upon request. The hotel's policy is to ensure that everyone, both adults and children, get the most out of their stay at Sunland Beach Resort 3.
