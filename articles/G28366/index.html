Rules of residence

The settlement of new arrivals is made from 14:30 local time. You can register from the age of 18 or with parents. Departure of departing persons lasts until 12:00. The hotel provides extra beds for young children, up to 2 years old, a cot is available upon request for free. For older children extra beds are not given.
