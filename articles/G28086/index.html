Wall-mounted speakers: overview, types, specifications and reviews

Acoustics for a home cinema only at first glance performs an auxiliary function of audio support. As practice shows, it is this component that provides the conditions for maximum immersion in the atmosphere of a movie, and even more so a musical work. Manufacturers produce such systems in various configurations, but wall-mounted acoustic systems occupy a special place in the lines as the most convenient from the point of view of operation.

Main characteristics

As in the choice of any audio equipment, the capabilities of a wall-mounted speaker system should be evaluated on the basis of parameters such as power, sensitivity and frequency range. For domestic use, you can focus on models whose power potential is in the range of 90-100 watts. At the same time, the power available to wall-mounted speakers should not be associated with volume. Rather, this indicator determines the reliability of the musical installation.





The quality and clarity of playback depends on the sensitivity. In this case, we can already talk about the power of direct sound. The optimal indicator for home use is considered to be 85 dB. Here it is worth paying attention to the spectrum of reproduced frequencies. The choice for this parameter should be based on queries. So, for a music lover, wall-mounted acoustic systems that process the spectrum in the range from 20 to 35,000 Hz are suitable. If the system is required as an auxiliary addition to a home theater, then you can limit yourself to a corridor of 100‚Äì20,000 Hz.

Varieties

In addition to operational characteristics, acoustics are divided by type of housing. Today, devices with a closed or phase inverter design are most in demand. In the first case, we are talking about the simplest version of the case, which fully meets the needs of the average user. However, for a better and more detailed reproduction of the lower resonance, a system with a bass reflex enclosure will be required. Both ceiling and wall-mounted speakers vary in material type. This point is especially important for systems with a hinged mount, since the additional load increases the requirements for means of fixation. Actually, to facilitate the installation task will help the plastic case, which is light in weight, but does not pamper the sound quality. Wooden cases give the opposite effects. They more accurately and more deeply lose the entire spectrum of frequencies, but due to the large weight they cause certain difficulties during the installation process.





Passive and active acoustics - what are the differences?

For the operation of any modern musical complex, amplifying equipment is required. Another thing is that there are different approaches to ensuring its function. The most winning solution in terms of sound quality are wall-mounted speakers with active amplification. In this case, each band in the form of a speaker will have a separate amplifier, to the input of which a filter unit is also connected. As for passive systems, they benefit from a simplified connection scheme. They do not need to connect a special linear signal. However, the clarity in this configuration will be significantly degraded.

By the way, the ability to adjust the volume on each band is another advantage that active speakers have. A review of the complexes presented below will allow you to make the best choice of acoustics.

How to choose the best option?

Depending on the requirements for the system, its characteristics are also determined. Moreover, it is not necessary to put at the forefront precisely the basic operating parameters. Often, the method of installation and placement in a room determines the merits of sound. At the same time, there are nuances on which saving is not recommended at all. This also applies to the materials used in the manufacture of the case, and fasteners, as well as auxiliary fittings and cables, due to which wall-mounted speakers operate. Wall acoustics gives many advantages to owners of modest apartments, but for their disclosure it is important to correctly install and connect the system to sources. Therefore, initially it is important to provide for accessories, due to which the overall installation of the acoustic complex will be implemented.





Conclusion

Acoustic manufacturers are improving systems in different directions using new tweeters and diffusers, as well as cables and fixing clips. In particular, wall mounts for new generation speakers are represented by magnetic elements and terminals, which have a minimal impact on the quality of the sound picture. But this also complicates the task of the user who needs to rationally and at the same time correctly complete the installation of components. After all, wall-mounted kits in terms of installation can be called the most complex.
