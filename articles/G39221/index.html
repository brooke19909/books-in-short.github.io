Electric kettles - the difficulty to choose



Electric kettles have become one of the essential elements of our everyday life. Currently, manufacturers offer a wide range of dummies. They come in various shapes and colors. Let's try to figure out which device is right for you.

Electric kettles give a cup of coffee or tea every morning to millions of people. There is a device in almost every home, and hundreds more are purchased daily. How to make the right choice among a huge variety? First you need to deal with the proposed range. First of all, it is recommended to study the manufacturers of electric kettles, reviews about each specific model, which will be to your taste.

The main criteria for choosing a device

The entire range presented by manufacturers can be conditionally classified according to several criteria.

Firstly, an important criterion is price. Now they offer electric kettles costing from three hundred rubles to several thousand. The price depends on the quality of the product, material, manufacturer and the availability of additional functions. There is plenty to choose from.



Secondly, the material. Most often, metal and plastic are used as the basis for the production of these devices, less often you can find electric kettles made of glass and ceramics. Let's consider in more detail.

Plastic kettles are the most common option. Low cost and ease of operation - these are the main factors that so attract consumers. The disadvantage of plastic is the chemical interaction with water, which can adversely affect the state of health.

Metal kettles are the most durable. With careful care, they can last for decades. The disadvantages of such models are the large weight (up to 2-3 kg), strong surface heating and high price.

People who strictly monitor their health are better suited for glass teapots. Such models have a significant minus - fragility.



The third important criterion when buying this device will be power. Today, electric kettles have a power of up to 3000 watts. This indicator determines the duration of water heating. The best choice would be a kettle with a power of 2 kW - it will provide quick heating of the water and protect against a power outage.

The fourth criterion for choosing the device in question is volume. It varies in a wide range - from 0.5 to 6 liters. Very popular teapots with a volume of 1.5‚Äì2 liters. This amount of water is enough for a small family. For convenience, many kettles have a special measuring scale that shows the water level.

The fifth important aspect is security. Most modern kettles have an auto power off function. In addition, there is also protection against inclusion. This option is indispensable for families with small children. After all, an object turned on without water can provoke a fire.

The selection of electric kettles is wide and varied. Analyzing all the pros and cons, we can make the right choice, focusing on personal preferences, needs and goals. Enjoy the shopping!
