Hotel New La Perla Hotel 3 *, Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt: photos and reviews of tourists

Despite the fact that most tourists dream of relaxing in a five-star hotel, there are people who value their impressions and natural beauties much higher than household comfort and service. The resort complex New La Perla Hotel 3 * offers its guests quite a budget holiday and relatively good living conditions.

Briefly about the hotel

Despite the fact that the New La Perla Hotel 3 * hotel cannot boast of a large number of stars, it is in no way inferior to its titled competitors. The rooms and the hotel have all the necessary conditions to make guests feel comfortable and relaxed.

Tourists have access to swimming pools (adult and children's), which host many recreational activities and sports games. And those who are inclined to spend their holidays calmly and leisurely are invited to simply soak up in sun loungers in the shade of umbrellas and palm trees. In addition, here you can improve your health and tidy up your figure using the services of a wellness center.

Of course, comparing meals in hotels with 4 and 5 stars with an assortment of dishes of the main restaurant of the New La Perla Hotel 3 * hotel, you can see that the choice is not so big. But this is exactly the case when a small amount is more than offset by excellent quality. As for the √† la carte restaurants, they can even compete with their titled neighbors.





Hotel location

Hotel New La Perla Hotel 3 * is compactly located in the village of Um El Side, which is located near Sharm El Sheikh. The hotel is 22 km from the airport, but only 5 km from the amazing picturesque Naama Bay. The hotel is located not very advantageous relative to the sea - 800 meters, which is typical for the second coastline. But this inconvenience is completely compensated by the fact that a regular bus carries tourists to the beach, which runs between the hotel and the coast all day with an interval of several minutes.

Hotel Apartments

Hotel New La Perla Hotel 3 * (Sharm El Sheikh) is a large three-story building, which compactly accommodates 106 guest rooms. Each room is equipped with all necessary furniture, and the most pleasant moment is, of course, a small balcony with wicker chairs, from which you can admire the pool and garden surrounding it. The minimum necessary to allow guests to feel comfortable is a TV with cable channels, an international telephone line, an alarm clock and a radio for those interested in local news.





Infrastructure and leisure facilities

Hotel New La Perla Hotel 3 * (Sharm El Sheikh) does everything possible so that its guests do not feel the slightest discomfort associated with the remoteness of the resort complex from large settlements and tourist infrastructure. The following facilities are responsible for a complete and comfortable stay:

  • the reception is engaged not only in the registration and resettlement of guests, but also in the resolution of all problems and disputes that may arise during your stay at the hotel;
  • for local tourists, as well as for foreigners who decide to rent a car for the period of rest, there is a large convenient parking with round-the-clock security and video surveillance;
  • if you are injured or feel unwell, then you have the opportunity to use the services of a doctor;
  • for maximum convenience, vacationers at the hotel operates a currency exchange office, ATM, as well as non-cash payment systems;
  • laundry and dry cleaning staff will help you to remove stains from things or to iron clothes crumpled in a suitcase;
  • You can find the necessary information on the global network or connect with friends and relatives using the services of an Internet cafe;
  • Turning to the tour desk, you will get to know all the local attractions, as well as the culture and traditions of the Egyptian population.




What does the all-inclusive system mean?

Each hotel makes its own adjustments and changes to the generally accepted system called "All Inclusive". So, the New La Perla Hotel 3 * hotel offers its guests the following services:

  • International buffet in the main restaurant for breakfast, lunch and dinner. This also includes drinks from local manufacturers, namely soda, tea / coffee, wine and beer.
  • At the end of breakfast and until late at night, you can have a snack with sandwiches, snacks and sweets in the lobby and other bars.
  • All hotel bars (except the 24-hour lobby) are open from 9:00 to 21:00 and offer a wide range of drinks. At the same time, a certain limit is set for wine and beer. So, each guest has the right to receive four glasses of beer and the same amount of red wine for free.
