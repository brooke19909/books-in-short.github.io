Refrigerator "Atlant". Consumer Reviews

Closed Joint-Stock Company Atlant began its history in the year nineteen fifty-ninth, when a government decision was made to create an enterprise for the production of refrigerators on the basis of a plant producing gas equipment. Until that time, similar household appliances were not produced in Belarus.





The first Minsk-1 refrigerator was released in nineteen sixty-two. After that, for the first time in the USSR, a two-chamber model of household appliances was developed and the production of freezers was started. At the same time, innovative thermal insulation from polyurethane foam was successfully used in production. The first delivery of goods to Greece in the nineteen seventy-third year opened the world market for the enterprise. Subsequently, the export of products began to be carried out in Belgium and Australia, England and France, Italy and the Netherlands, as well as many other European countries.





Currently, the Minsk Refrigerator Plant produces more than fifty different models of household appliances under the name Atlant, designed to store food. These are two-chamber and single-chamber devices having both the lower and upper locations of the chamber created for freezing. Different models are equipped with either one or two compressors, have a commercial or household purpose. Refrigerator "Atlant", customer reviews of which indicate its ability to satisfy all the necessary consumer needs, is easy to choose for everyone. This allows you to do a variety of manufactured models.





The Atlant refrigerator, whose customer reviews speak of its high quality, is manufactured using ozone-friendly refrigerant, an economical motor compressor, a foam evaporator and a soft line design. This became possible due to equipping technological cycles with the most advanced equipment made in Japan, Germany and Italy, as well as the use of computer design and modern materials.

The Atlant refrigerator, reviews of which testify to its remarkable technical characteristics, is on the same line with foreign counterparts in the elite class, and even surpasses them in some respects. Of particular note is the level of noise emitted. It ranges from thirty-seven to forty-one decibels. This sound is similar to the whisper of interlocutors who are one meter away from the listener.

The Atlant refrigerator, reviews of which confirm the high energy efficiency of equipment belonging to classes A and A +, is in full accordance with international standards. And this, despite the presence of two compressors! This technological solution allows you to save electricity by turning off the second camera (if necessary).

The Atlant refrigerator and its impressive useful internal volume have earned positive reviews. According to this characteristic, analogues of household appliances designed for long-term storage of products do not exist.

The remarkable design features that the Atlant refrigerator has, reviews of which speak of its undoubted convenience for users, allow it to be installed in any place you like in the kitchen. These actions can be carried out by re-hanging the door, and during the warranty period - for free.
