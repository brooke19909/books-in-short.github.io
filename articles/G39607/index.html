  • Drive selection. First you need to decide on the appearance of the watch. To do this, select the appropriate drive with an interesting image. It can be a musical record of some cool rock band or a film, for example, with a picture of the Terminator, Indiana Jones or the Mummy. In any case, the image should be impressive, as this is the face of future watches.
  • You must use a marker to write numbers from one to 12 on the front of the CD. Or you can experiment and stick the buttons from the old keyboard to the front of the CD using glue.
  • Next, you need to carefully remove the cover of the old watch, exposing two small screws. It is important not to damage the internal mechanism.
  • We glue the battery and the back of the CD.
  • Carefully insert the batteries and set the correct time.
