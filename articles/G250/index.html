Hotel Ola Club Maioris 4 * (Mallorca, Spain): description, services, reviews

Mallorca is the largest island in Spain. This resort is known for beach vacations. There is a clear sea that is well suited for diving, as well as mountains, vast forests and spectacular bays. Accommodation here is not cheap, but its price pays off with a high level of tourist infrastructure. A good place for a vacation is considered to be the Ola Club Maioris 4 * hotel. What can he offer his future guests?

Briefly about the main thing: location, basic information

The complex consists of 3 snow-white five-story buildings, which are located in a well-kept garden. In total, the hotel has 240 comfortable rooms. They are intended for small families with children, but there are apartments for friendly companies, as well as rooms for single use. Ola Club Maioris Hotel 4 * was built in 1971. Since then, it has been completely updated several times. The last update was completed in 2001. And in 2009, a complete redecoration of all rooms was carried out.





The hotel is located in a quiet location, which is remote from the center and the city beach. It is suitable for families. Cots are available for children up to two years old. The beach is about 800 meters away. Tourists can reach it on foot via a special staircase or take the free bus. Nightclubs and bars are 4 km away. The nearest airport is 16 km away. As in other European countries, settlement here begins strictly at 14:00. When registering, tourists will necessarily require an identity document, as well as a bank card (Visa, MasterCard).

Housing stock and room amenities

For its guests, the hotel has prepared comfortable and spacious apartments equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. Almost all rooms have a miniature balcony with a dining table and chairs. The windows of the Ola Club Maioris 4 * complex (Mallorca) offer beautiful views of the garden. Thorough cleaning of all rooms is also included in the price. Towels and bed linen are regularly changed. If contaminated, they can be laid on the floor so employees understand that they need to be replaced.

The hotel offers the following types of rooms:

  • Standard. It accommodates a maximum of three guests. There are two single beds and one bunk bed.
  • Doubles rooms. Superior room suitable for two adults. You can choose from either two single beds or one double. An extra bed is a folding sofa.
  • Family rooms. Apartments designed for a family of two adults and two children.
  • Superior Superior apartment for one person.




In addition, each room is equipped with a bathroom, which also has household amenities (toilet paper, shampoo, shower gel and soap). The rooms feature a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Several Russian-language channels are broadcast. Also in the room must have a telephone and a safe. Air conditioning only works in high season. The hotel staff includes it several times a day. Doubles rooms and Superior include a hairdryer for free.

Services offered to guests

Resorts in Spain are inferior in the variety of services provided to Turkey or Egypt. The hotel has a minimal infrastructure, but there is everything that tourists may need during their holidays. So, for guests of the complex free laundry and dry cleaning services are offered. You can park your car in the free public car park nearby. The 24-hour front desk can arrange car rental for a fee. This service is suitable for those who like to travel around the country on their own.





At the reception there is also a left-luggage office. You can leave dimensional bags and suitcases in it so that they do not take up much space in the room. Here you can always exchange money at the most favorable rate, as well as use a fax or photocopy machine. All buildings of the complex are equipped with modern roomy lifts. Internet at the hotel can only be connected at an additional cost. Its approximate cost is 6 euros per day. If you pay for several days at once, then a good discount is provided.

Local cuisine and catering facilities

Ola Club Maioris 4 * hotel offers its guests two types of food. Tourists who prefer to spend time in the city or on the beach can arrange breakfast only. The price includes one morning meal with drinks. You can also pay for the all-inclusive concept. It includes breakfast, lunch, dinner, and drinks and light snacks in the hotel bars. Local cuisine includes stewed vegetables, pork and seafood. Spicy food lovers will love the smoked sausage sobrasadu. You should also try stewed lamb with offal and signature stew from eggplant, zucchini and cabbage. You can try local cuisine in the hotel restaurant or in the establishments located in the resort.





Meals at the bar and restaurant are all-inclusive. All meals are served at the shared buffet. The bar offers light snacks, ice cream, sandwiches and pastries, cold and local spirits, as well as tea, coffee and hot milk. The restaurant regularly hosts theme nights of Spanish and European cuisine. The chef holds workshops on cooking seafood.

What can a hotel offer tourists with children?

This tourist complex is well suited for families with children. It is located in a calm area, in which there are no large night clubs. Tourists at the hotel prefer a quiet vacation, so you can not be afraid of noisy neighbors. Children under two years stay free. On request, children's beds are available in the room at a cost of EUR 6 per night. If parents need to leave, they can call a paid nanny.





There is a lot of entertainment for children. In warm weather, you can swim in the shallow freshwater pool. For children from 4 to 12 years old, a mini club is open daily. Animators entertain children, organize sporting events, contests and educational quizzes. You can sit with your child on a rainy day in the playroom. Here are children's books (in English and Spanish), toys, sets for drawing. For outdoor activities, the hotel offers 2 playgrounds, which are equipped with modern and safe swings and slides.

Types of entertainment

For adults, the Ola Club Maioris 4 * hotel also has a lot of entertainment. Some of them are provided to guests free of charge, and the rest - for an additional amount. The complex offers the following leisure options:

  • Freshwater swimming pool located on a spacious terrace with sun loungers and sun umbrellas.
  • Shared living room where tourists can relax or wait for their check-in. There are televisions, soft sofas, video games.
  • Playgrounds and equipment for playing basketball, billiards, volleyball, golf and tennis. There is also a mini golf course.
  • Rental of walking and mountain bikes.
  • Fitness center equipped with a modern gym. There are treadmills, as well as equipment for aerobics and strength training.
  • Sauna and Jacuzzi.
  • Show programs for adults and children. Themed parties, sporting events, and evenings dedicated to Spanish culture are held.
  • Tour agency.




What are the advantages of the hotel tourists note?

After a vacation, modern tourists are used to leaving reviews telling about the advantages and disadvantages of the hotel. Their opinions help other travelers decide where to stay. This hotel receives both good and bad reviews. They describe the following advantages of the complex:

  • Very beautiful hotel grounds. The garden is well maintained, it is nice to walk around it in the afternoon or in the evening.
  • A varied diet. Every day, tourists are given a huge amount of fruit (pineapples, melons, apples, watermelons, peaches) to choose from.
  • Good entertainment. Very funny evening shows. For adults, local wine tasting nights are held.
  • Clear sea with clear water.
  • Almost all the guests are English and German families with small children, so in the evenings it is quite quiet. It‚Äôs rare to meet a very drunk person.

What are the shortcomings of the hotel noted by tourists?

Hotel Ola Club Maioris 4 * reviews get not only good. Some of them talk about significant shortcomings of rest in this place. Tourists highlight the following disadvantages of the hotel:

  • The beach is very far away. If you walk, then on the way back you need to climb the high stairs. It is very difficult for children and elderly people to go this way every day.
  • The bottom of the sea is uncomfortable, there are a lot of stones and large boulders, so it‚Äôs worth swimming only in shales. In the presence of large waves, children can injure themselves on stones.
  • The nearby infrastructure is poorly developed. There are several shops and cafes, but all the entertainment is removed from the complex.
  • Small rooms that need repair. In some of them, plumbing in the bathroom does not work well.

conclusions

Hotel Ola Club Maioris 4 * will be a good option for relaxing in Mallorca. It offers accommodation at low prices, while providing a high level of service. Its only significant drawback is its location. The complex is located at a considerable distance from the beach and the resort center. Nevertheless, it can be recommended to those tourists who are looking for a relaxing holiday or who wish to save on it.
