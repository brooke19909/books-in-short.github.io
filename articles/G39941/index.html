How to calculate a mortgage: tips for future owners

Real estate in Russia can not be called cheap. Few of our compatriots can afford to buy a new apartment only at their own expense. The absolute majority is forced to use borrowed money for this purpose. It is unlikely that acquaintances or friends will be able to lend a couple of millions of years so for 15-20. So the only option is to contact a commercial bank.

Why you need to calculate a mortgage payment

Before answering the question of how to calculate a mortgage, I want to understand why this is done at all. After all, you can immediately go to the bank - a sweet girl consultant will calculate everything and explain everything in great detail. In principle, this is so. But before contacting a particular bank, you need to decide which one. In any city in Russia, several financial institutions operate at once, and each of them offers its customers the opportunity to obtain a mortgage loan. If you consistently apply to each of them, it will take a lot of time.

You can simplify the selection procedure using modern Internet technologies. Almost every bank has an online service that allows you to calculate the main parameters of the loan without leaving your home. This service is called a loan calculator. To obtain the necessary information, it is enough to enter the desired amount and term of the loan in a special form and click the "Calculate" button. After that, the borrower will receive full information about the size of the regular payment and the total amount of the overpayment on the loan.





Credit payment - the main criterion for making a decision

Now it became clear how to calculate the mortgage. Next question: "What to do with this information?" Everything is simple - choose a bank. But how to do it right? There are two main criteria: the total overpayment on the loan and the size of the regular payment.

At first glance it seems that the overpayment should be the main criterion: where it is less, there you need to take a loan. In principle, this is logical. But first of all, you still need to focus on the size of the monthly payment. There are two reasons for this:

  1. Regardless of how much the overpayment on the loan, you need to pay it every month. If the borrower chooses a loan with a lower overpayment, but too much regular payment, in the future he may have problems with paying off the mortgage. At the same time, any loan can be paid ahead of schedule, of course, if income allows it, thereby reducing the amount of overpayment.
  2. When making a decision on granting a loan, the bank will necessarily take into account how much of the proceeds the borrower will use to repay it. The smaller it is, the more likely it is to get approval for a loan application.

Mortgage repayment methods





In order to correctly evaluate the results of an automated calculation, it is advisable, at least in general terms, to understand how to calculate a mortgage payment. There are two ways to do this.

  1. Differentiated. In this case, the payment includes the same amount of the principal debt payable. The amount of interest that must be paid on a monthly basis is calculated based on the remaining loan amount. As the mortgage matures, it gradually decreases. The closer the loan expiration date, the smaller the differential payment.
  2. Annuity. With this method of repayment, the payment remains unchanged for the entire loan term. The amount of the monthly contribution primarily includes the amount of accrued interest, everything else goes to repay the main debt.

Knowing how to calculate the mortgage and by what criteria it is better to make a decision, you can safely proceed to the procedure for choosing a future lender. The undoubted leaders of the Russian market are two banks with state participation: Sberbank and VTB-24.
