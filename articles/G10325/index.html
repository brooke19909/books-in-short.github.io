Paper tanks. How to do it yourself?

For many boys, playing war is a great pastime. But to do without the necessary accessories will not work. Rather, it will, but the entertainment will be less fun. What battle is without tanks? And much more interesting if there are a lot of them. Then you can arrange a real battle. Of course, it’s easier to use purchased toys, which are not very cheap, but you can make them yourself, and then paint them in any color. So how to make paper tanks? The answer is below.





Paper tanks can be of any size, and it can be changed depending on the situation. How to make paper tanks will be described in detail in this article.

What you need for work

Before you start creating real masterpieces for the game, you need to have on hand:

  1. Album sheet.
  2. A square (9x9 cm) cut out of paper.
  3. Duct tape.

How to make a tank out of paper easily

The album sheet is folded in half lengthwise, then placed in a vertical position. In the next step, you need to bend the upper right corner to the long side, and then deploy. The remaining corners go through the same procedure.

So, from below and above, a “marking” appears in the form of a cross. The left and right sides of the cross are connected, and then they need to be pressed with the palm of your hand so that the edges form a figure in the form of a triangle. A similar operation is performed for the opposite edge.

The result is small triangles, they are located at the bottom and top of the crafts. It is necessary to connect the sides of the triangle located on the left and right sides, and then press down with the palm of your hand to obtain the already described geometric figure. The opposite edge undergoes similar actions.

Several small triangles appeared above and below, they turn to the left. The resulting element on the right is bent to the central part of the craft, and then half of it bends back. Thus, the tank appears caterpillar.

The opposite side is processed in the same way, at the last stage you need to return the triangles to their original position.

On top of the large triangles, you need to make two similar figures of a smaller size, so that the result is a part in the form of a rhombus.





The base of the tank is turned over, then you need to bend the upper edge back so that the bending line runs along the lower apex of the rhombus.

Smaller triangles fold in pairs, and then, holding them, you need to bend the bottom of the craft back to the corner in the middle of the small triangles previously located above. All bends must be ironed carefully.

The triangular parts located below are tucked under similar parts from above. This will fix the craft and form a tank tower.

The folds of paper located on the sides of the craft are straightened out, they are assigned the role of caterpillars.

At the last stage of work, it is necessary to pre-prepared a square of paper to form a tube and stick it on one side with duct tape. So it turns out the barrel for a toy. It remains to insert it into the lower part of the combat vehicle. That's all, the tank can be sent to battle! These instructions will help in the question of how to make a tank of paper (origami).





What models can still be made

How to make a T-34 tank from paper? This is another great model of the tank, which is known to all of the military chronicles. The easiest way is to use existing parts for a paper toy. Elements for the tank need to be transferred to thick paper and carefully cut out each painted part.

Facilitation briefing

The instructions for the craft are as follows:

  1. All cut elements have fold lines. Alternately, you need to apply a ruler to each of them, raise the free edges and iron it carefully. The ruler is necessary to get a smooth bend.
  2. At this stage, the model is bonded. First, the base of the toy is glued, which will be a body. It is recommended to use acrylic glue or PVA, which dries quickly.
  3. All minor parts must be glued.

At the gun, the base is first fastened, and only then equipped with additional elements. The finished part is glued to the main body of the T-34 tank.

The manufacture of tracks is as follows. The first step is to work on the inner circles, and then a single track band is attached. Prepared parts must be secured on both sides of the housing.





Little tricks

To create various models of tanks it is absolutely not necessary to use white sheets. You can put colorful paper on crafts. This approach will allow you to get more vibrant and interesting toys. In addition, you can create several divisions belonging to different troops.

Also, do not dwell on one model. You can come up with a huge number of different options for tanks. This is both the simplest crafts and fighting vehicles from science fiction films, which will be able to delight all friends, and at the same time catch the horror of a possible enemy.
