Ali Baba Palace 4 (Hurghada / Egypt): photos and reviews of tourists

Thousands of tourists come to Hurghada in winter and summer. For recreation, it has an ideal climate, a fantastically beautiful sea, hundreds of entertainment options and dozens of first-class hotels. Ali Baba Palace 4 * (Hurghada) - one of the best. He is one of the three hotels owned by a single owner. Guests can freely visit the territory of any of them, use the beach, pool, participate in entertainment programs and compare which of the three hotels is the best. Most tourists call Ali Baba Palace.

Infrastructure

Hotel Ali Baba Palace 4 * (Hurghada) welcomes its guests in a chic and very spacious lounge. The reception is open 24 hours a day. Number of rooms

Hotel Ali Baba Palace 4 * (Hurghada) began its activities in 2000. In 2007 and in 2009, reconstruction and redecoration were carried out here, so the rooms are all quite functional. For accommodation in the hotel there are three buildings: two two-story and one three-story. They are equipped with 654 rooms. Categories:

- Standard. Area - up to 35 squares. These rooms are double, but it is possible to install a cot, a folding bed or a folding chair.

- Standard triple. Room size is up to 35 squares. The difference from the double standard is the availability of three single beds.

- Family adjacent. Area - up to 35 squares. The room is designed for 4 people.

Superior rooms

- Family two-room. Area - up to 65 squares.

- Junior Suite. Area - up to 45 squares. Room is designed for 4 people.

- Standard for the disabled. Area - up to 35 squares. These rooms are double, located on the ground floor.

Sea

Hotel Ali Baba Palace 4 * (Hurghada) has its own beach, located immediately outside the territory. It is always very clean, sunbeds and umbrellas are available. The beach itself is sandy, but when you enter the water stones come under your feet, so it‚Äôs advisable to have special shoes on your feet. Near the coast there are coral islands. Swimming underwater in this area is an incomparable pleasure. In addition to their own beach, guests of the Ali Baba Hotel can relax on the beaches of the Jasmine and Aladdin Hotels. Hotel for children

Ali Baba Palace 4 * (Hurghada) is very fond of children and absolutely all employees are wonderful to them. Shopping

It is impossible to return home from Egypt without souvenirs. Vacationers in Ali Baba Palace 4 * (Hurghada) have many opportunities to purchase quality goods.
