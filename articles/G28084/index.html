Charm of the Seas - the largest liner in the world

"Charm of the seas" - this is the name of the largest ship in the world today. Before him, the "Oasis of the Seas" was considered such. It is curious that the difference between them is only ... 5 centimeters! In fact, these are twin ships, but the palm nevertheless passed precisely to the “Enchantment of the Seas”. We’ll talk about this.

In fact, the designers who created the largest liner in the world today claim that the difference between him and his predecessor of 5 centimeters turned out in a completely random way - due to the physical effect of the cold on the metal. But be that as it may, the Captain's Armband is now at the Charm of the Seas ship.



Nowhere else!

This ship (like its predecessor) is the property of the Royal Caribbean Fleet. The largest ship in the world has:

  • 16 decks;
  • 2,700 cabins;
  • 24 modern elevators.

It is interesting that in each of the rooms there is one iPad, which allows the ship to "keep up with the times." In addition , the liner can reach speeds of up to 22 (!) Knots per hour, weighs 225,282 tons, it is served by 2,380 crew members from 85 countries.

Floating city

The largest ship in the world is not just a ship, it is a whole floating resort city! Its infrastructure simply staggers the imagination of a modern person: dozens of restaurants, boutiques, bars, concert halls, swimming pools (two of which are equipped specifically for surfing), a jacuzzi, fountains and, of course, a water park.





In addition, the largest cruise ship is equipped with:

  • tennis courts;
  • basketball and volleyball courts;
  • jazz club;
  • climbing walls;
  • children's playgrounds;
  • French carousel
  • a golf course;
  • casino;
  • real tropical gardens;
  • football field.

Yes ... The designers took care of their passengers with interest! In most cases, they rarely have time to try half of the relaxation options offered on board!

Cleanliness is the key to health!

The creators of The Charm of the Seas have not forgotten about the environment. The ship is equipped with the latest wastewater treatment equipment, which the other largest cruise ships in the world do not have! In addition, the ship has special machines designed for crushing glass, aluminum and tin. Here's how the garbage can be destroyed at Charm of the Seas:

  1. Tons of garbage are compressed to the size of tennis balls in special machines.
  2. Trash balls are frozen in the world's largest freezer, staying there until the ship arrives ashore.




The Titanic ... Continued?

Many superstitious people are suspicious of this ship. The fact is that in their minds, all the largest liners in the world are associated with the legendary Titanic. Such people believe that the smaller the ship - the more reliable it is. It is interesting that not so long ago with the "Enchantment of the Seas" a really not very pleasant incident occurred. The engine room on the ship caught fire. But the tragedy did not happen thanks to the modern fire-fighting equipment of the liner, which brilliantly coped with this incident. Before evacuation, the matter did not reach.

Price for pleasure
