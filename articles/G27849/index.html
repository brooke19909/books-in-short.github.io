Medal "For Military Valor" yesterday and today

The medal "For Military Valor" was established in honor of the centenary of the leader of the world proletariat. She was awarded:

  • leading employees, employees of state departments, collective farmers, scientists and cultural figures who have distinguished themselves in their work on preparing for the festival;
  • persons fighting for the formation of the Soviets, for the defense of the homeland, the builders of socialism;
  • soldiers of the Red Army, the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Soviet Union.

There are three types of rewards:

- The first contains the phrase "For Valiant Labor". More than 11000000 such awards have been issued.

- The second option has the words "For military valor." Of these copies made 2,000,000.

- The third option contains no entries. This type of medal was awarded to foreign persons - members of foreign delegations. As mentioned above, about five thousand of these awards were presented.

The medal was introduced by a resolution of the Central Committee of the CPSU on 11/05/1969. In total, about eleven million people were awarded, including: working citizens, including 9,000,000, military - 2,000,000, foreigners - 5,000.

Nowadays

Today, the medal "For Military Valor" is awarded to Russian servicemen:

- distinguished in military preparations;

- distinguished during service, in exercises;

- selfless and courageous while serving.

- providing public peace and relative security.

Medal 1 and 2 degrees

Awarding the awards is the representative of the Russian defense. The medal is presented in two degrees. First, the Military Valor medal is awarded 2 degrees, then - 1. The medal is considered the departmental insignia of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Russian Federation.



Lump sum payments

In 2010, the Order of the Minister of Defense No. 500 was issued, according to which, along with the medal for contract servicemen, a one-time payment was equal to 75% of the monthly salary. According to the third paragraph of this order, monetary compensation is paid on the basis of the order of the chief, for example, a military unit. This order is issued within three days after the issuance of a higher order from the Ministry of Russia. True, the order was valid until the end of 2014.
