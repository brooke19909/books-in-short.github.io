New from Karcher - wiper



New from Karcher - wiper. Is it really able to facilitate the monotonous work of cleaning the room, more precisely, to bring smooth surfaces into a shiny appearance? Maybe this is a myth created by a venerable producer? Before answering these questions, it is necessary to test the novelty. It is precisely the opinion of those who have already tested the Karcher WV 50 plus wiper , we will rely on, drawing conclusions and deciding whether to purchase this device, offered by the famous German concern.

Options "Karcher"

A wiper is a small unit weighing about 700 grams and has a small tank-reservoir for collecting liquid. The device runs on battery power, so the wires will not interfere with cleaning during cleaning, but its operating time is strictly limited by the battery capacity. The kit also includes a spray gun with a nozzle made of soft cotton fabric. This is the main kit, but the manufacturer recommends purchasing a consumable - a concentrated glass cleaner.

Operating principle

Karcher is a wiper that works as follows: the surface is treated with a special tool and the dirt is wiped off with the included rag.





It is quite convenient to do this, since the spray bottle with washing liquid is equipped with a nozzle with a cloth. All that needs to be done after this procedure is to hold the main unit from top to bottom, collecting the draining dirt and detergent.

Criticism

It seems everything is extremely simple, but ... According to consumers, not everything is so rosy. Many complain that dirt is not collected and stains remain, someone is dissatisfied with the size of the battery, which requires frequent charging. Some argue that washing the windows with the old "grandmother" method is easier and faster. After such reviews, someone may decide that he can‚Äôt cope with washing Karcher glass properly. The wiper, however, has nothing to do with these negative characteristics.





Rebuttal

These statements have another side. Firstly, do not forget that this device is considered compact, designed for cleaning small surfaces. For huge areas in the line of the company Karcher there are real "masterpieces" that can work wonders. So, if you decide to purchase this unit, clearly calculate its capabilities relative to the areas that you have to clean.

By the way, stains and smudges will not remain if the final cleaning takes place not on damp glass, but on well-moistened, special means.

Moreover, some consumers do not take into account the force and pressure gradient on the unit, pinching the suction point. A little practice - and everything will work out.

Of course, a professional tool Karcher is not cheap, but it is much more economical than usual, since it must be diluted with water.

Give beautiful

The Karcher wiper, the price of which fluctuates around 3000-3500 rubles, will be a wonderful gift, as it is not only an original device for the home, but also a good help in the hard struggle for cleanliness.
