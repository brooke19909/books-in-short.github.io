Hotel room categories: features, requirements

When choosing a hotel in a foreign country, travelers, of course, first of all pay attention to its stardom. However, it is equally important when buying a tour to take into account the category of hotel rooms. Rooms for rent in hotels may vary in size, degree of equipment, ease of location, etc.

Room types by size and degree of comfort

When choosing rooms in a hotel, first of all, of course, it is worth paying attention to their size and equipment. In this regard, there are only 6 main types of rooms:

  • standard (STD, Standard);

  • studio (Studio);

  • Superior (Superior);

  • family ordinary and two-room (Family room and studio);

  • Suite and Deluxe (Suite / Deluxe);

  • apartments (Apartment).

Also in hotels duplexes can be rented (Duplex). This category of hotel rooms is rare and quite expensive. Duplex is a two-story, very large room.

In hotels of large cities, where people often come on business trips, Business class rooms can also be rented. In such rooms, in addition to the standard set of furniture and household appliances, office equipment and Internet access are usually provided.

What are the standards?

It is this type of room that forms the basis of the room stock of two-, three-, and sometimes four-star hotels. Cost of living in Standard is usually relatively inexpensive. But at the same time, such rooms are equipped with only the most necessary - beds, wardrobes, bedside tables, tables, air conditioners. Showers, with the exception of the cheapest hotels, are usually provided in the standards individually for each room. However, the size of the bathrooms in such rooms is in most cases very small. Typically, standard rooms have balconies. But in some cases, they may not be in the rooms of this category.

Sometimes in hotels, among other things, improved standards are leased. In terms of equipment, they are no different from ordinary rooms of this type. Such standards are usually called improved because they have a slightly larger area than simple Standard. Also in these rooms can be installed new furniture and appliances or recently made repairs.

Requirements for standards in the Russian Federation

This category of hotel rooms in Russia can be further classified according to the degree of convenience. There are five varieties of such rooms.

In the standards of category I in our country should be equipped with full bathrooms with baths / showers, toilets and sinks.

In standards of category II in Russia, according to the regulations, tourists should be provided with an incomplete bathroom with a washbasin and a toilet. Otherwise, these rooms are equipped in much the same way as rooms of category I.

Class I and II standards, according to the rules, should be designed for accommodation of no more than 1-2 people. In the rooms of the 3rd category in hotels, the settlement of a larger number of guests is allowed. At the same time, the bathroom in them is usually equipped with an incomplete one. According to the standards, per person in rooms of this variety must have at least 6 m2 of area.

A large number of guests can also be accommodated in category IV standards at once. At the same time, a bathroom in such rooms is usually not provided at all. For the convenience of residents, in this case only a sink is required.

Category V rooms are mainly equipped in hostels. These rooms often have bunk beds. In hotels in such rooms for one person there should be at least 6 m2 of area, in hostels - 4.5 m2 .

Studio is a category of rooms in hotels, which are quite spacious rooms with kitchens. In such rooms, guests, among other things, can use, for example, a stove, microwave, toasters, a set of dishes. According to the regulations, in Russian hotels the studio area should not be less than 25 m 2 . These rooms are usually rented by tourists who want to save money in a foreign city or resort on food.

Family rooms

Rooms of this class usually have a large area. Most often tourists with children rent such rooms. Family room in most cases consists of one room, and studio - from a bedroom and a living room.

Sometimes in a family room in hotels combine two adjacent ordinary. For ordinary tourists, such housing is rented as separate rooms. If the family settles in the hotel, the administration opens the door in the wall between the connecting rooms.

Accommodation categories in the hotel rooms: deluxe and deluxe

The rooms of the class Suite and Deluxe are primarily distinguished by an increased level of comfort. In most cases, a suite is a two-room, well-equipped room. Such housing, in turn, can also be divided into several categories:

  • Normal Suite.

  • Honeymoon suites for honeymooners.

  • Presidential suite - presidential suite.

The last type of suites is equipped only in the most expensive 5 * hotels. Rooms of this type consist of many rooms for various purposes and are equipped with the highest quality furniture and appliances.

Deluxe is a category of accommodation in hotels that differs from Suite only in that it additionally has some ‚Äúfrills‚Äù - noise isolation, a large bed, etc.

What are apartments

This type of room has the largest area. Apartments are whole apartments with many rooms. Living conditions here are similar to home. In addition to a separate kitchen, bedrooms and a bathroom, these rooms usually have a living room and a hall.

Classification according to the number of guests

In this regard, rooms handed over at the hotel can be divided into:

  • Single So-called rooms equipped for only one guest. This class of accommodation is not available in all hotels.

  • Double These rooms are designed for two guests. Double twin are equipped with 2 single beds. Simple Double - One.

  • Triple. These rooms are populated by three guests.

The majority of the hotel rooms in the world are based on Double rooms.

Designation of numbers depending on the type of window

Depending on the type of opening, rented accommodation in hotels can be classified into many types. Most often, according to the standard, the categories of rooms in hotels are as follows:

  • Pool View - with windows overlooking the pool;

  • Sea View - at sea;

  • City View - on the city;

  • Mountain View - on the mountains;

  • Ocean View - on the ocean.

Indicated in the catalogs of tour operators of the room, depending on the opening types, respectively PV, SV, CV, MV, OV. Run of house rooms can also be rented at hotels. The view from the windows of such rooms can be opened absolutely by anyone.

Hotel Categories

Thus, which rooms can be rented in hotels, we found out. What can be the categories of the hotels themselves? The comfort level of ordinary hotels is determined, of course, primarily by stardom.

Hotels "without stars" or 0 * provide their guests with a minimum of services. But at the same time, residents of such hotels can count on access to hot and cold water, ventilation and heating. The highest category rooms in a starless hotel are usually standards III or II class maximum.

3 * hotels offer tourists a slightly higher level of service. The requirements for rooms of various categories in hotels of this class are higher. In such complexes usually have a restaurant, swimming pool, parking. The rooms for guests can, among other things, include hairdryers, safes, air conditioning, televisions, minibars or refrigerators. This category of hotels is most popular among tourists. For relatively little money, in complexes of this type you can relax more or less comfortably.

Most often, hotels of this type rent standards, suites, deluxe rooms, family rooms and apartments.
