Promsvyazbank was opened back in 1995. To date, the regional network of this financial institution has more than two hundred and seventy offices and over ten thousand ATMs across the country. In addition, more than two hundred terminals also serve customers.

Employee Reviews

The first thing that catches your eye when you read the reviews of employees is messages about scant wages. For example, one employee from St. Petersburg was named the amount of 30,000 rubles. People write that at interviews with Promsvyazbank they are checked through a lie detector.

There are many reviews in which people report mass reductions for one reason or another. Moreover, the reduction, according to people, often occurs in violation of the law, as well as without payment of the necessary compensation.





Other complaints

Some employees complain that they have to work twelve hours a day without any breaks. Therefore, people do not even manage to have lunch.

In a series of comments, employees say that they have to sit on calls to customers too often. Also, customer dissatisfaction and their incorrect behavior towards bank employees are sometimes reported, which requires great stress resistance.

For the rest, it is said that in the team everyone is responsible only for himself, there is no any help or support from colleagues, there are also many complaints about the instability and futility of the work.

Thus, in many comments, Promsvyazbank employees advise others not to get a job in this organization. It is also emphasized that almost half of those who already work in it are looking for a new place and really want to quit faster.

Positive comments

In contrast to the negative reviews, some write that their work at Promsvyazbank is quite satisfactory. People call their work in this institution stable and interesting. Positive comments also note that reports about low wages are untrue, and those who really want to earn money can always do this at Promsvyazbank. Also, a plus is that wages are paid to people white and a full social package is provided.
