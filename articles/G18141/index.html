Twin Palms Resort 3 * (Pattaya, Thailand): hotel description, location, reviews

Pattaya is considered one of the best world resorts, which can receive up to a million tourists a year. The city is located on the shores of the Gulf of Thailand, a 2-hour drive from Suvarnabhumi Airport and 150 kilometers from Bangkok. People come here to rest from all over the world, especially rich Thais like this place.

Features of rest in Pattaya



Today, Pattaya is a fairly developed tourist destination. About 120 hotels have been built in the city: from the most luxurious and famous to cozy budget houses. Here you can find something for everyone: comfortable beaches or quiet coral reefs, water activities, scooters, surfing, water skiing and even fishing lovers will be interested here. And what a variety of cafes and restaurants can surprise Pattaya. A kitchen for every taste and color, you are sure to try something new and exotic here, perhaps even more and will not find it anywhere.

Pattaya is also very attractive because it is safe and there are practically no strong hurricanes and storms, the sea is always quiet. Coral reefs are considered one of the bright places, and here it attracts diving enthusiasts to swim in masks and enjoy the beauties of marine life.

Here are the most incendiary bars, restaurants, discos. Unique entertainment shows, outdoor activities: bowling alley, tennis courts, archery grounds, shooting ranges. You can do all this at the resort. Why visit shopping malls, local souvenir shops? There you can find a lot of interesting things for yourself and your loved ones. For fans of extreme shows, there is a unique place where famous Thai boxing is held. A huge selection of excursions and travels - all in order to get a lot of positive emotions.

Pattaya also offers you to visit the corners with animals. The excursion can be to the tiger zoo, crocodile or snake farm. You can go to a safari park, ride on elephants. For unforgettable emotions, you should watch the famous transvestite show, and be sure to go into the Thai massage room.

Lovers of luxury items here can visit jewelry stores and purchase something original for themselves or their soul mates. Shops with Thai silk will not leave anyone indifferent. In Pattaya, everyone will find a piece of himself and what he likes.

Weather in September




In September, it is better to refuse a trip to Pattaya. During this period, rains are raging here. Other places on the Black or Mediterranean coast will be suitable for relaxing on the beach, since during this period there is a "velvet" season. But lovers of outdoor activities are just waiting here. The sea is very favorable for surfing. It is possible to catch the wave and enjoy the water extreme in September.

This does not mean, of course, that Pattaya is closed for recreation in September. Bad weather lasts about 18 days. Half of them are quite clear and warm. Since the resort is close to the equator, almost all year the ratio of day to night is the same, that is, the average day lasts 12 hours.

What time of year you would not come, it is almost always very hot here, so when choosing a hotel, you need to pay attention to the availability of air conditioning. In September, the air temperature averages +31 in the afternoon, +26 at night. Of course, there are days when a thermometer in the shade can show +35.

For lovers of walks in the rain, it may seem that this weather is not entirely scary. But keep in mind that in early autumn the average rainfall in the aisles is 180 mm, which can drop a little more than 10 days. Sometimes rainy weather can last more than 20 days. At such a time, it is better, of course, not to leave the hotel. If you decide to go for a walk, stock up on a raincoat and rubber boots.

Even if you expect that the Gulf of Thailand is always warm (average water temperature +29 degrees Celsius), then you are unlikely to be able to enjoy his arms, as the water is very dirty and muddy. Visiting the beach in rainy weather is better not worth it.

In principle, Pattaya in September is suitable for recreation, but before the trip it is better to familiarize yourself with the weather conditions and stock up on necessary things.

It is also advisable to pre-determine the place of residence. In Pattaya, a huge number of decent hotels, one of which is the Twin Palms Resort 3 *.
