Overview of the resort village of Kvariati, Georgia - traveler reviews, features and interesting facts

Kvariati in Georgia, reviews of which you will find in this article, is a popular holiday village in the east of this country. It is located near the Turkish border. Officially refers to the Khelvachaur municipality of Adjara. Right on the Black Sea coast. A river of the same name flows through the village, and around it lies the picturesque Kakhaberskaya lowland.

Overview of the resort village of Kvariati in Georgia

So, more details. In recent years, a large number of tourists come to Kvariati in Georgia. The reviews of those who have already spent their holidays here encourage them to experience for themselves what it feels like to relax in these places.





The village itself is very small. Less than 500 people live in it, but the number of tourists who annually come to spend their holidays here is much greater. Basically, Georgians live here. Local residents earn money from agriculture (citrus fruits grow well here) and in the tourism industry.

To date, Kvariati in Georgia, according to reviews, remains one of the most popular resorts of the Black Sea coast in this part of Adjara. Here are deep-sea beaches that attract diving and snorkeling enthusiasts. Tourists from Ukraine and eastern Georgia come here mainly on vacation.

Holidays in Kvariati

The review of the resort village of Kvariati in Georgia should be continued with the fact that it is rightfully considered one of the most comfortable resorts in Adjara.

Tours to this place are offered by most operators specializing in various countries in this part of the world. Therefore, ordering a tour to this paradise is not difficult. This is an unforgettable place surrounded by mountains. Holidays in Adjara will be remembered for your whole life.





Adjara Hotels

You can stay here for the night in a comfortable hotel, and with local residents, many of whom rent rooms and guest houses to tourists. The last option here, by the way, is the most common.





If you are a fan of vacation at the hotel, then you can easily find even five-star hotels in this village. True, there are very few of them. Three star hotels are much larger. They offer budget accommodation options that require a minimum range of services.

For those who can not relax without decent service, there are four-star hotels. Mostly in the southern part of Kvariati in Georgia. Reviews of these hotels are quite good. There are, as mentioned above, several elite five-star hotels in this village. There are few of them, so it is better to book places in advance.

The number of rooms is very diverse. The advantage is that it is designed for a different number of guests. The hotels are located in convenient places, easily accessible by public or private transport. At the same time, the hotels themselves are located in picturesque corners, where you can easily reach the sea.

Currently, the guest infrastructure is developing intensively in the village, so the vacation price in Kvariati can depend on a number of factors. It is worth considering the number of guests, the conditions in which you are going to live, the quality of the services provided.

What to do in Kvariati?

The answer is simple. First of all, lovers of sea relaxation go to Kvariati in Georgia. The beach here is really comfortable and very long. On it you can get to neighboring resorts. For example, to Gonio or Sarpi. And on the way to find a truly secluded place on the sea, where there will be no one but you.





In Kvariati there is a pebble beach with a length of about one kilometer. It is considered one of the best on the coast. There is always calm and quiet, because the busy highway runs in the distance, behind the village. But very close by are the majestic mountains, completely covered by eucalyptus trees. This is truly an amazing sight.

All large rivers that flow into the sea are located in the Batumi region. Therefore, the beach in Kvariati is very clean. In the holiday season, sun umbrellas, sun loungers and all kinds of entertainment are offered for rent, which our tourists often do not have enough at foreign resorts.

The most popular tourist destinations in the summer months are vacations in Kvariati (Georgia). Reviews of vacations during this period convince you that you can really spend time in a cozy and relaxed atmosphere.

There are also things to do for fans of extreme sports. For example, diving is developed in Kvariati. Loving couples who dream to spend a honeymoon on the emerald sea, and couples with young children will find something to their liking. In Kvariati there is enough space for everyone. For entertainment, you can also go to Batumi, located nearby.

Sights of Kvariati

The next moment. It is worth recognizing that in this resort village there are practically no attractions. Kvariati (Georgia) is not famous for its interesting excursion program. But many amazing places are located nearby.





In Gonio there is a fortress, which was built by the ancient Romans who lived in these places. Until now, archaeological excavations are ongoing here.

True, if you wish, and in Kvariati you can find a place that will attract your attention. It is here that the only diving center in all of Georgia is located. It is located at the southern end of the beach. To get into it, you should go directly along the beach in the direction of Sarpi. As soon as you reach the cliffs, you will find the place you were striving for. Since many Ukrainians have a rest at the resort, the prices here are often quoted directly in hryvnias, but they are ready to accept another currency from you. Here, in a matter of days, you will be trained to dive into the sea with flippers, arrange diving on a sunken ship, help you learn the basics of diving and even get the appropriate PADI certificate.

Impressions of tourists

According to many who spent their holidays in these places, Kvariati is one of the best resorts in Adjara, of course, not counting Batumi. Many, having come here once, then return again and again. Some rent houses near the beach, others in the upper part of the resort village. The northern part is more noisy, there are always more discos. There are more entertainment, bars and cafes in Kvariati (Georgia).

In reviews of these places, tourists say that in terms of price and quality, this is one of the best places on the country's coast. And if you still do not have enough entertainment, of which there really are not many, you can always get to Batumi.
