Rest, Balkhash. Recreation area to choose from. Where to go this summer?

Balkhash is rightfully considered the pearl of the Kazakh region. The lake occupies the eastern part of the republic. This great attraction, the pride of the people of Kazakhstan, is incredibly popular among tourists. The lake is on the thirteenth place in the list of the largest lakes in the world and takes the second place after the Caspian Sea among the never-ending salt lakes.





What kind of vacation does Balkhash offer?

The recreation area (any) in this place will give you the opportunity to get to know better the pearl of Kazakhstan. The pond has a unique feature. Water in the western part of the lake almost always remains fresh, while on the other side of the strait it is salty.

For tourists there is a great opportunity to have a great time. On the territory of the lake there are many recreation areas that hospitably receive visitors.

What kind of rest can Balkhash provide? The recreation area "Blue Lagoon" will fully demonstrate all its charms.

Holiday Village "Blue Lagoon"

On the shore of the lake is a small recreation area. The picturesque view of the bay and the unique properties of the lake attract travelers to this place. The base can accommodate up to 54 guests. The complex consists of 3 cozy cottages, in which 2-, 3- and 4-bed rooms are located, equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay.





On the territory of the base there is a cafe with a summer terrace in the shadow of the alleys. You can also sit on the observation deck near the beach.

In the evening you will be offered to visit a disco, look at a bar or try your hand at various competitions.

Fans of outdoor activities are offered the opportunity to play beach volleyball, badminton, table tennis and basketball. A wonderful holiday offers Balkhash. The recreation area "Blue Lagoon" is glad to guests at any time of the year.

I would like to tell you about another amazing place on the lake. Recreation area "Samal" (Balkhash) is considered to be one of the most visited by tourists.

All about the hotel complex "Samal"

On the very shore of a unique reservoir there is a real family health resort. Vacationers have the opportunity to enjoy swimming not only in the cleanest waters of the lake, but also in the pools located on the territory. You can also visit the Finnish sauna and play billiards.

One of the best conditions for a comfortable stay is the Samal recreation area. Balkhash is a lake loved not only by adults, but also by children. The hotel complex has a huge playground. The cleanliness of the beach and the transparency of the waters of the lake will delight both young and older members of the family.

There is also a cafeteria, a bar, a wood-fired bathhouse and showers. On request, you can organize a banquet or a festive feast.





The price includes accommodation and 3 meals a day. Hotel "Samal" offers vacationers 24 comfortable rooms, each of which has a TV, fridge, shower and a bathroom. Superior rooms also have sofas and air conditioning.

On the picturesque shore of the lake is another comfortable relaxation area - "Pearl". Balkhash all year round pleases vacationers with its clear clear waters.

Features stay at the base

The hotel complex offers a relaxing break from the bustle of the city. Hotel "Pearl" provides visitors with 43 comfortable rooms with all amenities. In 7 deluxe rooms, 6 of which are two-room, and one is one-room, there is everything for a comfortable stay. All rooms have a shower, a bathroom, a TV. The base also has a VIP-house for those who want complete privacy. All rooms except 20 junior suites are equipped with double beds. The remaining rooms have two beds. All rooms are very clean and comfortable.





Additional hotel services include access to a cafe-bar, billiards, playground, sauna. On the territory there is parking, a summer playground. You can also rent a scooter or boat and explore all the beauties of an amazing natural reserve.

For those who prefer an active holiday, Balkhash (the Pearl recreation area is a wonderful place) will provide all the necessary conditions. Here you can rent a boat and fishing equipment and go on a quiet hunt. There are a lot of fish in the lake, so everyone will have the opportunity to catch at least one trophy.

Recreation area "Koktem" (Balkhash)

This complex is suitable for people of low income with modest demands. Although on the territory of the base there is everything necessary for a comfortable pastime. Recreation area "Koktem" (Balkhash) is famous for its surroundings. Firstly, it is located directly on the lake. Secondly, there are hunting grounds nearby, where for a small fee you can hunt wild boar and duck.

For those who prefer hunting fishing, there are also special privileges. At the base you can rent all the equipment and even a boat and go on an exciting journey in search of aquatic inhabitants.

Among the local population there is an opinion that it is able to provide the best rest for Balkhash. The Koktem Recreation Area is a prime example. People of any income can have a good time here. The base is notable for affordable prices, but at the same time good service.

With oriental hospitality and hospitality, they will meet the conquerors of Lake Balkhash recreation areas. Photos can not fully reflect the beauty of these places. Therefore, at least once it is worth seeing it with your own eyes.





Before going to this beautiful place, you should familiarize yourself with some of the features of relaxation on Lake Balkhash.

Main advantages

  1. Amazing mild climate. In these places, almost never rains. The heat of 35 degrees is very well tolerated thanks to the light breeze that blows constantly.
  2. Great fishing. Zander, roach, and common carp, and even catfish, live in the waters of the lake.
  3. Low prices for fruits in the summer.
  4. Residents of the Russian Federation can stay in the territory without registration for 30 days. As a rule, this time is enough for a good rest.
  5. Housing prices are relatively inexpensive compared to holidays in the Black and Azov Seas.

disadvantages

  1. The place is famous for its sandstorms. It is impossible to predict them. But I am glad that they last no more than 2 hours, after which there is complete silence.
  2. At night, the temperature drops to 15 degrees.
  3. Water temperature does not rise above 26 degrees, although there are exceptions, but very rarely.
  4. Mosquitoes can attack in the evening, but this issue is decided by the purchase of protective equipment.
  5. If you drive your own car, you can spend a lot of time going through customs.
