How to transfer money from "Life" to "Life": detailed instructions

Life subscribers can use the service of transferring funds from their balance to another. It is very useful, because when you need to urgently call someone, and there are not enough funds on the balance, you can ask your friend to throw off part of his money to you. In this article we will talk about how to transfer money from Life to Life. All known methods will be considered. In addition, since the Life operator serves two countries at once - Ukraine and Belarus, we will consider ways for each of the countries.

How to transfer money in Ukraine through a USSD request (first method)





In Ukraine, Life subscribers have three ways to transfer money from Life to Life, unlike Belarus, where there is only one. Now we will consider the first method, the essence of which is to send the corresponding USSD request.

In order to transfer money, you need to make a call by dialing * 111 * recipient's number * amount sent #. For clarity, let's give an example. Consider the case when you want to forward 100 hryvnias to the number 380621234567. In this case, you will need to make a call by the number: * 111 * 380621234567 * 100 #. After that, you will receive a message in response to confirm the operation. Follow the instructions to do this.

Please note that the price for the service is 3 hryvnias, and the balance should not be less than 10 hryvnias. That is, to send 100 hryvnias, you must have at least 113 hryvnias in your account.

How to transfer money in Ukraine through a USSD request (second method)





This was the first example demonstrating how to transfer money from Life to Life using a USSD request, but there is one more. It may seem very simple, unlike the first, so let's take a look at it.

Instead of a long combination of characters, as in the previous example, you only need to dial * 124 #. After you do this, on the phone screen you will see a special menu where funds are transferred. You will need to go to the section called Perevod balansa. You will be asked to enter the number of the subscriber to whom the money is addressed and directly the amount of funds.

Like last time, after that you will need to confirm the operation. The service fee is also 3 hryvnia, and the balance must be at least 10 hryvnia.

How to transfer money in Ukraine using SMS

So, we have already considered two options for answering the question of how to transfer money from Life to Life in Ukraine. But there is one more. Its essence lies in sending the appropriate SMS message. Let's look at this cash transaction.

Create a new message in which write the following: "PEREVOD number of the recipient payment amount." All these data will need to be sent to number 124. For greater clarity, let's give an example. If you want to send 100 hryvnias to the number 380621234567, then the text in the message should be like this: "PEREVOD 380621234567 100". Everything is pretty simple.

After that, you will also receive a message confirming the operation, as soon as you do this, the money will be sent. This was the last way to answer the question of how to transfer money to Life in Ukraine. Now let's move on to Belarus.

How to transfer money in Belarus





So, as you might guess, the steps for transferring funds from the Life balance in Ukraine and Belarus are different, although they are carried out similarly. So, now we will consider how to transfer money in Belarus using the USSD request on Life.

On the territory of this country there is a special service, it is called "Balance Transfer." Its essence is to send a request by dialing * 120 * 1 #. Then you just need to follow the clear instructions, as a result, you will be able to transfer your funds to another subscriber. As you can see, the method is very similar to that considered by us earlier.

As for the restrictions, they are present. For example, holders of the "Super life :)" tariff will not be able to make a transfer, they are only able to accept it.
