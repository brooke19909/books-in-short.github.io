Club Hotel Grand Efe 4 * (Turkey, Kusadasi): room description, service, reviews

Almost every Russian tourist, if asked about it, will be able to recommend several Turkish hotels to their friends who will give you a real fairy tale for the rest. In this case, it is very important for what purpose you fly on vacation. Someone dreams of hanging out all night and therefore needs a hotel complex located in the center of spa life with a large number of young guests. Some travelers go on vacation to be alone and look for a secluded and quiet hotel away from the hustle and bustle. And there are those who can not imagine holidays without a family, so only the hotel is suitable for them, where they will take care of all the guests, regardless of their age.

If you consider yourself in the latter category, then you will definitely like Hotel Club Hotel Grand Efe 4 *. This hotel primarily positions itself as a family complex, in which both parents and their children will be very comfortable. Today we will tell you in detail about Club Hotel Grand Efe 4 * in Turkey, and also analyze the reviews of guests who have already visited it.





Short description of the hotel complex

Hotel Club Hotel Grand Efe 4 * was built in the very beginning of the nineties. It is a large complex consisting of one eight-story building in the form of two buildings connected to each other and standing at an angle.

During its operation, the hotel was twice renovated: eight and three years ago. Therefore, many reviews of Club Hotel Grand Efe 4 * mention the good condition of the room stock and its excellent equipment. Also often guests talk about the territory of the hotel complex. It has an area of ‚Äã‚Äãtwenty thousand square meters and looks very well-groomed. There are a lot of infrastructure facilities for kids on the territory, so parents will not have to worry about the fact that children at the hotel will become bored and they will not let adults relax.

In total, in Hotel Club Hotel Grand Efe 4 * there are three hundred eighty-five rooms. It is noteworthy that more than eighty percent of them have sea views. This is a pleasant surprise for many travelers. It is worth noting that the hotel has quite a few youth. Mostly families from Russia, Turkey and Germany come here. Moreover, Russian tourists make up less than half of the total number of guests.

Hotel Location

Located Club Hotel Grand Efe in Kusadasi. This place is considered one of the best for those who want to breathe the purest mountain air mixed with the scents of the sea.

Despite its very convenient location, the hotel is quite far from the airport. Tourists from Russia fly to the city of Izmir, from where at least forty-five kilometers will have to travel to the hotel complex.

The nearest settlement to Hotel Club Hotel Grand Efe 4 * is the village of Ozdere. The distance dividing these two points does not exceed two kilometers. Therefore, it is here that tourists go to wander through the streets or to get something interesting in local shops.

Hotel guests in their reviews say that there is a bus stop across the street from the hotel. From here you can leave for Ozdere or Kusadasi. In the village, many praise the Saturday market, where products and unusual products of local craftsmen are inexpensively sold. People come to Kusadasi for different purposes: to see a beautiful city, to go to boutiques, which are very numerous here, or to sit in cozy cafes, watching how city life is seething around.





A few words about the beach

Any tourist will tell you that the most important thing in the hotel is the beach. Club Hotel Grand Efe 4 * has its own beach area with a length of more than one hundred and fifty meters. It consists of sand and pebbles, the entrance to the water abounds with stones. Guests write that on the beach there are two pretty good calls at sea. But in other areas it is better to use special shoes so as not to get hurt.

The coast is equipped with sun loungers and umbrellas. They are provided to hotel guests free of charge, as well as free to take in the hotel and beach towels. Since Club Hotel Grand Efe 4 * is located on the first coastline, it is quite easy for vacationers to get to the beach.

There is a bar on the beach where you can enjoy traditional fast food during lunch. Almost all guests speak about the sea with great enthusiasm. It is usually calm in the morning, and the temperature of the water at this time is very refreshing. After lunch, there are small waves on the water, and it warms up to a very comfortable temperature. And in the evening, according to tourists, the sea resembles real fresh milk, the water becomes so warm.

Territory and infrastructure

On the territory of Club Hotel Grand Efe 4 * there are two swimming pools. One of them is children's. Around the pools there are sun loungers, many of which are located on the lawn in the shade of palm trees. They are in great demand among vacationers.

It is nice that the hotel complex has a lot of water slides. They have different heights and tilt levels, so each guest can choose the one with which he will be most comfortable rolling. In addition, the kids have their own small slides and various facilities located in the children's pool.

There is one main restaurant and four bars for guests. In them you can always find something tasty for children and adults.

The hotel has three conference rooms, they differ in purpose and capacity. The smallest of them can simultaneously accommodate eighty people, the average one hundred and fifty, and the most well-equipped and large can accommodate four hundred guests.

The hotel has a small beauty salon and several shops. They sell basic necessities and souvenirs. If you wish, you can even get some things to relax here. However, the guests themselves are still advised to go shopping in the nearest village or resort town.





Club Hotel Grand Efe 4 *: service

Since the hotel described by us has four stars, our compatriots have very high requirements for service. Most of the guests were satisfied with the attitude of the staff and the quality of the services provided.

The hotel catches Wi-Fi perfectly, a stable signal allows you to access the Internet from anywhere in the hotel complex. In the reviews of tourists it is noted that even on the beach a signal is easily caught for communication, for example, with their loved ones.

At the reception you can freely leave your luggage in the luggage room, exchange currency or arrange a place in the free parking lot. However, keep in mind that the staff practically does not speak Russian. Therefore, be prepared in most cases to speak English.

The hotel rooms are cleaned daily, if necessary, guests can use the laundry service and get back not only clean, but also ironed items. In the apartment, guests have the opportunity to order food and drinks. But this service is paid separately when leaving the room.

Entertainment in the hotel complex

Any four-star hotel provides guests with a wide selection of recreational activities for every taste. First of all, free opportunities to make your vacation more interesting are popular. Usually, various sports games are used for this purpose: table tennis, golf and volleyball. The gym and aerobics, which are carried out every day by the animators working in the hotel complex, will help to keep themselves in good physical shape.

You can also ride on a water slide for free and find the right activity for you on the beach. Here guests can choose from a variety of water activities.

For an additional fee, guests can rent a tennis court and arrange an interesting game. For relaxation, guests go to the sauna, where professionals make an excellent massage. There is a large pool table where fans of this pastime often gather in the evenings.

Please note that there is a disco at the hotel every night. However, entry to it is paid.





Fun for kids

Kids at the hotel are never bored. For them, a whole town is equipped in the pool, from which I do not want to leave at all. Almost all the little guests spend most of the day here. Kids also like the playground, where colorful slides, swings and other designs are installed.

If your child is between four and twelve years old, then you can leave him at the kids club. It is decorated in a very colorful manner, and experienced animators working here will occupy the child with a mass of useful and interesting activities.

There is no children's menu in the restaurant, but parents will always be able to choose something suitable for the baby from the abundance of dishes. For feeding in the restaurant special chairs are provided.





Club Hotel Grand Efe 4 *: food

The restaurant operates on an all-inclusive basis, which is why families with children come here so often. Breakfast is available from seven in the morning and lasts until ten in the morning. Between lunch and dinner, you can always have a bite to eat fast food or fresh pastries.

Often guests mention in the reviews about the queues for food, but mostly they form at the beginning of lunch or dinner. The hotel restaurant is large enough, so there is always enough free space at the tables. Food in the hotel complex is very diverse, every day guests can choose between several types of side dishes, meat dishes prepared in different ways, vegetables and fruits. Vacationers celebrate the delicious salads served in the restaurant, and numerous sauces.

Cons of the hotel

Among the large number of reviews, there are very few that contain information about the disadvantages of living in a hotel complex. The total mass of guests are very happy with their stay at Club Hotel Grand Efe 4 * and only positive things are told about it. Of the minuses, there are lines in the restaurant, weak animation and not always clean pebble beach.

The advantages of a hotel complex

There are many reasons to choose this hotel for yourself:

  • polite staff;
  • varied and tasty food;
  • developed infrastructure;
  • a large number of facilities for children;
  • many free sunbeds by the pool and on the beach;

In conclusion, we can say that the hotel complex is well suited for people who want to spend their holidays quietly away from the city bustle.
