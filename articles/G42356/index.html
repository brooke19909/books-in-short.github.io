Where to begin

In order to get a thing that fits perfectly on you, it is important not only to know how to crochet a scarf pipe, but also to determine the exact size. It is necessary to measure the distance from the crown to the chin. It corresponds to half the perimeter of the pipe. If it turned out 22 cm, then the loops are gaining 44 cm. You will have to tie the sample in the same way of knitting as the original. Only after steaming can you find out the true width. A sample of approximately 10 x 10 cm will be sufficient as a sample.

Initially, it is difficult to determine the density of the fabric, which will be obtained from a specific yarn, since all types of threads are different. The nature of the knitting for each craftswoman is also her own. After final processing, measure the sample. Having made the proportion, you will find out how many loops you need to dial for your size.





When reading the text, the calculations seem complicated. But when you do them yourself once, then it will be easy. If, for example, in a sample of 25 loops it turns out 10 cm, then 40 cm will require 100 loops.

Crochet scarf with description

If you already have some experience, you can not use ready-made step-by-step instructions. Knowing the principle of manufacturing the product, some independently come up with a variety of options, changing, for example, color combinations or decorative elements.

To get the scarf pipe shown in the photo, the crochet scheme should be as follows:

  1. Gather the desired number of loops corresponding to the perimeter of the circumference of the snout.
  2. Connect the resulting chain into a ring.
  3. Then you start making the pattern itself. It can be performed both with double crochet and without crochet. So, knit any version of the column.
  4. You need to perform several air loops. The width of the resulting hole depends on their number. The more you tie them, the longer the mesh will be.
  5. Perform the second column through the appropriate number of loops in the chain.
  6. Actions are repeated. When choosing the width of the cell, calculate in advance how many loops you need to dial in the chain. The value should be a multiple of the number of air loops forming the length of the hole.
  7. When the first row is ready, go to the second.

The subsequent ones are carried out according to the same scheme until the desired height of the snail is obtained. Fasten the thread, cut it. So the scarf pipe is crocheted. For beginner needlewomen, such a product will be a good debut.

Tight knitting

The first option, shown above, is a light decoration. Now let's look at an example in which a warm scarf tube is made. The crochet pattern is as follows:

  1. Take a tool the size of 7 mm, the yarn you like.
  2. Perform a test sample and the necessary calculations to determine the desired number of loops.
  3. Gather a chain of the corresponding number of air loops. Lock them in a ring.
  4. The pattern will be as follows: 4 lifting VPs, insert the hook into the second loop and make 3 more VPs, grab the thread and pass through the two loops located on the hook, knit 1 VP. The whole series is done in a similar way.
  5. Go to the next one and do this to the end.

For beginners, this is not always easy. There is a very simple and quick option for manufacturing the product. Before joining the ring, it will look like the photo shows. The image shows that knitting does not occur with ordinary threads, but with a "semi-finished product".

Such a scarf pipe will look very original. Crocheting it from ribbon yarn is very easy. It is a wide strip assembled in a skein, along the edges of which a thread with loops is passed. It is with their help that the tapes are connected to the product. They are plain, colorful, openwork, with decor and even in the form of lace. Such yarn makes magnificent ruffles and just very airy unusual things.

The main thing is to work with her very simply. So, so that you get a scarf pipe (hook), the manufacturing scheme will be as follows:

  1. Cut several strips of yarn with a size equal to the perimeter of the snout ring.
  2. Connect them together along the long side with ordinary threads, for example, columns with two crochets.
  3. Sew or hook the fabric along the short side. All is ready. Fast and easy.

Snood from parts or with decor

If you want to make something original and simple, from separate elements connected into a single whole, you will get a magnificent scarf-pipe (crochet). The scheme of work will be as follows:

  1. Take a multi-colored yarn, from each shade you perform, for example, a square with single crochet columns.
  2. Make the right amount of elements. You calculate their length, width and number based on the size of the chest, taking into account the multiplicity.
  3. Connect the individual parts first in vertical rows with alternating colors, for example, in a checkerboard pattern.
  4. Sew the resulting strip into a pipe. At the junction, so that it does not greatly attract attention, you can make a bright strip of another pattern or shade.
  5. If desired, tie the upper and lower edges in a circle to give it a delicacy, using, for example, a simple system when you make several columns in one loop, then knit one in each previous row, and so on to the end. The number of repetitions is easy to calculate by the number of buttonhole edges of your scarf.

Children's snood





Very often, women begin to needlework, trying to please their baby with a beautiful thing of their own making. Understanding how to crochet a scarf pipe for a child is easy. The main thing here is not to make a mistake with the sizes and choose the right thread.

It is better to make elements of children's clothing from special baby-yarn, since it is hypoallergenic and does not cause redness or other reactions even on delicate sensitive skin. For small ones, it is better not to use lace knitting.

It is important to provide warmth inside the snout. In addition, numerous holes can attract unnecessary attention to the crumbs. He will constantly put his fingers in them, which will quickly lead to damage to the product. The original will be a children's scarf-pipe crochet, if you make it in the form of a mouse, chanterelle, kitten. You can knit in a circle or strip, and then carefully sew the ends into a ring.

Use the simplest texture that comes from single crochet or double crochet. In this case, you do not have to constantly count the loops to get a pattern. Separately fabricate the ears (flat or volumetric) and attach in the appropriate place. For older children, just use multi-colored decorative yarn. Big buttons, brushes, fringe, openwork flowers look beautiful.
