How to make an ax out of paper? Everyone can learn this.

Even in kindergarten, children are taught to make various things from paper and other materials. This is necessary so that they learn to distinguish features of things according to certain criteria: color, shape, size, and so on.

These classes develop thinking, finger motility in a child. Gradually, children learn to do more complex things. Let's look at an example of how to make an ax out of paper. Not every adult can do it.

How to make an ax out of paper (diagram, necessary tools)

The easiest and fastest way is to cut the ax template in two copies, then the sidewalls are cut out. Everything sticks together. The most important thing - when gluing, you need to consider how many centimeters to leave on the seam (usually one centimeter is enough). The finished product can be painted in the desired color. Tools used: scissors, ruler, glue, pencil, paints.




Consider how to make an ax out of paper using the origami technique. To do this, you need scissors, glue and paper. You can take colored paper, or you can take Snow Maiden paper (at the end of the work process, you will need to color it). A sheet of paper is folded so as to get a square. The excess strip is cut off. From it, in the future, the ax is made for the ax by bending inward (the resulting handle will be about one centimeter wide). The resulting square is folded in half, laid out back. Then it folds in half to the other side and folds back.

All corners of the square folds to the center. It is important when folding to make neat lines, everything should be symmetrical. The resulting middle square is turned over, then the resulting four corners are again bent to the center of the square. Next, the procedure is repeated again. The resulting small square is turned over. A corner is taken, which expands on both sides. Two extended angles fold together. The opposite side also expands and the corners fold. The blank is folded in half. The hatchet sticks in the middle of the ax.

Afterword

Of course, such classes develop attentiveness and perseverance in children. Therefore, they must be carried out not only in kindergartens and schools, but also at home. This will help bring parents closer to their children.

Having figured out how to make an ax out of paper, you can learn how to make any tools in the origami technique and not only.
