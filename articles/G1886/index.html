Tyumen is the largest city in Siberia. There are more than 17 objects of cultural heritage, as well as a large number of geothermal sources. This makes the city attractive not only for local residents, but also for tourists. Many ponds and forest parks are great for spending time.

The recreation center "House of Pioneers" (Tyumen), which will be discussed in the article, offers a comfortable stay in the forest. Below we will describe how to get to the place, what are the rooms and what is their cost.

general information

Most modern tourist establishments are aimed at entertaining guests, organizing leisure and catering. The recreation center "House of Pioneers" (Tyumen) is a place where you can recall the past. In the hotel complex, in addition to entertainment, there is a large museum. It contains exhibits and medals, clothes and other attributes of the pioneers.

Address

The hotel-museum complex "House of Pioneers" opens its doors to all guests at the address: Tyumen region, Tyumen region, 27 km of the Yalutor tract, building 1/1. On the official website of the recreation center you can find the phones by which reservations are made. You can get here both by your own transport, and by public from Tyumen (bus and minibus).

Moving your own car from the city center, you must adhere to the Yalutor direction. Before the start of 27 kilometers of this tract there will be a direction indicator to the recreation center.
