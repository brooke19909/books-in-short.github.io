Hotel Location

First you need to find out where the hotel is located, as well as get acquainted with its history.

Baross City Hotel Baross City 3 * is a three-star hotel located in Budapest. It is located in a restored 19th-century building near the old town, 50 meters from Keleti Train Station. The hotel was built by the Mellow Mood Hotels chain, which has been successfully operating in the tourism industry for 10 years.

Budapest is the capital and main administrative, economic, cultural and tourist center of Hungary. This state is located in Eastern Europe, some scientists believe that it is located in Central Europe. Hungary is a country with a rapidly developing economy, market transformations in it are almost complete. Industry rests on the export of engineering products and other industrial products.

The city of Budapest was formed in 1873 as a result of the merger of Pest, Buda and Obuda. The capital is located in the lowlands of the Carpathian basin, which is limited by the Carpathians, the Alps and the South Slavic mountain ranges. The Danube River divides the city into two parts. Moreover, Budapest is located on the fault site, so there are many thermal springs, because of which hundreds of tourists come to the city every year.
