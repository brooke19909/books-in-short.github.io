Inexpensive hotels in Lyubertsy: list, addresses, reviews

In Lyubertsy there are wonderful hotels where you can stay for one night or several days. There are both separate apartments and simply beds in dormitory rooms. Consider the most popular places:

  • "Eco house";
  • "Kotelniki";
  • "Moscow Nights";
  • "Town";
  • "Zhulebino";
  • "My sunny grandmother."

"Spark"

The average room rate is 1 thousand rubles per day. Located at: Marata street, 4a. The Iskra Hotel cannot accommodate children; in addition, extra beds are not available in the rooms. There is no internet. Free parking is available on site. No pets allowed. When paying for the room you need to pay 18% fee, this amount is included in the total price.

The hotel is next to a small shop and sauna. If you still want to take a pet with you, then you can try to coordinate this nuance with the administration.

The hotel has double rooms, which, according to residents, are quite comfortable. Registration of guests takes place around the clock. The rooms have a TV, fridge and kettle.

No smoking on site. The staff speaks Russian. Nearby there is a beauty salon. The hotel has an elevator. Rooms are cleaned every day.

The hotel reviews say that the rooms are in good repair. The furniture is new, the bedding is clean. Towels, cups are available. TV set up.

The airport is 48 km away.

Ecodomic

The cost of one room per day is about 3 thousand rubles. Located at: Gagarina Avenue, house 14. Guest house "Ecodomic" - an inexpensive hotel that is popular with tourists and people who often come to work in Lyubertsy. The rooms have televisions, washing machines. Each room has a kitchen, which is fully equipped.

In addition, guests of the capital often come here. The hotel is just 20 km from Moscow. Nearby there is a bus stop and shops.

There is Internet access, you can rent an iron and ironing board, hairdryer.

The reviews write that the rooms are spacious and bright. They are constantly cleaned, but not thoroughly. In general, the administration is friendly and the atmosphere is cozy.

Kotelniki

The price per day here is less than a thousand - only 750 rubles. The hotel itself is cozy. Next to it is the metro line of the same name. On the territory of the "Master Hotel Kotelniki" there is Internet. Located at Novoryazanskoye Shosse, 5a.

The institution has 21 rooms. Each is cozy and comfortable. They have everything you need to relax: a TV, household appliances. Nearby are two eateries. Due to the fact that the hotel has a convenient location, it is easy to get to anywhere in the city. For example, Kursky Station is 19 km away and the airport is 40 km away.

On-site parking is available. She is guarded. The rooms have heating. Family rooms available. Rooms are cleaned every day. All are soundproofed. No smoking on site. At the request of the resident, the administrator can call a taxi.

The reviews describe that the price of the rooms correlates well with the living conditions. There is a kitchen, you can cook something yourself. All staff are polite.

"Moscow Nights"

The room rate per day is 800 rubles. The hotel is located in the city center at 213 Oktyabrsky Avenue. Paid parking is available. Hotel "Moscow Nights" in Lyubertsy includes 98 comfortable rooms. They belong to different categories. Equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. There is a kitchen where every guest can cook his own dinner.

Near the hotel there is a metro station, an amusement park, a sports palace. There are also cafes nearby. There is a left-luggage office, Internet access, TV.

In the reviews, residents note that the quality and price are perfectly correlated with each other. The staff is polite and helpful. The rooms are clean, cleaned often.

"Town"

This hostel in Lyubertsy is inexpensive. The price of a room is about 500 rubles per day. The hotel is located in the city center at the following address: street 3 post office, house 29a. Nearby there is a traffic intersection. The rooms have a homely atmosphere, there is a set of bed linen, as well as the necessary equipment. Bathrooms are shared. Free internet access is provided. The building has a dining room where you can dine. Laundry services are available for 100 rubles. Parking is paid and guarded. For a place you need to pay 100 rubles.

In the reviews they write that the linen is fresh, the furniture is new. Living here is really inexpensive. The hotel in Lyubertsy monitors the work of the staff, always clean. If you come out of season, you can avoid the queues in the shower. There is a kitchen that is well equipped. You can cook something at any time of the day.

"Zhulebino"

Accommodation at this hotel in Lyubertsy is inexpensive, so the place is popular. The cost per day is about 700 rubles. Located at: Kirova street, house 9. There is free parking and Internet. You can store your things in lockers with locks. Near them installed video surveillance. If necessary, you can ask to wake up at a certain time. In addition, the administration can choose a special route in Moscow. Rooms are cleaned every day.

All rooms are well lit, they are clean. There are beds. A dryer, washing machine and hairdryer are provided. Each tenant is given bed linen and towels. On the floor there are three bathrooms with sinks and latrines.
