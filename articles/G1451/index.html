How to make a paper crane do it yourself: step-by-step instructions

Origami has long ceased to be just a form of needlework. This is art, outlet, a way of knowing oneself and the world. It takes your breath away when you see how an ordinary sheet of paper in the skillful hands of a master turns into a flower, beast or bird. You look and wonder: how to make paper cranes, bunnies, dragons from just one sheet of paper, without the use of scissors and glue? Is it possible for everyone to master the origami technique? The answer is simple: any person, even a child, if desired, can learn how to fold origami figures. The legend of the little Japanese girl is a confirmation of this fact.

Paper Crane: A Little History

For many years now, the legend of the girl Sadako, who suffered during the atomic attack in Hiroshima in 1945, has been passed from mouth to mouth. During her illness, she made paper cranes, thinking that she would recover when she made a thousand of these figures. But Sadako managed to complete only 644 cranes. She died. The rest of the figures were completed by her friends, dreaming that there would be no war and grief in the world. Since then, paper cranes have been a symbol of peace and prosperity. Almost every resident of Japan knows how to make a paper crane, knows how to create them with their own hands. People now believe that if you fold a thousand cranes out of paper and make a wish, it will certainly come true. True or not, but we can try.



How to fold a paper crane: step by step instructions

  1. Bend a square sheet of paper diagonally.
  2. The resulting double triangle fold vertically - corner to corner.
  3. Straighten the upper triangle into a square.
  4. Turn the product to the opposite side.
  5. Turn the long corner to the right.
  6. Straighten the triangle into a square.
  7. Fold the lateral corners of the upper square towards the center, and then bend it back to its original position.
  8. Unscrew the top square along the fold lines up.
  9. Turn the product over.
  10. Bend the corners to the center.
  11. Unscrew the top square along the fold lines up.
  12. Bend the side corners of the upper figure towards the center.
  13. Bend the bottom left corner upwards inwards.
  14. Bend the bottom corner to the right inwards.
  15. Bend one of the corners inward.
  16. Spread the elements of the head, tail and wings of the paper crane. Blow inside the product so that it straightens and takes shape.


How to turn paper cranes into exclusive souvenirs?

Although all origami cranes are made using the same technology, you can think about how to make a paper crane made with your own hands unique and original. Having folded a paper figure, you can paint it with paints. You can also decorate the craft with applique paper, fabric, natural materials. From paper figures sometimes create a whole composition. This can be a wall panel with cranes, a garland of a large number of birds, a voluminous postcard with an origami crane. Having invested a piece of your soul, your skills and imagination in the manufacture of a symbol of the world - a paper crane - you will create a beautiful exclusive souvenir. Such a gift will surely be appreciated by your family and friends. And maybe very soon they will ask you about how to make a paper crane with your own hand.
