Waterfalls in Bali: a photo with a description of the most beautiful and unusual

If you go to the central part of the island of Bali, namely away from the coastline, you can admire the nature of extraordinary beauty. She is really bewitching, attracts attention. It is nice to be here, walk and just relax. In this part of the island, a humid climate prevails, which can be maintained thanks to a well-overgrown tropical forest. And here are incredibly beautiful waterfalls. In Bali, they are unique, such as, perhaps, can not be seen anywhere else. And this is something that must be included in the must-see list for all tourists.

About Bali Waterfalls

Firstly, there are a lot of them. Some can be called ordinary, while some are considered one of the most beautiful on the planet. Secondly, they do not dry out even in the drought season. Therefore, you can visit them at any time of the year. Rather, it is a stone in the "garden" of Thailand, where they are small and really dry out in the dry season. Thirdly, waterfalls in Bali are included in a wide variety of island tours. They can be visited both independently and as part of a tourist group. You need to consider this moment: almost all of them are paid. You will have to pay a certain amount for entry. Now let's move on to the most beautiful of them.

Bali Underground Waterfall - Tukad Chepung

Very atmospheric place. It seems like you got to another planet. This underground waterfall in Bali has a height of 15 m. The passage to it is a narrow crevice between stones, overgrown plants and moss. The waterfall itself is an even stream of water falling from a ledge into a gap between the rocks. Below they are going to a small pool, which is convenient for swimming. Although you can stand under the jets. However, it is quite cool and the water is invigorating. It only gets warm in the afternoon. To get to this waterfall in a cave in Bali, you can use the following GPS coordinates: -8.17265, 115.10458.

Then you need to go down the stairs. The lagoon is deep, but small. And the reservoir formed by him looks like an emerald lake. Aling Aling has a height of about 20 m. The entrance costs 10 thousand rupees (less than 50 rubles). Coordinates: -8.17265, 115.10458.

Melanting

Deservedly takes its place among the popular waterfalls. It is located near Lake Tambligan, near the village of Munduk. The waterfall is surrounded by thickets of coffee trees and spices. The place is very beautiful and quiet, and also is closest to the south, so it is most convenient to get to it.

The waterfall in Bali Melanting has a height of 15 m. Its noise merges with the singing of birds, the rustle of foliage and the chirping of insects. An ideal place for a peaceful pastime and swimming. Entrance costs 5 thousand rupees (less than 24 rubles). Coordinates: -8.260067, 115.062812.

Leke Leke

Leke Leke Waterfall in Bali is another paradise place, which must be visited by tourists eager to enjoy the unique views of the incredible beauty of the island's nature. It is located in the central part. Here you can walk along the bamboo bridge, as well as ride on a swing that is stretched between two tall trees (250,000 rupees - 1160 rubles). Another paradise on earth. Those hungry for a walk can visit the small restaurant located here. In it you can not only have a snack, but also purchase drinks or plain water. Entrance will cost 30,000 rupees (140 rubles).

Secumpul

Another most popular place, and tourists often look for how to get to it. It is most convenient to go to the Sekumpul waterfall in Bali from the village of the same name. Upon reaching it, the car should be parked, as you can only go further on foot. The path will take about 30-40 minutes. First there is a concrete road, which then goes into a forest path. This is more of a wild trail, so the walk will leave a pleasant impression. Approximately in the middle of the route it will be bifurcated, you need to turn right. Then cross the bridge, climb the stairs, cross the river - and you are there.

Sekumpul falls from a height of over 80 m. It is the highest waterfall in Bali. The place is very beautiful, original. It is a complex of seven waterfalls, but only two of them are open for visitors. And each is formed by different sources. The water here is cool but comfortable for swimming. If you manage to visit here in sunny weather, then you will get a unique opportunity to watch the numerous educated rainbows. Therefore, the difficult path is worth it, and because of this, Sekumpul can be called the best waterfall in Bali.

Entrance costs 10,000 rupees (less than 50 rubles). Coordinates: -8.17398, 115.1821.

Coffin

No less popular natural attraction of the island. Located next to the mountain village of the same name, near Ubud. Reviews about the waterfall in Bali are also delighted, as well as about the ones described above. Therefore, you should definitely visit it. Several trails lead to Mounduk. There are both short straight lines and long ones laid along a cliff, which make it possible to admire the local colorful landscape.

The height of this waterfall is 25 m, but the noise is already heard for several hundred meters. By the way, it is located next to Melanting, which was already written about above. These Ubud waterfalls are incredibly beautiful, they are fascinating. Although tourists claim that the Mounduk is more beautiful. Entrance costs 5,000 rupees (less than 24 rubles). Coordinates: -8.25746, 115.0703.

Sebatu

Another waterfall located near Ubud in Bali. Shibatu (the correct name for Sebatu) is not just a beautiful place. This waterfall and the small pool formed by it are a sacred source. It is not popular among tourists, although there are some, it is very much appreciated by the Balinese. It is believed that if you stand under the streams of the waterfall, first facing the rock, and then with your back, you can cleanse yourself, get rid of sins, evil thoughts and even physical ailments. True, the water here is literally icy, and the descent is a lot of degrees.

Mandatory is the offering. It is sold in the parking lot. The waterfall is located next to the village of the same name, and the landmark is the Gunung Kavi Sebatu Temple. Coordinates: -8.399938, 115.295646.

Rang rang

Like the previous one, this waterfall is low, but very beautiful. Moreover, there are not many tourists here, so you can enjoy the comfort and free swimming. Rang-Reng is located near Guinyar. The waterfall is a delta of a mountain river flowing from a cave, the length of which is about 20-30 m.

A huge plus is that it is easy to get to it - you should go along the path. There are bamboo pavilions that allow you to enjoy the view of the waterfall from afar. The stones below are slippery, so those who are going to go down and plunge into the water should be careful.

The entrance costs 15 thousand rupees (almost 70 rubles). GPS coordinates: -8.51573, 115.33140.

Tegenungan

We can say that this is the best waterfall in Bali, as it is located closer to the tourist areas of the island, but it is also very beautiful. And it is easiest to get to it, for example, from Kuta the travel time will be only half an hour by car. Transport should be left in the parking lot, walk down a simple path. The hike will take about 10 minutes. You can also go up to the river bed. The waterfall is beautiful, bright, the water here is warm, comfortable for swimming. Surround its cliffs overgrown with vegetation. Entrance costs 10 thousand rupees (less than 50 rubles). Coordinates: ‚àí8.57539, 115.2898.

Gith gith

It is located on the most popular tourist route, on the way from Bedugul to Lovina, so there are many tourists here. The path leading to it is a florid path circling along the river, similar to a serpentine. It can not be called simple, as you have to wade through the jungle and cascades.

Git-Git is a group of waterfalls consisting of several levels. The review can be started both from above and from below. It is also very beautiful here, you can enjoy the enchanting nature and swim in warm water. Entrance costs 5 thousand rupees (less than 24 rubles). Canyoning is also available here. It will cost 1.4 million rupees in 2.5 hours (6500 rubles). Coordinates: ‚àí8.20246, 115.13974.

Tibumana

The waterfall is located in the village of Apuan, on the Bangunlem River, 15 km east of Ubud. You should drive to the temple of Pura Dahlem, there is a small parking lot where you can leave your car. And here a visit to the waterfall is paid - 10 thousand rupees (less than 50 rubles).

Next, you have to walk on the stairs. The path is beautiful, it runs along the river. It is surrounded by beautiful tropical vegetation. When there is a fork, you need to turn left to get to the waterfall. Tibumana and its surrounding nature are especially beautiful during the rainy season. For example, a waterfall only at this time is considered the most full-flowing. But in a drought, of course, it does not dry out completely, but decreases to the size of a trickle.

This is also one of the most beautiful waterfalls. The cleanest air and unusual landscape attract to this place, and I want to come back here again. The height of Tibuman is approximately 35 m. Below, a swimming pool is convenient for swimming. You can swim right under the streams of the waterfall - it is absolutely safe. Coordinates: -8.50257, 115.33066.

Dusun Kooning

This is one of the largest waterfalls on the island, which is also located near Ubud. It is quite powerful, strong, falling from massive rocks, so it looks pretty impressive. It forms a small lagoon where you can swim, and then continues to flow down the rocks, eventually flowing into a mountain river. A steep path through the jungle leads to Dusun Kuning. Here you can triple a picnic or just admire the surrounding beauties. The passage to the waterfall costs 10 thousand rupees (less than 50 rubles). Coordinates: -8.49048, 115.35735.

Kanto Lampo

Located near Gianyar, near Ubud. This is an incredibly beautiful stepped waterfall, which is ideal for creating unique photos. Photoshoots are best done between 11 a.m. and 2 p.m., as Kanto Lampo is in the gorge, and the sun's rays come here just at that time.

A stepped path leads to the waterfall. Like similar trails, it is surrounded by dense tropical vegetation.

It is said that this waterfall has incredibly powerful energy. This can be checked by sitting on a stone under the water flows of Kanto Lampo. The passage to it is paid, it will cost only 5 thousand rupees (less than 24 rubles). Coordinates: -8.53278, 115.332480.

Conclusion

There are a lot of waterfalls in Bali, both open and closed to visitors. Therefore, to describe all of them is simply unrealistic. Most of them are located in the central part of the island. There are those that are easy to reach, and the path to others is a real endurance test. But physical fatigue will pass, you just have to look at this charming natural phenomenon.

Being in Bali, you should definitely visit at least one waterfall. If you can visit several places - this is a huge plus. The easiest way is to join the tourist group to bring a guide to the destination. But you can also navigate yourself by entering the cherished numbers of GPS coordinates into the navigator. You can move around both on cars and on bikes. Near almost all waterfalls there are parking lots.

It is also important to consider some recommendations:

  1. Choose comfortable shoes. She should sit firmly on her leg, not push or slip. Do not take expensive shoes with you. Let her not be sorry to accidentally tear or wet.
  2. Take a swimsuit, towel, and dry clothes with you.
  3. Do not forget about water and snack. Not every waterfall has a cafe.
  4. Use repellents.
  5. It can rain in the mountains at any time. Therefore, it is better to take a raincoat with you.
  6. Although the waterfalls in Bali do not dry completely, in the dry season they can still become noticeably weaker. This should also be considered.

Following these tips, you can get the most of pleasant emotions from visiting waterfalls.
