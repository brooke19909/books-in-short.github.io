"Holiday Park Resort" (Turkey): reviews of tourists

It is not for nothing that Turkey has long and fairly firmly taken a leading position among tourist destinations. And this is not surprising: the sun and the sea, the excellent service that the hotels of this country are famous for, offering the all-inclusive system so convenient for families, the extensive entertainment program for adults and children - no one will be bored here.

Turkey Resorts

Tourists, judging by the reviews, feel equally well in all corners of this maritime state, well-known among Russians. Antalya and Kemer, Alanya and Belek - all of them give travelers an excellent vacation in the hot sun, magnificent beaches, the opportunity to taste the best dishes of national cuisine in numerous local restaurants and much more.



The fact that Turkey is in high demand among Russian tourists is unanimously claimed by all tour operators. The cost of beach, sightseeing and even ski tours depends on the category of the selected hotel, on the prestige of a particular resort, as well as on the time of year. It is maximum in July, and in October, many people in Turkey have much cheaper vacations.

In addition, Alanya is a more economical destination compared to Belek or Kemer. New Year's and skiing tours are very popular here, since almost all five-star hotels operate year-round.

Turkey Hotels 5 *

They are considered the most popular among tourists, including Russians. In order to get the championship line in the ranking of the best hotels in Turkey, designers sometimes build incredible size hotel complexes that amaze the imagination not only in scope, but also in engineering. No matter what resort they are built in, five-star hotels are located on vast areas. Their territory may include parks and ponds, attractions and shops, clubs and restaurants, as well as golf courses and, of course, magnificent beaches.

Holiday Park Resort (Turkey) is no exception, the photo of which indicates that you can really relax here.

general information

The hotel, built in 2001, is located between Alanya and Manavgat. This is one of the cheapest destinations in Turkey, however, the comparative cheapness of the tours does not affect the quality of the proposed vacation. Here guests are promised an unforgettable vacation. Hotel "Holiday Park Resort 5 *" (Turkey) is located in the village of Okudzhalar, which has recently been popular with Russians who prefer inexpensive Mediterranean resorts. Tour operators, he is positioned as a suburban, more suitable for a relaxing holiday. The territory on which it is located is twelve thousand square meters. Among the dense vegetation, two pools are arranged, there is a SPA center and an amphitheater, as well as tennis courts.

Animation

Turkey, Alanya, "Holiday Park Resort" (5 stars) in particular, is famous for the quality of entertainment programs. The hotel always has a group of animators.
