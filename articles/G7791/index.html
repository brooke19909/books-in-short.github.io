Hotel Monte Casa 4 * (Montenegro / Budvanska Riviera): photos and reviews of tourists

Montenegro is washed by the waters of the Adriatic Sea, and this fact automatically makes it a popular destination among tourists. And many people really come here. There are many hotels in Montenegro. Most of them were discovered long ago, in the second half of the 20th century. Monte Casa 4 * refers to those. It was built in the 80s. But in the mid-2000s, the hotel underwent a complete renovation. In 2007 it was opened, and before the eyes of tourists appeared a modern resort hotel.





general information

The full name of the hotel is Monte Casa SPA & Wellness 4 *. Petrovac is the town in which it is located. Less than 1,500 people live there. The beach at the hotel has its own, and you can reach it in just a minute. Another 150 meters from the building is a bus stop. From there, you can reach various attractions, such as the citadel and pier of Budva, St. John’s Church, Mogren Beach, TQ Plaza, Top Hill Club, Skadar Lake, etc. Tivat Airport is a 30-40 minute drive by car from the hotel. In general, the location is favorable, and this is good news.

What is in the hotel?

Monte Casa 4 * has everything that just might be necessary for a tourist. It really has everything: from free Wi-Fi to private parking.

In the lobby, guests are greeted by a 24-hour reception. Here you can expedite registration of both departure and arrival. There is also a tour desk in the lobby (where they also provide ticketing services), an ATM, a left-luggage office. Each guest has the right to use the services of a concierge.

The hotel also has such basic services as laundry, dry cleaning, shuttle service (both locally and to / from the airport), car rental, a beauty salon and a hairdresser, a service for delivering food with drinks and packed lunches, shops (including souvenir). It is forbidden to smoke at the hotel, but there are separate places intended for this. Needless to say, the presence of certain familiar services, even if there is even a church!

A few words should be said about the numbers. All apartments are hypoallergenic and soundproofed. There are bridal suites and rooms for guests with disabilities. There are elevators to access the upper floors. And here they provide VIP services.

And finally about the staff. Monte Casa 4 * employs highly skilled workers who also speak four languages. Among them are English, Croatian, Serbian and Russian. The latter is particularly pleasing.





Entertainment

Bored, being in Monte Casa 4 *, you definitely will not have to. Some people spend all their time on the beach, swimming and sunbathing. There, by the way, you can do water sports, diving and windsurfing in particular. Others remain in the hotel and brighten up their leisure time there. The hotel has a Turkish bath and library, a jacuzzi and gym, a solarium and a sauna. And here they arrange a free fitness program for both adults and children. It includes an assessment of the physical condition of a person, consultation with a trainer and classes throughout the stay. Interestingly, the emphasis here is on oriental techniques. Trainers in the hall, by the way, are Italian-made.

There is also an indoor pool, heated in winter, and a bike rental service. You can rent this 2-wheeled vehicle for free and go for a drive around Petrovac to explore its beauty.

For beauty and health

In Monte Casa 4 * (Petrovac) there is a SPA salon. There guests can spend time on a variety of procedures. In the local SPA, body peeling, massage of the feet, back and face are popular. Vichi massage is as popular as stone therapy, which involves the use of hot stones. But most of all people like Bora-Bora. This is a massage, although it must be admitted that it is something more. The Bora-Bora technique involves exposure to the body and muscles using rag bags with algae.

Beauty treatments, hydromassage, detoxification program, scrubs, peels, body wraps, masks - all this can be tried here. Moreover, in the SPA-center of the Monte Casa 4 * hotel (Petrovac), specialists with medical education will develop an individual program for each client individually, taking into account his physiological characteristics and wishes.

Finally, it is worth noting that each procedure is certified by the famous cosmetic brand Thalgo (France).





Restaurants

The main restaurant of the Monte Casa 4 * hotel (Montenegro) offers its visitors dishes of international, Mediterranean and Russian cuisine. The menu also presents a large number of local delicacies and delicacies. There are even individual dietary suggestions. It is also interesting that in the process of preparing dishes exclusively ecologically clean products are used, as well as ingredients that have not gone through freezing or preservation. Needless to say, the quality of the food, if the chef with Michelin awards is at the head of the culinary team.

The second restaurant is called the Ambassador. It is located near the sea, in the open air. Most often, fish and seafood are ordered here. Gourmet cocktails and fine Montenegrin wines are also served in this establishment .

Every week, Monte Casa SPA & Wellness 4 * holds exclusive gala dinners. And often you can get to a tasting of Mediterranean wines with the participation of upscale sommeliers.





Organization of private events

Holidays for many people are a reason to relax and have fun as it should. And this is the right approach. It’s better to generally celebrate some special event abroad. For example, in Monte Casa 4 *. Montenegro attracts many people who want to combine relaxation with a joyful event.

And often weddings are held here. Moreover, the organization of such celebrations is included in the list of basic services of the Monte Casa SPA & Wellness 4 * hotel. VIP services, luxurious furnishings, elegant design, taking into account every detail ... Do you really need something else to organize a memorable event?

If guests turn to the hotel and restaurant team with a request to arrange the best day in their life, then they will definitely be satisfied. Everything will be organized according to their desires. Guests only need to voice them.

It is also worth noting that the hotel organizes business events - conferences, trainings, seminars. The hotel has an excellent conference room with modern equipment. Premium audiovisual equipment, natural light, high-speed Internet and prompt staff assistance - these are the best contributors to quality and productive work.

Budget rooms

The most inexpensive accommodation option that the Monte Casa 4 * hotel offers is a standard of 22 square meters. There are 26 such apartments in the hotel. Of these, 16 overlook the park. From the windows of the rest you can see the pool.

All standards have a large double bed and a sofa bed. Although guests are given a choice. If they do not need one single bed, they can choose apartments with two separate, standard.

Each room also has a spacious and bright bathroom with a bathtub, an LCD TV, Wi-Fi router, powerful air conditioning, a minibar and a balcony. There are also video games, paid TV channels, a radio, a fan, a dressing room, and a highchair for a child. In general, everything and even more. And such apartments cost 40-50 thousand rubles a week (with breakfast). Prices are not fixed, as they vary according to seasonality. With half board, the maximum price for a weekly stay is 70 tons. for two. And with three meals a day, the price will increase to 84 tons. By the way, if guests come with a child under the age of 12 years, then he can live for free. But you have to sleep on the couch.





Family rooms

There is a Monte Casa Hotel 4 * and such apartments. There are thirteen of them. The highlight of family rooms is that the entire living space (45 sq. M.) Is divided into two rooms. That is, to the bedroom and living room. Again, there are two options - with a view of the park and the sea. And they also have a kettle.

Inside, there is everything that was listed earlier. Plus a plasma panel, tea accessories, two bathrooms, a large set of towels, hypoallergenic blankets with pillows, cotton linen, bathrobes, and a safe. Maximum capacity of this apartment is 2 adults and 2 children.

The approximate price of a room for 7 nights with breakfast is 65,000 rubles. With half board - 85 tons, and with three meals a day - 100 tons. But this is an apartment overlooking the garden. If you need numbers from where you could see the sea, you have to overpay for 15-20 tons.

Studio

These apartments in Monte Casa 4 * (Montenegro, Petrovac) are supposedly created for lovers of spaciousness and privacy. Studio, whose area is 42 square meters. m, designed for two people. Most often, couples in love settle here.

In total, the hotel has 18 studios. 9 - overlooking the sea. From the windows of the remaining nine you can see the park. All rooms have a bathroom with a large bath and bidet. Also, there is everything that was listed above.

A variant with a sea view will cost 60 tons. (with breakfast). Half board will cost 80 tons, and three meals a day - about 95 tons.





Premium class

There are especially expensive apartments in the hotel. These include suites, of which there are only four. Naturally, all of them overlook the sea. The space (107 sq. M) is divided into a living room with a dining area, two bedrooms, a huge bathroom, an additional toilet and a long balcony. The maximum number of people who can live here is 4 adults and 2 children. Or 5 adults.

Enough space for everyone. One room has a king size double bed. In the living room there is a folding sofa. And in the second bedroom there are two separate standard beds. The weekly price of a suite with breakfast for everyone is 135,000 p. With half board and three meals a day - 160,000 and 175,000, respectively. In principle, even in the latter case it comes out inexpensively - at 35-43 tons. per person.

But the most luxurious option is a presidential suite. Gorgeous 3-room apartment. With two bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, a guest toilet and a living room. Each room has a widescreen plasma panel. Instead of a balcony - a terrace with panoramic views of the sea. Above, by the way, is a photograph of one of the rooms of the presidential "luxury". It costs a week stay with breakfast 320 000 rubles for five. With half board and three meals a day - 370 and 385 thousand rubles, respectively.





What do the guests say?

Finally, it is worth noting the attention left on the hotel Monte Casa 4 * reviews. They are extremely positive, which is not even surprising.

All guests enjoy the hotel’s location. It is supposedly located in the depths - it does not reach the noise and smells from the streets, neighboring cafes and restaurants. They note the level of nutrition with attention. Dishes are not repeated, everything is really tasty and fresh. Breakfast includes great champagne.

Fans of walks like the health path - a path along the sea that goes through trees and pines. You can climb up a little along it, from where it will be possible to consider local beauties much better than from a normal position. Many advise to go further - you can go to a quiet, remote beach. And then - to the monastery. Where, by the way, hotel guests organize a free tour.

The service is up to par. As it should be in a 4-star hotel. The staff is polite, helpful. If something is needed, they react immediately, which cannot but rejoice.

In general, if you need a good, carefree vacation in a beautiful place, you can go here. The prices are rather high, but the vacation at this hotel is worth it.
