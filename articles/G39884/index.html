Hotel complex "Raida", Abkhazia: description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

An incredible hospitable and beautiful region with an original culture of Abkhazia is becoming more and more popular every year. Having been here once, I want to come here constantly. This is not surprising, because in Abkhazia the unique virgin nature of the subtropics, where against the backdrop of powerful green mountains with white snowy peaks and crystal clear water in mountain streams and turquoise lakes, there are pensions, hotels and rest houses. The hotel "Raida 3 *" is no exception.



Abkhazia annually receives thousands of tourists from all over the world, but most of them are our compatriots who like not only the relatively low prices and proximity to the city of Sochi. This is another opportunity to visit several resorts at once.

Where to stay?

And since analysts this year predict a large influx of tourists on the Black Sea coast, tourists should decide on a place to stay in advance. A good option would be the Raida Hotel. Abkhazia, prices at which are reasonably acceptable for ordinary Russians, thirty years ago was not so affordable. Not everyone could afford to buy a ticket. Today in the season, the cost of living per person, depending on the category of room, starts from three and a half thousand rubles per person. The amount also includes meals. The most expensive apartments in August will cost about nine thousand for each.

However, today the region, although it has lost its former glory, as before, attracts vacationers who buy tours to hotels or come on their own. Many combine swimming at sea with sightseeing of local attractions, of which there are many. You can stay both in the private sector, in guest houses, and in numerous hotels and pensions, for example, in the Raida Hotel. Abkhazia is a place where everyone will find rest to their taste and affluence. In addition, you can come here all year round.



Hotel "Raida"

Abkhazia is a place where many people come not only to relax, but also to improve their health. The local air contains a large amount of iodine, and sea salt helps restore the body after an illness. Especially many visitors are in Gagra. It is here that the hotel complex "Raida" is located. Abkhazia, which has a visa-free regime with Russia, tourists like fresh fruits, wonderful weather and affordable vacations. That is why hotels and pensions here are crowded.

Hotel Raida (Abkhazia) is located in Old Gagra, just a few steps from the beautiful beach. Nearby is the City Embankment, on which numerous entertainment centers are concentrated. There are many venues for discos and bars, there are cafes and restaurants, the most popular of which Gagripsh is already loved by those who came here. Within walking distance are Seaside Park and the Colonnade.



Infrastructure

Guests can come to the Raida boarding house year-round. Abkhazia is usually not crowded in winter . Here, even in January, the temperature does not drop below zero. Therefore, those who liked Raida, Abkhazia and Gagra can come here in the winter months, spending significantly less on vacation than in the summer.

The hotel area is closed to strangers. It is under video surveillance. Security service is open around the clock. Judging by the reviews, Raida (Abkhazia) has a fairly developed infrastructure, despite three stars. The hotel has its own secure parking. There is a shop on site, there is a laundry service. The reception is open around the clock. Regardless of the time of arrival, if there are free rooms, check-in is carried out immediately. There is also a tour desk.

Housing Description

Hotel "Raida" (Abkhazia) has two buildings: four and five-story. The latter is equipped with a ramp, which is very convenient for guests with reduced mobility. There is also an elevator.

In the first building are standard and superior double rooms, including attic rooms. Some overlook the sea and have balconies. Superior rooms have two single or one double bed, there are upholstered furniture, a TV and a refrigerator. The bathrooms have showers, daily updated hygiene items. Superior rooms include bathrobes, a hairdryer and slippers.

The second building is five-story. It consists mainly of standard rooms with windows overlooking the mountains and the beach, as well as several apartments with sea views. The entire housing stock is air-conditioned. The rooms have comfortable furniture, the bathrooms have high-quality plumbing. At the request of guests, an extra bed is possible - a folding bed.



Nutrition

Many, judging by the reviews, are satisfied with the rest spent at the Raida Hotel. Abkhazia is famous not only for its wonderful climate and magnificent sea, but also for its delicious cuisine. Between May and October, three meals a day according to the concept of an integrated meal are included in the price. Guests can also use the services of the restaurant, as well as a summer cafe-bar. It offers not only a variety of dishes of Abkhaz cuisine, but also Russian and European. The menu has a lot of meat dishes, seafood lovers will like sea and river fish cooked according to various recipes, as well as an excellent wine list. From October to May, only the full breakfast is included.

Beach

The sea from the hotel is only fifty meters distant. City beach, cover - pebble-sand. Sun beds, mattresses and umbrellas are available at an additional cost. A beach with a ground crossing separates the beach from the hotel. There is also a small bar on the shore. Drinks, ice creams and snacks need to be paid. For a comfortable bathing, tourists are better off carrying rubber slippers.





Sports and Entertainment

On the territory of the hotel there is a bath complex. Guests have access to a sauna and spa. Those wishing to engage in outdoor activities will enjoy horseback riding in the surrounding area. For an extra fee, you can get a massage course or visit the beauty parlor. In the evenings, vacationers can stroll through the botanical garden, breathing the amazing air of the arboretum, or along the promenade, where most of the tourists gather.

For children

Many come to the Raida Hotel as families. There is a playground for children on site. If desired, in the dining room you can order a special menu for the kids and get a highchair. Entrance to the sea is convenient for children. Parents can not worry about the safety of the kids, as lifeguards are constantly present on the beach. The hotel has a games room.

Additional Information

Guests can make use of the tour desk to explore the local attractions. Here you can also pay for the accompaniment of a guide. Most often, tourists go to Ritsa, to New Athos, ride horses on the Bzyb gorge. Throughout the hotel you can connect to the Wi-Fi network for free. When checking in, you should have a travel voucher from the company with you, as well as a passport.

For a child under fourteen years of age, a birth certificate is required. For children older than the specified age, a passport is required. In addition, a resort fee and accident insurance in the amount of two hundred and fifty rubles per person should be paid directly on the spot.



Impressions about staying at the Raida Hotel

Abkhazia, reviews about the rest in which is mostly positive, is very hospitable. Not only Russians come here, but for some time now Europeans. Judging by the reviews, many of the tourists liked the vacation spent at the Raida Hotel. Vacationers say that this is a wonderful hotel that you want to return to. And this is not surprising. Many people think that against the backdrop of a wide variety of local hotels, pensions and guest houses, the Raida Hotel fits into a four-star category.

The rooms are quite comfortable, with a good layout. Residents are not feeling crowded. The furniture is modern, comfortable, plumbing works fine. In the rooms you can easily connect to the Internet. The food, judging by the reviews, is very tasty and plentiful. For breakfast, fruit salad, pancakes and omelettes are always prepared, and a variety of soups are waiting for you for lunch and dinner. The menu is always a lot of meat and plenty of salads. Lots of desserts.

Of the minuses, tourists note the lack of dryers, since the climate in Abkhazia is humid, swimwear and towels dry for a very long time.

A lot of good feedback about the work of the staff. The rooms are always clean, the bathroom is polished to a shine. The hotel has two buildings, but judging by the reviews, it is better to stay in a new one, with an elevator, where fresh repairs are made and all furniture is updated.
