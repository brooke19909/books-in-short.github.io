Tunisia: Orient Palace is a beautiful hotel in Sousse

Thalassotherapy and oriental bliss - isn’t this why Russians go to Tunisia? “Orient Palace” as a palace-type hotel was liked by many of our compatriots. It is a couple of kilometers from the city of Sousse, and the nearest airport is Monastir. The hotel is extremely prestigious - it was built specifically for one of the Saudi emirs, who annually comes here with the whole family for a couple of days. None of the vacationers suffer from this at all, but the reputation of the hotel is very rising. The hotel itself is, of course, a five-star. But this is one of the cheapest hotels of this category, which can only be found in a country like Tunisia.



Orient Palace is well renovated. More than three hundred rooms and suites have a very large area. There is a free electronic safe and Wi-Fi. The rooms are located in a large enclosure surrounding a beautiful courtyard. From the windows you can see either the sea surface or a fabulous garden. Five bars and six restaurants delight guests with a variety of food with unprecedented oriental delights. The cuisine is very good, especially guests like lamb, quail, fruits, desserts, beer and wine - including local ones. One ice cream as many as six varieties. In addition, there are also theme cafes - with live music, in the Saracen-Moorish style, and so on. Pools are both in the air and indoors. In the latter, the water is heated in cold weather, because winter also comes to Tunisia.



Orient Palace traditionally pleases its guests with excellent service, and its level has not suffered at all after the revolutionary events and the change of power. It is aimed at family people who crave relaxation, tranquility and wellness. Perhaps the youth will be a little boring on its territory, but it is not far from Sousse with its casinos and night clubs. But this is not only why tourists come to Tunisia. Orient Palace boasts a sandy beach. True, its strip is not very wide, and therefore hotel beds are on the green grass a little further away. Tourists like both sand on the beach and relaxing on the lawn. The sea is clean and transparent, if there is no storm.



Among vacationers are people of different nations and continents. Many Germans, which speaks not only about cheapness, but also about the quality of service. The staff makes no difference between the guests, everyone greets with cute smiles. The large and green area of ​​the hotel makes it possible to make whole walks along the Orient Palace 5. Tunisia is a poor country, but it can still provide vacationers with a lot of entertainment. And they begin already near the hotel. For example, right outside its territory, in a nearby hotel, there is an excellent water park, where you can have fun both for children and adults. By the way, many people think that this is the best institution of this kind, and almost all of Tunisia spread its fame about it.

Orient Palace Hotel, whose reviews are mostly positive, is a kind of green oasis between two Arab cities. This is a great opportunity to spend a vacation with your family. It is calm, there is no hacking, noisy streets with obsessive merchants. Animation is one of the best, and tours are extremely interesting. Who doesn’t want to see the famous Sahara desert covered with legends or the ruins of the ancient, mysterious Carthage? Ten days or two weeks will fly by quickly and imperceptibly, but, despite the eastern leisurelyness of such a vacation, there will be something to remember at home!
