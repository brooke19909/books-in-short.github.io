DIY kitty toys: give joy to your furry little ball

Cats are big mischief-makers, therefore they cannot do without toys in any way. There are a lot of special accessories in pet stores, but you can make toys for kittens yourself. With your own hands you can make exactly what your pet will like, because you know him like no other. We will offer you some interesting ideas.

DIY kitten toys

Fluffy Girlfriend Fishing Rod

To make it you will need:

  • wand;
  • elastic band (30 centimeters);
  • weight;
  • the most interesting are feathers, a piece of fur or a soft little mouse.


Manufacturing process

Tie a gum to a stick, and a weight to it. The final stage - we attach feathers to the product. Such a fishing rod will surely appeal to your darling.

DIY cat mouse

To make it you will need:

  • fabric of different colors (gray, black, pink);
  • threads
  • needle for sewing;
  • pattern pattern (we will describe the manufacturing process);
  • cotton wool or rags (for stuffing);
  • rope (length of your choice).

Process of creation

  1. First you need to make a pattern, for this draw a mouse (only a silhouette, without eyes, ears). Your template is ready. Now you need to cut two identical parts from the fabric. We cut out another such part for the abdomen.
  2. Cut out small ears of pink fabric. From dark material - nose and eyes.
  3. Next, connect the two body parts, and then sew with a typewriter or manually sew them. Then sew the part for the abdomen, but not completely so that you can fill the toy with cotton.
  4. Cut off part of the rope - this will be the tail. Sew it, ears and eyes. That's all, the rodent for your pet is ready.
