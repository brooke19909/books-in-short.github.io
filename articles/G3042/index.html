What are soap made from scratch and how to make it at home?





Handmade soap is very popular these days. Many people ask themselves: what do those who are interested in hand-made make from soap from scratch ? Especially for all lovers of all natural, we will try to briefly talk about the most correct way to make handmade soap directly in your apartment - about making soap from scratch.

What, besides what do soaps need to be learned before making a product? This method is the most laborious, therefore we recommend it only to those who believe in themselves and already have experience in making soap. The process of making soap from scratch takes about 6 weeks, but trust the experienced soap makers, it's worth it.





What do you need to take?

To the question: "What are soap made from scratch?" we will answer that you will need:

- caustic soda;

- odorless base oil ;

- essential oils and flavorings;

- all kinds of decorations and scrubbing particles (coffee, oatmeal, zest, flowers, loofah, etc.);

- tools: measuring spoons, thermometer, 2 stainless steel pans, protective gloves and glasses, a scarf and an apron, a blender, a grater, a stirring stick and molds.

Note that we do not indicate the proportions by chance, they are different for different ingredients, and you can calculate them using a special calculator, which is additionally found on the Internet.

How is the cooking process going?

After the question "What is soap made of?" usually the question is how to do it. We will answer it, having painted step-by-step instructions for the manufacture of this product. So, you decided: "Today we make soap ourselves, and not buy in the store." Therefore:

  1. We make an oil base - melt solid oils in a water bath (if you took liquid base oils, skip this point).
  2. We make an alkaline solution. Before proceeding to this point, protect yourself with gloves, an apron and clothing that completely covers all parts of the body. After that, measure the required amount of water, which you calculated on the calculator. Then pour cool water into a glass jar and gently fill in alkali. And remember: this operation must be performed in that order, otherwise an explosion could occur!
  3. Now mix the alkali and oil solution. Remember: for the manufacture of soap, the temperature of the oil and solution must be the same. Pour the alkali into the oil and beat the mixture with a mixer or blender until sour cream.
  4. After that, you can add essential oils or fragrances, as well as other components to the mixture, and beat your future soap again.
  5. Now the soap mixture is ready. Lay it out in tins, wrap with a towel and leave for several days. After the time has passed, remove the towel and leave the soap for a month or two - over time, the acidity will become normal, and the pH will become neutral. Then the soap can be cut into pieces and start using it!

Conclusion

We hope that we helped you figure out what handmade soap is, how to make soap from scratch and what ingredients and tools will come in handy in this difficult task.
