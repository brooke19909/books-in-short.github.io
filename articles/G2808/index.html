Centara Karon Resort Phuket 4 *, Karon Beach, Thailand: hotel description, reviews of tourists

Relaxing at the Centara Karon Resort Phuket 4 * hotel, you will get the opportunity to enjoy the emerald greenery of the surrounding mountain slopes, as well as the warm and gentle waves of the purest Andaman Sea.





Hotel Description

Centara Karon Resort Phuket 4 * hotel is decorated in accordance with Thai design traditions - in a simple ecological style. Everything contributes to finding inner harmony in the bosom of nature.

Great attention is paid to wellness programs. So, you can get acquainted with various massage techniques, as well as treat yourself to spa treatments. A mass of sporting events awaits lovers of outdoor activities both on site and outside.

The main restaurant of the Centara Karon Resort Phuket 4 * hotel offers guests to enjoy fine light breakfasts prepared in the best Thai traditions and served in a buffet format. Other establishments working on the territory of the hotel complex can please gourmets.





Hotel Location

Centara Karon Resort Phuket 4 * hotel is located 45 km from the airport. The hotel is located on the territory of the world famous Karon Beach, just 300 meters from the sea. Within walking distance there are many shops, restaurants and other resort infrastructure. If you want to visit the city of Phuket, then you have to overcome only 15 km.





Guest rooms

In total, the Centara Karon Resort 4 * hotel (Thailand, Phuket, Karon) has 335 guest rooms. Depending on the size, as well as on the degree of equipment of various kinds of amenities, the apartments are divided into several categories:

  • Deluxe Cabanas are spacious rooms, consisting of a cozy bedroom and a spacious bright living room. There is a private balcony with comfortable rattan furniture.
  • Deluxe Honeymoon Spa Suites - apartments designed specifically for a romantic getaway. They represent a spacious room, which is conditionally divided into zones of the bedroom and living room. The en suite bathroom has a modern jacuzzi.
  • Private Plunge Pool Cabanas - the main feature of this category of rooms is that they all have a spacious and comfortable terrace with access to their own pool.
  • The Lagoon Deluxe - chic two-room rooms located in a cozy green area near a clean sandy beach.
  • The Terraces Superior - this category of rooms is a spacious bedroom with a large bed. The peculiarity of the apartments is a huge furnished terrace, which is a logical continuation of the room.
  • The Terraces Superior Sea View - these rooms are similar in plan and equipment to the previous category. The difference is that the terrace offers a gorgeous view of the sea.
  • The Tropicale Pre.Deluxe are the best rooms in this hotel. It has a spacious bedroom, a cozy bright living room, as well as a full-fledged small kitchen and a terrace.




Room Amenities

Phuket can be considered a paradise for lovers of the tropics and environmentally friendly recreation. Centara Karon Resort Phuket 4 * offers to take advantage of all these opportunities, while living in cozy comfortable rooms with all necessary amenities:

  • in each of the apartments, depending on the number of storeys, there is a balcony or a spacious terrace;
  • air temperature adjustment is carried out using an individual air conditioner;
  • the TV is connected to a powerful satellite dish;
  • the rooms have telephones with which you can make international calls upon prior request;
  • there is a minibar, which can also serve as a refrigerator;
  • tea and coffee set;
  • bath, shower or jacuzzi, depending on the category of apartments.

Hotel infrastructure

Resort infrastructure plays an important role in providing rich leisure and recreation for tourists. The Centara Karon Resort Phuket 4 * hotel has ample opportunities to make the stay of its guests in Thailand as comfortable and interesting as possible. The following infrastructure facilities are available to tourists:

  • three large freshwater swimming pools in the fresh air, each of which has a special children's compartment with a shallow depth;
  • restaurants and bars where you can fully eat, as well as get acquainted with the specific features of national Thai cuisine;
  • the spa center offers its guests a huge selection of services, thanks to which you can improve your health and improve your appearance;
  • several souvenir shops where you can buy products with the symbols of the hotel or traditional products of Thai craftsmen;
  • in a beauty salon you can put your hair and nails in order, as well as prepare for any celebration;
  • the clinic on the territory of the hotel offers modern methods of treatment and diagnosis of the whole organism;
  • there is a safe at the reception;
  • the hotel has several conference rooms, which, if necessary, can be used for banquets or converted or decorated for special occasions and ceremonies;
  • throughout the hotel complex operates wireless Internet;
  • there is a children's mini-club where daily animators prepare a rich entertainment program for the youngest tourists;
  • hotel guests can use the city beach, which is a three-minute walk from the main building (sunbeds and umbrellas are available at an additional cost, but beach towels are included in the tour price);
  • if you want to use the World Wide Web using a desktop computer, then this service is available in the Internet cafe.




Hotel service

The Centara Karon Resort Phuket 4 * hotel (Thailand, Phuket) makes sure that the guests stay at this resort complex is not only comfortable, but also eventful. For this purpose, tourists are offered a number of opportunities, the cost of which is fully included in the price of a tourist permit:

  • the gym, equipped with the latest sports equipment, invites its guests to go in for sports and bring their body into shape, as well as get advice from a trainer;
  • Two large tennis courts are available for tourists, as well as all the necessary equipment for the game;
  • going to the beach, you can take towels at the reception absolutely free.




A number of services provided by the Centara Karon Resort Phuket 4 * hotel (Phuket, Karon) require additional payment. These services include the following:

  • services of specialists working in the spa;
  • visit to the billiard club;
  • Possibility to use babysitting services by prior arrangement.




Positive reviews

The picturesque landscapes, as well as the emerald shade of tropical greenery can not leave anyone indifferent, so a lot of vivid impressions are caused by the rest at the Centara Karon Resort Phuket 4 * hotel. Reviews about this hotel complex contain many thanks and positive comments:

  • At the reception there is a Russian-speaking employee, which greatly simplifies the registration procedure, as well as resolving issues that arise during your stay at the hotel;
  • the hotel has a lot of greenery;
  • three pools have different depths, so that every vacationer can choose a comfortable environment;
  • good children's infrastructure: swimming pools, water slides, playgrounds, animation program;
  • cleaning is carried out daily - everything is very clean, no complaints;
  • Despite the fact that the beach is public, it is very well cleaned;
  • the hotel is very conveniently located: the beach is nearby, there are many shops and cafes, as well as a large local market.

Negative reviews

No matter how strong the desire to organize an ideal holiday for tourists, problems in hotels will certainly arise. The Centara Karon Resort Phuket 4 * hotel, reviews of which contain some negative comments and comments, is no exception:

  • the customer settlement system is illiterate, therefore, if you lose your check, it is likely that you will have to pay for the services again;
  • meat and fish are not included in the diet (the maximum that can be found on the table is bacon and sausages);
  • food prices in the hotel restaurants are very high, so it is cheaper to eat in establishments outside the hotel complex;
  • the furniture and sanitary ware in the rooms are very worn out; major (or at least cosmetic) repairs are required.
