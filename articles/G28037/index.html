Hotel Odeon, Adler: overview, description, rooms and reviews of tourists

In a cozy picturesque corner is the resort hotel "Odeon". Adler will delight you with the warm sun, clear sea and beautiful nature, and this institution will provide you with everything that a modern tourist may need for a comfortable stay.

a brief description of

The doors of such a resort as the Odeon Hotel are open all year round for travelers. Adler is one of the most popular destinations for domestic tourism. In 4- and 5-storey buildings, 56 comfortable apartments of various categories are equipped. In 2014, the restoration of the number of rooms was carried out.

Location

The convenient location boasts the Odeon Hotel. St. Honey, 50, Adler - the address of the resort. It's about a quarter hour walk from the main promenade and public beaches.





You can visit many entertainment venues, as well as cafes and restaurants. Travelers should pay attention to such tourist sites:

  • Sochi Aquarium (1 km);
  • dolphinarium (1 km);
  • water park "Amphibius" (1 km);
  • Caucasian Biosphere Reserve (7 km);
  • summer and winter theaters (17 km);
  • Green Theater (19 km);
  • Riviera Park (20 km).

Description of rooms

Hotel Odeon (Adler) provides its guests with the most comfortable living conditions. You can stay in rooms of the following categories:

  • Hostel is the most economical accommodation option. This quadruple room has 2 single beds and one bunk. Men and women are accommodated separately.




  • Apartments in their size and functionality are not inferior to a full-fledged apartment. A large studio combines a bedroom, a living room and a kitchen.
  • Standard - a double room with a pair of one and a half beds and a desk. It has a private furnished balcony.
  • Family rooms consist of two small bedrooms (for children and parents), as well as a spacious living room with a seating area. The bathroom is equipped with a modern shower.
  • Premium for 3 guests. Two of them can be accommodated in a spacious bedroom, and the third - on a folding soft sofa in the living room.
  • Art Nouveau has a large area and luxurious interior. The living room has leather upholstered furniture, and the bedroom is decorated in calm light colors with silver elements. A bathroom gives you the opportunity to take a shower or bath. Also has its own cozy balcony.
  • The oriental room got its name because of the characteristic interior with arches and colorful patterns. The bedroom has a king size bed, and the living room has folding sofa. There is a balcony.

Room Amenities

You will feel as comfortable as possible in the rooms that the Odeon Hotel (Adler) offers. Reviews indicate the availability of all necessary amenities in the apartment. You can use the following options (except for the hostel):

  • air conditioning system with the ability to cool or warm the air temperature;
  • hair dryer for drying and styling hair;
  • TV with dozens of satellite channels;
  • refrigerator for storing food stocks;
  • safe with combination lock.

What the hotel offers

All that is needed for a full and bright holiday will be provided by the Odeon Hotel. Adler will please you with hospitality, which is expressed in the following:

  • ironing rooms in each of the buildings;
  • delicious and hearty breakfasts in the hotel restaurant (except for hostel rooms);
  • free shuttle when booking a room for more than two days (with the exception of hostel rooms);
  • leisure facilities such as billiards, a sauna and barbecue facilities in the fresh air;
  • washing personal items in the hotel laundry;
  • access system for electronic cards, which excludes outsiders from visiting the hotel;
  • organization of sightseeing tours;
  • free use of wireless internet; taxi ordering;
  • number for disabled people;
  • car, motorcycle and bicycle rental;
  • Accommodation with pets, subject to prior agreement with the administration.




Additional Information

A number of rules should be studied before traveling to the Odeon Hotel (Adler). Reviews of tourists pay attention to the following useful information:

  • check-in of new guests to the hotel starts after 13:00, and check-out is done before noon;
  • upon cancellation of the reservation less than 2 weeks prior to the expected date of check-in, a fine of the daily cost of living will be charged;
  • if the cancellation of the promotional tour is canceled, the full amount of the prepayment is charged as a fine;
  • in addition to cash, plastic cards or electronic money can be used to pay for accommodation and other services;
  • in order to arrange a free transfer, the guest must inform about his arrival no later than two days before the expected date;
  • at the time of registration, a cash deposit (in cash or non-cash) is charged;
  • Mandatory condition for registration is the provision of an identity card with a photograph;
  • pets are allowed, the weight of which does not exceed 5 kg;
  • Children under 5 years old stay free in the room, subject to existing beds;
  • the cost of an extra bed for children under 12 years is 500 rubles;
  • additional accommodation for an adult guest will cost 1000 rubles for each day of stay;
  • Each room can accommodate up to 2 extra beds.




Positive reviews

Only vivid impressions await travelers from a sea holiday. It is worth noting that the reviews about the Odeon Hotel (Adler) are generally positive in color. It is worth highlighting the following advantages of this institution:

  • the optimal combination of cost of living and quality of services provided;
  • free transfer for long stays;
  • the hotel is located in a quiet place, where there is no noise from busy roads and entertainment venues;
  • Friendly and diligent maids conscientiously perform their cleaning duties;
  • good soundproofing in the rooms (sounds from neighboring rooms and the corridor will not interfere with you);
  • a satellite dish catches many channels;
  • pool water is perfectly clean;
  • happy with the presence of its own parking;
  • the hotel has a quiet family atmosphere;
  • there is an underpass on the way to the beach, which eliminates the need to cross a busy highway;
  • every day stocks of a cosmetic set are updated;
  • there is a good playground on the territory;
  • in the restaurant you can order comprehensive meals at an affordable price;




  • a couple of minutes walk from the hotel there is a bus stop with which it is convenient to get to the center of Adler and Sochi;
  • from the windows of the rooms offer beautiful views of the sea and mountains;
  • near the pool there are many sunbeds;
  • an electric kettle can be provided in the room upon request;
  • the hotel has a launderette;
  • There are a couple of small shops near the hotel.




Negative reviews about the Odeon Hotel (Adler)

It is worth noting that this institution is not without flaws. Reviews of the Odeon 3 * hotel (Adler) focus on the following negative points:

  • there is no heating in the pool;
  • monotonous breakfasts are repeated every day (three types of porridge and omelette);
  • too modest menu in the cafe (it is better to visit neighboring institutions - there is more choice and prices are cheaper);
  • during bathing, all water from the shower flows to the floor;
  • the bathroom is equipped with an outdated ventilation system, and therefore there is a constant smell of sewage;
  • the hotel is located on a hill, and therefore it will be difficult for elderly and sick people to get to it;
  • the road leading to the hotel is completely unlit;
  • Reception staff almost impossible to catch;
  • in the dining room you can often see cockroaches;
  • tea and coffee are too expensive and poor quality;
  • slow service in the restaurant;
  • towels are replaced no more than once a week (even if you put them on the floor, the maids will not replace them);
  • the hotel does not have a bar;
  • no security;
  • washed and holey bedding;
  • there is a lot of mold in the bathroom (both on the walls and in the shower);
  • the air conditioner filter is not cleaned, which is why you have to breathe dusty air;
  • the size of the bedding does not match the size of the bed (in addition, pillowcases, sheets and duvet covers from different sets);
  • the hotel does not give out beach towels;
  • Considering that many guests smoke in the bathrooms, the smell of tobacco smoke will enter your bathroom through the ventilation;
  • unstable wireless internet;
  • the nearby beaches are very crowded and dirty.

General impression

This hotel is a good example of the optimal ratio of cost and quality of services provided. Paying this or that amount for a room, you will receive an appropriate set of amenities, as well as a cozy interior. Also on the territory you will be provided with access to all necessary services. The service is average. Despite the fact that employees are not highly professional, the service is quite pleasant.

The hotel is far from noisy tracks and entertainment venues. That is why the atmosphere of this institution is cozy and quiet. The negative point can be considered the distance from clean comfortable beaches, as well as the fact that the hotel is located on a hill. This is extremely inconvenient for the elderly, as well as vacationing with small children.
