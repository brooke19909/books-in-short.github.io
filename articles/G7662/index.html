"Athena" is a camp site in the Volgograd region: rest in a nature reserve with urban comfort!

Without exception, residents of big cities like to relax in nature. Not every family can afford to maintain their own country house. Do not worry, the lack of a summer residence is not a reason to refuse a trip out of town. "Athena" is a camp site located in an ecologically clean area in the Volgograd region, which everyone can afford to visit!

Natural beauty or urban comfort? You no longer need to choose!



The natural park of the Volga-Akhtuba floodplain is a conservation area in the Volgograd region. This area is characterized by picturesque nature and favorable microclimate. It is here, on the banks of the Akhtuba River, in the pine forest that the Athena recreation and sports center is located. The hostel is a complex of modern residential houses and cottages of various capacities, as well as landscaped areas for recreation. This recreation area compares favorably with similar country hotels. The hostel pleases guests with minimalism combined with a high level of comfort. Accommodation and leisure activities



On the territory of the recreation center, guests will find cozy houses and cottages with a capacity of 2 to 14 people. All residential buildings were built recently, two-story houses have balconies. Inside, clean and spacious bedrooms, kitchens, large cottages have living rooms, ideal for friendly gatherings. Each house has everything necessary for life: from modern household appliances to small utensils. "Athena" - a camp site designed for outdoor activities. The landscaped area has tennis and volleyball courts, as well as an outdoor sports area. Guests are invited to go fishing, in the warmer months you can sunbathe on the beach and swim in the river. Some vacationers prefer to come to the camp without overnight. Especially for such guests there are awnings with barbecue and barbecue facilities. On the territory of the recreation and sports center, you can celebrate any holiday, conduct field registration of marriage. Among the additional entertainment for vacationers are sports equipment rental, billiards, table tennis, sauna, pool. The local bar hosts karaoke evenings and discos.

Cost of rest

How much will it cost to rest in a pine forest on the banks of the Akhtuba River? The smallest house, which can comfortably accommodate 2 people, costs 3000 rubles per day. The rental price of the largest cottage, designed for a one-time settlement of 12 people, is 15,000 rubles per day. Also at the camp site there are houses with a capacity of 4, 6 and 8 people. Come with family and friends, here everyone will find the most suitable accommodation option in terms of area and cost. Separately paid meals in a cafe, barbecue firewood, rental of sports equipment and some other additional services. For the convenience of guests there is a service for pre-booking of residential houses and cottages.

How to get to Athens (camp site, Volgograd)



How to get to these places? The recreation center is located in the Sredneahtubinsky district of the Volgograd region. It is not at all difficult to get here by public or private transport. You can get from Volgograd Central Station by bus No. 146 or minibus No. 264. You need to exit at the "Bridge" stop, then you will need to walk a little. It is more convenient to drive a personal car at coordinates 48.716153; 44.837689. The recreation center has its own guarded parking lot, where every guest can leave their car for free.
