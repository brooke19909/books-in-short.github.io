Hand scanner for documents and texts: photos and reviews

The scanning function is traditionally associated with large stationary appliances or tabletop appliances. But even in the period of the mass production of scanning technology, a class of portable but functional devices of this type took shape. The basis of this segment is a manual scanner for documents, a photo of which is presented below.





What is special about text scanners?

Portable scanners are high-tech devices in which the entire filling is subordinate to specific goals. To optimize the main task, manufacturers strive to approach the functionality in a balanced way. This determines the strict separation of devices in the direction of use. In this case, the device focuses on text recognition. The integrated sensor system also converts it into a text file or graphic image. A key feature that highlights this type of model is OCR technology, which allows you to visually recognize characters. A modern hand-held scanner for documents is able to automatically enter readable information in the input field of a connected device. The receiver of this information is a computer, which can also automatically open received files with special programs. Such models are common in institutions where employees work with financial documents, books and magazines, personal data, etc.

How to choose a manual scanner for documents?

When choosing, you should take into account such characteristics of the scanner as read speed, storage formats, resolution, memory size, etc. For example, the optimal speed for a representative of the middle class is no more than 1 second. for an A4 page. The resolution will be 300 dpi. This is a small indicator compared to the capabilities of stationary scanners, but it is enough for the utilitarian function of converting text to digital form. The amount of memory does not matter much, since most models are equipped with flash cards with a capacity of up to 32 GB. Nevertheless, just in case, it is useful to have a sufficient supply of internal memory. Usually it is about 128 MB, but there are more solid volumes. You should also consider what communication capabilities, in addition to the standard USB interface, a scanner (hand-held portable) should have. For documents requiring the fastest transfer to another device, USB is not the best option. The most technological solution is the Wi-Fi module. It provides wireless data transmission from the scanner to the same computer or another receiver equipped with a similar module.
