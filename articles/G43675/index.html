How to activate

The conditions for using a Gazprombank credit card begin with putting the plastic into working condition. When receiving plastic at the office, activation through an ATM is useful:

  1. Go to the ATM and open the envelope with the PIN code.
  2. Insert plastic into the seat.
  3. Enter the password from the envelope.
  4. Select an option from the menu, for example, display the balance on the screen.
  5. Replace the original password with your own four-digit design, understood only by the owner. You should not choose the year of birth and other prominent events.

Activation can also be carried out by an employee of KFU. But at the same time, the client must enter the password on its own. It is unacceptable to give the PIN code to strangers, no matter how nice, polite and helpful they may be.

How to find out the balance

Information about the balance of borrowed funds is available through the ATM options. If the device is inoperative, then at the office of the KFU office, if there is an identity card, the cashier will report the available balance.

Citizens with smartphones, tablets and laptops have access to an online personal account, where the holder is disclosed information about the financial parameters of the loan.

Cash withdrawal

Chip carriers of financial information for Gazprombank customers are served at GPB ATMs and partner banks.

To withdraw cash, you must insert the card into the ATM and enter the PIN code. A combination of four digits should be entered carefully: after three erroneous attempts, a lock will occur.

If the password is entered correctly, the ATM first returns the medium, then gives out banknotes. The entire operation takes 45 seconds. The user is not recommended to be distracted, as exceeding the time interval will lead to the capture of the card.

PIN code handling requires the following rules:

  1. Do not label the code on plastic and do not keep nearby, for example, in a purse.
  2. Do not advertise the password at the time of entry: cover the keyboard with your hand.
  3. Do not share the password with anyone.

Fear of withdrawing the entire received income should not be. Mandatory payment is equal to 5% of the debt plus interest for the period of use of other people's funds.

Interest is accrued daily for each calendar day. The due date is set after a month of using borrowed funds. If the appointed time of payment falls on a weekend, the obligation is carried forward to the next day.

For example, the payment deadline is the 28th day of the month. But in January it is Sunday, which means that the obligation to repay the debt in the prescribed amount will arise on January 29.

The next billing period will begin on January 30th.

The Bank dictates the condition for the minimum deposit amount - three hundred rubles of debt plus interest.
