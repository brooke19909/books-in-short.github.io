Popular Hotel Tirana Waterpark (Sharm El Sheikh)

Hotel “Tirana Waterpark Resort” until recently had the word “Sunrise” in its name. Therefore, sometimes it is searched on the net under that name. This hotel itself is not located on the seashore, however, this minus more than pays for the presence right on the territory of a beautiful water park (with which, in fact, its name is associated) and many excellent pools. Families with children who visit Tirana Aquapark Hotel most often recommend it to others and come here more than once. The hotel is not very far from the airport (only five kilometers), so it’s easy to get here after the flight, and moving will not take much time.



"Tirana Waterpark" (Sharm-El-Sheikh). Rooms and service

This hotel has five hundred and ten rooms. 310 of them are standard rooms, typical for a good resort hotel: large, comfortable, modern, with balconies, a safe and a minibar (every day a bottle of water is put there for everyone living). There are also family rooms of two categories and eight suites. All of them are air-conditioned, everywhere there is the opportunity to watch TV with Russian-language channels. The staff cleans every day and speaks fluent in many dialects. So you will always be understood. There is an Internet cafe in the lobby (you need to buy special cards to use the network). There is an opportunity to park a car, rent a car, wash clothes. The Planet SPA fitness club offers the services of massage therapists, a jacuzzi, a hammam and a sauna.



"Tirana Waterpark" (Sharm-El-Sheikh). Nutrition

In the hotel, the all-inclusive system operates in the main Shahrazad restaurant. And the rest of the institutions specializing in Lebanese, English, Italian, Chinese and Indian cuisines are served on the basis of an “a la carte” principle. But those who want to try dishes of different nations will not have to envy those who eat on the menu. In the main restaurant, too, every day is dedicated to some national cuisine, including Russian. The bar at the reception is open 24 hours a day. A lot of different food points near the pools and on the beach.

"Tirana Waterpark" (Sharm-El-Sheikh). Sea and water activities



The hotel has a beach; a free shuttle runs there every quarter hour. However, the entrance to the sea is not from the coast, but along the pontoon. If you are going to swim, it is better to have special slippers so as not to cut yourself on corals. Divers have a place to turn around - great reefs, there are special centers for training beginners and serving specialists. There are many places on the beach, and there are special wicker screens covering from the wind. In addition, a whole water park is at the disposal of visitors: eight pools (including a children's pool), artificial waves, various slides ...

"Tirana Waterpark" (Sharm-El-Sheikh). Tourists rating

Many hotel guests consider it a great place to relax. A special praise was given to the positively tuned, polite staff. Many were pleasantly surprised that at the reception they did not ask for tips for settling in the best rooms. Everything works perfectly in the rooms, and if something breaks, then the reaction of the managers is instant. Animators work great too. Their show programs amused everyone from young to old. Tourists praise the slides, noting not only the fact that you can ride them from morning to evening, but also the safety of entertainment.
