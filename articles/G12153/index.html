Queen Hotel Pattaya 3 *: hotel reviews

Resort Queen Hotel Pattaya 3 * offers a wonderful option for a vacation in Thailand at very affordable prices.





Brief information about the hotel

Resort Queen Hotel Pattaya 3 * pays very close attention to ensure that guests feel comfortable in the rooms of this institution. For this purpose, the apartments were decorated in a simple but quite elegant style. It is also impossible not to say that the rooms are equipped with the necessary set of amenities that will provide coziness and comfort to the guests.

Hotel Queen Pattaya Hotel 3 * boasts a fairly rich infrastructure for its low status. Here, guests are provided with a swimming pool and a cozy green garden, in the shade of which you can hide from the heat. You will also be invited to go on a tour or visit a restaurant serving national Thai cuisine.





Resort Location

Resort Queen Pattaya Hotel 3 * (Thailand, Pattaya) is located in the heart of the city, where all the tourist infrastructure is concentrated. So, having done a five-minute journey, you will find yourself on the main public beach. Just a couple of steps from the hotel there is a large shopping center where you can find absolutely any goods at very reasonable prices. The hotel is just a 2-hour drive from Suvarnabhumi International Airport.

Hotel guest rooms

Guests of the Queen Pattaya Hotel 3 * (Pattaya) can stay in one of the 100 available apartments. We can say that they practically do not differ from each other and can be assigned to a double standard category. It is worth noting that the difference may consist in the view from the window, as well as in the fact that some rooms have recently been renovated. Due to this, the prices for apartments may vary slightly.





Facilities

Each of the rooms of the Queen Hotel Pattaya 3 * Resort Hotel provides the following amenities:

  • a bathroom, in which the toilet is combined with a shower, bath accessories and a small cosmetic set with soap and shampoo are also provided here;
  • An individual air conditioner is responsible for the comfortable temperature in the room, which will allow you to create a microclimate in accordance with your preferences;
  • being in the room, you can always watch a lot of satellite channels on TV;
  • the apartment has a minibar, which you can always use as a refrigerator, if you are not interested in alcoholic and carbonated drinks;
  • Each room has a desk, which can also be used as a dining room or to place your cosmetics.




Resort Infrastructure

At the Queen Hotel Pattaya 3 * hotel, guests will be invited to take advantage of the following infrastructure facilities and services:

  • a small but very clean pool where you can enjoy swimming if you are too lazy to get to the beach or if the sea is too restless;
  • the laundry is responsible for the cleanliness and proper appearance of your belongings, where even the most difficult stains are very quickly and carefully removed (dry cleaning services are also provided);
  • on the ground floor, next to the reception, there is a safe where your valuables will be stored in complete safety during your absence from the hotel;
  • hotel guests can use the free car parking, which is guarded around the clock and is under video surveillance;
  • You can use the Internet (both wireless and cable);
  • inside the hotel there is a grocery store and a souvenir shop;
  • You can go on an interesting sightseeing tour if you want to use the services of a travel agency;
  • so that residents of the upper floors can easily get to their rooms, the hotel provides an elevator;
  • The reception works round the clock for you, which solves issues related to check-in and check-out, and also helps to cope with problems that arise during the stay;
  • In the courtyard of the hotel there is a green garden where unique exotic plants grow.




Positive reviews

According to tourists, it is still worth staying at the Queen Pattaya Hotel 3 * (Pattaya). The reviews contain many positive impressions, which are expressed in the following comments:

  • the hotel is located in the city center, and therefore all the most necessary and interesting is literally nearby;
  • very affordable prices for accommodation, as well as a number of related services;
  • daily for each guest give out a small bottle of drinking water;
  • spacious rooms are pleasing, where it is very bright and has a lot of living space;
  • the hotel staff is very friendly, friendly and tries in every way to help on all issues and to please tourists.




Negative reviews

A rather specific resort is Pattaya. Queen Pattaya Hotel 3 * also leaves an ambiguous impression. Speaking about the negative aspects that can be encountered in this hotel, it is worth noting the following:

  • bed linen and towels have a very unpleasant smell (apparently, due to the means by which they are washed), in addition, everything is very old;
  • monotonous breakfast menu;
  • in the evenings and at night the restaurant plays music very loudly, which is why it is simply impossible to fall asleep;
  • most rooms do not have balconies, and where they are, their condition can be regarded as emergency;

  • as the hotel is located in the central part of the city with heavy traffic, it is always very noisy here.

General holiday experience

Given the location in the central part of the city, as well as the very budget pricing policy, the situation in the hotel can be called predictable. We can say that this accommodation option is suitable for those tourists who spend all their free time on excursions or on the beach, and return to their room only to spend the night. Of course, you will not find transcendental service, perfect cleanliness and fresh repairs, but in general the atmosphere is quite pleasant. More demanding tourists are advised to settle in hotels of a higher level, the prices of which will be similar if the institution is removed from the city center.
