CASCO with a franchise - what is it? How does the franchise work in CASCO?

Before insuring a car, you should familiarize yourself with the basic terms that are used in this procedure, in particular the ‚Äúfranchise‚Äù. Insurance agents are sure to talk about all the benefits of acquiring a policy. But not the fact that they will explain the nuances of using CASCO with a franchise. What is it and on what conditions this service is made out, read in more detail in this article.

Definition

Franchise - the amount of money that a person will have to compensate for themselves upon the occurrence of an insured event. It can be indicated in% or in absolute value. Its size is agreed in advance and indicated in the policy. The client receives a discount if he agrees to purchase CASCO with a franchise in RESO.





Kinds

Conditional franchise: all payments that exceed its size are fully compensated by the company. But if the cost of restoring the car is negligible, then the insurer will not receive anything under the policy. Example: a franchise is 10,000 rubles, and repair costs are 8,000 rubles. According to the terms of the contract, in this case the client will not receive anything. But if the amount of costs is 12 thousand rubles, then the insurance will compensate for the damage in full and will not deduct the cost of the deductible. In practice, this option is not always used, as there are often cases of fraud, when the client specifically increases the amount of damage to the level of the deductible.





Most often, customers prefer to buy a CASCO policy with a franchise. What does it mean? In any insurance event, the client will receive compensation minus the predetermined amount (unconditional deductible). Example: a franchise of 3 thousand rubles is prescribed in the policy. The amount of expenses for the restoration of a car is 10 thousand rubles. The client will receive from them only 7 thousand rubles. The remaining amount he will have to compensate for themselves. Such policies are purchased more often.

Additions

CASCO insurance with an unconditional franchise is of two types. Part of the costs that the client will recover on their own can be expressed as a specific amount or as a percentage. In the second case, car wear is taken into account. The amount of the franchise and the period of use vary in direct proportion.

Example: CASCO insurance deductible is set in the policy in the amount of 15% of the amount of damage. 50 thousand rubles will be required to restore a car. From the company (SK), the client will receive 50 * (1-0.15) = 42.5 thousand rubles. And the balance - 7.5 - will pay on its own.

Here's how the franchise works in CASCO.

pros

Depending on the specific situation, driving experience, needs and capabilities of customers, such policies have several advantages.





  1. Saving on the purchase. A policy with an unconditional franchise is always cheaper than without it. Typically, the reduction is twice the amount of a fixed amount. This will be especially noticeable when calculating costs for young drivers (up to two years). Raising factors are usually set for them. The discount grows in proportion to a fixed amount. You can calculate the approximate cost of the policy using the online calculator on the website of the insurance company. But, by purchasing such a product, it is worth it to be prepared for the fact that part of the costs in any case will have to be compensated independently.
  2. Time saving. With minor scratches on the body or other damages, the client will not have to contact the company at all for compensation. Compensation of the franchise under CASCO in such cases is not provided.
  3. According to statistics, cars with a franchise get into accidents less. There is a psychological factor. The driver understands that even though the car is insured, he will have to compensate part of the costs himself. We can say that the franchise forces people to manage the vehicle more carefully.




Benefits of an Insurance Company

The presence of a franchise eliminates the need to launch bureaucratic red tape in order to pay a small refund of 2-3 thousand rubles. The paperwork is always the same. But the costs of this procedure can greatly exceed the amount of the payment itself. CASCO with a franchise saves office costs. The same advantage applies to the client himself. A person does not have to prepare a pack of documents every time to get a refund in the UK. Especially, keep in mind that the paperwork process, if the car needs repair, can drag on for several months.

Other options

In addition to the traditional franchises considered, SK offer other policies. They are intended for specific groups of customers.

Dynamic CASCO franchise, or payout from the second case. The essence of this proposal is that the first damage in the account is indemnified by the insurance company in full. From the second, a franchise begins to operate. Sometimes its percentage increases with each client's appeal to the UK. At the same time, it can change exponentially.

The preferential deductible is used only if the culprit of the accident is not the client. SK is obliged to fully compensate for the damage, even if the second driver fled the scene.





Depending on the time when the insured event occurred, a separate CASCO with a deductible may be used. What does it mean? The policy spelled out not only damage options, but also the time in which they occurred. If the insured event occurred sooner or later, then the client pays all costs independently.

Example: A driver uses a car only on weekdays. On weekends he travels by public transport. He decided to issue a CASCO with a temporary franchise. If the insured event occurs on a weekday, then all costs are compensated by the company. But if the driver crashes on Sunday, you will have to pay money to restore the car from your pocket.

When a franchise should be abandoned

According to statistics, this service is disadvantageous to customers who often get into accidents. In particular, this applies to inexperienced drivers and those who feel insecure behind the wheel.
