How to make a gun out of paper

The scheme, which will be further proposed, should be executed as directed. To make a gun, take heavy paper, such as cardboard.

Pre-find the gun template, and if you draw well, then make it yourself. Be attentive to every detail, draw carefully. Make sure that the left and right sides are the same size. To do this, bend the sheet in half. And only then outline the contours of the future crafts. As the drawing is ready, cut it carefully. Transfer to cardboard, circling with a pencil. Cut out.

The main element of the craft is the groove for the cartridge. Be careful when cutting this part. So, cut the narrow side of the rectangle. It is parallel to the end of the trunk. Bend the rectangle inward. Make cuts on the sides of the trunk and insert the Hungarian (thin elastic band). Pull and tighten it. The gun can be painted or made applique.

For a shot to occur, insert a cartridge into the groove. Make it out of the remaining paper by twisting the balls. With your thumb, remove the rubber from the bed. She with a sharp movement will give speed to the cartridge. If you are interested in this kind of craft, then here is another version of a simpler way to make paper weapons. Design a simple paper gun

Take a rectangular sheet, fold in half horizontally. Then fold the left and right sides in the middle.

So two more times. Take a square sheet and do the same. Fold the first workpiece in half. Expand. Divide the workpiece into three parts. Bend the left side down ninety degrees. Do the same on the right side. Now fold both sides together. The handle has turned out.

Now take the pre-folded barrel from a square sheet. Connect both pieces. Having done the above action several times according to the scheme, you will understand how to make a weapon out of paper. And without peeping in the future, you will collect it yourself.

Shuriken making

This weapon is used by the ninja. The boys will be delighted with such a craft.

First, carefully read the instructions on how to make weapons out of paper, and proceed. Take two square sheets. You can multicolored. Horizontal bend. Then expand. Bend both halves of the sheet to the center so that the parts mirror each other. Bend the corners diagonally.

The blank should resemble two triangles welded together. Repeat these operations with another leaflet. Only bend the corners in opposite directions. Take the first part and put on the second. Pass the corners of the lower workpiece into the pocket-gaps of the other. Flip the future shuriken and do the same. Craft ready for battle.
