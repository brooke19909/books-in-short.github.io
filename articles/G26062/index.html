Waterpark in Kapchagai: photos, description, reviews

One of the "oldest" complexes of water attractions is located in a small and comfortable city in Kazakhstan.

The water park in Kapchagai (photo is presented in the article) is one of the best attractions of the city and the most favorite recreational place for vacationers. You can learn more about it by reading the article.

About Kapchagai

The city is located at the huge reservoir of the same name created on the Ili River (it starts in China). The length of the artificial reservoir is approximately 100 km, and the width is up to 30 km.

There are different versions of the origin of the name "Kapchagay". From the language of ancient Turkic translation means "stone gorge" or "gorge". According to another version, during the raids of the Dzungars, Kaptagai-batyr (Kazakh commander) crossed over the river in this place and wanted to block its channel with sandbags (for Kazakh ‚Äúcap‚Äù - ‚Äúbag‚Äù).

This artificial pond was created to provide the Kapchagai hydroelectric station with water supplies. It should be noted that this power plant has no analogues in the world of structures of this type. This unique "sea" is the city of Kapchagay. The distance from it to the city of Almaty is 76 kilometers. Residents of many cities of Kazakhstan, and not only come to a water park in Kapchagai.



Landmarks

Many people who come to splash around in the water park will be interested in visiting other places of rest and sights of Kapchagai. This place is the most popular among residents not only of Almaty and the region, but of the whole of Kazakhstan.

The entire coast of the reservoir is strewn with numerous recreation areas, mostly private. Fresh and clean air, clear water, beautiful yachts, motor ships, jet skis and many other attributes of water recreation - all this every year attracts a huge number of guests not only from Kazakhstan, but also from abroad.

There are wonderful marine inhabitants: carp, grass carp, bream, pikeperch, asp, crucian carp, snakehead fish, catfish, and crayfish.

The Ili River, which is 100 kilometers long, carries its waters along protected areas. Tourists from many countries annually make rafting on it on rafts from the reservoir itself to Lake Balkhash.

How to get there? You can arrive to the Kapchagaya water park from the city of Almaty by shuttle buses from the Saykhat and Sairan bus stations, as well as by train from the Almaty-1 station. The journey takes approximately 1.5 hours.

Tourists reviews

What do vacationers say about the water park in Kapchagai? Reviews are mostly positive. According to the residents of Almaty, where there is also a similar complex, it is cheaper to travel and relax in Kapchagai, even if you have to get to it by taxi. For a relatively small entrance fee (only 400 rubles), you can stay on the territory almost all day.



Rest in the Kapchagaya water park, according to tourists, is wonderful both for families with children and for youth companies. A cozy beautiful complex and wonderful steep slides are an indispensable antidepressant. After his visit, the mood improves, and the body is energized for a rather long time.
