Kamchia (Bulgaria): location on the map, weather and resort reviews

Kamchia (Bulgaria) is a small village located near Sunny Beach - a famous resort. This is a fairly young place in the leisure industry, but at present it is actively developing. One of the directions is the organization of youth and children's pastime. There are several such complexes.



general information

Kamchia (Bulgaria) is a very picturesque and romantic place. It is located in the reserve of national importance Longoz among centuries-old trees. The weather in Bulgaria (in Kamchia) is due to the unique combination of a mild marine, mountain and forest climate. There is crystal clear air, which favors the treatment of respiratory diseases. The average temperature in January is +1.2 degrees Celsius, and July - +23 degrees. Due to the proximity of the sea, there is a high level of humidity - about 80 percent. The sea in Kamchia is very warm compared to Golden Sands and Albena, the water here is always 3-5 degrees warmer. The beaches are clean, fairly wide, with fine golden sand, the sea is shallow, which is very convenient for organizing children's beaches. Kamchia (Bulgaria) is an ideal place for a cozy and relaxing family and childrens relaxation away from the hustle and bustle of the city.

Mineral springs

Bulgaria (Kamchia on the map of the country is located north of the mouth of the river of the same name) is rich in mineral springs, the value of which is recognized by many experts. Water in them has an excellent strengthening and healing effect. Its use is recommended by doctors for the treatment of certain diseases of the nervous system, musculoskeletal system, cardiovascular diseases and so on.



Excursions

A variety of excursions for adults and children are regularly organized for tourists from Kamchia. Everyone can visit the city of Nessebar - this is an open-air museum; the sea capital of this country is Varna, where children can go to the Marine Park and the dolphinarium; Sunny Beach resort, where there is a wide field for violent children's activities (water park, swimming pools, playgrounds with swing-carousels, slides and so on).

Tourist complex on the Kamchia river

The mouth of the Kamchia River is one of the most beautiful places on the Bulgarian coast. It is located 34 kilometers south of Varna and 500 kilometers east of Sofia. This area is characterized by endless beaches that start in the north of the Paletsa area and end in the south at the Black Cape, in the vicinity of the village of Shkorpilovtsi.

Kamchia (Bulgaria) is also a sanatorium complex, which is divided by the river into two parts - north and south. The river flows through the mountains, wades through a forested area. More than forty species of very beautiful ferns, bushes and trees grow here. Yellow lilies are scattered here and there on the water surface. This is Bulgaria, Kamchia! Reviews of many tourists who have been here are very enthusiastic, someone calls this area a paradise on Earth, others call it a fairy tale. No one will remain indifferent.





Historical excursion

Once in ancient times, Kamchia was called Panisos, and the Slavs living here called her Ticha. The current name of the river is of Cuman origin. On its left bank used to be the Roman fortress of Erite. Lipans settled on the shores - these are the great-grandchildren of the Russian Old Believers who arrived here from the mouth of the Danube.

Kamchia Nature Reserve

Today, the river flood is a protected area, which is under the protection of UNESCO. This territory reaches forty kilometers in length, five in width. Deer, roe deer, sea hawks, eagles, wild boars, wild cats and others are found in the reserve. Here 245 species of higher plants grow. The river is home to 39 species of fish, most of which are representatives of the cyprinid family. Visitors to this conservation area can see a variety of representatives of amphibians. Over 250 bird species have been recorded in this area. Over the mouth of the river passes the migration route of some species of birds. In addition, rare birds for this area and even Europe breed in the reserve: black stork, European stag beetle, Lesser spotted eagle, and others. Passing through these places is allowed only on specially marked paths.



Shkorpilovtsi

Not far from the base "Kamchia" is another interesting object - Shkorpilovtsi. You can get here via the E87 highway. This is a tourist complex, which includes two campsites. Here is a beautiful nature, the highlight is the famous Black Cape, with all its bulk hanging over the sea. In addition, there are very beautiful white limestone cliffs. This is a great place for those who want to relax surrounded by virgin forest. Vacationers are offered boat trips, which will help to diversify leisure with new experiences.
