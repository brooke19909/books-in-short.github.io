Infrastructure

Once in the territory of this modern and comfortable hotel complex, tourists, of course, pay attention to a compact five-story residential building, a large clean pool located next to it, the royal palm trees of a tropical garden.

Beach vacation

Experienced travelers give new arrivals good advice: for the first three to four days it is minimally to be on the street, while using sunblock. You should get used to the local sun.

Lavanga Resort Spa 5 * is located on the first line from the sandy beach. He is well-maintained, provided with sun loungers and umbrellas. There is a buffet on the beach where you can buy refreshments. However, hotel guests recommend walking about seven minutes to the left along the coast to select a bathing place. In the bay located there, the sea is calm and the waves are smaller.

To the right of the hotel you should not look for places for swimming. According to tourists exploring this area, the coast there is crowded, dirty, uninteresting merchants pester tourists.

Here you should not swim alone, but it is generally safer for young children and low-swimming to swim in the pool. You can‚Äôt turn your back on the Indian Ocean, you should look at the waves. Nutrition

The restaurant of the hotel complex Lavanga Resort Spa 5 * feeds tourists according to the buffet method differentially, depending on what type of food (breakfast, half board or board) the tourist paid for.
