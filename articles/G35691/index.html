What are the beaches of South Goa?

One of the most quiet, calm and at the same time colorful and interesting resorts is South Goa. Here you will not find lively parties, giant markets and bustling tourists, but, on the contrary, you will find the perfect place for relaxation, which will distract you from all the earthly problems and take you to another reality. Here nature is in harmony with upscale hotels and cozy restaurants, and the beaches of South Goa are endless steppes of golden sand washed by the waters of the Arabian Sea.



It is worth noting that in this region there are many hotel complexes of various categories. Each of them has its own territory, on which there is a swimming pool, gym, spa. There are also restaurants adjacent to the hotels, which serve both local delicacies and traditional Russian and European dishes. Some beaches of South Goa are part of the ownership of hotels or cottages, so only guests of a particular institution can stay on them. But in this place there are a lot of urban, equipped areas, and quite wild, where only nature lives.



The main attraction of the resort is precisely sandy recreation areas, surrounded on one side by tropical vegetation, and on the other, by the waters of the blue sea that is washed by South Goa. Colva Beach is the first in our virtual tour. This is the most famous recreation area among tourists, and it is located in the village of the same name. A lot of cafes, souvenir shops and other shops are concentrated here, where you can buy Indian paraphernalia, eat something tasty and get acquainted with the features of this country. It is also worth noting that it was in Colva that a lot of middle-class hotels were built. Therefore, people often come here from different parts of the world who simply want to learn something new, enjoy this place and its nature.

The beaches of South Goa are distinguished by silence and peace. Such qualities are fully embodied in a resort called Benaulim. It is located 2 kilometers from Colva, and is the quietest on the coast of the Arabian Sea. You can go there with full confidence if you want to reflect on the meaning of life or just meditate.



Some beaches of South Goa have become a meeting place for oligarchs around the world, and among these coasts can be called Varca and Cavelossim. They are equipped to the highest level, and are surrounded on all sides by fashionable hotels, cottages and luxury bungalows.

The most beautiful place in this region is considered to be the resort of Mabor, which is located between the waters of the Arabian Sea and the Sal River. Local beaches are lost among the islands separating the two ponds, and lilies, tropical shrubs and trees grow around.

There are quite remote beaches of South Goa, photos of which are presented in the article. And most often fishermen live in such places, and travelers who like active entertainments stop at them. Among these beaches it is worth noting Palolem.
