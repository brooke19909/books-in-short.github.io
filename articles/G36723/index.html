Rooms Hotel Kazbegi (Georgia, Kazbegi): photos and reviews

When planning a trip to Georgia, everyone can be sure that a sunny, beautiful and incredibly sincere country awaits him. Georgia is rich in unique traditions and has an ancient history. Kind and helpful people, blue sky, gentle sun and warm sea - all this is about Georgia. A great option is to visit the Rooms Hotel Kazbegi in Kazbegi, which has been welcoming guests to its guests since 2012.





About the hotel

The elite hotel of the Georgian province with stunning mountain views has at its disposal a 5-story building, equipped with 156 spacious guest rooms:

  • the upper three floors are residential;
  • on the first floor there is a lounge and a reception;
  • on the ground floor there is a swimming pool and a modern gym.




The lobby of Rooms Hotel Kazbegi will surprise guests with its unpretentious appearance: leather sofas, glass walls, wicker rattan chairs and shelves with a variety of books that complement the interior in an original way. The flooring of the enormous hall is made of vintage wooden boards, giving the atmosphere a touch of provincial rural style. Passing through the hall, guests of the Kazbegi Rooms Hotel in Georgia find themselves on an outdoor terrace with breathtaking mountain views.

How to find

The hotel is located in the upper part of the resort of Kazbegi. It can already be observed at the entrance to the urban-type settlement Stepantsminda, which is located in the heart of the Kazbegi municipality. How to get to Rooms Hotel Kazbegi? Address of the institution: Vakhtang Gorgasali Street , 1. The road to the hotel runs from Gudauri and through the Cross Pass, where avalanches often descend. For this reason, it can sometimes be closed, and travelers should wait a while.

Rooms Hotel Kazbegi 4 * is located 176 km from Tbilisi Airport , and a 10-minute walk from Kazbegi Bus Stop.
