Club Tropicana 3 * (Monastir): hotel description, services, reviews. Holidays in Tunisia

Every person from time to time wants to relax and improve their health somewhere on the picturesque seashore. Of course, modern tourists have a huge selection, but many choose Tunisia as their vacation destination. It is a beautiful country with an interesting culture and history, which boasts not only the beauties of nature and attractions, but also also high-quality service.

One of the largest and most modern resorts in Tunisia is Monastir. On the territory of the city itself, as well as in its vicinity, there are a huge number of hotels with different prices and living conditions. One of the good places to stay is the Club Hotel Tropicana (Tunisia). Traveler reviews indicate that you can really have a good time here, enjoying your vacation without any minor problems.

Proper travel planning is the key to a good vacation. Naturally, before booking a place in a hotel, you should try to collect as much information as possible. For example, is a hotel location advantageous and how long does it take to get to the sea? What conditions of accommodation and room design can a guest expect? Do tourists offer decent food and interesting entertainment? The answers to these questions always affect the choice of hotel.

Hotel Club Tropicana 3 * on the map: description of the location





Naturally, when planning a trip, people primarily pay attention to the location of their temporary shelter. Where exactly to look for the Club Tropicana 3 * hotel complex. Skanes (Monastir) - a picturesque resort town located directly on the seashore. Monastir's vibrant city center is 11 kilometers away.

Brief description of the hotel: what should a traveler expect?





The hotel complex consists of several snow-white buildings built in a traditional, simple, but elegant oriental style. A large area adjoins the buildings. Reviews say that the whole area is beautifully arranged, decorated, and they look after it regularly.

You can walk along comfortable stone paths, chill in the shade of palm trees and pines, relax in gazebos under a thatched roof, in a word, spend time in unity with nature, enjoying the healing sea air.

Hotel Club Tropicana 3 * (Tunisia, Monastir): photo and room information





The hotel complex has 311 rooms - most of them are standard rooms, but there are several rooms with a high level of comfort. Naturally, many tourists are interested in information about what conditions of stay can be expected in the territory of the Club Tropicana 3 * hotel. Monastir is famous for its genuine hospitality and comfortable hotels. And this place is no exception.

The rooms here are small but cozy, distinguished by a modern setting and a calm design that creates the perfect atmosphere for a relaxing holiday. The room comes with a balcony with comfortable garden furniture, which overlooks the sea and the luxurious hotel grounds.

You can freely count on a set of equipment so necessary for comfort. For example, on TV you can watch satellite channels (there is even one Russian). It features a telephone, a powerful air conditioning system, and a small safe. Some rooms have free, wireless internet access. The bathroom is small (depending on the category of the room there is either a shower or a bathtub). Plumbing is in good condition and modern, there is a toilet, a washbasin, a set of some toiletries, as well as a mirror. Each guest receives a set of clean towels, which the maids change daily. Reviews, by the way, confirm that the rooms are kept clean, because they clean here really in good faith.

Where is the beach located?





Naturally, tourists who intend to spend a vacation in Tunisia are counting on a pleasant pastime by the sea. What can the Club Tropicana 3 * hotel complex offer? Monastir is a resort town famous for its sandy beaches. The hotel itself is built on the first coastline - a private beach site is a stone's throw away. Naturally, only guests rest here, so you can count on a calm, safe pastime. You can also use sun loungers and parasols for free. Large beach towels are provided on bail, and until 18.00 there is a bar where you can get drinks and light snacks.

Water recreation

People who prefer a more active holiday also need not worry, because there is a water sports center on the shore where you can rent a boat, canoe, boat and other types of water transport. You can go sailing and even scuba diving. There are instructors and even schools where travelers can take diving and windsurfing courses.

There is a large venue where animators often organize beach soccer and volleyball competitions. Fun activities are what Monastir has to offer the traveler. The prices here, by the way, are quite reasonable.
