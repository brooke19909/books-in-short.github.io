Hotel Kleopatra Tac Hotel & SPA 3 * (Turkey, Alanya) - overview, description and reviews of tourists

Alanya is one of the hottest and most picturesque resort regions of Turkey, attracting tourists with its beauty and luxurious hotels, which offer a wonderful vacation for travelers of different categories.

Kleopatra Tac Hotel & Spa 3 * is one of the most popular hotels in the area, in travel agencies located in different parts of the world, hundreds of trips are sold here every year.

general information

This resting place was built in 2012, since then travelers have been resting on its territory every year. At the time when the holiday season closes, small renovations are being carried out in the buildings, with the help of which a modern and fresh look of the hotel is maintained.

Location

The hotel is located in the central part of Alanya, a few tens of meters from the coastline of the Mediterranean Sea, where there is a fairly gentle slope into the sea and warm water for a long period. In the district there are a huge number of attractions and simply beautiful places that many travelers seek to visit.

The nearest airport is 130 kilometers from the Kleopatra Tac Hotel & Spa 3 *. Due to such a distance from the point of arrival of tourists to the place of rest, the hotel must include the payment of a transfer service for each trip.

Rooms

The general fund of the hotel is quite small - it consists of 60 apartments, which are divided into two groups: family and standard, depending on the proposed number of beds. All rooms that are presented here have individual air conditioners, which were installed during one of the renovations. Also in each room there is a bathroom, within which there is a plumbing necessary for personal hygiene. Each tourist at the time of entry to the room receives for use bathrobes, bath towels and slippers, as well as a set of soap accessories.



Standard

On 20 m 2 standard class rooms there is one room, which presents an excellent place for relaxation, designed for two. If you wish, you can rent apartments with two single beds.

The rooms have private balconies with stunning views of the gardens, the sea and simply beautiful surroundings. Here, many also recommend drinking coffee and tea in the evening, admiring the chic nature.

Of the furniture in the room there is a place for storing clothes and personal belongings, as well as a TV on which two Russian-language channels are broadcast. Tourists from the CIS countries often note this with positive comments. Guests staying in standard apartments can use the minibar, which at the time of arrival of guests is free of charge filled with cool water.

Nutrition

The reviews about Kleopatra Tac Hotel & Spa 3 * very often mention the food options in the hotel restaurant, as well as the excellent work of talented chefs who work in the kitchen. Guests note that here every day a buffet is assembled, in which guests staying at the hotel according to the all-inclusive concept can take part three times a day. Excellent hot dishes are served here, as well as light snacks prepared according to recipes of Turkish, Mediterranean and European cuisines.

In Kleopatra Tac Hotel & Spa 3 * (Turkey / Alanya) there is an opportunity to visit two bars located in the hotel building, as well as in its open area in the aquazone. There is a separate menu, the list of which includes snacks, desserts and a huge selection of drinks and cocktails.



Entertainment

The main entertainment for guests in this place are night discos, which are held outdoors, by the pool. During their holding, clockwork music is played here, which is to the taste of the guests. In their comments, they often note that a DJ plays at such events, who knows how to amuse the crowd. Entrance to the disco is paid.

Services

In Kleopatra Tac Hotel & Spa 3 * on a regular basis a number of services are provided, most of which are paid. Here at any time you can call a doctor in your room or visit his office on your own, which is located on the first floor of the building. If desired, tourists have the opportunity to use the services that are provided in the laundry or dry cleaning.

On the ground floor of the building, guests are presented with shops selling interesting goods and souvenirs. Here, tourists buy gifts for their friends and relatives.

In Kleopatra Tac Hotel & Spa 3 * (Alanya) there is a possibility of renting a car, which is provided by the day. By such a car, experienced travelers are advised to go on an independent tour of local attractions and very picturesque surroundings. They assure that from such trips you can get a lot of emotions.





Aquazone

While staying at the hotel, many of its guests spend most of their time in a large aquazone, which is located in an open area. It consists of a sun terrace surrounded by flowering greenery. In addition, it has a bar with various cocktails and drinks.

Guests who come here have the opportunity to sit on large sun loungers, standing under umbrellas. Here you can rent a towel or an air mattress.

In the very center of the terrace is a swimming pool in which purified water is updated every day. It works without heating, but with an automated cleaning system. On one side of the pool is a section for children, which is shallower in depth and has a specific coating.



SPA

The spa center, which is available at Kleopatra Tac Hotel & Spa 3 * (Alanya), is widely popular, even though all the services provided here are paid. You can visit it any day of the week from 9 a.m. to 8 p.m.

The spa area is divided into massage and thermal. In the thermal part there is a sauna and hammam - you can visit them completely free of charge from 5 to 6 pm. At this time, there is the largest flow of guests.

In the massage room there are several tables at which a team of professional masters works. They own unique techniques of different types of massage, which are easily used at guests.

Sport

Fans of a sports lifestyle who stayed at the Kleopatra Tac Hotel & Spa 3 * hotel (Turkey) have the opportunity to rent a bicycle. Often it is needed in order to go on a short trip around the neighborhood. Many tourists use it in order to get to the central part of Alanya, where there is a huge amount of entertainment interesting to travelers.

In the open area there is a small platform for playing mini-football, and along the coastline there are opportunities for water sports, such as skiing or surfing. Scuba diving is very popular here, which is done exclusively under the guidance of an instructor.



Beach

The hotel has its own part on a large city beach. The attendants carefully monitor the cleanliness of the area, as well as the comfort of the guests.

The length of the beach is quite large - it is about 150 meters, the area is completely covered with fine and very soft sand. Sun loungers and umbrellas are provided on a paid basis, the cost of their rent is approximately 180 rubles (3 dollars) per day from one person. Mattresses can be taken on a free basis, and many people recommend bringing towels with them, as they are not provided on the beach.

Prices

The cost of a package ticket to Kleopatra Tac Hotel & Spa 3 * (Alanya), according to reviews, is about 55-60 thousand rubles. This price is inclusive of transportation costs, including transfer. In addition, the price includes the cost of a weekly rental Standard room in Kleopatra Tac Hotel & Spa 3 * (Alanya). Such a package provides for accommodation according to the all-inclusive concept, which includes three meals a day in a restaurant and participation in a buffet.

Most tourists find this payment for a vacation on the Mediterranean coast quite acceptable. They often advise their relatives and relatives to relax here, showing photos taken at the time of their stay at the hotel and talking about excellent living conditions.
