Spa hotels in Finland.

Finland is attractive to tourists for its ski holidays. This is a northern country where the summer is extremely short. Russian tourists have long discovered for themselves the charm of Finnish relaxation. And although we all mostly love to soak up the sand and sunbathe in the sun, sometimes you want a harsh climate. In addition, hotels in Finland attract with their excellent service, and resorts - with developed infrastructure. This country is famous for its water recreation complexes. Spa hotels and water parks are especially popular here. Naturally, such a large number of water activities is connected with rather cold climatic conditions. Hotels in Finland's spa are causing frenzied interest not only among Russian tourists, but also guests from other countries. There are several dozen such complexes in the country, but we will talk about the most popular ones.

Spa-hotels in Finland is represented by Naantali Spa Hotel, which is one of the hundred best European institutions of this kind. It is located on the coast of the Baltic Sea and has 2 buildings. In one of them the Spa-hotel is located directly, and the other, in the form of a magnificent yacht, stands directly in the sea. It offers guests 120 rooms. And the list of Spa-services includes the opportunity to visit two Turkish saunas, five pools, four Finnish saunas and a beauty salon. You can enjoy a professional massage, an aromatherapy session, and body and face treatments.

There are hotels in Finland that offer guests mud therapy as well. These include SPA & Hotel Casino. There are several swimming pools, a Roman and Turkish bath, and wellness programs include physiotherapy, herbal baths, massages, electrical stimulation and various cosmetic services for facial skin.

Imatran Kylpyla Spa Hotel is located closest to the Russian border. There is a hydropathic center where you can go through various procedures, from massage to mud treatment. In addition, a lasting impression will leave a visit to the water park "Magic Forest". And the beauty salon, gyms, restaurants and cafes will be a pleasant addition to everything else. You can also play ball hockey or badminton.

Spa hotels in Finland are worthy of the Tampere Spa Hotel. There is a beauty salon, restaurants, a gym and bowling. And, of course, a balneological center is at your service: aromatherapy, mud therapy, physiotherapy, massage. You can also swim in the mineral water pool, sit in the jacuzzi, visit the Roman and Finnish saunas, and sunbathe in the solarium.

The water park at Turku Holiday Club Caribia is a great place to relax for families with children. 175 rooms have everything you need, from a TV to a minibar. And some rooms even have saunas. Eight pools and three water slides of different lengths work for you in the water park. You can also go fishing, get a horseback riding lesson, play golf or bowling and go skiing.

Holiday Club Tampereen Kylpyla is a well-known hotel in certain circles. Here you will visit several different pools, a peat bath and a Roman sauna. And in the local balneological center you can go through any wellness program, whether it be massage, all kinds of baths, stone therapy, mud therapy or sea face peeling.

Hotels in Finland that offer spa services attract a huge number of people. After all, such a rest energizes and invigorates at the same time. Positive reviews about hotels in Finland appear even more often because everything is thought out here to ensure a pleasant pastime for families with children. It will be interesting for kids to visit water parks and participate in other water activities. And adults are invariably attracted to wellness treatments. In addition, you can always combine them with urban recreation or outdoor activities.
