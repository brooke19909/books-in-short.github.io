Ora Holiday Village 4 * (Turkey / Bodrum) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Ora Holiday Village 4 is a large cottage complex in the village of Torba (near Bodrum), which is located on the seafront. It is located between two airports. The first of these is Milas, and the second is Bodrum. To the last distance of thirty-eight kilometers. Before Milas - twenty-five. To the city of Bodrum - twelve kilometers. The hotel is very unusual, interesting and original. Perhaps this is why tourists like it, who leave positive feedback about it. The hotel is an economy class, but the services in it are primary, and the staff tries, as in the "five", and works from the heart. The hotel belongs to the chain of hotels Cande, famous not only in Turkey, but throughout the world.





Bodrum - photos and reviews

This place is called the most party resort in the country. Bodrum on a map of Turkey is located in the very north of Gokova Bay, in the Aegean Sea. This is the border with the Mediterranean. And directly opposite Bodrum is the Greek island of Kos. In addition, it is the center of a huge resort area with fourteen bays. Surfers and active sports enthusiasts come here. According to tourists, this is a very good place to visit almost any corner of Turkey. From the local bus station, public transport leaves in Pamukkale, and in Izmir, and in Istanbul, and in Ankara. The town itself is unusually picturesque, especially in the evenings, and is famous for its port, markets and the ancient fortress of St. Peter. By the way, the castle has an exhibition of Islamic glass and the country's only museum of underwater archeology. And from the harbor, many wooden boats - gulets - go for walks. At this place once stood one of the seven wonders of the world. This is the mausoleum of Halicarnassus, and if you are a lover of antiquities, you can look at what remains of it. The village of Torba, where the Ora Hotel is located, is one of the resort places closest to the center of Bodrum. It is considered to be fashionable, not so many hotels have been built here, and the water is of amazing purity.
