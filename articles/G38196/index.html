Some artifacts indicate that in this place already in the V century there was an urban settlement, while others - that it became the center of the tribe of glades only in the VIII century. Whatever opinion they come to, one thing is clear: from the ancient settlement over the centuries a beautiful European city grew up, visited annually by millions of tourists.

In the capital of Ukraine, it is customary that guests here are welcomed not only by expensive hotels. Inexpensive hotels in Kiev will also leave the best fond memories of both themselves and the city.
