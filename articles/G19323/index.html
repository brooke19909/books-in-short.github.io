Service benefits

When connecting to the Mobile Bank service, Sberbank customers have the opportunity to:

Transfer by phone number

As already described above, it is possible to transfer funds from your card to another client of Sberbank, knowing only his phone number attached to the card. This allows customers not to give a bank card number to third parties, but to receive money thanks to a mobile phone attached to the card.

Limitations

For transfers, the following restrictions are introduced:

  1. Transactions are made in national currency - rubles.
  2. Transfers between currency cards are prohibited.
  3. The amount of transfer / receipt of funds per day should not exceed the amount of 8 thousand rubles.
  4. A maximum of 10 mobile operations are allowed per day.

Other ways to transfer funds

A mobile transfer in Sberbank can be carried out not only by means of SMS messages, but also carried out in a mobile application. Moreover, the utility also allows you to perform an operation by phone number and card number. You can also select any person from your phone book so as not to enter contact information yourself.

In the mobile application, all previous mobile transfers are saved. Sberbank offers to repeat regular payments in automatic mode. To do this, the Dialogs function is provided to the client, in which all previous customer transfers to each other are saved and visible immediately.

Customers can also send requests for funds transfers. For example, you have borrowed a certain amount. After that, you can unobtrusively remind the borrower that the funds must be repaid.
