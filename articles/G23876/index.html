How to get money back from Kiwi Wallet: tips and step-by-step instructions

Precautionary measures

In order not to think later how to return the transferred money from Qiwi Wallet, the user must follow the general rules for making payments. An unscrupulous user is trying to trick money into his account through:

  • Online purchases in the form of a prepayment or the full amount;
  • the provision of services, discounts on goods;
  • charitable assistance to various organizations.

Before making a transaction, experts advise you to carefully read the data that the account holder enters into the system. Providing the following information is a guarantee of reliability:

  • authorization and personal account availability;
  • personal identification - full name and passport data;
  • the name and address of the organization, the presence of mandatory details for payment and verification;
  • account registration date - one-day wallets are suspicious;
  • rating;
  • mailbox location - a serious enterprise uses paid domains.

Attackers try not to linger in the system, so they avoid entering contact information that helps track location. Therefore, there are not so many options for how to return the money transferred to the Qiwi Wallet of a scammer. If the paid goods do not arrive, and the seller does not get in touch, then there is only one way out - contact the police and consumer protection.

Wallet lock: what does the owner do

The question of how to return money from Qiwi can arise not only in case of an unsuccessful transfer of funds, but also in situations of loss of access to your personal account, on whose account a certain amount is stored.

The reasons for restricting access to the account are:

  • Suspicious account activity
  • the presence of complaints from other users;
  • The daily operations limit has been reached.

For unauthorized customers, an allowable amount of one hundred thousand rubles is set. If the limit is exceeded, the account is blocked for a day.

Verification of an account is a procedure for a client to provide complete, true, official information that arouses trust: copies of identification documents, contracts with a mobile operator.

Important! It matters if you have a contract with a cellular company, as suspicious individuals try to get access to your wallet and withdraw funds using calls from unknown numbers.

If a loss of access is detected, they contact the security service of the payment resource. For this:

  • in the menu bar "help" select the section "contact support";
  • item "security service";
  • option "unlock wallet".

The user must leave a request with personal information and a description of the problem. Copies of documents and a contract with a communications company or a certificate of ownership of a cellular number are attached to the application.

Important! Doubtful appeals about the imposition of access return services may be ignored by the support service. This will increase the likelihood of being left without funds. Therefore, it is important to take care of the quality of scans of your documents.
