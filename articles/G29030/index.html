How to make a pompom, rug and lampshade for a lamp from threads

How to make a pompom and lampshade from a thread for a lamp? In fact, it is not as difficult as it might seem. Many argue that they do not have the ability for manual creativity, but there are such techniques in needlework that will allow even a child to create an interesting and exclusive thing. For example, almost everyone knows how to make pompoms out of threads, and if not, you can master this simple art in a couple of minutes.

Pompom making

To make a pompom, we need a piece of cardboard, scissors, threads and a thick needle. From cardboard we cut out two circles with a hole in the middle, as shown in the photo.
We put together two blanks, we thread the needle into a needle and wrap it with our template several times.
The more layers of thread there are, the pompom will turn out more magnificent.
Now gently trim all the threads along the edge of the pattern.
We put a piece of thread between the cardboard circles and tighten our pompom with it.
It remains only to expand the pompom, deleting the details of the template.

How to make a pompom from threads so that it is multi-colored? Very simple! Use threads of different colors, winding them randomly or vice versa orderly, so you can get colorful or striped pompons.

Pompom Product Ideas

Now that you know how to make pompoms out of threads, you can use this skill to make interesting and practical things. For example, such a rug.

To make a rug you will need a masking net. It is sold by footage in all construction stores and will serve as the basis. Decide for yourself what size and shape your mat will be, and buy a net twice as much - it is better to fold it in half for durability. Then simply tie your pompons to the grid as your fantasy tells you, capturing both layers of the base.

Surprisingly, the pompom, with all its plainness, can become the basis for many interesting ideas.

How to make a lamp from threads

Now let's see how to make a chandelier from threads. For work, you will need a balloon or an inflatable ball, cling film, PVA glue, a small container and threads.

Inflate the ball and wrap its surface with cling film. Pour glue into a container and lower a ball of thread there. When the threads are impregnated well enough, we begin to evenly wrap the surface of the ball, leaving a hole for attachment at the top. For work, you can take threads of different colors, but it is not recommended to choose threads of cold shades, the light from such a lampshade will give an unpleasant glow to the entire space of the room.
We hang our ball until the threads dry completely. As a result, the lampshade will become hard.
We release air from the ball and carefully remove the plastic wrap with it.
On this, the manufacture of the lampshade can be considered complete. It remains only to install it in the chandelier and screw in the bulb. Such lampshades can be used not only for ceiling, but also for floor lighting.
If you know how to knit, then you can make a knitted lampshade by the same principle. The difference lies only in the fact that it is necessary to impregnate it with glue after pulling it on a ball or use a wire frame.

Now you know how to make a pompom and lampshade from a thread for a lamp. These simple tricks will help you to revive the interior simply and without extra costs.
