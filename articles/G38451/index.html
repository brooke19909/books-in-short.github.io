What is property tax for senior citizens? Property tax refund for pensioners

Today we will be interested in property tax for pensioners. Do they have to pay it? In what sizes? Can a tax deduction be made? What will be required for this and where to go? In relation to pensioners, the tax services of the Russian Federation provide for special rules. Not always, but in most cases. Of course, taxpayer status will be taken into account . This is an important area affecting property tax for senior citizens. So, what tax liabilities do older people have? How are they distributed and act?



Not all and not always

It all depends on many situations. It is worth noting that pensioners are a kind of beneficiaries. They have special rights in many areas of public and state life. Not always, but often. The thing is that many taxes are regional. That is, they are not regulated by the Tax Code as such.

This means if you are considering whether there is a property tax for senior citizens, you will have to consider your location. Somewhere older people are considered beneficiaries, somewhere not. Discounts apply to some property; some property must be fully "paid for". So in our current issue, the region where citizens live is playing a huge role. However, some general rules are available.

Disabled people

Which ones? The thing is that pensioners are kind of beneficiaries. This has already been said. Not always, but in most cases. Most often, such citizens make disability.

Persons with disabilities in Russia (groups 1 and 2) are fully exempt from property taxes. For example, they are "bypassed" by the transport tax. Therefore, if you are thinking about our current issue, keep in mind that people with disabilities and health problems (regardless of age) are completely exempted from most state penalties. And if we are talking about pensioners, then even more so.

Veterans

That's not all. It is also worth noting that property tax for pensioners is often absent if they are veterans. Or heroes of the Second World War. This also includes citizens who participated in general in armed battles.



It turns out that property tax for military pensioners is not levied. With a few minor exceptions. After all, not all the military participated in hostilities. This fact will have to be considered when considering the issue. In principle, such cases are not so common. What's next?

Payment or Benefit

A lot depends on what particular tax is involved. The property may be different. Therefore, pensioners are not always completely exempted from these obligations. Not many are aware of this.

Nevertheless, pensioners usually pay property tax. But not in full. But only on a preferential basis. Most tax collections, as already mentioned, are regional in nature. This means that in each city the size of the discount for pensioners varies. On average, it is 90%.

It turns out that a pensioner will have to pay only 10 percent of the total amount of penalties. For example, such a rule applies to transport tax. After all, cars and vehicles in general are a kind of property. You should not lose sight of this moment.

Thus, pensioners pay property tax. Not in full most often. There are exceptions. It makes no sense to list them - as has been repeatedly emphasized, each region has its own rules regarding this issue. Therefore, take an interest in the Tax Service, which is specific to you. Land

Tax benefits (property) to pensioners are provided. These discounts are not binding on all property. For example, if you carefully study the new rules, then from 2016 the land tax is paid by all citizens. More precisely, all taxpayers (that is, people from 18 years old) are required to pay tax on land owned on time. This is quite normal. All property in Russia must be paid to one degree or another.

Property tax for pensioners in this case takes place. And no discounts, concessions and exemptions in 2016 are not provided. That is, if a pensioner owns a land, he will have to pay the full amount of taxes annually. With no exceptions. It doesn't matter if a citizen works or not. The fact remains - if we are talking about property, expressed in the form of a land plot, then the tax should be paid. No discounts, benefits and delays. In this regard, all citizens are equal before the state.

Income

There is also a property tax on individuals (pensioners and not only), which is called income tax. It is paid when a citizen sells his property. This is usually real estate. This moment is extremely worrying for older people who decided to say goodbye to some of their property in their old age.



Property tax for pensioners in this case is paid. In full and without exception. If a person has owned the property or other property for less than 3 years according to the documents, then 13% of the transaction amount will have to be paid to the state treasury.

And no concessions. Moreover, the status of a citizen is not taken into account. It doesn‚Äôt matter if a pensioner works or not. Property

There is also the so-called property tax. It is popularly known as property tax. It is paid for that the citizen has something in the property. Under it is usually accepted to mean real estate.

Property tax for working pensioners (and not working as well), to be honest, you do not need to pay. That is, older people are completely exempted from it. Regardless of employment. But all other citizens are required to pay this tax.

We defend the rights
