The five-star Jasmine Beach Hotel (Turkey / Antalya) - a paradise for tourists

The five-star "Jasmine Beach Hotel" (Turkey) during its existence (since 1985) has established itself on the good side. This is one of the most popular complexes in Alanya, every year it is visited by thousands of tourists from around the world. The hotel guarantees comfortable accommodation on the Mediterranean coast, quality service, excellent food and lots of interesting entertainment.





The Jasmine Beach Hotel (Turkey) is located in a green village called Avsallar, 120 km from the airport of Antalya. Thanks to the rich external infrastructure, guests can freely visit local attractions, restaurants, shops, located a few meters from the complex. Here you will find an attentive attitude, a welcoming atmosphere and paradise relaxation at the sea's edge.

Jasmine Beach 5 * Hotel (Turkey) consists of 3-storey fully equipped cottages and a 6-storey building. Couples with young children are better off staying in separate villas, because it is calmer and quieter than in the main building, where until late at night the music is silent and a disco is held.

Number of rooms

All 340 rooms with magnificent views of the coast or the blooming garden. There are special rooms for people with various physical disabilities. For large companies, there are 2-bedroom apartments and villas. The rooms are equipped with a split system, a direct dial telephone and a cable / satellite TV.

In the individual use of guests - a bathroom with the necessary set of bath accessories. It features a free safe. Separate fees are charged for using the minibar. A 24-hour maintenance and cleaning service is available. Cots are available for children.





Nutrition

Jasmine Beach Hotel (Turkey) offers a European all-inclusive meal concept. The selection of dishes is decent and varied. The menu is always fresh pastries, fish and meat dishes, and, of course, fresh vegetables. An individual diet is selected for losing weight. For kids - separate meals and high chairs.

You can satisfy gastronomic addictions in the hotel restaurants. The institutions offer delicious dishes of international and national cuisine, as well as a wide selection of alcoholic beverages and freshly squeezed juices. In addition, 4 bars are open on the territory where everyone can enjoy unusual snacks and exotic cocktails in a pleasant atmosphere.

Beach

The hotel owns a small stretch of coast located 100 meters from the main building. The beach is strewn with small pebbles, so it is recommended to wear beach shoes. Entrance to the water is very convenient, especially for young children. Jasmine Beach Hotel (Turkey) offers its guests not only to sunbathe on comfortable sun loungers, but also to go water-skiing or motorbiking - to get a lot of positive and a dose of adrenaline.

Infrastructure





Among the abundance of green space there are two pools with slides, which also adjoins a children's pool. Your children will always be under observation and will not hide from the eyes of their parents. Around the pools fit beach attributes for a comfortable and relaxed pastime.

There are sports activities: a fitness center, playgrounds, a tennis court, a billiard room, chess and aerobics. No one will be bored and sad. Experienced teachers in a developing club are engaged with young guests. There is a games area on site.





Upon arrival at the Jasmine Beach Hotel (Turkey / Alanya) you will have the opportunity to conduct a course of anti-aging and beauty treatments. People who come to work can comfortably hold business meetings in technically equipped conference rooms. It features internet access, photocopying services and a business center.

Antalya is a great option for family, romantic and youth holidays. This hotel provides all amenities for children and their parents.
