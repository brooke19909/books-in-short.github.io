Loan from Alfa-Bank: reviews, conditions

Alfa-Bank is one of the largest and time-tested banking organizations in the country. Russians trust this company, many take loans here. According to reviews, Alfa-Bank offers really favorable terms for consumer loans. In the article, we consider what requirements this organization makes for borrowers, what it takes to get a loan, and what minimum interest rate a client can count on.

The popularity of Alfa Bank in Russia

Among the large number of banks that provide various financial services to the population, citizens are trying to choose the most reliable company. According to reviews, one of these is Alfa Bank. Evidence of people's trust can be considered not only the annual growth of the deposit portfolio, but also a significant amount of loans.

Conditions in Alfa-Bank are called by many extremely profitable and loyal. Here you can get a money loan without unnecessary references, attracting guarantors and collateral at the minimum interest rate. Alfa-Bank customers also like the quick review of a loan application, and the decision does not depend on whether the potential borrower has insurance or not.

A loan for individuals is easy to arrange and conveniently repay. Payments can be made in a variety of ways, including Internet banking and mobile banking options.

Consumer lending and interest rates

To date, the company offers to issue individuals various types of consumer loans in the amount of not more than 3 million rubles. The maximum loan repayment term, under the terms of Alfa-Bank, cannot exceed five years. The loan conditions and interest rates for different categories of borrowers are different and have their own characteristics. That is why the selection of a loan program is carried out individually.

The higher the loan amount, the less interest you have to pay - this rule applies to all current offers. So you can apply for a loan at Alfa-Bank for a period of one to five years on the following conditions:

  • with the loan amount from 50 thousand to 250 thousand rubles. the rate of 16.99-22.49% applies;
  • registering a loan ranging in size from 250 thousand to 700 thousand rubles, it will have to be repaid at a rate of 14.99-19.49%;
  • for borrowers who borrow from 700 thousand rubles. up to 3 million rubles., a minimum percentage of 10.99%.

Lending conditions for salary card holders and employees of partner companies are not much different. These categories of customers are entitled to count not on three, but on four million rubles in debt, that is, on the maximum amount of a consumer loan at Alfa Bank. Individuals who receive wages on the card of this bank usually get a loan with a better interest rate.

Customer requirements

Alfa-Bank has been cooperating with citizens of the Russian Federation for at least 21 years. Moreover, one of the prerequisites is the presence of a permanent place of work and registration at the place of residence in the region of circulation. The amount of official income and continuous experience in the last position also matter. To apply for a loan at Alfa Bank, you must work at least four months at the last job and receive a salary of at least the minimum subsistence level in the region.

The chances of approval with a tarnished credit history are slim. But for holders of salary cards from this bank, the probability of making a positive decision is higher than for other customers. On a good account, citizens who have previously issued a consumer loan here at Alfa Bank. The interest rate for them is calculated individually. If you believe the reviews, regular customers of the bank often make profitable personal offers.

All existing loan programs are reviewed within one to two business days. In addition, a loan is issued at Alfa Bank without guarantors. The provision of collateral and the purchase of insurance is not a requirement.

Loan without official work

If you meet a number of conditions that the bank puts forward to borrowers, you have every chance to get approval for a loan from Alfa Bank. According to reviews, the company provides loans not only to individuals with official income. Not so long ago, the availability of a permanent source of income was the most important requirement for obtaining a loan, but today most major banks accept applications from the unemployed. However, this does not mean that the revenue will not have to be confirmed at all.

To obtain a loan without formal employment for a minimum package of documents, the client must prove that he has a livelihood. As unofficial sources of income, the bank may accept, for example, royalties and profits from the rental of residential or non-residential real estate, car. All you need is to fill out an application in the form of a bank. For a loan in Alfa-Bank, you can provide the property owned as collateral.

Loan insurance

As already noted, the fact of acquiring insurance or refusing it does not affect the decision when considering a loan application. Insurance is not required for both consumer loans and car loans. At the same time filling out a non-targeted loan, the client has every right to protect himself from certain risks. You can purchase a policy at AlfaStrakhovanie OJSC or from another insurer.

Unlike other commercial banks, Alfa-Bank does not insist on the acquisition of CASCO. At the same time, the company recommends purchasing a policy that will cover all expenses in the event of an insurance event (accident, car theft, theft of additional equipment, etc.). In addition, obtaining credit insurance at Alfa Bank has several other advantages. Having insured himself against risks, the client will receive:

  • more favorable loan conditions;
  • protection against unforeseen expenses in case of damage to the car in an accident or its complete death.

Only those borrowers who conclude a mortgage loan agreement with the bank will not be able to refuse to purchase insurance. If desired, the client can additionally insure his life and health.

To refuse insurance, it is enough to inform Alfa-Bank loan manager about your decision. In the reviews, users indicate the need to submit an application confirming the reluctance to draw up a policy. You can refuse it even after registration by terminating the contract with the insurance company. This can be done by submitting the appropriate application to the insurer's office (the form can be obtained by contacting the employees of the insurance company). However, we must not forget about the terms: according to the law, the insurance premium can be returned only if no more than five working days have passed from the date of the agreement.
