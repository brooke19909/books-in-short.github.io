Interesting crochet patterns. Motives for plaids

It is recommended to knit a square trial pattern when studying each new pattern. Crocheting motifs can also be done on purpose by choosing the most suitable patterns. After collecting an additional amount, connect them into a plaid.

Simple square for a plaid or shawl

Its beauty is to impose a delicate border on a classic basis. Only connect such patterns using small motifs (crochet), squares. Their schemes are as simple as possible.

In a ring of five loops, connect 8 connecting posts. Eight air, two double crochet. Then three times repeat 5 aerial and two double crochet. Complete the row with another column and close it into the third aerial of the first eight.

The next row starts with three loops and two double crochets. They are followed by 5 aircrafts. The row continues with six double crochets. Two repetitions: 5 air and 6 columns. Again five air and 3 double crochet, close the circle.

The last row of the square: 3 air, 4 double crochet. Then 5 air loops and already 10 double crochets. Continue with two repetitions of 5 air and 10 columns. Complete the row with 5 air, 5 columns and close it in the third air beginning of the row.

Openwork round pattern

A ring of six loops is the basis of crocheting such a pattern. Motives can then be different. One of the options is presented below.

The first row must be filled with 15 columns with two yarn, alternating with one air. They will need 4 lifting loops. In the second row in each aerial and top of the column tie a single crochet. Here you will need 2 air to lift.

The third row consists of a double crochet in each third loop. They will need to be alternated with three air loops. The rise in this row is three air.

In the fourth, each arch is the basis for four columns with two double crochets finished with one loop. Between them, the space is filled with 4 air. Rise - 3 loops.

Rise - two loops. The fifth row is crocheted with crochets. The first arch is filled with the 5th, from the last dial 11 air loops. Attach it to the first and tie it with 18 columns, making two loops for lifting. The next arch is knitted with just five columns. Alternate this pattern to the end of the row.

One more square

Its pattern is the most common of those that are recommended in order to crochet a plaid of motifs. The scheme may vary slightly, but the basis is the same: alternating double crochet and air columns.

On a ring of 5 loops, connect the first row of the motive: 8 connecting posts. The second starts from the corner. It consists of 6 air loops, a double crochet at the base of the dialed chain. In the second column of the previous row, connect two columns with a crochet and between them one air. The third column will be the basis of the second corner, which is knitted from two columns and three air loops between them. In each even, repeat the pattern described for the second, and in the odd - for the third. So it turns out the corner.

Third row. The beginning is three lifting loops from the middle of the first corner. In the same arch: two double crochet posts. Air loop. Then comes the repeating pattern. In the side arch formed by one air loop, knit three columns with a crochet. Air loop. Into the corner arch: three double crochet, three aerial, three double crochet. Air loop.

The fourth row is similar to the third, only the double crochet need to be done in 4 and not to make air between them, except for the corners. In the middle of the corner elements, there should still be 3 air elements.

The last row of such a crochet pattern (motifs continue) consists of connecting posts. In each connection of the groups of columns and the top of the corners make a picot. That is, knit three air loops and close them to the initial one. The motives for these pico are connected.





Triangular pattern

They are performed similar to crocheted square motifs. With schemes you only need to work a little. Namely, to make the sides a little longer, and only three corners will be obtained.

By the way, the filling of the corner patterns should be slightly denser so that the picture looks neater. Pico will need to be knitted only in the corners of the motive. Because the sides are well adjacent to each other and without them. Although for airiness they can still be performed.





Volumetric flower pattern

A great gift for a girl is a plaid of squares, the basis of which are such flower motifs (crochet). Their schemes are simple, but the effect is amazing.





Start again in a ring of 6 loops. In the first row, you need to make 8 groups, which are formed by five double crochets. And you need to knit them from one base and close with one loop. Between them, connect one air loop.

The second row - these are the same groups, only you need to knit them twice as often. To the top of the previous one and to the air. So they will turn out twice as much.

The third row is necessary to design a motive in a square. First, connect the first corner: three loops, two double crochet posts, two loops, three double crochet posts - all in one airy flower. The next two double crochet. Then two single crochets. Continue to knit in the same way, gradually forming the remaining three corners.

Crochet a square motif with single crochet columns. In the corners make two air loops.
