Hotel reviews (Greece): choose the best hotel for your vacation

Many Russian tourists are happy to relax in the resorts of Greece. Most vacationers have only positive impressions of a trip to this beautiful original country, but there are those who don‚Äôt like something. Recently, it has been instituted to leave reviews about the hotel where you visited. This is done so that the following tourists who want to book this or that hotel know what to count on, relying not on the pictures provided by tour operators, but on real facts. This article provides hotel reviews (Greece). After reading, you can draw your own conclusions, choose a suitable place to relax based on generalized material.





The main resorts of Greece

The best Greek resorts are located on the largest islands of the country. Geography experts can easily list the main ones - Lindos, Kardamen, Chania, Kafelos, etc. The most popular resort cities in Greece are Kos, Halkidiki, Samos, Rhodes, Mykonos, Corfu and others.





Briefly about hotels of Greece

Tourists who have been to Turkey more than once know that Greek hotels differ from each other not by starryness, but by category. A five-star hotel is equal to the Delux category, 4-star - to category A, 3-star - to category B, 2-star - to category C. Hotels B and C are suitable for tourists who prefer an excursion vacation. They are usually small, comfortable and clean. Such institutions have everything necessary for a comfortable stay - air conditioning, TV, shower, etc. Every second tourist leaves reviews of hotels in Greece, after reading them, you can easily figure out the categories of hotels.

Beach hotels are not inferior in quality to the rest. On the coast, you can stay both in the luxurious Deluxe Hotel and in a simple category B hotel - a three-ruble note.

Having settled in the same hotel with a youth disco, you can expect that in the next one there will certainly be a large restaurant with national cuisine or an aqua park. More information about the hotels of the most popular resorts in Greece can be found below.





Rhodes Island Hotels

Rhodes Island is chosen as a vacation destination by tourists who like a pleasant and comfortable Mediterranean climate. The sun shines here almost all year round, so our compatriots often hide from the Russian cold in Rhodes. As for hotels, there are a lot of them. Most often, hotels of 4 and 5 stars are found, therefore, more affluent tourists are more likely to visit the resort. Of course, there are hotels for a budget holiday, but their number is much smaller. Service and maintenance of hotels are at a high level, especially welcome here tourists from Russia. Hotel reviews (Greece) are constantly replenished with the positive impressions of our compatriots, who are satisfied with the work of Russian-speaking guides and hotel staff.

For a family vacation, it is better to go to Faliraki, Lardos or Lahany. The best hotels are Lindian Viliage5 deluxe (Lardos), Esperides Beach 4 * and Grecotel Rhodos Royal 4 * (Faliraki). If you plan to visit hotels in Greece with children under three years old, then you should go to the resorts of Kallithea and Kalimbria. According to tourists, the best children's hotels are Aldemar Paraduse Mare (5 stars), Irene Palace Hotel (4 stars) and Aldemar Paradise Village (5 stars).

For young people, the best resort will be the resort of Faliraki. Every second hotel offers young tourists and students a varied entertainment program. Music on the streets of the resort does not subside until the morning, so it is just suitable for those who plan to spend their holidays or vacations vigorously.





The island has a place for romance. Couples in love can relax in the hotels of the resorts of Ixia, Faliraki and Kalafos. Tourists who spent their honeymoon there or just a joint vacation, leave only positive reviews about hotels in Greece (Rhodes).

Crete Hotels

Crete is considered the most popular of all resort areas in Greece. Hundreds of thousands of tourists rest there every year. In addition to the famous architectural and cultural masterpieces, Crete attracts travelers with high quality service and hotel services. The developed hotel infrastructure does not leave indifferent any guest of the resort. Sunny Crete is suitable for both family and for a noisy and fun holiday with a large company. The island has all the conditions for both some and others. In some you will find a quiet and relaxing atmosphere of Greek relaxation, in others - loud music and a lot of entertainment until the morning. According to vacationers' reviews, we can conclude that the hotel chain in Crete is distinguished by the quality of the services provided, courtesy and courtesy of the staff. Apparently, therefore, the island of Crete is so popular among lovers of Greek tourism.





Kos Hotels

As for holidays on the island of Kos, here tourists are forced to choose a hotel from a small number, since there are not so many hotels. This can be explained by the small territory of the island, so just physically the island cannot have a lot of hotels. But despite this, there are both inexpensive hotels and fashionable luxury hotels.

On the banks isolated from the noisy streets and crowded resorts, there are quiet beach-style hotels for families to relax. Also, the hotels of the Dodecanese archipelago are happy to shelter couples in love who want to be alone.

Guests often leave positive reviews about hotels in Greece (Kos). Basically, travelers select several hotels and advise others to stay there. This is the Blue Lagoon hotel complex, which pleases guests with silence, quality service and impeccable service; Neptune Hotels Resort and Gregotel Kos Imperial Thalasso. The listed hotels are the best on the whole island. You can relax on the highest level with due magnificence right there.

Halkidiki Hotels

Halkidiki resort attracts travelers with its unusual geographical position - the peninsula resembles a trident, on each coast of which there are famous cities - Athos, Kassandra, Sithonia.

Most often, tourists with children relax here, a calm atmosphere and silence are the main reasons for the popularity of family holidays on the island. Travelers, leaving reviews of hotels in Greece (Halkidiki), note the fact that almost all hotels are located away from the noisy center - on the coast. Some tourists were embarrassed by the fact that they had to go to shops and restaurants, but most of the walk along the picturesque and green peninsula brought maximum pleasure.

The clear leader in the number of positive reviews is the hotel hotel complex Potidea Palace Bomo Club. The four-star hotel located in Sithonia welcomes tourists with children with hospitality, as well as large and noisy companies.





Corfu Hotels

"Emerald" island is suitable for an elite and respectable vacation. He got this name because of the large number of green spaces - lemon and orange groves and cypress trees. De Luxe hotels annually meet wealthy tourists who can afford to relax in a big way. Of course, there are more budget options on the island - bungalows and youth camps.

Those who do not skimp and are ready to pay a considerable amount of money for their vacation, tourists recommend staying at the Grecotel Corfu Imperial Exclusive Resort, as well as at the Grecotel Eva Palace or the Kontokali Bay Resort & Spa. Hotel complexes are located on the coast of the Ionian Sea, quiet bays allow you to relax here with the whole family. Hotel reviews (Greece) Corfu in the absolute majority are also positive.

Mykonos Hotels

Mykonos has always been considered a bohemian vacation spot, so the price tag here is quite high. The number of hotels on the island is small, so it is better to book a room in a few months. As for the category, then mainly 4 and 5 star hotels prevail here, but you can find more budget options.

Samos Hotels

Samos Island attracts tourists with its calm and clean beaches and the opportunity to spend your holidays in private. Samos hotels are located near the mountain villages that tourists can visit if desired. General reviews of hotels in Greece-Samos are often positive, but there are also negative ones. For example, some vacationers in Samos definitely did not like the pebble beach or the descent to the sea. But this can not be called a serious drawback, because everyone chooses a hotel based on their preferences and desires. As for the service, almost all Samos hotels boast a high level of service.





General recommendations for finding the best hotel

The first thing you need to pay attention to when choosing a hotel is the number of hotel stars. A 4-star hotel is not always worse than a five-star one. Often the owners of such hotels simply do not pursue status, but the service and entertainment there are on the top five. Vacationers from Russia should not forget that Greece mainly relies on European tourists, so special attention should be paid to the presence of Russian-speaking staff at the hotel. It certainly will not work to meet compatriots in Crete hotels - Grecotel Amirandes, Domes of Elounda or Kalimera Kriti. It is better to book hotels in advance. Almost all Greek hotels operate on an all-inclusive basis; the optimal range of services can be found at Candia Park Village (Crete), etc.

Reviews of tourists about hotels in Greece are different, so before you travel you need to check with the tour operator about the availability of certain services that are most important to you.
