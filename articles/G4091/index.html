The Briza Beach Resort Khao Lak (Khao Lak / Thailand): tourists photos and reviews

Thailand is a country that attracts a huge number of tourists from different parts of the world with its attractions and original places to relax. On its beaches you can often meet tourists from Russia who appreciate an upscale vacation in a pleasant environment.

The Briza Beach Resort Khao Lak is one of the places that vacationers love to visit. As they mark their reviews, this resort offers some of the best conditions for living among all recreation areas in Thailand.

So, what features are inherent in this place, and what is offered to travelers here? About it further.



general information

A huge number of tour operators in different regions of Russia recommend The Briza Beach Resort Khao Lak 4 * as a vacation spot, perfect for a family vacation, as well as for a romantic trip. The hotel was built in 2005. Since then, a number of small repairs have been carried out on its territory, aimed at maintaining the general condition of the place for rest. Tourists, staying here, note the excellent conditions in the apartments, buildings and public institutions that are available at The Briza Beach Resort Khao Lak.

Location

The hotel is located in the small village of Khao Lak, which is located in the province of Phang Nga, a widely known among Russian tourists. According to travelers, a rather mild climate reigns in this territory, allowing guests of the beaches not to feel the heat, but simply to enjoy it.

The Briza Beach Resort & SPA Khao Lak 4 * is located on the first coastline of the Andaman Sea, which pleases its guests with white and soft sand, as well as the cleanliness of the beaches.

As for the location of relatively important transport hubs, the nearest international airport, to which all tourists arrive, is located at a distance of 80 km from the hotel. That is why the place for rest without fail includes in the price of the tour payment for the transfer service. In reviews of The Briza Beach Resort Khao Lak 4 *, tourists say that such a service is provided in a very comfortable car that quickly delivers to the destination.



Rooms

In total, the fund of the hotel's apartments consists of 181 rooms, each of which is decorated in the best possible way. In apartments of any class, only high-quality and modern furniture is used, some of its elements were made to individual orders.

Each room has an individual bathroom, which is equipped with modern fixtures, as well as furniture for storing bathrobes, slippers, and towels provided to tourists. In addition, each guest of the hotel receives a set of bath accessories, including without fail shower gel, soap and shampoo. The bathroom also has a hairdryer for drying hair.

In all apartments of The Briza Beach Resort & SPA Khao Lak 4 *, tourists are offered the opportunity to use free Internet access, so that they can constantly stay in touch with their relatives and friends. In addition, guests can always order a safe and a minibar in the room. The assortment of the latter, in case of use, is regularly replenished with new soft drinks that do not contain alcohol.

All rooms at The Briza Beach Resort Khao Lak are divided into two groups, depending on the proposed conditions: deluxe and grand deluxe.

Deluxe

The official description of the rooms of The Briza Beach Resort Khao Lak 4 * deluxe category contains several of their types, depending on what type of view opens from the windows of the apartments: the sea with a beach, a bright flowering garden or pool. As the guests note, each picture is beautiful in its own way, but most of them prefer to admire the view of the vast expanses of the Andaman Sea.

The interior of the apartment is mainly dominated by a combination of light shades, which have a relaxing holiday. Here you can see a large number of light textiles, the presence of which makes the overall picture of the interior more sophisticated. The deluxe rooms have a large balcony on which guests can take sunbaths - for this purpose a pair of sun loungers and a table are set between them.

As for furniture, in many photos of The Briza Beach Resort Khao Lak one can often notice the presence of huge beds in the apartments of the hotel, which are installed in each room. One such berth is designed for a couple of guests who value comfort and coziness. Near the bed there are two small cabinets for storing guests' small accessories, they are placed on both sides. The huge wardrobe installed in the apartment is set to ensure the proper safety of guests' clothes, and the relaxation area, represented by a pair of small armchairs and a table, is designed for morning or evening tea drinking.

If desired, hotel guests staying in deluxe apartments can watch satellite channels broadcast on a plasma TV, which is mounted in one of the walls. Many vacationers from Russia note in their comments that viewing of Russian-language channels is possible here.



Grand deluxe

Grand Deluxe rooms offer a stay of 54 square meters. m, which is visually divided into two spaces: a bedroom and a living area. The interior design also uses a combination of light shades that look harmonious with each other. The central place in the furniture set is occupied by a large bed for two, which in some apartments is replaced by two single beds. Guests on vacation can put their personal belongings and sets of clothes in a large wardrobe, as well as in cabinets on either side of the bed.

For the convenience of guests, this class of apartments has a dressing table with a mirror, as well as a seating area near a huge window, which offers panoramic views of the picturesque surroundings.

Guests staying in a grand deluxe room can also be in the large living room of the apartment, where there is a furniture set consisting of a large soft sofa and a pair of armchairs, as well as a low coffee table. Opposite this kit, on the wall, is a huge plasma TV with a large selection of channels.



Nutrition

Reviews of tourists at The Briza Beach Resort Khao Lak often state that the catering establishments located on its territory serve excellent dishes that will appeal to even the most sophisticated gourmets. In total, there are three large bars and two restaurants. The bars offer a wide selection of drinks, cocktails, as well as fresh fruits and light salads.

Fans of higher cuisine prefer to visit restaurants, in one of which a daily buffet is served for guests who live and eat according to the all-inclusive concept. As for the time of their work, the Panali restaurant invites guests into its walls from 6 a.m. to 11 p.m., and Maya works exclusively in the evening - from 18-00, in addition, this restaurant is open only in winter, when the resort is open a large influx of tourists.

In the reviews about the cuisine of these establishments, they say that all the dishes served here are prepared by upscale chefs whose experience affects the quality of the prepared food. The service here is also at a fairly high level - in restaurants and bars they are very respectful to each client.

Conditions for children

The description of The Briza Beach Resort Khao Lak very often says that it is ideal for a family vacation. These statements are due to the fact that in such a place for recreation there are conditions for young guests who can come with their parents. Tourists talk about some of them in their comments left after a vacation in Thailand.

First of all, vacationers turn their attention to the fact that the hotel has a small mini-club in which children of any age can play within the permitted category (from 4 to 12 years). Here the nannies and animators spend time with him, who look after the kids, and also entertain them in various ways.

In the catering establishments of The Briza Beach Resort Khao Lak 4 * there is an opportunity to seat your child in a separate chair, which will be most comfortable for him - the child seats that are available in restaurants were developed by special order.

If desired, parents can send their child to the aquatic zone, where he can have fun in two pools equipped with all the safety requirements of the child. Each of them has a coating that prevents slipping, the depth of the niches is small, which is also an indicator of safety.



For business

The hotel often stops people whose life is connected with doing business. It often happens that they have to solve business issues right in the building of The Briza Beach Resort Khao Lak. The services of this hotel include such an opportunity as renting a conference room, which is located in one of the buildings. According to those who used this opportunity, this hall is perfect for holding meetings with business partners, presentations, conferences, meetings, as well as other events of a similar nature.

Guests note that the room is well equipped in terms of technology. There is modern lighting and sound equipment, laptops, projectors, microphones, as well as tablets and a considerable number of office supplies that can be used if necessary.

Aquazone

A huge number of adult guests, as well as their children, love to spend free time in the aquazone area, which they often write about in their comments and reviews. The hotel has one very large pool with clean water. Around it is a sunny terrace on which vacationers can take sun loungers installed under umbrellas. Within the aquazone there is also a large bar where you can always drink soft drinks or a little snack.



SPA

The SPA zone in the hotel attracts a lot of attention of travelers, since, according to them, only true professionals work in it, who know a lot about relaxation and cosmetic procedures.

The spa consists of an outdoor massage area, which can only be reached by appointment and for a fee. According to vacationers, professional massage therapists work here who know the secret technology of immersing the client in a state of complete relaxation.



In addition to the massage area, the hotel has a jacuzzi. This part is a separate room in which a huge font with hydromassage is installed. You can also visit here for a small fee, which, according to vacationers, is affordable for everyone.

Sport

Fans of an active lifestyle prefer to visit places where you can seriously take care of your health and figure, even while on vacation. For this purpose, The Briza Beach Resort Khao Lak has a huge sports hall, in which exercise machines are designed to work with different muscle groups. In order to visit it, you should pay a small one-time fee.

Fans of active team games can visit the indoor soccer field located in the garden, as well as play beach volleyball at another venue.

Beach

The description of The Briza Beach Resort Khao Lak 4 * indicates that the hotel does not have its own plot on the coastline. However, this fact does not frighten tourists away at all, since there is a magnificent city beach in walking distance from the place of rest, where all guests go. Its territory is quite clean and picturesque, there is a bar where you can cool off with a cocktail.

Throughout the beach, you can sit on your own towel or on a rented sun lounger, under an umbrella, which can also be rented.

Prices

The cost of rest at the hotel attracts a large number of travelers. In their reviews, they often share the sums of their costs. The average price of a ticket to this hotel is about 150,000 rubles. This amount provides for an eight-day vacation for two, in conditions of increased comfort, on an all-inclusive basis.
