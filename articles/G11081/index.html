The pleasure of a country holiday in the center of Tolyatti - "Alpen Park"

An oasis has appeared in the center of Tolyatti for relaxation of body and soul. This is an amazing combination of a recreation center outside the city and a comfortable urban home. This recreation complex is called - Alpen Park ("Alpine Park").



Guest Accommodation

In Togliatti, Alpen Park invites you to relax in one-story cozy chalets. They are designed for a small company. You can choose a chalet with two and three bedrooms. Their area is 61 m 2 and 55 m 2 respectively. As well as a cottage of special comfort. Its area is 113 m 2 .

There are separate apartments for guests with disabilities. As well as large two-story cottages that can simultaneously accommodate up to 15 people. They have several rooms and a large covered terrace with pool or garden views. It is equipped with upholstered furniture and a large dining table with comfortable benches.

Each room has a modern cable TV. As well as a dining room with kitchen area. It has a refrigerator, stove, microwave, electric kettle and other items necessary for cooking (including barbecue facilities and dinnerware sets).





Leisure

Rest in Togliatti, in the hotel complex "Alpen Park" - is walking in the woods, playing football, volleyball and basketball in well-equipped areas. The administration of the base took care of guests in case of inclement weather. It offers vacationers a choice of many board games. It can be "Mafia", "Monopoly" (for children and adults), "Crocodile" (Alias), etc. And also there are halls with board games: lotto, checkers, backgammon, chess, dominoes. And there is an open court.

On the territory of the base there is a real Russian bath. Her rent is paid separately.

Young guests will find a game town with various swings, carousels and other attractions.

Tourists having a rest in Alpen Park (Tolyatti) leave good reviews with good wishes (the complex is still being completed) and a promise to return. Our guests especially love summer vacations. Perhaps because in the warmer months, an aquacenter - Aqua Land. Rest in it is included in the price.

Leisure facilities

The recreation complex in Tolyatti, Alpen Park, registers guests from 14.00, and check-out is made at noon. Upon check-in, a security deposit of 3,000 rubles will be charged.

To rent a chalet, you need to present an identification card, and for children under 14 years of age - a birth certificate with a document of registration at the place of residence.

Upon completion of registration, the guest receives the keys to the house and a guest card. It is possible to extend the rental of the chalet (unless it is booked by the following guests).

In the rooms of the recreation center (Tolyatti, Alpen Park), cleaning is carried out once every three days. At the same time, housekeeping staff changes towels and bedding sets.

General cleaning takes place once a week. It includes washing windows, cleaning blinds, carpets, upholstered furniture, etc.

Smoking in the chalet is strictly prohibited, as is the transport of pets.

When checking in, all guests are warned about the 24-hour outdoor video surveillance system.

Vacationers who have repeatedly violated the rules of residence are entered in the stop list and evicted.

The best recreation complex in Tolyatti, Alpen Park, offers its guests free parking spaces in a guarded parking lot.





City walks

Holidays in Tolyatti can be diversified by various excursions. An unusually interesting place in the city is the Museum of Prototypes of AvtoVAZ LLC. This is a huge exhibition pavilion, which belongs to the Technical Museum of Togliatti. At the site, guests are presented with exclusive models produced by the plant. Moreover, some of them are only in a single copy. Most of the exhibited models have never been used. Among the exhibits there are also special models made by VAZ. This, for example, Lada Sport, Lada Turbo, the first Kalina and others.

Journey to the Middle Ages

The recreation center "Alpen Park" helps in organizing excursions to the castle of Garibaldi. It is located in the village of Khryashchevka, near Togliatti. Garibaldi Castle is the future tourist complex. All of its buildings are stylized under neo-Gothic European themes, as are the already installed statues.

Since 2014, the castle complex has been open for excursions and photo shoots. From the observation deck offers stunning views of the bend of the Volga.
