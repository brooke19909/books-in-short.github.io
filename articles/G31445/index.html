Smartphone "Lenovo A536": owner reviews, review, specifications, photos

Now we look at all the details of the Lenovo A536 smartphone: reviews, specifications and features. This device is notable for its accessibility and simplicity, which ensured its success.

Appearance



So, here is the Lenovo A536 smartphone. We decided to start the review with the design of this device. This candy bar can be compared in size to the A606 model, which has a similar screen diagonal, but our hero is a little longer. Here, preference was given to gloss.

Let's look at the opinion of people who already use the Lenovo A536 smartphone; reviews suggest that the back panel is turning into a set of fingerprints. Of course, users are not happy with this outcome. In the sample we tested, the panel creaked slightly on the right side. Probably, we got a slightly special Lenovo A536 smartphone - owners reviews do not mention this drawback. There are no unpleasant sounds that could come from the plastic panel .

The case is decorated with a silver decorative border surrounding the front of the device. The front camera is located above the screen, as, in fact, a speaker, proximity sensor and logo. Below are the basic keys: "Back", "Functions" and "Home" with very clear notation.





Let's see what people who have been using the Lenovo A536 smartphone for a long time think of functional buttons. Customer reviews among the shortcomings here note only the lack of keyboard backlight. On the right side of the side panel are the volume keys and the screen lock. The microphone in this model is placed down; at the top, you can connect the cable to the micro USB connector, as well as the headphones to the 3.5 mm jack .

Ergonomics

Before us is the angular glossy and large smartphone Lenovo A536. Owner reviews indicate that this combination can not be attributed to the most convenient option for the palm of an ordinary user. There really are difficulties. Glossy plastic always strives to slip away from the user to freedom. Taking the phone with one hand, you need to hold it tight so that he does not inadvertently meet the floor. At the same time, it is difficult to control it in this position, the device is too big for such exercises. It is prudently provided for a small hole that allows you to conveniently remove the back panel.

Interface

The smartphone runs on Android 4.4.2, while supplemented by a proprietary shell authorship of Lenovo. There is no second menu with application icons, all content is added to a single screen, where it is sorted by catalogs, which can be supplemented with various widgets at any time. The device is in operation

The smartphone has a 1.3 GHz MTK6582M chip, complemented by the Mali-400 MP graphics subsystem. The phone does not freeze, but there are slight slowdowns that do not affect the work. The amount of RAM is 1 gigabyte. The permanent memory here is 8 GB. The microSD standard is supported. The maximum allowable memory card capacity is 32 GB, all devices have it.

In the Ice Storm test, the smartphone scored 2,863 units. Based on the ratio of performance and price, the device looks very attractive. Here the animation is smooth, it is noticeable when working in browsers, in the menu and when watching a video. Minor twitches do not overshadow the user's life, so the smartphone has earned a positive rating.





We tried the following games: Minion Rush, Dead Trigger, and Asphalt 8. The phone experienced light difficulties with only one of these applications, and it handled the rest perfectly. The sound of the speaker is decent, it will be easy to hear the ringtone. Camera

The phone creates photos with a quality of 5 million points. There is also a flash and autofocus. On the screen you can see the notation of the selected parameters, as well as a couple of large buttons for shooting video and photos. You can adjust ISO (100-1600), aspect ratio (16: 9 or 4: 3), timer, white balance, color effects, exposure, HDR and geotagging. Thematic plots are also available: action, fireworks, snow, beach, theater, landscape, portrait, party, sunset, night, cars.

Implemented the possibility of slow motion for a video camera. The user can change the resolution (QVGA - Full HD). Shooting starts from the menu, although a highlighted key on the lock display would not hurt. At the same time, during the creation of the photo, the A536 behaves not in the best way. The pictures are faded. Not bad with shooting text, but the device adds artistic blur. Frames are fuzzy, details disappear, the image is even more distorted in low light. At the same time, the flash proved to be on the good side. If we talk about video, it is possible to shoot Full HD at a speed of 30 frames. There is sound recording and electronic stabilization. The front camera is 2 megapixels. To take self-portraits, this is enough, for video communications - even with a margin.

Wireless interfaces





The smartphone supports GPS, as for the speed of determining the position, it will take about 20-30 seconds in the city limits in an open area. With Bluetooth Wi-Fi, everything is fine, the modules work stably, without failures. There is a pair of slots for SIM-cards. As for the standby mode, calls with it without difficulty arrive at each number. Receiving and sending messages is also good. HSDPA technology is also supported. There are no complaints about the operation of this communication channel.
