Kazan hotels in the center near the Kremlin: where to stay for city guests?

It is not for nothing that experts call Kazan a unique city: East and West are not just meeting here, they are harmoniously combined, creating at the same time a special atmosphere that attracts travelers from many cities and countries like a magnet. Everyone who comes to this city is primarily interested in the Kremlin, on whose territory there are significant sights of the city. That is why Kazan hotels in the center near the Kremlin are in special demand. In addition, other important historical and architectural sites of the city are easily accessible from here.

Sights of the Kazan Kremlin

The Kremlin in Kazan is a whole complex of significant facilities. First of all, it is the cathedrals - the architectural and cultural value of the city. One of them serves as the place of work of the President of Tatarstan, therefore, during important meetings, the neighboring buildings are patrolled by special services. Located on the Kremlin territory Spasskaya Tower - the oldest building of the complex, built by Pskov masters. Kul Sharif Mosque is nearby, and Annunciation Cathedral is nearby. According to an ancient legend, the place for the construction of the cathedral was chosen by Ivan the Terrible himself after he took the city.



From the windows of some hotels in Kazan in the center next to the Kremlin, you can admire the inclined spire of the Syuyumbike tower - another unique building. Historians claim that the tower was built at the same time as the Annunciation Cathedral. There is a legend that Grozny saw a very beautiful girl and wished to marry her, but she rejected his offer. Then the king ordered in just a week to build a tower and place an obstinate girl in it. When they brought her to the tower, she rushed down from the top tier so as not to become the wife of the formidable king.
