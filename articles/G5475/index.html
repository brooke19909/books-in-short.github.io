Hotel Dessole Sea Lion Beach Resort Spa 4 * (photo)

If you plan to spend a vacation in Vietnam and are looking for a comfortable hotel with an excellent beach and at an affordable price, then we recommend that you consider the Dessole Sea Lion Beach Resort Spa 4 * (Nha Trang) as an option for accommodation.



Photo, location, description

This hotel is new. So, it was opened in April 2014. Own area is 50 thousand square meters. meters. The hotel complex is located on the sea coast, in the Nha Trang region, near Nha Trang. The nearest airport can be reached in just 10 minutes. And the center of Nha Trang is half an hour away.

The Dessole Sea Lion Beach Resort Spa 4 * housing stock, the photo of which is presented in this article, consists of 300 standard and family rooms. The apartments are located in the main 12-story building and eight one-story bungalows. The windows of the vast number of rooms offer wonderful views of the sea. All apartments are comfortably furnished, there is an LCD TV with cable channels, air conditioning, a minibar, a bathroom with a hairdryer. Free internet access is available throughout the hotel.

Dining at the hotel is organized according to the ol inclusive system. The local restaurant offers guests excellently prepared dishes of both international and Vietnamese cuisine.

The hotel has sports grounds, two swimming pools, bars, parking, spa, gym, shop, laundry.





Dessole Sea Lion Beach Resort Spa 4 *: traveler reviews from Russia

Today, many tourists, when planning a trip, try very carefully to study the reviews of people who have already visited a particular place. This helps to make a picture more complete and closer to the real state of things. But hardly anyone would want to experience disappointment upon arriving on a long-awaited vacation. So we decided to save you time and energy and to offer generalized comments from Russian tourists staying at this hotel. Immediately, we note that almost all guests in the comments note that they were not disappointed in their choice. But we will understand everything in more detail.

Housing stock

Almost all tourists in their reviews note that they were very pleased with the rooms they provided in this hotel. So, according to our compatriots, all the apartments here are quite spacious, stylishly decorated, clean, bright, with a new renovation, excellent furniture and plumbing. In the bathrooms there are stocks of body gel, shampoo, soap, toothbrush. Cleaning was carried out daily, and they did it in a quality manner, unlike many other hotels. Towels were renewed several times a week, and bed linen once a few days. Maids also replenished drinks in the minibar and soap products in the bathroom as needed. A small minus was considered by some guests the fact that many balconies have outdoor air conditioning units from neighboring rooms, which creates some noise. However, as many tourists note in their reviews, nothing is heard in the apartments. The only thing that may not be very comfortable is sitting on the balcony, but you can do without it. In addition, a number of guests note that due to the warm air leaving the unit, the laundry dries very quickly.





Location, Territory

As for the own territory of Dessole Sea Lion Beach Resort Spa 4 * (Vietnam), then, according to our compatriots, it is quite large and very well-groomed. Here you can take a pleasant walk and find great places for a photo shoot.

Many tourists also liked the location of the hotel. According to them, despite the fact that the hotel is located outside the city and there is nothing interesting nearby, the sea and the beach here are much better than in Nha Trang. In addition, the surrounding area is much cleaner. If you want to go to Nha Trang, you can do this using the shuttle service provided by the hotel. So, the bus goes to the city three times a day. On the road, according to the guests, it takes 30-40 minutes.





Nutrition

Unlike many other hotels in Vietnam and other countries, Dessole Sea Lion Beach Resort Spa 4 * did not disappoint its guests in terms of food. So, our compatriots note that the food here is very tasty and very diverse. According to them, the menu always has superbly prepared dishes of fish and seafood, as well as salads, snacks, side dishes, fruits. Many guests mention excellent local pastries and desserts. So be mentally prepared to gain a couple of extra pounds during your vacation. On Fridays, an evening of Vietnamese cuisine takes place here. According to family tourists, their children also always had something to eat.

Beach vacation

This hotel, according to guests, boasts a magnificent beach. The sand here is very small, pleasant for the feet. The entry into the water is also sandy, gentle. So tourists with children will be very comfortable. The beach is equipped with a sufficient number of sunbeds for all comers with mattresses and sun umbrellas. There is also a bar with a wide selection of drinks for every taste. Water sports equipment can also be rented on the beach. However, according to our compatriots, the prices for this service are unreasonably high.





Entertainment at the hotel

On the territory of the hotel complex there are many opportunities for excellent relaxation. So, tourists really liked the hotel pools. There are two of them. One pool is located next to the bungalow, it has a section for children. Families with young children prefer to relax here. The main pool is always noisy and fun. Water aerobics is held here, guests play water polo. Both pools have sunbeds with sun loungers and parasols. Moreover, there are many sun loungers, and, according to our compatriots, there is no need to occupy them early in the morning. There is a bar next to the pools. Here you can have a bite to eat and enjoy an alcoholic or soft drink.

Animators work all day at the hotel. According to travelers, they do their job well. So, the animators will offer you to do gymnastics, aerobics, play volleyball, water polo, take part in fun contests, learn fun dance moves and much more. So no one will be bored here.
