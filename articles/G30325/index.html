Homemade toilet paper flower

Even from toilet paper you can make beautiful flowers that will surprise you with their grandeur and variety. For work you need only toilet paper and threads. There are several color models that are especially easy to make from improvised material.

What flowers can be made from toilet paper

Even when using material such as toilet paper, you can get beautiful and spectacular products. Especially wonderful are the flowers.

What flowers from toilet paper can be made quickly and easily:

  • Roses.
  • Peonies.
  • Tulips
  • Carnations.
  • Lilies

The buds of these flowers are simply made of light material. In this case, the flowers are obtained externally identical to natural samples.

What tools and materials are needed for manufacturing

The easiest version of a toilet paper flower is a carnation or peony. For the manufacture of buds you will need such tools and materials:

  • Roll of toilet paper. It is better to choose a white three-layer. When working, the canvas will not divide, tear, crease strongly. All elements of the product will lie down smoothly and easily. You can use not only a white base, but also a colored one.
  • We need a regular sewing thread, with the help of which all parts of the bud will be fixed.
  • Flexible wire with soft bristles. Thanks to this option, you can give the flower a more realistic look.
  • Additionally, you can use special inserts that will simulate the middle of the flower.

In the process, scissors are not used, since all the bends, transitions and joints are formed according to the principle of the origami technique. To work with wire, you can use clamps or pliers.

Flower making

A photo of a flower from toilet paper can please with its originality and similarity with natural. Moreover, the manufacturing principle is completely simple.

  • For the production of peonies or cloves, one algorithm is used, but only at the end is processing performed. First you need to fold 6 or more pieces of paper together with an accordion. Band the folded leaflets in the middle with a thread. Spread the resulting petals. If a peony was made, then further processing is not required. If a clove was formed, then you need to tear the tops of the petals slightly with your hands.
  • To make a rose or a tulip, you need to find an origami scheme suitable for yourself in complexity.
  • To make a lily, you need to collect a piece of paper into an accordion. Next, roll up the accordion with a loose tube. On one side, tie a tube. The free edge needs to be straightened.
