Hotels in Vladikavkaz: addresses, description of rooms, reviews

Vladikavkaz is one of the largest cities in the Russian Federation, which is the capital of North Ossetia. Many Russians and tourists from other countries seek to visit it. Where can I stay here? Consider the list of the best hotels in Vladikavkaz.

"Alexander"

Have a desire to relax in complete comfort? Many tourists and residents of the city prefer to stay at the Grand Hotel Alexandrovsky (Vladikavkaz). It is located in a very convenient place - in the central part of the city, where the main attractions are concentrated. Address: 29 Mira Ave. City guests note that the hotel is a 10-minute drive from the train station.

All rooms at the hotel are furnished in the same classic style. In the apartments you can watch a huge amount of soft fabrics, quality furniture, as well as stucco on the walls. It also installed modern technology.

The hotel has a large restaurant, which is famous for its menu, including molecular cuisine. Special attention is drawn to the huge lobby bar, where visitors can enjoy refreshing cocktails and strong drinks.

Grand Hotel Alexandrovsky has a large spa area, consisting of an indoor pool, a bath, a sauna, a beauty salon and a massage room.

The cost of living in a standard room of this hotel in Vladikavkaz is from 5 500 rubles per day.

"Breeze"

Hotel "Veterok" is also very popular among tourists who arrived in Vladikavkaz. Its comfortable rooms offer views of the beautiful garden, the surrounding nature and the mountains.

The guests are offered a small number of rooms, consisting of apartments designed so that any guest in them was pleasant and comfortable to be. New modern furniture is installed here, as well as high-quality appliances. Guests of any type of room have the opportunity to watch TV shows on the plasma screens installed in the apartments. All rooms at the Veterok Hotel have internet access (Wi-Fi) and a private bathroom.

The cost of renting an apartment is from 2,700 rubles per day. Guests note that for the money the guests are provided with excellent conditions, as well as decent service.

The hotel in question is located in Vladikavkaz at 24 Costa Avenue, a 10-minute drive from the city center.

Vladikavkaz

Considering the list of the best hotels located in Vladikavkaz, you should certainly pay attention to the place of the same name for relaxation.

Hotel "Vladikavkaz" (in Vladikavkaz) is a place that offers guests of the city a holiday at the level of three stars. It is located a couple of minutes from the embankment of the Terek River, as well as within walking distance from the city park of Khetagurov.

Arriving at the hotel, guests can settle in large rooms, each of which is equipped with a bathroom. There is furniture designed for relaxation, as well as for labor. All types of rooms have free internet access.

The building of the Vladikavkaz Hotel has its own restaurant, where guests often have lunch and dinner. There are also catering establishments in the district, the journey to them takes about 10 minutes.

Not far from the hotel in question is the railway station, the distance to which is 3 km. Many tourists note in their reviews about this place to stay that you can get to it 10 minutes after arrival by train Moscow-Vladikavkaz.

The lowest cost of renting apartments in this hotel is 2 800 rubles. The hotel "Vladikavkaz" is located at: Kotsoeva street, 75.

Kameliya-v

Kameliya-V is a small hotel that is ready to receive tourists who want to explore the sights of Vladikavkaz. Its room stock is a small number of apartments decorated in a simple style. Guests note that all the furniture installed in the rooms is quite comfortable. In addition, they really like the fact that on the ground floor of the building there is a large bar where you can enjoy light snacks and strong drinks.

The building of the hotel in question has a hall equipped for playing table tennis. Active holidaymakers can rent bicycles at the hotel.

Hotel Kameliya-V is located at Kalinina Street 1.

"Imperial"

Thinking over your vacation in Vladikavkaz, you need to pay attention to the Imperial Hotel, where all fans of a comfortable environment and good service stay. Here, according to the guests, each room has everything you need: an individual bathroom, air conditioning, Wi-Fi, satellite TV, tea and coffee making facilities, as well as modern furniture. Guests note that the hotel staff takes into account all the individual preferences of the guests.

The comments left at the Imperial Hotel say that this place is ideal for relaxing young couples, as the surrounding atmosphere is simply filled with romance. There is a restaurant in the building of the hotel, where you can have a tasty meal at any time.

The cost of daily rent of a room in the place in question is from 6,300 rubles. This price includes not only accommodation, but also continental breakfast in the apartment.

Hotel "Imperial" is located at: Vladikavkaz, Prospekt Mira, 19.

"Pushkinskaya"

Considering the list of the best hotels in Vladikavkaz, you need to pay attention to the hotel "Pushkinskaya", which is located near the city center.

This vacation spot offers its guests excellent conditions. Here you can stay in classic-style rooms with natural wood furniture and lots of fabrics. The hotel offers free Wi-Fi access and 24-hour room service.

The average cost of renting rooms at the Pushkinskaya Hotel is about 3,000 rubles. Guests consider on the positive side that the price includes a daily breakfast.

The hotel is located at: Pushkinskaya street, 8A.

"Cadragon"

In the central part of Vladikavkaz there is one of the best hotels in the city - Kadragon. This vacation spot is within walking distance of city attractions and the central park, where couples and families with children stroll in the evenings.

Guests of the Kadragon Hotel can treat themselves to living in comfortable conditions. Here, almost every room has a huge four-poster bed, as well as expensive natural wood furniture. Wi-Fi access and private bathrooms are available. All units have kitchenware and a fridge. Guests can spend their free time watching satellite channels on plasma TVs.

In the hotel building in Vladikavkaz "Kadragon" there is a large restaurant, where guests often eat. Hotel guests note that excellent cooked food is served here that will surely appeal to even the most demanding gourmets. In addition, the hotel has a large nightclub, meeting room and ticket service.

The minimum cost for renting rooms in this place is 6,000 rubles. It includes a daily breakfast.

The hotel is located at 20a Markova Street.

"Five Stars"

The small hotel "Five Stars", located in Vladikavkaz, allows tourists who wish to visit the city to live in comfortable conditions. According to the guests, all the rooms that are presented in the general fund are decorated quite simply, but tastefully. In the elements of the interior used a large number of soft parts, as well as objects made of wood. The rooms are small in size. Each has a plasma TV, private bathroom, and internet access.

Hotel guests who pre-booked their apartments can book a transfer service from the arrival point. The minimum cost of living at the Five Star Hotel is 1,200 rubles, which the residents of Russia consider to be a rather acceptable indicator. It includes a daily breakfast consisting of food delivered from a local cafe.

Mini-Hotel Five Stars is located at 86 Iristonskaya Street.

Planet Suite

According to the guests, the Planet Lux Hotel (Vladikavkaz) offers very good conditions. This place for relaxation is designed for guests of different groups and offers decent conditions. The Planet Suite is located within walking distance from the Spartak Stadium and a very popular place for walking - Komsomolsky Park.

The rooms of the Planet Lux Hotel are decorated in a classic style with new and modern furniture. All apartments are equipped with appliances, thanks to which any leisure will become more interesting.

Among the main advantages of the Planet Lux hotel, tourists highlight the fact that its building is within walking distance from the train station. As they note, this place can be easily reached on foot, getting off the Moscow-Vladikavkaz train.

Hotel "Planet Lux" is located at: Vatutina street, 50. The cost of renting rooms in it is quite high - from 5,500 rubles per day.

"Hippopotamus"

According to the majority of city dwellers, the Begemot hotel is an inexpensive and quite comfortable place for temporary residence. It is located near the famous Spartak Stadium, almost in the very center of the city.

All rooms of this hotel are small in size and are designed primarily for a couple of guests. In each of them, in addition to the basic furniture necessary for a comfortable stay, there is a plasma TV, air conditioning, as well as a small refrigerator and a private bathroom.

The cost of renting apartments in the Behemoth Hotel (Vladikavkaz) is 1,700 - 2,500 rubles, depending on the degree of their comfort. Guests are pleased that the indicated price includes the cost of the daily breakfast, which is delivered directly to the apartment, which they write about in their reviews.

Hotel "Hippo" is located at: 53, Dzhanaev Street, Vladikavkaz.

Amran

Hotel "Amran" is one of the new places for temporary residence in Vladikavkaz. This hotel offers its guests three types of rooms: deluxe, junior suite and standard. In total, the local fund has 49 apartments, furnished with modern and comfortable furniture, as well as all the necessary equipment.

In the building of the Amran Hotel, guests are offered a conference hall, a restaurant, as well as a huge sports and fitness center, consisting of a thermal zone, a billiard room, a pool. In the restaurant you can taste delicious dishes prepared in the style of Caucasian and European cuisine.

Room rental per day at the hotel is from 1,900 rubles per day. This price includes breakfast.

Amran Hotel is located at 28 Markov Street.

Fiagdon

The Fiagdon Hotel located in Vladikavkaz (Khidikus settlement) is ready to offer excellent conditions for tourists to relax. It is surrounded by magnificent nature and mountains. Its guests have the opportunity to spend a long time in the fresh air, enjoying gatherings for barbecue in the company of old friends or family.

The room fund of the Fiagdon Hotel is small - it consists of 9 apartments. Each room presented in them is decorated in a beautiful loft style with light and expensive furniture, as well as a large amount of light textile. All rooms have free Wi-Fi access, as well as plasma TVs with satellite channels. Tourists who have visited the walls of this hotel talk about the fact that the calm atmosphere prevailing in the rooms sets up a favorable stay.

The Fiagdon Art Hotel has an outdoor parking area, and there is also a small cafe where guests and casual guests can eat.

The initial cost of renting a room in the hotel in question is 6,000 rubles.

Cheapest hotels

Sometimes tourists look for the cheapest option for a place to stay. As practice shows, they can be found in Vladikavkaz. The cost of renting a single room is from 1,100 rubles. Among the hotels that are ready to offer decent, but inexpensive living conditions include: Plasma, Diacris, Altyn, as well as Diamond.

Some travelers prefer to check into hostels, where the price for a bed varies from 450 to 800 rubles. Examples of such establishments are Safar and Hostel One.
