How to check IMEI iPhone? Check iPhone Activation by IMEI

When ordering a new IPhone smartphone from abroad or buying from hand, the future owner often doubts the authenticity of the phone. After all, the market for such gadgets is teeming with counterparts and fakes, which are cheaper, but have nothing to do with a real smartphone. Check IMEI IPhone and carefully examine its appearance - the first thing to do so as not to run into a cheap and low-quality thing. However, it is not always possible to quickly carry out the verification procedure when purchasing, and not everyone knows how to conduct it.

How to identify a fake in appearance



Before verifying the authenticity of the iPhone by IMEI, a preliminary inspection of the device is necessary. Appearance, if the fake is not rough, may not always have significant differences with the original. Also, the complexity is the new IPhone models that are only on sale, and the domestic consumer is not always aware of the appearance details.

In most cases, you can notice some discrepancy in appearance. First of all, look carefully at the configuration and the device itself:
• The original IPhone does not have a retractable antenna, stylus or slot for a memory card.
• The back panel of this Apple device cannot be removed, you cannot see the battery, much less place a SIM card under it (as in standard phones).
• This gadget only works with one SIM card and specially cropped.

In appearance, you can independently distinguish a gross fake from a high-quality original thing. On a real IPhone on the case, you can find an inscription that makes many think that the device is not the original: “Designed by Apple in California. Assembled in China. ” In America, they are only developing the functionality and appearance of the device, and the entire assembly is carried out at factories in China, therefore, despite this inscription, you will get a high-quality device.

How to distinguish from the original IPhone in internal functions



If your appearance does not alert you, then to check the quality of the device, you should turn it on. Look carefully at the menu. Since the Russian language is one of the most difficult, and manufacturers of analogs of expensive phones clearly save on translation, sometimes delusional errors can be seen in menu items and application names.

Go to the AppStore app store, most often the connection fails, or the app store icon throws you to the Google Play Market.

What is IMEI?



Most often, users will learn about the existence of an IMEI code if the phone has been stolen. This is a code that cannot be faked, since it is written in that part of the device’s memory that cannot be formatted. It is absolutely unique for each device, it is possible to find a lost phone through a mobile operator. For owners of expensive models, this is extremely important.

It is also useful when buying to make sure that the box and instructions correspond to the device. So you can exclude the acquisition of stolen equipment. In stolen police databases, it will also be useful to check the IMEI IPhone. You should refrain from buying if a match is found in the database. You can find such a base on the Internet, as this is a common practice with law enforcement agencies and owners of iOS devices.

How to check IMEI IPhone

In order to know in case of loss of the gadget its identifier number, depending on the model, various manipulations must be carried out. The easiest way to find out this number is for owners of the first-generation IPhone, in which it is indicated on the back of the case.
Later, the manufacturer abandoned the practice of applying this code to the case. In subsequent models, the IMEI code, consisting of 15 digits, can be found on the box label and for comparison in one of the menu items or displayed using a special command.

Check IMEI iphone 5s or another model is possible using the command * # 06 # or find it through the menu item "About the device".

How to find out if your device is locked to work with one operator



On the Internet, there are several sites that by identifier number can show the maximum information about the device. For example, it is possible to find out information about locking a device per operator. In order to receive this data, enter the IMEI code on any of the sites that provide these services, and confirm it.

If the status of the phone is indicated as Locked, this means that the device can only be used with SIM cards of one operator. Information about the operator is most often shown below or next.

What is Activation Locked



On devices with iOS 7, a very useful service appeared - activation lock. Currently, more than 80% of users successfully use this option. In order to start using, you will not need anything but to check the IMEI IPhone 5 and see its serial number. On the iCloud portal, you can see the status of the activation lock. If it is turned on, then you need to ask the Apple ID seller and password for access to it in order to remove the block.

If the seller of the gadget cannot provide this information, then you should refrain from buying, as this means that the phone is stolen and blocked by the owner. If you find yourself in a situation where your device is lost, then through iTunes you can currently block it.

How to check smartphone activation date



There is another way to check the data of your smartphone. In order to get to the necessary section of the site to check the status of your smartphone, type in the browser search bar: "Check IMEI IPhone Apple.com". On the site in the field you must enter the 15-digit IMEI and using the manufacturer’s servers the request will be processed. Through this form, you can check IPhone activation by IMEI. If you see “activated” in the column, this means that the smartphone is not new and was previously in use. This is very important, since Apple devices have a worldwide warranty, which is calculated not by the date of sale, but by activation. Gadget lock can be removed with the help of specialists. They can be found via the Internet or in service centers, but you should be afraid of scammers who only take money for unlocking, but do not do the procedure itself. However, the registration date cannot be changed, in the best case, your device will have several months of warranty, and in the worst, this period will completely expire, and if a malfunction occurs, you will not be able to contact the official service.

Thus, when buying, it is worth checking IMEI IPhone Apple to know that the phone:

  • Officially imported into the country.
  • Not a fake.
  • It was not stolen.
  • It was not previously in use.

When buying, you should have on hand a laptop with preinstalled iTunes. With it, IMEI will be immediately received in electronic form, and for verification on the manufacturer’s website you only need to copy and paste it. A fake phone in the official iTunes will not be displayed. If you buy the device by hand, then you should ask the seller to inform IMEI in advance, you can check it at home before making a transaction, and if there are problems, you will refuse to purchase in advance.
