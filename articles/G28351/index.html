Hotel Prague Center Superior 3 * (Prague, Czech Republic): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Prague is not only one of the largest cities in Europe, but also a very popular cultural and historical center among tourists. There are a lot of attractions in the Czech capital , and its infrastructure is developed just fine. Among other things, there are good hotels in Prague that offer their guests good service and comfortable rooms. One of the best budget hotels in the Czech capital is considered to be the Prague Center Superior 3 *.

Hotel location

This hotel is located in the very center of Prague. Wenceslas Square is just a 10-minute walk away. Getting to significant places of the city from its territory is very convenient. The metro station from Prague Center Superior can be reached in just 5 minutes. Tourists have to spend no more than ten minutes on the road to the stops of any other public transport. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are a huge number of restaurants, cafes, bars and, of course, shops. Of course, there is in that part of the city where this hotel is located, and many interesting sights.





General description of the hotel

Represents the hotel Prague Center Superior 3 * 6-storey building dating back to the beginning of the last century. In fact, this house itself is one of the sights of Prague. The service here is offered pretty good. The hotel belongs to a three-star group, and, consequently, its infrastructure is well developed. Both tourists and business travelers or people who come for treatment are choosing this hotel for living in Prague. Judging by the reviews available on the Web, this hotel is perfect for both single guests and people who have come to the Czech Republic for families.

Number of rooms

In total, the Prague Center Superior Hotel (Prague, Czech Republic) offers tourists 67 comfortable rooms. If desired, in this hotel you can rent one of three types of rooms (standard):

  • Double
  • Triple
  • QUAD.

Cable TV is in all rooms of this hotel. Guests staying at the hotel have the opportunity to watch Russian-language channels. Of course, all rooms at this hotel are air-conditioned and heated. The rooms of the hotel Prague Center Superior 3 * and telephones are equipped. Internet (Wi-Fi) in most rooms is free.





The beds in the rooms rented by the hotel are double, new. An in-room safe is available for a fee. Especially for the convenience of residents of the upper floors, the hotel provides an elevator.

Hotel Prague Center Superior: accommodation prices

Cost of renting rooms in this hotel is inexpensive. The price for accommodation in almost all types of apartments is the same. Depending on the number of places in the room, the cost of renting it can be 4500-5000 per night. For a three-star hotel, and even located in the very center of Prague, this is very inexpensive.

Bathrooms

As already mentioned, the Prague Center Superior Hotel belongs to the three-star group. The bathrooms in it are located directly in the rooms. Hotel residents have the opportunity to take a shower at any time convenient for them. Bathrooms in all rooms include towels, shampoo, soap. You can use a hairdryer if you wish. Hotel guests are given bathrobes with slippers. Outer clothing guests have the opportunity to store in the dressing room.

Hotel infrastructure

The Prague Center Superior 3 * hotel offers its guests not only comfortable rooms, but also convenient service. If necessary, guests of this hotel have the opportunity to use:

  • Internet terminal
  • two rooms designed for meetings (for 60 and for 16 people);
  • summer garden.

The reception at this hotel is open 24 hours a day. Also at the hotel, if necessary, you can exchange currency. Under the car, the hotel provides parking. However, you can leave the car on it only for a fee (approximately 20 euros). Agree on the possibility of parking the car is in advance. By the way, you can rent a car directly at the hotel.





Most tourists wash dirty clothes right on site. Especially for this, the hotel has a large laundry room. The hotel also has a meeting room.

Dining at the hotel

Breakfast at the Prague Center Superior 3 * hotel (Czech Republic, Prague) is included in the price. Unfortunately, traditional cottage cheese and eggs are not served at this hotel. However, the menu offered to guests is quite diverse. According to most Prague guests who have chosen this hotel to stay, the Prague Center Superior food is very good. Breakfast is served from 7 to 10 in the morning according to the "buffet".

In addition to the restaurant, there is a cafe-bar on site. Here you can order coffee, tea, cookies, sweets, etc.

Hotel rest

One of the services offered by this hotel to its guests is massage. Of course, you can use it only for a fee. Guests of Prague have the opportunity to book a reflexology massage or stone massage at the Prague Center Superior 3 * (Czech Republic, Prague). The hotel also has a well-equipped wellness center. You can visit it for free. If desired, hotel guests have the opportunity to also go to the sauna. There is also a jacuzzi in this hotel.





Prague Center Superior 3 *: hotel reviews

Since the administration of this hotel is trying to take care of the convenience of guests, tourists have a very good opinion of it. The hotel received the best reviews, of course, for its good location. From here you can get to the central square of Prague not only by metro, but also just walk. The road takes no more than 10 minutes. Also, many tourists really like the fact that the service at this hotel is around the clock.

Judging by the reviews of the guests, the rooms in this hotel are small, but quite comfortable. At least they are stylishly decorated and clean them daily. Bed linen is changed once a day at this hotel. Service at the hotel, most of the guests, generally considers very good. The hotel staff is helpful and welcoming.

The disadvantages of the hotel guests include primarily noise. In the immediate vicinity there is a busy road. The hotel elevator is also not particularly quiet. At night, it prevents some residents from falling asleep.





City infrastructure near the hotel

Prague has long been loved by many tourists. Prague Center Superior 3 * is actually located in a very good location. In the immediate vicinity of it is the restaurant "Legend". Here you can, if you wish, order delicious dishes for a very reasonable fee. Also, this restaurant offers just a huge selection of beers.

Opposite the metro, next to the hotel, there is also an inexpensive, but very convenient cafe. If you wish, you can have a good breakfast or lunch here. Unfortunately, there are no too large shopping centers in the immediate vicinity of the hotel. However, nearby there are several ordinary stores of different specializations. You can make purchases in a large supermarket in the central square.

Prague Attractions

As already mentioned, in the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are many interesting places in historical and cultural terms. The most popular tourist attractions in this part of Prague are:

  1. Charles Square. Here you can see just a huge number of ancient churches.
  2. Temple of St. Stephen. This Gothic-style Catholic church was built in the mid-14th century. The interior of this temple is rather modest. And this is not about the poverty of the parish. The ancient architect conceived this design specifically so that believers could focus on fellowship with God. It is the scarcity of decoration that creates a special atmosphere in this temple, and also gives it a completely unique flavor.
  3. Summer Palace of Count Michna. This building was built in 1720. The baroque palace is decorated. In this ancient building costumed performances are often held, at which guests have the opportunity, in addition to all the delicacies offered, to taste the famous Becherovka drink.
  4. Dancing House. This unusual modern building, built in the style of deconstructivism, has for several years invariably attracted the attention of guests of Prague. This multi-storey building is an architectural metaphor for a dancing couple.




Wenceslas Square Prague

This place in the Czech capital is, of course, the most popular and frequently visited by tourists. Wenceslas Square is located in the very center of Prague and is connected to many other attractions. This is where all the significant city events take place. Directly next to the square is the Czech National Museum built in 1890, and its main attraction is the equestrian statue of St. Wenceslas. In addition, on Wenceslas Square, tourists have the opportunity to visit the Lucerne shopping gallery. The place is very busy. You can buy here almost everything that your heart desires - from food to clothing and souvenirs.
