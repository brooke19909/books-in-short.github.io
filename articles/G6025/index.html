Choosing a coffee machine - which is better

A coffee machine is a chic thing, the dream of any coffee lover. She is no longer a rarity in the office, and is often found in homes. This rather sophisticated device is constantly being improved; the demand for it is growing. The programmed machine is a combination of a coffee grinder and a coffee maker. In different models, you can fill in either ground coffee or in beans. The machine will boil both espresso and cappuccino, and can even just boil water. And you can also program it to prepare your own favorite drink recipe. It still costs a small fortune and is considered to be a luxury in the kitchen. True, it is increasingly found in company offices and will soon become an integral part of official meetings.





The best of the best

The choice of a coffee machine is determined by three main factors: reliability, quality and price. It is not necessary to buy an expensive sophisticated unit for operating a house. Inexpensive models are most likely to brew coffee of the same quality as expensive, elite coffee machines. Because the presence of a digital display or a second boiler for simultaneous brewing of coffee according to two recipes does not affect the quality of the drink. Most likely, the affordable price and the ability to brew several options for delicious coffee can affect the choice of a coffee machine for the home. These are the Nivona devices of the 6th series, as well as Bosch coffee machines of the brands TCA5201, TCA5601 (08), TCA5809 and many others. The selection of reasonably priced, reliable and convenient coffee machines is wide. The best choice of a coffee machine for purchase in the office is the model of the average price level. They have more functions than machines of a cheaper class, they are reliable and durable. These are Nivona brand cars of the seventh series, Bosch coffee machines of the TCA6401, TCA6801, TCA6809 brands, as well as Delonghi coffee machines. Very good and high-quality Jura cars.

Functions of automatic machines

Automatic coffee machines are very expensive, but at the same time very popular. The process of brewing coffee does not require human intervention. he has only two functions: pour water, put coffee beans in the hopper, and set the number of required cups of coffee and its strength. Then press the "start" button. Next, the machine itself will grind coffee, measure the required amount of water, set the required steam pressure. The number of functions incorporated into it depends on the manufacturer. This can be automatic shutdown, setting the cooking mode, water quality control, self-cleaning and much more. The heart of the machine is a custard mechanism. It is there that the preparation of coffee takes place. Automatic devices can be cleaned under running water if it is removable. Non-removable is washed only with the help of special tablets manufactured by the manufacturer of the machine. Therefore, the choice of a coffee machine for a home is with a removable custard mechanism.
