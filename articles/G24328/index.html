How to clean the microwave inside using improvised means

We all know that where cooking is done, you can not do without spray, which is very difficult to clean. The kitchen is a place that requires constant cleaning, but any housewife wants to find a way to help deal with dirt faster. Cleaning the microwave oven takes a lot of time , especially from the inside, since getting to the back wall is usually not always convenient.

You can go to the nearest store and choose a detergent. But be careful! Read the instructions carefully, as some of them must be diluted with water before use. They will help you clean the microwave inside, but they will certainly leave a chemical smell and plaque on the walls of the oven. It is impossible to completely wash away the cleaning products, which means that during cooking they will get into food. "So what?" - you say, because we are surrounded everywhere by chemical compounds that harm our body. Of course, there is some truth in this, but why poison yourself? After all, there are proven folk methods that will help you cope with pollution using absolutely safe improvised means.





Folk Pollution Control Methods

The easiest way to clean the microwave inside? - put a glass of water in it for 10-12 minutes. The main thing is to fill the water only in half to avoid splashing. The steam will quickly soften the grease that has fallen on the walls of the microwave oven, and you can easily remove it with a normal cloth. To combat severe pollution, vinegar or citric acid can be added to the water, and for a pleasant smell, put some dried orange peels.

It's no secret that soda can replace almost any detergent. And the question of how to clean the microwave inside, you can safely answer: "Using soda." If often this multi-purpose assistant is used by many housewives as an excellent abrasive, then in our case ordinary cleaning will not work, because soda will easily damage the protective coating, hopelessly ruining the appliance. How to clean the microwave inside the soda? Easy and fast! Take a deep container, pour a couple tablespoons of soda and add plain water. 10-15 minutes of heating such a liquid will not only help to cope with the most difficult spots, splashes of food, but also with unpleasant odors settled in the microwave.





Lemon - a universal assistant

How to clean the microwave inside the lemon? Yes, it is possible. Even suitable
an old, half-dried fruit. It should be cut in half, laid on a plate or saucer with the pulp down (this is important) and heated for 5 minutes. During this time, the door glass is covered with condensate. You, as usual, will only have to wipe the inside with a damp cloth without adding cleaning products. In addition to cleanliness, you will get a fresh lemon smell that will quickly disappear.

What to use - you decide, but with the help of soda, lemon and ordinary water, you can quickly and safely clean the microwave oven, without making any effort.
