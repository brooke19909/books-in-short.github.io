Hotel Sun Beach Lindos 3 * (Lardos, Greece): photos and reviews of tourists

Tourists love spending their holidays in different ways. Some people like luxurious noisy hotels, while others prefer to spend their holidays in a quiet beautiful place without overpaying for excesses. Fans of a relaxing holiday will enjoy the small Sun Beach Lindos Hotel, located in Greece, on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. In this small institution, guests will be provided with comfort and a fascinating pastime.

Hotel location

Sun Beach Lindos is located in the southeastern part of such a beautiful island as Rhodes. The nearest international airport, Diagoras, is 53 km from the hotel. The journey takes about an hour. The nearest village of Lardos is 1.5 km away. This is a small resort town where tourists can buy everything they need, stock up on provisions or buy souvenirs.





Hotel Sun Beach Lindos 3 * (Greece, Rhodes) is located just 5 km from the famous tourist city of Lindos. Tourists can get to the nearest settlements by bus, taxi or their own transport.

Description of Sun Beach Lindos

The hotel is in a picturesque area and surrounded by other hotel complexes. This beautiful cozy place is fascinating at first sight. The area is very well maintained and green. Magnificent trees surround 3 buildings of the hotel complex. Arbours are installed practically throughout the territory, which allows you to enjoy the beauty of the area in a quiet pleasant environment.

Many vacationers note the beauty of the gardens. Lawns are in excellent condition, and flower beds are fragrant with a variety of exotic flowers. The gardens are decorated with various figurines, which allows you to make excellent pictures in memory of the time spent here.





The hotel courtyard has an excellent pool. It is divided into: for adults and for children. Around him are sun loungers, which hotel guests can use for free. There are always plenty of places for those who want to lie down by the pool, even with the full occupancy of the hotel complex.

Infrastructure

Despite the fact that the Sun Beach Lindos hotel (Greece) is a three-star establishment, excellent conditions for guests are created here. The reception is open around the clock and you can always contact your administrators with your problems. The presence of a staff who is fluent in Russian makes it possible to resolve all emerging issues as soon as possible. Administrators will give useful tips on choosing interesting places to visit.

Guests can leave their luggage in a left-luggage office located in the hotel lobby. If necessary, laundry or dry cleaning services are available. Here, for an additional fee, they will quickly bring their clothes in proper form. Iron is available at reception and is available on request. A fax or photocopy machine is available for an extra charge.

Vacationers can buy everything they need or stock up on souvenirs in a small shop or in a mini-market located on the hotel territory. There is an exchange office right there. To park their cars, guests can use the free parking.

You can shorten the time in the Internet corner or TV room. Wi-Fi in the lobby is free. You will have to pay for the right to use the Internet in rooms or other areas of the hotel.

Number of rooms

Arriving at the Hotel Sun Beach Lindos 3 * (Rhodes), guests are offered 90 rooms of 3 types:

  • standard for 2 people;
  • standard superior, which accommodate a maximum of 3 guests;
  • family, designed for 4 guests.

Each room has a balcony or terrace with plastic furniture. This allows guests to admire the charm of these places.





In the standard rooms there is all the necessary furniture, which easily fit all brought things. Among the proposed options, you can choose rooms with two single beds and one double. A sofa is used as an extra bed. For small children, upon request of parents, a cot is delivered to the room.

Family rooms consist of two rooms. They have 4 beds. In all types of rooms there is air conditioning, a minibar with paid drinks and snacks, TV, telephone. A safe is provided for storing the most valuable things in the rooms. The shower in the bathroom and the toilet are always in working condition. Bath amenities and a hairdryer are also included in each room.

Thanks to daily housekeeping, the rooms are pretty clean. A daily change of towels is also provided. Linen is changed every other day. Often everything works fine in the rooms. If any malfunctions are detected and the administrators are notified about this, the masters carry out repairs as soon as possible, without annoying the guests for a long time.

Beach and sea

The popularity of the Sun Beach Lindos hotel (Rhodes) is due to its close proximity to the sea. Even in cozy gazebos on the premises you can enjoy the sound of waves. The beach is only about 100 meters away. Umbrellas and sun loungers are free of charge for hotel guests. Towels here are issued only for a surcharge.





Despite the fact that the beach belongs to the city, it is always clean. Its territory is quite large and, as many tourists say, very beautiful. The beach is covered with sand and pebbles. The entrance to the sea is convenient, the bottom is covered with small pebbles.

Nutrition

Sun Beach Lindos Hotel (Greece) provides all-inclusive meals. In the central restaurant you can sit in a cozy room or on the terrace. All guests have enough tables. The waiters are doing an excellent job, the restaurant is always clean and comfortable.

All tourists who have visited the Sun Beach Lindos 3 * hotel respond well to the quality of food, but the variety of dishes, according to many, makes us wish for the best. Pastries and desserts are served only for breakfast and are not impressive with a large selection. Some also complain of a shortage of fresh fruit. But still, hotel guests never remain hungry and among the dishes on offer they will find something to feast on. For a change, holidaymakers can enjoy light snacks at the beach bar. They offer great burgers, delicious french fries, pizza and other dishes.





Drinks are served at the restaurant and bar. Locally produced juices, water and alcohol are available for free. You will have to pay for imported drinks. Hotel guests are not complaining about the shortage of tea and coffee.

Sports and other entertainments

The hotel Sun Beach Lindos 3 * guests are offered a wide selection of entertainment. Here daily aerobics classes are held. Experienced instructors will also teach water aerobics exercises. You can engage in a game of darts or table tennis. Sports enthusiasts can try beach volleyball or basketball. The hotel also has a tennis court, which can be used at any time for free. For rackets and balls for the game, a fee is also not provided.

Many vacationers enjoy water sports. Scuba diving, water skiing, catamarans and other activities are available at an additional cost. And in water polo everyone can test themselves for free.





Entertainers of guests of the Sun Beach Lindos hotel are a group of animators. In the evening, they organize various show programs on the beach. Sometimes guests are entertained by visiting artists or groups with interesting performances. Fans of late walks at the hotel will be bored, because discos are not held here.

Children's entertainment

Sun Beach Lindos Hotel has a great time for young guests. Children are expected in a mini club. Experienced teachers, among whom there are Russian speakers, will always find activities for kids. It often hosts workshops on various exciting activities. Also, children will have fun visiting the mini-disco, where they can chat with peers.

For children, a part of the hotel pool is equipped. Kids can frolic for a long time in clean, warm water. The guys also have the opportunity to entertain themselves with exciting games on the playground. Animators amuse children by inventing various contests and competitions for them.

The restaurant has a special menu for young guests. High chairs are also provided for them. If desired, parents can request a crib at the reception, for which they will have to pay extra.

Excursions

Many tourists are attracted to the Sun Beach Lindos 3 * hotel by the opportunity to see many interesting places. Rhodes is famous for its amazing landscapes, beautiful beaches and monuments of ancient architecture. Fans of water activities will love the tourist village of Lardos. It has many water sports centers. Professional instructors will be happy to share the secrets of diving, sailing and knowledge in other water sports. You can get to the village on foot, on a comfortable bus or taxi.

Fans of shopping are expected to markets and markets of the nearest settlements. Here you can always find excellent products and various souvenirs. Gourmets will appreciate the variety of restaurants, cafeterias and bars where you can treat yourself to dishes of national or world cuisine. In search of nightly entertainment, tourists also travel to Largos or Lindos. In the summer months, various celebrations hardly cease here.

An indelible impression will leave the beautiful bays of the city of Lindos. They not only captivate with superb views, but are also famous for their rich history. Nature lovers will be amazed by the majestic view of the mountains here, the rocky foot of which is covered with lush greenery. It is interesting to visit the Valley of Seven Springs. According to the inhabitants of the island, the springs in this area have magical powers. Under their influence, a tunnel with a length of 186 meters was formed in the rock. Flowers in the valley throughout the year delight visitors with their beauty.





The city of Lindos is also impressive. You can get to the settlement by transport, but you will have to walk along its streets on foot. The houses of the city are painted in snow-white color, which looks very impressive against the background of rocky terrain. Almost all structures date from the 16-18th centuries, as evidenced by the commemorative inscriptions and coats of arms applied to them. In the city you can see the ancient burial Archocrateon and the grave of Cleobulus. For lovers of antiquities, the rock located near the city, on the top of which you can see the Acropolis and the ancient fortress, is of particular interest.

You can not deprive the attention and Christian shrines of Lindos. In the center of the city you can visit the Church of the Virgin, whose walls are decorated with bewitching frescoes made in the 15th century. In the nearby village of Archangelos there is a monastery of Panagia Tsambika, which is often visited by couples who want to have children.

Hotel staff

Tourists at Sun Beach Lindos Hotel very warmly remember the tourists. The reviews confirm that all employees of the institution are excellent in their responsibilities. Administrators warmly welcome guests who have arrived to them and without unnecessary delays are accommodated by numbers. They also as soon as possible solve the problems of the guests.

The cleaners fulfill their duties very well: every corner of the hotel amazes with well-groomed and clean. In the rooms, the staff cleans up quite quickly, without annoying vacationers. The area is cleaned up early in the morning, while hotel guests are still sleeping.

The courtesy of all the people working at the hotel is impressive. They are affable, but not intrusive. Even if the employees of the hotel complex do not speak the language spoken by the guests, they quickly respond and try their best to help vacationers.

Sun Beach Lindos 3 *: tourist reviews

According to people who were vacationing at the hotel, couples with children and those who wish to spend their holidays in a quiet and relaxed atmosphere will like it here. Young people who want to have fun at the hotel will be bored.

According to tourists, the Sun Beach Lindos 3 * hotel will fully meet the expectations expected from an institution of this level. The main advantage here is the proximity of the sea and the excellent beach. Guests are also pleased with the presence of free umbrellas and sunbeds by the sea, even though the zone is municipal.

Some tourists are not comfortable with slightly dilapidated furniture in the rooms. And despite all the efforts of the staff to fix the breakdowns, the antique situation definitely needs to be replaced.

Often, hotel guests complain of scarcity in the menu of the main restaurant of the hotel. It serves few desserts, pastries and fruits, which can only be seen with breakfast. But although the restaurant cannot boast of a special variety of dishes, the quality of food is at a high level. In addition, guests can occupy tables on the terrace and during the meal enjoy the beauty of the sea and local landscapes.

Most tourists are satisfied with their stay at the Sun Beach Lindos hotel. They argue that in this institution for little money you can spend time in fairly comfortable conditions. Many of them are not only satisfied with their vacation, but are also going to return to the hotel at least once more.
