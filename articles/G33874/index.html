Sultan Sipahi Resort Hotel 4 * (Alanya, Turkey): description, service, reviews

Turkey is a budget resort country, which is famous not only for low prices, but also for high quality service. Numerous tourists from Russia come here on vacation, hoping to have a good and inexpensive vacation. One of the most popular resorts in Turkey is Alanya. The local infrastructure offers travelers a wide variety of leisure options. In addition, in Alanya there are plenty of budget hotels and expensive top-class hotels. If you are looking for something average in terms of service, then pay attention to the Sultan Sipahi Resort Hotel 4 * complex, which we will cover in detail in this article.

Holidays in Alanya

This popular Turkish resort is aimed at different categories of tourists. Perhaps its only drawback is only the great distance from large airports, so travelers are forced to get to their hotels for several hours. The high season here begins at the end of April and lasts until the end of November. At the same time, in winter in Alanya, the temperature keeps above zero, but swimming in the sea and sunbathing is no longer possible. The resort itself is divided into several zones, among which there are party areas, and remote places for a relaxing holiday.

The popularity of the city brought its beaches, which are considered almost the cleanest in the country. Most of them are marked with special Blue Flags. There are many historical sights here. Coming here, you should definitely visit the Red Tower, built in the XII century, or the Byzantine fortress Ich-Kale. Alanya also has its own mosque and shipyard, built in 1228.

The city has a huge number of shops, shopping centers, restaurants and cafes. There is also a Turkish market. If you want to save on the purchase of clothes, shoes or souvenirs, it is best to go to the suburbs. In Alanya, there are many minibuses, and tourists can also order a taxi. A contact dolphinarium is open for children near the resort, and 30 km from Alanya is a huge water park, which is considered one of the largest in Turkey.

Hotel location

The favorable location of the hotel is the key to a good rest. Sultan Sipahi Resort 4 * hotel is built in the center of Alanya, on the Mediterranean coast. Alanya‚Äôs most popular beach, Cleopatra, is just 50 meters away. To get to it, tourists only need to cross the road, which is equipped with an underpass. Near the hotel there is a dessert cafe and pizzeria. A little further removed are the water park, sports hall and supermarket.

The nearest airport is in the suburbs of Gazipasa, which is about 40 km from Alanya. Get from there to the Sultan Sipahi Resort Hotel 4 * in Turkey in 1 hour. However, more often tourists fly to Antalya airport, because flights there are much cheaper. It is 125 km away, so the trip to the resort can take about three hours by bus. By taxi or rented car, travel time will be reduced, but, in turn, the cost of the trip will increase significantly.

Important hotel and check-in information

It is difficult to call the Sultan Sipahi Resort Hotel 4 * (Alanya) hotel new, because it was built in 1996. However, this does not affect the condition of the rooms and other public areas. Complete renovation of the complex was completed in 2014. The hotel has its own vast territory of 27,500 square meters. m. It has a spacious swimming pool, sun terrace and several public buildings. Guests can be accommodated in any of the three six-story buildings. In total, the hotel has 215 rooms, so this place can not be called quiet and not crowded.





When choosing a Sultan Sipahi Resort Hotel 4 * complex for relaxation, it should be borne in mind that it is forbidden to check into rooms with any pets. Upon arrival, all guests are required to register at the reception. A Russian-speaking administrator works here daily to help fill out the necessary documents. In addition, the hotel staff also speaks a little English and Russian. It is worth noting that registration starts here from 14:00. You will have to leave your rooms a little earlier - you need to leave them before noon. With available apartments you can always extend your stay.

Arrangement

For the convenience of guests, all rooms of Sultan Sipahi Resort Hotel 4 * have a standard set of amenities to help make your stay here truly comfortable. Most of them are included in the price, but for some, if you want to use them, you will have to pay separately. We list the main paid and free amenities that can be found in the rooms:

  • individual air conditioning with remote control;

  • mini-bar, which is replenished daily with non-carbonated drinking water;

Food concept

Many Turkish hotels operate on an all-inclusive basis. The Sultan Sipahi Resort Hotel 4 * complex was no exception. Meals are included in the price. The system operates daily from early morning until 23:00. At this time, tourists can not only have a bite to eat at restaurants and bars on site, but also order local soft drinks and spirits. Imported alcohol will have to be paid separately.

You can eat in the main restaurant, where guests can enjoy a buffet. It works on the following schedule:

  • breakfast, including late breakfast (from 07:30 to 10:30);
  • lunch (from noon to 2:30 p.m.);
  • dinner (from 19:00 to 21:00);
  • late dinner (until midnight).




In the afternoons, guests can also grab a bite to eat at the poolside bar, which serves light fare, ice cream and cocktails, or in the lobby bar. It serves fresh pastries and desserts. The main menu necessarily includes meat and fish dishes served with a side dish, as well as fresh vegetables and fruits, famous Turkish sweets. Free diet and vegetarian menus are available upon request.

Pool and beach

One of the main advantages of the Sultan Sipahi Resort Hotel 4 * hotel is the beach. The complex has its own stretch of coast, which is located across the road. There, tourists can rent sun loungers, sun umbrellas and towels for a fee. The beach is completely sandy, and the entrance to the sea is gentle, so you can swim here even with young children. There is also a paid bar and a water entertainment center on site.





You can swim in two outdoor pools located next to the residential buildings of the hotel. The water in them is not heated, so they work only in good weather. On the terrace next to them you can sunbathe on the free sun loungers. Towels here are issued only for a fee. The pool is additionally equipped with water slides, which adults and children can ride.

Service in Sultan Sipahi Resort Hotel 4 *

For its guests, the hotel has prepared a developed infrastructure system, with the help of which the most comfortable conditions for recreation are created. Conditions for accommodating children

The hotel is popular with tourists with kids. Upon arrival, they can ask the administrator for a free crib in their room. The children's restaurant has high chairs. A microwave and blender are also available.

The complex offers a standard set of entertainment. Toddlers can swim in a separate shallow pool. A mini club is on site daily. It is worth noting that baby pots and strollers are not rented here.

Positive reviews

Sultan Sipahi Resort Hotel 4 * most often leaves a pleasant impression. Tourists like to relax here, many return here again. Guests note that the hotel has its drawbacks, but the cons are fully justified by the low cost of living. They distinguish the following advantages of the hotel:

  • clean territory - employees regularly clean garbage and look after flower beds and shrubs;
  • daily maids carefully clean the rooms and change towels, even if this is not necessary;
  • a variety of delicious dinners that serve lamb, seafood, fish, liver, turkey and chicken;
  • friendly reception staff works at the reception, who check in the rooms even earlier than the due date;
  • The spa provides an excellent inexpensive massage;
  • Good hotel location close to many attractions of the resort.




Negative reviews

But, of course, it was not without criticism. When choosing this hotel for your vacation, be sure to pay attention to the following disadvantages that can ruin your vacation experience:

  • old rooms that have not been renovated for a long time;
  • almost all services at the hotel are provided for a fee, in addition, staff constantly begs for tips;
  • huge lines in the restaurant, while food is not enough for all guests;
  • the beach can hardly be called clean, the sand on it is strewn with numerous butts and plastic glasses;
  • in the sea you can see fuel oil and oil stains.

Thus, Sultan Sipahi Resort Hotel 4 * is an inexpensive Turkish hotel that provides a standard set of entertainment and services. It has many shortcomings, but is perfect for budget accommodation in the center of Alanya.
