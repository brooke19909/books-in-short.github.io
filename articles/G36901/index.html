Making a Tomato costume for boys with their own hands: options

On Autumn, kindergarten and school often require characters to portray vegetables. Indeed, it is this time of year that gives people the majority of fruits rich in vitamins. A boy can be appointed to play the role of Tomato. In the article, we will consider several options for making a Tomato costume with our own hands from different materials.

Cardboard Attributes

If you have a large piece of corrugated cardboard, then you can safely make this outfit of vegetable from it. This option is suitable for mothers who do not know how to sew. It‚Äôs easy to do. From cardboard you need to cut two ovals of the same size - on the chest and on the back. You can make them small. It is permissible to make strips on the shoulders not cardboard, but, for example, from a wide elastic band or satin ribbons of red or green color. Green leaves and a muzzle are painted on the front oval: eyes and a smiling mouth. You can do this with an application so that the Tomato costume does not get dirty from gouache. A good option is to buy self-adhesive, then the work will shine beautifully and the details will be bright.





The cap ends with the image of Tomato. You can put on your child a simple knitted green hat or make a cardboard one. To do this, measure the circumference of the head and cut a strip of corrugated cardboard of this length. For bonding, you need to add a couple more centimeters. Further, having tried the cylinder on the head, we fasten the edges with a simple stapler in several places. It remains to stick the sharp triangles of the stalk. Cardboard Tomato suit is ready!

Foam Tomato

To sew such an outfit for a boy, you will need a sheet of thin foam rubber, satin fabric of red and green color. If you decide to decorate such a Tomato costume with eyes and mouth, then you will need to have pieces of white and black material. We begin by cutting two identical circles of foam. Next, we put them on a red cloth, folded four times, and outlines. At the edges we leave one more centimeter of fabric for stitching. Cut out the details with sharp scissors.





Foam rubber is placed in the middle between pieces of matter and sewn in a circle. So we design the front and back of the Tomato costume. Parts of the dress are sewn together so that the neck and openings for the hands remain open. On the front part, an application of a cheerful muzzle of a vegetable is made, if desired. It remains to sew a hat. They make it from diamond-shaped wedges sewn together. At the top attach a sprig of the stalk. Under the suit you can put on just dark trousers and a white turtleneck or shirt.

Outfit for baby

The next Tomato suit is suitable for the child of the younger group. It is possible to sew a sweater and trousers with elastic bands on the belt and on the lower legs from knitted fabric separately. If you have a red turtleneck or t-shirt, then you can use the finished parts of the suit. The main thing is that the entire main outfit of the vegetable should be red. Additional elements will be a cap, acting as a stalk, and a collar of green leaves on the neck.





You can buy a knitted green hat and from felt to sew a bunch of petals sewn in the center. A collar can be made on the neck. On a strip of green felt sewn petals around the perimeter. It is fixed on the back with a button or hook.

T-shirt Tomato outfit

If dad has an old red T-shirt in his wardrobe , you can make a beautiful Tomato costume for the boy with your own hands from it. First of all, you need to pick up a collar in order to reduce the neck, and roll up sleeves on the shoulders and hem several centimeters. The bottom of the t-shirt rolls up and a rubber band hangs in. It remains to fill the middle with filler. As it is possible to use foam rubber trim, cotton wool or synthetic winterizer, folded in several parts.





Collar

The Tomato suit for the boy can be supplemented with a collar with leaves. For this, it is best to use a familiar felt sheet. Material is often used by craftswomen for crafts. It is soft, elastic and pleasant to the touch. Various details can be made from it, since the fabric is perfectly cut, glued, the edges do not crumble, so the details do not require an additional rim.





A leaf template is drawn on cardboard. Then, outlining the contours on the fabric, several such details are cut out. They are sewn on a strip of felt, which is worn on the boy‚Äôs neck. You can fix it on the back with a button or velcro.

Hat

The last detail of the costume will be the manufacture of a hat. Make it from a piece of a knitted t-shirt. For this, a strip of matter is cut out and, wrapping around the head, the edges are sewn together. It turns out a "pipe", the upper part of which must be pulled together and stitched in the center. To not stand out the seam, decorate the hat with leaves. They are made according to the same pattern as for the collar.





Sew them in such a way that the leaves look in different directions. The bottom can not be turned up, as the knitwear will remain in good shape for a long time and the edge will not crumble. In any case, such a hat will withstand the triumph to the end.

Choose any of the options offered, and your baby will be the most beautiful at the Autumn Festival!
