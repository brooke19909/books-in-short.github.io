Base "Three Bears" (Altai Mountains)

The tourist complex "Three Bears" (Gorny Altai) has been attracting visitors for many years with a cozy atmosphere, privacy and high service. Wildlife, almost untouched by civilization, clean air, crystal water - that is what captivates all tourists. A trip to this marvelous corner will give an unforgettable experience and help to improve your health.





Those who have been here consider the base one of the best in the region. There are a lot of lovers of active and extreme recreation. Almost all tourist sports are concentrated in this place: rafting, climbing, skiing and horse riding. For fans of high jumps, a bungee is offered. In addition to active pastimes, you will be offered fishing, horseback riding, hiking trails and educational tours of the picturesque corners of Altai.

In May of this year, a flood was destroyed a little by the Three Bears base (Altai Mountains). Fortunately, the complex did not suffer significant damage from the flood. Already in June, it was completely possible to restore power supply, to ennoble the territory and landscape park zone. So tourists can safely come here and enjoy the wonderful scenery.

Location and Description





In a quiet Altai village called Aya, a complex was built, which is a real example of Russian architecture with carved ornaments on buildings. The graceful appearance of the buildings harmoniously combines with the noble beauty of the surrounding park. Near the residential buildings is the crystal clear mountain river Katun.

A few kilometers away is a ski center with an ice rink, slides and various entertainment services. The internal territory is well-groomed, there are tiled alleys, open terraces with benches and barbecue facilities. Around the atmosphere of celebration and serenity. The attentive guide, which makes maximum efforts for quality leisure, contributes to a pleasant rest for guests.

Where to stay?

The Three Bears Country Camp (Gorny Altai) consists of two-story buildings and several one-story cottages. Room cleaning is at your discretion - the sign is at the administrator. Accommodation is quick in the afternoon.

Room Types





Customers can choose from several room categories:

  • Double: located in two-story villas on the 1st floor. There is a private bathroom with a shower. The room has a standard set of furniture and appliances - beds, wardrobe, refrigerator, satellite TV.
  • Triple and quadruple rooms include the following amenities: a bed, a folding armchair, a coffee table, a compact refrigerator, a TV and a necessary set of dishes. Separate toilet and bathroom.
  • Suite: consist of a bedroom and a living room. There is a private porch. The room has modern upholstered furniture, bedside tables, a set of toiletries, a color TV with satellite TV.

Supply system





Separately paid meals at the camp "Three Bears" (Altai Mountains). The menu in the hotel is custom-made - three meals a day, prepared from our own products. The territory has its own farm, where cows grazing on a green meadow give tasty and healthy milk, as well as poultry, sheep, pigs and turkeys. Vegetables and fruits are grown on the ground, which subsequently fall on the table.

Meals are served in the cafeteria. Chefs prepare delicacies not only of national cuisine, but also of European, Russian and Eastern. A bar with a wide range of drinks and snacks is open. In the evening, musical groups entertain the holidaymakers, a disco is held.

Internal service





An interesting leisure program awaits vacationers. The base "Three Bears" (Gorny Altai) invites everyone to swim in the spacious pool, which is equipped with a children's section with a fountain and a water slide. Around the pool are sun loungers. You can relax your body and soul in the steam room or sauna.





Since this year, the complex has expanded its services - a technically equipped conference room has appeared. It is convenient to hold both business and special occasions. Indoors you will find a round table, audio and video equipment. Use of the hall is paid - an hour will cost 750 rubles. Another innovation is the hostel for pets. Now you can bring your pets to the hotel and relax together.

For active leisure, a tennis court and sports fields are built on site. Bikes, rackets, balls and other equipment are available for hire. The building has billiards, chess and backgammon. Tourist routes are organized to the Alpine and Teletskoye lakes, as well as to the Kurai steppe.

Wellness Services





The Three Bears complex (Altai Mountains) is proud of the medical center. It will help you heal staggering nerves, improve heart function, lose weight, cleanse your skin and improve your health. Two baths are open for visiting (large and small). It offers guests healing procedures using phytobarrels, mud and clay wraps. Cosmetology and massage rooms work. According to the indications, acupuncture is prescribed.

Children's leisure





A versatile and fun holiday awaits the youngest guests of the resort complex "Three Bears" (Altai Mountains). In addition to the pool, there is a game corner in the adjacent area. There is a developing club where creative and visual classes are held for children.





Every day, children are entertained by a professional group of animators, aerobics in water is carried out, draws, competitions, contests, dances and sports tours are held. Cartoons are shown and interesting relay races are organized. In general, no one will be bored. The pride of the hotel is still a zoo. It is inhabited by real deer, lynxes, wild leopards, small rodents and birds.

Directions

You can get in several ways to the Altai Mountains. Three Bears (see photo in the article) carries out transfers by comfortable buses from Omsk, Novosibirsk and Barnaul to the bridge of the village of Aya, then transfer to public transport and travel about 5 km to the destination. The fare is included in the ticket.

By personal car: drive along the Biysk highway, passing the villages of Srostki, Maima and Souzga. We cross the Aysky bridge and turn towards the village of Aya.

A lot of positive and even admired reviews are found about this base. More than 90% of visitors are couples with children who enthusiastically talked about their holidays in this fabulous and surprisingly beautiful corner of the globe. Arranged people really high-quality and balanced diet.

Large selection of dishes for the child. I liked the quiet and calm atmosphere in the complex. Security strictly monitors order and safety. Different kinds of disagreements are quickly and without conflict resolved, which cannot but rejoice. Pleasantly surprised by the variety of services, including an excellent wellness center at an affordable price. Special thanks were expressed to the courteous staff.

All in all, a good base for a relaxing, active and romantic weekend. You can recommend it to people who want to live away from the dusty metropolis for a while, in a quiet and cozy place in Altai, in the middle of a dense forest.
