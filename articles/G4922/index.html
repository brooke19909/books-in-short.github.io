Resort Plyakho: private sector, rest

The resort of Plyaho is located near Tuapse. This is an environmentally friendly place with healing mountain air, beautiful nature and not yet too many people. Vacationers with children or romance lovers come here. This place is also loved by budget tourists. After all, the private sector Plyakho, the description of which our article will be devoted to, is designed for people with low incomes. With all this, the living conditions are very good, and the owners are often responsible and welcoming. Tuapse is about 46 kilometers from here. The season here begins in May and ends in October.



Plyakho village

The private sector is almost the only option to stay in this place. There are no huge sanatoriums and "tricked out" hotels here. The village is surrounded by forests and mountains, and the most popular activities here are horse riding and hiking along canyons, cliffs and rivers. Rest here is still traditional: noisy parties, night clubs and incendiary discos here you simply will not find. For all this you have to go to Tuapse. But in the children's camp "Eaglet" there is a beautiful park with relict trees. For attractions and entertainment for kids, come to the neighboring resort Novomikhailovsky. Moreover, to go there no more than five to six minutes.



Accommodations

Accommodation in Plyakho (private sector) is offered to vacationers different. These are guest houses, mini-hotels and separate rooms and lodges. Before you go here to relax, it is better, of course, to choose something cozy, comfortable, with reliable owners. Typically, tourists rent a room or apartment with air conditioning, a refrigerator and a TV. A bathroom, as a rule, with hot and cold water, can be individual, for several rooms or shared. Often the hosts provide different services to vacationers for an additional fee - they sell homemade wine, organize fishing, take excursions, warm a bath or sauna, and offer horse riding. Nutrition

Despite the fact that the village is small, the infrastructure is well developed here. There is a market where you can buy fresh fruits and vegetables. Fresh milk, directly from under the cow, fish directly from the sea - all these almost forgotten joys of a simple life can be acquired here. Most apartments, such as Residence of the Sun, Meeting and others, provide a common kitchen with a set of necessary utensils so that tourists can cook on their own. For mothers with babies, even blenders and food processors will be provided. Many owners of the private sector Plyakho cook for tourists themselves or keep their own cafes. Here you will be offered a full board or custom menus for every taste.

At what distance from the sea is better to settle

In Plyakho there are wide beaches and a smooth entrance to the water, which distinguishes this village from the neighboring resorts of Lermontovo and Novomikhailovskoye. In addition, the coast here is sandy, which is generally rare for the Caucasus. The beach is paid, but equipped and clean - it is cleaned twice a day. It is forbidden to drink alcohol. After all, it belongs to the children's camp "Eaglet". In practice, the private sector of Plyakho is located not far from the sea, the closest to the beach are located at a distance of 600 m. There is one road leading to the sea from the village that crosses the federal highway. However, drivers drive slowly, again because there is a nearby children's camp of national importance. But even tourists who are remote from the sea, tourists choose for their comfort. Indeed, despite the fact that Plyakho is a small village, minibuses often go along it, with which it is easy to get to the sea. In addition, some guest houses with a high level of comfort, such as, for example, Karina, deliver their guests to the beach in their own minibus.
