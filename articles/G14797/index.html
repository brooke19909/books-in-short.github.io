Recreation facilities, Gelendzhik. Rest in Crimea

Relaxing in Gelendzhik is one pleasure. However, you need to decide on the basis of any factors (for each they have their own), where exactly to stay. Below is a small number of recreation centers that delight with their unique simplicity, cheapness and beautiful nature.

"Pinery"

For those who prefer ecological holidays in the countryside, the Sosnovy Bor recreation center is ideal. Very close is the conservation zone, which is of great importance for the nearest territories.

What are the features of this recreation center Gelendzhik? It is located in an area filled with rare species of plants and trees. Crimean pine trees add an unusual aroma to the air, which is also healing. Some plants in the area are over several thousand years old.





A day for two adults, you should pay 1,500 rubles. To the sea - only 3 km. And the center of the district is 45 km away. There is Internet access, sports rooms, as well as various kinds of entertainment. Some houses are specially equipped for families with children.

The good news for potential residents will be that pets are allowed here. There is a pool, parking, fast Wi-Fi, and a playground is equipped. From this recreation center Gelendzhik to the sea can be reached by transport, which provides the institution itself. The trip promises to be as comfortable and convenient as possible.

"Garden of Eden"

This base is located in a place where the sun will not be especially hot, which means that summer days will pass unforgettably. Around the equipped area are pine forests, as well as mountain gorges.

The air is saturated with the aromas of plants (herbs, flowers and trees), a lake, a spring. There are arbors; all visitors are quiet and calm, so the trip is clearly worth it.

A tenant will be offered a house on one or two floors. As a rule, they are designed for 2, 3 or 4 people. There are various kinds of entertainment (bar, cinema, karaoke, sports ground).





Payment from two people a day - from 2800 rubles. To the sea from this recreation center Gelendzhik is only 350 meters, which, of course, pleases, but to the city center a little more - 4 km. You can safely come with children, there are all the necessary amenities. Each room has air conditioning. A swimming pool, sauna and bathhouse are what you need for the most comfortable stay.

Gelendzhik

Relatively close to the coast are recreation centers. Gelendzhik in this regard outperforms all the options described above. The base is only 200 meters from the coast, it will take longer to go to the center, because it is located 6 km from this place. Resettlement is possible for families with children of different ages and animals. There is a sports ground. Equipped a children's room. There is both cold water and hot. They are served around the clock.

Vacationers have at their disposal a kitchen where they cook on their own. If there is no skill or desire, then you can go to a nearby cafe or dining room.





For 2 adults a day you will need to pay 1800 rubles., Meals per day comes out on average 600 rubles. For additional investments, you can go on a tour. On the territory of the recreation center there is a wonderful place where barbecue is prepared, and after a fun picnic.

"Takeoff"

As much as possible comfortably equipped all described recreation centers. "Take-off" (Gelendzhik) is somewhat different from the rest. Only 100 meters from the sea, 6 km from the center. The rooms have air conditioning. There is no entertainment, self-catering. There is no shared kitchen. In principle, for those who love minimalism and low costs, this place will be ideal. 2 adults will pay 1380 rubles per day. The rooms are equipped as comfortable as possible. There is a TV, fridge, toilet, a small kitchen.

It is allowed to cook barbecue on the grill near the house. Like other recreation centers, "Rise" (Gelendzhik) works without a special schedule. The beach is not covered with sand, there is a grass carpet, but it does not interfere with sunbathing or frolic on the beach.



"Grace"

This is one of the most expensive recreation centers. Here for 2 people a day you will have to pay 3 thousand rubles. To the seashore - 300 m, to the center of the area even closer - you need to go only 220 m. Meals are included in the room rate per day. There are air conditioners in the rooms, and the base is quite suitable for families with children.

What can not but rejoice in service is food. It is carried out on the basis of the buffet. All rooms have a truly homely atmosphere and delight the eye with their interior.

Gelendzhik, whose recreation facilities are located in a special climate zone, cannot but attract a large number of tourists. For example, in the area where Grace is located, there are a lot of volatile products in the air. They positively affect the human condition. Allergies and asthmatics can improve the health here. Ambrosia does not grow on the territory, which complicates life for some.

"Black Sea and Co"

To the sea from this recreation center is only 500 m, to the central region - 740 m. The institution is located near the forest, along with the coast of a large water area there is a mountain river. Thanks to her, on especially hot summer days, the weather does not seem so tiring - the air quickly cools due to the close proximity of the water stream.

The houses are designed for 2, 3 or 4 inhabitants in one room. You can come with animals; nearby is a children's area, so it is safe to bring children. There is a parking lot, each room has air conditioning. There is also a sports field.

Gelendzhik is striking in its nature. Recreation facilities (each price is specified) are always located in flowering meadows or beautiful pine forests. "Black Sea and Co" is located in a place where evergreen trees have been standing for several thousand years. For two there is a fee of 1200 rubles per day.
