Grand Whiz Hotel Nusa Dua Bali 4 * (Indonesia, Bali, Nusa Dua): room description, service, reviews

Bali is an island in the Malay Archipelago, the most remote part of Indonesia. Nusa Dua is the resort area where the Grand Vis Hotel is located. Vacationers from all over the world come here for the sake of vivid impressions. After all, the radiant waves, white sand, quiet warm evenings and inexpensive hotels impress guests.

Address and Location

Grand Whiz Hotel Nusa Dua Bali is located at Block T, Kawasan Wisata, Nusa Dua 80363, Indonesia.

The nearest airport is Denpasar Bali, which is 8.5 km from the hotel.

Located nearby: one large shopping center, two restaurants and two markets. In addition, there are many attractions near the hotel. These are the Pacific Museum, the Veteran Monument, the Turtle Island, the large park, Benoa Harbor, Cape Tanjung Benoa and the largest Heger Beach.

There are more distant sights. For example, a water park, art market, four shopping centers and much more. Locals recommend visiting the cultural and spiritual city of Ubud with famous galleries for art lovers. You can also drive to Mount Batur, from where you can see the sunrise, the botanical garden, local waterfalls and the lake in the Bedugul area.

Terms of Accommodation

Rooms must be booked in advance, as there will be no seats at the right time. You can call in from 14:00 to 21:00. You must leave the hotel strictly from 12:00 to 12:30. Unfortunately, pets are not allowed.

If reservations are made in bulk, then a special discount system applies. This is discussed individually with the hotel.

After booking the apartment, a certain amount is deducted from the card. Therefore, at the time of booking, the card must have the necessary funds.

You can pay for your reservation with such cards:

  1. AMERICAN EXPRESS.
  2. Maestro.
  3. Visa
  4. MasterCard.

The agreement states: "The hotel administration has the right to block funds on the card until the guests arrive." On the day of arrival, guests must provide the same card that was used at the time of booking.

Grand Whiz Hotel Nusa Dua: room description

Guests are offered 130 comfortable and modern rooms in which good air conditioning and all necessary amenities. The apartments have: TV, safe, kettle, fridge, minibar, bathroom with necessary amenities and shower.

Grand Whiz Hotel Nusa Dua has superior double rooms with one double or two single beds. There are even better suites - deluxe. These rooms offer one large double bed or two small separate beds. There are hotel suites. They have: one large bed and a work area.

You can also rent a villa with 4 bedrooms and a private pool. The first and third bedrooms have two small beds, and the second and fourth bedrooms have one large double bed. Prices

Room prices depend on many factors. This is the time of year, living conditions, location of apartments and their size. The average price per room per day is about 3,000 rubles. If with breakfast, then a little expensive. At the height of the season, the cost rises for absolutely everything.

The rainy season in Bali is from November to March. It was at this time that the prices at the hotel were minimal. Especially in December, January and February. Even excursions are difficult to plan in rainy times.

You never know how long it will rain, 15 minutes or a day. But hotel guests do not despair. During the rain, the hotel has something to do.

The sun and heat season begins in April. Already in May, when the air warms up well, a slow but steady rise in price begins both for apartments and for excursions.

Nutrition

Guests can eat at the hotel's Anjani Restaurant, which serves exclusively Western and Indonesian cuisine. Here you can choose a dish not only from the menu, but also from the buffet. In addition, the hotel has a bar that opens in the evening.

If the guests are not satisfied with the restaurant‚Äôs menu at the hotel, you can always go beyond its territory, where there is enough cafe for every taste.

Each guest will find something for himself. Although many are convinced: you can get used to the local food, and then you do not have to walk around the area and look for dishes of the usual cuisine.

Territory

Grand Whiz Hotel Nusa Dua Bali is a great place to lodge. The hotel has two well-kept terraces. One of them is for sunbathing, and the second is just for relaxation. Tourists who love sports can rent bicycles, but this service is charged separately. For lovers of swimming, the hotel has three pools - indoor, outdoor and deep.

In addition, there are wellness services:

  1. Massage chair.
  2. Massage services.
  3. SPA-center.
  4. Health Center.

The last three services are not included in the price of the permit and are paid separately.

The hotel has many amenities: concierge services, luggage storage, 24-hour front desk, tour desk (the staff will offer you a choice of the best sights of the island), free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, car rental, safe, convenience for guests with disabilities, airport shuttle, non-smoking rooms, sun umbrellas, beach chairs, beach or pool towels, drinks and food delivery in your room, business center, banquet hall, parking. Some of the listed services are not included in the price of the permit. Therefore, before ordering, it is advisable to check prices with staff.

Service

The hotel service is excellent. Concierge service is at a high level. He is helpful and helps to solve some issues that relate to hotel accommodation. As they say, the concierge at the hotel is the person who tries to fulfill the wishes of the guests.

Room cleaning is daily. Ironing, cleaning and laundry services are also provided.

However, payment is charged separately. The staff speaks only English and Indonesian, which is not very convenient for Russian-speaking guests. Otherwise, the service at the Grand Whiz Hotel Nusa Dua exceeded all guests' expectations.

Vacation with children

Of course, many guests come to Bali with children of different ages. Therefore, each room has an extra bed. For a child under 5 years old, hotel accommodation is free. However, extra cots are not provided.

The hotel has a swimming pool and several swings for children. And beyond the territory there are enough playgrounds. And on the beach there are several slides that are made specifically for kids. Therefore, children can come to this hotel. Not only an adult, but also a child will rest.
