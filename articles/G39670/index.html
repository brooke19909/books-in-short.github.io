Digital calls

IP-telephony is a combination of old habits and modern technology. This is a set of communication protocols and methods that provide traditional dialing and two-way communication via the Internet and any other IP-based networks.

Unlike ordinary, landline phones, in which the interlocutor's voice is transmitted by an analog signal, IP-telephony encrypts sound in binary code and compresses it. This improves the quality of communication and reduces the load on the network. Other benefits of IP telephony calls are:

  1. Low cost of international and long distance calls.
  2. Independence from telephone lines.
  3. Make calls anywhere.

The last advantage should be the ability to block unwanted incoming calls at your expense by disabling the G8. Using IP-telephony is profitable and convenient, but, like everything new, you need to get used to it.

Types of IP Telephony

Internet calls can be made from a regular landline phone, from special IP equipment and even from a computer.

According to the type of device through which the connection is made, and there is a division of IP-telephony for the home into types:

  1. "Computer-to-computer" . For communication, subscribers need a PC with installed software and an Internet connection. The call in this case is similar to communication in Skype. This type of connection is the least common.
  2. Communication through the card. To make a call you need a regular landline telephone with tone dialing and an access card from the provider. To contact a friend, you first call the operator‚Äôs number, in tone mode, enter your identifier and PIN code, and then - the number of the called subscriber.
  3. Communication via IP phone. A dedicated IP phone is already configured for communication. All you need is an Internet connection. When making a call, the phone automatically connects you to the provider, connects to the proxy server and calls the subscriber.

Many probably have a question: what is an IP phone? It is a regular handset with a handset and a keyboard, it works independently of a computer and can accept a call at any given time.

Necessary equipment

Configuring communication through the adapter

When choosing an operator, ask what kind of equipment is needed to connect to a dedicated line, is it easy to buy and expensive.

Some providers offer their customers fully configured adapters for communication with other subscribers. In this case, the entire configuration process is reduced to the sequential execution of several steps:

  1. Use the instructions to connect the adapter to the network.
  2. Connect the landline telephone to the LINE1 slot using a standard telephone cable.
  3. Turn on the adapter by inserting the power supply into a power outlet, wait for the download to finish (2-3 minutes).
  4. Pick up the phone, wait for the dial tone.

As soon as you hear a tone on the other side of the line, you should know that you figured out what IP-telephony is and were able to connect the equipment correctly. Now there are no barriers to communication.
