The best hotels of Khabarovsk: photos and reviews

Khabarovsk hotels in their diversity offer the city guests the best conditions for a comfortable stay. A tourist with any level of income can find refuge in one of the following hotel complexes and at the same time not experience discomfort.

Boutique hotel





Boutique Hotel "Khabarovsk City" is a modern and beautiful building, which was born back in 2008. The hotel can offer visitors the latest sets of modern and comfortable furniture, central heating of all rooms. In a word, this wonderful complex has everything that is needed for a real rest and recharging with forces and positive emotions.

Location

Khabarovsk City Boutique Hotel is favorably located a few minutes drive from the center, not far from the square on the banks of the Amur River. The attention of city guests is offered 44 magnificent rooms, which are divided into:

  • Suite rooms in the amount of 4 pieces.
  • 16 rooms with a studio layout.
  • 16 double rooms with spacious beds and everything you need to accommodate the newlyweds.
  • 10 standard rooms, in which there are 2 single beds.
  • 5 standard rooms with one bunk bed.

Khabarovsk hotels of this level are distinguished by the availability of superior apartments.

Description of rooms

Each luxury room is individually decorated, apartments of other categories have differences only in the color of the walls, furniture and textiles. Also included in the package of any room are:

  • Satellite dish with many entertainment and news channels.
  • A dedicated line of wireless Internet (Wi-Fi) with good speed, which allows you to chat on social networks, watch videos on Youtube and other video hosting sites, and even play multiplayer online games.
  • A miniature bar with a refrigeration unit and many soft and alcoholic drinks to choose from.
  • A telephone with radio support that can operate at a distance of several hundred meters.
  • Bathroom with a combined toilet, equipped with shower, sink, towel and hand dryer, responsive to the shadow of the human body, a set of five towels, other hygiene attributes (there is even a hairdryer).

Infrastructure

In addition to all of the above, the Khabarovsk City boutique hotel has a well-developed infrastructure. Its guests have at their disposal a conference room equipped with a video projector for slide shows, comfortable armchairs and a wide office table, as well as audio equipment necessary for productive activities. The meeting room is about 80 square meters in size, which can easily accommodate 50 people without experiencing any inconvenience.

Khabarovsk hotels of this level provide visitors with many additional services.

Food and other services

The hotel complex has a buffet with a varied menu, including pastries, hot dishes, soups and drinks.

Additional services include:

  • Laundry, providing the ability to wash and iron things and even chemically remove stains of any complexity.
  • Room service (cleaning, breakfast delivery, airing).
  • Holding business meetings and banquets.
  • Services of a beauty salon and hairdresser, in which you can easily transform by changing your hair style or hair color, as well as undergo spa, stone therapy and others.
  • Order and purchase air and train tickets.

Unfortunately, the Khabarovsk City hotel is not suitable for families with children, however, this issue is still under development. As for the adult population, there are the following forms of leisure:

  • Carrying out various excursions and walks around the city and its environs, visiting a bar or restaurant with a variety of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, live music (wind instruments). Artists perform here every night.
  • In the lobby of the hotel there is a small restaurant in which each guest will be able to plunge into the atmosphere of extraordinary cosiness and home warmth, and at the same time, be reinforced with dishes from his favorite home cuisine.
  • There is also a bar with a wide selection of light and tasty salads, diet desserts, smoothies and cocktails for every taste. On request, a fresh and fragrant cappuccino will be prepared for the visitor.

Customer reviews

Some Khabarovsk hotels really deserve to be talked about only in a positive way. Basically, vacationers of this complex are satisfied with the service. Most of the guests noted that the hotel staff is very friendly and helpful. Many appreciated the degree of comfort of the rooms offered for living.

The miniature bar with a variety of drinks for every taste, according to guests, is also a huge plus. The Khabarovsk City Boutique Hotel is very conveniently located; it takes only 10 minutes of free time to the beach and the promenade.

Of the minuses, guests note fairly high prices in a local restaurant, as well as the absence of a children's room in the complex.

Hotel "Olympic"





This is one of the most modern and comfortable places to relax and accommodate guests located in the city. Hotel Olympic (Khabarovsk) offers its visitors the following types of apartments:

  1. Single single room, which is equipped with a one and a half sleeping place (bed), telephone, Internet access line (Wi-Fi) with a maximum speed that allows you to conduct video communication sessions (Skype and similar instant messengers), play multi-player games, without any special obstacles Watch movies of any length. Each room has a cell phone, a shower and a bathroom with the latest plumbing and repairs, wardrobes, satellite TV, a minibar with a variety of drinks (both alcoholic and non-alcoholic) needed to place clothes. The hotel has several restaurants, all the necessary infrastructure (a store within walking distance, a cafe, a beauty salon, etc.). In addition to all of the above, the room has a climate control system, an extra bed, slippers and a bathrobe, a hairdryer and other comfort elements. The staff is very polite, if necessary, you can ask for help. The room is allocated a place for work, where a person can spend time sitting at a laptop. The apartments are equipped with separate safes, protected by security codes, so that every vacationer can safely leave all the valuables and important items there. Single room has a double bed, an armchair, a set of two cabinets designed to store all kinds of little things, a coffee table, all the necessary electronics (telephone, TV, cooling system, etc.).
  2. Club deluxe room is equipped with a double bed, a set of armchairs, a pouf, a bar counter with a minibar and a set of dishes, household appliances, as well as a bathroom with personal hygiene products, a shower, a jacuzzi and a bathroom. There is a kettle and a safe for any valuables. A spacious loggia allows you to admire the beautiful views and landscapes of the city of Khabarovsk.
  3. The suite category room consists of two rooms (a bedroom and a guest room), separated from each other by a special screen made of glass or wood. The living room is equipped with a corner sofa, armchairs, a pouf, a coffee table, all the necessary equipment. The bedroom is equipped with a comfortable bed, bedside tables, safe, climate control and a standard set of equipment. The living part of the room, like in many other hotels, has a bar counter and a small semblance of a kitchen where you can have a snack or a full breakfast, lunch or dinner. As for the payment, breakfast at the hotel is included in the price of all services provided.

Hotel Lime





This is one of the most famous hotel complexes in Khabarovsk, located in close proximity to the central part of the city and the famous Amur embankment. The street where the hotel is located provides easy access to the airport and train station. Travel time in this case is only about ten minutes by car or taxi. On the territory of "Lima" is located:

  • Indoor pool measuring 9 x 20 meters.
  • Sauna with infrared radiation and other newfangled accessories.
  • Turkish hammam.
  • Zone for children.
  • A round-the-clock service for registering new visitors.
  • Conference room for business negotiations.
  • Beauty salon with experienced stylists.
  • A bar with a room for playing billiards.
  • Barbecue and cafe with oriental cuisine.
  • Lobby bar with free Wi-Fi.
  • Secure parking with a wide area in order to leave the car.
  • Laundry with dry cleaning services for complex dirt and ironing.

Hotel Lime (Khabarovsk) was successfully renovated in 2013. In the room stock of the institution there are apartments of the following categories:

  • Suite
  • Junior Suite
  • Studio.
  • Standart room.
  • Superior Standard Room.
  • 2-5-bed rooms (beds with subsequent sharing).

Each room is equipped with a minibar, refrigerator, modern TV-panel, tea set and hygiene items.

Nutrition

The hotel successfully operates a buffet system with a variety of dishes and the possibility of self-forming portions. There is also a sushi bar and a restaurant with authentic Caucasian cuisine. Continental breakfast is another great opportunity to eat.

Reviews about the Lyme hotel are only positive. Guests are satisfied with their stay in the rooms of the institution. A special delight is the presence of a swimming pool and a sauna on site.

Hotel Zarina





Hotel "Zarina" (Khabarovsk) has its own restaurant, where guests are fed skilfully prepared dishes of Russian, European and Oriental cuisine. For extra comfort, guests at the hotel have a concierge, as well as qualified and polite reception staff. To ensure that guests always stay informed of all world events and in touch, stable Wi-Fi network is established on its territory. Also, for a separate monetary reward, it is possible to arrange a transfer for the hotel employees: guests will be taken to the nearest airport or railway station.

Description of rooms

The apartments in the Zarina complex have all the necessary equipment for a comfortable stay. Each room has a kettle, telephone, Internet, TV, refrigerator, bar, double bed and other necessary furniture (coffee table, bedside tables).

Important information for hotel guests

In the presence of rooms not occupied by vacationers, free registration of visiting citizens is carried out. In other cases, the hotel offers both cash and bank transfer payment cards of Sberbank of Russia. Check-in is from 13:00, check-out is until 12:00. The distance from the hotel to the airport and train station is 9 kilometers.

Description of rooms

The hotel offers vacationers accommodation in the following apartments:

  • Double room with one double bed.
  • Double room equipped with a bed, air conditioning and all necessary accessories, an excellent bathroom with fresh towels, a bathrobe for the guest, all bathing accessories.
  • Double room with one shared bed.
  • Double room with twin beds.
  • Superior twin room with bathrobe for each guest. There is climate control.
  • Superior room for couples with children. This apartment has a microwave, sofa, refrigerator, kitchenware and satellite TV with digital broadcasting.

There are also single rooms with all necessary furniture and air conditioning.

Hotel Parus





The institution was founded in 1964. This hotel not only still accepts guests, but also is a very significant place in the history of the city. The last time this building underwent a global renovation in 2008. Hotel Parus (Khabarovsk) is very favorably located in the very center of the city. Around is a park area with many trees, nearby is the river embankment. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel rises the famous Assumption Cathedral, in the vicinity there are a great many historical museums. Thanks to a winning place of construction, this hotel, unlike others, is the most important architectural monument in the whole city.

The attention of citizens and guests of Khabarovsk is currently offered as many as 3 floors of this complex, inside which are apartments of various categories: from economy class to the most prestigious and comfortable extra-luxury class.

In total, there are 82 rooms for every taste and budget. All of them are equipped with free Internet connection, telephone, satellite TV with many channels, bathrooms with working bathrooms, bedding, comfortable beds, spacious wardrobes, bedside tables, mirrors and even a room cooling and ionization system. All visitors unanimously declare that this hotel is perfect for relaxation.

Hotel Prestige





The Grand Hotel Prestige (Khabarovsk) took a place in the historical center of the city. It offers its guests 68 comfortable rooms, from whose windows a picturesque view of the Amur. The impeccable decoration of the apartments will not leave indifferent guests even with the most stringent requirements. The interior is made in the European style. Each room is equipped with modern furniture and necessary household appliances.

The Prestige Hotel (Khabarovsk) offers its guests a huge list of additional services, such as high-speed Internet, facilities for making hot drinks, laundry services, secure parking, and left-luggage offices.

It has its own confectionery. Tasty lunch and dinner are available at the local restaurant. Buffet breakfast is included in the price.

According to numerous positive reviews, we can safely say that for business trips you simply cannot find a better place than the Grand Hotel. Khabarovsk is a city of great opportunities, so many people come here on a business visit. For them, accommodation in Prestige is an ideal option.

Hotel Enigma

For tourists who visit the center of the Russian Far East for the purpose of relaxation, the Enigma Hotel (Khabarovsk) can be an excellent accommodation option.





This is a small hotel with comfortable rooms, which have everything you need. Free parking, internet. Each room has all the toiletries for which you also do not have to pay.

Almost all the hotels in Khabarovsk offer guests good conditions for relaxation. It all depends on the wishes of the client and his financial capabilities. In the city you can find both elite housing and budget accommodation.
