Hotel "Ibis" (Moscow): address, description, reviews

Moscow is a very large city, and many visitors come here. One of the problems that tourists, business people and students who decide to visit the capital have to solve is the search for a place to live. Most guests of Belokamennaya prefer to rent inexpensive rooms in budget hotels. One of the most popular hotel chains in the capital today is Ibis. Here you can rent a pretty decent room for not too much money.

Ibis Network: A General Description

Ibis hotels were built in Moscow by the French corporation Accor. All hotels under this brand belong to the budget class. Moscow Ibis hotels are marked with three stars. In Europe there are two-star Ibis hotels. The first hotel with this name was opened by Accor in 1974 in the French city of Bordeaux. Now the network has more than 700 hotels located in many cities around the world.





Guests of Ibis hotels, including those opened in Moscow, have the opportunity to participate in the profitable loyalty program offered by the network. The hotel‚Äôs hotel administration has developed a special point system according to which residents can receive various bonuses, vouchers and discounts. In general, guests of the capital with not too much income should definitely visit the Ibis Hotel. Moscow is a huge city, and it is quite difficult to find a decent hotel at a not too high price in it. Here you can get good service and save money.

Moscow Ibis Hotels

To date, there are three hotels in the capital under this sign. One of them is located near the station. "Dynamo". Prices are the lowest. The second hotel is located in the Center of Bakhrushin, and the third - on Paveletskaya. Next, we consider what service each of these hotels offers its visitors, and what reviews are available about them.

Ibis at Dynamo metro station

This hotel, like other hotels of the chain, is chosen mainly by students, couples, middle-class business people, etc. who come to Moscow. Its unconditional advantages include low cost of accommodation, friendly service and relatively comfortable rooms.





How to get to Ibis on Dynamo

You need to go to this hotel along the Zamoskvoretskaya metro line. Exit should be at the Dynamo metro station. The distance from it to the hotel is about a kilometer. You need to go through Leningradsky Prospekt. It‚Äôs better to cross the underpass opposite the Khodynsky Boulevard.

Accommodation Features

The Ibis Hotel (Moscow, metro station Dynamo) provides its visitors with not too large, but well-equipped rooms. Each has a bathroom with shower. They also have refrigerators, comfortable beds and televisions. Unfortunately, there are no teapots and microwave ovens at the Ibis Hotel.

If you wish, guests can use free Wi-Fi. The hotel itself has a restaurant. Lunches, breakfasts and dinners are not too expensive here. Children under 12 years old can generally eat for free. The restaurant is open around the clock.

Motorists who rented their Ibis Dynamo license plates can park their cars in the hotel‚Äôs underground parking lot. True, the cost of renting a place on it is quite expensive.

There are no special places to visit near this hotel. The only exceptions are Petrovsky Park and located about a kilometer from the hotel Ice Palace CSKA.

Visitors reviews

In general, guests of the capital praise the Ibis Hotel (Moscow, Dynamo). Visitors note primarily the courtesy of the staff and the convenience of the rooms of this hotel. Wi-Fi at the hotel is pretty slow, however, for surfing on regular sites on the Internet, its speed is enough.

Many people like the comfort of the beds in the rooms. Sleeping on them is really comfortable. The hotel is located in a quiet place, and therefore noises do not bother guests at night. The mattresses on the beds, judging by the reviews, are very soft, and the blankets are warm. Some of the inconveniences of the hotel include only that the water in the shower can arbitrarily change the temperature, which, of course, is not too pleasant.





Hotel on Paveletskaya

Hotel "Ibis" (Moscow, metro station "Paveletskaya") offers its visitors 147 double and single rooms. If you wish, you can rent a room "standard" or "comfort". It is also a three-star hotel, excellent for middle and low income citizens.

Ibis Hotel (Paveletskaya): how to get

It is best to go to this hotel also by metro - to the Paveletskaya or Serpukhovskaya stations. From them to the hotel in just 10 minutes. If desired, the place of residence can be reached by tram. The location of this hotel is also very convenient for those citizens whose trains arrive in the capital at Paveletsky Station. It is located in the immediate vicinity of the hotel. The exact address of this hotel: Moscow, st. Pinch, d. 22, building 1.

Rooms

Hotel "Ibis" (Moscow, "Paveletskaya") offers its visitors rooms equipped with modern TVs and air conditioning. Some rooms also have refrigerators and safes. A very good solution would be to stay at this hotel for the disabled. For them, the hotel has 3 rooms specially equipped.

Like Dynamo, the hotel at Paveletskaya has free Wi-Fi. You can park your car in a nearby public car park. Meals here are also not included in the price. However, it can be ordered at an additional cost. The hotel has a restaurant and a lobby bar. Please note that pets are allowed in the rooms. However, this service is charged extra.
