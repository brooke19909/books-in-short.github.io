Where can I go without a passport?

When it comes to a vacation spot, everyone wants to go there quickly, without wasting time on preparing a visa. This is especially true for those who do not even have a foreign passport. There are countries of visa-free or simplified entry for citizens of Russia, border crossing with which is possible by internal passport. These include Tajikistan, South Ossetia, Belarus, Kazakhstan and others. Ukraine is also included in visa-free countries for Russians (2016), the list of required documents to enter its territory is still supplemented with a foreign passport.

As a rule, in order to travel, in any case, an identity card for each adult and a birth certificate for children are required. In addition, the documents of the child should have a stamp confirming the citizenship of the Russian Federation, even if the family travels to visa-free countries for Russians, where a visa and an international passport are not needed .

Most often in such states it is allowed to stay up to three months, and at the border, tourists will have to fill out a questionnaire or receive a migration card, which is issued on a train or plane for free.
