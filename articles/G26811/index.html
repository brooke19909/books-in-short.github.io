Concert Halls of St. Petersburg: Concert Hall "October"

St. Petersburg is the center of cultural life. And an important place in it is occupied by concert halls, which have become a popular stage for entreprise performances and various concerts. Among the most famous venues is BKZ Oktyabrsky.



St. Petersburg. The past

The face of the northern capital is constantly changing. And few people already remember what a corner of the city looked like at the intersection of Ligovsky Prospect, Starorusskaya and 4th Sovetskaya Streets.

One of the main urban development features of the layout of St. Petersburg is the orientation of a group of streets on the square as the center of the ensemble. In this regard, it seemed visually that the main structure of the square seemed to close the street opening. Thus, this St. Petersburg section was organized: the target of the Zhukovsky street, oriented specifically to the location of the current BKZ - the Summer Horse Square was closed by the Greek Church, founded here by decree of Alexander II in 1861, by an amazing neo-Byzantine-style master Roman Ivanovich Kuzmin.

BKZ. The origins

Due to the tendency to close churches after 1917, this church was also closed, only much later than many others in the late 30s. During the Great Patriotic War, a military training center was located in the building of the Greek Church, but after a bomb hit the building it stood for a long time inactive. When the bomb was taken out, the structure remained unowned and gradually collapsed. And by 1961 it was dismantled.
