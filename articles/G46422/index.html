How to make a bouquet of sweets with your own hands. Workshop for beginners

Presently, giving women flowers is not something that is not fashionable, not, rather, creative. An increasing number of young people who want to surprise the lady of the heart do not present her with roses, but with a chic bouquet made from various varieties of sweets. Such a bouquet will be an excellent gift not only to the chosen one, but also to the boss, girlfriend or grandmother. We will tell you how to quickly and easily create a bouquet of sweets with your own hands. A master class will help to create a real masterpiece without the slightest difficulty. What do you need?

Bouquet of sweets with their own hands. Master Class



Creating a real work of chocolate and seductively delicious art that can impress the most sophisticated connoisseur of beauty, you should remember the main points. The bouquet should be designed in the same style. If you plan to make a really beautiful gift, then try to comply with the measure. You should not draw up the finished composition in a multilayer package, trimmed with rhinestones, beads, ribbons and bows. In order for it to make a real sensation, you should choose good quality chocolate, and it must be in an individual package.

What form of bouquet to choose

To make a bouquet of sweets with your own hands, a master class to create which

we offer, you need: a box of good chocolates in individual packaging, wrapping paper or netting, long skewers or sticks, green leaves of living plants, the base of a bouquet or a floral sponge. Based on what composition you want to create, the choice of natural flowers depends. In order to create an unusual bouquet of sweets with your own hands, roses, daisies, gerberas, chrysanthemums will work best. You can choose any flowers with wide centers or buds. The main thing is that the leaves do not fall off after a second touch on the flower. Most preferred is the classic round shape. Remember what luxurious bouquets collected from small roses look like? This will be the perfect form. In this case, the candies will be located close to each other, so you don‚Äôt need a lot of greenery to decorate, and the person to whom the masterpiece is intended will receive a magnificent analogue of a flower bouquet as a gift.

What sweets are worth choosing



The ideal solution would be chocolates packed in foil. Thus, the middle of our flower will be not only tasty, but also beautiful. Perfectly fit Ferero or "Korkunov", you can also use Lindor or any others of your choice. However, if you plan to give such a bouquet to an extravagant and extraordinary person, then you can not be limited only to chocolate. For example, it would be quite appropriate for a girl to collect a bouquet of colorful candies or lollipops. Original look long, packed in transparent cellophane, toffees. In this case, it is better to arrange such a composition in a cellophane wrapper, on which some joyful motifs are painted along the edge. Add green leaves if desired. Thanks to the riot of colors, the bouquet looks bright and impressive and without green leaves. Design, sweets, packaging - it all depends only on your preferences and imagination.

Bouquet of sweets. Master class - a miracle made by yourself

To create such a composition, you must first decide what candy will be attached to. If these are flowers with wide cores, then each candy is pounded on a skewer, and then fixed on the flower. Try to make the skewer evenly enter the stem, then there will be much more chances that the composition will last long. If the bouquet does not plan on the availability of fresh flowers, then a candy is strung on a long skewer or barbecue stick, previously painted green. At the other end, the skewer is attached to a floral sponge or any other suitable material. Having thus strengthened all the prepared in advance skewers with sweets, we add a few twigs of natural greens to help hide the stems of our sweets. The final step is to arrange our bouquet either in the basket or in the net. Having fixed it, we decorate it with several ribbons or an artificial butterfly. That's it. We have a bouquet of sweets with our own hands ready. The master class was very simple, right?
