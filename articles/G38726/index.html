Crochet flowers - universal knitting motifs

Most of the fair sex at least once in their life thought about creating something handmade. Perhaps it should be a scarf, a napkin or just flowers crocheted. However, there are not so many who managed to carry out their idea. The main problem for beginners is that they choose too complex or voluminous projects and, after working for two or three hours, get very tired and disappointed in crocheting.



Before starting work

If you approach the search for a model, judiciously evaluating your own strengths and skills, then after a few hours you can enjoy the first finished work. Experienced knitters recommend mastering one simple motif and using it to create a small shawl, scarf or just a brooch on a dress or bag. The best option in this case is a crocheted flower. The layout of each model will be different, but there are common points in them. Firstly, the novice craftswoman will be able to get used to her hook and choose the appropriate thread size. Secondly, having mastered a similar motive and simply replacing the yarn with a thicker one, you can create large models of rugs and tracks very quickly. To crochet flowers, it does not take a lot of time or thread, you just need to choose the right pattern and have patience for the first twenty most difficult loops.

What kind of knit plant?

The most common models are red and pink poppies, delicate daisies and exotic Hawaiian plants. The latter option has already captured its niche not only in amateur knitting - many professional designers use crocheted colorful flowers to create their exclusive brooches, necklaces and hats for movie stars and glamorous bohemians.



If you want to create a variegated cape, a delicate napkin made of thin threads or a brooch that is called out, to calculate the total working time, you should first connect one motive. Then you can simply multiply the minutes or hours obtained by the total number of elements. To crochet Hawaiian flowers, you need to choose two or three contrasting tones of a suitable yarn. It is better if the threads are not shaggy, otherwise all the beauty of the sharpness of the lines may be lost. The hook is selected according to the thickness of the yarn.

Hawaiian knitting motifs

The crochet flower pattern uses the following abbreviations:

  • VP - the usual single air loop;
  • SBN - single crochet;
  • Psn - a simple half-column with one crochet;
  • Cc2H - a single column with two double crochets ;
  • CC is a simple connecting column.

Description of the Hawaiian Flowers motif

  1. Take the first thread color. 8 VP, then SS in 1 VP for ring formation.
  2. 1 VP to rise to the level, 12 SBN in the received ring, then SS in the first SBN.
  3. We tie a new color of yarn to any of the sc in the previous row. 1 VP, then SBN in the same loop, * one SBN in the next loop, 11 VP, one SBN in the same loop, 1 SBN in the next, 1 VP, 1 SBN in the next *. Repeat from sprocket to sprocket to the end of the row without knitting the last sc. Instead, you need to make an SS in the very first SBN.
  4. Knit in the same color. * 18 2 we knit in the resulting arch of 11 VP, then the SS in the arch of the previous row, which was obtained from 1 VP *. We repeat everything to the last petal and end the row with one SS.
  5. Attach a new thread. * 9 SBN, starting from the center of the side of the element, then 3 VP and vice versa, towards the center of motive 9 SBN, in the second-row arch, consisting of 1 VP, we knit 1 SBN *. We repeat to the end and to the beginning of the row makes one SS.
