"Dolphin" hotel (Pitsunda): a high-level vacation in an ecologically clean region

This year Abkhazia is one of the main vacation spots for the Russians, as popular as the Crimea or Sochi. However, rest in this wonderful country, unlike the Krasnodar Territory and the peninsula, will be more secluded and calm. In addition, the resort boasts wonderful landscapes and clean air.

Hotel base in Pitsunda

Mini-hotels are the most common accommodation option for travelers in Abkhazia. A small two-, three-story complex, a hospitable hostess and homemade food undoubtedly attract tourists.

As a rule, tourists come to the city and reserve and buy rooms on the spot, which is very risky due to their high workload, especially in the high season - from early June to late September. Therefore, it is more advantageous in this case to contact tourist operators - the cost is exactly the same as at the reception, and the problems are much less with checking into a hotel.

Pearl of the resort

Hotel "Dolphin" (Pitsunda) is a real unique place for these places, which are famous for modest accommodation options. Among the main advantages of the complex are the following:

  1. Freshness. The holiday home was fully built only in 2008, which is already unusual for Abkhazia. That is why a modern atmosphere reigns in all buildings and rooms, tourists use almost new furniture and good appliances.
  2. A large area with a park area for walks, exotic plants and benches for relaxation under the shade of majestic trees.
  3. Developed infrastructure is available in the complex, so if you want to just relax away from the noise and dust of the city, you can never leave the territory.
  4. Location on the shore of the bay.
  5. Indoor pool - thanks to him, you can come here to rest in the cold winter months.

Infrastructure "Dolphin"

Rest at the hotel is provided for tourists who are accustomed to high quality standards and excellent level of service.

  • The complex has a real Russian bathhouse and a hot Finnish sauna.
  • A spacious indoor pool with a depth of almost 2 meters, the use of which is included in the price. In addition, the water in it is fresh and is heated throughout the day.
  • A gym and sports games, including table tennis, volleyball court and tennis court, are offered for free by the Dolphin (hotel).
  • Pitsunda is quite popular among athletes in the summer - you can have a great time with an instructor from the center of water-motor sports, doing windsurfing, cutting waves on a jet ski or jet ski.
  • For children, the beach has colorful inflatable slides and a spacious playground under a canopy.
  • Also at the reception you can find a package of tours in Abkhazia and book your favorite.
