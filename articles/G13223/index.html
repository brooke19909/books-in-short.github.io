A great accessory for youth - plastic earrings

Every girl, girl, woman cannot remain indifferent at the sight of beautiful and original earrings, bracelets, rings and other jewelry. Today at the peak of popularity is jewelry made of polymer clay or, as it is also called, plastic. You can buy such jewelry on the market, in stores or at needlewomen. But it will be much more interesting to make them yourself. Moreover, by making jewelry yourself, you will save a good amount of money, as well as make an exclusive jewelry that is impossible to buy anywhere.



The presented master class provides information on how to make plastic earrings in the shape of raspberry berries with your own hands . First you need to purchase the necessary materials in the needlework store:

  • Polymer clay of green and raspberry color.
  • Stationery knife.
  • Latex gloves.
  • Metal, plastic or tile.
  • Baking paper.
  • Acrylic varnish.
  • Special accessories for the manufacture of jewelry (earrings).

In order to make earrings from plastic yourself, follow the instructions below.



  1. Put all necessary materials and tools on the table surface. Put gloves on your hands.
  2. Put a raspberry-colored polymer clay bar on a sheet and use a clerical knife to cut it into small pieces.
  3. Form balls from each of them. Next, blind them all together, giving the product the shape of a raspberry. Perform two such parts. Try to keep the berries the same size and shape.
  4. Roll green plastic into a 2-4 mm thick plate and cut the leaf with a knife. Try to convey the proportions and structure of a real raspberry leaf.
  5. Stick a leaf on top of the berry and lay the product on a baking sheet covered with heat-resistant paper. Since polymer clay is a very soft material, carry out all manipulations carefully and gently so as not to damage the parts of the product.
  6. Place the baking sheet in the oven, heat it to a temperature of 100 ¬∞ C and bake the product for 20 minutes.
  7. Allow the workpieces to cool, then cover them with a layer of acrylic varnish. Leave the product to dry completely. Lacquer gives earrings shine and extra strength.
  8. Proceed to the final stage of manufacturing - fixing accessories on earrings made of plastic. Shvenzy - special elements for making jewelry - are screwed into plastic with light and slow movements. That's all. Beautiful cool Malinka earrings are made and are already asking to decorate the ears of their mistress.

Such decoration will be to the face and little girls. If these are earrings for children, then you can glue eyes and a smile on the berries. It will look fun and cute. For older ladies, a necklace or bracelet can be made with earrings. Such an ensemble will add a touch of romance and charm to the female image.

You can make unique and inimitable earrings from plastic yourself. Pick up a piece of polymer clay, and you will immediately feel a surge of inspiration and imagination. Have a nice craft!
