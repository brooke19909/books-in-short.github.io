Black Sea new building. How it all started

The sanatorium "Blue Wave" (Gelendzhik), whose photos are presented in the article, began its existence in the distant 1964. It was then that the board of the Rostov regional union of consumer cooperation made the decision to build a large recreation center in the area of ‚Äã‚ÄãSolntsedar. After settling all issues, 5 hectares of land on the bay shore were allotted for the remake. These few hectares of land were more like a huge swampy wasteland with sparse vegetation. Nikolai Ivanovich Sheiko became the person who led this grandiose construction project, and part-time as the first director of the camp site. By the beginning of next year's season, the wasteland began its transformation. Several dozen wooden houses and a small dining room were built. June 1, 1965 the tourist town met its first vacationers, almost 500 people.

Development dynamics

The development of the complex continued throughout the following years. In the period 1966-1982. two residential and one administrative buildings appeared on its territory, a dining room consisting of two halls (with a total capacity of 900 people), its own boiler room, library buildings and a cinema hall. Since the construction of the indoor pool in 1982, the tourist center has the opportunity to work all year round. The Blue Wave sanatorium type pension received its status in 1996. And since 2000, a complete phased reconstruction of the health resort began.

Development in 1999-2001

Since the late 90s, single rooms of the first building have been converted into two-room junior suites. This significantly reduced the number of seats, but at the same time allowed to improve living conditions. Along with this, the dining room was completely converted into a cafe-bar "Meeting". Sports fields and a children's playground were repaired.

The forces of the health resort administration redesigned the boat station. A psychological assistance center and a speleological chamber were organized in it. But at the same time, it continues to fulfill its main functions. On its territory there is all the necessary special equipment - a boat "trophy", jet skis, inflatable slides and bananas, trampolines and catamarans. The indoor pool has also changed. The stained glass windows and the bowl of the tank were replaced. Installations of the seawater heating system and hydromassage, careful filtering of the water and its cascade supply make it possible to say that this structure is one of the best on the coast. There are also a bathroom, a phytobar, a gym. From 2007-2008 in the building of the former administrative building, the medical SPA-center began its work. There are new residential rooms. A dance floor and an outdoor pool have been rebuilt. In those same years, the central alley of the boarding house, whose true decorations are fountains and a chapel, gained its present beautiful appearance. The Blue Wave sanatorium, popular throughout Gelendzhik, is deservedly considered one of the main leaders in the tourism industry of the Krasnodar Territory. The staff of the institution in 2010 took pride of place on the Board of Honor of the resort city.

Year-round Black Sea coast is visited by a huge number of tourists. Someone goes to rest and gain strength before the working year, someone to improve his health, and someone - for both. Gelendzhik is especially popular. The Blue Wave Sanatorium, located in this city for up to 600 people, is an ideal place for any spa traveler. But not only vacationers will find here everything necessary for a pleasant pastime and recovery. The Blue Wave (a sanatorium in Gelendzhik) is always ready to hold business meetings, conferences, rallies and various seminars on its sites. According to the classification system of hotels and other accommodation facilities, this health institution has 3 stars.

Boarding house profile

For many years a unique medical and diagnostic base was created in the health resort. Its main profiles are diseases of the digestive and respiratory organs, circulatory system and central nervous system. In the health resort, diagnostics and therapy of metabolic disorders, diseases of the musculoskeletal system, genitourinary and endocrine systems are carried out. Over 7,000 people can get treatment here every year. The developed infrastructure of the center allows you to do this with maximum efficiency.

Medical service

Each newcomer to treatment is examined using DDFAO technology, which is intended for functional examination based on an analysis of the electrochemical parameters of intercellular fluid in the body. Laboratory-clinical and biochemical studies, ultrasound, ECG, etc. are also carried out. If necessary and according to indications, uroflowmetry, a computer assessment of cardiac activity are performed, consultations of a mammologist and other narrow specialists are held. According to the results of examinations, treatment is prescribed taking into account all the individual characteristics of the patient. The Blue Wave sanatorium, popular throughout Gelendzhik, is the only health resort of the Black Sea coast that has the Piezoson-300 shock wave installation in its arsenal. This unique medical device is able to relieve pain in osteochondrosis, arthritis, arthrosis and other diseases of the human musculoskeletal system. Dry and underwater traction of the spine will also help in the fight against these diseases.

Other services

Classic, vacuum, Thai, honey, soap and many other types of massage can be recommended for you to use. The physiotherapy department has almost all types of modern equipment, which is necessary for the treatment of a wide variety of diseases. Magnetic and laser therapy devices, lymphatic drainage, hirudo-and acupuncture, ozone therapy and much more. Psychological Relief Center will help those in need to find peace of mind, get rid of depression and stress. A specialist psychologist with the help of modern techniques will help the nervous system return to normal. The health resort has its own small pebble beach.



Location and transport

Pension "Blue Wave" is located in the resort city of Gelendzhik on the first line of the Black Sea coast of the Krasnodar Territory of Russia. Transport connection with the city is excellent. Shuttle buses will take you from the gates of the health resort to anywhere in the city. From the intercity bus station, which is located near the center of Gelendzhik, you can safely leave for the neighboring resort towns and villages. There are several airports in the region, one of them is located in the vicinity of the city.
