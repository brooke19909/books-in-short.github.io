Description of the recreation center "Lavender" (Kabardinka)

Rest is always associated with pleasant moments. If possible, people try to come to nature and the sea, as this helps to improve health and general condition. At the recreation center "Lavender" (Kabardinka), guests are waiting for good conditions at a moderate cost. In the warm season, this place is perfect for relaxing with your family or your loved one.

general information

The recreation center "Lavender" (Kabardinka) is located in a rather good place. Tourists can enjoy the clean air and beautiful nature. The village is part of Gelendzhik, therefore it is located quite close to the city. The coast is well known for the fact that families often come here from all over the country. In addition, the village has a resort base for children and youth.

There are several different options available for guests to stay at the base. Economy class houses are double, triple and quadruple. They have all the necessary furniture, and the toilet and shower are in a separate block. Economy class houses also allow you to rent a room for one or more visitors. There are beds, bedside tables and other necessary furniture. Shower and toilet are in the building with rooms.

In addition, the base has standard rooms suitable for the accommodation of two or three guests. They have furniture, air conditioning, TV, shower and bathroom. Superior room has two rooms and is designed for 2 guests. For guests prepared: furniture, TV, air conditioning, refrigerator. There is also a shower and toilet.

Reviews about the recreation center "Lavender" in Kabardinka are mostly positive. Experienced people write that they were satisfied with the rest. But there are those who recommend not to rest here. They did not like the conditions and attitude of the staff.

Where is the base

The exact address of the recreation center "Lavanda": Krasnodar Territory, Kabardinka, Revolutionary Street, building 61.

There are several ways to get to the base. By train, you need to get to the railway station of the cities of Anapa, Novorossiysk or the station "Tunnel". Next, tourists need to take a bus, transfer or minibus to the bus station of the city of Gelendzhik, and from it by bus or minibus to the village of Kabardinka. Exit should be at the bus station.

By plane, you can fly to the airports in Gelendzhik, Anapa or Krasnodar. From there, by minibus or bus, you need to get to the bus station in Gelendzhik. Here travelers can take a shuttle bus or bus to get to the bus station stop in Kabardinka.

Additional features

At the Lavender recreation center in Kabardinka, guests can cook their own meals in the kitchen, which is specially equipped for this with everything necessary. Crockery and other utensils are available for hire. For the smallest there is a special room for games and a playground. For adults, a sports field. There are special cameras for storing things. Cars are also available to leave in a guarded parking lot. On the territory there are recreational areas and barbecue facilities.

Pebble beach is quite close. From the recreation center "Lavender" in Kabardinka to the sea you need to walk only 400 meters. There is a cafe on the beach, as well as cabins for changing clothes. For entertainment, guests can rent a jet ski, parachute or ride a banana. Near the beach there are many cafes and food outlets, supermarkets, a market and various shops.
