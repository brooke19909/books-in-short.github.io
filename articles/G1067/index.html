How to enable roaming on MTS? International MTS roaming: tips, cost

Types of Roaming

To begin with, there are several types of roaming operating in the MTS tariff packages. It is national, international, network and "Crimean". Let's start with the latter, since the easiest way to explain how it works is the tariffs for communication with the peninsula. Since, due to the geographical features of the location, Crimea is serviced by local operators, specialized tariffs are introduced for Russian subscribers to communicate with it. With their help, you can, for example, communicate with relatives who have gone on summer vacations.

Another type of roaming is network. This is a term that characterizes the connection of a subscriber with users who are not related to his network. For example, if a MegaFon client calls a phone number served by MTS, this can be safely called network roaming. Calls outside the network are usually more expensive due to the fact that the operator involves another company in the provision of communication services.

However, the topic of our article today will not be these two types of roaming, but national and international. We will pay attention to them.

Country Roaming





Within Russia, given its size, there are also different coverage areas of the mobile network. Due to this, in the process of moving a subscriber between them, different operators serve him. For this reason, when traveling around the country, remember: in some cases, communication may be more expensive. How to use the services?

To begin with, before you use all the communication options abroad, you need to connect two services that MTS provides - International and National Roaming, as well as the International Access option. They are connected as a single team, provided that the user is serviced for more than 6 months and makes contributions to the mobile account at least 550 rubles a month, or is a subscriber for more than 12 months and at the same time simply does some replenishment of the account (regardless of the amount).
