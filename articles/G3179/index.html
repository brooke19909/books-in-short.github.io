Negative reviews

Room service, landscaping, food and other moments in this hotel, unfortunately, are imperfect. Travelers note such shortcomings of this institution:

  • dim lighting in the bathroom;
  • there is no shelf in the bathroom where you can put shower gel, shampoo and other cosmetics;
  • in the console - discharged batteries;
  • the ventilation system in the bathroom does not work well;
  • the room has no overhead lighting; inconvenient design of a shower cabin;
  • low-quality plumbing;
  • a fairly limited selection of dishes in the cafe;
  • during off-season, catering establishments on the territory close very early;
  • some rooms overlook the noisy roadway;
  • the rooms have some malfunctions and "scuffs" that the staff for some reason does not eliminate;
  • far enough from the central part of the resort (you need to travel three stops by bus);
  • slippery shower tray;
  • beautiful sea views spoil numerous buildings;
  • the pools are not carefully maintained;
  • on the carpet there are places burned with cigarettes;
  • the hotel area is heavily blown by the winds;
  • in the rooms you can see insects (this problem is especially acute for the first floor);
  • despite the fact that smoking is prohibited on the territory, everything (including rooms) smelled of tobacco smoke;
  • the free beach nearest to the hotel is very crowded, and therefore there are always crowds;
  • the cafe where breakfast is held is located in the basement, and therefore there is a rather gloomy atmosphere;
  • in the corridors of the hotel there is a steady and pungent smell of dampness;
  • some rooms do not have balconies, which are not warned when booking;
  • the minibar practically does not cool drinks;
  • unstable wireless internet;
  • poor sound insulation in the rooms;
  • sometimes the reception staff behave incorrectly, talking on the phone while the guests are waiting;
  • during off-season, not only the rooftop pool is closed, but also the terrace, and therefore you will not be able to enjoy beautiful views;
  • the hotel has virtually no territory, and therefore guests will have nowhere to walk.

General impression

Hotel California 4 *, despite significant shortcomings, is still one of the most popular in Gelendzhik. This is largely due to the optimal ratio of price and quality of services provided. Also, we can not say about the proximity of the institution to the sea. Perhaps the main advantage of the hotel is the heated rooftop pool. Guests will certainly appreciate the availability of a complimentary breakfast, as well as the possibility of ordering a comprehensive meal.

Unfortunately, this hotel is not without drawbacks. Despite the fact that the institution is relatively new, there are already a lot of malfunctions and scuffs that are not resolved in a timely manner. The distance from the city center creates some inconvenience for outdoor enthusiasts. It is also worth noting that if you relax during the off-season, some of the hotel services will not be available to you.
