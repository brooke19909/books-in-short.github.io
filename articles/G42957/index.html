Knitting pattern "Rhombuses" - description and diagram

Knitters are constantly looking for a way to make their products not only beautiful, but also unique. That is why many of them prefer to create models on their own, rather than using ready-made descriptions from the Internet and magazines. In this case, the choice of the pattern of the future product is of great importance.

Rhombuses in knitting

Geometric knitted patterns are very popular. Among them, the pattern "Rhombuses" with needles is widespread. It can be used in almost any product. This can be openwork blouses or warm sweaters, hats, scarves and mittens, men's or women's things. Rhombs look great on children's clothes.





Depending on the type of future product ( knitting is considered), the Rhombus pattern can belong to one of the following categories:

- simple, based on the alternation of front and back loops ;

- openwork;

- Arana or weave patterns;

- jacquard.

Patterns that combine elements of several categories also look unusual and chic.

Simple rhombuses

The simplest ones are rhombuses based on the usual alternation of front and back loops. With full adherence to the scheme, even inexperienced knitters will easily get chic convex patterns. Products associated with their use look relief and voluminous. In order not to lose such an effect, do not iron them.





One of those is the pearl rhombus pattern. It refers to two-sided patterns, because it does not have a distinct reverse side. The rapport of the pattern includes 14 loops, which means you need to dial as many loops on the knitting needles that will be a multiple of this number to get a diamond pattern with knitting needles. With a description, knitting it is quite simple. The main thing to consider is that only odd rows are given in it, even rows are matched according to the pattern. The loops enclosed in the * sign are rapport, which is repeated to the end of the row.

Openwork rhombuses

Knitted rhombuses pattern, knitting with needles, is most often used for women's and children's sweaters, as well as shawls. Due to the presence of yarn and loops knitted together, it is more complex and requires some experience in knitting.





In the process of creating a diagram of such a pattern, it is important to consider the fact that the crochets should not add the number of loops in a row. Therefore, they need to be done as much as reductions.

Drawn in order to knit openwork patterns with Rhombus knitting needles, the patterns often contain the terms: knit two loops with a slope to the right or left. But not all needlewomen know how to do this.

To get two loops knitted together with a slope to the right, you need to insert a knitting needle into both loops at the same time and knit a regular facial one. If a tilt to the left is required, the first loop is removed, the second is knitted with the front one and the removed loop is pulled through it.

Weave rhombuses

Many needlewomen consider the pattern "Rhombuses" (knitting needles), made on the basis of crossing loops, the most original. In some sources, the drawings obtained in this way are called aranes. Products made with their use are unusually textured and colorful.





When performing such patterns, it is necessary to take into account a number of features:

- yarn consumption may increase compared to smooth knitting;

- too tight knitting complicates the process of crossing the loops;

- it is convenient to use an additional knitting needle for weaving. The best option is a tool specially adapted for this, which has a bend in the middle that does not allow the loops to slide off.

A huge number of diamond loops based on crossed loops can be found. They can be used both on the whole product, and on its part.

Jacquard rhombuses

Jacquard refers to patterns based on two or more colors. They are successfully used in various products designed for both men and women of all ages. To make the fabric as beautiful as possible, it is important to follow a number of traditional rules for knitting multicolor patterns:

1. It is important to monitor thread tension. Broaches formed from the wrong side, if not performed correctly, can significantly distort knitting.

2. When changing the color of the thread, it should be twisted so that there are no holes on the product.

3. If you want to get rid of broaches, you can use the "lazy" jacquard patterns or knit each colored patch from a new ball.

Combined Techniques

Another excellent technique that can be used to knit a rhombus pattern with knitting needles is the combination of several techniques. Combining several schemes in one product, you can create real masterpieces.

It is important to monitor the compatibility of patterns. Many people prefer to arrange them symmetrically relative to the middle of the canvas. Then the wardrobe will be concise and complete.

The variety of ways in which patterns are made with Rhombus knitting needles, patterns presented in large numbers in various sources, require attention and perseverance from knitters. But those who are not afraid of difficulties will be surely pleased with the result.
