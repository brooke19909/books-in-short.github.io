Thomas Beach Guest House 1 * (Ashvem, North Goa): photos and reviews of tourists

If you have a desire to soak up on the golden beaches, enjoy the mountain scenery and learn the ancient secrets of Ayurveda, then it is worth visiting the mysterious and wise India. Today, this country is considered one of the most amazing in South Asia, still cherishing the ancient traditions of Hinduism and Buddhism. India is considered the most populous state in the world, but this does not stop falling in love with hundreds of tourists and travelers who come back here again and again.





Hotel Description

Thomas Beach Guest House is a cozy and quiet hotel located in the Ashvem region. The distance from the hotel to the airport is 60 km. The nearest city, located near Thomas Beach Guest House 1 *, is Morsim. Nearby there are a large number of local cafes and restaurants where tourists can try national Indian dishes and drinks. Easy and quick access to the hotel is possible by public transport or taxi. If you wish, you can rent a car during your stay in the country. Many establishments are located on the shore, so you can really relax and enjoy your vacation.

Thomas Beach Guest House Hotel was founded in 2014, in October. The region where the hotel is located, namely North Goa, is considered a truly amazing place. It is here that you can not only relax while lying on the beach, but also spend time actively, organize your thoughts and succeed in finding yourself.





Rooms

The three-story Thomas Beach Guest House Hotel has a total area of ​​900 m2, also has two blocks and has 17 comfortable rooms, namely:

  • 7 standard rooms in which there is no air conditioning;
  • 10 standard air-conditioned rooms.

Room Types:

  1. Non AC room (room without air conditioning).
  2. Standard AC (air-conditioned room).
  3. Deluxe (superior room with air conditioning).




The area of ​​one room, regardless of the presence of air conditioning in it, is 40 square meters. m. Each room is equipped with a small terrace or balcony, which gives the standard guest rooms more convenience. Each room has one double bed, chairs, a desk, a large mirror, bedside tables, toiletries. If necessary, or for children, the administration provides an extra bed. The flooring in the rooms is tile. For an additional fee (about 50 rupees per day), you can use services such as a safe (for storing currency or expensive items) or a mini fridge.





The rooms have:

  • shower / bath, bathroom (bath accessories are available) + hot and cold water;
  • fan;
  • cable TV;
  • telephone.

In 2016, Thomas Beach Guest House India was restored, making the appearance of the hotel more attractive, neat and modern. The rooms are equipped with new appliances, furniture is selected on the principle of "convenience and spaciousness in the room."





Hotel Policy

Thomas Beach Guest House 2 * offers free hotel accommodation for young children (under 5 years old). Cots can be provided for an extra charge, or use existing bedding in rooms. According to the rules of the hotel, children over 5 years old are considered to be adults. When booking 5 rooms or more, discounts for the period of stay may be provided.

Entertainment and sports

Tourists are invited to attend yoga classes with a professional instructor. Thanks to the sparsely populated beaches of Ashvem, guests can enjoy fine sand, flat beaches, clear sea and picturesque nature. In this region, you can see boulders of volcanic origin, which are located right on the seashore. A great option is to visit various excursions that can be ordered at the hotel. If desired, outdoor enthusiasts can rent bicycles or try water sports, namely:

  • diving;
  • kiting;
  • swimming;
  • water skiing;
  • diving, etc.

Despite the gentle slope and a wide variety of water sports, tourists are not advised to swim far, because in the Arabian Sea there are a lot of strong undercurrents. The maximum distance for swimming and swimming is 200 meters from the coast. The sand on the shore is slightly darker compared to the south coast. Thomas Beach Guest House is located 100 meters from the beach, namely on the 3rd beach line. On the beach, tourists are offered for an additional payment:

  • sun loungers;
  • umbrellas.

Quite often, open-air parties are held on the beaches, where incendiary music is played - Goan trance, and you can watch vibrant dances of both local residents and tourists. Many travelers tend to come to Ashvem to relax, visit the noisy theme parties and just enjoy delicious food. It is worth noting that the service and maintenance are at a high level, so that the rest will be doubly comfortable. The beach offers professional massage therapists. Having once visited a hotel, tourists tend to return again and again to Thomas Beach Guest House, reviews of which are filled only with positive emotions.

sights

In the Ashvem region, there are quite a few modern and historical attractions that travelers will be interested to see from anywhere in the world. Among the most popular places are:

  • Chapel of the Holy Cross (Holy Cross Chapel);
  • Banyan Tree Club;
  • the temple of Sri Bhagavati, Pernem, which is dedicated to the goddess Bhagavati, is world famous for its statues of two elephants at the entrance (was built 500 years ago);
  • Brahma Temple, located in Satari and built in the fifth century BC. e .;
  • "Shanti" - a club with delicious author's cuisine;
  • Ponda Temple, which is considered the most revered place in Goa (this is where you can observe an excellent combination of modern and ancient temple architecture);
  • Kuepem temple, Paroda, which is located on a hill and is dedicated to the god Shiva (in the temple you can see Shivalinga carved from stone, pouring streams of water, as well as fascinating carved bas-reliefs).

Dining at the hotel and in the region

The restaurant is open for hotel guests only for breakfast. A continental breakfast is offered, namely tea / coffee, toast, jam, butter and bread. Traditional Indian cuisine can be enjoyed at cafes or restaurants on the beach or in nearby towns. Among the dishes of national cuisine, it is recommended to try curry, pies called samosa, biriani rice with raita sauce, chapati, thali and others. Most restaurants offer delicious dishes based on fish and seafood such as lobster, lobster, shrimp, crab, etc.
