When is it better to go on vacation to Cuba - features, attractions, interesting facts and reviews

What time of year and when is it better to relax in Cuba? Liberty Island is an island country washed by the waters of the Caribbean Sea and the Atlantic Ocean , whose coast is glorified by bottomless bays and comfortable bays, in which there are more than enough reefs and other coral formations.

The climate here is hot, however, it is very mild, thanks to the sea currents that wash Cuba completely from all sides. Due to this, the island is warm almost the whole year. However, the season for months in Cuba and when it is better to relax on the island is the main issue that worries tourists

At the same time, the heat on the coast is perceived comfortably, because a fresh wind blows there, bringing cool with it.

In Cuba, two seasons are distinguished: the dry season and the downpour season. As a rule, the rainy season lasts from May to October, and the driest - from November to April. However, at the same time, March is able to absolutely amaze guests of the state with heavy rains, and in September it can be dry and quiet for weeks. Predicting the weather in Cuba is not so simple.

Also, many people call the climate of the island trade winds. Typical tropical humid climate, largely due to winds that regularly blow in the same direction. The trade winds are formed in the Atlantic Ocean.

There is no single answer to the question of when it is better to go on vacation to Cuba. Rest in this country is beautiful at any time. And why, read below.

What time of year is better to go to Cuba

Cuba is an ideal country for year-round recreation. It is warm almost the whole year, but in order not to spoil the vacation with wet heat, hurricanes, typhoons and heavy rains, it is better to refrain from traveling in the summer and autumn. March in Cuba is rightfully considered the high season. Despite the often cool winds that blow from the Atlantic Ocean, there are more than enough fine days here. The water is very warm, there are many tourists on the beaches. As a rule, tropical rains rarely occur in Cuba in the springtime: about 5 days for the entire season. But it is worth noting that when the temperature exceeds +28 ° C, cool, refreshing rains are very desirable.

In the evening it’s not so hot, since April the night temperature is within +22 ° , and cool trade winds soften the heat. In many Cuban cities, very colorful festivals take place in April. It is in this month that the high season ends, since in May, as a rule, rains begin. But they do not last long (about two hours) and not every day.

On the island, the beginning of autumn can bring travelers not very pleasant surprises: usually in September-October, the island's resorts are covered with storms that make the sea uneasy, the water is muddy and saturate the air with moisture. Tour operators are aware of this. At this time, they strongly attract tourists by making favorable discounts. The temperature of autumn time differs little from summer. Is that it is reduced by several degrees. Although the water temperature is still comfortable: + 26 ... + 29 ° . The air also contains a large amount of moisture, so you need to prepare for the fact that acclimatization will take 2-3 days.

Winter

The most suitable time of the year for traveling. The winter months have three times less rainfall than the summer months. In addition, in winter, the lowest probability of tropical hurricanes. That is why this is a great time to relax and the answer to the question of when it is better to go on vacation to Cuba. The average air temperature in December is +23 ° C, and in January-February - +22.6 ° C. The coldest month of the year is January.

Spring

The season is quite comfortable for relaxing, but in May the rainy season begins, the air temperature and humidity increase. On average, meteorologists note in March about + 23.8 ° C, in April - +24.9 ° C, in May - +26 ° C.

Summer

The average temperature in Cuba in June is +26.9 ° C, and in July +27.3 ° C. The hottest month is August. The weather in Cuba during the summer season is very hot and humid. The maximum rainfall occurs directly in the summer months, but showers are usually short-lived and provide relief after the heat of the day. Those who like rains should not worry about when it is better to go on vacation to Cuba. After all, this is the best time for such a trip.

Autumn

The rainy season that began in May does not stop, usually until October. For this reason, the weather in Cuba during the autumn is also very humid. The heat decreases slightly, but the temperature difference in the summer and autumn months is small. Regular air warms up to +26.9 ° C in September, +26.1 ° C in October and +24.6 ° C in November.

History of cuba

The history of Cuba is shrouded in many interesting and unusual legends. Many people come to this country to admire interesting sights and magnificent tropical nature, the ocean. If the purpose of the trip is not to rest on the beach, but to visit interesting places, then you need to decide where it is better to go to relax in Cuba. When in every town there are many interesting places, then this is not very easy to do.

The history of the country goes deep into the centuries. As you know, Columbus discovered new lands, and Pope Alexander VI transferred them to Spain. For a long time, Cuba was considered one of the colonies of Spain. For many years, Liberty Island fought with the United States and did not obey them. In the early 60s of the last century, the Cubans still managed to defend their country and achieved independence.

In the 90s, the USSR was an ally of this country. The leaders of the two countries jointly made various decisions and supported each other. In general, it is a developed and very beautiful country with a unique and interesting culture and traditions. The Cubans themselves are so hospitable that many tourists admire it. This is a people who loves music and dances great dances. You can recharge with energy there for one hundred percent. And the hot Cuban sun will conquer you, you can be sure.

Sights of Cuba

There are enough attractions in Cuba. The main ones are in the capital, but there are many interesting places outside. What to see in Havana? The capital of Cuba was built in the colonial style. They are trying to follow the preservation of the historical appearance of the city. Significant places in the capital include the following:

  • Revolution square. The square is the heart of revolutionary Cuba. The central element of this square is the Jose Marti spire , rising to which you can see all the surroundings.
  • Cathedral. An ancient building of the XVIII century, storing relics of the tombs of the Pharaoh, as well as exhibits that once belonged to famous people of the country.
  • The fortress of El Morro. It is a symbol of Havana and is visible at the entrance to the bay. The lighthouse was built in the 19th century.

What to see outside Havana

But not only in the capital there are interesting places. Outside of them there are also enough of them:

  • Hemingway Museum. Visitors can see a huge library and trophies brought from Africa.
  • Valley of Vinales. A huge natural park, surrounded by picturesque hills, can surprise tourists with unique caves, which are about 45 kilometers long.
  • The city of Santiago de Cuba. A significant city that will be interesting not only to history lovers, but also to divers who can find places convenient for diving near its shores.

Interesting facts about Cuba

Cuba or Liberty Island, the capital of which is the city of Havana, is an original and unique country. Interesting facts about Cuba will help you learn more about it.

  1. Monopoly on mobile communications. In Cuba, there is only one mobile operator - Cubacel. Mobile communications are very expensive there, so the local population has developed its own free messaging system. For example, one call may mean going home. Three calls, indicates that the subscriber is delayed.
  2. Tough censorship. Cuba is in ninth place among the countries with the most severe censorship. Internet restrictions apply to all citizens of the state. You can have only one email address that is also viewed.
  3. Completely free education and medicine. All Cuban children are educated at the expense of the state. They provide everything you need while studying. Universities in Cuba are also free. Therefore, education and medicine in Cuba are at a fairly high level.
  4. The country is a haven for hitchhikers. It is in Cuba that there is a law according to which all vehicles with state numbers are required to bring tourists hitchhiking. Moreover, the fare is absent or purely symbolic.
  5. The ban on Coca-Cola. Cuba is the country where this drink was banned for 60 years. The ban was lifted recently. It was impossible to buy legally Coca-Cola.

Reviews

To learn more about the island, you need to read reviews. When is the best time to relax in Cuba? Travel tips come in handy. According to them, Cuba is beautiful at any time of the year. She beckons and admires her identity. This is not a place where you can go just to lie on the beach. Although the beach holiday is unforgettable, postcard views are guaranteed. However, in this country it is definitely worth wandering around the streets, talking with locals in order to feel the full flavor. In any case, it will be unusual and unforgettable. Cubans amaze with their hospitality and cordiality. In their garden under the window you can find avocados, coconuts and even bananas. For us it is exotic, and for locals it is the most familiar fruit.

Of course, you should not expect anything supernatural from the service: Cubans are not particularly quick and may not live up to the expectations of a spoiled audience. Prices are sky-high in places, if you go on tourist routes. Therefore, it is better to look at the locals - they will show and tell you where to eat cheaper and tastier, and you can buy cigars as a souvenir. By the way, as it turned out, the most delicious cigars are those that the Cubans themselves smoke, and not those that sell to tourists an order of magnitude more expensive.

A little advice: it’s better not to change money at the airport, there the rate is far from the most profitable. And yet, despite all the shortcomings, Cuba is worth visiting at least once in a lifetime.
