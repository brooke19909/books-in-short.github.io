Country hotel complex "Russian Hunt" in Samara: rest for the soul

Each person needs a good rest, and the best place to restore strength is a country pension. Advantages of the hostel: the absence of gassed air, silence, picturesque nature, which tunes in positive emotions and gives spiritual harmony. Holidays outside the metropolis always remain at the peak of popularity, especially for families with children. It is advisable to start the organization and selection of a suitable suburban cottage with familiarization with the reviews of people and the rating of the hotel.

For a quiet and measured pastime on the shore of a small pond, you should choose the Russian Hunt hotel complex, located near the city of Samara. The hotel guarantees its guests impeccable service, quality service and a friendly attitude. And you can stay here for a few days or a couple of weeks. Pricing policy is democratic.

Introductory part





Located near the Kurumoch airport (5 km) and half an hour from the center of Samara, the Russian Hunt hotel. The hotel complex has a spacious territory with a picturesque landscape design. In walking distance there is a pine forest, inhabited by different animals. The environment promotes productive relaxation. Here are ideal conditions for active leisure, celebrations and corporate events. There are many entertainments for a children's audience and people who prefer a passive rest.

Accommodation options

You will be pleasantly surprised by the choice of accommodation. All buildings are stylized in the old Russian spirit, made only from natural raw materials, which is a weighty argument in favor of this hotel. This is one of the few pensions in the city where modernity and traditions of architecture are harmoniously intertwined.

Wooden towers





The hotel complex "Russian Hunt" (Samara) offers comfortable accommodation in spacious apartments that can accommodate about six people. Three two-story houses were built on the territory for people who value comfort and privacy. The first floor is a spacious guest area with upholstered furniture, a TV. On the second floor there are two bedrooms and a bathroom. Terem No. 3 has its own kitchen, cinema, jacuzzi and billiards.

Bungalow

Tourists arriving at the Russian Hunt hotel complex often choose this method of accommodation. The villa is equipped with air conditioning, beds, a compact refrigerator and a TV. Family bungalows have a seating area and a minibar with drinks.

Hotel with rooms





The following room categories are available: economy, standard, comfort and luxury. The smallest room is 18 square meters. m. This is a full room with beds, a landline telephone, a wardrobe, a coffee table, air conditioning and a TV. All rooms have a dedicated bathroom.

Supply system





Since 2014, the Russian Hunt hotel complex has been offering a complimentary buffet breakfast, served in the restaurant. A morning meal can be ordered to your room. The institution specializes in Russian cuisine. The menu is replete with national dishes from game, meat, fish. The restaurant sounds pleasant music, smolders firewood in the fireplace, and an atmosphere of complete relaxation reigns. Guests can also bring carps caught in a local pond, and prepared by qualified chefs.

Corporate service





You can combine relaxation with fruitful work at the Russian Hunt hotel. The hotel complex is equipped with four conference rooms of different sizes. Each room has an individual design and can accommodate from 25 to 400 people. The guests will have at their disposal the necessary equipment and devices (microphones, video and radio microphones, equipment for Internet access). During the break between meetings and negotiations, the staff organizes a mini-buffet at the premises so that you can relax and gain strength.

Infrastructure and leisure activities





All guests arriving by car will be offered a parking space in a guarded parking lot. A ticketing office (air and railway) operates in the territory. There is also a telegraph. For entertainment, the Russian Hunt hotel complex is pleased to offer fishing in a local pond, horseback riding, a boat ride, an ATV, and in winter - sleigh rides and skiing.
