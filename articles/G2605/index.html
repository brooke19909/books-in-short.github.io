Water park in Gagra - the best place for children

Abkhazia is a unique place called the country of the soul. The mountains surround all its resorts, and the cities came very close to the sea. It is the cleanest in Abkhazia. One of its best resorts is Gagra.





Weather in Gagra

The weather conditions of the resort are ideal for a beach holiday. Gagra is warm all year round: the mountain range surrounding the city protects it from cold winds.

Who is suitable for holidays in Gagra

In Gagra, everyone will find things to their liking: youth and children, middle-aged and elderly. Young people have a huge selection of cafes and restaurants, parties and discos where they will not be bored. Elderly people, as well as everyone who has health problems, will be able to relax and heal in the sanatoriums, where they treat with the help of local pulverized mud and mineral water. It offers outdoor enthusiasts - diving, rafting and skydiving. Those who are interested in history and attractions will have a good time on all kinds of excursions. Children will enjoy visiting the water park in Gagra.





Water park

Gagra compares favorably with other local resorts in that there is a water park that opened several years ago. The water park in Gagra is the only one in all of Abkhazia. Since its opening, it has gained fame as an ideal place for family vacations, despite its small size (compared to other modern water parks). Not only children, but also adults spend their free time here with pleasure. There are 7 pools in the park (five of them have fresh water, and two have sea water ). In addition, there is a cascade of 6 dizzying children's slides and a wide variety of attractions. The cascade includes: a steep descent "Kamikaze" (for teenagers) and three twisted slides. One of them with two parallel troughs, and the other, located in a small pool, is short, with a gentle slope, so that even the smallest visitors are not afraid. There are also locker rooms, showers and places to relax. Those wishing to enjoy dishes of Abkhazian and European cuisine can visit restaurants, cafes and bars, which are located in the water park. Every day in the cafe you can watch the performances of artists and enjoy live music. In the evening, dance lovers will enjoy spending time at the incendiary disco, which will last until the morning. The water park in Gagra will give positive emotions, enrich with a charge of health and plunge into the ocean of great mood. You will forever remember the feeling of gentle summer. Here you will have a wonderful time and stock up with pleasant memories for the whole year. In the future, you will again want to visit the water park, Gagra.
