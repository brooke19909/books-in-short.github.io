Base of Primorye: rest in Livadia, Andreevka and Zatoka

Primorye is a unique picturesque corner of our planet. This is an ecologically clean area where tourists can enjoy the healing coniferous aromas and bask in the rays of the hot sun on large sandy beaches. Cozy bases of Primorye are located along the entire perimeter of the sea coast. Rest here is comfortable and inexpensive.

Popular resorts

Primorye is on the list of the cleanest places on the planet. Here you can not only swim in the clear, warm sea water, but also enjoy the healing air. That is why people come here with health problems. On the beautiful sea coast are the bases of Primorye. Rest here is truly fabulous. In Primorye live animals and birds listed in the Red Book, and the vegetation is striking in its diversity and beauty. This area is considered a real health resort, which was created by Mother Nature. The most popular and beautiful resorts are the villages of Andreevka, Livadia, Zatoka, Yeysk, Nakhodka. Recreation facilities provide visitors with quality service and offer a wide variety of excursions.





Yeisk

The city is located on the coast of the Taganrog Bay, which is divided by a scythe with the Yeisk estuary. This is a picturesque oasis immersed in the greenery of the steppe region. Nature created unique conditions for recreation in Yeisk: the climate is mild, the sea is warm and clean, the beaches are sandy, and the air is healing. The swimming season at the resort begins in May and lasts until the end of September. The water of the Sea of ​​Azov here has healing anti-aging properties. Tourists from all regions of Ukraine and neighboring countries come here to enjoy the colors of nature and improve their health. In the city you can rent a cozy comfortable guesthouse, prices are reasonable.

Hotels "Harbor" and "Tornado", health center "Priboy", boarding houses "Flamingo", "Blue Lagoon" are considered the best in the region. "Primorye" is a recreation center (Yeysk), which was chosen by couples with children. The complex is located at the tip of the Yeisk Spit and is washed by the water of the Taganrog Bay. The recreation center includes 3 one-story buildings with comfortable rooms. The complex has a summer cafe, a green area, and nearby are an amusement park, disco clubs, a sports ground.

Sanatorium "Yeisk"

Tourists, without hesitation, book rooms that offer the base of Primorye. Rest here is truly unforgettable. The sanatorium in the city of Yeisk with the same name deserves special attention. It is located on the coast of the Taganrog Bay (Primorye). The recreation center "Yeysk" offers every vacationer to improve their health and relax on the picturesque sea coast. The sanatorium provides vacationers with standard rooms with all amenities. The attractiveness of the complex adds its convenient location - in close proximity to the sea.





Rest in Andreevka

For millennia, picturesque landscapes have been formed in this resort village. Such beauty is difficult to find even in the most exotic corners of our planet. The village is located on the coast of Trinity Bay. This is the most beautiful place in the Primorsky Territory. There are clean sandy beaches, clear sea water and mild climate conditions. Tourists who prefer a relaxing holiday at sea, are happy to come to the village. The recreation center "Andreevka" (Primorye) is the most popular in this region. Residents of Vladivostok and nearby cities make reservations in advance. The village has cafes, shops, restaurants, service stations, discos. You can also book lessons at your local diving school.

The recreation center "Andreevka"

“Andreevsky Fort” offers an inexpensive and comfortable stay. This is the second name of the camp site. She is especially loved by couples and young people. It would seem how this could be, because each category of citizens has their own interests, but the fact remains: the base is ideally suited to both. Tourists are offered both comfortable rooms with all amenities and standard tents. The latter option is usually chosen by teens and bustling youth. The recreation center "Andreevka" (Primorye) is located in close proximity to the sea coastline. The beach here is wide (100 meters), well-groomed. Each tourist is provided with a free sunbed. There is a rescue tower on the beach where professional rescuers watch the floating. Nearby there is a sports field and free car parking. There is a forest near the camp site. In Andreevka, you can perfectly combine beach holidays and evening or morning walks along forest paths. Lovers of outdoor activities are offered various excursion programs. Children and adults can choose from a variety of water rides.





Recreation centers in Zatoka

The resort-type region of Zatoka is located near Odessa (60 km). It is very popular among Ukrainians and tourists from neighboring countries. The resort is located on the southern coast of the Black Sea coast. The mild climate, warm sea and clean sandy beaches lure here more than 150 thousand travelers annually. Pensions, rooms in private sectors, recreation facilities ("Primorye", Zatoka) are available to every tourist. In the town you can not only relax well, but also improve your health. For these purposes, of course, it is better to choose not a private sector or houses on the coast, but recreation facilities. Primorye (Zatoka in particular) offers a wide selection of sanatoriums and pensions - from economical options to expensive ones:

  • The recreation center "Tira" is located on the very shore of the Black Sea, 50 km from Odessa.
  • Chalet "Dream" - offers an inexpensive vacation option. Located 40 km from the city.
  • The recreation center "Solar-positive" is located at the station of the same name.
  • Chalet "Birch" - located on the Black Sea coast in the Sunny area.




Holidays in Livadia

The city is located in a picturesque area of ​​Nakhodkinskiy district. The resort fully lives up to its name. There are white sandy beaches, clean bays and a clear sea. This is a favorite vacation spot of Primorye. The hostels provide visitors with a full range of services in Primorye (Livadia). Recreation centers offer rooms with all amenities. A more complete range of all-inclusive services is provided by the Rainbow and Dream complexes. You can order a comfortable room with all amenities in other pensions:

  • "Livadia."
  • "Reef."

  • "Sunbeam" and others.




The main attractions of Primorye

This region does not disappoint lovers of beach and sea holidays. The mild climate, warm sea and picturesque nature are the main attractions of this corner of the globe. Affordable service is provided by the Primorye base. Rest here for many tourists turns into a real fairy tale. There are many beautiful places in Primorye where you can go on an excursion:

  • Marine reserve with rare representatives of the animal and plant world.
  • Gamow Lighthouse.
  • Romantic island of the languishing heart.
  • Cape Gamow - nature reserve
  • House of the famous scientist I.M. Yankovsky and others.




Tourists leave positive reviews about holidays in Primorye. The recreation center "Primorye", the sanatorium "Yeysk", the fort "Andreevsky" are the most popular tourist complexes. Travelers who once visited the Primorsky Territory become his loyal friends.
