Skating rink at VDNH: photos and reviews

The skating rink at VDNH is the main open ice arena in Russia. The object received this status in 2014. Its infrastructure, size and capabilities exceed the characteristics of all other ice arenas not only in our country, but also in the world. The tradition of skating on the territory of the Exhibition of Achievements of National Economy originated exactly fifty years ago.

Every winter, the open arena attracts thousands of Muscovites and guests of the capital. Four years ago, the skating rink at VDNKh acquired a system of artificial ice. It stretched from the Main Alley to the Friendship of Peoples fountain. Its actual area exceeds 20,000 square meters. Arena infrastructure facilities occupy another 48,000 m¬≤. The arena can accommodate up to 4,500 people at a time.

The work of the skating rink at VDNH is supervised by a team of instructors. Coaches are always ready to help beginners. The main decoration of the arena is a picturesque bridge thrown over an ice mirror. This is a favorite meeting place for couples in love and old acquaintances.

Box office

Entrance tickets sales start in two shifts. The first lasts from 11:00 to 14:10, the second from 17:00 to 22:10. On weekends and holidays, box office pavilions open at 10:00 and 16:45. Monday is a day off.

Maintenance time

There is a break between morning and evening shifts. It starts at 15:00 and ends at 17:00. Visitors to the ice rink are urged to leave the ice about fifteen minutes before the start of maintenance activities. At this time, special equipment enters the alleys.

She is responsible for snow removal. The machines restore and level the surface of the ice. Special distributors remove chips, cracks and cuts. They polish the ice cover.

Fun

Come as a family to not only skate, but also have fun. On Saturdays, from 12:00 to 15:00, the administration of the ice arena organizes joint games. Visitors take part in old Russian amusements.
