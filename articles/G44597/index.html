Trip to Vietnam: Hanoi and its Attractions

Recently, Vietnam has become one of the most popular tourist destinations. Hanoi, the capital of the country, is a kind of starting point. People come here to enjoy the sights and historical monuments and go further around the country in search of exciting adventures.

The capital of the state is located quite far from the ocean, 100 kilometers. Therefore, beach lovers are not attracted to Hanoi. Vietnam, a holiday in which is known for its economy, is famous for its stunning azure shores. Therefore, lovers of excursions - welcome to the capital, but those who want to soak in the warm sand should go to other areas and regions of the country.

The main attractions of Hanoi

The main part of historically significant and attractive cultural monuments for tourists is religious buildings. These include the Pagoda on one pillar. The name speaks for itself: the temple really stands on a pillar carved from stone in the form of a cylinder.

In girth, the pillar is a little more than a meter. The pagoda is very small in size, tourists cannot enter inside. It is built of wood and looks like a beautiful lotus flower, blossoming right in the middle of the pond.

Similar religious buildings are rich in Vietnam. Hanoi is the epicenter of the country's cultural heritage. No less interesting is the Tai Pagoda. It is located on a peninsula in West Lake.

Like the Pagoda on one pillar, it was erected in the 11th century and was repeatedly rebuilt. For tourists, there are performances of the puppet theater, which look amazing, reflecting on the lake surface.

Among the non-religious attractions should be called the Museum of the Vietnamese Army, the Museum of History, the Serpentine Village (Le Mat). This village is located just 10 kilometers from the city. Most of the locals are serpentine. They will not only feed you snake meat delicacies, but also offer folk remedies from reptiles.

Hanoi Infrastructure

Good restaurants, an abundance of "eateries" in which you can quickly have a bite to eat, a large amount of entertainment - this is all Vietnam. Hanoi welcomes tourists with bars and clubs that work all night. Such establishments can be found anywhere in the city. For outdoor activities - bicycles and scooters for rent. These vehicles are the most popular transport in the city.



It should be said that it is not for nothing that most tourists prefer Vietnam. Hanoi, whose hotels deserve extremely positive reviews, is fully integrated for a comfortable tourist stay.

There are 16 hotels in the city. Even the three-star hotels in Hanoi have their own bar, fitness center and other services. The most popular hotels are Hanoi, Golden Key, Nikko Hanoi, Sunway.

Weather in Hanoi

Like all of Vietnam, Hanoi is located in the subequatorial climate zone. The end of spring, summer and the beginning of autumn is the time of rains in the capital. In the fall typhoons are not uncommon. All summer, the air temperature rarely drops below 28 degrees, and rises even to 40. In winter, it is cooler in Hanoi. The average temperature is 19-22 degrees.
