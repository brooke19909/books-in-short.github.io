We make crafts from boxes

From unnecessary packaging cardboard boxes, you can easily make various crafts. These are cars, planes, dolls, various logic games, cards, books. Boxes from under shoes, household appliances, products and others will be used. The result is low-cost entertainment for children.



The process of co-creation itself, when the crafts are made by children together with adults, provides an opportunity for additional communication, training and the acquisition of useful skills. In children, the manufacture of crafts develops patience, perseverance, attention, imagination, fine and large hand motor skills.

To make crafts from boxes with your own hands, you will need the boxes themselves, scissors, glue, paints, a stationery knife and other additional tools and elements, depending on the design of the product. Everyone has everything they need at home.
