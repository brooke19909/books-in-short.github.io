Best guesthouse, Kabardinka. "Victoria": living conditions, prices, reviews of vacationers

Resorts on the Russian Black Sea coast have long been in no way inferior to foreign ones. Their big plus is that here you can relax and heal. Such opportunities are open to people planning a vacation in the popular village called Kabardinka, which lies in a quiet, cozy bay of the Black Sea. There are several excellent health resorts, for example, the boarding house of the same name with the village “Kabardinka”, “Victoria”, “Primorsky” and others. Pension Victoria is considered one of the best.



Location, how to get

The resort city of Gelendzhik, located about 25 km south of Novorossiysk, is unusually popular with tourists. 13 km from Gelendzhik towards the same Novorossiysk is the village of Kabardinka. It is located in the famous Tsemess Bay, in a lagoon surrounded by the spurs of the Markot Range. Here the Victoria boarding house was built.

Kabardinka, Gelendzhik are famous for their unusual air, which is called a natural doctor. And juniper groves make it healing. The mountain range protects the village from cold air masses, the sea refreshes in the summer and warms in the winter. Already at the entrance to the village can offer a good pension Kabardinka. "Victoria" (as it is called) has such an arrangement that it is convenient to get here by private car or by public transport. In Gelendzhik there is an airport that accepts flights from many Russian cities. Buses and minibuses No. 30, 31, 103 go to Kabardinka from him. Of course, they are always ready to offer their taxi services. From Novorossiysk, where passenger trains come, it is very convenient to get to the boarding house by ordering a paid transfer. Travel to the boarding house costs about 500 rubles. You can also get there by public transport, but you will have to do a lot of transfers. First, from the railway station on trolleybus No. 6, get to the bus station, take the bus to Kabardinka and take a taxi to the boarding house in the village.



Infrastructure

One of the most beautiful in the village offers its guests a boarding house Kabardinka. "Victoria" - and it is he - occupies a not very large, but well-groomed territory, surrounded by greenery and flowers. There is even a pond and an orchard. On the territory you can find a small grocery store, a cafe (only open from May to September), a wonderful children's town, sports grounds, a payphone, laundry and ironing. The reception of the boarding house, located in the spacious hall of the main building, serves tourists around the clock. Here you can book a tour, call a taxi, book plane or train tickets. Soft sofas and armchairs are installed in the hall, the walls are decorated with paintings.

Guests are given the opportunity to conduct business events, for which there is a well-equipped assembly hall. The guesthouse area is fenced and guarded round the clock, as well as free parking.



Accommodation

Perhaps this is the most profitable in terms of pricing, allowing people with different financial capabilities to relax, a boarding house. Kabardinka (Victoria in particular) can offer guests several accommodation options. On the territory of the institution under consideration, the main building is located on 3 floors, functioning in winter and summer. Room categories:

1. "Standard". One-room, designed for 2 people plus 1 extra bed. Equipment - necessary furniture, refrigerator, modern TV, air conditioning. The hygiene room has a toilet, shower, washbasin. Balconies are in every room.

2. "Standard block". They differ from the usual "standards" in that the hygiene room in them provides one for 2 adjacent rooms.

3. "Studio". One room, very spacious, oriented to accommodate 2 people plus 1 extra bed. Equipment: necessary furniture, sofa, armchair, coffee table, TV, refrigerator, dishes for coffee and tea, electric kettle, air conditioning, kitchen area (without electric stove). The hygiene room has a toilet, washbasin, hairdryer and shower.



4. "Suite". Two-room rooms, designed for 2, maximum 3 people. Layout: a salon with upholstered furniture (a sofa can be expanded), a balcony, a TV, air conditioning, a kitchen area with a refrigerator, an electric kettle and tea and coffee utensils. The second room is a bedroom with beds, bedside tables and a wardrobe.

5. “Apartments”. Two-room rooms are designed for 2 people. They differ from the "suites" in size and design.

In the summer, a good option of accommodation in cottages offers its guests boarding house "Victoria" (Kabardinka). Reviews about the rooms in them are only positive. Cottages are located surrounded by juniper and other evergreen trees. The rooms in them are spacious, beautifully furnished, one-room. The category is “family superior”. Each has a TV, air conditioning, refrigerator, soft folding chairs, coffee table, double beds, wardrobe. The bathroom comes with a toilet, shower, washbasin and hairdryer.

The views from the rooms of any category open to the sea and mountains. Daily cleaning, linen change - 1 time per 5 days of stay, towels are changed every day.



Nutrition

The boarding house “Victoria” (Kabardinka) offers its guests excellent food. Reviews about the work of the kitchen, dining room and cafe are simply enthusiastic. Guests assure that the food here is plentiful and very tasty. Type of food - "self-service", dirty dishes should be assigned for distribution on their own. The guests of the boarding house eat in 2 shifts. The menu always has dairy and sour-milk dishes, soups of several kinds, meat in various variations, several types of side dish, all kinds of salads, sweets, pies, rolls, fruits. From drinks, you can take a compote of fresh fruits, coffee, tea, water (from coolers), milk. The best and most popular wines of the Kuban are also offered, and tasting evenings are arranged. Many drinks can be purchased at a boarding house store.





Leisure

For those who rest from May to October, an important issue is spending time at sea. The beach of the boarding house “Victoria” (Kabardinka) is located literally 70 meters away, but to get to it you need to cross the Sukhumi highway. This inconvenience somewhat brightens up the “zebra” of the pedestrian crossing and an adjustable (not automatic) traffic light. That is, you can, by clicking on the button, change its light from red to green. The beach at the boarding house itself is very spacious and clean, small pebble. Sunset at sea is convenient for children and the elderly. Despite the fact that the Tsemess Bay is the deepest in this water area, the depth of the sea on the beach does not begin at the very shore, and the bathing area for children is fenced. Sun beds, awnings, towels, showers, a toilet for guests of Victoria are free. Non-guests can relax on the beach, but they always get the worst places. As for entertainment, you can rent a catamaran, a pedalo, a jet ski, and snorkeling equipment.

Reviews of tourists say that it is very interesting to visit the village of Kabardinka. Pension "Victoria" is located within walking distance. Here, vacationers can see the Old Park - a unique creation of the local enthusiast Alekseev, who created many world landmarks in miniature in the park.

Those who wish can use the gym, sauna, order a massage, play billiards, table tennis, backgammon, chess, sign up for unique excursions to interesting places in the region.



Treatment

Many tourists seek to combine treatment and relaxation in Kabardinka. Pension "Victoria" for these purposes has a medical office, in which the therapist takes for free. In the institution, you can improve your health with problems with the respiratory system, musculoskeletal system, and nervous system. The following procedures are prescribed here (for a fee):

  • several types of inhalation;
  • halochamber;
  • manual massage and massage chair;
  • physiotherapy;
  • Cedar barrel.

The boarding house offers coupons for visiting these procedures with discounts.

Additional Information

The year-round guest house "Victoria" (Kabardinka) is open all year round. Prices for vouchers are different, depending on the category of the selected room and time of year. The lowest they are in standard-block rooms from November 1 to April 30 - 460 rubles per person for accommodation and meals. In high season, these numbers cost 1700 rubles per person. The highest prices are set for apartments. From November 1 to April 30, accommodation in them (with meals) will cost 1730 rubles per person, and in the high season - 2915 rubles. You can find out more when booking.

In the boarding house "Victoria" there are promotions and a discount program. For shares when booking a room before March 1, the cost of the ticket is reduced by 7%. The same discounts apply when visiting a boarding house a second time. And from the third time of rest here the cost is reduced by 10%. Children under 5 years old with meals are staying at the boarding house for free.
