The specifics of the resort Zlatni Pyasitsi

The Golden Sands resort, where the Madara Park Hotel 4 * is located, appeared on the map of Bulgaria relatively recently - in the middle of the last century. However, from that time on, he managed to turn into the most popular resort in the country, stably crowded with tourists every summer. And why? A long and wide strip of golden sand stretches along the western coast of the Black Sea. She gave the name to the resort, conveniently located between the cities of Varna and Kavarna. The distance to the airport is small - about thirty kilometers. The beaches in Zlatni Pyasitsi (the place is called in Bulgarian) are awarded the Blue Flag every year for cleanliness and comfort. The resort has all the necessary infrastructure for a standard vacation: discos, restaurants, cafes, shops, sports facilities, amusement parks and other entertainment. But that is not all. In Golden Sands, you can not only relax, but also heal on thermal sulfur springs. Despite the fact that the resort is new, there are also historical sights here. Tourists mention excursions to the rock monastery Aladzha, caves and cute, typically Bulgarian villages where you can buy handicrafts. Immediately beyond the beaches, a natural, state-protected park begins, consisting of hilly terraces covered with forest. The tourist season is classic for Bulgaria: the summer months and September. What is the specificity of Golden Sands? Due to the shallow sea, they are considered a children's resort. Balneotherapy hospitals attract those wishing to overcome joint diseases. A variety of entertainments make Golden Sands the most party place in Bulgaria for young people.

Nutrition

Madara Park Hotel 4 * (Bulgaria) accepts "package" tourists under the "All Inclusive" program. Breakfast is served in the main restaurant from half past seven to ten in the morning. So, late birdies, hurry. What tourists say about food

Almost every review of a stay at Madara Park Hotel mentions how delicious, varied, and nutritious food is at this hotel. Bulgarian cuisine is excellent in itself, and many Russians, having tasted the dishes, became its followers. The main restaurant often hosts themed dinners. But if you are a strict adherent of traditional healthy breakfasts, you can always find cereals, yoghurts, cereals, sausages, scrambled eggs, fried eggs and the like on the tables - at home. Reviews highly recommend going to a la carte restaurant. It offers sophisticated cuisine, impeccable service and a romantic atmosphere overlooking the illuminated pool. The reviews also note that if you arrived late when dinner is already over, you will still be fed. And this attention of the staff to the guests is very nice. The kids liked the ice cream - there were always five or six kinds to choose from.

Beach and pools at Madara Park Hotel 4 *

Golden Sands is famous throughout Bulgaria for its ideal coast. A wide strip of the beach stretched for many tens of kilometers. Only on the first strip of the sea in the resort there are about seventy hotels. Madara Park Hotel is not one of them. It is located on the fourth street, which runs parallel to the shore. Nevertheless, tourists do not at all denounce the long and exhausting road to the beach. After all, the relief in Golden Sands is flat, and you will not need to go down the stairs and climb the hill back to swim in the sea. The beach is six hundred meters, if you go through the main entrance of Madara Park Hotel 4 * and further along the street. But you can go through the Mimosa Hotel and fall on the golden fine-grained sand of the beach in six minutes. Sunbeds and umbrellas are there in huge numbers, but, unfortunately, for a fee. The hotel promises a transfer to the beach, but none of the tourists who wrote reviews used it - the distance was too insignificant. The hotel itself has an outdoor swimming pool with a section for children. The terrace around it is lined with sunbeds with soft mattresses and umbrellas. Using them for hotel residents is free. But the hotel also has an indoor pool with heated water up to twenty-seven degrees. Access to it by hotel guests is free from nine in the morning until six in the evening.



Service and Facilities

Madara Park Hotel 4 * (Bulgaria) takes part in seminars and other business meetings. For this, the hotel has an equipped conference room with eighty seats. The hotel takes care of the organization of weddings and other celebrations, because it has a spacious banquet hall. If you want to come to Bulgaria with your dog, the hotel will by appointment accept your four-legged friend. But the parking of the iron horse in the covered parking lot of the hotel will have to pay. A mini market and gift shop, currency exchange, hairdresser and laundry facilities are on site.

Excursions from the Madara Park Hotel

As already mentioned above, Golden Sands is a fairly new resort. But it is located on the ancient land of Bulgaria, where it is full of diverse historical, cultural and natural attractions. The Madara Park Hotel reviews are called a convenient starting point for small excursions in the region. First of all, tourists highly recommend visiting at least one of the ancient coastal cities of Bulgaria - Sozopol or Nessebar. There you can see the beautiful architecture and swim. A tour of Varna on a bunk bus will leave the most vivid memories. Also unforgettable will be a trip in a boat to zero kilometer along the Ropotamo River, the banks of which are covered with real jungle. Guidebooks of Veliko Tarnovo are called the true spiritual capital of Bulgaria . Although the ancient capital of the country does not lie on the coast, and it is two hundred and fifty kilometers from Golden Sands, it is the most visited city by tourists. Cape Kaliakra is a tourist attraction that attracts both those who are interested in history and nature lovers. A narrow ridge of a cliff protrudes into the sea for two whole kilometers. The sea, splashing far below, seagulls overhead, the wind in the face ... And at the end of the cape are the remains of an ancient fortress, the capital of the medieval princedom, the chapel of St. Nicholas and a viewing platform from which you can take wonderful photos. Cape Kaliakra is located only fifty kilometers from Golden Sands, but public transport does not go there. You can only get your own or as part of an excursion group. And very close to the resort - fifteen kilometers - is the Aladzha monastery dug in the rocks.
