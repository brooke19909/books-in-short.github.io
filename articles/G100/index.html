Cartago Nova 3 (hotel, Spain): traveler reviews

Summer is a time of rest and travel. And Spain today is one of the most popular tourist countries in Europe. Sandy beaches, warm sea, cozy hotels, fun parties and lots of interesting sights - all this attracts travelers from all over the world. Hotel Cartago Nova 3 is one of the popular holiday destinations in Malgrat de Mar. And many tourists are interested in information about the location and service at the hotel.

Where is the hotel located?





Hotel Cartago Nova 3 is located in the famous Spanish resort town of Malgrat de Mar. Moreover, the hotel is located very close to the sea, on the coast called Costa del Maresme, in the province of Barcelona. This resort area is very popular among tourists, and the hotel itself is famous for its convenient location.

For example, not far from the building there is not only the seashore, but also the main transport interchanges. Nearby there is a public transport stop and a train station. And the distance to the city center and its attractions is only 1 kilometer. Barcelona is 60 kilometers from the hotel, but a sightseeing bus stops here, which constantly carries tourists to see the sights of the famous city. Girona Airport is 35 km away, while Barcelona International Airport is 80 km away.

General description of the hotel





Hotel Cartago Nova 3 (Spain) is a large nine-story building located near the road and railways. The hotel has a small enclosed area where guests can relax and have fun. For example, there is a rather large swimming pool with water slides that delight both adult tourists and children.

By the way, the hotel was built in 1972. But do not worry about the age of the building, since only a few years ago (in 2009) a complete reconstruction was carried out here, and today the hotel's atmosphere fully meets modern requirements and standards.

Description of rooms





On the territory of the Cartago Nova 3 hotel (Malgrat de Mar) there are 219 standard rooms of different capacities. By the way, five rooms are specially equipped for comfortable living of people with physical disabilities.

Each room consists of a small corridor, a spacious bedroom and a bathroom. There is also a private small balcony with sea or patio views with a pool.

The rooms are furnished with all necessary furniture, including a wardrobe and a large comfortable bed. From household appliances in the room there is a telephone and a TV on which you can watch cable channels. There is also a safe for storing valuables and documents, but its use must be paid separately. It offers vacationers - wireless Internet access, for which you also need to pay extra.

The bathroom is also equipped with everything you need. There is a comfortable shower, washbasin, large wall mirror. Guests at check-in are offered a set of hygiene products, as well as a set of towels, which are changed daily.

Numerous reviews from tourists confirm that the rooms are always clean, as maids clean the rooms daily. Bed linen is changed twice a week, according to the hotel schedule.

What does the food scheme look like in a hotel?





Hotel Cartago Nova 3 offers its guests three meals a day. There is a large restaurant in the hotel building, where a buffet is served three times a day for residents. The hotel offers mainly classic international dishes, as well as fresh fruits, vegetables and delicious pastries for dessert. In addition, themed dinners are held at least once a week during which guests are invited to try the food of a particular country. By the way, seafood is often cooked on such evenings.

In the menu of the cafe and the hotel bar you can find a lot of drinks, interesting cocktails and delicious wines that only Spain can offer. Cartago Nova 3 Hotel offers its guests to pamper themselves. But you should know that drinks are paid here.

Does Cartago Nova 3 have a private beach?





It's no secret that traveling on the coast of Spain primarily means a beach holiday. So does Cartago Nova 3 have its own beach? Costa del Maresme is a fairly long coastal zone. And right next to the hotel is the beach of Platja de LAstillero. This place is not the property of the hotel, so not only residents, but also tourists from other places have a rest here. Nevertheless, there are always enough sun loungers, mattresses and umbrellas here, however, you have to pay extra for them separately. Like on any other beach, here for a small fee you will have the opportunity to ride catamarans, water skiing or do some other water sports.

Are there any conditions for children?

Of course, parents who are going on a trip with their children are primarily interested in the question of whether there are conditions on the hotel for the whole family. And the Cartago Nova 3 offers some amenities. For example, a cot will be delivered to the room for a small child. And the restaurant's menu has dishes that will appeal to children.

In addition, the pool has a safe area for bathing children - toddlers and older children can have a great time. There is also a mini club, which is designed for children aged 4 to 12 years. Here, experienced teachers and animators will be engaged with your child, who will make the child's vacation unforgettable. In the evenings, children are dancing, where you can also have fun.

Hotel infrastructure and services offered





Despite its relatively small size, the Cartago Nova 3 hotel can offer its residents some necessary services. For example, there is a small business center where you can make an international or long-distance call, send a fax, and use the Internet access.

There is also a spacious luggage storage room, which is simply necessary for tourists, for whom this hotel is just one of the stops on a long trip. The hotel has a car rental point where employees will help you choose the right option - your own transport greatly facilitates movement around the neighborhood.

Entertainment and sports on site





Of course, the beach hotel offers its residents a lot of opportunities to have a good time. As already mentioned, the hotel has its own rather large pool, which is perfect for people who do not want to go to a crowded beach. There are also small water slides. There are sun loungers and parasols around the pool, which guests can use for free.

By the way, fun competitions in water polo are regularly held in the pool. In addition, hotel residents have the opportunity to do water aerobics together with experienced and talented trainers.

The hotel also has a billiards room, where fans of this game can have fun. There is also a TV lounge. The hotel has a group of talented animators who provide performances both in the daytime and in the evening. In the evenings, live music is played, and at night the Tropicana disco opens its doors, where everyone can have fun, dance and make new friends.

Do not forget that the hotel is located very close to the city center. In just a few minutes you can reach bustling markets, boutiques and some interesting historical sights. And just a few steps from the hotel building is a huge shopping center where you can buy almost everything, from food and basic necessities to clothes, furniture and appliances.

The hotel also has a tour desk. The staff is always ready to help guests choose the right tour. Here you will be given special brochures, talk about interesting places in the vicinity of the city and even help organize a short trip to Barcelona.

For which category of vacationers is this hotel suitable?

Cartago Nova 3 Hotel (Spain) is recommended primarily for youth companies who want to have fun while having fun at concerts and parties. In addition, the hotel is perfect for families with children, as even the smallest travelers will have something to do here. Travel agents also recommend this place to people with disabilities. And, of course, the hotel is perfect for a quality budget vacation.
