How to weave Shambhala bracelets: materials

For weaving you will need a number of materials.

  • Beads . They will need from ten to fifteen pieces - depending on the length of the future bracelet. It is best to take 2-3 different species and alternate them with each other, forming a pattern. It must be remembered that the holes in them must correspond to the thickness of the cord.
  • Cord . It can be either a waxed thread or a regular lace. If you plan to weave a bracelet using two cords of different colors, you need to know that about 60 cm of thread will go to the base, and from one and a half to two meters to the decorative part.
  • Additional materials . For weaving, you may need glue, adhesive tape, scissors.

How to weave Shambhala bracelet: scheme

    1. Beads are strung on the base in the selected sequence. At the ends of the base knots are tied.
    2. The base is fixed to the table so that the cord hangs freely.
    3. The remaining piece of the cord folds in half so that its ends are the same. Then he is tied around the base over the first bead.
    4. At the end and beginning of the product, two knots are tied. One node is made between the beads. Weave should be tried so that there is practically no space between the beads.
    5. After braiding the main part and checking the length, proceed to create a fastener.
    6. To do this, put together both ends of the base and braid them with ten to fifteen nodes. In this case, the base should slide freely inside.
    7. One more bead is put on the free ends of the base, which is strengthened by glue.
