Yarn "goat down": products, reviews

Yarn from goat down is suitable for knitting almost any product except summer. She is very warm, soft and tender, the process of working with her is a pleasure. Products are soft to the touch, light and very warm. When knitting, the thread does not slip and lies very evenly. It is very convenient to work with this type of yarn . Even a novice craftswoman will cope with the process.

Recently, fashionable catwalks appeared leggings associated with goat down. They immediately gained popularity with fashionistas around the world. Previously, such products were used only in ordinary life by residents of the northern regions. Now this model has become a fashion brand and is worn around the world.





What is goat fluff for? The fluff of this animal has unique properties, light and delicate, as well as very practical. In order to get fluff, the goat is not sheared, but combed out with a special brush. Thus, thin and delicate hairs are obtained, which are much thinner and weightless compared to wool. Fluff allows you to bind light products that will not prick. This technology appeared in mass production when yarn manufacturers compared fluff and wool and came to the conclusion that fluff has many advantages.

Yarn specifications

The yarn is made from natural goat down. Spinning is carried out on industrial equipment so that the thread is the same throughout its thickness and even. Sometimes in the markets you can find skeins of yarn that have been manually twisted. This is now very rare, because the range and availability allows you to buy it without any problems.





Benefits of Goat Down Yarn

Goat yarn has advantages over ordinary wool:

  • dry heat, which provides the human body, has a therapeutic effect and is able to relieve pain in the spine, joints and muscles;
  • ecological cleanliness. Goat fluff is made without the addition of acrylic or polyester threads and is completely natural. Some manufacturers to add products from this yarn stability and elasticity add a thin cotton thread in the spinning process;
  • goat products keep the pattern well. Due to the long fibers, it persists for a long time. At first, a slight loss of hairs is observed, but after several uses it stops;
  • durability. Products can be used for a long time, since such a yarn is durable. It is better to wash a thing with your hands in warm water, and dry it on a horizontal surface;
  • yarn "goat fluff" does not prick. Unlike sheep wool, it is much more tender and more pleasant to the body. Therefore, from it you can knit children's things: hats, mittens, socks, blouses and others. They will not cause discontent of the baby with sticky touches;
  • does not absorb moisture. The yarn is able to warm without retaining moisture, so it is almost impossible to freeze in such a product.

Unusual uses of goat fluff products

Goat fluff began to be used in life a very long time ago and people have learned to use it for treatment and maintenance of health. Here are some popular recipes for using products from this material:

  • for premature babies whose thermoregulation system has not yet formed, knit a blanket and socks;
  • for pain in the lumbar spine, a goat down belt is used:
  • with headaches, the head is covered with a down scarf or scarf;
  • with diseases of the throat, a down scarf or scarf is tied to the neck;
  • when heel spurs occur, insoles are made from this material, which are placed in daily shoes;
  • with varicose veins, a leg wrap with a downy product is used or simply covered with a scarf.

Orenburg shawl

This is the most famous product made from goat fluff yarn. It is just amazing! Goat down scarves can be passed through the ringlet, they are so thin. They are also called the "cobweb" for the subtlety of the patterns and the transparent structure.

Knitting patterns Orenburg scarves can be found a lot. Knitters of Orenburg have their own secrets of craftsmanship, which they prefer not to share. In order to knit an openwork scarf or stole, you can take any pattern with crochets and do the work on large knitting needles. For a denser and warmer product, patterns with braids and aranas are suitable.

Handkerchiefs are knitted with goat down knitting with the addition of cotton threads to give the product structure and stability. They are unique and keep the warmth of the hands of their creator. A set of loops is made in an arbitrary amount, depending on the width and shape of the future product, as well as based on the location of the pattern.

Hats

Hats made from goat fluff yarn are very warm, not prickly and almost weightless on the head. Such a hat can be made for both an adult and a child. Before starting work, it is necessary to produce an increasing number of loops based on the circumference of the head. Knitting can be done on one knitting needle followed by stitching the hat or on circular knitting needles without a seam. First, an elastic band is executed, the width of which is determined arbitrarily. Next, knitting is carried out by the front surface with the addition of a pattern or without it. If you add a pattern of braids or arana, the cap will turn out to be more voluminous. For frost, you can knit double. If desired, you can decorate the finished product with a pompom, beads or brooch.

Socks

Knitting socks from goat down is made according to the classical pattern. But since this material is very gentle and prone to wiping with heavy use, it is worth adding a special thread. This will give socks extra strength, but retain the healing properties of down yarn. Socks from this thread have unique properties: they do not allow the feet to get wet and freeze, prevent the appearance of corns and heel spurs, and also relieve fatigue after a busy day. You can wear such socks at home and walk in them until relief comes in the body with fatigue, heaviness and pain in the legs.

Kids' things

Yarn from goat down is very widely used in the manufacture of children's clothing. It does not prick, it warms well, is light and comfortable. Due to the fact that knitting is done on large knitting needles, the products stretch perfectly, they will not hamper the baby's movements. Knitting baby clothes is very simple and quick. Experienced craftswomen recommend that young mothers be sure to make a goat's blouse for their baby and a small plaid, which is convenient to use on walks.

Yarn Reviews

Such warm and delicate yarn as goat fluff receives the most enthusiastic reviews. Those who at least once tried to knit something from it, enthusiastically respond both to the process itself and to the result.





There are reviews that the ordered goat down yarn turned out to be sticky and tough. Unfortunately, no one is safe from a fake. When buying, it is worth considering that it can not be cheap. It is better to purchase it from manufacturers from factories.

Very many craftswomen note in reviews of some difficulties that arose during the first experience of knitting. They say that the thread is difficult to catch and fix in one place. Experienced craftsmen recommend getting used to the peculiarities of this material a little and knitting any simple product with the front surface for a start. After the hands get used to constantly holding the thread in the right tension, the pattern will turn out.
