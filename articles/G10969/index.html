How to get a loan on "Tele2": teams and numbers for activation

What is an emergency recharge for Tele2 subscribers?

Category of subscribers who can replenish Tele2 balance on credit

Tele2 trusts the Promised Payment to customers who connected to their mobile provider 30-180 days ago (varies by geographic criteria). For other users, a loan from Tele2 will be available only with time.
