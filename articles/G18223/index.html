Anapa is a world of sun and sea, which merges with the sky. If you compare it with other Black Sea resorts, the cost of a trip here is much lower than, for example, in Sochi. However, before purchasing a ticket, it is very important to find a hotel suitable for your stay, which will suit you with the prices and quality of the services provided. So, meet: Anapa, Hotel "Dacha Del Sol". The reviews of tourists will help you make the right choice, we will give them along the way.

In fact, it is very difficult for a tourist to get lost in this city. Number of rooms

Living conditions - this is the first thing that excites tourists who go to the resort, Anapa is no exception. Hotel "Dacha Del Sol" (photos are presented in the article) is able to satisfy even the most demanding tastes. Among the abundance of rooms, you can choose the one that you most like.

Your attention is invited to 11 rooms of standard DBL. They are designed for two tourists. The room has a shower and toilet, kettle, double bed and minibar. Each room has its own private terrace, where it is so good to spend evenings.

If you are not comfortable with a shared bed, choose one of two TWIN rooms. Room equipment repeats the standard DBL, except that there are two twin beds.

If you are going to rest with the children, then you will need additional space for them. There are Triple rooms for this. The first type of these rooms is equipped with an armchair-bed, and the second - a sofa. A total of 18 such numbers are presented.

If your family has two children, then a double Junior Suite is suitable for you, in which you can equip two additional beds.

Finally, there is a quadruple Family House. There are only 4 beds, a double bed, two twin beds and two extra beds (sofas).

All rooms have hot and cold water all year round. As you see, it is not necessary to go abroad to be provided with comfortable conditions for relaxation, Anapa is much closer. Hotel "Dacha Del Sol" (photos of the rooms are striking in their sophistication) is an example of an ideal holiday where everything is truly beautiful.
