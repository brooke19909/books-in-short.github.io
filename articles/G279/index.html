Sunshine Resort Intime Sanya 5 * (China / Hainan Island): photos and reviews of tourists

Quite often, when searching for a place to spend a vacation, travelers prefer China. This is an exotic and mysterious country that attracts tourists due to its unusual history, deep traditions, beautiful nature and high quality service.

If we talk about resort holidays, then one of the most popular places for its holding is the city of Sanya, located on the island of Hainan. Of course, here tourists have a lot to choose from, as there are a lot of different hotels on the shore. One of the popular stopping places is the hotel Sunshine Resort Intime Sanya 5 *.

During planning, every traveler tries to learn more about the hotel. For example, it is useful to find out what conditions should be expected and what kind of food is offered to tourists. Is the beach close to it? Are there any shops and restaurants nearby? Do they offer amenities for a child? These are important points to pay attention to.

Location Features





As you know, the location plays an important role in choosing a hotel. So where to look for the hotel Resort Intime Sunshine Sanya 5 *? Dadonghai, a picturesque bay in the city of Sanya - it was here, just a few meters from the sea shore, and a resort complex was built.

By the way, this is a rather lively tourist area - within walking distance of shops, boutiques, shopping centers, restaurants, nightclubs, massage parlors and public transport. Sanya city center is only two kilometers away, and many tourists prefer to take long walks to the central area. Phoenix International Airport is a 20-minute drive away (both hotel and travel agencies provide transfers).

What does the hotel and its territory look like?

The hotel complex Sunshine Resort Intime Sanya 5 * occupies a large area and boasts original architecture. In the center are three residential buildings - multi-story buildings built in the form of ships. They are literally buried in the greenery of a tropical garden. Here guests can relax in the shade of palm trees, enjoying the coolness, sea views and mountain landscapes.

Of course, the infrastructure here is well thought out - there are areas for recreation and sports, walkways, pools, in short, what you need for a pleasant pastime.

In the hotel room, the tourist will always find the necessary things for a comfortable stay. Of course, you can count on the necessary furniture, including king-size beds and desks. A soft bathrobe and comfortable slippers will be waiting for you in your room.

You can also count on the availability of the necessary equipment. Of course, there is a powerful air conditioning with a convenient remote control, a large-screen TV (you can watch some satellite channels), a telephone, a radio and a small safe for storing the most important things. Tourists can count on the availability of a hairdryer, as well as hygiene items (brushes, toothpaste, shampoo, soap), which are replenished every day during cleaning and changing towels.

What kind of meal plan do guests offer?





Naturally, a quality vacation is hard to imagine without food. After all, arriving in exotic countries, especially when it comes to China with its interesting cuisine, I want to treat yourself to delicious dishes. So what can a guest of the Resort Intime Sunshine Sanya 5 * hotel expect?

Paying for accommodation, you also get the right to daily breakfast. They are held in the form of a buffet in the premises of the main restaurant. Reviews say that the menu here is quite diverse - toasts, fresh pastries, snacks, salads, side dishes, scrambled eggs, in a word, everything that is customary to eat for breakfast. In addition, guests can rely on chilled drinks, coffee and aromatic tea.

Where is the beach located?





Going to the island, travelers naturally rely on a beach holiday. What can the hotel complex Resort Intime Sunshine Sanya 5 * offer? Dadonghai is famous for its luxurious beaches. The hotel territory goes directly to the shore - the distance to the beach line is no more than 100 meters.

Reviews indicate that the beach here is really beautiful, covered with soft sand. They clean several times a day, therefore it is clean on the shore. The entrance to the sea is convenient, cleared of stones and algae. It is always a pleasure to spend time here - tourists just love to relax on the beach.

Guests can count on the presence of comfortable sun loungers and wide-brimmed umbrellas. Nearby is a bar with several tables where you can enjoy a drink and even have a snack, enjoying the sound of the waves and the sea landscape. Large beach towels are available on site.

Conditions for a beach holiday: entertainment on the beach

Sunbathing and swimming in warm water is always a pleasure. But many travelers prefer a more active holiday. Of course, guests of the Sunshine Resort Intime Sanya 5 * hotel can count on all possible entertainment.

A water sports center is equipped on the shore, where for a reasonable price you can rent, for example, pedalo, boat, canoe. Travelers enjoy kayaking, ride a boat and a banana, get a lot of vivid emotions on water skiing and parasailing. Tourists say the local coast is suitable for windsurfing and sailing, especially when it comes to the first lessons. By the way, here you can even take a few lessons from the instructor.

There are several well-equipped sports fields on the beach where you can play volleyball or beach soccer. Every traveler will surely find something to their liking.

Additional service for guests





The hotel has everything in order to save guests from small household problems. Here, guests can change currency, use a luggage storage room, take their clothes to the laundry or dry cleaning service, leave the car (or other type of transport) in a guarded parking lot.

In case of health problems, tourists can count on the help of a doctor, and the necessary medicines are sold in a hotel pharmacy. There is a newsagent and a gift shop where interesting crafts can be purchased at a reasonable price.

Throughout the territory there is free Internet access, which is important for almost every traveler. You can use the services of a professional photographer who will happily capture the most interesting moments of your vacation.

Are there any conditions for business people?

It's no secret that travel in China is often combined with business. Are there any relevant conditions on the territory of the hotel 5 * Sunshine Resort Intime Sanya. Dadonghai is a great place not only for relaxation, but also for work.

The hotel has a business center specially equipped for business travelers, where you can use computers, printers, faxes, scanners and other office equipment. For a small fee, you can hire a secretary and translator.

In addition, there is a spacious conference room, where business, scientific and holiday events are often held. There is also a negotiation room, as well as equipment for seminars and conferences.

How to brighten up leisure time? Entertainment at the hotel

Most of the hotel territory is reserved for swimming pools that resemble lakes with beautiful grottoes and are interconnected by artificial "streams". Umbrellas and sun loungers are located in large numbers on the terrace.

There is also a fitness club with a large gym for guests. You can relax in the sauna, and then go for a massage, as professional massage therapists work on site. For a small fee, you can spend time playing billiards. And in the evenings, entertainers entertain guests by arranging interesting shows. By the way, here you can relax and enjoy live music.

Family holidays with a child: amenities and entertainment





Naturally, many tourists prefer to travel with their child. What can the hotel offer Sunshine Resort Intime Sanya 5 * to parents? Dadonghai is a beautiful bay with a picturesque clean beach where children can play, sunbathe and have fun.
