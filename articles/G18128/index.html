The list of VTB 24 ATMs in St. Petersburg by region

PJSC VTB 24 is one of the largest commercial banks in Russia. Branches and ATMs of a financial organization are widely represented in the Northern capital. The addresses and operating hours of VTB 24 ATMs can be studied below.

VTB 24 ATMs in the Central District

ATM number 388185 is located at 140 Nevsky Prospekt, in the additional office of VTB Bank. 455 meters from Ploshchad Vosstaniya metro station, 778 meters from Mayakovskaya metro station. The ATM operates around the clock. It accepts both rubles and foreign currency; cash withdrawals are made in rubles.

VTB 24 ATM in St. Petersburg at the address: Nevsky Prospekt, 29, works on weekdays from ten in the morning until 19:00. Located 299 meters from the metro station "Gostiny Dvor" and 129 meters from the station. m. "Nevsky Prospect". Reception of both domestic and foreign currency is available. Cash withdrawal only in rubles.

On weekdays, the VTB 24 ATM operates in St. Petersburg at 32 Tchaikovsky Street. 320 meters from the station. m. "Chernyshevskaya". It works both for issuing and accepting cash in domestic currency - rubles.

ATMs in Admiralteysky district

ATMs VTB 24 in the Petrograd district

The address of VTB 24 ATM in St. Petersburg is 1 Ropshinskaya Street, Building 32. The terminal is open 24 hours a day. Very close is another VTB 24 ATM in St. Petersburg: 15 Chkalovsky Prospekt, building G, letter "G". Opening hours on weekdays from 10 a.m. to seven in the evening.

ATMs VTB 24 in the Kirovsky district of St. Petersburg

VTB 24 ATM in St. Petersburg at the address: Stachek Ave., Building 18 is located on the territory of the Administration of the Kirovsky District of St. Petersburg. ATMs are also located at the entrance and exit of the Narvskaya metro station. The schedule of the terminals coincides with the schedule of the subway. Services: cash withdrawal in rubles.

ATMs VTB 24 in St. Petersburg, Frunze district
