Magadan: attractions that are interesting to tourists

Today we look at the amazing northern city of Magadan. Its sights will be interesting to tourists. It is worth noting that this city is one of the youngest in Russia. Magadan was founded in 1929. At first it was a small working village, and the status of the city was assigned to it ten years later.

Amazing terrain

The nearby two peaks of the Upper Kolyma Upland - Snezhny Peak and Aborigine, made this area attractive not only for tourists, but also for climbers. And in the upper reaches of the Kolyma River there is a picturesque lake, named after the writer Jack London. It seems to be an accurate illustration of his stories. This lake is surrounded by a flowering meadow, mighty pine trees, in the water surface of which the peaks of the mountains are reflected and even ice floats in the summer. 300 km from the city there is a therapeutic thermal spring, and near it there is a balneotherapeutic resort "Talaya". The amazing landscape of the Magadan Reserve is best admired by helicopter ride. Only by helicopter can you reach the easternmost point of Russia - Ratmanov Island, located in the Bering Strait, and from it, figuratively speaking, you can easily reach Alaska.

Music and Drama Theater

Describing the main attractions of Magadan, we will talk about the musical and drama theater. Its building was opened in 1941, but the acting troupe and first productions appeared much earlier, even before the city itself. Together with the first builders, prisoners arrived on the shore of Nagaev Bay in the thirties of the nineteenth century. Many of them were ministers of art, who fell under the wheels of a hellish repression machine. The theater building was opened in the fall of 1941. For many decades, famous artists of his time performed on its stage, including USSR People's Artist Georgy Zhzhenov, who later was remembered by the audience brilliantly played the main roles in the films ‚ÄúCrew‚Äù, ‚ÄúResident's Error‚Äù and others.

Mask of Sorrow

What other sights of Magadan are known? The monument ‚ÄúMask of Sorrow‚Äù is dedicated to the memory of bloody pre-war and post-war political repressions. This is a face made in the manner of Cubism, from one eye of which tears flow in the form of masks, and a prison lattice is inserted into the other.





On the back of the monument you can see a sculpture of a kneeling crying woman at the crucifix. Inside the huge mask, visitors can see two rooms equipped as a prison cell and a prison cemetery. The author of the monument is Ernst Unknown.

Holy Trinity Cathedral and other churches

The sights of the city of Magadan can not only surprise or suggest, but also awaken a person's spiritual thirst. A tribute to people who suffered during the sinister times of repression is Holy Trinity Cathedral.





The white-stone building with five gilded domes majestically rises above residential buildings. The cathedral was built recently, in 2008. This is the largest, but not the only Orthodox shrine in the city.

Continuing the story of the harsh northern settlement called the city of Magadan, the sights of which we will describe further, we can not help but recall another iconic object. In 1996, the one-domed chapel of St. George the Victorious was erected on the Alley in memory of the soldiers-defenders. Twelve years ago, the church of St. Nicholas the Wonderworker was built.

In addition to the Orthodox, the city also has the Catholic Church of the Nativity of Jesus. The building attracts with its simplicity, the octagonal bell tower with a tented dome gives it a austere and at the same time elegant look.

Kozin Museum and Pilots Memorial

In Magadan, there is a museum dedicated to the life and work of the famous singer Vadim Kozin, one of the founders of the modern stage.





The pilots, who during the war transported aircraft from the USA to Krasnoyarsk via the airway by relay method, are dedicated to the memorial consisting of the wreckage of crashed aircraft.

Museum of Local Lore

Arriving in Magadan, you must see the sights. Which ones are worth seeing? Be sure to visit the museum of local lore. Its expositions are dedicated to the history of the region, starting from paleontological finds, ethnographic objects and ending with documentary evidence of the atrocities of the Stalin era. Given that among the repressed there were many talented cultural figures, the museum has exhibitions of their works.

Sculpture of Vysotsky

What other interesting sights of Magadan? At the observation deck of Nagaev Bay, the traveler will be able to see the sculpture of Vladimir Vysotsky.





Residents remember that the cult poet and singer visited the city in 1968 and dedicated two songs to him. The monument was installed just two years ago.

Some interesting places

The wonderful city of Magadan, whose sights are unique in their own way, is also able to please with a variety of social entertainments. Tourists can relax and enjoy delicious local cuisine at the Green Crocodile Bar, TorroGrill or Champs Elysees restaurants, as well as have fun at the Bilibin Club, Svalka or Cosmos entertainment complexes. Fans of outdoor activities are recommended to visit the Kolymsky ice palace, the Snegorka sports and entertainment complex.
