Rest in Naberezhnye Chelny: recreation centers, leisure activities, holidays with children

Beautiful nature, original architecture, lots of cultural and historical attractions will surely attract the attention of a traveler. Vibrant and diverse will appear before tourists rest in Naberezhnye Chelny.

The tourist bases and numerous hotels will welcome guests with a high level of service and a whole range of sports, recreational and recreational activities.

Leisure

Fans of outdoor activities in Naberezhnye Chelny can go skiing. In the resort "Fedotovo" there are long slopes of any difficulty level, where it will be interesting for both beginners and experienced skiers and snowboarders. Sports equipment and equipment meets European standards. The prices are affordable, with discounts in the low season.

Travelers can join groups for hiking in the forest or lakes and spend the night in camping tents. Extreme sportsmen can try mountain river rafting, hot air ballooning, bungee jumping or parachuting and diving.

Holidays in Naberezhnye Chelny at the bases will give you the opportunity to book horseback riding, biking, sledding, inline skating and kayaking. Adults will be interested in playing paintball, various quests or classes in a crossbow-beam shooting gallery, offered in active tourism clubs.

Children's entertainment

Camps are provided for children's recreation in Naberezhnye Chelny, in which there is everything to keep schoolchildren busy. Many of them are located near the city in the national park and near water bodies. Among the most popular are Zvezdny, Solnechny, Saulyk, Rosinka and Nika.

In the camps, children are offered intellectual and creative programs, a variety of thematic games, sports and art competitions and classes in foreign languages. Some of them are aimed at improving health and provide small patients with massage, water treatments and other necessary medical procedures.

You can have fun with the whole family with children in the city dolphinarium, where amazing sea animals will show incredible tricks and performances. In the Museum of Ecology it will be interesting to see exhibits from the plant and animal world. And in the park of culture and leisure there are attractions for absolutely any age.

You can have a great weekend or vacation in cozy houses on the river bank called Paradise Coast. In the summer, you can swim, have a picnic, barbecue and steam in the bath.
