Hotel Sugar Beach Xeno Hotels 4 * (Turkey, Alanya): traveler reviews

The beach hotel Sugar Beach Xeno Hotels 4 * until two thousand and eleven was simply called Sugar Beach, that is, the Sugar Coast. But then she became part of the Xeno chain. The hotel is country, club type, inexpensive. It is designed to spend time at sea and ride around the area. Getting from the airport is a long time, but closer than to Alanya, to which the Xeno Hotels Sugar Beach 4 * hotel is administratively assigned. Tourists write that it takes about four hours to go to the hotel with a transfer, and vice versa - less. But if the bus is air-conditioned, comfortable, with an interesting guide, then time flies unnoticed. But local authorities are already building a new international airport. It is located near the town of Gazipasha. From there, get to the hotel for about half an hour.





Location

Xeno Hotels Sugar Beach 4 * hotel on the map can be found between Antalya and Alanya. The fact is that it is located in the village of Mahmutlar. Administratively, this is Alanya, but at the same time it is separated from the international airport of Antalya by about a hundred kilometers. Around the hotel there is a village with a well-established infrastructure. There are many cafes, shops, entertainment and attractions. So, in addition to excursions, you absolutely do not need to go anywhere from here. But if you want to see Alanya itself, then you can get there by bus for one and a half lira. And there already visit the fortress, and the embankment, and old shipyards. On Saturdays, a fruit market is located next to the hotel. There you can cheaply buy sweets. The village of Mahmutlar itself is unusually beautiful - people go there to take pictures for memory. There is a very nice park with fountains and benches where local artists arrange to draw. Mahmutlar is considered the most budget resort in the vicinity. These places are famous for the warm water of emerald color, picturesque mountains and pine forests.





Alanya Hotels

Reviews of hotels in this resort area are the main source of information for vacationers. It is no secret that often the "star" of hotels in Turkey is simply a formality. Usually the most attractive hotels are located in Alanya itself. Their prices are lower than in Antalya or Kemer, and there are more opportunities to find entertainment. On the other hand, the cost of rest in hotels located in villages outside the city is even lower. But tourists often get bored there because there is nowhere to go and nothing to do. But this does not apply to our complex Xeno Sugar Beach Hotel 4 *. Reviews of vacationers recommend it as an ideal accommodation. The center of Alanya is quite accessible, and the village itself is very good for tourists in terms of shopping and entertainment. Hotels in Mahmutlar itself are basically the Quartet. Most of them are multi-story buildings with a not very vast territory and pebble beaches that are across the road. Prices are even lower than in the rest of Alanya.





Territory

This hotel is small, but the infrastructure of the Xeno Sugar Beach Hotel 4 * hotel (Turkey) allows tourists to get everything they need without leaving its gate. The building has two elevators. On the territory there are shops, a spa center, and a disco. In the lobby there are comfortable sofas and tables to sit with a laptop or tablet. A beautiful green garden - flowers, palm trees, bananas, benches among roses ... Everything is very clean and comfortable. No wonder they say that in this place the whole resort Turkey (Alanya) is concentrated. Xeno Sugar Beach Hotel 4 * - one of the most profitable, budget and successful options for accommodation in Mahmutlar. At least, so say those who have visited it.





Where do tourists live?

Sugar Beach Xeno Hotels 4 * has over a hundred rooms. They are mostly standard, with the usual for the "four" content. The rooms make a good impression. They have everything you need. Plumbing works, there are no breakages or rusts. The figures on the bed are made without any tip. The furniture is in good condition. On television you can watch Russian channels. Each room has balconies with plastic chairs. Often there is a sea view (at least side). Clean often and efficiently. Bed linen is changed three times a week. The mattresses are soft, the towels are clean. The interior of the rooms is elegant, in white and brown tones. On the floor there is a carpet.

Sea and beach

The coast in the Mahmutlar area is across the road. To get there, you need to go through the underpass. The beach, like almost everywhere on the Anatolian coast, has both sand and pebbles. Tourists are advised to go to the left of the hotel, it is more convenient there, or wear rubber slippers. The beach belongs to the hotel, it is equipped. There are sunbeds, umbrellas, mattresses. Towels are not issued, you need to take your own. There are many water entertainments on the beach - parasalting, bananas, boats. Animation works here during the day. You can play volleyball. However, the beach bar is free of charge - only soft drinks. Very clean, well cleaned. Employees put exactly sunbeds, adjust mattresses. Two showers and changing rooms work. The hotel itself has a pool that is cleaned every night. It is deep, and it is good to swim in it if the sea is agitated or the water is cold. There you can swim until six in the evening. There is a slide and a children's "paddling pool". Free seats on sunbeds by the pool, as well as on the beach, at any time of the day.
