Hotel "Topkapi Palace" (Turkey, Antalya): reviews and photos

Antalya is a famous resort region in Turkey, where every year a huge number of tourists from the CIS countries and Russia come to rest, it is also very loved by citizens of European countries. Many travel agencies recommend this region as a place for active and fun holidays, which is associated with the presence in the village of a huge number of nightclubs, bars, restaurants, as well as other institutions of this kind.

As for places for recreation, in Antalya for a long period the VOV Topkapi Palace Hotel has been considered very popular. It is he who often takes the leading positions in various tourist ratings based on traveler reviews.



Location and General Information

Hotel "Topkapi Palace" (Antalya) is located on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, in the tropical zone of the globe. Despite the high air temperature that stands here in the summer, tourists do not feel the heat and heat due to the action of the air masses of the Mediterranean. In the resort period, which lasts from April to the end of October, there is a large number of sunny days that delight vacationers.

Hotel "Topkapi Palace" was built in 1999 and already at that time was recognized as one of the most luxurious in the whole country. After some time, major renovations and a number of repair work were carried out on its territory and in the buildings, as a result of which the buildings acquired a modern and very beautiful appearance, and a beautiful blooming garden appeared on the territory. Rooms

All 818 apartments available in the hotel are located in five huge buildings, each of which consists of six floors. They have a very well developed infrastructure and everything is very beautifully arranged. Throughout the hotel, including the rooms, a modern air conditioning system is installed, which allows guests to feel as comfortable as possible under any temperature conditions on the street.

All rooms are divided into categories such as Standard, Family and Suite. The hotel also has a detached villa, designed for VIP guests.

All apartments, regardless of their comfort category, have an individual bathroom, fully equipped with the necessary fixtures. In it, holidaymakers are provided with a large set of bath accessories, which includes gel, soap, shampoo, towels, bathrobes, as well as slippers.

"Standard"

There are 676 apartments of the Standard group in the Topkapi Palace Hotel, each of which can accommodate a maximum of three people. This number consists of one room, where there is everything you need for a comfortable stay. Hotel guests often note that, despite the fact that the "Standard" is not designed for family settlement, this does not make staying in it with the children uncomfortable. Many tourists, resting here with a child, are satisfied.

As for the furniture equipment, there are three berths, represented by modern European-style beds, and one of them is designed for two, and the second - for one guest. If desired, guests can arrange their belongings in bedside tables, as well as in a wardrobe. The apartments are fitted with expensive carpets. The room has a furnished balcony, where there is a table and three chairs - it offers stunning views of the garden.

The "Standard" has the necessary set of electronics, which includes a large plasma TV - little by little satellite channels are broadcast, many of which are in Russian. There is also an electric kettle and a whole set of dishes for three and one child.

In the comments of tourists it is often said that the rooms of the "Family" category are very bright, they have a sufficient number of lighting fixtures, and guests are attracted by large mirrors that visually make the space much larger.



"Suite"

Apartments "Suite", of which there are 50 pieces in the hotel, offer guests superior comfort conditions. Each room can accommodate up to 4 people, as indicated by the established number of beds, represented by a large bed with an orthopedic mattress and a folding sofa, which can also serve as a sleeping place for two. The room also has a huge mirror that completely covers one of the walls - it often attracts the attention of vacationers. Tourists, vacationing in such apartments "Topkapi Palace" (Turkey), note that the interior is very well thought out, so it is pleasant to be in the room. VIP villa

In reviews of "Topkapi Palace" (Turkey), you can often find comments on the luxurious villa, which is located on site. As guests note, everything is arranged in it in such a way that a guest resting in it can feel like a real king.

Tourists conquer the interior of the room, which is made in the Arabic style. Here you can observe light and coffee shades and a huge number of gilded details that look very expensive. Such apartments deserve special attention also because they have a personal sauna, a swimming pool, as well as a fitness center.

The villa is a real cottage, which consists of two floors. The first is a living area where light furniture sets are set, consisting of sofas and armchairs, as well as coffee tables. Here vacationers can meet guests. There is also a spa area and a fitness room on this floor. On the second floor there is a luxurious sleeping area, where there is a huge bed for two and all the necessary furniture. On the floor of this room are white skins, which are an element of real luxury.

Entertainment

Tourists who relax in the hotel, in their reviews of "Topkapi Palace" note that, staying here, you do not have to be bored. For guests around the clock there is a room of intricate animators who can entertain representatives of any age category. All vacationers with children in the comments about the hotel noted that special attention is paid to guests with children. In particular, for children, children's programs are held here, performances are organized and various performances are made.

As for adults, in the evening and at night they can take part in discos, during which many foreign hits loved by many play. In the daytime, fans of relay races and sporting events can participate in them.

Most tourists note that members of the animation team are fluent in several languages, including Russian, German, English and French - which, in their opinion, greatly facilitates their communication with guests.

Nutrition

Almost every review of the Topkapi Palace Hotel (Turkey) says that its catering establishments are amazingly prepared. Each guest who lives here on the all-inclusive concept has the opportunity to visit the buffet several times a day, which is organized in the main restaurant.



Guests also note that in addition to the main restaurant of Turkish cuisine, the hotel has 4 more catering establishments of the highest category, which can offer Mediterranean, Italian and international cuisines. Tourists pay special attention to dishes prepared with seafood - in many comments left about the rest at the hotel, they recommend tasting them. Also, often a lot of positive reviews are addressed in the direction of the grill restaurant "Mangal Park".

In addition to luxurious large establishments, the hotel has 10 bars, which are located mainly on the beach, near the water zone, and a small part of them are located in the buildings. In such places, guests are ready to surprise mainly with snacks and desserts. Much attention is paid to the range of drinks.



SPA-center

According to vacationers, the spa center, which is located in one of the Topkapi Palace buildings (Turkey), deserves great attention. His visit is paid, but some services are provided free of charge. In particular, guests can go to the thermal zone, which consists of a hammam and sauna - there is no need to pay for their visit, in addition, bath accessories will also be provided free of charge. There is also a jacuzzi.

As for massage and cosmetic procedures, they are provided here by professional masters, but their services are charged according to a separate price list, which can be found on site or at the hotel.

Sport

Lovers of an active lifestyle will definitely not be bored staying at the Topkapi Palace Hotel (Turkey). According to vacationers, it is equipped with one of the best sports areas on the entire Turkish coast. In particular, as many as 9 tennis courts with professional lighting are equipped here, and the equipment for the game is provided completely free of charge. There are also several outdoor venues for playing volleyball, soccer, and basketball.

For guests who are watching their body sculptures, there is a huge gym that can accommodate a large number of visitors at the same time. Also in "Topkapi Palace" (Antalya) there is a fitness room where collective aerobics classes are held, which are preferred mainly by women and girls.

For business

Topkapi Palace Hotel often hosts formal events of various kinds. For such cases, six luxurious conference rooms are equipped in the recreation area, each of which has modern furniture, as well as lighting and sound equipment. If desired, guests can use the provided stationery, as well as equipment that is available here.

The total capacity of the halls is 1840 people, which is an excellent indicator. People who held business events here are satisfied with the results, justifying their feedback by the fact that the staff is very responsible in their organization and maintenance.



Beach

The hotel has its own private beach, which is covered with fine and clean sand. The length of this recreation area is 200 meters, it is quite large in width. A great addition for holidaymakers is a large wooden pier, which also has seating areas.

As for amenities, the beach is fully equipped in order to create all the necessary conditions for comfort for the guests of the VOV Topkapi Palace Hotel (Turkey). In particular, a large number of sun loungers and umbrellas are located here, on which everyone can sit. There are also several locker rooms, toilet stalls and, of course, showers are installed. On the beach there is a bar where guests can enjoy refreshing drinks and beautiful cocktails, as well as a wide selection of desserts on the menu.

Reviews of beach vacations at the Topkapi Palace Hotel (Turkey) often state that there is a lot of entertainment on the coastline. In particular, everyone can rent a yacht or boat and go on a small cruise with their loved one or the whole family. Fans of extreme sports can afford to go skiing or jet skiing, and lovers of a quiet holiday have the opportunity to take on an air mattress and simply lie on it in the water.

A rescue squad is on duty at the beach throughout the day.

Additional services provided at the hotel

Travelers often note that the range of services provided to customers in the Great Patriotic War Topkapi Palace (Antalya) is quite wide. Many of them note the presence of a cinema, which can be visited with the whole family or in the company of friends.

Prices

This ticket price usually includes flights, transfers and rental housing, it also includes a standard range of services provided at the hotel. In some cases, for an additional fee, the tour operator may offer to book an excursion tour in Antalya or nearby regions in advance, however, many travelers recommend choosing such offers in Turkey, since their cost in this case is much cheaper.



Tourists who spent their holidays here are often noted that for the conditions provided, such a price is more than acceptable.
