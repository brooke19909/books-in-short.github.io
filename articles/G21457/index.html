What is important to know at the preparatory stage?

The first and probably the most important question that precedes an interesting creative process should arise at the preparatory stage. After all, we all are well aware that people are significantly different in complexion. But do not forget that dolls come in different models. And the size of their clothes also depends on this. For example, clothes for a Baby Bon doll will be very large for a Barbie doll. In addition, the first type of darling is positioned by the manufacturer as a baby doll. While Barbie is a fashionista with a model appearance.

That is why, before proceeding to the study and execution of phased clothes for dolls with their own hands, we recommend spending some time and sorting out the dimensions of the pet. So how do you measure the doll? According to the reviews of beginner needlewomen, you can simply put your favorite on paper, outline its outline with a pencil, and then finally finalize the details. However, this method is often used when sewing clothes for a baby doll or another, more like a child than an adult. After all, every Barbie, like a real woman, has a decollete zone. That is why the above method of taking measurements is not suitable for her - clothes may simply not fit.

For this reason, experienced needlewomen write different advice in the reviews. If they are going to sew clothes for dolls with their own hands, they prefer to take measurements from their darling in the same way as they would do with a person. And then we will tell you in detail how to still determine the size of the doll.

How to take measurements with a pet?

In order to identify the correct testimony, we will have to undress the doll. After that, prepare a centimeter, paper and pen. When all the necessary manipulations have been completed, we boldly move on to measuring our pet. And you can do this together with your daughter. She will certainly take part in such an interesting and previously unfamiliar process with great pleasure.

So, we break to take measurements from the doll:

  1. We put it on the back and measure the growth from the heels to the top of the head. Then we write the resulting value on a piece of paper.
  2. Then note the distance from the heels to the neck, waist and base of the doll‚Äôs legs. Each new testimony is necessarily transferred to paper, so as not to forget or confuse with the pattern of details of clothes for the doll with their own hands.
  3. Next we need to determine the length of the sleeve. To do this, we measure, necessarily through the elbow, the length of the arm from the shoulder to the wrist. And also write down the resulting figure.
  4. After using a centimeter, we reveal the width of the chest and back, as well as the circumference of the torso and waist. The first two values ‚Äã‚Äãare obtained by measuring the distance between the hands of the pet, front and back. The third - taking measurements from the body of the doll strictly under the arms raised up. And the last - measuring the narrowest part of the pet's body.
  5. Do not forget to determine the circumference of the hips and the widest part of the leg. And by all means we record readings on a piece of paper.
  6. Then go to the hand. We measure the girth of the shoulder part (under the arm) and the wrist or, if the doll has splayed fingers or, conversely, fingers clenched into a fist, then the brush itself.
  7. The last value, which is also important to determine, is the girth of the head. It should be measured by finding the widest part.
