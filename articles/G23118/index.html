What is Blackberry? Mobile phones BlackBerry: reviews, prices

When we talk about smartphones, most often for some reason devices from Samsung, Apple, HTC, LG and other competing companies come to mind that produce touch phones that can literally do anything. And by ear, the BlackBerry brand, which represents smartphones is no worse.

We will talk about what Blackberry is and why this product of a Canadian company deserves special attention in this article. We will also reveal the main features of the newest models of this brand and their prices in electronics stores. We also give some customer reviews of these devices.

General view





So, as already noted, mobile devices are released by the Canadian developer RIM. In general, every Blackberry smartphone deserves attention for the reason that the company has its own corporate identity.

Take a look, for example, at the popular modern smartphones. They have a rectangular shape, a large screen, the same location of the navigation keys and, in almost all cases, the Android or iOS operating system. Uniqueness can boast except that Nokia with its Lumia on Windows Phone. Everything, nothing else to meet on the phone market!

At the same time, Blackberry should be highlighted separately. The phone is equipped with a QWERTY-keyboard, which is not used on most smartphones. The company has developed its own style - black color, strict tone lines. Finally, phones are not copies of other devices, only for this, developers already need to pay tribute!

Benefits

You can still list some of the strengths of these devices. For example, your own operating system. Due to the fact that the Blackberry smartphone does not work on Android, it responds well to the touch of the owner, does not slow down and does not allow delays in performing tasks.





In addition, if you still do not know what Blackberry is, it is worth mentioning a special encrypted method of transmitting data. Due to this, all the information that is on your BlackBerry device is strictly classified. Add the functions of setting passwords to folders and files here - and get the perfect phone for members of government and government officials at various levels.

Another important point is a full keyboard. Agree, this is a rather bold decision that most mobile device developers did not dare to make. Due to it, the Blackberry device (we will publish reviews of the company below) is a convenient tool for sending mail, correspondence and viewing Internet sites anywhere!

This is not to say that the BlackBerry brand is very successful in the smartphone market. As you can see, the company survived the crisis, but so far it has not been able to regain leadership positions in the telephone industry.

Popular models
