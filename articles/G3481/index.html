How to clear the cache in the iPhone of different models

The system blocks of any modern gadgets operate on the principle of the accumulation of temporary files and programs in a special storage. Knowing how to clear the cache in an iPhone, you can provide faster loading of pictures, music and other applications. In addition, the release of unnecessary information allows you to expand the RAM of the phone. Next, consider how to clean different iPhone models.





Why do I need cache flushing?

The longer the smartphone system works, the more unnecessary files accumulate in its memory. If you do not remove all this garbage in the form of temporary data in a timely manner, they can occupy gigabytes of operational space on the device. Removing all this is easy on your own.

What can the accumulation of informational garbage lead to:

  • device overload due to lack of memory;
  • the inability to save important information, photos and videos;
  • difficulty playing audio recordings and games;
  • slowing down the speed of the device in interactive mode.

Next, consider how to clear the cache in the iPhone of different models.

Where to begin?

It is necessary to start cleaning the gadget's memory by deleting temporary information that was saved automatically after visiting online resources. If you use the standard Safari gateway as a working browser, the update procedure will look like this:

  1. It is necessary to go from the working panel to the "Settings" category.
  2. Scroll through the list of points issued to the application "Safari".
  3. After activating the window, scroll down to the visible settings menu.
  4. Choose a position to clear history and sites.
  5. From the main desktop, go to the Settings app.
  6. Confirm the action in the pop-up window.

Knowing how to clear the application cache on an iPhone, you can control the process at any convenient time. At the same time, the active sections of the menu change the color from blue to gray, which indicates the release of space in the device's memory.
