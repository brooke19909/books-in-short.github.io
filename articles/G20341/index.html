"Telecard": how to pay for services

Telecard is a satellite television operator. Broadcasting is carried out using modern equipment that exists throughout Russia. Payment

Many users have connected the Telecard TV. How to pay it, interests them. In some cities there are no company offices. But since modern technologies are developed, you can pay for services online and offline.





QIWI

You can pay for Telecard services from QIWI wallet. This procedure is quick and easy. To do this, you just need to go to your personal account, create a payment. The QIWI system allows you to pay for a TV within minutes. This is done from a phone, tablet, computer. A terminal is also available for this.

Bank card

Still how to pay? "Telecard TV" is paid by credit card. To do this, go to the Uniteller website. The following information should be entered there:
