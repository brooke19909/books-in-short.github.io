Tromso (Norway) - chilling beauty outside the Arctic Circle

Not all travelers like hot beaches, spreading palm trees, tanned people in bathing suits and a cool cocktail in the scorching sun. There are those who are attracted by the harsh and impregnable beauty of the Nordic countries, mulled wine by the fireplace and delicious cheese soup made from fresh fish. For lovers of northern color, we advise you to go relax in Tromso. Since ancient times, Norway has been famous not only for famous stories about the ancient Vikings, but also for the icy waters of the fjords, the enchanting beauty of the Northern Lights and the cordiality of local residents. Visit this town - and you will understand that only the North can be better than hot countries!

Miracles hidden behind the Arctic Circle





Why is Tromso resort so attractive for tourists? Norway is a country, part of which is located beyond the Arctic Circle, for many tourists it seems mysterious and distant. This town is washed by the cold gloomy waters of the Norwegian Sea. Tromso is a small piece of the mainland plus two more small islands. Travelers will be delighted with the local unusual color: sheer cliffs, silence and tranquility, some majesty, opportunities for climbers and the noise of mountain rivers. Boat trips will give you an unprecedented pacification!

What to see in Tromso?





Norway is famous for its magically fabulous Northern Lights. This is just a spectacular sight! The best time to visit the Norwegian city of Tromso is spring. A long polar day suggests that winter is already receding, and welcomes the influx of travelers.

Be sure to ride the funicular, which will allow you to consider in great detail the local beauty from a bird's eye view.

Not everyone presents their vacation without an abundance of excursion programs and visits to cultural property. This is what the city of Tromso is famous for. Norway is just a storehouse of cultural institutions! And this small town is unthinkable without many museums and attractions, one of which is the cathedral.





Greatness of the Arctic Cathedral and museum exhibits

This cultural and religious institution is an honorary symbol and the main value of the city. The Arctic Cathedral is an impressive sight! It is unusual in that it is constructed entirely of glass and stone in the form of a fabulous and beautiful iceberg. Its edges and intricate stained glass reflect brightly the cold northern rays.

Everyone will enjoy visiting the museum, because you can choose any subject to taste! For example, what you should look at exhibits representing the flora and fauna of the Arctic, consider gems - minerals and stones, preserved remains of ancient inhabitants, learn more about Sami culture, listen to informative stories about local fishing. All this provides the tourist of Tromso.

Norway is proud of the town, because there is still a beautiful Botanical Garden in which you can see outlandish plants in a harsh or alpine climate.

Local national cuisine

Norwegian cuisine simply does not appear without freshly caught fish, which is served in Tromso in any form: in soups, cream, and baked. And what a joy after a walk along the snowy paths to stop in a cozy cafe and drink mulled wine with spices!





Travel tips and interesting facts

  1. A trip in November or January is as good as in spring, because the temperature does not drop below 4 degrees Celsius.
  2. The climate of the city is very mild due to the warm Gulf Stream.
  3. The famous temple of the Blessed Virgin Mary is truly considered the northernmost cathedral of Catholics.
  4. Also the most northern recognized brewery of the city of Tromso. Norway, whose photo can be seen here, is famous not only for its landscapes, but also for the excellent quality of beer.
  5. Trips to the town are not so expensive, so not only everyone will be able to visit here, but they will also have the opportunity to settle in the best hotel.
  6. Tromso Airport allows you to rent a car. This is a very convenient service that will add you even more freedom while traveling.
  7. In the complex "Polaria" you can watch the playful seals and catfish. Such an interesting and funny sight will not leave indifferent persons of any age, especially children. You will bring many photographs where your child will be captured, who communicates with representatives of the northern fauna.
  8. Among the excursions there is the opportunity to ride a boat on the fjords. It will be an incredibly romantic walk that can be arranged on a honeymoon.
  9. High snow-capped mountains and gloomy cliffs are perfect for lovers of such an extreme sport as climbing.
  10. What is worth noting New Year in Troms! Last minute packages will allow you to plunge into a real fairy tale about the Snow Queen. Give the kids true New Year magic in fabulous Norway. Be sure - this momentous event will be remembered for a long time by the whole family!
