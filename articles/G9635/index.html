Hotel Copthorne Hotel 4 * Sharjah, United Arab Emirates: overview, description, features and reviews

Copthorne Hotel 4 * Sharjah is a beautiful modern hotel that offers potential guests all the amenities necessary for a full stay and a decent vacation. This complex is located in the center of Sharjah. And this, I must say, is only one advantage of all that this hotel can boast of.





Briefly about the complex

So, Copthorne Hotel 4 * Sharjah is a tall attractive building, made in a modern style, which immediately catches your eye. It can be seen from afar. It is safe to say that this hotel is a real highlight of the local surroundings. It is located on a street called Cornish Road - this is the very center of Sharjah. The hotel rooms offer magnificent views of the Khalid lagoon.

This hotel has a really convenient location, thanks to which guests can quickly and easily reach such famous shopping centers as, for example, Mega Mall, Sahara Mall or Sharjah City Center. Still not far from the Copthorne Hotel 4 * Sharjah there are museums that are one of the main attractions of the resort, an aquarium, an amphitheater called Al-Mahaz and, of course, a water park. And a five-minute walk is the Sharjah Exhibition Center. So the place is very good and profitable from all sides.
