Hotel "Azimut Hotel Murmansk": address, description, photos and reviews

It should be comfortable even beyond the Arctic Circle. This is exactly what the founders of the modern hotel complex decided by opening the Azimut Hotel Murmansk hotel. In fact, with constant cold and harsh, variable weather, the building itself stands as a fortress, and its guests can feel comfortable. This is a large building located in a picturesque place.

If you need to register via the Internet, you can order a transfer service from the airport from the administrator. This is quite convenient, given that the city is located along the Tuloma River, which flows into the Kola Bay. Many travelers come to these parts to enjoy the amazing nature and remoteness from civilization. Literally driving out of town for a couple of kilometers, you can see not only the bay, but also the Barents Sea, which washes the rocky and dark coast of the region.

From the window of almost all rooms of the hotel you can see the port, which smoothly passes into the bay. There is also a walk, because the building is located in a busy place in which the infrastructure is constantly developing. The address of the "Azimuth Hotel Murmansk": Lenin Avenue, 82.

The object was formerly an administrative building. Later it went into private use, and entrepreneurs decided to reconstruct it, creating a great place to relax and conduct business meetings.

Accommodations

Hotel "Azimut Hotel Murmansk" offers various services, but at the same time establishes a number of requirements and rules for accommodation. One of the main concerns arrival. Reception of new guests is carried out at 14:00 local time. You can arrive and book a room earlier, but you will have to wait for the set time. Such requirements are not accidental, because almost all rooms, especially during the season, are occupied, and in order not to create queues, the apartments are distributed in a similar way.

Residents must check out of the room no later than 12:00. After that, they are drawn up accordingly at the reception desk. It is worth paying attention that in case of damage to the property of the hotel, fines for damage are set. If the guest refuses to pay for the damage caused to them in the room, they can bring him to administrative responsibility. Detailed fines are available at the reception.

Having issued a number and signed in the documents, new guests are given passwords from free Wi-Fi. It is valid throughout. Parking is available for vehicles. The cost of renting 1 place per day is 200 rubles. In the cold season, services are available for heating the car and warming it up before serving at the entrance. It is forbidden to leave transport at the hotel entrance.

Paid is a pet friendly service. It is worth paying attention to the fact that guests will have a pet, you must warn in advance. Otherwise, the placement may be refused. After specifying the breed and the necessary accessories before settling, the workers will install an aviary, bowls and offer high-quality pet food.

If tourists gather with their children, then it should also be agreed in advance with the administrator when booking a room. Children under six years old stay free of charge. In addition, upon request, the hotel can provide a cot, which will already be included in the room rate.

For children from 12 years old or when settling more people than indicated in the application, the fee will be 1,200 rubles per day additionally. You can only install one more bed in the room, so all the nuances of accommodation should be agreed in advance with the administrator.

Services

Services "Azimut Hotel Murmansk" provides various types. Food and drinks are available in a large assortment. There are several restaurants that are ready to deliver the order directly to your room. Internet on site is completely free. The speed is high and stable, which allows not only to correspond, but also to watch movies. Television is available with an expanded package of television channels also in each room.

There are many additional options in the lobby. Here, in addition to the rack, there is a waiting room and a lounge. On the right side there are cells for storing luggage if necessary.
