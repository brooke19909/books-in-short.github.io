Refinancing at Post Bank for individuals

Credit refinancing is a good financial tool. But only if you approach this process correctly, that is, pre-calculate options, choose the best program and bank. An active marketing policy at Post Bank offers favorable conditions for individuals in Russia. Let's try to figure it out.

Refinancing is a financial service that allows you to transfer credit obligations from one bank to another with the most favorable conditions.

Better interest on loans

Thanks to refinancing at Post Bank, you can transfer a loan to this organization to obtain more favorable interest and conditions. This allows you to reduce the size of the payment and thus reduce your credit load.

Thanks to the refinancing, it is possible to reduce the payment in the following ways:

  • Rate reduction. Over time, in the presence of great competition in banks, interest decreases more and more. This allows you to currently find the most favorable rates, compared with those at which the loan was issued before.
  • Extension of the term. Payment can be reduced by issuing another loan for a longer period.

In accordance with the new agreement, money is transferred from the current lender's account to a new account.

Benefits of this refinancing program

Is refinancing at Post Bank profitable? It happens that a loan issued earlier becomes unbearable for the borrower, and he is forced to revise his agreement with the bank. If the lender does not agree to meet him and offer comfortable and favorable conditions for the restructuring of the loan, then the only way out is refinancing in another bank.

There are many advantages to refinancing loans of other banks at Post Bank. Such a service gives borrowers the following opportunities:

Refinancing Terms

What are the requirements for a potential borrower?

To take part in the program, you must meet all the requirements that are presented to the borrower by the bank. First of all, it is necessary to observe conditions such as:

  • the presence of citizenship of the Russian Federation by the borrower and permanent residence at a specific address;
  • consumer age from eighteen years;
  • official employment;
  • the borrower has no delays in previous payments;
  • work experience at one enterprise for at least six months;
  • stable income.

Documents

In order to send an application for refinancing a loan at the Post Bank for individuals, the client must collect the following documents:

  • passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation;
  • TIN of the employer, as well as the SNILS number of the borrower.

After a positive decision is received on the application, it is required to provide documents that contain information about the refinanced loan:

  • time of conclusion of the agreement;
  • contract number;
  • name of the banking organization;
  • customer account number;
  • BIC Bank;
  • the amount of the loan received at the time of conclusion of the agreement.

What requirements should a loan meet?

Not all types of loans are subject to refinancing at Post Bank. There are a number of specific loan requirements that will allow you to re-arrange it in the future:

  • the loan must be consumer or provided for the purchase of vehicles, that is, a car loan;
  • the client who prepares the refinancing should not have debts and delays in the current loan amount;
  • checking the payment history; it is necessary that the borrower does not allow himself to repay the loan out of time at least for six months;
  • before the termination of the loan agreement, must remain more than three months;




  • Mortgages or consumer loans should not be issued by financial companies owned by VTB Corporation;
  • loans must be issued only in Russian currency, that is, in rubles.

If a consumer complies with all the requirements listed above, then he can confidently count on concluding an agreement with Post Bank on refinancing a consumer loan.
