In the Orenburg region there is an original and colorful city - Orsk. Sights, photos and descriptions of which are in this article, will help determine where to visit and what to see in this city. Neighborhood

One of the interesting Russian cities is Orsk. It is necessary to begin to see its sights from the vicinity. The territories around the city are dotted with ancient settlements, solitary mounds and burial grounds. One of them is Kumaksky. Burials were made in it in the Bronze Age.



There are mounds of 7-6 centuries BC. e., from the early Iron Age. Hryvnias and Egyptian vessels were found in them. In winter, in the vicinity of Orsk, you can snowboard and ski in the Ural Mountains. There is even a ski resort located a hundred kilometers from Orsk.

Wonderful mountains

Each city has its own "highlight". Orsk was no exception. Sights of the city will not be fully explored, if you do not visit Mount Transfiguration. Among its main advantages are the treasures of variegated and wax jasper, hidden in the bowels.

This Orsk mineral is world famous. Jasper shimmers with many colors and shades, has numerous amazing patterns. Orsk mineral can be found in the Winter Palace, the Louvre, the Hermitage, even in the Moscow metro and in Britain.



Another mountain, called the Colonel, is a natural monument. It is located in the south-east of the city, on the left bank of the river Or. The mountain is famous as one of the deposits of jasper.

Museums

Albeit small, but there is one interesting town in Russia - Orsk. Sights can be explored, starting with museums. All information about the history of the city is in the Historical and Local Lore. It is located on Lenin Avenue, 46. The museum contains magnificent collections of art, archaeological finds, coins and ancient household items.

The exhibition devoted to the valley of the Amazons attracts attention. All exhibits were found in Orsk. For children there is a special excursion "Russian Chamber", created in the form of a game. And in the vicinity was found a polished piece of jasper depicting Lenin. Now this exhibit is also kept in the museum.

There are architectural monuments in the city of Orsk. The main attractions include the station building. It is made in the spirit of German classicism, has a hipped roof and pointed spiers. And on the platform stands a monument to the engine, which traveled from 1934 to 1964.

Parks

There are green spaces and squares in the city of Orsk. The sights of the parks are enclosed in nature itself, in clear forest air. There are three large squares in Orsk: Central them. Polyanichko, Builders and the name of Malishevsky. The winding alleys and paths, the silence and birdsong - all this will give nature lovers peace of mind. In the parks there are secluded arbors.

There is also a Memorial of Glory. For the townspeople this is a holy place. At the memorial is a bronze plaque lined with a green-black coil. Eternal flame burns . And on the stove are lists of the dead and the eternal memory of 10 residents of the city.



Interesting in Orsk is the district of Sotsgorod. It was conceived as a single system linking industrial and residential complexes. Moreover, they should eventually become a garden city. The author of the idea was Hans Schmidt.
