Domina Coral Bay El Sultan 5 *: reviews, photos

Domina Coral Bay El Sultan 5 * - these are several palaces of rest and entertainment, as if descended from the pages of the Thousand and One Nights. This is a new complex with all modern types of tourist service, a first-class level of comfort and a well-developed infrastructure. We can say that this hotel will satisfy the most demanding customers. The hotel is a beach hotel, its buildings are on the shore - some are far away, and some are near the sea. In addition, they are buried in the greenery of the park, which is carefully looked after. The hotel belongs to the famous Italian line "Domina". She has her own concept of relaxation, which is not very familiar to this African country.



Charm, as tourists lovingly call it

This Egyptian resort with fantastic coral colors is not only a paradise for divers, but also an ideal place to relax on the beach. The one who is called the "seal." That is why not only beach hotels are being built here, but entire cities and palaces for wealthy vacationers. Such as Domina Coral Bay El Sultan 5 *. Reviews of this hotel indicate that this is one of the best solutions for tourists who come to Egypt for sea and sun baths.

How to get there

When travel companies flew to Sharm, charters to the international airport of this resort arrived from all over Russia. The average flight time from the central regions of the country was about four and a half hours. But this is how travelers of package tours arrive. Independent tourists can fly to Cairo, and from there get to Sharm by internal lines or by buses. Hotel Domina Coral Bay El Sultan 5 * is located six kilometers from Ras Nazran Airport, but the noise of taking off planes does not bother travelers. An air-conditioned shuttle bus will take tourists in ten minutes. The resort center is about four kilometers from the hotel; the same amount is reached to the famous Naama Bay area.



What is here

The territory of Domina Coral Bay El Sultan 5 * is very large. Its area is almost one hundred square kilometers. Here, at different distances from the sea, among the paths, alleys, artsy gazebos and luxurious tropical trees, there are eight hotels that are part of this resort. Each of them has its own name: "Sultan", "Harem", "Oasis", "Elizir", "Aquamarine", "Prestige" and so on. There are also private villas. Everything is planted with lime, pomegranates, figs - a real oasis in the desert! Hotel "El Sultan" consists of five beautiful buildings. The hotel has parking, shopping malls with shops, as well as seven conference rooms for seminars. And so that tourists can move around the entire territory of the complex, mini-buses and cars run along five routes, like the Thai "tuk-tuk" (here they are called taf-tafami). They walk every quarter hour. On the territory of the "Sultan" ply number 4 and 5. Vacationers advise at the reception at the settlement to take a hotel card so as not to get lost. However, not all restaurants, beaches and pools of the hotels of this resort can be used by the guests of the Sultan. What exactly is included in the ticket, we will describe below.



Number of rooms

In the hotel Domina Coral Bay El Sultan 5 * three hundred seventeen rooms, ready to receive vacationers. They all correspond to the "five-star" status of the hotel. In addition to the basic equipment of the rooms - air conditioning, Philips TV with satellite channels, a minibar, a safe, a modern bathroom - the rooms have a living area, a bedroom, a sofa and a balcony. Most of the buildings are turned to the sea, and from some rooms you can enjoy views of the island of Tiran, and from others you can see parks and pools. On the balconies - chairs, a table and an ashtray. The furniture is wooden, comfortable beds, there are several spacious chests of drawers for things. The rooms are both spacious and comfortable. Soap and shampoos in the bathroom are replenished as they are used. The same goes for the minibar. Every day they put two one and a half liter bottles of water and four cans of carbonated drinks.

Entertainment and services

At the Domina Coral Bay El Sultan 5 * hotel (Egypt), a reception is open twenty-four hours a day. Each building has its own registration of guests. The hotel provides laundry and dry cleaning services, here you can exchange rubles for local pounds and return things to a special storage room. Sports enthusiasts are also not forgotten: tennis courts, ball courts, golf courses - you can find everything here. Even a special school has been opened, where they teach horse riding. However, the gym and fitness center are paid. There is a chic spa, but the prices there are high, according to the guests. Volleyball, diving and snorkelling can be enjoyed on the beach. A safe playground is equipped for children, there is a club for kids. In the evenings there is a disco, the gambling audience prefers the Aladdin casino, and there is also a real cabaret here - Smaliyas Club. Wi-Fi works both in rooms and on the beach, by the pool, in general, throughout. But it catches best near the halls of the buildings. Animators entertain guests in the afternoon near the pool or on the beach, and in the evenings the shows are held at the Mosaic cafe or at the special Angel venue. This arena is built so that tables with chairs ledges descend to the stage, and at the top there is a tarpaulin canopy, and not a roof, which saves from stuffiness. Very beautiful scenery, powerful music and lighting equipment. Especially praised the show "Elvis is alive", "Cleopatra", as well as "Michael Jackson."

Staff

Guests say that the Domina Coral Bay El Sultan 5 * hotel can boast of its service. And indeed, the guest service here is at the highest level. Perhaps, due to Italian management, certain notes of a chic European holiday are felt here. The hospitality provided by tourists by hotel staff is not very common in Egypt. Fulfill your every desire, on the day of departure they feed until the evening, and if you need to take a shower, then give the room keys. If you forget on the territory or lose an expensive thing, do not worry. Employees will give it to you safe and sound. The animation is amazing, active people have something to occupy themselves with. The team has both Russians and Italians. Sea and pool vacations

Spending time by the water is the main thing that tourists crave at the Domina Coral Bay El Sultan 5 * hotel. Photos of guests show us a huge beach - almost two kilometers long! - and three large pools. Of course, umbrellas, sunbeds, towels can be used here for free. Moreover, regardless of whether you are sunbathing at sea or at the pools. Sunbeds have special visors under which you can hide your head. In the surf there are special deck chairs. But there is one thing to keep in mind. Towels should be returned at the place where they were taken. The hulls that are closer to the water have their own beaches. Each of them boasts some kind of feature. Almost all hotels have pontoons, from which you can admire the coral. And on the beach of "Sultana" there is a special plot, cleared for children. On the main coast of the complex there is a special department for people who do not know how to swim, but want to swim in the sea. On the central pontoon is an indoor pool where diving students are trained. There are a lot of beautiful fish in the sea. Tourists complain that they often do not have the opportunity to visit the pools, because they spend hours watching the sea fauna with a mask. The beaches are open from nine in the morning until six in the evening.



Shopping

Hotel Domina Coral Bay El Sultan 5 * (Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh) is located near the resort's shopping center. Of course, the hotel itself also has shops, and even a supermarket, but tourists are still advised to leave the resort and make purchases in the Old Town or on Naama Bay. Here you can buy good cotton underwear, t-shirts and clothes. The textiles are high-quality, and prices are lower than Russian. Of the products, pay attention to hibiscus, yellow tea, coffee with spices, especially cardamom. A lot of high-quality leather products - both ‚Äúcopies‚Äù of European brands, and products of local manufacturers. Popular are high-quality fragrance oils with a lasting odor. Excursions

Hotel Domina Coral Bay El Sultan 5 * (Egypt) accepts not only people who will be able to sit in its territory. It is also an excellent starting point for excursions around the Sinai Peninsula, to Israel, to Jordan. From Sharma you can take an airplane to Cairo or Luxor, to the monastery of St. Catherine, to nature reserves, including Ras Mohammed, Tsvetnoy and White Canyons, Nabq, Abu Galum, allow yourself to climb Mount Moses, take a desert safari, go to a camel ride, a yacht ride, including on the island of Tiran. The hotel has many travel companies with which you can make all these fascinating trips.
