Dolphinarium in Tuapse: description, address, reviews

When resting in the Tuapse, Agoe or Nebug areas, be sure to take the children to the AquaMir Dolphinarium. They react very vividly to various tricks of marine mammals. And there are enough of them in the dolphinarium. This is a sea lioness, whale whales, walrus and, of course, dolphins.





Description of the dolphinarium in Tuapse

The park received its first visitors in 2003. It is open-type and is located near the water park "Dolphin" in the resort town of Nebug (15 km from Tuapse). The pool for performing artists has a circular shape. Places for spectators are located in the form of an amphitheater and covered with a canopy. Therefore, the performance is comfortable to watch in any weather. But the guests from the first near the "bad weather" is still provided: animals, jumping and spinning, create fountains of spray.

You can shoot video and take pictures in the dolphinarium (Tuapse), but only without a flash (it is very harmful to animals). And they are especially cared for here. Water in the pools (for living and for performance) is regularly updated. All mammals undergo routine veterinary examinations. They have high-quality nutrition, enriched with special vitamin complexes, sparing training and a lot of time to relax. That is why the artists are always in a good mood, and they perform with great pleasure.





For guests arriving by car, a guarded paid parking is organized. Dolphinarium address in Tuapse: Novorossiysk highway, 9-b.

How is the show going

Not the territory begins to start up an hour before the start of the water show. At the entrance, guests are greeted with life-size puppets that play with children. Inside the park there is a cozy cafe with a buffet and tables where you can have a bite to eat while waiting for the show to begin.

The first part of the performance consists of the numbers of all members of the troupe. Each shows his skills, easily completing the tasks of mentors. This part ends with complex acrobatic somersaults from a trio of dolphins and their solo numbers.

Having fun - learn

In the Tuapse Dolphinarium, throughout the course of the action, the audience is told about each animal participating in the performance. Guests will learn their habits, lifestyle and many other interesting facts about mammals. And later, a competition is held for children. The prize is a close acquaintance with marine artists.

In addition, dolphin therapy sessions are conducted here. They have a beneficial effect on children with autism. Specially designed programs enable the child to interact with the outside world and teach communication. Sessions are held by prior arrangement with the dolphinarium administration.

Pleasure cost

Entrance ticket to the dolphinarium in Tuapse will cost 600 rubles, except for children up to five years old inclusive (they watch the show for free) and some members of low-income families. A discount is provided for them, but only upon presentation of a document.





timetable

The entire holiday season shows are held twice a day: at 11.00 and 17.00. The artists' day off is Monday. With a decrease in the number of vacationers, each show is announced in advance.
