How to sew a sundress on the floor with your own hands. Simple and effective models

Talking about the benefits of sewing with your own hands is not necessary. They are obvious and known to all. Many ladies dream of a summer sundress that will meet all the desires and needs. Long outfits are in fashion now. How to sew a sundress on the floor with your own hands? There are several simple and effective models that each fashionista, not even very familiar with sewing, can sew.





Sew a sundress to the floor. Model No. 1

The simplest and most affordable model is one that is without extra frills. All that is needed is a suitable fabric, thread and machine. Do I need a pattern? Not necessary. A sundress on two straps can be sewn without a pattern. We take measurements of a breast. Divide exactly in half. Add an allowance of half a centimeter. And mark these measurements on the fabric. By the way, it must first be folded twice, inside out. Next, select the desired bodice length. That is exactly to the place where the skirt will go from. You need to measure from a line that is slightly above the chest (not from the shoulder). The straps will be sewn separately. Before you sew a sundress on the floor with your own hands, select a fabric. It should be quite dense so as not to make a lining under it, but not hot at the same time. Color can be absolutely any one you like. When the bodice is marked on the fabric, it can be cut and sewn by hand with any stitches for fitting. Allowances should be on all sides.

Should we mark the waist by the standards? If the sarafan is fitted, then yes. If free or with a flare from the lower line of the chest, then no. After the bodice we make a skirt for a sundress. How? Measure the length from the waist (wherever it is). From the end of the bodice, simply put. The sundress should end on the floor. On the fabric, note the full length and the desired width. Then we sew and try on. The last step is stitching the bodice and skirt, stitching the elastic into the bodice (so as not to use zippers or buttons), sewing on the straps. Wide or narrow - your business. Here's how to sew a sundress on the floor with your own hands in an hour. Well, or a half.

To sew a sundress with your own hands. Model No. 2, any

The second simple model differs from the first only in that it needs a pattern. Anyone you like. The pattern needs to be customized, measure everything on the fabric and sew. All decorative elements are made last. If the fastener is meant, then it is also sewn at the very end. If your chosen model has a zipper, then it would be better for her to be on the side and hidden. So putting on an outfit is many times easier. Any model you like can be redone so that it will be even better and more profitable.



What to wear?

Before you sew a sundress on the floor with your own hands, think about what you will wear it with. It is worth saying a decisive "no" to the heels. This is an established rule that must not be violated. In addition, girls of short stature are better to say goodbye to the dream of a long sundress. So growth will seem even smaller. But if there is a cut in a skirt on a sundress, you can put on heeled sandals. Fashion in this regard is very controversial. It is necessary to follow not her, but that which really emphasizes the dignity. You can be beautiful and spectacular not only from nature, but from the correct perception of yourself.
