Resort Location

Before you talk about hotels in Aqaba (Jordan), you need to talk about the location.

Jordan is an Arab state located in the Middle East. About 90% of the country's territory is occupied by deserts and semi-deserts. In the economic sphere, Jordan has big problems, it imports almost all vital goods, unfortunately, the state has a lot of debts, and economic growth leaves much to be desired. From the positive - only a large export of phosphates, tourism, an abundance of labor and the port of Aqaba.

This town is both the only seaport and resort. It is located on the northern coast of the Gulf of Aqaba of the Red Sea, near the city of Eilat (Israel). The tourist season is here year-round, the temperature even in winter does not drop below +22 degrees. The best time to travel is autumn, during this period the summer heat begins to go away, the temperature does not rise above +30 degrees. If you are going on a cultural holiday and plan to spend only a couple of days by the pool, then winter and the first month of spring are good for you. It is hotter here in the summer, the temperature can rise to +45 degrees. By the way, in the New Year time there are a lot of tourists here, most of the planes are full.
