User's manual

Before you set up your Sho-Me 520 radar detector and start using it, you need to install it correctly in the passenger compartment. To ensure the normal functioning of the gadget, it is necessary to observe several basic requirements:

  1. Install the detector in the middle of the car windshield as low as possible.
  2. Do not install the device behind the wipers or the upper sun stripe. The metal elements that are part of their design degrade the quality of signal reception, which increases the response of alerts and warnings.
  3. The radar detector should not be in direct contact with the windshield.
  4. The reception quality of radio signals may change under the influence of some synthetic coatings - for example, Intraclear and Electriclear.




Installation of a radar detector

A radar detector is installed in two ways, one of which involves the use of special suction cups to attach the gadget to the windshield of the car. The bumper and suction cups are inserted into the holes on the bracket, after which the device itself is fixed.

The second installation option is to mount the Sho-Me 520 on the dashboard using a mat with a sticky bottom surface. The installation site must be clean, even and degreased. The device must be installed carefully: the serial number must not be sealed, otherwise service centers may refuse to repair the Sho-Me 520 radar detector under warranty.

After installing the gadget, it is connected to the on-board network of the car. To do this, just insert the power cable into the cigarette lighter socket. Then the radar detector is turned on by scrolling the wheel clockwise. You should not change the sequence of actions: there is a possibility of failure of either the power cable or the gadget itself.

Instrument Testing

After the first launch, the radar detector passes a self-test cycle, which is accompanied by the operation of the LEDs on the display and the sound signals corresponding to all lasers and frequency ranges. This function is necessary to check the health of the display and speakers of the gadget.

According to the results of all tests and reviews of independent experts, the Sho-Me 520 radar detector was recognized as the most effective and best device in its price class. During the tests, the device showed excellent work, fixing all the stationary cameras and radars of the traffic police. Tracking devices are detected by the device at a distance of 600 to 1000 meters. The only drawback is the lack of indication when the silent mode is on.
