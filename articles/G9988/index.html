Nokia phones on Android: review, description, reviews. Nokia x2

Who has not heard of the legendary Finnish company Nokia? Phones from this manufacturer have long been considered cult. For a long time nothing was heard about this company, because it was bought by Bill Gates brainchild under the name "Microsoft". And this office is known for its ability to kill legendary brands. It so happened with Nokia. But it was not there! The legendary brand is back with new phones and smartphones. And now the company will focus on devices running on the Android mobile operating system. I must say that in the past phones on the Android from Nokia took place, but there were very few of them. And now there will be many of them. Consider the most interesting models of the past and look at the new flagship under the control of the "green robot".

Nokia X2. Main characteristics

This is a pretty old smartphone, which the company released as an experiment. I must say that there have been several such experiments in the history of the old Nokia. What is interesting about the flagship Nokia X2? First of all, it has compact dimensions (especially in comparison with modern "shovels"). The screen size of this smartphone is only 4.3 inches.





Nothing at all compared to modern diagonals. The device runs on the Android operating system version 4.4 from Google. On board the gadget is a dual-core processor from Qualcomm with a clock frequency of 1.4 gigahertz. The RAM of the device is equal to a gigabyte. There is no need to say that this "old man" will not pull normal games. But he was not designed for this in his best years. And at that time there were almost no particularly demanding games. Therefore, this unit was positioned as a device for work and some innocent entertainment.

Opportunities

The amount of memory available to the user is 4 gigabytes. Compared to modern drives, this is a pretty ridiculous figure. However, Nokia X2 supports micro SD cards up to 32 gigabytes. Good help. It is better to remain silent about the support of modern communication standards. You cannot use LTE in this smartphone. Only 3G. But implemented full support for two SIM cards. And no hybrid slots. They were able to do it before! As for the camera, there is a 5-megapixel module with four-time digital zoom.





There are no delights like manual tuning. But there is autofocus, flash and the ability to record video in HD at a speed of 30 frames per second. That's just quality limping on both legs. But let's not forget that this is an "ancient" apparatus. Now a little about the battery. It uses a removable Li-Pol battery with a capacity of 1800 mAh. I must say that for a smartphone of this level this is quite normal. The device can "live" 6 hours when playing video at maximum screen brightness and 13 hours in talk mode. And if you do not touch the smartphone at all, then it will live 23 days on a single charge. Pretty good result.

Nokia 6

This exhibit is the new Nokia phone on Android. It appeared after the legendary brand announced its return. No doubt, the gadget turned out to be much more interesting than X2. However, not everything is going smoothly with him either. The "six" looks worthy: solid materials of the case, high-quality assembly. But inside, a morally obsolete processor is installed, which was used by the Chinese in Xiaomi a year ago - Snapdragon 430.





At the same time, as many as 4 gigabytes of RAM were installed in the gadget. What for? All the same, this processor will not let the RAM open up completely. The novelty is running the Android operating system version 7. The most interesting is that the manufacturer decided to abandon the proprietary shell, but in vain. That would be more interesting. It is not known that the engineers of the company with the OS have made up their minds, but running Android on Nokia is somehow strange: sometimes it thinks (with 4 gigabytes of RAM), sometimes it completely ignores the actions of the owner.

Features Nokia 6

As for the camera, a 16-megapixel photomodule of medium speed with autofocus and flash is installed here. It does not shine with outstanding characteristics and shoots as a camera of a device of the middle price segment would shoot. It does not reach the flagship level. Nevertheless, the photos are decent. Especially during daylight hours. The amount of internal memory is 64 gigabytes. However, there is support for memory cards.





The new product uses a 5.5-inch IPS screen. Screen Resolution - Full HD. The colors are vibrant and juicy. Fingerprint scanner a little upset. In order to recognize the print, he needs as much as five seconds. This is a lot. But after updating the firmware everything worked as it should. It was probably a software bug. The device is equipped with a 3000 mAh battery. But, given the technical characteristics, we can safely say that this gadget will live only one day in normal use. These are the phones on the "Android" from the "Nokia" of our days.
