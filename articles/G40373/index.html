Hotel "Imperial Park Hotel and SPA": how to get there

The institution in question is located in the suburbs in the village of Rogozinino. To get here by car, set the coordinates 55.529404, 37.196854 in the navigator. By public transport, you can get here as follows:

  • from the metro station "South-West" or "Toparevo" by bus number 304;
  • from the metro station "Teply Stan" by bus number 522 or 526.
