Belarusian sanatoriums. "Narochansky coast" (Minsk region). Reviews and photos

In Belarus there are a variety of health resorts based on their medical profile. “Narochansky coast” is one of the best institutions of the country of this kind, very popular not only among its inhabitants, but also among citizens of neighboring states. The resort is located in the Minsk region, in a coniferous forest, on the banks of a beautiful lake with crystal clear water. The territory of the sanatorium is part of the Narochansky National Park. The combination of unique natural factors and the beauty of the area annually attract a huge number of residents of Belarus and the former republics of the USSR who want to improve their health.

Location Features

Naroch is a resort village built in 1964 in the north-west of Belarus, 140 km from Minsk and 265 km from Vitebsk. In the immediate vicinity of it is an old village of the same name. The village itself is stretched along the lake for several kilometers. There are shops, cafes, there is a small market. The sanatorium "Narochanskiy Bereg" is one of the largest and most visited in this area. It belongs to ChSKUP Belprofsoyuzkurort FPB.





General information

On the territory of this resort and medical institution there are 7 buildings:

  1. Three separate medical buildings that host vacationers.

  2. Main building. There is a dining room, treatment rooms and an administration office.

  3. Hydrotherapy.

  4. Sauna.

  5. Rental equipment.

In total, at the same time the sanatorium “Narochansky Bereg” (Belarus) can accommodate about 430 people. Five meals a day are provided for vacationers: individual, according to diets. The menu in the dining room is custom-made, with elements of a buffet. Children are accepted for treatment and accommodation from the age of three.

Institution Profile

In the sanatorium "Narochansky Bereg" is definitely worth coming to people suffering from diseases:

  • Gastrointestinal tract;

  • respiratory organs;

  • nervous system;

  • of cardio-vascular system.

Medical complex

In the sanatorium “Narochansky Bereg” there is an opportunity to visit aroma and phytotherapy rooms, undergo such procedures as inhalation, irrigation, massage. Dental, physical therapy and physiotherapy rooms also work in the main building. If necessary, employees of the sanatorium will conduct laboratory tests of the tests, advise the vacationer regarding his illness, and prescribe the necessary procedures. Guests of the institution have the opportunity to take therapeutic baths, including dry carbon dioxide. Most vacationers for medicinal purposes drink mineral water “Narochanskaya”, similar in composition to the famous “Truskavetska”.

Infrastructure

In the sanatorium "Narochansky coast" you can not only improve your health, but also have a good rest. On the territory of the institution there are children's play, volleyball courts, tennis courts. Also, guests of the sanatorium have the opportunity to visit the gym, cinema, bar, sauna. There is also a private laundry on site. Unfortunately, there is no pool in the sanatorium "Narochansky Bereg". However, water lovers can visit the well-equipped beach. The pool is available on the territory of the rest house "Belaya Rus" of the Ministry of Internal Affairs. Not too high prices are what distinguish almost all Belarusian sanatoriums. "Narochansky Bereg" is located right next to the Rest House of the Ministry of Internal Affairs. Therefore, far away to go his guest in the pool do not have to. Pay for the session will need only about 150 rubles.





Convenience of living

Sanatorium "Narochansky Bereg" offers its guests several categories of rooms. You can purchase a ticket to a single one-room, 2-seater two-room, 2-seater one-room. All rooms are equipped with modern comfortable furniture, TVs and refrigerators. If desired, guests of the institution can use the electric kettle and a set of dishes belonging to the sanatorium. Each room has a shower and toilet.

Beach and Lake Reviews

There are very good sanatoriums in Belarus. “Narochansky coast” is considered one of the best. Regular guests of this institution visit it, including because it is located in a very beautiful area. Visitors to the sanatorium have wonderful reviews about Lake Naroch . This natural pond is one of the most beautiful and cleanest in Belarus.

On the beach of the sanatorium, which stretches along its shore, there are changing cabins, shady canopies and umbrellas. The safety of holidaymakers is provided by the water rescue service. Those who love waterfowl will be especially delighted with the beach and the lake. There are just a huge number of swans and ducks swimming here. The beach also has a boat station.





Paid services

You can relax in this sanatorium just by paying for a ticket. However, many guests of the sanatorium order paid services here. These include:

  • manual massage;

  • phototherapy;

  • hydro and herbal medicine;

  • instrumental diagnostics;

  • access to the sauna and spa capsules.

Sanatorium "Narochansky coast": reviews

The opinion of this institution among those who had already visited it was simply excellent. Former guests of the sanatorium respond well both to its infrastructure and medical facilities, as well as to the level of service. They especially praise the dining room of this complex and the possibility of buying excursions to interesting places.

As already mentioned, visitors to the sanatorium like its location. And not only for the beauty of the area. In pretty convenient places, almost all Belarusian sanatoriums have been built. “Narochansky shore” is superior in this respect to most of the country's similar institutions. His guests can purchase everything they need without any problems in the shops of the village of Naroch or in the local market. Getting to the sanatorium is very easy. To do this, from the railway station in Minsk you need to go to the Central bus station, located nearby, and take a minibus or intercity bus.





Sanatorium "Narochansky coast": prices

Its guests speak well of this institution and because of the not too high cost of accommodation and treatment. The price for a single room, for example, is 1650 p. per day, double - 1320 (per person - 1980) p. Two-room suite for two people will cost 1720 p. Superior - in 2060 p.

sights

Excursions to the sanatorium "Narochansky Bereg" are also offered to guests, but for an additional fee. So, you can look at:

  • Church in the village of Budslau.
  • Memorial complex Khatyn.
  • Mosar church.
  • St. Sophia Cathedral in Polotsk.

A bus sightseeing tour “On the Narochansky Ring” is also offered. Guests of the sanatorium can get acquainted with the natural features of the region, with the historical past, with the resort area.

Also in the sanatorium you can buy a tour of the significant places of the city of Minsk. Naroch is a resort village, which, unfortunately, has no attractions. Here the guests of the sanatorium can see only a rather interesting monument to the snail, installed on the square near the shops.





Holidays in Belarus (the sanatoriums here are really excellent) can be truly unforgettable. Places in this country are actually the most beautiful. The surroundings of the Narochansky Bereg complex are also among them. All those who wish to receive high-quality treatment and admire the Belarusian nature should definitely visit this resort.
