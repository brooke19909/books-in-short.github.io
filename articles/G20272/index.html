How many numbers are there on a Sberbank card? Sberbank card number. Sberbank card - what do the numbers mean

Turning to Sberbank of Russia for financial services, the client will certainly come across a proposal to issue a bank plastic card. And having received it in your hands and carefully studying it, the inquisitive will want to know how many numbers are on the Sberbank card and what they mean.



Today it is unrealistic to imagine the functioning of the world banking system without cards. Plastic cards are an integral attribute of the everyday life of almost every citizen.

International payment systems

International payment systems Visa, MasterCard, JCB, American Express, Diners Club operate through a cashless payment system around the world. The popularity of the system is due to its simplicity, convenience and reliability. Simply put, many customers keep their savings in bank accounts tied to plastic cards. If necessary, a person does not use cash to pay, but a card from which the amount demanded for payment is debited. Sberbank cooperates with two payment systems - MasterCard and Visa.



Any payment system is an international association of banks. There is a certain set of rules for the functioning of the system, and all financial institutions are required to obey them. Being a member of the payment system, the bank performs two functions. First: produces plastic cards. Second: conducts their maintenance. As a rule, a bank combines both of these functions.

Use of bank cards

Regardless of how many numbers are on the Sberbank card, its availability enables its client to have access to various financial services, such as:

  • payment for goods and services by bank transfer;
  • cash withdrawal;
  • obtaining various information about the status and maintenance of the account;
  • transferring money to another card or account, and so on.

It is important that the holder uses it, but at the same time the card itself remains the property of the bank.

Cashless payments are beneficial for all participants, as banks can continue to use the funds of customers who remain in the account. The client always has access to a larger amount than he could have with him in cash, moreover, the money is protected from theft. Payees do not need to hire collectors, guards, and so on to move and save funds, since the payment amounts are immediately credited to the account.



Thus, the payment system guarantees settlement operations between all participants in the system. The tool of the payment system was a bank plastic card.

What information is on the map

A bank card of Sberbank is an important tool of payment systems. Therefore, in order to standardize, plastic cards issued by the institution have standardized parameters.

The carrier of financial information is any Sberbank card. What the numbers on it and other data mean is clearly regulated. With their help there is an opportunity:

  • identify the industry and the issuing bank, number, name of its holder;
  • identify bank requirements related to card servicing;
  • authenticate the card and its holder even remotely.

The card must contain the following details: number, term and service code, special data that the bank can use for authentication.

Plastic Card Standards



Sberbank of Russia issues several types of bank plastic cards related to Visa Classic and MasterCard Standard payment systems . Regardless of the fact that the cards differ from each other in functional purpose, all of them without fail meet international standards in the field of plastic cards. Their characteristics are set by the ISO / IEC 7810 standard.

How many numbers are there on a Sberbank card

Sberbank is one of the largest banks in Russia. According to the official web resource of the institution, the number of its clients exceeded 800 thousand people. And many have at least one plastic card issued by the bank. And although all the cards are similar in appearance, each of them has its own different number from the others. Consider the number of digits on the card of Sberbank and what they mean.

The first figure defines the industry of the issuer. In the case of the banking sector, this may be the number 4, 5 or 6.

The next five digits are the identifier of the issuer. In particular, they can be used to determine which payment system the card belongs to:

  • 4 determines whether the card belongs to the Visa system;
  • in the range from 51 to 55 - MasterCard;
  • numbers 34 and 37 - American Express;
  • Maestro starts at 6.


The remaining digits are the customer account identification number . Sometimes another number is added to the card, the last one that performs the function of a check. To determine it, a special Lun algorithm is used.

The number of digits in the number of different cards can be 13 and from 16 to 19. Old-style cards have 13 digits. Most plastic cards issued today have a 16-digit number. The number of 18 digits on the Sberbank card is found on Maestro plastic cards. Cards with 19-digit numbers were most likely issued as supplementary to the primary.

Security

Many bank card holders are sure that by keeping their PIN code secret, they can protect themselves from fraudsters. Alas, this is not so. Naturally, the PIN code should not be reported to outsiders. However, this is not enough. In order to pay for goods on the Internet, it is enough to know how many numbers are on the Sberbank card, what are these numbers, the card expiration date, its security code. With this information, criminals can easily pay with other people's money. Therefore, any holder of a plastic bank card should understand that it is better to reliably store all the information that is printed on it.
