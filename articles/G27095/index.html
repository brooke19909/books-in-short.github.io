The main catch of the bank

Most of the negative reviews about Tinkoff Bank and All Airlines are left by those owners who have a misconception about the mechanism of operation of such cards. Credit cards were created so that a person could spend small amounts (10-30 thousand rubles) during the grace period, while his own money is in interest-bearing deposits. In the event that you are unable to pay off the debt on time, such a scheme is unlikely to be beneficial. According to the terms of the agreement concluded when issuing a Tinkoff All Airlines credit card, if the debt is not repaid within 55 days, the interest rate on it will be 30%. A regular bank loan goes at 11-16% per annum. If you overdue the minimum payment, your debt will increase by another 590 rubles, if this is the first delay, and by 1-2% per annum for the subsequent ones. Together with credit insurance, the interest rate in total can reach 43% per annum. For this amount, if the minimum payment is not paid, a penalty of the debt amount of 19% per annum is added. Thus, it turns out that you need to pay not 30, but all 70%! These are extremely unfavorable conditions that can make you big debtors. If the cardholder made a purchase in the amount of 20-30 thousand rubles at a time, and then during the year makes 2-3 thousand rubles a month, then after 12 months his debt does not disappear. That is why, not calculating their strength, people with surprise in a year discover that they owe the bank a round sum.
