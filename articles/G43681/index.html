Featured Hotels in Greece (Corfu)

Corfu - the name of one of the Greek islands, washed by the Ionian Sea. He is well known to connoisseurs of elite relaxation. Corfu is an island with a mild climate and paradise nature.

For a long time it was under the rule of the Venetian rulers, so in many buildings exquisite Italian style is visible. Hotels in Greece (Corfu in particular), surrounded by ancient and modern architectural creations, offer tourists a plunge into the atmosphere of comfort, luxury and coziness. Orchards, olive groves, unique beauty views, clean and warm sea - all this attracts lovers of a romantic getaway from around the world.

Climate Corfu

In the summer, air temperature ranges from +24 to +33 degrees, water - +21, +24. The island is dominated by a subtropical Mediterranean climate. In summer, precipitation is quite small. But, despite the sultry heat, a feeling of heaviness and stuffiness is not observed here. The refreshing breeze from the sea brings a pleasant coolness to the island. Since October, the temperature begins to drop markedly. In winter, the thermometer does not rise above +18. This period accounts for the largest number of cages. From early May to October, hotels in Greece (Corfu, Paxos, Ithaki, Lefkada) are crowded with tourists - the holiday season begins. In the period from March to May, the island noticeably changes, exotic flowers begin to bloom here, plants bloom.

Three Star Hotels

Hotels in Greece (Corfu) of category 3 * are very popular among tourists due to the high level of service, homely atmosphere and relaxing atmosphere. They are distinguished by developed infrastructure, convenient location and low cost of living.

Located in a fishing village, which allows tourists to fully enjoy a relaxing holiday among the few crowded sea beaches and magnificent nature.
