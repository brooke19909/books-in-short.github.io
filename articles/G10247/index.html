Mobile compressor for home use and some industries

Currently, in stores you can find a large number of different power tools, among which you can find drills, rotary hammers, circular saws, etc. All of them use electric current for their work, and at the same time consume it in accordance with the size of their power, which is not very economical and at the same time not very reliable and inefficient. That is why most enterprises use a mobile compressor or a whole line with compressed air.



The fact is that almost the entire pneumatic tool has a greater productivity and service life than a power tool, while its price is much lower. It is also worth noting that the pneumatic tool is recognized as one of the safest, because it can not be shocked or ignited.

It is also worth noting the fact that a portable compressor can power such tools, the analogues of which are simply not available using electric energy. Therefore, such devices are very popular among painters, sandblasters and road repair crews. Also, the mobile air compressor is widely used by rescue services and those structures that use a tool capable of creating high pressure (pneumatic pincers, pneumatic jacks, pneumatic scissors ) for their activities.



It is worth noting that a modern portable compressor is usually installed on various platforms that have their wheels and a device for attaching to a car. This applies to more powerful compressors, which create a lot of pressure. Other portable compressors designed for domestic use are equipped with wheels and a small handle for easy carrying. Therefore, one person can operate such a device.

An important point to consider when acquiring a portable compressor is that most of them run on diesel fuel. As a result, exhaust gases are released, which completely makes it impossible to use it indoors. However, this does not prohibit installing the compressor on the street, and connecting devices with a long hose.



A small portable compressor can provide working pressure for one, and in some cases two pneumatic tools operating at full power. Moreover, if you recalculate the power that is used when working with a pneumatic tool, and compare it with that used in power tools, and compare the result with the cost of electricity and diesel fuel, the result will show that use a pneumatic tool with a compressor much more economical than a power tool. You can also add to this the safety of a pneumatic tool and the simplicity of its repair and maintenance.

As a result, we conclude that the purchase of a mobile compressor is a profitable and practical purchase.
