Hotel Resort By The Sea 3 * (Phuket, Thailand): overview, rooms and reviews

The island of Phuket is simply very popular among tourists from all over the world. The weather at this resort is almost perfect for relaxing all year round. Even during the rainy season, Phuket is quite comfortable. Of course, tourist infrastructure is well developed on this island. There are a huge number of comfortable hotels offering vacationers rooms for every taste and budget. Inexpensive and very good apartments, guests of the island can rent, for example, in the hotel R esort By The Sea 3 *.

Where is the hotel situated

This hotel is located in a secluded quiet location on Cape Panwa, next to Khao-Khrad Beach. The full name of the hotel is By The Sea Phuket Beach Resort. The distance to Phuket from the hotel is approximately 10 km. A transfer minibus belonging to the hotel periodically goes to the city. Book a trip to the hotel in advance. A minibus is poisoned from the hotel twice a day - at 10:00 and 13:30.

From the airport to the complex, tourists travel about one hour. Vacationers who book rooms in this hotel get to the place, most often by bus provided by the tour operator who sold the ticket, or by taxi. The trip takes so much time because the hotel is located outside the city, and the bus along the way carries tourists also to other complexes.





General description of the hotel

The Resort By The Sea 3 * complex consists of three buildings - one four-story and two 2-story. All buildings have a pleasant architecture and a convenient layout. The reconstruction of the hotel was last held in 2005. From all sides the hotel is surrounded by a jungle . In the area the hotel is located really very secluded. Next to it in the jungle is only one more hotel and private villas. For the convenience of guests, plastic chairs are installed on the terrace in the courtyard of the hotel . In the courtyard of the hotel is a large pool.

When checking into this hotel, a deposit of $ 100 is charged to tourists. Hotels in Thailand are not classified by star. The category they are conditionally "assigned" by the travel agencies themselves for the convenience of customers. On some sites of such companies, the Resort By The Sea hotel is classified as three-star, on others - four-star. In any case, the service at this hotel is quite good.

Smoking in all hotel rooms is strictly prohibited. Pets are not allowed in the hotel.





Number of rooms

Hotel guests Dessole Sea Beach Resort 4 * offers a total of 36 comfortable bungalow and semi-bungalow type rooms. Each room has:

  • TV;

  • telephone;

  • safe.

For a fee, residents can use the minibar. Each room has a shower room. A hairdryer and towels are provided for tourists here. Bathrobes and slippers are also provided. In addition to the watering can, the bathrooms of the hotel rooms have bathtubs. Guests can relax in the evenings on the spacious balcony or terrace, equipped with outdoor furniture. For your convenience, the rooms include free Wi-Fi.

The kitchen in the rooms at the hotel has a stove for cooking. Room service utensils are available upon request. Every day, maids leave a set for making tea or coffee in the kitchenette. This service at the hotel is completely free. Of course, there is an electric kettle in the hotel rooms. Guests can boil water for themselves at any time.

Of course, all rooms rented out in the hotel have individual air conditioning. Their presence in the hot climate of Thailand is, of course, an undoubted advantage of any hotel.

Hotel Infrastructure

On the territory of the hotel, tourists can use:

Vacationers can wash the dirty things in a laundry. True, you will have to pay for this service separately. This hotel also has a convenient fitness center. Dining at the hotel

Rooms at Resort By The Sea 3 * hotel are most often booked by Russian tourists with breakfast. About the food in this hotel on the network there are also mostly only good reviews. Breakfast at the hotel can be served omelettes, eggs, ham, sausages, fried potatoes, noodles, etc. Also, pineapples and melons are offered to tourists. Of the drinks in the dining room, tea, coffee, milk and juices are most often found.

In addition to the buffet, guests at Sea Breeze Resort can enjoy light meals at the bar. Judging by the reviews of vacationers, quite tasty shrimps in batter and shakes are served here. Of course, in the hotel bar you can order various kinds of drinks - beer, cocktails, etc.

Experienced tourists advise beginners to have lunch and dinner at the Salaloy Seafood Cafe, located across the street from the hotel and to the left. Breakfast at this establishment costs about 300-400 baht for two adults. If desired, full board can be ordered at the hotel itself. Lunches and dinners served in the hotel dining room, as well as breakfast, tourists usually praise.

Road to the beach

Hotel Resort By The Sea 3 * is located on a small hill. But its guests do not cause any particular inconvenience to the beach. The hotel is located almost on the coast and to get from its territory to the sea you need to spend no more than a couple of minutes.
