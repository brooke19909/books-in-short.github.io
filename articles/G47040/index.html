How to make a bow from a ribbon for decorating a gift?

The ability to make a bow from a ribbon can be useful not only when decorating a hairstyle, but also in many other cases. For example, you can make a beautiful accessory on a hat while relaxing at sea. You can decorate them with a New Year card or bright packaging. Which material to prefer?

The material for our accessory can be selected depending on your personal preferences. Ribbons can be pink, turquoise, yellow, red. You can tie a bow of satin ribbon, satin, silk. The main thing is that he does not hesitate and holds his shape well. Consider also how you want to convey the mood of your gift: romantic, cheerful, joyful. Also choose a bow type suitable for the style.





How to make a bow from a ribbon in the style of a classic?

1 option

A classic bow is often used to decorate gift wraps intended for official presentations or gifts, for example, for the anniversary of the boss. Accordingly, it is better to choose standard colors, for example, red or dark blue. So, to cope with the task, you need to fold the ribbon 4 times. Thus, four segments are obtained. Next, we connect the first and second segments with each other, in the same way the third and fourth. Now we cross the resulting turns. We get a loop at the bottom, put one of the bends into it and fix the knot.

Option 2

There is a second way to design a classic bow. To make a cute accessory you need two ribbons. First, take the first tape, turn it into a loop, crossing the ends. In the center we fix with a thread. We fix the middle of the loop at the intersection of the ends of the tape and fix it. This is just the foundation. Now we take the second ribbon of a smaller size, wrap the bow in the center, fixing it at the back. Beautiful decoration for the gift is ready.





How to make a bow from a baroque ribbon?

This accessory will turn out very magnificent and effective. Suitable for decorating holiday gifts, for example, for a wedding. To get the product, you need to roll the ribbon in several rings, depending on how magnificent you plan to get a bow. Next, crush the circle in the middle. You get a strip with two folds. On them you need to carefully cut the corners of the tape, leaving it whole. Now again, we unbend our structure into the original circle, and then crush in the middle, but now the cut corners should be in the center. We fix the bow in the middle with a ribbon, carefully straighten all the loops, creating a three-dimensional and original model.

How to make a bow from a ribbon for a romantic gift?

A bow for the most delicate and exquisite natures is suitable for very personal holidays, for example, for a birthday. It is done very simply. We take the tape, folding it into several turns, stacked on top of each other as the size decreases.

In the center, the bow is fastened with a thin ribbon of the same color.

So, a beautiful accessory can become an unusual gift decoration for any occasion. The main thing is to do it with a soul, having a little patience.
