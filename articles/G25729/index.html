Samet Island - Paradise Lost

Unlike Samui, Phuket and Phi Phi, the Samet island in Thailand is known to few Russians. But he has long been popular with European tourists. It is a three to four hour drive from Bangkok. Then a ferry service awaits you. In fact, this is one of Thailand's National Parks - Khao Laem Ya Mu. Therefore, every vacationer must pay an additional amount for entry. But this does not really matter, because being on the island itself is a pleasure. Not only foreign tourists seek sunbathing here, but also the residents of the capital of the country themselves like to spend their weekends here.



If you look at Samet Island on a map of Thailand, you can see that it is located in the Gulf of Thailand. It is very close to the mainland: some ten kilometers, and you are there. This arrangement contributes to the fact that the rainy season is weakly expressed here, as in Pattaya or Hua Hin. Therefore, you can come here virtually all year round. Although in winter and early spring there are, of course, more people. And they come here with only one purpose - the sea, sand and the underwater world. The most popular and popular beaches, like Diamond, are located in the east of the island. They are wide, sandy and very well-maintained.

The coast of Auwong Dian is located in a perfectly round bay. There are many nightclubs, bars and discos with foam parties. But if you came to Samet island in search of relaxation, then at your service are the southern beaches. They are not so big, but picturesque and secluded. If you live in an exclusive boutique palace, and not in a modest "three rubles", most likely you will relax on the west bank. The local beach of Aoprao has to appease and meditate. And from there you can go on a variety of sea excursions.



Samet Island and the small archipelago near it, as already mentioned, are part of the National Park. Therefore, here you can find a lot of all kinds of exotic animals. This, for example, "hiccuping" geckos, huge lizards of two meters in size and monkeys. However, caution must be exercised with the latter. They are like people: some individuals can distract your attention, while others will pull something. However, the joy of contact with wildlife will be complete if you familiarize yourself with security measures. Samet Island (reviews of tourists confirm this) is called a paradise for divers and snorkellers. Of course, the underwater world of Thailand is not as bright as in Egypt, but the flocks of bright yellow banana fish that eat fruit right at your fingertips is an unforgettable sight.

Travel agencies also offer routes through the local jungle paths and excursions to several Buddhist temples. You can see the unusual shape of the stones, which have acquired such a bizarre appearance due to the wind and sea waves. They are located on Ao Puds Beach. And if you still liked diving, and you want to continue to explore the depths of the sea, go to smaller islands. There, at a great depth, you will see colorful corals, tens of thousands of gray-haired sea urchins with huge needles, giant shells that open and close valves and many other wonders. Samet Island is a real lost paradise! The only difference is that on vacation you can regain it.
