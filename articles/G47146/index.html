HTC Desire 326G Dual Sim. Overview and Features

The company HTC released in 2015 the budget device 326G. Like most smartphones, the device has an unbalanced price-performance ratio. So what is this device capable of?

Design





The device is made in the usual style for the company. It is not difficult to identify a representative of a well-known brand on the phone. The smartphone is made entirely of plastic, which is to be expected from the inexpensive HTC Desire 326G Dual Sim. The review of the case attracts attention with a glossy shine. This definitely adds brightness to the device, but makes the phone slippery.

The devices of the company differ in assembly and quality. However, in cheap devices there are often gaps and creaks, and the HTC Desire 326G Dual Sim device was no exception. An overview of the assembly notes minor creaks in the case, especially with pressure on the rear panel. Although the reliability of the device is not in doubt. With bumps and falls, the device copes without any noticeable consequences.

On the side of the device is a volume and power button. The front part was assigned to the speakers, camera, screen, sensors, indicator and logo. The control elements of the device are touch-sensitive and are located at the bottom of the display. The headphone jack was placed on the end on top, and the microphone and USB connector on the bottom. On the rear glossy panel is the HTC logo, camera and flash.

Model 326G is not very overall and weighs only 146 grams. The thickness of the device, as much as 9.7 mm, is a bit confusing, but this is a forgivable flaw. Weight hints that the user can work with the device with one hand. However, the slippery surface prevents this, the phone constantly tries to slip out.

Display





The company has not installed the best screen on the HTC Desire 326G Dual Sim. The review of the display causes only bewilderment and misunderstanding. The model uses a diagonal corresponding to the dimensions, 4.5 inches. Resolution is also acceptable, namely 854 by 480 pixels. These characteristics correspond to the price.

The main drawback is the HTC Desire 326G Dual Sim TFT matrix. The overview of the corners corresponds to the devices of 2012-1013. Even with a slight tilt, the picture is distorted. The problem will be working with the device in the sun. Constant flare and loss of brightness, this is what the user will be forced to face. The 326G screen is definitely not the best solution of the company, especially in 2015.

Hardware





Installed "filling" is quite consistent with the price of HTC Desire 326G Dual Sim White. The review of "iron" will satisfy the undemanding user. The manufacturer installed a gigabyte of RAM in the smartphone HTC Desire 326G Dual Sim. A review of the memory hints that the device cannot solve resource-intensive tasks. However, the phone will provide fast work of programs and switching between them.
