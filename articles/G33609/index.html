Popular hotels in Kotlas, or Where to stay on the way to Santa Claus

Kotlas hotels are located in different areas of the city. Therefore, visitors can always choose a hotel that will meet their wishes by location. But this small town in the Arkhangelsk region is visited by thousands of tourists annually. And more often - in the winter season. No wonder Kotlas is popularly called the "Gateway to the North." It is known for its Turovets graveyard, as well as a holy spring. But not only because of these attractions, they come to this city. This is the nearest railway station to Veliky Ustyug, the birthplace of Santa Claus.

A variety of Kotlas hotels allow you to book an option with the most appropriate ratio of quality, service and value. There are luxury hotels with excellent service - for those who like comfort. You can also settle in an economical hotel, which will save the tourist budget.

Gostiny Dvorik is a good hotel for modest money





Inexpensive hotel for living in Kotlas - "Gostiny Dvor". The cost of a room here starts from 1200 rubles. In addition to low prices, this hotel attracts with its cosiness and small size.

The rooms have TV, minibar. At reception, you can always buy drinks and sandwiches for a bite to eat.

Sapphire Hotel (Kotlas) - everything for relaxation, seminars, banquets

Sapphire is not just a hotel, it is a whole hotel and restaurant complex. Despite the fact that it is located away from large highways and noisy streets of the city, the center is only 10 minutes away.
