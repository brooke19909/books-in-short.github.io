Rest in the "Alpine Village" in Omsk: photos and reviews

Clean air, birch grove and silence ... Isn't this the dream of any city dweller? Such a vacation is provided by the "Alpine Village" in Omsk. This hotel courtyard is open all year round, therefore guests are surrounded by the warmth and care of the staff in any season.

Where is and how to get to the "Alpine Village" in Omsk?

The recreation center is located in the village of Oktyabrskoye, on the street. Lesnoy, 10. This area belongs to the Gorky district. You can get here by personal transport. To do this, from Omsk you need to move towards the street. 36-North. Then continue along the Pushkin tract. After about 22 km there will be a turn onto Muromtsevo-Sedelnikovo.





After another 7 km it will be necessary to turn onto Petrovka-Nikonovka. Then about 29 km will have to move straight. There will be a left turn. A pointer to the base is set along the path - move along it to the destination.

On weekdays, there are regular buses from Omsk to the village of Oktyabrskoye. Travel time takes about two hours. Departure at lunch time.

Description of the suburban complex

The territory of the "Alpine village" in Omsk is large. It houses several buildings and cottages for living, as well as buildings in which baths and restaurants are equipped.





The territory is landscaped as much as possible. Walking paths are laid along it. Many flowers have been planted that delight guests throughout the warm season. Near the buildings there are wooden arbors in which barbecue facilities are equipped.

Throughout the territory there are a variety of wooden figures. There are also many small decorative houses where children play with pleasure. On New Year's days, everything is decorated with colorful lights and it seems that you get into a fairy tale.

Accommodations

For guests of the "Alpine Village" in Omsk are offered to accommodate rooms of different levels of comfort. They are located in different buildings. It is possible to rent cottages by a large company.

In the main building 3 floors. The first is the Alpine restaurant and a billiards room. On the second - 14 numbers. Of these, 11 are one-room for a family of 3-4 people and 3 are two-room. On the third floor there are 15 rooms. Of these, 11 are one-room and 4 are two-room.





All are decorated in a classic style with many elements made of wood. Each room has a TV with satellite channels, a small refrigerator, necessary utensils, a kettle, furniture, a shower and a toilet. One room has two single beds, and the two-room has a folding sofa and a large double sleeping area.

The new building in 2007 was completely renovated. It has 8 rooms with a capacity for 2-3 people in a classic style. The rooms are equipped with TV, fridge, sofa or beds, there is a shower and toilet.

On the ground floor there is a conference room and a lounge. They are equipped with the necessary equipment and furniture for meetings and business negotiations.

Traditional houses

On the territory of the recreation center "Alpine Village" in Omsk there are two cottages that are built in the Russian traditional style. From a distance, it seems that in front is a tower from a fairy tale.

On the ground floor there is a lounge and a VIP sauna with a pool. The second and third placed comfortable rooms. Each has a double bed and a sofa bed. The rooms have TV, refrigerator, tables and chairs, balconies. The rooms are equipped with shower and toilet.

The Alpine House has 2 floors. It is built of chopped coniferous logs. The rooms can accommodate from 2 to 9 people. The rooms have all the necessary equipment and modern furniture.

On the territory near the cottages are equipped covered gazebos with barbecue facilities.

Baths and saunas

Lovers of the steam room will definitely be able to relax here. On the territory of the Alpine Village camp in Omsk there is a traditional Russian bathhouse and a Finnish sauna. During the construction, all technologies were followed, so the guests will get a real pleasure from the procedures.

The Russian bath is located in a wooden hut, which consists of a dressing room and a steam room. A heater is installed here, pouring water on it, you can adjust the amount of steam needed.

Special essential oils are added to this water, which make soaring even more pleasant and healthier.

On the ground floor in the administrative building is a Finnish sauna. Here the air has low humidity, so the steam room has the property of dry heat. Here and the heater is a source of steam and heat. It must also be watered with the addition of essential oils.





Entertainment

In the main building there is a hall for playing billiards. Nearby is the restaurant "Alpine" for the convenience of vacationers, so during the rest you can order drinks and snacks. You can choose between Russian billiards or an American pool.

At any time of the year you can play football in the complex. The field is always prepared and cleared before the arrival of guests. In winter, a slide works, which is arranged in the form of a spiral track. Its length is one hundred and fifty meters, and vacationers drive right up to the stairs along which an ascent to it is conducted. Thus, no time is wasted on the way to the hill at the exit from the attraction.

In winter, a ski run is laid. Its length is 7.5 km and 21 ascent and descent. This route is considered one of the best in the Omsk region. An ice rink is also being arranged, which is well lit in the evening.





On the territory of b / o "Alpine village" there is a base for renting equipment for outdoor activities. Here you can rent bicycles, skis, sleds and hockey sticks, skates, table tennis tables, fishing equipment, steam accessories.

On the ground floor of the administrative building there is a gym with modern equipment. Here you can carry out cardio training, pump muscles on weight training equipment.

"Alpine Village" in Omsk (Gorky District): reviews

There are many comments about the work of this recreation center. They are the opposite. Many tourists complain about the very poor soundproofing in the rooms. There are also many negative reviews about the work of administrators. Guests indicate that they often arrange small parties in the rooms, so the rest of the guests do not have to sleep because of the noise.

There are practically no negative reviews about the work of the dining room. Tourists claim that they feed here deliciously and relatively for little money. They cook well in a restaurant, but the cost of food is an order of magnitude higher.





Tourists complain that often in a local bathhouse and sauna it is not possible to steam up, as the premises of other companies are constantly occupied by visitors. The pluses include cleanliness, good attitude towards guests, goodwill. Guests are pleased that there is a small pool in the summer. Everyone who has ever been to the Alpine Village at least once notes the beauty and majesty of the surrounding nature.
