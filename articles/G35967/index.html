How to get there

How to get to the Richmond Hotel in Karlovy Vary? The main international airport is in the Czech capital, Prague. From there, to the famous city with healing waters - 120 kilometers.

Karlovy Vary also has its own airport, 5 kilometers from the hotel. But from Russia only flights of Czech Airlines fly there.

Other companies will have to travel through Prague. But it is much cheaper than flying directly to Karlovy Vary. It is better to get from Prague by direct bus from the Florence bus station. You can also come here by train from Moscow.

Bus number 8 runs from the airport to Karlovy Vary. Then you need to change the route - and after a couple of stops you will find yourself at the gates of the hotel.

It is very easy to get to this hotel on foot. If you walk along the bank of the Teply river against the current, you will go straight to its gate.

Where lodging guests

The Hotel Richmond in Karlovy Vary is a large hotel. It has 122 rooms. 94 of them are double, standard. 20 rooms are for one guest. There are 8 apartments.
