Rating of sanatoriums of Crimea. The best sanatoriums of Crimea and reviews about them

Rest in Crimea is a great opportunity to improve your health. The best sanatoriums of Crimea have a long history. Just 200 years ago, scientists laid the foundation for spa treatment here. After that, sanatoriums were built, whose comfortable buildings fit perfectly into the landscape.



It has everything for a comfortable stay - comfortable rooms, qualified specialists, a mild marine climate, parks. Today we will consider the best sanatoriums of Crimea, the rating of which is presented below.

10th place: "Miskhor"

Starting to consider the best sanatoriums of Crimea, the rating of which is presented in this article, it is worth highlighting Miskhor. This resort is included in a number of famous health resorts on the southern coast of Crimea. The resort is located at the foot of Ai-Petri, on the coast with a total length of about 7 km. The sanatorium takes its name from the village, which has not survived to the present moment, but left its name to a beautiful park, resort and beach.

This is the hottest point in the southern part of the Crimean peninsula. Such climate features make the Miskhor health resort a great place to relax. It treats respiratory and cardiovascular diseases under the supervision of experienced professionals.

Reading reviews of Mishor, you understand that everyone who has been here once wants to return here again. People say that the amazing climate here goes well with the professionalism of doctors.

9th place: resort "Sacropol"

The rating of the sanatoriums of Crimea continues "Sacropol". He recently opened the doors for visitors after reconstruction. During the reconstruction period, the rooms were updated, and a new water and mud bath was built according to the European standard for one and a half thousand visits per day. The treatment center is located on the shores of Lake Saki, three kilometers from the sea. Sacropol is ready to receive guests all year round for effective treatment and a pleasant stay.



The sanatorium has a modern high-tech medical base, allows for the treatment of the nervous system, inflammatory diseases of the genital area in women, diseases of the musculoskeletal system, urological (male infertility and prostatitis), metabolic and gastrointestinal disorders. In addition to therapeutic ones, SPA programs are offered here that are aimed at rejuvenation, as well as at improving the human body as a whole.

Reviews about the sanatorium "Sacropol" say that this is a great place for the treatment and prevention of various diseases, in which you can combine relaxation with healing.
