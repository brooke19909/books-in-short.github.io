How to rent a house in Moscow

Of course, the ideal option would be to housing in which your friends or relatives have already stayed.

Removal without intermediaries is the best option. Most experienced tenants understand that if you have an intermediary, you need to pay a lump sum of three times the sum for the housing (security deposit for the last month, current month and the agreed amount to the agent).

If you decide to rent an apartment, not a hostel, then it is better to use a long-forgotten way - ads. In the area where you are going to live, post paper announcements and communicate only with the owners. Inexpensive apartments in Moscow (secondary housing) can be found not only in the periphery. The main thing is to check all the documents.

Read the documents carefully (lease agreement). This applies to both rental apartments and hotels, hostels. The contract must necessarily indicate the owner, the deadline for the rental, cost. You can supplement the documents yourself with the necessary items (in case of damage to property or other circumstances).
