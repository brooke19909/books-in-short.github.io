"Watercolor" hot spring, Turinsk: description, reviews, photos

Recreation center "Watercolor" with hot thermal springs is located in the Turin city district of the Sverdlovsk region, in the village of Water Source . Hot thermal water is a real gift of nature. Since the 19th century, such methods of treatment with thermal waters have been familiar, such as baths, fonts and washing.





The range of diseases for which this procedure is recommended is very extensive: from inflammation and malaise to malignant tumors. However, in order to start such therapy should consult a doctor.

The hot springs

The Aquarelle hot spring complex (Turinsk) has three outdoor pools with warm springs. The water in them comes from a mineral well, the waters of which are provided with iodine, manganese, orthoboric acid, and for this reason has a natural peculiar aroma and color. Open ponds are cascaded and have temperatures up to 38 degrees. One of them, 35 cm deep, is specialized for children. The therapeutic and health outcome is due to the high content of iron and other useful elements.





A visit to the thermal keys and the use of such a systematic treatment is positive for well-being, beauty and youthfulness. The unique structure of thermal waters without impurities is mineral salts, biologically active components (iodine, bromine, iron) increase skin tone and it is for these reasons that thermal water is often part of various cosmetics.

In addition to the open ones, the Aquarelle hot spring complex in Turinsk has on its territory an indoor fresh water pool, the temperature of which reaches 30 degrees and an indoor thermal pool with a temperature of 35-38 degrees.

Bath complex

The bath complex combines a Finnish sauna, a hamam, a unique salt sauna with Himalayan salt, a BIO sauna with hot steam and a massage mountain stream for walking barefoot. The spa area has cedar baths and offers a variety of treatments in a cedar barrel. The bathhouses themselves are small, but very cozy and comfortable.
