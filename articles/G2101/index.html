Electronic cigarette: harm or benefit?

Around the same time, when real persecution began on smokers around the world, the wise Chinese came up with a device that most accurately simulates the smoking process. The electronic cigarette provided an opportunity for smokers to consume nicotine in an almost familiar way, while preserving the maximum sensations and associated habits inherent in smoking - all that neither chewing gums, nor plasters, nor tablets could give. The news of the miraculous invention instantly spread throughout the smoking world and thousands of people rushed to buy the desired novelty. At the same time, these products are almost never advertised anywhere except on the Internet, and even selling them in a number of countries has not yet been allowed, and the doctors' comments about electronic cigarettes are very restrained and uncertain. Experts say neither yes nor no, and consumers are still confused: so what is an electronic cigarette - harm or benefit?
