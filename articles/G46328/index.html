Sea vacation. Base "Ocean"

The beginning of the holiday season is always full of worries. But these experiences are pleasant, because they are associated with the upcoming vacation. To do this, you need to choose the best place to recharge your batteries for the whole coming year. Of course, you can go to overseas resorts, but in today's conditions it is quite expensive. But to soak up the gentle sun of domestic resorts is quite affordable for everyone with an average income. Recreation facilities located on the coast of the Black and Azov Seas offer excellent conditions for spending vacations.

Base "Ocean"





Especially popular with tourists is the Sychavka recreation area. Base "Ocean" is located there. Just 500 meters away is the wonderful resort of Koblevo. To Odessa - 40 km, to Nikolaev - 80 km. Crowds of tourists annually go to these places on vacation. Base "Ocean" will provide its guests with comfortable conditions for a wonderful pastime on the Black Sea.

Description of conditions

The base occupies a large equipped area. Many ornamental shrubs and fragrant flower beds have been planted here. The capacity of the base is up to 300 people. Vacationers can expect free parking. A lot of entertainment, children's and adult rides, comfortable living conditions annually attract crowds of people who come here on vacation. Base "Ocean" consists of several cottages and wooden houses, which are 2, 3 and 4-bed rooms. Each room is equipped with beds, bedside tables, a mirror and a refrigerator. Facilities are located on the street next to the buildings. Each room has a private entrance.

Children under 5 years old who do not require extra bed are accommodated for free. Payment for children 5-10 years old - 50% of the basic cost.

There is a shared kitchen at the base. For those who do not want to eat in a cafe located on the base, all conditions are provided to cook their own meals. You can also use the services of the local dining room, offering a wide variety of delicious dishes. Water at the base is supplied around the clock. After the beach you can take a summer shower.





One of the most popular Black Sea resorts is Koblevo. "Ocean" - a recreation center located in Koblevo, has its own beach. The season begins in May and ends in October.

Also invites you to visit the base "Ocean", located in with. Andreevka.

Trinity Bay





These places are truly considered reserved. Many birds live here, unique plants grow. And all the blame for the amazing climatic conditions due to the Tsushima current. There are several nature reserves, including "Cedar Pad" and "Barsovy". The only marine reserve in Russia is also located in these places. Numerous natural monuments, warm clear sea, mushroom forests - all this attracts the human mind. Rest in these places can be a great holiday, especially if you stay at the recreation center "Ocean".

Description





Before booking a room, a tourist needs to find out what conditions he is traveling to. The recreation center "Ocean" (Andreevka) occupies 8 hectares of territory, which is directly adjacent to the coastal zone of the cleanest sea. A wide sandy beach, majestic cliffs, a forest strip - all this is available to those who came to these places on vacation. Base "Ocean" even has its own water park, in addition to many other attractions.

You can stay at the base in buildings, a hotel or houses, which are located almost on the beach itself. Recreation at the base is available year round. A special building with 4-bed rooms is designed for this. There is also a hotel with 48 rooms.

Entertainment





Seafood in a local restaurant is considered one of the best. There are also own wells with fabulously delicious drinking water. Exciting excursions will leave in your memory a lot of unforgettable impressions. A supermarket is open around the clock in the base. There is even a salon where you can play various computer games.

There is a water park and pools on the coast. The beach is fully equipped for a comfortable pastime. Lovers of outdoor activities right on the beach can play volleyball, mini-soccer, ride horses in unique places. You can go on an excursion to the Marine Reserve. Water activities are also in abundance. Skiing, scooters, catamarans, windsurfing is available until sunset. You can rent equipment for diving.

30 meters from the coast there are places to go fishing. In these parts there are Larga sea seals, which are listed in the Red Book.

Base "Ocean" takes tourists all year round. An ice rink is open in winter. You can go fishing or hunting in the winter.

The recreation center "Ocean" in Kirillovka





On the Azov coast, you can not only relax well, but also improve your health. In the Zaporizhzhya region there is a village of Kirillovka. Base "Ocean", located on the Fedotova Spit - a great place for recreation. These places are famous for their beaches and warm sea.

Accommodation at the base is available both to wealthy people and people with low incomes. The base has deluxe rooms, two-room rooms in separate houses and cottages. There are also economy rooms.

The territory of the base is fully landscaped. There is a playground for children with swings, bars and cafes. The beach is equipped with sun loungers and parasols. There are gazebos with barbecue facilities.

It is believed that for families with children it is better to choose the Azov coast. The sea is warmer and rich in useful elements. A sandy beach with lots of children's entertainment and low cost of living - all this can be found in Krylovka.
