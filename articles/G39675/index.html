Hotel Jomtien Plaza Residence 3 * (Thailand, Pattaya): overview, description and reviews

Where to go to relax in the middle of winter? In winter, more than ever, I want to be in a paradise, enjoy the sound of the surf, the warm and gentle sea, get a beautiful even tan. There is no better place than Thailand. The season lasts from November to April - at this time the tours are most popular. Do not mistakenly believe that at other times of the year the resort is inaccessible to vacationers, everything is extremely simple: from April to October the rainy season continues here. But these are not the rains that many of you imagine - they are short-lived, but significantly reduce the cost of tours. The most famous Thai resort is considered to be Pattaya. This is a large city, offering tourists a lot of entertainment and at the same time the opportunity to spend time alone with nature, enjoy the picturesque views and local beauties. Of course, in many respects the comfort on vacation depends on the right hotel. Many tourists are inattentive to this item, believing that there is no point in wasting time choosing a hotel where they will spend only one night. In the case of Asian options, one should be extremely careful and attentive, because most of them do not fit their description. We suggest paying attention to the option quite popular among Russian tourists - Jomtien Plaza Residence. Based on the reviews of real tourists and vacationers, we will try to give him an objective assessment.





Features of Asian hotels

We want to warn all tourists who want to go to Pattaya: most of the hotels here simply do not fit their description. When choosing the appropriate option, you should build on the experience of real tourists, your relatives and friends. If you are used to relaxing in the luxury hotels of Turkey and Egypt, in Thailand you have nothing to do: here you will not find such a service and infrastructure. The staff is extremely polite and attentive, but the hotels do not have an adjacent territory, even the pools here are on the roof of the buildings. However, some regard this as a plus.

All the beaches here are public, that is, municipal. This is because you simply cannot find a hotel on the coast. At best, you have to cross a busy highway: the coastline is separated from the city by a wide road.

Rooms

Regardless of the chosen category, the room will not only have a bedroom, but also a living room, a kitchenette, a full dining room, and a balcony with amazing views of the coast. Just imagine: you can enjoy the sunrise and sunset with a glass of aromatic drink. A wide coastline, palm trees, pristine tropical nature - and all this Pattaya. Air conditioning is responsible for the comfort in the rooms - in conditions of hot tropics it is an absolute necessity. Each room has a TV with cable satellite channels, including Russian. Subtleties of settlement

All rooms are equipped with fire alarms and an electronic door lock, which only enhances the safety of your stay at Jomtien Plaza Residence. Reviews of tourists recommend that when checking into a room, first of all, pay attention to the condition of furniture and appliances. Entertainment

From entertainment, Jomtien Plaza Residence 3 * (Pattaya) can offer its guests an outdoor swimming pool located on the roof of the building. And, of course, on the beach, everyone will be able to rent water sports equipment and sports equipment.

The hotel is located in the center of Jomtien, surrounded on all sides by shopping centers, shops, nearby (only 10-15 minutes walk) is the most famous night entertainment street, as well as the popular night market.





A special entertainment, the opportunity to get acquainted with the culture, history of the resort and the country as a whole will be a rich excursion program. Be sure to visit the river Kwai, ride elephants - there will be plenty of emotions and delight. You will rejoice like a little child - and this is definitely not forgotten. Special attention deserves the legendary transvestite show - a local attraction. Its cost is 550-600 baht, and the sight is really worth the money spent.

In the hotel restaurant, all guests can have breakfast, and in the lobby bar you can enjoy the charming atmosphere and the taste of wine or cocktail. You can have lunch and dinner at local restaurants, cafes, since there are plenty of them here.
