Retractable car radio with navigator: overview, models, manufacturers and reviews

Retractable car radios are versatile and multifunctional devices that perform several functions at once. Firstly, they reproduce quality sound. Secondly, they provide an opportunity to watch movies and video files while driving. Thirdly, many models are equipped with high-quality GPS navigation. By installing one device, you get a complete navigation and multimedia system, which will take up very little space on the dashboard.

Design Features





A retractable display makes any car radio more functional. It is not cheap compared to conventional models, but it makes it possible to enjoy the full range of its options. The popular 1 din car radio with a sliding screen has many features:

  • it can be put on cars of any makes and models;
  • the screen has optimal sizes and resolution, so you can view the video on it;
  • the presence of a special tuner allows you to watch TV shows.

The retractable screen is easy to operate and has a convenient menu, and if you connect an additional module, you can use the navigation system. Many modern models have all the outputs for connecting parking radars, rear-view cameras, which improves the controllability of the car and increases the safety of its operation.

How to choose?





Retractable car radios are modern devices that delight drivers with unique technical capabilities and functionality. When buying such equipment should proceed from the following parameters:

  • the minimum display diagonal (ideally, the resolution should be 800 x 400 pixels, the minimum viewing angle is 120 degrees);
  • range of reproduced frequencies, linear power of the playback channel and the ability to additionally connect a subwoofer and speakers;
  • the ability to read such popular recording formats as CD, DVD, Flash;
  • connectivity to wireless communications, which allows you to synchronize the device with any mobile means.

All modern models have an intuitive interface that conveys picture quality to the maximum and reproduces sound well. As for the price range, in the line of leading brands you can find both cheap devices (up to 8000-9000 rubles) and premium models, the cost of which reaches 20 000 rubles.

China production models





One-screen car radios made in China are among the cheapest. Such marking indicates the versatility of devices that can be installed and successfully operated in any car. One of the popular inexpensive models is the EONON G1311, which is additionally equipped with a GPS navigator. This device has the following technical features:

  • playback of a wide range of formats, including through a USB connection;
  • the presence of a navigator with a built-in map of Russia;
  • control via touch screen or remote control;
  • Bluetooth support from a smartphone;
  • the ability to receive calls while driving through the built-in microphone.

There is also an input for connecting a rear view camera and a TV tuner. Prices for retractable car radios of the Chinese brand EONON start at 13,000 rubles. By the way, according to reviews, this technique is in no way inferior to more expensive counterparts, although many believe that high-quality and reliable equipment is not being created in China.

Hyundai H-CCR4701M





If you constantly have to be on the road, you need to somehow diversify your time, especially if there are a lot of traffic jams on the way. For this, the modern and functional multimedia station Hyundai H-CCR4701M is ideally suited. It has a 7-inch motorized TFT display and a TV tuner. Hyundai brand retractable car radios attract attention:

  • support and playback of various audio and video files of any extensions;
  • the presence of an HDMI input, which allows you to connect a smartphone, media player and any other equipment with a similar output to the device;
  • the ability to connect any Android phone with MHL support.

There are a lot of good reviews about this model, which focuses on the large display and sensitive sensor, with which you can work even with gloves on. The tilt of the screen can be adjusted. The only drawback that Hyundai retractable screen car radios have is not the easiest installation. In addition, the device protrudes from the panel by 2-3 cm, however, this drawback is not so important and significant.

Prology MDM 175T

Under the Prology brand, the real navigation multimedia systems are created.
