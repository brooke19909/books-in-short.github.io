About Venice in the Sands ...

Where did such a romantic name come from? There is something in common in the architecture created by the Egyptians with Venice, although the climate here is tropical, deserted - there is practically no rain in the summer, but there is a lot of sand. Therefore, the resort, the construction of which, at the request of the Egyptian billionaire Sami Saviris, many venerable architects had a hand in, is called "Venice in the Sands." Many Arab and European billionaires built their villas here. This place has long been chosen by artists, photographers and people of other creative professions.

Some of the resort's hotels are located on islets that connect canals and bridges. Shuttle ships cruise between these islands. Water communication from the hotel Panorama Bungalow Resort El Gouna 4 * (Egypt, El Gouna) is systematic, constant and regular. Minibuses are transported to the infrastructure of tourists on the islets themselves. Since the entire resort is the property of Orascom Development, owned by Sami Saviris, all regular transport links for tourists are free. To the delight of guests throughout the resort, the Dinner Round service is available. Its meaning is that if during dinner the tourist is not in his hotel, then he can dine in the restaurant of any other El Gouna hotel closest to him.

Numerous passenger motor scooters equipped with cabins serve as an alternative to regular flights. The fare to any corner of El Gouna is 5 pounds (35 rubles).

There are two marinas, a brewery. Wine is also bottled in El Gouna. We recommend that you take a walk in the Abutik Marina port - this is the place where luxury yachts moor. There is something to see here, sitting down at a table in a restaurant on the promenade with a cup of coffee. On the island of Zeytuna there is a free aquarium, a golf club, a go-kart track, an El Arena disco, restaurants.

About the hotel complex

It has been working since 2004. The hotel was built on the shore of a picturesque lagoon on the first line, from it to the water's edge of the beach - less than two hundred meters. Not far from the hotel is the center of El Gouna - Down Town. For eleven years of tourist service, the hotel has earned a good and stable reputation. Among the guests, the hotel complex is famous as an updated first-generation hotel, which provides cost-effective leisure services. This is a complex consisting of the main one-story building, where the reception is located exclusively, as well as buildings for guests: five- and three-story buildings, one-story comfortable bungalows. Overhaul was carried out in 2012, in subsequent years, individual buildings and cases were selectively repaired.

Beaches

Of course, readers of the article are interested in how comfortable the Panorama Bungalow Resort El Gouna 4 * hotel offers its guests. There are two of them, and both are free.

The nearest Lagoon Beach, sandy, equipped with sun loungers and umbrellas, with a smooth entry into the water, is ideal for families with children. By the way, beach towels are issued here to vacationers, too, for free.

The transport facilities of El Gouna allow guests of the Panorama Bungalow Resort El Gouna to swim among coral and fish. They leave by shuttle boat (which runs every hour) to the beach, located on the island of Zeytuna. The shuttle saves there in five minutes. Using the beach on the island of Zeytuna, as well as delivery to it, are free. On the island beach, guests of the hotel complex use sun loungers, umbrellas, towels. A buffet with local beer, soft drinks, and pastries, operating under the all-inclusive system, has been functioning here since 9 am.

Guests of the Panorama Bungalow Resort El Gouna 4 * hotel have breakfast from 7-00 to 10-00, lunch from 13-00 to 17-00 and have dinner from 19-00 to 22-00.

The menu of the hotel meets dishes of Oriental, Egyptian, European, Italian cuisine. In a word, the cuisine is international.

Resort nightlife

Where in the resort of El Gouna can a tourist have fun after a day on the beach? Experienced holidaymakers recommend spending the evening on the central island of this artificial archipelago - on Kafra. Its architecture is attractive, stylized as traditional Egyptian. Low neat buildings with domed arches and courtyards are built along wide straight alleys.

Tourists willingly visit the Italian restaurant and artistic cafe, which are popular in Kafra. Buy souvenirs in the shopping arcade Khayamia. Bargaining, of course, is necessary. However, the prices in the local shops (there are no shops in El Gouna) are not the most affordable, because the majority of vacationers in the resort are from Western Europe.

Kafra boasts resort life around the clock. El Gouna's nightlife takes place at local nightclubs, discos and bars. Guests of the Panorama Bungalow Resort El Gouna 4 * hotel in their reviews mention the Cin-Cin bar, Arena disco.

Pools, sports infrastructure

The hotel complex has two outdoor pools. One is for adults, the other is heated for children. To the delight of swimmers, two water slides work. There is a playground near the children's pool. Moms and dads bring the kids to the mini club, while being present there themselves. As such, there are no animators, but there is the necessary infrastructure of a mini-club. The control over their children is carried out directly by mothers or fathers - one of the parents of each child must be present. These are the rules of the hotel mini-club.



For the smallest parents can hire a nanny, but this service is paid.

Extensive sports and therapeutic infrastructure of the hotel Panorama Bungalow Resort El Gouna. Panorama Bungalow Resort El Gouna 4 * offers its guests both paid and free health promotion services. Excursions

From the travel agency the hotel offers various excursions in the land of Egypt. Decent price competition is provided by city travel agencies. The latter offer the same services, but twenty to thirty percent cheaper. Excursions to Cairo, Luxor, to the favorite place of Egyptian diving divers - to the beach of Abu Dabab are in the greatest demand among the guests of the hotel complex we are considering. Car enthusiasts will enjoy jeep and ATV safaris.

Tourists on excursions to Cairo have the opportunity to see the famous pyramids of Giza and the Sphinx, as well as the Cairo Museum, which has the largest collection of exhibits of ancient Egyptian culture in the world.

Luxor (in ancient times - Thebes), as the ancient capital of Egypt, is famous for the Sphinx Avenue, the temple, the Valley of the kings, the Valley of the queens, funerary temples, necropolises.

However, guests of Panorama Bungalow Resort El Gouna enjoy sea excursions with no less enthusiasm. Responses of tourists testify to the popularity of diving excursions, visits to the ‚ÄúDolphin House‚Äù, boat trips with a transparent bottom.
