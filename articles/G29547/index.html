Minuses

They are everywhere. Louis Creta Princess 4 * in Greece is no exception. This hotel has few disadvantages, but it would be unfair not to mention them.

Most tourists are upset by poor sound insulation. Out of season, when the hotel is half empty, this flaw does not manifest itself. But at the height of summer, everything that happens in the neighboring rooms is audible.

There are also not very well-located apartments. Rooms overlooking the sea are quieter. On the opposite side are generators, and some right under the apartment. People living in these rooms say that vibration even with closed windows is palpable. But you can always ask to change the apartment. The administration does not refuse such requests, and no surcharges are also required.

Another nuance, perceived by many as a minus, is the presence of a huge number of small children. But as already mentioned, this is the concept of the hotel. Therefore, people who dream of a quiet, peaceful and relaxing vacation, it is better not to go here. There can be no question of any romance in restaurants and near pools when the kids are making noise around.
