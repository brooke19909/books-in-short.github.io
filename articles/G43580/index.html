We will tell you how to make a house for Barbie with your own hands: the stage of creating parts and assembling the structure

In order to make a roomy and durable home for dolls, you need the following materials and tools:

  • piece of MDF or thick plywood;
  • jigsaw;
  • nails
  • hammer;
  • primer;
  • paint for facades;
  • breadboard knife;
  • adhesive tape (regular and double-sided);
  • PVA glue;
  • adhesive pencil;
  • trimming fabrics (for room decoration);
  • pieces of wallpaper, linoleum;
  • plasticine;
  • various decorative elements and accessories;
  • polymer clay.

The first stage of work on the implementation of the "doll house for Barbie" crafts is the manufacture of all the necessary fragments that will serve as walls, roof and floor.

On the prepared piece of MDF or thick plywood, all the details are marked out as shown in this figure (1 inch equals 2.54 cm). After which they are neatly cut. Then the resulting fragments are assembled into a single design. The assembly plan of the house is as follows. The back, front and side walls (A, E, D, respectively) are attached to the upper part of the base (part B). For fastening, nails and a hammer are used.
Tip: if ugly cracks appear at the joints of the panels, you can close them with a primer. Windows in the walls are cut with a jigsaw. After bonding all the fragments, the house will take on a realistic shape, it will have a roof, and floor, and walls.

Product Decoration Stage

After the frame of the house is ready, you should proceed to the primer and painting of the facades and roof. Do not forget to paint all the details from the outside, as well as the ceilings from the inside. After staining, you can leave the house for Barbie for a while alone. After drying the paint should proceed to the design of "residential premises".

How to make a home for Barbie with your own hands similar to a real home?
