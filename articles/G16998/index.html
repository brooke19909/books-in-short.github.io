The principle of operation of a vacuum cleaner with a water filter

The main fundamental difference between a vacuum cleaner with an aquafilter is that all the air drawn into the device enters the container with water, which is the main filter element. The liquid impregnates particles of dirt and dust. Large fractions settle to the bottom, small ones dissolve in water. The efficiency of cleaning with the help of such devices is much higher than that of classical products equipped with ordinary bags (disposable or reusable) for collecting garbage.

Currently, it is the products with aquafilters in the ratings of the best vacuum cleaners that are confidently occupying a leading position.

On a note! The use of such a highly effective device not only allows you to quickly and efficiently perform cleaning, but also additionally moisten the air in the room. Which has a beneficial effect on health (especially on hot summer days).

Varieties

According to the principle of technological design features, all water filter elements are divided into three types:

  • Hookah In such devices, air is passed through a container filled with water along a complex path.
  • Injection. In such products, the contaminated air is first mixed with water under pressure using a special injector, and then passes through the box with liquid. That is, two-stage aquafiltration is carried out.
  • Separator A special centrifuge is installed in the water tank. Air with particles of dirt and dust spins along with water to very high revolutions. This allows you to "break" even air bubbles containing any microparticles, and completely clean them from the air returned to the room.

The main disadvantage of separator vacuum cleaners with an aquafilter is their high cost. Therefore, they are not so in demand as adaptations of the first two types.

Specifications

Vacuum cleaners with aquafilters are characterized by:

  • suction power - from 200 to 700 W;
  • dust collector volume - from 1.2 to 5.8 liters;
  • the degree of air purification (which depends on the type of water filter): for hookahs - 92-95%, for injection ones - 99-99.9%, for separator ones - up to 99.999%;
  • power consumption - from 650 to 2400 W;
  • noise level during operation - from 66 to 87 dB.

The convenience of everyday use and subsequent storage is also affected by indicators such as weight and overall dimensions.

Advantages and disadvantages of products with a water filter

The main advantage of cleaning devices equipped with an aquafilter (in comparison with traditional analogues having dust bags) is a high degree of purification of the air returned to the room. This is due to the ability of water to "capture" the smallest particles of dust, up to tenths of a micron.

Another indisputable advantage of such vacuum cleaners is the stable suction power throughout the entire cleaning process. Whereas in classic models, this indicator invariably drops as the dust collector fills with dust.

An important plus is also the simplicity and ease of use. Before cleaning, pour clean water into the container, and at the end of the process, simply drain it (along with particles of garbage and dust).

On a note! By adding a small amount of flavoring liquid to the dust container, you will not only clean the room, but also get rid of unpleasant odors.

Of the shortcomings, users (especially fragile representatives of the weaker sex) note a greater weight of products (compared with classic counterparts). This is not surprising, because the mass of the device itself increases the volume of water poured into it.
