3 star hotels in Protaras, Paphos

In comfortable 3 * hotels with elegant interiors in Cyprus, tourists who are constrained in means, families with children, young people who rely only on budget vacations, and those who need peace, privacy and peace prefer to stay.

Inexpensive hotels, despite the fact that the range of services in them is small, provide unpretentious tourists a decent vacation on the island of Cyprus. 3 star hotels with a modest number of rooms maintain the service at a high European level. Most hotel complexes were reconstructed, equipped with functional furniture, plumbing and household appliances with a modern design.

Infrastructure

On the island of Cyprus, 3 star hotels are mostly equipped with a swimming pool and a guarded parking lot. They have hairdressers, exchange offices, shops, conference rooms and business centers. The hotels are equipped with fitness centers and SPA salons.

Beaches

Although the "starryness" of the hotels is small, some of them are located on the 1st coastline. The proximity to the sea coast, framed by beaches, affects the price of living on the island of Cyprus. 3 star hotels with their own beach increase the cost of living. 5 * hotels, located at a decent distance from the sea, can turn out to be much cheaper than modest "three rubles".

If 3 star hotel complexes do not have their own beach, vacationers are recommended the nearest hotels with access to the sea. For a fee you can relax on any beach.

Nutrition

In most hotels, only breakfast, called continental, is included in the stay. Guests are supported by traditional sandwiches (bread, sausage, cheese), juices, boiled eggs, omelettes, yogurts, granola, pastries with tea or coffee.

Of course, there are 3 star hotels on the island of Cyprus offering half board and full board. All-inclusive hotel complexes are extremely rare. If desired, guests book a table in the restaurant, paying for the ordered dishes, or have a snack in the bar.

However, not all hotels have their own restaurants. For treshki, the maintenance of restaurants is not a prerequisite. Restaurants in Cyprus are usually equipped with the best 3 star hotels. When choosing a hotel, tourists prefer hotels with restaurants, although accommodation is a little more expensive. Understanding this fact, the owners strive to equip a cafe or restaurant on the territory of the complex.

Protaras: 3 * hotels





Comfortable 3 star hotels in Cyprus (Protaras) have relaxation areas with pools, sun loungers and umbrellas, tennis courts, squash and volleyball fields. Guests relax in the superbly equipped gyms, saunas, and relax in the hot tubs. Water fun is available to them.

3 * hotels are equipped with restaurants, lounge bars and beach cafes, where breakfast is served (buffet). The elegantly furnished apartments come with a minibar, a balcony and a TV. For the children on their territory, playgrounds and children's pools have been built. For guests arrange animation and dance shows, folklore holidays.

Paphos: 3 * hotels

Cyprus, Paphos, 3 star hotels - all these are the components of a wonderful holiday. 3 * hotels in the elite resort of Paphos are adjacent to restaurants, taverns, souvenir shops. Those hotels located near the central quarter are surrounded by boutiques, supermarkets, nightclubs and entertainment venues.





All Paphos hotels are designed for British and discerning Europeans who prefer pools to the beach. Therefore, even in the territory of three-star complexes several pools were built. Paphos has many hotel complexes bordering the 1st coastline in contact with the sandy beaches of the Mediterranean.

Hotels with original architecture are equipped with gyms, sports grounds, SPA salons, and children's rooms. They offer car rental and excursions. Families with children try to occupy apartments with mini-kitchens, where there are microwave ovens, and living rooms.

The cost of living in hotels in Cyprus 3 *





The cost of living in three-star hotels in Cyprus varies greatly. There are 3 star hotels in Cyprus, the prices for accommodation in which are higher than in individual "fives". Prices in hotel complexes of the Larnaca resort are 15-25 percent lower than in Ayia Napa or Paphos, where 3 * hotels are adjacent to the coastline bordered by sand braids.

In general, Cypriot 3 * hotels are pleased with their low cost, for which they provide a traditional range of services that allow you to unwind with a minimum of funds.
