Choose the name of a real estate agency

So, you are a businessman, your area is transactions in the housing market. But what is the name of a real estate agency if there is not too much creativity? Or simply nothing comes to mind?

In this case, there are simple algorithms for generating a suitable name. Consider the main ones.

The magic of foreign pronunciation

Not everyone is fluent in a foreign language. Having picked up a sonorous foreign language name, the owner of almost any business can add mystery and ambiguity to his own sign. The phrase should be short, uncomplicated, consist of 1-3 words, more or less understandable to the average consumer, and be pleasant to hear.

At the subconscious level, he should associatively induce a recognition motive among potential customers. For the first time people going to an agency should feel that this place is already familiar to them.





It is important to ensure that the name of the real estate agency, even a foreign one, does not turn out to be a random, meaningless verbal set. There will always be a meticulous client who is not too lazy to analyze the true meaning of a word or phrase. And the case may end in serious failure.

Thoughts on a note

What other advice can be given to a newly-made businessman racking his brains over what to call a real estate agency?

Here are some useful tricks. Do not use words that are not specific to your niche in the name. For example, if you focus on a more or less affluent audience, the slogan "affordable housing" only harm yourself.

The opposite situation - ordinary buyers sign "Elite Real Estate" is likely to scare.
