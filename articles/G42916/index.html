If an iPhone falls into the water, the first thing to do is to turn it off. The plan for further action is as follows:

  • After releasing the back cover of the device from the fixing elements, slide this housing part up and lift it up.
  • Carefully unscrew the two mounting bolts of the frame that secures the battery connector.
