A beautiful wreath of flowers on his head with his own hands

Once upon a time, fresh flowers were the only jewelry available for girls. Of them weaved wreaths that were worn during holidays and ceremonies. Wreath weaving in those days was a needlework school for young girls. Subsequently, this skill gave them the opportunity to more successfully master the science of knitting, sewing, weaving, embroidery and weaving.



By the end of the last century, only one of the ancient traditions remained - a rite with floral wreaths for the feast of Ivan Kupala. But sometimes a well-forgotten old comes back into our life. So it happened with the wreaths. Today, more and more women and girls prefer to decorate their hair with flowers, which makes them more feminine and cute. A wreath of flowers on her head is gradually gaining a leading place in wedding fashion. Jewelry from famous florists is expensive, but which bride will refuse on the main day of her life a pretty multi-colored wreath that clearly emphasizes her charming image! Sometimes a wedding dress code assumes the presence of all women and girls in dresses and flower wreaths at the event.

Do-it-yourself wreath of flowers on your head

You will need:

  • Natural flowers;
  • tape;
  • scissors,
  • nippers;
  • two pieces of green wire.

Step 1

Measure the circumference of the head and cut the wire with wire cutters. Do not forget to add to the length of 2-3 centimeters of allowances for loops. Loop at both ends of the wire.



Step 2

Choose fresh natural flowers. Chop them.



Step 3

Apply flowers one at a time to the base wire, wrapping around a second piece of wire. Weave them almost to the end.



Step 4

Closer to the end of the wreath, weave the flowers in the opposite direction. Make the transition as inconspicuous as possible.



Step 5

To make a wreath of flowers on your head look prettier and fix better, thread the ribbons of the required length into the loops. Tie them already when trying on. Everything, the wreath is ready!



Note: how to make a wreath of artificial flowers

If the idea of ​​creating this beautiful decoration was to your taste and you really want (or really need) to make such a decor from artificial flowers, then you should think in advance what style the wreath will be made of, what colors it should consist of, how many will be needed. Also decide on the material of the flowers themselves. They can be paper, fabric or molded from polymer clay. You can independently master the manufacture of artificial flowers, but it will take a lot of time. Therefore, as an option, look for the necessary blanks in hand-made stores or order a master florist.



How to make a wreath on the head of artificial flowers? Just like from living ones, it’s only a little faster, because their stems are usually made of wire, which means it will be easier to bend and take the desired shape. In addition, it is not difficult for you to weave between large flowers smaller inflorescences and various leaves. A wreath from this - and, accordingly, your image - will only benefit. A little tip: do not mix pastel and pure colors in this jewelry. It is better to make a wreath of flowers on the head or in gentle pastel shades, or use only bright colors to create it.
