The wonderful city of Faro. Portugal is waiting for guests!

There are thousands of the most beautiful places on our planet that I definitely want to visit. Some people like exotic countries where the sea, beaches and timeless fun, and someone more attracted to the architectural ensembles of conservative Europe. Well, if you like both, then by all means visit the city of Faro. Portugal will gladly reveal to you the history of an ancient country, captured in marvelous architecture. And it will delight you with its warm marine climate, the gentle ocean and magnificent beaches.



The ancient city of Faro

Portugal is a very ancient country, spread out on the Iberian Peninsula. She is the westernmost country of continental Europe. Here, no matter the city, it is a historically significant place. Portugal is beloved by many tourists. And this is no coincidence. It has everything: quiet old towns, modern cities, mountains, the ocean. Fun for every taste and budget. And if you want to visit Portugal, but do not know which city to choose, then go to Faro. Portugal will become your favorite country where you want to return again. So, Faro is located in the southernmost part of the country. This port city is the capital of the Algarve province. The town is located in a picturesque place - the Gulf of Ria Formos, not far from Cape Santa Maria. It should be noted that the Gulf of Ria Formose is a national park where very rare birds live . Arriving in Faro, you will successfully combine a beach holiday with visiting museums and shopping. And you will plunge headlong into the history of the city and country.

A bit of history

The city of Faro, with its historical past, goes back to the distant times of the Roman Empire. At first, the city of Ossonoba appeared on this site. It was a large administrative and shopping center. Later, the port was conquered by the Moors and Arabs. In the seventeenth eyelids of the Moors, the third king Afonso expelled the Portuguese. But in 1722 and 1755 Faro suffered very much from earthquakes. Subsequently, the city successfully developed and became the center of the Algarve province.



Faro through the eyes of a tourist

The city of Faro is very popular among travelers. Portugal is a country with a very mild climate, thanks to the ocean, but because many tourists tend to come here to spend an unforgettable vacation. Lovers of small beaches and the warm sea will be pleasantly surprised. Also in the city there are a lot of various entertainments: water slides, water parks, cosmetology and health centers, there is everything for practicing all kinds of sports, night clubs, bars, restaurants, which offer local cuisine.

About restaurants it is worth saying separately. If you want to enjoy real Mediterranean cuisine, then you should definitely visit Faro. Only here you can taste delicious fish dishes. But still, this is a city for non-spoiled tourists - you will not find much luxury here. Well, if you really want the comfort of DeLuxe, then near the city there is resort areas Vale de Lobo and Quinta do Lago. It has everything you need for a five-star vacation: luxury villas, luxury hotels, seven first-class golf schools, tennis courts, a riding school and much more. And, of course, gorgeous beaches. In other words, Faro is exactly the place where it will be comfortable both for a budget tourist and people who know a lot about luxury.



Faro (Portugal): sights of the city

In addition to beach holidays and all kinds of entertainment, here you will find food for the soul and plunge into the world of beauty. You will see museums, beautiful cathedrals and churches. And the old city center, which is more than two hundred years old, will immerse you in the atmosphere of medieval Portugal. All the main attractions of the city are located behind the fortress wall. Here you can see the Cathedral of the thirteenth century, the palace of the Bishop (eighteenth century), next to which is an old monastery. This monastery now houses a local history museum. Of particular note is the Nossa Senhora do Carmo Church. Tourists are attracted by gilded wood carvings. It is also worth a visit to the Naval Museum, which exhibits exhibits of warships and merchant ships that plowed the local seas for many centuries.



How to get there

You can get to Faro by all means of transport. But if you prefer air flights, then Faro International Airport (Portugal) will hospitably welcome you. After Lisbon Faro Airport is the second most important and passenger in the country. It is located two and a half kilometers from the city. You can get to the center of Faro by regular buses. The trip takes only twenty minutes.



Travel Tips

In addition to viewing medieval sights, tourists can be advised on shopping. I must say that Faro has a large number of boutiques, the assortment of which will please the most sophisticated fashionistas. And be sure to buy yourself a pair of Portuguese-made shoes. After all, the manufacturer of the highest quality shoes in Europe is Portugal. Faro reviews deserve the very best. Therefore, be sure to pack your suitcase and go to the very edge of Europe for an unforgettable experience!
