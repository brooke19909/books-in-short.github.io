Hotel Corallo Nord 2 *: description and reviews of tourists

Hotel Corallo Nord 2 * is located in Italian Rimini on the Adriatic coast literally 40 meters from the beach. The hotel is a ten-minute drive from Miramare Airport, 40 kilometers from Luigi Ridolfi Air Harbor. The road to the Rimini Viserba train station takes 5 minutes, so the location of the hotel is very convenient for those traveling alone.





A little less than three kilometers separate the hotel from the train station Torre Pedrera. Rimini city center is half a kilometer away, a 10-minute walk from the hotel. The hotel is open to guests all year round.

Accommodation

Corallo Nord 2 * guests are offered accommodation in double, triple and quadruple rooms, so even a large family will have a place to stay. In addition, a pleasant surprise for all pet owners will be the opportunity to stay at the hotel with them (provided that the pet is small), while there will be no additional fee for guests staying for a furry pet.

Each room has a private bathroom with a shower and a free hairdryer. From the balcony you can see all the beauty of the Adriatic Sea (side view). All rooms are equipped with direct-dial telephones, a TV, fan and safe with electronic lock.

Infrastructure

Reception Corallo Nord 2 * is open around the clock. The hotel has a TV room and a porch for relaxing or waiting. In addition, for the convenience of departing or arriving tourists, there is a luggage room, a bar is open around the clock, and the restaurant is open until evening.





In the hotel premises, you can access the Internet by connecting to a free Wi-Fi network, if you are leaving somewhere, you can return the key or documents with a wallet to the safe at the reception, you do not need to pay for this service. In addition, on the territory near the Hotel Corallo Nord 2 * there is a parking for guests (it is free, but therefore not guarded).

All public areas of the hotel are equipped with air conditioning, which operate from a common system, so even in hot weather you can enjoy the coolness.

Nutrition

In the lobby of the Corallo Nord 2 * hotel (Rimini, Italy) a breakfast buffet is served every morning. If you want to have dinner, you can choose from a-la-carte dishes, if you want to taste the local exotic, you must pre-order.

Attractions nearby

Near the hotel Corallo Nord 2 * (Italy, Rimini) there is a public transport stop. After waiting for the bus, you can drive around the city and explore the surroundings. And you can walk, visit the city dolphinarium and other attractions of the Viserba area. Do not forget to go to San Marino, an excursion is sure to be offered at the Corallo hotel: from the mountain on which this autonomous republic is located, a wonderful view of the entire coast opens.

Reviews

The hotel is small, and two stars in its category mean that it is good in order to be satisfied with the minimum living conditions. There is no need to talk about luxury. Nevertheless, the guests had a very pleasant impression of Corallo Nord 2 *. Guest reviews contain many opinions that staying in this hotel can be compared to staying at home, because the hotel is small, clean and very comfortable. Most likely, the hospitable Italians themselves, the owners of the hotel, who were mentioned by more than one traveler, also bribed tourists.





The rooms, according to the guests, are very small, but they are quite comfortable, all the conditions for living are created. The cleaning was done daily, but many complained about the shabby linen and its irregular change. Still, some did not like the fact that the hosts do not speak English, but basically the international sign language and the words generally accepted in the tourism sector will be understood by everyone. A very modest breakfast - two slices of bread, a croissant, yogurt, a drink. It will be difficult for a man to eat up. But there are numerous cafes and restaurants nearby, which serve truly gigantic portions - Italians know a lot about food. And those who ordered a hotel dinner say that he was very satisfying. On the menu you can order all kinds of fish, meat dishes, various risottos, pizza and kebabs. A wide variety of fruits and delicious homemade desserts are served.

The proximity to the sea also appealed to the guests, it takes two minutes to go and you can go swimming, so some quietly leave extra clothes in their rooms.

The noise from the railway prevented some guests from sleeping. As such, there is no animation at the hotel, but in the evenings on the streets of Rimini you do not have to get bored, especially in the high season. Singers and dancers perform, concerts take place, and cafes and bars are open until late in the evening.

Convenience for tourists was the proximity to the hotel of the Billa supermarket, as well as the Wind office, where you can buy a local sim card for phone calls and Internet connections. The nearby Bounty Bar with live music is open until 1am daily.
