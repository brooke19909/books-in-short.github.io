Caution! Clones

From January 1, 2017, insurance companies are obliged to sell CTP insurance policies. In case of failure to comply with this requirement or the presence of permanent technical failures, the insurer may be fined.

Such innovations immediately spurred fraudsters to create ‚Äúclone‚Äù sites selling electronic CTP. Among the first on the market appeared visual copies of Rosgosstrakh, VSK and RESO. The addresses of these sites are similar to real ones, but they are framed by anonymous persons. There are also many sites of "intermediaries", which are generally prohibited by law in electronic OSAGO. Already even received the first complaints from affected customers. In case of purchase of the policy from scammers, funds are debited from the account, but the contract for e-mail is not received. Or, conversely, a letter arrives in the mail with redrawn PDF files of these forms, but with fake stamps and signatures. Such policies are not registered in any database, insurers for them are not responsible.
