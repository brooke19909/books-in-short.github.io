Hotel Palm Galleria Resort 3 *, Thailand: description and reviews of tourists

Palm Galleria Resort is a three-star hotel located in one of the most mysterious and picturesque areas of Thailand - Phang Nga. This is a beach hotel, designed both for measured rest and relaxation, as well as for excursions and active pastime. The hotel is small, but its infrastructure is a real resort. Most of the tourists who visited there recommend this hotel and leave very good reviews about it. We will also get acquainted with what attracted them and what advice they give vacationers. By the way, this hotel a couple of years ago could be found on the network under the name Best Western Palm Galleria Resort 3 *. Mostly independent travelers come here. Most of the hotel guests from the "package tourists" settle in it, using the "Palm Gallery" as a short overnight stay during two-day excursions.



Khao Lak

This is the name of the resort where the Palm Galleria Resort is located. Khao Lak is one of the most secluded areas in the Thai province of Phang Nga. People come here to relax and be alone with the picturesque nature. The white sandy beaches of the Andaman Sea, the jungle, waterfalls, bizarre cliffs, turquoise water, mangroves and slowly wandering monitor lizards - all at your service. As in any Thai resort, there are shops, restaurants, cafes, and markets. Only instead of nightclubs and discos are centers of yoga and relaxation. It is not crowded, there is no fuss, like in Phuket, and the prices are quite moderate.

How to get there

The only hub closest to the resort and, accordingly, to the hotel is located on the island of Phuket. Aeroflot and Es seven flights fly from Moscow directly to Moscow. You need to fly about 9 hours. You can also take advantage of other connecting flights in Bangkok. From the capital of Thailand to the Phuket hub, you need to fly about an hour and a half. And from Kar Lak to him more than 80 kilometers. To get from the airport to the Palm Galleria Resort hotel (in the reviews indicate that mostly tourists take a taxi), you need at least an hour, or even two. Buses also come here - both from Bangkok, and from Phuket and from Krabi province.

Located Palm Galleria Resort on Pakarang Beach, on the shores of the Andaman Sea. This place is considered one of the most beautiful in the vicinity. Here, white, as in the mural, sand, and around the mangrove forest. By bus, you can reach Takua Pa, where there are shops and markets. So to any signs of civilization from here you need to go more than half an hour. True, next to the hotel there is a small fishing village with several not too expensive restaurants, including those that serve seafood. Massage and yoga centers are open during the season. Territory and infrastructure

The hotel consists of several small buildings. All of them are two-story, built in traditional Thai style. Palm Galleria Resort has a reception and reception, relaxation areas with swings, a children's playground, and a large pool. The territory is not too big, but green, well-groomed and cozy. A lot of artificial ponds with fish, trimmed glades, flowers, palm trees. Around - real tropical jungle.



Number of rooms

Hotel Palm Galleria Resort can be called medium in size. It has almost a hundred rooms. They belong to several categories. Many of them are designed for one guest. There are 4 rooms with several rooms. Each guest room has air conditioning with individual split system, shower with bathroom and hairdryer. Despite the fact that the hotel is positioned as a "three rubles", the level of comfort in the rooms - and this is confirmed by tourists - is much higher. Any room has a small refrigerator, a free safe, an electric kettle, a coffee maker, and satellite TV. There are no Russian-language channels. They even give slippers, which is very unusual for a hotel of this class, as well as an umbrella for rainy weather and a beach bag. An extra cot can be requested. The rooms are spacious, often with panoramic views. The beds are large, sometimes there is also a sofa. Cabinets are built into the walls. On the balcony there is not only a table and two chairs, but also a bench. Every day, guests are given two bottles of water and a stick with tea and coffee. They put a lot of towels, change daily. Cosmetic accessories - gels, shampoos are reported in the bathroom. They clean perfectly, and even if your things are scattered around the room, they will be neatly folded.



Services

The hotel is surrounded by greenery, although its area is not too large. All paths are well-groomed, any garbage is instantly cleaned. The hotel territory is closed from strangers and is guarded round the clock. Laundry and dry cleaning work at the hotel. The administrator can arrange car rental. If you want to organize a seminar or conference at the Palm Galleria Resort 3 * hotel, you will be provided with a specially equipped room with two projectors, systems for playing audio and video, as well as Wi-Fi for web-based negotiations. A fax service is available at the business center. The hotel has its own currency exchange, however, the rate here is slightly lower than in banks. You can also reserve babysitting services if you come with a small child, but want to go on an excursion. Wi-Fi is free, catches throughout the hotel, including in rooms, the speed is excellent.

Entertainment

Hotel Palm Galleria Resort 3 * in reviews is described as one of the few budget hotels where guests are given the opportunity to engage in such aristocratic sports as golf. A specially equipped field is a stone's throw from the property. There is also a spa with a sauna and Thai body, face and foot massage. But you can order other procedures, such as body wraps or herbal scrub. The hotel has a good tour desk, with which you scout all the surroundings, and they, as our guests assure us, are worth it. Tourists are advised to rent scooters at the reception. Rental is inexpensive, and helmets are free. If you are afraid to ride a scooter, you can rent a bike. And four hours a day you can ride on it for free.

Sea and pool vacations

Pakarang beach near the hotel is almost empty. Walk to him for about five minutes from the hotel buildings. But this area is intended more for beauty, and not for swimming. Excursions

In Khao Lak, tourists mostly visit natural attractions. In the resort there are immediately three reserves. The most popular of them is Khao Sok, as well as Phang Nga Bay, where the James Bond Island sung in cinema is located. One of the most famous excursions, which, as a rule, is organized in Phuket, is a trip to the Similan Islands, known for their wild pristine nature. Such trips last for several days, with overnight stays at various hotels, including the former Best Western Palm Galleria Resort. But there are other offers in the field of ecotourism - kayaking, jeep safari, boat trips along the mangroves, elephant riding. And Khao Lak is considered an ideal place for diving and snorkeling. The underwater world is amazing - you will see real marine gardens of coral and colorful fish. You can even watch leopard sharks here.



Shopping

Despite the seclusion of the hotel, you will not find in Khao Lak, as in the surrounding area, problems with markets and shops. The entire route along the beaches is literally lined with shops of the Seven-Eleven chain. In towns and cities, night markets work. Well, if you are not satisfied with the range, then you can go to the provincial capital - the city of Takua Pa. There is a huge supermarket "Big Tse". Everywhere, including near the beaches, there are shops selling fruit. There you will find a lot of shops selling clothes and souvenirs for tourists. But the cheapest things and food you need, of course, to buy in the night markets. Moreover, in the evenings there are free performances and shows. You can go shopping in Phuket, especially in Patong. Moreover, buses run frequently along the highway and pass directly along the highway where the Palm Galleria Resort stands.
