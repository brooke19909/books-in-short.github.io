"President" (hotel, Zelenogorsk): address, service, reviews

If you want to spend a quiet family weekend, corporate event, or just relax from the bustle of the city, go to the "President Hotel". Zelenogorsk will give you silence, clean air and beautiful nature.





a brief description of

Of great interest is the "President Hotel". Zelenogorsk is perhaps one of the best places for lovers of peace and nature. The concept of the hotel is travel. Each room, hall, corridor or corner of the territory is stylized so that you recognize one of the distant countries in it. Thus, being not far from home, you can feel like a participant in a round-the-world trip. Closed guarded territory will create a feeling of comfort and security.

Accommodation

"President Hotel" (Zelenogorsk) has a number of rooms, which includes 20 guest apartments. Each of them is named after a city, and inside you will find certain decorative elements that will take you to another point on the globe. So, the following numbers are presented to your attention:

  • Standard one-room numbers ("London", "Amsterdam", "Imatra", "Venice") are characterized by an area of ​​27 square meters. m. In a cozy niche is a bedroom, represented by a twin or large bed, and on the contrary equipped with a working area. The room has a large wardrobe, coffee table and large windows through which a lot of sunlight penetrates. All rooms offer views of the Gulf of Finland.
  • Family rooms ("Bangkok", "Barcelona", "Rio De Janeiro", "Kyoto") with an area of ​​34 square meters. m. Equipped with a large bed, as well as a sofa bed. Thus, it can accommodate up to 3 adults or 2 parents and 2 children. The entrance hall has a large wardrobe and coat rack. The windows offer enchanting views of the forest.
  • The area of ​​junior suites (Baltic, Moscow, Antalya, Paris, Teriyoki, Syracuse, Costa Blanca, Deauville, Rimini) varies from 31 to 41 square meters. The rooms are divided into two functional areas: the bedroom has a large orthopedic bed and dresser, and the living room has a large soft sofa.The entrance hall has a wardrobe with a mirrored door and you can enjoy the view of the Gulf of Finland from the windows.
  • The hotel has two suites - "Cannes" and "Hollywood" with an area of ​​75 square meters. m, "Nice" with an area of ​​107 square meters. m. A luxurious bedroom, a spacious living room with dining table and its own kitchen area - what else is needed for a comfortable stay? Natural wood furniture will be an additional advantage. It offers views of the Gulf of Finland.




Cottages

If you like a secluded vacation, cozy cottages will be offered to you by the President Hotel. Zelenogorsk will leave only the best impressions if you choose one of the following options for your stay:

  • Small cottage has an area of ​​75 square meters. m and can accommodate up to 4 guests. Two bedrooms can be fitted with large or twin beds. The cottage has its own kitchen, which is combined with a living room and dining room. The bathroom is equipped with a comfortable shower. It is worth noting the presence of a washing machine and dishwasher.
  • The average cottage can comfortably accommodate 6 guests on an area of ​​160 square meters. m, guests have 3 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms (one of them is equipped with a jacuzzi). You can cook in your own kitchen, dine in the dining room, and relax in the cozy living room. For business people, a full-fledged office is equipped. And, of course, the most pleasant moment is the presence of your own terrace.
  • Large cottage covers an area of ​​180 square meters. m. Four bedrooms, a kitchen, a living room, three bathrooms, a living room and a terrace - all this will make your stay comfortable and complete.

What to do in the hotel

A lot of interesting activities and entertainments were prepared for guests by the President Hotel (Zelenogorsk). Swimming pool, playgrounds and sports fields - this is not all. A pleasant moment will be for you the presence of a small zoo, as well as a complex of cozy gazebos. In the cold season, a fireplace is lit in the main restaurant, next to which you can bask in a glass of mulled wine. A picnic trip to the countryside can also be arranged for you.

Positive reviews

A lot of positive impressions will be presented to you by the President Hotel (Zelenogorsk). Reviews allow us to conclude about the following advantages of the institution:

  • professional and well-coordinated work of the staff;
  • delicious and hearty breakfasts;
  • the hotel can relax with pets;
  • incredibly beautiful green area;
  • comfortable orthopedic bed;
  • great playground for kids;
  • large clean pools;
  • bike rental is free;
  • there are comfortable hammocks in the garden;
  • From the windows of the hotel you can see the Gulf of Finland.




Negative reviews

Even the most luxurious hotel may have some flaws. Speaking about this institution, it is worth noting the following negative points:

  • SPA center services are unreasonably expensive;
  • the bath is cold enough;
  • the bathroom has a lot of black mold;
  • the outwardly beautiful furniture is actually made of cheap and low-quality materials;
  • it’s not clear why, but the apartments are “decorated” with artificial flowers;
  • animals in the mini-zoo are poorly looked after, and they are kept in uncomfortable small cages;
  • a busy track runs near the hotel, because of which you will not be able to relax in silence;
  • weddings and other events that are regularly held at the hotel create a lot of noise.

Given all the pros and cons, you can call the "President" a worthy option for a quiet vacation in the countryside. Surrounded by beautiful nature and quality service, your weekend will be unforgettable.
