"Burning" (washing machine): customer and specialist reviews

The Gorenie company was founded in 1950. The main office of the company is currently located in Slovenia. Since 1961, Gorenie was exclusively engaged in the manufacture of agricultural products and the question of washing machines did not arise. However, time passed, and soon the management of the company became interested in household appliances.

First of all, the company began to produce gas stoves and refrigerators. The world saw the first washing machines of the above trademark in 2002. Since then, they have improved many times, and the technology as a whole has changed.





Features of Gorenie washing machines

Modes washing machines "Gorenie" have a lot. They suit well for various types of fabrics. There is a separate mode for delicate washing. The synthetics do not stretch during the spin cycle. For easier washing, the Normal mode is provided. Power consumption is within normal limits. To rinse things more, the Allergy function is used. Washing machines of the new Gorenie series have an optical drum. Washing is much more efficient.

In addition, it should be noted that the machines are quite spacious, and linen can be put up to 6 kg on average. From water leaks there is an Aqua-Stop system. During operation, a special valve is able to block the hose and turn off the water supply. Thus, in case of malfunctions, the washing machine immediately stops working. The average cost of the Gorenie model is 20 thousand rubles.
