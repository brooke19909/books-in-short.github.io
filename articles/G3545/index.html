A bit of history

Work on the construction of a new giant complex began in 2004. The ideologist of creating an innovative enterprise was the international union of two major companies: the Swedish Ikea and the Russian Belaya Dacha. For three years, a unique complex was erected, the equal in size of which is not yet in Europe. The total construction costs slightly exceeded the amount of twelve billion rubles.

Where is the mega-center located





The family trade and leisure center is located in the Kotelniki suburbs near Moscow, in the Lyubertsy district. Branded Mega buses departing from Lyublino, Vykhino and Kuzminki metro stations will easily deliver visitors directly to their destination - to the Belaya Dacha Ikea shopping center.

The address of the family trading giant: the 14th kilometer of the Moscow ring road, Kotelniki, Pokrovsky passage, 5.

In addition to buses that run with minor breaks, you can always get to the shopping center by shuttle bus.

How to get there by car
