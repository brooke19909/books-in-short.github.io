Holidays at sea. Taganrog invites tourists to the Sea of ​​Azov

Taganrog is a small resort town in southern Russia. This village, in addition to natural attractions in the form of the sea, also has a very rich history. At one time it was both an Italian and a Greek city. This is the first port built by Peter I. It is also the only city in the Empire that was built according to a clear architectural plan. Holidays on the Sea of ​​Azov (Taganrog is famous for it) was popular even then.

The streets of the village were fighting during both wars, the city survived a two-year occupation. For all these services he was awarded the title “City of Military Glory”.



A small characteristic of the city

Currently, Taganrog is a large industrial and port center of Russia.
Tourism is also developing here, but at a slow pace. Nevertheless, this town offers an excellent and unforgettable vacation at sea. Taganrog has a climate. The temperate continental belt prevails, with warm summers. The holiday season here lasts, as usual on the Azov coast: from May to September. The sea is shallow, sandy beaches.

You can get here in every possible way: by car, bus, train, plane (the nearest airport is 60 km away in Rostov-on-Don).

Central beach

Taganrog city beaches welcome vacationers. A common and oldest beach is Central, located in the central part of the city. Thanks to him, any person will enjoy a vacation and will always remember a vacation at sea. Taganrog has nice, clean and wide sandy beaches. A convenient approach to the sea is available, a clean bottom. To relax on this coast, you will have to stay in the private sector, apartments (from 400 rubles per person per day), mini-hotels, hostels (from 1000 rubles per person per day).

In addition to the Central, recently been equipped with new beaches - Eliseevsky and Sunny.



Eliseevsky and Sunny beaches

Eliseevsky beach can be called the best place to relax in the city. Compared to others, the sea in this part is really deep. And the beach itself is clean and sandy. All amenities are nearby - toilets, cafes, showers, massage services and a water park. Paid entrance.

Sunny beach - similar in all respects to Eliseevsky, only there is more space and free entry.

Due to the abundance of equipped places for spending holidays, many will gain a very good impression of spending time in such a settlement as Taganrog. Holidays at sea, the prices of which are very bright, are available to any family. In addition to city beaches, on the outskirts of cities there are also “wild” places.

Price category

Rest in the city ​​is mainly focused on those vacationers who prefer peace and quiet. Therefore, the most successful solution would be to stay in the private sector. The choice of housing is diverse, and the prices are minimal - one of the lowest on the Azov coast. Apartment houses provide their services from 400 rubles. per person per day. In villages close to the city - this amount is halved. Therefore, vacation at sea (Taganrog) will be quite cheap.

However, reservations must be reserved in advance. Fortunately, now there are enough online forums where you can not only reserve a place for yourself, but also get reviews and impressions of people who have already rested there.



Recreation facilities

In addition to the private sector, in Taganrog you can improve your health in sanatoriums and dispensaries. There are also complexes on the territory of which the most comfortable rest at sea. Taganrog is adapted for children - there are camps. There are only 4 of them on the territory of the settlement: DSOL “Mir”, DSOL “Sputnik”, DSOL “Chaika” and MLC “Camomile”.

Metallurg in the village The Golden Spit (25 km from the city) is the largest base of the Taganrog coast with a developed infrastructure, its own water park, and a wide selection of places from economy to elite. The bases "Rainbow" and "Quiet Harbor" are located only 10-15 km from the city.

Two sanatorium dispensaries - "Ivushka" and "Topol" - are located in the city, work all year round.
