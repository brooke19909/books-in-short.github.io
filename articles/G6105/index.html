Broadband access

The second method is most widely used and is called DSL, in other words, a telephone line is also used here. However, the possibility of transmitting information is significantly increasing, since it uses broadband access, which significantly exceeds the capabilities of the dial-up approach. DSL modems provide a completely digital exchange of content, while there is no need to convert analog-to-digital information, which improves the quality of communication. With this type of connection, the dependence of the network on telephone calls disappears: the Internet and telephone communications exist independently of each other, which further enhances the convenience of this type of connection with the network.
