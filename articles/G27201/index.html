The best hotels of Vyborg: accommodation, photos, reviews of tourists

The city of Vyborg is one of the most interesting corners of the Leningrad region. In this city there are so many diverse historical monuments that it is very interesting to visit any Russian person. Tourists who have been here immediately make a reservation that it is in this city that it is advisable to live in the center so that you can see all the sights. And for this, you need to study Vyborg hotels in advance so that you know exactly where you can stay. The city itself is very interesting, beautiful and stunningly green. Since we need to consider a lot of great hotels, let's not waste time and proceed to consider them.



Hotel "Friendship"

Not everyone is interested in five-star, luxury hotels in Vyborg. This city is popular among different segments of the population, so many tourists prefer good three-star hotels to expensive apartments. We will start our story with just such hotels that provide the opportunity for a quality vacation without unnecessary luxury. And the first on our list is the Druzhba Hotel (Vyborg). This is a six-story hotel located in the city center, right on the stunning promenade. Very close to the bus station and train station. Already by location, this hotel can be considered the most successful in the city.

Appearance, services and number of rooms

In fact, this is a Soviet-era building, but in 2005 a complete reconstruction was made, so today you can visit the modern and very comfortable Druzhba Hotel. Vyborg is famous for this magnificent building, which in shape resembles the layout of a six-deck liner. So it was erected, and during the repair work, the designers did not change anything. Upon entering, you find yourself in a spacious hall, decorated in bright colors, the counter is made in the shape of a boat. The hotel has 102 rooms, each with its own style and design. The rooms are spacious and comfortable, cleaning and washing linen are impeccable. It has two restaurants, a bar and a nightclub on Fridays and Saturdays. So you will have something to do, there is no time to be bored here.



Cost and reviews

Choosing a standard room with two single beds, with breakfast included, you can plan for the amount of 2125 rubles. Superior room with one double bed will cost a little more, at 2295 rubles. City hotels in Vyborg are not inferior in price to the regional center, so the cost in most other hotels is higher. A suite with a double bed costs 3825 rubles, a presidential suite - 5225. In the reviews, tourists note that the rooms are very comfortable, clean and tidy, but many complain that the door locks are so weak that they open when you click on the door (for the safety of their things have to worry).

The name itself attracts attention. Its creators also counted on it, but there is more to come. Hotel Viking (Vyborg) is a modern hotel, which was built in 2008. Hotel "Northern Crown"

This is a very interesting, even outstanding city hotel. The Northern Crown (Vyborg) occupies an old building of the 18th century, which is a historical monument. The building itself is small, only four-story, but it has been preserved just magnificent. And inside you will find a real fairy tale. The interiors are very modern and beautiful. The hotel offers you comfortable accommodation at affordable prices, as well as secure parking. The hotel is supposed to accommodate in double rooms of the type "economy" and "standard". There is no pricing information on the official website, however there are many reviews from tourists. Most of them are words of gratitude to the staff who performed their work quickly, professionally and quietly, but there are also those who report shortcomings. Hotel "Bat"

The unique appearance of this city has developed over more than seven centuries, and therefore architectural monuments represent different eras and ethnic groups. In addition to the Vybor Castle and Clock Tower, as well as several medieval temples, the city has preserved seven buildings of the Swedish period of the XVIII century. These are the House on the Rock, the Church of Hyacinth, the House of the Merchant Verkut and the House of the Rich Citizen, as well as several other buildings. Moreover, all of them were built before the appearance of the first urban development plan. In order to examine them all, you must first select a hotel with a convenient location. This is exactly what the Bat Hotel (Vyborg) is. Only two minutes walk separates it from the old part of the city, where the main attractions are located. And just across the road from the hotel, the stunning promenade of the Gulf of Finland begins. In total, the hotel has 21 rooms, including rooms for honeymooners, that is, the hotel can accept up to 50 guests. From the reviews of regular visitors to the hotel, we find out that a very convenient online booking system is in place. There is everything to make your vacation unforgettable. Cleanliness and comfort in the rooms, friendly staff, a sauna and a restaurant on site - all this allows you to relax and unwind.
