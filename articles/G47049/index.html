Hotels in Rostov-on-Don in the center: an overview of the best options, a description with photos, reviews

Rostov-on-Don is the largest city in southern Russia, founded in 1749. The tenth most populated city in the country is located on the banks of the Don River. It is a major scientific, industrial, cultural and transport center. And the people call the city "Gateway of the Caucasus." If you want to visit Rostov-on-Don, hotels in the city center are an excellent choice.
