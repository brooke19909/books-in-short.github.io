Hotel Nova Park Hotel 4

Description: Nova Park Hotel 4 is located in Sharjah, close to the beach. From Sharjah Airport to the hotel, the trip takes no more than half an hour. Nearby are shopping centers, shops, bazaars, banks, museums. A free shuttle bus to Dubai departs regularly from the hotel.



Nova Park Hotel Sharjah was built in 1980. In 2009, the last repair was carried out.

Rooms: Tourists are accommodated in Standart and Studio apartments. All 250 rooms are equipped with a balcony and air conditioning. The rooms have a TV, telephone, satellite TV, minibar, radio, refrigerator, electronic safe, scales.

The bathroom has a shower, sink, toiletries, mirror and hairdryer. The rooms are equipped with an electric kettle, sugar, coffee bags.

Cleaning is carried out daily, linen is changed according to the schedule. Service is provided in the apartment.

The hotel rooms are non-smoking. Children under 12 years stay free of charge without extra beds. No pets are allowed in the hotel.



Beach: The distance from Nova Park Hotel 4 to a private sandy beach is about 400 m. It offers a paid shuttle service to the beach.

It offers guests - awnings, mattresses, sunbeds and towels.
