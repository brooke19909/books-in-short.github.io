Old Town Boutique Hotel

Those travelers who want to get to know the old city closer choose hotels in the center of Dresden. One of the most luxurious is the boutique hotel Gewandhaus Dresden. It is located in the heart of Altstadt, in 2015 a large-scale reconstruction was carried out in it. In total, 97 rooms and apartments of various categories became available to guests.

High-quality, custom-made furniture and modern equipment, cosmetics, a bar, a safe, a flat-screen TV are standard rooms. Superior rooms also have a coffee machine, a jacuzzi and a separate relaxation area.

Buffet breakfast is served in the hotel restaurant (it is included in the room rate). Exclusive dishes are offered for lunch and dinner: beef steak, fresh fish, tartare in various variations and cakes that look like works of art.

The wellness center with a pool and gym, which is exclusively for hotel guests, is open daily until 23:00.

The five-star boutique hotel is within walking distance of the main attractions:

  • Frauenkirche - 650 m.
  • Zwinger - 900 m.
  • Semper Opera House - 1 km.
  • Dresden International Airport is 12 km away.

Traveling with children

For travelers with children, special conditions are required, such as a children's menu in the restaurant, a playground or babysitting services.

Hotel Residenz Am Schloss is conveniently located in the southern district of Dresden (Lokwitz) and offers comfortable stay for couples with children. The center of Dresden can be easily reached in 20 minutes by car or public transport. A bus stop is located directly opposite the hotel entrance.

In the morning, guests are offered freshly prepared dishes, there is also a menu for those who are on a gluten-free diet.

The hotel has a wonderful territory with a cozy garden and barbecue facilities, a children's playground is available for children, and you can rent a bike (for an additional fee). For the entertainment of young guests there are books, cartoons with cartoons and board logic games.
