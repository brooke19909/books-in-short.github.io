Vilamendhoo Island Resort & Spa 4 * (Maldives): description of rooms, entertainment, service, reviews

Maldives - a paradise, still unknown to some tourists. Therefore, many are interested in whether a vacation there will be good. The answer is definitely yes. The Maldives is one of the few destinations whose photographs, printed in advertising booklets, are fully consistent with what can be seen in reality.



General information about holidays in the Maldives

By the beauty of nature, this place has practically no competitors. The coast of all islands is the finest white sand and clear water - the cleanest in the Indian Ocean. Tall palm trees, as if descended from frames of famous advertising, create a feeling of paradise. Shallow water for bathing young children is the best option, and the climate in the Maldives is so mild that there are practically no problems with acclimatization, despite the long flight. Out of almost two thousand islands, about eighty are accessible to tourists today. You can move between them by boat, seaplane or boat. As a rule, hotels here occupy the whole island, so the beaches are quiet and not crowded. In general, the hotel infrastructure in the Maldives is very diverse. There are hotels for every taste: guest houses, considered a relatively cheap accommodation option, three-star hotels, four-star complexes, such as Vilamendhoo Island Resort & Spa, and, of course, the luxurious apartments that the Maldives is famous for. Accommodation prices depend on the season.

Weather on a paradise island

In terms of temperature, it is comfortable all year round. Tourists definitely will not need warm clothes on the islands. You need to know that the high season begins in November and lasts until the end of spring. It was at this time that many tourists prefer to go to the Maldives. Prices in hotels during this period are higher than during the rest of the time. For example, at the hotel Vilamendhoo Island Resort & Spa (4 *) the cost of a seven-day tour for two with three meals a day and living in a bungalow will cost about one hundred eighty thousand rubles without a ticket.





According to tourists, it is better not to come to the Maldives from May to September, because during this period the wet season begins. It rains a lot and in large quantities, sometimes all day. Although you can relax at this time, because the water in the ocean resembles fresh milk, and rainfall stops as abruptly as it begins. But the prices in hotels this season are significantly lower. At this time, only those who are little interested in the weather come here. Vilamendhoo Island Resort & Spa, for example, offers rooms much cheaper. For example, in September you can book a standard double room for eighty-eight thousand.

Hotel Description

Vilamendhoo Island Resort & Spa is built on the small island of Vilamedu, located in the Ari Atoll. It is a paradise surrounded by tropical palm trees and surrounded by crystal clear water. The hotel complex is eighty-two kilometers from the nearest international airport in Male. The size of the island is relatively small: nine hundred meters in length and two hundred and fifty in width.

Therefore, in addition to this hotel, there is no other infrastructure on it. The nearest settlements are approximately eighty kilometers distant. They can be reached by seaplane. The hotel is part of the AAA Hotels and Resorts hotel chain. The highlight of it are its magnificent spa programs. The hotel area is designed like a garden.

Beach

The fact that in the Maldives you can spend an unforgettable vacation on the ocean, has long been proven. Vilamendhoo Island Resort & Spa, whose beach is twenty meters from many rooms, offers guests all the services to sunbathe, swim safely and enjoy nature. The entrance to the water is comfortable, gentle. The beach at this hotel is private, sandy. It has many sunbeds and umbrellas that you can borrow for free. Of course, judging by the reviews, some of our compatriots recommend taking coral slippers with them, but for many they were not useful. In the water in the lagoon there are many amazing creatures: beautiful fish, stingrays, moray eels, there are even small sharks that are safe for people.

Entertainment and sports

The hotel often organizes interesting programs, including slide shows about the culture and history of the state of Maldives, about the local marine fauna, show videos filmed by divers, etc. In the evenings, there are discos, a dance show called Bodu Beru, crabs running, musicians perform, saxophone sounds.
