Hotel Coconut Grove 4 * (India, South Goa): description, photos, reviews of tourists

Goa is the southern state of India and, in combination, the country's most famous tourist center. Often travelers, having visited Goa, begin to make up their minds about the mysterious and multifaceted India based on this short tourist experience. But do not rush, as often happens, the most famous state of Goa is completely different from the rest in the country.

Goa is divided into South and North. But the differences between them are not only geographical. These two areas of the same resort are strikingly different from each other. South Goa is a favorite resort for wealthy tourists from Europe, prosperous Hindus and holiday lovers in the Bounty style. The advantages of the South in the form of paradise beaches, serene tropical landscapes are clear to everyone, but the prices in the South are relatively high and not only by Indian standards. Students from Europe and Asia are happy to come to the relatively budgetary north of Goa, lovers of new experiences against the background of wild exotic, crazy shopping in the Indian style and club nightly courage.





Resort villages in the south of Goa can also give tourists a very diverse experience. Some have a very diverse infrastructure, many nightclubs, restaurants and shopping centers. But our compatriots, spoiled by the benefits of civilization, fly to Goa for impressions of a completely different nature. Give them the primary natural serenity, relaxation, relaxation in comfort, but against the backdrop of pure nature. Such seekers of meditative nirvana can be safely advised resort villages, such as Betalbatim.

Betalbatim Resort

This is a cozy, not crowded resort village with paradise protected natural beauties. The village of Betalbatim ends with a 1,500 m long sea coast. The wild tropical beauty of the ocean coast is balanced by the tranquil views of the pine forests that frame the village from the mainland. In the area there are a lot of small bungalow type hotels that are distant from each other by groves. These nice hotels successfully complement the idyllic picture of the sea coast.

The bustling and developed resort villages of Colva or Margao are within 3 km of Betalbatim. You can go there if you feel a longing for civilization in the atmosphere of beach relaxation.

General information about the hotel Coconut Grove Beach Resort 4 *

This cozy boutique hotel is located in the resort village of Betalbatim, 250 meters from the wide sandy beach. The hotel consists of several two-story cottages deep in a pretty tropical garden. In total there are 36 rooms. The hotel complex was built in 2004, and in 2012 it was overhauled and refined.

There are no conditions for disabled guests at the hotel.





The hotel has a Russian-speaking staff at the reception, as well as the staff communicate well in English.

The hotel accepts payment by credit card. Check-in after 12:00, you need to leave the room on the day of eviction before 11:00. If you believe the numerous reviews of tourists, then, subject to availability, the administration will quietly populate newly arrived guests before 12:00.

Hotel location

Coconut Grove Beach 4 * is located in the south of Goa, 13 km from Dabolim airport and 250 m from the large sandy beach of Betalbatim.

The developed and bustling resort village of Colva is less than a kilometer away. On the beach you can walk in 20 minutes. unhurried step. Margao Railway Station is 7 km from the hotel. The capital of Goa, Panaji, is 28 km from the property.





For travel to other villages, it is best to order a taxi. You can bargain with taxi drivers, you even need to, because they knowingly and very deliberately raise prices for tourists. For locals, a taxi ride of up to 10 km rarely costs more than 500 rupees.

You can also get to Kolva or other not so distant villages on a rented bike or bike.

Driving a rented car is only possible for very experienced drivers. For trips to the big cities of mainland India, it is better to buy package sightseeing tours and join in tourist groups.





What excursions you can diversify your leisure time in Betalbatim

You will not find great cultural attractions that are popular among tourists in Goa. But there are plenty of interesting places and picturesque natural beauties in the state, and they leave few people indifferent.

Along the coast of Betalbatim, dolphin colonies often swim. Local fishermen can book boat excursions along the coast to see the habitats of these cute, cheerful animals. And an attentive tourist can watch the ‚Äúschools‚Äù of swimming dolphins right on the beach.

For those who like to stare at the beauty of the underwater world, in the south of Goa there are several diving centers. The closest thing to Coconut Grove The Goan Beach Retreat 4 * is Bogmalo Bay, where you can dive and look at corals, wildlife and how these underwater creatures inhabit sunken sailboats. The price of diving without training, including equipment rental is 2.5 thousand rupees per one.

Shopping lovers are best off to North Goa, where there are many large shopping centers, the most famous of which is Ajuna. In Colva or Betalbatim you can buy only tourist trifles or local products, and even at very illogical prices.

If the soul really asks for a cultural program, you can visit Old Goa, it was the capital of India in ancient times, when the country was a colony of Portugal. The city is not without colonial chic and European comfort. The architecture of the old city is very remarkable, there are many beautiful and majestic temple complexes.

An excursion to the Dudhsagar Falls is an expensive pleasure, because you can go to it only in jeeps, accompanied by a reliable guide. But if the vacation budget allows you to view the waterfall, it is included in the top of the most beautiful in the world. Also a bonus during a trip to the waterfall you can visit a large plantation of spices, see how vanilla, cinnamon, different types of peppers, pineapple, papaya, cashews, etc. grow.

The natural beauties of Goa leave no one indifferent, but if you want refined impressions from seeing the breathtaking wild flora and fauna, you can look into the Kotigao nature reserve. There you can meet exotic birds, wild boars, monkeys, panthers, elephants, etc. Elephant rides are a popular tourist divorce, as camel rides in Egypt. To agree or not to agree to such an exotic but risky adventure is a matter of taste for everyone. But you need to know the price of the service and the route in advance.
