Annual interest depends on the down payment, total loan amount and maturity. In the agreement, the bank provides a pledge of the purchased housing until the borrower has completely fulfilled the loan obligations.

To whom and where can a Sberbank of Russia mortgage loan be provided?

You must be a citizen of the Russian Federation. You can get a mortgage at a branch of Sberbank, which can be located at the place of permanent residence of the borrower, at the location of the employer, the organization of the loaned, or at the location of the housing under which the Sberbank housing loan is taken.



The loan application will be examined during the working week. Repayment occurs in equal monthly installments. It is possible to deposit a large amount to pay off the main debt. This can be done on payment days that are set by the payment schedule.

There are special lending conditions for young people - this is Sberbank's ‚ÄúYoung Family‚Äù housing loan. For a family with one or more children, the down payment can be 10%, for a childless family - from 15%.

There is a delay in the payment of the main debt if a child is born. Deferral is granted for a period until your child reaches 3 years. Please note that this service is provided only for the main debt, interest should be paid as usual.

There is another additional option if you use a Sberbank housing loan. This is a tax deduction, its amount is 13% of the amount of interest paid. Moreover, the value of the property tax deduction has increased. Previously, it was 1 million rubles, and now it is 2 million rubles, which means that you have the opportunity to return more than 250 thousand rubles.
