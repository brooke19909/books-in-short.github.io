The funeral cult in the religion of the ancient Egyptians

The religion of Ancient Egypt, which shaped the worldview and culture of the people, was a collection of various cults that underwent numerous changes. It was characterized by an abundance of gods, including the pharaohs - the lords of the state, who, according to ordinary people, were intermediaries between heaven and earth, were subject to deification. The ascension to the throne of the new ruler was the beginning of a new era. The kings responsible for the welfare of the country received the greatest heritage - the Egyptian land, which they had to keep as a priceless treasure.

In the religion of ancient Egypt, it is no coincidence that a memorial cult held a special place. People believed that death was the beginning of the afterlife. In order for it to be prosperous, certain conditions must be observed. The main thing is to preserve the body, which was the receptacle of the immortal spirit of the deceased. A unique embalming technique was invented, which, supposedly, was taught to the priests by the patron of the dead - the god Anubis.

Royal tombs

After the physical death of the pharaohs, their bodies were mummified, because only in this case the spirit that came out of the body would connect with it and continue to live. If ordinary tombs were built for ordinary people, then for the governors of God on the earth erected giant pyramids that symbolized the ascent of the heavenly stairs. Inside the labyrinths, in hard-to-reach chambers, both a sarcophagus with a mummy and objects that were useful in the afterlife were placed.

The giant pyramids, which were a kind of "stairs" on the way to the sky, were not built at all to show the creative abilities of the masters of that time. The tombs of the kings, always distinguished by their size, symbolized the high rank of the pharaohs in relation to all other people.

Often the tombs were empty, and the mummies of the pharaohs were buried in places inaccessible to the robbers. This fact did not prevent the pyramids from fulfilling the function of ascending to heaven rulers gaining immortality. And after death, the kings received divine status.

Landmark included in the list of seven ancient wonders of the world

The most famous tourist attraction of Cairo - the Giza pyramids, located in the suburbs of the capital of Egypt. This is a whole complex of monumental structures, consisting of important monuments of civilization. Included in the UNESCO World Heritage List, it is located on the Giza plateau - part of the virgin desert, in the sand of which hides a unique evidence of the greatness of the culture of the ancient Egyptians.

Dwellings intended for the posthumous life of the pharaohs have long been the hallmark of the country. These are the oldest buildings in the world that have been reached for posterity in satisfactory condition, and each visitor will be able to touch the history of mankind, while feeling the connection of time.

The most interesting tourist site

The complex of ancient monuments includes the tombs of Mykerin, Chefren and Cheops, the second pharaoh of the 4th dynasty of Ancient Egypt. It is known that this was a cruel ruler, who directed all the resources of the state to the construction of his "home after life." The Cheops Pyramid in Cairo, created from several million smooth blocks, is the most interesting object of this place. It was built in three stages over several decades and, according to historians, the construction was completed in 2560 BC.

In a large-scale building of antiquity there are three burial chambers, one of which was supposed to become the tomb of the king. Modern archaeologists have discovered a sarcophagus made of pink granite, without a lid. The mummy of the pharaoh was absent, and this was a huge disappointment for the ministers of science, who concluded that the ruler was buried elsewhere.

The height of the monumental structure is more than 146 meters, and its weight is about 6 million tons. It is considered one of the largest structures erected in the entire history of mankind. Over five thousand tons of limestone and eight thousand tons of granite were spent on the construction. It is curious that three small companion pyramids are located around it, which, most likely, were intended for the wives of the pharaoh.

Mysteries of monumental construction

You can talk about the Pyramid of Cheops for a long time, but any narration will be incomplete, since it holds many unsolved secrets. This seems unbelievable, but the ancient Egyptians knew geometry about the so-called golden ratio, which was reflected in the proportions of the pyramid in Cairo and its angle of inclination. And to this day, the method of its construction is not unraveled, as well as who exactly acted as a labor force in those distant times.

Scientists have found that the top of the gigantic structure is directed to the North Pole and "looks" at the North Star. There is even a version according to which an amazing monument of antiquity is actually an astronomical observatory. It is not clear how the ancient builders were able to calculate everything so accurately, because they did not have special devices. It is no coincidence that supporters of mystical theories claim that gigantic structures are the work of representatives of alien civilizations.

Tomb of Pharaoh Mikerin

The smallest building located on the Giza plateau, in the suburbs of Cairo (Egypt), is the Mykerin pyramid, where tourists are not allowed to enter. Due to its modest size (height 61 meters), it does not cause much interest among tourists, and once it was called the most beautiful of the three. Lined with red granite and white limestone, it made a huge impression on people.

The architectural masterpiece was covered with sand, which ensured its good preservation. Next to it are three small pyramids, one of which was intended for the wife and sister of the pharaoh. And the rest were unfinished.

Archaeologists have found several statues depicting a ruler. But the most curious was the composition, called the "triad" - a sculpture of the formidable king, surrounded by two goddesses (Bat and Hatkor).

The only entrance to the pyramid is at a height of only 4 meters. Scientists are not interested in the burial chamber itself, but in a nearby building with six niches. About his appointment and now there is not a single assumption.

An entrance ticket can be bought at the box office located at one of the entrance to the complex. Its cost is 80 Egyptian pounds / 300 rubles. To get into the pyramid of Cheops, you will have to pay another 100 pounds / 371 rubles.

Participants in organized excursions to the pyramids in Cairo buy a one-day tour (approximately $ 85/5500 rubles), the price of which includes only an entrance ticket. A visit to the interior of the complex is paid separately. The ticket must be kept, as it may be useful in some cases.

Travel Tips

Those who suffer from cardiovascular diseases and claustrophobia (fear of enclosed spaces), hypertensive patients, asthmatics are best to refrain from exploring the pyramids in Cairo.

You can spend the whole day visiting the entire complex. However, for tourists who want to go around only the tombs, one hour is enough.

It is worth remembering that only 300 people a day can visit the Cheops pyramid. Therefore, those who dream of getting inside should come here early in the morning, when tickets have not yet been sold out. Pyramid with a romantic name

The complex in Giza is not the only pyramids located within the capital of the country. In total, there are about 118 monuments of ancient Egypt, but many of them have not been preserved and now represent a shapeless pile of stones. Fortunately, time spared an architectural work invaluable to world culture, unique in its form and construction technology.

The design of the Pink Pyramid in Cairo, located 26 kilometers from the administrative center of the country, in the village of Dakhshur, looks the same as the masterpieces of Giza. However, it has a low inclined degree of the walls. A regular isosceles triangle has a height of 104 meters, and the size of its base is 220 meters. This is the first attempt to build a classic pyramid.

Above the burial chamber of the pharaoh there are 5 discharge cavities, between which stone slabs lie. This is done to evenly distribute the weight of the powerful structure.
