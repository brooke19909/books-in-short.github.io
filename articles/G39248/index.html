Tropical Dreams Hotel Apartments (Cyprus, Protaras): description, number of rooms, services and reviews

Tropical Dreams Hotel Apartments is a simple and small, but cozy and comfortable hotel in Protaras (Cyprus). Designed for families with children, as well as for those people who seek solitude and tranquility. There are opportunities for active pastime, but not enough.

Location

Tropical Dreams Hotel Apartments is located in the city center of Protaras. The convenient location gives guests the opportunity to quickly, literally a few minutes walk to get to the beach, as well as to freely visit the shops, bars, cafes and restaurants available here. It takes less than an hour to get from Larnaca International Airport , but the journey from Paphos will be long - about four hours.





What is Tropical Dreams Hotel Apartments?

Outside and inside it is an unremarkable building - everything is quite simple, there is nothing superfluous. The hotel occupies one five-story building, next to it there are swimming pools and a restaurant building. There is a bar on site. There is also a car park, which they can use for free. Terraces for sunbathing and relaxing in the fresh air are also available. Tropical Dreams Hotel Apartments has been around for quite some time - since 1989. The last reconstruction was carried out in 2005.





Guest rooms

The hotel has 53 rooms in two categories:

  • Apartments.
  • Studios.

They are equipped with everything that tourists may need for a productive vacation. On the other hand, there is nothing superfluous. The arrangement of studios and apartments is standard. There is:

  • safe;
  • kitchenette with kettle / coffee maker and fridge;
  • air conditioning;
  • a bathroom with a hairdryer and toiletries;
  • telephone and radio;
  • furnished balcony;
  • TV with satellite channels.

Studios in Tropical Dreams Hotel Apartments 4 * are perfect for couples and honeymooners. Self-catering apartments are ideal for families with children, especially with two or more children. Despite the simplicity of the atmosphere, you can feel at home in these rooms, enjoying peace and relaxation.





Recreation, entertainment and services

The main attractions here are the pools: separate for adults and children. They are surrounded by sun loungers and umbrellas for the convenience of guests. Nearby there is a fitness center, which belongs to another institution. But vacationers Tropical Dreams Hotel Apartments 4 * have the right to visit it for free. The Internet is paid - probably this is for the best, so that the guests of the apart-hotel have a rest from their phones and tablets. It is better to play the games that are regularly held here, or to start a sporting event to have fun and get to know other guests better.

For children, unfortunately, there is only a pool. Playgrounds and rooms are not provided, so it is better to take your favorite toys with you. You can also head to the beach for additional entertainment. You just need to consider that sun loungers, umbrellas and other necessary items are paid. For a towel they will ask for a deposit, which will be returned after its delivery.

Among the services:

  • daily housekeeping (included in the price);
  • exchange office;
  • laundry and ironing;
  • delivery of iron and ironing board to the room upon request;
  • safe at the reception;
  • luggage storage;
  • parking (free);
  • car rental;
  • Office equipment;
  • children's menu and chairs in the restaurant (free);
  • Internet cafe;
  • minimarket;
  • babysitting services.

In addition, in search of adventure, you can go to the hotel opposite. There is Tsokkos Beach. Tropical Dreams Hotel Apartments Apts is part of the Tsokkos chain, so guests can freely visit its territory and use the services (not just a fitness center).





Dining at the hotel and beyond

Only a complimentary breakfast is served in the restaurant. But you can also buy a tour marked All Inclusive, so as not to think about what to cook, or where to go for dinner. There is an opportunity to buy products in a market located on the hotel territory and prepare meals yourself, as all the conditions for this are provided. The restaurant and bars are constantly working. They serve delicious, fresh dishes and refreshing spirits and soft drinks. Going for a walk in the surroundings, you can visit one of the catering places. They offer not only local cuisine, but also other dishes and snacks.





Tropical Dreams Hotel Apartments: reviews

In general, this hotel was liked by many vacationers. Cons, of course, also have. Where without them ?! But there are much fewer negative points than positive ones. First of all, tourists write about the quick check-in and the friendly attitude of the staff and the administration, no matter what time of day the guests arrive. Secondly, the guests liked the view from the windows and from the balcony, which opens part of the hotel and the beach area. If there are any violations in the agreement between the hotel and the tourist (that is, for one reason or another the administration cannot fulfill its obligations), in addition to an oral apology, the guests will receive some other gift as compensation for moral damage.

Some vacationers believe that the cost of living in Tropical Dreams Hotel Apartments (Cyprus) is overstated, as the hotel is not so comfortable. In addition, there is practically no entertainment. Families who stayed at the hotel with children note that it is convenient to stay with the kids in the apartment: there is a separate room, and meals can be prepared for the child at any time. But, perhaps, the main thing that captivates the guests is the cleanliness and friendly attitude of the administration. Since tourists come to the sea, and the beach is close, this is another definite plus. Many people have been here many times, are going to come again and advise others. Relatively low price, the possibility of privacy, peace and quiet are wonderful conditions for families and couples in love!
