AMC Royal Hotel 5 *, Hurghada: tourist reviews, hotel description, prices

Many Russian travelers planning a vacation in Egypt want to find a comfortable, but at the same time inexpensive hotel. Tours at AMC Royal Hotel 5 * are very popular with our compatriots precisely because this hotel meets the above requirements. We offer a closer look at the hotel.



Location

This hotel is located on the beach in the Hurghada area called Al Ahiya. The city center is 11 kilometers away. Nearby is El Gouna, about 20 kilometers away. A big plus of this hotel is its proximity to the airport. The air harbor is just 17 kilometers away. So upon arrival you can get to the hotel very quickly.

Hotel itself

Almost all of our compatriots who visited this hotel, in their comments respond positively to the rooms provided to them. Restaurants, bars

Sea

According to our compatriots who spent their holidays at the AMC Royal Hotel 5 * hotel (Hurghada), reviews of which we are currently considering, their beach holiday here was definitely a success. Tourists note that the hotel complex has at its disposal a magnificent beach. It can be reached from the residential building in just two minutes at a slow pace. The beach here is sandy, large and very clean. There are a sufficient number of sunbeds with mattresses and sun umbrellas. There is also a beach bar with a wide selection of drinks for every taste and a restaurant where you can dine.

Sunset at sea from the beach is very gentle. According to vacationers, 500 meters will have to be walked to the depths. So, small children can play here absolutely safely in the water. However, our compatriots advise tourists to acquire special shoes so as not to injure their feet on the pebbles found at the bottom. This is especially true for the youngest travelers.

Those who wish to swim can swim from the pier. Judging by the reviews of our compatriots, he is very large and well equipped. You can go down from it both in a place with medium depth and where it is already deep enough. Nearby is a coral reef. But its inhabitants are not as bright and diverse as, for example, in Sharm el-Sheikh. However, tourists still enjoyed watching the life of the underwater world.

Entertainment at the hotel

The hotel complex has a large swimming pool with a section for children. Our compatriots really liked the fact that there are always two rescuers at once here. Moreover, they very closely watch the swimmers and are not too lazy to intervene if, for example, children accidentally swim in the deep part of the pool.

Nearby there is a large sun terrace with sun loungers and parasols. There is also a bar. All day long guests of AMC Royal Hotel 5 * (Hurghada) are unobtrusively entertained by animators. They offer those who wish to do aerobics, yoga, step, learn belly dancing, take part in fun contests, play team games - volleyball, water polo - and much more. In the evenings, the animators organize entertaining shows, which guests of the hotel gladly attended. After the end of this program, a disco began.

Those who wish to relax can always visit the spa located in this hotel complex. It offers several types of massage and various beauty treatments. Not all tourists visited the spa center, but those who visited it, judging by their comments, were satisfied with the quality of the services provided.

Family tourists are pleased to note that the hotel has all the conditions for families with children. So, in the afternoon there is a mini club. According to the parents, their children enjoyed visiting him. In the evenings, a disco is held for the kids. The hotel has several well-equipped playgrounds.



Staff

The hotel staff had an extremely positive impression on our compatriots. According to tourists, the staff here are very attentive and friendly. All the staff of the hotel do their job well and are always happy to help guests solve any problem and resolve the problem. In addition, the fact that almost all of the workers here speak Russian was a big plus for our compatriots. Taking into account the comments of our compatriots, it is safe to say that if you chose Hurghada (Egypt) for your vacation, the AMC Royal Hotel 5 * hotel will be a great place to stay.
