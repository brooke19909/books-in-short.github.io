Short description and reviews: Samsung tablets



Six or seven years ago, a simple computer user could not even dream of playing games, working with text, reading books, listening to music, watching movies and much more, not only at home, but everywhere: on the street, in train, in the audience at a lecture, at a meeting. This opportunity appeared thanks to unique devices - tablets. Until recently, which seemed so impossible, today they are produced both by world leaders in computer technology and by little-known companies. The Korean company Samsung is no exception.

For the short existence of this technique, certain reviews have appeared. Samsung tablets are both scolded and praised. Let's try to figure out what is the strength, and what is the weakness of the mentioned laptops. But in order to be as objective as possible, it is necessary to determine what exactly we want to get from a mobile computer. Suppose that for an ordinary user we need a tablet with a screen with a diagonal of at least 10 inches, which will clearly transmit the picture, respond not only to the touch of fingers, but also to the stylus commands, to support the maximum possible number of video and audio formats.
