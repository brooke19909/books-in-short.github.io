Round Lake in Lobny. Description, features, how to get

Kruglye Lake in Lobny is a unique body of water. This is one of the few glacial water reservoirs that has been preserved in the Moscow region. The Meshcherikha river flows into this reservoir, which connects it with Lake Dolgim , located in the Dmitrovsky district. About the features of the Round, the types of rest on it will be discussed in this article.

Description

Krugloye Lake in Lobny is located in an ecologically clean area and belongs to the Myshetskaya Lake Group. The water contains a large amount of radon, which is typical for water bodies of glacial origin. Thanks to this, this object is used as a wellness center, which has its own unique microclimate. Due to the healing properties of water, several health centers have been built nearby.

In these places people who prefer ecotourism love to relax, especially a lot of them can be found here in the summer, when they flee from the heat of a pleasant pond. Also on Lake Krugloye in Lobnya you can often meet fishing lovers.

In the winter season, the frozen expanse of a reservoir is used by athletes who are engaged in snowkiting and train here, also competitions are held here for this sport. Fans of winter fishing are often met on Lake Krugloye in Lobny, fishing on ice.

Features of the reservoir

The water in this natural water reservoir is notable for its purity and transparency. In addition to radon, water contains hydrogen sulfide, and the soil is saturated with minerals and trace elements. It, like water, is used for recreational purposes in boarding houses.

Thanks to clean water, a large number of fish are found here. Often you can find such types of it as:

  • Burbot.
  • Pike.
  • Crucian.
  • Perch.
  • Bream.
  • Carp.

Fish in Lake Krugloye in Lobny is excellently caught at any time of the year. In the summer, regional sport fishing competitions are held here. A feature of this place for fishermen is that the bottom of the reservoir has a different landscape. So, for example, the bottom in some areas is sandy, in others silty, and in others it has mixed soil with vegetation.

Krugloye lake in Lobny: how to get there

From the center of Moscow there can be reached in several ways. You need to travel by car from the city center along Leningradsky Prospekt. Passing the Sokol metro station, turn right onto Leningradskoye Shosse. Upon reaching the Moscow Ring Road, you need to turn right again, and then left, onto the M-11 road. Upon reaching the Sheremetyevo highway, you need to turn left and then right again onto the M-10 road. After that, following the road signs, get to the water body.

Many people ask how to get to Lake Krugloye in Lobna in another way. It is not recommended to build another route to get to this reservoir, as the road will take more time. For example, following the described route, you will spend about 1 hour 10 minutes, while others will take from an hour and a half or more, plus traffic jams.

In the event that you do not want to go by car, you can get to Lake Krugloye in Lobny by bus. From the Belorussky railway station to the Lobnya station, the bus leaves every half an hour. After you arrive in Lobnya, you will have to take bus number 23, which goes to Krugly.

Round Lake in Lobnya: reviews

According to the reviews of tourists who visited the water body in question, these places are great for eco-tourism. It combines local beauty, clean air and a pond. There is an opportunity to relax as well as on your own with a tent, and to settle in one of the wellness centers at which the hotels work.

Holidays on Lake Krugloye attract many fishing enthusiasts. Here they are pleased with both a large amount of catch and a variety of fish. Also, this reservoir of water attracts connoisseurs of this type of recreation with the opportunity to fish at any time of the year.

Tourists who prefer to combine business with pleasure, namely relax and improve their health, recommend these places. There is an opportunity not only to enjoy the beauties of nature, but also to heal in one of the health centers located next to the water body.

We can unequivocally conclude that Lake Krugloye in Lobny is a unique place for relaxation. Here you can relax with the whole family, enjoying the amazing beauty of these places. You can go fishing or use the services of one of the wellness centers. Having once visited these beautiful places, you will definitely want to come back here again.
