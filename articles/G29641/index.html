Documents

When planning a trip from St. Petersburg to Norway or following a different route, you need to have a number of documents with you. In addition to a passport, you will need to apply for a visa. It can be long-term, short-term or transit. For traveling around the country, it is better to draw up the second version of documents. This is a Schengen visa, which is issued for travel.

Traveling to Finland, Sweden, Norway requires a standard visa. It is received by citizens of any country. To get it, you need to collect a number of documents. A questionnaire is filled out, which can be obtained at the visa center or downloaded online. Be sure to get a passport. You also need to prepare a photo of 3.5 x 4.5 cm. A prerequisite for obtaining a visa is proof of the financial security of the tourist. To find out the amount you need to have in your account, you need to multiply the number of days that you plan to spend in this country by 50 euros.

Provide data on financial solvency is necessary in a standard form. It is necessary to take an accounting certificate from work (details, salary and position are indicated). A bank statement or letter from the sponsor is given. The second option is suitable for those who do not have an adequate income for such a trip. In this case, a job certificate and account statement are not needed.

You also need to draw up an insurance policy in the amount of more than 30 thousand euros, make a copy of a regular passport (all pages, including blank pages). You should also provide copies of tickets or a document on their reservation.

To obtain a visa, you must write down the route of the trip in writing. Information on where the tourist will stay for the night is indicated here. You need to confirm this with a copy of the reservation at the hotel or hostel. If you plan to travel by car, prepare documents for the vehicle. You also need to draw up a "green card".

If a person has real estate in Norway, you must provide a copy of the ownership document. Relatives of citizens living in this country may present an invitation sent by their loved ones. For children, it is required to present a birth certificate, parental consent to travel (if the child travels without them or with only one parent).

Application forms are also filled out in English or Norwegian. You need to write clearly. Otherwise, the document will need to be redone.

Some nuances of paperwork

When planning a travel route in Norway, much attention should be paid to the design of documentation. You can apply for a visa at a travel agency, consulate or visa center. A standard fee will be required. He is 35 euros. However, you should be prepared for the fact that during the preparation of such documentation there will be additional costs. The cost of obtaining the necessary documents can be about 100 euros.

A visa is issued free of charge for students, relatives of Norwegian citizens, people with disabilities, scientists, diplomats.

For residents of the Murmansk, Arkhangelsk regions, obtaining documents is carried out according to a simplified scheme. In this case, it is not required to receive an invitation from citizens living in Norway. For people registered in these areas, it is possible to get a multivisa at the first submission of documents. This document is also called a "Pomeranian visa."

When planning a trip to Norway, you need to know the reasons why a visa refusal may come. First of all, it may be doubts about the financial viability of the tourist. Also, a visa is refused if the application form is incorrectly completed. You cannot make mistakes, write inaudible. The application form must be completed exclusively for obtaining a visa to Norway.

A visa is refused if non-exhaustive information is provided, if there are recorded cases of violation of the rules for using this document. Need to get an interview. A visa may be refused if the person is nervous during the conversation. Also, they can refuse to visit this country if there are refugee relatives.

Please note that it is impossible to cross the Norwegian border without a visa. This document is required.

When to plan a trip?

Many tourists are interested in the question of when it is better to plan a trip to this country. You can come here at any time of the year. The country is located in the northern latitudes. However, the harsh climate requires special planning of a trip from Norway to a tourist. Video travels around the country, as well as photos will remind you of the unforgettable beauties of this mysterious area.

Since many tourists go to this country to see the Northern Lights, they go to the polar regions. This beautiful natural phenomenon can be observed all year round. Only the weather (for obvious reasons) should be clear. In most parts of the country, winters are cold, snowy and dark. The south and west coast at this time of the year becomes rainy. Some tips

Traveling to Norway will give a lot of positive impressions. Go to this country is, despite its high cost. It is a safe, peaceful journey. The beauty of nature, developed tourist infrastructure will not go unnoticed by the traveler. Here you can not be afraid that a bag accidentally left somewhere will disappear without a trace with money and documents. All things will be returned to the owner.

Although Norway is considered an expensive country, tourists can always hope for discounts. They are arranged in grocery supermarkets, on the railway. You can buy a tourist card and travel for several days, without worrying about the cost of excursions, travel by public transport.
