LCD "New Ostrovtsy": customer reviews, infrastructure, the developer, how to get there

Moscow is a multi-million city, a city of wide opportunities. Many change their small provincial cities to the opportunity to touch all the advantages of a full-fledged metropolitan life. It would seem: good work, a lot of opportunities for organizing leisure activities, personal growth, but there is one problem - the lack of their own living space. Housing issue - what comes first in not only for visitors, but also for Muscovites. Of course, at first you can rent a house: an apartment or a room. But many of us know from experience how expensive this pleasure is. Sooner or later, everyone will face the choice: rent housing for life or acquire their own housing. And here, most likely, many will choose the second option.





Why Moscow suburbs?

Of course, buying an apartment in Moscow, even on the terms of the most profitable mortgage lending, is an impermissible luxury for many, even a pipe dream. That is why residential complexes of the Moscow Region are so popular at present. Geographically, they belong to the Moscow region, due to which it is possible to reduce the cost per square meter. But in fact, due to transport accessibility and developed infrastructure, not a single resident will feel discomfort during their stay. We want to bring to your attention an excellent project - LCD "New Ostrovtsy". Customer reviews will help to make the most objective review of the complex and evaluate the conditions that the developer offers modern residents and potential buyers of the apartment.

About the area

For the implementation of the project was chosen Ramensky district, Ostrovtsy. It is located southeast of Moscow. On its territory is not only the rather famous city of Ramenskoye, but also the famous science city of Zhukovsky. In the past few years, real estate in this area has gained tremendous popularity. And there are quite objective reasons for that. Modern residents want to buy an apartment near Moscow, but at the same time live in a clean, quiet and comfortable area.





The social sphere and transport accessibility of the Ramensky district do not cause any complaints. If you have been thinking about purchasing an apartment for a long time, the New Ostrovtsy residential complex will be excellent options for you. Customer reviews, first of all, focus on its good location in relation to the capital. But we will prove within the framework of this material that this is far from the only advantage of the project. Most of the buildings have already been commissioned, which allows us to summarize the first results.

about the project

Surely everyone is interested in what is the LCD "New Ostrovtsy". The project description focuses on the concept that formed its basis. The developer tried to create a large-scale development project, which implies the presence of not only residential real estate, but also the necessary infrastructure, a kind of "city in a city."

The project provides for the construction of 26 buildings with variable number of storeys. Currently, most of the houses are commissioned and put into operation. Completion is scheduled for the second quarter of 2019.

Developer

"RTD-Group" is the company that owns the project of the residential complex "New Ostrovtsy". The developer is quite well-known in Moscow and the Moscow region, on his account dozens of successfully implemented projects. This is a large-scale construction holding, represented by several companies, which allows for a full range of construction work, from development and design to service of the buildings commissioned. In his projects, the developer uses exclusively advanced technologies and innovative materials, which makes it possible to build high-quality and reliable houses that are in special demand in the market.





Technology

For the construction of residential buildings, the monolithic-panel technology so popular these days was chosen. The basis is a monolithic reinforced structure finished with panel slabs, giving the building the necessary external aesthetics.

In order to isolate the apartments from noise from the street, two-chamber double-glazed windows of European production were used. Many people believe that panel houses are deprived of comfort, cannot boast of the necessary heat and sound insulation. But such houses were erected in Soviet times, today, thanks to unique materials and technologies, it is possible to endow each apartment in an apartment building with much-needed comfort.

Transport accessibility

LCD "New Ostrovtsy" (Moscow region) is located only 13 km from the Moscow Ring Road in the direction of the Novoryazanskoye Highway. Currently, it is under reconstruction, it is planned to expand the highway, erect new transport interchanges that will relieve it of traffic jams and make the way to work and home as comfortable as possible. If with car owners everything is more or less clear, then what about all those potential buyers who do not have a personal car? Will they also be comfortable living in the residential complex "New Ostrovtsy"? How to get to the complex? We will try to answer these questions.





You can get to the capital by public transport directly from the complex: bus and minibus stops are within walking distance of residential buildings. The first residents of new buildings note transport accessibility as one of the main advantages of the project.

True, there are no railway or metro stations nearby, which simply does not leave residents a choice and strengthens traffic in this direction. Today, residents of the Ramensky district (Ostrovtsy) can reach the capital exclusively by bus or minibus, plying to the Kotelniki metro station. In the near future, additional public transport routes will be launched in the direction of the complex, which will only increase comfort.

Infrastructure

What else can offer buyers LCD "New Ostrovtsy". The infrastructure of the complex is another of its strengths. Two schools for elementary and senior classes, designed for 1650 students have already been commissioned. Parents do not have to take their children to the school every day, because even first-graders will be able to get to it on their own. For the smallest tenants, two kindergartens with a swimming pool will be built on the territory of the complex. Real professionals will work here, engaged not only in upbringing, but also in the physical and mental development of the kids. Parents will not have to look for additional circles and sections for their children - everything will be available on the territory of the kindergarten.





Sports development

The Moscow government is devoting huge funds to the development of sports in the capital. No exception, and LCD "New Ostrovtsy". Customer reviews of apartments that have already settled in, emphasize that the complex has so many sports fields that there is no need to visit fitness centers and sports sections.

More than 15 playgrounds and sports fields that meet all modern requirements, a hockey court, a football field, as well as a sports club with a swimming pool - this will help maintain an active lifestyle.

Parking

Almost every family today has a car, in some families every family member has it, which is why the issue of availability of parking spaces is very relevant. The builder faced a difficult task: to provide a sufficient number of parking spaces on the territory of such a large-scale project, thereby isolating the yards from through traffic. Five modern car parks with a video surveillance and heating system, designed for 3800 cars, are a great alternative to garages, an opportunity not only to park your car, but also protect it from aggressive weather conditions.





Flats

Many note that in the LCD "New Ostrovtsy" apartments for every taste. The project provides for one-, two- and three-room solutions of improved layout. Everyone will find housing for themselves. Newlyweds are increasingly paying attention to small studios and one-room apartments, and for families with young children, spacious two- and three-room apartments will be an excellent option.





Due to the correct zoning of the space, it was possible to create the most comfortable conditions for each tenant. The basis of all planning decisions was the concept of separation of zones - common and relaxation. This is a great solution for large and friendly families, where everyone lives in their own mode.

Beautification

A picturesque, rather green and well-groomed area is something that all residents emphasize. Within walking distance are three gorgeous ponds, which have already become a favorite place for organizing picnics and outdoor recreation. If you choose this complex for your stay, you do not have to look for a place to relax in spring or summer. For the convenience of residents, it is planned to organize full-fledged barbecue areas, build comfortable gazebos, organize a rental point for boats, fishing tackle - one could only dream of such a while ago. The developer also envisaged the construction of several squares and parks; currently, active improvement and landscaping of the complex is underway so that every resident feels the delights of suburban life.

Terms of purchase

If you are limited in means, you cannot afford an expensive option in the capital, an apartment in the New Ostrovtsy residential complex will be a really great option for you. Customer reviews confirm that this is a great investment, an investment in your future. The cost of apartments in the complex starts at 1.2 million per studio - no other developer can offer such prices. For this money, you will get not only your own apartment in an excellent residential quarter, but also access to its developed infrastructure.





Today, the developer offers favorable conditions for the acquisition of apartments, mortgage programs from major banks. Moreover, housing can be purchased here with the involvement of maternity capital and military mortgages.
