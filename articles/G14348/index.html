A bit about ADAS

What does the ADAS system in Xiaomi gadgets notify (on the example of the 70mai Dash Cam Pro Midrive D02):

  • about the beginning of movement in front of a standing car (helps traffic jams for not very attentive drivers);
  • violation of a safe distance approaching cars;
  • about the approach of the marking line left or right.

All three are disabled due to uselessness:

  1. In the first case, due to the frequency of operation. In traffic, traffic moves in jerks. Accordingly, as soon as the driver in front is moving forward by half a meter, an alert will follow, and so on throughout the traffic jam. Values ‚Äã‚Äãare zero, therefore it is turned off.
  2. In the second - due to the rare observance of a safe distance.
  3. The third case is suitable for truckers or other drivers over long distances: it prevents falling asleep while driving due to the monotonous landscape before your eyes. Notification of the approach of the marking line brings to the senses. In the conditions of the city it turns off due to a lot of interference: trees, poles and traffic lights increase the frequency of false positives.

The following functions are popular: notification of an uncontrolled start of movement (for example, when a vehicle accidentally lifted from the hand brake and rolled downhill), a parking camera and an emergency services warning system about an accident.

Verdict

According to reviews of Xiaomi DVRs, the line of models was conceived for the maximum convenience of motorists: Wi-Fi, a camera with a wide viewing angle, stealth from the street, an intelligent help system ADAS, G-sensor and many other features.

Here are just purchased, focusing on: price, dimensions and ease of installation. After the acquisition, the driver pays attention to the quality of the video. The remaining "buns" remain unclaimed after the detection of incorrect operation.

Calibration of the G-sensor is difficult due to the lack of a Russian-language interface: everything has to be mastered by the icons and firmware in English. Low sensitivity of the shock sensor will lead to loss of video with the details of an accident, and high - to frequent false positives (sharp turns, braking, or bumps).

If an electronic accelerometer is not needed (for example, the driver lives in sparsely populated areas) or the design does not provide for calibration, it is simply evaporated from the system board. It turns out that the funds for the gadget were wasted.

The above products from Xiaomi do not work on the determination of devices for fixing the high-speed mode. For this purpose, motorists choose the Vizant 750st DVR-Radar: works with emissions of type K, Ultra K, Ka, X and Ultra X.
