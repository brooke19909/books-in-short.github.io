Bouquet of satin ribbons with your own hands. Roses, satin ribbon tulips

Today we will tell and show you how to create a bouquet of satin ribbons with your own hands. The main advantage of colors from satin ribbons is their durability. They will never fade and will delight their beauty for quite a long time.





Satin Ribbon Tulips

This is a spring flower that can easily be made from such an affordable material. Please yourself and loved ones with this beautiful piece of spring. To create it does not require any special skills, so you will certainly cope with the task.

To create tulips from satin ribbons, we need the following materials:

  1. Needle.
  2. Satin ribbons of different colors.
  3. Scissors.
  4. Thread.

Tulip Creation Procedure

Work order:

  1. First of all, satin ribbons should be folded together with the wrong sides to each other. The ribbon located on top will be the inside of the tulip, and the bottom will be the outside.
  2. Bend both strips at an angle of 90 degrees and fasten everything with a clip or invisible.
  3. You do not need to remove the clip, but again it is worth bending the ribbon. Fold the top down.
  4. Flip the composition to the left at a 90 degree angle.
  5. Fold the upper right piece of strip and fasten.
  6. Repeat the previous paragraph again.
  7. Repeat steps 4‚Äì6.
  8. Flip the song to the other side.
  9. Trim the excess piece of base material so that you get a square.
  10. Sew the threads marked with the dashed line that formed at the clamping point.
  11. Now pull the thread.
  12. Expand the resulting flower.

You can make tulips using another method. You can even make several flowers and create a whole bunch of satin ribbons with your own hands.





Satin ribbon roses

Creating roses from satin stripes is a simple process. It will not be difficult for needlewomen to make of them a real masterpiece. You can collect a whole bunch of roses from satin ribbons. They can be used as an interior item or for decoration of a festive celebration.

To create a solid rose, we need:

  • Scissors.
  • Long satin ribbon.
  • Threads are the same color as the ribbon.
  • Needle.

How to make a rose

The procedure for creating a fabric rose is quite simple:

  1. Grasp the edge of the satin ribbon and bend it at a 45-degree angle. Remember to leave the ponytail, but small. The place of the fold must be sheathed with thread of the same color as the ribbon itself.
  2. Now form a triangle. Bend the strip, only now on yourself, in order to get a geometric shape. Leave a small ponytail again and trim.
  3. Bending again. Do it as if in a circle, but do not be surprised if you get a square. The tape should cover the tail remaining after the first bend. Sew again. Make another fold.
  4. Turn the resulting square so that the free part remains from the bottom, and continue to work. The tail that you got at the very beginning of the work is now visible on the right side of the square. You should turn the product so that the ribbon lies on your fingers.
  5. Bend the tape into a square at the same angle, but do not stitch it, but simply fix it with your fingers.
  6. These squares will be the petals of your rose. Consequently, the splendor of a flower depends only on their number.
  7. Form a core from the remaining piece.
  8. Bend the petals slightly.

You can make a bouquet of satin ribbons with your own hands using the resulting roses.



We create a bouquet of satin ribbons with our own hands

The main materials for creating a bouquet are:

  1. For one rose, you need a 65 cm ribbon, the width of which should be 3 cm.
  2. Ruler.
  3. Stapler.
  4. Scissors.




To make a bouquet of satin ribbons, you need:

  1. Cone for a bouquet.
  2. A ball with a diameter of 10 cm, preferably it is made of foam. It needs to be cut in half.
  3. Glue gun.
  4. Wooden round handle.
  5. Rhinestones and a narrow ribbon for decoration.
  6. Satin ribbon 4 cm wide.
  7. 25 collected from ribbons of flowers.

The procedure for creating a bouquet

Making bouquets of satin ribbons is also very simple and even a beginner will succeed, you can rest assured. Above all, stick to the points below:

  1. In the foam, you need to cut a recess in width equal to a wooden handle and a depth of 1.5 cm.
  2. Using a glue gun, glue the handle to the foam.
  3. Extend the base to the size of the handle.
  4. On its circumference from the upper side we apply hot glue and insert and press the foam handle. Wait for it to stick.
  5. You should get a pretty pretty foundation.
  6. Seal the bottom of the handle with a white ribbon.
  7. Tape the base of the cone with a satin ribbon, while making folds.
  8. Seal the entire handle.
  9. Glue roses from the top so that the base is not visible.
  10. Decorate the handle with a narrow ribbon and rhinestone.

Your bouquet is ready!

The bride's bouquet

This is the most important element in the image of the bride. The shape for such a bouquet is a ball. This day is the most important for the girl, and what could be the best reminder of him than a bride's bouquet of satin ribbons? To create a bouquet, we need:

  • White ribbon - 10 cm.
  • Multi-colored ribbons - from 2 to 6 cm.
  • A variety of pins, with beads at the end.
  • Beads that will be in harmony with the ribbons.
  • Impervious canvas.
  • Super glue.
  • Newspaper.
  • Cord.
  • Needles.
  • Wooden stick.
  • Scissors.
  • Ruler.
  • Threads.
  • Pencil.
  • Compass.

Getting to work

The very first thing you should do is prepare a round base for the bouquet. This is the main part to which all other elements will be attached.

To create the foundation, you must do the following:

  1. Form a ball from the newspaper. First you need to crumple the newspaper. The diameter of the resulting circle should not exceed 10 cm.
  2. Keeping a round shape, you need to rewind it with your cord.
  3. Make a recess for the stick in it and fill it with glue.
  4. Mount the handle and wait for it to dry.
  5. Wrap the wand with a cord.
  6. Decorate the open end of the handle with tape.
  7. Wind the entire handle with satin ribbon.

At this stage, the process of creating the basis for the bouquet is completed, and we proceed to its further design. To do this, we will need to glue all the roses prepared in advance to the ball. Here you need to give free rein to your imagination, but the main thing is not to overdo it. You can use all kinds of rhinestones, beads and other elements to decorate the bride‚Äôs bouquet.

Another way to create a bouquet

Materials:

  • Disk.
  • Polyurethane foam.
  • Satin ribbon.
  • Plastic pipe 15 cm.
  • Studs and beads.
  • Scissors.
  • Candle or lighter.

Work order:

  1. Squeeze a small amount of foam onto the disc and let it dry.
  2. Trim any excess.
  3. Glue the pipe with hot glue to the disc.
  4. Using the same glue, attach the roses to the base. The main thing is to glue the flowers to the base as tightly as possible.
  5. Glue the disc around the edge with lace.
  6. The center is best glued with a simple white ribbon.
  7. Do not allow gaps to form.
  8. Make ribbons of bows.
  9. They need as much as the resulting gaps.
  10. Wrap the handle with ribbon and decorate as you wish.
