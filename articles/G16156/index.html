"Coronelli" - recreation center: overview, description and reviews of tourists

"Coronelli" - a recreation center for the whole family, located in the village of the resort type Beregovoe, 3 km from Feodosia, on the Crimean peninsula. The close proximity to the popular Black Sea Golden Beach is one of the main advantages of the base location.





Description

A small historical reference: the name "Coronelli" the recreation center received from the former name of the village of Borovoye - Koronellovka, given in honor of the owner of the village, a nobleman with Spanish roots Antonio Coronelli. The village was presented to him by Catherine II at the end of the 18th century. There is still a street in Beregovoy in honor of Coronelli.

It offers a calm and measured beach holiday, combined with opportunities to engage in active sports. Near the base there is a developed infrastructure (shops, supermarkets, market) and a bus stop.

Nutrition

Guests can eat in the dining room, which operates from early morning until late in the evening and provides its customers with full, balanced three meals a day. A professional team of chefs is trying to bring their dishes to perfection. You can choose dishes suitable for children, vegetarians and those on a diet.

On the territory there is also a wonderful cafe-bar, where a wide variety of both refreshing and alcoholic drinks, salads and snacks, desserts and fruits.

In addition, at the request of the tourist, food can be brought to the room. It is very convenient for travelers with small children. Standard 1 rooms have cutlery.

Services

The creation of a rich infrastructure is aimed at meeting all the possible needs of holidaymakers at Coronelli. The recreation center has a beautiful pool with sea water, which is nice to swim in the midday heat.





You can just sit on the terraces or in the gazebos among the fragrant plants.

Fans of outdoor activities can play volleyball on a specially equipped playground or football on a good field. It is also possible to play Russian or American billiards, tennis. For an additional fee, small fishing tours in the Gulf of Feodosia are organized, as well as diving master classes for beginners and diving for experienced divers.

Younger guests will be pleased with the magnificent playground with slides, sandpit and swings. For motorists traveling on their own cars, a secure parking lot is provided. There is an equipped left-luggage office.

Women who care about their beauty are invited to visit the cosmetology center, which provides hairdressing services, manicures and nail extensions, face, body and hair masks, skin cleansing and rejuvenation procedures, etc. Umbrellas and fans, mini refrigerators, teapots are available for rent. .

The administration of the recreation center is happy to organize transfer and excursion services along the most interesting routes for its guests.

Attractions nearby

Coronelli, a recreation center in Beregovoy, is located near the unique Scythian settlement, which archaeologists date back to the 4th century BC. e. Here you can see ancient tools and household items, jewelry, coins. There is also another parking lot for people of the Bronze Age, where clay tiles and fragments of jugs are shown.





The main attraction is the Golden Beach with clear water and a small yellow sand, equipped with sunbeds and umbrellas. On the beach there are many water activities. Just a few minutes drive (by sea or bus) - Feodosia. The most popular tourist attractions of the city:

  • The majestic Genoese fortress with a tower of St. Konstantin.
  • A bright and asymmetric building in oriental style is the Stamboli Palace and Cottage.
  • Chapel of St. Elijah and the lighthouse of the same name.
  • An interesting Karadag reserve with a landscape of extraordinary beauty and bays.
  • Ottoman-era mosque Mufti Jali, built at the beginning of the XVII century.
  • Museums of Feodosia (hang gliding, A. Green, money, fish and fishing).
  • Orthodox churches (All Saints, St. Sergius).
  • Picture Gallery I.K. Aivazovsky with beautiful sea masterpieces.

In addition, the city has many entertainment venues, markets, beautiful fountains and beaches, a wonderful water park.
