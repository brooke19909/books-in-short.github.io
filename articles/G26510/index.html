What is maternity money? How to calculate maternity benefits: step by step instructions

When going on maternity leave and caring for a child, a woman is entitled to payments, so she needs to know the rules for calculating them. Employers must also comply with the law on the payment of benefits to such employees. Maternity money is calculated according to a special formula, which is approved by law.

Pregnant women have many benefits. They are not allowed to be fired at work. At night, working in this position is prohibited, it can only be with the consent of the employee herself. The employer has no right to burden with duties. A woman's activities during pregnancy should not be dangerous.





The concept

Maternity leave - maternity leave. Usually, the term "decree" means the time of motherhood. Women take leave until the child is 3 years old. By law, 2 types of allowances are due:

  • maternity;
  • for child care.

They correspond with the types of recreation that are prescribed in Art. 255 and 256 of the Labor Code of the Russian Federation. Given the nature of these payments, it will be easier to navigate in the calculations.

Who can get paid?

The payment of maternity money is fixed by law. Given its norms, the following groups of citizens can receive benefits:

  • women working under an employment contract;
  • full-time students studying at universities and colleges;
  • women who have lost their jobs due to the liquidation of the employer or the reduction of the organization.

In addition to maternity payments, which are provided at a time, you can receive financial assistance during the execution of a child of 1.5 years. This maternity money can be obtained for a larger circle of persons:

  • Russian citizens, including those who live in other countries because of work;
  • foreigners who live in Russia;
  • stateless persons with compulsory insurance.

The law says that there is a right to choose a person who will look after the child until the age of 1.5 years. When the vacation ends, the mother must decide whether to continue the vacation to care for the baby. A woman has the right to return to work. Relatives or legal guardians may be required to take care of the child. Sometimes it is shared by several people.

The procedure for payments to unemployed women

Non-working women are also paid maternity money. Gold coins, although not provided, but the allowance is 14,497.80 rubles. This amount can be received by all women who do not have a job. How are maternity money accrued? Young mothers have the right to receive monthly payments if the following conditions are met:

The registration procedure for the unemployed involves applying to the place of registration or study for the transfer of payments. To do this, you must provide the documents:

Only after registration will they transfer maternity money. Such payments protect the rights of mothers and children.

Decree increase

Maternity leave may be increased due to the number of days:

  • if the pregnancy is multiple;
  • difficult childbirth;
  • birth of 2 or more children.

When childbirth is difficult, and there is a threat to the life of the baby, then another 16 days are provided. An additional sick leave may also be issued if an additional period for recovery is required. If more than 1 child appears, then the entire period of disability is 194 days.

Decrease Decree

By law, an employer cannot require a pregnant employee to go to work. This period should not be shortened. Therefore, the payment of funds is not determined by the employer, and dismissal is considered illegal.





The vacation period can be reduced due to the desire of the employee herself. When an abortion occurs, you can also go to work. In this case, a sick leave is provided for the time necessary for recovery.

By law, tax is not calculated from maternity allowances . This is evidenced by Article 217 of the Tax Code. Therefore, no one has the right to withhold any percentage.

Special cases
