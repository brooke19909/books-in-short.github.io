How to connect a light sensor for street lighting?

Everyone is trying to come up with a device that will make life much more comfortable. Over the past century, computers, various types of household appliances, and cars have been created. The light sensor for street lighting was no exception . It is necessary for comfortable movement in the dark. Thanks to the potentiometer, the light sensor for street lighting automatically detects the on and off times. Its design also includes adjustment, with which a person can independently set the response time. For people living for a long time in cottages or in private homes, this unit is a very useful purchase.

General information

What is a street light sensor? It has another name - photo relay. The first part of the word (photo) means that it starts to turn on when light rays hit, the second (relay) is a switch.

The principle of operation of the device is quite simple. If a sufficient amount of light does not pass through the sensor, then the contacts are closed and the lighting is turned on. When the sun rises, the equipment automatically shuts off.

Where else use such a device?

It can be installed not only near private houses, but also used in the entrances of multi-story buildings. Regardless of the time of day, the terrain will be illuminated. In addition, the device significantly saves electricity.

Its main purpose is to turn on and off the light. For example, to irrigate the garden every night automatically, the sensor is connected to an irrigation system. Further, everything happens automatically.

It is also used for the smart home system. It is needed to simulate the presence of the owners. When the owner is not in place, the light periodically turns on and off. In addition, the system itself is used for other features of a smart home.

Device

The light sensor for street lighting can be of two types:

  • Built-in is located in the electrical panel.
  • The remote is located near the building.

The last type of photo relay must be durable, not subject to mechanical stress. In addition, it must be airtight and protect against atmospheric precipitation. The principle of operation of this equipment is quite simple. The main thing is that the configuration has built-in and remote sensors. Depending on the degree of illumination of the street, one of them transmits information to a relay located in the electrical panel. When a certain indicator is reached, the electric network is closed and the lighting is turned on.

Any photo relay can be programmed according to the personal preferences of the owner of the site or premises. The device works from a regular consumer network with a voltage of 220 V, and the current strength in the switched circuit is more than 10 A. The process of illumination depends on the adjustment. Light sensor for street lighting: connection

The wiring diagram for this device is either on the packaging or in the instruction manual. Derived wiring have a different color. This is done in order to avoid an incorrect connection. Each of the three wires has its purpose. You can only guess about them with the help of colors. So, the black wire indicates the phase, green - zero, and red - the phase to the light fixture.





How to connect a light sensor for street lighting? Before starting the connection, you must read the instructions. The wires are connected using a junction box. It is placed side by side on the wall and is attached via brackets.

To connect a switched load, voltage must be applied continuously. To do this, you need to connect the green wire, which indicates zero, to a regular consumer network with a voltage of 220 V. Such equipment has a current strength of more than 10 A. With a high power of switched load, it is necessary to use more powerful switches to control street lighting.

How to make a sensor for lighting yourself

To assemble the simplest lighting equipment, you need to purchase an LED that performs the function of a sensing element, resistors, instruments for regulating sensitivity, the necessary wires. You also need to choose a protective cover that will protect the photo relay from mechanical stress and precipitation. For its fastening, you need brackets and bolts of a certain diameter, as well as a prepared place.





A do-it-yourself light sensor for street lighting should be assembled according to the same scheme as a conventional photo relay. Here the main element will be a diode. You can also use the photodiode, which is located in a computer mouse. When it falls on a given element of light, an electrical circuit begins to close. The signal comes through a power source and turns on a light fixture located indoors or outdoors. Such devices are used for street lighting of streets, summer cottages, garages and other places.
