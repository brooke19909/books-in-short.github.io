Phone Jinga: reviews, photos

Hong Kong-based cell phone and smartphone maker Jinga is just starting to gain popularity in the Russian market. Its products are mainly budget models, which are distinguished by a good ratio of price and quality. Each of them is of high build quality.

Are the Jinga mobile phones as good as the creators say? Reviews of some models and brief reviews are presented in this article.





Jinga Simple F115

It looks like a regular push-button telephone from the past. The screen is small, non-touch, from such users must have already managed to wean. However, turning this model in your hands, you will come across a first surprise: the camera is located on the rear panel. However, the camera with a resolution of 0.08 megapixels is of little use. It is useful only for creating icons for contacts in the address book and wallpaper.

The benefit of the camera is not limited to additional functionality. The phone is equipped with two SIM card slots and supports microSD memory cards (up to 8 GB). All slots are under the battery, that is, to get to them, the battery will have to be removed every time. The date and time will be confused, which is not very convenient, but also not catastrophic, because for copying files the phone can always be connected to a computer via a USB cable.

At the top there is a flashlight that turns on by long pressing the 0 key. The keyboard for this must be unlocked. The phone also boasts multimedia features such as an audio player and radio. Without headphones, they do not work, but, anyway, the speakers are not a strong point of the device. A few words about the screen: 1.77 inches, resolution - 120 x 160 pixels, color.

Overall, the Jinga Simple F115 should be a worthwhile purchase. He copes with his basic functions with a bang, the price for it is more than pleasant - 800 rubles.





Jingo IGO L2

By purchasing this model, you will receive a fully functional smartphone with the Android operating system for only three thousand rubles. The design is not unusual - a typical smartphone. The model is sold in black and white.

Now about the filling: the processor frequency is 1 gigahertz, RAM - 512 megabytes. Built-in Flash memory - 4 gigabytes, which can be increased with a microSD card. Headphones are not supplied. By the way, users complain that choosing a suitable headset is very difficult.

Separately highlight the screen. Specifically, in this model there are no complaints about it. But the battery performance, frankly, is not impressive.





Jingo IGO L4

Unlike their Chinese counterparts, Jinga does not seek to make phones look like Apple devices. If anyone recalls this Jinga phone (photos are presented in the article), then, rather, Sony Xperia. The same angular design inherent in the notorious line of smartphones, slim body (about eight millimeters), dark tones.

You can find fault with the screen: the resolution is low (960 x 540 pixels), it is extremely small for a five-inch display. The sensor works as it should.

You should not expect too much from Jingo IGO L4. Do not forget that the phone is primarily budget, but for its price is really good.





Jingo IGO M1

Budget smartphone based on Android. It is equipped with everything necessary for a modern gadget, this is a camera, Bluetooth, GPS, two SIM cards, support for memory cards. Android version is 4.4.2. The smartphone is not the weakest processor and good RAM (512 megabytes) for such a price. The creators of the device made sure to reduce the price as much as possible: simple, cheap packaging, no headphones included. As a result, the Jinga IGO M1 phone costs 2500 rubles.

4 inch screen. Its quality is not the highest. The quality of the camera is also frustrating. Most likely, having made a couple of shots for the sample, you will forever refuse to use it. But for emergencies, the camera is definitely useful, so it's still not worth discussing the feasibility of its presence in the smartphone.

Appearance is unlikely to surprise you: exactly the same design with many other smartphones. The main thing - the assembly is done in good faith. When squeezing, nothing creaks, everything is tight, without gaps.

In standby mode, the smartphone will work for about 240 hours, but with the active use of the battery, it will last no more than on a daylight hours. In this regard, there are better options on the market, including among budget models.





Jinga basco l3

This model is more powerful than the previous one, but its price is almost three times higher. The smartphone runs on the Android 4.4.2 platform. KitKat. Display - 5 inches, resolution 1280 x 720 pixels. Two cameras: a 2-megapixel front and an 8-megapixel camera on the back. Quad-core 1.3 MHz processor, RAM - 1 GB. Support for two SIM cards.

Build quality, again, at a very good level. In addition, now special attention is paid to design: the outlines are smooth, the color of the plastic is not boring black, but a bluish texture under the turned pebbles. With the battery, everything is still bad: it is hardly enough for half a day.

Here is a pretty remarkable Jinga phone. Reviews about him are generally good. If users complain, then a weak battery and some software flaws, which, however, can be fixed by firmware.

Jinga HOTZ M1

The newest model of the Hong Kong manufacturer and the most expensive at the moment. Its cost varies from seven to nine thousand rubles.

The filling of this smartphone is very serious, the performance is impressive, and both cameras are really good. But the appearance compared to the Jinga Basco L3 has become worse - this is the only thing that gives out a budget model in this device.

The chagrin of design will quickly evaporate as soon as you start using the Jinga HOTZ M1. The developers, finally, took into account the wishes and gloriously worked on the battery. Now, battery life is one of the nicest features. Judging by the reviews, the battery is able to hold a charge of up to four days when using a smartphone in a gentle mode. Very strong for a budget model.

Of the pluses, the screen should also be noted: five inches with HD resolution. IPS-matrix produces no less than 320 ppi.

It is simply impossible to find an equally productive smartphone for such money now, so be sure to take this model with a pencil.
