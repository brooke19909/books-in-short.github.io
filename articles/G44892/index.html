If your friend loves to read, recommend an e-book

Paper books are gradually losing ground to electronic media; more and more devices are available for reading: tablet computers, iPods, smartphones. But there are also devices specially created for reading. Recommend an e-book Book lover friend, if you want to please him.

From the rich assortment that electronics stores offer, it is necessary to choose exactly the model that will be optimally adapted for reading. It should support the maximum number of formats, be safe for the eyes and convenient to use. The best option is a small, compact e-book, which is convenient to hold when reading in your hands.





Stores offer models of electronic readers with a screen diagonal of ten or more inches, but if your friend likes to read on public transport, recommend a smaller e-book and light in weight. For example, a reader with a display of five or six inches is suitable. If a loved one is enthusiastically studying encyclopedias or educational material, then a color tablet computer with Android OS is suitable for him. But an e-book is quite suitable for reading fiction. It is important to know a few basic characteristics of these devices in order not to make a mistake with the choice.





The best option would be an electronic ink e-reader. These include PocketBook e-books. that are great for long trips.

The battery of this device is designed to read eight thousand pages of text without recharging, it saves energy in standby mode. The weight of the reader is one hundred ninety-five grams.
