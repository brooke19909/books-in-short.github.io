DIY crafts from vegetables: original ideas, workshop

Autumn is a wonderful time of the year when nature gives people a rich harvest of vegetables, fruits, berries. This material is not only for culinary delights, but also for the embodiment of creative ideas. Indeed, from the gifts of nature, you can create real handmade masterpieces. Crafts from vegetables can be exhibited at the autumn fair to lure customers. And advertising, as everyone knows, is the engine of trade.

Children also love to create from products. Schools often hold contests for the best DIY vegetable crafts. Works are done by both junior high school students and high school students. Of course, the works of children of different ages will significantly differ in the complexity of the compositions, but both of them can be interesting to the viewer and original.

In our article, we will consider several interesting crafts made from vegetables; both simple and complex works will be presented. We will tell mothers who cannot teach a child to eat vegetables, how to lure a baby with their effective arrangement on a plate. We will also help housewives to learn how to serve vegetables on the festive table in an original way so that they become the decoration of the entire feast.

What is carving?

The art of carving from vegetables and various fruits originated in Asian countries many centuries ago. Now carving is taught to children from 11 years old in all Asian schools. Such a popularity of this art is associated in these countries with a small variety of food. To decorate rice and vegetables, people came up with carving figures and characters from them. Crafts from vegetables visually pleased the eye of the eater.

Residents of Europe were not immediately interested in such a design of dishes, because they did not suffer from uniformity anyway. The table of the European contained not only vegetable food, there was meat, and fish, and poultry. Therefore, more recently, in connection with the development of a network of elite restaurants, culinary art and the need to attract visitors, European masters began to engage in carving.

How can I cut figures from vegetables?

To create crafts from vegetables with your own hands, use a sharp knife. There are elements that are cut with straight scissors, but it is believed that the best tool is a Thai knife. If you decide to make an ornament, cut a bouquet of flowers, then you will need special knives that have V-shaped, square or serrated blades.

In order to create thin plates of vegetables, use a vegetable cutter. When round balls are needed to create a composition, then you need to take a spoon-noisette. There are also knives that make lateral grooves on the peel. But all this will be needed if you seriously decide to do such work in large quantities, for example, if you are a cook in a restaurant.

Fun learning

When preparing a child for school, you can not only cram letters, forcing children to read the ABCs, but also add a playful moment to learning by cutting letters from vegetables. Make them from potatoes. Letters are created voluminous. This task is for the parents of the baby. How to play with such an original alphabet, we will tell further.

Game task number 1. Using ink or carrot juice, you need to print the word spoken by my mother on paper.

Didactic game "What is gone?" A few letters are laid out on the table (up to 10). The child should look at and remember them carefully for a short period of time. Then the baby closes his eyes, and the mother hides one letter. After his eyes open, he must name what was gone. The game develops not only memory and attention, but also helps to remember letters in a playful way.

Task number 2. On the sheets of paper painted any subject picture. Under each of them, the child must print its name.

Task number 3. Collect the word from the letters on the table.

There are a lot of options for drawing up exercises, fantasize, read pedagogical literature, and diversify tasks for your child yourself.

Beginner Tips

Those who want to do carving for themselves, providing a beautiful and original presentation of dishes for holidays or family dinners, can give some important recommendations.

  • Vegetables are best used seasonal. They are more dense and juicy. Their colors are brighter and richer than those that have been stored for a long time.
  • For work, absolutely any vegetables that can be consumed fresh are suitable - tomatoes and cucumbers, carrots and beets, salad peppers of different colors and shapes, radishes, parsley root and celery. In addition to vegetables, fruits and berries can be used.
  • If you create a simple table decoration that will not be eaten, then connect the potatoes and pumpkin, eggplant.
  • So that the potatoes and eggplants do not darken, the slices must be wiped with lemon juice.
  • For autumn crafts from vegetables, it is advisable to take dense vegetables without a soft core. Tomatoes are chosen with the least amount of seeds.

Start small with the simple options given in the article. For the performance of such work does not need special knives, which are expensive. A sharp and thin knife, scissors and a vegetable cutter will suffice. Attract to the crafts of children. It is very exciting!
