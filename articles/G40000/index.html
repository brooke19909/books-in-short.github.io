Slow cooker "Redmond RMC-M4502": reviews, instructions

The Redmond RMC M4502 is a multi-function multicooker with many cooking programs. However, the main feature of this device is the new Multi-Cook function, which allows you to choose both the temperature and the cooking time.

Model Overview and Features

The Multicooker Redmond RMC M4502 has 16 automatic programs and 18 manually configurable. The model is available in two colors: black and white, so you can choose a device that fits any kitchen interior. In addition, the device is decorated with metal inserts with a florid pattern, which gives it a stylish and modern look.





The RMC M4502 is controlled by pressing a few buttons around the LCD. It displays the cooking time, the selected program and temperature.

There is a handle on the lid, thanks to which the multicooker can be moved to another place. Manufacturers also took into account consumer requests about the complexity of washing the device and in the RMC M4502 model eliminated this drawback by equipping the device with a removable inner cover and a valve for releasing steam.

Instrument complete set

In addition to the multicooker itself, the RMC M4502 kit includes 10 devices for ease of use:

  • 180 ml measuring cup;
  • deep fryer basket with a removable handle (provides compact storage);
  • several spoons, or rather 3 (one of which is flat);
  • plastic container for cooking steamed dishes;
  • a five-liter bowl (although the marking ends at 3 liters);
  • forceps for its extraction;
  • holder for spoons (it is attached to the side);
  • power cord;
  • instruction;
  • cookbook "120 recipes" (it has a menu for the smallest).

Manual

The manufacturer provides a Russified user manual and a two-year warranty, which should be written in the warranty card after purchasing the RMC M4502 model. The instructions included in the kit are very detailed and written in several languages, including Russian.

The content immediately catches the eye with the technical specifications, then the section on the operation of the device and a description of each individual program. For example, in the "Cooking" section it is described in detail that this function is suitable for the preparation of friable cereals, buckwheat, rice and other cereals.





The following describes the temperature regimes and subfunctions ("express cooking" or "porridge cooking"). And then the manufacturer gives some practical advice before cooking:

  • rinse cereals until the water becomes clear;
  • Before putting the ingredients in the bowl, it must be greased with butter;
  • strict adherence to proportions is necessary;
  • if in the process of cooking it is planned to use whole milk, then it must be diluted with water 1: 1.

Another instruction contains detailed instructions for the care and washing of the device. For example, cleaning should be done when the device has completely cooled down. To get rid of the sharp smells of seasonings, it is necessary to wipe the inside of the lid and bowl with a 9% vinegar solution, and then boil half a lemon in a slow cooker, turning on the "Steam Fish" program.

They also give tips on cooking and sort out questions, for example, about why a dish burns / boils water / does not bake. There can be many reasons, and the manufacturer tried to answer all the questions and highlighted the ways to solve these problems in a short instruction.

What is the "Redmond RMC-M4502" multicooker capable of?

The device has an auto-heating function to maintain the temperature for 24 hours. This is very convenient: if you cook the same pilaf in the afternoon, it will be warm until evening. There is also a delayed start function. Also a very useful program, thanks to which the ingredients can be put in the bowl in the evening and in the morning breakfast will be ready at the right time.

The Multicooker Redmond RMC M4502 has 16 automatic programs that greatly simplify the cooking process. The device can not only cook, stew and fry, but also bake muffins, pies and cook yoghurts.





You can not remain silent about 3D heating, thanks to which each dish warms up evenly. The heating elements are located on the sides, bottom and on the top cover of the multicooker.

Multi-Cook Function

One of the functions should be discussed separately - this is "Multipovar". This program allows you to make settings manually and select the desired cooking temperature, where 40 degrees minimum, and 160 - maximum. You can also set a timer, the time range of which is configured from five minutes to one hour.

The program allows you to cook yogurts, nuggets, french fries and cottage cheese. In addition, this feature is ideal for experimenters. For example, if you are tired of dishes from the attached cookbook, then "Redmond RMC M4502" provides an excellent opportunity to cook according to your own recipes.

Feature List

  • Deep-frying.
  • Baking bakery products.
  • Sterilization of used dishes.
  • Cooking fondue / yogurt.
  • Proofing the dough.
  • Pasteurization of liquid products;
  • Warming up / making baby food.

Device advantages

The main advantages of the Redmond RMC M4502, according to customers, is the compactness and interesting design of the device. And the functions of maintaining the heat of cooked dishes, the delayed start and the Multi-Cook program are significant advantages.





In addition, a book with 120 recipes serves as a pleasant addition, with which the preparation of each dish turns into pleasure due to the exact doses scheduled and a step-by-step guide.

The disadvantages of the device

Model RMC M4502 collects negative reviews, even despite the fact that the manufacturer tried to eliminate the previously identified shortcomings.

The most common problems of the device are malfunctions in programs and poor assembly, due to which the latch on the cover often breaks. And although the repair service center recognizes such cases as warranty, but due to the lack of spare parts in warehouses, the elimination of this problem can take a month.

In addition, as noted by buyers, rubber seals during operation absorb odors of spices and serve relatively little. It is also inconvenient for many consumers to remove the bowl (which is not equipped with a handle) from the multicooker with tongs.





There is still no condensate collector, and after cooking, the water that has accumulated on the lid drains into the food.

Unfortunately, a lot of complaints are heard towards the delayed start program. The problem is the inaccuracy of time. This applies directly to steaming vegetables, which are steamed later for 15 minutes. Moreover, some consumers are unhappy with the 3D heating function, because the crust of the finished bread is not toasted, and it remains white.
