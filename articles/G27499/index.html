Hotel Karona Resort & Spa 4 *: description and reviews of tourists

Thailand has recently been very popular among tourists. In this picturesque country you can enjoy the beautiful beaches and swimming in the ocean. There are many hotels in the resort town of Phuket, ready to provide their guests with excellent living conditions. The Karona Resort & Spa 4 * hotel is no exception. Here guests are always welcome and make every effort to ensure that guests have only positive memories of the rest.

Location and Description

Hotel Karona Resort & Spa 4 * is located south of the popular Karon Beach. There are many interesting places nearby, including the Kata Porpeang Market, Dino Park Mini Golf , Patong Bay and Kata Yai. The nearest airport is Phuket Airport, 47 km from the property. Eight kilometers from the hotel there are many entertainment centers.

Tourists who visited the Karona Resort & Spa 4 * (Phuket) hotel with enthusiasm speak about its territory. Everything is buried in lush greenery. Guests can enjoy palm trees and other exotic plants. On the territory there are artificial waterfalls, near which it is very pleasant to spend time in solitude. Everywhere well cleaned.



The hotel consists of the main building and 9 houses located on its territory. Near the main building there is an excellent pool with a jacuzzi. Sun beds and umbrellas are always available and are issued to vacationers for free.

Infrastructure

In the Karona Resort & Spa 4 * hotel, you can contact the reception with any questions 24 hours a day. There are almost no Russian-speaking personnel here, but it will work out with an elementary knowledge of English. Here, they react to the requests and complaints of the guests very quickly, trying to eliminate the problems that arise in a matter of minutes.

The hotel has parking, where vacationers can leave their transport. If you wish, you can rent executive cars here. Motoblocks and bicycles are also available for rent, but require a passport as a deposit.



For business people the opportunity to use the services of a business center. The hotel provides assistance in conducting business meetings and negotiations.

Near the reception there is a left-luggage office. There is also a tour desk where you can book fascinating tours to nearby attractions. If necessary, managers can order an airport transfer for a certain price.

Dining at the Karona Resort & Spa 4 *

Guests can eat in the main restaurant, which serves a complimentary breakfast. The menu is quite varied, and vacationers have the opportunity to choose the right dishes. Lunches and dinners in the restaurant are paid. They are quite diverse, but many tourists find prices too high. Meals at restaurants and cafes located a couple of meters from the hotel are much cheaper.



You can eat and quench your thirst in the pool bar. It serves various snacks and many different drinks. Concierge services and room service are available.

The main attraction of the hotel

The spa is the main attraction of the Karona Resort & Spa 4 * hotel. The reviews of tourists who visited it confirm the high qualification of the specialists working here. Vacationers can order various cosmetic services at the center, undergo a massage or aromatherapy course.

Prices for spa services are quite attractive. Guests visiting massage rooms in the city claim that they had to pay a lot more for such services. The same situation with the bathhouse or sauna, which can also be used in the spa.

Beach vacation

Guests of the Karona Resort & Spa 4 * hotel can access the city beach, located 200 meters away. Sun beds and umbrellas here are issued for an additional fee. The beach is sandy, pretty well maintained. The water is clean and warm. Swimming here is comfortable for tourists of any age.

You can eat during your stay on the beach in the bars working here. Tourists note the good quality of the cuisine and the variety on the menu. Prices will also please holidaymakers.

Description of rooms

All rooms of the Karona Resort & Spa 4 * hotel leave a pleasant impression with guests. They are quite spacious. The interior is made in bright, saturated colors, which creates a very cozy atmosphere. The furniture in the rooms is not always new, but is in good condition.



The rooms are cleaned daily and very carefully. Towels are changed daily, and bed linen is changed twice a week.

Each room has a TV, but it is impossible to watch Russian channels. A minibar is also present in every room. For drinks available in it, you must pay extra. There is also a coffee maker and tea making facilities, a fridge. Air conditioning in the rooms works well. The bathroom has a shower, hairdryer and toiletries.

Guest reviews

Tourists who had the pleasure of visiting the Karona Resort & Spa 4 * hotel often leave positive reviews. The admiration is caused by the hotel gardens, comfortable and clean rooms. Guests are also pleased with the beach and swimming.

The presence of theft at the hotel leaves a negative impression. Some guests claim that even valuables disappeared from the safes located in the rooms. The administration of the hotel in such cases does not meet the guests and is in no hurry to find the thief.

Summing up, we can say that the Karona Resort & Spa 4 * hotel provides excellent conditions for living at a relatively low cost. Despite some shortcomings, you can spend your vacation quite pleasantly here.
