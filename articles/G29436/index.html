Autumn crafts: owl from plastic bottles

From a variety of plastic containers: bottles, boxes, food packaging - you can make a lot of interesting, useful and beautiful crafts. Plastic is inexpensive, and often costs us almost free of charge, which makes its use as a consumable for hand-made products pleasant and unprofitable. All that is needed to make unusual crafts is a good mood and a little imagination.

In this article, we want to share with you ideas on how to create a craft called Owl from plastic bottles. There is nothing super complicated, and the result of your work will delight you and your loved ones!





Owl made of plastic bottles: step by step instructions

To create this decorative decoration you will need various materials:

  • 2 liter clear plastic bottle;
  • acrylic paints;
  • brushes;
  • construction stapler ;
  • awl;
  • twine.

Mark the bottle with a marker: draw 3 lines, dividing the container into 4 unequal parts. The first line should be 2 cm below the cover, the second - 10-12 cm, the third 10-12 cm above the bottom. On the planned lines cut the container. For crafts, you will need two of the four parts - the bottom and the one below the neck. Feel free to throw out other fragments. The bottom of the container will serve as the head of the bird, and the "top" - the body. Now with the help of a stapler we carefully fasten two fragments. Now an owl from plastic bottles has acquired its body. The second stage of the work is the use of paints. It is advisable to purchase acrylic for the design of the product, since they fit perfectly on the plastic surface. How to color the Owl craft from plastic bottles? The photo shows a possible option. Of course, you can show your imagination and make your own, unique bird color. After applying the paint, let the product dry thoroughly. Everything, your craft is almost ready!

If you plan to place an owl indoors, you can make a small house or perch for your bird from twigs, pine and spruce cones, rowan berries or viburnum. The special charm of this composition will add bright yellow, red, crimson or orange leaves. Decorate any home with similar original crafts from plastic bottles. The owl will now delight you and create a special pleasant atmosphere in your home. A charming bird will take pride of place in your apartment, attracting the attention of all household members.





If your owl from plastic bottles is intended to decorate a summer cottage or garden, you need to make mounts. At the bottom of the craft, make several holes with an awl and pass a twine through them. Now your beauty can be attached to any tree trunk or placed on a branch. Everything - a cute owl from plastic bottles is ready! You see, there is nothing complicated in making such a garden decoration. Be sure to try to create an Owl craft out of plastic bottles with your child; he will like such a creative and entertaining pastime. We hope that everything turned out right for you!
