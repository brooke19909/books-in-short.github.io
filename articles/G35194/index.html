A bit about the resort

As we already mentioned, the Blue Waters Club Resort 5 * hotel (photo is given in the article) is located in the small resort village of Sorgun. The resort's hotels are considered one of the best on the Mediterranean coast, therefore they are invariably popular among tourists who want to relax in a quiet and peaceful place. All complexes are located on the coast and have excellent infrastructure, because outside their territory there are no special entertainments. According to reviews of Blue Waters Club Resort 5 * (Side), the hotel can be classified as one of the most popular institutions in the village.





The entire resort is surrounded by pine forests. The cleanest air, sea breeze, the aroma of pine needles - all this creates a special atmosphere. In addition, a huge advantage is the absence of noise from nightlife. But at the same time, the village is located near Side.

Sorgun is incredibly popular for families with children. Many hotels have all the necessary conditions for kids: water parks, attractions, playgrounds. The coast seems to be created for children in the village, it is gentle and very comfortable, so parents can not worry about their children.

But shopping lovers in Sorgun can not find a decent lesson. There are no shopping centers, and local shops are not of interest. Therefore, shopping is worth going to Side, which is famous for its retail space.

The sea coast in Sorgun stretches for three kilometers. Local beaches have a sand and pebble coating. Some hotels have built platforms for their guests to descend into the water. The beaches of the resort are very gentle and comfortable for swimming. The only moment is that not everywhere there is sand, in some places there is small pebble. You can go snorkelling and diving in Sorgun.

Almost all the entertainment is in the hotels themselves and in their territories. Guests can also enjoy horse riding or jeep safari. There are no significant sights in the village. But the rest in Sorgun is filled with tranquility and peace. If you belong to the category of tourists who like to see the sights, then you should go to the ancient city of Side. The journey takes only twenty minutes.

Sorgun is an incredibly beautiful place, it is surrounded by coniferous forest. Therefore, there is always clean air, which is filled not only with coniferous aromas, but also with sea breezes.





The climate of the village belongs to the Mediterranean, so the holiday season here lasts from March to October. Forests reliably protect the village from the winds, so the sea here is almost always calm. From May to October, the water temperature ranges from +22 ... + 27 degrees, so at this time it is very comfortable to swim.
