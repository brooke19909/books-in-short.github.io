Kalanchevskaya station - a magnificent transport interchange in Moscow

Probably, everyone will agree that in Moscow, as, indeed, in any other city in the world, there are places that are especially popular. Station Kalanchevskaya. general description





The official name of the station is Moscow-Kalanchevskaya. People call it easier - Kalanchevskaya. This Moscow station is located on the Alekseevskaya connecting line. It is part of the Moscow-Kursk region of the Moscow Railway.

Station Kalanchevskaya. The history of the construction of the facility



In 1865, it was marked by the opening of the Moscow-Kalanchevskaya railway station. The station ‚Äôs western platform includes the station building itself, which is a pavilion built in 1896. Its architect was G.V. Voynevich.

Even before the station was built, it was decided that it was through it that the most important trains would pass, the passengers of which would be members of the government and other famous officials. By the way, Emperor Nicholas II passed here, heading for his coronation.

The design of the station premises was chosen accordingly. It was built of facing bricks of extraordinary beauty, and decorated with Tarutino stone. The domed roof and the tower with a spire looked luxurious and majestic. The station was especially magnificent due to its rich interior decoration. The carved roof covering the platform added elegance and originality to the building. The square and the square surrounded the station building.

But historical events unfolded in a completely different way. The coronation train got to another station - Brest. But still, sometimes the imperial family used its special station. After the revolutionary events, the station building was used far from intended. It was decided to place the district council of the Moscow Railway District there. After some time, the station began to function as a passenger station intended for commuter trains.

Station Kalanchevskaya. Features and reason for popularity





The station includes 2 side platforms. Turnstiles for passengers are mandatory on them. Between the platforms, a single transition is organized, which is a flooring through the paths. It is noteworthy that the northern part of the platform has a curved shape.

From this railway station you can get to Kalanchevskaya, Krasnoprudnaya, Bolshaya Spasskaya streets, as well as go to Komsomolskaya Square and to Groholsky Lane.

Another name for this station - Moscow-Technical-Kurskaya - arose because it is here that the preparation of long-distance trains with a southern direction is made.

Immediately, station workers monitor the composition of long-distance trains of Moscow affiliation. The equipment of restaurant cars with necessary products is also in the department of Kalanchevskaya employees. They provide passenger cars with coal, bed linen and water.
