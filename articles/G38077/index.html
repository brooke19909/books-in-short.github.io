Cozy Beach Hotel 4 * (Pattaya, Thailand): description and reviews of tourists

If you decide to spend your vacation in the country of smiles - Thailand - and you are looking for an inexpensive, but comfortable place to stay near the sea and close to the main attractions, then Cozy Beach Hotel 4 * (Pattaya) may be a suitable option.





Location

This hotel is located on a hill in a small bay, three kilometers from the center of Pattaya. The nearest Cozy Beach is just 100 meters away. The hotel is 80 kilometers from Bangkok International Airport. Near the hotel there are a lot of cafes, restaurants, bars, massage parlors, travel agencies, transport rental offices, there are markets and other infrastructure.





General information, photo

The Cozy Beach Hotel began operations in 1992. It has a common territory with another hotel - Cozy Beach Hotel Ocean Wing 4 * (Pattaya). There are newer and more modern buildings. The housing stock of the hotel consists of 293 rooms of the categories Standard, Suite, Superior and Mini-Suite. Some apartments have sea views. All rooms are freshly renovated and decorated in a pleasant style. They have everything you need: a TV, a telephone, a minibar, a safe, air conditioning, an electric kettle, a balcony, a bathroom.

The hotel has a restaurant, bar, two swimming pools, sun terraces, gym.

Cozy Beach Hotel 3 * (Pattaya, Thailand): reviews

We remind you that the hotel actually has 4 *. Every traveler knows that during a trip to another country the hotel becomes a kind of second home. Therefore, it is very important that staying at the hotel is a joy, even if you do not plan to spend a lot of time in it. Therefore, tourists on the eve of their vacations abroad do not spare time to study the reviews of those who have already happened to visit this or that place.

After all, former guests in all colors describe their impressions of a particular hotel, which allows you to make a choice. Today we invite you to get acquainted with the opinion of our compatriots about Cozi Beach Hotel. Reviews for the hotel Cozy Beach 3 * (Thailand, Pattaya), I must say, almost all have a positive character. In addition, this hotel boasts a rather high rating, which is 4.5 points out of a maximum of five.





Rooms

Our compatriots offered apartments in this hotel were mostly satisfied. So, according to them, the rooms here are quite spacious, pleasantly decorated, with beautiful views. They are equipped with comfortable high-quality furniture and properly working appliances. Guests liked the fact that all apartments have an electronic safe (located in the closet). In their reviews, tourists are advised to take a room with a sea view if possible. After all, waking up and falling asleep while admiring the azure waters is an unspeakable pleasure. In addition, all apartments have balconies with armchairs and tables. So you can, comfortably located, enjoy the sea. By the way, travelers do not recommend choosing apartments with windows facing the street, as it can be too noisy.





Cleaning service

According to guests who have left feedback about the Cozy Beach Hotel 4 * (Pattaya), the maids at the hotel are quite good at their job. So, according to tourists, rooms are cleaned every day. True, some guests complained that carpet flooring was not vacuumed every day. However, overall the apartment was always clean. Maids renew their bed linen and towels several times a week. Also, as you spend, replenished stocks of bath and toilet accessories. In addition, daily maids bring bottled drinking water to guests.

As for the other hotel employees, there were no complaints from travelers on their part. On the contrary, according to our compatriots having a rest at Cozy Beach Hotel 4 * (Pattaya, Thailand), the hotel staff is always friendly, friendly and ready to help in solving any problem.

Nutrition

The cost of living at Cozy Beach Hotel includes breakfast only. They, as tourists note, are quite standard for hotels of this category. So, in the mornings, guests have the opportunity to taste eggs, sausages, chicken dishes, soups, noodles, vegetables, fruits, pastries, granola, juice, tea, coffee, milk cooked in different variations. The choice of dishes and products every day was almost the same, but most vacationers were not at all embarrassed.

During the day, travelers could also have lunch and dinner in the hotel restaurant, but at an additional cost. According to our compatriots, the cooking is good here, and the prices are very reasonable. However, most tourists preferred eating outside the hotel. Moreover, both in the vicinity of Cozy Beach Hotel and in the center of Pattaya there is a large selection of cafes and restaurants where you can have a tasty and inexpensive meal. Travelers are also advised to buy food in mashinnitsa (mobile food trays) and in local markets.





Beach vacation

The indisputable advantage of Cozy Beach Hotel 4 * (Pattaya, Thailand), according to many tourists, is its proximity to the sea. So, guests can walk to the nearest beach in just a few minutes. True, the road runs at an angle, so it can be difficult for older people to climb back. As for the beach, it is municipal. Tourists can rent sunbeds and umbrellas for a fee (about 50 baht for a whole day). The beach itself is not very big. It is regularly cleaned of garbage, but still tourists cannot call it very clean.

Many travelers also preferred to visit other beaches. So, for example, experienced tourists are advised to go to the island of Koh Lan. You can get on it by boat from Cozi Beach or by ferry from the port in the center of Pattaya. Here you will find excellent beaches with pure white sand and a lot of cafes and bars.
