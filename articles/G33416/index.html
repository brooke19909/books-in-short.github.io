Hotel Stardust Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Kemer) - photos, prices and reviews

Kemer is a large resort city, which offers vacationers an excellent beach by the Mediterranean Sea, as well as a large number of water activities. In order to relax in this place, you need to choose the right hotel for yourself. Many tourists try to choose three or four star hotels. Among the latter, a great option could be Stardust Beach Hotel 4 *. Having settled in this hotel, the tourist will receive a large number of benefits, ranging from a good level of service to a close proximity to the beach.





Hotel building

Stardust Beach Hotel 4 * Family is a great place to relax with the whole family. The hotel management is trying to do everything possible so that vacationers can feel at home. The hotel is relatively young since it was built only in 2005. The last restoration was carried out in 2012, which once again proves the seriousness of the hotel in its intentions to attract a large number of tourists. The facade of the building is a four-complex, where there are seventy rooms. The total occupied area is about 4600 square meters. As for flowers, Stardust Beach Hotel 4 * Large did not use exotic coloring in coloring, but settled on a combination of white and red more familiar to Turkish hotels. The entire building is painted white with the exception of the roof and visors, which are painted in red.

How to get to the hotel?

This issue may arise immediately after the tourist decided to settle in this place. Stardust Beach Hotel 4 * Standard is located not quite in Kemer, but at a distance of 5 km from its center. That is, we can say that the hotel is located on the outskirts of the spa town in the Camyuva area. There is no city airport in Kemer, therefore, to get to the city, you must first fly to Antalya, whose airport is closest to Kemer. You can fly to Antalya through both fixed and charter flights. The ticket price can vary from 300 to 400 US dollars. After you arrive at the airport, you need to go to the bus station and wait for the flight to Kemer. The journey time from Antalya to Kemer is about an hour. If you do not want to wait for the bus at the station, you can use the service provided by Stardust Beach Hotel 4 * Standard. The meaning of this service is that you can specify in advance in the conditions of settlement that a taxi is transporting you from the airport to the hotel. This service is paid, but for those who want to save their time and effort, it will be very convenient.



Location

Hotel Stardust Beach Hotel 4 * (photo above) has both advantages and disadvantages, due to its location. It is 5 km from the center of Kemer in an area called Camyuva. The advantage of this arrangement is its proximity to nature and its beauty in the form of forests and mountains. Also an undoubted advantage is the close position relative to the beach of the Mediterranean Sea. The distance to the coastline is only 200 meters, which allows tourists to choose between relaxing on the beach or in a sun lounger and under an umbrella, but near the pool. Among the minuses of this hotel location, it should be noted its greater remoteness from the airport than other hotels. But even despite the distance of 62 km, from Antalya Airport to Stardust Beach Hotel 4 * can be reached in 40 minutes by taxi. It is 5 km from the center of Kemer. This can create difficulties for those people who want to visit the museums and attractions of the city. So before choosing this hotel, you need to clearly decide for yourself what you want to do in Kemer. If the goal is to rest by the sea, then this option is very good.





Hotel Services

The hotel is a four-star hotel, which means that all the services it provides correspond to this class. Conventionally, all services can be divided into those that are provided internally and those that are provided outside the complex. Among external services, first of all, the possibility of transfer from the airport to the hotel should be highlighted. Such a service is paid, and it should be agreed upon even before flying to Antalya. Among other paid hotel services, one can single out the possibility of renting a car for more comfortable movement around the city. Also, for guests, the Stardust Beach Hotel offers the opportunity to exchange their own currency for the local one. This service is very convenient, because almost all goods in the city are sold only for Turkish liras. The exchange rate is not much different from that set by the Central Bank of the country. Internal service includes the possibility of receiving spa massage (also for a fee). Among the free services provided by the hotel, it is worth highlighting the possibility of using a swimming pool and sauna, a fitness area for exercising, as well as breakfast and dinner in a local restaurant in the case of the all-inclusive option. For those people who want to relax on the beach by the Mediterranean Sea, the service of providing sun loungers and umbrellas is provided.





Sport and leisure

The four-star hotel Stardust Beach Hotel 4 *, reviews of which are quite decent, provides its settlers with a wide variety for an interesting pastime within their walls. Those people who like to spend their holidays actively will be glad to know that there are two large pools on the hotel territory, the water in which changes daily, and therefore it is always transparent and crystal clear. In addition to the pools, the hotel has a sauna and a fitness room. For those tourists who do not want to lose time in the gym, a relaxation room is provided. You can play billiards or table tennis there, as well as compete with other guests in the game of darts. Stardust Beach Hotel 4 * (Kemer) also takes care of the smallest visitors. For them, there is a special play area where they can have a carefree time while their parents relax by the pool. There is also a shallow pool for children. Fans of water activities can go to the shores of the Mediterranean Sea and enjoy water skiing or banana. Tourists also have the option of diving under the patronage of specialists.
