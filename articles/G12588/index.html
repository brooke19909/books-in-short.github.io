general information

The hotel, built in 2007, is located on the Halkidiki Peninsula, eighty kilometers from Thessaloniki. Around it is a large garden, in which there are many palm trees, olive trees and rose bushes. The last redecoration in it was carried out in 2014. Iris Hotel Siviri 3 * was built near the village of Siviri. Its territory is eighteen thousand square meters. Thessaloniki Airport - the closest to the hotel - is ninety kilometers away.

Infrastructure

Three-star Iris Hotel Siviri 3 * represents eight two-story cottages. In its main administrative building there is a registration desk, dry cleaning, and an ATM. There is a possibility of renting a safe-cell, as well as a left-luggage office. The reception is open around the clock. Although the hotel indicates a fixed time of arrival, however, subject to the availability of a room, check-in can be done outside of normal hours. Check out time is noon.



You can also use fax at the reception, or arrange a transfer or taxi. The hotel has rooms for disabled guests, as well as all conditions, including ramps for wheelchair access. Pets are not allowed in the hotel. The territory has its own free secure parking, which is not required to be reserved in advance.
