The very first iPhone - features and benefits

Since the release of the iPhone has become a constant subject of talk, speculation and discussion. This is quite true, since this gadget is capable of qualitatively performing many functions. The very first iPhone since its inception has become one of the most popular devices among users around the world.





Functionality

The gadget combines a mobile phone with very good call quality, Ipod functionality with an expanded interface, as well as a well-integrated PDA and various Internet devices. At the same time, the iPhone performs all these tasks surprisingly well. Some of its features are simply superb. However, the very first iPhone is far from perfect. Users complained about the short battery life and insufficient network speed. These deficiencies clearly needed to be addressed. However, positive qualities prevailed markedly.

One of the features of this gadget is a harmonious combination of its capabilities. So, when a call comes in while listening to music, it is easily turned off by a simple click of the switch on the headphones, and pressing the microphone will allow a call back. To complete it, click on the microphone again, and the music will turn on from the moment it was paused.





Screen

The web browser interface allows users to enlarge the image by tapping the screen with two fingers and slightly pulling in different directions. This movement is easily and quickly absorbed. Thanks to this and similar features, the very first iPhone has become one of the classic devices with multi-touch function. In addition, the iPhone automatically switches the screen orientation from portrait to landscape when you turn the device. When you put the phone on your ear to make a call, the screen turns off. If you reject it to end the call, the display lights up again.

Other functions

The SMS function shows correspondence in the form of an instant messaging session, which makes it easy to track the entire communication process. The ability to synchronize with a calendar, address book and bookmarks, as well as listen to music and watch videos make iPhone a kind of laptop replacement.





As already mentioned, the very first iPhone 1 has two drawbacks - a slow network connection and a short battery life. Many users noted that the battery life of the gadget is one of the areas that needs to be improved. Since technologies such as Wi-Fi and Bluetooth require significant battery consumption, the phone discharges very quickly when used.

Slow internet connection is another device problem, but it only happens if you used EDGE with Wi-Fi turned off. In all other respects, the very first iPhone was much ahead of the gadgets coming out at that time.

It is natural that the price of the iPhone was initially high enough, which made it inaccessible to many consumers. In the end, the cost of the first generation model began to decline, since the subsequent ones were equipped with more advanced functions. Today, a gadget such as a smartphone is developing really leaps and bounds. The above problems were almost eliminated in subsequent models, and photos of the very first iPhone today can be seen in reviews covering the history of the development of modern technologies.
