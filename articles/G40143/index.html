Hotel "Bear Corner" in Yaroslavl: hotel description

This hotel complex was opened in 1985, after which it has already been reconstructed twice and undergone major repairs. In 2004 there was a complete replacement of furniture.

The hotel has a wonderful restaurant of the same name. Not only residents, but also employees of neighboring organizations, just passers-by, like to drop into it.

The building itself is noticeably from afar. In winter, it is decorated with many luminous garlands, and in summer - flowers and beautiful shrubs. A roof is made from the entrance to the road, so when leaving the car, you immediately find yourself under a canopy. This design helps in bad weather. These and many others, the hotel "Bear Corner" in Yaroslavl, visitors reviews of which are only good, won the hearts of many tourists.

The appearance of the hotel is quite ordinary. An average building in a classic style with inconspicuous cladding. The name of the hotel is large and can be seen from afar.

Near the hotel there are such attractions as the Yaroslavl Kremlin, Kazan Monastery and the railway station.

Hotel "Bear Corner" (Yaroslavl): address

The hotel is located on Sverdlova street, house 16. With your own transport or taxi, it will not be difficult to get to the right place. Where is the hotel "Bear Corner" in Yaroslavl, you already know, it remains to figure out how to get to it.

From the Yaroslavl-Glavny train station, you can get to the hotel in several ways. You can use bus number 20 and trolley bus number 1. You need to exit at the stop "Youth Square". Then it is worth going a little along Republican Street to the intersection with Sverdlov Street, and then turn right.

To get to Sverdlov Street from the Yaroslavl-Moskovsky station, you need to take a trolley bus number 9 or bus number 1. Get off at the Red Square stop, to this point you can take a minibus 71, 73 and 91. Next, go through Pervomaisky Boulevard and follow to Sverdlov Street, and then immediately turn right.

Hotel "Bear Corner" in Yaroslavl, the address of which was indicated above, in terms of location - the best option. From here you can easily get to any place in the city.
