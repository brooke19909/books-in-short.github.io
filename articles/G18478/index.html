Samsung Galaxy S3 Duos: review, specifications and reviews

An exact copy of the flagship smartphone of 2012 from Samsung with improved specifications and support for two SIM cards is the Galaxy S3 Duos. Despite the fact that quite a lot of time has passed since the start of sales of this device, its characteristics still continue to be relevant. They, as well as reviews about this gadget will be examined in detail in this article.





Smartphone niche

From a performance standpoint, the Galaxy S3 Duos can be safely attributed to entry-level solutions. But at the same time he has a slightly overpriced price. But this significant drawback is easily compensated by a number of characteristics (for example, the increased diagonal size of the touch display, the impressive capacity of the integrated drive, and the improved parameters of the camera‚Äôs base). Therefore, this smartphone model is of most interest precisely for those who want an entry-level device, but with slightly improved parameters compared to competitors. Well, for these improved options you will have to overpay. But, on the other hand, it is quite justified, because you get a device on which it is much simpler and more convenient to work.

Another important accessory is a memory card. She is also not on the list of supplies, and therefore have to buy separately. Although, in principle, the owner of this device can do without it. The capacity of the integrated drive allows this.





Design

In terms of design, the gadget is completely identical to its predecessor Galaxy S3 Duos. A review of the appearance of these devices even allows you to distinguish between them. The only difference is the inscription on the front panel, at the end of which the word Duos was added. The front panel of the gadget, as expected, displays a display with a diagonal of 4.8 inches. Below it is a familiar control panel, which consists of a central mechanical button and two extreme touch buttons. Unlike most similar devices, in this case, the buttons have a backlight, which greatly simplifies the work on the smartphone in low light conditions. Above the screen is a conversational speaker, a ‚Äúpeephole‚Äù of the front camera and light sensors and distance sensing. On the upper edge of the smartphone is only a wired audio jack 3.5 mm. On the left side of the device, buttons for adjusting the volume are disconnected, and on the right - locks. At the bottom is the micro-USB connector and the hole of the conversational microphone. On the back cover, in addition to the logo of the manufacturer and the inscription DUOS, there is also a hole in the main camera and LED backlight. The main speaker, which is hidden behind a metal mesh, is also displayed here.

CPU

Galaxy S3 Duos uses a time-tested and highly reliable chip as a computing platform - this is the Snapdragon 400 developed by Kualkom. It consists of 4 computing models of the Cortex A7 architecture. Each of them can at maximum load accelerate to 1.4 GHz. It is also made according to the 28-nm process technology. All this allows him to combine an acceptable level of computational performance and high energy efficiency. To solve most everyday tasks, this semiconductor crystal is more than enough. This list includes playing videos, listening to music and radio, reading books, browsing the Internet. But the most demanding games of the latest generation certainly will not start on this device. Most of them are optimized for 64-bit computing, and this chip can process only 32 bits in one clock cycle. Hence the problems with their launch.

Cameras

Just a great main camera in the Samsung Galaxy S3 Duos. She has an 8 megapixel sensor. Also implemented autofocus technology, there is a digital zoom. Memory

1.5 GB is the amount of RAM in the Galaxy S3 Duos. 16 GB is the capacity of the integrated drive. The average user will be quite enough for work and leisure. If for some reason you need to increase the amount of memory in the device, you can install an external flash drive. Moreover, its maximum volume can be equal to 64 GB. For greater reliability, it is recommended to store the most valuable information on cloud services. This will avoid its loss in the event of theft or breakdown of the smart phone.

Device autonomy

High autonomy can not boast of Samsung Galaxy S3 Duos. The main reason here is the 2100 mAh battery capacity. In a case with a thickness of 8.6 mm, a larger battery is difficult to put. Add to this the presence of two SIM cards and a diagonal of 4.8 inches. To a certain extent, the problem is solved by an energy-efficient processor, but this is not enough to fully resolve this issue. As a result, all the previously mentioned factors lead to the fact that at maximum load, this smart phone can last a maximum of 12 hours. If you reduce the load, we get 2-3 days of confident work on a single charge of the battery. If this is not enough, then we buy an additional external battery. Of course, this is not an entirely elegant solution, but your phone will not let you down at the most inopportune moment for this.
