The location of the resort

The resort complex Maritim Jolie Ville Resort & Casino 5 * is located in the heart of a quiet bay of Naama Bay right on a clean sandy beach. This place is incredibly popular among vacationers, not only because of the natural beauties, but also because of the huge opportunities here for having fun.

Hotel guest rooms

By choosing Maritim Jolie Ville Resort & Casino (Naama Bay), you will have the opportunity to stay in these apartments:

  • The standard room consists of two rooms, one of which is a large and bright bedroom, and the second is a compact, but very comfortable living room. It is very important that the apartment has a balcony where you can sunbathe, sit, admiring the scenery, as well as dry linen and towels.
  • Superior is a huge room, or rather, a bedroom with windows overlooking the garden. Exotic green spaces can also be admired from the balcony.

  • Junior Suite - similar to the previous territory in everything except the landscape. From its windows you can clearly see the green, well-groomed garden.
