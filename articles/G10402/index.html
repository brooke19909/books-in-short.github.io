Where are Cannes located in which country? Cannes (France): population, attractions

Where are Cannes located, in which country, what is interesting there, is it worth it to come there, when is it better to do it? You will find the answer to all these questions in the article.

Cannes is a city on the south coast of France, known to the rest of the world for its film festival, which takes place annually in this small town. How did it happen that a small place lurking in the hills of Esterel suddenly became so popular.

Correctly called the city - Cannes or Caen. It is written in French - Cannes. When the French pronounce it, they say a clean, not nasal sound "a". The fact is that in France there is another Caen, which is written differently - Caen, they pronounce its name with the nasal "a". This is a completely different place located in Normandy.

Cannes Square is small, only 19.62 km 2 . At the place where the city is now located, there were ancient settlements of the Romans in 42 BC. e., bore the name of Egitna. But there is another historical theory that ligurs lived in this place in the II century BC. e. The territory often turned out to be a battlefield, many wanted to own this wonderful place on the coast of the Gulf of Naples.





Already from the X century, the name Cannes or Canua is found in historical documents. Translated, this means height. But there is another version that the city is named after the plant name of the same name.

A small population of Cannes was fishing. There was also a port on the shore that facilitated the development of trade with the inhabitants of the Lerins Islands. Almost 74,000 people now live in the city.

Why has the city gained popularity?

In 1834, the English lord and former Chancellor Henry Peter Broome went to Italy, but having learned that there was an epidemic of cholera there, he decided to stop on the way in a small town. He was so fascinated by this place that he changed his route and stopped to wait for the epidemic here. He lingered much longer than planned. Arriving at home, he talked a lot about the beauty of Cannes, and his family next year all came to see what really struck the Lord, who even built a mansion there. Soon, not only English, but also Russian aristocrats began to visit this place. The popularity of the town grew every year. Since the 19th century, many celebrities have built beautiful houses there. The city blossomed before our eyes. Guy de Maupassant, who lived there for some time, described the city as follows: "Princes, princes are everywhere." Even the Russian Empress, wife of Alexander II, Maria Alexandrovna visited him in 1879. Now the Rothschild villa is a museum.

Nowadays, the city of Cannes is one of the most popular and expensive resorts of the Cote d'Azur of the French Riviera. It stretches for 9 km along the Mediterranean coast from the Bocca quarter to the rocky mountains of Fourcade. The central part of Cannes is Antibes street, Liberty Alley and the famous Croisette.

Not so long ago, it was just a country road along which beautiful rich mansions and chic hotels lined up. Now, along the famous promenade, numerous boats and yachts are streaked with water. The famous cinema, where the world-famous film festival, and the prestigious Palm Beach casino in the region are located, is also located there. Tourists can listen to the tour, taking a tram to the most important places in the city. It is broadcast in headphones, so everything is clear to Russian-speaking tourists.





A very popular place for tourists in Cannes is the hill of Suquet, whose narrow ancient streets are crowded along the slopes of Mount Chevalier. There is a beautiful square of the same name and a 22-meter tower. This is part of the old city where the ancient port, Castres castle of the XII century, the picturesque Alley of Freedom is located, on which the grateful citizens erected a monument to the English Lord Henry Broome.

Also behind the fortress walls is an old beautiful church of Our Lady of Hope.

Holidays on the coast

The most prestigious resort of the French Riviera is famous for its magnificent beaches, which are kept by citizens in perfect condition. But be prepared for the fact that basically all of them are private and staying on the wonderful sandy coast is not free. But there are also free public beaches: du Midi beach and de la Bocca beach.

Cannes in France is considered a very fashionable holiday destination, so the prices for all types of services are appropriate. It is a city of relaxation. People come just to relax under the rays of the warm Mediterranean sun, take a leisurely stroll along the Croisette, sit on the famous blue chairs, which many take away for memory.

Rest here is sedate, tourists sit nonchalantly in numerous cozy cafes and restaurants, although food and drinks here are unusually expensive. Almost all hotels overlook the promenade, so vacationers spend a lot of time on the beaches, water skiing and biking. The best time to relax in Cannes is from April to October. Although winter is moderately warm and dry there.

Not far from the coast of the town are the Lerins Islands. There you can ride on a yacht. Often, a pleasure boat departs from the seaport, which takes tourists to their destination.

City of Festivals

The townspeople decided to turn their tourist city into a venue for various festivals, competitions and congresses. The eternal holiday contributes to the unique atmosphere of glamor. In addition to tourists, many celebrities come here every year. These are movie stars and politicians.

Film Festival

This year the anniversary festival was held. Exactly 70 times in May of every year, famous actors, directors, screenwriters, producers and composers came from all over Europe and the World. People closely associated with the creation of cinematic masterpieces.





The 2017 Cannes Film Festival hosted guests from May 17 to May 28. This year, the jury of the famous competition was led by Spanish filmmaker and famous screenwriter Pedro Almodovar. The main opening and closing ceremonies of the festival were led by the beloved Italian actress Monica Bellucci.

The emblem of the festival remained the same, a palm branch. And here on the official advertising poster of this year was Claudia Cardinale, the famous Italian actress.

The opening of the screenings of the competitive films began with the screening of "The Ghosts of Ismael." This is a film by the French director Arno Depleshen.





The main award at the Cannes Film Festival in 2017, the Golden Palm Branch, received the painting Square, directed by Ruben Estlund. This is a joint film from France, Sweden, Denmark and the USA.

Russian director Andrei Zvyagintsev was also awarded a jury prize for his film "Dislike."

Yacht festival

In the city, living under the motto "Life is a festival", since 1977, an exhibition of tourist and modern yachts. Manufacturers from all over the world bring luxury boats for boat shows. This year, 35 countries took part, more than 800 yachts flooded all 5 km of berths and piers of the city.





Rich people from all over the world come to consider new models, make deals, buy an updated version of the ship. This contributes to the development of the yacht building industry, and to attracting even more tourists to the city on the Cote d'Azur.

Pyrotechnics Festival

One of the most impressive shows is the Cannes Pyrotechnic Festival . It is held every year in late July or early August, starting in 1967. The winner of the contest, who presented the best fireworks, receives the Silver Vestale prize. Every five years, the winners gather and play among themselves the most prestigious prize, called Golden Vestale.





Each performance under the terms of the competition lasts at least 25 minutes. During this time, the jury should have time to evaluate the quality and ratio of the music and light shows, the rhythm, pace of the performance, as well as the originality of the production number. Spectators receive unusually vivid impressions throughout the festival. This performance of professionals from all over the world is remembered for a lifetime, and you can watch it absolutely free at 22.00 from any bench or beach on the Croisette.
