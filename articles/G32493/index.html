Hotel Barry Boutique Hotel 4 * (China, Sanya): photos and reviews of tourists

China is a mysterious and even somewhat exotic country, not only for Russian tourists, but also for travelers from almost all corners of the world. Here you can find anything you want for an eventful and impressed vacation.

Of course, many tourists exploring the country at least once come to the sea. One of the popular resort cities is Sanya. This is a real metropolis, grown right on the seashore. Naturally, there are many hotels of different sizes and categories. One of the most popular holiday destinations is Barry Boutique Hotel.

It's no secret that many tourists carefully plan their trip and are responsible for choosing a hotel. So where exactly is it located and how close is the beach to it? Are there conditions for business events? What will the child do here? How do former guests respond about the hotel?

Location and its features

The hotel has a fairly favorable location. As already mentioned, the hotel is located in a busy area of ‚Äã‚ÄãSanya, almost on the seashore. Various shops, restaurants and boutiques are within walking distance. On foot you can reach the huge shopping centers, where people not only shop, but also relax and have fun.

Nearby is a public transport stop, with which you can reach almost any part of the city. A seven minute drive away is the beautiful Luhuitou Park. Sanya Airport is a half-hour drive away.

Hotel Barry Boutique Seaview Hotel 4 * (Dadonghai): infrastructure description





This is a large hotel, which is a modern high-rise building on 33 floors. Despite the fact that there is no courtyard or garden here, inside the building there is everything that is needed, including a system of elevators, a parking lot, roof terraces and pools, shops, restaurants and other institutions.

By the way, the Barry Boutique Hotel 5 * (4 *) is relatively new - it was opened in 2012. Reviews say that you can really have a great time here.

How many rooms are on site?

The hotel can offer its customers check-in in one of 386 rooms. Regardless of which room you are accommodated in, you can be sure that the stay will be comfortable and pleasant. The hotel offers deluxe, suite and superior rooms. For the most part these are double rooms, but there are several more spacious apartments.

The rooms are divided into living and sleeping areas. There are rooms with two separate or one king size bed. Some rooms have a separate and fully equipped kitchenette with all appliances and utensils. Almost everywhere there are balconies or terraces. Of course, rooms on higher floors offer simply superb views of the surroundings and the seashore.

Photo and brief information about living rooms





According to guest reviews, the rooms at the Barry Boutique Hotel are nicely furnished: discreet design, comfortable furniture and breathtaking views make the stay here truly enjoyable. Of course, you can count on comfortable beds, a wardrobe, bedside tables, as well as a dressing table with a mirror. Some rooms even have a large desk.

All rooms are equipped with a powerful air conditioning system - you can control the temperature in the room using the remote control. A large-screen TV is at your service - not only local, but also satellite (even some Russian) channels are shown here. There is a telephone and a small safe, as well as the necessary ironing facilities. You can count on the availability of an electric kettle and a set for making coffee and tea. There is a cable Internet connection, which guests can use for free. Hotel guests are also provided with soft bathrobes and comfortable slippers.

The bathroom is large, tiled and equipped with modern fixtures. Tourists can take a shower, relax in the hot tub. There is a hair dryer, branded cosmetics (shampoos, soap, toothbrushes, etc.), as well as large clean towels. Room cleaning is carried out every day, and judging by the reviews, the staff really care about cleanliness. Bed linen is usually changed twice a week.

Catering facilities on site





Of course, guests at Barry Boutique Seaview Hotel can count on the opportunity to eat well. Paying for accommodation, tourists can independently choose a convenient scheme. By the way, you can completely refuse food. This does not mean that you will not have the right to have breakfast or dinner at the hotel. You just need to pay for food each time separately.

The hotel has two large restaurants with an original design, a cozy atmosphere, delicious food and, of course, high-quality service. Visiting Bai Bu Xian, you can enjoy the most popular and delicious Chinese dishes. But the Barry Restaurant offers classic international cuisine. And you can have a good time in the local bar, having a glass of quality booze, enjoying the emotional atmosphere and the company of new friends.

Relax on the beach: conditions and amenities





Is it possible to count on a beach holiday staying at the Barry Boutique Hotel? Sanya is a city located on the coast, so there are, of course, places for recreation by the sea. The public beach is located about 300 m from the hotel building, you just need to cross the road.

The beach strip here is not very wide, but covered with soft sand. The beach is cleaned regularly - it is always clean here. The entrance to the sea is really convenient. For a small fee, you can rent sunbeds and wide umbrellas. If you wish, you can go in for water sports or even play beach volleyball.

Do they offer additional services?

Of course, customers at Barry Boutique Hotel can count on some additional amenities. To begin with, it is worth saying that the staff will gladly relieve you of some household trifles, including washing and ironing, shoe shine.

The entire hotel has wireless Internet access (the speed is often not bad). Here you can, if necessary, exchange currency at a fairly favorable rate. By the way, credit cards are also accepted at the hotel, which is also convenient. Guests can rent a safe at the reception - there is 24-hour video surveillance, so your things will be completely safe. There is also a left-luggage office where you can leave, for example, unnecessary luggage. This is especially convenient for travelers who plan to spend only a few days here and go to another city.

You can count on concierge services. The reception is open around the clock - they are always ready to provide the guest with the necessary information. Employees, by the way, can arrange a transfer to the airport, call a taxi or even book tickets.

Business center and conditions for business leisure





It's no secret that China is a country where business life is constantly boiling. Almost every hotel has conditions not only for relaxation, but also for work. The hotel complex Barry Boutique Seaview Hotel 4 * is no exception. It has a large, fully equipped business center. Here you can use a computer, other office equipment and, of course, Internet access.

The hotel has a spacious furnished conference room with all necessary audio and video equipment. By the way, for a small fee, employees themselves organize business meetings, meetings and seminars. You can use the services of a secretary and translator. By the way, here you can rent equipment for online conferences. There is also a post office.

How to spend your free time? Leisure and entertainment





Of course, on the territory of the hotel you can not only live and work, but also relax. For guests equipped with a large outdoor pool located directly on the roof - here you can not only refresh yourself after a hard day, but also relax and enjoy the breathtaking views of the surroundings. Large sunbeds with comfortable mattresses are placed on the terrace. Clean, large towels are also provided.

Travelers who prefer to keep fit will enjoy the fitness center and gym. There is the necessary equipment, as well as several professional instructors who are happy to work with beginners.

The local spa is another tourist attraction. It offers a lot of different procedures for healing, relaxation and rejuvenation. By the way, you can count on professional massage. There is a steam room in the center where you can relax or warm up your muscles before training.

Hotel Barry Boutique Seaview Hotel (China): baby-friendly facilities

Parents traveling with their child are primarily interested in the availability of amenities. Of course, the work of the Barry Boutique Hotel 4 * hotel is excellently established - not only adult tourists, but also kids can find something to their liking.

As for amenities, then, of course, you will be provided with a large folding bed. The restaurant serves not only traditional Chinese dishes, but also the more familiar international food, so choosing a healthy and tasty meal for your baby will not be difficult. By the way, you can ask the waiter for a high chair for feeding. And you can leave the child for a while under the supervision of professional nannies.

The pool has a special section for children - the water here is always warm, and the depth is shallow. Therefore, the kids here have a great time under the supervision of their parents and attentive staff. There is also a spacious games room with toys, sports equipment and attractions. Do not forget that a lot of interesting entertainments can be found in the city center and on the seashore.

How do travelers respond?





The opinion of tourists who have already managed to visit one place or another can be very useful. What does the Barry Boutique Hotel say? Reviews are for the most part positive.

The building is really large, modern and well-equipped, the infrastructure is convenient and clear, you can use spacious elevators at any time of the day, and the windows offer breathtaking views of the city and the sea (especially when it comes to upper floors). The rooms are also well equipped: new furniture, cutting-edge technology and excellent service delight even the most demanding tourists.

The Barry Boutique Hotel is suitable for almost any type of vacation, whether it is a family vacation or a business trip. In addition, the prices for accommodation and meals here are quite reasonable.
