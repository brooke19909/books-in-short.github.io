Hotel "Palmira Palace", Yalta. Palmira Palace: traveler reviews

Crimea annually receives thousands of tourists both from Russia and from other CIS countries. This is not surprising, because there are not only magnificent beaches, but also a variety of natural and historical sights. One of the most popular Crimean resorts is Yalta. This ancient city offers travelers a lot of hotels, restaurants and a variety of entertainment for every taste. If you have chosen Yalta as your vacation destination, the 4 * Palmira Palace Hotel can be an excellent option for accommodation. Today we offer a closer acquaintance with this hotel complex and find out what impressions he left with the tourists who had already visited here.



Location

Hotel "Palmira Palace" (Yalta) is not difficult to find on the map. So, it is located on the southern coast of Crimea, in the village of Kurpaty. The hotel complex is eight kilometers away from Yalta itself. This distance can be overcome both by public transport and taxi. A big plus of this hotel is the fact that it is literally a hundred meters from the Black Sea coast and has its own beach. Not all hotels in Crimea can boast of this.

Palmira Palace (Yalta): guest reviews

When planning a trip to a particular place, modern travelers are no longer content solely with official information about a particular hotel and the recommendations of tour operators. Today, many people also prefer to read the comments of other people who have already visited the hotel of interest to them. We decided to make your task a little easier and provide generalized reviews of tourists regarding their vacation at the Yalta Hotel "Palmira Palace". In general, it should be noted that most of the hotel guests were very pleased with their choice of place of residence. And even despite the rather high, in their opinion, the cost of accommodation, she quite paid off. But we learn more about everything.

The hotel itself and its territory

As for the territory, then most vacationers respond exclusively positively about it. According to them, it is not so big, but very well-groomed, green. Many note the magnificent scent of flowers. Almost all the guests were very pleased with the rooms * 4 * (Yalta) offered by him for accommodation in the Palmira Palace Hotel; So, according to them, all the apartments here are very spacious, well furnished, clean. They have everything you need for a comfortable stay. Tourists were pleased with the presence of large furnished hotels, where it is nice to sit in the evening with a glass of wine. Beach vacation

Since almost all tourists who come to Crimea in the summer plan to sunbathe and soak up the warm sea, this issue is very important.
