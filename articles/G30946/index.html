Amari Havodda Maldives Resort - overview, features and reviews

The transparent ocean with paradise beaches, magnificent hotels, privacy - this is a vacation in the Maldives. These islands for many are the embodiment of "earthly paradise", where you can disconnect from the eternal bustle and bad weather. Here along the entire coast there is white sand, which contrasts with the sapphire surface of the water and the emerald green of palm trees.



general information

Judging by the reviews of the lucky ones who have already managed to visit the Maldives, tourists immediately feel themselves in the most beautiful place on the planet. Thanks to the mild tropical climate, any person feels great here. Holidays in the Maldives according to the all-inclusive concept works real miracles: here you can throw off fatigue and recharge your batteries for a whole year. Even despite the many hours of flight, many Russians come to this paradise with children, considering this destination ideal for a family vacation.

Nobody will be bored here: lovers of extreme entertainment can plunge into the underwater kingdom to admire fancy corals, fly into the sky by seaplane. Those who are used to combining holidays with educational excursions are recommended to visit one of the smallest capitals in the world - the ancient city of Male, where there are many Buddhist temples, magnificent palaces and ancient tombs.



As a rule, in the Maldives, each atoll is one large hotel complex, designed to accommodate only a limited number of guests. Such hotels have mainly four or five star categories and are built as bungalows. Most of them have completed their number of rooms in wooden houses on stilts that rise directly above the water in the ocean. The main advantage of such a residence, judging by the reviews, is the unobtrusive quiet lapping of the waves, heard under the floor. One of these hotels is Amari Havodda Maldives 5 *. Reviews about him, photos, provided services, as well as infrastructure features - all this is described in this article.



Description

The hotel opened in 2016, so it is not yet on many maps. It is located on Tinadhoo Island. The address of the hotel Amari Havodda Maldives 5 * is Gaafu Dhaalu Atoll. The atoll itself is quite small: it is about three kilometers long and four hundred meters wide. The nature around, judging by the reviews of the Russians, has unique beauty. The distance from the international airport in Male to Amari Havodda Maldives is 450 kilometers. Transfer lasts about an hour. Fifty minutes on a small plane, local airlines bring tourists to the Kaadedu airport terminal, and from there ten minutes by boat to the hotel pier.

Amari Havodda Maldives has everything for a comfortable stay. There are several small shops, including jewelry and souvenir shops, a first-aid post, laundry, concierge services, ironing, an exchanger, and dry cleaning.

Infrastructure

At the round-the-clock front desk you can order tickets, call a boat, buy sightseeing tours. There is also a sports equipment rental point in the administrative block, there is a left-luggage office, an ATM and a safe-deposit box. On the territory of Amari Havodda Maldives 5 * there is also a beauty salon and a library. Free use of a computer in the lobby. There is also a spacious TV lounge.

Housing Overview

In total, the hotel has one hundred twenty detached villas, each of which has only one room. Beach Villa is 95 square meters. meters. They are freestanding wooden houses overlooking the open ocean. They have a king size bed and an ottoman for relaxation. Bathrooms have a bathtub with shower. There is also a terrace with sun loungers and a coffee table. The highlight of all apartments at this hotel is the open-air shower.



Rooms at Sunset Beach Villa are similar to Beach Villa. They have the same content. The only difference is that they offer a view of the sunset, which is incredibly beautiful on the ocean. Beach Garden Pool Villa Villa apartments are quite large. Their area is 121 square meters. meter. They feature a terrace and a private pool.

Numbers Overwater Villa are presented in Amari Havodda Maldives 5 * (Male) in the amount of fifty eight pieces. Their area is 77 square meters. m. These are surface villas with reef views, an outdoor shower, a surface hammock, a terrace with sun loungers, a relaxation area and direct access to the water. In addition to standard furniture, these apartments also have fans.

There are two superior rooms in the Amari Havodda Maldives 5 * hotel - Deluxe. Their area is 153 square meters. m. They are similar to apartments category Overwater Villa. These rooms are only for children over seven years old. The entire housing stock of the hotel is furnished with quality furniture, there are air conditioning, ceiling fans, free Wi-Fi, a hairdryer, a bathrobe and slippers, everything you need for making coffee / tea, a minibar and bottled water. The rooms are equipped with a TV, DVD and CD player, and a safety deposit box.

Nutrition

Tour operators offer three packages at Amari Havodda Maldives. Full Board with three meals a day in the Amaya Food Gallery restaurant, an unlimited number of soft drinks and water during meals, a free tour with a guide for snorkeling on the "home" reef, the right to go to the gym, do yoga, volleyball and unlimited Internet traffic.



The All Inclusive package also includes alcoholic and non-alcoholic cocktails, wine and beer, juices, mineral water, coffee, tea. Subject to a minimum stay of four days, guests can purchase Premium All Inclusive. It provides everything that is in the previous packages, plus one free dinner upon reservation in the Robatayaki restaurant, a free minibar daily, cooking courses by the chef, a sunset cruise and an hour spa.

The menu of the main restaurant Amaya Food Gallery serves not only international, but also Asian cuisine. The hotel also has a pizzeria and two bars. At the Japanese restaurant you can enjoy delicious grilled rolls and seafood.

For children

Amari Havodda Maldives offers its little guests a fairly well-developed infrastructure - a swimming pool, a kids club, a playground. Parents can get a cot or babysitting service on request. High chairs are freely available in the restaurant.

Amari Havodda Maldives: reviews

According to the vast majority of Russians, the Maldives, including the atoll on which this hotel is built, is a paradise. Many of our compatriots liked it here, so some are even surprised by the presence of negative reviews. Nevertheless, they are. And first of all, they relate to the second flight, which is carried out by plane, so often with a delay. This is a big minus for those who travel with transfers and may not be in time for the flight.

However, despite this drawback, the vast majority of Russians liked the hotel. The food is very good. Shrimp dim sums and borsch from the chef were especially remembered by many. The hotel is mainly inhabited by Italians and British, while there are few Russians. Therefore, among the staff, no one understands our language. And this is another minus of the hotel.

As for the rooms, there are complaints as well: some did not like the constant noise of the waves, which interferes with sleep. Therefore, they recommend that this circumstance be taken into account when choosing a villa. But the Russians left a lot of enthusiastic reviews about rooms with a private pool.

As a result, we can conclude that Amari Havodda Maldives corresponds to the five-star category in almost all respects.
