How to pay parking via SMS? Payment of Moscow parking via SMS: step-by-step instruction

Many drivers in large cities are interested in how to pay for parking via SMS. This process greatly facilitates the process of depositing funds for parking. But is it possible to pay via SMS in Moscow? If so, how to do it? What features of the operation will you have to pay attention to? Let's figure it out together.



Paid parking - a reality or a fairy tale?

Before thinking about how to pay for parking via SMS in the center of Moscow, you need to understand how real this procedure is. Is there really a paid parking in the capital of Russia? And how can it be paid? Answers are given below.

Now throughout the country, free parking is actively being removed. Especially in large cities. In the center of St. Petersburg and in Moscow there is no longer free parking. You will have to pay for a car left in a designated place. This is quite normal.

Payment Methods

How to pay for parking? Can this be done via SMS ? Or are there any other approaches to solving the problem?

In Moscow, you can pay for parking in the following ways:

  • via the Internet (on the site "Moscow Parking");
  • mobile application;
  • payment terminals;
  • using the parking meter;
  • bank plastic;
  • subscription card;
  • SMS request.

In fact, everything is much simpler than it seems. Payment for parking does not give modern citizens any work. Next will be described in more detail about this process.



Messages

How to pay for parking in the center of Moscow via SMS or the Internet? Any citizen will be able to bring the idea to life. The main thing is to act correctly in a particular case.

Let's start with the most convenient method for solving the problem - sending an SMS request. In this case, the driver must write a letter of the established format and send it to a short number. After the first payment, the user will automatically receive a parking bill. It will be used for parking operations in the future.

It should be noted that depositing money for parking via SMS has several advantages. It is not only quick and easy, but also convenient. 15 minutes before the end of the parking time, the driver will receive a message with a proposal to extend the parking!

Step-by-step instructions for payment via SMS

How to pay for parking in the center of Moscow? The instructions below will help you bring the idea to life in a few minutes. The formation of an SMS request does not require any special knowledge and skills from a person.

Accordingly, all actions are reduced to the following steps:

  1. To park in the parking lot.
  2. Form a message of one type or another on the mobile device and send it for processing.
  3. Confirm parking.

This is where all the actions end. How to pay parking via SMS? A huge role here is played by the formation of the message sent for processing. With its help, you can not only deposit funds for the parking, but also interrupt / extend the parking.



SMS Commands

How to do this? Moscow residents need to focus on the following teams:

  • official parking number * state identifier of the car (number) * hours - payment for parking;
  • X hours - service renewal;
  • S or C - early termination of parking.

All SMS must be sent to number 7757. Money for unused parking time will be returned to the driver‚Äôs parking account. But initially they will be deducted from the balance of the mobile device.

Flat parking

Payment for Moscow parking via SMS can be done differently. For example, when it comes to flat parking. In this case, you have to act with some changes.

Flat parking in the center of the capital of Russia can be paid by sending an SMS command. This will require:

  1. Park and take a parking ticket.
  2. Write a message and send it to a special number.
  3. Wait for notification of successful request processing.

In this case, the letter format will be as follows: parking ticket number * car number * time (hours).

Now the tariff for a flat-type parking in Moscow is 100 rubles per hour. Depending on the length of time your car was parked, this or that amount of money will be deducted from the account on the citizen‚Äôs mobile phone.



application

Instructions for paying for parking via SMS are already known. But quite often, drivers use other options for depositing funds for parking in the capital. For example, you can download a special mobile program called "Moscow Parking" to your phone and use your Internet connection to implement an idea.

The process of transferring money through this application is reduced to the following manipulations:

  1. Download and install on your mobile "Moscow Parking".
  2. Register with a unique identifier in it. To do this, you need to send a message with the text "pin" (without quotes) to the number 7757.
  3. Log in to the utility. In the field "login" write the phone number of the driver, in the "password" - the sent password.
  4. Deposit money into your new account.
  5. Click on the "Park" button.
  6. Indicate the parking place and confirm the operation.

It may seem that everything is quite confusing. But this is not so. This method is in special demand among the population. Money is not debited from the mobile device, but from the current parking account. Similarly paid flat parking. To do this, the program will need to enter the number of the parking ticket, which you receive at the entrance to the parking lot.



Using site

How to pay for parking? We have already written through SMS, but there are other ways to solve this problem. Active Internet users can be offered to use the "Personal Account" on a special site in Moscow parking. With its help, it will be possible not only to pay for the car left in the parking lot, but also to buy a subscription.

In such circumstances, it is necessary to act as follows:

  1. Go to parking.mos.ru.
  2. Click on "My Account" in the upper right corner of the page.
  3. Log in to the service.
  4. Top up your new account balance. For example, a bank card.
  5. Indicate parking and car details. This can be done using an interactive map.
  6. Confirm the operation by specifying the number of parking hours.

We found out how to pay for parking via SMS and the Internet. All these are quite simple methods that are in great demand among the modern population.

Bank card

But you can do otherwise. A credit card is also allowed to pay for parking in the capital, as well as St. Petersburg. To do this in the main city of the Russian Federation:

  1. Go to the parking meter and click on the "Pay for parking" button.
  2. Confirm parking zone number.
  3. Select "Bank Card".
  4. Attach the card to a special validator and wait for the operation to be processed.

Pretty comfortable. You can act in a similar way if a person has a parking subscription card.

Drivers can replenish parking accounts with the following manipulations:

  2. In the "My Account" through the "Moscow Parking". You can replenish your account with a credit card, through payment systems and using your mobile phone.
  3. Through payment terminals "Eleksnet".
  4. In the Euroset and Svyaznoy stores.
  5. Sketch Cards. To do this, you need to remove the protective coating and send the number written on the card to number 7757.
