How to hem jeans so as not to spoil the product?

Surely many had to deal with a situation when perfectly fitting in width jeans are large in height. How to be Unsubscribe from a purchase? Do not despair, because hemming the bottom of the trousers is not at all difficult. Of course, you need to know how to hem jeans properly so as not to spoil the appearance of the product. To do this, you will need the simplest sewing machine and knowledge of several secrets of this process.





Choose the desired length

Most importantly, before starting work, correctly determine the required length of the product. To do this, put on jeans (without shoes), stand to the mirror. Tuck the excess fabric inward and stab it with pins. Near the heel, the hem line should reach the floor. You can make the length even a little longer (this applies to female models if you are going to wear high-heeled shoes). Now put on your shoes and see how your chosen length looks. If everything suits you, the fitting can be considered complete.

Hem jeans on a typewriter

Lay the trousers on a flat surface, carefully level them. Use a chalk (bar of soap) and a ruler to draw a line that will be the final length of the finished product. Step down one centimeter and draw another parallel line. This is necessary for bending.





We pass to the most important thing. How to hem jeans on a typewriter? Turn out a product. Fold the fabric along the first line, then along the second. To facilitate your work, it is better to iron the place of the hem. Thread the machine with threads of the same color as all the lines on the jeans.

How to hem jeans manually

But what if there is no sewing machine in the house, but there is no time to go to the atelier? Do not worry, jeans can also be hemmed manually. This will naturally take longer. The initial stage (fitting and marking) is no different from the previously described. We bend the fabric along the first line and stitch it with a ‚Äúneedle forward‚Äù seam. Then we bend a second time and iron the leg. Now we need to lay a more even and neat seam. It is called "for the needle." Outwardly, it is no different from a line on a car. Its quality depends on the accuracy of execution. The needle moves from right to left.





How to hem jeans if it is frayed

Many people are familiar with the situation when their favorite jeans still have a pretty decent look, and the bottom is worn and rubbed. Do not rush to put them in a distant box or send them to the cottage. There is an original way to ‚Äúreanimate‚Äù a favorite thing. In the sewing supplies store, buy the most ordinary zipper, usually it is sold per meter (you do not need a lock). Trim the worn bottom. Divide the zipper into two halves. Attach it edge to edge of jeans and sew on a typewriter. The line should be as close as possible to the snake. Wrap the seam inside the product and make another line, retreating from the edge by about a centimeter. This way will save the bottom of the jeans from abrasion and will become an additional decor.

Today you learned how to hem jeans correctly. Choose a method convenient for you. The main thing is that jeans please you with a good fit, and you feel comfortable in them.
