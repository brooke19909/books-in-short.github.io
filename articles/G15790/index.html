Hotel Marco Polo 4 * (Tunisia, Hammamet): photos and reviews of tourists

Tourists come to Tunisia every year more and more. Modern hotels, warm sea and pristine sandy beaches make this country an attractive option for a vacation. In addition, there are many ancient sights that will appeal to people interested in history. The inexpensive cost of a voucher is also a significant plus. The Riu Marco Polo 4 * complex (Tunisia) is popular with travelers from Russia. What attracts them to this hotel?

general information

The construction of this resort was completed in 1999. It consists of one four-story building, built in the Arabic style. Around it is an outdoor pool, as well as an extensive garden, which consists mainly of palm trees. The hotel is suitable for family or romantic holidays. It will appeal to all tourists who love a relaxing holiday. The hotel offers guests over 450 rooms. The staff cannot boast of excellent knowledge of the Russian language, but they are fluent in French and English.





Check-in to rooms, as in other hotels, starts at 14:00. Hotel Marco Polo 4 * (Tunisia) accepts children of any age, but pets are not allowed here. The peak season occurs in the summer months, when the country is hot weather. Nevertheless, you can relax here throughout the year. You must leave the hotel at the end of your stay before 12:00. If the flight at the airport is in the evening or night, guests can extend their stay for several hours for a fee. This service is only available subject to availability.

Location

A favorable location is a decisive factor when choosing a place to stay. The tourist complex is located on the sandy shore of the Mediterranean Sea. To reach the beach, tourists need to walk about 100 meters through the garden surrounding the hotel. A bus stop is 500 meters away. From here you can drive to Hammamet , where tourists can visit restaurants, cafes, nightclubs, bars, shops and other entertainment facilities. Yasmine Hammamet is a fully traveler oriented area. It is located only 1 km from the hotel Marco Polo 4 * (Tunisia).

Enfida International Airport is 40 km away. From here you can reach the hotel by taxi, rental car or bus. Paid shuttle service can be booked at checkout. A little further is Monastir Airport (95 km). Near the hotel is the amusement park Carthageland, which is worth a visit to all tourists with children. You can go shopping at the large shopping center, which is about 500 meters from the complex.

Number of rooms and its equipment

The hotel has 464 comfortable rooms. Their latest update was in 2009. Each has a spacious balcony with views of the pool, garden or sea. Ground floor apartments have a similar terrace. For guests who are forced to move in a wheelchair, there are 6 rooms equipped with special amenities (wide doorways, a special sink and shower). Large companies and families are offered adjoining apartments with a common door.





The hotel has both standard and family rooms. The area of ‚Äã‚Äãeach of them is 30 square meters. In the Family rooms, a folding sofa is also provided, which is used as a sleeping place. All rooms of the Marco Polo 4 * hotel (Tunisia, Hammamet ) are designed for up to two adults and one child. The apartment consists of a living room and a bathroom. Maids clean their living quarters daily. They change bed linen and towels on request or every 3 days.

The bathroom is equipped with a bathtub, washbasin and toilet. A hairdryer is provided free of charge, as well as toiletries and toilet paper. Central air conditioning is managed by the hotel staff and works well on schedule. On the contrary, in the winter, the hotel turns on the heating. All rooms are equipped with a modern TV, electronic safe and small fridge. Some rooms have free Wi-Fi . For a separate amount, you can use a direct phone from which you can call anywhere in the world.

Facilities and service

Like other Hammamet hotels , Club Hotel Marco Polo 4 * (Tunisia) offers its guests a wide selection of services that contribute to a quality stay. For businessmen staying here, the complex has prepared a business center equipped with the latest computer technology. The spacious conference room located in the hotel can accommodate more than 400 delegates. If tourists feel unwell, they may contact their local physician. It is worth knowing that his services are not included in the insurance, so each reception will be paid separately. You can buy snacks , alcohol, cigarettes, clothes, shoes, newspapers and souvenirs in a small store located in the hotel building.

For a comfortable stay, there is a spacious living room, which is equipped with sofas and TVs. It also has a cooling system and free Wi-Fi . Tourists will be able to leave their car in the car park. In the hairdresser for an additional fee you can make a beautiful hairstyle or fashionable haircut. Reception is open around the clock. Here you can not only talk with the administrator, but also exchange currency, return things to the laundry.

Food concept

Tourists vacationing at the Marco Polo 4 * hotel (Tunisia) are offered the all-inclusive concept. It works daily from 7:30 until 01:00. The system includes breakfast, lunch and dinner in the main restaurant, as well as an unlimited number of local drinks, including alcohol (beer, wine, rum, whiskey, gin and cocktails). They are served in plastic and glass glasses.





The main restaurant serves European and Oriental cuisine. They are served at a common buffet. Guests can have a snack in the lobby bar , which is located next to the reception . Tourists are offered a wide selection of pastries, desserts, snacks, fruits, ice cream and warming drinks. There are also Asian and Italian restaurants on site. For all the time of rest they can be visited for free only once. To do this, you must reserve a table in advance at the reception. At 18:00, a lounge bar opens where guests can order imported and local spirits, as well as try real hookah. Refreshing drinks are available at the poolside bar.

  • large outdoor pool;
  • free sunbeds and umbrellas located on the sun terrace;
  • heated indoor pool;
  • water sports center on the beach;

  • animation program, organization of theme nights and discos;
  • night club;
  • billiard room;
  • golf school;
  • playgrounds for bowling , volleyball, tennis, mini-golf ;
  • fitness center , where the gym is located, as well as classes in water gymnastics.




Conditions for children

Most often in the Marco Polo 4 * complex (Tunisia) families with children stop. For its young guests, the hotel has prepared the following conditions:

  • baby cradle, which is provided in the room free of charge;
  • high chairs for feeding are in every restaurant;
  • a playground equipped with slides, swings and a sandbox;
  • mini-club for children over 4 years old;
  • a children's pool watched by experienced lifeguards;
  • animation program for children;
  • paid babysitting services available on request.




Positive reviews

The many positive opinions of tourists are an indicator of the high quality of local services. Hotel Marco Polo 4 * (Tunisia) gets good reviews. In them, guests talk about the following benefits of relaxing in this place:

  • Well maintained hotel area. Employees promptly clean, care for the lawn, flowers and shrubs.
  • Attentive staff serving tourists by European standards.
  • Developed infrastructure near the hotel. Everywhere there are many entertainment facilities, restaurants, shops and bars.
  • A varied diet. 5-6 types of tea are served in individual teapots, several types of fried eggs. For lunch and dinner, chicken, pork, lamb and beef, as well as red fish, are cooked. The buffet is promptly updated even by the end of the dinner.
  • A huge swimming pool with clean water, in which people of high stature can freely swim.
  • Spacious rooms decorated with fresh flowers. All rooms are clean and tidy. Every day, maids make a swan figure from the sheets on the bed.




Negative reviews

Like other complexes, Club Hotel Marco Polo 4 * (Tunisia) sometimes gets negative reviews. Most of them describe small flaws that have little effect on the vacation experience. Nevertheless, tourists planning a vacation at this hotel should definitely pay attention to them. According to guests, the complex should eliminate the following disadvantages:

  • The toiletries in the rooms smell unpleasant. Shampoo does not wash hair, and the gel does not foam well.
  • The beds are equipped with hard mattresses on which it is uncomfortable and unusual to sleep.
  • The pool does not have enough sun loungers for all guests, so you have to get up very early and occupy them.
  • For dinner, men in shorts are not allowed in the restaurant. The dress code suggests wearing long trousers, but even in the evening they are very hot here.
  • The maids forget to leave a bottle of drinking water in the room, so they have to go down to the bar on their own.




Draw conclusions

The Marco Polo 4 * complex (Tunisia) fully complies with its price category. It can be recommended to families with children who will not save on vacation. Budget tourists, by contrast, are unlikely to find a suitable room here. Often, elderly couples from France, Germany and the United Kingdom rest at the hotel.
