Kemer: Amara Club Marina is a fun club hotel

Picturesque nature, numerous attractions, beautiful beaches and well-developed infrastructure is famous for the Turkish resort of Kemer. Amara Club Marina is one of the most prestigious and popular hotels on the coast. Tourists will find spacious rooms, well-groomed territory, a gym, fun animation, mini-clubs for children, excellent food - in general, all that is necessary for a successful vacation and enjoyment.

Hotel Description

Amara Club Marine is an excellent club hotel. Kemer traditionally presents the best hotels in Turkey on the coast. The hotel has beautiful green spaces from exotic plants, magnificent flower beds, a pine park. The complex has 319 standard rooms, among them there are several rooms for people with disabilities. In addition, there are 43 family rooms, 16 superior, 11 suite rooms with a bedroom and a living room, and ten deluxe rooms. All apartments are equipped with air conditioning, the rooms have a private bathroom, and there is a safe (fee), hairdryer, minibar. Family rooms have a balcony.

Bars and Restaurants

Amara Club Marine (Kemer) is distinguished by excellent service, reviews about this hotel speak about its popularity among tourists. There are several restaurants on its territory, the main of them is Splendid, as well as three restaurants that offer different cuisines. Among them - Anatolia with traditional Turkish cuisine, there is a fish restaurant called The Hook. Excellent Italian cuisine is served by Il Bagatto. In addition, a snack bar is open around the clock, there are many bars. The highlight of the hotel is meeting guests with a cocktail.





Beach

The best recreation areas are located in Kemer. Amara Club Marina has a pebbly and sandy beach, on which changing cabins are equipped, tourists can use a shower. The sea and the sun - this is the most important thing for what they come here on vacation. The hotel's beach has sun loungers and parasols.



For children

The hotel has a mini-club for children from four to twelve years old, an outdoor children's playground is located on site. In the pool for the child, experienced trainers will conduct swimming lessons. Babysitting services are available at an extra charge.

Services and entertainment

The hotel has swimming pools with water slides, a Turkish bath and a sauna. Sports enthusiasts are invited to visit the tennis courts, an excellent gym. In the evening, vacationers will be entertained by entertainment programs and animations. You can also learn various water sports here, windsurfing and scuba diving instructors work. An amazing vacation spot is the resort of Kemer. Amara Club Marina will give you unforgettable days and a lot of impressions!
