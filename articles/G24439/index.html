Moscow - Chelyabinsk. Time difference: Moscow - Chelyabinsk

If you plan to visit the Urals, then it is best to start a visit from the capital of metallurgy - Chelyabinsk. Traveling along the route Moscow - Chelyabinsk can be accomplished by plane, train or car. Choose the type of transport that is most convenient and comfortable for you, but be sure to consider the duration of the trip.

Chelyabinsk

If you plan to go the route Moscow - Chelyabinsk, then you will find yourself in the heart of Eurasia, this city is a large industrial center. It is home to just over 1.2 million people. The city was founded in 1736 and today is a kind of capital of the Southern Urals. About 25% of all metal produced in Russia is created here. The products of local industrial enterprises are widely in demand not only in the territory of the Russian Federation, but also abroad.

Sociologists consider Chelyabinsk one of the most convenient settlements for living, it is here that the most housing is commissioned annually. Drivers claim that the most convenient conditions for motorists are created in the Chelyabinsk region , this applies not only to high quality roads, but also to road transport services. According to statistics, the average salary in the region is 28-30 thousand rubles, so it is on the list of priorities for development by the Russian government.

Is it far?

There is an important thing for those who are going to pass the route Moscow - Chelyabinsk - the distance. It will directly depend on which vehicle you choose to travel and which route it will go. For example, if you go by car through Kazan, you will need to overcome more than 1700 kilometers, which is quite a lot. There are other options. For example, if you go through Yekaterinburg, then the distance will increase by 800-900 kilometers and you will have to spend much more time traveling.

If you plan to travel by train, options are also possible here. If you go through Ufa, the distance between the two cities will be 2048 kilometers, and if through Perm and Yekaterinburg, 2020 kilometers. It will be easiest to fly by plane, in this case the distance between Moscow and Chelyabinsk will be only 1,505 kilometers.

We are driving





Are you planning to go the route Moscow - Chelyabinsk? It will be much easier to overcome the distance by car if you arm yourself with all the necessary spare tools. The average travel time is 24 hours, on the way you will pass through Ufa, Vladimir, Cheboksary and a number of other cities where you can stop and examine them in detail. But the amount of time spent in this case will increase significantly.

1700 kilometers will pass unnoticed; there are a large number of campsites, cafes and hotels on the highway where you can stay. In the event of a difficult situation on the highways, traffic police crews, tow trucks, and technical assistance vehicles are constantly on duty. Be sure to get the phones of all the necessary departments before you go on the road, then you can protect yourself from various problems along the way.

Is it more convenient by train?





The schedule of JSC Russian Railways for the winter of 2015/2016. only four pairs of trains and one trailer car run on the route Moscow - Chelyabinsk. Three pairs and a trailed car drive through Ufa, the average travel time is 1 day and 12 hours, one pair (No. 145/146, St. Petersburg - Chelyabinsk) travels through Perm and Yekaterinburg and arrives at their destination in 1 day and 8 minutes. All trains run every other day, so the exact timetable is best found at the box office.

The fare in this case will depend on the selected train and the wagon category. The cheapest way will be to take train number 391/392, there you can even buy a compartment ticket for 4 thousand rubles, but from Chelyabinsk to Moscow it takes a day and 17 hours, which is a lot. The most expensive and one of the fastest is the train number 013/014, the coupe in it costs 5,700 rubles, and the luxury - 13,500, but the train arrives in just 1 day and 9 hours.

Conclusion





If you are planning to travel by train or plane, remember that you must arrive at the station or airport at the right time. Moscow - Chelyabinsk is a route on which, like on any other, transport always leaves according to the schedule, and they will not wait for lagging passengers. Remember the time difference when planning your next steps: to clarify Chelyabinsk time, you must add two hours to Moscow.

Be sure to visit the architectural monuments in Chelyabinsk and Moscow, all of them are worthy of your attention, because they have their own history, full of mysteries and secrets. When traveling to Chelyabinsk in winter, you need to take warm clothes with you, it is often too cold there for a resident of the capital who is not used to it.
