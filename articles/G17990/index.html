Samarkand hotels: list, addresses, services, pricing, visitor reviews

If you are traveling to Uzbekistan, but you can visit only one city without hesitation, choose Samarkand. This is one of the oldest cities in the world, its age is more than 2500 years. Here you will find yourself in a real oriental fairy tale, and Samarkand hotels will give guests their coziness and hospitality.

Boutique Hotel Rabat

The highest rating in Samarkand is at the Rabat boutique hotel. It occupies an old building built in 1906, which has historical value. From the hotel in just 10 minutes walk you can walk to Registan Square, Bibi Khanum Mosque, Siab Bazaar. Within walking distance are a supermarket and a restaurant. There is no parking at the hotel, access to free Wi-Fi is free. It features a courtyard garden and an outdoor dining area. A continental breakfast is served in the mornings. Each room has satellite TV, air conditioning, a coffee maker or kettle, and a private bathroom. It offers garden views. Reservations can be canceled free of charge up to three days before arrival. To accommodate guests, the following rooms are offered:

  • double rooms with extra long beds - 4655 rubles per day for two people, and 3325 rubles per day for one person.

Room rates include breakfast. Only eight rooms. Payment at this hotel is by cash only. If you are interested in hotels in the old city in Samarkand (Uzbekistan), then staying at Rabat Boutique Hotel will leave you the most pleasant experience. The hotel is located at: Bukharskaya street, 26.

Hotel Bibikhanum

The next in the ranking of the best hotels in Samarkand can also be called the oriental-style hotel Bibikhanum, located in the heart of the historical city, next to the Siab bazaar and supermarket, a few steps from the Bibi-Khanum mosque and a five-minute walk from the Shahi-Zinda complex. The hotel is decorated using traditional types of applied art, which gives its decoration a unique flavor. Each room has a balcony with mountain views. Parking and Wi-Fi are free. You can rent a bicycle, a mini market and a tour desk are available for you. The hotel has picnic areas and barbecue facilities. In the morning, the hotel serves a buffet breakfast. For accommodation, guests are offered several categories of rooms:

  • standard double with one double or two single beds - 4655 rubles per day for two people and 3724 rubles per day if one person lives;
  • standard triple - 5985 rubles per day for three people, 4788 rubles per day for two people, and 3591 rubles per day for one person.

Room rates include breakfast. Each is equipped with a TV and air conditioning. Cancellation and prepayment policies vary by room type. The Bibikhanum Hotel is located at 10 Tashkentskaya Street.

Hotel "Registan"

The four-star Hotel Registan is located in a modern building with an interesting design solution. Within walking distance from it there is a market, a supermarket, cafes and restaurants. Walking to Registan Square will take you no more than twenty minutes. This hotel is preferred by tourists who are interested in a hotel in Samarkand with a pool. Relaxing by the summer pool will help you unwind after an eventful day. Meals are organized in the hotel's own restaurant. There is an ATM, currency exchange, bicycle rental, mini market and gift shop for you. Parking and internet are free. You can book a room in the hotel by contacting the administration, or through the site. For accommodation, guests are offered the following room options:

  • single with one single bed - 4655 rubles per day;
  • double with one double or two single beds - 5653 rubles per day;
  • triple with three single beds - 6318 rubles per day.

Room rates include breakfast. If desired, you can pay for the room without meals. Each room has a TV and air conditioning. The Registan Hotel is located in Samarkand at the address: Mirzo Ulugbek Street, 16.

Afrosiab Palace Hotel

This four-star hotel is the largest among the hotels of Samarkand. It is located on the border of the new and old cities, includes several buildings made in different architectural styles. From here, only two minutes walk to Registan Square. It offers indoor and outdoor pools, a car park, a gift shop, and a currency exchange office. The on-site restaurant serves national, European and Korean dishes. The business center has all the necessary office equipment for conferences and business meetings. Guests can relax in the sauna, and massage services are available. Wireless internet is available for a fee. There is a playground for children at the hotel. Each room has air conditioning, TV, telephone with international connection. For accommodation, guests are offered the following room categories:

  • single standard - 2925 rubles a day;
  • double standard - 4225 rubles per day;
  • Luxury - 7150 rubles per day;
  • business suite - 9750 rubles per day;
  • apartments - 12 350 rubles per day.

Room rates include breakfast. The Afrosiab Hotel is located in Samarkand at the address: 2 Registanskaya Street.
