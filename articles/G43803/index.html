Holiday Village "Terema" (Tver): description, reviews, photos

Is vacation coming or long-awaited weekend? It is worth spending this time so as to restore strength and peace of mind. So I want to run away from problems! The dream of a good rest takes real shape only when it becomes possible to go on nature.

A civilized vacation harmoniously combines with picturesque paintings in a delightful place. Terema, a recreation center, Tver 10 km away, is popular not only among residents of this region, but also far beyond its borders.

Take a break from the hustle and bustle of the city

Amazing places near Tver are captured in the paintings of the famous artist Valentin Serov, who created his masterpieces near the current recreation center. Guests arriving from the city cannot breathe in the crystal clear air, and the deafening silence immerses a person in a different reality. Spread out surrounded by centuries-old forests on the shore of the reservoir, the complex is ready to receive guests both for the weekend and for a long stay.

For a decent vacation for families with children, pastimes of corporate or youth companies, the recreation center Terema (Tver) is considered a good choice. Room or gazebo reservations can be made by phone or online. The official website provides up-to-date information on availability. When planning celebrations (anniversary, presentation, wedding), it is recommended to contact managers in advance.

Accommodations

The recreation center "Terema" (Tver) has its own zest, attracting vacationers with apartments cut down from a century-old cedar. A distinctive feature of the houses is the interior decoration. Designers used natural material, which does not need decoration, to create the original style of each room, shading the attractiveness of drawings created by nature itself, well-chosen textiles and furniture.



The aroma of cedar wood has a beneficial effect on the human body, therefore, vacationers are offered as a “compliment” also a free course of aromatherapy as a gift. Entertainment and relaxation all year round

The recreation center “Terema” (Tver) welcomes guests with a Russian scale, offering the best, which is why guests are eager to return here. The complex, successfully functioning all year round, regardless of the season and weather outside, is ready to offer exciting events for vacationers with different needs. Adherents of an active lifestyle and those who prefer solitude will not be allowed to get bored here, because there are:

  • cycling in summer and skiing in winter;
  • swimming on a private beach or in an indoor pool;
  • recreation center "Terema" (Tver) is actively used for sporting events;
  • darts, hunting, fishing all year round;
  • ATV or snowmobile rides
  • boating or horseback riding;
  • ping pong, table tennis and billiards;
  • Russian bathhouse and Finnish sauna healing from ailments.

The apple orchard is good for walking at any time of the year. Here, successful photo shoots are held that warm the soul with memories of a great time.





Nutrition

Those who wish to cook their own meals are provided with the necessary kitchen equipment. Fans of barbecue and barbecue will be able to cook their favorite dishes outdoors, renting a site. Fishermen will taste the fish soup. And those seeking a break from culinary cares are invited to shift the chores onto the shoulders of professionals, because the recreation center "Terema" (Tver) is famous for its cuisine. A full menu for lovers of good food and weight watchers, vegetarians and children is offered in the bar and restaurant. Cooks - real virtuosos of culinary affairs - sensitively guess the preferences of customers and offer only the best. There is also a thematic menu dedicated to secular and Orthodox holidays.





Additional services

Thanks to the national color and the environmentally friendly atmosphere of this place, people who want to spend unforgettable events and family events, corporate celebrations and retreats tend to get here. It has become a good tradition to hold weddings and proms here. Wonderful rooms are prepared for the newlyweds, and in the houses of the fisherman and hunter there is everything necessary for the ceremonies. For couples with children, a play room is equipped in which the baby will be busy and will give parents the opportunity to relax. Separate apartments are offered for smokers, where they will not cause discomfort to people who advocate a healthy lifestyle. Sports equipment rental and shuttle services are available at an additional cost.





The recreation center "Terema" (Tver): reviews

Being not far from the city, the complex gives communication with nature, without depriving guests of their usual comfort. Thanks to the natural material, wooden houses keep cool in the summer and warm in the winter. Conditions of stay, well-groomed territory of holidaymakers are not satisfactory, on this occasion you can only hear rave reviews. But regarding the payment, often the guests express their dissatisfaction, because when renting the houses of a hunter and a fisherman, in addition to the hourly payment, 100 rubles are charged for each guest arriving. Also, paid entry to the territory of their vehicles does not cause delight among visitors who rent gazebos, because you have to unload the car at the gate and carry groceries and inventory in your hands.

However, these little things can not spoil the overall impression of being in the "Terem", because at any time of the year it is great. Wise parents strive to bring their children here to demonstrate the best Russian traditions in building houses, celebrating Christmas, Shrovetide, Trinity, Ivan Kupala. Those who come here note the beneficial effects of the special atmosphere that prevails here on the microclimate of the family.

Guests leave peaceful, well-being, with a lot of positive emotions and impressions, which gives the recreation center "Terema" (Tver). Photos taken as a keepsake will beckon those who have been here once and will be forced to return here again.
