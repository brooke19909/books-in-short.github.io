At the initial stage, the main thing is to receive not so much earnings as satisfaction and joy from work. One way or another, the work of a "seasoned" designer or rookie fashion designer is aimed at creating fashion trends for the consumer market. How to make sketches of dresses? Illustration is the main tool used in the work of stylists and designers. And computer programs can be mastered gradually. The most basic of them are: InDesign, Illustrator and Photoshop.



Often, a fashion designer wakes up even in a child when he asks his mother to sew clothes to his liking. To better understand, you have to explain your plan with a pen in hand on a piece of paper.
