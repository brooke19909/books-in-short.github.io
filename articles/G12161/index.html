New buildings of Voronezh: reviews

Voronezh is recognized as one of the most dynamically developing regional centers of Russia. The city has registered more than fifty development companies engaged in the construction of high-rise and low-rise housing.

Active settlement of dilapidated houses erected in the forties of the last century. Beautiful and modern residential complexes grow on the site of the emergency property. The capital of the Black Earth region is gradually changing its appearance.





Expanding its borders, it persistently conquers more and more new territories. If the set rate of demolition of obsolete buildings continues, then in ten years Voronezh will begin to replace the dilapidated five-story buildings.

High-altitude outskirts. Right bank

Almost all new buildings in Voronezh are concentrated in several areas of the city. So, once empty areas of the VGAU, stretching along ul. Shishkova. The construction of real estate is carried out on Ostrogozhskaya, which rests in the village of Shilovo.

Mass buildings captured Podgornoye and Antonova-Ovseenko Street leading to the village. Tower cranes are also visible on the right side of the Voronezh ring road.





New buildings of Voronezh. Left Coast

The construction of modern housing is carried out not only in the Comintern, Lenin, Central and Soviet districts of the metropolis, but also on the opposite side of the reservoir. The best example is the VAI quarter in the vicinity of Ilyushin Street. Reviews of those who have already purchased an apartment in this part of the city confirm the low housing prices and convenient location of the neighborhood.

New buildings of Voronezh are present on the maps of the Left Bank and Railway districts. We are talking about the areas of Artamonov, the embankment, the Telman plant. High-rise buildings are being erected on Leninsky Prospekt, as well as in the territories adjacent to it. Real estate on the left bank is the choice of those who are not used to overpaying. In their comments, Voronezh residents argue that it is here that you can find the most advantageous offers.





Developers and Developers

The leaders in the primary real estate market of the capital of Chernozemye are VDK, GK Razvitie, BiK, KIT, VMU-2, LLC Vybor, firms Stel-Invest, VestStroy, Domostroitelny Zavod, Instep.

If we compare the real estate prices of Voronezh (new buildings) with the cost of housing in other regions of Russia, then they are lower by an average of 1.3 times.





Current trends

The beginning of this year was marked by a systematic drop in housing prices. A square meter fell by three and a half thousand rubles. The most inexpensive new buildings in Voronezh are located in the Left Bank, Soviet and Railway regions. The most expensive apartments are in Leninsky, Central and Kominternovsky urban districts.





Consumer demand is influenced not only by the location of the residential complex, but also by the layout, presence or absence of social infrastructure, transport accessibility, proximity to recreational areas and facilities.
