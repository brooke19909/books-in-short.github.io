Hotels in Tula: overview, description, service, reviews

Tula is a large administrative center located on the banks of the Upa River. The history of the village dates back to the XVI century. Not surprisingly, there are many historical and cultural sites on the territory that have survived to this day. Also, do not forget about the industrial potential of the city. All this determines its high attendance. Hotels in Tula will provide all guests with a decent welcome.
