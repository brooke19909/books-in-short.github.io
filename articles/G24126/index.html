Rest in a bungalow in Thailand: photo, description

Modern bungalows in sea resorts are good mainly for accommodation during short trips, and the option in the form of a hut is an exotic type of housing that romantics who want to feel like real "robinsons" will like it more.

As a rule, such bungalows are located on the beach, and they are rented literally for one or two days, as they are unreliable in terms of storage, most often they have nothing but a bed inside. But this type of housing is quite comfortable, you can relax in it for quite a long time. The article will focus on vacation in a bungalow in Thailand.

What is a bungalow?

Today, quite often tours include accommodation in a bungalow, but many travelers have no idea what it is. In their view, these are huts in which local residents live.

A bungalow in Thailand on the shore is an ordinary house made in the architectural style inherent in this country. It can have 2-3 small rooms, and there can be huge rooms. The thing is that local residents do not divide dwellings into cottages, houses, apartments in houses and villas. All living quarters for them are bungalows. But earlier, small huts were called so, more similar to tents in which the whole family lived and hid from torrential rains and heat.

Holidays in Thailand in a bungalow by the sea

Is it not a paradise vacation when a stone's throw from the mighty azure ocean on snow-white sand among exotic tropical thickets, in a place where the minimum number of people is, there is a bungalow in which you can settle in and have a good time? Such a dream is quite feasible and real, including with a modest budget.

Below are some interesting accommodation options.

Koh Payam, Koh Phangan and Koh Lanta Islands

Today, such traditional fishing-style bungalows on the beaches of Thailand are becoming less common, as many other areas are moving higher and higher in the tourism industry. But there are still such huts in sufficient numbers on the islands of Koh Phangan, Koh Lanta and Koh Payam.

When choosing a holiday in a bungalow in Thailand, you should pay attention to the island of Koh Phangan, which gained popularity and worldwide fame for the Full Moon Party (noisy parties on Had Rinh beach) - the most beautiful part of the island in the shape of a crescent. It should be noted that this place is not very crowded with vacationers. Parties that are attended by about a thousand party-goers last only a few days a month, the rest of the time Koh Phangan is very calm.

Less well-known among these islands for travelers is Ko Payam. The relaxed hippy atmosphere prevails here. Until now, this place is considered a secret island of the Thai state.

All three islands have beautiful sandy beaches. Holidays in Thailand in a bungalow in these places can bring a lot of pleasure. The beaches have retained their original atmosphere of solitude and natural attractiveness. They can accommodate in a hut worth about 800 baht (1670 rubles) per day. The price depends on the location of the bungalow, the number of beds and the availability of air conditioning.

When choosing between the islands of Koh Phangan and Koh Lanta, you should consider the seasons. Since the first is in the Gulf of Siam, and the second is in the Andaman Sea, the rainy season in them occurs at different times. On Koh Lanta, the rainy season lasts from June to October, and on Koh Phangan - November-December.

Vacation Bungalows in Phuket

Hotels with bungalows in Thailand are very popular among lovers of a quiet relaxing holiday. Of course, accommodation does not guarantee that peace and quiet will reign, but, having settled in them, you can get rid of many neighbors. The advantage of this holiday is that right from the room you can get to the beach or pool.

Bangtao Village Resort 3 *

Holidays in Thailand in one of the bungalows of this fairly comfortable hotel will be quite enjoyable. Separate cottages with free Wi-Fi from Bangtao Bay Beach are just a 5-minute walk away. Surrounded by a beautiful tropical garden, the hotel has a swimming pool. Massages and a variety of water sports are offered. There is an opportunity to fish or see the life of the underwater world (diving). Bike hire and horse riding are available.

Vacationers can also take sightseeing tours to local attractions. This resort corner is suitable for both family and youth and romantic vacations.

Holidays in Thailand in the bungalows of this hotel, located on the shores of the magnificent freshwater Lagoon of the Andaman Sea (southwest of Phuket), by the beach of Bang Tao, will leave a lot of pleasant impressions in your memory. Not far away is the Laguna Phuket (golf club) and one of the most picturesque sights of this place is Phuket Fantasea Cultural Theme Park. The hotel itself is surrounded by lush exotic greenery.
