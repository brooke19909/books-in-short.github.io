Lenovo S860: reviews, specifications, photos and price

One of the most interesting phablets in terms of price / quality ratio today is the Lenovo S860. Reviews, cost, specifications and quality of photos - this is what this article will be devoted to.





"Iron"

The main components on which the performance of any smartphone depends are the central processor and graphics adapter. It is their characteristics that determine the capabilities of the device. As a CPU, this smartphone uses the time-tested MT6582. It consists of four cores of the Cortex-A7 architecture, each of which can operate with a clock frequency from 0.3 GHz to 1.3 GHz (depending on the level of complexity of the task being solved). This processor belongs to the middle segment of devices. For most everyday tasks, its resources will be enough. Starting from browsing the web and ending with complex 3D toys, he will pull them without any problems. A similar situation with the graphics adapter in Lenovo S860. Testimonials indicate that he has a normal, average level of performance. This gadget has 400 MP2 from Mali. The result is an excellent combination of a processor and a mid-range graphics adapter.

Screen

As noted earlier, the S860 belongs to the class of phablets, that is, to devices with a diagonal of 5 or more inches. In this case, we are talking about 5.3 inches. The display is based on a high-quality IPS matrix with a resolution of 1280 pixels in length and 720 pixels in width. The image is high-quality, rich color reproduction. It is almost impossible to distinguish a single pixel. Supports processing of up to five touches to the surface of the sensor at the same time.

Like most similar devices, two cameras are installed in the Lenovo S860. The reviews of the owners of the device indicate that the one located on the back allows you to receive photos and videos. It is based on an 8 megapixel matrix. It is also equipped with LED backlight for shooting at night, there is an automatic image stabilization system. The maximum resolution of the photo received from it is 3248 x 2448 pixels, and the video, in turn, can be recorded in the format of 1280 x 720 pixels. More modest parameters in the second camera. Memory size

Different amounts of RAM can be installed on the Lenovo S860. A review of existing offers on the Internet indicates two modifications of this device: 1 GB is present on one of them, and 2 GB is on the second. So it is necessary to check with the seller before buying how much RAM is in this model. By the way, the acquisition of the second modification is preferable.

The situation with the built-in flash drive is simpler: 16 GB are installed in the S860, 1.2 GB of which are reserved by the operating system itself. The rest of the internal memory is allocated to the user's needs. If for some reason this is not enough, then you can always install an external drive with a maximum capacity of 32 GB.





What is in the box?

This smartphone model has standard equipment. This is an entry-level device in which there is nothing superfluous. A warranty card and an instruction manual are the complete list of documents that come with it. In addition to them, the box also contains:

  • The device itself with a built-in battery.
  • Charger.
  • USB / micro-USB adapter cord. Using it, the battery is charged or connected to a personal computer.
  • OTG cable. It allows you to connect various USB devices (for example, a flash drive) to your smartphone.
  • Acoustic system entry level.

As noted earlier, the case and screen protector are not included in the package, so they will have to be purchased separately.

Battery

A serious plus compared to competitors is the Lenovo S860 TITANIUM battery. The feedback from the satisfied owners of this mobile device is another confirmation of this. In this case, we are talking about a battery capacity of 4000 milliamps / hours. Connectivity options

A fairly rich set of communications in the Lenovo S860. Specifications in this regard, he has the following:

  • The main way to connect to the Internet is Wi-Fi. It allows you to transfer data at speeds up to 150 Mbps. You can easily and easily download files of any size. Its only drawback is its small range.
