Hotel Arambol Plaza Beach Resort 2 * (Goa, India): overview, description and reviews

Goa is a picturesque island, which every year becomes more and more popular among people looking for a pleasant beach holiday. Here you will find the exotic beauty of nature, long beaches and, of course, a host of hotels of different categories. One of them is Arambol Plaza Beach Resort 2 *. But how do tourists respond about it, and is it worth going here?





Description of territory and location

Immediately it is worth noting that the Arambol Plaza Beach Resort 2 * hotel is a small, cozy place, which is famous not only for good living conditions, but also for real home comfort. The hotel is located in the northern part of Goa, very close to the famous public beach "Arambol". Dabolim Airport is 70 km from the property.

The hotel occupies a very small territory - its area is 2.5 thousand square meters. Here is a two-story main building, as well as several small cottages built of wood. The territory is decorated with greenery, flowers, trees, traditional Indian sculptures. There are terraces for relaxation and a swimming pool, in short - everything for a pleasant stay. By the way, the hotel was opened in 2004, and in 2012 it was completely renovated.

Hotel Arambol Plaza Beach Resorts 2 * (Goa): description and photos of rooms

This is a very small hotel with 20 rooms. These are standard rooms, the walls and floors of which are made of high-quality wood, which gives tourists more relaxation. The rooms on the second floor have small balconies from which you can enjoy views of the surroundings and the flowering garden.





Guests are provided with necessary appliances for comfort. For example, you can spend a free evening watching TV. There is a fan and air conditioning, with which it is easy to create comfortable temperature conditions in the room. There is a direct dial telephone. There is, of course, a minibar in the room, but it is empty - drinks are brought here only upon request, and their cost must be paid separately.

The bathroom is combined with the toilet. There is a spacious shower, washbasin and mirror. Guests receive clean towels, which are changed every time during cleaning. Some toiletries are also available. By the way, maids work well - the rooms are always fresh and clean.





Dining at the hotel

Paying for accommodation at the Arambol Plaza Beach Resort 2 * hotel, you can refuse meals or immediately pay for breakfast, lunch and dinner. The local restaurant serves international and Indian cuisine, so there are plenty to choose from. You can have dinner at the hotel, even if you did not pay for meals in advance - the prices here are quite affordable. And in the bar you can have a good time enjoying aromatic tea, spirits and interesting exotic cocktails.

By the way, the hotel is located on a busy street, next to which there are many cafes and restaurants. You definitely won‚Äôt have problems with food, because here you can always eat tasty and satisfying, and for a small fee.





Is there a beach? Water leisure tourists

As already mentioned, Arambol Plaza Beach Resort 2 * hotel is located very close to the seashore. Arambol Public Beach is just 300 meters away, with a convenient path leading to it.

The coast here is very beautiful, the entry into the sea is gradual, and the bottom is covered with soft sand. On the beach for a small fee, you can rent a comfortable lounger with an umbrella. There are volleyball courts, rental points for various sports equipment. If desired, tourists can go boating, canoeing, water skiing, windsurfing, sailing and parasailing. In a word, there are conditions for a pleasant, fun pastime.

Additional services and entertainment for guests

Arambol Plaza Beach Resort 2 * hotel guests tried to make life as comfortable as possible. To begin with, it is worth saying that the reception, room service and security work on site around the clock. By the way, you can ask employees to wake you up in the morning - this service is free.

You can rent a safe from the administrator. Workers will be happy to help you call a taxi or book a transfer to the airport. The staff understands English, but there may be some difficulties with communication in Russian. The laundry is constantly working. For a small fee, guests get access to the Internet, which is available throughout the hotel. There is a small business center where you can use a printer, copier, computer and fax machine.





It should be said right away that most tourists prefer to rent a transport immediately upon arrival. The hotel will help you rent a motorcycle, scooter or car (of course, this requires documents). Renting on the island is quite affordable, and fuel prices are low. You will have the opportunity to explore the surroundings on your own.

In the courtyard there is a small but deep pool, where it is always nice to refresh yourself. On the sun loungers arranged around, guests relax, relax and take sun baths. There is also a shallow children's pool, where the smallest travelers like to spend time.

And do not forget that there are a lot of interesting places around the hotel, including historical and cultural attractions, bustling markets, cozy restaurants and even nightclubs. Here you can really have a great vacation, having gained positive impressions a year in advance.
