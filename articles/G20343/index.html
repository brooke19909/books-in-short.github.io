Private sector in Golubitskaya: choice of housing

The village of Golubitskaya is located on the coast of the Taman Peninsula. On one side it is surrounded by the Kuban steppes. On the other hand, the waters of the Sea of ​​Azov are washed. The resort is famous for its excellent opportunities for a beach and wellness holiday.

Quick introduction

There are legends about the healing properties of local volcanic mud. Sludge is extracted from the crater of an almost dried-up salt lake. From time to time, a mud volcano erupts directly into the waters of the Sea of ​​Azov. Water in the coastal zone is saturated with mineral and biologically active substances.

Welcome

There are no large chain hotels in the village. Visitors are accommodated in the private sector of Golubitskaya. Travelers are offered small guest houses and cottages. There are recreation centers and campsites on the coast. The cost of living at the resort is almost the same everywhere. The price depends on the proposed level of comfort and remoteness of the hotel from the beach.

Cost of living

When booking in advance, the owners of the estates offer significant discounts, which reach 30%. The lowest prices in Golubitskaya are in the off-season. In May and September, you can rent a room with all conveniences for two people for 800 rubles per day. A similar option in June will cost 1,100 rubles.

When paying for accommodation in the private sector of Golubitskaya, the traveler receives not only a roof over his head, but also a lot of additional options:

  • free car parking space;
  • barbecue for cooking meat on coals and an open fire;
  • BBQ facilities
  • a set of kitchen utensils and tableware;
  • equipped dining area;
  • dining room and self-catering corner.

The rooms of the selected category of tourists expect fresh linens. Towels are provided to travelers. Most facilities are equipped with individual air conditioning systems. The rooms have televisions connected to cable TV.

Private sector facilities in Golubitskaya are three-story residential buildings with shared terraces and separate room entrances. In the courtyards of such hotels there are sun loungers for relaxation. There are playgrounds with sandboxes. Some hotels have fixed pools without water heating. Climbing to the upper floors is carried out by stairs.

Reservation

To reserve a room in your favorite hotel, prepayment is required. Those who choose a holiday in the private sector of Golubitskaya without intermediaries transfer to the account of the homeowner an amount equal to twice the cost of stay. Usually we are talking about 2000 or 3000 rubles. In case of early cancellation of the reservation, hotel owners in most cases return the full amount of money.

If the traveler refuses the hotel’s services at the last moment, then they will not transfer the prepayment to him. The final calculation for the entire period of residence is made on the spot. Some visitors turn to intermediary services. The cost of housing remains the same, but the vacationer receives additional guarantees from representatives of the travel company.

How to get there

To get on holiday in the private sector of Golubitskaya, you need to use a taxi. Intercity buses run from Anapa to the village. Their stop is located on the outskirts of the village. The best option is to seek help from homeowners. They organize a meeting of tourists at the airport and at the railway station.

Transfer is carried out by experienced drivers. All cars are new and equipped with air conditioning. The meeting person helps to carry luggage and settle in the car.
