Andaman Seaside Resort Bangtao 3 * (Thailand, South, Bang Tao Beach): description, photos and reviews

Andaman Seaside Resort Bangtao is a popular Thai hotel located literally at arm's length from the famous Bang Tao Beach in Phuket. Many people stay in it, having arrived in Thailand with the intention of having a good rest.

What is he good at? What are its features? How developed is the infrastructure and level of service? This will be discussed now.

Location

Bang Tao Beach, which houses the Andaman Seaside Resort Bangtao, is located in the northwestern part of Phuket. In fact, the place is very developed, judging by the infrastructure.

Within walking distance are the Bliss Beach club, Bang Tao temple, Plaza Surin shopping center, Tesco Lotus supermarket. You can also quickly get to the famous parks of Khao Phra Theo and Phuket Fantasy, a monument to two heroines, a local cabaret, Wat Srisuntorn temple and Kathu waterfall.

Phuket International Airport, by the way, is also relatively close. To go about 14 km. Krabi International Airport is 77 km away.
