Kernos Beach 4 (Greece / Crete): photo and reviews of tourists

Many people, deciding to relax in Greece, go to Crete. This is a pretty popular and good choice. There are many different guest houses and hotels. Among them - Kernos Beach 4 *. This is a hotel that hosts thousands of guests every year.





Briefly about the hotel

Kernos Beach 4 * is located in the resort village of Malia, which is located 35 kilometers from Heraklion. There is the nearest airport to the hotel. You can get from there to Malia by KTEL bus (the fare costs 3.8 euros). From the city station, flights run regularly, every hour, from 6:30 to 21:30. A taxi will cost about 40-50 euros.

In general, getting to the hotel is easy. And it is located on the first coastline. Just a few minutes walk to the sandy beach, which is especially pleasing to tourists.

By the way, the location of the hotel is very good. Literally a few minutes drive from the village are the ruins of the palace, erected at the same time when the Knossos Palace was built (and this is approximately in 2000-1700 BC).

The hotel itself is not new. It was built in 1976, and the last repair was carried out in 2008.

Recreation and entertainment

If the weather is bad at sea, or you just want to change your occupation, then in Kernos Beach 4 * there is definitely an activity. There is an outdoor terrace for sunbathing, as well as a swimming pool. The hotel also offers equipment for various sports, mini-golf and table tennis. The hotel has a billiard room, massage room, and a fitness center. It has all the simulators that are necessary for a full strength training. By the way, a full-fledged tennis court is also here.

People traveling here with their children may not worry about the fact that their little ones will have nothing to do. There is a playground on site.





Services

In Kernos Beach 4 * there is everything that makes visitors more comfortable. Firstly, there is free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel. Secondly, a private car park. This is especially for those guests who decide to rent a car. By the way, this service is also provided by the hotel. Like the transfer. But in order to receive it, guests must inform the accommodation in advance about the flight.

The hotel also has a luggage storage room where guests can leave their luggage. An office with fax and photocopying services is also available, as well as a tour desk with currency exchange. Other services include ironing, a conference room and a 24-hour reception desk.

The hotel staff speaks not only English and Greek, but also French and German. Although, as guests have noticed, many already speak Russian, as many guests from our country stop there.

Bars and Restaurants

In Kernos Beach 4 * (Greece, Crete, Heraklion) there are several institutions where you can eat deliciously. On the territory there is a main restaurant, and on the seashore - a second, fish one, offering a wide selection of amazing seafood dishes.

There is also a snack bar called Tavernaki. They serve salads, snacks, and also grilled treats. There is also a coffee shop. Be sure to look there to try real, famous, ground Greek coffee. By the way, there is also a cafeteria on the territory. They serve good hot and cold drinks.

As the guests assure, the hotel is excellent food. Themed dinners are especially pleasing. By the way, in the main restaurant you can not only treat yourself to a buffet, but also order from the menu. On Sunday evening, the restaurant serves red caviar and champagne.

If guests leave the hotel early, they can order a packed breakfast. Only you need to do this a day earlier. Breakfast is not bad - sausage, cheese, bread and coffee.

Reservation

Hotel Kernos Beach 4 * accepts its guests from 15:00 to midnight. Departure starts at 5:30, ends at 12:00. But you should not arrive at the hotel with the intention of booking a room on the spot. There are practically no free apartments, especially in high season. Therefore, it is better to reserve them in advance. There is even an opportunity to get a discount (it depends on the tour operator and season).

This hotel can accommodate children, however extra beds are not available. So, as you could understand, a maximum of 2 adults and 2 children can live in one room.

When creating an application, you will need to provide your credit card details. But full payment is made on the spot, at the registration of guests. However, a deposit for the number is charged - funds are blocked on the card. This is a guarantee that a person will definitely come to rest.
