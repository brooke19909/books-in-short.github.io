Unforgettable holidays in Karkaraly. Holiday homes in the mountains.

Karkaralinsk is a marvelous pearl of Kazakhstan. The nature of pristine beauty, the sight of which is breathtaking. The cleanest air, combining the smells of the forest and mountains, which is impossible to breathe. The crystal clear lakes are alluring and beautiful. It is not surprising that people travel alone and with families to Karkaralinsk on vacation. Holiday homes in the area are very popular all year round. You can come here and "savages" and go along tourist routes with a backpack behind. In any case, the impressions of the time spent will remain unforgettable.

A place covered in legends

In the heart of Kazakhstan, not far from the mining capital, the handsome Karkaralinsk is spread. Rest in holiday homes in this region is very popular with both the local population and visitors from other countries. There are no industrial enterprises, and the entire infrastructure is aimed at the development of the tourism industry.





Karkaralinsk, located in the center of Kazakhstan, is called "Kazakh Switzerland". The nature of marvelous beauty glorified this part of the republic far beyond its borders.

Everything here is fanned with legends and myths, rooted in ancient times. It is worth listening to the names in order to understand what fabulous places will appear: Falcon Top, Red Rock cliff, Deer Mountain, Red Beard Hill . And what poetic lakes: Pashennoe, Shaitankol.

Tour operators laid tourist routes of varying degrees of difficulty to these natural attractions, which made the rest in Karkaralinsk diverse and unforgettable. Holiday homes offer guests a choice of these routes and other diverse holiday programs.





Hiking trails

The route to Shaitankol is long, but not very difficult: you can go with the whole family. On the way, amazing views of the Karkaralinka river, mountain springs and moss-covered rocks open up. Depending on the season, you can pick mushrooms or berries along the way, so that not only memories, but also delicious gifts of nature are left from the rest.

There are two legends about the origin of this lake. According to one of them, the beautiful Sulushash drowned in him, fleeing from a tiger. According to another legend, the shaitan himself settled in these mountains, and also drowned himself fleeing from a formidable predator.

Another Pashennoe lake is distinguished by clear spring water. Its banks were overgrown with thickets of birch and aspen. People who come ashore tie ribbons on tree branches, making wishes.





Lake Basin is unique in that its depth of 5 meters does not change either in rainy weather or in drought.

Not far from Karkaralinsk there is a unique museum of nature, which presents a variety of flora and fauna of this amazing region. In open-air cages on free pasture you can see roe deer, wild boars, argali and other animals.

Holidays in Karkaralinsk in holiday homes will be an unforgettable gift for the whole family. Here, great fishing, exciting hiking trails of different difficulty levels. During the season, you can collect the gifts of the forest and stock up on healthy vitamins from an ecologically clean area for the whole year. Sanatoriums and pensions in Karkaralinsk offer extensive programs of healing and cultural recreation. Each guest will find something to their liking.

"Pearl"

Rest house "Pearl" (Karkaralinsk) is located 224 km from the mining capital, Karaganda. Every day, buses leave the bus station in the direction of the complex. The buildings of the sanatorium are located in a beautiful mountain range, near Lake Pashennoye. It offers wellness programs, hiking trails and cultural activities all year round. The cost of living depends on the level of comfort of the room.

Tasbulak

Tasbulak Holiday House in Karkaralysk is very popular among holidaymakers. Rooms can be selected in 6- or 4-storey buildings or in cottages. The prices are reasonable, the service is top notch. Lovely mountain hotel! In addition to wellness programs, guests are also offered walking tours, as well as horse trails with experienced guides.





"Miner"

Workers in the Karaganda mines go on vacation to Karkaralinsk to the Shakhtar holiday home. In 3- and 4-storey buildings there are rooms from the "economy class" to the "suites". Three meals a day and a variety of wellness programs help mining workers recover their health. And the wonderful nature and outdoor activities add vivid emotions to the rest.

In Karkaralinsk there are many rest houses, sanatoriums and boarding houses, and every year it becomes more and more. Both large and small are waiting for their guests.
