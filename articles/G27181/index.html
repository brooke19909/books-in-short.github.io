Vacation in Tenerife in December

Tenerife is called the Island of Eternal Spring. In winter, this is one of the most popular destinations due to the mild and pleasant ambient temperature. In December, in Tenerife there is no scorching sun and crowds of tourists. This Mediterranean resort will welcome excellent quality of service and paradise landscapes.

Some features of the climate of the island

The Canary Islands, although they belong to Spain, are closer to Africa than to Europe. This largely determines their climate. Winter, such as we used to see it, is not here. The lower limit of temperatures fluctuates around 16 degrees above zero, and this already seems to be a resident of terrible frosty weather.



If you are going on vacation to this region, you need to consider several nuances:

  • In the hot season, the difference in climate between the northern and southern parts of the island is not noticeable.

  • The weather in Tenerife in December will differ depending on which part of the island vacation is planned.

  • The northern part is often blown by cold winds and is more suitable for tourists making an excursion pilgrimage. In this region, the temperature is slightly lower than on the opposite side of the island.

  • On the south side you need to go with the children. This part is warmer and less susceptible to cold winds.

You need to take two sets of clothes with you: autumn and summer. In the afternoon, the temperature will be warm and you can walk in shorts, and in the evening you will need a warm sweater or windbreaker.

What to do in December

First of all, this is an extensive excursion program. You can get to know the indigenous people of the island, their culture and traditions.

In early December, Tenerife hosts a feast of oranges. A day in honor of sweet fruits is even declared a day off. The central squares of cities are turning into orange rivers. Everywhere they offer to taste citrus varieties and buy a couple. At the end of the day, the results are held and the best gardeners are selected who receive valuable prizes.

In December, the most important holiday of all Catholics is held - this is Christmas. Already a week before December 25, the streets of the city are filled with a pleasant atmosphere of New Year's bustle. Only the usual snow and Christmas trees are not here. And there are palm trees, fruits and artisans fairs.

What to fear in December

In December, in Tenerife you should not get involved in water sports. You can go diving near the shore or drift on a yacht, not leaving far from the resort. But going out into the open sea is not recommended. The ocean at this time of the year is very troubled. His mood can change very quickly, and it is almost impossible to predict.

If you plan to relax with children, then it is worth remembering that swimming in the pool is also a very pleasant experience, sometimes even better than in the sea. In winter, the waves are high, the water is turbulent. And the pools always have a warm comfortable environment with minimal health risks. Tourists note that in some hotels the pools are so warm that the water seems hot. As, for example, in GrandKanaree, where the mark was + 28 ¬∞ .
