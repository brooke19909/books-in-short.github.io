Where to go to Anapa for a tourist?

The resort city of Anapa is open year-round for everyone to visit this wonderful place.



Many people come to Anapa year-round to relax on the seashore. For guests deciding where to go in Anapa, there are many cultural and entertainment venues. Most vacationers come in the summer with children. Along the seashore stretched for many kilometers the golden sands of the beaches of Anapa. This is “Central”, located in the center of the city, and “Healing”, so named because the surf brings a lot of medicinal algae there, and the High Coast beach with clear sea water located on a cliff.

But even in the summer the weather can go bad, and then the question arises for those who have come to rest in Anapa: "Where to go?" In the evening, many vacationers can be seen on the promenade, beautiful views and clean sea air will add charm to an unhurried wellness stroll. Those who love adrenaline will enjoy the Sea of ​​Pleasure water park and two Golden Beach and Tiki-Taki water parks. In the water ski park you will truly enjoy the sea of ​​many European attractions, water skiing or windsurfing. In water parks, you will also get a lot of positive emotions, rolling down the many slides and splashing in the pools of the water park.





If you are still wondering “where to go to Anapa”, then the dolphinarium on Pionersky Prospekt works for you. The dolphinarium is equipped with special ramps for the disabled, there is convenient parking here. You will enjoy dolphin games with great pleasure and be able to take pictures with them as a keepsake. Dolphinarium on Pioneer Avenue is the only one on the Black Sea coast. Eight young penguins brought from Peru and Chile live in it. Against their background, you can take a picture.

Where can I go in Anapa yet? At the intersection of Chernomorskaya and Krepostnaya Streets there is a sports ground, which can be visited by all sports fans. Here you can play volleyball, basketball, table tennis and roller skate.





Anapa also has a miniature zoo, which is located in the city center. Here live monkeys, large talking parrots, kangaroos, ostriches, crocodiles and many other animals that can be hand-fed and photographed. Where to go with children in Anapa? Of course, to the exhibition of moving dinosaurs. Here you can see long extinct animals in full growth: triceratops, brachiosaurus, tyrannosaurus and others.

On the promenade is the "Ancient city of Gorgippia." This is one of the unique preserved Greek cities, the excavations of which you can inspect and photograph. By visiting the Gorgippia Museum, you will learn a lot of useful and interesting things.

Where to go in Anapa in the evening? Many restaurants and clubs are open in Anapa in the evening and at night. Here you can enjoy wonderful cuisine, live music and dance all night. In the summer, various theater groups come to the City Theater on tour. Classic lovers can enjoy the theatrical atmosphere in the evening. On the summer stage and in the Victory concert hall, stars from all over Russia perform all summer.
