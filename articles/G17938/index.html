Hotel Emerald Hotel Jomtien Beach 3 * (Thailand, Pattaya): photos and reviews of tourists

Pattaya is a picturesque city in the southeast of Thailand. The resort has the best hotels and beaches. It is located on the warm shore of the Gulf of Thailand, about 150 km from Bangkok, in the province of Chonburi. Here you can visit unique natural places, incredible parks and botanical gardens.
Pattaya city occupies a small area, it consists of only five main streets. The main ones are located parallel to each other: Beach Road Beach Road is a continuous recreation area. Second Street - Second Road intersects with central Central Pattaya Road. Here you can find everything you need for relaxation and entertainment. There are two other equally interesting streets, North Pattaya Road and South Pattaya Road.

Beach



It is in Pattaya that Jomtien Beach, adored by tourists, is located. Its length is about 6 km. It is located near the Pratumnak hill. If you prefer to rest on the first line, this place is for you. There is everything for a good rest: hotels, restaurants and even bungalow complexes. On the beach, you can rent sun loungers and enjoy cocktails and Thai snacks. And also at a low price you will be offered famous Thai massage therapists. It is safe to swim near the shores of Jomtien Beach, because of strong waves there is practically no water transport. But thanks to the strong wind, this is a great place for surf lovers.

Orchid Park "Nong Nooch"

You will never regret that you visited this resort town. In addition to nightlife and the beach, there are a huge number of amazing places. The first thing to visit is the Nong Nooch Orchid Park. These flowers occupy a small part of the territory, but there are the rarest species of this beautiful plant. Sometimes this park is called - orchid park. And also there is the most amazing cactus garden, their sizes and shapes will surprise everyone. The park has an area with colorful butterflies and birds, and you can also see exotic animals in the mini-zoo. The largest collection of palm trees is also located in this complex, there are other famous botanical gardens that you need to see, such as a French park, a bonsai park and an elephant farm. A unique landscape design has been created here, the territory is decorated with beautiful tropical plants. And finally, you must definitely visit the elephant farm and watch the unforgettable performance of these courageous animals.

Coral islands



If you want to see all the beauty of the marine fauna, then it is worth spending a little less than an hour on a sea trip to the irresistible coral islands, where you will be amazed at the unbelievably clear water and relax on the quiet sandy beaches. The most popular island for visitors is Koh Lan. It has the most developed infrastructure, it contains more than ten comfortable beaches with all amenities. There are a small number of cafes and bars where you can have a tasty snack and taste refreshing cocktails. Thanks to crystal water, diving enthusiasts gather here, you can see incredible fish and find amazing shells and corals. It is also worth using a jet ski, ski rental, parachuting and having fun on the mass of other water attractions.

For those who do not like to swim in a mask and flippers, there is an alternative solution. These are wonderful wonder boats with a transparent bottom. Thus, you will observe the delights of marine life, see the most exotic fish and other inhabitants of the seabed. And sea urchins and colorful stars will not leave anyone without impressions.
