Helmet camcorder: how to choose a dash cam for a bike or motorcycle

Everyone who is involved in active sports wants to capture the moment of his triumph. Unfortunately, standard video recording tools are not able to work in the mode of shaking and constantly moving objects. To take pictures of life on wheels, whether it's a mountain bike or a motorcycle, you just need to purchase a special helmet camcorder. These recorders are adapted for harsh conditions and are able to create the highest resolution videos. At the same time, the gadget does not interfere while driving.

What to look for when choosing a camera

When choosing a device for recording video while driving, you must adhere to the following recommendations:

  • Before buying, you must definitely hold the camera on a helmet (moto or bicycle) in your hands. The DVR does not have to be very heavy.
  • It is worth considering that this type of camera uses lightweight SD and MicroSD cards, so it‚Äôs better to purchase several spare devices for storing information at once.
  • If the device is equipped with a removable or integrated display for viewing video, then the weight of such a device will be more.
  • The camera must be in a reliable sealed enclosure.
  • The optimal viewing angle for such devices is considered to be 70-90 degrees. If the lens ‚Äúlooks‚Äù wider, then this may affect the quality of the video, in which distortions appear.
  • It is worth paying attention to the number of FPS (frames per second). The greater the value will be indicated in the characteristics of the gadget, the better. In addition, it is necessary to pay attention to the fact that high-quality action cameras are vibration and moisture resistant.
