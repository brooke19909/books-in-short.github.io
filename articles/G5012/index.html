Yarnart - knitting yarn loved by craftsmen

Hand knitting professionals know: for a thing to turn out beautiful, the craftsmanship of the needlewoman is not enough, the material from which the product is made is important. Conclusion: the quality of threads must be given maximum attention. In the product market, the Yarnart brand (yarn) is known for its first-class properties. What is so wonderful about these threads, why are they so loved? A detailed analysis of the questions will help resolve them.

About Brand

The beginning of thread production dates back to 1993. The creator of the brand is a Turkish company of the same name. For almost twenty-five years, the company has entered the top manufacturers of goods for hand knitting. Today, Yarnart is a yarn that will satisfy even the most demanding needlewoman.





Why you should choose these threads:

  • a variety of rulers, differing in composition, texture, color shades;
  • excellent quality, the thread is flat, does not roll;
  • affordable price;
  • Along with popular articles, the manufacturer regularly offers new products.

Yarnart - yarn with exceptional properties. The reason is carefully selected materials. The composition contains natural sheep wool and high-quality plant fibers. A small amount of added synthetics increases the durability of the socks of products.

Thread classification

In the Yarnart catalog, yarn is grouped by composition. There are threads for knitting warm winter sweaters, jumpers and cardigans, cotton and viscose for summer topics and blouses, as well as skeins of thread in the form of ruffles, fringes and pompons.

Needlewomen prefer the following articles.

Baby Threads are specially created to knit things for babies. The composition includes acrylic fiber (100%), but thanks to its hypoallergenic properties, the baby will feel comfortable. In addition, the artificial thread provides resistance to the formation of spools and loss of color, which is also important for children's things. The color palette of yarn is diverse: from delicate pastel shades to rich red or blue. Hanks have a small weight - 50 grams, the length of the thread - 150 m. To start knitting, you need knitting needles or a hook No. 2.5-3.5.





Begonia . Since the basis of the yarn is 100% cotton, it is ideal for summer things. A brilliant thread along with noble colors is what is valued at Begonia. Needleworkers make models using a hook, knitting needles or knitting machine. The convenient weight of the skein (50 grams) allows you to accurately calculate the required number of threads. The length of the fiber in the ball is more than three times its weight - 169 grams.



Jeans . The yarn composition is only 55% natural (cotton). The remaining 45% is polyacryl. Rich color gamut, unpretentiousness in leaving, good hygroscopicity, softness of fiber - Yarnart Jeans yarn has all of the listed properties. Basically, she goes to knit summer patterns for women and children; home objects (rugs, curtains), as well as children's crafts, are excellently obtained from her. A small ball of 50 grams contains 160 meters. For knitting need needles or hook number 3-3.5.





Harmony The brand also presents warm thread options. Wool with acrylic in pleasant melange shades will look great in knitted accessories. Interesting color transitions do not require complex patterns. A skein weighing 50 grams accounts for 80 meters of fiber. Knitting needles number 5-6.





Model Examples

Yarnart brand - actual yarn for knitting spring and summer wardrobe items. Thanks to the even thread, the pattern can be simple, for example, an elastic band or front surface. Openwork stripes will look interesting.





Yarn Yarnart: reviews

On the thematic forums and sites, knitters confirm the properties of threads declared to manufacturers. There are no complaints about quality. The only drawback that lovers of this yarn have recently encountered is the lack of flowers and the small product range of online stores.
