Canary Islands, La Palma Island: attractions, hotels, beaches and tourist reviews

Canary Islands - a popular destination for sea holidays. If you decide to go to them, we recommend paying attention to the island of La Palma. The Canary Islands are better known for the resort of Palma de Mallorca. Each of us certainly heard of him. In our article we want to talk about the island of La Palma, its attractions and the nuances of relaxation.

The island is very beautiful and attractive for tourists in terms of a relaxing holiday. It is of volcanic origin, as a result of which most of the coast is covered with rocks. There are many mountains on the island of La Palma, the highest point reaches 2387 meters. And in the northeast there are laurel cherry forests, which are unique in their kind. The climate of the region

La Palma Island (Canary Islands, Spain) is an ideal place to relax. No wonder it is called the island of eternal spring. The average sea temperature is +22 degrees, and it remains unchanged not only in summer but also in winter. In summer, the air temperature does not rise above +25 degrees, and in winter does not fall below +18 degrees. A moderate subtropical climate creates ideal conditions for a comfortable life. There is no heat on the island, as climatic conditions soften the northern winds blowing from the Azores.

Rains on La Palma are possible only in late spring and late autumn. In the summer, in September and October there is practically no rainfall. The island of La Palma (Canary Islands) is called the most beautiful and green.

What to see on vacation?

What to see on the island of La Palma (Canary Islands)? Tourists should definitely visit the capital. Santa Cruz will delight guests with its beautiful narrow streets and ancient buildings. In the center of the city is a small triangular square, which is called the Plaza of Spain. It is decorated with a stone fountain. There you can admire the Cathedral of the Savior with a bell tower and town hall. In addition, in the capital there is the Castillo de Santa Catalina fortress and the Maritime Museum, which is in the form of a life-size Columbus ship.

The town of Santa Cruz is very small. All of its attractions are located within a few blocks of the beautiful Avenida Maritima Promenade. In the city, it makes sense to see the 17th century Salazar Palace, take a walk along Dali Street, visit Constitution Square, admire the 16th century town hall. Tourists often go to the Tobacco House, where you can visit an interesting tour and buy real cigars.

National park

If you are interested in the sights of the island of La Palma (Canary Islands), then one of the places that is certainly worth a look is the National Park "Caldera de Taburiente". On its territory you will see a crater of gigantic dimensions, the diameter of which reaches 8 km, and its depth is 2 km. It was formed as a result of a powerful eruption of a volcano. The fourth National Park was created in 1954. On its territory, specially designed for guests hiking trails. Some of them are quite complex and require good physical preparation. During the walk you can watch the ‚ÄúGorge of Fear‚Äù and the volcanic rock. From Santa Cruz, the park can be reached by bus or by car.

One of the highest peaks in the reserve is Pico de la Cruz. You can climb it in five hours. One of the trails in the park allows you to admire the highest peaks and take wonderful photos from the observation platforms. On Mount Muchasos, the best site on the island has opened. You can get on it only during the tour, as it is located in the protected area. If you are not physically prepared, you can choose the easiest path for a walk. In this matter, you will be guided by park employees. According to tourists, the reserve is very beautiful and interesting. If you are a supporter of natural beauties, it is certainly worth a visit. During the tour you will have a great opportunity to make beautiful photos as a keepsake.

Los tilos

Another attraction of the island of La Palma (Canary Islands) is Los Tilos. Here, on the rocky slopes of the gorge, an ancient laurel forest has been preserved. In 1983, UNESCO declared Los Tilos a biosphere reserve. On its territory laid 5 km of hiking trails. One of the trails leads to the observation deck. Others abound in steep climbs and lead to waterfalls.

Los Cancajos

Los Canjacos Beach is located in the eastern part of La Palma Island (Canary Islands), just two kilometers from the airport and at the same distance from the capital. The beach is a bay, which is protected by a breakwater in the form of a rock of volcanic origin. On the coast throughout the year, the sea is incredibly calm and warm. Fans of scuba diving here are invariably impressed by the rich rich flora and fauna. The beach is one of the best places on the island for diving.

The main feature of the coast of the island of La Palma (Canary Islands, photo given in the article) is black sand of volcanic origin. Los Canjacos is no exception in this sense. If you want exotic, then the beach will please you with a black sand cover and warm water. The coast is equipped with all necessary infrastructure. Restaurants and cafes are nearby. You can get here from the capital by bus. In addition, there are several three and four star hotels on the coast.

Puerto Naos

According to reviews, on La Palma (Canary Islands) one of the best beaches is Puerto Naos. He is awarded the Blue Flag. The beach is located on the southwest coast of the island, in the tourist center of Puerto Naos. Paragliding fans regularly gather here. Black sand on the coast is of volcanic origin. It has a slightly greenish tint due to olivine rocks. On the coast there is a promenade with coconut trees, in the shade of which vacationers are hiding from the sun. There are also cafes and restaurants, and hotels are nearby. On the beach, for diving enthusiasts in the evenings in the dark they organize immersion in water. Tourists note that the night sea is no less beautiful.

Bahamar Beach

In the east of the island is the beach "Bahamar", which is considered the most visited. It is located near Santa Cruz, which is why it is popular with locals. It is equipped with all amenities, parking and restaurants.

The beach reaches a length of 700 meters and is covered with black sand. A beautiful promenade is equipped along it, along which tourists walk in the rays of the setting sun. By the way, guests from all over the island come here to enjoy the beauty of the landscape at dawn.

Beach among the volcanoes

According to tourists, on La Palma (Canary Islands) is worth a visit to the "Ecentive". The beach is located in the south of the island. He can safely be called the youngest, since he appeared only in 1971. The reason for its formation was the eruption of Tenegy volcano. The length of the beach reaches 275 meters. You can get to it by car or on foot. The advantage of this section of the coast is that there are beautiful natural reservoirs in which you can safely swim even during periods of strong waves. Nearby is the healing source Fuente Santa, which also appeared after the eruption. Tourists note that it is interesting not only to relax on the beach, but also to walk through the picturesque surroundings. To admire the picturesque corner of the island.

Puerto de Tacacorte

The Puerto de Tacacorte beach, located on the west coast of the island, is not less attractive. It will appeal to those people who like to relax surrounded by natural beauties, but at the same time have access to all the benefits of civilization. The beach is far from the city, but it is equipped with bars and cafes. It is recommended for families. The coast is protected by a breakwater, so there are no sea currents and waves. The beach is suitable for swimming even for the smallest guests.
