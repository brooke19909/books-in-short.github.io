Hunchun Hotels: list, top rated, addresses, room reservation, services, pricing, visitor reviews and tourist tips

Chinese Hunchun is located in the southeast of Jilin, the name translated from Manchu means "end of the river."

Recently, the city has changed so much that on the territory of this, though former, village, the economy, infrastructure, trade are rapidly developing, and with them the number of hotels and hotels is increasing. Hunchun can also be called a budget health resort (due to reasonable prices for medical services) and a tourist center.

How to get there

The best way for residents of the Primorsky Territory is to buy a bus ticket, which leaves twice a day from the village of Slavyanka. The rest will have to buy a ticket to Vladivostok and use the services of travel agencies.

Weather

The climate in Monsoon is marine. It is quite warm throughout the year: the temperature does not drop below +5. Winter is dry, summer is rainy.

Shopping

According to tourists, this is a great place for bargain shopping. Nice prices and high quality. Bargaining is appropriate everywhere, so there is an opportunity to save money.

Interested in? Then start packing your bags, and we will help you choose the best option.

Rating of the best hotels

In the Chinese city of Hunchun, it is easy to find an accommodation option for any budget. Here you will find both cheap hotels from 700 rubles (80 yuan) per day, and expensive 4 * hotels offering more comfortable conditions.

Before telling about the most popular hotels in Hunchun, we suggest reading a tourist guide:

  • check-in and check-out are from noon to 2 p.m.
  • usually guests are offered the minimum set of accessories necessary for personal hygiene: shower gel, shampoo, toothbrush and soap;
  • almost everywhere breakfast is included in the room rate, and in more expensive hotels dinner is included;
  • upon check-in, a deposit is paid, which is returned on the day of eviction.

So, the most popular among tourists are hotels:

1. Shengbo International Hotel.

The four-star Shenbo is located at the very end of the city's shopping street. Walking distance to a mini market, karaoke bar, Longyuan Park and a wellness center. The hotel was built in 2011, and the last restoration was carried out in 2015.

  • Address: Yanhe West Street.
  • Cost of living: from 2 150 (business room) to 6 250 rubles (deluxe suite).

According to Hunchun hotel reviews, Shengbo International Hotel is considered the best in city by comfort.

2. Tumenjiang Business Hotel 4 *

The hotel was built 8 years ago. The rooms are located on floors 2-8. Across the street from the hotel are a sauna and a Chinese restaurant, a bit further the Arbat shopping center and the Amusement Park.

  • Address: Longyuan West Street, 1339.
  • Cost of living: from 2,200 (standard double) to 8,500 rubles (lux).

3. Xierdun Hotel Hunchun 5 *

Travel tips: where to go and what to see

After checking into the Hunchun Hotel, it's time to take a sightseeing tour to get to know the city. On average, pleasure will cost 500 rubles.

For shopping it is better to go to the Gomao shopping center. For example, good and stylish jeans can be bought here for 300 rubles!

Be sure to visit the "8 horses." Each resident in his own way interprets the significance of the monument. But 3 sides are important here, each of which symbolizes China, Russia and North Korea. It is not surprising, because Hunchun is located on the borders of these states.

Another place where tourists recommend going is the city square. Every day from 6 to 9 pm people of different ages gather here. They stand in neat rows and ... begin to dance, repeating the movements of the volunteers. Anyone can join the universal flash mob. So go ahead, because movement is life!
