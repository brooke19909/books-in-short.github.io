How does a bank credit card work?

Many banks offer credit cards to their customers. But before using them, you need to understand the conditions under which the funds are provided. If you know how a credit card works, it will be very easy to understand all the intricacies of a financial product.

Definition

Credit card - a banking product offered to customers of a financial institution. On it you can take a loan for a certain amount and term. The service is similar to a standard consumer loan, but still has its own differences. How a credit card works, you can find out by the terms, requirements, principles of provision.





Conditions

This is set by the bank. Each institution offers its own conditions. Somewhere they are beneficial to people, but somewhere they are not. How does a credit card work in most banks? She always has a bet, a limited period and amount. Some have a grace period.

The average rate on Russian banks is in the range of 24-35%. Not always a low percentage means benefits, since an additional amount may be taken for insurance. The lending period is usually 3-5 years.

Borrower Requirements

To receive a credit card, a client must meet certain requirements:

  • citizenship of the Russian Federation;
  • age - from 18 to 70 years;
  • good credit history.




These are the basic requirements. For some programs, an income statement is required. Typically, loans are issued without guarantors and collateral.

Getting

How does a credit card work in receiving? Banks issue it by various methods. Often plastic is provided at the bank with a personal visit. It is often sent by mail if the client has already used the services of a financial institution.

There are banks that deliver cards to the house by courier, but usually these services are only available in large cities. To receive you need to apply at the office or online. A convenient delivery option is sometimes offered.

Documents

Depending on the bank, the following documents may be required:

  1. Passport.
  2. INN




Sometimes additional papers are needed, for example, when applying for a large loan, a certificate of employment is requested.

Features of work

Understanding how a credit card of a Sberbank or other institution works is very simple. It functions in the same way as the standard salary. You can withdraw funds through an ATM if you need cash. It is also possible to pay in stores for purchases and services.

You just need to know before registration what interest is charged in each case, since the rates for receiving cash and for bank transfer usually differ. It is necessary to pay the debt in a timely manner so that no fines are accrued, because of which a large amount will have to be returned.

Grace period

How does a grace period work for a credit card? This feature is in almost every bank. This is the period during which you can pay debt without interest. Usually it is 50-60 days. If during this period the loan is not paid, then the amount is recalculated at the current interest.





Limit

There is a limit on cards. Usually it is 300-500 thousand rubles. Often its size is set based on customer income. Some banks offer special cards, such as car loans. Then the limit can reach up to 1 million rubles.

The limit is determined by the specifics of the bank, as well as the offer. Why a credit card does not work, you can find out from an employee of the institution. This may be due to various reasons, for example, lack of activation.

How to use?

Will credit cards work to pay for purchases? They are designed for this. Some banks have bonus systems that provide customers with benefits.





You can withdraw money through ATMs. If the device belongs to the bank, then, as a rule, there will be no commissions for such services. If the apparatus of another institution, then there will be a fee, the size of which may be different.

Repayment of a debt

It is necessary to pay the debt in a timely manner so that fines are not charged. You can deposit funds in several ways:

  1. Through the terminal of any bank - a commission of 2-5%.
  2. Depositing funds using transfers from other cards.

If you do not deposit funds in a timely manner, then the percentage valid under the contract will be established. It is fixed. Usually, a clear rate is set, specified in the contract, which is signed at the time of loan processing.

Some banks provide a delay if the client does not have the opportunity to pay the debt. Only this must be warned in advance. In case of late payment, credit history worsens. Then in the future it will be difficult to get a loan from this bank.

Pros and cons

Using such programs from the bank has advantages and disadvantages. From the pros, you can distinguish:

  • settlement in stores;
  • bonuses and accumulations;
  • Grace period;
  • minimum requirements;
  • clearance of large amounts;
  • many payment options;
  • Internet banking;
  • registration of an online application.

Among the shortcomings are high interest rates, instability of the exchange rate (if the dollar card). There is also a commission for cash withdrawals.
