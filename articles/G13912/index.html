Hotel Xeno Sugar Beach Hotel 4 *, Alanya, Turkey: description, features and reviews

Alanya is one of the most popular resort places in Turkey, where millions of tourists flock annually. Hotel Xeno Sugar Beach Hotel 4 * - one of the best places for a summer vacation.





Short hotel description

One of the main advantages of the Xeno Sugar Beach Hotel 4 * is a large private beach, equipped with a huge number of sun loungers and umbrellas to protect from the scorching Turkish sun. In case of bad weather and strong waves there is the opportunity to swim in the pool with water slides.

Much attention is paid to the comfort of the rooms, as well as to the presence in them of all necessary amenities and household appliances. It is also worth visiting the steam sauna or Turkish hammam at least once to relax or relieve stress after a busy day.

Guests of the Xeno Sugar Beach Hotel 4 * have a lot of entertainment to fill in their leisure time. Also, do not forget about the food, which is offered in the form of a buffet. And during the day, guests will not remain hungry, because the bars are ready all day to treat their visitors with snacks and drinks.

Location

Xeno Sugar Beach Hotel 4 * is located in the Mahmutlar district, which is located about 10 km from the city center, on the seashore, quite remote from the center of Alanya. The nearest airport to the hotel is located in Antalya and is 140 km away.

Guest rooms

More and more tourists are attracted to their shores by Turkey. Xeno Hotels Sugar Beach 4 * hotel has 144 guest rooms, which are compactly located in a large six-story building. The hotel has apartments of two categories: standard and suite. The difference between them is mainly in the area.

Standard rooms are very small rooms (23 sq. M.), Which can accommodate two guests, as well as another one on an extra bed. The suite is 2 times larger - 48 square meters. There are two spacious bedrooms for four people. For two more guests, extra beds can be accommodated.





Room Service and Facilities

Xeno Sugar Beach Hotel 4 * hotel cares about the comfort of its guests, so the maids daily carry out a full cleaning of the guest rooms. Three times a week, linen is replaced, and towels - as needed.

The following amenities are available in the apartments:

  • each room has a balcony from which you can enjoy the sea or pool;
  • in the bathroom, depending on the category of apartments, there is a bath or shower;
  • floors are covered with warm and soft carpet;
  • temperature control by means of an air conditioner is made individually for each room;
  • the minibar can be used as a refrigerator or replenished with drinks at an additional cost;
  • modern TV connected to a satellite dish;
  • safe, the use of which is paid;
  • hair dryer;
  • telephone for communication with the reception and calls to all countries of the world.

Hotel Xeno Sugar Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Antalya) has a rich tourist infrastructure, due to which the remoteness of the resort and hotel complex from the city center is practically not felt. So, guests can take advantage of the following facilities and facilities:

Nutrition

The system by which the food is provided at the Xeno Sugar Beach Hotel 4 * hotel is All Inclusive. In each hotel, this mechanism is somewhat modified depending on stardom, as well as management policy. So, in this All-Inclusive hotel, it implies the provision of the following food services to guests:

  • breakfast, lunch, lunch, afternoon snack and dinner according to the buffet system in the main restaurant of the hotel;
  • after lunch, you can have a snack in the bars with light snacks and snacks;
  • at midnight, the main restaurant opens for guests so that they can satisfy their hunger with soup and a variety of sandwiches;
  • local and local alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks are available in bars throughout the day (imported alcohol and fresh juices are available only in restaurants for an additional fee).




Fun and relaxation

It is impossible to imagine a Turkish resort without incendiary entertainment and active sporting events. Hotel Xeno Sugar Beach Hotel 4 * (Alanya) has prepared for its guests a rich and vibrant program:

  • daily morning exercises, as well as fitness and water aerobics classes with a sports animator;
  • entertainment program and evening show;
  • visit to the gym with the most modern equipment and professional trainers;
  • in one of the bars you can play billiards in a cozy atmosphere;
  • there is a water entertainment center on the beach, which provides a huge selection of fun, and most importantly - safe rides;
  • You can improve your health and relax in the massage room, where skilled masters work, owning a variety of techniques;
  • lovers of actively spending time will certainly be glad to have the opportunity to play beach volleyball or table tennis (all the necessary equipment is provided).




Additional Information

Going to the Xeno Sugar Beach Hotel 4 * hotel (Alanya), do not forget to read the internal rules that apply in this resort and hotel complex, namely:

  • check-in of newly arrived guests in the rooms starts no earlier than at 14:00, if you want to receive the room keys immediately upon arrival, then for this service you will have to pay the additional fee set by the hotel;
  • on the day of departure, you must leave your room and hand in the keys for reception no later than noon, otherwise you will have to pay extra every hour of excess occupancy in the room;
  • Children under 11 years old stay in the room free of charge, subject to the use of existing beds;
  • for babies under the age of two, an extra crib is provided at no extra charge;
  • placing a guest on an extra bed is estimated at 70% of the cost of living in a room;
  • pets are not allowed in the hotel.
