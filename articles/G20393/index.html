Bulgaria, Sunny Beach. Kiparisite 4 *: photos, prices and reviews of tourists

One of the most popular resorts in Bulgaria, which has long been loved by Russians, is Sunny Beach. This fact is explained very easily: here the mild and healthy climate and the clean Black Sea are perfectly complemented by a variety of entertainment and a wide hotel base, where everyone can choose their accommodation in accordance with their taste and financial capabilities. If you also plan to spend your vacation in the resort "Sunny Beach" (Bulgaria), the hotel "Kiparisite 4 *" will be a great option for a comfortable, interesting and affordable holiday. We suggest you get to know this hotel more closely and find out what impression our compatriots left in it.





Where is the Hotel Kiparisite

This hotel is located in the northern part of the Bulgarian resort region of Sunny Beach, in a small village called Zora. Just eight kilometers from the hotel is a famous attraction - the ancient city of Nessebar. As for the air harbor, it is 35 km away from Kiparisite (Sunny Beach). Thus, upon arrival in Bulgaria, your journey to the hotel will take no more than an hour. As for the sea, the beach is only 80 meters away. So literally in a couple of minutes you can walk to the coast and plunge into the warm waters of the Black Sea.





What is a hotel "Cypress"

Hotel 4 * Kiparisite is a modern and comfortable place, featuring original bright and memorable design. He received the first guests in 2006, and since then there have been several reconstructions. The hotel consists of one five-story building, decorated in yellow, red and white colors. The architects of the hotel did their best, so even when you come here for the first time, you will immediately recognize ‚ÄúCypress‚Äù against the background of other buildings and structures. The hotel has 85 comfortable rooms, stylishly decorated and equipped with everything necessary for living. On the territory of Kiparisite 4 * there is a restaurant, bars and a pool with a sun terrace. This hotel is perfect for family people planning a vacation in Bulgaria with children, as well as for young cheerful companies.

Posting Rules

As in the vast majority of hotels, check-in is after 14:00. If you have arrived earlier, then if you have free rooms you will be settled immediately. Otherwise (this happens often in high season) you will be asked to leave your luggage in the storage room and go for a snack, take a swim in the pool, take a walk in the surroundings or go straight to the beach. In accordance with the rules of Kiparisite 4 * (Sunny Beach), on the day of departure, the room should be vacated before noon. When handing over the keys, it is necessary to make a payment for accommodation, as well as the use of additional services. The hotel accepts payment both in cash and with plastic cards ("Visa" or "Mastercard").





Accommodation for children and animals

The four-star Hotel Kiparisite is always happy for guests traveling with their children. If you place your baby in your room up to two years old in a crib, then you will have to pay 4 Euros for each night of stay. If you want to place an older child or adult in your room on an extra bed, then this will cost you 80% of the room price per person per day. Keep in mind that in each room you can install only one extra or a baby bed. You should also notify the Kiparisite 4 * hotel in advance of the need to provide this service and receive confirmation.

As for pets, here we are forced to disappoint people going on vacation in the company of their four-legged friends. So, in accordance with the rules of the hotel, the accommodation on its territory of guests with pets is prohibited.





Number of rooms

As already mentioned, the Kiparisite 4 * hotel (Sunny Beach) has 85 comfortable and stylishly decorated rooms. 76 of them are standard double rooms, and nine are apartments. In addition, the hotel has several rooms equipped for disabled guests. All rooms can accommodate extra beds. The hotel must be notified in advance of the need to provide such a service. Each air-conditioned room has a TV, minibar, bathroom, hairdryer, telephone and a balcony with beautiful views. The apartments also have a kitchenette and kitchenware. Rooms are cleaned and towels are regularly changed. Also you have the opportunity to order a room service.

Nutrition

The cost of living in the hotel Kiparisite 4 * (Bulgaria) includes only breakfast, which is served in the restaurant as a buffet. Here you can have lunch and dinner for a fee. Skilled chefs will be happy to prepare for you a variety of dishes from both Bulgarian and international cuisine. You can also pamper yourself with great fresh seafood dishes.

In addition to the restaurant with an outdoor terrace for 90 people, the hotel has bars: in the lobby, by the pool, as well as a panoramic rooftop bar with a beautiful view. Here you can drink tea or coffee, as well as treat yourself to your favorite soft drinks and alcoholic drinks.





Beach, sea

Just 80 meters from the Kiparisite 4 * hotel (Bulgaria) is a sandy and clean city beach. Here you can use sun loungers, umbrellas and mattresses for a fee. Also on the beach there are bars, restaurants and opportunities for relaxing on the water. The entrance to the sea is sandy and gentle, and the water in the summer is very warm and pleasant.

Pools

Hotel Kiparisite 4 * has its own pool. Near it there is a sun terrace with comfortable sun loungers and umbrellas. There is also a bar where you can order a variety of soft and alcoholic drinks. The hotel did not ignore the smallest guests. So, for them there is a separate children's pool.

Entertainment

As a rule, Kiparisite 4 * guests go to the beach or to the city for entertainment. But if you want to stay at the hotel, then you have the opportunity to play table tennis, swim in the pool, sunbathe on the terrace, do aerobics, attend a massage or go to the gym.

As for the smallest guests of Kiparisite 4 *, for them the hotel has a shallow pool, a playground, and in the evenings there is a mini disco. You can also use the babysitting service.

Cost of living

The cost of accommodation in the Bulgarian hotel "Cypress" is fully consistent with its level of stardom. So, accommodation in a standard double room will cost you from 50 euros per night, and in apartments - from 60 euros. At the height of the season, the cost will be significantly higher: about 85 and 115 euros, respectively.
