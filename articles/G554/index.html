Where to take a walk in winter in Moscow? What does the snow capital look like? Places and attractions of the city

Constantly many people come to the capital in order to explore the main attractions of Moscow. In winter, as at any other time of the year, you can see the famous ensembles of Red Square, visit the Bolshoi Theater and other institutions with big names. But, in addition to this, the capital offers a lot of winter entertainment. They are attended by all residents of the city, regardless of age and affluence. Where to take a walk in winter in Moscow? What places will be the most picturesque? What is interesting to meet in the winter in the capital? Read about it below.

Boring Garden

The recreation area is located next to PKiO them. Gorky and goes to the promenade. Here, the gaze reveals magnificent landscapes in which the nature and movement of the city are mixed. In calm weather, you can stroll through the park with a group of friends or with a full family. If you are a fan of extreme entertainment, the slides of the Neskuchny Garden will be your next discovery. Here they are for every taste and color. There are gentle, straight, there are winding, steep, with sharp changes in height. It is especially interesting to descend from the local slides on snow scooters. They can be rented directly in Neskuchny Garden, if you do not have your own.



Favorite place of city dwellers

As a rule, if people are looking for where to go in Moscow in winter, then we are talking about winter active entertainment. Most often, children and their parents attend ice rinks, which are more in the capital than, probably, in all of Russia. If you want to not only ride, but also learn the basics of figure skating, then go to the Krasnaya Presnya park. There is a large ice rink with artificial turf. Plunge into the summer

Everyone is looking for the most interesting place where to take a walk in the winter in Moscow so that both cheeks and nose are red from frost. But we offer flora lovers to plunge into the real world of summer and explore all varieties of winter orchids that bloom in the Pharmacy Garden in December, January and February. This is one of the branches of the Botanical Garden of Moscow State University, which is located on Prospect Mira. The floristic corner of the capital was founded at the beginning of the 18th century and still, despite the noise and bustle of the capital's streets, remains quiet and calm. Here, the attention of visitors are presented orchids of various varieties, among which Dracula is most popular. Compositions of tropical plants - ferns, bromeliads and, naturally, orchids themselves are exhibited in the palm greenhouse.





The Hermitage in the modern capital

Where to take a walk in Moscow in winter, so that it would be cold, as it should be on frosty days, and at the same time cozy? The Hermitage Garden, which is located on Karetny Ryad Street, can please its visitors with these pleasures. Two ice rinks are open here with the possibility of renting and sharpening skates. They are surrounded by endless silent coffee houses. Among such institutions there are ordinary cafes where schoolchildren can have a snack and drink tea, as well as elite restaurants, albeit small ones. From the windows you can watch the winter life of the capital, enjoying hot drinks.



Kolomensky park

Where to go for a walk in winter in Moscow is cheap and incredibly interesting? An ice rink, a warm dressing room and a small restaurant operate on the territory of Kolomensky Park . But this is by no means its most important attractions. There are two ski slopes, the length of which is approximately equal to five kilometers. Ski equipment rental - 200 rubles per hour. The most interesting thing is that the tracks go beyond the territory of the park and stretch along the Moskva River and the surrounding streets. Both ski roads are isolated from traffic and are completely safe.





We are participating in the quest

In Kolomensky park, renting skis on the Fairground, you can take part in the quest. To do this, you need to activate this game through your smartphone and hit the road. This quest is unique, it is possible to see the beautiful and ancient sights of Moscow. In winter, when it is cold around and we all occasionally look out of the windows of cars or public transport, the cathedrals and the most beautiful buildings of past eras go unnoticed. When skiing in the open air, you will pass by the museum of wooden architecture, the house of Peter the Great, you will see the Ascension Church and a number of ancient temples.

Other Moscow parks in winter

Park areas of our capital attract both tourists and residents of the city at any time of the year. In the cold season, numerous ice rinks open here, cozy coffee houses are equipped, so everyone can spend time both outdoors and in a pleasant place surrounded by the company. In many parks (Gorky, Izmailovsky, the same Kolomenskoye) they offer such an attraction as an inflatable sled banana. This is an analogue of a sea banana that rolls along the waves. On such sleds you can freely roll and shout, recharge with positive and new impressions for the coming week.

Traditional metropolitan entertainment

The city of our country, which at any time of the year pleases with bright entertainment and attractions, is Moscow. VDNH in winter is a traditional place for spending a weekend for all residents of the capital. Here, most of the extreme attractions that are open in the summer are valid. In addition to them, the rink is being poured - one of the largest in the city. The number of cafes is increasing dramatically. In winter, ice slides are open for children at VDNH, and there is always a fair with home-made goods. Therefore, if you still have not decided where to go in Moscow in the winter, go here. On the expanses of VDNH you will find everything from extreme sports to handmade soap.
