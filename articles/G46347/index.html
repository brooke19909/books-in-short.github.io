How to remove smells from the refrigerator experienced housewives?

Everyone faced the problem of unpleasant aroma from the refrigerator at least once. You may be surprised, but not only old appliances with completely filled shelves smell, but also completely new aggregates, in which no more than 10 names of products are stored. What to do if a fragrance has appeared? How do experienced housewives remove odors from the refrigerator at home?





Determine the source of aroma

If an unpleasant odor suddenly appears in the refrigerator , it 's time to carefully sort through its contents. It is likely that in one of the far corners you will find a package of yogurt, forgotten a week ago, in the open form or a stick of sausage, aged several months. We all know that the best treatment is prevention, therefore, the first rule is to regularly throw out spoiled foods and check everything by the timing of use. Some dishes also have a fresh, specific aroma that will only harm other foods. We are talking about fish, smoked meats, seasonings with a strong smell. How to remove odors from the refrigerator from fresh food that smells strongly? The easiest way is to keep each dish or product in a hermetically sealed container, but if the aroma has already settled in the refrigerator, you should use one of the odor absorbers. You can use both a store and a home remedy, but we will talk about this in detail below.

Remember to rinse thoroughly before using new appliances designed to store food inside. If after processing with a regular cleanser the aroma does not disappear, try rinsing it with a solution of water with vinegar or lemon, diluting in a ratio of 1: 1. You can use baking soda, which not only removes odors, but also kills bacteria.

How to kill the smell in the refrigerator: an absorber from the store or home remedies?



In the stores of household appliances and household goods, you can easily find ionizers or odor absorbers for refrigerators. It is enough to buy this accessory and periodically clean or replace it - and freshness will always reign in your household appliance. There are also home alternatives. It is enough to put several slices of brown bread in different places of the refrigerator - and the problem of unpleasant smell will never be relevant for you again. Coal is also suitable, it can be wood or activated. Grind it into powder and also lay out on shelves. How to remove odors from the refrigerator with soda? This substance can be used both in dry and in dissolved form. You can put small containers with mortar or dry powder in the unit. Removing odor from the refrigerator will not cause you any serious trouble if you wash the appliance thoroughly every three months. To do this, it should be thawed and completely unloaded, then washed inside, after pulling out all the shelves, drawers and food stands. All these elements are washed each separately, then dried and distributed in their places. It is especially worthwhile to rinse the gum on the doors, as food residues often accumulate in them. Before turning on the refrigerator after washing, it must be ventilated - just leave it for several hours with the door open.
