Exclusive panel in original technique





Silk painting has been one of the most popular hobbies in Europe for many years. Demanding perseverance, such a hobby can give real masterpieces. For work you will need:

  • any motive you like;
  • a piece of silk of the appropriate size and neutral color;
  • paints for work on silk fabrics ;
  • contour pen for transferring a picture to a material;
  • varnish for fixing paints;
  • frame for painting on silk;
  • cardboard;
  • frame for the final decor of the work.

First of all, you need to pull a piece of silk on the frame. Then you need to transfer the pattern to the fabric using a special pen.

Thoroughly dry the circuit. Drying the first lines makes it possible to more clearly draw each element. After the contours are fixed, you can proceed to the most interesting thing - coloring. Having completed this stage, it is also necessary to wait for the complete drying of the material. After that, the picture is removed from the frame and the excess material is cut off. The finished work must be fixed on a cardboard sheet and inserted into the frame. Everything, an exclusive New Year‚Äôs panel, made with your own hands, is ready. Such a decoration, by the way, can be a great gift.

Christmas panel from salt dough

From such fairly ordinary products that are available in absolutely every home, you can create real works of art! In addition, even a child can perform a New Year's panel of salt dough , so do not forget to attract your children.

In order to prepare a mass for such a panel, you will need only four ingredients: 1/2 cup flour and salt, 1/4 cup plain water and 1 tablespoon of any vegetable oil.

So, we mix all the dry ingredients, add oil and pour cold water in a thin stream. Knead until the mass for the future panel becomes homogeneous and elastic.





We begin to sculpt. The selected motif must be printed in full size and put under the glass. At the last, it is necessary to model each of the elements of the future panel. Leave to dry completely (this will take several days). After the panel has dried, it can be glued to the cardboard base, painted with paints and inserted into the frame.

New Year panels of fabric can be made using the patchwork method. To do this, select a sketch and select materials. A few hours at the sewing machine - and you are the author of an amazing piece of folk art.

New Year's panel (patchwork) from polyfoam





If you don‚Äôt like to sew, you can use the fairly common foam-based patchwork technique. To work, you will need:

  • a foam sheet 2.5-3 cm thick;
  • motive (avoid drawings with very small elements);
  • the cloth;
  • sharp clerical knife;
  • glue.

The technique is simple: we transfer the motif to the foam, cut each line of the contour with a knife to a depth of 1.5-2 cm, insert a fabric of the corresponding color into the slots (we cut the patches to fit the size of the element, leaving a few centimeters to keep the fabric firmly on the foam) . Lubricate the edges of the material before putting them into the foam. All! New Year‚Äôs panel, made with your own hands from improvised materials, decorates your wall. Children can be involved in creating panels, in particular, at the stage of filling out the contours of a drawing.

New Year wreath panel

You can decorate the walls or the front door with New Year wreaths very beautifully. This simple enough element will bring a fresh stream and add a festive mood.

In order to make a wreath of Christmas tree branches, you will need:

  • branches of various conifers (preferably thin);
  • ring - the basis for a wreath with a diameter of 20-25 cm;
  • wire.

Branches should be cut into pieces about 5-6 centimeters long, assembled into small bouquets. Using wire, fix the Christmas tree blanks on the ring, placing them so that the base is not visible. Garnish with flowers or artificial berries of viburnum.





You can decorate a ring not only with branches of conifers. A wreath made of moss will also look great. You can decorate such a panel-wreath with bows or any available natural material.
