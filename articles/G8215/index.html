Wadduwa, Sri Lanka: weather, hotels, reviews

The place where the resort town of Wadduwa is located is Sri Lanka. This is one of the most exotic and mysterious territories on the whole island. Her alluring nature has become the star of numerous advertising posters and brochures. On the pages of travel magazines you can see amazing pictures that attract travelers to these lands. Colorful pictures promise a chic vacation among palm trees, swimming in crystal blue and clear ocean waters.

This pearl of tourism is located near the capital of Sri Lanka - Colombo. In its deep history, the city of Wadduwa was famous for the work of local doctors. The Veddas lived here in large numbers. The people called them "smiling residents." From this came the name of the city of Wadduwa (Sri Lanka).
