Often inexpensive trips to the UAE can be bought in the summer. But Russians usually do not like to go there at this time. The fact is that in the Emirates in the summer months, real hell reigns. Tours in Sharjah

This emirate in itself is a great option for a budget holiday in the United Arab Emirates. Last-minute tours to the UAE (Sharjah) can be even cheaper. Traditional culture, modern skyscrapers, green oases of parks and the amazing beauty of the lagoon, as well as excursions, shopping and a variety of entertainment await those who decide to come here. But still, travelers should know that Sharjah is the exact opposite of Dubai.

Strict morals reign here, alcohol is completely banned, and even the morale police work. Therefore, tourists should dress in accordance with local decency. But almost all the hotels here are located on the first line, there are more beaches, unlike Dubai, and bathers can choose between the Gulf of Oman and the Gulf. Most often people come here not only because food and accommodation in Sharjah are the cheapest, but also because only here you can see the real Arabic flavor, try local dishes and buy original souvenirs.

Tours in Abu Dhabi

If you have already decided to travel to the Emirates, you can consider another not too expensive travel option. This is Abu Dhabi. Such a tour in the UAE can also be last minute, and also very interesting. This emirate has quite affordable prices, although, of course, there are many hotels here, the cost of living in which is simply sky-high. This is the actual capital of the country, there is the seat of government. Here you can count not only on beach vacations, shopping and excursions, but also on entertainment in night clubs and discos.

This emirate has the largest park in the entire Middle East - Cornish Road, which stretches along the coast for ten kilometers. And over its entire length more than 60 fountains are installed. If you value the romance of the desert combined with modern technology - this is the emirate for you. A cheap last-minute trip to Abu Dhabi is a wonderful opportunity to appreciate this fastest growing resort in the UAE.

But the smallest price for an all-inclusive package at a hotel of this type in Dubai will be one and a half thousand dollars. Of course, this is the cost of a regular trip.
