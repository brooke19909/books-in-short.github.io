Hotel complex HolidayInn

We begin the review of hotels in Samara from this hotel complex. Hospitality, comfortable living conditions, a large-scale aquacomplex and high-level service are offered to guests by the Holiday Inn hotel, located on the banks of the Volga. There are apartments for the disabled. Children under 17 years old stay with their parents for free. According to experienced, a good location and a full range of services led to an endless stream of guests in this institution.

The interior of the room is classic with modern elements of decor. The furniture set includes a bed, a coffee table, a sofa, a chair, a desk, a wardrobe. The floors are carpeted. Snow-white walls and ceilings visually increase dimensions. On the walls are paintings. There is air conditioning with a TV. The minibar is filled with alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks upon check-in. A bathrobe, hygiene products and slippers are located in the bathroom with a shower. Smoking is strictly prohibited.

Food and pastime

You can satisfy your hunger in one of the functioning restaurants: Atrium and Brazil. The first serves a buffet breakfast. The second - specializes in dishes of Russian and European cuisine.

The main specialization of pastime is water. Swimming pools, a Turkish bath, a sauna, a hydromassage and a spa center will allow vacationers to relax after a busy or excursionly busy day. The fitness center will allow you to maintain excellent body shape and cheerful mood.

Wellness at the Dubrava Hotel

One of the great places to spend an unforgettable weekend or vacation is the hotel complex "Dubrava", which is located in the forest zone of the city of Samara. Walking along the well-groomed alleys, shrouded in green slender trees from which it blows with freshness and invigorating strength, you can not only relax, forgetting about the unbridled pace of life, but also increase immunity.

The history of a three-star hotel dates back to 2007. The main building of the Dubrava hotel (Samara, Krasnoglinskoye sh. 40) contains 34 comfortable rooms located on four floors.
