Hotel Hotel Stella Maris 3 * (Spain, Blanes): description and reviews

Every year, Spanish resorts are visited by a huge number of travelers who wish to relax in comfortable conditions arranged by European standards. Often, guests of the country travel by bus, picking up housing at the place of arrival, but many still get tickets from trusted tour operators, the package of which includes accommodation in a comfortable hotel room with all amenities. One of the most popular places of this type is Stella Maris Hotel 3 * in Blanes (Costa Brava).



Location

This hotel is located near the sandy sea coast called S'Abanel, in the very center of entertainment: near the place of recreation there are a huge number of restaurants, bars, night clubs and other places for entertainment. Not far from it there is a public transport stop with which you can get anywhere in the city.

Tourists, resting here, can enjoy the mild and warm climate, here is especially pleasant sunny weather in the midst of the resort period - from mid-April to mid-October.

Rooms

The hotel has 98 apartments, which are located in its main building. The windows of each room overlook the sea, so that guests have the opportunity to enjoy beautiful views of the picturesque landscapes. All rooms of the hotel are divided into two types: "standard" and "family".

Each apartment has a private bathroom with a shower, sink, toilet and mirror. Bath amenities include soap, shampoo and shower gel. On an ongoing basis, guests can enjoy free access to the Internet (Wi-Fi), so they always stay in touch with their family and friends during the entire period of rest. All apartments have a private balcony and climate control, so residents can independently adjust the temperature conditions based on individual preferences.

In some rooms of both one and the second category there is a kitchen corner, which is equipped with everything necessary for cooking homemade food. There is also a telephone in some apartments, for which you have to pay.



"Standard"

Apartments of the "standard" category are divided into two groups: those that are designed for one person, and those in which two guests can live.

As for furniture, it is ordinary. As sleeping places there is a large or small bed with a hard mattress, a cupboard for personal items, a desk for work and a chair.

"Family"

This type of apartment is designed for a much larger number of guests - up to 4 people. This number is often chosen by guests who come to relax with the whole family. Here you can sit very comfortably with small children - a separate bed is provided for them.

As for the furniture equipment, it is similar to what is presented in the standard rooms. In addition to all this, there is a plasma TV with the ability to broadcast satellite channels.



Nutrition

Each guest of Stella Maris Hotel 3 * (Costa Brava), despite the huge selection of catering establishments near the hotel, has the opportunity to eat without going beyond it. To do this, there is a bar and restaurant.

The bar serves light snacks and salads, fancy desserts and homemade specialties. In hot weather, bright cocktails and soft drinks are in great demand, and in the evenings, guests love to spend time here with a cup of tea or coffee.

The on-site restaurant serves buffet meals. It serves delicious dishes daily, prepared according to traditional recipes of Mediterranean and European cuisines, there are a lot of seafood.

Entertainment

At Stella Maris Hotel 3 * (Spain) there is no team of animators, however, guests never have to get bored staying here. Vacationers have the opportunity to play billiards, darts, chess, as well as table tennis for free.

For a fee, you can learn horse riding, canoeing, and go to the tennis court or golf course, which are located next door to the hotel.

At night, guests can have fun at a disco, where they play music performed by talented local DJs.

Around the pool is a small sundeck, which has comfortable sun loungers, beach umbrellas and towels. Also, if you wish, you can rent an air mattress.



In colder times, the indoor pool, which is located in the main building, is gaining popularity. Guests can also visit it for free and relax.

Beach

Literally a five-minute walk from the Hotel Stella Maris 3 * (Spain / Costa Brava) building there is a municipal sandy beach, which is equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable pastime. There are all amenities: locker room, toilets and showers. In addition, there is a rental point for air mattresses, towels, sun loungers and umbrellas, which will save you from direct sunlight.

The most active tourists have the opportunity to have fun right on the sea coast: here you can ride on an air cushion, water ski, and also master the skills of diving to depth with scuba diving.

Services

The hotel guests are provided with a wide range of services on an ongoing basis, which they can use if they wish.

There is a 24-hour reception desk where guests can consult on any issues of interest to them related to living conditions and the provision of services. The hotel has attentive and responsive administrators, some of whom are fluent in Russian. Here you can rent a storage room for large luggage for a certain period of time - it is paid daily and depending on the size.



For a fee, residents can use the laundry and dry cleaning services. Here you can rent a car or bike and travel around the surrounding area, admiring the incredible beauty of the surrounding places. A rented car can be left at a paid car park, which is under 24-hour security.

Prices

The cost of a ticket to the hotel directly depends on which travel agency it was purchased. Also of considerable importance in determining its price is what airport is planned to fly to Spain. So, for residents of the capital of Russia a weekly stay alone in a comfortable room of the Standard category will cost about 60 thousand rubles. As a rule, the operator includes not only the cost of a round-trip flight, but also all necessary fees, room rental and meals.

Guest reviews

Tourists who rested at the Stella Maris Hotel 3 * in their reviews, as a rule, talk about what living conditions they were provided with, what they managed to see, and share the general impressions received during the trip.

As a rule, all hotel guests are satisfied with the general atmosphere that prevails in the territory of this vacation spot. As many guests note, it is here that you can feel in a state of complete comfort, which is facilitated by good service and a homely atmosphere in rooms that are decorated in pastel colors. Residents of family-class apartments often indicate that it is very comfortable to be with small children: firstly, the size of the area allows, and secondly, here you can request a crib for a baby in your room for free.

Connoisseurs of a sports lifestyle living in Stella Maris Hotel 3 * (Blanes) note that even on vacation you can engage in maintaining your general health and improve your figure - for this you can rent a bike and play sports while exploring the surrounding area. Judging by the comments left on social networks, guests of Hotel Stella Maris 3 * spend a huge amount of time near the pool and on the sea coast - this fact is constantly reflected in the photos that vacationers attach to their comments.



Most hotel guests will certainly wish to return to its territory again in order to immerse themselves in the atmosphere of warmth and comfort, as well as a wonderful holiday. Very often they recommend a trip here to their friends and acquaintances.
