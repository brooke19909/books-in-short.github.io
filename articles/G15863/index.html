Spain. Zaragoza - an amazing and bewitching corner of the country

Spain is beautiful for its natural landscapes and spectacular sights. Zaragoza is the capital of Aragon and one of the largest cities in the country. The history of past centuries and the modern world is closely intertwined here, the architecture of Zaragoza surprises with its bizarre and unusual forms, and this is not a secret, because here is the birthplace of the great Goya.

Location and history of the city

Spain has many beautiful cities, Zaragoza is one of them. It is located in the very center of the autonomous region - Aragon. Zaragoza is comfortably spread on the banks of the Ebro River and its tributaries, surrounded by beautiful natural landscapes. The distance to the nearest airport is only ten kilometers; it is connected by air routes with many European countries. To travel by car around the city of Zaragoza (Spain), the map will be the first assistant. The history of this region has many centuries, it is believed that the city was founded by the Romans BC.



Climatic conditions

The city has a mild Mediterranean climate, there are only sunny days throughout the year. In the summer, it is very hot and dry, there is little rainfall at this time. Winters are not cold, with a plus temperature, but frequent fogs.

All about attractions

Zaragoza is rich in exotic natural landscapes, beautiful waterfalls and amazing tropical vegetation.

In the very center of the city lies the large Plaza del Pilar, the oldest cathedral, an amazing basilica and many different museums. Not far from the square is the building of the Exchange, made in the Renaissance style, as well as a huge fountain called Fuente de la Ispanidad. The whole of Spain is filled with various architectural structures of the Middle Ages. Zaragoza includes many amazing structures and museums in its sights. Thus, the church of Santa Maria Magdalena impresses with the unsurpassed beauty of the bell tower, and the museum of Zaragoza has collected the largest number of works by the famous Goya. The Moorish castle of Alhaferia is the most beautiful complex in all of Aragon. At the entrance to the city, the ancient gates of Puerta del Carmen rise. The real open-air museum is the country of Spain. Zaragoza is a wonderful world of architectural art, its multiple buildings are included in the World Heritage List.


Features of the kitchen and entertainment

The whole region of Aragon, including Zaragoza, is famous for its unsurpassed cooking of lamb. In local restaurants you can enjoy delicious local dishes. Bars of Zaragoza are famous for amazing Spanish tapas. This city has various attractions, well-developed infrastructure and plenty of entertainment that attracts a huge number of tourists to relax on the banks of the Ebro River. In addition to historical buildings, the city is rich in modern hotels and hotel complexes. A wonderful experience will be remembered by a walk around the city of Zaragoza (Spain), photos taken among its unique architectural structures will leave forever a memory of this amazing place.
