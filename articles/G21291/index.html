How do I pay child support debt?

Today, for most citizens, the maintenance of child support causes a lot of difficulties. Some try to get away from their duties, hide incomes, drag time. In such cases, the recovery must be carried out through the bailiffs. For more information on how and where to find out the debt for child support, read further in this article.

Causes

Debt on alimony arises due to non-payment or transfer of the amount in an amount less than what was established by a court decision. There can be many reasons:

  • deliberate evasion of payments;
  • the inability to repay the debt due to illness, unemployment or due to life circumstances;
  • erroneous deduction of understated amounts by accounting;
  • failure to provide information on details;
  • Recipient's refusal to transfer;
  • and many others.

Each of these cases is regulated by the Family Code of the Russian Federation.



Costing

The determination of child support debt is provided by the bailiff. To do this, he requests information about income for the period of debt. Based on these data and performs the calculation. The determination is made from the moment of collection to repayment of the debt. If the payer did not fulfill obligations for valid reasons (illness, delayed salary, lack of work, etc.), then three years are taken for accounting, otherwise, the entire debt period.

And in the period of time in which the debtor did not work or did not submit documents confirming the amount of income, the calculation is made on the average earnings. If the amount of debt on alimony is calculated incorrectly, then the bailiff's decision can be appealed in court.

A judicial act on sentencing for an administrative offense falls to the bailiff after the expiration of the appeal period, and in some cases - the period of voluntary execution. Therefore, you should know in advance what debt on child support exists, so that later you do not pay late fees.

Discharge from obligations

If the repayment of the debt occurs by agreement of the parties, then you can agree on the termination of payment. If alimony is paid for children, then you can be released from the obligation only through the court, and then in the presence of significant circumstances that were absent at the time of the decision to pay the alimony.



Debt collection

This process takes place through the court once. In the event of a change in the marital, material or property status of the parties, a counter statement is submitted to the bailiff. At the court, such a question will be resolved again only if, during the first consideration of the case, the defendant concealed the proceeds from which the amount of the child support debt is calculated, or if the payer has died. In the second case, the rest of the debt will be recovered from its heirs.

The debt on alimony, which can be found out by the methods described below, is calculated by the bailiffs, and is most often repaid by deductions from the debtor's income. Amounts are transferred by the accounting department of the enterprise. But even then, the enforcement officer exercises control over the execution of the decision.

Calculation Methods

1. By the value of the subsistence level in the region. The first method is used if deductions are assigned by the court or are carried out by agreement of the parties. In this case, the amount of deductions does not depend on income and is assigned multiple of the cost of living indicator. The bailiff carries out the calculation on a quarterly basis. If at the same time payments are not made, then the basis for calculations is the last value. If the payer fulfills his obligations, then the amount of alimony is simply indexed.

Example

Payment is assigned in the amount of 150% of the minimum wage established since January of the current year. The calculation is carried out on September 30. According to the Federal State Statistics Service, the minimum wage for the III quarter for the region is 6432 rubles. The calculation takes place according to the formula: (1.5 x 6432) x 9 = 86,832 rubles.

2. In terms of average salary in the state. The second method is used if the payer does not have documentary evidence of income or official salary. The same method is applied if the debtor-SP refuses to provide primary financial documentation. Alimony is set as a share of earnings. For the period without accruals, the average salary at the current moment is used. The result is multiplied by the number of months.

Example

The average salary in Russia for the first quarter of 2013 is 27,339.40 rubles. The court has appointed alimony in the amount of 25% of revenues since June 2012, but payments have not been made. The bailiff carries out a calculation in February 2013:

(27339.40 x 0.25) x 8 = 54678.80 rubles.



3. Based on the income of the payer. The third technique is used if the debtor has an official source of funds, but no payments were made. In this case, the bailiff subtracts 13% of personal income tax from the data on the salary level and multiplies the amount by the number of months of delay.

Indexing

Since the end of 2011, debt on alimony, which can be found out by the methods described above, is recovered in a solid amount. The size of monthly payments varies depending on the cost of living. For calculations, the growth of the minimum wage established by the subject of the Russian Federation or the Government for a certain calendar period for the corresponding population group is used. In case of debt evasion, the Family Code provides for the liability of the non-payer. It is recovered in the form of a penalty for delay and compensation for losses arising from the debt.

It is easiest to get such information through the FSS website and applications of the same name.
