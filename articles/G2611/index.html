Mallorca beaches: overview, photos, location on the map and reviews of tourists

Spain has long won the hearts of our compatriots with its comfortable resorts and luxurious beaches. The most popular and most visited island of the Bolear archipelago is Mallorca. The beaches of this island have established it as the most luxurious world resort, thanks to its stunning nature, unique monuments of history, architecture and art.





Mallorca beaches

There is no doubt that beach holidays are the main thing on this island. For this, more than 200 beaches are organized and perfectly equipped on it. The coastline of the island stretches for 554 kilometers. It perfectly combines vast beaches, rocky cliffs covered with pine forests, and picturesque bays.

Mainly on the island of Mallorca, sandy beaches. However, this does not mean that lovers of pebbles, gravel or rocks will not find a suitable place to relax. 63 beaches in Mallorca have been awarded the distinctive Blue Flag awarded by the EU for cleanliness, safety and excellent service.

It is important that on the island of Mallorca the beaches are all municipal, free. Payment is taken only for additional services - umbrellas, sunbeds, etc.

Rocky beach lovers

These are probably the most beautiful beaches of Mallorca. There are thirty-three of them on the island. Today we will tell you about the most visited of them.





Cala Fonoll Beach

This is a small rocky area near the city of Andratx (2.2 km). On one side of it is a modern hotel, and on the other - residential buildings. The slope is covered with round boulders and gravel.

To get to this beach is quite simple - you need to go through the sailing club, and then go onto the Cala Fonoll road, which will lead you to this quiet and uninhabited beach. It is ideal for a relaxing holiday when you want to isolate yourself from the city noise and retire.

It is located forty kilometers from the city of Soller. The beach two hundred meters deep into Mount Serra de Tramuntana. The entrance to the sea is semicircular. The beach is surrounded by a luxurious pine forest. At the water, the beach is covered with sand, and then goes into gravel and rounded stones. You can arrive here on a yacht, but anchoring it here is not recommended due to strong winds. There are more vacationers here than on Cala Fonoll beach.





The stony beaches of Mallorca receive very conflicting reviews . Some are happy to spend their holidays in a quiet and peaceful place, while others find such a pastime too boring.

Those who prefer a more active holiday, in the company of old and new friends, it is better to choose sandy beaches - they are always crowded, have a well-developed infrastructure. In addition, in such places you can always make a new acquaintance.

Sandy beaches

The beaches of Mallorca (they are clearly visible on the map of the island) are mostly sandy. And I must say that 80% of all those who come on vacation choose them.

In the south of the island is the magnificent small beach of Cala Mondrago (Cala Mondrago), located between two bays with perfectly clear water. There is a small bar (right on the beach).

One of the most beloved beaches by tourists is rightly considered Calo des Moro - there is a breathtaking landscape, majestic pine trees surrounding the beach, calm, crystal clear water and a sandy bottom. The size of the beach is small, and there are quite a lot of people who want to spend time here. Therefore, you need to arrive early to occupy the attractive place on the white sand.





The most idyllic place of Mallorca is Formentor beach (Formentor). Mountains, pine forest, turquoise clear sea attract thousands of tourists here annually. But not only the extraordinary nature made him so popular. The famous hotel, in which many famous world figures stayed at different years, also played a significant role.

One of the most beautiful beaches of Mallorca, according to vacationers, is Calo Mondrago. It is located in the eponymous nature reserve. An unusually beautiful beach with fine white sand, surrounded by a pine forest. Water has a rich turquoise color - it is simply mesmerizing. The beach is well equipped, there is a picnic area in the forest. At the peak of the beach season, there are a lot of vacationers who want to enjoy nature in this paradise.

Playa de Muro (Playa Muro)

This six-kilometer sandy beach is a great place to relax with children. Here is a very gentle, convenient entrance to the sea. The main part of the beach has been preserved in its original form, but in the shade of pine trees between the dunes there is a picnic area.

Family Beaches

It is no secret that many families with children seek to get to Mallorca. This is due to the fact that on this island many beaches are provided specifically for such a holiday. As a rule, they have many facilities and services for all family members.





Santa Ponsa ("Santa Ponsa")

It is a sandy beach with a long and wide coastline, located in the southwest of Mallorca. Behind him is a beautiful pine forest.

Camp de Mar is perhaps the best beach for families with children. In addition to the excellent conditions created for them on the beach, young tourists appreciated the wooden bridge that connects the beach with a restaurant on a miniature island. With your whole family, you can take a walk to the sixteenth-century watchtower and enjoy the wonderful views that open from it.

Es Trenk (Es Trenk)

This comfortable beach attracts many tourists, so it is often crowded, despite the fact that it stretches for almost three kilometers.

Every year, more and more vacationers from all over the world prefer to visit the beaches of Mallorca. Reviews of regular guests of the island suggest that all vacationers get here exactly the vacation they dreamed about. There is something for everyone to enjoy.

Today we are talking about the island of Mallorca, the photos of the beaches of which you see in our article. They are undoubtedly very important for a good rest. However, this is not the only thing to admire on this fabulous island. Thousands of tourists are interested in the unique natural monuments of Mallorca.

Art Caves

They were discovered back in the sixteenth century in the northeast of the island. First of all, they amaze with their gigantic size. In addition, the main attraction of this ancient natural monument is a 22-meter stalagmite. Guided tours in the caves are accompanied by a light and music show, allowing visitors to appreciate their acoustics and beauty.





In the east of Mallorca there are no less interesting Drac caves, in which there are six underground lakes. During the tour you can see a unique show - right on the waters of one of the lakes. Musicians perform famous classical music, slowly sailing in boats in front of amazed spectators.

In the north of the island is Cape Formentor. It has a convenient observation deck. It offers a unique panorama of the island and the vast expanses of the sea.
