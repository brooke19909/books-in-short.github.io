Green slime



Now you will learn how to make a slime from water and paint. Among the various recipes offered in craft books, the simplest is the one that requires the following components: a bottle of PVA glue or a regular stationery, school, borax or borax powder, water, food coloring, or brilliant green. How to make a slime from water and paint, as well as other components? Very simple. The main thing is to choose the right proportions of the components. Take half a glass of water (hot). Add a tablespoon of borax to it or pour in a borax solution, mix to dissolve. Next, pour a bottle of glue into a glass of water, also stir until smooth. Add some dye there. Mix green and yellow - you will get a nice soft green color, like young grass. If you take more than the first, the ghost of a more saturated shade will come out, respectively. Add borax solution gradually, stirring. You will get a gelatinous colored mass that can be rolled into a ball, wrinkled and even slap it on the door. She will slide down, gather in a lump - well, just like a Disney phantom. Here's how to make a slime from water and paint. However, this method is not the only one, and you can also think of many friendly ghost options. Just try not to get it on woolen products, fur, etc., because the fibers will stick to the ghost.





Luminous slime

A cute toy can be varied a little. For example, add luminous elements and it will phosphorize. To learn how to make a slime from water and paint, zinc sulfide and glue, read on. The ratio of parts can be this: 3 parts of the same PVA or another glue and 1 part of borax + a little dye and fluorescent paint, as well as water. The luminous component can be replaced with a quarter of a teaspoon of such a powder as zinc sulfide by stirring it in 2 tablespoons of polyvinyl alcohol. So how to make a slime? From water (warm) and PVA, make a jelly-like mass (a glass of water for 1/3 cup of glue), add dyes and sparkles. Stir, wait a couple of minutes for the components to react with each other. Then give the toy a shape, remove it from the bowl, rinse under the tap and can play with your child. By the way, turn off the light - a funny ghost will begin to glow in the dark. And you can make it not only green, but also any color that you like.
