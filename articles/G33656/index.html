Honeymoon room in Moscow: overview, features and reviews of tourists

A wedding is an event that is celebrated on a grand scale. However, not only the wedding day should be special for the newlyweds, but also the first wedding night. To preserve the solemnity of the moment, an ideal option would be a beautiful hotel room, rented specifically for this night. And today, many hotels in Moscow provide one designed specifically for young people.

What to look for when choosing a honeymoon suite

First of all, we recommend booking a room in advance. Most hotels, as a rule, have only one for newlyweds and one or two suites suitable for these purposes. And in the hottest wedding season, your ideal honeymoon room in Moscow can be booked long.





A good room will be, firstly, very beautiful - with a wide bed, a chic bath, large windows and a beautiful view. It will also be specially decorated. These are usually flowers or rose petals. When booking, ask what is included in the price. Often there is included a compliment from the hotel, for example, fruits and champagne in the room.

A good bonus is the possibility of round-the-clock ordering of food in the room, suddenly the newlyweds are hungry, because at a wedding party they often do not manage to have a bite. And, of course, an important detail is the possibility of free late eviction, because no one wants to leave the room at 12 noon after a difficult day and a long sleepless night.

Here you can feel like real noblemen in pre-revolutionary Russia - this is facilitated by the classic Russian style in the design of rooms, decoration with cherry wood and marble.

Spend no money and rent the Ritz Carlton Suite, over two hundred square meters with a chic marble bathtub. A piano standing in the living room will allow you to invite a musician and dance alone to beautiful melodies. The cost of such a tale for one night is about one hundred thousand rubles.





The National Hotel is more than a hotel; it is a living story. To spend a wedding night here means to capture this moment in memory forever. The rooms here are rich in antique furniture and paintings, unique ceilings and other rarities.
