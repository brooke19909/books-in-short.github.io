Where is the Playboy Mansion located?

In the world there are many paradises where everyone would like to be. One of these great places is the Playboy Mansion, which is located in the heart of California. Here you can have fun and enjoy the company of sexy girls.

A little background





Hugh Marston Hefner (future owner of the mansion and founder of Playboy magazine) lived an exciting life. He served in the army during World War II, studied at the Department of Psychology at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, and worked for Shaft and Esquire magazines.

Hugh came up with the idea of ‚Äã‚Äãcreating his own magazine as a student. But the idea came to life only after the dismissal from the advertising department of Esquire magazine. According to Hefner himself, he had to do this, because the authorities refused to raise salaries at all.

Self-collecting money, Hugh Hefner published the first issue of the magazine with Marilyn Monroe on the cover. At that time (the 50s), the Playboy circulation was 70 thousand copies, three quarters of which were sold out in the first week!

Gradually, the affairs of the owner of Playboy magazine began to improve, and he allowed himself to purchase one of the oldest houses in Los Angeles. Then the purchase cost Hefner's company $ 1.1 million. A little later, this house became known to many as the famous Playboy Mansion.

Mansion History





The true story of the Playboy Mansion begins when it was built, not acquired by Hefner.

Designed by Arthur R. Kelly back in 1927. The mansion was made in the then fashionable Gothic-Tudor style.

The first owner of the house was Arthur Lates Jr., the son of the founder of The Broadway Department Store. Later, Louis D. Statham (a famous engineer, inventor and chess lover) became the owner of the house. It was from him that Playboy bought the mansion for Hugh Hefner in 1971.

What is a mansion?





The mansion is located in the south of the bright and sunny state of California, in Los Angeles, namely in the Holmby Hills area. Nearby are the Country Club, the University of California, Los Angeles and the Bel-Air Country Club.

The mansion itself covers an area of ‚Äã‚Äãabout 2 hectares. What's inside?





The way the Playboy mansion looks from the outside is already known. This is a large, majestic estate, made in the Gothic-Tudor style, surrounded by parking, a grove, areas for recreation and sports. But inside, the main house looks even better.

The Playboy Mansion is a large two-story building consisting of more than twenty rooms, many of which are bedrooms. On the ground floor there is a kitchen, dining room and main hall, made in a classic style. On the walls you can see portraits that were made at different periods of Hugh Hefner's life, as well as his own metal statue, standing alone in the farthest corner.

Leaving the hall, you can get into the remaining rooms of the mansion. For example, after a long, exhausting day, you can relax in a room equipped with a cinema. You can also visit the wine cellar, gym, a small library, a games room or go up to the second floor to choose one of the bedrooms for going to bed.

Of course, besides all of the above, in this large house there are a lot of other, no less interesting premises. For example, there is a corner for carnal pleasures, called the Grotto cave. It was a favorite place of many celebrities who came to Hefner to relax and have a good time in the company of pretty girls.
