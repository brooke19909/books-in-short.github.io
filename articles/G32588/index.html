Selection tips

How to choose a smartphone up to 15,000 rubles? This question is asked by a large number of buyers. Of course, the selection criteria are an individual matter, however, some of them can be distinguished.

There are people who pay attention only to the design, trying to pick up an apparatus that looks very similar, for example, to the "apple" flagships. And this is the main mistake. It is recommended that particular attention be paid to specifications. That "filling" will allow you to enjoy interacting with the gadget. Let's look at the criteria by which to choose a smartphone:

  • Screen. Diagonal directly affects the size of the phone. Resolution is also very important. Currently, preference should be given to either Full HD or HD. The first, of course, is better.
  • CPU. Performance characteristics will depend on the chipset model. The best in this category are Helio X20 / X25 / X27 / P20 (MediaTek), Snapdragon 820/821/650/652/653/625 (Qualcomm), Kirin 950/960/655 and Surge S1.
  • Memory. Here, special attention must be paid to the amount of RAM. Most gadgets in this segment offer the user 2 GB, but there are also models in which 3 or even 4 GB are installed. Let's just say: the more the better.
  • Optics. A smartphone with a good camera up to 15,000 rubles is popular with buyers. When buying, you should pay attention not only to resolution, but also to other options, such as stabilization, autofocus, flash, aperture, number of lenses and more.
  • OS As a rule, low-cost devices are running Android. An important role is played by the presence of a proprietary shell and software optimization.
  • Autonomy. In this segment, one trend can be seen: manufacturers install a powerful "stuffing", but save on battery capacity. It is recommended to purchase devices as balanced as possible.
