Alara Hotels Star 5 * (Antalya, Turkey): traveler reviews, leisure

If you want to spend an excellent vacation in Turkey, while staying in a comfortable but inexpensive hotel, then Alara Star Hotel 5 * (Alanya) can be a very good option.



Location

This five-star hotel is located three kilometers from the resort village of Avsallar and 23 kilometers from the city of Alanya. The tourist capital of Turkey, Antalya, is 100 kilometers away. It takes about two hours to get from the airport to the hotel.

Hotel Alara Star Hotel 5 * was built in 2005. Since then, renovation has been carried out here several times. All apartments have the necessary furniture, a TV with cable channels (including in Russian), a refrigerator, a safe, air conditioning, a furnished balcony and a bathroom with a hairdryer. Alara Star Hotel 5 *: traveler reviews from the Russian Federation

As you know, many modern tourists with great responsibility approach the issue of vacation planning and, in particular, to choosing a hotel in a particular country in the world. And it's not just about money. After all, no one wants to experience disappointment upon arriving on a long-awaited vacation. Therefore, travelers not only study the official description of hotels, but also try to get acquainted with as many comments as possible of real people who have already stayed in these places recently. This allows you to make a more complete and close to the real state of affairs picture of the hotel. Therefore, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with the generalized reviews of our compatriots regarding their stay in the hotel under consideration.





Travel agencies offering tours to Russian residents in Turkey, Alara Star Hotel 5 * characterize as a hotel with an excellent combination of price and quality. Immediately, we note that the majority of vacationers who have been here completely agree with this assessment and were quite satisfied with their choice of a place to stay, and are also ready to recommend this hotel to their friends and relatives. But we learn about everything in more detail.

Hotel itself

In general, almost all tourists were satisfied with the rooms offered to them. So, according to them, since the hotel is not new, you will not find modern furniture and appliances here. However, everything is in good and working condition. If something failed, then, according to our compatriots, after contacting the reception, the breakdown was eliminated as soon as possible. The rooms themselves are quite spacious, with furnished balconies and freshly renovated. True, some guests complained that the apartment is a bit dark. However, this did not become a problem, since most tourists came to their rooms only to change clothes or in the evening before going to bed.

According to travelers, all rooms at Alara Star Hotel 5 * (Turkey) have safes that can be used on a free basis, and minibars, where two half-liter bottles of water are reported every day. As for cleaning, then, as our compatriots say, it was carried out every day. The maids renewed towels and bedding a couple of times a week. By the way, as for beach towels, they can be exchanged every three days in a hammam.

Own territory

According to our compatriots, Alara Hotels Star 5 * occupies a small territory. So, there is only a swimming pool with a sun terrace and a bar, as well as an amphitheater. However, no crowds, as noted by the guests, are not observed. The territory itself seemed to tourists very well-groomed, beautiful and green. Next door are two hotels of the same chain as Alara Star. So guests can freely enter the territory of all hotels and use the entire infrastructure, with the exception of restaurants and housing.



Population

As our compatriots note in their reviews, in most cases Alara Hotels Star 5 * adhere to internal rules stating that new arrivals can only enter their rooms after two in the afternoon. However, this mainly concerns the height of the holiday season. If you are on vacation in September or October, then even with an early check in you will most likely be settled almost immediately. But, according to some travelers, they managed to resolve this issue even in July and August, paying the administrator for the early check-in service. In most cases, a free room with a good view was for them almost immediately, even if before that they assured the reception that all apartments were occupied.



Nutrition

In this matter, the vast majority of guests showed solidarity. So, according to our compatriots, the food at Alara Hotels Star 5 * is organized at a very decent level. The main restaurant for lunch and dinner has always had a large selection of different dishes. They were mainly represented by birds and fish. A couple of times a week, as the hotel guests assure, the cooks cooked something from beef and turkey. In addition, visitors to the restaurant could always eat soup, treat themselves to a variety of salads, snacks, pastries and sweets. For breakfast, the choice of dishes also seemed quite worthy for tourists. In general, as guests note, you can have a meal at this hotel 24 hours a day. For example, at night there is an opportunity to satisfy hunger with soup, and during the day (from 10 to 16 hours) you can eat french fries, hamburger, pizza, pasta, etc. in the snack bar.

Entertainment

Since the hotel area is not very large, a special variety of entertainment, according to tourists, is not here. So, during the day you can relax by the pool.
