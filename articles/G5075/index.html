Silent Vacuum Cleaner. Silent Bosch Vacuum Cleaner



Vacuum cleaner today is an indispensable assistant when cleaning any apartment or office. It is quite difficult to imagine a housewife who would prefer a broom and a scoop to this type of household appliances. And if the first models were of the same type and could fulfill only one function, then at present the manufacturers of this equipment offer a very wide assortment range. Using modern devices, you can not only quickly carry out high-quality wet or dry cleaning, but also remove dirt from the sofa and dust from any of the most inaccessible places. At the same time, cleaning will not be accompanied by constant noise and buzz, since the latest models are equipped with a noise reduction system.

Silent vacuum cleaners - what is this ?!



Silent vacuum cleaner in its essence is no different from traditional devices. It is also designed for cleaning all kinds of surfaces, however, unlike the latter, it has a high degree of filtration and low noise. This is the best option for use in places where silence is especially required, for example, in restaurants or hotels. In addition, a silent vacuum cleaner is absolutely indispensable in hospitals. The level of extraneous hum in such devices is, as a rule, in the range of 56-65 dB. This means that when a vacuum cleaner is operating, two people, without straining their hearing and voice, can communicate with each other quite calmly. It should be especially noted that the power of the device in this case does not suffer at all. According to manufacturers, for example, Bosch, all this allows you to clean even at night, without fear of disturbing neighbors or waking up children.

Bosch brand vacuum cleaners



Vacuum cleaners of this brand are very widely represented in the modern market of household appliances and are of high quality, reliability and durability. All devices undergo a mandatory quality control and have the appropriate international certificates. The silent Bosch vacuum cleaner is a guarantee of purity and silence in the house or office. All devices of this type efficiently combine low noise and high suction power with minimal maintenance requirements. According to the German manufacturer, the special system that every silent vacuum cleaner of this brand is equipped with makes them one of the quietest vacuum cleaners ever made. In the process of their creation, in order to reduce vibration and noise, all details were thoroughly analyzed, starting from the exhaust grille and ending with the nozzle. As a result, practically tight joints and aerodynamic air flow allowed to significantly reduce the level of hum emitted by the vacuum cleaner.

Bosch special technology

The special system that underlies all new Bosch devices is called the SilenceSound System. This technology includes four main components, thanks to which the Bosch silent vacuum cleaner works really silently. Firstly, the device‚Äôs engine is placed in a sound-absorbing double capsule, the main task of which is to dampen vibrations. Secondly, every silent Bosch vacuum cleaner has a two-level top cover design that allows you to reduce the volume of air flow. Thirdly, the company's specialists ensured the tightness of the hose connection directly to the body and improved the brush to reduce vibration. And, finally, fourthly, all joints of parts are laid with special porous gaskets with high soundproofing properties.





The benefits of silent vacuum cleaners

The main advantages that all silent vacuum cleaners have (only the most positive reviews about them) are, first of all, the excellent cleaning quality due to the intense air flow that can pick up the smallest dust particles. In addition, it is worth mentioning the increased power and ergonomic design that all modern models often have. Style decisions in such devices are made in accordance with all laws of aerodynamics. In addition, a number of models use compressor-type motors, which, while maintaining a high suction speed, are very quiet and economical, especially compared to conventional engines.

Key selection criteria



Choosing the best silent vacuum cleaner, the first thing you need to pay attention to is power. If you plan to use the device in a small area with a rather low pollution indicator, then a model with a suction power of up to 300 watts is best suited. If the space is very large and dirt is constantly accumulating in it, then models with a power of 300 watts or more should be preferred. The second important selection criterion is a dust collector. It can be disposable (paper) or reusable (tissue). Moreover, in all cases, it looks like an ordinary bag. Paper is more effective because it allows dirt and dust to pass through several times less. After the next cleaning, the old dust collector is thrown away and just a new one is installed. The fabric bag is much more profitable due to the possibility of reusable use, but it allows a lot more dirt to pass through. In addition, it should be said that in some models of vacuum cleaners there is the possibility of using two types of dust collector at once. This is very convenient, because if necessary, one of them can always be replaced by another.
