Chinese market in Perm: where is it located and how to get there

The Chinese market in Perm is one of the most popular and largest in the district. People come here specially even from other cities, because the choice is wide and the prices are pleasantly pleasing to the eye.

Market appeal

Five pavilions and shopping arcades - all this guarantees the most comfortable selection of necessary goods. The prices here are really very attractive, akin to wholesale, and often wholesale. At the same time, no one requires bulk purchases, but will provide if necessary.



The market is located at: Perm, st. Kronstadt, 39, district Dzerzhinsky. If you drive your own car, then check-in is from the side of Plekhanov street. In addition, there is a large free parking. To make it more convenient, absolutely all drivers recommend using the navigator - so it will be much easier to find. Despite the fact that the market has been located at this address for less than 5 years, it is already very popular in the city and the region. Additional guidelines to find the Chinese market faster are the Vivat hypermarket and the Danilikha microdistrict.

How to get to the Chinese market in Perm

Actively runs on the Chinese market public transport. There are many options here - from almost any area of ​​the city you can quickly get there.

The nearest stops to the Chinese market in Perm are Manege Spartak and Blucher Street. Several shuttle buses run to the Bluchera Street stop: No. 3, 19, 30, 42, 56, 106. Trolleybus routes to the Manezh Spartak or Bluchera Street stops are numbers 10 and 12.

From time to time, free minibuses go to the Chinese market in Perm. This type of transport is usually launched during fairs. Therefore, having predicted for such an event, you can save a lot. You only need to know the schedule of minibuses in advance.



For nonresident

The market is located in a convenient part of the city, where you can quickly get both from stations and from the airport. Traffic jams in this section of the path are extremely rare, although they sometimes occur. Usually during fairs the regulation of the road is carried out by patrolmen. Therefore, traffic jams at this time do not become a critical problem.

For those traveling from the suburbs, you can take the electric train routes to Perm 2 station from the Khert, Shalya, Shamara, Kukushtan and Kordon stops. For nonresident hotels, hotels and motels are very conveniently located near the market. Prices are quite affordable, so you can stay for a few days or usefully spend the weekend.

The Chinese market usually operates from 9.00 to 18.00, on weekends - until 19.00.
