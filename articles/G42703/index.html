Continental Plaza Beach Resort 5 * (Egypt): photos and reviews of tourists

Today, Naama Bay is one of the most popular resorts in Egypt. Every year thousands of Russian tourists spend their holidays here. Naturally, there is everything you need for a comfortable stay, including numerous hotels and luxury resort complexes. And the Continental Plaza Beach hotel managed to earn a pretty good reputation.

It is only natural that many travelers are interested in more information about this place. Where is the hotel located? What accommodation conditions does it offer? Can I go here with a child? Do tourists speak well of him? The answers to these questions will be of interest to many readers who are going to relax on the sea coast.

Location of the hotel complex





The large resort Continental Plaza Beach Hotel is located in Sharm El Sheikh, one of the most popular tourist cities in Egypt. It is worth noting that the location is quite profitable. The sea coast is within easy reach - you can reach the beach in just a few minutes. The center of the Old Town with all its historical, architectural and other attractions is 8 kilometers away. The scenic Naama Bay is just 7 kilometers away. The nearest international airport is located 10 kilometers away - getting to the hotel is very easy and fast, especially since a shuttle service is organized for guests.

Brief description of the territory and infrastructure of the hotel

Immediately worth saying that the Continental Plaza Beach Resort is a large resort complex that can accommodate more than a thousand vacationers. The hotel was built in 2002, and the last thorough restoration was carried out in 2008, after which this place has become even more convenient for foreigners.





The hotel territory, whose area, by the way, is 140 thousand square meters, is divided into two zones under the name "Coral" and "Fireous" (the area around the pools). The hotel itself consists of a main three-story building and 26 small two-story buildings designed for guests.

Naturally, there are pools, restaurants, sports fields, places for recreation and entertainment. The whole territory is divided by comfortable, well-groomed walking paths and decorated with palm trees and exotic plants.

Living conditions: room description

On the territory of the Continental Plaza Beach Resort 5 * hotel there are 600 rooms of various categories:

  • 283 Standard Rooms;
  • 163 Standard Sea View Rooms;
  • 10 Suites;
  • 40 Family Rooms;
  • 4 Junior Suites.

There are separate rooms for smokers and non-smokers. In addition, the hotel has so-called combined rooms, which are perfect for large families or companies.





The rooms themselves are simple, elegant and comfortable. The floors are covered with ceramic tiles, and large windows let in enough light into the room. By the way, the room has access to a terrace or balcony, depending on the floor. As for the view from the window, it all depends on the location of your room. There are rooms with sea views, and there are those with windows facing the courtyard or garden.

Guests are provided with the necessary set of furniture and household appliances. There is a separate air conditioning, as well as a TV with satellite channels (there are some Russian and English), a telephone (calls outside the city are paid separately), and a safe. Housekeepers can deliver an iron and ironing board to the room upon request. There is a minibar - upon arrival, guests receive a bottle of drinking and sparkling water, as well as juice. Subsequent filling is paid separately.

The bathroom is equipped with working plumbing. There is a bathtub, a fairly spacious shower, as well as a sink, a large wall mirror, a toilet, and a set of clean towels. Of course, there is a set of personal care products and cosmetics, which is very convenient. Bathrobes are not included in the price, although they can always be ordered, prices are not too high.

Cleaning and linen change is carried out every day. The rooms are maintained at a maximum level of cleanliness.

The scheme of food at the hotel

The resort complex Continental Plaza Beach Hotel 5 * provides its customers with an all inclusive system. This means that the price includes breakfast, lunch, dinner, as well as desserts and light snacks throughout the day, soft drinks and spirits from a local manufacturer. Freshly squeezed juices and imported drinks need to be bought separately. Once a week, guests have free access to the local Italian restaurant.





The main meals are organized on the territory of the main restaurant, where you will be served by polite and attentive waiters. According to reviews of tourists, the choice of dishes here is quite large - fresh fruits, vegetables, meat dishes, side dishes, cold dishes, fish, seafood, as well as homemade cakes, ice cream and sweet desserts.

In addition, on the territory of the Continental Plaza Beach 5 * hotel there are also three √† la carte restaurants, which will offer you a huge selection of dishes of classic Italian and Indian cuisine, as well as a barbecue. The services of these establishments are paid separately, and places should be booked in advance. There is also a cozy cafe with sea views, four bars and a hookah bar, where you can also have a good time.

Beach and water activities

The resort Continental Plaza Beach Resort 5 * is located on the first beach line - you can reach the sea in a matter of minutes. The beach here is private, so outsiders will not bother you. Soft sand and clean, warm water - this is what awaits the traveler in the territory of one of the most famous bays in Egypt.





A convenient pontoon is built to enter the sea. In addition, there is a small stretch of coast, completely cleared of corals - here you can also swim. Sunbeds with mattresses and umbrellas are arranged in even rows on the beach. There is also a bar serving cooling drinks and light snacks. A beach towel can be borrowed free of charge .

If you enjoy more active leisure activities, you will not be bored at Continental Plaza Beach. The most attractive attraction here is a coral reef, which is located almost off the coast. This place is extremely popular among diving enthusiasts. Nearby there is a scuba diving center where you can rent the necessary equipment. Beginners can enroll in courses and make the first dive in the company of professionals.

There is also a volleyball court on the beach. For an additional fee you can ride a boat, water skiing, banana, as well as a boat and catamarans. This coast is also favored by surfers. And if you are one of them or just want to try something new, then you can rent a board, suit and other accessories here on the beach. And you can do parasailing.
