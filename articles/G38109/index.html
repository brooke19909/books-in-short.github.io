Bahamas: hotels, tours, leisure. The most expensive hotel in the Bahamas

The Bahamas is an island nation north of Cuba. The archipelago consists of 700 islands, of which only 30 are inhabited. Each year, the resort is visited by several million tourists, tours to the Bahamas are organized. This picturesque country attracts with its exotic nature and modern infrastructure. Tourists enjoy underwater diving, go out in the open water under the sail, surf.

The Bahamas is a symbol of luxury and wealth. Those who were lucky enough to relax in this paradise place know firsthand about the beautiful snow-white beaches, warm water of the Atlantic Ocean and all kinds of entertainments.

Access to the islands is only possible by plane to Nassau or Freeport, there is no railway or public transport. You can travel by land on cars, motorbikes and bicycles. You can get to the neighboring island by boat, ferry or helicopter.

Holidays in the Bahamas

New Providence Island is located in the central part of the archipelago, on it is the capital of the Bahamas - the city of Nassau. The main part of the Bahamians lives on the island. Numerous tourist sites have been built at New Providence, and the harbor has expanded to call cruise ships at the port.

Atlantis, Paradise Island - City Hotel

Atlantis, Paradise Island - tourists who want to find the turquoise ocean water, white sand, bright sun and impeccable service come to this chic hotel complex. Hotel guests can visit the huge water park on site, beaches, pools, restaurants and bars.

There are 20 restaurants and 19 bars and lounges in the Atlantic that offer dishes ranging from light fast food to fine dining. Bahamian food, dinner on the beach or a family meal - any whim of the guest will be fulfilled.

The hotel is simply impossible to get bored. Constantly organized tennis matches, golf championships. Experienced instructors teach yoga and Pilates classes at the spa.

Atlantic is ideal for families with children. The huge underwater world of sea creatures, water slides and pools, white and clean sand on the beach - this is a wonderful pastime during the holidays.

In the hotel you can organize an unforgettable wedding, party, celebrate a birthday.

The hotel is located 100 meters from the beach, all rooms are decorated in bright colors and equipped with a flat-screen TV, internet, and a minibar.

Atlantis is one of the best hotels in the Bahamas.
