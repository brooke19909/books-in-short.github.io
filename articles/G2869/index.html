About the developer

For ten years now, Aquamarine has been operating in the Russian Federation. "Fisherman's Fort" is one of the latest projects of this developer. The main office of the enterprise is located in the city of St. Petersburg. Construction until recently was not the main activity of the company, but now it is actively conquering this niche.

The developer company does not have its own website, therefore, all available data about the enterprise in question can be found exclusively in the project declaration. However, for projects implemented by Aquamarine, separate sites are created. This happened in the case of the project "Fort fishing" (Nevsky district). According to reviews about the Fort Fisherman project, the date of delivery was shifted five times. Naturally, this has repeatedly caused waves of concern among equity holders. How to get there

Will there be problems with traffic? Residential complex "Fort Rybatsky" is located near the public transport stop "Sovetsky Prospekt 23". It can be reached by buses from the Rybatskoye metro station.

If you prefer to use your own car, you must follow the instructions described. When you are in the square near the Rybatskoe metro station, turn onto the Fourth Rybatskiy passage. Drive along it to the intersection with Bugrova Street, from where you can go to Volodarsky Street. You need to follow it to the intersection with the street of the Third Five-Year Plan and turn left on it. Next, you need to drive to the road leading to Metallostroy, and follow along it to the Central passage. On the passage you will need to move to Central Street, which will lead to the Petrozavodsk highway. Following it, you will very soon see a turn to Sovetsky Prospekt. He will lead to Slavyanskaya Street, along which you will have to drive only one hundred and ten meters to the final destination.
