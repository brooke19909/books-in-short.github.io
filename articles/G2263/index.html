Aviline secure parking system (parking sensors)

Eviline is a well-known company engaged in the production of parking sensors throughout the world. Previously, the company cooperated only with car dealers of eighteen automobile brands from different countries. Currently Aviline Parktronic brands can be installed independently. The company began to produce its products specifically for centers for additional equipment of automobiles. The manufacturer offered a whole line of AAAline equipment. In addition to the name, it does not differ from that installed in a car dealership.

Features of functioning

The Aviline (Parktronic) system is powered by ultrasonic sensors. They are installed on the bumpers of the car front and rear. Thanks to them, the distance to the nearest object is measured. The device gives a signal. It can be a sound or a picture on the display.

The sound is intermittent. It starts to work if the distance between the objects is 1-2 meters. When approaching an obstacle, its frequency increases. If the distance decreases to 10-30 centimeters, then the signal becomes continuous.





Features parking sensors provide comfortable work with him. Separate models allow you to completely turn off the system. This can be useful, for example, when driving on the road.

Most often, sensors mounted on the rear bumper are connected to the rear light circuit . In this case, Aviline (parking sensors) is activated automatically when reverse gear is engaged. Sensors on the front bumper begin their work when the speed of the car is small (up to 20 kilometers per hour).

Advantages of the Eviline system

Car owners value Aviline products. Parktronic of this trade brand differs from its analogues in some features:

  • Reliability.
  • The presence of the Russian language in the module.
  • Intuitive display images.
  • Information from all sensors is indicated on the screen.




In addition, the devices of the brand "Evilayn" are universal and are suitable for almost all cars. The protruding parts on the body absolutely do not interfere with their work.

Self-parking sensors

The installation of a secure parking system begins with marking. For this purpose, construction tape is glued at exemplary installation locations. It makes all the necessary markup. It is recommended to install parking sensors half a meter lower than the upper edge of the sidelight. At least 50-70 centimeters should remain to the ground. From sensors to the edge of the bumper leave about 40-45 cm. Central sensors are installed with an interval of about 50 centimeters.

Further, holes are made in the marked places. To do this, use the mill included in the delivery. Scotch tape is no longer needed and can be removed. Sensors are inserted into the resulting holes. Wires extend inward. Behind the wires extend through the existing technological holes into the trunk. The wires from the sensors located in front stretch under the plastic into the passenger compartment. It will only be necessary to remove some plastic parts on the panel. This can be done using an ordinary screwdriver.

System connection

After installing the sensors, you must connect Aviline (parking sensors). The installation instructions included in the package will help you do this if you have any questions.





The power supply is usually installed in the trunk. It connects to the taillight connector. This is done sequentially, taking into account the color of the wires:

  • Black - on the case.
  • Yellow is a plus.
  • Green joins the brake light.
  • Red is connected to the ignition.

Next, the display is installed. You can choose a place for him at your discretion. Having connected the control unit to the display , it is necessary to check the operability of the entire system.

To do this, insert the key into the ignition (you do not need to start the car), turn on the reverse gear. A solid object must be brought to the sensors on the rear bumper. By changing the position of the gearbox, we check the operation of the front sensors in the same way.
