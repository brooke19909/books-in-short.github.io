White City Resort 5 * (Turkey, Alanya) - hotel description, photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Alania is one of the most popular resorts of the Republic of Turkey among Russians. Every year, thousands of our compatriots go here on vacation. This region boasts a wide hotel base, offering tourists both budget accommodation and luxury hotel accommodations. If you are accustomed to the comfort and service of the highest class and plan to spend a vacation in Alanya, be sure to consider the White City Resort 5 *. We offer today to learn more about this hotel, as well as find out what impression our compatriots have on living in it.





Location of White City Resort

This five-star luxury hotel is located in the resort village of Konakli twenty kilometers from the center of the region - the city of Alanya. So, if you wish, you can always make a trip to this large tourist center, known for its developed entertainment infrastructure. You can get here both by taxi and by minibus, plying in the direction of Konakli-Alania. As for the airport, the nearest to the White City Resort Hotel 5 * air harbor is 100 kilometers away. So after arriving on the way to the hotel, it will take you a couple of hours. But do not worry about this, as time on the road will fly by unnoticed, because you can admire the beauties of this region of Turkey through the window, as well as listen to the entertaining story of the guide. Since the vast majority of tourists travel here for a beach holiday, the proximity of the sea is one of the most important factors when choosing a hotel. As for the hotel complex we are considering, it has its own magnificent beach. Go to it just a hundred meters.

Check-In / Check-Out

According to the internal rules adopted by the White City Resort 5 * , check-in of guests arriving at the hotel is carried out after two in the afternoon. But if you arrive earlier than the specified time, the administration will do everything possible so that you can occupy the number of the category you selected as soon as possible. Problems with this can arise only at the peak of the tourist season, when the hotel is completely full. In this case, you can leave your bags and suitcases in the luggage room and go for a snack in the restaurant, relax by the pool, take a walk around the hotel or go directly to the sea on the beach. As for the day of departure from the hotel, the occupied room should be vacated before 12 noon. When handing over the keys, it is necessary to pay for the entire stay. If you have already paid with a travel agency for accommodation, then you will have to pay for additional services if you resorted to those. For payment at White City Resort & Spa 5 * both cash and plastic cards of payment systems such as Mastercard and Visa are accepted. If you use a card of another payment system, then in advance about the possibility of its use, you should inquire with the administration of the hotel complex.





Accommodation with children

White City Resort Hotel welcomes guests with children of all ages. In addition, if you place in your room a baby under two years old in a crib or a child under eleven years old on existing sleeping places in the room, you will not have to pay anything extra. For staying in your room, a child over 11 years old or an adult in an extra bed will have to pay 70% of the cost of accommodation per night. The need to provide such a service must be notified in advance to the White City Resort 5 * administration and wait for confirmation from it. Also note that in each room only one can be accommodated - either a crib or an extra bed.

Accommodation with pets

This point should be paid close attention to those people who prefer to go on vacation together in the company of their four-legged pets. So, the rules of the hotel we are considering prohibit guests staying with pets. Therefore, you have to make a choice: either leave your pet for a vacation at home, or choose another hotel with a more loyal policy in this regard.

Beach and sea

The five-star White City Resort Hotel has its own sand and pebble beach. The distance from the main building of the hotel is only a hundred meters. The beach is equipped with everything necessary for a great vacation. So, here you can use comfortable sunbeds with mattresses, sun umbrellas, equipment for water entertainment and sports. There is also a bar where you can freshen up with your favorite drink or have a snack in between main meals.





Entertainment

On the territory of White City 5 * there are two swimming pools (outdoor and indoor). Near them there is a sun terrace with comfortable sun loungers and parasols. There is also a bar with a wide selection of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. In addition, the hotel complex has a children's pool, as well as water slides, which both adults and young travelers are happy to ride.

Fans of active pastime can play tennis, play ping pong, badminton, beach volleyball, go in for water sports, go to the gym and do yoga or aerobics classes. The whole day the hotel guests are entertained by a team of professional animators offering tourists to play fun games or take part in fun contests. In the evenings, the hotel complex hosts shows and a disco.

Guests can relax at the hotel's spa. There is a Turkish bath (hammam), a sauna, a massage room and a jacuzzi.

The smallest travelers will not get bored here. For them there is a children's pool, water slides, a games room, as well as a mini club and a mini disco.

Cost of living

Despite the fact that the White City Hotel belongs to the five-star category, the prices in it are quite affordable. So, during the low season, the price of hotel accommodation starts from 3 thousand rubles. At the height of the tourist season, the cost of accommodation will be an order of magnitude higher.





Turkey - White City Resort 5 *: tourist reviews

Just want to note that the vast majority of comments from our compatriots staying at this hotel are more than positive. So, everyone appreciated the novelty of the hotel complex. So the rooms here are newly renovated, the furniture and appliances are modern and stylish. Several times tourists had small problems with cleaning the rooms, however, this issue was very quickly resolved after contacting the reception.

As for nutrition, there seems to be no dissatisfied here. So, many Russians note a wide selection of dishes. Therefore, everyone could find food to their taste here. A varied children's menu with cereals, fruits, etc., pleased the tourists.

Travelers also respond positively to the beach of the White City Resort Hotel. Although it is small, everyone has enough space here, and there are always plenty of sunbeds. The sea is clean and warm.

As for the pools, here there were no complaints as well. According to our compatriots, the water in them is clean and pleasant. The kids were delighted with the water slide.

The animation was also mostly liked by vacationers. Our compatriots note that the animators are not intrusive here, as in some other Turkish hotels.

Summing up, we can confidently say that the vast majority of tourists really enjoyed their stay at this hotel. Moreover, they would love to come here again and are ready to recommend it to their friends and relatives.
