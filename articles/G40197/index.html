Sandalwood Hotel & Retreat 4 * (Goa): reviews and hotel description

If you are planning a vacation in Goa and looking for a cozy comfortable hotel away from the hustle and bustle of the city, then the Sandalwood Hotel & Retreat 4 * (Dona Paula) may be a suitable option for you.



General information, location, photo

This hotel is located in the Dona Paula district in the suburbs of the capital of Goa - Panaji. Distance to the sea is about 300 meters. Dabolim Airport is 30 kilometers away and Dabolim Railway Station is 21 kilometers away. The distance to Panaji is 7 kilometers.

The housing stock of the hotel is located in 3- and 4-storey buildings and consists of 72 rooms. There are standard, premier and luxury apartments. All rooms are equipped with furniture, flat-screen TV, air conditioning, ceiling fans, refrigerators, safes, balconies. Free Wi-Fi access is available throughout the hotel.

Sandalwood Hotel & Retreat has a restaurant that serves both breakfast (included in room rate) and lunch and dinner (for a fee). The hotel also has a bar, an outdoor pool, a sun terrace, a fitness center, laundry facilities and much more. Rooms are cleaned every day. Guests can also order room service.



Sandalwood Hotel & Retreat 4 *: reviews of Russian travelers

The choice of a hotel, according to many tourists, is one of the most important points in planning a trip abroad. Indeed, the impressions of foreign vacations will depend on how much you like the hotel. An invaluable source of truthful and reliable hotel information is the reviews of those people who have already happened to visit this or that place. Indeed, in their comments, former guests try to tell both about the pros and cons of a particular hotel. To save you time, we offer you the generalized reviews of tourists from Russia and other CIS countries regarding their stay at Sandalwood Hotel & Retreat (Dona Paula, Goa). Looking ahead, we note that most vacationers were satisfied with their choice of hotel. But find out everything in detail.



Housing stock

Guests at Sandalwood Hotel & Retreat 4 * (Goa) were generally satisfied with the rooms provided to them. So, the apartments, according to them, are spacious, bright, clean, with a pleasant design. Many liked the stylish furniture. As for technology, everything is modern, in excellent condition. But if something went wrong, then after contacting the reception all the malfunctions were fixed very quickly. As a plus, tourists note the presence of spacious furnished balconies. By the way, some vacationers advise upon arrival at the hotel to ask that you not be accommodated in the rooms, whose windows open onto the area where karaoke and other entertainment are held. Otherwise, loud music in the evenings may disturb you. True, at 22:00 it is turned off, so no one will disturb the night's sleep in any case.

Our compatriots also attribute the advantages of the apartments to the availability of roomy safes in the rooms, as well as the fact that free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel. Moreover, the connection speed seemed quite good to the guests. So you don‚Äôt have to spend extra on the Internet here.



Cleaning service

Our compatriots, judging by their comments, were quite satisfied with the work of the Sandalwood Hotel & Retrit maids. So, cleaning and change of towels in the rooms were carried out every day. Also, as they were used, maids replenished the contents of tea sets and supplies of bath accessories (shampoo, soap, shower gel, toothpaste, etc.). Many tourists note that almost every day, upon returning to their apartments, they found amusing figures of towels in the form of hearts, swans, etc. on the beds. Such attention from the staff was very pleasant for the guests.

As for the hotel staff as a whole, our compatriots characterize them as friendly, responsible and always ready to help. Almost nobody speaks Russian here, however, remembering a little bit of English from the school curriculum, you can quite explain yourself.



Nutrition

The cost of living in Sandalwood Hotel & Retreat 4 * (Dona Paula) includes breakfast only. They are served in the hotel restaurant. Here you can eat both indoors and on the outdoor terrace. Breakfasts, according to vacationers, are the same almost every day. However, there is always something to choose from. So, in the mornings, hotel guests can eat soup, cereal with milk, sausages, sausage, cheese, pastries, fruits and vegetables, a dish of Indian cuisine (often very savory to the taste of our compatriots), dessert, tea, coffee and juice. In addition, for each guest, the cook fries an omelet to his taste and with the ingredients as desired.

Lunch and dinner are also available in the hotel restaurant. Travelers say that they cook here very tasty. However, many tourists considered the cost of food at the hotel unreasonably high. So, according to them, in the city you can taste the same dishes at a price of two, or even three times cheaper than at Sandalwood Hotel. Therefore, most vacationers preferred to eat outside the hotel.

Sea

Hotel Sandalwood Hotel & Retreat 4 * (Goa, India), judging by the comments of travelers, is about five minutes walk from the sea. The beach here is not very large, however, there are usually very few people on it. Tourists can sit both on sunbeds and on rubber mats, which are issued free of charge at the hotel. By the way, beach towels are also provided for guests.

Judging by the reviews of vacationers, the beach here is not bad, but far from the best in Goa. Therefore, most tourists used the opportunity to visit other places.
