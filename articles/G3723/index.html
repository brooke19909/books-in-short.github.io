5 star hotels in Phuket with private beach: description and reviews of tourists

Phuket Island is located in the western part of the famous Thailand. Although it is located in the center of trade along with India and China, the province still receives the main profit from tourism and resort services. A lot of tourists and guests come to the island. The main shopping centers, shops and restaurants, entertainment centers, entertainment venues and nightclubs in Thailand are located in Phuket. But besides all this, there is still sea, sun and beaches.

Hotels with private beaches. Is this real?

Travel agencies, arranging a ticket to Thailand, and in particular to Phuket, tell travelers that, according to the laws of Thailand, the beach area is all public and it is forbidden to block it with a separate fence. It so happens that the hotel may not have its own beach. But due to the geographical location and the relief of rocky terrain, the beaches of many hotels are separated by stones, jungle or capes. Thus, it turns out as if a separate beach near the hotel. There are also hotels with a private beach in small, cozy and quiet lagoons. When choosing a hotel, you need to consider comfort and category. It depends on whether there is a beach on the territory, how convenient a place for swimming.

This article will present the best 5 star hotels in Phuket with its own beach. Reviews from tourists will help you make the right choice.

Evason Phuket Bon Island 5 *



The beautiful hotel is decorated exclusively with natural materials. He just drowns in luxurious decoration. Here you can spend an unforgettable honeymoon or a magical, carefree vacation. Graceful handsome hotel Evason Phuket Bon Island 5 * captivates with its green territory, tropical exotic gardens. Southern nature personifies a completely new perception of a chic resort and satisfies the taste of every tourist.

The hotel complex is middle-aged. It was built in 2001 and is constantly updated. The area is 256,000 square meters. The hotel consists of 5 buildings and 260 rooms. Of these, 122 are studio, 91 deluxe, 17 juniors, 21 duplex pool suites, 7 pool villas, 5 family rooms, 1 bridal room.

Description of rooms

Studio type - rooms are small in size with a capacity of 2 people with a picturesque view of the sea and beautiful green gardens.

The deluxe is a bit larger, with a living room and a bedroom, for three people. Offers views of the sea and gardens.

Junior rooms are designed for 4 people. They also offer amazing views of the sea and the garden.

Duplex rooms are large and have 2 levels. The first floor has bedrooms, and the second has its own pool.

The complex also has villas surrounded by beautiful nature. Owners of these rooms can swim in their own pool and enjoy pleasant conversations in cozy gazebos, contemplating sea beauty.

Of particular note is the villa on the secluded island of Bon with a separate bedroom and living room. It is designed for 2 people.

The number of rooms in the hotel complex is very convenient and comfortable. The rooms have all the amenities: a balcony with a terrace, air conditioning, cable and satellite TV with numerous channels, a music center and a player, Internet (paid), telephone, hairdryer, shower in the bathroom, a work desk with sofa and a minibar.

Almost all 5 star hotels in Phuket with their own beach (all-inclusive is the service system that is usually used in them) offer buffet meals. Evason Phuket Bon Island 5 * is no exception. Breakfast is included in the price.

The hotel is located near the airport, on the south side of Phuket, not far from Patong Tourism Center.

The hotel has earned numerous positive reviews from vacationers.

Visitors Comments

The territory of the hotel complex is impressive in size. For guests there are restaurants, cafes and bars with Asian, Thai, international and French cuisines, business business centers, swimming pools with open views, various shops. The hotel has its own wine cellar. Leisure of guests and tourists of the hotel will satisfy the volleyball court, table tennis, fitness center. I am glad that all this is free. There are also paid forms of entertainment and sports: windsurfing, Thai massage and fascinating snorkelling.

You can definitely go here with children. For them there is a swimming pool, a playground with carousels, a children's entertainment club. There is a babysitter on site.

5 star hotels in Phuket with their own beach for families with children are represented by another worthy complex.

Hotel Trisara 5 * (Phuket, Thailand)



The hotel is located on the northwestern side of Phuket, surrounded by exotic and tropical forests with enticing aromas, on the shores of a quiet cozy bay with coral reefs. Nearby is the airport and Kamala beach.

The guests of the complex are presented spacious rooms with comfortable rooms, terraces and private pools, lined with marble and other natural materials. All villas offer a picturesque and unique view of the exotic garden and the sea. Oriental-style villas are equipped with elegant, comfortable furniture and art, kitchens and living rooms.
Hotel guests can use the spa, which offers exclusive facial and body treatments, including a unique and world-famous six-arm massage. The hotel is a member of a private club. It offers guests - professional golf courses of world level. For lovers of water sports - diving, exciting snorkelling, sea adventures on a yacht, fitness and tennis.

In cafes and bars, as well as restaurants, you can taste seafood, various exotic snacks and spirits. On weekends, the beach bar organizes jazz nights with grilled seafood, desserts and dancing. Outdoor swimming pools. For visitors to business meetings, their doors will be opened by a cozy and comfortable conference room. The hotel also offers car rental.

Entertainment at the hotel is available for every taste. For sports enthusiasts - aerobics, gym, water activities, scuba diving. For women - beauty salons and beauty services, a sauna and an unforgettable massage.

This hotel has its own beach with a gentle entrance to the sea.

Each villa has its own private pool. Breakfast is served directly in the room for free. According to tourists, the food here is excellent. The food is varied and very tasty. Centara Grand West Sands Resort & Villa Phuket 5 *





This hotel is great for families with children. After all, it has not only its own beach, but also a water park. Large families can stay in two-room apartments with a kitchen. The hotel occupies a vast territory resembling a botanical garden.

Banyan Tree Hotel 5 *





Arriving in Thailand, if possible it is better to choose 5 star hotels in Phuket with its own beach. "Banyan Tree" is one of them. The hotel is located 20 km from the airport. The hotel consists of 115 ultramodern villas. Everything here is immersed in luxury. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, TVs, safes, minibars and refrigerators.
