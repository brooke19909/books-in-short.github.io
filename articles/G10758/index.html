Skiing in the suburbs: ski resorts

Winter activities are becoming more popular every year. Russia is not in vain famous for its cold and snowy winter: here you can enjoy a huge amount of winter entertainment. Muscovites do not even need to travel far from the city, you can master mountain skiing in the suburbs. There is no point in spending fabulous amounts on holidays in European ski resorts, when there are so many places for winter holidays with good service and reasonable prices.



"Shredded"

The most prestigious and expensive place where you can find downhill skiing in the suburbs is Sorochany. There are a large number of tracks and the most comfortable conditions for an active winter holiday. Many convenient chair lifts will allow skiers to quickly reach the desired peak, and the variety of views opening from the slopes of this ski resort will make every descent unforgettable.

In total, the Sorochany resort near Moscow has 7 tracks with a length of 400 to 1000 meters. On each slope there is equipment to create artificial snow, which ensures the stability of the snow cover even during thaws and temperature changes. In the dark, the tracks are lit with high-quality lighting equipment, which allows ski lovers to enjoy their favorite pastime longer.

Sorochany also offers a wide range of ski equipment for rent and its own workshop for equipment repair. For families, there is a cafe, a restaurant and a children's playroom.

"Free"

If you are looking for skiing in the suburbs, Volen is ready to offer everything that a skier needs. He started this ski resort with just one descent, a ski lift, a couple of cottages, and was intended for Volen employees. But there were much more people wishing to ride on the snowy slopes than was calculated. Therefore, now the ski resort "Volen" is one of the largest in the Moscow region. It has 13 tracks and 7 cable cars, and in the evenings the slopes are well lit. Prices are quite reasonable, even poor people can relax here.

In addition to skiing, Volen offers many other attractions, such as horse riding, paragliding, cafes, bars, billiards, sleigh rides. If you want variety, then just 3 km away is the new Stepanovo ski resort, equipped with forest trails.





Yakhroma

Sports and entertainment complex "Yakhroma" is located in the north of the Moscow region, not far from the village of Stepanovo. This ski park has a wonderful infrastructure. The complex itself is made in the Alpine style and includes restaurants and hotel rooms. Yakhroma has ten ski slopes, each of which is equipped with a ski lift. For lovers of night riding, lighting equipment is installed. Special guns maintain an optimal level of snow cover.

The complex also has many different restaurants and bars, a night club, a bathhouse, a conference room and gyms. For those who are looking for skiing in the suburbs, Yakhroma can be a real find. Here you can rent a horse or take a helicopter ride, and winter sports enthusiasts will be able to go skiing as well as skating.

Ski club Leonid Tyagachev

This is the oldest ski resort in the suburbs. The Russian national ski and snowboard team is training on its slopes. This ski resort got its name in honor of the winner of the USSR sports day Leonid Tyagachev. The complex has 8 ski slopes that meet all international standards. You can relax after hard training in the SPA center or pool. The presence of the hotel complex will delight those who love skiing in the suburbs. There is a sports bar, restaurant, tennis court, skating rink, you can ride a snowmobile or ATV, and if you are pulled into the sky, then a motor hang glider is at your service.

Zvenigorod

60 kilometers from Moscow is the sanatorium "Zvenigorod". When it organized a ski base. There are no guns, but in winter there is always enough natural snow. The ski resort "Zvenigorodsky" has a ski slope with lifts, located very close to the sanatorium. Downhill equipment can be rented. "Zvenigorodsky" is a ski resort that will satisfy anyone who is looking for a vacation in the Moscow Region: skiing, a swimming pool, a restaurant, a library, billiards, a tennis court, a bathhouse, a gym - all this and much more will help vacationers to spend their time with pleasure and pleasure time.



Paramonovo

In the north of the Moscow Region, the village of Paramonovo is located. This ski resort is known to many admirers of winter recreation, because it is here that the largest hill in the Moscow region is located. Therefore, if you are looking for skiing in the suburbs, then pay attention to Paramonovo, you will definitely like it. Trails pass along the slope of Paramonovsky ravine. Here you can find steep slopes for experienced skiers and gentle slopes for beginners. Snow in Paramonovo lingers longer than in other ski resorts, since all the slopes face north. Lighting allows you to ride not only during the day, but also at night, and cafes and restaurants provide delicious meals for hungry skiers.
