Hotel "Volna", Nizhny Novgorod: photo, address, reviews

In summer, tourists flood Nizhny Novgorod every year, and this is no accident. Stunning nature and a huge number of architectural monuments, mild climate and magnificent river landscapes, an abundance of various recreation areas - all this makes it one of the best places to spend a vacation. However, the focus of our attention today is not the whole city, but only one of the most famous hotels - the Volna Hotel. Nizhny Novgorod has always cordially greeted its guests, the staff of the hotel continues this tradition, which throughout your vacation will become your best helpers.

Location

This is another reason why the Volna Hotel has gained such popularity. Nizhny Novgorod is the most important historical center of the country; it is never deprived of the attention of tourists. The hotel is located near the international airport, on one of the main highways of the city, which leads to its center. Hotel infrastructure

We continue to consider the most popular hotel that awaits you upon arrival in N. Novgorod. Hotel "Volna" offers guests not only accommodation in comfortable rooms, but also a large number of opportunities for entertainment and relaxation. For the convenience of guests, non-smoking rooms are specially equipped. The hotel has laundry and dry cleaning services, as well as a beauty salon. The concierge service is constantly operating here, it is very convenient for ordering services related to sightseeing of the city and booking seats in restaurants.

The business center of the hotel includes 4 modern conference rooms, which enable business people to conduct a variety of events. All these halls are equipped according to the most modern requirements, it is possible to connect almost any electronic equipment, which is extremely convenient for holding remote conferences. The hotel also provides 24-hour room service.



Restaurant "The Seagull"

Of course, a very interesting object for tourists is the restaurant in the Volna Hotel. Nizhny Novgorod is rich in good cooks, but this place is very special. Seagull is one of the oldest luxury restaurants in the whole city. He is known and loved by connoisseurs of sophisticated cuisine, and elegant interiors create a magical atmosphere. The menu is very diverse, including hot and cold dishes, salads and desserts. The highlight of the restaurant can be called a bar with a rich collection of whiskey and signature snacks. A lobby bar offers a wide selection of coffee, desserts and soft drinks.

Sports and recreation

The Volna Hotel (Nizhny Novgorod) is not only a place for a calm and quiet holiday. There is everything for active sports. Guests especially appreciate the local wellness center, which has everything to keep fit. This is a sauna with a steam room and a swimming pool, a relaxation room with a TV. You can freely play billiards, spend time in the fitness room, which is equipped with modern aerobic and weight training equipment. You can take a subscription or pay a one-time visit.
