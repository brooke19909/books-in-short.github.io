Hotels on Nevsky Prospekt St. Petersburg: addresses, reviews

When planning a vacation in St. Petersburg, most of the tourists decide to settle in the city center to be within walking distance from the main attractions. This is the right decision, especially since the hotels on Nevsky Prospekt are always open to their guests.

The most famous street of the city rarely empties even at night, especially in summer. So many cozy cafes, restaurants and bars are concentrated here that spending time there after tiring sightseeing is a great end to the day.

Mini-hotels: "Polikoff"

Choosing mini-hotels of St. Petersburg for staying on vacation, city guests have quite obvious goals:

  • firstly, their prices are much lower than in large hotels;
  • secondly, the minimum number of rooms allows you to get the long-awaited silence and relaxation after a tiring day;
  • thirdly, it is in such places that they manage to create a home environment that is so lacking when you feel like a guest in a strange city.

Mini-hotel "Polikoff" (Nevsky Prospect, 64/11) offers 15 comfortable rooms of categories ranging from single superior to deluxe.





Located just a couple of minutes walk from the Gostiny Dvor metro station, it is adjacent to such attractions as Anichkov Bridge, the Russian Lefty Museum, and the Comedy Theater named after Akimova.

All rooms at the Polikoff Hotel belong to the business class level, the price of which includes breakfast, and are equipped with:

  • wooden furniture;
  • telephone and TV;
  • a desk and a chair;
  • a wardrobe and a mirror;
  • a bathroom with necessary bath accessories.

According to vacationers, not all mini-hotels in St. Petersburg allow free pets, and at Polikoff you can take your pet with you, provided that it does not damage the furniture. Otherwise, the owner will have to pay for the damage.

Mini-hotel "Anichkov"

Some hotels on Nevsky Prospekt are located in ancient and respectable buildings, occupying the second, third and even tenth floors. So the mini-hotel "Anichkov", comfortably located on the 2nd-3rd floors of the house at the address: 64/11 Nevsky Prospect, offers its guests 6 classic and luxury rooms.





All of them are made in pastel colors, allowing you to relax and rest. Each room has:

  • cushioned furniture;
  • TV and telephone;
  • table and chairs;
  • wardrobe;
  • bathroom with shower and toilet;
  • hairdryer, a set of products for shower and personal hygiene, slippers and a bathrobe.

Breakfast is included in the price of the room, and the cafes and restaurants located a few steps from the hotel offer delicious lunch and dinner, which saves time on a long search for a snack, as guests say in their reviews.

Near the hotel are the Alexandrinsky Theater, Anichkov Bridge, the Great Gostiny Dvor and the Fontanka Embankment.

Hotel "Lampshade"

Peter's hotels on Nevsky Prospect vary in both the number of stars and the number of rooms. The latter is often of greater importance, as they create an atmosphere of privacy in a large and noisy city with the same quality and level of service as in large hotels. In the Abazhur Hotel on Nevsky Prospekt, 23, there are only 5 rooms, but each of them allows you to fully relax, restore strength and energy.

As guests of the city have a rest here, each room is furnished with care for people with upholstered furniture, there is a table and chairs, a TV and bedside tables, a wardrobe and even utensils. In the bathroom there is a hairdryer and necessary bath accessories, and in the guest corner in the lobby you can warm up food in the microwave or relax and have tea or coffee, which are available at any time.





Near the hotel there are the Wax Museum, the Juventa Theater, the fountain near the Kazan Cathedral and several metro stations at once, which allow you to choose any direction for viewing the city.
