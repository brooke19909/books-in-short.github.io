Portugal hotels - charm and comfort

The hospitality of the inhabitants and the bright sun, mild and moderately warm Mediterranean climate, the delightful coast of the Atlantic, famous attractions and resorts attract tourists to Portugal. It organically combines antiquity and modernity. This is a country where dreams of an affordable, comfortable vacation surrounded by picturesque nature, but with real European infrastructure become reality. Many hotels in Portugal are located in old buildings and are distinguished by a special charm and respectability.


The pride of the country is a network of historic hotels - pause. They are built according to the concept developed by Antonio Ferru to create "small hotels that are not like hotels." They are located in restored ancient castles, monasteries and fortresses. These hotels of Portugal are distinguished by the highest quality, comfort and convenience. The restaurants serve a variety of dishes prepared according to old recipes by skilled chefs. Hotels vary in location and theme. On the territory of architectural monuments, preserved in its original form, there are historical pauses; on the coast - romantic; in the countryside - natural. They provide an opportunity to see and experience medieval Europe, known to us only from fiction novels and films.



Tourists can choose for themselves the option of a temporary stay on the coast or in the mountains, as well as for belonging to a particular gastronomic culture. The magnificent Portugal hotels located in Lisbon, Evora, Braga and other crowded cities will give you an excellent vacation and a variety of entertainment. If you are interested in spending time away from the hustle and bustle, it is better to go to the Azores. There, surrounded by beautiful nature, you can relax in small hotels with a cozy homely atmosphere and European comfort.

The Madeira archipelago is recognized as a true Atlantic paradise. The picturesque island is one of the best destinations that Portugal represents. Madeira, whose hotels are surrounded by well-kept gardens and parks with an unimaginable variety of exotic plants, offers a variety of accommodation options. A unique symbol of the island and the embodiment of romantic golden splendor, luxury, elegance and old-fashioned charm is Reid's Palace, located on top of a high cliff. This is a standard luxury accommodation. Other hotels in Madeira are also distinguished by an atmosphere of special hospitality, comfort and an excellent level of service, regardless of the number of stars.



Booking hotels in Portugal is conveniently done using online booking systems. They allow you to select in real time the hotel, the room you like for certain dates of the trip, book it and receive confirmation. Choosing a hotel is based on viewing the galleries of hotels, photos in catalogs and reviews of tourists.

Portugal will give you an unforgettable vacation at any time of the year. The guests of the country are waiting for beautiful cities, excellent amusement parks, carnivals, outdoor activities. You can draw up an individual travel plan, or you can use the services of travel agencies, which provide many hotels in Portugal, and go on exciting trips to the mysterious and historical places of the country.
