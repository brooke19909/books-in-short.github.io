Ski resort Jasna, Slovakia: reviews, description and features of the rest

Unknown, picturesque mountain archipelagos and Carpathian massifs are attracted to our compatriots by the small state of Central Europe - Slovakia. This land beckons with medieval castles, pristine ponds, stalactite caves and snowy slopes. Among the abundance of ski resorts, Jasna (Slovakia) is in special demand.

Mild climate, healing air and moderate cost are the main selection criteria for visitors. Comfortable hotel complexes, which in quality of service are not inferior to expensive hotels in Switzerland, offer an unforgettable year-round vacation.

Virtual dating





Professional skiers and beginners, mastering all the subtleties of skiing, assure that Jasna (Slovakia) is one of the best resorts located at the foot of the Chopok Mountain in the Low Tatras. Numerous national protected areas and impeccable ecology make this place attractive for different age categories.

In addition to pristine nature and an abundance of attractions, the highest demand is on the mountain slopes, wrapped in a snow blanket. Peak influx of tourists occurs in the winter months. From December to April, the resort is crowded with vacationers eager for endless fun and a dose of adrenaline.

The village is equipped with climbing slopes, road routes for conquering mountain peaks. There are well-groomed walking paths and hiking trails for beginner skiers. Safely laid routes provide an opportunity for people of varying degrees of preparation to admire the mountain ranges in snow clothing.

Trails and lifts





Ski Slovakia (Jasna in particular) holds a leading position in the ranking of the best European resorts. Having once visited this marvelous corner, you will undoubtedly return here again. Fans of skiing will be interested in landscaped areas for skiing in the amount of 18 tracks. Burton Snowpark with interesting obstacles is offered to hone skills.

Beginner skiers will appreciate the low snow-covered slopes. There are schools open at the resort where everyone is taught skiing, snowboarding and skiing. You can take advantage of individual lessons. It should be noted that all the slopes comply with international standards and safety: in the evening, lighting is provided.

Especially for children built a fenced park "Maxiland" with spectacular slides, lifts, attractions. Under the guidance of an experienced instructor, the guys will quickly master the technique of skiing. The park hosts animation shows, competitions with prizes and awards, which delight even adults. Children from 3 years old are accepted.

Entertaining activities

After visiting the ski slopes in the resort of Jasna (Slovakia), you will clearly have a brutal appetite - to satisfy your hunger you are offered public catering facilities. There are an unthinkable number of those in the village. Delicious Slovak cuisine is served in local restaurants and bars. After a hearty lunch, guests will enjoy unforgettable excursion routes to significant places.





In addition to nightclubs, discos, be sure to visit the Tatralandia Water Park, located in the provincial town of Liptovsky Mikulas. You can get a lot of positive impressions and improve your health in the thermal waters of Beshenov. In the summer season, entertainment does not decrease. It offers mountain bikes, scooter, roller skating, hiking, visiting nature reserves.

It will be useful to go on an excursion to the Demyanovskaya and Bystrian caves or to the medieval city of Kosice. Tours to waterfalls are organized in the Vayskovskaya valley. Traveling to the outstanding objects of Slovakia will bring true pleasure to the most sophisticated tourist.

Places to stay

Hotels (Jasna, Slovakia) are built near ski slopes and lower lifts. There are many accommodation options - from two-star hotels to four-star pensions. Pricing depends on the category. Accommodation services are offered by both small economy-class hotels and chic cottages equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay.





The Grand Hotel is very popular with well-developed infrastructure and a high level of service. The price includes two meals a day. The complex provides chic opportunities for active leisure with children. Rental facilities for the rental of sports equipment and equipment are open in the territory.

One of the best hotels in Slovakia includes the Economy Hotel Ski Hotel, located within walking distance from the Zagradki ski lift. Available standard rooms, superior apartments and individual cottages.

The third place of honor is the Mikulashska Hata hotel with a treatment center. A full rest in combination with wellness procedures will give vivid emotions.

The road to the village of Jasna (Slovakia)

Getting to the resort is easy. Charter planes fly daily from Moscow to the Poprad-Tatra Airport. Travel time will be no more than three hours. From the airport you should drive to the city of Poprad (5 km), from there by bus or car - get to the resort. Many hotels offer transfers.
