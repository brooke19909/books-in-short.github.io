How to earn an artist, being a wage worker? The occupation of the vast majority of modern brush masters is associated with wage labor and the implementation of a wide variety of orders. Below is a detailed description of creative directions for people who want to realize their potential through employment.

Makeup

Can an artist make money doing makeup exclusively? Of course, yes, and quite a lot! This profession is for those who like to create images with the help of applying makeup, to connoisseurs of all colors and their shades, to connoisseurs of human facial expressions. This profession can become very profitable, but it is quite responsible and requires long-term, diligent training and a lot of patience. To create beauty images at the proper level, you need to be diligent and patient, and also have a good imagination.

Tattoo

This is a profession for fans to decorate the human body with drawings. In order to become a good tattoo master, you need to master the technique of drawing on the body, because any slip can be expensive. This work requires high concentration and patience.

Creating a site with proposals to create paintings / drawings to order

First you need to create your own online platform for convenient direct communication with customers, then place your drawings or paintings there, that is, create a portfolio of your work. You can also specify the estimated price of each work of art. With the help of self-promotion and Internet connection with potential customers, you can create portraits, drawings, paintings, landscapes to order, while receiving good money.

Reproductions of famous paintings to order

Due to the circumstances of life, some artists became the most important historical figures, and their paintings became legendary masterpieces, which have a huge number of fans around the world. Among them are those who want to see a reproduction of a work of art that comes to life during their era. Reproduction of legendary paintings can be very profitable for a talented author.

Logos and corporate identity for companies

This profession is for those who have artistic talent, able to hide the important meaning in a simple and exquisitely delineated image. It is important to have a good imagination and be a good psychologist in the field of human manipulation.

It is certainly suitable for those who like to work in the fresh air, draws inspiration from nature, landscapes and the beauty of the environment. Art dealer

Another answer to how to earn an artist in modern conditions. This profession is directly suitable for a person who has the means to rent a room or has the ability to use his personal property. Also, this is a good option for where you can earn an artist without having experience working behind him. But art dealers are mainly fans of artistic skill with experience. They must understand this art and have a good sense of true talent.

People of this profession buy paintings from artists at one price, and sell them at other auctions to visitors to the gallery. That is, in fact, they perform a kind of intermediary function. Also, this lesson is well suited for those who, plus everything listed above, want to promote themselves as an artist and connect their professional life with contemporary art. Paintings bought by art dealers are subject to severe moderation before going to exhibitions or shows, because there are set fairly high standards for work.

Creation of their works for the purpose of sales on the Internet, on ad sites, on their own site

This work is for those who do not want to step back from their creative worldviews in any way. In this case, you need to be ready to create something unique, original, new, to some extent ingenious. Online training, webinars for those wishing to learn

Do artists earn money for those who want to paint? Naturally!
