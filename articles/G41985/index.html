Three easy options for stitching a full skirt for a little princess

Little girls always want to look like princesses. Therefore, many mothers want to make outfits for their babies. Even though the stores now have a large abundance of different dresses. Women's forums are full of questions about how to sew a fluffy skirt for a daughter. Today we will talk about these fantastically beautiful, fabulous and magnificent elements of clothing.

Skirt for the first appearance

It is very easy to make such a light and airy skirt, despite all its real adult elegance and sophistication. In order to sew a fluffy skirt for the first publication, no special skills are required.





To make it, you will need:

  • a satin ribbon with a length of one and a half meters of a milky shade;
  • the same tone cut tulle width and a half meters;
  • elastic band with a length equal to the waist circumference plus 2 cm.

Fatin is folded in half and cut along the fold line. The resulting parts are again folded along the length of the widest part.

At a distance equal to the width of the elastic gum plus 1 cm, we perform machine stitching with a minimum tension of the lower thread.

Now the top of the part is pulled together to form folds along the entire length, and the elastic is stretched. The ends of the gum are sewn together.

At the place of gum fastening, we sew a satin bow folded in half in the bend area.

How to sew a fluffy skirt for an angel

Girls from birth always want to be beautiful. For those mothers who want to create a new outfit for the baby in the first year of life, you can offer the following model. So how to sew a fluffy skirt for an angel?





Sewing begins with the choice of fabric. The base of the skirt will be a blue knitted cotton fabric with a length of 20 cm and a width of 70 cm. For shuttlecocks, an elastic mesh of 70 cm in length and a half meter is required. You will also need a linen elastic with a width equal to the waist circumference plus 2 cm.

First, we will cut the fabric. Ribbons are cut from the grid one and a half meters long and 7 cm wide, so we get 10 ribbons.

Now we make the basis of the skirt, for this we grind our knitted fabric into a ring.

Next, sew the ruffles. Starting from the edge of the skirt, we customize our stripes of mesh, making a line in the middle. At the same time, folds are constantly laid along the entire length. The ends of the tapes are hidden in the resulting clutches. We make the line so that the ruffles are found one on top of the other. The more folds, the more magnificent the skirt.

Now we sew in elastic elastic at the very top, and from the remnants of the fabric we form a decorative flower for the bandage on the head.

Little Fairy Skirt

But how to sew a fluffy skirt if there is no sewing machine at home? Very simple.





First of all, we choose the fabric for the future dress. It is necessary that the edges of the fabric do not crumble, and the material itself is light and airy. Fatin is best suited for this. The model used purple, green and blue cut. The length of each of them is 1 meter with a width of 1.5 m. In general, the width should be equal to 2 skirt lengths plus 5 cm per waist.

You will also need an elastic elastic band for a belt with a width equal to the waist circumference, a wide satin ribbon in the tone of the model, we have violet, threads and large decorative flowers for decoration.

Now you can start creating a fabulous outfit. First, cut the tulle into strips 10 cm wide and one meter long. So from three cuts we get 60 tapes.

Next, we chop off each of the colored ribbons in half and begin to put them on an elastic band. To do this, make a loop at the fold of the tape and tighten it already on the elastic band. Ribbons alternate in colors. At the end of the work, they should all be evenly attached to the elastic band. The base of the skirt is made.

Now we fold our satin bow in half and manually sew it to the skirt. The following is a fitting. Call your little fashionista and adjust the width of the skirt, as well as specify the location of decorative flowers already on the model itself. Sew the ends of the elastic to each other, then fasten the decorative flowers. Do not forget to leave one bouquet for making a hoop on your head. Our outfit of the spring fairy from the fairy forest is ready. Surprisingly, working on such a beautiful product took very little time. To make such a sundress skirt is much faster than deciding how to sew a tulip skirt.

In order for the work to come out neat, you must especially carefully fasten the tape on the belt.
