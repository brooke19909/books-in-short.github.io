Heart of balloons: how to make it?

More recently, people decorated their homes with flowers and garlands for the holidays, but today balloons are much more popular. They began to hang in the rooms, decorate them with the corners of tables and chairs. It is quite natural that such a solemn event as a wedding also cannot do without balls. But if no one knew how to make figures out of them before, now dozens, or even hundreds of people around the world demonstrate such skill. Some make simpler figures, while others create whole masterpieces. Today we will tell you how to make ball figures and decorate a banquet hall, for example, with colorful hearts.



If we talk about those for whom the art of making figures from air fun is a profession, you need to remember about aero designers. And do not be surprised, such a profession really exists, and make a heart out of balls for such people, as they say, just spit. However, since this phenomenon is quite new, and the demand for such products is very high, the services of qualified specialists are a very expensive pleasure. And precisely in order to save at least some part at such an expensive event, like a wedding, many prefer to make a heart out of balloons on their own. It is also worth considering that the manufacture does not take a huge amount of time, money and effort.



There are many ways and options for making ball shapes. All of them are divided into categories according to the levels of difficulty and the events at which they must be present. If you want to make hearts for a wedding ceremony and surprise the newlyweds with creative ideas, then we will tell you how to implement our plan. In the meantime, we list what is needed in the manufacturing process of our masterpiece.

If you are planning to make hearts from balls with your own hands, without wasting money on the expensive services of specialists, you need to buy some "details" that will make it possible to create a truly wonderful creation. So, we will need: insulated aluminum wire AP-16 or AP-25, several packs of balloons, adhesive tape, thick thick fishing line and a pump. Before you start making crafts, make sure that there are really a lot of balloons . After all, the more they will be, the better your heart will turn out. Naturally, balls of the usual diameter will not work here. To make a heart out of balls, it is necessary to purchase small-diameter products that would look beautiful in large numbers. Another important detail is the pump, since it is almost impossible and even dangerous to health to inflate such a number of balls independently.





First, cut the wire to the size we need. It all depends on your imagination: you can make one huge heart out of balls, or you can make several smaller ones. We twist the wire in the form of a heart and fasten the joint with tape so that it does not split.

Next begins the most uninteresting process - the inflation of numerous balls. It is recommended to inflate 2 pieces at a time, then fasten in groups. Aero designers call this method ‚Äúfastening fours,‚Äù since it is they who add volume to our work of art.

Now it's time to fix the balls on the fishing line. It all depends on your imagination and dexterity. But remember: the better you fix the balls, the more reliable the heart will be. We string the "fours" on the frame and hang on the wall. Best of all, such crafts look with backlighting or colors, since some balls look a little boring and dull.
