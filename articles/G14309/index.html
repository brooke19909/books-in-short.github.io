Delphine El Habib Resort 4 * (Tunisia / Monastir) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Hotel Delphine el Habib Resort is a fairly large complex located on the beautiful coast of Tunisia. And today, many travelers are looking for more information about this place. Where is the hotel located? What accommodation conditions does it offer? What type of vacation is it for?

Where is the hotel?





Hotel Delphine el Habib Resort is located in the famous resort town of Monastir, almost on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. The nearest international airport is only five kilometers away, and the hotel provides a shuttle service to its customers. The hotel complex is located on the territory of the old city - it is always pleasant to walk along the quiet, picturesque streets, especially in the evening.

Description of rooms





The hotel has 188 spacious, comfortable rooms. There are rooms of different capacities, including large family rooms. Depending on the floor, either a balcony or a terrace with summer furniture is attached to the room - it is always nice to meet the dawn in the morning or just enjoy the soft sea breeze and the sound of the waves.

As for the situation, then, of course, the rooms have all the necessary furniture. If necessary, an extra rollaway bed can be delivered to the room (for example, for a child). You can skip the evening watching Russian satellite channels on TV. Of household appliances, the room also has a telephone, air conditioning and a safe for storing personal belongings. There is also a minibar, but its content is not included in the price.

The bathroom will please with cleanliness and modern fixtures. There is a bathtub, shower, washbasin and bidet. In addition, each tourist receives a set of clean towels and branded cosmetics.

Food scheme for vacationers





Hotel guests are invited to choose a convenient meal plan - it can only be breakfast, half board (breakfast and dinner) or the all inclusive system, which gives the guest the right to three meals a day, snacks and drinks throughout the day.

Three times a day, tables are set up for guests in the main restaurant. The menu of the hotel is quite diverse - here you will find light snacks, various types of cheese, meat, fish and seafood dishes, as well as locally made ice cream and pastries, desserts and an abundance of fresh fruit.

You can have a great time in the bar, where employees will certainly offer you to try national drinks and various exotic cocktails. You should definitely visit the cozy Moorish cafe, decorated in a traditional style - here guests can enjoy the masterpieces of local cuisine. And by the pool there is a pizzeria with a large terrace, where guests can eat outdoors, watching the entertainment on site.

Beach holidays for tourists





Hotel Delphine el Habib Resort 4 has its own beach area, which is located just 300 meters from the residential complex. Here you will find soft white sand and clear sea waters. The beach is fenced, but adjacent to the public area. Here, comfortable loungers with mattresses and umbrellas are placed for guests. There is also a pick-up point for clean beach towels. The descent into the sea is gentle and safe, and the coastal area is suitable for bathing children.

Naturally, here everyone will have the opportunity to engage in more active types of recreation. For example, you can swim in a boat or kayak, ride a boat, water ski, do parasailing and scuba diving. There are also sports fields for beach volleyball.

Additional services on site





Of course, such a large hotel complex can offer its customers some additional services. For example, there is a private laundry room, as well as a small parking lot. There is also constant access to the Internet, which is also very convenient.

The hotel also specializes in organizing business vacations. On its territory there is a large conference room for up to 350 people, where you can hold a meeting, seminar or business meeting. Entertainment and sports for vacationers





What leisure and entertainment facilities does Delphine el Habib Resort provide? The photos show several outdoor pools located in the complex. And in the shade of palm trees sun loungers and umbrellas are placed - here travelers can relax or just enjoy chatting. There are water slides that work on certain hours - they will appeal not only to children, but also to their parents. And in cold weather, you can swim in the large indoor heated pool.

Of course, the hotel has a beautiful gym. Here you can play minigolf, darts or chess. There is also a billiards room, table tennis tables and large tennis courts. By the way, aerobics, aqua aerobics, and dance classes are constantly held for hotel guests.

The large SPA center will also please you, where customers are offered a huge selection of wellness and relaxation treatments, including massage sessions, balneotherapy and thalassotherapy. Hotel Delphine el Habib Resort 4 (Monastir): customer reviews

The city of Monastir is a great place for both beach and sightseeing holidays. And today, many Russian tourists choose the Delphine el Habib Resort hotel as a temporary residence. Reviews about this place are mostly positive.

For example, almost all hotel guests praise cozy rooms and the hard work of the maids, who maintain cleanliness and order in the rooms. There are a lot of enthusiastic reviews regarding catering - there are several restaurants on the hotel premises. Guests are offered a huge selection of dishes, including cold appetizers, fish, meat, desserts, delicious ice cream and pastries. A huge advantage of the hotel is its location, since from here you can quickly reach both the beach and the city center.

Tourists also thank the team of animators, which constantly entertains guests by arranging competitions, shows, performances and even competitions.

As for the cost of rest, depending on the date of settlement, features of the room and the chosen meal plan, a day at the hotel will cost about 3,500-5,000 rubles per person.
