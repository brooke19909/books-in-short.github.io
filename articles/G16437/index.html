Pha Le Xanh 1 Hotel 2 * (Vietnam / Nha Trang): hotel reviews

Hotel Pha Le Xanh 1 Hotel 2 * is a fairly comfortable place to stay in the heart of the popular resort city of Nha Trang.





Hotel Description

Pha Le Xanh 1 Hotel 2 * offers its guests incredibly comfortable living conditions at a very reasonable price. In the rooms of this hotel you will find everything you need that a traveler may need in order to regain strength after an eventful day. Also, you will certainly appreciate the professionalism and responsiveness of the hotel staff.

Pha Le Xanh 1 Hotel 2 *, despite its low status, provides quite high-quality services, the list of which is quite wide. It is also important that their cost is low compared to similar services in other similar hotels. Here you can use the Internet, bike rental, organization of excursions and much, much more.

One of the main features of Pha Le Xanh 1 Hotel 2 * is its colorful design, which demonstrates Vietnamese culture and values. The halls are decorated in a traditional artsy style, while the rooms, on the contrary, are characterized by elegant restraint. The originality of local culture is also emphasized by Vietnamese cuisine, which you can get to know more closely in the hotel restaurant.





Hotel Location

Pha Le Xanh 1 Hotel 2 * Hotel (Vietnam) is located in the very center of Nha Trang, near the public beach. Also very close to the hotel is such a famous architectural landmark as the Stone Church. Not far from the hotel there is a large market with groceries, clothes, souvenirs and other interesting things. You will have to walk to the train station in just 10 minutes, and if you take a taxi, you will need even less time.





Hotel rooms

All rooms of the hotel Pha Le Xanh 1 Hotel 2 * (Nha Trang) can be attributed to the standard category. Their difference consists only in the area, and, accordingly, and spaciousness, as well as the view that opens from the window. So, the following accommodation options are available to you:

  • Most of the apartments in this hotel are quadruple. They are a spacious bedroom with 2 large king-size beds. There is also a bathroom, which is a toilet combined with a shower. These apartments are just perfect for tourists with families, as well as friendly companies. These rooms can only differ in view. Some of them overlook the sea, and the rest open up a city landscape.
  • The hotel has several rooms equipped with a double bed. Most often, they are intended for a romantic getaway for newlyweds or just couples in love. The decor is decorated with beautiful sea views from the windows of the apartments.




Room Amenities

Hotel Pha Le Xanh 1 Hotel 2 * provides for the following list of required amenities in the guest apartments:

  • air conditioning, which allows you to adjust the temperature in the room in accordance with the vagaries of the weather;
  • a refrigerator for the products you bought, because the tour only provides breakfast;
  • TV broadcasting a huge package of cable channels, which will allow you to fill your leisure time while in the room;
  • the phone gives you the opportunity to not only contact the reception, but also make an international call;
  • the bathroom has a built-in electric hairdryer;
  • free wifi;
  • a wardrobe with hangers to accommodate your belongings;
  • an electric kettle so you can always make hot drinks for yourself.




What you need to know about the hotel

It will be useful for tourists to possess some data that relate to the rules of accommodation in this hotel:

  • all issues regarding check-in in guest rooms, as well as check-out from the hotel are resolved after 14:00 and until noon, respectively;
  • Children under 5 years old stay in apartments for free, provided that they are accommodated in existing beds;
  • an extra bed for both an adult and a child will cost $ 6 per night;
  • It is strictly forbidden to bring any animals to the hotel;
  • the hotel has international payment systems, which allows you to use bank plastic cards in payments for services;
  • If you need a parking space for your personal or rented transport, you must notify the hotel in advance.

Positive reviews

As tours to Vietnam are in great demand , travelers are becoming interested in budget hotels, among which Pha Le Xanh 1 Hotel 2 * is not the least. Reviews about it are rather ambiguous, nevertheless, positive comments are enough to make up a certain impression for this institution:

  • towels and bed linen are replaced every day, and supplies of cosmetics for the shower are replenished;
  • an undeniable advantage is the availability of wireless Internet in the rooms (although in some rooms its signal was rather weak);
  • right next to the hotel there is a large local market where quite budgetary prices for food are set;
  • the price of the tour is consistent with the quality of service and the condition of the rooms;
  • the hotel staff is very polite, smiling and friendly to tourists.




Negative reviews

When planning a trip, it is worth considering the low status of the hotel Pha Le Xanh 1 Hotel 2 * (Nha Trang). The reviews reflect a number of negative aspects of budget holidays, which are expressed in complaints and comments on the hotel:

  • the hotel has not undergone any major overhaul or redecoration, which, of course, affects its condition;
  • hotel staff do not pay enough attention to cleanliness (it is dirty both in the hotel and around it);
  • since the hotel is located in the very center of the city, the noise level in the rooms is very high (this becomes especially noticeable in the evening and at night, when the movement of people walking becomes the busiest);
  • the rooms are cleaned purely symbolic, while the dust is practically not wiped, and the floors are washed extremely rarely;
  • the problem of this hotel can be considered the presence of huge cockroaches;
  • the air conditioner is located uncomfortably, as the flow of cold air is directed directly to the bed (you can seriously catch a cold);
  • very noisy air conditioning, which also works intermittently due to its antiquity;
  • the hotel is far enough from the sea (it takes about 15 minutes to get to the beach on foot);
  • housekeeping (if you can call it that) is carried out very early - from about 8 in the morning the maids begin to knock on the door and wake guests;
  • food at the hotel is better not to take, as it is very expensive and modest enough (you will still remain hungry);
  • since the water in this hotel is heated by solar panels, it is almost cold in bad cloudy weather.
