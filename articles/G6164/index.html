Taksim International Obakoy Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya, Obakoy): description, service, reviews

One of the many resorts in Turkey - Obakey annually invites tourists to its open spaces who choose their vacation in comfortable conditions. Here, as in many other resort areas of the country, a large number of hotels are offered that are recommended by travel agencies for holidays in the summer and not only. One of them is Taksim International Obakoy Hotel.



general information

This vacation spot was opened relatively long ago - in 1989. Since then, the general condition of its buildings has been maintained and improved in the process of repair and restoration work. One of the last was committed in 2012. It was then that the rooms were upgraded in the rooms and in the entire building, and the climate equipment was significantly improved.

As noted in the official description of the Taksim International Obakoy Hotel, this place is ideal for families. All this is due to the fact that among his services a huge number of those are provided that are aimed at a comfortable pastime for kids and their parents.

Location

Taksim International Obakoy Hotel is located near the central part of the major entertainment city - Alanya. Here at any time of the day you can have a lot of fun in your free time, fully enjoying your vacation. The main advantage of this hotel is that it is located near the shores of the Mediterranean Sea and has its own, which lies 50 meters from the main building.

All tourists who arrive in Turkey begin their journey in Antalya - this is where a large international airport is located. It should be noted that the distance from it to Taksim International Obakoy Hotel 4 * is about 135 kilometers. That is why all operators provide for the transportation of travelers from the destination to the destination, which, according to the guests, is carried out on a very comfortable car in which air conditioning is installed - so the time spent on the road passes almost imperceptibly.



Rooms

Here, 184 rooms are presented to the attention of guests, in which everything is arranged in such a way that each guest is as comfortable as possible to stay here. All apartments are divided into three categories: standard, suite and family.

All rooms have a private bathroom, which is equipped with everything to make guests comfortable taking a bath. Each guest is provided with a bath kit at the entrance, which includes bathing accessories, a bathrobe, slippers, and a towel. In addition to all this, a large mirror and a hairdryer are provided for drying hair.

Each room has modern climate equipment, which was updated during the last renovation. With its help, tourists have the opportunity to adjust the desired temperature conditions, regardless of what weather is currently on the street.

Standard

The largest number of apartments in the hotel offer standard living conditions. In their reviews, tourists often note the beautiful, restrained interior in which they are made. The general color scheme is represented by a wide variety of brown shades: from beige to coffee. Guests note that in such an atmosphere you can relax and fully enjoy your vacation. Huge windows, from which a beautiful view of the Mediterranean Sea is observed, are covered with heavy and very dense textile, with which you can protect your room from sunlight. This type of room has a small terrace where tourists can relax on high chairs at a small wicker table, enjoying beautiful views.

As for furniture, it is designed for two. The Taksim International Obakoy Hotel standard class apartments have a huge double bed, a pair of side tables next to it, as well as a working area opposite. On the wall there is a TV with a large diagonal, which provides for connection to a satellite broadcasting system.

As for additional conditions, tourists can use Wi-Fi, as well as an electronic safe, absolutely free of charge. With a separate payment, the range of possibilities is expanded using a landline telephone and a minibar.



Family

Do not know where to go to Turkey with a child? This hotel will be the ideal option, as it provides for as many as 18 rooms for families with children. A crib is installed in each of them, and this is done completely free of charge.

These rooms are divided into two bedrooms, each of which has a pair of single beds, which can easily be combined into one. The rooms are installed on TV, as well as a small closet for storing things, the surface of which can serve as an excellent working area. There is a chair, two soft chairs and a small coffee table where you can relax.

Suite

The suite apartments, of which there are only 4 in Taksim International Obakoy Hotel, offer superior conditions for guests who want to relax together. Here, the entire space is also divided into two parts, only one room is designed for sleeping and relaxing, and the second is the living room.

The bedroom has a large double bed, next to it there is a small pedestal with a telephone connected to the international line. Opposite there is a cabinet with a working area, a large mirror hangs, and a plasma TV flaunts on the wall, where you can watch your favorite shows.

In the living room there is a large furniture set, consisting of a sofa and a pair of armchairs, next to them is a coffee table. The walls of the living room are decorated with beautiful paintings.



Conditions for children

The hotel is the best option for where to go with a child in Turkey - this is what the guests themselves say in their comments and reviews, telling in parallel about the opportunities that are provided to kids and their parents.

First of all, the convenience of such a vacation is due to the presence of a number of possibilities, one of which is the possibility of free installation of a berth for the baby in the room. Parents very often note the lack of rental strollers as a negative factor.

In the open area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe hotel there is a small pool with a slide for children. It is designed to meet all safety standards that are provided for by international standards. Its niche has a coating that prevents slipping, as well as a shallow depth that grows at the place of descent from the hill. A pool for children is located next to the playground, where everything is arranged for active pastime of kids.



Parents in their reviews very often talk about the children's club operating at the hotel, where animators work. They are always engaged in the development of babies, offering them games, production shows, as well as the opportunity to watch cartoons together. Some guests recommend using the services of a nanny, which are paid: their cost is about $ 10 per hour.

Nutrition

Tourists often respond positively to the food organized by Taksim International Obakoy. They celebrate the excellent work of the chefs who are in the main restaurant. According to tourists, it serves original and delicious dishes of Italian, Mediterranean, European and, of course, Turkish cuisines. Meals are made on an all-inclusive basis - every day guests are invited to a buffet.

In addition to the main restaurant, the hotel has five bars where you can taste delicious snacks and unusual drinks. The local menu offers a wide selection of diet meals.

Parents vacationing in a hotel with children often mention in their reviews that there are facilities for children in schools. They are expressed by the presence of highchairs and a separate menu, the content of which attracts the attention of young guests.

Service

The Taksim International Obakoy Hotel has a wide range of facilities that can be used by all guests relaxing here. Tourists often talk about the possibility of renting a car in a hotel. On it, they recommend going to explore the surroundings, as well as the sights, which in Alanya and in its district are many.

On the ground floor of the hotel there is a medical office in which a doctor is on duty around the clock, you can call him at any time, subject to payment of services. There are also a number of shops where it is possible to purchase interesting goods and souvenirs.

If necessary, vacationers can use an ATM and a currency exchange for small financial transactions.

Sports and spa

Fans of an active lifestyle do not miss the opportunity to engage in maintaining the state of their body and health, even while on vacation. Taksim International Obakoy Hotel reviews often state that the hotel has a ton of possibilities. Most guests prefer to visit the gym or outdoor areas designed for team games. Fans of extreme sports prefer to go in for water sports, which can be done under the guidance of an instructor, whose services are paid.



The spa zone every day collects on its territory a huge number of guests who love relaxation. According to tourists, professional massage therapists and cosmetologists work here, who can perform a number of procedures and immerse the client in a state of complete relaxation. There is an excellent thermal zone, represented by a combination of a sauna and a hammam.

In the open air there is a large aquazone, consisting of two pools with a total area of ‚Äã‚Äã600 square meters. m. In the largest of them there are three slides on which guests can have fun.



Beach

Almost all guests love to visit the beach, which is assigned to the territory of the hotel and is closed to visitors by people who do not live in Taksim Int-l Obakoy Hotel. Its territory is completely covered with sand and has a length of 80 meters along the coast. This place has everything for the comfort of vacationers: umbrellas, wooden deckchairs and even a bar where you can not only drink cocktails, but also have a bite to eat. Guests in personal reviews very often praise the original sweet dishes that are offered here. Many of them, as they say, are prepared according to ancient Turkish technologies.



Dressing rooms, showers and public toilets are evenly located throughout the territory. Along the coastline there is a mass of water activities designed for different tastes, many of which are extreme.
