Olga Nikishicheva: biography and interesting facts

Many of us want to learn how to sew, but refuse to try because of fear of difficulties. All these fears are successfully destroyed by the radiant and bright Olga Nikishicheva. With a confident movement of her hand, she cuts the fabric with tailor's scissors, transforms the flaps into elegant and stylish dresses with fast stitches and poses herself in models that turned out in literally half an hour. It is not surprising that every new Olga Nikishicheva workshop becomes a hit.

Biography

Olga Nikishicheva, although it has become popular due to its simple and effective master classes for beginners, is herself more than a titled and educated designer-fashion designer. She graduated from a textile institute and was educated in the capital of the fashion industry, in her very heart, in Milan, at the Carlo Secoli Institute.

She also completed an internship at the Sant Martins fashion school in London. And she is considered one of the best in the world for the training of high-class designers. The secret of popularity

The main feature of the workshops presented by Olga Nikishicheva is that her models are very simple and do not require complicated cuts, fit, expensive fabrics. So, any master, even a beginner, will cope with them.

It is also important that her models are universal and perfect for both a young girl and a stylish lady. A simple cut allows you to create an outfit for any complexion. Often, her models have only one or two seams, and the brightness of the model is achieved by selecting an interesting fabric or using decorative techniques and accessories. Often sewing with Olga Nikishicheva turns into entertainment. After all, her models are transformers, when one thing can serve as a dress and skirt or dress and blouse.
