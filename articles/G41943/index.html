Hotel Kleopatra Ramira Hotel 4 * (Turkey): description, reviews of tourists

One of the largest resorts in the Mediterranean coast of Turkey, of course, is Alanya. Among tourists, this city is really just very popular. Alanya is located far from the main resort areas of Turkey. And in order to break into the market, the owners of local hotels are forced to offer tourists the most favorable prices for accommodation. This hotel is located directly in Alanya itself, in its northwestern part. The distance from the hotel to the city center is approximately 1 km. Tourists who book rooms in this complex arrive from Russia most often to Gazipas airport. This transport hub is located about 25 km from the hotel, and in this case vacationers can get to it quite quickly. Sometimes travelers who decide to relax in this complex fly to Turkey and to Antalya Airport. This transport hub is approximately 125 km from the hotel. In this case, the path to the hotel, of course, will take much more time. Tourists get to the hotel from Antalya Airport in about 2.5 hours. This complex is located, unfortunately, not on the first coastline. The distance from it to the sea is approximately 800 meters. That is almost a kilometer. Between the hotel and the beach goes to everything else and quite a busy road.
