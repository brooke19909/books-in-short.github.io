Novorossiysk central market - a wide selection of fresh products!

In the city center, on the street. Biryuzova, 3, is located in Novorossiysk Central Market. Organized trading places and a wide selection of products and manufactured goods allow you to spend time with maximum benefit. The prices here are moderate, but you can always save on bulk purchases. You can bargain in the market, reducing the price: this is not a store!

Modern look

Progress has reached the Central Market in Novorossiysk: neat shopping malls, modern equipment, polite sellers, the territory is clean and comfortable. Shopping places are arranged right on the street, there is a covered area, which is no worse than a supermarket, where there is everything, but only fresh, more choice and a little cheaper.

The stalls with local "gifts of nature" are pleasantly pleasing to the eye: vegetables and fruits are fresh, very often in the season - only from the garden or from a tree. You can successfully purchase them in bulk, which is especially beneficial during the period of conservation / blockage.

In Novorossiysk, in addition to products, the Central Market offers: clothes and shoes, household goods, cosmetics, children's toys; there are hairdressing salons, stalls with shoe repair services, key making and other standard market attributes.

Fair in the market

Every year in Novorossiysk at the Central Market a periodic fair is held, which during its operation is open every day from 6:00 to 18:30. Local manufacturers offer residents of the city a wide range of products at competitive prices:

  • agricultural products;
  • folk art goods;
  • grocery group of products;
  • household and industrial goods;
  • products for gardening.

In this way, manufacturers are given good support. And customers like low prices and a large selection of products from the proposed range.

Despite the opening of numerous supermarkets and hypermarkets in the city, life in Novorossiysk in the Central Market is in full swing: new points are opening up, the assortment is expanding, especially on weekends many people are jostling in line at the counters. Here is a special atmosphere that you will not find in any store.
