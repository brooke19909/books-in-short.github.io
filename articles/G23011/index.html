Hitler's favorite car (photo)

Adolf Hitler is known throughout the world as a dictator of the Third Reich. He commanded the armed forces during World War II. There were always many secrets and gossip around this German politician. But special interest was always aroused by Hitler's favorite car, on which he traveled almost half the world. It is known that he was even lost for a while, and then again found.

History of creation

Hitler's favorite car has a rather interesting story. So, for the first time the Mercedes model was introduced in 1938 as a new generation car. The car was so luxurious that it was simply impossible not to love it. Adolf Hitler also liked the model, and the Reich Chancellor immediately wished to purchase it, despite the fact that this car was the most expensive at that time.

All spare parts for the vehicle were made in duplicate, so that the car did not stop on the road for a long time. In addition, Hitler's car, a photo of which is in this article, could easily be transformed from a limousine to a convertible and vice versa. The Reich Chancellor liked the car so much that exactly one year later he demanded to make several more models for his subordinates.

Mercedes was not only the most expensive car, but also fast and, most importantly, heavy. The latter characteristic was justified by the fact that the armor of such vehicles was still far from ideal. The car body is designed so that the passenger did not need to worry about anything. And the windows of Mercedes were not only armored, but also had a thickness of at least 5 centimeters.

The engineers thought everything through so much that they placed not only the armored back, but also spare wheels, which also had the function of armor. That is why Hitler rode so quietly among the soldiers and could even drive close to them. However, he tried not to leave his car, as he reliably protected him from bullets and any attempts.

Hitler's personal car, at the request of his driver, was also changed in driving functions. So that at any moment, for example, during an attack, you could immediately leave immediately. But nevertheless, this transport also had an unprotected place - an open top.

On this transport, which had an engine capacity of 7.7 liters and a horsepower of 230 horsepower, the leader of Nazi Germany always went to ceremonial parades. Car restoration

In the 2000s, Hitler's car was sought after by a group led by Vadim Zadorozhniy. Their main goal is the delivery of this transport to Russia. They wanted to not only track the evolution of the machine, but also get a valuable exhibit. The delivery process was quite lengthy. But after the car still ended up in Russia, it turned out that not all the details were in it. Spare parts were very difficult to find, since there were no more than a hundred of such machines in total.

And yet, in some incredible and miraculous way, all the necessary spare parts that were located in different corners of the globe were found and bought. It took 14 years to fully equip the car. However, it was only 90% complete. Currently, Hitler's car is still located at Zadorozhniy, who is engaged in its restoration. In the world of such machines, there are no more than five.

In addition to painting and collecting "native" parts for the car, you will need to further test it in the ride. If he can travel at least three hundred kilometers, then the restoration was not in vain, and everything was done correctly. And only after that he will go to the museum.
