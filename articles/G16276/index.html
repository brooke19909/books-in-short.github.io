Casa Blanca Beach Hotel (Icmeler, Marmaris, Turkey): description, service, reviews

Icmeler village is primarily a favorite vacation spot of European tourists. But there are usually a lot of domestic vacationers at this resort. This southern settlement attracts travelers including its developed infrastructure. Accommodation here will be easy to get used to comfort, as well as tourists who want to relax by the sea for little money. There are many hotels of different price categories in Icmeler and its environs. For example, vacationers, including domestic ones, can rent rooms here at the Casa Blanca Beach Hotel.

Features of the location of the complex

Icmeler city center is just a few hundred meters from Casa Blanca. Guests of this hotel can get here simply by walking. This complex is not too far from the city of Marmaris. Those wishing to visit this resort, guests of the Casa Blanca Beach Hotel must travel by public transport only about 8 km.





Conveniently enough, this hotel is located in relation to the beach. The hotel was built on the beach. Guests of this hotel can get to the beach in just 2-3 minutes.

Unfortunately, Casa Blanca is quite far from the airport. The nearest air hub, through which the most frequent connection between Turkey and Russia, is located about 100 km from it. That is, domestic tourists usually need at least 1.5 hours to go to the hotel upon arrival having rented rooms at this hotel.

Casa Blanca Beach Hotel: room description

This hotel accepts guests in one large five-story building. The snow-white building, built in an oriental style, looks, according to vacationers, simply magnificent. The courtyard of this complex has its own. Tourists also respond very well about its appearance. All platforms and paths in the territory are revetted with paving slabs “under a stone”. Everywhere in the courtyard of the hotel there are neat lawns and exotic southern trees and bushes planted. The total area of ​​the hotel is 2000 m 2 .





One of the main features of Casa Blanca is that only adult guests can be accommodated in the rooms. Children under 16 are not accepted at the hotel. The conditions for people with disabilities have not been created on the territory of this complex. Pets are not allowed at Casa Blanca.

Tourists come to Marmaris in September, summer and even winter. They can book rooms at this hotel both in low and high season. This hotel operates around the clock.

This hotel was built in the last century. Therefore, the experience of communicating with clients from his staff has been considerable. But the furniture and decoration are, of course, quite old. Moreover, judging by the information available on the websites of tour operators, repairs in this complex (for 2017) were last carried out in 2010.

What kind of accommodation does the hotel offer Kemer guests

There are not too many rooms for rent at Casa Blanca Beach Hotel (Marmaris). In total, this complex offers tourists 40 rooms. The hotel rooms are equipped with standard and Sea View. All rooms for rent in the hotel include:

  • air conditioning;

  • kettle;

  • telephone;

  • mini bar.

Wi-Fi in the rooms of the complex is free. Equipped in the hotel rooms and balconies. The weather in Icmeler during the season is usually hot. Tourists at this resort can enjoy the cool only in the evenings. And at this time of day, guests at Casa Blanca can sit on the balcony while admiring the surroundings, most likely it will be very pleasant. The rooms have hygienic ceramic tiles. There are also safes.





Reviews of tourists about apartments for rent

Casa Blanca Beach Hotel belongs to the class of 4 * hotels. Of course, to judge the degree of comfort in a hotel by its stardom can only conditionally. Sometimes in Turkey, hotels of a lower category can provide tourists with a better service than high. But fortunately, this does not apply to Casa Blanca . The rooms rented here, for example, have earned good reviews from vacationers.

The advantages of the apartments of this hotel guests include primarily their cleanliness. The hotel maids are cleaned carefully. Regarding the cleanliness and whiteness of bed linen, tourists also have no complaints. Sheets and towels are changed daily.





The furniture in the hotel rooms, as noted by its guests, is set quite comfortable. But about household appliances, unfortunately, there are also not very good reviews. And no wonder, because the complex is actually quite old. Air conditioners here, for example, may not do their job well enough. The weather in Icmeler during the season is usually very hot. And without a good air conditioning in the hotel, tourists here, of course, may feel discomfort.

Some vacationers do not recommend renting rooms on this ground floor hotel. Next to them are various kinds of utility rooms (staff can interfere with relaxation), and the floors here are not covered with tiles, as in other apartments, but with paving tiles.

Hotel Infrastructure

Thus, tourists can stay in rooms of Casa Blanca Beach Hotel (Turkey) with relative comforts. The quality of service, judging by the reviews, guests are also offered the appropriate star rating. At the hotel, tourists can use:

  • outdoor pool;

  • luggage storage room;

  • safe at the reception.

In table tennis, guests have the opportunity to play for free. There is also a billiard room in this complex. But you need to pay for her visit separately. Also, for the convenience of residents, the hotel has a massage room.





Infrastructure Reviews

Service at Casa Blanca Beach Hote l touristic, judging by the reviews, is offered quite good. The pool, for example, according to vacationers, is equipped very comfortable. The water in it is warm and always clean. Near the pool in the courtyard there are sun loungers of a very convenient design. There are sun loungers and a lawn broken by the pool. Here, for example, tourists looking for privacy can sunbathe.

The main contingent of the hotel is the Germans. About 90% of them live here. Also in this hotel are Russian tourists and vacationers who come from the Netherlands and Poland. Thus, there are usually not too many domestic guests in this hotel. But the staff of this hotel still speaks Russian. This, of course, makes the stay of domestic holidaymakers in the hotel a little more comfortable.

Casa Blanca Beach Hotel Food Reviews

This hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis. Dinners and dinners in the local dining room, as noted by tourists, are served quite good. But breakfast at the hotel, according to tourists, leave much to be desired. Unfortunately, they are oriented mainly to Germans. And tourists of this nationality do not eat too much in the morning. Accordingly, breakfast in the hotel guests are served meager. In the mornings, guests can order in the hotel restaurant only scrambled eggs, specific sausages, ham and all kinds of jams.





Dinners and lunches are more varied than breakfast. If desired, at this time the hotel guests can take in the dining room including meat or fish dishes. Chicken is served daily. Very tasty local chefs, judging by the reviews, also prepare desserts. The selection of drinks in the dining room is also quite wide.

What is a hotel beach?

The weather in Marmaris in September and summer is hot. And choosing a hotel, tourists, of course, pay attention also to the convenience of its beach. Hotel Casa Blanca is built on the bay. She has a shared beach equipped with the nearby Munamar Club complex. To its advantages, guests include primarily the absence of large waves. The sea near Casa Blanca is always calm. In the mornings, judging by the reviews of the guests, the water in it is usually clean. But closer to dinner, the sandy haze stains the water in the bay in a slightly brown color.

People on the beach usually relax a lot. However, guests at Casa Blanca Beach Hote l have some priority over other tourists sunbathing here. An employee of the beach at their very first request provides them with a sunbed and brings drinks from the bar working on the shore.





Beach towels are free of charge for guests staying at Casa Blanca . This majority of vacationers, of course, also refers to the advantages of the hotel. Residents can change towels every day at the reception.
