About the manufacturer

The creation of a powerful manufacturer of equipment began in the 80s of the last century in Stuttgart. R. With the onset of the 20th century, the activities of a company with a worldwide reputation were expanded, and the development of the latest ignition system became a key production. Today, Bosch is a global manufacturer and continues its development in various industries.

Precautionary measures

Bosch KGN39VI15R is a good refrigerator. In order for the device to serve faithfully for a long time, it is imperative to follow the operating rules that are prescribed in the attached instructions. The very first requirement of the manufacturer regarding the rules for safe operation is the appropriate location of the device. The refrigerator should not be placed close to heating elements, for example, next to a hob or oven. This leads to additional load, and over time, the device may simply fail.



In addition, it is not recommended to place the refrigerator close to the wall. The minimum distance that must be observed is 1-2 cm. It is imperative that the air circulates and does not allow the refrigerator to overheat. It is not recommended to place the device so that it is exposed to direct sunlight. This may cause overheating. Installation of the device must be carried out by level. For leveling from the bottom, special twisting legs on a rubberized base are provided.
