Performance

The device runs on a quad-core processor from MediaTek. All of them have a frequency of up to 1.2 GHz. Graphics received a power of 650 MHz. Supports some standards required for games. Offline work

The advantage of the model is the battery life. The battery is designed for 4 thousand mAh. If you load the phone medium, using only 50% of the brightness, the device can last about two days. When activating only the basic functions, it will not require recharging for about three days.
