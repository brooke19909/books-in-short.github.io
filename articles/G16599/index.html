Overview and prices of hotels Plesetsk

In Plesetsk, there are several hotels that will please with their prices and quality of service. In the article we will consider the most optimal options. The described hotels should be noted. Reviews about them are positive. The rooms are clean.

The traveler

This hotel Plesetsk (Arkhangelsk region) is inexpensive. Cost per day - from 300 to 1500 rubles. There is a transport interchange near the hotel. It offers comfortable rooms. All of them are made in bright colors, and are also equipped with the necessary equipment. The bathrooms are individual. Internet access is available on site.

Meals are provided in a nearby cafe. They serve national dishes. There are grocery stores nearby. In 2017, a major overhaul was carried out at the hotel.

"Swallow's Nest"

Another good hotel Plesetsk. Cost per day - from 600 rubles. The hotel is located in the city center. It offers free parking, as well as internet access. If necessary, laundry and shuttle services are available.

There are 8 rooms in the room stock. They are different in cost, but all are comfortable. Each room has a bathroom, except for the economy class. On the floor there is a bathroom and a toilet. Equipped kitchen where you can cook. If you do not want to do it yourself, you can visit the cafe. Nearby are beautiful places where food is delicious. In the shared kitchen - everything you need: microwave, TV, fridge.

Plesetskaya

In the hotel "Plesetskaya" you will have to pay from 600 to 1600 rubles. It is located near the main attractions of the city.

There are 20 rooms in the room stock, they are bright and comfortable. Each has the necessary furniture for a successful stay. You can eat in the dining room, food prices are optimal, the dishes offered are delicious. Pets allowed.

Medvedov

A good hotel, in which rooms cost from 300 to 1200 rubles per day. Free private parking and internet are available. The rooms are equipped with a cable TV. There is a private bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer. 2 km from the hotel motorway. On the territory of the hotels there is a kitchen, sauna and pool. Shuttle services are available at an extra charge. Rooms are cleaned every day.

"On Youth 10"

Room rates per day are from 500 to 800 rubles. There are several double and four rooms. The rooms have comfortable beds, towels and bed linen. A shared bathroom and shower are available on the floor. Have to eat in nearby cafes or restaurants, there are affordable prices. A fridge is available on site. Rooms are cleaned every day. Car parking is provided. The registration desk is open around the clock, which is very convenient.
