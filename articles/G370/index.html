Hotel Butua Hotel 4 * (Montenegro, Budva): reviews and photos of tourists

Butua Hotel 4 is a small, very cozy and comfortable apart-hotel located in the very center of the small town of Montenegro - Budva. Only 500 meters away are the main beauties of this place. Let us consider in more detail what Budva represents.

general information

A small resort town with a very ancient history. It was founded 2500 years ago on the shores of the Adriatic Sea, surrounded by beautiful rocky mountains, beautiful sandy beaches, which, of course, made it one of the most attractive places for a crowd of tourists from all over Europe.

Now it is the largest resort center of Montenegro with a whole system of cultural, entertainment and sightseeing programs. Rest in such a wonderful place will be remembered by any tourist, because only here the beauty of nature, ancient history and the comfort of hotels are combined.





Opposite the central part of the old city is the island of St. Nicholas. At the end of the bay, crashing into the blue waters of the Adriatic, there is a citadel and ramparts. It is pleasant in the evening to walk along the embankment of Budva, dotted with small souvenir shops. The narrow streets of the center can accommodate only three people walking nearby. Out of habit, you can get lost in such tiny passages.

If you decide to come to this paradise on Earth, then stay at Butua Hotel 4 *, because this hotel is homely. Let‚Äôs take a closer look so that the reader will feel the comfort of this place.

Hotel Description

Budva Butua Hotel 4 * is located in the central part of the city at the address: Nikole Tesle 11, 83510 Budva, Montenegro. This 4-star apartment hotel is located just 150 meters from the beautiful golden sand municipal beach. A feature of this hotel is its fitness for people with disabilities. Already at the entrance you can see a separate convenient ramp for wheelchair access. From the check-in table, access to the elevator with the required handles begins, rooms with special devices for the disabled in the bathrooms are equipped. This is the only hotel in Budva that has such a comfortable environment for people with disabilities.

Solar panels are located on the roof of the four-story building, because it is not for nothing that the hotel is located on a street named after Nikola Tesla. Modern systems contribute to the conservation of the ecology of such a marvelous place.





Butua Hotel 4 * has a convenient spacious underground parking for cars. European city, with a good access from the highway, the so-called Adriatic tract, connecting Montenegro with Greece, Albania, Bosnia, Croatia. Therefore, many Europeans come to rest in their cars.

Each room has spacious balconies from which tourists take wonderful photos of both the Adriatic with the island and the rocky mountains covered with thorny bushes. There is a 4 * terrace with a garden at Butua Hotel. You can take books in the library and read while sitting on comfortable benches.

Room Categories

The hotel has 23 rooms of various categories, but they all have very comfortable living conditions. There are 1-, 2-, 3-, 4-bed rooms, apartments, rooms for the disabled, studio type rooms. Each, regardless of level and location, has a balcony or a large terrace, a small refrigerator, a TV with satellite channels (there is one Russian), a telephone, a private bathroom, which has a toilet, bath or shower, hairdryer.





All rooms have different layouts, depending on the level of comfort and location on the floor. They are equipped with air conditioning, Wi-Fi internet access, a safe and a minibar.

Hotel Bathrooms

In Budva Butua Hotel 4 * there are several types of bathrooms. Different floor coverings: tiled, marble. Depends on the room category. They also differ in the fact that in some there is a bathtub, and in others - a modern shower. In expensive rooms also give cosmetics: shampoo, liquid soap, napkins for shoes.





According to the reviews of our tourists, we can conclude that not everything is as beautiful as in the photographs. Many guests of Butua Hotel 4 * in Budva complained about dirty baths, poor cleaning, although everyone noted the cleanliness of towels, which changed 2 times a week.

There are complaints about showers, where water does not come off after bathing, and a real flood forms in the bathroom. Comments and wishes were negotiated with the receptionist at the reception, but only the threats that they would leave the hotel forced them to take some action.

Hotel reviews

Basically, reading the reviews of vacationers, you can get a favorable impression of this hotel. The rooms have good soundproofing, so that the rooms are always quiet and comfortable, music from bars and discos located on the coast is not heard, and this is a definite plus. People really like the spacious rooms, soft comfortable beds, in winter the hotel is well heated.

There are also bad reviews. They mainly relate to cleaning and catering. Some rooms had poorly washed bathrooms. People did not like the food, some believe that they ordered breakfast in vain. In the cafe next to the hotel, food is cheaper and tastier.
