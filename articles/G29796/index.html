How to make a Christmas wreath on the door with your own hands?

Do you think that wreaths are hung on the door only on the eve of the New Year? Nothing like this. Craftswomen make jewelry for any occasion. They can serve as the onset of autumn, the birth of a child or the celebration of Easter. How to make a wreath on the door with your own hands, read below.

Hemp decoration



How to make a wreath on the door with your own hands? Build it from hemp. Where to find them? Take a trip to the forest. A walk in the fresh air cheers up, and if the trip has a purpose, then walking among the trees is doubly pleasant. A girl can take friends to the forest, who will saw her from a fallen tree hemp. These blanks for the future wreath need to be well dried. But do not put the tree on the battery. Saws must dry naturally. This can take a week or a month, depending on the thickness of the workpieces.

When the saw cuts dry, you can make crafts from them. We put a circle from the blanks and glue the parts together with a hot gun or super glue. When the first ring is ready, you can begin to manufacture the second layer. You need to create a circle that will be slightly smaller in diameter than the workpiece that is available. Dry twigs need to be glued to the lower right needle. From a burlap we tie a bow and decorate it with cones. The composition can include fresh or dried flowers, physalis, pine or Christmas tree branches, as well as soft pompons.

Wreath of cones



Such decoration of the door of the craftswoman is sculpted for the main winter holiday. Even a child can make a Christmas wreath on a door made of cones with his own hands. There is no need for any special skill and talent. Gathering cones together is very simple. But before you do this, you need to do some preparatory work. Cones should be painted white, silver or gold. In the meantime, they dry, you can make a stencil for a wreath. On a piece of paper whatman should draw a circle. The easiest way to get an even geometric shape is to circle a plate. When the cones are dry, they can be glued together. This should be done with a glue gun. We spread the cones in a drawn circle and fasten them together. You don‚Äôt need to stick anything to the paper, the stencil was designed so that the wreath had a round shape. When the composition of cones is finished, you can supplement the product with decorative details. For example, glue rowan berries or red beads. You can buy plastic birds or make them from plasticine.

Autumn wreath



Such a decorative product can often be seen on the doors of shops and children's centers. In general, those institutions that devote a lot of time to the decoration of the room. How to make a do-it-yourself wreath on an autumn-themed door? For its manufacture we will need: willow branches, small yellow leaves, cones, acorns and berries. First you need to make a base. It is made from willow branches. They twist together, and then a torus curls from a thick braid. The ends of the branches should be fixed with threads. Now you can decorate the form. Cones are attached to the wreath in the first place. They are large and take up a lot of space. The next line of leaves. They can be ironed beforehand so that they are preserved for a long time and not deformed. Lastly, a small decor should be glued. Acorns and berries take up little space and can fill the free space between cones and leaves.

A wreath of branches



To make such a craft, it is not necessary to go to the forest. You can go to the park. What is a wreath made of? From thick branches. No need to trim them from the trees, you can pick up already broken ones. Cutting branches is most convenient with secateurs, but you can do this with a knife and even scissors. How to make a do-it-yourself wreath on the door? Draw a circle shape on the paper. This will be a stencil, with the help of which the wreath will get an even shape. We spread the branches in a circle in a chaotic manner and fasten them with a hot gun. When the wreath is ready, it needs to be decorated. To create such a product is best in spring, when nature blooms. And accordingly, you can decorate the wreath with branches of a blossoming apple tree, cherry or lilac. If desired, live branches can be replaced with artificial ones.

Balloon Wreath



This cozy product reminds of winter. Making a wreath on a door with your own hands is very simple. To make it, you will need balloons, threads and glue. We inflate the balls, but not so much that they just take on a round shape. We take a ball, we stick its end in a needle and we pierce a bubble with glue. We stretch the thread and wrap the ball around it. You need to wind in different directions and in different directions. When this stage of work is completed, cut the thread and hang the ball to dry. Thus, from different yarns, it is necessary to make blanks of different colors and sizes. While the balls are dry, make snowflakes. You can either buy them in a store or make them yourself from liquid silicone. Making them is very simple: you should warm up the gun and draw a snowflake on a piece of paper. Until the silicone has dried, you need to sprinkle it with white powder or sparkles. Now we take the blanks, burst the balloons that are inside, and spread the circle from the made balls. Moreover, we form it not in an even geometric figure, but in an arbitrary, slightly asymmetric. We decorate the resulting wreath with snowflakes.

Original wreath



This decor looks very appropriate not only during the New Year holidays, but throughout the winter. How to create a wreath on the door with your own hands? A photo of such a product is given above, for its manufacture willow branches, thick woolen threads and plastic will be needed. The first stage is the manufacture of the mold. From the branches you need to twist the tourniquet and give it the shape of a circle. In this position, you need to fix the workpiece with threads. Again, take the branches, this time wrap them in shape. So that the wreath does not crumble, its right half should be tightly pulled with a thick white thread. The base is ready, you can go to the decor. From white plastic, you need to cut out the stars. This can be done both with the help of a clerical knife, and with the help of an igniter. We glue stars on a wreath. In the middle of the finished product, willow branches can be placed, which should first be sprinkled with foam or artificial snow. And you can also hang plastic balls filled with stars.

Christmas wreath



To make such a decorative element will be very simple. How to make a Christmas wreath on the door with your own hands in a similar technique? We take willow branches as a basis, which we twist into a ring. In order for it to hold, we wrap it with several rods. The form is ready, it remains toned. To do this, dip a sponge in white paint and paint over the upper side of the wreath. Now, snowflakes, beads and other small decor can be glued to the base. In the center of the wreath we place the silhouette of the Christmas tree cut out of plywood. Such a blank can be purchased at the store for creativity. Glue snowflakes and hearts on the Christmas tree. We outline the blank along the contour with white paint. You can tie ribbons and threads to the leg of the tree, which are combined with the overall color scheme of the craft.

Spikelet wreath



Such a product is created elementarily. A round shape is made of willow branches. And spikelets stick to it obliquely. You can use threads as a fastener. Wine cork wreath



What do people do for the New Year? They drink, eat and go to visit. But not many people know that from wine corks you can make a wreath at the door with your own hands. New Year is a holiday that requires careful decoration of the apartment.

How to create a wreath of wine corks? In order to fold an even circle, you need a round pattern. It can be made from newspaper or paper. We circle a large dish and lay out corks along the pencil outline. We fasten them together with a glue gun. The right side of the wreath should be decorated with branches of spruce or thuja. You can stick colorful balls on top. And we sprinkle colored beads in clusters in traffic jams.

Spring wreath



We will create such an ornament from the newspaper. We divide the printed publication into pages and twist them into bundles. We attach these tubes to each other using a stapler. Now you need to form a circle from the newspaper. When the base is ready, it should be filled with glue. This is done so that the wreath keeps its shape well. When the workpiece dries, it must be wrapped with willow branches or a vine. On the lower inner part of the wreath, stick figures of birds and nests, and live branches will help to complement the composition.
