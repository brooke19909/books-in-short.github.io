Charlotte Casiraghi - Charming Princess of Monaco





The mother of this beauty of royal blood is Carolina - Princess of Monaco. Charlotte Casiraghi was born on the third of August 1986. From fate, she received as a gift all possible virtues: intelligence, charm, kindness. The girl early lost her father, the famous Italian businessman Stefano Casiraghi. He died as a result of an absurd accident in speedboat racing. Dad was replaced by uncle - Prince Albert.

Charlotte Casiraghi, like her mother, is very similar to her famous grandmother (American actress Grace Kelly). All of them are real beauties. I must say, this always bothered a little girl who, already in early childhood, decided to achieve that those around her notice not only her appearance, but also her mind.

At the Paris Lyceum "Fenelon" Charlotte Casiraghi was a diligent student, receiving only the highest points. Especially the little princess loved the humanities and excelled in literature. As a result, received a certificate with honors.

The mother, who married again, took her children to France, trying in every possible way to protect them from the annoying press. She tried to raise them in a democratic "format", away from palace rituals.

Since childhood, Charlotte Casiraghi, whose photo is often adorned with glamorous magazines, was fond of horse riding, participated in many competitions. She even managed to win the Grand Prix in Valencia.



The girl graduated with honors from the University of Paris with diplomas in philosophy and journalism. A few years ago, she completed an internship in the London newspaper The Independent, where her works were printed.

Recently, Forbes magazine named the princess one of the most charming heirs - multimillionaires. A twenty-seven-year-old young girl is perfectly able to keep herself at dinner parties and dinners, apparently inheriting grace and charm from her grandmother.

Together with Albert, she opened the building of the new Consulate of Monaco in London last year, and a few months later she was present at the launching of the boats of the naval police of her principality.

Despite the fact that most of her royal relatives settled in the United States, Charlotte herself prefers to live in Paris, spinning at the epicenter of world fashion. Nevertheless, she happily spends all family holidays among her family and friends in her homeland in Monte Carlo.

Today, the spectacular Charlotte Casiraghi - the owner of a charming appearance, is called the sexiest among royalty, especially since she is not at all alien to the sense of style. This is confirmed by the fact that recently this charming princess of the smallest principality became the face of Gucci, with whose designer - Frida Giannini, she has been friends for a very long time.



The princess, being the standard of femininity, grace and beauty, practically does not bring joy to the paparazzi. She does not participate in brawls or scandals, rarely gives rise to gossip.
