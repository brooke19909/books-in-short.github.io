Napkins for cleaning the house. Their types and advantages

Every housewife cares about the cleanliness and safety of her home. To do this, she has in her arsenal a considerable amount of funds for the care of furniture, dishes, clothes and many other things. Cleanliness in the home promotes well-being and good health.

To significantly simplify the cleaning process and reduce the amount of used products based on chemical components, scientists in this field have invented special wipes for cleaning the house, which have various positive properties, which contributes to the effective fulfillment of the functions assigned to them. From what kind of material they are made and for what purposes are intended to understand further.

Viscose cleaning wipes

Viscose is a natural material derived from wood pulp. Due to the special processing of the fabric and the addition of silver ions to the composition, wipes made of this natural material are ideal for daily cleaning. They do not scratch surfaces, effectively clean and disinfect them. Using viscose wipes for cleaning, you can be sure that the fungus and other parasites are no longer scary. Suitable for wet cleaning in the kitchen and bathroom.



Microfiber cloth - the latest development for home cleaning

Appearing not so long ago, microfiber wipes made a splash. This latest development won over all the housewives by the fact that the cleaning process ceased to be painful and, best of all, no longer required the use of any detergents. How is this possible? Very simple. Microfiber wipes are made by weaving two polymers (polyester and polyamide) into one very thin thread. The result is a napkin consisting of ultra-thin fibers. They, in turn, give it an antistatic effect. This provides a deeper and more effective cleaning of the contaminated surface, destroys all bacteria that are on it. There are different types of microfiber cloths that are used for different types of cleaning :

  • terry texture: suitable for all types of surfaces, for wet and dry cleaning;
  • smooth cleaning wipes: mainly used for glossy surfaces and glass. Their application provides incredible
    shine and sterile cleanliness;
  • suede: designed for polishing wood, metal and glass furniture. Also used for porcelain and tableware.

Cellulose cleaning wipes

Such wipes are considered environmentally friendly and safe, as they are made from plant fiber. They perfectly absorb moisture, so they are suitable for cleaning kitchen surfaces and washing dishes. Choose such napkins should be in terms of thickness and size. The thicker the napkin, and the larger its size, the better and more durable it is. Cellulose wipes have antibacterial properties. Destroy fungi, unpleasant odors and bacteria.

When choosing cleaning wipes, do not save on cost, since it is an indicator of the quality and durability of the goods in this case.
