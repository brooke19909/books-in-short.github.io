Hotel Beds Patong 2 * (Thailand, Phuket): photos and reviews of tourists

Today, the topic of our article will be one of the inexpensive Thai hotels in Phuket, namely Beds Patong. As the name implies, this is "kopeck piece" located on Patong - the central resort of the island. The hotel is not by the sea, it can be called urban. This is a representative of the type of hotels that are called "bed and breakfast," that is, "bed and breakfast." Most tourists choose it so that it is cheap and convenient to spend the night. We can safely say that this is one of the best budget hotels in Patong, and we will prove it to you now. The hotel is dearly loved not only by backpackers, but also financially secure people, all thanks to the excellent management and work of the owners. The hotel is conceptual - its walls, lobby, rooms are painted with various English quotes and maxims, thematically related to the word "bed". So the name of the Bedz Patong hotel is played out.

Budget hotels in Patong

Although you will not find peace and comfort in this city during the day with fire, and even more so at night, and prices are higher than in Pattaya, but there are a lot of pretty good and cheap housing here. The most inexpensive guesthouses and hotels are located on and around Nanai Road. In this area is Beds Patong 2 * (Phuket). Budget hotels can be found right by the sea. Of course, they are across the road from the beach, because there are simply no hotels in the area closer to the coast. An example is Patong Beach Bed and Breakfast. This hotel has similar rooms and almost the same services as the Beds Patong, except for a slightly larger area and included breakfast. But the prices for this service are much higher, solely due to the location of the hotel. Therefore, tourists who know how to count money prefer to settle a little further from the sea, but save.



How to get there

Beds Patong Phuket is located 40 kilometers from the international airport, since Patong itself is located on the other side of the island. Airplanes from different countries fly here, including from Russia and Ukraine (mainly charters). From other cities in Thailand, you can get here on Thai airlines, including Air Asia. To get to Patong from the airport, you have several options. You can use taxi services - but this is a very expensive way to travel. The most popular are minibuses, which for 150 baht will take you to any place in Patong. And if you are completely limited in funds, then it is best for you to take a shuttle bus to the island's capital - Phuket Town, and from there transfer to another minibus. It goes to Patong, but unlike the minibus it does not transport passengers to hotels, but has a fixed stop near the beach.



Location

Beds Patong Hotel is a good base for exploring Phuket. If you are by car or motobike, then half an hour drive - and you are already in Phuket Town. Around the hotel there are many inexpensive bars and restaurants, as well as large shopping centers, such as Jungseilon with its entertainment and shows of singing fountains. The hotel is located on a small hill, so it offers views of the entire city. To get to Patong Beach and Bangla Road, you need to walk about ten minutes (or a quarter of an hour at a slow pace). And if you are a fan of nightlife, then consider yourself lucky. Clubs and go-go shows on Nanai Road are very close. Right next to the hotel you will find a Family Markt type store, and Banzaan Market, where you can buy fruit, is about a kilometer away. Patong's Boxing Arena is seven hundred meters from the hotel.



Territory and infrastructure

Beds Patong 2 * Hotel is located in a modern, functional four-story building, built in the modern high-tech style. There is almost no territory here, but the hotel offers direct access to shops, bars and nightclubs. Therefore, if you want silence, choose housing on the upper floors. If you are attracted to nightly entertainment, and you value the price-quality ratio, then you can also settle on the lower ones. The rooms are upstairs. On the ground floor there is a lobby, a small bar and sofas for guests. There is also a beauty salon and a small business center where you can send a fax or print tickets.

Rooms

The Beds Patong is small, with only sixteen rooms. They are decorated in Thai style. Standard rooms are small, with fifteen square meters in area, with double beds. There are rooms for families or companies. They are more spacious, their size is 25 meters. In addition to a large bed, there is also a bunk bed. Mattresses are comfortable, orthopedic. The rooms are quite comfortable - they are all air-conditioned with cable TV. The rooms have safes and refrigerators, as well as built-in wardrobes. Each room has its own bathroom with hot and cold water, shower, hairdryer and toiletries. Wireless Internet, catches well, high speed. It can be used free of charge directly in the rooms. Televisions are modern, flat-screen. Family rooms have balconies with sea views. Cleaned in rooms every day. Children under the age of twelve can be accommodated free of charge, if you do not need to put a separate bed for them.



Services

From nine in the morning until eight in the evening, the Beds Patong Hotel has a reception, the staff of which will not only accommodate and comfortably arrange you, but also answer all questions and help with any problems. When the owner or employee is absent, they can be called by mobile phone at any time. If you are by car, then the hotel has parking. The staff will store your items in a separate room if you arrive too early or depart late. The administration is happy to arrange a transfer to the airport or arrange a car or bike rental. Bicycles are taken free of charge, as tourists have repeatedly mentioned. You can send things to the laundry directly at the hotel. During cleaning, bottled water is put in the room. A welcome drink is offered upon arrival. The hotel has a hairdresser. The staff speaks good English. On the ground floor you can take boiling water. If it rains, umbrellas are offered at the reception.

Nutrition

In the lobby of the Beds Patong 2 * hotel there is a bar where you can drink soft drinks, tea and coffee. Food and drink can be delivered directly to your room, as usual, and tourists who buy a room with breakfast order it. But if a morning meal is not included in your stay, this is not a problem - there are lots of cheap food around. Many international and Thai chain restaurants and bistros are located in the Jungseylon shopping center. The food there is very tasty, and the service is of high quality. The Banzaan market behind this shopping center is also a food court. There for 40 baht you can get a large dish with rice and meat or seafood, and for 30 - a shake. In the evenings at Jansailon, the audience admires the fountains while tasting the ice cream from the Dairy Queen venue. In addition, shrimp or fish can be prepared for you free of charge in the same shopping center if you buy it there.



Where is it better to swim and relax on the beach?

It is wide, with fine white sand and a gentle entrance, located in a very beautiful bay. Where and how to travel around?

First of all, you need to start with Patong itself. To see a transvestite show, you do not need to go to Pattaya - tickets can also be purchased for a local performance in Phuket. And in some bars, the elements of this show are generally shown for free. Another impressive sight is Thai boxing, since the stadium is located very close to Beds Patong Hotel 2 *. If the crowds on the beach bother you, then at a distance of less than ten kilometers from Patong there are several more interesting options for sea holidays - Karon, Kamala and Tri-Tra beaches. But the problem is that if you do not have a rented car or bike, a trip there is possible either on an expensive tuk-tuk (300-400 baht one way), or on a local bus with a transfer in Phuket town (and this can take a lot time). Near Kamala Beach, there is the Fantasea Entertainment Center, where stunning Las Vegas-style shows take place in the evenings.
