Where are the best ski slopes in the suburbs

Many alpine skiing enthusiasts live in Moscow, but when it comes to winter holidays, most of them prefer to relax abroad, spending large amounts on tickets and holidays abroad. However, the Central Region offers a wide selection of resorts. The ski slopes of the Moscow Region provide excellent service and high quality ski runs, while not emptying the wallet with the terrifying speed that European ski resorts are famous for.





What prevents Muscovites from saving time and money? Why do they take a vacation to go skiing if every weekend they have at their disposal comfortable and high-quality ski slopes of the Moscow Region? The thing is the lack of awareness of people about the ski resorts near Moscow.

Sports complex "Kant"

A wide range of services for skiers and snowboarders is provided by the Kant Sports Center. The ski slope is almost in the center of Moscow. Which of the residents of the metropolis, fond of active winter holidays, did not dream about this? Kant has 17 slopes, as well as many other useful services. Here you can find an ice rink, tubing, a gym, tennis courts, trampolines, a track for cyclists and a cross-country ski run. Everything that a modern skier wants is in the Kant sports complex.

Kant also has its own ski school, in which young athletes are taught the basics of this difficult task. Training takes place not only in winter, but also in summer: special exercises for coordination and stretching, as well as skateboarding and roller skating on the asphalt slope, will help students feel more confident in steep bends with the onset of cold weather. Snowboarding and skiing can be taught not only to kids, but also to adults.

Also in the sports complex "Kant" you can rent equipment, conduct seminars, organize sports competitions, conduct corporate skating and much more.





Ski slope "Kurkino"

The Kurkino ski slope is a small but convenient resort complex near Moscow. This winter resort has only two slopes and is ideal for beginners. The ski slopes here are quite gentle and long, which allows novice athletes to work out the basics of technology with minimal risk to health. There is also a team of professional instructors and lifeguards monitoring the safety of vacationers. In the dark, the slopes are illuminated, allowing you to continue descents even after sunset.

The Kurkino ski slope is an ideal place to relax with your family. There is a training track for children, where young talents will be able to take their first steps in skiing, without fear of injury. However, for experienced athletes who are used to riding on steep, difficult slopes, the Kurkino may seem boring and unpromising.

The greatest number of skiers is visited by Kurkino in December and January. Despite the simple, gentle slopes, many families come here year after year, because this ski resort is already many years old, so some families have traditionally visited this slope for generations. Another reason for the popularity is low prices. Here you can ride for your own pleasure, without parting with a lot of money, and the rental of ski equipment here is not very affordable.

Here you can cheaply rent ski equipment or take skiing lessons from an experienced instructor. Ski base "Chulkovo"

Just 20 kilometers from Moscow is the ski club named after Guy Severin. Here, skiers can find a slope to their liking. There are gentle, unpretentious slopes for beginners and difficult tracks for experienced athletes. If you came with your family to ride on "cheesecakes", then you will also get to the address.





At the ski base "Chulkovo" there is a school in which everyone can learn the secrets of skiing. Here you can rent equipment in parts or in full sets, and a small cafe on the territory of the ski complex will allow you to warm up and relax tired athletes.

Sports and ski complex "Fox Mountain"

Not far from the Pekhorka River is the all-season sports and ski complex "Fox Mountain". If you like extreme sports - here you are. There are ski slopes for beginners - gentle and safe, there are also tracks for experienced, confident extreme people. There is also a descent for those who first started skiing.

In the summer on the Fox Mountain you can go karting or play paintball. If you do not have your own equipment - it does not matter, here you can rent everything you need for outdoor activities. Every weekend on the territory of the sports and entertainment complex all kinds of entertainment events are held.
