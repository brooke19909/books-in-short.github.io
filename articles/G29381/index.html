Steam mop "Korcher SC1030": reviews. Steam Mop Brush

Among the most affordable household steam cleaners is the Karcher SC1030 mop. User reviews emphasize its undoubted functionality and obvious usefulness in the household.

A few words about the manufacturer

The history of the German company "Karcher" began in the pre-war period and has more than 80 years. Initially, its products were heating equipment and heat electric heaters (TENy). The logical development of this direction was the release from the mid-70s of the last century of electro-hydraulic devices designed for cleaning and cleaning industrial premises and equipment, as well as motor vehicles (everyone knows the famous Karcher mini-washers). Twenty years after this, in 1995, the first industrial and household steam cleaners of the company went on sale. Since then, she has been continuously improving their design and supplementing the lineup with ever more advanced products.



How the Karcher steam mop works

User reviews about these products, which are mostly positive, still cannot, due to their brevity, explain to the interested reader the main advantages of this type of household appliances. We will try to fill this gap.

Outwardly, the Karcher steam cleaner resembles a conventional vacuum cleaner. What is the difference between a steam cleaner and a vacuum cleaner? The fact is that inside the floor container there is a container with a heating element, which heats the water poured into it to a boil. The steam generated in this case accumulates in a closed volume, its pressure rises (a kind of mini steam boiler), and when the user opens the passage through the ‚Äúgun‚Äù handle, the steam rushes under pressure through the hose, handle, connecting tubes and brush to the surface being cleaned.



Steam cleaning principle

Steam is an all-pervading substance, for it there are no barriers. He is able to get to particles of dirt inside the pile of the floor carpet, in the cracks of the wooden floor and in the covers of auto seats. It penetrates into all inaccessible places: along the plinths of the floor, under the window sills, between sections of radiators, etc. At the same time, it heats and destroys particles of dirt, which become easily separable from the surfaces and are carried out by a microfiber cloth worn on the brush, or by the bristles of the brush head.

At the same time, no buckets or basins with water, as well as periodic rinsing of floor rags, are needed. All dirt is collected on a microfiber cloth worn on a brush. Then it can simply be rinsed and used again. Of course, no matter how much the microfiber absorbs dirt into itself, but if the floors are very dirty, then it will have to be rinsed during washing.

The main thing when washing with a steam mop is that the minimum amount of moisture gets on the floor. Therefore, it is very useful when washing a laminate floor or parquet, which is generally criminal to wash with a rag.



Disinfection and deodorization of surfaces to be cleaned

Steam also kills bacteria and therefore disinfects cleaned surfaces. Plus, steam almost completely removes unpleasant odors from traces of cat urine. According to the opinions of many pet lovers, cleaning the rooms with a steam allows you to completely get rid of unpleasant odors. In addition, after it, none of the residents are guaranteed to have any allergic reactions.

After all, such a cleaning method does not need any detergents. Therefore, one of the most hygienic and environmentally friendly devices for cleaning floors, tiles, kitchen surfaces, taps and much more without the use of household chemicals is the Karcher steam mop. User reviews, however, force to make a reservation. The fact is that steam is a high-temperature product, and not every surface can withstand its effect. Therefore, boldly cleaning the carpet, painted wooden floor and porcelain tiles with steam, be careful with the tiles, as it is prone to microcracks due to steam.
