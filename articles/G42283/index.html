Club Herakles Hotel 3 * (Turkey / Kemer / Kemer Center): how to get? Description and feedback

The popular Turkish resort of Kemer is located about 50 km southwest of Antalya. The swimming season in this city lasts from May to October. And, of course, during this period in Kemer there is simply a huge influx of tourists from around the world, including Russia.

A lot has been built about the calves in this resort town. For example, many domestic vacationers book through operators or on their own rooms at the local Club Herakles Hotel 3 *. Russian vacationers are responding about this hotel, unfortunately, not particularly well. How to get to the complex: transfer

This city mini-hotel is located in Kemer, not far from its center. It was once built on the third coastline. The distance from it to the sea is approximately 650 meters. That is, the guests of the Club Herakles Hotel 3 * have to walk to the beach for about 15-20 minutes in an unhurried step.

Domestic tourists arriving who have rented rooms at this hotel, usually to the airport of Antalya. The latter is located about 45 km from Club Herakles. Usually, domestic vacationers do not have to look for ways to get to the hotel from Antalya. Russian tour operators selling vouchers to Turkey with accommodation at this hotel provide their customers with convenient transfer buses as well.

How to get there on your own

However, sometimes domestic vacationers, mainly those who have been traveling to Turkey for more than a year, book their places in Club Herakles on their own. Such tourists, in order to get to the hotel, first need to come from the airport to the city. On the territory of the Antalya transport air hub there is also a bus stop. It is located at the exit from the terminal.





In order to get to the Antalya bus station, tourists should take bus number 600 at the airport. These minibuses go to the city with an interval of only 30 minutes. At the same time, travel for vacationers will cost about 5 lire (77 rubles). At the Antalya Bus Station, tourists should transfer to a bus to Kemer. If you wish, you can buy a ticket for a regular flight or for an express route that follows without stops.

You can also get to Kemer from Antalya Airport by taxi. This method is by far the most convenient. However, a trip in this case will cost tourists no less than 150 lire (2300 rubles). At night, a taxi will have to pay even more (50%).
