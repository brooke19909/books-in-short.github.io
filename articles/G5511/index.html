Thermal imagers of Russian production. Thermal imaging systems NPO "AMB"

Nowadays, in the Russian Federation there are several manufacturers who are engaged in the manufacture of thermal imagers. However, the leader in this industry can be safely called the company NPO AMB. It produces thermal imagers of the most diverse orientation. Such devices are used both in everyday life and in the military sphere. Today, such systems can most often be found in various enterprises that are involved in monitoring the penetration of objects.

The movement of people, as well as any technology, is capable of fixing modern systems. In addition, thermal imagers can show objects even under water. Thus, their installation on the coastal site is very appropriate. According to their characteristics, Russian-made thermal imagers are not far behind foreign analogues. Specifically, the system "AMB" are able to boast of good tightness of the case. Sensors, as a rule, are installed on the basis of a cooled matrix.





Models of the company "Titan"

Russian-made thermal imagers "Titan" are developed primarily for video surveillance. Additionally, they can be used to guard airports and various terminals. Models of the indicated trademark are distinguished by good matrix resolution. It should also be noted many options. Thus, a person has the ability to customize the extender.

The spectral range of many models ranges around 12 microns. In this case, the field of view averages 28 degrees. The sensitivity of the devices is pretty good. The frame refresh rate is at 8 Hz. The focus of many thermal imagers is constant. In turn, the extenders are used in the T24 and T32 series. Digital zoom is available on many devices.

Characteristics of the model "Titan 46"

This thermal imager has the following characteristics: matrix resolution - 240 by 180, and the extender is used by the T32 series. The spectral range of "Titan 46" is 7-10 microns. In this case, the field of view maximum is 30 degrees. The sensitivity temperature of this thermal imager is in the region of 25 degrees. Focus is fixed, with digital zoom function. The signal processing for this model is carried out using the Digital Detail system, and the Titan-46 thermal imager is on the market for 200 thousand rubles.

Application of the Titan-36 model

Russian-made thermal imagers "Titan-36" are most often used at nuclear power plants. They are distinguished by their sensitivity, as well as multifunctionality. You can configure the extender in this model. In addition, the Titan-36 thermal imager is equipped with a viewfinder with an integrated display. The matrix resolution of the device is 240 by 180.





The detector in this case is used uncooled. The specified model is quite resistant to shock, it has an IP67 series protection system. The humidity of the device transfers up to 60%. At the same time, it is necessary to store the model at a temperature not exceeding 50 degrees. The video output of the Titan-36 thermal imager has a composite type. Focus is applied constant. The indicated thermal imagers (market price) are worth about 230 thousand rubles.

Optical and thermal imaging complex "Sych"

To protect various objects often use the specified thermal imager. Its production is carried out in compliance with all world standards. This device differs from other models in the high frame rate. The temperature sensitivity is exactly 20 microns. The field of view of the kit is at 25 degrees, and the spectral range is in the region from 5 to 10 microns.

There is no digital entrainment function in this modification. The viewfinder is set in color by the manufacturer. The device can be used at temperatures from 0 to 45 degrees. When assembled, the "Sych" thermal imager weighs exactly 750 g. The indicated modification is worth about 300 thousand rubles on the market.





Constant Focus Thermal Imagers

Constant focus thermal imagers are mainly manufactured by the Titan company. However, the NSA recently also presented its model. These devices differ primarily in high thermal sensitivity. Their emissivity coefficient is on average 1.2. The spatial resolution of the NSA model is at 4 mrad. Thermal imagers are made with displays for visualization.

Such devices are not suitable for isometric analysis. However, they are able to do automatic search. This process takes place according to previously entered parameters. There is no temperature correction mode in the devices. If we consider the model of the company "Titan", then it has an extensive memory slot. Thus, these devices are well suited for storing documents. Targeting with a thermal imager can be done. To this end, the company "Titan" has developed a red laser pointer at 2 kV.

High Precision NSA Models

High-precision thermal imagers of the Russian production "ANB" are distinguished by a small measurement error. However, they also have shortcomings and should be taken into account. First of all, experts note a low temperature range of devices. In this regard, the indicator of thermal sensitivity suffers. Their emissivity coefficient is in the region of 1.3. Lenses for such models are most suitable for permanent. Viewing angles can vary quite a lot.

The average spatial resolution parameter is 4 mrad. The scaling function in such devices is available, however, the imager is not suitable for isometric analysis. Automatic search can be turned on by the minimum and maximum temperature. At the same time, far from all modifications possess target indicators. On average, these thermal imagers (market price) are in the region of 240 thousand rubles.





Features of Variable Focus Thermal Imagers

Thermal imagers of this type are very compact and can be used in various industries. Detectors in this case are used with a focal plane matrix. The spectral range of such devices is 6-13 microns. Temperature sensitivity is in the region of 25 degrees. Thanks to this, the frame refresh rate is quite high. If we consider the models of the company "AMB", then they have high-quality viewfinders. At the same time, the device is powered exclusively by the batteries that come with the kit.

The manufacturer's protection system is provided for by the IP67 series. At full charge, such a thermal imager can work for about 120 hours. The model can be used at a temperature not exceeding 50 degrees. The humidity of these devices withstand no more than 40%. Extenders in the ANB thermal imagers are installed the most diverse. Signal processing in this regard can be time consuming. The average cost of the NSA model of this type is around 310 thousand rubles.





Thermal imager "AMB 640"

This thermal imager has a field of view of exactly 30 degrees. Its refresh rate is 8 Hz. The focus in the device is constant. You can configure the extender in the device. Digital zoom function is available. The viewfinder is installed with a digital display. In this case, the palette is switchable, and the analog connector is used as the video output. Humidity of the specified model is capable of withstanding no more than 60%. The manufacturer's protection system is provided by IP67.





Models "AMB" on a rotary basis

Russian-made thermal imagers on a rotary basis are excellent for protecting large objects. The palette in this case is white hot. Digital signal processing in them is fast due to high-quality extenders. The devices are designed for the spectral range from 5 to 15 microns.

Detectors in this case are used with a focal plane. Viewfinders are present in some models. Such devices are resistant to shocks, they are able to withstand falling onto a concrete surface from a meter height. Humidity devices do not tolerate well. Their temperature sensitivity is in the region of 25 degrees at 50 microns. An average thermal imager of this type costs around 240 thousand rubles.

Uncooled Devices

Russian thermal imagers of this type are distinguished by the fact that they are capable of processing the signal very quickly. However, they also have flaws. First of all, it should be noted the small spectral range of such devices. Additionally, they are able to work only with a white-hot palette. The protection system of such thermal imagers is pretty good. The company "Titan" has been producing such models for a very long time.

In general, they are in good demand because of their cheapness. At the same time, they are ideal for security agencies. Humidity of these devices carry no more than 50%. However, they are very susceptible to shock, and this should be taken into account. In terms of dimensions, uncooled thermal imagers are quite different. If we consider the model "Titan", then the average mass of the device is 800 g.





Stationary systems

Such systems are used in large areas where a wide viewing angle is required. Tricks for such devices are more suitable for a permanent type. In this case, the matrices are most often installed with a resolution of at least 160 to 120. Thermal sensitivity - no more than 30 degrees. The main manufacturer of such systems is considered to be the company "AMB". She offers customers in this regard a large selection. In particular, it is possible to choose models with detectors, as well as with designators.

There are still modifications that are capable of performing isometric analysis. For the military industry, these devices are in demand. The automatic search function is set according to the parameters. The average image rate varies around 50 Hz. There is a model of this type on the market in the region of 400 thousand rubles.
