How can a family with a child choose the right accommodation on vacation?

Going for the first time to the Sea of ‚Äã‚ÄãAzov, tourists very often wonder what kind of housing they should choose for a family vacation with a small child. As a rule, there are various options to choose from: from a simple room in the private sector to apartments in a hotel.
