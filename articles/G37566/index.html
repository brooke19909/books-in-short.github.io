A simple option for a volumetric butterfly

The next option for making crafts is quite simple, it is enough to have two small bright pieces of fabric. How to make a butterfly from fabric with your own hands, read on. It is most convenient to use cardboard templates that have the shape of a rectangle with rounded corners. Two strips 10 cm long are cut out for the front wings, and only 6 cm for the hind wings.

The parts are sewn together on the wrong side on three sides, then the workpiece is turned over to the front side and the last side is sewn carefully, the stitches are sewn together. The same procedure is with another tissue.

Then smooth the wings with an iron, after having straightened all the seams. If you wish, you can insert an additional layer of fabric, or thin foam rubber, or synthetic winterizer, so that the craft looks more voluminous. Standard Long-End Butterfly

You can make a bow tie with your own hands from fabric with hanging ends. There may be several, we will consider the simplest version of such a craft with two ends. The tie itself is cut out of a fabric rectangle. The width is equal to the double size of the butterfly itself, and the length is measured in the same way. Do not forget to leave 5 mm on all sides of the pattern for seam allowances.

First, the fabric is stitched in length along the wrong side. It turns out a pipe, which, after sewing, is turned on the front side. The seam is centered and ironed carefully with a steamer. Then the workpiece is bent in half, and the edges are sewn together at the back. The central jumper is sewn in the same way and before stitching the ends on the back, the main part is inserted.

To make long tails, rectangular strips are folded in half, bending the edges of the fabric inward. On three sides, the seams are made on the wrong side, then with the help of a long needle they are turned on the front side and ironed. The last seam is already done along the remaining raw edge on the front side. Sewing tape around the neck is also done. At the end, attach the fittings. Such a tie can be worn by both a man and a girl.

Bow Tie Folding Cloth

You can sew a butterfly from fabric with several folded loops of fabric. Such a tie can be made from the same fabric from which a skirt or dress is sewn. A long strip of fabric is sewn together on the wrong side of the pipe. Then the fabric is turned on the face with a seam inward and the workpiece is turned so that it is in the middle of the back. Be sure to iron the part with a steamer. Then butterfly loops are made, carefully laying out all parts of the future product. Start folding in the middle of the fabric. The remaining ends hang down in the center with a slight turn in different directions. The edges of the strips are cut at an angle and sewn, bending the bottom of the fabric inward by 5 mm.

The butterfly is fastened in the center with stitches of thread to match the main fabric. The brooch hides the hinges, pinned in the center.

How to sew a male butterfly

Sewing a male butterfly from a fabric with your own hands is easy, since the principle of the work is similar to the female version of the product. The fabric is selected dense costume or cotton. The pattern consists of two parts. This is the butterfly itself and the jumper. Sew parts in the same way, acting according to the manufacturing scheme described above. The cut rectangles are sewn along the long side with a seam on the back, then the workpiece is turned on the front side and ironed with a hot iron, turning the seam to the middle. Then the ends of the butterfly are sewn together behind the crafts. The jumper sewn in the same way is put on a tie in the center.

It remains to insert an elastic tape and adjust the fittings to the size of the neck. You can sew a male butterfly whole, with one ribbon according to the pattern depicted in the photograph. After stitching the edges of the fabric, the tie is tied in a knot.
