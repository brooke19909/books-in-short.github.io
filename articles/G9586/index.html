Bone-conduction headphones: technology description, types and reviews

Before proceeding directly to the list of models, we will figure out what headphones are with bone-conduction sound, and what is the feature of this technology.



Such gadgets allow you to transmit sound waves through the bone structure of the skull to the inner ear directly, that is, bypassing air and other conductors. This technology was developed several centuries ago. The most striking example is the famous musical figure of those times - Beethoven, who took her into service when he started having hearing problems.

Bone-conduction headphones do not block the inner ear and allow you to fully perceive the outside world, in our case, music and the other person on the other end of the wire.

This technology is especially in demand in the sports field, allowing the owner to control the world around him and respond in a timely manner, for example, to warning signals of cars or communicate with the right people without losing concentration. In addition, headphones with bone conduction are enviable among drivers and employees of large offices due to the specifics of these works. In general, in all places where you need a clear distribution between external noise and the desired audio information.

In the market of mobile gadgets you can find a lot of such devices, but not all of them are of proper quality and properly assembled. Therefore, we will try to identify a list of the most sensible bone conduction headphones. User reviews, expert opinions, as well as advantages and disadvantages of models will be considered in this article.
