Description

There are many interesting animals in the contact zoo. There is a steppe she-wolf Gerda, who is very gentle and sweet in character. You can also see the fox Christina. There is a small African ostrich Ivan. This cute guy will not let visitors get bored.





There are also other animals in the zoo, such as:

In addition, you can see birds (pheasants, budgerigars, long-haired owl and others).

Rules for visiting the zoo

  1. At the entrance, outerwear is rented to the wardrobe. Shoe covers are worn on the legs.
  2. On the territory of the contact zoo there are bins for cups and used shoe covers.
  3. Children under 14 years old are allowed only accompanied by their parents. Their communication should take place under the supervision of adults.
  4. It is forbidden to scare animals or throw objects at them.
  5. Some enclosures (with signs) can be visited only in the presence of an institution employee.
  6. Flash photography is not allowed; it can scare zoo pets.
  7. You cannot tease animals.
  8. You can feed animals only with food that is sold at the zoo.
  9. Do not throw objects into the enclosures of animals.
  10. Entrance to the zoo with its animals is prohibited.
  11. You can not drink alcoholic beverages on site. The same applies to smoking.

Ticket price, address and office hours

How much does a ticket to a contact zoo in Lyubertsy cost and how does the institution work? Monday to Sunday - working hours from ten in the morning (Monday from 12:00) to 21:00. The ticket price on weekdays is three hundred rubles. Children under three years old are entitled to free admission. On holidays and weekends - 350 p. For pet food (for one glass) you need to pay fifty rubles.

Pensioners on weekdays can visit the institution with a pension certificate for 150 rubles. The same rule applies to large families.

Where is the petting zoo in Lyubertsy located? October Avenue, 193 - this is where the zoo is located. It is located in the Pyaterochka store on the third floor.
