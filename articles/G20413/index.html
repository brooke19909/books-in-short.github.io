Best ceiling speaker system: reviews

Connoisseurs of high-quality sound unanimously argue that perfect sound can only be achieved with the help of an outdoor speaker system. Moreover, the speaker should be single-band. This statement is fundamentally false. There are a huge number of speakers of other designs that are able to provide very high-quality sound without any problems. Shelf and floor speakers can do all this, especially if they are based on modern technologies and the latest technological progress.

One of the latest innovations is the ceiling speaker system. It is used in places where for some reason it is impossible to use standard floor speakers. This solution saves valuable room volume, provides high-quality surround sound and gives the interior an aesthetic brilliance. However, in order to choose good acoustics for the ceiling, you need to know something. What exactly? We will try to answer this question.





What to look for when choosing?

Ceiling speaker system is a very complex device that requires an individual approach when choosing. It all depends on the characteristics of your ceiling. If it consists of GCR, then you need to purchase the appropriate speaker. For a stretch ceiling or PVC panels, there are speakers. They differ in fastenings and installation method. To choose, you will have to determine the type of ceiling.

Now about the qualities of the speaker system itself. Remember: the larger the diameter of the subwoofer, the better. For an apartment, it is better to choose two and three-way speakers. This will allow for better and surround sound. Mounted speakers in the ceiling on the principle of spotlights. So even those who have never done anything like this will have no problems installing.





What else do you need to know? It should be noted that behind the mounted speaker there should be an empty space of a certain size (depends on the speaker system itself). But it is best if this space itself is occupied by soundproofing material (mineral wool, cellulose). This will provide high-quality sound and suppress vibration. It is also necessary to choose acoustics, which has two front channels and one central. This will achieve maximum realism when watching movies.

But these are all general recommendations. Now, it's time to consider speaker sets from reputable manufacturers. It is worth noting that only those companies that have achieved certain success in this industry are engaged in the production of speakers of this plan. Beginners do not risk undertaking such a difficult job. So let's get started.
