How old is Ryazan? History of the city

Ryazan is in the thirty largest cities in Russia, it is located 198 km from Moscow. The population is 515 thousand people, originally the city was called Pereyaslavl. Before answering the question of how old Ryazan is, we will plunge a little into its history.

This city gradually grew up on the Oka River, on fertile lands, water meadows, forests full of various wild animals, and ponds where there were a lot of fish. For many centuries, the Oka was the main route leading to Europe; it connected Pereyaslavl with other Russian lands, with the Asian East and Byzantium. Some chronicles indicate that once upon a time there was a certain transition or link through the Oka to the Don through Pereyaslavl-Ryazan.

Time for a change

During the internecine wars, the ancient Kremlin of Pereyaslavl-Ryazan occupied a small territory in total area. And further along the ancient architectural buildings, you can trace the growth of the Kremlin, because there were monasteries along the borders of the city. In the XIV century, towards the Spassky Monastery, the city developed in a southern direction. In the XV century, the Dukhovskaya monastery was founded, which became the northern protrusion of the Kremlin. Then, in different directions - Troitsky, Solotchinsky, etc. The Ryazan Kremlin soon consisted of a chain of fortified posts. At that time, it was a powerful system of defense fortifications.

By the fourteenth century, Pereyaslavl became the largest city of Pochye and the capital of the principality, its heart was a wooden Kremlin with 12 towers, which was a defense located inside the grand ducal court and the houses of wealthy citizens.

The construction of the Kremlin with stone buildings began under Prince Fedor Olegovich. The Assumption Cathedral was one of the first to be built, today only the apse of the once white-stone masonry of the 15th century, cleared for modern viewers, has been preserved from it.





History of the big city of Pochye

Once in 1460, the khan of the Great Horde, Akhmat, wanted to take the city, but could not, since the Upper Posad of the Kremlin already existed, and around the settlement of the bishop and Borisoglebsky cathedral there was a prison - on the outside there was a reinforced ring constructed of a wooden stockade and watch towers where market, inns, parish churches and taverns.

The 16th and 17th centuries were marked for Ryazan by numerous peasant riots against the landowners who ruined their households. This plight of the peasants led to famine and epidemics in 1601-1603. In 1606, Ryazan joined with its suburbs to the people's leader Ivan Bolotnikov.

In 1611, Ryazan was one of the first to initiate the convocation of militias for the liberation of Moscow, Prokopy Lyapunov was elected the head of this movement, who led the detachment to Moscow.

In the XVIII century, Ryazan governorate was formed, and then - the province. The city of Pereyaslavl-Ryazan received the name Ryazan with its own coat of arms, which depicted a warrior with a sword and a hat of Monomakh.

In the Patriotic War of 1812, people were evacuated to Ryazan and transported thirty thousand wounded. Then 15 thousand Ryazans entered the fight against the enemy.

After the 1917 revolution, widespread demonstrations and rallies began. The people did not have confidence in the Provisional Government because of the famine that had begun. On October 30, 1917, Soviet power was established in Ryazan.

The war with the Germans and the beginning of the world

In continuation of the answer to the question of how old Ryazan is, it is worth noting another very important fact in the life of this legendary city. The front line approached the Ryazan region in the fall of 1941. Then there was a terrible bombing, which claimed many lives and destroyed houses. The city has been under siege since November 1941. The difficulties consisted in the fact that there were no units of the regular Soviet army nearby, so the defense was held by ordinary citizens. As a result, German troops were stopped from the city 30 km.

Literally immediately after the war, a sharp revival of the city begins. Industry, science are developing, institutions are opening. Today Ryazan is known for its valiant airborne training center.





Administration of Ryazan

It is important to note that the city received particularly powerful development during the chairmanship of the people's deputy and the mayor of the city of Chumakova Nadezhda Nikolaevna. The Ryazan city administration immediately took up the construction of 20 urban areas, trams, trolleybuses and buses went. The city was awarded the title of winner in landscaping and gardening among the cities of the USSR. For her effective 26-year-old work, Chumakova Nadezhda more than once received the passing Red Banner.

Since November 24, 2015, Ryazan began to wear the honorary title of the City of Military Valor. Today, the City Duma consists of forty deputies chaired by Frolov Vladislav. The mayor of the city is Oleg Bulekov.
