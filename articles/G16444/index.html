Fun and relaxation

The hotel has everything you need for a pleasant and positive pastime. Two terraces (one of which is for sunbathing), an outdoor swimming pool, barbecue and garden. Bars and Restaurants

In the Hotel Palais Des Roses 4 * (Agadir) there are several institutions offering gastronomic variety. The restaurant serves its guests a buffet, but a menu is still available. About booking and accommodation

At Hotel Palais Des Roses 4 * (Morocco, Agadir), check-in starts at 15:00. It is worth knowing, as in many hotels everything happens an hour or two earlier. But if tourists arrived in the city earlier, or simply there was no other flight, individual check-in can be carried out. It is better, of course, to notify this in advance so that the staff can prepare the room.

Departure, by the way, is also later here - it is carried out not until noon, as everywhere else, but up to an hour.

The hotel is allowed to stay with children of any age. If their age is less than six years, then for the stay of the kids you do not need to pay. For older children (from 6 to 12) you need to pay 50% of the total cost of the apartment.

By the way, if you need a crib, you should notify the administration about this in advance - they will try to provide it. And you need to do this in advance.
