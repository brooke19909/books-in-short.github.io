Maison Souquet - a piquant past

The Maison Souquet is a 5-star hotel located on the outskirts of Montmartre. The interior decoration of the hotel was carried out by Jacques Garcia, a well-known architect and modern designer. The Maison Souquet building has its own interesting history. Initially, a school for girls was organized in it, and in 1905 a brothel, which lasted only 2 years. In 2013, a large-scale reconstruction of the building began to create an elite five-star hotel.

Maison Souquet consists of 20 rooms, including 6 apartments and two apartments. Each room is named after the famous courtesan and is decorated with silk, embroidery and fabrics. The hotel is considered one of the best in Paris, in 2017 it was awarded the Best Romantic Hotel and Best Luxury Hotel Award.
