A carpet of money with your own hands can be made for a gift. The birthday boy will be delighted! Making it in a couple of hours would not be difficult even for an office clerk. Master class "Carpets from pom-poms with your own hands"

Most often, craftsmen order such carpets for a children's bedroom. They are soft, fluffy and very beautiful. And completely safe - a baby‚Äôs fall on such a carpet can be compared to a fall on a large soft toy. Do-it-yourself pompom carpets are easy. Both the process and the result will bring you a lot of pleasure. It will take you only a couple of hours to master the simple technique. You will spend much more time creating the pompons themselves. So, prepare colored yarn, a net (a special carpet is sold in needlework stores, but can be replaced with an ordinary building net), scissors and a pattern (optional).



Step-by-step instruction

  1. Make pompons in any way convenient for you: reeling on two fingers or on a cardboard. Tie the wound yarn in the center with a long thread, and gently cut along the edges.
  2. Or you can use one of two templates. With them, do-it-yourself carpets will be much more neat and more beautiful. (I - two cardboard rings, II - ice cream sticks are glued between two discs).
  3. Decide on the picture. In order not to be mistaken, you can apply it with colored markers directly on the grid.
  4. Tie the long ends of the thread to the stack. Determine the gap between the pompons yourself: the larger the ball, the greater the distance between them.
  5. The edges of the carpet can be tied or trimmed with strips of fabric.
