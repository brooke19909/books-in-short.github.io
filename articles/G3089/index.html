Completeness

A typical set of accessories and components, as for an entry-level device, in this model of smart phone. This list, in addition to himself, includes:

  • Charging adapter.

  • Typical interface cord.

  • Accumulator battery.

  • A modest stereo headset with very mediocre sound quality.

There are clearly several important components missing from this list. First of all, this is a protective film for the front panel of the device. There is also a typical list of documentation for an entry-level smartphone with which the ZTE Geek 2 phone is equipped. A description of the functions and capabilities of the device is described in detail in the instruction manual. Besides him, there is also a warranty card.

In terms of design, this device refers to typical monoblocks that are equipped with a touch screen for organizing the input of information into the device. In order to somehow solve this problem, you need to stick a protective film on the front panel. A typical smartphone control panel, which consists of 3 buttons, is displayed under the screen. Even lower on the lower edge are wired port connectors (microUSB and 3.5 mm) and a spoken microphone. A conversational speaker, a front camera for making video calls and a number of sensors are displayed above the screen. On the right side are the physical control buttons. They allow you to lock the gadget and adjust the volume. The buttons themselves perform well above the side surface of the smart phone and finding them ‚Äúblindly‚Äù is not difficult. On the back there is only the main camera and LED backlight to it.

Silicon base of smart phone

The time-tested and well-proven processor solution is used in ZTE Geek 2. Reviews characterize it only on the positive side. This is MT6582M from the company-developer of the CPU based on the architecture of the workstation - MediaTek. It is based on 4 energy-efficient "A7" architecture modules. At maximum load, each of them operates at a frequency of 1.3 GHz.





This silicon crystal is manufactured according to the 28 nm process technology. Its computing and hardware resources are enough to run any application today. The only thing to note is that the most demanding gaming applications on it will not go at maximum settings. But you certainly do not have to expect more from a modest entry-level CPU.

Graphics Accelerator and Display

Phone ZTE Geek 2 equipped with an entry-level graphics accelerator - "Mali-400MP2". Of course, he cannot boast a phenomenal level of performance. But, again, this is not to be expected from a budget class video accelerator. But its resources are quite enough to solve most problems today. The same ‚ÄúAsphalt 8‚Äù will go on it. Even if not at the maximum settings, but still this is one of the most demanding games to launch on this smartphone today. Although on some sites of online stores in the specifications of the device appears the IPS matrix, but this is not true. This is easy to verify if you reject the gadget screen by 45-60 degrees. A characteristic color distortion will occur. This indicates that the gadget uses a TFT matrix. Its resolution is 720x1280, that is, the image on the display is displayed in HD quality. Well, the screen size of this model of smart phone is quite impressive for today five inches.

Cameras

A very high-quality main camera is installed in ZTE Geek 2. Photos and videos obtained with its help once again convince of this. She has an 8 megapixel sensor. Autofocus system is implemented in software and there is a single LED backlight. At a normal level of illumination, photos and videos turn out to be of very good quality. But in the dark or in low light conditions it will be very difficult to achieve an acceptable result. It is not necessary to expect the best in terms of photos and videos from an entry-level device. The developers did not forget to install the front camera. According to the documentation, she has a 5 megapixel sensor. But in reality this value is much less. It is difficult to say how many megapixels in a sensitive element she has. But the claimed 5 megapixels are obtained by interpolation. Hence the sharp deterioration in image quality. For a full-fledged selfie, this is not enough, but for making video calls using the same Skype, it will be quite enough.





Information storage and data processing

The memory subsystem is typical for an entry-level device in ZTE Geek 2. Features RAM in this case are: 1 GB of the DDR3 standard. At the same time, about 600-700 MB of them will be used by the operating system itself. That is, the user has to rely on 200-300 MB. If you run 2-3 demanding applications, then there will be no problems with the smooth operation of the smartphone interface. With a greater load, certain problems are possible and the quality of the device will not be right. The capacity of the built-in drive is 8 GB. A user can only use half - 4 GB to install programs and store personal photos and music. This is clearly not enough today, but this issue can only be resolved using an external flash drive. Its maximum volume in this case can be 32 GB.

Battery

Phone ZTE Geek 2 equipped with a removable 2300 mAh battery. To evaluate this value, we will take into account some characteristics of the device. First of all, this is the diagonal of the display (it has 5 inches, and its resolution, as noted earlier - 720x1280). Next, we pay attention to the processor - 4 cores at a frequency of 1.3 GHz and based on the "A7" architecture. Given all these parameters, with an average level of use of the device, you can safely count on 1-2 days of battery life. More to achieve on this device certainly will not work. But if you run the same "Asphalt 8", then the previously indicated value will be reduced to 3-4 hours. And this is quite a normal picture - a smartphone is, after all, an entry-level one. And the optimization of energy consumption is certainly not a strong point of the mobile devices of this Chinese brand. A similar situation with autonomy will also be in the case when the phone will be intensively used to exchange data with the global web.
