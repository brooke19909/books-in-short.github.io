Location

Hotel "Love" (Yaroslavl): how to get

By public transport, the easiest way to get to the hotel is for those who arrive at Moskovsky Station. From there, about a dozen city routes go towards the 2nd Mill. Among them are buses No. 72, 33, 76, trolleybuses No. 9 and 5, minibuses No. 94, 91, 98, 97, 47, 73, 71. You need to get off at the Bolshaya Fedorovskaya stop, and then on foot.

For those who have arrived at the Central Railway Station, only minibus No. 45 is suitable. You need to get off at the Malaya Proletarskaya stop. Other transport links to the hotel with transfers.

From Yaroslavl airport, located in the village of Tunoshna, 19 km from the city, you can get by bus No. 183a, but only to the bus station, and then transfer to a suitable minibus (No. 47, 91, 97, 98). The transfer takes about 20 minutes.
