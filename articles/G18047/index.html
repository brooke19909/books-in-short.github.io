Natural features

One of the most beautiful Baltic countries is considered to be Lithuania. Palanga is a vivid confirmation of this. This city with its picturesque landscapes leaves no one indifferent. Thanks to the beauty of nature, Lithuania is so popular among tourists. Palanga is a city in which the air is filled with the aroma of a fresh sea breeze mixed with the smell of plants.
