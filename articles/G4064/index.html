What does a decorative universe consist of?

Start preparing for the manufacture of this original craft with a trip to the pharmacy. You will need glycerin and cotton wool (get the usual medical, cotton pads will not work). In addition, some kind of transparent glass container, food coloring and decorative spangles are needed. Thoroughly rinse and dry the selected vessel beforehand. Sequins are suitable for any cosmetic, for example, eye shadow or for nail design. The souvenir will turn out to be more voluminous and interesting if you take several shades. As auxiliary tools, tweezers, a large needle and a stiff brush can come in handy. Did you manage to find all the materials? Great, let's figure out how to make space in a bottle.





Making the "Universe": Step-by-Step Instructions

Take a piece of cotton and twist it with your hands into a tourniquet. Roll the resulting workpiece in sparkles. If they do not stick well, lightly moisten the cotton wool with water. Carefully place the resulting shiny flagellum in a prepared container and place it beautifully (you can use tweezers or a large needle for this). In a separate container, dilute the dye with water. Gently pour a little into the container with cotton and add glycerin. The most important thing is not to shake or shake the container.

"Cosmos" in a bottle is beautiful precisely because of the heterogeneity of color and stains of paint. Your decorative composition is almost ready. You can make it even more interesting by adding another layer, using new cotton wool and a dye of a different color. Now you know how to make space in a bottle. Do not forget to close the lid tightly, but rather put it on universal glue.

A worthy alternative to dyes

Food colors are not found in every kitchen. Is it possible to replace them with something? Theoretically, any paint will do. You can even use banal gouache. Dilute it in water, then carefully pour it into a container with cotton. How to make "space" in a bottle without dyes? Is it possible to do without paint? Useful Tips
