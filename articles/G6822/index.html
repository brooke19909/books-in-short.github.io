Location

Green Noise (Serpukhov) is located on the 97th kilometer of Simferopol highway on the banks of the Oka River in the life-giving zone of the Prioksko-Terrasny Nature Reserve. In its open spaces you can see many beautiful plants and animals that you will not find in other parts of Russia. Many representatives of the flora growing in the reserve are listed in the Red Book as endangered species.

Complexes for accommodation

Children's camp "Green Noise" is represented by six two-story buildings that have all the necessary conditions for a comfortable stay. Buildings designed by the best specialists are located on the territory of a pine forest.

At the disposal of a separate group of children (detachment) - their own floor, if necessary closed by counselors. For personal use, each vacationer is allocated an individual place in the closet, cabinet, as well as hangers.

Laundry facilities and drying rooms are equipped for laundry care. Bedding sets change on a bathing day, twice a shift. The rooms in which children live are designed for four to six beds. The furniture in them is very ergonomic, comfortable and safe. Bathrooms are equipped with the maximum level of comfort in accordance with the gender of the child. Also, children can use showers, which have all the necessary hygiene products. Residential buildings are supplied with hot water around the clock. There are common coolers on the floors.

Methodology for the formation of detachments

In the morning hours (10.30 - 11.00), the children gather in the assembly hall for the formation of units. Initially, groups of the smallest children are completed. For this, girls and boys of the same age category are invited to the stage, a squad is formed from them. When distributing, first of all, the opinion of the child is taken into account. Relatives and friends who want to stay in the same group must communicate their intention to counselors or leaders. The best option would be for the guys to gather in the squad with an age interval of two years. This difference has a beneficial effect on communication within the group. If the interval exceeds the required indicator, then children will most likely not be able to find common hobbies and interests that will unite them. This factor will negatively affect the emotional state of the child and the general spirit of the detachment.
