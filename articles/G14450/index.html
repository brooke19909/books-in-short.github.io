Why visit China Hotels

Surprisingly, for some reason, our compatriots rarely travel to China for the purpose of relaxation. This country, at the same time both traditional and modern, seems more attractive from the point of view of business and business negotiations, in not spending holidays. But China is not only modern technology and the most accurate electronics, but also amazing poetry, science and art, the magnificent beaches of Hainan, the eternal snows on the mountain slopes of Tibet, traditional medicine that does not lose popularity and, of course, hospitable and comfortable hotels in China.

This ancient state is able to receive tourists throughout the year. At any time, you can visit the famous Beijing Opera and the equally famous, legendary Shaolin Monastery, take a walk along the Great Wall of China and the streets of the Forbidden City, or admire the power and grandeur of the terracotta army. Everyone will be able to learn something new about this extraordinary country.

It should be noted that excellent reviews about hotels in China and leisure not in the territory of this state diverge not only within the immediate "neighbors" - Russia and Japan, but also in other countries. In local hotels, guests will find an excellent level of comfort, the attractiveness of the hotel itself and the design of each room, as well as the convenient location, which distinguishes almost all the hotels in China, and a large list of services they provide. Due to the fact that the government is allocating more funds from the budget to the development of tourism and hotel infrastructure, many hotels in China not only meet international standards, but also significantly surpass them.

An example is the Park Plaza Beijing Wangfujing Hotel - luxurious and modern, located a few kilometers from Beijing Airport and East Railway Station. The Forbidden City Street and Tiananmen Square are nearby. Everyone will stay in this place - with high-quality rooms and great views, a modern gym and large elite restaurants. Ladies can indulge in relaxing massages and a visit to the beauty salon, and the whole family will appreciate traditional Chinese dishes and a long tea ceremony. Of the shortcomings of this hotel, it should be noted only the high cost of living in it, for most tourists with an average income inaccessible. However, as you know, you have to pay for quality.

Shanghai guests will be able to stay at the Oriental Riverside Bund View Hotel, an elite, upscale and modern hotel, equally suitable for family vacations or business meetings in an informal setting. Like some other hotels in China, this one is located in the city center, not far from People's Square. Spa centers, beauty and massage rooms, saunas, restaurants and cafes - this is an incomplete list of everything that is available to hotel guests. For example, the hotel "Pearl River Garden 4 *", in the territory of which is the largest and most popular medical and health center of traditional Chinese medicine.
