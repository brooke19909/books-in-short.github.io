Bad Kreuznach, Germany: photos and descriptions, weather, attractions

Bad Kreuznach (Germany) is the oldest climatic and balneological resort of the country and the whole world. It is located in the east of the state in the mountains, in the valley of the Nae river, which is 80 kilometers from Frankfurt. In addition to the beautiful nature, there are many monasteries and ancient castles in the area.

Climate

The city has a mild climate, it can be described as Mediterranean. Therefore, the weather in Bad Kreuznach is almost always dry and warm, that is, it is ideal for people who come here for wellness treatments and for a relaxing holiday. It is best to come here in the warm season, but the weather will not upset you in winter, as there are no severe frosts in the resort, and fresh air will contribute to a speedy recovery and well-being. And the surrounding forests of the Salinental Valley and the vast territories with vineyards will delight the eye throughout the rest.

Historical reference

Bad Kreuznach became a resort several centuries ago. Ancient settlers appeared here in the 5th century BC, in those days the Celts lived here. In the I century, the city is part of the Roman Empire, later the settlement often passes "from hand to hand" in different countries. Naturally, such a diversity of cultures was reflected in the appearance of the city. Historians claim that the first hospitals appeared in the city during the period of the Roman Empire.

In 1708, the city became an official German resort. And during this period, flowering sets in, hospitals are being rebuilt. Famous people gather at the resort, in 1843 Karl Marx arrives, who even holds a wedding ceremony with Jenny von Westfalen. And in 1917, William II rebuilt his residence in the resort.

Now it is a modern, European-level resort, attracting thousands of tourists annually.

Resort Features

Bad Kreuznach in Germany became famous primarily for its radon adits of natural origin. There are mineral springs and thermal pools. It is in Germany that the largest inhaler in all of Europe is located, where almost all respiratory diseases can be cured simply by inhaling salt fumes.

Sanatorium complex Crucenia Thermen

On the territory of the complex there is a four-star hotel and 3 pools where you can swim in thermal radon water, the temperature of which is always + 33 ° C. The territory also has a medical therapeutic complex. In the sanatorium you can take a course of physiotherapy, cold therapy, a course of massage, wrapping with mud and salts of mineral water.

The main indications for treatment:

  • cleansing the skin of toxins;
  • activation of the immune system;
  • allergic reactions;
  • the need to improve and normalize metabolism;
  • Ankylosing spondylitis;
  • problems with the respiratory and pulmonary systems.

Reviews about Bad Kreuznach and this resort are mostly positive. In particular, admired reviews of the salt grotto and inhalation in the radon adit, after which there is an unprecedented surge of strength.

For too demanding vacationers, individual treatment and recovery courses are provided. The complex is open from May to October.

Wellness complex Baederhaus

The second most popular resort, offering a wide range of health and beauty treatments. The complex was opened in 1912. On its territory there are several thermal pools, there are baths and saunas, as well as a fresh pool with attractions.

Judging by the reviews of Bad Kreuznach, a cosmetic procedure, more precisely, a peeling called “Scheherazade Bath”, offered in this sanatorium, just works wonders. Sanatorium specialists use only the best Bador cosmetics. However, it should be remembered that it will be necessary to order such a service 3 weeks before its implementation and confirm at least 1 hour before the start.

Baederhaus is closed from June to August. The resort has many more clinics and hospitals. For example, Augusta specializes in new diagnostic techniques, postoperative rehabilitation and rheumatology. And in a complex called “Karl Ashhof-Rey Pfalz” you can undergo treatment in the presence of internal diseases. Sanatorium Reingrafenstein specializes in cardiovascular diseases and neurological pathology. You can solve problems of orthopedic and rheumatic nature in the clinic "Paracelsus-Rotenfels".

Absolute contraindications

However, there are a number of diseases in which visiting the resort of Bad Kreuznach is not recommended. No radon procedures are used if a person currently has an exacerbation of a chronic disease. They will not be admitted to the sanatorium in the presence of an infectious disease or the presence of an inflammatory process. You can not visit the resort for cancer or a pathology such as thyroid hyperthyroidism.

Attractions and entertainment

For lovers of outdoor activities and sports, Bad Kreuznach has golf courses and tennis courts. You can take a walk along the flat or mountain hiking trails, or go cycling.

In the central part of the village there is a park in which there is a sports and fitness complex and a fitness center. Casino doors are open for gambling people.

At the end of June, the Roses on a Midsummer Night festival is held annually in the Resort Park, the whole action is accompanied by music, and in the evening, dancing and fireworks are required.

There is a cable park on Mount Kooberg, where you can travel at an altitude of 8 meters, of course, with reliable insurance and under the care of instructors. In the city forest you can master the most ancient skill - archery. Fishing enthusiasts can go fishing on the designated bank of the Nae, the main thing is not to forget to get a fishing permit, which is sold in travel agencies.

In addition to these entertainments, tourists can taste traditional German cuisine in cozy cafes and restaurants. Here, visitors will be told interesting stories about the area and will offer real wine.

Fans of architectural monuments will also find something to “pamper” with their look. The main attraction of Bad Kreuznach is an old stone bridge, which was built back in 1300. Its length is 135 meters. During the Second World War, three flights were destroyed, but with the efforts of local residents, the bridge was completely restored.

On the outskirts of the village, the ancient fortress Kauzenburg, which was built in 1206, has been preserved. Today, there is a restaurant inside, where the decor and menu will completely plunge into the atmosphere of the Middle Ages.

Bad Kreuznach is a magnificent radon resort, where you can improve your health and have a great time at moderate prices.
