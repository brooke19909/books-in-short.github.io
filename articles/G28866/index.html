The best Crete hotels for families with children



Greece attracts many tourists with its beautiful warm sea, sandy beaches and hospitality. She is also famous for her Mediterranean cuisine, which does not leave indifferent any of those who at least once tried it. One of the popular destinations to visit is the island of Crete. Most believe that you should not go to Crete with children, but they are mistaken. Despite the fact that there are few attractions and children's entertainment centers in Crete, this is offset by the excellent sea and well-equipped beaches.

Crete hotels for families with children are represented in large numbers. Both standard hotels and bungalows and houses are offered. On this island, children are treated in a special way, as adults. Despite the absence of a children's menu in many restaurants and cafes, the staff will always find a high chair for your child, surround him with care and serve in the first class.

If you want a first-class vacation, choose five-star hotels in Crete. For

many of them offer babysitting services (at an additional cost), children's entertainment clubs, and even meals for babies. The ranking of hotels in Crete is led by Fodele Beach & Water Park Holiday Resort. In addition to the nearby beach, the hotel provides a lot of entertainment for children: a mini club, 2 freshwater pools, a water park with 7 slides and 6 pools.

Other Crete hotels for families with children offer almost the same services. For example, the five-star Grecotel Amirandes Exclusive Resort, in addition to chic rooms, excellent location and several restaurants, offers to visit the children's cafe Tasty Corner, a kids club with games and a show program. It offers guests free children's drinks (from 11 to 17).

It is worth noting the fact that if your child is not yet a year old, it is better to choose not the hottest months for travel. Refuse the trip in July and August. If the baby is not breast-feeding, you should take his food with you. Older children will like the saltwater aquarium, which is located near Heraklion (admission is free for up to 5 years). Also indelible impressions will leave water parks of Crete. Children from 7 years and older will be interested in many sightseeing routes that will show the life of ordinary people in Crete, will be introduced to ancient crafts.



If you want to find less expensive Crete hotels for families with children, take a look at four- and three-star hotels. Such hotels are characterized by less luxurious apartments and the lack of full board, but for children, almost every hotel provides a babysitter upon request, a children's pool and a mini club. In addition, you can be sure that in any institution of the island you and your child will be surrounded by attention and necessary care, so if you still have doubts about whether to go on vacation with children, feel free to decide.

You can always choose the appropriate options for a comfortable stay. To all tourists offers numerous hotels Crete. Holidays with children - family idyll against the backdrop of the azure sea and sunny Greece.
