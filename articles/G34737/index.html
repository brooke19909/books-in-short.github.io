Where to get a credit card - features, conditions and recommendations

Credit cards are very convenient, because there is no need to accumulate money for the desired purchase for months, constantly take new consumer loans, and you can pay off debt without interest, well, and unlike a large pile of money, it is easily placed in your wallet, and in case of its loss will not come a catastrophe of universal proportions.

This banking product is very popular, and therefore hundreds of banks are ready to provide a huge number of offers and various types of credit cards. We‚Äôll figure out where to get a credit card.



What you should pay attention to?

First of all, it is necessary to carefully consider the terms of lending and use. The most important indicators are: the credit limit, the cost of services, the annual interest rate on the loan and the duration of the grace period, the complexity of registration, the terms of repayment of the debt. To date, the lending rate in many banks ranges from 22 to 35%, and the limit on loans, as a rule, does not exceed 750 000 rubles.

Many people wonder where it is better to get a credit card without inquiries. Let's figure it out together.

The cost of issuing a credit card and its service varies for each bank. And if plastic is usually issued free of charge, then a fee of between 300 and 6,000 rubles per year is charged for its service, based on the card category. You also need to learn more about the availability and duration of the grace period - the time during which you can pay the debt without interest. As a rule, it equals 50-60 days, although exceptions are possible.

Remember that repaying a loan is a regular process, and therefore it should be as convenient as possible. Better when there are many repayment channels. Today, banks provide an opportunity to pay off debt not only in their branches, but also through payment terminals, ATMs, as well as using online services. Access to Internet banking and the presence of SMS alerts also make using a credit card more camouflage.



Below we will tell you where it is better to take a credit card.

Pay attention to the commission for withdrawing cash from a credit card. Credit cards are primarily designed for cashless payments, but they also allow you to withdraw money in cash. But the commission for this procedure can reach up to 7% of the withdrawn amount. Be sure to find out about the presence of partner banks. In their ATMs, a lower commission is charged (as in your bank) compared to third-party ones. Also, these ATMs allow you to repay the loan by depositing cash. The more partners your bank has, the better.

Ultimately, some credit cards have additional features, for example, they provide discounts at certain stores and restaurants, allow you to accumulate bonuses in various airline programs, and sometimes you can even get a small percentage of your purchases, it is also called Cash Vask. If you are interested in such advantages of a credit card, then check all the details with the bank.

So where is the best place to get a credit card? We will talk about this later.

Manual for the recipient of a plastic credit card

  • All loan conditions should be carefully studied: find out the loan rate, the size of the credit limit and the cost of annual servicing.
  • Find out if there is a grace period and its period: the more, the more profitable for you. You also need to clarify whether it applies to cash withdrawals.
  • It is necessary to study the possibilities and conditions for repaying the debt well: to find out about the minimum monthly payment, when it is necessary to make it, how to do it and what penalties for its delay. Study the contract very carefully, because often this information is given in small print or in footnotes.
  • Familiarize yourself with the conditions for withdrawing cash from an ATM, how much the commission is at "native" ATMs and third-party ATMs, and request a list of partner banks. As a rule, it is posted on the website of the card issuing bank. Remember that some banks do not have "their" ATMs and even branches.
  • Carefully read the bonus programs on the card: think carefully about how much you need it. If so, you should choose a card that best suits your nature of expenses and lifestyle.

Not sure which one to choose - Visa or MasterCard? For citizens of the Russian Federation, there are practically no differences between these systems. The main payment currency for Visa is the dollar, and for MasterCard is the euro.
