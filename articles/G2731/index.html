Additional Information

Before the trip, it is important to familiarize yourself with the information related to the organization of the resort. Speaking about this hotel, it is worth noting the following:

  • Registration and check-in of newly arrived guests begins after 14:00, and check-out is necessary on the day of departure before noon;
  • hotel rules do not provide for extra beds in rooms;
  • pets are not allowed in the hotel;
  • You can pay for the services provided to you in non-cash form;
  • a prerequisite is the provision of a credit card, the number of which was indicated when booking the tour;
  • if the credit card does not belong to you, but to a third party, you must provide the written permission of the holder to use it;
  • the guest is required to present a valid identification card with a current photo;
  • the hotel does not guarantee the fulfillment of special requests indicated upon booking (this may also require an additional payment);
  • persons under the age of 18 are not provided with alcohol;
  • Using the privileges of the Ultra All Inclusive system is available only to hotel guests (guests invited by you do not have this right);
  • the beach, swimming pools, catering establishments, as well as animators work in accordance with weather conditions.

Negative reviews

It is worth highlighting a number of shortcomings that characterize the hotel Limak Lara De Luxe Hotel Resort (Turkey). Reviews focus on the following points:

  • the menu of the Turkish restaurant is quite modest, and the dishes taste too fresh, which is not typical for oriental cuisine;
  • restaurants and bars primarily serve Germans (since this hotel is focused on them), and the rest of the guests have to wait a long time for their order;
  • approximately 20 minutes before the end of the time allotted for food, waiters begin to clean the tables, hinting to guests that it is time to leave;
  • inflated prices for procedures in the SPA salon and massage room;
  • too cold water in the pools;
  • gala dinners are not held if the hotel is less than half full;
  • in the "low season" the range of free drinks is significantly reduced;
  • little active entertainment for young people;
  • despite the fact that more than 2 people are accommodated in the family room, there will be only 2 bath and cosmetic sets (the problem is solved after contacting the reception);




  • wireless Internet is intermittent;
  • quite often maids carry out cleaning early in the morning, when guests still want to sleep;
  • the carpet in the room and other rooms is very dirty;
  • no dust in the lobby area;
  • the restaurant menu has few fruits and vegetables, which is not typical for Turkey;
  • substandard instant coffee in vending machines;
  • maids periodically forget about the need to update a cosmetic kit;
  • on the one hand, the work of the control service is a positive thing, but questions about the quality of services are annoying after a few days;
