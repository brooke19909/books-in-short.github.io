Connecting two computers to the Internet

Imagine a situation: you purchased a second computer, and you want the Internet to be on it, just like on the first. Here is the question: how to do this? In order to connect two computers to the Internet, there are several options, the most common of which is, perhaps, setting up the Internet using a router.

All that is needed is a cable to the computer, a router, and a little time. It is assumed that the Internet is already configured and working on the first computer, so we will not consider its configuration. Connect the cable from the ISP to the router and one more cable to each computer. Now configure the router. When you purchase services from an Internet provider related to opening the Internet for you, you received data that should have been saved. It's time to get them and read them again.

So, open the Internet browser and enter the following address in the address bar: 192.168.1.1 is a static ip address - it is assigned to a personal computer by default. In the window that opens, we write the username and password, they are the same - admin (remember! The register matters).

Recently, another method of setting up the Internet has become widespread - via wifi. This is the most technologically advanced and convenient way, because computers can be moved, and they are not limited by cable.
