A little bit about the resort ...

As we already said, Hotel Odyssee Resort 4 * is located in the town of Zarzis. It is the least known among other Tunisian resorts to Russian tourists. Zarzis cuisine is considered one of the best in the country. The city is located in the southeast of the country. It is a port of the Mediterranean. Tourist trips to these places have become less popular nowadays due to the proximity of Libya.

Zarzis boasts a long history; references to it date back to the period of the Roman Empire. In those days, it was already incredibly popular because of its advantageous location. Trade was actively developing here, the city was a rather large port of Tunisia.





However, from those times and past glories there were not even traces left. Now Zarzis is a small seaside town. Its local residents live due to the fact that they grow olives, and then they make oil from them. As for hotels, here you will not find fashionable complexes. All hotels are 3-4 star. Local beaches are always half empty, you will not see crowds of tourists on them, it is comfortable and calm to relax in these places.

Due to the fact that Zarzis is the southern region of Tunisia, it is also the warmest. In autumn, hot weather lasts much longer. Due to the fact that the desert is located very close, there is almost no rain in the city even in winter.

Local Attractions

Relaxing in the Odyssee Resort 4 * (Tunisia, Djerba, Zarzis), you can briefly distract from the beautiful sea and devote time to excursions to local attractions. The town of Zarzis itself is very ordinary, and there is nothing interesting in it. However, those who first came to Tunisia, it will probably be interesting to walk along its streets and see the life of local residents.

In the town you can admire the Jewish synagogue, a mosque with minarets, a city museum.

If you have long wanted to visit the desert, then Zarzis is an ideal place for this. Local travel agencies vying offer guests interesting tours:

  • Jeep and ATV riding in the desert.
  • Inspection of the settlements of the Barbary nomads. Vacationers can see how such people live, try their dishes.
  • Sightseeing trips to the Sahara (it is supposed to spend the night in the open air and exciting camel riding).
  • Matmata is an exotic troglodyte house.
  • "Country of Ksars" - ancient landscapes.
  • Gabes is a small coastal town famous for the spice market.
  • Paintball club.
  • Thalassotherapy.
  • On Djerba you can see the sights and visit entertainment venues.

Odyssee Resort 4 *: reviews
