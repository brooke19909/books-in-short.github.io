How to get a duplicate TIN for an individual: documents and procedure

How to get a duplicate TIN to an individual? A question of a similar plan arises among many citizens. The thing is that a certificate with TIN data is not a mandatory documentation. And draw it up under certain circumstances. The form of the established form can be lost. In addition, it may become worthless or stolen. All this makes us think about restoring the evidence. We will talk further about how to cope with such an operation. With proper preparation, achieving the desired result is easier than it seems. And even the student will be able to re-issue the certificate of the established form.

Description of the document

Certificate TIN - a certificate of the established form issued by individuals. It is issued at the request of a citizen.

The certificate contains information about the taxpayer, as well as his unique individual number in the tax system. Such a combination never changes; it remains with a person throughout his life.

The TIN certificate confirms the tax registration of a person in the Federal Tax Service. Using this paper, a citizen will be able to open / close the IP, as well as confirm his TIN if necessary. For example, if you need to pay taxes or check them.

Reissue option

How to get a duplicate TIN to an individual? Is this real or not? The answer is pretty easy.

In Russia, you can re-issue or restore almost any document. And a certificate of registration with the tax service as well. Under such circumstances, a person will receive an established form marked "duplicate".

There are various ways to recover a TIN certificate. And not everyone knows how to use them. Some of the techniques suggested below are available only under certain conditions. So, you will have to prepare for operations in advance.

Recovery Methods

How to get a duplicate TIN to an individual? The answer directly depends on what kind of alignment the citizen has chosen. In Russia, as has already been said, certificates of registration with the Federal Tax Service are issued in different ways.

At the moment, you can cope with the task:

  • by contacting your local tax authority;
  • using the services of the MFC and the services of "one window";
  • Having ordered service through "State services";
  • through the website of the Federal Tax Service of the Russian Federation.

The last 2 receptions require thorough preparation. To work with "State services" you must first register, and then - confirm the account. If you want to order a document through the website of the Federal Tax Service, you will have to register in the "Personal Account of the taxpayer."

Personal Instruction

Where to get a duplicate TIN? More answer to a similar question will not cause any difficulties. And what to do in this or that case?

To begin, consider the simplest scenario. This is an independent appeal to the authorized services. The instruction presented for attention can be considered universal. It is suitable for the MFC, the Federal Tax Service, and the "one window" services.

Instructions for ordering a duplicate TIN in Russia are as follows:

  1. Prepare a specific set of documents. It is not too extensive, but sometimes people experience some difficulties at a similar stage.
  2. Write and fill out an application for issuing a duplicate TIN. The form of the established form can be downloaded on the website of the Federal Tax Service, State Service or taken at any authorized body.
  3. Make payment for services on re-issuing a tax registration certificate. Document recovery is not expensive. But the initial receipt of the certificate is not subject to duties.
  4. Contact the authorized body with a request of the established form.
  5. Pick up a ready certificate of tax registration at the appointed time.

That doesn't sound too confusing. But is it so easy to restore a tax registration certificate? Or is everything simple in theory, but not in practice? Yes, if you prepare for the operation in advance, you can achieve the desired result as soon as possible.

Government services and orders

How to get a duplicate TIN to an individual? The second trick is working with the State Services service. With its help, the population can receive electronic services without leaving home. The main thing, as already mentioned, is to have a verified account at ESIA.

In order to get a duplicate TIN on the State Services, the user has to perform the following actions:

  1. Open Government Services and log in to your account.
  2. Expand the catalog of available options, and then find the "Tax Registration" command there.
  3. Select the appropriate option, and then expand the "Lost TIN? What to do?" Section.
  4. Click on the "Order duplicate" hyperlink. You can immediately find the appropriate command in the directory of available services using the search bar.
  5. Fill out the application form electronically. Some information about the person will be automatically copied to the block from the "My Account".
  6. Choose a place to apply for documentation. You have to choose in a special line of the Federal Tax Service according to the registration of a citizen.
  7. Send a request for processing, while paying for the services of producing a duplicate of a certificate of registration with the tax authorities.

At this stage, you can finish the action. All that remains now is to wait for the document to be prepared (the user will be sent an invitation to the Federal Tax Service), and then pick it up at the authorized bodies. Nothing incomprehensible, supernatural or difficult.

Important: problems with such a technique can arise only for new users of "State services". They, as already mentioned, will first have to confirm the account. It takes a similar service for about two weeks.

"Personal account of the taxpayer" and registration

How to get a duplicate TIN to an individual? A person can choose one of the proposed options for the development of events. It is best to personally contact the authorized bodies. This technique is less of a hassle.

You can order a certificate of a standard form through the website of the Federal Tax Service of the Russian Federation. To do this, the user must have registered "Personal Account of the taxpayer."

After that, you will have to act something like this:

  1. Go to nalog.ru and enter your personal account.
  2. Find the item "Tax registration" in the "Services" section.
  3. Select the appropriate operation.
  4. Fill out the application and pay for the service.
  5. Submit a request for processing.

It remains only to take the finished certificate from the authorized bodies. To order a duplicate TIN in a similar way, according to reviews, is problematic. And so few resort to this alignment.

Service cost

The state duty for a duplicate TIN must be paid without fail. The initial issuance of a certificate of the established form does not provide for any payments. But how much to pay?

The state duty for re-issuing a tax registration certificate is not too high. She is 300 rubles. It is recommended to deposit money into the state treasury before applying for restoration of paper. This technique will significantly save the applicant‚Äôs time.

Service Documents

Need to order a duplicate TIN? This can only be done if certain documents are available. Without them, the desired result cannot be achieved.

Ideally, at the time of applying to the Federal Tax Service, a person should have the following papers:

  • identification;
  • statement;
  • check on payment of state duty.

Additionally, they may come in handy:

  • damaged certificate with tax number;
  • certificates from the Ministry of Internal Affairs about the loss of the document or its theft.

If there is a reissue of the TIN for the child, it is additionally recommended to bring the passports of legal representatives.
