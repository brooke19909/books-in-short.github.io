Ski resorts in Slovakia: prices, description and photo

At the present stage, ski resorts located on the territory of Slovakia have become very popular. This was facilitated by excellent environmental conditions, relatively low prices and infrastructure, which is developing quite rapidly. The most famous ski resorts in Slovakia are Jasna Chopok, Donovaly, Skalka, Liptov, High Tatras, Rachkov, Lgota, etc.





What the resort of Strebske Pleso is famous for

The High Tatras are famous for the presence of the National Park, which has about 300 peaks and mountain lakes. Approximately 26 peaks in height reach 2.5 kilometers. The most famous ski resorts in Slovakia that are located in this territory are Podbanske, Smokovec, Tatranska Lomnica, Strebske Pleso. All of them are characterized by the presence of a variety of routes and low cost.

It takes about 30 minutes to get to the resort of Strebske Pleso from the airport. This place is the most popular among numerous athletes. In addition, it is here that winter sports are usually held. This is the best alpine resort located in the High Tatras. It is located on the picturesque shore of a mountain lake, which bears the exact same name. About 120 days a year the resort operates as a ski center. In the role of the medical resort of Shtrebske Pleso, it is in the summer and in the off-season.

Technological component

Speaking about the ski resorts of Slovakia, it should be noted that Strebske Pleso is characterized by a well-equipped, modern ski station, which includes several ski jumps, ski tracks and stadiums that can accommodate spectators with amenities. Ski slopes are located very close by , on which both professionals and beginners can test their strengths. They are equipped with two chairlifts, three towbars and one children's lifts. If necessary, the tracks are treated with snow cannons. In addition, the resort is well lit in the evening.

The slalom descent called Interski is very popular. It is here that a children's ski school is located, equipped with special lifts. In addition, on the slope there are excellent conditions for those who wish to go cross-country skiing, for biathlon and for those who are not averse to jumping on ski jumps and try their hand at mountain climbing. The price for accommodation in a double room is from 126 euros per day. Breakfast is included. Price for hire - from 15 euros. Here is such a wonderful place Slovakia, the Tatras.

Ski resort Tatranska Lomnica





To get to this resort from the airport, you need to spend about 20 minutes. This place is a kind of starting point for most tourist routes. As a ski center in this resort is a small village called Skalnate Pleso. It is located at the foot of the Lomnicky Shtit. To get to this village, you can use the cable car, which starts from the hotel called "Morava". From the village on Lomnicka, the Saddle can be reached by means of a double chair lift. The resort is divided into areas - Skalnate Pleso and Pits. The first area is suitable for professionals, the second - for beginners.

Resort Podbanske

What other skiing trails does Slovakia have? Ski resorts, reviews of which are good, are numerous. And one of the country's most popular ski centers located in the High Tatras is Podbanska. This resort is located at an altitude of 940 meters. Judging by numerous reviews, the nature of the National Park leaves a huge impression. It is from this ski center that a magnificent view opens up Mount Krivan. And it is worth noting that this mountain is a symbol of the High Tatras.

Trails are for both beginners and professional skiers. They are located at altitudes from 950 to 1100 meters. These slopes are serviced by several lifts, the length of which is 350 and 550 meters. In addition, the resort has well-equipped slopes for those who prefer snowboarding or sledding. In addition, there is the opportunity to go cross-country skiing.

Oldest ski center

What other amazing places can one of the ski areas that Slovakia is rich in the High Tatras boast? A ski resort called Smokovec simply cannot be ignored in this review. From the airport it can be reached in just 15 minutes. This ski center is the oldest resort in the country. It combines such places as Dolny, Gorny, Old and New Smokovtsi. The resort is famous for its cozy guest houses and traditional Slovak style hotels, which are built directly in the mountains. The resort area has all the necessary conditions for mountaineering, ice skating, skiing and sledding. The most popular track for sledding, the length of which exceeds two kilometers, is considered a place located in Old Smokovec. It is here that every Wednesday night skating and various competitions take place. What should be noted when speaking about the ski resorts of Slovakia? Prices, reviews about Smokovec can leave only a pleasant impression. The cost of one day of living - from 100 euros per adult.

If you belong to the category of beginner skiers, then specifically for you there are several tracks of medium difficulty. Your first steps are best done in Novy Smokovec. This is where the track called Jalubkova Luka is located. Next to it is the Hotel Park. This route is characterized by a gentle slope, the length of which reaches 860 meters. Served by special tow lifts, guns. Lighting turns on at 18.00, and turns off at 21.00.

More professional athletes can choose a steep slope located in Gorny Smokovec as a warm-up track. Here is a hotel called Bellevue. The descent is serviced by rope tows.

The main center of the resort





The main ski center in Stary Smokovec is Grebienok. In 9 minutes you can use the cable car to climb to a height of 1263 meters.

For beginners, simple slopes are suitable, the length of which does not exceed 370 meters. Experienced athletes will be able to climb to a height equal to 1,500 meters. In addition, professionals can try their hand at the "black" slalom track called Homa Luka. The comb is equipped with drags and cannons. There is also a special ski lift for children, the length of which reaches 70 meters. In addition, the ski center has a special children's school.

Ski resort "Yasna"

Slovakia, in its presence, has such an area that is considered the most environmentally friendly in comparison with other places in Europe. It is about the Low Tatras. The territory of this region is completely a national park. Regardless of the fact that the Low Tatras do not have too high mountains, the tracks are always full of snow. The total length of the resorts exceeds 20 kilometers.





It is here that one of the most popular resorts is located - Yasna. Judging by the many reviews, this place is the best ski center.

In order to get to the ski center "Yasny" from the airport, it will take about 1 hour 15 minutes. The resort is located next to Chopok Mountain in a national park. The best conditions for skiing are offered by the Jasna ski resort.

Slovakia, the reviews demonstrate this very well, it belongs to those international tourist centers that are characterized by a high category. And in many respects this success was achieved thanks to the resort "Yasna". In this place every year major alpine skiing competitions are held, which are included in the calendar of the European Cup.

Technological component

The resort's tracks are equipped with 13 lifts, which have a total capacity of 8 thousand people per hour. The ski center, in its composition, has about 21 kilometers of highways, which roll away every day. The degrees of difficulty can be very different. The most popular trails are equipped with snow cannons, which allows skiers to conquer the peaks even in the smallest winters.

The ski center Jasna has gained popularity among snowboarders, as it is here that all the necessary conditions for such sports as freeride, jump, jibbing are presented. The cost of one day of living - from 237 euros per adult.

Conclusion
