Hardware stuffing





Inside the device you can find a processor with 4 cores. This is a chip from Media Tek. One should not rely especially on the processor. Yes, it provides good performance, but still for the most demanding games, its power will not be enough. Just recall that even the manufacturer himself talked about the main purpose of the presence of this chip. If someone forgot or simply did not know, such a processor was installed so that the owner of the phone could work with fourth-generation cellular communications. It is also often called LTE.
