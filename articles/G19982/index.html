Service "Trust payment" ("MegaFon"). Connection methods and terms of use

Why do you need the service "Trust payment"? MegaFon takes care of its subscribers, tries to provide communication in any situations. It happens that you are talking on a cell phone about something very important, and you run out of money. Or another situation: you are late somewhere, you need to call home, warn that they do not worry, and they say to you: "Not enough money." Nearby there is neither a terminal nor a mobile payment point where you can top up your account. In this case, this service will come in handy.

This SMS indicates the code that must be dialed in order to use this service.

MegaFon provides many ways to activate a trust payment. You can dial * 138 # 1 or send SMS with the number 1 to 5138, thus connecting the service. The service can be provided both for free and for money. It all depends on the amount that you spent over the past three months on communication, and the duration of use of the current phone number. If you registered as a subscriber more than four months ago and made an amount of more than 600 rubles, MegaFon will activate the trust payment for free. The credit limit is calculated from the average monthly cost of communication.





For a paid connection, you need to come to the salon with a passport or use the code number. The manager in the salon will tell you how to take the promised payment. MegaFon provides several options for a Trust Loan. When choosing a package using SMS or number * 138 # 1, you need to take into account that the selected amount will be debited from your account immediately, so that when you need it, you can use the service. The size of the loan can be from 75 to 1500 rubles. When the amount in the account reaches the preferential cut-off threshold, the subscriber will not be able to use the phone, he will be blocked. In order to clarify the threshold, you need to dial the code 138 # 3.





MegaFon provides bonuses for choosing a paid connection to the Trusted Payment service. According to the MegaFon-Bonus program, three months after connecting to the Trust Loan, you begin to receive bonuses - points, due to which you can use free calls within the network, send SMS or use a certain amount of Internet traffic. Bonus points are awarded during the year until the entire amount spent on a trust payment is covered. You can connect to the program by calling 0510.

If you want to stop using the "Credit of Confidence", you need to dial the code number * 138 * 2 # or contact the manager in the communication salon. The rest of the unused amount is returned to your account, bonus points in this case are not awarded.

A "credit of trust" is connected only to individuals, corporate clients and subscribers with several telephone numbers cannot use it.
