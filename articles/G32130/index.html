Zener diode TL431: wiring diagram

TL431 is an integrated zener diode. In the circuit, it plays the role of a reference voltage source. The presented element is used, as a rule, in power supplies. The device at the zener diode is quite simple. In total, the model uses three outputs. Depending on the modification, up to ten transistors can be located in the case. A distinctive feature of the TL431 is its good thermal stability.

2.48 V switching circuit

At the zener diode TL431, the switching circuit for 2.48 V has a single-stage converter. On average, the operating current in the system reaches a level of 5.3 A. Resistors for signal transmission can be used with different voltage conductivity. The accuracy of stabilization in these devices varies around 2%.

To increase the sensitivity of the zener diode, various modulators are used. As a rule, it is precisely the dipole type that is selected. On average, their capacitance is not more than 3 pF. However, in this case, much depends on the current conductivity. To reduce the risk of overheating of the elements, expanders are used. Zener diodes are connected through the cathode.
