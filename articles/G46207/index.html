What is the city of Tosno of the Leningrad region known for

The city of Tosno, Leningrad Region is open to tourists from all over the world. It is a nice town with its own atmosphere and friendly people.

History

According to one source, this place was the army of Alexander Yaroslavich before the Neva battle in 1240, his camp was located there. He got his famous nickname after this battle, as you know. Scientists are sure that the city of Tosno in the Leningrad Region was first noted in the long chronicles of the 15th century. Varyazhsky way was at the intersection of this place. And the Tosno waters for merchants were a kind of lighthouse during the transportation of their goods, so as not to get lost.





Tosnenskaya Sloboda had a railway station, near which they began to build up the village. Rich people came here for outdoor recreation. Country houses, which are still preserved, are now considered cultural monuments. Photos of the city of Tosno, Leningrad Region with these houses can still be seen on postcards. In the XX century, Tosno officially received the status of a city and began to be built up.

Canyon

Sablinsky caves are the main man-made tourist sites. They appeared in the late XIX - early XX century. Quartz sand has long been mined in this place. Tourists from all over the country come for excursions to the four main parts of the cave: Pants, Trash, Rope, Pearl.





The canyon near the river gives a lot of fossils: Cambrian clay, marcasite with pyrite, considered gold for fools. Tolstoy even chose this place to indicate the Country of Fools, since there were quite a lot of pyrite at that time. But it is not completely known whether the writer lived here, since after the war there was practically no information about him.

Small waterfall

In Tosno there is a waterfall in Sablino, it is considered the largest in Europe. It is noted that the waterfall changes its pressure and can reach from 2 to 4 meters. Despite its size, the waterfall was called mini-Niagara, for its magnificent appearance. When the time comes for floods, it is better not to get close to the waterfall.

Visiting this place is allowed independently, without additional excursions. People take wellness baths in this place, so everyone can try the power of the Tosno waterfall on themselves. This place was also chosen by the inhabitants of nearby villages.

Temple in the village of Lisino-Korpus

About a hundred years ago, the Tosno church was expanded according to the plan of the architect Nikolai Nikitich Nikonov from the diocese. Part of this church is the Transfiguration Church, the Tosnensky orphanage, as well as three chapels: all located in a small village, it is considered a cultural monument.





During the Second World War, the Germans occupied the city, at this time the temple ceased to function in 1943. After the war and the liberation, the building was rebuilt and made into a district House of Culture. But manuscripts, spiritual estates are still preserved.

Farm Maryino

The Maryino farm, which is located in the village of Primernoye, has been operating as a mini-zoo for 8 years. The peculiarity is that animals do not huddle in cells, they are kept in enclosures, which provides them with decent living conditions. There are also winter aviaries for each beast.





This is a kind of pet farm, where there are no exotic species of birds or horses. Here, decorative species of rabbits, ducks, goats live beautifully. Tourists are allowed to bring pet food with them, pet them, the animals are quite friendly and are used to attention. The farm has its own fair, offering natural dairy products from Yakut cows and goats, children are allowed to ride a horse, donkey or camel.

How to get there

The city of Tosno in the Leningrad Region borders on the highway St. Petersburg - Moscow. There is a convenient transport interchange, from the local railway station you can get to St. Petersburg, Gatchina, Veliky Novgorod, Kirishi, Volkhov, Kolpino, Chudovo, Tver, the capital.

City transport is no different from other cities, you can get to the center by bus or minibus. The streets of the city of Tosno, Leningrad Region are located not far from each other, so the path from one end to the other will be short-lived.

There is a large bus station in Tosno, transport regularly goes to Kolpino, Pavlovsk, Novolisino, Lyuban, Fedorovskoye, Pushkin, Rublevo, Otradnoe, St. Petersburg and back.

What tourists discover in Tosno

In this wonderful place there are many memorable places, memorial complexes. Recently opened a tour desk that organizes hunting tours.

Not far from the city of Tosno, Leningrad Region, there is an amazing village of Lisino-corpus. It is notable for its structures of the middle of the XIX century, which include:

  • forestry school;
  • stone church;
  • green pavilion.

Famous scientists were born here, hundreds of poems and songs, ideas for movies were invented.





Walking through the streets of the town, you will notice old buildings that have not been reconstructed, the city administration has specially preserved them for tourists to visit and to feel the old atmosphere of the past.
