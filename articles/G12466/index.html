Sunny Spain. Alicante is waiting for you!

Diverse and welcoming for tourists from all over the world is the warm country of Spain. Alicante is one of its resort cities, which is located on the Costa Blanca, under the waters of the Mediterranean Sea. Located in the historical region of Valencia, this cozy town still retains the charm of the fishing village that it used to be. Today, however, like the whole of coastal Spain, Alicante is a large trading port, which is engaged in the export of seafood and fish.

Alicante is a resort where you can quickly and directly from Moscow. There are charter flights that take you to the sea in a few hours. The airport in Alicante is 9 km from the city, and you can get to its center by minibus, bus or taxi. Here you can also rent a private car and travel on it during the whole vacation. Alicante is a museum city in which you will find ancient Greek ruins, and buildings of the Middle Ages (Romanesque architecture, Gothic), and Moorish modest cathedrals. A striking example of local Gothic architecture is the Basilica of Santa Maria, which was rebuilt in the 14th century. Later it was supplemented by Baroque-style architectural elements, so you can admire this creation forever. Not far from it is the Museum of La Asegurada, which mainly presents the works of masters of the XX century. Among them are Julio Gonzalez, Juan Gris, Eusebio Semper - people who are represented in the cultural arena on the world stage Spain.
