Jomtien Holiday Hotel 3 - a welcoming hotel in Thailand

Description: The cozy and welcoming Jomtien Holiday Hotel 3 was built in nineteen ninety-nine. It is located in the south of Jomtien, located in the resort town of Pattaya. The hotel staff perfectly takes care of their customers who have chosen amazing Thailand for holidays or vacations. The hotel is one hundred and fifty kilometers from the international airport of Bangkok. Near the hotel, travelers will find a considerable number of shopping centers and nightlife.



Rooms: The ten-story Jomtien Holiday Hotel 3 has two hundred and forty-six rooms to stay. The rooms are exquisitely decorated and very pleasant to look at. Spacious rooms have all the amenities needed for an unforgettable vacation - these are modern soft beds, air conditioning, satellite TV with Russian and music channels, a large safe, telephone, free minibar with an extensive selection of soft drinks, and a balcony with furniture. The guest bathroom has a toilet, shower, washbasin, hairdryer and hygiene items. Customers are also given bathrobes and slippers. Upon request, a cot is brought to the room. Thorough cleaning and linen changes are made every day. Room service is available for a fee.

Meals: There is no better way to start the day than with a mouth-watering breakfast provided free of charge by staff. At the Jomtien Holiday Hotel 3, guests will find a cozy restaurant, where caring waiters serve delicious and high-quality dishes of local as well as international cuisine. All of them are diligently prepared by highly qualified chefs. Light snacks, cocktails, alcoholic, cold and other drinks can be easily ordered at the bar.

Beach: A wonderful sandy beach, called Jomtien Beach, is only four hundred meters from the hotel. Sun loungers, umbrellas, mattresses and towels are provided for everyone. On the beach, guests of the Jomtien Holiday Hotel 3 have the opportunity to either simply sunbathe or swim in the clear sea, or learn diving and other sports.



Holiday Information: All residents of Jomtien Holiday are entitled to visit outdoor pool, jacuzzi, fitness center, as well as a conference room designed for business negotiations. Any tourist has the opportunity to rent a car, park it, gain access to high-speed Internet, and exchange currency. Upon request, all clients are offered the services of a doctor, a nanny, dry cleaning, laundry and ironing. There are many souvenir shops, shopping centers and nightclubs nearby.



Reviews: Hospitable Jomtien Holiday Hotel 3, reviews of which travelers leave extremely positive, is able to satisfy many of the needs of its customers. Travelers with enthusiasm recall the calm atmosphere prevailing in spacious, tidy hotel rooms, polite staff, the convenient location of the hotel and affordable prices for various services and goods.
