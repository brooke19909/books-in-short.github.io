Sochi - hotel rating: living and service conditions

Sochi is a favorite vacation spot not only for domestic tourists. This is an international resort. After all, there is everything that a person needs for an excellent holiday. The unique climate, sea, air, mountains, combined with comfortable conditions of stay and European service attract in Sochi a huge number of vacationers.

In order for the rest to leave only pleasant impressions, it is necessary to carefully study the rating of hotels for Sochi . Below is a list of hotels in which the living conditions and services are at the highest level. Rating of the best hotels in Sochi:

  1. Golden Tulip Rosa Khutor.
  2. Apart-hotel "Coastal quarter".
  3. Hotel "Gorky Gorod".
  4. Grand Hotel "Pearl".
  5. Hotel "Actor".
  6. Hotel "Chebotarev".

Below is a detailed description of each hotel, starting with the first in the rating.

Golden Tulip Rosa Khutor

One of the most attractive hotels in Sochi is the newest Golden Tulip Rosa Khutor. It is located in Krasnaya Polyana, within walking distance of the Olympic ski lifts. This is a good choice for anyone interested in snowboarding and skiing. The hotel has the most modern sports equipment, as well as five conference rooms for business negotiations. Sochi International Airport is 45 km away. The beach is 47 km away. The historic center is 70 km away.

Food and living conditions

Hotel guests are offered one or two meals a day in the form of a buffet. Each room has beds with orthopedic mattress and non-allergenic linen, as well as a table, wardrobe, air conditioning, televisions, minibar, telephones, Caucasian mineral water, tea, free Wi-Fi. Children traveling with children can be provided with a cradle upon request. Of the additional accessories available hairdryer, bathrobe, slippers.

Number of rooms

The Golden Tulip Rosa Khutor Hotel has 162 rooms of different levels of comfort.
Standard includes climate control, a double or two single beds, wired Internet and Wi-Fi, a shower, a telephone, a flat-screen TV, a minibar, a tea set and a safe for storing documents and valuables.
Superior rooms are larger and have a bathtub.

Junior Suite - double room. The living room is divided into a working, dining area and a place to relax. The bedroom has a large double bed. The rooms have two LCD TVs.

Suite - two-room suite. The large living room is furnished with comfortable upholstered furniture. The dining area has a table for eight people and a bar. There is access to a balcony.

Executive Suite - a suite on the sixth floor with panoramic windows overlooking the central square, mountains and the Mzymta River. It has its own kitchen with electric stove, fridge and sink.

According to many, this is the best place to stay in Sochi. The rating of hotels puts him in first place.

Hotel infrastructure

On the territory there is:

  • sports equipment rental;
  • paid car parking with security;
  • sauna;
  • fitness center and gym;
  • ski storage;
  • BRANCHE Restaurant & Bar and a lobby bar.

Apart-hotel "Gorki Gorod"

Sea, sun, exotic plants and fruits - all this is Sochi. The rating of hotels is continued by Gorki Gorod, an international hotel located in Krasnaya Polyana. A kilometer away is the ski slope Laura.

Residents of the hotel can visit the water park, which is located on site. The hotel has a cozy restaurant. It is possible to book wellness treatments at the local spa.

Infrastructure and number of rooms

On the territory of the hotel there is a beauty salon, a sauna, a shop, a left-luggage office, secure parking and a hairdresser. The apartments of the hotel consist of a spacious hotel, bedroom, bathroom and equipped kitchen. The room can have from 1 to 5 bedrooms. A safety deposit box is available. Free internet is provided in each room. The bathroom contains all the necessary toiletries

Grand Hotel Pearl

The four-star Zhemchuzhina complex (Sochi) combines classic design and modern comfort. It provides rooms with views of the Caucasus Mountains and the Black Sea coast. Sochi city center is only five kilometers away. The international airport can be reached in 25 minutes. Hotel "Pearl" Sochi was renovated in 2007. Vacationers can visit interesting sights of the city. Nearby are the Botanical Garden, the garden with the Friendship Tree Museum, the Riviera Park, and the beach with a bar.

Sanatorium "Actor"

Even in Soviet times, this famous Sochi sanatorium was chosen by movie stars. Hotel Actor is favorably located on a hill in the middle of the arboretum, near the sea with beach areas. The city center with attractions and entertainment can be reached in 10-15 minutes. The train station is seven kilometers away, the airport is 23 kilometers away. In walking distance - public transport and taxis.

Treatment profiles

In the sanatorium, the prevention and treatment of various diseases is carried out. These are diseases of the cardiovascular and nervous systems, musculoskeletal system, respiratory tract, skin, and some occupational injuries.

Experienced doctors provide initial and final appointments, and in addition, constant monitoring of the condition throughout the stay in the institution. It is possible to consult with specialists: gynecologist, dentist, neurologist, otolaryngologist. And also to conduct a series of examinations: phonocardiography, bicycle ergometry, ECG, rheovasography, rheoencephalography.

Balneotherapy, massages, healing showers, ultrasonic inhalations, naphthalanotherapy are carried out. There is an opportunity to choose diet food in accordance with medical indications. After the appointment, doctors are prescribed physiotherapy, laser therapy, physical exercises individually and in a group, magnetotherapy, underwater traction of the spine, thalassotherapy, heliotherapy, aerotherapy, climatotherapy.

The territory of the sanatorium is huge - more than five hectares in the park area, which offers long walks. Ecologically clean air of the reserve, saturated with oxygen, has a beneficial effect on the body. For active guests there is also a gym and a pool with warm sea water.

Hotel Description Chebotarev Hotel 4 *

Hotel "Chebotarev" - a modern hotel complex in Sochi. It is located near the city center, the Black Sea coast, comfortable beaches and the Riviera Park. All rooms have great city and sea views. Cypress and palm trees grow on site.

"Chebotarev Hotel" offers apartments for every taste. There are rooms for honeymooners, families with children, non-smokers, rooms with soundproofing. All are equipped with air conditioning, televisions, telephones, safes and a paid minibar. There is heating and access to the balcony. The bathroom is equipped with a washbasin, bathtub and shower. Toiletries, a hairdryer and towels are provided.

At the guests' disposal are various services provided by the polite hotel staff. Among them: housekeeping with a change of bed linen, washing and ironing, shoe shine, room service.

Different types of food are provided: ready-made lunches in packaging and a buffet. You can visit the restaurant or bar. For business negotiations, conference rooms with fax, high-speed wired and wireless Internet are equipped.

At the reception, working around the clock, you can order tickets, transfer to the airport or an excursion program to interesting places of the city. Secure car parking and luggage storage are provided.

The Chebotarev Hotel cooperates with the Sochi sanatorium, which enables its guests to use its medical services. The complex has an outdoor pool, sauna, massage parlor. From entertainment - table tennis and billiards.
The staff speaks several foreign languages. There are specially equipped smoking areas. For people with disabilities, ramps and amenities are equipped.
