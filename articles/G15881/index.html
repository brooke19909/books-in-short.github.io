Ease of use

It is impossible to say that the phone ideally lies in the hand, because for such a "landing" you need a rather big palm. But in any case, the phone is convenient to use. How is it shown?

  • Buttons. Just below the power key is the volume rocker. Buttons made of different materials not only give the device a special charm, but also help not to be mistaken when using them. And due to the fact that all the keys protrude slightly above the body, they are easy to blindly detect.
  • Connectors Since the phone is not separable, a SIM card and an additional memory card can be inserted from the opposite side of the buttons. The special connector is covered with hard plastic and is almost invisible. As for the other controls, they are located on top of the device, from the end. There you can find a standard 3.5 mm audio jack, micro HDMI and micro USB.
  • Forms. The back of the device has a protrusion on which a speaker, a noise-canceling microphone and a camera are located, but it's not even about the functionality.   • Feel. The entire side surface is made of Soft-touch plastic, which means that the device will not slip even in a wet hand. The Kevlar part of the smartphone feels like a mixture of rubber and soft-touch, so the phone is tactilely pleasant.
  • Purity. The smartphone on each side has a special coating, which is a perfect protection against dirt, without collecting fingerprints and dust.

This is not to say that the display is delightful. This is an average PenTile matrix with a diagonal of 4.3 inches. As a budget device, the smartphone has something to show.

As mentioned earlier, the screen is covered with tempered glass Gorilla Glass, which protects it from cracks and scratches. Moreover, such a coating perfectly repels dirt and grease. Sound

In addition to the fact that the battery characteristics allow you to listen to music on the Motorola RAZR MAXX XT910 for a long time, the sound system also makes it possible to do this efficiently.

One of the two built-in audio players has a special design that is able to pull up cover photos and group images from the Web. He is able to download texts and some information about the track. He also quickly and easily works with the radio. You can control it through a locked screen.

Despite the fact that the device has a mono speaker, its sound is quite clear and loud. A ringtone or just music will be heard both in a quiet house and in a noisy subway. In the settings, you can not only call the track you like, but also adjust its sound both in volume and in the bass component, and so on.

Memory

To understand why everyone is so paying attention to the amount of RAM, it is worth remembering one simple fact. Which one? In fact, this is a special storage, fast device memory, which stores all kinds of dynamic data and executable programs. This indicator greatly affects multitasking, the speed with which different applications are launched and the transition between the tabs in the browser will be made. Therefore, it is very important not only to know how much RAM is in the device, but also not to confuse it with the built-in terms of significance in the smartphone.

The Motorola XT910 features in this regard are as follows:

  • RAM - 1 gigabyte.
  • Built-in - 16 gigabytes.
  • Additional memory up to 32 gigabytes.

RAM is enough to work with 3, and sometimes even 5 programs. But what is internal memory and how is it used up? This is the place that stores all the data in itself, for example, music, videos or photos. In addition to personal data, 3.5 gigabytes are allocated for programs and part for system information. But what if this memory is not enough?

For such purposes, there is a microSD slot on the device on the left side near the SIM cards. Its volume should not exceed 32 gigabytes. In order for the Motorola XT910 to work more stably, it is recommended to put the card at 16 gigabytes.

And here the main question for Motorola RAZR XT910 is how to Russify this miracle of engineering?

Some craftsmen download MoreLocale, which allows you to change the language. But there is one drawback - only the interface language of the smartphone becomes Russian. How to be

The problem is solved by the lack of official software. How is this possible? It's simple: all custom firmware often contain root-rights, which give access to some system functions.
