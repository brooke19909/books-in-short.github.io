Turkish Airlines is one of the oldest carriers of the Shining Port

Turkish Airlines is an experienced company. She began to work in 1933 as a state. Initially, she had only three aircraft, which mainly flew from Istanbul to Ankara. After the war, overseas flights began to be carried out, in particular, to Athens. Then the number of flights and destinations began to increase rapidly, and the fleet of aircraft increased greatly. Now the company has private co-owners, including Lufthansa.



Today, Turkish Airlines is based at 34 airports in the country and actually takes first place in domestic passenger transportation. In addition, the company also has a low-cost branch called AnadoluJet. International flights usually depart from Istanbul Ataturk Airport. The company's planes fly to one hundred and twenty countries of the world and 324 cities, including the cities of the USA and Europe, but mainly these are Asian destinations - Bangkok, Tokyo, Beijing. Turkish Airlines has been operating in Moscow since 2012. Their main airport is Vnukovo. But not only the company's planes fly to Moscow: it also flies to other cities of Russia - St. Petersburg, Novosibirsk, Sochi ... It has four stars by international standards.



According to travelers, the Turkish Airlines carrier is very punctual and almost never loses or damages baggage. The site has a fairly convenient ticket reservation system. By the way, those who want to independently choose their favorite place can also go through online registration - there is such an opportunity. In addition, on the company's website you can find discounts on different directions. Turkish airlines fleet is now updated. All of them meet modern technology standards and the latest design.

Many note the excellent service and well-trained staff. They especially praise the quality of food provided on airplanes, which is far from always natural for food in flight. Food begins to spread literally half an hour after departure. They feed almost all flights of any duration. If the flights of Turkish Airlines last more than four hours, then hot and alcoholic drinks can be ordered in unlimited quantities. In addition, on long flights, passengers will find a huge selection of entertainment - large televisions, films, music, games. Children will not be bored either. Many programs are in Russian.
