What to choose speakers in the car: an overview of the best models and reviews

Which car owner does not like to ride with the breeze, and even to the sounds of his favorite composition? But in order to enjoy the high-quality sound of music, you should carefully approach the choice of speakers in the car. Consider what to look for when choosing, we will analyze the main types and types of acoustics, as well as the rating of speakers depending on various indicators and user reviews.

Types of acoustics in a car

Before deciding which speakers to put in the car , it is worth familiarizing yourself with the types of speakers.

Types of acoustics for cars:

  • Broadband - the sound is reproduced by one speaker, the system is easy to install, while there is no clear sound quality of music, but such speakers are in a low price category.
  • Component - there are several speakers, because of which the sound is reproduced cleanly and without interference, the effect of ‚Äúpresence‚Äù is created, as it were, but the system requires certain knowledge during installation and is also expensive.
  • Coaxial - the average option between broadband and component acoustics, the sound quality is high, the installation is simple, but you need to further configure the speakers.
  • Mid-range - used with twitter, but not very popular with car enthusiasts.
  • Cabinet - all speakers are built into the body, so the installation of the system is simple, but such speakers are dimensional, which requires a rather large space in the car.

Where is better to place?

Good speakers in the car - this is not only a properly selected technique according to the parameters, but also a good choice of location in the car. It is this parameter that determines to a greater degree how the system will sound and whether it will be heard at all by both passengers and the driver of the vehicle.

Speakers are not placed in the side latches of the rear shelf and in the kick panels. The optimal location for the acoustics is the front doors of the car, while the speakers should be located at the level of the head and ears of the driver. Thus, the sound covers a large area of ‚Äã‚Äãdistribution and is well audible in the rear seats.

There are also some special places for speakers already installed in some cars. But they are not always chosen correctly, therefore, in order to get high-quality sound, the design should be redone. For example, in the front doors of Daewoo Lanos there is a broadband speaker with a diameter of 10 cm in plastic catwalks, but even if you replace it with a more expensive look, you will not get high-quality sound. If you replace the plastic podium with a wooden one, then it will be possible to install larger speakers and significantly improve the quality of music playback.

Criterias of choice

When choosing a speaker in a car, you should pay attention to the following indicators:

Manufacturing firm

It is worth choosing the brand that has been on the speaker market for a long time, and its equipment is very popular. For example, MTX, Focal, DLS, Infinity, Hertz, Alpine, Morel, Magnat and others. Users also noted the Pioneer speakers in the car, which, although inexpensive, provide high-quality sound.

Speaker and tweeter suspension

It is better to choose a rubber suspension, not cloth, tweeters - silk. Thus, the sound will be cleaner, smoother and softer.

Space availability

If the space in the cabin allows, then it is better to take larger speakers. The rear speakers in the car are usually selected in sizes of 6 x 9 inches or up to 20 cm in diameter.

Radio Features

Any speakers are selected depending on the characteristics of the radio. There is a rather "finicky" technique, which requires the installation of only certain models or types of speakers.

The main characteristics of acoustics

Car owners and experts advise you to pay attention to the following characteristics when choosing speakers in a car:

  • Playback frequency - there are such systems that reproduce the bottoms or only the tops well, and there are universal options that perfectly cope with sound of different frequencies.
  • Size - round or oval (the larger the size, the cleaner the lower frequencies sound).
  • Power - rated and maximum (to be specified upon purchase).
  • Frequencies - a wide range on which the sound quality depends.
  • Sensitivity is an optimal parameter from 85 dB.

Recommendations

Experts, as well as users of car speakers, give some tips regarding the purchase, installation and type of speakers in the car:

  • Acoustics should be purchased only at specialized points of sale, the presence of a quality certificate and a guarantee is a mandatory purchase criterion.
  • The speaker input must match what the car has, because this parameter may differ depending on the make of the car.
  • If a car radio with a GPS module is installed in the car, then the acoustics should be selected depending on the brands recommended by the manufacturer, otherwise the speakers will interfere with the navigation system.
  • Each type of radio has certain parameters that are taken into account when choosing speakers (if the model is cheap, then buying expensive speakers for it is simply pointless).
  • If the car has taken measures to insulate the cabin, then the sound will sound clearer and louder, regardless of the installed speakers.
  • Installation is best entrusted to specialists.

6 x 9 speakers or subwoofer?

Many car owners are wondering which speakers are best to put in a car, or maybe you should choose a subwoofer? To do this, you need to know several characteristics. So, 16 cm speakers reproduce bass better than 13 cm counterparts. But even in this case, low frequencies are not enough. Installing a subwoofer will help to complement this playback area, which will provide both loud and low sound.

An alternative to the subwoofer is a 6 x 9 inch speaker. They have good sensitivity (up to 92 dB / W), but subwoofers up to a maximum of 89 dB, and even finding such parameters in the technology is problematic.
