Lake Velie in the Novgorod region

Lake Velje (Novgorod region) is a popular tourist attraction in Russia. Thousands of tourists come here from year to year. Some spend only holidays and weekends, others stop for a week or more. Why is vacation here so popular? The answer to this question can be found in the article.

Velier Lake: Description

Velho is a reservoir located in the northwestern part of the Russian Federation. Administratively, this territory belongs to the Novgorod region. The lake is part of the Valdai National Park. To the north of it there is another famous reservoir - Lake. Seliger.

Lake Velje (see photo in the article) is quite deep. In 2004, bottom surveys were carried out that revealed large pits. In one of them, the depth reached 42 m. The average figure is 10 m.

The shores of the lake are distinguished by relief. For example, it is almost impossible to travel to the south and east by transport, so they are completely uninhabited, unlike the north and west. The water is clean, sandy beaches are chosen by tourists from different parts of Russia. There are no large industrial and agricultural facilities in the vicinity. That is why this area is environmentally friendly.

Given the climatic features of the region, the lake freezes in November-December. For the whole winter period it is covered with a thick layer of ice, thanks to which winter fishing thrives here. The reservoir opens in the middle of spring. By May, all the ice is completely gone.

The water in Velha is flowing. Several rivers flow into it. The largest is Yavon. It is through it that the natural flow of lake water is carried out. Yavon flows in the western part of Velho. At this place a dam is built that regulates the level in the lake. Currently, it is raised by 2-4 meters. An artificial canal has been built on the pond in the eastern part, thanks to which part of the water flows into the rivers Shlina, Libya and Lake. Shlino.





Features

Lake Velje (Novgorod region) is glacial in origin. Its history spans several centuries. Locals have many legends associated with this pond. They are pleased to tell interesting stories, which, however, do not have official confirmation. But this is not important. The main thing is that these facts prove the uniqueness and mystery of these places.

The lake is surrounded by magnificent nature. It is truly unique. Dense pine forests, spruce and birch groves make the air not only clean, but also healing. Flora and fauna to this day has been preserved almost in its original form. There are many wild animals in the forest belt. These are foxes, wild boars, moose, roe deer and even, with luck, you can meet a bear. The world of birds is no less diverse. Majestic eagles and hawks often fly over the water surface. Such an abundance attracts hunters who always leave these places with good trophies. In the reservoir itself there are more than 20 species of various fish.

The climate in these places is temperate continental. The air vaguely resembles the sea. Seasonality is clearly expressed. In summer, sunny weather is set, the temperature reaches +30 ¬∞ C, but there is no peculiarity for cities. Winter is mild with persistent snow cover. The beach season begins at the end of June. By July, the water in the lake warms up to +25 ¬∞ . Tourists come to the coast who stop either at recreation centers, or "savages" with tents.





Fishing

Will Lake Velie (Novgorod Region) be able to surprise fishermen? Reviews of professionals allow us to draw some conclusions. And let's start with the inhabitants of the underwater world. In the reservoir there are different representatives. In large quantities, roach, pike, bream, perch, perch and others live here. Also, with luck, you can catch the most valuable specimen - vendace. In the role of gear suitable any options: spinning, spinners, donka and more. The choice will depend on the type of fish you want. Fishing can be carried out both from the shore and from boats. Very important: it is not recommended to go out into the water without life jackets.

Those who first chose Lake Velha will find it useful to find out about the weather. Strong winds often blow in the area. The temperature can drop sharply. Especially such fluctuations are characteristic of the autumn period. Many fishermen noticed that during the day the temperature can be +30 ¬∞ , and in the evening it can drop to +7 ¬∞ . Therefore, it is recommended to take warm clothes on the trip.

So how to fish on the lake? Knowledgeable people recommend using bait. It can be mixed with vanilla, fried flaxseed, sunflower cake (makukha). It is also recommended to use assorted cereals (pearl barley, millet, rice, wheat and others). If you adhere to these tips, then a good catch is guaranteed even in bad weather. Most often, pikes from 1 to 3 kg are caught on the hook (individuals up to 10 kg are found in the lake), roach - 0.3 kg, perches - 0.2-0.4 kg. The bream is a capricious fish, so you need to fish on it only in calm weather and at a depth of at least 4 m.

Holidays on Lake Velie in the Novgorod Region are rapidly gaining popularity every year. And this is understandable. In the summer, you can swim, sunbathe, arrange walks in the woods, go fishing. In winter, you won‚Äôt be bored either. Many people come to these places in order to ski, snowmobile or engage in extreme sports. Even in this season, you can go fishing or hunting. It is better to adhere to this rule, as there are frequent checks on the lake. If there is no desire it is so difficult to get to places with parking, then it is recommended to choose the north or west coast. It can be easily reached by car.

Although Lake Velje is a popular tourist attraction, there is practically no rubbish, which will please any tourist. Parking lots are many. Tent camps are built near each beach in the summer. If you want privacy and silence, you can stay away from crowded places.

Sandy beaches, of which there are many along the entire coastline, are ideal for swimming. Depth is increasing gradually. The only thing that can cause inconvenience is algae. In late spring and early summer there are a lot of them, but by July almost all the beaches have been cleared.

Given that the lake is located in a conservation area, rest here is paid. Entrance to the territory - about 100 rubles., Parking - 50 rubles. Lake Velie: how to get there?

There are two ways to get to the lake: by private car or public transport. Let's look at each option in more detail.

By car, traffic is carried out along the highway Moscow - St. Petersburg. You need to go to the sign for the city of Valdai. After it, after a hundred meters there will be a turn to Demyansk. Then you need to drive about 25 km along the main highway. The road will be asphalt. Near Ivanteyevo it will be replaced by concrete. At the fork, to which the distance is about 3 km, you need to turn left. Continue on until the next fork. Here, turn right and continue along the dirt road. In 2-3 km B. Ukleina will be. This village is located at the extreme northern point of the reservoir. If you need to get to the west bank, then there will be a right turn near the village.

Now about the trip to Lake Velha using public transport. If for some reason there is no car, then you can use the train and bus. Trains from Pskov run to Valdai.
