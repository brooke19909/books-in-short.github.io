Battle tactics



Combat tactics, as part of military art, are a combination of theory and practice in preparing and conducting combat operations, starting from the offensive and ending with tactical regroupings, units, formations or units of armed forces and troops on land, sea or air.

The tactics of warfare involves the use of some tactical principles that allow you to achieve victories.
