Hotel Olympic Hotel 3 * (Montenegro, Ulcinj): overview, description and reviews

Montenegro is a very picturesque and at the same time quite popular country, including among Russian tourists. You can relax in the resorts of this state very well. But, of course, only with the right choice of hotel. One of the best budget hotels hosting tourists in Montenegro is the Olympic Hotel 3 *. This hotel is located in the southernmost city of the country - Ulcinj.

Location Features

Olympic Hotel was built almost on the seashore, about 4 km from the city center. Unfortunately, it is very far from the airport - 100 km. Very few Russian tour operators work with hotels in Ulcinj. Domestic vacationers usually go to this resort on their own. In this case, the easiest way to get from the airport to the hotel is by taxi. For holidaymakers who have bought vouchers with accommodation at this hotel, a shuttle bus is usually provided. The road to the hotel on it takes about 3 hours. On the way, the bus calls at other hotels.





Thus, it takes a long time to go to a hotel located in Ulcinj (Ulcinj Olympic Hotel 3 *, Montenegro). However, most tourists do not consider this a particular inconvenience. The roads in Montenegro are very good, and the views from the bus window open just magnificent. The climate in this country is hot. But transfer buses are in most cases air-conditioned.

A distinctive feature of Montenegro is the almost complete absence of public transport. Riding from Ulcinj to other cities is possible only by taxi. Moreover, this service is quite expensive in Montenegro. But many tourists rent cars at this resort. This allows you to save including sightseeing.

What is a hotel

Hotel Olympic 3 * (Montenegro, Ulcinj) is part of the Big Beach complex. It was named so because it was once built specifically for the athletes of the Olympic reserve of Montenegro. This hotel was built a long time ago. However, in 2005 there was a reconstruction.

The Olympic Hotel (Ulcinj, Montenegro) is a solid multi-storey building of modern design. Its guests consider the territory of the hotel quite cozy and comfortable. Large trees grow in the courtyard, in the shade of which it is very pleasant to relax. Also in front of the hotel are large lawns. Hotel "Olympic" shares the territory with another recreation center of the city of Ulcinj - Bellevue 2 *.





The front desk at this hotel is open 24 hours a day. Smoking is not allowed in the rooms of the complex. For adherents of this bad habit, the hotel has special places. Also, pets are not allowed in Olympic. Children are accepted to the hotel from any age.

Check-in at the hotel is registered after 13:00, check-out is until 10:00. Check in to the hotel, judging by the reviews of its former guests, very quickly.

Description of rooms

If you wish, in this hotel you can book ordinary standard class rooms or a family junior suite. Of course, the hotel rooms are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay of guests. In the apartments handed over to Olympic 3 * (Montenegro, Ulcinj Riviera), in addition to bedside tables and beds, there are:

  • air conditioners;
  • TV sets
  • minibars.

Many rooms have balconies from which you can admire the surrounding (in most cases quite attractive) views. Here you can dry beach items. In each room, among other things, there is an individual shower room. In total, this hotel offers 132 rooms for tourists.





Guest reviews about apartments

Most vacationers consider the interiors of the rooms of the Olympic Hotel 3 * hotel (Montenegro, Ulcinj) not too rich, but rather pleasant. The rooms in this hotel look neat and comfortable enough. In any case, maids are often cleaned in them. Bed linens and towels are always fresh in the rooms.

The furniture in the rooms, according to vacationers, is old, but quite comfortable. Household appliances in the rooms are also not new, but in most cases they work properly. Very good reviews on the network are available about the plumbing in this hotel. The area at the apartments in the hotel, according to most tourists, could be a little larger.

Hotel Infrastructure

Since the Hotel Olympic Hotel (Ulcinj, Montenegro) was built for the Olympians, there are many sports grounds next to it. If you wish, hotel guests can play volleyball, basketball, tennis, keep fit on treadmills, etc. Also on site there are sites for throwing a core and long jump. Of course, the hotel also has its own pool.

Also for the convenience of guests, the hotel provides:

  • sauna;
  • gym;
  • Terraces for relaxation and sunbathing.

Wi-Fi is available at the hotel for a fee. He catches a network in this hotel, including in the rooms. The hotel also has a banquet and conference room. If desired, guests of Ulcinj can hold here, for example, some kind of celebration. Businessmen can order a conference room for a corporate event.





Hotel guests living on the upper floors can take the elevator. There are two of them in the hotel. Unfortunately, it often happens that only one elevator works in a hotel.

Hotel Olympic 3 * (Ulcinj, Montenegro): service reviews

Opinion about this hotel among its guests was quite good. The staff at the hotel are polite and the guests are very friendly. All hotel staff can speak English. Reception workers also know some Russian phrases. For domestic tourists it is certainly very convenient. In any case, the Serbian language is a little similar to Russian, and the guests from Russia in terms of communication with hotel staff usually do not have problems.

The pool at the hotel, according to tourists, is large enough and convenient for swimming. The water in it is always clean. The only thing is that the hotel staff spills too much bleach into the water. The hotel pool is open from 11:00 to 18:00. This, of course, is not too convenient for guests. The gym at the hotel operates on the same schedule. However, none of the guests visited him. There is a gym in the basement of the hotel and the equipment in it is installed, judging by the reviews of tourists, outdated and idle. Thus, the availability of a gym is declared by the hotel administration, apparently, only for advertising. Which, of course, can be attributed to the minuses of the hotel.

Nutrition Reviews

Rooms at the Olympic Hotel 3 * (Montenegro, Ulcinj) are rented with breakfast or half board. Reviews about food in the hotel are mostly good. According to most guests, the food in the hotel is pretty monotonous, but the dishes here are delicious. In addition, portions are provided for guests quite large. Breakfast and dinner are served at the hotel on a buffet basis. Guests can take scrambled eggs, first and second courses, pastes, jams, toasts, sausages, etc. Vegetables are also served in the hotel's dining room. The guests really praise the local ice cream and yoghurts.





The cooks of this hotel, as in many others in European countries, have not yet begun to use palm oil. Therefore, all the food in the dining room is natural. Most of all, the hotel restaurant serves fried dishes.

Residents of the Olympic Hotel 3 * (Montenegro, Ulcinj) can dine in the cafes and restaurants located next to it. Catering establishments in the city of Ulcinj also earned good reviews from tourists. The food in the cafes and eateries of this resort is good. But the dishes in the city's catering establishments are quite expensive.

Beach Hotels

The sea is not too far from the hotel. Guests need to walk about 200 meters through the hotel itself, then about 100 meters through the park and a little more through the sand. A total of approximately 500 meters of the path. The length and width of the beach located next to the hotel is quite large. However, the entire infrastructure on it is concentrated at about fifty meters.

If desired, guests at the beach can use sun loungers and parasols for a fee. They must be taken with a kit. You cannot use your umbrella on this beach. The coastline in this place is covered with soft fine sand.

Of course, vacationers on the coastline opposite the hotel are offered various kinds of water activities. Tourists can ride a banana, a tablet, a catamaran, or even fly with a parachute. There is a toilet on this beach. True, you must also pay for his visit. There are usually quite a lot of people in this place on the shore.





On the beach, of course, there are bars where you can dine. Some of these establishments even offer their customers free internet.

Activities near the hotel

Unfortunately, no animation is provided at the Olympic Hotel 3 * (Montenegro, Ulcinj). But its guests can visit such an entertainment event at any time in a neighboring hotel. Of course, there is something for the residents of the complex to do in the city itself. Near the hotel are, for example, as many as two amusement parks. There are carousels in them are not very expensive.

Instead of a conclusion

The list of hotels in Ulcinj (Montenegro) is actually quite extensive. Among European tourists this city is very popular. Vacationers, if they wish, can rent rooms, for example, in hotels such as:

  • Villa Oleander;
  • Dajana;
  • Kopernikus Apartments 3 *;
  • Hostel Pirate (very cheap);
  • Hotel Senator 5 *, etc.

Among them, of course, are much more comfortable than the Olympic Hotel. However, the prices in such hotels, of course, are much higher.





Experienced tourists are advised to come to this complex with independent booking of rooms. In this case, rest in Montenegro will cost an order of magnitude cheaper. The fact is that since Ulcinj has not yet gained much popularity among domestic vacationers, there are few tour operators working with this resort. And those that are available, greatly overstate the price of tickets. Sometimes the commission of such companies is even more than 50%.
