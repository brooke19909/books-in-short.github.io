Hotel Sentido Phenicia Hotel 4 * (Hammamet, Tunisia): photos and reviews of tourists

If you have long dreamed of meeting Tunisia, pay attention to such an option as Sentido Phenicia Hotel 4 *. This hotel will give you a feeling of comfort, homeliness and demonstrate high standards of service.





Short hotel description

Sentido Phenicia Hotel 4 * Resort will delight you with beautiful architecture and a cozy interior. A feature of this institution can be considered that its territory is surrounded by tropical gardens of 9 hectares. In the courtyard for guests there are blue pools with both fresh and sea water, and an extended well-kept beach is located nearby.

In the apartments of Sentido Phenicia Hotel 4 * everything is equipped according to the needs of vacationers. Comfortable furniture, modern appliances - all this will make your stay in the room the most comfortable. In addition, it is worth paying attention to the interior, in which elements of local culture are traced.

In the restaurant Sentido Phenicia Hotel 4 * you can taste not only local cuisine, but also dishes from other nations of the world. The bars offer a wide range of local and imported drinks. An animation team, as well as numerous sports facilities, are responsible for a good mood and leisure.

Hotel Location

On the seashore is a luxurious hotel Sentido Phenicia Hotel 4 *. Tunisia, Hammamet, Moncef Bay, 8039 - an address that will serve as a guide for travelers. To get to the city center, you have to overcome about 5 km. Just 2 minutes away are two huge golf courses.

Accommodation

Hotel Sentido Phenicia Hotel 4 * (Tunisia) has 390 rooms. You can choose the following accommodation options for yourself:

  • Standard rooms have double or half beds. You can choose an option with a view of the garden or the sea. For those who prefer independent travel, a single apartment option is provided.
  • Family rooms are spacious and have two bathrooms. Each of the two bedrooms has the same equipment as a standard apartment.
  • Presidential Suite is an example of luxury and comfort. The bedroom and living room are decorated in light colors with red and gold decorative accents. The bedroom has a four-poster bed, and the living room is equipped with leather furniture. The bathroom has a jacuzzi.




Apartment Amenities

If you want your vacation to be at the highest level, pay attention to the hotel Sentido Phenicia Hotel 4 * (Tunisia, Hammamet). Hammamet is characterized by traditions of hospitality, which is confirmed by the presence of a large number of amenities in the rooms:

  • a small private balcony or a spacious terrace, equipped with sun loungers and parasols;
  • central air conditioning, which is carried out from June to September;
  • bathroom with shower or jacuzzi;
  • built-in electric hair dryer;
  • landline telephone with access to an international line;
  • TV with satellite channels, two of which are Russian-speaking;
  • a refrigerator where you can always store a small grocery stock;
  • the minibar is filled on request subject to an additional payment;
  • the room has a safe with a combination lock, the use of which will cost you $ 1.5 per day;
  • soundproofing.




All Inclusive Concept

Sentido Phenicia Hotel 4 * hotel offers travelers to relax on all their favorite all-inclusive system. As part of this concept, guests can use the following services for free:

  • use of sun loungers and umbrellas located near the pools and on the beach;
  • daily visit to the gym;
  • hour rent of a tennis court (sports equipment is available at an additional cost);
  • archery equipment;
  • daily hourly use of a catamaran or windsurfing equipment (optional);
  • transfer to golf courses by electric car;
  • unlimited coffee and herbal tea in a Moorish cafe;
  • Free local spirits in bars.

Hotel Infrastructure

The resemblance of a small tourist town is a resort complex Sentido Phenicia Hotel 4 * (Hammamet). The 2016 reviews allow us to conclude that the following infrastructure is available on the territory:

  • three pools, one of which is equipped with equipment for heating in cold weather;
  • a spacious terrace with numerous sun loungers and parasols to protect from the sun;
  • large tropical garden with palm trees and flowering shrubs;
  • free use of wireless Internet in public areas (this service is also available in some rooms);
  • own comfortable beach;
  • free parking area.




Entertainment

It is impossible to imagine a full-fledged vacation without vibrant and active entertainment. In this hotel you will be provided with the following options:

  • on the beach you can do water sports with the passage of preliminary instruction;
  • in the SPA salon you can undergo cosmetic and wellness treatments;
  • equipment for playing table tennis;
  • playroom;
  • own hippodrome, where riding lessons are held (horseback riding is possible);
  • a gym with modern equipment for full training;
  • a night club in which lovers of vibrant entertainment can enjoy unbridled dances to the music of popular DJs;
  • a playground for the smallest;
  • massage room (it is also possible to call the master in the room);
  • round-the-clock grocery store;
  • retail outlets with souvenirs;
  • evening shows from the animation team.




Catering establishments

Choosing for your vacation Sentido Phenicia Hotel 4 * (Hammamet), you can enjoy the skills of local chefs, as well as a wide range of drinks. You can visit the following places:

  • The main restaurant, where guests are offered full buffet meals. You can sit both in the dining room and on the outdoor terrace.
  • Dragon Restaurant specializes in Asian cuisine. The most popular are sushi and rolls.
  • Le Panache - A restaurant specializing in Mediterranean dishes. The largest number of menu items is reserved for seafood.
  • The Moorish Cafe is a great place for a bite to eat. Here, guests who love to sleep are offered a brunch. Tea, coffee and local sweets will be offered throughout the day.
  • Pizzeria is a favorite place not only for adults, but also for children. You will be able to independently form your dish by choosing from a huge assortment of fillings.
  • Six bars where guests can enjoy free local drinks under the all-inclusive concept.
  • Carillon is the only smoking establishment in the hotel. A visit to the bar is paid, and there are also age restrictions (strictly after 18 years).




Facilities and service

This hotel offers a wide range of services. It is worth focusing on the following points:

  • round-the-clock reception provides a quick solution to problems associated with accommodation;
  • laundry and ironing of personal belongings of guests in dry cleaning;
  • currency exchange operations (commission is charged);
  • transfer to the airport;
  • food delivery from the restaurant to the room.

Additional information for guests

Going on vacation, try to learn as much as possible about the hotel where you are going to stay. Speaking about this institution, it is worth bearing in mind the following information:

  • The check-out time associated with registration and eviction is set for noon;
  • cots for babies up to 2 years are free;
  • the cost of an extra bed for children under 12 years old is 50% of the price of accommodation, and for adults - 70%;
  • pets are not allowed in the hotel;
  • there is the possibility of paying for services through plastic cards;
  • if you need special diet food, it is worth filling out a special form at the reception, which will be handed over to the restaurant chefs.

General impression

The reviews of the Sentido Phenicia Hotel 4 * hotel allow us to conclude that this is not an ideal, but quite a decent option for accommodation in Tunisia. Guests are impressed by the vast clean area, as well as the long sandy beach. The food is quite acceptable, but with its own nuances. Perhaps the most significant drawback is the need to tip everywhere. However, this can be considered a distinctive feature of all Tunisian hotels.
