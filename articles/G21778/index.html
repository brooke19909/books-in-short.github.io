Benefits of Traveling by Car

The south can be reached in various ways: by train, by plane, by boat, by bus, by car. Consider the advantages of the latter method, since the main topic of the article is a trip to Sochi by car. Traveler reviews allow you to highlight the following points:

  1. Absolute independence from anyone. You leave when you want, and in the future are guided only by your needs or family members, if you are not traveling alone.

Tips for motorists based on traveler reviews

  • Make a route of your trip and study it carefully, paying particular attention to places of rest and overnight.
  • Calculate the amount of time you will be on the road.
  • Make sure that there is a full tank of gasoline, you can also take a filled canister with you.
  • Going on the road is best in the early morning.
  • The best speed on the road is 90-100 kilometers per hour. But in some sections of the road it may be 40. Look carefully at the road signs.
  • Difficult sections of the road, such as serpentine, it is advisable to drive during the day, especially if there is no driving experience.
  • If you feel tired and want to sleep, then you need to stop for rest and sleep. It is better to lose time, but to maintain the health of one's own and loved ones.
  • There is a saying: "Not sure - do not overtake." Think better than going overtaking, especially on difficult sections of the road. Remember one more proverb - "Slow down, you will continue."
  • You must take care of booking cheap hostels or hotels for overnight stays. If this is not done, you will have to pay more money, since there may no longer be inexpensive options. Of course, about where you will live in Sochi, you also need to think in advance and arrange with the owners or book a hotel room.

Hazardous areas

According to reviews, the road to the south by car does not cause much trouble if you go, observing the rules of the road and not exceed the speed more than expected. But there are several sections on the highway that require increased attention and accuracy of the driver.

During the journey, at times you will have to drive along a narrow, winding, mountain road. Also overcome the passes. I would like to pay special attention to the difficult section of the road in the Dagomys area. Its length is about 200 kilometers. What is he like? This is a mountain serpentine. Turns are steep and closed. Huge trucks and double-decker buses can leave you at any time. Here you need to be especially careful. It is better to drive through this area in the morning or in the afternoon, and in front of it you must definitely have a good meal and rest in order to restore your strength. Then everything will be in perfect order.
