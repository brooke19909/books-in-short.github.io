Korolev hotels: what do the city's hotels offer?

To see something new, it is not necessary to go far. And it is true. For those who live in the capital, it is enough to make a short trip to Korolev, where there is something to admire and something to be surprised.

Attractions Queen





The first thing that comes to mind when mentioning the name of the city is astronautics. One of the objects that every visitor of the city wants to visit is the Center, which manages space flights. Moreover, employees themselves organize visits to their organization, hosting both Russian and foreign tourists.

In addition, the city has many other important objects that are related not only to space, but also to the past of the country, so a trip here is always an interesting event. In order not to overshadow your stay in this unique city, you must make a reservation in advance. Korolev hotels are a huge selection of hotel establishments of different levels of stardom and comfort. Next, a few of them.

"Noble Nest"





Like other hotels in Korolev, this hotel is open around the clock, so you can check in at any time of the day. The design style is modern, which includes elements of Russian architecture. The interior, including furniture, is made by special order, therefore it is original. Many design details - stained-glass windows, chandeliers, wrought-iron staircases are made by hand so high quality that they can safely be called works of art.

To recreate the noble atmosphere inherent in noble estates, seven-layer paint in which gold leaf is added was applied to walls and ceilings . The rooms, and there are 67 of them, are presented in three categories, which allows visitors with different income levels to live here. All two-room rooms have a living room. For this, TVs are installed, there are refrigerators. The institution has a bowling club with a large range of entertainment. There are paths for children and adults, billiard tables, a bar.
