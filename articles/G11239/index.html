Hotel 3 * Santiago Resort, India, Goa: description, reviews

Hotel 3 * Santiago Resort belongs to the budget, but comfortable hotels of one of the most popular resorts. And this, as you might have guessed, is Goa. Briefly about the hotel

Hotel 3 * Santiago Resort has a very favorable location. This hotel is well located along two beaches - Baga and Calangute. To the sea - just a few minutes on foot. In addition, very close to the hotel there are various shops, restaurants, bars and even night markets, which are in special demand here.

Hotel 3 * Santiago Resort can offer its potential guests comfortable and spacious rooms, furnished with modern comfortable furniture and everything you might need.

By the way, the airport is also close enough. The distance from the hotel is 45 kilometers. It is comparatively close to drive, and far enough so that airplanes do not interfere with rest.

In general, the place is good. No wonder it is recognized as a favorite part of the city of Calangute among those people who have already managed to visit here.





Accommodation

The choice of apartments at the 3 * Santiago Resort is small. There are double rooms and the so-called family rooms for four. Of course, the hotel cannot boast of suites, deluxe, but those apartments that are here are a very comfortable option.

Take, for example, a standard number. It is quite spacious, in addition there is a balcony with a picturesque view of the surroundings, and a private bathroom with a toilet and various accessories. You can book such a room for about 55-62 thousand rubles for two (duration of stay - 14 nights). Breakfast, by the way, is included in the price, like all taxes with fees (which make up 17.42 percent of the total). The price may vary depending on what season it is (i.e. the time of year), and on the presence (or absence) of stocks and special super offers. So if a person gets into such a good time when discounts will be valid, then it will be possible to save several thousand rubles, which is by no means superfluous. By the way, the hotel 3 * Santiago Resort still gives the opportunity to cancel the reservation (and for free).





Conditions and facilities

And now a few words about what the apartments themselves are. The administration of the Santiago Resort 3 * hotel (Goa) took care of the comfort of potential guests. The rooms are really beautifully decorated, tastefully furnished with new and comfortable furniture. When booking a standard room, guests can choose beds. The apartments can stand either two standard or one double. Moreover, it is only stated, in fact it is a bed of simply incredible size. Another standard room can have two standard and one double (in case another guest is added).

Santiago Resort 3 * reviews get mostly positive. Guests assure - do not be afraid of the low price. The rooms have everything: TV with satellite and cable channels, telephone, air conditioning, separate seating area, fan, sofa, shower, minibar, kettle and even a wake-up service. In general - everything that may be needed for a comfortable stay.





Entertainment and leisure

An inexpensive stay at this hotel can be very memorable. People who come here mainly spend time on the beach, swimming in the sea, or on excursions, admiring the local attractions. But if you are tired of traveling or walking somewhere, then you can have a good time at the hotel. For example, swim in the outdoor pool. Or play darts. Inside there is even a billiard room. And lovers of relaxation in body and soul will definitely enjoy the sauna. Needless to say, even a wellness center and spa with massage services in the hotel are available. And those people who just wanted to sit in an armchair in the evening and read will enjoy a large selection of books in the library, which is also available at the hotel.

And of course, not without an evening program, which is organized by the animators at the end of each day by the pool. In general, the Santiago Resort 3 * hotel is a place where you can not only live comfortably for several days or weeks, but also have fun.

Nutrition

Many of the guests who spent their inexpensive holidays at this hotel claim that the restaurant is served at the highest level. So, the institution is called Las Masalas, and there guests are served refreshments of Mediterranean, Indian and continental cuisines. Guests who have been here assure us that people who love various delicacies and delicacies will definitely like it here.

Still lodgers recommend to go to Thai Bazaar restaurant. Thai dishes are prepared there, which you should definitely try. And of course, the bar. It is called Rendezvous and is located on the roof of the hotel. Delicious and varied cocktails are served there - both soft, non-alcoholic and strong.





Service and maintenance

This hotel is so popular not only because it is inexpensive and comfortable, but also because of its excellent service. So, guests are offered free parking (and, of course, car rental), room service, drinks and food, airport transfers, ticket service and even trouser ironing. But that is not all. The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day so check-in or check-out can be arranged at any time. Plus, guests can be offered luggage storage or a personal locker. The hotel also has dry cleaning, laundry and even a trouser press - all these guests can also take advantage of. Needless to say, there are even shops in the hotel, which once again proves that the level of service here is really decent.

Thousands of Russians have already managed to visit here and spend an unforgettable vacation in Goa. And many of them claim that it is worth going here. A beautiful hotel that is ideal for relaxation after walking, sightseeing and sea vacations. They clean the rooms daily, everything is comfortable in the apartments themselves, the breakfasts are excellent, the atmosphere is excellent, and the climate is favorable. Plus, they are pleased with the very low prices for Goa. And what else is needed for a successful holiday?
