Recreation centers, Kemerovo: "Boyarsky estate", "Crane", "Fortune", "Paradise", "Hippo"

In order to have a good summer vacation, it is not necessary to go to foreign resorts. In the Kemerovo region there are ample opportunities for a good rest. You can spend time on the shore of beautiful clean reservoirs. It is on them that numerous recreation centers are built.

Kemerovo is a city through which two rivers flow - Iskitimka and Tom. The latter is one of the largest right tributaries of the Ob. There are also many other bodies of water in this region. In sunny warm weather, people sunbathe on the beaches, stroll through the woods and simply enjoy the clean fresh air. Numerous recreation centers (Kemerovo) have been built on Lake Aya and the Tom River . They are located in ecologically clean areas, equipped with everything necessary, comfortable rooms and houses are provided for living. So, let's take a closer look at the popular recreation centers located in this region.

"Paradise"





30 km from the city there is a recreation center "Paradise". People come here in order to restore strength, improve their health, enjoy the beauty of nature. A mixed forest grows on the territory of the base. Standard, standard plus, junior suite and deluxe rooms are provided for living. The minimum cost of living per day is 1000 rubles. The rooms are equipped with modern furniture, a TV and a kettle. Open loggias have a coffee table and chairs. The suites and junior suites have showers and a bathroom. There are gazebos on site. They are designed for 10-15 people. There are barbecue areas, a playground. Fishermen are provided with the necessary equipment for rent. The Tom River flows 150 m away. There are a lot of fish in it, so fishermen will always come with a good catch.

"Crane"





The recreation center (Kemerovo) is located in a pine forest. You can get to it both by public transport and by private car. It is located on the banks of the Tom River, just 20 minutes from the city. It is ideal for relaxing on weekends or holidays. For banquets, equipped halls can accommodate up to 50 people. On the territory there is Wi-Fi, two saunas, a billiard room. Like many recreation centers (Kemerovo), Zhuravel works all year round. The cost per day starts from 2000 rubles.
