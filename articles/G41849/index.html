Personnel number - what is it?

In principle, from the name itself it becomes clear what a personnel number is. This is a kind of numerical value that is assigned to a specific employee. This is the code that designates the employee.

The features of personnel numbers include the fact that they are individual. That is, there are no workers whose code would be exactly the same. At the same time, the personnel number is also retained for the dismissed employee. If in the future he continues to work with the employer, then he will be assigned his old number.

It is also worth noting that the personnel number of the employee is assigned to him during employment, in the future he does not change. That is, registration for work fixes a certain code, which subsequently remains the same, regardless of a change of position or increase in salary.

Specific examples. Why do I need a personnel number?

To understand by whom and how a personnel number is assigned to an employee, it is necessary to fully understand why this parameter is needed.

Who can assign?

Personnel numbers are assigned to employees by a specialist in the personnel department. However, it is worth noting that the legislation does not regulate the position of an employee engaged in the assignment of ciphers. However, when applying for a job, it is precisely the HR engineers that are directly present.

Heads of structural divisions or even the head of an enterprise can also do this. In the absence of a personnel employee, his place in this matter may be taken by an accountant.





Who can be assigned the personnel number

Can all employees be assigned a code? Accounting of personnel numbers is usually carried out for all employees of the enterprise. Namely:

  • Full time employees.
  • Temporary or seasonal workers.
  • External part-timers.
  • For those who work part-time.

In fact, each employee has his own personnel number, regardless of how long he is employed. It is noteworthy that even students who apply for practice in any department receive their own code.

It is worth noting that if an employee has any combination of work or profession within the same enterprise, he still gets one personnel number. The employee, who works part-time at two firms, has in each of them his own, individual code.





How is the personnel number assigned to the employee. What does the law say?

It is immediately worth noting that the legislation speaks rather vaguely about the assignment of a personnel number at the enterprise. In fact, there are no specific articles in the Labor Code or federal laws governing how personnel numbers are assigned to employees. Each employer for themselves develops a system that helps him to keep a competent and convenient record of cipher data. Here, an important role is played by internal documents of the organization. In them, for example, you can determine the position of the employee engaged in the assignment of numbers.





How to keep records? Practical recommendations

It is generally accepted that a personnel number is assigned to each employee, and should begin with the first number. In fact, it becomes identical to the concept of "serial number".

Such records can be kept in two versions:

  • Using special programs.
  • Manually by an employee.

The latter method is used less and less. This is due to the fact that the most popular 1C program allows you to automatically assign numbers to each newly established employee of the enterprise.





Practical tips

Assigning a personnel number can seem like a time-consuming process. However, it is not. You should follow a few simple rules, and this action will be carried out automatically.

First of all, the internal documents of the organization should be assigned this obligation to a specific employee. This will help to avoid bickering in the future. It will also exclude a situation where no one is responsible for this operation.

Secondly, the employee should be assigned the number directly on the first working day of the employee, and ideally at the time of the conclusion of the employment contract. In this case, the numbering will be correct and without omissions.

A specific numerical code needs to be communicated to employees, as this not only increases their importance in their own eyes, but also helps to avoid errors in payroll. This is due to the fact that when writing any application for material assistance or for making a vacation, the employee will indicate, among other things, his personnel number.

Also, this parameter must be specified in the order of employment. It can be printed in the program or written by hand by a representative of the human resources department.

Logbook. Fixed Personnel Numbers

Enterprises that are forced to use personnel numbers for their employees often use a document such as a personnel number book.

  • Specialist position.
