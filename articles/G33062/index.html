Hotel Wanbo Club 3 *, Hainan Island, description

Description: China is not such a popular tourist destination as Greece or Turkey, but in this country for travelers there are a huge number of opportunities not only to get acquainted with the local culture, but also to relax on a comfortable beach. One of the best resorts here is the island of Hainan. Hotels in this area offer a service that meets all international standards in quality. These are new and modern institutions with a well-developed infrastructure. These include, for example, Wanbo club 3, located in Sanya Bay, a few hours drive from the international airport. The last restoration of the hotel took place in 2010. This modern institution offers quality service and all the amenities to its guests.





Rooms: The Wanbo club has 3 * 108 rooms. All are air-conditioned. The apartments have a balcony with scenic views of the courtyard or overlooking the pool. Cleaning is done several times a week. It includes a TV, private bathroom, cable internet, minibar.

Meals: Wanbo club's restaurant serves local and European dishes. In the morning, a buffet is served. At the poolside bar you can enjoy barbecue (Chinese dishes). The restaurant has a diet and special children's menu.





Beach: Guests of Wanbo club 3 can visit the municipal beach, here they have their own plot. You can get to him from the institution in just a few minutes. Vacationers are offered a variety of entertainment and water sports.

Tourist information: The hotel offers parking for tourists traveling by their own transport. In addition, guests have at their disposal 4 swimming pools, a beauty salon and a business center. For travelers who are accustomed to an active lifestyle, there is a gym and playgrounds for various games (golf, volleyball, etc.). Business people are invited to take advantage of two conference rooms that accommodate small groups of tourists, equipped with modern high-quality equipment.

For children, amenities are provided here, such as a special pool, as well as a mini club where interesting classes and games are held (including in English). A doctor is available upon request. If necessary, babies can be left under the supervision of a nanny. Near the pool, animation is often carried out. Here you can rent a bike to explore the surroundings on your own. The sensitive hands of local massage therapists will deliver a few unforgettable minutes to vacationers. Tourists can visit the sauna, jacuzzi.

Digest: Wanbo club 3 offers quality family and youth vacations for tourists. The hotel also has facilities for business travelers who are visiting the area. Helpful and attentive staff will help with accommodation, solve problems. The hotel is a budget accommodation option in this part of China. The facility has all the modern amenities. Nearby are entertainment centers, shops.
