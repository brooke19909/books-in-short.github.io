Tricolor TV: user reviews

Reviews about Tricolor TV are interesting to everyone who is going to use the services of this digital satellite television operator operating in Russia. Currently, more than twelve million households are connected to it across the country. At the same time, almost ten million have access to HD-television. According to 2017 data, the company's total revenue from subscription payments is more than 19 billion rubles with an average check of one and a half thousand rubles.

History

About the largest operator "Tricolor TV" reviews can be found the most diverse. Some are satisfied with the work of the company, others are not. At the same time, the history of the company itself has relatively little time - less than fifteen years. In 2005, it was founded under the name "National Satellite Company", and then began broadcasting. First, only to the European part of Russia.

Since 2008, the operator began to cover Siberian regions, and by 2009 the number of subscribers increased to five million people. In 2012, it became known about a radical change in the company's development strategy. At the telecommunications exhibition, which took place on the territory of Russia, Director General Alexander Makarov officially announced that he plans to include foreign television channels in the packages provided, which the operator had previously refused to broadcast for fundamental reasons. In addition, it was decided to form a package in HDTV format.

In 2013, Tricolor became the first Russian operator to provide public satellite television broadcasts organized in ultra-high definition format.

Customer opinion

According to Internet reviews from Tricolor TV, users are not always satisfied with the service provided. When a company promises a maximum speed of up to 20 Mbit / s, in reality it is only 5-6 Mbit / s. This upsets the customers.

And from the next month, the speed drops to 300-400 Kbps. Any page on the Internet with such data transfer opens about a minute. As a result, many users even get the feeling that the company has a rule: in the first month after connecting, provide more or less high speed, and then reduce it as much as possible, thereby forcing to switch to more expensive tariffs.

This attitude towards their customers is openly outraged by many. There are still a lot of negative reviews about Tricolor TV.

Without stocks, the cheapest set with a conditional access module will cost 4,990 rubles. In the reviews of the receiver for the Tricolor TV, users say that they necessarily purchase it with a plate, they install everything. Then they call the wizard, which connects and configures everything.

Conditional Access Modules

In the reviews of the modules for Tricolor TV, customers say that the equipment itself is of a fairly high quality. Which, unfortunately, cannot be said about the work of the provider. In fact, a module is a plate into which a smart card of any digital satellite television operator is inserted. It can be installed in any TV with a corresponding slot, a built-in satellite receiver.

Users acknowledge that they have no complaints about the device itself, it is really high-quality. But when using it with the Tricolor TV provider, problems regularly arise, as a result of which channels get lost, their usual order is violated, and quality is reduced.

Because of this, it is increasingly possible to meet the opinions of customers who promise to swear that they will go to competitors as soon as possible.
