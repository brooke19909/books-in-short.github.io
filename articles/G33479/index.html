Hotel Seneschal (Solnechnogorsk): description with photos, infrastructure, service, reviews

If you are tired of the bustle of a bustling city, want to breathe in the fresh forest air and enjoy the magnificent views of Lake Senezh in the suburbs, the Seneschal Hotel in Solnechnogorsk, Moscow Region, invites you to visit it. In the original wooden houses you will feel like residents of a real noble estate.

Description

In the small exquisite five-star boutique hotel Seneshal in Solnechnogorsk, you will find spacious author-registered apartments located in two three-story buildings Chalet and Banya. The buildings are wooden, on high porches you can climb the openwork stairs, spacious terraces are provided for relaxation. Standing on the balconies of their rooms, guests enjoy amazing views of the forest expanses and the lake. For the decoration of the hotel used environmentally friendly materials and natural fabrics. In addition to handmade furniture, your stay here will be decorated with antique stylized objects - tiled fireplaces, porcelain lamps, carved dressing tables and classic paintings. The hotel's own restaurant, which serves Russian cuisine, offers panoramic views of the lake. The exquisite menu will be complemented by wines produced by famous wine houses of Italy, France and other countries. In warmer months, guests can enjoy tables on the hotel terrace or on the boat pier by the lake. Here you can rent a car with a driver or a helicopter. Free secure parking and a helipad are available on site.

In the estate, everything is thought out both for a quiet and for active recreation. Here you can go fishing, take a walk in the woods, play billiards, play football, as well as ride boats and catamarans, fly in a balloon, and go horse riding. It has a good bath complex. The hotel "Seneschal" is located in Solnechnogorsk at the address: Solnechnogorsk district, the 3rd kilometer of Timonovsky highway.

Guest Accommodation

Vacationers at the Seneshal Hotel in Solnechnogorsk can choose from the following room options:

  • standard - 21,500 rubles a day for two and 13,000 rubles a day for one person;
  • Deluxe - 26,000 rubles per day for two people;
  • Junior Suite - 26,000 rubles per day for two and 17,500 rubles per day for one person;
  • Suite with a lake view - 30 500 rubles per day for two people;
  • Premium Suite - 35,000 rubles per day for two people;
  • exclusive suite - 48,000 rubles per day for two people;
  • five-bedroom chalet - 240,000 thousand for ten people.

The price of all rooms (except for the chalet) includes accommodation and three meals a day. At the request of guests, payment is only possible with breakfast or with breakfast and dinner. The price includes breakfast only. The rooms are well soundproofed.
