Hotel D Varee Mai Khao Beach 4 * (Phuket, Thailand): description, reviews and photos

If you want to temporarily escape from a noisy metropolis and spend an unforgettable vacation on the ocean far from the bustle of the city, be sure to pay attention to the four-star D Varee Mai Khao Beach Hotel, located on the island of Phuket in Thailand.





Location

The hotel we are considering is located on the beach of Mai Khao Beach, in a quiet place, away from major cities. The airport is only 15 kilometers away. So the way to the hotel after arrival will take no more than a quarter of an hour. In the vicinity of the hotel there are several shops, cafes, souvenir shops. Also within walking distance is the market and massage parlors. The hotel is a 20-minute drive from Saracen Bridge. It takes half an hour to Phuket Town and 40 minutes to Patong Beach.

Rooms

As already mentioned, D Varee Mai Khao Beach has 114 apartments at its disposal. They are presented here in the following categories: deluxe pool view, deluxe pool access (with direct access to the pool), suites (85 sq. Meters, jacuzzi and bathtub) and duplex pool suites (160 sq. M.).

Regardless of the type, all rooms are spacious and stylishly decorated. They are equipped with high-quality comfortable furniture and modern appliances. Each room has air conditioning, TV with cable channels (including in Russian), telephone, minibar, kettle, tea set, bathroom, balcony or terrace. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel. Rooms are cleaned every day. Sheets and towels are updated several times a week. Beach towels are also provided.
