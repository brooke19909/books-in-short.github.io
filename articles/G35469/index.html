Educational program

Apart-hotel is primarily a completely commercial project, which involves the construction of real estate for the purpose of its further sale to private customers, who, in turn, can use the purchased meters for the following purposes:

  • like residential apartments;
  • as an office space to meet their own and personal needs;
  • as an object that you can rent yourself;
  • as an object of an agreement with a management company, which, in turn, incurs all the costs of operating the apartments, and in return pays their owner a certain percentage of income.
