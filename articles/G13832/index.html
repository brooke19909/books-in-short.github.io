Tips for beginner cutters and tailors

How to sew? For those who know how to hold a thread with a needle, this will not be difficult. But for those who do not know how to sew at all, I want to give some advice. Start with a T-shirt. Transfer its size to the fabric, indicating the places where the neck and armpits are located.

Before you start cutting, you should add a few centimeters to the seams on each side of the measurement. Cut and fasten the parts with pins, then, using a sewing machine, stitch everything along the marked seams. The neck will need to be sheathed with a strip of fabric five centimeters wide. All military children costumes can be decorated with a St. George ribbon, a five-pointed soldier's star, badges and epaulets.
