How to choose a hair dryer

Hairdryer is one of the most popular household appliances. Most often, ladies use it, since they feel the need for a variety of hair manipulations: from simple drying to a variety of styling at all levels of difficulty. And therefore, it is they who often have the question of how to choose a hair dryer.

The variety of hairdryer models in the modern market is huge. In order not to get lost in this abundance of goods, you should, first of all, decide on the functions of this device that you need. Then you will not be disappointed with the result and do not overpay for additional properties that you are not going to use. How to choose a hair dryer correctly and what features should you pay attention to?

How to choose a hair dryer: what types are

  • If you need this device only for drying hair, then you should not purchase an expensive tricked-out hair dryer, which is used by professionals in salons. Choose the most reliable small size hairdryer with a minimum of nozzles. The only requirement is the presence of a cold stream. Such a compact hairdryer is useful to you if you travel a lot or go to the gym, pool, etc.

  • If you often do styling, it is best to purchase a professional or semi-professional model. If you have short or medium length hair, then a hair dryer is also suitable for you, which helps to model your hair and give it shape.

Which hair dryer is better? Let's talk about power, temperature, airflow

The power of a hair dryer is one of the stumbling blocks between consumers and home appliance experts. Many people think that additional power does not give special advantages in the quality of styling, and this is just a subtle marketing move that allows you to make the device more expensive. Nevertheless, most professionals who use hair dryers for more than a year claim that it is much more convenient to work with a powerful hairdryer. The high power of this device is not only a high temperature, but also the strength of the air flow, which allows you to quickly dry your hair without burning it. If you buy a mini-hairdryer for traveling, it is best to take a high-power one, so as not to waste time on long hair drying.

Choose hair dryers with a large number of temperature modes and preferably with different buttons for adjusting the strength of the air and temperature - this will allow you to combine different modes.

How to choose a hair dryer: shape and weight

First of all, the hairdryer should be comfortable and not too heavy (especially for travel appliances). The "nose" of the hair dryer should not be too long - otherwise it will be inconvenient for you to do styling on the back of the head. The angle at which the hair dryer handle is attached to its body should not be more than 90 degrees.

Looking for a hairdryer: how to choose nozzles and additional features

The only extra feature you'll need in any case will be the cold air mode. It helps the head to "cool down", and also fixes the styling.
