Goryachiy Klyuch: Hotels, motels (reviews). Goryachy Klyuch: private hotels

Today we will tell you about the wonderful city of Goryachy Klyuch. Hotels of this sunny corner are very popular among residents of the Krasnodar Territory, as well as guests from neighboring regions and countries. It is located 56 km from Krasnodar, on the banks of the Psekups River. On three sides it is surrounded by beautiful forests. The gentle sun and amazing air, as well as excellent mineral springs make this city a unique holiday destination in Russia. Unlike many other balneological resorts, there are three sources, the waters of each of them differ in their characteristics. Since the city is a resort area, many hotels are located on its territory. Today we will talk about the most popular so that you can make the right choice.

Estate "Cold Stream"

Beautiful nature every year attracts tourists to Goryachiy Klyuch. Hotels, hotels and guest houses are always ready to receive guests and provide comfortable conditions for spending their holidays. Want to plunge into the Caucasian fairy tale? Then you will find the charming estate "Cold Stream". It is located 2 km from the famous sanatorium in a beautiful forest. You will find comfortable cottages, a Russian wood-fired sauna with a swimming pool, as well as a restaurant with the most exquisite dishes of European, Kuban and Caucasian cuisine.
