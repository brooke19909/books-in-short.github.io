Instant Print Camera: Fujifilm Instax mini 8, Polaroid. Description, specifications, cartridges

In the initial segment of photographic equipment, traditional compact digital cameras have firmly established themselves. Users appreciate such devices for mobility, ease of use and good image quality. The class representing the Polaroids is not so popular. Fujifilm is supporting him by perfecting his Instax family instant print camera. The next modification of this line is represented by the Instax mini 8 apparatus, which received modest sizes and decent functionality for its class.





Construction and design

In developing the device, the creators adhered to the previous concept of a light and easy-to-use camera. The modification even surpassed the previous version in compactness. For example, a camera with instant printing in this design can be easily placed in a pocket or worn with a cord on your wrist.

The form factor itself has not changed. Only controls that have shifted to the front of the small structure have undergone revision. The device is also turned on by extending the lens, but there are some differences from the previous variation. Now, to start the instant printing camera, you need to press a special button. It is also worth noting the important technical feature of this modification - the lens rotates, allowing you to manually adjust the focus.





Specifications

The Polaroid segment is quite narrow, so deviations from the main line of development of such devices often lead to the appearance of original, even piece goods. In part, the Fujifilm instant print camera can be attributed to these, the characteristics of which are presented below:

  • Dimensions - 116 mm - width, 118.3 mm - height and 68.2 mm - thickness.
  • Weight - 307 g without accessories.
  • Photo parameters - 62 x 46 mm.
  • Battery life - enough for 10 cassettes.
  • The delivery set includes an apparatus, a wrist strap and two batteries.
  • Functionality - Hi-Key mode, designed to control contrast and increase brightness.

Functionality

The model received an active light meter, which greatly expanded the shooting capabilities. Exposure is adjusted automatically, but the operator also requires certain skills to use this option. In turn, the device also acts as an assistant to the process, selecting the optimal frames. The red indicator, which is equipped with a camera with instant printing mini 8, lights up when the target objects in the frame under optimal lighting. This means that the best settings for the picture are selected. Space is also provided for the full realization of the creative abilities of the operator. The new High-Key mode of operation eliminated the automatic adjuster and prompts. By activating it, the user can also use the original effects for portraiture.





Cartridges for Polaroid

For the operation of the device, it is necessary to purchase special cassettes with film material. One standard set is designed for 20 shots. Actually, thanks to this consumable, instant printing is also performed. Branded cartridges for instant cameras from Fujifilm are characterized by high definition and contrast playback. Also, with skillful use of the device‚Äôs settings, the picture will reflect natural color reproduction and brightness. The cartridges are made in a reliable case, and thanks to the new technology for creating an emulsion, the user can take photos under conditions of temperature conditions from -5 ¬∞ C to +40 ¬∞ C.





Positive feedback about the model

Users praise the model for its ease of management and a generally successful form factor. If the previous representatives of the series pleased the owners with their compact size and small weight, then in this device the same qualities were strengthened, which, of course, causes reviews with admiration. Many also emphasize the quality of the case. Reliability of assembly and durability of used plastic - these advantages are especially appreciated by lovers of travel and tourism. The functionality that a Japanese camera with instant printing has also earned high marks. Of course, there are more productive models with wide capabilities on the market, but this already applies to a higher class, where prices are higher and the design is more massive. According to users, the developers provided the model with successful tools with four shooting modes and a viewfinder.





Negative reviews

Not all owners share positive judgments regarding the viewfinder. Critical reviews addressed mainly to the location of the device. Especially inconvenience is felt after the transition from a conventional digital camera. The viewfinder is located in the upper left corner, so the first time it is possible to obtain images that do not correspond to the originally conceived frame. There are complaints about the very quality of the images that this instant camera makes. Reviews indicate that a good photo, in principle, can be obtained, but only with proper dexterity and the ability to use the functions of this device. In the hands of an inexperienced user, such pictures may be rare. The main problem in this regard is the selection of optimal lighting and focus adjustment.

Separately, the model is criticized for its high cost. A camera on the Russian market is available on average for 5-6 thousand rubles. If you add a little bit, you can get the opportunity to create better pictures through the device a level higher. Cassettes for Instax Polaroid are also expensive. In particular, a cartridge for 10 shots is estimated at 300-400 rubles.





Conclusion

In general, the model adequately coped with the task of continuing the concept of instant printing devices. Obviously, compared to previous devices of this family, the model took several steps towards increasing ergonomics and functionality. But at the same time, the Instax mini 8 instant print camera is an expensive accessory without practicality. To a large extent this is due to the high cost of the camera itself and consumables in the form of cassettes. Such costs can be forgiven on the condition of obtaining truly high-quality images. But even here the camera does not inspire confidence, since even experienced users do not always have successful shots. This is not to say that the format of pictures the size of a plastic card does not in itself seem so attractive. True, lovers of the Polaroids, and in this feature find their beauty, which deserves overpayments.
