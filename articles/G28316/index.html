Laguna Yug Chalet is a perfect vacation for everyone

Often, Russian resorts are surrounded by amazing landscapes or close to unique healing springs, but at the same time the service remains at the level of Soviet times, which is not very pleasant for vacationers. That is why many people prefer a trip abroad. The recreation center “Laguna Yug” is radically different from the rest. Here a bet has been made on the most comfortable rest of the guests, the menu and the entertainment program are well thought out. The only negative is the price, rest is not at all cheap.





What is a recreation center?

This is a fairly large and very cozy village. “Laguna South” covers an area of ​​about six hectares. A truly Russian landscape adds special charm: on the one hand, a mixed forest surrounds the base, where it is pleasant to stroll on a sunny day, and on the other, a huge water area, the mouth of the South River.

The territory is well equipped, every corner is thought out and landscaped. There are special paths for walking, rollerblading, bicycles. In addition, there are many specialized platforms for active games, sports and other entertainment. In winter and summer you will truly enjoy your stay at the Laguna South base.

Holiday Accommodation

Despite the large territory, the management of the base "Laguna South" is not chasing the number of tourists. The maximum number of guests is 120 people. All people are different, and therefore vacationers are provided with several cottages to choose from. For those who come with their family, there are three cottages with double rooms. They are called the elements: "Water", "Earth", "Air". All rooms are made of wood, so they provide comfort and warmth at any time of the year. The delicate woody aroma blends wonderfully with the natural environment. Each of the cottages has its own unique design: so, for newlyweds and couples in love, they often offer "Air". Round four-poster beds and pastel-colored décor create a special, romantic atmosphere.





Each room is equipped with a bathroom, comfortable furniture, TV and kitchen utensils. Guests at the Laguna Yug recreation center can make tea at any time or reheat snacks in the microwave. There is a minibar. All rooms have a separate entrance from the street.

A large family can book a cozy two-story individual cottage "Teremok". On the ground floor you can get together as a family. It has a living room with fireplace, modern furniture, a fully equipped dining room and a kitchen. The second floor is occupied by 2 bedrooms and a bathroom. Cottages are in great demand among visitors to the base "Laguna South".

Are you going to a corporate party or want to celebrate your birthday with a big and fun company? Especially for you there is a cottage "House by the Water", which can simultaneously accommodate 18 people. There are all conditions for a comfortable stay: there is an equipped kitchen and a large dining area, a living room with a huge plasma panel, a fireplace and a pool table. A set of board games will provide an opportunity to have fun in a friendly company. The second floor is six separate double bedrooms. You can take sleeping bags (by agreement with the administrator), and then the "House by the Water" will accommodate a much larger number of people.

A nice addition is the perfect cleanliness of the rooms, the availability of fresh towels (3 for each guest), bedding, shampoos, shower gels and bathing caps. Room cleaning is done during the absence of vacationers daily.

Infrastructure

Having a good car park makes your vacation more mobile. Arriving by car, you get the opportunity to explore the surroundings, visit neighboring recreation centers. Lovers of beach vacations are waiting for the cleanest beaches, equipped places for swimming and fishing. Adults and children enjoy having fun in the water park. Boats and catamarans are moored on the shore, those who wish can ride them at any time.

If you enjoy outdoor activities, if your family has children, then be sure to come here. Laguna Yug is a recreation center that takes care of the entertainment of its guests. There are equipped areas for volleyball, tennis and basketball, on the territory there are tracks for roller-skating and bicycles. You will receive all the necessary sports equipment at the base; you will not need to carry anything with you.

Parachuting or having a romantic hot air balloon ride is an adventure you will remember for years to come.

In the evening, a cozy cafe opens where you can spend time quietly; for the most active, there is a disco.

A luxurious playground has been created for children, divided into several zones, so that children of any age feel comfortable and safe.

Nutrition



The cooking here is really very tasty. Laguna Yug Chalet offers its guests a wide selection of dishes prepared with fresh and high-quality products. The system of three meals a day is operating, a buffet is practiced. Separately, a children's menu is offered. For example, for breakfast you will be offered a couple of types of cereals, scrambled eggs, casserole and sliced ​​(cheese, sausage). For lunch - several types of the first and second, a variety of side dishes and snacks. Great herbal tea is a signature drink. The bar always has a selection of wines, beers and classic tinctures. The all-inclusive system eliminates the need to pay extra.

The service in the dining room is excellent, the staff is friendly and welcoming, always ready to help with a choice, answer questions, replace a dish if something is not pleasant. Since Laguna Yug (Nizhny Novgorod Region) is located close to the city, you can go for dinner outside the base for a change, but since excellent food is already included in the price, this is not very necessary.

Entertainment

The bath complex is presented here in all kinds of variations. A Japanese bath made in folk traditions helps to carry out a whole ritual of relaxation and rest. There is also a steam room with fragrant brooms, and a phytobarrel with medicinal herbs. Local experts will tell you the traditional subtleties: ofuro - these are oak boxes with heated sawdust and sea stones, furaco - oak barrels on an open veranda with hot and icy water.

The Laguna Yug base (Nizhny Novgorod region) is located almost in the center of Russia, so a vacation without a Russian steam bath with an ice bath, a relaxation room and a massage room would be unthinkable!

Communication with animals

You will be pleased with your own stable “Laguna Yug” - a recreation center, where handsome horses live, with shiny hair and a luxurious mane. They are looked after by professional instructors who know everything about horses and are completely in love with their job. They will tell you about the character traits of pets, different breeds, teach you the basics of equestrian sports and give you the opportunity to organize your own horseback riding.

The Laguna Yug recreation complex contains a small living area where your children get to know rabbits and help feed a goat, burro, ferrets, raccoon, ground squirrels, badger, fox and other interesting animals. On the river live domestic geese, who will gladly accept a gift in the form of bread crumbs.





Summer rest

The ideal way to get rid of the worldly bustle is to take a walk in the forest, pick mushrooms and berries, breathe fresh air and listen to birds. The local forest is light and clean, consists of conifers and birches. In addition, the “Laguna South” on the Gorky Sea will give you unforgettable water adventures, starting from swimming and visiting the water park, and ending with a walk along the reservoir on a yacht.

On the surface of the water you will find many more entertainments. Skiing, bananas and scooters will make your holiday bright and unforgettable. There is a hanging town all year round, which attracts children and adults. It is a climbing wall and a whole maze of suspension bridges. A safety net is pulled down from below for safety, which gently catches the one that has broken off.

The territory is stylized as a Russian village with mills, bridges, small backwaters and lush flower beds, so evening walks will bring a lot of pleasure.

Winter holidays

In winter, an ice circuit runs on the South River . Want to take extreme driving courses? There is a great opportunity to try! Instructors will show you the basics of ice fishing, ride a snowmobile and a winter banana. Snow slopes are ideal for skiing, and the flooded skating rink opens the door to those who wish to go ice skating. On cold days, a hot bath and a pool are very relevant.





Activities for your children

The service "caring nanny" is provided for your kids. With an experienced teacher, you can safely leave your child and have a good time with your soulmate.

Interesting classes in the "House of Masters" will captivate older children. Various classes are held here every day: drawing, modeling, painting on wood and making crafts.





In the games room, you can have plenty of fun in the pool with balls or build a huge house from soft designers. In summer, children ride roller skates and bicycles, in winter - on skis, skates and snowmobiles. Every night discos are held, and in the afternoon animated performances.

A children's camp opens here for the holidays. Magnificent outdoor recreation will give the guys “Laguna South”. Nizhny Novgorod is famous for its beautiful nature, huge forests and vast reservoirs. Children's birthdays spent here become unforgettable.

Weddings on the Gorky Sea

The same type of ceremony in restaurants has long bothered everyone, but here you will be offered completely exclusive services. Marriage registration on the seashore under a magnificent arch, a banquet in the fresh air, a romantic boat trip or a hot air balloon ride - all this can be provided to you by the Laguna South base. Feedback from couples who spent their most important day here is filled with heartfelt gratitude. It is impossible to survive so many warm moments celebrating a wedding in the city.

Room for the young is decorated with a special taste. All the days spent here after the wedding, the abundance of flowers and snow-white decoration of the rooms will remind you of the brightest day.

Laguna South: how to get there



The most convenient way is to travel by train. If there are no direct flights from your city to Nizhny Novgorod, then choose a direction with a transfer in Moscow.

The situation is similar with regular buses: all the nearest cities are connected by direct communication with Nizhny Novgorod. For those who value time and do not want to spend much time on the road, there are airlines.

Excellent car routes make it possible to safely travel by car. You can get from Moscow in about 5-6 hours. There will be no problems with parking for vehicles.

The south is a navigable river, so it is possible to get there by boat or yacht.

Rest at the recreation center "Laguna South" - this is an unlimited amount of entertainment, excellent cuisine, comfortable rooms and professional staff. If you decide to spend your holidays in Russia, then it is better not to find a place.
