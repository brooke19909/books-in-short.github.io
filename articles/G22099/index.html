How to download ringtone to smartphone?

You bought yourself a new phone and think what ringtone to put on the call? Standard sounds are no longer in fashion, moreover, you can set your favorite song or something fun as a ringtone - technology allows. This will cheer you up every time someone calls, and maybe others. But how to download a ringtone to your phone? Now let's figure it out.

Android or iOS

First you need to figure out what operating system your smartphone is running on. Currently, the most popular platforms are Android and IOS. IOS exclusively uses iPhones and other Apple devices. A huge number of gadgets are released on Android; most manufacturers work with it because of its simplicity and accessibility. These are the smartphones of Samsung, Fly, Alcatel, Lenovo, Sony, Huawei, now popular Xiaomi and others.

For a Russian person, Android is a more convenient and acceptable operating system, since most of the phone‚Äôs functions will be completely free. But on the "iPhone" to put a melody will already cost money. By the way, there are phones on another platform called Windows Mobile. It is similar to Android, but only a few work on it, such as Nokia and HTC.

Instructions for downloading a ringtone for Android

In this case, everything is pretty simple. You can put a whole song on a call, as well as a piece of it (ringtone). To do this, download the file to your phone and select the desired track in the sound profile settings. Songs can be downloaded from many resources on the Internet, "thrown" from a computer or laptop via a USB cable or via Bluetooth.

The easiest way, of course, is to download a ringtone on the Web. To do this, type in the desired song in any search engine through the browser and download it to the device. There are special sites that offer you to download already chopped ringtones and comic tunes, for example, "E-mine, mom calls!", And all in that spirit. It is completely free and takes a couple of minutes. On Windows Phone, the algorithm is almost the same.

How to download ringtone for iPhone?

Everything is a bit more complicated here. Just download the song and put on the call will not work. To do this, go to the iTunes application, which is installed by default on each iPhone. In the search you can find the desired song or melody, download it. This service is paid and costs from 15 rubles and above - the cost of each track is individual. Money is debited from the card that you need to attach to your AppleId account. After the ringtone is loaded, it can be selected as a ringtone in the corresponding settings from the library.

By the way, there are in iTunes and free ringtones and songs. There are few of them, but they can be found in the "Free" tab.
