Marabout Aquapark - Excellent Value for Money



Description: Marabout Aquapark 3 * - a cozy hotel located on one of the private beaches in the central part of Sousse. It is perfect for leisure as a youth company, and families with children. It is 30 kilometers from Enfid Airport . In addition, the city of Monastir, which is only 15 kilometers away, and Port El Kantaoui are located nearby. The area on which the hotel rises is surrounded by a palm grove. Its building - a four-story building - is completely restored. In addition, the price is fully consistent with the quality of service. Nearby are many attractions, restaurants and nightclubs. Nearby are the Stade Olympique and the casino.

Rooms: For your holiday marabout aquapark offers to stay in one of 257 rooms. All of them are spacious and comfortable, decorated in contrasting colors. Almost everyone has a balcony (double occupancy), and those without a balcony are much larger (quadruple). Most rooms have sea views. All have air conditioning. Cable TV, satellite TV. There is a bath or shower and necessary toiletries. Installed telephone, desktop.



Meals: Marabout Aquapark offers its guests the following types of food: breakfast only (BB), half board (HB), full board (FB), served in the main restaurant of the hotel. Local drinks are available throughout the day. There is also a bar, snack bar and Moorish cafe.

Beach: It has its own sandy beach. Please note that beach towels are not issued.

Additional Information: For those who have chosen to stay Marabout Aquapark, a number of services are provided. There is a car park and parking. If necessary, you can rent a car. For those who want to be attractive and relax, there is a hairdresser. In the evening concerts are held, a disco.

Throughout the day, you can swim in the outdoor pool, there is a jacuzzi, a sauna and Turkish bath, and an indoor pool. In addition, the wellness center has a professional massage therapist who provides excellent relaxing massages. A cosmetologist works here, you can improve your skin with nourishing and moisturizing masks.



Marabu Waterpark Tunisia has not forgotten about children. For them, there is an outdoor pool and a great playground.

For outdoor enthusiasts, a number of sports activities are offered, including surfing, a scooter, a parachute, and a catamaran. On the territory there is a water park, a gym. You can also play volleyball, basketball, mini-golf, billiards or tennis.

Marabout aquapark For convenience, invites guests to use the safe installed at the reception. Here you can leave your valuables for the entire stay at the hotel. There is also a separate luggage room.

Reviews: According to numerous reviews, the hotel is fully consistent with its class. The service is excellent and the rooms and grounds are always kept clean. A huge amount of entertainment on site. Attendants are polite and attentive to their guests. Vacationers respond positively about nutrition.
