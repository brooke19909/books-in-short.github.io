Partenita Hotels in Crimea

The most prestigious resort on the southern coast of Crimea is Partenit. Partenit hotels are famous for European service, comfortable rooms, a wide range of services offered. All the hotels of this resort village of a high level.

Hotels of Partenit

The small and cozy village of Partenit is located on the Crimean coast, between Yalta and Alushta. Partenit hotels attract tourists with tranquility and comfort. The resort coast is simply buried in an abundance of tropical greenery, which saturates the air with oxygen. Of the attractions in the village of Partenit, there is only Aivazovsky Park, Ayu-Dag (mountain), Museum of Stone and Dolphinarium. Infrastructure facilities are quite modest: a local mini-market, several shops, cafes, restaurants and tour agencies, where you can book an excursion to Simferopol, Yalta and Alushta.





On the neat streets of Partenita is always clean, quiet and calm. There are practically no private hotels and holiday homes. For tourists on this coast, there are two large sanatoriums and luxury hotel complexes. Partenit hotels are new buildings, built in an unusual architectural design, with clean and well-groomed territory, a high level of comfort in the rooms.

Hotel complex "Europe"

Hotel "Europe" (Partenit) is a unique design project combining the traditions of the East and modern Europe. The hotel is located along the seashore, the view of which opens from absolutely every room. The highlight of Europe is the panoramic elevator, which offers stunning views of the sea landscape.

The infrastructure of Europe includes the following facilities:

  • a restaurant of European and Italian cuisine, grill and lounge bars;
  • gym;
  • beauty parlor;
  • parking;
  • conference hall;
  • pool;
  • children's room for games.




The hotel's own beach (pebbles), equipped with sunbeds and awnings. The comfortable rooms are equipped with air conditioning, telephone and LED TV. The price includes: meals (3 meals a day), use of sun loungers and umbrellas on the beach, use of the gym at certain times, animation, use of the parking lot, swimming pool, access to the game room, and Internet access.

Hotel Nord

Hotel "Nord" (Partenit) is located on the Black Sea coast in a mountain valley. Rest in the hotel will appeal to both lovers of a relaxing holiday, and tourists who prefer active activities. For guests, horseback riding in the mountains, boat trips along the coast and excursions to the sights of Crimea are organized here upon request .

There are health parks near the hotel. They are famous for their rich flora and unique haircuts of plants that quietly pass into the vegetation of Ayu-Dag Mountain. In total there are more than 500 species of various plants. Accompanied by a guide, visitors can visit the bays in secluded places of Ayu-Dag, they can be reached by land or by sea.





Hotel Nord (Partenit) has 33 rooms for accommodation. All of them are equipped with modern stylish furniture, equipped with everything necessary (refrigerator, TV, telephone, hairdryer) and shower cabins.

Hotel infrastructure:

  • hall for a banquet with 20 seats;
  • sauna;
  • bathhouse;
  • indoor pool;
  • table tennis;
  • tennis courts.

The beach at the hotel is rented, located near the hotel (200 m) and belongs to the Crimea resort. Hotel Nord offers free use of sun loungers. The cost of the trip includes three meals a day, the use of the pool, bath or sauna (by appointment), car parking, a playground and a games room, a visit to the gym.

Hotel Flamingo

The Flamingo Hotel (Partenit) is located at the foot of Ayu-Dag Mountain. Rooms - 14 comfortable rooms of various categories. The rooms have all the necessary amenities: air conditioning, refrigerator, free internet access, TV, shower. Five hundred meters are two beaches (hotel property). Guests can visit the spa, hammam, Finnish bath, fitness center or spend time in the special room for board games. The organization of excursions.





For business meetings, presentations or seminars, the hotel has a conference room. Dishes of European and Russian cuisine can be tasted in the buffet, where the catering is organized.

All Partenit hotels are located in picturesque places with a mild climate, which is recommended for healing the body.
