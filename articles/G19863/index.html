Courmayeur ski resort: location, overview of hotels and restaurants, description of the ski area, reviews

Courmayeur ski resort in Italy was known in the eighteenth century. It was a resting place for aristocrats. At first, the resort was famous for the healing property of thermal springs, and then - for the ski slopes, which stretched more than a hundred kilometers. Most of them are designed for experienced skiers and professionals. In the article we find out where the fashionable resort is located, how to get there and how it is interesting for tourists.

History

The sources of thermal waters of Courmayeur are known from the era of Ancient Rome. In 1834, baths were erected on this site for the rest of the monarchs, their family members and the aristocracy. Today, warm pools with healing water in the open air against the backdrop of snow-capped peaks are a true exotic of these places.

Due to the location of the Mont Blanc mountains in this place, skiers gathered in Courmayeur. This gave impetus to the creation of the first school of guides. Today there are two of them. In the forties of the last century, the first cableway was built. This was the beginning of one of the most prestigious ski resorts.

Location

The resort of Courmayeur is located in Italy in the Val da Ost region, which is called the "Celestial Empire", on the border with France. At this point, the Mont Blanc Mountains rise 4807 metersÔºâ and Monte Rosa 4634 meters. At their foot is the Courmayeur ski resort.

One of the most fashionable places of active recreation in Italy looks like a traditional alpine town. Narrow streets and stone houses have not changed their appearance since the time of Napoleon Bonaparte. Nevertheless, the resort of Courmayeur is famous for its many fashionable shops, chic restaurants and hotels. It is not for nothing that mainly wealthy representatives of society rest here. sights

Five kilometers from the town is the Pre San Didier thermal complex, which operates all year round. Its waters are rich in iron and help in the treatment of rheumatism, cardiovascular diseases and skin problems.

At an altitude of 2173 meters, there is a botanical garden where you can take a walk and enjoy the beauty of the landscape. The medieval tower of Malluquin is another curious attraction of the resort. It will be interesting to visit the mountain museum, which is located at Piazza Henry 2.

Restaurants

Most of Courmayeur's catering establishments are concentrated on the main street of Via Roma. Almost all the best cafes are open until 22 hours. In order to visit one of these establishments on Friday or at the weekend, it is recommended to book a table in advance. On such days, the influx of visitors is especially great.

Prices in Courmayeur restaurants are reasonably reasonable. This applies to both dishes and alcohol. A bottle of beer costs around 1-2 EUR, and champagne - about 28 EUR. You can find restaurants where a full set lunch will cost no more than 4 EUR. There are no establishments with traditional Russian cuisine here. Since the resort is among the expensive, restaurants with low prices are represented in smaller numbers.

The ski season starts from December and lasts until April. The resort of Courmayeur is surrounded by beautiful mountain peaks. It is more suitable for experienced skiers. There is the possibility of skiing at night. At the service of vacationers there are thirty ski lifts.

The tracks are perfected. They begin from sky-high heights and descend along steep slopes. In whatever condition the tracks were in the evening, in the morning they are perfectly flat and dense.

The ski area consists of two slopes: the left (Chekruit) includes tracks of moderate difficulty, and the right (Val Veni) is high. They are all laid in a forest area. It is mostly sunny, which makes it doubly pleasant to ride.

According to tourists' reviews, there are not too many pistes for professionals, and Courmayeur ski resort creates many opportunities for experienced mid-level skiers. The red tracks are a bit simple. Noteworthy is the Internazionale Medium Difficult Track. Nice downhills Youla and Lazey.

According to reviews of the Courmayeur ski resort, it can be concluded that for beginners, this place is quite complicated. For them, only a descent in the region of the peak of Mot Chetif is suitable. In general, the reviews are positive. Vacationers speak positively about the lack of queues for the lifts, but note that in the evenings in the town it is boring, not enough entertainment facilities.

activity

At the Courmayeur ski resort, each season opening is accompanied by colorful action. The tradition is to close the holiday with the descent of professional skiers with lighted torches. It looks so impressive that it usually causes applause.

A procession is organized on Valentine's Day, and in the spring a Carnival is organized.

In the Christmas holiday season, Santa Claus wears a chimney sweep suit, descends from Monte Bianchi and gives gifts to children.

Courmayeur ski resort is a vacation spot where every tourist will find something interesting for himself and spend time with pleasure.
