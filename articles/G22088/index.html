The capital of Burgundy is the charming city of Dijon

Burgundy is one of the most famous gastronomic areas in France. But these lands are popular not only due to vineyards, but also to other beautiful places and sights. The capital of Burgundy is Dijon. It is also one of the most beautiful French cities.

Short description of the city

The capital of Burgundy stands out for its stylistic design. In Dijon you can see charming classic French houses and cathedrals made in the Gothic style, a combination of Renaissance architecture and modern trends.

Walking along the well-groomed streets, you can admire the old mansions and multi-colored roofs. Colored roofs are a distinctive feature of Burgundy. They are made of colored terracotta, covered with glaze and laid out in a geometric pattern. The capital of Burgundy is famous for its mustard, which it is called Dijon, whose production began in 1856.

Dijon is also known for blackcurrant liquor, which is the main component of the Cyrus cocktail. Felik Cyrus served as mayor of Dijon, he liked the drink of blackcurrant and white wine, so this cocktail was named after him.

In Dijon, you can admire its other attractions. The capital of Burgundy has a special charm, which is obtained through a mixture of different architectural styles.

Attractions Dijon

Getting to know the capital of Burgundy is best done with Place Darcy. It was built in the 18th century. In the 12th century, Dijon was surrounded by a fortress wall, and one of the gates was located in this city. In the 18th century, the gates of Guillaume were installed, and behind the square is the Darcy Garden, which is the property of the capital of Burgundy.

The main street of the city is Freedom Street, where shops and old mansions are located, many of which are architectural heritage. One of the main attractions of the capital of Burgundy are the cathedrals. St. Venigne Cathedral is one of the monuments of France and the property of Dijon. This cathedral was built in the Gothic style.

Also Dijon's attraction is the church of St. Philibert, which was built in the 12th century. It is the only building in the city of Dijon built in the Romanesque style. In 1862, the church was included in the list of historical monuments of France. Also in Dijon there are a large number of museums that display interesting exhibits.

Museums

If you like art, then visit the Museum of Fine Arts. There you can see an extensive collection of exhibits from the Middle Ages. This museum also has the work of local and Flemish artists, and on the top floor are the works of the Impressionists.

In the natural science museum you can see exhibits of animals, museums, as well as visit the planetarium. The archaeological museum houses rare unique ancient artifacts. It is located in a magnificent room made in the Romanesque style.

What to bring?

Of course, you can bring postcards or other souvenir trifles, but it is best to purchase something authentic than Dijon and Burgundy itself are famous.

  1. Dijon mustard. If you do not know what kind of taste to choose, it is best to take a set of assorted.
  2. Burgundy wines. Tasting and buying these wines is a must for a Burgundy holiday program. The best wines in the world are made in this region. You can buy this drink at affordable prices, as well as more expensive items.
  3. Cassis blackcurrant liquor. You can make a pleasant and refined cocktail "Cyrus" at home by mixing this liquor with white wine.
  4. Get something with an image of an owl, which is a symbol of the city.
  5. Burgundy is known not only for wines, but also for sparkling wines that look like champagne. They are called Cremant, there is even a special route past the wineries where they are made.
  6. Also one of the famous symbols of Burgundy are anise sweets, which are made in one small village located in a picturesque place of Flavigny-sur-Ozren.
  7. Nut-based vegetable oil.

Burgundy is one of the main gastronomic areas of the country, so it is best to bring delicious gifts from the trip.

French castles

These are one of the most beautiful treasures of architecture. Previously, they served not only as the residence of noble persons, but also reflected their greatness. One of the most famous French castles is Versailles, located near Paris. It was the residence of the French kings and became one of the most magnificent palaces in the world.

Also one of the most beautiful castles is Chambord, which is located in the Loire Valley. Chambord Castle is one of the best creations of the Renaissance. In the Loire Valley is another famous castle - Chenonceau, which was a reflection of the Enlightenment with its original design and decoration.

What to see in France? These are not only castles, but also cathedrals, museums, parks. Each region of the country has unique attractions that will immerse tourists in the magical atmosphere of France.
