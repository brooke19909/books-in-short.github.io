Minsk, hotel "Yubileinaya": description, photos, reviews of tourists

Planning a trip to Minsk? Not sure where to stay? The Yubileinaya Hotel is an excellent option for a comfortable stay in the capital.

Hotel "Yubileinaya". Minsk and central attractions





In the very center of the city for more than forty-five years, a three-star hotel has been hosting guests and residents of Belarus. Just a few meters from it are the Sports Palace, the Nemiga shopping center, the Moscow cinema, as well as a huge number of cafes, bars and restaurants. From the upper floors of the hotel offers a wonderful view of the Svisloch River and the Trinity Suburb, or, as the people of Minsk call it, the Old Town.

Staying at Yubileinaya, on the Svisloch embankment you can rent a catamaran or a small boat for romantic walks. If you prefer a more active holiday, then you will not have to be bored; you can easily rent a bicycle or roller skates and ride along the river along the bike path.

In the evening, in summer cafes or nearby restaurants, you can treat yourself to delicious dishes of Belarusian, Asian and European cuisine. And for fans of incendiary dances and modern music, the Overtime, Puck, and Madison nightclubs, which Minsk is famous for even abroad, work near the hotel. The Yubileinaya Hotel also boasts a strip bar.





If you want to spend time in a more relaxing environment, you can visit the hookah bar open until the morning, located in the Trinity suburb. And in winter, one of the largest ice rinks in the capital runs across the road from the hotel. Due to its rather good location, the Yubileinaya Hotel is very popular.

Room categories and their description

Tourism development is included in the list of the main tasks of the Republic of Belarus, therefore, every year the requirements for the level of service and hotel service increase. Capital hotels are in a hurry to meet all the standards of the international hotel business, therefore every year there are more and more people who want to visit Minsk. The Yubileinaya Hotel provides a choice of 239 rooms of both economy and business categories:

  • Standard economy rooms. Both single and double occupancy are possible. The room has everything you need for a comfortable stay.
  • Superior rooms (updated). Single or double occupancy, air conditioning, shower.
  • Two-room and three-room suites. Comfortable rooms with a large double bed, a separate sitting area and a bathroom (bath, hairdryer, slippers, bathrobe).
  • Junior suite for newlyweds.
  • Rooms for people with disabilities.




The modern design of the rooms will not leave indifferent even the most demanding travelers. Depending on the category, rooms in the hotel are equipped with a refrigerator, minibar, telephone and TV. For an additional fee, you can use the safe in your room and ensure the safety of personal belongings. In addition, the hotel provides wireless Internet access.





Services provided by the hotel for an additional fee

At their discretion, hotel guests can take advantage of a wide range of services for an additional fee:

  • Secure parking throughout the day. You can leave your car in the place assigned to you and not worry about its safety.
  • Restaurant "Anniversary". Opening hours: 07.00-24.00. Breakfast, lunch, dinner. Holding celebrations, ordering banquets.
  • Express bar with a wide range of soft drinks, hot drinks, as well as alcoholic drinks of local and foreign production. Opening hours: 08.00-23.00.
  • Bar "Sabrina". Opening hours: 2 p.m. - 3 p.m.
  • Strip-bar "Texas". Opening hours: 20.00-06.00. Daily show program starting at 23.00.
  • Bowling. 4 game tracks, the bar is designed for 24 visitors. Possibility of early booking.
  • Meeting room for about 200 people. Holding conferences using modern technical means.
  • Meeting room for up to 20 people.
  • Laundry and dry cleaning.
  • Barbershop.
  • Car rental.




In addition to the main list of paid services for guests of the capital, assistance in organizing excursions in Belarus or the city of Minsk from the administration of the Yubileinaya Hotel is possible. Professional guides will introduce you to the sights of the city or to the historical places of Belarus. Excursions are group and individual both in Russian and in foreign languages.

Rules of settlement and eviction

According to international standards, check-in of guests arriving at the hotel can be done until 14.00, and check-out time is limited to 12.00 hours. 2 hours are allotted for preparing and cleaning the room. If for some reason you do not want to leave your room, then for a fee you can extend your stay at the hotel to a time convenient for you. We warn you that this service is possible only if there are free rooms. However, it is worth taking care of this in advance so that the personnel responsible for the reservation can redistribute the reserved hotel rooms.

Distance from the airport

The hotel lies 35 km away from Minsk airport. The Yubileinaya Hotel is ready to provide you with a transfer service for an additional fee: meeting the guest at the airport and accompanying him to the hotel reception. Since the international airport is located outside the capital, this service will help you avoid unforeseen situations and save your time. In order to use the transfer service, you need to contact the hotel and discuss all the details of your arrival.

Nevertheless, there are more economical options. To get to the hotel on your own, you can use the public transport services, city buses go from the airport to the Moskovsky bus station every 30 minutes.
