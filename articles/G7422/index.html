Stavropol Territory: attractions. The most interesting places in Stavropol

Essentuki, Kislovodsk, Pyatigorsk - these and other cities leave unforgettable memories for tourists visiting the Stavropol Territory. Sights and natural beauties for every taste, the opportunity to improve health, engage in extreme sports and have a great rest - all this awaits travelers. Where to start exploring this wonderful place?

City of Stavropol: what to see

Of course, to start exploring this rich in interesting places territory is with the administrative center. The city of Stavropol was born from a fortress built at the end of the 18th century. The new settlement as soon as possible became the center of economic activity of the North Caucasus. The first people appeared on this earth much earlier. The most ancient archaeological finds date back to the Eneolithic era.





You can not visit the city of Stavropol and refrain from a visit to Central Park, spread over 12 hectares. There are many legends associated with this attraction of the administrative center. Once upon a time, such famous personalities as Pushkin and Griboedov loved to breathe air, admire chestnut alleys and flower arrangements. Do not go past flower beds created in honor of the heroes of the war.

It is easy to combine a walk with a visit to the zoo, stopping at Victory Park, where interesting monuments are located. Stavropol is especially proud of the size of this attraction, occupying about 200 hectares. Parents will be able to ride toddlers and older children on attractions, which are about 30.

Other interesting places

Fans of natural beauty must visit the Stavropol Territory. The sights of the administrative center are not only monuments and parks, but also wildlife museums. Various species of reptiles, fish, birds await tourists in the exotarium.





Guests of the city who are interested in the events of past centuries will be able to admire the Fortress Hill, recognized as the historical core of Stavropol, take a picture opposite the monument to Suvorov. Or take a walk along the German bridge, completely made of shell rock, because in the process of its creation, concrete was practically not used. The construction was preserved from the railway, which was built in the pre-war years.

Churches, cathedrals - Stavropol Territory is rich in majestic religious buildings . Sights and at the same time places for communication with God are found literally at every step. For example, the Cathedral of St. Andrew the First-Called, belonging to the pseudo-Russian style, the construction of which dates from 1897. Or the Temple of the Kazan Icon of the Mother of God, built in the middle of the 19th century. The reconstruction of the cathedral, destroyed, began in 2004.

Where to go in Pyatigorsk

Such interesting buildings as the Temple of the Kazan Icon of the Mother of God can be seen not only in Stavropol. Pyatigorsk is a destination that invariably attracts many tourists who come here not only for the sake of improving their health. In addition to 40 mineral springs, the city has other advantages.





Once here, you should definitely visit the Pyatigorsk necropolis, which has collected many historical monuments that glorified the Stavropol Territory. The sights of this place are the burial places of war heroes, famous figures of art, science. Initially, it was here that the poet Lermontov was once buried.

The Flower Garden Park, created in 1830, deserves attention due to the beautiful Diana's Grotto, built in honor of the first conquest of Elbrus. Lermontov Gallery, Ermolovskie baths will appeal to guests. You will be able to see the bicentennial field maple adjacent to the three hundred year old oaks in the Kirov Park.

Tourists who are occupied by the architecture of past centuries will have a good time at the Museum of Local Lore, located in the Mikhailov Hotel. The building has rare archaeological collections, interesting specimens of weapons, firearms and cold are also presented.

Essentuki City: what to visit

Pyatigorsk is far from the only place that lures travelers to the Stavropol Territory. Cities like the settlement of Essentuki also cannot be deprived of attention. First of all, a visit to this area is shown to people who are worried about health problems.





Mineral springs of Essentuki is a find of many years ago made by the Cossack army. After a study conducted by Professor Nelyubin in the early 18th century, healing water began to be actively used in treatment. A total of 28 sources were found, some of them intended for drinking, others for procedures, not only medical, but also cosmetic.

Anti-aging and restorative procedures are not all the reasons to visit the Stavropol Territory. The cities that grew up on its territory are famous for their beautiful buildings, Essentuki is no exception. The resort park falls in love with visitors with an original fountain, "immersed" in a spacious pool with miniature bowls floating in it. Especially spectacularly, the water focusing on various levels looks in the evening when the time comes for lanterns.

Zero kilometer of love, located on Theater Square, attracts many couples. If you believe the legend, lovers who visited this place may not be afraid of a break in relations.

What to see in Kislovodsk

When choosing a city or village in the Stavropol Territory for recreation, it is worth thinking about this place, rich in attractions. Kislovodsk fortress - the building that laid the foundation for the city. The gates that the building possesses are recognized as a symbol of the settlement, are even depicted on its coat of arms. Here, visitors will find a local history museum that will help them get fascinating information about the development of the region and the country.





Lermontov Rock is a must-see place for everyone who likes Princess Mary. It was she who became the territory on which the legendary battle of Grushnitsky and Pechorin took place. The rock captivates with a bizarre shape, beautifully framed by grass and shrubs. By the way, nearby archaeologists discovered traces of ancient settlements.

Chaliapin's dacha is the place where the famous opera artist spent many pleasant days. Its real owner in those days was the merchant Ushakov, who leased the building. Roerich‚Äôs murals, Art Nouveau style, rare art exhibits - all this awaits visitors to the sights.

Mountains of the Stavropol Territory

Visitors to Pyatigory must definitely get to the outskirts of the city. There they will be able to admire such a natural work as Iron Mountain. It is a volcano, the magma of which failed to break through to the surface. Its name is also associated with local water, which has a rusty color, provided by precipitation. Iron Mountain is buried in coniferous-deciduous trees. You can spend time with health benefits by tasting water from sources.





An equally fascinating sight is the highest point of the Stavropol Upland. This is the mountain of Strizhament, whose height is about 830 meters. The name of the natural monument was given by the eponymous fortress, erected here at the end of the 17th century. Mount Strizhament recalls a trapezoidal massif endowed with a flat top. The dark forest located on its northern slope is easy to see from afar.

Golden Mound is also among the natural assets of the region. The height of the mountain reads over 880 m. According to legend, in the distant 14th century there was a tent of Emir Timur, covered with gilding.

Where to go in Zheleznovodsk

Visitors to this village in most cases are forced to tear between the bright sights. You can begin your acquaintance with the resort city from the Cascade Stairs, created at the beginning of the last century. Her visit will make a huge impression on children who like the numerous sculptures of fairy-tale characters. Neat flower beds framing the stairs, iridescent decorative lanterns enhance the effect.





Residents of Zheleznovodsk love the work of Pushkin, as evidenced by the monument to the great poet. The monument, reaching a height of two and a half meters, can be seen walking in the Pushkin Square. It is worth looking into the gallery, named after the poet, admiring its unusual architectural forms and elegant dome.

Tourists will never forget the majestic Palace of the Emir of Bukhara, decorated in an oriental style. It will seem to visitors that a bright building with an openwork blue fence looks at them from the pages of fairy tales about Scheherazade. Construction completed at the beginning of the last century.

Mineralnye Vody: what to see

Russia, Stavropol Territory is a route worth choosing for travelers who are ready to go sightseeing in virtually every city they meet. If guests of the resort are impressed by horses, they should definitely plan a visit to the Tersky stud farm. Animals of Terek breed on the territory of our country are bred exclusively here, the wealth of stripes is amazing.

The steam locomotive on the pedestal is a monument that the inhabitants of the city are proud of. Its construction was dedicated to a special date - the celebration of the fiftieth anniversary of the Stakhanov movement. It was this transport that once contributed to setting the record; its developer is the famous Pole Lopushinsky in this field.

Guests of the museum of local lore will be able to get acquainted with the main stages of the development of the city, receive new information in the field of paleontology, and improve their knowledge in the field of history and archeology. A whole section is allocated for exhibits preserved from the time of World War II.

Secrets of the Stavropol Territory

The Stavropol Territory is a territory, the study of which allowed scientists to discover dozens of ancient fortifications. Visitors to Budenovsk will be able to visit the true center of the Golden Horde. Once close to Kislovodsk, it is worth visiting the sites of our distant ancestors found in this place, which archaeologists give more than three thousand years. For example, the fortress of Majara deserves attention, especially the local museum, which stores valuable relics.

Extreme vacation

Guests of the Stavropol Territory, dreaming of an active pastime, chose the right route. Basically, "dangerous" sports have one way or another to conquer the mountains. Invariably popular with climbers, professional and beginners, enjoys the south, where the famous Caucasus Mountains are located. Great time, plenty of skiing, easy in Dombay.

Once in the Kochubeyevsky district, tourists can decide to rafting in the Kuban. The great thing is that the route is developed individually. There are many other rivers that provide the opportunity for extreme swimming. Hunters who will be able to indulge in their hobby ... on horses, with the support of hounds, will not get bored in Stavropol Territory. Trained horses are supplied by the Terek factory mentioned above.

Wellness tourism

No matter how wonderful the sights of the Stavropol Territory are, the possibility of improving health remains the main attraction for tourists. Mineralized therapeutic mud is offered by the resort, located on Lake Tambukan, bordering Pyatigorsk. Narzan, nicknamed the population "sour water", is waiting for travelers in Kislovodsk. Here you can swim in a narzan bath, visit the gallery of the same name. A visit to such a place as Essentuki will also be useful: the waters of the city are famous for their healing qualities.

Vacation with children

Should I take my son or daughter with me when I go to the Stavropol Territory? The answer to this question is unequivocal, as there will be entertainment for children of all ages. For example, guests of the administrative center will be able to bring their offspring to the Stavropol Zoo, where the most comfortable conditions are created for animals. Crocodiles, leopards, ostriches - animals for every taste are presented here.

Once having visited this sunny piece of land, everyone is eager to return there to see the remaining sights that are not covered: historical buildings, monuments. Stavropol and other cities of the region cannot be explored in a year.
