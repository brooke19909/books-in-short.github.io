Mangal zones, Chelyabinsk: rest with barbecue

Modern tourist suburban bases, among other things, usually offer their guests including well-equipped mangal zones. Chelyabinsk in this regard, of course, is no exception. The list of amenities offered to tourists at local bases in most cases includes, among other things, gazebos with braziers.

The best bases of Chelyabinsk

Of course, there are many popular tourist complexes with mangal zones near this large Ural city. Sufficiently comfortable recreation facilities are considered, for example:

  • Kurochkino complex;
  • rental club "Adrenaline";
  • Center "White Sail".




All these complexes offer their guests quite comfortable and inexpensive mangal zones. Chelyabinsk is a big city, and there are, of course, not only the recreation center itself, but also well-equipped beaches. For example, there is an excellent brazier zone in the Peschanka quarry, located 25 km from the city on the Bro Dokolamsky tract. The local beach is called Rainbow Trout.

Complex "Kurochkino"

This convenient base is located just 10 kilometers from the city by the eponymous artificial lake. You can get to it from Chelyabinsk in just 15-20 minutes. By car you need to move towards the village of Starokamyshinsk. There is a base on the western side of the lake.

Housing "Kurochkino" complex offers its guests quite comfortable. If desired, tourists can rent a room here in a two or four-bed cottage. The rooms in the houses are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay (irons, hair dryers, refrigerators, microwave ovens, safes, etc.).

Actually on the territory of the base itself there are: a parking, a sports equipment rental, a banquet hall, a playground, a pool, karaoke, a video room, and a shooting gallery. In winter, an ice rink is poured in the center, as well as snow slides.





Mangal zone of the base

Frying kebabs in the Kurochkino complex, according to most tourists, is very convenient. Many vacationers call this center a real barbecue park. There are just a huge number of arbors of various sizes and layouts. Indoor bungalows with barbecue facilities are also available at this base.

Those who wish to fry kebabs in Kurochkino can come, including simply light. Pickled meat, poultry and coal are sold directly at the base. There is, of course, in this complex a rental point for skewers and other necessary equipment. About about 1000 rubles in 12 hours - the arbor with the barbecue area costs so much in this center . Chelyabinsk is a city of metallurgists, and the salaries of its residents are quite high. So this price of tourists is usually not annoying.

Rent Club "Adrenaline"

It offers its residents and guests, and right in the city, convenient mangal zones of Chelyabinsk. The ski base "Adrenaline" is located in the 13th microdistrict of this metropolis. Her address: Komsomolsky prospect, 92.

Located on the territory of this rental club a total of 6 mangal arbors. Each of them is designed for 6-20 people. It is worth renting a gazebo, depending on its size, 250-400 rubles per hour. These structures for frying kebabs, according to most vacationers, are very comfortable. For the convenience of the guests of the base there are tables and benches. There is also electricity in the gazebo. So, in Chelyabinsk, who decided to fry kebabs in the Adrenaline club, problems with charging, for example, a phone or laptop will not arise.





Thus, this center of relaxation offers its guests comfortable mangal zones. Chelyabinsk is not a southern city. And therefore, many bases offer winter entertainment to its residents. "Adrenaline" in this regard is no exception. In the cold season, an ice town is established on the territory of the center. Guests of the base, who rented gazebos, can go to its territory for free.

Rainbow Trout Beach

The popularity of this recreation area with barbecue facilities among residents of Chelyabinsk is due primarily to its availability. It takes no more than 25 minutes to drive from the city center to the Rainbow Trout beach, located in the Peschanka quarry . Entrance to the lake for adults is paid here. Children are allowed to go to the beach freely.

Mangal zones based on the Rainbow Trout

Chelyabinsk residents, as already mentioned, like to relax in this place. Sand on the beach "Rainbow Trout", despite the fact that it is located on a quarry, is available only directly by the water. The rest of the coastline is densely covered with grass with wildflowers. Some tourists like it, others not so. At least picnic spots are really convenient here. Sand has the ability to fill in all the cracks and get rid of it in the future can be very difficult. Shish kebab on this beach can be fried, spread on soft grass. There are, of course, gazebos in the picnic area at the Rainbow Trout base.





Unfortunately, you can use barbecues here only for a fee. The same goes for car parking. This, of course, many tourists attribute to some of the shortcomings of the beach.
