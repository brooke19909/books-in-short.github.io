Hotel Holiday Inn Downtown 4 * UAE, Dubai: description, photos and reviews

Every year, holidays in the United Arab Emirates attract more and more tourists. Popular with tour operators is not only the capital of this state, but also the city of Dubai. Here, the construction of new facilities and complexes is constantly ongoing according to unique projects of the world's best architects and engineers. In addition, the existing buildings amaze the imagination of everyone who sees with their own eyes, which is worth at least the archipelago of artificial islands and the tallest building in the world. One of the hotels with a lot of positive reviews is Hotel Holiday Inn Downtown Dubai 4 *. About him, his features, the pros and cons of living in it will be discussed in this article.





Description

The eight-story building of the Holiday Inn Downtown 4 * hotel (UAE, Dubai), the photo of which you see in the article, appeared on this place in the beginning of the winter of 1999. In 2012, a large-scale restoration took place, its external and internal appearance was completely updated. Those who managed to compare the service before and after this event claim that the level of service has significantly increased. Today, the Holiday Inn Downtown Hotel has 139 cozy rooms that are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. Guests are provided with many additional paid and free services. The administration of the Holiday Inn Downtown hotel provides guests with complete security, video surveillance is carried out around the clock, the facility is under close attention of professionals. Every season tens of thousands of tourists from around the world stop here. All of them are ready to share their experiences, tell about their vacation. Usually the story is divided into several parts, and special attention is paid to the arrangement of rooms and maintenance.

Check-in is from 14:00, check-out is until 12:00. If tourists arrived at the Holiday Inn Downtown Dubai 4 * hotel (UAE, Dubai) before check-in time, they can leave their luggage in the luggage room and have a tasty lunch in a local restaurant or wait in the hotel lobby on soft comfortable sofas where the staff will offer a refreshing drink.

Hotel Location

The actual and legal address of the hotel: Al Rigga Street Deira, Dubai, UAE. It is located near the city center, on the third coastline. The distance to the sea is about 1000 m. The nearest airport is 20 km. Absolutely everyone who was relaxing in the Hotel Holiday Inn Downtown, noted that the hotel is extremely well located on the city map, because the main attractions and shopping centers, as well as the metro station and the oriental market are within walking distance from it. Nearby there are several restaurants, a hospital and a pharmacy.





How to get there

There are several ways to get to the hotel:

  • As part of a tourist group. Upon arrival, everyone is met by a bus pre-ordered from the hotel, which delivers vacationers to the place of their planned stay.
  • Alone by taxi. It is important to remember that landing in a taxi (from 06:00 to 22:00) costs 3 dirhams (local currency), at night - 3,5 dirhams. At the same time, taxi drivers on duty at the airport ask for a fare of 20 dirhams. The calculation of the amount payable on the counter depends on the distance that the vehicle has traveled (based on the assumption that 1 km of the route is from 1,5 dirhams). The hotel is 5 km from the international airport. You only need to choose cars equipped with a luminous TAXI sign on the roof, and women are not recommended to ride alone.
  • Alone on public transport. The metro station, which is located near the main entrance to the Holiday Inn, is called Al Rigga. You can get here directly from the airport (for independent tourists this method seems the most profitable and fast, since the airport is located within the city limits and if you have a small amount of baggage you can save a lot).

Do not try to catch a ride, in the UAE movement is thus prohibited (the exception is an emergency, which must be confirmed by either doctors or police officers).
