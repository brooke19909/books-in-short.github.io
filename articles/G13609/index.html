Waterpark in Omsk: photos, opening hours, reviews

AquaRio is a popular and beloved by the townspeople water park in Omsk. It is located on Zavertyaev Street. The current director is Anna Pyatkova. Near the building of the water entertainment center is located the sports complex "Azart".

The nearest public transport stop is Uspashnaya Station. Buses No. 11, 29, 47H, 72, 111, 140, 330P and minibuses No. 39, 94, 222, 276, 350, 414, 503 stop here.

Rides

Slides "AquaRio" (water park in Omsk) are designed for every taste. There are extreme and smooth descents:

  • "Free fall".
  • "Loop rocket."
  • Multiband.
  • "Cosmic hole."
  • "Compact Slide for children."
  • "Wave".
  • The bodyslide.

"Free fall"

The slide is designed for male bathers who have reached the age of 14. The growth of vacationers should exceed 160 centimeters. A distinctive feature of the descent is the minimum inclination of the open pipe. The sliding speed down this hill is maximum.

Loop Rocket

Adults and teenagers over 14 years old are allowed on this hill. Bathing height should be above 160 cm. The descent is closed, equipped with a translucent panel.

Multiband

This slide is a favorite of the regulars of the water park in Omsk. It is similar to those installed in water entertainment centers in Turkey, Bulgaria and Spain. Its design is a series of three multi-colored open slopes with high sides. Only adults are allowed to ride this hill. There is an age limit of 16 years. The minimum height of vacationers should be more than 140 cm.

"Space hole"

The famously twisted slide ends its journey in a closed sphere. In it, the movement gradually slows down, after which the bathers fall into the outdoor pool. To ride this hill in the best water park in Omsk, you must be over 14 years old and above 160 cm.

Compact Slide for Children

The descent is for schoolchildren. Bathers must be more than 6 years old, but less than 12 years old. There is a growth restriction for children. Those above 110 but below 150 cm are allowed on the hill.

"Wave"

A wide and open descent involves gliding on airbags that are designed for two or one bather. Children over 10 years old are allowed on the hill. Their height should be above 140 cm.

Bodyslide

The traditional swirling slide of the closed type is very popular among young visitors to this water park in Omsk. Photos serve as a clear confirmation of this. All teenagers who are 10 years old are allowed on it. Their height should be above 140 cm. Near the slide there are sun loungers for parents. The instructor monitors the order.

Aquacenter

The main entertainment pool consists of several segments. Wide steps with a rubberized coating lead to the main bowl. Directly from the pool you can go to the hydromassage zone or move to the jacuzzi. In the center of the aquazone fountains beat. The water temperature is 30 ¬∞ C.

Guests over 16 years old are allowed in the entertainment pool. All others must be accompanied by adults. Children who are shorter than 140 centimeters are required to wear life jackets.

In the children's pool, the water is much warmer. Its temperature is 31 ¬∞ C. Depth is very shallow. Visitors under 14 are allowed to enter it. Another pool is the Wave. Judging by the reviews of the Omsk water park, it is small, so on weekends it is very crowded. A lot of people are crowded, all interfere with each other. There is no such problem on weekdays. The pool simulates the excitement of the sea. The depth in it increases gradually. They let people over 16 years old into it. All others must be accompanied by adults. Life-saving vests are put on children whose height is below 140 centimeters. The water temperature is 29 ¬∞ C.

Welcome

Opening hours of the water park in Omsk "AquaRio":

  • From Monday to Thursday, the center is open from 12:00 to 21:00.
  • On Friday, the entrance to the complex is from 12:00 to 22:00.
  • On Saturday, Sunday and holidays, visitors are allowed from 10:00 to 22:00.

In June, the institution switches to a summer schedule. All days of the week, entrance to the water park is from 10:00 to 22:00.

Tariffs

The cost of a subscription for adults on weekdays is 1,000 rubles. The ticket is valid all day. Children under 17 years old get a 50% discount. Kids who are not yet 4 years old are allowed free of charge. The subscription provides an opportunity to visit the water park and use the services of the bath complex.

On weekends, the cost of admission for adults is 1,200 rubles. A visit to the water park alone will cost 650 rubles. One hour in the SPA zone costs 300 rubles. When staying in the water complex for more than two hours, each visitor receives a loan in the amount of 2,000 rubles. Payment for park services in the aquazone occurs through electronic bracelets that are issued at the entrance.

Comments

Almost all reviews about visiting AquaRio are positive in color. Clients of the water entertainment center like a large selection of slides and additional services offered by the bath complex. All rooms are warm and clean. The floor next to the pools is completely non-slip. It is covered with special soft rugs.

Several cafes operate at once in the aquacenter. The prices are affordable. The menu is standard for such establishments. These are juices, soft drinks, cookies, pastries. The staff and staff are friendly and welcoming. Workers are ready to help at any time. Instructors carefully carry out their duties.

Negative experience

Among the visitors of AquaRio there were also those who were disappointed by a trip to the water amusement park. According to them, in the last minutes of the work of the aquacomplex, the staff behaves inappropriately. Employees rush home, so they are rude and even allow themselves to shout at slow customers.

They say that in the complaint book there are a lot of such negative reviews. The administration has almost no reaction to them. In addition, there are comments on the flooring in the spa area. They clearly saved on it. It is not rubber like that which is next to the pools, so the floor is very slippery. People leaving the steam rooms are falling.

Sometimes there are queues for lockers in which visitors store clothes and personal items, plus cells are very narrow and small, it is extremely inconvenient to use them. Changing cabins are also constantly busy. It is necessary to queue in advance.

Alternative

In addition to AquaRio, other water entertainment centers operate in Omsk. List of water parks in Omsk:

  • "Pirate Island".
  • "Fairy tale".
  • "Political Department."

Pirate Island is located five kilometers from the city center. It is located in nature, therefore it works only in the warm season. Its pools are equipped with heating systems. The water temperature is 27 ¬∞ C. Waterpark "Fairy Tale" in Omsk operates year-round. This is a small complex in which there are only two slides.

It has a sports pool. There is a hydromassage area. There is a jacuzzi. Go down the hills can those whose height exceeds 150 centimeters. For children, a separate aquazone with a shallow pool and water attractions is equipped.

Waterpark "Politotdel" is part of the recreation center of the same name. It is fifty kilometers from Omsk. There is a water entertainment center all year round. The water temperature in the pools of the complex is 28 ¬∞ C. Indoor air warms up to 30 ¬∞ C. The administration of the water park assures that an ultraviolet irradiation unit and a filtration system are used to disinfect and disinfect water.

Visitors to the water complex can enjoy the services of the Finnish sauna, Turkish hammam and Russian steam rooms. In the SPA zone there is a pool filled with thermal water. There are fast food points, a bar with herbal cocktails and a restaurant.
