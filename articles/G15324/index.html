Need a shirt shirt (crochet)? Scheme and description of its elements

In bad weather, I want to shelter children from the cold as reliably as possible. But how to do it if mom is not around? For example, in a kindergarten or school. Where children dress independently and caregivers with teachers do not always follow how the scarf is tied. The shirt shirt will come to the rescue (crochet). Her scheme is most often simple and much less yarn is required than on a scarf.

What parts does the product consist of?

The answer will be the same, regardless of whether the shirt-front is sewn or crocheted. The scheme will always consist of two elements: a collar and a drape, connected together.

It can be wholly knitted or crocheted. It all depends on the skills of the needlewoman. Those who own both tools are in the best position. Then the collar can be tied with an elastic band on the knitting needles, and the pelerine can be made openwork with a hook.





How to tie a neck?

The most common is the shirt-front (crochet), the scheme of which is thought out so as to smoothly fit the neck and slightly expand to the bottom. She is knitted across. To do this, dial a chain of loops, which will provide a length from the clavicle to the very top of the neck. If the yarn is dense enough, then for an adult it will be loops 17. When you need a shirt for a girl (crochet), the pattern will consist of fewer loops. Therefore, the number of columns will have to be counted.

Continue to knit these two rows until the shirt-front (crochet), the diagram of which is given earlier, begins to completely wrap around the neck.





Possible Drape Pattern

With this detail, even the simplest shirt shirt for a girl becomes beautiful. Any hook scheme can be implemented. Here is an example of the one that every needlewoman will handle.

In the first row, evenly connect fans consisting of two columns of CH, two air loops and two more columns of CH. The second row involves expansion. It is carried out by increasing the number of columns in each fan. To do this, on a pair of columns, make one the same, and in the arch of air loops, tie 2 columns of CH, 2 air and 2 more columns of CH, complete this element with another column of CH on the second pair from the fan of the previous row.

In the third row on the columns on the sides of the arch you will need to connect two columns of CH already, and in the arch repeat the same pattern. The fourth row repeats the third. And in the fifth column should already be three. The sixth row is a repetition of the fifth.

Option when the shirt-front looks like a collar

In this case, the cape is not knitted. All work stops after making the neck. It can be knitted both along and across.

The collar is beautiful if you use crochet. The dickey (the description scheme is presented below) goes from top to bottom. An example is such a pattern. For him, you need to dial a chain of loops, the number of which is divided by ten, plus three more.

The first row (the second is the same): three lifting loops; air; CH column in the third loop of the chain; 3 columns of CH in the 7th loop, an air loop and three more columns of CH in the 7th loop; CH column in the 11th, then again airy and the pattern is repeated from the moment where the element connected in the third loop is indicated. So until the end of the row, which should end with an element of two columns of CH, separated by one air loop.

Third row: swap elements. Where there was a fan, make an arch of two columns of CH and an air loop. And over a similar arch tie a fan. The fourth or fifth row continues this pattern, that is, a fan goes above the fan, and an arch above the arch.

The last two rows are a repetition of the first two. Manishka is ready. It remains only to sew on the buttons and draw a hem with eyelets for them.
