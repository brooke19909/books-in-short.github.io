Ski resorts of Karelia. Description of winter holidays

Fans of active winter recreation have long chosen the mountain slopes of Karelia. And although the ski resorts here are just beginning to develop, their prospects are promising. From this article you will learn about the most popular ski resorts in Karelia, as well as all about the features of local winter holidays.



Ski complex "Malaya Medvezhka"

The beauty of Karelian nature is known all over the world, and it was on this land that the most picturesque places were chosen for vacationers. Like all ski resorts in Karelia, "Malaya Medvezhka" is perfect for all sports. Typically, the season lasts from early November to the end of April, but prolonged cold weather can extend it until the beginning of May. For guests of the resort are equipped with wonderful pine houses for living, wonderful slopes for skiing and many entertainment venues. Everyone can dine at reasonably affordable prices in the Medvezhka restaurant, and in the evening visit one of the Russian baths or a Finnish sauna with a pool. Experienced and novice athletes will always find something to do here. Beginners are advised to start training under the guidance of an experienced instructor, and rent equipment at a local inventory outlet. For fans of extreme sports, the resort offers a virgin skiing descent that will leave a lasting impression in your memory. If you do not like extreme sports and want to enjoy the views of the local nature, then use the cross-country ski track. So, you can admire the beauty of forests and mountains in complete silence, walking along specially equipped tracks 2 and 5 km long.



Ski resort in Karelia Yalgora

Not far from the capital of the region, the city of Petrozavodsk, is one of the most popular ski resorts in Karelia. Yalgora differs from similar centers in that you can not only have fun here, but also prepare for serious competitions. There is a training center for children in the complex, where they receive and develop skiing skills. Like many ski resorts in Karelia, Yalgora begins to function in November, and closes the season in April. The complex has two lifts and five tracks of varying degrees of difficulty. They include a training descent, where beginners and children are trained. Although there are no hotels or guest houses in the resort, anyone can find suitable housing in neighboring villages.



Entertainment Center "Gorka"

Residents and guests of Petrozavodsk have the opportunity to visit a ski resort located in the city. A few years ago it was impossible to imagine how convenient it is to enjoy skiing without leaving the village. Gorka, like other ski resorts in Karelia, pleases its visitors with a lot of entertainment. It offers slopes designed for both professionals and beginners. Vacationers can use the lifts that regularly go to each descent, and save energy for vigorous activity. At the sports equipment rental center, anyone can take the necessary ammunition for themselves. For those who only comprehend the basics of skiing, experienced instructors work in Gorka. If you are tired of outdoor activities and want to have a bite to eat, you can visit the cozy cafe located in the center.



Spasskaya Guba

This resort is located near noisy cities. Those wishing to visit the ski resorts of Karelia come here for the sake of fresh air, magical landscapes and excellent ecology. Skating enthusiasts can take advantage of three tracks with a height difference of 70 meters. The resort administration warns that two of them are designed for experienced skiers or professional athletes. Everyone can rent the necessary sports equipment for the required period, but you need to book a ski lift in advance. The resort also has instructors who help beginners learn the wisdom of skiing. Those who wish to relax and warm themselves can use the guest house located in the resort.

Ski and biathlon complex "Kurgan"

On the territory of Petrozavodsk operates a ski complex that positions itself as a base for professional athletes. Guests of Kurgan can ride on two ski slopes 400 meters long, use cross-country skiing tracks, a biathlon shooting range, as well as sports equipment rental. On site you can dine in the cafe or visit the local sauna after skiing.



Conclusion

If you look at the ski resorts of Karelia on the map, then you can make sure that they are located in very good places. Of course, the local mountains are lower than the slopes of the Caucasus, but they are no less popular. Children and adults who have chosen the ski resorts of Karelia are happy with additional entertainment. For example, winter fishing, snowmobile tours or dog sledding.
