Calpe, Spain: travel reviews of tourists

Spain can be called a country of holidays. Probably nowhere else are there so many carnivals, traditional street processions and costume parades. Moreover, the Spaniards celebrate secular and religious holidays with the same scale. Here is the largest number of restaurants and clubs in Europe. Clear sunny weather is in Spain almost all year round. People from all over the world come here to relax in order to plunge into the festive and joyful atmosphere of the sun, sea, smiles, Mediterranean cuisine, bullfighting and flamenco. A striking representative of this holiday is the small town of Calpe. This article provides a brief overview of holidays in Calpe (Spain), reviews of tourists and photos.

Calpe - Mediterranean Pearl of Spain

Calpe is one of the most respectable and popular resorts on the Costa Blanca. With the invasion of tourists, a small fishing village has turned into a fashionable resort with a population of more than 30,000 people. In the season, tourists increase the number of people living on this stretch of coast to 200,000.

The history of Calpe has always been connected with the sea. For centuries, the inhabitants of the village were engaged in fishing and cultivating olive plantations. For several centuries in a row, the coast was subjected to pirate raids. The ruins of the fortress wall and watchtowers testify to those times. The territory of Calpe has always been inhabited by a variety of peoples.





Interestingly, traditions have been preserved: in the modern city, most of the townspeople come from other countries (mainly English and German pensioners), fishing continues to develop in the city.

The tourist center of Calpe has become closer to the middle of the twentieth century. At one time, this place was very popular among literary bohemians. Now lovers of fresh seafood are gathering here. The symbol of the city is Mount Ifach (322 m.). She stands far into the sea, forming two charming bays. In Calpe, there is everything for a carefree vacation: beautiful hotels, comfortable beaches, restaurants, clubs, lots of entertainment. Reading tourist reviews about Calpe (Spain), I want to quickly buy a plane ticket.

Hotels and entertainment

Reviews of tourists about Calpe (Spain) contain many enthusiastic responses about the variety and number of hotels in this resort. Calpe offers guests hotels for every taste and opportunity. There are inexpensive hostels and affordable hotels. There are luxury hotels of world class.





Most of them are located along the coastline, but there are hotels in the city itself. Almost anyone has a view of Ifach Mountain and the sea. In all Calpe hotels, the European level of service is represented by a full range of modern hotel services, including restaurants, bars, conference rooms, spas, pools and fitness centers. Apart-hotels where you can rent a separate apartment are very popular. Among local entertainment, the first place is occupied by active types of water recreation: sailing, diving, surfing. If desired, you can learn to manage a yacht.





By the way, a huge number of snow-white yachts are always moored off the coast of Calpe. You can also visit golf courses, tennis courts, volleyball courts. Not far from the city is the famous amusement park Terra Mitica. There are many clubs and bars, as well as hotels, a huge number of restaurants with Michelin stars and ordinary eateries. In total, about 200 establishments await guests in the city. Most of them are concentrated at the fish exchange. Calpe is very popular among sea food lovers.

Holidays and carnivals

The most emotional descriptions and reviews of tourists about Calpe (Spain) are devoted to holidays, which are abundant in this country. In Calpe, holidays are traditionally held noisy, colorful and wide. For example, the festival of "Three Kings" - a bright costume show. Or the festival of gigantic figures "Las Fallas". In August, the annual Virgen de las Nieves fiesta is held with a carnival, colorful fireworks and dancing.





In autumn, citizens celebrate the Spanish religious festival "Moors and Christians", which is a historical costume reconstruction of the city‚Äôs defense from the Moors. The holiday is no less bright than any social event.

Climate and weather

In this part of the world, as noted by tourists from Calpe in Spain in reviews, a mild subtropical climate reigns. There are almost 300 sunny days a year. It is warm in winter, air rarely cools below +5 degrees Celsius. In summer, the thermometer can go up to +40 degrees. The water temperature at the height of the season reaches +30 degrees Celsius. The wonderful climate and sunny weather allow you to sunbathe in the resort from spring to late autumn.

Beaches

Calpe beaches are some of the most beautiful in Spain. Conventionally, they can be divided into three main groups.





The first is sandy beaches, wide and spacious, the most popular of which are located in the bays formed by Ifach rock. Arenal Bol beach stretches for 1500 meters. It is marked with the Blue Flag for its cleanliness and well-being. The beach is very popular and provides vacationers with the highest level of service.

Another fine sand beach of La Fossa, which is 950 meters long, has its own sports grounds and a children's amusement park. In addition to the Blue Flag, he was awarded for quality by the Spanish Institute of Tourism.

The second group - these are small pebble beaches with their regular fans: Puerto Blanco, Manzanera, Caballa. They are very well maintained and have excellent service.

The third group of beaches is small picturesque corners, which are better accessible by water. They were chosen by divers and fishermen.

Judging by the reviews of holidays in Calpe in Spain, tourists still call a lot of wonderful coastal corners where you can sunbathe and swim in the crystal clear water.

Vacation with children

Family vacations can be very comfortable in Calpe (Spain), reviews of tourists with children confirm this. Almost all hotels provide cots for babies, highchairs and night pots. On the city beaches for children a lot of entertainment is provided: from children's playgrounds to attractions. Some hotels have their own water parks and pools with children's slides. Feeding a child in any restaurant or cafe is also not difficult. After all, local supermarkets have absolutely everything your imagination can tell you. Good transport links and high-quality roads allow you to safely move with children in any direction. In the reviews of tourists from Calpe (Spain), one can often find words about the very welcoming attitude of local residents to families with children.
