"Protasov Yar" - the only ski resort in the center of Kiev

Protasov Yar (Kiev) is a historical district of the capital of Ukraine. But that is not all. This is currently the only ski resort in Kiev. It is located almost in the city center. Protasov Yar opened in 1998 and has been working without interruption since then. Here, Kievans and tourists like to ski, and professional athletes, including snowboarders, also prepare for competitions. But the complex is also known for the fact that holidays and corporate parties are often held on its territory. Let's get to know him in more detail.

Trails and lifts

Of course, in Kiev, not mountains, but only hills. Therefore, the ski complex "Protasov Yar" for a long time possessed only three slopes. Two of them have a height difference of 70 meters. But one track is steep, and the other is gentle. They are different in length. A steep track stretches for 500 meters, flat - for three hundred. There is also a training descent designed for beginners. Its length is 150 meters. Recently opened a fourth - short track. There is also a special park for snowboarders, with a complex of jumps and rails. Lifts correspond to the tracks. There are two of them, and they are all drag. There are lifts for two and one person. Their length is 650 and 350 meters. Queues can form for the lifts. But you don‚Äôt usually have to wait too long - a maximum of 15 minutes. The track for athletes has an international certificate, and there members of the Olympic team train. On the territory of this complex there are five ski schools.



Complex "Protasov Yar": prices, where to stay

This ski resort has both one-time tickets and seasonal passes. Night skiing is paid as unlimited - 300 hryvnia (a little more than 610 rubles) from ten in the evening to four in the morning. But most often skiers pay for one-time climbs. On weekdays until six in the evening, the price of one yoke on the top of the slope is 15 hryvnias (30 rubles), and after 18:00 - 20 (41 rubles). On weekends and holidays, the cost of lifting increases. This is a common practice for ski resorts. A one-time hike on Sundays and holidays costs 35 hryvnias (71 rubles). Would you like to buy a season ski pass? For this you will have to pay at least 2500 hryvnias (5105 rubles). Ski rental in Protasov Yar costs 200 UAH. (410 rubles) per day, and snowboarding - 250 UAH. (510 rub.). However, prices may vary depending on the season. In Protasov Yar there is also a hotel where you can stay for the weekend so as not to tear yourself away from skiing. This is a mini-hotel "Fortel". A double room costs there from 450 hryvnias (920 rubles). There are also two cafes on the mountain. One of them is located at the very top of the ski slope, it offers a good panoramic view. This is the Do-Ski restaurant. The cost of an average dish in these cafes is from 80 hryvnias (163 rubles). They try home-made cuisine, serve good coffee and mulled wine. On Saturday, cafes are open until six in the morning, until the metro begins to walk.





Reviews

Kievans love to ride in Protasov Yar. True, some argue that the mountain is small, the snow cover is often mixed with ice floes, and the rental base for the most popular skis is rather small. Nevertheless, a lot of people gather here on the weekend. After all, this is the only place where you can get from anywhere in Kiev in almost half an hour. Therefore, it is easy to get here on the weekend, and it does not take much time. Good instructors work here, therefore, having learned to ride these slopes, you can already ride on tracks of any complexity, even in the Alps. Well, those who knew how to ski would have a great opportunity to train and hone their skills. An excellent bonus of the complex Kiev called night skating.
