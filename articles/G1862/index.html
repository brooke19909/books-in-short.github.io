Travel to Tomsk. Recreation facilities: description, services, photos

Everyone wants a good rest on the sea or on the lake. Many people like to just be in nature, for example, surrounded by forests. The recreation center will make your vacation more comfortable and unforgettable. What is she like? People from many cities come here to rest, Tomsk is no exception. Recreation centers are small companies that provide some services to their visitors. Depending on where the complex is located, it can be said exactly what types of offers are provided: climbing, beach vacations or rafting. Some tourist sites are located in the natural zone, where there is a huge forest around the houses, they can also be located on the banks of a small rivulet or large waterway. This is especially felt when considering institutions in a city such as Tomsk. Recreation facilities that are located in the mountains, as a rule, are in demand among climbers. The level of comfort depends on the price category of the room where tourists live. There are those rooms where there are all amenities: furniture, appliances, appliances and other appliances. More budget options include a bed, bedside tables. A bathroom can be located both in the room and on the street.

The recreation center "Ivushka"

The estate "Ivushka" is quite popular among visitors. There are houses on the territory of the base, each of them includes four standard rooms. These rooms have several single beds. The rooms have a bedroom, shower, kitchen and bathroom. For the best comfort, the room is equipped with a kettle, refrigerator, dishes. There is a TV and bed linen is provided. For those who have a car, parking is provided.

A resident will have to pay a check for 625 rubles per day. This is a reasonable price for establishments that are located near a city such as Tomsk. Recreation facilities, as a rule, do not include the cost of food in this amount. It is possible to order three meals a day on your own. For it a day will have to pay 800 rubles. Breakfast costs 150 rubles., Lunch comes out the most expensive - 350 rubles., And 300 rubles. - fee for dinner.

You can arrive at this base at the beginning of the season. As a rule, tourists are accepted on any day of the week. The recreation center "Ivushka" makes a check-in at 12 o’clock in the afternoon, and departure - at 16 o’clock.



"Crystal"

At the recreation center "Crystal" you can step back from the noisy bustle of cities. Here they admire the Siberian nature, located on the banks of the Ob. On the base there are places for living of various price categories. We are talking about both rooms that can accommodate 2-4 people, as well as two-story cottages. In order to enjoy a measured rest, the institution offers various conditions: a dining room, a bathhouse, summer gazebos, barbecue facilities and so on. There is a football field and sports equipment. They definitely will not let the person who likes outdoor activities get bored. You can park your vehicle for free in the parking lot. “Crystal” is a recreation center (Tomsk a few kilometers from it), the cost of living on which starts at 400 rubles.





"Olympus"

The recreation center "Olympus" is located on the banks of the Ob River, surrounded on all sides by a pine forest. There is a fairly good microclimate. It is also worth noting that vacationers should not be afraid of ticks. There is a special treatment annually. The whole area is fully lit. Residential houses are divided into two types: summer and winter. As a rule, they are designed for 2 and 4 places. In winter homes are additional items to improve comfort. It's about a refrigerator, stove, kettle. Residents of each house have a barbecue. Olympus is simultaneously ready to accept no more than 160 people.

On the territory of the building there are basketball and volleyball courts, futsal. There are ping pong tables. It is allowed to rent bicycles, skates, skateboards. All tracks are asphalted, therefore there are a lot of places for skiing. As already clear, this recreation center operates year-round.





Lukomorye

The recreation center "Lukomorye" is located near the village of Victory. Next to it flows the Ob River. Near the houses is a pine forest. Other recreation centers on the Ob (Tomsk) provide the same conditions. The air here is really clean and pleasant. There are several types of rooms: economy, standard, junior suite, suite. This recreation center is open all year. The price per day starts from 1500 rubles. You can eat what the cook prepares, or do the cooking yourself. All the necessary equipment for this is provided.
