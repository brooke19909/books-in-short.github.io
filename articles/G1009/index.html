Lamp "Plasma ball" - purpose and principle of operation

The light sources that fill our houses are various types of chandeliers, sconces, lamps, floor lamps. They give warmth and comfort, complement the interior. Therefore, to choose an unusual light source so that it fits perfectly into the design of the room is a responsible matter. An excellent decoration for an apartment can be the original lamp "Plasma Ball", which has many useful functions.

Description

Externally, the lamp resembles a magic ball on a stand, similar to an artifact from science fiction films. In its manufacture, modern technologies are used, so the quality of the original invention meets the highest standards. Of the wonderful properties that a lamp has, one can name its ability to relieve stress and fatigue.





When the Plasma Ball is turned on, electrical discharges can be observed inside it. They look like colored fireworks, which spread from the center of the lamp. Like a magical little thing, a glass ball is able to respond to sounds, touches and voices. When the hand touches the ball, the electric lightning inside it gathers in one stream and begins to strike at the place that your fingers touch. You can watch this spectacle for a long time, it fascinates with its beauty. Moreover, the movement of the discharges are never repeated.

The lamp can be used not only for relaxation, the Plasma Ball can be a wonderful addition to the interior of the apartment. It is nice to give it to friends, relatives and acquaintances. If you admire the electric discharges inside the glass ball, you can feel peace and tranquility. You can constantly admire him as a beautiful and unusual little thing, which will take up little space in the apartment, but will bring a little magic to its design.





Way of working

The lamp seems like a magical subject. To dispel this impression, it is enough to consider a device that has a "Plasma ball", the principle of operation of the device. The diameter of the lamp bulb can vary from eight to twenty centimeters. An electrode is placed inside the decorative night light, to which a high voltage current is supplied. Therefore, lightning occurs inside the lamp. This explains the name of the lamp, because this is how plasma shines. The glass ball of the lamp contains a discharged inert gas, which gives the glow a certain shade. During operation, the lamp consumes little electricity. Nevertheless, it is impossible for it to work for more than two or three hours, otherwise overheating is possible.





When purchasing such an unusual lighting device, do not forget about safety measures. Follow the instructions for its use. The device can be recharged from a USB port or a 220V outlet. The lamp "Plasma ball" will help relax intense eyes after a long work at the computer. The lamp can be a useful thing in your home, help relax the nervous system and relieve you of the effects of stress.

The lamp is made in various designs, including a very original one. For example, in the form of a black dragon, which embraces its wings "Plasma ball", which makes it even more attractive and magical.
