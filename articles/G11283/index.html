Marittima 3 * (Rimini, Italy): photos and reviews of tourists

Italy is a beautiful country that is hard to meet in a week or even ten days. Therefore, many of our compatriots quite often plan their trip in such a way as to visit several cities for their trip. This can be done by renting, for example, a car and driving along beautiful Italian roads, enjoying the scenery and small picturesque villages. If you prefer to spend your holidays this way, you must pre-book hotels in several cities at once, so as not to waste valuable time on this tedious task. The theme of our article was the sweet and comfortable Marittima 3 Hotel in Rimini. We are sure that our information will be useful to you, because this city is included in the list of obligatory points to visit in Italy.





General description of hotel Marittima 3

Rimini attracts tourists with its historical center and amazingly beautiful sea coast. Therefore, they usually come here at the end of their Italian trip to relax and enjoy a lazy beach holiday.

It is worth noting that the city has a fairly wide selection of hotels of all categories. But the most popular, of course, are democratic "treshki". Especially in Italy, many of them are pretty good. These are the hotels Marittima 3 in Rimini belongs to.

The hotel complex is located in a multi-storey building and consists of four floors, in which forty-four rooms are located. Some travel directories indicate that the hotel was built in 1968. But the last reconstruction of Marittima 3 (Italy, Rimini) underwent recently - in 2013. The hotel has been remodeled, the rooms are renovated and the furniture is updated. At the moment, the hotel complex appears before tourists in all its glory, pleasing with interesting design and cleanliness.

Keep in mind that the Marittima 3 hotel complex does not have its own territory, and there is a street right behind its entrance door. But many tourists liked the cozy terrace of the hotel, where you can drink delicious Italian wine on warm nights and jam it with jamon. This slightly compensates for the lack of territory, and also adds a special charm to the hotel.

Almost all vacationers noted that the hotel has very few reviews, so they went here at their own peril and risk, but were very pleased with the rest. The guests were impressed by the design of the hotel complex, especially the snow-white hall with leather sofas and armchairs, where you can watch TV and read books.





Hotel location

What primarily attracts tourists to the hotel complex Marittima Hotel 3 (Italy)? Adriatic Riviera Rimini. Therefore, many travelers, looking at a hotel in the catalog, are interested in its location relative to the city center and the beach.

The hotel is located one hundred and fifty meters from the sea. They can be overcome on foot in ten minutes or using public transport. Moreover, the stop is located not far from the hotel complex, and the bus interval in Rimini usually does not exceed fifteen minutes.

The bus to the historic city center is also short-lived - no more than ten minutes, on foot you can overcome this distance in about half an hour. Many hotel guests were pleased that a large shopping center was located nearby, selling delicious Italian snacks and great wine. The store staff will even be happy to chop ham or jamon if you ask. Also, all tourists praised the magnificent king prawns, which are a great alternative to dinner at the hotel. In addition, you can eat all these delicacies on the veranda of the hotel complex, watching the unhurried life of local residents.

Keep in mind that the hotel rooms are quite small. But this is very common in Europe, here you can rarely find a room of more than thirty-five squares. The usual room size does not exceed sixteen square meters.

The hotel has all rooms carefully renovated, they are simply decorated, but with a special taste and taking into account the needs of guests. Elegant equipment rooms can compete with five-star hotels and always receive excellent reviews from guests of the hotel complex.

Accommodation Features

If you decide to stay in this particular hotel, please note that at the time of check-in, documents with a photograph must be provided to all guests. Payment by credit card is accepted with more pleasure than cash. In addition, the established restriction does not allow paying in cash in an amount exceeding three thousand euros.

Pets are allowed at the hotel, but must be notified at the time of booking.

Check-in is from twelve in the afternoon to ten in the evening, check-out is from seven to ten in the morning. Guests are not charged a deposit upon arrival.

Accommodation with children

The hotel accepts guests with children of any age. But note that it is not possible to put more than one extra bed in a room. Toddlers up to two years old can stay free in the same bed as their parents.

In the restaurant, the child will be happy to be provided with a high chair and a special menu.
