Negative reviews

A number of shortcomings are noted by travelers in the work of the Agrabella hotel (Hersonissos, Greece). From the reviews you can draw such negative information:

  • old leaking plumbing, which is covered with rust and soapy coating;
  • low-quality cleaning, which does not include mopping;
  • unpleasant stains on the mirrors in the room and bathroom;
  • saving on cosmetics in the bathroom;
  • uncomfortable work schedule of catering establishments (breakfast ends at half past nine, and dinner - at 8 pm, which is quite early);
  • paid wireless Internet not only in guest rooms, but also in public areas (this disadvantage is somewhat offset by the proximity to a large number of cafes, where this service is provided for free);
  • some rooms do not have air conditioning (this applies to economy rooms);
  • poor breakfasts, which often include low-quality and stale foods;
  • for using a hairdryer you need to pay extra;
  • sound insulation between rooms is practically absent;
  • the beach closest to the hotel is very small, and therefore at the height of the season there is quite crowded (there is an option to go to neighboring beaches);

  • the bathroom is very cramped, which will be especially noticeable for people with a large physique;

  • there are practically no fruits in the restaurant menu;
