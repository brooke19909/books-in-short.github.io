Electronic CTP: customer reviews

When insuring it is important to remember that in any case, a person living in the territory of our country, after acquiring a vehicle, is first obliged to insure him. For the absence or expired date of insurance, large fines are prescribed in the legislation of the Russian Federation.

For those who do not have the ability or desire to visit the office to arrange insurance, there is the opportunity to do this via the Internet. Currently, almost all insurance companies offer their clients this opportunity. It is very convenient, fast and costs much cheaper than a regular paper policy issued in the office.

Now e-MTPL reviews collects numerous. So far, many car owners are confused by this method of insurance. They prefer to draw out CTP in the old fashioned way. At the same time, they overpay quite significantly.

What is an insurance policy?

What are the collected insurance policies? CTP reviews? This question interests many.

Compulsory motor third party liability insurance begins its history from the 20s of the last century.

Initially, such a policy was introduced in the United States, but after a few years (by the 40s) it became widespread throughout Europe. It was intended for financial guarantees of damages in case of accidents on the roads.

In Russia, CTP finally took shape much later. The Soviet authorities considered introducing insurance compensation for accidents on the roads, but considered it necessary to postpone it. Only on July 1, 2003, Federal Law No. 40- On Compulsory Third Party Liability Insurance of Vehicle Owners entered into force. Now, the car owners of our country have certain guarantees in case of traffic accidents that have caused damage to the insured property.

Compulsory insurance is considered one of the guarantors of safe road traffic, as drivers are more attentive to the rules. This document has the same force as a paper policy. E-insurance means that the car owner is included in a single database. All coefficients remain in the database, as a result, the driver will be able to receive a discount for new insurance for driving without accidents. And vice versa, if the driver often gets into traffic accidents, this will necessarily be reflected in the database.

Insurance rates for electronic CTP

Insurance compensations provide for their tariffs, which are officially set from the moment of introduction of compulsory insurance in our country. In the future, this law has changed slightly by making some amendments, but the main base remains unchanged to this day.

The basic rate of a tariff depends primarily on the type of vehicle (motorcycle, passenger car, tram, bus, etc.). It forms the basis of each of the tariffs, and is further multiplied by coefficients that depend on many points.

These points are provided by e-insurance. Reviews about this are available.

Factors affecting the cost of insurance:

- car engine volume;

- region of residence (or registration) of the car owner;

- the number of owners of the insured vehicle;

- the length of service of the driver for whom the CTP policy is issued;

- The region in which the office of the insurance company is located.



Thus, the higher your experience and the smaller the volume of the engine of the vehicle that is owned, the lower the cost of insurance.

Experienced analysts foresee the increase in the base rate of tariffs soon, which will significantly raise the direct price of insurance. A distinctive feature of an electronic policy is its lower cost in comparison with a paper policy.

The procedure for obtaining electronic insurance

Electronic CTP (reviews confirm this) is simple to issue:

- on the official website of the insurance company you need to fill out an application;

- an individual can sign the form through SNILS;

- pay the insurance premium in any convenient way.

It is important to understand that this type of insurance is entitled to issue only officially registered companies. A broker, agent, any intermediary will not be able to do this.

If everything is done correctly, after a while, an email with an attached policy will come to your email. It must be printed and carried with you. This is how the CTP insurance policy is drawn up. Reviews can be found on the web.



Fraud in this area is not uncommon. In order to verify the authenticity of the policy, you can verify its number in the SAR. The name of the insurance company is also verified.

An electronic policy looks the same as conventional insurance. Even the color scheme is preserved.

Major insurance changes in 2017

New amendments in the field of OSAGO were officially published at the end of August 2016. Presumably, by July 2017, they will come into force. Car owners should pay attention to the following changes:

  • The usual payment of compensation will now be difficult. First of all, the insurance company will give directions to the service station for free car repair.
  • The limit on the Euro protocol in case of an accident has been increased to 100,000 rubles (in the cities of Moscow and St. Petersburg to 400,000 rubles). This already provides for an electronic insurance policy of VSK (reviews are available).
  • There will be a ban on collecting additional payments from pedestrians guilty of an accident, who were injured in an accident.
  • It will be possible to receive personal data from the RSA database (Russian Union of Auto Insurers) without requesting an insurance company. In this regard, electronic insurance is becoming more popular. After all, you can issue a policy without leaving your home.

Another change that will be necessary for car owners will be the new principle of calculating the KBM. It will be assigned individually.

Positive innovations of OSAGO

So, here is a list of positive innovations.



  • Registration of CTP in electronic form (reviews are mostly good).
  • The ability to increase the MSC without reference to the vehicle.
  • Making group insurance will be easier.
  • Recalculation of MSC upon the introduction of amendments to the fundamental law will become annual. Previously, it was made only after the expiration of the contract.
