Characteristics and types of sound devices

The beginning of Sony's business was marked by the manufacture of audio systems. This brand in Japan released the first device that recorded on magnetic tape. Now the Sony speaker systems are the perfect technique not only for listening to music, but also for creating a decent home theater.

Modern acoustic devices are presented by the manufacturer in multiband and broadband versions. Several speakers are built into multiband speakers, each working on its own frequency band, and broadband systems involve the use of only one dynamic head.

When comparing the characteristics of these band solutions, it is seen that the more bands in the columns, the wider the sound range from the equipment.

By type, acoustic systems are divided into passive and active. In active models, a power amplifier is built in, and the design of passive ones includes a crossover and a radiator, while it is extremely simple.
