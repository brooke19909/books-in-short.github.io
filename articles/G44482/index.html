Camp "Swallow" for those who want to please their children

Camp "Swallow", located in the village of Kriusha, will certainly attract the attention of the children and their parents. In this modern place of rest, the child will not have to be bored. And the Swallow camp is interesting for parents in that the rest of boys and girls is possible here both in winter and in summer.



Features of the camp "Swallow" in the Nizhny Novgorod region

The most important thing that may interest moms and dads in the process of choosing a camp for their beloved child is the level of staff qualification. By betting on the Swallow camp, you can be sure that the child will fall into safe hands. The staff exclusively accepts teachers of the highest category who have extensive experience working with children.

In the camp "Swallow" all the historically built features of children's recreation in such institutions are preserved. Boys and girls live here according to the regimen, do exercises, eat on time.

In the center there is everything that is necessary for recreation, sports and entertainment. Every corner of the children's health camp "Swallow" is thought out to the smallest detail so that boys and girls feel comfortable in its territory.

Accommodations

Children are housed in buildings, in each of which from one hundred to one hundred and fifty places to stay. Three to eight children can live in one room. In rooms for up to 6 people, amenities are in the room. In the rooms where 6 to 8 children live, there is a toilet and shower on the floor. All buildings are brick-built with autonomous heating, so the rooms are warm regardless of what temperature is outside the window.



Fun for kids

In the camp "Swallow" neither small nor adult children will have to get bored. The program is varied, so that each child will choose for themselves such entertainment that is to their liking. Among the leisure options are the following:

  • Indoor pool where you can swim at any time of the year.
  • Computer class.
  • The venue where concert programs and events take place is always open for talent search.
  • Sports enthusiasts can head to the tennis court, stadium or sports grounds.
  • There are creative classes where children are taught new and interesting things.
  • There is also a library where you can immerse yourself in the world of knowledge and fascinating travels.

For the youngest boys and girls on the territory of the children's camp "Swallow" there is a games room in which animators are happy to welcome visitors at any time of the day.



These are not all possible ways to have a good time. The professional composition of the counselors will help children find their calling and choose for themselves entertainment according to their interests.

What a child needs to take with him

When a ticket is purchased and it is time to pack your suitcase on an independent trip, many parents are faced with difficulties. In order to avoid misunderstandings and add up everything that is necessary for a son or daughter, it is worth studying the list of obligatory things:

  1. Help from the clinic of the required form.
  2. Personal hygiene items: brush, paste, shampoo, soap, napkins, towel, washing powder.
  3. Shoes and clothes for the season: sports sneakers or sneakers, slippers for a room, a sports suit for active physical education events, a set of elegant clothes for a disco and special occasions, sets of clothes depending on the number of days of rest (pants, sweaters, t-shirts), underwear , socks, tights, a hat (a panama hat or a warm hat), pajamas for sleeping.
  4. It is also necessary to lay down the child accessories that he will need for swimming in the pool.
  5. It is allowed to give your child several magazines, books or a player to listen to music.




Camp "Swallow" kindly welcomes everyone to their friendly embrace. For parents, this is an opportunity to give their children a variety of fun. And for children, this is a whirlpool of emotions, filled with new acquaintances and knowledge. Having a rest in the Children's Swallow Training Center, boys and girls will be charged with positive energy, strengthen their health and just have fun.
