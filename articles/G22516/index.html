Alexander Belousov - the best general of the Russian army

Belousov Alexander Vasilievich is a military commander and general of the Russian army. He was born in 1952 on September 8, in the city of Rivne, Ukrainian SSR. This is one of the most prominent and talented military leaders who has made a truly huge contribution to the development of military culture and the Russian army.

Biography

Alexander Belousov, whose biography is full of interesting events, is a very responsible person and a rather tough leader in life. He began his military service in the army of the Soviet Union in 1969. In 1973 he graduated from the Higher Combined Arms School. And from 1981 to 1984 he studied at the Frunze Academy of Military Training. In 1995, Alexander Belousov graduated from the military academy of the General Staff.





General's military activities

Throughout his life, from his youth until his retirement, he devoted himself to serving in the Russian army. On his track record are the most dangerous parts of warfare. Alexander served in the rank of platoon commander in Germany. It was then that a group of motorized rifle Soviet troops was founded.

From 1978 to 1981, he worked as chief of staff in the military district of Central Asia. And from 1984 to 1989 he led the motorized rifle regiment in Leningrad. Until 1991, he worked as a deputy. commander, and until 1993 he became commander of the 131st division of Lozov. In 1993, Alexander Belousov received the title of Major General.

From 1999 to 2003, he spent a lot of time on business trips in Chechnya, and after business trips in 2003, he became deputy commander of emergency forces in the North Caucasus District.

After a long journey through battle places, Alexander became the first deputy minister of defense, and in 2006, by order of the President of the Russian Federation, he was awarded the rank of General of Russia.

Until 2009, he served as the head of the Military Academy of the General Staff of the Armed Forces. And at the end of 2009, he resigned due to the retirement age.





Honorary titles

Alexander Belousov received the rank of general for a reason. To get to this post, he had to go a lot and visit many places. He began his career ladder from the bottom, and during his long service he managed to visit both the company commander, the platoon chief, and then the commander of an entire regiment. He also served as deputy commander in many military districts.

For invaluable services to the country, Alexander Belousov was awarded numerous orders and medals, and at the end of his service, the Ministry of Defense presented him with a registered pistol.

When Belousov became deputy defense minister, he repeatedly visited the military units of the country with checks. He personally became convinced of the preparedness of the officer corps of the units, and also tried to fully and in-depth study the service, life and life of all military personnel. He obtained from the officers complete military training not only in theoretical knowledge, but also in practical. He strictly demanded constant training of officers at the training grounds and the complete testing of all kinds of military operations.





Fame and fame

Alexander Belousov, whose photo cannot be seen in ordinary magazines or newspapers, very rarely gives interviews, because he does not like "empty talk and gossip." Despite the many difficulties that awaited him in the military field, this man did not lose heart and was always full of strength and confidence. His military exploits, his tactics of warfare, as well as his lifestyle in general, set an example for modern students of military schools.

Social work

During the service, the general periodically checked the knowledge of soldiers who served under the contract. He believed that knowledge is power during a war. But, unfortunately, not all soldiers were trained and passed exams safely.

When Belousov was appointed head of the military academy, under his guidance, the academy began to train specialists with higher education in operational and strategic appointments. He personally developed a training strategy and put forward requirements for the training of officers, as well as all troops and executive bodies, which are required by law to undergo military service, proposed strategies for working in the country's defense and military security sectors.

Belousov did his best to consolidate the status of the academy as the central military and scientific center for the training of the military forces of the Russian Federation, since it was his academy that took a direct part in the development, and then in the implementation of the development strategy of education in military affairs and the training of highly qualified officers who were sent to the military districts of the country.





Family and relatives of the general

Despite almost full employment at work, Alexander Belousov did not forget about his family. He has a beautiful wife and two already grown children, a daughter and a son. And more recently, the general became a grandfather, his daughter gave him a grandson Artem. Due to the fact that the general occupies such a high post, for the safety of all his relatives are carefully guarded. For this reason, the press has very little information about his family.

Life after service

This year, September 8, General Alexander Belousov celebrated his 64th birthday. The retired general received congratulations from the club of military commanders of the Russian Federation, and the congratulation was sent by the club president and his friend A. S. Kulikov. In a congratulation to the general they wished happiness, health and long life.

Alexander Belousov after retirement and resignation did not stop there. He headed the Veterans Council and became president of the Veterans Union in the Moscow District.





Now he puts forward the following goal: the importance of politics and military education for young people. The general believes that modern youth lacks military education and love for their own country. As president of the union, he often gives speeches at All-Russian military conferences.

Military service in many parts of the country gave him true military training and endurance. He never stood still and always went forward, he still inspires the same with his wards.

Despite the fact that Belousov is retired, his opinion is still listened to and trained soldiers who serve under the contract, in accordance with those requirements that were approved by the general and repeatedly worked out. The general is highly respected, because all his methods related to military training work flawlessly, as evidenced by the fact that he never had a single miss in the conduct of hostilities.
