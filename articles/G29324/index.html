How to disable MTS auto payment from Sberbank card? Shutdown Options

Automatic payment from Sberbank allows customers to forget what a zero balance of a mobile phone is. One of the largest mobile operators in the country, MTS, is available for connecting auto payment by Sberbank card. But not everyone wants to recharge the phone automatically using a free service. How to disable MTS auto payment from a Sberbank card to pay for cellular communication yourself?

Briefly about the service

Sberbank car payment for mobile communications is a free service that saves time when replenishing the balance of a mobile phone. The principle of the service is simple: as soon as the cellular balance drops to the minimum value, the account automatically replenishes the account for the set amount. The client himself chooses what amount of payment will be optimal for him.

What is MTS auto payment from a Sberbank card in numbers?

  1. Replenishment upon reaching the minimum threshold of 30 rubles.
  2. The transfer amount is from 50 to 10 000 rubles.
  3. The daily limit for debiting funds from the card is up to 10,000 rubles.

Disabling Methods

Sberbank card holders can get rid of a boring service at any time and without commission. There are several options for how to remove MTS auto payment from a Sberbank card:

  1. At terminals or ATMs.
  2. In the branches of Sberbank.
  3. Through Sberbank Online.
  4. Using the "Mobile Bank".
  5. In a mobile application.
  6. Through the support service.

If the client addresses the bank employees in the office or by the telephone of the support service, he must provide the data of the DUL and the bank card. When calling the contact center, you also need to name the code word (or customer code received in the self-service device).

How to get rid of the service at Sberbank terminals?

One of the easiest ways to disable MTS auto payment is to use ATMs or Sberbank terminals. They work around the clock. To disconnect, you only need a client‚Äôs bank card.

How to disable MTS auto payment from a Sberbank card in the terminal?

  1. Insert the plastic carrier into the device, enter the code.
  2. Go to the "Information and Services" section.
  3. Select "Auto Payments".
  4. Find the necessary auto payment, click on the data window.
  5. Select "Disable Auto Payment".
  6. Confirm the operation.
  7. Pick up a card from the terminal.

When the service is disabled at the Sberbank ATM, the client can receive a check in which the details of the operation will be indicated. Deactivation of the service will occur within 24 hours from the date of the request for disconnection.

If desired, the cardholder can check the availability of the service one day after the disconnection. To do this, follow the first 3 steps. In the "Payments" section, information on the availability of the service with the MTS number will be absent.

How to deactivate auto payment in a bank office?

A proven way to get rid of the service is to disconnect at Sberbank branches. You can perform the operation in any additional office. To do this, you should bring your passport and bank card with you.

If the office is equipped with an electronic queue, you should take a ticket.

You can disconnect MTS auto payment from a Sberbank bank card using the "Connect / Configure" or "Issue / Receive" coupon.

After calling the coupon, you should go to the window and provide a card with documents to the bank manager. Disabling the service takes no more than 7 minutes. A certificate is issued to the client about the absence of MTS auto payment.

How to get rid of automatic MTS number replenishment via online banking?

A method that allows you to disable the service without leaving your home is associated with the use of online banking. Sberbank Online is another free service of Sberbank. In order to use the Internet banking, you need a plastic card with the activated SMS-informing service and login to enter the system.

How to disable MTS auto payment from a Sberbank card in Sberbank Online?

  1. Log in to the Internet bank using the username and password written on the check. Clients can take the details for entering the system at the terminals and ATMs of Sberbank.
  2. Enter a one-time password that comes from 900.
  3. Click on the active line "My auto payments", select "Manage auto payments." Information is on the right side of the screen.
  4. Find MTS auto payment among the connected services and click on "Disconnect".
  5. Confirm the operation with a code from SMS.
  6. Check the status of the request (the electronic message ‚ÄúCompleted‚Äù should appear).
  7. Log out of online banking.

Application for smartphones when disconnecting a service

Clients can use the Internet Bank not only with a computer or laptop. The mobile application of the largest bank in the country allows you to perform the same operations, but only using a smartphone. It can be downloaded and installed for free on the AppStore or Google Play.

How to cancel MTS auto payment from a Sberbank card in a mobile application?

  1. Log in to the app. It uses a 5-digit code that the cardholder invents himself, or a fingerprint.
  2. Go to the "Payments" section.
  3. Find the Auto Payments tab.
  4. Select car payment MTS.
  5. Click on "Disable".
  6. Confirm the operation.

The operation in the Sberbank mobile application takes 2 minutes. After canceling the service, the client can update the application and check the absence of auto payment in the list of services.
