Panthea Holiday Village 4 * (Cyprus, Ayia Napa): hotel description, services, reviews

Panthea Holiday Village 4 * is an apart-hotel located in the heart of one of the most popular world resorts. It organically combines modern standards of service and local flavor.





Location

In the center of Ayia Napa, a few minutes walk from the beach, the Panthea Holiday Village 4 * hotel is located. Cyprus, Nissi Avenue, 20 - the address where tourists can find this institution. It is just a few steps from the resort's main promenade. It is also worth noting that the distance from Larnaca International Airport is 50 km (this is about an hour away by car).

Accommodation

More than 140 comfortable rooms are provided to accommodate guests in Panthea Holiday Village 4 *. Prices for accommodation are quite affordable (when compared with other hotels), and also fully consistent with the quality of service. So, you can settle in these rooms:

  • The studio can accommodate from 2 to 3 guests. The layout of the room is a good combination of a bedroom and a compact living area where you can relax while admiring the scenic view from the window. There is also a desk and a nightstand for luggage. The balcony provides comfortable furniture for relaxing and a device for drying clothes (from 3128 rubles per day for two).
  • Apartments are a comfortable room designed for long stays. The bedroom is a separate room with a large bed, a comfortable sofa, and a desk. The living room is combined with the kitchen area. Here there are not only the necessary appliances for cooking, but also a dining table (from 5360 rubles per day for two).

Negative reviews

Having chosen Panthea Holiday Village 4 * (Ayia-Napa) hotel for relaxation, you should be prepared for some negative moments. The most significant disadvantages of this institution can be considered the following:

  • there is no private beach, and therefore guests are forced to use the municipal, where it is always very crowded;
  • do not give out beach towels (you need to bring with you or spend 5 euros on it at a local store);
  • besides liquid soap, there is no longer any makeup in the bathroom;
  • in the kitchen area old microwave ovens that do not work at full capacity;
  • the ants crawl in the rooms;
  • noisy air conditioning that keeps you awake at night;
  • too rocky beach, making it difficult to walk without special shoes;
  • despite the fact that the hotel has a lot of vacationers from the CIS countries, none of the employees speak Russian;
  • overpriced food prices;
  • Wireless Internet works only at the reception (but in the evening it is almost impossible to use it because of the large number of people);
  • drinks must be paid separately during dinner;
  • use of the safe in the room is paid;
  • no Russian-language channels;
  • the beach is not suitable for families with small children;
  • there are practically no fresh vegetables in the restaurant menu;
  • there is a lot of mold in the bathroom;
  • there is no first aid kit at the reception;
  • old furniture in the rooms;
  • the bathroom has a strong smell of dampness;
  • poor mattresses on the beds;
  • the bedspreads are full of holes and dust.

In addition to outdated repairs and not the most modern equipment, it is difficult to find any significant shortcomings in the Panthea Holiday Village 4 * hotel (Ayia-Napa). A friendly attitude and highly qualified staff smooth out many unpleasant moments.
