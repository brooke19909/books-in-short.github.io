Light Music Scheme

It is difficult, but possible, to assemble a unique design with your own hands.

The figure shows a diagram of how to make light music with your own hands. The circuit works with three different lights. The power of the spotlights can reach up to 100 watts. Guided by this scheme, you can easily assemble the installation.

Consider the main thing in this scheme. The main input is used to connect directly to the amplifier. To make a connection, remove one of the speaker wires and connect the wire from the light music. You can also connect in parallel without disconnecting the wires from the speaker.

With this connection, the maximum effect is achieved: the LEDs will work in the same beat as the music. At the same time, the VT1 or VL1 lamp will blink on the HF, and the other two, respectively, on the MF and LF.

Tip. In order for the lamps or spotlights to work with maximum heat and not to burn the thyristors, the mains voltage is regulated using diodes. Build homemade light music

Getting to the assembly.

  1. Take a few Christmas garlands. They should be no longer than 1-2 meters.
  2. Prepare a special twisted-pair Internet cable.
  3. Use wires to connect the adapter to the LEDs.
  4. We turn on the audio player, and already in it we find the ‚Äúflash zone‚Äù function, which is responsible for setting up the light music.

Still light-music installation can be done using plexiglass, which has clear advantages. With its low weight, it has good light transmission, and this is exactly what we need.

Essential Light Music Components

Like another electronic device, light music also consists of many components that, when combined, form an electronic target, and by opening and closing some elements, we control the entire process of flashing LEDs.

For our project we will need the following electronic components:

  1. Powered by a 12 volt adapter, or a Krona battery.
  2. Any wire, preferably thin and copper.
  3. You also need a transistor, for example, KT817.
  4. Glue gun.
  5. LEDs, if there is RBG, then it is better to use them.
  6. Solid factory glass.
  7. Fine sandpaper for polishing.
  8. Electric wire.

Execution algorithm.

Let's start with the box. Mark up his scheme on paper.

Instructions for creating records:

  1. We transfer the picture to plexiglass and cut it out.
  2. We drill 2 holes in one of the plates (one of them will be intended for power supply, and the other for the headphone cable).
  3. Using fine skins, we gently matte plexiglass.
  4. In the same way we skin the lenses of LEDs.
  5. We fix the case using a glue gun.
  6. We assemble, as shown in this diagram.
  7. We pass our prepared wire into the hole drilled from the back.
  8. We insert the power connector there and fix it with a glue gun.

The foundation is ready. You can make such boxes as many as you like. Now you need to connect our pre-prepared bulbs. It turned out beautifully, and most importantly - the lights blink merrily to the beat of the music.

Beginner Tip

If you are wondering how to make light music from an old garland, then note that the garland itself is connected to a 220 volt network, and this is the voltage with which it is quite possible to kill an adult, not to mention a schoolboy. Follow all safety precautions, namely:

  1. Do not touch the bare wires connected to the network.
  2. Watch the integrity of the wire, if this is a problem, it is better to spend the extra 100 rubles and buy a high-quality wire.
  3. When working with transistors, thyristors, capacitors, be careful in use, because if they are connected to a larger current than expected, then the explosion of the element is inevitable.
