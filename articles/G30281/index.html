The beaches of Antalya are wild, luxurious, comfortable ... What are they

Antalya is good because it offers tourists several bonuses at once. Firstly, these are a variety of hotels, from budget to luxurious palaces, where they will fulfill your every desire. Secondly, there are many historical sights, party places and beautiful landscapes. And finally, thirdly, these are the beautiful beaches of Antalya. They are clean, with clear water and equipped for relaxation as well as possible. Here, not only umbrellas, deck chairs and cabins are at your service. Garbage is removed everywhere, there are toilets, showers. In addition, the transport system and very convenient infrastructure are well developed here.



What does the local coast look like?

Many hotels (Turkey, Antalya) have their own beaches. The use of their equipment is usually included in the price of the permit. But there are those where umbrellas and sunbeds are paid. Then you need to either shell out or settle in the shade on a towel.
