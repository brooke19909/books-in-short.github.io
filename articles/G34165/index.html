Studio apartment: pros and cons of real estate, reviews of owners

It follows from the latest UN statistics that population growth has begun on our globe. In the light of these events, the real estate and rental markets are constantly struggling to meet the high demand for housing. How to satisfy the growing demand, what type of apartment is currently in demand? Studio apartments managed to occupy a good segment of the market, and with each year the demand for them is growing. In the article, we will talk about what a studio apartment means, what are the pros and cons of such housing.

The target audience

Studio apartments are new generation apartments that differ from the ordinary type of housing in that the living space is combined, there are no stationary walls and partitions. This type of housing is mainly intended for people who have chosen efficiency and practicality, rather than the pursuit of square meters. As a rule, a studio apartment consists of a room in which the living space and a full kitchen area, a bathroom with a plumbing unit are ergonomically combined. If there is a loggia, it can be converted into a study by installing a computer there. Apartments of this type are an ideal option for newlyweds; they are in demand among students, people in creative professions, single people, mostly pensioners.

The main thing that buyers of such real estate value is the opportunity to realize the most daring design decisions. And if we talk about the pros and cons of a studio apartment, in modern realities a small area of ‚Äã‚Äãhousing can significantly save on utilities.

Studio Apartment Sizes

Studio apartments are smaller than standard one-room apartments, but, due to the openness of space and the absence of partitions, the room seems larger. Typically, studio apartments are from 40 square meters. There are also large studio apartments that can accommodate more than 100 square meters, being a studio apartment. Typically, for such apartments use attics located on the upper levels of a residential building, they are also called penthouses.

Layout

According to the definition, a studio apartment (photo below) is a room for all the functions of an ordinary living space: a bedroom, a living room, a kitchen, a place to work. In such an apartment, you can use all the functional space with the help of a designer or independently divide it into zones where you can work and relax. Commonly used separators, which are of different types. It can be a bar that separates the kitchen corner from the general space of the room. Visually separates the kitchen space from the rest with the back turned to the kitchen sofa. An alternative option is also used - a translucent partition located where and when it becomes necessary.

Problems and solutions

If the privacy problem can be eliminated by dividers and partitions, another equally important issue arises - storage of things. Any owner of a studio apartment, acquiring it, must understand that neither dimensional furniture nor a large number of things are compatible with the space of the apartment. Therefore, unnecessary things should be abandoned in order to leave a place for life. Everyone sees in this approach their pros and cons of a studio apartment.

Advantages of a free layout

Studio apartments, according to customer reviews, are best suited for creative people, students and honeymooners. For example, having parties, it is very easy to move the sofa and chairs and arrange a dance floor. A classic dining table of this category of residents is not needed. A bar is very popular with modern youth, where you can quickly organize a buffet. It forms an optical gap, separating the kitchen and living space.

The pluses also include savings on household appliances. One TV can be watched from different points of the apartment. Still, according to the owners of the studio apartments, despite the small area, the living space seems large due to the lack of corridors and good lighting.

How to overcome kitchen odors?

There is another big or small minus of living in such an apartment. The owner must understand and acknowledge that the combination of kitchen and living space has the pros and cons of a studio apartment. In this case, we will talk about the lack of a separate kitchen room. It is believed that the minus in this case will be the smell that will fill the entire room when cooking certain dishes, especially when frying. But, when compared with apartments where there is a separate kitchen, then the aromas also spread quite quickly, and the door to the kitchen does not help. The issue can be solved with a good hood.
