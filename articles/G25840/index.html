Siesta Juniper Hotels 3 *: description, reviews

Our tourists love Turkey for a large selection of accommodation options and an extensive list of services that provide hotels on their territory to vacationers. Therefore, most often the quality of rest in this country is determined by the level of the hotel.

It so happens that three-star hotels in Turkey are not inferior in service, and sometimes even superior to hotels of a higher category.

And this phenomenon is understandable: in small holiday homes they know all their guests. There, the relations between the service staff and the guests are chamber, as hoteliers are well aware that their reputation directly affects the level of income.

One of the budget vacation options is the Turkish hotel Siesta Juniper Hotel 3 *, reviews of which sometimes reach 4 points out of 5 possible. Get to know him better.

Description

Siesta Juniper Hotels 3 * hotel was built in 1996 in Marmaris. It is located on the Aegean coast of Turkey.

The resorts of this region are less numerous in the number of tourists resting than those that differ in the abundance of green spaces and more sparing temperatures in the summer heat. Therefore, do not exclude this convenient place when choosing the option of spending holidays with children.

The distance to the airport is 106 km, and a round-the-clock shuttle takes guests to the place of rest within an hour. The hotel is located 8 kilometers from the center of Marmaris and 300 meters from the coast (3 coastline). The hotel is small, the area - 1800 square meters. meters, but it has everything for a comfortable holiday for tourists.

Accommodation

Siesta Juniper Hotels 3 * hotel is located in two four-story buildings and offers guests only 46 standard rooms, so if you want to book a room in this hotel, you should take care of this in advance.

Room size is 16-17 square meters. meters. It can accommodate two people in the main places and one on the additional one. The Siesta Juniper Hotel 3 * standard (room category) is equipped with individual air conditioning. Each room has a balcony, necessary furniture, a TV with a Russian-language channel, a telephone. The bathroom has a shower, bath accessories (gel, shampoo) are available. Flooring - laminate.

Services

Room cleaning at Siesta Juniper Hotels 3 * is carried out daily, linen is changed 2 times a week. Communication services are provided for a fee in the Internet cafe, and there is a free Wi-Fi zone in the lobby. For the safety of documents and valuables, a safe is available for rent at the reception for an additional fee. The hotel has its own cinema, guests are offered laundry and medical services (doctor on call).

The concept of food at the all-inclusive hotel. You can visit the local restaurant, which, according to visitors, is more like a dining room, because it looks very modest. Nevertheless, tourists talk about a fairly diverse menu, the presence of meat dishes and a large assortment of salads. Many complain that there are no pastries and any delicacies. But the food is of high quality, and there is a sufficient selection of dishes. There are three bars on site that offer refreshing drinks and light snacks.

Entertainment

The hotel does not have an animation team and entertainment programs, which is typical for three-star budget hotels. But doing your favorite things in your free time is not difficult. You can rent billiards, spend time in an organized recreation area by the outdoor pool (free sunbeds and umbrellas), where a section for bathing children is provided .



Guests at Siesta Juniper Hotel 3 * (Marmaris) can use the free beach at Letoile Hotel, as well as umbrellas and sunbeds.

In the village of Icmeler, where the hotel is located, there are many excursion bureaus where you can buy various programs at a very attractive price: a water park, boat trips, trips to historical places and visits to cultural monuments. It is only necessary to recall the need to obtain additional insurance in order to avoid the cost of medical services, which are very impressive.

Most visited excursions in Marmaris:

  • Walk along the Dalyan River with a visit to the complex of rock Lycian tombs.
  • Acquaintance with Istanbul.
  • Aegean islands in Hisaronu Bay. Camellia island with a visit to the Byzantine church of the 12th century, about. Teeth of volcanic origin, about. Rabbits. The program includes swimming in the beautiful bays of the Aegean coast.
  • Visit Demre, the Basilica of St. Nicholas the Wonderworker.

Reviews

Hotel Siesta Juniper Hotels 3 *, whose rating is almost 4 points (3.8 out of 5 possible) - fully corresponds to its price category in terms of service. Most vacationers (88.9%) recommend it for visiting. They note the quality of food, a good beach. There are comments on guest services, but there are still gratitude.

It is noted that if guests behave in a friendly manner and in accordance with the standards of stay in a foreign country, the attitude towards them is correct, and there are no problems. Therefore, when analyzing assessments, it is worth paying attention to the style and content of comments, which very often indicate the true causes of possible conflicts.
