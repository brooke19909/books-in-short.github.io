Marina Byblos: hotel, room description, service

Dubai is a city in the UAE, which is located on the Arabian Peninsula in the south of the Persian Gulf. This is one of the most visited resorts in the world. It is here that affordable prices for holidays, inexpensive cafes with exotic dishes and a lot of different hotels. One of the inexpensive hotels is Marina Byblos hotel 4 *. This is a budget option, but it is no worse than other, more expensive hotels.

Terms of Accommodation

Guests can check into the Marina Byblos Hotel starting at 14:00, and must leave strictly before 12:00. Allowed to stay with children of any age. A child under 6 years old can stay at the hotel completely free of charge, but using existing bedding. An extra bed will have to be paid, the service costs about 2,264 rubles. (130 AED). Children from 6 to 12 years old will be charged 1,741 rubles per night. (100 AED).

Only one extra bed is allowed in one room, which is available only upon request. However, the hotel must be notified in advance of the need for a bed. Unfortunately, pets are not allowed, even for a fee.

We accept 4 types of payment cards:

  • VISA;
  • JCB;
  • MasterCard;
  • AMERICAN EXPRESS.

When booking a room, the necessary amount is blocked on the account until the moment when vacationers arrive at the hotel.

During check-in, guests are required to pay a security deposit of RUB 8,707. (500 AED). However, upon departure, funds are returned to the client.

Prices

Consider the cost of rooms for two adults for a day at the Marina Byblos Hotel. The most profitable option is a double deluxe. Its price is 6 129 rubles. At the same time, cancellation is free and no prepayment is required. However, breakfast is paid - 1,049 rubles (at the request of guests).

The cost of a representative room is 9,864 rubles. This also includes breakfast. Junior suites are the most expensive apartments (11 988 rubles). Breakfast is included in the price, you have to pay in local currency. Prices in rubles are provided for reference.

Nutrition

There are six restaurants on site. They prepare local dishes and world cuisine. Some restaurants offer not only a menu, but also a buffet. If vacationers are not satisfied with meals at the hotel, you can always have a bite to eat in another place, there are many cafes for every taste outside the territory.

The hotel also offers special diet menus upon request. And not far from the pool is a bar where you can enjoy local soft drinks.

Territory

The Marina Byblos Hotel has a comfortable terrace for relaxing. For lovers of active pastime, a night club with a DJ, darts and billiards is offered.

The hotel has three swimming pools. One indoor is located indoors, the second is in the fresh air, and the third is on the roof, which offers amazing views of the countryside. The hotel has a Turkish bath and a roomy sauna.

As for the services, the hotel offers luggage storage, a tour desk, currency exchange, a 24-hour front desk, photocopying, a business center, a banquet room, smoking area, air conditioning, car rental, a gift mini-shop, a beauty salon, and an ATM.

"Marina Byblos." Service

The hotel service is top notch. Firstly, you can always contact the concierge who will help in resolving any issue that relates to residence. Secondly, there are shoeshine, ironing, and laundry services.

Note! Some of the listed services at the Marina Byblos hotel are not included in the price of the permit and are paid separately. Therefore, check the cost of services in advance so as not to get into an uncomfortable situation.

The hotel staff speaks seven languages: Arabic, English, Spanish, Hindi, Russian, Filipino, Ukrainian. Therefore, guests have no barriers in communicating with the hotel staff.

sights

200 meters from the hotel there is a promenade with yachts and boats for rent. You can also visit the cinema or promenade. Not far from the hotel there is a complex of restaurants overlooking Pier 7. There are many places where you can eat or just relax culturally. It is usually crowded, but there is no fuss.

You can go to the lake, which is only 100 meters from the hotel or to the sea, as well as to one of the nearest beaches.

More distant sights: water park, golf course, Mall of the Emirates and City Walk, Gurunanak Darbar Sikh Temple, Burj Khalifa skyscraper and many others.

Reviews

Guests write not only positive comments, but also negative ones. Firstly, some guests are sure that the hotel prices are too high for this quality of service. And secondly, the hotel is quite old, and it needs a good repair. However, for the most part, guests are satisfied with the service, comfort, location, food, room amenities.

Most of all, guests are impressed by the clean beaches, walking distance to the sea, picturesque views, courtesy and courtesy of the staff. That is why many vacationers are ready to come to the Marina Byblos Hotel again.
