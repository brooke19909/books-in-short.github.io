Phuket Island, Thailand

One of the most popular resorts in the world is Phuket, Thailand. Of all the islands that are under the auspices of this state, Phuket is the largest, but at the same time the most tranquil resort. Constantly businessmen gather here, families with children and couples who wish to spend time in silence. The island has a lot of beautiful beaches, rich green vegetation and friendly locals.



Everyone who decides to visit Phuket should know how to travel around this country. Thailand is an island nation, so most of the transport from one resort to another takes place on ferries or boats. To Phuket most often fly from Pattaya, using local airlines. The flight takes about an hour, and you can get from your local airport to your hotel by taxi.

Acceptable for relaxing at any time of the year is a resort called Phuket. Thailand is a hot country in which even in the winter months the air temperature does not drop below 25 degrees. From May to October, the period of heavy rains begins in this region. Tropical showers are most often observed at noon and last an hour or two. The rest of the day is acceptable for beach vacations and outdoor activities. From November to April, a real arid heat is established in this country, which smooths out only a light sea breeze. By the way, the water always warms up to 28-30 degrees, so there are always so many kids here.



Every traveler knows that the turquoise of the Andaman Sea, lush tropical vegetation and snow-white endless beaches, Thailand is different. Phuket, tours for which are sold at any agency, is no exception. There are fashionable recreation areas, and there are also deserted beaches located in cozy and quiet bays. The central, most expensive and luxurious among them is Laguna Beach. Only five-star hotels are located near it, which offer their guests spa treatments, gyms, swimming pools, massage services and so on. Also, on the territory of such hotels there are always luxurious restaurants where you can taste local cuisine. A relatively calm place is Surin Beach, which is located in one of the miniature villages of Phuket. Thailand in this place is manifested in all its glory. Here you can see many cultural monuments, and nearby are the neighborhoods where the local population lives. The beaches are comfortable, equipped with everything necessary and amaze with natural beauty. The most secluded place is Kamala Beach. This place fully comprehends such art as meditation and the ability to relax, which ultimately is what any trip to Thailand is aimed at.
