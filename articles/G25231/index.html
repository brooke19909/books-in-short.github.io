Aerogrill "Redmond": reviews of housewives

This device is intended for use with a conventional multi-cooking pot. A kitchen accessory is in the middle price segment, so almost everyone can afford it.

Features of the air grill

A complete recipe book is attached to the device. It contains step-by-step instructions and photos of dishes that can be prepared in the Redmond air grill. User reviews say that before the first use it is necessary to start the device in self-cleaning mode. Such actions will get rid of the "technical" smells of the new device. To do this, just pour water into the container and add a dishwashing detergent.

It is recommended to wipe the air grill with a damp cloth after each use. Owners report that grills can be washed even in the dishwasher. At maximum power, the device consumes 700 watts.

Public opinion

In order to form an objective opinion, it is enough to study numerous real reviews. Judging by them, the Redmond air grill has small dimensions and low power, which saves energy.

Some users did not manage to cook the meat perfectly the first time, but after a couple of days the owners adapted to the features of using this device.

Many owners are pleased with the Redmond-241 hot air grill. Some comments claim that this device has little power for cooking meat products.

Many users like that the device is ideal for summer cottages and country houses, since the Redmond air grill is universal. Reviews say that in it you can quickly cook not only tasty meat, but also a magnificent cake.

User reviews

Many comments provide information on the exceptional usability of the air grill. Users note that the device is made with sufficient quality and is a great addition to the multicooker. It practically does not get dirty during cooking, so you can just wipe it with a damp cloth.

With this appliance you can quickly cook hot sandwiches, sausages and grilled vegetables, which is confirmed by numerous customer reviews. It is also very easy to use an air grill, since the mechanics of the device are intuitive for every owner.

Reviews note that the device is indispensable in small kitchens, since it has a compact size. Many users liked the special grill for frying, which comes with the appliance. It allows you to cook juicy and tasty meat in minutes. Even a child can use the device, it is so simple.

Negative reviews

Some owners note that it is difficult to distinguish mode labels on the device. Reviews report that the bowl of air grill is very small, so no more than 2 chicken breasts can be cooked at a time.

Users say that the device has low power, so it works for a long time in heating mode. Negative reviews indicate that the air grill is short-lived, as many people have broken the device in the first two weeks. Some owners of the device talk about the high volume at which cooking takes place.

Brief conclusion

Air grill Redmond RAG-241 is an interesting kitchen appliance, which is distinguished by a narrow specialization. He can become a good helper for completing auxiliary tasks and preparing small portions of a wide variety of dishes.

In the process, a multicooker bowl is used, and the air grill itself does not take up much space. In this regard, the use of the device is advisable in small families who seek to save space in the kitchen.

User reviews claim that this appliance is not suitable for cooking large volumes of food. This is due to the fact that not many products are placed in a multi-cooking pot. The air grill is ideal for making sandwiches, portioned pieces of meat and poultry.

This article contains comprehensive information about the Redmond Aerogrill, reviews and price, so potential buyers will be able to objectively assess the advantages and disadvantages of this device.

Satisfied users report that in this price category this device has virtually no competitors. Many note its high build quality, reliability and power. User comments indicate that the purchase of this device will be very useful for the New Year.
