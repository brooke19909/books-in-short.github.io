Taraz hotels: rating of the best, addresses, choice of room, convenience of booking, quality of service, additional services and visitor reviews

In southern Kazakhstan, Taraz is located, a city with an ancient history. He is already more than two thousand years old. The Great Silk Road once passed through it; now archaeological excavations are underway. In the vicinity of the city about 1,500 cultural monuments. It is clear that there are many visitors here, so the question of their placement is quite relevant. Taraz hotels are pleased to offer guests of the city home comfort and the most comfortable living conditions.

Hotel "Comfort"

Hotel "Comfort" in Taraz has the first place in the rating, this hotel is considered the most popular in the city. From other hotels in Taraz, the hotel is conveniently located near the train station. In walking distance there is a supermarket, on foot you can walk to the market. Parking and internet access are free. Meals are organized in the hotel's own restaurant. The hotel has a mini market. Laundry and ironing services are available at a surcharge. Airport transfers are free of charge. To accommodate guests are offered a choice of several room options:

  • standard double with a double bed - 2250 rubles per day;
  • standard double with two single beds - 2880 rubles per day;
  • Junior suite with two single beds - 3240 rubles per day;
  • Suite with a double bed - 3600 rubles per day;
  • Deluxe Studio - 3959 rubles per day;
  • family suite - 4319 rubles per day.

The room rate is based on a good breakfast. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, TV, telephone. Free cancellation is possible three days before arrival. The Uyut Hotel is located in Taraz at the address: Baizak Batyr Street, 249.

Hotel Voyage

Next in the ranking of city hotels is the Voyage Hotel. The hotel has a category of three stars. Within walking distance there is a supermarket, cafes and restaurants, within walking distance there is a market. The hotel area is fenced, has its own secure free parking. Wireless internet access is available for free. Guests can cook their own meals in the well-equipped shared kitchen. The hotel also offers bike hire. Laundry and ironing services are available at a surcharge. An ATM, a vending machine with drinks are installed on the territory. Transfers can be arranged from or to the airport. To accommodate guests in the Voyage Hotel located in the city of Taraz, the following room categories are offered:

  • triple comfort with three single beds - 2250 rubles per day;
  • four-seater comfort with one single, one double bed and a folding sofa - 2610 rubles per day;
  • Suite with two bedrooms (designed for four people) - 3300 rubles per day.

Price includes full English breakfast. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, TV, telephone, coffee maker or kettle. Cancellation of a reservation in a Suite room is free of charge four days before arrival, in other rooms it is free of charge, regardless of the date of cancellation. The Voyage Hotel is located at 1B Michurina Street.
