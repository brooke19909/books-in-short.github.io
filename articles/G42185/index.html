Tool list

Most likely, if you have been fond of knitting for a long time, then you already have everything you need at home, because a knitted bow tie is made from ordinary leftover yarn. And if you don‚Äôt have them, then you can find everything you need in the shops for creativity:

After everything you need is ready, you can safely start crafting knitted ties.

Manufacturing Process: Crocheted Bow Tie

  1. Guided by the scheme, start knitting with a set of air loops. For an average tie size, you need to dial 45-50 loops, but remember that depending on the type of yarn and hook, the sizes will vary. So make sure in advance whether this length suits you (keep in mind that the entire number of loops will be folded in half).
  2. Fold the chain in a circle and continue knitting according to the scheme with single crochet. At this stage, you can change the colors of the yarn, make simple splashes, or even try simple patterns. The total thickness with a length of 45-50 loops should be about 15-20 rows.
  3. With a needle with a thread matching the knitted base of the tie, make several large stitches in the center to form the correct fold in the middle of the bow, as can be seen in the photo above. Thanks to the soft yarn, the fabric is very pliable, so it‚Äôs very easy to get a beautiful shape.
  4. We also knit a jumper according to the scheme, but already from 15 air loops. In this case, it is not necessary to lock everything in the ring.
  5. Knit 3-5 rows of single crochet to the required width, cut the thread and use it to sew a jumper into the ring, wrapping around our knitted base.
  6. Make a strip for the neck from any material by attaching fasteners to its ends for easier use. For a tight-fitting butterfly, even a dark elastic band that can be beautifully designed is suitable, and for a free version, a knitted strip that matches the main accessory will look great.
  7. It remains only to fix the ribbon for the neck on the bow-tie with inconspicuous seams, after which this wonderful bow-tie can be put on and put out immediately!
