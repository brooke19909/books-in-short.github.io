Hotel Dendro Gold Hotel 4 *, Vietnam, Nha Trang: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Located in the south of Vietnam, the city of Nha Trang is considered the capital of beach holidays and the most popular resort. Two hundred thousand people live here, whose main source of income is tourism. A huge army of travelers comes here every year to relax in the lap of wonderful nature and improve their health.



Nha trang

Spend a vacation in Vietnam in recent years, many Russians prefer. And this is not surprising. Swimming in the transparent sea and sunbathing on snow-white sand, mud therapy, wellness treatments at mineral springs with a special composition and medicinal properties - this is not the whole list of benefits of a vacation in Nha Trang.

You can come to the resort all year round. Even in January or February, the weather here is warm, you can swim and sunbathe, however, from October until mid-December winds blow. At this time, surfers come here, for which high waves are simply necessary to indulge in a favorite pastime.

There are many divers in Nha Trang who are attracted by the numerous coral reefs of the coast. In the bays under water you can see sunken ships, underwater grottoes and caves, and admire amazing fish. Tourists at the resort are waiting for equipped beaches - the best in Vietnam, massage parlors and a varied selection of bars, nightclubs and restaurants.



Overview of Nha Trang hotels

Due to its specificity, the resort is replete with hotel complexes and guesthouses. Most of them are located on the coast, literally across the road from the sea. And only one single five-star Ana Mandara is built directly on the coast. In the sector of 5 * or 4 * category hotels, the exception is Dimond Bay and Vin Pearl, which are located far away: one of them is ten kilometers from the resort center, and the other is on the island, which can be reached by the longest cable car in the world, laid above the sea.

Proponents of a budget holiday will always find a guesthouse here, located within walking distance from the beaches, with a cost starting from five dollars per room. In general, Nha Trang is primarily chosen by the Vietnamese themselves.
