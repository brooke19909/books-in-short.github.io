Decoration of a summer cottage. DIY plastic bottle palm

Today, when life is impossible to imagine without plastic bottles, the human imagination regarding the use of this subject knows no bounds.





A bottle of plastic is especially widely used in summer cottages and playgrounds. The figures of animals, flowers and other plants made from it can decorate any interior or exterior.

For example, a palm tree made of plastic bottles, created and installed on a summer cottage, looks amazingly spectacular. And to make it is not at all difficult. To do this, you will need:

  • drill for making holes of various diameters;
  • plastic bottles of green and brown (green for the crown, brown for the barrel);
  • iron rods with a length of 1.5 meters or more (they will play the role of the frame).




Begin tinkering with the trunk. To get the most attractive palm from plastic bottles, you need to cut the bottom of the brown bottle with your own hands at a height of about 20 centimeters, and then cut the cloves about 3-5 cm wide. After that, the workpiece must be put down on the table with the teeth so that it bends the outside, and press down a little. Next, you need to drill a hole in the bottom with a drill of the same diameter as the prepared iron rods, and put the part on the rod. The same actions should be done with all other brown bottles. At the very top, you will need to do about 8 holes staggered for the leaves.

In order for a palm tree made of plastic bottles to create a green crown with its own hands, it is necessary to cut all the bottles of the corresponding color in half (lengthwise), and then use scissors to cut about 5-6 petals on each half on each side and attach the leaves to the trunk using plastic rods (to achieve a more realistic movement, the fasteners should not be too rigid). Depending on the flight of fancy, the design can be of varying degrees of originality.





Threefold tree is better to make a different height and under a different slope. Such a decoration as a palm tree made of plastic bottles, constructed with your own hands, will give a unique chic to any courtyard. If desired, you can create a whole palm grove, which will turn green all year round in any climatic conditions. At the summer cottage, plastic birds and animals will also fall to the place.

Similarly, you can build products from plastic bottles with your own hands and for other purposes. For example, to decorate a playground or city park. Such places will favorably complement all kinds of fabulous animals from plastic bottles. With your own hands, a vase with a bouquet made of this material will decorate any table.





A big plus is the ability to use paint to expand the range of such creations. Not a single child will remain indifferent when they fall into the expanses of a fabulous town inhabited by plastic penguins, colorful parrots sit on beautiful exotic trees, and a motley butterfly or dragonfly is located on a beautiful flower of fantastic shape.
