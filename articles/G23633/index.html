Pension "Kuban" in Gelendzhik: living conditions, reviews

Choosing a place to spend a vacation, many carefully consider different options, study reviews. Thus, it is possible to protect yourself from unpleasant surprises in the form of uncomfortable rooms and poor service. A good local for recreation is considered a boarding house in Gelendzhik "Kuban". Reviews about this place can be heard mostly positive.

About boarding house

Gelendzhik has long been popular with both domestic and foreign tourists. Pension "Kuban", the photo of which can be seen below, is a great place to spend a vacation. The holiday home is located in the heart of Gelendzhik Bay. To the sea - less than a kilometer. Pine park, which is located in the immediate vicinity of the boarding house, makes the air clean, protects from the noise of the resort town. Pension "Kuban" in Gelendzhik is an ideal place to relax with young children.



The area is 0.86 hectares. Here are cozy gazebos, a playground, a pool with sun loungers. The boarding house was built in 2006, so in all rooms the repair remains fresh. Kids in the holiday home are accepted from any age. If necessary, a cot can be added to the room. The boarding house is designed for 250 people. It is almost impossible to book a room during the hot season. Therefore, those planning a vacation at the end of July or in August, should be interested in the availability of available seats at this time in April.

Number of rooms

The apartments are presented in various categories. The most popular, according to reviews, are double rooms of the standard category. In the summer, you will have to pay 1,650 rubles for a daily stay. The room has two beds, a bedside table, a TV, air conditioning, a refrigerator and a bathroom.

Young couples often choose a double standard plus room. Its difference is that the room has a large double bed. For daily accommodation in the summer you will have to pay 1750 rubles. If you believe the reviews, studio rooms are also popular. The room has a large area. In addition to a double bed, the room also has a dining table.



For families with children, two-room rooms with four sleeping places seem convenient. If necessary, one child or adult bed can be delivered. The cost of daily living in such apartments is 2700 rubles. For an extra place, you will have to pay an additional 1200 rubles. For babies up to 4 years old without providing an extra bed, you will have to pay 300 rubles per day.

Nutrition

Good nutrition is an essential element of a good rest. Where to eat in Gelendzhik? Pension "Kuban", reviews of which you can hear for the most part positive, offers high-quality three meals a day like "buffet". Chefs prepare original dishes from organic products. Such food is suitable even for the smallest vacationers. The plus is that guests have the opportunity to independently choose what to eat for breakfast, lunch or dinner. A wide selection of seafood dishes is offered.



The interior of the dining room deserves special attention. Vacationers confirm that it is homely here. The walls are decorated with beautiful still lifes depicting gourmet dishes, which only warms up the appetite.

Entertainment

Who should buy a ticket to the Kuban boarding house? Gelendzhik is a resort city that can please with a huge number of entertainment places. A boarding house should be visited by anyone who wants to get a huge amount of unforgettable emotions during their vacation.

What the beach is worth! Vacationers say that the sand is clean. Therefore, the smallest guests can have a great time building castles. Adults can ride on "bananas" and jet skis, ride off extremely high water slides, etc.



The Golden Bay water park deserves special attention. This is a huge complex of water attractions that are suitable for both children and adults. Incredibly beautiful pools with clear water are a great place to create vivid photos.

Who else should visit the city of Gelendzhik, boarding house "Kuban"? Reviews show that this place is perfect for extreme tourists. A lot of positive impressions will leave a safari park. Here you can see exotic animals in their natural conditions.

Reviews about the rest house "Kuban"

The boarding house in Gelendzhik is not by chance popular. Guests note that it is really cozy and comfortable here. At the same time, housing prices remain affordable even in the hot season. The only negative is that rooms must be booked in advance.

Pension "Kuban" in Gelendzhik gives an excellent opportunity to have a great time with friends or family with minimal cost. The holiday home is open from May to October. But the largest number of visitors is observed in August.
