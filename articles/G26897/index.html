Will the underwater five-star Poseidon Undersea Resort in Fiji be opened?

Hotel Poseidon Undersea Resort is a fantastic project of a five-star hotel underwater in the ocean.

This type of exotic underwater recreation, despite the fact that it is expensive, is very popular. Someone may feel uneasy at the thought of even diving with scuba diving, while others delighted in the opportunity to live in the incredible comfort that is provided in such projects. There are already many such hotels in the world, various seas and oceans can now be explored in Dubai, China, the Maldives, off the coast of Sweden, but the Poseidon Undersea Resort in Fiji should surpass them in luxury and become the best in its category.

Unusual relaxation under water

Using the luxury of an elite hotel, guests of the Poseidon Undersea Resort will be able to observe the life of the underwater world around the pristine reef around the clock, feel the fusion with nature, experience new emotions and impressions. The life of underwater inhabitants takes place in a special, smooth rhythm, which will help to achieve complete relaxation for tireless tourists.





The global goal of such a project in Fiji was to preserve nature. Everyone who wants to enjoy the underwater world goes to special rooms and gets unlimited opportunities to study the life of fish, rays, sharks, without disturbing their peace and ecosystem.

How the Katafinga Island Hotel is structured

The hotel rooms are located at a depth of 12 m, the descent to them takes place in a special elevator.

Everyone is looking forward to the opening.

The opening of the hotel is postponed for various reasons, but the first 150,000 customers who have already expressed a desire to book rooms are ready to fly out tomorrow. They are not afraid of the price of a weekly stay of $ 15,000.





An exciting program has been prepared for them with swimming in spacesuits, flying on planes to neighboring islands, diving on submarines, golf courses, courts, spas and other delights of an expensive vacation.
