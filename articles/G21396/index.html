Robot polisher: models, instructions. Appliances

Washing floors can be easier, faster, more convenient - this guarantees a robot polisher. What is it? And this is a modern device that has unique manufacturability and functionality. Now you can increase the level of cleanliness in the house much faster, because an automatic appliance can do the cleaning. But the most important thing is its versatility: the polisher can do dry or wet cleaning.

What are the features?

A modern robot polisher is a device with an intelligent guidance system, which makes the device easy to move on the floor. A well-thought-out design along with unique technical characteristics makes such devices not only convenient and practical, but also multifunctional. So, a polisher can not only wash floors, but also clean furniture or carpets - however, these functions depend on the characteristics of a particular model.





Such devices are made in an ergonomic smooth form with a rectangular body that extends downward. A high-strength case is used to manufacture the case. Such a robot consists of the following components:

  • the robot itself;
  • power supply unit;
  • navigation block;
  • two batteries;
  • additional accessories in the form of rags, napkins for dry or wet cleaning.

Household robots are easy to control. Due to the navigation unit, cleaning becomes more accurate and easier, and the device itself builds a cleaning route. It is also important that the systems can cope with the cleaning of walls or furniture - for this, their side is used. During operation, the device moves in the same place several times, and this serves as a guarantee of cleanliness. If the navigation unit is turned off, the robot will simply go around the furniture.

What are the benefits?

This household appliance has a number of advantages:

  1. Low noise level: the device works so noiselessly that you can run it in one room and relax in the next room yourself.
  2. Excellent work in an intensive mode of operation both during dry cleaning and with the use of detergents.
  3. Ease of use: the robot polisher simply involves pouring water into a special container, then you need to put on a rag, and the robot can already begin to work.
  4. Perfect surface finish.

With its compact size and light weight, the device can work offline for about three hours, while the device affects even hard-to-reach places when cleaning.

No sounds

This electronic assistant helps to greatly facilitate the cleaning process, and noise can appear only in some cases:

  • at the end of work, the device makes a sound;
  • in the event of a collision with the furniture, a muffled sound may be heard.




The robot polisher can be safely launched even at night: its work will not bring any discomfort for sleeping. As already mentioned, floor polishers are a real smart system that senses swings in the floor. So you don‚Äôt have to worry that when the ladder is found, the robot will fall - it will simply go around such areas. All light things lying on the floor will simply be pushed to the wall. Equally efficiently, the floor polisher copes with hard-to-reach areas regardless of whether it processes parquet flooring or just wooden boards.

How does it work?

The robot perfectly copes with any open spaces, moving around the room from right to left. Furniture for him is a high obstacle, so the robot uses the side specially adapted for these purposes to clean it. A microfiber cloth is simply attached to the side, which will not scratch the floor. After cleaning, the floor polisher returns to its original position - that is, the place where he started cleaning. With dry cleaning, the device copes well with the collection of fine debris, coping even with corners. After dry wet cleaning may be carried out . The instructions noted that wet cleaning is advisable to carry out after dry.

Cleaning Features

If you plan to do the cleaning daily, then the dry cleaning mode is suitable for these purposes. Using this polisher you can remove wool, hair, dust, in general, any household waste, even the most microscopic, from the floor. In this mode, irobot braava 380t can work for about 4 hours, as it does its job thoroughly and leisurely.

Wet cleaning involves thorough cleaning of the floor from dirt, stains, and the floor will be slightly damp. The device is equipped with a special Pro-Clean panel, which has a water tank. It provides uniform wetting wipes throughout the cycle. At the same time, water does not affect the floor itself, so you can not worry about possible damage to the coatings. The robot moves forward, making left and right turns, which makes cleaning efficient and quality. According to the instructions, wet cleaning lasts for 2-2.5 hours.

The manual also says that the robot can be launched in quick cleaning mode. It is good because it lasts faster due to processing only open areas without obstacles. To start the quick cleaning mode, press and hold the Sweep or Mop button for three seconds.

Why do I need a navigation unit?





Not every modern home appliance store will offer to choose a robot polisher that will cope with cleaning automatically without attracting human resources. The work is carried out in accordance with the room map, which is compiled with the NorthStar navigation unit included in the device. According to the instructions, the cube should be located above the floor so that the entire room, which should be cleaned, is clearly visible. Thanks to the navigation cube, several tasks are performed:

  1. An exact map of the room is being built.
  2. The areas to which the robot has not yet reached and which need cleaning are indicated.
  3. Priority is given when cleaning several rooms.

The kit includes one navigation cube, but if you buy several of them, you can make the cleaning area even larger. This component is considered the main one in this type of household appliances. The instructions for the robot-polishers indicate all the features of the operation of the navigation cube.
