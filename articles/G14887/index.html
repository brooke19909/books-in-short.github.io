Hotel Smartline Vasia Village 4 * (Greece, Crete): overview, rooms and reviews

Quite often, people who want to have fun on the beach, choose Greece, namely its island part, as a temporary refuge. And what could be better than Crete? After all, here not only picturesque beaches await the traveler, but also the revived heroes of ancient myths and, of course, sincere hospitality.

On the island there are many resort cities and hotels of various sizes and categories. A rather good place to spend a vacation is the hotel complex Smartline Vasia Village 4 *.

Of course, when choosing a hotel, an experienced tourist pays attention to a lot of factors, including its location, food and accommodation conditions, entertainment and amenities on its territory. Since the hotels actually look and what the former guests say about this place.

What does the territory look like? Location and General Information





Of course, to begin with, it is worth figuring out exactly where the Smartline Vasia Village 4 * hotel complex is located? Crete, a picturesque resort village very close to Sissi beach - it is here, surrounded by other hotels, shops, restaurants and entertainment centers, that you have to spend your vacation.

At 25 km there is an interesting tourist town Agios Nikolaos. By the way, the capital of the island, the city of Heraklion, is also not so far away - about 45 km. You will have to overcome the same distance to the airport (the trip does not take much time).

The Smartline Vasia Village 4 * hotel itself is a complex of small two-story buildings built in a simple but elegant style. Of course, there is a rather spacious private territory with terraces, grounds, lawns, pools and even a private garden. Reviews of tourists say that the hotel complex looks beautiful not only from the outside, but also from the inside.

Hotel Smartline Vasia Village (Greece): description and photos of living rooms





The hotel has 100 rooms - these are both standard double rooms and spacious family rooms, which consist of a bedroom and a spacious living room. Of course, you will have at your disposal a spacious balcony with comfortable furniture, where you can relax while enjoying the fresh sea air.

All rooms are furnished with quality handmade wooden furniture. Of course, guests are provided with household appliances, without which a truly comfortable stay is simply impossible. You can watch TV, use the phone and radio. At your service is an air conditioning system, which is indispensable during periods of summer heat. There is a small safe with a combination lock, where tourists usually store documents and some valuables. There is a minibar with a cooling system in the room, but drinks are delivered only on request.

The bathroom is equipped with quality fixtures. You can use a spacious shower, toilet, washbasin and mirror. Bathrooms have hair dryers for added convenience. You can count on high-quality cosmetics, as well as clean towels that are replaced every day.

Dining at the hotel





Naturally, the question of nutrition is of interest to every tourist. On the territory of the Smartline Vasia Village 4 * hotel there are several catering establishments. The guests themselves are free to choose the scheme suitable for them or even completely refuse to eat at the hotel.

Thalassa Restaurant is located in the main building, where guests are offered delicious dishes of international and Greek cuisine. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served buffet style. Reviews say that the menu here is not bad and the dishes are always fresh, aromatic and tasty.

In the evenings, the Pelagos tavern invites guests to enjoy the most popular Cretan dishes. By the way, there is an outdoor terrace, so guests have dinner in the fresh air. For dinner you can also go to the cozy Italian restaurant La Trattoria. In addition, the hotel has several bars (one of which is located by the pool), where visitors are served light snacks, chilled drinks and strong drinks.

Where to look for a beach? Convenience and entertainment by the sea





Of course, planning a vacation in Crete, tourists are interested in the question of what is the distance between the coast and the Smartline Vasia Village 4 * hotel. Sissi (Sissi), one of the most popular public beaches, is located about 900 m from the hotel complex. A convenient road leads to it, so the constant walks of tourists do not bother.

Vacationers here can easily rent a comfortable sunbed and umbrella - rental prices are quite reasonable. A rescue team is constantly working on the shore. There is also a bar with chilled drinks and some snacks nearby. According to tourists, the beach here is very clean and beautiful, and the entrance to the sea is convenient and cleared. Travelers can go boating, boating, water-skiing, windsurfing and other water sports.

Can I count on an additional service?





On the territory of the Smartline Vasia Village 4 * hotel, they tried to do everything to ensure that the vacation of tourists was not overshadowed by small household problems. Here you can use the laundry service or give clothes to the ironing. Unnecessary things are left in the storage room. Here you can exchange currency (normal rate). There is also free internet access at the hotel.

The staff will help you arrange a transfer or call a taxi. The hotel also offers car and bicycle rental. For a small fee, you can even hire a personal driver. By the way, in case of health problems, be sure to contact the reception - a doctor will be called to you.

Smartline Hotel Vasia Village 3 * (Crete, Greece): how do guests spend their free time?





It's no secret that vacationers spend most of their time on the beach. Nevertheless, you can have fun at the hotel. There is a group of animators who entertain guests until late in the evening, after which they invite to a local night club to dance and relax.

At your service - a swimming pool and a small water park with water slides and other attractions - not only kids but also adults are having fun here. You can have a good time playing football. A large, fully equipped gym is also equipped on the territory - guests can visit here at any time of the day. You should definitely visit the spa center, where clients are offered wellness and beauty treatments, including several types of massage.

And do not forget about excursions, because the island of Crete is the cradle of civilization. There are many museums, architectural and historical monuments worth seeing.

Conditions for a vacation with a child





The Smartline Vasia Village 4 * hotel complex is perfect for parents who decide to spend a vacation with their children, because here tourists are offered modern comfort and a lot of entertainment. Of course, a baby bed will be brought to the room, and in the restaurant you can always pick up something tasty for the baby.

Every day, children can visit the kids club, where they are engaged in art, sports and just have fun in the company of experienced educators and animators. Kids love to splash around in a special shallow pool where the water is always warm. And your child will be able to have a good time on a spacious playground with swings and interesting attractions.

How do tourists respond to this hotel?

It is always interesting to find out what former guests think about the Smartline Vasia Village 4 * hotel. Reviews, by the way, are positive. The hotel is really beautifully and competently equipped, here the guest is offered everything that is needed for a comfortable and fun pastime. The rooms are also well decorated - the furniture is comfortable, household appliances are working properly, the maids are really good at cleaning.

The food is not bad, although the menu is sometimes somewhat monotonous. The distance from the seashore is small. The advantage of the hotel is the availability of conditions for relaxing with a child, which is important for every vacationing parent. Travelers recommend the Smartline Vasia Village 4 * hotel. Sissi is a wonderful place for an unforgettable beach holiday. And the cost of living in a hotel is not so expensive.
