How to adjust the steering wheel in games: tips for gamers

Computer games are unprecedentedly popular. Among them there are full-fledged driving simulators in which you can control the car with the help of the steering wheel and pedals. This conveys the atmosphere and feelings of the driver when driving. However, not everyone knows how to configure the steering wheel for the game. To do this, you must follow clear instructions.

Types of devices

There are many models on the market, among which it is very difficult to choose a decent device. They are expensive, cheap, with vibration, pedals, a handbrake and so on. There are many devices on the market from manufacturers Hori, Defender, Sven and so on. There is no need to wait for the complete transfer of driving from the products of these companies, because these are simplified and cheap models. More serious steering wheels are manufactured by Logitech, Thrusmaster. Models from these brands are of high quality. They are more suitable for a real car simulator, as they well convey all the subtleties of driving.

How the steering wheel conveys sensations

Cheap models cannot offer any additional features. Often, you can just turn the steering wheel and press the pedals on them. More expensive models allow:

  • Feel the return. Thanks to this function, a person feels every hole and roughness on the road. It can also convey the feeling of a car sliding. On heavy turns, the steering wheel can literally break out of the hands.
  • Feel the vibration. High-quality devices are able to transmit the movement of the car through vibration. Also, thanks to this function, a person feels the car more realistically.
  • Realistic twist steering wheel. A variety of manufacturers produce models that can rotate from 180 to 900 degrees.
  • Shift gears. On some devices it is. This allows a person to feel close to real driving.
  • Tactile sensations. Most rudders are made using high quality materials. Some parts have rubber pads. This allows a person to drive virtual cars more comfortably.

Everyone chooses a steering wheel from personal preference. Someone will have a fairly inexpensive model with limited functionality. The main thing is to approach the issue of purchase as responsibly as possible, because it costs decent money.
