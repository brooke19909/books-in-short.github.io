Pearl of the Adriatic - Rimini hotels

Travelers who repeatedly visit different countries of Europe note the constant attractiveness of Rimini, an Italian city in which you can spend a summer vacation with taste and a huge number of impressions .

Tours in Rimini suggest excellent rest on the cleanest beaches, visits to the most interesting monuments of history and culture, as well as accommodation in comfortable hotels. In general, this small Italian town is very nice and convenient, located near the most important excursion points - Venice and Rome, San Marino and Pisa, Bologna, Urbino and the castle of San Leo. It is from this town that it is convenient to travel in any direction, have fun, expand your horizons, and then return to Rimini hotels, comfortable, elite and created in order to serve the pleasure of its guests.

Fun, diverse, popular and loved by many tourists, the resort of Rimini offers an unrivaled vacation on the beaches and in the waters of the Adriatic Sea - a true Italian holiday. This can be done, for example, in the wonderful Accademia Hotel, which is located in the Rivabella area on the Adriatic coast. This complex can offer its guests comfortable rooms with modern decoration, free parking and an excellent restaurant with dishes of not only original and unique Italian cuisine, but also dishes from around the world. This hotel, like many other Rimini hotels, is very well located - from it you can get to the Fiera di Rimini exhibition center, as well as convenient areas for cycling, within a few minutes .

The hotel offers ample opportunities to relax on its own territory - sun terraces and pools, gyms and fitness centers, restaurants, cafes, bars and gardens, bike rental and the opportunity to use the Internet terminal. The hotel is convenient primarily for families who come on vacation with children, as they have all the opportunities for relaxation.
