How to call from Android tablet to phone

You constantly use an Android device and don‚Äôt know for sure whether it is possible to make a phone call through a tablet? Yes, it is quite possible. All you need is the right software with which you will call on landlines and mobiles.

It is no coincidence that many tablets have a built-in microphone and speakers. What software is right for the job? Good question. Below are a few tips on how to make calls from your Android tablet.





Skype

Surely, this idea occurred to everyone. Skype is the most famous analogue of telephone communications and, unlike most Microsoft products, is available on any platform, including Android. What is the quality of communication? Almost all users who met with Skype say that calls and Internet access are fast enough. Of course, there may be occasional failures, but this is more an exception than a rule.

As for the cost of the service, it may vary. Some countries have a subscription that provides unlimited calls to a specific region for a fixed amount. In addition, you can pay per minute. At the same time, calls to landline phones are cheaper than to mobile. In other words, using Skype is the best solution to the question of how to make calls from an Android tablet.





Fring

Skype is probably still the leader in the field of IP-telephony, but this is not the only application. When it comes to VoIP, we can say that new programs appear with enviable regularity. So, the rapid development of the Fring application is primarily due to its positive characteristics.

The Fring service offers calling services to regular telephones called Fringout. The sound quality is very good, but some users complain of a noticeable echo compared to Skype. The tariffs for this connection are reasonable, although very fragmented. In order to test this program in action, install it from GooglePlay and personally test how well it works and how to make calls from the Android tablet when using it.





Google voice

This application is worth talking separately. After a large advertising campaign, launched in 2009, many Android users consider themselves deceived. Despite the fact that the program was announced as a major analogue of Skype, it is still available only to users from the United States. At the same time, it is a very good service that provides users with the ability to call mobile and landline phones, as well as online SMS and voice mail. In this case, the way to make a call is very simple - you just need to click on the button that will connect the user with the caller of the entered phone number. The only inconvenience - to use this application you have to log in to your Gmail account. Therefore, it is worth the wait when the program becomes available everywhere to test it personally and check how to make calls from the Android tablet using it.

However, please note that, depending on the model of your tablet, communication quality may be impaired due to hardware limitations. It happens that users hear the interlocutor poorly, despite the fact that a good headset is connected to the device.

In the end, your gadget is not designed for you to spend hours talking on the phone with it. Nevertheless, it is convenient to use it for calls periodically, and the fact that you can make calls through the tablet cannot but rejoice.
