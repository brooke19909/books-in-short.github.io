Anthea Apt 3 * (Cyprus, Ayia Napa): tourist reviews and photos

Ayia Napa is the youngest, but already very popular tourist resort in Cyprus. The sea in this place of the island is shallow and at the same time very warm. The sand on most local beaches is shallow and soft, and therefore it is convenient for vacationers to sunbathe here. Of course, tourist infrastructure is also well established in Ayia Napa. This also applies to the hotel business. The hotels in this resort are comfortable, and renting rooms in them is inexpensive. For example, guests of Ayia Napa can rent good rooms in the complex Anthea Apt 3 *. Reviews of tourists allow us to judge this hotel as quite comfortable for living.

Location

This comfortable three-star complex is located almost in the center of Ayia Napa. Right next to Anthea Apt 3 * there are bars, cafes and attractions in the city. Not far from the buildings of this hotel there is also a car, bike and motorcycle rental point.





The hotel earned good reviews from tourists, including for its relative proximity to the sea. The city beach is just 400 meters from the complex. Thus, the road to its residents does not take too much time. If desired, the hotel guests can easily reach other popular beaches in Ayia Napa. For example, they will not have to travel to Limanaki for more than 10 minutes, and to Nissi Beach - 20 minutes.

The nearest airport is 45 kilometers from the hotel - in the city of Larnaca. Arriving at the hotel upon arrival at the transfer of tour operators domestic vacationers in about 40-50 minutes. Of course, this is not too long.

What is a hotel

They accept guests at the Anthea Apt 3 * hotel in several buildings of stylish design - one main and eight additional . The territory of this hotel has its own. Judging by the reviews of the guests, she looks pretty pretty. Cleaning in the courtyard of the hotel is often done by its employees. And therefore, the dirty territory of the hotel never happens. In the courtyard of the hotel is a pool of original shape, palm trees grow and lawns are broken.

Reception in this complex is open around the clock. The check-in time at the hotel is 14 hours. On the last day, tourists must leave the room before 11 hours. This hotel was built a long time ago - in 1985.





Most of the tourists in this hotel are young. They usually leave good reviews about the hotel on the Web. This complex is located far from the quietest area. Its guests can hear music from nearby bars until two in the morning. Older vacationers, so the hotel is likely to be not too comfortable. Many tourists do not advise coming to this hotel with children. Young people can have fun at the hotel quite noisy, drink beer in the pool, etc.

Hotel rooms

Apartments in the Anthea Apt 3 * hotel (Cyprus) , of course, are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay of guests. In total, this complex offers Ayia Napa guests about 200 well-furnished rooms. The rooms for tenants rented at the hotel include:

  • safes;

  • wired Internet;

  • air conditioning;

  • refrigerator.

Shower facilities at the complex include hairdryers. Some rooms also have kitchenettes. On the stoves installed here, guests can cook food, and in kettles - boil water.





What do residents think about the rooms rented out at the hotel

The opinion of tourists for 2017 about the rooms of the Anthea Apt 3 * hotel was ambiguous. Some apartments of the complex were renovated in the hotel this summer, while others were not. Accordingly, the hotel rooms differed significantly in terms of comfort. In some rooms, furniture and appliances were installed new, in others - old.

As for the cleanliness in the rooms handed over at the hotel by tourists to the administration of the complex, there are no special complaints in any case. Housekeepers often clean the hotel apartments. And the hotel rooms look quite neat and tidy.

The balconies in the rooms at this hotel are quite large. These guests, of course, are also attributed to the advantages of the hotel. On the balconies in the rooms of this hotel, residents can not only dry beach accessories, but also sit, for example, with a cup of tea. Especially for this, plastic tables and chairs are installed here. But balconies in this hotel are not available in all rooms.





There are not too good reviews about Anthea Apt 3 * due to the fact that many ants live in the apartments of this hotel. To leave food here unpacked or to crumble in any case is not worth it. Also, some tourists note that in the showers of this hotel in the rooms there may not be a hairdryer. The lack of utensils and tools is sometimes observed in the kitchens in the hotel rooms. Therefore, tourists who decide to rent a room in this complex for tourists, knives, sponges and detergents, may be worth taking with them on the road from home.

Infrastructure

The hotel for tourists, of course, also provides a number of amenities. Hotel guests can check in things at the luggage room. True, tenants have to pay separately for this service in Anthea Apt 3 *. Wi-Fi is also available at this hotel. But for the password in the hotel, tourists also have to pay a certain amount.

Free at this hotel guests can swim in the pool or sunbathe on the sun loungers installed in the courtyard. There is no need to pay at Anthea for car parking. The hotel also has a paddling pool . In addition, a cot is available in the room upon request.

Opinion of vacationers about hotel infrastructure

In general, the rooms of the Anthea Apt 3 * hotel (Ayia Napa, Cyprus) were served by relatively good reviews of tourists. The infrastructure of this hotel is aimed mainly at young people. By the pool, for example, here, from ten in the morning until seven in the evening, music plays, and quite loud. In the same pool, teenagers are constantly sitting and drinking beer. Young people in this hotel can talk, sing and make noise loudly on balconies. And sometimes even until three in the morning.





The administration of the hotel usually does not react to complaints from older guests about noise. That is why the hotel is recommended, including on the sites of some tour operators, as the most suitable for young people.

The staff of this hotel, most tourists do not consider particularly polite. Tourists from around the world have a rest in this complex. But judging by the available reviews, hotel guests sometimes treat the Russian guests, unfortunately, not too kindly.

Beach Hotels

On foot to the sea, guests of Anthea Apt 3 * (Ayia Napa) need to go no more than 10 minutes. If desired, the residents of the hotel, as already mentioned, can visit in Ayia Napa and other beaches popular with tourists. A trip here by taxi will cost the residents of the complex not too expensive.






In any case, judging by the reviews, the sea on all the beaches of Ayia Napa is very clean, warm and calm. Spending time almost anywhere on the shore in this city will be very nice.
