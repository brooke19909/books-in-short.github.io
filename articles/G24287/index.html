How to get guardianship benefits

Not many people cope with this task and are ready to assume such complex responsibilities. This is evidenced by a huge number of boarding schools and orphanages. However, those who undertook to do such a good deed will never regret it.

The state does not leave such families alone with their misfortune. Monthly they receive guardianship allowances, organize special charity programs for orphans and half- orphans to promote their development. However, in order to receive such benefits, it is necessary that the child undergo training in educational institutions. If he does not, guardianship benefits are not paid.
