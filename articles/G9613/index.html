Here, guests are taken care of even before they set foot on the land of the Perm Territory. At the airport, train station or port, a minibus driver will be waiting for you.



general description

The city of Perm is located on the banks of the beautiful Kama River. Park-Hotel 4 * is one of the best hotels, which is located 15 km from the city center. As a result, guests get the opportunity to completely immerse themselves in the atmosphere of comfort and tranquility, take a walk in the park area, and can also head to the historical center of the city without interference. Park-Hotel (Perm) is surrounded by a pine forest on the banks of the Kama River and a 20-minute drive from the airport. The windows offer a wonderful view of the river valley and the forest.
