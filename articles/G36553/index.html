The best hotels of London: description and photos

Whoever would like to visit one of the most beautiful capitals of the world - London. Everyone knows such attractions of the city as the Thames River, the Tate Gallery, the mysterious Tower, the wax museum and, finally, the Big Ben tower clock is the symbol of the city. Central London hotels are a great choice to see all these places with your own eyes. The city constantly receives guests - tourists arriving from different parts of the world, the business elite, the European aristocracy. Therefore, of course, there are many hotels of different categories: approximately 600 different places to choose an overnight stay. The best London hotels are not cheap. But, if possible, you can book a room in advance in one of these hotels. This article will help to understand this issue.

Hotel Savoy

The Savoy hotel complex is made with London chic and British nobility. Hotels in London (5 stars) are often visited by various famous personalities. Among the guests of Savoy are members of the royal family, presidents and ministers, and stars of show business. The fact of history that a ball was held in honor of the coronation of Elizabeth II already speaks of his status. The Savoy Hotel (London) allows its guests to plunge headlong into the world of the British aristocracy, to imbue with the spirit of great historical events, and at the same time to enjoy a comfortable luxurious atmosphere.
