Euronapa Hotel Apartments 3 * (Ayia Napa): Travel Reviews

Cyprus is a very popular tourist country that has a lot to offer travelers. For example, you can count on clean beaches and warm sea waves, as well as the indescribable beauty of nature and decent living conditions. Indeed, on the coast, especially when it comes to such famous resort areas as Ayia Napa, many hotels and pensions of various categories have been built. One of the popular is the Euronapa Hotel Apartments 3 * hotel.

Of course, to begin with, every tourist will try to find the maximum amount of useful information. For example, where exactly is the hotel located and will you have to go far to the sea? Are there any conditions for relaxing with a child? What kind of service and accommodation can guests expect? How do other people respond to this hotel?

Description of location and hotel territory

Of course, travelers are primarily interested in the location of the Euronapa Hotel Apartments 3 * hotel. Cyprus, namely, the vibrant resort town of Ayia Napa - this is where the cozy hotel is located. Nearby are many shops, cafes and restaurants, and the distance to the city center with all its capabilities is only 300 m. But Larnaca Airport is 55 km away. It is not worth worrying about the long distance, as the hotel, as well as travel agencies, offers its customers a comfortable transfer.





The hotel itself is a complex of small two-story bungalows, which, in fact, are reserved for living rooms. The hotel territory is a real garden with dense groves, palm trees, bright flowers, walking paths and terraces with evenly trimmed lawns. You can relax in peace and quiet, enjoying the fresh air and the incredible beauty of nature.

Hotel Euronapa Hotel Apartments 3 *: photo and a brief description of the rooms

The hotel offers tourists check in one of 83 rooms. These are either spacious studios designed to accommodate 1-3 people, or large two-room rooms that are great for families and large companies. Depending on the location, you will have either a spacious terrace or a balcony.





You can count on comfortable furniture and a set of necessary household appliances. There is a heating and air conditioning system. For a small fee, you can rent a safe. The room has a TV, radio, telephone and minibar, which, however, can only be filled on request and at an additional cost. Some units include a well-equipped kitchenette with a dining table, electric kettle, stove and kitchenware.

The bathroom is equipped with new fixtures. You can relax in a warm bath or take a shower. Towels and hygiene products are also provided. Some rooms have a hairdryer.

What kind of meal plan do guests offer?

Delicious food is an essential attribute of a really good vacation. So what does the hotel Euronapa Hotel Apartments 3 * offer? Reviews say that in a local restaurant you can eat deliciously. By the way, paying for the room, you also get the right to free breakfast. If you wish, you can pay for dinners or reserve a table and pay each bill separately.





The menu is quite varied - you can enjoy delicious dishes of international, Cypriot and Mediterranean cuisine. In the lounge bar you can relax with a cup of coffee or a glass of strong drink. There is also a snack bar in the courtyard, which serves cocktails, chilled drinks, salads, light snacks, desserts and fresh pastries.

Do not forget that near the hotel and in the city center there is a huge number of cafes and other catering establishments where you can eat, have fun and just have a good time.

How close is the beach?

Hotel Euronapa Hotel Apartments 3 * is located very close to the beach. For example, the popular Sandy Bay Beach is just a 5-minute walk away. A 15-minute walk takes you to Limanaki Beach and Nissi. As you can see, the walk will not take much time.





The beaches here are public, but you can always find a relatively calm place. The weather is warm, and the sea is calm in most cases. There are shallow areas that are suitable for bathing children. For a small fee, you can rent a sun lounger with a mattress, a beach towel and an umbrella. Near the shore there are many bars and cafes.

Water activities for tourists

Fans of more active relaxation will not have to be bored with boredom, as it is easy to rent a boat, canoe or catamaran on a public beach.

A vivid experience will give a boat trip or water skiing. There are conditions for windsurfing and sailing. Near the beach there is a sports center where you can not only rent the necessary equipment, but also take a few lessons from professional trainers. Tourists can also go scuba diving, as there are diving schools.

Does the hotel offer an additional service?

Guests of the Euronapa Hotel Apartments 3 * hotel can count on some additional conditions. For example, there is a spacious parking lot, which tourists use for free. By the way, in the city you can easily rent a car or other type of transport.





The hotel also offers laundry services and currency exchange. Almost throughout the territory there is wireless Internet access. In case of problems with well-being, a doctor will be called to you, assistance of which is paid separately. The hotel also has luggage storage, which is convenient for tourists planning to travel around the island.

How do guests have fun? Leisure and free time

A beautiful beach and entertainment in the central part of the city - every traveler can count on it. Nevertheless, you can find an interesting lesson on the territory of the hotel itself. For example, in the morning or in the afternoon summer heat it is always pleasant to refresh yourself in the large pool. Around him are sunbeds and sun umbrellas. Guests can rely on clean beach towels.





Tennis enthusiasts can have a good time on the local court, next to which you can rent rackets and balls. Also, guests love to have fun playing billiards, darts and table tennis.

A sports and fitness center with a spacious gym is constantly working, which will delight tourists who want to keep fit. Here you can sign up for a professional massage or use the sauna.

In the evenings, the hotel often organizes interesting performances and dances with live music. And do not forget about excursions, because the history and culture of Cyprus is really interesting.

Are there any conditions for spending time with children?

Of course, parents who decide to travel with their children first of all wonder whether they should stay with their kids at the Euronapa Hotel Apartments. Ayia Napa is a great place that can offer young tourists a lot of entertainment and attractions. And the hotel itself has the necessary amenities. For example, you will be sure to bring a rollaway bed to the room, although most parents prefer to stay in spacious two-room family rooms. In the restaurant, a caring waiter will offer a highchair, and among the dishes it is always easy to choose something tasty and healthy.

In addition, the hotel has a spacious, but shallow pool, where kids can splash and have fun. And for young tourists there is a good playground with swings, slides and sandboxes. Here, children can spend time with parents and meet peers from all over the world. For an additional fee, you can hire a professional nanny who will be happy to look after your child for several hours.
