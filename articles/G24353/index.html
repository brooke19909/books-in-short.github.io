Herald costume: how to make it yourself

Modern New Year's morning performances are distinguished by complex scenarios in which any animals, people, objects, etc. can be involved as heroes. That is why any mother should be prepared that one day her son or daughter will be informed that they have been assigned the role of an alien monster, a forest sorcerer, dough or fairy mountains. In such cases, you have to rack your brains over what to wear a boy or girl, since no one saw what these characters should look like on stage. What the herald costume consists of

If we are not talking about a particular era, then you can make an outfit in medieval European style. It should consist of:

  • blouses
  • pantalon below the knees;
  • vest;
  • hats with wide brim;
  • a shoe or shoe;
  • stockings;
  • belt with a large buckle.

What fabrics and accessories to choose

To make a beautiful herald costume (see photo above), different textiles will be required.

For a costume, you can take emerald gabardine, a slanting inlay of blue color, the same satin, thin lace lace of white color and satin lace of dark blue. In addition, you will need a canvas made of cotton, shoes, a lush feather and a brooch. You will also need to purchase a hat with wide brim matching color and white tights.

Blouse

A herald costume cannot be imagined without this wardrobe item. She is sewn from blue satin using the simplest pattern that can be found in any fashion magazine. Further:

  • the neck of the blouse, which is fastened on the back with an air loop, is trimmed with white lace;
  • white cotton sew cuffs;
  • trim them with thin white lace;
  • sew to the sleeves.




Vest

The herald costume will not be complete without this wardrobe item. For its manufacture:

  • take any suitable pattern from the fashion magazine;
  • they cut light emerald gabardine;
  • stitch the parts on a sewing machine;
  • they are decorated with patch pockets and armholes with an oblique edging, with it the sides and bottom of the vest are treated with it;
  • sewn to the neck a large collar trimmed with fine lace and made of white cotton.

Trousers

Pantaloons are cut and sewed from emerald gabardine. For this purpose, a pattern of free shorts is suitable. When they are ready, they bend and sew the bottom, decorate with a thin satin braid. Thread the gum and tighten it slightly.





Hat

It is not necessary to make such an accessory yourself. Instead, you can take a finished hat of a dark color, bend the fields from front to top and attach a brooch and a fluffy feather.

Stockings

Of course, some boys will resist wearing a girl's wardrobe item. In this case, show them a portrait of some formidable medieval king and explain that many centuries ago, everyone dressed like that, from artisans to brave knights. As a complement to the suit, white translucent tights of the appropriate size are suitable.

Belt

It can also be purchased ready-made. However, the manufacture of such an accessory also does not require much time and effort. In particular, you can take a buckle from some old belt. It is necessary to paste on both sides with a thin double-sided tape and wrap satin braid on it. The belt itself is sewn from the remnants of fabric in the form of a strip reinforced with non-woven fabric. Then the belt is sewn to the buckle.





Shoes

Surely, your little son will have a pair of dark-colored boots. Just make large gabardine bows and fasten on shoes.

Trumpet

As you know, heralds in ancient times informed the people of royal decrees. In order to gather people and read a letter to them with news from the palace, such servants had to blow a pipe. Making this accessory is not difficult. To do this, you need to twist a cylinder with a diameter of 5 cm and a length of about 30 cm from thick cardboard, as well as a cone 10 cm high and a base radius of 5 cm. Then:

  • the second preform is inserted into the first and glued from the outside using a heat gun;
  • take a piece of thick, hard bend wire;
  • make two holes on the pipe;
  • insert the handle into the pipe;
  • glue the resulting design with gold foil and get an accessory that will successfully complement the herald costume.

If all these manipulations seem complicated to you, then you can look for the old pioneer horn or purchase a toy. In the latter case, it may have to be painted with bronze paint.
