Fly DS107D: flawless economy mobile phone

Fly DS107D - This is an entry-level push-button cell phone. It has everything you need to make calls, send and receive MMS and SMS, as well as view sites. Another important plus of this model is a very, very democratic price. All this makes it one of the best deals in the segment of economy class mobile phones.

Cell phone niche

Phone Fly DS107D is the solution for the most budget segment of mobile devices. In this niche, the cost of the device comes to the fore, and only then do potential owners look in the direction of technical specifications.





The price of the gadget is really democratic, but the technical parameters are acceptable. This mobile phone is aimed at those who need a familiar device with a regular keyboard and basic functionality.

Design and execution

This mobile device is made in the form factor of the case, which is called a monoblock. It does not have a touch screen, but there is a familiar button keyboard for many, with which the device is controlled. Above the keyboard is a display with a modest diagonal of 1.77 inches by current standards, manufactured using TFT technology. Its resolution is only 128x160. Even higher is the earpiece. An audio port is displayed on the left side of the device, and a USB port on its upper end. On the back cover there is only a camera and a flashlight diode.

Mobile device specifications

Not bad with hardware stuffing in Fly DS107D. Specifications he has in this regard:

  • The SC6531DA processor was developed by Speactrum. It has only one processing core, and the frequency is 312 MHz.

  • The amount of RAM is 32 KB. The exact same capacity of the built-in drive.

  • It is possible to install a memory card. Its maximum size can reach 8 GB.

  • The device can be installed immediately 2 SIM cards. Second generation cellular networks (i.e. GSM) are supported.

  • When connected to the Internet, data can be transferred using WAP or GPRS technology. This allows you to get a speed of several tens of kilobytes, and this is enough only to download mostly mobile versions of Internet resources.

Camera

The camera is quite modest in this cell phone. She has a sensor element of only 0.3 megapixels. Expect photos of acceptable quality in this case is not necessary. The same can be said of video recording. The resolution of the rollers is 320x240. Normally, you can view them only on the phone screen, but using modern TVs for these purposes is not recommended. The image will turn into squares, and something to parse in such a record certainly will not work. Device autonomy

The capacity of the complete battery for this cell phone is 800 mAh. According to the assurances of the developer, this in communication mode is enough for 3 hours. If you use this device only in standby mode, then one battery charge is enough for 200 hours of continuous operation.





In reality, with an average mode of operation, you can count on 2-3 days. You can increase this value by connecting an external battery to the phone. But to reduce the previously indicated value is possible only in the case of active Internet surfing. In this case, you have to charge the phone every day.
