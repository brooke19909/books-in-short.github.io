Uralochka - recreation center on the Taman Peninsula

Uralochka is a recreation center located at the southern tip of the Taman Peninsula. The village of Veselovka, in which the hotel is located, is a cozy and quite quiet place on the shores of the gentle Black Sea. Immersed in greenery and flowers, this base will be a great place for a summer vacation with the whole family. And in Russia, relaxation is possible, close to all inclusive, although it is much more modest.

Where is Uralochka (recreation center)

On the shores of the sunny Taman Peninsula, just a few kilometers northwest of Anapa, lies the village of Veselovka. Still, the warm gentle sea, beaches of sand and small shell rock, deposits of medicinal mud in the vicinity make the peninsula a real gem of the south of Russia. There are more sunny days than in neighboring Gelendzhik or Sochi.



Number of rooms

The recreation center "Uralochka" in Veselovka represents 19 separate buildings. In total, 90 rooms of various comforts are available. Nearby free parking for 20 cars. The rooms are various, from standard to luxury. All houses are equipped with modern furniture and appliances. In principle, the living conditions are quite simple, but usually a person who comes to the sea does not sit in the room.

Uralochka is a recreation center (photo in the article), which has accommodation options for any wallet. There are 11 rooms of category economy II. They cost from 800 to 900 rubles (for each resident), depending on the time of year. They are a fairly simple dwelling with two single beds, bedside tables, chairs. There is a mirror and, importantly for the resorts of the Krasnodar Territory, a fan. A bathroom and shower are in the courtyard. Almost the same living conditions in economy category I rooms with the difference that there is also a TV, refrigerator and air conditioning (and not just a fan). Prices for accommodation here are also higher: from 1000 to 1100 rubles per day.

There are also 12 standard rooms in Uralochka. It can accommodate from three to four people, so the cost of living is lower: from 650 to 900 rubles per person, depending on the time of year. A bathroom and shower are already located in the room.

Suites at the recreation center are one- and two-bedroom. They can accommodate from three to five people. A hairdryer, a kettle and other pleasant little things are added to the above amenities. There are luxury rooms from 2400 to 4800 rubles per day.

All prices must first be checked with the operator when ordering a room in Uralochka by phone.

For those wishing to relax, it is important to know that to guarantee a reservation, an advance payment of 10 percent of the cost is required. Rooms are booked by phone. Interestingly, in the absence of tickets at the ticket office, employees themselves book a train ticket to Anapa, and a transfer is provided from the city itself: guests get by bus to the base itself.

Children under three years old stay free of charge. Then there is a 10 percent discount for those who are not yet ten.

Unfortunately, pets are not allowed to bring pets.

What is included in the stay

"Uralochka" (recreation center) provides vacationers with wireless Internet, laundry, ironing board with iron, satellite TV, microwave. You can use the barbecue or barbecue, sit in the evenings in cozy gazebos on the premises. Little guests can play in the playground. Room cleaning is done every three days. Towels and bed linen are changed once a week. As in the resorts of Turkey and Egypt, the Uralochka also has animation: there is a DJ, discos and competitions are held. Guests will definitely not get bored.

For guests the opportunity to take a steam bath in a real Russian bath. If you get tired of going 200 meters to the sea, you can swim in the pool on the territory. This is a great option for leisure activities if the water is cold or if there are strong waves.





For an additional fee, excursions are offered along the Taman Peninsula and to other places in the Krasnodar Territory: the Hephaestus mud volcano, crocodile and ostrich farms, dolphinarium, etc.

Food at Uralochka

For 750 rubles, guests of the recreation center receive complex lunches, breakfasts and dinners. On the territory there is a dining room, as well as a cafe at the recreation center "Uralochka". Reviews about nutrition are generally positive. This is a simple Russian cuisine, generous gifts of the Kuban. The only thing that often visitors scold service. Here, up to 120 people can be served at the same time, so there will be enough space for everyone.





What is nearby

The base is within walking distance from key places in the village.

To the sandy beach walk 5 minutes.



The center of Veselovka is 20 minutes from Uralochka. The recreation center is also within walking distance from the entertainment center and grocery stores (5 min.). The nearest bus stop is also just a few minutes away.

To the north is Salt Lake and its healing mud. It can also be easily reached on foot.
