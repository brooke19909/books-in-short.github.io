The route "Izhevsk - St. Petersburg": options for the path

The city of St. Petersburg is the second largest settlement in our country. Izhevsk is a large industrial center of the Urals, connecting the European part of Russia with the Urals and Siberia. The direction "Izhevsk - St. Petersburg" is one of the most popular. How can I get from Izhevsk to St. Petersburg? What is the most profitable way to overcome the route?

Izhevsk - St. Petersburg: distance and route options

You can get from Izhevsk to the northern capital by your own transport, train or plane. The distance between cities, measured directly, is about 1370 km.





Izhevsk - St. Petersburg: by car

The length of the route when traveling from Izhevsk to St. Petersburg by car will be about 1800 km. The path will pass through the main transport routes of the Vologda, Kirov, Kostroma, Leningrad, Yaroslavl regions and the Republic of Udmurtia. The time that travelers will need to overcome the route will be from 28 to 32 hours when traveling non-stop. You should also take into account the consumption of gasoline - about 1 liter per 1 km of track, because stops for refueling will be simply necessary. Thus, the cost of a car trip will be from 5 to 7 thousand rubles, depending on the cost of gasoline. Some travelers prefer to go to St. Petersburg through Moscow due to the fact that the roads on the direct route are not of the best quality. However, the cost of the trip, as well as the total travel time, will increase significantly.

By train

The second option to overcome the route is traveling by train. Trains running along the Izhevsk-Saint-Petersburg route depart only on even numbers and arrive at Ladoga Station. The total length of the route is 1955 km, and the travel time is about 32 hours. The cost of tickets in the reserved seat is about 2 thousand rubles, and in the compartment - about 4. The route passes through Moscow (parking at the Kursk station is assumed). Tickets can be purchased at ticket offices of transagencies or via the Internet.





By plane

The fastest transport plying in the direction of Izhevsk-Saint Petersburg is an airplane. Direct flights with I8 925 number are operated from Izhevsk airport to Pulkovo Petersburg every 2 days by the Russian regional airline Izhavia. The direct flight is 1,376 km or 855 miles, and the total flight time is about 3.5 hours. The cost of plane tickets is from 4 to 10 thousand rubles, it depends on the class of service chosen by the passenger and the season in which the trip is made. Also, passengers can buy tickets for connecting flights with transfers at one of the major regional airports where Izhavia operates flights, for example, to Domodedovo, Koltsovo, Strigino. It is important to note that the cost and total flight time in this case will increase.

Combined Options

In addition to the above methods of overcoming this route, it is possible to get to the northern capital from Izhevsk in a different way. For example, a passenger can travel to Moscow by train or car, and then fly to St. Petersburg by plane. Or vice versa - first use the plane, then by train or car.





Thus, the route "Izhevsk - St. Petersburg" can be overcome by plane, train or car. The fastest and most comfortable way is to fly a plane. The most reliable and less expensive option is traveling by train. The length of the route between cities is from 1370 to 1955 km, depending on the chosen travel method. When planning a trip, it is also worth considering its cost and total travel time.
