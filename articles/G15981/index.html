All-inclusive hotels in the UAE: overview, description, reviews

In the world of tourism today, the United Arab Emirates (UAE) is one of the most interesting and promising areas. Here tourists can offer everything that their soul desires. This country, consisting of 7 independent emirates, is known for having the tallest buildings in the world. In the coastal waters of the Persian Gulf, many artificial bulk islands have been created on which the most luxurious (all-inclusive category) hotels in the UAE are located.

In this article we will consider those that, according to many tourists, deserve special praise because of the excellent service by the hotel staff, varied and tasty food in restaurants, the thought-out infrastructure of hotel complexes, allowing you to relax in comfort, etc.

Having made the decision to buy a tour to this fabulously beautiful country, you can be sure that you will never regret it and bring with you from your trip unforgettable impressions for life!

As mentioned above, the UAE state includes seven independent emirates, completely different from each other. These are Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman, Fujairah, Ras al-Khaimah, Abu Dhabi and Umm al-Quwain. And before choosing the right hotel for yourself, you need to decide on the emirate itself.

Which hotel is better to stay in

If this is your first trip to the Emirates, it will be interesting for you to find out which hotels are best to choose from all-inclusive in the UAE. Experienced tourists in their reviews often advise before settling in a hotel to test it according to some criteria, since it is clearly not enough to distinguish one or another hotel from many others simply because of good service and beautiful design. Consider some of the nuances:

  • If the hotel is not on the first line, then it does not have its own beach. At the same time, he provides a free shuttle service to one of the public ones. As a rule, such beaches are not equipped, so before you settle in such a hotel, find out if it is possible to rent such things as a deck chair, mattress, beach umbrella, etc.
  • If you have chosen the type of food "breakfast and dinner" when you check into the hotel, then you should ask if it is possible to exchange dinner for lunch if you wish. This is very convenient, since you will not lose a paid meal if you have planned an event at this time. All expensive hotels, as a rule, provide such an opportunity.
  • In almost all budget hotels, one may encounter such a problem as poor Internet. If during the holidays you must constantly keep in touch with your office, or your activity is related to freelance, then you better get acquainted in advance with the reviews of tourists who have already encountered this in this hotel. In luxury hotels, there are no such problems.

Royal M Hotel Resort Abu Dhabi 5 * (Abu Dhabi)

More recently, in the UAE capital, Abu Dhabi, such an industry as tourism, despite the fact that there is also a sea, was not there. Now in the largest emirate, intensive construction of hotels has begun. The rapid development of the tourism industry began. Today in the emirate of Abu Dhabi, the Royal M Hotel & Resort Abu Dhabi is considered the most famous resort hotel.

It is located in one of the best places in the capital, which makes it the most popular among city guests, in comparison with other hotels. One of the attractions of the emirate is the medieval fortress of Qasr al-Hosn, it is located just five kilometers from the hotel. At a distance of 6 km, next to the desert, there is a large and beautiful park Umm al-Emirat. Abu Dhabi International Airport is only 35 km away.

Royal M Hotel & Resort Abu Dhabi offers its guests comfortable deluxe and premium double rooms with air conditioning and free Wi-Fi. Concierge services are available. The hotel rooms are equipped with a private bathroom. From their windows a wonderful view of the sea opens.

It features an outdoor pool, and a fitness center. There is a restaurant. It serves a daily breakfast buffet.

Nasima Royal Hotel 5 * (Dubai)

When the UAE is mentioned in a conversation, then everyone immediately imagines the most famous of all emirates - Dubai, its skyscrapers, the bulk island of Palma, as well as the world-famous hotel in the form of a huge sail.

Here, not far from the World Trade Center, the luxurious 5 * Nassima Royal Hotel is located. The hotel offers its guests 471 rooms and suites, decorated in a modern style. Each room has air conditioning, high-speed free Internet, TV. There is also a bathroom and toilet.

The hotel has restaurants serving dishes for every taste, as well as several bars. There is an outdoor pool with wonderful views of the Jumeirah beach. Judging by the reviews, some vacationers noted high prices for accommodation as a small drawback of this hotel, everything else is at the highest level.

Miramar Al-Aka 5 * (Fujairah)

Fujairah is the only emirate located on the coast of the non-Persian Gulf in the UAE. All-inclusive hotels in the first line harmoniously fit into the surrounding landscape, lining up along the coast between the Indian Ocean and the foothills of the Omani Range. If you came to the Emirates for a beach holiday, then you will not find a better place than Fujairah.

Numerous vacationers in their reviews call the 5 * Miramar Al Aqah Beach Resort, the most suitable for those who are fans of various types of water sports, as here they can engage in windsurfing, diving, kayaking, etc.

It offers luxurious apartments with balconies with fantastic ocean views. All apartments are equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV and a minibar.

The hotel has 2 swimming pools: indoor and outdoor, a spa with two saunas and a hot tub, restaurants and bars. For tennis lovers there are several courts.

Palm Beach 4 * (Umm Al Quwain)

This Umm Al Quwain Emirate Resort & Spa is just a 45-minute drive from Dubai International Airport. Palm Beach - 4 * hotel, which serves its guests on an all-inclusive basis. In the UAE, hotels of this class, due to their affordability, are gaining more and more popularity every year among tourists from Russia and the CIS countries.

Restaurant Palma offers vacationers a wide range of national dishes prepared with fresh products. The coffee shop of the same name, located on the beach, serves light snacks and drinks.

Palm Beach Resort has a private equipped beach, spa, three swimming pools, one of which is for children. It offers rooms with free Wi-Fi, satellite TV, a DVD player and a minibar.

Holidays in the UAE with children

In the UAE, all-inclusive hotels are always especially popular with those tourists who usually go on vacation with their families. The best hotels where you can relax with your children, according to reviews by many beach lovers, are located in such emirates as Ras al-Khaimah and Fujairah. All of them offer three meals a day, including a specially designed children's menu, which means that the question of how to feed a child disappears by itself.
