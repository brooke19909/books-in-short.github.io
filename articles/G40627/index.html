Hotel "Metallurg", Moscow: reviews, photos, address

This article will examine in detail such an interesting establishment as the Metallurg Hotel (Moscow): address, how to get to it, reviews, advantages and disadvantages of living in it.

Total information

Hotel Metallurg (Moscow) is an excellent choice for those who want an inexpensive and comfortable stay near the center of the capital. Metallurg is located in the Meshchansky district. The public transport network in this quiet area is well developed. There are many land transport stops in the area. From Metallurg to the Kremlin and Red Square some 10 minutes by metro.



Metallurg was built in 1988. It can be classified as an economy class - the hotel owns 3 stars. The number of rooms is 169 units of excellent rooms, there is an elevator.

The hotel can accommodate up to 380 people. Thanks to the block structure of some rooms, it is possible to accommodate groups of guests. Groups from 10 people are offered a discount (for stays of three days or more). The hotel provides four categories of rooms. 24-hour internet is available on site.

Number of rooms

Hotel Metallurg (Moscow) can provide rooms with one room for single and double occupancy of the category "standard" and "improved". Affordable accommodation of groups of guests in block rooms from two separate rooms is available. Such blocks are designed for three to five guests - the best option for those who prefer an economical stay.



Internal equipment includes a telephone, a TV, a kettle and a set of dishes for tea / coffee, a refrigerator (when placed in blocks - one refrigerator per block) and a separate bathroom. Some rooms are air-conditioned. There are family rooms and non-smoking rooms.

Business services



Hotel Metallurg (Moscow) can offer guests the Virtual Office service. In addition, the hotel has a meeting room for up to 20 people. The hotel staff helps arrange meetings at any level. The room is equipped with sofas, a conference table, a plasma panel, amplified Wi-Fi signal. In addition, the hotel offers a variety of business lunches and translation services.

Other services

For an additional fee, the Hotel Metallurg (Moscow) offers its guests the service of ordering a taxi and transfer from and to the airport / station.

Guests arriving by their own vehicle can use the parking lot. In addition, it is possible to deposit valuables in a safe for storage, and leave luggage in a cell for storage. The hotel has a terminal for receiving payments, as well as an ATM and vending machines.



In addition, services such as buying tickets for airplanes and trains, renting a car, ordering excursions in Moscow, fax and photocopying, laundry, dry cleaning are available.
