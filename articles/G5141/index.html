Acer Liquid Z200

For adults who have used a push-button telephone for 10-20 years, it is quite difficult to switch to new-fangled smartphones. Specialists from Acer understood this very well, which is why they created the Liquid Z200 model phone. In fact, the Acer Liquid Z200 is an entry-level gadget, you can say a ‚Äútraining‚Äù smartphone.

Specifications and software





The fact that the Acer Liquid Z200 smartphone is designed for people who have not previously held a touch phone in their hands says at least ‚Äúhardware‚Äù. The "filling" on the phone is frankly weak. On board the device is a dual-core Mediatek, whose clock frequency is only 1 gigahertz. This power is enough to surf the Internet and run undemanding applications. However, advanced users will obviously not have such performance. In terms of software, the Acer Liquid Z200 runs on the latest version of Android.

It is also worth noting that the Liquid Z200 implements an interesting technology called Acer Rapid. It is a special button on the back of the phone. By pressing this button, you can unlock the device, turn on the display, or answer an incoming call. This function is very convenient and practical.

Appearance

Design is one of the strengths of this device. Acer Liquid Z200 is very stylish and attracts attention. In addition, the smartphone is sold in various colors. Tired of everyday life? Does a strict black gadget catch up with melancholy and gloom? In this case, the Acer Liquid Z200 is a great solution. In addition to the standard white and black smartphone, there is a blue, yellow and even pink version of the device on sale. Specialists from Acer give users the opportunity to express their vibrant personality and individuality.





As for the material, the phone is made of thermoplastic polyurethane. It lies well in the hand and is quite pleasant to the touch. There is a camera on the back of the phone (just 2 megapixels).

Acer Liquid Z200: reviews

Ordinary people took the release of the new smartphone from Acer without enthusiasm. If we talk about the pros, then many buyers liked the interesting and custom design of the device. In addition, many praise the sound system. Specialists from Acer installed speakers on the front of the gadget. Thanks to this, the sound is very deep and distinct. And a new technology called Studio Soind allows you to play tracks in good quality.





It is also worth mentioning the shortcomings, which are quite a lot. The phone is rather weak in technical specifications, the quality of the camera also leaves much to be desired. Many users are also not happy with the capacity of the lithium-ion battery (just 1300 mAh). However, all these shortcomings are fully justified by the price of the device. Anyone who has at least 4 thousand rubles in their wallet can get Acer Liquid Z200.

Conclusion

Acer Liquid Z200 is a good budget smartphone with an amazing design. Advanced users should not even focus on this gadget. However, the Acer Liquid Z200 is perfect for those who have never used smartphones before. For example, if a child wants a smart phone, but due to his young age he cannot be trusted with expensive models, then the Liquid Z200 will be a great option.
