Hotel Territory

Pension Europe is easy to recognize by its beautiful facade, plastered in creamy yellow colors. The name of the hotel can be seen on the wall of a three-story building, and on a sign glowing in the evening at the gate. Pension Europa 3 *: room description

This hotel is family run. It is controlled by a mistress from Russia. Therefore, guests from the Russian Federation will not feel any discrimination here compared to other guests. There are only twenty one rooms. This allows all the attendants to know you by sight and name on the second day, and on the third - to be well-versed in your life interests and food preferences. There is no classification in the available rooms. They differ among themselves only in area and, accordingly, in capacity. There are rooms designed for one guest, two and three. All rooms are equipped with brand new furniture. The bedrooms have a safe, fridge and TV. Throughout the hotel Pension Europa 3 * (Prague) operates free and fast Wi-Fi. Each room has its own bathroom. It has a shower cabin. A hairdryer, as well as an iron with an ironing board, and utensils are available for use upon request. All guest rooms are individually decorated and very stylish. The atmosphere of home comfort and silence reign in them.

The hostess of the Europa Pension 3 * hotel (Prague) is directly connected with travel agencies. Prices

All reviews about Pension Europa 3 * (Czech Republic, Prague) claim that this is the best value for money among all hotels in the capital. Low prices and high quality services attract tourists here, so often all rooms are full. Prices depend not only on the room (and, as we recall, they are single, double and triple), but also on the season. The low period is the time from January 4 to April 1 and from November 1 to December 24. This season, room rates are CZK 600, 900 and 1100 (1580, 2350 and 2900 rubles, respectively). In the middle period, they increase slightly. So, a single room costs 750 crowns (1980 rubles) per night, and rooms of larger capacity - 1100 (2900 rubles) and 1350 (3570 rubles). The average season is the longest. It includes the period from April 2 to October 31, excluding the May holidays. They belong to the high season, as well as the period of Christmas and New Year (from December 24 to January 3). Then the prices in the hotel (and in most hotels in Prague) soar. A single room costs 950 (2500 rubles), a double room costs 1500 (3950 rubles), and a room for three guests costs 1800 Czech crowns (or 4750 rubles).
