Crimea, Black Sea, boarding house "Black Sea": address, beach, sea, vacation

Reflections on where and how to spend your vacation in the summer begin to dominate people, probably from the very New Year. The Crimean peninsula attracts tourists with its unique nature, clear sea, many monuments of architecture and a variety of resorts. If you want to spend your holidays in the Crimea, near the sea, but do not want to overpay, then you should pay attention to the northwestern part of the peninsula.

Black Sea - a resort on the western coast of Crimea

The urban-type village of Black Sea is located in the north-western Crimea, on the Tarkhankut Peninsula, and is the administrative center of the Black Sea region. This is the largest settlement of these places.





In the Soviet years, the village was unknown as a resort, but recently the village of Black Sea has been developing rapidly. There are new boarding houses, health resorts, holiday homes, owners of private houses offer a huge selection of comfortable housing at budget prices. The infrastructure of the village is quite developed. There is everything for relaxation: attractions, parks, markets, shops, souvenir shops, cafes, restaurants and many other services.

Holidays in the Black Sea are perfect for lovers of peace and solitude. The western coast of Crimea is the most environmentally friendly. This fertile place is ideal for families with children, as the beaches are wide and sandy, the water in the sea is the cleanest and warmest. Recently, the village is popular among divers.

In the vicinity of the village of Chernomorskoye there are lakes - freshwater Ak-Mechetskoye and salty Mayakskoye. The mud of these estuary lakes is healing.

Crimea, Black Sea, boarding house "Black Sea": description

Pension "Black Sea" is located fifty meters from the sea, in the cozy village of the same name. At this point, the coast is washed by Karkinitsky Bay, the water in the sea is crystal clear, the bottom is gentle and gentle.





A wonderful place to relax is, of course, the Crimea, the Black Sea. Pension "Black Sea" - one of the health resorts of the village, which was built back in the 80s, when this area was closed to tourists. The boarding house is a complex of three residential buildings and a dining room building.

Accommodation for vacationers

Pension "Black Sea" can accommodate up to 250 people at a time.

Building number 1 - a five-story large building, which houses the administration of the boarding house and the main number of rooms:

  • Standard (2-3 people) - 20 square meters. m - a standard set of furniture, TV, refrigerator.
  • Superior standard (2-3 people) - 20 square meters. m, newer furniture, TV, fridge.
  • Superior room (2-3 people) - 25 square meters. m, furniture - as in an improved room. To services - TV, refrigerator, air conditioning.

Each room of any class has a shower, toilet and washbasin and a spacious balcony with sea views.

Building number 2 is a one-story building, building number 3 is a two-story building. In these buildings there are double, triple and quadruple rooms of economy class, without amenities. Bathrooms and public showers are located in a separate building next to the buildings.

Catering in a boarding house

Dining is carried out in the dining room of the boarding house, which is located in the main five-story building.

Three meals a day - breakfast, lunch and dinner. The cuisine is European.

Meals are organized for all vacationers the same, regardless of the degree of comfort of the room. There is no possibility of self-cooking in a boarding house. Room service is not available.

Boarding house beach and distance from the sea

Of course, Crimea and the Black Sea are famous for their beaches. Pension "Black Sea" is located on the 1st coastline, remoteness from the sea - 50 meters.

It offers vacationers - city beach. It is free, sandy, wide.





Pension "Black Sea" has no beach of its own. However, this is not a problem. There is a lot of space on the city beach, no one is lying on each other's heads, in the morning you do not need to run and take a place, as in other places of the Crimea. For an additional fee, you can rent beach equipment: umbrellas, deck chairs.





There are cafes, attractions, water slides and other entertainments by the sea.

Prices for holidays in the "Black Sea"

Prices for vacations in a boarding house are very affordable. For example:

  • double standard - from 1200 to 1600 rubles (depending on the season);
  • Superior double standard - from 1300 to 1750 rubles;
  • double room with air conditioning - from 1500 to 2100 rubles (depending on season and room size);
  • Triple standard - from 1150 to 1500 rubles;
  • Superior triple standard - from 1250 to 1650 rubles.

Such prices were in 2016. The price includes accommodation and three meals a day and is indicated for one person per day. Children under 5 years old are free of charge without a place and food. Extra bed is paid with a 20% or 30% discount.

The boarding house is open from June to September.

How to get there

So, you intend to spend your vacation and decided to go to the Crimea, Black Sea, boarding house "Black Sea". How to get to a vacation spot from Simferopol or Yevpatoriya?

The village of Chernomorsky is located 120 km from the city of Simferopol. You can order a transfer from the airport or railway station, numerous Crimean carriers will help you with this, the "Taxi at Sea" service is available.





A bus runs from Simferopol bus station to the village of Chernomorskoye.

In the event that you arrived by train in Yevpatoriya, getting there is even easier. From Evpatoria to the Black Sea - about 75 kilometers. Buses run frequently.

Pension "Black Sea" has the following address: Russia, Crimea, urban village Chernomorskoe, st. 60 years of October, 1.

Boarding house and village infrastructure

The territory of the boarding house is not very large. On the territory there is a bar, a small cafe, a first-aid post, a massage room. There is a guarded parking lot around the clock. There is no animation in the boarding house, however there is a small children's room with toys and board games.

Not far from the boarding house is the city park Komsomolsky, where several cafes and restaurants are located. The main resort infrastructure is concentrated on the beach. There you can buy souvenirs, fruits, pasties, nuts and other goodies.





The most popular entertainment in the village of Chernomorskoe is diving. You can dive with scuba diving at Cape Tarkhankut, which is thirty kilometers from the boarding house. It is here that you can fully see the beauty of the underwater kingdom. Tarhankut is the most beloved place of avid and experienced divers. In that case, if you are not yet ready to dive, you can simply take a mask with a snorkel and swim between the snow-white cliffs - the impression is indescribable. Crystal clear water will allow you to observe the seabed, floating on top.

For outdoor enthusiasts, local guides prepared an unusual trip around the Tarkhankut Peninsula with ATVs that will not leave anyone indifferent. Before vacationers will discover the unusual picturesque nature of these places.

You can go on a boat trip directly from the village and see Tarhankut from the sea. This fascinating tour will be interesting to everyone, without exception, even children.

Numerous excursion agencies offer vacationers various routes to the sights of Crimea. However, it should be understood that the village is located far from them and the excursions are quite tiring due to the long road.

Pension "Black Sea" (Crimea): reviews

Reviews about the rest in the boarding house are mixed. Among them are positive and negative, and completely opposite.

So, for example, some guests of the boarding house speak about simplicity, but the cleanliness of the rooms, indicate comfortable beds and a pleasant atmosphere of the rooms. Others report shabby furniture, creaking beds and dirt in the rooms.

The same reviews about nutrition. Some praise delicious dinners and talk about a normal, nutritious diet, while others call the cuisine filthy, tasteless and meager. At the same time, reviews are divided approximately 50 to 50.





There are more positive opinions about the staff, but some vacationers note the rudeness of the administration.

Guests consider the proximity of the boarding house to the sea and to the amusement park a plus. Also, vacationers talk about reasonable prices, rest in the "Black Sea" is considered a budget.

Almost all tourists consider the beach of the village to be clean and spacious. The infrastructure of this settlement meets the basic requirements of spa residents. Tourists say that the village has all the entertainment. Inflatable slides and bananas occupy vacationers on the beach, daily boat trips are organized, of which the most popular are excursions to Tarhankut.

From boat trips to Tarkhankut all tourists are delighted: the turquoise sea, snow-white cliffs, swimming past dolphins. However, many are saddened by the remoteness of the village from the main attractions of Crimea.

The village of Black Sea attracts tourists with its housing prices, spaciousness and comfort. There is everything for a good rest. The hospitality of the locals touches, their hospitality and simplicity make them come back here to rest again and again.

If you want to relax in a quiet, cozy town for a small fee, enjoying the clean warm sea, wide sandy beaches, in the most environmentally friendly place, then come to the Crimea, the village of Chernomorskoye, the boarding house "Chernomorsky".
