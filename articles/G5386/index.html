How to sew a Kolobok costume with your own hands: two sewing options

Parents of young children often face the question of tailoring a carnival costume for some holidays, events, performances. Renting an outfit is expensive and you don‚Äôt want the child to wear it after someone else‚Äôs. After all, you do not know for sure how these costumes are actually processed after each donning. Most likely, nothing. Especially when the season of the New Year holidays and there is an entry for costumes. One rents, another takes an hour later. It is better to try a little and sew a suit yourself. Then your child will be safe, and you don‚Äôt need to run a rental after the holiday to return the outfit on time.

This article will explain in detail about two different ways of tailoring a Kolobok costume. The photo shows how these outfits look ready-made, a step-by-step description of sewing is presented and what materials for this need to be available.

DIY Kolobok costume

Such a suit has an element depicting a fairy-tale character. The rest of the outfit can be selected from the ready-made things in the wardrobe, or you can sew a cape and a hat as an addition to the image of Kolobok. To sew such a suit, you need to prepare a yellow cloth (it is more convenient to use cotton: it is easier to work with), orange fleece, threads, needles, a thin sheet of foam rubber, scissors, a sheet of Whatman paper, a pencil.

First you need to measure the distance from the base of the neck to the navel. This will be the diameter of the circle. You do not need to do more, as the child will be uncomfortable moving or sitting in such an outfit. Transfer the dimensions to the paper of whatman paper and draw a circle. Next, we transfer this pattern to matter folded in half to cut two identical parts at once, and we cut a foam rubber circle according to the pattern.





The next step is to fold the prepared workpiece with the wrong side out and sew or stitch it in your hands in a circle, leaving a small hole for subsequent insertion into the foam. We turn it on the front side, iron it and insert the foam rubber, gently spreading it throughout the interior. Then the remaining hole is sutured with an internal seam so that it is not visible.

Costume decoration

After preparing the foundation, it remains only to make a mouth, eyes, rosy cheeks and a forelock, and the Kolobok costume, sewn with your own hands, is ready. Some advise making small pens on both sides. But this is optional. Eyes can be made with two oval buttons. For rosy cheeks cut two small circles of fleece. They can be glued, but it is safer to sew. The mouth can be made using a gypsy needle and knitting thread, stitching along the drawn contour with a decorative seam. Chub fairy-tale hero can be made of fleece, cut into strips, as in the photo.

The resulting circle is fixed either with the help of ribbons on the back, like a backpack, or by sewing it on a child‚Äôs waistcoat or shirt.

Costume Kolobok: second option

Another option for sewing such an outfit for a holiday consists of two similar circles. The principle of tailoring is the same, only you can use a thin synthetic winterizer instead of foam rubber. The pattern is also a little different. If in the previous method there was just a circle, now on the front and back you need to cut out armholes under the sleeves and neck. Sleeves can not be sewn, as this requires skills and the presence of a sewing machine. You can simply put the resulting Kolobok vest over a yellow shirt or golf under your throat.





The back of the circle, which will be on the back side, can not be stitched with a synthetic winterizer. So the child will not be hot at the matinee. Enough if the front will be voluminous. From the bottom we leave a part of the circle without a seam so that it can be worn over the child‚Äôs head. Pants can be any color: black, blue, yellow, brown.

So, the do-it-yourself Kolobok costume creation is complete! This is not difficult, and in one evening you can create an outfit that the child will remember for a long time. Yes, and the memory photo about such an interesting role will be reminded of the efforts of the mother for her baby.

Tips & Tricks

Usually, many guests and children are present at the holidays. The kindergarten room is small, the air warms up instantly. Even in winter, the matinees are very hot and stuffy. Since this suit has foam or sintepon details, then the baby will warm them. So that the little artist does not feel hot, you need to make the rest of the Kolobok costume easier. For example, wear a yellow T-shirt and shorts with socks.





Before the performance, it is necessary to try on an outfit so that the child in it walks at home, teach him to sit on the chair correctly so that the suit looks aesthetically pleasing, does not crumple. Then the baby will not be nervous and worried due to some problems. Yes, and errors will be immediately visible. Before the holiday, there will be time to tweak the details or fasteners.
