Salary of an IT specialist in Russia

Aichi specialist is considered one of the most popular today, both in Russia and in other countries of the world. Software developers are especially valued. Now almost no organizations exist in which there are no modern programs for work.





Employees can carry out activities both together and separately. The demand for them is associated with the development of computer technologies that are used in all areas of human life. If you are interested in what kind of salary they have, an IT specialist is a highly paid position. But the amounts may vary depending on the profile.

Salary level

Today there are 2 categories of named workers:

  • applied: carry out work with ready-made applications;
  • system: create software on their own.

Specialists of the first type have the opportunity of career growth, as they can learn how to create their own programs. System specialists usually occupy important positions in large firms. What is the average salary of an IT specialist? It is 70,000 rubles, but the specific amount depends on the type of position.

If you are interested in a specific salary, an IT specialist in the field of 1C receives about 60,000 rubles. Employees creating C ++ applications have a salary of 60-80 thousand rubles. JAVA programmers receive 120-200 thousand rubles, and a PHP worker - 30-100 thousand rubles.

Such data includes popular employment sites. But the salary of an IT specialist depends on many different factors. So, having considered all the offers, you can see vacancies with more modest pay. For example, if numerous skills and experience are not required, then a salary of 10,000 to 20,000 rubles is offered.

What affects the size of wages?

In terms of the salary that an IT specialist has, Moscow is a leader, which, in general, is not surprising. In other regions, the amount of income is slightly less.

Specialists working in popular companies, for example, Apple, Google, Facebook, have a higher income. This also applies to independent developers who independently launch the project. For example, Pavel Durov, who is considered the creator of the VKontakte network, is one of the richest Russian developers.

By the way, in this area quite often working with customers without intermediaries is more profitable than stable employment. An IT specialist can work remotely.





To raise your income, you need to constantly improve. To do this, there are computer courses to improve skills. For example, if an employee has the skills to work with JAVA, then he should study PHP, NET JavaScript. Firms value employees with various skills more. In addition, a professional with several specializations can use various methods to create software.

What will be the salary, an IT specialist can find out based on his work experience. Beginners in the company also take, but first the salary will be small. A programmer who has worked in the field of software for more than 3 years can get a higher position. But at the same time, he always needs to improve his knowledge, for example, take computer courses.

Salary varies by region.

In Russia, so far the earnings of programmers are less than in the USA and Europe. But thanks to the development of computer technology, such a specialist can work from anywhere in the world. It is only necessary to learn how to allocate personal time.

And in small Russian cities there are not many information technology companies. And there are demanded job vacancies for technicians who perform optimization of ACS and 1C tuning.

What is taken into account when applying for a job

The work of an IT specialist is quite in demand in the country. A competent resume is necessary for the device. If you have the skills and experience, then, as already mentioned, you can apply for a higher position. When applying, employers evaluate the following criteria:

  1. Level of education - graduated higher education is appreciated, although there are many self-taught workers.
  2. Experience - if you have at least 3 years, then such an employee is considered reliable.
  3. Age - in this specialty young employees are preferred, because they are active and purposeful.
  4. The ability to work with several areas - for this the employee must have knowledge in several industries.
  5. Teamwork - IT specialist interacts with other employees to achieve common work goals.
  6. Foreign language proficiency - English is preferred.
  7. Learning - the employee must be ready to constantly improve, for example, to take IT specialist courses.
  8. Print speed.
  9. Skills of working with created programs.
  10. Ability to work with financial systems.




Programs and applications are constantly being updated, so the employee should have time to get acquainted with them. It is on the basis of these criteria that a decision is made on hiring. The specialist must be active, competent and attentive.

Employee Requirements

In order to successfully get a job, the applicant must meet several more mandatory requirements:

  • age from 18 years;
  • citizenship availability;
  • availability of necessary documents.




Many companies provide formal employment, so that contributions to the Pension Fund are paid for the employee. His salary may consist of salary and percent of completed orders, but there are other payment systems.

Types of professions

The following vacancies are open in modern companies:

  1. JavaScript programmer. He creates application programs.
  2. Content manager. He is engaged in the maintenance of sites and working with groups in social networks.
  3. Project manager. It is necessary for harmonious work of layout designers, web designers, copywriters.
  4. Creators of applications. They create programs for mobile devices.
  5. Networking Specialist. Works with the network functioning of computers.
  6. Technical support specialist who solves various issues with equipment.
  7. Business Intelligence. They process data, create forecasts, programs.

Prospects

Which employee will have a salary depends on him. In this profession there is no limit difficult to achieve. This area is constantly being improved, and in the future it will only evolve, since IT-technologies are firmly embedded in human life. And to improve them will always require a lot of employees.





The audience of Internet users is constantly growing. Almost every company has its own portal. To optimize their work, professional developers are also needed.

Employment of novice specialists

The number of graduates with diplomas of programmers, web designers and system administrators is constantly increasing. But every employer wants to find an experienced reliable employee who can perform various types of activities. And how to get experience for a novice programmer?





It is advisable to acquire the necessary skills already from the 1st course of study. Practice, self-training is of great importance. Such an employee will be more appreciated. For specialists in this field, there are professional trainings that allow improving skills. You can, in addition, go to foreign language courses, as well as classes on creating sites.
