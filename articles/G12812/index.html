Hotel Poseidon Nha Trang Hotel 4 * (Vietnam / Nha Trang / Nha Trang): photos and reviews of tourists from Russia

When planning an annual vacation or a romantic weekend in Asian countries, many opt for Vietnam. In this country you can get not only a dark-skinned tan, but also relax actively. Vietnam is a solid excursion base with many temple complexes of uncharted civilizations and natural scenic national parks. One of its coziest resorts is the town of Nha Trang, which annually attracts a large number of tourists.

Nha Trang and its advantages

This settlement is rightly called the beach capital of the country. Noisy, energetic, cheerful and unusual, this resort appears before travelers. This is a new fairy-tale world, which is represented by amazing landscapes in the form of a cascade of green hills and a secluded island in the middle of the bay. It combines bustling nightlife, round-the-clock bars and discos, clubs and restaurants with other pleasures of the resort, namely first-class spa treatments.

The notorious Cham towers, towering in the city center, are a decoration of the holiday, being an original note in a single aroma of unbridled cheerfulness.

The hotel business in these parts is superbly developed. Its representative is the hotel Poseidon Nha Trang, justifiably won the status of four stars. Hospitable and smiling staff, wholesome and natural food, comfortable living are the main advantages of living here.





general characteristics

The hotel complex Poseidon Nha Trang Hotel 4 *, the photo of which you have the opportunity to see in the article, took its first guests in 2015. A large number of tourists arrived at the brand new year-round functioning hotel. The hotel is located on the first coastline. Only a distance of 100 meters separates it from the coastal strip. Renovation was made in 2016.

The main and only building of the Poseidon Nha Trang Hotel 4 * hotel (Nha Trang) is represented by an eighteen-story building, which is equipped with three panoramic elevators. On the top floor of the building is a swimming area. The architecture of the building combines Western design features, instantly possessing a modern and luxurious look.

Only 35 km separate the establishment from the international airport. The absence of long bus or taxi rides appeals to visitors. Pets are not allowed according to the rules. Disabled rooms are not available. Starting check-in time is 14:00. Friendly, friendly and smiling staff speaks Vietnamese, English and Russian, which significantly reduces the possibility of a language barrier. This is often said by tourists in their grateful reviews.
