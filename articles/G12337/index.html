About the hotel and its services

Club Beach Park Hotel 3 offers guests a fairly wide selection: 16 apartments and 71 rooms (double rooms).

The rooms have air conditioning, telephone, TV, hairdryer, private bathroom and even a balcony with scenic views of the city and the sea. Wi-Fi is available for a fee. You can rent a car, use the shuttle service.

The rooms are cleaned, the reception is open without breaks and days off, and guests are also constantly served. There is a laundry service, luggage storage and currency exchange. For entertainment, guests can use the pool, play table tennis or billiards. And for children there is a separate pool. So entertainment is provided for all guests, regardless of their age. And, of course, not a single hotel can do without a bar and restaurant, and Club Beach Park Hotel 3 is no exception.

Type of rest

Each hotel is characterized by the type of vacation for which it is recommended. Youth companies, adults, independent travelers are the main guests of the Club Beach Park Hotel.

Club Beach Park 3 by European standards is a fairly budget hotel (for three stars). The cost of a standard room varies from 40 to 120 euros. Higher class rooms are more expensive - from 60 to 160 euros. A variety of people of different status and nationalities come to this hotel, so everyone is welcome at the hotel.
