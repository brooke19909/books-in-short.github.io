Simple flower crochet: diagram, description, photo

Always, at any time, anywhere in the world, a flower is an ideal part of jewelry - clothes, interior, hairstyle, etc. However, their delicate and magical beauty for a long time is not enough. An excellent alternative to a living plant is crocheted flowers made of thin threads. This is a fairly simple task, and each novice craftswoman can easily cope with the schemes. Simple crochet flowers with a description and a schematic pattern are easy to create. Choose the thread of the desired shade, the type of flower and the appropriate number hook, be patient and go!

You will knit daisies, lilies, roses, forget-me-nots, violets and many other flowers and decorate your things with them.

Conventions used in the article:

  • conn. Art. - connecting column;
  • air p. - air loop;
  • Art. b / n - single crochet;
  • halfway. - half-column;
  • Art. with 2 / n - a column with two double crochets;
  • Art. with 3 / n - a column with three crochets;

What are they for?

Knitted flowers can be used as decor for completely different areas.

  1. Decorate their hats and caps - large or small.
  2. Decorate pillowcases for cushions.
  3. With such knitted flowers they create excellent jewelry - they also participate in the manufacture of knitted bracelets, necklaces, earrings. When knitting with very thin hooks and yarn, you can get almost jewelry with grace elements.
  4. With flowers of various sizes, you can design a knitted handbag.
  5. Bookmarks for books, clips for serving napkins.
  6. Having placed the knitted flowers in the frame, create a three-dimensional panel and decorate the room with it.
  7. You can use them to create gift wrapping, cards. On the card you can depict a bouquet of flowers.
  8. Bouquets created using yarn and hooks look very original. Made with love, they will be a great gift for relatives.
  9. Crochet some simple flowers according to the pattern and use them for serving - put in the middle of the plates or fasten each napkin with them.
  10. Even such an accessory as a delicate bouquet of the bride can be created with your own hands. It is clear that in the manufacture it is necessary to show all possible diligence and skill, then it will be excellent! Such a bouquet can never fade, and it can be stored for a very long time!

Flowers fit in any shape and size, voluminous and flat - it all depends on the purpose. A volumetric flower may consist of several layers of leaves. The simplest crochet crochet flowers have simple patterns and descriptions - amigurumi rings or air loops and alternating columns with different numbers of crochets.

There are also different varieties of petals: pointed ones make for daffodils, long ones are ideal for lilies and daisies, and narrow and short they are made for carnations. A tiny cup is suitable for lily of the valley and bluebells, and large and wide petals are just right for orchids and poppies. From the elements of an oval shape, an amazing spring tulip is obtained.

There is a technique for creating flowers from lace ribbons, ruffles - when a long strip is knitted in advance, and then it is assembled into a spiral or accordion. In this way roses and carnations are made.

There are many variations on this subject, but first you need to learn how to crochet simple flowers according to patterns.

Only by constantly developing skills can masterful execution of more complex products be achieved.

Tools and materials

Crocheting simple flowers according to the pattern is carried out from completely different composition of yarn - cotton, wool, synthetic, silk, etc. The only rule here is the texture of the thread - dense, twisted and not breaking up into fibers.

Then the hooks are selected - they must correspond to the thickness of the thread. In the case of improperly selected tools, the finished flower may not have the look you would like. It will turn out either loose and not holding the shape, or too tight and skewed.

By tying a flower, you can decorate it with beads or beads, stringing them directly on a thread or sewing them on an already finished flower with a needle and thread.

Simple color crochet patterns for beginners

So what is the simplest knitting diagram?

First, the middle of several loops is knitted, which is tied with single crochet columns, or immediately go to the petals, alternating different types of columns - lower and higher.

Often in simple colors, it looks like several columns with a different number of yarn crocheted in one stitch. The end of the petals here is a half-column (connecting loop).

When it is necessary to leave a hole in each petal and tie an openwork flower, a row of arches of the desired size is made, then tied with columns with a crochet or without it.

Similar principles - with a round center and petals - are the basis for creating almost any flower. All color schemes are just a few complicated versions of it.
