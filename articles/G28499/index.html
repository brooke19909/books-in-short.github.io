BlueBay Villas Doradas Adults Only (Dominican Republic / Puerto Plata / Playa Dorada) - overview, features and reviews

Puerto Plata is the most popular resort in the Dominican Republic among tourists from all over the world. This city is located about 235 from the capital of the republic - San Domingo. Among the sands of the beaches of Puerto Plata, you can also find unusually beautiful black amber (ambergris). The climate at this resort is truly paradise. The tourist infrastructure in the exotic Puerto Plateau is developed, of course, simply magnificent. And of course, many convenient hotels have been built here. For example, the cozy BlueBay Villas Doradas hotel is quite popular among vacationers, including domestic ones .

Where is the hotel located

This complex was built on the beach about 5 km from the center of Puerto Plata. Most tourists consider its location to be very convenient. In this regard, about the BlueBay Villas Doradas Adults Only on the web there are mostly only positive reviews. One hundred meters from this complex is the beach of Playa Dorada, popular with tourists from all over the world . In the immediate vicinity there is an 18-hole golf club. In this field, created by Robert Trent, from time to time, among other things, international competitions are held. The distance to the slopes of the nearest mountains from this hotel is 5 km.





In relation to the airport, the complex is also located, according to most tourists, very well. The nearest Gregorio Luperon international air hub is just about 25 km away. That is, tourists arriving by plane on a shuttle bus can literally take 20-25 minutes.

General description of the hotel

This hotel was built quite a long time ago. And since the last reconstruction in 2017, more than 10 years have passed here. That is, the BlueBay Villas Doradas hotel is quite old. Tourists who want to rent rooms in it should keep in mind.

This hotel accepts guests in several low-rise buildings of modern architecture. The territory of this large four-star complex, of course, has its own, well-groomed and well-landscaped. Tourists can move around the courtyards of the hotel buildings along wide, paved with paving slabs, convenient paths.





Check-in at BlueBay Villas Doradas 4 * hotels is registered at 15.00. At the end of the rest, guests must vacate the room at 12.00. In the immediate vicinity of the buildings, this hotel provides ample parking. You can leave cars here for free.

According to many tourists, hotels in the Dominican Republic can in no way be compared with hotels of the same level of class, for example, in Turkey or Egypt. The level of service here is an order of magnitude better. This applies fully to hotels e BlueBay Villas Doradas Adults Only 4 * . Many of her former guests leave about her on the Web even not just good, but really rave reviews.

What you should know about

A feature of this complex is the fact that only adult tourists can live here. Children under 18 are not settled here. At the same time , only Americans and Europeans live in this hotel . Sometimes Dominicans relax in the complex. There are usually very few Russian tourists here.





"Sharpened" this recreation center is mainly for tourists retired from 50-60 years. Experienced vacationers are advised to young travelers to choose a hotel in Punta Cana in the Dominican Republic . At BlueBay Villas, due to the large number of elderly people, they may not be too comfortable.

Description of rooms

If desired, guests of the Dominican Republic can rent standard art , deluxe rooms (platinum or gold) or upgraded in the BlueBay Villas Doradas . Races with read hotel rooms can be for 1-3 guests. The size of the rooms in this hotel is determined both by their classiness and by how many people occupy them. So, for example, the standard rooms here are not too large. Their area is 25 m 2 . For deluxe, this figure is already 45 m 2 .

In all rooms of the hotel for guests the following services are provided:

  • mini bar;

  • shower;

  • safe;

  • TV;

  • telephone;

  • iron and board.

In the showers of the hotel, its residents can use a hairdryer. In all rooms, residents are offered a convenient wake-up service. They also have modern coffee machines.





BlueBay Villas Doradas: hotel guest reviews

The rooms in this hotel, judging by the reviews available on the Web, are offered tourists a bit oldish, but still quite comfortable. Of course, the guests include the availability of an iron, an ironing board and a coffee machine as their unconditional advantages.

Maids are cleaned in the rooms of this hotel, according to most residents, well. Sheets and towels are never dirty here either. Safe in the room, according to some residents of the hotel, a little expensive. To be able to put valuables in it, guests need to pay two dollars.

A feature of the rooms of this hotel is that the plugs of domestic gadgets here may not be suitable for sockets. Therefore, experienced tourists advise vacationers who have rented rooms in this complex to immediately go to a store located near the hotel and buy adapters. Such devices cost $ 2 in the city.

The showers in this hotel, as well as the rooms themselves, earned good reviews from guests. Hair dryers here, as tour operators selling holiday packages claim, are indeed available. Of course, in the showers of the hotel there is also a standard set of toiletries, as well as towels. Watering cans in the hotel are separated from the toilets by transparent partitions. Therefore, when guests take a shower on the floor in the bathroom, nothing pours. Judging by the reviews, there is always water in this hotel, both cold and hot.

Guest opinion about the service

Tourists respond well about the hotel staff. In relation to their guests, employees of BlueBay Villas Doradas Adults Only, reviews of which are good on the Web, and therefore, behave in a precautionary and responsible manner. But English, since this hotel was built mainly for American and European tourists, is understood here, of course, by all workers.





BlueBay Villas Doradas 4 *: hotel restaurant reviews

Of course, rooms for rent in this four-star complex with meals. In this regard, the hotel also earned good reviews from tourists. There are three restaurants in this hotel - one main and two additional. The first serves, in fact, breakfast, lunch and dinner, included in the price. Additional restaurants free of charge tourists who bought tickets from Russian operators can visit only once.

Reviews about the menu at the hotel are also only positive. The guests are fed here not in the dining room, as in the hotels of Turkey and Egypt, namely in the restaurant. Tables in this establishment are served with glasses, cutlery, and quick waiters serve the guests. The restaurant menu includes dishes of meat, chicken, fish. Seafood in different variations is served every day. In the evenings, the restaurant hosts themed dinners. All the dishes in this hotel are, of course, delicious and fresh.

Of course, there are bars on site. Guests can visit their guests for free on an all-inclusive basis. The only exception is the bar located in the lobby. Here you can take alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks only for money. Its residents also speak very well about the bars of the complex.

The sea is very close to the B lueBay Villas Doradas. Tourists can reach it in just 5 minutes with a calm step. The beach at this hotel, judging by the reviews of vacationers, is quite spacious and comfortable to relax. There are a lot of sun beds. There are also stoles. In the morning, tourists have to occupy the sun loungers on the hotel beach in the morning only in high season.





Despite the fact that the BlueBay Villas hotel is large enough, there is enough space on the beach for its guests for all guests. Entrance to the sea, according to many residents of the hotel, on the shore here is quite convenient. You need to go into the water on a soft sand. There are no boulders or stones on the beach of the hotel. The sand here is very clean.

In some places in the sea, seaweed grows on the hotel's beach. But since the water here is absolutely clear, they are clearly visible and can be easily bypassed. There are no hedgehogs and burning jellyfish on the beach of the complex. For this, the hotel also earned, of course, good reviews from its guests.
