The best hotels of Cuba: description, rating and reviews of tourists

When you think of Cuba, pictures immediately appear in your head with the bright sun, blue sea, warm sand and incendiary Latin American dances. This place is visited by crowds of tourists every summer. Indeed, here, in addition to excellent climatic conditions, there are many interesting sights and excursions. In order to make your vacation as comfortable as possible, select a place of residence in advance. In the resorts of Cuba, hotels for every taste: from simple two-star options to luxurious luxury complexes. The article provides an overview of the most popular hotels among holidaymakers.

Sea sunbeach

A wonderful island-republic with unique landscapes and constantly happy residents is located on the Caribbean Sea. Cuba is a place where it is always warm, which is why travelers love it so much. Going on vacation, you need to take your passport and immigration card with you, it is filled out at the border control. You do not need to apply for a tourist visa if the trip does not exceed 30 days. Cuba is a state consisting of small resort towns. Which are worth visiting in the first place? We recommend starting with the capital of Cuba - Havana. Then visit the fashionable tourist resort - Varadero. You will be delighted with the local beaches! Among resort cities, Holguin and Santiago de Cuba are also popular.

sights

Cuba is a place rich in beautiful architectural monuments that are worth a visit. The main cultural center of the country is the city of Havana. Educational excursions can be ordered here at the Cathedral of St. Christopher. This magnificent Baroque building is decorated with many columns. Inside the walls of the cathedral are covered with frescoes, over which the best craftsmen worked.

Also, everyone can visit the Ernest Hemingway House Museum, where the writer lived the last years of his life. In addition, almost every resort has its own national park, where you can enjoy the charming beauty of local nature under the calming flow of waterfalls.

  • Nacional de Cuba is a five star hotel in the very center of Havana. The building is striking in its grandeur. All interiors are made in the old style - this is the main highlight of the hotel.
