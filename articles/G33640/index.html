"Alexandria" (hotel, Gelendzhik): description of rooms, food and services





The hotel will delight its guests with a great location - by the sea and near the city center, a wide range of delicious dishes of Russian and European cuisine in the restaurant, bright and spacious rooms with all necessary amenities, a beauty salon, a swimming pool and a sauna, free wireless Internet, and wide wireless range of services provided.

Here lonely travelers and people who are used to taking pets with them on trips will spend an excellent vacation - the hotel administration allows you to bring four-legged pets. Also, families with children will like this place as well - toddlers at the hotel are accepted from any age.

A warm sea a stone's throw from the hotel, delicious food, a wide selection of entertainment and a high level of service - these are the main advantages of such a place as "Alexandria" (hotel, Gelendzhik). The description of the hotel indicates that you will spend a wonderful vacation here.

Location





The hotel primarily attracts tourists with its excellent location - only twenty meters from the city beach and two and a half kilometers from the city center. Gelendzhik International Airport is five kilometers from the hotel, and Anapa Train Station is seventy kilometers.

The Alexandria Hotel (Gelendzhik, Schmidt Street 1) is located five hundred meters from the water gallery, a little further, a mile and a half, is a mountain amusement park. Also within walking distance are the local history museum, safari park and the city administration building. In a word, you will definitely not be bored here.

Beach

Located on the promenade of such a wonderful resort town as Gelendzhik, the Alexandria Hotel (the photo of which you see in the article) is located by the city's sandy beach - the largest in the resort. Its length is measured in several kilometers. The water is clean enough, the entrance is gentle, which will be very convenient for relaxing with young children.





The beach offers many water activities for every taste: from scooters and bananas to flying in a balloon and boat trips on a yacht. Here you can also rent sun loungers and umbrellas. The beach has all the amenities: changing rooms, showers, water fountains, lifeguard station.

Number of rooms

Hotel Alexandria (Gelendzhik) offers its guests thirty-five stylishly decorated rooms of various levels of comfort. They are divided into three categories: double one-room standard and junior suite and two-room suite.

All rooms have the necessary conditions for the most comfortable stay. The small size and number of rooms create a special homely and cozy atmosphere. Rooms are cleaned daily; towels and linen are changed every few days.





Standard

These are 18 square meters for two people. There is a bed, air conditioning, satellite TV, safe, telephone, minibar, tea accessories.

Each room is adjoined by an equipped bathroom with a bath or shower, in which guests will find a hairdryer, towels, as well as cosmetic and hygiene items.

Junior Suite

The next category of rooms differs from the standard in large size - 30 square meters. Tourists staying here will be pleasantly surprised by the presence of a spacious balcony with magnificent sea views.

The rooms have the same amenities as in the previous category. But in order for you to feel truly at home, soft terry bathrobes and disposable slippers are provided here.

For children

Children are accepted to the hotel from any age. Accommodation for children up to three years is free. A cot is provided. In order to have all the amenities prepared for you upon arrival, it is advisable to inform the hotel in advance that you plan to come with a child.

Terms of accommodation

"Alexandria" (hotel, Gelendzhik), photos and reviews of which are presented to your attention, allows check-in at the hotel from 15:00, check-out - until 12:00. It is possible to accommodate guests in additional places, the maximum number of additional places in the room is one. The cost of placing children over three years old and adults in additional places is 600 rubles.





Room reservation can be carried out both on the official website and on auxiliary ones. The cancellation policy depends on the room category you have chosen, so you need to clarify this information in each case.

Pets are allowed, but it is advisable to first coordinate this fact with the hotel. This service may incur additional charges.
