Relative humidity and how it affects health

With an absolute value, everything is clear: this is the number of particles of water vapor that is contained in one cubic meter of air. Therefore, it is advised to put saucers with water near the batteries, although this is not a panacea.

Using two simple thermometers, you can build your own hygrometer. How to measure air humidity at home, without reagents? We place both thermometers in the shade, however, we wrap the bottom of one of them with a piece of felt dipped in water. Moisture evaporation cools the thermometer. And if low, the cloth dries quickly, and the evaporator-coated meter gives lower readings.
