Do-it-yourself Halloween Death Costume: pattern, recommendations and reviews

At different times and eras, each nation in its own way represented and personified death. Nevertheless, her image as a separate integral being was present in almost all cultures and religions. It was believed that joking with her, just mention it is impossible. It is unlikely that anyone would dare to put on a Death suit a couple of centuries ago. However, times and customs are changing, and today this image is incredibly popular for masquerades and theme parties on Halloween.

The modern face of Death

Today, most often, Death is depicted as a skeleton in a black cloak-mantle with a scythe behind his shoulder. The main attribute of clothing is often very long and has a deep hood. According to some legends, Death sometimes at the most unexpected moment dropped her hood, showing a naked skull with empty eye sockets. Curiously, this mythological character does not have a specific gender. Accordingly, a Halloween Death suit is suitable for both sexes. It is not necessary to make him too sinister, this character is sometimes portrayed in a well-caricature style.





Classic Death Costume

If you chose this image for Halloween, you can purchase all the necessary clothes and accessories or make them yourself. A self-made costume will cost much cheaper than the purchased analogue and will be the one and only of its kind. So what does Death look like? The most important accessories are a black cloak with a deep hood and a scythe. Death is most often depicted as a decrepit old woman or a revived skeleton. Accordingly, you can make a suitable make-up or choose a mask. Best of all, the Death suit looks in the classic black color for him. But if there are difficulties with the selection and creation of clothes, you can choose dark gray or dark blue shades.

Where to get the braid?

A Death Costume cannot consist only of a mantle and ordinary clothing. An important attribute of this outfit is a braid. The easiest way is to make it out of durable cardboard and paint it with suitable paints - a black or brown handle and silver - a blade. The cutting element can also be glued with foil. A Death Suit with a scythe will look even more spectacular if you manage to get a real agricultural tool somewhere. This is an option for men who decide to reincarnate on the eve of the feast of All Saints. Remember that a real braid weighs a lot, and it will not be easy for a girl in a Death suit to wear it all evening. An important condition is that the tool must not be sharpened, otherwise you risk injuring yourself or injuring others.





Add-ons & Accessories

What to wear under the mantle? A good option is simple black clothes, such as a turtleneck and trousers. It is desirable that things be monophonic and have no bright decor. An interesting option is to complement the Death suit with a purchased skeleton jumpsuit. However, you can also do it yourself by drawing bone on white clothing with black paint. It is better to comb your hair smoothly, although many girls who choose such a suit prefer to do hairstyles based on loose strands. Perfectly complement the image of the corresponding makeup. The face can be specially aged or painted under the skull of a skeleton. The Halloween Death Costume can also be complemented with ready-made carnival masks, such as the one of the main villain in the movie "Scream", or something similar to it.
