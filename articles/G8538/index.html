Koblevo hotels: description and reviews

Koblevo is one of the most popular resorts located in Ukraine. Hundreds of thousands of vacationers annually visit these picturesque places. Guests of this resort area have the opportunity to spend time at specialized bases or stay at one of the local hotels. Koblevo hotels are the topic of this article.





Before visiting this picturesque place, you should collect as much information as possible about the hotels located in the resort area. The most famous Koblevo hotels are Victoria, Dniester, Atlantic, and Millennium. But before starting to describe each of them, a few words should be said about the features of the village and its attractions.

Holidays in Koblevo

The resort village is located sixty kilometers from Odessa, which partly explains its popularity. According to tourists reviews, there is no need to book a hotel room in advance. Perhaps, only at the very peak of the season, Koblevo hotels are crowded. The village is popular among vacationers since Soviet times. But despite this, the offers of the restaurant and hotel business exceed demand.

At the entrance there is a zone with recreation facilities and pensions. It is always crowded here. In order to appreciate the beauty of these places, you should drive into the depths of the resort area, where the hotels are located. Koblevo is a resort that is suitable for both family and youth recreation.

Main attraction

The water park, which is located on the territory of this village, is one of the largest in Ukraine. There are over twenty attractions here. In the territory of the water park there is a water town, focused on the youngest visitors. Near the entertainment complex - cafe, restaurant and guest house. However, those who plan to spend most of their time in the water park do not need to rent a room in this hotel. Not more than a hundred meters from the entertainment complex is the Dniester Hotel. Koblevo is a small resort village. Everything is located within walking distance.

Winery

There is an enterprise on Odessa Street, which includes vineyards and factories in which wine is produced. These places are very popular among guests of Koblevo. Tours are constantly held here, during which you can not only observe the process of preparing the drink, but also taste it.

In addition to the winery and water park, in Koblevo there are no less interesting places. Turkish fortress is a historical landmark of Koblevo. Hotel Victoria

Koblevo - a village that exists due to the tourism business. There are only boarding houses over fifty. Victoria is one of the oldest hotel complexes. However, reviews about it leave vacationers, both positive and negative. The complex includes ninety rooms of different levels. The hotel has a 24-hour restaurant "At Petrovich", a dining room, a sauna. Rooms at this hotel range from deluxe to standard double. However, according to reviews of some guests, the level of service in Victoria leaves much to be desired. And the numbers "Lux" allegedly do not correspond to the cost. Nevertheless, this hotel complex is visited annually by several hundred guests, and most of them, most likely, would not agree with such negative reviews. Victoria is located at 1 Kishenevskaya Street.

Hotel Atlantic

Koblevo is a resort village where both Soviet-era buildings and new buildings are located. Atlantic is a modern hotel complex. It is an eight-story building, erected recently, in 2013. Paid parking and a pool are located on the territory of the Atlantic. All hotel rooms are classified as "luxury" and equipped with modern furniture. The beach is a hundred meters from the hotel. Hotel Dniester

This hotel is suitable for nightlife lovers. Not far from it are clubs, restaurants and cafes. However, as already mentioned, Koblevo is a small and cozy tourist center. Everything is nearby. Therefore, this hotel often stops with families. The water park - the main attraction of the village - is a fifteen minute walk from the Dniester Hotel. The hotel is a five-story building, equipped with rooms of various levels. Those who happened to relax in Koblevo's boarding houses are familiar with their layout. Outwardly, these buildings have changed little. One can only hope that the furniture in one-room and two-room rooms has been updated over the past decades.

On the territory of the Dniester there is a small restaurant and a paid parking lot. Hotel address: 3 Kurortny Avenue.





Hotel Millennium

Koblevo is a village on the Black Sea, where you can find a hotel for every taste and budget. "Millennium" is a hotel complex that differs from others in interior and level of service. In addition to the restaurant and parking, there is also a beach bar, a gym and a concert hall. The Millennium is located on the same Resort Avenue, where the hotels are located, the description of which is presented above. However, even outwardly this complex is different from the surrounding ones. Although, according to the guests of Koblevo, who lived in the Millennium in the summer of 2016, the service at this hotel does not correspond to the cost of living.

Hotel "Duke"

Koblevo is a resort area, the center of which is one hundred meters from the beach. It is in the very center of the village that the Duke Hotel is located. In addition to the four-story building, the complex has a cafe, bar, playgrounds, a shop and even a small market. Guests of the Duke Hotel leave various reviews about the rest, among which are extremely negative. First of all, guests do not like the service, namely, the sullen manner of communication between administrators. Perhaps the unpleasant impression of the interior and furniture is based precisely on the rudeness of the staff. Duke Hotel is located at 1-A Beach Beach Alley.





Hotel "Dolphin"

Koblevo, of course, can not be compared with most resorts in the Krasnodar Territory or the Crimean coast. And it's not about the geographical location, but at the level of service. Ukrainian tourism business lags significantly behind the Russian one. "Dolphin" is a hotel, which, according to its guests, is at a decent level. The beach, located near the hotel, is in perfect condition and is equipped with tents that can be rented for a day for a small amount.

On the territory of the Dolphin Hotel there is a pool and a playground, but there is no parking. The hotel rooms are equipped with modern quality furniture. The hotel corresponds to a three-star European level. Address of the Dolphin Hotel: 37, Kurortny prospekt.

Hotel "Black Sea"

Koblevo is a village that is conditionally divided into Ukrainian and Moldavian parts. The Black Sea Hotel is located in the center, like almost all the hotels described in this article. This part is also called Moldavian. The Black Sea Hotel is a two-story building, built in 2009. There is a beach on one side of the hotel. On the other - a pine forest.

The hotel complex "Black Sea" is a building that includes only sixteen one-room suites. The hotel has a playground, a cafe and a grocery store.





Hotel Paradise

This hotel complex consists of four four-story buildings. The distance from the beach to the hotel is a little over two hundred meters. On the territory there is a basketball court, a small small town, a pizzeria, cafes and canteens. Near the hotel there is a recreation center "Vinnitsa". The pool adjacent to its territory can be visited by everyone for a fee. Not far from the Paradise there are a number of drinking establishments and the classic version of the resort market, which sells mainly magnets depicting local attractions and other souvenirs that are always in demand among tourists.
