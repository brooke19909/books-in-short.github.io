Headphones Philips SHE3590: review, photo and description

In-ear headphones costing less than 500 rubles. most music lovers cause an ironic smile. Indeed, what can you expect from a device from the lowest price category? Nevertheless, for practical users who use headphones as a mobile and universal means of playing music, this option is best suited. Moreover, the budget segment is densely occupied by models of different origins and there are many options of fairly tolerable quality in it. This includes the version of Philips Bass Sound SHE3590, which, due to its low price and balanced sound, is popular with a wide mass of audiophiles.

Model Overview





This is the classic performance of the ergonomic plug-in earplug form factor. The model occupies a strong position among low-cost representatives of the initial link, although it stands out with the brand name. By the way, eminent manufacturers of the same level are reluctant to produce economy class models, which explains the success of the Philips SHE3590 headphones. The price varies from 500 to 700 rubles, which also attracts the attention of the mass consumer. The equipment is standard and does not indulge in wealth - in addition to the device itself, the package includes three interchangeable nozzles of different sizes. This is not to say that the developers have seriously invested in the creation of this model, but the headphones still got a certain reserve of quality, characteristic for a Philips -level brand. This was reflected in the design and in ergonomics, as well as in the sound characteristics.

Headphone Specifications





If we leave aside the brand and quality of materials, then among the competitors the model will move forward with its operating parameters. According to the owners, the nominal characteristics of the Philips SHE3590, an overview of which is presented below, are optimally disclosed during operation:

  • Type of device - dynamic in-ear headphones.
  • The connection method is via cable.
  • The type of wire is symmetrical.
  • Cable length - 120 cm.
  • The lower frequency limit is 12 Hz.
  • The upper frequency level is 23 500 Hz.
  • The sensitivity level is 102 dB.
  • The resistance is 16 ohms.
  • Headphone power - 50 mW.
  • The cable connector is standard, 3.5 mm.

Again, if we turn to other representatives of headphones of this type with a democratic price tag, then the difference in characteristics will be noticeable. Of course, you can find models with more favorable parameters, but this is rather an exception to the rule. Even less often, the practice of using such devices confirms the stated performance. But in the case of the Philips SHE3590 model, the described data correspond to the real picture.

Design and ergonomics





In terms of stylistic delights, the headphones have nothing to boast of - a typical performance, but with taste. Minimalism in this case does not reflect the poverty of content, but the honest position of the device. Designers have provided several color schemes, including pink and black shades. Although there are no additional fasteners, the Philips SHE3590 silicone ear cushions provide a snug fit to your ears. In general, small liners can be called one of the main advantages of this model - they not only increase the comfort of use, but also act as a good barrier to external noise. Soundproofing is especially effective in medium-sized ear pads.

Sound quality





If you evaluate the ability of the model to the overall study of different frequencies, taking into account the budget class, then the estimates will be the highest. Bass for such headphones is one of the most difficult frequency levels - as a rule, cheap devices either fill it with excess booming energy, or completely drown it out, preventing it from opening. In turn, Philips SHE3590 headphones accurately and gently reproduce the lower case, without trying to falsely take the track to the side with the addition of wheezing and other distortions. Mids are usually not particularly problematic for economy-class models, but in this case, everything is not so simple. Headphones muffle the sound a little, although the separation of instruments is very well expressed.

Perhaps the model demonstrates the highest quality reproduction at high frequencies. First of all, accuracy and purity in broadcasting music is noted. The small speakers Philips SHE3590 confidently cope with acoustic compositions. In general, the device definitely exceeds its level in terms of sound quality. Obvious failures are not observed, and with the disadvantages of processing the middle spectrum it is quite possible to come to terms with such money.

Positive feedback about the model





Headphones have a lot of flattering reviews for the appearance, ease of fit in the ears and good sound insulation. For example, a wide range of colors provides opportunities for the implementation of stylistic needs when creating an original image. There are also laudatory opinions regarding the strength of the materials used. This applies to the optimum length wire as well as the plastic in the enclosures for the Philips SHE3590 speakers. Reviews regarding sound quality emphasize clarity of playback. According to the owners, even in complex areas, the speakers stretch the composition without diluting it with noise and crack. Although for targeted use as a means to play complex amateur tunes, this option is not recommended.

Negative

Most critical reviews also address the sound characteristics. The weaknesses of the direct function of the device still include disadvantages in working with bass and at medium frequencies, where notes are torn. There are claims to the Philips SHE3590 wire, which is enclosed in a soft and thin rubber shell. Users are confused by the feeling of his insecurity, although cases with cliffs are not so common. There are also negative opinions about the plug. The standard 3.5 mm format is still dominant in the budget class, which reduces the functionality of the headphones. But they criticize the model for a loose entry into the connector. The rest of the quality of materials claims from users almost never occurs.

Conclusion





Headphones are ideal for active users who value ease of use and high-quality sound. The model will not impress with the characteristics of the music playback of a sophisticated music lover, but the creators did not expect this. Budget headphones Philips SHE3590 sharpened for versatility in listening to different tracks. And this goal was achieved - the upper range is played cleanly and without blockages, the bass is also played with moderately expressed lows, and the middle frequencies are balanced, although they are slightly restrained. To find headphones in the same price segment that can confidently cope with the entire range is almost impossible. This can not be said about the development of Philips, but the absence of obvious failures and noise distortion in complex batches is already an achievement.
