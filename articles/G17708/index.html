Lazarevsky water parks - great places to relax

Rest in water parks has many advantages. That is why an increasing number of people prefer to visit such complexes. After all, here you can have a great time with family, children, in the company of friends or work colleagues. And most importantly, even on the hottest day you can find your own corner of coolness.

Water parks in Lazarevsky are one of the largest such complexes on the Black Sea coast. In total, two centers with water attractions are provided for the services of adrenaline lovers. Everyone will find entertainment for themselves, based on their own preferences.

Nautilus





Describing the water parks of Lazarevsky, perhaps we will start with Nautilus. It is located in Lazarevsky, on Victory Street, 2/1. The complex is located near the sea coast, on the Psezuapse River. It offers a breathtaking view of the Caucasus Mountains. Here visitors can enjoy skiing on 6 extreme slides and relax in 4 pools. For small visitors, a whole town is offered called Fantasy, where everyone can find a fascinating activity. There is a refreshing fountain, and a shallow pool, and you can also play with water pistols and frolic on a variety of slides. There will be no limit to children's delight. And when the forces are running out, you can eat at the pizzeria. Also at the attention of visitors are bars and cafes where you can enjoy refreshing drinks.

Working hours

How do water parks of Lazarevsky work? Each similar institution has its own schedule. For example, Nautilus opens in June and runs until early September. You can visit the complex from 10 to 18.00, seven days a week.

How to get to the place and how much does a ticket to a water park cost?





The traffic intersection is very convenient. To get to the center of water attractions you can take minibuses No. 164, 165, as well as buses No. 67, 155-158, 166, 170, 182.

The cost of a 4-hour stay is 700 rubles. You can also stay for the whole day, it will cost you 850. Children under the age of 5 can go without a ticket, the parents of the rest will have to pay from 550 to 650 rubles, depending on the length of stay in the territory.

"Starfish"

Describing the water parks of Lazarevsky, one can not help but remember about the "Starfish". The main entrance is in the village on 108 Lazareva Street. The total area exceeds a hectare. Attention visitors are invited to 11 water slides, among which, by tradition, there are the most extreme and more gentle. Children can enjoy games and have plenty of fun in the town, where there is a special playground, pool. And, of course, little visitors can not do without slides. However, this is far from all the surprises that Lazarevsky's water parks prepared.

In the "Starfish" there is a unique attraction where the descent is made inside an inflatable balloon. At all moments of vivid impressions, visitors are ready to shoot using the service "Random frame". Now vivid emotions are guaranteed to remain in memory. Outdoor activities require a lot of energy. To nourish it, you can visit the fast food, dining room or cafe located on the territory of the water entertainment complex. Here you can enjoy homemade dishes. What could be better than a hearty lunch on vacation when you visit Lazarevsky's water parks?





Even on the hottest day in the "Starfish" will not be hot. The administration took care of visitors by planting exotic trees on the water park area. The pools are filled with both fresh and sea water. So everyone can choose an option that is pleasant for the body. In addition to water attractions, entertainment shows are held on the territory with the participation of funny animators.

In addition, the safety of vacationers is monitored by a team of rescuers who, if necessary, will provide first aid without delay.

Ticket price and opening hours of the Sea Star water park

How much does a water park in Lazarevsky cost? For a ticket for children from 5 years old, you need to pay from 450 to 650 rubles. depending on the length of stay. Admission for adults is 650-850 rubles. Group visits receive a discount.

The water park is pleased to meet guests from 10.00 to 20.00. During evening skiing, the backlight turns on.





Which water park is better in Lazarevsky? This question cannot be answered exactly. After all, each person has his own individual taste. Therefore, when choosing a place to stay should take into account the convenience of location. Although, of course, the main thing in water parks is water rides. Of course, to make the right choice, you should visit both.

Conclusion

The best option for relaxing in the summer heat - water parks in Lazarevsky. Visitors' reviews indicate a high level of service on the territory of water attractions, the variety of entertainment offered by the complexes, as well as the general atmosphere that uplifts the mood. Those who have already visited the water parks of Lazarevsky, note that it is especially nice to be in the pools on cool days. Since the water temperature is maintained at a comfort level. In the heat, you can refresh yourself, and on a cool day there is no chance of freezing.
