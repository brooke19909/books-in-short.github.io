Sanatorium "Brotherly seaside": reviews and prices

Irkutsk region is a great alternative for those who are looking for a place to spend a vacation. It’s not necessary to go far away to find good service, amazing nature and the possibility of recovery. Why not visit a city called Bratsk? Sanatorium “Brotherly seaside” is a place where tourists from all over Russia have been gathering for more than 50 years. Among the taiga forests, clean lakes, fresh air, this institution is located, which cordially opens its doors all year round.

Medical profile

Sanatorium “Brotherly seaside” is engaged in the prevention and treatment of many diseases. These include problems of musculoskeletal tissue, digestive organs, diseases of the nervous system, metabolism, kidneys, etc. If you have diseases of the heart, blood vessels, respiratory organs, nasopharynx and oral cavity, gastrointestinal tract, then here they will definitely help you. There is also a rehabilitation department in the sanatorium, which is intended for patients after operations with pancreatitis, peptic ulcer disease, removal of the gallbladder, and diabetics.



“Brotherly Seashore” is a sanatorium where you can go through clinical trials, biochemical, functional studies using vouchers and courses. The list of medical services of the institution also includes various health procedures. These are therapeutic baths (whirlpool, mineral, with medicines), speleo, magnetotherapy, mud therapy, healing souls (circular, Charcot, ascending, underwater). There is also a dental office, a treatment room for medical treatment, droppers and injections. Sanatorium “Brotherly seaside” offers physiotherapy to patients. Reception is conducted by a urologist, psychotherapist, surgeon. You can go through cleansing procedures such as microclysters, irrigation of the gums, gastric lavage, etc. Various inhalations are offered to patients: with herbs, alkaline, with medicines, with mineral water.

Natural healing factors

The sanatorium “Bratskoye Seaside” enjoys well-deserved popularity among Russian tourists due to the natural source of Bratskaya mineral water. Two wells are located directly on site.



Climatotherapy also attracts patients. The air on the beach is healing and clean. Mud therapy is another popular activity of the sanatorium.

Number of rooms

The sanatorium “Brotherly seaside” is a complex consisting of two sleeping buildings, a dining room and a medical department. All of them are connected by warm transitions. Guests are offered accommodation in standard rooms and category "Suite". All of them are comfortable and clean.

Standard rooms are single and double. Each has comfortable beds, a wardrobe, a table with chairs, a refrigerator, a TV, a bathroom with a bath or shower.



The rooms of the "Lux" category are represented by such options: No. -204, -221, -322, -206, -209, -300, -302, -306, -307. All of them are two-room, consisting of a living room and a bedroom. They are fully equipped with various furniture and household items depending on the type: folding bed, bed, heater, kettle, dishes, TV, fridge, clothes dryer, hairdryer, wardrobe, etc. Some have a shower cabin in the bathroom, and there are rooms with a jacuzzi.

Nutrition

Sanatorium “Brotherly seaside” receives good reviews regarding food. It has its own dining room, which works on a custom menu. Meals are provided 4 times a day. A specific diet is prescribed by a doctor. Guests staying in suites can use the banquet room. An extensive custom menu has been prepared for them.

Leisure and rest

The camp "Brotherly seaside" is waiting for guests all year round. And at any time you will find something to do here, except for recovery. For tourists work out gym, bathhouse, cinema, library. You can play table tennis, checkers, chess, billiards, darts, mini-golf. Guests are invited to visit the bowling club, the Solnechny pool, and the local drama theater. The resort provides the opportunity to order excursions in Bratsk. It regularly hosts concerts, dance evenings.



Winter holidays in the sanatorium deserve praise. Tourists can arrange golf on ice, go skiing, have fun rolling down the hills. In summer, you will be happy to go on a picnic, do yachting, play tennis, volleyball, and relax on the beach.

Prices, contacts, additional information

Sanatorium "Brotherly seaside" offers prices that are quite reasonable. It all depends on which package you choose. Prices start at 2400 rubles per day for one.

On the sanatorium's official website you can find phones by which you will find out all the information you are interested in. There is also an email address and a feedback form.



Many tourists are interested in how to get to the sanatorium “Brotherly seaside”. There are different options. If you travel by train, you need to get off the platform at the Padunsky Porogi station. Then transfer to the bus. “Brotherly seaside” is not too far away. But from the train station you will first need to get to the bus station. And from there already by bus number 2 to the sanatorium.

If you travel by bus, there is no direct flight to the Padunsky bus station. You will need to first get to the bus station of the city of Bratsk.

If you wish, you can order delivery from the administration by transport.

Sanatorium "Brotherly seaside" provides additional services to its guests. For example, "Wedding Number" for the newlyweds. This refers to a chic bridal night in a suite with a jacuzzi. This pleasure costs about 7000 rubles. This amount includes a festive decoration, breakfast in the room and champagne.

If desired, guests of the sanatorium can rent a picnic area - a cozy gazebo with barbecue facilities, a table and benches on the seashore. The cost of this service is about 2500 rubles.



The sanatorium also provides the opportunity to hold ceremonial receptions, various conferences, business meetings. You can rent a cinema or conference room for 700 rubles per hour.

Opinion of tourists

People who have been here describe the resort as a good place to spend a vacation. They admire nature, beautiful landscapes, the possibility of walks in the fresh air. Many note professional medical procedures that give good results. A particularly pleasant impression was the feeding of squirrels, of which there are many in these parts. Nutrition is rated differently. Cosmetic rather than major repairs of premises are also noted as disadvantages. In addition, some had difficulty getting to the Brotherhood Seaside sanatorium. The reviews say that if you want to save time, the best solution is to take a taxi.

Siberian nature - a magnet for vacationers

Siberian land leaves a special impression. It is unique, solemn, unforgettable. This region is recognized by all Russians as rich and beautiful. It is nice and relaxed at any time of the year. Summer provides the opportunity for walks, swimming in the sea, picking berries and mushrooms. In autumn, the nature of the region is painted in amazing colors. Meadows, ponds, tree leaves are as if shrouded in a special radiance. In winter, there are huge opportunities for healing and active pastime. And in spring, nature comes to life again to give tourists who come here joy, vibrant emotions and good mood.



If you want to have a great and profitable vacation, then you can safely choose this wonderful sanatorium. It has everything you need for the happiness of a tourist who wants to combine rest with recreation. You will be met by professionals, real experts in their field, at your service a huge assortment of procedures and medical examinations. The rooms are always clean, comfortable and cozy. Nutrition is fully consistent with the needs of those who support a therapeutic diet. Pleased with the ability to select dishes from the menu. There are enough options for spending leisure time. Throughout the year, the resort offers various promotions that will allow you to save. For example, before the New Year, the administration always makes a discount on permits (minus about 7000 rubles from the usual price for 14 days of rest). There are promotions for mothers with children. Almost every month has “happy days” when decent discounts are in effect.
