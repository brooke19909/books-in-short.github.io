Pension "Agatha", Anapa: reviews and photos

The village on the Black Sea coast of Vityazevo has long been famous for its chic air, the aroma of plants in which intoxicates from the first breath. Mud, estuaries, healing springs, in a word, everything is created here for a safe and memorable vacation.

All the sandy beaches of the resort are covered with quartz sand of unsurpassed whiteness. This fact was the decisive factor for the construction of numerous hospitals and pensions near the beaches.

Wellness rest

Also, in the treatment in local sanatoriums-dispensaries, the use of marine vegetation, healing mud and water is practiced. The method of therapeutic inhalation, massages and other manipulations is used.

Herbal medicine procedures and the use of teas and infusions from local herbs in spa medicine are in great demand. It is because of the abundance of effective procedures that many vacationers go to Anapa.

Qualified specialists will always help vacationers not only diagnose, but also cure many pathologies in the body.

Anapa is rightfully considered the main health resort city of the entire Black Sea coast. In this resort town, various entertainments are successfully combined with the therapy of diseases of the gastrointestinal tract, nervous, cardiovascular and digestive systems, musculoskeletal system. In addition, the resort's natural resources are able to assist in the treatment of gynecological and skin ailments. In the village of Vityazevo, for many years, the beautiful representatives of humanity have been pleased with the cosmetology and dietetics industry.

People who have undergone surgery or serious injury in this area will also help, because all the conditions for this are created. Vityazevo holiday destinations deserve close attention because of delicious food and excellent rooms of all classifications. In particular, this can be said about the Agata boarding house (Anapa). Photos and description of the complex will be given below.

Entertainment

The entertainment sector in Vityazevo is at the highest level: numerous clubs, karaoke, restaurants and cafes, water parks, in general, a great many places where people of any age can find something to their liking.

The price factor in the resorts of Vityazevo is the lowest, which attracts tourists even more. The choice can fall not only for sanatorium treatment and rehabilitation. Here there is a huge selection of boarding houses that are ready to offer vacationers from all sides a comfortable and complete vacation. This article will describe the wonderful pension "Agatha" (Anapa). Guest reviews will tell you about all the features of staying in this place.

Most boarding houses offer visitors a huge range of medical procedures, which not even every sanatorium can offer. All resorts have their own beaches.

Pension "Agatha". Description





Pension Agat 3 (Anapa) is located on the Black Sea, 400 meters from its own beach. At a distance of 100 meters from the boarding house is Paralia Boulevard, where life boils round the clock, especially in the evenings. Nearby there is a taxi park, restaurants, cafes, pharmacies, supermarkets and other resort infrastructure. Nearby is a center for bowling, a huge water park with many children's slides and other entertainment. Having been there, neither the child nor the adult will be left without impressions. The grocery market is within walking distance of the boarding house.

Accommodation



Receptionists always welcome guests with open arms. At the disposal of vacationers there are 2 cozy comfortable cottages with 24 rooms in each. The territory has round-the-clock hot and cold water supply, which allows you to take a shower or bath at any time.

Treatment is provided to the guests of the boarding house at the expense of the Aquamarine Wellness Center base. Vouchers can be purchased for a fee directly from the administrative staff.

The boarding house has a rich landscape and many cozy gazebos for relaxation and private conversations. Drinking fountains with artesian healing water are installed at every step.

Pension "Agatha" (Anapa) has rooms for 2, 4 and 6 people. Each of them has a split system, bathroom, Internet, TV, shower with everything you need, several beds and other necessary equipment.

Cottages can accommodate a total of up to 140 people at a time.

Path to the sea





For guests, the pension offers its own beach, the path to which takes only five minutes. Previously, there was a children's camp. Many features of the beach area, adapted for children, are preserved today. A shallow bottom is very convenient for swimming with young children.

The beach is equipped with shade canopies, fountains with drinking water, sun loungers, toilets and locker rooms. Rescuers are on duty around the clock.

On the shore you can have some fun. There are many attractions on the beach. You can ride a catamaran, banana or paraglide. Many are grateful for a great stay in Anapa. Pension "Agatha" is one of the worthy pearls of this resort.
