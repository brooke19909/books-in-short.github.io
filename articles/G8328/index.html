Lomeniz Hotel 3 * (Greece / Rhodes): hotel description, photos and reviews

Inveterate tourists increasingly prefer to travel to Greece. This country is one of the most popular in the tourism industry. And this is not surprising, because there is a pleasant climate, beautiful nature, a large number of attractions. One of the favorite places for tourists is the Greek island of Rhodes. Every year a huge number of vacationers visit it. And for every traveler, the issue of residence is an urgent issue. An excellent establishment for spending leisure time can be the Lomeniz Hotel 3 * (Rhodes). There are all the necessary conditions for a complete immersion in relaxation, relaxation and comfort.

Short description





The proposed hotel is located in one of the quietest areas of the island, so here you can relax in complete silence and at least temporarily forget about the city bustle. The administration approaches each guest individually, trying to provide an atmosphere of comfort and carefree. All rooms are equipped with new furniture, the atmosphere pleases and does not bother the eye. And the main highlight is the sandy beach, which is within walking distance. The universal location of the hotel pleases: although it is located in a quiet corner of the city, it takes only 20 minutes to get to all the necessary sights and shops.

Hotel Lomeniz Hotel 3 * (Rhodes Town) - small but very cozy. It is better to stay here for those who like a calm and quiet holiday. It will be especially comfortable for families who have come to relax in company with children. The hotel has been recently refurbished. All rooms have been updated and now comply with European standards.

Children

The hotel takes care of all its guests, especially for children. When checking in with a child aged 2-12 years, a bed is delivered to the room for free. If the child does not fall into this age category, then parents will have to pay 30% of the cost of living for an extra bed.

Service





The hotel rooms are cleaned daily. Free luggage storage is available on site. For guests who do not smoke or come with children, there are special non-smoking rooms. This service is very convenient for those who suffer from intolerance to tobacco smoke. On the territory there is a restaurant, a bar, a small shop with fresh products, there is a tour desk, you can rent a car. Swimming pools are available for children and adults. There are playgrounds, as well as all the equipment for sports.
As an entertainment, guests are offered sporting events, a gym, water rides. There is also a pool table, massage services are provided. In addition, you can have a wonderful time on the beach, which has all the conditions for a comfortable stay.

For guests with a private car parking is provided, which is guarded around the clock.

The good news is that the staff knows several languages, namely: English, Italian, German and Greek. Therefore, to find a common language is quite possible.

In general, the rest in this hotel is pleasant and comfortable, the staff created all the conditions for everyone to feel at home here. Find out more about Lomeniz Hotel 3 * hotel reviews from tourists.

Rooms





Almost all rooms offer scenic sea or pool views. The bathroom is spacious enough. The furniture is new, the bedding is clean, the towels are snow-white. The walls need repair, but the eye quickly gets used to this shortcoming. Of the toiletries, there is only soap and paper. Everything else must be brought with you.

They clean the rooms efficiently and quickly. Towels are changed daily. Bed linen - once every three days. Guests must clean the balcony on their own.

Beach vacation

The sea is very close. Just cross the road and you will find yourself on a wonderful beach. Cars practically do not drive here, and there is a traffic light. The beach is equipped with sunbeds, umbrellas, locker rooms and showers. You can eat and quench your thirst in a beach tent.

Sunset at sea is convenient. There are stones, but they do not interfere if you swim near the coast. After the ridge of stones, sand is again, so you can just swim over it. The sea is amazing in color, very warm and clean. No waves, no storms in the area. But on the opposite coast, the sea is constantly raging.

Of the inconveniences, it is worth noting the fact that by evening the wind rises on the coast and carries sand. It is almost impossible to be ashore.

the Internet

Communication in the hotel is not always. The Internet sometimes does not catch at all, and sometimes it can be used even in the room. The speed is low. But in all the nearest cafes you can get free Wi-Fi access.

Car rental

In Greece, you need to rent a car. This will greatly simplify and reduce the cost of sightseeing trips. The cost of the car is about 30 euros per day, along with insurance, and do not worry that you will somehow damage the car. Locals do not follow the rules of the road. You need to be prepared for this. But the intense movement is only in Rhodes. You need to ride calmly and carefully.

Places near the hotel

There are wonderful taverns along the promenade. Prices are different, but the food is delicious everywhere, except for the pizzeria. Here, pizza is not impressive, and the service, according to vacationers, is poor.

On the other side there are also cafes, but more expensive and crowded.

The best choice is a tavern located at the crossroads, if you walk along the pool. Here are the lowest prices and the largest portions. For 15 euros you can get a salad, side dish, sweets and drinks. One serving is enough for two adults.

Almost all taverns bring bread that is not in the order, but then it is included in the bill. If you do not eat it, then it is better to immediately refuse. At the same time, they can bring you a dessert that you also did not order, but in most cases it is a gift from the establishment.

Near the hotel there is a supermarket and a large store with low prices. On the contrary - a fruit market.

Interesting places

By renting a car, you can travel to almost all the attractions of the island. The road to the old town takes only 20 minutes. This is an incredibly atmospheric place. There are many shops, there is an aquarium, a promenade. Greek cuisine is very satisfying, so evening walks along the sea are simply necessary.

The old city is a real fairy tale. Here you can stay forever and every time you feel new emotions. Shopping, dinners in taverns, coffee, sunset - all this is fascinating and makes you come back here again and again.

There are not many interesting places on the island. You should definitely visit 7 sources. Butterfly Park - an attraction for an amateur. It is better to come here at the end of summer.

There are a lot of temple buildings in Rhodes. Particularly noteworthy are places such as Lindos, the ancient city of Kamiros, the Baths of Kalisei. On the way there are many picturesque bays where you can swim and sunbathe, taking with you an umbrella. The sun in Greece is just merciless.

Filerimos is famous for its viewing platform, church and peacocks, which can be hand-fed.

Experienced tourists say that at least once you need to meet the sunset in the castles of Monolithos and Kritinia. It is completely free, and the views that open leave a lasting impression.

By ferry you can go to the island of Symi. A ticket at the port is much cheaper than tour operators. You can even go to Turkey for a day. By the way, in Marmaris there is an opportunity to get new things.

Rhodes is definitely worth a visit. The most friendly Greeks live here. And the Lomeniz Hotel 3 * hotel will allow you to spend your holidays with high quality and inexpensive.
