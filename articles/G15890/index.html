Hotel "Slavyanskaya", Minsk: photos, reviews

If you are arriving in Belarus for tourist or business purposes, then pay attention to such an accommodation option as the Slavyanskaya Hotel. Minsk is a large and beautiful city, and a properly selected hotel will only complement the range of impressions.





Brief description of the hotel

If you want to have a good rest after a busy excursion or working day, then you will certainly like the Slavyanskaya Hotel. Minsk attracts many tourists and business people, and this hotel will appeal to those who appreciate quality service. Friendly staff awaits you here, as well as many services, including private parking, wireless Internet and other features.

As for the rooms, this is one of the most pleasant moments that attracts tourists to the hotel "Slavyanskaya" (Minsk). The photo shows that everything is decorated in a modern minimalist style. At the same time, the atmosphere of home comfort is maintained, thanks to warm shades. There is also a wide range of amenities.

If you are interested in nutritious and high-quality food, then this service will also be provided by the Slavyanskaya Hotel (Minsk). The hotel's restaurant serves breakfast and dinner in buffet style, as well as set lunches. All the rest of the time you can make orders on the menu with the possibility of delivery to your room. Banquets and coffee breaks can also be arranged here.





Hotel "Slavyanskaya" (Minsk): address

The hotel is located in the capital of Belarus at 6. Narochanskaya Street. It is about a 10-minute walk from the forest belt with beautiful nature and clean air. Also nearby is a reservoir where you can also admire the picturesque scenery. Very close (5 minutes walk) is a huge water park. As for the international airport, it is almost 40 kilometers from the hotel, but the road to the railway station will not take 25 minutes.





Guest apartments

The following categories of apartments will be available to you in this hotel:

  • One twin room includes twin beds. It should be borne in mind that it is quite possible to check in one guest (the second guest will not be shared unless absolutely necessary). There is a full range of amenities that are needed for a comfortable stay.
  • Double apartments differ from the previous category in that they are accommodated in a large double bed. Also, the room has a more impressive area.

Executive apartments

Luxury apartments consist of two spacious rooms, one of which acts as a bedroom, and the second as a living room. It is worth noting that, in addition to a large bed, there is also a folding upholstered furniture, which can serve to accommodate additional guests.





What facilities are provided in the apartment?

Hotel "Slavyanskaya" (Minsk) offers its guests to use the following amenities in the apartments:

  • a bathroom, which is a bathroom combined with a toilet (there is a window for ventilation);
  • comfortable orthopedic upholstered furniture;
  • Modern TV with a wide LCD screen, which is connected to a satellite dish;
  • individual air conditioning system that helps you establish a comfortable microclimate in your room;
  • high-speed wireless Internet access to which is provided to guests at no extra charge;
  • a refrigerator to maintain the freshness of the products you bought;
  • a landline telephone serves not only to communicate with the reception, but also to make international and long distance calls;
  • The bathroom includes a hairdryer, cosmetic set, and bath amenities.




Hotel facilities

Hotel "Slavyanskaya" (Minsk) has a developed infrastructure, which includes the following objects:

  • the bar invites you to enjoy your favorite drinks to the sounds of relaxing music;
  • in the restaurant you are offered a huge selection of national and international dishes with the possibility of order delivery to your room;
  • free wireless internet is available throughout the hotel;
  • at the exit of the hotel there is a private car parking, which can be used by all guests free of charge;
  • the reception is open around the clock, and therefore, at any time, you can apply for a request or for advice;
  • for bulky suitcases a luggage room is provided;
  • near the reception there is an ATM where you can cash your plastic cards;
  • in the laundry room, your soiled clothes will quickly be put in proper shape;
  • the conference room has all the conditions for seminars, meetings and other business events;
  • You can use the services of hairdressers;
  • in the banquet hall of the hotel your celebrations will be held at the highest level;
  • A souvenir shop is located on site.




Hotel "Slavyanskaya" (Minsk) - service rating

Based on the guest reviews, the facilities and services of this hotel can be assigned the following rating:

  • The hotel infrastructure was awarded 4.8 points. It has a very convenient access to the parking area. Also highly appreciated is the work of the security service. As for the improvement of the adjacent territories, there are some complaints. Also not very convenient is the schedule of the bar and restaurant (they close too early).
  • Service at the hotel was rated at solid 4. The main disadvantage is that there is practically no entertainment. In addition, receptionists work quite slowly. Nevertheless, all the staff is very polite, smiling and friendly.
  • For comfort in the rooms, a total rating of 4.5 was set. Particularly impressive is the bright design, as well as high-quality furniture and modern appliances. As for noise isolation, here you can put a bold minus due to the strong audibility between the rooms.
  • As for living with children, the hotel did not take care of this issue at all, and therefore there is a well-deserved deuce on this point. There is no animation, no game room or playground, no special children's menu.
  • As for the location, it deserves a rating of 3. There are a lot of shops and cafes in the district, but as far as entertainment facilities and objects of interest are concerned, they are far enough to get to.




Positive reviews

Many tourists choose for themselves such an accommodation option as the Slavyanskaya Hotel (Minsk). Guest reviews indicate that this institution has a lot of positive characteristics:

  • perfect cleanliness in the rooms (everything is cleaned efficiently even in hard-to-reach places);
  • the hotel is quite conveniently located, it is very easy to find, even if this is your first time in the city;
  • the staff is very polite, friendly and helpful;
  • beautiful, pleasant interior, made in a modern minimalist style;
  • beautiful view from the windows on the upper floors;
  • convenient layout of the room;
  • on each floor there is a cooler with drinking water (both hot and cold).

Negative reviews

The Slavyanskaya Hotel (Minsk) has not only advantages, but also significant shortcomings. Guest reviews are characterized by the following negative comments:

  • sometimes there is confusion at the reception, and therefore you may be accommodated in the room where someone already lives (or vice versa - someone will be placed in your room);
  • a fairly modest buffet menu, as for a hotel of such a high level; complex nutrition is quite modest and monotonous;
  • very strong audibility between numbers;
  • lack of a minibar;
  • fairly high prices in a restaurant;
  • lack of an electric kettle in the room;
  • most channels broadcast economic news (almost no entertainment);
  • a safe is available only at the reception.

General impression

If you arrived in Minsk for tourism or business, then feel free to choose the Slavyanskaya Hotel for your accommodation. It offers comfortable conditions for accommodation, as well as a high level of service. A feature of the hotel can be considered perfect cleanliness in the rooms. But if we talk about the shortcomings, it is worth noting not very satisfying breakfasts (both buffet and complex), as well as excessively high prices for accommodation.

Speaking about the location of the hotel, it should be noted that it seems very successful to nature lovers. If you want to get quick access to infrastructure, tourism and business facilities, then this option is most likely not suitable for you.
