Dolphinarium in Dubai - a fascinating place that will give a lot of pleasant emotions

Located on the best man-made island in the world, the Dubai Dolphinarium in Atlantis is one of the largest dolphin habitats in the world. This is one of the most popular tourist attractions, especially among families with children. The Dolphinarium in Dubai boasts that its establishment is the first rescue and rehabilitation center for injured animals. Here you can not only swim with dolphins, but also feed these charismatic creatures. Dolphinarium (Dubai) is a modern four-level lagoon establishment with three bays and sandy beaches.

Opening hours

This is an amazing place that both children and adults aspire to, open from 9:30 to 17:30 every day. Here is a great entertainment program that can take you all day to familiarize yourself with.

What does the dolphinarium in Dubai offer?

Communication with dolphins is a lot of positive emotions. Depending on your preferences, the dolphinarium offers several entertainment options to choose from. What program does the dolphinarium offer:

  1. Photoshoot with dolphins. What could be the best way to keep unforgettable moments of hugs with dolphins in your memory if not a photo shoot? These are 10 funny minutes of touching animals, hugs and kisses. Standing on the shore, you can still chat with these animals and get the best shots. Take home more than memories and collect valuable pieces of information about these lovely creatures.
  2. Royal bathing. Recommended for confident swimmers. In addition to just swimming for a 30-minute duration, you can surf next to them. Although this sounds commonplace, dolphins directing you forward at speed across the lagoon look very attractive. According to several tourists, the excitement that they experienced at the same time, it is impossible to describe in words.
  3. Cognitive programs. The educational trip includes a story about the conservation of marine mammals, followed by fascinating communication with dolphins in shallow water for 15 minutes, where students meet with one of the dolphins. The presenter teaches students the physiology of marine mammals, explains the difference between species of animals and talks about saving their lives, and all this through interactive games and presentations. Duration: 2 hours (starts at 8:30 a.m.) Age: 6 years and older. Group size: 15 to 30 students.
  4. Diving. Recommended for confident swimmers. Scuba diving is one of the most popular activities in the dolphinarium, designed for people who crave adventure. Adrenaline rises when you watch majestic beings swimming in deep waters. Feel what it feels like to swim next to them underwater, as if you are one of them. This rare opportunity includes touching, hugging, everything under water, thereby making your swimming unforgettable. Where else can you get a chance to go scuba diving with dolphins? Please note that only certified scuba divers will be eligible for this program. All diving equipment will be provided by staff to ensure the safety of dolphins.
  5. A fascinating adventure in the world of dolphins. Swim together, hold on to the dolphin as it swims, making you feel nimble and weightless like them. In addition, you can touch them, stroke, hug, swim next to them. This is not in shallow water, so adults who do not know how to swim are not recommended to choose this program. Recommended for children over 8 years old.
  6. Swimming in shallow water. Designed for children and adults who can not swim. Dolphins are very friendly animals, you can cuddle, kiss and play with them, as they are going to share their love with you. Although children of all ages are allowed, up to 12 years of age is required to be accompanied by a guardian.

Address

Dolphinarium Location: Dolphin Bay, Atlantis The Palm, Crescent Road, Dubai.

How to get there

By public transport: Take the Dubai tram to Al Sufouh subway station. Get off at Palm Jumeirah.

By taxi: Aquaventure water park is a 25-minute drive from Deira (city center).

Dolphinarium in Dubai, cost

Entrance ticket will cost about 110 AED.

Additional prices at the dolphinarium in Dubai, for the show program:

  • 695 AED - "Meeting with the Dolphins";
  • 860 AED - "Adventures with Dolphins";
  • 1000 AED - Royal Swimming, Scuba Diving.

At the Dolphinarium in Dubai, guests can choose unique programs to choose from, suitable for different ages and swimming opportunities. Incredible experience, fun, educational and conservative approach at the same time.
