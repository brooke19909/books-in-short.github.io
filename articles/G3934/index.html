Motel "Voyage" (Kondopoga): address, service, reviews

Kondopoga is a small cozy town in the south of Karelia, very conveniently located in a picturesque area close to the main natural attractions - Kivach waterfall, Martsialny Vody, the ancient volcano Girvas, historical-architectural and ethnographic museum-reserve "Kizhi". The fascinating history and richness of local attractions invariably attract guests here. The city offers a choice of a wide variety of options for accommodating visitors. Motel "Voyage" (Kondopoga): reviews
