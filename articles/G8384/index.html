A bit about the resort

St. Vlas is a small town that was named after the patron saint of Veles trade. In recent years, the young resort is rapidly developing and gaining more and more popularity among tourists. And the reason for this is a healing climate and a very convenient location. The city is located five kilometers from another resort in Sunny Beach. Sveti Vlas is a balneological resort: a special microclimate reigns here, which combines a sea breeze enriched with iodine ions and clean mountain air. Many people come to the city precisely for the purpose of healing lungs.

In the resort, the sun shines from May to October. And the temperature in the summer is + 27 ¬∞ C. The climate here is so mild and pleasant that it has a healing effect on the human body.





The beaches of the resort are quite wide and sandy, with small bays that are convenient for swimming. In total, the city has three beaches with a length of 1.5 kilometers. All of them are protected, after the spa people disperse, and are removed.

There are a sufficient number of bars and restaurants on the coast, there are sports fields, rental points for water equipment. All the beaches of the city are completely free, you only need to pay for the rental of sun loungers and umbrellas.

Transport

In St. Vlas, transport links are well established. From Burgas to the city can be reached by bus, making a transfer in the city of Sunny Beach. In addition, you can rent a taxi, which will cost 160 BGN. You can get to neighboring cities from St. Vlas by shuttle buses (to Nessebar, Elenite, Sunny Beach), which run every half hour. There are two fixed-route taxis in the city itself, delivering people to water parks.

Entertainment and Attractions

In St. Vlas has two main tourist streets. One of them is Tsar Simeon, where shops and vegetable stalls work. The second is Mack Street, with many entertainment venues and restaurants. The city also has an open amphitheater, on the stage of which during the season you can watch beautiful theatrical productions and shows. The port of Mariina Denevi will delight tourists with regattas, motorcycle parades, fashion shows and beauty contests.

Moonlight Hotel 5 *: reviews

Speaking about the hotel, I certainly want to refer to the reviews of tourists who managed to relax in it. Moonlight Hotel 5 * (Bulgaria, Sveti Vlas) has slightly conflicting reviews (from enthusiastic to negative). Therefore, let's discuss in more detail the impressions of vacationers. The hotel has a very good location. Proximity to the sea is the main question that interests vacationers. There is a bus stop near the complex, so you can travel on your own if you do not rent a car. Getting to the city center is easy, and you can take a walk along the coast.

As you know, European hotels do not have their own beaches. Tourists use the municipal coast. The proximity of the hotel to the sea is an indisputable advantage of the complex. However, many vacationers are unhappy with the state of dilapidated wooden steps leading down to the beach. In their opinion, a five-star level establishment should have built a safer descent or repaired the existing one.





The beach itself near the hotel is small. The coastline in this region is quite narrow, and therefore represents a narrow strip. During the high season, an incredible number of vacationers accumulate on the beach. Enterprising tourists, so as not to crowd on the coast, just relax on the sun loungers by the pool, and go swimming down to the sea. Sunset at sea is convenient, algae appear very rarely, and the water itself is clean.

If you walk along the coast, you can walk to another beach, which has sun loungers and umbrellas.
