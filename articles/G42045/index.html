How to choose a car vacuum cleaner: reviews and recommendations

Along with maintenance, the vehicle must be cleaned regularly. Using a conventional vacuum cleaner or rag with detergent to solve this problem is not recommended. Firstly, it is inconvenient, and secondly, the effectiveness of this approach is extremely low and does not allow providing full care. Moreover, a special car vacuum cleaner is available on the market for these purposes. Reviews note not only the ergonomics of such devices, but also a good cleaning result. The main thing is to decide on a suitable model and not to forget about the necessary set of components.

Basic vacuum cleaner requirements





The main obstacle that does not allow the use of traditional household appliances in car care is binding to the mains. When choosing a special vacuum cleaner, you should also consider the way of food. There are models powered by the cigarette lighter, and fully autonomous devices that are powered by a battery. Next, you need to determine the power that a car vacuum cleaner will have . Reviews emphasize that cleaning efficiency directly depends on this indicator. The power range varies from 30 to 180 watts. Accordingly, 50-70 W is enough for easy cleaning, but if it is planned to perform cleaning rarely and in large volumes, it is better to look at the devices at 100 W and above.

To use the vacuum cleaner rationally, you should consider the list of its functions. The main division in this regard is due to wet and dry cleaning. The first option allows you to perform a full spring cleaning, but it is not recommended to abuse it. Dry cleaning is not so effective, but its regular implementation will limit itself to such care. Also on the market you can find combined models - for example, the Phantom car vacuum cleaner in the PH2002 version has two cleaning modes to choose from.

Nozzles and filters





Like conventional vacuum cleaners, their automotive counterparts are equipped with different filters and nozzles. As for accessories of the first category, there are cleaning and air batteries for garbage. A good option is the HEPA filter, which has an effective airflow screening. But the best solution may be the combination of an aquafilter and a HEPA device, which are simultaneously installed in a car vacuum cleaner. Reviews indicate that such a combination, even if there is no special cleaner in the design, eliminates clogging of the internal surfaces of the apparatus and eliminates the smallest specks on the surfaces of the interior.

The choice of nozzles depends entirely on the type of coating that the car‚Äôs interior is decorated with. The mandatory set should include the main brush for cleaning the carpet, a device with a tip for cleaning in the corners, as well as a rough turbo brush for collecting wool.

Reviews about Karcher models





The Karcher company offers motorists a whole line of self-service vacuum cleaners. According to users, such devices are economical in terms of energy costs, and ease of use. In addition, some models provide special protection for the suction hose from dirt and rain. They also praise the Karcher car vacuum cleaner for its automatic filter cleaning technology while maintaining a stably high suction power. At the same time, the models of this brand are distinguished by modern design and reliability, which saves owners from unnecessary maintenance operations.

Black Decker Model Reviews





The manufacturer Black Decker offers a fairly extensive selection of models, but the most successful variants of this brand belong to the segment of compact vacuum cleaners. Such devices mainly work from the cigarette lighter and provide a good quality of cleaning. Among the minuses of almost all devices of this brand, the owners note a weak cartridge that connects to the cigarette lighter. Otherwise, the Black Decker car vacuum cleaner demonstrates high performance. But, again, it is recommended to contact this manufacturer for small devices for easy dry cleaning. Such models differ in well-thought-out ergonomics and low cost. By the way, despite the modest size of the equipment, users note a decent suction function even with small power indicators within 15 watts.

Reviews about the models "Aggressor"

The domestic company is also well known among many motorists. Its range includes compact manual models and productive units for professional cleaning. Owners respond positively to the design of models in which the dust collector is separated from the engine compartment. This solution eliminates the passage of debris through the filters and the seal. I must say that the Aggressor car vacuum cleaner is considered one of the best options in terms of sealing, since its body is reliably protected by woven material. The disadvantages of the models of this brand, users attribute the unsatisfactory work of brushes, which can leave granular debris. And this despite the fact that the suction capacity of the apparatus practically does not cause complaints.

Conclusion





As the experience of experienced motorists shows, the choice of a vacuum cleaner according to certain criteria does not give the expected results. This is due to the fact that all components of the device organically complement each other. For example, high power will not be effective without nozzles suitable for a particular coating, and a weak filtration system will reduce to zero all efforts for wet cleaning. In other words, only taking into account additional functions and accessories, you can choose a high-quality and efficient car vacuum cleaner. Reviews, for example, highly value the performance of universal models. But in this case, there is a risk of getting an extra option, which also requires additional money. For servicing several cars, such kits may be the optimal solution, but for one passenger car it is still better to focus on compact medium-power vacuum cleaners.
