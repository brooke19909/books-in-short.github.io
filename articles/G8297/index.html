Hotel Al Bostan 4 (Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt)

Description: A small cozy tourist complex Al Bostan 4 is conveniently located on the Red Sea in one of the most popular and most beautiful resorts in Egypt - in Sharm El Sheikh. This makes it even more popular and visited, because it is here in the heart of Egypt that you can find relaxation for every taste, as well as clean sandy beaches with coral reefs. A couple of miles from the hotel are concentrated all the significant places of the city, entertainment centers and architectural structures.



You don‚Äôt have to spend hours looking for sights (Colorful Canyon, Temple of St. Catherine, Waterpark "Titanic"), since they are all located within walking distance - this is another weighty argument in favor of the Al Bostan Hotel. The complex itself consists of two interconnected two-story buildings in which rooms of various categories are located. The surrounding area is very well-groomed and landscaped, it fit a large pool and outdoor terrace.

The airport is just 7 km away. Hotel Al Bostan 4 is more recommended for a budget holiday - a relaxed atmosphere, the absence of animation and any noise will make your stay a relaxing one. It is here that you can "wallow" on a snow-white beach and go diving.



Rooms: It offers 119 air-conditioned rooms with balconies. All rooms are equipped with necessary furniture and a bathroom. There is a TV with satellite TV (there are Russian channels), a direct dial telephone for communication with the administration and a hairdryer. Cleaning is scheduled. Scheduled bedding replacement.



Meals ( HB , ALL ): Full and warm breakfasts are served daily in the fairly spacious dining room, which is served as a buffet. The menu is always fresh pastries, meat and fish dishes, as well as dairy products. For those living on an all-inclusive basis, non-alcoholic and alcoholic drinks are free of charge throughout the day.

In addition, there is a coffee shop on site, a wonderful restaurant with stunning views of the Red Sea and a lobby bar. Another bar is located near the pool, where you can order amazingly delicious cocktails and light snacks.

Beach (private): 100 m from the Al Bostan Hotel there is a beach line strewn with white sand. Employees are free to use all the beach amenities for their guests. There is a bar on the beach with refreshing drinks and sweets. From entertainment: canoeing, water skiing, parasailing, bananasailing, diving, boats and boats.

Additional information: In the courtyard of the complex Al Bostan 4 There are two outdoor pools with sunbeds and umbrellas. For guests there is a tennis court, five-a-side football, basketball / volleyball. The building has a bowling alley, table tennis, a fitness center, a modern gym and treatment rooms.



In addition, the internal infrastructure includes: laundry department, retail stores, exchange office, reading room, beauty salon, medical office and conference room. A group of animators comes to the hotel several times a week, which entertains the audience with fun performances. There is a swimming area and a playground with swings and slides for your children. If you wish, you can use the services of a nanny.

Digest: The modest and inexpensive resort center Al Bostan 4 is an ideal place for a relaxing, relaxing and productive vacation. Tourists praised the excellent beach and location of the hotel. The cozy rooms also did not cause any particular complaints - everything is quite simple and comfortable.

Lovers of sunbathing near the pool will be able to appreciate the bar with a huge assortment of products. Couples relaxing with children spoke positively of the playground and the safe, clean pool. Some complaints arose about food, but overall the hotel left a pleasant impression and positive emotions.
