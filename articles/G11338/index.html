Bouquet of paper how to make? How to make a bouquet of paper with your own hands

Want to make an original home decoration? Paper flowers look very beautiful and easily made . They can decorate curtains, they can be put in a vase. You can also make a decorative composition for a wall or table. How to make a bouquet of paper is described in the article. Every home usually has everything you need. Read, choose ideas, try to implement them yourself.

Materials and Tools

Do not believe that you can make a beautiful bouquet of paper? How to make it? You will be surprised, but any quality material will do. Even from the "waste" you can make a wonderful composition. So, you need the following:

  • Newspapers
  • Magazines
  • office and designer paper;
  • corrugated decorative and wrapping paper;
  • scissors, knife;
  • pencil;
  • PVA glue or thermal gun;
  • wire (preferably floral green), rods for stalk frames, self-adhesive tape for decorating them.




The list depends on the specific type of flowers that you will make, and technology execution. I would like to pay special attention to such a device as a thermal gun. This is a very convenient thing, designed for various decorative works. This equipment uses special glue sticks that melt under the influence of temperature. The composition in a viscous form is conveniently applied to any elements and surfaces that are immediately glued.

Do-it-yourself paper flower bouquets how to make? Step-by-step instruction

The technology of each product is, of course, its own, but the principles of work are similar for everyone. Any composition is created like this:

  1. Choose your favorite sample.
  2. Do the blank elements on the sheets.
  3. Cut them out.
  4. Twist, fold, glue the parts into flowers.
  5. Make leaves and stems.
  6. Gather a bouquet.
  7. Install and fix it in a vase, a pot, if necessary.

General principles are common to each technology. The methods for making paper bouquets are as follows:

  • bonding of template blanks;
  • quilling;
  • origami.

Flowers from layers of carved petals

If you want to learn how to make a bouquet of paper quickly, then this method is for you. You can make different flowers: ordinary chamomiles, gerberas, asters, daffodils and even roses.

The meaning of technology is as follows:

  1. Draw blanks of petals located on a circle, or individual elements.
  2. Cut out the pieces.
  3. The petals can be slightly twisted with scissors to give them a curved shape.
  4. Glue the flower.
  5. Perform leaflets, sepals, stems.
  6. Collect the blanks in flowers, and them, in turn, in a bouquet.

Such a composition may consist of three to five elements, or be voluminous, including in the form of a ball.

Flowers from the petals with folds

This is another easy way to make a bouquet of paper with your own hands. It is suitable for classes with children. In this case, the flowers are decorative, stylized, similar to daisies.

The technology is as follows:

  1. Cut blanks from paper.
  2. Make fold lines in the right places, giving the petals volume.
  3. Glue them together and the base (stalk and sepals).
  4. Collect in a bouquet.

Origami

If you are mastering this technique of working with material, then you may have already wondered how to make a bouquet of paper. For this, modules are used, which are then connected into a flower.

The template and assembly scheme for all will be the same. Variety can be obtained by combining elements of different colors. Flowers made using this technique resemble aquilegia (catchment) and look very impressive, especially collected in the form of a spherical composition. If you include fantasy, you can come up with other options for colors made using the modular origami method.

Roses twisted from a cut circle

This is another option on how to make a bouquet of paper with your own hands quickly and easily. The only drawback of the method is that all the flowers are the same. Variety can be achieved by the size and shades of the paper-made colors.



A circle is used as the source blank. It is cut with a pair of scissors in a spiral, moving from edge to center. The resulting ribbon is bored tightly in the form of a stylized simplified rose.



The uniformity of all objects can be a plus if you want to assemble a geometrically correct composition from them.

Quilling flowers

If you want to learn how to make a bouquet of flowers from colored paper, metallized, special design, study the following technology. Quilling involves twisting elements from strips. Partly the previous method can also be attributed to this technique. Most often, blanks are made of strips about 5 millimeters wide. In this way, delicate stylized bouquets are made, which are used as decor, as well as for interior decoration.

The meaning of the work is as follows:

  1. Cut strips of any color, mother-of-pearl, metallized, textured paper.
  2. You twist from them various forms or directly the petals, leaves, the middle of the flowers.
  3. Glue the blanks in bulk flowers, fix them on the stem of the frame, wrapped all the same strip or a special adhesive tape.
  4. Gather a bouquet.

Corrugated paper

The most realistic paper flowers with their own hands from paper can be made from packaging decorative corrugated or crepe material in different colors. The latter is finer, and the folds in this case are smaller, so the flower will look like a real one. It will take patience.



The elements of the bouquet in this case are folded in two ways:

  1. A rose can be made from a strip twisted in a spiral.
  2. Other flowers are made according to patterns. They can be either in the form of individual petals, or several, connected together.

In general, the technologies coincide with the work with the workpieces described above. The difference is only in the material. You can also put candy inside a corrugated paper flower . Get a great gift for any holiday. Sweet souvenirs are better to choose a round shape in a golden wrapper or pre-wrap them in the appropriate foil. They will be a kind of frame around which the petals wrap.

Realistic paper flowers for printer

To make a bouquet similar to the real one, according to the previous example, you will need to buy the appropriate material. There is another way that you can use to make realistic flowers without leaving your home. It is suitable if you have printing equipment.

Using this method, it is easy to make a bouquet of flowers that have a complex color of petals, such as lilies or irises. They have not only color transitions, but also dots, stripes.



The meaning of the work is as follows:

  1. Do it yourself on the computer or take ready-made petal templates, on which the corresponding drawing is applied.
  2. Print blanks.
  3. Cut the petals, and then do everything as in the previous method.

I want to learn how to make a beautiful bouquet of paper flowers for this special day? The answer is simple: by any of the methods described above. There is no specific option, the main thing is to choose the shade, appearance and shape of the bouquet. It is better if it is made in the form of a ball in which the flowers and stems themselves fit tightly against each other.

So, you have learned how to make a bouquet of paper in different ways. Any of them can be used both for children's creativity of children of different ages, and as an entertainment and interesting hobby for adults. By choosing any method, you can easily make nice souvenirs and gifts for relatives and friends.
