Sanatorium "Falcon stone" (Pervouralsk): description, features and reviews

"Falcon Stone" is an ideal place where you can relax in the natural landscape and get well. You don‚Äôt have to spend much and go far. After all, there is a sanatorium only forty kilometers from Yekaterinburg.

Description of the health resort





The Falcon Stone Sanatorium was founded back in 1972 specifically for employees of the Uraltyazhtrubstroy trust. During all this time, he managed to receive a huge number of guests and build a good reputation. In 2009, a complete reconstruction of the buildings was carried out, furniture and equipment were replaced.

Around the health resort is a pine forest on the southern slope of Mount Veal, providing clean air. And in one kilometer the Chusovaya River flows.

Number of health resorts





There are 57 rooms in the Falcon Stone sanatorium, which can accommodate 150 guests. There are five types of apartments available for guests: single, double, triple, junior suite and suite.

All rooms have the necessary set of furniture for a comfortable stay: double beds, bedside tables, wardrobes, table and chairs. There is also a TV and a small fridge. The bathroom is equipped with a shower or bath. Luxury rooms are equipped with air conditioning and a jacuzzi.

Prices in the Falcon Stone sanatorium for accommodation start from 1800 rubles per day (as of May 2017). Accommodation in a suite, for example, costs from 3,100 rubles per day.

Services and services of a health-improving complex





In the Falcon Stone health resort, the following types of service are available to guests:

  • free guarded parking;
  • the opportunity to book excursions in the nearby district (the sanatorium has a personal sightseeing bus);
  • transportation to a local bowling club;
  • pay phone;
  • transfer.

On the territory of the Falcon Stone sanatorium there is also a pharmacy, a conference hall, an assembly hall that can accommodate up to 100 guests, which is equipped with projection equipment for watching movies on a large screen, a library.

Among the sports and recreational activities available at the health resort are:

  • indoor pool, where you can swim or do water aerobics, there is a hydromassage, water slides;
  • gym;
  • billiards;
  • beauty parlor;
  • solarium;
  • Russian and Turkish bath, sauna.

Around the sanatorium extends a landscape park where you can take walks. For children on the territory of the sanatorium there is a children's play town and a sanatorium and recreation camp.

All meals are served in the dining-restaurant. Most meals on the diet menu. For their preparation, fresh and environmentally friendly products from local farmers are used: vegetables, meat, fruits, dairy products. Also on the territory of the sanatorium there is a bar with karaoke.

Wellness activities of a medical nature





Various types of treatment are the main feature of the Falcon Stone sanatorium. They specialize in the treatment of diseases of the genitourinary, musculoskeletal, nervous, cardiovascular, endocrine systems, respiratory, digestive, gynecological, urological, and various types of allergies. The main medical profile of the health resort also includes pediatrics and occupational ailments. Duration of treatment lasts from 5 to 28 days.

Within the walls of the health resort, guests are invited to:

  • hardware physiotherapy;
  • balneotherapy (various types of therapeutic baths and showers: carbon dioxide, aromatic, fresh and so on);
  • mud applications and baths;
  • paraffin and ozokerite applications;
  • phytotherapy;
  • oxygen therapy and other types of inhalation;
  • psychological recovery (aroma and music therapy);
  • various types of massage;
  • monitor bowel cleansing;
  • diet food and more.

Also, the health resort has a good diagnostic base. Here, laboratory (biochemical and general clinical analyzes) and functional (ultrasound, ECG, dynamometry and so on) diagnostics are done.

The healing process in the Falcon Stone sanatorium is monitored by 17 doctors and 15 nurses. Among the staff there are certified specialists in the field of gynecology, cardiology, neurology, cosmetology and so on.

Location of the health resort

You can find the Sokoliniy kamen sanatorium at the address: Sverdlovsk Region, Pervouralsk, Papanantsev Street, 36. It is located just half an hour from Yekaterinburg (40 km). You can get from the city by electric train, following to the point Kurgan. You need to leave at the station Kamensk-Uralsky. Then you should take a bus that goes to Sanatorium Lane.
