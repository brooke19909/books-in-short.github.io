Saratov - Yellow Mountain

This city was founded in 1590 as a fortress on the outskirts of Russian territories. It got its name thanks to Falcon Mountain, which towered above the surrounding area. Tatars called this beautiful landmark on the Volga - Yellow Mountain, which in Russian sounds like "Saratov". Since the end of the 19th century, the city is referred to as the largest industrial hub, the capital of the Volga region. The main industries in the region are metalworking, the chemical and petrochemical industries, and mechanical engineering. Today, Saratov is a major port city, one of the scientific and cultural centers of Russia. The territory of the region covers more than 100 thousand square meters. km, more than 2.5 million people live here. Every year, many tourists prefer a vacation on the Volga. The Saratov region is also included in these river tours.

Volga - the main river of Russia

Why isn‚Äôt the highest water, not the longest river, called the main river of the country? And all because it is here, on its shores, that a third of Russia's population lives. The name of the river comes from the Old Slavonic word "moisture". Once, due to its geographical location near the Volga, Moscow became the center of Russia, the capital of the Russian state. It is a beautiful, powerful river with decent depths and picturesque islands. It is inhabited by 76 species of fish and 47 subspecies. The Volga is used as the largest transport highway, and upstream, it supplies bread, salt, fish, oil, coal, and metals. Down deliver wood, construction and industrial materials. Therefore, it is of great industrial and agricultural importance for the country. Since the 30s of the last century, hydroelectric power stations have also been built on the Volga. Today there are 9 of them in its waters. Of course, all this leads to serious environmental problems that local ecologists are working on. The Volga has a significant place in Russian culture, in Russian folklore; it is called the Mother River. Many songs and poems have been written about her, the great Russian river has become one of the symbols of a vast country.

Fishing on the Volga

Saratov region is rich in fishing places. Fishing in these parts allows you to enjoy the surrounding nature, to be alone with yourself and your thoughts and get an excellent catch both in winter and summer. There is an abundance of fish - pike, carps, perches, catfish, pike perch and many others. Saratov travel agencies offer fishing tours to specialized bases, and even floating bases - specially equipped houses right on the water. The fishing season can be divided into summer (May-October) and winter (December-April). In June, floods, and fish around everywhere. This is a favorite time for fishermen. At the end of the summer season, the "velvet" begins and predator fishing begins.
