Yasaka Saigon NhaTrang Resort Hotel Spa (Nha Trang, Vietnam): overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

If you chose Nha Trang (Vietnam) as a destination for overseas holidays and are looking for a comfortable modern hotel on the first line, then Yasaka Saigon NhaTrang Resort Hotel Spa 4 * may be an option for you.



Location, general description, photo

This hotel is located near the beach in Nha Trang Bay. The nearest airport is Camrani Airport, 35 km from the property. A five-minute drive from the hotel is the train station. In the vicinity of "Yasaki" there are a lot of cafes, bars, restaurants, massage parlors, markets (including night ones), shopping centers, shops, tour agencies, currency exchange and car rental points and other tourist infrastructure facilities.

The housing stock of the hotel is represented by 200 rooms located in a high-rise modern building. The upper floors offer magnificent panoramic views of the sea or the city. The hotel offers rooms in the following categories: Superior, Deluxe, Senior Deluxe and Suite. Balconies are not provided in the apartments. Towels are cleaned and changed every day.

The hotel has two outdoor pools with sun terraces, several bars, a restaurant, spa, fitness center. All guests can use the room service (extra charge). Wi-Fi access is also available throughout the hotel.

Nutrition

Hotel Yasaka Saigon Nha Trang Hotel 4 * (Nha Trang, Vietnam) offers tourists, when booking, choose one of the options regarding food - only breakfast or half board.
