Hotels in Vladimir: overview, addresses, reviews

Vladimir is a large Russian city that many Russians want to visit. Almost all those people who plan a trip here have a question related to where is it best for them to stay for the entire period of their stay in the village. There is a certain list of hotels in Vladimir recommended by local residents for settlement. Let us consider it in more detail below.

"Orion"

In the descriptions of hotels in Vladimir, compiled by seasoned tourists, it is stated that most places intended for temporary residence offer tourists comfortable conditions for relaxation. The Orion Hotel, located on Second Nikolskaya Street, 3. is no exception. Tourists note in their reviews the advantageous position of the hotel: it is within walking distance from the main city attractions (Golden Gate, Assumption Cathedral, Torpedo Stadium).

In the room stock of the Orion Hotel there are only the most comfortable and well-equipped apartments. The cost of their rent per day starts at 6,000 rubles. Each room has underfloor heating and a private bathroom.

In the building of the Orion Hotel there is a large restaurant, a fitness center, as well as a thermal complex, which includes a Finnish and Turkish sauna, as well as a large swimming pool with hydromassage function.

"Russia"

Among the best in reviews of hotels in Vladimir, Russia is very often found - a place for temporary residence, chosen by fans of comfort.

Hotel "Rus" offers its guests the best rooms, decorated in neutral colors. Here, guests are provided with a large number of amenities, including a plasma TV, air conditioning, Wi-Fi access, and a private bathroom equipped with standard toiletries and a hairdryer. The main feature of the rooms at the Rus Hotel is that all apartments have a bed with orthopedic mattress.

In the building of the Rus Hotel there is a separate restaurant where guests can eat. Within walking distance from the hotel there are entertainment venues, cafes, restaurants and city attractions.

Tourists note in their reviews that "Rus" is a great place to relax. Most of them consider the cost of daily rent of a room (from 4000 rubles) quite reasonable and acceptable.

This hotel is located in the center of Vladimir, on Gagarina street, 4.

"Gold ring"

Studying the list of hotels in the center of Vladimir, you should definitely pay attention to the Golden Ring Hotel, which has been operating for a long time, and receives a lot of positive reviews. These comments state that the Golden Ring Hotel has a good number of rooms, the apartments of which are equipped with modern appliances and beautiful furniture. In any room of the hotel in question there is a minibar, a TV, a fridge, as well as uninterrupted access to the Internet. Many rooms have a balcony, from which you can enjoy beautiful views of the urban surroundings.

The Golden Ring Hotel has a large billiard room restaurant, as well as a bowling alley and sauna. The cost of living in the apartments of the hotel in question is from 2500 rubles, which most tourists mark as an acceptable indicator.

The Golden Ring Hotel is located at Tchaikovsky Street 27.

"At the Golden Gate"

Studying reviews of hotels in Vladimir, you should definitely pay attention to those that residents and guests of the city leave to the place of rest called "At the Golden Gate".

This hotel is located within walking distance of the same name attractions. The place for rest offers guests a large restaurant, free parking, as well as round-the-clock service. Tourists who have been here note in their reviews that this location is the best in the whole city, and they are also very pleased with the living conditions provided for a relatively small fee - from 3,100 rubles per day per room.

All rooms that make up the general fund are equipped in a classic style. They are equipped with appliances and modern quality furniture. Moreover, all types of apartments have air conditioning, a refrigerator, and a plasma TV.

Hotel "At the Golden Gate" is located at: Vladimir, Bolshaya Moskovskaya, 15.

"Vladimir"

A huge number of tourists who want to see the city, seeks to settle in the hotel "Vladimir" (in Vladimir). Outside, the building in which this hotel is located resembles an old building, which, in fact, attracts tourists.

The interior decoration of the rooms of the hotel in question is pleasing to the guests. In their reviews, they write that here they are attracted by the classic interior, as well as beautiful furniture made to order. The apartments of any class of comfort have a large TV, telephone, air conditioning, as well as a minibar.

The restaurant is located in the beautiful building of the Vladimir Hotel. Chefs delight visitors with deliciously prepared Russian dishes. There is also a tour desk and laundry facilities.

Hotel "Vladimir" is located in Vladimir, at the address: Bolshaya Moskovskaya, 74. The minimum cost for renting a room in this place is 3,700 rubles per day.

Monomakh

Within walking distance from the Golden Gate is another worthy hotel - "Monomakh". Its facade resembles a medieval building, which attracts tourists assigned to the group of admirers of beautiful architecture.

All apartments have a good climate system, as well as plasma-screen TVs and satellite TV. The rooms are equipped with high-quality furniture made in classic style.

Within walking distance from the Monomakh Hotel are the best attractions of the city. Tourists themselves believe that this is the best accommodation option for travelers who want to visit the most interesting places in this wonderful village.

The minimum cost of a day at the Monomakh Hotel is 5400 rubles per room. The place for rest is located at: Vladimir, Gogol street, 20.

"Russian village"

The list of the best hotels in Vladimir, the addresses and descriptions of which are presented in the review, includes the Russian Village located on Moskovsky. The comments of those who have visited here say that the hotel is surrounded by very beautiful nature, on the very outskirts of the city, which attracts tourists.

The rooms of the Russian Village Hotel are characterized by a very concise design. In addition to good furniture, each of them has climate control equipment, as well as a refrigerator. In their free time, guests have the opportunity to view satellite channels on plasma screens. The apartments, regardless of the level of comfort, have a private bathroom.

The cost of renting a hotel room is relatively small - from 2300 rubles per day.

"Dawn"

The list of the best places for temporary residence in Vladimir includes the Zarya Hotel. In its main building is a relatively small number of rooms, from the windows of which you can observe a beautiful view of the city streets. All apartments presented in the hotel are equipped with everything that is necessary for a comfortable stay. Moreover, in every room of the presented fund there is a bathroom with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

Not far from the Zarya Hotel are the main city attractions, one of which is the Golden Gate. Within walking distance from its building are cafes and restaurants, the Assumption Cathedral, as well as a park.

Hotel "Zarya" is located at Studenaya Gora Street, 36A. The lowest cost of renting a room in this place is 2000 rubles. Many tourists consider this price to be very low, which ensures the popularity of the hotel among visitors.

"Prince Vladimir"

The large hotel "Prince Vladimir" in Vladimir is a great place, which is located in the historical part of the city. Travelers consider this location to be excellent, since it is very easy to get to any attraction from the hotel in question.

The room fund of the hotel "Prince Vladimir" (in Vladimir) is presented in the form of spacious apartments. All of them are furnished in a classic style, using a huge amount of furniture made from light materials.

In the hotel building, a large restaurant and a sauna are presented to the guests - tourists can visit these objects at any time convenient for them. Also on the ground floor of the building there is a small lobby bar, the menu of which offers an excellent selection of drinks for every taste.

The cheapest apartments at the Prince Vladimir Hotel will cost guests 3150 rubles, which most tourists consider to be a normal indicator, considering all the conditions that are provided at this place.

The hotel is located on the street Rastopchina, 1D.

"Film strip"

Considering the list of hotels in Vladimir, you need to pay attention to a wonderful place to stay, called the "Movie". This hotel is located within walking distance from the main historical sights of the city - the Museum of the Chamber, the Dmitrievsky Cathedral, the Mother of God-Nativity Monastery, the Vladimir-Suzdal Museum-Reserve, as well as the Golden Gate.

According to the guests, the rooms of the Kinolenta Hotel have a beautiful decoration, decorated in a modern style, using high-quality furniture and bright elements of decor. The hotel building has a lounge area and a small kitchen, where guests have the opportunity to prepare simple dishes, which saves on visits to catering establishments. As for the bathrooms, each of them is designed for guests of two neighboring apartments.

The cost of living at the Kinolenta Hotel is about 2,000 rubles, which most Russians consider an acceptable price tag. Hotel "Kinolenta" is located at: Vladimir, Kremlevskaya street, 12.

"Mon Plaisir"

In the ratings of the best hotels in Vladimir very often there is a place to stay called "Mon Plaisir".

"Mon Plaisir" is famous for the fact that in its vicinity there are many places where you can actively relax. Within walking distance from the hotel there are parks, alleys and simply beautiful streets, along which couples and families with children walk every evening.

The guests of the Mon Plaisir Hotel have comfortable apartments equipped with modern furniture and good appliances. Moreover, every morning they are offered a delicious continental breakfast, the cost of which is included in the price set for renting a room (from 3500 rubles per day).

In their reviews of this hotel, tourists note that it is an excellent choice for those who like to visit interesting and beautiful places. "Mon Plaisir" is located at: Razin Street, 20A.
