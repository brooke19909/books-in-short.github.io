Do you want to have a beautiful and stylish scarf-collar? We will learn how to knit it with knitting needles!

Hand-made collar (snud, scarf pipe) - an accessory beautiful and stylish. It suits people of all age categories. And a teenage girl, and a young girl, and a middle-aged woman, and even a lady of old age will look charming and feminine with a similar accessory. What won the trust of all women on the planet with a knitted scarf collar? How to do such a piece of clothing with your own hands? Let's talk about this in the article.



What does a beginner needlewoman need to know before starting a snood?

Are you burning with the desire to knit a scarf collar ? To ensure that the result of the work meets your expectations, adhere to the following recommendations for choosing materials for its manufacture and knitting methods.

  • Winter scarf-collar is made of woolen, half-woolen, mohair or cashmere thread. The thing is soft and warm. If you plan to wear this accessory as a headdress, then opt for thick yarn. A product made from it will retain heat well and look beautiful.
  • Knit the snood that you plan to wear in the summer and which will serve not so much as protection from the cold, but as an addition and decoration of the overall image, knit from silk, cotton, acrylic yarn.
  • A warm scarf-clamp knitting with knitting needles in dense patterns. These can be drawings with both a smooth structure (front and back surface, elastic band, "iris"), and relief (harnesses, braids, and hooks).
  • During knitting in each row, remove the first loop unbind, and at the end of the row, always do the last one from the wrong. If you adhere to this rule, the edges of the canvas will be smooth.

We master knitting: scarf-collar. We are preparing materials

Your attention is given a description of the implementation of a simple snud. Despite the fact that the product is knitted with ordinary facial smoothness, outwardly it looks simply amazing. And the whole secret in the selection of yarn.





In order to tie a similar accessory, you will need two kinds of threads: fluffy (mohair) and regular (acrylic). The combination of them in the work and creates a beautiful visual effect. So, for work we prepare 100 grams of mohair / wool and acrylic yarn, knitting needles No. 7.

Scarf collar. How to knit ?

The product will be made of two identical parts. Type 75 stitches on the knitting needles. Knit two rows of fluffy thread. Next, put in the acrylic yarn. Follow the next two rows with it. Repeat this sequence of knitting until the end of work (52 rows). As a result, you get a rectangular canvas, the structure of which consists of smooth and lush stripes. Similarly, complete the second half of the product. Sew the pieces of the snout into one piece. The accessory is about 36 x 108 centimeters in size.

Such a scarf-collar can be supplemented with decorative elements: a brooch, a knitted flower, pompons, beads. They wear such an item of clothing with any outerwear.

Knit your favorite scarf yoke. We wish you great pleasure from needlework and even more from the process of wearing this accessory.
