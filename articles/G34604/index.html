Grand miami

Miami, Florida is the peninsula's premier resort. American Casablanca, Reception of God - these are the nicknames given to this city. Almost forty million people come here every year. In the suburbs of the resort is the very Miami Beach - the beach where the most famous vacationers gather.

Miami (Florida) is not only a worldwide resort, but also the capital of a cruise business. Here begins the routes to the Caribbean and Bahamas, to Haiti and to Mexico. The liners depart from the port on Dodge Island or their Fort Lauderdale, forty kilometers distant from the city.

The city itself consists of several independent administrative units, but it can be conditionally divided into four parts: Southern, Northern, Western and Central Miami. In the southern part, the oldest in the city, there are a large number of night clubs, bohemian shops, prestigious food establishments. This is a place of parties for youth and students. Northern Miami has become the habitat of many national minorities, whose representatives are mainly associated with art. The west of the city was chosen by emigrants, at first they were Jews, and now come from Cuba and Central America. The central part of the resort has sheltered businessmen, as the largest banks of the world, company offices, cultural centers, luxury housing, golf and tennis courses, clubs and the South Beach are concentrated here.



City life

Miami (Florida), whose photo you will find in our article, is a night city. It is famous for its discos, night clubs, elegant restaurants, sports bars. In any place you can dance to incendiary tunes, enjoy a variety of show programs, listen to Comedy Club jokes, try cocktails. In the catering establishments any cuisine is presented - Caribbean, Cuban, European, Oriental, and also local, based on seafood. Almost at every step there are shopping centers, boutiques, souvenir shops. Shopaholics should head to Prime Outlets and Dolphin Mall.

Beaches in the City of the Sun

Why do most travelers come to Miami (Florida)? Recreation, entertainment, cruises, shopping? For all at once. But the main goal is, of course, sunbathing on the beach and swimming in the sea. The city stands on the shores of the Atlantic Ocean, there is no high or low season, since it is warm all year round. Here, a tropical climate reigns with a season of hurricanes and storms, and local beaches attract white sand, a clean and even bottom, warm sea waves off the coast.



There are free public beaches in the city where you can engage in a wide variety of water activities and sports. There are also showers, toilets, changing rooms, rescue towers with trained young guys, an alert system based on flowers. There are special beaches at the resort for topless sunbathers. Closed and paid are the beach areas of the hotels.

Instead of an afterword

Miami (Florida) is a very welcoming place with a highly developed infrastructure. Hotels here are found at different levels of stardom, but they are all very comfortable. A huge number of hotels are located on Collins Avenue - the main avenue of Miami Beach. It is noteworthy that only odd numbers have access to the water and the farther south the hotel is located, the more expensive the rooms are.

Choosing a Miami vacation, the tourist will never regret. Here he expects entertainment for every taste, chic beaches, first-class service and a very comfortable climate. There is something to see and where to relax in body and soul. See for yourself!
