How to get to Croatia?

From Moscow you can fly to Zagreb using Aeroflot airplanes. These are regular flights. In Zagreb you will need to transfer to an internal flight and go to the city nearest to the desired resort. However, there are also charters to Pula, Dubrovnik and Split. They are valid only during the beach season. Sometimes there is a charter to the island of Krk. By the way, you can also go to Croatia by rail: from Moscow there is a daily train that leaves from Kievsky station. How to get to the hotel Polynesia Apartments 3 * (Croatia / Istria)? Of course, it is most convenient to fly to Pula on a charter flight, and from there to get to the resort village of Umag either by taxi or by minibus, or by a rental car. Unfortunately, the hotel does not provide a transfer from the airport, but the travel agency from which you purchased the tour can do this.
