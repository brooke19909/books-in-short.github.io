Holidays in Marmaris: reviews of tourists about beaches and hotels

Since Turkish resorts are still leading in popularity, we want to devote our article to one of them. Marmaris is rightfully considered one of the best places to stay in Turkey. It was he who was originally considered a European resort, since tourists from England, Germany and Holland rested in his hotels. But gradually all the advantages of Marmaris were appreciated by our compatriots. Let's discuss the reviews of tourists about holidays in Marmaris.

Weather at the resort

Reviews of tourists about holidays in Marmaris will certainly relate to the local climate, which can be safely called ideal. The fact is that here, even in August and July, there is no intense heat. The sea on the coast is always calm and clean, because the city is located in a closed bay, which is covered by an island and a peninsula from the open sea. On the beaches it is almost impossible to catch the waves. And the entry into the water is very smooth and gentle.



In Marmaris, the climate is drier than in the same Antalya, so people with pulmonary diseases feel very comfortable at the resort. In winter, frosts are extremely rare, but sometimes it rains with wet snow. According to tourists, you can go on vacation in Marmaris in the month of May, but the sea warms up fully only in June. This is worth remembering if you are planning a vacation with children. The season at the resort continues until the end of October, and swimming in the sea is possible until the beginning of November.

How to get to the resort?

You can not get to the resort from Russia by plane. The nearest airport to Marmaris is in the city of Dalaman. In this direction, the planes of the Turkish Airlines company fly. There are no direct flights to Dalaman, so in Istanbul you need to change trains. The flight takes about three hours. Round-trip tickets cost around $ 190. But tickets from St. Petersburg are much more expensive, about $ 300. Of these cities, there are Aeroflot flights. From Dalaman to Marmaris can be reached by bus. In the summer, which covers the period from May to October, direct charter flights to Dalaman are opened. The cost of such tickets is much less than getting with transfers.

It is worth noting that Dalaman Airport is located 120 kilometers from the resort of Marmaris. Therefore, the hotel will have to be reached by bus another 1.5-2 hours. With all the advantages of Marmaris, getting to it is not very convenient.



From April to the end of November, you can reach the resort by ferry, which arrives from the Greek island of Rhodes. Therefore, tourists can first get to the island by plane, and then by ferry to Marmaris. The only inconvenience is the fact that a Schengen visa is required to visit Greece.

Resort Areas

What you need to know about holidays in Marmaris? Reviews of tourists and their advice allow you to navigate the resort for those who are planning to visit it for the first time. Marmaris includes four main resort areas - Armutalan, Sarigerme, Turunch and Icmeler. They all have their own differences, so it is worthwhile to think in advance in which of them to choose a hotel for your stay.



Icmeler is very famous for its stunningly wide sandy beaches and the equally famous thermal springs. The area is perfect for families with children and youth. A lot of family hotels with playgrounds have been built on the local coast. But at the same time, there are many cafes and restaurants in Icmeler, there are all kinds of entertainment venues and night clubs. According to vacationers, the coast was chosen by divers, because coastal waters are clean and calm.

Turunc

The village of Turunc is located next to Icmeler. This area is the most calm and quiet place of the resort. The pride of the village is a beautiful beach, which is awarded the Blue Flag. The coast of the village is surrounded by pine forests. Reviews of tourists say that in Turunch there are no fashionable hotels and expensive restaurants. The entire infrastructure is quite modest and no frills. People come here just for the sake of natural beauties.

Sarigerme

If you are looking for hotels in Marmaris for families with children, then you should head to it in Sarigerme. The resort area is located near the airport and is focused on children. In each institution for young tourists there is everything you need: from a special menu to animation and venues.

Armutalan

Armutalan is not just the largest resort area, but also a place of accumulation of all kinds of entertainment. It is suitable for those tourists who plan an active vacation in Marmaris. Reviews of tourists in 2017 confirm this fact. Currently, there are many nightclubs, shops, restaurants. The pearl of the area is Jamiavlu Falls, located in the forest. Tourists, in addition to the usual water activities, offer walking and cycling in the forest.



For nightlife lovers, Barov Street is open until morning. It has all the entertainment venues that meet people from 23.00 hours and serve until the morning. Entrance to all nightclubs and bars is completely free, and some establishments even offer welcome drinks.

Resort Hotels

Many mistakenly believe that Marmaris is an exceptionally party resort. But this is not a true statement. Hotels for party-goers are located in the very center of the city, from where you can easily reach nightclubs and bars. In addition, the resort has club hotels with extensive territories. The rest of the hotels are family-friendly; they are located away from noisy places and equipped with good infrastructure. City hotels practice half board, but family hotels, on the contrary, use the all-inclusive concept.

Rooms in five-star hotels per day cost from $ 80 and above, and in four-star hotels from 50 to 100. Budget three-star establishments offer apartments from $ 20.

Holidays with children in Marmaris

The resort is largely focused on children. The local beaches seem to be created for kids. For this reason, many institutions are focused on family vacations. If you plan to spend a vacation on the sea with a child, you can pay attention to the following hotel complexes: Julian Club 4 *, Grand Pass 5 *, Ideal Premium 5 *, Aida Club, Tekin and many others others. The peculiarity of such hotels is that they have playgrounds, animation teams, separate pools for kids, and even water parks. Agree that in such a complex, relaxing with a child is much more pleasant and interesting.

Marmaris reviews

Reviews 2017 about holidays in Marmaris, as always, are full of compliments to the popular resort. Many tourists note that of all places in Turkey, it is in Marmaris that it is cleaner and more beautiful. The resort itself was built in the European style, and it remains so to this day. The city has a beautiful promenade, which is nice to walk in the evening. Marmaris is simply adored by Europeans, there are a lot of them resting here. But there are much fewer representatives of the post-Soviet space. The clean streets and beaches of the resort are striking in their beauty and well-groomed condition. Those who have repeatedly rested in other cities of Turkey will immediately see the difference between Marmaris and other resorts. Tourists highly recommend visiting this beautiful place. Despite the fact that the city is considered a party place, many hotels are focused specifically on family vacations. In the center of Marmaris there is a free park for mothers and children. It can be a very interesting time. In addition, the resort has two water parks, where you can have fun with the whole family. Holidays in Marmaris, confirmation of this, certainly can not be boring.



The main property of the resort is the beach. According to tourists, the coastline of Marmaris is incredibly beautiful. In many places coniferous forests approach the sea. It is thanks to the coastline that tourists fell in love with the resort.

Marmaris Market

Resting in Turkey, you should definitely visit one of the local markets. The central market of the resort of Marmaris can safely be called an incredibly colorful place. It sells suede and leather products, jewelry made of onyx, copper, bronze, Turkish carpets, embroideries, fabrics, souvenirs and paintings, pine honey, handicrafts, fruits and vegetables. A large two-level building is reserved for the bazaar. It works on Sundays and Thursdays. In the evening market prices are significantly reduced.

Shopping centers

The city has shopping centers, which will be interesting to visit holidaymakers. At the very center is the Charshi. You can buy fabrics, shoes, clothes, silk products, handmade lace, cosmetics, bath accessories, sweets and souvenirs. Another similar center is located in Netsel Marina (city yacht port). It sells shoes and clothes of not only local, but also international brands.



Resting in Marmaris, you definitely need to buy local honey. The resort is famous for its diverse varieties: from flower to thyme. Honey is sold at grocery supermarkets. The most famous is Tansas

In general, Marmaris has something to see and what to buy.
