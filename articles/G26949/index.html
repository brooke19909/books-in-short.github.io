Cottages in Crimea: description, cost

It seems that the summer began just recently, but before we had time to look back, almost a month and a half had passed. How many of you have already had time to rest? Probably, many have just begun to plan their vacation. Here the main question immediately arises: "Where to go this time?" We advise you to turn your attention to the Crimea, because this is a great place for a relaxing family vacation, and for a fun cool vacation for young people. Here, everyone will definitely find a place for themselves. As the Crimea is becoming more popular, housing prices are rising and rising, and if you eat with a big family, then vacation will definitely come out expensive. What to do in this case? That's right, rent a cottage in Crimea.

Crimea is a republic located on the peninsula, washed by the Black and Azov Sea. More recently, a highway was laid to the Crimea, so getting to the sea has become even easier and cheaper.

"Village on the sea"

Magnificent cottage in the Crimea by the sea. It is located on the first coastline, 20 km from Yevpatoriya.

The cottage has only 1 bedroom.

It has a large double bed. The room is bright and very comfortable. The house itself is made of white wood, inside everything is decorated with a lining of pleasant light brown color. In addition to the bedroom, there is a spacious living room with cable and cable TV. In addition, the living room has large windows, which offer beautiful views of the Black Sea. The house also has a bathroom. The cottage accommodates 5 people, the cost is 8000 rubles per day.

Complimentary amenities include high-speed Internet, sun loungers by the sea, an English-style breakfast and a 24-hour reception.

Villa "Victoria"

Stylish cottages in Crimea on the seashore. They are located on a small hill with a magnificent view of the sea.

Here you can rent two types of cottages.

  1. Standard villa. The house accommodates two people, it has a spacious bedroom in bright colors, its area is 28 square meters. m. Cost for one night - 3500 rubles.
  2. Deluxe Villa. Luxury home that accommodates 4 people. It has a beige-brown bedroom, a bathroom, a living room with a fireplace and a terrace with stunning views. Cost - 6900 rubles per day.

Guests can use free Wi-Fi, and they also have the opportunity to take a walk in the garden and fry meat or vegetables in the barbecue area. A good continental breakfast is served daily at this property.

"Holiday home on 4 Sosnova"

A large cottage in the Crimea, located in Yalta on a small mountain.

This house has 3 separate bedrooms, each of them has its own design. The house is made in calm light shades. It must be said right away that the cottage accommodates 6 people. In addition to the bedroom, there is a private bathroom, a living room and a terrace with magnificent views of the city and surroundings. The living room is made in warm brown shades, it has a fireplace. The cottage also has an equipped kitchen and free parking. Cost - 10,000 rubles per day.

Near the house is a magnificent garden. Guests can use the barbecue facilities, private pool and sun loungers. Remember that the pool is only open during the season.

Nearby is the Seaside Park, the Roman Catholic Church, the house-museum of A.P. Chekhov.

Villa Belaia

A truly stylish and cozy cottage in the Crimea. The house is located in Yalta, nearby are attractions such as the Massandra Palace, Nikitsky Botanical Garden.

In total, the cottage has 3 double bedrooms. All of them are designed in a single simple style, in black and white. In addition, the house includes a spacious living room with a TV and a fireplace, a fully equipped kitchen with a breakfast bar. The bathroom comes with a shower, free toiletries and hygiene products, bathrobes and slippers. All other rooms are made in the same style. Holds a house of 6 people, area - 127 square meters. m, the cost is 18,000 rubles.

Cottage by the sea in the Crimea, from its balcony offers magnificent views of the mountains. All guests can use free Wi-Fi and reserve a free private parking.

VIP Villa

Magnificent cottage in the Crimea from a dark wooden bar. This house, designed for a luxurious vacation, is located in the village of Coastal, near the seashore.

In total, the villa has 3 bedrooms, made in calm beige and brown tones in the Russian style. In addition, there is a large living room with a huge leather sofa, a fully equipped kitchen for cooking, and a bathroom.

In addition, there is a balcony with beautiful views of the sea or the pool, there is also a very cozy terrace. Throughout the house there is air conditioning, paid cable and free satellite channels.

Opposite the exit there is a large private pool; private free parking is not far away.

Villa "Yalta"

Large and bright cottage (house) in the Crimea. It is located in the village of Otradnoye, within walking distance are attractions such as Massandra Palace, Nikitsky Botanical Garden, Yalta embankment.

This cottage in Crimea has only one bedroom, which is made in rich blue, remotely its style resembles a renaissance. In addition, there is a modern kitchen, made in lilac, there is also a dressing area. In the backyard there is a private small pool, next to which are sun loungers. In total, the cottage can accommodate two people, the cost of living per day is 20,000 rubles.

The villa has a 24-hour front desk, free parking. Guests can use the pick-up service or pick-up service at the airport.
