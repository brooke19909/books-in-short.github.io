Working documentation

Since the advent of writing, people began to actively complicate, and possibly vice versa, simplify their lives, coming up with more and more innovations. It was not without limitations itself all sorts of regulations, the creation of documents and other things.

Now documents accompany us everywhere, and sometimes this paperwork just eats both morally and physically. As for the construction - it is simply impossible without documents. When erecting a new facility or reconstructing or redeveloping an existing one, one should clearly know about the upcoming work plan. It is necessary to think over everything during the design process, because assuming any blunder will have to be hard. Therefore, such a concept as working documentation in construction appeared. What does it mean?

Working documentation is that (textual or graphic) with which the implementation of the adopted project decisions , technical solutions for changing the facility or its construction is ensured. Provision of necessary equipment, tools, materials and the like. Also, the working documentation includes working drawings and additional documents that are attached to them, the necessary specifications and more.



So, the fundamental document in the restoration or construction of the building is the project. But, unfortunately, he is far from the only one. Based on the terms of reference, which includes a detailed description of customer requirements and plans for each floor, the project is based. Everything that is necessary for the contractor for construction and installation works is indicated in the specifications for equipment, materials and tools. Using these papers, it is possible to determine with the greatest accuracy the costs of construction, restoration or redevelopment of an object.

Like all documents, working documentation has certain requirements for design and content, which are determined by GOST SPDS. There are two distinct from each other types: design and working. Project documentation includes the most detailed description than the project, which contributes to full compliance with the constructed facility. In some cases, the customer will only need project documentation, and there will be no need to carry out work. Project documentation for large-scale projects is always sent for examination, and the working one can be accepted at the request of the customer. In fact, this applies only to small objects.



As for the cost of processing these securities, it is determined on the basis of the coordination of two parties - the customer and the person who directly carries out all matters related to registration.

Over time, the role of construction is gaining momentum, to prevent many errors it is very important to responsibly approach the design of documentation. As can be seen from the above, all these papers act not only as a regulator, but also as a visual plan for the future object.
