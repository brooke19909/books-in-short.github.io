Guest houses in Lermontovo: where to stay in the village?

In one of the most beautiful valleys surrounded by mountains lies the village of Lermontovo. Among vacationers, it is famous for its excellent pebble and sand beaches, the calm relief of the sea day and the area around the village itself. Due to the fact that the depth of the sea increases gradually, it is very convenient for children here, therefore it is families with toddlers and older children who rush from all parts of the country to this village. What else can vacationers find here?

Resort Features



Despite the not-so-large size of the village, the tourism industry here is large enough to make recreation in Lermontovo as eventful as possible . The reviews of those who have already been here are replete with such words as “great”, “delightful”, “really liked”, “I will always come”, “the children were delighted”, “the service was at a high level”. And this is the most important and unbiased assessment of the work of both the administration of the village and the owners of various institutions, including hotels of different levels.

In addition to the huge beaches, the length of which exceeds 2 km, here you can find the type of activity that will appeal to you the most. At the service of vacationers:

  • horse rides;
  • water attractions;
  • large water park;
  • exciting excursions;
  • funny camping trips;
  • jeep tours;
  • ATV rides and many other offers.

Fans of spending time near the sea have a unique opportunity to get to one of the most famous beaches of the country - the “Golden Coast”. It starts not far from Lermontovo and stretches along the coast with a ten-kilometer strip.

Hotel selection

Today, a lot of vacationers try to spend not only vacation time, but also weekends in Lermontovo: Krasnodar Territory is one of the most beautiful places in the country with a wonderful climate and a lot of cultural, historical and architectural monuments. But so that the days of rest are not overshadowed by everyday troubles in the form of a lack of a place to sleep, you must find a hotel in advance.



Since they often relax here with children, the best option in this case is guest houses in Lermontovo. There are a lot of them in the village. Offers differ in the number of rooms, location, distance from the coast, cost and other parameters. Choose the most suitable option of labor will not be. The guest houses below are included in the list of the most requested accommodation.

Green Paradise

Location - a quiet area of ​​Zelenaya street. The territory is quite spacious with green spaces. There is a fountain in the center, the paths are well-groomed - it is always clean here, therefore families with kids like these guest houses in Lermontovo.

In separate cottages rooms of various levels of comfort are located. Two bathrooms and four showers are located separately and are intended for use by all guests living in the institution. To reach the sea, it will take about ten minutes, and half to the water park.

Alina



If you choose guest houses in Lermontovo, then you should pay attention to this hotel. Location - the center of the village on Zelenaya street. Although the institution was opened only 5 years ago, it is already familiar to many tourists. And this is not surprising: the cozy atmosphere of rooms with all amenities, balconies in each of them, and free parking near the house contribute to popularity. Anyone who wants to visit the local attractions is provided with a jeep. If necessary, you can order a transfer.

To reach the beach, it will take a maximum of 6-7 minutes. Nearby, just a few minutes away, there are such important leisure facilities as pharmacies, post office, ATM, shops, market. But the most important thing is that the dolphinarium and the water park are almost nearby - the most favorite places for children and adults too.

"Magnolia"



No less popular is the guest house in Lermontovo under the beautiful name "Magnolia", located in the central part of the village on ul. Lenin. It is located not far from the similar institution “Alina”, therefore within walking distance there are all the same necessary facilities - shops, pharmacies, a water park, a dolphinarium, as well as a landscaped area where you can relax or take a walk.

The territory of the guest house is a cozy well-kept courtyard with a gazebo. There is a kitchenette, barbecue, a place of rest, free parking. As for nutrition, you can cook your own meals or have lunch and dinner in the cafe of the institution.

Room categories:

  • Standard. These are double and triple rooms. Each of them has everything you need, including a TV, a fan and a private bathroom. Number of rooms - 2.
  • Economy Rooms for 2 and 3 people with everything you need. Exception - the bathroom is located on the floor and it is used by all those living in three rooms.

"Chocolate"



Since these guest houses in Lermontovo are popular, which are located in the center of the village, an institution with such a delicious name is in demand, and quite high. Therefore, to stay here, you must book a place as early as possible. The rooms in the institution are one- and two-room, they can accommodate 2-4 people, respectively. Each room is equipped with everything you need to relax, including a bathroom.

The courtyard near the building is also well equipped - there are:

  1. Recreation areas.
  2. Playground for kids.
  3. Tennis table.
  4. Playground.
  5. Pool for children.
  6. Swimming pool for adults.
  7. Covered parking.

Since food is one of the important components of a good rest, we also took care of “Chocolate” about this: in the spacious dining room we offer complex meals or a standard menu. What to choose, vacationers decide for themselves.
