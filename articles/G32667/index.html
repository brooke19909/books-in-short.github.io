How to draw up maternity capital? Features of the procedure

Maternity capital is often asked for by families whose recently had a second or third child. The fact is that not everyone knows how to properly exercise their right. Therefore, this article gives a general concept of how to formalize maternal capital.



First of all, it should be clarified to whom it can be obtained. The Cabinet of Ministers decided that families who have recently had a second and subsequent child can receive assistance. However, this rule is valid from the beginning of 2007. And also parents who decided to adopt a second baby can receive money. At the time of registration of the capital, the child must already be registered. The recipient of the funds is mainly the mother. As for the father, they can also give him money, but for this it is necessary to confirm that the mother died or she was deprived of the right to a child.

Before issuing maternity capital, you need to know that if you lose a certificate, you need to order a duplicate of it, but the help itself is issued once. As for the size of the sum of money, it is indexed annually, while taking into account the level of inflation in the country. Capital can be used only when the baby is 3 years old.



Before you issue maternity capital, you need to know what the law allows you to spend it on. First of all, it is given to improve housing conditions, to receive education by children who are not yet 25 years old, and also to increase the funded part of the guardian or parent's pension . Therefore, one should not hope that money can be received "on hand" and used at its discretion. It should be noted that the period during which you can seek help is not limited in time.

Before you issue maternity capital, you should collect all the necessary documents. All procedures for obtaining and using assistance are under the control of the Pension Fund. You can bring the documents in person to a government agency or send them by mail. You will need a passport that will prove your identity, birth certificates for all children, and they should bear a note that the babies are registered in the country. Naturally, copies must be taken from the documents.



Making maternity capital is not a difficult task, but it takes a lot of time. Therefore, try to seize all originals and copies of documents during your trip to the Pension Fund so that you do not have to return to the government agency several times. If you want to send everything by mail, you need to find out the details of the representative office of the fund in your city (address, zip code). In addition to providing documents, you must write an application for assistance. Its sample can be found on the official website of the state body (Pension Fund). However, this method takes time.

You can also entrust the execution to a legal representative, but for this you will need a notary public who will confirm the right of a third party to conduct your affairs.

So you have learned what maternal capital is and how to deal with its design. Good luck to you!
