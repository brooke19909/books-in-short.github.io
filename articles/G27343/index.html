Acquaintance with the hotel Pariya Boutique Hotel 3 * (Phuket)

The modern four-story building is comfortably located in the beautiful town of Kathu, 15 km from the center of the island and 40 km from the airport. The complex was built in 2011, reconstruction was carried out in 2013. The residential building is literally buried in lush subtropical vegetation. Nearby there are noisy nightclubs and youth centers. Peace and tranquility will be the main companions of your holiday.





Meanwhile, the external infrastructure is equipped with retail, souvenir shops, bars and cafes. A few steps from the residential building there is a cabaret, entertainment centers, a boxing arena and other facilities that will interest visitors. For children under 12 years old, the Pariya Boutique Hotel 3 * hotel offers free accommodation, but only on existing beds.

By purchasing a tour to the complex, you get high-quality service and excellent mood for the entire stay. There will be no problems with arrival; public transport and taxis run to the hotel. If desired, you can order a transfer service. The employee of the complex will meet you from the airport or train station and deliver to your destination in a matter of minutes.

Accommodation options

The hotel complex Pariya Boutique Hotel (Phuket) 3 * has cozy rooms. Some rooms have balconies from which guests can watch the local flora and admire the flowering exotic plantings. The number of rooms is represented by the following categories: Standard, Deluxe, Superior, Deluxe Balcony. High-speed Internet is available throughout the building: visitors can use Wi-Fi unhindered.





What's in the room?

The basic equipment of the room includes the necessary set of equipment: a refrigerator with a small bar (drinks are chargeable), a TV with cable broadcasting. The room has an air conditioning system, a direct telephone line for communication with the administration. The rooms are equipped with a bathroom. Each guest is given a set of hygiene products: shower gel, shampoo, toothpaste / brush. Every day, dirty towels are replaced with clean towels. According to the planned schedule, cleaning is carried out in all rooms.
