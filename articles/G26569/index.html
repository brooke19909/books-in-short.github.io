Amerotel Royal Oasis Resort 4 *: photos and reviews of tourists

It is no secret that Sharm El Sheikh is one of the most popular and famous resort areas in Egypt. Every year, thousands of tourists, including Russian travelers, visit this city. The choice of hotels here is really huge - there are both economical options with small rooms and luxurious complexes with spacious apartments and other amenities.

The Royal Oasis Resort 4 * hotel, which is located in a picturesque corner of the city, very close to the seashore, is considered quite good. And today, many travelers are interested in additional information about this place. How to get to the hotel? What does the territory of the complex look like? What rooms and meals await the traveler here? Can I go here with my children? Answers to these questions will be useful to many readers.

Description and photos of rooms





Of course, tourists are primarily interested in information about living conditions. So what can the Amerotel Royal Oasis Naama Bay Resort 4 * hotel complex offer? Regardless of the category, all rooms on the territory of the hotel are quite spacious, bright, with large windows. Most rooms have access to the private terrace or a private balcony with views of the courtyard, garden or surroundings (depending on the location of the room).

The floors are covered with ceramic tiles, the room itself is furnished with enough furniture and decorated in a simple classic style. Naturally, the room also has the necessary appliances. In particular, air conditioning will save you from lunch heat. On TV, you can watch your favorite satellite channels (there is even one Russian). There is also a telephone and a safe with an electronic lock. A minibar is filled on request, all drinks, except water, are paid.

The bathroom comes with a shower. There is also a toilet, wall mirror and dressing table. Guests are entitled to a set of towels. There is also a hairdryer for drying hair. By the way, bathrobes and slippers can also be obtained at the hotel, but for a separate and very modest surcharge. Cleaning, linen and towels are changed every day.

Catering conditions for guests

In the hotel complex Royal Oasis Sharm Resort 4 *, guests can choose from several food schemes at once. Having ordered a tour with BB system, you can have breakfast in the main restaurant of the hotel. In addition, there is an HB scheme, which gives you the right to have breakfast and dinner on site. The all-inclusive system provides four meals a day, as well as drinks and light snacks in the hotel bars and cafes. Tourists can also pay for Palm Bar - this service allows you to get drinks (except beer, wine and imported products), as well as light snacks in the hotel bars and on the beach.





Reviews of travelers who managed to visit the Royal Oasis Resort 4 * indicate that you can really eat well in the main restaurant. Here you will be provided with a large selection of fresh fruits, vegetable dishes, as well as meat, slices, fish dishes, various desserts and fresh homemade cakes.

There are two bars by the pools. The lobby bar can be visited in the evening, and the small beach cafe serves a wide selection of soft drinks. Do not forget that you can eat in any of the institutions of the city - there are cafes, family pizzerias, and luxurious restaurants, so everyone will find something to their liking.

Hotel Royal Oasis Resort Hotel 4 * (Sharm-el-Sheikh): beach and water recreation for tourists

For the most part, travelers come to Sharm El Sheikh just to enjoy a beach holiday. That is why tourists are interested in questions about the provided entertainment. A small cozy beach is located just 250 meters from the hotel territory - you need to go down the stairs there. By the way, the hotel provides a free shuttle service to the beach. This stretch of coast is private, so outsiders will not bother you.





The beach here is clean, well-groomed, covered with soft sand. There are even rows of sunbeds and umbrellas. Beach towels are provided free of charge. There is a convenient area for beach volleyball. In addition, for a small fee you can go boating, canoeing, catamarans, bananas.

There is a lively coral reef near the shore, so this place attracts scuba diving enthusiasts. If you are a beginner, you can take preparatory courses at a diving school and take the first dive with an instructor. If exploring the underwater world is not new to you, then here you can rent the equipment you need for this.

Are there any conditions for spending time with children?

Today, many parents prefer to travel with their children. So is the Royal Oasis Resort 4 * hotel complex suitable for this? Of course, the hotel has all the conditions for a comfortable stay.

Upon request, an extra rollaway bed or a cradle for a child will be delivered to your room, and a highchair will be provided in the restaurant. There is no special children's menu, but among the abundance of dishes, you can definitely find something suitable for your child. You can also rent a stroller for walking, which is very convenient.





As for entertainment, the hotel has two small children's pools. For children aged 4 to 12 years, a mini-club works where teachers work with them. In the daytime, children are entertained by animators who organize various competitions, team games and performances. In the evenings, dances are organized for the youngest guests, where they can have fun with their peers.

Additional service on site

Of course, the hotel owners tried to create all conditions for a comfortable stay of tourists. For example, people traveling by their own transport, by the way, will have a small parking lot. It is possible to rent a car, motorcycle or even a bicycle.

Dry cleaning is available on site, and its services are billed separately. Here you can exchange currency, so you should not be afraid of problems with local money. Also, almost in any building there is a wireless Internet connection, which can be used for free.

Entertainment and leisure

What conditions for entertainment does the Royal Oasis Sharm Resort 4 * hotel provide? Egypt is a country that is suitable not only for beach and wellness, but also for sightseeing holidays. Around there are lots of interesting places, historical, architectural and cultural attractions that are definitely worth a visit. By the way, you can always use the services of a hotel tour desk.

As for entertainment on the territory of the hotel itself, there are plenty of them here. Guests are invited to relax by the pool - there is a large outdoor pool and another, smaller, but with a heating system. You can also use the outdoor jacuzzi for free.

There is a small gym. You will also have the opportunity to attend massage sessions, a sauna and a real Turkish bath. There is a basketball court, as well as a billiard room and ping-pong tables. And in the evenings you will be entertained by funny and groovy animators, which you definitely will not be bored with.
