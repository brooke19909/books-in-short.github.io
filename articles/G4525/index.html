Sights of Zlatibor

Attractions on Zlatibor in Serbia are attractions that are numerous in this area and they are all amazing. From time immemorial, mountain slopes were considered a health resort and aristocrats came here to treat various diseases. The Serbian king built his country house in a small village, which was called Kraleva Voda. Then the resort began to develop actively, today there are cozy holiday homes that will appeal to even the most demanding guests.



Among the main attractions you need to highlight such as:

  • Stopich cave;
  • Old village;
  • Shargan Eight;
  • Kremna;
  • Drvengrad.

Stopich Cave is considered one of the most famous natural attractions located in the vicinity of Zlatibor. In addition, it is considered the most beautiful cave in Serbia. It got its name thanks to the small village of Stopichi. The cave consists of several parts, some of them are filled with water.

Another amazing attraction is Staro Selo. The museum exposition consists of several houses, residential and commercial buildings dating from the XIX-XX centuries. In such houses rural families lived earlier. All houses are equipped with tools, utensils and various appliances. The buildings are partially rebuilt and adapted to the needs of the museum, which hosts various concerts, exhibitions and seminars.

The village of Kremna was widely famous, due to the fact that the Trabich family lived in it, who at one time were considered prophets and predicted the future. The house in which this family lived was preserved in the village. Near it is a large rounded stone, which, according to legend, flew from space.

Another famous attraction is the railway, which is called "Shagan Eight." In the Shagan Pass area, it forms a figure eight when one branch of the railway is located above the other.

Drvengrad is a museum exposition based on the ancient city, from where its name came from. In the museum exposition you can see old buildings and structures.

Hunting at Zlatibor

In the hunting season, not only local hunters, but also tourists tend to get to Zlatibor (Serbia). Quail hunting is permitted in this area, a huge number of which are simply amazing. Hunters prefer to go to big game, namely bears, wolves, deer.



In addition, in the lush vegetation of the mountains there is a fox, wild boar, badger, partridge, marten, and also squirrel. Only in this area above the mighty centuries-old pine trees can you see a white-headed vulture and an eagle. These feathered predators are considered a rare species and in other areas are almost completely exterminated.

For lovers of fishing, local lakes are of great interest, in which gudgeon, trout, taimen and many other types of fish are found in large numbers. That is why true lovers of male occupations seek Zlatibor in Serbia. They prefer fishing and hunting to luxurious comfortable hotels.
