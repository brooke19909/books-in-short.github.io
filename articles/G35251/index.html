Where in Abkhazia is it better to relax with a child? Choose together



Do you want to spend an unforgettable vacation on the beach with your family, but for some reason do not plan to fly abroad? There is an exit! The Republic of Abkhazia is the place where you will feel as if in paradise. The clear sea, pebble and sand beaches, eucalyptus groves and mountains ... Abkhazia is an oasis of beauty and tranquility on the Black Sea.

Where in Abkhazia is it better to relax with a child?

Family vacations include comfortable accommodation in a comfortable cottage or boarding house, an abundance of tasty and healthy food, a clean sea and a number of entertainment. All this you can find in resort towns such as Gagra and Pitsunda. These settlements are famous for a wide selection of sanatoriums and hotels for both family and youth recreation. High-class hotels have an entertainment program, rooms are equipped according to European standards, and nature is striking in its extraordinary beauty. We can offer economical tourists to stay in private houses, rent an apartment or a cottage near the sea, or choose an inexpensive boarding house. Where in Abkhazia is better to relax with a child, parents decide, but it is worth noting that children, in addition to the beach and sea, are interested in entertainment, excursions and trips. In Pitsunda, for example, there is one of the largest and most famous monkey nursery on the entire Black Sea coast . In Gagra there is a small water park. Adults and children who prefer long and exciting hiking trips should not drive past waterfalls and Lake Ritsa. The republic is famous for its nature and unique landscapes.



Where in Abkhazia it is better to relax with a child is an interesting but not simple question. If for some the priority is nature and fresh air, and the budget does not allow you to spend a vacation luxuriously, others will want to stay in the largest and most famous hotel, where the attendants will provide an entertainment program and delicious food. Fans of historical places, ancient architectural ensembles will like Sukhum. There is a botanical garden, as well as a small arboretum. A fortress was preserved in Sukhum, with which the city began its development. Pupils and students will be interested in Brezhnev and Stalin's dachas . Guides will be happy to tell tourists about the life of the heads of state and about their vacation in Abkhazia. How and when is it better to relax in Abkhazia - you decide. But do not miss the opportunity to plunge into the world of history, see the life of the people, understand the mentality and touch a foreign culture.



Where in Abkhazia is it better to relax with a child if he is not yet 7 years old? The child needs comfort and safety. Choose proven hotels for yourself and do not risk staying with the owners of private houses far from civilization and the sea.
