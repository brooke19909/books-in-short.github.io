Accounting Methods

To register a private entrepreneur, it is necessary to prepare and submit to the tax authorities a package of documents, the choice of a registration method depends on the preferences of entrepreneurs.

There are several options for registering an enterprise:



- self-registration, is the cheapest way, you have to spend 800 rubles on state duty, suitable for advocates of savings;

- IP registration by mail, convenient for residents in another city;

- registration of an IP with the involvement of a law firm, in this case, registration may cost a little more, but the correctness of filling out the documents is guaranteed and time is spent on filling them out;

- execution by proxy.

After registration and the start of activities, there are obligations to submit reports to the tax authorities. It is recommended to choose a taxation method at the registration stage, since an application for a simplified taxation variant must be submitted no later than 5 days after registration. Documents for the simplified tax system are best submitted simultaneously with the filing of documents for registration of the enterprise.

After going through all the procedures and receiving a certificate of registration on hand, there is a need to keep accounting. It is worth noting that not all entrepreneurs have an accounting education or the ability to maintain an accounting department, especially since changes periodically occur, for example, the new UTII-4 form in 2014

. Especially for such cases, there are organizations that provide accounting services.

There are also websites on the Internet where you can find a sample invoice and other forms of documents of a standard form: invoices and payment invoices, invoice, letters of attorney, waybills, payment orders, various orders and agreements, as well as commonly used forms: Sberbank receipts , power of attorney for a car, a contract of sale of a car. All that needs to be done is to enter data in the fields, documents are created dynamically in a specific format: they can be saved on a PC, printed on a printer and sent by e-mail.

For some forms (consignment note, power of attorney for a car, official letter of organization letter), there are several options for printing forms from which you can choose the right one.

Many forms are equipped with a detailed description, from which you can learn how to correctly fill out a power of attorney for a car, travel certificate or see examples.
