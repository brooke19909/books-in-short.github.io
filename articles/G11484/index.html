Hotel Sol Beso Mandrem 4 * (North Goa, India): description, reviews

India is a country that is ideal for a beach holiday. After all, here the traveler is waiting for miles of beautiful, clean beaches, warm sea waters and, of course, the incredible beauty of nature. A huge number of hotels have been built on the coast over the past few years. One of them is a small hotel complex Sol Beso Mandrem 4 *.

Of course, every tourist is trying to get the maximum amount of information. When paying for trips to India, you will probably want to know where exactly your hotel is located, how long you have to go to the beach, are there any conditions for relaxing with your child, etc. Answers to these questions will be useful to many travelers.

Description of location and territory of the hotel





Sol Beso Mandrem 4 * is a small hotel located on the coast of North Goa, namely next to the famous Mandrem beach, the distance to which is only 300 m. The nearest international airport is in the city of Dabolim, at a distance of about 60 km.

This is a small hotel that consists of a main building surrounded by a beautiful tropical garden. It is always pleasant to spend time here, enjoying the healing sea air and incredibly beautiful nature.

Hotel Sol Beso 4 * (North Goa, Mandrem): description and photo of rooms

This hotel is quite small - in its territory there are 17 deluxe rooms. You are waiting for small but comfortable rooms with European renovation, comfortable furniture and access to a private balcony or terrace.





Of course, guests can count on the necessary equipment. For example, you can use a modern air conditioning system, as well as a small fan. There is a TV connected to the satellite dish, as well as a radio and a telephone. Guests can use the small safe. At your service is a set for making hot drinks, as well as a minibar (it is free of charge filled only with drinking water).

In the bathroom you can take a shower or relax in the foam bath. There is a toilet, a mirror, a set of clean towels, which, by the way, are changed every day.

Are there any catering establishments?





Good nutrition is an important point for a truly quality vacation. Paying for accommodation in the Sol Beso Mandrem 4 * hotel room, tourists can count on daily breakfasts in the local restaurant. In the morning, a buffet is organized for guests, and judging by the reviews, the menu here is quite diverse. Guests can enjoy traditional breakfast options including toast, freshly baked pastries, cereals, light Indian soups, salads and, of course, fresh fruit.

At other times of the day, tourists can also eat at the hotel restaurant, however, orders are taken √† la carte, and the bill must be paid separately. By the way, they cook well here, and the choice of dishes is wide. Mostly they offer traditional Indian delicacies, but you can also order something more traditional, European. There is also a bar where you can get a chilled drink or a delicious cocktail at any time.

Beach and water activities





Naturally, most tourists visit Goa to have fun on the beach, because this place is famous for its picturesque coast. Mandrem's beautiful public beach is just 300 meters away. A convenient path leads to it, and on the way to the sea you can always enjoy the picturesque scenery or spend a few minutes in a colorful Indian shop.

The beach here is clean, covered with soft sand, which is regularly cleaned. Nevertheless, special beach shoes will not hurt. The entrance to the sea is convenient, and the waves are small, and therefore the coast is suitable for swimming. Since this is a place of public relaxation, you need to pay for the rental of sunbeds, mattresses, umbrellas and towels, although the prices are quite reasonable. The same applies to water activities, including boating and boat trips, water skiing, scuba diving, etc.

Do guests offer additional services?





Of course, guests of the Sol Beso Mandrem 4 * hotel can count on certain additional services, without which it is difficult to imagine a quality, comfortable stay. To begin with, it is worth saying that the hotel has a currency exchange point with a favorable rate. Laundry facilities are also available. If there are health problems, an experienced doctor will be called to your room to help, and for a small fee.

There is a luggage storage room, which is convenient for tourists who are going to travel along the coast. By the way, here you can rent transport for a small fee. Guests enjoy free parking. And almost throughout the territory there is access to the Internet, which is very important for many tourists.

Leisure and entertainment for travelers

Hotel Sol Beso Mandrem 4 * is a small hotel, which is designed primarily for a relaxing holiday for travelers. Here you can relax in silence and enjoy the unity with nature. There is a small pool in the courtyard, which is popular with hotel guests. Here they can refresh themselves, relax in the shade of trees or sunbathe on a comfortable sun lounger, and just chat with each other.

Do not forget that India is a country with a unique culture, interesting traditions and ancient history, and therefore ideal for sightseeing holidays. The hotel staff will help you with advice and tell you about the most interesting sights.

Are there any conditions for children's relaxation?

Of course, many tourists prefer to travel with their children. So is it worth buying family trips to India to choose this particular hotel? As already mentioned, the hotel here is small, but cozy and quiet - this atmosphere is perfect for children. If we are talking about beach holidays, then there is a shallow area where guys can splash around.

In the courtyard of the hotel there is a special pool for children, and in an open area, children can have a good time in the company of peers and parents. As for food, the restaurant always has a choice of dishes, despite the absence of a special children's buffet. By the way, parents speak positively about family vacations at the Sol Beso Mandrem Morjim 4 * hotel. Here both young tourists and adults have a good time.
