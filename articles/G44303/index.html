Galliot Hotel 4 *, Nha Trang: hotel reviews

If you plan to spend a vacation in Vietnam and are looking for an inexpensive, but comfortable hotel, located in the center of the resort town near the sea, then the Galliot Hotel 4 * in Nha Trang may appeal to you.



Location

Galliot is located in the heart of the resort town of Nha Trang in Vietnam. In the vicinity of the hotel there are lots of entertainment and shopping facilities. The city beach is 300 meters away. The nearest international airport is 35 kilometers from the hotel.



Nha Trang, Vietnam, Galliot Hotel 4 *: general description, photo

The housing stock of the hotel includes 134 rooms of the categories Standard, Superior, Deluxe and Suite. Their area, depending on the type, is from 28 to 50 square meters. All apartments have comfortable furniture, air conditioning, satellite TV, electronic safe, minibar, telephone, hairdryer, bathroom with bath or shower. Not all rooms have balconies. Bathrobes, slippers, and hygiene products are provided. Guests who choose the Galliot Hotel 4 * as a place to stay can use free Wi-Fi in their apartments.

The hotel has a restaurant. Breakfast is included in the price. On the top floor of the hotel is an outdoor pool with a sun terrace, which offers magnificent views of the center of Nha Trang and the sea.

Housing stock

Judging by the comments of our compatriots, they were mostly satisfied with the accommodation at the four-star Galliot Hotel (Nha Trang, Vietnam). However, experienced travelers are advised to choose rooms with balconies if possible. In this case, you can not only admire the surroundings, but also dry clothes here. To do this, enterprising tourists even bring with them from the house a string, which they pull on the terrace. If this factor is not critical for you, then you can save a little and book deluxe apartments. They have large floor-to-ceiling panoramic windows . The view from them is also excellent. Also, guests warn those who book the cheapest hotel rooms that they will most likely have tiny windows. If this is not critical for you, then there will be no problems. But still, it is advisable to find out in detail about the features of the reserved apartments so as not to experience disappointment upon arrival.

The rest of the tourists note that the rooms of the Galliot Hotel 4 *, reviews of which we are considering, are quite spacious, pleasantly decorated, bright. Furniture and appliances are not new, but everything is in good and working condition. If something fails, then report it to the reception. Hotel staff will quickly repair the problem. Guests considered the availability of wireless Internet access in the rooms to be a big plus. Moreover, Wi-Fi here has a fairly high connection speed and is free.



Cleaning

In general, our compatriots did not have any complaints about the quality of work of the Galliot Hotel maids. So, the guests liked that the rooms are cleaned at a convenient time for guests. For example, when leaving the apartment, you can press the corresponding button near the door. Be sure that the number will be removed by your return. As for tips, some travelers say that if you leave 1-2 dollars every one or two days, you will notice how much better cleaning will begin. However, most of the guests were satisfied with the work of the maids. Rooms are cleaned and towels are changed every day, but bed linen is updated approximately once a week. As expenditures, maids replenish supplies of bottled drinking water and personal hygiene products.

Service

Many guests of Galliot Hotel 4 * (Nha Trang, Vietnam) appreciated the attentive attitude towards guests by the hotel administration. So, for example, if for the period of your stay here you have a significant date - birthday, wedding anniversary, etc., then you can count on a present from Galliot. True, according to our compatriots, it is advisable to notify the administrator at the reception in advance about such an event. So, according to the guests, on their birthdays they received a compliment from the hotel in the form of a bottle of wine or champagne, a basket of fruit and a bouquet of flowers (for ladies). And on the wedding anniversary couples were presented with tasty small cakes with wishes.

Also, if necessary, you can always order a transfer from or to the airport. This service is paid, but its price, according to tourists, is very affordable.



Check in, departure

Many travelers who previously rested in hotels in Turkey and Egypt, arriving at the Galliot Hotel 4 * (Nha Trang, Vietnam) are mentally prepared to spend some time waiting to check in to the reserved rooms. However, even if you arrive at Galliot early in the morning, then most likely the keys to the apartments will be given to you within a quarter of an hour. Therefore, you do not have to waste precious vacation time waiting for arrival. In addition, upon arrival at the hotel you will be met by the receptionist, who will pick up your heavy bags and suitcases and deliver them to your room. In the lobby you will be offered a soft drink that will help you refresh yourself after the journey.

Location, Territory

Many tourists in their comments indicate the fact that this hotel was chosen by them largely due to its successful location. So, Galliot is located on the third coastline, almost in the very center of Nha Trang. Therefore, all objects of the tourist infrastructure of this resort city are within walking distance for hotel guests. So, in the neighborhood you will find a lot of cafes and restaurants, shops, massage parlors, supermarkets, souvenir shops, a market, car rental points, excursion bureaus, exchange offices, etc.

As for its own territory, then, since Galliot is a city hotel, it does not have it. So when you exit the hotel you will immediately get to the busy Nha Trang street.



Nutrition

The cost of living in Galliot Hotel, Nha Trang 4 * (Vietnam) includes breakfast only. They pass in the hotel restaurant from six to ten in the morning. According to most of the guests, the choice of dishes and products here made them very pleased. So, in the morning you can eat omelet, fried eggs, boiled eggs, bacon, cheese, Pho soup, various cereals, delicious pastries, fresh fruits. Of the drinks here are offered juices, tea, coffee. A big plus is the fact that cooks replenish stocks of dishes as necessary. So, regardless of when you arrive for breakfast, you can taste everything on the menu.

As for lunch and dinner, the guests of the Galliot can also eat in the restaurants of the hotel. It serves dishes of both Asian and European cuisine more familiar to us. According to our compatriots, they cook here very well. But still, most tourists preferred to eat outside the hotel. Moreover, there are plenty of places where you can eat in its vicinity.



Sea

According to travelers, the nearest beach can be reached in a leisurely walk from the hotel in 7-10 minutes. The road runs through city streets with a lot of shops, cafes, shops, etc. So along the way you can have a bite to eat, drink fruit juice or buy something. The beach here is municipal. You will have to pay for the rental of sun loungers and parasols. However, many tourists prefer not to rent a sun lounger, but to sit directly on the sand, laying a mat. The beach here is very clean, with a small yellowish sand. The only minus some tourists considered the lack of a shower.

As for the sea, it is usually calm in the morning. After lunch, waves often run up.

Guests of the Galliot Hotel 4 * also pay attention to the fact that beach towels are issued near the hotel reception. However, they must be returned every day until nine in the evening. If you do not, you may be fined. Therefore, it is better not to forget about it.
