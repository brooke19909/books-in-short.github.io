Bishkek hotels: overview, addresses, reviews

The capital of Kyrgyzstan, Bishkek, is famous for Lake Issyk-Kul, an excellent mountain zone, clean air and friendly international people. People from all over the world come here. In this city you can relax quite inexpensively, and Bishkek hotels will offer you comfortable living conditions and homeliness.

Hotel Versailles

This hotel has a very convenient location, from here you can walk to Panfilov Park, the Frunze Museum, the Art Gallery, and the Aquarium. There are two supermarkets within walking distance of the Versailles Hotel, and several cafes and restaurants are located nearby. Guests have access to an indoor pool, a sauna, free private parking and free Wi-Fi. The shared kitchen includes a microwave and washing machine. There is a relaxation room with a pool table. Shuttle services from or to the airport are available at an extra charge. Accommodation for guests is offered in the following rooms:

  • standard double with a double bed - 1690 rubles per day;
  • standard double with two single beds - 1690 rubles per day.

Each room at Versailles Hotel has a TV, a seating area, and a private bathroom with a shower. Some rooms have a balcony. This hotel is located in Bishkek at 10 Tashkentskaya Street.

Hotel Ambassador

If you are interested in hotels in the city center, then the four-star Ambassador hotel is for you. Ala-Too Square and the train station are just a five-minute walk away. The hotel is conveniently located near banks and offices of international companies, which may interest business people. You can also walk to most city attractions.

The on-site restaurant serves European cuisine. You can use the services of free parking, free Wi-Fi, luggage storage. An ATM is installed on the territory of the hotel, there is a currency exchange office. The hotel has its own library. Bicycle rental is free. For guests with small children, babysitting services are available. Guests are accommodated in the following rooms:

  • standard single - 6572 rubles per day;
  • standard double with one double or two single beds - 7511 rubles per day for two people, and 6885 rubles for one person;
  • family (for 4 people) - 14084 rubles per day;
  • Suite room with a balcony - 15022 rubles per day;
  • Executive Suite - 18778 rubles per day;
  • Presidential Suite - 21595 rubles per day.

The room rate is based on a good breakfast. All rooms are soundproofed, equipped with TV, air conditioning, safe, minibar, free toiletries, many have a balcony. Compared to other Bishkek hotels, the Ambassador is one of the most modern and expensive. It is located at: st. Orozbekova, building 32.

Hotel "Flagman"

Some of the hotel's windows offer wonderful mountain views. Within walking distance there is a supermarket, to walk to the river, you need to walk only 500 meters. Breakfast is served in the mornings. There is a dining area on the street. Picnic enthusiasts can use the barbecue facilities. For tourists who come to Bishkek to ski, a ski storage room is provided. A sauna, massage services, free parking and free wireless internet are provided. Guests can bring their pets with them. Accommodation at the Flagman Hotel is provided in the following rooms:

  • single budget (shared toilet and bathroom) - 1189 rubles per day;
  • budget double (toilet and shared bathroom) - 1565 rubles per day;
  • standard double - 2003 rubles per day;
  • Deluxe Double Room - 2817 rubles per day;
  • family (two bedrooms, designed for 4 people) - 3756 rubles per day;
  • Luxe room (two bedrooms, there is a kitchen for 4 people) - 4069 rubles per day.

The room rate is based on a good breakfast. All rooms are equipped with a TV. Some have air conditioning. The hotel is located at: Bishkek, st. Logvinenko, house 139.
