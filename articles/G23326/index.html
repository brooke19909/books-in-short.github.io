Anapa, boarding house "Sofia": reviews

The Black Sea resort of Anapa is a city of sun, sea, fun entertainment, children's health resorts and good memories, carefree relaxation and an unforgettable pastime. Recently, construction of new and updating of old objects of the tourism business has been actively conducted here. A curious fact - they love this ancient feminine name in a glorious city called Anapa. Pension "Sofia", hotel "Sofia", medical center "Sofia", guest house with the same name, sanatorium ... How can a tourist who has come here not to get confused in all this? We will try to introduce you to all the "Sophia" available in Anapa, to tell in detail what service in each of these objects you can get and how much you will pay for it.

Pension - hotel "Sofia" (Anapa): how to get?

This tourist attraction is called a spa hotel, a sanatorium, and just a hotel. In fact, this is a private boarding house in which there is an opportunity to heal. It is located on the most famous and longest Anapa Avenue, stretching right up to the village of Vityazevo and called Pionersky. House number is 19-a. Nearby there are stops of public transport, a water park. Sanatorium "Sofia" (Anapa) is located 6 km from the railway station Dzhemete station, where trains come from any Russian city. From the station you can get to the boarding house by taking a taxi for 500 rubles, or by bus No. 100 for 22 rubles, which stops right at the station. She does not go so often. Therefore, another cheap option is to walk from the station (about 450 meters) towards the water park (it can be seen from the station). There is a bus stop number 128, 134, 114. The traffic interval is no more than 5-7 minutes. All minibuses go along Pionersky Prospekt, and, in fact, the village of Dzhemete is located on it.





You can arrive in Anapa not only by train, but also by plane. From the airport, it is expensive to get to the guesthouse by taxi, and cheaply by minibus, which goes to the central bus station, where you can transfer to public transport.

The territory of the boarding house

The beautiful Anapa is famous for its bright, not fading summer or winter flower beds and evergreen gardens. Pension "Sofia" does not fall out of the general background. Its territory, although very compact, is all buried in blooming roses of various varieties. In this, one can say, small, but always shining cleanliness of the territory, there is a huge pool surrounded by green lawns with sun loungers and umbrellas, an excellent bar, whose tables cover the colorful tents from the sun, a playground, a parking lot. Around are laid wide paths paved with tiles. Along them are flowerbeds, rockeries, cypress and junipers planted. But the most beautiful and green corner is located on the top floor of one of the buildings, where there is a winter garden with exotic trees from all over the Earth and a small pond, the water surface of which is decorated with pink lotuses. For guests of the boarding house there is a bar, there is a cozy place for rest and relaxation.

Hotel Sofia

This cozy little hotel, which many call the "guest house" Sofia "" (Anapa), is located on Astrakhan Street, house number 37. It is quite far from the sanatorium of the same name. Astrakhan Street is located a bit further from the sea, about 10 minutes walk, but closer to the city center. The hotel has a total of 17 comfortable rooms. They are designed for 2 or 3 people.
