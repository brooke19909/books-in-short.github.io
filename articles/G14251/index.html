The American dream





The game "Monopoly" has been released by Parker Brothers since 1935. She appeared on the market under the following circumstances. In 1934, the unemployed engineer Charles Darrow came up with the idea to create a game in which one could trade real estate. At first, the Parkers did not see anything unusual in her and refused the inventor.

However, Charles did not stop there and, at his own peril and risk, ordered the 5000th edition of the game from the printing house, which sold out in no time. Seeing the success of the entrepreneurial Darrow, Parker Brothers acquired the rights to Monopoly. A year later, she became the most popular game in the United States. Darrow himself received the title of "living embodiment of the American dream."

This is how this legendary game was created through thorns and difficulties. And every business that needs to be started from scratch undergoes the same. But you can learn how to overcome difficulties today, just by purchasing the "Monopoly".
