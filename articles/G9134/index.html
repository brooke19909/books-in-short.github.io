Jolly Roger, Engels: review, description and reviews

On the banks of the picturesque Volga, on the Sazanka archipelago, Jolly Roger is waiting for its guests. A modern tourist complex is located in a beautiful area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe city of Engels: it is surrounded by an oak forest and on both sides - the waters of the Volga. Open year-round.



Accommodation

Guests check into the Golden Doubloon Hotel. This is a new three-story building. The windows overlook the bay or the pool area located on the Jolly Roger (Engels) complex. Prices for rental apartments - from 3,500 rubles.

The junior suites have a large double bed, there is a shower with a set of necessary accessories, a telephone for communication with the reception, a TV with satellite antenna. Air passes round-the-clock purification. For an additional fee, you can arrange another bed.

Towels in the rooms are changed daily, bed linen every four days.

The luxury apartments are equipped the same way, but have a large area and a set of upholstered furniture for comfortable watching TV.



Infrastructure

The Jolly Roger (Engels) is a huge territory with equipped beaches, a large banquet hall, several sports fields, a sauna with a warm pool, a Russian wood-burning sauna, a conference hall, etc.

Three meals a day is provided by the restaurant. It offers dishes of Russian and European cuisine. The menu is very diverse, products of local manufacturers.



On the camp site there are several rental points for sports and beach equipment. And also equipped with covered gazebos with barbecue areas. They have large tables and comfortable benches.

The best time to relax

The warm season is perfect for restoring strength and recharging with solar energy. Undoubtedly, summer is the perfect time to travel with friends or family, or spend the next vacation.

But is it worse in winter? Immersion in the snow-white beauty, as in a fairy tale, will provide a sea of ‚Äã‚Äãpositive emotions.

Jolly Roger (Engels) will give a breathtaking New Year's vacation for the whole family. Unforgettable adventures set in a positive mood for the year ahead.

Weekends, especially if they are elongated, are also a good reason to relax at this camp site.

Entertainment

Guests, regardless of the holiday season, always have something to do. For example, you can go fishing, both in summer and winter.

Jolly Roger (camp site, Engels) always organizes many sporting events. Family ice skating has already become a tradition from late December to mid March.



At any time of the year, you can relax with friends or family in the electric sauna. It is located on the ground floor of the hotel complex and, in addition to the steam room, has a warm pool and a cozy relaxation room. It is possible to invite a full-time massage therapist (paid service).

The Jolly Roger Recreation Center (Engels) offers several walking routes through a stunningly beautiful forest. Untouched corners of nature, the lack of crowds of tourists and acquaintance with local culture and customs. Such a quiet vacation charges with positive and energy for the whole year.

For lovers of active sports, there are bicycles, skateboards, roller skates, tablets, rowing boats and much more at the rental points.

There are several table tennis tables on the camp site, there are open areas for badminton, indoor soccer and volleyball. There are also equipped places for playing darts and an area with weight training equipment.

Summer vacations are great for water skiing and jet skiing. Help may be provided by a qualified instructor.

The beach is a separate pleasure

For lovers of summer flax vacation at the camp site organized beautiful beaches with comfortable sunbeds, tents and lounge areas.

Has its own beach "Jolly Roger" (Engels). This is the whole universe. It starts with parking. You only need to pay for car security, time is unlimited.

On the beach there are rental points for catamarans and other boats. An experienced instructor will teach and show how to use any sports equipment. The order on the water is watched by rescuers.

A summer bar is also organized here. The tent offers many soft drinks for both adults and children, a friendly bartender. For the convenience of guests under the awning are tables and comfortable chairs.

A cafe is open on the other side of the beach. On his menu, dishes are more serious: kebab, french fries, baked meat and vegetables, various sandwiches, slices (meat, vegetables, fruit), several types of ice cream, etc.

Almost every evening in the warm season, discos, theme parties are organized on the beach and closed mini-concerts of our pop stars are held.

The beach is constantly being cleaned. Fine-grained sand, imported. The bottom is shallow, ideal for families with children. For them, a whole site with swings and carousels has been built.

A changing area and showers are organized on site. There is a tap at the exit to rinse your feet off the adhering sand.



Bath complex

A unique bathhouse - a log house is very popular among guests of the recreation center "Jolly Roger" (Engels). After all, you can plunge directly into the Volga from it!

The blockhouse is two-story and can simultaneously accommodate up to 20 people. The covered terrace, located on the second floor, offers beautiful views of the islands of the Sazanka archipelago and the bend of the Volga. For guests of the bath, a separate secure parking is organized.

By prior arrangement and for a fee, you can invite a bath attendant. He knows the healing types of massage with brooms with aromatic oils.

There is also the possibility to order caviar, fish and crayfish, various drinks.

At the end of all procedures, guests are offered tea (black, green, herbal) with homemade jams and honey.

Organization of events

Jolly Roger (Engels) is an incredibly successful place for organizing and conducting a celebration or banquet of any complexity. In addition to the banquet hall, an outdoor event can be arranged in a cozy covered gazebo.

For receptions, weddings or corporate parties, it is possible to rent tents. The price includes the necessary furniture, lighting, tablecloths and covers, a stage complex. For a separate price list, you can order an acoustic system, photo and video shooting, lighting equipment, waiters, a host, etc.

All events are organized taking into account all the wishes of the customer, taking into account existing standards and at the highest level.
