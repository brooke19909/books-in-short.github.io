Bijouterie

When thinking about what can be made from old discs, consider an option such as making women's jewelry. Earrings made of this material look very unusual and interesting. For work you will need:

  • one disk;
  • color adhesive tape;
  • soldering iron with nozzles for decorative work;
  • accessories for earrings;
  • cardboard puzzle;
  • marker;
  • needle.

Tape the surface of the disc with colored tape. Put a puzzle from a children's game on top and circle with a marker. With a heated soldering iron with a sharp nozzle, burn out the painted part along the contour. Run the second blank in the same way. At the top of each puzzle, use a red-hot needle to melt the hole. What to make from old drives for children?



Bright and funny toys can be made together with a child from laser discs. For example, such a fish. For its manufacture you will need:

  • two disks;
  • double-sided color cardboard;
  • glue "Dragon";
  • pencil, scissors;
  • ribbon;
  • markers.

On the cardboard, draw patterns of details of the fins, tail, eyes and mouth of the future fish, cut them with scissors. Put glue on the disk, put all the blanks on top and cover with another disk on top. Leave the product to dry. Fold the accordion (side fins) out of a rectangular sheet of cardboard and insert it into the center hole in the discs. Glue the details of the eyes. Flakes can be drawn with colored markers. Pass the tape through the hole in the product and secure it with a knot. The toy is ready.
