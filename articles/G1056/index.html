Popular Nizhny Novgorod Hotels

Nizhny Novgorod - is in third place in the Russian Federation, in terms of population. This city is a great place for recreation and tourism, many hotels, which will offer various services and at different price categories. Nizhny Novgorod hotels are located both in the city center and on the outskirts. The most popular hotels in Nizhny Novgorod are: Volzhsky Slope, Gostiny Dom, Nizhny Novgorod.

The Volzhsky Slope Hotel is located almost in the center of the city. Guests are offered a variety of rooms: apartments, standard, deluxe, superior. This hotel provides many services such as luggage storage, laundry, beauty salon, hairdresser, taxi call. You can visit the cafe, which is open around the clock. There is also a conference room, and a business center. There is a parking lot near the hotel complex.

One of the most famous hotels in the city is Zarechnaya. It is located in the very center of the city on the main street. Various numbers will be offered to visitors: single, double, luxury. There is also a conference room in this hotel complex, which can accommodate up to fifty people.
