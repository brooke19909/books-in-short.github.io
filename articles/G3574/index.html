A shuttle bus also leaves here. He departs from the Zhiguli bus station. A bus also leaves for the village of Shiryaevo. You can board it at the bus station in Togliatti. Camping has its own pier. Small boats can moor here. In the "Park" you can rent a house for one day or for a longer period.
