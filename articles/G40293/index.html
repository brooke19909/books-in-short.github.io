Real assassins

In fact, the assassins presented in the game are extremely far from the original.

A stable legend lives in the world, supposedly assassins - this is such a medieval special forces of the Arab world, an analogue of the ninja from Japanese culture. In fact, this is not so at all. At its core, these killers are closer to kamikazes, and if we draw analogies with the world today, then ordinary suicide bombers. Only the tasks they set themselves were different: not to scare the Gentiles, but to destroy a specific goal. As a rule, immediately after this, the assassin died himself.

The movement was organized in 1094. Adherents of the son of Caliph al-Mustansir, led by the Persian Ismaili Zasan ibn Sabbah, announced that Abu Mansur Nizar is the ancestor of the invisible imam. In the ranks of the assassins recruited young men who quickly turned into fanatics, able to give their lives for their chapter.

Now similar communities are still preserved in some parts of the Earth. They are mainly located in 20 countries.

The basic knowledge about these warriors is given by the very first part of the Assassin Creed game. In it, they still looked like their real colleagues, but later completely changed for the sake of the plot and entertainment.

Raising Assassins

In order to create the perfect fighter, boys were recruited at a young age. The Elder was engaged in the selection. The boys grew in severity and starving. A little matured youths were given the opportunity to find out what luxury is. They were transferred to the Garden, where there was an abundance of appearances, fruits, and female bodies for comfort. The warriors ready for the fight lived together, had nothing of their own. They waited their turn to become elected for the murder. It is a great honor for them.

Do it yourself

One of the most famous assassin weapons in the game is a hidden blade. He is a deadly retribution in the capable hands of the order. And how to make an assassin‚Äôs weapon at home, let's try to figure it out.

It works quite interestingly. A ring is attached to one of the fingers, to which a thread is drawn. She, in turn, clings to the spring. As soon as the assassin correctly jerks a finger, the blade immediately jumps out of the bracers (part of the armor). From there it appears with the help of a spring, and it is also pushed back.

Such a thing allows you to perform many hidden actions, which is what the assassins use. For example, you can approach the victim in the crowd and cut the throat with one touch, and in the meantime, the blade will instantly disappear in your hand.



Of course, the fans of the game have already found those craftsmen who are able to realize their wishes. We will try too.

For this we need a knife blade, gloves and guides (ball). They should be similar to those in computer desks. On such guides the little table for the keyboard leaves. The blade must be long and the same size as the carriage. They can be very easily combined using special glue, fasteners or adhesive tape. There are holes on the guides. A glove can be tied to them, which should be made of strong leather. For an aesthetic and more warlike look, you can come up with various decorations. Here it all depends solely on imagination.

Except for the visual part, the blade is already completely ready. It is thrown out due to a sharp wave of the hand. Of course, this is not the original - it was activated using a complex system of springs. In general, there are many ways to make a hidden blade. But this one is the easiest.



However, it is worth remembering that it is illegal to walk along the street with such non-toy devices. Officially, such blades and swords fall under the "cold weapon" class, because the "Templar hunt" can quickly end at the nearest police station.
