Hotel "Kars", Divnomorskoe: reviews, address, services

Krasnodar Territory has long been famous as a Russian tourist Mecca of beach holidays. There are many popular resort villages with excellent infrastructure, reasonable prices and good service. One of them is Divnomorskoe (Gelendzhik). In the very center of this village is the Kars Hotel. His address is very simple: Chernomorskaya street, 21-b. Below we will consider the reviews of guests about how they lived in this hotel, about the level of its services and the quality of rest. The hotel is not seasonal, it works all year round, so you can go here not only on vacation. This is a chain hotel, it belongs to the line of the same name, which has hotels in different areas of Greater Gelendzhik.



Recreation, Divnomorskoe

Hotel "Kars" is located in the village where Russian tourists like to go. Many spend their holidays here annually. The sea is cleaner here, beaches are more convenient and air is more transparent than in Big Gelendzhik. And to the city center - only some twelve kilometers. It can be reached by bus.

But in Divnomorskoye, tourist infrastructure is so good that many people don‚Äôt need to go to Big Gelendzhik. The climate here is almost Mediterranean, and the nature is very beautiful. Nearby there is not a single industrial enterprise that only speaks of good ecology. No wonder the resort is positioning itself as a child. Young people love to come here. In Divnomorskoy there is where to walk and "break away". The local night clubs, especially those in the open air, are famous throughout the Krasnodar Territory.

Of course, the main attraction of the village Divnomorskoe is the beach. Although it is not wide, it is comfortable and clean. There are a lot of people in the center, but if you go a little left or right, you will always find secluded corners.



Territory services nearby

Hotel "Kars" (Divnomorskoe) reviews are called modern and comfortable. And indeed, it is a completely new building. It opened only in 2013. It does not accept guests with very young children (up to a year), so when booking a room you need to consider this. It is also advisable to make a 10 percent advance payment in advance so that after your arrival there will be no troubles when checking in. For customers with cars, an underground parking and outdoor parking are available.

Since the hotel is located in the center, shops, pharmacies, a market, a bank, a minibus stop, all the entertainment of the village, including a night club, are just around the corner. The hotel has a conference room for seminars. You can put things in the wash. On each floor of the building you can use an ironing board and an iron. At the reception there is a safe for valuables.

At your request, the administration can arrange a transfer to the station or airport to Krasnodar, Anapa, Novorossiysk or Gelendzhik. An on-site pool with a sun terrace, a gym and a playground for children are on site. In the courtyard of the hotel there are many summer tables, there are places for barbecue and cooking meat or fish on the grill.



Rooms

Describing the Kars Hotel (Divnomorskoe), reviews indicate that guests are accommodated in rooms of different categories of comfort. There are, for example, two-, three- and four-seater standards, with and without balconies. Some of them are under the roof, in the attic, and are upholstered in wood. There are double and triple junior suites, as well as suites. The latter have several rooms, each of which has a balcony.

All rooms have Wi-Fi, good plumbing, quality furniture, a different and original design. Everywhere there are flat-screen TVs with a large selection of programs, refrigerators, split-system. The rooms are very clean, cleaned every three days, but can more often if you declare this at the reception. Beautiful corridors with lights, sconces and sofas in the corners. The rooms are very nice to go.

Nutrition

Now let's talk about how the Kars Hotel (Divnomorskoye) feeds its guests. Reviews say that food is not included in the price, it must be paid separately. The resort has a dining room, and there is a cafe-bar near the pool. The food is delicious. The dining room is spacious, offers three meals a day. The menu is very varied, the portions are large, and the prices are quite inexpensive. Even beer at the pool bar for bottling is cheaper than on the promenade.

You will find many different catering establishments right outside the gates of the hotel. One of them - under the name "Big Spoon" - is very popular with tourists, and they repeatedly mention it in reviews. There is a cafe on the way to the sea, and on the promenade. Barbecue can be fried directly on the territory, there are all conditions for this.



Beach and sea

The coast of the village is five hundred meters from the hotel. As in all of Gelendzhik, the resort Divnomorskoe beach is pebble. Go to him no more than five to six minutes. This is the central beach of the village, along it there is a beautiful promenade where you can walk and have a snack. The hotel gives out special towels in order to sunbathe on them. If the beach is crowded, you can always find the so-called wild coast, cliffs and secluded bays. No wonder nudists love to visit this area. In addition, the hotel itself has a heated pool and a children's area. Around it are umbrellas and deck chairs, there is a ramp for people with disabilities. This is a great solution if there are waves or bad weather at sea.



Hotel "Kars" (Divnomorskoe): guest reviews

Many tourists liked the location of the hotel and the amenities in the rooms. The street is quiet, and not far from the sea. Some vacationers note that pets can also be brought here - you only need to coordinate this issue with the administration in advance. The hotel is considered to be the best and most presentable in Divnomorskoye. The hotel is constantly expanding, improving. Its building and grounds are very beautiful, some even compare it with Turkish hotels. Everything is planted with beautiful flowers, a lot of greenery, there are tents to create additional shade. The staff is attentive and helpful, constantly monitors the order and cleanliness. Employees love children and are always ready to help. Therefore, many families go on vacation in the village of Divnomorskoye. The Kars Hotel has established itself well, and most of those who lived there last year recommend it to other guests.
