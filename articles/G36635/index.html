Apery

Small and curious tourists will certainly appreciate a visit to the monkey nursery. That is why during a family vacation with children in Adler, you must definitely visit it.

This nursery is located in the village of Vesyoloye and is a large Research Institute of Medical Primatology, which is the only one in all of Russia. He appeared in 1927 and currently has collected in its considerable territory 4,500 individuals of primates, among which there are representatives of Asian and African monkeys.

If during a vacation with children at sea in Adler, the family comes to this nursery, guests are offered a choice of one of three types of excursions: "Sightseeing", "Traditional" and "Discover science." Depending on the orientation of the tour, guests will be able to get acquainted with the views of primates living here, observe their life and habits, as well as participate in real scientific experiments, and all this in 40 minutes!

The nursery is located at Adlersky district, the village of Vesyoloye, Mira street, 177. You can visit it any day from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m.
