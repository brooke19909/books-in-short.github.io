Is a designer a lucrative profession? How to find out how much a designer earns?

A designer is no longer a new, but a very relevant profession. An experienced and talented specialist will help create a unique project. It can emphasize the individual style of your home, country house or create a unique style in clothing.

To become a good specialist, it is not enough to have a good taste. First of all, you need to get an appropriate education, best of all a higher education. After all, a good designer combines an artist, stylist, architect, foreman and constructor. It will also require ownership of certain computer programs.





And how much does a designer earn in various fields of activity? What kind of designers are there and how is the work of creative people appreciated?

How does a designer work?

The salary of a designer largely depends on how he works. Specialists of this kind can make a living in several ways:

  • Freelance - independent work, without a boss, alarm clock and Monday;
  • work in a private company or agency at a rate;
  • organization of own design agency.

If you want to work only for yourself and not depend on the vagaries of your bosses, the first option will suit you. There are many exchanges where one-time orders are offered with good payment for the result. To take advantage of the offer, you need to register on the site, make a small portfolio of your work and apply for the work. The customer himself will contact you, after which it will be possible to discuss all the details of the work and agree on payment.

You do not want to spend a lot of time on independent searches of the customer? It is worth paying attention to design agencies. Web Designer and Graphic Specialist

One of the most popular areas in this area is a web or graphic designer. Such people can easily help you develop business cards, websites, landing pages, posters, avatars and even develop a corporate identity for any organization.

How much a designer of this kind earns depends on several factors:

  1. The presence of a diverse and high-quality portfolio. A potential customer should understand the level of your skills.
  2. The average price for services of this kind - do not overestimate the price for services, this will scare away customers. But to appoint too low a price is also not worth it. Of course, this will attract you more customers, but very soon you will be tired of sitting out all day for miserable pennies.
  3. The time taken to complete the order. How much a designer earns depends on how much he works. If you take one order a week, you will not see large profits.

Landscape designer

Another fairly popular profession is a landscape designer. The salary of such a specialist also depends on many factors. A good landscape specialist has been appreciated more and more recently. This is due to the fact that many families, tired of the bustle of the city, tend to move to nature, which means that they are faced with the need to equip the adjacent territory.





If your profession is a landscape designer, your salary will depend on which region you work in. In some regions, the price for such services ranges from 20 to 30 thousand rubles per project. In the immediate suburbs, you can already ask for 50-60 thousand rubles for one compound. And if you know how not only to create a beautiful picture from flowers, trees and fountains, but understand the construction, geodesy, water supply and other areas of activity, then your earnings can rise to 100-120 thousand. Clothes designer

The salary of a clothing designer depends on the level of the company in which he works. Small firms will offer 25-30 rubles per month. Salaries in large metropolitan firms can reach up to one hundred thousand.
