How to check the balance on a megaphone without the hassle and worries

Modern man seeks to keep everything under control. And the most important assistant in this matter is the mobile phone. True, in the hustle and bustle of the most important thing they sometimes forget. Namely - to replenish the balance of your phone. Therefore, mobile operators provide a lot of ways to control their account.



Of course, today the company will surely remind its subscribers of the need to replenish their account so as not to be left without communication. But usually such a message is sent when there is very little money left. Therefore, it is still better to know in advance how to check the balance on the Megaphone.

Such information is usually provided when connected to a network. Also, it can always be clarified on the company's website. This information is available, as Megafon seeks to provide its subscribers with the most reliable information on cash flows. Therefore, the company does everything so that each client knows how to see the balance on the Megaphone, and at any time convenient for itself.

You can clarify the cash balance in different ways: using USSD * 100 #, SMS to number 000100 and a call to number 0501. If you are within the home network, checking the balance is free. But in SMS roaming and calls will be paid. And if the mobile phone is not nearby, then you can check your balance with a landline phone. You just need to call the number +79271110501 and in tone mode dial your phone number and the # symbol.



But even knowing all this, some still forget to replenish the account. Therefore, for such customers, the company developed the "Living balance" service. By connecting it, you can simply forget how to check the balance on the Megaphone. Using it, you can at any time by dialing 0500755 listen to information about the balance of another subscriber.
