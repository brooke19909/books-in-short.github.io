Cash registers for IP: price and registration. Is a cash register mandatory for IE?

Let's talk about the activities of individual entrepreneurs. Who are PIs? These are individuals registered as entrepreneurs. They do not have the status of a legal organization, but they have many similar rights in organizing activities, as well as obligations that are imposed on them as entrepreneurs, and not ordinary people. At the beginning of the opening of IP, citizens have many difficulties and questions in paperwork. Let's look at some of them. After registering, IPs ask themselves whether they need a CCV to carry out activities.

Cash registers for IP

The law defines those types of activities of individual entrepreneurs and organizations for which the use of cash registers is mandatory:

1. Trade in goods.
2. Performance of work.
3. The scope of services (cash payment, bank cards through the terminal).





There are, of course, exceptions.

What types of activities can do without the use of a fiscal apparatus?

The cash register can not be used in such cases:

  1. Trade at exhibition centers, markets, fairs.
  2. Small retail take-out of food, non-food items from trays, carts, baskets.
  3. Trade in tanks for milk, kvass, vegetable oil, beer, live fish, vegetables, and melons.
  4. Reception from citizens of glass containers and metal.
  5. Sale of newspapers, lottery tickets, religious literature, magazines.

In all these cases, they do not use a cash register, regardless of whether it is a legal entity or individual entrepreneur.





Whether cash registers are needed for individual entrepreneurs is determined primarily by the type of taxation system they are located on.

Legal entities and individual entrepreneurs who are single tax payers for certain types of activities can afford to do without the use of CCP. However, one should not forget that this is possible only if a document (receipt, check) is issued at the request of the client to confirm receipt of money for the goods and services provided.

The IPs on the patent may also not apply CCP. In fact, you can pay without a cash register, but use strict reporting forms. There are approved forms of such documents equivalent to cash vouchers.

Let's answer the most common questions of individual entrepreneurs:

1. Is a cash register for IP (simplified) needed in 2015? - Definitely needed.
2. Is it mandatory to use a cash register for private entrepreneurs when carrying out activities?



The main factor in the application of CCP will always be the type of activity, place and availability of receipts confirming the sale or provision of services.

Registration of cash register for IP

CCP is not just established at the place of business, it must first be registered in the tax. To do this, submit such papers:

1. Application for registering a cash register.

2. Passport KKT.

3. A document on the conclusion of a technical support agreement with a service organization.





Registration of the cash register will take place no more than five days from the date of application. The tax office will issue a card on registration of the device and return the documents attached to the application. If necessary, you can go through re-registration and deregistration. The procedure will take up to five days. The application is accompanied by a passport of the device and a registration card.

Penalty for not using CCV

For violations in the application of CCP, an administrative fine is imposed. For example, in such cases:

1. Broken check (if there was a cash register, the check was not issued).
2. The use of CCPs that do not comply with the standards.
3. Failure to issue a sales receipt upon purchase.

The size of the fine is from one and a half to two thousand rubles for individual entrepreneurs.
