Best Alanya Hotel 3 - Budget Hotel

In Alanya, tourists from Russia have long preferred to relax. The resort offers travelers numerous entertainment, an interesting excursion program and a rich hotel base. Mostly people come here on vacation looking for comfort, fun and variety, combined with an economical vacation.

The bulk of the hotel base here falls on three- and four-star hotels with an affordable price level. In general, in Alanya for little money you can count on comfortable accommodation and quality service.

For those of our compatriots who want to have a great rest in this part of the Turkish coast, the budget Best Alanya Hotel 3 * is suitable.





general information

The hotel is located two hundred meters from the sea. Alanya's promenade, where vacationers flock in the evenings, is only ten minutes walk. The hotel is a multi-story building. Not far from it there are shops, restaurants. There is a public transport stop nearby. In just 2 lira, in five minutes you can be in the central part of the resort, where there is a huge bazaar and supermarket.

The hotel has its own free parking. The airport is one hundred and thirty kilometers, but the transfer time runs unnoticed and quickly, since the landscapes surrounding the road are very exciting.

Infrastructure





The Best Alanya Hotel 3 * hotel, although small, however, it offers a number of the most necessary services for a comfortable stay in it. For the convenience of guests there is a luggage storage and safe deposit boxes. At the exchange point, you can exchange the currency for lira. The reception is open around the clock. Here you can call a taxi, send a fax, and copy documents. Dry cleaning and laundry services are also available (surcharge).

Alanya has many places to visit. In order not to spend money on buying sightseeing tours and guide services, many prefer to rent a vehicle, including a bicycle, and explore the surroundings on their own. There is an elevator in the only hotel building and there is an express check-in service.

Rules and prices

As in other hotels, there is a checkout time at Best Alanya Hotel 3 (Alanya) . Guests are checked in after fourteen hours. On the day of departure, the room must be vacated before noon. If tourists arrive at the hotel earlier than the set time, then if they have a free room, they will be given the keys immediately. Otherwise, you have to wait until the previous guest leaves the occupied room, the employees will prepare it properly and check in.

In Best Alanya Hotel 3 * it is forbidden to smoke not only in the rooms, but also on the territory. But for those who suffer from such a bad habit, there are two apartments where smoking is allowed.

Tours to this hotel are offered today by few tour operators. Therefore, there are few Russians. Prices start at sixteen thousand rubles for two with full board, eight-day stay in a standard room and departure from Moscow.





Housing stock

Built in 1989, Best Alanya Hotel 3 (Turkey) was thoroughly renovated in 2012. Its territory is small - only two and a half thousand square meters. It is fully equipped. The hotel rooms are located in a six-story building, in which there are two elevators. The apartment is 18 square meters. meters. Standard and family rooms can accommodate three people at a time.

Apart from comfortable furniture, individually adjustable split systems as well as satellite TV contribute a lot to comfortable accommodation. The combined bathroom has a shower, hairdryer and a large mirror. In all public areas and rooms of the Best Alanya Hotel 3 * hotel there is a possibility of connection to the wireless Internet. A minibar with a safe, in which you can place a small laptop, is provided for payment.

Nutrition

As in many other hotels in Alanya, this hotel offers meals on a "full board" or "half board" system. Tourists themselves choose the type, based on preferences and, of course, opportunities. Russians, as a rule, prefer not to worry about food, so they stop at the "full board", which is offered as a buffet in the restaurant Best Alanya Hotel 3. Reviews about the cuisine here are mostly positive. But there are those vacationers who do not eat at the hotel, preferring to eat in restaurants or cafes in the city.



Entertainment

Since Best Alanya Hotel 3 * is small, it does not provide an entertainment program. And so for those who prefer animation, it makes sense to choose another hotel. In this hotel, you can spend your day swimming in the pool and sunbathing on the comfortable sunbeds located around it. You can also play ping pong or billiards (for a fee).

Water sports equipment can be rented on the beach for a fee. The bulk of entertainment is concentrated outside the Best Alanya Hotel 3 *. You can book a tour at the reception or, having rented a car, see the sights on your own.

Beach





It is located two hundred meters from the hotel. You can reach it by crossing the road. Underpass is not provided. On the municipal beach, sunbeds and umbrellas are available for a fee. Entrance into the water is convenient, but the presence of special shoes is desirable.

Impressions

First of all, the Russians note a good location of the hotel and its proximity to the sea. So, the distance to the beach is only about three hundred meters. According to many, another plus is that Best Alanya Hotel 3 * is built at some distance from the center of Alanya. This is especially convenient for family tourists, who are disturbed by the noise of putting their children to bed. In this hotel, it is always quiet. Those who live an active nightlife can spend time in nightclubs on the waterfront.



As for the rooms of Best Alanya Hotel 3, reviews of tourists indicate that they, although small, are always clean. They are furnished only with the most necessary furniture.

There are different opinions regarding the food in the hotel . Some find food acceptable, while others find that there is no variety. However, their opponents respond that it makes no sense to expect a sumptuous buffet with gourmet dishes from a budget hotel. There are no complaints about the staff. The only negative is that employees do not understand the Russian language, and English is difficult for them to give.
