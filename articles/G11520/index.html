Extract from the Unified State Register of Land on the land: where and how to get

Many citizens are thinking about how to get an extract from the Unified State Register of Land for a land plot or any other property. This document plays an important role for most transactions. What kind of paper is this? Why is it needed? Where can I get a statement of the established form?

Description

What kind of document are you talking about? What does a sample of extract from the Unified State Register look like on a land plot?



We are dealing with a document describing the property. This means that we are talking about paper, which contains information about the characteristics of the land (in our case) and about its owners.

The extract looks like a form of the established sample printed on sheet A4. It, as already mentioned, contains data about the object.

Types

It is important to understand that statements are different. Depending on the type of document, its value will change.

Today you can come across the following types of securities:

  • on property rights;
  • incapacity;
  • with general characteristics of the property;
  • extended type;
  • with data on encumbrances, arrests, restrictions.

In fact, there are a lot of references. But most often, citizens need an extended type of discharge. Maximum - with the characteristics of the object.



About extended statements

It is on them that we will stop. An expanded extract from the Unified State Register of Land for a land plot can be issued only by the owner of the property. Or a citizen with a power of attorney. Third parties cannot request such a certificate, while strangers are able to order ordinary statements.

What is in extended help type? Usually this document not only gives information about the land, but also shows the full history of the transfer of property rights from person to person. Accordingly, a citizen will be able to find out exactly who and when disposed of the land.

Registration Authorities

Where can I order an extract from the Unified State Register of Land for a land plot? Answer definitely will not work. After all, this service is provided by different authorities. And citizens can choose their own place to seek help.

So, statements of the established form are issued in the following organizations:

  • MFC;
  • cadastral chambers;
  • Rosreestr;
  • single window services.

But that is not all. In Russia, the mentioned certificates are issued online. To do this, you must resort to the help of some sites. These include the website of the State Register of the Russian Federation and "State Services".



Some owners say that extracts from the Unified State Register on land and other real estate can be ordered on third-party resources. This is true, but it is not recommended to use them. Indeed, among such sites is full of deception and fraud.

About the timing

Obtaining an extract from the Unified State Register is easier than it seems. How quickly do they issue relevant certificates?

In electronic form, they are made from several hours to three days. The same amount of time should be expected if a citizen decided to apply to the Rosreestr with a request. If you want to receive a certificate through the MFC, then the waiting period for a document is increased to 5 days.

Personal receipt

To obtain an extract from the Unified State Register, the owner will have to follow some algorithm of actions. What is it about?

A guide to obtaining a certificate from the state register of rights looks like this:

  1. Form a package of documents. They will be described below.
  2. Write a request for a certificate.
  3. Pay the fee.
  4. Submit an application to the selected authority.
  5. Pick up a ready certificate at the appointed time.

As practice shows, there is nothing incomprehensible or difficult in this. And everyone can order an extract from the Unified State Register on the land plot. Especially if you mean paper help and personal receipt.

"Public services" to help

The next trick is the use of government services. On this service you will have to register in advance. Otherwise, getting a service will not work.



As soon as the owner has a verified and confirmed profile, he will need:

  1. Open gosuslugi.ru.
  2. Log in to your account.
  3. Go to "Public Services".
  4. Write in the search bar "Extract from the Unified State Register". Search for data.
  5. Select the appropriate item in the search results.
  6. Click on the "Get a Service" button.
  7. Indicate the type of object "Land".
  8. Fill out the request. This can be done with the help of tips.
  9. Choose a payment method for the service. To do this, you usually need to click on the bank icon and enter the data of bank plastic. Or provide information about the electronic wallet.
  10. Confirm action.
  11. Indicate the form for extracting the statement.
  12. If necessary, fill out information about the authority in which you want to pick up the finished paper.

That's all. Now you just have to wait for the alert on readiness. If the user ordered an electronic extract from the Unified State Register of Land for the land, the file will be sent by e-mail. It will also be duplicated in the "My Account" on the "State Services".

State registry site

Almost the same way the order of the certificate through the website of Rosreestr. The only difference is that in this case there is no need to register. In addition, the document is paid a little differently.

Otherwise, the algorithm of actions will be similar. Difficulties with ordering help usually do not arise. After all, when filling out a request, a citizen will be able to use the tips.

Cost

How much will the extract from the Unified State Register on land cost ? As we have said, it all depends on the type of document and where it is drawn up.

So, an electronic certificate will cost 150 rubles, paper - 200-300 rubles. An extended statement will cost from 1,500 to 2,500 rubles.

Some sources indicate that an extract costs individuals 450 rubles, and an electronic counterpart costs 200 rubles. Organizations pay more for the mentioned certificate - 600 and 300 rubles, respectively. More accurate information can be obtained by submitting a request to the registration authority.

About Documents

Extracts from the Unified State Register from individuals require the presence of a citizen:

  • passports
  • statements;
  • receipts of payment of duty.

For an extended statement, the citizen will have to present title documents on the property. And for organizations - constituent documents and a written power of attorney addressed to the one who submits the request.
