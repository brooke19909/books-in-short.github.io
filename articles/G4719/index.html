Food guests call sufficient, but not designed for the capricious eater. The fact that guests do not finish their meal at lunch will be served for dinner, the authors of the reviews share. It is better to forget about meat dishes during your stay at Konar & Doruk for those vacationers who eat exclusively at the hotel. Many praise local coffee and jams. Guests call the kitchen designed for the average eater. They cook dishes that are not too fatty, and not too sharp. Food at the hotel, the authors of reviews write, is still more suitable for vegetarians who feel full and satisfied, eating greens, fresh cheese, tomatoes, vegetables. Nevertheless, hotel guests believe that the institution received its stardom (3 *) precisely because of the more or less tolerable organization of food.

Vacationers amend the hotel information offered in advertising booklets, as well as in some reviews: from the hotel to the sea you should go not 250 m, but all 400, but this road does not cause any difficulties. Nevertheless, the Konar & Doruk hotel is fully consistent with its 3 * category, vacationers say.
