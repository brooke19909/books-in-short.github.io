Bosch products: hob. Model selection, description, characteristics

Currently, in stores you can see a large number of various household appliances. Some devices are designed to greatly facilitate everyday work. However, there are those without which it is simply impossible to do. The hob belongs to the latter. Bosch is a trademark that has a great reputation for a long time. Its products can hardly be called cheap, but the quality and functionality of the devices justify the set price.

Let's see why the hobs are so necessary? Without this technique, you can simply stay hungry. And according to all nutritionists and doctors of the world, without a wholesome and healthy food a person will not be able to exist normally. Of course, many (especially bachelors) will say that in grocery stores you can buy a variety of convenience foods and heat them in the microwave. But what benefit will the body receive from such a diet? In order to avoid such consequences, each person should eat first meals daily, meat products, fish, cereals and vegetables. Therefore, if you ask the housewives what item in the kitchen is most necessary, the answer will be unequivocal - a stove or hob.



Manufacturer's choice

In the modern market for household appliances, you can see many models of built-in hobs, which are made by different manufacturers. They, of course, have numerous differences both in the external design and in the technical characteristics. But among many brands, the German Bosch brand is the most popular among consumers. The cooking surface of this manufacturer is characterized by a long service life, high-quality assembly, high-class materials, a wide range.

Types of hobs

Bosch offers its customers three types of hob: gas, electric and combination. The latter can operate both on the basis of blue fuel and switch to power supply. The Bosch gas hob is designed with four burners and rotary controls. Devices of this type of power supply with touch switches are not manufactured. Modern attractive appearance and the principle of work clear to everyone are their significant advantages.

Currently, an electric induction panel is especially popular. It works thanks to a high-frequency magnetic field in which induced eddy currents are generated . The number of rings can vary from 2 to 4. The control panel is touch-sensitive. When choosing just such a panel, it is very important to check the wiring power in the house.



Varieties of material

Different materials for the production of this type of equipment are used by the German manufacturer Bosch. The hob due to this is represented by a wide range of models. Let's look at what materials are used to make them.

The most common are enamelled appliances. Such models are made in white, black and brown, it is very rare to find a different shade. Cooktops made of stainless alloy are particularly popular with consumers. They are made in a neutral shade, so they can easily fit into the interior of any kitchen and make an excellent duet with a classic sink. The aluminum panel in its color practically does not differ from the stainless one, but has a lighter tone. The matte surface is comfortable - small scratches are almost invisible on it, fingerprints do not remain. Caring for such a device is much easier. Glass-ceramic surfaces at the price of the most expensive. It is this coating that the induction panel has. The appearance of the equipment is particularly beautiful and aesthetically pleasing. Basically, these models are black. Sometimes they are decorated with graceful patterns. You can also find white surfaces made of glass-ceramic, but much less often.

Bosch PRP 626 M 70 E





Model PRP 626 M 70 E (Bosch trademark) - gas hob. It has a classic design, made in black. Four burners. Coating - glass ceramics. Dimensions of the device: 61x53 cm. Cast-iron grate. Mechanical control by rotary switches. There is auto ignition and a gas control function.

Bosch PIF 651 R14E





Let's look at the PIF 651 R14E model of the famous Bosch brand. Combined hob. It has four rings: the rear ones are Hi-Light technology, the front ones are induction. The design of the panel has beveled edges, thanks to which one level is maintained with the countertop. Rated power - 6.8 kW. One burner is equipped with an expansion zone. The control panel is touch, there are 17 power levels. Additional functions: Power Boost, automatic shutdown, button lock, timer.

Bosch PKN 675 N14D

The electric hob Bosch PKN 675 N14D is available in black. It is equipped with four rings, among them there is a double-circuit and with an oval heating zone. All work on Hi-Light technology. Management is carried out using touch buttons. The device is equipped with 17 power levels. Dimensions: 57 x 51 cm.
