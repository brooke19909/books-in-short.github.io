What kind of service is this?

A call at the expense of the Tele2 interlocutor is a unique opportunity to reach a relative or close person without having money on your mobile account. But do not forget the main feature of this service, which consists in the fact that the subscriber to whom you dial up takes the payment. And when the call itself is made, the user receives a notification of a paid incoming call. He may agree or refuse this, therefore the fact of the occurrence of fraud is completely excluded. So, we turn to the information about the procedure for using this service.

How to use it?

  2. Dial the command: * 140 * number of the called party, click on the call.
  3. The user receives a message about a paid call.
  4. If you agree, then a connection will occur.
  5. If he refuses, the call will be dropped.

Carefully follow all these steps, try not to make mistakes and clearly follow the recommendations.
