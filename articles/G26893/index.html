Beach and water recreation for tourists

Of course, many travelers are primarily concerned about the quality of a beach holiday the Limak Limra Hotel 5 * hotel complex can offer. Kemer is a city that is designed specifically for a fun holiday by the sea. The hotel owns its own 300-meter stretch of beach, which can be reached immediately from the courtyard.

The beach is pebbled and cleaned here every day. The entrance to the sea is convenient, but you should still get beach slippers. There is a shallow area for bathing children. The water is clean and warm.



On the shore there are large comfortable sunbeds with protection from sunlight. Mattresses are available free of charge from the hotel. Here, every day you can get fresh beach towels. From morning till night there is a beach bar, where guests are offered chilled drinks and light snacks.

Fans of more active recreation at the Limak Hotel Limra Hotel Resort 5 * will also not be bored. Completely free, you can ride a boat or catamaran. Also, windsurfing enthusiasts regularly come here - beginners in this sport, by the way, can get some professional lessons, rent a board or suit. Guests wishing to get a more thrilling experience are invited to ride a banana, water ski, do parasailing or make a short boat trip. These services are paid separately, but their cost is quite reasonable.
