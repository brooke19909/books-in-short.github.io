Tips for making textile dolls

In the process of manufacturing a textile toy, any trifle matters, starting with the pattern and development of the image and ending with the choice of materials.

The first step in working on a new toy is developing an image. It is the careful design and sketching of the sketch that distinguishes the process of making Konnet dolls. A master class with a detailed description can be bought only from the master, but some secrets are revealed on her page on the Web.





After the image has been invented (it can be a mischievous girl, a bunny, a mouse - yes anyone!), You need to draw a pattern or use one of the suggested by experienced craftsmen and transfer it to the fabric. Then follow some technical, absolutely not creative stages of the birth of a masterpiece:

  • The traced pattern on the fabric must be sewn along the contour without cutting (leaving unplaced places for turning parts).
  • Carefully cut out already stitched details (you can use scissors with a raised edge for this, or you can make notches around the edge after cutting so that the parts turn out well).
  • Fill all parts with filler (sintepuh or holofayberom). To do this, you can use a special wand, or you can purchase a Billroth straight clamp surgical instrument, which is very convenient for stuffing doll parts.
  • All parts must be sewn together with a special long needle and a strong thread.

Doll image

After the doll's body is ready, it needs to be dressed. If the clothes are not removable, then the sleeves and trousers can be put on the arms and legs before being sewn, and all other parts of the clothes are sewn to the body of the doll with an invisible seam. If the clothes can be removed, then they are sewn according to special patterns, and only then they are put on a doll.

Sometimes, at the stage of developing a sketch for a doll, its final image is already ready, but it also happens that it turns out to be completely different from what it was meant to be, and in the process of sewing clothes some details are added or removed.
