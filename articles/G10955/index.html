What to do if OSAGO is urgently needed

CTP - compulsory car insurance. This policy must be purchased by each vehicle owner. Of course, if the contract is urgently needed and there is no desire to argue with a representative of the insurance company, then you can agree to the terms of the company and buy all the additional services.

If you do not want to overpay, then you can use the following algorithm. These instructions will help you buy only the policy of compulsory insurance.

Buying a policy without additional services

The first step is to fill out an insurance application. The application form can be obtained at any office of the insurance company or downloaded from the World Wide Web. The application must reliably fill in all the fields.



After the application is completed, you need to find out the cost of your contract. To do this, you can use the online calculator, which is presented on the official website of each insurance company. To find out how much car insurance costs, you will need to indicate: type and power of the vehicle, registration of the owner and drivers who will be allowed to drive. After entering all the necessary information, the calculation will be generated automatically.

Application Documents

Copies of documents must be attached to the application: passport of the owner and policyholder, TCP and driver's license. If the vehicle is older than three years, a diagnostic card will be required.

The entire package of documents should be given to the representative of the insurance company. In this case, you must ask to register the documents and give the second copy of the application with a mark of acceptance.



According to the law, after the adoption of the application, the company is obliged to draw up an agreement within 30 days. In this case, the insurer may ask to provide the car for visual inspection (in pure form). Be sure that after these steps you will be sold car insurance without life insurance and other additional services.

Additional insurance

When issuing a compulsory MTPL insurance policy, all insurance companies are literally forced to purchase a life insurance policy. If you think carefully, this is a very profitable type of insurance for a minimal fee. Thanks to this policy, you can count on decent compensation in the event of an insured event.

On average, 8-13 thousand rubles are car insurance. The cost of additional health insurance is only 1-2 thousand rubles. Tricks of insurance companies

Many insurance companies, to take out insurance with additional services, go for little tricks. One of them is the refusal of instant clearance. Customers who do not want to overpay should provide a car for visual inspection and sign up in the queue for registration.



This need can be explained by the fact that there is a car insurance department with life insurance only, in which everything is executed in the order of priority, and there is a CTP insurance only department, which draws up exclusively by appointment.

Some companies immediately voice to the client that registration will only be with additional services. If the client refuses, the representative of the insurance company settles and announces that the discounts are not checked on a single PCA basis, and therefore it will not be possible to draw up an agreement.

It turns out that the CTP policy is not so simple. Car insurance is a legal requirement that forces many drivers to go through a complicated registration process.

Proper insurance



It turns out that it is possible, but difficult, to issue an insurance policy without additional services. To save money, it is necessary to engage in insurance in advance, a month before the end of the insurance period.

To do this, send the application and the necessary package of documents to the insurance company and wait until you are invited to insurance and vehicle inspection.

Car insurance without life insurance will be issued, and you can move on your vehicle to the insured.

If possible, it is better to use online insurance and buy an electronic form of compulsory insurance. This significantly saves not only time, but also money.
