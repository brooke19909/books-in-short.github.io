La Grace Resort 3 * / 4 *, Benaulim, India: hotel description, reviews

La Grace Resort is a popular Indian hotel located in the famous resort town called Benaulim. Many people come here to relax, sunbathe, enjoy the beautiful beaches and the sea. Why is this hotel so attractive and why should you choose it for your vacation? Worth telling.





Briefly about the hotel

La Grace Resort is located in a very convenient place for tourists. Firstly, next to the airport - to go to it for a maximum of half an hour, or even 20 minutes. Nevertheless, the distance is more than 20 kilometers, so that planes flying by regularly will not annoy travelers with their noise.

What about the sea? It is also nearby. Just over 500 meters you need to walk to get to Benaulima beach. Many do not like that this is not the first coastline, but the second. But what to do, anyway, 500-600 meters - this is not two or three thousand kilometers, so this nuance can be endured. In addition, this is still considered a plus (at least among the locals). It is pleasant to live in a place located on the 2nd coastline, since you do not have to constantly encounter a crowd of other tourists.

There are also local attractions nearby. One of the most important is the Old Church, it is 25 km from the hotel. By the way, the railway station is also located nearby - only three kilometers you need to drive to it.

From the season or from whether the hotel has established any discounts and super offers. But if you get to just such a good time (and the hotel arranges discounts regularly), then you can save a large amount.

Leisure activities

All popular hotels are known for offering not only comfortable rooms, but also various ways to spend their free time to good use. On the territory of the hotel La Grace Resort 4 * is an outdoor swimming pool. The hotel itself has a good fitness center with various exercise equipment. This place is actively visited by those guests who cannot imagine their life without physical exertion.

Fans of relaxation with both body and soul will definitely enjoy the wellness center located here in the hotel. Reviews recommend a spa, where you can please yourself with various treatments.

Even at the hotel they decided to focus on the popular pastime. Cycling! Many people cannot imagine life without it. And if this is true, then the hotel can rent a bike. As you can see, the administration of the La Grace Resort 4 * hotel foresees wishes. In addition, the local surroundings are amazing, and it is much more pleasant to inspect them, riding a bicycle, with the breeze, and not in public transport.





Nutrition

Many people, going on vacation to this or that hotel, learn in advance about how the issue of nutrition is organized there. Well, in the case of La Grace Resort (Goa), you can be absolutely calm about this topic. On the territory of the complex there are two pleasant establishments where you can eat deliciously. The first is a restaurant called Imperial Venus. It serves fresh and original dishes related to international cuisine. The restaurant room is designed for 56 guests. The institution opens at 12:30 for two hours (that is, until half past two) and then at 19:30, after which it works until 11 pm.

And the second institution is a cafe called Sun Dial. It can fit 48 people. It works from seven to half past ten (as you might guess, breakfast is served there). Then the cafe is open from 12:30 to 14:30 and in the evening according to the same schedule as the restaurant of La Grace Resort.

The reviews of tourists who visited these establishments are inspiring. People claim that they do not spare portions in the restaurant and cafe, and the food is varied, original and tasty every day. Service

Hotel La Grace Resort Benaulim boasts not only good rooms and a restaurant, but also a level of service. The registration desk here works around the clock, so if any unforeseen circumstances happen, you can call in or check out regardless of the time established by the rules (it is only advisable to notify the staff in advance).

The hotel has a currency exchange, as well as a laundry room, which anyone can use. The rooms are cleaned every day, and very high quality, as evidenced by reviews. If the tourist wishes, food with drinks can be delivered to the room, as well as a fresh newspaper. Non-smelling people can provide a non-smoking room.

And finally. In addition to renting bicycles (as described above), car rental can also be offered here. In general, the staff is very pleasant and polite, which definitely can not but rejoice.





Additional Information

And finally, a few more important nuances that you should know when setting out to relax at the La Grace Resort 4 * hotel (south of Goa). Firstly, the hotel can still offer a transfer from the airport, which is a very useful condition for a non-native person. True, it may be paid.

If guests come to the hotel with a small child who is two years old or less, then you do not need to pay for it. An extra bed can be provided if agreed in advance. And with existing beds, accommodation is absolutely free. Also, only pre-registered guests can be accommodated in the rooms, so that when booking, you must specify in full - each tourist.

And reviews are advised to reserve apartments long before the start of the vacation. Since the hotel is quite famous and the available rooms can be simply sold out, if you catch your mind a week before the trip.
