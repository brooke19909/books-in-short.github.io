Smart device

A slow cooker is a multifunctional kitchen appliance. This device is equipped with programmed control, designed for cooking a variety of dishes. In this case, the device operates in automatic mode. Simply put, when the hostess is resting, he cooks.





The complete set of a modern multicooker includes a main bowl (pan), an electronic apparatus, a steaming tank, as well as some additional accessories. Among them is a flat and special tablespoon.

How to use a slow cooker? Very simple! In principle, it is necessary to open the lid and put the products included in the dish into the bowl. Having established the necessary cooking mode, you can go do your own thing. The smart device will do the rest for you. Its main modes are the following: "Steaming", "Baking", "Milk porridge", "Pilaf", "Buckwheat", as well as "Stewing".

What can a multicooker do?

The modes that this appliance is equipped with make it possible for any housewife to cook a lot of delicious and varied dishes. At the same time, you won‚Äôt have to spend a lot of time on their creation.

How to use a slow cooker with baking function? If such a device appeared in your house, then this means that there was a great opportunity to easily cook home-made fragrant bread, wonderful cottage cheese casseroles, cakes, etc.

For example, you decide to please your loved ones with chocolate muffins. How to use a slow cooker at the same time? The appliance bowl should be greased with oil and then sprinkled with flour. The dough, which includes two eggs beaten with a glass of sugar, two hundred and fifty grams of sour cream, one glass of flour and two tablespoons of cocoa, is laid on the prepared surface of the multicooker bowl. After that, you should turn on the "Baking" mode for one hour. After the device is finished, the cupcake is removed from the bowl and decorated with cream or chocolate icing.

Kids also love the wonderful semolina porridge that caring mothers cook with the help of a "smart" appliance. The recipe is simple. One serving of such porridge should be prepared from a glass of milk, eight tablespoons of semolina, two hundred milliliters of water and four tablespoons of sugar. You might want to change the temperature or time of the modes offered by the manufacturer. In this case, you can use the "Multi-cook" function and configure the necessary parameters. The main program of the device and their features

The simplest multicooker models can be programmed for six tasks. Conventionally, they are divided into two categories. The first of these includes programs that work fully automatically. The second category of tasks includes stewing, baking and cooking. Moreover, the cooking mode of such dishes differs only in time.

Using a slow cooker, it is worth bearing in mind one nuance. Cooking time when stewing or baking begins immediately after starting the device. In cooking mode, the timer counts down only after boiling water.

After buying a slow cooker, it should first be washed with water and wiped. First use should be preliminary.
