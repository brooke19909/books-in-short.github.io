Hotel "Green Park Yalta-Intourist": photos and reviews of tourists

Yalta ... This once-small seaside village in the nineteenth century turned into a popular resort. "Russian Riviera" is the tourist capital of the southern part of Crimea. The administrative center called Big Yalta includes several districts: Alupka, Foros and others.

Resort Information



The length of the city is seventy-two kilometers, and the total territory is nine hundred hectares. Half of them are built up, and the rest is planted with numerous trees and exotic shrubs. Yalta has seen many conquerors. She was in the possession of the Golden Horde, under the rule of Genoa, was under the yoke of the Ottoman Empire, was part of the USSR, then part of Ukraine. Today this resort is the territory of Russia.

Yalta, like the rest of the South Coast, experienced its economic recovery after, in 1838, simultaneously with the formation of the county, it was given the status of a city. Members of the royal family erected their summer residences here. Who has not heard of Livadia or Oreanda. Yalta gained fame as a fashionable resort, where the whole Russian aristocracy came. The air of the resort was declared healing: it was recommended by doctors for the treatment of bronchopulmonary diseases.

However, as a tourist center, Yalta began to develop only after the October Revolution. In 1921-1922 it was called the Red Army, but this name did not take root, and the city was returned to its former name.

Rest in Yalta

You can come to rest in this amazing resort in any season of the year. No one here will ever be bored. In winter, tourists can go to Mount Ai-Petri, where you can ski, sled, ride a snowmobile. And few people are upset that this season it is impossible to swim in the sea, since the vast majority of sanatoriums, boarding houses and hotels in Yalta are equipped with indoor pools with heated water coming from the sea.



The climate of the resort resembles a subtropical Mediterranean. For visitors, acclimatization is quite easy and fast. Winters in this region are very warm, especially for Russia, in addition, they are mild. In January-February, the average temperature ranges from four degrees of heat to five frosts.

It is cool in the spring, hot in the summer, the fall season is warm and long. The sun shines about two thousand three hundred hours a year. I must say that Nice and Cannes also have such indicators. The swimming season is quite long: it starts from the end of May and lasts almost one hundred and fifty days - until the end of October. Water on average warms up to twenty degrees.

Yalta Hotels

A lot of tourists come to the resort every year, especially Russians. Each of them finds a hotel or a hotel suitable for both price and comfort. Those vacationers who prefer a quiet and relaxing holiday, stop far from the noisy bars and restaurants in the upper Yalta or on its outskirts. Those that rest with the whole family, consider hotel complexes, which are built a lot in the resort, as the best option.

There are large hotels with a very developed infrastructure, and mini-pensions with a green courtyard, an indoor pool and a playground. For those who prefer beach vacations first of all, hotels located on the shore are suitable.

All hotels in Yalta, including Green Park Yalta-Intourist, comply with European standards, and although not even each of them has a star category, the conditions and services provided are very good.
