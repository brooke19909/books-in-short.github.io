How to stitch knitted parts: tips

It often happens that, having spent a lot of time on the manufacture of the model you like, the needlewoman is completely disappointed in the result. Although, it would seem, everything was done strictly, as indicated in the description, but the result for some reason absolutely does not coincide with expectations. The reason for this lies in the fact that the needlewoman does not know how to sew the knitted parts correctly. As a result, elements connected with violations completely degrade the appearance of the finished product.



First you need to understand that the elements of the product can only be stitched after they are completely dry after washing. To do this, you need to build a pattern of the manufactured product. You can use oilcloth for this. Then, after wetting the details, fix them, distributing them over the pattern. This will allow each element to give the desired shape in advance and ensure dimensional accuracy.

Then you need to choose a seam with which the details of one product will be connected together. It is important to remember that in this case, you can achieve different results. You can sew a decorative stitch or vice versa to make the seam become invisible. To do this, it is worth learning how to sew knitted parts in the first and second case.



First you need to get the necessary tools. If you do not know how to sew knitted products, then get a darn needle with a blunt end and a large eye. With its help, you can achieve amazing results. In this case, the formed seam will not spoil the pattern. Indeed, very often when using a regular needle, the thread is excessively tightened, and therefore the finished clothing loses its appearance. If desired, a sewing machine can also be used for these purposes. By stitching a knitted fabric, you can securely fasten the parts. The main thing is to moderately tighten in the process of connecting the elements.

If we talk about how to sew knitted parts manually, then first of all it is worth paying attention to the horizontal seam, which is often called ‚Äúloop to loop‚Äù. This is the best way to connect the elements that were created using the pattern of hosiery. In this case, open loops of two adjacent canvases are sewn together, thereby providing a durable seam. When creating it, you can make it a decorative element, if you select threads of a contrasting color. Also, this seam can connect parts connected in different directions. In this case, the loop of the horizontal web is connected to the edge loop of the vertical.



The side seam is a little different. If you do not know how to sew knitted details in this case, then it is worth noting that the work can be performed both on the front and on the wrong side. Both in the first and in the second case, edge loops will be captured.

Now, knowing how to sew correctly, you can finish your work without any problems by creating a product that will completely suit you in appearance. With high-quality performance, the finished jacket or dress will keep their shape well, without being deformed during prolonged wear.
