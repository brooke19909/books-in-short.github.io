Campsites of Kirov and the Kirov region

Many of us are already tired of spending all our weekends and holidays in concrete city walls. I want to break out into nature, visit the most beautiful places. But enough to succumb to advertising and dream about foreign tours, it's time to fall in love with the nature of their native places. Kirov camps are waiting for guests and are ready to provide the best vacation, which is no worse than Egyptian or Thai. Did you know that in the Kirov region there are about two hundred natural areas that are protected by the state. Reserves, zoological reserves, resort areas, tourist camps - all these objects will help to unite with nature, relax and enjoy life.





Campsites of Kirov and the Kirov region

All natural areas of the Kirov region are as far as possible from highways, large cities. This allows you to create ideal conditions for a relaxing holiday, enjoying nature. Kirov camps are located in the middle of mixed and coniferous northern forests. These light planets naturally allow all vacationers to improve their health. In the pine and spruce forests, the air is permeated with volatile, their healing properties simply work wonders, our body, weakened by modern ecology, is filled with oxygen.

Many recreation centers of the Kirov region are located in the forest, and this allows you to organize a real exciting hunting game for vacationers. Hunting tourism in these places is developing very intensively. Only here run - huge hunting grounds. The range of birds and animals allowed to be shot is very wide. In the Kirov region, shooting of ungulates such as moose, wild boar is permitted; as well as wild (bear, wolf). The hunt for fur animals is open : fox, raccoon, badger, otter, hare, muskrat, marten, ermine, beaver; waterfowl and pine forest: woodcock, capercaillie, duck, black grouse, and hazel grouse.

Fans of hunting at the Kirov camp sites feel very comfortable. The services of an experienced ranger are provided at many bases. Hunters are provided with escorts, dogs, various means of transportation - snowmobiles, motor boats, SUVs. These places began to be popular among tourists from Germany, Switzerland, Austria, Hungary, the Czech Republic and Turkey.





Geographical location of the Kirov region

The Kirov region took its place in the northeast of the Russian Plain. Its border passes with Udmurtia, Perm Territory, with the Arkhangelsk Region and the Republic of Komi, with the Nizhny Novgorod, Vologda, Kostroma Regions, with Tatarstan and the Republic of Mari El. The relief of the entire regional territory is hilly. The northeast covers the Verkhnekamsk Upland, the central part is Vyatka Uvaly, and the north is Northern Uvaly. The total length of the rivers of the region is 66.65 thousand km. In total there are 19 753 rivers and streams in the territory. Most of the rivers belong to the Vyatka river basin . The largest rivers here are Tansy, Luz, Moloma, Cheptsa, Cobra. The largest lakes of the region are Muserskoe, Oryol, Akshuben. The deepest lake is Lezhninskoe, its depth is 36 meters. Kirov camps located on the banks of rivers provide an opportunity for tourists not only to hunt, but also to fish. Most of the Kirov region is swampy. The largest swamps are Kaysinsky, Salamatinsky, Volmensky.

Climate

The climate of the region is temperate continental. The proximity of the Arctic Ocean allows cold winds to easily penetrate the territory. As a result - cold winters, possible frosts in the summer. The temperature minimum reaches -45 ... -50 degrees, the maximum +38 ... +40 degrees. The climate is quite humid, precipitation often falls, per year - 500-600 mm. Wind gusts (prevailing southwest, south) in summer are minimal, reach strength in winter, and are up to 30-40 m / s.





Winter holidays

Winter holidays in the Kirov region deserve special attention. It was at this time that favorable conditions were formed for active pastime. Kirov's camp sites in winter are very popular, and it is better to book rooms in advance.

For active winter recreation, many ski slopes, ice, snow slides, ice rinks, and recreational areas are open. Ski, skate, sled, and snow scooter rentals are organized everywhere. Winter lasts for five months. Very popular in the field of winter sports. In January, athletes gather in Kirov for diving from a 90-meter springboard.

Hunters from many countries come here for winter hunting. Hunting areas occupy 11 million hectares. Winter fishing also attracts lovers of this type of recreation. In Vyatka you can catch catfish, pike, asp, burbot, perch. Snowmobile racing, speedway and ice climbing competitions are held in the winter region.

If you decide to come to rest in the Kirov region, you can pre-choose a place for you, we present the most popular camp sites in the region.





Chalet "South" (Kirov)

Cozy and comfortable tourist base "South" is located on the banks of the Vyatka River, near the village of Borovitsa. Built in a picturesque place surrounded by a pine forest. Vacationers can enjoy the silence, the natural beauty of nature, clean air. The buildings are made according to European standards, with an increased level of comfort. A three-story cottage was built on the territory of the base, which can simultaneously accommodate up to 40 people. All rooms are well furnished, each with its own design style. The cottage has a dance hall, kitchen, fireplace room, Finnish sauna, rooms of different color shades. Guest houses are also built on the territory of the base, each of them can accommodate 12 people.

At the camp site you can have a great time both alone, enjoying peace and quiet, and relax with a fun company. In summer, you can relax on the sandy shore of Vyatka, swim in the clean river. Rent rubber boats, catamarans, bicycles. In winter - skiing, snowmobiling, sledding. Everywhere there are sports fields for outdoor activities.

"Swan"

Lebed Chalet (Kirov) is located in the village of Skozyryatka, which is 45 km from the city. The area is surrounded by birch grove and pine forest, which enriches the surrounding air with pure oxygen. Vyatka flows near the camp site, where you can relax, swim and go fishing. At any time, the recreation center offers comfortable accommodation and relaxation for both adults and children.





Cottages are built from environmentally friendly raw materials. The hostel offers three types of houses, everyone can choose their own accommodation at will and upon request. In addition to holidays, tourists are offered various promotions and discounts.

The following entertainments are available to vacationers: Russian bath; barbecue grill; a golf club with a golf course; playground; billiards; table games; laser tag game; outdoor playground with an inflatable pool, swings, slides; bicycles for rent; picnic arbors; tent rental; fishing; walks on water; sand beach; bathing. In the cafe you can order any celebration. A parking lot is open for guests.

Poroshino

Poroshino Chalet (Kirov) is located in a unique forest area, where many cross and ski tracks are equipped. Nearby is the village of the same name Poroshino. Guests can choose a room in the hotel or in a separate cottage. In summer, a camp for children opens here.

On holidays, fun events are organized for the New Year, Shrovetide, March 8. Competitions, corporate parties, children's parties and even weddings are held.

On the territory of the base there is an equestrian club where the instructor will teach you how to ride a horse. Horseback riding, excursions, hiking are carried out.





The bath complex consists of a beach area with an outdoor pool. There is a playground nearby. A separate bath has its own relaxation room.

Pavilions are located in the forest part, equipped with barbecue facilities, can accommodate many guests.

From outdoor activities - rental of winter and summer sports equipment.

Yaho

The hostel "Yaho" (Kirov) has its own equestrian club. It offers an active, entertaining vacation for families, children, adult groups. The base is located in a pine forest, near the Matantsi railway station. The equestrian club organizes one-day programs based on communication with animals. Horseback riding training is provided and then horseback riding.

You can stay in one of the cottages, each of which has its own name and style: "Family", "Deck", "Tourist House", "Pyramid", "Izba".

For a day's rest, barbecue facilities are provided; sports grounds; bicycles for rent; steeply inclined crossing; bathhouse; horseback riding; horse rides; ski and snowmobile rental; paintball; organization of seminars, conferences, corporate parties, holidays; transfer.





"Pine Beach"

Pine Coast Chalet (Kirov) is located on the Bystritsa River, which is 30 km from the city of Kirov. Vacationers can stay in comfortable rooms of detached large and small cottages. For guests fishing is organized, all facilities for outdoor activities are rented: bicycles, boats, catamarans, in winter skiing and sledding, ice drifts. Employees of the camp site at your request organize corporate events, various conferences, family holidays.

"Forest Tale"

The cottage "Forest Fairy Tale" (Kirov) is located on the Nikulinka River. Surrounded by her pine forest. Environmentally friendly terrain makes it possible to enjoy pure oxygen and relax from the bustle of the city. The territory of the base is closed, 4 cottages for guests are built here, you can rent them with your family or a small company. The restaurant complex offers a varied, delicious cuisine. Several banquet rooms allow you to arrange any celebration here - a wedding, anniversary, corporate party. A Russian bath will provide an opportunity to relax, relax, sit in a relaxation room with a group of friends.
