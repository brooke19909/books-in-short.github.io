How can a client receive a tax deduction of the first type?

It is worth recalling that choosing such an account is beneficial for people with official confirmed income. To obtain a tax deduction on contributions, you need to contact the inspection with the following documents:

  • Submission of a tax return in the form of 3-personal income tax. It must be submitted at the end of the year (i.e. the tax period) until the thirtieth of April of the following year.
  • Papers that officially confirm the income of the client from whom the thirteen percent tax was regularly paid during the year (most often this is a standard certificate of employment).
  • Documents that confirm the contribution to the IIS.
  • Submission of a standard form statement on the refund of the deduction for the amount of the contribution. This statement requires you to indicate your bank account details in order to receive a tax refund.

How can a client receive a tax deduction of the second type?

In order to be exempt from taxation, it is necessary for the broker to provide a certificate from the inspection that the client did not receive any deductions for his contributions throughout the entire period. In such a situation, the broker will not be charged the required thirteen percent of income tax on profits. In the event that the IIA was closed before the deadlines, then thirteen percent of taxes will be deducted from the closing profit.
