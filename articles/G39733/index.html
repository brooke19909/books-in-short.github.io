Caring for the washing machine





The Siemens WS10G160OE washing machine does not require special efforts and costs. Repair of a household appliance should be carried out in a service center, the only way to avoid unpleasant moments during washing.

Immediately after the washing process, unplug the plug from the outlet. Do not use solvents, flushing gasoline, or other chemicals that could damage the machine parts to care for the machine.

Residues of powder and other detergents should be removed. The case must be cleaned with a damp cloth. Do not direct water into the control panel. In this case, it may fail. The drum must not be cleaned with chlorinated products. To clean it, abandon steel nets.

If the dosage of detergents has been observed, then, as a rule, scale does not form on the PETN. If the scum on the PETN has a place to be, then it must be removed by special means, in accordance with the instructions.

To clean the cuvette from the remnants of detergents, it is removed from the compartment, washed with water using a stiff brush, wiped or dried. After the above manipulations, the cuvette is returned to its place. And left open so that the remaining moisture can dry to the end.

From time to time, the washing solution pump may become clogged. Before you clean it, you need to wait until it cools down, because during washing it heats up very much. Need to close the water tap. In this case, no water will be supplied. The device is disconnected from the power supply. Open the cap of the base, which is located below and unscrew the cap, which closes the washing pump. Soapy water, in this case, will begin to drain. The pump is cleaned inside, the covers are returned to their place.

In case of more serious damage to the device, contact the service center.
