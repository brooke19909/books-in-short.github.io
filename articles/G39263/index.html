Severka River - a place for a great vacation

According to legend, the muddy water in the river Severka because, going on a campaign against Mamaia, Moscow Prince Dmitry Ivanovich ate a handful of lighted land. This was done by him to prove his love for the homeland. Spit from the steep bank with black saliva into the water, and since then the water in the river is muddy and gray. And along the coast grow old willows, bending their branches to the water itself.

River description

The Severka River flows in the southeast of Moscow and the Moscow Region - this is the right tributary of Moscow. She takes the source from the village of Stepygino, located in the Domodedovo district. It flows through the fertile Russian land for 98 kilometers and is mastered by the whole person. The Severka River (Moscow Region) has a small number of forests on its banks.





The size and name of the river

The river has a fairly fast flow, and the width is not very large, about 3-4 meters. But the bed is meandering. Some of its areas are completely overgrown with willow, while others are shallow, for example, near the village of Lipkino. In summer, due to a lack of water level in the reservoir, there are places overgrown with reeds. Near the village of Mescherno a dam is installed. The river got its name from the temperature of the water: in any weather, it is cool. Not cold, but refreshing and invigorating in the summer heat.





Severka River: swimming and relaxation

Someone prefers to relax abroad, someone at Russian camp sites. And there are lovers of relaxation "savages" with tents. It is such people who should recover to rest in the North.

Along the coast there are many places that are not bought by camp sites, and therefore are not equipped and wild. On the shore, parking lots of previous travelers often come across, some try to leave something useful after themselves: for example, prepare a descent for entering the water or hang a bungee on a tree. And the places on which it is better to put up a tent are visible to the naked eye.

The pristine nature, the silence and the expanse of the river - all this calms and tunes to an optimistic mood. Great places to walk, sunbathe and take pictures, and in winter, sorting through photographs, again recall pleasant moments.





Thoughtful relaxation on the river

Severka is quite equipped, along the coastline you can find many recreation centers. Each of them is different from the other. Consider the most remarkable of them.

Base "Severka"

The location of the recreation center is the village of Nikonovskoye, through which the river Severka flows. Families with children come here to relax or to hold small events. On the basis of 10 new houses: four one-story, five two-story and one three-story. There is also a bathhouse. Each house has its own name and is different from the others. They include kitchens, so vacationers cook their own meals. Each house has a gazebo with barbecue, which is barbecue, and some here on warm summer evenings drink tea and talk.

Base "Severka" is able to accept a large, up to 100 people, the number of guests. In summer, the organizers of the base offer their guests pay attention to fishing, go boating, ride bicycles or segways or visit the beach on the river. Severka has a public beach, and it is located in the village of Nikonovskoye. It is sandy, so children can comfortably sunbathe and swim on it. In winter, there is also something to do: go ice-skating, go skiing in the woods, ride a snowmobile or tubing.

A food platform has been allocated on the territory of the base, where locals sell agricultural products.

The proximity of the base from the city of Moscow (only 55 kilometers) allows you to reach it in two hours by public transport.

The number of rooms in the base consists of 12 two-story buildings, in which single, double and triple rooms are located. Facilities, and this is a toilet and shower, are located on the floor. Guests are hospitably fed three times a day, this is included in the price.

On the territory adjacent to the base there is a place for gazebos where kebabs are fried. Barbecue, skewers are rented at an additional cost.

The house of creativity houses a library and a reading room. Here they play board and computer games.





Rafting on the Severka River

Travel club "Transition" carries out rafting on the river. The Severka River is ideally suited for this: numerous rapids, ruined dams, rapid flow along a hilly plain. There are many places on the shore where you fish.

The rafting lasts two days, this is a great way to recharge your batteries from Mother Nature.

During the rafting, participants admire the grandeur of the river and the virgin landscapes under the twitter of birds and the splash of water. At the end of a difficult day, tourists will find pleasant muscle fatigue, songs with the guitar, communication with other participants of the trip, dinner cooked on the fire.

Anyone can go on a trip along the Severka River; age and inexperience are not a hindrance. You can always try yourself for the first time in such a thing as rafting on the river. Severka for this is an ideal option. It is inexpensive, interesting, safe, and most importantly, does not require special physical preparation.

In the club "Transition" the necessary camping equipment is rented. Accommodation, meals and local transfers are included in the price of the rafting. The organizers take care of the issues that arise in preparation for launching. A traveler is required to learn how to kayak and be in a good mood.

Kayaking is safe: the features of its construction and lightness add stability to the water. It is proposed to raft on triple inflatable models "Khatanga" and "Viking". The kayak equipment and personal belongings can be easily immersed on a kayak. And now a small boat goes on a journey. You can take photos or videos from the boat if you wish.

10-25 people are sent to the rafting, the minimum age of the trip participants is 3 years. Children from 3 to 10 years old get a 10% discount. An adult heading for a two-day rafting in Severka will cost 4 thousand rubles.

The price of alloy includes:

1. Equipment rented (three- or four-person tents, bowlers, saw and ax, tent, first-aid kit).

2. Rented life jacket and kayak.

3. Meals along the route.

4. Hermome bag.

5. The work of instructors.

6. Committing, if necessary, local road transport.

Responses of rafting travelers are positive. Despite the sometimes unfortunate weather, blockages from fallen trees on the water or calluses on the hands of the oars, everyone came rested and satisfied. And most importantly, most want to repeat the alloy again.





Fishing

The river Severka is rich in fish, the fishermen proudly show their photos of the catch to friends. Therefore, they come here all year round from all around Moscow.

Meanwhile, the places where it bites are known only to local residents. Paid fishing is organized at recreation centers along coastlines. They also rent gear and a boat here. Spinning on the Severka River is carried out for pike, carp or perch. Ruffs, crucian carp, bream and roachworms also live there. But these fish are caught on other gear.

Fishing Reviews

According to fishermen, the place where fish is caught is the village of Golubino. This is a dam in which pike and crucian carp, bream and roach swim. Here, to fish, you need to pay a fee, but a symbolic one - 100 rubles. Given that this is the suburbs, here is one of the few places where fishing is profitable. Here, even a beginner can catch fish. Those who come to this place more often quickly learn the secrets of mastery. He catches fish more than others, and she is larger.
