Phuket Graceland Resort & Spa, Phuket: hotel description, reviews

Thailand is a country that every year becomes a resting place for thousands of tourists. After all, here you can find an unsurpassed combination of clean beaches, beautiful nature, interesting culture and affordable prices. Naturally, in recent years a lot of cozy hotels and luxurious, large hotel complexes have appeared here. The latter include the Phuket Graceland Resort Spa. Phuket (Phuket) - a place that is ideal for a fun holiday on the beach. So what is this hotel and what quality of accommodation can a traveler expect?

Description of infrastructure and hotel location





Naturally, every traveler is interested in the question of where the Phuket Graceland Resort Spa is located. Phuket is a picturesque resort town with many attractions and entertainment. It is here, almost on the seashore, that the hotel is located. It is a successful mix of modern architecture, an exotic garden and traditional Thai decorations. The hotel territory is spacious, there is a swimming pool, playgrounds for sports and recreation. The airport is about 30 km away.

Hotel Phuket Graceland Resort Spa 4 *: description of rooms and photos





The hotel is considered quite large, since there are 480 rooms on its territory:

  • 82 Superior Rooms (triple, area - 42 sq. M);
  • 258 Deluxe Rooms (triple);
  • 56 Deluxe Rooms Pool View (overlooking the pool);
  • 14 Grand Deluxe Rooms;
  • 47 Family Rooms (area - 62 sq. M);
  • 14 Graceland Suites (82 sq. M., Private jacuzzi and small sauna);
  • 2 One Bedrooms Family Suites;
  • 4 Two Bedrooms Family Suites.

Regardless of which room you are accommodated in, you can count on spacious and clean rooms with an interesting Thai design and modern equipment. For example, you will find a large-screen TV and a modern air conditioning system. Guests can use the telephone, safe, minibar, refrigerator. The bathroom has either a bathtub or shower. The suites even have their own jacuzzi. Residents are provided with a set of towels, hygiene products and soft bathrobes. There is also a hair dryer for drying hair.

Dining at the hotel





Paying a ticket to the hotel, tourists can choose a convenient meal plan for them. In most cases, guests pay for breakfast, although some choose a good meal. In the main restaurant, a buffet is organized three times a day. Breakfast is standard, consisting of toast, fresh buns, pancakes, cereals, fruits, etc. For lunch or dinner, you can have lunch in the same restaurant, which works on the menu, however, such dishes are paid separately. By the way, masterpieces of international and Thai cuisine are served here.

You can eat outside the Phuket Graceland Resort Spa. Phuket is a large city with a huge number of different cafes and restaurants, so there will be plenty to choose from. By the way, in the hotel and next to it there are several bars where you will certainly be offered delicious drinks and snacks.

Patong Beach and Water Vacation Travelers

Thailand is interested in tourists for various reasons, one of which is a mild climate and a calm sea. Approximately 300 meters from the hotel is Patong Beach - the famous public beach. By the way, the area near the hotel is not too crowded, so you can relax while enjoying relative peace.

On the shore for a small price you can rent a deck chair, umbrella, beach towel. There is also a bar where you can cool off with drinks from the refrigerator or even have a bite to eat. Naturally, tourists who prefer a more active pastime will not have to be bored either. You will have the opportunity to ride a boat, boat, water ski, try yourself in windsurfing, sailing, parasailing.

Is it possible to relax with a child?





Many parents are interested in the question of whether to take their child with them to Phuket. Graceland Resort Spa Phuket is a great place for a family vacation. Naturally, you can count on extra beds. In the courtyard there is a rather spacious pool where the guys can swim and play. A mini club is open every day. Here, children are engaged in funny, experienced animators who can brighten up the leisure of every kid. The hotel also has professional babysitters, for a small fee they will be happy to look after your child.

Does the hotel offer an additional service?

Immediately it is worth saying that Phuket Graceland Resort and Spa provides all the necessary conditions for a truly comfortable stay. For example, you can deposit especially valuable things for storage in the administrator's safe; this service costs a penny, but guarantees the preservation of values. Luggage storage is available.

The hotel constantly runs laundry and dry cleaning, which is convenient, especially if you plan to spend a lot of time here. Tourists enjoy free parking. It is easy to rent transport in the city. Even the hotel itself will help you with this. Here you can hire a driver.

Almost throughout the territory there is constant access to the World Wide Web, which is very important for modern travelers. There is a conference room where seminars, speeches, business meetings are held. By the way, the hotel organizes not only business events - it also organizes holidays and weddings.

Hotel reviews





Thailand is very popular among tourists from all over the world. For example, Phuket in December is a great place for a fun beach holiday, especially if you want to escape from the winter cold. So what do travelers say about this hotel? Is it worth it to stop? Of course, yes. This is the perfect place to spend an unforgettable vacation.
