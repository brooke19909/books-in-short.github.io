How to make a snowball out of cotton wool for the New Year?

The beauty of the falling snow creates a fabulous atmosphere. It can be moved into the house with a lump of snow. But he will melt at home. And at the same time, very often artificial snow is needed for decoration or for games . Therefore, the question arises of how to make a snowball out of cotton wool, the photo of which you see in our article. Is it so difficult, and what materials are needed to fulfill this beauty, which makes the celebration of the New Year so mysterious. You can create a holiday fairy tale with your own hands.

There are several ways to make a snowball out of cotton wool. It will be environmentally friendly and will not turn into a puddle in a few minutes. It can be added as individual parts of crafts, you can simply decorate it with a Christmas tree or a festive table.

First option

How to make a snowball out of cotton? First, prepare everything you need for crafts. We need: starch, a small saucepan, cotton wool, a teaspoon, water. So, in a small saucepan, pour two teaspoons of starch, pour a glass of cold water, mix. Then bring to a boil. Get a paste. It must be cooled so that you can work with your hands. Now we make balls of the required size from the cotton wool, dip them into the paste one at a time. Having taken out, we distribute glue evenly on all surface. We spread it on a tray and leave to dry, periodically turning over so that they do not crack or lose shape during drying. When the products are dry, they need to be slightly wrinkled to give a fluffy shape.

The second manufacturing option

How to make snowballs from cotton? We will use the previous description, only when preparing the paste you need to add sparkles to it. Then after drying, the snowballs will be like real ones. If they are put under a Christmas tree, they sparkle no worse than real ones, conveying all the beauty of New Year's toys.

New Year's product from cotton and varnish

How to make a snowball out of cotton wool using hairspray? First, we take a piece of cotton wool, drip PVA glue on it, roll it so that the glue is inside. Now, on a lump (at a distance of about thirty centimeters), you need to apply hair spray. It will fix the snowball and give it shine. You can also decorate products with glitter nail polish.

Another way to create

Still how to make snowballs from cotton? For these snowballs you will need: water, PVA glue, cotton wool. First, prepare the glue of the desired consistency. It is necessary to take one part of this ingredient and three parts of water, mix well. Everything, our staff is ready. We take the right amount of cotton wool and apply a thin layer of glue, evenly distributing it on the surface of the snowball. When it dries, add the next portion. So we continue until we reach the desired snowball size. If it is very small, then you can add cotton wool and evenly continue to apply glue. When the snowball has reached the desired size, it is necessary to place it in a warm place to dry. At the same time, you need to turn it so that it dries evenly. The result is a snowball. He, of course, will not melt in his hands.

With a newspaper

And for this snowball, in addition to cotton wool, you will need another piece of newspaper. Take a small piece of paper and crush it into a ball. Now it must be wrapped with cotton and sheathed with gauze. The result is a soft but durable snowball. Here's how to make a snowball out of cotton.

Snowfall

The snowfall can add fabulous New Year's Eve. So, we stock up on material: it is better to take synthetic wool, it is more fluffy. We make a New Year's miracle toy. It can be used for home decoration or as a decoration for a matinee in kindergarten. To make a great New Year's snowfall, we will prepare pieces of cotton wool and thread them with a needle. Now with the glue prepared in advance, grease the balls using a brush. Our New Year's garland is ready. You can safely hang it in the chosen place.





Little conclusion

Now you know how to make a snowball out of cotton. As you can see, everything is simple. It is a pleasure to work with the whole family on making snowballs. Materials for crafts are inexpensive, they are available to everyone. This means that this is a great opportunity to familiarize the child with work. And yet this simple way will help transform the room, instill in it the New Year spirit. We hope that you can create an artificial snowball at home.
