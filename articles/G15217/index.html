Favorite by many water park "Octopus" (Rostov)

"Octopus" (Rostov-on-Don) is the largest water park in the city, which is an open-air entertainment and water complex. Its territory is very large. It has 8 slides, 7 large pools, all kinds of attractions, cafes and a sauna.

It is difficult to imagine, but earlier on the site of this water park there was an ordinary dump.
