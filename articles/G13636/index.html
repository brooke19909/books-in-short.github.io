Hotel "Imera", Vityazevo, Anapa - overview, rooms, services and reviews of tourists

Guest house "Imera" is a large beautiful seven-story, but at the same time very cozy building located almost in the center of the village of Vityazevo. The hotel took its first vacationers in 2008. If you want to relax on the Black Sea coast, pay attention to the Imera Hotel. Vityazevo, the resort village in which the hotel is located, is located near the resort of Anapa. Here you will always be welcome, and the staff and hotel management will do everything to make guests feel as comfortable as possible.





Hotel location

Hotel Imera (Vityazevo) is an ideal place for relaxation and walks. It is located very close to the beach (only 4-5 minutes leisurely to go there). There is a convenient transport interchange near the guest house. The presence in the immediate vicinity of bus stops is especially valuable for those vacationers who want to diversify their stay in the Black Sea resort not only with trips to organized excursions, but also just take a walk around Anapa and its environs. The hotel is also close to the following entertainment centers:

  • dolphinarium;
  • bowling Club;
  • an amusement park located almost ten meters from the guest house;
  • Wellness sanatorium (walk from the hotel for 5-7 minutes with a calm step);
  • about 50 meters from the hotel there is a walking boulevard "Paralia" where cafes, restaurants and dining rooms with various cuisine for any wallet, the Byzantium amusement park and the Olympia water park are waiting for vacationers.

Also nearby, for the guests of the village there are many souvenir shops, teahouses and coffee shops where you can not only enjoy sitting, but also choose a souvenir for yourself. The hotel has a category of 3 stars.





Guest Accommodation

Imera, a hotel in Vityazevo, offers rooms in several categories. The hotel has 88 comfortable rooms.

  • Double room without a balcony. Room with all amenities with new furniture, soft beds, a combined bathroom (there is a shower).
  • Double room with a balcony. Room with all amenities, also new bedroom furniture, combined bathroom with shower.
  • 3-bed room without a balcony, soft beds, new furniture, combined bathroom (shower).
  • Triple room with a balcony. A comfortable room, all amenities, a bathroom is combined, there is a shower.
  • 4-bed room without a balcony with all amenities, there is a shower.
  • 4-bed room with a balcony, comfortable, all amenities.
  • 3-bed family room, comfortable conditions, new furniture, a bathroom is combined.
  • 2-room suite. There is a large double bed in the bedroom, upholstered furniture in the living room, there are dishes, an electric kettle. The combined bathroom has a shower and cosmetics.

All rooms are equipped with digital satellite TV, refrigerator, split system. There is hot and cold water around the clock.





"Imera" 3 *, Vityazevo. Prices

The cost of living at the Imera hotel varies depending on the time of year. So, in 2017, prices for rooms of various categories are set in the following limits:

  • A double room without a balcony costs from 1200 to 2400 rubles;
  • for a double room with a balcony, the fee will be from 1400 to 3500 rubles;
  • A triple room without a balcony will cost tourists from 1,500 to 3,400 rubles;
  • A triple room with a balcony will cost from 1600 to 3900 rubles;
  • price for a 4-bed room without a balcony - from 1700 to 3800 rubles;
  • the cost of a 4-bed room with a balcony - from 1900 to 4000 rubles;
  • 3-bed family room will cost you from 1600 to 4000 rubles;
  • for a 2-room suite the fee will be from 3000 to 5800 rubles.




Room reservation

To be more confident in their upcoming vacation, many tourists prefer to pre-book rooms. If you want to spend your holidays on the sea in Vityazevo (Anapa), Imera invites you to familiarize yourself with the booking conditions.

  • Room reservation is not charged.
  • To book a room, you need to pay 20% of the total cost of the stay at the hotel within five business days. Full payment is due upon arrival.
  • Upon check-in, a passport and a prepayment receipt must be presented. If you have children, then their birth certificates are presented.




Services included in the price

Hotel Imera (Vityazevo) has a fairly large list of services that are included in the price of the room.

  • This is a transfer from the airport and from Anapa Train Station.
  • Also a transfer to the airport and railway station.
  • Car parking is free.
  • Use of the pool is also included in the price.
  • Use of children's playground and room.
  • Animator services for both children and adults.
  • If necessary, call an ambulance.
  • Free WI-FI both in the rooms and throughout the hotel.
  • Use of a personal safe.
  • Wake up service.

Check-in time is 12.00, check-out time is 14.00.





Additional paid services for guests

In order for the guests to have the most complete and memorable stay, the Imera Hotel (Anapa, Vityazevo), in addition to the services included in the room price, offers a number of additional services.

  • A visit to the Finnish sauna or infrared sauna will cost you 800 rubles per hour.
  • Also, guests are offered a hammam.
  • The hotel also offers bike rental, which will help you reduce the time to get to the place you need in the city or village. In addition, you will get the opportunity to enjoy the beauties of the resort surroundings and attractions for longer. The rental price for one hour is 100 rubles.
  • A karaoke bar awaits lovers of singing in the evenings. Here you can not only demonstrate your talents to others, but also order alcohol and tasty snacks.
  • Late check out.
  • Extra bed for a child from 4 to 11 years old - 300 rubles per day.
  • Extra bed for a child from 12 years old - 500 rubles a day.

Catering

Meals are not included in the price. But on its territory there are catering facilities.

  • Firstly, it is a large dining room, which receives visitors from eight in the morning until ten in the evening. Imera Guest House offers tourists a wide selection of delicious home-cooked dishes.
  • Secondly, this is a barbecue cafe, where skilled chefs will prepare for you unforgettable meat dishes according to their special recipes. For delicious meat in the cafe you will be offered soft drinks. In the cafe, karaoke lovers can sing their favorite songs.
  • In summer, summer dining areas open on site.

Fun for kids

Hotel "Imera" (Vityazevo) pays a lot of attention to the rest of children. For young guests, the hotel has a modern colorful children's town, which consists of two parts.

  • The first is an open area with swings and slides.
  • The second is a children's room where children can enjoy exciting games and watch cartoons.

Designers have taken care of the safety of your children, providing playgrounds with a special safe coating. Animators also work for children, who prepare for them a lot of fun entertainment programs, competitions and contests. Also for children there is a compartment in the indoor pool, which starts working on April 15. The water in the pool is fresh, heated. The large outdoor freshwater pool, which starts operating on May 25, has two compartments for children.
