Rest in Svetlogorsk, Kaliningrad region: music of the sea

The city of Svetlogorsk, Kaliningrad Region, is located in a small bay on the shores of the Baltic Sea and has about 12 thousand inhabitants. In its district there are also the villages of Primorye, Donskoye and Otradnoye.

An excursion into history: Rauschen

The city was founded by the Knights of the Teutonic Order in the XIII century and was then called Rausche-Mother. Since the XVI century, he received the name Raushen, which means, according to various sources, ‚Äúdew‚Äù or ‚Äúnoise of the sea‚Äù.





In the 19th century, travelers from East Prussia and Central Europe began to visit Rauschen, and gradually he became fashionable among wealthy tourists. This has contributed to the rapid growth of the local population, as well as the construction of villas, hotels and entertainment venues. Since 1840, after a visit by the king of Prussia, the city gradually turned into a real resort.

Since 1900, Raushen was connected with the Konigsberg by the Zemland Railway, and in 1906 it was extended to the coastline (now Svetlogorsk-2). A cableway was also built to Georgianswalde (Otradnoye settlement). This significantly increased the number of people who want to relax and improve their health on the coast.

During the Third Reich, a sanatorium for the military was built in the city, in which the Nazi general rested. The water tower of his hospital has become a symbol of Rauschen.

Today's Svetlogorsk is a developing resort center with a large number of sanatoriums and hotel complexes, good infrastructure and excellent natural conditions.

Geography and climate

Svetlogorsk is built on high dunes (from 30 to 50 meters), and all descents to the sea are pretty steep. You can get to the beach using the cable car or elevator. There are quite comfortable stairs.

The city is planted with trees that amaze with its diversity - for a long time, sailors and guests of Raushen brought here seedlings of overseas plants that took root and strengthened sand dunes with their roots. Local conifers also grow.

The sea here is surprisingly clear, thanks to the sea currents washing the coast. The beaches are mostly sandy, but there are also pebble ones. After the storms, everyone hurries ashore in the hope of finding pieces of amber. And many succeed - not without reason the city bears the name Svetlogorsk: the city of sun stone.





The length of the city beach is more than two kilometers, and then the coast is quite deserted. Therefore, rest in Svetlogorsk, Kaliningrad region is suitable for those who want to stay away from civilization. Here you can find completely deserted places to enjoy the sound of the surf and the smell of the sea in solitude.

The swimming season here is quite short: from about June 15 to the 20th of August, and the water temperature does not exceed 20 degrees. In winter, the sea is not covered with ice.

Treatment

Svetlogorsk today is a resort with many sanatoriums, boarding houses and rest houses, offering therapeutic and preventive procedures to city guests.

For this, mineral water from local sources and peat mud are used. Recently, amber therapy has been used.
