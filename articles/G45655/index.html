Hotel Magic Hotel 4 (Kemer / Turkey)

Description: The small tourist complex Magic Hotel 4, built in 2005, 55 km from the airport in the resort town of Kemer, covers an area of ‚Äã‚Äã1800 km. It looks like a classic four-story building with a small house territory and compact rooms of various categories. The hotel is clean, neat, great for an economical vacation with your family. It can also be recommended for older tourists, honeymooners and people with disabilities.



If you want to stay away from the bustle of the city, then this is the place where you can spend your holidays calmly and solitary, enjoying the clean sea air and the atmosphere of comfort. The Magic Hotel 4 is surrounded by a pine forest, next to the building there are many grocery stores, souvenir shops, travel agencies and entertainment venues. Reception at the hotel is open around the clock. Express check-in services are provided for guests.

Types of rooms: consists of 80 rooms: Standard (with or without balcony), Suite and Vip. There are equipped rooms for the disabled. Room service is carried out daily at a convenient time for customers. Beds and towels are changed at least three times a week.



Guestrooms have pool or forest views. The floors in the rooms are covered with carpet, for the comfort of vacationers, air conditioning (individual) is installed. There is a color TV (satellite TV), a telephone with a direct line, a safe and a bar for a fee. The bathrooms have cosmetics and toiletries (replenished as they are used).

Meals: The Magic Hotel Kemer complex operates according to the European All inclusive system + snacks and drinks of various strengths throughout the day in the hotel bars. Table setting in the main restaurant as a buffet. For lunch and dinner, meat and fish specialties are always served.

The bride and groom are given symbolic gifts in the form of a bottle of wine and a basket of fresh fruit. In addition to the main restaurant with an open terrace, there are four more bars with different working hours. In one of the bars dancing evenings are periodically arranged.

Beach: The city's pebbly and sandy beach is 1 km from Magic Hotel 4. Guest reviews are varied: some were satisfied with the coast, others did not like the beach service. At about the same distance, there is a paid indoor beach where you can relax all day. You can get to the municipal beach on a free bus that runs every half an hour, a walk to the coast takes 20 minutes.

Additional information: Service at the Magic Hotel 4 is quite multifaceted. Two outdoor pools for adults and children have been built on the territory. There is a waterslide in the adult pool, and free sun loungers are nearby. The building has a Turkish bath, a jacuzzi, an indoor heated pool, a wellness center, a gym, a sauna, a jacuzzi and a beauty salon. There are massage and medical rooms, an Internet cafe, a games room.



For fans of sports entertainment and outdoor activities - table tennis, billiards, darts and playgrounds. Business travelers will have the opportunity to conduct their presentation or negotiations in a technically equipped conference room. Helpful staff will help in organizing any event. Guests have access to laundry facilities, parking (reservation), a library and car rental.

Digest: Complex Magic Hotel 4, according to tourists, is an ideal place for a measured, leisurely and secluded pastime. Young people will be bored here, as the hotel does not have animation programs and discos. The advantages of the hotel are delicious, satisfying and varied dishes.

High-quality and attentive service is also a huge plus. Many noted the good design of the rooms. The only drawback is the remote beach, well, for some guests this is not a problem, because after a hearty lunch it is useful to take a walk to the sea and at the same time enjoy local attractions.
