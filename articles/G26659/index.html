Betta Village, Krasnodar Territory. A place to relax from the hustle and bustle

Resorts of the Krasnodar Territory - a favorite summer vacation destination for our compatriots. In recent years, the tourist infrastructure has been rapidly developing on the Russian Black Sea coast. In large cities in the summer season it is always noisy and a variety of entertainment for holidaymakers is available. What about a traditional quiet and relaxing holiday? If you want to relax from the hustle and bustle, you will definitely like the village of Betta (Krasnodar Territory)!

General information about the village

Betta is a small resort village that appeared on modern maps in the late nineteenth century. The settlement was named after the cape located in the immediate vicinity. The local population in these parts still does not consider tourism the main source of income. Today, like a hundred years ago, agriculture and winemaking are flourishing. The village is surrounded by a pine forest. Thanks to this, the local microclimate is incredibly healthy. The air is literally saturated with coniferous oils, aromas of fruits, herbs and the sea. What recreational opportunities does Betta offer its guests?





Krasnodar Territory is a region in which most vacationers come primarily in order to have time to buy enough and sunbathe. The beaches in the village are pebble, with convenient entry into the water. There are also quite wild recreation areas among them, where you can admire nature and enjoy a small number of people. Tourists who like to relax in comfort will prefer comfortable beaches with rental of water attractions and other benefits of civilization.

Accommodation options and tourist infrastructure

Despite the fact that the village of Betta (Krasnodar Territory) is only beginning to develop as a tourist center, there are already quite enough small cozy hotels and guest houses. If you wish, you can choose a fairly comfortable option, for example, a suite, designed in a modern design with a bathroom equipped in accordance with the latest trends.

Fans of a quiet relaxing holiday will like the option of accommodation in the private sector. Many locals rent rooms and guest houses. Choosing such housing, vacationers can count on refreshments or the purchase of natural products at symbolic prices and pleasant communication with the owners.

How to get to Betta?

During the tourist season, many public transport routes from neighboring settlements go to Betta. You can get here from Gelendzhik or Krasnodar. You can also take the train to Novorossiysk and then by bus - to Betta. Many vacationers from Russia prefer to travel to Krasnodar Krai in personal cars. And this option is suitable if the end point of your path is Betta village. An alternative is to take a taxi from any nearby city, which you can reach by public transport.





If you have chosen the Krasnodar Territory as the place to spend your vacation, Betta is a good option for those who value natural beauty and a relaxed atmosphere more than modern night clubs and other traditional attractions.
