Ski base "Planernaya": description and how to get there

In a big city it is difficult to find time and a place where you can really relax from the hustle and bustle. Most people prefer to do this in nature, as fresh air has a beneficial effect on the general condition of the body. Therefore, many depart from the capital to the city of Khimki, where the Glider ski resort is located. This is a unique place where each guest will find an interesting activity for themselves. In winter, they ski and sled, and in summer they walk, play sports and ride horses. The complex also has where to stay guests. The sports center regularly hosts competitions and marathons, which are attended by athletes from all over Russia.

general information

The ski center OUSTS "Glider" began to exist in the last century. In the beginning, they specialized more in equestrian sports, which are now in the complex. The base got its name from the Planernaya railway station of the same name, which was nearby. Equestrian sport has been developing since 1935, and in 1948 the ski base itself with a springboard appeared. Further, the complex continued to develop; new sections and departments opened in it. Now guests can choose ski slopes with different distances. Available at 5, 10, 20 or 30 kilometers. All necessary conditions are created for visitors. There is a parking lot for cars, changing rooms for guests, a dining room and even a shop.

The size of the complex exceeds 90 hectares. The base is regularly used for skiing competitions and marathons. Not only athletes already know about the center, but also many residents of Russia. Here you can enjoy skiing, as well as watch the competitions of real professionals. If desired, there is an opportunity to see the participants of the Moscow ski track. A wide meadow located in the lowland was chosen for the start. Therefore, it is reliably protected from the wind. Novice skiers usually choose flat areas for themselves. And for professionals there are difficult tracks with ascents and descents.

Address

Ski base "Planernaya" (Khimki) is located 10 km from the Moscow Ring Road. Open from 9.00 to 21.00 hours. The complex is open all year.

How to get there

There are several ways to get to the sports complex. This is available not only by car, but also by public transport. There is a minibus number 154 to the Planernaya ski base to the stop at the Planernaya Microdistrict. You can also get to the Filino stop by bus 22, 370, 482, minibuses 532k or 873. You can walk to the stadium from it.

Additional features

Ski base "Glider" will be interesting not only for lovers of skiing professional athletes. Here, guests can find many interesting activities. Horse lovers will especially like the base, as there is a whole complex with a hippodrome. For travel, special fields are highlighted. Visitors can ride horses. And for younger guests, an entry to the youth school on equestrian sports is open. You can find out all the important details right on the spot. Many visitors come for the weekend, so they can stay in a hotel located in the complex. A great addition to skiing or horse riding can be: billiards, darts, shooting range, sports and gym. For fans of outdoor activities offered a baseball stadium. It regularly hosts competitions. For guests of the base there is a sauna, as well as an outdoor pool. In the warm season it is as interesting here as in winter. Beautiful nature is conducive to wonderful walks and sports jogging. If desired, you can order a carriage with a horse.
