“Personal Beep” service: option management

Standard beeps that people have to listen to while waiting for a subscriber's response can get bored quite quickly. If you are a client of Megafon, then it is in your power to make such an expectation more pleasant and positive for your friends and relatives, as well as colleagues and friends. The Personal Beep service is a good way to refuse standard beeps and please other people with famous compositions, jokes and other ringtones. Under what conditions this option is connected and used, how much it costs to order a melody and how to refuse it - we will consider all these issues in the current article.





“Personal Beep” service: get acquainted with its functions for free

In order for the subscriber to get acquainted with the functions of the service, evaluate its merits and understand whether he wants to use it, Megafon introduced a so-called test drive. Everyone can connect a personal dial tone for free for a week.

What is this measure aimed at? Many customers find it convenient enough to "test" the daily subscription fee for changing standard beeps and test the service. If they like it, then disconnecting it after a week will not have to. Otherwise, it is enough to enter a deactivation request before the eighth day, and the service will be excluded from the list of options activated on the number.





We connect a test drive

So, in order to get acquainted with the additional service we are considering, just connect it by typing the request: * 606 # . In the response message that will be sent by the operator, the subscriber will find information on how the “Personal Beep” service is managed.

Terms of use of a personal beep on an ongoing basis

If after seven days the option is not excluded from the number, then from the next day the subscription fee starts to be charged. It is impossible to name its exact size: it depends on which music channel will be used on the room. A standard payment is provided for the service itself - 1.7 rubles. The music channel also has a monthly fee - it can range from one to five rubles (more details can be specified in the description of a particular channel).

If, after a while, the Megafon personal ringtone bothers the subscriber, and the latter has a desire to turn it off, you can perform this operation for free.

Please note that ordering a melody is also a paid action - compositions can cost from 30 to 90 rubles. At the same time, creating your own ringtone will cost ninety rubles.





To learn about the options, settings, cost of music channels, as well as to order a beep and create a personal greeting, call the toll-free number 0660 . Through this service, you can also manage the service we are considering.

Personal Buzzer Connection Modes

You can set a melody in several modes:

  1. The ringtone for all calls at any time is “by default”. That is, no matter who calls your number, he will not hear standard signals - only the established composition.
  2. For a specific time. Only those people who will contact you at the specified time will be able to hear your “personal ringtone”.
  3. For specific people. In this case, a maximum of ten groups is allowed, and for each such group you can assign your own composition.
  4. To the number of a specific person.
  5. Playing a melody for any subscribers calling in a specific time period.

Why the “beep” may not work?

One of the common problems often encountered by Megafon customers who use the service we are considering is playing standard signals in some situations.





We remind you that a personal beep does not work in cases:

  • when the subscriber on whose number the option is used is outside the home region, that is, in roaming;
  • the number has the status “Financial blocking” (that is, the subscription fee for options cannot be debited).

At the same time, even if the melody is not played, and the service is connected, the subscription fee is charged as usual.

If the number is financially blocked, the service is suspended until the payment necessary to exit the current status is paid to the balance. After the system fixes the status of the “Active” number, the option will be provided on the same terms (cancellation of the monthly fee and music instead of beeps).





How to disable a personal beep on the "Megaphone"

The issue of disabling a melody, joke or other alternative to a beep is quite relevant. After all, the option is connected to new SIM cards, and not always the subscriber who recently bought a number in Megafon guesses that “non-standard” beeps are a paid service. As a rule, they begin to find out about this precisely after they notice regular charges from the number.

How to disable a personal beep? The easiest and most convenient way is to enter the USSD command - * 606 * 12 # . By the way, it also disables the option that operates in test drive mode.

Please note that for ninety days after the option is deactivated, all data (songs, ringtones, parameters) will be saved in the user profile. That is, if he wants to reconnect the service, he will be able to take advantage of the existing tunes and settings. If the service has not been reactivated, then on the 91st day all parameters and the user profile will be deleted. And this means that when you reconnect, you will have to re-acquire or "create" melodies.
