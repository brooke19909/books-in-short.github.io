Hotel Centara Villas Samui 4 * (Koh Samui, Thailand): reviews

Samui is one of the most picturesque resorts in Thailand. It is located in a fairly warm place on the planet, where in the midst of the spa season a lot of tourists from different parts of the world gather. Centara Villas Samui 4 * is one of the most popular hotels in this area. Travelers who rested in it, in their reviews often talk about the excellent service and living conditions presented here.

general information

Centara Villas Samui 4 * hotel is located in a very picturesque place, in the bay. Around it are magnificent landscapes created by the greenery of tropical plants, sand and water. Tourists who come here for the first time often admit that such natural beauty conquers from the very first glance. Most vacationers really like the remoteness of this resort town from civilization - the nearest densely populated point is 15 km away from the hotel. As for the transportation routes, the largest airport is located 21 km from this place.

Such an original hotel was built in 1992. Since then, regular reconstructions and repairs have been carried out in its place in order to maintain its appearance and improve in the technical equipment industry. To date, its total territory is 32,000 square meters. m, which is quite decent for a vacation spot of this level.



Rooms

The number of rooms Centara Villas Samui 4 * is located in separate small cottages, which are built on a hilly area, on the seashore. In total, 102 villas are presented here, each of which has apartments of a certain class: "standard", "deluxe" and "premium". Each villa has its own private sun terrace, where vacationers can sunbathe on comfortable sun loungers, admiring the surf.

Individual air conditioners are installed in the houses, with the help of which you can create the desired climatic conditions for living, regardless of the weather on the street. Also in any room there is a bathroom, fully equipped with plumbing and bath accessories.

Conditions for children

Centara Villas Samui Hotel 4 * (Natien) is recommended by many travel agencies as a place ideal for families. Here, in each room, a children's bed is compulsory for up to 12 years old. Parents who come to the Centara Villas Samui 4 * hotel (Thailand / Samui) tell in their reviews that there is no children's pool in this place for relaxation - many do not like it.

Nutrition

Hotel guests can eat in the well-equipped oriental restaurant, which organizes a daily buffet. He works on a specific schedule - from 6:30 a.m. to 11 p.m. Tourists who come here for breakfast, lunch and dinner, often respond positively to the talented work of cooks. According to them, the dishes prepared here are very tasty, original and nutritious. In addition, each of them has a special presentation that even the most demanding gourmets like.

In addition to the restaurant, the hotel also has a bar serving light snacks, most of which are prepared from fish. Tourists note that here you can taste real Asian desserts, as well as original cocktails, most of which are branded - their authorship belongs to the bartenders of Centara Villas Samui Hotel 4 *. He works from 8 a.m. to midnight every day.

SPA

The SPA zone in the hotel attracts a huge number of tourists with a massage parlor, which is part of its structure. Tourists note that only Thai professionals who work here can do a real Thai massage. Most vacationers positively evaluate the interior in which the hall is arranged - its beautiful and original finish sets up a state of complete relaxation.

If desired, fans of cosmetic procedures can go to the beauty center, which is located next to the massage room. It seems possible to carry out a number of cosmetic procedures, which, tourists say, are very inexpensive, especially when compared with prices in Moscow salons.

For lovers of a "hot" vacation in Centara Villas Samui 4 * there is a large thermal zone, which consists of a bath and a sauna. Professional parmeisters who know a lot about hot temperatures work here.



Sport

Fans of an active lifestyle can find for themselves a number of sports activities that will help maintain their body in shape, as well as the level of body health. First of all, for such guests a large gym is located, which is located in a separate building - a considerable number of units are installed in it, using which you can perfectly influence different parts of the body.

Fans of table tennis can visit the hall on a free basis, which has all the equipment necessary for the game. The hotel also has a bike rental service - it is often used by those travelers who like to explore local attractions on their own, traveling around the neighborhood.

Aquazone

For lovers of water procedures at the Centara Villas Samui 4 * hotel, a beautiful aquazone has been created, which is divided into three components - pools filled with crystal clear water. All of them are in the open air, the water in them is heated under the influence of direct sunlight. An automatic water purification system is installed in the niches, which allows it to maintain its normal state daily.

Around the pool there is an excellent sun terrace, which is not small in size. It is furnished with everything necessary for guests to relax: there are a small number of sun loungers and umbrellas. Tourists in their reviews of Centara Villas Samui 4 * often talk about the fact that, staying near the pool, you can truly enjoy the beauty of the gardens blooming in the hotel.



Services

The Centara Villas Samui Hotel 4 * provides a wide range of services that contribute to a more pleasant pastime of guests. In particular, they include the possibility of renting a car, which can be used in the same way as a bicycle - to go sightseeing. When renting a car, a place for parking it in a closed hotel area will be provided completely free of charge.

If desired, guests of Centara Villas Samui 4 * (Samui) can use the paid laundry and dry cleaning services. The hotel also has a currency exchange, a beauty salon and several shops selling interesting goods and souvenirs that guests will certainly purchase for their friends and relatives. Plastic credit cards are accepted at the hotel.



Beach

The beach strip of the hotel is located on the outskirts of its territory, it is completely covered with fine sand and small pebbles. As tourists, vacationing at the hotel, being here together with young children, note that they must be looked after, since at the entrance to the sea you can stumble on small stones. Otherwise, the rest here is simply magnificent.

The entire area for recreation is organized so that guests can comfortably spend their time here. It has several dozens of sun loungers, which are quite large in size, and huge umbrellas are installed near them, with which you can protect yourself from direct sunlight. If necessary, guests can use public toilets, as well as changing rooms. There are several showers on the beach.
