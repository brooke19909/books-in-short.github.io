E-insurance policy: reviews and expert opinions

The main change that occurred on July 1, 2015 is the ability to issue a policy electronically. The purpose of the innovation is to make insurance affordable, even in remote regions. How can I buy an electronic insurance policy? Reviews of customers who managed to use this service are different. According to the Russian Union of Auto Insurers (RSA) , 27 insurance companies will sell electronic policies.

The initial stage of issuing an electronic policy

To apply for an insurance policy, you must go to the official website of the insurance company and register. After registration, a personal account will be available through which client identification and verification of personal information is carried out. After checking the data, an electronic signature key is issued.



You can get an electronic key to sign an insurance policy for OSAGO in several ways:

1) On the official website of the insurance company. To do this, you will need to enter your last name, first name, middle name, passport information, mobile phone and email address in your personal account. After checking the data, a secret code will be sent to the mobile phone, which will serve as an electronic signature key, allowing you to draw up an insurance policy. Reviews of customers who bought the policy in this way indicate that this is a very simple and quick option to purchase insurance coverage.

2) In person at the office of the insurance company. To obtain an electronic signature in the company, the client must first register on the official website and contact the nearest branch with a passport.

Filling out an application for CTP



As soon as access to the site is obtained, you will need to fill out an application. It is no different from the usual, in paper form, which is filled in at the office when purchasing OSAGO. After filling in all the fields, the application must be registered by electronic signature. If the registration is positive, the client will be billed for payment of the policy. You can pay by credit card. After payment, the MTPL policy is electronically sent to personal mail.

Getting a policy

As soon as the policy is sent to personal mail, it can be printed out and put into the vehicle. According to the law, the purchased OSAGO form can be provided to police officers in electronic form: on a mobile phone, tablet or laptop. It is also worth noting that all OSAGO policies are registered on the PCA website, and anyone can check for free a form by the vehicle identification number.



It turns out that in practice the purchase of a policy is simple and affordable for everyone. But how do you actually get an electronic CTP insurance?

Currently, the World Wide Web is actively discussing the advantages and disadvantages of e-insurance. Let us consider in more detail all the pros and cons of acquiring an electronic form.

Benefits of Online Insurance

Consider the advantages of issuing an electronic OSAGO form:

1) The main advantage is the quick execution of the contract. You can issue an insurance policy at any time convenient for you. To obtain a contract you need a personal computer and 15-20 minutes of free time.

2) No need to overpay for additional services, without which many insurance companies refuse insurance.

3) The finished document can be stored electronically and, if necessary, downloaded from the site.

Disadvantages of Online Insurance

Those motorists who tried to purchase an electronic insurance policy, reviews were left behind on the World Wide Web and spoke about all the shortcomings.



Consider the disadvantages:

1) You can‚Äôt buy a policy for a new car that is not registered. The thing is that when applying for a policy, you must enter the state registration mark of the machine. You cannot get numbers without insurance. It turns out that insurance for new cars is available only at the sales office.

2) The discount that the driver accumulates for each accident-free year of driving is not always displayed. It turns out that when you purchase a policy via the Internet, you overpay and lose the discount.

3) Insurance companies can customize the application menu at their discretion and automatically check the boxes next to additional insurance items. At registration it is very difficult to replace these items and uncheck. It turns out that there is a risk of acquiring unnecessary additional services.

4) If you make a mistake when filling out the application, you will not be able to make changes. Have to re-fill it. If you register the policy and after you find a mistake, you will need to personally contact the office of the insurance company, write a statement and wait until changes are made and a new CTP insurance policy is provided.
