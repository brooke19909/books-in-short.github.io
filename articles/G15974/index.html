How to use a Sberbank ATM: step-by-step instructions

Today we will find out how to use an ATM from Sberbank. What kind of car is this? What is it used for? What should every modern citizen know about working with ATM? Description

How to use a Sberbank ATM? To begin with, a few words about what kind of devices these are.

ATM - a machine from a particular financial company, designed to serve customers. This is a kind of self-service terminal that allows you to work with bank plastic.

Next, we will consider the basic operations with ATM from Sberbank. All the functions described below are quite often used in practice. And therefore, they will certainly be useful to the modern population.

What can ATMs do?

How to use an ATM? Instructions for working with the corresponding machine will be presented later. First of all, it is important to figure out for what purpose the device is used.

Today, financial companies offer several types of self-service machines - terminals and ATMs. The former are distinguished by their simplicity. They do not give out cash.

ATMs are used:

  • to withdraw funds from a credit card;
  • checking the balance of the plastic;
  • payment for services (including duties and taxes);
  • mobile phone recharge;
  • connecting some banking services to the card;
  • plastic pin code changes;
  • credit card replenishment in cash.

In fact, modern ATMs are highly functional devices. And so they are of interest to many citizens.

Important: ATMs are located in different places of the city. Most often, they can be found in the branch of a bank, in shopping centers and lively areas of the city.

Quick guide

How to use an ATM? The step-by-step instruction, presented below, briefly describes the process of working with ATM.

To cope with the task, the client will need:

  1. Insert a bank card in the designated hole and enter the password to start work.
  2. Select the desired command in the main menu of the machine.
  3. Following the instructions on the screen, set the operation parameters. For example, find a payment and check the details of the recipient.
  4. Confirm your actions.

It would seem that working with an ATM does not cause any problems. In particular, if you want to carry out an operation with a bank plastic account. But with the search for paychecks and taxes, troubles can arise.

Checking balance

To cope with the task, you need:

  1. Exit to the main menu of the ATM.
  2. Select the button "Operations with a bank card."
  3. Indicate the desired function. For example, "Check balance." Next, consider this service.
  4. Choose a way to display information. The balance can be displayed on the ATM screen or printed on the check.
  5. If necessary, pick up the check and get acquainted with the information.

Card replenishment and withdrawal

How to use a card at an Sberbank ATM? This is not the most difficult task. The next popular feature is replenishment of plastic and withdrawing funds from it.

A guide to using ATM to bring your ideas to life looks like this:

  1. Get started with an ATM.
  2. Select the item in the "Card Transactions" section. Suppose "Top up account".
  3. Deposit money into the bill acceptor. The corresponding "window" will appear after selecting the operation. Money can be paid in one banknote or immediately in a bundle.
  4. Confirm procedure.

But that is not all. Quite often, people withdraw cash from cards. It is proposed to do this only with the help of ATMs. Payment terminals are not endowed with such a function.

Withdrawals have approximately the following form:

  1. Click on the "Withdraw funds" button.
  2. Choose the amount of money you want to receive. If there is no suitable option in the window that appears, you will have to click on "Enter another ...".
  3. Use the numeric keypad to dial the amount of cash you want to withdraw. The specified amount should be on the plastic. Otherwise, the operation will be impossible.
  4. Confirm transaction.
  5. Take the money and the check issued by the machine.

It is done. Further, as in the previous case, the user will be prompted to finish or continue working with the device.

Phone account replenishment

How to use a Sberbank ATM? The instructions below allow you to easily transfer money to your mobile device. For example, on a regular SIM card.

The instruction will be as follows:

  1. Click on the line "Recharge mobile."
  2. Choose a mobile operator.

Important: Sberbank often does not charge a fee for making payments and replenishing a mobile. Therefore, the devices of this financial organization are in special demand.

Connect "Mobile Bank"
