Vasilia 4 *, Corfu: reviews, description of the hotel. Holidays in Corfu

Greece includes 1,400 islands, each of which is a gem thrown by the Creator's generous hand into the Adriatic Sea. Not all of them are settled, and only on 78 islands the number of inhabitants exceeds 100 people.

Corfu enjoyed the deserved love of travelers at all times. A convenient location favored the development of trade and crafts, and modern residents of the island, if not involved in the tourism business, grow olives or grapes. Since ancient times, these places were famous for their olive oil, wines and the famous Corfu cheese. They are also popular now, but the main thing that local residents can offer is an unforgettable vacation in Corfu.

Sidari - a beach with all amenities

According to tourists' reviews, although the island is small (64 km long and 32 km wide), the beaches in different places are very different. Sidari is one of the best among them. Here is what tourists value most on the sea coast:

  • clean sand;
  • clear warm water;
  • convenient entry into the sea;
  • developed infrastructure.

Sidari has it all, plus low housing prices here, which makes it even more attractive in the eyes of vacationers. If you do not take into account the central beach, crowded with families with children, since there is strong shallow water and you will have to walk about 200 meters to a decent depth, then it is better to turn a little to the side to get to a quiet place.





The area is as if divided into 3 parts by rocky ledges extending 300 meters into the sea, forming small bays with a surprisingly beautiful color of water. There are not many people here, so renting a sunbed and an umbrella is not difficult.

Equipped paths lead to any part of Sidari beach from hotels, and Vasilia 4 * (Corfu) is no exception, and traveler reviews confirm this.

Hotel Description

Just half a kilometer from the resort village of Sidari, a small hotel Vasilia 4 * is comfortably located. The northern tip of the island attracts tourists with its unusually picturesque area. Here are the most beautiful bays and sunsets, the turquoise sea and pristine nature. Fans of nightclubs and round-the-clock entertainment will not be interested here, as they are concentrated in the central and eastern part of Corfu. But for travelers who crave silence, beautiful nature and beach holidays, Vasilia 4 * (Greece) is the best suited.

The hotel consists of two-story and one-story buildings located in a garden full of vibrant colors and palm trees. Perhaps the laziest ones will be unhappy that he is on the second line from the sea, but in fact, it takes no more than 5 minutes to go to the beach. It should be prepared for the fact that the coast in this part is pebble, but fully equipped with sunbeds and umbrellas. Smooth entry into the water with a gradual increase in depth is ideal for couples with small children.





No less attractive is the location of the Vasilia 4 * hotel (Corfu). Reviews of vacationers claim that the center of the resort village of Sidari is only 700-800 meters, and it is there that the best taverns, fish restaurants and bars are located. You can get there by local bus, the stop of which is located near the entrance.

Hotel rooms

The number of rooms is represented by several types of apartments:

  • The studio is designed for three adults, it has a bathroom, a small kitchen with the necessary equipment and utensils, air conditioning, TV, beds.
  • The apartment has 2 bedrooms, a balcony with garden views, shower and toilet, equipped kitchenette, satellite TV.

It is important to know: the hotel has its own free parking, and for the included air conditioning you will have to pay separately. Linen is changed once, and cleaning 3 times a week. Hotel Vasilia 4 * (Greece, Corfu) has free Wi-Fi.





As noted by hotel guests in their reviews, the rooms in it are not distinguished by sophistication, but they have everything for a comfortable stay. Sometimes there are complaints that the staff does not speak Russian, so he is not obliged to do this. The main way to communicate on the island is Greek and English.

Legend of sidari

One of the most legendary and romantic places on the island is the beach with the Channel of Love, located just six hundred meters from the Vasilia 4 * hotel. He is famous for the fact that if a lonely person manages to swim along it, then he or she will be destined to meet his soul mate.

Oddly enough, but apparently, the passionate desires of unmarried women are doing something with this place, since some tourists who have conquered the Canal of Love really get married in a fairly short time. For married couples such a swim guarantees a long and happy life in marriage.

Such legends are a guarantee that the Corinthians will not end in the clientele, but the island itself conquers the hearts of the guests.
