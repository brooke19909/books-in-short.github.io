Khanty-Mansiysk population, description of the city, climate

The city of Khanty-Mansiysk is not large, but today it is very cozy. It is the capital of the region, where oil is produced on the right bank of the Irtysh. Previously, a settlement was located in this territory, which was under the control of the Prince of Ostyak Samara. To the north of the city you can see the Ob floodplain, stretching for many kilometers. In the south is the Irtysh. We learn a lot of interesting information about this city later in the article.

Demographic situation

At the time when the filling of this paragraph with people began, the population of Khanty-Mansiysk was no more than 10 thousand people. When the period of perestroika came, it increased approximately three times. Further, the trends were also positive, since there was a migration of the external and internal type.



In 2014, statistics were collected, according to which the population of Khanty-Mansiysk amounted to 93 thousand people. As for middle age, it is not high, since this settlement attracts many able-bodied residents. Adults come from other cities. Children born here can call themselves full-fledged natives of this land.

There are many new buildings and facilities of the private sector. Most rent housing here. Statistical reports can please anyone who looks into them, as the population of Khanty-Mansiysk is growing steadily. Numerous new kindergartens are being built for children, which, however, are still in short supply, given the rapidly growing population. Parents have to take a very long line. As a rule, in such situations, a nanny is hired or a child is enrolled in a kindergarten in the village of Shapsha, which has to travel 20 kilometers.

The influx of residents and the local population

New people constantly come here from neighboring regions and states, including from Asian countries that were formerly part of the USSR. It is interesting that the native here is a real rarity.



As a rule, the Nenets, Mansi and Khanty occupy solid positions in institutions whose work is focused on culture and education. This is the intelligentsia of the nation. Another positive detail is that the population of Khanty-Mansiysk receives high-quality knowledge in the local educational institutions. Scientific structures are well developed here.

Weather

The climate here is continental, winter drags on for a long time and is severe. The city of Khanty-Mansiysk can wrap frost in minus thirty in the fall. In the midst of cold weather, the temperature does not rise above -20. The minimum level is a mark of -49 degrees. In addition, there are also strong winds.

Summer is the exact opposite. A 40-degree heat may begin in July, but it quickly subsides. At night, the temperature decreases significantly. So the weather in Khanty-Mansiysk is very changeable, and here you can meet with a variety of its conditions.





Ecology is at a high level, since there are no large-scale enterprises of industrial type. The houses are heated by gas-fired boiler houses, which also do not cause severe pollution. Of course, it is difficult to avoid the harmful gases that result from the operation of vehicles, however, given the small number of people, as well as vehicles, it can be concluded that the damage is not great.

When the cold weather sets in Khanty-Mansiysk, looking at the snow, its impeccable whiteness, you can understand that the air is really clean. There are many parks and plants of a natural type.

Management and territorial division

The administration of Khanty-Mansiysk consists of the Duma, which performs legislative functions. Its staff includes 25 deputies. Executive tasks are assigned to the government, which is governed by the district governor.

Geographically, the city is divided into 9 districts, the district - into 24 villages, 173 villages. There are also numerous camps in which people settle for a certain season to engage in national crafts. The largest cities are Surgut, Nizhnevartovsk, Nefteyugansk, Nyagan, Kogalym.

Living conditions

New housing is constantly appearing. The streets of Khanty-Mansiysk are replenished with comfortable new buildings. The most young areas are called Hydronamyv, as well as the South. They were built on loose soil. The infrastructure is in excellent condition, which the Khanty-Mansiysk administration takes care of. Many modern houses have been built that can withstand any local frost and protect residents from it. Plastic windows and glass on the balcony are installed in the best quality, heating works well. As a rule, small autonomous type boiler rooms are used.



The water supply system, which is being cleaned, is well established; harmful chlorine is not added to it. From such a liquid there is no scale and sediment. The stoves are made gas, and the electricity meters are double tariff. Thanks to this, you can save on the cost of utilities.

Transport

The authorities make sure that the roads are in good condition. There is a special technique with which the streets are cleaned and repaired. So motorists are extremely pleased with the condition of the tracks. Most residents have their own cars, so this issue is very important for them. Also pleasing is the absence of traffic jams. The maximum inconvenience is the need to drive to the second traffic signal, and not to the first. This is what rush hour usually looks like in this peaceful city.

Public transport operates regularly and regularly, however, the intervals between flights are still large. When frost sets in, it can be a nuisance. Buses and minibuses work. If you want to get here from Moscow, please note that the distance to Khanty-Mansiysk will be 2601 km.

Where to go

In addition to comfortable living conditions, the city also has many interesting sights that attract tourists here. One of them is the archaeopark, which has bronze figures in the form of animals that lived in the Paleolithic era.



There is also an excellent ski complex. Here, residents are engaged in biathlon and admire the beautiful forest that surrounds the sports center. Attracts people and a museum dedicated to man and nature. It has many interesting local history exhibits. He is called one of the best in Russia.

In addition, an art gallery is organized in the city. Here is a collection of national painting. These are both icons dating from the 16th century and the achievements of contemporary artists.

Cognition and entertainment

There are places significant for the Christian world, for example, the Church of the Resurrection of Christ, whose architecture is striking in its beauty. You can learn a lot of interesting things in the museum complex dedicated to geology, as well as gas and oil. It contains interesting collections of stones.



Under the governor A. Filippenko, tourism began to develop actively, numerous hotels and restaurant complexes, sports and entertainment centers were being built. Lovers of skiing can take advantage of the descent, skiing or snowboarding. There is a lift designed by modern technology. Here you can gain a lot of new knowledge, benefit the body with active pastimes, as well as have fun in the venues of large parties.
