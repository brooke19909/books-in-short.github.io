Japan Technology

Phones from Japan appeared not so long ago. Although the country is considered a leader in high technology, the percentage of its products on the world market is not as high as manufacturers might like.

Kyocera

This is a Japanese company that has been operating since 1959. Its headquarters is in Kyoto. Kyocera is now working with high-tech ceramics: kitchen knives, cutting tools and electronic components. The manufacturer is also known for its cell phones in Japan and office equipment.

The company is sensitive to the environment and promotes its protection, and also focuses on improving the quality of life of society.

Sharp

Sharp is a leading Japanese electronics company. The company was founded in 1912 and is located in Osaka. The manufacturer is engaged in Japanese cell phones, video and audio systems, household appliances, information equipment.

Also known printing and copying systems, microcircuits, electronic components and liquid crystal displays developed by this company. The company has a division that specializes in the production of high-end equipment.

Sony

And although the iPhone X is considered a popular phone in Japan, the Japanese also do not forget about domestic production. Therefore, Sony Xperia with all its models are still considered very high-quality and powerful devices.

One of the iconic models was the Z1. Now this smartphone is many years old, so it was replaced by a new line of XZ. The XZ1 model is equipped with powerful hardware, has 4 GB of RAM and a 5.2-inch screen. As always, the phone works according to the IP68 standard.

Models of these smartphones have become popular in the CIS countries. They subdued the buyer with their appearance and powerful "iron". In addition, Sony phones were among the first to work easily in the water and not collect dust.

The most popular was the Z line. Starting with the Sony Xperia Z1, the Japanese released a lot of new models that were getting better. For some time, the popularity of these phones has subsided, so the company decided to rebrand and released the XZ line.

New smartphones have got an updated design, a fingerprint scan button and a camera module. Sony developers tried to leave the corporate identity, so most fans of the brand were not scared away even by the high cost of new products.

Which phones are popular in Japan? Of course, the Japanese love their manufacturers and often purchase smartphones from Sony or Sharp. But, as statistics show, it was the American company Apple that was able to convince the Japanese of the quality of its products, so they definitely give preference to gadgets "with an apple."
