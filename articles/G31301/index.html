Park Talalikhina (Podolsk): photo and address

Podolsk is rightfully considered a strikingly green city, a real corner of nature in the heart of civilization. Almost a hundred years ago, a large-scale improvement campaign was launched here. Landscaping affected not only the parks, but the streets and boulevards of the city. Today, the authorities, residents of Podolsk itself and the okrug continue this wonderful tradition, decorating not only the center, but also small streets, as well as adjoining territories with greenery .

One of the most beautiful green areas of the city ‚Äã‚Äãis Talalikhina Park. Podolsk has many parks and squares, but this is perhaps the most beloved among its inhabitants. Various entertainments for children are arranged here, and on holidays solemn events and festivities are held.

History of creation

Talalikhin Park (Podolsk) was created in the second half of the XIX century. The inspirer of its construction and the immediate founder was Count Arseny Zakrevsky, Governor General of Moscow. Already then some alleys were laid and various entertainments were organized for the residents of the city.



And as a place of leisure, the park opened its doors to the Podolsk people in 1896. Beautiful arbors were built in it, and behind dense green trees cottages of white stone hid secretly. The park hosted fun festivities and entertainments, which were attended not only by residents, but also by Muscovites.

The development of the park as a central resting place

The park was especially active in the past century. In the twenties, a summer theater was built here, on the stage of which first representatives of the city amateur performances performed, and after the reconstruction, professional creative groups of Podolsk and even invited Moscow artists.

After the war, the Talalikhin Park in Podolsk underwent significant reconstruction and improvement. At this time, new paths were laid out here, rides and playgrounds were erected, sculptures and numerous pavilions were erected. In 1959, a monument to Victor Talalikhin, the pilot and Hero of the Soviet Union, was solemnly erected. Today it adorns the memorial part of the green zone. By the way, in our time, competitions are held near the monument among young people dedicated to the memory of the famous pilot.

Talalikhin park today

About ten years ago, the Podolsk authorities decided to improve the Talalikhin park in order to preserve it as a monument of landscape architecture and adapt it to modern requirements. The reconstruction was carried out in two stages, the first of which was completed in 2011. The implementation of the second began in 2012. This large-scale project made the city of Podolsk even more beautiful. Park of Culture and Rest Talalikhina today covers an area of ‚Äã‚Äãabout 28 hectares. Almost a third is a landscaped area.





There are many alleys, lawns and flowering flower beds. All tracks are covered with special fine-grained asphalt, which ensures the safety of riding bicycles and rollers. The city authorities monitor the appearance of the park, regularly planting young trees and updating entertainment. If you look at the old and modern photos of the park, the difference will be obvious.

Outdoor activities in the park

In the Talalikhin park, all conditions are created so that the residents of the city can enjoy outdoor activities and play sports. In addition, safe lanes for cyclists are equipped here and a whole venue has been created where competitions for workout and skateboarding are held. The park has an ice rink with an area of ‚Äã‚Äãabout 800 meters, and skate rental is organized. And in the winter season, a ski track is laid along the alleys.

Talalikhin Park (Podolsk) is an ideal place for outdoor activities. In summer, free yoga classes are regularly held here. And in the near future, the opening of a boat station for water recreation and a bike rental point.





Attractions and entertainment for the whole family

But, perhaps, the most important thing that attracts vacationers to the park, especially with young children, is fun rides. Today there are more than a dozen of them. Both children and adults adore them. The most beloved and exciting is the Ferris Wheel, which offers amazing views of the city.

All rides are completely safe and regularly tested. Ticket prices range from 50 to 200 rubles. It is noteworthy that for large families visiting the park is free of charge.

For the youngest visitors, an extensive children's town with an area of ‚Äã‚Äãmore than 600 meters is organized. Also in the park there are many cozy cafes and gazebos where you can relax after active entertainment.

Those who want cultural activities and have chosen the Talalikhin park for this purpose, Podolsk will give such an opportunity. A modern dance floor was built here, and the summer theater, which was also reconstructed, accommodates almost two and a half thousand spectators. On weekends, brass band concerts are held.



The Talalikhin Park of Culture and Rest has become a favorite place for city festivities. It is here that events are held in honor of Maslenitsa, May 9, Children's Day and other events.

How to get to the park? Address and mode of operation

Where is Talalikhin Park (Podolsk) located? Green Zone Address: 5b Rabochaya Street. The park is open daily and around the clock, so you can visit it at any time. The rides are open every day, except Mondays, from 11 to 20 hours. On weekends, they open from 12:00. On rainy and cold days (if the air temperature is below 12 degrees), the rides do not work.

You can get to Podolsk from Moscow by train, train or bus. In any case, the road will take about an hour.
