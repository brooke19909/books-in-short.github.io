My Home Resort Hotel 5 * (Turkey, Avsallar): description, service, reviews

Popular Turkish resorts are still incredibly in demand among our compatriots. Among them is the famous Alanya. In our article we want to talk about one of the five-star resort complexes called My Home Resort Hotel 5 *.

A bit about the hotel

My Home Resort Hotel 5 * is located in the village of Avsallar, near Alanya (22 kilometers). The nearest airport in Antalya is located a hundred kilometers from the hotel, so it will take a while to get there. The hotel complex is relatively new, since it began to work only in 2008. Its area is 14 thousand square meters. The latest renovations of the housing stock were carried out in 2012.



The hotel has a beautiful and green area, which is well watched. There are many ponds and recreation areas. The complex consists of a main five-story building and two more five-story buildings.

Catering

My Home Resort Hotel 5 * offers guests an all-inclusive concept. The main restaurant, My Home, is a buffet. The food concept includes locally-produced alcohol as well as soft drinks. Turkish coffee, ice cream and fresh juices must be paid separately, since they are not included in the system.

There is also a Turkish a la carte restaurant on site. It can be visited once for free upon prior registration. The hotel has a wonderful patisserie, which pleases with delicious Turkish sweets, and six bars.

Hotel infrastructure

The infrastructure of My Home Resort Hotel 5 * includes standard facilities for the hotel. The complex has indoor and outdoor pools. A conference room is provided for business meetings, and a banquet room is equipped for special occasions.



The hotel has several shops with all kinds of goods for tourists, several shops with souvenirs. Service in My Home Resort Hotel 5 * is also represented by paid services, these include the possibility of using dry cleaning and laundry.

Sports and Entertainment

Entertainment in My Home Resort Hotel 5 *, as in any Turkish hotel, is associated with the opportunity to engage in sports and the work of animators. The activity team offers recreational activities throughout the day. The kids club also has animators. In the evening, kids can visit a disco, and adults can enjoy shows and a night disco.

Vacationers have the opportunity to play bocce, darts, futsal, basketball, billiards and tennis, as well as attend aerobics classes. The hotel has a jacuzzi, massage salon, hammam, beauty salon, sauna, fitness center, hairdresser.

Beach vacation

My Home Resort Hotel 5 * beach is 130 meters from the hotel, the road to it takes no more than five minutes. The coast has a sand cover, it is equipped with umbrellas and sunbeds. There is a bar on the beach.

A bit about the resort

As we already mentioned, My Home Resort Hotel (Avsallar) 5 * hotel is located in the village of Avsallar. The busy resort village is 140 kilometers from Antalya, which is not very convenient for tourists who have to overcome such a distance after a flight. However, local hotels are still popular, because the local coast of the Mediterranean Sea is covered with beautiful sand, sometimes there are small pebbles. A lot of good hotels have been built in the village, among which there are many institutions of four-star and five-star levels. The main feature of the famous resort is its beautiful nature. Lush, rich vegetation makes it easier to tolerate summer heat.



But in Avsallar, tourists are attracted not only by its beautiful nature, but also by its active nightlife. The hotel also attracts people who want to use spa services for relaxation on vacation. The resort has many establishments offering a wide range of thalasso and spa treatments.

It is worth noting that Avsallar is the warmest place on the coast of Antalya. This is due to the fact that the local bay protects the coast from the winds, even the sweetest little royal bananas have time to ripen here.

The village has a large number of shops and boutiques, so shopping lovers will find something to do here. But the most colorful place, of course, is the bazaar, it is on it that you can buy real oriental souvenirs. Alara River flows through Avsallar, on the banks of which there is a beautiful Alaran palace dating from the 13th century. Once the building served as a haven for wanderers, but now against its background vacationers like to take photos. Local guides offer tourists to go rafting on the river, but this is only possible from March to April, since it is during this period that the water flow is very powerful.

Reviews of My Home Resort Hotel 5 *

Continuing the review of the hotel, it is worth paying attention to the reviews of vacationers who visited it this season. After all, it is the opinion of tourists that gives us the opportunity to navigate how good or bad this or that hotel is. It is no secret that representatives of travel agencies do not always talk about the true state of things, not telling the truth.



As for My Home Resort Hotel 5 *, reviews about it are very contradictory and ambiguous, which gives reason to talk about them in more detail.

The hotel, like the village of Avsallar itself, is at a fairly large distance from the airport, therefore, choosing it as a place to relax, be prepared for the transfer to your destination to take about two hours. My Home Resort Hotel 5 * (Alanya) is in a very picturesque place. The spacious area is very beautiful and well maintained. It can be seen that gardeners regularly look after the vegetation and flowers, everywhere it is very clean. In general, the territory is thought out, all objects are conveniently located, there are malls along which it is nice to walk in the evening. And there are plenty of places for exotic photos as a keepsake.

The number of rooms My Home Resort Hotel 5 * (Alanya) is not very diverse, there are only a few categories of apartments. However, according to tourists who visited the hotel in 2017, the rooms in which they stayed are very good. It is noticeable that the housing stock after a good repair. The apartments have beautiful interiors done in pastel colors. Good furniture and new plumbing in good condition, which is very pleasing, since there is the possibility of a comfortable stay away from everyday problems. Air conditioning in the rooms is working properly.

But with what there is a problem in all apartments, it is with the Internet. The hotel is positioning free Wi-Fi as an integral part of room service. But actually this item is missing. The fact is that the quality of wireless Internet is such that it simply is not possible to use it. The same situation is observed throughout the complex. Many vacationers try to use the Internet in the lobby, but even there it practically does not work. In general, such a situation with Wi-Fi for a modern five-star hotel is unacceptable. Since all tourists want to contact relatives or get in touch on business matters, but this is not possible at the hotel.

Nutrition Reviews

Regarding food in My Home Resort Hotel 5 *, hotel guests have a mixed opinion. Reviews of tourists are quite different - from complete enthusiasm to discontent. All guests note a large selection of dishes in the main restaurant, but at the same time, many believe that they are not diverse, and taste sometimes fail. The chef of the restaurant prepares mainly traditional Turkish dishes, which not all tourists like. Especially there are difficulties with feeding children who do not like spicy and salty dishes. According to some tourists, the level of food does not match the stardom of the institution.

Despite the mixed reviews, in general the food is scarce at the hotel, because the tables have meat dishes, salads, grilled vegetables, Turkish sweets and much more. During the high season in the restaurant there is a large crowd of people. Waiters do not always have time to clean the tables.



The complex has many bars that prepare all kinds of snacks throughout the day. Therefore, it is difficult to get hungry here. Bartenders also offer good drinks and cocktails. There is a bar on the beach. It mainly offers fast food from food, and water and beer from drinks.

General impression of the hotel

The resort My Home Resort Hotel 5 * offers its guests not only relaxation, but also an animation program. The hotel has an activism team that offers its services quite unobtrusively. In terms of recreational activities, the opinions of tourists also differ. Some believe that the level of animation is quite good and quite acceptable for lovers of measured and relaxing holiday. But young people, on the contrary, believe that entertainment is not enough, so the complex is not suitable for active young people.

In the evenings, the amphitheater hosts a mini-disco for kids, and after it an entertaining show for adults. Programs consist of hotel animator group numbers. Several times a week, invited artists participate in the evening show, sometimes there is even a fire show, which is always popular with holidaymakers.

As for the opportunity to play sports, the situation here is slightly better. The complex has a volleyball court and even a golf course. And yet, all vacationers agree in the common opinion that supporters of a relaxing holiday can visit the complex.

The hotel has a kids club. It is very small and not very clean. It can be seen that the toys do not wash. In addition, children are not accepted in it by appointment, which means they can leave it themselves, which is unacceptable for parents who plan to leave the child to play themselves, and in the meantime attend wellness treatments and massage.

Instead of an afterword

My Home Resort Hotel 5 * (Avsallar) is a fairly good hotel on the Mediterranean coast. Summing up the conversation, we can say that the institution is undoubtedly worthy of attention from tourists. Therefore, if you are going to spend your vacation in the hotel My Home Resort Hotel 5 * (Turkey), we wish you to have a wonderful stay and not be overshadowed by any problems.
