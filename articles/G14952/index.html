Architectural features

The city of Yeysk (Krasnodar Territory) is included in the list of historical places of the Russian Federation. The first general plan of the settlement was approved by Vorontsov. It should be said that the prince was, in fact, the founder of the city. The plan was approved in 1849. According to the project, it was supposed to realize a perpendicular perspective of 22 streets. A central avenue and several squares were also envisaged. By 1856, Gostiny Dvor was built. Today it is a monument of architecture and history. The area inside the courtyard and the surrounding streets were paved in the 1890-97th years. By decree issued by Nicholas I, all streets should be constructed in accordance with the adopted project.





The architectural appearance of the center reflects all the directions that existed in Russian architecture in the second half of the XIX - early XX centuries. Each merchant house built at that time has its own characteristics - it is unique and beautiful in its own way. At the same time, all buildings can be combined according to the variety of decoration elements. Mandatory details were turrets, porches, laid-out kokoshniks, terraces, openwork wrought-iron elytra, tents, cornices. In the architecture of the XIX century, this direction was called "pseudo-Russian style." Its use was mainly associated with the general enthusiasm of provincial and metropolitan circles for "fashion for Russian", a tendency to tried-and-tested, well-known architectural forms.

Modernity

Today the city of Yeysk (Krasnodar Territory) is a complex of buildings that were created in the architectural directions of three eras: the monarchist, Soviet and the period of the late 90s. A new style is under development. Its implementation is expected during the development of microdistricts 39 and 40, a cottage village and Taganrog embankment. All this activity has a direct connection with the new leadership of the city - Alexander Laschenkov and Sergey Tulinov.

Areas

Conventionally, you can divide a settlement into several parts. Of greatest interest to tourists is usually the Yeisk Spit. Many tourist centers are concentrated here, the main beaches are located. There is also a yacht club and an amusement park. The Yeisk Spit is adjacent to the zone, which includes a seaport, the territory of the railway station and a number of plants. In accordance with the new general plan, it is supposed to transfer the territory of the berths beyond the boundaries of the settlement.

In the south-west direction begins the historical part of Yeisk - the Merchant city. The main development of these territories was carried out in the XIX century. That part is conditionally limited to the modern streets of Bogdan Khmelnitsky and Sergey Roman. The territory of the Merchant Yeisk itself is presented in the form of a complex of streets located strictly perpendicularly. The so-called Center is separately highlighted. This territory is adjacent to the main city market and Gostiny Dvor. In general, the Merchant Yeisk retained the appearance of the southern provincial port town.

In the west, the village is limited to the airport. Adjacent to its territory is one of the most populated microdistricts of Yeysk - the Military Town. This part includes a housing estate and a site occupied by the local Military Aviation Institute. In the same territory is one of the main attractions of Yeysk - Park them. Poddubny. In the south direction from the Merchant part is a new housing estate and industrial zone. The housing estate includes several microdistricts: 38th and 2nd, "Field of Tears" and "Sunny". Tourism

The number of visitors to the city of Yeysk (Krasnodar Territory) for the summer period is increasing annually. Reviews of many tourists are filled only with positive emotions after a stay at the resort. It should be noted that in 2007 local residents themselves took the initiative to develop resort infrastructure. Particular attention was paid to the development project of the Taganrog embankment. The general plan of the city included the construction of an oceanarium, a sanatorium complex with mud and balneological hospitals, a new concert hall and many shopping and entertainment and hotel complexes.

Beaches

This type of holiday is considered the most popular. As visitors themselves note, the resort has all the conditions for a wonderful active pastime. Vacationers especially appreciate the landscaping and well-groomed beach areas. Every year, thousands of tourists come here from all over the country. The sea here is not very deep, and the coastal zone is equipped with everything necessary for relaxing with children. This, according to tourists, is one of the main advantages of the resort area.





The main beaches, including city and children, are located on the Yeisk Spit. At its end is the so-called youth zone. This beach is of particular interest. Its feature is the presence of underwater current from the Yeisk estuary. The depth here is maximum. Kamenka beach is also very popular with tourists coming to the city of Yeysk (Krasnodar Territory). This zone is located south of the port. The coast here is mostly sandy on the north side, and pebbles begin to prevail towards the south. There is a very shallow sea, and during low tides, a large number of shallows are formed on the surface, due to which many sandy islands are formed. Short beaches are also located near the 2nd microdistrict on the coast of the estuary.

Family tourism

This part of the resort industry is developing most actively. As vacationers themselves note, there are a lot of places in the city where you can go with your child. Children are especially delighted when visiting water attractions. A dolphinarium was built on Taganrog Embankment in 2007, and by mid-2008, an aqua park was built near it.

Several cafes are open for children in the city. Especially popular among young guests are such establishments as "Ficus", "Penguin". In the park to them. Poddubny open many attractions. Entertainment venues also work near the central city beach. The city of Yeysk (Krasnodar Territory) is also known for its children's camps. Among them, it should be noted Siverko, Friendship, Salute, Seagull. The time spent here will be remembered for a long time by the child. Many parents bring their children to the camp annually. There are all conditions for a comfortable stay of the child in the resort. In addition to entertainment programs, children really like wellness activities.



Active pastime

Often the city of Yeysk (Krasnodar Territory) is called the mecca of surfers. The very first competitions were held back in 1999. Since that time, lovers of this spectacular sport have become more and more year after year. In 2006, more than 150 athletes came to the May competitions, and the number of guests from all regions of the Russian Federation amounted to more than one and a half thousand. In addition to windsurfing, in Yeysk there are horse, parachute, fishing clubs, as well as yacht centers (one of the most promising in Russia).
