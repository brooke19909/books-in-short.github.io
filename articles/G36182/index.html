Sudak, Cypress Alley - a place for a pleasant stay

With great pleasure, many tourists regularly come to rest in Sudak. Cypress Alley is one of its main attractions. It is located in the very center of diverse youth entertainment.

In general, a very bright and cheerful city of Sudak. Cypress Alley is its central street leading directly to the embankment. Here you can find everything you need for a great vacation.



Zander. Cypress Alley - City Center

So, more details. Every lover of a spa holiday, of course, is familiar with a city called Sudak. Cypress Alley was laid back at the beginning of the last century. Here are concentrated a lot of different cafes, shops, entertainment venues. This is a place where you can buy tickets for any excursions, original sweets, famous Crimean wines.

This street is the hallmark of the city (Sudak). Cypress Alley every night turns into a real "gallery of talents." Dancers, musicians perform here, artists paint pictures. And artists perform their numbers not so much for the money ... They are driven by a love of art. They completely give themselves to their beloved business, raising the spirits of passers-by.



There is where to go

Starts at the boarding house "Star" Cypress Alley. Sudak is a city that offers its guests and residents a lot of entertainment. But the main street simply amazes them with plenty. At the beginning of a number of cypresses planted on both sides of it, the Tatar market is located. Here you can buy fresh vegetables and fruits, Crimean sweets and wine for bottling. Along the entire length of the alley are pizzerias, karaoke bars, cheburech, bistros and cafes.

Almost at every step there are kiosks with tickets for excursions, souvenir shops and ice cream tents. Beach and summer accessories, clothes, jewelry are also sold here.

In the summer, the alley "does not sleep" for a minute. There are enough vacationers here, day and night. Cultural events are constantly held on the street, all kinds of contests and holidays are organized. The exit to the embankment completes the Cypress Alley. It offers a magnificent view of the Genoese fortress.



Mass of tourists

Many people are attracted by the chic city of Sudak. A cypress avenue, the photo of which is in every tourist catalog with advertising on holidays in the Crimea, delights everyone who wants to relax in the Black Sea resort. People from all over the world come here.

The avenue bears its name, as already mentioned, from cypress trees planted on both sides of it, from the beginning and right up to the very shore of the sea. These trees are durable, very old. They are unpretentious and do not require special care. Due to their moisture resistance, trees survive without problems during the sunny dry Crimean summer. Thus, the name was fixed among the locals.

In addition to numerous cheburechki, pizzerias and bistros, where you can have an inexpensive and tasty snack, you can find quite large restaurants. Among them: Baron Munchausen, Calypso, Biker Pub, Oasis, Sail, Shantan, Dostluk, Surf, Eve, Old Fortress, Gourmet other. It regularly hosts nightly shows.



Rest with the whole family

Crimea ... Sudak ... Cypress Alley ... All this has a beautiful, pleasant and fun pastime. The central alley is ideal for fun and relaxation for the whole family. For lovers of bowling, the Bowling City is located here. Those who wish to play billiards can choose any of a variety of offers (separate billiard or billiard tables installed in every self-respecting restaurant).

For kids, playgrounds with various swings and carousels have been built here. For older children, slot machines are installed. At peak season, the alley is filled with people for days. Thousands of vacationers who want to go to the promenade or the central beach pass through it.

By the way, in order to get the right to trade here, an individual must pay in advance for his place in the local budget. Only in this case they will be able to sell their goods or provide their services to those who came to rest in Sudak.

In a word, visiting this beautiful city, you cannot but visit the Cypress Alley. It is here that all cultural events take place. It is here that the center of the magnificent Black Sea resort is located.
