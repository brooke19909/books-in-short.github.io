Hotel Garden Home Kata 3 * (Thailand, Phuket): description and reviews

Thailand is a country that many Russian tourists like to visit for a good rest and a pleasant pastime. As they note in their reviews left after a trip to this country, here you can observe not only a unique nature, but also a huge number of interesting sights.

One of the foreign guests' favorite hotels in this region is Garden Home Kata 3 * in Phuket. Here, according to vacationers, a pleasant atmosphere is offered for a good pastime and a decent level of service.

So, what features is this place famous for and what can the hotel offer guests? About it further.

general information

Garden Home Kata 3 * is an economy-class hotel, which, despite its three-star level, offers guests very good conditions for living near the sea coast. It was built in 2009 and currently occupies a large territory, the total area of ‚Äã‚Äãwhich is more than 6.5 thousand square meters. In this territory there is a huge modern building, many places for recreation and entertainment, as well as a beautiful garden, which tourists often mention in their reviews of Garden Home Kata 3 *.

Many tour operators offer such a hotel for families, as on its territory everything is provided for the stay of representatives of different age categories. Great attention is paid to young children for whom there are separate conditions.

Location

Some tourists in their comments note the advantageous location of the Garden Home Kata 3 * hotel - on Kata Beach (Thailand). They share their impressions of the picturesqueness of the surrounding region, and are also pleased that the place to stay is within walking distance of a clean and beautiful sandy beach. The nearest village is less than 1 km away - the entire infrastructure of the area is concentrated on its territory, you can also find night and daytime entertainment for the whole family. The large resort center (Phuket) is located 25 km from the hotel - many vacationers get to it by transport, which can be rented at the hotel or in special points.

As for the sea coast, it is located 400 meters from the hotel. However, tourists are very positive about the beach, which is offered here.

Transport links are at a fairly impressive distance from this vacation spot. The international airport, from which the journey of many foreign guests begins, is 50 km from Garden Home Kata 3 *. It is from here that a comfortable minibus takes all the tourists, delivering tourists as part of the provision of a paid transfer service, included in the price of each ticket. If desired, vacationers can abandon it and independently get to their vacation spot. To achieve this goal, you can use public transport or taxi services.

Rooms

The entire number of rooms, which consists of 258 apartments, is concentrated in one large building, which looks like new, despite the fact that it was built in 2009. His condition was achieved through regular repair and restoration work.

All apartments in the hotel have been renovated in a high-quality and modern way, and only new and functional furniture has been installed. All types of rooms have excellent technical equipment, which allows guests to feel more comfortable. In addition, each of them has an individual bathroom, equipped with everything necessary to maintain hygiene: plumbing, as well as bathing accessories, bathrobes and towels. In addition, the bathroom has a hairdryer for drying hair.

Guests of the Garden Home Kata 3 * hotel (Phuket) have the opportunity to use free Internet access throughout its territory, including in their rooms. Each of the three types of apartments has an individual climate system, which allows you to set the optimal temperature in the room, regardless of weather conditions. In addition, guests can request the installation of an electronic safe in the room - it may be needed to ensure the preservation of small values. This service is paid.

Mini

The hotel offers small rooms of category mini, which are especially in demand among guests who relax either alone or together, and their placement does not require a large space. They offer guests a small one-room space with a private bathroom and a balcony with views of the surrounding space. In addition, there is an air conditioner with a personal control system, which guests can control, setting a favorable temperature regime for themselves.

As for the furniture equipment of such apartments, they have a double bed (or two single beds), a pair of small tables, as well as a soft sofa, which can serve as a great place to relax. This room has a small built-in kitchen, which guests can use for cooking. It has a built-in refrigerator, equipped with a minibar with a small selection of soft drinks.

Deluxe

Apartments of this group are also designed to accommodate a couple of guests, but they are located on a much larger area. Guests who relax in rooms of this class very often share their interior photos and comments on them.

The furniture in the Deluxe Apartment offers a large double bed with expensive linen and a hard mattress, as well as a large wardrobe for storage of brought things. Here on the balcony there is a wonderful relaxation area, presented in the form of a pair of deck chairs and a small round table, where you can drink drinks.

The rooms have good lighting, which is provided thanks to a large number of appliances.

Suit

Apartments of this group belong to the category of superior, they offer the most comfortable conditions for spending time for all groups of guests. Suit rooms are designed to accommodate two vacationers, for whom there is a large bed, covered with expensive and high-quality linen. A large structure consisting of cabinets and shelves is installed along one of the walls - its niches are perfectly suitable for temporary placement of personal belongings and clothing of guests. In addition, in front of the bed there is a huge plasma TV connected to the satellite broadcasting system. By the way, some guests in their reviews about Garden Home Kata 3 * (Phuket) note with positive comments the presence of a Russian-language channel in the general list.

Like other rooms, Suit has private balconies furnished with furniture suitable for day and night stays. If desired, guests of the apartments can use the phone, which is connected to the international communication line - calls on it are charged separately from accommodation.

Conditions for children

Many tourists come to Garden Home Kata 3 * (Kata Beach) with all the family members. This is due to the presence of good conditions for children. An example of such is the possibility of providing each group of guests with a baby crib, which is carried out on a free basis. Much attention is also paid to baby food - for the youngest guests, the hotel restaurant offers a unique menu, which contains only the most delicious and mouth-watering dishes made from simple and healthy ingredients.

In their reviews, vacationers on the negative side consider the lack of the possibility of renting strollers with which you can transport the child. In addition, some vacationers warn that it is advisable to pre-book a cot for a child before arriving at the hotel - their number is significantly limited.

Nutrition

The guests of the Garden Home Kata 3 * hotel are offered two catering establishments, which serve deliciously prepared dishes of Asian, European and international cuisines. For guests to visit, there is a large bar where you can enjoy mouth-watering snacks and refreshing cocktails. In addition, under the all-inclusive system, tourists vacationing at Garden Home Kata 3 * have the right to attend the buffet, which is served every day in a large restaurant.

Feedback from visitors to such establishments often refers to the positive aspects received here.

Aquazone

Many tourists vacationing in Garden Home Kata 3 * prefer to spend most of their free time in the aquatic area. It is represented by a large sun terrace, located in the middle of a flowering garden. Comfortable sun loungers are located throughout its territory, which can accommodate guests. If desired, all vacationers have the opportunity to visit the bar, where it seems possible to taste delicious cocktails, as well as fresh fruits and snacks.

In the very center of the aquazone is a large swimming pool where tourists can swim. Its water is constantly clean, as an automatic cleaning system that works daily is installed in a niche. Heating of the filling occurs due to sunlight.

Sport and massage

Fans of an active lifestyle can go in for sports without stopping their vacation. For this purpose, in the Garden Home Kata 3 * territory, a platform for playing mini-football was built, as well as a hall for free playing table tennis. Many guests are very fond of having a game of billiards, for which the hotel also has an equipped room.

If desired, tourists can visit the massage room, which is located in the main building of Garden Home Kata 3 * (Phuket). Here, according to travelers, real masters of this art work who can quickly and efficiently immerse the client in a state of relaxation. All services that are provided in such an office are paid.

Beach

Despite the fact that the coastline is at some distance from the territory of Garden Home Kata 3 *, it is very often visited by tourists relaxing in this hotel. In their reviews, they often share photos taken against the backdrop of beautiful landscapes, and also talk about the fact that here you can relax surrounded by beautiful vegetation, clean and soft sand, under hot, but not burning sun rays.

Tourists can sit anywhere in the beach. If you wish, you can rent everything sun loungers, towels and air mattresses here - for this you need to pay a small fee. Along the coastline, you can find a lot of entertainment for both adults and children, which undoubtedly pleases travelers - they write about this in their comments left on the open spaces of tourist sites or on personal pages on social networks.

Prices

The cost of tours to Garden Home Kata 3 * depends on the desired conditions. Tourists write about some prices in their comments. So, for example, in reviews of Garden Home Kata 3 * 2017, you can often read about the average price for a trip, which is about 200,000 rubles. This price is based on a 10-day stay for two in Deluxe conditions. The cost of a Mini, according to vacationers, will cost only 50,000 cheaper. Moreover, judging by the comments of the hotel guests, the tour operators include in the declared price not only the cost of living, but also all expenses associated with the transfer and meals. In their opinion, this pricing policy for a vacation in the picturesque region of Thailand is quite acceptable.

Also, many vacationers note that at the entrance to the hotel you need to make a deposit of about $ 100. It can be paid both with a plastic card of international format and in cash, in fact, like all other possible services.
