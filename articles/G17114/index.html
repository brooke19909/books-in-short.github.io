Its modern U-shaped hotel building, directed by the "legs" of this "letter" directly to the sea, covers a fairly spacious, albeit compact, recreation area around the pools. This arrangement provides its guests with a gorgeous seascape, as well as a view of a very well-groomed territory with neat lawns, a garden of palm trees, exotic and flowering plants.

The water area adjacent to the hotel beach is characteristic of the coast of Hurghada. It is not very rich in corals, the beach is sandy. It is convenient for families with children.

What time of year to buy a tour?

So, you decided to fly to Egypt. In this case, we highly recommend that you take into account the climatic features of this country.



According to guest reviews of Sea Star Beau Rivage Hotel 5 *, a tropical desert climate is felt here almost all year: the temperature of the Red Sea is not very unfavorable for swimming only in the winter months, the sea is cool, in addition, a piercing cool wind constantly blows. Because of this, the winter beach season in Egypt is problematic. Hotel guests for the above reasons prefer to swim at this time in the heated pool, and not in the Red Sea. Although the cold weather (in our understanding) in the winter in Egypt, of course, does not reach. Indeed, the local January average temperature is +16.4 0 . Therefore, the hotel complex is in demand by tourists almost all year round.
