Alize Lanagold - yarn for creating unique things

Craftswomen-needlewomen know that not only the result of work, but also the process of its creation depends on the quality of the source material. Ease of use, economy and attractive appearance of the product - this is what they say about Alize Lanagold hand-knit yarn. What else do you need to know about this material?





About the manufacturer

Turkish goods have gained their popularity in the market because of quality and affordable prices. Yarn is also one of them. "Alize Lanagold" - the most popular of those produced by Turkey. It is used in work not only by professionals, but also by lovers of hand knitting.

This yarn has been produced at the Yunteks factory since 1984. During this time, she became the brand that every needlewoman heard about. A large selection of yarn types, a huge color palette, the relevance of the texture - all this is the key to the popularity of "Alize" in many countries of the world.

Domestic buyers can purchase yarn in specialized or online stores.

Alize Lanagold (yarn): composition and properties

This type of yarn consists of 49% wool, 51% acrylic. It has earned its popularity due to its properties - good density, softness and practicality. The fleece in the composition makes the item warm, supple and hygroscopic.

Yarn is suitable for knitting and crocheting, which at times expands the range of its application. From "Alize Lanagold" needlewomen make things for both autumn and cold winters.

Sweaters, hats, mittens and other products are socks, practical and durable. Even if you wear such a thing for several seasons, it will look new.

The elasticity and strength of the thread allows you to use any pattern for knitting - from simple elastic bands to the most complex braids. The quality of the yarn is also manifested in the fact that the thread in the skein is integral, does not break even without knots.

Benefits of Woolen Yarn

Alize Lanagold - half-woolen yarn. In addition to the natural component, it contains acrylic - an artificial part. This synthetic fiber has several useful properties:

  • threads become more durable;
  • thermoplasticity;
  • products do not fade or fade;
  • yarn is well dyed, which increases the number of its colors;
  • less susceptible to the harmful effects of insects (e.g. moths);
  • things are less peeling (formation of pellets);
  • in its properties resembles a natural material, therefore products are warm, comfortable, soft, breathable;
  • things made of a mixture of wool and acrylic hold their shape better, less stretch or sit down;
  • yarn is more accessible materially.

What to knit from yarn "Lanagold"?

The composition of the thread and the variety of colors allow you to create any warm thing: soft sweaters and sweaters, comfortable dresses and sundresses, all kinds of hats, scarves, mittens, mittens, socks, slippers and more.

Yarn consumption depends on the size of the product and the volume of the pattern. On average, it turns out to use:

  • on a women's sweater - about 500 g of yarn;
  • on a men's sweater - 600-800 g;
  • for a children's sweater (up to 3 years) - about 200 g;
  • a set of hats and scarves - up to 400 g;
  • 1 sq. plaid m - about 600 g.

Based on the characteristics, we can conclude that Alize Lanagold - the yarn is quite versatile and easy to use. The elastic thread practically does not delaminate during the knitting process, the pattern is uniform and pronounced. All that is needed is the right knitting needles or crochet hook and basic knitting skills.
