Holidays in Turkey are now popular all over the world. Location

On the territory of the ancient, glorified by Homer Lycia, there is a wonderful village, the appearance of which is made up of modern handsome hotel buildings, drowning in the colors of the street, various shops, shops and benches and smiling tourists.

It is called Tekirova. Turkey pays great attention to the development of the tourism business. Thanks to this, new parks were opened in the village, first-class roads were laid, and a wide network of services was created. Getting here is easy. Antalya Airport is approximately 60 km away , where a shuttle can be booked. Many hotels provide free transportation on the day of arrival and departure. In addition, you can take a minibus to Kemer, and from there to Tekirova just a stone's throw (only 12 km), and buses run every thirty minutes. The village is geographically somewhat remote from the rest of Kemer, which makes it possible to relax without unnecessary noise and fuss. The endless sea distances provide an excellent opportunity to surf, and the snowy peaks of Takhtala will present a unique opportunity to treat yourself to playing snowballs and sculpting snowmen in the summer (until June), and then go down to the foot and plunge into the warm sea waters.

Sea and beaches

Tekirova is located on the Mediterranean Sea. The coastline is flat everywhere, the entrance to the water is convenient for everyone. True, in some parts of the coast, large stones create a problem.

The beaches throughout Kemer are mostly small-pebbled. The bottom is also pebble. If the stones are covered with slippery algae, it becomes difficult to move on them, so it is advisable to have special shoes on your feet. For the convenience of their guests, on their own beach areas many hotels in Tekirova are sanding. Turkey is very closely monitoring the state of water in all four of its seas and the cleanliness of its coastline. That is why many of its beaches have the international Blue Flag. Tekirova is no exception. The sea here is so clean and transparent that you can freely watch the movement of fish and crabs in the water column. Everywhere along the shore there are sun loungers, umbrellas or awnings. You can use them on the municipal beach and on the territory of "your" hotel, but just walking is not prohibited along the entire coast. It is also not forbidden to set your own umbrella and lay your own towel. Along the entire promenade there are many bars, convenience stores, souvenir shops and various trinkets. On the beaches you can rent various equipment (balls, masks for scuba diving and the like), rent catamarans, jet skis, diving clubs work here.

sights

Lykia was once a haven of pirates. Over the centuries, it was conquered by the Persians, Romans, Turks, and the dyadohs of Alexander of Macedon. The ancient country left a rich legacy to its successor - the village of Tekirova.

Turkey is world famous for its monuments of ancient culture. In Tekirova there is also something to see. The remains of the city of Fezalis, built more than two and a half thousand years ago, are of constant interest. It is located north of the village, and to the south you can admire Cleopatra's Bay. It will be interesting for fishermen to go to Ulupinar, where they provide an opportunity to fish for trout. Immediately, on the spot, they are preparing it. In addition, you can order excursion tours to Kemer from Tekirova. Turkey is an unusually interesting country. There is even Mount Chimera here with eternally burning lights escaping directly from the stones. She and the village of Cirali with a beach of turtles are half an hour from Kemer. In addition, you can visit the ruins of ancient Olympus, the Beldibi Paleolithic Cave, the city of St. Nicholas the Wonderworker Miru and Yoryuk Park with a curious exposition of nomad Turks, which will certainly offer you to try national drinks from koumiss. At 12 km from Tekirova there is a suspension road with a length of 4350 meters. She raises to the top of Tahtal. The journey takes place in booths with excellent visibility. The climb lasts about 15 minutes. At the top, in addition to the observation platforms, there are two restaurants. In the village itself, a park of exotic plants and reptiles has recently been opened. Animals can be photographed, picked up, which always causes delight in children and adults.
