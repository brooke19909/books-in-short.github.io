Hotel Diamond Cliff Resort Spa 5 * (Thailand / Phuket): overview, description and reviews

One of the most famous southern provinces of Thailand is Phuket. There are a lot of all kinds of hotels. But, of course, every tourist wants to stay in one that is one of the best. general information

The largest and most developed beach in all of Phuket is Patong Beach. It extends several kilometers in length. Patong is famous for its yellowish sand and smooth entry into the clear, turquoise water. True, it becomes muddy during low tide and in the midst of the tourist season. However, this is not scary. By the way, in the water the beach is fenced with a special protective net from predatory marine life.

At Diamond Cliff Resort Spa 5 *, guests are presented with everything that can only make their stay on vacation more comfortable. restaurants and bars

Each hotel complex of a high level has at least several establishments in which guests can satisfy their hunger, thirst, and also miss a couple of glasses of strong drinks. Diamond Cliff Resort Spa 5 * hotel is no exception.

The Diamond Hut Restaurant is located on the seafront and is open from 10:00 to 22:30. It serves a variety of cuisines, and you can also enjoy a barbecue.

Franco Roma Restaurant serves Italian and Egyptian dishes. Especially gourmets will like this place. After all, it is here that seafood is perfectly prepared and they offer mature, good wine. Franco Roma is open only in the evening - from 18:00 to 23:00.

Lovers of Japanese cuisine will love the Kiko Restaurant. It is open from 11 a.m. to 3 p.m. and from 6.30 p.m. to 11 p.m. If you want to grill and barbecue, you can go to Korean Hut. It starts at 11:00, and closes at 22:30.

Helpful information

In the Diamond Cliff Resort Spa Phuket 5 *, check-in starts at 14:00, and departure lasts until noon. However, if the guests arrived early, and the room ordered by them is free, then they will be issued earlier as guests. The main thing is not to be shy to ask about it.

You can come here with children. And if the child is not six years old, then for his stay will not have to pay. For a child under the age of two, the administration will provide a cot.

When applying for a reservation, you will need to provide your personal credit card details. This property accepts Visa, Master Card, American Express and JCB. The card will then need to be provided upon arrival. Therefore, it is important to use only your credit card so that the names of the guest and owner coincide.

It is also worth knowing that guests can take advantage of a free one-way transfer to Patong Shopping Center. A bus runs from the hotel four times a day. To find out all the details, you must contact the reception.
