Hotel "Sputnik" (Voronezh): address, description of rooms, services, photos and reviews

Sputnik Hotel (Voronezh) is an attractive place for tourists. Voronezh is a transit city for tourists, as well as a place of various memorable places and attractions.

The article will discuss what kind of service the Sputnik hotel offers, which is located on the outskirts of the city. The cost of living and reviews of tourists will also be described below.

Overview

The hotel is part of a large chain of hotels "Amaks". It is located on the outskirts of the city, in a picturesque forest zone. There is a highway nearby, so tourists who travel to Crimea often stop at this place.

The entrance to the hotel is wide and comfortable. There is a large sign with the name of the hotel. From the parking lot to the door there is a canopy that shelters from rain and snow.

Upon entering the hotel, it becomes clear that everything here is designed in one design decision. Glossy walls and light-colored floors are in perfect harmony with the dark-colored reception desk. Here you can find out the time in different parts of the world.

Spacious corridors are covered with carpet, so when moving guests do not interfere with other clients to relax. The interior of the hotel is classic European. Curtains are in perfect harmony with small wall paintings and panels.

Hotel "Sputnik" (Voronezh): address

The hotel is located in a forested area on the outskirts of the city. Address: Moskovsky Prospekt, house 145. The city center from this point is about 8 km, the airport is 9.5, and the railway station is about 6 km. At the hotel you can order a taxi or transfer to the airport or other point of the city.

Services

Near the hotel there is a spacious car parking where guests can park their cars. Around the hotel there are many sidewalks, which are convenient for walking in the picturesque surroundings. The building has a pool for guests, as well as billiards. Some board games are available at reception. There is Internet and multi-channel TV in all rooms.

The reception is open around the clock, you can contact the registrars at any time with a question or request. Sputnik Hotel (Voronezh, pr. Moskovsky pr. 145) provides guests with a safe and luggage storage. Laundry facilities are also available.

There is a restaurant on the ground floor where you can have breakfast or lunch. It offers a menu with European cuisine. Meals are served until 22.00 (until the closing of the kitchen). Guests have a minibar in the lounge area, which offers drinks and snacks.

Conference rooms

The place is also suitable for negotiations and business meetings. For this, the hotel has 4 conference rooms with a total capacity of up to 400 people. The organization of such events is the "chip" of the hotel. In addition to well-equipped halls, guests are offered coffee breaks, lunches and dinners. Also, everyone can be placed in comfortable rooms.

Each conference room has its own name and specific staffing. The Dialogue Hall has a desk, a computer, a magnetic board and a projector. Business partners are often found here to discuss contracts. In the "Cabinet" hall, the situation is somewhat simpler and freer. It also has a computer and a projector.

In the VIP room you can arrange meetings like a round table. There is a TV and a fireplace, which create a unique atmosphere of comfort and home.

Hotel "Sputnik" (Voronezh region) makes it possible to rent its largest hall - "Forum". It occupies 144 square meters and can accommodate up to 150 people. The room is equipped with a computer, projector, microphone and additional attributes. Congresses and meetings are often held here.

Rooms

Single standard hotel room will cost from 2500 rubles. The room has a bed, a work area (table and chair), a TV, a wardrobe, a bedside table and a bathroom with a set of towels and toiletries.

A business double room with a double bed costs from 3,500 rubles per day. The interior of the room is classic, in soothing colors. The spacious apartments are equipped with a TV, air conditioning, a desk, bedside tables, a mirror and a wardrobe. A minibar and fridge are also included. The combined sanitary unit with a shower cabin has a set of towels and cosmetics in disposable bottles.

Hotel Sputnik (Voronezh ) also offers double rooms with two beds. The room is made in blue shades and has a large window. That is why it is light and airy here. A standard set of furniture and services is also present in this room. The cost is the same as in the previous version.

The studio offers chic apartments with a kitchenette. The room has a large bed and a seating area with a sofa and a table. A TV, fridge, air conditioning, minibar, hairdryer and bath amenities are provided. The cost of living in this room is from 4,500 rubles.

A suite with an unusual fashionable interior is more expensive than everyone else in a hotel (from 5500 rubles). Guests are given the opportunity to sleep in a comfortable large bed, and you can relax in the evening in the area with leather sofas watching TV. The hotel includes breakfast, which can be ordered in the room.

Reviews

A large number of reviews from guests indicates that the hotel is visited, and it is interesting to many customers. A good location for transit transport makes this place for tourists who follow the direction of Crimea very convenient.

In their reviews, the guests did not agree on a hotel. Some talk about boorish and defiant behavior of administrators. And someone is the other way around.

Guests note that at the time of booking it was indicated that the rooms are spacious with all amenities. As it turned out, there was no hot water and this was reported only upon arrival. The rooms are small and have an unpleasant odor.

There are customers who are satisfied with the room with a kitchenette. They note that you can cook for yourself on it, although breakfast is included. The hotel provides all the necessary things for a comfortable stay. I liked the food in the restaurant. It was quiet and calm.

Guests in the reviews say that the Sputnik Hotel (Voronezh) is an ideal place to relax from the road. The hotel is located near the highway in the forest zone. There are no shops and cafes nearby (this can be a minus for someone). The rooms are not as large as it seems from the photos on the site. The service is also not top notch. In order to spend the night and take a shower, the hotel is quite suitable.

Hotel Sputnik (Voronezh): vacancies

As in any other hotel, the Sputnik hotel always has free working positions. Administrators, cleaning service, waiters and cooks - often these vacancies are free. In order to get a job in this hotel, you must meet all the requirements. All personnel are tested for professional suitability, as well as for the quality performance of their duties.

Conclusion

In Voronezh, you can find many places to relax. Most tourists who come to see the city prefer to settle in the center or near the train stations. Hotel Sputnik (Voronezh) is more suitable for those traveling along the nearest highway or for companies who want to celebrate a celebration in a hotel restaurant. Many guests choose this hotel because of the spacious conference rooms in which they conduct negotiations and meetings, and then accommodate visiting guests in the apartments of the hotel.
