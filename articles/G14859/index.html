How to make a voodoo doll at home? Practical tips

The fact that there is such a mystical teaching as voodoo, many of our compatriots know from Hollywood films. There, the old Creoles curse the unlucky white travelers, pick up the dead from the land, turn into obedient but bloodthirsty zombies those who succumbed to their charms. And one of the most powerful artifacts is the voodoo doll. With its help, you can control human life and even death.

Manufacturing process

Surely, many have thought about how to make a voodoo doll at home? Let us turn to the experts on esotericism for an answer to this question. As the magicians say, there are two main ways to make a voodoo doll at home. The first one is more traditional. To implement it, you need to take two small sticks.



You will also need threads, a fabric such as burlap or canvas, some natural stuff for stuffing. Straw, dry grass, moss or flower petals are quite suitable. How to make a voodoo doll? Before creation, it is necessary to clearly and thoroughly imagine the person or the spirit whose personification it will be.

So let's get started. First you need to make a "skeleton". We knit a cross from two sticks so that one is divided in half, and the second in a 1: 2 ratio. So, the first will become the future hands of the doll, and the second - its body and head. The second stage comes in the master class.

Our skeleton will be overgrown with muscles - tightly wrap a wooden cross with straw, grass or moss. If you use the latter option, try not to break it - the integrity of the moss is necessary for the ritual. After this, it is necessary to tighten the fabric over the doll and cover it with cloth. On the head, you can leave a little filler to simulate hair, and at the ends of the hands - for the image of the palms. Now our voodoo doll, made at home, is almost ready.



Further, for greater personalization, and, as a result, to enhance the effect of magic, you can give it the features of a specific person. To do this, you can sew her clothes. Especially valuable will be something made from his former thing. You never know, maybe you have a T-shirt or a piece of cloth lying around from the outfit of this or that whom the doll will designate.

You can also give personality to the face. To do this, sew buttons, eyes, denote charcoal or pencil, or even lipstick, mouth and eyebrows. It is especially important to depict the heart on the doll‚Äôs body. Also, to enhance the effect, you can sew beads, amulets or pieces of hair on the person you imagined while doing it.

Another variant

The second way to make a voodoo doll at home is more modern. To do this, you need fabric, a photo of a person, and also a filler. Print a photo on the printer in A4 format, and transfer it to the fabric or simply stick it. Cut the figure along the contour.



Then, make the second one the same silhouette, and sew both parts together. Leave a small gap to fill in the figure to add volume. Next, we follow the previous scenario to personalize our dolls.

Conclusion

Many people mistakenly believe that voodoo rituals are designed to harm a person. But this is not so. Any magic can be oriented towards good.

And therefore, to conduct such a ritual to attract good luck, love or wealth to a person, it is enough to choose pins with bright tops - red, if this is a romantic relationship, green - in order to attract money, yellow - to provide positive emotions.

But it is better to avoid black pins - after all, bad thoughts about people and wishes of negativity can return a hundredfold to the caster himself.

Now you know how to make a voodoo doll at home. Good luck
