Reserve formation

After analyzing the reviews of people who understood how a family can live economically on a small salary and save money, as well as those who are just mastering the savings, we can conclude that the fundamental point is the mandatory formation of reserve cash.

The creation of a reserve is a prerequisite, every month it is necessary to save 5-10% of the salary in your own fund. This is a kind of ‚Äúuntouchable reserve‚Äù, it will be a defensive front in case of unforeseen circumstances. If the stock was not spent in the previous month, you should not attribute it to the income of the coming period. On the contrary, continue to add 5-10% of earnings to the reserve fund every month.

No wonder the reserves of entire states are thus formed. Continuing in the same vein, after a few months you can afford to buy an expensive thing, and picking up a little more, save up for a vacation with a small salary or make cosmetic repairs in the apartment.

Save on Products

Most buyers are familiar with the situation when they went into a store for bread and went out with a full cart of products, and not of prime necessity. Saving on products is quite real, and it is not necessary to switch to low-quality food or starve, a reasonable approach to business is important.

How to live on a small salary, buying products:

  1. Proper nutrition. Refusal from semi-finished products will not only save money, but also preserve health, the same can be said about fast food. Purchased rolls and donuts can be replaced with dairy products or baked on their own, an alternative to meat and sausage semi-finished products is boiled or baked beef roll, and dried fruits can be sweets.
  2. Menu for the week. A planned menu for the week excludes options when you need to run into the store for just one ingredient and come home with a full package of products. In addition, excess products will not deteriorate, therefore, money is spent correctly.
  3. Grocery list. Writing a list of necessary things is a very good habit. Firstly, it will save you from impulsive spending, and secondly, there will be no need to return for a particular product, and it is already known how it ends.
  4. Cash or card. Numerous surveys revealed that people are more likely to part with money by paying with a card, because they do not have visual and tactile contact with money. However, payment with a card gives many advantages in the form of discounts and bonuses, therefore it is not worth excluding it at all. No matter how you pay, you need to keep checks and keep records.
  5. Shopping on payday. Most people, having received an advance or a salary, tend to make purchases on the same day. It is better to postpone this in order to cool down a little and plan the costs for the coming period.
  6. Who should go shopping? According to some statements, men behave more rationally when shopping, but many women can argue with this. Not all men study the price tags and often take the goods without having familiarized themselves with the price, or buy the wrong products that are required by the wife in the kitchen. Communal payments

With the right consumption of resources, you can save on monthly payments for utilities. How to save money with a small salary, saving on utility deductions:

  1. It is better to make tea or coffee with water from a thermos.
  2. The refrigerator should stand as far as possible from heating appliances and direct sunlight.
  3. Cooking on an electric stove is better using dishes with a thick bottom. So the heat is stored longer, and the preparation of some dishes is allowed, just before they are fully cooked.
  4. When buying household appliances, you should pay attention to the energy class.
