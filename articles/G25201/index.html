Hotel "Silver Dream" (Gubakha, Perm Territory): address, reviews

Increasingly, people tend to relax in complexes where they are provided with comfortable living conditions and a wide variety of entertainment services. Tourists want to get maximum positive emotions during their vacation. The "Silver Dream" in Gubakh gives you the opportunity to enjoy beautiful landscapes, actively relax and then relax in a comfortable environment after a busy day.

It provides everything you need for the convenience of both adults and children. The service is at a fairly high level. Entire business companies stay here to spend active free time at a ski resort.

"Silver Dream" (Gubakha): address, mode of operation

The entertainment center is located near the Rudyansky spoy mountain complex. "Silver Dream" is located in the Perm region, in Gubakh. The complex itself is located on the street. Zhdanova, 24. Nearby are shops and small retail outlets.





The hotel is open throughout the year. In any season, guests feel comfortable and find something to do. You can pay for the room in cash, by credit card or by money transfer.

Accommodations

The hotel has 42 rooms with different levels of comfort. Economy rooms are block-based. This means that for several rooms one bathroom and a shower are provided. This apartment has two single beds, bedside tables, chairs. TV Triple rooms are equipped with the same system. But differ in the presence of three beds.





Junior Suite consists of one room. It provides a double bed, bedside tables, wardrobe, large mirror, TV, dishes, electric kettle. The room is equipped with a private bathroom and bath.

The suite consists of two rooms. The bedroom has a large bed, TV, bedside tables, wardrobe. The living room has upholstered furniture, dishes, an electric kettle. The room has its own bath and toilet. Terry towels and personal care products are provided.

All guests of the "Silver Dream" in Gubakh can use the free internet in the hotel. Access password must be requested at the reception.

Nutrition

The hotel often stops skiers who come here on vacation. Therefore, the restaurant "Silver Dream" in Gubakh provides 3 meals a day for tourists at affordable prices. The menu includes a variety of hot dishes that give you the opportunity to warm up after skiing.

The guests of the complex are also offered a "continental breakfast", which includes the main course (cereals, fried eggs, cheesecakes) and a dessert with aromatic coffee or tea. The average price for it is 170-200 rubles per person.

In the evening, tourists can dine in the common room, choosing dishes from the main menu. At this time, the institution often hosts various show programs with performances by artists.

Service

At the Silver Dream Hotel, coolers with cold and hot water are installed on each floor in the corridors. Guests can use them at any time. And also on the floors where the economy class rooms are located in the halls are electric kettles and refrigerators.





The hotel has a small laundry room. There you can wash personal items and iron them for an additional fee. Rooms are cleaned regularly. Reception staff are on duty around the clock.

The hotel allows children with extra beds. Check-in and eviction are carried out at any time of the day.

Entertainment

In the entertainment complex in Gubakh, Perm Territory, a number of various services are provided for guests. There is a spacious bowling club on the ground floor. It is equipped with several modern paths and there are places for recreation. Meals from the restaurant menu can be delivered here.

The complex also has a billiards room. It has several large modern tables. At the disposal of vacationers all the necessary accessories.

The tourist center "Silver Dream" has a large fitness room. It can hold several trainings during your stay at the hotel or buy a subscription for regular visits. The gym has modern fitness equipment and there is the necessary sports equipment.

Hotel guests can spend their evenings playing table tennis. The entertainment complex has several large tables. All necessary equipment for the game is leased.

Leisure

In the "Silver Dream" (Gubakha) all conditions for playing sports are created. Volleyball and five-a-side football courts are equipped here. They have a special modern coating that makes it possible to engage in sports games throughout the year.





Near the complex operates a ski resort. The slopes of varying difficulty are equipped here. Tourists often come here to ski. Therefore, a hotel near the resort will be a great place for lovers of sports and outdoor activities.

"Silver Dream" (Gubakha): negative reviews

On various sites there are a large number of comments regarding the operation of the complex. Many of them are negative. First of all, guests are not satisfied with the quality of soundproofing in the rooms. They claim that in the evening, music is heard in the rooms that comes from the restaurant. And also in the rooms you can clearly hear what the other guests in the corridor are talking about.

Tourists are outraged by the fact that skiing equipment cannot be entered in the room. The administration justifies its position on this issue by the fact that during the transfer of accessories, floors are damaged.





Guests of the complex are forced to leave their expensive equipment near the battery in the lobby of the hotel. Thus, personal belongings are in danger of being stolen. Tourists note that for the safety of inventory, you have to agree on how to leave it in the utility room, but you will have to pay extra for this.

Often there are negative comments about broken plumbing. Vacationers complain that in the economy class rooms pallets leak in showers and after water procedures the floor is always wet and slippery, it is dangerous to get up on it.

According to tourists' reviews, televisions are installed in the rooms, but, unfortunately, it shows only 2 channels in normal quality. And also the speed of the Internet leaves much to be desired.

Positive reviews

On the Web you can find good comments regarding the hotel. Tourists note that the rooms are always warm and clean. Bed linen is in good condition and has no unpleasant odor.

According to vacationers, breakfast in the hotel restaurant serves nutritious and delicious. Almost all tourists are satisfied with the opportunity to relax in the bath after an active day. And also there are always fans to play bowling or billiards.

Tourists note that the staff is friendly and of high quality. Families with children claim that they were provided with an extra bed for a small surcharge without any problems.





The complex is always clean and comfortable. In winter, snow is cleared on time and tourists can easily get to the entrance. Vacationers are pleased that reservations for hotel stays on holidays begin in advance. Already a few months before the New Year, you can order a room and calmly wait for the arrival of a winter weekend, without worrying that there will be no empty seats these days.
