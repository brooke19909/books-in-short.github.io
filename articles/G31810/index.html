Beeline: unlimited Internet tariff for modem

Mobile Internet is quite popular not only among owners of tablet PCs and smartphones. Beeline SIM card is actively used by many subscribers in modems. On what conditions is the Internet provided in this case? What is the monthly fee? Is it possible to connect unlimited Internet on the Beeline number? In this article, these issues will be discussed in more detail.





Modem Internet

When using a SIM card in a modem, you can refuse to connect various options for unlimited Internet. However, in this case, it is worth considering that the cost of one megabyte of data will be quite high - 3.30 rubles. For a modem, it is more profitable to use specialized Beeline options. Unlimited Internet, in the full sense of the word, the subscriber will not be able to receive, at least with a speed acceptable for work and communication. But in the list of options available for connection, you can choose the most optimal conditions.





Tariff plan "Internet forever"

Such a tariff is available for all SIM cards that are planned to be used in a Beeline modem. ‚Äù Unlimited Internet is not provided on it. It is understood that the client will choose the option that will be more interesting and beneficial for him when using the Internet. "Internet forever" does not have a monthly fee and monthly on it two hundred megabytes are charged to the client. They can be used at no extra cost. An unpleasant fact is that for modems such a rule does not apply. Only owners of tablets that use a SIM with this TP can use this "bonus".

Highway Options Series

Beeline unlimited Internet traffic is provided as part of the Highway line options. The client can choose the service he likes according to the conditions and activate it on the tariff ‚ÄúInternet forever‚Äù. In the list of available for connection the following options:





  • a package of eight gigabytes - on it a monthly fee of six hundred rubles is charged monthly;
  • a package of twelve gigabytes can be connected for seven hundred rubles per month;
  • a package of twenty gigabytes is provided per subscriber. a fee of one thousand two hundred rubles.

The principle of using options is the same: within the billing period, the client can use the traffic set by the connected option. In this case, the remaining megabytes / gigabytes will not be transferred to the next month. On the day the subscription fee is charged (what happens on the day the service is connected), unlimited Beeline traffic will be provided in full. At any time, if there is not enough connected traffic, you can reconnect the option by choosing the most optimal option.

Highway options are also fairly common among owners of gadgets such as a smartphone and tablet. After all, with their help you can connect unlimited Internet. Traffic extension

In a situation if traffic within the connected option is spent, and the billing period has not come to an end, an auto-renewal package will be automatically connected. The additional package implies a size of one hundred and fifty megabytes. Twenty rubles are debited for connecting the package. In this case, the speed will not be changed. Additional packages will be connected until:

  • there will not be enough money on the balance to write off for the next hundred and fifty megabytes;
  • a new volume of traffic provided at the beginning of the billing period is activated;
  • Internet usage will not stop.

Also, the purchase of additional traffic can be carried out through the option ‚ÄúProlong speed‚Äù. They are connected at the request of the client on the following conditions:

  • a package of four gigabytes (five hundred rubles);
  • one gigabyte packet (two hundred and fifty rubles).




Each of these packages connects for 30 days. Moreover, if the traffic was spent earlier, then the shutdown occurs automatically. A similar situation occurs if a monthly fee is charged and a new amount of traffic is connected according to the main option.

Disable Auto Renew

Beeline reserves the right to the client to refuse the option of auto-renewal and leave only the Internet unlimited. The Beeline modem at the moment when the main traffic is finished in this case will provide a speed of no more than 64 Kb / s. You can refuse auto-renew by typing * 115 * 230 #. Conclusion

For owners of such devices, the black-and-yellow operator offers two options for use: with paid traffic and unlimited Internet. In the first case, the client does not have to additionally activate any options. With the Internet forever tariff, the cost of a megabyte will always be the same.
