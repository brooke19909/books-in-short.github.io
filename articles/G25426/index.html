Amazing Adventures: Sky Park, Kazan

The most valuable memories throughout the life of a person remain about new impressions and unusual sensations. Especially if they were received in the company of friends or relatives. In search of such significant moments it is not necessary to travel far or abroad. On the territory of Russia there are a lot of places worthy to remain in memory in the form of photographs in family albums.

Kazan is a city of amazing sights

Kazan is one of the cities of Russia, whose sights are gathered by people from all over the country. This is one of the oldest cities with a long history. It offers connoisseurs of thousands of architectural, cultural and natural attractions. Arriving even for a couple of weekends, each guest can get a lot of unforgettable impressions.





There are many entertainment places in the city that are open to visitors all year round. Clubs, bowling alleys, cinemas, attractions, parks, an aqua park and even an aquarium are all that can be interesting after hours of viewing architectural monuments.

Offers an active family holiday

The best for both active family vacations and entertainment with a fun company in the fresh air can be Sky Park. Kazan is not only ancient monuments and perfectly clean streets.

The city is located on one of the banks of the Volga. Almost in half it is divided by the Kazanka River. In the urban area there are many large and small lakes, the largest of which are:

  • group of water bodies - Lower, Middle and Upper Boar;
  • Lake Lebyazhye;
  • Deep;
  • Blue.




The suburbs are distinguished by dense forests. This is all a wonderful occasion to leave the stone jungle of the city and relax, enjoying the sound of foliage and the singing of birds. Unforgettable impressions and a full-fledged active vacation are offered by Sky Park (Kazan).

"Swan" is a sports complex. It is located within the city forest park of the same name, the natural zone of which is protected by the state. The recreation center offers its guests all the gifts of nature:

  • clean air filled with forest scents;
  • the smell of needles and the sound of wind in pines, which have grown over the centuries by many meters up;
  • the opportunity to swim in the crystal clear waters that fill Lake Lebyazhye and Glubokoe.

"Sky Park", Kazan: an unforgettable vacation with friends or family
