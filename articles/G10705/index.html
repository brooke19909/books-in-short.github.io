Wireless Phone Battery - Review, Features and Reviews

In this article, we consider options for external batteries that are in demand. What are their features and what to look for?

Choosing an external battery

Cordless batteries for the phone (prices can be seen below) are divided among themselves into three criteria: by capacity, by type of battery and outgoing current.

Most of the devices are made of plastic. If the options are expensive, then they are created from metal and with a waterproof surface. But such a device is more suitable for those who often go camping and so on. For everyday use, completely different options are used. For an ordinary person who needs to recharge his device, Power Bank made of plastic is quite suitable.

External wireless batteries for the phone are made for both all models and for individual devices. They have such a design that they can be attached to the body of the device. However, you need to understand that such devices are much more expensive than conventional universal batteries. The latter are noticeably better: they can be used with almost all devices that have a standard port for connecting a USB cable.

A good external battery should be at least 2500 mAh. This figure is suitable for phones. But what to do with tablets? For them, it is better to purchase a model with a capacity of 5 thousand mAh. These minimum requirements will allow you to charge most of the smartphones to 100% at least once.

The selection of the battery is also affected by the current strength supplied by the phone. As a rule, this indicator varies from 1 to 2 Amperes. Each charger has a special sticker on which all the characteristics of the device are painted. The necessary voltage is also indicated there.

How is the charging process carried out? It is enough to connect your phone with a cord and the charging process will immediately begin. Some battery models must first be turned on using a special button. The battery itself is charged automatically. It must be "fed" with the same cord from the outlet. The most popular and best models will be considered later in the article.
