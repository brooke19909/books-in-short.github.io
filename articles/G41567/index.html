Crocheted Teddy Bear: Workshop

Among knitted toys, cute, tender teddy bears are popular. In the classic version, Teddy is dominated by gray-blue gamma. Although at the moment you can find different colors of bears. So, let‚Äôs take a look at several workshops on crochet knitting cubs.

Teddy Bear - Amiguri

Amiguri are very small toys. Their size can barely reach the baby‚Äôs little finger or fit in the adult palm. What will require from the needlewoman:

  • thin hook;
  • yarn;
  • synthetic winterizer.

Make a drawing first. On the sheet, draw Teddy in full height with all parts of the body.

So the size of the paws, head, ears, nose, tail will be visible. If the form is not visible, then draw each detail separately, maintaining the dimension. Next, each part is crocheted. Teddy Bear will turn out no worse than the scheme.

Start knitting your head. From two loops, knit six columns. Then add two loops to these six elements. So to the fifth row you get thirty loops. Next, knit four rows with the same composition (without adding). The next three rows cut the columns in two loops.

Thus, the twelfth row consists of twelve loops. Fill your head with a synthetic winterizer. Reduce the six columns, tighten the loop. Please note that if the Teddy bear is crocheted and sewn, then you work according to the scheme without changes. If it is on the mounts, then insert the cotter pins to a sixfold decrease and close the loops.



Paw knitting

The hind legs are shorter than the front. Gather four loops, knit eight columns. Now leave their number unchanged for three more rows. The fifth circle is reduced to two columns. Next, knit six stitches three times without changes, stuff with syntepon. If the coat is thick, then filler is not needed.

If you want a rolling bear, then insert the mount at this stage. Then, in a circle, make gradual decrease in two loops until you close your paw. Also knit the second part.

Now form thin forepaws. Pick up two loops. In a circle, increase two loops. So the third row consists of six columns. Then reduce one loop, knit the next five circles on five columns.

On the ninth circle, stuff the part with a padding polyester, insert the mount if you have a moving Teddy bear. Crochet further reduce the loop until the last one closes. Also knit a second paw. Thread trims are hidden inside the part.



Torso and assembly

Now go to the torso. Close two loops, double them in a circle, knit six single crochet columns. By the fourth row you get twenty-four columns. Now lengthen the waist by knitting these twenty-four columns four more rows. At this stage, attach the hind legs to the body.

Now, knitting will not go in a circle, but in the direct and reverse order to get a hole from the back. To do this, gain an air loop, knit back with six diminution. So in the tenth circle there will be eighteen columns. Attach the front legs.

The next circle is again reduced by six stitches, and the next two rows knit twelve columns unchanged. So continue until the loops are closed. Fasten your head, stuff the body with a synthetic winterizer, sew a hole with a hidden seam. Now tie the ears, tail randomly and attach to the body. In the eye area, make a string with a thread, glue the beads, embroider a black nose on the resulting ‚Äúknoll‚Äù. The result was a cute knitted teddy bear (crochet).



Simple Teddy with an extended muzzle

A bear can be connected in a simplified way. To do this, knit your body like a regular napkin with simple columns. You increase the loops until you are satisfied with the bottom of the body. Further knitting goes unchanged until the part is rounded and the shape of the abdomen is outlined. Now you can reduce, forming an oval-pear-shaped body. Stuff it, close the loop with a hook (Teddy bear is knitted from thin, soft yarn, so choose a synthetic winterizer or holofiber).

Paws are also knitted in a wide circle, proceeding to decrease loops. In this case, the packing goes in parallel with knitting. The shape should form paws that look like a long pear or a rattle. Sew them to the body.

Start your head with your nose. That is, they tied an elongated ‚Äúhat‚Äù, then start a double-triple increase in order to proceed to knitting the head. Fill with padding polyester, do weight lifting, embroider eyes, nose. Tie your ears separately, sew to the head. Fix all the parts together. Tie a scarf around your neck.



Unusual teddy bear crochet: master class

The needlewomen are constantly experimenting on color, yarn, paw shape, accessories to get the original teddy bear. Consider how to tie a festive teddy.

  1. The body begins with two loops. Knit six columns in a circle. Next, increase in each row six loops. By the fourth row you get thirty loops. Without changes, knit five circles, proceed to the removal of ten loops. So the tenth row consists of twenty columns. Next, work with them without changes four rows. In the fifteenth circle, cut five loops, stuff with padding polyester. Then again, reduce the five columns, close the remaining.
  2. The hind legs are knitted with six loops. Crochet twelve single crochet stitches. Teddy Bear will look impressive with heels, hearts. To do this, simply tie a circle of three rows with cap and connecting posts. If you want to leave simple paws, then increase the loop to eighteen and twenty two. You knit two more rows without changes. Then make a decrease in a circle of one, five, four loops. You knit six rows without changes, fill them with padding polyester, close the remaining twelve loops.

Continuation of knitting an unusual bear

  1. Front legs start with four loops. You knit eight columns in them. Then add four more loops. The resulting twelve columns in two circles are knitted without changes. Decreasing one loop in the fifth row, keep this number for another four circles. Next, reduce the circle to nine loops, the next four rows save this number. Next, reduce three columns, fill with padding polyester, tighten the loops.
  2. You start to knit a head from a nose from two loops. They knit six columns in a circle. In the second row, add up to fifteen loops. With them, you work without changes for two circles. At this stage, due to the connecting posts , the thread can be replaced in order to highlight the nose with color. If there is a one-color, knitted Teddy bear, crochet from the grass, knit another row of fifteen columns. Next, increase the number of loops to twenty-four, then to thirty. Five rows work unchanged with these loops. Then, in the next three circles, reduce five loops, getting fifteen columns. Without closing the knitting, sew the bead eyes, eyebrows, nose.

Toy assembly

  1. Knit ears from two loops. First, knit six single crochet into them. Then knit a row without changes. End with connecting posts. There is another option for knitting the ear. Knit a circle, bend in half, tighten in two places, giving the desired bend.
  2. Knit a tail arbitrarily, like a ball. Fill with synthetic winterizer, tighten the loop, sew to the body.
  3. Sew ears to the head, paws to the body. Connect the received parts among themselves. Add a heart, and the Teddy bear is crocheted (a description of the accessory is not required, since the heart can be sewn or bought in needlework stores).

Another option for creating an unusual image is the "outfits" of the bear. You can embroider classic scars and patches, sew clothes, decorate with knitted patterns, combine bright shades of wool, unusual yarn. Experiment with eye and nose fittings to express sadness, sadness, joy, surprise, shyness on the face.



Little tip

To get the original knitted Teddy bear (crochet), a description of the scheme is not necessary. But color photography is needed when selecting materials, knitting parts and embroidering lines. The original has a whole bear gray, a tail with a muzzle of white color, a blue nose, black scars. As for the texture of the yarn, combine weed with cotton or Angora wool.
