Cleansing the whole body

Sauna is a certain room with dry and wet steam. The high temperatures allowed for the human body allow you to sweat, as a result of which pollution and dust come out from the surface of the skin with sweat. Due to this, the pores open and the epidermis is cleansed.





You can distinguish ordinary saunas and infrared. In ordinary ones, air is warmed up, and in infrared, various materials are used for heating: hydrocarbon fibers, charcoal and the like. Saunas are called Finnish, as they are one of the main symbols of Finland. It is not surprising that in this country every house has its own sauna.
