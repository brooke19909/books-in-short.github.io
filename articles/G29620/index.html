Tourist base "Shtykovsky Ponds"

Base Features

The landscape design of this place is impressive. Neat green lawns, lots of trees and shrubs, original garden furniture made of natural materials and winding stone paths - all this awaits guests in the courtyard of the Shtykovsky Prudy recreation center.





Wooden cottages and small houses that are offered to guests as housing, quite naturally and harmoniously fit into the overall picture of the landscape.

Accommodation and facilities

Those who wish to stay in this place can stay in the special houses mentioned above. They are located in the Berry Village, which was built in the park of the Shtykovsky Prudy base. Accommodation prices are calculated for almost any wallet.





Among the housing offered there are simple one-room houses with so-called ‚Äúvillage‚Äù amenities, as well as comfortable cottages with a separate kitchen, dining room, terrace, sauna and living room. What to choose - it's up to the guests only!

Local entertainment

On the territory of the Shtykovsky Prudy base there is a whole complex of workshops that are aimed at both children and adults. Here everyone will be able to learn the intricacies of pottery, felting from wool, as well as bending, fusing and making the original author's postcard. And kids can enjoy finger-drawing.

Shtykovsky Ponds Park is a great place to have fun! A large open space away from busy highways where you can play active games, go to the playground and just breathe fresh air and relax with the whole family.



In addition, in the warmer months, guests can relax by the local lake, ride a boat, play futsal or badminton and enjoy fishing.

In winter, guests can enjoy skiing, tubing tracks and an ice rink. In any weather, guests can use the local bathhouse.

Nutrition

On the territory of the base there is a cafe "Shtykovskie Prudy". Here, tourists will find a small room for 40 people, excellent cuisine and friendly staff. Here you can either have a bite to eat or get a full lunch or dinner. For younger guests, the cafe provides special chairs and a children's stool for the washbasin.

Well, guests of the local Berry Village will be able to order lunch right in their house or cottage.



This place is definitely worth a visit for those who are tired of the daily bustle of the big city, work, noise, dust and city life in general. Adults will be able to unwind, relax and get a boost of strength, and children will play enough and get a lot of safe space for active games. In addition, they will simply be interested in visiting local workshops and participating in exciting activities. So the rest here will be unforgettable for both adults and kids!
