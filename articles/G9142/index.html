Holiday Village "Manor Volzhino"

On the banks of the picturesque Volga River, there are about a hundred tourist camps, resorts, boarding houses and children's camps, which provide an opportunity to relax and spend time with health benefits. In summer, people are attracted by sandy beaches, in winter - Volga fishing, for which lovers of fishing rod come here from many Russian regions.





"Volzhino Estate" (Saratov) is located on the very banks of the Volga. Natural beauties are combined here with modern comfort and high quality service. The company organizes an unforgettable vacation for tourists and gives them a lot of positive emotions, in addition, the hostel offers services for organizing weddings and banquets, conducting business meetings and seminars.

"Volzhino Estate"

More recently, the leaders of the recreation center carried out its complete reconstruction, which is why it has become even more cozy and comfortable. A successful architectural solution allowed to place everything that is needed for a good rest on a noticeably increased territory. Local color combined with urban comfort will allow each guest to connect with nature at any time of the year.

Number of rooms

On the territory of Volzhino you can settle in a large guest cottage with two-room and one-room suites or rent one of the houses from glued beams. All offered rooms are equipped with modern furniture and appliances, air-conditioned rooms are equipped with showers and bathrooms.





In the evenings, you can sit by the fireplace or sing karaoke in the cozy fireplace room located on the ground floor of the complex.

Nutrition

Manor Volzhino offers a restaurant and bar on its territory. The restaurant has an excellent menu, which includes dishes of traditional, oriental and European cuisine. The bar offers a wide range of drinks and snacks. For those who want to cook a barbecue or barbecue on their own, barbecues are located on the territory of the recreation center.





Infrastructure

The Volzhino Estate (Saratov) has a developed infrastructure, which includes an open-air swimming pool with heated water, sunbeds, a small and a large pontoon, a tennis court, a sandy beach, barbecue areas, a tandoor, gazebos, and a guarded parking lot.





The special pride of the complex is the Russian bath: a steam room with a stone stove (in winter it is a real ice hole). Bathhouse decoration - wooden furniture, necessary equipment, bar. On the street barbecue, there is an outdoor area and a covered terrace. Each guest is provided with bath accessories: towel, sheet, slippers. Real honey and herbal tea are also offered free of charge.

In addition to the Russian bath, Volzhino also has a Finnish steam room and Turkish sauna with a relatively low temperature. Soft steam with aromas of mint and eucalyptus will not only cheer you up, but also improve your body.

Rest in Volzhino with children

Volzhino Estate will appeal not only to adults, but also to children of all ages. There is a well-groomed and clean green area, where children can play and expand their social circle, children's and sports grounds, children's room. A team of first-class animators works with the guys, who hold contests and relay races, organize master classes, sports programs for children, and board game tournaments. Animators individually communicate with each child and are able to find an approach to any baby. In winter, children can skate. Holidays with children at the tourist base "Volzhino Estate" is the best choice of caring dads and mothers!





Accommodation and prices

Children under two years are free of charge (a separate place is not provided).

  • Two-room VIP-room - from 4 100 rubles.

The price includes breakfast.

Reviews

Everyone who has visited this recreation center wants to return here again. Judging by the reviews, Volzhino has a very nice and polite staff, the restaurant serves delicious and beautifully decorated dishes. Many guests really like the convenient location of the complex (the Volzhino Estate camp site is 15 minutes from the city), the Volga coast, and beautiful nature. Parents having a rest with children also had only good memories about the base: quiet, comfortable, calm, a great vacation spot for all family members.

Among the minuses, guests distinguish only a bad road to the complex.
