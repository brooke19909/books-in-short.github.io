The five-star tourist complex Majestic Club Beach (Kemer) is a great place for a youth and romantic weekend. The hotel was founded in 1987, a few kilometers from the resort village of Goynuk, just 6 km from the center of Kemer and 50 km from Antalya Airport. Cozy two- and three-story villas and one main building, decorated in a national style, are built on a huge landscaped area.





During its existence, the Majestic Club Beach guesthouse (Kemer) has been reconstructed and ennobled more than once. Today it is a comfortable center with a rich internal infrastructure. Guests enjoy a relaxing holiday on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, surrounded by unspoiled nature, where eucalyptus, orange and pear trees grow. The hotel has its own travel agency, where you can book an interesting and informative tour of the historical sites of Turkey at the lowest cost.

There is a parking lot for private transport, shopping enthusiasts will find numerous pavilions concentrated around the entire perimeter of the well-groomed territory and beyond. You can have fun and plenty of dancing at local clubs. For active customers, the hotel offers a lot of opportunities and options for sports. Unforgettable water activities, daily animated shows and concert programs await young guests.





Guest Fund

Comfortable rooms of different categories are offered by the Majestic Club Kemer Beach complex. Reviews of tourists about the number of rooms, mostly positive, many noted the modern amenities and equipment of the apartments. From the balconies of all rooms you can enjoy a view of the flowering tropical park or the blue sea.

The premises have a convenient layout, including a seating area, a sleeping area and a separate bathroom with a set of hygiene and cosmetics. Amenities include an LCD TV with cable channels, a telephone line, air conditioning and a minibar.





Meal concept at the Majestic Club Beach Hotel (Kemer)

Guests staying at the hotel eat on an all-inclusive basis. The choice of dishes is varied and rich in fresh vegetables, fish and meat delicacies, oriental sweets. It features 5 restaurants, 4 bars and an internet cafe.

Beach

Just 300 m from the hotel building is a sand and pebble coast. It offers vacationers the necessary beach equipment and plenty of water entertainment for every taste. Everyone can rent a catamaran and ride on the water surface, admiring the beauties of wildlife.





Infrastructure

Athletes await at the Majestic Club Beach Hotel (Kemer) nightly entertainment with an interesting show program and all kinds of competitions. There is a gym with modern equipment. There is also a tennis court, a billiard room, playgrounds and minigolf.

Among the abundance of green spaces, outdoor pools with water slides and beach amenities are built. Visitors are offered wellness treatments at the spa. For meetings, negotiations and various events - a conference room and banquet facilities. Attentive and educated staff will be happy to assist in organizing celebrations.

There is a playground for young tourists. There is a developing club for children of different ages and a shallow pool. Parents can take advantage of babysitting services. In the afternoon, a professional group of animators works.
