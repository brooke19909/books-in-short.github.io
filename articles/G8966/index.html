Sanatorium association "Adlerkurort": reviews

One of the most famous health resorts in Sochi is the Adlerkurort health resort association. In addition to relaxation and a lot of entertainment, everyone is offered treatment and prevention of diseases of the heart, nerves, lungs, bronchi, bones, vision, genitourinary system.

brief information

Pension Adlerkurort is located in the resort town, on Lenin Street 219, in Sochi, at a distance of 50 meters from the beautiful beach.

On its territory there are buildings:

  • "Dolphin", "Frigate" and "Coral" (15 floors each);
  • "Neptune" and "Change" (5 floors each).

The rooms of the sanatorium have excellent medical equipment and talented, highly professional specialists. The spa work experience of this health resort is 50 years, which is a weighty argument when choosing where to buy vouchers.

Basically, Adlerkurort SKO uses natural healing programs: hydrotherapy, hydromassage, therapeutic massage, sea bathing, inhalation and so on.

On the territory there is a playground, a children's splash box for the youngest guests and a sports ground, a swimming pool for adults.





History

In 2017, the sanatorium complex celebrated its 50th anniversary! According to historical information, the foundation was laid in 1965, and the grand opening of the All-Union health resort "Adlerkurort" was carried out in June 1967.

Involved in its construction is Alexei Kosygin. It was he who signed the document, which makes it possible to carry out such a large-scale and noble task.

As for this sanatorium, the whole country watched its construction - there would be such a grandiose project on the Black Sea coast!





Huge pine trees that had to be removed once grew from the natural plantations on the territory of the current sanatorium. Then the trees were planted again and the buildings of the complex were built. By the way, some of the builders of the health resort after the completion of the construction and improvement works remained here to work as maintenance staff.

From the very beginning, Adlerkurort received very good reviews about his work. They came here from all over a huge country called the USSR - from Moscow, Novosibirsk, Arkhangelsk, Perm, St. Petersburg, Minsk, Kiev, Tashkent, Baku. We also came here from fraternal countries - the Czech Republic, Slovakia, Germany, Poland, Romania. And nowadays they are resting here from all over the world. The health resort accepts up to 44 thousand people a year.

In our time, the organization began to attach special importance to the organization of family vacations, for which his team was encouraged and awarded.

Prices for Adlerkurort

For a good rest on the territory of the Black Sea health resort, there is everything you need: sun, clean air, water, all kinds of procedures - aimed at prevention and promotion of health, comfortable rooms, excellent food, everything for a children's vacation.

As for the cost, the prices of the sanatorium are acceptable, compared to other resorts - 15% lower.

It is important to note that the prices for tickets to this health resort depend on which building tourists are accommodated in. The cheapest accommodation in Smena. Here, the minimum payment for a room in the wellness program is 4130 rubles, and the maximum is 5279 rubles per day. The most expensive accommodation in the Dolphin building. Here for a similar number you have to pay from 4630 to 5990 rubles per day. The prices for vouchers also depend on the season and on what program tourists go to the health resort, for general health or general therapeutic, which involves obtaining physical procedures.

When booking a room, it is advisable to check its cost with the administrators of the health resort, because during certain periods promotions are held here that can also change prices.
