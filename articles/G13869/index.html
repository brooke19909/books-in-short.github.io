Rest houses, Zvenigorod - rest to your liking

One of the oldest cities of the Moscow Region, Zvenigorod, is proud of the beauty of its protected places and the world-famous memorable places of ancient Russian architecture.

Tourists like Moscow and its region, as well as other regions of our country, love these places. In addition to the unique nature and monuments of architecture and architecture, in Zvenigorod the cleanest air and high sky, giving a sense of flight.



Modern Zvenigorod is the proximity of the capital, thoughtful infrastructure and the cradle of Russian Orthodoxy. Naturally, guests are waiting for a holiday home. Zvenigorod is a year-round resort city.

"Russian Switzerland"

So the guests call the rest house "Lipki" (Zvenigorod). It snugly fit into the forest park zone surrounding the city. The natural beauty is emphasized by the guesthouse's winter garden. Under the arches of the rotunda, complementing each other and inviting guests to a fairy tale, there are a fountain and amazing plants. There are secluded places to relax and live music almost always sounds.

Meals are organized according to the "buffet" system and are included in the price of the ticket. Children's menus can be ordered separately. Also, for guests of the holiday home (Zvenigorod), the Under the Dome bar, the restaurant of European and Russian cuisine Fires of the Moscow Region, and the phytobar are open almost around the clock.



Guests are accommodated in apartments of various classes: from economy to luxury. There is a special room for honeymooners.

Great leisure facilities

In Lipki there is, for example, a spa complex. A full range of relaxing treatments is available. These are various types of massage, bathtubs, individually selected for each client spa courses or a course of hydrothermal procedures. They did not forget about the baths in the rest house. There are three of them: a hammam, a Finnish and an infrared sauna. The pride of the whole complex is three huge pools: a children's, an adult and a special one for hydromassage.

There are many leisure options. For outdoor activities outdoor areas with exercise equipment or fields for football, tennis and volleyball are prepared (there are equipment at the rental points). In the buildings several halls with exercise machines are open. Exciting games with animators await children. Young people are happy to relax in nightclubs and many indoor barbecue areas for picnics.

Free Wi-Fi is available in public areas.



In "Lipki" you can organize any major event: from a large corporate holiday to a Caucasian wedding.

Pension with noble roots

In the picturesque surroundings of Moscow, guests will find a rest house "Ershovo" (Zvenigorod). It was located in the estate, which once belonged to the count family of the Olsufievs. Here are cozy shady alleys and large flower beds. Ornate bridges are thrown over many ponds scattered around the manor park. Peace and quiet after the bustle of the city set up for a relaxing holiday.



For those who want to spend an active vacation or weekend, in Ershovo there are also many opportunities. There is, for example, a large indoor pool for adults, two rooms: a gym and a sports one. As well as a billiard area, a dance floor and a small cozy movie theater. There is karaoke in the music room. For those who do not accept lazy rest, in the "Ershovo" there is a zone for playing billiards and ping pong. In the warm season, badminton, basketball, indoor soccer and volleyball courts, as well as a tennis court, are open.

Taking care of every guest

All holiday homes (Zvenigorod) are a spiritual rest near the water, picking mushrooms and berries, horseback riding, bicycles, ATVs, segways. Just like any sports equipment, in Ershovo you can rent a catamaran or a rowing boat. In winter, these places are no less beautiful: ice skating on frozen ponds and skiing and hiking in the fairy forest.

Children's rest in "Ershovo" thought out thoroughly. There is a children's play area with swings, slides and other attractions. Hire of cycle cars, rollers and scooters is organized. But most of all, little guests love animators. They conduct developmental classes, various master classes and simply play their favorite children's games.

Guests are accommodated in rooms from economy to luxury. There are also several family large apartments.

More options

Rest houses (Zvenigorod) is a great opportunity to organize a holiday of any scale, whether it be a corporate party, anniversary or wedding. No less interesting here you can spend a vacation or weekend.

The cost of a ticket or organizing a banquet can be found on tourist portals or on the official websites of holiday homes. Here you can go on a 3D tour of the boarding house, book a room you like, find out what is included in the price of the ticket, what services are provided and get acquainted with the reviews of tourists who have already rested here.

Today, almost every holiday home in Zvenigorod with a swimming pool, gym, cinema, dance floor and other entertainment. Pension administrations organize excursion tours to local cultural and architectural monuments and various public events.
