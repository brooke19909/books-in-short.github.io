Manathai Surin Phuket 4 * (Thailand, Surin Beach): description, service, reviews

Resorts in Thailand are characterized by excellent, constantly evolving infrastructure. Countless many different hotels are scattered throughout their territory - cheap hostels, low-budget hotels, cozy houses, five-star complexes. It’s not necessary to list everything, but it’s not necessary either, since now I would like to touch upon only one option. And this is Manathai Surin Phuket.





Location Features

The hotel is located in a very advantageous place from a tourist point of view. It is important for every visitor that it is easy to get anywhere from the hotel in which he decided to stay. Well, Manathai Surin Phuket wins in this regard.

The large Plaza Surin Shopping Center can be reached in 2-3 minutes. Bliss Beach Club is approximately 15 minutes away. Tesco Lotus Supermarket is just 2.5 kilometers from the hotel. The popular Laguna Phuket Golf Club is 4.4 km away.

It is also worth noting that in just 10 minutes you can walk to Laem Sing, a little-known beach located in a small but very picturesque lagoon. Clean and calm sea, magnificent views of the jungle and mountains, the absence of annoying merchants ... If you want to be in such a place, then definitely worth going to this beach.

You can also quickly get to Kathu Falls, Kalim Bay, Phuket Fantasy Park and Khao Phra Theo National Park from the hotel. And also, importantly, to the international airport. It is only 15 kilometers away.

Beach

Manathai Surin Phuket is a short walk from Surin Beach. In length, it is slightly less than a kilometer, and is limited to huge stones in the water, which are hard to miss. The beach is wide, covered with sun loungers and umbrellas, which even in the midst of the swimming season are enough for all vacationers.

I am glad that the ebbs and flows in Surin Beach are not so pronounced as in the same Bang Tao, located in the neighborhood. And here is very pleasant, fine, delicate cream-colored sand. The sludge is absent, which ensures transparency and purity of water, which is not without reason called azure.

Cleanliness is constantly monitored here, regularly cleaning up the garbage and other consequences of tourist vacations. However, it is not difficult for the guests of the resort themselves to maintain the tidy condition of the beach, since there are enough waste baskets on it.





Service and Facilities

Manathai Surin Phuket Hotel offers its guests comfortable living conditions - on its territory there is everything that you may need during your vacation. There is free high-speed Internet, private parking, a beautiful and unusual pool with a sun terrace. In the lobby itself, guests are welcomed with a 24-hour reception. There is also a tour desk and a left-luggage office.

There is also a laundry room and a small business center with a meeting room. All guests can use the concierge service. A fresh press is also delivered daily for them.

It is worth noting that even guests with disabilities will feel comfortable in the hotel. There are rooms with all the necessary amenities.

By the way, the hotel staff speaks both Thai and English.

A wide range of services is offered, and here are just the most popular ones:

  • Masks with natural ingredients. Contribute to detoxification and deep cleansing of pores.

In addition to the above, in the SPA of the hotel they do manicure, pedicure, nail and eyelash extensions, depilation of all zones and much more. And everyone can get acquainted with the full list of procedures directly on the spot.

When is the best time to fly?

Phuket is always warm. The tropical monsoon climate reigns here, and this feature divides the year into two seasons - dry and wet. The first lasts from November to April, and the second from May to October. Tours to Thailand at any time of the year are in demand, but when is it really best to fly here?

Definitely not in the rainy season. Showers here are very strong, short-term (do not drag out longer than 30-40 minutes), and are amazingly frequent. Lulls do not last more than 15-20 minutes. Of course, during this period on the island there is extremely high humidity, which sometimes reaches 90%.

The rainy season begins in August, near the end of the month. In September there is a peak - this is the wettest month of the year. And everything subsides in October. Tours to Thailand in the second month of autumn are bought up especially actively. Why? Because the showers are retreating, the weather is normalizing, but hotel prices remain as low as in August and September.

High humidity is also observed in April and May. In June it is hot, 32-34 degrees, but showers often. Also during this period, monsoons bring winds that cool the air a little.

The best months to relax are January and February. The driest and sunniest of the year! And the sea, moreover, at this time is very calm, warm and clean.
