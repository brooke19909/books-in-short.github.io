Pearl Beach Hotel 4 * (United Arab Emirates, Umm Al Quwain): Travel Reviews

Choosing a place to spend a vacation, many tourists consider the United Arab Emirates as an option, because this country is suitable for almost any type of vacation, from excursions to shopping tours. By the way, one of the most popular resorts in the country is considered to be the city of Sharjah, in the vicinity of which the hotel complex Pearl Beach Hotel 4 * is located.

Of course, when going on a trip, you should find out more about the chosen place to stop, because no one wants the vacation to be spoiled by domestic turmoil. Where is the hotel complex Pearl Beach Hotel 4 * (UAE) located and what does it look like? Reviews, descriptions, information about additional amenities - these moments are interesting to many tourists.

Where to look for a hotel: location features





Of course, every experienced traveler first of all pays attention to the location of this or that hotel, because this factor is important when it comes to a truly comfortable stay. So where to look for the hotel complex Pearl Beach Hotel 4 *? Umm Al Quwain (Umm Al Quwain), a picturesque town near Sharjah - it is here, almost on the seashore, you can spend several days.

It is worth saying that the location is quite convenient, because there are restaurants, shopping centers and other attractions nearby. In just a few minutes you can get to Sharjah, and Dubai is not far away, but there is plenty of entertainment here. By the way, the international airport is about 40 minutes from the hotel building.

What the hotel looks like: a brief description of the infrastructure





Pearl Beach Hotel 4 * is a new hotel complex that can please its guests with modern architecture, freshly renovated, a high degree of comfort and, of course, a relaxed atmosphere. The hotel consists of the main building, which houses the reception, lounges, as well as some other public areas, and several separate, small bungalows located in a semicircle.

The hotel area is small, but faces the very shore of the sea. The yard is well-groomed and beautiful - neat lawns, flower beds, bright exotic shrubs, palm trees and other plants delight the eye everywhere.

Hotel Pearl Beach Hotel 4 * (Ajman, United Arab Emirates): room categories

This is a very small hotel, which consists of 23 spacious rooms. Of course, tourists can choose a room of a suitable category.

  • Superior Deluxe Suite - apartments consisting of a bedroom (separated from the rest of the room by a door), a living area with soft sofas, and a spacious terrace furnished with garden furniture. Suitable for 3-4 people.
  • Deluxe Studio - a double room with a high level of comfort. It offers guests a huge king-size bed, modern appliances, as well as a balcony, which offers breathtaking views of the beach and the sea.
  • Garden Studio - double rooms (one double or two single beds to choose from) with a large balcony overlooking the exotic garden.

Additional information and photos of rooms





As already mentioned, the hotel was recently completely renovated, so the rooms delight travelers with a new renovation. The rooms are spacious, with wooden floors, large windows and a huge terrace (or balcony), which is literally created for a perfect holiday.

The situation here is calm, the design is stylish - tourists are offered comfortable beds, upholstered furniture, spacious wardrobes. You can rely with a calm heart on the availability of modern and, most importantly, working equipment. Guests use air conditioning, watch their favorite satellite channels on the big TV screen. There is a telephone and a small safe.

Of course, the bedroom also comes with its own bathroom, equipped with modern fixtures. Here, hotel guests take a shower and relax in the bathroom (some rooms have it). Claims for the work of maids by tourists also do not arise - employees visit the rooms every day, leaving behind fresh towels.

Meals: what should hotel guests expect?





The hotel complex Pearl Beach Hotel 4 * has its own restaurant, which you can eat during the day. Breakfast is held in the form of a buffet (they should be ordered and paid in advance), and for lunch and dinner guests are free to choose their favorite dishes. By the way, guests are offered the most delicious delicacies of international, Mediterranean and Arabic cuisine. Reviews say that the menu is really good, despite the fact that for some tourists it seems a little monotonous.

During the day, guests are also expected in the snack bar - here they are offered aromatic coffee, chilled drinks, exotic cocktails and good alcohol. Light salads, snacks and fast food are served throughout the day.

Food is unlikely to be a problem, because you can eat outside the walls of the Pearl Beach Hotel 4. In the city, which can be reached by taxi or bus, there are many cafes and restaurants for every taste.

Holidays by the sea: beach amenities





Of course, every tourist who chooses this place to spend a vacation is interested in the question of what quality beach vacation the Pearl Beach Hotel 4 * hotel complex can offer? UAE is a country famous for its luxurious beaches. And the hotel is located almost on the seashore.

Guests can relax on a private stretch of beach. It is clean, beautiful and calm, at least the reviews testify to this. Pearl Beach Hotel 4 * provides its guests a comfortable stay on the beautiful beach. Since the beach is private, it is rarely crowded here. Guests enjoy free sunbeds and umbrellas, and the hotel can get a clean towel.

Water activities for hotel guests

What in terms of entertainment can the hotel complex Pearl Beach Hotel 4 * offer? Umm Al Quwain is a calm area, there are not too many tourists here. Nevertheless, you can have fun on the beach. There is a large volleyball court for guests.

During the high season, there is a water transport rental on the shore - you can rent a boat, jet ski or catamaran. In addition, tourists are offered boat trips and yachts (their marina is not so far).

List of additional services





This is not a very large hotel, which is mostly used by travelers for an overnight stay. Nevertheless, the hotel offers its guests an additional service that helps to organize life during the holidays.

An indisputable advantage is the availability of their own laundry and dry cleaning services, the cost of which is quite affordable - guests do not have to worry about the wardrobe. By the way, clothes can be handed over for ironing. The hotel also offers a cleaning service.

The reception is open around the clock - tourists can always get the necessary information or inform employees about the problem. By the way, you can always order a taxi at the reception. Workers can easily arrange a convenient transfer for you, which will take you to the city and airport.

By the way, in Dubai or Sharjah, you can easily rent a car, motorcycle or other type of transport. Guests using their own car can leave it at the hotel parking (no need to pay for a place here).

Business service

It's no secret that modern tourists are increasingly combining leisure with active work. Hotel Pearl Beach Hotel 4 * is suitable for business people. For example, the availability of the Internet is an important factor. There is no own business center in the hotel, but at the reception you can use fax, telephone (for international calls), a printer and other office equipment.

The hotel has a small but functional conference room. For an additional fee, guests can rent video and audio equipment necessary for business and educational events. By the way, the hotel staff can organize the conference - they have enough experience for this.

Leisure of guests: what to do in the hotel?





It is worth noting that in the hotel, if you wish, you can find interesting entertainment for yourself. For example, in the courtyard there is a small pool with clean, fresh water, in which you can always refresh yourself. It offers loungers and umbrellas. Guests have a good time playing darts or billiards. There are also massage rooms - here talented specialists offer their services.

Entertainment can be found not only in the territory of the Pearl Beach Hotel 4 *. The UAE is a country that ideally combines the mystery of eastern culture, ancient history and progress. As already mentioned, a 30-minute drive is Dubai and Sharjah, and in these cities there is a huge amount of entertainment. By the way, the hotel has a travel agency with a Russian-speaking guide, in which you are always happy to pick up an interesting tour, whether it be a day of immersion in ancient culture or an unstoppable shopping.

Facilities for parents with children

Today, almost every parent, when organizing holidays, takes into account not only their own preferences, but also the needs of the child. Children need relaxation and new experiences no less than adults, and healing sea recreation has a positive effect on a growing organism.

Is the Pearl Beach Hotel 4 * hotel complex suitable for families? Of course, this is a good place in which tourists are offered the appropriate conditions. For example, couples with children are advised to settle in spacious two-room apartments. Even if you stayed in a standard room, do not worry, because the staff will deliver a folding bed to the room upon request.

The breakfast menu is quite diverse, so parents always have the opportunity to choose something healthy and tasty for their child. The kids have a great time on the local beach, swimming and building sand castles. By the way, in the hotel yard for special guests there is a special playground equipped with swings, slides, sandpits - here the guys play, get to know each other and just have fun. For an extra fee, you can hire a nanny - an experienced employee will be happy to look after your baby.

If you need a hotel with animators who will completely take care of organizing the leisure of the child, then it is worth taking a look at other options. If your child has enough beach and fun with parents, then this hotel will be ideal.

Hotel Pearl Beach Hotel 4 *: traveler reviews, impressions and recommendations

Acquaintance with the official information about the hotel is an essential part of vacation planning. But sometimes much more valuable data can be gleaned by talking a little with former guests. So what really is a hotel complex Pearl Beach Hotel 4 * (UAE)? Reviews tourists leave mostly positive.

The hotel really has several advantages. Travelers are delighted with spacious rooms with modern furniture and stylish designs. The hotel building was recently completely renovated, and the area here is well-groomed, beautiful, clean.

There are not too many entertainments, but if desired, tourists can always rest and relax. By the way, the food is also good, although most of the guests only have breakfast at a local restaurant, not wanting to tie themselves to the hotel for the whole day.

The beach is beautiful, clean and secluded. The cost of living, by the way, is also attractive, especially considering the level of service and comfort. On the other hand, the location seems to many tourists not very successful, because in part it is an industrial area. It is also worth mentioning that on weekends the hotel often gathers vacationers from all around, so the territory can be slightly noisy.

Nevertheless, many compatriot travelers stay at the Pearl Beach Hotel 4. Its rating on international booking sites is high enough to inspire confidence in any tourist.
