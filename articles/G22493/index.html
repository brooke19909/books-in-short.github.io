LCD "Smorodina", Yekaterinburg: address, reviews

Yekaterinburg is a city among the Ural Mountains with a population of more than 1.5 million people. It is the largest educational, scientific, cultural center. The metropolis is located among the forest, surrounded by lakes and rivers. It is divided into several areas, one of which is the Broad River. It is in the specified area that the residential complex Smorodina (Yekaterinburg) is being built.





Location of the residential complex, transport accessibility

The first phase of the economy class residential complex has already opened its doors to buyers. The complex is located in the Broad River microdistrict on Sukhodolskaya and Tenistaya streets at a distance of just over seven kilometers from the city center. The easiest way to get to the Smorodina residential complex (Yekaterinburg) is by public transport, as the complex is located in a very convenient traffic interchange area with direct access to major roads and the ECAD. Also, the complex can be reached by public transport - minibus. Travel time will take no more than 15-20 minutes.





Near the complex is the Akademichesky microdistrict, built up with multi-storey buildings with a very developed infrastructure. The city authorities plan in the future to provide transport links with the specified microdistrict and, accordingly, with the Smorodina residential complex (Yekaterinburg).

Developer

The construction is carried out by the developer of the residential complex Smorodina (Yekaterinburg) Atomstroykompleks - a company that has existed in the construction market for residential and commercial real estate in the Urals region for over 20 years. This gives the specified project more solidity. Everything that Atomstroykompleks produces is notable for reasonable prices oriented to the middle class of the population, clear deadlines for fulfilling construction obligations.
