Attractions Hersonissos and Crete

Hersonissos is considered by many to be the unofficial capital of tourism on the islands. This place is famous for its drive, fun and noise, incendiary dances that never cease. But it also has its drawbacks - for a quiet and family holiday it would be better to choose another resort. In this place you can find gourmet restaurants, fashionable boutiques, souvenir shops, discos, nightclubs and, of course, attractions. Hersonissos is not very rich in them.



The city is famous for its wide coast, which runs a strip of bars and restaurants, mainly Irish and Dutch. Therefore, the main number of tourists in this city is the Irish, British, Dutch and Germans.

Attractions Hersonissos

Every year, the popularity of this resort is increasing, due to which the number of various entertainments is growing. On the map of Crete, the city of Hersonissos has an incredibly convenient location:

  • 20 km to the south - and you will find yourself on the Lassithi plateau. Here you can admire stunning landscapes, as well as go to the cave, in which, according to ancient legends, the all-powerful Zeus was born;
  • 25 km to the west you can appreciate the attractions of Hersonissos and Heraklion;
  • after driving 25 km to the east, you will find yourself in the town of Agios Nicholas, where you can learn about the ancient traditions and features of Greek culture.




Therefore, by renting a car or using local transport, you can see the main attractions of Hersonissos in a few days. The ancient Greek culture, famous throughout the whole planet, left a huge imprint on the architecture of the city.
