How to make an owl out of gum without using a machine?

If you are fond of weaving from elastic "irises" of the "Fanny Lum" series, you will probably be interested in an owl from elastic bands. How to weave such a figure? Arm yourself with detailed instructions, any Fanny Loom kit and a good plastic hook. You can get to work!





Magic ring

You will need to weave a special magic ring, which forms the basis of all the figures in the style of "lumigurumi". An owl from gum is no exception. The canvas layout consists of this magic circle and an arbitrary number of rows imposed on it from above. So:

  • Throw a black rubber band in four turns on the hook. Crochet a second black Fanny Lum.
  • Drop all the loops from the hook onto it.
  • Of the two remaining loops, make one by stretching the left loop through the right.
  • Insert the hook immediately into the four loops that were dropped on the elastic in the first step.
  • Pull the black "iris" through them. Pull the left loop on the hook through the two remaining rings.
  • Repeat the previous steps five more times. Thus, you spun the first row of the canvas - that same magical ring "lumigurumi".

Continuation of work

Anyone who is interested in how to make an owl out of rubber bands will need information on how to properly make the base of the figure.

  • To make the base ("bottom") of the figure, we need to continue weaving in the same way, only in the second row, knit two elastic bands in each loop. In the third row, weave two ‚ÄúFanny Loom‚Äù into each second loop, and in the fourth - into every third.
  • Continue to work, but now start one rubber band in each loop, alternating colors: the 5th row - black elastic bands, the 6th-7th - white or transparent, the 8th-16th - black "Fanny Lum", the 17th - white or transparent, the 18th - black, the 19th - white or transparent. You will get a torso. At this stage, it can already be filled with syntepon or holofiber.

Torso Processing

You almost got an owl out of gum. How to weave the rest of the details? Follow the directions below.

  • To connect the parts of the body, insert the hook into the first two loops of each of the two fragments located opposite each other. Pick up the black rubber band with the tool.
  • Pull this ‚ÄúFanny Loom‚Äù through the loops and throw the other half of the rubber on the hook.
  • Pass the left loop through the remaining rings.
  • Continue weaving in this way until both parts of the body are joined. The last loop must be tightened and tucked into the toy. If you have already made such figures, then you probably know that such a little thing is convenient to use as a keychain. A gum owl will be a great souvenir and a true friend!

Ears

You can make ears for your beauty:

  • To form the ears, insert the hook into the middle loop of the last row.
  • Pull the black rubber over this loop.
  • Pull the left loop on the hook through the right, forming a loop, and then repeat the previous steps again.

Eyes

How to make an owl out of gum if you don‚Äôt have a single machine? Very simple: just one hook and any set of "irises" of the "Fanny Lum" series is enough. If you choose multi-colored or black rubber bands for the body of an owl, try to make the big eyes contrast, that is, black and white.

  • To make eyes, weave a new magic ring "lumigurumi" of black rubber bands, and then make the second row of white "Fanny Lum". Pull the last white loop to the wrong side, pass the black rubber through it and make a ringlet. The second eye is made in the same way.
  • If you already understand how to make an owl out of elastic bands, then you will probably guess for yourself how to attach eyes to the body. To do this, just select the place where the owl‚Äôs eyes will be located, pull out a loop from there and pass a ring through it left on the wrong side of the part. Then, unfasten the eyelet from the hook and put it on the base of the eye.

Beak

The beak is usually made of white rubber bands:

  • Throw on the hook ‚Äúiris‚Äù in three turns, pick up two more rubber bands and throw loops on them from the tool.
  • Pull two more ‚ÄúFanny Lum‚Äù through the rings on the hook, and then return the dropped loops to it.
  • Hook a new rubber band and discard all the assembled rings on it.
  • Transfer the two loops from the hook to your fingers.
  • Attach the beak to the body: insert the hook into the loop located between the eyes and pull the right loop of the beak through it. Put the left ring on the base of the left eye, and the right one on the base of the right.

The figurine is ready. Now you know how to make an owl out of rubber bands using a regular hook.
