Hotel Panorama Hotel (Greece / Crete / Chania): reviews and photos

Grand Panorama Hotel is a great place to relax, designed for a great time with the whole family or a noisy youth company. It attracts tourists with its pleasant atmosphere, which reigns both in the open territory and in the rooms. In addition, many tourists note that Panorama Hotel has a great service, thanks to which every guest can feel completely comfortable.

general information

The date of construction of the hotel is considered to be 1998 - it was then that he received his first guests. Since then, on its territory, as well as in a large five-story building, repair work has been carried out more than once, during which the interior and technical equipment of the rooms improved.

Location

The Panorama Hotel is located in Marmaris (Armutallan region), in a rather secluded place, among beautiful nature, on a small hill. The central part, where all the entertainment places are concentrated, is located at a short distance - 4 km from the hotel. The nearest beach can be found three kilometers from the Panorama Hotel, which is quite inconvenient for those tourists who prefer to relax on the sea coast, under the sun.

Rooms

This place offers very comfortable holiday apartments, of which there are a total of 104. All of them are located in one building. The fund does not provide for their division into different categories, so they are all presented in one form. Each room is a separate room, fully equipped with furniture necessary for a comfortable stay, with a bathroom combined with a bathroom. There is also a sink, and there are also several shelves that store bathrobes, towels and bath accessories for each guest.

The high level of comfort of the guests is achieved due to the climate equipment installed in the rooms in the form of modern air conditioners. With their help, tourists can independently adjust the desired temperature in the room, based on their wishes.





Furniture equipment

All apartments feature furniture designed for a couple of guests. If desired, the number of guests can be increased to three - for such purposes a small sofa is provided here, which is laid out. The main beds here are two separate beds, which in some apartments are replaced by one double.

Near the places for rest in the rooms there are cabinets where guests have the opportunity to store a small amount of personal belongings, a large wardrobe made of light wood is installed for clothes. There is a TV in front of the bed.

If you wish, for an additional fee, tourists staying at Panorama Hotel can install a minibar or a safe in their room. You can also use a landline telephone that is connected to an international line.

Conditions for children

For young guests, the hotel provides a number of services and conditions that make their pastime much more comfortable and enjoyable. First of all, all guests pay attention to the possibility of free cots for a child under the age of 12 years. In order for such a service to be provided immediately upon arrival, you must pre-book such a bed.

In the catering establishments, which are available in the Panorama Hotel, special chairs are arranged for children, where the process of eating will be more pleasant.

In the hotel building there is a small hall where a kids club is arranged. Here kids will find entertaining and educational games, as well as interesting cartoons and programs. In addition, in the hall, children have the opportunity to communicate with their peers. There is a team of animators who will develop the child throughout the entire period of his stay in the mini-club.

In the open area there are also several places for their active pastime. The most popular among them is a small pool, designed with all the considerations regarding the safety of young guests. Also in the gardens there is a large playground where obstacles, labyrinths and everything that is needed for active pastime are equipped.

Nutrition

Much attention from tourists staying at the hotel is paid to the food, which is organized in his restaurant. Every day, guests can visit the restaurant, which serves a buffet. It presents gourmet dishes of Turkish and Mediterranean cuisine. If you wish, you can also taste alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, but only those that are produced locally. In order to visit such an institution, it is necessary to book a table in advance and make a small deposit to the account.





If desired, guests have the opportunity to go to four bars. They work until 10 pm and until 4 in the morning. Each of them has a menu that offers several options for snacks, exotic fruits, as well as sweets. In addition, the bar's card has a huge assortment of cocktails and soft drinks that delight vacationers in hot weather.



Aquazone

The reviews of hotel guests say that they spend a huge amount of time within the aquazone. This is primarily due to the distant location of the Grand Panorama Hotel (Marmaris) from the sea. The aquazone is a combination of a large sun terrace and a swimming pool with clear water.

On the terrace there is everything that is necessary for guests to relax: sun loungers, umbrellas, towels and air mattresses. In addition, it has a bar, which offers very popular bright cocktails. In the very center of the terrace is a pool of 110 sq.m. It is equipped with one slide, on which vacationers can have fun frolic. Water here is heated only under the influence of sunlight, and is purified thanks to an automated system.





Spa and sports

Fans of relaxation very often share their impressions obtained in the process of visiting the thermal zone located on the first floor of the hotel building. It consists of two parts: a hammam and a sauna. Here, parmeisters who can do massage, body wraps and some cosmetic procedures that are very beneficial for the skin of the whole body can offer their services.

At the exit of the thermal part there is a small pool, the area of ‚Äã‚Äãwhich is 35 sq.m. The water in it is cleaned and heated to the optimum level every day. In addition, there is a large niche with a jacuzzi, where you can pamper your body with hydromassage. All services provided in the spa are paid.





Fans of an active lifestyle have the opportunity to continue to engage in their figure, even while on vacation. Hotel guests note that, unfortunately, there is no gym in its building. However, here you can engage in open areas, as well as arrange small competitions in team sports.

Additional features

The hotel building has several salons, where guests can be provided with some services: hairdresser, beauty parlor, medical center. All services that are provided in them are not included in the price of the permit and are provided at an additional cost. If desired, tourists can exchange cash at a special point, as well as visit shops that sell interesting goods and souvenirs.





Beach

Hotel guests in most cases consider it a minus that it is far from the beach strip. However, the place to stay compensates for this inconvenience by the possibility of daily free transportation of tourists to the beach "Nirvana", which is 10 km from Panorama Hotel. Reviews also often mention the beauty of this beach. Here, according to vacationers, offers a beautiful view of the mountains and brightly flowering exotic gardens.

Staying on it, you can rent sun loungers and umbrellas; experienced tourists recommend bringing towels with them, as they are not even given out for payment.

Prices

As a rule, tourists are satisfied with the cost of a ticket to the Grand Panorama Hotel. In their reviews, they often talk about the fact that it is here for a very reasonable price that services are provided at an excellent level, as well as good conditions for recreation. In addition, the regular price includes buffet meals at a local restaurant.

The average cost of a tour at the Grand Panorama Hotel (Marmaris) is about 40 thousand rubles, based on a trip for two for a week.
