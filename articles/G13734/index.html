Do they always pay

Answering this question is sometimes not easy. After all, much depends on the specific situation. Property tax (apartments, houses, cottages) is a very delicate point in Russian law. And to draw the appropriate conclusions only with all the attendant circumstances.

Property

For example, there is a so-called property tax. Among the population, it is better known as property tax. Most often applies to apartments and houses. Requires annual tax payments from taxpayers.

Workers purchase

The next situation that may concern real estate is its purchase. Very often, citizens are entitled to a deduction from the transaction. And the tax when buying an apartment by a pensioner is not paid. Elderly workers are entitled to a deduction for the specified time period.

This is a very simple process. For him, he will have to present all the declarations, as well as certificates of expenses, of the right of ownership. And you will receive 13% of the completed transaction back.





In practice, this feature is used very often. Nobody wants to miss her. But it is advisable to hurry - the pensioner will have only 3 years to fulfill the request for a tax deduction from the purchase of an apartment. After this period, such a right is burned out. And it will not work in any way.

For the unemployed

By the way, apartment tax for pensioners who do not work, also has its own nuances. If an elderly person suddenly buys real estate, then he can receive, as in the above case, the corresponding deduction. In this situation, you will have to take into account many conditions.

For example, you can apply for a deduction for any year prior to the purchase, but not more than 3 years. In addition, you are entitled to a refund of 13% of the transaction. And while the maximum amount of deduction cannot exceed 2 million rubles. These are the rules that exist in modern law.





Keep in mind that if a citizen who has gone on a well-deserved rest does not buy land, he should pay all taxes in full. In this regard, the state does not give any concessions. Land tax is paid in full by all taxpayers. And no matter who you are - a senior citizen or a working citizen.

Sale

Often, older people agree to sell their property. This is a common case. And many are wondering whether it is necessary to pay tax on the sale of apartments to pensioners. After all, such citizens do not pay property contributions!

It does not change anything. Property tax (real estate) has nothing to do with the contract of sale. According to modern laws, all taxpayers must pay income taxes. That is, those that are accrued for income received by citizens.

No one exempts pensioners from them. It turns out that even the elderly should pay full income tax. It appears when selling real estate or any other property. Dodging him does not work. In Russia, this rule applies to all taxpayers.
