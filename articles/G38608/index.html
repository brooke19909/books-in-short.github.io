Turkey's best hotels with a sandy beach: a brief overview

Many tourists prefer to spend their holidays on the sandy beaches. In Turkey, hotels with such beaches are more than enough. Most of these hotels are located on the coast of the Aegean Sea and the shores of the Mediterranean. The purpose of this article is to describe in detail which Turkish hotels with a sandy beach are the most popular and why.





Antalya Hotels

Antalya is one of the most favorite resorts among Russian tourists. Vacationers do not need to go to the sea, sandy beaches can be found right in the city. This resort is mainly chosen by lovers of urban recreation, shopping, restaurants and other entertainment.

But which hotels in Turkey with a sandy beach are best visited in Antalya? Perhaps the first on this list will be the Wow Kremlin Palace - a luxury hotel that looks like a building of the Russian Kremlin. The hotel has its own beach, and the amount of entertainment is simply amazing. Here you can go diving, sail, ride catamarans or play beach volleyball.

No less popular is the hotel complex IC Green Palace. This hotel annually attracts thousands of tourists from all over the world, only the complex's pool area is 4000 sq. m. A private sandy beach with its own sun loungers, umbrellas and an open-air bar attracts guests from morning to evening.

The top three is completed by the no less luxurious hotel - Lara Park Hotel. It is located near the famous Lara Beach. He attracts tourists with his snow-white sand, which is washed by the boundless clear waters of the Mediterranean Sea.

Belek Hotels

The first place in the rating "The best hotels of Turkey with a sandy beach" is the resort of Belek. The sandy beaches of the Mediterranean stretch for several tens of kilometers. The advantages of this coastal zone are soft sand and safe descent into the water, and most importantly, belonging to the Blue Flag zone . This means that the entire territory is under strict supervision, the condition of the beaches is carefully monitored.





One of the best hotels with a sandy beach is the Voyage Belek Golf Spa. Own beach, clean sand, high level of service - all this makes the described hotel so popular with tourists. In addition, the fact that the hotel complex has 14 bars, 8 restaurants and 4 swimming pools cannot go unnoticed.

Alanya Hotels

As for Alanya, here hotels with sandy beaches meet mainly lovers of a relaxing holiday with their families. Yellow sand and a gentle slope to the sea - this is what attracts tourists with children.





A popular holiday destination in Alanya is Alara Kum Hotel - a hotel with a beautiful sandy beach, however, it is located 23 kilometers from the resort center. For tourists, this hotel is known for its proximity to the legendary Alanya beach - Incekum. The hotel can rightfully be considered one of the best, it has everything for a comfortable stay - several pools, water slides, an open-air restaurant, a lobby bar and much more.

Not inferior to Alara Kum and Alara Park Hotel - a hotel with 9 elevators and 370 rooms, 3 pools, 3 restaurants and 5 bars. Alara Park is also located near Incekum Beach and every year it gathers more and more vacationers within its walls.





Side Hotels

The best hotels in Turkey with a sandy beach can be found in the East of Side - a small town located on the peninsula. The best sandy beaches are located in Side mini-resorts - Kumkoy, Cholakly, Sorgun, Kyzylagach and Kizilot.

In Colakli, the most popular complex can be called Kamelya World. This hotel has gathered the best stretches of the coast. Kumkoy hotels are no worse - Asteria Side and Sunrise Queen have excellent sandy beaches, equipped territory and quality service. However, they are definitely not up to the level of Voyage Sorgun Select Sorgun. This luxury hotel is chosen by wealthy tourists who appreciate beauty, proximity to the wild, as well as those who come to Side to enjoy the calm sea and the ideal sandy beach. No doubt, Turkey hotels with the best sand beaches are located in Sorgun. The five-star hotels of Kyzylagach and Kizilot are beautiful in their own way. They are also designed for tourists with a thick wallet, the quality of service and service is at a high level.

Kusadasi Hotels

Kusadasi resort attracts tourists precisely with its sandy beaches, one of which is called Pamuchak. It stretches for 5 kilometers along the Aegean Sea. Those who are not interested in relaxing alone with the wildlife, choose a well-equipped urban sandy beach.

The best sandy beach in Kusadasi has the Atlantique Holiday Club hotel. The hotel complex is good in that it has the most beautiful and cleanest beach in the entire resort, and the availability of equipped territory, modern infrastructure and all the necessary conditions for recreation for kids rightly put this hotel in the category of the best.

Also, do not neglect Tusan Beach Resort and Grand Blu Sky. All these hotels have a well-kept sandy beach, a varied entertainment program and a good level of service.

The number of hotels in the resorts of Turkey with a sandy beach is huge, the one that will meet your requirements, wishes and opportunities will be the best for you.
