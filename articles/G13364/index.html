Bird Park "Sparrows": reviews, photos, address, birds of the park

What a wonderful place - this Sparrow Bird Park. Here you can not only get acquainted with rare species of birds, animals, but also have a great time with family or friends. By the way, there is everything necessary for a complete leisure. And beautiful nature will provide an opportunity to have a good rest after working days.

Description





This wonderful place is located in the Kaluga region, so every tourist tries to visit here in order to admire the beauties of nature, living animals and birds, to be away from the bustle of the city. The birds of the park are represented by various species and, of course, not only sparrows are present here. You can meet and talking parrots, and pigeons, and other birds. There are about two thousand different birds. At the moment, it can even be called not a park, but a small zoo. It will be interesting not only to children to visit the Sparrow Bird Park, but, of course, to the adult population. Special entertainment venues have also been created here. Therefore, be sure to stock up on time to fully experience the beauty of this picturesque place.





Not so long ago, the Sparrow Bird Park was organized. In 2015, he celebrates his first ten years of life. But, despite this, many have already fallen in love with him and come here very often. Here there is the opportunity not only to spend free time, but also to celebrate holidays with pleasure. It can be a trip to the New Year holidays or another significant day for your family. Or, for example, you can have a great children's birthday here. Each child will be delighted with such an unforgettable gift!

Zones

The territory is divided into two sections:

  • the first is the area where the wonderful birds of the park are located, the exotarium is located;
  • the second - there are: a stone park, "World of the Tropics", an ostrich farm, as well as a farm of small pets.

You can get to the bird park every day from 10 am, and you will be welcome here until late in the evening. Ticket price

Entrance to the park is paid. To get there, you must purchase a ticket. There is an opportunity to buy it at a reduced cost. Moreover, for each of the zones will need to present a separate ticket. The cost is different on weekdays and weekends. For example, on Sunday an ordinary ticket to a bird park will cost approximately 300 rubles, and for a privileged category of citizens - 100 rubles. On weekdays, the price of a regular ticket is 200 rubles. The preferential category of citizens includes: pensioners, schoolchildren, students. On weekends in the park of stones tickets can be purchased at 200 rubles (on weekdays - 150 rubles.) - For the regular category, 50 rubles - for preferential.

Park of birds. How to get there by car and public transport?

If you plan to travel by car, you can move along the Kaluga or Kiev highway. There are signs on the highway, so you can easily reach your destination or use the map. It is also possible to use public transport.

You can get from the Kiev station by train to Obninsk (or Balabanovo). Then you need to transfer to a minibus to the stop of the Victory State Farm.

This wonderful bird park is located in the village of the same name. The territory that covers his possessions is quite huge. Where is the bird park located? The address of this wonderful place is as follows: Kaluga Region, Zhukovsky District, Victory State Farm.

Where do animals and birds live?

A spacious aviary has been created for every animal or bird, which is very good. Usually they are placed in cramped cells, which causes discomfort. Here, in the open space for birds and animals, good conditions are created. And for visitors, by the way, everything is done at the highest level. Almost every enclosure has a sign with an inscription that contains information about the inhabitants themselves, their habitual habitat, characteristic features, as well as other interesting information.

In the summer, all birds live in open-air cages, so one or another species can be carefully considered. You can capture these beautiful inhabitants of the park as a souvenir! They are really beautiful, as they are represented by completely different species, with different colors. Particularly eye-catching are bright, unlike each other lovely parrots. Among them are speakers. So do not be surprised if after you someone screams in an inhuman voice.

The inhabitants





Basically, all the birds in the park are familiar to all species of birds, but there are also exotic options. This is a beautiful flamingo, and a beautiful ostrich, toucans living with their family, and others.

There are a large number of diverse pigeons. If you stop and look at them, you can see that each chick is completely different from the previous one. Each has its own unique personality. Here you can also observe the crowned pigeon from the islands of New Guinea.

Penguins

How beautiful penguins are walking among the cliffs created for them. They are so fluffy, and they can even be stroked. By the way, they really like it. Penguins strive to substitute their fluffy bodies so that they are stroked by the guests of the park. They are located in the designated enclosure for them, so everyone present will be able to get to know them better.

Cranes

The birds of the park and beautiful cranes of various species are represented. For example, there is an African crowned crane. They are notable for the fact that they often perch on tree branches, and sometimes they nest for themselves there.

Here you can clearly see the Japanese crane. He is the largest of his class. In Japan, he is extolled, it is believed that this is a symbol of a happy and faithful married life.

Animals

In addition to the birds present, there is a place reserved for unusual animals. For example, in this wonderful park you can meet a white kangaroo. There are also mischievous lemurs here, they can please those present with their sophistication. And wonderful monkeys, now and then jumping from branch to branch, will not leave indifferent absolutely no tourists.

You can relax by the pond, which is inhabited by small turtles and red carps. True, not everyone can see them.

What else is there in the park?

In fact, in the park everything is thought out to the smallest detail. You can periodically relax on benches located in the shade, if you are suddenly tired. You can buy delicious chilled soda or ice cream at the stalls that get in your way if the walk takes place on a hot summer day. If the visit to the bird park was on a cool autumn day, then you can, on the contrary, warm yourself with a cup of hot tea or coffee. And for lovers of chips, various types are presented. For children in the central part of the park there is a wonderful entertainment playground, where you can spend some time with pleasure to change the atmosphere.
