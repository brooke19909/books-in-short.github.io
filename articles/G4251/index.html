How to withdraw cash from an MTS phone: description of methods, recommendations and reviews

Time does not stand still: today, to withdraw any amount from the balance of your mobile is not a difficult operation at all. If you are interested in how to withdraw cash from an MTS phone , then we are in a hurry to tell you several effective and proven methods at once. Choose what suits you best!

Method number 1: USSD request

For many interested in how to withdraw cash from an MTS phone, this is the most convenient method. However, it is suitable only for owners of bank cards "Visa" and "Master Card". The instruction is simple:

  • Send the following request from your phone: * 611 * (bank card number - 16 characters without spaces) * (amount to be cashed) # and a call button. For example: * 611 * 1234567887654321 * 3000 #.
  • A dialog box will then appear stating that your request has been accepted.
  • Then a message will come asking you to confirm the transfer of money to the card - in response to the service addressee, you need to send SMS with any text. If you change your mind, then the number 0.
  • That's all - after a while the amount will go to your card.

Before you withdraw money from the MTS phone in this way, pay attention to the following:

  • At a time, this way you can withdraw to the card no more than 15 thousand rubles.
  • The limit for 24 hours is 15 thousand rubles, for a month - 40 thousand rubles.
  • A commission is charged for the operation - 4% of the withdrawal amount. Moreover, this value cannot be less than 60 rubles.




Method number 2: via SMS

Is it possible to withdraw cash from an MTS phone through SMS services? Yes, but again with a credit card. Thus, the operation is not more difficult:

  • You need to send a message of the following content to number 6111: 16 digits (without spaces) of the bank card number, space, amount of withdrawal. For example: 1234567887654321 5000.
  • After the response message from the service arrives, you need to send SMS to 6111 with any text to confirm the transfer. If you change your mind - 0.
  • Then a message appears indicating that the transfer is complete. Behind him is SMS, which already says about the transfer of this amount to your bank card.

Note that in rare cases, the transfer has to be expected up to 5 days. Limitations on the withdrawal amount, commission - everything is exactly the same as in the first method. Plus additional expenses for SMS correspondence.

Method number 3: through the MTS ATM

How to withdraw cash from an MTS phone if you do not have a card? Imagine another method - it works if there is an MTS ATM or SMP Bank near you. There you can withdraw cash from the balance of your mobile. You need to act like this:

  • At 3232 send SMS: RUB (amount). For example: RUB 4000.
  • After you receive a response message, send to 3232 SMS with any text, except 0 (zero - cancel the operation).
  • In response, you will receive a message with a PIN code, which is valid for 3 days. If you do not manage to cash out money for this period, the amount will become unavailable for withdrawal and will be returned to your mobile balance, and the password will be useless.
  • Next - SMS with confirmation that the money is available for withdrawal.
  • On the ATM screen, in the main menu, find "Cash from phone balance".
  • Click on "MTS Money".
  • In the next window you need to enter your phone number, the PIN code sent to you and the withdrawal amount.
  • An ATM will give you money and a check.




At one time in this way you can withdraw 5 thousand rubles. The same amount is the daily limit. For a month you can cash out 40 thousand rubles using this method. Commission - 5.95% of the withdrawal amount.
