Mystery MMK-750U

This is a relatively compact stereo system that fits perfectly into the kitchen or bedroom. In general, the Mystery company differs from other brands in that it produces, without exaggeration, the best inexpensive music centers. This model is no exception and deserves special attention.





The MMK-750 stereo system is made in strict designer colors with a slightly perceptible retro-flight. Model Features

This quite good music center can be used not only for listening to music, but also for watching videos (of course, coupled with a TV). Like many other models of this brand, the center is extremely dependent on the remote control, that is, all basic and advanced settings are performed only from it. The output power of the MMK-750 stereo system ranges from 30 watts.

Owners speak very warmly about the model. They appreciated the compactness of the stereo system and convenience (multifunctional remote control). This is a good music center for the home, it just fits perfectly into the familiar apartments and other small rooms.

Advantages of the model:

Disadvantages:

  • the model is too dependent on the remote control;
