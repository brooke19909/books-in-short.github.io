Principle of operation

It is very easy to get confused in the tariffs of this card. Three tickets are recorded on the Troika card: 90 minutes, Unified and TAT. In addition, there is a "Wallet". This is a kind of balance from which various amounts are debited, depending on the type of trip.

Tariff "Wallet"

"Wallet" can be replenished, and then spend money on travel. For the trip by metro, ground transportation or monorail, 38 rubles will be debited.

For the ticket ‚Äú90 minutes‚Äù (one entrance to the metro plus any number of transfers on the tram, trolleybus, bus for 90 minutes) - 58 rubles.

"One"

There are two options: the number of trips or days. What is the difference? 1, 2 or 60 trips are recorded on the card. After purchasing tickets for 1 and 2 trips, there are 5 days for activation, for 60 trips - 90 days. You can record tickets valid for a certain period of time. There are options for a different number of days or for a month.

TAT

If a person rarely uses a monorail or metro, it makes sense to purchase a TAT ticket for a trolley bus, bus and tram. Tickets are sold for 30 days or 1 trip.

An unlimited ticket for 1 month costs 1194 rubles. If you make trips twice a day, then one trip will cost about 20 rubles, which is very cheap for Moscow.

"90 minutes"

On this ticket you can go once in the subway, and also make any number of trips by bus, tram and trolley bus for 90 minutes.

You can get out of the subway, money will not be debited additionally. One trip costs 59 rubles.
