Ribbon brooch tie

With our own hands creating any thing, we begin to create magic. Just now it was just a piece of ribbon, a few beads, a beautiful button, and suddenly a unique product appeared!

Brooches are exquisite accessories that emphasize the individuality of their owner. Handwork in jewelry has always been very appreciated, because it gives the appearance of exclusivity and originality.

Remember the old films - the ladies are very often presented there very elegant, refined, and a brooch was necessarily present as the main accessory. Let us also try to create it in the form of a tie, taking as a basis the rep ribbon.

So we need:

  • Lacy ribbons (wide and narrower).
  • Sections of rep ribbons of shade 1, width 2 cm.
  • Reps shade 2 4 cm wide.
  • A beautiful button.
  • Centimeter tape (can be replaced with a ruler).
  • Glue.
  • Thread and needle.
  • Felt and pin.

Let's start making brooches from rep ribbon.

Cut four strips of 14 cm from the ribbon of color rep. 2. Do the same with lace.

Combining lace and reps, make loops, bending the tape to the center, and fix with glue. Now put everything in a bow and sew it together.

The next step is to cut the ribbon from rep 1 into 3 pieces 25 cm long. Also fold and sew into a bow.

Take a wide rep and lace ribbons, cut 2 pieces of 21 cm. Fold together and make a bow.

All the received details - fold the bows together, sew and wrap the place of fastening with lace. Put a ring from a narrow ribbon on top, gluing in the middle to a large bow.

Now you need to prepare the strips for a long tie. Cut 2 strips of rep 2 and lace (their length will be 14 cm) and rep 2 2 11 cm long.

Reps 2 and lace must be connected and cut obliquely. Cut the end of a thin strip in the form of a wedge. Fold all the strips together, placing a narrow one in the middle. This item is attached to the back of the bow.

Now cut a small square from the felt patch and use it to attach a pin. A button with glue will need to be attached in the center.

Everything, the tie is ready. Dress up!

Brooch from rep ribbons: master class

The next option is a very simple, but no less stylish brooch. A wonderful little thing for your wardrobe, and a great gift.

Materials and tools:

  • Ribbons from rep - for example, yellow and blue.
  • Metal button, cabochons.
  • Pin for fastener.
  • Felt.
  • Glue gun.
  • Sewing threads, needle.

First decide what size the result will be. Based on this, cut several strips - three (2 yellow and 1 blue) of the same length, one yellow shorter and two (yellow and black) even shorter. Fold to the middle with loops, glue.

Now we collect all the bows together - first two yellow and blue, then a smaller yellow one on top, and yellow and blue combined together on it.

After everything is fastened together, cut off 2 strips of 10 cm from both ribbons and lay on each other - this is the "tail" of the brooch. Cut the edges along the oblique, singe and fix on the back of the bow.

We sew the button, and it remains to attach the fastener to a felt piece, and then to the brooch itself.

Stylish brooch from rep ribbon is ready. You can choose your color combinations, focusing on your own taste and preferences.

Simple brooch for baby

For little ladies, you also need to look at 100. Therefore, the presence of jewelry in clothes is important. Let's make a brooch from a rep ribbon for the baby.

For it, cut a narrow (2.5 cm.) Ribbon into 8 segments 16 cm long. Fold each strip to the center with the edges and fix. Now, put all 8 bows you got on top of each other, forming a rounded shape, and sew in the middle.

Hide the stitching place with a decorative button, a bright cabochon or simply embroider with beads. Glue the clasp and a piece of thick fabric to the back.

Done. Now your little fashionista will be irresistible!
