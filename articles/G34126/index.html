Strandja 3 * (Golden Sands, Bulgaria): photos, prices and reviews of tourists

One of the best resorts in Bulgaria, so beloved by our compatriots, is Golden Sands. It is located in the same reserve zone and has excellent ecology and a healthy climate. An excellent vacation here awaits both lovers of a relaxing and relaxing pastime, and those who prefer to have fun, as they say, "to the fullest." Local tourist infrastructure allows you to find entertainment for everyone. There is a wide hotel base in Golden Sands, offering accommodation for every taste and size of the wallet. If you decide to spend your vacation here and are looking for a good hotel at an affordable price, then pay attention to Strandja 3 *. For our part, we will try today to familiarize you with this hotel, tell about the prices for accommodation, and also find out whether our compatriots liked to live here.

For a fee, here you can use sun loungers and parasols. In this area, most beaches are sandy, but when you enter the water in some places there are stones.





Hotel Description

I immediately want to note that this hotel, located in the Bulgarian Golden Sands, is often confused with the Strandja 3 * hotel of the same name (Sunny Beach). Meanwhile, apart from the name and location on the Black Sea, these establishments have nothing in common. The distance between Strandja (Golden Sands) and Strandja (Sunny Beach) 3 * is more than a hundred kilometers. As for the hotel under consideration, it was built a long time ago. Several years ago, large-scale repair work was carried out here, so today the hotel meets its guests with updated rooms equipped with everything necessary. It consists of two three-story buildings connected by a hall and a restaurant. In total, the hotel has 110 rooms. Among them are both standard rooms and studios and apartments. It offers guests a restaurant, bar, pool, a terrace for sunbathing, sports fields, a picturesque garden and playgrounds. This hotel is suitable for an excellent holiday for all categories of guests.

Room features

All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV, telephone, private bathroom with shower and a balcony with scenic views of the garden or the sea. The floors in the rooms are carpeted. If necessary, an extra bed (folding bed) can be installed in your room. The administration of the hotel must be notified in advance of the need for such a service and confirmation must be received from it. As for the cleaning of the rooms, it is carried out on a daily basis. Linen and towels are changed several times a week. To the delight of people who are accustomed to traveling in the company of their pets, the 3 * Strandzha Hotel (Golden Sands) permits their placement on its territory. However, you should notify the property in advance of your plans on arrival at the hotel with your pet and receive confirmation from it.

restaurants and bars

Hotel Strandja 3 * when booking a room offers tourists to choose a food system that best meets their requirements. So, you can take only breakfast, half board (breakfast + dinner) or all inclusive. In the latter case, in addition to three meals a day, you will be offered free soft and alcoholic drinks throughout the day, and in the afternoon tea and coffee with pastries.





Sea and beach

Hotel Strandja (Chaika) 3 * is located on the second coastline and does not have its own beach. Previously, on the way from the hotel to the sea, vacationers had to overcome the roadway. However, several years ago, this area was made exclusively pedestrian, which only added peace and comfort to tourists who came to this resort. As for the beaches, they are mainly municipal. Therefore, for the use of sun loungers and parasols, as well as other beach equipment, you will need to pay. The beaches here are mostly sandy, but stones can be encountered when entering the water, so you should be careful and watch out for the children.

Entertainment

In addition to a beach holiday, Strandja 3 * guests can have a great time at the hotel's pool. It is open and large enough. There is also a section for the youngest tourists. Near the pool there is a sun terrace with plenty of sun loungers and parasols. While sunbathing, you can enjoy a drink at the bar.

At the height of the tourist season, the hotel has animators. They will offer you to do water aerobics, take part in funny contests, play boccia, futsal or darts. Also on site you can play billiards, volleyball, tennis, visit the gym or aerobics classes. Those who wish to relax can have a good time in the local SPA center, where there is a sauna, jacuzzi, massage room and beauty salon. Please note that some of the listed services are provided for a fee.





Infrastructure

The Strandja 3 * hotel (Golden Sands, Bulgaria) has all the conditions for a comfortable stay of guests. So, the reception at the hotel is open around the clock. In addition, most of the hotel staff are fluent in Russian, so it will not be difficult for you to communicate with them.

For the convenience of guests, the hotel has a restaurant, bar, snack bar, small shop, and an Internet corner. If desired, for an additional fee, you can leave valuables in storage in a safe located at the reception. The hotel also has ample parking. Therefore, if you want to come to Bulgaria with your own car or plan to rent a car on the spot, you will not have any problems with parking. By the way, if you contact the receptionists at the reception, they will be happy to advise car rental agencies and tour agencies. In addition, you can exchange currency at the reception.

For children

The Strandja 3 * hotel has all the conditions for the stay of the youngest guests. So, there is a children's pool and a playground. There is also a mini-club where you can leave your baby for almost the whole day, and in the evenings mini-discos are held, where children are happy to dance to funny music.





Cost of living

The cost of accommodation at the Strandzha Hotel (Golden Sands) is consistent with the services provided here and the level of starryness. So, a day of staying here for an adult guest in high season (July-August) will cost 20 euros (double standard room, type of food - breakfast only), 23 euro (studio, type of food - breakfast only), 32 euro ( standard room, all inclusive) and 35 euros (studio, all inclusive). In other months, the price of accommodation will be slightly lower.

Strandja 3 * (Golden Sands): reviews of tourists from Russia

In order for you to have a more complete picture of the Bulgarian Strandzha Hotel, we bring to your attention the opinion of our compatriots who have already stayed here. In general, we can say that most tourists were quite satisfied with this hotel. However, they would not recommend choosing it for the elderly, travelers with sore joints and problems of the musculoskeletal system, as well as families with small children. But let's figure it out in order.





As for the location of the hotel, due to the fact that it is located relatively far from the center of Golden Sands, you can relax here in peace and quiet. In addition, there are noisy roads near the hotel, so you won't be bothered by the sound of passing cars either day or night. For some tourists, the minus of the Strandja (Golden Sands) 3 * hotel was its remoteness from the sea. Despite the fact that the distance to the nearest beach is only three hundred meters, you will have to go down rather steep ladders along the way. In this regard, as already mentioned above, travelers with small children are not recommended to come here, since the constant need to carry the stroller up the stairs can create discomfort. It will also not be easy for older people and tourists with diseases of the musculoskeletal system. As for our compatriots, the majority were even happy with such physical exertion, since delicious food at the hotel and large portions have the potential to quickly gain excess weight during the holidays.

For the most part, the Russians were also satisfied with the rooms. So, our compatriots found the rooms quite spacious and well renovated. Furniture and appliances here, they say, are not new, but in excellent condition. Some guests were embarrassed by the lack of curtains in the bathroom, which caused a lot of water to fall onto the floor during bathing. But this trifle can be ignored. The rooms were cleaned daily, and bed linen was changed a couple of times a week.

As for meals in Strandja 3 * (Bulgaria), then its level, of course, can not be compared with hotels with more stars. However, according to our compatriots, the menu here is quite diverse, and the dishes are delicious and of high quality.

Very satisfied with the tourists were the staff of the Strandja hotel. So, according to our compatriots, all employees without exception are very friendly. Also pleased with the fact that most of the staff is fluent in Russian. Therefore, you will not have any language barrier here.
