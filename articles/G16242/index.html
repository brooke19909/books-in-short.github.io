Hotel "Olympic", Moscow: address, description, service and reviews

Moscow is a city that attracts a large number of guests from different parts of the world. There are a huge number of attractions that many seek to visit. Very often, guests come here for a business trip or for shopping. Regardless of the reason for his visit, many tourists, for lack of housing in the capital, are forced to look for it in hotels. One of the best and most prestigious among them is the Olimpiysky Hotel in the Olimpiysky Sports Complex.

general information

In this place to relax, there is always an atmosphere of comfort and expensive home furnishings. It is located in the building of the sports complex of the same name in Moscow, on its third floor, next to the Prospekt Mira metro station. Not far from here is the famous Catherine Park, which many foreign and non-resident tourists who visited the capital of Russia tend to see.

In their reviews of this hotel, guests talk about how each visitor is treated with great attention. In addition, the guests are pleased with the pricing policy set here - the daily cost of apartments in the hotel starts at 2500 rubles.



Rooms

Hotel "Olympic" in Moscow is a rather small place to stay; its room fund offers only 24 apartments for rest, presented in different categories. All guests staying here are strictly prohibited from smoking in rooms - a fine is prescribed as a punishment. Unlike many other similar places, pets are allowed here.

The room types provided at the Olimpiysky Hotel are presented in two formats: general and individual.

In all apartments of the standard and superior class, there is a mandatory bathroom with a set of plumbing necessary to maintain good hygiene. Bathroom with bathtub combined. Bathrobes, bathrobes and slippers are provided.

To create a more favorable climate, the rooms have fans.



Shared rooms

Hotel "Olympic" 2 * (Moscow) provides dormitory rooms for two and four persons, depending on the number of beds. In them, travelers can be taken a bed, the cost of which is about 850 rubles per night.

The furniture has the required number of single beds, there are cabinets for a small number of things, as well as one working area in the form of a desk and a chair. The general types of rooms at the Olimpiysky Hotel have wireless Internet access, which absolutely everyone can use, without any restrictions. In their free time, guests can watch cable channels broadcast on a small TV.

The general type rooms do not have private bathrooms and toilets - guests can use the shared rooms on the ground floor of the hotel.



Standards

Standard rooms at the Olimpiysky Hotel (Moscow) are quite well arranged for living, they are designed for two guests, but one person can also stay here. This type of apartment has a bathroom and an individual bathroom, fully equipped with the necessary fixtures.

The place to sleep here is represented by a large modern double bed, on which there is an orthopedic mattress, two curbstones are equipped near it. A large and very spacious wardrobe is provided for vacationers' things, and for a pleasant leisure - a plasma TV connected to cable TV.

The standard rooms have an excellent work area, represented by a desk and chair. Often, guests of the Olimpiyskiy Hotel (Moscow) who are staying on a business trip respond to it in a positive form.



Improved

Apartments of this group are double, but, if necessary, they also provide single occupancy. Also, if necessary, an extra rollaway bed can be installed here. According to vacationers, the rooms are decorated in a harmonious combination of white and gray colors, which is not annoying and sets up for a quiet, favorable pastime.

As for the furniture equipment, the standard version of the superior apartments includes a bed, a wardrobe, a pair of nightstands, a desk and a chair. There is also a medium-sized plasma TV. Guests have free Wi-Fi access.

From the windows of the rooms there is a beautiful view of the streets of Moscow. In the room stock there are several apartments of this type that do not have windows - in all other respects their device is identical.

The guests of these apartments have the opportunity to attend the buffet, the cost of which is included in the price for daily accommodation.



Service

The hotel "Olympic" provides a pretty good service, which is reflected in the comments of tourists regarding accommodation in his apartments.

According to the guests, the room service is excellent here, which provides for restaurant service in standard and superior rooms, as well as food delivery directly to the apartments. Twice a week in all types of rooms, a general cleaning is arranged, as well as the replacement of bed linen and towels.

All travelers who relax at the Olimpiysky Hotel (Moscow) can contact the administrator for recommendations regarding accommodation, the location of urban infrastructure, and the provision of certain services. It can be found at the front desk, which works around the clock. If necessary, luggage storage can be arranged here, which is carried out on a paid basis.

All guests of the Olympic Hotel (Prospekt Mira metro) can take the opportunity to call a taxi to the hotel for free, as well as deliver mail to the room.

Of the paid services, dry cleaning and laundry services, which are paid extra, are very popular.



For business

The description of the Olimpiysky Hotel often says that it has all the conditions for living people associated with business, as well as for doing business. A large number of reviews in this category indicate that the hotel has a large conference room. It is well equipped from a technical point of view: there is video equipment, lighting equipment, as well as a standard set of office equipment. During events, guests can sit in comfortable chairs at the tables that are installed here.

This place is perfect for presentations, meetings with business partners, negotiations, etc.

Additional features

The Olympic Hotel (Moscow) has the opportunity to visit the shops located directly on its territory. In them, many travelers purchase souvenirs for their near and dear ones, as well as traditional Russian goods. There is a minimarket where it seems possible to purchase a small amount of food.

The hotel provides a shuttle service for guests arriving at major transport hubs. It is paid and, according to guest reviews, is provided in a rather comfortable car with air conditioning, the presence of which allows tourists not to languish from the heat in the warm season and not to freeze in winter.

For guests who arrive at the hotel with their own car, it is possible to park it in a guarded parking lot, which is located on the territory.

Nutrition

In the place where the hotel is located, there are a huge number of restaurants, bars and cafes in the district where you can have a tasty snack. Often, travelers share comments on the network regarding certain interesting objects, as well as the features of their visit. However, many tourists are still advised to drop in a restaurant at the Olimpiysky Hotel, where they can be pleased with excellently prepared dishes of Russian and European cuisines.

Here, according to vacationers, there is a wide selection of fairly nutritious and vibrant dishes, the presentation of which is very interesting. The menu also has an excellent selection of wine and other alcoholic drinks, a small assortment of cocktails is presented.

Upon request, a children's menu can be provided.

Prices

The cost of living at the Olimpiysky Hotel is pleasantly surprising, since its rates start from 850 rubles - this is the cost of a bed in a quadruple room. If we take into account the common rooms designed for two, then the cost of a place in them is somewhat more expensive - it is 1250 rubles.

If you want to rent individual apartments, you need to be ready to pay from 3200 rubles - this is the cost of living in a standard room. If a guest wants to settle in more comfortable conditions, then it will cost him at least 3,500 rubles per day. In the event that you are interested in comfortable living, but it does not matter if there are windows in the apartments, then an ideal option in this case would be a double double for 2500 rubles per day.

Guests consider this cost of living quite acceptable, especially when compared with the figures given in other hotels in Moscow, and for the same conditions.





Reviews

Often on thematic resources you can find reviews regarding the rest in this hotel. Many of them say that there is excellent service and a fairly highly qualified working staff. Administrators working at the front desk can easily communicate with foreign tourists, as they are fluent in English and some other languages.

The work of the room service is often evaluated positively, guests talk about the fact that the maids reserve absolutely clean apartments. As for the work of cooks in the restaurant, it is also at its best. Tourists also note on the positive side the availability of restaurant service in rooms, if you wish, you can order breakfast here for only 120 rubles - this, according to the guests, is very cheap.

Many guests are pleased with the decoration in the rooms. They celebrate the excellent homely atmosphere that prevails in the apartments of the hotel. It is quite comfortable to be here and relax, which is facilitated by a pleasant color scheme in the interior, as well as modern comfortable furniture.

Non-resident tourists often note the advantageous location of the hotel and the proximity of interesting sights to it. The most popular among them are: Durov Theater, Suvorov Square, Catherine Park, Pushkin Museum of Fine Arts, as well as the Sphere Theater. Also, very often guests of the Olimpiysky sports complex become its guests.

How to get there

Hotel "Olympic" is located almost in the central part of the capital of Russia - 3.5 km from it. Such a favorable location is often noted by tourists in their reviews.

Near the hotel there are two metro stations: Prospekt Mira and Dostoevskaya, so you can get to this place by this type of public transport. If you want to use land-based public transport, the best option would be bus number 24, the stop of which is located almost at the entrance to the hotel.

If the guest is going to drive a car, then he needs to move towards the intersection of Olympic Avenue and Durov Street. On the right side there is a parking lot where you can park the car.

Hotel address "Olympic": Moscow, Olympic Avenue, 16, building 2.
