Bolshevik, Kherson region - map, pensions

If you are thinking about where to go on vacation so as not to spend large sums of money on a trip and a ticket, pay attention to the village of Bolshevik. Kherson region, in which it is located, is located in the south of Ukraine. If you choose a boarding house on the Black Sea, then during the holiday season you can enjoy plenty of fun in the warm waters, sunbathe and spend your vacation unforgettable and at the same time budget.

Several boarding houses have been built in the village and its environs. You can choose any one you like and have a great rest on the Black Sea coast. The map of the village will help you find your way.

"Tatyana"

This pension is located in a quiet corner, in the eastern part of the village, at the address: Kherson region, the village of Bolshevik, 32 Stepnaya street.

From the boarding house to the sea you will reach in 10-15 minutes. If you want to travel faster, you will be brought to the beach, as well as other residents of Tatyana. You can arrive by your car, leave it in a free parking lot and at any time drive out not only to the beach, but also to car rides.

Very close to the pension there is a shop where you can buy groceries and cook in the equipped kitchen. A bar is also nearby. Here you can spend some evenings to relax and unwind.





In the closed, round-the-clock guarded territory of the boarding house, there is Wi-Fi, a children's playground. The territory is well-groomed. It is nice to sit in the shade of openwork arbors, admiring the colorful flower beds and flower beds.

You can rent a 2, 3 or 4-bed room. Those who wish can use the sauna for 100 hryvnias per hour.

Primorye

This guesthouse also has Wi-Fi and free parking. Here is the address of "Primorye": Bolshevik, Kherson region, Primorskaya street, 13.

For guests to choose from - 2-4-bed rooms. The pension has a kitchen with hot water. You can buy food in a nearby store and cook your favorite dishes in the kitchen.

You will reach the beach in 10-15 minutes. Those who have already visited Primorye, note the well-groomed territory, they say that there are a lot of flowers. Reviews concern the cleanliness of showers and toilets. The staff often cleans the bathroom, which is like vacationers.

Also, many speak positively about the beach, talk about the fact that there is a lot of entertainment, and the sea is clean and warm.

It will tell you where these or those pensions are located, a map. The Bolshevik (Kherson region) was also chosen by the Parus boarding house. It is located on Morskaya street. As you can see on the map, this street fully lives up to its name and is located in close proximity to the sea. It is only 30 meters away. Therefore, at any time you can plunge into the warm waters or lie down on the beach and sunbathe.





It is easy to get to the pension, as there is a bus stop (terminal) nearby. There is also a market where you can buy inexpensive fruits and products.

Parking for cars at the hotel is paid and is 10 hryvnia per day. Meals are also paid, but inexpensive - breakfast, lunch and dinner will cost you 100 hryvnia.

You can cook in the kitchen-dining room, while a fee is charged from room 20 hryvnia. You will also have to pay for Wi-Fi, but the Internet works not only on the territory of the hotel, but also on the beach.

The boarding house staff will offer you exciting excursions. You can visit the Skadovsk Dolphinarium, go fishing or visit a geyser. All questions of interest can be asked by phone: 0985426625.

"Amazon"

This guesthouse is located at: Bolshevik village, Kherson region, Odessa street, house 9. It is located very close to the sea, which you can reach in 1-5 minutes. Vacationers are provided with 2, 3, 4-bed rooms, which have air conditioning, furniture, TV, a bathroom with hot and cold water.





On the territory there is a landscaped barbecue area. There is a barbecue with grill and skewer. Vacationers in the boarding house are provided with a comprehensive 3 meals a day.

An important condition is prepayment. Without this, booking a room is not possible. If you fail to arrive, then the prepayment is not refundable.

Rest in Bolshevik (Kherson region) - this is primarily swimming in the sea, beach holidays. On the Black Sea coast, which is located next to the Amazon, there is a sandy beach. At sea, you can ride a jet ski, yacht, boat. The resort has an amusement park, children's attractions. You can visit nightclubs, discos, go to bars, cafes.

"Assol"

This pension is located on Primorsky Street, at 44 V. The three-story building has double, triple and quadruple rooms, which, in addition to furniture, have televisions and refrigerators. The block, which consists of two rooms, has a toilet and shower.

If you want to stay in one of the junior suites, you can use the kitchen available here and enjoy the coolness on a hot day, thanks to the presence of air conditioning.

Internet, parking are available to all residents of this pension.
