First, draw a template on plain paper. To do this, select the size of your bookmark. The best option is the side is 7 cm. Therefore, carefully draw a 7 by 7 cm square. Now that you have made the pattern (which, incidentally, can be used for other bookmarks), put it on a cardboard and circle with a pencil. Now cut it out!
