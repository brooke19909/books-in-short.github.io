Cheap hotels of Simferopol: for tourists

The largest administrative and cultural center of autonomy - Simferopol, is located in the very center of Crimea. The city has a central airport, railway and bus station. In the summer months, the airport operates at full load. Airplanes arrive from many cities of Russia. In winter, the main departure points are Moscow and St. Petersburg. The city, which is the main transportation hub, is used by tourists as a transfer point on the way to the sea.

Help for travelers

Arriving at night or with a long wait for transport that will deliver to your final destination, you will feel the need to stop somewhere.

The main thing that should be done upon arrival is to study the inexpensive hotels of Simferopol located near the airport and railway station, depending on what type of transport the tourist arrived.

Hotels near the airport

Near the airport, a 15-minute walk away, is the Hotel-Profilaktory. The second TEShotel is a 5-minute drive from the airport. In both hotels, round-the-clock registration, comfortable rooms with a kettle and TV, Wi-Fi is available throughout. Breakfast is included. These are the most convenient hotels in Simferopol. Inexpensive, near the airport, which is also important.



If the train arrived at the railway station at night, or some other unforeseen circumstances appeared, and you do not want to spend the night in the forecourt, you can rent a room in hotels nearby.

Hotels near the railway station

There are four inexpensive hotels in Simferopol, the prices of which are consistent with the service, and hotels are located near the station.

  1. Hotel "Lux" is 5-7 minutes from the station. The rooms are equipped with everything necessary, there is free Wi-Fi, breakfast is included in the price of the room.
  2. Hotel Victoria is located a 10-minute walk from the railway junction. Beautifully furnished, well-furnished rooms with Wi-Fi. Breakfast is included.
  3. Mini-hotel "On South" (10 minutes walk from the station), in which Wi-Fi throughout, breakfast. The rooms are well-maintained.
  4. Sportivnaya Hotel is also a 10-minute walk from the property. The hotel has Wi-Fi, comfortable rooms, breakfast is included.

Despite the fact that these are inexpensive hotels in Simferopol, nevertheless they are hotels that meet European standards. In addition to the obligatory cafes and dining rooms, they have conference rooms with all the necessary equipment. The hotels are working around the clock and have rooms of any cost and level of comfort.



Simferopol is a historically interesting place like the whole Crimean peninsula. If there is one or two days, then you can find Simferopol hotels inexpensively, next to the monuments of architecture and combine the wait with a pleasant pastime.

Hotels near the Botanical Garden

It is possible to have a good rest and gain strength before the further road in the Botanical Garden (Salgirka Park). There are gazebos and fountains in the park. You can wander along the alleys and admire the flowers. Near the garden are cheap hotels of Simferopol.





  1. Hotel Marseille, 700 meters from the garden, 5 km from the train station. There are comfortable rooms, a cafe where you can order three meals a day, Wi-Fi, your own taxi. Nearby is the countryside estate of Count Vorontsov.
  2. Zvezdnaya Hotel is located a 10-minute walk from the Botanical Garden, 4 km from the railway station and next to the bus station. It offers excellent rooms with balconies, Wi-Fi and a cafe. Nearby is the Cave of Chokurcha.

If you have to wait for a transplant with a child, it is best to visit Gagarinsky Park, which stretches between the railway station and the city center. Adults can rent a catamaran and enjoy the beauties of a local pond, while numerous attractions await children.

Hotels near Gagarinsky park

The cheapest hotels in Simferopol are located next to the park. Gagarin.



  1. Hotel Viktoria. From it 750 m to the park and 10 minutes walk to the station. There is a sauna, pool, luggage storage. Nearby is the Chocolate Museum.
  2. Hotel "Dayana" (400 m to the park). There is a restaurant, bar, playground, pond with fish for fishing, free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel. Comfortable rooms of various cost and comfort.
  3. Mini-Hotel Prague is located 5-8 minutes from Gagarin Park and 700 meters from the railway station. There is free Wi-Fi, a bar. Well-appointed rooms range from economy to deluxe. Near art museum, supermarket and market.

Hotels near the Ethnographic Museum

By winter, when the influx of tourists is reduced and the rooms are free, the prices of Simferopol hotels are necessarily reduced. Accommodation is not expensive, for example, in November.



  1. Hotel Simple Things (360 meters to the museum, 15 minutes to the railway station). The exterior of the hotel resembles a castle. All rooms are well-maintained, have their own restaurant, Wi-Fi. Nearby is the strange house of Taras Belozerov (the first nursing home and the first free hospital in the country).
  2. Hotel Serov (475 meters to the museum, 15 minutes walk to the railway station). Next to it is the Puppet Theater. The hotel has: comfortable rooms for smokers and non-smokers, laundry and hairdresser, car rental, Wi-Fi, room service. On places for children when placing a discount. Children under 3 years stay free of charge.

In autumn you can visit the Ethnographic Museum and, before setting off on a further journey, stock up on luggage of knowledge about the history of Crimea. Within walking distance from the museum are several hotels.
