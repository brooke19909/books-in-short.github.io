What documents are needed for a mortgage: assistance to the borrower

With the help of a mortgage, citizens can become full owners of residential properties. This is the most common option for buying an apartment in Russia on the security of the acquired or existing property. In case of non-payment (non-execution) of the loan, the mortgaged property is put up for sale, and the proceeds from the sale go to repay the main debt. Today, almost all credit organizations issue mortgages on favorable terms, but you can find out what documents are needed for a mortgage at banks.





In Russia, mortgage programs are supported at the state level and in special mortgage agencies. First, the borrower will need to pass a solvency test at a credit institution. What documents are needed for a mortgage? The main documents are a passport and a certificate confirming income (2 personal income tax). It is also permitted to provide income in the form of a bank. After preliminary selection, the future borrower will require documents that confirm their labor, marital and social status.

What documents are needed for a mortgage in the second stage?

The creditor bank will require copies of the following documents: employment record, tax identification number, insurance certificate, diploma of education, military ID, marriage (divorce) documents, birth certificate and description from the place of permanent work. At the next stage, documents of title for the loan facility, as well as a BTI passport with a technical description of the property are submitted. This is a standard package that Sberbank necessarily requires.

Mortgage: what documents are needed additionally





Each credit institution puts forward its own conditions and a list of documents. The loan manager thoroughly examines the files of the borrower and considers applications on favorable terms for the client. For example, a social mortgage with state subsidies requires highly targeted documents from a person. The exact list can be found in local authorities that deal with these issues.
