Excavations

Each visitor, regardless of age, can do archaeological excavations in search of traces of prehistoric animals. A person will be given a simple inventory - a spatula, a bucket, a brush. On the territory of a rather impressive sandy quarry, you can try to find the remains of ancient dinosaurs. Nobody will limit you in time, voluntary "archaeologists" conduct searches absolutely free.



How to get there

Dinopark is located twenty kilometers from Antalya. You can get here on the intercity express train, heading towards Kemer. He leaves from the bus station or from a stop at the Migros shopping center. You can get a sightseeing bus, as part of a group or on your own.

Dinopark (Phuket, Thailand)

The island of Phuket is famous for its huge number of attractions and excursions to memorable places. Among them, Dinopark is especially popular. There are many attractions that will be interesting to visitors of any age. This "Dinopark" is an unusually designed area designed for mini-golf.

The park has a beautiful green area, on which there are caves, waterfalls, artificial reservoirs. But the main "heroes", of course, are dinosaurs and other prehistoric creatures.

The figures of the lizards, as well as in Turkey, are made in full size. This creates a special flavor to this place. Amazing animals are buried in thickets of rare shrubs and trees. As soon as dusk sets in, all this beauty is filled with light and sound effects.
