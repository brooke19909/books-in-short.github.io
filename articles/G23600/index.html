Hostels in Sergiev Posad: an overview of the popular

Sergiev Posad is a small city in the Moscow region, which is famous for its rich cultural heritage. The settlement was included in the Golden Ring of Russia in 1969. Often tourists come here, who have a question, where to stay. Hostels in Sergiev Posad offer quality services at an affordable cost. Consider the most popular.

Fox House

The facility is located on 96 Red Avenue, 800 meters from the railway station. This place is often chosen by people who come to Sergiev Posad on a business trip. The hostel was opened only in 2014, so all the furniture here is new and well maintained. A total of nine rooms are presented. You can check in here any day from 14:00, check out from 12:00.

Fox House is a hostel in Sergiev Posad with low prices. For one berth here you need to pay about 600 rubles. For that kind of money, the guest will be accommodated in a six-bedded room without meals. You can also stay in a quadruple room for 700 rubles. There are also family apartments with two single beds and a bathroom. For the daily rent of such a room you will have to pay 1,500 rubles.

There is also one room for a couple with a large double bed. The cost of such accommodation is from 1800 rubles. Each room has wireless internet. Toilet and shower on the floor, as in many other hostels in Sergiev Posad. Absolutely free of charge, guests can use a shared hairdryer, iron and ironing board. The hostel has a kitchen with the necessary equipment.

Hostel "Like at home"

This is an economy class hostel in Sergiev Posad. The cost of a bed here starts from 400 rubles. For that kind of money, the guest will be accommodated in a dormitory room with five bunk beds. There are separate rooms for women and men. Amenities are offered on the floor. Meals are not included in the price. A little more expensive will be accommodation in an eight-bedded room. For a bed here you will have to pay 550 rubles.

Reviews show that the hostel daily provides wet cleaning. Bed linen can be replaced upon request. Everyone can use the library or wireless Internet. You can cook in the shared kitchen. Quality cookware is offered. Grab a bite to eat in cafes and restaurants located in the immediate vicinity.

Hostel "Like Homes" is located at 22 Zheleznodorozhnaya Street, one and a half kilometers from the city center.

Nice hostel

On the side street of the Red Army (house 212) in Sergiev Posad, one of the hostels of the Nice Hostel network provides its services. Anyone can stay here at an affordable cost. For 650 rubles per day, men and women can live in a common room with bunk beds. There is no toilet and shower in the room. Amenities are provided on the floor.

For a double room with one double bed you will have to pay 2100 rubles per day. Such a room already has its own toilet and shower. Extras include a safe. Wireless internet is available for free.

Hostel "Nice" in Sergiev Posad attracts guests with a convenient location. From here you can get by public transport to anywhere in the city. Near the hostel there is a parking lot, which guests can use for free.

"The Enchanted Wanderer"

If we consider hostels in Sergiev Posad, located near the village, it is worth paying attention to this institution. It is located at the address: Valova street, house 50. For one bed here you will have to pay about 600 rubles. For that kind of money, a guest can be accommodated in a quadruple room with two bunk beds. Amenities are provided on the floor. There are separate female and male numbers.

Those who want to stay comfortably should pay attention to the room with two single beds. This room is ideal for family living. You will have to pay about 1000 rubles per day. There is no bathroom in this room either.

The hostel has its own kitchen. For 200 rubles, guests can order breakfast. Each room has wireless internet. Free of charge, you can use a hairdryer, iron, dishes in the kitchen.

Hotel Orthodox

If other hostels in Sergiev Posad are busy, you can pay attention to this place. A small well-kept hotel is located in the very center of the city in a church house connected to the bell tower of the Church of the Archangel Michael. Most often, guests stay here Sergius Lavra. The exact address of the hotel: 21 Berezovy pereulok.

Guests can be accommodated in one of ten comfortable rooms. For a day you have to pay about a thousand rubles. Facilities are provided on the floor, and there is no possibility to cook here. On the territory there is a dining room with a wide range of dishes. Ready meals can also be brought with you. Bedding is provided free of charge. Everyone can use a hairdryer, ironing board, and iron.
