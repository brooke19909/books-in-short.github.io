Details on why the screen does not flip over the iPhone

Often, the newly-minted iPhone owners have a question about why the screen on the iPhone does not flip. The fact is that this feature is provided by a small device built into the device - a gyroscope, and it, like any other part, may fail, but the reason may be different. How to find out why the screen on the iPhone does not flip and what to do about it?

Signs of malfunctioning gyroscope

To understand that the gyroscope has stopped working is very simple. There are a number of symptoms that indicate problems:

  • the screen image does not flip when you change the position of the gadget in space;
  • the desktop does not always rotate, arbitrarily changes its position and does not coincide with the position of the device.

It should be borne in mind that the screen may not rotate when the iPhone is on a horizontal surface. In addition, auto-rotation is not supported by all programs, so the best way to check the health of the gyroscope is to run some standard application, for example, ‚ÄúCalculator‚Äù. If the orientation and dimensions of the calculator do not change when you turn the smartphone, the problem exists.

Incorrect settings

First you need to perform the simplest steps: restart the iPhone and delve into the settings. Very often, the reason may be that the function is simply turned off.

This is easy to check - when the option is disabled, a characteristic icon appears in the status bar (an image with a lock and an arrow that goes in a circle). If this icon is on the smartphone screen, then here's how to launch a function that will flip the screen on an iPhone:

  • go to "Settings" using "swipe", up on the main screen from the bottom of the display;
  • Find and click on the icon described above;
  • check the auto-rotate in the "Calculator".

Interfering modes enabled

Owners of recent iPhone models, such as 6 Plus, 6S Plus, 7 Plus, may also be wondering why the screen on their iPhone does not flip. In their case, this may be due to the fact that the zoom function is enabled. This mode allows you to display larger icons. This is very convenient for using small menu items, but prevents the rotation of the picture. In order to correct the situation, you need:

  • go to the "Settings";
  • select the item "Brightness";

Mechanical breakdown

The most unpleasant and difficult to fix reason why the screen on the iPhone does not turn over may be hiding in a mechanical breakdown. The gyroscope is very fragile and often breaks when the device falls or when it hits hard. A feature of mechanical failure is that its consequences begin to appear gradually:

  • First, the iPhone starts to respond incorrectly when you rotate the screen;
  • then the orientation of the desktop on the screen begins to change several times, regardless of the change in position of the smartphone in space;
  • in the end, the image stops turning over at all.

Professional help

If the cause of the idle screen rotation is still a mechanical breakdown, it will not work to fix the problem without a specialist. It is recommended that you contact an Apple Service Center, especially if your iPhone is under warranty. It should be remembered that the warranty repair of the device often takes some time, because specialists carry out diagnostics and full examination of not only the display, but the entire smartphone as a whole.
