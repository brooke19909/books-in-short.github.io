Men's and women's knitting sweater: scheme

In the new season, the leading position in fashion items is held by a knitted collection. In boutiques you can get a lot of different things from machine or handmade yarn, but if you have knitting skills, then everything becomes much simpler. You do not have to buy knitted sweaters with knitting needles, which the novice will quite cope with the schemes. You can independently create any model you like.

You can find popular models of men's and women's sweaters with knitting needles with schemes in this article.





Yarn selection and patterns

Before you start creating a masterpiece, you need to prepare and fulfill some conditions. Choose your knitted knitting sweater. The layout of the model should be carefully studied. Decide on the color and material of the yarn. It can be natural wool or threads containing synthetic material. It all depends on what kind of result you want to get in the end.

Tool selection

After you have chosen yarn, you need to pick up knitting needles for it. Remember that the thickness of the thread should be approximately equal to the thickness of the tool. Only in this case you will get a harmoniously connected canvas.

There are many options for knitting a sweater. The creation scheme may indicate the need to have standard long knitting needles or a circular tool. Also, when creating patterns, you may need additional stocking knitting needle.

Test item

There is another stage of preparation for work, which must be completed before knitting a sweater with knitting needles. Schemes of all works require the creation of a so-called probe. You need to select a tool with a suitable yarn with a yarn that has a size of 15 loops in 15 rows. After that, you need to measure it with a ruler or centimeter and use the initial mathematical knowledge to make a calculation. Your task is to find out how many loops and rows is one centimeter of work.





Knitting patterns

Sweaters for women, men and children are now a great many. This can be a model with a high neck or, conversely, a deep neckline. With classic long sleeves or without them at all.

Knitting technique

In the described models, the following knitting elements are used.

Elastic:

  1. first row: two wrong loops, two front loops;
  2. second row: two front loops, two purl.

In fact, you should have purl over purl, facial over facial.

Facial surface:

  1. all front rows are knitted with facial loops;
  2. all seamy rows have seamy loops.

Shawl binding:

All rows are knitted with facial loops, regardless of the revolution of the product.

Pearl knitting:

  1. first row: one front loop, one wrong side;
  2. second row: one front loop, one wrong side.

In work on the front loop, it is necessary to knit the wrong one and vice versa. Be sure to consider the revolution of the product.





Knitting sweater for men : work scheme

Before you start a set of loops, you need to determine its quantity. To do this, measure the man for whom the work will be done. You will need the following sizes:

  • waist circumference
  • shoulder width;
  • torso height;
  • the length of the sleeve;
  • wrist circumference;
  • distance from neck to shoulder;
  • neck circumference.

After taking measurements, you can start knitting a sweater with knitting needles. The scheme of this model is quite simple and even beginner will be able to do it.





Knitting back

To get started, calculate how many loops make up the circumference of the hips. After that, divide the resulting number into two, as the back will be connected separately before the product. Dial the required number of loops and tie ten centimeters with an elastic band. After that, proceed to the creation of the main canvas. Knit the front surface as much as the height of the male torso.

When the required size is ready, carefully close the working loops without tightening the threads. Next, start creating the second part of the work.

Knitting front

Similar to the previous step, dial the loops on the knitting needles and tie ten centimeters with an elastic band. Next, knit the main fabric with the front stitch, but about 15 centimeters before the end of the work, you need to start creating a neck.

To do this, you will need additional knitting needles, as the work will have to be divided into two parts. Close the three middle stitches in a row and knit one side first. In each front row, close two loops. Continue to do this until you have the required length of the shoulder segment.

Do the same with knitting the symmetrical part. Close the working loops.





Sleeves

The knitting pattern for sweaters requires a separate creation of sleeves. Dial the number of loops equal to the size of your wrist and tie an elastic band ten centimeters. After that, knit the sleeve with the front smooth to the elbow. Next, in each front row, you need to add one loop. To do this, knit the last loop of the front and immediately make the wrong one. As a result, you get two out of one loop.

Knit the sleeve to the desired size, then close the loops. In a similar way, tie the second part. Remember that a knitted men's sweater with knitting needles should be symmetrical. The scheme for creating symmetrical parts should be the same.

Assembly

When all the parts are ready, it is necessary to assemble them correctly and accurately. Knitted men's sweater with knitting needles assembly patterns are different. If you are a beginner, then choose the simplest zigzag seam. Sew the parts on the wrong side of the product and iron the fabric. The sweater is fully ready for the intended use!





Women's seamless pattern

Knitting patterns for women's sweaters are somewhat different from each other. You can create a model that will be connected according to the scheme of a male product. You can also show imagination and do an original and unique work. Before you start a sweater with knitting needles for women, the product circuit requires taking measurements. Find out the size of the following parts:

  • breast volume
  • volume of hips or waist (depends on the desired length of the product);
  • the length of the sleeve;
  • wrist length;
  • torso height;
  • height from waist to armpit;
  • neck volume.

Knit such a model is necessary on long circular knitting needles. Consider the required number of loops and choose the length of the tool under it.

Type loops on the knitting needles and start knitting work in a circle. Tie ten centimeters with a garter stitch. After that, move on to using the pearl pattern. Knit with such a pattern to the level where the hole for the sleeve will be.

Be patient, as the work will seem long and painstaking. All due to the fact that knitting the back and front occurs simultaneously. Your efforts will not be lost, thanks to your diligence you will receive a seamless sweater.

When you reach the level of the sleeve, you need to divide the work into two parts for this, take another circular knitting needle and transfer exactly half the loops to them. Continue to knit the rest as much as the distance from the armpit to the neck. Close the working loops. The back is ready.

Next, you need to refine the front of the product. Think about the collar of a sweater. At the required level, close part of the loops. Your work will again be divided into two halves. Knit the first side first. To do this, close two loops in each front row. When the required height is connected, proceed to work with the second part. Similarly to the first, close the loops in each front row.

At the end of knitting the main part, sew the work on the shoulder joints.





Knitting sleeves

In order to tie the sleeves for the sweater and not use the seams to connect them, you must use the following instructions.

Stretch the cutout for the sleeves with your hands a little and draw new loops from it with free working thread. In this case, you can use stocking knitting needles or give preference to a circular tool, but already having a shorter length.

So you scored a loop. Now you need to start knitting sleeves. To do this, knit in a circle all the same pearl knit. When you reach the level of the elbow, you need to close a few loops. To do this, through an equal number of connected elements, knit two loops together. This item can be skipped if you want to get an even wide sleeve.

When part of the product is almost ready, make cuffs. To do this, knit ten centimeters with a handkerchief and close the loops tightly.

Similarly, knit the second sleeve. Remember that they must be symmetrical. Only in this case you will get a beautiful and even product.

Neck

Before you finish the sweater with knitting needles, the scheme recommends making a neck. In this case, the product will look harmonious.

Stretch the head cutout with your hands a little and draw new loops around its perimeter with the help of free working thread and stocking knitting needles. If the sleeves were connected by a circular tool, then in this case it is also better to use it.

Tie three centimeters with a double elastic band and carefully close the working loops.

An alternative option for creating a collar can be a separately connected strap, which will subsequently be sewn to the main part of the work. In the same way, you can make a wide or high collar.

Hide the protruding threads with a hook or knitting needle and iron the product.





Conclusion

A self-made sweater will be an excellent gift option for a birthday, New Year or other celebration. You will save money on buying a presentation and present an original and useful gift.
