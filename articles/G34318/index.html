"Svetly" - residential complex, Yekaterinburg: location, description

Yekaterinburg is a city lost in the vast territory of the country, among forests and lakes. This is the largest administrative, scientific, industrial and educational center of the Ural region. The population in the city is already more than one and a half million inhabitants. Therefore, construction is ongoing. One such construction site is the Svetly LCD.





Location of the complex

In the southeast of the above city is located "Svetly", a residential complex. Yekaterinburg allocated a place for him right next to the Ring Road, from which an exit is made along a perfectly asphalt road leading directly to the residential complex. This is an excellent location given the proximity of the lake. The distance from the city center to the Svetly LCD is approximately nine kilometers.





Thus, residents moving to a permanent residence in the complex will be able to not change their usual way of life, since the developer located it at a fairly close distance from the city.

Description of the complex

Real estate in Yekaterinburg is represented by a variety of new buildings. Among them, the Svetly complex stands out. According to the project of the developer, it will be a whole quarter, five high-rise buildings will rise to the sky using a monolithic-frame technology. The height of each of them will be from twenty-five to thirty-one floors.





These buildings are being built one after another, as if alternating. The project of the developer was not limited to this. For owners of their own cars, and they are likely to be a sufficient number in the complex, open parking lots are equipped. Parking on which will be possible for one and a half thousand cars.

Apartments, decoration, cost

Svetly offers not very large families to move in, as the apartments are designed only in studios, one-room and two-room. That is, a large family is unlikely to be able to choose spacious and large apartments. They are simply not provided for by the project.

But the studios are being built quite spacious, their minimum area is twenty-nine square meters. The maximum reaches forty-nine meters. Next come one-room (up to fifty-three meters) and two-room (up to seventy square meters).





All premises are being repaired by the turnkey developer, including non-residential (commercial). Not all real estate in Yekaterinburg boasts such concern for its residents and tenants. Finishing includes laying the laminate, gluing white wallpaper for painting, installing all the necessary plumbing, tiles on the loggias and in the bathrooms. In addition, meters are installed for the consumption of heat, water and electricity. All loggias are glazed with PVC windows.

The minimum cost of the studio begins with the figure of one million seven hundred seventy thousand rubles. That is, a little more than fifty thousand rubles per square meter of living space.

External infrastructure

Svetly, a residential complex (Yekaterinburg), was built at a distance of about eight kilometers from the center of Yekaterinburg. But in order not to create additional inconvenience to its residents, the developer even provided for a minibus stop near the complex. It offers residents of the complex the entire infrastructure of the city, which can be easily reached.
