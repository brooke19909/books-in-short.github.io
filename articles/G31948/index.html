Biography of professional poker player Tom Dwan

Professional career

Until the age of 21, the guy played exclusively in online poker. The first significant tournament was in London in 2005. The prize pool of the tournament was 3 million pounds. He managed to earn a little over $ 12,000.

After the tournament in London, Tom began playing in other online tournaments. For example, he signed up for Poker Stars, Full Tilt Poker. In the same year, the guy was lucky to participate in live tournaments in the United States. At the World Poker Championship, Tom took fourth place, which brought about 350 thousand in prize money. The following year, at a tournament in Atlantic City, he took second place and won 250 thousand dollars.

Additionally, Tom has constantly participated in the World Series of Poker in Las Vegas.
