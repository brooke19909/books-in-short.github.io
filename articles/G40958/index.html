The receiver is a modern and technological device

The modern world cannot be imagined without radio technologies, with the help of which data can be transmitted over great distances. In the most general case, a signal transmitter, radio waves and a receiver are involved in this process. This is the simplest scheme that clearly describes the path that information travels at the time of its transmission.

Realities of the modern world

But now mankind practically does not use the technology of radio transmission, relying entirely on the Internet connection. The presented device has lost its meaning. In this regard, now the receiver is a compact device that monitors its own location, directly communicating with the satellite through the GNSS system.

The accuracy of such devices depends on several factors: design, implemented technologies, weather conditions, and even on terrain objects. Moreover, the transmitted and received signal may be distorted by the user's body. But usually the error in determining the location is insignificant - no more than 3-5 meters.





Professional equipment

The scope in which the receiver is used is quite extensive - it is determining the coordinates for building objects, conducting geodetic works, creating maps, reconnaissance, determining the location of a crashed vehicle. This was the main incentive to create a professional type of devices. This species is presented on the market in two varieties:

  1. Geodetic receivers. They include a special antenna and a controller that introduces errors in the measurements, thereby achieving the most accurate result.
  2. GIS-class devices. They are an industrial version of a pocket personal computer, in which a transmitter, especially software and a receiver, are built-in. This significantly reduces the error in determining the coordinates.

Now, due to the development of technology, it is impossible to draw a clear line between two types of devices - both equipment can replace each other under various circumstances.





Personal equipment

An ordinary person resorts to a similar kind of device in two cases: in order to determine his location and not get lost, to track any object or subject for the purpose of protection. Depending on the task, a personal satellite receiver can be portable. In this case, it is a small compact device. In addition, the receiver can be built into any other equipment, such as a laptop or phone.
