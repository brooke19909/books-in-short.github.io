Hotel Ideal Panorama Holiday Village HV 1 * (Turkey, Marmaris): photos and reviews of tourists

What is remarkable Marmaris? This Turkish city is more marked than others by a touch of Europeanism. Discos, night clubs, a free, if not to say, libertine way of life. It also has an ideal climate. The cool sea breeze disperses the heat even in July-August. Around Marmaris is a very beautiful nature. Mountains covered with pines surround the city in a semicircle. And there is a very calm sea. Sedir Island closes Marmaris Bay from storms. The coast is safe for swimming for children, but also interesting for divers - there are about fifty underwater sites in the district. And, in addition, EU citizens and Schengen visa holders can visit Greek Rhodes in one day - ferries sail to this island in only fifty minutes. But where to stay in Marmaris? Room Categories

Guest rooms are divided into Standard and Family Room. The last category is more expensive. Family rooms consist of two rooms - a bedroom with a door and a living room. And the size of the Family Room is more spacious (thirty-five square meters). This type of room is ideal for families with children. A clear advantage of the Family Room is kitchen and tableware, an electric kettle and bags for self-brewing of hot drinks. But, in general, the differences between family rooms and "Standards" are limited to this. The latter are available in both buildings and in the bungalow. And the panorama of them all opens up perfect, as stated in the name of the hotel Ideal Panorama Holiday Village HV-1. Where is better to order a room? Reviews in this matter contradict each other.
