How to make a sheath with your own hands?

The knife is one of the oldest inventions of mankind. Since antiquity, everyone has been using it one way or another. It has many applications and a huge number of advantages. But there is a drawback - inconvenience during transportation. Carrying a sharp knife is inconvenient, in addition, it is subject to environmental influences. And for a very long time a special device was invented for storing and carrying knives - the scabbard. They are different for different knives and perform different functions. And if a good hunting knife is available, why not make a beautiful and comfortable sheath with your own hands for it?



Material

It is necessary to choose material for the sheath, proceeding only from your desires and capabilities. If wooden scabbard is the result of carpentry, making leather scabbards is more suitable for tailoring lovers . The main thing is that the material should be light and strong enough. You can, of course, make the sheath of aluminum or steel, if you think that a hunting knife will look appropriate in them.

Leather sheath

Leather is a serious and stylish material, and the scabbard can be made of it with the help of a needle with thread and the same knife. First you need to mark out a piece of leather from which this miracle case will be made, with a margin of eight to ten millimeters per seam. You should also make an insert in the form of a cutting edge of a knife and a width of the same eight millimeters. It will be sewn into the seam so that the knife does not cut the threads. After that, the skin is softened with water and stitched. The bend can be made thinner in advance so that the knife does not hang in the sheath. When stitching, it is worth leaving a small area at the end not wired - for water drainage. After that, the skin is molded with a knife, treated with cream, wax or skin paint. Also, a thin plastic frame can be inserted inside the sheath to maintain shape. Simple do-it- yourself sheath for a knife is ready!



Problems

However, the leather sheath has one main drawback - they are solid. Therefore, it will be difficult or simply impossible to clean them. And since the knife is hunting, it is constantly dirty. Before sticking it into a leather sheath, it will have to be washed thoroughly. You can do it easier - make a folding scabbard with your own hands. The best material for this is wood.



Wooden scabbard

Do-it-yourself wooden scabbard is not so difficult. They can consist of two halves and a leather strap or tie cord. To begin with, each of the halves is individually cut out. They should be made so that one half is inserted into the other, but both can "ride" relative to each other. The inner surface of the parts is machined under the knife. He must freely enter and exit the sheath. After which the blanks are polished and varnished. It‚Äôs best to pick a tree to match the color of the knife handle. The halves are connected and tied with a cord (wrapped in a narrow strip of leather). You can attach a special spring plate inside so that the knife does not fall out of the scabbard. As you can see, making the sheath with your own hands is simple and useful - the tool will always be protected and safe for others!
