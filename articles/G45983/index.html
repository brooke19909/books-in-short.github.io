Gasoline motokosa: how to choose?

In the summer, the owners of household plots have a new problem - mowing the lawn. To achieve its attractive appearance, it is necessary to mow the lawn not only regularly, but also correctly. That is why it is so important that the gasoline brushcutter was chosen correctly, taking into account all the features. The indisputable advantage of this unit is its complete independence from the power source, that is, your lawn will be perfectly trimmed even if it is very far from the outlet. That is why for many the problem of choosing such a device as a gasoline scythe is quite relevant.



It is worth considering what to look for when buying it. The first thing to consider is the difference in petrol scythes by type of cutting tool. It can be plastic or steel knives, nylon fishing line or saw blades. Before the final selection of a suitable device, you must clearly determine which plants you have to cut. For simple grass, nylon fishing line will be enough. For shrubs, this type is no longer suitable. It‚Äôs best for the gasoline scythe to work with both the fishing line and the saw blade.



It is worth noting that this device works quite noisily, which causes a lot of discomfort. That is why people usually wear headphones when working with him. Gasoline brushcutter can be equipped with a two-stroke or four-stroke engine. The second option is a bit heavier, but it works an order of magnitude quieter. This is where the difficult choice arises: either a heavy unit and less noisy work, or a lightweight device, but a lot of noise. A good option would be to buy a lightweight device and noise-canceling headphones.

Gasoline motokosa differ in the shape of the handle. The choice of a particular model depends on the exact conditions under which you intend to work with it. For example, a device with a T-shaped handle is well suited for more open areas. The D-shaped form of the handle is more focused on working with hedges, shrubs, small trees and the like. The J-shape of the handle is the safest for the worker, since at the same time the person is at a certain distance from the cutting part and gearbox. This brushcutter works well when working with tall grass, and it should be used with saw blades.



Motokosa gasoline can be equipped with a straight or curved bar. In the first embodiment, the direct iron shaft serves as the transmission mechanism, and in the second case, the movement is transmitted by a steel cable. Although the first option is more reliable, it is also heavier. The second option is considered more maneuverable, which is very convenient.

In conclusion, we can say that there is no ideal model, there is a great dependence on the conditions of use of this tool and on the physical data of a person working with a scythe. Well-established products of such brands as Honda, Stiga, "Calm", "Foreman". However, the final choice is still yours.
