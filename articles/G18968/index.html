How to transfer money from Yandex to WebMoney



Particularly active users of electronic money sooner or later face the need to transfer their funds between wallets of different payment systems. Most often, this is Yandex. Money and Webmoney. In order for the translation procedure to be successful, it is necessary to observe several specific conditions, which we will discuss below.

So, before transferring money from Yandex to WebMoney, you need to get an account and, accordingly, a wallet in these payment systems. First of all, you need to identify your Yandex account, and for this you will have to submit to the service all the necessary documents that prove your identity. Usually this is passport data and identification number.

The next step, how to transfer money from Yandex to WebMoney, will be to receive a certificate in the Webmoney system , the level of which will not be lower than the formal one. To draw it up, you should contact the Certification Center of this system and also provide (enter in the form) all the necessary data. Plus, you will need to download a copy of your passport and ID number. Thus, the moderator will be able to check how much the data indicated in the form corresponds to reality, and will increase the level of certificate. Be sure to clarify whether the data in both systems coincide. If you find an error somewhere, you need to fix it.
