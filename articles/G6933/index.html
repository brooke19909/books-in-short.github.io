H7 bulbs: which shines better? Best H7 bulbs: manufacturers rating

Each car owner knows very well how important it is to watch the road well at night or in conditions of poor visibility. To make the process of driving on a car comfortable, safe and confident, the headlights should well illuminate the roadway. Therefore, the choice of lamps should be approached very responsibly. Often in many modern cars, H7 bulbs are installed as the head light . Which is better, try to find out based on the ratings.

H7 car lamps: specifications and description

Element H7 is a single filament lamp. It is intended for installation in four-headlamp optics of head light in cars where there is a separate reflector system for high and low beam. However, in the vast majority of cases, these lamps are installed only in the near.

For products of this type, a flange is characteristic. But the distance from the flange to the spiral is constant and fixed. This is done so that the glow point is always in focus.

Modern industry offers a variety of lamps with an H7 socket in different versions. All of them are manufactured according to their technologies, have certain parameters that characterize light, as well as power. Although the standard power for bulbs of this type is 55 watts. On sale you can find models with higher rates. But it is recommended to use them only in off-road conditions. In an ordinary car, which is designed to move around the city, the use of such bulbs will only lead to the on-board electrics being disabled due to overloads.

There are three types of lamps on the automotive market. These are halogen models, xenon and modern LED products. Let's try to determine the best H7 bulbs based on the data obtained in the tests.

If you install this lamp in the headlight, the driver will have more time to make a decision. After all, with such optics, he will notice an obstacle much earlier.

The light that the bulbs from this series generate is white when compared with standard models. The company has applied innovative solutions. The flask is filled with xenon, the design of the spiral is optimized, the spraying of the flask is partially blue. Light is close in temperature to daylight. This makes the eyes less tired and makes moving more comfortable.

As a result of tests conducted by experts, the light bulb in this series was able to show the best brightness results - 1,500 lumens. As for the indicators of illumination on the right side of the road, here, too, everything is much better than other representatives. In general, this lamp showed good results, and its characteristics are several times higher than the requirements. In addition, the product is completely safe for all surrounding drivers.

About the choice of lamps

When choosing, the consumer always wants to purchase the best model. It is recommended to consider factors that are among the most important. This is brightness, light comfort, lamp durability, price.

You should not buy inexpensive models, especially if the product is of dubious quality. Your safety while driving depends on which lamp will stand in the headlight. Choosing the right lamp, you need to focus on the reputation of a particular brand. However, sometimes there are specimens that shine better than models from a well-known manufacturer.
