Sonesta Beach Resort and Casino Hotel, Egypt: traveler reviews

Most of the tourists who go on vacation to Egypt, choose a hotel in Naama Bay, planning a fun and pleasant pastime not only at sea, but also in the bars, restaurants, nightclubs, which are famous for this tourist center and the most popular resort in Sharm El Sheikh. Hotel Sonesta Beach Resort and Casino 5 * is ideal for such a diverse and full of unforgettable moments of relaxation. It is built on a large scale in order to surprise with the richness of infrastructure and provide maximum comfort in everything. Rest here is equally comfortable all year round. The guests always have a lot of opportunities to have a wonderful time both on the hotel territory and outside it, the benefit is only to go out of the gate and in fact immediately get into the colorful festive cycle of fun and entertainment.



Location

Sonesta Beach Resort and Casino is located in the famous Egyptian resort of Sharm El Sheikh, in its most beautiful bay and the same city district of Naama Bay, not only in the central part, but closer to the edge, but still there are no interesting places of entertainment and good shops more than 10-15 minutes of calm walking. Next to the hotel, right along the coast, there is a promenade where you can walk, admiring the local beauties, or you can reach popular supermarkets among Russian tourists with fixed prices, for example, Karfur and Pyramids. Opposite the hotel are McDonalds, a bank branch, cafes and bars. The Old Town, attracting with its architecture and a large colorful market, is slightly less than 10 km. You can get there by taxi, paying from $ 3, or on local buses. For tourists, the ticket price is from $ 1, which must be negotiated with the driver when boarding. The airport from the hotel is also quite close, about 7 km, which the transfer overcomes in 10 minutes.

Rooms

Hotel Sonesta Beach Resort and Casino 5 * received its first tourists in 1994. Since then, the numbers have been updated several times. The last innovation (partial) was carried out here in 2009, which somewhat affects the state of furniture and appliances. The hotel was built in the design of the Egyptian village and consists of a central building, where there is only a reception service, and 32 two-story cottages, harmoniously arranged throughout the vast territory. Their location depends on the view from the windows, proximity to the sea, swimming pools, restaurants, access to the central street of Naama Bay, the ability to sleep at night and, in some way, the security of your stay. Of course, it is more comfortable to live in buildings closer to the center of the hotel. To get a room in them, according to tourists, it is advisable for reception staff to present a present in the form of a sum of money (from $ 10). The interior, functionality and quality of service do not depend on the location of the buildings.



The design of many rooms at Sonesta Beach Resort and Casino is quite curious. So, in them there is a hallway area from which you can get into the bathroom. The living room has an additional exit to the balcony (on the second floor) or to the veranda (on the first floor). In the same room there is a separate corner for bags and suitcases. Most of the rooms are quite spacious, very comfortable, have internal arched structures unusual for Russians, partitions, decorated with paintings and small colorful rugs on the floor. The total number of rooms in the hotel is 505 units.

Nutrition

The food at Sonesta Beach Resort and Casino 5 * is quite well organized. Reviews of tourists on this subject are somewhat divergent. Some people think that the menu does not have much meat and fruit, and someone is absolutely happy with everything, people thank the chefs who prepare delicious treats and the waiters who, with their excellent work, create a pleasant atmosphere for breakfast, lunch and dinner. According to the all-inclusive concept, which is offered by almost all travel agencies selling tours to this hotel, tourists can eat and receive drinks for free from 10 am to 12 pm. The main buffet meals are served at Le Dom and Citadel restaurants. Tourists, if desired, can choose any. More crowded in the "Citadel", which is associated with a more convenient location. In every Sonesta Beach Resort and Casino restaurant, the food is delicious and fresh, with many desserts, pastries, meat and fish dishes. The only gripe is that there are few fruits, but you can always buy them very cheaply in a supermarket or in a market. In addition to the two central ones, there are three a la carte restaurants - Indian, Italian and Lebanese. Each of them can be visited once a week for free. For a bite to eat, try the poolside bar and the beach. And in the Blue Wave bars, near the pool of the same name, and Cactus, free ice cream is served every day from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m.

The hotel has 5 bars - in the lobby, on the beach and near some pools. Here you can take beer, wine, tea, coffee, water, powder drinks, cola, phantom, juices. Bottled drinks and cans - for the money.
