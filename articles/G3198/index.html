Stone forest in Bulgaria: description, history and interesting facts

There are many unique places on our planet, saturated with mysticism and special energy. Scientists have argued for many years about the origin of natural phenomena, which conceal many mysteries, who have not come to a common point of view.

Amazing corners make people think a lot, and the Stone Forest in Bulgaria, which grew 50 million years ago near Varna and is an unusual ensemble of pillars of the most incredible forms, is such a miracle. From time immemorial, a huge massif had the glory of a sacred place, but the first mention of it appeared in the 19th century. But the natural monument, which is under the protection of the state, it was declared relatively recently.

The first mention of a mysterious place

It is curious that the first journalist who described the Stone Forest in Bulgaria was the Russian war correspondent V. Teplyakov. From his pen came a fascinating story about cylindrical giants driven into the ground, arousing interest from all scientists of the world, who set out to find out the true origin of the corner that had become popular.

Scientists trying to shed light on the origin of the valley

There are more than two dozen hypotheses of the origin of frozen sculptures. The most popular version says that a few million years ago there was a sea on this place, and at its bottom from different rocks stone layers formed, tightly connected to each other. Over time, the climate changed, the pond dried up, sand outgrowths turned into frozen tall columns resembling stalactites in caves, and rains and wind trimmed figured statues.



Some scientists are inclined to think that methane rising from the sea is the main reason for the appearance of such a popular attraction as the Stone Forest (Varna, Bulgaria). Thin streams of gas rushed upward, hitting the organisms that lived in the sea, which for a long time seemed to meld with the sand into a single whole. It is believed that precisely in the places where the gas was released, empty cavities appeared inside the colossus.

There is another hypothesis that experts support. It says that stone pillars are ordinary corals, and all the voids inside them were previously filled with plants that rotted over time.

Alien Versions

Not without theories related to alien civilizations. Some researchers believe that on the territory of the stone valley there was an ancient temple or observatory, and lighthouse pillars with a diameter of two or more meters received important signals from space.

Local residents are convinced that the Stone Forest in Bulgaria can not have a natural beginning. In fairness, it should be noted that while no version is correct, no one has guessed the secret of the valley.

Dividing stone pillars into groups

Natural formations located on the territory are conditionally divided into four groups.

The first is stone trees, which are lined up perpendicular to the road in several rows. The second is of interest due to the fact that powerful pillars are set on top of each other by some incredible force. The third group includes giants with a height of more than six meters.



But the most interesting stone forest in Bulgaria is the cliffs that form a ring, in the center of which stands a tall pillar. Ancient legends go about the fourth group, telling that one who goes around all the figures and touches the main one in the circle will catch luck by the tail, and the desired desire will certainly come true.

Near the stone columns, cameras and cameras often fail, the batteries are discharged, but if you move a little away, the technique starts working again. The fact that a powerful energy zone lies in the valley of stone sculptures is a universally recognized fact. Tourists openly declare the impact on the body of certain forces that relieve fatigue and give positive emotions.

Shuttle buses do not go to a popular tourist attraction located 18 kilometers from Varna, so the country's guests choose a guided tour. Many travelers get by rented vehicles, pre-devising routes, among which there is always Stone Forest (Bulgaria).



How to get to the corner saturated with magical atmosphere? You can get here if you take a taxi to the ancient Shumen.
