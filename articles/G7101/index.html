Grand Hotel Krasnaya Polyana - Russian answer to Courchevel

The Krasnodar Territory is famous for its many resorts, but only one of them stands out as it offers its visitors not a vacation at sea, but a ski resort. Such a place is the Grand Hotel Krasnaya Polyana, whose territory is approximately 65 hectares. Its location is extremely successful, as it is located near Adler Airport. The hotel is surrounded by the mountains of Aibgo, Achishkho and the Main Caucasian Range, whose height is 2.5 thousand meters, which allows almost completely excluding spindle weather, and the climate here is mild and warm. The very location of the grand hotel in Krasnaya Polyana dictates his occupation. This is the most famous Russian ski resort that meets international standards for the tourism business.

On its territory there are a cable car station, restaurants, hairdressers, beauty salons, fitness centers, spa salons, pools, a tennis court, a night club, bars, a children's center "Hat" and much more. The hotel offers its visitors to choose one of 124 rooms or 26 villas for living. The room stock available at the Grand Hotel Krasnaya Polyana includes standard rooms, standard rooms with a balcony, a suite, a studio suite, a family suite and villas.
