Mask sketch

Before we begin to make the costume, we will draw a sketch of the mask of the main character-maniac. If you don‚Äôt remember what the mask looks like, then watch the movie. Or we recommend using our sketch. We take paper and pencil.

How to make a Scream costume and mask

So, apply plasticine on top of the prepared sketch. We fashion a three-dimensional shape. Then we take an ordinary bandage and cut it into strips of thirty centimeters. Put them on a plasticine mold. Next, take a construction gun with silicone.

Squeeze out a bandage with a thickness of three millimeters. We put on gloves and carefully level the silicone. We wait for complete drying and apply another layer. Leave the mask. Dry and weather it.

Take the material and sew a hooded cloak. Matter choose a rectangular shape. In the middle, make a cutout for the head to make the suit look more realistic. Make cuts at the edges of the cloak.
