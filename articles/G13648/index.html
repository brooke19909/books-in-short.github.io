Koh Chang Resort & Spa, Thailand, Koh Chang: hotel description, traveler reviews

If tiredness from the frantic urban rhythm makes itself felt, you want a measured and relaxing holiday on the coast, surrounded by unspoiled nature, then a trip to the island of Chang, which belongs to Thailand, is what you need. Here, even an experienced traveler will not be indifferent to the white sandy beaches, the richest underwater world, amazing coral reefs, exotic vegetation, picturesque waterfalls and delicious Thai cuisine. And if at the same time you want more comfortable accommodation and first-class service, then you should pay attention to the resort complex Ramayana Koh Chang Resort & Spa 4 *.

Description

The four- star Koh Chang Resort & Spa welcomed its first guests in 2004. Since then, it was reconstructed more than once. Today, tourists are expected by a comfortable hotel, surrounded by pristine nature.





Guests are accommodated in bungalows decorated in the style of the ancient Asian epic Ramayana. Here, modern furniture and the latest technology are ideally combined with the eastern decoration of the premises. A distinctive feature of living rooms is a natural wood floor.

The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a TV with the maximum possible number of channels, a free safe, a telephone, wireless Internet, a minibar and a set for making coffee and tea.

Each room has a balcony, upon reaching which guests can enjoy beautiful views of their hotel with green areas.

The personal bathroom for tourists is carefully prepared by the hotel staff with personal hygiene products, as well as slippers and a bathrobe.





However, it should be remembered that when checking into this hotel, like in many other hotel complexes in Thai resorts, a deposit may be required. It can be a copy of a resident's credit card or the cost of one day in cash.

There are three restaurants and a bar in the Koh Chang Resort & Spa hotel complex, where you can have a snack and a glass of cocktail or other drink.

The hotel is ready to offer its vacationers a choice of different types of food: a buffet or a custom menu.





But no matter what option the guest chooses, all the dishes served will be equally tasty, fresh and healthy.

Service

It offers guests on the territory of the complex:

  • an outdoor pool surrounded by a tropical garden;
  • jacuzzi;
  • gym for individual training;
  • a spa center, whose specialists will be happy to offer a variety of beauty treatments, including Thai massage;
  • tour desk and organization of island tours for one day;
  • conference room for formal events;
  • bike and car rental;
  • parking;
  • organization of water sports.

For children, a playground is equipped, a shallow pool is built, an experienced babysitter is available on request.

All hotel guests can use the free round-trip transfer to Klong Prao Beach and to Koh Chang Waterfall, but there will be a one-way trip to it without payment.

Beach

Klong Prao Beach is about 1 kilometer away from the hotel. However, this does not bother tourists who choose the Koh Chang Resort Spa for an overnight stay, as all guests receive a free shuttle to / from the beach at a very convenient schedule. A bus leaves the hotel every hour and half an hour at the height of the tourist season.

On the coast, everyone can use beach hotel towels, sun umbrellas, sun loungers, mattresses.





The beach itself is very wide and large, completely covered with white sand, there are areas with a shadow.

The entrance to the sea is gentle, which is a great advantage for bathing young children.

The bulk of tourists are concentrated in the southern part of the Klong Prao coast, there is a well-developed infrastructure: shops, cafes and massage.

The farther to the north of the beach, the less tourists. Here you can easily find a secluded place and stay in silence, contemplating exotic landscapes.

Guest reviews

By reading comments on the Internet over the past few years from tourists who have visited these places, you can once again be convinced of the veracity of tour operators and their brochures. The resort complex Ramayana Koh Chang Resort & Spa 4 * is really what you need. Here silence and peace reign.

All guests note that the hotel complex has a fabulous territory surrounded by exotic greenery with numerous streams and bridges, spacious rooms with all the necessary accessories for a good rest, excellent, high-quality service and friendly, honest staff.

Tourists also give excellent marks to the chefs and waiters working in the hotel restaurants. Most guests admire the delicious and varied dishes served both buffet style and a la carte.

There are also valuable tips in the reviews. For example, many people recommend not renting a car, but a bike, for traveling around the island, since it will be much more convenient to travel on mountain roads.





In this Thai paradise, some tourists were able to find a negative point: there is no possibility for independent walks. Many wanted to walk independently from the hotel to the sea coast. At the same time, the tourists were not scared at all by the remoteness of the sea from the hotel, but the route itself, passing along the highway without a dedicated zone for pedestrians.

In general, all travelers were satisfied with their stay, if there were any trifles, then they were completely compensated by other advantages of the hotel.

Koh Chang Resort & Spa fully met their expectations. Most tourists would not mind returning to this Thai island, namely to this hotel.
