Hotel "Laguna", Lipetsk - review, description and reviews of tourists

One of the first questions people ask after returning from vacation is: where did you stay? The choice of the hotel is of paramount importance, and the level of comfort in the place of residence will have a huge impact on the quality of rest. Hotel "Laguna" in Lipetsk belongs to the category of premium accommodation.

Overview Information

"Laguna" in the city of Lipetsk is a premium hotel, rooms of various levels of comfort (from economy to VIP) are provided here, high-quality service, qualified staff perfectly cope with their duties. Guests are impressed by the excellent location of the hotel, comfortable rooms, friendly staff and amazing food in restaurants. Banquets are often organized here, wedding wedding registrations, conferences are held, business negotiations are held.

The nine-story building was built and commissioned in 2009. Since then, the reputation of the hotel has never been thoroughly criticized by the guests. The administration reacts very insignificantly to the comments very quickly, eliminating trifles that can negatively affect the quality of rest of the guests.

Location

Hotel "Laguna" in Lipetsk is located on the banks of the Voronezh River, there is also a sandy beach. The road to the business center of the city will not be long, within walking distance is the Lower Park, and in the evening, hotel guests can go to the Tolstoy Theater.

You can get from Chertovitskoe Airport in 1.5 hours by taxi or book a transfer. Tourists traveling on their own by car go to the Hotel Laguna at the address: Lipetsk, pl. Mira, 1d. Near the hotel there is a private secure parking, which guests use for free.

Which number is better?

In the Hotel Laguna (Lipetsk, Russia), you can choose the economy, standard, superior (Twin, Double), suites and VIP rooms for accommodation. The difference between them is in area, degree of comfort, view from the room and, of course, in price.

All 115 rooms have a modern interior, more reminiscent of the style of classical minimalism, when there is everything you need without unnecessary pretentiousness. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning systems, comfortable bathrooms with a hairdryer and toiletries, towels. The room is divided into a sleeping and working area. The first one has a comfortable bed with bedside tables, a flat-screen TV hangs on the wall, and the second one has a desk, a chair (there are sofas and armchairs in the suites and VIP categories), and a telephone.

At the Hotel Laguna (Lipetsk), newlyweds often stay on their wedding night. A beautiful room in romantic colors has been created for them; there is the opportunity to take a fragrant bath with an air massage.

Food in restaurants

There are two of them. The first is made in a traditional design and is called "Lagoon", it offers European and traditional cuisine. But the most favorite place for hotel guests is the second restaurant, it is called Sky (in the lane with English - "sky"). Almost every enthusiastic review writes about its good location on the ninth floor, where large, panoramic windows offer a unique view of the river and the city.

The hall is divided into zones, so fans of club music and dancing will not interfere with those who prefer a romantic candlelit dinner at ChillOut. A popular tepan show is organized for visitors, when a Japanese master masterfully cuts and prepares fish or another seafood dish in front of amazed guests. Judging by the reviews, banquets are often held in this place, they celebrate an important event in life, even local residents regularly come to this restaurant to take a break from everyday life.

Guests can use the room, breakfast, lunch or dinner delivery service. At the request of the client, employees will prepare a packaged lunch that you can take with you to work (if the tourist is on a business trip) or for a walk around the city.

Services at the hotel "Laguna"

A large number of business tourists come to Lipetsk. Here, ideal opportunities have been created for them to hold both short events designed for a small number of people (meetings, briefings, trainings, negotiations), as well as full-fledged conferences and seminars. To do this, either a meeting room with convenient places (up to 30 people) around the round table is provided, or a spacious conference room (area - 200 m 2 ). Both rooms are equipped with a DVD projector, light and audio-acoustic equipment, microphones, portable and stationary screens, LCD TVs, etc. Business travelers are also provided with a business center where you can use a printer, scanner, and photocopy machine.

A small spa complex has been prepared for the beautiful half of humanity, it offers beauty and wellness treatments. Guests respond with enthusiasm about the bath, which has a swimming pool and two lounges. After hot steam it is pleasant to plunge into a phytobarrel in the fresh air.

The small fitness center has all the necessary fitness equipment so that vacationers can keep fit on vacation or a business trip. A visit to the hall is included in the price of this hotel in Lipetsk.

Reviews

Tourists leave very enthusiastic comments about the Lagoon (Lipetsk), but among them you can also find words from those who were not satisfied. For example, some guests who reached the hotel by car noted a very uncomfortable crossing over the bridge and the roundabout near the Laguna. Others got into the hotel at the moment when there were interruptions in water due to an accident. The guests complained about the poor soundproofing in the rooms (however, the administration received a proposal to move to a more peaceful place of the hotel). Another unpleasant moment indicated in the reviews, which affected the quality of rest, was an alarm that worked several times. The guests were not evacuated, but no one explained the reason for the night commotion.

There are much more positive reviews, and the hotel has earned a rating of 8.5 out of 10. Guests describe the comfortable and sound furniture, comfortable beds with snow-white linen, on which they got enough sleep. The cleanliness of the rooms and the quality of the food did not cause any complaints. The guests are very pleased with the restaurant on the top floor of the hotel, with club parties that are held once a week. Vacationers staying in superior rooms (studio) appreciated the spacious bathroom with a bidet, a full set of personal hygiene items.

Many found the hotel pricing very democratic. Room rates start from 3000 rubles per day. (includes buffet breakfast, use of parking, gym).

Summarize

The hotel is more suitable for tourists visiting Lipetsk for business purposes. Here you can enjoy your vacation in comfortable rooms, enjoy amazing dishes in restaurants, use the spa complex and gym. The friendly staff tries to satisfy even the most demanding tastes of customers, quickly responds to comments, eliminating them in a short time.
