Artemis Hotel Apts 3 * (Cyprus / Protaras): hotel description, reviews

Protaras is a small resort town that is famous for its rocky bays and sandy beaches. Most often, families with children and tourists come here who are looking for a place for a relaxing holiday. A feature of the resort is a large number of apart-hotels located along the coast. Artemis Hotel Apts 3 * is one of the budget complexes, which are often chosen by budget travelers. What can he offer them?

general information

For its guests, the hotel opened its doors in 1996. It has a good location, which provides walking distance to the sea and to the central part of the resort. The complex is located on the second beach line, so you will have to walk 600 meters to the coast. Tourists note that the road to the sea with a leisurely step usually takes 15-20 minutes. Near the hotel is the famous beach of Fig Bay, as well as the Orthodox Church of Elijah the Prophet. Ayia Napa Monastery is 5 kilometers away, as is Cape Greco. Water World is 10 km from the hotel.





On a small territory of the complex Artemis Hotel Apts 3 * ( Protaras ) is a five-story building built in a classic Mediterranean style. It houses 106 rooms that are designed for small families. There are also spacious apartments in which a large friendly company can live. Pets are strictly prohibited. Check-in starts here at 14:00, and the hour of eviction is considered noon. Children and teenagers, as well as tourists staying in extra beds, receive a small discount.

Housing stock

Spacious apartment offers its guests Artemis Hotel Apts 3 *. They are distinguished by simple furnishings and attractive prices. All rooms have a small balcony or patio for tourists living on the ground floor. It offers beautiful views of the sea, mountains or cityscape.

All rooms are divided into studios and apartments. The studio consists of one room, which is divided into sleeping and living areas, as well as a kitchenette . Two people and a child can be accommodated here, who will be accommodated in two single beds and a sofa. Apartments are considered multi-room. It has a bedroom, a living room, a bathroom and a kitchenette . They can simultaneously accommodate four adult tourists. The equipment of the Artemis Hotel Apts 3 * apartments provides guests with a comfortable stay. Thanks to the availability of kitchen utensils, they can cook their own food at any convenient time. The room has a refrigerator, stove, electric kettle and microwave, as well as a set of dishes. The bathroom is equipped with reliable fixtures. Tourists can also find a hairdryer, ironing board, iron and cosmetics. The living room has a satellite TV, telephone and radio. Individual air conditioning is available only for a fee. The price includes daily cleaning, change of towels and linen twice a week.

Local infrastructure

Developed infrastructure, created specifically for tourists, offers the island of Cyprus. Artemis Hotel Apts 3 * is designed for vacationers who spend a lot of time outside the hotel, so the service here is not very diverse. The complex has its own car parking, intended only for guests. Leaving your car here is free. At the reception, tourists can also rent a car. The administrator will always help you arrange a quick currency exchange, tell you about interesting places on the island. Room service or wake-up service can be arranged for a fee .





Paid laundry facilities are available on site. Wi-fi works in all public places. You can connect to it for free by asking for a password from the administrator. You can also rent a small safe from him for storing money, documents and other valuables.

Food concept

When buying a ticket to the Artemis Apart Hotel , you should know that there is no all-inclusive system that Russian tourists love. Guests usually pay only breakfast, and the rest of the time they eat at Protaras restaurants and cafes . Half-board is available during high season, which includes breakfast and dinner. Meals are served buffet style in the hotel‚Äôs main restaurant. If there are not very many tourists in the complex, then they are usually served by the menu. Breakfast is continental. It also includes tea, coffee and other cold drinks. For lunch and dinner, they are not served.





There is also a poolside bar on site. Here guests can buy alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, cocktails, desserts, ice cream and fresh fruits.

Entertainment Organization

The choice of entertainment here is also small. For guests of the Protaras Artemis Hotel Apts 3 * hotel, an outdoor pool is open daily. Next to it is a small sunbathing area, where you can take sun loungers and umbrellas for free. Beach towels are not provided at the hotel. The hotel also has an indoor pool where you can swim in rainy weather. On the city beach, you can visit the water entertainment center, rent equipment for surfing or diving. You can also play volleyball.





The hotel has a gym. His visit is free for guests. There are also classic tennis courts, squash equipment, billiards and volleyball. Walking bikes are available for a fee. It is worth knowing that the hotel does not have a spa center and a beauty salon, but there is a small sauna. Non-gambling slot machines are located in the lobby, which will appeal to both adults and children.

Conditions for children

Hotel Artemis Hotel Apts 3 * is perfect for families with children. Most of the time there is a quiet environment, so parents may not worry that something will disturb the kids' sleep. A cot is available in the room upon request. If you need to leave, you can always call a qualified nanny who will sit with your child both day and night. Her services are provided for a fee.





In the outdoor pool there is a shallow children's section where children can swim under the supervision of their parents. For entertainment, a playground and a games room are also available. There is no animation program and a mini-club in the hotel, so parents will have to organize their children's leisure activities on their own.

  • Bad housekeeping. On the floor in the bathroom you can find someone's hair. Dust remains on the furniture. Sometimes even torn bedding is found .
  • There are always a lot of people in the pool, so you won‚Äôt be able to swim freely. The water in it is not very clean. Sunbeds and umbrellas also have to be occupied early in the morning.

  • The beach is quite far away, and you have to go back uphill. In the heat to overcome such a path can be difficult.

  • The hotel staff hardly speaks not only Russian, but also English. The administration ignores the comments of the guests, and the employees can be rude at all.
  • Music plays loudly at night in the bar, so it is impossible to sleep with the windows open.

Afterword

This is not to say that the Artemis Hotel Apts 3 * complex is an exemplary place for relaxation. The hotel has many shortcomings that can affect the quality of the vacation. However, the cost of living here is one of the lowest in the resort, so you still should not find fault with the service too much. Most often, young couples with children stop here, who prefer a relaxing holiday, older people, and sometimes youth companies also meet. The hotel can be recommended to economical tourists who prefer to spend most of the time outside their room. In this case, it will be an ideal place for an overnight stay.
