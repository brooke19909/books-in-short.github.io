LCD "Slavyanka-Skolkovo" ("Western Gate of the Capital"): the progress of construction

In Moscow, modern, most comfortable housing buildings are annually built and commissioned. LCD "Slavyanka" was supposed to be one of them, and became a leader among the capital unfinished buildings. It began to be built in 2002. The developer promised interest holders not only spacious apartments, but also a rich infrastructure, including a kindergarten, school, and playgrounds. Over the past 15 years, children managed to grow up, and their parents never received the treasured keys. The notorious project is now called the LCD "Western Gate of the Capital." His current developer has actively undertaken to catch up and plans to hand over the object by the beginning of next year. The construction came to life again, and the interest holders revived hope. This article offers detailed information about this housing complex, its advantages, disadvantages and the possibility of acquiring real estate here.

Location

For the construction of the residential complex "Slavyanka" ("Western Gate of the Capital"), a place was chosen in the Odintsovo district, in the village of Novoivanovskoye, near the village of Marfino, 550 meters from the Moscow Ring Road. Literally 1000 meters from the residential complex there is the Skolkovskoye highway, and 350 meters - the Mozhayskoye highway. There is a bus stop where 17 city and suburban routes stop. In addition, just 950 meters from the LCD there is a railway station called Nemchinovka, which is followed by trains from the Belorussky Train Station. Thus, the transport accessibility of this complex is very good, convenient for motorists and those who plan to use public transport.

Construction technology

RC "Western Gate of the Capital" is being erected in a monolithic way with the obligatory insulation of the external walls and glazing of loggias and balconies. All cases are built in a single design, based on the severity of forms, the consistency of smooth lines and angles. The buildings are located so as to form two spacious courtyards, closed from the noise of nearby streets. The developer planned to protect the territory and establish a security post. Entrance groups in the buildings should be bright and spacious, with two elevators, with a place for strollers and a concierge room.

The facades in the complex are built ventilated, the entrances to the entrances are equipped with intercoms.

Construction stages

For the past 15 years, the LCD ‚ÄúWestern Gate of the Capital‚Äù has been built. The deceived real estate investors went to court, the city authorities convened commissions, passed resolutions, and journalists made special issues of television programs dedicated to this record long-term construction. Initially, back in 2002, KSV (Soyuz Vozrozhdenie Corporation) began to build this residential complex, and Social Initiative and Co. acted as a co-investor, but in 2004 the latter terminated the contract and left the project.

At this stage, the construction of the 1st building continued somehow, the rest were frozen. In 2007, after the next meeting of the commission from the Ministry of Construction of Moscow, NBM was involved in the construction of the residential complex. As a result, the first complex was completed, but the commission did not accept it, since the developer did not install elevators in it, did not connect utility networks (water, heating).

NBM continued to fight for the LCD in 2014. And, finally, it put into operation the long-suffering first complex, equity holders got the keys.

The new builder could not do anything else, and in 2016 the Moscow City West Gate entrusted the PIK group of companies, famous for the fact that it never abandons the facilities that it started.





Construction progress in 2017

The new developer, in the first place, conducted a series of appraisal activities and issued a verdict that at the beginning of 2017, the second building was ready in 70% in the residential complex, 50% in the third and 35% in the fourth. Of the infrastructure, a kindergarten is 50% ready and 70% one of the parking lots. At the moment, construction work has been resumed. In the second building, the exterior walls are lined, the communications are connected, and the interior works. Shifts are also noticeable in the third building. Here the building is being completed, and on the lower floors, wall lining is being conducted in parallel. PIK company promises to hand over these two buildings in early 2018.

The fate of the fourth corps has not yet been decided. There is a proposal to demolish it in order to make way for the construction of the fifth building.

Therefore, the decision of his future fate again hung in the air.

LCD "Western Gate of the capital": reviews

Due to the fact that the complex has become a record long-term construction, the opinions of both equity holders and potential investors about it are far from enthusiastic. This project has many advantages, among which:
