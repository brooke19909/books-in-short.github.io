Healing vacation in the village of Guseletovo, Altai Territory: reviews, photos

Over the past few years, Russian tourism is rapidly growing. Our citizens have changed foreign resorts to their native expanses: the vast expanse is full of unexplored cities that are rich in natural resources. If "finances sing romances", which is typical for the present time, then you can go to the resort village of Guseletovo in the Altai Territory.

You may ask: what is the significance of the steppe region? Of course, hydromineral salt lakes, healing mud and picturesque natural beauties. In this picturesque place you will take a break from the noisy and dusty metropolitan life, improve your health, restore strength and emotional state. With the advent of summer, the shores of local lakes are filled with visiting tourists who dream of privacy, silence and better health.

We are going for treatment to the Gorky reservoir





The name of the lake does not correspond to taste, the water in it has a salty-sweet taste, somewhat reminiscent of soda. To the touch is very slippery, framed by fluffy foam, like a deep sea. Locals call it "alkaline lake". Guseletovo (Altai Territory) abounds with similar reservoirs with muddy and very warm water.

The bottom is covered with a thick layer of silt, to the touch resembling velvet and softly enveloping your body. The lake itself is long and wide, with small islands on which bulrushes, birches and pine trees grow. The depth is acceptable even for children - to buoys no more than 1.5 meters. Thanks to the silt coating, it is comfortable to move along the coast, there are no stones or algae.

This is a real alkaline bath with therapeutic mud containing natural antibiotics (orthoboric, metasilicic acid). After bathing, the epidermis seems to be renewed, each cell is restored, pain, fatigue and depressive state go away, that's what tourists say. Nearby is a fresh lake called Gorky-Pereshechnoe, where you can splash around for days. Another indisputable advantage of the area is the pine forest surrounding the shores of the reservoir.

Go about your health in the mud of Lake Mormyshansky





One of the smallest reservoirs is Mormyshansky, located in the village of Guseletovo (Altai Territory). The salt lake is completely unsuitable for the life of aquatic inhabitants. The only creature that feels great in the water is a small red crayfish. Under a thick layer of salt there are entire deposits of healing mud, which has high mineralization and a whole range of biologically active substances.

Salt crystals gradually grow and grow, forming spiky plantations. Upon contact with the skin, they instantly dissolve and do not damage the epithelium. In warm time, the pond is filled with vacationers eager to improve their health: from afar, people resemble tree trunks, as they are coated with mud. Outdoor treatments turn into fun entertainment.

A promising vacation in Guseletovo Altai Territory





The tourism industry has recently begun to expand. At the moment, the village operates a summer recreation center "Guseletovskie Reaches", offering comfortable houses for living, equipped with comfortable furniture. Some have a bathroom with shower. All houses are connected to the water supply.

The territory is guarded around the clock. At your service - a real Russian bath with brooms. In stock - grocery stores where they sell environmentally friendly products of local production: milk, cottage cheese, fruits, vegetables, honey. You can buy soap with the addition of dirt, made by skillful hands of masters.

For a more economical vacation - camping in the village of Guseletovo of the Altai Territory. People live in tents on the lake. It should be noted that it is very clean: garbage cans are placed everywhere. The atmosphere of peace and relaxation reigns in this amazing place. For people, drinking water columns with clean water are installed; on the beach there is a shower with a toilet. Food points and shopping centers are open.
