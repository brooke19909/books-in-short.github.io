"Yurkin Park" - dinosaur park in Kazan

The year 2016 will be remembered by the residents of Kazan with the opening of a large dino park for children. On the territory of 6.5 hectares near the Kazan Arena, prehistoric lizards of all types and sizes settled for the entire warm season. Dinosaur Park in Kazan is a great place for a family vacation. Here you can not only admire the giant figures, but also find out a lot of interesting facts about the ancient dinosaurs.





Opening of the largest dino park

On June 5, 2016, the largest dinosaur park in the country was opened in Kazan. This event was preliminarily covered in the media, the President of Tatarstan Rustam Minnikhanov spoke with reporters about plans to create such an entertainment facility . The head of state personally participated in the opening ceremony of the park. Kazan Mayor Ilsur Metshin, as well as many other high-ranking city officials and honored guests, did not miss this event. Dinosaur Park in Kazan is a pilot project of a Kirov businessman. At the opening of the entertainment complex, it was announced that it would work until mid-autumn. After that, the dinosaurs will gain a permanent "residence" in Kazan in the presence of high interest from the population or move to another city in its absence. The park was named "Yurkin Park", and its symbol is a mischievous boy who stole the letter "c" from the word "Jurassic".





Kazan dinosaurs in numbers and facts

On the territory of 6.5 hectares, visitors to the dino-park can see 115 figures of ancient dinosaurs, many of which move, growl and perform various actions. Dinosaurs were made in a specialized factory in China. The pangolins have silicone rubber skin, which makes them look quite realistic. The height of some interactive dinosaurs is about 20 meters. In addition to interactive exhibits, the dinosaur park in Kazan will delight guests with small lizard sculptors. There are about 50 such figures in the park. Lizards are grouped by type. Near each exhibit there is a sign with a description.





Additional entertainment for guests

Finding the Kazan "lost world" is not difficult at all, since its main landmark is the Kazan Arena. Dinosaur Park offers its guests not only to admire the figures of ancient dinosaurs and make spectacular photos as a keepsake. The entertainment complex has various attractions. There is a go-kart track, guests are invited to ride a “live” dinosaur. The real highlight of the entertainment area is the artificial lake, where you can swim in dino boats.

Dinosaur Park is not only an entertainment facility, but also a place for an interesting education. The complex has its own paleontological museum, where you can look at real archaeological finds. During the tour, young researchers will learn many interesting facts about the life of ancient dinosaurs. On weekends, master classes and entertainment programs are held. Dinosaur Park in Kazan is a place where you can have fun all day with your family if you wish. The complex has a cafe and souvenir shops. The cost of toys and items with the symbols of the park from thirty rubles.

Cost, visit rules and address

How much does a ticket to a dino park cost? The fixed cost of entry to the complex is 500 rubles. The use of attractions, participation in master classes and meals in the cafe are paid separately. Children under the age of three and disabled people of all groups under the age of 18 can visit Yurkin Park for free. Benefits for other categories of citizens are not provided. Where is the dino park located? The main landmark is the Kazan Arena stadium. The dinosaur park has the following address: Kazan, 115 A. Khusain Yamasheva Ave. It can be reached by car, there is a large free parking near the stadium. There are no age restrictions, but children under the age of 14 are allowed into the complex exclusively accompanied by adults. You can take pictures and shoot videos in the dino park for free and without restrictions. During shooting it is not allowed to touch interactive figures with your hands. Dinosaur Park in Kazan offers a memorable photo to make very original. Prehistoric lizards are placed here in the most natural landscape.





When will the dino park close?

According to the initial plan of the investor, the dino park was supposed to work near the Kazan Arena until mid-October 2016. Due to the high popularity of the entertainment complex among residents and visitors of the city, it was decided to extend the stay of giant dinosaurs in Kazan. Yurkin Park will work on the territory of the Kazan Arena stadium until March 2017. During the cold season, themed winter entertainments await visitors , in particular, cheesecake rides, an ice rink and the opening of Santa Claus's residence.
