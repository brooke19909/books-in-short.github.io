Homemade toys. How to make a paper phone?

The phone is one of the most beloved toys of children. Both girls and boys play with them with great pleasure, therefore this attribute wears out very quickly. And if it so happens that the baby needs a new phone very urgently, and there is no way to go to the toy store, then try to make it yourself. For this you need simple materials and a little time. How to make a paper phone? This is what we will talk about in the article. Two master classes are presented to your attention, which describe how to perform such a product.

Mobile phones made of paper. Do-it-yourself toys for daughters and sons



The above method is the simplest of all presented in the article. The child will be able to make it yourself. You will only need to tell him how and what to do. So, take the usual cardboard packaging of a rectangular shape from any goods (tablets, tea, soap) and paste over it with colored paper. Designate on the prepared blank the places where the buttons will be attached. They can be cut out of cardboard, leather or dermantine. Glue these details on the base, write all the numbers and icons. The screen is made of foil or glossy paper. The product is ready.

Toy "Clamshell phone". Master class for kids and their parents

We will perform this product in the paper origami technique. A small child will not be able to make such a phone on his own. But he will be your faithful assistant and inspirer.

A toy telephone that can be opened and closed is made of one sheet of thin cardboard of A4 format. To design the product, you will need felt-tip pens or colored pens.

How to make an origami paper phone? We study the instructions.

  1. Fold a sheet of cardboard from left to right, smooth the place of the fold with your fingers.
  2. Flip so that the fold is on the left. Bend the upper right corner at a right angle, not reaching the place of the previous addition of 2 centimeters.
  3. Fold the top sheet of our "little book" 1 centimeter inward.
  4. Wrap the upper side of the product towards you. This bend should be the same width as the previous one, about 1 cm.
  5. Bend the left and right sides of the product inward (1 cm on each side). It turns out a rectangular part with a "window" at the top of the craft.
  6. Bend the entire product in half from top to bottom. The base of the toy is made.

Making a cardboard clamshell phone. We carry out small parts

How to make a paper phone, you already learned. But on it there are no buttons and designations yet. They can be drawn or glued. From the cardboard, cut out the details of a square shape measuring 1x1 centimeter and attach in the right places. Write numbers and icons on them. The external side of the product can be decorated with stickers, applique, and painting.



We hope that the workshops presented in this article will be useful to you. Focusing on them, you can show and tell your children how to make a telephone out of paper, and together with them perform such crafts. Creative mood and good results in needlework!
