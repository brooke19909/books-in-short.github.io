Grand Palladium Punta Cana Resort & Spa 5 * (Dominican Republic / Punta Cana): photos and reviews of tourists

Dominican Republic is a country that attracts tourists who want to plunge into the exotic with their heads. Incredibly beautiful nature, snow-white beaches, mild climate, unhurried rhythm of life and rich culture - what could be better for an unforgettable vacation?

On the east coast of the country there are a huge number of hotels. One of them is the large, spacious hotel complex Grand Palladium Punta Cana. It is worth saying that tourism in the country is developing very quickly, every year the living conditions, as well as the level of service, are getting better and better. So what is a hotel and what impressions do travelers have? This information interests every person who decides to spend a vacation here.





Hotel Location Information

As you know, the location is an extremely important point when choosing a place to spend a vacation. This hotel complex is located almost on the beach of Bavaro. Nearby are two more hotels, namely the Grand Palladium Punta Cana Resort & Spa Casino and Grand Palladium Bavaro - together they all form a huge five-star resort complex called Grand Palladium Resort.

The location of the hotel is very convenient, as there are cafes, shops and resort complexes nearby. The beach is within walking distance, and the presence of a picturesque, lively coral reef makes this place extremely popular with divers. The distance to the nearest airport, namely Punta Cana, is only 20 km, so a long, uncomfortable transfer immediately after arrival does not threaten you. The city of Higuei, interesting for tourists, is 60 km away.

Dominican Republic, Grand Palladium Punta Cana 5 *: what does the hotel look like?

The first thing that travelers note upon arrival is a huge territory that amazes with lush greenery and bright colors. Its area is 320 square meters. Guests of the Grand Palladuim Punta Cana 5 * hotel can freely move and use the services in all the hotels included in the resort complex. There are real coconut groves, and pools, and artificial ponds, and walking paths, and areas for recreation and sports, and secluded arbors, and curly bridges, and grottoes - all to make tourists feel as if in a fairy tale.

By the way, the hotel was built in 1992. In 2005, a complete reconstruction was carried out here, and from time to time cosmetic repairs and the introduction of innovations take place - thus, the hotel can satisfy even the most demanding tourist. It consists of three large three-story buildings and three two-story bungalows located directly in the middle of a picturesque garden.

How many rooms are in the complex?

The hotel complex Grand Palladium Punta Cana Resort 5 * is considered to be large, as it offers travelers a choice of one of 451 rooms. There are rooms of the following categories:

  • 425 Deluxe Double Rooms are spacious rooms with a modern decoration, designed for two adult tourists (and a child). By the way, the bathroom here is equipped with a hydromassage system, so you can use the jacuzzi at any convenient time.
  • 12 Junior Suites - larger rooms with extra amenities. There is a bedroom, as well as a spacious living area with a large sofa and upholstered furniture, there is also a large jacuzzi.
  • 14 Loft Suites are two-story rooms located in a bungalow. The first floor consists of a fully furnished living room, a spacious bathroom with a jacuzzi, and on the second floor there is a bedroom.

You can be sure that your vacation will take place in truly comfortable conditions. From the windows and balconies you can enjoy breathtaking views of the ocean or a garden lush with greenery and flowers.

Photo and additional information about the rooms

What do the rooms at the Grand Palladium Punta Cana look like? Traveler reviews suggest that relaxing here is a pleasure. All rooms are spacious and bright, and on the balconies or individual terraces there is garden furniture. The design of the room is pleasant, there is comfortable furniture, including a king size bed.





Since the life of a traveler is difficult to imagine without modern amenities, the rooms are equipped with modern technology. For example, you can fight heat using a convenient air conditioning system. In your free time you can relax and watch TV. The room, of course, has a telephone and a safe with a secure lock, where you can store documents and valuables.

Guests will enjoy the presence of a minibar with a cooling system. It is filled with drinks three times a week, which is very convenient. You can also make tea and coffee yourself - the room has the necessary utensils and an electric kettle. And you can count on an iron and an ironing board.

The bathroom is very large. There is a toilet, a washbasin, a table, as well as a spacious bathtub with a hydromassage system and a shower head - it is always nice to relax in your own jacuzzi after an eventful day. You can count on hygiene items, quality cosmetics, clean towels. By the way, maids visit the rooms every day, so tourists enjoy the cleanliness and tidiness of the room.





What kind of meal plan does the traveler offer?

Paying for accommodation at the territory of the Grand Palladium Palace Punta Cana 5 * hotel, guests receive the right to good nutrition. You can have breakfast, go to lunch, dine and relax with a delicious dinner in the main hotel restaurant. By the way, guests have the right to eat in any of the restaurants or bars on the territory of all three hotels (restaurants are an exception to the a la carte map).

Reviews indicate that the food here is really good. The choice of dishes is simply huge, and the food is always freshly prepared. The undoubted advantage is that guests can enjoy seafood and a wide variety of fish varieties daily.

On the territory there are also more than ten bars where guests are served drinks, including interesting cocktails, freshly squeezed juices (sports bar), high-quality strong drinks, light snacks. There are several barbecue cafes where you can cook grilled meat and vegetables.

Hotel Grand Palladium Punta Cana invites its guests to a la carte restaurants. Here in a relaxed atmosphere you can enjoy the culinary masterpieces of Spanish, Mexican, international and Italian cuisine. There is also a fish restaurant, where guests are treated to seafood. Some restaurants can be visited for free (a certain number of times), while visiting others has to be paid. In any case, it is advisable to reserve a table in advance.





Where is the beach located? Water activities

The territory of the Grand Palladium Punta Cana Resort & Spa 5 * hotel goes directly to the beach. This is an incredibly beautiful place where you can have a great time. The coast is covered with white sand, and the soft waves of the ocean create ideal conditions for windsurfing. The entrance to the water is cleared, there is a special place for bathing children and, of course, the beach is cleaned daily of algae and garbage.

Convenient sunbeds and sun umbrellas are in sufficient quantity on the shore. Guests can use them for free. Here you can get clean beach towels. There are also four bars on the beach, where you can order a refreshing drink or even light snacks at any time of the day.

Fans of outdoor activities will also not be bored. Very close to the coast is a luxurious lively coral reef, which is considered one of the most striking attractions in the vicinity. Divers constantly come here, and scuba diving is one of the most favorite activities of travelers. On the shore, you can easily rent the equipment necessary for diving or take training courses.

In addition, guests can ride boats, water skiing and motorbikes, catamarans, boats, bananas and enjoy other attractions.





What additional service does the traveler offer?

At the Grand Palladium Punta Cana, guests are provided with everything necessary to ensure that their vacation is not overshadowed by minor problems. Directly at the hotel you can rent a car, bike or any other type of transport. By the way, most tourists do just that, because having your own means of transportation opens up new possibilities for traveling. Also nearby is a parking lot with video surveillance system and security - here you can leave your transport for free.

For a small fee, you can visit the Internet cafe, where there is always the opportunity to use a computer and access to the World Wide Web. Also, a photo shop is constantly working for guests, where, among other things, you can hire a talented photographer who will help you capture the brightest moments of your vacation. The hotel has a large shopping center where it is easy to purchase almost everything, from food and household items to clothing, cosmetics, souvenirs and jewelry. Also, in case of problems, you can visit the medical center, where at any time one of the specialists is on duty.





Description of entertainment: leisure guests

Grand Palladium Punta Cana Resort & Spa is part of a huge resort complex. Travelers can relax on any part of the territory, even if it belongs to a neighboring hotel. There are four large outdoor pools where you can swim, do water aerobics, and take part in water polo competitions. Of course, umbrellas and sunbeds are located nearby on the terraces. Here you can get a clean towel and a soft drink.

Sports lovers will not have to be bored. At your service are six tennis courts, a football field, four badminton courts, two basketball courts, the opportunity to play volleyball on the beach. There is also a mini-golf course, and if you wish, guests can practice archery or air rifle shooting.

Almost on the beach is a spa. Here tourists are offered a lot of wellness and anti-aging treatments and several types of massage. A beauty salon and a fitness center are constantly working, where you can consult with a professional instructor. If you wish, you can visit the gym at any convenient time. There are tables for billiards and table tennis.

By the way, hotel guests are invited to attend dance and Spanish classes. In the daytime, guests are entertained by groovy animators, and in the evening, interesting performances and vivid shows are held in the amphitheater or on the site. At night, you can have fun visiting the local disco. And, of course, do not forget about excursions, because the Dominican Republic is capable of boasting a huge number of interesting sights.

As you can see, the Grand Palladium Punta Cana hotel complex is perfect for any type of vacation, while providing the traveler with the comfort and atmosphere of genuine hospitality.





Family vacation: conditions for parents with children

The Grand Palladium Punta Cana is designed primarily for families. Reviews indicate that most of the guests are parents with children. Of course, you can count on some amenities, in particular an extra rollaway bed in the room, a feeding chair and a special menu for children.

In terms of entertainment, the hotel also has something to offer. In particular, in almost all pools on the territory there is a children's section. There is also a large children's park with swings, slides, sandboxes and even small labyrinths in the shade of trees. Every little traveler is invited to a club where professional animators play with him. There is not only a games room, but also a room where you can play video games. Children have the opportunity to participate in productions and competitions, to draw and dance, in a word, to spend time not only fun, but also to good use.
