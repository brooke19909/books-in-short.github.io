Beeline SIM card quick activation

Modern man cannot imagine his existence without a mobile phone. This is the thing that accompanies a person throughout the day. Outgoing calls relate to family, friends, work. The main task is to choose not only a good and functional phone, but also a reliable operator. The Beeline mobile operator is very popular today. Beeline SIM card can be purchased at a specialty store.

Beeline has an interesting and popular service for choosing the desired phone number. This service is provided to users of a prepaid payment system. A subscriber can visit a special Beeline page on the Internet and choose for himself the free number that he likes.





Before a person starts using the card, activation of the Beeline SIM card will be required. The entire activation process takes place in several stages.

When the Beeline starter pack is already in your hands, you need to remove the SIM card from it. By opening the package, the user will find a plastic card similar in size to an ATM card. Having carefully examined it, you can see the treasured SIM card, it is attached to this plastic card. You can carefully disconnect the SIM card with your hands or with small scissors. Try not to damage the Beeline SIM card.





After removing the SIM card and for its further activation, you must insert the card into the phone. The card is inserted at the side or back of the phone, first you need to remove the panel. For detailed instructions on how to place a card in your phone, see the user guide for your phone.

The next step is to enter a special Pin-code into the phone. You can recognize it by looking at the starter pack, or read from the surface of the plastic card where the SIM was located. When entering the code, you should be careful, because in the case of a 3-fold incorrect introduction of a special code, the SIM card is blocked. For such cases, the Puk code is intended, the introduction of which unlocks the SIM card.

Before activating a Beeline SIM card, it should be borne in mind that if the Puk code is entered incorrectly ten times, the card is finally blocked and cannot be restored. To prevent this from happening, you can disable Pin-code verification in the phone‚Äôs security settings. If the PIN code is entered three times incorrectly, and the Puk code is lost, you will need to contact your mobile operator to restore the card.





In order for Beeline SIM card activation to be completed, you should be in a good network coverage area. You can find out by looking at the phone screen. The icon is displayed in the upper corner, according to which the network is determined. If the network is completely absent, then the message "Only emergency calls" will appear on the screen .

So, being in the coverage area, you need to dial * 101 * 1111 # from the telephone keypad and press the green call button.

After that, by dialing the number * 102 # and pressing the call button, the account is checked. After making sure that the account has a starting balance, you can use the card to the full. Now you know how to activate Beeline SIM card.
