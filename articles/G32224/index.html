South Vietnam: attractions, weather, hotels, beaches. Holidays in South Vietnam

Usually, if people go on vacation to Vietnam, they choose the southern region of the country. Especially Russian tourists. Holidays here will be no worse than at other popular beach resorts in the world: there are attractions, hotels for every taste and budget, beautiful coastlines. But before you go to South Vietnam, you need to at least partially plan the trip. From this article you can find out all the most necessary information about the country: when is it better to go, where you should definitely visit, which hotel to choose.





Geographical position. Differences from North Vietnam

South Vietnam is called the region that lies between the extreme southern point of the state (Cape Kamau) and the conditional border (approximately 17 parallel to the northern latitude - near the province of Quang Ngai).

The first difference from the North is the lack of clear division of months into seasons. South Vietnam is located in a tropical and partially subequatorial zone, due to which favorable conditions for a beach holiday are formed here. Another interesting difference is that southerners have their own dialect, which is somewhat different from the north. In general, South Vietnam is more popular in all respects, and people here live active, enterprising and easy-going.





South Vietnam Attractions

First of all, it is recommended to visit one of the largest metropolitan areas of Indochina and the trading port, founded in 1698, Saigon (now it is called Ho Chi Minh City). The architecture of this settlement reflects everything that he had to go through for several hundred years of its existence. There are many monuments, all kinds of restaurants, shops, markets and even merchants who sell products directly on the sidewalks. In this city you can see:

  • interesting pagodas: seven-tier and imperial Jade;
  • museums: sculptures, military history, historical, as well as the Dragon House museum on the pier;
  • Trang Hang Dao Temple;
  • Cathedrals: Duc Ba and Notre Dame;
  • palaces: Thong Nyat and Independence;
  • old Bien Soup shop;
  • Hotel "De Ville" as a vivid example of French colonial architecture;
  • a zoo and a botanical garden with a sports center, water park and the Royal Garden.

Not far from Ho Chi Minh City (South Vietnam) there is a truly unique creation - Ku-Chi tunnels. They are located near the village of Bendin, which is 70 km from the city in a northwest direction. This is a complex system of manually dug (!) Tunnels with a total length of 250 km. They are located on several levels, there are secret loopholes, as well as warehouses, living quarters, catering facilities, hospitals and workshops. In general, the whole city. At one time, they delivered a lot of trouble to the American army.

And 128 km south of Ho Chi Minh City is Vung Tau - a resort city, which is the most popular in the state. There is a sculpture park, a Buddhist temple, monasteries, beautiful pagodas and a lighthouse on top of Mount Nuynyo. River markets, the island of Cannes Zo, cave temples, and, perhaps most importantly, the ethnic villages of the delta are popular.





What can be done in this part of the world?

South Vietnam is ideal for any type of vacation, whether it is beach and water activities, sightseeing, acquaintance with the culture and life of the country.

The island of Fukok is the largest in the country. The coastline is 120 km long, where you can easily find a secluded corner and give yourself to silence, enjoying the sounds of nature. There is amazing flora and fauna. Especially nature is pristine and luxurious. Under water there are many coral reefs, vegetation and unusual inhabitants, and on land the rocky mountains, tropical forests and sandy beaches predominate. By the way, only here and nowhere else in Vietnam can you see virgin forests.

It is worth paying attention to Phan Thiet, which is also one of the best resorts in the South of the state. There are 10 km of beaches bordered by fresh, bright and lush vegetation, with coconut palms and the best hotels. Even a three-star hotel in Phan Thiet can easily become on a par with a five-star hotel in another part of the world.





Weather in South Vietnam

Considering this factor is equally important when planning a trip to South Vietnam. The weather is generally stable, but there is a rainy season, and even fifteen-minute showers can ruin the mood. It is best to go on vacation from December to April. At this time, in the southern resorts, there is practically no rain, the temperature of water and air is kept within 26-27 degrees. And from May to October, the wet season lasts.

Beach vacation

The coastline in the country is very long. The beaches of South Vietnam should be chosen depending on where you want to go on vacation. The most popular places have already been listed above - these are the Fukok archipelago, the island of Kon-Dao and the resort of Vung Tau. A bit more detailed:

  1. The island of Kon-Dao is characterized by beaches with white sand and is ideal for a relaxing holiday, watching turtles, as well as for fishing, boat trips and diving.
  2. Fukok is part of a vast archipelago, here are beautiful and comfortable beaches and picturesque mountain hills.
  3. Vung Tau resort has a developed infrastructure, ideal beaches, picturesque surroundings and many attractions.




South Vietnam: hotels

Hotels in this country are beautiful, characterized by a high level of service, and not only those with 5 stars, but also with a lower rating. For example, on Con Dao, you can book a private villa with its own pool at Six Senses Con Dao, which will cost about 42,000 rubles per night. Or rent a room in a simple Hai An Hotel with one star, which will cost a little more than 1000 rubles per day.

Fukok Island (South Vietnam) offers hotels such as Bamboo Cottages & Restaurant, Lavita, Mango Bay Resort and Salinda Premium Resort and Spa - all of them are inexpensive but have great reviews. And for those wishing to stay in the Vung Tau resort, it is recommended to pay attention to Ana Mandara, Anoasis Beach Resort, Bon Bien and Doi Sut. All hotels have a convenient location and relatively low price, offer guests a quality and relaxing holiday with interesting entertainments.





Holidays in South Vietnam: reviews of tourists

This state at the moment is not as popular as, say, the French Riviera, the Maldives or Goa. But soon the situation will change, because every year more and more tourists flock here.

South Vietnam, a vacation in which some vacationers from Russia chose, provides such an opportunity as enjoying the scenery, because even photos reflect the beauty of this resort area. Also, it is worth choosing for several more reasons:

  • A lot of hotels, including budget options.
  • Interesting and beautiful sights.
  • The ability to choose a sightseeing tour on land and water.
  • A huge number of recreational activities.

So, if you want to have a good rest for little money, South Vietnam will be an ideal option for couples, families with children and groups of friends.
