Hotel Thu Hien Hotel 2 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang)

Many Russian tourists, especially from Siberia, from the Urals and the Far East, often choose the Vietnamese resort city of Nha Trang for their holidays. The mild tropical climate, the blue sea and the picturesque, completely green coastal mountains attract here vacationers. Vacations in Vietnam cost domestic vacationers usually not too expensive. And especially, of course, when choosing a budget hotel. Russian tourists can stay in Nha Trang, for example, in a small cozy two-star hotel Thu Hien Hotel 2 *. Reviews about this hotel, although it is actually very cheap, are mostly good.

Where is

The advantages of this budget hotel domestic holidaymakers include primarily its good location. The Thu Hien Hotel 2 * Hotel was built once in the very center of Nha Trang - in a small quiet lane in the European quarter . It is this part of the city that is especially popular with Russian tourists. The main attraction of the European district of Nha Trang is the lotus tower. Next to it is the city administration building. And of course, in the European quarter of Nha Trang there are many shops of different specializations, cafes and bars. Located near the hotel and the main city beach.





On the sites of tour operators it is usually noted that the Thu Hien hotel is located on the first coastline. This information, in principle, is true. However, in order to get to the sea, the guests of the complex actually need to go through a very busy highway. Judging by the reviews, this can cause quite serious inconvenience to residents. The fact is that Vietnamese drivers like to ride on highways at tremendous speeds and pay little attention to pedestrians. Therefore, crossing local busy highways is actually very dangerous. There is even information on the Web that tourists saw on the road separating the Thu Hien Hotel from the beach an accident with a tragic outcome (a person died). The danger of the route passing near the hotel to travelers who decide to rent rooms here should be taken into account.

Road from the airport

Tourists arrive who decide to become in Nha Trang at the Thu Hien Hotel 2 * hotel, usually, of course, at Kamran Airport. This air hub was alternately an American and Soviet military base. Therefore, vacationers who have arrived in Vietnam are greeted by military-style landscapes. The road from the airport to the hotel is likely to take no more than an hour. This transportation hub is located about 35 km from the center of Nha Trang and Thu Hien Hotel.

General description of the complex

Represents the Thu Hien Hotel 2 * hotel (Nha Trang) a typical city Vietnamese hotel. This complex accepts guests in one multi-storey building. Unfortunately, he does not have his own territory.





Despite the fact that Thu Hien is only a two-star hotel, the reception is open around the clock. That is, the guests of the complex can contact the administrators with any problems they have at any time.

The head of everything at this hotel is a young Vietnamese family. Its members are, in fact, the hotel staff. The views from the rooms of this hotel can open to the sea, a quiet street or picturesque mountains.

Hotel Infrastructure

Of course, the Thu Hien Hotel 2 * hotel (Vietnam, Nha Trang) is just a two-star hotel . But quite a lot of amenities are provided to guests on its territory. At the hotel there are:

  • currency exchange point;

  • scooter rental point;

  • laundry.

Guests of the complex can rent cars rented in Vietnam in a small public parking lot located next to the building. If you wish, of course, the residents of the hotel can book a transfer to the airport here. Of course, this hotel also has luggage storage. Guests living on the upper floors can go to their rooms by elevator.

For the rest of the whole family, this hotel, as many traveler tourists note, is completely unsuitable. Unfortunately, absolutely no services are provided for children here, and the administration usually does not populate them here. There is also no private pool in this hotel.
