GSM and CDMA

Under these abbreviations are the names of various technologies for transmitting a cellular signal. In general terms, the difference between the standards is that GSM separates the signal using frequency and time modulation, while CDMA uses only code modulation for the same purpose. As a result, the subscriber in CDMA networks receives a higher quality of communication, and the operator can provide coverage with fewer stations, besides having more flexible settings.





In Russia, networks of both standards are used. Along with GSM, which provides wide coverage, CDMA is actively developing, providing fast data transfer. What is the difference in the iPhone 5S GSM or CDMA models, how to find out before buying a phone, so as not to have communication problems, we will figure it out in the next section.

Apple Product Labeling

All Apple products, which include iPhone modifications, traditionally have alphanumeric designations, which can determine their preferred region of use and frequency range. It looks as follows - A0000. Instead of zeros, of course, a digital model code is affixed. For iPhone 5S, marking is applied in two places:

  1. If you purchased the phone in its original packaging, in a reliable store or from an authorized dealer, then just look at it on the box.
  2. If the purchase is made by hand, then it is better to inspect the phone itself, not trusting the attached packaging. The second marking place for the iPhone is its back cover.
