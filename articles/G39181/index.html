Holidays at sea - in Russia or abroad?

Probably imagine a more pleasant time of the year than the period of vacation is impossible. Especially if this vacation turned out to be spent in the summer - during the hot season, when everyone strives to distant countries in order to organize a rest at sea for themselves and their family. Why vacation at sea? The answer is obvious - the sea coast is always fresh air, excellent sunny weather, gentle cool water and, of course, a well-developed tourist infrastructure.

Holidays at sea in its best form should take place on the territory of any country abroad. Unfortunately, the infrastructure of the resorts of our state, in most cases, "does not reach" the level of similar representatives abroad. In addition, the service and the level of service can by no means be considered satisfactory in all cases. And this despite the fact that the cost of recreation at resorts located in our country is often at the same level as in countries abroad.

Nevertheless, you can find a place to spend your holidays at sea without leaving the country. It should not be forgotten that due to the diversity of natural landscapes, the wonderful combination, as well as the natural uniqueness of many of the territories - plains, mountain ranges covered with eternal snows, or succulent subtropical greenery, aquatic azure, which is given by the Black and Azov Seas, unique mineral springs with healing water and resorts with healing mud ... All of this and much more can give a rest at sea in the Krasnodar Territory, which has no analogues in all of Russia.

The territory of the Krasnodar Territory occupies a vast area exceeding 76 thousand square kilometers. This territory is located in the southern corner of the European part of the Russian Federation, occupies the northwestern part of the Greater Caucasus (heights reach 3256 meters above sea level). Holidays at sea in the Krasnodar Territory is the most popular holiday destination among our fellow citizens. Here tourists can take advantage of many hiking, horseback, water, and transport tourist routes. The main resort areas include cities represented by Sochi, Anapa, Gelendzhik, Yeisk, Hot Key, Tuapse, Temryuk.
