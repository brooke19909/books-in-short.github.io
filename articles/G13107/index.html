Where to go in Yekaterinburg with a child on the weekend

Many parents often think about where to go in Yekaterinburg with their child. It is important that the baby remembers this tour for his whole life. When such a question arose before you, then pay your attention to the fact that you must solve this together with your children.



When you have already chosen the route and time, you will decide where to go first with your child in Yekaterinburg so that he is interested. In the event that your baby has just turned seven recently, then visit with him an extreme and interesting adventure park called Mowgli. Being in this unique place, your child will be able to try his hand at those obstacles that are made of ropes. Also, get him a ticket to the climbing wall.

Main places

When choosing a place to go with a child in Yekaterinburg in winter, pay attention to the recreation park named after the poet V.V. Mayakovsky. There is an ice rink in the park. The employees of this interesting place will offer you skates. Putting on your shoes and going out to the rink, you can have fun with your children.





There is always where to go with a child in Yekaterinburg

However, the most interesting and beautiful place, very beloved by the children, is the Grand Park in the Shopping Center, which is called "Carnival". When you come there with your children, they will be able to have fun with all their heart, thanks to a large number of attractions, to run through the winding mazes. And hungry babies will be able to strengthen their strength by visiting an ice cream parlor. When deciding where to go with a child in Yekaterinburg in order to relax, do not lose sight of the water park called Limpopo. There, your baby will find a fascinating lesson on such slides as "Anaconda" and "Waterfall". In Yekaterinburg there is a zoo where various unusual animals live, as well as birds. It will be very interesting for future biologists to be there. For lovers of exoticism and thrills, a trip to a farm called Crocodilville will be unforgettable. The guides will take a fascinating walk with the children to where the spiders live - tarantulas, snakes and crocodiles. However, in order to get to all these extraordinary places, tickets at the box office or on the website must be purchased in advance. Children will also be able to visit theaters, museums, as well as attend master classes. Those children who study the laws of physics will be interested in visiting the Galileo Wonderland Park. Little princesses will love the Butterfly Park. Those kids who celebrate their name days will be able to feed the fish there and take part in a competition with awards.

If you have not decided where to go with your child in Yekaterinburg, then be sure to visit the water park. Take a ride on the merry-go-round, take a steam bath or swim in the bagel. Believe me, children will be happy playing with you!

Conclusion

If you are interested in where you can go alone in Yekaterinburg, be sure to visit the shopping centers located in the city center . You can also visit the center of family recreation, which is called "Magnet". Be sure to visit this fascinating and beautiful place with your children, and then you will have something to remember!

I hope we answered the main question: "Where to go with a child in Yekaterinburg?" Good luck.
