How does a modern electric stove work? Glass-ceramic - basic coating

Glass ceramics is located on top of the asbestos base, on which a flexible ten is laid in a spiral . The tinted surface completely covers it. On the glass there is a marking of the heating zones. They are called burners.



Glass-ceramic material is characterized by high thermal insulation properties. In this regard, he splendidly lets heat pass from the Ten up. In this case, the rest of the surface practically does not heat up. Thus, only the cooking zone is hot on the surface of the stove. The rest of the space is cold.

Glass-ceramic plates are produced in a variety of models. Some of them have double-circuit burners that allow you to change the heating area. This function allows the use of pans and pans of various sizes in cooking.

There is a multi-function electric stove. Glass ceramic on it has oval designations of the heating zones. This will allow you to evenly warm up the products placed in ducklings, trays for cooking fish, etc. Such burners can be switched using an additional segment, becoming round.

A reliable and indispensable assistant in a small kitchen is a tabletop electric stove. Glass ceramic, of which its surface is made, fits perfectly into any interior. Such plates are good for summer residents. They are convenient for those who like to relax "savage."

Advantages of a modern electric stove

Glass ceramic has a very attractive appearance, which emphasizes the design of the kitchen. She is easy to care for. The coating is cleaned with special detergents and sponges. A significant advantage of such a plate is the built-in segment indicator, which gives indications of residual heat. This appliance allows you to identify an unheated cooking zone. It can be set dishes to maintain them in a heated state.
