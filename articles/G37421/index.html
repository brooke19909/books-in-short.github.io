Krasnoyarsk reservoir: description, features, rest

It is not for nothing that the Krasnoyarsk reservoir is also called the sea. After all, it really looks more like a real sea, and not like an artificial pond! The reservoir was created in the years 1967-1970. The dam of the Krasnoyarsk hydroelectric station was able to block the channel of one of the largest and most full-flowing rivers of Russia - the Yenisei. And it occupies a reservoir second place in Russia in terms of full-flow.



Briefly about the reservoir

The Krasnoyarsk reservoir (map below) has a very impressive size. Its length reaches 388 km, and the width in some places can be estimated at 15 km. In some places, the depth of the reservoir is about 105 m (maximum value).

The reservoir was built to control the water level in the river so that no obstacles interfere with navigation. The Krasnoyarsk Sea is also used for industrial purposes, in particular, for rafting timber. The shores of the reservoir are very different. It can be sandy beaches, and muddy dams, and piles of rocks.



Bays of the Krasnoyarsk reservoir

After the construction of this artificial reservoir at the confluence with the Yenisei, bays were formed. One of them is Tubinsky. It is located closer to Minusinsk, the distance from it is only 18 kilometers. The bay is very picturesque and suitable for recreation to many people. Here the vacationer will be greeted by sandy beaches framing the shores of pine and a smooth increase in depth. Nearby, Mount Tepsey resembles a giant lying mammoth. Also in its environs and the mouth of the Tuba River you can find petroglyphs - rock paintings of ancient people.

Lovers of sailing can be advised by the Gulf of Hum. There is a yacht club called Admiral. Club yachts sway gracefully on the waves, decorating this place. The hype is the most popular bay in the entire Krasnoyarsk Sea.

Next to it is the second large bay - Biryusinsky. It is famous for its picturesque cliffs. Further downstream, they pass into a dense and impenetrable taiga. The best way to get to it is to swim in a motor boat, crossing the Krasnoyarsk reservoir. A land route also exists, but it will require a lot of effort and time. Well suited for those who like complex category trips.

Good sandy beaches can provide others and the bay, located near Primorsk. There are also a lot of vacationers, but not like in Shumikha. Perhaps this is due to the remoteness of Primorsk from Krasnoyarsk. Go to him for about three and a half hours.



Holiday Village "Scarlet Sail"

On the territory of the yacht club is located the recreation center "Scarlet Sail". This is a fairly large complex, which in the summer can simultaneously accommodate up to 65 people. Krasnoyarsk reservoir rest will give an unforgettable experience! And comfort will provide many accommodation options. Starting from double or triple rooms in a log house and ending with separate houses for two or three people.

As an active pastime, the base offers a volleyball court, water rides, as well as a sailing yacht with a capacity of up to 10 people.
Fans of a relaxing holiday are invited to enjoy the view from the equipped gazebos, sunbathe on the beach or take a steam bath.

The recreation center "Berendey"

At some distance from the base "Scarlet Sail" is the base "Berendey" (Krasnoyarsk reservoir). Its uniqueness is that the whole territory is surrounded by water. It can no longer be reached by land by car. Only in winter on a paved winter road. In summer, visitors are taken by boat or boat. This base can accommodate about a hundred people at a time.

For leisure activities, a guided tour of the reservoir by boat or yacht is offered. During it, you can enjoy the picturesque scenery, clean air and blue sky. On the base there is a rental of equipment for water sports - water skiing, kite, board. You can rent a boat or kayak. At the base there is where to moor and own water transport.

The bulk of visitors for the weekend trying to take a sandy beach. But you can also have a good rest on the rocky one. Again, people come most often for the weekend, and during the week there is practically no one here.



Fishing

The Krasnoyarsk reservoir, due to its bays, is considered the most popular fishing spot. As a rule, fishing is conducted mainly from boats. Fishes, perches, pikes, zander and even graylings fall into fishing rods. Fishermen speak well of the reservoir. The fish comes in quite large sizes. This is due to good living conditions. After all, the reservoir is large enough, and the rich flora provides excellent nutrition for different types of fish. Winter ice fishing is also popular on the Krasnoyarsk Sea.

The Krasnoyarsk Sea simply can not leave anyone indifferent, everyone can find a place and occupation to their liking. Both in summer and in winter there is enough entertainment: fishing, snowmobiling, surfing and much more.
