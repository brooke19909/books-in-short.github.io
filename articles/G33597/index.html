What is a tour: definition, types

Traveling is the most beautiful part of our lives. The untouched nature, cities and towns, impressive architecture, fascinating sights and much more inspire you to do things, write poems, books, paintings and simply excite your imagination. To visit a foreign land, to delve into an unknown culture and tradition - everyone dreams of it. And for this there are tours: travel to different countries. So what is it?





Tour: Definition

A tour is a trip with a specific purpose to another city or country for relaxation, development or self-knowledge. It is not connected with working moments, that is, it is a trip without engaging in paid activities. You can visit Spain to learn the language, go to Tanzania and build a comprehensive school there, fly to Jerusalem and touch the ancient architecture. The tour may include a variety of services, excursions, transportation, guides, translators, etc. All of the above is provided in connection with the purpose of travel. This is what a tour is.

Make the right choice

In order to break loose and go to open new horizons, you do not need much: just your capabilities and motivation. But before you do this, you should decide what a tour is and what direction you want to choose.





Traveling can be of different types:

  1. Author's tour. An exciting adventure in which the guide not only describes the standard program, but also shares his tourist discoveries.
  2. Language tour. Visit a country with native speakers of the speech you want to learn.
  3. Agricultural tour. A new acquaintance with nature, merging with it through work, living on a farm, food. Housing is paid for by help and work at the same facility.
  4. Pilgrimage tour. A spiritual journey, an adventure that includes visiting holy places and understanding the cultural heritage of peoples.
  5. Charity tour. Trips that bring with them changes in themselves and the lives of other people for the better.
  6. Bike tour. Mainly acquired skills of orientation on the ground and strength.
  7. Survival Tour. Dangerous type of vacation. What is a survival tour for extreme sports? This is a favorite type of travel, which thus tempers the spirit, expanding their limits and possibilities.
  8. Gastronomic tour. The desire to cook a variety of national dishes is reinforced by actions on this journey. These are attempts to understand your own taste sensations.

There is no limit to travel

Many prominent contemporaries went on trips to other countries and looked into unusual, mysterious worlds. A vivid example is the famous explorer of the oceans, Jacques-Yves Cousteau. The inventor of the diving apparatus showed people the underwater secrets and the beauty of the depths.

Bear Grylls, an English traveler, a television presenter on a sea otter Discovery, sees no boundaries. The list of his adventures is replenished very quickly. In an inflatable boat, he crossed the Atlantic, flew over the Angel Falls and the Himalayas, became the leader of the expedition to the distant unconquered peak in Antarctica. In addition, most of his tours have a charitable emphasis.

The most creative is Jason Lewis, who abandoned any technology and benefits of civilization. He led the traveler's lifestyle for 13 years, and instead of transport he chose a bicycle, rollers and a boat for himself. With their help, a man conquers countries and continents. He crossed America on roller skates, sailed across Australia and cycled through China, India and all of Europe.





Conclusion

Each trip in its own way is interesting and exciting. And if you go on a tour at least once, the journey will change you. You will no longer be the same, you can touch the fascinating world of discoverers. Worldview and views are changing, new branches of self-development are opening up, self-confidence is being added. This is the only way to fully understand what a tour is.
