Hotels in Togliatti. Best offers

The hotel is not only a place of temporary residence, but also a wonderful opportunity to escape from worries and have a good rest. Hotels in Tolyatti offer guests quality service at a reasonable cost.





When planning to visit the city or just retire for a while, you need to book a hotel room in advance, because on the day of arrival it may not be available.

Hotel "Yubileinaya"

The hotel is located in the business center of the city, in close proximity to Victory Park, a favorite vacation spot for citizens. The number of rooms includes apartments of various categories (standard, luxury). The comfortable rooms are equipped with furniture, household appliances (TV, refrigerator and air conditioning). Throughout the hotel provided the opportunity to access the Internet.





The hotel guests can visit the gym, sauna and swim in the beautiful pool.

Payment for accommodation is taken per day. Check-in and check-out are available around the clock. The Yubileinaya Hotel has its own restaurant, Terrace, specializing in the preparation of European dishes. In addition, guests can visit the Tarantino bar and the lobby bar, which offer visitors a beer menu and sports events. For corporate events (seminars, meetings), there are three conference rooms with a capacity of thirty to one hundred people equipped with modern equipment.

Hotel Vega





Hotel Vega (Togliatti) is located in the city center near the main office buildings. The windows of the rooms offer a gorgeous view of the famous Zhiguli Mountains. The decoration is made in cozy brown and beige shades. The original design of the apartment provides a comfortable stay and stimulates a positive mood among the guests. Such cozy rooms can please tourists not all hotels in Togliatti.

Entertainment at the hotel

Dining is provided at the Hollywood Restaurant. The hotel also has a Verona pizzeria serving Italian cuisine. For entertainment, guests can use the spa, pool and gym. Guests can also watch movies in the modern four-screen movie theater. In the evening and at night, there is a night club for vacationers. Thus, the hotel "Vega" (Tolyatti) provides guests with a diverse range of services and entertainment.

Hotel Lada





The hotel is seven kilometers from the city. Registration is subject to a passport. The hotel has rooms of various categories (standard, deluxe). The cost of living is relatively low. Professional staff is distinguished by a conscientious attitude to work.

Meals are provided in a cozy restaurant and are included in the price. Various celebrations are also provided. Hotel "Lada" (Tolyatti) provides all the conditions for accommodating guests with children. For them there is a playroom for children. For guests there is a fitness center, a sauna. Also, guests have the opportunity to visit the pool. Laundry facilities are available.

Hotel "Zhiguli Star"

Some hotels in Tolyatti are extremely conveniently located. The Zhiguli Hotel is located in the central part of the city in a prestigious area. Nearby are the central city park and square. In the immediate vicinity there is a complex of city administrative buildings in which state authorities and local governments, organizations and institutions, banks, various offices of commercial structures are located. There are also numerous restaurants and cafes where you can eat deliciously at any time of the day. On weekdays, most city catering establishments offer business lunch at attractive prices.

Comfortable conditions for a good rest





The bathroom in the room is equipped with modern fixtures. The deluxe rooms also have a jacuzzi. There is a conference room. For business people, office equipment is provided. For guests who come with children, there is a children's room, the staff of which carries out classes with small guests and supervises them. Vacationers can visit the wonderful pool and have a good time in the comfortable sauna. A modern spa and gym are also available.

Hotel "Zhiguli" (Tolyatti) is located within walking distance from the local museum of local lore and the embankment of the Volga River. For an additional fee, a shuttle service from the hotel to Kurumoch International Airport, located sixty kilometers away, as well as to the train station , is possible. For motorists there is a secure free parking.

The security of guests' stay at the hotel is ensured by professional security and 24-hour video surveillance.

Togliatti has a diverse selection of hotels for tourists who decide to visit this young, successfully developing modern metropolis. Hotels in Togliatti offer almost the same conditions for guests. Therefore, guests need to pay attention to their location, take into account proximity to attractions, administrative buildings. The cost of living in most city hotels is quite affordable. This, together with a high level of service, makes them very attractive to numerous visitors to the city.
