How to get there

The main thing is to know where Kirillovka is located. "Coral Island" is no longer difficult to find. Residents of the nearest cities (Zaporozhye, Dnepropetrovsk, Nikopol, Melitopol, Kharkov, Kherson) can reach the village by regular buses or special minibuses, which work on request during the season and can be taken to any base at the request of passengers. For Russians and Ukrainians living far from this region, the most optimal and convenient option is to get to Zaporozhye by train or plane, or even better, to Melitopol. From it to Kirillovka about 60 km or an hour's drive. Minibuses depart as passengers fill directly from the railway station. Even closer, only 40 km, is Kirillovka from the Akimovka station, but not all trains stop at it.

By car you need to follow the Simferopolskaya highway. At the fork (Akimovsky turn, the landmark is a silver monument to a deer), turn onto the T0820 highway, to the left in the direction of travel. At the end of the route will be the Utlyuk estuary and Kirillovka. Coral Island can provide its guests with its own small transfer, but this is only by prior arrangement. There is no such service specifically.

Territory and infrastructure

Summer in the south of Ukraine is hot. Fedotova spit, the main soil of which is sand, cannot boast of special natural beauties. But the recreation center "Coral Island" (Kirillovka) through the efforts of employees appears to its guests a cozy green oasis with flower beds, colorful flowers, comfortable gazebos and swings, neat paths along which various trees are planted. There is even a pretty alpine hill with a small fountain here.

Among this natural and man-made beauty are a children's playground, cafe, bar, parking lot, sports ground, administrative building, 4 specially equipped glades with barbecue facilities. There is no pool at the base, because the sea is nearby. Given that this is the age of the Internet, Coral Island (Kirillovka) on its territory provides Wi-Fi, which can be used both in rooms and in any corner of the base. The cost of this pleasure is 20 hryvnias.





Rooms

The recreation center "Coral Island" (Kirillovka) for guests offers several buildings. Three of them are single-story, with spacious terraces, one two-story and another new three-story. The numbers in them are available on any wallet and on any, even the most demanding, taste.

The budget option is considered to be in the Standard class rooms, which are ideal for unassuming vacationers, whose main priorities are sea and entertainment, rather than chic amenities. Standard rooms are designed for 2, 3 and 4 people, and, accordingly, differ in area and variety of furniture. Other amenities are the same: shower, toilet, air conditioning, refrigerator, TV, electric kettle.





The next category is Luxury. These rooms are also designed for 2, 3 or 4 residents. The rooms of this category are individually designed, renovated, beautiful furniture is installed, including double and single beds, a wardrobe, a table, a bedside table. Televisions here with satellite and cable channels, there is a kitchenette, all the plumbing is new, for the convenience of holidaymakers there are spacious terraces.

A beautiful building made of wooden logs (log cabin) was built on the base. It provides very cozy and comfortable rooms, designed for 2, 3, 4 and 5 guests. They have all amenities, cable TV, refrigerator, electric kettle, comfortable furniture, euro windows, new modern plumbing.

The recreation center "Coral Island" in Kirilovka: reviews

The work of any enterprise depends, first of all, on the professionalism and conscientiousness of its employees. The same applies to Coral Island. On the Internet you can find many negative reviews about this base of those who have been here in past years. For the 2015th year, reviews are at least positive, but there are a lot of enthusiastic ones. People who came here for a week, regret that they have to leave, and are certainly going to return again. Among the advantages they note:

- attentive, helpful, professional staff;

- cleanliness in the rooms and on the territory;

- Tasty food;

- proximity to the sea;

- inexpensive services;

- lack of mosquitoes and other insects interfering with a quiet rest;

- unobtrusive and tireless rules of residence at the base.

The administration of the base and all its employees made a lot of efforts to make Coral Island one of the best vacation spots in Kirillovka. Still, there are still flaws. Tourists note the following:

- poor soundproofing in the rooms;

- Too small refrigerators;

- noisy air conditioners;

- European-style repair almost did not touch the rooms of the Standard category;

- in the shower is not fresh, but sea water (purified);

- proximity to the fishing base;

- noise from a cafe located on the shore (not the property of Coral Island);

- a small number of barbecues (sometimes they are not enough for all kebab lovers, you have to take a queue);

- uninteresting children's animation;

- Not quite a good location for the playground.

Conclusion: the recreation center "Coral Island" is great for beach holidays for children and adults who like to relax with mood. Here you can have a wonderful vacation without spending unnecessarily large amounts on it.
