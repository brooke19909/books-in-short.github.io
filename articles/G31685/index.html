Fleece - material for gloves

This material is very popular with sportswear manufacturers. Needleworkers also like to work with fleece. It is particularly soft and holds heat well. In this case, if the skin sweats, then the moisture is absorbed by the material, and the body remains dry. It is for this property that sports companies give preference to this fabric.

Many people think about how to sew fleece mittens. In fact, there is nothing complicated. The color scheme of the material allows you to experiment and choose any shades. Especially cute look mittens for children's hands. Fleece does not slip during prolonged use, does not fade. The fabric is easily stitched with a machine; no additional edge treatment is required. This is a very practical and convenient material, it is a pleasure to sew from it.

Patterns for mittens

Children's mittens, as well as adults, are sewn on pre-prepared blanks. It is important to correctly determine the size of the palm. Mittens should not be narrow and cause discomfort. Therefore, before work, you should circle your hand or the palm of the child on paper. So it will be possible to correctly determine the proportions of the future product.





A pattern of fleece mittens is made from this blank. Any mitt consists of three main parts. They are sewn together with a needle or with a machine. Of course, products sewn on a sewing machine are more durable. Inside, a lining layer is made. So the mitten will be warmer and will not get wet through, even if the child decides to play snowballs.

Hedgehog Mittens

The pattern of fleece mittens for decorative accessories is done in the same way. Simply, in addition to the main details, elements such as ears, paws, tail or mane are thought out. To make cute hedgehog mittens, you will need two colors of fabric, eyes (two pairs) and some black material for the nose. When digging out the base, do not round the four-finger section, but make it slightly pointed. This form will mimic the face of the animal.





After that, cut a few voices that will serve as needles. They are sewn onto an unstitched mitten. Eyes will complement the face of the hedgehog. Today, hardware stores offer a large selection of eyes for toys. If you cannot find anything like this, then use ordinary beads or buttons. The nose is made of a black circle. It is sewn on the sharp tip of the product.

Such a cute hedgehog will please any child. Thus, you can make different animals. It all depends on the preferences of the children and your imagination.
