Accommodation

The hotel has single and double 336 rooms, guests are offered a variety of accommodation methods. The maximum possible option for three guests is in a two-room suite according to the 2 + 1 scheme.

Fiesta Hotel Playa Paraiso Complex has Standard and Junior Suite rooms. Standard rooms are equipped with two double beds, a refrigerator, bathrobes and slippers. Junior Suite rooms have an expanded area, which is represented by a separate living room with two sofas.
