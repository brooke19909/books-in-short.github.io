Five rules for safe use of cards

Judging by the reviews of Sberbank, almost every citizen has cards today. Cases of card fraud also occur almost every day. Remember the basic rules for using cards:

  • It is not recommended to tell other people the secret code from the card, as well as write it in a conspicuous place or store it in your wallet.
  • If the client has changed the number of his personal mobile phone, it is necessary to notify the bank immediately. In case of loss or theft of the phone, the client must immediately notify the bank about it and block the SIM card.
  • All consumables in the virtual space are protected by 3D-Secure. When performing operations, the client receives a secret one-time code on his personal phone. It‚Äôs never advisable for anyone to give the code, even if customers haven‚Äôt used it.
  • It is not recommended to leave the card unattended. According to reviews of Sberbank cards, fraudsters will certainly take advantage of the good time and make a duplicate client card, and then make purchases in virtual space without the knowledge of the card holder. When making expenditure transactions, try not to release the debit card from your hands. POS-terminal in restaurants, shops should bring to you.
