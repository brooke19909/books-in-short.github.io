Rose ribbon embroidery: a master class for beginners. Luxurious satin ribbon roses: embroidery pattern

Nowadays, this type of needlework as embroidery with ribbons is becoming quite popular . Especially popular was the embroidery with rose ribbons . A master class for beginners regarding this particular flower will be considered in this article. Using flowers embroidered with ribbons, you can easily give a new life to an old bag or blouse that was stained by accident.

Roses are loved by many, which means they are versatile and perfect not only for decorating clothes or home decoration, but they can also be used to create greeting cards or to pack gifts. Learning to create such beauty is not at all difficult. All that is required of you is a lot of desire and a little patience. Well, of course, a few tapes.

Preparation of materials for work

So that embroidery with rose ribbons step by step can be easily studied, you should select the necessary materials and tools. In general, you can use absolutely any fabric for embroidery, but still choose a canvas or burlap for training.

The needle for embroidery with ribbons should have a large eye and a sharp tip. Of course, if you embroider on the canvas, then you can use the tool with a blunt tip. But this option is suitable exclusively for canvas. The tape you choose can be of any width. But still, the width of one and a half or two centimeters is considered most suitable. In this case, embroidery with satin ribbons of roses is considered the most correct. Also in some cases, you will need a thread for embroidery. To make the work look neat, choose a thread that will match the ribbon as much as possible according to color characteristics. And do not forget about the hoop for stretching fabric and scissors.

If you still think that it is difficult to embroider with ribbons, but you really want to make such a product, there is another option for you. It is simpler than embroidery. How to get started

Before embroidering with ribbons of roses, a master class for beginners offers to learn some important points. First of all, create a neat little knot on the ribbon. It must be large enough to securely fasten the tape. She should not jump out at the most inopportune moment so that all embroidery with rose ribbons would not be spoiled. Schemes in this work are used infrequently, but it is still worth some markup. The markup will allow you not to violate the correct proportions in your composition. You will see in which place you need a flower or other element, as well as represent its size.

So, to make the fastening knot, you just need to bend the edge of the tape two times and flash it. Now your tape in the work just does not slip anywhere. Most importantly, try to make it look neat on the wrong side of the product.

Embroidery ribbons roses. Schemes

Even the most experienced craftswomen use schemes when creating large works. This allows you to better navigate in the picture and not miss the size of the flower. If you have a ready-made scheme, then embroidery will be much easier.

You can prepare templates for your favorite plot for reuse. In this type of needlework, the same option can be so modified that no one will guess. You just need to change the color or size of the ribbon or complement the work with beads. To create a similar pattern, sketch on thick cardboard, and then use the thick needle to create holes in the areas of the proposed stitches. Using a pencil, transfer the dots to the fabric and proceed to embroidery.

Working process

In this section, embroidery with rose ribbons will be considered step by step. To get started, take the ribbon and twist a small bud out of it. This will be the center of your future flower. So that he has no chance to fall apart, sew it and attach it to the canvas.

After the bud is fixed, take a piece of tape about two meters long and begin to form a flower. This length is enough for a lush rose, and if you want a smaller flower, then you need a smaller amount of ribbon. When creating medium-sized stitches, lay them around the bud so that the shiny side of the ribbon stays constantly on top. This will give your picture a brightness. Try also experimenting with ribbons of greater width, then you will get larger and more expressive roses.

Naughty tapes

You are deeply mistaken if you expect that the tape will immediately fall as you want. Not at all. At first, she can twist in the wrong direction or puff in the most unpredictable places. And in this case only patience and training can become your assistants. Inspect the critical moments of your flower and, armed with a thread to the tone of the ribbon, just boldly hem the petals where you consider it necessary. With this action, you will probably be able to "lay" your rose in the way the plot requires.

Shutdown

You need to fantasize a little more and work a little to get a complete embroidery with ribbons. A bouquet of roses should be supplemented with small buds. And also do not forget about the leaves and stems.

To make the stems, you either need to flash them with a thin green ribbon using a stalk seam, or twist the tape and sew it to the surface of the fabric using threads. Leaves are made with a wide ribbon in one stitch or narrow, but in two stitches. To make the plot complete, sew the beads imitating dew on the petals, or add some other sewing elements. Originality and complexity in the composition will add a basket or a vase in which you can put your bouquet.



First, sew the basket with horizontal stitches with a tape of a lighter shade. Then twist the second (darker) ribbon into a bundle and stitch it in the vertical direction so that the new stitches form a checkerboard pattern with the previous ones.

Now you need to create a pen. First, sew small stitches of the same color along the contour, and then take a piece of the other ribbon more authentically and go through each stitch with it, making twisting movements. You can make a basket of ribbons of different colors. The main thing is in no case to tighten the tape so that the basket does not turn out flat.

Embroidery roses on threads

Another interesting and uncomplicated method can be embroidered with rose ribbons. A master class for beginners using threads is ideal for those who first encountered embroidery. In this embodiment, you do not need to twist the inner bud separately. It is necessary to fix the thread on the fabric in some specific place and from this point make five stitches in different directions.

In general, the picture should resemble a star, and all the stitches should be the same in length. The degree of fluffiness of the rose will depend on the length of the thread stitch. Bring out the tape at the point where the stitches come out, and begin to lay it in a circle. In this case, in order of priority, draw the tape over or under the thread. Do not tighten the flower. Instead of thread, you can use a thin tape. The flower should turn out quite lush and voluminous.

Clearance and care

To get the final completed embroidery with ribbons of roses, paintings or other products with it, you need to finalize them. If this is a picture, then it should be taken to the workshop and a frame should be selected for it.



And if it is a detail of clothing or an interior item, then you need to finish this product. If you are going to put a picture under glass, then you should use special frames that will allow you to save the volume of the picture.

Things with embroidery can get dirty quite quickly, they should be washed or even dry cleaned. But such products should be washed by hand and in no case should they be wrung out or rubbed. Otherwise, the pattern may be significantly deformed.

Do not wash those products on which the ribbons were tinted.
