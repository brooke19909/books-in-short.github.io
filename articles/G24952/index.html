Posting Rules

Check-in to the boarding house is made from 12 hours daily, check-out is at 10.00.

The standard set of documents that must be presented to an adult in order to stay at the Radiant boarding house is a passport, a ticket.

There are a number of rules for relaxing with children. In the pension guests with children from 1 year old can stay. When you check in a child, a birth certificate (with a note of Russian citizenship), a certificate of vaccinations, and an epidemic environment are presented. Children 1-3 years old can live, but a separate place and food are not provided to them.

Those who plan to undergo wellness and treatment procedures will definitely need an extract from the medical history or a spa card.
