Anapa, resort hotel "Atelika Dolphin": photos and reviews of tourists





Anapa is a recognized resort in the Krasnodar Territory. Favorite place for family vacations. In the city, much attention is given to recreation areas with children of different ages. Since it is the center of children's health camps and resorts, with a history of more than half a century the formation of the resort.

Recognition of the resort as a children's health resort took place in the first half of the last century. Kuban was rightly called the breadbasket of the motherland. At that hungry time, they tried to improve their health in children's pioneer camps. After World War II, the resources of the region were restored and the village in Anapa was named Pionersky Prospekt in memory of children who died in the war years.

Anapa - children's resort of the Black Sea coast

Currently, it is a resort city with a developed infrastructure. Throughout the region there are no analogues to the option of children's recreation created in the city of Anapa.

Nature itself helped in choosing his destination. A wide beach strip with a gentle descent to the sea, natural landscapes with unique conditions for the natural recovery of children, the composition of sea water - all natural factors are used for recovery in the sanatoriums of the region.

Rest in Anapa at an affordable price

It is very important that the recovery is affordable for vacationers with different income levels.

It is for those who are ready, at the expense of saving on luxury, to give children the opportunity to strengthen and restore their health, budget options for rest in the city of Anapa are calculated. Dolphin Atelika is one of those.

These are hotel room options that at a minimum price provide vacationers with everything they need for relaxation: meals, accommodation, entertainment.

The resort village of Dzhemete has a motor connection with the city of Anapa, located seven kilometers from the city center. The cost of living in the village is lower than in similar hotels in the city.

Complex services

The houses of the Atelika Dolphin complex are designed for double and triple occupancy. The rooms with services have their own bathroom with shower, air conditioning, TV. The administration provides free household appliances for rent: iron, electric kettle.

If you purchase a ticket to the Dolphin Atelika Resort Hotel from tour operators, its price will be seven hundred rubles for a double room without meals and one thousand five hundred rubles for a room with full board.

The price includes: accommodation, three meals a day, using your own equipped beach, on-site pools (adults and children), a children's room and a playground, wireless Internet, a library and luggage storage.

On the territory there is a sports ground for playing soccer, volleyball, basketball. There are also mangal zones.
