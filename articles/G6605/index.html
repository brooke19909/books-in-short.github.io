Sentido Flora Garden Beach Club Club Hotel 5 * (Turkey / Side / Kyzylot): hotel description and photos

Unforgettable moments of vacation give you the resort complex Sentido Flora Garden Beach Club Club Hotel 5 * . This is a unique establishment located on the Mediterranean coast, surrounded by beautiful nature. The hotel first opened its doors to tourists in 2009. Its territory occupies almost 83,000 square meters. It is worth noting separately that the hotel is designed to accommodate guests over 16 years old, which makes it an ideal place for a relaxing romantic holiday.





Location

On the very shore of the Mediterranean Sea is a luxurious resort complex Sentido Flora Garden Beach Club Club Hotel 5 * . Turkey, Kyzylot, 07600 - address of the institution. It is 20 km from Manavgat and 85 km from Antalya International Airport. There is a good transport interchange near the hotel, regarding which it is important to indicate the following characteristics:

  • Buses run to Manavgat every 20 minutes. The last bus from Manavgat leaves at 22:00.
  • Minibuses to Antalya depart every half hour.
  • Every 20 minutes there is a bus passing by the hotel to Alanya. The last return minibus leaves at 22:00.
  • You can get to Side with a change in Manavgat.

Comfortable living conditions provides its guests the resort complex Sentido Flora Garden Beach Club Hotel 5 *. Here are the room categories available to you to choose:

The villa includes a cozy bedroom and a spacious living room with a separate kitchen area. There is also a dressing room. The room has a spa area with massage table and exercise equipment.
  • Senior Suite is designed for three guests. It is worth noting that the apartments have access to a spacious common terrace with a view of the pool. The cozy bedroom and spacious living room have a transparent glass wall. The bathroom is equipped with a shower, sauna and jacuzzi.

  • The standard in design and equipment resembles the previous category of rooms. The main difference is the terrace overlooking the garden.
