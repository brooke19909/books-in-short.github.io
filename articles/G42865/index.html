Hostel in Zelenograd: overview and prices

The article will consider hostels that are located in Zelenograd. Most often they stop here by car, trying to choose the most suitable institution for themselves, both in price and in quality. For this, hotels of various price categories will be described below.

Alla

Alla is a cheap hostel in Zelenograd. The cost of living here starts from 700 rubles. The facility has parking. Internet is available for guests. There are cafes and restaurants near the hotel.

There is a beautiful garden. Pets are also welcome. Allowed to order products. Additional services include a 24-hour front desk, the availability of family rooms, as well as non-smoking rooms, and housekeeping.

In the reviews of this hostel, many write that the rooms are comfortable and the staff are friendly. The cost of a parking space is small - 100 rubles. The rooms are spacious and clean. There is a track near the hostel, but the rooms are quiet. Some guests note that the rooms are stuffy.

Zelenogradsky

The minimum price per day at this hostel is 500 rubles. The hotel has internet access. Next to it are the main streets of the city.

The multi-bed rooms have the necessary furniture, as well as comfortable beds. Bathrooms are both shared and individual. A restaurant is open on site that serves Russian dishes. There is a bar.

The rooms have heating and a safe. You can leave things in the locker for free. There is a transfer service. No smoking on site. The reception is open 24 hours a day. The hotel has an elevator.

Hotel reviews are positive. It has a convenient location and check-in / check-out is at any time.

Firsanovka

This hostel in Zelenograd is quite popular. A room per day will cost the Firsanovka guests a room - from 600 rubles. The hotel is located near the center and has a low cost. It offers free Wi-Fi, bed linen, and in-room safes. For smokers equipped with a special area. There is a shared kitchen where you can prepare your own meals. For cars, the hostel in Zelenograd offers a parking space.

The reviews say that for a budget holiday this place is perfect. The interior is simple but pleasant. Furniture and appliances are in excellent condition. The toilet and bathroom are constantly cleaned.
