Powers of operators when communicating with individuals

Even those who often use the MTS contact center number do not know the full list of opportunities that open after they get through. So, the powers of Call-center operators include the following:

  • Connect or disconnect various services. Exceptions are made only for such features as invoice delivery, the provision of detailed information on the movement of funds, the installation of an additional number to which data or faxes can be transmitted.
  • The installation of international and national roaming and international access is possible only for those people who replenish their balance monthly on a yearly basis for the last year or six months, but in the latter case they must spend at least 650 rubles on communication every month.

  • Change phone number.
  • Providing support for various network services.
  • Connecting a subscriber number if the debt has been paid off.
  • Issuance of lost PIN and PUK codes.

If you can wait for a response from the operator, then any of these services must be provided by the MTS subscription service. A call from a mobile phone will be enough. You can safely complain about the actions of the center operator if he refuses to provide any of the listed services.
