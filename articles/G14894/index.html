Hong Kong - the capital of harmony of East and West

The history of Hong Kong is very interesting. This city-state is an autonomous part of China, although the political system in it is far from communist.

A brief history of Hong Kong

In the middle of the XIX century, on the site of present-day Hong Kong there was a group of islets, the inhabitants of which were fishing. They wandered from place to place, looking for a more suitable corner for their simple living. The name Hong Kong was only one island. So, it was during this period of time that the United Kingdom became interested in the islands, which needed a suitable harbor for trade with the Celestial Opium, and Hong Kong became exactly the port that perfectly suits them. British politicians and businessmen proclaimed the island their colony, having achieved its sovereignty, and hereinafter Hong Kong is the capital of the east developed by Britain.




However, China also did not stand still, but intensified its strength and development, however, in the completely opposite direction from democracy and capitalism. And when it came time to return the island, the authorities of both countries faced an ethical and moral problem. Communist China could not put up with the democratic system prevailing on the island, and in 1997 both countries made Hong Kong an autonomous part of China for fifty years. Hong Kong is still the capital of political and economic freedom , but the Chinese army is now stationed on its territory.

Hong kong today

If you love travel, love to get acquainted with amazing and peculiar places of our planet, which have no numbers, then Hong Kong is an amazing find for those who like to explore the culture of peoples, and even for an ordinary traveler. The capital of Hong Kong is a place where eastern and western culture harmoniously blends.



Hong Kong is a truly luxurious city. A true explorer here will be captivated by literally everything, from fashionable hotels to gourmet dinners served on the open promenade, made from the freshest seafood. City tours offering walks along Victoria Peak at sunset, offering a wonderful view of the sparkling, like a pearl, Hong Kong, Kowloon and New Territories.

Unforgettable will be a walk with lunch in the areas of Lei YuE Moon, Sai Kung or a visit to the island of Lamma. As you know, Hong Kong is the culinary capital of Asia, which presents bright dishes from all over the world and makes the process of tasting food itself unforgettable from both the taste and aesthetic points of perception.

Knutsford Terrace, located on Kowloon, Lan Kwai Fong or SoHo, which are located in Central, is perfect for satisfying gastronomic interest.

Wonderful purchases can be made at Stanley Market, and next to it is a wonderful beach, one of the best in Hong Kong, which will delight you with an unforgettable view and give your soul a real relaxation. You should definitely pay your respects to the local temple, in which fate will be predicted with pleasure. The amazing structure of the Ocean Park, as well as the famous Disneyland, cannot be ignored. And, of course, do not forget to visit the local massage parlors, whose masters can do real miracles with the body, relaxing each muscle and at the same time filling it with fresh vital energy, renewing the soul and mind.

Hong Kong is a city that has no time to sleep. Night shows, many luxurious nightclubs, dance floors with live music, karaoke bars - a real abundance of nightlife awaits Hong Kong guests.

In addition, Hong Kong is the capital with the largest administrative and financial center, which cannot but impress with its impressive size. What are the urban advertising towers of Bank of China, Lippo, HSBC, IFC. In every such skyscraper, elevators literally shine. It is impossible not to say about the innate cleanliness of the Hong Kong people.

The city is constantly washed to the point that children can easily crawl along the sidewalks and paths without causing nervous remarks from caring mothers.
