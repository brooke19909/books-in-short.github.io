The device of a hoverboard: magic or technology?

This compact and environmentally friendly form of transport has become popular all over Russia, but not everyone knows the device of a hoverboard. To ignorant people, the fact of the operation of such a mechanism sometimes just seems impossible. After all, when you look at a relatively small board managed by a large person, the question really arises as to how it holds its balance.

Accessories

In fact, the whole secret of this transport is equipping with a gyroscope - a system widely used in various equipment, for example, smartphones. This device is designed to detect changes in the position of the plane to which it is attached.

In general, the device of the hoverboard is several nodes:

  • strong plastic case;
  • central processor for processing information;
  • gyroscopic sensors;
  • lithium ion battery;
  • electric motor (wheels act as drives);
  • additional elements: display, indicators, etc.




Hoverboard: device and principle of operation

Transport management is one of the key factors that determine its functioning. The basis of this mechanism is the gyroscope. It determines the position of the driver and transmits this information to a special panel. That, in turn, ensures the maneuverability of the gadget. So, a slight tilt to the side is enough - and the board will change the direction of movement.





However, the device of the hoverboard is such that the sensors have very strong sensitivity. Because of this, for many people, the process of mastering the gadget is almost painful, because in trying to maintain balance they set the board in motion. Most often, this ends with a very painful fall, so it‚Äôs worth remembering a simple rule: a gyro scooter will not leave under your feet if the driver is relaxed and does not make unnecessary gestures.

About the advantages and disadvantages

The hoverboard, the device and principle of operation of which exclude a sudden failure, is in itself a very convenient transport. It is reliable, has no limitations on the term of operation and is capable of developing considerable speed. However, you still have to change the power supply every few years, since lithium-ion batteries are not able to last too long. And, as you know, they are the key to the successful operation of transport, because it is simply dangerous to drive through the city streets with a faulty element.

The charger for the hoverboard looks like a laptop. Therefore, you should not expect increased reliability from it either.





Why buy a hoverboard

Given the constant increase in the cost of engine fuel, it is logical to consider a more economical mode of transport. Electricity at the moment is still the cheapest and most environmentally friendly (no matter what) option. In addition, the principle of the device of the hoverboard does not imply high energy costs, because the charge is quickly filled and it lasts for a long time.

The gadget and the fighters for saving the environment will delight. There is no exhaust system in this transport, and the electric motor does not release any substances outside. Although in a polluted metropolis this is not so noticeable, because, despite the deplorable situation on the road, few are ready to refuse cars.





Another advantage that the device of the hoverboard gives is the small dimensions of the vehicle. It is believed that it is designed to solve the problem of traffic jams, but so far these are just words. The gadget develops considerable speed and could very well replace cars, but a number of restrictions do not allow this to happen. For example, the inability to conquer a forest overgrown path, insufficient tightness of the hull, due to which moisture for the device is fatal, or the lack of winter tires.

Conclusion

So, as it turned out, there is nothing magical about the device of the hoverboard except for the sensors that control it. Perhaps someone does not even turn their tongue to call this gadget high-tech, but this is still no reason to refuse to try it in action.

And the advantages of this invention are still great even against the background of all the disadvantages. The gyro scooter is truly a breakthrough, albeit in the use of long-known technologies.
