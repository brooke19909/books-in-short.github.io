Bowling "Factory" (Vladivostok): features, menus and tournaments

A trip to the "Factory" bowling alley (Vladivostok) becomes a kind of family holiday for many residents of the city. Groups of friends and couples in love have fun here. People come to the institution to play an interesting and gambling game, as well as just sit with friends or celebrate a birthday.

Company Features

In ancient times, such entertainment was available only to very rich people. Bowling was somewhat primitive, but still quite popular entertainment. Now this type of leisure is available to everyone, and even with high-quality equipment.





The city has several branches for fans of this type of game. Representatives of the Factory entertainment network are located in different areas of the city. Bowling centers are located at the following addresses: Prospect 100th Anniversary of Vladivostok, 54-A, and Svetlanskaya Street, 189.

In the city, only they have equipment from Brunswick installed. Two centers have 20 modern tracks. Each has a cozy cafe with a varied menu. At lunchtime, guests are offered a business lunch.

Bowling "Factory" (Vladivostok) at "Gaydamak"

This is the name of the bowling center on Svetlanskaya Street, by the name of the stop. This establishment is in high demand. All kinds of theme days are held here. For example, the campaign "Family Thursday" started. On this day of the week, reduced prices for the use of the playground are offered for parents with children. The family must have at least one child under 14 years old.

Bowling "Factory" (Vladivostok) on this day invites kids to attend master classes with teachers from the studio "Aunt Rose". Here, children will be taught to make interesting and original crafts from plasticine, paper and other improvised means.

All questions can be answered by the club employees by phone published on the official website. Bowling "Factory" (Vladivostok) updated the menu, new milkshakes appeared in it. They have an unusual taste and fillers. Children will also like raspberry-mint donuts or cranberry marshmallows.

Bowling club "Factory": tournaments and promotions

In institutions often bowling competitions are held. Athletes of different ages can take part in them:

  • student tournament;
  • school league;
  • League of lovers.

Winners receive prizes from the company and competition sponsors. Children's League is free for participants. Adult competitions provide a minimum contribution from each member of the team.





Bowling "Factory" (Vladivostok) from Monday to Thursday holds all kinds of promotions and discounts on games:

  • when buying two desserts - tea as a gift;
  • the birthday person receives a free right to an hour of playing on the track (when providing supporting documents);
  • family thursday.

Periodically, a competition of children's drawings for visitors is held with the presentation of prizes. Children from 7 to 12 years old are allowed to participate.
