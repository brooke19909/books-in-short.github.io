Karelia in the fall: northern fairy tale in a bright cover

Going on vacation, most people when choosing a vacation destination are most often guided by the fact that they want to swim in the sea, sunbathe in the sun, and feel carefree. Therefore, the main directions at any time of the year are those that involve beach activities. But there is a separate category of travelers who are attracted exclusively by picturesque places. Every year they pack not suitcases, but pack backpacks. And they are sent not to Turkey or the Dominican Republic, but to Karelia.

A little help about the region

Karelia is not an ordinary subject in Russia. This is a republic in the north-west of the country with a population density of almost 4 people per square kilometer. This is a region of forests, lakes (more than 60 thousand) and rivers (more than 20 thousand).

It is here that the largest fresh European reservoir of natural origin is located - this is Lake Ladoga. Despite the fact that only half of its lake surface is in the republic, it is the most beautiful coast.





The land attracts outdoor enthusiasts. Every year tens of thousands of tourists plan their vacation here. You can raise adrenaline in various kinds of alloys on catamarans, rafts, rafts, kayaks.

Rest in Karelia in the fall

In the tent, of course, no longer live. It's quite cold even in early September, especially at night. In October, the temperature may drop to below zero. But wondering: what to see in Karelia in the fall and why to go there, do not think that this is a waste of time and money.

Without stinting on colors, nature paints forests and parks in incredibly fantastic colors. Karelia in the fall dresses up in perhaps her most beautiful outfit. As mixed forests prevail, the color palette is simply limitless. Karelia in the autumn in the photo is a fabulous masterpiece of art.





The region can offer its visitors an excellent catch in the ponds. This is trout, salmon, pike, bream. Mushroom pickers and forest berry hunters will not be left with empty baskets. They are waiting for cranberries and cranberries, traps, mushrooms, earrings, white, aspen, boletus. The hunting season also starts in autumn in Karelia: ducks, capercaillie, black grouse, woodcocks, hazel grouse, geese and partridges.

Autumn weather

In fact, Karelian autumn does not begin simultaneously with the calendar, but in August. November is more like winter, at this time even sufficient snow cover may lie on the ground.

Those who are planning to go to Karelia in autumn should not rely on it, they should prepare for the trip exclusively, according to reports on the Web. It is necessary to take into account large daily temperature differences, and heavy rainfall. On average, the weather can look like this:

Sights of Karelia

Judging by the reviews of holidays in Karelia in the fall, almost all the sights of the region remain available, despite the worsening weather. First of all, experienced tourists are advised to visit Kizhi and Valaam. Following the traces of the history of the region, you can follow to the towns of Olonets, Kem, Kondopong, Lahdenpohja, Sortavala and Petrozavodsk.





The main natural attractions can be enjoyed by the waterfalls of Yukankoski, Kivach and Ruskeale. The last place is also famous for its mountain park. Waiting for visitors and national parks.

Balaam

Going to Karelia in autumn, it is impossible to ignore a visit to Balaam. Despite the rainy weather, he is incredibly beautiful. This is the place where the visitor feels like a living creature, and not one of the cogs of the system.

The beauty of the island inspired writers, artists, composers. This is how the paintings of Shishkin I. I. “View of Valaam”, Kuindzhi A. I. “On the Island of Valaam” and Roerich's “Holy Island” appeared.





On Valaam, there are several museums - reserves, a working monastery and several monasteries. The Valaam Monastery dates back to the 14th century. For so many centuries, he has experienced fires and destruction more than once. Only at the end of the 20th century, the monastery buildings and hermitages were rebuilt and put in order.

Marcial waters

With joy, Karelia will meet in the fall and people who want to improve their health. Sanatoriums specializing in the treatment of certain organs or systems may be presented to their attention. The most famous among them is the one located in the small village of Marcial Waters.





Undergoing treatment in a sanatorium, you can improve the cardiovascular, digestive, musculoskeletal, nervous and respiratory systems. The resort has several sources of miraculous power, and a lake with healing mud. It is on their basis that a treatment program is built.

Karelian souvenirs

Having visited Karelia in the fall, you should not leave without souvenirs. These, of course, are not commonplace magnets. A sea of ​​vivid impressions will help to remember products made of warm Karelian birch. This plant is endemic to this region. A casket, pendant or chess will keep warm memories for a long time.

Ladies will like beads and bracelets with shungite. This stone can be found only on Karelian soil. It can be shiny or matte. He is credited with a magical and healing effect. This fact has not been scientifically proven, but in any case it will be a wonderful reminder of a visit to Karelia.

As a souvenir, you can bring a completely unusual drink - Karelian balm. It is based on almost three dozen herbs of the region, giving healing properties. This is not a panacea for all ills, but, according to reviews, it can affect vitality and mood.

A fabulous journey through Karelia will be remembered for a long time with special warmth, because it gives the same magical memories.
