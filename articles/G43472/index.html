Among the green coconut palms, a short walk from Benaulim Beach (southern Goa), half an hour from Dabolim Airport and fifteen minutes from Margao train station, The Star of The Sea Resort is located.





According to travelers' reviews, the hotel staff, thanks to the sensitive and kind attitude, manages to create a feeling of complete security for guests arriving at the resort, which is so necessary for everyone in a foreign country.

Benaulim

According to reviews, Benaulim is a picturesque village: bulls, cows, pigs ... Chipmunks are jumping on trees, dogs are everywhere on the ground. The authors of the reviews urge vacationers to take caution during their walks: along the curb along the road to the beach there are a lot of palm trees with coconuts, you need to make sure that a huge, heavy coconut does not fall on the head of a walker.

Benaulim, according to many, is one of the best places in southern Goa. There is plenty of everything: people, vegetation, the benefits of civilization. In other places, beaches are either overpopulated, or, according to the authors of the reviews, there is "something, but not enough." All animals live here in the yards, tourists assure you that you will not see pigs or cows walking along the beach. A huge number of dogs, but they are mainly sterilized and vaccinated, which can be found on the cropped ear.

In Benaulim, there is a currency exchange (near the supermarket at the crossroads) with an acceptable rate. Travelers are not advised to change money at the airport. There are also two ATMs.

Location

The guests of Star of The Sea Resort report that the hotel, as indicated in the advertisement, is really located very well: nearby, across the street, is the Kadar store (by local standards, this is a real supermarket). They sell water and other necessary goods here.

You can buy fruits from roadside merchants: strawberries, watermelon, pomegranate tangerines, grapes, etc. The most delicious juice in the heat on the beach, according to vacationers, is made from mango. Merchants always greet good-naturedly, unobtrusively inviting themselves to their shop. They also behave very modestly on the beach: they immediately leave after the offer of services.

A lot of people speak about the good location of the hotel. Nearby are shops, a bank, a pharmacy. Near the hotel there is always a taxi on duty, a little further you can take a tuk-tuk. The hotel is in the midst of commercial life. It is convenient to get to the sea: only 5-7 minutes walk or, as vacationers say, you can drive up a tuk-tuk. Unusually delicious pastries are sold near the supermarket, where you can also drink tea and coffee. About rest

The routine in the hotel is subject to local environmental conditions, as elsewhere in the resorts.
