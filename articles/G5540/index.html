Dessole Seti Sharm - Holidays in Sharm El Sheikh

Description: Dessole Seti Sharm is an excellent hotel, occupying a small territory on the sea coast of the resort center of Sharm El Sheikh, in a cozy and calm bay of Sharm El May. It has a four star category. In order to get to the nearest airport, Sharm el-Sheikh, you must overcome the distance of 22 kilometers. In addition, in the immediate vicinity are many interesting places. Nearby is the Old Town, which is famous for its magnificent architectural structures.



The hotel itself consists of ten one-story and two-story bungalows, which are evenly scattered throughout the territory, surrounded by greenery.



Rooms: Seti Sharm Housing Fund consists of 312 rooms, among which 235 are standard rooms designed to accommodate a company or families of 3 people. There are 67 Superior apartments and 10 family. Designers skillfully created a cozy and homely atmosphere in all rooms. Despite the remote location of the buildings, pets are not allowed. Bathroom, shower and toilet are separate. A hairdryer, bathrobe and slippers are provided. Each room has a telephone. There is a minibar, but there is only free drinking water, which is replenished every day. All rooms have air conditioning and safes. There is satellite TV, but there is only one Russian-language channel. All rooms have tiled floors and a balcony. If necessary, you can install one extra bed.



Meals: Vacationers who choose Seti Sharm to stay are offered four meals a day. All Inclusive, which takes place in the main restaurant of the hotel (buffet). In addition, all day snacks and local drinks are served free of charge in the bars located on site. But for freshly squeezed juices or ice cream have to pay.

Especially popular is a fish restaurant decorated in the form of a fisherman's hut. It is located directly on the seashore. Vacationers are offered a huge selection of fish and dishes from other seafood. Another restaurant, where there are always a lot of visitors, is located here, but closer to the palm groves that create a shadow. In addition to a varied menu, there are excellent conditions for relaxing during the hottest daytime hours. There is a bar and a snack bar on the beach. There is also a pool bar and a disco, which offers a variety of cocktails and all kinds of drinks.



Beaches: Not every hotel can boast its own beach and pier, but Seti Charm has them. This is the cleanest sandy area, which is spread over 250 meters. All necessary equipment is provided free of charge. There is an excellent shower and dressing room. Right after the beach begins a palm grove. On its territory there are several attractions. The entrance to the sea is smooth and smooth, without sudden changes. Here you can do all kinds of water sports, ride a boat, parachute, dive and just play volleyball. The sea is almost always calm.

Information for tourists: On the territory of Seti Sharm there are two pools of various depths, three water slides of various degrees of difficulty are installed. For those who combine a pleasant stay with useful work, there is a well-equipped conference room that seats up to 60 people. Conferences and various presentations can be held here.



There is a shopping center and spa (for a fee). For those who want to be irresistible always, a beauty salon works. Laundry and dry cleaning services are available. There is a safe at the reception where hotel guests can store their personal valuables for an additional fee. If necessary, a doctor is available. Among other sports activities, table tennis, billiards, darts, parasailing should be noted. There is animation and live music.

Reviews: You will not be mistaken if you choose Seti Sharm Hotel for your stay during your vacation . Reviews confirm that it has several advantages: convenient location, beautiful landscaped area, beautiful surrounding landscapes, smooth and calm sea. It is also positive that residential buildings are only one-story and two-story bungalows, which provides a small number of neighbors during their stay. The beach is very close, so there is no need to spend a lot of time on the road to it and back. Restaurants offer a huge amount of food.
