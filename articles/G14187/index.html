Hotel Sovereign Beach 4 * (Greece, Kos island): description, services, reviews

Kos Island - the third largest Greek island in the Aegean - many call it truly a paradise. Recently, this direction has been very popular among Russians. And this is not surprising: sandy and small-pebble beaches, combined with the resort resorts of Kos Island, which are buried in verdure and numerous historical sights, including ancient castles, become indisputable pluses when choosing a place for relaxation. Another advantage is the developed tourist infrastructure, as well as the availability of numerous hotels from which you can choose the most convenient for the cost.



The second most popular place on the island after the city of Kos itself, where Russians prefer to buy tours, is Kardamena. There are many hotels of various categories, an abundance of bars and taverns. In general, Kardamena on the island is considered the center of nightlife. Bars, clubs and taverns are open all night. Moreover, many of them are not only in no way inferior to the capital's counterparts, but sometimes even better than the latter.

Kardamena itself is a fairly small town with interesting streets. Blue balconies and carved shutters on the windows, bushes of grapes crawling along the walls of the houses, a small church with an azure roof, numerous taverns - all this creates an amazing atmosphere that tourists like so much. And, of course, there are a lot of hotels in the city, among which Sovereign Beach 4 * (Greece, Kardamena) is not the last. Kos, being a relatively small island, receives about four hundred thousand tourists every year, most of which stop on its southern coast. It is here that Kardamena is located.

Hotel Sovereign Beach 4 *: description

Built in 2000, this tourist complex annually receives several thousand guests, including from our country. This is greatly facilitated by its good location and the infrastructure corresponding to the declared category.

Sovereign Beach Hotel 4 * is located on the first line from the sea. Nearby are many entertainment facilities: a water park, taverns and bars. The center of the resort town of Kardamena, where life begins at night, is only two kilometers away. In addition, those who choose this hotel can independently visit the famous Asklepion and the national museum. The last overhaul in Sovereign Beach 4 * was carried out in 2014. It was at this time that the furniture was updated and the plumbing was completely replaced in the rooms.

Justification of choice

The indisputable advantage of this hotel is the maximum proximity to the international airport of the island of Kos - only ten kilometers. I must say that some find this a drawback, because sometimes the sound of aircraft going to land is heard. But those who come to this hotel with small children consider this fact a plus, since the transfer time to the hotel complex is minimized. And this is undoubtedly very convenient, because the kids do not tolerate the long road.

Sovereign Beach 4 * has a small area, fully planted with lush greenery. It has everything you need not only for family, but also for a romantic getaway. The hotel, judging by the reviews, is an ideal option so that tourists can completely relax and disconnect from the hustle and bustle.



A shuttle bus leaves from the hotel complex several times a day to Kardamena. If you wish, you can order a taxi, which for six euros will take you to any bar or club in this small resort town.

Services

The infrastructure of Sovereign Beach 4 * includes both a small parking for cars and a luggage storage. Concierge and room service are available. The hotel has several shops, a souvenir stall, and a currency exchange office. Judging by the reviews, the garden, established on its territory, is excellent for relaxation.

The reception is open around the clock. For the convenience of guests, a Russian-speaking girl works during the day, who will explain and answer all questions of visitors. There is an elevator between the floors of the residential building. In addition, the registry has a paid depositary safe. There is a car and bicycle rental service in the lobby, and there is also a tour desk. Those who have not booked a return transfer can do this right at the hotel.

Housing stock

Sovereign Beach 4 *, whose rooms are equipped not only in four- and six-story buildings with an elevator, but also in a bungalow, fully corresponds to the declared category - four-star. Regardless of the type of residence, preliminary fixing is not carried out here. In total, the hotel has one hundred eighty-five rooms of the following categories: family double and triple with sea or garden views, bungalows and two-bedroom apartments.



Bright and comfortable - this is how many tourists describe the rooms of this hotel. Indeed, the rooms, renovated in gentle colors, have peace and tranquility. In front of everyone there are small balconies with views of the sea or the inner garden. There is a small coffee table and chairs. A mosquito net is provided on the door leading to the balcony. Therefore, unlike many Greek hotels, special devices that repel mosquitoes do not need to be used.

Rooms

The rooms have a paid minibar, refrigerator, flat-screen TV, all necessary furniture, including a wardrobe, luggage rack and dressing table. Beds, as guests wish, can be either single single or double. There is a clothes dryer on the balcony. The floors in the room are tiled. In Sovereign Beach 4 * (Kos), combined bathrooms are provided. Every day guests are given soap and bath gel, updated toiletries and towels. Rooms are also cleaned at the same frequency. Bed linen is changed three times a week. In the bathrooms, in addition to the normal shower, a panel with three modes of water supply is provided on the wall. For the provision of Internet you need to pay extra.



Hotel Policies

According to the established canons, check-in is at two in the afternoon, and check-out is at noon. However, if a tourist arrives outside of office hours, the receptionist may, in the presence of empty seats, give him the keys immediately. This is especially true for vacationers with young children. In Sovereign Beach 4 * it is allowed to live with kids of any age. Parents can request a cot in the room. When staying in a room without an extra bed, children do not have to pay separately.

It is not allowed to check in even with the smallest pets, according to the rules of the hotel. At the discretion of the administration, the category of a pre-booked room can be changed in favor of the vacationer. Sovereign Beach has wheelchair access. No smoking is allowed throughout this hotel. If desired, guests are given the opportunity to accelerate check-in and check-out, as well as VIP services

Nutrition

Sovereign Beach Hotel (Kardamena, 4 *) offers two concepts: all-inclusive and half board. Meals are provided mainly in the buffet restaurant. Judging by the reviews, the stands always have fresh fruits and vegetables, for breakfast delicious pancakes, yogurt and other dairy products are always served. There are many traditional Greek dishes, although the chef cooks excellent European cuisine.



In addition to the restaurant, the hotel also has two bars: a snack and a pool. Those who come with small children are offered high chairs. If desired, you can order a diet menu. When traveling on full-day excursions, those tourists who skip lunch can receive a lunch box on request.

At the pool bar, all-inclusive residents can order soft drinks, beer and local strong drinks in unlimited quantities throughout the day. Residents can drink coffee from the apparatus installed in the restaurant hall. Those who wish are provided with everything necessary for barbecue. There is a service "food in the room."

Services for children

On the territory of the hotel for the youngest guests there is a small pool with a jacuzzi type. Toddlers can splash around in it, while parents will bathe in a big one.



The hotel also has a playroom for children of different ages. Here, specially trained staff is involved with the kids. If desired, parents can also order a nanny service to care for their child.

Sports and Entertainment

While the guys are busy in the game room or in the club, their parents can engage in table tennis, visit the gym. Those who wish can rent tennis courts. There is a small football field on site. The ball can be obtained from the registration desk. On the beach there is the opportunity to play volleyball. There is also a water entertainment center, for which you need to pay extra.

For adults, the hotel has an indoor pool and outdoor jacuzzi. In general, they gradually began to be installed in all hotels on the island. The reason is that with the onset of the October winds on an open beach it is uncomfortable to swim. A street jacuzzi covered with a glass cap is a great option for a low season. They work only from October to April. There is a sauna and Turkish bath., As well as a spa and wellness center. Judging by the reviews, the hotel can be relatively inexpensive to get a full course of massage.

Additional Information

Kardamena resort, located in the south of Kos, with its sandy beaches and calm clear water, is great for kite or windsurfing. There is a special club for beginners on the beach, where beginners swim under the supervision of an experienced instructor.



Those who come from the Sovereign Beach 4 * hotel remember for a long time all those attractions that they managed to see and photograph. Around the small town of Kardamena, you can see several ancient ruins at once: the castle of the Order of St. John, the Temple of Apollo and a small ancient theater. Those who are interested can visit the workshop "Tsukalaria", where they are engaged in the traditional processing of ceramics. Clay souvenirs for friends can also be purchased inexpensively.

Flights from the port of Kardamena to Nisyros volcano island are regularly sent. In addition, the list of excursion tours offered by numerous "agencies" both near the hotel and in the port offers a huge variety of boat trips.

Excursions

Those who want to rent a car in order to travel around this small island on their own should take into account that rental is an expensive pleasure, but nevertheless it will cost you significantly cheaper than if you bought tours. For example, the cost of a small open SUV taken for three days is one hundred thirty-five euros. But in it you can ride in the mornings to the thermal springs in Kos, visit the village of Zia, where the cheapest souvenirs are on the island, take a ride to the fishing village of Mastichari in order to taste amazingly delicious fish and fried cheese in the local tavern. You can rent a car in the Sovereign Beach 4 * hotel itself.





Reviews

Those who stayed at this hotel complex were mostly satisfied with the services provided. Many liked the bungalow rooms. The Russians especially note the delicious food provided by the chef. Mussels, shrimps, fish, grilled octopuses, a variety of meat and chicken - everything is cooked deliciously at home. Very well equipped and a playroom for children.

There is some dissatisfaction with the small number of seats in the pool bar and the lack of hygiene products in the rooms. As for the beach, it is sandy and very comfortable. Many tourists express their special gratitude to the team of animators who occupy the guests from morning till night. Summing up, we can say that this hotel is great for families.
