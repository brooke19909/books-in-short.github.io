The city of Gelendzhik is considered the pride of the Black Sea. Holidays, the prices of which are available for most residents of Russia, are becoming increasingly popular. Every year, thousands of tourists visit the resort. This once again proves that the city has many advantages, as well as recreational potential.



Briefly about the resort

This resort has a lot of advantages, which to describe in a nutshell is not possible. However, something can be assured for sure: all the conditions for a comfortable pastime are created here. At the service of vacationers:

  1. A large number of comfortable private hotels.
  2. Many boarding houses and resorts.

  4. Cable cars.

Guests can enjoy a walk along the longest promenade in the world. The beaches are located both in the city and beyond. Summer seasons are remembered by carnivals and holidays. Rest here will not leave anyone indifferent. You can always have a good time at the resort, and the guests will have positive memories of the vacation for a long time.

Location Features

Gelendzhik is located in the center of the Black Sea coast. It is one of the most popular holiday destinations in the Krasnodar Territory. The distance from the capital of Russia to Gelendzhik is about 1,500 kilometers. On the one hand, the city is washed by the Black Sea. The water in the Gelendzhik Bay is very warm. On the other hand, the city is surrounded by the Caucasus Mountains, namely the Markotkh Range.

Weather in Gelendzhik





The Mediterranean climate prevails here. It is transitional from moderate to subtropical. It is very warm in the summer. Winters are mild. Guests can come here on holiday year-round. The best time to visit this place is considered the summer-autumn period. It was then that the most comfortable temperatures prevailed, and the amount of precipitation was minimal. This period is also different in that at this time there is no overabundance of vehicles and tourists. Consequently, prices for all goods and services are reduced.

Optimal route selection

Airplane is the fastest and easiest way to get to Gelendzhik. This route is most suitable for guests from remote regions. The local airport receives capital airplanes year-round. In summer, numerous flights from other cities of Russia arrive here. There is no railway on the territory of Gelendzhik yet. However, you can drive to the nearest stations. Among them: Tuapse, Krasnodar, Goryachiy Klyuch and Novorossiysk. After arriving at the station, you can catch a bus or taxi. You can also come to Gelendzhik by car. To do this, use the federal highway M4.
