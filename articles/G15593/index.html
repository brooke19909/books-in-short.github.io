General rules of the game "Fool" in the cards - the beginning of the game





A simple, translated, flip "Fool" begins with the preparation of the deck. Usually use the one in which 36 cards, in rare cases, play 52-piece.

Cards need to be well shuffled. Usually the dealer, holding the deck in his hand, hands it to the player sitting to his left with the words: "Remove the cap from the fool." This must be done, because the dealer can "cheat" and peek at the last card or put himself a trump card.

These are the rules in the game "Fool." The loser is appointed as the dealer in the next horse. Take part in a tabletop battle from 2 to 6 people.

Distribution and moves





After the deck is shuffled, 6 cards are dealt to everyone. He puts the first manager of the deck to the participant sitting on his left hand. When one card is dealt, also in a circle, clockwise, it is dealt in the second. And so on until everyone has 6 cards.

The next card from the deck is turned upside down and placed on the table. This is a trump card, the remaining pile is stacked on it perpendicularly.

The one with the smallest trump card begins to walk. The goal of the game is to get rid of your cards as quickly as possible. Whoever does this first is declared the winner. The latter remains in the "fools", loses and deals cards for the next con.
