Budva

Budva is a small resort place, which is often called Montenegrin Miami. Around it are beaches, gardens, mountain ranges. Budva is the center of world tourism. In addition, while relaxing in this resort, it is impossible to isolate oneself from history, since Budva is the most ancient city of Montenegro. The old town is located near the resort. There is the medieval fortress of St. Mary, houses in the Venetian style, temples and churches built in the Middle Ages. Among all the old buildings in a special place is a complex of six temples. Special attention should be paid to the Archaeological Museum, established in 1962. An interesting attraction of Budva is the Monastery of Podostrog, which often became a place of looting and attack from various warring parties in ancient times. The spirit of the Middle Ages is in the book kingdom of the city library. All this is the national treasure of Montenegro, which cannot be ignored while resting there.
