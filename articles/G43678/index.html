Holiday Village "White Swan", Dzhemete

Dzhemete is a resort village located near Anapa. Its main feature is a strong elongation along the coast. Due to this arrangement, it takes no more than 10 minutes to get from any part of it to the beach. Reviews of tourists this resort has earned excellent, including the fact that there are many convenient hotels, resorts and pensions. One of the most popular complexes, annually hosting hundreds of tourists, is the recreation center "White Swan".

Where is located

This complex is located on the famous Pioneer Avenue. In total, its territory occupies 10 hectares. Regarding the beach, the recreation center "White Swan" (Dzhemete) is located on the first coastline. To get to the sea from its gate you need no more than 5 minutes.





The exact address of the complex: Pionersky Prospekt, 233, Anapa, Dzhemete, "White Swan". In the immediate vicinity of this center there are several public transport stops. Having sat on one of the minibuses passing along Pionersky Prospekt, you can very quickly get to any place you need in Anapa. For example, public transport that stops near the White Swan base goes to the popular Tiki-Tak water park, to the Dzhemete market, to the resort region of Vityazevo, etc.

Base territory

Thus, a truly vast area is occupied by the White Swan recreation center. Dzhemete - the village is very small, and therefore to find this center, if necessary, will be completely simple. The complex has several one-story wooden houses with economy rooms and one modern, built in 2012, four-story building. The huge base is simply surrounded by greenery. Tourists have the opportunity to relax in the shade of huge spreading poplars and Pizunda pines. Also throughout the base are flower beds with southern luxurious flowers. The hotel area is under 24-hour security. Throughout the area of ​​the base there are special places for recreation. In the courtyard, among other things, there are two small barbecue houses.





Base Rooms

What exactly does “White Swan” offer to tourists who come to rest in Dzhemete in terms of accommodation? In each of the wooden houses located in the center, there are two economy rooms with a common veranda. All rooms are designed for 2-3 adults. The rooms at this base are well equipped and well suited for a comfortable stay. In each of them there are:

  • two comfortable single beds ;

  • large bedside tables;

  • mirror.

A clothes hanger is provided. The verandas have a table with chairs and a fridge. Residents of both rooms can use them. In the new building, tourists are offered rooms (standard and luxury):

  • double one-room;

  • four-seater two-room.

These rooms are additionally equipped with refrigerators, televisions, cabinets and air conditioning. One-room apartments have individual showers and bathrooms. Residents of other rooms have the opportunity to use these amenities on the floor. In each of the rooms of the new building, additional beds can be rented. In the lobby of the building there is a large soft sofa with armchairs, a TV, air conditioning and a refrigerator.





Cost of living

The price of rooms in the center is not too high. For example, in the new building, the usual standard costs 680-1400 p. per person depending on the season. For a suite you will have to pay about 800-2070 p. Living in summer houses is even cheaper - 400-600 p. Administration can put a child under 4 years old in a room for 200 rubles. (without the main place). The deluxe apartment also includes breakfast. For an extra bed, 50% of the room price is taken.

Check-in at 12 a.m., check-out at 11 a.m. In order to check in, you need to present a passport, a certificate from the skin and sexually transmitted diseases dispensary, a medical insurance policy, and a document on the epidemiological environment. Children are allowed in the rooms only with a birth certificate and vaccination certificate. Accompanying persons must present a power of attorney from their parents (notarized).

Infrastructure

Of course, the recreation center "White Swan" (Dzhemete) has everything you need for a comfortable pastime for tourists:

  • playground;

  • tennis court;

  • sauna;

  • playground;

  • billiard room.

In addition to tennis, tourists can engage in sports such as volleyball or basketball at the base. All necessary equipment for this is leased at the rental point. If desired, guests can relax in a room specially equipped for this (armchairs, chairs and a TV). In addition, the base has a laundry room, a shop and a cafe. It offers its guests the White Swan Hotel (Dzhemete) and the opportunity to receive qualified medical care. For this, an equipped office operates on the territory of the complex.





Additional services

Tourists have the opportunity to store documents and money in the safe available at the reception center. An iron is provided to iron laundry. Vacationers can take valuables to the storage room. Those who book an extra bed in the room are provided with a Euro sofa. Directly at the base, you can buy a tour of local attractions. There is no private pool in this center. This, of course, is not too convenient. But if you wish, guests of the center "White Swan" have the opportunity to visit the pool in the neighboring base. The cost of this service is 100 rubles.

Nutrition

A very well-developed infrastructure is what distinguishes Anapa, Dzhemete. The recreation center "White Swan", however, despite the fact that there are many cafes and dining rooms in the village, offers its tourists the opportunity to purchase food directly on its territory. In the courtyard of the complex is a comfortable rather spacious dining room. Meals for tourists are offered three meals a day. The cost of breakfast, lunch and dinner is 500 rubles per day per person. If desired, you can order two meals a day. The cost of such a service is 300 rubles. The base also has three summer kitchens. There is everything you need for self-cooking.

The area near the base

In the immediate vicinity of the White Swan complex (Dzhemete) there are many bars, cafes and shops. Also, guests of this base have the opportunity to visit several fruit markets and one large market without significant time costs. For those tourists who did not want to buy food at the base, you can think about visiting one of the three canteens located nearby (Uzbek, number 7 and "Wait a minute!"). Food in all these places will not cost too much. In particular, tourists speak well of the Uzbek cafe.





Beach

Most tourists find the coastline in Dzhemete very convenient and picturesque. Base "White Swan", as already mentioned, is located in close proximity to the beach. He is in this place Dzhemete wide, gentle and well equipped. If desired, you can rent a deck chair or umbrella. There is a convenient playground on the beach. For adult tourists, a standard set of entertainment is provided: a banana, a tablet, a parachute, etc. You can also rent a scooter, go water-skiing, take a boat trip on the boat, etc. Tourists praise this beach, including the fact that it is covered. soft sand, which is very comfortable to walk on, as well as for its few people. The coastline in this place looks very picturesque. The beach is surrounded by natural, beautiful sand dunes.





Service

The staff at the White Swan base, judging by the reviews of vacationers, is rather polite and helpful. The administration of the center reacts very quickly to all the requirements of tourists. Kettles are installed in the corridors of the new building. Which, of course, adds to the base of attractiveness in the eyes of the guests. Linen can be dried on the balconies. Clothespins and ropes are available in sufficient quantities. Tourists who have arrived in Dzhemete by car have the opportunity to leave it in the parking lot belonging to the complex for free.

"White Swan" (Dzhemete): reviews of vacationers

The center staff makes sure that tourists have a rest here as convenient as possible. And therefore, almost all the reviews available about him are usually positive. In most cases, tourists recommend this base to their relatives and friends as one of the best in this resort village.





Vacationers praise the White Swan complex (Dzhemete) for their proximity to the beach and for the convenience of the rooms. Some tourists note the fact that they sometimes turn off the electricity. Also, the dissatisfaction of a certain part of vacationers is caused by rather high prices for food and rent of sun loungers at the local beach. But in general, the service offered by the base is considered quite worthy by tourists. Moreover, the cost of renting a room at this base is, by and large, not too expensive.
