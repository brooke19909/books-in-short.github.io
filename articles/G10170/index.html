Shopping in Greece: competition for Milan

If in Milan, which, as you know, is not on the coast, fashionistas and women of fashion go to buy, then Greek cities and towns can compete with this Italian fashion capital. After all, there you can combine shopping trips with relaxation on the beach and interesting excursions. Shopping in Greece is calm and measured, like the whole lifestyle of the locals. Shops work with a huge lunch break (here it is called messimery). Small boutiques close in the summer at 14.30, and open only at 17.00. In winter, the mode of operation changes slightly: the siesta is reduced by an hour, but stores also close earlier. Greeks are known for their cool attitude to work. Therefore, some private shops on Mondays, Wednesdays and Saturdays are open only until noon. And that's not all. Many shops, except Sunday, are closed on Saturday.



But almost always and everywhere you can make a successful shopping in Greece: Halkidiki, Thessaloniki, the islands and, of course, the capital of Athens are the best examples of this. In every town there is at least one street left to the merchants. Typically, such shopping areas are located in the city center. This is the Athenian street of Ermou, in Thessaloniki - Tsimiski, in Rethymnon - Arkadiou.
