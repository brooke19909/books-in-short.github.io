Samsung, push-button telephone: reviews, photos

The development of technology does not stand still. Most recently, the best push-button phones, Samsung and other manufacturers took first place in the device market. However, today smartphones have become more popular, which are endowed with huge screens and great functionality. Nevertheless, push-button phones have not completely disappeared from the shelves, and Samsung brand devices are still especially popular. A push-button telephone from this company is attractive and reliable.

The article will consider the most famous models of the named manufacturer.

Samsung GT-E1200

A fairly simple and inexpensive mobile phone. It is endowed with good functionality, which is inherent in cell phones from the Samsung company. The push-button telephone has a strict design, its body is made of good enough material that provides a comfortable position in the hand. Due to its small dimensions, the phone easily fits even in a small pocket.





One and a half inches - the diagonal of the screen in this unit from the company "Samsung". The GT-E1200 push-button telephone, as expected, has a keyboard that is pleasant to use.

It is endowed with a bright and contrasting display that perfectly reproduces colors. All symbols and icons are clearly visible and well readable. The interface is designed and allows you to quickly and conveniently navigate through the menu items.

Due to the fact that 800 mAh is the battery capacity of this Samsung brand mobile phone, a push-button telephone will allow you to be in touch for a long time.

The device will cost about one and a half thousand rubles.

Samsung B2710 Xcover

Photos of Samsung push-button phones (and this model, including) are presented in the article. The main feature of the B2710 Xcover is its impact resistance. In addition, the phone is protected from moisture and dust. The latter allows you to use it in the most extreme situations. It is especially suitable for travelers.





The phone is in the form of a candy bar. It contains all the necessary technologies for accessing the Internet. The 2 MP camera allows you to take fairly good pictures, as well as shoot video.

The phone has a 2-inch screen with a resolution of 320 x 240 pixels. The case is made of a special material, so the device does not slip and lies comfortably in the hand. A Samsung push-button telephone in a metal case would, of course, be more durable, but even these materials do a good job. The buttons are made convex and pleasant to the touch. They are comfortable to press even with gloves.

The phone speaker is also made with sufficient quality. When it was created, noise reduction functions were introduced. Thanks to this, the interlocutor's audibility is excellent.

The phone has fifteen megabytes of internal memory. However, it can be expanded. The device has a fairly capacious 1300 mAh battery. The phone can remain without recharging for about a week. The cost of the device is approximately four thousand rubles.

Samsung C3322

Button phone with a large screen "Samsung" S3322 is primarily notable for its low price. It is quite stable in operation.

The phone is equipped with two ports for a SIM card. Provided with 50 MB of internal memory, which can be significantly expanded. The screen size is 2.2 inches. The display is quite juicy and looks great even in the sun.





By the way, how to unlock a Samsung push-button telephone is a rather complicated question for many users. For this, specially assigned keys are used, which can be easily found in the settings.

Samsung C3322 is endowed with the means to watch videos and listen to music, in addition, it has an FM radio. The phone has a 1000 mAh battery. This battery capacity is more than enough for many.

Samsung C3530

This unit has a pleasant appearance and is immediately evident. Many lovers of push-button phones will like it. Great for people who spend a lot of time in communication.

The device is endowed with a display with a 2.2-inch screen, whose resolution is 320 x 240 pixels. The phone and camera are not deprived. She has 3 megapixels and will help to make quite good photos and videos.





To store information, the device has 50 MB of internal memory. However, it can be expanded to 16 GB using memory cards. There is a lot of entertainment functions in the device. The player allows you to watch videos and listen to music. On the device, you can easily play many games.

This model boasts a fairly long battery life. Thanks to the 960 mAh battery, the device is able to work up to fifteen hours of talk time. In standby mode, it can be in working condition for almost a month.

User reviews, however, highlight that this model can occasionally fly off the back cover.

The cost of the device is approximately three thousand rubles.

Samsung E2232

A fairly standard model with a classic look. He is not deprived of many popular functions, but he copes best with the main tasks of the phone - calls.

The phone supports two SIM-cards. Therefore, it becomes possible to interact with several mobile operators. Image output is carried out through a sufficiently high-quality display, which has a diagonal of 1.8 inches and a resolution of 160 x 128 pixels.





However, this unit cannot boast of excellent image quality. A 0.3 MP camera is installed here, which is suitable for creating only insignificant frames. But it will not let you get bored with a media player that plays most of the well-known formats, as well as FM-radio.

The device is endowed with built-in memory, the volume of which is 10 MB. However, it can be easily expanded with memory cards. Also, for many it will be pleasant to have a sufficiently bright flashlight.

For the autonomy of the device, a high-quality 1000 mAh battery is responsible. The phone is able to live in call mode for about fifteen hours. You can buy such a device for about one thousand rubles.

Samsung S5610

Very high quality phone based on a metal case. The dimensions of the device are very small, it is quite convenient to carry it even in a small pocket or purse. Metal and gloss give the phone an attractive look and set it apart from a number of similar devices.

One cannot but mention the stunningly juicy screen. The screen size is 2.4 inches with a resolution of 320 x 240 pixels. Everything is perfectly visible on it even in direct sunlight.





Separately, it should be said about the quality of the pictures that this device is capable of taking. Thanks to the 5 megapixel camera with autofocus, you can easily take good photos and shoot videos.

The phone has a built-in media player that allows you to listen to music and watch videos. It is equipped with all the necessary technologies for accessing the Internet.

A 1000 mAh battery can last fifteen hours without charging in talk mode. The price of the device is approximately five thousand rubles.

Samsung C3782

A model that will definitely appeal to those who require the phone to perform the basic tasks of receiving and making a call. There is support for two SIM-cards, so that the user can easily share their calls.





The device has a screen with a diagonal of 2.4 inches, and it should be noted that it is quite good. There is a 3 megapixel camera that does a good job. The device has 32 MB of internal memory, which is expanded using memory cards.

The sound of the device is also very good. To listen to music and watch videos there is a media player. A good battery will allow you to not recharge the device for almost a week. It will cost about four thousand rubles.

Samsung SM-B312E

This model is a classic push-button telephone, which is not crowded with various functions. Its main task is calls and SMS. It is perfect for people who do not need great features from the phone. It is possible to use two SIM cards.





A good screen has a diagonal of 2 inches with a resolution of 160 x 128 pixels. The device can not boast of excellent pictures, since it is endowed with a 0.3 megapixel camera. A 1000 mAh battery allows you to stay connected for approximately twelve hours of talk time.

You can buy a phone for two and a half thousand rubles.

Samsung E2202

This unit has quite good functionality and at the same time boasts a small price. First of all, it pleases with long work without recharging. The device supports two SIM-cards, which is a definite plus.





Almost all Samsung push-button telephones, reviews of which are mostly positive, cannot boast of a huge display. The screen of this unit has a diagonal of 1.8 inches with a resolution of 160 x 128 pixels. He can not boast of the quality of images, since he is endowed with a rather weak camera - 0.3 MP.

There is 8 MB of internal memory that can be expanded with memory cards. A 1000 mAh battery will help the device work for thirteen hours without recharging. You can buy a device for two thousand rubles.
