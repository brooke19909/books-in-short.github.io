Hotel Baikal 3 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Bulgaria provides travelers with inexpensive and comfortable hotels located on the Black Sea coast. They are constantly being reconstructed, improving the quality of their service. For an economical vacation, you can choose a small hotel Baykal 3 * in Sunny Beach, which is perfect for a budget family vacation in Bulgaria.

General description of the hotel and its location

Convenient location is the main advantage of the hotel. It is located just 150 meters from the public beach. The entrance to the sea is gentle, and the sea itself is not too deep, so it is perfect for swimming with young children. 500 meters from the Baikal 3 * hotel (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) is the resort center, where tourists can visit local restaurants, bars and sports grounds. You can get to it on a special tourist train that runs throughout Sunny Beach. Nearby (3 km) is the city of Nessebar, which hosts many excursion programs telling about ancient sights.





The hotel itself is a complex consisting of four buildings. The largest of them has seven floors, and the smallest - three. In total, they offer hotel guests about 300 rooms, suitable for families and single travelers. The complex was built in the second half of the 20th century, but in 2004 it underwent a complete renovation. Tourists come here every summer. It is worth remembering that check-in to the rooms starts at 14:00, and you need to leave the hotel before 12:00.

Number of rooms

In total, Hotel Baikal 3 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) has 302 rooms. Depending on their area, from two to five people can live in them. All apartments are equipped with single or double beds. An extra bed for a child can be delivered upon request. Some rooms have folding chairs, which you can also sleep in. The bathroom has a shower, toilet, washbasin and a large mirror. In addition to the beds, the furniture room has a desk, a wardrobe and bedside tables. All rooms have a private small balcony or terrace.





In addition, the apartment offers the following amenities:

  • Refrigerator for storing food and drinks. Available at extra cost.
  • An older model with cable TV. Tourists from Russia can watch several channels in their native language.
  • Radio. Only Bulgarian radio stations are broadcast.
  • Direct telephone. You can contact the administrator of the Baikal (Baikal) 3 * hotel (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) or order room service.
  • Individual air conditioning. Not in all apartments, but using it is free.
  • Hairdryer and toiletries in the bathroom. Guests are provided with soap, shampoo, shower gel.
  • An empty minibar is provided free of charge.
  • Daily housekeeping, as well as regular linen and towel changes.

Food concept, bars and restaurants.

Hotel Baikal 3 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria) offers tourists a fairly popular all-inclusive concept of food. Paying a ticket, travelers receive three meals a day, as well as local soft drinks and spirits. For an additional fee, you can buy imported alcohol, soft cocktails from it, freshly squeezed juices, desserts and snacks. Most dishes are prepared according to local recipes. All meals are served in the form of a buffet, so guests can choose and take their favorite dish.

Conditions for children

The tourist complex Baikal 3 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach) is a favorite place for families who are trying to relax cheaply but efficiently. For children there are all the necessary amenities. A baby cot is available in the room upon request for babies. Its use is not included in the price, therefore it is paid separately. Children's restaurants also have high chairs. A child can always be left with a nanny who works around the clock.

For a comfortable and safe bathing for babies in the pool there is a shallow children's section. Here, children can swim under the supervision of their parents. There is also a playground for outdoor games. Children can ride the slides or spend time in the sandbox.
