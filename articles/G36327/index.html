Compressor car refrigerator - what are its features?

A compressor refrigerator is an analog of a traditional home appliance with a compressor. However, unlike him, a special design is used here, designed specifically for use in cars. This option is the most economical in terms of energy consumption, picks up the required temperature much faster, and is also able to store frozen products for a long time. A compressor refrigerator has such advantages as a quick set of cold, as well as maintaining the temperature for 6 hours after the power is turned off.





Why is this device needed?

As you know, hunger can catch a person in any situation, even on the road. However, if in the city you can go somewhere to eat, then outside the city this possibility is completely excluded. In this case, a compressor refrigerator comes to the rescue of a car enthusiast. Many experienced car owners can remember how the heater in the car used to be the ultimate dream , and now there are so many different devices that can make life easier on the road. Despite the fact that the compressor auto-refrigerator is quite a functional device, until it has received such wide distribution as, for example, air conditioners.

In what situations is it useful?

There are many cases where an auto-refrigerator may be required. It will be useful to outdoor enthusiasts in the countryside outside in the countryside. With this device, you can leave the city for several days without fear that you have to eat for some reason. Such a device will also be appreciated by fishermen. The whole catch can be put in the refrigerator, where it definitely will not go bad. If you have children, then this device is simply indispensable on the road, because you can put ice cream there. A compressor refrigerator is often used to deliver food to cafes and restaurants, as well as to transport blood to medical facilities.





The difference between a compressor refrigerator and a thermoelectric cooler

These two species are most common today. If we are talking about how good a compressor refrigerator is, then you need to figure out why it is better than a competitor.

The thermoelectric device has a simple design, a thermocouple is placed there. More complex models involve the use of two elements. It is convenient if such a device has a removable cover. In this case, the entire mechanism is placed in the cover, which can be removed to wash the case. If the device is able to work from the cigarette lighter and from the network - this is very good. The presence of refrigerant in the kit is another important point.





The Waeco compressor car cooler cools in the same way as its counterparts. The principle of operation of such devices is completely analogous to traditional home refrigerators. There are two compartments here: one for freezing, and the other for easy cooling.

If we talk about which device is better, then it is important to understand that a thermoelectric refrigerator takes on exclusively cooling functions, while a compressor is also capable of freezing.
