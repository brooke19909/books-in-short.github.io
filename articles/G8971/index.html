How to make a rose out of paper for a gift?

A growing number of men are thinking about how to make a rose out of paper. With the help of such a little thing you can make a pleasant and unusual surprise for your

second half. There is no particular difficulty in this matter. Almost everyone can cope with such a task. All you need is the availability of tools, materials, great desire and free time. With the rest, usually there are no problems. Before carrying out the work, it is necessary to prepare everything necessary. When making crafts, you can‚Äôt rush, you need to do everything clearly, confidently.

Paper selection

At first glance, this moment seems to be not so important. But if you look, this is not so. If not all, then a lot depends on the right choice of paper. For example, whatman or cardboard is not suitable for such a craft. But ordinary colored paper will fit much better into the appearance of such a flower. Even better, if we make a rose out of corrugated paper, which was originally created for such purposes. It is with its help that you can give the bouquet the most natural look. This is the best choice of material that will suit both a novice and an experienced master.

Before work

Before making a rose out of paper, it is necessary to prepare all the components in advance. First of all, these are tools. When making such a flower, you

will need:

  • stationery scissors;
  • ruler from 15 to 30 cm long;
  • a simple pencil (the type does not play a special role, and you can use any that suits you);
  • pliers;
  • side cutters.

From materials it is necessary to prepare in advance:

  • 2 types of wire: one with a cross section up to 1 mm 2 and the second about 10 mm 2 ;
  • paper of 2 colors (red and green);
  • glue.

Process of creation

Now you need to figure out how to make a rose out of paper at home with your own hands. First you need a sheet of red, whose length is about

20-30 cm and a width of 5-6 cm. On one side, along its length, cuts are made randomly every 2-3 cm to a depth of 1 cm. Then, a half-centimeter is rounded to one side. It turns out that on the one side everything was as it was, and on the other side a "braid" appeared. Further, this workpiece is wound in length. It turns out a kind of barrel, the height of which will be equal to the width of the sheet. The braid must be on the outside. In the process of twisting, on one side, the workpiece can be periodically fixed with glue (for example, every 2-3 centimeters). For greater reliability, the base of the bud is twisted with a wire with a diameter of up to 1 mm 2 . The next step is to have green paper. It is glued to a thick wire with a diameter of about 10 mm 2 . If desired, you can make leaves. To do this, use the same green paper and thin wire. Also, with its help, the previously made bud and stem are combined. Their joint is pasted over with green paper, and at the same time a smooth transition of one element to another is obtained. So we got acquainted with how to make a rose out of paper at home with our own hands.

Conclusion

After the glue dries, the craft is ready. It can be put in a pot and due to this decorate the room or give to your beloved. In any case, this will be a pleasant surprise for her. Any paper craft, including a rose, will become that trifle that can bring something new and special to your relationship.
