Hotel "Inn Park Pulkovskaya", St. Petersburg: description and photos

When planning a trip to St. Petersburg, where to spend the night should be taken care of in advance. For a carefree vacation in the Northern capital, it is better to choose a 3-4 star hotel with a convenient location, where guests are offered impeccable service, comfortable rooms and many additional services. The Park Inn Pulkovskaya 4 * hotel meets all these requirements. It will be discussed.

General information

Park Inn Pulkovskaya Hotel (St. Petersburg) is a multi-storey modern hotel complex that is part of the international Radison Hotel group. It is located in one of the most beautiful areas of St. Petersburg and is equally close to the historical center, railway stations and the airport.





How to get there

"Inn Park Pulkovskaya" is located near Moscow Avenue St. Petersburg:

  • 5 km separate the hotel complex from Pulkovo Airport, travel time on a free shuttle - 10-15 minutes;
  • 10 km to the river port and 16.5 km to the seaport will be more convenient to take by taxi (public transport - only with transfers);
  • 30 minutes takes the road to the main attractions in the city center by shuttle or metro;
  • a 10-minute walk along Moskovsky Prospekt is the nearest metro station of the same name (line 2);
  • 30 minutes by public transport to the railway stations;
  • 40 minutes by public transport to historic palaces and parks in the suburbs of the Northern capital (Tsarskoye Selo, Pavlovsk, Petrodvorets);
  • 3.4 km to the ring road.

Number of rooms

The hotel "Inn Park Pulkovskaya" is ready to offer 841 bright rooms of varying degrees of comfort.





Most of the hotel rooms are standard, but there are also “business” and “luxury” categories:

  • "Standard" (22 sq. M):

The room is equipped with one or two beds, air conditioning, television system, telephone and personal safe. The bathroom has a modern shower, hygiene kit and hairdryer.

  • "Business" (22 sq. M):

In addition to all the amenities offered in the standard category, the business class has a workplace with free Wi-Fi. There is also an iron, ironing board, bottled water, coffee, tea, bathrobes and slippers.

The room price includes free access to the swimming pool with saunas, a sports center.

  • "Suite" (46 sq. M):

The room category "suite" consists of two rooms. In addition to all the amenities offered in the business class, the deluxe room in one room has a king size double bed instead of standard beds, and comfortable upholstered furniture in the other. The bathroom is equipped with a bidet.

For a fee, an extra bed is provided in each room for a child over 12 years old or an adult. Children under 12 years old are entitled to free use of existing beds in the same room as their parents. For children under two years old the hotel provides a free cot.

Nutrition

Room rates include a continental breakfast in the main restaurant.





There are 5 restaurants and a bar in the building of the Inn Park Pulkovskaya hotel:

  • Park Inn Restaurant serves international buffet cuisine.
  • Hagakure Restaurant will delight guests with a wide range of Japanese dishes;
  • Bavarian restaurant-brewery Paulaner is known in the Northern capital for its unfiltered beer and meat dishes;
  • Atrium restaurant is designed for a banquet with the number of invited guests from 50 to 300 people;
  • the lobby bar is open 24 hours a day.

Additional services

  • spa, heated pool, saunas and baths;
  • Fitness Centre;
  • concierge service;
  • guarded parking lot;
  • beauty saloon;
  • currency exchange point;
  • dry cleaning and laundry;
  • medical service;
  • souvenir shops;
  • congress hall;
  • meeting rooms.




Full information about the hotel's services is available in the public domain, there is the possibility of booking a room online. If at the right time you cannot leave a request for accommodation via the Internet, you should call the reservation department of the Park Inn Pulkovskaya hotel complex. Phone: +7 812 740 3900.

The Inn Park Pulkovskaya Hotel is an excellent choice for business people and tourists who come to the Northern capital to explore its attractions. The hotel is popular with Russian and foreign tourists.
