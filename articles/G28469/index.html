Balance check

It is worth paying attention to the fact that mobile banking offers many different functions. Consider the most popular queries used in real life by Sberbank customers.



Let's start by checking the balance of Sberbank's bank card. SMS commands to number 900 are sent in the form of messages with the text "Balance". You can replace this word with other inscriptions.

If several plastics are tied to the phone, after the space after the main word, write the last 4 digits of the desired card. Everything is extremely simple and clear. This will have to be done when sending other requests through mobile banking.

Plastic Data

Sberbank teams "900" allow you to get the most information on a credit card. It is very comfortable. For example, you can find out which citizen cards are connected to the studied option. Or request details of recent plastic operations.

In the first case, the Sberbank SMS team will help. The word "Help" must be sent to number 900. If you want to get details, you will have to write the text "History" or "Extract" in the message. This operation can be replaced with numbers. Then in SMS the client should write "02".

These are far from all SMS teams of Sberbank. 900 is a short number with which mobile banking of the mentioned financial institution works. And if desired, the option can be locked or unlocked.

USSD replacement

We found out which Sberbank teams for 900 can be sent. But, as you can already see, it was only about SMS-requests. There are also USSD commands.

  • * 900 * phone * amount # - transfer money to the phone of another person;
  • * 900 * 12 * phone * money # - transfer to a card attached to a specified mobile phone.
