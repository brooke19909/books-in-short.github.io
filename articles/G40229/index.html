Excursion program

In addition to entertainment at the hotel, tourists are usually used to visiting the country in which they relax for a variety of relaxation. In Finike itself, you can visit Luna Park and the water park on your own. Also, in Presa Di Finica Hotel Suites 5, any vacationer can purchase excursion tours with a guide to local attractions (there are no attractions in the city itself). For tourists who do not like long excursions, it is advisable to visit the ancient city of Limira, it is 10 kilometers from Finike; the ancient necropolis, theater and baths are well preserved here.

You should definitely visit the town of Demre (30 km. From Finike), the Lycian city of Peace and the famous church of St. Nicholas the Wonderworker. It should be noted that this tour is very entertaining, many tourists vacationing in the resorts of Turkey, despite the long journey, come here to see these historic cities with their own eyes. The history of Demre-World is very interesting. The local church has long been considered a place of pilgrimage, believers come here from all over the Earth. Inside the temple is a sarcophagus where Nikolai the Wonderworker was buried. Not so long ago, a miracle happened with the sarcophagus in the church of Demre - he began to myrrh.

In walking distance from the church are the ruins of the city of Mira with rock burials and a beautiful amphitheater.





To the north of Finike are the ruins of ancient Aricanda, the mention of which was found in the 5th century BC. Here you can explore the complex of tombs, the ruins of the temples of Trajan and Helios, the acropolis and the theater, built in the 2nd century.

A little further from Aricanda on the shores of Kekova Bay, are the ruins of Simena. The history of this city also dates back to the fifth century BC. The city's harbor was popular among sailors. Here, sarcophagi preserved in the form of inverted boats, the remains of buildings and the main attraction - the ruins of the Lycian fortress.
