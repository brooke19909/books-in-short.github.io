Voluntary medical insurance policy for foreign citizens staying in the territory of the Russian Federation

In Russia, there is a compulsory health insurance program (MHI), which gives the right to receive free assistance to citizens of the country and non-residents. The policy is issued to all employed persons at enterprises. But this applies only to persons residing temporarily or permanently in the territory of the Russian Federation. In other cases, you will have to buy a voluntary medical insurance policy for foreign citizens (VMI). Read more about the conditions of this program later in the article.





A few words about compulsory medical insurance

The policy is issued to foreigners only if they are officially employed at domestic enterprises. The employer enters into an agreement with the insurance company and the city health insurance fund. The policy is limited by the duration of the employment contract. To obtain a document, a citizen should contact the personnel department and write a statement. Unemployed foreigners with a residence permit can also get a compulsory medical insurance policy, but already through an insurance company (UK). Children under one year of age, pregnant women receive medical care without insurance. This also applies to ambulance and ambulance. In case of loss of a document, you can get a duplicate either through the personnel department or in the UK. To gain a foothold at a particular clinic, you need to write a statement to your local health department. A copy of the passport and policy is attached to the document. Non-working persons can use the services of paid medical care or arrange a voluntary medical insurance policy for foreign citizens.





Who is the program for?

VHI can be used by foreigners temporarily or permanently located in the Russian Federation. Moreover, under the law, employed citizens are required to purchase a policy not only for themselves, but also for all family members. Without this document, it is impossible to draw up an employment contract or a residence permit to qualified specialists.

Voluntary Health Insurance Policy for Foreigners

To draw up a document, you need to contact the UK and choose one of two maintenance programs:

  • Specialized - the provision of highly targeted services.
  • Generally directed, within the framework of which the treatment is carried out by a general practitioner.

Voluntary medical insurance for foreign citizens in Moscow is far from all institutions. The choice is affected by the place of residence and the location of the clinic.



It should be noted right away that the document only takes effect 5-7 days after execution. Therefore, if a person was admitted to the hospital without a policy, he will have to compensate all the costs on his own. You cannot backdate a document. It is better for pregnant women to purchase a policy at the maximum price. It will cover not only diseases, injuries, dental, diagnostic procedures, but also possible complications during childbirth. Note that pregnancy is not a disease, so it is not included in the insurance. Births themselves in the territory of the Russian Federation are free.

Algorithm

The VHI program applies to people aged 18 to 60. The policy can be purchased for a period of 3 to 12 months. Migrants are not served in the district clinic. In case of malaise, a person turns to the UK, which sends him to a medical institution with which a cooperation agreement is concluded . If a person experiences acute pain, an ambulance must be called. This team serves foreigners for free. This is how voluntary health insurance works for foreign citizens. The law, which entered into force in January 2015, obliged all persons arriving in the Russian Federation for the purpose of employment to purchase VHI policies.





How much do you have to pay

The cost depends on the set of services. For registration, you need to provide an identity document. According to statistics, a voluntary medical insurance policy for foreign citizens is 1.5-2 times more expensive than for residents. This is because most of the country's guests do not know Russian. They have to turn to specialized institutions in which the staff speaks a foreign language.

The basic package includes obtaining outpatient, inpatient medical services in emergency situations. Starting price - 1.3 thousand rubles. This is three times cheaper than previously voiced by local authorities. Initially, it was planned that voluntary medical insurance for foreign citizens and pensioners will allow participants not only to receive advice at the clinic, but also to take sick leave, take an X-ray, ultrasound, ECG, and consult a dentist. But at this price, coverage is minimal.





Even at maximum cost, it does not apply to oncological, sexually transmitted diseases, tuberculosis, mental disorders, hepatitis and type I and type II diabetes. The most expensive packages allow you to get physiotherapeutic treatment, sick leave, prescriptions for drugs, MRI, ECG, RVG, REG and other types of diagnostics.
