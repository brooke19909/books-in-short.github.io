Flamingo Sunny Beach 4 * (Bulgaria): photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Bulgaria is the best place to spend an unforgettable vacation with your beloved family and children. Beautiful sandy beaches, mild climatic conditions, an unusual striped sea and delicious food, almost like in native Russia, are all ideal conditions for an amazing vacation and recovery of strength after long working days. High-quality and proven service is provided at the Flamingo Sunny Beach 4 * hotel (Sunny Beach). Reviews of “experienced” travelers characterize the resort very positively - thanks to what?





Choosing a resort

The most popular resort on the whole warm Bulgarian coast is Sunny Beach with a wide multi-kilometer promenade filled with souvenir shops with all kinds of cool toys, rose oil and homemade trinkets; artists who create their works right in front of bewitched spectators; a myriad of cafes and restaurants with rich local cuisine. It is in this resort, where incendiary events and events are constantly taking place, that the 4-star Flamingo Sunny Beach Hotel is located .





Beach

The family-friendly complex “Flamingo” is successfully located on the second coastline, that is, at a distance of two hundred meters from the Black Sea. This can be both a plus and a minus. On the one hand, sometimes with children it is not easy to overcome several road passages. But on the other hand, some distance from the most noisy places will allow you to sleep in the morning. Flamingo Sunny Beach 4 * (Sunny Beach) is famous for its wide and long sandy beach with a gentle entrance to the sea, convenient for children. All the paraphernalia necessary for a sea holiday - large umbrellas to protect from the sun, towels, comfortable sun loungers with soft mattresses - are provided at an additional cost (for the whole day guests are charged about 8 euros).

Number of rooms

All guests of the Flamingo Sunny Beach 4-star recreation complex are located in a large heated hotel complex, in one of 150 rooms of different categories. Clients are offered double rooms - especially for families - children in one bedroom, and parents, as a rule, in the living room. About 18 square meters is the total area of ​​each room in the Flamingo Sunny Beach 4 * hotel. "Tophotels" - a site popular with travel agents and travelers - speaks about the quality and attractiveness of the rooms: bright and pleasant rooms in pastel colors that do not hurt the eyes delight all guests.





"Equipping" the number of rooms of the hotel "Flamingo"

In each room, guests can use satellite TV, a free telephone to contact the administrator, a small personal safe for storing important documents and money, a set for making tea and coffee, a minibar that needs to be filled independently (all drinks can be purchased at a nearby supermarket) hairdryer in the bathroom. For greater comfort, a carpet that is pleasant to the feet is provided.





There are also specialized rooms suitable for people with disabilities - they are equipped with handrails and several ramps.

Entertainment

The hotel is considered an inexpensive, but very high-quality vacation spot with a surprisingly developed infrastructure for the usual Bulgarian "Quartet".

  • Near the hotel building there is a large outdoor pool, the water in which is periodically heated. Near it there is a small relaxation area with sun loungers and umbrellas.
  • A small sauna where you can bask after an eventful day.
  • Massage parlor and fitness center to keep fit.
  • You can take a walk with the children or with your soul mate in a beautiful green garden.
  • For children, there is a playroom and an outdoor playground with bright, colorful attractions.
  • The evening can be spent in the library with magnificent Russian literature from the best authors.

Additional services of the hotel Flamingo Sunny Beach 4 * (Bulgaria)

For a comfortable stay of guests, the complex provides additional services, which for the most part are provided for a separate fee.

  • Business people can ask for help from the cleaners who wash and iron all the necessary things and clothes by the specified time (payment for the laundry is purely symbolic).
  • If desired, clients of the complex can order food and soft drinks directly in the room.
  • When visiting Bulgaria on their own, Flamingo staff will help arrange transfer delivery from Burgas Airport to the hotel itself and back at the end of the trip.
  • On the ground floor of the hotel complex are a beauty salon and a hairdresser with professional craftsmen - they serve only by appointment.
  • The tour desk provides mini-trips to any part of the country from both local and major Russian tour operators.
  • It catches high-speed wireless Internet throughout the complex (only for an additional fee, which does not exceed a few euros per week of stay in the Flamingo Sunny Beach 4 * complex ). Hotel reviews confirm its quality and speed. After check-in number and payment for Internet access resources for a certain period of time each visitor is given a personal access code with a password.




The work of the staff of the recreation complex

The employees of the Flamingo Hotel never doze off and come to the rescue of customers in any situation - perhaps this is the main positive factor in deciding to choose a medium-sized complex - the whole family will be ensured with a welcoming attitude.

  • The 4-star Flamingo Sunny Beach staff can arrange car rental for guests. At the reception there is always a small catalog with different brands of cars. However, often, in order to drive a car, it is necessary to have a 1-3 year driving experience, be over 21 years old and have international driving rights (they can be easily and quickly obtained at any branch of the State Road Safety Inspection at the place of registration) . A car is issued for several days with a completely filled tank for gasoline - it is in this form of car that must be returned.
  • A receptionist at the Flamingo Sunny Beach 4-star hotel is on duty around the clock, who will always help in solving any problem.
  • The staff of this hotel speak several languages ​​at once for more convenient communication with customers of different nationalities - Dutch, English, German, Bulgarian and, naturally, Russian.

Life in Sunny Beach

As has been said more than once, Sunny Beach is the most lively, densely populated by travelers resort in the high season of Bulgaria. There is no time to be bored here - there is always the opportunity to stroll along a fun promenade filled with artists day and night; have a tasty and relatively inexpensive meal in one of the local eateries and, of course, get to know someone from fellow countrymen.





You can also go to the magnificent old town of Nessebar, located literally ten minutes by bus from our shore. This sparsely populated town amazes its visitors with its beautiful buildings and churches of past centuries. Nessebar is located on a small amazing island and is connected to Bulgaria by a small driveway - it has both interestingly decorated cafes with national cuisine, and magnificent sights, the history of which will be told by any local resident.
