Novopushkinsky Square: Muscovites favorite vacation spot

Novopushkinsky Square is well known not only to residents of Moscow, but also to its guests. This place is regularly visited by many people. Here you can relax from the bustle, feed the birds and enjoy the fountain. Near the square there are many interesting places and attractions.

general information

The opening of the green zone is usually attributed to the 70s of the last century. Novopushkinsky square appeared instead of houses that were no longer suitable for living and were destroyed. The place quickly turned into a recreation area, which is very popular with residents of the capital and tourists. The square has a very good location, as getting to it is very convenient. There are other attractions near the place of rest, which residents and guests of the city also often include in their travel plan. Nearby is not only the Kremlin, but also Tverskaya Boulevard, many theaters, museums, cafes.

On the territory of the park, trees are planted, lawns are broken. In 2013, a lot of attention was paid to the resting place during the reconstruction. Specialists came to grips with the lighting of the square, and a new tile was also laid. The fountain is a key object that attracts people from all over the city. In the cold season, it does not work, but still has a presentable appearance.

Fairs and exhibitions, various festivals, festivals are regularly held in Novopushkinsky Square.

How to get there

Novopushkinsky Square is located on Tversky Boulevard. You can get to the place by many routes by metro and bus. Very close to the square are the metro stations Pushkinskaya and Tverskaya. You can also get here by bus, they stop at the Tverskaya street stop. Finding a vacation spot is not difficult. Many citizens will immediately be able to tell his location. In addition, on the map Novopushkinsky Square is immediately clearly visible among other objects.

Mode of operation

You can visit a favorite place of Muscovites any day. The square operates seven days a week. Sometimes various actions or pickets take place here, then the townspeople transfer their walks. Novopushkinsky Square in Moscow is also well known for its fountain. It works on weekdays from 9 a.m. to 10 p.m. And on weekends and holidays, visitors can enjoy the fountain until 12 a.m.

Why is the square so popular

The recreation area has become a real oasis for citizens. A sufficient number of benches for rest are installed on the territory. People try to come here whenever possible in order to relax in the shade of trees and admire the fountain, which was opened in 1980. Novopushkinsky Square is often associated with this particular fountain, since this attraction is one of the key in it. The object is surrounded by original flower beds, alleys diverge to the sides of it. The whole square looks very harmonious and allows a person to enjoy the beauty of nature. In addition to a well-chosen floral design of the alleys, it is worth paying attention to the lights. They have a very original design and fit perfectly into the overall look.

The fountain in the center of the square always attracts visitors. It was reconstructed and became more perfect. When the object is working, thanks to a special supply of jets, the impression of a water bowl is created. The design of the fountain was carefully thought out in order to end up with a really worthy attraction. The jets are sloping, shimmering with a thousand drops in the light. Around are planted various varieties of flowers that perfectly frame it. In hot weather, it looks especially impressive. Water jets in flight are broken into large drops, which not only attract the eye, but also give the long-awaited coolness.
