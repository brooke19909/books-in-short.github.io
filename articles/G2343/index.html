Hotels in Vityazevo: rating and reviews

In the resort village of Vityazevo, located on the Black Sea, everything is provided for the relaxation of the most demanding tourist. Well-equipped beaches, well-developed infrastructure and friendly hotels of Vityazevo will make your stay here pleasant and comfortable.

Hotel Golden Sands

The Golden Sands Hotel is equipped with everything necessary for a good rest. A wide sandy beach near a shallow, well-warmed sea is located about five minutes walk from the hotel. The guarded area has a playground for children, a heated pool with a sun terrace, free parking. Young tourists can spend time indoors in the games room, during the day they will not be bored by animators. Vacationers with children have the opportunity to use the services of a nanny. Upon request, you will also be provided with a pram. In the dining room of the Golden Sands Hotel in Vityazevo you can eat three times a day. Meals are not included in the price. Nearby are several shops, shopping pavilions, where vacationers buy fruits and souvenirs, Paralia Boulevard. The following room categories are provided for guests:

  • deluxe double with a balcony;
  • deluxe triple;
  • triple superior;
  • family (5 people can be accommodated).

Tourists enjoy free Wi-Fi. Hotel Golden Sands occupies one of the leading places in the rating of hotels in Vityazevo. Located at: lane LOK Vityaz, house 21.

Hotel reviews

The hotel captivated guests with its homely atmosphere and comfort. They liked the hospitable staff, great clean rooms. Vacationers were impressed by the new furniture in the rooms. Separately, tourists celebrate excellent cuisine. A big plus they believe that the food in the dining room is varied and tasty, and there was no need to eat somewhere else. Children were satisfied with the playground. Parents are pleased that the animation staff entertained the kids in the evenings.

Absolutely everyone liked the clean and well-groomed area. The hosts professionally perform their work. Also, guests were pleased with the good WIFI.

All are satisfied with the convenient location of the hotel, the presence of a nearby amusement park, shops. True, it seemed to someone that the hotel has a small territory, but compared to other Vityazevo hotels located in the center of the village, this one is not the smallest.

French Quarter Hotel

In Anapa (Vityazevo) hotels are new and modern. In the six-story French Quarter Hotel in Vityazevo, 180 people can relax at the same time. The beach is a six-minute walk from the hotel. Just 500 meters you need to go to the Pyaterochka supermarket. Three meals a day according to the buffet system are organized in the restaurant. Bicycle, diving and fishing equipment can be rented. For a fee, you can organize interesting excursions. There are playgrounds for children on site and indoors, baby strollers are available upon request, and babysitting services are available. Entertainment is offered in the evenings. The hotel has a swimming pool with a sun terrace, free parking is equipped. Massage and fitness services are available. Tourists are offered the following options for accommodation:

  • double room with a balcony;
  • luxury room with a balcony;
  • triple room (there is a balcony, the type of bed is offered for choice).

There is wireless internet available for free. The French Quarter is one of the best hotels by the sea in Anapa (Vityazevo).
