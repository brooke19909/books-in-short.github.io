Skirts from old jeans

Denim clothing has been the most popular in the world for over 70 years. That is why in every house, over time, a huge number of men's, women's and children's jeans trousers accumulate. Instead of throwing them in a landfill, you can try to come up with a way to give such rubbish new life. For example, any men's jeans easily turn into a women's skirt of the desired length. To do this, you just need to open a few seams (do not touch the zipper, it is still useful to you) and make triangular inserts in front or behind from the bottom of the trousers or from any other fabric. Then you will have to hem the hem and, if you wish, decorate the skirt, for example with metal rivets or spikes.

Those who are interested in the question of whether it is easy to sew new clothes from old clothes with their own hands will be able to answer it themselves after they turn the T-shirt into a fashionable blouse. There are several ways to do this:

  • On the back of the T-shirt in the center, many parallel cuts are made, the length of which grows from bottom to top. Then they are intertwined with a long ribbon of contrasting color, its ends tied in a bow.
  • In a T-shirt (preferably a larger size, for example, for men), the upper part is cut so that the cut is 15 cm below the neck. Remaking a skirt
