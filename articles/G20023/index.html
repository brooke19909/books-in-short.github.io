Sony Xperia acro S: specs and model overview

Protection

One of the main features of the gadget is the availability of security standards. The Sony Xperia acro S LT26w housing characteristics can withstand immersion of the device to a depth of one meter, and also prevent the penetration of sand and other dirt.

Display

The device is equipped with a fairly high-quality 4.3-inch TFT-matrix with a spread of 1280 by 720 pixels, which is quite enough for ordinary needs. Working with the sensor is more than comfortable: the screen is responsive, there are no problems with the control.
