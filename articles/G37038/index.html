Holiday Village "Slava" (Vityazevo): living conditions and reviews

Relax on the Black Sea coast is provided by the recreation center "Glory" (Vityazevo). Family vacations are especially popular here. For him, the necessary living conditions and a variety of entertainment.

How to get there

The village of Vityazevo is located near Anapa. The city can be reached by plane or train. At Vityazevo Airport, it is possible to meet vacationers with vacationers for the agreed payment. In this case, the day of arrival and time are announced at the time of booking. You can get from the airport on the minibus number 113 with a transfer to number 114 at the city bus station. You need to get out at the stop "Holiday Village" Glory ".

You can take a train to the railway station in Anapa. It also provides a shuttle service, which is agreed in advance. This service is available for a fee. Tourists can get to the base on their own by minibuses No. 100 with a change at the bus station at No. 134. The necessary stop is "Slava" Holiday Village (Vityazevo).

Accommodations

Tourists are provided with 63 rooms with different conditions. On the territory there are one-story houses and cottages. Small houses are designed for one family vacation. They can accommodate up to 4 people.





The rooms have the necessary furniture for a comfortable stay. The rooms also have:

  • TV
  • air conditioning;
  • refrigerator;
  • toilet and shower.

Two-story cottages can accommodate up to 6 guests. On the ground floor there is a lounge with the necessary furniture. As well as a bathroom and shower. On the same floor you can cook your own food in the kitchen. Above is another bedroom with a spacious balcony. Cottages are equipped with appliances and working air conditioners.

To accommodate guests in the amount of 2-3 people comfortable rooms are provided in a three-story building. The rooms are equipped with comfortable furniture and household appliances. Almost all rooms have balconies. In this cottage you can book a two-room room for 4 people with all amenities.

Affordable cost has gained popularity at the recreation center "Glory" (Vityazevo). Prices vary, depending on the date of entry, type of cottage and the number of residents, from 1200 to 4700 rubles per day per room.

Entertainment and food

The territory of the base is surrounded by greenery. Here you can relax in the shade of trees on the many comfortable benches or in the gazebos. The recreation center "Glory" (Vityazevo) has everything you need for a vacation with children:

  • modern playgrounds;
  • fenced territory under protection;
  • green lawns.




On the territory there are 2 pools. Professional animators work who do not let kids or adults get bored. Children‚Äôs bicycles and strollers can be rented. On the territory there is an equipped medical station with a doctor on duty.





Here you can play billiards or table tennis. A sandy beach by the sea is 5 minutes walk from the base. There you can ride on water rides and take pictures with your favorite cartoon character. Nearby (15 minutes walk) are an aquapark and promenade. Here you can find supermarkets with everything you need for relaxation and groceries.

The recreation center "Slava" (Vityazevo) has a cafe-dining room on its territory. Here you can order a comprehensive meal for the entire period of rest or directly on the menu at the right time. The cafe is open until 24:00. The menu contains dishes for children of different ages.

Guests staying in two-story cottages can cook their own meals in their kitchens. They are fully equipped with appliances and utensils. You can purchase the necessary products in the store near the base.

Holiday Village "Glory" (Vityazevo): reviews

Tourists positively note discounts of 50% for children under 16 years old. Also, many are pleased with the presence of a parking lot at the base. Many emphasize the cleanliness of the area around the houses and delicious food in the dining room. Guests are encouraged by the availability of laundry, albeit at an additional cost.





There are some minor remarks regarding old televisions in the rooms, but they do not sound often because they go to the sea to relax and not watch programs. According to tourists, the maids clean and change the bed on time.
