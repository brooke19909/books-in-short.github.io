Park Club "Dubrovsky", Volgograd: address, infrastructure, entertainment, recreation, customer and visitor reviews

When they say that nature is able to give an energy charge, they mean amazing places that you fall in love with at first sight. Such a place is located 18 kilometers from Volgograd in the small town of Great October. On one of its outskirts is a fairly popular holiday destination - Dubrovsky Park Club. Volgograd with its international airport remains far away. It offers a cozy private beach, a children's playground, an outdoor pool and a 24-hour reception.

Infrastructure

The park club is located in a shady oak grove on the Erica coast in the large Volgo-Akhtubinskaya floodplain natural park. Remoteness from noisy tracks and bustling cities is the key to good rest with both soul and body. On the camp site there are houses with terraces, numerous recreation areas, a bathhouse and an equestrian club.

The bath, by the way, is built on the shore. It is a log house from the Arkhangelsk pine. All necessary bath accessories are leased. After the steam room, you can plunge into the river, and then, wrapped in a warm blanket, conduct intimate conversations over a cup of herbal tea or beer. The descent to the river is equipped with convenient steps. In winter, for extreme people in the ice cut a window.

Near the pool a cozy relaxation area is organized. Here are sun loungers, beach chairs, sheltered from the sun by umbrellas. There are beach towels.

Mangal zone is equipped in the center. For all the necessary devices you can contact the administration (what to buy, what to rent).

Guests arriving at the base all day are offered to rent large comfortable gazebos. There are summer options with a capacity of six to ten people and from 30 to 40 vacationers. The winter gazebo is ready to receive up to 16 guests, on the water - up to 10. Guest toilets (male and female) are located near the gazebos. There are also shower stalls with a changing area. Immediately behind the gates there is a spacious guarded parking lot.

The territory of the Dubrovsky camp site in Volgograd (this is separately noted in the reviews) is treated with special compounds against mosquitoes. And the guests believe that this adds to the rest of comfort and peace.

Park Club Mission

The organizers attribute the joy of communication with wondrous natural beauties to the main goals of relaxation in this place. Among the advantages of rest at the Dubrovsky camp site, the following can be noted:

  • ample opportunities for organizing and conducting trainings and various corporate events (up to one hundred people) in a new, well-equipped conference room;
  • keeping your body in shape - this is facilitated by comfortable modern sports fields in the fresh air;
  • cozy cafe "Dubrava" with soulful home cooking;
  • Unique residential log houses and townhouses.

To raise their vitality throughout the territory of the Dubrovsky Park-Club (Volgograd), guests are greeted with inscriptions on wooden tablets with phrases that can inspire, give a good mood for the whole day or come up with a creative idea.

Apartments

All rooms overlook the garden. All rooms are equipped with a modern TV with a large selection of satellite channels, an electric kettle, toiletries, a fridge, a wardrobe and beds.

Some apartments in Dubrovsky (Volgograd) have their own not large, but very convenient kitchen area. Absolutely all have a seating area, Wi-Fi, underfloor heating and climate control.

Nutrition

Dubrovsky Park Cafe serves a continental-style breakfast daily. It is included in the price. There is also a buffet and a Russian restaurant. All dishes are prepared from natural products of local manufacturers.

Entertainment

In case of bad weather, guests of the Dubrovsky Park Club (Volgograd) can play table tennis on the large cozy terrace or simply sit in one of the large comfortable chairs with a book or magazine.

At any other time, rest should be active - as the organizers of the camp say. Therefore, they offer their guests specially designed walking routes in the Dubrovsky park (Volgograd) and fishing - both from the shore and from the boat. You can also take a steam bath in a bathhouse, melted according to old Russian customs, and even ride a horse. Attentive professional instructors will help everyone.

In the park-hotel "Dubrovsky" in Volgograd, catamarans, boats (rowing and motor) and bicycles are offered for rent. It is possible to ride a boat and water ski.

For sports here everything is organized at the highest level: sites with modern equipment and high-quality coating and a wide selection of equipment. In addition to the above, there can be rollers, ping-pong and badminton rackets, ice skates, skis, clubs, water skis, ice drums, jump ropes, Finnish sleds, balls and much more.

The complex is ideal for families. There is always something to do for both adults and children, and at any time of the year. In the warmer months, these are walks, swimming, fishing, sports, walking or horseback riding. In winter, guests with a breeze ride on sleds, skis and skates and, of course, sculpt snowmen and play snowballs.

During the day, Dubrovsky park-club (Volgograd) offers a lot of entertainment for all ages, and vacationers spend quiet evenings watching movies, reading (the local library is quite extensive) or talking over a cup of tea.

Also at the camp site there is the opportunity to organize a professional photo shoot. Sitting at home, it will be nice to remember how everyone rode horses together, had a romantic dinner or played giant chess.

For wealthy guests, the campsite administration offers horseback riding with a personal chef who organizes a picnic. As well as cooking classes, thematic lunches or dinners and live sports events.

Wi-Fi is available throughout the park-club "Dubrovsky" (Volgograd).

Affairs

Corporate trainings, yoga classes, business seminars and any similar events can be held in a large, beautiful and modern conference room. It offers showers, changing rooms, a yoga room, rooms for coaches, a kitchen area for a coffee break, 60 seats and presentation equipment of the latest generation.

Tourist note

It is possible (by prior arrangement and at an additional cost) to bring a pet of medium-sized breeds and weighing no more than 5 kg.

When booking, prepayment is required. Its size varies depending on the selected apartments. Prepayment must be made within 24 hours after booking. If the check-in is booked less than one day in advance, no prepayment is required.

The reservation can be withdrawn no later than two weeks before the check-in date. If the reservation changes occurred later or the check-in is canceled altogether, the administration reserves the right to apply penalties in the amount of 50 percent of the total amount of the reservation.

Prices per day on weekends, holidays and weekdays are different. Detailed information is available from the administrator by phone number posted on the camp site's official website. Only cash is accepted.

Judging by the reviews, this is the best place to stay in terms of price / quality ratio. Vacationers believe that they get more in Dubrovsky than for the same money in another similar place.
