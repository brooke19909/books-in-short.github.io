How to remove T9 on an iPhone and why is it needed?

The age of progress sometimes plays a cruel joke with ordinary inhabitants. What is T9





Before you turn off something, you need to understand what it is and why. It will be about a special dictionary T9 for mobile devices. By the way, this engineering miracle was invented even before the appearance of the first smartphone from Apple. And it was considered quite a progressive and useful technology. However, someone still thinks so.

So, T9 is a system that predicts which keys you press by sending a message. Sometimes the system works falsely, and this causes various incidents. However, it is useful.

How it works





Before answering the question of how to remove the T9 on an iPhone, let's try to understand the principle of operation of this system. This built-in program is a dictionary consisting of preloaded words. In addition, it is possible to add new words to it, simply repeating the text. T9 allows you not to press the same keys at the same time, but use only nine. From there and the name. The more often you use any words, the more often T9 will offer you to write them.

Thanks to this feature, funny incidents sometimes happen, many of which are captured on social networks.

Pros and cons

Of the positive features of this system - it sometimes helps to send a short and competent message to the interlocutor. This is convenient, for example, at a time when the user is very busy with something and he does not want to divert all attention to his mobile device. For example, while eating or at school. However, such situations can come up with the sea.

Of the minuses - T9 sometimes distorts the texts so much that you then have to get out in front of the interlocutor or at best retype everything. However, the function is definitely quite useful. But not always.

How to remove T9 on iPhone

In fact, the statement of the question itself is slightly incorrect. On the Iphone, in fact, there is no keyboard, but there is a large touch screen. Accordingly, this function is called auto-correction.

Nevertheless, people are asking questions about, for example, how to remove T9 on an iPhone 5s. There is an answer. It is necessary to do a little manipulation, namely: go to the settings item, then select the "General" sub-item and go to the keyboard section. It remains only to remove the "Auto Correction" item.
