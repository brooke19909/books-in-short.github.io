When buying a home, what to look for: expert advice

When buying a home what to look for? This is a completely normal question that arises among potential property buyers. A house is not an apartment, but a more spacious object. And it needs to be looked at more seriously and more carefully. What factors do experts recommend paying attention to? How to determine that a transaction is executed with a bona fide seller? A few tips will help prevent mistakes when buying houses and summer cottages.





Appearance

Are you planning to buy a private house? You will have to pay attention to many factors. And the first component is the appearance of the property. Usually people try to buy houses that do not require major repairs. An exception is if a complete overhaul is planned. But in this case, it is recommended to look at the usual land.

Often, the appearance of the house is reflected directly in the ad. So it turns out to evaluate the conformity of price and offer. If somewhere in the appearance of the building there are flaws, you can "bring down" the value of the property.

Basic data

When buying a home, what should I look for first? Each buyer clearly knows what he wants to buy. Accordingly, you need to look for an object that meets the advanced requests.

It is usually recommended to focus on the basic data of the property. Namely:

  • locality;
  • area;
  • remoteness from the city;
  • infrastructure;
  • neighbors;
  • building parameters (number of rooms, their size and so on).

All this will help to understand how the upcoming purchase meets the initial requirements. In addition, for all of the above parameters, many can conclude how good the house or cottage is.





Communications

When buying a house, what should I look for already during the initial personal inspection of the property? On communications that are held to the facility. The fact is that in some private houses for sale, there is neither water supply nor light. Somewhere it will not work to carry out a phone or the Internet. All of this is recommended to focus attention.

Also, when examining the condition of the house, it is necessary to evaluate the pipes and other components. Not too smiling the prospect of buying a building that will have to be repaired before moving. And thoroughly.

If there is no communication, it is recommended that the seller or realtor clarify why there are no options. If you want to conduct the Internet in advance, it is best to find out the address of the building and call the provider to clarify this possibility. It is likely that the house is being sold due to the fact that certain communications refuse or cannot lay it. Then you can refrain from buying. But that is not all!

Price

Have to buy a new home? The buyer should pay attention to such a factor as the value of the property. Often, this indicator indicates the integrity of the seller, as well as the problem of the house.

It is worth remembering: in a good area and in perfect condition, real estate is very expensive. And if the price tag is low, this is an occasion to think: what is wrong? At low prices, houses are sold only in case of urgent need. But this is an extremely rare occurrence. In Russia, it practically does not occur.





If the price is suspiciously low, it is best to ask the realtor or seller why this price was chosen. Most likely, either a specific problem in the house or cottage will be called, which ‚Äúknocks down‚Äù the price, or no exact explanation will be given at all. In the second case, it is recommended to postpone the purchase and find out exactly why real estate is cheap.

Plot

What other tips can I give? When buying a home what to look for? Experts also recommend a closer look at the site, which is adjacent to the house or cottage. Usually there is at least a piece of land free from buildings.

Very often in private sectors near houses there are gardens or gardens. If the site does not look the best, abandoned for a long time - this is a good reason for bargaining. It is best to look into all the buildings that are located on the territory. And in advance to stipulate what will happen to the contents, for example, in a shed near the house.

In a buiding

What else? Further it is necessary not only to check communications, but also some other components. At the moment, it is recommended to even invite special appraisers to help quickly understand the real state of the home. Usually this responsibility lies with the seller. But the buyer has the right to call an assessment group.

When buying a home what to look for? Experts advise to look at the following points:

  1. Doors How they open, close, what is their condition, a separate item - the presence of locks.
  2. Window. Density at closing, ease of opening, whether a draft is created.
  3. Attic. Here, most often the landlord hides the "skeletons". For example, leaves old and unnecessary things.
  4. Boiler. In private homes, heating boilers are installed. It is required to check the correct operation of this component.
  5. Walls and floor. It is required to check for evenness and correct installation. Also, the walls are checked for cracks and other damage.


Now there is nothing to fear if you have to buy an old house. What to pay attention to in this case is already clear. But this is not all the basic nuances. The previously listed factors must be taken into account, even if the house is new. The last step is a must. Without it, the risk of contacting scammers is high.

Documents

The last and, as already mentioned, the most important point is the verification of documents. It is at this stage that it may turn out that a citizen is buying a house with certain problems. Or even deals with scammers.

Upcoming home purchase? Pay attention (documents that will be listed below are extremely important) is necessary for the availability of original papers. Which ones? Check required:

  • host identification card;
  • number of land and house owners;
  • the presence of consent (certified by a notary) for sale;
  • certificates of ownership of land and building;
  • cadastral passport;
  • evaluation report (if any);
  • a certificate of the absence of one or another burden at the house and the plot.




Additionally, you can request from the seller:

  • building permit;
  • Spouse's agreement to the transaction;
  • Marriage certificate;
  • acts on the operation of buildings and structures on the site;
  • extract from the house book about those registered in the house.

Accordingly, only after these documents are checked, it is advisable to talk about buying real estate. These tips will help to avoid fraud and check how good a house is bought.
