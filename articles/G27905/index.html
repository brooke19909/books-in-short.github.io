Naama Bay - a beautiful corner of Egypt

Egypt is a country that has been fascinated by tourists for several decades. There are sandy beaches, clear sea, fresh air and unforgettable hotels located along the entire coast. By all means, this country is worth visiting not only because of the pyramids and various sights, but rather a vacation in Egypt - a vacation in one of the most luxurious hotels in the Arab countries.

Central Sharm El Sheikh





If you want your vacation in a Muslim country to remain in your memory for a long time, then you should choose a place to spend your vacation in the center of all hotel and resort areas - in Naama Bay. This is a fairly long place, offering many different leisure options, from not very expensive to five-star hotels operating on the all inclusive system.

The location of this "hotel paradise" is also considered attractive - a small bay, sheltered from the winds and providing tourists with quality relaxation under the caressing rays of the warm sun. It is noteworthy that the beach areas here are relatively flat, with golden sand and turquoise waters, in which there are many beauties. No need to worry, there are many boats in Naama Bay that will take all diving enthusiasts to the "hot" places of the underwater world.

However, lovers of parties and city bustle will not be bored here either. Along the main street there is a huge variety of shops, boutiques, clubs and restaurants, where visitors can enjoy national dishes and all the delights of nightlife. The main attraction is the Hard Rock Cafe, it is worth a visit for everyone.

But Naama Bay is more famous for its hotels. To make the right choice in favor of a particular location, you should study the map of the bay a little.

Here are a few options that are worthwhile and may be of interest to most visitors to Egypt.



Sofitel 5 * - its main difference from others is that this hotel has several beach areas at its disposal: two of which are sandy, and three - coral, for outdoor enthusiasts.

A high level of service, excellent room service - all this, of course, is characteristic of a five-star establishment.

Hilton hotel connoisseurs have something to think about, as there are two such hotels in Naama Bay, you have to make a choice.

Either it will be the Fayrouz hotel, located on the first line, or Dreams, located on the second line, but providing all-inclusive services. It is noteworthy that these hotels have the longest beaches in a small bay.



There are many other good options that can attract the attention of visitors to the country, but of the most expensive of the above, there are no doubt favorites. Cheaper hotels in Naama Bay - Sonesta club, Gufyresort, Ghazala Garden. Here you can save.

In general, Sharm El Sheikh, namely its central part, is a rather picturesque place that is highly appreciated by tourists, so no matter what hotel you choose, you will be able to relax at "5+". The main thing is not to miscalculate the choice of travel time, as there is also its seasonal schedule. For example, on New Year's holidays on sandy beaches it is quite deserted. These days, Egypt, Naama Bay in particular, is resting from crowds of tourists. In the winter months, prices drop significantly.
