Sony HDR-CX405: specifications and reviews

The high-tech segment is characterized by a balanced fusion of professional capabilities and amateur-level ergonomics. In the best models of equipment, manufacturers also combine wide functionality and cost, affordable for ordinary users. The latest developments cover an increasing space of multimedia devices. They are most clearly manifested in photo and video equipment. So, the Flash HD model Sony HDR-CX405 demonstrates by its example the successful symbiosis of a well-thought-out optional, easy-to-use design and modern software filling.

Model Overview

Recently, Japanese developers have released many interesting offers from the highest segment of video cameras. For the most part, these are expensive devices that have also acquired individual innovative functions.





Not so willingly, the company pays attention to the initial class, but in this direction the Sony HDR-CX405 model provided a significant reserve, which was distinguished by optimized capabilities and decent shooting characteristics. The device compares favorably even with its class competitors in modest size, sufficient light sensitivity and good resolution. We can say that the creators foresaw possible flaws of the potential state employee and tried to surprise the fans of the brand with the absence of such shortcomings. In key moments, this worked out, but there was still room for surprises.

Model Design Sony HDR-CX405

Perhaps, the main thing that knocks this model out of the general series of similar video cameras is miniature. The case is made in ultra-small sizes, which was reflected in the mass, which amounted to only 215 grams. On the whole, the form factor retained the familiar constructional solution, with the exception that when folded, the device can be put in the shirt pocket.





Another unexpected decision, the feasibility of which is being debated. This is almost the complete absence of external controls. The Sony HDR-CX405 side panel , located under the display, hides a niche for the memory card and Micro-HDMI video input. By the way, the menu can only be controlled using the five-way joystick located on the display frame. I must say that the small design made it possible to provide the camera with a 1240 mAh mobile format battery.

Interface

The camcorder does not have a built-in wireless adapter, which is a rarity for modern models. The HDMI output can both transmit a live image, and provide broadcasting along with graphic processing - that is, with content in the form of information and interactive elements that are drawn on top of the recording.





The settings of the model are standard for amateur-level devices, but there is an important difference. It consists in the service menu, returning to which the user is in the place of the last stay. In terms of physical control, the first time the Sony HDR-CX405 camcorder may seem defiantly uncomfortable. But as you operate, you can get used to it. The joystick is represented by a small element of mechanical control, but its presence largely saves the ergonomics of the device.

Positive feedback about the model

First of all, many emphasize the shooting functionality. So, the optical 30x zoom allows you to shoot at long distances, but most importantly - it retains the relative sharpness of the image. Even more praise is received by the stabilization system. This is one of the strengths of the model, which all users unconditionally recognize. Even with active walking, setting to the intelligent stabilization mode virtually eliminates the trembling and vibrations of the Sony HDR-CX405 picture.





Reviews also note the characteristics of macro photography, which may resemble the similar capabilities of budget digital cameras. True, to maximize the disclosure of this ability, you must purchase the appropriate tripod. As for the lack of external controls, far from all users recognize this solution as a plus. These include perhaps fans of modern approaches to touch control systems with minimal sets of mechanical controls, buttons and selectors.

Conclusion

The camera is small, balanced in characteristics, provides good shooting and easy handling. The price tag was also attractive, amounting to an average of 16-17 thousand rubles. This is a reasonable price for the representative of the Handycam line, confidently shooting in HD formats. Sony HDR-CX405, rather, is suitable for practical users who are carefully suited to the selection of optional content. The advantages of the model include moderate energy saving, so the camera can be used in conditions of complete autonomy. This option is also suitable for those who do not want to spend a lot of time on reconfiguring complex parameters - the model is simple and accessible in this regard.
