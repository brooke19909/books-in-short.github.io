Dahab Resort 5 * (Egypt / Dahab): reviews, photos

In this article we will comprehensively consider the resort hotel Dahab Resort 5 * (Egypt, Dahab). For lovers of relaxation on the Sinai Peninsula, this is the most ideal place. Let's say right away: this is not even a hotel, but a whole wellness center. In addition, "with a name." At least the fact that the hotel belongs to the Hilton Hotels & Resorts line testifies to the high quality of services. Many tourists may know its former name - Hilton Dahab Resort.

The current name of the five-star hotel appeared only at the very beginning of 2014. The hotel is located on the first line. The hotel has its own beach on the Red Sea. Like many hotels in Dahab, this one specializes in hosting divers and windsurfers. The hotel even has a school for teaching these sports. But it is not necessary to dive into the depths of the sea. Even the most lazy tourists in Dahab Resort 5 * will have an unforgettable vacation. This is evidenced by those who have already been there.

Where is the hotel located and what is it like

From Sharm El Sheikh International Airport, you need to overcome 87 kilometers to get to the hotel. It takes even longer to go from Taba - after all, it is separated from Dahab by about 140 km. Dahab Resort 5 - Country Hotel. From the distance from the center of Dahab for three kilometers. The area in which the hotel is located is called Laguna due to the artificial bay and the network of canals. The hotel area is very large, green and well-groomed. An artificial lagoon crashes into the coast, and beautiful bridges need to be crossed everywhere. The hotel consists of two-story buildings built in the Nubian style. The hotel was built in 1997, but since then it has been repeatedly updated and reconstructed.

Where live

The room fund of the hotel Dahab Resort 5 is one hundred sixty-three rooms for guests. All rooms are located in two-story white bungalows. The rooms are divided into the categories of "standard", "suite" and "executive room". Reviews recommend choosing a second floor, because in such rooms there are spacious terraces with a hammock. All rooms in the hotel are equipped with air conditioning, minibar, work desk. A TV with satellite channels brightens the evening. If you want tea or coffee, then in the room you will find everything you need for the independent preparation of these drinks. As befits a five-star hotel, rooms include an iron and ironing board. Naturally, the bathrooms have a hairdryer. High-speed Internet is also included in the list of services that are provided directly in the room. They clean the rooms every day very carefully.

Sea and pools

Dahab Resort 5 is located in a small bay next to two more network fives - Ibis Style and Swiss Inn. The golden beach smoothly turns into a sand spit. The entrance to the sea is smooth, and the water is clean. The beach is carefully cleaned and completely freed from shards of coral. Already from about half the road to the buoys you can observe fish. One of the attractions of the local shallow water is moray eel, which lives in a jug surrounded by corals. An alternative to the sea can be a spacious outdoor pool. It is surrounded by a terrace with umbrellas and sunbeds covered with mattresses. Beach towels are free.

Hotel facilities

When you meet you will be given a cold fresh juice, and the minister will take your luggage directly to your room. Hotel Dahab Resort 5 (Dahab) has a business center where guests can scan out the necessary documents or send a fax. It is also possible to organize a conference or a seminar at the hotel, especially since all the necessary equipment is provided for this. Currency can be exchanged for Egyptian pounds without leaving the hotel. If you arrived before the check-in time or depart late in the evening, it is recommended that you leave your luggage in the storage room at the reception. By the way, the hotel staff speaks several languages, so all your wishes will be heard. Recently, there have been more Russian tourists. Therefore, at the reception it will be easy to arrange for those who are not strong in foreign languages. The hotel administration pays great attention to the health of its guests and openly promotes sports. At the hotel you can take short scuba diving or windsurfing courses and even try your hand accompanied by an experienced instructor. Favorite exercise will be more effective in the gym. It features a tennis court, squash court and a pool table. Well, beautiful ladies can relax under the skillful hands of masseurs, visit a hairdresser or beauty salon. There is no animation as such at the hotel, but oriental shows are held three times a week.



For children

No less tutelage than adults, surrounds the Dahab Resort 5 (Dahab) hotel of its young guests. The main restaurant always has a children's menu and high chairs for feeding babies. A playground with slides and swings is a great alternative to building sand castles on the beach. This room can accommodate a baby cot upon request. For a fee, the hotel provides a highly qualified nanny (you need to book it for two days). Children over four years old can be recorded in a mini-club - animators on the beach will be engaged with them. And after eight years, the child is already able to attend diving or windsurfing courses. The hotel has a children's pool.
