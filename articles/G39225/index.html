Resort Feature

Budva does not belong to cheap coastal cities, although here you can always find decent budget accommodation options, and those in which there is an ideal combination of price and quality. This type of hotel includes small mini-hotels, including Villa Duletic (Cat A) 3 * (Montenegro, Budva). As for food, in Montenegro in cafes and restaurants the prices are quite reasonable, and the food, although uncomplicated, is tasty, and most importantly, environmentally friendly.





The greatest good that can be found in Montenegro, in particular in Budva, is the warm attitude of local residents who will always be welcomed by the welcome guests, they will smile, provide assistance if necessary, explain and tell everything.

Coast

Why do tourists go to sea resorts? That's right, first of all, for a beach vacation, so for many, the cleanliness of the water and the beach, the equipped entrance to the sea, etc. are very important. From this point of view, the coast of Budva is simply magnificent. In addition, there are islands with beaches. For example, about. St. Nicholas, where tourists get by sea. As in most European countries, all the beaches of Budva are municipal, which means they are free, but beach equipment (sun loungers and an umbrella) is paid.

How to get there





If you are going to relax in Villa Duletic 3 * in Montenegro (Budva), then you can get there from Moscow and some other cities of Russia by air. However, there is no airport at the resort itself. You will have to fly to Tivat, which is located 20 km from Budva. The approximate flight time is three and a half hours. Ticket price (round-trip) from 280 EUR. You can also first fly to Podgorica or Dubrovnik. However, the airports of these cities are located at a greater distance than the city of Tivat. In the first case, the journey from the airport to the Villa Duletic 3 * hotel will take about 20 minutes, and a taxi ride will cost about 20 euros. By the way, you can order a group transfer, and then it will cost you a little cheaper.

Description and location of the hotel

Villa Duletic 3 * is located at the following address: Montenegro, Budva, Tsetinska Boulevard 3. It is 200 m from the city center and a 2-3 minute walk to the bus stop. Within walking distance of the hotel are supermarkets, cafes and small restaurants. The location is very good, it seems that you are in the very center, but at the same time away from the hustle and bustle. Hotel Villa was built in 2009. It is ready to offer its guests seven comfortable rooms with all amenities and a balcony, overlooking the sea or the surrounding area.

Room Facilities

Cleaning, replacement of towels and replenishment or updating of personal cosmetics at the hotel are carried out daily. Lingerie is changed every five days. Beach Villa Duletic 3 * (Montenegro, Budva)





The sea is very close, literally five minutes walk, you just need to cross the road. As already noted above, umbrellas and deck chairs are provided for a fee, there are no towels, they need to be taken with you. Here you can find many options for beach activities, from volleyball to catamaran riding, water skiing, parachuting, etc.

Nutrition

Villa Duletic 3 * does not have a restaurant, but there is a buffet where a continental breakfast is served in the mornings. Everyone can eat at the restaurant "LM", which is located 150 m. If meals are included in the price of the hotel, then the rest will cost tourists a little cheaper. Otherwise, vacationers can always dine in the city, in one of the many restaurants, or cook their own meals in the room.

Villa Duletic 3 *: reviews





If you read the reviews of guests who have already spent their holidays in this charming villa, you can hardly find negative ones among them. The thing is that, despite its modesty, the hotel provides tourists with a fairly high level of service. The guests also note the friendliness and hospitality of the owners of the hotel. Of course, there is no such entertainment as a fitness club or a mini-golf course, but you can completely relax away from the hustle and bustle, sit with a cup of tea on the balcony, breathing in the fresh and cool sea breeze. Entertainment can always be found in the city, within walking distance. And tourists note a very convenient location of the beach. Just a couple of minutes on foot - and now you are already on the beach. The beach is magnificent, it is clean, well-groomed, sand and gravel.
