How to make a cardboard box?

Going on holiday, we often acquire special packaging material. It can be various packages or cardboard boxes. The main thing is that they fit perfectly in size and have a beautiful surface. Manufacturers usually offer standard products, which are in high demand among most buyers. If among the available offers you could not find a suitable option for packing your gift, then it's time to find out how to make a cardboard box with your own hands.



First you need to carefully and with utmost care measure the size of what you decide to give. Measurement must be carried out at the most extreme points. If you make a mistake in the smaller direction, then your gift simply does not fit in the box. In the opposite case, it will constantly move inside the package, and this does not guarantee its integrity and safety.

True, for this purpose, most likely, not cardboard was used, but some other, less dense paper. Usually this is taught in the elementary grades, thereby developing the students' volumetric thinking. If you have not encountered this yet, then draw a square on the sheet that will correspond to the bottom of your future box. On all four sides, add rectangles to it that will correspond to the sides of the manufactured product. After the contours are outlined, draw allowances along which the box will stick together. It remains only to cut it and assemble it correctly. The cover is made similarly. Only the square is made a little larger, and the rectangles are smaller.



In the labor lessons, of course, they did not talk about how to make a box with a round bottom. However, if desired, you can glue and such. Its better to make the team. For this, a circle of the required diameter is cut. The circumference is calculated, which, if someone has forgotten, is equal to two radii times 3.14. At the same time, allowances for seams are left, along the entire length of which cuts are made. Then the bottom and side are connected using tape or glue.



Probably, it is also worth mentioning how to make a box of cardboard in a triangular shape. In this case, draw a triangle. At the same time, its sides should twice exceed the desired packaging dimensions. Then we denote the fold lines. To do this, we note the middle of the parties. Connect them to the vertices. Be sure to leave allowances on all sides. We place the gift in the central triangle and collect the box. It remains only to decorate it, after which it is safe to go to the celebration.

If you still show a little imagination, then you can easily guess how to make a cardboard box of any other shape. If nothing interesting comes to mind, then take a beautiful wrapping paper and glue it over the shoe box. This option is not laborious and requires only accuracy.
