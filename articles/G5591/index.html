Hotel Majesty Club La Mer Art 5 * (Kemer, Turkey): description, photos and reviews of tourists

In Turkey, there are many beautiful resorts where tourists are waiting for comfortable hotels, wonderful service and lots of entertainment. Goynuk stands out among them for its unique natural monuments - intricate grottoes, caves, canyons. The second plus of the village is its proximity to the leader of the Turkish tourism business - the city of Kemer. Some do not share these two settlements at all, considering them to be parts of a single whole. Here in such an interesting place from the point of view of various tourist bonuses there is a good, but inexpensive hotel for its category, the Majesty Club La Mer Art 5 * hotel. I would like to note that its territory is adjacent to the Majesty Club La Mer, also a five-star hotel. Many vacationers do not pay attention to a slight difference in the names, which causes confusion in the reviews, because the conditions of rest in these two hotels are not quite the same. We will try to avoid inaccuracies and give the most complete information specifically about Majesty Club La Mer Art 5 *, a wonderful hotel that has been pleasing tourists with its excellent service for many years.



Location

The village of Goynuk occupies a small strip of land in a place where the majestic Taurus Mountains retreated from the sea into the continent. It is 45 km from Antalya, and only 7 km from Kemer. Majesty Club La Mer Art 5 * is the third hotel in a row from Kemer. It is located on the outskirts of the village of Goynuk, right on the shores of the delightful Mediterranean Sea, in a place where the D400 heavy-duty highway makes a big loop, providing peace to the rest. From the center of Goynuk it is about a 20-30 minute walk. Nearby is an asphalt road leading to the village, as well as onto the D400 highway leading to Antalya and stretching along the entire coast. You can get to the hotel on the company‚Äôs company transfer of the travel agency, where you purchased tickets, as well as on your own, on regular buses that run from early morning until the evening with an interval of 30 minutes. In Antalya, you can take them either at the central bus station, or near the large shopping center Migros. They come here from the coast. And it is most convenient to get to Kemer by dolmushi. The road takes only 5 minutes.



Majesty Club La Mer Art 5 *: hotel description

On the territory of almost 5 hectares, teeming with flowers, evergreen shrubs and trees, among which there are a lot of conifers, all the buildings of the Majesty Club La Mer Art 5 * (Kemer) are located. Designers have worked hard to harmoniously, without disturbing the natural balance, to enter four swimming pools between the green spaces of the building, a free parking lot, sports grounds, restaurants, bars, an amphitheater, and a playground for children.

Everything here is thought out to the smallest detail and is designed to create maximum comfort for guests, the opportunity to stroll along the alleys, enjoying the aromas of needles and flowers, and if necessary, easily and quickly get into your building or reach the desired infrastructure. The Majesty Club La Mer Art 5 * hotel was built in 1987, the last time the innovations were held in 2005, therefore, not everything sparkles with novelty, but everything is functioning properly. The reception is located in the main building of the hotel, where tourists are served 24 hours a day. In the spacious lobby there are several shops with beach accessories, shoes, clothes from leading Turkish manufacturers, with accessories and souvenirs, a library, slot machines, many comfortable sofas, armchairs and tables, a billiard area, a stroller and car rental, a lobby bar . Everything looks nice enough, clean, neat, creates an excellent atmosphere for relaxation.

Rooms in the central building

For accommodation of tourists the hotel Majesty Club La Mer Art 5 * has rooms in the central building and in the bungalow. Reviews of housing conditions in the main building indicate some shortcomings, namely outdated furniture, plumbing, poor cleaning. The building of the main building is four-story. There are several elevators in the hall that always work properly. All room categories are with balconies and without them. The balconies are rather modest in size, but there is a table, two chairs and a swimsuit dryer. In the room - a standard set of furniture, including beds, bedside tables, wardrobe, table, chairs. Of the equipment - air conditioning, TV, safe, refrigerator, where every day the hotel staff replenish the supply of water, and on the day of arrival they provide a bottle of mineral water and two bottles of refreshing drinks. The rooms have carpet floor. In the hygiene room there is a bathtub with a shower, a washbasin with a countertop, a toilet bowl, a hairdryer, a wide range of personal hygiene products, which include, in addition to shampoo, soap and shower gel, toothpaste and brushes, as well as razors and shaving foam.

Room categories:

  • Standard with a balcony in the amount of 174 units. Their area is up to 23 "squares", but there are also numbers in which there are only 16 square meters. They are designed for two persons plus one additional berth in the form of a folding bed.
  • Standard without a balcony in the amount of 78 units. The design and equipment of rooms of this category are exactly the same. The only difference is the availability of a balcony and surcharge for it.
  • Junior Suite in the amount of 27 units, 8 of which are not equipped with a balcony. The area of ‚Äã‚Äãthese rooms is up to 29 "squares", and they are designed for 3 people plus one extra bed. Layout - two bedrooms and a small living room area. All suites are angular.

Bungalow rooms

Majesty Club La Mer Art 5 * (Turkey, Kemer) offers its guests 14 two-story bungalows scattered throughout the hotel. These are quite compact buildings, each having only 8 rooms. There are bungalows located in quiet shady corners, where the noise of the hotel‚Äôs active life does not reach, but there are also those located next to a disco or amphitheater. In order for tourists not to have problems with rest, it is necessary to stipulate in advance which conditions of accommodation are preferable to them. The first floor of all bungalows is equipped with a spacious veranda, where there is a table, chairs, a clothes dryer. Room size - up to 23 "squares". They are divided by divisions into zones of the bedroom and living room. The decor and design are based on the principles of minimalism and simplicity. The furniture is not new, but functional. The same can be said about bedding. It is clean, but also serves not the first year. Room equipment is about the same as in the main building. The difference is that there is no carpet on the floor.

Regarding the service in the bungalow Majesty Club La Mer Art 5 * reviews are mostly positive. Maids try, clean well, change towels every day, sheets and pillowcases every three days, regularly replenish personal hygiene products in hygiene rooms. Only in relation to sound insulation and the ability to relax at night, there are claims of those guests who got bungalows near entertainment centers. There, of course, it is very noisy.

Sea and beach

Turkey is famous all over the world for its magnificent sea coasts. Majesty Club La Mer Art 5 * is located right on the beach. The hotel has its own beach and begins where the hotel territory ends, which is a big plus and is liked by absolutely all tourists. On the positive side, there are also enough sunbeds, a lot of space under a canopy from the sun, cleanliness on the beach, the presence of locker rooms and showers, a large and comfortable pier, small pebbles underfoot, which does not cause any inconvenience. However, not everyone likes entry into the water from the shore, and not from the pier, since on the right side there are many large stones in the water that make it difficult to move. There are no stones on the left side, but in the water after the previous pier there were concrete blocks with reinforcement, about which you can cut the feet. If you go down to the sea on the stairs from the pier, then the depth immediately starts on the right side, which is not suitable for children and adults who can‚Äôt swim, and only on the left there is a bottom under your feet. In view of these circumstances, many guests from the Majesty Club La Mer Art 5 * (Kemer) use the beach of the neighboring Sherwood Hotel, dragging sunbeds there. For the convenience of guests on the beach there is a vending machine with soft drinks. The sea in the beach area is warm even in autumn and clean.



Leisure

In order for tourists to fully enjoy their vacation, many entertainment facilities are offered to their attention by the Majesty Club La Mer Art 5 * hotel. The photo above shows one of three outdoor swimming pools for adults, where entertainment events are held every day (competitions, water games, water aerobics). For those tourists who are looking for peace, the hotel in the shade of pine trees and orange trees has a quiet cozy corner with a small but relaxing outdoor swimming pool for adults. The third pool is with water slides. The hotel also has a fourth pool, which is convenient to use in bad weather.

For sports lovers, there are wonderful tennis courts, as well as volleyball, darts, bocce courts, tennis and billiard tables, and a fitness center.

For a relaxing stay, tourists are offered a Turkish steam room and a sauna for free, and for a fee you can get a good massage, take a jacuzzi, visit the beauty parlor and hairdresser.

So that vacationers do not get bored, the hotel has a group of animators. During the day, they hold fun sports, aerobics on the grass, teach some dances, and in the evening arrange interesting shows, for example, Lady Against Gentlemen, Miss and Mr. and others.

A disco is held every day at the hotel, and live music concerts are held once a week.

On the beach, everyone can go diving (lessons are given in the hotel pool), windsurfing (under license) and other water sports. The necessary equipment is available at the hotel's rental office. You can also rent a bike there.

Outside the hotel there is also a lot of interesting things. In Goynuk itself, it‚Äôs hiking in the mountains, to waterfalls, to the famous canyon. A ticket costs $ 2 one way, travel time is 5 minutes.

Service for children

Hotel Majesty Club La Mer Art 5 * shows great concern for children. For them there are cribs and playpens (on request), stroller rental is organized. The restaurant has comfortable highchairs and dishes for baby food (dairy products, cereals, mashed potatoes, soups and some others). On the territory of the hotel, not far from the adult pool, there is a children's playground with a depth of 75 cm. A playground is equipped a little further. In the central building there is a kids club where specialists are involved with children. There kids sculpt, draw, play, watch cartoons. After lunch, interesting contests, mini-disco are held for children, certificates and prizes for active participation are awarded. Children under 12 years old without a bed are not charged.
