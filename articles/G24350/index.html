Check In Rules

Advance booking, or rather the purchase of a voucher, is mandatory for accommodation in the boarding house "Volna". Crimea is a popular place for vacationers, so in order to avoid various misunderstandings and problems it is best to book a room in advance.

To check in, visitors must provide:

  1. The permit.
  2. Passport (Russian or foreign).
  3. Insurance policy.
  4. When checking in with a child, you need to submit documents for him - a certificate of vaccinations and a birth certificate.
  5. If the child did not come with his parents, but with other relatives, they need to present a notarized permission from the father and mother.


For families who come to rest with children, the institution makes a pleasant surprise: children under 3 years old can stay here for free if they do not need food and extra bed. For children older than 3 years and under 6 it will be necessary to pay only for utilities (per day - 160 rubles), but also without the provision of space and food. If a family comes with a teenager and you need extra bed, you can take advantage of the discount and pay only half the cost per child.

Check-in takes place from 13.00, you can leave before 9.00.
