Hotel Policies

In accordance with the internal rules of the Royal Adam and Eve hotel complex (Belek, Turkey), check-in of travelers to their reserved rooms starts at two in the afternoon. However, if you arrive at the hotel earlier than the indicated time, the administration will do everything possible to accommodate you right away. But keep in mind that sometimes this is not possible (in the case when the hotel is full "to the eyeballs"). As a rule, such a situation happens at the height of the tourist season or during symposia and other business events. In this case, you will be asked to leave your bulky items in the luggage storage room and stroll through the picturesque hotel grounds, have a snack, swim in the pool or go to the beach. On the day of departure, your room should be vacated before noon. Then it is necessary to pay for the entire period of stay at the hotel, as well as for the use of additional services. If you previously paid for your room with a travel agency, then at check out you will only have to pay for services not included in the price of the tour, if you resorted to them. As for pets, it is possible in the rooms located in the villas. But prior coordination with the hotel complex is required.
