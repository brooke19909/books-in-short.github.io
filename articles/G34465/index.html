Paid polls: reviews. What is it - real earnings or scam?

The expression "moneymaking" came from the English. moneymaking. It means a kind of activity that involves making money on the Internet. Moneymakers are virtual businessmen. There are a lot of niches in the network where they can earn money. One of the sources of their income is paid surveys.

What does this concept mean?

Often through the Internet paid surveys are conducted. The reviews of those who participated in them indicate that in the end, some compensation was received for the answers given.



Asked questions relate to goods and services, service, etc. Their main focus is to identify the opinion of the client, which allows to assess the level of consumer demand that develops for certain types of goods.

Organizers

Any sane person will certainly want to find out everything about the customer who conducts paid surveys for money. Reviews say that there is a demand for such services. And the organizers of such surveys are special agencies that play the role of intermediaries between certain large companies.





Firms that produce and sell goods, as well as provide various services, want to know the opinion of their products on the ordinary consumer. At the same time, everyone understands that it is very difficult to make such a client work for free. That is why paid polls are conducted. Reviews on the Internet indicate that making money by filling out such questionnaires is quite realistic.

What a moneymaker needs to know

So, you decided to get some income from paid surveys. Reviews previously participated in such events indicate the need for knowledge of some points.

Before answering questions, be it a Russian-language or an English-language site, you will need to fill out a large amount of preliminary information. It includes personal data of a moneymaker, his hobbies, interests, knowledge, etc. How to get more money for paid surveys? Reviews of money-makers suggest that the entered personal information should be as reliable as possible. However, some are tricky at this point. They deliberately give incorrect data, believing that this will significantly expand the range of profiles available to them.

Another significant point in organizing such surveys is checking the completed information for compliance with the topic of interest to the customer. Only after that a decision is made whether to send a questionnaire to a specific moneymaker. For example, a cosmetics company conducts paid surveys. The reviews indicate that it is unlikely that a male moneymaker will be selected to answer her profiles.

It should be borne in mind that it is hardly possible to get a serious amount of money when answering online questions. The reward will only be credited some time after checking the information. At the same time, services have a certain minimum amount for payments.

In other words, earning a moneymaker is not easy. Its main advantage is income generation without any investments.

Virtual Business Stages

How to get money for paid surveys on the Internet? Reviews from those who have already gone this way indicate the need for registration. It is produced on sites engaged in paid surveys, which can be organized both by Russian companies and foreign ones. Unfortunately, fraudsters are often found in this area. There are dishonest paid survey sites. User reviews are advised not to enter a personal phone number and not confirm activation. If these actions are required by the organizer, then this is the first sign that the project is fraudulent.



There are decent paid survey sites. Reviews of moneymakers recommend completely filling out the relevant sections on them. You will need to indicate age, gender, education, hobbies, work, etc. The quality of the information provided will determine the possibility of an invitation to expensive polls.

It is advisable to leave a mention of knowledge of a foreign language. This will allow you to get more profiles that will come not only in Russian. Some of them may have English or German text. Keep in mind that fees for online polls are much higher than for Russian ones.

Should I do this kind of business?

Earn on paid surveys on the Internet, mostly lazy users. These include those who do not want to do serious business, preferring hard money to hard fruitful work. However, such money-makers should know that this is not so easy bread - paid polls. Reviews indicate that the real amount of such earnings can be only one hundred and five hundred rubles a month. And this is in the best case scenario. And if you run into scammers, then your money can never be seen.



Of course, the earnings that users want to get from participating in online surveys cannot be called stable. First of all, the sites-organizers of such profiles do not regularly send invitations. In addition, in order to withdraw funds from a personal account, you will need to accumulate a certain amount on it, which can range from two hundred rubles to a thousand. This will take some time. In addition, the amount of earnings will directly depend on the personal information of the respondent. Of great importance is the time spent participating in paid surveys.

Getting earned funds

Each of the questionnaire sites has the right to independently determine the amount intended for withdrawal. The smaller this value, the faster it can be accumulated. Beginning moneymaker invitations from survey sites rarely come. Therefore, the first real earnings can only be expected after two months. However, it happens that the right amount is collected much faster. This will be facilitated by participation in focus groups or in forums where a reward of five hundred to two thousand rubles is guaranteed. It should be borne in mind that not every survey site has such capabilities.



You can withdraw funds from a personal account located on the Internet to a Webmoney electronic wallet or to a phone account. If you want to have cash, you will need to order it by postal order.

It is important to understand that making money online is real. You can use any of the already proven methods. The question is only how much reward will be received by you, as well as for how long.
