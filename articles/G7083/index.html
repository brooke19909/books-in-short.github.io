Unforgettable holidays in Paris

The most romantic city on Earth is considered Paris. It is here that all the lovers dream of spending their vacation. Its streets, cafes, residents, history - all this is saturated with tenderness and love. One cannot imagine a cultural holiday in Paris without visiting the Louvre Museum - the largest art gallery in the world. Original works by such great masters as Picasso, Gauguin, Monet, Manet, Leonardo da Vinci and many others are presented here. Be sure to look at the entrance to the Louvre Rivoli metro, which is decorated with many colorful glass balls. Not far from the museum itself is the closed gallery Galerie Vivienne, which was opened in the 19th century.

The Eiffel Tower is a symbol of the city and the whole of France. You remember her when it comes to this country. It is impossible to spend your holidays in Paris without ever climbing the Eiffel Tower. The most beautiful view from it to the whole city opens from the third floor. Having gone there on an excursion, you should dine in the wonderful Jules Vern restaurant, located on the second level of the tower. After this amazing walk, you can have a picnic on the Champ de Mars, following the example of Parisians.

Paris abounds in interesting places. This is Montmartre Square, and the Champs Elysees, and the Arc de Triomphe, and the Museum of Arts and Crafts. Every street, every house is filled with history, great meaning and beauty. For example, you can spend the whole day on Montmartre Square, admiring the old Gallet mill, the small church of Saint-Pierre, the famous Sacre-Coeur Cathedral, where a mosaic image of Christ is laid out. Typically, last-minute tours to Paris are bought by students and people whose income does not allow them to purchase a ticket for the full price. Every person would like to visit at least once in his life in Paris. Small financial difficulties should not get in the way of a dream.
