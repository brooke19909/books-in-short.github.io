Rocketbank: reviews of employees and customers

Among the fastest growing segments of the Russian economy is mobile Internet. More and more Russians are starting to use the corresponding online services, are actively acquiring gadgets, installing applications for them. Against the backdrop of this trend, entrepreneurs are creating various startups. Among these are projects based on the concept of using mobile Internet as a communication channel between banks and their customers. At the same time, the functionality of the corresponding application becomes more significant in terms of satisfying the needs of the user than, in fact, the services of a financial institution. An example of such a project is Rocketbank. What are its features?

How did the project come about

The project in question was brought to market by entrepreneurs involved in businesses such as Groupon. Businessmen decided to create a service in Russia that would greatly simplify the work of customers with banks, but at the same time they would not legally be a financial institution. The founders of "Rocketbank" at the same time set themselves the task of launching a service that would be perceived as a bank by customers. In order to solve it, it was necessary, first of all, to find a reliable partner among, in fact, credit and financial organizations.

As the founders of the startup admit in an interview with the media, negotiations were held with about 20 Russian banks, and in most cases partnerships could not be established due to a lack of understanding of the project‚Äôs features by traditional financial institutions. Banks, as noted by top managers of Rocketbank, are used to perceiving a client as a consumer, in particular, of credit services. In turn, the startup, based on its concept, did not imply work in such a segment. Rocketbank did not intend to issue credit cards (see the photo of the corresponding product from the brand below).





The main thing that the founders of the startup tried to get from partners was the consent to connect to the internal corporate network infrastructure so that it was possible to manage customer accounts. Many of the employees of financial organizations were not ready, therefore, to support the initiatives of the Rocketbank project; feedback from security officers of credit institutions regarding the prospects for such cooperation was not the most positive.

However, over time, the founders of the project managed to find a partner. That was the Interkommerts Design Bureau. This organization, with which Rocketbank has an agreement, occupies very decent positions in the ranking among banks. In particular, it is among the top 70 Russian financial institutions in terms of assets. With the joint efforts of partners, the startup was thus launched on the market in 2013.

Target customer group

The founders of Rocketbank identified the target group of project clients as users of mobile Internet. The founders of the startup expect the formation of a significant influx of customers from the IT segment - in particular, people who are used to regular use of online services can be especially active users of the service. Thus, the Rocketbank project began to develop actively in the mobile segment of the Internet. However, the founders of the startup are also considering the possibility of supplementing mobile services with the traditional Internet banking system as well. But at the same time they note that a request for a similar service in the required volume has not yet been formed.





Now let's move on to exploring the capabilities of the application that Rocketbank developed. A review of this solution can be carried out by examining its key functions.

Features of the mobile application ‚ÄúRocketbank‚Äù

Among the most noteworthy features of the Rocketbank application, available to users of mobile gadgets, is cost analytics. Such an application displays clearly. The user‚Äôs expenses are classified into several categories. They are also displayed in the form of a percentage graph, which allows the user to track the most common costs.

A plastic card

One of the aspects that bring Rocketbank's activities closer to traditional credit and financial organizations is the issue of plastic cards. Each client of the service in question receives a plastic card of the VISA payment system. Possessing such means that a person has the right to significant preferences. Among them are discounts in stores, various service programs, personification of services.

Cards issued by Rocketbank (customer reviews regarding this aspect are the most positive) are connected to accounts in which funds placed by the user increase by about 9% per annum. At the same time, the corresponding amounts are not considered a deposit - they can be freely spent or increased due to account replenishment. The interest on cash that is placed on the card is accrued every day, but actually paid once a month. The main condition for increasing the amount on the account is the presence of 30 thousand rubles on it. If on any day of the month there will be less money, then interest will not be accrued until their value reaches 30 thousand rubles. The maximum amount for which interest can be accrued is 300 thousand rubles.





Another opportunity to increase income for a client of the ‚ÄúRocketbank‚Äù system (reviews indicate that this option is also very pleasant for Russians) is to return bonus interest on purchases to the account. So, for example, if a user has connected the ‚ÄúAll Inclusive‚Äù tariff, then he will receive 1.5% of the costs. The tariff "Light" guarantees a return to the account of 1% of purchases.

Refill

There are several ways to replenish the account to which the Rocketbank client card is linked.

First, you can transfer funds from the account of another financial institution by using a plastic card attached to it. In order to carry out this procedure, you need to launch the ‚ÄúRocketbank‚Äù application, then select the ‚ÄúTop-up‚Äù option, and then enter the necessary data about the card from which the withdrawal of funds is supposed. Secondly, you can deposit cash through the terminals of the United Settlement System (or OPC) or the ICD ATM. It can be noted that in the Rocketbank application (customer reviews confirm this) there is a function for displaying the coordinates of the corresponding points. Thirdly, you can replenish the card balance using a conventional bank transfer. This method is not the fastest - money when credited is credited in about 1-2 days.

How to get a Rocketbank card?

So, the user is convinced that the project we are interested in is interesting to him, and the credit and financial organization with which Rocketbank cooperates takes an acceptable position in the rating of banks - the matter is about registering an account in this unusual service. How to solve this problem?

First you need to fill out an application for a plastic card issue on the brand website. After some time - about 1 week - the courier will have to meet with the applicant, who will have a Rocketbank card and an agreement on hand. Support service features and reviews

It can be noted that the support service of the Rocketbank project (feedback on the work of startup employees confirm this) works seven days a week.
