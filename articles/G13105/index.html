How to transfer contacts to iPhone? Simple ways

Finally, you bought yourself a brand new iPhone. Hands are already reaching for removing a wrapper from it ... For a couple of minutes, and your long-awaited phone is ready to use! You have already pumped a bunch of music, movies, themes, downloaded your favorite applications and are ready to forget about your old phone forever . Do not hurry! What about your contacts? To whom and where will you call?





The problem of transferring contacts to iPhone worries a lot of users. They go to different workshops and service centers, complain about misunderstood masters, dwell on the thresholds of forums and curse iPhone developers. How to be Sit and enter contacts one at a time?

Not worth it! Manually interrupting all contacts is a long and timeless process. How to transfer contacts to iPhone quickly and without too much trouble? Making your little brother do it is a great option!

In addition to jokes, transferring contacts to the iPhone is quite simple. You can use different special programs, especially since a fairly large number of them have appeared recently, and there are plenty to choose from. If you are interested in how to transfer contacts to iPhone without any technical troubles, you can offer you a simple elegant option:

  1. We copy all the contacts from the old phone to the old SIM card (in any phone there is such an option in the "Contacts" menu).
  2. We carefully trim the old SIM card and insert it into the iPhone.

That's all the torment is over, all your contacts with you again! Easy and simple, without programs and problems.

In the event that your plans do not include dancing with a tambourine around the SIM card, and if you are not ready to lose the old SIM card with all the contacts, if your hand still trembles - do not be discouraged! Transferring contacts will help you a special program called "iTunes", which will also be able to synchronize them with the programs on your personal computer or laptop.





So, below is a step-by-step instruction on how to transfer contacts to iPhone:

  1. Save contacts from your phone to Outlook. To do this, use the software that was bundled with your phone.
  2. Open the contact list copied from your phone in Outlook.
  3. Connect your iPhone to your computer.
  4. Launch iTunes.
  5. In the program, select the "Information" window of your iPhone.

But before you transfer contacts to iPhone, pay attention to some important points:

  1. Do not use the phone while data is being transmitted to it! Do not turn it off at this moment!
  2. If your contact was signed in the "Name" field on your old mobile phone - everything is in order, the records will be transferred without problems. If you wrote something in the "Last name" field - the signature may change or partially transfer.
  3. If the contact in the old phone had more than two numbers, they have every chance of getting lost. Keep this in mind when migrating!
  4. Email addresses and groups are likely to migrate without problems.

Good luck transferring your contacts to your iPhone! The main thing - do not be nervous, and everything will turn out!
