The basis of earning options

A binary option is a stock exchange investment product that allows you to increase your capital. In countries with well-developed financial markets, options have become auxiliary tools for large companies to function. They hedge risks and implement a more competent and reliable financial policy. An option is a product that reflects the direction of analytics. Wanting to capitalize on the growth of quotes, the trader buys the call option, and waiting for it to fall, he buys put.





Such a transaction is limited in time, that is, it has its own expiration period. Then the position will be closed and profit will be paid if the forecast was correct. An error in forecasting will lead to the loss of the amount by which the option was purchased. Knowing this principle, it becomes clear how to make money on Olympus Trade.
