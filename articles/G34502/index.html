The best hotels of Volgograd in the city center

Volgograd is a city in the south-east of Russia, the regional center of the Volgograd region. Until 1925, it was called Tsaritsyn, and until 1961, Stalingrad. During the defense of Tsaritsyn and the Battle of Stalingrad, the settlement was of strategic importance, for which he was later awarded the title of a hero city. Volgograd is rich in historical and architectural monuments, for which tourists are very fond of it. A warm welcome to travelers will be provided by hotels in Volgograd in the city center.

  • Convenient location relative to the main infrastructure and tourist facilities.

  • 149 comfortable rooms of different categories of comfort. The cost of accommodation is from 2295 rubles per day.

  • Rooms for guests with reduced mobility.

  • Bavarian restaurant on the ground floor of the hotel.

  • A cozy lobby bar for meetings with friends and business negotiations.

  • 5 modern and fully equipped conference rooms for business events.

  • Private secured parking.

  • Free high-speed wireless internet access.
