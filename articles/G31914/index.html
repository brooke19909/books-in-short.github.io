The recreation center "Trinity", Mytishchi district. Description, reviews

Fans of a quiet country holiday awaits a paradise in the suburbs - recreation center "Troitskoye". The health resort is surrounded by a beautiful forest, nearby is the picturesque Klyazma reservoir. The hostel invites guests all year round.

Description

Muscovites come to the Troitskoye recreation center on the weekend with pleasure. Here you can be alone with nature in comfortable conditions, not inferior to urban ones. The hotel complex on the territory of the camp site can simultaneously accommodate 230 tourists. Several sandy beaches are equipped on the shore of the reservoir. Meals are organized in the Argo restaurant, which features Georgian and European cuisines. In addition, vacationers have the opportunity to enjoy original dishes that combine various culinary traditions.

In summer, you can also have a meal at several cafes located on the beach. Horseback riding enthusiasts come here for the occupation of which all conditions are created in the local equestrian center. For a walk on a pond, you can rent a boat or boat. Game sports fans can compete in sports fields. Along the shore there are gazebos with barbecue facilities. For the organization and holding of corporate events and family celebrations, banquet rooms are equipped. Children will enjoy visiting the zoo. Vacationers can leave personal transport in a large guarded parking lot. The recreation center is located at the address: Troitskoye village, Mytishchi district, Moscow region.

Accommodation in a hotel complex

In a hotel complex located on the territory of the health resort, three hotels offer their services for tourists:

  • Hotel Argo is currently closed for renovation.
  • The Prichal Hotel is represented by one-room economy class rooms - the cost is from 2,000 to 2,500 rubles per day.
  • At the Troitskoye recreation center in the Moscow region, you can live in the Bagration hotel-ship. Here you will also find economy class rooms - the cost is from 1,700 to 3,000 rubles per day.

Each room is equipped with a double bed, a TV, a bathroom with a shower, toiletries.

Accommodation in cottages

In the cottage village at the recreation center in the village of Troitskoye, 11 houses are offered for accommodation:

  • in cottages designed for 10 main beds and two additional ones, the cost of living is 17,500 rubles from Monday to Thursday and 25,000 rubles from Friday to Sunday;
  • in cottages designed for 4 main places and two additional, the cost of living is 10 500 rubles from Monday to Thursday and 15 000 rubles from Friday to Sunday.

All houses are equipped with a TV, fridge, bathroom with shower, toiletries. Each has access to wireless internet.

Holiday Village "Troitskoe": additional services

To stay at the camp site as rich and diverse as possible, guests are provided with various services and entertainment:

  • In the equestrian center, experienced instructors will teach you how to ride.
  • It offers guests a platform for playing football, tennis, beach volleyball, golf, paintball.
  • Equipped with three sandy beaches with gentle descents into the water and different levels of depth.
  • In cozy pavilions on the banks of the Klyazma reservoir, everything is provided for picnics.
  • In banquet rooms corporate events, family celebrations are held. If you need to organize a business meeting or conference, you will be provided with additional multimedia equipment.
  • Experienced screenwriters, directors and directors will help you organize your wedding at the Troitskoye recreation center in Mytishchi district.
  • Guests can enjoy delicious meals at the Argo Restaurant.
  • In the cottage village you can rent a bath, which provides a relaxation room and billiards.
  • Here, tourists have the opportunity to rent a boat or boat.
  • The camp site has its own zoo, where children are happy to communicate with animals.

Pets' corner

  • The two-humped camel Argo, who once lived in Kazakhstan, was very fond of snowy winters.
  • In the male Lama Stepan, the former owner changed his place of residence, and the animal found a new haven at the camp site.

Holiday Village "Troitskoe": reviews of tourists

The guests of the camp site share in their reviews both positive impressions about the rest in the health resort and comments.

  • Tourists who come here from Moscow for one day, first of all, note the presence of a large convenient parking, in which there is enough space for everyone.
  • Guests enjoy spacious, well-equipped beaches with convenient access to the water. There is a special fenced beach for kids.
  • Around the beautiful nature, you can also admire the passing yachts and motor ships.
  • The restaurant is very tasty, there is a cafe. If you want to cook kebabs, gazebos with barbecue facilities are for rent.
  • Vacationers made comments about the fact that in the summer the animals are very hot and their cages are cramped.
  • Tourists know that car entry is paid, but some guests complain that they additionally demanded 50 rubles per person, including a small child.
