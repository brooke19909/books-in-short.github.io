Hotel Melpo Antia 4 * (Cyprus / Ayia Napa): photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Melpo Antia 4 * is a fairly famous hotel. And he became famous thanks to the fact that he provides excellent rooms and services at very modest prices for a four-star complex. That is why the apartments in the hotel are never empty - tourists always come here, and there are many reasons for this.





Resort Attractions

Ayia Napa is a resort that is never empty. And this is not only because there are magnificent beaches, a unique climate and perfect nature. Here, many tourists are offered a variety of excursions and entertainment. Why just fly by helicopter to the picturesque Cape Greco or the mysterious ghost town of Famagusta. And, of course, the world-famous city of Paphos, which is definitely worth a visit, being in these places on vacation. It is worth noting that in Cyprus there is a huge number of diverse hotel complexes. It is difficult for many potential tourists to make a choice - they simply get lost in this variety. But Melpo Antia 4 * will be a great option, especially for those people who like to live on the all-inclusive service, appreciate the high level of service and affordable prices. Despite the fact that the complex is still quite young, it quickly became popular and won the recognition of many tourists. Few hotels can boast such advantages that Melpo Antia 4 * has. And they should be discussed in more detail.





Location

This hotel is located in the heart of Ayia Napa. A beautiful building, made in a stylish modern design, is immediately evident. By the way, this impression is not deceptive, since inside everything is decorated with taste. Only a few minutes walk separates it from nightlife entertainment, which include clubs, restaurants and various cafes. Also nearby is the port. This is a great place to have fun. It must also be said that the hotel is close to the sea (second coastline). Seven minutes of a calm step - and you can be on the beach. The distance from the airport is a little over forty kilometers. The hotel can be reached in 40 minutes. In general, if the location of the complex is important for a person who plans to come to Cyprus, then you should make a choice in favor of Melpo Antia Suites 4 *.

Rest on the territory

In addition to the sea, there are plenty of other entertainments and ways to relax. So, for example, by the pool, located on the territory of the hotel, there is a spacious area where guests can sunbathe and enjoy the calm atmosphere. There is also a hot tub, which will definitely appeal to people who want to relax with their whole body. It should also be noted that a variety of theme nights and shows are regularly held. People who simply wish to retire can spend time in the picturesque garden, enjoying the shade of the trees.





Customized services

Service is an important aspect when it comes to choosing a hotel. First of all, it is worth noting that throughout the Melpo Antia 4 * free Wi-Fi is provided, as well as parking. No need to book a place in advance - it will be provided to the guest upon arrival. At the registration desk, there is a record of arriving and leaving around the clock, which allows you to be independent of time. There is also a currency exchange office on the territory, which can also come in handy. The luggage of guests can be placed in a special left-luggage office. I must say that the hotel has everything you need for a comfortable stay - it's a laundry, shops, several rooms (for banquets and conferences), and much more. The service at Melpo Antia Suites Apts 4 * is really high-quality - they make sure that guests are comfortable.





Rest and leisure

If guests have a desire to relax and have fun, then there will be no problems with this. In addition to all of the above, guests are offered a sauna, table tennis, a fitness center equipped with all the necessary fitness equipment. In fact, guests do not stay at the hotel, because there are a huge number of attractions and various excursions around that attract tourists. If you want something else, then next to the hotel there are lots of clubs and bars where you can have a good time. By the way, while staying at this resort, you can not help but pay attention to local taverns, which serve very tasty and varied treats.

Restaurants

In Melpo Antia Hotel Suites 4 * there are several institutions where you can try delicious dishes and enjoy a pleasant atmosphere. There are two restaurants - one of them works according to the "buffet" system, and the other offers guests a menu. There are also two bars where guests can try high-quality soft drinks and strong drinks and order any cocktail. It is worth noting the attention of the bakery and grill bar, which are also located in the complex. In general, Greek cuisine is famous for its originality, so you should try those dishes that are offered to guests of the island in local establishments. By the way, if guests do not want to eat in restaurants, then this is not a problem. After all, each room has a separate kitchenette, where each guest can cook their own breakfast and enjoy it while sitting on their balcony.





Residence

Separate attention should be noted rooms that have Melpo Antia Suites 4 *. Potential guests are provided with 130 rooms to choose from, among which there are standard, as well as studio apartments. Radical differences are here except in the area. For standard rooms, it ranges from 30 to 42 square meters. m, while the studio - about 25 square meters. m. By the way, it is worth noting that the apartments of the first type have, in addition to the room, a living room. Inside, there is everything you need for life - from the refrigerator to the air conditioning. It includes a fully equipped kitchen, an individual safe, a hairdryer, a large TV with cable TV, a spacious bathroom and much more. By the way, each room has a balcony, which is also a definite plus, because the hotel windows offer excellent views of the surroundings.





Accommodation cost

It was previously said that the hotel sets very affordable prices for accommodation in their rooms. In fact, it is. For example, a luxury studio for two will cost less than 70 thousand rubles (for one week of stay). It is worth noting that breakfast and all taxes are also included in this price. Guests are provided with all the amenities mentioned earlier. If you want to book the same number, but for three, the amount will be 85 thousand rubles, which is even cheaper. And one of the most advantageous offers of this hotel is a luxury apartment for four for 100 thousand rubles (25,000 each for a week). For a four-star hotel, these are really low prices. That is why it is so popular with visitors. Therefore, if a decision is made to stay at Melpo Antia 4 * (Cyprus), you should pre-worry about booking a room - otherwise it may simply not remain at the time of arrival. Speaking about the features of accommodation, it is worth noting the attention of another nuance. If guests wish to come with their children, an extra bed can be added to the room. By the way, if the child is not two years old, then he can live completely free. By the way, coming to this hotel to rest with children is a great idea. There is everything that is necessary for young guests - a children's playground, a separate pool and teachers who can look after them.
