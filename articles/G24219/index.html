High-quality Lenovo phones: reviews, technical specifications, and other specifications

Relatively recently, Lenovo phones appeared on the domestic mobile communications market . Reviews, technical specifications and other important information will be given in the context of the most popular models: the A390, A820 and Vibe X. The first model is a budget- class device based on a 2-core processor. A820 represents the average price range. It already uses a 4-core CPU, and its performance level is an order of magnitude higher. But the last device is a premium device. He can solve all problems without exception today.





Model A390

A390, A376, A369i and A630 are the most popular Lenovo phones in the budget price segment today. User reviews speak in favor of the first of them. A376 is built on the basis of a less productive CPU with a comparable cost of the gadget. In A369i, all other things being equal, a weaker camera is installed (3.2 megapixels versus 5 megapixels). But the A630 is more expensive. Therefore, the most optimal choice is the A390. It comes in 2 versions: with the T index (works in CDMA networks) and without it (oriented to the GSM standard). Its computing heart is 6577 from MTK. It consists of 2 revision cores A9, operating at a frequency of 1 GHz. The graphics subsystem is implemented using the PowerXR SGX 531. The RAM in this device is 512 MB, and the internal memory is 4 GB. There is also an expansion slot for installing microSD-cards. The 1500 mAh battery provides up to 3 days of battery life. All of the above, allows you to say with confidence that this is a great entry-level smartphone.

Model A820

A800, A820, S750 and S720 are the main Lenovo phones in the average price range. Reviews and relatively equal cost confirm this. But there is only one leader - A820. Other things being equal, its computing part and the memory subsystem are better. All models except A820 are 2-core. But CPU 6589 from the same MTK company is installed in it. He has on board 4 revision A7 cores operating at a frequency of 1.2 GHz. There is also more RAM (1 GB vs 512 MB). But the built-in as much - 4 GB. It is also possible to increase it by installing a memory card up to 32 GB in size. The 2000 mAh battery with a mediocre load allows you to do without recharging up to 2 days. As a result, we can say that this is a great device in the middle price segment. Its hardware and software resources are enough to solve most everyday tasks.





Vibe x

K900, S939 and Vibe X are the most expensive Lenovo phones. Reviews nevertheless note that their technical parameters correspond to the price. The first device is based on a chip from Intel, and there may be problems with launching applications. In turn, the S939 has a diagonal of 5.5 inches - not everyone is comfortable with such a large device. By this criterion, it is closer to tablets than to smartphones. But Vibe X (aka S960) is devoid of each of these shortcomings. It has a diagonal of 5 inches, and the 6589W processor is based on the ARM architecture. The composition of this CPU includes 4 A7 cores operating at a frequency of 1.5 GHz. The RAM in it is 2 GB, and built-in 16 GB. That is, it can do without a memory card. The Lenovo Cell Phone is equipped with a 2050 mAh battery. It is enough for 2 days of battery life. Vibe X is a great solution for those who want maximum performance at a reasonable price.





Finally

As part of this review, Lenovo A390, A820 and Vibe X mobile phones were considered. The first of them is a budget device with a basic set of functions. It is perfect for undemanding users. A820 is a representative of the middle price range. The level of its performance is similar. The device is enough for most everyday tasks. But if you need maximum processing power, then better than Vibe X, it's hard to find something. And given the cost, he simply has no competitors.
