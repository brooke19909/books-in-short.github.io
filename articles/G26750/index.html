It is no secret that Egypt, in particular Sharm el-Sheikh, is a center of world tourism. Where is the hotel situated?





As already mentioned, the Gardenia Plaza Resort 4 hotel complex is located in the large and lively tourist city of Sharm El Sheikh, namely in the area called Sharks Bay. The distance to the historic city center is approximately 12 kilometers - this distance can be covered by taxi or use public transport. By the way, the international airport is located very close - about 4 kilometers from the hotel building. Moreover, the hotel, as well as a travel agency, provides tourists with a transfer - you should not worry about this.

Regardless of which category you choose, you can count on a spacious, clean room decorated in an elegant style. It has the necessary amount of furniture, and some rooms also have kitchen utensils. What kind of food is offered to guests?





Naturally, for many tourists, nutrition is an important issue. Like almost all hotels in Egypt, the Gardenia Resort & Aqua Park (ex Gardenia Plaza Resort) hotel operates on an all inclusive system. Three times a day, tourists are invited to visit the main restaurant. Buffets are organized here, where you can choose your favorite dishes. The restaurant's menu is quite diverse - there are cold and hot snacks, fish and meat dishes, as well as fresh vegetables, salads, fruits, delicious oriental desserts.

The hotel also has several bars - by the pool and in the lobby, as well as on the beach. Here, guests of the Gardenia Plaza Resort Aqua Park can get light snacks, cooling drinks (soda, fruit cocktails), as well as Egyptian-made alcohol (beer, wine, etc.). For a relatively affordable price, you can dine at the seafood restaurant, which serves wonderful seafood dishes. Naturally, if necessary, you can eat in any of the institutions of the city, which, by the way, is simply a huge number.

Beach and water recreation for tourists





The Gardenia Plaza Resort hotel complex is located on the second beach line - the distance to the sea is about 1500 meters. It is easy to overcome on foot, as a convenient road leads to the beach. In addition, every half hour to the shore there is a hotel bus, which guests can use for free.

According to reviews, the beach here is well-groomed, with clean soft sand. Sunset at sea is also sandy, although tourists are advised to use special shoes because of the coral reef. On the shore there is a sufficient number of sun loungers and straw sun umbrellas. Here you can get a beach towel.

Naturally, travelers who prefer a more active vacation will not be bored either. You can go boating, water skiing, boating and catamarans. The underwater world is really interesting here - you can swim with a mask or make a real scuba dive. Nearby is a diving school where you can get a couple of lessons from professionals or rent the necessary equipment.

Are there any conditions for children's relaxation?





Today, many tourists travel as a family. Therefore, an important point is the presence or absence of the necessary amenities at the hotel. Gardenia Plaza Resort is suitable for families with children.
