Pioneer, controller: specifications, descriptions, comparison and reviews

The Pioneer company is quite large. According to statistics, goods of its own production are stubbornly fighting with each other for first place in the market. One of the well-known production positions of this company can be called DJ accessories that help create a club atmosphere.

It should be noted that the market in which studio equipment is offered is very narrow, and Pioneer has long been stable in it. The specifics of the organization are clear. New manufacturers are not able to compete with those already held. Quality and price must strictly correspond to each other. Really good firms can be counted on the fingers of one hand.

DDJ-Wego

A compact device that is equipped with a very decent set of functions. Most suitable for those who love electronic music. For people who want to win DJ ratings, the Pioneer Wego controller is not suitable. The device will become the "king" of home parties, it is able to give unforgettable and pleasant moments. The controller works, like many other devices of the company. You need to connect it to a laptop. Thanks to the manufacturer, Pioneer (controller) allows you to easily manage tracks, melodies, flexibly changing them. This is done using the downloaded software, which is called Virtual DJ.

It should be noted that in each mode the colors are different. That is, any experienced DJ who has been dealing with the Wego model for a long time will be able to understand at a glance how his device works at the moment.



Office DDJ-Wego

Most of the buttons are located on the front of the controller. An exception was the standard on / off key. All buttons are close enough to each other, to some extent even flattened. This is to save space and materials.

In general, judging by the reviews, the control and ergonomics of this device are, as always, at the highest level. This is exactly what was expected from the manufacturer Pioneer. The controller is compact, it seems to some that the fader knobs are too small, but this is not so. The latter effect occurs only in those who are accustomed to full-size. All knobs and buttons work quickly. They do not need to be pressed hard; they are squeezed smoothly and without sharpness. For those who want to deal with scratching, the Pioneer DDJ-Wego controller is also suitable. The crossfader is quite light and functions quite quickly.

A great impression for all users appears after working with rotators. It is recommended that you carefully study them, since they often fail the very first. This is due to the fact that rotators are used most often.
