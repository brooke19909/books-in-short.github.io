Hotel Golden Age Bodrum 4 *, Turkey, Bodrum: photos, reviews

Turkish hotels have long had no competitors among overseas hotel complexes. If there is a choice, our compatriots usually stop after all on a trip to Turkey. This country far beyond its borders is famous for its hospitality, magnificent resort areas, clear sea and luxurious hotels that meet any needs of tourists. It is noteworthy that the abundance of resorts allows travelers to see the country in all its glory. After all, Turkey is an extraordinary place that can present many incredible adventures. In recent years, the resort of Bodrum has been very popular among Russians. Most of the hotel complexes located here have four and five stars. But it is known that it is these hotels that our compatriots usually choose. In our article you will learn about the Golden Age Bodrum 4 * hotel. It is located in a very picturesque place with a magnificent beach, considered one of the most beautiful in the country.





Useful information about the hotel complex

First of all, I want to say that the reviews about Golden Age Bodrum 4 * left by our compatriots contain an abundance of admiring comments about the time spent here. And this is quite natural, given that the hotel has four stars and is fully consistent with its high status.

The hotel complex is represented by the main building and a group of cottages. Bungalows were erected in the second half of the nineties of the last century. The main building belongs to newer buildings; it was built only fourteen years ago. Despite the fact that the hotel was always proud of the excellent condition of its number of rooms, it was renovated five years ago. As a result, guests are accommodated in magnificent rooms and can enjoy a pleasant and unobtrusive design.

A few words about the location of the hotel complex

Hotel Golden Age Bodrum 4 * refers to the suburban complexes. However, it is located in close proximity to tourist centers and large villages. Advertising directories indicate that the location of Golden Age Bodrum 4 * is Bodrum. However, in reality, there is a distance of twenty kilometers between the hotel and the resort center.

The closest to the hotel complex is the village of Yalikavak. It is necessary to get to it about two kilometers.

The hotel is located on the first coastline. The rooms offer magnificent views of the picturesque Tilkigik Bay. The water on its beaches is considered the cleanest. This is confirmed by numerous reviews of Golden Age Bodrum 4 * (Bodrum).





Description of the beach area

The hotel complex has its own beach with a small yellowish sand. It stretches for almost three hundred meters, which allows you to stay on the beach for a large number of vacationers at the same time.

There are cabanas and showers on the coast. In addition, there are sun loungers on the beach, you can use them for free. Towels for sun loungers are secured on site.

It is noteworthy that not so long ago the beach was awarded the Blue Flag. It is international and proves the fact that the water in this part of the coast is extremely clean.





Infrastructure

Photo Golden Age Bodrum Hotel 4 *, posted on the Internet, can not convey the beauty that will surround vacationers throughout the holiday. On the territory of the hotel complex there are two outdoor pools, near which there are many sun loungers. Beach towels are available here.

In the off-season, when the weather does not allow swimming in the fresh air, the indoor pool begins its work. Keep in mind that the water in it does not heat up.

It features three restaurants and five bars. One restaurant is considered the main, the other two operate on an a la carte system.

In Golden Age Bodrum 4 * there are three conference rooms. Each of them is designed for a certain number of guests and is well equipped with the necessary equipment for events. If necessary, guests can send a fax from here, but this service is paid extra.

There is also a car rental service in the reception area, however, tourists rarely rent vehicles in Bodrum. For those who still prefer to move around the resort independently and freely, free parking is equipped. There is enough space here even if all the guests will have their own transport.

In Golden Age Bodrum 4 * there is an excellent beauty salon where you can quickly put yourself in order and return home noticeably refreshed. It has several halls that provide spa treatments. Here you can radically change your image.

If you do not want to spend the whole day searching for souvenirs for friends in Bodrum, then keep in mind that you can buy everything you need in a store located directly in the hotel complex.

Free services

Service at Golden Age Bodrum Hotel 4 * is at a high level, so guests have the opportunity to use a large number of free services. For example, throughout the territory it catches Wi-Fi perfectly. This is confirmed by many guests who freely communicate with their home via the Internet at any time convenient for them.

The rooms are cleaned daily, and guests say that the maids do their job well and without a tip. The linen in the rooms is changed three times a week.

Guests have the opportunity to use the safe in the room and at the reception for free. This greatly facilitates the solution of the problem with the preservation of valuables and money.

Paid services

Laundry and dry cleaning services are available for a small surcharge. This service is very popular among tourists who want to look good and not spend their own precious time allotted for the rest.

If desired, guests can arrange a transfer from Golden Age Bodrum Hotel 4 * to anywhere in the resort. This service is quite in demand, especially when vacationers dream of fast delivery, for example, to the airport. Often, even at the stage of booking rooms, tourists indicate that they need an individual transfer from the airport. This significantly reduces travel time. After all, the hotel is located forty kilometers from the main air gate of Turkey.

Paid services include massage and water sports. Keep in mind that during a disco, the entrance to which is accessible to absolutely everyone, drinks and snacks are paid on the menu.

If you need a nanny for the baby, then her services will also have to be paid separately.





Entertainment at the hotel complex

In Golden Age Bodrum Hotel 4 * there are five water slides. They work according to the established schedule, and it is here that small guests of the hotel complex most often gather.

Very popular in the Golden Age Bodrum Hotel 4 * hotel is a sauna and jacuzzi. Great professionals work here who will make you completely relax and forget about all the problems.

The hotel has a large selection of sports games. For example, the hotel has its own golf course. Here you can try yourself in the game of aristocrats, as it is often called. In addition, there are volleyball and basketball courts. In the main building there are halls for playing table tennis and billiards.

A lot of good reviews were left about the animation team. They work with great dedication, but nevertheless do not become intrusive. This is very much appreciated by many vacationers who want to spend time separately from the noisy crowd.

Fun for kids

Families with children often come to the Golden Age Bodrum 4 * hotel; therefore, it is important for them what the child will do during their vacation. Kids spend most of their time in two pools. They were built specifically for children, so they have a small depth.

If your child prefers to play in the sandbox and climb various constructions, then he will be happy with the playground. It is replete with interesting buildings, so children are happy to stay here even without parents.

A child from four to twelve years old can be taken to a kids club. The animators working here are highly qualified and have developed an extensive program for children of all ages. Therefore, small guests will definitely not be bored here.





Dining at the hotel

The hotel complex operates on an all-inclusive basis. In the main restaurant, the buffet concept is included in the food concept. According to this scheme, breakfast, lunch and dinner are served. For lovers of sleep, there is such a thing as "brunch." During the day, vacationers can try a variety of snacks, locally-made drinks and sweets.

A la carte restaurants are open by appointment only. Once a vacation, tourists can dine in one of them for free. The hotel bars are very well located in the hotel complex. They all start working at ten in the morning.





Number of rooms

The apartments include a safe and minibar. Water is replenished daily, and carbonated drinks are put into the bar only upon arrival.

Guests can use individual air conditioning, satellite TV and a private telephone. Each room has a balcony or terrace. Usually light furniture and a dryer for wet clothes are installed here.
