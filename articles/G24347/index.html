Description

Dessole Ruspina Hotel is a hotel located between two famous Tunisian resort towns - Monastir and Sousse. It was here that director George Lucas filmed a series of episodes about Indiana Jones and several episodes from Star Wars.

Dessole Ruspina Hotel 4 * (Tunisia) was built in 1970. The last major renovation was carried out in 2012. Since January 1, 2013, he has been a member of the Dessole Resorts & Hotels hotel chain.

This is one of the first hotels in the region. It is positioned as a place for a quiet measured rest, despite the fact that the city of Monastir, only fifteen kilometers distant, has a very developed entertainment infrastructure.



The airport is located only three kilometers away, which is especially convenient for tourists vacationing with young children. There are regular charter flights from Moscow to Monastir.
