Beach vacation

A big plus of Andaman Sea View, according to tourists, is its location near Karon Beach. This beach is considered the best in Phuket. You can reach it in just three minutes. However, according to our compatriots, it is necessary to cross a busy road along the way. And despite the fact that you can cross the crosswalk, you must do this very carefully, as local drivers are unlikely to stop to let you through. This fact must be borne in mind.





As for the beach itself, it is large, sandy, quite wide. A big plus is the presence of trees along it, creating a shadow. Tourists can sit right on the sand on the mats brought with them. By the way, beach towels are offered to guests of Andaman Sea View Hotel 4 * free of charge. You can even change them several times a day.

Entertainment

According to our compatriots, Andaman Sea View is a calm hotel, more focused on family guests than on cheerful and noisy youth. So you will not find any special entertainments here. There is also a bar where you can enjoy a drink.

According to travelers, in the vicinity of the hotel there are a lot of cafes, restaurants, massage parlors, shops, souvenir shops, tour desks, rental cars, etc. Many tourists prefer to rent a scooter and explore the island on their own, visiting different beaches, settlements and attractions.
