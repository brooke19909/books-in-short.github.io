Hotel Checkin Pineda 3 * (Spain, Costa del Maresme): photos and reviews of tourists

Hotel Checkin Pineda 3 * is positioning itself as an institution for family vacations. Here you can fully enjoy the beauty of Spain and quality service.





a brief description of

The history of this hotel began back in 1977. Even then, he won the love of travelers from all over the world. Thanks to the complete reconstruction carried out in 2015, the establishment looks quite modern and provides guests with all the necessary amenities. The hotel is an elegant five-story building, which includes 156 comfortable apartments.
