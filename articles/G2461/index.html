The most amazing places on the planet

Something was created by Mother Nature, and something was created by ancient people. All this causes incredible interest. But far from all the questions we get answers. Let's make our own small review, which will include only the most amazing places on the planet that attract the eye and make you think about their origin.

Sphinx in Giza

We begin our story with the Sphinx in Giza. It may seem incredible to many that this building is made of a monolithic part of the rock. This giant reaches 60 feet in height, 20 in width, and 240 in length. It is hard to imagine how many people or what technology the Egyptians used to create such a masterpiece. Today, almost all scientists agreed that the Sphinx plays a purely symbolic role. Statues were mainly installed near temples, tombs, as well as pyramids. The Sphinx is a vivid example of this; it is installed near the pyramid of Hafra. According to most researchers, the face of this particular person is shown by a statue. Many are still puzzling over who and when such a giant was erected. Many call the figure in 2500 years BC. e. But the biggest mystery remains how the Sphinx was built. Even in our time it will be very difficult to make such a statue.

Great Barrier Reef

If there are amazing places on the planet whose photos are simply mesmerizing, then this is the BBR - the largest coral reef formation on the whole planet Earth. Along the tropical coast of Australia is this huge system of living organisms. The length of the reef is almost 3,000 kilometers. There are approximately 900 islands, as well as 3 thousand different reefs. This is one of the most beautiful natural wonders that humanity has ever known. The cleanest Coral Sea makes this place a real paradise for scuba divers and divers. The incredible fabulous underwater world attracts a huge number of tourists from all over the world. It is noteworthy that the water here is always the same temperature. When it becomes at least 1-2 degrees colder or warmer, the reefs begin to die.

Sak-Aktun - an incredible underground river

Mexico has the longest underground river in the world on the Yucatan Peninsula . Its total length is 20,000 kilometers. Currently, no more than 2-3% of the underground world of this place has been studied. According to scientists, he is at least 65 million years old. A system of underground rivers formed, most likely due to the fall of a large meteorite, the crater from which eventually filled with water. Tourists from all over the world come here to see the most amazing places on the planet. Aesthetes find in Sak-Aktun those unique shots that cannot be obtained anywhere else, and extreme divers can enjoy all the beauty of this place. What is unique is that, regardless of the time of year, the water temperature here is 23-24 degrees Celsius. According to many tourists, here under water are the most amazing places on the planet, photos of which everyone wants to make.

Mount Ararat and its secrets

This amazing place is located in Armenia. Not far from each other, at a distance of 11 kilometers, there are two mountains: Big and Small Ararat. The first rises 5165 meters above sea level, the second - at 3 927. Until now, scientists do not know when the last volcanic massif erupted, there are suggestions that this was back in the 3rd millennium BC. But what causes the greatest surprise and attracts researchers from all over the world is the legend that Noah's Ark is located on Great Ararat. Back in the 15th century, Marco Polo, who saw the most diverse amazing places on the planet, with a description of Ararat and Noah's ark returned home. According to his records, it is there, under the thick snows, that the remains of the ark are located. The lieutenant of the Russian army back in 1916 equipped an expedition to the top of Ararat. There he was discovered. He was photographed in parts, measured, but as a result of the revolution, all the data was lost.
