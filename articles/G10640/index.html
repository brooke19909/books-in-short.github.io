The tablet does not turn on? What to do?

No matter how perfect the technique may be, sometimes there are times when its work, to put it mildly, cannot be called clear and harmonious. For example, there are situations when, after a short time after acquiring a tablet, it suddenly stops turning on. However, you understand that the device did not have any mechanical damage. Then what is the reason that the tablet does not turn on?

Calm and only calm

Try to understand this problem yourself without emotion. As a rule, this is the most rational solution in such situations. Usually not very correct operation of this type of device is due to several reasons. Firstly, the operating system may not work properly. In this case, you must press the on / off button for 15 seconds. Better to take a paper clip and run Reset. After these simple manipulations, you can turn on your favorite gadget again. What is the second reason why the tablet does not turn on? These are nutrition problems. In this case, you should check out a number of key points. First of all, you need to connect the charger to the gadget and activate it. If the device could not be started, this procedure should be performed again in about 10 minutes.





Do not repair yourself!

It happens that Android tablets behave differently. For example, some users encounter a problem when the video adapter does not work correctly, that is, when the gadget starts up in normal mode, but the display does not work. This is often caused by a banal overheating of the screen. If this problem repeats more often, then feel free to take the tablet to a service center. Take note that if the tablet does not turn on, you should not immediately sound the alarm. However, if you, having calmed down and sorted out the situation, tried several times to start the device and it did not respond, then seek help from specialists. And by the way, do not try to disassemble and repair the gadget yourself. This can lead to even greater damage. This is especially true for situations when the Chinese tablet does not turn on. In this case, immediately contact the service center and demand the fulfillment of warranty service conditions. If during the purchase all the documents were completed correctly and you followed the operating conditions of the device, then there will be no problems.
