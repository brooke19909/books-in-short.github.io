Hotel Issara Resort 3 *, Phuket: overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

One of the most famous and, as a result, congested beaches of Thailand is Patong. Several kilometers of the coast with white sand and a warm sea attract tourists not only for its beauty, but also for its proximity to the heart of Phuket night: many nightclubs and bars, restaurants and entertainment for every taste. To make the rest comfortable and memorable, you should pay more attention to the choice of housing in the area.





One of the main factors is the proximity to the sea and infrastructure, markets and attractions. They recommend paying attention to the Issara Resort 3 * hotel, which harmoniously combines a convenient location, quality of service and price.

Location

The hotel is located in the southern part of Patong City. A landmark for tourists is the Thai Farmers Bank building located opposite. You can cover the distance from the airport in 40 minutes, and to the spacious beach - about five minutes of a leisurely walk. Located in the heart of the area, the hotel offers its guests many entertainment options. Just 500 meters away is the famous Phuket Simon Cabaret, where you can enjoy an exotic evening show, the large Jungceylon shopping center, market and Bangla Road, known worldwide for its nightclubs and bars.

The design is bright and interesting, the color scheme is quite logical for a seaside resort: shades of the sea, sand and sun.

The interior is made in a simple style, on the floor ceramic tiles, wooden furniture. Rooms are regularly cleaned and linen changed. The windows of the accommodation offer a picturesque view of the mountains or the bustling street, but the noise of the guests will not bother.

Meals: bars and restaurants

Resort Hotel Issara Resort 3 * (Thailand, Phuket, Patong) has three stars, and catering to its guests offers only BB (breakfast). A table is served at a nearby restaurant, and alcoholic and cold drinks are available at the bar throughout the day. This does not upset the hotel guests at all, because Thailand is a country where you need to travel and try something new and unusual, including amazing food that excites the taste of inexperienced travelers, and makes experienced travelers return to these places again and again. At night, Patong streets are filled with makeshift markets, not only with souvenirs and things, but also with food prepared in front of you with fresh seafood.





Near the hotel there are several cafes and a restaurant, which have proved themselves to be the best among tourists not only from Russia but also the whole world. The first, Home Dining Cafe & Lounge, is located between the Blue Ocean Hotel and the popular 7 Eleven store, which is just five minutes walk from the southern part of Patong Beach. A small cozy restaurant offers dishes not only Thai cuisine, but also European. A large selection of seafood and fish, everything is cooked literally before your eyes.

The second cafe, You & Me, is also nearby, and although it has a not too luxurious interior (plastic chairs and wooden tables), it is still very popular due to its delicious food, fast service and low prices.

In addition, you can visit Lemongrass Restaurant & Bar in the same hotel near Issara Resort 3 *. Prices are higher, but the food is always tasty and fresh.

Pool and entertainment

A distinctive and very interesting feature of the hotel is its swimming pool, which is located on the roof, adjacent to it is a spacious terrace. Sun beds and umbrellas are provided free of charge, you can sunbathe and swim, while enjoying a good panoramic view.





For entertainment, the hotel offers its guests billiards, a small fitness center and a games room for children. If desired, and for a fee, you can undergo a massage course.

Sea and beach

The hotel is well located in Patong Beach - the most crowded, noisy and largest beach on the island. It is famous for its bars and restaurants, night clubs. To get to it from Issara Resort 3 * you can literally 15 minutes on foot (600 m). The beach impresses with its beauty, hot white sand and clear sea. But do not look for solitude here, it always reigns fun and pleasant bustle. Sun loungers and umbrellas are available for a fee. It is worth noting that prices for services and products, entertainment in Patong are higher compared to other resort areas, however, you can find very budget options.





Hotel Issara Resort 3 * (Phuket): location reviews

As tourists who visited this hotel noted in their reviews, one of its main advantages is its location. If you are a supporter of beach holidays and can not live without the sea, then it is only a 10-15 minute walk. For those who prefer a pool, there is a nice roof terrace and hot sun for even tanning. Fans of entertainment will have a place to take a walk, nearby there is everything: restaurants, night clubs and bars, discos. With the onset of dusk, numerous souvenir shops, mobile markets open, food carts and much more appear on the streets.

Of the minuses - the complete absence of its own territory at the hotel: only rooms and space on the roof. Many are upset by the view from the window - another hotel is literally 2-3 meters away. On the network you can sometimes find information about Issara Resort 4 * (Issara Resort 4 *), the description of which fully corresponds to the hotel, which is discussed in this article. Do not let yourself be fooled, the hotel is actually a three star hotel.

Service and room reviews

Most of the tourists in the reviews note that check-in at the hotel is quick and easy. The staff is very polite and friendly, which in principle can be said about the whole "country of smiles." And even if you arrived in the early morning, and according to the rules, check-in is only from 2 in the afternoon, then you will be ready to make concessions.





The rooms, according to the guests, are clean, but need redecorating. Sound insulation is not too good, so you can hear how the air conditioning works outside the window. The most demanding of tourists note that it would be more correct to say Issara Resort 2 *, not 3 *. In many ways, not the best reviews are associated with breakfasts, which in reality turned out to be very modest, by choice of dishes not rich, but of decent quality, with local fruits. However, this did not upset many, because there are a huge number of places nearby where you can always eat deliciously for little money.

Reviews about the beach and the sea

It is worth noting one interesting fact: by a royal decision in Thailand, hotels are forbidden to own their own, private beaches. Therefore, there are only public places of rest! Patong is always crowded and noisy compared to secluded Kata Beach and Karon Beach. However, the sea and sand are no worse. Tourists note that due to the abundance of water transport and entertainment, the water is often muddy, but you can see the fish. Strong ebbs in the morning. In the afternoon heat, even umbrellas will not help to hide from the scorching sun, so try to choose the time to visit the beach in the morning or in the late afternoon. Of the minuses in their reviews, visitors note some pollution of the water and the territory itself.

In general, the Issara Resort 3 * Hotel Tophotels (a popular site about tourism) rates its visitors on a solid four on a five-point scale. The quality of the services provided and the price are quite consistent with each other and what tour operators say about him. The hotel is not new, but solid, clean, and most importantly - it has an excellent location.

Travelers who have already visited Patong advise young people to stay at this hotel, because rest here is more for entertainment than relaxation and restoration of strength, as well as couples, but without children, since there are practically no amenities and entertainments for small guests .
