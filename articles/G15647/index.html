Hotel 3 * Issara Resort (Phuket): reviews of tourists, photos

Hotel 3 * Issara Resort is a small resort establishment that offers excellent living conditions characterized by high comfort.





Brief description of the resort hotel

3 * Issara Resort Resort offers its guests comfortable accommodation in cozy and bright rooms. There are many technical devices that will provide you with home comfort and a level of relaxation that meets the requirements of a modern traveler. In addition, it is worth noting the incredibly pleasant atmosphere in the apartment and the elegant interior design.

The 3 * Issara Resort hotel complex offers guests the use of a variety of infrastructure facilities that meet all the requirements and standards of quality relaxation. Travelers are happy to plunge into the pool located on the roof of a high-rise building, and also admire the city panorama, slowly sipping an exotic cocktail.

One of the main features of Thailand is its unusual exotic cuisine. You can get acquainted with all its features in the restaurant that operates at the 3 * Issara Resort hotel. Here you will be offered not only national dishes, but also European culinary masterpieces that tourists are used to. You can also order room service if you want to have breakfast without getting out of bed, or have dinner in a secluded romantic setting.





Resort Location

Issara Resort 3 * hotel is located near the popular Patong Beach (no more than a 15-minute walk from it). It is also worth noting the relatively convenient location of the hotel relative to the airport, the road from which will take no more than 40 minutes. This is the central part of Patong, where all city and resort life is concentrated.

Resort Hotel Rooms

Hotel of category 3 * Issara Resort 3 * (Phuket, Thailand) has 76 guest apartments, which are divided into two main categories:

  • Superior room is a spacious apartment designed to accommodate two adult guests. They are not allowed to accommodate an extra bed, and therefore, if children come with adults, they must be accommodated in existing beds in the room. There is a small seating area where there is a pair of soft armchairs and a floor lamp.
  • Deluxe room is the embodiment of elegance and luxury, as well as a high level of comfort. It is worth noting that these rooms are two-room: that is, the bedroom and living room are separate. Three guests can accommodate as much as possible here, as upholstered furniture can serve as a place for an additional guest.




What is provided in the apartment

Issara Resort 3 * hotel (Phuket) gives its guests the opportunity to take advantage of the following amenities provided in the guest apartments:

  • a modern, albeit small, TV that allows you to watch both satellite and cable channels;
  • a landline telephone is configured for internal communication within the hotel, and if necessary, you will be given the opportunity to use the international line;
  • thanks to the safe, you will feel calm and full confidence in the safety of your things, which are of material value;
  • your minibar, subject to an additional payment, will be filled with alcoholic and sweet carbonated drinks;
  • Wireless Internet is absolutely free of charge available not only in the public areas of the resort hotel, but also directly in the rooms;
  • due to the fact that the climate in Thailand is quite hot, you will certainly be pleased with the presence of an air conditioner with the ability to individually control the air temperature;
  • in the bathroom, which is combined with the toilet, you will be provided with bath and cosmetics;
  • since there is an electric kettle in the room, you can always prepare hot drinks or boil water for your own needs;
  • on the balcony you can dry clothes and towels, as well as sit in comfortable plastic chairs.




Free services

The resort hotel Issara Resort 3 * (Phuket, Thailand) provides a range of services that are included in the price of your tour package:

  • at the reception you can take a hairdryer for temporary use, as well as an iron and ironing board;
  • in an Internet cafe you can access the global network from a desktop computer;
  • Going to sunbathe and swim on the beach, be sure to contact the reception desk for a beach towel.




Paid services

Part of the services provided by the hotel is not included in the total cost of the tour and requires an additional payment. Among them are the following:

  • modern laundry service, which operates at the hotel;
  • you have the opportunity to take an additional electric kettle for an additional fee by contacting the reception or the maid;
  • for the use of equipment for billiards charged hourly;
  • if you want your minibar to always have a wide range of drinks, you should order and pay for this service;
  • You can order food delivery from the restaurant directly to your apartment.

Useful information for tourists

The hotel has a number of mandatory rules:

  • settlement hours associated with check-in and eviction last from 2 a.m. to midnight, as well as from 6 a.m. to noon;
  • the hotel is not designed to accommodate young children - there is no special furniture and no appropriate infrastructure;
  • guests are not allowed to stay in the hotel with any animals;
  • You have the opportunity to use international payment systems for non-cash payment for services;
  • buffet breakfast will cost 100 baht for each guest;
  • if you leave the hotel prematurely, the administration is not obligated to refund the cost of the paid reservation;
  • use of the safe does not require additional payment;
  • if you take a hairdryer or iron for temporary use, then a deposit of 100 baht will have to be paid for it;
  • Checking into a hotel, you are required to make a deposit of $ 100, which will be returned to you in full if you do not cause material damage to the hotel.




Positive reviews

The Issara Resort 3 * resort (Phuket, Thailand) is quite popular with travelers. Reviews of tourists indicate the following positive aspects that accompany rest in this institution:

  • the linen on the bed is clean and fresh;
  • Wireless Internet works very well, in addition, this service is provided for free;
  • in the package of cable channels, among others, there are also Russian-language programs;
  • twice a day, guests are given a small bottle of drinking water (the one that flows from the tap is best not to drink, even if it is boiled);
  • One of the main advantages of the hotel is that on the roof, which offers a picturesque view, there is a clean pool, a modern bar and a small gym;
  • every day, bed linen is changed.




Negative reviews

There are some drawbacks to the Issara Resort 3 * hotel. Reviews of tourists highlight the following negative points:

  • when the deposit is returned to you, carefully look at the bill (hoping for the carelessness of tourists, you can slip a regular candy wrapper, which is nothing more than a souvenir product);
  • in rooms old, full of holes and dirty towels - it is better to bring your own from home;
  • the bathroom is in poor condition - everything is rusty, the plumbing is leaking, and the tiles are covered with mold in the Issara Resort 3 * hotel (photos of tourists confirm the neglect of the bathroom);
  • cleaning the room is very superficial, and tips do not fix this situation (so it is better not to waste money in vain);
  • the windows of some rooms practically abut against a neighboring hotel (and the point here is not only that the sun does not fall into the room, but also that someone is constantly watching you from the windows opposite);
  • breakfasts are served very early, so if you went to bed late, then in the morning you can just skip this event (the menu is also very modest, and nobody will allow you to take food with you);
  • there are very few sun loungers around the pool;
  • before hot water flows from the shower, you need to lower it for several minutes and languish in anticipation;
  • after you inserted a key card into a special connector, the ability to turn on electricity will appear no earlier than in 10 minutes;
  • if you have booked an apartment in advance , this is not a guarantee that you will be accommodated in a reserved room, or that the hotel will generally have free places;
  • if you wake up late, then around noon they will refuse to clean your room, reasoning that the staff has already left.
