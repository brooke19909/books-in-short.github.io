How to transfer an image from Android to a monitor

Smartphones and tablets have tightly entered our daily lives, but after using these gadgets for a long time, our eyes quickly get tired, because their display diagonal is far from the largest. But all this is solvable, because devices on the Android system are easily connected to a widescreen TV, on the screen of which you can watch movies or use the Internet with pleasure.

This article will discuss how to transfer the image from the monitor of an Android-based smartphone to a TV. All possible methods will be examined, so that by reading the article no one will have questions. It is worth saying in advance that some methods may not be suitable for you due to the mismatch of the model or the absence of certain inputs, outputs and options of the device itself .





How to connect "Android" to a monitor or TV?

There are several fairly simple ways to synchronize your tablet with a TV screen. Almost all manufacturers of modern devices provide such an opportunity, so everything will be quite simple for new generation devices.

Why connect the tablet to the TV?

First of all, the eyes get tired quickly after using the android monitor for a long time. Secondly, no matter how modern your TV is, applications for watching movies and surfing the Internet leave much to be desired, so why not use a more productive gadget, where the video player and other applications are adapted for convenient use .





HDMI cable connections

If you have a sufficiently powerful Android tablet, connecting a monitor using an HDMI cable is very easy. In most cases, a new generation of tablet computers has a miniHDMI port. Using a cable with HDMI to miniHDMI, which you can purchase at any electronics store or order from an online store, a device with the appropriate port can easily be connected to a monitor and TV with HDMI support. To do this, you insert one end of the cable into the connector on the tablet, and connect the other to the TV.





Next, in the TV settings, go to the Input section and select from the proposed options the corresponding HDMI port to which you connected the cable. It is the HDMI cable that will give you the greatest image clarity, because at the moment this technology is the most advanced. In addition, this is the easiest and most convenient way to connect .

Dock Sync

If the first two methods did not suit you for one reason or another, then you should find out if special docking stations are produced that allow you to connect your model's gadget to a TV, monitor or personal computer. The docking station is a design with connectors that allow you to connect a tablet to it. There are several types of such devices, some suitable for charging phones, but in this case, you need to look for a station with the ability to transfer data from the Android system to a monitor or the latest generation TV.

Wi-Fi connection

In order to use this method, your TV must support the Smart TV function, with the help of the program, the Android monitor device can be synchronized with the picture on the TV screen. To do this, you need to use Wi-Fi Direct technology, which will allow you to synchronize devices directly, without connecting to a router. The main advantage in using this method is that with the help of a wireless network you can not only transfer the image to the TV monitor using the "Android", but also manage it thanks to special applications.

Conclusion

So we sorted out all the ways how to transfer a picture to the screen of your TV from a smartphone based on Android.
