The origin of artificial flowers

Such accessories are a great addition to any outfit with the right selection of textures and shades. Every woman, whether a child or a lady of age, loves jewelry. They can be absolutely any, and the sewn flowers on a dress made of fabric with your own hands are generally unique and inimitable!

Note that the use of cloth flowers for clothing decor came to us from ancient times. Even in ancient China and Egypt, similar art was developed, and wonderful crafts of craftsmen were insanely expensive. It was customary to water such flowers abundantly with perfume to attract the opposite sex. Decoration of the image with fabric inflorescences was accessible only to rich and noble ladies, for commoners this pleasure was closed. But time passed, and in the Renaissance, the French established a wide production of such a decor - accordingly, it became much cheaper.

And nowadays, the amount of inexpensive materials and decor has flooded the markets, so anyone can make such a miracle - there would be a desire!

How to make flowers on a dress made of fabric with your own hands in a simple way?

It is much easier to go the easy way and get a flower in the store, but how interesting it is to create everything with your own hands! You will not find such an accessory in anyone - it is unique! To make flowers you need to purchase:

  • material;
  • needles with thread;
  • beads, rhinestones, sequins;
  • candles or an alcohol lamp.

Be sure to be patient - you will need it!

To give the inflorescences a natural color, the leaves are stained with special dyes. Well, if there are none, then take ink or gouache. Sometimes they dye the entire canvas of the material, but it happens that the petals are already cut out.

The fastest way to make flowers is to twist from a strip of fabric. This is done very simply!

To work, you need:

  • burlap or linen;
  • glue;
  • lace;
  • beads.

First, cut a strip of fabric 10 cm wide and about a meter long. To create a flower in the style of Provence, glue lace onto a strip.

Next, fold the inside out and bend on one side at an angle of 45 degrees.

Twist the resulting sharp corner and begin to twist the strip, while wrapping around the center.

Fill the end of the strip under the flower, on the lower side, and fix with glue. Also gently glue every place with a poor fit to the fabric strip.

From a felt, cut out a circle of a suitable size and glue it from the bottom to the rosette. Attach the clasp.

The brooch is ready!

Weightless inflorescences - organza accessories

A very delicate and airy flower of tremendous beauty can be made from this airy matter. How to sew a fabric flower on an organza dress?

First, cut a few circles of various sizes. If there is such an opportunity, it is better if smaller details are darker, medium ones are lighter, and the largest extreme ones are light. The greater the number of petals in your flower, the more voluminous it will turn out.

So, the petals were cut, now on a candle flame process them so that the edges melt, but do not turn black!

Insert elements into each other, starting from large to smaller. Carefully sew and attach stamens purchased either from a bundle of thread, or sew on beads and stones.

From a thick, non-crumbling fabric, cut out a circle that is suitable in size and glue it while attaching the clasp. Done!

Such a flower uncomplicated in manufacture will become an excellent decorative accessory for everyday occasions, as well as for a celebration!

Bright flower made of chiffon ribbon

The accent in clothes will be a voluminous, unusual element that will attract the attention of others.

For work you will need:

  • dress or t-shirt;
  • 2.65 m chiffon ribbon about five centimeters wide;
  • 40 cm of a narrow green ribbon;
  • needle with thread.

We cut a wide ribbon into 10 segments of 25 cm. We process the edges with fire so that they do not crumble. Now each piece must be folded in 4-7 layers, fixed from one edge with pins.

On the bodice of the dress, lay out the first 5 petals in a circle. The middle remains free. Sew with a needle and thread.

Next we sew another 5 petals, securing them closer to the center.

We make the middle of the fifteen centimeters of the remaining tape, folding it with a bow.

Cut the green ribbon into 2 parts and sew them as stems.

That's all, a bright flower is made!

It can be located anywhere - in the middle, side, down on the skirt - as you like!
