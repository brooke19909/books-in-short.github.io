Review of the washing machine Indesit IWSD 5085

Currently, automatic washing machines are popular. Prices start at 10,000 rubles. The well-known brand name for budget products is Indesit. Devices are assembled in Russia. Numerous service centers operate in the country. Due to this, there are no problems with the repair and purchase of spare parts.

The lineup of the Italian company is replete with diversity. It is recommended to pay attention to Indesit IWSD 5085. This model ideally combines functionality and ease of operation. You will have to pay 17,000-20000 rubles for this device. It is worth noting that such an amount is justified.



Short description

Before you buy Indesit IWSD 5085, you need to study this device in more detail. The model is presented in a classic design. The white color of the case allows you to harmoniously fit the washing machine into any interior style. The exterior design is concise and elegant. The hatch is decorated with an original shape handle. The control panel is informative, equipped with a small display. There are also buttons for selecting additional options. With the help of mechanical regulators, you can set the optimum temperature, select a washing program, change the spin speed. Near the display is a button that allows you to activate the delayed start option. It is possible to set a timer for 3, 6, 9, 12 hours. On / off button located near the detergent dispenser. She is at some distance from the rest. In order to start the wash cycle, press the "Start / Pause" button. With its help, you can also suspend the operation of the washing machine without resetting the settings. All modes are highlighted with LED bulbs. The display is small. It displays the remaining washing time.
