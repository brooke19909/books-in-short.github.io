We make the costume of Ivan Tsarevich

Costume parties have become an integral part of the life of a modern person. And if girls are pleased to come up with an image and dress up, then many men are dismissive of this ritual. They believe: the simpler the suit, the better. But nevertheless, representatives of the stronger sex understand that it is worthless to dress every time as a pirate or a comic book hero. I want to stand out from the crowd, creating an original image. Today we will tell you how to make a costume of Ivan Tsarevich. This image is non-trivial and will definitely help to be in the spotlight.

Costume for kindergarten





A needlewoman mom can create an amazing image of Ivan Tsarevich for her son. The costume will have to be made more than one evening, but it's worth it. If you have the strength and desire to put your son on a matinee, even a mother who rarely sits at a sewing machine can. How to make a costume of Ivan Tsarevich for a boy? The most difficult thing is to sew a camisole. The rest of the details should not scare you. For sewing a camisole, you will need two cuts of red fabric. One of them should have a gold print in the form of complex vegetation, and the second can be just an atlas.

Our camisole will not have sleeves, and the rear shelf should be longer than the front and end in a semicircle. Cut and sew. We cut openings for the hands in the side seams. It remains to decorate our camisole. Sew on the collar fur, gold planks turn the front side. Do not forget about the buttons and the holes for them. The camisole is ready. Under it you need to wear a white shirt and black pants. A crown must be placed on his head, and golden or black boots on his feet.

The image for the student





This costume of Ivan Tsarevich will be similar to the previous one. The difference will be in the details. We sew the camisole on the same principle, but this time it should be long, to the knees, and its front and back should be made of the same fabric with a gold pattern. The trim with planks will decorate not only the front side, but also the back. You can leave the fur decor on the collar, or you can replace it with braid. The shelf and back of the camisole will be the same length. This time, to the white shirt that will be worn underneath, we must sew the gold ribbons that decorated the outer clothing. Sew them to the neck, hem and sleeves. Legs will need to be sewn on legs. They will complement red shoes. On leggings, you can embroider a geometric ornament with gold threads or glue ready-made applications. This time we‚Äôre not putting a crown on our heads, but a hat. It can be sewn from the same material from which the camisole was made. The decoration of the headdress will be a yellow crown-shaped back-door, the center of which must be decorated with a brooch. The carnival costume of Ivan Tsarevich is ready. In it you can go both to a school party and to a festive celebration, for example, on the occasion of the New Year's Eve.

Costume for the holiday





To create such an outfit will be difficult without the help of an experienced seamstress. Therefore, a man can ask for help from his mother, girlfriend or sister. The costume of Ivan Tsarevich is very festive, almost royal. It will be easier to sew not from scratch, but to take as a basis a wide red dress. The first thing to do is cut a circle of red fabric. It will adorn the neck, so it should end on the shoulder line and go a little on the chest. Sew it to the dress. Cut a red wide strip adorning the front bar. Sew it in place. And with a wide strip of the same fabric we customize the suit for the height of the man. Sew it to the hem. Now, to your taste, the dress needs to be decorated with gold ribbons. Sew fur to the sleeves. It remains to decorate the camisole with rhinestones and sew a hat. We also sew a fur cut to it and do not forget to glue a few plastic jewelry. The costume is ready. You can complement it with black boots or shoes.

National dress



If you need to create an image in a hurry, then you can use this idea. The costume of Ivan Tsarevich will not require a pattern. You just need to find a national shirt or remake it from an existing one in the wardrobe. Of course, if you want, it can also be sewn using the pattern below.
A red piece of fabric needs to be sewn to the hem of a loose white shirt. Black braid decorate the shoulders. It remains to gird the shirt with a thin rope with bells, put on harem pants and boots. The image of Ivan Tsarevich, going on an adventure, is ready.

Party costume





To create this image, you also do not have to make titanic efforts. After all, making a costume of Ivan Tsarevich, whose photo you can see above, will not take much time. To create it, we need a light or blue jacket. Along the perimeter, we sheathe it with gold tape. And from it we make decorative overlays for buttons. We decorate the sleeves with gold fabric, and the collar with fur. The final stage is the manufacture of hand holes in the sleeves. We make them at the elbow level. On the head you need to wear a hat, made in the style of a camisole, and the image will be ready. It is worth completing it with black pants and boots.
