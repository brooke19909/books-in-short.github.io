Hotel Location

An overview of the Asia Pattaya Hotel must begin with its location. As we said, he is in Thailand.

Thailand is a state located in Southeast Asia. It is located in the western part of the Indochina Peninsula and in the northern part of the Malacca Peninsula. Thailand from the west is washed by the Andaman Sea, from the east by the Gulf of Thailand of the South China Sea. It is worth noting an important feature, Tai is a year-round tourist center. The tourism season here smoothly flows from one part of the country to another. This feature is due to the unique climate. Thailand is booming. The economy is based on natural gas production, rice cultivation, fisheries and rubber production. Moreover, pineapples, sweet potatoes and coconuts grow here.

Asia Pattaya Hotel is located in Southeast Thailand, Pattaya. The resort arose on the east coast of the Gulf of Thailand. The climate here is subequatorial. The average annual minimum is + 24-25 degrees, the maximum is + 36-40 degrees. Typically, the temperature is kept in the region of +30 degrees. The resort has a distinct wet season, which lasts from March to November. The amount of precipitation directly depends on the level of solar radiation. The peak of rains falls on May, September and October. The dry season is short, lasting from November to February. This is the perfect time for a vacation.
