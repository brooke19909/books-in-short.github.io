Hunchun: attractions, prices, reviews

The Far East is a great choice for traveling. If you are tired of the seas and mountains, you want something else: both untouched pristine nature and cultural features at the same time - we advise you to go there. The first item that Russian tourists usually choose when leaving Vladivostok is the Chinese city of Hunchun. Convenient location on the border of three states at once - the Russian Federation, the People's Republic of China and North Korea, significantly influenced the arrangement of the tourist infrastructure of the city. This place is known throughout northern China as a first-class resort center, rich in various monuments. In this publication, we have collected the most interesting information about Hunchun, (attractions, descriptions, prices).

History of the city

Hunchun is quite young for Chinese cities, often spanning millennia of history. The first mention of it can be found from the middle of the century before last. In the territory where Hunchun is now located, for hundreds of years there lived tribes of nomads-Jurchen, related to the Mongols, who gave the local river and, accordingly, the city a name, which means "borderland". Today, Hunchun belongs to the city county of Yanbian as part of the Korean Autonomous Region in the Chinese province of Jirin. It is located on the right bank of the Hunchunhe River.





Hunchun as a tourist center

The rich history of the city, despite the fact that it is relatively young, was also reflected in the many monuments that are definitely worth a look. Hunchun City, whose sights due to its beauty and unusualness can leave behind many European cities, does not cease to attract many tourists. True, one should know where to go, since this place is not very well known to many. When you get to the city, you will understand what interesting sights are in Hunchun. The prices will also pleasantly surprise you: a lot of restaurants where you can have a bite to eat as well as have a hearty lunch, and relatively cheap hotels will greatly enhance your stay. It is worth saying that if you wish, you can eat not only Chinese cuisine: there are many restaurants with Russian dishes in the city, so do not miss the opportunity to try how the Chinese prepare our usual food.

What can be seen in Hunchun?

Describing the main attractions of this place, it is worth pointing to the architectural ensembles of the local Buddhist temple, built in the form of a traditional Chinese building - the pagoda and Lin Bao Monastery. Here you will see a huge statue of a seated Buddha, as well as stone forests that are several thousand years old. It is here that extreme lovers should come, as near the monastery there is a river along which you can ride. We guarantee that you will be pleasantly surprised by the so-called European street, which differs from the traditional or modern Chinese urban landscape.





Ussuri tigers

Heard about the Ussuri white tiger? Well, if you want to see this rare and beautiful animal, you have to pack your bags in Hunchun. Senlin Mountain is located in close proximity to the city. Right at its foot, the Chinese government has created two natural reserves - Fangchuan and Jingsin. It is in them, preserving and maintaining the pristine landscape of the Amur Region, that the endangered species of the Ussuri tigers lives. By paying a certain amount, you can take a tour of the reserve and see the animal with your own eyes.





Sands Nature Reserve

Hunchun, whose attractions can surprise even the most demanding tourists, is famous for its nature reserves. Near the Russian border there is a conservation area called "Sands". The local Tumadjiang River brings sand to this territory, which, due to the unusual landscape, draws unusual paintings and patterns when exposed to wind, and all this is right before your eyes! You will get especially vivid impressions if you visit the Sands Nature Reserve in the summer.

Xingsheng Village

You can have a good rest from tiring trips and walks in the resort village of Xingsheng. At your disposal is a swimming pool, a paintball field, a lake full of fish for fishing enthusiasts. Caught cooked right there, right in front of you. For children there is an opportunity to ride horses and donkeys, which also will certainly be remembered for a long time.
