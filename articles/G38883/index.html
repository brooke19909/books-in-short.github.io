Crafts from wheels: fantasize

Crafts from tires - an old and very convenient way to decorate a garden, suburban area or lawn, located near the entrance.

The source material is usually obtained free of charge, and with a certain knack of tires and old wheels you can do everything: flower beds, figures of fairy-tale heroes, all types of vehicles (toys), swings, exercise machines, etc. Crafts from tires, photos of which adorn many pages devoted to design sites are striking in their originality and creativity.

Crafts from wheels for flowers

Most often , flower beds, flower beds and flowerpots are made from used stingrays. The simplest option will be mastered even by a baby. The tire is laid in the right place, in the middle the earth is falling asleep, the flower garden is ready. Crafts from wheels can be installed on supports (for this, rubber cannot be removed from the disk: it will be the bottom),

dig in the ground with a rib or, laying one on the other, build a slide. You can make a pot out of old rubber, but for this you need a grinder or a sharp carpentry knife. The tire is laid on the ground (do not remove the disc!) And is cut off next to the tread. Two halves, lower and upper, need to be twisted. You will get a vase that can be decorated with an ornament of stones or simply painted in different colors. It is important to take into account one detail: Russian tires, especially winter ones, are difficult to cut and turn. Imported is much easier to process. In our city there are more and more swans carved from tires. Such crafts from the wheels are cut out with a knife, then painted. In the same way, you can build a rooster, pelican, horse or hippo. The help in creating a pattern is usually provided by professional cutters, people who know how to sew toys, or just gardeners with good imagination and drawing skills. Crafts look great on flowerbeds, playgrounds, etc., if you simply hang the ramp on the wall and pour earth into it, you get an original decoration for country walls.
