Samsung Galaxy Trend Smartphone - Customer Reviews

If you want to follow the fashion and follow the latest electronics, pay attention to the models of well-known manufacturers. The products of the South Korean company Samsung are deservedly in demand. The first Samsung Galaxy Trend phone model received good reviews, as it had remarkable characteristics and was affordable. In order to trace the evolution of the device and the attitude of customers towards it, it is enough to consider the responses to the electronics.

Features of mobile devices from Samsung



The manufacturer offers very high-quality devices at a very reasonable price. The design from model to model of smartphones does not change, it remains recognizable. Earlier modifications were equipped with special programs, such as "S Health", "S Translator", "S Voice". And, as a rule, devices operate on the Android 4.2 operating system. It is characterized by remarkable speed, beautifully designed and offers the user three desktops.

Model feature



The first Samsung Galaxy Trend was met with approval by the public. Model GT-S7390 has a classic design, ease of operation. The device is equipped with RAM "Android 4.1". A four-inch screen perfectly conveys image, color, brightness. On a smartphone it is convenient to use the Internet, play games, listen to music.

The internal memory of the device is four gigabytes, operational - five hundred and twelve megabytes. It supports bluetooth and WIFI. Wide multimedia features: audio player, voice recorder, FM radio. GLONAS and GPS modules allow you to use the device as a navigator. The battery charge is enough to talk for eight hours. There is a camera - three megapixels. The smartphone can be safely recommended to parents as a wonderful gift for the child.

Smartphone Market New



In new versions, the manufacturer tried to take into account all the pros and cons of the previous options and introduced a new product - the Samsung Galaxy Trend Plus smartphone, which deserves attention. Customer feedback



An earlier model of the smartphone met with mixed reviews. There were objective reasons for that. Young and "advanced" were not always satisfied with the characteristics of the proposed device - for example, a small memory capacity, only four gigabytes, of which half is used by the phone for its own needs. But this problem is easily solved by microSD cards. In addition, the Samsung Galaxy Trend has a single-core processor with a frequency of one gigahertz and a camera of three megapixels.

But every thing has its good points that cannot be underestimated. No wonder the Samsung company is classified as a first-rate electronics manufacturer. The South Korean giant in the development of mobile devices uses a targeted approach. Girls of Samsung Galaxy Trend will surely appreciate. Reviews on the LaFleur model correspond to its appearance. After all, this smartphone in bright red creates a spring mood. It has a large touch screen. The multimedia player plays many formats of audio and video files. The battery provides five hours of browsing the Internet or up to forty-two hours of listening to music.

The advantages of a smartphone from Samsung

Despite some criticism from users, it is worth emphasizing the following. The phone is very elegant, lightweight and easy to use. Both a child and an elderly person can deal with it. Compactness Samsung Galaxy Trend customer reviews are called one of the positive characteristics, it is sometimes not enough for other devices. Therefore, it can be carried in the pocket of any clothing or in a small handbag.

The smartphone display is equipped with special proximity and position sensors. The budget model offers a wide range of features at an affordable price: listening to MP3 music, the Internet, navigation, maps, email, games, social networking, video recording and much more. The new models take into account all the comments, the Samsung Galaxy Trend smartphone will probably receive the most wonderful reviews and will win many more fans.
