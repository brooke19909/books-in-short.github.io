Air Purifier Reviews

Clean air in our homes is, first and foremost, health and well-being. A large amount of dust, dirt, bacteria and microbes contained in the air, can be the cause of quite serious diseases, among which are allergies. My son began to suffer from this ailment, the medication that we practiced did not give the desired results, then the allergist advised me to buy an air purifier that would remove the very cause of this disease. I heard quite a lot about them, and now I had to face up close. As always, when buying new household appliances, I turned to the Internet and studied air purifier reviews .





How to clean the air

To clean the air from it, you need to pay a lot of different pollution:

- large particles, such as soot, dust, coarse suspension;

- microscopic dirt - smoke particles, fine suspension, as well as bacteria and microbes;

- neutralize extraneous odors;

- If possible, give the purified air a pleasant aroma.

A universal filter for these tasks does not exist, because for each pollution it is necessary to apply different cleaning methods. So for coarse dirt, a mechanical filter is quite enough, which can successfully cope with dust retention. For smaller fractions, a fine filter, other than mechanical, is needed. Only adsorbent filters can handle odors.
