Hotel Overview: Acg Hotels Orient Family (Alanya)

More recently, Alanya in Turkey was a simple small town. And now this place is one of the most visited resorts in the country. This place is suitable for those who prefer a quiet, secluded pastime to relax in noisy companies. All Alanya hotels (including Acg Hotels Orient Family 5) are located on the first coastline, which is especially nice.

In addition to the beauty of nature, such a small but cozy resort town offers its guests to visit a variety of shops, specialized boutiques. It is here that you can buy excellent things made in Turkey, such as carpets, jewelry, leather goods and more. Different Alanya and a large number of bays with nature of amazing beauty. Among other advantages, this resort town is famous for its abundance of historical and cultural monuments. Guests of Alanya should visit the red tower, whose date of construction is considered to be 1228. The Museum of Ethnography is located within its walls. Many tourists visit the citadel, which housed the historic city. The best time to relax here is considered to be the period from the beginning of April to the end of November. The air in these months warms up to twenty-five degrees. Although, it is fair to say that cloudy weather is extremely rare here, so tourists from all over the world visit Alanya all year round.





Alanya Hotels: Acg Hotels Orient Family

To begin with, this hotel belongs to the category of "5 stars". He began his work relatively recently, namely in 2004. It is located not in the city of Alanya itself, but 132 kilometers from it, in a place called Mahmutlar. Based on the name of the hotel, it can be assumed that it is designed to relax with the family, and this is actually so. It consists of 4 buildings. The entire hotel complex is located on a mountain, which allows guests to enjoy the unique sea views. There are several pools (3 children and 7 adults), as well as a huge number of bars and an excellent restaurant, which allows you to spend your time as pleasant as possible in the family circle. Acg Hotels Orient Family has a high level of service. On the territory of the complex there is always at your service laundry, a photo studio, dry cleaning, a medical office and even several cinemas.





In addition, Acg Hotels Orient Family has its own spa, whose friendly and friendly staff will forget about all the problems. It should be noted that the hotel also houses a hairdresser, a comfortable and multi-functional fitness center. And how on vacation in Turkey to do without the well-known Turkish sauna and bath? By the way, guests' leisure is organized at the highest level at Acg Hotels Orient Family. The reviews speak for themselves. We list only a few activities. Firstly, these are night discos. Secondly, a variety of programs (dance, comedy shows), educational events (belly dance lessons, step aerobics and others). Well, those who do not like to walk will especially like the service of renting a vehicle.

Car rental, a cozy Internet cafe - these and many others are pleased with the hotel management of their vacationers.
