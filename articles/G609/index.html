Hotel Holiday Inn in Samara: address, room selection, booking, quality of service, additional services, visitor reviews

Samara is a large million-plus city, which nonresident residents often come to both for work and leisure. In this regard, we decided to devote this article to one of the best places to stay in Samara - Holiday Inn Hotel (address: 99 Alexei Tolstoy St.).

The hotel has a high status both among local residents and among visiting guests. It features a high level of service and a variety of services. Famous people, pop stars, athletes often stop here, rest the "cream" of society. The complex also hosts various business congresses, celebrations, weddings and exit registrations.

Description

The Holiday Inn in Samara was opened in 2007, it is one eight-story building, made in a modern style. At the moment, the hotel is considered one of the most comfortable business class hotels in the city. This is an ideal place to host, organize a festive event, hold a conference or seminar. The professional team of the complex will be happy to help organize their clients as a small family celebration, as well as an international forum for a thousand guests.

Location of the complex

The hotel is located in the heart of Samara. From the hotel to the Volga embankment no more than five minutes walk. Near the complex are located: the State Philharmonic, Kuibyshev Square, a museum, two theaters.

On the official website of the organization you can find out all the necessary information about the hotel, upcoming events, as well as current promotions and discounts.

In addition, on the Internet you can choose and book the apartments suitable for you. The phone number of the Holiday Inn Hotel (Samara) can also be viewed on the website.

Nutrition

In total, the hotel has three establishments where you can satisfy your hunger.

  1. "Atrium". Outdoor restaurant located on the ground floor of the complex. Breakfast is served here on the basis of the "Buffet". The menu presents a large selection of light hot dishes, slices, cereals.
  2. Cafe Atrium. Located in the central part of the Holiday Inn Hotel (Samara). The institution offers its customers dishes of European cuisine, a wide selection of elite alcohol and seasonal news from the chef. Great for a business meeting or an early breakfast.
  3. Restaurant "Brazil". Receives guests daily from noon to one in the morning. It offers a menu consisting of signature desserts and delicious dishes of Russian, Italian, European cuisines. For wine connoisseurs, a wide range of table and sparkling wines from around the world is presented here. For younger guests, the institution has a play area and a children's menu.

The chef of Braseria is a regular participant in culinary exhibitions, the winner of the Volga Chefs Festival, a professional who truly knows and loves his job. Braseria is an institution with exquisite dishes, beautiful interiors and high-class service, which will be a great place for a family dinner or celebration. The average check is 1000 rubles per person.

Conference rooms

For business people, the Holiday Inn Hotel (Samara) has thirteen conference rooms with a capacity of 10 to 450 guests. Each room has modern equipment and daylight. It also features a conference video service for online communication and live broadcasts.

Reviews about Hotel Holiday Inn (Samara)

Judging by the many reviews, the hotel described in this review is an excellent luxury hotel that fully meets high standards.

Customers celebrate its unique, modern and elegant interior, beautiful rooms, high level of service, a large territory inside the hotel. A lot of good words were said about the staff of the complex, the staff are very polite, attentive, always happy to help.

Clients of the hotel highly appreciate the spa center, note its good location, spacious and clean pool, excellent saunas, a jacuzzi. The gym also meets all the requirements: modern equipment, cleanliness, comfort. A big plus is that hotel guests can go to the spa and gym for free.

Among the shortcomings in some reviews, guests note "tired" furniture and a poor breakfast.
