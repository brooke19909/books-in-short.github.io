Kemer Botanik Resort Hotel 4 *, Turkey, Kemer: reviews, photos

If you like to relax on the sea and are used to comfort, but do not want to spend much on expensive hotels, then as a suitable option for living on the Turkish coast, we suggest considering the four-star Kemer Botanical Resort.



Location

This hotel is located in Kemer (Turkey), on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. The city center is 1 km away. The nearest airport is Antalya, 55 kilometers from the hotel.

Kemer Botanik Resort Hotel 4 * (Kemer): reviews of tourists from Russia

Modern travelers are increasingly trying to approach the process of choosing a hotel both at home and abroad. So, they not only study official information and are interested in the advice of tour operators, but also read reviews of people who have already happened to visit a particular place. Thanks to this approach, a closer to reality idea is formed about what will await you during your vacation. After all, no one wants to experience unpleasant disappointment on a long-awaited vacation. Therefore, we decided to make your task a little easier and offer to familiarize yourself with the generalized comments of our compatriots who recently had a rest at the Kemer Botanic Resort Hotel. Looking ahead, we note that most tourists were satisfied with the choice made. They note that the hotel corresponds to its category and price. Now let's find out the details.





Hotel itself

Our compatriots, judging by their reviews, were very pleased with the rooms he offered at the Kemer Botanik Resort Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Kemer). So, our compatriots claim that the rooms here are very spacious, bright, stylishly decorated. It is immediately clear that the hotel is new. Many were pleased with the interesting design. According to travelers, the rooms have everything you need: comfortable furniture, modern appliances, a spacious bathroom, and a balcony with a pleasant view. The hotel guests did not complain about the quality of cleaning. According to them, the maids came daily. Towels and linen in the rooms were changed several times a week. In addition, many guests in their comments are pleased to note that Wi-Fi is good for almost the entire territory of the hotel, which is an important point for most modern tourists.





Restaurant and Bar

As for food, this issue almost always causes a lot of controversy among guests in a variety of hotels in Turkey and other countries. We could not pass this fate and the Kemer Botanik Resort Hotel 4 * hotel under consideration (Kemer). So, some tourists in their comments say that the food in the hotel is neither quality nor a special variety. However, most vacationers claim that in reality everything is different. In their opinion, dissatisfied guests may have simply been to more expensive five-star hotels before. Many guests are sure that the food in the Kemer Botanical Resort is consistent with the category and cost of the hotel. According to them, the menu always had dishes of fish, meat, poultry, salads, snacks, sweets, fresh fruits. However, a number of guests attributed the drawbacks to the fact that the tables with dishes in the restaurant were not very comfortable. Therefore, with an influx of visitors, queues are often formed, which could have been avoided by setting tables a little differently. Some of our compatriots expressed these considerations to the receptionists in the hope that this information will reach the hotel manager and the situation will change for the better. Otherwise, most tourists had no complaints about the restaurant.





Beach vacation

Most tourists in their reviews note that Kemer Botanik Resort Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Kemer) has at its disposal a very good beach. However, on the way to it you need to walk a couple of hundred meters along the road. According to our compatriots, there is no sidewalk yet, so you have to go along the roadway, which is not entirely safe. However, according to hotel employees, next year there will be a sidewalk, so there is nothing for future travelers to worry about. As for the beach itself, it is small-pebble, quite large, with a gentle entry into the sea. There is a bar, but most drinks are paid. There is a sufficient number of comfortable sun loungers and parasols on the beach. By the way, guests are warned that beach towels are available at an additional cost. Therefore, if you want to save money, then grab them from home or purchase them upon arrival in Turkey in one of the many shops.

A number of our compatriots also mention in their comments that the hotel beach is sometimes not very clean. But they do not blame the staff responsible for cleaning the shore, but the vacationers, who often do not bother to throw cigarette butts, empty glasses and other garbage into the bins available in sufficient quantities.





Entertainment at the hotel

In addition to a beach holiday, guests of the Kemer Botanik Resort Hotel 4 * can have a great time at the hotel. So, there is a swimming pool and a sun terrace. There is a bar nearby with a wide selection of drinks for every taste. Also on the territory of the hotel are equipped sports grounds, a gym. During the day, guests are entertained by a team of animators. So, tourists will be offered to do aerobics, yoga, play volleyball, tennis, billiards. In the evenings, entertainment shows are organized for guests. After them, in the company of animators, you can go to one of the Kemer clubs.

Personnel Impressions

As for the staff of the four-star hotel "Kemer Botanical Resort", then, according to reviews, our compatriots were quite satisfied with them. So, according to them, the staff here is attentive, coping with their duties, friendly. Many employees speak Russian, so you will not have any communication problems here.
