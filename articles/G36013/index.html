How to set up channels on your LG TV do-it-yourself



Sociology knows everything. She says that in front of a blue screen a person spends an average of about five hours daily. To ensure a more comfortable viewing of popular programs, it’s enough to properly configure the channels. Television, thanks to modern technology, offers new formats and higher quality broadcasts. If you want to watch your favorite shows bring more positive emotions, then connect digital channels. They can accept many brands of televisions, especially those produced by well-known manufacturers. Let's look at a few options for setting up channels on an LG TV, and what steps to take. Television receivers exist of different types and types: LCD, plasma, LCD, Smart TV. Digital channels can support televisions running on a DVCrypt conditional access network. Connecting them is quite simple, for this there is an instruction.

Smart TV and its settings

The new generation of devices supports the Smart TV function - these are the so-called “smart TVs”. The user has a great opportunity not only to watch his favorite movies and shows, but also to use the Internet access. Therefore, initially, before you configure the channels on your LG TV, you must ensure that you are connected to the network. Suitable for both wired and wireless, using a Wi-Fi router. Turning on the television receiver and providing an Internet connection (for example, via a cable from a router to the LAN connector), you need to go to the settings. Then select "Network", in it - "Network Connection". Having connected, click “Network selection”. In the list that appears, you will need to specify the desired type (wired, wireless), select and click "Update". Now your "smart TV" will see the Internet. Finally, click on the “Finish” button. After that, you need to register on the LGAPSS portal, create an account, enter a password, and receive an email at the email address. In it, select the link “Complete registration”. Now you can start with the most important thing, namely, consider how to configure the channels on the LG TV, gaining access to the portal. First you need to install the special application “SS IPTV”. Now let's go directly to the process itself.





Channel Sort

After the application is installed on the TV, a large menu with the icons of the TV channels will appear on its screen. Your task is to consistently select the ones you are interested in and press the OK button on the remote control. The channels are sorted in the same way on the LG TV, everything is quite simple. True, in some cases, a problem may arise in their incorrect and inconsistent position, then your intervention will be required again.

Auto Channel Tuning





With sorting, everything is clear, but how to set up channels on an LG TV? Consider the whole process in order. First, open the menu (on the remote control). In it we select “Options”, where we need “Auto-tuning”. Next, you need to specify the language and country in the proposed list. Launch Auto Search. Confirm the selection with the OK button. In the new menu you need to select "Cable". In the event that this item does not appear, you will need to repeat all the steps again, and select Finland or Germany as the country. To search for digital channels, select “Settings” from the menu. Then click "Search". In the auto search window, you need to set the necessary parameters: modulation, search type, frequency, symbol speed, etc. The last item on the ID network indicates “Auto”. If all the steps were performed correctly, then the entire setup process will take very little time - only five to ten minutes. When the search is over, click OK.

Satellite antenna



A television receiver can transmit broadcasts not only via the Internet, but also in the traditional way - by cable or satellite dish. The latter option has gained great popularity. "Plate" allows you to catch a television signal not only in a city apartment, but also in the country. Initially, you need to choose a company whose products are equipped with a satellite built-in tuner DVB-S2. South Korean television receivers fully meet these requirements. To show LG TV well, setting up digital channels is required. But first you need to connect the antenna. To do this, you need to connect the cable from the satellite dish to the television (you will need the Satellite IN input). Then connect the DRE CRYPT MPEG-4 module. Now we are starting to configure the channels. First, pick up the remote control and select "Menu", and in it - the desired channel. Then - the satellite antenna. Yes, you still need to enter a pin code. Then you need to select a satellite and put a tick in the corresponding window. After that, go to “LNB Setup”. After making the necessary changes and setting the parameters, proceed to tuning the channels, following the transponder table.

Useful tips in conclusion

A great choice of home appliances is the LG TV. How to configure channels on it, you now know. In principle, this is not so difficult. When the search is completed, all digital channels and available radio stations appear on the screen. LG TVs are undoubtedly a masterpiece of modern technology. But they have a feature: auto-update channels. It is recommended to disable this function, otherwise a reconfiguration is required.
