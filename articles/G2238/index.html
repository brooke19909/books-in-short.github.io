The pearl of the Mediterranean, the beautiful island of Cyprus has always been and continues to be one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world. Every year, hundreds of thousands of tourists from all over the world come to the homeland of the goddess Aphrodite to plunge into the atmosphere of antiquity and enjoy a vacation on the magnificent beaches of the island.

Cyprus is especially popular with tourists from Russia. Perhaps this is facilitated by the relatively close location of the island, or perhaps the possibility of free, visa-free entry of citizens of the Russian Federation into the territory of this country.





From Moscow to Larnaca or Paphos can be reached in just three and a half hours. It is in these two cities that there are international airports that accept flights from around the world, including from Moscow. By the way, in season charter flights from many large cities of Russia fly to Cyprus. Both Paphos and Larnaca are excellent tourist centers, but there are many other resorts on the island (Ayia Napa, Polis, Limassol, Protaras, etc.), where you can go by bus or taxi upon arrival.

By the way, the best option is pre-booking a car. With wheels under it, it will be possible to travel the entire island far and wide and explore each of its corners. Protaras

This resort is one of the youngest in Cyprus. It is located in the southeast of the island. There are not many attractions here, but there are wonderful golden beaches, sea landscapes, bizarre cliffs and blue lagoons, and most importantly - peace and quiet.





Protaras (see photo in the article) primarily attracts fans of a secluded and lazy beach holiday. Not so long ago, on the site of luxury fashionable hotels, there were bushes and fig trees. When it became necessary to build a new resort, the choice fell on this particular section of the beach. It is located in a bay and is protected by cliffs from the winds. Due to this, the weather conditions in Protaras are very comfortable for a beach holiday: there are practically no big waves, the air temperature even in the winter months does not drop below + 17 degrees, but in summer it is not hot at all, the air warms up to a maximum of 27-28 degrees. All this attracts thousands of tourists who long for comfort and peace.

Features of rest

Since the resort of Protaras is one of the youngest on the island, almost all the hotels here are new and built using modern technology. On the beaches you can not only sunbathe in comfort, but also engage in water sports.

Tsokkos Gardens Hotel (Cyprus / Protaras): General Description and Location

This hotel, like all other apartment hotels, is ideal for families, as the rooms are equipped with kitchenettes. This makes it possible to organize meals for all family members (especially babies) at their discretion. Tsokkos Gardens Hotel was built in 2001. At that time, it was rated as a three-star hotel, but after being renovated in 2005, one more hotel was added to its three stars.

Today, this four-star hotel provides tourists with a high-class service that meets European standards and the concept of the Tsokkos hotel brand. It consists of several buildings. The main one is five-story, in front of it there is a rather large pool, around which there is a solarium area with sun loungers and umbrellas.

Location

The hotel is located between the district of Pernera and Protaras, to which, as already mentioned, is only one and a half kilometers. You can walk to the city on foot or by bicycle, or you can order a taxi at the reception. Tsokkos Gardens Hotel does not have its own beach, but only 150 m from it there is the magnificent Marlita Bay beach. Larnaca Airport is a 30-40 minute drive from the hotel.

Number of rooms

Tsokkos Gardens Hotel 4 * has 184 comfortable standard rooms. All have windows overlooking the Saint Elias Temple. This is just an idyllic sight. Rooms are 22 square meters. meter. They are designed for a maximum of 3 people.





All rooms have balconies, equipped with furnished plastic furniture, a kitchenette with fridge, tea and coffee making facilities, a bathroom with shower, hairdryer and bath accessories. There is a direct dial telephone, air conditioning and central heating, a safe and a minibar (for an extra charge), a radio, and a TV with satellite channels. Rooms are cleaned daily and linen is changed every 3 days. By the way, non-smoking hotel has private rooms.

Hotel infrastructure

As already noted, the Tsokkos Gardens Hotel (Cyprus / Protaras) consists of several buildings, which include both rooms and public areas: lobbies, bars, restaurants, a gym, etc. The buildings have an elevator. In front of the central building is a beautiful, and most importantly, always clean outdoor pool of an original form, next to it is a small and shallow children's pool. There is also a bar.

Indoor facilities also include a heated indoor pool. It is usually used by guests who come to rest in late autumn or early spring, as well as on Christmas and New Year holidays. After all, the Tsokkos Gardens Hotel operates year-round. For this purpose, all the premises of the complex are equipped with a modern heating system. Car parking is also available on site. In the internal premises there is a restaurant with a terrace, a cafe, a TV room, a conference room and a business center, a currency exchange, a medical room, a mini market, and a gift shop. For children there is a room and a playground for games, a mini club, a hall with slot machines. The hotel has a spa and beauty center, as well as a gym, sauna and jacuzzi. Vacationers are offered dry cleaning, laundry and ironing, although the administrator of the floor can always ask for an ironing board and an iron in the room.

Sports and Entertainment

Carrying out an extensive animation program for entertaining guests at the hotel is an extremely rare phenomenon. In their reviews, tourists who visited the hotel sometimes complain about this circumstance, while others, on the contrary, rejoice that they were finally able to really take a break from the hustle and bustle. However, there are also reviews in which tourists talk about a wonderful organized and rich entertainment program, especially for children. This suggests that the organization of animation at Tsokkos Gardens Hotel directly depends on the contingent of vacationers: if most of the guests are people of retirement age, fun animation is canceled, and if there are many children, then interesting contests and quizzes will be held for them, as well as children's disco. In any case, the children will not have to be bored, they can always find entertainment on the playground or in the mini-club.

And if parents wish to spend the evening in restaurants or nightclubs in Protaras, then for a fee they can invite the nanny to look after their baby. Once a week, the hotel hosts Greek evenings with folklore rooms. For fans of sports entertainment in the complex there are tennis courts with night lighting, ping pong and billiards, darts, etc.

Beach

As already mentioned, Tsokkos Gardens Hotel Apts does not have its own beach. However, this does not matter, since the well-equipped public beach of Marlita Bay is located 150 meters from it. The only nuisance is that you have to pay for sun loungers and umbrellas. But showers and changing rooms are free. On the beach you can find many water activities: catamarans, scooters, parachutes, bananas, water skiing, etc. Diving instructors will help you make an underwater trip to the coral reefs.





The water in the sea in the area is crystal clear, which contributes to these activities. The beaches of Protaras are not completely bare: fig trees approach the shore tightly. It is especially beautiful here in April, when the beaches are framed by flowering figs and tropical shrubs. It is difficult to describe in words, this beauty is simply breathtaking! On the promenade, which is also surrounded by green trees, you can sometimes see funny pelicans. In these parts they are not at all afraid of people and calmly walk in small flocks.
