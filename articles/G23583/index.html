  • Two-room first (31 sq. M.). It is equipped with a double bed, a sofa, a pair of armchairs, a refrigerator, a TV, a split system, a bathroom. Price - from 2100 rubles per person.

  • Junior suite of the first category (18-28 sq. M.). Equipped with a double bed, sofa or armchair, fridge, TV, split system, bathroom. Price - from 2050 rubles per person.

  • Deluxe first category (35-38 sq. M.). Equipped with a double bed, two armchairs or a corner sofa, refrigerator, TV, split system, bathroom. Price - from 2200 rubles per person.

  • Studio of the first category (38 sq. M.). Equipped with a double bed, corner sofa, armchair, refrigerator, TV, split system, bathroom. Price - from 2150 rubles per person.
