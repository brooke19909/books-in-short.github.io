Ergonomics and design

Nokia Lumia 1520 completely inherited the features of its younger sister Lumia 925. An exception is some nuances. Our heroine is an oversized Nokia Lumia 925. The main difference is that it lacks a metal frame. As a result, the device turned out to be recognizable, and this, in general, is not surprising.





Its front is covered with Gorilla Glass 2, which has rounded corners. A rubber seal is located around its perimeter. The upper part of the glass has a slot designed for the earpiece, under which is the logo of the manufacturer. On the left is the front camera window , and on the right are proximity and lighting sensors. The hardware keys that control the device are located below the display. They can be seen well off.

The left sidewall has two slots at the upper end. One (bottom) is for Nano-SIM, the second is for MicroSD. Access to them can only be obtained using a sharp object, which is rather inconvenient, but, unfortunately, this is becoming the standard for modern smartphones. The opposite side carries a power button, a two-position camera button, a dual volume control key - a rocker. Each of them has a ceramic coating. They almost do not protrude above the surface of the case, which causes some inconvenience in use. On the top of the device is a headphone jack.





The lower one bears the traditional Micro-USB input. Conversational microphone found a place at the junction of corporate glass and rubber seal. The multimedia is located on the back between the flash for the camera and the headphone input. The total number of microphones carried by the smartphone is 4.

The back of the Nokia 1520, as well as the sides, is made of rough polycarbonate. The main feature of the case is its integrity, it is not multi-layered. Therefore, scuffs on it are not noticeable. The Lumia 1520 camera, unlike the 1020th, does not protrude above the body and is therefore almost invisible.

In the horizontal position on the phone (on its left side), you can see the speaker grid for calls. In the center is the manufacturer‚Äôs logo, camera, its optics, flash, which consists of two LEDs, as well as openings for microphones.

operating system

What does the review of its software say about the Nokia 1520? The system basis for this smartphone is Windows Phone 8.0 (Nokia Black). He is the first device to have a Full HD screen. In general, it has everything inherent in this operating system.

The following main applications are preinstalled:

- Folders. This application allows you to group any programs and files available on your smartphone. It can be taken out to the main screen.





- Change of focus. This is for amateur photographers who like to focus on pictures after they are taken. To have a choice, the camera takes up to eight pictures.

- The projector. This application makes it possible to display a smartphone image on a monitor or TV screen. Unfortunately, the usefulness and functionality of the application is very low, so there is no point in comparing it with something else.

- Stories. It provides sorting of both video and photos into interactive groups depending on the place of creation.

- The camera. This application combines two other familiar to users of other Lumia models - Nokia Smart Cam and Nokia Pro Cam.

In addition, the Nokia 1520 has a new lock screen - Nokia Glance 2.0, disabling automatic screen orientation, the ability to save images in DNG format, support for devices with Bluetooth LE technology.

The rest is familiar Windows Phone.

Display

Nokia is a company that uses both AMOLED screens and IPS displays in its devices. Our smartphone has an IPS matrix of 6 inches and a resolution of 1920 x 1080 pixels. The screen is covered with Gorilla Glass 2, which has an olefobic coating and anti-reflective layer. Screen settings have a large number of additional items that allow you to make the image clear, adjust the saturation and color temperature.

The brightness is adjusted in another section. The user is given the opportunity to choose one of the proposed settings or automatic mode. You can adjust the brightness in the range from 3.8 cd / m¬≤ to 589 cd / m¬≤. Good visibility provides high brightness and anti-reflective layer. The contrast of this display is very good - 1550: 1.

Camera

She is the best. The company always uses the most advanced cameras in its flagships. The Lumia 1520 has a 20 megapixel module with Carl Zeiss optics and an image stabilization system. On the Nokia Lumia 1520, photos are maximally obtained with a 4: 3 aspect ratio in a resolution of 19 megapixels, and at 16: 9 - in 16 megapixels. At the same time, the camera takes a double shot, except for the main one, it saves the image in a resolution of 5 megapixels. In addition, only small pictures can be seen on the phone, and natural ones after connecting to a PC on the monitor.

Shooting and video playback is in 1080p format, and this at a frame rate of 24/25/30 frames in one second. During playback of the recording, zoom is available without loss of quality, which is provided by the PureView function.
