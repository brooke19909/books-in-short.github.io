Island holidays always attract the interest of vacationers. Holidays in Greece: Rhodes Island

This resort is one of the most visited in Greece. The number of sunny days in a year is more than three hundred. Thanks to the sand and pebble beaches, the water is transparent, the entrance to the sea is comfortable. The presence of historical sites makes the island an attractive place to relax.

Hotels can be embarrassed to choose the average level. The Greeks often "trick" with the stars to save on tax deductions, and their "treshki" superior in quality to mainland European hotels. About hotel

Sunland Hotel Aquapark 3 * is located two kilometers from the village of Ialyssos, the distance to the city of Rhodes is ten kilometers. This is the western part of the island with a well-developed infrastructure, a round-the-clock activity zone and the work of bars, clubs and night discos.

Less than half a kilometer from the hotel (second coastline) is an excellent private beach with an extended line, where you can use sun loungers, parasols and beach towels for a fee.

Tourists reviews

Concluding a short story about the advantages of the hotel Sunland Hotel Aquapark 3 *, reviews about which they talk about him as a good vacation option in Rhodes, it is worth sharing the features of the resort.

According to guests, the sea is restless for families with children, and there are very large waves. Therefore, children are suitable for swimming in the pool, specially fenced places, or you need to find a convenient bay. On the other hand, it is a paradise for surfers. Therefore, a special training center for this type of water recreation was opened - Pro Centro Rhodos (in the village of Trianda, with a rental point for equipment).

Not far from the Sunland Hotel Aquapark 3 * is the Kalopetra Monastery and the Knight's Church built in the 15th century. These historical places can be visited independently.





A sightseeing tour of the island is best booked in a specialized office. They are usually organized at a very high level, and professional guides deserve special respect for their attitude to their work.
