Vigo Hotel 4 * (Nessebar, Bulgaria): hotel description, services, reviews

Bulgaria, as before, remains a popular tourist resort. That is why in our article we want to talk about one of the hotels in the ancient city of Nessebar. Vigo Hotel 4 * is a gated complex with an impressive territory and well-established infrastructure. It is located on the beach in the southern region of Nessebar. And next to the hotel there is a beautiful city park, stretching along the sea. It is not difficult to find the complex, since it is located 500 meters from the city center and 25 kilometers from the Bourgas airport. A little bit about the resort ...

Nessebar is a unique pearl of Bulgaria. The city is rich not only with beautiful beaches, but also with historical monuments that have survived to our times. The city is located on a peninsula, north of Burgas. It is believed that Nessebar is one of the most ancient cities in Europe. His story began three millennia ago. Archaeological excavations are ongoing here to this day. Every year they make new discoveries of ancient peoples. Nessebar is included in the list of UNESCO assets.



The ancient district of the city can be considered surprisingly surviving. Two centuries ago, he went under water as a result of an earthquake, and on land only a small part of it survived, on which all the temples are located. And now the underwater part of the city can be seen from a boat in calm sunny weather.

The pride of Nessebar is its unique beaches. They are rightly considered the most beautiful in Bulgaria. The local coast even holds the Blue Flag.

How to get to the hotel?

When planning a vacation, you can think about how to get to the Vigo Hotel 4 *. Of course, you can order a transfer, this service is provided by the complex. But you can get to it yourself. The nearest transport hubs are located in Burgas (railway station, airport), about thirty kilometers away. From Burgas to Nessebar can be reached by bus. The road will take no more than forty minutes. In addition, there are buses going to Sunny Beach. Using them, you will need to make a transfer to the vehicle to Nessebar. You can still use a taxi, but it will cost more than public transport.

Weather in Nessebar

The climate of the resort can be described as temperate continental. Weather in Nessebar allows you to swim and sunbathe on the beaches from May to October. However, at other times you can also meet tourists who skillfully combine relaxation with an entertainment program. The city is intertwined with the past and the present, luxury hotels are adjacent to the ancient ruins. The high season lasts from July to August. At this time, the city is crowded with vacationers.



But in the low season (from October to April) Nessebar practically returns to normal life, there are very few tourists here. You can swim in the sea from June until the end of September, when the water temperature still holds at around 21-22 degrees.

Local Attractions

Resting in Nessebar, it is worth taking the time, breaking away from a beach holiday, for exploring local attractions and the most beautiful places. The ancient part of the city is most interesting for tourists. It is located on a small peninsula, which is connected to the land by a small isthmus. Old Nessebar has a special atmosphere created by medieval churches, ramparts, old houses with wooden stairs.

Churches are the main attractions of the city. Each has its own unique history and architecture. The Metropolitan Church of St. Stephen is the oldest of them. Legend has it that it was built on the site of the temple of Apollo. The frescoes, the bishop's department and the iconostasis are well preserved in the temple.

For guests of the city, a windmill made of wood and a Turkish bath are no less interesting. Ethnographic and archaeological museums function in Nessebar. Their visitors can see ancient coins, antique figured ceramics, gold jewelry of the Middle Ages.



In addition to historical attractions, in Nessebar you can see natural beauties, such as natural sand dunes and White Rocks. The city has numerous entertainment. Tourists will certainly appreciate the water park and Luna Park, to which free buses run. The city has a huge number of cafes where you can taste delicious seafood, which is prepared simply amazing.
