"Warsaw", hotel (Moscow): address, phone number, reviews

Warsaw is a hotel (Moscow), which, thanks to the high standards of service, recently (namely in 2013) received the coveted three stars. This has made the hotel a lot of positive changes.

Hotel "Warsaw": address

One of the main advantages of this hotel is its favorable location. A few meters from the Kremlin and Red Square is the hotel "Warsaw". Leninsky Prospekt, 2/1, - the address of this hotel. It is literally a couple of kilometers from the railway stations (Kiev, Paveletsky and Belorussky). Also within walking distance is the metro station "October". Hotel "Warsaw" will give you the opportunity to access the main tourist and infrastructure facilities of the city.

Guest apartments

In this hotel you can choose for yourself one of 136 rooms, which belong to the following categories:

  • Single standard room is ideal for those who love independent travel. This is a small room with a bed and a compact work area. There is also a spacious wardrobe for your belongings.
  • The single superior room is more spacious than the room of the previous category. Here, among other things, there is a relaxation area with a soft sofa, which can act as a sleeping place for an additional guest.
  • Double standard apartments are equipped with one and a half beds, as well as a coffee table with comfortable armchairs. The bathroom has a bathtub with safety rail.
  • The superior double room, unlike the previous one, is equipped with a sofa and a huge window through which you can enjoy beautiful metropolitan views.
  • One-room business-style room is designed as a studio. Here, within one room, an entrance hall and a spacious bedroom are combined. Visually enlarges the room area with a large mirror on a wardrobe.
  • Two-room business room includes a spacious bedroom in a minimalist style with an incredibly cozy design. The living room has upholstered furniture for relaxing, as well as a full working corner.
  • The business format of increased comfort is characterized by a bright design, as well as new original furniture. In addition, there is a rather large area, and household appliances are the most modern. In addition to the bedroom and living room there is also a dining room.

It is better to book hotel rooms in advance, because there are almost never free apartments. You can do this on the official website, special reservation resources, or by contacting directly by phone.

How to book an apartment

It is worth noting that the Warsaw Hotel is almost always crowded, and therefore the question often arises of how to book a room. For this purpose, you can contact the special department that deals with the reservation of apartments for guests. Phone number: 8 (499) 238-19-70. In addition, the official website, as well as other specialized resources, has the option of ordering apartments online.

It is worth noting that a prerequisite for booking is to make an advance payment by credit card or electronic payment system. Otherwise, after a certain period of time, your reservation will be canceled.

Each guest has the right to refuse the reservation with the return of pre-paid funds. However, if you do this later than one day before your expected arrival, you will be charged a fine of the daily rate in the room.





Hotel Policies

The Warsaw Hotel has the following policies:

  • the estimated hours associated with the registration of check-in and check-out are set respectively at two in the afternoon, as well as at noon;
  • if children under 7 years old do not need an extra bed, then they stay at the hotel for free;
  • cots for babies up to 2 years old are free;
  • guests are not allowed to bring animals to the room, as well as to the territory of the hotel;

  • if foreign citizens arrive at the hotel, then they can be provided with visa support, the cost of which is 400 rubles.

Positive reviews

An excellent accommodation option in the capital is the Warsaw Hotel. Reviews about it are full of positive comments:

  • the hotel is quite well located - in the central part of the city, close to the main attractions;
  • very spacious and bright rooms;
  • incredibly beautiful views from the windows of the rooms;
  • the elevator is very fast;
  • fairly affordable rates for accommodation;
  • good noise isolation between neighboring rooms;
  • Inexpensive all-inclusive dinners breakfast is included in the price;
  • the bedding is new and smells very nice.

Negative reviews

Not all guests are satisfied with the Warsaw Hotel. This placement option is characterized by the following negative aspects:

  • the air conditioning system in the hotel is turned on only in the summer, and in the spring you have to sleep in stuffy rooms;
  • for a long time there was no major or at least cosmetic repair (carpets and wallpaper are worn and stained);
  • TV shows very poorly;
  • tasteless and very poor breakfasts;
  • the infrastructure around the hotel is rather poor: there are no cafes or grocery stores within walking distance;
  • the room is not cleaned well enough;
  • not enough microwave and electric kettle;
  • since the windows of most rooms face the road, a lot of noise and dust enters the room.

General impression

The general positive impression of the Warsaw Hotel is created due to its good location. A little stroll will take you to Red Square. And thanks to the proximity to the metro station, you can quickly get to any part of the city. Nevertheless, in everything that concerns shops and cafes, you will be disappointed, because there are practically none nearby.

As for the living conditions at the hotel, they are quite comfortable. The rooms are fairly bright and feature a pleasantly bright decor. Nevertheless, the decoration and equipment of the apartments are a bit outdated and require repair or a complete replacement. This disadvantage is compensated by the low price of accommodation. Despite the fact that breakfast is included in the price, you can hardly get enough of it, because the assortment and quality of dishes are not encouraging. Nevertheless, the hotel is quite suitable for tourists who want to get to know Moscow as close as possible.
