What is an e-book and why is it needed?

E-books are increasingly entering our lives, replacing traditional paper editions. Why it happens? Firstly, it is convenient, and secondly, on a small device you can put together a huge library of a wide variety of literature: from technical manuals to the collected works of your favorite poets. An electronic book is a portable device - a tablet computer, which is designed to display text information. These gadgets differ from tablets and netbooks in the duration of their work, as well as limited functionality. About what is an electronic book, in our time is already widely known. But these devices appeared in 1998 and were equipped with LCD screens, but did not receive wide popularity. E-Inc technology has breathed new life into book readers. It significantly reduced the energy consumption of devices and improved the quality of the text on the screen. Since that time, sales of electronic reading devices have increased, and manufacturers are not limited to the ability to display text files.

What is a modern e-book? These are unique technologies that allow to convey the highest quality reality of a paper page. The paper-like display does not blink, so reading books from the device is as convenient as possible. One battery charge is enough to read 10 thousand pages. If you want to read a book together, the wide reading angle allows you to do this also without stress. Paper-like e-books will allow you to forget about charging the battery, so you can safely take the device with you on the go.





The e-book library today is very large. To make it convenient to read them, the e-book display is better to choose 6-6.4 inches. As for the built-in memory, here, as they say, the more the better, however, chasing gigabytes in this case is not worth it. If you want to buy an e-book only for reading texts, then, for example, 100-500 books will occupy 512 MB of memory. In addition, e-books have slots for memory cards SD and microSD. Some models allow you to increase memory up to 16 GB or more. In addition, mp3s are supported in modern e-books , so you can easily listen to music or audio books. Also, the device can be used to view photos in jpg format.





Softbook Press and NuvoMedia first released the e-book. Today, these devices are no longer on the market, and they were replaced by Kindle models with an E-Inc screen, Sony, which has already released a dozen different models, and in the middle price category you will find PocketBook, Wexler and TeXet.

What is a modern day e-book? This is a device that is gradually approaching the tablet in terms of functionality. They use operating systems, for example, Linux. So reading texts is only one of their many functions that an electronic book can perform. The price of such devices, of course, is much higher than conventional devices for displaying text.
