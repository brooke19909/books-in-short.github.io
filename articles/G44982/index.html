Knitting cap: diagram, description. Knitting hats

In recent years, the leader in fashion trends have become knitwear. They can be worn by women, men and, of course, children. If necessary, you can purchase the desired product in the store or in the market. However, many women prefer to create such things with their own hands. At the same time, they save money and invest their love in the product.





What to bind?

A homemade product from yarn will be an excellent gift option for any celebration. You can present a hat, scarf, mittens, socks or a sweater to almost everyone. Such a present can rightly be called priceless, because it was created with love.

If you do not have the patience to knit large and voluminous work, then select a small and simple thing to start with. One of the popular activities for needlewomen is knitting hats with knitting needles. The schemes, description and final result will depend on who the model is created for. So, let's get to work.

Selection of tools required for work

Before you start knitting hats with knitting needles, patterns and description should be selected accordingly. Decide for whom the model will be made. If you knit a baby hat, then give preference to soft, airy yarn. For men, you can choose thicker and thicker threads. Women's hat can be created from any material.

After acquiring yarn, you need to choose the appropriate knitting needles. Remember that they should not be much thicker than the threads. Otherwise, the product loops will not fit snugly together. An ideal option would be tools of the same thickness or thinner than the working thread.





Knitting cap: scheme

When creating a product, you must proceed step by step. You will get a good result only if you follow all the instructions. Regardless of which model of headgear you are making, you must first knit the test piece. This is the only way to correctly calculate all sizes and tie the perfect thing.

Knitting a probe and sizing

So, you have decided what kind of hat will be made with knitting needles. The outline of the headgear must be memorized by you, otherwise you will need a description with sketched fragments.

Pick up fifteen loops on the tools. Knit fifteen rows of the selected pattern. Close the product and carry out calculations. First you need to measure the created probe and calculate how many loops and rows are contained in one square centimeter. Next, measure the head circumference of the person for whom the product will be created. Using a school math course, count the volume of the caps in the loops. You will also need to find out what is the approximate height of the finished product. To do this, measure the head of a person from the forehead to the crown. Remember that this is the minimum numerical value. More work is needed in the work of the ranks. When all the calculations are completed, you can proceed to the set of loops for knitting.





Women's knitting hat: scheme

For women, the so-called caps-hoods have recently become very popular. Such products are also called "wide snud." They look like ordinary large scarves, but if necessary, they can be worn on the head and used as a hat.

In order for such a hat to be knitted with knitting needles (female), the layout of the set of calculated loops should be slightly changed. Knitting of the product begins with the neck, which is why you need to put on the knitting needles the number of loops equal to the girth of the neck. Remember that such a wide hat should lie on your shoulders, and not tighten the throat.

Dial the desired number of loops on the tools. In order to avoid seams on the product and make it as accurately as possible, give preference to circular knitting needles. Start knitting a hat in a circle using a matched pattern. If you are a beginner needlewoman, then prefer a handkerchief. To do this, knit one row of the neck with facial loops, and the next - purl. Stick to this pattern until the end of knitting.





Tie a neck length of 15 to 20 centimeters. After this, proceed to create an immediate area for the head. Mark the middle of the product and in each front row knit two loops in this area together. After a few closed loops, a hat with knitting needles for women will begin to emerge. The knitting pattern of the joint loops must be observed until the very end of knitting. When you have made the required cap size, you can begin to close the circular loops. Do not overtighten the thread. You need a free edge that will naturally hang from your head. After completing work, hide the remaining length of thread in the product.

Children's headdress model

In a slightly different way, a children's hat can be knitted. Product layout may be of different options. You can work with ordinary double tools or give preference to circular or stocking knitting needles.





Headpiece with ears

A knitting cap, the scheme of which provides for the creation of small ears, fits quite simply. Dial the number of loops equal to the volume of the child‚Äôs head. After that, knit five centimeters with a double elastic band. You need to knit two loops wrong side, and the next two - facial. After creating the elastic, proceed to knitting the canvas. Here you can choose any pattern, but novice needlewomen should give preference to shawl, front or wrong side. When knitting the front fabric you will get a double-sided product. Which side to wear the hat, you and the baby will decide.

Knit the product as high as the area from the forehead to the crown. After this, it is necessary to knit a few more rows so that the cap sits freely. Next, close all working loops, leaving the thread in a free state, and assemble the product.

In order to get a flat hat with knitting needles, the assembly scheme should be as follows: first, a vertical seam is performed (from the beginning of the elastic to the top of the product), then the horizontal region of the crown is sewn.

Straighten the resulting corners so that they stick out with the point up. These will be the ears of the cap. After the child puts on a hat, they will look strictly up.





With pompom

A knitting hat for a girl whose knitting scheme requires the use of circular or stocking tools is made so that she fits her head tightly. Therefore, give preference to the softest yarn, which will not rub and cause itching.

Dial the number of loops on the instruments, which is equal to the girth of the head. After that, knit the product with a single elastic band. Knit one loop on the wrong side and the next one on the front. In this way, tie five centimeters of the canvas, and then go to the garter stitch. For it, you need to knit one row with facial loops, and the next - purl.

When you tie another ten centimeters of the product, start reducing the loops. This must be done as evenly as possible. Knit two stitches together every five stitches. The number of skipped stitches can vary depending on how many were originally knitted. Reduce every two rows. When there are no stitches left, knit the two together and complete the job.

After that, you need to sew on the pompom. It can be purchased in specialized stores for needlewomen or made with your own hands from the same yarn that connected the product. Recently, fur pompons have been very popular. They look rich and feminine. Therefore, you can give preference to this particular option. Gently sew the accessory by turning the cap inside out. Immediately after this, you can start using the headgear for its intended purpose.





Finally

When you create a knitwear, remember that when washing the yarn can sit a little. That is why it is better to make a hat a little larger than then to be upset that it is small. Wash the hat with hands only. Use special detergents to clean wool. In this case, the hat will serve you more than one season and will delight you with its rich and vibrant color.

Knit with pleasure for yourself, your children, relatives, relatives and friends. Please them, and they will appreciate your work and remain grateful.
