IPad evolution from first to last

The evolution of the iPad affects the beginning of the 21st century. In the mid-zero, Steve Jobs introduced the new product - the first tablet in his company, which was based on the iOS operating system. The idea was to make the buttons virtual, located on the sensor itself. The glass multitouch made a splash - a small square machine made it possible to print, view images, and videos.

The prototype with the inertial scrolling function prompted the creator to introduce the function of dialing a phone number, but the idea was postponed until better times. They returned to her only after the release of the iPhone. And the project of the ingenious device was implemented a few years later. Now we can observe the evolution of the iPad from the first to the last generation.

Apple's pioneer in nanotechnology

Only in 2010, the tablet was presented to the world from the apple leader. First-generation iPad devices were different from tablets on the electronics market. They were compared to iPod Touch, which was based on the work of steroids. The same design, the same functions, but completely new goals and opportunities. It was possible to taste the highlight only after the start of use:

  1. This type of technology stood between the rows of smartphones and computers.
  2. The "tablet" had a diagonal of 9.7 inches.
  3. Resolution reached 1024 x 768 pixels.
  4. Performance was carried out due to the A4 processor with a frequency of 1000 MHz.
  5. RAM is only 256 MB.
  6. The modules were a choice - either Wi-Fi, or Wi-Fi- and 3G-module.

But the built-in memory could already be calculated in the range from 16 to 64 GB. Bill Gates commented on the release of technology:

This is a good reader of books without a stylus and keyboard, and it is hardly possible to use it fully.

However, the first iPad was a hit. They bought it no worse than the iPhone.

A powerful analogue of the third tablet model: better, faster, smarter

Six months later, the developers introduced a new model. This is the fourth-generation iPad, dubbed the richest epithet. In 2012, the demand for the purchase rose to 87%, which indicated the success of Apple. Brief characteristics:

  • Dual core A6X processor.
  • The frequency reached 1.4 GHz.
  • The front camera has acquired a resolution of 1.2 megapixels.
  • The range of network support has grown to 4G.
  • A new Lightning connector has appeared - now on all devices instead of the 30-pin standard.

Before the new year, the company sold all tablet models, and in early January 2013 a fourth tablet model with an internal memory size of up to 128 GB was on sale.

Reduced version of the fourth tablet: 2012 makes changes

Along with the release of the fourth model, Apple reviewed the iPad in a smaller version. A more compact, convenient and miniature powerful computer, about which there were rumors back in the previous year. Apple iPad mini - the so-called "little brother" of the fourth tablet:

  • The diagonal was only 7.9 inches.
  • The lateral interval of the frames narrowed to the possibility of wrapping it around with one hand.
  • The display remained "old", without Retina technology.
  • Resolution, like the first tablet.

The model was identical in characteristics to the penultimate model, the second, only the size was slightly reduced. Demand for it was scanty, which is why sales fell to 78%.

The prefix Air was chosen for a reason. The manufacturer emphasized the lightness of the new tablet, focusing on functions and performance that did not suffer, despite the similarities with the miniature version of the four:

  • The flagship iPad in 2013 became thinner by 2 mm.
  • The dimensions of the iPad Air were 16.2 mm - this is 20% less than the previous one.

Second Generation Mini Generation

Together with the Air-tablet released the same year the second version of the Mini model. It should have arranged users more than the first miniature device. The changes have been significant:

  • The design has remained unchanged, but the functions are completely improved.
  • The diagonal is reduced to 7.9 inches.
  • The filling of the equipment is identical except for the processor - performance is slower at 100 MHz.

Why it was necessary to reduce the already not impressive functions of the "little brother" is unclear. However, the purchase went quite sharply - more than 90% of sales were realized in the first quarter of the year. As it turned out later, the price fell significantly, which allowed some segments of the population to please themselves with an apple suite.
