Cyprus is an island full of ancient legends. Paphos - a treasury of unique attractions

Many tourists know about the Republic of Cyprus, shrouded in legends. We can say that this is one of the most popular destinations, and a comfortable climate and an abundance of attractions makes the resort attractive in the eyes of foreigners who dream of a wonderful vacation. Every year, hundreds of thousands of travelers rush to a hospitable exotic island.

Paphos, located on the Mediterranean coast, is considered the historical capital of Cyprus, consisting of an officially recognized Greek part and unrecognized Turkish.

Climate and weather

The ancient city offers a lot of entertainment for everyone arriving for a good mood. The UNESCO-protected tourist center welcomes guests year-round, but the high season lasts from June to September. It was at this time that the average air temperature reached 27 degrees.



And in winter, the resort, immersed in silence, offers hotel rooms at several times reduced prices. The humid climate, characteristic only for January, is not an obstacle for those who wish to touch Greek history. By the way, the weather often pleases with sunny days and warm temperatures, reaching 17 degrees, so we can say that a completely new format of relaxation begins for tourists who come to have fun on the island in winter.

Paphos is loved for the long-lasting swimming season, which begins in May and lasts until October, and sometimes guests take water baths in November.

Tourist centre

The city with developed infrastructure has long established itself as a popular tourist destination. At the resort, offering its guests various apartments for every budget and taste, everyone will find accommodation. Here you can book luxury villas with sea views, pools, spa, and cheap apartments, not inferior in quality of service, which is famous for the island attractive for tourists.



Paphos: hotels

Among the four-star hotels, the most popular are Cyprotel Cypria Maris, Aliathon Holiday Village, Amphora Hotel & Suites, Avlida Hotel. Located on the seafront and designed for a relaxing holiday, they are waiting for foreign travelers to offer them the best. The staff who knows the Russian language will help to understand all the intricacies and tell about interesting places that the colorful island is famous all over the world. Paphos is a real storehouse of man-made and natural monuments, and all tourists note that they spent their holidays not only on the beaches, but also saw unique corners that have no equal in beauty.

Homeland of Aphrodite

It is not in vain that the ancient city is called an open museum, because cultural and historical treasures cannot be counted here. The resort center is not only white beaches and nightly entertainment, but also a huge number of attractions that make Paphos one of a kind.



The rich cultural heritage and varied excursion program attract all travelers who choose a picturesque island as their vacation spot. Paphos is considered the birthplace of Aphrodite, and its most beautiful corners are connected with the goddess.

The bay, where, according to legend, the ever-young daughter of the great Zeus came out of the foam, is the most popular place for city guests, and it seems that the local landscape has not changed for several centuries, and the grotto, in which the lady took baths, is amazingly beautiful. Tourists seek to swim on the beach next to a natural attraction in order to get a gift from the beautiful goddess - beauty and youth.

Ancient burial places

An interesting excursion to the tombs, in which local aristocrats were buried, will appeal to connoisseurs of ancient history. Burials made before our era are hollowed out right in the hill and look like underground chambers. Because of their special luxury, they were called royal. Spacious halls decorated with carved columns and frescoes, the legendary island is incredibly proud. Paphos (Cyprus) takes care of preserving for the descendants of the world's oldest tombs. Now archaeological work is being carried out, bringing annually new finds.



Ancient monuments, natural attractions, comfortable hotels, hospitality of local residents - these are good reasons to visit the island of Cyprus. The city of Paphos awaits guests and offers an interesting program for adults and children.
