The best hotels of Beloretsk

The bases and rest houses in each city are universal, they can differ in exclusive cuisine, elite design, availability of luxury rooms and other features. If we take, for example, the Beloretsk hotel, we can distinguish several similar signs: homely and cozy atmosphere, affordable prices for room rental and a huge amount of vegetation around the building. Friendly service is also highlighted separately, which allows each visitor to feel at home.

A bit about the city





Beloretsk is a rather small but incredibly beautiful city. Today its number is a little less than 70 thousand people. Most of the population here is occupied by Russian people, Bashkirs, Tatars and people of other nationalities also prevail. The city is located in the Republic of Bashkortostan. There are several reasons to visit this unique place:

  • Unbelievably beautiful nature prevails here, saturated with the most unusual vegetation. In the Iremel nature park, you can conquer an incredibly high peak, in the Kapova cave you can enjoy the mystery of the underworld, and in the territory of the Mratkino ski complex you can see how fabulously snow shimmers in the sun.




  • Many people come to this place in order to take a picture with the sculpture "Elk", visit the country's best museum of local lore or see the old water tower.




  • In addition to all the beauties, one can also note a good entertainment service: a spacious cinema "Metallurg", an art gallery, a sauna and much more.

All this is an occasion to visit this unique city. For residents of the farthest points of the country there is an opportunity to stay here for a long time, Beloretsk hotels are distinguished by their hospitality.

Mratkino





The best, but at the same time as affordable as possible, is the complex designed for recreation, "Mratkino" (Beloretsk). The hotel includes more than 20 rooms, which can accommodate up to 48 people at a time.

The most affordable are standard double rooms, the rental price of which is only 700 rubles per person per day. The room is equipped with a large mirror, two single beds, a refrigerator, a TV, a ventilation system, a bathroom, a kettle and the necessary utensils.





The most expensive is a two-room quintuple room that is additionally equipped with a modern shower, soundproof windows and a wardrobe. It should also be noted and the excellent view from the window on the slope. The approximate cost of renting a room is 4.5 thousand rubles per day.

In addition, this hotel is equipped with free parking, good repair and all the most necessary for a full stay. G. Beloretsk invites everyone to visit this complex. Room reservation is carried out in just a few minutes, and for families with children discounts are provided.

"Manor"





A huge number of people who visited the city note that the best place to stay here is the Usadba hotel. Beloretsk cannot boast of another institution in which the same number of different services would be concentrated.

First of all, it is worth noting that the hotel is located in the very center of the city. Having settled in it, you can enjoy all the sights as much as possible.

On the territory there are two food blocks, in one of them you can enjoy the original cuisine, and in the other, try gourmet drinks. All guests are provided with free TV, Internet access and comfortable rooms, the rental price of which is from 1 to 7 thousand rubles. It also provides secure parking, billiards and a pool.

"Banker"





Some hotels have the lowest prices. For example, the Bankir hotel complex belongs to such. It, like all Beloretsk (Bashkiria) hotels, is equipped with a modern bathroom, satellite TV and Internet access.

The hotel is located at a distance of only 20 kilometers from the city center, near it there is a large parking lot, a children's playground and an elite entertainment center. The complex has several different rooms that can be rented at the lowest prices - for only 500 rubles per day.

"Cottage"





Not all people are satisfied with Beloretsk hotels, someone likes to be as isolated as possible from nature and enjoy their vacation. For such visitors, an ideal option is the guest house "Cottage", which is located near the ski resort.

At the same time, up to 25 people can relax in this complex. It is equipped with a swimming pool, a bathhouse, several cozy bedrooms, a hotel and a spacious dining room.

The approximate rental cost is 12 thousand rubles per day. This is a great option for those who want to fully relax with a large company.

The recreation center "Zakharovo Lake"





The recreation center is famous for its private beach, where visitors can sunbathe in the warm season. The complex has several spacious houses with different capacities. They can accommodate from three to fifteen people. Each of them is equipped with satellite TV, wireless Internet, a bedroom, a kitchen, a living room, a shower, a bathroom, necessary furniture, utensils and household appliances.

In addition, there is a bathhouse on the territory of the center, the necessary equipment for fishing, as well as a barbecue. The approximate cost of renting a house is from 3 to 10 thousand rubles per day.

Beloretsk hotels are the best place for a good rest at the most affordable prices.
