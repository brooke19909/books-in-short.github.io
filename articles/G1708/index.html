Types of rest

Almost all recreation centers in Bashkiria are open all year round. Given the unique climatic conditions and a variety of landscapes, tourist agencies can offer a huge number of different types of pastimes.

Recreation facilities in Bashkiria in the summer are:

  • river rafting;
  • fishing and swimming in rivers and lakes;
  • horse riding;
  • all kinds of water activities: boats, yachting, jet skis, etc .;
  • hunting.

In winter, recreation centers of Bashkiria provide their guests with:

  • skating;
  • ski slopes;
  • ski walks;
  • winter fishing;
  • snowmobiles.

Here are possible annually:

  • bus excursions with the study of the cultural heritage and history of this region;
  • hiking in caves and mountains;
  • corporate vacation.




Recreation centers in Bashkiria are actively developing the entertainment industry. Here it flourishes, while the range of offers is expanding rapidly. At the moment, tourists are offered excellent rooms of various categories, high-quality service and wonderful infrastructure with a huge selection of different entertainments.

At recreation centers prices are reasonable. They are designed for everyone. Almost all recreation centers have bars, saunas, conference rooms, billiards are working, discos are held, there are bars, gyms, massage rooms.

The places where the recreation centers of Bashkiria are located deserve a separate word. Most of them are located on the banks of the Nurgush reservoir, picturesque lakes, the rest - in the mountains and along the banks of the rivers. Many are in unique places. Excellent ecology, a remote location from settlements and a climate that has a beneficial effect on the state of health, make staying here a real pleasure.

Rest can then be called successful when it was carried out with full dedication: a charge of vivacity and positive was received, various sports were mastered, while the time passed with benefit for the body and soul. All this will be presented to you by the sanatoriums and recreation centers of Bashkiria.

Agidel Kaga

Unforgettable and picturesque open spaces of Bashkiria mountain. It is in this place, among the mountainous peaks, in the Ural centuries-old taiga that the base "Agidel" is located. This is an island of peace, relaxation and silence, remote from the bustling and noisy civilization.

Only the sound of mountain streams and the many-voiced bird choir breaks the silence. Those who want to hide from the hustle and bustle of the cities and go fishing in Agidel will certainly find what they have been looking for.
