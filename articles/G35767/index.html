How to make a mini reborn? Do-it-yourself master class on creating the head and face of a mini reborn

Mini reborn is a mini version of dolls for girls. We are all familiar with Barbie or Bratz dolls, but mini reborn is a completely different kind of doll. These are small newborn babies. They are portrayed in those poses in which children most often lie, sit or sleep. Each wrinkle and part of the baby's body is so accurately and reliably transmitted in a small chrysalis doll that there is a slight embarrassment from almost one hundred percent similarity with a real baby. How to make a mini reborn? Such kids are created by professionals, special clothes are sewn for them. But every lover of the art of modeling clay or plasticine can take on the creation of such a cute mini reborn.

The first stage of the mini-reborn workshop

How to make a mini reborn with your own hands? For this not-so-easy matter, tools are needed that closely resemble the accessories of any dentist. But the main component of all the work is polymer clay, from which a small child will be created. The tools needed to create are shown in the photo.

So, polymer clay is ready, the tools are laid out. Now we proceed to the sequential creation of each part of the body of the mini-reborn.

How to make a mini reborn? Execution order (second stage of the master class)

First of all, we plant a small piece of clay on the fourth tool (navigate in the photo above). From it we will form the head of the baby. We remove all irregularities with our fingers, creating a kind of future skull from a piece of clay. We check from all sides for irregularities and put in order. The penultimate tool (found in the photo above) will help us in creating a smooth surface.

Next, the latest tool is used. We turn the future head facing us and outline with dashes the location of the eyes, nose and mouth. We tear off a small piece from clay and place it on the marked spot on the nose. In the best formation, the second tool will help him (search in the photo above). Using the same tool we create recesses for the eyes. We tear off three more pieces from our main piece of clay, make small pancakes from two and stick to the place of the cheeks. The remaining piece should be of a longitudinal form, which will go in place of the future forehead. Using the second and penultimate tools, we smooth out all the irregularities and give the face smooth, chubby features. Now you know how to make a mini reborn, or rather, his face.
