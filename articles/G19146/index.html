Turkey is a country that is especially popular with our compatriots. This place is famous for its clean beaches, luxurious resorts, rich history and relatively affordable prices. Where is the hotel located?





The large hotel complex Cesars Resort Side 5 is located on the picturesque shores of the Mediterranean Sea, almost in the center of the resort village of Kumkey. About 13 kilometers away is another small town, Manavgat, which is worth a visit if only to look at the waterfall. Near the hotel there are shops, shopping centers, as well as various historical attractions, including the Temple of Apollo and the ancient part of Side.

The nearest international airport is 65 kilometers away. The hotel, as well as travel agencies, provides a transfer to your destination, so you do not have to worry about this.

The scheme of food on site





Like most hotels in Turkey, Cesar Resort Side operates on an all-inclusive basis. This means that the price includes full three meals a day, as well as light snacks throughout the day, cooling drinks and alcohol from a Turkish manufacturer.

Where is the beach located?





Today, many beach vacation fans prefer Side as their place of vacation. Cesars Resort Side is located on the first coastline. The hotel owns its own section of the beach, which is adjacent to the territory - you can get there in a few minutes walk. The beach here is clean, well-groomed, covered with soft sand.

The entrance to the sea is wide and convenient. Despite the fact that the bottom is covered with sand, it is worth getting appropriate shoes for swimming. There is a large shallow area, which is perfect for bathing children - it is safe here, and the water is always well warmed, warm.

There are sun loungers and umbrellas on the shore, which, by the way, are always enough here, even during periods of maximum occupancy at Cesar Resort Side. Their guests can use for free. Here, on the shore, you can get a clean beach towel.
