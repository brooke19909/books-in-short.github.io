We knit a scarf on our own

A scarf is a unique element of any wardrobe. It not only allows us to warm ourselves in bad weather, but also performs an important aesthetic function. With it, you can make your image complete by placing important accents. That is why when we knit a scarf, we begin to plan from the beginning, with what we will wear it and in what weather. This allows you to determine not only the color of the yarn, but also its composition.



If we knit a scarf for the summer season, then, most likely, we will give preference to thin threads. Manufacturers offer a wide range of monotonous or melange yarn of high quality. We can choose, for example, threads with lurex, which can be located not only along the edge of the material, but also inside it. In this case, a final decision is necessary, being guided solely by your personal preferences.

You will also need to select the appropriate pattern. Given that it is warm enough in the summer and this product is more likely to perform a decorative function rather than warm it, it is quite possible to make it openwork using a crochet hook. This knitting technique allows you to get a product not only of a rectangular shape. If the work is carried out in a circle, having previously connected a chain of air loops, the length of which is slightly less than desired, then you can make a scarf with a beautiful openwork edge.





Also, this effect can be achieved using yarn of appropriate quality. Recently, special threads or ribbons for ruffles have been especially popular. In this case, the desired product can be manufactured quickly enough. Experienced needlewomen cope with such work within half an hour.

If we knit scarves with knitting needles, we can count on the finished product to be quite dense and warm. Especially if we were able to pick up a woolen and fairly thick yarn.

Knit a scarf

If you can not decide on a model, then be sure to visit the thematic forums where needlewomen who knit together are traditionally going to. Especially such "part-timers" will be relevant if you have preferred yarn that you have not previously used in your work. Among the participants there will certainly be one that actively works with her, and therefore will be able to suggest nuances and note features.





If we knit a scarf for a child, then we can make it not quite a standard shape. A product made in the shape of some kind of animal or decorated in a special way will look especially interesting and funny.

If we knit a men's scarf, then when choosing a yarn color, it is necessary to take into account the opinion of its future owner. Do not think that if this product is intended for a representative of the stronger half, then it must be made in gray-black-white colors. Many young people prefer brighter colors.

When making a product for a woman, you can not really burden yourself with the choice of colors. Each lady will certainly be able to find a suitable outfit with which she will wear it, as well as a reason to tie it. However, it is still better to refuse dull yarn.
