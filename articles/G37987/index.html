Hotel Merperle Hon Tam Resort 5 * (Vietnam / Nha Trang): description and reviews

For those tourists who love beach and sea resorts and dream of relaxing on vacation from the noise of megalopolises and everyday problems, the exotic island of Hong Tam in Vietnam can be the ideal place. It has long attracted people with silence and amazing landscapes. In our article we want to talk about one of the resort hotels - Merperle Hon Tam Resort.

A bit about the hotel

Vietnam is known to tourists from all over the world as a place of budget vacation. However, besides the mainland resorts, the country is rich in exotic islands, on which numerous hotels are built that offer rest in the "bounty" style. One of such places is the island of Hon Tam, located next to the well-known resort of Nha Trang.

Merperle Hon Tam Resort is a small hotel located on the beach. The complex consists of bungalows surrounded by a green garden. It was built in 2008 and consists of 49 rooms.



The direct distance from the airport to the island is 20 kilometers. The hotel will appeal to those tourists who prefer a relaxing holiday on the sea coast away from the hustle and bustle. Despite the fact that the complex is located on an island, not far from it are the most interesting sights of Nha Trang, which we will talk about later.

Number of rooms

Merperle Hon Tam Resort offers its guests rooms in bungalows. All apartments have balconies or terraces. Among them there are numbers of the following categories:

  1. Deluxe Bungalow - Deluxe Bungalow with a double bed (area 52 sq. M.). The apartments are equipped with satellite channels and a flat-screen TV, refrigerator, safe, wireless internet, fan, air conditioning, minibar, safe, bath accessories, and a set for ironing.
  2. Deluxe Bungalow with Two Single Beds. These rooms do not differ in equipment from the previous category of rooms.
  3. Family Suite - family suite (area 64 sq. M.). If the first two categories of rooms are designed to accommodate no more than two people, then three guests can be accommodated in family suites. This apartment has one large double bed and one single. The rest of the room equipment is no different.

All apartments overlook the ocean, as the bungalows face the coast. The cottages are arranged in several rows, the closest are almost on the beach, the rest are in the garden. Therefore, guests have the opportunity to choose the appropriate accommodation option.

Additional privileges for children

Merperle Hon Tam Resort provides a number of privileges for the youngest guests:

  1. Any room can accommodate no more than two children, whose age does not exceed 12 years.
  2. Kids who are not yet five years old can stay for free, provided that they will not be provided with a separate bed.
  3. When settling at the reception for children from the age of five to twelve years, a breakfast fee is charged (this is done directly at the hotel, and not when booking rooms).
  4. Children over 12 years old are considered adults, and therefore their accommodation and meals are paid in full.
  5. If you need to install extra beds, you should contact the reception. The cost of the service depends on the category of apartments.

Catering

Merperle Hon Tam Resort 5 * offers food in the main and only restaurant. The price includes breakfast only. However, if you wish, you can arrange full board upon arrival. In the mornings, food is served as a buffet. The rest of the time, you can order dishes on the menu. If you wish, your portion can be brought to your room, and no one takes delivery charge.



Many operators offer New Year tours in Merperle Xon Tam Resort 5 *. The peculiarity of such trips is that from the cold winter you find yourself in amazingly warm weather on the ocean. On New Year's Eve, the hotel organizes a festive banquet, the cost of which is included in the price of the ticket. For this reason, New Year's tours are more expensive than ordinary. In general, such trips are very popular among our tourists.

Hotel infrastructure

Merperle Hon Tam Resort (Vietnam) has a very beautiful and well-groomed territory, on which numerous exotic trees and bushes grow. In addition, there is an incredible number of flowering flower beds along the paths. It can be seen that the hotel staff carefully monitors the cleanliness and order, not forgetting to care for the plants. The territory is so well-groomed that it is pleasant to walk along it in the shade of tropical plants.

The complex has a very large swimming pool, which is divided into separate zones of different depths. There is also a children's section. The pond is beautifully decorated with lush vegetation, harmoniously fits into the overall composition.

Merperle Xon Tam Resort (Vietnam) has a gift shop, tennis court and spa. You can move around the hotel in tuk-tuk if you do not want to walk. However, the road in any direction does not exceed 200 meters.

Despite the fact that the hotel is located on an island, tourists always have the opportunity to go to Nha Trang for shopping or for a walk. Bots are sent to the coast every hour, so there are no problems with transport.

Guests can play billiards on site, while kids can play in the games room.

Beach vacation

Merperle Hon Tam Resort 5 * (Vietnam) is located on the coast. Therefore, the main advantage of the hotel is the fact that during the whole vacation you are at the sea and admire its beauty. The large and wide beach is equipped with numerous sunbeds of various kinds. There are so many of them on the coast that you can find free space at any time. Beach towels are issued at the reception in unlimited quantities.

A little bit about the resort ...

If you have chosen the Merperle Hon Tam Resort hotel in Nha Trang, then it will not hurt you to familiarize yourself with the resort in advance. Hon Tam Island is just 4 kilometers from the coast. It is not difficult to get here, since bots ply on a strict schedule. The island has the best conditions for relaxing on snow-white beaches. Many tourists vacationing in Nha Trang come here to go diving.

Despite the fact that rest on the island is calm, even here tourists can find suitable entertainment for themselves. It offers parasailing and paragliding, the most courageous ones can try their hand at mountain climbing, as well as go windsurfing, diving, ride a motorcycle or ski.



Guests can enjoy a visit to the ethnic village. Her visit will allow you to get acquainted with the culture and customs of the people. The village was recreated in full accordance with the traditions that local residents adhered to when building their dwellings. Here you can look at the elements of household utensils, clothes and musical instruments. Numerous shops are located near the village, offering tourists to buy traditional Vietnamese souvenirs.

However, experienced vacationers recommend not limited to viewing only the village, but are advised to go to the mainland in order to be able to see the most interesting places of the resort.

Local Attractions

The main attraction of Nha Trang can be considered the towers of Nagar. Buddhist temples are well preserved to this day, despite the fact that they are more than a thousand years old. Once the towers were part of a large temple complex, which was built in 7-12 centuries BC. e. The Vietnamese endow this place with special spiritual strength. It is for this reason that Buddhist ceremonies and other ritual ceremonies are held here.

Numerous tourists come to Nha Trang not only in search of a beach holiday, but also for recovery. On the outskirts of the resort is the famous Thap Ba mud bath. The wellness center has a stunningly beautiful area, surrounded by flowers and tropical vegetation. Here visitors can plunge into the waters of the mineral spring, swim in the pools, plunge into the healing mud baths. After all the wellness treatments, visitors have the opportunity to enjoy delicious dishes of European and Vietnamese cuisines. There are shops in the center where you can buy not only souvenirs, but also dirt and local cosmetics.

The most popular among tourists is the amusement park "Winperl", located on the island of Hong Che. The huge tourist complex includes a chain of hotels, restaurants, shopping centers, spas and all kinds of entertainment. Winperl is the largest and best entertainment center in Vietnam, which has no analogues in the country. Tourists who visited the island are waiting for all sorts of entertainment. The vast territory of the park is divided into zones. There is a water park, an amphitheater, a shopping center, an amusement park, a playground and much more. In addition, guests of the island can visit several cinemas, karaoke, children's attractions.

One of the most interesting places in the resort can be considered Monkey Island, located 20 kilometers from Nha Trang. Currently, about 1,500 monkeys live on it. It will be interesting not only to children, but also adults to see these cute animals.



The Institute of Oceanography is one of those places worth showing to children. Under the roof of the institution currently is not only an oceanographic research center, but also a large aquarium, as well as a huge skeleton of a real whale. The institute began to work in 1922, during its activities in the study of the South China Sea and other seas and oceans, a large collection of exhibits was collected, which became the basis for the creation of the aquarium. Back in 2005, the research center received the status of a cultural and historical landmark, so now it is open for tourists.

No less interesting for visitors is the Chi Nguyen Aquarium. Locals call it Neptune's Palace. This establishment owes its appearance to one local fisherman, who treated him with an unlimited love of nature. He decided to independently create a maritime museum.It is noteworthy that almost all the money spent on the construction and opening of the institution was his own means. With the help of builders, a large pond was created for marine inhabitants. Currently, guests from different countries visit the aquarium to see the results of many years of work of an amazing person who devoted his dream to many years.
