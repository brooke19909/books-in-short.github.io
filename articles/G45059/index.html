Hotel Rainbow Castle 3 * Hotel (Turkey, Side): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Cute family-run economy hotel Rainbow Castle Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Side) is decorated very creatively. It was as if he descended to the earth from some vivid childhood fairy-tale dream. Indeed, why in our everyday life are houses and, consequently, cities, for the most part, dull and monochromatic? Is that why their people are often confused and alarmed, and smiles are a rare guest on their face? In a word, if you decided to spend your family vacation with children in a reasonable price for the city of Side, known not only for its chic Mediterranean holidays, but also as a museum city, then Rainbow Castle 3 * hotel will be a good option for this.





The average price of the tour (including flight and transfer) for one person staying in a double room of this hotel for a period of 7 nights is 18,273 rubles. The hotel, which has one of the best price / quality indicators in its business niche, offers its guests sound resort services.

Location

An hour by car (68 km) from Antalya Airport, and newly arrived tourists get to the hotel complex described in this article. Check-in occurs almost immediately upon arrival. The resort where the Rainbow 3 * hotel is located is overpopulated. The reason for this is a modern, developed and diverse resort infrastructure, the prosperity of which is promoted by a large investment program that stimulates the development of hotels, restaurants, and entertainment complexes.

The hotel complex is built in the second line, 400 meters from the local hospitable sandy Mediterranean beaches.

It’s not very long to go to the center of Side from the Rainbow Castle 3 * hotel (distance - 3 km), best of all - just along the promenade. According to reviews, this is how tourists, residents of the hotel, got to the city. They liked the embankment: paved with original tiles made of small pebbles, it was as if created for a walk.

The best season for your holiday in Side

For those who buy a tour to the Rainbow Castle Hotel 3 * (Turkey Side), an important factor is the consideration of climatic features. The tourist season of the Side resort begins in late April, when the water warms up to +19 0 and ends in mid-November, when its temperature is 22-23 0 . There are a lot of vacationers in the month of May, when the water temperature is 21-22 0 .

In the high holiday season (it occurs in July-August), the water temperature is 28-29 0 C. At this time, mainly residents of Western Europe and Turkey itself rest here. The velvet season in Side falls in November. It is optimal for residents of temperate latitudes, who finally can safely stay for a long time, sunbathing and not burning, under the rays of the Turkish sun.

Fans of excursions who want to see the evidence of ancient millennial civilizations in the high holiday season are at risk of sunburn. For this, April, May, the second half of October and November are optimal.

Beach vacation

Rainbow Castle 3 * (Side, Turkey) also has its own small beach, it is called "Dolphin". It can be reached by a fun, fun-filled hotel bus. There is nice sand, the water is clean, there are a lot of fish in it.




However, its guests successfully use a variety of beaches in the resort town of Side. Generally speaking, from the hotel to the nearest Fantasy beach, go 5-7 minutes. It is very suitable for bathing children. Resorts can use a special underground passage with a ramp to go to it across the road.
Based on the recommendations of colleagues who have already rested here, it is advisable for newly arrived beach people to visit the hotel hammam on the day of arrival. Open pores and cleansed, refreshed skin will be more ready for the healing effects of the local climate. Also, on the day of arrival (it’s not worth pulling), be sure to purchase a sunblock, they do not sell such an effective house. In doing so, pay close attention to the SPF sun protection index. It should be selected in the range of 50-70.

The beaches in the vicinity of the city are all sandy, cleanly furnished, equipped with sunbeds and umbrellas. Municipal beaches are always free, on public you should pay for a sun lounger and umbrella. The west coast of the city is more crowded, more lively, and its beaches are more comfortable. There are many restaurants, bars and cafes. With them there are small paid private beaches, the entrance to which costs $ 2, however, having bought ice cream for the same price, the spa man rests on them for free.
However, on the beaches of Side there is no surplus of holidaymakers, as in Kemer, therefore, Side's popularity has been growing recently.

The east coast relative to the museum city is more relaxed. Wild beaches are almost deserted. The water is clear, the sand is clean, the entrance to the sea is smooth. Rest here is calm, not for everybody.

Excursions

Hotel Rainbow Castle Hotel 3 * (Side) is a short walk from the ancient architectural monuments of Side. The beauty of such excursions is that they are free. No need to buy tickets, you can not hire a guide, but use the appropriate online information. After all, the city of Side has been an open-air museum for centuries. Here are the ruins of the Roman theater, the temple of Apollo, the agora, (the market square), the ruins of the Nymphaeum fountain, etc.

Shopping

Experienced guests of Rainbow Castle 3 (Side), those who arrive here regularly, as a rule, know a lot about shopping. After all, a developed infrastructure has been created for him here.

Their reviews contain a warning for tourists who have bought tours in the high holiday season in July-August. At this time, prices for fur coats, leather, etc. maximum and the buyer is quite difficult to negotiate a discount. The fact is that these months account for the maximum vacation of Europeans.

What Side stores are the most rated among buyers of economy tours? Obviously, those where there is no need to bore tediously: what Turkey is so famous for. Rainbow Castle Hotel 3 * is an excellent launching pad for shopping. Its guests on the first position in the ranking of trade institutions identified the LC Waikiki shopping center, located 7 kilometers from Side, in the city of Manavgat.
There the prices are fixed. The first floor sells women's clothing and shoes, the second - male, the third - children's. The goods are high-quality and inexpensive: the price of t-shirts - from 160 rubles., Sweaters and jeans - from 330 rubles. Many women dream of purchasing original gold jewelry in Turkey. A good choice is Cadaro Side Jewelery, located on Side Colonnade. If you liked the product, call the director, because only he has the authority to determine the size of the discount.





On Ataturk Boulevard (Side) there is a good leather and fur store Side Leder. You should bargain here. In this case, sometimes it is possible to halve the price.

To buy souvenirs, tourists visit the local eastern bazaar. Here you can bargain plenty, because the original price offered is usually inflated by sellers in 2 -2.5 times.

Conclusion

Having a rest here once, as a rule, they wish to relax in the Rainbow Castle 3 * the next season. Reviews about it indicate the real level of service 3+. Advantageous location, good living conditions, tasty and healthy food, and, of course, the emerald-clean gentle Mediterranean Sea with comfortable sandy beaches. The hotel is really good.

However, it is characterized by the common problem of all hotels in Turkey: at the end of the holiday season, when the flow of tourists is poor and few people rest in the hotel, the range of their meals is objectively reduced in proportion to how the income of hotel complexes decreases.

If you evaluate objectively, then the Rainbow 3 * hotel is a vivid example of the fact that there are still cases in the hotel business when the "troika" located in the heart of the resort looks preferable to the "five", which is out of the way.
