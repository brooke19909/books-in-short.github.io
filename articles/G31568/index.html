Electronic photo frame - a worthy replacement for a traditional photo album

Digital technology has firmly entered our lives. Now few people use ordinary film cameras. Too troublesome this matter is to charge the film, develop, print the pictures. Much easier to use a digital camera, especially since it is available on almost every modern phone. Having thus captured a lot of interesting moments, we are faced with a choice - where to put all this? To print in the nearest shop or on the printer, to put forever in a bulky dusty album or to store in electronic form, arranging for yourself periodic views using a computer? It is here that you will need such a beautiful modern device as an electronic photo frame.



Its convenience is obvious. Having small dimensions, it can store a large number of photos, for viewing which you can use several modes. In addition, it is very convenient to hold it in your hands or go with her on a visit to show friends or relatives the most significant moments of family life. An electronic photo frame can also be used as the most ordinary one, which is easy to hang on the wall to replace portraits or paintings.

When choosing a photo frame, be sure to pay attention to the amount of its memory and the ability to connect external media to it. The larger it is, the more photos you can upload there.

The modern electronic photo frame allows you to not only view pictures, but also acts as a small home theater or music player. And therefore, a device that, in addition to the graphic formats JPEG, GIF, BMP, TIFF supports AVI, MOV, WMA and MP3, will not be amiss. Also, the presence of a built-in clock, calendar and alarm clock does not hurt.
