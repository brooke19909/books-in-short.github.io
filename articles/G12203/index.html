How to choose an electronic piano? Brand, specifications, expert advice

Anyone who at least once thought about buying a piano, piano or other keyboard instrument, of course, understands that he will have to consider various options. What to give preference to? Today, among keyboards, electronic (or digital) piano holds a special place. It is a modern alternative to acoustic. In addition, the electronic piano is most adapted to our active urban lifestyle.

Benefits

An electronic digital piano is an excellent option for those who can not allocate space in the apartment for a bulky acoustic instrument. But small size is not everything. A digital musical instrument weighs quite a bit. In addition, there are models that can be folded and stored in a niche or in a closet, freeing up space in the room. This is especially true for owners of small apartments.





Another advantage of an electronic keyboard instrument is that it has a special headphone jack. This will allow you to make music, without interfering with the household and neighbors to relax after a hard day.

One of the advantages of the electronic piano is the lack of strings in its construction. This frees the owners from the need to search (several times a year) for the wizard to configure, as well as pay for its services.

For those who have already learned to play musical compositions, the electronic piano provides another interesting opportunity. The tool is connected to a computer on which the results of hard work can be recorded. These music files are later listened to.

Kinds

These tools are divided into two types. The first one is the digital piano. The second type of such instrument is a synthesizer. What are their main differences?
A digital piano is like an acoustic piano. It repeats the number and size, as well as the height of the keys of the classic instrument. The digital piano has pedals, as well as a cover with a music stand. But the most important thing is that the digital instrument has a balanced keyboard mechanics.

The synthesizer has smaller dimensions and fewer keys. This tool has a semi-weighted keyboard. It is equipped with many useful features and is very compact.



Which of the two instruments is recommended for those who are just mastering the piano technique? Of course, this should not be a synthesizer. The electronic piano is the instrument whose capabilities are as close as possible to the classical version. Of course, a synthesizer will become a more convenient tool for members of a musical group or lovers of arrangements.

Keys

How to choose an electronic piano so that a reasonable ratio of the price and quality of the goods is observed, because this instrument will serve its master for more than a dozen years? First of all, you should not choose a model in a rush, but you should understand all the nuances and subtleties of the purchase. And first of all, you need to pay attention to the keys. The best electronic piano has 88 of them. Moreover, the keys must be full-sized.





This part brings the digital instrument closer to the acoustic one, for which such a quantity is the standard.

If an electronic piano is purchased for a professional or you want to save a little, then you should pay attention to a cheaper option. It can be a 76-key digital instrument. An electronic piano with a reduced key size is also suitable in this case. But if the instrument is purchased for a child who takes lessons from a teacher with an acoustic model, then the children's electronic piano should have 88 full-sized keys. Otherwise, it will be very difficult for your child to master musical lessons. An electronic piano for a music school should be as close as possible to the parameters of an acoustic instrument.

Before making a purchase, you will need to test the keys and make sure that they are:

  • do not knock during the game;
  • have minimal reel amplitude;
  • hard pressed.

Keyboard mechanics

How to choose an electronic piano for this characteristic? The keyboard mechanics of the model you are considering should be as close as possible to a classic instrument. What does this mean? The keyboard should be balanced and balanced. It requires the presence of a decrease in the stiffness of pressing, starting from the keys located in the lower case.





And here it is necessary to study the description of the model. If her passport indicates the presence of a half-weighted keyboard and easy keystrokes, then for students of a music school such an instrument is far from the best option.

Housing

This parameter is also worth paying attention to those who do not know how to choose an electronic piano. This is especially important if the tool is purchased for a child. A beginner musician needs to master the correct setting of hands and landing. And this is possible only on the piano, whose keyboard is at the same height as the acoustic instrument.

Polyphony and acoustics

In which case will the electronic piano sound fully? Reviews of experts indicate that a good instrument should have 128-note polyphony. It is this amount of notes that simultaneously sounds in the best digital piano. But in addition to this, your chosen model should have a powerful speaker system from 40 to 60 watts. This will provide the instrument with real sound.



Experts also recommend that you study such a characteristic of your chosen model as AWM dynamic stereo sampling. It is desirable that its level ranged from 3 to 4 samples (this concept means examples of the sound of piano sounds recorded in the memory of a digital piano). This characteristic brings the digital instrument closer to the classic. An electronic piano for children can be selected with one or two or with two or three samples. Such an instrument will be in a lower price category, however, it sounds much simpler.

Experts give these recommendations on the question of how to choose an electronic piano not by chance. After all, the basis of a good playing on a keyboard instrument is the correct landing of a novice musician and the setting of hands, as well as the necessary sound extraction. If you start to learn musical literacy, using a tool with a light keystroke, or located not at the level necessary for the game, then this will certainly affect the quality of the performance.

Price factor

What else is worth paying attention to when buying an electronic piano? Prices are also an important factor in choosing any keyboard instrument. The fact is that a good piano from any manufacturer costs a lot of money. This is due to the fact that the basis of high-quality sounding of the instrument is modern electronic and technical solutions. The main task of the digital piano, acquired for training, is to maximize the approximation of its characteristics to the classical counterpart. This applies not only to the sound quality, but also to the sensations that the musician experiences during the game. That is why prices for an electronic piano depend on the quality of all systems included in it.

As for professional tools, their cost is quite impressive. It is in the range from 120 to 160 thousand rubles. Lovers and students should not buy such an instrument. These models are suitable only for people who are well versed in playing the piano.

The highest quality digital piano models in terms of their external characteristics and sound can be bought for 90-160 thousand rubles. By playing them, a person gets the same sensations as when playing musical compositions on a classical acoustic instrument.

The lower price range of electronic pianos is between 40 and 100 thousand rubles. The characteristics of such an instrument satisfy only very inexperienced listeners. The fact is that such models are very far from the classical piano. And the more a beginner musician begins to master the game, the less listeners will be satisfied with the quality of its sound.

You can buy an electronic piano inexpensively. The lowest price for such an instrument is up to forty thousand rubles. However, models purchased at this cost have a low sound quality, and their parameters are very different from the classic counterpart.

Which digital tool to choose? You decide. But in any case, it is worth approaching its acquisition with all responsibility, so that after a month he will not be forgotten by his owners.

Manufacturer's choice

There is another important factor that can be decisive when buying a particular model. This is a manufacturer of electronic piano. When buying a tool, you should pay attention to the line of products offered by the company.

If the range of instruments produced by her includes professional models, this will indicate the presence of technologies that provide high-quality sound for this manufacturer. In this case, the budget digital pianos of this company are probably distinguished by good characteristics. Digital Pianos for Advanced

We can safely say that the Yamaha P155, despite its compactness, is quite a serious thing for performers, including those who speak in front of large auditoriums. The price range of this model ranges from 45 to 55 thousand rubles.
