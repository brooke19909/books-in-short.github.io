Guest House "White Lion" (Nikolaevka, Crimea): description, reviews

Lovers of relaxation in the Crimea can spend their holidays in the White Lion guest house located in Nikolaevka . Nikolaevka is a resort village located 43 kilometers from Simferopol, on the shores of Kalamitsky Bay. Guest House "White Lion" is a private pension, was built in 2010.





Description

Together with the third building, a large pool measuring 14 by 11 meters and a children's pool were commissioned. There are sun loungers around the pool. The clear sea and the free sand and pebble beach are located at a distance of 300 meters from the hotel. To get to the beach, you need to go through the field and go down the stairs. The guest house has its own cafe and bar. If vacationers want to cook themselves, a shared kitchen is provided at their service. It is equipped with the necessary kitchen equipment. The hotel territory is ennobled by greenery, for guests there are gazebos for relaxation, barbecue facilities are provided for those who want to cook barbecue. Tourists who come in their own car are provided with a parking space in a guarded parking lot. There are two markets in the village. On the promenade there are various attractions, many cozy cafes, there are souvenir and grocery stores. Here, on specially equipped points, you can book an excursion to various attractions of the Crimea. The boarding house has its own electric generator, which provides buildings with electricity in the event of a malfunction in the power plant. The coast near Nikolaevka is famous for its unique climate, combining the soft sea air and dry steppe. Weather in Nikolaevka in the summertime is mostly sunny, the sea is warm. Check-in time at the guest house is after 12.00, room must be vacated before 09.00.





Accommodation

Guest House "White Lion" offers its guests accommodation in fairly inexpensive rooms of various categories.

  • The cost of a standard (double) room is 700 rubles.
  • Triple standard costs 1050 rubles per day.
  • For a double deluxe room, payment is 900 rubles per day. The room has a balcony.
  • The cost of a triple deluxe room with a balcony is 1300 rubles.
  • Double deluxe room with sea view costs 1000 rubles per night.
  • The cost of a deluxe triple room with sea view is 1500 rubles.
  • Triple deluxe in a new building with sea views costs 1700 rubles per day.
  • The cost of a four-bed deluxe room with a sea view is 2000 rubles.
  • Accommodation in a five-bed deluxe room costs 2500 rubles per day.

The presence of four- and five-bed rooms makes it convenient to place a large family or company in one room. The price includes the use of the pool.





Room Amenities

In the guest house "White Lion" (Nikolaevka) in each room, vacationers are provided with the following amenities:

  • flat-screen TV (satellite TV);
  • safe for storing documents;
  • private bathroom with shower with round-the-clock hot and cold water.

The deluxe rooms also include:

  • air conditioning;
  • refrigerator.

For guests staying in standard rooms, refrigerators are located on the floor.





Services

The guest house "White Lion" (Nikolaevka, Crimea) for the convenience and comfort of its guests provides the following services:

  • Wi-Fi access is free;
  • secure parking within its territory;
  • the hotel has a playground for children;
  • large outdoor swimming pool;
  • housekeeping;
  • delivery of products;
  • arbors for rest;
  • use of barbecue grill;
  • designated smoking areas;
  • use of the conference room;
  • there is a table for playing billiards;
  • shared kitchen with necessary utensils and kitchen equipment;
  • laundry and ironing services are provided for a fee;
  • organization of complex meals in a cafe is possible;
  • organization of transfer from or to the airport or railway station (for a fee);
  • call a taxi in place.




Transfer Organization

The guest house "White Lion" organized several types of transfers:

  • A trip in a passenger car up to four people from the airport to the boarding house costs 1700 rubles, and from the railway station - 1400 rubles.
  • A trip in a minibus with a capacity of up to eight people from the airport to the pension will cost 2600 rubles, and from the railway station - 2300 rubles.
  • If the trip is carried out in an air-conditioned car, then a surcharge of 20% is provided for the trip.
  • If a round-trip transfer is ordered and immediately paid, then a discount of 100 rubles is provided for the return transfer.

Guest reviews - negative

Also, "White Lion" - a guest house in Nikolaevka - receives not very good reviews:

  • some guests believe that there is not enough dishes in the shared kitchen, because they take it apart by numbers and do not return it;
  • there was a case during check-in, when the reservation was lost, the guests were moved to another room;
  • some vacationers believe that the coastline is not very well-groomed, a very uncomfortable descent to the sea;
  • there are complaints that some rooms do not catch Wi-Fi;
  • some vacationers who lived on the fourth floor complained about the lack of an elevator;
  • a number of vacationers were dissatisfied with the fact that the rooms were rarely cleaned.

According to almost all tourists, rest in the guest house "White Lion" (Nikolaevka) is one of the most inexpensive in the Crimea and at the same time quite comfortable. The hosts and staff are trying to constantly improve their service and are always happy to welcome new guests.
