Moscow. Gorky Park





Despite the fact that this oasis in the stuffy desert of the Russian capital is located in its very center, once you get here, you just forget about it. If you wish, you can easily find a secluded place. Gorky Park, whose address is Krymsky Val Street , 9, invites all Muscovites and guests of the capital to spend several hours on their territory for a pleasant stay.

What to do when you come here? Gorky Park is the largest entertainment area, here everyone will find what he will like. Even before he was given the name of a great writer, the first sports pavilions were opened here. The sports orientation has remained to this day. The park provides yoga classes, a running club. All conditions for playing tennis, including table tennis, soccer and beach volleyball, are created. On any day of the week, you can rent a cycle car, scooter, rollers, runbike, bike, etc.

If you are far from sports, but respect contemporary art, then you should definitely visit the Garage center. Here, shows and exhibitions of experimental works by contemporary young artists, various master classes and film screenings are held with enviable regularity.

Krasnodar

Gorky Park here is also called the "City Garden". The time of its creation is the middle of the XIX century. By the beginning of the next century, the park had become a real natural gem, the main dendrological attraction of the city. The Great Patriotic War did not spare this picturesque corner. The park was almost completely destroyed, but city authorities and landscapers made sure that it was reconstructed. Gradually, he acquired an attractive appearance.





Another park

Gorky Park, whose address is Krasnodar, Postovaya street, 34, at the entrance greets guests with the monument "Reconciliation and consent." It is dedicated to the locals who became victims of the civil war. One of the main attractions of the park is the giant oak, which is considered its peer. This is a peculiar symbol of the park, which personifies the connection of times and generations.

Both Krasnodar residents and city guests like to be here. Communication with nature always brings tranquility and joy. Shady alleys and cascades of fountains invite you to plunge into the pleasant atmosphere and feel easy and comfortable. But people do not come here for peace. Here you can have some fun too. Numerous attractions, a shooting gallery and a cafe await visitors. A new Ferris wheel was installed here this year. Its height is 57 meters. At the same time, 144 people can ride on it, and the "journey" will last eleven minutes. There is also a dolphinarium, amateur concerts take place on weekends.

You can get here by bus (routes 127a, 126a, 124a, 107a, 101), tram (2 or 4) or minibus (7a and b, 49, 44, 177).

Kharkiv





We will leave the borders of the Russian Federation and move to Kharkov. It should be noted that Gorky Park, whose address is Sumskaya Street, 81, is the largest corner of nature in this city. The first plantings here were made in 1893, and the grand opening took place already in 1907, when these trees grew. The park owes its appearance to the residents of Kharkov, thanks to which 98 hectares of forest were allotted for improvement.

Today, many Kharkivs call Gorky Park the local Disneyland. Here is the largest Ferris wheel in Ukraine, whose height is 55 meters. Children and adults are waiting for a roller coaster, a rope park, an autodrome, a house of fear and other fun and breathtaking attractions. In total, here you can count about 40 types of various entertainments. This natural corner is created especially for family holidays.





Convenient separation

Each visitor will be able to easily navigate in the park. Its entire area is divided into 5 zones, each of which is devoted to a separate theme: retro, French, extreme, medieval and children's. Representatives of the older generation will surely enjoy the retrozone - here every evening in the evenings there are dances to music performed by a live orchestra on a covered stage. Everything breathes the atmosphere of the middle of the XX century. Do not describe all the opportunities that Gorky Park offers its visitors. Kharkov (the address of the park, recall - Sumska St., 81) is waiting for guests and invites them to spend unforgettable hours in a unique green corner!
