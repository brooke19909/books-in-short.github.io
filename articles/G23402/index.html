Ukraine is famous not only for relaxing by the sea, but also for its beautiful, picturesque mountains - the Carpathians. This mountainous region has long attracted the attention of tourists with its mystery, some mysticism, color and healing properties of mineral waters and coniferous air. There are many historical monuments represented by castles, fortresses, wooden structures.

General characteristics of the Carpathian region

If you want to go on vacation or recreation in Western Ukraine, you need to know how the Transcarpathian region and the Carpathian region differ.

Carpathians are several settlements located right at the foot of the Carpathians. The most popular resort town is Truskavets. The main healing factor in this area is mineral water, in particular, "Naftusya", "Maria", "Sofia". In the towns and villages of the Carpathian region, in addition to clean air and healing water, you can get aesthetic pleasure. There is a forest and mountains, there is a mild climate and high humidity, which allows you not to languish in the heat in summer and not to freeze in winter.

Description of the features of Transcarpathia

In turn, Transcarpathia is also famous for the presence of healthy and healing mineral waters, salt mines, muds that contribute to healthy skin, and clean, fresh air. Mineral water here differs in composition from the waters of the Carpathian region. The cleanliness and freshness of the air gives a large number of conifers growing in these places. Prices for holidays in the "Carpathians"

Accommodation in sanatoriums differs in price depending on the number of people living in the room, equipment and comfort of the room, food program (how many times a day) and infrastructure. This is an internal price distribution. Also, inflation and the exchange rate at a particular time affect pricing.

It should be noted that the Karpaty sanatoriums, whose prices do not differ much from each other, are located in the mid-price segment, which makes them affordable for different segments of the population. The exact prices per day at any time of the year can be found on special sites.

Are there any discounts?

In recent years, social programs have been provided for various families. For example, in the Carpathians of Truskavets children under 4 years old can stay free of charge, and children 4-10 years old will receive a 50% discount on accommodation. In addition, many other motels have seasonal discounts.
