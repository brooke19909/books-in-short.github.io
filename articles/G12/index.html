Hotel complex "Alexandria" (Vityazevo)

Hotel Alexandria (Vityazevo) is a beautiful beach hotel complex in the famous resort village near Anapa. It was built in an ecologically clean and beautiful place. The complex consists of two hotels called "Alexandria" and "Akhtuba". Vityazevo has a wonderful, ideal climate for relaxation. It is especially healing for the healing of children. There are many sunny days, swimming is safe. Wide beaches, shallow water and clean air - all these factors combined, attract many tourists here. And which of them does not want to relax with all this comfort?





Location and Territory

Hotel "Alexandria" (Vityazevo) is not directly on the beach. Its location can be called, rather, the second line from the sea. Nevertheless, it is located in the coastal zone. The hotel offers excellent views of the sea and the fine sand of the beaches. On the territory of the complex there are two beautifully decorated green areas intended for recreation, as well as arbors. The main building of Alexandria has a column in order to wash sand off its feet. All this is being watched, guarded, it is very clean here. Given that in almost all Anapa hotels, even the most modern, the territory is a stone or concrete site, this is a big plus. There is a bazaar between the sea and the hotel, which is very convenient if you want to buy food. Not far from the hotel there are an aquapark and a dolphinarium, which Vityazevo is famous for. Alexandria Hotel is located between the Greek-style Paralia Embankment and Svetlaya Street, so you can choose several roads to the sea if you wish. In addition, the territory is not very noisy, thanks again to a good location.

There are 88 rooms in all eight buildings of the Alexandria-Akhtuba complex (Vityazevo). Hotels are not very large, rather, homely. 23 rooms are classified as standard, 8 are luxury. There are also improved rooms, studios of different comforts and apartments. Five buildings belong to the Alexandria Hotel, and three to Akhtuba. Nutrition

And how are vacationers fed in this hotel complex in Vityazevo? Hotel "Alexandria" offers guests the services of a dining room and a bar. The dishes are delicious and home-style, the portions are large. In the summer cafe very tasty, fried "with soul" barbecue. In addition, if you want variety or do not want to bind yourself with food at the hotel, then in the village itself there are many cafes with not too expensive food. Borsch, pickles, various side dishes, stuffed peppers, fried fish, meatballs and compotes - all this is very well prepared and at affordable prices.





Services

The Alexandria complex (Vityazevo) carries its guests free of charge to the railway station and airport. You are greeted with a sign and help bring things to the transport. Two playgrounds were built on the territory. Animation works for kids. Programs for children are designed for two hours. It includes dancing, outdoor games. Children are having a lot of fun. For games and other entertainment between the buildings of the hotel there is a special artificial turf. In addition, babies are always under supervision. It turns out that when you come here on vacation, you are as if handing over siblings to a children's camp and can safely relax. The staff are polite, respect vacationers. At the reception you can solve any problem. Valuables and documents can be put in the safe for free.





Beach

As a rule, tourists go for tanning and sea bathing in Vityazevo. Hotel "Alexandria" in this case is a very good choice. The hotel building is located one hundred meters from the sea. The beach here is sandy, good. The entrance to the water is very gentle. You need to walk a few tens of meters, so that the depth is chest. Therefore, it is suitable for children, and parents have nothing to worry about. In addition, the beach is very wide, and there is always a place, even by the water. There are many kinds of entertainment here - bananas, boating, "pills", sea excursions. The beach is equipped - there are sunbeds, toilets. But all these services are paid. In addition, the hotel Alexandria (Vityazevo) has four more pools. One of them is children's. They are clean, but with cool water, without heating. Vityazevo, Alexandria: reviews
