Smartline Numa Konaktepe Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya, Konakli): photos and reviews of tourists

Konakli is a noisy and colorful Turkish resort. It is located on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. It offers vacationers developed infrastructure of the village. It has grocery stores, souvenir shops, a post office, telegraph, hospital and clinic. At the disposal of tourists are oriental steam rooms, saunas, stadiums and gyms.

The city has about thirty hotels of various levels. Smartline Numa Konaktepe Hotel 4 * consistently tops ratings based on traveler reviews. It is located next to a pebble beach and a municipal culture park. The taxi route to nearby Alanya takes no more than fifteen minutes. The resort is 120 kilometers from Antalya. Travel time is two hours.

Every Wednesday in the regional center a noisy bazaar opens its doors. Barkers come to the square early in the morning. Following them are the first customers. Excursion escort

Experienced travelers recommend visiting the ancient city of Demre. A string of tourist buses arriving from all over Turkey stretches daily to its ruins. A trip to the remains of the fortress is very popular among hotel guests. They are happy to share their impressions.

In addition to antiquities, Demre boasts modern entertainment complexes. Among them are the towers of the church of St. Nicholas. This is the main attraction of the municipality. It was built in the IV century BC. Partial reconstruction took place in the second half of the XIX century. This is an amazing and unique example of Byzantine architecture, which has been preserved in Turkey.

Miracles still happen in the temple. Mortally sick are cured. This is due to the fact that the relics of St. Nicholas were stored in his sarcophagus until the 11th century. It is believed that they were seized from the monks and transported. But locals claim that the ministers managed to conceal the true burial.

Kekova Island is another place that everyone who is on the coast wants to visit. Its area is just under five square kilometers. He is famous for the fact that on his lands are the remains of several ancient Greek settlements. And here they ride a boat with a completely transparent bottom.

Olympos is a resort forgotten by the tourist community. In its open spaces there are no high-rise hotel complexes. expensive restaurants, fashionable boutiques. Its main value lies in the reserved nature. People come here to admire the azure of the sea, cliffs and coniferous forests. Olympos is given the status of a national park. It is protected by the Turkish state regulatory authorities.
