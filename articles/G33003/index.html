Hotel Ivory Coast Hotel 2 * (Vietnam): description, traveler reviews

Located in the central part of Vietnam, the city of Nha Trang is a popular holiday destination mainly for Russian tourists. There are not so many vacationers from Europe and other parts of Asia. Therefore, the entire infrastructure of this resort is mainly aimed only at the Vietnamese themselves and our domestic vacationers.

In any case, a lot of good hotels have been built in this southern city. For example, some domestic tour operators sell holiday packages to this country with accommodation at the Ivory Coast Hotel 2 * to people wishing to relax in Vietnam for Russian vacationers .

Where is the hotel located

This convenient complex is located directly in the city of Nha Trang. Ivory Coast Hotel 2 * was built in the northern zone, which is somewhat less popular with tourists. This area begins behind Cape Hong Chong, the Stone Park and ends in the mountains. A feature of the beaches in the north of Nha Trang is a smooth entrance to the sea. This, of course, can be very convenient for tourists who come to rest in Vietnam with children.





This complex is located in a narrow quiet alley. Opposite it is a pagoda.

This hotel is quite far from the sea . To get to the nearest beach, hotel guests need to overcome about 850 meters. In the vicinity of the hotel, in addition to various cafes, restaurants and shops, there are several interesting sights. For example, 2.5 km from Ivory Coast Hotel 2 * are the towers of Po Nagar.

The city center is about 10 minutes drive from the hotel. Camran Airport is 43 km from the hotel. That is, to get to the hotel upon arrival, its guests need about 1 hour.

Hotel description: general

The hotel Ivory Coast Hotel 2 * (Vietnam) was built in 2014. That is, this hotel, in fact, is quite new. They take guests here in one fairly large multi-story building. This hotel belongs to the class of two-star. Therefore, living here is relatively inexpensive. But, since the hotel is budget, it, unfortunately, does not even have its own territory.

It reads that this hotel is best suited for families with children. Also a lot of young people stay at this hotel. This property is non-smoking. The same goes for pets.





Among the advantages of this inexpensive hotel, tourists include, among other things, the fact that they do not take a deposit at the entrance from the guests. Meals at this hotel are not offered to residents. But not far from the complex there are quite good cafes that have earned good reviews from tourists (for example, the institution "Sailor").

On the territory of the Ivory Coast Hotel 2 *, no special amenities are offered to guests, as already mentioned. The only thing in this complex, residents can use the Wi-Fi network.
