Country hotel "Heliopark Lesnoy": reviews, photos

Regardless of whether you want to spend a weekend with your family in the countryside in comfortable conditions or organize a conference for your employees, clients or partners, the Heliopark Lesnoy hotel will be an excellent choice. It has comfortable rooms, a gym, restaurants, a spa complex, animation programs, a conference room - in short, everything so that every guest can find the services he needs. Such a place as "Heliopark Lesnoy" remains in the memory of its customers for a long time and each of them certainly wants to return here again.

Hotel location and infrastructure

This hotel is located at a distance of 37 km from the capital of the Russian Federation and 27 km from Sheremetyevo Airport. In this regard, not only residents of Moscow like to go on vacation here, who are tired of the daily bustle and want to relax in soul and body, but also those who flew to the capital on a business visit or for sightseeing purposes. It is pleasing to hotel guests that it works year-round.





It also offers developed infrastructure of this institution. There are two restaurants on its territory: Shamrock, where a daily buffet is served, as well as the opportunity to choose dishes from a special menu, and European Lesnoy, which offers a large grill menu and a huge selection of traditional Irish drinks. Shamrock also has a children's menu, which is good for families with small children.

In addition, the hotel has a huge territory, which looks especially beautiful in summer, spring, and also in autumn. At any time of the year, guests of the Heliopark Lesnoy Hotel have the opportunity to enjoy leisurely walks through the territory and surrounding forests.
