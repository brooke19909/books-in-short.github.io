Chaika Chalet (Samara): photos and reviews of tourists

Do not have large funds for holidays abroad? Do not despair, it is no less interesting for an adequate amount you can spend a vacation on the territory of the Russian Federation. There is a small picturesque island in the Samara region under the interesting name Zelenenky, located between the village of Krasnaya Glinka and the suburban village of Gavrilova Polyana.

Nature generously rewarded this place with amazing vegetation and a sandy coast. The natural beach is quite long, stretching along the entire valley facing the Volga River. In the summer season, the island is strewn with tourists. Here are concentrated about 40 hotel complexes and campsites that offer paradise holidays at affordable prices.

Very popular among nonresident guests is the Chaika camp site (Samara). You can get to it only by water: a tourist boat runs several times a day. Private vessels offer their services: boats, boats.

Acquaintance with the complex

Bonfire night gatherings, bright sunshine, sandy shore, warm pond, plenty of leisure activities - all this attracts people to the island. The Chaika country camp site (Samara), whose photo illustrates the object, opened its doors in 1969. It is an old tourist complex that has won the love of thousands of citizens. By the way, it was built according to the project of the famous aviation designer Kuznetsov.



On a spacious landscaped area there is a chic promenade, two berths, a sports stadium, residential facilities and retail shops. At the same time, up to 500 guests can stay at the base without interfering with each other. You fall in love with this place from the first minute: there are no noisy highways nearby, continuous peace and peace of mind.

Accommodation

Chaika Chalet (Samara) charges a security deposit at the entrance upon arrival - the amount is refunded upon departure. The choice of accommodation options is diverse: one-story houses with outdoor amenities, two-bedroom villas, as well as two-story cottages consisting of four isolated rooms. All rooms are renovated, equipped with electricity.





The improved houses have hot water, their own kitchen, bathroom. The house has an open veranda with a picturesque view of the expanses of water and lush vegetation. In the presence of the necessary bedroom furniture, bedside tables. There are special bins for garbage that are cleaned daily.

What about food?

Three meals a day are provided for an additional payment - from about 600 rubles per day per person. Meals are served in the spacious dining room. The morning meal consists of sandwiches, cottage cheese casseroles and tea. For lunch, serve lean borsch, chicken goulash with a side dish and some salad.

Dinner includes meatballs or meatballs, fritters with condensed milk, sour cream. Chaika Chalet (Samara) is equipped with a cafeteria and retail stores. You can cook your own meals on the grill or grill (rented out).

Summer fun

What rest without bathing and sunbathing? Upon arrival to the island you will have the opportunity to splash around in a warm pond, bask in the sun and take a walk along the promenade. The beach in this place is strewn with sand, which is very convenient for children. The entrance to the river is gentle and safe.



True, there are a lot of algae in the water, so it is recommended to wear special shoes. On the shore, you can play volleyball, ride a boat, ATV, catamaran or just enjoy the beautiful scenery that the Chaika camp site (Samara) has. Reviews of tourists report that this is a real oasis of cleanliness and freshness, where you feel like Robinson Crusoe.

Entertaining activities

For fans of active leisure, the administration of the complex offers sports fields located in the stadium for playing volleyball, badminton and football. The necessary equipment is hired: rackets, balls. Table tennis, backgammon and darts are available. Chaika Chalet (Samara) organizes outbound fishing tours - a boat and a boat are provided.

After a rich catch, you can cook a fragrant ear on a fire or bake a carcass. Soul gatherings for delicious food will deliver a lot of positive emotions. There is also a Russian steam room. For those who like nightlife, there is a dance hall, or, in modern language, a disco.



You will enjoy a karaoke bar, fiery music and a lot of exciting contests. Sleep will take off like a hand. Your children will also have something to do. For children, there is an outdoor play area with swings, an inflatable trampoline, horizontal bars. It offers the organization of corporate events and team building.

There are just a ton of options on this subject: spending time away from the hustle and bustle of the city is the dream of any director. Any celebration, business meeting or banquet will be professionally organized by the staff of the complex. The menu is selected taking into account individual taste preferences and is agreed personally with the customer.

Camp "Seagull". Zelenenky Island (Samara): guest reviews

Based on the numerous reviews of tourists who have repeatedly visited the picturesque island, we can understand that the complex is that corner of paradise where the emotional state returns to normal. There is no need at all to give up city goods, all the necessary amenities are available at the base: light, water, furniture, a bathroom. The air here is so clean that you feel dizzy from an excess of oxygen.



The aroma of lush vegetation is floating in the air, birds singing and the calming rustle of leaves are heard. A full vacation at an affordable price is organized by the Chaika camp site. Zelenenky (Samara) - an island on the Volga River, which will give harmony. Everything here has a serene, relaxing and productive pastime. Especially like the complex for young guests.

In the spacious area you can find many exciting activities and games. It is allowed to bring bicycles with you, ride a scooter. How much pleasure and fun you get from swimming and sunbathing, the mind is incomprehensible! If you want to save money and at the same time have a comfortable vacation, then, without hesitation, plan a summer vacation in Samara, on the island of Zelenenky.
