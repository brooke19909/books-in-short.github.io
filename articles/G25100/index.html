Hotel Description

Vikingen Quality Resort & Spa 5 * hotel is located directly near the picturesque promenade. Pools, bars, restaurants, entertainment - all this creates a relaxing atmosphere of serenity. Everything in this hotel is aimed at making guests feel at ease and comfortable.

The hotel impresses with its modern, original architecture. Much attention is also paid to the decoration and equipment of the rooms. A separate pleasant moment is the presence in most apartments of panoramic sea views.

Do not forget the hotel Vikingen Quality Resort & Spa 5 * and refreshments. The main meals are served in buffet style in the main dining room. But do not stop there. Be sure to visit the a la carte restaurants to taste the real masterpieces of Turkish, French, Asian and other world cuisines.

As the hotel specializes in spa treatments, of course, a large modern wellness center operates on its territory. You will feel truly refreshed and rested after undergoing massage, sauna, hammam and other relaxing manipulations.





Where is the hotel situated

Vikingen Quality Resort & Spa 5 * hotel is located at some distance from city bustle and many infrastructure facilities. So, Alanya International Airport is already 90 km from this resort. If you want to walk around the city, you will have to overcome 30 km to its center.

The remoteness of the Vikingen Quality Resort & Spa 5 * (Okurcalar) hotel from many benefits of civilization is compensated by the incredibly beautiful natural sites located near the hotel complex. So, not far from the resort is the famous Manavgat waterfall, which is certainly worth a visit while in Alanya.





Hotel rooms

The hotel has 560 apartments. Accommodation is possible in the main multi-storey building or in one of three detached villas. Guests are available rooms of the following categories:

  • Standard - these are small rooms, which can accommodate from one to three people (rooms of different capacities are provided). It is also possible to install an additional cot. It is worth noting that several rooms of the standard category are specially adapted for guests with disabilities.
  • Family room - consists of two spacious, bright bedrooms, each of which adjoins an individual bathroom equipped with a shower cabin. Beds are a large double and two small beds. A folding sofa can also be used.
  • Suite - two-room suite with a spacious bedroom and a small cozy living room. The room has upholstered furniture, which is laid out and can also be used as an extra bed.
  • Royal Suite - a luxurious room with a sophisticated sophisticated design. In area, it is slightly larger than the previous category. The bathroom has both a shower and a bathtub with hydromassage. Perhaps the biggest advantage of the royal suite is the balcony, located directly above the sea.
  • Villa - ideal for a large family or a noisy group of friends. They are located separately, not far from the seashore and have a small private garden. Inside there are three spacious bedrooms, each of which is adjoined by a bathroom, as well as a small kitchen and a cozy living room, which also acts as a dining room.

Hotel reviews

Tourists from all over the world have long loved the Vikingen Quality Resort & Spa 5 * hotel. Reviews about him contain a lot of positive, as well as some comments. And indicate the following:

  • The hotel is located in close proximity to the sea and has its own well-kept beach. The impression is slightly spoiled by an uncomfortable entry into the water, strewn with stones and laid out with slippery slabs.
  • Great varied food. From a huge assortment of dishes, everyone will choose something to their taste. Pleased with the opportunity to negotiate with the cooks a diet or vegetarian menu.
  • Regular cleaning and replacement of bedding and bath accessories (although, in the absence of a tip, the quality of service is noticeably reduced).
  • In the rooms everything is working properly. The only gripe is caused by old air conditioners, which practically do not cope with the function of cooling the air.
  • A good exciting animation program for both children and adults.
  • Despite the fact that there are plenty of sun loungers on the beach, they are sorely lacking mattresses.
  • The hotel is located very far from any infrastructure. The only thing nearby is the local rural market and a small water park.
  • For those who do not want to leave the hotel, there is the opportunity to ride a roller coaster in the pool.
  • Despite the fact that cable TV shows a huge number of channels in the hotel, there are only two Russian-language channels.
