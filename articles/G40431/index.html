Comfortable hotels "Rosa Khutor": a list of the best complexes of Krasnaya Polyana

The European-level resort "Rosa Khutor", located in the vicinity of Sochi, in the village of Krasnaya Polyana, today is a popular ski resort. Among the snowy hills and pristine nature are concentrated hotel facilities with developed infrastructure, first-class rooms and an abundance of entertainment services.





This resort is dynamically developing, constantly being equipped, which makes it even more attractive for foreign guests. Today it is also the largest Olympic facility in Sochi with numerous lifts, slopes and descents. It is attractive for both winter and summer holidays.

Rosa Khutor hotels harmoniously combine modern high-tech conditions and traditionally Russian culture. On the territory of the village are concentrated ancient caves, a zoo-sanitation complex, narzan springs, historical sights, children's clubs, restaurants, McDonald's and much more.

Business Hotel Heliopark Freestyle





A cozy place near the "Mountain Carousel", expressways and lifts is ideal for active pastime. A lot of pleasure will bring you a stay in this complex. From the windows of the rooms tourists can enjoy the beauty of the mountain peaks, unusually clear lakes and picturesque rivers. The country hotel is equipped with modern amenities.

Heliopark Freestyle Hotel operates year-round. In winter, it offers fascinating adventures on the winding high-speed slopes of Krasnaya Polyana, in summer you will find exciting descents along mountain rivers, as well as fishing, walking along picturesque forest paths and swimming in local lakes. Almost all Rosa Khutor hotels provide wellness services, and Heliopark Freestyle is no exception.





It offers massage services, swimming pools and various beauty treatments. To stay, visitors can choose from several options of rooms decorated in a modern style. For families with children, there are large two-room apartments with a seating area. At leisure, you will be offered to demonstrate skill in the billiard room and karaoke room.

On its territory there is a health club, a children's area, conference rooms, bars, restaurants, a sauna, a jacuzzi, a fitness center. A wide range of diverse services awaits you at Radisson. All Rosa Khutor hotels (even budget ones) are more focused on winter vacations. Therefore, they are located near the ski slopes. Near the Radisson Hotel is the Laura Ski and Biathlon Center. The price includes a morning meal.

Four-star Park Inn complex





Absolutely all Rosa Khutor hotels are distinguished by their practicality, high functionality and impeccable order. These include the Park Inn Hotel, located in the center of the alpine village. Lifts and entertainment centers are just 200 meters away. It offers cozy rooms with a private seating area, TV and furniture.

It offers car rental, free internet, a luggage room for sports equipment and a gym. According to the rules of the complex, children under the age of 17 stay free with their parents (bed for a fee). Buffet breakfast is included.

Three-star hotel "Tulip Inn"

An unforgettable experience, joyful emotions and a bunch of adrenaline will give a large first-class complex "Tulip Inn", located in the resort "Rosa Khutor" (Krasnaya Polyana). Ski resort hotels offer all tourists accommodation in fully equipped rooms, located near the slopes and cable cars.





It will not be difficult to get from here to the center of Sochi or Adler by shuttle bus or the Lastochka electric train. Fans of outdoor activities will be delighted with the cable cars "Reserved Forest", "Wolf Rock", "Olympia", which were used for international competitions. The bewitching panorama from hotel windows will amaze you.

For business tourism, meeting rooms and conference rooms with modern presentation equipment and the Internet are offered. Visitors can enjoy the pleasant atmosphere and gourmet dishes in the local restaurants. The hotel can be recommended to fans of extreme relaxation, as well as to all those who like to spend time in the bosom of wildlife.

Comfortable villa "Peak Hotel"





The Peak Hotel mountain club, consisting of small cozy cottages and two five-story buildings, was built a short drive from the slopes. Frequent guests of this complex are senior officials and the president of Russia himself.

The club has several well-equipped conference rooms, a press center, and a room for business negotiations. In the free use of high-speed Internet. All rooms are distinguished by impeccable quality and style. The complex has its own spa and wellness center. Individual weight loss programs are being developed.

It offers chocolate wraps, all types of massage, a sauna and a full range of cosmetic procedures. We must say right away that living in this place costs a lot of money. Measure your capabilities and desires.
