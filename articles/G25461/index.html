Hotels in Orsha welcome guests

A small Belarusian city of a regional scale is located at the crossroads of many roads, rail and road. Once it was deservedly called the nodal center. In Orsha, hotels are located in different corners for the convenience of guests and the possibility of choice.

Orsha: some statistics

But first about the city itself. This is a large settlement with a population of 140 thousand inhabitants at the moment. The origins of its history are rooted in ancient times. For the first time about the city there is a mention in the "Tale of Bygone Years" dated 667. Orsha is conveniently located on the banks of the Dnieper. The city is a major industrial and cultural center for its republic with centuries-old traditions. Orsha residents - residents of the city - honor them and are proud of their history.



There are several hotels in the city. This is not a "stone casemates" with cheap furniture rooms. Orsha hotels have long said goodbye to the cheap heritage of the times of socialism. Hotels in Orsha are modern rooms and apartments, which in their comfort are not inferior to metropolitan or resort hotels.

Orsha often becomes the center of various events and activities; groups of tourists, business travelers, and delegations constantly flock to the city. Orsha guests are always happy and try to please them in terms of recreation and entertainment. It is for such purposes that hotels work in the city of Orsha. This is cosiness, comfort and relaxation.

Hotel "Orsha" (3 stars)

The largest and most popular hotel is Orsha. Belarus loves and respects guests. For their complete relaxation, a high level of service is offered, including the entire infrastructure of this three-star hotel. This is a beautiful eight-story building in the city center, built in 1991 and renovated in 2008.



For hotel accommodation there are 130 rooms of various categories and service. Among them: double, single, family, "suites" and one apartment. Total number of beds: 224 beds. The building of the Orsha Hotel has an elevator, a restaurant, a bar, a cafe, and a reception.

When paying, bonus discounts and benefits are applied for children under 12 years old. Payment is made according to different types of calculation. Minimum room rate - 10 US dollars (approximately) per day. And for the apartments you have to pay a substantial amount: almost 60 US dollars.
