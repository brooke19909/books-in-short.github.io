What is a false collar?

First, let's figure out what this patch collar is and what it is for. So, a removable collar is an addition to the image, similar to a necklace or any other decoration of the neck and decollete. Its essence is the same - to diversify the appearance and make the onion brighter. In general, girls and women who are self-confident, know their own style and are ready for experimentation choose this accessory.

Types of Collars

If you have the feeling that there is not much difference between the patch collars and they are all about the same thing, then this is not so. On the contrary, today the assortment and ideas of designers are so diverse that every season we can see new models again and again.

Here is a sample list of what to choose from:

  • Rack. A stand-up collar is, in fact, a shirt collar that is familiar to everyone and is closely adjacent to the neck.
  • Wide collar. This style is called the "mantle" and resembles the robes of kings and queens. It is divided into many options. From knitted to stone embroidered
  • Acute. The pointed corners of the collar visually stretch the neck, making it more elegant. And the accessory itself looks strictly and presentably.
  • Semicircle. Rounded corners will suit persons romantic and feminine.
  • Clasps. They can be located in front or behind. Or the bow can serve as a fastener. In this case, the necklace is tied with ribbons on the back of the head.

  • Textile. The collar does not have to be decorated with rhinestones or something else. Made only of fabric with a fancy print, the collar will remind you of a retro theme.
  • Embroidery. Another of the most fashionable options for today. Embroidery can be both on the edges of the collar, and around the entire perimeter.
  • Decor Fully embroidered collars made of beads and beads are the most popular among those who follow fashion trends.

Pluses of detachable collar

As with any purchase, you should evaluate all the advantages that you will receive from this acquisition:

  1. A patch collar will add sophistication to any, even the most ordinary dress. Will make it more elegant.
  2. Focus on the face. Because the collar is right next to the face, then the emphasis will be on the upper body. This is especially true if a woman is heavy and does not want to once again focus on the lower body.
  3. If you choose a black and kipelno-white high collar, complement them with a plain dress, then you instantly get an official look for a business meeting or important negotiations.

What to wear?

There are no uniform and clear rules for wearing a false collar. However, certain points should be adhered to. The main advice is to combine the collar only with simple-cut things, preferably monophonic.

The second tip says that when choosing a collar, consider the shape of the face and length of the neck. For a round face, a pointed shape is suitable, for an oval or square one, on the contrary, a rounded collar shape.

How to make a collar with decor on your own?

In order to make a collar of beads and beads with your own hands, you will need the following set:

  • skin or dense heavy material;
  • beads and beads;
  • threads matching the color of beads and fabric;
  • needle;
  • satin ribbon in fabric color.

Having prepared everything you need, you can begin to work:

1. Cut 2 identical collars from leather or fabric. Create a form yourself, depending on taste and preferences.

2. Then sew, starting from the edge, beads in the order in which you need. Having sewed beads from the edges, you can begin to fill the middle of the collar.

3. After all the beads are sewn, beads fill in all the gaps that are.

4. Turning the finished collar over, sew the second cut blank to its back. Thus closing the seams. Sew tape on both sides between two collars. And sew up everything to the end.

5. The bead collar is ready! Put it on with a simple black, burgundy or blue dress and see how it has changed!

Stylish necklace made of beads and chains

If your style involves some drive and rocker motifs, we advise you to pay attention to bead collars with chains and metal elements. This style gives the image an audacity and at the same time looks trendy at almost all times.

So, in order to make a stylish collar of beads with your own hands, prepare:

  • black skin or black cloth;
  • beads of gold or silver color;
  • metallic color beads;
  • threads
  • scissors;
  • black satin ribbon;
  • decorative chains.

The execution technique begins with cutting two leather collars. For such a slightly rough decor, the pointed shape of the corners of the collar is perfect. But focus primarily on the shape of your face, it is on the basis of it and choose a style.

Next, you need to sew the largest beads. After them, beading around follows. Chains can be sewn both around the edge and with sagging inside the pattern itself. After all the decor, carefully sew the second collar and sew the black ribbon for tying. The collar is ready!

This collar will be a great addition to a simple white shirt or plain short dress. Also, metal beads and a metallic color palette look great in combination with leather products. For example, a leather jacket or a leather skirt.
