A few secrets on how to sew an elastic skirt on the floor

For several seasons in a row, a maxi-skirt remains popular among fashionistas. Oddly enough, but the skirt on the floor suits everyone: low and tall, thin and not very, it is only important to choose the right fit to achieve a perfect fit. Given the ease of sewing this thing in a woman‚Äôs wardrobe and the abundance of choice of fabrics of various textures and colors, it is easy to guess why more and more girls tend to master the basics of cutting and sewing. After all, it is not so difficult to sew a long skirt with your own hands, although there are still a few secrets. It is precisely about them that I would like to know in order to exclude all possible misses of a cut.



How to sew a skirt to the floor with an elastic band and what is needed for this? First, you will need fabric, thread, elastic and a sewing machine. Secondly, desire. It would seem that it could be easier, just take a piece of fabric, make one seam, sew an elastic band on top. This option is very simple, requiring neither time nor skills. But as always there is a ‚Äúbut‚Äù - such a skirt is suitable only for thin people, but if you are the owner of magnificent forms, then a beautiful fit with such a cut cannot be achieved. So the question arises: " How to sew a skirt on the floor with an elastic band or with an original belt so that it looks harmoniously on a chubby or figured girl?" To answer this question, consider a couple of options for cutting long skirts that are easy to sew and fit evenly on any figure.



Option 1 - sun skirt

Someone may object, saying that the skirt-sun will not look at a full lady, and therefore you should not risk it and spend a lot of money on two lengths of fabric. But if you sew a sun skirt on an elastic band of the finest chiffon with the same thin, but not luminous lining, and the length of the lining is mini, probably many will like this option. For such a skirt, two semicircles of thin chiffon and two semicircles of soft lining fabric are cut out . In the middle of the semicircles in the volume of the skirt landing line, the top of the skirt is cut out. Determining this neckline, in several places check the length from the bottom to the top of the skirt, this length should be the same everywhere. Next is the turn behind the side seams of the upper skirt and the linings. After that, both parts are folded with the wrong side to each other and a bar with elastic is sewn along the notch.

The strap with an elastic is done as follows:

  • measure the volume of the hips, add 4 cm to the measurement of free fit;
  • cut out a rectangle of fabric measuring length + 4 cm and a width of 8 cm;
  • this strip of fabric is folded along in half (inside out) and a line is laid 3 cm from the fold;
  • the elastic line is cut off along the line of the skirt‚Äôs fit and is threaded into the workpiece (to prevent the elastic from twisting during wear, it must be sewn along the bar, stretching the elastic to fit the fabric);
  • sections of the bar are stitched and processed.




Option 2 - a straight skirt on the floor

How to sew a skirt on the floor with a straight-cut elastic is already mentioned above. But if you are not the owner of a thin figure, then it is better to cut out two details of the skirt, with slightly slanted sections up, in other words, in the form of a trapezoid, with only a slight slope. This is done in order to remove excess folds near the gum. Further, the sewing process is the same as in the first version: seams and strap.

Having figured out some of the nuances of how to sew a skirt on the floor with an elastic band, you can easily get to work and be sure that at the end of all your work there will be a great reward in the form of a fashionable new thing.
