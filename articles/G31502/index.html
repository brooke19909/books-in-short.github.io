Location

The Rossiya Hotel (Borisoglebsk) is located in the very center of the city, located at the confluence of the Khopra and Vorona rivers and named after the saints Boris and Gleb, who are especially revered in Russia. Borisoglebsk - the city of military pilots, as well as one of the historical cities known in Russia - owes its origin to Emperor Peter the Great. According to reviews, it is a fairly quiet place, still preserving the atmosphere of the county town. The address of the Rossiya Hotel: Borisoglebsk, Voronezh Region, Svobody Street, 180 / Lenin Square, 2. Opposite the hotel is the city administration building, a little further there is a memorial complex, a municipal drama theater and a historical and art museum. The distance from the Rossiya Hotel in Borisoglebsk is:

  • to the airport - 245 km;
  • to the center - 0.5 km;
  • to the train station - 1.5 km.
