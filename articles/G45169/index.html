Year-round recreation center "Pavlovsky Park", Ufa. Holiday Village in Bashkiria

Open all year round, Pavlovsky Park (Ufa) is a place where people tired of problems are energized, distracted from everyday routine and learn all the delights of idle life. Let's consider all this in more detail.

Description

The recreation center in Bashkiria "Pavlovsky Park" is located on the shore of the Pavlovsky reservoir, which is 7 km from the village of Pavlovka, and from the city of Ufa - 20 km. It has everything: exceptional nature, and the Ural continental climate with lots of sunny clear days, and clean air. This is a comfortable zone, created to restore energy, strength, as well as a study of historical sights and natural monuments.





Guest Accommodation

Pavlovsky Park (Ufa) is a modern hotel complex consisting of a main building and a service center, which occupy an administrative building of 3 floors, and three 2-storey cottages.

Categories of rooms located in the cottage and office building differ in terms of comfort.

Attic

This is a one-room room located on the 2nd or 3rd floor, with amenities. There are 2 beds, a coffee table, 2 nightstands, a TV, 2 ottomans, a sofa, a wardrobe, a bathroom (toilet and shower). Attic rooms 20 pieces.

Suite

This is an increased category of comfort. This room can accommodate a family of 4-6 people. Two-room room with a living room, a spacious bathroom, which for greater convenience is fitted with floor heating, and a storage room. The furniture consists of a double (one) and single beds (two), a sofa, a cupboard, a coffee table, a TV, a dressing room. The room has a kettle, fridge and kitchenware. There are 4 such numbers.

Superior Suite

Three-room suite with a living room, a bathroom with a floor heating, a pantry and a toilet. The rooms are equipped with: one 2-double bed, two single beds, sofa, dressing room, 2 nightstands, coffee table, kettle, refrigerator and TV. There are 2 suites in this category.

Standard

There are one-room rooms on the 2-3rd floor in the main building, or on the 1st floor in the cottage. Room with all amenities. The furniture consists of two beds, 2 nightstands, coffee table, sofa, TV, wardrobe, 2 pouffes, an absorption refrigerator. In the bathroom: shower and toilet. The rooms are standard 30 pieces.

Dining at the hotel

Pavlovsky Park (Ufa) is a cafe-bar where you can comfortably sit both together and celebrate a celebration with a large number of guests. The banquet room seats 70 people.

Guests staying at the hotel eat 3 times a day (the price includes meals). In winter, on a cold day, you can warm yourself up with mulled wine or a cup of hot chocolate. And in the summer, on the contrary, under a fresh breeze, sit on the summer terrace, enjoying aromatic meat on coals and nature.
