general information

The hotel, built in 1976 according to the original design, in the Soviet years was a hostel of the Krai Committee of the CPSU, in which party workers stayed. Due to this circumstance, from the very first day of creation, an appropriate level of customer service quality was laid here . When in 1991 the building was transferred from the status of party property to the State Property Committee, it became a legally independent object - Oktyabrskaya Hotel joint-stock company.

Krasnoyarsk annually receives several hundred thousand guests. Many of them have long made their choice. This is Oktyabrskaya (hotel).

There are few hotels in Krasnoyarsk that have such a good reputation. This complex, located in the center of Krasnoyarsk, offers a high level of service combined with excellent quality of service, and therefore is quite popular.

This is the first hotel complex in the region that has passed the classification system and confirmed the assigned category. In 2014, it was reconstructed. The hotel is only four hundred meters from the center of Krasnoyarsk. The train station is within walking distance. To the airport - half an hour drive.
