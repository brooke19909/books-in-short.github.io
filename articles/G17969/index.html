The prefix "Dandy": the story of creation

Few people know that such a popular console is just an unofficial copy of the third-generation NES consoles. The fact is that the products of the Japanese company Nintendo have never been officially released on the territory of the former CIS. The local market was not interesting to foreign investors due to relative poverty and flourishing piracy. But thanks to the latter, domestic gamers were able to taste most of the game hits of the time.

The enterprising company Steepler occupied a free niche in 1992 by launching a game console for sale in Russia, which was assembled in Taiwan and was a hardware clone of Nintendo consoles. At the same time, the logo (an elephant in a red shirt and blue cap) was developed by domestic animator Ivan Maximov.

The prefix "Dandy", the price of which at that time was about $ 94, was very fond of the local public, which allowed the company to sell more than a million copies of the console by 1994.

Lineup of Dandy consoles

Over time, the Steepler product range has changed. More sophisticated and equipped models were born.





Initially, in 1992, Micro Genius IQ-501 and 502 consoles entered the domestic market (in Russia they were called Dendy Classic I and II, respectively). Both variations were very different in appearance from the original NES, and the joysticks looked more like toys than devices.

In 1993, more advanced models with built-in support for the gun controller became available to consumers from the CIS. It's about Dendy Junior, who had much more in common with his Japanese original. In addition, the supplier company produced other clones of the console (for example, the Subor prefix), which had a slightly different device.

The Dandy brand and its life in the media

Such a stunning popularity of consoles in the domestic market led to the fact that the simple business of distributing consoles overgrown with a whole commercial network, and then the cultural heritage.

In 1994, Steepler, with the participation of Inkombank, founded the Dandy subsidiary, which distributed game consoles.





She even managed to get exclusive rights to implement the official NES consoles in the same year. A similar decision by the Japanese company was caused by the intention of a domestic company to boycott the sale of another Sega Mega Drive console known at that time.

The media did not leave aside the fact of the success of virtual products in Russia. Several thematic programs have appeared that tell about the novelties and subtleties of the game world. Tournaments were held for various games, which were also broadcast by television. In general, it was the appearance of Dendy that marked the dawn of the gaming industry in the territory of the former USSR.

Nintendo and Sega competition

Sega Master System and NES were the two most popular 8-bit consoles in the late 80s and early 90s of the last century. In the future, experts will confirm that Nintendo won this competition in the US market due to the lower price of the game console and high-quality exclusives. The situation in the domestic market is approximately the same.

Consoles with a portable NES system, as follows from the reviews, were more popular due to the elementary nature of copying the console architecture.
