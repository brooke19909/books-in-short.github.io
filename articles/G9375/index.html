Soltsy (Novgorod region) - acquaintance with the city

Soltsy (Novgorod region) is a small historical town located 80 km from the regional municipal center of the region, spreading its expanses on the banks of the Shelon River. The village is located at the intersection of roads: Novgorod-Porkhov-Pskov with the Volot highway. It also has a railway connection to the Vitebsk direction (St. Petersburg - Kiev, Chisinau, Pskov).

The population of the city is more than 10 thousand people. Most of the residents are quite friendly and hospitable to the large flow of visitors who come to see Soltsy (Novgorod region). The map will help you find this settlement, which is a fairly visited city in the area.

As for economic development, the city has well-developed production of magnetic wires, gloves and mittens. There is a bakery and two food plants. The city is producing brick clay, peat and limestone.



Historical background (beginning)

The first mention of the city of Soltsy (Novgorod region) dates from the end of the XIV century. Since ancient times, the settlement, and later the merchant village, has been the main stopping point on the trade route from Novgorod to the Pskov region. In 1471, walking on the right side of the river. The troops of the ruler of Ivan III crossed the riverbed and completely defeated the troops of the Novgorodians. This battle was called Shelonsky and introduced a decisive factor in the history of this area on its accession to the Moscow unified state.

At the end of 1579, by decree of Tsar Ivan the Terrible, the city of Soltsy (Novgorod Region) was organized in the Wheel Suburb. Tax-free carts were required to manufacture cannon carriages and wheels. Thus, for about 200 years the great army of Russia was supplied with wheels.

Over time, the managerial position of the village began to grow, which passed into the village, and then into the settlement. In 1914, Soltsy Posad was proclaimed a city by the High Council of Ministers. In 1915, one of the merchant families (Bogovskoye) opened a sanatorium for injured officers in its "mansions", after which the city received the status of a resort. At the end of the 1920s, there were already 3 sources of mineral waters on its territory. At the same time, a mud and balneological resort begins to function.



City development: from 1927 to the present day

In 1927, the city was transformed into the center of the Soletsky district of the Leningrad Region. But after administrative division, he became directly subordinate to the Leningrad Executive Committee.

During the Great Patriotic War, under the influence of fascist occupation, most of the buildings and structures of Soltsy were destroyed. But after its completion, the city was rebuilt almost anew, while restoring a large number of monuments of architecture and urban planning. That is why Soltsy is considered a historical settlement.



Sights of the city of Soltsy (Novgorod region)

Outstanding and unique chic to the city is given by ancient buildings of the central areas: merchant houses, Pokrovskaya wooden church, Ilyinsky Cathedral of the 1920s. In the suburbs is the 840th aviation bomber regiment, which became part of the 37th strategic air army. In the former home of the merchant Krasikov, a rather interesting and unusual city museum is located, where you can admire various artworks of local masters of painting and decorative art. The diverse buildings of the ancient estates and the picturesque green park will amaze you with their beauty and grandeur. Here everyone is invited to visit the Vasilkov family estate in the village of Vybyti, which is located 10 km from the settlement in question. That is why the city of Soltsy (Novgorod region) is so popular among tourists. Numerous cultural institutions make it a very interesting area for visitors. Here you can visit the famous house of culture, the city leisure center, where there is a weaving club, a union of spiritual chants, and a drawing studio. It is also worth a visit to the district library, which is active in reviving folk customs and maintaining crafts. The Novgorod branch of the regional philharmonic society operates in the city.



Infrastructure

Of the modern buildings, one can distinguish: the Ellipse factory, a flax factory, a regional communications center, a hospital, a music school, several construction facilities, a lecture hall, kindergartens and modern buildings for residents.

In the city of Soltsy (Novgorod region) there is a hotel, public and river transport, which helps any visitor to feel comfortable without feeling inconvenience when viewing local attractions.

Total

It is definitely worth a visit to this city. It is quite interesting and promising. Those travelers who did not bypass the village never regretted stopping by here. Heat, light rain, rainbow - typical weather for this region. Soltsy (Novgorod region) is a rather interesting and prosperous city.
