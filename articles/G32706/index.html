Master class how to make a bumper on the phone with your own hands

Today you rarely see a phone without a case. It helps not only protect the gadget's body from damage, but also diversify its appearance. We suggest you learn how to make a bumper for your phone yourself.

Changeable pictures





The easiest option to decorate the bumper on your phone with your own hands is to use a ready-made illustration from a magazine, book or poster. You can also print any picture you like.

To make such an accessory you will need to buy a transparent bumper case for your phone. With your own hands, circle it on a piece of paper with a pencil or pen. Cut out the pattern. This will be a blank for further crafts.

Attach the pattern to the back of the desired picture and circle it. Carefully cut out the illustration. Do not forget to make holes for the camera and speakers, if any.

Now it remains to insert a picture inside the case. Everything, the bumper on the phone with your own hands is ready. The main thing is that you can make as many pictures as you like and change them as often as you need.

Glued elements





The second popular way to decorate the cover with your own hands is to glue decorative elements to the purchased bumper. This is pretty fast, and the finished result has an effective look.

A master class on how to decorate a bumper for a phone with your own hands:

  1. Take an old unnecessary case or buy a new one.
  2. Prepare decorative elements. These can be rhinestones of various colors, sizes and shapes, beads, rivets and spikes, unusual fittings (buttons, small brooches) and so on.
  3. If the cover is not fully pasted over, but only partially, then take care of the background. Most likely it will need to be painted over. Then you need acrylic paint or spray.
  4. Lay out the decorative elements on the back of the bumper in the order they should be in the end. If you want to lay out the decor linearly, and not in the pattern, then take a ruler and lay out objects on it. So you can make straight lines.
  5. Take tweezers and glue-moment.
  6. Gently glue each element, taking it with tweezers.

All is ready. The only drawback of such a bumper is that if you want to change the cover, you will need to buy a new one.

Coloring by varnishes





Using nail polishes is also a good way to decorate a case. True, it requires much more time than those described above. After all, some varnish needs to be applied in two layers, and also it must be allowed to dry well.

So, for work you will need an old or new cover and nail polishes. Come up with a future design. The easiest option is to make a graphic drawing (for example, zigzags or stripes). If you dwell on this decor, then you will need to take another masking tape.

Operating procedure:

  1. Degrease the cover with nail polish remover.
  2. Make markings on the bumper with masking tape. To do this, stick it in the necessary places. For example, if you want to draw strips, then glue parallel pieces of a stitch with a small distance.
  3. Take the first color of the varnish and paint over the place free from adhesive tape. Let the layer dry.
  4. If you have more than two colors, remove the first cattle and stick a new one in other places.
  5. Take another varnish and draw more strips.
  6. So make as many colorful stripes as you need.
  7. Let the varnish dry, and then cover the cover with a colorless top coat.

Do-it-yourself color bumper on the phone is ready. If, after some time after use, the varnish begins to crack or crumble, then this can be easily corrected using the same colors.

Glitter Case





Such a bumper on the phone with your own hands will turn out not only stylish, but also very effective. Such an accessory will be especially relevant during the winter holidays, when everything sparkles around.

For work, you need to take some kind of cover, sequins, decoupage glue or diluted PVA and a brush. To get started, pour part of the glue in the saucer and pour most of the sparkles into it. Shuffle everything with a brush. Then apply a shiny mixture to the case. Sprinkle the rest of the spangles on top. Let the cover dry thoroughly.

Thus, it is possible to make not only plain bumpers. The gradient effect will look very nice - a smooth transition from one color to another.
