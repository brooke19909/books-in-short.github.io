Sanya Hotels - Upscale Holidays in South China

This city is located in the southernmost point of China - in a place that combines a mild tropical climate and picturesque landscapes, a variety of entertainment and the most comfortable stay in the world. That is why the city attracts so many tourists who find here all the best and most interesting. Sanya resort offers the perfect vacation - here you can swim and sunbathe on the beaches literally all year round, both in summer and in winter.

Many call this place a kingdom of entertainment. It offers guests the opportunity to sail, jet ski or ski, dive, or just lazily lie on the beach and enjoy the fabulous South Chinese atmosphere of the sea, sun and tranquility. Also for tourists from all over the world, Sanya hotels open their doors, comfortable, well located, guaranteeing a wonderful vacation and a developed entertainment infrastructure. In their territories, guests will be offered to make stunning excursions or take part in competitions held by local golf clubs, to become a spectator and fan of various spectacular events or to participate in them personally.

A lot of Sanya hotels are out of competition, because their guests get the opportunity to indulge in restaurants located directly on the territory of the complexes, tasting traditional Chinese, many European and even Russian dishes. All local restaurants, restaurants and cafes are usually located near the coast, so eating will turn into an exciting adventure - immersion in the natural world of South China. Some tourists, leaving reviews of Sanya hotels, noted that their owners often combine the culinary services of their guests with a guided tour - for example, organizing breakfast or lunch in an ethnic village, in a cafe or restaurant belonging to the indigenous population.

One of the most popular hotels in southern China is Sanya Marriott Resort and Spa 5 *. It is located on the shore of the "Dragon Bay" - Yalong Bay, on a sunny beach where you can have a wonderful vacation without doing anything, just enjoying the warm sunshine and the gentle sea.
