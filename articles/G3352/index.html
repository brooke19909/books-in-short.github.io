The recreation center "Korogon" (Altai Mountains, Chemal district): description, reviews

When planning a family vacation, you should carefully choose the time and place of the trip. It is also necessary to take into account the wishes of family members who prefer forest, sea or mountain recreation. In this case, it is best to choose a specialized boarding house or rest house, in the territory of which you can easily relax for all family members. One of such institutions is the notorious recreation center "Korogon" (Altai Mountains). Where is she located? What services and types of relaxation does it offer?





General information about the recreation center

Korogon is a large recreation center located in the Chemal district on the banks of the Middle Katun River. It is located 338 km from Barnaul and 512 km from Novosibirsk. It is famous for its unusually picturesque views, remoteness from noisy city life and a large number of options for entertaining family leisure.

The rebellious and proud Altai Territory

Translated from the Mongolian "Altai" is translated as "golden". This is truly an unusual region, comfortably located in the very center of Eurasia and affecting the territory of the following states: Mongolia, Kazakhstan, China and Russia. It is famous for its bright and impressively versatile relief, since here you can meet not only wide basins and picturesque mountain ranges, but also unforgettable landscapes of the steppes and forests of the taiga.

All the delights of the weather in the Altai Territory

Beautiful and unique weather in Altai. It is characterized by an uneven distribution of precipitation by season. So, in the western regions of the region, approximately 40% of precipitation falls. Moreover, in winter, the thickness of the snow cover is 2-3, less often 5 m.

In the summer, Altai is very hot. This is due to its location and distance from the oceans. In winter it is cold and windy, but often the weather is clear and frosty. Cottages, recreation area, recreation area

The territory of the recreation center is small. But, according to experienced vacationers, everything is placed conveniently and compactly here. And most importantly, it is practically isolated from extraneous noise.

Since the boarding house is intended exclusively for seasonal recreation, its residential complex includes 45 summer houses and 27 Alpine buildings with a total of 144 main places. There is a guarded parking lot, a children's room, a children's and adult pools, an area where people can cook barbecue, sports grounds parking and rollerdrome.

The guesthouse also offers bike hire and other sports equipment. It is planned to organize excursions and conduct various sports events.

How to get to the camp site?

Going to the Korogon tourist complex, try to develop the optimal route for yourself. For example, if you plan to go by car, then your path will lie through the Biysk-Maima-Ust-Sema highway section. Then you need to get to the fork and go in a straight line towards Chemal. Very soon you will arrive in the small village of Uznesia, and just a stone's throw from it to the tourist complex (another 600 m).

If your choice fell on public transport, you should know that you can get to the camp site by daily scheduled buses from Gorno-Altaysk, Barnaul, Novosibirsk and Biysk.

In the period from June to the end of September, the recreation center "Korogon" (Gorny Altai - the region of a wonderful family vacation) also organizes bus delivery for vacationers. Moreover, transport leaves from Omsk, Novosibirsk or Barnaul, and departures and arrivals are carried out on Fridays and Sundays.

From what age are children accepted at the base?

According to the visitors, the children at Korogon are admitted without limiting their age. The main thing is that they be accompanied by one or more adults. Moreover, children under the age of 10 can settle with their parents, but without providing a place for them. An extra bed is available if necessary, but it must be paid separately. Children under 3 years old can stay without paying for meals. If desired, a dairy kitchen can work for kids of this age. Starting from 3 years, payment of food is required.





The recreation center "Korogon" (Altai Mountains): what is included in the price of the permit?

The price of a voucher for one person includes the following services and types of service:

  • accommodation in comfortable rooms (with amenities or not);
  • Three meals a day;
  • provision of a parking space in a guarded parking lot;
  • visit to the pool;
  • use of equipment for swimming and relaxation (deck chairs);
  • provision of equipment for sports;
  • the ability to use public showers and toilets (in the pension);
  • the provision of teacher services and visits to children's rooms;
  • visit and use of rooms for ironing.

Speaking separately about nutrition, then its purchase is required. Its cost is calculated taking into account the established tariff per person. Full-fledged complex meals include breakfast, lunch and dinner in a small summer cafe located in the boarding house. For young children, the cafe has special high chairs with protection.





What kind of entertainment is provided?

There is a playground and a pool for children at the camp site. For preschool kids there is a mini kindergarten. The teacher works in it and there is a set of free toys, games, audio and video equipment. It is here that parents can safely leave their kids, for example, during their stay on excursions.

During their holidays, parents can visit the pool, go on excursions, rent sports equipment and bicycles, rent equipment for a fire (barbecue, skewers), visit baths, a rollerdrome and arrange rafting.
