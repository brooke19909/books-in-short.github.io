Choose yarn

Knitting winter cardigans with knitting needles (patterns for women will be further) is best done with thick wool yarn. In its pure form, it can be merino wool. This yarn is very warm, soft, does not irritate the skin and does not cause itching. You can also use blended yarn, for example, a combination of wool and acrylic. A product knitted from such a yarn is warm and not prickly.

For a 42-44 size cardigan, 1000-1100 grams of yarn will be required.

Also for work, you need circular knitting needles that are suitable in size to the selected yarn. If you take the needles one and a half to two sizes larger, the product will be more loose and voluminous.





Basic Cardigan Pattern

Instead of knitting cardigan patterns for women, we offer a detailed description, since the pattern is very simple:

  • In the first (wrong) row, knit one wrong side of the loop, then double crochet, knit the next two stitches together on the wrong side. Repeat, and at the end of the row, knit the edge loop from the wrong side.
  • In the second row, the first loop goes first, then remove one loop on the right knitting needle, then two facial ones. Now pull the removed loop through the two knitted.
  • The third row begins with two wrong seams, followed by the yarn. Continue to alternate to the edge.
  • In the fourth row, the first loop must be removed on the right knitting needle, then two front ones, stretch the shot through the knitted ones. There should be a facial loop at the end of the row.
  • The fifth row begins with the wrong side, then comes the double crochet, two wrong side. Knit once from the first to fifth row, and then repeat from the second to fifth.

The elastic in this cardigan model is 2x2.

Knit a cardigan





Knitting patterns for cardigans for women, as well as patterns, can be completely different. In this example, consider a model that fits into a single fabric.

Left shelf:

  • Type about 30 stitches into circular knitting needles. Start knitting from the wrong side: hem, then five loops with a garter stitch, then 22 loops with an elastic band.
  • Knit 10 cm and add one loop.
  • Next, in the wrong row, after the hem and five loops of the garter stitch, start knitting the main pattern that was described earlier.
  • After 20 cm, add one loop in front of the cardigan strap. Next, in each fourth row, add one loop (a total of nine times).
  • Having knitted 38 cm, add on the right side for the sleeve, first one loop, and then 2, 3, 6, 10, 20.

The right shelf fits in the same way.

Knitting patterns for cardigans for women can and should be adjusted individually for each specific model. Do not be afraid to experiment, choose unusual models and combine them with schemes of interesting drawings.
