What is ATF Bank Almaty?

Today ATF Bank Almaty is firmly entrenched in the field of lending; it is one of the members of the UniCredit Group in Kazakhstan. The Bank works with corporate and private clients, representatives of medium and small businesses, legal entities and individuals. All JSC products allow customers to increase wealth and ease their financial situation.





About the bank

Alma-Ata Trade and Finance Bank is considered the largest in the Republic of Kazakhstan. Among the other banks in terms of assets, it is in fourth place.

ATF Bank has been operating since 1995; it was this year that it received a General License. The re-registration of the organization in ATF Bank JSC takes place in 2003. In 2006, the company ranks third among the banks of Kazakhstan and the thirteenth largest asset among CIS countries.

In the Kyrgyz Republic , ATF has a subsidiary bank, ATF Bank-Kyrgyzstan OJSC.

Bank services

The main services of the bank are divided into branches:

1. Operational service.

This department provides services to legal entities and individuals. Here, control over ongoing operations and transfers is carried out, customer service is improved . Also, banking products and services are sold in this department, and the currency regime is controlled. You can immediately get advice and international settlements.

2. Deposit department.

For a long time, the Bank has been a leader in issuing deposits to individuals. This is influenced by high deposit rates and the reliability of the bank.

ATF provides its customers with ideal conditions for growing savings. A deposit can be opened both with interest capitalization and without. The Bank provides for a system of replenishment and partial withdrawal of a deposit.

Types of deposits offered by the bank:

  • Deposit "Convenient".
  • Cumulative payment card “Piggy bank in your pocket”.
  • Deposit “On Demand”.
  • "ATF - Legalization."

3. Credit

This is one of the main branches in the bank, which brings most of the income. The credit department includes: retail credit, commercial and collateral.

A loan decision can be obtained in fifteen minutes with a minimum package of documents. The bank also provides preferential offers. All loan terms are affordable and profitable.

Types of loans:

  • Without collateral "Easy."
  • Car loan to buy a domestic car.
  • A loan secured by real estate.
  • A loan secured by a car.
  • Car loan.
  • Refinancing loans.
  • Mortgage.
  • Mortgage "Green Quarter".
  • Mortgage refinancing.




ATF Bank Almaty: reviews

In the field of lending, the organization presented itself as a high-quality and promising bank. The company from the beginning of its existence very quickly received regular customers who still use its services.

The organization is popular among different strata of the population. Customers are very pleased with the extensive list of bank services and the quick decision to issue a loan. That is why the reviews about the bank are mostly only positive.

Another distinguishing feature of the bank is its reliability. The terms of the loan agreement after signing remain unchanged, deposits are insured, borrowers borrowed money receive everything to the penny. This all attracts ATF customers, so they go there again and again.

ATF Bank Almaty: addresses

The Bank has 99 branches in all regions of the republic and in cities of republican significance. In total, 35 cities of Kazakhstan are covered .

The main office of the bank is located at: Almaty, st. Panfilova - 98A. The central offices are also located in the cities: Astana, Atyrau, Aktobe, Kostanay.





From the very beginning of its opening, ATF Bank Almaty has established itself as one of the leaders. Over time, he became the best throughout the Republic of Kazakhstan. The impetus for this was the introduction of Western technologies and methods by the bank into its structure.
