What is a bad story

Some financial institutions provide a service of familiarization with their own history. They will give you a printout listing all your loans and credits, their amounts and terms, as well as everything that you have delayed or not paid at all. Access to these data is available to all banks and organizations involved in lending to the population. Any case when you borrow money is recorded in special databases and becomes visible to all other similar institutions. So banks are trying to protect themselves from customers who will not bring them profits and even possibly organize financial losses.

How to find out your credit history

Some banks may provide you this information for free, others will take some kind of commission. All such stories are contained in the so-called Credit Bureau. They are stored for 15 years since the last recording. The bank is entitled to transmit this document to the bureau only with your consent, and it is also possible to obtain information from it only with your appeal. The moment you fill out an application for a loan or sign a contract, you automatically give such permission. You can find out the code of your history on the website of the main bank of Russia, as well as through any financial institution upon presentation of an identity document. Once a year, the story is provided free of charge. If you access this data more often, you will have to pay.
