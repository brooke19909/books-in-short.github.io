How many chips are in backgammon. Differences in the rules of the game

Many have heard about backgammon, but they believe that this game is difficult or long, even without ever playing it. This game is full of symbolism - 12 cells - months, 30 chips - days / nights, and on the bones the sum of the opposing sides is equal to the number of days in a week.

Modern rules appeared in 1743 in Britain. How many chips in backgammon - depends on the game, their number can vary both up and down. It is easy to play backgammon (players from 6 years old), it has simple rules, one round lasts about 10-20 minutes. Another interesting fact is that the game depends on the random value of the dice, so you do not need to calculate each move like in chess.

Short backgammon or long?

There are two main types - short and long backgammon. Short ones require a higher level of player skill, so tournaments are held mainly for this type of game. They are called short not because of how many chips are in backgammon, but because of their dynamism and fascination. With their great similarities, these games have a number of differences that change the gameplay.



The common thing in them is that they are designed for two players, each of them receives 15 one-color chips, they are placed in the house - their corner of the playing field.

One of the main differences is the arrangement of chips and the ability to play broken checkers. During the game, the chips go through the entire playing field (in short backgammon moves are carried out in opposite directions, in long backgammon - in one, counterclockwise). Going around the circle, the chips return to the house and are removed from the field. Whoever does this first is the winner.

The playing field, dice and chips are the same when playing any backgammon. How many chips should be depends not only on whether it is long backgammon or short, but also on the variation of any of these games. Usually there are from 5 to 15 for each player.
