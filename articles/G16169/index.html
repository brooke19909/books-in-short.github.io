Sights of Abrau-Durso (Krasnodar Territory): excursions, photo. What to see in Abrau-Durso?

Abrau-Durso (Krasnodar Territory) was previously an urban-type settlement. Currently it is a rural district. The latter includes three villages - Bolshoi Khutor, Abrau and Durso.

Famous lake

The sights of Abrau-Durso include the creations of not only man, but also nature. In some places, the depth of the lake reaches ten meters. A river with the same name flows into it. At the bottom of Abrau, the keys beat, and although there is no runoff by the lake, the water disappears somewhere.



Another mystery of the reservoir is the light streak periodically arising on its surface. Most clearly, it stands out at night. In the cold season, this strip freezes last. This feature is explained in different ways: some are of the opinion that a similar effect is observed due to the special interaction of wind and water, while others believe that the key is in the keys breaking at the bottom.

Lake Abrau-Durso has amazing beauty. The water in it has a pleasant emerald color. In the warm season, it warms up to 28 degrees Celsius. Freshwater fish live in the lake, including common carp, bream, ram, grass carp, trout, silver and gold crucian carp, silver carp, rudd and Abrau fish.

Since 1979, the reservoir has the status of a natural monument. For this reason, motor boats are not allowed to move around the lake. Fish can only be fished.

In the vicinity of the described reservoir is the village of the same name Abrau. Together with another small village - Durso - they form a single recreational area called Abrau-Durso. The shores of the lake are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable beach holiday. Church of St. Xenia of Petersburg

Some sights of Abrau-Durso attract pilgrims from all over the country. Among such places is the temple, majestically towering above the lake surface. It was founded in 1996. Four years later, the construction of the temple complex intensified. It was blessed by the Metropolitan of Yekaterinodar and Kuban Isidore. The church in a festive atmosphere consecrated at the end of 2010 the bishop of Maykop and Adygea Tikhon.

In 2011, the fifteenth anniversary of the founding of the Church of St. Xenia of St. Petersburg was celebrated. A video material on the process of building a sacred place for all Orthodox people was prepared for the solemn date.

Sparkling Beverage Production

"Abrau-Durso" - a factory of champagne wines, built in the nineteenth century. Then, by order of Alexander the Second, an estate was built near Lake Abrau to live the imperial family. Two years after the construction, the emperor‚Äôs personal agronomist went to Germany. From abroad, he brought twenty thousand Rhine-breeding Riesling vines. In order to produce red wine near Vienna, he acquired the Portuguese vines. In the same period, the best examples of Sauvignon, Aligote, Cabernet and Pinot Blanc varieties were delivered from Livadia and Crimea. The first vineyards were broken in the estates of Pilenko and Alexander II. Later, both of them acted as examples of the development of viticulture in those places.



At the Yalta Exhibition, held in 1884, the wines of the Abrau-Durso factory were met better than the Crimean wines, which were considered the undisputed leaders of domestic winemaking.

Sweet Estuary

This lake is located on the Black Sea. Several kilometers separate it from Novorossiysk and Abrau-Durso. Excursions at this reservoir are held regularly. Numerous tourists are attracted by the secrets of the lake, which no one still has the power to reveal.

Sweet Limanchik is separated from the Black Sea by a thin stone strip. It is assumed that the pond arose as a result of a severe earthquake, as a result of which the blocks that fell from the mountain formed a rash. The height of the latter is currently thirty-five meters.

And now a little about the above secrets: the water in the lake is fresh and surprisingly clean, although the sea is very close by. Scientists are still trying to solve this mystery of nature.



An asphalt road leads to Sweet Limanchik. The difficulties with the passage to the reservoir are that it is located near the recreation center of the RSU. In the warm season, tourists organize tent settlements on the picturesque shores of the lake. In the same period, a recreation center opens. Travelers are prohibited from washing things and washing dishes in the clear waters of Sweet Estuary. And although this rule is strictly observed, the reservoir, unfortunately, continues to degrade.

Beautiful river

Listing the sights of Abrau-Durso, one can not help but mention this waterway. Its source is located in the southwestern slope of Mount Gudzak. Durso joins the waters of the Black Sea near the BO Sputnik. In its valley is the village of the same name. Since 1871, it has been part of Abrau-Durso.

In the 1970s, archaeologists discovered a medieval burial ground in the Durso Valley. This monument of antiquity is only partially preserved, it remained after the newcomers. An interesting fact is that this burial ground has no analogues in the entire Caucasus, however, similar burials were found in the Crimea. According to the version of scientists, the latter left the Crimean Goths.

The Durso River is notable for a short length of only seventeen kilometers. Nevertheless, travelers rush to her to enjoy beautiful views of the valley planted with vineyards.

The main excursion program

What to see in Abrau-Durso? Local guides will help you answer this question. A five-hour excursion starts from Anapa. There, tourists get on a bus and follow to the Abrau-Durso agricultural complex.



1 hour and 15 minutes are spent on a walk through the French tunnel and wine tasting. You can listen to a lecture in the historic room and explore the cellar. Tourists are given a unique opportunity to learn all the features of the production process of a sparkling drink and its aging. Six brands of champagne, sixty milliliters each, are available for tasting.

You can stroll through the Alexander tasting room. The program of this tour includes a thematic lecture and a visit to champagne workshops. Then you are invited to view a phased technological exposition and, of course, a tasting of the most popular brands of sparkling drink. Travelers are advised to have warm clothes with them, as the temperature in the cellars is maintained at fourteen degrees.



Neighborhood

Then travelers leave the famous factory to explore other sights of Abrau-Durso (photos are allowed to be taken on their own, no fee is charged). The guided tour continues on the shore of Lake Abrau. Tourists learn a lot of interesting things about this pond. Some time is given for an independent walk (about half an hour). Swimming in the lake is not provided.



Tourists under the age of eighteen are not allowed on the tour. Travelers with children leave their children under the supervision of educators. A special room is organized for the kids. The service is paid.

Conclusion

The natural and man-made sights of Abrau-Durso have attracted travelers from all over the world for more than a year. In this rural district famous for its champagne wines, you can achieve unity with nature, relax from the bustle of megacities and learn a lot.
