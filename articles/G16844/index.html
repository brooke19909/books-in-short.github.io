Base "Source" (Donetsk, Rostov region) - a pleasure somewhere nearby

On one of the most beautiful bends of the Seversky Donets in the Rostov region, guests are waiting for the Istok.

Welcome

Base "Source" (Donetsk, Rostov region) operates year-round. There are all conditions for a comfortable and complete rest: cozy large rooms, a restaurant with a spacious hall and European cuisine, a sauna, a pool (heated on cloudy days) for adults and children, water activities, walking or jogging routes along shady forest paths, walking squares, fine sand beach, umbrellas, sunbeds, a marina, a guarded parking zone, etc.





Apartments

The Istok base (Donetsk, Rostov Region) offers several accommodation options for vacationers. Prices start at 2,500 rubles.

The one-room house is designed for two people. Equipped with a large bed, TV, fridge and air conditioning. The room has a shower with toilet, water (hot and cold) and a small bar. In the cold season, the room is heated.





In the one-room house with a separate veranda there is a double bed, a TV, a fridge, a split, a toilet, a shower, water (hot and cold). In winter, the house is heated.

The Istok recreation center (Donetsk, Rostov Region) offers VIP apartments to accommodate a large family or company. The room is designed for six people and has a veranda and a covered balcony overlooking the beach and river bend. Each room has a kitchen combined with a seating area, a sofa, a TV, a fridge, a split system, a bathroom with a shower, a minibar and a sauna. The house is surrounded by its own courtyard with a barbecue area. In winter, the room is heated. This superior apartment has a private access to the beach and an individual sauna, three hours of which are already included in the price of the room.

A restaurant

The Istok base (Donetsk, Rostov Region) has in its possession a cozy large restaurant with high-level staff. It is located in a separate building, on the ground floor of which there is a lobby bar, open until midnight, on the second floor there is a banquet hall. The bar is designed for 50 people, the hall - for 120.

The conditions created in the restaurant make it possible to hold various festivals, including graduation balls, corporate parties, banquets, anniversaries, family holidays, exit registrations, weddings and children's celebrations.





The Istok recreation center (Donetsk, Rostov Region) has a small VIP-hall (accommodates no more than 15 guests) and an open terrace with beautiful views of the bend of the Seversky Donets.

The menu is composed of dishes of European, Caucasian and Russian cuisines. For special events, the chef prepares exclusive and unique author's dishes.

By phone, you can book one of the halls for a special occasion or several tables for a soulful evening in the company of close friends. In the warm season, on Fridays, the restaurant hosts show programs with guest artists.

Taking care of every guest

Since last fall, the Istok base (Donetsk, Rostov Region) has been renting out double houses on an hourly basis. If necessary, an extension is possible. Entrance to the complex is now free.

As a gift for the New Year holidays at the reception you can buy gift cards with a face value of 5,000 to 25,000 thousand. You can present them to friends or give to your beloved people.

Indoor gazebos for recreation are organized at the base. They can easily accommodate a large company. Several fountains, walking paths in the park area and many landscaped areas for relaxation delight vacationers.





Entertainment for children and adults

In addition to the picturesque nature, the Istok base (Donetsk, Rostov Region) offers entertainment designed for both adults and children. One of the favorite places for young guests is the pool (it is heated on cool days) with many attractions. Kids ride from the hills, shoot from water cannons, frolic under a waterfall. It is not only cool and fun here, the most important thing is safe. Instructors monitor every movement of the child.

The adult pool has an aero and hydromassage area. They give an amazing relaxing effect. And this is very important in the modern constantly rushing and tense world. In addition, these types of massage have a beneficial effect on health: pressure and blood circulation are normalized, the work of the heart muscle is stabilized. Pool water is heated. The surrounding recreation area is equipped with sun umbrellas, comfortable sun loungers and tables for a comfortable stay. Perhaps that is why this part of the complex is very popular.

The Istok base (Donetsk, Rostov region) welcomes guests all year round. Reviews of vacationers, especially about winter holidays, are positively charged. Here, a stunning Finnish sauna is open almost round-the-clock; walks in winter extreme modes of transport and hunting are organized.
