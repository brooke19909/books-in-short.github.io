Rest in Marmaris: a fairy tale in reality.

Hundreds of thousands of tourists from different parts of the world come to Turkey annually. This country offers various leisure and pastime options. Probably every traveler has a favorite city in which he likes to be. Marmaris is worthy of staying in your heart forever. This is one of the most beautiful cities in Turkey. Holidays in Marmaris attract thousands of tourists. This city is located at the place where the Mediterranean and Aegean seas merge . Previously, it was an ordinary fishing village, which could later turn into a center of Turkish tourism. Last-minute tours to Marmaris are always popular and in demand.

This resort town is charming with its eucalyptus groves and palm alleys, spacious streets, restaurants on the waterfront, oriental bazaars, small shops that sell luxury carpets, and most importantly, a developed entertainment industry. Holidays in Marmaris are chosen by people, by nature, party-goers and lovers of fun, active and vibrant pastime. This city is considered a youth resort due to hundreds of discos, bars and restaurants. Last minute trips to Marmaris are very popular among people who are active, energetic and cheerful.

Travelers admire the magnificent local fresh air - a mixture of the smell of the sea and the pine forests that surround the city. This resort is recognized as the most environmentally friendly resort in Turkey. There are many picturesque natural corners, pristine sandy beaches, bays with clear turquoise water and gorgeous tropical vegetation. Holidays in Marmaris are attractive due to the climate. You can have a great time here for all 12 months of the year. Summer in Marmaris is hot and winter is warm. In addition, this city has a unique microclimate. Due to the presence of a wonderful bay, the air humidity does not exceed 35 percent and it is saturated with essential oils.

Holidays in Marmaris can be not only club or beach. This city has its rich history, so the excursion programs offered to tourists will appeal to you. The main attraction is the fortress, built in the 14th century. It is located in the center of the peninsula, and residents and visitors can see its strong battlements from afar. In addition, there are 2 museums on its territory.

It is definitely worth a visit to the Old Town, where there are many shops and the Caravanserai, built in the 16th century. There is also an old castle in the city, from the walls of which there is a stunning view not only of the whole city, but also of the Mediterranean and Aegean Seas.

Every tourist should see a natural monument that was created without human intervention. Nature created white stone cascades, as well as large terraces with shallow pools. It is believed that the water in them has anti-aging properties.

Near Marmaris is the island of Cleopatra with white sand. Scientists have proved that such sand is found only in North Africa, and it was brought here many centuries ago.

You can also take a boat ride from Marmaris to Venice. Boat trips, jeep safaris, rafting, yacht trips - all this will appeal to active and restless tourists.

A laid-back light atmosphere reigns in this city. It is worthwhile to come here more than once, because Marmaris is freedom, entertainment, the sun and a great mood.
