Tours to Florence (Italy)

Rome is called the heart of Italy, but Florence is its soul. This main city of Tuscany has not in vain attracted many centuries painters, sculptors, poets, writers and musicians. One has only to step on the pavements of the Arno embankments - and inspiration comes upon you. Medieval bridges, magnificent cathedrals, museums, which are considered the most visited in the world, all this fills the heart with delight (and the memory card of the camera with thousands of excellent pictures).

In this article we will look at how to get to this wonderful city, Florence. Independent travel requires a lot of energy, because you need to book a hotel, get tickets. And where to go in an unfamiliar city? An alternative to independent voyage will be a tour organized by the agency in Florence. What does he happen to be? How much does it cost? What cities does it cover? Read the article further!

How to get to Italy

If you live in the Asian part of Russia, then only one mode of transport will deliver you to Florence - an airplane. Aviation will help to get to other cities in Italy. But if you are a resident of the western regions of the Russian Federation, you have a certain alternative to air transport, moreover, it is tempting.

In the last decade, bus tours to Italy / Florence and beyond have become extremely popular in neighboring Ukraine. The groups leave both Kiev and the western cities of the country - Lviv, Lutsk and Uzhgorod. At first glance, it seems that it is unprofitable. After all, you have to independently get to the start of such a tour. And the ticket should be purchased "remotely", transferring funds to the account of a travel agency. In addition, you should be most concerned about the opening of a Schengen visa. But the price !!! From one hundred and thirty euros for a five-day voyage (about 9,000 rubles)! And along the way you will see not only Italy, but also Hungary, Austria or Slovenia.

Ukrainian bus tours are divided into two categories - with and without night crossings. The latter are more expensive, because they contain prices for hotels. For independent travelers, note: from Ukraine regular buses and private minibuses run to Italian cities, including to Florence. The issue price is about one hundred euros (7000 rubles).

What are the tours to Florence

Do you want to visit only this city? There is nothing easier. You can walk the streets of Dante, Petrarch and Michelangelo for three days. During this time, you can thoroughly visit all museums, including the Uffizi Gallery, as well as most temples. But if one gets out of Russia so far to Italy, how can one resist the temptation to see other cities? The classic tour "Rome - Florence - Venice - Milan" is very popular. It includes flight, hotel accommodation with breakfast and several excursions, most often sightseeing.

Along with the classic "excursions", agencies offer so-called "combined" tours. They provide a familiarization trip to Florence and plus beach holidays in the sea (summer) or ski (winter) resorts in Italy. There are also tours for events. So, you can watch Florence, and at the same time take part in the Venice Carnival or get to "Black Friday" in Milan. You can order themed tours - gastronomic, wine, medical, in the towns of Tuscany and others.

Typical Package Program

What services does a traveler who buys a ticket to the City of Dreams, Florence get? Russian and Ukrainian operators provide flight and group transfer from the airport to the hotel. By the way, the price of the tour (Florence) depends not only on the number of days spent in Italy and sightseeing, but also on the level of the hotel. You can book a hotel for three or four stars, as well as different categories of rooms. True, all hotels in this case only offer breakfast.

A classic tour to Florence provides a bus-pedestrian city tour. Entrance to museums is usually paid separately. Each package also provides for the so-called "optional" excursions - within Florence or to nearby towns - Luca, Siena. But they also have to pay separately. The Russian tour operator, which is important, provides visa support.
