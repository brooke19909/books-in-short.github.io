Review of Ibis Styles Nha Trang Hotel 4 * hotel (Vietnam)

Styles Nha Trang Hotel 4 * is a magnificent hotel in Vietnam, which will be discussed in this review.





In recent years, Vietnam has become a very popular resort among many tourists from both Russia and other countries. It has a wonderful climate, warm sea, the sun shines year-round. Many come here to enjoy the exotic and visit fascinating excursions, which are abundant here.

If we talk about the most common resort areas of Vietnam, we can distinguish several of the best and most popular:

  • Danang.
  • Dalat.
  • Fantiet.
  • Ho Chi Minh City
  • Phu Quoc.
  • Nha Trang.

The last corner is perhaps the most popular among tourists. There are a large number of different hotels, hostels and hotels that will be your great place to stay. It is here that the hotel is located, presented below.

Ibis Styles Nha Trang Hotel 4 *

A completely new tourist complex was built in 2017, consists of one building on 24 floors (second coastline). The road to the beach takes no more than five minutes walk. The hotel is very beautiful, has a modern interior design, very comfortable rooms. On the territory of "Ibis" there are two swimming pools, a wellness center for SPA-procedures, a sports ground, free parking, a magnificent playroom for children.

Hotel Location

Ibis Styles Nha Trang Hotel 4 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang) is located in the central area, three hundred meters from Tram Huong and a two-minute walk from the night market. The main square is a 5-minute walk away. The international airport is 26 kilometers from the hotel.

Number of rooms

The hotel offers guests 311 comfortable rooms, among special:

  • non-smoking guests
  • family
  • for guests with disabilities.




Each room provides for:

  • Air conditioner.
  • Fridge.
  • New furniture.
  • Kettle.
  • TV.
  • Private bathroom.
  • Phone.

Almost all rooms of Ibis Styles Hotel 4 * (Nha Trang) have free Wi-Fi. Drinking water is delivered to the room daily.

When you check in, a deposit is made - 500 thousand Vietnamese dongs. Upon departure, after checking the room, the money will be returned.

The tourist complex offers an outdoor pool, a bar, a restaurant, free internet, parking, car rental, a fitness center, a spa, a sauna, currency exchange, luggage storage, a laundry service and a tour desk.

Nutrition

The price of a ticket to the Ibis includes buffet breakfast. The complex's two-seat restaurant serves daily international and Vietnamese cuisines, a la carte dishes for dinner and lunch, and street food from traditional local dishes. The lobby bar offers light snacks and drinks. In addition, the complex has a snack bar, and there are vending machines with snacks and drinks.





Beach

100 meters from Ibis Styles Nha Trang Hotel 4 * is the sandy Changfu Municipal Beach. This beach area is considered the most popular in the city, since you can come here from any part of Nha Trang. The beach is connected to the beautiful promenade street, where there are numerous cafes and shops.

The beach offers an extensive range of services: scuba diving, surfing, yoga, spa, bike excursions, meditation.

Sun beds, umbrellas and deck chairs are available at a price of 40 to 120 thousand dong per day.

As for the cleanliness on the beach, there are minor problems with this. Due to its popularity and the large number of vacationers in the sea and on the sand, garbage can be found, but at the end of the day the beach is cleaned.

Accommodation with children

Ibis Styles Nha Trang Hotel 4 * accepts on its territory families with children of any age. One child under six years old without extra bed is free.

For young travelers, the hotel has a modern games room, a swimming pool, a free mini-club, children's cartoons, and books. The restaurant offers a children's menu.





Prices

Tours in Styles can be purchased from various tour operators at affordable prices. Roughly a weekly stay with breakfast in a double room will cost from 65 thousand rubles. A trip for 10 days will cost about 75,000 rubles. Visa to the country is not included in the price.

What do its guests think about the complex?

Judging by the reviews, Ibis Styles Nha Trang Hotel 4 * is a great new hotel. Tourists have a very high opinion of him. According to them, the complex is clean, beautiful, cozy, comfortable, interior is made in a modern style. The hotel staff is smiling and polite, the guys are always interested in what is needed. Many guests recommend the hotel and claim that they would love to return there again.

Exotic food: a lot of local fruits, bacon, eggs in different cooking options, chicken, pork, noodles, rice, vegetables and much more. Everything is always fresh and tasty.

The hotel has a great location, close to many restaurants and places for entertainment, but at the same time, the walls of the hotel are quiet and calm.

Conclusion

For all people who want to go to Vietnam, Ibis Styles Nha Trang Hotel 4 * will be an excellent place for accommodation. This new, comfortable and modern hotel will leave every tourist with only positive memories of the city of Nha Trang.
