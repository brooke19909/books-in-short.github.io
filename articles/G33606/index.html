Koltushskoe Lake - a great place for recreation and fishing (Leningrad Oblast)

The Leningrad region is truly rich in its reservoirs. These are such "giants" as Onega and Ladoga or the famous Vuoksa and Otradnoe. Among the 1800 reservoirs belonging to the water network of St. Petersburg, a small, little-studied Koltush lake also found its place. Reviews about him are only positive. Here you will find something to your liking and fishermen, and just tourists who came to swim and sunbathe.





Location and natural features

The reservoir is located in the village of Koltushi, Vsevolzhsky District, Leningrad Region, and has an elongated shape from southeast to northwest. The largest lake is small, about 500 m long and about 120 m wide. The total area is approximately 3 hectares.

Koltushskoye Lake belongs to the relict form of water bodies, that is, it was formed as a result of the outflow of the ancient sea. It has no river water, but at its bottom there are a large number of springs. They allow the lake not to dry out in the hot summer period.

The maximum depth is 4 meters. Such places are located approximately in the central part of the reservoir. The bottom is mostly flat, without drops and potholes, silty, and closer to the shore - sandy. In the shallow summer, duckweed appears. The coastal zone is soft, with a small number of backwaters. The shores are low, with a convenient approach to the water.

Name history

Koltushskoe Lake owes its name to the village, which is located near the reservoir, - Koltushi. The name of the settlement comes from the Finnish word kelttu or geltu and translates as "yellow".





Flora and fauna

The lake is located on the territory of the Koltushsky heights reserve. This is a stretch of nature, partially of glacial origin. Most of the protected area is the forest. Rare, endangered animals and birds are found here, such as garden oatmeal, partridge, hawk, and hare. Coltus Lake itself has become home to such a unique plant as Dortmann's lobelia.

The forest belt on the territory of Lake Koltush is rich in lingonberries, blueberries and lilies of the valley.

Noteworthy is the park adjacent to the water surface. At one time, celebrities such as Herbert Wells and Niels Bohr walked in it. It is surprising that a house was built on this territory for academician I.N. Pavlov. True, he did not have time to live in it, and now they plan to remodel the building into a museum of physiology.

Amateur Fishers

For connoisseurs of catch on the lake, paid fishing is organized. And you can fish out here not anyhow, but the California catfish, carp, beluga, sturgeon, or even a mixture of sterlet and beluga - bester. You can prepare your catch yourself, for which they simply rent a brazier. And for those who do not want to mess with fish, they offer the services of professional chefs. Fishermen remain satisfied, as evidenced by their many reviews.

Resting-place

The water in Lake Koltushinsky is clean, without debris and snags. For visitors, a beach and a volleyball court are equipped . There is also a children's zone. It is quite interestingly designed, little tourists will not be bored.

Koltushskoe lake is popular among holidaymakers. Therefore, camping sites were specially organized here. Those who wish can rent a tent, barbecue, fishing equipment, boat or catamaran. Also on the coast you can celebrate holidays with large companies. A restaurant serving European and Russian cuisine is open all year round on the lake, which will happily feed its guests.
