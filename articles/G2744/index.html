Reviews about the hotel Al Nabila Grand Bay Makadi Hotel & Resort 5 *

If you, like thousands of other tourists from around the world, want to spend your vacation on the Red Sea and at the same time count on comfortable accommodation and high-level service, then we suggest you consider Al Nabila Grand Bay Makadi Hotel as an option for your stay Resort 5 *.





Location

This five-star hotel complex is located on the shores of the Red Sea, in a bay called Makadi. The nearest airport is in Hurghada. The distance to it is 15 kilometers.

Photo and description of the hotel

The five-star Nabila Grand Bay Makadi was opened in 2007. It was built in a traditional Egyptian style. The housing stock includes the main multi-storey building, as well as several small bungalows. In total, the hotel complex has 500 apartments. All rooms are spacious and equipped with items necessary for a comfortable stay. So, the apartments have air conditioning, TV, refrigerator, safe, telephone, bathroom, balcony with a view. Rooms are cleaned every day. Linen and towels are updated several times a week.





The hotel has several swimming pools. Next to them there are sun terraces with comfortable sun loungers, as well as bars where you can always enjoy your favorite cold or alcoholic drink. Children have the opportunity to ride a water slide.

Tourists can visit the spa, beauty salon, gym, exercise. From morning to late evening, animators work on site. In addition, you can always diversify your leisure time by going on one of the excursions. You can order them here from your local guide.

The hotel operates on an all inclusive system. All guests can eat in the main restaurant of the hotel complex three times a day. Here, skilled chefs serve a buffet consisting of a variety of well-prepared dishes. In addition, there are three √† la carte restaurants on site. In them you can order from the menu.

Sea

According to the vast majority of tourists, the Al Nabila Grand Bay Makadi Resort 5 * hotel is a great place for those who dream of a great beach holiday. So, our compatriots enthusiastically describe the large lagoon in their reviews, in which their own sandy beach of the hotel complex is located. According to them, the water here is very clean. Near the coast there is a densely populated reef. So lovers of watching the life of bright and amazing marine life will love it here. By the way, experienced travelers are advised not to forget to take a mask for snorkeling from home or already in Egypt. Also, flippers will not be superfluous.
