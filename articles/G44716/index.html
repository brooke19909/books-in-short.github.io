Local Attractions

The Garden Cliff Resort Spa, which we describe in the article, is a quiet and peaceful place for families. However, being close to the famous city, it is simply impossible to resist, so as not to visit its most famous sights and simply beautiful places.

This resort has long been appreciated by tourists from all over the world, not only for its beautiful beaches and active, eventful nightlife, but also for its natural and cultural attractions. The most important object on the must see list is the famous Big Buddha Hill. It is interesting to see from the point of view of acquaintance with the cultural heritage of an Asian country. In fact, this is a large complex of religious monuments, the most important of which is considered to be a gilded Buddha statue.

To get to the monument, you have to climb the stairs, decorated with dragons and snakes. There is even a tradition of recounting steps: if a person is not mistaken in counting, then everything is fine in his life, and if he is mistaken, then something needs to be changed. The statue is at a height of one hundred twenty meters. The figure of the Buddha reaches fifteen meters in height. It really makes a huge impression, it can be seen from anywhere in Pattaya. The statue looks even more spectacular at night in the light of lights.



For parents with children it will be interesting to visit the village of elephants. This reserve can be called unique. Here sacred animals live in natural conditions in complete safety. Currently, about thirty elephants live in the village. Animal shows are shown daily. Employees of the reserve tell interesting facts from the life of good-natured giants, and elephants demonstrate their achievements: the ability to play musical instruments, draw and much more.

Children will be delighted to visit the Khao Keo Zoo. The animals in it are kept in a natural environment, and at the same time they can be stroked. Currently, the zoo occupies eight hundred hectares of land and is considered one of the largest such institutions in the world. Its entire territory is divided into different zones. Guests can not only chat with animals, but also watch performances with their participation.

To appreciate the nightlife of Pattaya, you should go to the famous Walking Street in the evening. From six o‚Äôclock in the evening, it becomes a pedestrian mall illuminated by a thousand lights, along which go-go bars are lined up. However, if you came to rest with children, then you are unlikely to appreciate the nightlife of the city, since Walking Street with all its temptations and unusual shows is not a place for kids.

Garden Cliff Resort Spa Pattaya: reviews

Concluding the conversation about the hotel, I would like to refer to the reviews of tourists who visited it. Is Garden Cliff Resort Spa 5 * really good? The reviews of the guests of the complex are a bit contradictory in some issues, but overall very positive. The main point that vacationers emphasize is the successful location of the hotel. As we mentioned, it is located outside the city. For couples with children, this is a significant plus, because loud music does not interfere with relaxation. The hotel is perfect for a quiet and measured relaxation. Young people who want to be closer to the rich nightlife are likely to prefer the complex closer to the center of Pattaya.



Garden Cliff Resort Spa (see the article) is on the first line and has its own beach, which is quite a rarity for Thailand. Who is not the first time resting in this country, already knows that the sea on the coast is not always clean. To enjoy the clear water, it is better to go to the islands. Absolutely all hotel rooms are beautiful, spacious and comfortable. They have everything that guests need for a carefree vacation. Quality furniture and traditional Thai interiors create a special atmosphere. Apartments are cleaned regularly. On the day of arrival and check-in, a small surprise awaits guests in the form of a fruit basket. In addition, the hotel staff is very courteous and nice. Settlement is fast. What sets Garden Cliff Resort Spa (Pattaya) apart from other hotels is its beautiful and large area, surrounded by greenery and flowers. Ponds create a special charm.
