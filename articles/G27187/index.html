Sea Club Aquapark 5. Hotel in Nabq Bay

Description: Sea Club Aquapark 5 is located in Nabq Bay. The rooms offer views of Tiran Island.



The hotel was built a kilometer from the beach, from it by car in 20 minutes you can get to Sharm El Sheikh.

The infrastructure includes several shops, a laundry, ATMs, a left-luggage office, elevators, concierge services, a round-the-clock medical office.

This brand new hotel was built at the end of 2012. It is part of the Sea Group hotel chain, known for its impeccable service and increased attention to its customers, so residents can use some of the infrastructures of the neighboring Sea Club Resort, Sea Life hotels. Nearby are many shops and restaurants.

At the 24-hour reception you can order a transfer, rent a vehicle, use the depository safe.

A shuttle bus leaves from the hotel to Naama Bay daily, which brings tourists back in the evening.

The distance to the nearest airport is 5 kilometers.

Rooms: Sea Club Aquapark 5 has 302 of them, they are equipped in a three-story main building and in 18 three-story buildings, and correspond to the categories Standard, Family, Junior and Duo. Of these, 105 are for non-smokers.

All elegantly furnished rooms have individually controlled air conditioning, a small safe, a landline telephone, a TV, and smoke detectors. The bathrooms combined with the toilet have a bathtub with a flexible shower, as well as a hair dryer and everything necessary for hygiene. Some rooms have a dedicated sitting area.

The hotel allows pets, there are apartments for guests in wheelchairs.
