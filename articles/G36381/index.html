Frunze Village (Crimea): brief information

The village of Frunze, which will be discussed in this article, began to develop rapidly as a resort area in the 90s of the last century. Currently, this resort is popular with tourists mainly due to affordable prices for accommodation and food. Young couples, families with children and those who prefer a holiday full of romance and tranquility love to come to Frunze (Saki district, Crimea) .

General information

The small village of Frunze is located near the famous cities of the Crimean peninsula: Saki, Yevpatoriya, Simferopol and Sevastopol. It is the largest settlement of the Saki region. The history of Frunze dates back to 1921. At this time, the village of Krasnaya Gorka was formed at the location of the village. Later, in the 30s of the 20th century, the Frunze state farm began to function in this territory. Before the Soviet Union ceased to exist, in the immediate vicinity of the village there was a large poultry farm that provided work for a large number of local residents.





The main attraction of the village of Frunze (Crimea) is Lake Bogayly. This beautiful shallow pond is famous for its mud, which has healing power. It is used for procedures in sanatoriums and boarding houses of Saki and Yevpatoriya. Bogail has a liman structure. It is filled with water due to rainfall and underground sources. This lake is separated from the sea by a short isthmus. Bogayly Lake is an excellent fishing spot.

Climatic conditions in this resort village, replete with orchards, pamper vacationers with sunny days. In the summer, precipitation in these places is quite rare. Air temperature fluctuates within 25 degrees.

How to get to Frunze

The nearest airport, located in Simferopol, is located at a distance of 65 kilometers from this resort village. From it you can directly reach Frunze (Crimea) by minibus, taxi and local bus. From the airport, buses to Yevpatoria stopping regularly in Saki.



To get to this resort village is really through Anapa and Krasnodar. From there you need to take the bus next to the Kerch crossing, and then continue on your way to the Crimea by ferry. This journey to Frunze takes a lot of time.

Resort infrastructure

The village of Frunze (Crimea), the rest of which is saturated with comfort and tranquility, has a developed infrastructure. There are various shops, bars and cafes, bank and post offices. Near the village is the MTS mobile communications tower. Frunze has a clinic, a network of pharmacies and a hairdresser. In the village itself and on the beach area, there are two markets where you can purchase various souvenirs and accessories necessary for relaxing on the beach. Frunze offers a wide selection of excursions. Those who wish also have the opportunity to visit nearby resorts.

The beaches of the resort village

On the territory of Frunze (Crimea) extends several beach areas. Nature lovers often opt for wild beaches, and vacationers in boarding houses prefer the central beach, located in the northwest of Lake Bogayly. In the immediate vicinity there is an entrance to the lake itself, in which guests of the resort prefer to take mud baths. On this beach are cafes, parking for cars, changing rooms.





In the southern part of Lake Bogayly, a summer camp for car tourists is formed every year. Everyone can post their tent here absolutely free. On this sandy-pebble beach area, the infrastructure is completely absent. Beach areas are located from Frunze at a distance of 1.5-2 kilometers. To get to them is not difficult, in this direction minibuses regularly run. Those who want to strengthen their health often get to the beaches on foot.

Places to stay in Frunze

Housing stock for vacationers in Frunze (Crimea) is represented mainly by the budgetary private sector. Many guest houses are private cottages, converted into a tourist reception. There are very few hotels in the village, the largest of which is Olga. On the territory of Frunze there are also family-type recreation centers : Wave, Sail, Frunzenec. The price of the ticket here, in addition to accommodation, includes: breakfast, lunch and dinner, use of the beach area and the infrastructure of the base.
