Eden Village El Borj 3 * (Tunisia, Mahdia): hotel description, reviews

Tunisia is a place that has attracted tourists from all over the world for decades. And they come here not only for the sake of beautiful beaches and five-star hotels, but also in order to plunge into the eastern culture and see interesting sights.

Of course, in the country there are dozens of resort towns and hundreds of hotels. Quite often, travelers decide to spend several days at the Eden Village el Borj 3 * hotel, because this place is in good standing with experienced tourists. So is it worth spending a vacation at the hotel and what do people who have already managed to visit here think?

Hotel Eden Village el Borj 3 * (Tunisia): location and brief information about the territory





Of course, the location is an important factor, because most tourists want to relax in peace, while enjoying all the possibilities of modern civilization. Where is the hotel Eden Village el Borj 3 * located? Mahdia, a popular resort town, famous for its beauty and hospitality - here, on the beach, you can spend a rich and memorable vacation.

The international airport is worth a look in the city of Monastir, 45 km away. By the way, there are many shops, cafes and bars, supermarkets and entertainment centers near the hotel. Travelers consider the location one of the advantages of the hotel.

The complex consists of seven two- and three-story buildings built in an exotic oriental style. Not so long ago, all buildings were renovated. Tourists note a large and generously decorated area with greenery, where you can walk, relax, play sports, in a word, have fun.

What do hotel rooms look like?





Hotel Eden Village el Borj Mahdia 3 * is considered quite large. Its number of rooms is represented by 228 rooms. The rooms are standard, but very bright, with large windows and private balconies. The room decor looks very elegant, combining a minimalist European design with a traditional oriental decor. The furniture is new and the beds are very comfortable.

Of course, there are household appliances in the rooms - they tried to provide guests with maximum comfort. You can watch TV, use the phone, adjust the temperature using the air conditioner. The bathroom was recently equipped with new, modern fixtures. By the way, you can count on a hair dryer, clean towels, as well as soap and shampoos. Former guests note the fact that the rooms are kept clean - employees clean their conscience, replenish hygiene supplies on time, and also change their linen three times a week.

Food: what kind of scheme is offered to guests?





As is usual in most hotels in Tunisia, in Eden Village el Borj Mahdia 3 * guests are offered good food, as well as light snacks throughout the day. Buffets are served three times a day in the main restaurant. Former guests note that they feed well - the food is always fresh, and the menu is quite large. By the way, fresh fruits, pastries and ice cream are always and in sufficient quantities.

In the evenings, many tourists relax in the hotel bars (there are three of them). Free chilled drinks and strong local drinks are served. There are imported products, but they are paid separately. It is also worth a visit to the local pizzeria (they cook very tasty here) and the Moorish cafe, where you can enjoy not only the local cuisine, but also the appropriate atmosphere.

Distance to the beach, amenities and leisure of tourists





The hotel complex Eden Village el Borj 3 * is located almost on the very shore of the azure sea - there are only a few steps to the beach. This section of the coast belongs to the hotel, and only guests have a rest here. Sun loungers and umbrellas are usually enough for all tourists, and you can use them for free.

The beach here is very clean, covered with fine golden sand. The water is clean, which is what tourists snorkeling use. There is a sandbank where children love to dabble. And of course, guests are offered a whole range of beach activities, from volleyball to water skiing and scuba diving.

Should I count on an additional service?





On the territory of the hotel Eden Village el Borj Mahdia 3 * there is everything in order to make your vacation as comfortable as possible. If we talk about amenities, then for the guests there is a spacious parking lot. You can rent transport both at the hotel and in the city, this is a quick and affordable procedure.

You can count on the availability of an Internet connection, although sometimes the speed leaves much to be desired. The guest can get a safe deposit box from the administrator for storing important papers or other valuables. Here, at the reception, you can exchange money for local bills. You are invited by a small shopping arcade - there are four shops selling souvenirs, food, various household items and hygiene items, you can even buy clothes. Prices are quite reasonable.
