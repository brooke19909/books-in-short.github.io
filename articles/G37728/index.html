BenQ MW705 Projector - The New Standard for Brightness

Presentations, photo materials, videos - today you no longer need to convince anyone that visualization contributes to better learning of school material by students and students, and to create the right impression on the presented project from colleagues, partners and clients. Today, projectors are becoming more popular equipment, both in educational institutions and in the offices of modern companies.

So, what should you pay attention to first when choosing a projector? The main requirements for a modern projector, of course, are:

  • high quality image transmission;
  • simplicity and ease of setup and use;
  • long service life without the need for service;
  • affordable price

But to really enjoy using the projector, pay attention to such a technical indicator as brightness, measured in lumens. Until recently, in the budget price segment there were only projectors with a brightness of 3000 or, at best, 3500 ANSI lumens. At the same time, the brighter the projector, the noisier it worked.





But technological progress does not stand still! BenQ engineers managed to combine the best with the best, in this case - the highest image brightness and silent operation of the projector. The latest model to open a new era of brightness standard for projectors is the BenQ MW705 projector.

So, the MW705 Projector: Key Features and Benefits

  • Almost silent operation mode - only 31 dB. For comparison: in terms of sound pressure, this noise level corresponds to a barely visible whisper, or it is the same as in a bedroom at night with closed windows overlooking a quiet courtyard.
  • High contrast ratio (13000: 1) and unrivaled brightness level (4000 ANSI lumens), which together give previously unavailable image clarity. You can discern all the nuances of color, see the smallest details, easily read the text that accompanies the slides, or subtitles in the video even from the last row in the conference room or from the last desk in the classroom / audience.
  • Easy and quick image correction - adjustment horizontally and vertically up to +/- 30 degrees. With the BenQ MW705, you can easily get rid of keystone distortion and skewed images. This function is indispensable in cases where it is not possible to place the projector directly in front of the screen (board), and you have to place it somewhere on the side.
  • Compatibility with mobile devices (on Android OS), which makes it possible to organize stable streaming of multimedia data from a smartphone or tablet.
  • A modern approach to energy consumption. Thanks to the use of SmartEco and Eco Blank technologies, the ‚Äúsmart‚Äù MW705 is able to reduce / increase the lamp power depending on the mode of use of the device. For example, if within 3 minutes there is no signal from the input source, or there is a need for some time to draw the attention of the audience to the speaker himself, and not to the presentation, with one touch you can reduce the lamp power by 70% at once. and also, with one touch, resume the show. In standby mode, the MW705 consumes less than 0.5 watts. This saves both energy and lamp life.
  • 3D image support, subtitle optimization, the presence of pre-installed training templates (letter layout, sheet music, grid, etc.) - the BenQ MW705 projector will help you get the most out of a business presentation or training material.

Projection technology

BenQ has long established itself as an expert in the production of image transmission devices, being a leader not only in Russia, but also in the global market for projection technologies. The most modern and human-eye-friendly technology used in BenQ projectors is DLP (Digital Light Processing). The same technology is used in most digital cinemas in the world, in particular in all IMAX cinemas. In the BenQ MW705 projector, among other things, DLP optics is also reliably protected from mechanical damage and dust due to the sealed design of this model.





Nice additions

Like most new BenQ projectors, the MW705 can work wirelessly. The projector housing has a special compartment for connecting the QCast wireless adapter, which allows you to preserve the elegance of the projector lines and protect the adapter from prying eyes.

For teachers, the built-in teaching templates will be useful and convenient: writing a letter, a sheet of music, a worksheet, a coordinate grid, which are available both in regular school and in a special whiteboard.

To summarize

So, the BenQ MW705 is an excellent projector for solving business problems, educational goals or home use. And yet, it is quite budget for devices of this class. When deciding to get such a useful, multifunctional and pleasant assistant, pay attention to certified points of sale.
