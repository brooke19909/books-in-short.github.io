LCD "South Park" (Kazan): description

Kazan is a modern metropolis that does not stop being built up. The largest construction companies offer new complexes, housing estates for a comfortable and safe life. LCD "South Park" (Kazan) is a rather attractive project. In the framework of this material, with the help of the reviews of the first tenants, we will give him an objective assessment.





about the project

LCD "South Park" (Kazan) - a large-scale residential development project, a new microdistrict with developed infrastructure and excellent ecology. On the territory of 36 hectares it is planned to build a large complex in several stages. The first stage consists of seven five-story brick houses, and the second - six nineteen-story houses.

Location

Quiet, calm, and, most importantly, a place remote from the noisy metropolis was chosen for the construction of the South Park residential complex (Kazan). Reviews of the first buyers focus on ecology as the main advantage. The attention of the developer fell on Laishevsky municipal district. Opposite the village of Usady, a new microdistrict is being built, it is surrounded on all sides by a forest, due to which excellent living conditions are provided. By purchasing real estate here, you are making a profitable investment in your future.





Transport accessibility

14 km separates the city center from the LCD "South Park" (Kazan, Usady). Reviews of potential buyers affect the topic of transport accessibility, because not every family has a private car. As the object is completed, additional public transport routes will be launched in its direction, stops will appear near the houses, so residents of the microdistrict will be able to reach any point of the city without any problems. For car owners, the complex provides ground and underground parking - there is definitely enough space for everyone.

Infrastructure

LCD "South Park" (Kazan) is remote from the city, so the issue of infrastructure is relevant no less than transport accessibility. It is planned to build a kindergarten on the territory of the complex. The first floors of the houses will be reserved for commercial infrastructure: shops, bank branches, service organizations. All residents will be able to use the infrastructure of neighboring complexes and villages.

Landscaping is represented by modern playgrounds and sports grounds, recreation areas for children and adults.

Apartments, layouts

All apartments in the LCD "South Park" (Kazan) are for rent with rough finish. But the developer carried out all the minimum necessary work: water and electricity were supplied, high-quality double-glazed windows and heating radiators were installed. The first tenants note that they got quite good apartments, albeit with a rough finish: all surfaces are fairly smooth, which saved on repairs.





The choice of buyers offered apartments of improved layout from small studios of 37 square meters to more spacious three-room apartments of 75 square meters. The variety of layouts allows you to choose the best option.

About pricing

The project attracted attention not only due to the cleanliness of the chosen place, but at affordable prices. Today, a one-room apartment can be purchased for just 1.4 million rubles, a comfortable treshka will cost 3.2 million rubles. Cooperation with the largest banks allows you to arrange a mortgage on favorable terms.

To summarize

LCD "South Park" (Kazan) - comfortable housing in an ecologically clean area at an attractive price. If you want to feel all the charms of country life without losing access to the necessary infrastructure, you will definitely like the project. Today you can buy an apartment here on favorable terms.
