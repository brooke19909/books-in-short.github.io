DIY desktop puppet theater: patterns and description

Even during a technical breakthrough, when many children are interested in electronic games, the interest in puppet theater is not lost. This cute and funny activity is interesting for both young children of preschool age and schoolchildren. After all, dolls are interesting characters who can tell various fables, fascinating stories and tales. And this is very attracting the attention of children. The so-called fun nativity scene is always in fashion.

What is a puppet theater for?

Such a simple play set is often used for the intellectual development of young children, and is also used to organize useful home leisure activities for a child. This is a hobby that allows you to reveal children's fantasies as such, to determine its facets of creative abilities.

To organize interesting entertainment at home, you do not need to run to the store and look for something like that. Making a puppet theater with your own hands is simple, especially if you apply all the examples below.

What tools and materials are needed for creativity?

To make a home puppet theater with your own hands, you will need small and large scissors (separately for paper and fabric), such material for crafts as multi-colored foamiran, threads, a needle, a sewing machine, a glue gun, utility household appliances in the form of brushes, dense plain patches colored material, empty cardboard boxes of various sizes, plastic pipes and corners for joining, a pencil or marker for drawing patterns, a ruler, a set of colored paper sheets of various densities. Here, in general, is the whole elementary set that may be needed in order to fulfill the samples given here.

If you still think that making a children's puppet theater with your own hands is difficult, proceed to further study the material in this article, and you will realize that there can be no easier task for parents. It turns out that we miss a lot of things in life, and when a child grows up, we find great ideas that could be very useful in the upbringing and development of the baby in the first years of life.

Do-it-yourself home puppet theater is best done together, it is interesting not only for the child, but also for parents, it is especially fun and exciting to use its result after fruitful work. Fantasy helps create a wide variety of characters.

A few tips for beginners

So, do-it-yourself puppet theater, everyone can make patterns and description of samples in this article. The main thing is that there is a desire to develop creative inclinations in your children and not to miss a very valuable time in education - preschool age, when the child needs special attention.

A cheerful fairytale atmosphere is needed for kids all the time. They learn certain behavior from the heroes of fairy tales. The richer the repertoire of puppet theater, the better. So to stop on creativity only animals are not worth it.

In order for the puppet theater to last as long as possible, in its manufacture it is worth using only new materials - paper, fabric, foamiran rags. It is necessary to store such a game set separately from all children's toys in order to prevent its damage. After all, the decor of the screen and many details on the dolls themselves require a special thrifty attitude.

Ready-made theater sets are used for role-playing games, in which the whole character of the baby opens. He must be directly involved in voicing a character. After all, a passive viewer is not for children. Therefore, when making a puppet theater, always listen to the wishes of the child.

Unusually simple ideas can bring home comfort, fun and the keen interest of its inhabitants. Often, the whole friendly family gathers at the puppet theater, especially on holidays. So use the collected material and improve it at your discretion. All the ideas proposed here do not require excessive financial costs and a large amount of free time for their implementation. In just a couple of hours, you can create a theater screen and several elegant and cute finger-operated dolls.

Puppet theater is not a ready-made toy, you need to work hard on its creation, and in order to discover all its secrets, it’s worth trying a little and imagining yourself.

If you don’t know how to captivate a child today in the era of many modern toys, which to a greater extent are of little use, immediately start creating such an original offspring.
