Hurghada - one of the oldest and most traditional Egyptian resorts

This name, known to many in ancient times, had a different meaning than now. Once upon a time, the route from the Roman Empire to the mysterious eastern countries passed through the southern part of this resort, and from there they brought rare expensive fabrics for the nobility and spices for fine cuisine. Quarries and mines were located nearby, from where they took material for the construction of many architectural monuments, which we admire now. Until now, not far from Hurghada, the so-called "Claudius Mountain" has been preserved, where the remains of the Roman camp are located. There, archaeologists discovered dwellings, workshops, walls for defense against nomads, and much more. But with the fall of the Roman Empire, these places became an abandoned province, and for many years Hurghada was famous only for the production of carpets and good fish. The decisive event for these places was the Arab-Israeli war, when water was brought here for the needs of the army. What else is needed for the desert to bloom?

Therefore, in recent years, Hurghada has become a world famous resort. Beautiful sandy beaches, coral reefs, warm climate have made it a center of attraction for many vacationers from different parts of the world. And from Russia, of course, too. Being here is not only pleasant for body and soul, but also not burdensome for a wallet, especially if you choose a hotel like Festival Shedwan Golden Beach Resort 3 * (Hurghada, Egypt).
