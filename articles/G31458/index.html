Hotel Yalta Holiday Village 3 * (Bulgaria, Sunny Beach): description and reviews of tourists

Bulgaria is one of the most popular budget holiday destinations. Indeed, in this country, a traveler can be offered a lot, starting from interesting historical sights, ending with beautiful beaches and sincere hospitality. Of course, on the coast there are many resort towns with hundreds of hotels. And one of the popular holiday destinations is the Yalta Holiday Village 3 * hotel.

Of course, before booking, you should familiarize yourself with additional information about the hotel, find out about its location, living conditions and, of course, about the reviews that travelers leave about this place.

Features of the location and description of the hotel





The hotel complex Yalta Holiday Village 3 * has a very favorable location - it was built in the world famous resort of Sunny Beach. The area is calm, but within walking distance of guests not only beaches, but also shops, bars, entertainment complexes and other attractions.

The hotel complex has a large territory with pools and terraces for relaxation. It consists of the main building, as well as several small two-story bungalows, which, in fact, are reserved for living rooms. Not so long ago, the hotel buildings were completely renovated, so guests live here, enjoying modern comfort.

Hotel Yalta Holiday Village 3 * (Sunny Beach): photo and room information





This is a medium-sized hotel, which offers its guests accommodation in one of 40 rooms. Here, tourists will find both standard rooms and more spacious apartments with a separate living area. If necessary, employees deliver another bed for the child to the room.

Guests can spend time enjoying modern comfort. All rooms are equipped with powerful air conditioning. There is also a TV, a small safe, a telephone, and a mini fridge. By the way, guests get free access to the Internet, and the speed here is quite normal.

The bathroom is also equipped with new appliances - there is a toilet, sink, mirror, as well as a spacious shower. Maids replenish the stock of hygiene items and clean towels every day during cleaning.

Can a tourist count on food?





Paying for a room at the Yalta Holiday Village 3 * hotel, guests are free to choose a convenient meal plan for them. As a rule, tourists enjoy visiting local breakfasts, which are held in the spacious restaurant hall. The menu here is quite varied, and the dishes are always freshly prepared and tasty. In addition, the hotel offers guests lunches and dinners - they can be paid in advance when booking a room, or pay the bill after each visit to the restaurant.

And the hotel also has a small bar where you can sit with a glass of strong drink or order aromatic coffee. Many guests prefer to spend their evenings here.

In any case, food will not be a problem, because you can eat outside the walls of the Yalta Holiday Village 3 * hotel. Sunny Beach is a popular tourist area. It is natural that the city has a large number of cafes and other establishments where you can eat.

Beach holidays: amenities and entertainment on the beach





As already mentioned, Bulgaria is a popular destination for a beach holiday. So what can guests expect at the Yalta Holiday Village 3 * hotel? Sunny Beach, aka Sunny Beach, annually attracts thousands of tourists. From the hotel in a few minutes you can walk to the coastline and choose a beautiful beach. The coast here is very beautiful and clean, the entrance to the sea is convenient, and the water during the season is very warm.

For a small fee, you can rent sunbeds and umbrellas, as well as get a beach towel. Of course, travelers spend time here actively, not only swimming and sunbathing, but also riding on boats, boats, water skiing, doing parasailing and sailing.

Hotel complex Yalta Holiday Village 3 * (Bulgaria): additional services and leisure for guests





Hotel guests can count on additional service, which greatly facilitates accommodation on its territory. For example, there is a laundry service and a currency exchange. Guests at Yalta Holiday Village 3 * can use the safe at the reception and luggage storage. You can also rent a bicycle or car here. And in public areas there is access to the Internet. The staff will be happy to help you call a taxi or arrange a transfer.

In the hotel area, you can have some good fun. Travelers like to spend time by the pool, sunbathe on comfortable sun loungers and just chat with new friends. You can have a good time playing billiards or darts. There is a large playground for children on site. In the evenings, you can visit the local bar and sing karaoke.

What do tourists say? Reviews and recommendations

Of course, in addition to official information, it is always useful to get acquainted with the opinions of tourists who have already managed to visit a particular place. So what do they say about Yalta Holiday Village 3 * hotel? Sunny Beach is a great place to relax. It has everything you need, including a picturesque coast, interesting sights and, of course, quality service.

This hotel is perfect for an inexpensive but good vacation. The rooms here are spacious and clean, the food is good, although not always varied, and the staff are always ready to help and suggest something. Naturally, the advantage is the proximity to incredibly beautiful beaches where you can swim and have fun in the company of other travelers. This is really a good place for a summer vacation, which will give strength and improve well-being.
