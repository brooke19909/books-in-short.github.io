Pinery. "Komandor", recreation center: accommodation, infrastructure, reviews

Not far from the Northern capital of Russia there is the city of Sosnovy Bor. "Komandor" - a recreation center located next to it - opens its doors to fans of country recreation. It is here that the St. Petersburg and pine fighters love to relax. The atmosphere of this place envelops with warmth and comfort, which in our time is so lacking for residents of megacities.

Sosnovy Bor, "Commander"

A recreation center on the coast of the Gulf of Finland, which allows you to enjoy the tranquility and comfort of country life all year round.





The complex consists of several wooden log houses, the interior of which has a large number of various handmade objects made from natural materials and allows you to feel the atmosphere of a quiet house in the country.

The recreation center is great for relaxing with a fun company, holding family holidays or corporate parties, there are also equipped picnic areas. There is a grill house for ten people.

A hundred meters from the base there is a well-groomed beach, catamarans and boat rides are possible. The complex has a cafe, volleyball court and a wood-fired sauna.

Comfortable conditions, the cleanest air, pine forest, breathtaking views of the Gulf of Finland will make your vacation unforgettable, help you relax, find peace of mind and relax from the bustle of the city.

Accommodation

The recreation center "Komandor" (Sosnovy Bor) offers several wooden cottages for accommodation to its guests:

  • Two-storey suite for ten people. On the ground floor of the house there is a huge living room and kitchen with all necessary utensils and appliances. On the second - two bedrooms.




One-story economy is able to accommodate up to five people. The house has two rooms (living room and bedroom) with the necessary furniture, appliances and utensils. In warmer months, you can sit on the cozy veranda.

Infrastructure

Despite the fact that the complex is surrounded by a pine forest, Komandor (recreation center) has a developed infrastructure. It has everything you need for a comfortable stay (light, hot water, communications).

In addition to equipped cottages, a heated beer tent, a grill gazebo are at guests' disposal, for lovers of pair treatments - a free-standing bathhouse with an equipped bathing place.

Also on the territory of the "Commander" there are camps for campers, a guarded parking lot, a stage with stands, a volleyball court, and an equipped beach. For those who want to eat, there is a cafe.
