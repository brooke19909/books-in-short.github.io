Hotel "Long Beach Resort" (Turkey / Alanya) - a place for true gourmets

The trip will give fireworks of feelings, vivid memories and a lot of positive emotions. From the first minute, you will feel like a privileged person who finds herself in a fairy-tale magical land. Here you will find an attentive attitude, interesting leisure and, of course, the gentle sun with the azure sea.

Rooms





Upon arrival, guests will find a pleasant and unexpected surprise in the form of a bouquet of flowers and fruits. At will, tourists will be able to choose for themselves an acceptable room category - from standard to deluxe. About 60% of the rooms have a view of the azure sea, the rest have a view of the hotel. Disabled people are provided with special rooms with private facilities.

All rooms have split systems working for cold / heat. In free access - safe. The rooms have a TV with the ability to view Russian channels. In addition, a telephone was provided. For a surcharge, you can fill the bar with strong drinks or natural juices. You do not have to think about the cleanliness of your rooms, as well-trained professional maids will do this for you (cleaning daily).





Nutrition

According to the all-inclusive concept, the Long Beach Resort Hotel (Turkey) operates . Reviews of vacationers about nutrition are only positive, or rather enthusiastic. Every day, guests are served tables in the spacious restaurant. The menu is rich in fresh vegetables, fruits, meat and fish delicacies. You can also order diet food.

On a vast territory there are 11 more bars and 4 restaurants. In the institutions you will be offered to taste exclusive dishes of different cuisines: Ottoman, Chinese, Italian and Turkish. Seafood lovers can enjoy the culinary masterpieces that will be prepared for you by qualified, experienced chefs at the Long Beach Resort (Turkey).

For delicious food in a pleasant company you will feel great pleasure. Especially for the sweet tooth, there is a cafe house serving amazing cream cakes, chocolate mousses and crumbly cookies. In the evenings, entertaining quizzes and fun contests are held in the institutions.

Beach (own)

A couple of miles from the hotel stretches a wide sand and pebble coast with clear water and a pier. You will not only be able to relax on the beach and get a beautiful chocolate tan, but also go water skiing, banana, catamaran or parachute.

The guesthouse administration has prepared a full service for its valued customers: beach amenities, interesting leisure activities and a free bar located right on the beach.
