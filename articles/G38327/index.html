Hotel Neptune Mare 4 * (Gennadi, Greece): photos, reviews

The island of Rhodes is a legendary place that attracts thousands of tourists who want to have a good time on the beach and plunge into ancient history and unique culture. Of course, over the past few decades, many hotels and pensions of various sizes and categories have been built here. One of the rather popular stopping places is the Neptune Mare 4 * hotel.

When planning a trip, you should ask for more information about the selected hotel. For example, it is important to know where the hotel is located and how far you have to go to the beach. What conditions of food and accommodation can a tourist expect? Is it possible to have a good time here with the children? What reviews do guests leave? The answers to these questions are really important.

Hotel Location





Of course, to begin with, it is worth figuring out exactly where to look for the Neptune Mare 4 * hotel complex (Greece). Gennadi (Gennadi) is a picturesque resort village, spread on the very shore of the sea in the southern part of Rhodes. The town center can be reached in a 10-minute leisurely walk - here you will find shops, colorful taverns, a medical center. This is a quiet area, which is designed to relax on the beach.

By the way, cities such as Prasonisi and Lindos are located very close by - here you can visit the ancient Acropolis and no less interesting ancient castle. Rhodes Airport is approximately 70 km away. As you can see, the location of the hotel is very convenient - it is easy to get anywhere from here. Tourists can take a taxi or book a transfer at the hotel.

What does the hotel area look like?





Hotel Neptune Mare 4 * is a complex of several two-story buildings. Its area is about seven thousand square meters. The yard is literally drowning in flowers, and guests are pleased to stroll along neatly arranged lawns and relax in the shade of cypress trees. Of course, in the courtyard there are terraces for relaxation, a swimming pool, sports grounds, in short, everything you need for a pleasant pastime.

By the way, the hotel was built in 1992. Of course, since then it underwent several reconstructions, the last of which took place in 2007. The hotel territory is constantly being improved, and from time to time the equipment is changed to a more modern one. Thanks to comfort and coziness, tourists like the Neptune Mare 4 * hotel so much. Gennadi is a really good place to spend your vacation.

What rooms provide guests?

Neptune Mare 4 * is a medium sized hotel. Guests are offered a choice of 73 rooms. For the most part, these are standard rooms with spacious balconies that can accommodate two people (+ child).

But there are more spacious family rooms, which consist of a bedroom and a living area, where upholstered furniture and folding sofas are located. These rooms are perfect for parents with two children, because there is enough space for everyone.

What kind of food can you count on?





Of course, the issue of nutrition is important for every tourist. What can guests of the Neptune Mare 4 * hotel count on? Rhodes is an island famous for its hospitality. And this hotel is no exception. Guests are free to choose the food scheme convenient for them. In most cases, tourists prefer the so-called half board, which includes two meals a day. Also, guests can pay for the "all inclusive" system or even refuse to eat at the hotel.

Guests are invited to the spacious restaurant building, where meals are served at the buffet. According to reviews, the menu here is quite diverse. Breakfast includes toasts, cereals, granola, fresh pastries, salads, fruits and light snacks. Lunches and dinners are more varied: guests are offered meat, fish and fresh seafood dishes, as well as soups and traditional Greek delicacies.

Food is unlikely to become a problem for tourists, because in the village there are several cafes and taverns where you can eat, and the prices here are quite reasonable.

Relax on the beach: entertainment and amenities





Of course, many tourists prefer to spend their vacation basking in the gentle rays of the sun on the beach. What can the guests of the Neptune Mare 4 * hotel (Greece) count on? O. Rhodes is famous for its incredibly beautiful coast. The hotel is located almost on the seashore - the distance is not more than 50 m. The beach here is covered with sand and small pebbles. According to reviews, the water here is clean and warm, and the entrance to the sea is cleared and quite convenient.

Comfortable sunbeds and wide sun umbrellas are arranged on the shore in even rows. For a small fee, they can be rented for the whole day. The hotel also offers clean, large towels. There are several bars on the coast where you can enjoy a chilled drink or even a good snack while enjoying the sea view.

Water activities by the sea





If sun bathing and swimming are not enough for you, then on the beach you can find ways to spend time more actively. Of course, on local beaches you can easily rent a catamaran or a boat. A boat trip will also bring a lot of positive impressions.

You can get a dose of adrenaline and vivid memories of a vacation by water skiing or parasailing. Often, surfers ride here, and swimming with a mask is also considered good entertainment. There are plenty of opportunities on the island to have some really good fun.

Do guests offer additional services?

Despite the fact that the hotel is relatively small and most often used by tourists as a place to sleep, some additional services are offered on its territory that make living here more comfortable. For example, here you can exchange currency at a good rate. The reception, by the way, is open around the clock - here you can always find one of the employees. You can rent a safe from the administrator for a small fee.

In public areas there is free internet, which is very convenient (the speed is not bad, reviews indicate this). If you wish, you can rent a laptop or use a computer and some office equipment. And the hotel also offers rental bicycles, motorcycles and cars - the prices are quite reasonable. Near the hotel there is free parking, which is also convenient.

Leisure at the hotel





Hotel guests have a good time on its territory. At your service is a large swimming pool where you can refresh yourself at any time of the day. Nearby, on the terrace, umbrellas and sun loungers are placed - here tourists relax, sunbathe and just enjoy chatting with each other.

At your service there is also a fitness center with a small but fully equipped gym - this fact will delight travelers who prefer to always keep in good shape. You can have a good time playing a game of table football or billiards.

Entertainment can be found outside the Neptune Mare 4 * hotel complex. Greece, in particular Rhodes, is a great place for an excursion vacation, because there are a lot of historical and cultural attractions nearby that are worth seeing. In the city, and in the hotel itself, it will not be difficult to get an interesting excursion.
