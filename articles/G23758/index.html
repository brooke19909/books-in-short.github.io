Ritz-Carlton Hotel, Moscow: description and photos

Making the trip to Moscow unforgettable will help the right choice of hotel. It should be with excellent service, excellent amenities, a good location and proximity to everything that the capital has to offer. The five- star Ritz-Carlton Hotel (Moscow) fully complies with these requirements .

Description

The luxurious eleven-story hotel building is located in the heart of the city. All 334 rooms of the hotel are decorated in the classicism style of the Russian Empire of the 19th century.





In the magnificent restaurants and stylish lobby lounge of the hotel complex, you can always try the true masterpieces of international culinary art. For work and business meetings there is a business center equipped with modern equipment. For a complete rest of the guests, the hotel has a fitness studio and SPA-center with a swimming pool, sauna, steam rooms and a jacuzzi.

Also, the hotel administration is ready to offer vacationers the services of a butler, who organizes a meeting and seeing off the guest, will be an accompanying person on trips to city sights and shops, and will make foreigners who do not speak Russian language comfortable in staying in the country.

How to get there

Arriving in the capital, not all tourists seek to use the services of a taxi or a pre-ordered transfer. Sometimes traffic jams on roads force travelers to change to public transport.

But how to find the Ritz-Carlton Hotel (Moscow) on your own in a big city ? How to get to it by public transport from the airports and stations of the capital?

From the airports Domodedovo, Sheremetyevo and Vnukovo to the capital there is a high-speed train "Aeroexpress", which arrives at one of the railway stations in Moscow. From where you can instantly get to the hotel by metro.

The Ritz Carlton Hotel (Moscow) has the following address: ul. Tverskaya, house 3. It is removed at a distance of 5 minutes walk from the interchange stations of the metro "Teatralnaya", "Okhotny Ryad", "Ploshchad Revolyutsii".

Nutrition

The Ritz Carlton Hotel (Moscow) is able to satisfy a variety of gastronomic gourmet preferences:

  • The rooftop bar and restaurant O2 Lounge are famous not only for their beautiful panoramic views of the main square of the capital, which has an atmosphere and pleasant musical accompaniment, but also for delicious cocktails and excellent Japanese dishes.
  • The luxurious interiors of The Ritz-Carlton Bar & Lobby Lounge, a diverse assortment of drinks, live music incline to confidential conversations and a long rest.
  • The menu of the restaurant Caviarterra is dominated by dishes of Georgian and Russian cuisines. A popular breakfast buffet is served here each morning.
  • Petrus Hall with a unique collection of wines.

Services

The Ritz Carlton Hotel (Moscow) is the best place to host any event. At the service of the organizers:

  • 11 banquet rooms;
  • spacious ballroom;
  • meeting rooms and conference rooms.

To maintain excellent physical fitness and overall health of the body, rest and relaxation, guests can visit:

  • SPA-center with a jacuzzi and a swimming pool, baths and saunas;
  • beauty workshop;
  • Fitness Centre.

All hotel employees are highly qualified and will not disregard any desire of the client.

The Ritz Carlton Hotel (Moscow), whose photos in the tourist catalogs amaze travelers, is truly beautiful, luxurious and fully worthy of its location. Despite the high cost of living, the hotel is popular with tourists.
