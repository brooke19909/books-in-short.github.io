3D glasses for Samsung TV - the most realistic picture in an ergonomic frame

Not so long ago, the first 3D TVs appeared on sale. It was in the recent 2010. Then they failed to make a special sensation for various reasons: consumers' distrust of a completely new technology, its high cost, as well as the lack of relevant content. To date, a great many 3D TVs and accessories have appeared.

Any 3D TV

To view a three-dimensional image is equipped with special glasses. In this article we will discuss 3D glasses for Samsung TV. Its essence is to alternately demonstrate the image for the left and right eye with the help of the so-called shutters, alternately shading either one lens or the other. Such glasses are a complex device with a built-in microchip and its own power supply, the image from the TV to them is transmitted using infrared radiation. As a battery , conventional batteries (in cheaper models) or a battery can be used. Depending on the cost of the model, 3D glasses for Samsung TV can work from 40 to 150 hours.

At the moment, Samsung 3D glasses have a huge model

th row. In 2011, Samsung launched the new SSG-3100, SSG-3300 and SSG-3700 models, an important feature of which is the use of Bluetooth technology. According to the manufacturer, this technology has several advantages compared to the connection via infrared. In particular, a drop in energy consumption of twenty-five percent, a larger frequency range, as well as a decrease in the influence of various interference from infrared or radio sources. These glasses provide a larger viewing angle and a greater distance to the TV when watching. Up to a dozen pairs of glasses can be connected to one TV at a time.

Let's take a closer look at 3D glasses for Samsung TV models SSG-3300 and SSG-3700. The material for them is flexible, non-toxic nylon. This achieves high reliability and durability of the product with a relatively low weight (38 grams for the SSG-3300 and 28 grams for the SSG-3700, respectively). The battery and microchip are located behind, behind the ears. High ergonomics allows you to watch movies in 3D for hours on end, without getting tired and without feeling any discomfort. The cost of SSG-3300 in Russia is about 3000 rubles, SSG-3700 - about a thousand more.
