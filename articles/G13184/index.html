Imperial De Luxe (Turkey, Kemer)

If you want to go on vacation to Turkey and want to stay in a magnificent five-star hotel that meets the highest quality standards, then Imperial De Luxe, located in the village of Beldibi, located in the tourist region of Kemer, will be an excellent option. We suggest further details to find out what this hotel complex is, as well as to understand what conditions of rest are provided for tourists here. We will also ask for comments to people who have already visited this hotel.





Where is the hotel complex

Imperial De Luxe 5 * is located on the cleanest coast of the Mediterranean Sea, awarded the Blue Flag for its environmental friendliness. It is located in the village of Beldibi in the tourist region of Kemer, part of the Turkish province of Antalya. The nearest airport is only forty kilometers from the hotel complex. This is very convenient, because after landing in the air harbor, the path to the hotel will take you no more than 40-50 minutes. This fact is surely appreciated by people traveling with young children. Due to the fact that Imperial De Luxe is located in a small resort village and is located 12 kilometers from the center of Kemer, you will not be disturbed by the almost round-the-clock noise characteristic of most tourist cities both in Turkey and other countries. At the same time, you can always take a taxi or local minibus to the busiest areas with lots of bars, restaurants, shops, souvenir shops, nightclubs and discos. As for the sea, since the hotel is located on the first line to get to the beach, you will have to cover a distance of only a few tens of meters.

Kemer, Imperial De Luxe: Description

This hotel first opened its doors to guests in 1998. Since then, several large-scale restorations have been carried out here, the last of which occurred in 2010. Thus, today the hotel is quite modern, comfortable and equipped with everything necessary for an excellent pastime for guests. The territory of Imperial De Luxe cannot be called large. So, its area is only eight thousand square meters. However, the area is very comfortable, green and clean. In total, the hotel has 137 comfortable rooms, including family rooms and apartments for the disabled. In general, Imperial De Luxe (Kemer) is a great place to relax families with children, as well as newlyweds and groups of young people.





Hotel Policies

According to the rules adopted in the hotel complex "Imperial De Luxe" (Beldibi, Kemer), the settlement of newly arriving travelers in their rooms begins after two in the afternoon. If you arrived earlier than the indicated time, and your room is already ready, then you will be settled immediately. If you arrived earlier than 14:00, but your room has not yet been vacated by previous guests, or hotel staff has not prepared it yet, you will be asked to leave things in the luggage room and go for a snack, take a walk around the territory or swim in the pool or sea . On the day of departure, you must leave the room before noon. When handing over the room keys, payment should also be made for accommodation (if you did not pay the cost of accommodation to your travel agency), as well as for the use of additional services (of course, if you resorted to those). You can pay both in cash and with plastic cards of the Visa and Mastercard systems. The possibility of paying with cards of other payment systems should be obtained in advance from the administration of Imperial De Luxe.





As a family-run hotel, travelers with children of all ages are welcome here. In this case, it is possible to place them on extra or cots. The administration of the hotel complex should be warned in advance about the need to provide this service.

Regarding the placement of pets at the hotel, unfortunately, tourists who prefer to go on vacation in the company of their four-legged friends, placing the latter in the Imperial De Luxe is prohibited.

Number of rooms

As already mentioned, Imperial De Luxe hotel 5 * offers its guests to stay in one of 137 comfortable and tastefully furnished rooms. In general, the hotel is designed for simultaneous accommodation of 288 guests. We offer a more detailed stay on the categories of apartments.

Standard rooms

Imperial De Luxe 5 * standard room is a stylishly furnished room with new furniture. There are 127 of them in the hotel, plus one room for the disabled. The standard room has either two single beds or one double bed. It is also possible to install an extra single or baby bed. Each standard room has a balcony with excellent views of the sea, the pool or the mountains and the garden, a TV with satellite channels, a telephone, a minibar, air conditioning, which works both for cooling and heating, a bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer, as well as a safe, the use of which is free.

Junior Suite

Hotel Imperial De Luxe has four rooms in this category. Each of them consists of two rooms separated by a wall and a door. The room has two bathrooms with a Jacuzzi, hairdryer, bathrobes and slippers, as well as a minibar, central air conditioning, flat-screen TV, telephone, tea set, safe. The apartments have a balcony with magnificent sea views.

It consists of a bedroom, a living room and two bathrooms. This type of room has a huge double bed, a desk suitable for meetings and meetings, a couch, an aquarium with beautiful fish and plants, satellite TV, telephone, minibar, air conditioning (winter-summer), safe, marble floors, tea and coffee sets. Two bathrooms are equipped with a Jacuzzi, shower, bathtub, hairdryer, bathrobes and slippers. This room type has a balcony with luxurious sea views.





Service

In all types of rooms located in this five-star hotel, both cleaning and linen and bath towels are changed on a daily basis. As for the replacement of beach towels, this procedure is carried out every other day. Hotel maids also regularly replenish toiletries in all bathrooms. In addition, you can always order room service by calling the reception.

Nutrition

Meals at the five-star hotel complex Imperial De Luxe are organized according to the "ultra ol inclusive" system. This means that hotel guests can enjoy refreshments and local alcoholic drinks around the clock, as well as some foreign drinks and freshly squeezed juice. For a fee, you will be offered fine wines, cognacs, premium drinks and cigars.





Three times a day a buffet is served in the main restaurant of the hotel, consisting of various and skillfully prepared dishes, appetizers, desserts of both national and international cuisine. Here you can always enjoy fresh fruit. During the day, guests can have a snack in the snack bars, as well as taste delicious ice cream. In addition to the main buffet restaurant, there are three √† la carte restaurants on site. Here you can order delicious Italian dishes, fish dishes as well as sushi from the menu.

Bars

There are several bars in the hotel complex: in the lobby (open 24 hours a day), by the pool (open from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m.), on the pier (from 10:00 to 18:00), disco bar (from 11 a.m. pm to three in the morning), ice bar (from noon to five in the evening). The hotel also has a snack bar where you can enjoy pizza, sandwiches, french fries and other snacks throughout the day. In addition, a wine and hookah house is located on the territory of the hotel complex.

Entertainment

Hotel Imperial De Luxe 5 * offers its guests a lot of entertainment for every taste. So, animation deserves special attention. It is divided into daytime (boccia, aqua aerobics, futsal, archery and air rifle, table tennis, stretching exercises, step aerobics, as well as various competitions and tournaments) and evening (show programs, live music, discos , theme nights, etc.). There is also a special entertainment program for the youngest guests: a kids club, a mini-disco, competitions, etc. Also, for an additional fee, you can use the services of a nanny.

The hotel has three pools: outdoor, indoor and children's. Near them there is a spacious sun terrace with sun loungers and parasols.

You can also visit the fitness center, Turkish bath (hammam), sauna, beauty salon, massage room.

Sea beach

Hotel Imperial De Luxe has its own sand and pebble beach with a pier. There is a sufficient number of comfortable sun loungers and parasols. On the beach you can ride a banana, water skiing, parachuting, as well as take surfing or diving lessons.





Imperial De Luxe: traveler reviews

Since it is extremely important for many travelers to find out what impressions a particular hotel has left for people who have already been here, we have generalized the comments of our compatriots who spent their summer vacation at Imperial De Luxe (Beldibi, Kemer).

First of all, guests pay attention to their rooms. So, according to our compatriots, they, as stated, are indeed recently renovated, comfortable and furnished with quality furniture. However, some travelers were confused by such facts as an unclosed bathroom door, very noisy air conditioning without the ability to adjust it, etc. In this regard, they would have awarded the Imperial De Luxe hotel 4 stars, but not 5.

As for nutrition, in general, the quality of the dishes was on top. However, some guests noted that the choice of dishes in a five-star hotel could be a little more diverse. Also, some visitors were confused by the lack of soups for lunch.

Many travelers appreciated the animation at Imperial De Luxe. According to them, the animators work on their conscience and put a lot of effort to prevent guests from getting bored. Also at the height was animation designed for kids.

As for the pools, there were no special complaints from the guests. The greatest delight among tourists left, of course, the beach. The vast majority of travelers were very pleased with the clean territory and warm sea, as well as the water activities available here.

Mostly positive impressions of our compatriots were left by the staff of the Hotel Imperial De Luxe. So, despite the fact that the vast majority of employees of the hotel complex did not speak or understand Russian, they were always very friendly towards the guests and tried to do everything possible to please them.

Tourists also liked the local spa. According to them, they make an excellent massage, peeling and other cosmetic procedures.
