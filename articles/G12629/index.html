Balneological Center - Sergiev Mineralnye Vody

The main feature of the resort center is Sulfur Lake, filled with healing sulfur-water springs that help people to improve their health. Clean air and wildlife untouched by civilization allow tourists to recharge with powerful energy and get a boost of energy. The village is famous for needle mud and mineral water.





Coming here, you can heal your health with the help of hirudotherapy, coniferous baths and therapeutic exercises. On the territory of the village there are numerous sanatoriums where professional doctors work. The outback of Russia is also rich in cultural and historical sites. Mysterious places, unknown streams, unpopular trails - everything here attracts tourists who seek solitude with nature.

Ski Balneological Resort - Lower Sergi

The ski resorts of Russia are scattered throughout the fertile land. One of the best and most comfortable is the city under the interesting name Lower Sergi (Urals). The tourist area is surrounded on all sides by chalk cliffs, which have a beneficial effect on the body - a scientifically proven fact. The ski resort is famous for its mountain ranges, coniferous forests, comfortable conditions, baths and picturesque lakes.





A huge resort complex with ski slopes has been built here. Come here - and you will have the opportunity to ride on the Ural mountain slopes, breathe a breath of fresh air and enjoy the surrounding natural landscapes. In addition to entertainment services, medical and health procedures are carried out here (magnetotherapy, hirudotherapy, mud treatment, speleocameras, massage). Living in this mysterious reserve corner, you will plunge into the world of healing springs, mountain elevations and Ural hospitality.

Pyatigorsk - a diversified mud balneological center

The list of Russian resorts is famous for outlandish places with mineral springs, such as the beautiful city of Pyatigorsk, located in the Stavropol Territory. Today, over forty radon, carbon dioxide, hydrogen sulfide and mineral waters are discovered on its vast territory. An excellent diagnostic base and modern techniques have been created here. In sanatoriums and health resorts of Pyatigorsk they treat cardiovascular and gynecological diseases, diseases of the nervous system and musculoskeletal system.





Absolutely all boarding houses and balneological institutions accept people with ENT diseases, pathologies of the spine and biliary system. Every year, tourists from different countries come here to relax and improve their health. Among the most popular health resorts of the resort are the following: "Dawns of the Stavropol Territory", "Rodnik", "Lenin Rocks", "Pyatigorye" and "Pyatigorsk Narzan".

Anapa is a sunny city in the south of the Russian Federation
