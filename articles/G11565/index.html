How to sew a fur vest with your own hands quickly

Fashion always comes back, and the latest trends in bohemian magazines dictate new rules in choosing clothes. Not every girl can afford to constantly replenish her wardrobe with expensive novelties, and if she is also a non-standard figure, then buying an exclusive little thing can become a real problem. How to sew a fur vest with your own hands without an appropriate education, professionals suggest.



Preparation for work

First you need to decide on the material and model. Here you must immediately take into account that working with natural fur is quite difficult, in addition, it is very expensive, and it will be unprofitable to spoil it for the family budget. Therefore, for the first test it is worth using a high-quality imitation that you can now buy at any fabric store. The main advantages of this material are as follows:

  • A huge number of colors and textures. You can even choose the fur of a "polar bear" or "wombat" without threatening rare animals and your wallet.
  • Such fabrics are very easy to process, and even without a lining, such a product will look good. There is also a kind of faux fur, which is already sold with a stitched and trimmed inside.
  • Convenience of cut - no need to try to lay out the pieces on your pattern, you can safely cut the desired piece from a wide roll.

V-Neck Model

For work, you need to choose the appropriate material, sharp scissors, some vest or a well-fitting jacket, lining fabric, if necessary, chalk or a special marker for cutting, threads and additional jewelry. For example, it can be a leather belt with metal inserts.

Before you figure out how to sew a fur vest yourself, you need to think over the model. Very long clothes with a belt will make the low figure visually even smaller and wider. Short vests are suitable for almost everyone, except for girls with a pronounced pear-shaped body type (with very wide hips). The selected model with a V-neck is the most optimal option, which suits almost everything.

Making a fur vest

The whole process rarely takes more than three to four hours, if everything you need was prepared in advance. How to sew a fur vest with your own hands faster? Use suitable clothing from your wardrobe: turn the product inside out and gently transfer the pattern to a piece of thick paper. By the way, the same method allows you to solve the problem of how to sew a fur vest for a child. You can not prepare the pattern, but simply attach the selected sample to the wrong side of the fabric and circle around the contour. Be sure to add 2 cm to the allowances, and the pile of fur should be directed clearly down. Cut the workpiece: you should get two identical parts. We leave the back so, and cut the shelf into two equal parts, carefully adjusting the corners of the neck to get a V-shaped neckline. When deciding how to sew a fur vest with your own hands easier, you need to look for the opportunity to sew all the seams on a typewriter. However, the manual seam will be strong enough with the right choice of thread. The lining is already cut out on the vest and also allow seam allowances. Next, they attach the wrong side and decorate the product with a finished belt or an original brooch.
