Services

In Alkyon Resort 4 * customers are provided with free Wi-Fi and parking (no reservation is required). The registration desk here works 24/7, so check-in and check-out are possible on an individual basis. However, all this is discussed separately.

The hotel also has an office with photocopy and fax services. Tour services and ticket booking are also available for guests. By the way, people who think that the sea is far away may not have to worry - free transport regularly goes to the beach from the hotel, taking tourists back then.

Leisure

On the territory of Alkyon Resort 4 * (Halkidiki) there is not so much entertainment as in many other hotels. Nevertheless, they are here too. There is table tennis, a billiard room, and a pool with a nearby bar where you can enjoy a variety of refreshing drinks. And on the lawn, guests can play badminton.

Guests can also spend time alone in the picturesque hotel garden, or watch shows in the lobby. For children, by the way, there is a separate pool with a shallow depth, as well as a playground. There is a slide, a trampoline, and a swing.

There are no animated shows here. So the hotel is perfect for people who want to spend a calm and quiet holiday.
