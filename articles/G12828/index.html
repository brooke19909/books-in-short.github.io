Alarm "Starline A91": reviews. StarLine A91 - Car Alarm

For car owners, a high-quality and reliable alarm system with wide functionality is literally worth its weight in gold. One of such unique systems is the Starline A91 alarm system.

Functional

Car Alarm Starline A91 has wide functionality. You can activate options by pressing the remote control keys, or in automatic mode.

The functionality of the key fob with a liquid crystal display includes not only control of the system‚Äôs commands, but also displaying information on the status of the security system, engine temperature and in the car, time and setting an alarm. The keychain is convenient to use in the dark due to the built-in backlight. Sound alerts option available.





Protected Areas

When the alarm sensors "Starline A91" are triggered, an alarm is activated, and the corresponding information is displayed on the key fob display. The protected areas of the complex are controlled by certain elements of the system:

  1. Limit switches are responsible for the hood, doors, trunk and brake.
  2. The shock sensor responds to effects on the car body.
  3. Using an additional sensor, the interior can be monitored.
  4. The ignition circuit control input is responsible for ignition.
  5. The relay controls the engine.

Alarm "Starline A91" is equipped with a switchable anti-robbery function with the option of switching to hidden mode. When you press a key combination on the key fob, the car engine shuts off and the alarm is triggered. The immobilizer mode allows you to turn off the car engine, provided that there are no keys in the ignition for 30 seconds. The turbo timer keeps the engine running after the ignition is turned off.

The car engine lock is programmed in two stages. Emergency shutdown of the alarm is carried out by means of an individual code.





Remote control

The function of remote starting and stopping the engine has wide settings:

  • The choice of engine type and transmission.
  • Increased running time of the engine.
  • Starter overload protection.
  • Automatic engine start by temperature, timer and alarm.

Monitoring the operation of the car engine is based on data from the tachometer and voltage changes in the on-board network. The key fob display shows the operating time of the motor.

Cryptographic strength of the system

Alarm "Starline A91" has a good level of cryptographic strength. The keys to the dialog code set by the user do not allow hacking the system. All security complex settings are saved after a power outage, which avoids reconfiguration.





LED lightening

LED indication notifies the driver about the status of the system and possible problems:

  • Faulty security sensors.
  • Alarm zone and reasons for alarm activation.
  • When the security mode is on, it is about inactive zones.
  • General condition of car alarms.
  • The state of the limit switches.

Service functions of the security complex

The Starline alarm service functions include 25 settings and operating modes, the most important of which are the following:

  • Protection with the engine running.
  • Silent work.
  • Search for a car.
  • Panic.
  • Silently enable and disable the security mode.
  • Utility mode.
  • Call a car.

Users in reviews of Starline A91 alarms note the possibility of the most flexible and convenient system setup thanks to a wide range of parameters and modes.





Keyfob programming

Information about four different trinkets can be entered into the memory of the security complex at the same time. Connecting to the car alarm remote controls as follows:

  1. When the car engine is turned off, the Valet service button is pressed seven times in a row, which starts the activation process of the key fobs. The button itself is located in the passenger compartment in a place accessible to the driver during the installation of the alarm.
  2. After starting the engine, the system should emit seven signals in a row, which informs about the readiness of the complex to memorize key rings.
  3. The buttons 2 and 3 are simultaneously pressed on the control panel. The keys are held until a siren signal sounds, indicating activation of the key fob.
  4. A similar process is performed with the remaining trinkets, after which the engine is turned off.

Car alarms are a very sensitive system, which is why many car owners are faced with frequent causeless alarms. In the case of the Starline A91 security complex, the reason lies either in poor mounting of the shock sensor and central control unit, or in incorrect sensitivity settings.

The instructions for Starline A91 alarms describe a method for adjusting the sensitivity of sensors through a control unit to which a service button is connected.

Setting the time is carried out only using the key fob. To do this, key 3 is held until the clock icon flashes on the screen. Hours and minutes are set by pressing buttons 1 and 2. To save the set time, just press the 3 button again.

Car owners in their Starline A91 alarm reviews point out the following benefits:

Possible alarm malfunctions

Car owners most often face the following problems:

  1. False system triggering. The reason is incorrect sensor sensitivity settings.
  2. Lack of alarm response to commands from the key fob. It is eliminated either by replacing the batteries in the remote control, or by purchasing a new control panel.
  3. Do not lock the car door. The causes of the malfunction can be different - from system malfunctions to problems with door locks.
