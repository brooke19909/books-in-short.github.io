Lithuanian cities are a great choice for tourism

The Republic of Lithuania is located in the eastern part of the Baltic Sea coast . This country has a rich centuries-old history. Now Lithuania is a highly developed European power with a strong economy and rich cultural life. The country is very popular among tourists. The cities of Lithuania are very beautiful and peculiar, with numerous attractions and unique natural landscapes. Lithuania is also the beaches of the Baltic Sea, mirror rivers and lakes, picturesque valleys and meadows.

Vilnius

The capital of Lithuania is the beautiful and ancient city of Vilnius. This is the center of the political, cultural and economic life of the country. Thousands of tourists visit the city every year, a huge number of magnificent monuments of architecture, churches and cathedrals, unique natural attractions are collected here.

In the Old Town is a huge number of ancient structures that are of great historical value. Cities of Lithuania, such as Vilnius, store many buildings of the Middle Ages. Cathedral Square is located in the very center - this is the most favorite place for romantic dates and meetings. The main church of the country is Vilnius Cathedral. And to enjoy the panorama of the whole city, you should climb the Mountain of Three Crosses, amazing landscapes open here.

Kaunas

The beautiful cities of Lithuania, such as Kaunas, annually attract a multimillion crowd of tourists. This is the second largest city, it amazes with its amazing beauty and grandeur. Kaunas was founded in the thirteenth century in the valley of the confluence of two large rivers Neris and Namunyas.

Its constructions and buildings represent the great art of architecture of various centuries. The city hall rises in the very center of Kaunas, the building dates back to the 16th century. This area is considered to be rich in cultural heritage and traditions. Outstanding artists, writers and composers of Lithuania lived and worked here. One of the most picturesque and beautiful places of the city is the central street - Laisves Alley, which connects the New and Old Towns. A map of Lithuania with cities will help tourists traveling around this unique and colorful country well and quickly.

Klaipeda

Lithuanian land is rich in many interesting sights. Cities of Lithuania are amazing architectural buildings, old streets, beautiful landscapes and sea landscapes. Klaipeda is a small and cozy town.

This city is the only port of Lithuania, in addition, it is considered the most northern and ice-free port of the Baltic. Many centuries ago, the port of Klaipeda was an important trading center for the northern and western countries of Europe. The city is perfect for relaxing, it has beautiful white sand beaches, a unique climate and beautiful landscapes. Having visited Klaipeda, you have the opportunity to visit the most interesting Sea Festival, which is held on the last weekend of July. And you should definitely visit the famous Museum of watches, here is a large collection of watches of various types and times. Lithuanian cities such as Klaipeda are an ideal place to relax on the Baltic coast.
