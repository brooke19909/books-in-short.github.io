Vacation calculation: formula, example. Calculation of parental leave

The calculation of vacation pay is a very important issue for both the employer and the employee. Far from the intricacies of bookkeeping, it may seem to people that charging an employee the amount due for payment is quite simple. However, in this case there are many nuances that must be taken into account in order to organize the right conclusions.

So, you need to consider how many days an employee worked before starting his vacation, whether he had sick leave, absenteeism, days "at his own expense", etc. All these points directly or indirectly affect the processes for determining the amount of vacation payments.

Basic components

Consider the main positions that are taken into account when calculating vacation pay:

  • The time that the employee was actually on vacation.
  • The average vacation time is 28 days;
  • If the employee has been working for less than a year, then leave can be granted only after six months from the start of work. There are also some exceptions.
  • Vacation is divided into parts, and each part must be paid before it begins.

The initial data included in the formula for calculating vacation pay are:

  • The actual vacation time in days. This parameter is easy to determine.
  • The size of the average salary. This indicator needs to be calculated, as will be described below.

Consider a specific calculation example that has been used since 2017.

Suppose a company employee wants to go on vacation for two weeks (for 14 days). The conditions of his work:

  • From 01.03.2016 to 01.04.2017 the time was fully worked out.
  • 450,000 rubles were accrued during the working year.
  • Of the amount of accruals 35,000 rubles. amount to tuition, 37,000 rubles - payment on sick leave.

Subtract these amounts from the charges:

450,000 - 35,000 - 37,000 = 378,000 rubles.

Next, divide the amount received by 12 months and by a coefficient of 29.3.

378000/12 / 29.3 = 1075.08 rubles.

Multiply this amount by the number of vacation days:

1075.08 x 14 = 15051 rubles. This is the amount of vacation pay.

Consider how to determine the average salary.

The foundations of such a calculation are laid down in the Labor Code of the Russian Federation (Article 139).

Use the following parameters:

  • All amounts actually accrued to the employee.
  • All payments are added up one year before going on vacation (i.e. 12 months);
  • The average daily earnings is the quotient of dividing the total amount of payments by twelve months and the number 29.3.

Thus, the formula for calculating the average daily earnings for vacation pay has the form:

ZP cf = (OV / 12) / 29.3 (rubles).





How to calculate vacation period

This value is no less important than the accrual of monetary amounts. The formula for calculating the vacation period in the form of a conditional number of days worked is as follows:

RD = 29.3 x Mes + RNM / DNM x 29.3 x KNM, where:

  • Mes - the number of fully worked months.
  • RNM - the number of calendar days in the worked period in incomplete months.
  • DNM - the number of calendar days in incomplete months.
  • KNM - the number of incomplete months in a period.

The value of 29.3 is established by law and has been used since 2017.

The value of the duration of vacation is determined by the formula:

  • DO = (OS * DTK / 12) - ON, where:
  • TO - the duration of the vacation (days).
  • OS - vacation experience (months).
  • DTK - the number of vacation days for a full year of work (28 days).
  • PO - the number of vacation days used.

If an employee quits and receives a payroll, he is compensated.
