Bowling in Togliatti "Cosmos": how to get there? Reviews

Recently, such a sport and leisure activity as bowling has become very popular among people of all ages. In particular, this game is in demand during the winter holidays. Outside, the weather is cold and bad, and the bowling club is warm, comfortable and has a wonderful opportunity to have a good rest and meet with your beloved friends.





What is bowling?

Bowling is like bowling. Balls with different weights need to shoot down pins arranged in a row (there are ten in total). The game is quite interesting and difficult, not only men and women like it, but also children. In addition to the pleasure that bowling provides, it also brings benefits: physical activity, the development of coordination, accuracy.

Now almost all cities have bowling clubs where you can play this exciting game. The city of Togliatti is not an exception, the Cosmos bowling center located on the territory of the eponymous store gives all citizens the opportunity to enjoy a real bowling alley.

Bowling club is located in the shopping center of the same name. It is adjacent to the Central Park and thereby expands the possibilities of entertainment and recreation in Togliatti. Bowling "Cosmos", a cinema, playgrounds, attractions, shops - this is not the whole range of entertainment that a shopping center boasts. Hundreds of citizens come here daily for shopping and relaxation.

Many residents of the city of Togliatti often go here . Bowling "Cosmos" is a great place for them to relax with family or friends.

The main rules in bowling

One game batch includes ten frames, in each of which the player rolls 2 times. If he manages to put all the pins on the first try, he gets a strike, which is indicated in the game by the ‚ÄúX‚Äù sign. From the second time it turns out spar - "/". When non-adjacent pins remain on the track after the first throw, the combination is called split. If after throwing the pins remain on the track, the frame is considered open. If a player knocks a strike, the total amount of points is equal to the number of pins knocked down. When a player knocks out sper in a frame, the sum of the points is equal to the number of pins knocked down in the current frame, plus those that fell during the first throw of the next frame. When the frame is open, points are awarded for the total number of pins knocked down.

The last frame is of particular importance. If during it the player throws the strike during the first throw, he is entitled to two more throws. If a spar is knocked out, the player has the right to one throw. If not all pins are shot down for both throws, the frame remains open, and the game ends.
