Hotel, Emelyanovo Airport, Krasnoyarsk: description, reviews of tourists, how to get

Large international airports are usually located outside the city. And increasingly, in order not to be late for the flight, passengers prefer to leave in advance and spend the night at a hotel near the airport. The hotel "Airport Emelyanovo" offers its services to such passengers. Krasnoyarsk is thirty kilometers from here, so this hotel is quite in demand.

Hotel "Airport Emelyanovo": description

The hotel is located very close to Yemelyanovo International Airport, just 350 meters away. What is important for transit passengers, the reception is open around the clock. Guests enjoy free Wi-Fi access in public areas. On the territory of the hotel "Airport Emelyanovo" in Krasnoyarsk has its own bar, mini-market, a vending machine with drinks. In the morning, a continental breakfast is served.

Car owners can use the free public parking nearby, for this you need to make a reservation. Tourists can leave their belongings in a luggage storage room at an additional cost. The hotel offers pet-friendly accommodation. The service is possible with a preliminary request, paid separately. There is no elevator in the hotel.

This accommodation option is suitable for those passengers who want to rent a hotel at the Emelyanovo airport in Krasnoyarsk inexpensively. For guests, rooms are provided for any wallet. You can pay for a place in the common room, as well as a separate, comfortable standard or superior room. The hotel is located at the airport: Emelyanovo, Russia.

How to get to the airport

If you want to stay in a hotel at the Emelyanovo airport of Krasnoyarsk, you need to know how convenient it is to get there. From Krasnoyarsk it can be a taxi or a bus.

On regular buses No. 513, which regularly runs to the international airport and vice versa, the journey time will take you one hour.

Another affordable way to get to the airport or hotel is with a fixed price taxi. The cost of one trip is about the same as what you pay to local carriers. You just need to leave a request. The operator will call you immediately and place an order. The driver will not only pick you up at the indicated time, but also help you load your luggage. If you are traveling with children or comfortable conditions are important to you, this option will be preferable.
