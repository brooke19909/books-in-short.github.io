Acer AL1916W Monitor Overview

Monitor Acer AL1916W - this is not a new model of the company. This monitor first appeared on sale back in 2006 and almost instantly gained popular fame. The whole reason for the popularity was that for a very reasonable and affordable price, users were offered a widescreen version with excellent characteristics and a resolution higher than the standard 1280 x 1024. Today this monitor can no longer be found in stores, but they are full in the secondary market, where they are are not so expensive. However, now about everything in order.





Contents of delivery

Initially, the Acer AL1916W monitor was delivered in an ordinary cardboard box, which did not attract attention. Now this model in the original box is extremely difficult to find and, rather, even impossible.





As for the configuration, it is not very rich. In addition to the monitor itself, a collapsible leg (also known as a stand), a standard VGA cable for connecting to a computer, and a power cord came with it. There was also a warranty card and user manual. In fairness, it is worth noting that the equipment of modern monitors is practically no different.

Appearance

The design of the Acer AL1916W monitor is pretty standard, and by today's standards, it‚Äôs completely boring. In total, 2 types of colors were provided - a completely black version and with a silver front frame. It was the first option that looked most interesting than its silver "brother". The materials from which the case is assembled are high-quality plastic with a slightly matte, so to speak, coating. And this is very cool, because there are practically no fingerprints left.





The screen also has a matte finish. The prints are not visible, but spots, splashes and other contaminants are visible very well during operation, and this interferes. Just rubbing it with a slightly damp cloth or cloth does not always help, and sometimes you have to spend some time to clean everything.

As for the stand, it is quite good. In the assembled state, there are no backlashes, all parts are very tightly fitted to each other. The stand also has an engraved company logo, which has a slightly glossy finish.





The monitor has only 2 output connectors, which are located behind the protective cover (you can remove it if necessary). On the left is a connector for connecting a VGA cable, and on the right is a socket for a power cord. By the way, the network cable does not hold in place quite reliably, so, for example, when dusting, the cable can be slightly hooked, as a result of which the contact will break and the monitor will not work.





Actually, the rear also houses a power supply unit with ventilation holes. On individual Acer AL1916W models, this unit had 4 holes for mounting the monitor on the wall. There is also a sticker that indicates the year of manufacture, model name and other information.





The front side is completely occupied by the screen space. The monitor model is marked on the top right of the frame, and a bulging logo is glued to the bottom center. A little lower is a separate unit with controls. Only 5 buttons: Auto, left, right, Menu and power button with backlight. Presses feel very good, the course is small but clear.

Monitor Specifications

Now it's time to get acquainted with the characteristics of the Acer AL1916W. The type of matrix installed TN, the diagonal is 19 inches, and the aspect ratio is 16:10. Such parameters were rare at the time of release and represented a kind of middle ground between monitors with a resolution of 1280 x 1024 and 1600 x 1200 models. The resolution of the Acer AL1916W is 1440 x 900, which is not so bad, but it is not a standard. Repeatedly, many users of Windows 7 and older were faced with the fact that there was no necessary permission in the system settings, and it was necessary to configure it manually.





The response time is only 5 ms, and the maximum refresh rate is 70 Hz. As for color rendering, it is very good here. The brightness margin is also pleasing, its rate is 300 cd / sq. There are no problems with contrast either. The viewing angles are decent - 160 degrees both vertically and horizontally.

User reviews

Reviews of the Acer AL1916W show that this monitor has practically no shortcomings, with the exception of one thing - a power capacitor is very often out of order on a power supply unit, a factory capacitor goes out, due to which the monitor starts to work incorrectly, namely it turns on and goes off immediately. This problem can be fixed easily, just replace the damaged "Conder" with a new one, only from a trusted manufacturer. Otherwise, there are no complaints.





Cost

At the moment, you can buy Acer AL1916W in the secondary market or flea markets for 1500-3000 rubles. It is very cheap and the monitor is definitely worth the money. The most important thing is that it is in full working condition, the rest (condition of the case) is no longer so important. This model, if followed and carefully treated, will be able to work for more than one year without any problems, every day delighting its owner.
