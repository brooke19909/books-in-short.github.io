"Utkino Country House": where is it, description, photo

At the end of April 2017, a unique hotel complex ‚ÄúUtkino Country House‚Äù was opened near Rostov-on-Don, which can amaze even the most experienced travelers and connoisseurs of comfortable relaxation with the scale.

A huge territory (494 hectares), a spa, horse and shooting clubs, a restaurant that serves dishes made from products of local farms and game - such exclusive services are not always available even at well-known international resorts, and for Russia this approach is unique.



Where is the complex located?

Utkino Country House is located 160 km from the glorious southern city of Rostov-on-Don, on the banks of Manych. A two-hour walk in the executive car from the airport, organized by the staff of the complex, is just the beginning of the holiday. A pleasant road through the steppe, beautiful views of Don nature will have a calming effect on guests.

Complex owner

A successful businessman from Rostov-on-Don A. Frolov, who founded the Flash holding and several companies in our country and abroad, in early 2012 decided to create Utkino Country House. For five years, the owner has created a green oasis on the Don expanses among the steppes.

In January 2017, the brand received international status - the creation of the Utkino Spa & Lifestyle Resort hotel in Croatia (Tisno city) was announced.





Number of rooms

The Utkino Country House hotel offers cottage-type suites and 48 superior rooms for accommodation, which meet international 5 * standards - high-quality English Burlington sanitary ware, Ascona orthopedic mattresses, Samsung LED TV panels.

In presidential cottages, guests will be pleasantly surprised by the Woodland porcelain collection dishes of the famous English brand Spode. All cottages are built from the famous Angarsk pine, which is famous for the powerful tonic effect on the body. The interior of the rooms looks stylish and original at the same time: vintage furniture harmoniously coexists with neobarochnye mirrors and lamps, the walls are decorated with hunting trophies and old guns, which, as you know, are the attributes of every aristocratic country house.





Exquisite, custom-made by the best masters, cast-iron lace staircase in a hunting house would probably be able to decorate the interior of a French mansion dating back to the late 19th century.

Complex services

At Utkino Country House, guests can enjoy many services. They are provided by sensitive and highly professional staff.

SPA-center

Located on the river bank and has a stylish and very cozy interior, a wide range of services. Among them: a hammam and a rasul, a steam room on grasses and a Russian steam room, an ice font and warming infrared rooms, a jacuzzi and a fifteen meter pool. As you can see, the list allows you to compete with many upscale foreign resorts.

Separately, it must be said about the salt room, which is built of dense salt bricks from the Himalayas. The course of treatment consists of ten procedures. It has a beneficial effect on the bronchi and lungs, is recommended not only for adults, but also for children. And of course, it is necessary to say about saltwater flotaria, which produces the effect of swimming in the Dead Sea.





A restaurant

The restaurant chef A. Maistrenko hone his skills under the guidance of French and Japanese masters who worked in our country over the years. He managed to form an excellent restaurant team of the hotel, to rally young but experienced professionals who love their job.

The cuisine of Utkino restaurant meets the philosophy of the complex and is aimed at an experiment based on folk traditions. A complete rejection of harmful additives, environmental friendliness, the use of only high-quality farm products and game, harmoniously combined with advanced technologies. For example, twelve-hour longing at low temperature turns hard pheasant meat into tender, similar to foie gras paste, while retaining taste.

Rest in Utkino

The hotel complex helps adults and young guests to establish close contact with nature. These are classes at the equestrian club, which has eleven thoroughbred horses, and excursions to the pheasant garden accompanied by the huntsman, and to the Martynovsky forest, where roe deer, mouflon, fallow deer live, trips to Shaminskaya Gora on ATVs, boat trips.





Fishing

Fishing in Manych is an exciting and exciting activity. Carp and common carp, bream and ram, scavenger and rudd, predatory zander, pike, catfish, and, of course, the famous Manych crayfish are found here.
