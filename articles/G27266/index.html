Grand Atilla Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya): description, service, reviews

Everyone who wants to relax cheaply and on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, many tour operators offer to go to the Grand Atilla Hotel 4 *. As many of them cite in their data, a trip for two to this place costs an average of 30,000 rubles if they stay in standard class apartments for a week.

The climatic conditions in Turkey allow you to relax here comfortably throughout the resort period. A considerable number of tourists come here in September - the weather in Alanya, where the hotel is located, at this time allows you to spend a long time on the beach, enjoying the warm waters of the Mediterranean Sea.

general information

The hotel was built a long time ago - in 1989. However, despite this, at the moment it still has a modern look, its body looks as if it had just been built. This result is supported by the regular conduct of various kinds of renovations, one of which was made in 2015.

Many guests in their reviews of the "Grand Atilla" describe it as a fairly calm and cozy place, which is perfect for a family holiday. Couples in love who want privacy are often resting here.

Today, its area is 2400 square meters. m, which is a little, but it has an aquazone, a small garden and a building in which the entire fund is located.



Location

The hotel is located in one of the districts of Alanya - Obakey. It is located near the center of this area - literally three kilometers. The Mediterranean coast is located almost under the windows of the building - 50 meters from it. This circumstance is very pleasing to hotel guests, since they do not have to travel long distances in order to get to the sea.

All tourists who fly to Turkey get to the international airport of the city of Antalya, from where they are picked up by a comfortable car provided by Grand Atilla Hotel 4 * as part of the transfer service, which is mandatory in every package. This is due to the distance of the airport from the hotel - the distance between them is 140 kilometers.

The attention of tourists is often drawn to the presence of famous attractions near this place. One of them is the Red Tower, which is 4.5 km from the vacation spot. Six kilometers from the hotel is Alanya Fortress, which is depicted on almost all the souvenirs that tourists purchase in the markets from merchants for their relatives and friends.



Rooms

For guests, the hotel provides 102 rooms, which are located in one large seven-story building. All of them are presented in one category - the standard.

The description of the Grand Atilla Hotel 4 * states that all the apartments that are in the hotel are quite comfortably furnished, they have a beautiful interior, which, according to vacationers, sets up for a wonderful holiday and a relaxed atmosphere.

Each room is under the influence of a central air conditioner, which operates on a specific schedule. Despite this, many tourists in their reviews note that in hot weather there is coolness in the building, which, of course, pleases.

Each room has a private bathroom, in which each guest is equipped with everything necessary to maintain hygiene. In addition to modern plumbing, there are bath towels, slippers, bathrobes, as well as soap accessories. Each bathroom also has a hairdryer.





All apartments are equipped with a rather large balcony, on which there are a couple of chairs and a table where you can relax and enjoy the very beautiful expanses that can be seen from here.

Room features

All apartments have a standard furniture set for a couple of guests. The main place in it is occupied by a large double bed of the European type, near which there is a pair of pedestals (on both sides). Opposite it is a wardrobe, which is mounted on the wall to save space. There is also a surface on which you can place a small number of things. On the wall of the room there is a large plasma TV connected to a satellite broadcasting system. A huge number of guests is quite enough because there are several Russian-language channels.



The apartments also have a well-equipped work area, which includes a desk and a comfortable chair with a soft seat. Staying here, tourists can enjoy access to the Internet, so they always stay in touch with their friends and relatives, even when being far from them.

If desired, guests can take advantage of the possibility of renting a safe, a landline telephone and a minibar.



Services

Service at Grand Atilla Hotel 4 * provides for a number of additional services that guests can use. As guests themselves noted in their comments, the possibilities are quite wide. In particular, a lot of their attention is paid to the rental of cars and bicycles, which operates in a place for recreation. Often, vacationers recommend using a rented vehicle to travel around the area and explore the sights.

If desired, tourists can hand over their belongings to dry cleaning and laundry services, the services of which are paid. Also, guests are offered the services of a doctor who is on duty in his office around the clock.

On the ground floor of the main building there is an exchange office, a tour desk, as well as a beauty salon where you can visit several types of cosmetic procedures, it offers the services of a hairdresser and a manicurist.

Nutrition

The Grand Atilla Hotel 4 * provides excellent food in a rather large restaurant, where a daily buffet is organized, in which tourists who live according to the all-inclusive concept can take part. According to them, they serve quite tasty and vibrant dishes prepared according to recipes traditional for Turkish and European cuisine.

The bar, which is also located in the building, has a wide selection of light salads, snacks and fresh exotic fruits. Guests will find a wide range of drinks, among which there are both alcoholic and non-alcoholic.



Entertainment

Grand Atilla Hotel 4 * (Alanya) has a small team of very funny animators who can find an approach to guests who are in different age categories. According to tourists, some of them are fluent in Russian, which greatly facilitates communication with guests from CIS countries.

Every day, entertainment events of various kinds are held here, especially discos are especially popular among them. Very often, companies of vacationers gather in rooms specially designed for playing darts, table tennis and billiards. Sports guests are very fond of staying on the site designed for playing volleyball, the equipment for which is issued in a special paragraph completely free of charge.

Beach

The hotel is located on the second coastline, but nevertheless has its own beach on the coast, which is completely covered with fine pebbles and soft sand. The guests who live in the Grand Atilla Hotel 4 * with children often recommend visiting this beach with them, as it is perfectly arranged for kids to relax - the descent into the water is gradual. You can get here in just a minute, which also pleases vacationers who do not like to travel long distances, especially in tropical climates.

Several dressing rooms and showers are evenly spaced throughout the area, and there are also public toilets. If desired, vacationers can rent a sunbed and a beach umbrella - they are issued at a special point. Towels and mattresses here do not provide what many guests of Grand Atilla Hotel 4 * (Turkey) consider a big minus.

Aquazone

In the open area there is an aquazone, incredibly popular among travelers who relax at the Grand Atilla Hotel. It consists of two parts - a sun terrace and a pool.

The pool provides daily water treatment, so it is crystal clear. There is no automatic heating in the niche, however, this process is perfectly carried out under the influence of sunlight. Near the large pool there is a niche designed for bathing small guests - it is designed taking into account all safety requirements.

There is a well-equipped terrace around the pool. It presents several dozens of sun loungers and beach umbrellas, under which tourists can sunbathe without being exposed to the harmful effects of sunlight.

Reviews

Vacationers at the hotel often recommend it for vacations to all their friends and acquaintances, which is not always associated with the low cost of the permit. Often they accompany their advice with stories about a decent level of service and the beauty of nature. Travelers very often talk about the fact that the local apartments have a great decoration, and they also maintain cleanliness very actively: 4 times a week, rooms are cleaned and towels are replaced twice. If you want to visit the restaurant, vacationers recommend paying attention to dishes prepared with seafood and meat - according to them, they have the most striking and unusual taste.



If you have any questions, you should immediately contact the administrator, who is on duty around the clock at the reception. Only friendly and polite people work there, as evidenced by numerous reviews, who will always try to solve any guest's problems associated with living. Many comments contain information that some hotel staff are fluent in Russian.

As for the time chosen for the rest, most tourists recommend going to Alanya in September - the weather this month is very mild and favorable for a good time and, of course, visiting interesting places.
