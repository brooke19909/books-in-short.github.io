"Tau spa" (Almaty): description, reviews

Location

Tau spa (Almaty) is located on the territory of the Tau Dastarkhan tourist complex, located in the Almaarasan gorge of unusual beauty, at the foot of the Zailiysky Alatau (a ridge in the northwest of the Tien Shan mountain system). Therefore, now there is no need to travel thousands of kilometers to relax and unwind, a European-level spa complex is located very close to Almaty. Only a quarter of an hour by car separates residents and visitors from the meeting with the health complex of baths and saunas.





Having open and closed territories, the Tau Spa (Almaty) in the summer has the opportunity to host up to 500 vacationers, and in the winter - only 250 people. You can come here for one day or several, staying at a hotel.

Services

From ten in the morning until two in the morning every day at any time of the year, the guests of Tau Spa (Almaty) are waiting for:

  • two adult 25-meter swimming pools (1.3-2.5 meters deep);
  • water slides and attractions for visitors of different age categories;
  • two pools for children (75 centimeters deep);
  • indoor pools filled with water from the Mediterranean and the Dead Seas;
  • comfortable saunas and baths in a modern design (Russian with a font, Turkish, Finnish, Japanese, Alpine and Eastern);
  • solarium;
  • a gym with the latest sports equipment for people of various physical fitness;
  • several hot tubs;
  • restaurants with a wide range of dishes of national cuisine.

Visitors reviews

The guests of the resort respond positively to the time spent on vacation in the Tau Spa (Almaty). They are happy to post photos and comments on them on social networks.





I liked almost everything: fresh air and chic nature, excellent clean baths and a variety of pools, responsible lifeguards and friendly administrators. The only criticism expressed by many guests relates to the increased cost of food in the center.

Tau spa (Almaty) is an opportunity not only to spend time actively, but also to make new acquaintances, recharge with energy and energy for a long time. The conditions created for recreation here are able to satisfy even the most rigorous and demanding customers.
