Holiday Village "Lebyazhye" (Sysert): description and how to get there

Modern people always seek a break from the frantic urban rhythm in the lap of nature. This allows you to improve your health, as well as usefully spend your vacation. Therefore, many plan their trips in advance to take a good place. On the recreation center "Lebyazhye" (Sysert), guests are waited by excellent conditions and good service. The place is known for its beautiful nature and fresh air. In addition, near the base is Sysert pond.

general information

The recreation center "Lebyazhye" (Sysert) is well known to many tourists, as it is located directly in a pine forest. Thanks to this, the air here is clean and very healthy. To stay guests are offered 10 houses made of wood. Each dwelling is designed for six people, so it is suitable for families or groups.

The house has three bedrooms, a kitchen, a bathroom, a relaxation room, a corridor and a terrace. For guests prepared all the necessary furniture and appliances. There are barbecue facilities near the houses. Meals can also occur in the cafe. Almost all reviews about the Lebyazhye recreation center (Sysert) are positive. Guests celebrate excellent service, attentive workers, good conditions and beautiful nature.

How to get to the base

There are several ways to get to the resting place. Tourists can take the Southern Bus Station (Yekaterinburg) to any bus that travels to the city of Sysert. From there you can take a taxi or take a trip by public transport.

You can come to the Lebyazhye recreation center (Sysert) by your own car. To do this, you need to leave Yekaterinburg and go along the Chelyabinsk tract. The path will lie through the village of Kashino and the city of Sysert. From the last point it is worth driving to the Sysert reservoir. A guide for travelers will be the city beach and the base called Spartak.

Additional features

The recreation center "Lebyazhye" (Sysert) offers its guests a lot of interesting options for their relaxation. Lovers of an active lifestyle can play volleyball or soccer. Near the lake there is a beach for relaxation. There are playgrounds for the youngest guests, and a swing near the houses.

Guests often decide to try extreme sports. There is a rope town here. And especially courageous guests find a fascinating bungee above the river. In the evening, many visitors relax in a real Russian bath with oak and birch brooms. Since there is a cafe on the territory, it regularly hosts weddings and anniversaries. For guests horseback riding, excursions to the museum and Sysertsky Park of Culture and Leisure.
