Montenegro Beach 4 * (Montenegro / Budvanska Riviera): photos and reviews of tourists

Montenegro is a very small state located on the Adriatic coast of the Balkan Peninsula. On its entire territory, half as many people live than in the same Rostov-on-Don. However, among tourists, holidays in Montenegro are quite popular. Yes, and good hotels are enough. And one of the most popular is Montenegro Beach 4 *.





general information

This hotel complex is located in a resort town with a population of several hundred people. And it is called Becici. This is three kilometers from the much larger city of Budva, which is the center of the eponymous municipal district. And about 30 km from Tivat airport.

Many local hotels were built a long time ago. Montenegro Beach 4 * is no exception. This hotel is 56 years old. But in 2005, major repairs and reconstruction were carried out, so that everything looks new, fresh and modern. And in 2006, the hotel received the prestigious Wild Beauty Award in the nomination ‚ÄúThe Best Hotel in Montenegro‚Äù.

Interestingly, the town of Becici in 1935 took the Grand Prix in Paris as the owner of the most beautiful beach in Europe.

There is a bus stop just around the corner. By shuttle you can get to the pier and the citadel of Budva, the TQ Plaza complex, the church of St. John. Another of the bright sights can be noted with attention to Skadar Lake and Lovcen National Park. And of course, the most important thing is the sea. From the hotel to the private sand and pebble beach is literally within easy reach. In addition, there are sun loungers and umbrellas.





Service

Montenegro Beach 4 * is a hotel with a considerable list of services. It features high-speed free Wi-Fi and a private car park. In the lobby, guests are greeted by a 24/7 reception, a tour desk, an ATM and a left-luggage office.

For the little ones there is a games room with children's TV channels, books, films, cartoons and music, a playground, board games. A babysitting service is also available, which can look after the child if guests want to be alone.

There is also a laundry room with dry cleaning, where guests are also ironed and shoes are polished. Of the general services, it is worth noting the opportunity to order a transfer, delivery of drinks and food to your room or packed lunches on the road. If you need personal transport, you can contact the car rental point available at Montenegro Beach 4 *. The hotel also has a beauty salon with a hairdresser, a souvenir stall, several shops and rooms with amenities suitable for the needs of people with disabilities. In addition, the area is equipped with ramps, and there are elevators for access to the upper floors.

And also pleased with the level of staff training. The people working here speak Serbian, German, English, French, Croatian, Italian and Russian. So none of the tourists should have problems with the language barrier.

Many people staying at the Montenegro Beach Resort 4 * (Montenegro) spend most of their time on the beach. But the hotel has something to keep busy. There is a gym, a beauty salon, a Turkish bath (hammam), two saunas, two Jacuzzis, and three more hydromassage showers and an indoor heated pool. Open also available. Nutrition

Montenegro Beach 4 * (Montenegro, Becici) has a service system that all tourists love, without exception. This, of course, is all inclusive. The system includes breakfast, as well as lunches and dinners according to the buffet system. During the day and evening meals, guests are served local beer, water, juices, as well as wines (white and red).

From 16:30 to 18:30, guests can enjoy snacks and drinks at the Stars Club. It offers delicious sweets and pastries.

For guests who are vacationing on an all-inclusive basis, a 20 percent discount is available on drinks offered in other restaurants and bars of the hotel complex. Guests who pay all inclusive are given bracelets upon arrival - it is very important to wear them constantly.

Guests on Nutrition

As people who were in the Montenegro Beach 4 * restaurants (Montenegro) assure, the choice is huge. Everything is tasty and of high quality. Residence

In Montenegro Beach 4 * (Becici) only 172 rooms. Of which 168 are Standards.

Their area is 22 square meters. m. Inside, in brightly decorated rooms, there are two 1-bed beds, densely pushed to each other. There is a furnished balcony, cable TV, and a minibar. Each guest is also given a bathrobe and hygiene kits. The bathroom with shower and toilet has a hairdryer. In addition to the above, the room has a safe, air conditioning (with heating function) and a dressing room.

The cost of such apartments for two is less than 30,000 rubles per week. But it is with breakfast. With the all-inclusive service it will be more expensive (you need to find out the price individually).

The remaining four options in Montenegro Beach Resort 4 * (Becici) are called apartments. Their area is 52 square meters. m. And the main "highlight" is that the entire living space is divided into two rooms. The cost of apartments is also specified individually.





Reviews

Finally, you should turn to the comments made by tourists who stayed at the Montenegro Beach Resort 4 * (Montenegro, Becici). In general, the reviews are positive. Guests pay special attention to the rooms in the hotel - comfortable furniture, thoughtful interior, a normal sized shower, and the view from the window. It is best to take apartments on the upper floors. From there, a more impressive landscape opens up.

The staff is polite and attentive. If you ask for something, they will never refuse. Lunch boxes on the road are prepared very tasty, housekeeping is organized daily (as well as towel replacement), populated without delay. Check-in starts at 14:00, but if there are free apartments paid by guests, they will not be forced to wait (in case of early arrival). Transfer also flawlessly. But if you need it, then to clarify all the details you need to contact the reception in advance.

And the people who stayed at the Hotel Montenegro Beach 4 * are satisfied with the atmosphere. And this applies not only to hotels, but to Becici as a whole. You can safely walk in the evenings, drop by cozy cafes, souvenir shops and bars. Becici is very safe. You can safely walk the streets and swim even in the dead of night.

Many people like this hotel. And guests who come with children, too. Kids Club - beyond praise. There, children are engaged in animator girls who in every way entertain the kids. They play with them, draw, sculpt, dance. In addition, the club itself is well equipped - it has everything that kids need. From swings and playhouses to designers and dolls. The club operates from 9:00 to 23:00, which also can not but rejoice.

In general, this hotel is ideal for people who dream of a relatively budget holiday in Montenegro. And numerous reviews are a direct confirmation of this. No wonder many people even come back here.
