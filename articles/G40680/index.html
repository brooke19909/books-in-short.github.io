Treatment and rest

The first health resorts appeared in Crimea in the 19th century. It was treated for tuberculosis and pulmonary diseases. In Soviet times, a whole network of sanatoriums appeared, in which they worked with such serious diseases as neurocircular dystonia, impaired functioning of the vessels of the brain and musculoskeletal system. Nowadays, clinics operate year-round and accept patients of any age for treatment. Therefore, children suffering from nervous diseases and cerebral palsy are specially brought here. Ordinary citizens are attracted by the healing air of the peninsula, which, according to scientists, is saturated with ions and volatile. Modern hotels offer their guests fashionable SPA treatments aimed at rejuvenating and restoring the body.

Massandra

This small resort village is located so close to Yalta that it is often called a resort suburb. This picturesque place attracted people from ancient times and was first inhabited by the ancient Greeks.
