Hospitable Turkey. Icmeler - a quiet and picturesque resort

With picturesque landscapes, favorable climatic conditions, a huge variety of entertainment, all-inclusive hotels, Turkey lures tourists into its networks. Icmeler is one of the best developing resorts in the country. More recently, he was part of the popular and wealthy Marmaris, but thanks to the skillful planning of the city authorities he managed to get out of custody and start an independent life. It is located in a beautiful and quiet bay, 7 km from a more famous competitor. The name of the city in translation means "lost paradise", which is fully true. Lush tropical vegetation, a blue lagoon near the mountains and golden beaches is surrounded by Icmeler.



Turkey receives mostly positive reviews from tourists, and this is not surprising, because most of the country's resorts meet the highest requirements and can boast of developed infrastructure. Icmeler, although it has recently gained independence, is already confidently moving forward and occupying its niche in the tourism business. This city is much better than many "promoted" resorts, because the prices for accommodation here are lower, and the conditions are no worse.

With its pricing policy, Turkey stands out against the background of other resort countries. Icmeler is notable for the low cost of land, so businessmen did not stint and bought large territories for hotels. The buildings themselves were built later than in other Turkish cities, so they are more modern and meet high requirements. But the main advantage of the resort is not chic and comfortable hotels, but the picturesque landscapes of the mountains, sandy beaches, the azure sea, exotic vegetation.

Resorts with different capabilities and characters have Turkey. Icmeler belongs to the quiet and calm towns, where everything is saturated with the atmosphere of harmony and comfort. It is ideal for couples with children, the elderly and those who want to be alone with nature, away from the hustle and bustle of large cities. In Icmeler, young people can also relax, there are plenty of entertainment venues, and if you want more drive, you should visit the noisy and crowded Marmaris, located only 7 km away.

Tourists who want to visit an exotic country, have a good rest and at the same time save money, get tours to Icmeler. Turkey provides all conditions for beach and outdoor activities. The sea is clear, emerald in color and absolutely transparent; its bottom is visible several meters in depth. In Icmeler, everyone can dive, and there are all conditions for water skiing and windsurfing. The resort is suitable even for young children, since the descent into the water is gentle, and the beaches are sand and pebble.



Turkey has many interesting architectural, cultural and historical sights. Icmeler is located near Marmaris, which has survived several eras and has preserved ancient monuments to this day. Here you can look at Bedesten (covered market), an ancient fortress. Also nearby is the island of Cleopatra. Rest in a calm and quiet Icmeler will appeal to absolutely everyone, and so as not to be bored, you can visit the nearest famous resorts.
