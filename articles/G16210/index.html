"Academician" (LCD), the city of Mytishchi: description, layout, developer and reviews

All Muscovites dream of their own apartment. New buildings of the capital for many - impermissible luxury. That is why modern residential complexes near Moscow are very popular. LCD "Academic" - a kind of attraction Mytishchi. All residents of the city are aware of the project, where all the necessary conditions for a comfortable and safe life are created. Currently, the first stage project has been successfully implemented. Its success and a positive assessment of experts became an incentive for the design of houses of the second and third stage. As part of this material, we will evaluate the Akademik residential complex from the developer DOM-Spetsstroy.





about the project

Mytishchi is a city near Moscow region. Until recently, it did not attract attention, since it did not have all the infrastructure facilities. But today the situation has changed radically: modern residential complexes began to be actively built here. The city has enough parks, squares, places for walking and recreation.

LCD "Academic" (Mytishchi) - one of the best projects. To date, a first-stage house has been commissioned, represented by a two-section 25-story house. LCD "Akademik-3" in Mytishchi has also been commissioned and populated. Residents leave their feedback, comment on their impressions of the project and apartments. All this allows for a more sober approach to buying an apartment in the Akademik-2 residential complex (Mytishchi). House commissioning will take place in the third quarter of 2017. The first two houses were commissioned with a slight delay, but this time the developer took into account everything, made adjustments to the construction, so no delays are expected.





Construction Features

For the construction of houses, a monolithic-brick technology was chosen, which allows the construction of strong and reliable structures. The first floors of the houses are reserved for commercial real estate. There will be office premises, shops, cafes, service industry organizations. Elevators in the porches of European production, provide high speed and quiet operation. In addition, the entrances are equipped with a video surveillance system and fire alarms. The project provides for the improvement of the complex: the organization of lawns and flower beds, the construction of modern playgrounds and sports grounds.

Location

The developer has chosen a great place to implement his project. The houses are located just 5 km from the MKAD in the direction of the Yaroslavl highway. The houses are surrounded by all necessary infrastructure. The developer has tried to preserve the green spaces during construction to the maximum to provide each inhabitant with excellent ecology.





Ecology

Near the houses lies the busy Yaroslavl highway, spoiling the ecological picture. But in this case, this drawback is compensated by a forest belt adjacent to the complexes. It has already become a favorite place for walks in the fresh air of residents of commissioned houses.

Transport accessibility

Residents of the first buildings already commissioned are delighted with the location of their home. You can get to the capital in just 15-20 minutes, both by personal and public transport. The direction of the Yaroslavl highway is busy, traffic jams and congestion are quite common. But due to the reconstruction of the highway in the near future it will be possible to establish a road situation. Near the complex there is a railway station, from where you can get to the Yaroslavl station. Well, buses and minibuses will take you to the nearest metro station in 15-20 minutes.





Infrastructure

LCD "Academic" - an excellent choice for those who are not ready to say goodbye to the developed metropolitan infrastructure. In walking distance from the complex are kindergartens, schools, shopping and entertainment centers, pharmacies, clinics.

Near each house there is an excellent playground: children frolic on it from morning to night. And security is ensured by round-the-clock protection of the territory and equipping it with a video surveillance system. Closed yards exclude through traffic.

For car owners, the developer has provided an underground heated parking. It will become a worthy alternative to individual garages and provide transport with the necessary level of security.

Apartments, layouts

A variety of planning solutions that meet all the requirements of modern residents is what the Academician residential complex can offer. Customers are offered one, two and three bedroom apartments to choose from. By combining the bathroom and toilet in one-room apartments, it was possible to provide residents with a spacious kitchen and bedroom. Non-standard two-room apartments are an excellent solution for young families with children. These apartments are in special demand. Buyers are attracted by a successful layout: two bathrooms, a wardrobe, isolated rooms, two balconies. Well, three-room apartments of 85 square meters have become an excellent option for large families.





Apartments in the Akademik residential complex are rented out without finishing, but the developer offers a limited number of apartments with a preliminary and fine finish. Today, there is still the opportunity to purchase such an apartment. Happy owners of the finished apartment note that all the work was done efficiently, it remains to have furniture. The proximity to the Yaroslavl highway is compensated by high-quality glazing.

Price policy

The cost per square meter in the residential complex "Academic" starts from 60 000 rubles. This is an advantageous offer for an apartment located just 5 km from the Moscow Ring Road, in an area with developed infrastructure. The developer offers favorable conditions for mortgage lending. The developer offers a discount of up to 8% on the purchase of apartments in a rented house.

To summarize

If you are looking for an apartment near Moscow, but are limited in funds, the Akademik residential complex will be a great option for you . Here all the necessary conditions for the most comfortable life are created: well-developed infrastructure, spacious apartments of improved layout, good ecology. Residents of the first commissioned facilities are still under a pleasant impression. The developer offers a profitable mortgage lending program, as well as the ability to track every stage of home construction. We recommend to take a closer look at the complex: for sure you will find a dream apartment here.
