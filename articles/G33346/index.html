How to get from Evpatoria to Simferopol

All roads to any part of Crimea go from Simferopol. From here begins most of the routes on the peninsula. "Evpatoria - Simferopol" continues to be the most popular destination of the Crimea. Currently, many Russians come here. This is due to the fact that recently Crimea became part of Russia, and now it is not necessary to cross the border and pass customs control.





Evpatoria - health resort of Crimea

In the west of the Crimean peninsula, the ancient city of Yevpatoria is located. Simferopol is located at a distance of 71 km from the highway. The Crimean city of Yevpatoriya is a famous resort famous for its unique healing mud and brine. Among them are the mud of the famous Moinaki Lake, which come from all over the country for treatment. Also here are sources of Yevpatoriya mineral water. This is a unique sodium chloride water of a slightly alkaline reaction. Since Soviet times, there are many sanatoriums in which more than one generation of the country's inhabitants has been treated.

People come here not only for treatment, but also just relax, sunbathe and swim in the sea. It is in the Evpatoria region the quietest on the coast. Evpatoria beaches gained great popularity. How to get to Evpatoria

It is not surprising that many wish to visit the city. Evpatoria, with a population of about 106 thousand people, annually receives up to 900 thousand vacationers. But this is not the limit. By the number of health resorts, hotels, tourist centers, boarding houses, mini-hotels and private housing, it can take up to three million people for treatment and rest.

Now getting to the Crimea has become more difficult. The main flow of tourists is moving from Kerch, where there is a ferry crossing connecting the peninsula with the territory of Russia. The whole automobile and railway stream goes along it. Huge traffic jams scare people. Nevertheless, the number of holidaymakers is growing. Most of the travelers arrive at Simferopol airport. There are many ways to get to Yevpatoriya, so the question of how to get from Simferopol to Yevpatoriya is relevant for those who want to visit the city for the first time.

Transport communication in the Crimea has been debugged since Soviet times. To get anywhere in the peninsula is not difficult. Good roads will allow you to get to Yevpatoriya by any type of road transport. Buses and minibuses come here. There is a railway to Yevpatoria, which connects it with Simferopol and other cities of the peninsula. It can be reached by train. The schedule "Evpatoria - Simferopol" for buses and trains is convenient, you can leave for the city at almost any time of the day.





Bus service

In case of arrival to Simferopol by train, you need to go to the Kurortnaya bus station, which is located next to the railway station. Daily shuttles and small buses leave from it, following to Evpatoria. If you want to know how much to go from Simferopol to Yevpatoriya, then we can say for sure: it will depend on the flight. Some buses overcome the path in 1 hour 20 minutes; some take a different route, and the travel time will be about 2 hours.

If you flew to Simferopol by plane, you must remember that there are buses near the airport that go along the route "Simferopol Airport - Yevpatoriya." Travel time is from 1 hour to 1 hour 20 minutes.

The cost of tickets is very affordable. From the bus station the Resort ticket costs 114 rubles, from the airport to Yevpatoriya the ticket costs 98 rubles. Tickets can be bought at the box office or directly from drivers. You can also buy tickets in advance at the Gosavtobus.ru website.





Getting on the train

You can get to Evpatoria from Simferopol by train. From the railway station, many flights depart towards the city. They come to the station Evpatoria-Resort. The return flights Evpatoria - Simferopol are also carried out from here. Travel time will be about two hours. The fare in the train is 110 rubles.

From the railway station of Yevpatoria there are buses, minibuses and regular taxis. You can get to the city without difficulty.

Get to Evpatoria by taxi

The question of how to get to Yevpatoriya is not a matter of principle. Between the two cities runs the P-25 highway. For a good motorist, a journey of 72 kilometers along a good road does not seem burdensome. The trip lasts about an hour.
