The popularity of the resorts of the Caspian Sea is due not only to a comfortable and attractive coastline, but also to the convenient location of the reservoir. Holidays on the Caspian Sea (only positive reviews about it) attract several thousand people from different countries annually. It is worth noting that most of the tourists are completely satisfied with the living conditions, as well as the reservoir itself. This is due to the beauty of the landscapes, excellent weather conditions, clear water and beautiful beaches.

Rest in Aktau (Kazakhstan)

Fans of intense relaxation should pay attention to the city of Aktau. That is what tourists advise. They convince that rest in the Caspian Sea will be the most complete in this region. Reviews about him are more than favorable. Praise hospitality, coziness and comfort. This is the largest resort on the shores of the Caspian Sea in this country. A large number of low-cost hotels, as well as a developed cultural industry will make the vacation vivid and memorable. The only drawback that tourists note in their reviews is the rocky beach. In Aktau district, small resort villages are equipped. Significant benefits include a relaxed atmosphere, clean air and a sandy beach.

Caspian coast in Astrakhan

If a traveler is looking for an inexpensive and fun way to spend his summer vacation, then Astrakhan will be an ideal option. Why? There are many benefits. The main ones: location, transport, infrastructure. Reviews of vacationers give reason to believe that a vacation will really cost much less than in other territories.

So, in more detail about the city of Astrakhan. Rest on the Caspian Sea here will be excellent. This is due to the fact that this resort of Russia boasts a unique match of attractive wildlife and modern arrangement of beaches. Given the large number of vacationers annually, the tourism industry is well developed in Astrakhan, which offers vacationers a wide range of beach activities, nightly entertainment, and excursions to places of interest.

The city cannot boast of a large number of hotels for vacationers, but recreation centers are closely equipped along the coast. Some are equipped with modern multi-story buildings, while others are a myriad of private, comfortable houses. Among the most attractive recreation centers should be attributed such as the "Golden Lotus", "Lighthouse" and "Berezhok". It is in these institutions that a 100% vacation on the Caspian Sea will take place. Reviews of people confirm this. You can familiarize yourself with the service and book rooms in advance by phone.



The coastline of the sea in Dagestan

Dagestan boasts a unique combination of relief and nature features, where high mountain cliffs almost reach the waters of the Caspian Sea. This is the best option for those who want to combine an active mountain vacation and a relaxing lying on a sandy beach. Tourists of Dagestan may seem quite interesting. Holidays on the Caspian Sea in this area will really be unforgettable!

Derbent is the most favorable resort city, which will provide tourists with a rich and comfortable stay. Located at the very edge of the warm waves of the Caspian Sea, the settlement is also a national cultural monument, the walls of which preserved medieval forts, old defensive structures, as well as vivid examples of ethnic architecture. Group tours organized in the resort will not only allow you to get acquainted with the features of the state, but also get vivid photographs for a family album.
