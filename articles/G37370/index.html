Hotels in Vnukovo with transfer

Vnukovo district is included in the Western district of the capital. The metro does not go here. But this does not prevent residents and guests of the city from quickly moving with buses and express trains. This place is the oldest international airport. It was erected in the highest part of Moscow. There is also a sports club, a cultural center, playgrounds and stadiums, a center for social assistance to children and family. In this article we will look at the most popular hotels in Vnukovo.



Hotel "Vnukovo-Kartmazovo"

Considering hotels near Vnukovo, you should immediately highlight this hotel. It is 4 km from Moscow, and 7 km from Vnukovo Airport. For the convenience of guests there is a restaurant and a sauna.

The elegant rooms are decorated in a traditional style with warm, soft colors. All rooms have plasma-screen televisions, bathrooms, and minibars.

The Kartmazovo Hotel (Vnukovo) has an open 24-hour restaurant of the same name serving Caucasian, European and domestic cuisine.

Near the hotel there is a bus stop. Guests can reach Yugo-Zapadnaya Station in just 10 minutes. A shuttle service is available to / from Vnukovo, Domodedovo and Sheremetyevo airports.

Hotel Antis House Uninn

A popular three-star hotel located in Vnukovo. It is only 3 km from the city center.



Wi-Fi access is available on site. If you arrived here by car, there is parking available. And if we consider hotels in Vnukovo with a transfer, then here it is also provided. This place has a 24-hour reception.

You can use a service such as ordering breakfast, lunch or dinner in your room. Laundry facilities are open for guests. At the same time, reception staff will book tickets for cultural events or transport for you. Non-smoking throughout.

Guest house "Maryino"

Here the cafe offers a wide selection of dishes of Russian and European cuisine.

A massage session will help you gain strength and also relax after a difficult day. A barbecue area has been created in the surrounding area.

Here guests can use the free Internet, which will allow you to always be up to date with the latest news. Airport transfers can be booked at an additional cost.

Here, guests can also dance, sing karaoke, smoke a hookah. The cafe is perfect for business meetings, as it has access to free Wi-Fi. In summer, guests can rent barbecue facilities and cook meat in a specially equipped outdoor area.

The facility is equipped with a wellness center, in which a full relaxing rest is possible. This hotel in Vnukovo offers a real Russian bathhouse, a pool with warm water and a font with cold water. Guests can also relax in the jacuzzi. For guests who want to stay fit even on vacation, a fitness center with exercise equipment is suitable.

There is free Wi-Fi here, which means you can always be in touch with your home. For tourists who travel by car, a secure free parking is provided.

The 17 rooms at this hotel are decorated in warm colors and include LCD TVs. In each room, the bathroom is equipped with high-quality fixtures.
