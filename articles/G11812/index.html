How to put money on WebMoney: several ways

A great variety of goods and services on the Web attract users. But not every one of them has electronic cash, the calculations of which can be done using payment systems. There are quite a few of them on the Internet, but they are reliable, proven ones with a long service life. One of them is WebMoney. Even with high percentages of commissions of this settlement system, it remains the most popular among netizens, who will definitely at some point wonder how to put money into WebMoney. There are several ways to replenish an electronic wallet.

How to put money on WebMoney: the main ways

You can list the equivalents of dollars, rubles or another currency in several ways. Each of them differs in the size of the percentage of commission and the time it takes to complete the operation. In addition to these differences, there are some restrictions for each method of crediting funds to an electronic wallet account. For example, put money from the phone. In this case, the WebMoney wallet can only be replenished using the most popular mobile operators in Russia: Mobile TeleSystems (MTS), Beeline, Megafon and Baykalvestkom.





You can also transfer money using a bank card of any commercial bank, payment cards of the system itself, using QIWI terminals or retail cash desks, or a postal order.

How to put money on WebMoney: the fastest way

Not so long ago, a mobile phone account could be replenished using payment cards of a certain face value. Their cost ranged from 50 to 1000 rubles. WebMoney has applied the same practice to its wallet recharge system. Payment cards are purchased not only offline, but also on some specialized sites of dealers.

For this option, replenishment of an electronic wallet requires a special program. It is called WM Keeper Classic. Download it offers the official website of the system. And only after installing WM Keeper Classic the user can deposit money into the account. WebMoney distributes the program for free.

Further actions are elementary. Perhaps this is the easiest way to put money on WebMoney. But it is also the most expensive. The fact is that the commission of the system when replenishing the account with payment cards is up to 10% of the nominal value.

Replenishment of an electronic wallet from a mobile phone account

For this option of transferring funds, there is another limitation, in addition to mobile operators. From the accounts of "MTS", "Beeline", "Megafon" and "Baykalvestkom" you can put money on "WebMoney" only in ruble terms or in hryvnias. The method itself is also uncomplicated, as is the option with WebMoney payment cards.

Note that there is no such discrimination if you need to put money on your phone. Replenishment of WebMoney wallet through cash desks of retail chains

This option is convenient if at some point there is no Internet access. You can replenish your account at some exchange offices, post offices, communication stores.

Also, a fairly large number of entrepreneurs who have system certificates have the right to transfer funds to WebMoney clients. That is, a person who urgently needs to top up an e-wallet account does not need to panic to look for the nearest post office. It is enough in any large shopping and entertainment center to pay attention to the shop windows.





Outlet owners place signs with the WebMoney logo. This means that through the cashier of this store you can conduct transactions with electronic cash.
