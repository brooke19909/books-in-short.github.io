Hotel "Dolphin", Sochi. Hotel Dolphin: description, address, reviews

Hotels in the Krasnodar Territory attract tourists at any time of the year, but the largest number of holidaymakers traditionally come here in the summer. At this time, prices at resorts are increasing sharply, and finding housing suitable for cost and comfort is becoming increasingly difficult.

Hotel Dolphin (Sochi) offers its guests excellent service for little money. And regularly appearing special offers and promotions not only allow you to enjoy all the advantages of the hotel, but also save a lot.

About hotel

The Dolphin Resort Hotel is an elegant hotel surrounded by the picturesque nature of the city of Sochi on the Black Sea coast of Russia. You can come here to rest at any time of the year. Nearby there is a beach, a pedestrian promenade and a wonderful park, which guests of the hotel visit for free. And after dark, many bars and restaurants, discos and nightclubs in the neighborhood await their visitors.





The small remoteness of the hotel from the well-known Sochi attractions provides a unique opportunity to visit them without long transport trips. On foot you can walk to the famous Kurortny Prospekt, the Sochi Arboretum, the circus and the Winter Theater.

Couples with children or businessmen on a business trip will find a suitable room for themselves at the Hotel Dolphin (Sochi). The hotel will suit everyone who needs a good comfortable vacation by the sea.

Where is?

The building of the Dolphin Hotel is located at Sochi, Chernomorskaya, 19. It is only a few meters from the sea, two kilometers from the seaport and five kilometers from Sochi Central Station. The nearest airport of the same name is 25 kilometers from the hotel, but it can be easily overcome in 30-40 minutes by a comfortable bus, a modern Lastochka electric train or car.

Number of rooms

The Dolphin (Sochi) hotel, which received the title of one of the most "luxury" hotels in its category in the central district of Sochi, has only 29 spacious comfortable rooms decorated in Biedermeier art style. In its interiors beauty and harmony reign. And from the windows of each room a bewitching view of the infinitely blue sea or the rich vegetation of the subtropical climate opens.





The hotel has 3 apartments (84 sq. M.), 2 suites (70 sq. M.), 13 junior suites (50 sq. M.) And 11 standard rooms (36 sq. M.).

All rooms are equipped with a small refrigerator, wardrobe, upholstered furniture, comfortable beds, electric kettle and dishes, air conditioning, telephone, wireless internet, flat-screen TV and personal safe. Each room has a bathroom with a large bathtub, washbasin, toilet and even a bidet. Also at the guests' disposal are a hairdryer and hygiene products. Iron and ironing board are available upon request.

Restaurant "Pomegranate"

"Dolphin" (Sochi) the hotel is surrounded by a large number of bright and memorable bars, cafes, restaurants, where you can have a tasty meal and have a great time. However, Dolphin Hotel also has its own restaurant, Granat, whose menu offers dishes of Russian, European and Caucasian cuisines.

Enjoy the culinary masterpieces of chefs helps calm music. And to better reveal the taste of signature dishes, it is recommended to order a glass of good wine from the richest collection of the institution.





Breakfast hours in the restaurant are traditionally set from 8 a.m. to 11 a.m. Breakfast can be complex in Russian, English, French or Italian manner or ordered according to the menu.

Conference Hall "Aquamarine"

For organizing business meetings, the hotel has a magnificent Aquamarine conference room with panoramic windows overlooking the embankment and the sea. It is equipped with air conditioning, a stationary screen and a projector, at the same time it can accommodate 50 people.





During long negotiations, a break for a coffee break or buffet is possible.

Pool, beach, park and other features

Having settled at the Dolphin Hotel, vacationers can swim in the sea and sunbathe in comfortable sun loungers under awnings in the private coastal zone or, without leaving the hotel, splash around and dive in the open sea pool.

After solar and water procedures, you can go to the park to meet amazing nature and art objects, fight an opponent on the tennis court, improve physical fitness in the fitness room or take care of your body by contacting the wellness center. In order to treat or prevent certain diseases, you can go through all the necessary procedures.

Additional services

The Dolphin Resort offers a wide range of additional services, including not only the use of the pool and private beach, equipped with everything necessary for a good rest, but also the order of breakfasts in the room, the organization of hotel staff excursions and transfers, the purchase of tickets for entertainment events, bike rental and car rental. For foreign citizens, visa support is provided.





For guests arriving at the Dolphin (Sochi) hotel by private car, free secure parking is offered.

Many hotels in the Krasnodar Territory refuse to accommodate vacationers traveling with their pets. But at the Dolphin Hotel, accommodation in a pet room is practiced, but the service is paid extra.

Positive reviews

Guests of the Krasnodar Territory at any time of the year with great pleasure choose the hotel "Dolphin" (Sochi), reviews of which on tourist portals are mostly positive. Vacationers were not satisfied with their stay at all because they arrived at the legendary Black Sea resort. They were satisfied with the price and quality of the service offered at the hotel:

  1. Design rooms are extremely comfortable, cozy, clean. Even the smallest standard room seems like a small kingdom.
  2. The restaurant's cuisine is just wonderful, the portions are huge and delicious, the interior of the restaurant helps increase appetite.
  3. Wonderful location of the hotel and amazing views.
  4. Great service at the hotel, polite and friendly staff.




Negative moments or things to work on

Despite the mass of positive comments, the Dolphin Hotel has directions that guests would like to improve:

  1. Reduce the waiting time for ordered dishes in the restaurant. Some guests say that they waited 30-40 minutes for breakfast.
  2. Revise the cost of breakfast, perhaps offer cheaper options.
