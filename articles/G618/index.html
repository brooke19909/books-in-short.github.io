One of the most necessary tools during construction or repair is a perforator. With the advent of this device, which is able to withstand high loads, the craftsmen perform the work with ease and comfort. The choice of such devices in the market of building materials is huge today, they are offered by almost every company that specializes in the manufacture of tools. But if you decide to prefer the Dewalt puncher, then you need to understand the models to make the right choice, because you have to spend a considerable amount.

Features of choice





In order to purchase a model that will satisfy your requirements, you need to familiarize yourself with the principle of operation and design features of the equipment. Tools of the mentioned brand can work in one or two modes. In the first case, you can drill, and in the second, you can also use the impact function. You can prefer a model with three modes, a chiselling will be added to the previous two.

Among other things, such devices are divided into light, medium in weight and heavy. The first will have a weight that does not exceed 4 kilograms, but with average weights this parameter varies from 4 to 5 kilograms. As for heavy appliances, they can weigh up to 10 kilograms or more.

Main characteristic - impact energy





If you decide to buy a Dewalt punch, then you must first pay attention to the impact energy, which is one of the main parameters of the described device. It is measured in joules. In this case, it is worth being guided by the mass of the model.

Thus, if the Dewalt puncher belongs to the easy version, then the force of its impact will vary from 1 to 2 joules. As for the middle-class rotary hammers, the mentioned parameter varies from 8 to 15 joules. With an increase in impact energy, you can count on a more impressive hole diameter. When this value is small, the device does not work as efficiently. In this case, the tool will not punch, but drill holes. If you are interested in the Dewalt puncher, then, as experienced craftsmen emphasize, you should pay attention to the frequency of strokes.
