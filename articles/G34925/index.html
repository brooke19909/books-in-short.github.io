Should I go to Tunisia in September? Rest, prices, weather and reviews of tourists

A small African state, located on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, is not in vain that attracts travelers. Firstly, it has a very mild climate and unique landscapes, first-class infrastructure and a rich historical past. And secondly, this is a country where the tourist does not have to leave the space amount, but staying here will be comfortable. And he will remember him for a long time.



When can I go to Tunisia?

Tunisia is an amazing country. It can rightly be called a paradise for tourists, because there is something to see. On its territory at every step there are natural and historical miracles. Museums and mosques, stunning Sahara, the ruins of Carthage are complemented by modern resorts. They can be visited all year round, enjoying the highest service, thalassotherapy and a relaxed atmosphere.

Although it is worth noting that the best time to visit the country for the purpose of relaxation is from June to November. Summer here is the hottest time, both in terms of temperature and the number of vacationers. But in the fall, the weather becomes more comfortable, and prices are slightly reduced.

Tunisia in September



Should I visit an African state in early autumn? The answer will be unequivocal: of course, yes! The weather in September in Tunisia is similar to summer. During the day, the Mediterranean sun warms the air up to +30 ¬∞ , and at night it cools not lower than + 22 ¬∞ . A little cooler in the eponymous capital of Tunisia - +18 ¬∞ C. Sea waves are still warm and comfortable (+24 ¬∞ C). True, closer to the beginning of October it gets cooler due to a light breeze. But the rains are still rare: only one or two times a month, rain can fall, which immediately disappears in dry land.

Mandatory for inspection

A rich and varied excursion program - that's what Tunisia is worth visiting in September. Here is just a small list of places worthy of attention of an experienced traveler, a demanding tourist, and a person who first went abroad.



  1. The ruins of Carthage, the eternal rival of Ancient Rome (located near the capital).
  2. The city of Tunisia (capital of the state). It is worth visiting the mosque of Zitun, the founder of the legendary Carthage, the Bardo National Museum (famous for its collections of Byzantine and ancient Roman mosaics, marble statues and unique jewelry made of precious metals), walk through colorful markets where there is absolutely everything.
  3. Hammamet is proud of the old fortresses (Ribat, Medina), the museum of ancient wedding dresses Dar-Hammamet, the port of Marina, which easily houses seven hundred snow-white beautiful yachts. And in this resort is a fabulous performance called "1001 Nights."
  4. Sousse is a dynamic resort city where fashionable beaches, an eleventh-century minaret tower, a unique museum, modern night clubs, Port El Kantaoui (an entertainment town) are located.
  5. Monastir attracts the ancient city, the Ribat Hartem fortress, the Nador tower, the Bourguiba mosque.




Entertainment

The weather in September in Tunisia favors not only lazy lying on the beaches and sightseeing, but also attending various events. In the middle of the month, the colorful festival "Neptune's Trident" takes place, where local fishermen compete in their skills to handle a spear for fishing. Coral Festival, which is also organized by Tunis in September, attracts lovers of underwater walks and professional divers. Guests of the Kerkenna Islands can take part in hunting octopuses with a jug and tasting dishes prepared from the catch, which are held as part of the Sea Festival. The city of Kairouan invites you to the Cavalry Festival, Grombalia - to the Wine Festival.

This does not end the list of holidays: Tunisia in September marks another important date - the beginning of the independence movement. City streets are filled with people who actively participate in rallies and demonstrations.

Going to Tunisia in September, you can book an unforgettable trip to the Sahara desert (on camels or in jeeps), go on a tour of the caves, against which the shooting of Star Wars (troglodytes dwellings) took place. And also - to follow the traces of the ancient Greeks and Romans, Arab and Byzantine merchants, admire the monuments of the Middle Ages.



Women's holidays in Tunisia in September: thalassotherapy

Many people, mostly women, choose Tunisia because of thalassotherapy. This skill was taught to the locals by the French, who once had colonies here. Due to the high quality, and in addition, rather low prices, the African country has become the center of this type of wellness treatments. Almost every hotel has its own center, where masters will make gommage, wrap, massage, treat with algae, color and sea water. Thalassotherapy is another reason to visit Tunisia in September. Reviews of travelers about the trip are the most enthusiastic: the appearance really improves after a course of procedures, overweight goes away, body and soul are resting.



Travel cost

How much is a ticket? An important question for many people whose vacation falls in the fall. You can safely choose Tunisia in September: prices are slightly lower than in August. This is because the peak of the holiday season has passed, families with school-age children have already rested. If in the last summer month a week of rest for one person will cost an average of $ 600, then in September this amount will decrease by 30-50 US dollars. A stay in a five-star hotel with meals included will cost $ 700 and more. Buying a last-minute tour can cost a penny in general, and a thirty percent discount is provided for early booking vouchers.

You can reduce costs by organizing a trip yourself, without the help of travel agencies. Housing will cost about 10 euros per week for one (if you rent a villa or a cottage for several people). Even cheaper is a room away from the beach and places of entertainment, with amenities in the yard and so on.
