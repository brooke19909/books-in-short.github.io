Hotel Patong Pearl Resortel 3 *, Phuket, Thailand: holidays, reviews

Increasingly, tourists who want to relax in the winter months, choose Thailand to relax. It attracts them not only with the warm sea and clean beaches, but also with the variety of leisure options. Here you can explore historical and religious sights, try original local dishes, or dance in nightclubs. At the same time, the cost of rest is not too high. For example, Patong Pearl Resortel 3 * hotel is suitable for budget accommodation, which we will consider in detail in our article.

Where is the hotel located?

Patong is the most populated resort of Phuket, which is very popular among tourists. This stretch of coast is a rather noisy place, therefore it is not suitable for lovers of a calm and quiet holiday. In Patong there are a lot of budget hotels, including Patong Pearl Resortel 3 *. The resort is suitable for young people who come to the country in search of famous night clubs.

The hotel itself has a good location, it is built in the center of the main tourist street. The city beach is 500 meters from the complex, so you can get to it in five minutes. There are many restaurants, shops and bars nearby. The famous Bangla Road, where the biggest parties in Thailand are held, is located half a kilometer from here.

The nearest airport is in Phuket, and the distance to it is about 40 km. For a fee, the hotel can arrange a shuttle service for arrivals upon request. But, as a rule, many tourists get to the hotel on their own. The average travel time by car or bus will be a little over 1 hour.

Important Hotel Information

Hotel Patong Pearl Resortel 3 * in Phuket was built recently - in 2009. It is a six-story building, decorated in a simple style and decorated with neon signs. He practically does not have his own territory, because it is located on a busy street, where every square meter is important. It is important to note that tourists should not count on new repairs; the last scheduled renovation of all the premises was carried out here back in 2010.





The hotel has Russian-speaking staff, but most of the staff speaks only Thai and English. Registration of guests here begins in the middle of the day - from 14:30. Eviction, on the contrary, is carried out strictly in the morning, so you need to leave your rooms before noon. It is allowed to come to the hotel even with the youngest children, but it is better to leave pets at home.

Tour to Patong Pearl Resortel 3 * can be purchased at any time, because it takes tourists all year round. The cost of living will depend not only on the selected room category and additional services, but also on the season. As a rule, it is better to come here in autumn or winter. In summer, on the contrary, it rains quite often here. The price for one night in a hotel in high season is approximately 3000-4500 rubles for a standard double room.

The Patong Pearl Resortel 3 * complex in Patong is not a crowded place to relax. In total, it offers 72 rooms, most of which are designed for 2-3 guests. They made a classic repair: the walls are painted in pastel colors, ceramic tiles are laid on the floor. There is a minimal set of furniture, which includes beds, bedside tables, armchairs, a desk and wardrobes. Extra beds are available for a fee. All rooms have king-size beds. The windows of the complex overlook the neighboring city buildings.

Arriving at this hotel, you can choose apartments of the following categories:

  • Standard room - double rooms located on floors 1-4 of the hotel. Consist of a bedroom and a bathroom. The total area is 24 square meters. m. Some of the rooms are equipped with a full bath, not a shower.
  • Superior Family Suite - a family room with an open balcony. Accommodates three adults. Area - 28 square meters. m
  • Junior Suite room - superior apartments, which are located on the sixth floor of the hotel. From the previous version they differ only in the presence of their own spa bath.
  • Deluxe Suite room is the only room on the ground floor. It has an open terrace with access to its own small pool. The area is also 28 square meters. m




What facilities are there in the rooms?

Despite the fact that the complex is a three-star hotel, it has a large selection of additional amenities. Most of them are included in the price, so guests can use them for free. In addition, all guest rooms are cleaned daily by maids. Clean linens and towels are provided several times a week.

Checking in the rooms, guests can count on the following amenities:

  • medium-sized plasma TV with a connected set of cable television channels (there are also Russian channels);
  • own air conditioning with remote control;
  • a minibar, the filling of which is provided for a fee (some rooms have full refrigerators);
  • a telephone used to contact administrators, order food and drinks to your room, and also to make international calls;
  • an electric kettle, dishes and all the necessary products for making tea or coffee;
  • towels, a hairdryer and toilet paper in the bathroom (free hygiene products are not provided);
  • Wi-Fi, which can be connected at the reception.




What kind of food is provided at the hotel?

Unlike Turkey or Egypt, in Thailand it is quite difficult to find a hotel that offers all-inclusive meals. Most often, tourists who come here prefer to eat on their own, because local restaurants and cafes offer hearty and original dishes at low prices. The complex described by us was no exception. When buying a ticket, you can pay for breakfast or a full full board. It includes not only a morning meal, but also dinner.





For breakfast, a shared buffet is served for all guests in the main restaurant of the complex. At the same time, guests are not spoiled for exquisite cuisine. Hotel infrastructure

Despite the low category, the Patong Pearl Resortel 3 * hotel (Phuket, Patong) offers its guests high-level service. On its territory there are many infrastructure facilities, the use of which is either included in the cost of living, or is paid separately. So, tourists can rent a car or motorcycle for the rest, filling out a special form at the reception. Here you can connect wireless Internet, exchange currency, order tickets or an excursion program.

For the safety of guests on the territory of the hotel, qualified security guards work around the clock. Laundry services are available for a fee, and a safe and luggage storage are available for hire. For smokers, there are special smoking areas.





Beach vacation

Since people come to Thailand primarily for a beach holiday, Patong Pearl Resortel 3 * focuses on the category of tourists who prefer a quiet pastime in the complex. For lovers of water activities, an outdoor pool is equipped here, and next to it is a spacious sun terrace, where there are numerous sun loungers and sun umbrellas. The pool is open daily, however its schedule should be checked with the administrator.





However, the hotel has no private beach area. The city beach is located half a kilometer from the complex, and tourists get to it, as a rule, on foot. The road takes only 5-10 minutes, so a transfer to the coast is not provided. On the beach, tourists can rent sun loungers and umbrellas, as well as buy a ride on a pleasure boat or visit the water entertainment center. All services here are provided only for a fee.

What entertainment program does the hotel offer?

Judging by the reviews of the Patong Pearl Resortel 3 * hotel, there are not so many entertainment options here. Coming here, it is worth knowing that there is no animation program here, so most of the tourists independently organize their vacation outside the complex. However, traditionally, the hotel has a spa that offers a lot of wellness treatments, including the famous Thai massage.

Conditions for staying with small children

The Patong Pearl Resortel 3 * complex is suitable for unpretentious tourists with children, as there are quite few conditions for their relaxation. All children under the age of 2 years stay free of charge. Cots in the room can be obtained by leaving a preliminary request when booking a ticket. At the same time, they may refuse you if they are not available.

Therefore, they remain satisfied with the hotel accommodation, despite the presence of significant shortcomings. But first, we list the advantages that guests highlight in their reviews:
