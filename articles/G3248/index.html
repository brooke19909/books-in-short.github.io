Oysters navigators: an overview of the best models

In 2006, the Oysters company appeared on the automotive electronics markets, the first devices of which stood out compared to analogues of other brands and brands.

Navigators Oysters

From the first days of its appearance, car navigators manufactured by Oysters have been distinguished by a combination of attractive appearance, high quality, wide functionality and modern materials. The creation of new devices includes the use of innovative developments and achievements in the field of automotive and automotive technology, thanks to which devices of this brand were able to conquer the world market.

Product range

The first device combining a 3G modem with the ability to use high-speed Internet, appeared in 2010 thanks to the efforts of Oysters developers. The first serial production of navigators was launched in 2012 - the models were equipped with the Android operating system and supported the GLONASS positioning system.





The use of new technologies and unusual solutions allowed Oysters navigators to be in the TOP-10 in terms of sales - the company's products took eighth place in 2011. Given the specifics of the domestic market, such results were very good in comparison with other companies involved in the production of similar equipment. Oysters navigators, like other devices manufactured under this brand, are popular and in demand among consumers. They can be found in almost all retail chains that sell electronics.

Navigators Oysters Chrom

The Oysters product line includes various devices that solve various navigation problems. Given the diverse technologies and modern level of development, positioning devices are increasingly reminiscent of tablet computers, as they have equally wide functionality. Navigators Oysters Chrom 3G have become one of the most extensive lines. The very first model in it was the Oysters Chrom 1500.

Technical Parameters of Oysters Chrom 1500

The capabilities and cost of the device make it an ideal representative of the budget class. However, with its main task - positioning - the gadget copes surprisingly well. The hardware component of the navigator is the Centrality Atlas V processor with 64 MB RAM and a clock speed of 500 MHz. The Oysters 3G navigator is capable of quickly processing data and is notable for good performance, but it is not able to properly perform two processes at once (for example, play music and position) due to the small RAM resource. The fairly popular SIRF Atlas V is installed in the navigator as a GPS receiver. The component is highly reliable.





As the firmware of Oysters Chrom navigators, the CityGID software is installed, the whole system is controlled by Windows CE 6.0 OS. The gadget has a relatively small size: the size of its screen is only 4.3 inches, but the display clearly displays positioning information or multimedia files. In addition, the compactness of the navigator allows you to use it as a pocket gadget for a short time - it has a small battery life. Additional device functionality allows you to listen to music, view photos, videos, text and image files.

Oysters 5500

The Oysters 5500 navigator became a later model in this line. Compared to previous devices, this gadget provides Internet access through a modem port and using Wi-Fi technology. Comfortable communication and work with the navigator are possible thanks to the pre-installed Android operating system.





The navigator is equipped with a processor with a frequency of 1.2 GHz and RAM 512 MB. Technical characteristics allow you to perform quite complex tasks, supporting the work of several processes at once. Oysters Chrom 5500 simultaneously supports two positioning systems: GLONASS and GPS. The diagonal of the display of the gadget is 5 inches, which practically turns it into a full-fledged tablet computer. Gadget management is completely touch-sensitive.

Oysters Chrom 1000

Among all navigators, the Oysters 1000 does not seem to be something unique and original: a classic navigation device with a stylish, seasoned design that can fit into the interior of any car. The advantage of this model is not only good technical specifications and attractive appearance, but also an affordable price. The functionality of the device includes everything only the most necessary for navigation, which allowed to maintain a low cost of the gadget.





In the line of devices of the same name, Oysters 1000 is the base model, which may require certain modifications and improvements from the manufacturer. Despite this, the playback of multimedia files and drawing maps occur at a fairly high level. The ratio of the size of the gadget and the diagonal of its display in this model has been slightly changed. The manufacturer managed to enlarge the screen, maintaining the compact size of the device. The display resolution is 480 x 272 pixels, while the device control is completely touch-sensitive.

The development of the Oysters Chrom 1000 navigator was carried out on the SirfAtlas V platform, in which the processor operates at a frequency of 500 MHz. The GPS navigator has 64 MB of RAM and 2 GB of internal memory, which is enough to download the necessary maps to the device. Oysters Chrom 1000 navigators have additional functions: a media player that plays almost all files, a video player with which you can view videos and pictures, various games supported by the device, and the ability to open text documents.





GPS-navigator Oysters 1000 is a basic model of a navigation gadget that has good functionality, thorough map rendering and attractive design.

Model 2011 3G

Oysters Chrom 2011 3G navigators have become a prime example of how the digital industry can progress rapidly. The diagonal of the gadget's display is 5 inches, the latest version of the installed Navitel 5.0 system and a powerful processor ensure fast operation of the device. The ability to connect third-party devices (mouse, keyboard, modem, flash cards) significantly expands the functionality of the device thanks to the presence of a USB adapter. Oysters 2011 3G is one of the few devices that support external USB gadgets.

The navigator is enclosed in a plastic case with a chrome side rim. The color scheme, chrome details and an impressive size (10.5 mm) make the Oysters Chrom 2011 a stylish and attractive gadget. On the body of the device there is only one key that turns the gadget on and off. Management is completely touch. The only minus of the touch display is the resistive technology used, which has long been outdated.





The microphone and speaker are located on the back of the device, not far from them is the Reset key. On the sides of the gadget are the main inputs, outputs and connectors: miniUSB port, headphone input, flash card slot. The presence of a USB port allows you to connect the navigator to a computer to charge or load additional cards. Complete with the device is a special holder and frame that allow you to mount the device on the windshield in the passenger compartment. The cable allows you to connect the navigator to the charging adapter via miniUSB port. This is explained by the fact that the full functioning of such devices requires an external power source.

Technical specifications Oysters Chrom 2011 3G

The navigator hardware is built on the Sirf Atlas V chipset, which is considered one of the best in the line of devices. The processor operates at a frequency of 500 MHz. The basic version of the model includes 128 MB of RAM and 2 GB of flash memory. Expand the memory of the navigator using MicroSD-cards. The operating system installed on Oysters Chrom 2011 is Windows CE 6.0. A variety of installed programs located on the desktop of the navigator facilitates the management of the device and provides access to its basic capabilities and functions.





The navigation system supports modern maps of Asia, the CIS, Europe and Latin America. The gadget copes with the functions assigned to it perfectly, maintaining a high reaction speed and performance, clarity and accuracy of map display and route planning, and the simplicity of finding the desired addresses. Connecting the navigator to the Network allows you to use it for communication on social networks. The latest version of the software, a high level of performance, a low price make the gadget one of the most popular and sought after.

New - Oysters Chrom 6000

More recently, Oysters has released a new model of car navigator - Chrom 6000 3G. In the new product, a six-inch TFT-display with a resolution of 800 x 480 pixels stands out quite favorably, which allows you to display images in high quality on the screen. Additional display backlighting makes it possible to work with the device both in the dark and in the daytime. The gadget is easily and clearly controlled using touch technology.
