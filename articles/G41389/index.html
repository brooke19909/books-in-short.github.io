The specifics of this Cuban hotel

When you do not have your own children, the presence of strangers is all the more undesirable. Especially if you are in love or have recently married. And you want to devote yourself entirely to your soul mate. The hotel described by us is just designed for such vacationers. In tourist avenues, he is listed as Melia Las Antillas Adults Only 16+ 4 *. This means that only people over the age of sixteen are accepted into the hotel. There are no children snooping around, especially their mothers, who scream that your kisses will shock her chaste child. You can indulge in bliss in the pool, and the screaming gang of kids will not splatter nearby. For newlyweds, the hotel provided compliments and various bonuses. You can even have a wedding here! Hammocks in the shade of palm trees, panoramic elevators and room service - how can a honeymoon be sweeter?



Enclosures and territory

Contrary to its name, Melia Las Antillas 4 * does not belong to the world famous Melia hotel chain, but to the equally popular Sandalz Resorts chain. The territory of the hotel is compact, but very green. Gardeners are trying their best, and wherever you look, you can see flower beds, flowering shrubs and palm trees. The hotel was built in 1999.

The hotel complex consists of one main five-story building and several villas. Reviews of tourists were divided on where to live better. Some newlyweds were offered rooms on the top floor of the building. They liked the ocean views and the service with a romantic atmosphere. Other newlyweds had a chance to live in a bungalow. Also laudatory reviews: quiet, cozy, romantic. Between the buildings there is a swimming pool with ponds and bridges. And this quiet corner of paradise is located just nine kilometers from the center of Varadero and thirty-five from the international airport.



The number of rooms of the resort

Hotel Melia Las Antillas 4 * offers its guests spacious and comfortable rooms, simply created for a secluded romantic getaway. All guest rooms are junior suites. Room classification starts with a housekeeper and ends with a concierge. The cheapest guest rooms are located on the lower two floors of the main building and in the bungalow. The windows of the Junior Suite Economy overlook the garden. On the third floor of the main building overlooking the ocean and in the villas are Premium Junior Suite. Their area is thirty-nine square meters. There is also a junior suite superior - on the upper two floors of the main building. Their balconies also look at the endless blue of the sea. For newlyweds, concierge junior suites are provided. There, guests are provided with additional services in the form of breakfast in bed, daily replenished minibar, personal service.



What is in the hotel rooms

The decoration of bright and spacious bedrooms in the Melia Las Antillas 4 * hotel has a comfortable rest for two. Even in the junior economy suite there is a matrimonial bed and a sofa, individually adjustable air conditioning, a safe, a TV with satellite channels (one Russian), a minibar, a kettle or a coffee maker with the necessary accessories for making hot drinks, an iron with an ironing board. Bathrooms include a hairdryer. Every day, the rooms are not only cleaned, but also linen is changed. The floors in the rooms are tiled, which is pleasant in a tropical climate. The higher the category of rooms, the more services there. A large terrace adjoins the junior suite superior, and the living room is divided into a sleeping and living area. The concierge offers slippers and bathrobes.



Meals at Melia Las Antillas 4 *

This hotel is all inclusive. And this is not easy in the communist "paradise", which is Cuba. But tourists appreciated the skill of cooks who were able to create real masterpieces of culinary art from local and seasonal products. Of course, exotic fruits grow in abundance in the tropical climate, and the local waters give wonderful fish and seafood. But reviews speak of a variety of meat dishes. At breakfast alone, there are at least twelve types of pastries! So not only children should not go to this hotel. It is dangerous to visit and dieters - the temptation to try everything is too great. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served at the Los Tainos main restaurant. All meals in it are held in buffet style. About food tourists leave only positive reviews. You can always choose dishes to your liking. The restaurant is kept clean, dishes are replenished quickly, and no queues arise (except for breakfast at pancakes).

Other restaurants and bars

Los Tainos is not the only catering establishment in the Melia Las Antillas 4 * hotel complex. It has six bars and five restaurants. The latter, unlike Los Tainos, operate in a la carte mode , with menu service. These restaurants have their own specifics. For example, Don Peperone offers visitors dishes of Italian cuisine, El Conuco - Caribbean, El Romantico - French. Reviews highly recommend visiting a fish and seafood restaurant. Between meals, you can treat yourself to light snacks, refreshing or, conversely, warming drinks. From ten in the morning until five in the afternoon, the Dune and La Tortola bars operate on the beach and by the pool. But with the onset of darkness, the fun does not end. 24 hours a day there are Las Casop bars with live music, El Mirador in the lobby, Ranshon, Fan Pub with karaoke. Reviews say that in addition to the wonderful Cuban rum, you can also taste imported drinks, as well as good alcoholic cocktails.



Beach and Pool

Melia Las Antillas 4 * (Varadero) is a first-line hotel. So he has his own piece of the coast. Reviews say that the sandy beach of the hotel is carefully guarded and cleaned. The entrance to the sea is gentle, ideal for those who are poorly kept on the water. Reviews say that during the season there are no waves and dangerous currents. There are no jellyfish and burning plankton either. Naturally, sun loungers, deck chairs, hammocks, umbrellas and sunshades are free for guests. Tourists can not find agreement on the question of how many pools in the hotel. Some reviews speak of two, others speak of one. In fact, this is a huge curved swimming pool, consisting of two compartments. It is filled with crystal clear fresh water that has passed the latest purification system. There is also a jacuzzi in the hotel. The use of the hot tub is also free. Many reviews focus on beach activities. All kinds of non-motorized sports (kayaking, windsurfing, pedal boats) are free. You can even freely and without money take an introductory diving course.

Infrastructure

This hotel provides its guests with all the services that socialist Cuba can pamper with foreign tourists. Hotel Melia Las Antillas 4 * fully meets the stated four stars. And even more. The hotel has not only daytime and evening animation (according to reviews, not bad), but also a small spa. It features a sauna, hammam and massage treatments. The hotel has free bike rental, two tennis courts, basketball courts, boccia, darts and a pool table. Every week, a free cocktail party is held on the beach.
