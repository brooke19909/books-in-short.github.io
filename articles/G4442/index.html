LUKOIL Savings Card: Benefits, How to Get and How to Use the Discount

Sellers of goods and services with constant regularity develop various bonus programs and promotions for their regular customers, thereby encouraging them to cooperate with them for a long time. And motorists are no exception, they also become participants in bonus programs.





Car enthusiasts choosing Lukoil gas stations may be interested in the eponymous funded card. Although the benefits of participating in such a bonus program are significant, not all customers use it. They are probably scared by the card activation procedure. But the activation process is not complicated - it will require a mobile phone, Internet access and the Lukoil storage card itself.

How to get a card?

Before you learn how to get a Lukoil accumulative card, it must be emphasized that only one card is issued to one person. It is issued absolutely free of charge at sales offices. To do this, you just need to fill out the form, indicating your full name. The card has an unlimited validity period, if for some reason the gas station terminal does not accept it, call the hotline number. Operators will help you fix the problem.

How to activate the Lukoil bonus card?





Lukoil cards are valid in most regions of Russia. The activation procedure is quite simple and does not require complex actions.

  1. You need to wait for the SMS message with the registration code;
  2. enter the received four-digit code in any terminal that you will find at gas stations.

After these simple actions, the card is considered active and can be used.

After activating the card, its holder automatically becomes a member of the Lukoil club. To make the use of the card even more convenient, we recommend opening the "My Account" on the club‚Äôs website. It makes it possible to track spending statistics and the number of bonuses accrued.

If you filled out the questionnaire, but the SMS message with the code did not come, we recommend that you contact the hotline operator, he will answer all questions related to bonus cards. Let him know that you have not received a message with an activation code. Then the necessary code will be sent to you again. The hotline telephone will also be useful to you if you lose your card.

How to check card balance?

You can check the status of the accrued bonus points both in the "My Account" on the club‚Äôs website and by asking to print a check at the Lukoil gas station .





Features of the Lukoil card

The Lukoil accumulation card allows its holder to reduce the cost of gasoline and diesel fuel. How beneficial it will be depends on how often you use the gas stations of this company. The Lukoil bonus card is an accumulative one, every 50 rubles that its holder spends on the purchase of gasoline and any other products in the company mini-market, give one point to the card balance. It is worth noting that when accruing points, the spent amount is rounded down: that is, if you made a purchase for 2930 rubles, 58 points will be returned to the card. And one bonus point is one ruble.

The points that the Lukoil accumulation card has can be spent as easily as earned. If you want to take advantage of the discount, inform the operator about it and present a bonus card. The cost of your purchase will decrease by the amount of bonuses that are on the balance. But it is worth noting that when using the discount, points within the program will not be awarded.





Other card products "Lukoil"

In addition to bonus cards, Lukoil offers its customers-individuals more advanced co-brand cards:

  • LUKOIL-Petrocommerce-MasterCard;
  • LUKOIL-Uralsib-VISA.

They have the same advantages as the Lukoil accumulation card, but even using them you can get various bonuses from the financial organizations themselves. They offer holders of such cards additional discounts and privileges from their partners - hotels, retail outlets, fitness clubs and so on. Among other things, you will have an open account in these banking institutions, which you can replenish or withdraw funds from it completely free of charge.

Lukoil fuel cards for organizations

Lukoil has not forgotten about its legal clients: business owners can also issue Lukoil-Inter-Card fuel cards.

To make it necessary:

  • legal entity status;
  • fill out an application and a card application form;
  • send the completed forms to the email address of the subsidiary Lukoil - Likard.




Advantages of fuel cards for legal entities

We will name the main advantages that the use of such a product as Lukoil fuel cards has.

  1. The ability to refuel at any gas station of this company within the Russian state and beyond.
  2. Monitor fuel consumption.
  3. There is card protection with a PIN code.
  4. The ability to associate a card with a specific car and with a specific type of fuel.
  5. Ability to set refueling limits.
  6. The ability to use your personal account to manage settings.
  7. Using the Likard-Transit program, you can save up to 6.5% of the cost of fuel.
  8. Particularly large companies get their own personal manager who will help resolve issues related to service.
  9. The ability to get fuel on credit.
  10. With the card, you will receive a discount not only on fuel, but also on additional services of the company - gasoline delivery, technical assistance on the road, tire inflation and washing.

Conclusion

We described the main advantages that Lukoil fuel and storage cards have, where to get them and how to use them, was also considered. It is safe to say that Lukoil card products are another way to save the budget - business and family.
