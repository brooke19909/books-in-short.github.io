Description of acoustics Morel Tempo Coax 6

At the moment, the brand "Morel" is very popular. She is quite famous among motorists, as she produces speaker systems. In this article, we will talk about the Morel Tempo Coax 6. We will consider what the described device is, as well as what its advantages and disadvantages are.

Description

This system is considered a good combination of price and quality. It should be noted that the described device is quite popular. In principle, any Morel speakers are currently one of the best in the existing class of systems.

The diameter of the tweeter is about 28 mm; it is made of a soft dome. When it was created, EVC technology was used. Thanks to this, you can see that acoustics has a new standard of work. This solution is able to provide the owner with good sound quality, long life, as well as bright colors during playback.

A new tweeter can be installed in several ways. It should be noted that the midbass of the described Morel Tempo Coax 6 system, which is reviewed in this article, has special magnets. They are ferrite type. This made the speakers highly efficient. It should be noted that the size of the magnets, when compared with devices of this class, was slightly smaller. Due to this, the depth of the product has become minimal, respectively, the device is easier to install.

The diffuser is made so that the reproduction is as smooth and clean as possible, especially when it comes to medium frequencies. The bass is sensitive and dynamic.

It is also necessary to pay attention to the design of the Morel Tempo Coax 6 system. It is made of high-quality materials, so any obstacles to the signal transmission will be eliminated. In order to provide good sound, the manufacturer provided an excellent frequency response. The system power is 200 watts.

Specifications

This Morel speaker system has a coaxial structure. Size is 6 inches. Built-in two stripes. With regard to power, the maximum is 200 watts, and the nominal one is 110 watts. Sensitivity - 90 decibels. The range of reproducible frequencies is represented by an interval from 40 to 22 thousand Hz. The impedance is 4 ohms. The crossover frequency is 4 thousand Hz. The speaker has a swivel design. The magnet is a ferrite type, its depth reaches 64 mm. The dimensions of the speaker are 165 mm.

Advantages

Among the advantages of Morel Tempo Coax 6 is the balanced sound. In addition, users notice that the device is highly sensitive, has good detail. Vocals are displayed perfectly. Build and quality at an excellent level.

Some lack bass, but it all depends on the user's preferences. You can use the device not only in a car, but also for home acoustics. It should be noted that in the latter version it fully reveals all its capabilities.

The advantages should be attributed directly to the manufacturer itself, since its reputation is at a good level. During installation, you can install in such a way that the speakers will not be visible.

Small size, as well as low price will allow the user to enjoy their purchase as much as possible. The owners also note that when operating the device at high volume there are no wheezing and distortion. The price of Morel Tempo Coax 6 is approximately 6 - 7 thousand rubles.
