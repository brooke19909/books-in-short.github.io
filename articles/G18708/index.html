Board game "Activity": rules

"Activity" is the most popular board game not only among young people, but also among the older generation. Despite this, you can find people who do not know the rules. In "Activity" there is a field with cells, as in many board games. Teams compete, and if they win, then their chip moves, and if they do not succeed, the chip remains in place. Speech



One of the methods of guessing, which is prescribed in the rules of the game ‚ÄúActiviti‚Äù, is an explanation using words. Many like this option more than active pantomime. In unfamiliar companies, there is another unwritten rule. More often than not, one of the players explains the word to his team, but everyone can guess in the open round. Picture



Considering the rules of the board game "Activiti", you can find a third way of explaining the word. And it will be drawing. When a player stands on the appropriate square on the playing field, he must explain the word with a pencil and a piece of paper. And again, there are a few taboos. Naturally, you cannot write words. Yes, actually, in general, even depicting individual letters of the alphabet is prohibited. And then what can be done? Draw objects that are written on the card. But not every company has professional artists. This is the whole interest of the game. It's funny to see how the person who took the pencil for the first time in five years is trying to portray a boar. But if animals with grief can be drawn in half, then what about more complex concepts? Break the drawings into several parts. Let us examine the example of the word "seaman". The first part of the word can be depicted as the sea, and the second - in the form of walking legs. Putting these two parts together is definitely easier than drawing the whole concept. By the way, it is not forbidden to use mathematical symbols.

Why do we need an hourglass?

In the rules of the game ‚ÄúActiviti‚Äù (the original) it is written that all the actions by which players show words are performed for a while. Why do I need cards



In order not to get confused and not to go astray, clear rules of the game ‚ÄúActiviti‚Äù were invented. Everything is possible thanks to cards. How do they look? They are somewhat similar to ordinary playing cards. Although there are many differences. Cards for the game "Activity" have a boring one-color shirt, they are equipped with numbers. This is not a serial number. The number on the map shows its complexity. The player must soberly assess his strengths in one way or another explanation and choose for himself a suitable option. The difficulty level is only three. The lightest cards are marked with the number 3, the most complex - with the number 5. 4 is an intermediate step. For example, a player cannot draw, but he needs to explain the word in this way. Then he can ease his task and pull out a card with the number three. But if you need the word "tell", and a person knows that he is good at this, he can take the cardboard, which is marked with the number 5.

There are tasks on the back of the card. There are only 6. It is not the player who decides which word to tell. The figure is taken from the playing field.

How does the round go



Let's try to give a clear explanation of the rules of the game ‚ÄúActiviti‚Äù. All participants are divided into two teams. The right of the first move can be played in different ways, for example, toss a coin or solve this issue by drawing lots. The winning team selects one candidate, and this person in 10 seconds explains any word from any card. The way a person will communicate is selected at the discretion of the player. If his team guessed the word, then it goes forward. The number of cells that you need to advance is easy to figure out by looking at the card shirt. Now it‚Äôs the turn of the other team to do the same. After the start is successfully overcome, the game begins. Each of the team members in strict order demonstrates the word in the way that is shown on the cell of the playing field where the chip is. The task number on the card must be sought there. But the complexity of the card can be chosen independently. Someone thinks that you are going quieter, you will continue, and some, on the contrary, are pulling difficult tasks all the time. The chip moves across the field by the number of cells that is drawn on the back of the shirt. But you can only move if the team guessed the word. But if the players do not understand the vague explanations of a friend, the chip will stand still. The team that comes to the finish first wins.

Children's Game Rules

As an attentive reader could already notice, all of the Activity games are very similar. The tasks and their complexity are changing. But the methods of explanation remain unchanged. What is the difference between the rules of the Activiti game for children from the adult version? In words. In the children's version there are no complex concepts that sometimes show an adult as an impossible task. All the concepts written on the cards will be familiar to the child. And you can find a game in which instead of words pictures will be displayed. In this case, preschoolers who do not yet know how to take part in the general fun. Why, then, do we need a game where the child will not be able to learn new words, but will operate with already known concepts? By showing animals, birds, and surrounding objects, children train imagination, logic, and acting.
