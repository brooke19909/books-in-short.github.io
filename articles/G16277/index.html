How to choose the cheapest Tele2 tariff: review of tariff plans, recommendations

Tele2 subscribers are quite different. Some seek to make their mobile life as comfortable as possible, while others try to save as much as possible and are always in search of the cheapest rate. Tele2 has few tariff plans, but each subscriber can find something suitable for himself.

"Classical"

When considering tariff plans, in the first place it is worth paying attention to the "Classic" option. It can be safely called the cheapest tariff on Tele2 without the Internet. It does not provide any daily and monthly charges. The subscriber pays only for what he spends.

Call charges vary by region. For example, in Moscow and the Moscow Region there is a single cost for outgoing calls to all numbers in Russia. She is 1.95 rubles. in 1 minute of communication. The same price is set for SMS messages to absolutely all numbers. But in the Altai Territory, the conditions are slightly different:

  • 1 minute of communication with a subscriber of any operator in the home region costs 0.60 rubles .;
  • 1 minute of conversation with a Tele2 Russia subscriber - 2 rubles;
