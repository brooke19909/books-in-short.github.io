Hotel "Aurora", Belgorod: description, rooms and reviews

A worthy option for accommodating business people and travelers is the Aurora Hotel. Belgorod will delight you with tourist facilities and business opportunities, and this hotel offers quality service.





Location

In the very center of the city is the Aurora Hotel (Belgorod). Her address: 86 Preobrazhenskaya Street. It is literally 500 meters from Victory Park. The distance from the train station is 4 km, and from the international airport - 9 km. Guests are advised to visit the following nearby attractions:

  • Transfiguration Cathedral (0.2 km).
  • Cathedral Square (0.5 km).
  • Drama Theater. Shchepkina (1 km).
  • Art Museum (1 km).
  • Selivanov House (2 km).

Room Amenities

Everything you need for a comfortable stay will be provided by the Aurora Hotel (Belgorod). In your room you can take advantage of the following list of amenities:

  • a minibar, which for a fee is filled with your favorite drinks (can be used as a refrigerator);
  • a bathroom, which in addition to plumbing is equipped with a hairdryer, a makeup mirror and a hygiene kit;
  • access to wireless internet;
  • landline telephone, which will provide communication with the reception and the ability to make long distance calls;
  • safe for valuables.

Catering establishments

Hotels in the center of Belgorod are very popular among tourists. The hotel and restaurant are the two main places that provide comfortable accommodation and delicious food. In this hotel the following establishments are presented to your attention:

  • The Preobrazhensky restaurant is a new establishment with luxurious decoration and an extensive menu. It serves Russian and European cuisines. The hall is designed for 100 guests. Every evening there is live music.
  • The hotel has a banquet hall "Aurora", in which events for up to 200 people can be held. The staff will not only prepare meals and serve guests, but will also help you organize the event.
  • The 24-hour lobby bar has a relaxed atmosphere. Here you can not only enjoy your favorite drinks, but also enjoy delicious snacks.
  • In the warmer months, an outdoor cafe is open. Admiring the views of the city, you can enjoy dishes from the menu of the Preobrazhensky restaurant.

Additional Information

Hotel "Aurora" (Belgorod), like other similar establishments, operates in accordance with certain rules. It will be useful for you to know the following:

  • check-in of guests starts after 13:00, and check-out is before noon (early check-in or late check-out is available for an additional fee);
  • during check-in, a cash deposit is paid from the guest in cash or by credit card;
  • a prerequisite for registration is the provision of an identity card with a current photo;
  • smoking is strictly prohibited in all areas of the hotel;
  • foreign guests are charged a migration fee;
  • Pets are allowed at the hotel for an additional fee (this need to be informed in advance);
  • Parking will cost guests 150 rubles per day.

Negative reviews

The Aurora 4 * Hotel (Belgorod, Russia) is not without its drawbacks. From the reviews you can find out about the following negative points:

  • in some rooms the TV does not work well (fuzzy sound and blurry image);
  • instead of a crib, a playpen is brought to the room;
  • a delicious, instant coffee is served for breakfast;
  • for two guests, only one cosmetic set is provided (you have to make a remark to the administrator several times to correct the situation);
  • small package of satellite channels;
  • there are no cotton swabs or sponges in the bathroom;
  • due to the fact that only one elevator is working, queues are constantly gathering around it;
  • due to poor ventilation, the rooms have a musty smell;
  • there are no mosquito nets on the windows, and therefore mosquitoes flood the room during airing;
  • in the corridors of the hotel poor lighting, which is fraught with injuries;
  • the rooms have few lighting fixtures (there is no chandelier, but only spot lighting);
  • due to the fact that the shower is fixed, it is almost impossible to swim without wetting the head;
  • antediluvian irons and sealed ironing boards.

Despite numerous shortcomings, travelers consider this hotel one of the best in Belgorod. Although it will be fair to say that his five-star status is not true.
