The recreation center "Borovoe" (Tyumen) is waiting for guests

Tyumen is a small Russian city in Siberia. Its economy is focused on the production of petroleum products. Residents, tired of city noise and dust, choose to relax in nature. Among the many options worth noting recreation "Borovoe". Tyumen is located just a 10-minute drive from the base, but here fabulous forest, silence, fresh air and plenty of entertainment await vacationers.

Description, infrastructure of the complex





The recreation center "Borovoye" (Tyumen) is located in a pine forest (hence the name), near natural forest lakes with clear water. The complex offers visitors several comfortable cottages of various capacities, cozy gazebos, playgrounds and sports grounds, a football field, an equipped place for playing paintball, a parking lot and a Russian bath with an excellent steam room. On the territory there is a cafe, the menu of which includes delicious homemade dishes. There are also several banquet rooms where you can organize a corporate party or any home holiday. If you wish, guests can rent a catamaran or an ATV. All issues are quickly resolved by the administration, the building of which is located within walking distance from the cottages.

The Borovoye recreation center (Tyumen) fell in love with visitors for their well-being, developed infrastructure, safety (the territory is under round-the-clock protection) and affordable prices.

Summer and winter holidays





Many recreation centers try to diversify the leisure of customers. Tyumen, Borovoe is no exception. Everything has been done here so that guests have a good time. In summer and winter, guests are not bored. In good weather, many people enjoy taking quiet walks through the woods, in the warm season they swim in lakes, fish (according to the rules of the base, all caught fish are released into the lake), arrange outdoor picnics with barbecue, participate in sports and thematic competitions.

In winter, everyone, both adults and children, enjoy playing snowballs, sculpting a snow woman, building fortresses. Not all recreation facilities offer so many options for active pastime. Tyumen, Borovoye occupies leading positions in this matter. Those who wish can go sledding from a hill, ski on a specially equipped track, and skate on ice. Hockey fans drive the puck on the rink, many practice figure skating. For safety, the roller is separated by sides with a protective net. By the way, there is no need to worry where to get sports equipment. Holiday Village "Borovoe" (Tyumen) offers a variety of rental equipment from Fischer and Salomon.

The visitors really loved the real Russian wood-fired baths. And on Baptism you can plunge into the font.

Residence





The complex has several cottages for living. They are designed for both couples and large companies of 10, 20, 30 and even 40 people!

Each cottage has all the amenities, a kitchen, a bathroom, a barbecue area is located nearby. The houses are warm in winter and cool in summer, meet all sanitary standards, built of environmentally friendly materials.

Around the lakes there are comfortable tables with benches where you can sit, eat, enjoy beautiful views. I am glad that the tables are provided absolutely free of charge in a queue.

Prices





For many, the Borovoye recreation center (Tyumen) has become a favorite weekend venue. The prices here are affordable:

  • Being at the base and using its infrastructure is 150 rubles per person per day. Children under 7 years old, veterans of the Great Patriotic War, participants in hostilities and pensioners visit Borovoye for free.
  • Rental of cottages - from 10 to 20 thousand rubles for the entire cottage per day. The price depends on the number of visitors and the day of the week (on weekdays rent will be cheaper). The price includes travel to the base of cars and use of parking.
  • Bath rental - 1.5 thousand rubles per 1 hour, with renewal each next hour is paid at the rate of 1 thousand rubles. At the same time, up to 10 people can take a bath.
  • Large tables and gazebos - from 3 to 10 thousand rubles per day (from 10-00 to 23-00) depending on the number of seats.

Separately paid rental of sports equipment, as well as coal and firewood for barbecue.

How to get there

Borovoye (Tyumen) - a well-known base. Therefore, almost every citizen will explain how to get to it. From Tyumen you need to go along the bypass road to Omsk, drive through CHP-2, at the 20th kilometer and the base is located. Its doors are hospitably open for everyone daily from 10-00 to 20-00.

Important! Pets are not allowed in Borovoye, drinking alcohol on the territory is not welcome, as the base is intended for families.

Reviews

All customers who visited the base unanimously give the complex the highest ratings. Families with children and youth companies can have a good time here inexpensively. Visitors approve of developed infrastructure, plenty of entertainment, cleanliness and order on the territory, responsiveness and friendliness of staff.
