Restaurant "Dastarkhan" in Pushkino. Institutional Overview

In the suburbs there is a wonderful mental institution - a restaurant-teahouse "Dastarkhan" in Pushkino. Here you can not only meet with friends or spend a family evening with a cup of tea, but also enjoy a delicious meal with excellent taste.



Interior

Restaurant "Dastarkhan" (Pushkino) inside is decorated in an oriental style. The interior of this institution was decorated by a team of professional designers who also created the furniture design, which is located here. In total, the hall of the institution is designed for 70 visitors.

In the middle of the large hall, guests can sit at small round tables made of dark wood, on wicker chairs with wooden backs. Near the window there are places designed for large companies - here visitors sit on soft colored sofas. It should also be noted that each such zone is separated from the other by light textiles.

The main hall also has a large bar, behind which bartenders work. It is decorated in oriental style and decorated with painted plates.

The ceilings of the hall are supported by thick square columns on which plasma TVs hang - they often broadcast sports events and music videos.



Menu

With dishes of Oriental and Russian cuisine, Dastarkhan in Pushkino is ready to surprise its visitors. The menu of this establishment offers its guests a huge assortment of cold (eggplant with syuzma, "Balykcha", "Nishon", "Kazi of Uzbekistan", assorted) and hot snacks (fried suluguni, chuchvara with veal or lamb, dolma with veal or lamb, mushroom julienne) . There is a good selection of salads (Til, Baysky, Caesar, Budur, Aichi-Chuk, Shakarop, Aigiyul), soups (combined meat hodgepodge, Ugra Osh, Lagman Uygur "," Nuhut "shurpa," Pete ") and side dishes.



The restaurant "Dastarkhan" (Pushkino) prepares dishes on the grill (kebab of different types of meat and fish, "Upanpan" of lamb, grilled tiger prawns, sea bass, dorado, charcoal vegetables), as well as hot dishes (manti with lamb, "Lazzat", "Romshteks", pike perch with vegetables in a creamy sauce, tongue in a creamy sauce with potatoes and mushrooms).

For dessert, Dastarkhan offers baklava, several types of cheesecake (Chocolate, New York), hot cakes (with strawberries, cherries), strudel, kutaby, as well as home-made ice cream.



The restaurant also bakes branded baked goods. Here they can surprise you with original shawarma (with lamb and chicken), kutabs, khachapuri, samsa, pies, as well as tasty and juicy meat whites.



Bar and Hookah

The bar card of the institution is represented by a set of alcoholic drinks (beer, vodka, cognac, whiskey, wine), as well as soft drinks that do not contain alcohol (soft drinks, carbonated drinks, fruit drinks, juice). In addition, the restaurant has a good assortment of cocktails (Aperol Spitz, Daiquiri, Cosmopolitan, Campari Orange, Orgasm, Blue Lagoon), among which there are several types of hot drinks (beylis coffee, Irish coffee, mulled wine).

Additional Information

Restaurant "Dastarkhan" (Pushkino) regularly provides guests with a number of favorable offers and discounts.

For regular visitors to the restaurant there is an accumulative system of discounts, which is presented in the form of discount cards with discounts of 5 and 10 percent. In addition, when making an order with a total amount of 1,500 rubles, the restaurant presents a liter of drink as a gift to guests.

Restaurant "Dastarkhan" (Pushkino) can serve as an excellent venue for organizing a banquet to celebrate any event. The institution also provides the customer with a 20% discount on the entire menu, but this only happens when the banquet is scheduled for Monday, Tuesday or Sunday.

The restaurant also offers a targeted food delivery service. You can make an order by calling the phone number listed on the official website of the institution or by filling out the online form on the website. Delivery is carried out only in the Pushkin district and on the territory of Ivanteyevka. When ordering in the amount of 1000 rubles, this service is provided free of charge.



Address of the institution and mode of operation

The restaurant-teahouse "Dastarkhan" can be found at the following address: Pushkino, Moscow region, m. Dzerzhinets, 24A - this is not far from the Dzerzhinets housing cooperative.

The restaurant's doors are open daily from noon to midnight, and on Fridays and Saturdays - until two in the morning. By the way, on weekends you can relax here with oriental music that is performed live, as well as with a belly dance performed by a dance group.
