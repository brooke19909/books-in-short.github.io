Where is Vancouver located? What city is Vancouver in?

Many have heard about this city; it is interesting for its traditions, culture and events. However, not everyone knows where Vancouver is located. This Canadian community is located in British Columbia County and is the largest port in North America. But, besides important economic importance, it is also a center of international tourism. Vancouver attracts vacationers with its beauty and excellent ski resorts.

Vancouver's Top Attractions





Stunning beauty of the city of Vancouver! Canada can rightfully be proud of it. There are so many places that you just need to visit, that tourists will need at least two weeks. In order not to miss the most interesting, you can make a plan of excursions.

The first thing you definitely need to see is the Lion Bridge. The half-kilometer construction is faithfully and faithfully guarded by statues of white lions.

Von Langley Park captivates tourists with its amazing landscapes and buildings with a rich history.

Go to the Maritime Museum. Both adults and children who love stories about sailors should visit this place. The museum is known for a huge number of interesting exhibits, one of which is a whole ship.

Closer to nature





Remember where Vancouver is located geographically, and you will immediately understand how amazing and unique its nature is. Here are some examples.

Stanley park

Favorite vacation spot for citizens and tourists. And this is not surprising. A thousand acres of green forest is a fantastic sight. The designer in the design of this amazing place was nature itself. Of course, now Stanley Park is ennobled by people, but in it untouched forests occupy almost the entire area. And starting from spring, you can admire a huge number of roses in the rose garden. Children will be interested to see a large aquarium with such huge marine inhabitants as whales and beluga.

Classical Chinese Garden

Unexpectedly, in the very center of Vancouver, you can come across a piece of China. This is a garden made in the classical manner inherent in the times of the Ming Dynasty. The park occupies a small area - less than a quarter hectare, but the efforts of Chinese landscape masters are highly appreciated by tourists who once discovered this amazing city and will never forget where Vancouver is located.

State Aquarium

It is recommended to visit this place not only for children, but also for adults. It should be noted that this institution in Vancouver is rightfully considered one of the most beloved places for families.

Here you can see a huge number of marine life, listen to entertaining lectures on the underwater world with illustrative examples, watch the feeding of cute mammals. In addition to the underwater inhabitants, on the territory you can look at a corner of the rainforest, the main and most striking inhabitants of which are fantastically beautiful butterflies.

Courage Test





Thrill-seekers in Vancouver also have something to do. For example, visit Capilano Suspension Bridge. Its length is 136 meters, height is 70 meters. A bustling mountain river with the same name flows under it.

Now the bridge is privately owned, and a tour of it costs money. But this does not deter the daredevils. Walking along the bridge when a strong wind blows and the structure sways from side to side is a great way to tickle your nerves.

The reward for courage will be the most beautiful views from the bridge. Here you can take stunning pictures that will become a real decoration of a family photo album.

The bridge was modernized, and the passage along it is absolutely safe, but people with poor health should still refrain from walking.

No less extreme entertainment await tourists when visiting the Kuropatki mountains. You will have to get to the top by funicular - and this is only the beginning of exciting adventures. You can test yourself for dexterity and courage by riding a snowboard, flying a paraglider or descending with a breeze on a cable rope on a winch. For those who prefer traditional skiing, there are several gentle slopes.

Schools





In Vancouver, there are several world-famous higher education institutions. For example, Simon Fraser University or the University of British Columbia. The last of them is known not only for its excellent training programs, but also for the Museum of Anthropology, located on its territory. There is a unique collection of exhibits from around the globe.

Venue of the Winter Olympics 2010

Winter sports enthusiasts will never ask a question like: "Vancouver ... What country is this city in?" Do you know why? Because they warmly recall the 2010 Olympic capital.



By the way, the holding of the Games in their native land, despite some difficulties in organizational aspects, brought the Canadian team victory in the medal standings.
