Villa Predrag 3 * (Budva, Montenegro): description, service, reviews

Popular and one of the main resorts of Montenegro is Budva, which is located in the center of the Adriatic coast of the Budva Riviera. This is a resort area, and one of the sunniest places on the coast. Here is almost everything you need for a good outdoor activity. It often hosts festivals and holidays. Over the past century, the city has grown so much that it began to spread to the small villages surrounding it from all sides.

Centuries-old town

It has a typical Mediterranean climate with warm summers and mild winters, and the number of sunny days per year is 297. Therefore, this city has become a favorite destination for vacationers and has a developed resort and tourist infrastructure.

The city consists of two parts - the New Town, which stretches along the promenade and goes deep into the mainland, and the Old Town with unique architecture, fortresses and narrow streets. There are cafes and restaurants everywhere , cultural life is organized and the doors of hotels for different tastes and purses are open. One of these small guest houses is Villa Predrag 3 *, located 50 meters from the sea, in the heart of the Old Town. Budva is well known at the regional level as the capital of nightlife. The local discos arose in the eighties as dance clubs at hotels, but club life began to gain momentum in the 90s, and this trend continued in the 2000s. Currently, a huge number of bars, restaurants, clubs and dance floors are located on the promenade and in the vicinity.

On the resort streets and along the promenade of the New City, there are also many restaurants, shops, and attractions for children. On the pier with small boats and boats, for a fee, you can ride with the breeze, go to another area or go fishing. In addition, here you can buy the freshest seafood. Walking on a large pier, you can admire the many yachts and boats, relax and enjoy the water surface and beautiful views of the bay, sitting on comfortable benches. And in the evening there is a very romantic atmosphere: the lights turn on, pleasant music plays from the coastal cafes, and various evening events are held.
