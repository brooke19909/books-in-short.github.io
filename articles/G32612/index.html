Train "Novorossiysk - St. Petersburg": route, prices, reviews

The branded train with the symbolic name "Chernomorets" runs on the route "Novorossiysk - St. Petersburg" relatively recently, since 2004. Therefore, all four trains of this train consist of new cars, which are equipped with the latest Russian car building. "St. Petersburg - Novorossiysk" is a route that is very popular among passengers, especially in the summer, when millions of Russians travel to the affectionate Black Sea and back. When leaving from Novorossiysk, the train has number 044; when leaving from St. Petersburg, it is assigned number 043.

Prices

The cost of a ticket "Novorossiysk - St. Petersburg" depends on the seasonal coefficient, so the final prices must be found at the box office or on the Russian Railways website. On average, a reserved seat full ticket will cost a passenger in the region of 5,000 rubles, a compartment car - 10,000 rubles, and deluxe seats in sleeping cars (CB) - about 20,000 rubles.

Train description

The Novorossiysk-Saint-Petersburg train consists of seventeen railcars: seven reserved seats, seven compartments, one sleeping extra comfort, a restaurant car and a staff car, which provides several specialized places for people with disabilities.

The train is equipped with air conditioning, modern dry closets and information boards showing the temperature of the air in the car, its number and toilet occupancy. The dining car has a large selection of various hot dishes and soft drinks. And you can order food from the restaurant right in your compartment.
