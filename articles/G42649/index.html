Term deposits of Sberbank of Russia for individuals: conditions, interest

Sberbank regularly replenishes the line of investment products. This also applies to term deposits of individuals. Consider their most relevant types to date. By the way, this financial service is popular with the bank's customers, which indicates its winning marketing strategy: it regularly produces high-yield financial products designed for different categories of citizens.





Concept and criteria of contribution

What are the most profitable deposits at Sberbank of Russia? Next, we consider the types of deposits, interest and define the very concept of contribution.

For starters, what is a ‚Äúterm deposit‚Äù? The name itself implies that this financial instrument has a certain shelf life - several months or years. It is stored in the bank in the amount of the amount of money deposited in the account and is returned to the owner along with the accrued interest. There are three main criteria by which contributions of this type are divided:

  • there is the possibility of replenishment and partial withdrawal;
  • without the possibility of replenishment and withdrawal;
  • only with the possibility of replenishment.

Do not forget that no matter what type of product you are considering, you should always conclude a term deposit agreement.

Let's consider each in more detail. Which one to choose is up to you.

These are three types of term deposits of Sberbank of Russia, aimed at the needs of various categories of citizens. The amount of interest accrued on the deposit varies depending on the amount of the deposit, the chosen currency and the term.

Save Deposit

It is sent for storage of a monetary amount on a deposit in order to generate income. Its term is from one month to 3 years (for deposits opened in euros - from one to 3 years). There is a choice: to store finances in rubles or foreign currency. During the storage period, the deposit cannot be replenished or withdrawn from the account. The smallest amount is one thousand rubles, 100 US dollars or euros. The interest rate varies based on the term and amount of the deposit, the interest on the term deposit at Sberbank of Russia is regularly charged to the account in accordance with the conditions.





Interest Rates:

  • from 3.8 to 5% in rubles .;
  • from 0.05 to 1.15% in US dollars;
  • 0.01% in euros.

If you place a deposit for a maximum period, then the income, respectively, will be greater.

Deposit "Replenish"

This product is aimed at those who expect to save money for accumulation on a regular basis. Its key distinguishing feature: if you can replenish your account, you can not partially withdraw any amount. A deposit is opened for a period of 3 months to 3 years in Russian currency (for deposits that are open in foreign currency - from a year to 3). Interest rates are as follows:

  • from 3.7 to 4.5% in rubles .;
  • from 0.05 to 0.95% in US dollars;
  • from 0.01% in euros.

The lower threshold of the deposit amount is one thousand rubles. or 100 euros or US dollars.
