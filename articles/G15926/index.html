DIY Patchwork Pillow

The patchwork technique is a special style of sewing when several rags of different material are used to create one thing. Bedspreads, pillows, chair covers are most often performed in this style. The patchwork technique is also used for sewing clothes. The result is slightly eccentric and slightly exotic things.

The patchwork technique is extremely popular in our time. Of course, few decide to completely transform their apartment and create a completely new interior. But here the patchwork pillow will successfully fit into any room. It will become that detail of the interior, which will create a feeling of home comfort. After all, a house without such copyrighted things and cute trinkets remains impersonal and empty.



Material selection

Pillow "patchwork" with your own hands is quite simple to make, especially if you use the patches of the correct form. It is much more difficult to correctly select the material. Theoretically, you can take any fabric, combine textures, combine the most unthinkable colors - there are no strict restrictions. But in practice it depends on the ability to make the right color composition, how beautiful a thing you will succeed. So, on the one hand, you need to use all your creative potential, and on the other hand, try to stay within the framework of good taste. If you select the material of the desired texture and combine the colors correctly, you will get very beautiful patchwork pillows.

Master Class

In order to evaluate their own strengths, first try to sew a mini version of the pillow. We take any four cuts of fabric and cut each square 6 √ó 6 cm (taking into account the allowance for the seams). We cut one square 11 √ó 11 cm from another fabric. We sew four flaps together, and then sew them with a large square, leaving one small hole for the packing. Then the needle bar is stuffed with any suitable material.





If you have a mini version, you can try your hand at creating a full-size pillow. The number of flaps can be increased, but at the initial stage it is better not to depart from the rectangular shape. But later it will be possible to try to sew flaps of complex shape (along an oblique, zigzag, circumference, etc.) and even make applications.

Imitation style

The patchwork pillow does not have to be created using patchwork technique, which, admittedly, takes a lot of time. In fact, this style has become so popular that you can buy ready-made fabric in the store that mimics an assembly of several flaps.
