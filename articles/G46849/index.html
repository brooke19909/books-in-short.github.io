Mix Colombo Hotel 3 (Mallorca, Spain): description, photo, reviews of tourists

In search of a place to spend a vacation, tourists quite often consider Mallorca as an option. This Spanish island is a real paradise for travelers, because here they will find picturesque nature, a festive atmosphere and, of course, quality service. For tourists there are hotels all year round. And one of the good places to relax is Mix Colombo Hotel 3 *.

Of course, first of all, you need to get as much information as possible about the chosen place. What conditions of food and accommodation do guests offer and is the hotel suitable for families with children? What impressions can tourists share?

Where to look for a hotel complex?





Of course, for starters, tourists need to find out exactly where the hotel complex Mix Colombo Hotel 3 * is located. Mallorca, a picturesque resort village called S'Illot-Cala Morlanda - is here, just a few meters from the famous Sillot Beach, and the hotel is located.

Cala Millor Resort Center is just 5 km away, with lively beaches, parties and a host of other attractions awaiting the traveler. The capital of the island of Palma de Mallorca is 68 km from the hotel complex, and the distance to the island's only airport is about 65 km. By the way, the hotel staff is happy to arrange a transfer for you, and you should not worry about a long trip.

Hotel Information





The hotel complex Mix Colombo Hotel Mallorca 3 * consists of a large eight-story building with snow-white walls. Of course, the hotel has its own territory with pools, terraces and places for relaxation. By the way, this is a calm area - there are many other hotels, cafes, restaurants and shops nearby.

Hotel Mix Colombo Hotel 3 * was built in 1960. The last reconstruction was carried out in 2015 - after the repair everything here meets international requirements. The building is equipped with an elevator system.

Hotel Mix Colombo Hotel 3 *: photo and description of rooms





The hotel complex consists of 139 rooms - these are standard rooms of different capacities with stylish decor and a private balcony with a view of the beach. Of course, guests can count on the necessary furniture and appliances for a comfortable stay. Upon request, staff will deliver an extra bed for the child in the room.

In particular, a modern individual air conditioning system is at your service, which is simply indispensable in the summer months. During the cold periods of the year, central heating is available on site. You can use the telephone and safe, watch satellite and local channels on TV. There is also an electric kettle, hot drinks facilities, a small fridge and a minibar, which are stocked with drinks for a fee.

The bathroom is small, but there is everything necessary for hygiene procedures, including a shower, toilet, washbasin and toilet mirror. Guests can count on a set of shampoos, soaps and other body care products. There is also a hairdryer for drying hair. Clean towels are delivered to the room regularly during cleaning. By the way, the maids here work excellently - the rooms are always clean and fresh.

Should you count on good nutrition?





Of course, when planning a vacation, an important point is nutrition, without which it is difficult to imagine a quality vacation. What does the hotel complex Mix Colombo Hotel 3 * offer tourists?

Paying for accommodation on site, tourists receive free breakfast. In the morning you can go down to the restaurant and eat or ask to deliver breakfast directly to your room. The menu is quite varied - there are light snacks and salads, several hot dishes, fresh pastries and toasts. The rest of the day, you can also eat in the hotel restaurant. It serves local and international cuisine. There is also a bar where you can relax with a glass of strong drink or order a delicious cocktail.

Of course, there are other catering facilities in the village, including luxurious restaurants or small cozy cafes.

Beach holidays: distance, amenities and entertainment on the beach





Of course, deciding to spend a few days on the island, tourists expect to relax on the seashore. What should the guests of Mix Colombo Hotel 3 * expect? Mallorca is famous for its beaches, and the hotel is located just 100 meters from the seashore. Sillot Beach can be reached in just a few minutes of leisurely walk.

Travelers speak well of this place. The beach here is clean, covered with soft sand. The sea is mostly calm, but from time to time waves appear, which pleases fans of windsurfing. There are plenty of sun umbrellas and sunbeds on the beach, and beach towels can be obtained at the hotel. It is worth considering that this section of the coast is public, and, therefore, the rental of sun loungers is paid separately.

Of course, tourists can ride boats and water ski, rent non-motorized water transport, engage in various sports, in a word, have fun from the heart. Other beaches are within walking distance, some of them lively, while others are for quiet, secluded relaxation and romantic walks.

The list of additional services offered by the hotel





Despite the fact that the hotel, by and large, serves tourists only as an overnight stay, additional services are offered on its territory in order to make the stay as comfortable as possible.

The hotel has Internet access, both in public areas and in rooms, but this is a paid service. Guests can also use the parking lot. Renting a transport on the island is not difficult, as rental points are everywhere - most travelers move around the island by car and bicycle. The hotel offers laundry and ironing services, and the prices are quite affordable.

In case of health problems, contact one of the employees - they will call you a qualified doctor. By the way, the reception here is open around the clock, so at any time you can get the necessary information or ask for a service.
