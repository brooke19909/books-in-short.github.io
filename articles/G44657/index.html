Several ways to put money on Webmoney

Payment system "Webmoney" ("WebMoney") is the most popular service to date, which is intended for electronic payments.

Convenience, functionality and security attract a huge number of users around the world. It is difficult to have fun and work on the network, not being able to easily pay for a product or service, as it happens in reality. By placing money on the Internet, I want to be sure that they are under reliable protection, and no one except the owner has access to them. In order to satisfy all these needs, this service has been created. This is the ease of use and security of any operation: how to deposit money on Webmoney, how to store and use them.

What is the WebMoney service?

WebMoney is not just a payment service. This is the environment that serves any settlement operations on the Web and beyond.

Widespread around the world, it supports transactions between users in euros, dollars, rubles and other currencies. The user creates an account and receives an electronic wallet. There may be several. Each user is assigned an ID number. A high degree of protection, identity verification, serious limitations on the ability of those people who do not want to share their data - all this allows us to think about the responsible attitude of developers to their project. The service is supported by all operating systems, there is the possibility of access from mobile devices. Ever since its inception (in 1998), WebMoney e-banking has not been implicated in any scandals related to the loss or theft of user funds. The reputation of Webmoney as an affordable and simple payment service is supported by the ease of depositing and withdrawing funds. For those people who are just about to start their cooperation with an electronic bank, it will be useful to learn how to transfer money to Webmoney and withdraw it from there.

How can I deposit funds?

It is quite simple to deposit money and replenish an electronic account. There are many ways to carry out these actions, they differ only in the amount of commission that the system takes and the duration of the wait. To find out how to deposit money on Webmoney, you need to log in to your account and go to the appropriate section of the menu. You can deposit funds into your account in various ways.

The most popular options for replenishing an electronic wallet are self-service terminals and the transfer of funds from a mobile phone account. It is only necessary to decide how to transfer money to Webmoney will be more convenient. So, about all the ways in order.

Top-up from a mobile phone account

You can transfer funds to an electronic wallet from any mobile phone account. To do this, you need to attach a number to your wallet in your account. Operations are carried out in rubles and hryvnias. In order to transfer funds, you must log in to your personal account using your username and password and create an application to replenish your account. At the same time, the mobile phone number is indicated as the source. The system processes the application and sends an SMS notification with the instruction to the specified number. In accordance with it, it is necessary to confirm the operation.

To date, payment can be made to customers of such mobile providers: Beeline, MTS and Megafon. Moreover, the system charges a commission of 8 to 13% of the transfer amount. This is the main disadvantage, since it is quickly possible to deposit money on Webmoney only with a serious commission.

Cash deposit through retail cash desks

In all cities of Russia, the WebMoney system has agreements with some retail chains, at the cash desks of which you can also replenish your wallet. As a rule, mobile phone shops and post offices act as reception points.

Money transfers

The service provides various methods of conducting operations within it. You can also replenish your e-wallet by money transfer. This is not the easiest way to transfer money to Webmoney.
