Hotel Ibis Al Barsha Hotel 3 * (United Arab Emirates, Dubai): photos and reviews of tourists

Ibis Al Barsha Hotel 3 * hotel offers its guests comfortable living conditions, as well as a huge variety of services.





Short hotel description

The hotel complex Ibis Al Barsha Hotel 3 * can offer travelers to stay in comfortable rooms that give a feeling of home comfort. This is due to the fact that all apartments have warm carpeting, as well as a large set of household appliances. The rooms have a large set of furniture that meets orthopedic and ergonomic standards. In addition, drinking water will be delivered to your apartment daily.

It is simply impossible to ignore national Arabic cuisine. The opportunity to get acquainted with local culinary traditions will be kindly provided by a luxurious restaurant that operates in the Ibis Al Barsha Hotel 3 *. There are also two bars in which there are quality drinks for every, even the most demanding, taste. Having visited the cafe in the hotel, you can taste the most unusual and delicious varieties of tea and coffee, as well as purchase a drink you like.

Ibis Al Barsha Hotel 3 * is characterized by a rather favorable location, because next to it there are not only attractions, but also all kinds of shopping and entertainment centers. In addition, there are shops in the hotel itself, so you will have no difficulty in acquiring souvenirs. Also, the hotel organizes trips for its guests to various points of sale of clothes, as well as to stores of fur and jewelry.





Hotel location

The resort is located in the famous Al Barsha quarter, which is about a kilometer from the central part of Dubai. To get from the airport to this hotel will have to overcome about 25 km. The hotel is located near the metro station "Sharaf", as well as 2 km from a large shopping center, which sells everything you can imagine. Businessmen will be interested in the fact that the hotel is located about 20 km from the Jebel Ali Special Economic Zone.

Resort Rooms

Only standard rooms can offer its guests the Ibis Al Barsha Hotel 3 * hotel. The photo of the apartments demonstrates conciseness and practicality and at the same time quite comfortable atmosphere in the rooms. They are designed to accommodate two adults, and it is also possible to provide additional space for the child. The room may have a large double bed or a pair of single beds.





In each apartment, guests have the opportunity to use the following amenities:

  • the bathroom has a shower, as well as a hairdryer, bath accessories and quality branded cosmetics;
  • You are given the opportunity to view a large package of satellite channels, among which are also Russian;
  • for international calls and communication with the administration there is a landline telephone;
  • if such a need arises, you can request an iron and an ironing board in the room.




Hotel Infrastructure

The Ibis Al Barsha Hotel 3 stars offers the following infrastructure facilities:

  • a spacious outdoor terrace intended for sunbathing or admiring beautiful city views;
  • the territory has its own nightclub, where famous DJs are constantly invited;
  • to pass the time, you have the opportunity to play darts;
  • the restaurant, bars, as well as a small cozy cafe are responsible for food and providing guests with drinks.
  • the entire hotel has wireless Internet access, which is free to use;
  • the hotel has its own car parking, where each guest can put a personal or rented car without paying an additional fee;
  • The reception desk is open around the clock so that you can ask your questions, as well as express wishes or requests;
  • there is a point in the lobby where you can exchange currency;
  • in the tourist office you can choose for yourself an excursion route;
  • it is possible to check in luggage;
  • in the laundry room quickly and efficiently remove stains from your clothes;
  • the hotel provides special facilities for conferences, business meetings, as well as banquets and celebrations;
  • there is a vending machine on each floor where you can buy coffee or refreshments;
  • The hotel has special non-smoking rooms.

Positive reviews

Many travelers from all over the world prefer to stay at the Ibis Al Barsha Hotel 3 *. Reviews about him contain a lot of information that reflects the positive side of the rest in this institution:

  • daily and very high-quality housekeeping, even if you do not leave a tip;
  • the internet works very well;
  • the beach is far enough from the hotel, so tourists are provided with a free shuttle;
  • a breakfast buffet is served with a huge selection of delicious dishes (but the assortment every day, unfortunately, does not change);
  • daily cleaners bring potable water to their rooms, as well as tea and coffee bags (even if previous supplies have not been used up yet);
  • At the reception there is a Russian-speaking employee.




Negative reviews

In addition to the positive comments, there are also comments on the Ibis Al Barsha Hotel 3 * hotel. Reviews of tourists contain a certain amount of negativity about the quality of service and the work of the hotel as a whole. Despite the fact that the advertisements stated carpet in the apartment, in some rooms it was not. Although the channel package is large, there is practically nothing to watch, since only Chanson is presented from Russian-language programs.

The windows do not open in the rooms, and therefore it can be very stuffy in the room (even despite the presence of air conditioning), and it is also impossible to dry things, towels are always wet. The hair dryer in the bathroom does not work very well, so the hair has to be dried for a very long time. The windows of the rooms located at the end of the building practically abut against the neighboring building, so ask for other numbers when checking in. The area in which the hotel is located is quite new, and therefore there is practically nowhere to walk there.
