Get by bus

From Novorossiysk bus station to Gelendzhik, buses leave every 2 hours, so you can leave without problems. The bus going to Gelendzhik calls at the railway station, but not the fact that there will be empty seats.

In order to get to the bus station, you need to take the trolley bus, which in the city runs along the only route. Therefore, sit on it boldly, and in 10-15 minutes you will be at the bus station. You can also use a taxi, but if you are not very loaded with luggage, then the best option is a trip on a trolley bus.

A bus "Novorossiysk - Gelendzhik" runs from the bus station. The first flight leaves at 11:24 local time, the last at 18:08. There are 7 flights a day.

In addition, you can leave the bus station by passing buses that go to Gelendzhik. These are buses from Tikhoretsk and Rostov-on-Don. They stop (if available) at the train station.

You can also leave for the Janhot from the bus station. The bus performs 5 flights per day. The first leaves at 06:15, the last at 19:10.

How to get to Gelendzhik by car

Getting to Gelendzhik by car is possible in about 40-60 minutes. The Gelendzhik-Novorossiysk road itself is good, but when choosing this type of transport, some features must be taken into account. Although the distance is small, it is necessary to go along the serpentine road, which starts right after Novorossiysk and ends near Kabardinka. The speed of movement on it is limited. If your journey takes place on the weekend, it must be borne in mind that most locals in cars rush to the sea at this time, the road is very busy, and there are even traffic jams.
