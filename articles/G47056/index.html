Powerful WiFi router for the home and its range

For many potential buyers, choosing a router for your home environment comes down to an affordable price and the number of gain antennas. After all, many users want the WiFi signal to be present in all rooms and work without fail. It is interesting what experts in the field of IT technologies think about this. From this article, the reader will learn how to choose a powerful WiFi router in the network equipment market. Recommendations, reviews and feedback from the owners will help the buyer decide on the purchase.





Drive, hold, watch and see

Marketing wars on the world and domestic markets have led to the fact that buyers in 99% of cases acquire equipment that in fact does not meet the objectives. This fact is discovered after the purchase, so most people just put up. Network experts suggest looking at buying a router from a different angle.

Many users unreasonably believe that the main criterion that a powerful WiFi router for home has is a radius of action. Naturally, devices with antennas attract the attention of buyers. But only few people pay attention to the packaging of the router, where it is written in black and white that the device works according to the international standard ISO IEEE 802.11. It turns out that the radius of action is the same for all routers: 150 meters in direct line of sight and 50 meters in the room.

Experts recommend installing the network device not near the computer, but placing it in the middle of the room (for example, in the corridor) to better cover all rooms or offices. Do not install the router on the floor or attach it to the ceiling. The signal must have a field for reflection from surfaces, which means that you need to place the router at a height of about 1.6-2.2 meters.
