Adelina hotels 4 * - comfort, variety and low prices

Speaking about the hotel Adelina Hotels 4 *, which was recently renamed the Club Eva Hotel, which is associated with a change of ownership, it is worth first talking about the resort where this hotel is located.

Turkler

Turkler (or Turkler) is a small place located near the world famous Alanya. Many tourists who visited this resort show a repeated desire to be there again. The picturesque nature and colorful atmosphere made Turkler a special holiday destination.



A bit of history

This area has a famous past. There are legends about Turkler. Indeed, in ancient times, this resort was a pirate den. Its favorable location on the peninsula was the cause of such circumstances. Pirate ships sailed from all over the Mediterranean to sell the loot, to rest, to hire sailors who were ready to serve under the pirate flag. But it was a long time ago.

Resort Location

Now Turkler is an excellent resort on the Antalya Riviera. Low cost of rest and excellent service with a developed tourist infrastructure - this is a vacation in the resort of Turkler. Many residents call this resort Incekum, in honor of the famous sandy beaches. On this stretch of coast stretch gorgeous areas of beaches, stretching over 8 kilometers, with golden soft sand. These beaches are able to compete with the famous Cleopatra beach.



All resort hotels are slightly offshore, which is not typical for Turkish resorts. However, this distance is small. On one coast of the peninsula there is a small port for yachts and boats. You can rent a boat there.

Turkler - a small place, everything is in full view. In the heart of the village are concentrated shops, markets, boutiques, cafes and restaurants. There are travel agencies offering cheap excursions to nearby attractions. The most popular cave trips where pirates once hid their treasures.

Turkler is a young resort, actively developing, designed at this stage for recreation of various contingents with a small budget. Even a holiday in a 5-star hotel in this resort is lower than in Bodrum and Belek, although its quality is not inferior.

Adelina Hotels 4 *

One of the most popular hotels in Turkler is the former Adelina Hotels 4. Reviews about this hotel complex are only the most positive. Tourists are attracted by the convenient location of the hotel, beautiful rooms, excellent service and good cuisine.

If we talk about this hotel, then it is worth mentioning its remoteness from the "center of civilization":

  • from Alanya - 17 km;
  • from Antalya - 117 km;
  • from the airport - 108 km;
  • from the large village of Konakly - 5 km.

Hotel Description

Club Eva Hotel 4 * was built in 1996. Constantly updated and undergone modifications. The names, owners, appearance of the buildings changed, but the essence remained the same:

  • close location to the sea;
  • good service;
  • large contiguous territory;
  • spacious rooms.

The last restoration was done in 2014. At the same time, all furniture and technical equipment was replaced. The external parts of the six three-story buildings of the hotel have undergone changes.



The territory of Adelina Hotels 4 * is 0.6 hectares. Not only the hotel buildings are located on it, but also an amphitheater, a small shop, a water pool with a slide, a spa center, an outdoor restaurant and recreation places in the shade of palm trees and trees.
