Oceanarium in Komsomol (Volgograd) - a fascinating place for leisure

Modern shopping centers have long ceased to be a simple set of stores in one room. As a rule, for a pleasant pastime with the whole family, shopping and entertainment are successfully adjacent to each other. The oceanarium in Komsomol (Volgograd) is an example of such a successful symbiosis.



Having opened its doors to visitors relatively recently (in 2013), it has already gained popularity among both residents and visitors. There is no stationary zoo in Volgograd . In order to see representatives of flora and fauna, one has to visit various mobile menageries, in which animals are often in a deplorable state.

The oceanarium in Komsomol (Volgograd) is a place where fish, turtles and even small sharks live in conditions close to real ones. The low price of admission allows you to visit it with the whole family. A big plus is the fact that for children under the age of three years, admission is not charged. And if you want to take a memory photo in such an interesting place or capture reptiles and exotic fish, for a small fee there is the possibility of using your own camera in a mode with the flash turned off so as not to frighten the animals.



Shopping and entertainment complex "Komsomol" in Volgograd has a good geographical location - it is located almost in the city center, in close proximity to the legendary Mamaev Kurgan. Thanks to such a successful neighborhood, it will not be difficult to include the aquarium in the tourist route through the sights of the city. In addition, it has something to see - a variety of aquarium fish, small sharks and crocodiles, giant turtles that you can touch. It is interesting to watch the crocodiles from the tunnel leading directly to the aviary - it is made of durable transparent material, standing in it, you experience an incredible adrenaline rush, while a toothy predator creeps past your face.



The oceanarium in "Komsomol" (Volgograd) is not much different from its counterparts in other cities, except for the size and short life. Every month, new residents and elements of the decor of the room appear in it. Many are delighted with the transparent floor-bridge, where you can see bright fish, small fountains, beautifully decorated halls. Children like to watch big turtles, crocodiles, a fur seal, which frolic in the water and waves fins to visitors.

The oceanarium in Komsomol (Volgograd), the photo of which will decorate any album, pleases its visitors regardless of their age. The oceanarium is represented by three rooms. Passing into the second of them past the glass ceiling and walls, thanks to which small colorful fish are seen floating above their heads, adults and children experience a lot of positive emotions.

The oceanarium in Komsomol (Volgograd), into the third hall of which there is a bridge with a transparent floor, is filled with aquariums with representatives of various oceans and seas, far from the middle Russian latitudes. Observations of its inhabitants will help to acquire new knowledge and broaden their horizons.
