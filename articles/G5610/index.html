DIY plaster crafts: we make an original home decoration

What could be more interesting than those crafts that were created independently? They are able not only to decorate the housing, but also serve as the subject of pride of the owner. In addition, with independent design, you can make such a thing that will be not only beautiful, but also unique, inimitable. But it should be understood that the creation of souvenirs requires increased accuracy and care. Yes, and imagination will need to be fully utilized to make the product truly original and eye-catching. So, the topic of our conversation today is crafts. You can create a real masterpiece with your own hands from plaster. Do not want to try?



Wonderful hobby

DIY plaster figurines are an interesting and fascinating hobby that will allow you to immerse yourself in the creative process, resulting in a wonderful decoration for your home or garden. In addition, gypsum figures will serve you for a very long time, in almost any environment, which cannot but please the creator of such products directly.

What is needed to create gypsum figures?

Not much is needed to translate what was conceived into reality. Everything can be handled with the help of improvised tools that are almost always available. If you feel the awakened talent in yourself, and are ready to prove yourself a sculptor, then you will need the following materials:

  1. Sculptural gypsum. This strong enough material is great for creating shapes. It can be purchased at specialized stores.
  2. Water. The most common, from the tap.
  3. Plasticine for modeling. You can use both a special and a children's product.
  4. Foil (the usual one is suitable for food).

In addition to the above consumables, to create DIY crafts from gypsum, you will need to stock up on the necessary tools. You will need:

The first steps in creating gypsum products

Having prepared everything you need for sculpting, you can go directly to the process itself, at the end of which gypsum crafts will decorate your home. Doing it yourself is not so difficult as it might seem at first glance. Of course, do not forget about accuracy so that they acquire the desired appearance.



The first thing to do is to put a board on a flat surface and mold the desired figure from plasticine. Using a thin knife, along the whole figure you need to outline the connector line. Next, along the intended line, gently push small pieces of foil. Naturally, they must be dense. Due to the foil it will be possible to prevent the adhesion of gypsum. After the solidification of the figure, the foil can be removed without applying much effort.

Pour water into the container and add the gypsum itself. In this case, it is necessary to gently stir the mixture until it reaches the state of fluid pastry for pancakes or thin sour cream. Only from such a mass can you create crafts with your own hands from gypsum, which will delight you with their beauty and durability.

Using a small brush, gypsum is applied to the plasticine figure, layer by layer. After the first layer has hardened, the next one is applied over it, then one more and one more.

Final stage





The number of layers that you need to apply in the process of creating DIY crafts from gypsum depends on what strength you want to give the product. After applying the last layer, you will need to leave the figure for a while, until it finally hardens. This can take about thirty minutes. After this time, it is necessary to remove all unnecessary from the figure and again postpone the work until it finally dries. After that, it will be possible to remove its plaster form from the plasticine base.

Ready-made, well-dried gypsum crafts emit a slight ringing when tapped. Figures can be decorated or left in their original form. In this case, it all depends on your imagination and creativity.

Create and enjoy!

DIY gypsum crafts, as a rule, are much more interesting and original than the standard ones purchased in the store. In addition, they can be made by the whole family, when everyone is able to add something of their own - unique, inimitable and unexpected. We hope that your gypsum crafts, the master class on the creation of which was given above, will become a worthy decoration of the interior and will take their rightful place among other products created with their own hands.

In the end, I would like to wish you good luck in this creative and interesting process, we hope it will bring a lot of pleasure and give you a good mood.
