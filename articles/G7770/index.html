What should I think about in advance?

Experienced knitters claim that they pick up the thread and the hook, focusing on the inner flair, and the drawing is taken directly from the head. So, in order to determine these two criteria, you need to answer a simple question: “What season does it take?” After all, if you want to get a summer hat, you should pick up thin threads. For example, Iris threads praise a huge number of experienced knitters. And all because this yarn is thin enough, delicate and pleasant to the touch. If the reader is interested in crochet beret technology, which he will wear in the fall season or even winter, it is worth paying attention to wool yarn, mohair, angora. And for the spring, it is better to knit an acrylic or nylon product.

The scheme was presented above, well, now we will consider the technology of crocheting a beret for beginners:

  1. So, this is the most elementary of the presented headdress. After all, he knits with a series of air loops and double crochets.
  2. To get started, take a ball of prepared threads and crochet eight loops out of them.
  3. Then we connect them into a ring.
  4. Then we bind them, making one air loop, and then fourteen single crochet. And close the row, fixing the last loop of the circle.
  5. Next, we knit, focusing on the scheme. The beginning of the row is where the air loops are indicated. They are depicted using circles.
  6. We knit four air loops.
  7. And then from the first loop of the previous row we pull out a new one, making a double crochet. Then we knit an air loop. We repeat these manipulations fifteen times. And fix the last loop of the circle.
  8. We pass to the second and knit three air loops.
  9. After doing another, followed by a double crochet, another air and two double crochets, derived from one loop of the previous row, between which you need to make one air loop. We repeat the described actions six more times.
  10. And then we knit an aerial, double crochet, still aerial, again a double crochet and aerial. Fasten the circle.
