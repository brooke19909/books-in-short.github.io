Upper Bor (Tyumen): hot spring, relaxation and prices

Upper Bor (Tyumen) is a unique natural corner, protected from the harsh winds by centuries-old pine trees. It is located on the shore of. Crooked. The visiting card of this amazing place has long been hot springs. For this reason Tyumen (Upper Bor) attracts not only residents of nearby cities - Yekaterinburg, Chelyabinsk, Novosibirsk, Kurgan, but also guests from more distant regions of Russia. In addition, foreign tourists are also not averse to know all the delights of a year-round bathing season.

Source at the hotel complex

This is an outdoor thermal pool. Its area is four hundred square meters. There is a division into how many zones - swimming, children‚Äôs, Jacuzzi. In the pool, the temperature is constantly maintained within 39-40 degrees of heat. Depth in the adult zone - 150 cm, and in the nursery - 70 cm.





"Verkhny Bor" (Tyumen) is a recreation center, which was designed and built according to the most modern technologies. So, when working on the cleaning system of the aforementioned thermal pool, the best practices of Italians, Spaniards and French were used. The uniqueness lies in the following: mineral water is purified without breaking the structure, in addition, it is thoroughly disinfected. The specialists of the recreation center monitor the quality of the liquid every three hours, and the sanitary and epidemiological station does this twice a week. The presence of a comfortable relaxation zone, showers and locker rooms in the immediate vicinity of the pool will allow you not to worry about anything during your vacation.

Working hours

The pool, located in the hotel complex, opens at nine in the morning. Sanitary days are held every Monday (from nine to four) and Thursday (from nine to two).

Spa hotel

The pool and hotel "Source" were modernized in 2010. Since then, guests of the Upper Bor (Tyumen) base have been pleased to use the services of massage and beauty parlors, a solarium, a font, a jacuzzi, a hamam, infrared and Finnish saunas. On the second floor of the updated SPA-hotel "Source" there are fifteen rooms of various categories of comfort. This complex works at the same time as described above. The only difference is the sanitary days schedule (Tuesday from eight to six, Friday from nine to two).





Baths and saunas

The recreation center "Verkhny Bor" (Tyumen) has two classic Russian baths (1 hour - 1400 rubles), heated not with electric heating elements, but with wood. Steam rooms are located on the shore of. Crooked. Near each of them there are places for cars, gazebos and barbecue facilities.

In winter, ice-holes are made on the lake so that there is an opportunity to plunge after staying in the steam room. By prior reservation, you can agree on the provision of the services of a bathhouse attendant.

Connoisseurs of dry heat prefer to visit Finnish saunas (1 hour - 800 rubles), equipped with swimming pools. On the second floor of the building there are rest rooms. While in the bath or sauna, you can use the restaurant, ordering food and drinks by phone.

Outdoor recreation

Upper Bor (Tyumen) offers a popular picnic service. In total, there are twenty-six wooden arbors in the base. Their capacity is different - from five to twenty-five vacationers. All arbors are equipped with comfortable benches and tables, and barbecue facilities are provided. The recreation area is regularly treated for ticks.

Accommodation options

You can opt for a spa hotel called "Source". In its first building there are 15 rooms, and in the second - 40. Apartments for two, four and eight vacationers are offered.

The hotel complex with the uncomplicated name "Outdoor Thermal Pool" has eight comfortable rooms. All of them are in a separate wing of the building. Parking is available.

You can choose one of the cottages. Comfort level and capacity vary. Various options are offered - both for noisy companies, and for couples who want to retire.





Sosnovaya Hotel is located in the very center of the base. This is a beautifully furnished wooden structure on two floors. Nineteen economy rooms are available. Capacity - from two to six people. This hotel is usually chosen by large groups of vacationers.

Expenses

You can enjoy swimming in the pools for 900-1100 rubles on weekdays and for 1100-1300 on weekends. Children and senior citizens receive discounts.

As for accommodation, it will cost from 2,300 to 3,000 rubles at the Sosnovoy Hotel. per person per day. For the cottage will have to pay from 2450 to 4250 rubles. You will be charged from 2450 to 3950 rubles / day for accommodation in the hotel complex "Outdoor Thermal Pool", at the SPA-hotel "Source" the cost of rooms is the same.

Keep in mind that on the basis of "Verkhny Bor" (Tyumen), room prices are the highest when placed for one day. Children under six years of age live free of charge, but they will have to pay extra for their meals.

What is included in the room rate?

- Three meals a day.

- Use of thermal pools.

- Visit to the spa.

Bora Bora

Recently, a beach club was opened at the recreation center. It is aimed at an audience of different ages. The animation team is ready to entertain holidaymakers from 14 to 20 hours daily. After eight in the evening, fascinating karaoke battles will start. The beach opens if the weather is sunny and the air temperature is not lower than 22 heat.

How to eat?

There is a restaurant complex at the base. On the ground floor there is a multifunctional conference room, on the first - a cafe and a children's room, on the second - a banquet room with a capacity of 350 people. In addition, there are three more cafes - at the SPA hotel, an outdoor thermal pool, as well as on the beach.





Things to do?

In summer, you can sunbathe on a large sanctified beach, play volleyball, basketball or football. Hire rings, balls, rollers, skateboards, bicycles, badminton, mattresses and bicycles.

In winter, slides and skating rinks are poured, a ski track is equipped. There are skates, sledges, snow scooters, skis and sledges at the rental point.





In bad weather, you can play billiards, table tennis, paintball or darts.

Conclusion

The main pride of this recreation center is a hot spring. Upper Bor (Tyumen) is also famous for its modern spa complex, real Russian baths and comfortable saunas. It is noteworthy that tourists here are happy to receive daily and around the clock.
