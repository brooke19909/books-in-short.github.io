Hotel address "Chocolate" in Tolyatti

The hotel is located in the central part of the city on Avtostroiteley Street, 11B. The easiest way to get here is to order a transfer (from the airport - 1500 rubles, from the bus station in Avtozavodsky district - 300 rubles). Another option is to call a taxi. If you want to get to the hotel on your own, use the following instructions:

  • From the central bus station - minibus number 93, 96.
  • From the bus station in the Avtozavodsky district - minibus No. 20, 93, 96, 116, 118, 127, 303, 311.
  • From the railway station - minibus number 127, 303.

Hotel Restaurant

The Hotel Chocolate in Togliatti has a cozy restaurant, Dolce Vita. This is a great place to relax after a busy day. The luxurious dining room is decorated in a classic style. Subdued light and comfortable soft sofas create a warm and comfortable atmosphere that contributes to a friendly conversation or a romantic dinner. The banquet room is designed for 60 people.

Guests can enjoy hearty, nutritious dishes as well as diet or vegetarian products from a chef with over 15 years of experience. The restaurant operates according to the following schedule:

  • Buffet breakfast from 07:00 to 11:00.
  • Business lunch from 12:00 to 16:00.
  • Menu service from 16:00 to 23:00.
