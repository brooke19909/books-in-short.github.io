Veliky Novgorod hotels: addresses, description

For a long time, Veliky Novgorod was considered a rich city. Trade routes through rivers connected the Black, Caspian and Baltic Seas. Handicraft actively developed - the work of local craftsmen was in demand in Europe.

In Veliky Novgorod two centuries earlier than in Paris, wooden pavements appeared. In the XII century there were already plumbing and sewage.

There are many surprising facts in history. For example, the city managed to avoid the Mongol invasion, thanks to which unique monuments of ancient Russian architecture were preserved.

We are talking about the St. Sophia Cathedral (1050), the Cathedral of the Nativity of the Virgin Mary of the St. Anthony Monastery (1119), the St. Nicholas and Dvorishchensky Cathedral (1136), the Paraskiev Friday Church on the Market (1207) and other ancient temples.



Talking about the sights of the city, it is worth mentioning the Novgorod Kremlin, the Faceted Chamber, the Derevyanitsky Monastery. The list can be continued for a long time, but the main idea is the popularity of this destination among travelers. Fortunately, hotels in Veliky Novgorod are ready to receive a large number of guests.
