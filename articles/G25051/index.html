Romance Club Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Marmaris): description, service, photos and reviews

Today, Turkey is one of the first places in the tourism sector. Millions of people from all over the world come here every year to enjoy the gentle subtropical sun, marine tranquility and hospitable service. The Romance Club hotel 3 * in Marmaris won a lot of praise. We will get acquainted with the description of the service. What is remarkable for the surroundings? And what are the reviews of Russian tourists about the hotel?



Marmaris Features

The city is located in the south-west of Turkey, where the Mediterranean and Aegean seas merge. It has been open as a resort since the 1980s. Marmaris is surrounded on all sides by mountains so that it is a bay, protected from cold winds. The nature of the Turkish city is fascinating. Here, the majesty of mountain landscapes, and the refreshing greens of forest groves, and the colorful extravaganza of exotic flowers and shrubs. All this is originally combined with historical monuments, modern attractions and architecture. Currently, Marmaris is one of the most popular Turkish resorts. In 2012, it attracted nearly two million tourists from all over the world.

Hotel location

There are three ways to get to the hotel. Just three kilometers away is the marina. It is convenient for tourists who travel by sea from Greece to Turkey. From the airport in Dalaman to the Romance Club hotel 3 * (Marmaris) the distance is 95 kilometers. You can overcome it by bus or minibus (dolmushi). The third option is the bus station in the north-east of the city. It connects Marmaris with Antalya, Dutch, Mugla, Dalaman and other resorts.



Description

Description Romance Club hotel 3 * should start with a well-groomed, cozy adjacent territory, reminiscent of a paradise with exotic palm trees and cobbled paths. The hotel is guarded around the clock, so guests can feel safe.

The three buildings of the complex frame a spacious courtyard, in the center of which a swimming pool flaunts. The balconies are decorated in a Greek style. At the entrance, guests are greeted by friendly staff. Reception according to the rules of the service works without interruptions. The interior of the hall and restaurant is light, spacious and sophisticated. The rooms of the complex are in the same spirit. At night, the hotel is illuminated with colorful lanterns, which creates the atmosphere of a fairy tale.

Accommodation options

In total, the Romance Club hotel 3 * hotel has 59 rooms. They are divided into two types: standard and family. In the standard maximum three people can be accommodated (2 + 1). Family rooms, in turn, distinguish between one-room (1 bedroom + living room) for five people and two-room (2 bedrooms + living room) for seven people.



Each room is equipped with air conditioning, a fridge and a minibar. It is connected to satellite TV (catches one Russian channel), a direct dial telephone and free Wi-Fi. The rooms are furnished with comfortable new furniture, a balcony and a bathroom with a hairdryer, toiletries and towels. The family rooms have kitchenware and an electric kettle. On request, cots are available. Safe is paid. Rooms are cleaned daily; bed linen and towels are changed twice a week.

The view from the windows of the rooms on the one hand reveals a serene sunny landscape of the sea, on the other hand it allows you to enjoy the freshness of the green area and view the surroundings.

Nutrition

In the hotel Romance Club hotel 3 * meals are provided by the program "All inclusive". The buffet is open from 8.00 to 23.00. Snacks and drinks can be ordered to your room if desired. The menu in the hotel restaurant matches European cuisine. There are dishes of fish, chicken, stewed vegetables, salads. Breakfast includes dairy complexes, various types of cheese. Everything according to the rules of the service is prepared from fresh products. There are special high chairs for feeding children (free of charge).



There is also a poolside coffee bar on site. Its menu has a fairly wide range of cocktails and snacks for children and adults. You can try Turkish delicacies only outside the complex. The doors of Marmaris cafes and restaurants are open to visitors from early morning until late at night.

Entertainment and sports

Service Romance Club hotel 3 * has a number of entertainment. The list of paid services includes a sauna and a hammam, a jacuzzi and relaxing massage, a gym and billiards. You can also rent bicycles and take a fascinating journey through Marmaris. A refreshment will be made by relaxing at the hotel's pool. Umbrellas, chaise lounges are provided free of charge. In the evening, you can play darts and visit the cinema (also free). True, the films are in Turkish or in English. For children, entertainment is also provided in the form of a playground and water slides.



Excursion service

Fans of hiking can book an excursion to the historical sites of Marmaris with the help of the Romance Club hotel 3 * administration. One of the main attractions of the city is the old castle fort. Today it is open to visitors as a museum. Young people will be interested in walking along the famous bar street. Here you can enjoy a variety of drinks and cocktails, try national cuisine. The street of bars is located in the eastern part of the city, always lively and rich in adventure. But an unforgettable experience for children will remain after visiting the Aqua Dream water park. It is located on a hill, offering stunning views of the resort and the sea.

Services

The list of services of the Romance Club hotel 3 * in Marmaris is varied and designed for guests with different goals. So the equipment and conditions at the hotel include accommodation for disabled people. Business people here will find a business center where you can hold a conference or online negotiations. In addition, there is a currency exchange, free parking and car rental on site. The hotel complex offers medical assistance and laundry services. The shop on the ground floor has swimming supplies: masks, flippers, beach shales, swimwear and more.



Beach

The Romance Club hotel 3 * (Marmaris) has its own beach. It is just 100 meters from the hotel. Sandy-stony shore, never seen storms. Due to the status of the bay, the water here is always several degrees warmer than in the open sea. In summer, the air temperature in Marmaris ranges from +30 to +35. The tourist season lasts from the second half of May to October. The average water temperature is +23 degrees.

Sun loungers and umbrellas are provided on the beach. For entertainment, you can book a fishing and diving tour. Near the coast there are three picturesque islands. Visiting them can also bring a lot of pleasure, especially to romantic couples. But truly magical will be a voyage on a yacht. For children, the beach has a water park Marmaris. You can eat deliciously without returning to the hotel. For this, various bars, cafes and pizzerias are open.



Reviews

The best advertising of any resort are, as you know, not vivid brochures of agencies, but real travel reviews. About Romance Club hotel 3 * there were mixed opinions. Many vacationers took into account the status of three stars and positively evaluated the following points:

  • Accommodation by numbers, according to vouchers, is fast, without delays and a long registration of documents. This is especially nice after a long flight and arrival at the hotel late at night.
  • The cleanliness and grooming of the surrounding area is respected. Pool water is filtered daily. There were no complaints about the service in the cafe-bar.
  • The interior of the Romance Club hotel 3 * is called luxurious by some tourists. The rooms in the hotel are spacious, comfortably furnished. There is tile or laminate flooring. All necessary attributes are present. Cleaning and linen change is done according to the rules of the service. At the request of tourists to change the room everything happens quickly and at no extra charge.
  • Local entertainment has a relatively low cost. They are designed for visitors of different ages, social status and preferences.




By cons of the service, Russian tourists attributed:

  • Staff knowledge of only Turkish and English. When communicating with the administration of the service, misunderstandings may arise on this basis. And hotel translators are not provided. Although outside the Romance Club hotel 3 * in Marmaris, you can find Russian-speaking operators.
  • Water at the hotel is sometimes intermittently supplied. This is especially inconvenient when tourists return from the beach, want to take a shower faster and go down to the restaurant. There is also a recurring unpleasant smell of sewage in the toilet rooms of the rooms.
  • The Internet signal is weak in the territory of the Romance Club hotel 3 *. And every day, guests have to re-enter the password to connect to the Wi-Fi network.
  • The younger generation found the service more focused on family vacations, as there are no noisy discos and animations here.
  • The road to the beach takes no more than three minutes. But the busy road, near which the hotel is located, makes the path difficult. Tourists warn to be extremely careful. Often flickering pedestrian scooters are not allowed.
  • The beach is clean but small in size. Guests of two complexes have a rest on it (Romance Club and Romance Beach). Because of this, sun loungers are close to each other.
  • Food received a double assessment. Some tourists liked the dishes and menus of the hotel restaurant. Others remained dissatisfied with the monotony of choice and the look of the products themselves.
  • In addition, in the middle of the season the hotel has a large influx of tourists, which sometimes creates inconvenience, queues and confusion in the distribution of rooms. People who got into the Romance Club Hotel 3 * at the end of the season due to the small number of tourists in the midst of relaxation may be asked to move to another hotel. It also causes some inconvenience.

In general, the Romance Club hotel 3 * in Turkey is fully consistent with its status both in price and quality of services. And the cozy and picturesque Marmaris is ready to fill in the shortcomings in entertainment and the kitchen.
