Mina Mark Beach Resort (Hurghada) 4 *, Hurghada, Egypt: hotel reviews

Mina Mark Beach Resort (Hurghada) 4 * is a resort hotel located on the sea coast in the popular Egyptian city of Hurghada. This place is popular among visitors, as there are many interesting sights.





First impression

Mina Mark Beach Resort (Hurghada) 4 * consists of two buildings. The hotel is elegantly designed and immediately attracts attention. Definitely the first impression sets in a positive way, and, I must say, it is not deceptive. It is worth noting another important point - this hotel complex is located in the very center of the city, in an area called Sakkala. Tourists are attracted by the fact that the hotel is located right on the first coastline. Only five minutes from the beach are just a few minutes away! But that is not all. In the immediate vicinity is the airport - a little less than ten minutes by car. Also nearby is the promenade, which is popular, especially among young people. It is here that you can dine in the best restaurants, visit upscale nightlife venues of an entertaining nature or just take a walk in the picturesque surroundings.





Entertainment on site

Mina Mark is not only a great option for staying there during your stay in Egypt. The hotel is also a place where you can fully relax. After all, all the necessary conditions are created for this. There is a spacious outdoor pool that is open throughout the year, a sun terrace, a garden where you can enjoy peace and quiet. Sports lovers can play billiards or table tennis, and visit the fitness center equipped with all necessary equipment. And, of course, while staying here on vacation, it is necessary to allocate at least one day for water sports. It can be diving, diving or surfing. And guests who want to completely relax will enjoy the Turkish bath, sauna or the services of the SPA salon and wellness center. All this is offered to the attention of guests who have settled in the hotel Mina Mark Beach Resort (Hurghada) 4 *.

For the smallest

People who come to rest with their children may not worry about the fact that the kids will be bored to be at the hotel. After all, the administration made sure that the smallest travelers were interested here. A spacious playground is equipped for children, there is also a mini club. In addition, young travelers will definitely enjoy the animated shows held at the hotel daily. It is also worth noting that if a child who came with his parents is not yet 12 years old, then he can live for free. If you need an extra bed, then you need to arrange this with the administration in advance. Only then the cost of living the child will be 50% of the amount paid for the placement of an adult family member.





Nutrition

In Mina Mark Beach Resort (Hurghada) 4 * there are two main restaurants - Sunshine and Water Blue. In any of these establishments, guests can have breakfast, lunch and, of course, dinner. Pleasant atmosphere and excellent service are provided. Guests are served buffet style at these establishments, and there is also an open buffet. There is also a poolside bar in the complex. He also works on the All inclusive system, and all day long. There is another bar on the beach. The hotel guests can order any alcoholic or soft drink and snacks. The assortment is diverse, so that everyone will find something that will appeal to him. And, of course, as in any self-respecting hotel, there is a lobby bar. It is an ideal meeting place, in which there is always a wonderful atmosphere, European drinks and friendly staff. It should also be noted that at the request of guests, breakfast can be delivered to your room. And if guests follow a special diet, then in MinaMark Beach Resort 4 * they will be offered a special diet menu (upon individual request).





Service and maintenance

A person who intends to check into a particular hotel usually wants to know what level of service awaits him. After all, this is indeed a very important aspect. Mina Mark Beach Resort offers free parking, room service, concierge and luggage storage, and express check-in or check-out. And this is not the whole list. Dry cleaning, laundry, ironing, photocopy and fax services are all offered here. Needless to say, the complex has a beauty salon, halls for banquets and conferences, and a large souvenir shop.

Finally, it is worth noting that the administration even provides VIP services. The only thing that can cause some inconvenience is paid Wi-Fi. But the prices are small, only 5 dollars per day (or two dollars per hour). Guests can also be delivered a press in the room and provided with a heating system. And for guests who smoke, in Mina Mark 4 * separate places are intended. Since on the territory of the hotel smoking is prohibited. In general, all the conditions for a full stay are created here.





Terms of accommodation

This four-star hotel can be safely attributed to the budget, because the prices for holidays in here are really low. A week of vacation for two in a double room will cost only 32 thousand rubles. And this is an all-inclusive system! If you book such a room for one person, you will need to pay 25 thousand rubles. However, this is not so much, if you compare Mina Mark Beach Resort (Hurghada) 4 * with other hotels. By the way, for an improved double room you only need to overpay 2 thousand rubles. Deluxe apartments in this hotel complex cost 41,000 rubles for two (also for 7 nights). If you book such a room for three, you will need to pay 59 thousand rubles. As you can see, the prices are really reasonable. That is why the hotel has become so popular among visitors. What about the rooms? All of them are cozy, spacious, decorated in pleasant colors and equipped with comfortable furniture. All necessary amenities (air conditioning, telephone, TV, hairdryer, Wi-Fi, etc.) are also provided to guests. There is also a bathroom, toilet and terrace. When booking, guests can indicate their personal preference, for example, the number of beds or the view from the window.





Comments of tourists

And finally, a few words about what travelers leave about MinaMark Beach Resort 4 * reviews. Tourists vacationing in this place note that here is a real paradise, and for a small price. The clear sea, its own beach and a restaurant with its varied menu deserve special attention. Many guests are pleased to note that this place is very friendly to hotel guests. The attendants are also very responsible: the maids are cleaned regularly and in good faith, the apartments are always clean. In addition, there are many Russian-speaking people, which cannot but rejoice. In a word, this is a hotel for those people who want to enjoy all the delights of a vacation at a low price.
