Water Park in Gorky Park - a paradise in Almaty

Without exception, everyone loves going to the water park in Gorky Park . Indeed, it is good not only for children, but also for adults, because skiing from high slides leaves no one indifferent.





Water Park in Gorky Park

In fact, this amusement park is called the Eighth Wonder of the World, but people often call it differently. It is located in the southern capital of Kazakhstan, the city of Almaty. By the way, the water park is the largest and most famous in the city. Perhaps it's all about the location. After all, it is located right in the center of the city, and perhaps in the fact that hills and trees are green around the hills. If you add to this the local climate, mostly sunny and warm, you get an almost paradise. You can find a water park simply, its address: Almaty, st. Gogol, 1.

The institution itself has both open and closed areas, on which there are 8 fun water slides of varying difficulty and 6 pools. Outdoor pools are open from early spring to early fall. The institution can be visited from 11 to 22 hours. So, on the territory you can choose such popular slides as "Kamikaze", "Black Hole", "Abyss", as well as several multi-colored slides. The water park in Gorky Park also offers visitors a wave pool, saunas, a jacuzzi, a spa and opportunities for fun sports.

Separate shallow pools are provided for young children, in which small slides are installed and waterfalls are created.

Near the water park there is a whole entertainment complex, which includes a children's town, dinopark, extreme park and many shops and restaurants for every taste.





Cost

The prices for tickets to the water park are quite moderate. So, an adult entrance ticket to the pools will cost 4500 tenge, which is approximately equal to 850 rubles, and it will be possible to ride a roller coaster for 5000 tenge.

Many different slides for children and adults are offered by the water park in Gorky Park. Discounts are valid for children. So, the cost of admission to children's pools will be 3300 tenge, and access to Multislide, Kamikaze and the Abyss can be obtained for 3800 tenge. At the same time, children under 1 year old can go to the water park for free. Please note that the difference in price does not depend on the age of the child, but on his height. So, children are considered persons below 150 cm.

Also, when visiting, it should be borne in mind that the amount of the entrance ticket may differ on weekdays and weekends.





Reviews

Apparently, visitors are always glad that they chose a water park in Gorky Park for relaxation. Reviews only confirm this. So, visitors emphasize that in the summer it is a pleasure to walk here, because the territory is simply surrounded by greenery and pleasing to the eye. Only some visitors rate the park as bad, mostly they associate it with prices. However, the vast majority of the work of the water park, the staff and the rides themselves are pretty.

The water park in Gorky Park is a place where visitors mostly go with their children. The Russians especially note in the comments that the territory is clean and not drunk, unlike domestic resorts.
