Hotel "Otium Aloha" (Sharm-El-Sheikh, Egypt): reviews, photos

If you like a beach holiday on the shores of the Red Sea and prefer comfortable but not expensive hotels, then Otium Aloha (Sharm El Sheikh) can be a great option for a vacation stay in Egypt.



Location

This beachfront hotel is located near Ras Um Sid Bay, four kilometers from the center of Sharm El Sheikh and seven kilometers from Naami Bay. The nearest airport is 18 kilometers away.

Otium Hotel Aloha (Sharm El Sheikh): reviews of Russian tourists

It's no secret that modern travelers with great responsibility come to the process of choosing a hotel to stay in a particular country in the world. Indeed, the quality of rest depends on this in many respects. Therefore, tourists today do not spare time and effort to collect information about a particular hotel of interest to them. At the same time, they pay great attention to the study of reviews of real people who have already been here. After all, this allows you to learn not only about the advantages, but also about the shortcomings of the hotel. To save you time and effort, we offer you the generalized comments of travelers from Russia who have recently stayed at Otium Aloha. Immediately, we note that almost all the guests were not completely disappointed in the choice made. On the contrary, they consider this hotel to be in accordance with the declared four-star category and price. But we learn about everything in more detail.



Number of rooms

Judging by the reviews, our compatriots were quite satisfied with the apartments offered to them in Otium Aloha (Sharm-El-Sheikh). According to tourists, one cannot count on special luxury here, however, the rooms have comfortable high-quality furniture, working equipment. If some kind of breakdown occurs, then after contacting the reception the master will solve the problem as soon as possible. The guests did not make any special complaints about cleaning the rooms. According to them, the maids carried out their duties quite efficiently and regularly. So, cleaning was carried out every day, towels and bed linen were updated several times a week.



Population

Judging by the comments of tourists, when you arrive at this hotel early, in most cases you should not hope that you can get the keys to your room right away. As a rule, you will have to wait for the checkout time. However, this does not mean at all that you will have to sit for hours at the reception waiting for check-in. So, the bracelets put to hotel guests, administrators will put on you immediately after arrival. Thanks to this, you can use the entire infrastructure of the hotel - go for breakfast or lunch, swim in the pool, enjoy a drink in the bar or go straight to the beach. By the way, the Otium Aloha Hotel (Sharm-El-Sheikh), reviews, practices the same approach to its guests on the day of their departure. So, if you plan to leave the hotel, for example, in the evening, then you will have to vacate the room before noon. However, the remaining hours, you again can enjoy the same opportunities as the rest of the tourists, because no one will take off your bracelets. Therefore, many of our compatriots did not find the need to renew their rooms for an additional fee.



Nutrition

Despite the fact that not all tourists were satisfied with the work of the restaurants in Otium Aloha, most of the guests still did not express any complaints on this item. So, according to them, the menu here, of course, does not reach luxury hotels. But the cost of living in this hotel is much lower than in five-star luxury establishments. As most travelers note in their comments, the food in Otium Aloha is organized at a very decent level. So, visitors to the restaurant always have the opportunity to treat themselves to excellently prepared dishes of meat, poultry and fish, as well as soups, snacks, salads, excellent pastries and a variety of desserts. Fresh vegetables and fruits are constantly available. You can eat at the hotel not only during breakfast, lunch and dinner. So, at 11 a.m. a brunch is organized in the beach bar, after a few hours guests are offered pizza and hamburgers, and between 15:00 and 17:00 tourists are treated to delicious ice cream.



Sea

As our compatriots note in their reviews, the Otium Aloha Hotel (Sharm El Sheikh) will surely appeal to beach lovers. So, tourists call the big plus of this hotel complex the fact that, thanks to a fairly compact territory, from any residential building, you can reach the coast in a maximum of seven minutes by an unhurried step. The beach here is quite large. Loungers and umbrellas, according to travelers, are in abundance here, so there is no talk of the need to occupy a deck chair in the early morning. You can enter the water either directly from the shore or by going down the ladder from the pier. In the first case, however, it is advisable to have special shoes on your feet, since there are corals at the bottom. Parents with young children and people who swim poorly entered the sea from the shore. If you go down to the water from the pier, then very close you will find a large and beautiful coral reef with a lot of interesting inhabitants. So, according to tourists, here you can see a variety of bright fish, as well as a sea turtle, moray eel, etc. Therefore, going to the beach, be sure to bring a mask for snorkeling.
