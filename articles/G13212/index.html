Georgia Hotel 3 * (Greece, Rhodes): reviews of tourists

Greece is an extremely popular resort for tourists. Thousands of tourists come to this country annually from literally all over the world. Of course, Greece and our domestic holidaymakers also liked it. One of the most popular resorts in this country is Rhodes. On this island, among other things, you can rent a very comfortable accommodation. For example, quite good reviews of tourists were earned by Georgia Hotel 3 *.

Where is the hotel situated?

This budget complex is located on the western side of the island of Rhodes, about 1000 meters from a convenient pebble beach on a small hill . A few kilometers from the hotel is the popular Greek resort of Tholos (Theologos) . The nearest restaurant and shopping center are literally a 20-minute walk away.





Rhodes International Airport is approximately 8 km from the hotel. Tourists who book rooms in this complex can reach their destination in literally 15-20 minutes. Most often , the hotel operator , including the Georgia Hotel 3 * (Tholos) , is transported by the tour operator who sold the ticket. But of course, if you wish, you can get to the hotel and take a taxi. Also, many tourists right at the airport of Rhodes rent a car. In the future, it can also be used for trips to various sights of the island. Exact hotel address: Theologos, Rhodes Island, Greece.

General description of the hotel

The Georgia Hotel 3 * accepts guests in two convenient low-rise buildings like a bungalow. The facades of the complex are painted in traditional for hotels in Greece, white and blue. This hotel was built recently - in 2007. At the same time, in 2009 large-scale repairs were carried out here.

The hotel courtyard is decorated with a pleasant to the eyes and a very even pink coating. Everywhere on the territory of the hotel lawns are planted and southern trees planted. During a storm, guests can take a dip in the small pool.

According to most tourists, Georgia Hotel 3 * hotels are well suited for both individual and family vacations. If you wish, you can rent rooms here for those who came to Rhodes in the company of friends.

Georgia Hotel 3 *: beach hotel reviews

Hotel guests need to go to the sea, as already mentioned, no more than 15-20 minutes. First, tourists have to go down a rather steep hill. After 250 meters, it ends and a flat path begins. Former hotel guests do not consider the route to the beach to be especially picturesque. However, the sea is always visible ahead. Therefore, it will be difficult to get lost even for beginners on the road. The shortest road to the sea from the hotel leads to a wild beach. Reviews about him on the web are only good. Its length and width are large. And therefore, there are never many people here and you can relax separately from everyone.

The beach is opposite the hotel with pebbles and stones. Therefore, walking along the shore here and swimming in the sea is better in shales. But, judging by the reviews of tourists, the water in it is always clean and warm. No dangerous animals in the sea in this place does not swim.
