Samsung Galaxy S3: owner reviews and smartphone features

Smartphones these days will not see anyone. Until relatively recently, these gadgets were perceived by a large number of consumers as something new, supernatural and inaccessible to the average Russian, but today it is very difficult to imagine your life without them. These stylish and versatile devices will help pass the time on a trip or in long lines, and a large number of different features makes them really indispensable.





The South Korean company Samsung is one of the manufacturers of mobile equipment. A few years ago, mobile phones manufactured by the corporation from South Korea were not of good quality, quickly failed and were extremely unreliable. For several years, the South Korean manufacturer managed to make a significant leap in its development, increase production volumes and significantly improve the quality of its products. All this allowed Samsung to become one of the leading smartphone manufacturers for the first time in many years.

One of the most popular line of smartphones of the brand allowed the company to take a leading position in the global mobile industry. One of the most successful gadgets of the South Korean production Samsung Galaxy-S3, reviews of which are mostly positive, will be discussed in this article.

Device delivery set

In most cases, the Samsung Galaxy-S3 package reviews are positive. According to the owners of this stylish device, together with a smartphone, the user receives almost everything that is needed to work with him. The Samsung Galaxy-S3 comes with the following items:

  • the phone itself;
  • charger to it;
  • user's manual;
  • USB cable
  • wired headphones;
  • lithium ion battery.




Design

The appearance of the device is one of its significant drawbacks (according to the owners of the gadget). The smartphone looks too simple, its design is almost copied from previous models. Nevertheless, several color schemes, a thin body that fits well enough in the hand and the relatively small dimensions of the device as a whole create a pretty good impression of the gadget.

This model is being promoted under a rather interesting motto of the unity of nature and technology, so we can assume that the rounded shape of the device is somewhat similar to a stone off the sea coast. The pattern covering the back of the device also enhances the impression. The smartphone case is presented in white, blue and black colors. All three versions look quite good and it is really difficult to choose an obvious favorite among them.

Dimensions

As for the dimensions of the flagship smartphone of 2012, it must be said that they are relatively small - 13.66 by 7.06 by 0.88 centimeters with a device weight of 133 grams. As you can see, the device turned out to be quite thin, and the small mass of the Samsung Galaxy-S3 also pleases. The device lies quite well in the hand, it easily fits in a trouser pocket, does not interfere with walking and does not take up too much space.

Display





We are all starting to get used to the super-large display diagonals of mobile devices operating under the Android platform. The Samsung Galaxy-S3 phone was no exception. And although the diagonal of its screen is not 6, but "only" 4.8 inches, it clearly cannot be called small. Nevertheless, such a display, if we take into account the reviews of the owners of Samsung Galaxy-S3, was to the taste of many buyers.

Main and front cameras

Nowadays, one of the main criteria for competition between modern gadgets is the quality of the camera. The best devices shoot at about the same level. Most users like to store captured images on their smartphones, so very rarely photos go beyond the internal memory or memory card of the device. The capabilities of most cameras of modern phones are so great that they allow you to upload your photos immediately to social networks, not to mention the highest quality video recording.

The smartphone from Samsung is equipped with a main camera, the resolution of which reaches 8 megapixels. The module also has an LED flash and, of course, automatic image focusing. The smartphone has a quick shooting function when the device takes pictures immediately after the shutter button is touched. This function, unfortunately, does not always work correctly, so sometimes pictures are blurry. The maximum resolution of photographs is 3264 by 2448 pixels. The main camera can also record video in FullHD resolution - 1920 by 1080 pixels.

Samsung Galaxy-S3: smartphone features

The device runs on a quad-core Exynos-4412 processor - a proprietary Samsung company. The frequency of this processor reaches 1.4 GHz. The graphics adapter here is the Mali-400MP. As for RAM, its volume is 1 GB, while there is plenty of internal memory - 16, 32 or 64 GB, depending on the configuration of the device. Moreover, if necessary, the user can use a microSD-format memory card, the capacity of which should not exceed the mark of 32 GB.

operating system

The device from Samsung is running the Android 4.0.4 platform, which is complemented by a proprietary shell called TouchWiz. The smartphone is quite fast, and using it is a pleasure.

Communications

Among the communications in the device from the South Korean company Samsung it is worth highlighting the presence of the fourth version of Bluetooth, USB connectors, as well as a Wi-Fi module, which is designed specifically for wireless access to the Internet. It is also worth highlighting NFC technology and S Beam.

Battery and battery life





The smartphone from Samsung has a lithium-ion battery, the capacity of which reaches 2100 mAh. The advantages of this battery can be attributed to the fact that it is replaceable and, if desired, consumers can purchase a spare battery if there are concerns that one will not be too convenient.

If you go into the settings of the Samsung Galaxy-S3, you can turn on the energy-saving mode, which will significantly extend the battery life of the smartphone. You can make the processor work at a lower frequency, lower the brightness of the display, or set a different background.

The manufacturer claims that the battery life of the device is 9.5 hours during a conversation, as well as 290 hours in standby time. In normal mode, with not too frequent use, the phone is able to work during the day. In this case, it all depends on the loads on the device.

Conclusion

Summing up, I would like to say that you can read more reviews about the Samsung Galaxy-S3 than negative ones. The spoken speaker, which clearly and clearly conveys the voice, also pleases. As for the ringer volume, it is above average, and the vibration is average in strength. If the case materials were a little better, and the design was not so primitive, we could safely say that the Samsung company turned out to be the perfect smartphone.

In any case, a product from a renowned South Korean manufacturer is unlikely to disappoint consumers. The device turned out to be really high-quality, but it can be purchased at a price approximately equal to 15 thousand rubles. Of course, the cost is slightly overpriced, but for this money the consumer will get a really high-quality and reliable device that will delight him with his work for a long time.
