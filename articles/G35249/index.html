Firebird: DIY costume

Children are always looking forward to the holidays. This is an occasion not only to show your skills and talents, but also to dress up in the costumes of your favorite fairy-tale or cartoon characters. Regardless of gender, the child wants to stand out among peers and express their identity. Little bee, Bunny, Snow White or Firebird - the costume of any of these characters will appeal to the baby. This is an opportunity to reincarnate at the festival, to take a step towards real magic.





A fire bird

The image of the Firebird is found in mythology around the world and is associated everywhere with kindness, truth and the sun. A character who brings happiness, illuminates everything around with his fire, is the protagonist of many Russian folk tales.

It is not surprising that children love to dress up in a fiery bird costume . And a great occasion to try on such a role can be a children's theme holiday or New Year's carnival.

Firebird is a costume that is perfect for a girl. The fire bird is associated with magic and fairy tales, therefore it will not leave indifferent any guest.

You can make a bright outfit for a child yourself. Of course, in order to make a Firebird costume, you need to choose the right materials.





Colors

For the costume, fabrics of red and gold shades are suitable, because it is a fiery bird. Some craftswomen like to use other colors for dresses. For example, blue-green fabrics with tints are suitable. If you choose the right shades and texture, then the clothes will become even more elegant. Appliques on fabric, stripes and other decorative elements will also give brightness, making the image of the child even more original.

Style

For a girl, a skirt with an elongated back, which will be used as a tail, is suitable. The stitched cloak looks interesting (it can be combined even with a black leotard).

Of course, the main attribute of a fabulous bird is its wings. They can be made from a single piece of fabric and decorated with an application that imitates plumage, or you can use the technique of batik, applying color patterns directly to the fabric.

Firebird is a costume that will look good in any combination thanks to bright accents.

Headdress

Another element that emphasizes the realism of a fabulous bird. There are no restrictions. You can just take a diadem with yellow stones or make an original crown. A feather headband will do. And even modern aqua-grill techniques allow you to paint your face and complement the image, making it as bright and organic as possible.

Firebird is a costume that is easy to sew with your own hands. With minimal time and effort, you can get a very good result, and your child will be in the spotlight.
