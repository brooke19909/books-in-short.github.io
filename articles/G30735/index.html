Thailand in September - is it worth it to go?

Some tourists choose to travel the first autumn month, when most are already returning home from vacation. However, not everyone visited Thailand in September and would like to know if it is worth going to this country at all in the fall. It is this month that unpredictable changes in the weather are noted here. However, you can come to this country at any time of the year, including on the first autumn days.

Weather in Thailand (in September)

The beginning of autumn in this country is characterized by frequent rains. It is with them that the doubts of tourists planning a vacation here are connected. However, Thailand in September is especially beautiful and can offer vacationers a lot of interesting things. Difficulties await you except with a beach holiday, but there are a huge number of other attractions.





The biggest fears in September are caused by huge rainfall, sometimes causing flooding. But the strong wind makes Thailand in September the best place for surfers. One should not even dream about sea cruises and calm sailing during this period of time. However, the air temperature in September here varies between 28-32 degrees, so tourists are quite able to lie on the beach and perfectly tan.

Which cities to visit

If you are planning a trip to Thailand at the end of September, then do not choose the northern areas. The best resorts in this period will be: Samui, Pattaya, Bangkok, Krabi and Phuket. In other cities, it is better to relax from November to March, when the heavy rainy season ends.





September holidays in Thailand: benefits

Sunny beaches, festivals, surfing, excursions and a time of positive emotions - this is exactly what Thailand will give you in September. Reviews of tourists say that if your vacation fell precisely for this month, feel free to go to this exotic country for adventure. The sudden downpour characteristic of this period will help you cool off on a hot day and get a boost of energy.

Thailand in September resembles a beautiful garden. Here, everything is actively blooming and captivating holidaymakers with its appearance. On the beach, you can spend as much time as you like outdoor games or the development of various sports. Active recreation is especially diverse here. Tourists can learn watercraft, water skiing, diving and more. You should definitely visit the underwater depths accompanied by an instructor.





What to do in Thailand?

To know in advance about possible ways to have a good time here, you can study the reviews of tourists. Thailand in September, as they say, is one of the best places for a measured family vacation. If you do not like the crowd, then this period is quite suitable for you.

In September, tourists get great pleasure from the local landscapes. The lack of crowds of vacationers, pleasant cool weather make it possible to walk around the surroundings and get out on excursions.

Beach vacation

If the main purpose of tourists is to sunbathe and visit the beach, then you need to choose vacation spots very carefully so that the probability of a storm is not high in the coastal area. It is also worth paying attention to the total number of cloudy days in September. A good option would be a visit to Phuket. However, the beach will not be as busy as in season, it may even be empty. Often raging waves and gusty winds interfere with a calm swim. Even the water in September is cloudy, unlike turquoise and clear in winter.

But September beach attracts professional servers. It is this weather that is needed for their sport. However, it is better for beginners to put off water skiing until the sea subsides.

It is not recommended to go to Krabi province for a beach vacation. The best places to relax on the beach in September are Samui, Koh Tao and Phangan. It does not rain very long, and the water in the sea is relatively calm, although cloudy days are not excluded.





Entertainment and excursions

If you go to Thailand (September-October), then you should definitely visit local attractions. In this country there is something to see: national parks, entire complexes of architectural monuments, fascinating temples, nature reserves. However, thinking over the tour, it is important to take into account the weather forecast for the coming days.

One of Thailand's most popular vacation options in September is fishing. It was at this time that a good nibble was noted, so the money spent on organizing the process will be fully justified. On rainy and rainy days, tourists can take care of their health and visit spa centers or massage parlors, of which there are a huge number. In addition, it offers vacationers shopping centers, cafes, nightclubs.

Holidays and festivals

Many tourists in September will be able to get to seasonal holidays and festivals. For example, World Gourmet is a festival of delicious food, as well as songs and dances, which bring together hundreds, even thousands of participants. Tourists in Juan Hin should visit the Royal Elephant Polo Cup. This is a very unusual and exciting sight that will give you a lot of positive emotions. A lot of good music and fun awaits visitors to the Full Moon Party, which takes place on Koh Phangan.

Some vacationers like to visit the Nan River in September. Colorful boats and fairs are organized in the surroundings. In popular cities for tourists, such as Pattaya, Phuket and Samui, also in September there will be many interesting events.





September holidays in Thailand: discounts

Of course, during this period, significant discounts apply to vouchers and events in the country. At the same time, it cannot be said that vacation prices here are quite cheap. Tourists will especially appreciate the low price of air travel. Tickets to Thailand will not be so difficult to buy cheaper. Some tourists do not even bother about housing and find it already upon arrival in the country. Be sure to demand a discount, the local population will gladly provide them to you.

In early autumn, you can have a great time in Thailand. However, it is important to choose the right resort and hotel where you will be staying. There is a threat of floods or landslides, so some regions are extremely unsuitable for recreation. But travelers who are burdened by high humidity and do not like sudden showers, it is better to wait for November to arrive in Thailand.
