WWII generals: list. Marshals and generals of the Second World War

Of course, the victory in the war was the merit of the Soviet people, who at the cost of countless sacrifices gave the future generation a peaceful life. However, this was made possible thanks to the unsurpassed talent of the Soviet commanders. Generals - participants of the Great Patriotic War forged a victory together with ordinary citizens of the USSR, demonstrating heroism and courage.

One of the most famous personalities of the Great Patriotic War is considered to be Andrei Ivanovich Eremenko, who was born in the settlement of Markovka in 1872. The military career of an outstanding commander began in 1913, when he was drafted into the Russian imperial army.
