After that, you need to measure the girth of the head. Subtract two centimeters from the resulting measurement, taking into account that the yarn stretches during socks. Multiply by the number of loops in one centimeter. It turned out the approximate number of loops to start knitting the beret from the bottom.

If you have a beautiful booked or sectionally colored yarn, then you do not need to choose a pattern for a beret. Type on the knitting needles the desired number of loops, preferably a multiple of 8. Tie the rim of a garter stitch three centimeters high. Take the knitting needles thicker and then continue knitting with the front smooth surface. In the first row you need to add evenly eight loops. How to knit beret knitting circular on the basis of the front surface?

Calculate the required number of loops, tie a five-centimeter-wide stitch with the front stitch. Now do the twisted crochets every four loops.
