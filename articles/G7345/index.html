Introducing the puzzle

So you decided to buy yourself such a toy. Congratulations on your purchase of a 5x5 Rubik's Cube! It is also called the Professor‚Äôs cube. Now, for sure, you would like to master a method of assembly that would not consist of complex and intricate schemes. But in order to understand how to collect a Rubik's Cube 5x5, you need to get to know the device of the puzzle more closely.





A cube consists of smaller cubes, each edge a row of five such small cubes. All puzzle layers can rotate independently, regardless of other layers - this is why the components can be rebuilt on the surface of the cube.

Each of the six faces is represented by its color so that the corner parts include three colors, the edge - two colors. In turn, the central part of each face is of the same color and, as in the classic Rubik's Cube 3x3, never changes its position, because it is fixed by a frame.

So, the Professor‚Äôs cube consists of: centers, edge centers, corner centers, outer edges, center edges and corners. This complex mechanism was patented by Udo Krell on July 15, 1986.

How to collect a Rubik's Cube 5x5: a variety of ways

No matter how complicated the 5x5 Rubik's Cube may seem, nowadays there are several assembly methods. Moreover, the volume of schemes and formulas that are proposed to be remembered determines the assembly speed: the more there are, the more the number of moves is reduced to achieve the desired result. We present you three methods for solving the puzzle:





  1. The solution to the problem is to master a simpler 3x3 cube. This method is recommended for those who already have experience with puzzles. First, the assembly of the centers is carried out, then the assembly of the edges, from it they proceed to the assembly of the last two edges and parities. This ends the new formulas, because then the Professor‚Äôs cube is assembled in the same way as the 3x3 cube.
  2. Collect the cube from the upper layers to the lower. It should be noted right away that this method is good, but for a 5x5 cube it is too long and inefficient.
  3. Assembly is carried out from the corners. The essence of the method is that the upper and lower corners are the first to assemble and only after that go to the edges and the remaining centers.

The most common mistake of newcomers: many people collect only one side and believe that now they will be able to collect all the others in the same way, but they immediately notice that adjacent cubes do not take their places, and when you try to shift their harmony is broken.

How to assemble a 5x5 Rubik's Cube is presented in this optimal and affordable algorithm: centers of the upper face, upper corner centers, lower corner centers, lower edge centers, upper angles, lower angles, edges of the lower face without one of the edges, edges of the upper face, the final stage assemblies of edges of the lower face, lower and middle faces, upper and middle edges, centers of middle edges, lateral corner centers and, finally, lateral middle centers. Speedcubing: what is it and what are its advantages

If you are interested in how to quickly assemble a rubik's cube, then you need to master the speedcubing technique - the fastest assembly. Its feature is a large number of schemes for various positions of the components of the cube. Thanks to the variety of formulas, the assembly speed increases to several seconds.

Very popular among speedcubers is the Jessica Friedrich method . There are even competitions that the WCA regularly holds, at the world level, where participants compete with each other in speed assembly. To date, the official assembly record is 48.42 seconds.
