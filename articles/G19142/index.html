Excellent stay in Khakassia: recreation facilities, services, photos

"Mirage"

Speaking of recreation facilities in Khakassia, the Mirage should be noted separately. Next to it is the beautiful Itkul Lake. It perfectly combines the price category and a high level of guest service.

"Mirage" is a recreation center that offers guests to stay in comfortable rooms. They differ in spaciousness, good furniture. Some have kitchenware, a fridge and an electric stove. In the village of Shira, there is a restaurant, discos are organized, there are various other entertainment programs, a market. Therefore, the guests of the recreation center will be where to buy things, products and have a good time.

Tortuga

With wonderful emotions you can spend your vacation in Khakassia. The recreation facilities of the local area are sometimes surprising in their resourcefulness. An example of such a complex is Tortuga. She looks like a pirate ship. Its dimensions: 25x15x20 meters. Electricity is provided, there is heating. Water is supplied both hot and cold. There is a bath from which it is possible to dive into the Yenisei. The cabins, which are provided to guests as accommodation, have double beds. One option is equipped a little differently: there are four small berths.
