Handsfree in the car: how to do, how to connect, how to configure?

In our fast-paced world, being in touch all the time is not only convenient, but also practical. But what to do when it is physically impossible to use the phone? Often this question is asked by drivers who are driving their car. After all, talking on a mobile and driving at the same time is not always comfortable, and sometimes very dangerous. At the moment, the solution to this problem was the speakerphone in the car. Using this type of gadget greatly facilitates driving at the time of a call, does not affect safety, and besides, this pleasure is quite accessible to everyone.

Voice Types

It is worth noting that many modern cars already come from the factory with built-in speakerphone systems, which are controlled by buttons either on the steering wheel or on the center console.





But if the car is not equipped with such functionality, it can be done independently.

There are several different ways to install this type of electronic assistance in the interior of your car, using both professional and assistive devices. How to make a speakerphone in the car yourself and without much effort? Motorists will find this information in this material.

Easiest way

You can provide speakerphone in the car by connecting a mobile to the car radio with an additional microphone installation, or you need to purchase a device specially designed for this. You can buy this device at an audio equipment store. It is a small signal receiver that works directly in tandem with the phone via a Bluetooth connection. How to talk in the car through the speakerphone in this way? You just need to configure the receiver, which takes a few minutes.

Hands free

As an alternative to such a device, you can use the hands-free gadget, which is attached using the built-in clothespin or to clothes, or directly to the steering wheel. At the same time, during conversation, complete freedom of hands is ensured.

The advantages of the above communication methods include ease of equipment, installation and configuration, as well as low cost. You have the opportunity to connect various models of phones that support the Bluetooth function in this way.

Speakerphone selection options

Due to the large number of devices on the car market, it is quite difficult for motorists to choose a device that will meet all the requirements and properly perform its functions. Therefore, when choosing one, it is recommended to pay attention to such factors as:

  • Manufacturer. Handsfree in the car can be provided only with a high-quality speakerphone. To do this, when choosing, you should pay attention to the country in which the device is manufactured. It is important to note that Chinese gadgets do not have sufficient reliability and durability due to the use of low-quality materials.
  • Battery capacity It should be large enough so as not to bother with frequent charging processes.
  • Fasteners. This element should be reliable and convenient, since in the opposite case, the device may simply fall away.
  • Mandatory availability of the ability to charge the device from the cigarette lighter outlet, which is more convenient than constantly removing the device and charging from other sources.
  • The presence of the Russian language in the settings and use menu.
  • Price. As you know, avaricious pays twice. Therefore, it is recommended to immediately purchase a more expensive speakerphone of good quality and use it for a longer time than buying a new one from time to time.
