General information, the history of "ATB"

This bank is one of the largest in the Siberian and Far Eastern districts. It is included in the top 100 banks of the Russian Federation.

The bank has been operating in Russia for 26 years, since February 1992. Changed its name many times. In 2006, together with all organizational and legal changes, it was given the name "Asia-Pacific Bank", which still exists.

The number of staff of this organization is more than 3,000 people, the activities of ATB are carried out in 108 settlements, covers 19 regions of the Russian Federation and has more than 200 branches. Served by the bank more than 880 thousand people.

In addition to additional and operational offices, in Ulan-Ude, Yekaterinburg, Moscow, Blagoveshchensk, there are bank branches. Its assets amount to 108.7 billion rubles, and its own funds - 265.7 million rubles.

All existing products and services of banks for individuals and legal entities can be purchased at any branch. These include:

  • provision of loans;
  • mortgage registration;
  • issuing credit, debit cards;
  • opening and closing deposits;
  • various transfers and foreign exchange transactions;
  • insurance;
  • brokerage services;
  • depository services, etc.
