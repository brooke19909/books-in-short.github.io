Hotel Sunwing Resort Spa Bangtao Beach 4 * (Thailand / Phuket): overview, description, services and reviews

Thailand is an amazing place. It is beautiful, exotic, and also warm and sunny. For these reasons, the places of Thailand have gained great popularity among tourists. In particular, those located in close proximity to the sea. In one of these places is the luxury hotel complex Sunwing Resort Spa Bangtao Beach 4 *. I would like to tell you about it.





general information

Modern hotel Sunwing Resort Spa Bangtao Beach 4 * is located in one of the areas of Phuket, which is called Chengtale. To the sea from the hotel - within easy reach. Interestingly, the local beach, known as Bang Tao, is one of the largest and cleanest on the island. Its length is about 8 kilometers. The beach is designed for a quiet and relaxing holiday. What Bang Tao is good for is the lack of waves. Even in bad weather. After all, there is a beach in the bay. By the way, the hotel has a private zone on it. Therefore, guests will not have to look for the most attractive place, since there is an opportunity to stay in private territory.

The airport is also nearby - a 30-minute drive from the hotel. And in the immediate vicinity of the complex is the Bliss Beach club, minigolf, the excellent Tesco Lotus supermarket and Plaza Surin shopping center. Bang Tao Temple can also be reached quickly - in about 15 minutes.
