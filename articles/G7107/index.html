4 * Diamond Bay Resort & Spa (Vietnam, Nha Trang): hotel description, reviews

Hotel 4 * Diamond Bay Resort & Spa is a luxurious resort and hotel complex, which is ready to offer its guests paradise conditions for relaxation. Here you can not only have a good time, but also improve your health and significantly improve your well-being thanks to the huge list of spa treatments that this resort specializes in.

Short hotel description

The 4 * Diamond Bay Resort & Spa is just a luxurious place that can give you an unforgettable vacation in an atmosphere of solitude and tranquility away from the hustle and bustle of the city. The entire territory of the hotel is literally immersed in the greenery of gardens, in which rooms, restaurants and other objects of the resort infrastructure of this hotel are sheltered.

Hotel location

The 4 * Diamond Bay Resort & Spa Hotel is located in an incredibly picturesque place between two green hills, as well as near the purest golden beaches. This is the southern part of the popular tourist city of Nha Trang, which is only 25 km away from the international airport. The hotel is located almost on the beach, so if you have a desire to plunge into the warm azure waves, then you will have to go no more than 200 meters to the sea.





Number of rooms

For all fans of eco-tourism, Vietnam should be a priority. Diamond Bay Resort & Spa 4 * has all the features for this. Of the 342 rooms, 176 apartments are located in cozy three-story buildings, and the remaining 166 are small cozy bungalows that are lost in the dense shade of green gardens. Each room without fail has a small balcony or a spacious outdoor terrace. At the same time, some of them overlook a picturesque garden, while the rest open up amazing mountain landscapes in front of you.

Guests are offered to stay in the following categories of apartments:

  • The standard double room is equipped with a large double bed or a pair of single rooms. It is a cozy room with a balcony and a bathroom. It offers amazing garden views.
  • Superior double rooms have a large space. In addition to the bedroom, there is also a small living area with comfortable soft chairs, a coffee table and a floor lamp. Standing on the balcony, you can admire the garden.
  • Suite is a spacious two-room suite. There is a cozy bright bedroom, as well as a small living room with kitchenette. From the windows and from the balcony of the room offers a bewitching view of the mountains. Special attention deserves a bath with hydromassage.
  • Sapphire Bungalows are small, secluded houses that are lost among the dense green spaces of the garden. They are spacious enough to accommodate a large family, and at the same time very comfortable, which is very important for a secluded romantic getaway.
  • Rubin Bungalow is a series of detached mansions located close to the beach. Sitting on the spacious outdoor terrace, you can spend days enjoying the seascape and listening to the sound of the surf.




Services and Facilities

Diamond Bay Resort & Spa 4 * hotel cares about the comfort of its guests, and therefore offers to use it a variety of services that meet the highest standards of service:

  • there are 7 theme restaurants in the hotel complex where you can try local or international cuisine (if you wish, they will deliver food directly to your room from any institution);
  • if you want to independently explore the surroundings and explore all the sights, then for this purpose you can rent a car;
  • You can order a transfer to the airport or to any point you need on the map;
  • Reception staff work for you around the clock;
  • if you are tired of the road, you can use the express check-in service (the same opportunity is provided for those guests who leave the hotel);
  • at the reception you can exchange currency at a fairly favorable rate;
  • You can pick up an interesting tourist route and go on a fascinating study trip using the services of a tour desk.




Fun and relaxation

At the Diamond Bay Resort & Spa 4 * hotel (Nha Trang) everyone will find something to their taste. Regardless of whether you like to relax peacefully or spend time actively, there is entertainment for everyone:

  • large tennis court with the possibility of night lighting with spotlights;

  • almost all over the country there are many modern tables for playing table tennis;
  • You can do your figure in the large fitness center;
  • for those who want to relax properly, it is recommended to visit the SPA salon, which operates on the basis of a large wellness center;
  • Feel the professionalism of massage masters on yourself;
  • the youngest tourists can have fun in the games room or on the playground in the garden;
  • You can sing karaoke at the bar.




Additional hotel information

Diamond Hotel (Vietnam) has the following internal rules:

  • You can register and check into the room no earlier than 14:00, but leave the hotel on the day of departure before noon;
  • two children under 6 years old without extra beds can stay in the apartment for free;
  • if guests arrived at the hotel with a child under 2 years old, then a cot is provided to him at no extra charge;
  • an extra bed for adults and children over 6 years old costs $ 44 per night;
  • if an extra bed is not required for a child from 6 to 12 years old, then his accommodation per day will cost $ 10;
  • pets are not allowed;
  • upon arrival at the hotel, each guest is required to present identification documents with a photograph, as well as a credit card (always your personal, personal);
  • Booking a room in this hotel requires an advance payment (bank transfer);
  • regular guests have the right to make special requests about their stay during the booking of the tour;
  • if the purpose of your trip is to celebrate a birthday or a honeymoon, be sure to inform the administration about it, and you will receive a birthday cake as a gift.




Positive reviews

Many tourists prefer a holiday at the Diamond Bay Resort & Spa 4 * hotel. Reviews about him contain many positive comments:

  • a picturesque, clean, well-kept beach, which is located in a quiet bay;
  • Friendly, helpful and qualified staff;
  • huge green garden, which occupies almost the entire territory;
  • a good buffet with a large assortment of local and European dishes;
  • good cleaning and daily towel change;
  • the hotel is very quiet - it is an ideal place for a relaxing family vacation;
  • excellent clean, well-kept pool;
  • despite the fact that the hotel is far from urban infrastructure, the prices in its cafes and restaurants are quite affordable.




Negative reviews

Quite popular among tourists for a holiday destination is Vietnam, Nha Trang. Diamond Bay Resort & Spa 4 * provides a fairly high level of service. Nevertheless, in his work there are some gaps that are reflected in the negative reviews of tourists:

  • inconvenient schedule of buses running to the city center;
  • the bottom of the sea on the beach is covered with silt and sharp shells, in addition, the water is very shallow due to constant ebbs;
  • tasteless packaged juices in the breakfast buffet;
  • there is practically no animation, and entertainment shows are rather rare;
  • many ants and spiders in the rooms.
