Logopark (Kamensk-Shakhtinsky): price

Many, having learned about all the advantages of this park, thought that the entrance to it was probably expensive. Not at all. Entrance to the park is free, as is the use of toilets. Near each toilet there is an original signpost and a bench for waiting.

Near the entrance to the park there is a fairly ample parking, which is also free. But the most amazing is the free Segway rental. Logopark (Kamensk-Shakhtinsky): how to get there

From Rostov you need to go along the M4 "Don" highway, which runs directly to Kamensk-Shakhtinsky. Next, you have two options. The first one is to drive through the town along Karl Marx Avenue (the town is not very busy, there are practically no traffic jams) and cross the Seversky Donets River via the pontoon bridge (crossing is paid: passenger car - 20 rubles, SUV - 30). Next, you need to turn onto Budenny Street, and from it to Bolshevik. It remains only to turn right. This option is faster. The second is longer, but it's free. On the M4 Don highway, you need to go to the roundabout, which is located behind the Seversky Donets. One turn - and you will see Logopark (Kamensk-Shakhtinsky). The address of the park is: Kamensk-Shakhtinsky, s. Old Village. The street is not indicated, apparently due to the size of the park. It can be seen from different angles.

By public transport, everything is also very simple. Bus number 25 takes you from Kamensk-Shakhtinsky Train Station to the park. As you can see, it‚Äôs not at all difficult to get to Logopark (Kamensk-Shakhtinsky). The park is open 24 hours a day.
