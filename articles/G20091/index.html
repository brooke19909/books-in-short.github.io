Which bank is better to invest in? Overview of deposit rates at banks

For those who want to invest their funds profitably, save them and increase them, the question always remains open: "Which bank is better to invest money in?". In the financial services market, there are a large number of organizations ready to offer favorable conditions and sometimes very high interest. What lies behind their tempting offers, and how to protect yourself from deception? About this in the article below.

When a long history matters

Before investing money in a bank at interest, you need to take this issue seriously. Undoubtedly, everyone wants to get the maximum benefit from their honestly earned money, therefore, first of all, he pays attention to profitability. Those who are not the first time dealing with deposits know that the advertised increased rates are far from everyone and are suitable only for those who have a large amount and are willing to leave it in a bank account for a long time.

It is imperative that after a year or two the bank does not disappear or go bankrupt. Therefore, history is of great importance. A long-term presence in the financial market speaks of stability and reliability, willingness to offer customers stable service conditions. As a rule, such banks have a wide branch network and are available to their depositors. This means that the client will be able to contact any office and get high-quality and fast service.

Reputation is what has been gained over the years, and all major financial institutions value it. Therefore, opinions and customer reviews are taken into account in the work and improvement of further service. When choosing which bank you can invest in, it is important for a modern client to know that his rating and reputation are highly reliable. However, it also happens that a bank with a long history loses its license. How can an investor protect himself in this case? To clarify in advance whether the organization is a participant in deposit insurance, reliability and stability class. Data on this can be obtained at the bank itself, as well as in reviews of rating agencies.

Reliability Assessment

In order to determine which bank is best to invest in, experienced investors advise to study the assessment of independent agencies that provide data on the reliability of a particular organization. Those banks that are in the top 10 deserve attention. This indicates a high rating not only by experts, but also by clients of these financial institutions. It is important to understand that despite the high reliability of banks, the amount of deposits subject to insurance does not exceed 1 million 400 thousand rubles. Even if several deposits are issued for the same name, their total amount is taken into account.

Profitability, the absence of sanctions by the Central Bank of the Russian Federation and a large loan portfolio are also criteria of reliability. These points are as important as many years of practice and a wide branch network. The availability of bank services, as well as the ability to use and maintain your account remotely make the bank attractive in the eyes of a modern client.

Dependence of bids on the term

Before investing money in a bank at interest, you need to study the rates, the conditions for the availability of capitalization. They speak not only about the future profitability of invested funds, but also allow us to assess the prospects for the development of the bank in this direction. After the events of 2014, large financial institutions are afraid to offer their customers high rates for a period of more than a year. This is due to relative mobility in terms of changes in both the refinancing rate itself and the bank's proposals.

If you look at the list of the best banks, including Sberbank VTB, Rosbank, Raiffazenbank, Gazprombank, Intesa, Nordea, Citibank, Alfa Bank, all of them offer the highest percentage for a period of 3 to 12 months. At the same time, it is important to understand that the deposit with the highest yield will not include withdrawal and replenishment operations. In a word, the less transactions on the account, the higher the percentage. This rule is followed by all financial institutions offering to open a deposit account.

Before agreeing to open an account for a period of more than six months, it is necessary to assess the likelihood of its early termination. Since in this case, interest is accrued either based on 2/3 of the rate, or ‚Äúon demand‚Äù - 0.01%. In order not to be left without interest and additional income in general, it is recommended to choose an account, albeit with a lower rate, but with the possibility of partial withdrawal or for a shorter period. This will allow the account holder to get a small, but income.
