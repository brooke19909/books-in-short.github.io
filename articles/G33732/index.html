Decor of tights bottles: master class

Today, things created by your own hands have gained special popularity and value. And this is not surprising! After all, masterpieces made with love, in which the soul is invested, can never be compared with things purchased in a store. If you want to give your family or friends a bottle of champagne or wine, then with the help of simple equipment you can quite create a real work of art. To receive such a gift is always pleasant and interesting. No less exciting and create it. In this case, for the manufacture you will need a variety of improvised tools, for example, ordinary women's tights. So, consider how to create a bottle decor with tights.





Determine the purpose

Before we look at how to create a bottle decor with tights, it is necessary to determine what role this thing will play. If you want to make a unique interior item, the basis of your creation should be the color scheme and style of the room. When creating a gift bottle, for example, for a birthday, try to take into account the wishes of the birthday person.

And do not be afraid to connect your imagination. Be creative in this lesson. And your bottle will certainly become a wonderful creation.

Necessary tools

If you plan to create a bottle decor with tights, the master class below will allow you to understand the technique of such a product.

To implement your idea, you need to stock up on tools and materials. Harvest everything in advance. This will greatly facilitate your task. In addition, thinking over the unique and interesting decor of bottles with tights, you will not be distracted by the selection of tools. After all, everything you need will be at hand.

So you will need:

  • bottle for processing;
  • craquelure varnish (it is necessary to create the effect of the smallest cracks);
  • degreaser;
  • nylon pantyhose or stocking;
  • glue (you can use PVA);
  • napkins;
  • vintage varnish made of bitumen;
  • cotton pads;
  • acrylic paints;
  • oil intended for artistic processing;
  • gold outline;
  • printed cards or napkins for decoupage;
  • sponge;
  • braid;
  • alkyd varnish;
  • white acrylic matte paint.




Bottle preparation

When all the components are ready, you can proceed to the initial stage of creating a unique masterpiece. The decor of bottles with tights begins with the proper preparation of glass containers.

To do this, get rid of the label. The easiest way to do this is to soak the bottle for several hours in plain water. In this case, the label will lag behind.

Then degrease the surface. You can use any tool that is in the house.

For these purposes, they are excellent:

  • nail polish remover;
  • ordinary alcohol;
  • acetone;
  • liquid for washing glasses or dishes.




Walk over the entire surface of the bottle with your chosen degreaser.

We create a bottle decor with our own pantyhose

Now you can begin to work. The work is quite interesting, but requires great care and attention.

It will help to understand how to create a bottle decor with tights, a master class:

  1. Take a nylon stocking and lower it into PVA glue. Then pull it over the bottle. Form folds in the shape of the product. They should be on top of the bottle. At the bottom, it is desirable to level the surface, since a picture will be glued here.
  2. Take the napkins. Initially dip them in glue. Then form flowers from them like papier-m√¢ch√©. Gently stick them to the bottle in the places where the creases end.
  3. Take the braid and glue it to the product with PVA glue. It should distinguish the transition of folds to a flat surface.
  4. Now you need to give the bottle time to completely dry. Such a process can last almost two days.




Product decoration

We continue to consider decorating bottles with nylon tights:

  1. The surface of the bottle must be coated with black acrylic paint. The cork is also painted over. Only one area should not be black. The bottom of the bottle remains unpainted where the surface was even. It is planned decoupage. This area should be coated with white paint. Wait for the product to dry again.
  2. Select the desired pattern. It is recommended to print it on photo paper (matte) with an inkjet or laser printer filled with pigment ink. Otherwise, the pattern may begin to spread. The front surface of the picture must be opened with acrylic varnish. It is recommended to apply three layers with an interval for drying in 30-40 minutes. Cutting the pattern is recommended by the master in a day.
  3. Soak the printout in water for a couple of hours. Then put it face on a sheet of file. Roll it from center to edge on white paper until the pattern is reflected. Then the print is glued to the bottle with PVA glue diluted with water in a ratio of 1: 1.
  4. When the picture is dry, take a sponge, gold acrylic paint and tint the product, coated in black. Be sure to let the bottle dry.
  5. Then, with a golden outline, work through the transitions from the picture to a darker shade. Do not forget to draw a cork.
  6. After this, apply cracks with small cracks. They are recommended to be created as follows. A synthetic layer is applied to the surface of the first layer. After three to four hours, the procedure is repeated. And again they wait 3-4 hours. You yourself will notice how cracks begin to appear.
  7. Using a bituminous patina applied with a soft brush, the cracks are overwritten. This allows you to create an aging printout. After about 40 minutes on a cotton pad , apply art oil. It is used to eliminate excess bitumen patina.




The work is completed. You can enjoy your creation. Indeed, in your hands is really a unique bottle.

Conclusion

After learning how to create a bottle decor with nylon tights, take this technique as the basis for your creations. Show your imagination. And perhaps your composition will become an unsurpassed masterpiece of art.
