Surely you know that you can buy a subwoofer with a built-in amplifier. Yes, such a miracle of engineering, of course, is. Such "barrels" / "boxes" are called active. But it is worth considering that active subwoofers, nevertheless, do not belong to high-end technology. If you want to create a truly high-quality sound in the car, you will need a passive speaker and an intelligent master.

What can be distinguished from such tips?

Amplifiers for cars there are 2 channels, 4 channels, 5 channels and monoblocks. Having a fairly limited amount of money, it is not difficult to purchase an amplifier with access to two channels. Best with bridge support. The principle is based on the fact that you can send a signal from the left and right channel to the subwoofer simultaneously with a load of 4 ohms. In such a simple way, a device designed to support stereo sound is transformed into an amplifier for a subwoofer. The price of such a device starts at $ 150. Such amplifiers are usually endowed with a frequency filter and all kinds of protection against overload and short circuit. Monoblocks, in general, is another story. These devices are usually designed for powerful acoustics. Such an amplifier for a subwoofer most often has a rated power of 100W and supports a load of 1 ohm. It is best to connect only one speaker to it, but support for two, well, a maximum of three speakers is not ruled out. Such devices cost from $ 200 and are most often used by professionals not only as an amplifier for a subwoofer, but also for separate support of absolutely every channel of the speaker system.
