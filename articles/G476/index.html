The purpose of the contact zoo

Cases of introducing into the practice of human-animal communication during the rehabilitation of patients or mentally injured have been known for a long time. This type of treatment is called animal therapy. This is especially true for domesticated pets.

In animal therapy, cats, dogs, aquarium fish and parrots are most often used. Aquariums have developed a system for treating people by communicating with dolphins. Rules for visitors

To avoid problems when people come into contact with animals in the menagerie, when buying tickets, each visitor, regardless of age, is given shoe covers and his hands are disinfected. This is a precaution, rather, for the safety of animals, since clean and grafted animals do not threaten people.

Not all inhabitants of the zoo can be touched. For example, an enclosure for raccoon dogs can be entered only in the presence of a worker who carefully monitors that children do not scare animals.
