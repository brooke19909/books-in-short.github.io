Hotel Sun City Side Hotel 4 *, Side: overview, features and reviews

One of the favorite destinations for travelers is Turkey with its wide selection of multidirectional resorts. One of them is a convivial Side, whose residents are distinguished by friendliness and cordiality. It harmoniously combines the amazing landscapes of untouched nature with a developed hotel infrastructure. Here you can not only soak under the hot rays of the sun, but also visit a number of attractions, learning the new facets of this settlement.

The four-star Sun City Side Hotel, located on the Mediterranean coast, welcomes every guest. Excellent living conditions, cheerful staff that welcomes vacationers hospitably from the first minutes of their acquaintance, delicious food, which allows you to get enough after spending energy on active entertainment - all this offers its guests an institution. Sun City Side Hotel 4 * receives negative reviews extremely rarely. Basically, they are all positive. Does the hotel complex really deserve attention? This will be discussed in the article.





general characteristics

The building of the hotel complex Sun City Apartments 3 * (Side) was erected at the turn of the millennium in 2000. From the very beginning of its existence, it tirelessly attracts new tourists to its walls. The last renovation was carried out in 2013. The total number of residential buildings is 6 units. The area is dotted with manicured lawns, fragrant flower beds and leafy palm trees. The distance to the nearest airport is 70 km. Check-in with pets is not allowed. Non-smoking rooms.

Accommodation

The room fund of the hotel Sun City Side Hotel 4 * (Side) includes 151 chambers, which vary with views from the windows. The interior of the rooms is simple, without elaborate elements and gloss, however - it is cozy and spacious. The floors are covered with laminate. There is a kitchen area, a balcony or a private terrace. The color scheme is dominated by bright shades. For sleep lovers, the opportunity to use the service "wake-up call". Wi-Fi is free throughout.

The apartment furniture is standard: beds, chairs, a table, bedside tables, a wardrobe. There is air conditioning, satellite TV, telephone, safe.

The kitchenette is equipped with a dishwasher, oven, toaster, electric kettle and a set of dishes.

The bathroom is tiled. Equipped with a cosmetic mirror, shower or bathtub, washbasin, hairdryer and snow-white towels. Personal hygiene products are represented by soap, shampoo, shower gel.

There were no complaints about the cleanliness of the hotel. Tourists in their reviews note that it is homely here. Cleaning is carried out every day, linen is changed twice a week.





Nutrition

The power supply system at the Sun City Apartments Hotel 4 * (Side) is organized according to the buffet system. According to the All Inclusive program, delicious culinary masterpieces from local chefs are able to satisfy the gastronomic needs of each visitor. Drink consumption in bars - included in the concept. There are institutions that operate on a custom menu. However, they are open in the evening and require a dress code. Turkish coffee, fresh juice and imported alcohol are subject to additional charges. As noted by numerous hotel guests, the food here is fresh and tasty. Even the most sophisticated tourist can choose a dish to his taste.





Aquazone

The hotel complex Sun City Side Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Side) has a developed network of swimming pools, bathing in which is of great benefit to the body. The main pool is surrounded by comfortable sun loungers. To avoid direct sultry rays, you can hide under an umbrella or canopies that create a shadowy space. In the vicinity is a children's pool, where kids can dive in plenty, getting indescribable pleasure. For them, a water slide is set in the form of a fiery blue dragon, a ride with which every child likes.

The mini-water park is represented by two slopes that diversify the monotonous swimming of vacationers. They are located in a separate pool.

Water aerobics classes are held daily in the pool area. After all, starting the day with active classes - a great mood is guaranteed throughout the day. Light exercises not only support the shape of the body, but also contribute to its improvement. You can also play water polo.

For the cold season and during bad weather, travelers relax in the indoor pool.





Beach

The waters of the Mediterranean Sea are about 800 meters away. A free shuttle runs to the promenade and back. However, walking can be reached on foot.

A golden sandy beach dotted with sunbeds with umbrellas will allow you to get a dark-skinned tan, which is the goal of the lion's share of spa residents. A gentle entry into the sea allows the kids to splash aground. The beach infrastructure includes showers, changing rooms and toilets. Rescuers, without closing their eyes, monitor the safety of swimmers.

You can diversify the time spent on the beach with the help of water attractions and a game of volleyball. Slides, scooters and riding a banana like, according to reviews, all without exception. For more extreme entertainment, not everyone decides. Diving and parachuting for a boat are honored with the most daring and risky. Excursions on yachts to the open sea, where the opportunity to go fishing, are to the liking of inveterate fishermen.





Children leisure

When implementing a family vacation, it is important for each parent that their child receive new, memorable emotions. For this, as noted by numerous tourists in their positive reviews, there is everything you need. Open in the daytime kids club is a real paradise for every kid. There is a huge trampoline complex with labyrinths and descents, slides, swings and transitions made of plastic. Each child will be delighted with the pool with a million plastic multi-colored balls in which you can lie. Toys, from simple baby dolls and cars to tracks and simulated stores, will certainly occupy children for a long period of time. Their stay in this room is supervised by a qualified caregiver.

In addition, animators entertain children throughout the day, transforming into various fairy-tale characters. Cheerful collective games, sports competitions and a trick contest are what awaits young guests at this hotel.

Fresh air is incredibly healthy. That is why various gaming and sports grounds are located throughout the territory. Children are given the opportunity to frolic in the open air and spend energy for a night's sleep. The evening ends with a disco.

Feeding chairs in restaurants - in the public domain. Manege is available upon request.

Pastime adults

In addition to caring for its excellent appearance, the hotel has a number of entertainments that will bring bright colors to the rest. This fact is noted in numerous grateful reviews of guests. Here you can play table football, billiards or table tennis, making new friends and comrades. The slot machine hall will allow you to check the success of each guest. Additional services

Tourists who visited the hotel were satisfied with the provided additional services. Reception is available for guests 24 hours a day: the friendly staff settles guests, answers all questions and solves any problems. For the convenience of transportation, vacationers are offered a transfer service to the airport and vice versa. In the shortest possible time and with safety for health, experienced drivers will deliver visitors to their destination. Sun City Side Hotel has a currency exchange and luggage storage facilities.

Car rental is one of the popular services that tourists use. Explore the city with comfort and a roof over your head, visit outstanding sights and get acquainted with unique local landscapes - absolutely everyone can do all this. An opportunity to use the services of a tour desk is also provided. Car parking is free. Bicycle rental not only allows you to enjoy the picturesque surroundings, but also physically develops the human body.

Souvenir shops and grocery minimarkets are located on site. Doctor on request. Beauty salon is glad for every client and is ready to create a delightful new look for every visitor.
