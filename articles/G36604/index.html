How to use the diary? How to make unusual diaries with your own hands?

Meet friends, prepare lasagna, buy a present for your niece, take pills, check mail ... The list of tasks that await us every day can be continued indefinitely. Each business requires timely and accurate implementation. Not everyone can boast of an impeccable memory. To help the leaders of large companies, office workers, mothers with many children and housewives, an indispensable thing for today has been created - a diary. In fact, there is quite a replacement for the diary - these are modern gadgets that offer many programs for recording cases and plans, but despite this, few have abandoned the diaries.

Why keep a diary?

If you are a forgetful, unassembled and very busy person, then you need to start keeping a diary. In the future, he will teach you to do all the things on time, not to postpone plans, and also to follow discipline. Only for this you need to at least try to start keeping your own diary.

If over time you suddenly realize that keeping a diary does not give you pleasure and does not help you at all, then it is better to quit this matter right away.





How to keep a diary?

There are several ways to keep diaries, it all depends on what format you choose. In stores you can buy a ready-made notebook that can be kept all year. In such a business diary, the first page begins on January 1. Each day is divided into hours, it also has a calendar, a page for recording phones, important dates and more. Such a diary calendar is convenient for businessmen and office workers who are expected to meet a lot of meetings, phone calls and negotiations during the day.

Fancy diaries can also be bought at the store. It can be a thick notebook in a hard cover in a ruler or a cage. Original diaries with printed pictures are also for sale.





How to choose a diary?

If choosing a business notebook for office workers is fairly easy to determine, then those who choose a daily planner more for themselves than for work, to write down their thoughts, secrets, plans, etc., will be much harder. Indeed, in any store where stationery is sold, there are a lot of different diaries for every taste.

Quite unusual diaries are sold in bookstores. Their price is a little more expensive, but the pleasure from such an everyday man is much more. Firstly, a large number of pictures adorn inside them, as well as individual lines decorated with flowers or a frame, to record the name of the owner. The very beautiful diaries are released by the famous Moleskin brand. Diaries and sketchbooks from the Italian company Moleskin adored such legendary personalities as Van Gogh, Hemingway and Picasso.





Some teenagers also keep diaries, especially girls. In their diaries they write down thoughts, ideas, experiences, plans and even secrets. That is why it is important for teenagers that this book, which they will trust a large number of their thoughts, be beautiful and unique. For teenagers created funny diaries in colorful pictures or with unusual covers. By the way, the diary will be a wonderful gift.





How to make a beautiful diary from a regular notebook?

Creative individuals can try to design a notebook themselves, turning it into a beautiful notebook.

Buy a thick notebook with a beautiful cover and multi-colored stickers. Divide the notebook into several sections with adhesive stickers. Each section can be devoted to a separate topic. For example, in the first you can write down a to-do list, in the second section a list of necessary purchases, in the third culinary recipes, etc. Such completely different original diaries can be obtained by any person.

How to make a diary with your own hands from scratch?

Many girls are fond of needlework and creating their own diaries. In order to create your own, you need to have thick cardboard, sheets of paper, glue, scissors and scrapbooking paper.

The needlewomen often share patterns with each other, with the help of which they create unusual diaries. Typically, these patterns consist of strings decorated with flowers or pictures. You can print them, and you will get quite nice pages for your future diary. If you want to create a sketchbook, you can use ordinary white sheets of paper.

The internal pages need to be sewn. After that, you need to cut out the cover from cardboard and glue beautiful scrapbooking paper on it. Then you will need to glue the stapled pages onto the cardboard. Crystal glue is suitable for this; it holds cardboard well. Let the glue dry. Following these rules, you can create your own unusual diaries.

It is very easy to make a diary, because even small children make books themselves from paper in which they draw and write.
