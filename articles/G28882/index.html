Zoos in Kazan - exciting and informative leisure

Zoos in Kazan are an ideal place for tourists and local residents to diversify their leisure time, to escape from everyday troubles and everyday hustle and bustle. It has a special atmosphere that promotes relaxation and stress relief.

Zoos in Kazan, along with the aesthetic function - contemplation of the wonderful world of animals, perform educational tasks, conduct educational activities. These institutions, originally created for the conservation of rare and endangered species, their demonstration and scientific research, now act as developmental and educational centers.

Amazing near

Where else, if not in zoos, in the modern world of industry and global industrialization can a person be in close proximity to the inhabitants of our planet and learn about the habits, living conditions, and features of our smaller brothers? Contact Zoo (Kazan) is a place where you can join in caring for pets, feel a surge of vitality and optimism from communicating with never-ending residents. It is here that a person discovers the harmony of pristine nature, admires its perfection and begins to realize how important it is to preserve it for posterity.

Along with an amazing park landscape and a recreation area in zoos in limited areas with close to natural living conditions, representatives of the fauna world are kept.





Employees of the institution study the biological characteristics and psychological characteristics of their pets. They take the necessary measures to conserve species and their reproduction for further resettlement in the natural environment. They help restore the endangered representatives of the animal kingdom in the wild.

A negative consequence of the development of civilization was the disruption of natural ecosystems and the disappearance of entire biospheres, which only zoos (in Kazan, in particular) were able to resist, where rare animals were kept .

The oldest in Russia: the history of creation

Kazan Zoological Botanical Garden is one of the first open Russian institutions; it is one of the five oldest in Europe.





It dates back to 1806, having been born at the city university thanks to the plan of Karl Fuchs. In 1829, on the acquired coastal areas near the picturesque Lake Kaban , a botanical garden of 6.7 hectares was laid. The construction of the greenhouse was completed in 1834, and the nature park was open to the public. A small zoo began work a century later. The institution became known as the Zoobotanical Garden.

Modern zoo

Since March 1997, is a permanent member of the Euro-Asian Regional Association of Zoos and Aquariums. On its territory in 2009, an unmatched children's contact zoo (Kazan) was opened - Lukomorye. In a fabulous town, made in an original and colorful design, children can chat with animals and spend unforgettable time on the playground.

By the beginning of 2014, there were over 160 species of animals in the collection of the institution, and more than 1,000 plant varieties in the arsenal of botanists.

The institution in its activities is in contact with 50 zoos and 30 botanical gardens of near and far abroad.

Highly professional staff conduct a variety of excursions and thematic lectures, demonstrate animals even outside the zoo. Promising direction for the reproduction of rare species of animals and plants. A technique has been developed and introduced that has no analogues in world practice for the adaptation of captive-born brown bears to wildlife conditions. A scientific study is underway to breed polar bears. Employees are also actively involved in conservation programs for the endangered white-tailed eagle, burial eagle, Steller's sea eagle and black neck. The year 1991 was marked by a significant event when a litter was obtained from burial eagles, the second recorded in the world.
