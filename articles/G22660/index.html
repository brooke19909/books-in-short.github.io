Alanya, "Life Green Hill" is a tasteful family-friendly hotel!

Life Green Hill is a hotel (Alanya), which is conveniently located on the sea coast 10 km from Alanya. It was built in 1995, and was restored for the last time in 2004. The hotel complex is located on a coastal elevation. You can go down to the beach by funicular or stairs. The complex consists of 18 two- and three-story cottages. Life Green Hill is a hotel that will make your vacation dreams come true!

Nutrition



Restaurants operate on an all-inclusive basis. Visitors can enjoy Turkish and international cuisine at breakfast, brunch, lunch or dinner. A buffet is served here , ice cream and snacks are available at specific times. There is a diet menu, non-alcoholic and alcoholic drinks are served from 10 am to 11 pm. Imported bottled drinks, Turkish coffee, freshly squeezed juice and room service are available at an additional cost. After 11 pm, food and drinks are paid.

Alanya, Life Green Hill. Hotel and beach

The hotel has its own sand and pebble beach with a total length of 100 m. There is a pier on the coast. Vacationers are provided with free use mattresses, umbrellas, deck chairs. Beach towels are available at an additional cost.





Accommodation: Turkey, Alanya

5 star hotels always offer upscale accommodation. This hotel complex is no exception. There are 420 rooms, 255 of which are standard and 165 are family. Everywhere there is a shower, a balcony, the floor is decorated with ceramic tiles. In addition, each room has air conditioning, a hairdryer, a minibar (free water is provided on the day of arrival), and a telephone. Linen is changed 3 times a week, the room is cleaned daily. Room service is available 24 hours a day (paid).

Alanya, Life Green Hill. Hotel and its infrastructure



The territory of the complex includes a main restaurant, four bars, two swimming pools, two water slides. There are shops, a hairdresser, laundry and dry cleaning services. Doctor - on call. You can rent a car, call a taxi. Luggage storage and parking spaces are available to all vacationers. You can go to the gym, have fun on the tennis court or in the classes in aqua and simple aerobics, play beach volleyball, table tennis, go to the movies. In addition, hotel guests can use the services of a beauty center and go to the sauna or Turkish bath, to a massage therapist, soak in the jacuzzi. The hotel often hosts entertainment shows, performances, concerts. Those who wish can visit the disco.

Children's holiday

The smallest babysitter can look after. Older children will be able to go to the mini zoo, play on the playground or splash around in two shallow pools. In addition, the hotel has a mini-club for children from 4 to 12 years old, animators are constantly working - you will not be bored with them! The restaurant has a separate buffet for children. Have a nice, truly memorable trip! Alanya, "Life Green Hill" - a family-friendly hotel with taste - is waiting for you!
