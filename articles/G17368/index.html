What is DNS and what qualities does the technology of this company have

First, let's clarify what is DNS? Many will say that this is a domain name system for obtaining domain information. Others will say that this is a domestic manufacturer of computer equipment. And someone, perhaps, for the first time hears such a name. In any case, everyone will be right.



But the article will focus on the DNS company. So what is DNS, what is remarkable for this company? We can say that this is a kind of retail chain with a federal scale. The company specializes in the sale of not only high-quality computer, but also digital technology. On the consumer market, it has existed for more than 10 years and has specialized stores in almost all of Russia.

Goals

The main goal of DNS is to provide Russian customers with the opportunity to purchase equipment that is of high enough quality but at a fairly low price. Having been in the consumer market for several years, the company is a partner of the largest suppliers and leading world manufacturers of computer equipment. Huge selection and the best price - this is her motto.





Products

What is a DNS technique? First of all, this, of course, is a decent quality product and affordable price. The gadgets that the company offers belong to the line of the budget series so popular recently. Let's take a closer look at the devices offered by this company.

The DNS tablet is a quality, functional gadget. The device supports almost all music and video formats. The high-resolution screen allows you to surf the Internet with sufficient comfort, read electronic books, play the latest generation of games, and even work with electronic documents.

The operating system is based on the popular Android. The only thing that can slightly reduce the joy of shopping is the camera. On almost all models, the images are not of very high quality, but clear enough not to think about who is shown in the photo.



The DNS smartphone also supports all the functions a user needs. Almost all models support two SIM cards. This is very convenient, because to access the Internet you can buy a special SIM card with an unlimited tariff, which will allow you to make calls and write SMS.

Despite the low price, the smartphone DNS has such useful features as Wi-Fi and GPS navigation. The Android operating system will allow you to install the necessary applications. In addition, it is possible to increase the built-in RAM using an additional flash card.

Summary

So what is DNS? One thing is for sure: this is a fairly high-quality functional computer equipment at affordable prices. Most recently, the company announced the release of high-performance laptops and netbooks. The cost of new products is still quite acceptable for any circle of consumers.

The company also places particular emphasis on the quality of its products and provides the buyer with two years of free warranty service.
