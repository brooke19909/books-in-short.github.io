Hotel Royal Jinene 4 * Sousse (Sousse, Tunisia): photos and reviews of tourists

Tunisia is a country that attracts travelers from all over the world. After all, here you can count on comfort, quality service and the opportunity to immerse yourself in a new culture, learn traditions and an interesting history. Of course, do not forget about the opportunity to have a good time on a snow-white beach.

When planning a trip, the choice of tourists often falls at the hotel Royal Jinene 4. Sousse (Sousse) - a beautiful resort city, which is ideal for a beach holiday. Naturally, to begin with, wanderers try to collect the maximum amount of information about the chosen stopping place. Where is the hotel located and how far are the beach, shops, restaurants and cafes? What living conditions can a guest expect? Does the hotel offer additional services and entertainment? Is the place suitable for a pleasant and convenient time with the child? How do tourists respond about the hotel? Answers to these questions may be useful to many readers.

Where is the hotel complex located?





For many travelers, the issue of the location of a boarding house is extremely important. So where is the Royal Jinene 4 Hotel located? Sousse (Sousse) - a picturesque town on the beach. It is here, in a quiet area, almost on the seashore, that the modern hotel is located.

The city center with all its entertainment facilities and attractions is just 3 km away. This distance can be easily overcome on foot, because sometimes it is nice to stroll through the evening city, beautifully lit by lights. In addition, travelers can use taxi services or public transport, the prices here are not too high. Nearby are the picturesque harbor and port of El Kantaoui. Monastir Airport is 25 km away. Travelers paying for the tour can count on a free and convenient transfer to the hotel.

In general, the location of the hotel is excellent: the area is calm, and the beach and city center are within walking distance.

Hotel Royal Jinene 4 Sousse: photo and description of rooms





This is a medium sized hotel with 102 rooms. For the most part these are comfortable standard rooms, but there are several more spacious suites. Travelers can enjoy garden or sea views from the balconies that come with the rooms.

Of course, all rooms are comfortably furnished, including a large desk. By the way, not so long ago, repairs were carried out here, so you can count on a new carpet, real home comfort and elegant atmosphere.

A set of household appliances, without which today it is hardly possible to imagine the comfort of a traveler, is also available. You can relax and unwind while watching satellite channels on TV. The air conditioning system will help to cope with the summer heat and create comfortable temperature conditions. You can make calls, but they are not included in the price. For a small fee, you can rent a safe. A minibar with cooling system is also available, but can only be filled on request.

The room adjoins a tiled bathroom and new, modern fixtures. Depending on the category of the room there is a spacious bathtub or shower. Also at your disposal will be an individual toilet, washbasin, dressing mirror, as well as a hair dryer, a set of hygiene products and clean towels.

Reviews suggest that the rooms here are really clean. High-quality cleaning is carried out daily (or at the request of guests). Once a week, clean bedding is delivered to the room.

Meal plan for guests





It is hard to imagine a really fun and quality vacation without delicious food. So what can the guests at Royal Jinene 4 (Sousse) expect? Tours purchased from agents, as a rule, already include a full three meals a day. If you book the hotel yourself, then you can choose a convenient scheme for yourself, whether it is only breakfast or lunch.

Reviews suggest that the local restaurant and food scheme are really good. The spacious building is furnished in a cozy oriental style. There are a sufficient number of tables, chairs and utensils.

Breakfast in most cases consists of snacks, salads, flavored pastries, fresh fruits, different types of scrambled eggs, pancakes and, of course, desserts. Lunches and dinners are more varied. The menu includes traditional national dishes of Tunisia, as well as more familiar European food. Guests can enjoy delicious salads, meat and fish dishes, as well as seafood and homemade cakes.

The all inclusive system includes free drinks, in particular juices, lemonade, aromatic coffee, several types of tea, delicious wines and much more. Some can be found in the restaurant, while other drinks are served exclusively at the local bar. Here you can just sit, relax and chat with other guests.

By the way, you can eat outside the territory of the Royal Jinene Hotel Sousse 4. Sousse is a large resort city where there are a huge number of restaurants, cafes and pizzerias. Also, travelers sometimes visit shops and local markets where you can buy both food and prepared delicious local dishes.

Where is the beach located?





Of course, most tourists arrive in Tunisia in search of picturesque beaches. What can the Royal Jinene 4 hotel complex offer in this regard? Tunisia, Sousse is an ideal holiday destination by the sea. The hotel territory goes directly to the coast, the road takes no more than 5-10 minutes.

The hotel owns a coastal area: your beach will be closed to strangers, since only guests have a rest here. The coast is covered with soft sand, the entrance to the sea is gradual and convenient. The beach is freed from algae and other garbage every morning, so it is always clean. It also has comfortable sun loungers and large umbrellas that perfectly protect against direct sunlight. You can use them for free, but you need to pay extra for renting a soft mattress. Also, guests receive large clean towels (they are issued on bail, which is then returned).

There is another bar on the beach from the hotel. Here tourists can enjoy a delicious cocktail or a chilled drink. The menu even has light snacks and homemade ice cream. It offers guests a well-equipped volleyball court, where you can have fun in the company of new friends.

Water recreation: what awaits a tourist?

Sunbathing and swimming in the sea are, of course, the components of a pleasant pastime. But some tourists prefer more active types of recreation. On the beach you can rent canoes, a small spoon or a catamaran. For a fee you can enjoy water skiing, boat or banana. Many guests enjoy parasailing. The local coast is ideal for windsurfing. Experienced tourists can rent a board and other devices right on the beach. Beginners, for a small fee, hire an experienced instructor who helps them get comfortable and explains the basic rules.

One of the favorite activities of travelers is, of course, scuba diving. The sea world here is rich and diverse. Near the hotel there is a diving school where you can buy a tour, rent the necessary equipment, or take a quick course from professional instructors.

How do the guests have fun on site?





What entertainment can the Royal Jinene 4 hotel complex offer? Sousse is a beautiful city where every traveler will find something to his liking. Nevertheless, you can have a good time on the territory of the hotel itself.

Guests love to relax by the large pool in the courtyard. The water here is always clean, and there are comfortable sunbeds on the terrace. Here people rest, relax and chat with new friends. From time to time, aerobics classes are held in the pool, and water polo is organized.

There is an indoor pool with a powerful heating system. Here, tourists usually relax in the evenings or in bad weather. You should definitely visit the local Turkish bath, after which you will find an unforgettable relaxing massage from real masters. For an additional fee, guests can use the jacuzzi or sauna.

There is a good fitness center with a fully equipped gym, which is free for travelers who prefer to keep fit. From time to time, instructors conduct group exercises in gymnastics, aerobics and yoga. They can also be visited free of charge, and morning exercises on the lawn can be a great start to the day.

As for other entertainment, the hotel has a real casino where fans of gambling can have fun. There is a hall with slot machines, where there are not only children, but also adults. Guests can also enjoy a game of billiards, chess and table tennis. In the evenings, you can sing karaoke at the bar.

There are several tennis courts on the hotel grounds. It is worth saying that the rental of rackets, balls, as well as night lighting are paid separately. Just three kilometers from the hotel there is a large golf course (of course, this refers to the category of paid entertainment). At the hotel you can easily rent a bike for walks and even go horse riding.

Throughout the day, tourists are entertained by funny animators who organize various contests, competitions, team games, group classes and other, no less interesting events. And in the evenings you can attend performances, enjoy live music, watch dance and acrobatic shows.

Do not forget about excursions. On the territory of the city itself there are many interesting places that are worth visiting. You can also travel around the area. The hotel will help you choose an interesting sightseeing tour or make a route for an independent trip. The city center also has a large number of travel agencies, whose services are not so expensive. As for evening entertainment, there are practically no night clubs, but you can always find a cozy bar.

Are there any conditions for relaxing with a child?

For parents who are going to spend a vacation with the whole family, along with the children, comfort issues become priority. What can the Royal Jinene Hotel Sousse 4 offer? Reviews suggest that this hotel complex is suitable for a relaxing family vacation. Moreover, the main contingent of vacationers is precisely parents with children of different ages.

For starters, of course, it's worth talking about amenities. Children under the age of two years stay at the hotel for free, and children from 2 to 12 years old are entitled to discounts. Naturally, upon request, a folding bed with a comfortable mattress is delivered to the room. Unfortunately, the restaurant does not have a separate children's buffet or food, but the choice of dishes is very large, so it is always easy to find something healthy and tasty for a child of any age.

In the courtyard of the Royal Jinene hotel complex there is a small but roomy pool where children can splash around in warm water and complete safety. There is a small playground with swings. There is also a mini club where children also have fun under the supervision of educators and animators. The children play, draw, dance, take part in various productions and team sports games, in short, have fun and enjoy.

The hotel has several experienced professional nannies who, for a relatively small fee, are happy to look after the child, giving their parents a little free time.

Entertainment can also be found outside the Royal Jinene 4. Sousse is a beautiful tourist city offering plenty of ways to have fun. Very close to the hotel there is a large water park, where you should definitely take the child. Large and small slides, pools, various water attractions - all this is liked not only by children, but also by their parents. And in the city there is a zoo with a large number of different species of animals, fish and reptiles (here children can also get a lot of positive emotions). In any case, the kids have a great time in the city, while improving their health with fresh sea air.
