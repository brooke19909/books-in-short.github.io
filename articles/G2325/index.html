Cross-stitch embroidery: patterns

Our everyday life is decorated with trifles - nice trinkets that look decorative, toys, figurines, paintings, napkins, sofa cushions. All of them are able to give the room a special charm and comfort. And pillow embroidery will add even more charm to the interior.

Nice hobby

Despite being completely busy with life problems, people strive to find free time for creativity. For many, embroidery becomes an outlet for everyday worries. The history of this art is undergoing a rebirth in the present - more and more people, of different ages and sexes, are passionate about embroidery. The creation of these man-made paintings in various techniques is a very complex and painstaking task. But embroidery on the pillow is available to anyone who wants to do creativity with a thread and a needle and at the same time decorate the interior of the room with something original.





Little sofa thoughts - beautiful and bright

Embroidery on a pillow is one of the most common ways to create comfort in a house for a long time. After all, all manual work had its own sacred meaning - such things served as amulets, protecting home from evil spirits. Keeping this purpose, cushions over time have also become an indispensable decorative household item. Small in size began to be called little thoughts, apparently because they beckoned to take a nap on the couch, resting his head on their patterned surface, rest, think. The embroidery on the pillow could be very different in style, in drawing, in the technique of execution.





Beauty cross-stitch

This, incidentally, is not very difficult - embroidery. Cushions decorated with this technique can be a great proof of this. After all, you do not need to embroider a multicolor picture on a sofa cushion. Sometimes a one-, two-color ornament is quite enough. You can put it around the edge of the pillow or embroider a corner with a simple pattern - and the ordinary thing will immediately turn into something original.

In cross-stitch, the most difficult thing is color transitions, when you need to accurately count the number of squares of a particular color. But if there are only one or two shades of floss, then there is absolutely no difficulty in such work.





And crosses are different

The pattern of embroidery of the pillow that will decorate the house can be very different, as well as the patterns with which it is decorated.

It is most convenient to embroider with the simplest cross - a stitch on the diagonal of the square, from left to right, and then on the other diagonal - from right to left. With such crosses, it is most convenient to embroider tracks and sections of one color, changing the thread when moving to another section.

That is, first diagonal stitches are sewn in one direction on all the squares of the same shade, then these same squares are quilted along the second diagonals in the opposite direction. Such a cross is the most primitive.





But there are also quite complicated methods - elongated crosses, when the stitch goes into two conditional squares. Or half-elongated crosses - one side stitch for two squares, and the other - only one.

The so-called double or Bulgarian cross is even more complicated - the stitches are first sewn on two diagonals, and then on the centers of the sides of the cell - from bottom to top and from left to right. How to create a picture, decide the needlewoman. But cross-stitch pillows still prefer using the most ordinary embroidery cross.

All the time

What is comfortable embroidery on the pillow? The fact that it is not at all necessary to fill out the entire field with patterns. Sometimes they are enough only in the corner, and your little thought will become an adornment of your favorite sofa. You can sew a pillowcase from several parts, one of which will be embroidered with a cross, and the rest will be just fabric. You can position the embroidered piece in the center or on the edge - as you see fit in a particular version.

How to use fabric properly? You can choose a section that is large enough to fit several medium-sized embroideries, make them all, and then use them in different pillows. Thus, you get a whole set of little thought in one style. If there is no suitable piece of fabric for pulling on the hoop, then they add it to another fabric using suturing stitches. In any case, the sofa cushion embroidered with a cross is a cozy detail of the interior, which is both decorative and practical, and therefore it can be as you wish.





Kids - fairy tales

Undoubtedly, for a child, the world should be primarily joyful and interesting. And the interior of a children's room or children's corner must meet these requirements. The embroidery of children's pillows, made by the hands of loving relatives, can bring great joy to the baby if it depicts a beloved character from a fairy tale or cartoon.

And for the tiniest babies, you can embroider pillows for the crib. Each, for example, is decorated with embroidery with a children's pattern, and then from several small pillows, sew the sides into a crib. Things created with love by their own hands possess the power of a talisman, as our ancestors believed.





For a long joy

Any manual work is labor, painstaking, thoughtful, creative. Undoubtedly, I want the results of creativity to please as long as possible. Therefore, when the embroidery is ready, it is washed and steamed through the fabric from the wrong side. Then they collect the entire pillow. Embroidered products should be washed by hand, rinsed in slightly salted water, then they are dried and ironed from the inside through the fabric, putting the fabric inside the product.
