Hotel Manaus 3 * (Mallorca, Spain): description, rooms, reviews

Mallorca ... The name of this place sounds truly fabulous and magical. Most of the population of the island of the same name lives in the capital of the Balearic archipelago. Every day, a huge number of tourist liners, completely packed with tourists, moor on the island.

The vast majority of Mallorca's income comes from the tourism industry, which is the most developed in these parts. There are a huge number of hotels of different classifications. You can stay in the premium complex and in a simple budget hotel. A worthy accommodation option in the resort is the Manaus 3 * hotel (Palma de Mallorca).

The favorable climate of the island has a calm and respectable rest at any time of the year. Swimming on the beach begins in late April or early May, then a huge number of tourists come to the island.

The water temperature rises to a maximum of +38 degrees, which allows even small children to bask in the sea foam. In winter, the water temperature drops to +8, which pleases many "walruses" who arrived at the resort. There is practically no snow in Mallorca, there can only be the thinnest layer that melts quickly.





The holiday season begins in the second half of April, and ends in October. The maximum air temperature at this time rises to 28 degrees, and sometimes you have to dress up in warm clothes. But this does not spoil the impression of a wonderful time.

This article will describe one of the most popular hotel complexes Manaus 3 * (Mallorca). Reviews of numerous tourists indicate a decent standard of living in this complex.

Description




The most important hotel of Mallorca is located 7 kilometers from the main airport of the city. A special beach at the hotel is 600 meters away. All amenities on the beach (mattresses, sunbeds, deckchairs) are provided for a fee.
There is a promenade between the hotel and the beach. The number of rooms in this complex includes 100 rooms. The hotel was last renovated in 2005, and was built back in 1967.

The Manaus 3 * complex (Spain, Mallorca, El Arenal) is located on the territory of the forest, inside there is a bar and restaurant, and in the adjacent territory there is a heated pool and a jacuzzi. The hotel has a playroom for the whole family and children of any age.



There is a cozy and bright sitting area for reading your favorite books, equipped with the necessary furniture and equipment for making tea or coffee. Each room is equipped with a fridge, TV, internet, telephone, comfortable furniture and a bathroom.

All apartments have air conditioning, a split system, a fan, a mini bar and a safe for a fee, an extra bed is available on request for a small visitor. Pets are not allowed in the hotel.

Beach vacation





The beach here is one of the best. Such clear sea and sand can only be found in the Caribbean. Nevertheless, on the Mediterranean coast of Mallorca is no worse. You can even see in the water how grains of sand move from the waves. The sea is shallow, so you can safely come here with young children. There are a lot of people on the beach in season. For an additional fee, sun loungers and umbrellas are available. Between the sunbeds can be located for free. Beach mats are inexpensive, you can not carry with you. All stores are littered with goods for vacationers. Prices are low, but the quality is not very. When buying an air mattress, you need to check it well for holes. Although even the sellers themselves say that the fact that it is periodically blown away is normal. Nobody will change anything for you.

All day long drinks, fruits, glasses and other trifles are worn on the beach. Massage therapists advertise their services.

Prices are not the cheapest, but no one has regretted it yet. In addition, you can use free internet and enjoy the gorgeous view. Some restaurants do not always bring what the customer orders. For example, in "El Puerto" often bring only part of the dish. But perhaps there are some errors in the menu. Near the hotel there is a tent with fruits, fast food, waffles and yogurt.

Sightseeing trips

The hotel Manaus 3 * (Mallorca, Spain) has a tour desk. Vacationers are advised to purchase excursions only through a guide at the hotel. So you can save and have a good time. Most often advised to go on an inspection of the island. Mallorca has many attractions, including the Luke Monastery. You can get here only with a sightseeing tour. The trip involves a visit to a leather factory, a monastery, a picturesque bay, the port of De Soller. The whole trip is made on different modes of transport. A ship delivers tourists to the port. There is a local tram to Soller, and an old train to Mallorca.

It is also worth going to Alcudia and visiting Cape Formentor. Mallorca is not a very large island, but it is incredibly hot here. If you want to enjoy not only a beach holiday, but also sightseeing, you must come here in early spring or autumn.

It is worth a visit to La Seu Cathedral. You can also get here by bus number 23, which runs from Hotel Alegria. You can also go to the Aquarium. But all these late ones are much cheaper if you arrange them through a guide.

With children, you can visit Kathmandu Park. A 5-minute drive away is a good water park. On guided tours, guides mainly talk about the history of Mallorca.

Do not be afraid to travel on your own. Sometimes it is much more effective and informative than with a group.

Go shopping

There are practically no normal stores in El Arenal. Mostly small stalls with cheap beachwear, fruits and other little things.

For big purchases it is better to go to more developed areas. Spain has a "tax free" system (value added tax refund system). In large shopping centers, you can purchase various goods and at the same time collect checks. On the last day of shopping you need to present all the checks and passport in the store. Managers will draw up a form, which then should be presented at the airport. They will put a stamp there, after which you can get back about 20% of the amount spent. Even if you do not succeed in doing this in Spain when drawing up all the necessary documents, the money can be withdrawn in your country.
