Dnepropetrovsk camp sites: an overview

In the desire to leave the bustling city for a short while and enjoy the beauty of the surrounding nature, many residents of Dnepropetrovsk try to leave the city on holidays and weekends. Listen to birdsong, breathe clean air, forget about everyday problems and immerse yourself in a completely different world - this is the beauty of a country vacation. The great thing is that it is not necessary to go far. Many tourist centers in Dnepropetrovsk are located a short distance from the metropolis. The article provides an overview of the most popular of them. Distance - no more than 40 km from Dnepropetrovsk.

Camps of the Butt: Orel OE

On the left bank of the Dnieper River, at the confluence of the Orel River, the village of Obukhovka is located (until 2016 - the village of Kirovskoye). In this area there are a large number of sanatoriums, hotels, rest houses, tourist centers, as well as Obukhov plavni - a unique landscape complex. This is an island of wild, pristine nature between the Dnieper (the modern name of Dnepropetrovsk) and Kamensky (formerly Dneprodzerzhinsky), which became the basis for the creation of the Dnieper-Orelsky reserve.



Where to organize a great vacation from the bustling Dnepropetrovsk? At the Orel camp site, located 300-400 meters from the river of the same name, houses of various categories and prices are available for guests. Children under 7 years stay free.



  • No amenities. Includes: a set of clean linen, amenities (toilet, shared shower) on the street. The kitchen and refrigerator are also shared. The cost is from 200 to 320 UAH / day per house, depending on the number of people.
  • With amenities. 3- and 4-seater. A set of clean linen, refrigerator, shower, toilet. 380 UAH per day
  • Log houses - small and large houses with amenities and a bath, designed for two. 350-480 UAH / day The bath can be ordered only when renting a log house. It is paid in addition, payment hourly.

If the vacation is planned without spending the night, in a good big company, then you can rent a gazebo (for 20 or 40 people) and a barbecue for cooking a delicious barbecue. Also in the recreation center "Orel", located at: pos. Kirovskoe, st. Nekrasova, 1 - guests can swim in the pool or take a horse ride.

"Highlander's Cottage"

In the village. Obukhovka suburban vacation offers BO "Cottage Highlander." Clean air, pine forest, river - what else does a person need, and especially a city dweller, for unity with nature? It offers guests cozy rooms with amenities, a restaurant with fireplaces, decorated in a hunting style, a sandy beach, a wood-fired sauna, gazebos with barbecue facilities, the Hata Karasy cafe, an incredible hookah (including stones). All these components in the complex will provide high-quality relaxation and a wonderful charge of energy for the coming week.





Separately, it is worth mentioning fishing - a special form of relaxation, which for many is not just a habit or a hobby, but a state of mind. There are pike, catfish, silver carp, and mirror carp in the pond. Caught over 7 kg. You can prepare your catch here, on your own on the grill, or with the help of experienced chefs.





BO "Dacha Gortsa" offers the organization of show programs, weddings, booking transfers, placing guests in comfortable rooms - in general, everything is possible and impossible, but so that every visitor likes it and wants to come back here again. Moreover, it is not far from Dnepropetrovsk.

The camp site is located in the village. Obukhovka (Kirovskoe), st. Orelskaya, 3A.

"Meridian"

Describing the campsites of Dnepropetrovsk, it is worth mentioning the Forest Park. The territory located in the village of Diyovka (near the village of Sukhachevka) covers a huge part of the wild forest, lakes, tracts, and Dnieper plains. The close proximity to the city, namely Kommunar, Parus, Parus 2 housing estates, is an advantage when choosing a place for recreation. The camp sites of Lesopark (in Dnepropetrovsk) offer their services to those who want to change the city noise for a relaxing holiday. One of them is the Meridian.





On the picturesque bank of the river. Dnipro is the center of family recreation "Meridian". Cozy rooms with all conveniences, a restaurant with a spacious hall, plenty of greenery and clean air make for an excellent pastime. Address; st. Narva, 288, Art. Suhachevka.

In the territory of the Dnipropetrovsk region, a huge number of tourist camps offering quality relaxation for every taste and budget. The choice is yours!
