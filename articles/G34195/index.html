The capital of Jamaica and its holiday resorts

Very many who come to this island with the aim of relaxing or making a trip are met by the capital of Jamaica - the city of Kingston, which is surrounded by the charming Blue Mountains and the calm waters of the harbor, protected by a narrow and long strait.

The capital of Jamaica was founded in the 17th century by the British colonialists and gained fame as a very large center for the sale of slaves in the West Indies. At the beginning of the XX century, the city of Kingston, as a result of natural disasters, turned into ruins, and with them the masterpieces of old English architecture disappeared. But by the heroic efforts of the inhabitants of Jamaica, the city has risen from ruins, and at present it is the cultural, transport and commercial center of the state.

Kingston Seaport passes through almost all the goods that Jamaica exports or imports. Nearby Poliseidos Airport accepts international flights from all over the world, and students from many countries located in the Caribbean are trained at the famous University of the West Indies.

The main attractions of Jamaica are concentrated in this city, the inspection of which will bring a lot of pleasure and experience. After visiting the museum of the history of the state, the house-museum of the famous Bob Marley, as well as the main residence of the ruler of the country, you will have something to tell your family and friends about. The resorts of Jamaica have many hotels for accommodation, varying both in price and in the range of services provided. These can be modern hotels with chic bars, pools, restaurants and discos, or secluded hotels of small sizes. But whatever your preferences, the capital of Jamaica will be cordial to you and provide all the opportunities for a good rest.

Water sports such as windsurfing, water skiing, sailing on yachts are very popular on this island, and diving is especially exciting. Even if you are not a very big specialist in scuba diving, you can be trained at a local school under the guidance of experienced instructors and enjoy watching the underwater world of flora and fauna. And even if the capital of Jamaica is not considered the most first-class resort of this state, in any case, it will give you a lot of different impressions and a unique tan.

To really relax with the whole family, as well as with friends, you need to go to the northwestern region of the island, where the Montego Bay resort area is located. The coastline at this resort is over sixteen kilometers long. This is how the famous beaches of Jamaica - Cornwall, Mosquito-Cove, Doctor Cave - attract tourists and travelers with their cleanliness and white sand. Lovers will have plenty of fun and have a good rest, Montego Bay offers a lively nightlife with discos, bars and nightclubs open until the morning. Couples can enjoy quiet evening walks with their children in the beautiful park of the resort or spend serene fishing time. You can not deny yourself the pleasure of visiting the Blue Lagoon - one of the attractions of Jamaica, which attracts tourists from all over the planet.
