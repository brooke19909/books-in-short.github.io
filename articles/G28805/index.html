"Planet Bowling" Krasnogorsk: description, services, reviews

Planet Bowling is a large network of stylish and ultra-modern bowling clubs, each of which has its own design and unique atmosphere. The latest equipment, billiards, bar, excitement and fun are the necessary components of the Planet Bowling network. Krasnogorsk, Samara, Krasnoyarsk, Rostov-on-Don, Yekaterinburg and several other major cities of our country can boast of this entertainment facility.





Planet Bowling in the Park

Krasnogorsk is a very beautiful and developed city, where many establishments are concentrated, where you can have a wonderful weekend and organize your time so that it will be useful. Club Planet Bowling (Krasnogorsk), located in the SEC Park, is one of such places.

The organizers of the institution have thought out everything to the smallest detail so that vacationers would be interested and comfortable in it. The club has modern equipment, quality service, professional staff who are always ready to help beginners. For children, special bumpers are installed here to make it easier to roll balls. During the game, you can order various dishes of European cuisine and drinks from the menu of the bar, designed for forty people.





In addition to the bowling zone, there are billiard tables, a lot of slot machines. The sports bar broadcasts sports shows on large plasma screens.

The club hosts birthdays, corporate events, theme parties and children's parties. You can arrange a tournament on the game.

Description

"Planet Bowling" Krasnogorsk is:

  • Sixteen tracks.
  • Two tables of the American pool.
  • Seven pool tables.
  • Several slot machines.
  • Air hockey.
  • Bar.
  • Subscription system and accumulative club cards.
  • A shop selling professional bowling accessories.
  • Sports bar.
  • Wireless Internet.
  • Storage of customer accessories.
  • Free car parking.
  • Children's room.

For children

For young customers, Planet Bowling (Krasnogorsk) is a great place to frolic and play. It introduced a system of discounts for children, there are special children's shoes, light balls weighing up to nine pounds, paths with sides. There is also a bowling school where your child will be taught how to play.

Additional services

Every day from seven in the evening until the close, modern music hits are heard in the club, and ultraviolet light is turned on. Luminous balls and pins flicker everywhere , an unusual atmosphere is floating in the air.





Every Thursday, Planet Bowling (Krasnogorsk) holds an amateur handicap tournament, in which anyone can participate. Those who win the tournament receive cash prizes, and a box of beer is also played out.

Location

Where is Planet Bowling located (Krasnogorsk)? Organization address : Tushinskaya metro station, 50th October Street, 12, (in the building of the Park shopping center, fourth floor).

The institution is open daily from 12 noon to 6 in the morning.

The cost of one bowling game varies: from 430 to 790 rubles per hour on weekdays and from 880 to 1250 rubles per hour on weekends. We accept cash and credit cards.

Visitors reviews

If you consider yourself to be a category of people who prefer outdoor activities, then be sure to visit the Planet Bowling center (Krasnogorsk). Reviews about this place suggest that it is very pleasant to spend time here.





pros

The club staff always greets guests with a smile, employees do everything quickly and efficiently. The club is great for families with a lot of children, especially on weekends. For those who do not like large crowds, it is better to come to play during the day on weekdays.

Many positive reviews from people who celebrated birthdays at the club. A great place, calmly, without fuss, make out tracks, quickly bring orders, set tables.

Attendants will always help inexperienced players, explain what and how to do. The club constantly holds various promotions and discounts (birthday parties, schoolchildren, students), which you can play at half the price than usual.

Judging by the reviews, the club has a good cuisine, excellent pizza, desserts, delicious fried cheese sticks with Neptune sauce, super-cocktails. Recently, strong alcoholic beverages have appeared in the bar. Prices are more than loyal.

Now about the cons

If you believe the reviews that write on various Internet resources, the condition of shoes and balls in the club leaves much to be desired. The latrines are not quite clean and smells strongly of tobacco smoke. When ordering food and drinks here, people are advised to pay attention to the dishes in which they are served: it happens that it is poorly washed. On weekends, when there are a lot of people in the club, it becomes stuffy in the room.

However, not very picky clients who come to Planet Bowling for rest and good mood do not pay attention to such trifles and turn a blind eye to minor flaws.
