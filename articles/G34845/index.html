Description of Hainan Island

This island in the Middle Kingdom is the second largest after Taiwan. And it is located in a tropical climate zone on the same latitude with Hawaii, for which it received the name "Eastern Hawaii". Its area is thirty-four thousand square meters. The population is about eight million people. The capital of the island is the city of Hayoku. Once it was a small provincial town, but now it is a beautiful modern metropolis. The leisure industry is developing rapidly. Since the reign of the Tang Dynasty, it has become the custom that all routes along the island begin in the city of Hayoku.

There are two more cities on the island that are especially loved by visitors - these are Sanya and Tunzha. Sanya is located in the very south, surrounded on three sides by mountains, and on the fourth it is washed by the azure tropical sea. Tongzha is interesting in that the indigenous population of the island lives here - the Miao and Li peoples. Ethnographic villages are located here, in which the life and rites of the indigenous population are represented. Few people will be indifferent to a country such as China, and rest on Hainan Island - all the more so. It has everything: mountains, waterfalls, lakes, thermal springs, bays, beaches, entertainment, fashionable hotels and much more. All in order to make your holidays in China (Hainan Island) unforgettable. If we talk about local cuisine, then many tourists are sure to try four famous dishes: crab "Hele", black goat meat "Dunshan", chicken "Wenchang" and the famous Chinese duck.

How to fly to the most beautiful island of China - Hainan?

But how to get to this island? After reading the enthusiastic reviews of tourists, many will want to visit Hainan Island (China), tours of which are offered by many agencies. And it should be noted that quite often there are hot deals where you can save half the cost of the trip. The direct flight Moscow - Hainan is operated by Transaero Airlines. Flight time is ten hours. The island can also be reached through Shanghai, Beijing or Hong Kong. The flight from Shanghai will take two and a half hours, from Hong Kong - one hour, and from Beijing - four.

Entertainment

International Olympic shooting range, some of the best golf courses, safari park, rafting, diving, fishing, extreme sea activities - all this awaits you on Hainan Island.

What you should definitely see

There are a lot of entertainments on the island, and, of course, there is something to see. If you suddenly get tired of lying on the beach all day, be sure to organize yourself excursions. You can visit such places:

  • Valley of the Pearls. Here you can see firsthand how pearls are mined and processed.
  • Valley of the Monkeys. Eight tribes of monkeys will appear before you in all their glory, one of which will show the "monkey circus." You can also feed these bullies.
  • Butterfly Garden and Shell Museum, which are located in Yaluvan Bay.
  • The temple, which houses the statue of the goddess Guanyin. This statue is listed in the Guinness Book of Records.
  • Visit the incredibly beautiful coast of Tianyahijiao, where, according to legend, the mythical king of the monkeys - Sun Wukong, visited.
  • Zoo safari, where from two thousand of the first year a strange beast bred under the name of tigers is bred.
