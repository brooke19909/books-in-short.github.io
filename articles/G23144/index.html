How to find the phone when it is off: tips and opinions

Unfortunately, a situation where a mobile phone has just been here and suddenly disappeared somewhere is not uncommon. In most cases, they simply call to find the device. But this will only work when the device is working. How to find the phone if it is turned off? This task is much more complicated. But still, such a situation is not a reason for despair, because most of the devices can be found in other ways.





How to find a switched off phone at home

If the device was simply lost indoors, then the first thing to do is to recall the circumstances under which it was last used and where. Perhaps he just fell over a table or a nightstand, the back cover opened from a blow and the battery flew out. If this does not help, you can restore order in the apartment. The likelihood that the phone will be found in the process, as well as a few things previously lost, is very high. If neither memories nor cleaning help, you can move on to more radical measures.

The answer to the question of how to find the phone if it is turned off depends on the model and capabilities of the device. For example, if an alarm clock is set on it, which works regardless of whether the gadget is currently working or not, it remains only to recall at what time it should ring, and wait for this moment.





How to find a turned off phone on the street

Many believe that if the device was stolen or lost, you can say goodbye to it. After all, to call him to find a place of position, will not work. And all versions using computers, special programs, satellites, etc. - this is nothing more than a myth. Actually, it is really difficult to do this, but there are still options for finding a phone if it is turned off. Firstly, you can send an SMS message to it from another number with a notification that the device is lost, and a promise of remuneration for the return. If he is not stolen and someone finds him, he will surely turn on the device, see SMS and will gladly return the device to the owner. After all, getting a reward from the owner is much safer and easier than trying to sell the phone you found without documents.

Using special services



In general, you should think about how to find the phone if it is turned off before you even lose it. Modern devices have various functions that provide blocking of devices during theft, as well as their search using GPS navigation and other resources. It is recommended that you set up phones equipped with Android OS and Apple devices immediately after purchase in such a way that if you lose them using a regular computer, the device can be locked. And not the card, but the phone itself.

There are also programs that capture and report the coordinates of the point at which the device gave its last signal before turning off. If the phone was just lost and is still where it happened, such information will be quite enough.

If all the methods have been tried and none have helped, it remains only to contact the law enforcement authorities and hope for their work. Or say goodbye to the phone and buy yourself a new one. According to the law of meanness, the old will immediately be found by itself.
