Greece, Rhodes: 5 star all-inclusive hotels. Best Rhodes Holiday Hotels

Rhodes is the most beautiful resort in Greece. The island, surrounded by greenery, with a comfortable climate and a huge cultural and historical heritage attracts the attention of tourists from different parts of the globe. If you want to fully enjoy all the pleasures of a holiday in Rhodes, you prefer a beach holiday, 5-star all-inclusive hotels will be an excellent option for you. Greece, Rhodes is a European resort where there are not so many hotels of such a high level, but we will present only the best that are really worth all the money spent. Reviews of real tourists and vacationers will help to produce the most objective review.

Bellevue suites

I want to start the review from this hotel. The complex is located right on the beach of Rhodes, just 2 km from its medieval part. This is a great and truly win-win option for those vacationers who are not lovers of tiring excursions, long trips, but would love to stroll through the narrow Greek streets in the evening.





It offers elegant, spacious rooms, each with a balcony overlooking the calm Aegean Sea. The territory of the complex is small, but it has everything that is necessary for a comfortable stay: two sea-water pools, a restaurant with traditional European and international cuisine, a snack bar with cocktails and cold drinks, a fitness center. All those who spent their holidays here celebrate impeccable service, really comfortable and spacious rooms, as well as impeccable food performed by a local chef.

An excellent hotel option for those who are not ready to spend their whole vacation on the beach, loves short city walks and shopping.

Esperos Village Resort

We have just presented a hotel for active tourists. But this option will surely appeal to all those who are looking for 5 star hotels on the first line. Rhodes is significantly different from the very popular resorts of Turkey and Egypt, so finding a complex with its own beach and access to the sea is very problematic here. That is why all lovers of beach holidays will appreciate this option. Esperos Village Resort is awarded the highest ratings from tourists and vacationers: all those who spent their holidays here once, come back here again and again.

The hotel occupies a hillside, 12 km away from the city, so all vacationers can enjoy the peace, tranquility and regularity of the island beach holiday. The rooms are equipped with modern technology, quite spacious, comfortable, with a spectacular view of the sea from the balcony.





The vast territory of the complex, well-developed infrastructure is what sets the Esperos Village Resort apart from other hotels. Several restaurants, bars, conference rooms, currency exchange, a pool with sun loungers and umbrellas, tennis courts, sports grounds, a variety of entertainment programs are something that all vacationers will have access to.

The food is varied, high-quality, balanced - certainly no one will remain hungry. An excellent hotel for family holidays, which every year only gets better.

And here is another great five-star hotel located in the picturesque part of Rhodes, surrounded by stunning fruit trees. Comfortable rooms, first-class service and a pleasant atmosphere are what all tourists celebrate. A choice of standard double and family rooms - everyone will find the best option for themselves. All rooms, regardless of category, are equipped with modern stylish furniture and all the necessary equipment: air conditioning, satellite TV, mini fridge, player, telephone, radio, modern bathroom equipment in the bathroom, which does not cause any complaints.





A gorgeous green area, a lot of entertainment - what makes a stay at Atlantica Club Aegean Blue 5 * so high-quality. It is of particular interest for outdoor enthusiasts. The territory has a full-fledged fitness center, several pools, including sea water, a gym, massage, basketball and volleyball courts, a tennis court, a golf course and much more. On the territory of Atlantica Club Aegean Blue 5 * there are entertainment for children, so it is so often chosen not only by young people, but also families with small children.
