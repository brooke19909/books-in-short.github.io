Hotel Christofinia Hotel 4 * (Cyprus / Ayia Napa): overview, description, rooms and reviews of tourists

From time to time, every person needs to take a break from work and daily routines, change the scenery, have fun and just relax. For this purpose, travelers, as a rule, choose resorts on the seashore, because here you can not only have a good time, but also improve your health.

Quite a popular destination among tourists is Cyprus. The last few decades, the tourism business has developed rapidly, and therefore the country has a lot to offer the traveler. Here you will find not only soft sandy beaches, but also entertainment centers, interesting historical sights and, of course, high-quality service. Christofinia Hotel 4 * - a hotel with a good reputation. Every year thousands of tourists stay in it.

Of course, during travel planning, you will probably try to collect all the possible information. For example, where is the hotel located and what distance do you have to travel to the beach every day? What conditions in the rooms can you count on? Hotel complex Christofinia Hotel (Ayia Napa, Cyprus): description of the territory

This hotel is considered not too big. It consists of a large multi-storey building, in which, in fact, are living rooms, restaurants, entertainment and office facilities. But the territory of the hotel is quite spacious, decorated with palm trees, greenery and bright exotic flowers. By the way, in the evening, the courtyard, building and pools are beautifully lit, and therefore it is always pleasant to walk around the territory.

By the way, the hotel was built in 1988. The last major restructuring was carried out in 2013, and today the hotel meets all international tourism standards. This place combines the charm and hospitality of Cyprus, all the benefits of modern civilization and high-class service.

What do the hotel rooms look like? Photos and brief info





Even if you prefer to spend your holidays having fun all day on the beaches, walking around the city and exploring the surroundings, in the evening you always want to return to a comfortable room and relax on a soft bed. Therefore, living conditions are important for every tourist. What does the Christofinia Hotel 4 * (Ayia Napa) offer? Reviews from people who have already spent some time here say that the rooms here are actually comfortable and clean.

To begin with, it is worth saying that the hotel has 130 rooms. For the most part, these are standard rooms that are designed for 1-2 adults (plus a child). But there are several large family rooms that are perfect for family vacations or for tourists who travel in large groups.

Almost every room has access to a private balcony, where you can enjoy the fresh air, views of the sea and the beautiful territory of the hotel. The atmosphere here is modern, the furniture is new and comfortable. Of course, you can count on a set of household appliances, because without it, you must agree, it is difficult to imagine real comfort.

Convenient and easy to manage climate control system will help to create the most comfortable temperature conditions in the room. Evenings can be spent watching TV - there is the opportunity to watch satellite channels and even some Russian. It features a direct dial telephone as well as a radio. There is a minibar with a refrigerator, but it is filled only by order of guests, and this service is not included in the price.

Of course, the room has a private bathroom, equipped with modern fixtures. There is a toilet. You can also use a shower or bath, sink for washing. There is also a stationary hair dryer for drying hair, which is convenient, especially for women. You can count on a set of quality cosmetics for personal hygiene, as well as a daily change of towels. By the way, cleaning is carried out every day, and, according to reviews, maids work very responsibly.

Conditions for water recreation and entertainment?

Not all tourists just need to swim in the sea and take sun baths - they need activity and entertainment. Can the guests of the Christofinia Hotel 4 * hotel count on this? Cyprus is a place where lovers of sailing and motor sports, scuba diving and other activities gather.

Near the coast it is easy to rent a boat, catamaran or canoe. For a small fee, tourists have the opportunity to go water-skiing, banana or boat. And on almost any beach you can rent a board and a suit for windsurfing. You can even take a few lessons from professionals. There is also a scuba diving center. Travelers with the necessary certificates here can get the necessary equipment and get an excursion to a place with the richest underwater world. But beginners will first have to take a training course and get acquainted with safety precautions.

In addition, you can take a short excursion on a yacht, explore the sights of the coast and simply admire the exotic beauty of the island. On the beaches, as a rule, there are sports grounds where you can stretch yourself playing volleyball or futsal.

The list of additional services





What service does the Christofinia hotel offer its customers? 4 stars "Christofinia" (Cyprus) received well-deserved, as it is an excellent hotel. Naturally, guests can count on everything you need. For example, in public areas there is free access to the Internet, however, reviews indicate that the connection here is sometimes very slow.
