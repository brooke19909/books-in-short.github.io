The best resorts in Greece. What a good resort of Greece to choose: reviews and photos

Vacation is a time when you can forget about pressing problems, free yourself from the shackles of work and life, plunge into the world of your dreams. Today, many are attracted to holidays in neighboring countries and Europe. We will get acquainted with one of the most attractive countries for tourists and find out what a good resort in Greece is suitable for a good rest.

general description

The location of the country is very interesting and attractive. It is washed by the Mediterranean, Aegean, Ionian and Libyan seas. This provides a lot of options for vacationers and tourists. You can choose a place for active spending time or a beach option. Many exotic species and nature are available due to the presence of more than two thousand islands.



In addition, the climatic conditions, which are represented by the Mediterranean, temperate and alpine types, also give a choice.

For lovers of wildlife, the country is interesting for the presence of a variety of plants and animals, which are listed in the Red Book. For example, a carriage tortoise or a monk seal. Although today the nature of Greece is somewhat impoverished due to the centuries-old extermination of exotic animals and vegetation.

For climbers and lovers of mountain tourism there is where to take a walk. The presence of a variety of rocks, grottoes. From a bird's eye view, picturesque landscapes open up, the best resorts in Greece, photos of which you can see later in the article.





Capital

Resorts in Greece can be divided into several types:

  • continental;

  • island

  • peninsula.

We begin the review from the first and, having included all our imagination, we will go to the capital - Athens. This is a good resort in Greece for all who are interested in history, visiting museums, exhibitions and historical sights.

The city is famous for its architectural buildings of the past. Here you can visit the Temple of Zeus, the Acropolis, the Parthenon, the Tower of the Winds and dozens of fascinating places. Many tourists believe that it is Athens that should open up acquaintance with Greece.

Active, inquisitive people rest here will give a lot of vivid impressions.

However, even ordinary metro rides will not leave you indifferent. Each station is a mini-museum with exhibits of antiquity.

Be prepared that this is a city suffering from overpopulation: a huge number of cars, difficulties with free taxis. But at the same time, you can plunge into an amazing and lively world, just walking along the narrow streets and discovering new secrets for yourself.





Thessaloniki

When planning your holidays in Greece, the best resorts of which are open for tourists all year round, consider visiting the capital of Macedonia. The city combines historical and modern motifs. Here you can visit museums, exhibitions, see excavations that are carried out right on the streets, and at the same time look into fashion boutiques.

The city is famous for its cultural life, which does not stop with the onset of darkness. Night lights open up completely different pictures. Luxurious restaurants, cozy cafes and nightclubs with incendiary parties.

The symbol of the city is the White Tower, located on the waterfront. It used to be part of the wall. Not far from it is the archaeological museum, one of the best in Europe, churches belonging to the Byzantine period, monasteries on Mount Athos, belonging to the period of early Christianity.





Hair

Another good resort in Greece - the capital of the county of Magnesia. According to ancient Greek myths, it was from here that the Argonauts sailed to Colchis for the Golden Fleece. The third largest city is Volos. This is a major shopping and tourist center.

The location of the resort is quite favorable. Being approximately at the same distance from the capital and Thessaloniki, you can easily visit these two beautiful scenic spots. Moreover, the traffic intersection is quite convenient.

Getting lost in Thessaloniki is simply impossible. All streets are located strictly perpendicular or parallel to the coastline.

Interesting to visit are various attractions. For example, neoclassical palaces built in the 19th century, or churches in the Anavros area. You can see interesting landscapes by taking a ride on an old miniature train that passes settlements on Mount Pilion.

You can quench your thirst for gastronomic and aesthetic impressions in the Velendzas bakery, where the walls are painted by the famous artist Theoflis.





Crete

This place can rightly be called a highlight of tourist Greece. The island will open for you starry nights and give you unforgettable sunny days. Crete is located in southern Europe and is washed immediately by 3 seas.

Beach holidays are quite convenient and comfortable. Moreover, there are hundreds of picturesque landscapes. The minimum difference in temperature between air and water makes the island just perfect for swimming. Judging by the reviews, it is interesting here for water sports professionals and beginners. There are many special institutions on the coast, ready to teach everyone.

Climatic conditions allow you to enjoy your vacation for a long time: from May to October. This is a place with very soft and clean air. Dependent on pressure drops, people will feel comfortable here.

If you are looking for the best resorts in Greece for youth, then the island of Crete is perfect. For example, Hersonissos is one of the most visited resorts. Stormy life boils here from dusk to dawn. Lots of cafes, bars, trendy dance floors and clubs.





Rhodes

One of the sunniest places on the planet. Rhodes is the real gem of the Mediterranean. Differences in relaxation - luxury and high cost.

Washed on both sides by different seas, the Aegean and the Mediterranean, it has two guises. The western part is suitable for windsurfing enthusiasts: high waves, the raging turbulent sea. In the east, you will find calm water, sandy beaches - all to enjoy the silence.

If you are looking for the best resorts in Greece, reviews from tourists can help you find your way. For example, many choose Rhodes for a solitary time. For someone, this is a great chance to get acquainted with history, culture. Here you can visit the Palace of the Grand Masters, built in the 14th century. Amazing spacious halls with mosaic floors, excellent panoramic sea views.

In the east of the island there is a valley of butterflies, filled with lush greenery and flowers, with many streams and waterfalls. From June to September, it becomes home to a million colorful butterflies that carpet her.





Zakynthos

This island opens for tourists the best beach resorts in Greece. Zakynthos is washed by the waters of the Ionian Sea, among the picturesque cliffs and lush greenery. Landscapes of the island remind of fabulous groves. The length of the beaches is more than one hundred and twenty kilometers. The mild continental climate is the key to a great vacation at any time of the year.

Incredibly beautiful natural landscape, thousands of amazing species of plants, beautiful underwater world. The island attracts tourists who are interested in diving and exploring sunken ships.

The calling card of Zakynthos is Navagio Bay. In 1983, a modern ship wrecked in this place, and now you can see it with your own eyes, because it still remained on the beach.

There are many hotels. Starting from 2 stars and above. Therefore, regardless of wealth, you can enjoy the beauties of nature and snow-white sandy beaches.

Peloponnese

This resort is located in the south of Greece. This place is attractive for its attractions of the ancient period. In particular, Sparta, one of the most famous states where strict order reigned and real feats were accomplished. Or Mycenae - the center of ancient civilization, where you can see huge statues of lions decorating the entrance to the palace. Here is the birthplace of the Olympic Games, to this day, a fire is lit in the ancient stadium.

Tourists will be able to visit the oldest theater Epidavros, which operates today. The architecture of the building is created in such a way that strikes with its acoustics.

This is a good resort in Greece, offering a lot of additional entertainment. Interested in sports? Then welcome: rafting, horseback riding, mountain hiking - all this is here.

The beaches on the peninsula are sandy and pebble. Cozy cafes, shops and bars are located along them.





Northern part

What else can be included in the list of "The best resorts of Greece"? Halkidiki is a peninsula that has an interesting shape resembling a trident. Each of its so-called ends has its own zest.

Kassandra is a great place for a beach holiday, it has clean white beaches. Sithonia is filled with greenery, amazing landscapes. This place is chosen for unity with nature. Athos is called the kingdom of monks. There are many churches of the Byzantine period.

The largest city of Chalchidiki is Nea Moudania. In it you can find everything you need: shops, hotels, banks, hospitals. Many tourists visit the city for hiking in fur boutiques. Not far from the center is the city beach.

Whatever you choose, the best resorts in mainland Greece or the island, you will get a lot of vivid impressions and good mood.
