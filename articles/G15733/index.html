Convex shuttlecocks

A dress with a sleeveless flounce will turn into a real masterpiece if these wavy details are attached to the seams according to the relief of the product. Although such a model will not have sleeves, the hanging flounces will create a deceptive effect of their presence. The procedure for modeling such a dress is as follows:

  • The basis of the model is the basic pattern of the dress.
  • Shortened by 2 cm shoulder length.
  • On the vertical of the product, relief seams are modeled.
  • On these seams, segments of the desired length (front and rear) are marked for stitching shuttlecocks. Stitches for sewing and the width of the shuttlecocks can be different.

Wavy sleeve on cuts (armholes)

The coming spring-summer season was marked by flying dresses with frills and ruffles, which playfully fall down layers and playfully flutter in the wind. The model may be closed, have long chiffon or silk sleeves, and may have a deep neckline. Especially chic look evening dresses.

This season, the trend is a dress with a flounce sleeve. The accent in the form of ruffle can be both on short and long sleeves. Many designers use this detail when developing street-style models. She gives the image playfulness, lightness. Baby-dollar dresses have become popular this season. They are distinguished by a free waist and defiant voluminous sleeves in the form of a gramophone.

An elegant dress with a flounce sleeve can be made using the classic model of such a sleeve, that is, insert it into the armhole. Such shuttlecocks differ in size, multilayer. To add splendor and volume, they can be gathered around the circumference of the sleeves. Such shuttlecocks are sewn as follows:

  • four elements of shuttlecocks are cut out;
  • Using a gasket, copy two external elements;
  • the outer edge is subjected to processing;
  • determine the length of the sleeve on the shoulder and from the armpit;
  • measure the length of the cutout, shelves and backs;
  • make a pattern of two parts of the shuttlecock with an arc, retreating for seams of 1.5 cm;
  • shuttlecocks sew in armholes.

One-shoulder asymmetric cut

Chiffon fabric in two or more layers will ideally look like a shuttlecock on the shoulder. The top of this item can be slightly tucked up. The pattern of such ruffle is done in a spiral, then the waves are more dense. The modeling process is as follows:

  • take the basic pattern as a basis;
  • the dotted line for sewing the shuttlecock is carried out at the top left of the chest tuck;
  • model a recessed neckline and strap (3 cm) for the right side of the product;
  • simulate the back in the same way;
  • measure the length of the segment for ruffle and draw a spiral, adding 20 cm for assembly;
  • the width of the shuttle should be 20-22 cm;
  • 4 elements of shuttlecocks are cut out;
  • sweep the outer edge of the parts;
  • perform assemblies on the upper segment;
  • fold together and along the marked lines on the shelf and the back they grind a shuttlecock.

Features patterns of short and long flounce sleeves

If you know how to pattern a classic sleeve, then making a wavy part is not difficult. Such an element is a bit like a wing. A short sleeve pattern is done as follows:

  • a paper shuttlecock template is cut into strips 2-3 cm wide, leaving 4-5 cm for the okata;
  • they take a second sheet of paper, lay a pattern on it, unfold it in the form of a fan so that the okat becomes a circle;
  • outline the pattern;
  • Sew to the armhole along the edge of the inner circle.

An extended flounce sleeve is also able to decorate a simple dress model. Perform such an element according to the following plan:

  • on the dress, measure the length of the outline along which the quilling will be sewn;
  • take measurements of the shuttlecock itself;
  • draw a circle equal in length to that indicator that was obtained in the first stage;
  • should get a donut pattern;
  • 1.5 cm allowances for seams;
  • process the outer edge of the shuttle;
  • sew the part along the inside of the circle.

How to sew a dress with a flounce sleeve

For a holiday exit, a dress with a frill on one shoulder of an asymmetric cut is perfect. Most often, a multilayer shuttlecock is made of chiffon, which is additionally collected from above. To make such a quilling thick, it is cut out in a spiral.

So, how to make such a dress? The bodice of this product is modeled on the basic pattern. Now you need to attach a shuttlecock. To do this, from the left breast tuck, marking is made to grind the shuttlecock. It turns out a model of a deep neckline of a dress. After the quilling is cut out and overcast, it must be picked up and stitched according to the marking in the seam. There may be different options for a dress with frills on the sleeves. The relevance of lush frills

It is very easy to make frills, even a beginner will cope with it. The romance of such a dress is guaranteed. As always, the basic pattern is taken as a basis. Then perform the following steps:

  • shoulder length is reduced by 2 cm;
  • convex seams are made on the shelf and back;
  • the notch of the neck is divided in half and a line is drawn from the convex seam to the armhole;
  • model a set-in element for sewing frills;
  • Mark the frill stitching lines;
  • cut out 3 frills of the required width;
  • the length of the frills is determined by the pattern, multiplying the figures by two.

How to grind frills? First, they are washed along the lower edges with the help of a role seam. Using a stitch, pick up each frill to the desired size. Allowances are covered with an overlock seam. Then they tune one frill after another. The armholes are machined with slanting piping.
