How to make doll houses for children with their own hands

The current toy industry is truly amazing in its variety of products. Indeed, modern children have access to what their parents did not even dream of in their distant childhood.

Relevance





Plush animals, dolls of various sizes, houses, furniture and clothing for them, radio-controlled cars and even helicopters. It is not surprising that often parents themselves are not averse to trying out all these new products with their children. But there was a time when all this had to be done with one's own hands, and therefore each such thing was unique and became the subject of boasting and pride in front of friends. So let's bring these wonderful moments to life again and remember how to make doll houses for our beloved daughters. In addition, the process itself will greatly delight you and your children, as well as significantly strengthen relationships with them.

Training



Perhaps the cheapest option is a cardboard dollhouse . It is not difficult to do such a thing: for this you need to cut the old box from under the TV or a washing machine (they are the most suitable in size) in half, taking the first half as a base, and from the second making partitions for floors, stairs and, if left, small furniture. It remains only to decorate it with colored paper, self-adhesive wallpaper or paints and equip the premises. However, such a house will last quite a while, even if you handle it carefully. Therefore, the best and only option for us is nothing more than to make the dollhouse itself out of wood or a finished small bookshelf by adding a roof to it. In this case, it would be better to introduce the second parent of the family, having previously come up with a sketch. After all, who, if not a man with golden hands, will tell you how to make doll houses reliable and strong.





And he will independently acquire the material and make the basis for our creativity from plywood or particleboard, without forgetting about the slots for doors and windows.
