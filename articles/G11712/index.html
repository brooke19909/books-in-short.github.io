Erroneous accrual of KBM when renewing an insurance policy





When extending insurance, many drivers are faced with the fact that its cost is very high, and the discount, on the contrary, has decreased or completely disappeared. The fact is that since 2014 all insurance information has been entered into a single database, when entered into which information may be indicated incorrectly. As a result of this, when renewing car insurance, the final cost of the policy may be incorrectly calculated. Moreover, the reason for this is not the intention of the insurer to deceive his client, but in the human factor.

Discount size decreased: what is the reason?

If you want to save on insurance for your vehicle and are thinking about how to return money for KBM under CTP, then you first need to figure out why the size of the discount can be reduced. This can occur for several reasons, which will be discussed later.

Change of type of insurance policy

As you use the insurance policy, each driver accumulates points, which he can then use for discounts. However, when changing the insurance program, for example, when switching from a regular program to a policy with an unlimited validity period, these points can be reset. How to return KBM for compulsory motor third-party liability insurance in this case? This is entirely possible only if a new insurance policy is issued for the same vehicle by one owner. Otherwise, the accumulated points are burned out, and you can not count on any discount.

Approval to drive multiple vehicles

If the driver, due to his activity, has the right to drive different cars at the same time, and he is indicated in several insurance policies, the insurer may not take into account points when extending insurance. How to return money under the CBM OSAGO policy in this case? Almost nothing. The thing is that the insurance company, when calculating the cost of the policy, takes into account the number of points from the driver who has fewer points. This is prescribed in the contract, therefore, the right to overstate the coefficient is completely legal and is reserved by the insurance company.

Accident or accident insurance





The presence of accidents involving the driver also affects the number of points and the size of the discount. If the interval between accidents is less than 12 months, then all accumulated points are completely burned out. If the driver drives very carefully and does not get into traffic accidents, then the cost of the insurance policy for him decreases by 5 percent every year.

How to restore KMB: a step-by-step guide

So, you have prepared all the necessary documents and installed a mistake that led to a reduction in the insurance discount. Solving the problem directly with the insurance company





If you are considering the question of how to return CBM CTP in Rosgosstrakh, an application to the Central Bank of the Russian Federation will be one of the most effective ways, since this body can deprive the insurer of a license, which absolutely all companies are afraid of. Therefore, each insurer considers all complaints and appeals of its customers and tries to resolve them as soon as possible so as not to make public.

Today, the websites of most insurance companies have a special section, by going to which the client can fill out a special form, describe the essence of the problem and attach scans of all necessary documents. In order to further motivate the insurance company in considering your appeal and solving the problem, along the way, you can send by registered mail a separate appeal with an attached package of necessary documents.

Complaint to the Central Bank of the Russian Federation

How to return CBM for compulsory motor liability insurance in Rosgosstrakh by filing a complaint with the CBR? This method is the most effective today, because the Central Bank is very tough on any complaints about insurance companies received from citizens. To file a complaint, you need to do the following:

  1. Go to the website of the Central Bank and go to the "CTP Information" section. At the bottom of the page there will be a corresponding button, clicking on which an online form for processing a complaint will be opened.
  2. In the form that opens, select "Incorrect use of KBM."
  3. You will be redirected to the information page, on which you will need to click the "No, proceed to file a complaint" button, fill in all the fields and attach scanned copies of the necessary documents as an attachment.
  4. On the next page you need to specify your details and contact phone number.

The time for consideration of customer complaints by the Central Bank of the Russian Federation takes 10 calendar days, for which your problem will be completely resolved.

How to return an overpayment on an incorrectly calculated insurance coefficient?
