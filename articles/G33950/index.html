Club President and Tunisian Village 3 * (Tunisia, Hammamet): hotel description, services, reviews

If, for any reason, the vacation had to be moved from summer to autumn, this is not a reason for despair and worries that a full rest will not work. On the contrary, there is a chance to go to those countries where all the fun begins in the fall. For example, in Tunisia. Very good news is that tourist packages to this country in October are cheaper than in other periods.

Tunisia weather conditions in October





The weather in Tunisia in October is quite favorable in order to receive many tourists here.

The climate in these places is subtropical, however, there are times when nature allows people to relax from the scorching sun and enjoy the coolness emanating from sea waves. This time is considered the height of the velvet season in Tunisia. This climate, due to its mildness, is suitable for those vacationers who suffer from cardiovascular and pulmonary ailments, and simply for those who can not stand the sharp jumps in air temperature. In addition to all of the above, this month it is better to prefer a trip to Tunisia to married couples with children, since the risk of complications during climate adaptation at this time is minimal. The weather in Tunisia in October is not constant, which cannot be said about the summer months, when there is a monotonous increase in temperature and an increase in humidity. A very high probability of a large amount of precipitation at this time. It is almost impossible to predict in advance the moment when the solar disk will again appear in all its glory and will delight you with warmth.

Features of the autumn holiday

According to statistics, around mid-October, clouds disperse, and sunlight more and more often breaks through to go outside. However, as soon as the third decade of the month comes, the situation with weather forecasts changes radically, and the clouds become denser. That is why those tourists who are not too attached to the time, it is better to set a departure date for the second half of October. According to the workers of the hydrometeocenter, this year it is expected only 9 rainy days from the whole season. The island of Djerba and the North of Tunisia are considered the wettest areas, and then things are much calmer with humidity. As for temperature values, everything is arranged a little differently. Djerb and Mahdia in the first 10 days of the month boast a temperature of about + 28-30 degrees, and in the last - from 25 to 28 degrees. In the evening, the temperature drops to 24 degrees. Approaching the North, you can notice that the temperature indicators from time to time become lower and lower. If we are talking about Sousse, Monastir and Sofax, then there the column of the thermometer grows in the direction of the interval of 24 and 26 degrees. In the evening, the column rises from 18 to 20 degrees. As for the capital, here the temperature is 2-3 degrees lower, and in the evening you generally have to wear warm clothes, as it gets colder up to 15-16 degrees. By the way, at other resorts of the South of the foreign countries during this period, vacationers also need to warm up a little, since October in these areas is a time of winds, which also do not please. In the afternoon, the weather can also suddenly deteriorate, but not enough to start worrying about it. What corner of Tunisia will go to? There are many beautiful resorts here, one of which is Hammamet.

Resort Description



The resort town of Hammamet is located 70 kilometers from Tunisia itself. This cozy town is built in a Muslim manner, the main distinguishing feature of which is the presence of high gold domes. The reception area in Hammamet spans a distance of approximately 14 kilometers. The north side of the city borders on a town called Nebel.

Club President and Tunisian Village Hotel 3 *





This wonderful hotel is located in the North of Hammamet. Nearby there is a spacious golf course and a private beach, equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. Near the beach there is a beautiful water park with lots of entertainment for children, which, incidentally, come to the city a lot. The path from the water park to the central part of Hammamet is about 6 kilometers. The entire hotel complex consists of one building with 5 floors and original, but at the same time very cozy and bright bungalows that are scattered throughout the hotel and are buried in greenery. One of the main assets of the Club President and Tunisian Village 3 * hotel is the number of rooms.

Accommodation





It offers tourists 67 spacious rooms, each of which, even regardless of class and cost, has a spacious loggia with a picturesque view from the windows. Each room is made in pastel colors, which is a great addition to the natural light in the rooms. The layout of all hotel rooms is concise and comfortable.

Infrastructure





Regarding the issue of food, guests are invited to visit 2 restaurants of Tunisian and European cuisine, where the atmosphere of celebration, comfort and peace reigns.
The hotel has three swimming pools of different depths, one of which is designed for children, the second is equipped for adults and older people, and the third is suitable for all without restriction. The last pool is even equipped with a miniature waterfall, which, without a doubt, will attract a lot of attention.

The most important property of the Club President and Tunisian Village 3 * hotel is the beach. This is not only a place for sunbathing.

Beach activities



For leisure people relaxing on the beach, special courses for mastering the Dominican dance, called Bochata, have been created, and there is also a convenient platform for active ball games (football, volleyball, baseball and others). Every day, guests of the Club President and Tunisian Village 3 * hotel (Hammamet) will enjoy exciting contests and entertainment programs that will never leave anyone bored. These events last all day and flow into the evening, until dusk. Very often, cheerful competitions and raffle of trifling prizes are held, uplifting. After night descends on the city, young people seek to visit open-air discos and clubs with relaxing music and cocktails.

Go shopping

On the territory of the Club President and Tunisian Village 3 * hotel (Tunisia) and near it, vacationers can find souvenir shops that sell all kinds of little things that give a memory of beautifully spent days. In these shops you can find crafts made from natural materials, seashells, local perfumes, alcohol and more. In general, there is everything you can only dream about. Among other things, next to souvenir shops you can find a jewelry salon, an office for organizing excursion trips, as well as a small, but having all the necessary self-service store.

Service

Club President and Tunisian Village 3 * hotel guests have access to wireless Internet, a left-luggage office, a new guest registration service, guarded 24 hours a day, and free parking for cars. The hotel has a high-speed elevator and currency exchange. There is a souvenir shop and a beautiful garden with many landscaping figures.

Sport in this hotel also does not go unnoticed: vacationers are invited to play a game of golf, play on the tennis court, ride a bike on the territory. There is also a gym. There are windsurfing, horse riding, volleyball and basketball.

As for the sphere of building a business, Club President and Tunisian Village 3 * hotel offers the services of a spacious conference room. Experienced secretaries can also send important documents by fax.

Room amenities include: shower, toilet, sink, TV with many educational channels, miniature bar, refrigerator and climate control system (air conditioning). Rooms are heated during the cold season.

It is noteworthy that the hotel, in addition to ordinary apartments, also has those that are suitable for guests with disabilities. The hotel also has family rooms.

Speaking about transport, it is worth mentioning the presence of a transfer and a rental service for vehicles. In order to clean clothes from stains and dirt, you can use the laundry and dry cleaning services.

Leisure

Of the entertainment in the Club President and Tunisian Village 3 * hotel, vacationers allocate a karaoke bar, a billiard room, a night club and animator services. For those who like checking accuracy, the hotel is equipped with darts and a target for playing darts.

For the female half of the population, the hotel offers a beauty salon, massage services with all types of massage and a hairdresser.

For children, Club President and Tunisian Village 3 * provided a separate pool, a games room with a nanny and lots of toys, a children's bed in each room, and a kids club.

Getting to the hotel is very simple, just change a taxi and get to Hammet. Karthaj Airport is just 62 kilometers away by car. Nearby is a hotel called Magic life Manar.
