Holiday homes in Ryazan and the region: overview, features and reviews

From the bustle of the city I want to break out into nature for a short time and relax in the fresh air in silence. Holiday homes in Ryazan are located in different parts of the region. Many of them are located on the banks of water bodies or have artificial pools on their territory. Sanatoriums and rest houses in Ryazan have different levels of comfort and pricing. A list of these institutions in the article will help tourists decide on a place to spend their holidays or weekends.

"Wave"

This holiday home in Ryazan is located 40 km from the city on the shores of Lake Selnoe. This complex is designed for family pastime or relaxation with friends. The house is located in a pine forest. The complex is surrounded by greenery and flowers. In the morning, a pine aroma is clearly felt here.

Here you can stay in single, double and triple rooms. An upgraded room is also provided. The complex has guest houses, which can accommodate up to 6 people.

In the "Wave" for outdoor activities tourists are provided with:

  • billiards, table tennis, disco, minibar, karaoke bar, wireless Internet access;
  • contests, shows, and concerts;
  • a playroom is equipped for children - both on the street and in the building;
  • sports equipment rental;
  • gym and sauna;
  • conference hall.

The complex has a cozy dining room. Here you can order complex meals or individual meals. A day's stay with meals will cost a tourist an average of 2000 rubles.

Park-Hotel "Festival"

This complex is located near the Meshchersky National Park. The holiday house in Ryazan "Festival" is built according to European principles. It is located in a protected area. Near the complex there are several ponds with clean water.

Guests can pre-book their stay in the following cottages:

  1. VIP is a two-story house with three bedrooms and a spacious lounge. Equipped with a sauna, relaxation area, kitchen, leather furniture and several bathrooms with bathtubs. Cost per day - 18 600 rubles.
  2. Russian Estate - a two-story cottage built in a traditional style. 3 bedrooms, on the ground floor there is a spacious studio room, sauna, equipped kitchen and bathrooms. Price per day - 22 600 rubles.
  3. Summer fairy tale - a small house on two floors. Spacious beds, a kitchen, a bathroom are equipped, there is a private outdoor veranda and landscaped area for picnics. Cost - 9 800 rubles.
  4. Austrian buildings are an ideal offer for a family of up to 4 people. Comfortable furniture, a small kitchen, own territory. Price - 6,100.
  5. Rooms of different levels of comfort in the hotel "Vikings" and "Provence" (from 3500 rubles).
  6. Cottage "Rainbow" - can accommodate up to 7 people. There are all necessary furniture and appliances. Price - 7,100.

There are equipped places for sports and outdoor activities. There is a cafe, a bathhouse and a sauna on site. Guests can rent sports equipment at the property.

"Makarovo-dacha"

This recreation center in Ryazan is located in the Klepikovsky district on the banks of the Pra River. Here usually rest youth companies. The complex offers its customers accommodation in cottages.

The houses have all the necessary furniture, bathrooms and their own equipped kitchens. Guests can take a dip in the clear river water. Sports grounds for playing soccer, volleyball and basketball are equipped on site. Bicycles are available for rent.

The complex has a bathhouse and a sauna. In the evening, discos are organized. Movies are broadcast in the summer cinema, a place for hammocks is equipped.

"Meschera"

This holiday home in the region of Ryazan is 72 km away. It was built about 20 years ago, but was regularly reconstructed. Guests can stay in the hotel in rooms from 2 to 4 people, as well as in separate cottages.

Paid parking is available on site. In the evening there is a small cafe. The recreation center has a Russian bath and a sauna. Guests can rent sports equipment at the property.

Tourists can organize fishing here or go hunting at the time permitted by law. The complex has a pool and table tennis area.

A children's health camp is open every summer. Each shift, it takes up to 300 people.

Rest house "Solotcha" in Ryazan

The resort is surrounded by a pine forest near the village of the same name. Not far from it flows the Staritsa River. The buildings of the complex are equipped with rooms of different levels of comfort.

There is a dining room on the territory of the sanatorium, in which three meals a day are served as a buffet. Also, tourists can eat in the cafe-bar.

It has a modern spa. In it, vacationers can take various healing baths. The center has several massage rooms where experienced professionals conduct sessions.

The sanatorium has a good diagnostic base. And also provides a number of procedures for the treatment and prevention of diseases of the digestive system, respiration, heart and blood vessels.

Guests of the complex can use sports equipment for outdoor activities. The resort has a Finnish and Russian baths, as well as a modern sauna. In the summer, vacationers can swim in the river and sunbathe on the equipped beach. In winter, tourists enjoy visiting the pool.

Holiday homes in Ryazan with pool

Most often, tourists are looking for a place to spend their holidays surrounded by forests and with a pond. But the presence of a pool in the complexes attracts more and more customers, especially in the cold season.

Balthazar is one such place. The recreation center is located in the resort village of Solotcha.

Guests are invited to visit the restaurant with European and Russian cuisine. The complex has a sauna and a large pool. Vacationers can rent sports equipment.

One of the most unique centers among sanatoriums and rest houses in Ryazan is the White Stones complex. It is equipped with freestanding small cottages and hotel rooms for vacationers.

Indoor gazebos are available for rent. A small eco-farm works here, where tourists can purchase fresh and natural products:

  • quail meat and eggs;
  • hens;
  • pheasants.

A squirrel aviary is established on the territory, where many fluffy red animals live. They are quite used to people and are happy to take goodies from their hands.

In the center there is a bath complex located in a two-story cottage. There is a fairly large pool. There is a spacious cafe with banquet facilities on site.

For young visitors, the complex was equipped with a modern playground on the street. Tourists can organize a fishing trip here or for berries and mushrooms.

"Royal fun"

This recreation center is located in the village of Lunino, Shilovsky district. Cottages are equipped here, which can accommodate from 4 to 12 people. On-site rental of ATVs, jet skis and motor boats.

It has a playground and a beach with water activities. On the territory there are gazebos with barbecue facilities.

The houses have all the necessary furniture and appliances. Each cottage is equipped with a kitchen with all necessary equipment.

Reviews

There are a lot of comments on the Internet regarding the work of holiday homes in Ryazan and the Ryazan region.

For example, tourists are a little disappointed with the arrangement of rooms in the Volna complex. They compare the design of rooms with the times of 80 years. Despite this, vacationers indicate that the rooms and grounds are always clean. Guests are happy with food and entertainment. They note that the prices for renting sports equipment are somewhat overstated.

In the Park Hotel Festival, tourists are pleased with the work of the water park. As well as vacationers celebrate quality food in the complex. A few comments arise about the deterioration of some sidewalks and the decoration of buildings in the center.

Makarovo-Dacha and Meshchera are considered a great place to relax for young people and small companies. Tourists note that a rather economical but interesting vacation is coming out. The comfort and equipment of the cottages correspond to the cost of living.

As for the rest of the complexes, tourists have practically no complaints, with the exception of some domestic nuances. In the sanatorium, tourists note a good medical base and delicious healthy food.
