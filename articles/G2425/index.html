Where was the city of Pyatigorsk founded?

This city is located in the Stavropol Territory of our country. Since January 2010, Pyatigorsk has been recognized as the administrative center of the North Caucasus District of the Russian Federation, and is also the largest city of the Caucasian Mineral Waters and the second largest after Stavropol.





In addition, Pyatigorsk is one of the oldest balneological and mud resorts of federal significance, and it is also the industrial, scientific, commercial, cultural and tourist center of Mineralnye Vody.

Five mountains

The name of the city also tells us where Pyatigorsk is located. How to understand this? Simply and easily.

The story is this: back in 1828, the domestic architect Giuseppe Bernardazzi designed the city. Two years later, the committee of ministers considered it, and General Emanuel proposed options for the names of the future city. Among them were: Konstantinogorsk, Novogeorgievsk and Pyatigorsk.

The choice was obvious. The city turned out to be located at the foot of Mount Beshtau, which literally translated from the Turkic language means five mountains. The question "where is Pyatigorsk" can be answered "at the foot of the five mountains."

In February 1830, the committee approved the city, as well as its name, and in May the Senate ordered that architects begin to build the city. This is the story of the appearance of Pyatigorsk on the map of the Russian Empire.





Geographic features

Pyatigorsk is a great place to relax, and all because the city is located on the Stavropol Upland on the banks of the mountain river Podkumok. There are still small rivers with clean and cold water. Tourists do not bathe in them. The region is rich in salt lakes with healing mud. The most popular of them are Tambukan and Lysogorsk lakes.

There are 4 mountains in the city:

  • Mashuk (there is a television tower on it) - 994 m;
  • Dubrovka - 690 m;
  • Picket - 565 m;
  • Post - 556 m.

The weather is hospitable. Pyatigorsk can be described as a city where mild winters and hot summers. Since there are many rivers and lakes around the area, the humidity here is moderate. And this contributes to the fact that in Pyatigorsk there is a huge number of forest zones. The annual rainfall is not more than 600 mm. Most precipitation is in June, least in January. As you already managed to understand that the climate here is favorable, which means that geographers would call it temperate-continental, without sharp and sudden fluctuations in temperature in the days.

Recreation, tourism, culture

Since Pyatigorsk is a resort city, this means that a large number of sanatoriums, boarding houses and various hospitals are located on its territory. This land is famous
an abundance of healthy mineral water. Among them: carbon dioxide, hydrogen sulfide, radon. There are also nitrogen and methane baths.

In order to improve their health, Caucasians also use healing mud. But you need to know that not everyone is equally useful.





In their free time from treatment, tourists have the opportunity to go to museums, visit Orthodox churches and monasteries. You can feel the extreme mountain, cycling, horse riding, climbing trips. There are many tour desks that offer a variety of routes with travel outside the city. This is a great opportunity to get into the mountains, where pure air, silence and peace reign. In the mountains you will find elegant vegetation, alpine meadows, noisy and beautiful waterfalls, as well as stunning mountain valleys. Among the majestic mountains, cafes have been built where tourists can taste the national cuisine of the highlanders, buy their outfits and other items as souvenirs and souvenirs!
