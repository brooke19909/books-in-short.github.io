How to make a bow out of a ribbon: mission, principles, examples

To decorate gifts and as an element of home decor, as part of the evening toilet - a beautiful bow is suitable for a wide variety of purposes. It can easily be done independently, you just need to buy a satin ribbon. And how to make a bow out of a ribbon - read the article.

What is a satin bow?

This product is made of satin ribbon, which is a strip of fabric woven from viscose or polyester with a smooth shiny surface. The bow itself is a magnificent knot (with loops loosely released) made of laces, ribbons, threads, decorative ribbons for wrapping gifts and more. How to make a bow from a satin ribbon? Just like with laces on sneakers, boots, sweatpants. How to make a beautiful ribbon bow? This will have to learn.

The principle of tying a bow

If beauty does not matter, then everything is quite simple. Making a bow from a ribbon with your own hands is easy. Surely, every person knows how to tie shoelaces. The principle is the same. Then simply spread the loops and lay the remaining ends of the tape.

Such bows are suitable for tying flowers in a bouquet, decorating gifts. If you want, you can not pore for three hours over satin ribbons in attempts to create a masterpiece. If you are satisfied with the simplicity of execution, there is no need to torture yourself, tie it like a lace and that's all.

Satin bow as an accessory

Probably, everyone will agree that without accessories, the image seems unfinished. Bows can play the role of such an important addition. Perhaps many will think about how to make a bow out of a ribbon that will not look ridiculous and ridiculous, and most importantly, to make it appropriate.

First, you need to figure out what role the bow will play. It can either be done separately by attaching to a purse, hat, gloves. A small bow to match the clothes will help fit into the image of sandals that differ in color, the same gloves or beret. Or on the waist you can make a bow from a ribbon with your own hands, which will be a bright addition to the image against the background of a plain blouse or dress. Secondly, make sure that the bow size is appropriate - it is unlikely that a bow the size of a soccer ball will look perfect on a tiny hat.

How to make a bow from a ribbon for interior decoration

There are many options for using satin bows for interior decoration. And in everyday life, and on holiday - you always want to create a cozy atmosphere, transform the room. At the celebration, bows can be tied to the backs of chairs as decorations. This will slightly dilute the uniformity of the design of the celebration and focuses the attention of guests on the solemnity of the moment.

Many people prefer not to leave bottles on the festive table without decoration. As an alternative to all kinds of stickers and pompous costumes, satin bows are suitable for bottles.

Such bottles will serve as an additional decoration of the table. In addition, by tying a bow in the middle of the bottle, you can cover the name of the drink so that guests do not realize that you did not spend a fortune on a drink.

Also, satin ribbons can be tied curtains, and tie the ends in neat bows. Tapes to choose either the colors of the curtains, or to match the walls or furniture. However, you do not have to use color suggestions.

Thus, it is possible to solve the problem of "dangling" curtains, additionally decorate the room and distract attention from any shortcomings in the choice of furniture or the color of the walls. Satin decoration is chosen either to match the curtains, or make it a bright accent.

conclusions

How to make a bow from a satin ribbon? Firstly, first you need to decide what you need a bow for. There are many options: finish packing the gift, arrange a bouquet, complement your image, decorate the room. Secondly, decide what the bow size will be. Do you need a gigantic size massive decoration from a wide ribbon, or do you prefer to use a small and neat bow that does not attract much attention. Thirdly, it is also important how to make a bow - tie or make.

If a bow is used to eliminate excess ribbon, then you just have to tie it. And if you need to decorate something, then you can make this detail to transform a specific object. When you figure out the purpose, appearance and method of manufacture, then you can start watching the training videos and direct training to make a beautiful bow from a satin ribbon with your own hands.
