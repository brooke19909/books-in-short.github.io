Sun City Apartments & Hotel 4 * (Side, Turkey): description, service, reviews

When deciding to spend a long-awaited vacation or an unforgettable family weekend in a sunny and hospitable Turkey, travelers gain a number of advantages. One of the cozy and popular places for tourists is the town of Side, which is characterized by the unique beauties of untouched nature and a developed hotel industry. One of the institutions that accommodates guests, providing high-quality service, a warm welcome and a lot of entertainment, is the four-star Sun City Apartments & Hotel.

general characteristics

The hotel complex Sun City Apartments & Hotel 4 *, the description of which will be presented to your attention in the article, opened its doors to receive guests at the turn of the millennium - in 2000. Having rich experience and the desire to improve, over the years of its operation, it has undergone many renovations, the last of which was carried out in 2013. Six detached residential buildings are surrounded by tropical palm trees, fragrant flowers and green lawns. The guests are delighted with the cleanliness of the walking alleys and the park area, which is in order daily.

The nearest airport is 70 km away. This distance allows you not to overwork when you reach the final destination for relaxation. Please note that pets are not allowed in the apartment. Accommodation

The total number of available rooms in the hotel Sun City Apartments & Hotel 4 * (Turkey) is 151 units. They differ in views opening from the window, layout and area.

The design of the spacious rooms is represented by a modest design, no frills and a special gloss. However, as guests note, living here is convenient and comfortable. So, the floor here is covered with laminate, and the zoning of the space includes a kitchen area. Each room also has a balcony or a private terrace. The color scheme contains bright shades that harmoniously fit into the overall decoration of the room.

A call-alarm service is available, according to which each guest will wake up at the scheduled time thanks to the work of the staff. Maids clean the room daily, bed linen is changed twice a week. There were no complaints about the cleanliness and order in the reviews left by tourists.





Arrangement

The furniture set in the room is standard: soft beds, chairs, a table, a wardrobe and bedside tables. There is a multi-channel TV, electronic safe, telephone and a set of dishes. The kitchen is equipped with a dishwasher, toaster, stove and other kitchen appliances.

As guests note, the bathroom has a modest design and is decorated with high-quality ceramic tiles. It is complemented by a shower, mirror, sink, a set of personal hygiene products and towels.

Wi-Fi - in the public domain. Iron and ironing board on request.





Satisfying hunger

Sun City Apartments & Hotel 4 * food offers wholesome and healthy. Tables with dishes served in the buffet way are bursting with an unprecedented variety. As guests note, there is everything here: vegetables, fruits, salads, snacks, desserts, seafood, meat, cereals and much more. Each guest independently puts on his plate what his heart desires. Aromatic Turkish coffee, freshly squeezed juices and foreign elite alcohol are paid extra.

In addition to the cozy main restaurant, which has a modern design and bright space, thanks to the French glazing, there are establishments that operate on a custom-made menu. Here you need to book a table in advance and follow the dress code when visiting.





Aquazone

The buildings of Sun City Apartments & Hotel 4 * in Side are surrounded by swimming pools, bathing in which is invaluable for human health. The central pool is surrounded by sun loungers with parasols. Next to it is a children's aquazone. The shallow pool is equipped with a waterslide, skating with which leads the kids into an unprecedented delight. Bathing of adults will be no less entertaining: two set descents of different modifications will diversify time spent in the fresh air and give unforgettable emotions to vacationers.

Experienced instructors conduct water aerobics classes daily. Light invigorating exercises appeal to all age groups. Maintaining the body in good shape and a charge of a positive mood like all participants in the workout. In the pool you can play water polo, for this there is the necessary equipment.

And in bad weather, vacationers can use the indoor pool.





Beach

The distance from the hotel Sun City Apartments & Hotel 4 * in Side to the coastal zone reaches 800 meters. In order for the spa guests to feel comfortable, a bus is launched in both directions, which runs for free. However, many guests note that the sea can be easily reached on foot, walking and enjoying the clean air.

The beach infrastructure includes sun loungers with awnings, a towel dispenser, showers and changing rooms. The entrance to the water is gentle. The golden sandy beaches are especially pleased for the children who, with genuine pleasure, build sand castles here, mold Easter cakes and dig tunnels.

There is a lifeguard service, which at any second are ready to come to the aid of every swimmer.

Fun on the beach

Busy leisure awaits travelers on the beach. Here you can not only visit attractions, including water scooters, riding a banana or a circle behind a motor boat, as well as parachuting, but also play volleyball on a specially equipped field.

However, the most interesting, as guests note, are diving and walks in the open sea. Both experienced divers and beginners can get acquainted with the unique underwater world of these parts.





Holiday for children

Positioning itself as a place for family vacations, the Sun City Apartments & Hotel 4 * service for creating a positive mood for young guests offers a bright and memorable - this is a kids club, which is designed for the age category from 4 to 12 years. He will allow each kid to visit the paradise of fabulous toy corner. Here you can jump on the trampoline complex, go down from different slides, run through the mazes and lie in the pool with colorful balls.

The children are looked after and monitored by a qualified teacher. In his competence - conducting master classes, entertaining games and exciting activities. Every little guest receives new emotions, vivid impressions and an unforgettable pastime.

No less attractive is the outdoor playground. During the day, a team of cheerful animators entertains the kids with fun contests, scenes and competitions. And the evening ends with a mini-disco.

Entertainment

Reviews about Sun City Apartments & Hotel 4 * are extremely rarely negative. The hotel received a huge part of the positive feedback for the variety of entertainment. Among the widespread facilities of the complex, the SPA salon is of particular importance. It allows not only to relax and experience frantic pleasure, but also to rejuvenate the skin, restore the functioning of internal systems. Wellness procedures using therapeutic mud and seaweed will saturate every cell of the body with oxygen, restoring its structure.

For those who care about their appearance, a gym is open to visitors. There are fitness classes, there are many different simulators.

Guests are also invited to visit the hammam, bathhouse and sauna. Games such as billiards, darts, table tennis and football are available. In the evening, an exciting show program will give a lot of joy and smiles to each viewer.





Additional services

One of the important points that is included in the positive reviews about Sun City Apartments & Hotel 4 * is the desire to satisfy the needs of each individual guest. So, for an instant response to questions, the reception operates around the clock. Shuttle service is available to everyone at an additional cost. And in order to exchange money, it is not at all necessary to leave the walls of the hotel. Luggage storage is also available.

On-site leisure activities include exploring local attractions and admiring the natural environment. To implement this, you can use the car or bicycle rental service. Parking spaces are provided free of charge.

Commemorative souvenirs and products are available at stores located on site. A local beauty salon will also welcome every visitor. And professional photographers offer to conduct a photo shoot and capture unique shots and enthusiastic emotions for many years.
