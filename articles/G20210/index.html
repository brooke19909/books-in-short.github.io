Kaspiysk hotels: description of rooms, addresses, infrastructure and reviews of tourists

The small satellite town of Makhachkala - Kaspiysk - is located on the shores of the Caspian Sea. It can not be called a tourist mecca, however, travelers come here to bask in the sun and relax on the seashore. For the convenience of holidaymakers, there are several hotels in this village that are equipped in a European style and provide travelers with quality service and comfortable rooms. Among the hotels of Kaspiysk, every tourist will be able to choose an acceptable option and have fun in this city.

Hotel Arena

This hotel complex can be found on the street. Akulinicheva, 21, which is 2.5 km from the central part of the city. To accommodate guests there are 38 rooms designed for different numbers of residents.

In the Arena Hotel you can choose a single or double room, as well as relax in a suite with increased comfort. The cost of living ranges from 3000 to 4000 rubles per person. And this is quite a reasonable price, because this hotel of Kaspiysk has 4 stars.

Each room is equipped with comfortable and modern furniture, a minibar, a TV and a fridge. The room has a private bathroom, hygiene products are free of charge.

The only thing that displeases the guests is the impossibility of living with pets, even for a fee.

Thanks to the developed infrastructure of the hotel, every tourist feels comfortable and convenient. The hotel has:

  • Free parking.
  • Sauna and hammam.
  • Aquapark.
  • Shops and hairdressers.
  • Free internet access.
  • ATMs and a 24-hour front desk.
  • Laundry and bike rental.

Opinion of tourists

In the city of Kaspiysk this accommodation option is considered at the same time one of the most expensive, but also the most convenient. Some tourists complained in their reviews of the slow check-in and lack of daily housekeeping. But in most cases, travelers were satisfied with their choice and advised this hotel to their friends and acquaintances.

Grand Hotel Caspian

Another comfortable place tourists unanimously call the "Caspian Sea", which is located almost in the city center and at the same time 100 from the sea coast. You can find this hotel in Kaspiysk at: st. Halilova, 1.

The status of a 4-star hotel here is maintained by qualified staff and administrators. The hotel has a 24-hour front desk, where the tourist can contact with any matter he is interested in.

The traveler can stay in a single, double room or relax in a junior suite and suite. The cost of living can not be attributed to the budget category. For a day you will have to pay a tidy sum - from 2000 to 5000 rubles.

However, all rooms for residents fully correspond to the status of this hotel in Kaspiysk. They are equipped with huge and soft beds, practical cabinet furniture and a TV. The room has a private bathroom with a shower and bidet. A hairdryer and toiletries are provided for free.

The hotel territory itself has a fun holiday in a hospitable city. Unique dishes from the local chef will delight connoisseurs of Caucasian cuisine, and lovers of the Russian bath will be able to spend time with health benefits.

Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the complex. Daily cleaning and towel change are provided in the guest rooms.

If desired, for a fee, you can order airport transfers and food delivery to your room.

What visitors said

Despite the rather high cost of living, it was not possible to find negative feedback from guests. They claim that the service of the hotel is quite consistent with the price, and plan to visit this pleasant place in the city of Kaspiysk more than once in the future.

Daghos Hostel

For those who are looking for more affordable housing, experienced tourists are advised to pay attention to the hostel hotel in Kaspiysk at the address: st. Stalin, 1A. Dangkhos is located half a kilometer from the Anji Arena Stadium, a 3-minute walk from the sea coast.

It offers dormitory rooms with 2 bunk beds. Judging by the reviews of tourists, all the furniture in the room is comfortable and not old. In addition, each room has a microwave and an electric kettle, so that with breakfast the vacationers will not have any problems.

Travelers called the free parking and Wi-Fi, as well as the friendliness and quickness of the staff, a pleasant bonus. They advise this hostel to those who do not want to spend a lot of money and at the same time want to relax on the sea coast.

Guest house "Fatima"

For a cozy family vacation, tourists are advised to choose the guest house "Fatima", located on the first coastline. Travelers recommend booking a room in advance in this place, as it is practically impossible to do this in season.

On the territory of the mini-hotel guests are provided with free parking for cars, Internet access, as well as a huge terrace, where you can relax in the evening silence with a cup of fragrant tea.

The room has a comfortable 2 double bed, a table and chairs, as well as an individual bathroom. There is also a dining area, where you will find all the necessary utensils and a microwave.

Positive feedback from vacationers at the Fatima Guest House is not in vain. Every client is valued here, they can find a common language with him, and even the most prestigious hotels can envy the courtesy of the staff. Tourists recommend this place for a pleasant family vacation.

There are no huge number of hotels in Kaspiysk, but this does not prevent the city from accepting tourists from different parts of the country and sharing its hospitality and good mood with them.
