Nour Palace Thalasso Aqua 5 * (Tunisia, Mahdia): hotel infrastructure, room description, entertainment, reviews

In Mahdia, which is one of the most visited cities in Tunisia, there are countless hotels. And all of them are rarely empty, because people from different parts of our planet come here who want to enjoy their holidays on the famous local beaches with white sand. But some hotels are a little more popular than others. These include the hotel Nour Palace Thalasso Aqua. And about it should be told in more detail.





general information

Nour Palace Thalasso Aqua is located at arm's length from the white sandy beach, which is one of the best in Tunisia. The hotel, which underwent a major restoration in 2012, cannot be overlooked - a 5-story long building, the architecture of which clearly shows the local flavor, is located on a well-groomed green area with palm trees.

It takes about five minutes to get to the center of Mahdia from the hotel. Nearby, by the way, is a bus stop. The airport closest to the hotel is Habib Bourguiba Airport in Monastir, and it takes about 30-40 minutes to get there.

Service

Service at Nour Palace Thalasso Aqua 5 * is exactly what it should be at a hotel of this level. It has everything that guests may need - free Wi-Fi and private parking, a tour desk and ticket office, currency exchange, luggage storage, laundry, several shops (including a souvenir), a beauty salon, and a car rental service. The front desk is open 24 hours a day, and concierge and babysitting services are available.

By the way, the hotel staff speaks several languages. Among them are Arabic, German, English and French.
