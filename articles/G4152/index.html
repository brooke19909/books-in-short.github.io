Tax refund when buying an apartment: application, documents, terms of return

Today we will be interested in tax refunds when buying an apartment. What kind of operation is this? Who is she put to? And how can you bring it to life? To understand all this and not only for us to come. Only with proper preparation will a citizen be able to easily request a deduction. Description

What is a tax refund when buying an apartment? It is from this issue that we begin to consider the topic.

The tax refund is called a tax deduction. It is a reimbursement of part of the costs for certain operations against listed taxes (PIT). In fact, the FTS pays a certain share of the funds to the taxpayer. In our case, for the purchase of real estate.

Conditions

It is worth paying attention to the fact that not every modern person can take advantage of the studied state bonus. You will have to familiarize yourself with the conditions for receiving a refund.

At the moment, it is necessary that:

  1. The applicant was a citizen of the Russian Federation. Foreigners do not provide deductions.
  2. The person submitting the application in the established form must have official employment. Registration of IP or LLC will also be taken into account.
  3. A citizen paid income tax of 13%. Other tax rates are not taken into account.
  4. At the time of the appeal, the applicant has not yet exhausted the limit on deductions of one type or another. After that, the right to reimbursement of specific expenses is abolished.
  5. The purchase of goods and services for which you want to return the money is carried out at the expense of personal funds and in the name of the applicant.

Perhaps this is all. In fact, the income tax refund for the apartment does not provide any special conditions. In fact, if a person decides on a mortgage, he will also have to comply with such requests.

Important: when buying an apartment with the participation of capital, it is also possible to qualify for reimbursement of expenses. But with some features. We will talk about them later.

Return Types

Strange as it may seem, the income tax refund for buying an apartment may vary. After all, real estate transactions also differ from each other.

The thing is that the population can demand money from the Federal Tax Service when buying:

  • apartments on the secondary market (covering the entire amount under the contract immediately);
  • in a mortgage;
  • by installments.

In addition, if a loan is taken to purchase a home, it becomes possible to apply for a return on interest on a mortgage. The main thing is to properly prepare for the operation.

How much to return

Want to request a tax refund when buying an apartment? Return dates are quite flexible. But the citizen has the right to implement the task only for a certain period of time. After all, the operation has a limitation of calls.

According to the current rules, personal income tax can be returned no later than 3 years after the conclusion of the transaction. And demanding money is allowed immediately for 36 months. This is very convenient, especially if initially the applicant transfers not so many taxes to the state treasury.

After 3 years, if the return limit is not exhausted, a person will be able to apply to the Federal Tax Service minus, but for other transactions. There is nothing incomprehensible or difficult in this.

Senior Citizens and Deductions

Special attention is paid to the tax refund for pensioners when buying an apartment. Elderly people are divided into 2 categories:

  • unemployed;
  • working.

Accordingly, depending on this moment, further actions will take place. Unemployed pensioners can apply for a deduction within 4 years from the date of dismissal. This is a kind of tax transfer. It is available only to older people.

Working pensioners receive a deduction on the same basis as ordinary employed citizens. As a rule, the application procedure does not cause any problems.

Important: if there is any doubt about the right to transfer personal income tax upon tax refund, you can contact the Federal Tax Service to clarify the situation. Workers will quickly figure out what years a person will be able to demand money.

Amount Limitations

Almost every potential applicant is interested in the amount of tax refund when buying an apartment. How much money is allowed to refund?

There are many nuances in this matter. The thing is that the tax deduction allows you to reimburse 13% of the amount under the contract. But with certain conditions and limitations. Namely:

  • more money cannot be returned in a year than a person transferred taxes to the state treasury;
  • property return allows you to return a total of not more than 260,000 rubles;
  • the mortgage deduction reimburses a total of 390,000 rubles.

That's all. Accordingly, it is far from always that citizens will be able to return money for certain transactions. All potential recipients of funds will have to remember these restrictions.

Step-by-step instruction

Now consider the whole operation step by step. How to proceed to receive income tax refund?

We turn to the method of filing an application through the MFC or the Federal Tax Service. In this case, the citizen will need:

  1. Conclude a contract of sale and wait for the new calendar year. The right to deduction does not appear immediately.
  2. Prepare a specific set of documents. Their list depends on the situation. Below are various packages of papers.
  3. Contact the registration authority with the appropriate application.
  4. Wait for an answer from the Federal Tax Service. It comes in about 1-2 months.
  5. Get money to the account indicated in the application. This operation will take another two months.

It is done. Refund of income tax when buying an apartment does not cause any special trouble.

Response Timeout

How long do you have to wait for an answer from the Federal Tax Service for the implementation of the task? In general, tax refund when buying an apartment in one way or another is far from the fastest operation. It takes a lot of time.

How much exactly? There is no definite answer to this question. It all depends on the load on the local tax authority and on where exactly the application of the established form is submitted.
