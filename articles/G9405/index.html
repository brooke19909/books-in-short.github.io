How to get from Yekaterinburg to Simferopol?

The route "Ekaterinburg - Simferopol" is rather big. Nevertheless, every year many people have to overcome it. Before you leave, you should consider all the possible options for a vehicle moving in that direction, and choose the most suitable option for yourself.





Car

What is a personal vehicle? For each driver, it is the second house. In it, he rests, spends time, communicates with people and does many other things familiar to him. The distance from Yekaterinburg to Simferopol is 2865 kilometers. If you wish, you can try to overcome it by car.

There is only one option for moving in this direction. To do this, you need to drive along two long-distance routes: E30 and E105.





It is impossible for one driver to overcome such a distance on his own. Therefore, it is recommended to make a stop for rest and overnight in large cities. The following settlements will meet along the way: Yekaterinburg, Voronezh, Samara and Tolyatti. In them you can find several options for hotels, gas stations from several suppliers and many entertainment complexes. The total road time without stops will be 1 day and 12 hours.

Advantages and disadvantages of a personal vehicle





There are several advantages of traveling in a personal vehicle:

  • no need to adapt to the tight schedule of the railway station, departure can occur at any time;
  • in another city there will be a car, which will make the trip very exciting;
  • You can create a route yourself, having previously planned all the stops.

The main disadvantage is the length of the journey, it will be very difficult for the driver and passenger to overcome it.

A train

The most convenient way to get from Simferopol to Yekaterinburg is by train. But once, due to disagreements with the Ukrainian government, this flight was canceled. After Crimea became part of the Russian Federation, it was never restored. Unfortunately, connecting with the transplant will also fail. Perhaps alternatives will be offered soon.

By bus





The road from Yekaterinburg to Simferopol is quite long. It is easy to guess that there is no direct bus service between these two points. You can use this vehicle, provided that in order to reach your destination you need to make at least five transfers. The total travel time will be more than three days. It is unlikely that anyone would want to make such a tiring journey.

Airplane

The fastest, most convenient and comfortable way to get from Yekaterinburg to Simferopol is by plane. In these two large cities, airports are located, and at a short distance from their centers. Nearby there are several stops for public transport, taxi drivers parking and a metro station. Consequently, you can get to the desired place without any problems. There are three possible options for moving this way:

  • the first flight leaves Koltsovo at 6 a.m., after 1 hour 49 minutes the passenger will be in the very center of the sunny city.
  • several times a week, namely on Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, Friday and Saturday, an additional flight departs at 15:05;
  • The last departure of the aircraft occurs at 17:00.

The schedule is not always this way, there may be some changes or cancellation of departure due to weather conditions. It is recommended that you first call the airport and clarify all the information of interest. It should be borne in mind that a huge number of tourists want to visit this place in the summer. It is recommended that you book your tickets at least one month in advance of your expected departure date. You can buy a ticket at the box office or on the official website through the electronic form. Its cost will be approximately from 5800 to 7000 rubles.

The most ridiculous ways

To date, there are no known cases that someone decided to get from Yekaterinburg to Sevastopol on foot. However, there is a sidewalk on almost the entire route from one point to another. Theoretically, a person can get to the sunny city in this way, but he will have to spend more than 22 days on this. Exactly a day earlier, a brave traveler arrives who decided to use a bicycle. A motorcycle, moped, ATV and other similar personal vehicles are able to cover this distance in the same amount of time as a car.

The central part of Crimea is an incredibly beautiful sunny place. Unfortunately, there are not many ways to get from Yekaterinburg to Simferopol. You can do this only on your own car or on an airplane; there are no other options. Also in the summer seasons you can find special offers from travel agencies. This makes it possible to purchase a vacation ticket at the best price.
