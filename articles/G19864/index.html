Auto loan partner organizations

By issuing such a service at a relatively small percentage, Sberbank seeks to minimize the risk of loan defaults. In this regard, one of the basic conditions for car loans is an additional mandatory insurance of secured vehicles. In other words, CASCO insurance policies.

Each bank (and Sberbank is no exception) interacts with a specific list of insurance companies whose activities are verified and satisfy all the requirements of the financial structure. Among these, it is worth noting: VTB Insurance, Ingosstrakh, VSK, Alfa Insurance, Regiongarant, Zhaso, Sberbank Insurance, Helios, Adonis, Sputnik and others.

Conditions to be met by the organization

A credited citizen has the right, at his discretion, to choose the most suitable company for himself. The main thing is that she satisfy the following conditions:

  • Has been active in the market for more than three years.
  • Be sure to comply with all necessary norms and rules imposed by Russian law.
  • Comply with banking requirements in terms of solvency and financial stability.

As part of the confirmation of the above factors, the borrower agrees to produce the relevant documents.

Features of car loans in Sberbank

Most financial institutions, including this one, prefer to issue a loan only subject to a CASCO policy. What is it and what advantages does it give to the client? The fact is that this service compensates for the risk associated with the theft of a car or an accident, causing damage to a vehicle, the occurrence of various accidents, such as, for example, a tree falling, a collision with a wild animal, and the like. Compulsory motor third party liability insurance covers only the risks associated with road traffic accidents.
