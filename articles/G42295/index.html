Mezmay: high quality hotels and resorts

Mezmay village is located in the Krasnodar Territory, in the Absheron region. The population does not exceed even a thousand people, but it is popular among tourists, hotels and recreation centers in Mezmay are always full of vacationers. What attracts tourists to the village?

Mount Lenin or, as the locals call it, Mount Ilyich. An unusually interesting natural object, attracts, first of all, with its shape. It has two pointed peaks, and between them is a dense forest. In the morning, a picturesque view opens up at Lenin Mountain.

The most famous attraction of Mezmay is the Guam Gorge. This is one of the most beautiful places in the Caucasus mountains. It stretches for three kilometers, the Kurdzhips River flows downstairs. Many artists, writers and musicians come here to seek inspiration for their work and stay in Mezmay hotels, in which they take care of a comfortable stay.

The parking lot of an ancient man is especially popular with archaeologists, geologists and tourists who are interested in history. It is noteworthy that during the stay of an ancient man in the parking lot, instead of the dense forests that the village is famous for, there were meadows.

You can get to the village only by bus or by personal transport, a place to stay can be found in hotels and recreation centers in Mezmay.

Guest House "Forest Glade"

The house is located in the village, so getting to it is not difficult. The guest house has 6 double and 2 triple rooms. In general, the "Forest Glade" can simultaneously accommodate 18 people. The house has a kitchen, a hall and a meadow in the yard. You can cook it yourself, but staff can take care of meals for a fee: three meals a day and even a vegetarian menu are possible, so mostly guests choose masterpieces from local chefs. A room costs from 1200 to 2000 rubles, it all depends on the number of people.

Guest House "Solar Wind"





There are 4 houses on the territory of the base, in which there are 4 beds in each, there are also 3 four-bed hotel rooms. In the "Solar Wind" created the conditions for a comfortable stay. Sauna, barbecue, musical equipment and a common room where visitors can relax and socialize in a warm circle. The cost of living per day is 500 rubles. A bath costs 800 rubles per hour, regardless of the number of people.

Complex "Sphere Mezmay"

Complex "Sphere" is located in a beautiful and picturesque place. It consists of two round cottages. One large house is designed for four people, but you can order two more extra beds. The price per day is 3000 rubles, and for an extra bed 500 rubles.

Manor "Colchis Gate"





The estate is located three kilometers from the village on the outskirts of the Lagonaki Highlands. In the estate "Colchis Gate" built several houses, comfortable for tourists. On the estate there is a bathhouse, a recreation area, as well as a farm, which attracts a large number of tourists from other recreation centers and Mezmay hotels. Room rates range from 3000 to 4000 rubles per day.

Hotel Mezmay

The most famous hotel of the village is located at the very source of the Kurdzhips river, between two ridges. Two-story hotel "Mezmay" fully complies with all international standards. At the same time, it can accommodate almost 50 people in standard rooms, as well as deluxe and junior suites. All rooms overlook the majestic mountains of the Caucasus. Each room has everything you need, bath, refrigerator, TV, shower, etc. The hotel has parking, barbecue facilities, various gyms, a pool table and much more. It is noteworthy that the Mezmay Hotel with a swimming pool, recreation centers and other hotels does not.
