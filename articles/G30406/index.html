Hotel Erkal Resort Hotel 4 * (Kemer, Turkey): photos and reviews of tourists

If you want to get to know Turkey better and not spend too much time on vacation, pay attention to such an accommodation option as Erkal Resort Hotel 4 *. Here you will find everything you need for a comfortable stay.

Location

600 meters from the well-kept municipal beach is a small resort hotel Erkal Resort Hotel 4 *. Kemer, Turkey, Ataturk street, 7 - the address where you can find this resort. It is just 2 km from the center of Kemer. Antalya International Airport is about 60 km away.

Where to go?

Kemer is rich in natural and cultural attractions. If you decide to stay at Erkal Resort Hotel 4 *, be sure to visit the following objects:

  • Kemer Seaport (3 km);
  • theme park (3 km);
  • Moonlight Beach (3 km);
  • the ruins of the ancient city of Phaselis (16 km);
  • Cableway (17 km);
  • Tahtali funicular (18 km).

Standard rooms

The greatest demand in Erkal Resort Hotel 4 * are standard rooms. This category is a small bedroom with one large or a pair of twin beds. There is also a sitting corner with a coffee table and a comfortable soft armchair. There is a furnished balcony from which you can clearly see the garden, pool, and picturesque surroundings.





Family rooms

If you prefer to relax in the Erkal Resort Hotel 4 * (Kemer) with your family or friends, then pay attention to family rooms. They consist of two separate bedrooms. The rooms can be equipped with large and one and a half beds. Bunk beds can be provided for children. There are two bathrooms. One of the bedrooms has a balcony.





Room Amenities

The hospitable Kemer resort is very popular with travelers. Erkal Resort Hotel 4 * provides you the opportunity to use the following amenities in the rooms:

  • a bathroom equipped with the necessary fixtures, as well as a hairdryer and bath accessories;
  • a minibar, which can be used as a refrigerator (drinks are available at an additional cost);

  • individually controlled air conditioning system;
  • TV connected to satellite or cable antenna;
  • landline phone, which you can use to communicate with the reception, as well as international calls (this service is paid);

Additional Information

Before traveling, you should get some additional information about the hotel. So, you should pay attention to the following information:

  • check-in is after 14:00, and check-out should be done before noon on the day of departure;
  • in case the guest incurs material damage during registration, a deposit in cash or non-cash form will be charged (upon eviction, he will be returned in full or the amount of loss will be withheld)
  • a prerequisite for checking into the hotel is the presentation of an identity card with a photograph;
  • self-registration is possible starting from 15 years old;
  • one child under 6 years old can stay in the room free of charge without extra bed;
  • pets are not allowed in the hotel;
  • only registered guests can stay in guest rooms;
  • children under 6 years old are not allowed in the spa area;
  • transfer order must be made no later than one day before the expected date;
  • a visit to the massage parlor requires prior reservation;
  • during registration, you must present a personalized credit card, the data of which coincide with that indicated when booking;
  • The cost of transfer to the airport and back is 100 euros.

Negative reviews

Budget holidays are not without drawbacks, as evidenced by the resort hotel Erkal Resort Hotel 4 * (Kemer, Turkey). Reviews about this institution contain such negative comments:

  • if on the day of check-in you did not manage to come to breakfast, lunch or dinner, you will not be fed (you will have to wait for the next meal to be hungry);
  • maids do not put hygienic cosmetics in the bathroom (you need to take them yourself at the reception);
  • in a nearby hotel until late at night music plays loudly that keeps you awake;
  • there is very little vegetation on the territory, and nobody cares for what is available;
  • Wireless Internet is very slow, and its signal is only in the lobby area;
  • plastic glasses in which drinks are served are used repeatedly;
  • on the day of departure, immediately after noon, guests are denied all services, even if you intend to leave in a few hours;
  • family rooms, where they usually sit with young children, open onto a disco;
  • maids do not wash the floors in the room (they only cover the beds and wipe the dust);
  • the pool is small enough, and therefore if all guests want to swim, they are physically not enough space;
  • to get to the beach, you need to overcome the busy roadway;
  • the owner is constantly on site and closely monitors guests, which is very annoying;
  • in the corridors they rub the floors with mastic, which gives off a terrible smell;
  • the rooms have dirty curtains with greasy spots;
  • a small assortment of sweets (there are no traditional Turkish desserts on the menu at all);
  • the appearance of the hotel and the list of services provided do not correspond to the information presented on the official website;
  • there is no drinking water in the rooms (you need to independently buy or type from a cooler in the corridor);
  • rarely change bed linen and towels;
  • it is forbidden to bring food and drinks to the beach, but there is no bar where you could buy this;
  • there are no fermented milk products in the restaurant menu;
  • reception staff are not very welcoming guests, which can significantly spoil the first impression of the institution;
  • there is not enough variety in food (the restaurant menu is repeated day by day);
  • the hotel lacks animation (which is why there are most pensioners and vacationers with children);
  • when the guest leaves the room and picks up the key card, all electrical appliances (including the refrigerator) are turned off.

This hotel evokes the most diverse experience among travelers. It is worth noting that the Erkal Resort Hotel 4 * hotel does not fully correspond to its "stardom".
