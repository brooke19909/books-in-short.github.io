A cozy hotel-club, shining with cleanliness, is good for family tourists and ideal for a cloudless holiday.

The hotel complex is equally popular among young people, families and reputable tourists who value peace above all. The gentle rays of the sun, the sea and the air, filled with the aromas of pine trees, the cozy atmosphere, the minimum amount of household chores fill the souls of guests of all ages, nationalities and classes with joy.





Club Hotel Location

The hotel club lurks in the pine forest of the village of Titreengel, from which it is six to the town of Manavgat and seven kilometers to Side. It is 60 kilometers from the international airport in Antalya.

Side is a resort town, chosen by travelers from all over the world. It is recognized as a major shopping center and open-air museum at the same time. Tourists come into contact with ancient history, visiting ancient theaters and temples, and purchase worthwhile goods at a bargain price, looking at modern shopping complexes and at the ancient markets of the resort town of Side. Maya Golf Hotel is 400 meters from the Mediterranean coast. Own spotlessly clean beach, framing the sea with azure waters, is located across the street from the hotel.

Hotel infrastructure

The snow-white buildings of the club hotel were erected in 1985. The last renovation in it was made in 2012. Guests are accommodated in two four-story buildings and several bungalows surrounded by a beautiful park.

The Maya Golf Hotel's garden is carefully monitored: lawn and shrubs are regularly mowed, they look after flower beds and constantly clean. In the evening twilight, when the lights next to the pools light up, and lanterns flash along the paths and next to the trees, the park turns into a fairy garden.

Rare plants and typical palm trees, pomegranate and orange trees are used in the arrangements. A freshwater pond inhabited by fish and turtles organically blended into the landscape of the park. Nimble lizards hastily scurry along neat alleys and land turtles slowly move. In the crowns of the trees , bell-voiced birds found their abode, their restless trills of the day-day are heard in the vicinity of the park.





Guests are offered to use the services provided in dry cleaning, laundry, hairdresser, currency exchange and car rental, in the parking lot. Guests in need of medical services are received at the doctor‚Äôs office. In the shopping center and souvenir shops of the Maya Club Hotel Golf 4 club, tourists purchase the necessary goods, gifts and products in memory of a wonderful vacation.

For business meetings, tourists are provided with a conference room for one hundred people. The room is equipped with the necessary technical equipment, contributing to the success of the planned event. Presentation and business meetings are organized by the hotel staff.

In the hotel complex there is a studio in which professional photographers work. Tourists are happy to order them photo sessions. After filming, studio staff offer to see the frames made to clients on a computer monitor and choose the most successful photographs for printing.

On the second day after being accommodated in the club-hotel, guests are invited to visit the tour desk. Experts talk in detail about the available tours and excursions around the country, saturated with oriental flavor. Free Wi-Fi is available in the lobby.

Pools

The Maya Club Hotel Golf 4 is equipped with two outdoor pools with water slides of different heights . Part of one water complex is equipped with a section designed for children's bathing. Umbrellas, sun loungers, mattresses and towels by the pool are used by vacationers for free.





A pool with slides is the center of animation during the day. The game of water polo attracts the attention of not only tourists relaxing in sun loungers, but also those who enjoy the taste of refreshing cocktails from a misted glass, hiding under the shadow of a bar.

restaurants and bars

There are 5 restaurants and 4 bars. Most of them are covered by the All Inclusive power system. This scheme offers dishes in the main restaurant, in A'la carte establishments and several bars.

At the Maya Golf Hotel HV's main restaurant, 2 guests are invited to the generous buffet for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. The table is always full of colorfully decorated delicious dishes.





A La Carte restaurants specialize in Italian and Turkish cuisine, fish menus and barbecue, they serve tourists who have pre-booked a table. The barbecue restaurant accepts guests by appointment, and meals are only paid (it is not included in the all-inclusive system).

The food in the restaurants at Maya Golf Hotel HV is varied, of excellent quality. Vacationers always find for themselves several dishes that meet their taste preferences. The buffet is abundant in snacks, ice cream, pastries, alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. The choice of meat dishes is huge - dishes from turkey, beef, chicken and lamb.

Apartments

The number of rooms here is formed by 180 apartments, which created excellent conditions for a comfortable stay. By type, they are divided into apartments of the category ROH, Standard, Eco Room. Guests are offered accommodation in standard and family rooms, in deluxe rooms and for the disabled. Housekeepers daily housekeeping with clean towels. Lingerie changes three times a week.

The air-conditioned apartments with elegant interiors and laminate floors feature LCD TVs that broadcast Russian channels. They are furnished with new furniture. In the room, in addition to beds, there is a table with a mirror and an ottoman, a small sofa or chair. On the French balcony there is a table with two chairs. The terrace offers a panoramic view of the park and the sea.





Guest rooms at Maya Club Hotel Golf have a wardrobe. The apartments are equipped with a safe (extra charge) and a telephone. They have a place for a refrigerator and a daily replenished minibar. The bathroom comes with a shower and toilet, hygiene facilities and a hairdryer.

Beach vacation

A well-groomed sand spit that borders the warm sea leads to a park path paved with wooden flooring. The length of the path, winding through the park, four hundred meters. The beach, which has a gentle slope to the sea with clear, clear blue water, is located next to the hotel.

At the entrance to the water here, the sandy bottom suddenly gives way to large pebbles, which causes inconvenience to swimmers (especially children). Travelers who have visited the Maya Golf Hotel more than once recommend swimming where green beach umbrellas are located. Pebbles are noticeably smaller in that area, and in places it completely disappears, giving way to sand.

On the beach, covered with clean velvety sand, vacationers indulge in bliss in sun loungers or play beach volleyball. There are plenty of coastal resorts belonging to the Maya Golf Hotel 4 HV club hotel. Free beach accessories - umbrellas, deck chairs, mattresses and towels are enough for all tourists who are in love with beach vacations, sea bathing and entertainment.

The staff helpfully offers a ride on a water bike, a banana, a cheesecake, or take an exciting parachute flight. In the beach bar, which is part of the all-inclusive food system, tourists pamper themselves with soft drinks, beer, juices, ice cream and light snacks.

Seductive Turkish tortillas are baked on the coast under the open sky. Having a rest, having watched the process of preparing national pastries, they do not deny themselves the pleasure of buying a delicious hot cake. They say it is especially tasty if you eat it with a cool ayran.

In the evenings, incendiary discos with bold exclusive special effects thunder on a sand spit. During them, a foamy waterfall is launched, causing a storm of positive emotions, giving new joyful sensations of unrestrained fun. Fluffy foam clouds, combined with rhythmic music and flashes of light and laser beams, immerse guests in the merry atmosphere of a colorful show.

The Maya Club Hotel Golf 5 is impeccably clean throughout. The staff is engaged in continuous cleaning in the park, buildings and on the beach. In the evening, sand is sieved and leveled on the coast. In the morning, the beach sparkles with cleanliness, as if yesterday there was not a single person on it.

Entertainment and sports

Free animation for vacationers is carried out by boys and girls with a cheerful disposition. All day they kindly engage guests in lively entertainment, offering to engage in water aerobics, participate in sports, merging into football or volleyball teams. They organize darts and table tennis competitions. For evening shows, artists at the Maya Golf Hotel come in for a variety of performances.

Since the hotel specializes in the All system, almost all leisure activities are provided to vacationers for free. Additional payments require water sports, a game of billiards and massages. The services of a beauty salon and spa center are not included in the All package. At the disco, only admission is free. Tennis rackets and balls are hired for a moderate fee.

For all other entertainment you do not need to pay. Vacationers enjoy basking in the sauna or Turkish hammam. Go to water aerobics classes or to the gym. They have free access to the tennis court, which has a hard surface, and to the mini-golf course.

For evening walks, tourists are selected on the avenue of fountains, along which there are cafes, pharmacies, a hairdresser, an excursion bureau, shops with souvenirs and various trifles, a shopping center.

Children's holiday

In Turkey, children are treated with special trepidation, so they are trying to create excellent conditions for family vacations at the resorts. Maya Golf Hotel 4 has formed a number of children's zones that contribute to calm and outdoor games. Children's animators work great here. Mini disco impresses both children and adults. Children like to visit the games room, have fun on a special platform with sand and a sports and game complex in the form of a ship, swim in the pool with water slides.





Tour Office

Those who want to see the sights of the country buy guided tours at the Maya Golf Hotel or travel independently to the surrounding cities. From the hotel to the city of Side with its antique ruins, antique architecture, excursion minibuses regularly go. Comfortable buses go to Antalya from the fleet.

They go to the town of Manavgat for the purpose of shopping. There, on Mondays and Thursdays, a market opens where traditional oriental ceremonies reign. Sellers entice buyers, praise products, bargain desperately and give tourists who made a deal memorable souvenirs.

There are wonderful shopping centers in it. The city is replete with decent cafes, excellent restaurants and bars. In institutions passable in Russian mixed up with English words. You can always understand each other, agree and get what you want. It's nice to walk along its streets. Also, tourists like to climb the mountain, from where a stunning waterfall falls, and enjoy a bird's eye view of the panorama of the magnificent resort town.

Tourists reviews

Although tourists on the snow-white facade of the hotel with a simple architecture at first glance do not make the proper impression, they leave reviews full of praise about the Maya Golf Hotel. In the hotel club, in their opinion, there are three valuable factors: a delightful park, high-class service and great animation.

A magnificent garden with organically mixed conifers and tropical trees, a beautiful pond and the harmless living creatures that populate it gives guests minutes of joy. Walking among smooth lawns, bright flower beds, pines and palm trees along neat avenues with orange and pomegranate trees, tourists often meet with turtles and squirrels.

In apartments without any frills, the decor is always clean. The smiling and friendly staff is well-trained, clearly does their job, cleaning every corner of the room to shine. Moreover, employees fulfill their duties of conscience, even without getting a tip.





The generous buffet has cold and hot dishes, sweets and pastries, fruits and vegetables. Trying everything for the first three days, for the fourth guests already know exactly what dishes they liked and what food should no longer be put on their plate. And the point here is not the poor quality of the dishes, but a specific combination of tastes, enhanced by oriental spices. What Eastern people eat with enthusiasm is not at all accepted by Europeans.

The sand and pebble beach with a moderately steep entrance to the water is simply amazing. Waves running along the coast, rolling pebbles and blowing grains of sand into the deeps of the sea, and staff diligently sifting sand and leveling it, constantly change the outlines. In the morning, washed sun loungers stand on a flat and clean sand cover. The beach bar offers satisfying unpretentious dishes and quench your thirst with delicious drinks.
