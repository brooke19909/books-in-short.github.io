Bulgaria, Plamena Palace 4 *: photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Bulgaria is a wonderful country in which hospitable and sympathetic people live. It is extremely diverse: the Black Sea coast with beautiful beaches, majestic mountains, ancient monasteries and cities, life-giving mineral springs. This is a mysterious country of roses, vines, amber, numerous gardens. Landscaping hotels with beautiful romantic names have incredible charm: Plamena Palace 4 *, Hotel Penelope, Magnolite and others. Bulgaria is a country very generously and generously gifted by God.

City Primorsko

A significant part of the coast of Bulgaria, located south of the city of Burgas, is distinguished by excellent ecology, due to the remoteness from large cities. It is such a clean place that Primorsko is.





A small town located on a relatively small territory is known for the most beautiful hotel Plamena Palace 4 *. Primorsko is removed from the airport and is located in the marvelous zone of nature reserves. On the one hand it is Arkutino, and on the other - Ropotamo National Park.

In Primorsko, one of the newest water parks in Bulgaria, Aqua Planet, has been built. The amazing natural reality of the city is endless beaches with natural dunes. There are two beaches in the city: the northern one with developed infrastructure and the southern one, which attracts lovers of water sports.

Climate

The large territory of Bulgaria is mountainous, and the warm climate is due to access to the Black Sea. On the coast of the country there is no excessive heat (maximum temperature reaches 30 degrees C) due to the refreshing breeze coming from the sea. The best months for rest are July, August. This is a wonderful time for sunny warm weather. The wettest month is often June, and the driest time is the beginning of autumn.

It is believed that in August, September you can get a real pleasure in a vacation in Bulgaria. You can swim safely throughout September. And even in early October, the sea still pampers guests with its warmth.

Plamena palace

In a paradise called Primorsko, the beautiful Hotel Plamena Palace 4 * is located. This is a comfortable hotel with a stylish look and a cozy atmosphere. The hotel was built in 2006. Just 200 meters from it is the north beach. Such an arrangement guarantees the guests a wonderful vacation among truly beautiful nature.





Cape Maslen is located to the north of the hotel, the cape of Kyupriya borders on the south side. And on the sides of the hotel are the most beautiful bays of Stomoplo and Devil's.

It is located near the main street with many retail outlets. The main attractions of the city are also close to the hotel. It is simply buried in a variety of institutions, cafes, discos, bistros and fish restaurants.

Rooms

The hotel is a modern building of 5 floors. Guests are offered 134 comfortable rooms, four of which are suites. The building has three buildings and for convenience - two elevators. The hotel mainly offers single and double rooms. However, Plamena Palace 4 * provided for more spacious rooms, which can comfortably accommodate 4 people. The hotel also took care of the disabled, offering them specially equipped rooms.





The guests are simply enchanted by the well-equipped Plamena Palace 4 * double hotel. Primorsko guarantees comfortable accommodation not only in deluxe rooms, but also in double standards. All apartments have modern amenities, including a bathroom with a hairdryer, telephone, central air conditioning, satellite TV, free Wi-Fi. The rooms have an extra bed and a minibar. Each room has a balcony. Ceramic tiles on the floor emphasize the beautiful, stylish design of the room.

The hotel guarantees superior service including daily cleaning and towel change. Linen is changed three times a week.

Beaches

In Primorsko, there are two free beaches, stretching for about 2.5 km along the Black Sea coast. Therefore, there is no problem with a free place for swimming and sunbathing. The beach is sandy and very gentle. A calm and serene sea distinguishes the southern beach from the windy north. However, near the latter you can admire the majestic sand dunes protected by the state. Their height reaches 19 meters.





An excellent public and completely free beach was offered to guests of the Plamena Palace 4 * hotel. Bulgaria is widely known for its fine sand, perfect for families with kids. Umbrellas and deck chairs on the beach are paid. Adherents of active pastime are offered a lot of entertainment.

Attractions Primorsko

Bulgaria makes it possible to make unique excursions not only in space, but also in time. Inveterate historians will be able to enjoy the traces of disappeared civilizations.
