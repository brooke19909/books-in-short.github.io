How to get from Kostroma to St. Petersburg?

Many residents of Kostroma often travel to the cultural capital of Russia. What are they doing this for? Of course, with one goal - to enjoy the beauties of this beautiful city. Fortunately, there are several types of vehicles that allow you to get from Kostroma to St. Petersburg.





Method 1 - by bus

Many people prefer to travel by bus. What is the reason for this? First of all, with a low fare on it. One way tickets will cost much less than any other form of public transport.





Flights from Kostroma to St. Petersburg are carried out only twice a week - on Tuesdays and Fridays. Of course, this is not very convenient, since this option does not coincide with the weekend schedule of some citizens. Departure takes place at midnight and already at one in the afternoon the passenger will be in the very center of the northern capital of the country. It is easy to guess that the total travel time will be about 13 hours. The approximate ticket price is 1800 rubles.

It is worth noting that although this is a popular transport with passengers, you can make a ticket purchase directly a few minutes before the trip, at the box office of the Kostroma intercity station.

Method 2 - tours

In the summer, many travel companies offer interesting tours. Destination Kostroma - St. Petersburg is one of the most popular with travelers. Departure takes place late in the evening, tourists are on the road all night, they can sleep and have a good rest, because in the afternoon they have to study the beauties of this famous city located on the Neva River. Similarly, the road in the opposite direction. Do not be afraid that there is a difficult path ahead. The trip is carried out on comfortable buses, they are equipped with air conditioning, seats with adjustable backrests and television. The approximate cost of such a tour is about 7000 rubles per person.





The most exciting is the tour from Kostroma to St. Petersburg on the boat. Typically, travel agencies offer directions to several cities that are part of the Golden Ring of Russia. It is worth noting that this is not a cheap pleasure. The approximate cost of the trip will be from 15,000 rubles per person.

Method 3 - the plane





Flight is the most convenient, fast and comfortable way that allows you to get from one point of the country to another. Not far from the city is the "Kostroma airline". In this popular organization you can buy a ticket for a flight mode of transport. Unfortunately, flights are not operated every day, but only on Monday, Wednesday and Friday. There are two departure times - 13:00 and 16:20. Exactly two hours after departure, this type of transport will be in the appointed place. Departure time may vary slightly, it is worthwhile to familiarize yourself with the updates on the scoreboard on the official website. It is worth noting that this is the most expensive method among the others listed, the cost of a trip for one person will be about 2000 rubles.

Method 4 - Train





The most comfortable option for many passengers is the train. Unfortunately, there is no direct flight moving in this direction. First of all, you should get to Yaroslavl or Moscow, but only from this intermediate city you can come to St. Petersburg. The total road time is approximately 16 hours 19 minutes. There are usually no problems with buying tickets, as there are always a lot of empty seats in the cars. An exception is the summer season. The total cost of the trip will be no more than 3000 rubles. It is worth noting that for the preferential category of citizens for this type of transport there are favorable discounts. This is the most convenient vehicle that allows you to overcome the distance from Kostroma to St. Petersburg. Children under 10 years old can travel for free, however, without providing them with additional space.

Method 5 - by car

Owners of a personal vehicle can easily drive from Kostroma to St. Petersburg by car. It is worth noting: the path ahead is not long, the total distance will be 879 kilometers. In order to get to the appointed place, you have to drive past three major cities: Yaroslavl, Cherepovets and Tikhvin. Unfortunately, on some sections of the road there is an uneven track, which is not the most favorable factor for many drivers. But there are many establishments along the length of the route, in which you can fully relax. Approximately the total road time excluding stops will take 11 hours 30 minutes.

Kostroma and St. Petersburg are two excellent cities located on the territory of the Russian Federation. They are distinguished by their beauty, unique history and famous sights. It is impossible to describe in words the beauty of these magnificent places; each person must visit them himself, see and be charmed. Fortunately, there are four main types of transport, with which you can most conveniently, quickly and safely get from one settlement to another.
