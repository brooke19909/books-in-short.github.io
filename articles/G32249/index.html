Waterfront Suites Phuket by Centara (Thailand / Phuket): photos and reviews of tourists

The island of Phuket is the largest of all that belongs to Thailand. It is located southwest of the coast, in the Andaman Sea. A large long bridge connects this island to the mainland. Seventy percent of its territory is occupied by beautiful relict forests. The climate in Phuket is very mild, tropical. Basically, this, as well as well-developed infrastructure, explains the popularity of this resort among Russian vacationers.

On the island of Phuket, vacationers are waiting for a warm sea, hot sun and a huge number of various entertainments. Of course, many comfortable hotels have been built here. At the same time, judging by the reviews of tourists, one of the best hotels is Waterfront Suites Phuket by Centara. Available Attractions

As already mentioned, on the territory of the Waterfront Suites Phuket by Centara 4 * hotel, reviews of which are good also for this reason, there is also a tour information desk. Attractions on the island of Phuket and in Thailand in general there is just a huge amount. In the immediate vicinity of the hotel, its guests can visit:

  1. Wat Karon Temple on the beach. This religious building was built in 1895. Its main building is guarded by two emerald green snakes, and the life of the Buddha is depicted on the inner walls.
  2. Small uninhabited islands. A very good solution would be to visit, for example, Phi Phi Ley or Racha Noi. Buying a one-day or two-day tour to such an island is an excellent opportunity for a while to completely forget about civilization, plunging into the pristine nature.
  3. Butterfly Garden. You should definitely come to this beautiful park with your children. In the garden you can admire not only beautiful butterflies, but also lizards and bright fish.

Also on the island of Phuket there are such interesting sights as the complex of temples Wat Chalong, Tiger Kingdom, Phuket Trickeye Museum, etc.
