Akyan St Petersburg 4 * (Russia, St. Petersburg): description, photos, reviews of tourists

Many guests of St. Petersburg note that the Akyan St Petersburg Hotel is an excellent option for temporary accommodation in the cultural capital of Russia. This place for rest regularly receives a lot of positive comments, which will be analyzed later. In addition, we consider the main features of accommodation in hotel rooms, as well as the subtleties of service.

General information

Akyan St Petersburg Hotel is an amazing place to relax for tourists who prefer to spend their time in a state of complete comfort. Repair works are regularly carried out in his building to keep the building in good condition - one of the last reconstructions was made in 2014, during which the hotel was replaced by a climate system, as well as the interior of some apartments was updated.

At present, Akyan St Petersburg has a rather high rating, as evidenced not only by high marks on international tourist portals (according to the Bucking website, the hotel has a rating of 9.3 points out of 10 available).

The considered vacation spot has several awards and is included in the ratings of the best hotels in Russia. So, in the description of Akyan St Petersburg, it says that the institution has a TrvellersChoice award from Tripadvisor, according to Style Wedding magazine, it is in the TOP 10 best places to stay in St. Petersburg, and in 2018, according to Melagrana Persona and Brands magazine , he became the best romantic hotel of the city.

Location

Akyan St Petersburg reviews often say that this hotel has a prime location. It is located in the historical part of the city, where travelers are very fond of visiting, wishing to study the special facts related to the foundation and development of the city.

The hotel is located within walking distance of the famous Nevsky Prospekt. Moreover, not far from it there are many interesting places. According to the guides, within a radius of 500 m from the hotel there are more than 300 attractions, so the tourist who got here will definitely not be bored - literally in a day he will have time to see a lot of interesting and fascinating things.

Basic infrastructure is also located at a fairly close distance from the hotel. In particular, here you can find a huge number of shops, shopping centers, bureaus, as well as restaurants and cafes nearby. In the reviews of tourists, a list of the best institutions located within walking distance from Akyan St Petersburg is often noted. These include Bao Mochi, a Chinese restaurant, where you can sample Mr. Mr.'s delicious seafood dishes. Bo and By Steak Steakhouse.

The hotel in question is located near the Mayakovskaya and Ploshchad Vosstaniya metro stations - from here you can easily get to any corner of the city. The international airport (Pulkovo) is a 15-minute drive from the considered place for recreation and temporary residence. Ladoga Train Station can be reached from here in 30-40 minutes.

Address Akyan St Petersburg: St. Petersburg, Vosstaniya street, 19.

Rooms

In the reviews of guests left to the address of the hotel in question, it is stated that the rooms presented in its fund are endowed with a certain atmosphere. Moreover, according to them, it is very pleasant to be in local apartments - they provide everything that is necessary for a comfortable stay surrounded by elegant elements. The hotel consists of 120 apartments, which, depending on the degree of comfort, are divided into several categories: Standard, Deluxe and Suite.

In accordance with local regulations, smoking is strictly prohibited in any type of apartment available at the hotel - a penalty is imposed for violating this rule.

Each room has a large bathroom, equipped with everything you need to maintain a clean body. Not only bath accessories are provided here, but also bathrobes, slippers, towels and even a hairdryer.

The pleasant temperature in the rooms of the Akyan St Petersburg hotel (you can find the photo of the establishment in the article) is ensured by the smooth operation of the climate system.

A restaurant

Akyan St Petersburg Hotel draws special attention to the local restaurant, where, as many say, you can enjoy excellent dishes of Russian, European, and Italian cuisine. Here, according to visitors, each guest is given special attention. Regular guests of the establishment often note that, having been here, you should certainly try traditional soups, signature desserts, as well as delicious pasta and steaks.

The restaurant's interior is presented in a classic style. It contains a large number of parts made of natural wood, as well as a considerable number of light fabrics, giving the whole picture a certain airiness. In the interior you can also observe masonry made of decorative bricks, unique hanging chandeliers, as well as stylish furniture made in the style of a traditional English loft.

Often such an atmospheric atmosphere of a restaurant attracts couples into the walls of the establishment for romantic evenings.

All those who wish to visit this institution are strongly recommended to pre-book a table, which can be done by the administration phone number indicated in the Restaurant section on the official website of the Akyan St Petersburg Hotel (St. Petersburg).

Honeymoon offer

In reviews of Akyan St Petersburg, it is often said that the restaurant of the hotel in question is ideal for celebrating a wedding, and its Suite room is for holding a wedding night.

If you wish, you can organize an unforgettable evening here. A honeymoon room is always equipped in a suite. The price of his reservation (6500 rubles) includes not only accommodation, but also breakfast, an unlimited amount of coffee and tea, a compliment from the chef (when ordering dishes from the restaurant), as well as the possibility of making a late check out. Moreover, at the entrance to the apartment, the newlyweds receive a bottle of sparkling wine and a basket of fruit as a gift.

If desired, the customer can purchase a package for newlyweds worth 10,000 rubles. In addition to all standard features, it includes a transfer from the hotel to the place of residence (within the city), as well as the possibility of an even later check-out - at 6 pm. Young chefs get a couple of signature desserts from the restaurant's chef, as well as a buffet breakfast with individual delivery to the apartments. Before arrival, couples in the room are decorated accordingly.

In accordance with the rules established by the hotel, when ordering a room for the newlyweds, a 100% prepayment is required, and this must be done at least a couple of days before the event. As for the possibility of canceling the package, it is available, but this can be done at least 2 weeks before the scheduled date.

Prices

The pricing policy set for accommodation at the Akyan St Petersburg hotel undoubtedly pleases many Russians, who often note the acceptable prices in their reviews left for this place for temporary residence.
