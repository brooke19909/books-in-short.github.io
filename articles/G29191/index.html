Resort Town Life

This town is on the list of the cleanest settlements on the planet, because at one time someone wise forbade the construction of industrial enterprises here. Today, more than 30,000 people live in it, most of which are engaged in the services of tourists and sanatorium patients.

In the distant 1864, this place was chosen to create a hospital where officers of the tsarist army could improve their health with the help of healing mineral waters. The name of the place was due to its hot springs, the temperature of which at any time of the year is +62 degrees.





During the years of Soviet power, sanatoriums and dispensaries were built here, in which patients with diseases of the musculoskeletal system and gastrointestinal tract were sent for treatment.

Nowadays, in addition to specialized medical institutions, guests are expected to have Goryachiy Klyuch hotels, of which there are many. A convenient service in sanatoriums is the ability to select individual wellness procedures and undergo them without having to stay on site. This allows local residents and visitors to recover at a convenient time for them.

City Hotels

Gradually, from the Soviet sanatorium resort, the city turned into a tourist center where people come not only for health, but also for ordinary relaxation. Everything has to do with this: virgin nature, and clean air, and the beautiful foothills of the Caucasus, and cozy Goryachiy Klyuch hotels.

The city has a well-developed entertainment sector, tourists are expected to hike and ride along the Psekups River, famous for its picturesque banks and the Rock of the Cockerel, standing in its waters. Dantovo Gorge, where it is pleasant to relax in the summer forty-degree heat, Iverskaya Chapel, near which a spring with rejuvenating water beats, Psifabe - an old dilapidated fortress, night clubs, restaurants and discos are at the disposal of vacationers.





At any time of the year, guests are welcomed by hotels. Goryachiy Klyuch (Krasnodar Territory) offers a choice of guest houses, recreation centers and hotels, with swimming pools or SPA salons, comfortable rooms and excellent service.
