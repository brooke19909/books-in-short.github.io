The best hotels of Crete

Greece has always attracted tourists from all over the planet with its excellent landscapes. Recently, the island of Crete has been unusually popular, which offers the most luxurious, rich and comfortable hotels. In this article, we will consider the top three hotels that can give any tourist the most unforgettable emotions and impressions.



The best hotels of Crete: detailed overview

Capsis Ruby Red Regal

An excellent hotel that opens its doors to everyone, it is located on a private peninsula near the village of Agia Pelagia. This luxurious luxury venue has been specifically designed as a Minoan palace. Wide terraces and magnificent blooming gardens surround the main building of this hotel. And the amazing view of the church of St. Athanasia gives it a special zest and charm. Room service is provided for the highest level. A constantly replenished minibar, bath slippers and bathrobes are just a small fraction of what hotels filling the island of Crete can offer you. Hotels with only positive reviews are distinguished by first-class service, and this is no exception. A whole ceremony awaits you upon arrival: a welcome Greek drink at the entrance and a bottle of wine in the room. Everyone staying at this hotel can visit 11 restaurants, 7 pools, 8 bars. Children will also not be bored, because it is here that you can walk through a private zoo, buy a ticket to a children's amusement park and enjoy plenty of fun at the water park. And the botanical garden and the famous "dancing fountains" will not leave without an adult emotion neither an adult nor a child. Thanks to its superbly developed infrastructure, this chic place is included in the best hotels of Crete.



Sea Side Resort & Spa

Among the locals, it is called Mononaftis Castle. The hotel is perfect for those who prefer a quality vacation along with amazing natural landscapes. It is rich in a selection of first-class restaurants. One main with a children's menu and buffet, as well as 2 additional restaurants and several bars. In addition to four swimming pools, a TV room, sports grounds and conference rooms, the hotel offers to visit one of the most famous SPA centers. This center includes services such as a sauna, hammam and, of course, a beauty salon. The hotel has 245 rooms of the most diverse class, from economy to premium. Thanks to this wealth and wonderful Spa-center, he was included in the best hotels of the island of Crete.



Aquila rithymna beach

This hotel has its own sand and pebble beach, and also has a chic room fund. Here you can choose whatever your heart desires - from bungalows to royal apartments. In total, this hotel can offer 520 rooms for every taste. What is noteworthy, it has special offers for people whose opportunities are limited. The luxury and variety of entertainment allowed this place to be included in the list of "The best hotels of the island of Crete." It offers guests 5 swimming pools, conference rooms, business centers, Internet cafes, shops, a TV room, a hospital, a sauna, a massage room, tennis courts, fitness rooms and mini markets. The animation program is also well developed, which will not let anyone get bored.

When planning a trip, do not forget to pre-analyze all the best hotels in Crete. Greece has always been famous for being ready to offer a vacation for any tourist, regardless of his financial capabilities. Therefore, if you are still in doubt about the upcoming vacation, feel free to go to this wonderful country.
