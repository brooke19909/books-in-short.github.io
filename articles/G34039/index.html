Alex Beach Hotel 4 * (Gagra, Abkhazia): photos and reviews of tourists, a description of the hotel, prices

Before the start of the holiday season, everyone asks a question: where to go to relax? If you are tired of Antalya and Egypt, and there is not enough money for an expensive vacation somewhere on the tropical islands in the middle of the southern seas and oceans, then it is better to pay attention to the revived resorts of Abkhazia. For several years, Pitsunda, Gagra, Sukhumi and other once popular resort towns on the shores of the Black Sea have been gladly opening their doors to tourists from Russia and other countries.

Abkhazia - Black Sea Paradise

Abkhazia with its wild untouched nature, clear sea, wide beaches, ancient history and culture is truly a paradise for all lovers of beach, educational, extreme relaxation. Of course, you can visit these places throughout the year, but a summer vacation in Abkhazia can be a real holiday for you. After all, the beauty of nature, which you will encounter in these parts, will lead you into a state of delight, reverential ecstasy. The combination of sky blue with emerald shades of lush tropical and coniferous forests and a delicate turquoise hue of the sea is the most beautiful thing your eye can ever see.



However, the biggest plus of these places is still Caucasian hospitality and hospitality of the local population. Along with this, the Abkhazians are very calm and balanced, they do not like to create fuss and noise around themselves. Indigenous people always treat their guests with incredible respect and care, and are ready to fulfill all their desires. Fans of secluded relaxation can always find wild beaches, cool waterfalls deep in lush forests, quiet places far from prying eyes.

Summer holidays in Gagra

So you decided where to go to relax in the summer. And, of course, your choice fell on a delightful Black Sea resort in sunny Abkhazia. Gagra is perhaps one of the most famous recreation areas. This town was founded in 1903 by the prince of Oldenburg. The resort has always been considered a great place for families. Its special microclimate, comfortable beaches, tropical trees and flowers, creepers, magnolias, fragrant needles and laurels - all this will not leave indifferent even the most selective tourist.



Compared to those distant times, much has changed here. Since the days of the USSR, the city was divided into two zones: Old and New. In recent years, the new district has undergone many transformations, has become more comfortable and beautiful, modern and presentable. There is quite developed tourist infrastructure. There are many hotels, guesthouses and houses, restaurants, bars and cafes. Also in Gagra there is a well-equipped water park and many other extreme attractions.

How to get there?

From Russia to Abkhazia can be reached in various ways. Those who prefer the quick option, of course, choose air transport. Regular flights fly from many large cities of Russia to Adler, and from there to the resorts of Abkhazia - just a stone's throw. You can reach your destination by taxi or by bus. In addition, to Sochi from different settlements of Russia can be reached by train.



There is another true option - your own car. At first glance, this is tiring. But on the other hand, while on vacation, you can explore all corners of a country rich in historical and natural attractions. By the way, to the attention of those tourists who choose "star" hotels in Abkhazia, for example, Alex Beach Hotel 4 * (Gagra): having arranged a transfer when purchasing a tour, you will be met at the airport or at the railway station in Sochi and comfortably taken to the door hotel complex.

Alex Beach Hotel 4 * (Abkhazia / Gagra): general description

This hotel is one of the best in Abkhazia. Here, each of the tourists will meet what he came to Gagra for. Regardless of this, all vacationers will receive complete relaxation and comfort. Clean, intoxicating, slightly brackish and very aromatic air will fill the lungs with special strength and energy that will spread throughout the body. The sun's rays are somehow special: they do not sting, do not bite, but only gently caress the skin, covering it with an incredibly beautiful tan. Wonderful landscapes that open from the windows of the hotel will give you real aesthetic pleasure, but the highest service will bring complete satisfaction. What awaits you at the new four-star hotel in Gagra?

So, you are in Abkhazia, in the wonderful hotel complex "Alex Beach Hotel". The five-story modern hotel building was built in 2010. Until now, it is considered the best on the entire Abkhaz Black Sea coast. For guests and guests of the hotel there are many areas for leisure: the Four Pearls SPA center, a heated pool, a beauty salon, rooms with personal jacuzzis, restaurants, cafes, bars, games rooms and playgrounds, a variety of sports activities and, of course , a beach that pleases with its well-groomed, magnificent natural landscape and clear sea water.

In addition, Alex Beach Hotel 4 * is perfect for holding business seminars, scientific conferences and symposia. For these purposes, there is a modernly equipped conference room with a meeting room and a business center with all the necessary conditions for fruitful work.

Location

As befits a hotel of this category, Alex Beach Hotel 4 * (Abkhazia / Gagra) is located on the first beach line. It is only 24 km from Sochi Airport, that is, a 15-20 minute drive. It is located in an area with lush flowering vegetation and entrancing air. The territory of the complex is well guarded, so tourists feel completely safe here.





Number of rooms

The hotel has 76 rooms. Of these, 68 are standard, 6 are deluxe and two are luxury. The latter are equipped with a jacuzzi and have a gorgeous view of the sea. These rooms are mainly reserved for honeymooners. At the entrance, they will find a bowl of fruit and champagne. All rooms are decorated in four basic colors: coral, amber, azure and pearl. Each of the rooms is equipped with a bathroom with shower, and some are equipped with a jacuzzi. They also have a minibar, air conditioning, TV, safe, hairdryer and Internet access. Guests at the hotel can feel completely safe because all categories of rooms have the SALTO electronic lock system.

Nutrition

Depending on the time period, the hotel has different types of food. At the height of the holiday season (from 1.06 to 30.09), as well as during the New Year holidays (from 28.12 to 11.01), tourists eat according to the "full board" system, which includes breakfast, lunch and dinner like a buffet.





Before the start of the beach season (all of May) and all of October, guests are offered only breakfast and dinner. In full off-season, only buffet breakfasts are served for rare visitors, and the rest of the time, tourists can order dishes according to an individual menu (for a fee). In addition, on the territory of the complex, or rather, right on the beach, there is a grill bar where you can order excellent Caucasian barbecue and enjoy it right on the seashore.

Prices and Services

Prices for accommodation in this hotel, depending on the season, as well as the type of room, vary from 1,500 to 14,000 rubles. At the same time, the price includes food and some types of services. The hotel has an outdoor heated pool (free of charge), around which there is a sunbathing area with sunbeds and umbrellas that protect from the sun. For lovers of active leisure there is a sports and entertainment center called "Sphere". In addition to fitness equipment, there are billiard tables and a field equipped for bowling (billiards and bowling at certain times of the day can be used for free). It should be noted that in the sports complex you can train for free. For fans of board games - checkers, chess and backgammon - rental is available (for a fee). A gourmet restaurant "Alex", a grill cafe with the Caucasian name "Mangal", a lobby bar "Hemingway" are at the disposal of gourmets.





Since the Alex Beach Hotel 4 * hotel is a hotel and spa complex, tourists can combine business with pleasure here. Namely - to take various health and spa treatments in the modern sanatorium "Four Pearls", which is also part of this complex. For interesting and eventful leisure of little tourists there is a children's creative gazebo, as well as a playground with slides and swings. The hotel has a service for organizing various celebrations: weddings, anniversaries, banquets, corporate parties, conferences, presentations, sports tournaments, etc. Tourists at the hotel's territory are equipped with a tour desk, souvenir shop, currency exchange office, Alex Beach Hotel 4 *. SIM card sales; Laundry and dry cleaning, parking. In addition, taxi can be ordered (round the clock), in the presence of wireless Internet (in public areas).

Services for children

Thanks to the care of the administration of the Alex Beach Hotel 4 *, families with children can find here all the amenities for the comfortable accommodation of their children. By the way, for babies up to 2 years old you will not have to pay, they are provided with comfortable cots. Children from five to thirteen years old are entitled to an extra bed plus food (for a fee). Hotel of the same name in Greece

In different parts of the world there are hotels with the same name. Often they belong to the same hotel chain. Far from Abkhazia, in Greece, there is the Alex Beach Hotel 4 * (Rhodes). Let's look at the commonality and differences of these heavenly corners. Well, firstly, both hotels are four-star, but unlike Gagrinsky, Rhodes is not located in a multi-story building, but in several comfortable bungalows. That's why it is called 4 * Alex Beach Hotel Bungalows. In addition, there are much more rooms, almost twice. The biggest plus is the patios, decorated in the Greek style.





The territory of Alex Beach Hotel 4 * (Rhodes) is much larger than that of the Abkhazian. There are tennis courts and bike paths. Near the hotel there is a buffet shop where anyone can stock up on groceries, because each room has a refrigerator. In addition to the sunbathing area, there is a pool bar by the pool. Both hotels have their advantages, as they serve guests at the highest level. Both Greeks and Abkhazians are hospitable peoples, and for them to receive guests with all their intrinsic hospitality is a matter of honor.

Nevertheless, for the Russians, the Abkhazian hotel has several undeniable advantages. Firstly, Gagras are much closer than Rhodes. This means that much less time and money will be spent on the journey than for a trip to sunny Greece. Secondly, the prices here are noticeably lower. Do not forget that one of the most expensive countries for living in the Mediterranean is Greece.
