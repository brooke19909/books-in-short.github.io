CPU

It is the high performance and phenomenal energy efficiency of the CPU that is one of the key advantages of the Huawei Ascend Mate 7. Premium - devices from other manufacturers with similar characteristics cannot boast of such a combination. The device is based on the computing platform Kirin 925, developed by a subsidiary of Huawei - Hisilicon. According to the documentation, this chip includes 8 real computing cores that operate according to the big.LITTLE scheme. That is, at maximum and average load on the device, 4 computing modules based on the Cortex-A7 architecture are in operation. They are capable of overclocking at maximum load to 1.3 GHz. Their main advantage is high energy efficiency. It should also be noted that if 2 cores out of 4 are enough to solve the problem, the remaining 2 just turn off. Do not forget that with an excess of computing power, the frequency of each active module automatically decreases. As a result, we get a very energy-efficient system that adapts to the needs of the problem being solved. When the computing resources of the first computing module of four cores are not enough, it turns off and the second computing cluster automatically enters the work. It also consists of 4 cores. But only in this case we are talking about the architecture of the ‚ÄúCortex-A15‚Äù. It provides an uncompromising level of performance. The clock frequency of each of them can reach 1.8 GHz. Again, the frequency of the computational modules in this case dynamically varies depending on the degree of complexity of the problem being solved. In exactly the same way as in the previous case, kernels not involved in the operation are deactivated. This allows you to run almost any task on this smartphone. The only exception is 64-bit applications, which are now not so many. It's just that this chip is built on the basis of two 32-bit architectures - such software will definitely not be ‚Äútough‚Äù for him. All of the above makes the Huawei Ascend Mate 7 very similar to the two flagship solutions from Samsung: S4 and S5. But this Chinese manufacturer went further and equipped this chip with another educational computing module, which received the code name ‚Äúi3‚Äù. Its clock frequency is only 230 MHz. He is responsible for the maintenance of sensors and sensors. Such a constructive solution allows you to significantly unload the main computing resources of the smartphone and thereby further increase its performance and energy efficiency. No other manufacturer of mobile gadgets, including Apple or Samsung, can boast of such hardware optimization. Finally, it should be noted that this semiconductor crystal is made in accordance with the standards of the 28-nm process.

The battery of the gadget and its autonomy

The Huawei Ascend Mate 7 smartphone has a built-in 4100 mAh battery. In reality, one charge is enough for 1-2 days of battery life. Given the diagonal of the display, the size of the device and the processing power of the central processor, this is an excellent indicator. If you use the maximum saving mode (it is possible only to make calls and send and receive SMS), then you can count on 3 days. Something more could be achieved with such technical parameters and with such a battery capacity.
