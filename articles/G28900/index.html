DIY Pencil: The Three Most Interesting Ways

The picture, when pencils and pens are scattered throughout the house, can be observed in almost every house where there are children. How to teach your baby to keep all writing accessories in order? DIY pencil and twine. What is the connection?

A container for storing writing instruments at home can be made of waste material, such as a can and a piece of jute rope or twine, as it is also called. In addition to these items, you will also need satin ribbons, Dragon or Moment glue, decor elements (beads, sequins), acrylic paints.

Work on the implementation of such crafts will take no more than 30-40 minutes, and it will stand in the service of your child for a long time until he wants to make a new pencil box. And for such a case, we still have interesting ideas.



Do you like to drink juice? Then you have a new pencil storage tank

Do not rush to throw an empty box out of juice or milk. This is a practically finished cardboard pencil box. With your own hands you can make an original "house" for stationery in a matter of minutes. To do this, follow these steps.

  1. Wash and dry the box.
  2. Cut off the top of the container and discard it. For further work, we do not need it.
  3. Glue the bottom part with colored paper and decorate in any way you want. This can be an application of paper, natural material, fabric, ribbons.

This is how fast it becomes a simple, but at the same time practical in use and beautiful in appearance do-it-yourself pencil case.
