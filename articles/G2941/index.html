Best campsites in Lermontovo

Where would you like to go in the summer? Most Russians will say: in Anapa, Tuapse or Sochi. Luxury hotels and nightly entertainment, cafes and restaurants - all this awaits guests and promises to make a vacation unforgettable. But what about those who crave a simple rest in the lap of nature? Especially for the romantics there is a campsite in Lermontovo. This village is famous for its beach called "Golden Coast". It stretches for 10 km, and the water here is clean and clear. This is not the only local attraction.



What will please Lermontovo

The second famous beach is called "Central". It is located along the promenade, which houses shops, cafes and bars. Here you can rent sports equipment and water equipment. There are catamarans and jet skis, paragliders, in general, dozens of ways to tickle your nerves. No less popular is the Kiseleva rock. It offers a gorgeous view of the sea. Here is the most popular campsite in Lermontovo, and a 15-minute nudist beach where you can sunbathe, hiding from prying eyes behind the rocks.

Here you get the opportunity to sleep under the stars and relax from civilization, and not far away is a huge water park called "Black Sea". There is a huge selection of catering places. And you can go to the Tenginsky waterfalls. Let's continue to consider camping in Lermontovo, or rather, individual campsites and their features.



Raccoon: relax at home

One of the youngest camping sites in the tent. But I must say that the service is pleasantly surprising. This is an excellent campsite in Lermontovo, the owners of which try to do their job as well as possible, and not just collect money from tourists. Camping is located on the edge of a deciduous forest. For vacationers, washbasins and toilets are provided, in addition, the owners share the concept of "wild relaxation" and support the ban on music. In the aviary, a raccoon lives as a symbol of camping. True, it is full and wild. They come in the evenings and enjoy eating leftover food. Of the minuses, it can be noted that this campsite in Lermontovo is located quite far from the sea, about a 15-minute walk. But if you don‚Äôt feel like going back on foot, call the owners, they will pick up by car.

Camping "Filat"

It is located 3 km from the village of Lermontovo. A very comfortable place to relax with children, thanks to the sandy beach and the shallow beach. Judging by the reviews, throughout the summer season you will find an excellent vacation in Lermontovo. Camping "Filat" is rather a recreation center, very comfortable. Here you can rent an economy class room. For self-catering, two shared kitchens are equipped. Showers and toilets are located on the campsite, there is a relaxation area and gazebos with barbecue facilities. Nearby is a playground and places to relax in tents. In addition, there is a cottage for 6 beds with all amenities. That is, you can choose for yourself the most suitable type of vacation. Nearby there is a cafe that you can use if you do not want to mess with cooking. This is a homely camping in Lermontovo. Reviews emphasize that the owners are very attentive and pleasant. They really try to make your vacation the best.



Camping "Kubanets"

Judging by the many reviews, people often relax here every year. This is a great campsite in Lermontovo (Krasnodar Territory). In fact, this is another recreation center. A haven for those who are looking for a quiet and relaxing holiday, with a sparsely populated beach. The territory is located in a gorge between the mountains, near a deciduous forest. In addition to the campsite, there are cozy houses twined with vine and climbing roses. The rooms have all the amenities. Large wardrobe, comfortable bed and excellent plumbing, there is always hot water. At the base there is a dining room where you can eat three times a day. The food is very tasty. And you can buy products at the agricultural fair and cook yourself.

What else can be noted from the pros? Entertainment - until 23:00, silence at night. If you want to have fun until the morning - the village of Lermontovo itself is nearby, where there are enough opportunities for this. At night, the territory is guarded, the watchman constantly makes a detour. The beach at the campsite is wild, but there are not too many people on it.



Camping "Horizon"

Reviews Lermontovo (Krasnodar Territory) is called the ideal place to relax in peace and quiet in the bosom of nature. Judging by the reviews, this is one of the most comfortable bases. It is located at A-147, a 14-minute walk to the city center. The area is very clean, there are parking spaces under the trees for cars. It offers rooms with a mobile home and a tent.

Here you can order a transfer from and to the airport. The territory is very green and well-groomed, provides free access to the Internet. Water sports facilities are available at the property. The summer vacation is especially good here, because the beach is nearby. Nearby there is a place for beautiful fishing. A grocery delivery service is available upon request. Do not forget that if you bring pets with you, you must inform about this in advance.



Rest and knowledge of the world

If you are going to relax with your children, then camping "Arizona" in Lermontovo will be the best choice. All vacationers are met personally by the owner. He will navigate through the territory, show and tell what and where there is. The territory is very clean, comfortable, well-groomed. There are toilets and showers, water and light. Sockets are located on all poles, which makes it possible to use all the necessary devices.

The territory is clean, there are many spreading trees, as well as an excellent cafe. By order, you can cook sea fish and barbecue on coals. Raccoons often come here who enjoy food with great pleasure. The whole territory is equipped with cameras and the owner monitors the appearance of uninvited guests. Only a few flights of stairs to the sea. There are changing cabins on the beach. Coating - large pebbles. The sea is very clear, and dolphins often come here. They let pets into the territory, which is very pleasing.



Camping "Cedar"

Located in the center of a quiet resort, 300 meters from the beach. The choice of accommodation on the campsite depends entirely on your preferences. Here accept applications for the collective rest of children's groups. On the territory there are one-story houses with rooms for 2 and 3 people. In addition, there are tent places with parking for cars. All guests are provided with a summer equipped kitchen. The beach is located just 5 minutes walk, which is very convenient for families with children. A large public dining room will completely dispense with self-cooking.

Autocamping "Dolphin"

It is located 3 km from the village of Lermontovo, in the direction of Sochi. On the territory there is a grocery store, a sandy beach just 10 meters from the campsite. Excursions to waterfalls and mud baths are regularly arranged. Next to it there is an ostrich farm and cable car. The cost of one parking space for a car and a tent is 400-550 rubles. This price includes a shower, toilet and barbecue. If you do not have your own tent, but want to enjoy spending the night under the stars, then you can rent a tent. The cost is 150 rubles.



Instead of a conclusion

If you are bored with guest houses on the Black Sea coast, then welcome to the bosom of nature, that is, to the territory of any campsite. Photo Lermontovo very often becomes a determining factor. Seeing how beautiful it is here, tourists are no longer looking for other options for themselves. Most travelers stop from such trips the lack of basic amenities that you lose when choosing a wild vacation. Here, romance of wild relaxation and comfort are successfully combined. In addition, wild rest is always a security concern. After breaking the tent, you will not go far from it, so as not to lose your property. There are no strangers on the campsite, video surveillance is installed and there is security. Often the owners of the campsite are ready to guide you through the base and show its merits, after which you can decide for yourself whether you will stay here or go on.
