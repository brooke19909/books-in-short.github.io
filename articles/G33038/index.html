Luzhniki metro station Sportivnaya

Even those who have never been to Moscow know this name well. It has been associated with top-level sports since the Soviet era. Everyone who cares about hockey or football knows the way to Luzhniki well. The Sportivnaya metro station of the Sokolnicheskaya line is a kind of gateway to big sport. It is closest to both the Big and the Small Sports Arenas.

Luzhniki Stadium, Sportivnaya metro station. Architectural features

"Sports" was opened in May 1957. It was one of the last stations of the Moscow Metro that did not fall under the destructive influence of the well-known Khrushchev company against architectural excesses. The decoration of the interior is traditionally in the good sense of the word. It corresponds to the architectural style of the previous historical era. The predominant finishing materials are marble, granite and bronze. The track walls are decorated with glazed ceramic tiles of different colors. In its constructional type, Sportivnaya is a three-vault pylon station of deep laying. There are two vestibules, one north and one south. On ordinary days, the flow of passengers at the Sportivnaya station is quite small. But often she has to work in a very stressful mode. During grandiose sporting events at the Luzhniki Stadium, the metro station alternately works only at the exit of passengers, and after the end of the competition, only at the entrance. Sometimes clashes between different groups of sports fans occur in the surrounding yards and at the station itself.
