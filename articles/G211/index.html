How to sew the pillow letters with your own hands. Pillow letters - a great gift

This article will focus on such interesting subjects as pillows in the form of letters. The soft alphabet made of textiles is very relevant today. Why? We talk about this further.



The purpose of the interior pillows in the form of letters

Such textile products are multifunctional. They can serve as a decorative decoration of the interior of the house. For kids, this is a great educational toy with which they can learn the ABCs, learn how to lay out words and read them. And, in the end, such pillows can be used in letters for their intended purpose - for sleeping. Given all these facts, we conclude that such a thing would be a wonderful holiday gift for both a child and an adult.

If you like the pillows with the letters that you see in the photo, if you have a burning desire to have such cute little things in your house, we invite you to study the information presented in the article below. It tells you how to make a soft alphabet with your own hands at home. Possessing basic sewing skills and having all the necessary materials, you can easily carry out such textile products.



Learning to sew a soft alphabet. What is needed for this?

In order to independently perform pillows in the form of letters, you will need some materials and tools. We list them below:

  • fabric (cotton, linen, fleece, felt, flannel);
  • threads
  • needle;
  • scissors;
  • filler (synthetic winterizer, holofiber);
  • paper;
  • pencil;
  • pins
  • sewing machine.

Making pillows in letters. Method number 1 (the easiest)

This model of a product can be executed even by a beginning seamstress. The manufacture of such elements of the alphabet is manual. We study the instructions.

On paper, draw a full-size sketch of the letter. To ensure that all elements are proportional, use a ruler. Cut the resulting template and transfer it to a double folded fabric. Pin the paper part with pins and circle it with a pencil. Now remove the template. Cut the textile blank exactly along the contour. Since the letters will be sewn together manually on the front side of the product, no allowances should be left.

When the product is almost completely sewn up, put the needle down and take the filler. Fill the pillow with small portions of syntepon (holofiber). Try to keep this material from clumping inside the letter. Do not fill the product too tightly, otherwise the seams may break. Next, sew the hole, fasten the thread and cut it. That's all. Similarly, follow other pillows with letters. Such soft elements of the alphabet can be decorated with lace, embroidery, textile applique, beads.

We sew voluminous soft letters. Method number 3

The pillow model, the manufacturing method of which is described below, differs from the previous ones in that it turns out to be more voluminous and stable. And all because in this version the letter has not only the front and back sides, but also the end sides. In the photo you can see examples of such products. How to make such soft pillow letters with your own hands?

On paper we draw a sketch of the letter and separately - its end faces. Here you should be careful not to make mistakes with measurements. The width of all lateral, upper and lower elements of the letters should be the same. We transfer the patterns to the fabric and cut out. You can stitch the product on the wrong side or on the front - that's how you want. If you decide to connect the parts in the face, then use decorative seams. Fill the letters in the same way as described in the previous instructions.



How to make a soft alphabet of foam rubber?
