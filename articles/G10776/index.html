Unusual pineapple flowers

Dried pineapple flowers are an unusual decoration for your cakes and muffins, and lovers of all pineapple can use these flowers without additives.

Instruction:

  • Take a pineapple, peel it.
  • Cut it into thin circles.
  • We will make notches on the outside of the circles, which will turn into elegant petals when dried and rise.
  • Lay out the pineapple circles on a baking sheet covered with special paper.
  • We put in a preheated oven for about half an hour. As you will see that the circles are browned, done!

Comfort starts at the door

Another creative thing that can be done quickly is the entrance mat.

  • Take a thick cord or rope. The thicker the product, the thicker our mat will turn out.
  • We also prepare a tube of the strongest and colorless glue.
  • Twisting the cord in a spiral, grease the outer side of the resulting circle. So do it turn by turn.
  • We give the rug several hours to dry and enjoy using it.

Who else fruit?

You can also easily and quickly create a fruit bowl.

We will need:

  • A skein of twine or any suitable rope.
  • PVA glue diluted by one third with water.
  • Dishes for glue.
  • Cling film.

Any suitable container around which we will wrap twine.

We take the twine, soak it in a solution of glue with water. Wrap a suitable cup first with cling film, then with a wet cord. You can wind in a spiral, but you can - in an artistic mess. Dry the workpiece. We separate the resulting dry bowl and we can safely find the scope of use of such a creative thing.

In such a bowl you can put not only fruits.

Creative things from yarn can easily be knitted for your home or for yourself. Original coasters for a hot, knitted pillowcase for a sofa cushion ... And a plaid associated with the repetition of your favorite motive will warm even in the most severe frosts.
