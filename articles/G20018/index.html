Varieties of Men's T-Shirts

Each man in the wardrobe must have several types of t-shirts that he puts on depending on the season, event (business meeting, going to the office, meeting, leisure) and mood.

But there is a certain unspoken classification of them:

  1. White T-shirt (more often it is made of cotton fabric) - worn for formal, special occasions or just for a trip on a yacht or around the city with your family.
  2. With a V-neck, it is often chosen by a man to relax, walk near the water, to the beach. And also preferred as a sports men's t-shirt.
  3. "Polo" - from cotton fabric, with a small collar and several buttons. The favorite of many men, regardless of occupation and tastes.
  4. Hanley. It is also a cotton T-shirt, without a collar, but with a few buttons. Can be worn instead of Polo.

Men's t-shirt pattern

How to sew a men's t-shirt? Next, a simple straight cut model will be considered. The measure with this method is the minimum amount, which greatly facilitates the task.

Thus, it can be noted that such a cut minimally takes into account the peculiarities of the figure of the person for whom this T-shirt is made. It's always best to start with a simple one. And already becoming a more experienced master, you can take for more complex types of cut, which take into account the shoulders, and posture, and so on.

Building a pattern of the back of a T-shirt

To carry out the intended task, it is necessary to take such measurements:

  • half neck circumference - N;
  • half-chest - Cg;
  • product height - Di;

Starting to build a pattern for a men's t-shirt, according to the data obtained, it is necessary to build a rectangle whose width (AB). In fact, this is the width of the future T-shirt and it consists of a half-chest, plus a small allowance for free body fit. The range of increase for a men's T-shirt of the cut chosen by us can very fluctuate. In this case, it is enough to stop at 10-12 cm.

And the height of the rectangular workpiece (AN) will be equal to the measurement of the height of the product.

Building a pattern in front of a T-shirt

T-shirt with raglan sleeve

The fabric for it can be either knitted or cotton. In addition, a fringing ribbon may be needed (for complete processing of cuts).



What is a raglan t-shirt? This is when the sleeves of the product are covered with the shoulder part of the front and back. The neck passes smoothly into the shoulders, in simple terms. Consider building a raglan t-shirt pattern.

To start, there are a few general recommendations:

  • In order for the fabric to remain elastic in the places where the seams are made, the details of the pattern must be sewn with a narrow zigzag stitch.
  • And in order for the lower edge to remain extensible, it is necessary to overcast the hem with an overlock. Then grind, using a double needle for this purpose.
  • The same applies to the decoration of the other edges of the product, it is important that they retain their elasticity.

As for the pattern of men's raglan t-shirts, in some ways it is similar to the pattern of a regular sports t-shirt. The figures show the contour lines of the construction of the back and front, as well as the sleeves.





The measurements are taken the same as for a regular T-shirt: half neck, chest, product height and sleeve length, only now taking into account the fact that it will go from the neck.





Raglan is being built on the pattern of the front and rear halves. Set aside 4 centimeters from the highest point of the shoulder along the neckline. And now you need to combine these points with the highest point of the side seam. Separate the raglan details and transfer them to the sides of the sleeve corresponding to them.

When all this is done, it is necessary to remove the details of the raglan from the front and back of the pattern.

All details must be signed so that nothing is confused when sewing T-shirts.
