Ares Blue Hotel 4 * (Kemer, Turkey): description, service, reviews

Holidays in Turkey every year become more popular. Such activity of tourists in this direction is connected with the all-inclusive system, according to which most hotels operate. And also the cost of the tours is quite attractive given that the price includes food and entertainment. Ares Blue Hotel 4 * cannot boast a large territory, but the service here for an average price is pretty good.

Where is

The hotel is located in Kemer. Distance to the coast is approximately 800 meters. The airport in Alanya is about 60 km away. Near Ares Blue Hotel 4 * there are many shops, a market and restaurants.





Kemer city center is a 5-7 minute walk from the hotel. Therefore, guests are available all the possible entertainment of the city. The complex provides tourists with a shuttle service. However, for this you need to agree in advance.

Description of Ares Blue Hotel 4 *

The hotel occupies a small territory. The complex consists of two buildings in 2 floors. The territory is well-groomed and has many green spaces. Sidewalks and lighting are installed everywhere. It organized various recreation areas. This can be a place for sunbathing or cozy gazebos in the shade of luxurious palm trees.

Many areas are planted with flowers that delight with their colors throughout the season. The hotel was built in 2004. In 2017, it was restored. The complex occupies 7,000 m 2 . The hotel has 130 rooms.

Accommodations

Ares Blue Hotel 4 * at the discretion of tourists provides rooms that can accommodate from 2 to 5 people. All rooms are equipped according to approximately the same principle and have a similar content:

  • double and single beds;
  • bedside tables;
  • cabinets;
  • air conditioning;
  • hair dryer;
  • TV with Russian channels;
  • WC and shower.

For an additional payment, a telephone, a minibar, and a safe can be provided. At the request of customers with small children, a cot with sides is provided. The rooms do not have access to the Internet.





Furniture can not be called modern. But she is in good condition. The rooms are small in size, but very comfortable and well maintained.

Entertainment

On the territory of Ares Blue Hotel 4 * there is a playground for playing volleyball and soccer. Outdoor enthusiasts can also leave darts at the darts and use the tennis tables.

The hotel organizes entertainment events with animators. Once every 2 weeks, a show program "Turkish Night" is held. Outdoor games are arranged with young guests.





For a fee, tourists can visit the Turkish bath, sauna and massage room. And there is also the opportunity to ride horses. The hotel has a billiards room.

Since the hotel is located directly in the city, tourists can visit discos, restaurants, various shows, without being attached to transport. All these services are within walking distance.

Water activities

Ares Blue Hotel 4 * in Kemer has on its territory an outdoor pool. It is divided into two zones. The nursery is fenced, and there is a small water slope in the form of a rainbow. Adult - quite spacious and makes it possible to actively swim. It also has a slide, only a larger one.





Guests can enjoy water skiing and canoeing at the beach. There is also a diving center. Beginners can pay for trial classes, and craftsmen can actively explore the local bottom. All the necessary equipment in the center can be rented.

The city has a modern large water park. It has a variety of slides and other entertainment. Also, tourists can visit the large aquarium. Many underwater inhabitants from around the world are represented here.

Beach and Pool

Ares Blue Hotel 4 * in Turkey has its own outdoor pool. It is not very large in size, but there is enough space for an adult to swim freely.

There is a slide with a descent into the pool. A special anti-slip coating is laid around . There are also sun loungers and parasols. In the same area there is a small children's pool. It is fenced with a special low fence, and it has its own small water slide.

The beach is 200 meters away. On the way to it you need to cross the road with fairly active traffic. There is no underpass here. The beach is urban. It has sun loungers and sun protection umbrellas.

There are various cafes and souvenir shops. You can rent water transport and ride a banana. There is no separate private area from the hotel on the beach. The entrance to the sea is gentle and sand and gravel.





In season, a lot of people rest on it, since everyone has access to it. Locals and tourists from other nearby hotels come here to sunbathe and swim.

Food in the complex

Ares Blue Hotel 4 * in the ranking among holidaymakers occupies the top positions, due to the affordable cost of tours, as well as high-quality food. The complex operates on an all-inclusive basis.

The spacious dining room has special racks on which a variety of dishes are displayed during each meal. There is always a lot of vegetables and fruits. And also in the menu throughout the day there is meat and fish in different variations.





The restaurant has high chairs for babies. Also, electric kettles are displayed throughout the day, in which you can pick up boiling water for personal needs at any time.

Drinks are also included in the tour price. During the main meal tourists can choose according to their taste. There is also a poolside bar, where until 18:00 various cocktails are served free of charge.

Ares Blue Hotel 4 *: reviews

On various Internet resources there are all kinds of comments about the operation of the complex. Basically, guests note that the hotel corresponds to a solid three stars, not four.

Tourists indicate that check-in often takes a very long time. Due to the average cost of tours (about 20 thousand rubles per person for 7-10 days) there are always a lot of vacationers and the process of placing in the rooms at the reception is significantly delayed.

The rooms, according to guest reviews, are simple furnishings, and not all of the rooms feature double beds. Therefore, couples have to shift two standard together.

Communications often do not work well in the shower, and water from pallets falls onto the floor during bathing. Maids are cleaned once every few days and often only after the guests contact the reception.

There are various opinions regarding nutrition. Tourists note that a variety of meat dishes are not enough for all guests. Side dishes are represented by a wide assortimenot. Stewed vegetables are also available. Assortment of fruits usually consists of 2-3 positions. There is always a lot of fresh greens on the racks.





All kinds of pastries are mainly served for dessert. From drinks there is always aromatic coffee and tea. Also, guests are offered wine that is poured from large bottles. Tourists often notice that such containers at the bottom are not completely clean. But there are no cases of poisoning in the reviews.

Tourists point out that the pool is quite deep and the water is clear. Concerning the amenities on the beach, the reviews are mixed. Many hotel guests complain that sunbeds come across often broken.

From customer comments it follows that in Ares Blue Hotel 4 * service is noticeably lame. Guests should bring towels with them or buy at local stores. The bedding is not perfect cleanliness, and the rooms are cleaned only after contacting the reception.

Tourists note that the windows and balconies offer beautiful views of the mountains. But they consider loud music that comes from the nearest disco to be a drawback. Objectively speaking, at 22:30 it ends and the rooms become quiet.

Tourists note that in the spa for $ 20 you can get quality services. This price includes a visit to the Turkish bath, sauna, massage room and some skin care treatments.

In general, tourists have a positive impression of a hotel stay, because the cost of trips here is much lower than in other hotels located in this area.
