Incekum Su Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Avsallar): description, service, reviews

Turkey is primarily associated with travelers with beach vacations, so tours to this country are incredibly popular in the summer. The most popular destination is the Antalya coast. The vast majority of the local hotels operate on an all-inclusive basis, have a large territory and well-developed infrastructure. One of the resorts favored by the Russians is Avsallar.



general information

It is located in southern Turkey, twenty kilometers from Alanya. Avsallar is a relatively small resort town with a population of twelve thousand. It is famous for its beautiful nature, a huge number of green spaces and a wide selection of entertainment: bars, restaurants, discos, shops, etc. are located everywhere. Tourists like beaches that are mostly sandy and pebbles are found only occasionally. Avsallar visitors have many spa services. Tourists can, for example, visit a hammam, massage parlor, take a course of thalassotherapy.

This resort town is one of the warmest corners of the Antalya coast, as it is located in a bay that protects from strong winds. When choosing a season for a trip, it should be borne in mind that the climate here is Mediterranean. Therefore, the weather in Avsallar in summer is characterized by high temperature. Therefore, late spring or early autumn are best suited for a beach holiday. Judging by the reviews, it is especially good here in late September - early October, when the heat was already relatively quiet, but the sea is still quite warm. At this time, the weather in Avsallar is best suited for a comfortable, relaxing stay.

Due to the variety of hotels offering relaxation for every wealth and taste, there are always many tourists here. Most Russians buy tours to four-star hotels, one of which is Incekum Su Hotel 4 * (Avsallar).



Description

The vast majority of Russians were satisfied with their stay at this hotel. Until 2013, it was called Aska Baran. Incekum Su 4 * hotel is built almost on the seashore. It offers residents a private beach with a sandy entry into the sea, two pools, a sauna, a hammam and a fitness room.

The hotel area is small but very cozy. Avsallar city center is only eight hundred meters away. Within walking distance are many shops, souvenir shops, cafes and bars. Tourists can stroll to the colorful bazaar, which sells fruits and delicious fish. The airport is a hundred kilometers away. Transfer lasts one and a half hours. You can drive to Alanya in twenty minutes, to Managavt in forty. About ninety percent of Russians were satisfied with their stay at Incekum Su Hotel 4 *.

Service

Like many other Avsallar hotels, this hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis. He has a fairly developed infrastructure, which is quite consistent with the assigned category. The hotel has a mini market, hairdresser, and a currency exchange office. Paid ironing, dry cleaning and laundry services are available. The administrative block also has a left-luggage office and a rental car.

The reception is open around the clock. Here, if necessary, you can order a paid transfer in any direction, call a taxi, Xerox documents, send a fax. Guests can check in at the shared lounge - a living room with a TV. If necessary, you can book a safe box, visit a medical office for a fee and book tours in an office located on the ground floor of the main building.



Housing stock

Incekum Su Hotel 4 * (Avsallar) is a two compact five-story building with an elevator. Its housing stock is one hundred twenty-three comfortable rooms, fully or partially overlooking the sea. They are equipped with the most necessary furniture.

Standard rooms can have three single beds, as well as two single and one double beds. They can simultaneously accommodate up to three people. The rooms of the category "comfort" are designed for four vacationers. There is also the option of Comfort Two-Bedroom Double Room with one double bed, designed for two residents.

Room filling is standard: guests can use the built-in safe (for three dollars a day), watch satellite channels on TV, including one Russian, use a paid minibar, air conditioning. All rooms have marble flooring. There is also a wardrobe, luggage rack, table and chairs.

All rooms have small balconies. The vast majority of them go directly or partially to the sea, and the rest - to the surroundings. Furnished balconies: plastic furniture and dryers are installed on them.

Bathrooms in the rooms are combined. They have showers with clear glass, a hairdryer and a minimum set of accessories. Towels are changed every day. Rooms are cleaned daily.

In the housing stock of this hotel there are two rooms for residents who move in a wheelchair. Pets should be arranged in advance. Please note that this service is paid.



Nutrition

Like the vast majority of Turkish hotels, Incekum Su Hotel 4 * also offers its guests all-inclusive service, which includes a breakfast buffet, as well as lunch and dinner. Residents from ten in the morning until midnight can receive local alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks at the bar. Here they are offered sandwiches and snacks. Imported alcohol, as well as freshly squeezed juices or Turkish coffee, can only be obtained at an additional cost. Three meals a day "all inclusive" is provided in the main restaurant on schedule. Hours of his work can be seen on the stand or on the memo that the receptionist gives to all residents.

Guests can enjoy Oriental cuisine in the pre-ordered restaurant on the outdoor terrace. Guests are also invited to relax in the lobby bar enjoying alcoholic drinks.

Prices

The cost of standard double rooms in October starts at four hundred and fifty dollars for two in six days. This price includes three meals a day, all-inclusive service. Airfare and transfer must be paid separately.

The cost of comfort rooms for four in October with all-inclusive accommodation for six nights starts at thirty-nine thousand rubles without a ticket. Depending on the season, the price changes.

Rules of residence

Check in at Incekum Su Hotel 4 * is scheduled for two in the afternoon, and check out at twelve. Allowed to live with children of any age. When placing in a room with parents on existing beds, one child under six years old does not have to pay. For babies under the age of two, a cot is provided free of charge.

Additional services that guests used during their entire stay are not automatically included in the total price: they are paid separately directly at the time of their provision.

Free internet is available only in public areas. For connecting to Wi-Fi in the rooms you need to pay five dollars a day.

Entertainment

The hotel has two swimming pools - outdoor and indoor. Around him you can always freely find free sunbeds and umbrellas. There is also a bar. All-inclusive residents can receive free juice from packages, local beer, etc. during the day.
