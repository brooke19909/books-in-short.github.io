Regarding the future infrastructure in the residential complex "Hometown" (October Field), the reviews of residents differ. Construction progress

The developer promises at the beginning of 2018 - until March - to hand over all the lines of the residential complex "Hometown" (October Field). The construction features here are such that at the initial stage, builders dug one large common foundation pit for all buildings at once, in order to begin their construction at the same time. At the moment, the construction of buildings number 4, 3 and 2 has already been completed, here the internal work has begun. In building number 1 completion of the upper floors ends. The buildings of the complex are built using monolithic technology, which ensures maximum reliability and good thermal protection. Additionally, a layer of insulation is laid in the outer walls so that there is no heat loss. In the territory where the "Hometown" is being built, according to the plan, new communications will be laid with equipping them with high-tech equipment.
