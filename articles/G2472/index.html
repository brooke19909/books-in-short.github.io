How to find out the balance of another MTS subscriber: all possible ways

It is not possible to list all situations where it may be necessary to find out the balance status of another subscriber's number. Nowadays, when it is impossible to do without communication, it is important to be able to check the balance of relatives and friends in order to replenish the account on time and not leave them without communication at the right time. How to find out the balance of another MTS subscriber? How can I get such information and under what conditions?





Information Options

There are several options for obtaining information about the status of the account on MTS: find out the balance of another subscriber via the Internet or through a request on your mobile phone. The first option is perfect if you need to monitor the status of the account on the number of a loved one (spouse, child, mother, etc.). After all, along with the ability to view information, you will receive the right to dispose of the number (the services that are activated on it, receive call details, etc.).

How to find out the balance of another MTS subscriber via the Internet?

In order to be able to view information about the balance status of another number via the Internet, you must assign a unique password for authorization in your personal web account for the subscriber whose balance will need to be checked. You can do this if you have at hand a mobile device with this SIM card. You can assign a password once. In the future, you can go to your personal account using only this combination. Of course, not every person will be ready to provide a similar opportunity to another. After all, in your personal account there are the necessary tools for obtaining any information about the number, including about calls made, etc.
