Personal appeal to the office

In order to easily extend the CTP insurance policy at VSK, when you first visit the company‚Äôs office, the insurer will be required to fill out an application form. This is the basic requirement for motor third party liability insurance. The document is being developed by the Union of Auto Insurers and has a single form throughout Russia. It must indicate:
