How does a bread machine work?

When a person sees a bread machine, he does not think about what principle this technique works on. Sometimes, some people think that the mechanism is quite complicated, because it knows how to mix the ingredients, distribute them correctly. However, in reality, everything is much easier. Let's consider this question in more detail.

In the main container, which is a baking dish, there is a shaft. Where it is fixed, there is a scapula kneading the dough. The mold itself is coated with non-stick coating so that no product burns. It is in this capacity that you need to put everything in the right proportions, and most importantly, in the correct sequence.

After that, the form is placed in a baking chamber. The Panasonic SD-257 bread machine is equipped with a drive that rotates the blade and a special heating element. If you need to cook baked goods with additives, then pour them into the dispenser. It is located in the lid of the device. On the control panel, you need to select the desired program and then additives will intervene in the product evenly.

Baking process

Panasonic SD-257, parts of which can be easily purchased at any service center, prepares products in four different stages. Their duration depends on the selected program and the corresponding options. In fact, this factor is affected by the time taken to equalize the temperature, kneading, raising the dough and the baking process itself. The minimum period for which the product will be ready is two hours, the maximum is six. As a rule, French bread takes so long to prepare. Additional programs work much less - from 20 minutes (dough for dumplings) to 1.5 hours (jam).

A simple recipe for bread dough

In order to prepare a yeast-free dough for bread, it is necessary to sift the flour through a sieve, and then add the baking powder to it. Then sprinkle with sugar and salt. Then it should be mixed to obtain a homogeneous mass.

In an additional container you need to combine butter, milk and beaten eggs. Mix the mixture so that the resulting solution becomes homogeneous.

It remains to combine the two available masses into one dough. Then it is necessary to place it in the form of the device and put on baking.
