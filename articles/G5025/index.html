Hotel Safira Palms 4 * (Tunisia): tourist reviews, description, rooms and features

Hotel Safira Palms 4 * (Tunisia), reviews of which have become the material for writing this article, are in fairly good standing with vacationers, including from Russia. This is a large suburban resort complex, whose buildings are located directly on the seashore. It was built in two thousand and seven. Therefore, the hotel is equipped very modernly, and its concept takes into account all the requirements for the leisure of tourists of the twenty-first century. Until two thousand and thirteen, the hotel belonged to the Iberostar chain. But do not think that with the change of ownership, the level of services in this tourist complex has decreased.
