Hotel Mayor La Grotta Verde Grand Resort 4 * (Greece, Corfu): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Hotel Mayor La Grotta Verde Grand Resort 4 * for many reasons is ideal for spending your holidays in it. Firstly, it is in a good place. Secondly, it serves guests according to their favorite all-inclusive system. Thirdly, despite this, there are fairly reasonable prices. Enumerate its advantages can be a long time. However, at the most important points, attention should be paid.





Location

Mayor La Grotta Verde Grand Resort 4 * is located on the island of Corfu - the northernmost in Greece. There is such a place as Agios Gordis. This resort town is located in the western part of Corfu. Its distinguishing feature is a long wide beach strip with a superbly developed infrastructure. There are countless taverns, bars, cafes and hotels. Here Mayor La Grotta Verde Grand Resort 4 * and is located on the very shore. To the sea - just a stone's throw.

Nearby is the capital of the island. And in about 15 minutes you can drive to the international airport. Nearby attractions include the Achillion Palace, the Byzantine Museum, the Church of Panagia Vlahernon and many other interesting places. All of them can be reached in 10-15 minutes. There is also a bus stop just 400 meters away.

By the way, many people are interested in seeing the capital of Greece - Athens. It is worth knowing that from Corfu airport planes fly there. If you use the Aegean Airline, you can buy a ticket somewhere for 20 euros. The journey takes only an hour.





Service and Facilities

In Mayor La Grotta Verde Grand Resort 4 * there is everything that guests may need. Starting from the parking lot and ending with Wi-Fi (everything is free, of course).

The hotel has a luggage storage room, currency exchange, 24-hour reception. There is a laundry room, an office with a photocopy and fax machine, a mini market and several shops, car rental, a souvenir stall and even a beauty salon with a hairdresser. Room service is also available.

The staff speaks English, French and Greek. Workers do not speak Russian, so it is better to take a phrase book with you on vacation. But the staff is friendly - they will not refuse advice and help.

Entertainment

When reviewing the Mayor La Grotta Verde Grand Resort in Corfu, one cannot but notice how to have fun at this hotel. The hotel has a swimming pool and sun terrace. There is a good gym with a variety of fitness equipment and conditions for full exercise.

Here you can do aerobics, play billiards or tennis. If you like a more relaxing pastime, then you should go to the SPA center. There is a sauna, massage rooms, a jacuzzi and conditions for aqua aerobics and gymnastics. There is also a heated indoor pool.

In the evening, all guests can enjoy live music. There are no animated shows here, but such performances for art lovers perfectly replace them.





Bars and Restaurants

Mayor La Grotta Verde Grand Resort (Corfu Island, Greece) has several places where people can enjoy delicious food. As mentioned above, the hotel serves all-inclusive guests, so there is no need to deny yourself.

The Androniki Restaurant serves international and Mediterranean dishes. There is also a vegetarian menu. The Olive Tree Restaurant serves traditional cuisine. Kukutsi Sushi Bar, as you might guess by its name, serves Japanese dishes.

If you want to treat yourself to cocktails, drinks and alcohol, you should go to one of the bars. There are three of them. One is by the pool, called Lagoon. The second is the Calypso lobby. There is a large selection of aperitifs. And in the open-air Tunnel Bar, you can enjoy ice cream and drinks.

Guests on Nutrition

A couple of words are worth mentioning about the impressions made by bars and restaurants, according to people staying at Hotel Mayor La Grotta Verde Grand Resort. Many claim that everything is tasty and varied. Breakfast, as elsewhere, is standard - bacon, fried eggs, scrambled eggs, boiled eggs, sausages, natural yoghurts, cereal, fresh vegetables. For lunch and dinner, in addition to the main dishes, you can taste various fruits - melons, watermelons, grapefruits, kiwi, apricots, peaches, apples. And the desserts are also varied, delicious.

For lunch and dinner, various dishes of meat, poultry, rabbit, duck, fish, seafood are served. Guests are spoiled a little less with pork loin and grilled pork sausages.

What is more good than the main restaurant - themed evenings of Italian, Greek and other cuisines are held there.

Helpful information

Now it is worth noting some nuances regarding the hotel. It is intended for those people who want to spend a calm, quiet and peaceful holiday by the sea, enjoying high quality service and comfortable rooms. The hotel is really quiet, even in high season. Due to the fact that it is forbidden to stay with children. Persons over 16 years old are allowed to register as a guest. Therefore, mainly lonely travelers, couples in love and groups of friends come here.

Check-in starts at three in the afternoon. If you need to check in earlier due to an early flight, you should talk about it with the administration. Better in advance, before departure. When the hotel is not crowded and the apartments reserved by guests are free, they can be settled right away. But at the height of the season this is unlikely to succeed. Departure, by the way, is carried out before noon. On the day guests leave, drinks and meals are not provided.

By the way, since the reviews about Mayor La Grotta Verde Grand Resort are mostly good, many people, having read them, wish to go here. The hotel is popular - by the beginning of the season there are practically no free dates and rooms. Therefore, it is better to reserve the apartments in advance. You may even be able to get a discount.
