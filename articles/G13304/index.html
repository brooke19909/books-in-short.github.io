Hotel "Liner" (Tyumen): address and organization of work

The Liner Hotel is located near the terminal building at Oleg Antonov Street, 5. This hotel is conveniently accessible from both the city and the airport building. Just a few steps from the terminal - and you are already there.

From the city to the desired point of the hotel visitors can deliver a transfer, taxi, own transport. Getting on public transport is uncomfortable, but also possible. The Liner Hotel in Roshchino (Tyumen) is conveniently located not only in relation to the airport terminal, but also in the city itself.

Check-in is available from 14.00 daily. Departure - until 12.00 the next day. If you need a late eviction, you must pay extra time. Additional services are paid at the reception desk. At the hotel you can pay with any plastic card. On the ground floor and in the airport building there is an ATM network.

Services

The hotel provides comfortable accommodation for guests. The cafe on the ground floor serves breakfast, lunch and dinner, or just enjoy a cup of coffee. Hotel "Liner" (Tyumen) works for the convenience of customers, so there is a 24-hour snack bar near the reception.

A hairdresser offers guests all services for styling and cutting hair. Ladies and gentlemen can put themselves in order before the upcoming flight.

The hotel has a laundry room (for a fee), which provides a full range of clothing care services. Ironing facilities are also available upon request.

Near the hotel there is paid and free parking for cars. There is a safe at the reception where you can leave valuables and documents.

For city guests, the hotel offers the organization of excursions. Tourists will be shown the sights of the city and told about its history.

Description of floors

All five floors have classic halls. They have tables and leather chairs. The floor is tiled, the walls are painted with ordinary paint. On the floor are potted plants and decorative trees. Everything is very reminiscent of Soviet times. Here are coolers with cold and hot water. Hotel "Liner" (Tyumen), reviews of which will be given below, does everything possible to ensure that guests have a comfortable rest. That is why smoking is prohibited in the rooms and hotel building.

Number of rooms

Hotel "Liner" (Tyumen), the photo of which you can see in the article, offers rooms of different comforts: standard single, double and triple, as well as superior comfort. There are also large apartments for up to five people. Accordingly, their cost is the lowest. All rooms have their own bathroom, TV, fridge, necessary sleep equipment and furniture.

Single Room

The apartments are in classic style. Bright walls and floors are combined with emerald satin curtains. Near the bed on the floor lies a soft rug for the convenience of guests. Nearby on the pedestal is a TV and a table with a chair. The bathroom is combined and has a shower and washbasin. The cost of living is 2150 rubles per day.

Double Room

In apartments of this class can be two single beds or one large. There is also a standard set of furniture, a refrigerator and a TV. The room is spacious and simple, without frills and frills. The bathroom has everything you need for personal hygiene. Cost - 3220 rubles.

Triple room

Apartments for three people have the appropriate number of comfortable beds, as well as a minimum set of furniture. There is a TV, a fridge, a table and a carafe for water with glasses. Towels and cosmetics are in the bathroom. The cost of accommodation is 3840 rubles per day.

Superior Room

Superior apartments are a two-room suite. In one room there is a large bed with all the accessories for sleeping, and in the second - a leather sofa, TV, desk, refrigerator. A standard set of tools is available in the bathroom. Cost - 4530 rubles.

Attractions nearby

Many guests and tourists staying at a hotel near the airport want to see the sights of the city. Seven kilometers from the airport is the beautiful Zatyumensky park. Here you can relax from the noise of the aircraft and the hustle and bustle. Walking through the streets and enjoying the fresh air, tourists can feel the sincerity of this city. On the way, you may meet small shops with souvenirs that can be brought as a souvenir to your hometown.

A little further is Tyumen embankment, which all tourists seek. Ten kilometers from the hotel is the Tyumen Monastery, known throughout the country. Hotel "Liner" (Tyumen, Oleg Antonov St., 5) is located in close proximity to the city's memorable places. You can get here by taxi in 10-15 minutes.

Also, many guests rush to Tsvetnoy Boulevard and to Alexander Park, which are located in the city center. Many people want to visit the local market. Finding it is very simple - any resident of the city will tell you the way. You can order a taxi or transfer from the hotel.

There are also theaters, museums and thematic exhibitions in the city. The city center is 21 km from the airport, and the main attractions are somewhat closer. Hotel Liner (Tyumen) itself is located 300 meters from the airport terminal.
