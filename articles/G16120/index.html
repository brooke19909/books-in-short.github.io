The best tourist camps. Rest: Peresyp, Kirillovka

Rest on the Sea of ​​Azov has always been considered budgetary. Every summer families with children and lovers of silence and unity with nature flocked here. The shallow, quickly warming sea, the absence of nearby megacities and industrial enterprises, the steppe air saturated with iodine - what more could my soul wish for? Far inland the washed-up sand spits advanced. On the one hand is the sea, on the other is a freshwater estuary ... Here, excellent fishing. But the best that tourist camps give visitors is relaxation. Pounding is one of such braids. It starts from the center of the balneological resort Kirillovka (Zaporizhzhya region, Ukraine). About recreation, housing and prices on the spit Peresyp read in this article.





Where is Kirillovka located

The village is located in the very south of Zaporizhzhya region, quite far from the gas-polluted industrial metropolis. This is a seaside village. Two sand spits adjoin to it : Fedotova and Peresyp. The first separates the Sea of ​​Azov from the Utlyuk estuary. Spit Fedotova began to be built up recently, but very intensively. Now there is a very developed recreation infrastructure. The rash stretches over a nine-kilometer chain and separates the sea from the Milk Estuary. Wellness camps and tourist camps lined both sides of the two streamers. Rest The rash gives a quiet, peaceful. For noise, you should go to Fedotov’s scythe. It is separated from the mainland by the deserted island of Stepok. The resort village of Kirillovka from the city of Melitopol is 65 km away. The nearest railway station, Akimovka, is 42 km away.





Tourist bases - rest. Peresyp, Kirillovka

In the seventies of the last century, when Fedotov’s spit was resting only as a “savage” in tents, pioneer camps, boarding houses and sanatoriums began to be built with might and main in the tents. These monuments of the past still rise on the shore. Some of them underwent a complete reconstruction and turned into modern hotels and tourist centers.

Rest Peresyp offers a budget than on the spit Fedotova, but more expensive than in the center of the village of Kirillovka. You can also reduce the cost of your stay by living on the so-called second line. These are bases and hotels near the water, but fresh - Milk estuary. You will have to walk about a hundred meters to the sea, which, you see, is not so much. In some boarding houses or recreation centers in Peresyp there is a cafe with loud music, but they are incomparably smaller than on Fedotov’s spit. This area is for wildlife lovers. A narrow strait separates Peresyp from another spit - Stepanovskaya, which is a wildlife sanctuary.





Tourist infrastructure

All noisy entertainment venues are located either in the center of Kirillovka or on Fedotova Spit. In the resort village you can visit the water park "Treasure Island". The northeastern tip of Kirillovka is left at the mercy of lovers of a relaxing holiday. At the very beginning of the Peresyp nightclubs Rio and Galaxy, as well as an amusement park, work. Further down the scythe nothing breaks the silence. Only the lapping of the surf and the cries of seagulls ... Nevertheless, the summer inhabitants of the braid do not feel like Robinsons, isolated from any civilization. There are also upscale hotels that meet the standard of three stars.

Recreation facilities on the spit Peresyp amaze with the variety of services offered. They can be either comfortable apartments or shabby shadows of the Soviet past. How to get to them? From the center of the village to the end of the spit in the summer, minibuses ply every quarter of an hour.

The recreation center in Kirillovka - "Peresyp"

This tourist and health complex is located in a secluded part of the spit. Two-story modern buildings accommodate 88 rooms that meet European standards. Guest rooms are divided into the following categories: “Family”, “Studio”, “Suite”, Lux + and “Apartments”. This recreation center has not only its own sandy beach, but also a swimming pool with designated areas for children. Animators entertain guests all day, and chefs spoil guests with delicious dishes three times a day. Rescuers are on duty at the beach. There is a guarded parking lot, exercise machines, a playground, a first-aid post, a computer club with Internet access. For a better stay, excursions are organized. All beach ammunition is provided to guests absolutely free of charge. You can rent a catamaran. There is a cafe on the beach with drinks and snacks.





"Seaside Galatea"

This is another very popular recreation center in Kirillovka. Kosa Peresyp in the place where this cultural and health center is located (4 km from the center of the village) is noticeably narrowing. The base consists of eight buildings and cottages, standing on a well-groomed shady territory. The numbers here, as in Peresyp, are divided into categories. This is “Economy” (shower on the floor), “Standard”, “Superior” (with air conditioning), “Suite” (two-room). Two dining rooms of the base offer three meals a day according to the "buffet" system. Umbrellas and sunbeds are available for hire on the private beach.
