Mission number 1. Get to the battery

The first step will help us partially answer the question of how to disassemble the iPhone-4.

  1. From the bottom end of the gadget we unscrew two fixing apple-screws.
  2. A shift up - and the back cover is removed.
  3. At the bottom of the smartphone on the right, near the battery, we unscrew the fixing screw of the protective cover of the battery connector.
  4. Gently pry the terminal and, pulling the liner, we withdraw our battery.

Mission number 2. Turn off the optical equipment

  1. First of all, you need to remove the SIM tray - a clip to help you.
  2. We unscrew the two horizontally located bolts above the contact pad of the battery connector and, carefully prying off with a spatula, disconnect it from the system board.
  3. Move aside (to the right) the detached train. Under it, at the bottom, near the side of the hull, you will see a screw - twist it.
  4. The upper part, near the camera, is covered by a plate, which is fixed with 5 bolts. Unscrew everything and then dismantle the shield.
  5. Finally, our goal is the camera: disconnect the connector and carefully remove the optical device.

Mission No. 3. Command Center

The most important task: to dismantle the system board.   2. Now you have to unscrew the fixing screws on the system board. In total, the fourth iPhone device has 4 pieces, and the follower of the S-series motherboard is fixed with 6 screws.
  3. The location of the bolts: the upper part of the case, near the On / Off button - 1, another one nearby on the left, the middle of the system board and the left end edge are the other two.

Mission No. 4. Total Disarmament

So we came to the final part of the review-instructions, answering the question of how to disassemble iPhone-4, with clear recommendations.





  1. At the bottom of the smartphone, on the right, unscrew the screw that holds the polyphonic module. Dismantle it.
  2. Now you have to unscrew the 3 bolts at the top, at the audio jack, one of which is the internal-end.
  3. Remove all hinged elements of the device: speaker, coaxial cable, vibration motor, etc.
  4. At the bottom, unscrew the two bolts that secure the bottom cable.
  5. Release the microphone from the groove (lower right side of the case, inside).
  6. Disconnect the flex cable for the Home button.
  7. Remove the system connector.
  8. Unscrew all screws around the perimeter of the inside of the housing. It should be noted that the side bolts do not need to be completely removed - just loosen them.
  9. Carefully separate the chassis frame from the display module. And you should start from the bottom of the smartphone, since the top of the device is equipped with screen cables.
