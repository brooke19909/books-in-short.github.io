Tariff "My conversation", "Tele2": reviews, description

The actively developing network of the mobile operator Tele2 has developed the tariff My Talk for its economical subscribers. Thanks to him, it is possible, within a small budget, to continue to use all the necessary services: calls, SMS, Internet. But you have to be prepared for the fact that you will not be able to fully use the Internet. So the service package is not interesting to fans of social networks. But what will the reviews about the tariff “My Talk” from “Tele2” say?





What does the My Talk tariff offer?

The tariff "My conversation" from "Tele2" offers its users the following list of services:

  • monthly fee - 199 rubles. It is paid every month;
  • outgoing and incoming calls throughout Russia within the framework of the operator are absolutely free and have no restrictions;
  • outgoing calls to numbers of other operators within their area can be made free of charge within 200 minutes;
  • 50 free SMS per month to various numbers in the field of connection;
  • Internet services: 2 GB per month. At full use, an extension is automatically carried out (at the same time 50 rubles are charged) ;
  • outgoing calls within the country to numbers of other operators amount to 1.95 rubles per minute;
  • outgoing calls in the region to the numbers of different operators will amount to 1.95 rubles (with 200 free minutes spent);
  • outgoing calls to the CIS countries - 30 rubles per minute, to Europe and the Baltic states - 49 rubles, to other countries - 69 rubles per minute;
  • when dialing to satellite numbers, the cost will be 240 rubles per minute;
  • SMS cost - 1.95 rubles in Russia (as soon as the free amount is used up);
  • the cost of 1 MMS is 6.50 rubles;
  • SMS abroad will cost 5.5 rubles.

Fans of "climbing" on the Internet can exchange free minutes for calls to gigabytes of the Internet.





Advantages

For 199 rubles, users of the Tele2 operator get a pleasant package of services that includes all the necessary options. Also, subscribers should know that:

  • Internet traffic is not consumed when using Zvooq and Tele2 TV smartphone applications. The Internet for these applications is unlimited in the system;
  • if within a month the provided free minutes of calls and SMS, Internet are not used up, then their balance is transferred to the next month.

As the reviews about the “My Talk” tariff from “Tele2” say, these additions come in handy and very helpful.

disadvantages

The presented package of services from “Tele2” also revealed a number of shortcomings that can significantly “hit” the wallet:

  • the cost of calls abroad is high;
  • the cost of a subscription fee for different regions may vary;
  • the amount of traffic provided is small, in fact, as is the number of free minutes for SMS and calls;
  • The tariff plan is not active for residents of the Crimea.




Subscription fee in various regions

In various cities and surrounding regions of Russia, the cost of a monthly subscription fee may differ:

  • Moscow, Vladimir, Tula, Pskov, Rostov, Chelyabinsk - 199 rubles;
  • St. Petersburg - 240;
  • Novosibirsk, Saratov, Bryansk - 270;
  • Perm, Nizhny Novgorod, Irkutsk - 190;
  • In Samara - 290.

You can find out about the cost of services in your region on the official website of Tele2.

How can I connect the tariff?

Connect the tariff “My conversation” from “Tele2”, as the main package of services, can both new subscribers of the mobile operator, and existing. However, the latter can either purchase a new mobile number with a tariff, or change the current service package.

To connect the tariff, you can use one of the convenient methods. The whole procedure takes no more than 3 minutes.

  1. Make a call to number 630.
  2. Visit your account on the official website of Tele2 and select a service there.
  3. On the mobile keyboard, enter a certain combination of numbers and symbols: * 630 * 2 # and press the "Call" button.




Switching from a different tariff to My Talk

Owners of other mobile service packages are wondering: “How do I switch to the My Talk tariff from Tele2? But first, it’s worth noting that changing the old tariff to My Talk is absolutely free.

Upon transition, a full subscription fee of 199 rubles is charged. Further, monthly debiting will take place if there is the necessary amount on the balance of the phone.

Go to the new tariff "My conversation" in the same ways that you connect. As the reviews about the My Talk tariff from Tele2 show, the procedure is really quick.

How to disable the tariff?

If necessary or unwilling to continue to use this service package, it can be disabled without any problems. But this is permissible only under the condition that the service package will be changed to some other tariff from the operator.

To carry out the shutdown procedure, just do the following:

  1. Call 630 and personally contact the operator, who will make the tariff change.
  2. Type a specific combination of characters on the phone keypad. The code will be: * 630 * N #, where N can be the following: "1" for "My Tele2", "2" for "My online", "3" for "My online plus".
  3. Visit the "Personal Account" on the official website of the operator and make a change there.

Immediately after completing one of the procedures, My Talk will be automatically turned off.

Reviews on the tariff "Tele2" - "My conversation"

According to most users who switched to this package of services, it is convenient for making calls, but it is absolutely useless for visiting the Internet. The thing is that 2 GB is not enough to get all the necessary information from the Global Network.

Another discontent of people is that the tariff is expensive, although the company itself claims the opposite.

Positive reviews indicate a convenient addition - the transition of unspent minutes to the next month.

From the statements of subscribers, we can conclude that the tariff is suitable only for those who use their mobile gadget specifically for its intended purpose - for calls. To communicate on the Internet or to obtain information from it, this tariff is completely inconvenient, because the traffic ends quickly, and the cost for an additional connection is high.





PS

The description of the “My Talk” tariff from “Tele2” suggests that it is more suitable for business people who solve business issues via mobile communications. It will be convenient for them to use elderly people who do without the Internet.

For young people, the conditions of this tariff are disadvantageous, since they do not allow free use of social networks and visit various sites.
