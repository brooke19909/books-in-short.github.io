Seifert 4 * (Prague): reviews

In their reviews, guests at Seifert note that it is located in a very convenient location. The hotel rooms are small, but larger housing, according to the authors of the reviews, is not necessary, because you only come to sleep here. The rooms have an electric kettle, you can make tea yourself. The employees in the rooms are cleaned, according to the tenants. Internet in the room works pretty well.





The advantages of the hotel include first location: a walking distance to the center can be reached in 20 minutes. Many also liked the location because there were wonderful places where you can eat.

In the hotel rooms, guests are given, according to the residents, an unusually soft bed, they serve excellent breakfasts, and towels are changed daily. The rooms, in the opinion of the authors of the reviews, are quite comfortable.

In addition, many tourists note the unusual friendliness and unobtrusiveness of the staff. Seifert is called a wonderful clean hotel with very helpful, friendly staff, clean, comfortable rooms and a good location: within walking distance to the joys of some tourists and the abundance of excellent pubs.

Breakfast provided in the hotel, vacationers called "very decent." Moreover, the buffet is open during the holidays.

Tourists living in the hotel note that the services provided combine: affordable price with pretty good quality, helpful professional staff and good, unobtrusive service.

Negative reviews

Among the tourists who left their reviews about the Seifert Hotel, there are those who noted the shortcomings. So, some guests point to the noise in the rooms, due to good audibility: if someone is talking in the hallway, those in the room have the feeling that the conversation is directly under the door. It is also mentioned that bed towels and bedding are not white enough. Someone notes the unpleasant smell present in the room, the presence of ants. Some people think that breakfasts are still too modest, for people who like to eat, such a breakfast is not suitable.

Price and quality, according to some guests, at the Seifert Hotel, do not correspond to each other. The star rating of the hotel is such reviewers are willing to question. The location of the hotel they also called not very successful. Fortunately, Prague is small in size, and it‚Äôs easy to reach the main attractions on foot.
