Solnechny Holiday House (Abkhazia) welcomes guests

Speaking about holidays in Abkhazia and about places of rest, it is worth paying attention to the country itself, its population, location and the main resort areas. Holidays in Abkhazia differ from standard holidays on the coast of distant countries.

Abkhazia

The tiny smiling Abkhazia shone again in the arena of the tourist sky. The status of the Black Sea resort country is gradually returning to it. Cleanliness and level of service once again attract tourists. There are no previous ambitions, no pessimism, there is only a single-minded look into the future. As before, the river cakes toasts at the tables, the pines, pines and cypresses are also fragrant, and, as in former times, the Abkhazian wine is magnificent. Some omissions are compensated by the cheapness of recreation, improvements in the developing infrastructure, hospitality of the local population and picturesque places of recreation.



The main resorts of Abkhazia are the capital of the country Sukhum, Gagra, Pitsunda, New Athos. These cities were previously popular among tourists.

First of all, a favorable climate attracts. In the summer beach season from May to October, the air temperature rarely drops below +24 degrees, and the water temperature, even in October, is rarely below +20 degrees. In the hot months off the coast, sea water warms up to 28 degrees. A gentle sand and pebble beaches are ideal.

Going on vacation to Abkhazia, do not think about choosing a currency. Everywhere throughout the republic, the Russian ruble is used in calculations. This simplifies a lot and solves frequently arising economic issues among tourists abroad.

Gagra

The most popular holiday destination in the resort of Gagra is the Solnechny holiday home. Its convenient location, low cost of trips, good service, and the availability of many attractions have made "Sunny" a favorite vacation spot for local people and tourists from all over the world. Vouchers to the Solnechny holiday home (Abkhazia) were once in short supply, but now everything is simpler. Using early booking, you can freely purchase a tour, buy treatment. There was an opportunity to purchase permits in installments by bank transfer and for cash.



Fairytale Gagra has become an affordable resort, popular among young people, couples and families with children. The presence of developed infrastructure, plenty of entertainment, a good coast and the proximity of various attractions make the rest of the resort full and memorable.

Among the attractions for tourists:

  • Jet skis.
  • Nice water park.
  • Lots of sports fields.
  • Diving center.
  • Tennis courts.
  • Lots of cafes and restaurants.
  • Excursion bureaus.

Many architectural and historical sights are the wealth of Gagra. For example, the most famous restaurant in the district is Gagripshu, which is over 100 years old. A visit to this restaurant will be a celebration for anyone.

Local attractions include:

  • Joekwara is a fortress built by the Romans in the 4th century AD.
  • Gagra temple of limestone, where the historical museum of Abkhazian weapons is located.
  • The Art Nouveau Prince Oldenburg Castle, built in 1902.
  • Gagra Colonnade.
  • Lake Ritsa, located in the mountains, is unique in its flora and fauna.
  • Cottage I. Stalin on the shores of Lake Ritsa.
  • Monkey nursery, which sheltered more than 300 individuals from around the world.
  • Gagra Central Park.
  • The cable car connecting the park and the castle of the Prince of Oldenburg.
  • The ancient restaurant "Gagripsh", where the great and famous poets, politicians, writers, artists of different times dined.
  • Temple in the village of Tsandrishl, built in the 6th century.
  • Mount Mamzyshha with a viewing platform at an altitude of 2 kilometers above sea level.

"Solar"

All these wonderful places will be available to vacationers in the resort of Gagra. Solnechny Holiday House is an ideal place for such a vacation.

This guesthouse is located in a pine forest near the resort in the village of Cold River. The calling card of the prosperous resorts of the Republic of Abkhazia, the boarding house "Sunny" has long been. Large territory, comfortable rooms, good service, picturesque sea views, clear sea water, a beautiful park, clean air, comfortable rest - these are just some of the attractive properties of this vacation spot.



Solnechny Holiday House (Abkhazia) is a huge and spacious guesthouse for 400-450 beds, located on the slope of the Gagra Range. The territory of this resting place is spread over 105 hectares. The proximity of the sea is only one of the fundamental factors in attracting tourists. The uniqueness of the rest and the possibility of conducting wellness treatment fundamentally affect the preferences in choosing a vacation spot for vacationers. The air in this place is always filled with volatile tropical plants and thin coolness. The mountain-valley air currents and the swift mountain river Bagherepsta crossing the territory of the boarding house give coolness to the heat and heat of summer afternoon. No wonder Stalin himself once chose a nearby place for his country residence.

Rooms

All rooms have a sea view. Number base - this is several types of rooms, designed to stay in them 1, 2, 3 people. There are 1-room and 2-room rooms:

  • 1-room with bathroom and shower in the block, with a TV in the lobby. The block consists of 2 such numbers. The sizes of one-room places of settlement are different. Each such room has single or double beds, a table, a chair, bedside tables. Many rooms have a balcony.
  • 2-room rooms, similar in configuration to one-room, with the possibility of settling 3 people.
  • 2- and 3-room suites. Designed to settle in them 2-3 people in conditions of improved comfort. These rooms have: bathroom and toilet, upholstered furniture, beds with orthopedic mattresses, refrigerator, TV, kitchenette, telephone. There is a spacious balcony. In 3-room rooms, one room is designed for a comfortable stay for children.

Room service is available regularly. Cold water is available constantly. Hot water is supplied on schedule.



For those who prefer an economical vacation, without special claims to comfort, there are houses with conveniences on the guesthouse territory.

Nutrition

Meals in a boarding house are a buffet. Entertainment and additional services

The complex "Solnechny" has a sports complex and an indoor pool with sea water. Using these services is included in the price of the tour. In the evenings, entertaining shows are held, discos are organized on the dance floor.

Solnechny Holiday House (Abkhazia) is located 300 meters from its own beach, where a serpentine path and steps lead. However, you can take advantage of a special elevator connecting the plateau of the boarding house and the coast.
