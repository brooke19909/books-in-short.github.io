The most beautiful architectural monument, named after Alexander 3, is a bridge in Paris

Paris is famous for its beautiful old bridges across the River Seine. Each of them has its own history, unique architecture and design. One of them is named after the Russian emperor Alexander 3. The bridge in Paris, built according to the plan of Russian architects, is rightfully considered the most elegant in the French capital. Looking at him, you are mentally transported to past centuries, in those distant times when France and Russia were monarchies, and their peoples highly esteemed their emperors. Those who know the history of this architectural creation recognize that Alexander 3 bridge is a small piece of Russian land in elegant France. It was built by Nicholas II as a symbol of the Franco-Russian Union and named after the father of this emperor.





Bridge of Alexander III: the story of creation

In 1891, during the reign of the Russian Emperor Alexander the Third , a Franco-Russian alliance was concluded between France and Russia. By order of Nicholas II, in the very heart of Paris, 9 years after this momentous event, a bridge was erected over the River Seine. It was named after the crowned father of the current Russian emperor. The construction of the bridge began in 1896. It was at this time that the first stone was laid in the foundation of this structure. At its grand opening was attended by the Ambassador of the Russian Empire in France L.P. Urusova, as well as the current president of the French Republic, Felix Faure and other political and public figures of this European country.



Description and features of architecture

Built in honor of Alexander 3, the bridge in Paris is single-arch. It is 160 meters long, while the height of its arch is six meters. For the end of the century before last, a bridge raised to such a height was a complete innovation, and many considered this to be more than a risky idea for the architect. However, the authors of the project took this step and raised the bridge to a height of 6 meters for a reason. This was done so that the construction of the structure does not obscure the beautiful panorama of the Champs Elysees from the observers. After all, the Alexander 3 bridge is thrown over the Seine and is located on a stretch of river in the area between the Invalides and the Champs Elysees. Therefore, it is one of the most visited places in the French capital.



Decoration and style

Thanks to its beautiful Boz-ar decorative decoration, this bridge in Paris is recognized as the most elegant and elegant. The decoration details that adorn the bridge over the entire length of 160 meters are mythical and biblical characters: angels and pegasuses frozen in flight, tender nymphs emerging from the abyss of water, as well as a huge number of lanterns. At the entrance to the bridge from both ends, the first thing you can see is huge seventeen-meter columns with giant lanterns that give this building a special solemnity. Above them hover bronze figures, which are symbols of agriculture. In addition, the bridge named after Alexander 3 in Paris is crowned with symbols of science and industry, art and battles - all that unites the two great nations. In the center of the arches there are coats of arms of friendly powers - Russia and France. They are supported by the nymphs of the Seine and Neva. All these architectural compositions are cast from copper alloy by the French sculptor Jean Recipo and are a symbol of friendship between the two peoples. By the way, there is a similar bridge in St. Petersburg. Its architecture and decor is very similar to that of Paris. From the very first day of its foundation, it is still called the Trinity Bridge. It was built by the French almost at the same time as Aleksandrovsky, again as a symbol of friendship and cooperation between the two states.



Beliefs related to Pont Alexandre III

In 1975, the bridge named after Alexander 3 in Paris was recognized by the French government as a historical and architectural monument, which should be protected by the state. It fits so beautifully into the cityscape of Paris that it has become his hallmark. Moreover, it is not only a way of crossing from one bank of the Seine to another, but also a place with which many associate the fulfillment of their cherished desires. So, according to legend, if you make a wish, getting up late in the evening in the middle of the bridge, it will certainly come true. And couples in love sacredly believe that if they, holding hands, walk across the bridge past the bronze figures of mythical characters, then their love will be strong and eternal. And bachelors and single women think that this magical bridge, recognized for beauty as the most elegant bridge of Paris, will bring them good luck in finding the second half and love.
