Troitskoye beach - affordable vacation in the suburbs

Clean air, beautiful nature and calm atmosphere - it seems that there is no such corner in the Moscow region. But this is an erroneous opinion. Literally 10 km from MKAD is Troitskoye beach.

general characteristics

Entrance to the beach area is free. If you want to call in a car, you will have to pay 200 rubles. The bathing area on Troitskoye beach is sandy, and the surrounding area is lawn. There are locker rooms and a free toilet, umbrellas that allow you to hide from the sun.

For children, a bathing area is specially equipped, so parents will not need to worry that the child will drown or swallow water. There is a medical center and a rescue station in sight.

Near the coastline are gazebos with barbecue facilities. You can simply relax in them or prepare a barbecue, or order in a cafe.

Zoo

Troitskoye Beach is famous not only for its sandy beaches, but also for its mini-zoo. All these animals once suffered from the cruelty of people, and now are under the watchful eye of employees of the recreation center. Everyone can visit the zoo for free. Park animals:

Beach activities

Naturally, you can just lie on a deck chair and swim. And you can rent a catamaran or jet ski, ride a pleasure boat or a yacht, on a wakeboard, water skiing. In the summer season, the beach hosts many interesting events for vacationers of any age.

If you get tired of lying on the beach, you can play football or volleyball.

Entertainment for every taste

On the Troitskoye beach, the recreation center of the same name operates, which has its own equestrian center. Here you can ride children on a pony, take part in a horseback riding master class or just ride a chaise. And if you came to rest in the winter, then for everyone, they organize sleigh rides with the Russian troika. Even if you are afraid of horses, then you can just have a photo shoot with them.

And for fans of the air element there is the opportunity to carry out parasailing, that is, parachuting a boat. There is also a helicopter for vacationers, where you can inspect the local beauties.

The reservoir is rich in fish, here you can catch bream, roach, perch and ruff. If you are very lucky, then you will be caught by a buster, pike or even zander.

Where to settle

On the shore of the Klyazma reservoir (the Troitskoye beach is located nearby), the recreation center of the same name is located. The total occupied area is 11.5 hectares. The territory is fully landscaped, there is a developed infrastructure.

The hotel complex of the recreation center includes 3 hotels and 11 cottages. The most interesting building is the Bagration hotel-ship. This is a real ship moored to the shore and installed on concrete piles. Here the Economy rooms are located on two decks.

On site there is a restaurant and cafe, which is located on a swan pond. There is also a bar, where you can enjoy panoramic views of the reservoir.
