Why is Roshen?

Vinnitsa, the Roshen fountain became famous within a few months. In 2011 alone, about a million people visited the Ukrainian city. The musical fountain is striking in its scale and beauty. But not everyone knows why he got such a name. Roshen Fountain was named after its sponsor. From the fund of Petro Poroshenko sponsored construction work, cleaning the channel of the Southern Bug River and the restoration of 700 meters of the embankment. The work was carried out for a year and a half. During this time, about 70 million hryvnias were allocated from the fund of the current President of Ukraine. It is not surprising that the embankment and fountain were subsequently named after their creator.



Roshen discovery

The fountain in Vinnitsa first surprised residents of the city on September 4, 2011. On this day, its official opening took place. More than 70 thousand people gathered on the embankment wishing to see the water show. Musical and light fountain amazed everyone. The water show began with a concert on the specially equipped stage of the promenade. After the audience went out to Petro Poroshenko. He solemnly stepped to the control panel to launch the dancing fountains. And a few minutes later the audience saw a gorgeous water sight. Vinnitsa Fountain - the main tourist attraction of Ukraine





Over the past three years, the most popular tourist city is Vinnitsa. The Roshen fountain, located on the promenade of the same name, is the main tourist attraction in Ukraine, which thousands of tourists from all over the world come to see. There are only a few such large-scale projects in the world. Vinnytsia dancing fountain "Roshen" many compare with similar in the United Arab Emirates (Dubai). However, Dubai's Roshen design is inferior in size. In addition, with the advent of frost, the musical fountain "falls into hibernation", and in the spring it again begins to delight people. In the summer season, Roshen works day and night. But it is in the evening that the musical and light show with special effects begins, which lasts 15 minutes. In the afternoon, the fountain is also magnificent: it rises majestically against the backdrop of sparkling river water in the sunlight. Experts say that there are no similar analogues in any European country. Vinnitsa fountain is included in the TOP-10 of the most spectacular places in the world.

Water light show

Vinnytsia, Roshen fountain are the real pride of Ukraine. A water show is worth the time and money spent on the journey. Every year, the singing fountains are improved. This backlight with a laser provides an incredibly colorful and spectacular show. The fountain has a floating base. And he dances under both classical and contemporary musical works. Roshen sprays water upward, to the sides, spirally, with a curtain. The creators of the fountain constantly come up with new special effects in order to expand the repertoire of a unique hydraulic engineering structure.

How to get there?





Vinnitsa Fountain regularly runs public transport. First, tourists need to come to the city of Vinnitsa itself. From there, the fountain can be reached in five ways.

  1. Trams Nos. 1, 4, 6 to the stop. "Kotsyubinsky Museum".
  2. Trolleybuses No. 5, 6, 11 to the stop. "Kotsyubinsky Museum".
  3. From the Central Bus Station can be reached on foot (5 minutes).
  4. From the Western bus station you can take tram number 4, which goes to the stop. "Kotsyubinsky Museum".
  5. From the Eastern bus station there are trolleybuses No. 1, 2, 10, 12 to the stop. "Nansen Street."

Future plans

Roshen embankment is a popular holiday destination for both Ukrainians themselves and visiting tourists. The founder of the grand fountain Petro Poroshenko promises that his brainchild will be constantly improved and transformed. Improvement of the embankment and adjacent territories does not stop. In 2011, beautiful flowerbeds were created here, flowers and trees were planted. With the advent of spring, singing fountains are especially attractive. The photo taken during this period reflects all the beauty of the Roshen embankment. The new complex should be a worthy decoration not only of Vinnitsa, but of the whole Ukraine, which will attract even more tourists to the country.
