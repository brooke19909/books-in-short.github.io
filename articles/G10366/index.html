How to get to Kudinovo (Noginsky district)?

The village of Kudinovo of the Noginsky district is located 25 km from the capital. The village is located 19 km from the regional center, the city of Noginsk. The nearest large settlement is the city of Elektrougli, Moscow Region. It is not often possible to meet tourists here, however, Kudinovo is a popular place among lovers of country and fishing vacations.

History and description of the village

D. Kudinovo of the Noginsky district is one of the ancient villages of the Moscow region. There is evidence that this settlement existed in 1742. There was a lot of clay, and serfs from neighboring villages mined this mineral in the village.

In later times, industrial production developed in Kudinovo. In 1900, the businessman Zhokhovs organized a brick factory here. He worked until 1914, and then temporarily ceased operations in connection with the First World War. In the 1920s, the work of the plant resumed. This enterprise exists to this day, now it is called Kudinovsky Combine OJSC.

In 2010, the population of the village of Kudinovo of the Noginsk region was about 3,000 people. This is a fairly large settlement. In 2004, the nearby village of the state farm named after the 50th anniversary of the October Revolution was annexed to the village of Kudinovo. This led to a sharp increase in the number of villagers. Today Kudinovo is the largest village in the Noginsky district.

The weather in Kudinovo of the Noginsky district practically does not differ from the capital, as this village is located relatively close to Moscow. The P109 Aniskino-Elektrougli highway passes through the village of Kudinovo in the Noginsk district. There are 3 bus routes in the city: 28, 29 and 31. They connect the village with the nearest town of Elektrougli and the district center of Noginsk.

Pokrovsky temple

The Intercession Church in Kudinovo is an ancient church of the first half of the 19th century. Its construction began in 1820 and was completed by 1835. In 1901, a new bell tower was built in the church. There was a parish school, a sobriety society and a large church choir at the temple.

In the 1930s, the church was closed. The stoves were heated with icons from the church, and the building itself was transferred to the collective farm for storing grain. The church was returned to believers only in 1989, by which time this building was almost destroyed.





Currently, the Intercession Church has been completely restored, and services are being held there. There is a Sunday school at the church, as well as a ringer school. In 2012, the first festival of church bell ringers was held here.

In the 1990s, a new temple was built in the village . It is consecrated in honor of John the Baptist. This small building is intended for the sacrament of baptism.

Kudinovsky quarries

Clay and sand were once mined in the village. This is how the Kudinovsky careers came about. They even had a special railway line. Then the quarries were abandoned and naturally filled with water. Ponds formed on the outskirts of the village.

It used to have very clear water. The nearby Biserovsky fish factory used quarries for breeding valuable breeds of fish. But over time, the situation has changed for the worse. In quarries, waste began to be drained, as a result of which the quality of water and the quantity of fish significantly deteriorated.

However, people still come here for summer and winter fishing. The fauna of the reservoir has decreased, but here you can still catch even such valuable fish as trout or pike perch. Roaches, bream, pike, and perch are also found in quarries. Paid fishing for carp is organized at one of the ponds.





In the summer in quarries you can meet bathing summer residents. People come to ponds from nearby cities and towns. Many consider the Kudinovskie career a good place for a beach holiday. However, it is officially believed that you can only sunbathe on these ponds, as the water does not comply with sanitary standards. Those who nevertheless decided to immerse themselves in the Kudinovsky quarries, need to remember that there is a very large depth - about 18-20 meters.

Sale of apartments

There are practically no new buildings in the village. Most houses here have 5 floors or less. On the network you can find ads for the sale of apartments in Kudinovo Noginsk district. This is mainly real estate in the secondary housing market. Apartments are sold in old five-story houses. However, this option may be suitable for some customers. From Kudinovo you can quickly get to Moscow, currently the metro stations in the capital can be directly reached by minibus. At the same time, the houses are located in a quiet green place.





The price of a one-room apartment in Kudinovo is about 1.8-2 million rubles, a two-room apartment is from 2.3 to 2.5 million rubles, and a three-room apartment is about 3-3.5 million rubles. For many, this is an appropriate option, especially for those who want to live in an environmentally friendly place and come to work in the capital.

Sale of summer residences and summer cottages

There are a lot of garden non-profit partnerships (SNT) in the Kudinovo Noginsk district. This place has always been popular among summer residents. Here are SNT "Vegetable grower", "Ivushka", "Forest Glades", "Priozerye" and others. Often you can find ads for the sale of cottages in this village. Their cost varies depending on the type of house and the size of the plot. On average, the price of cottages in Kudinovo is from 1 to 4 million rubles.





Also, the village offers land in the cottage village of Kudinovo-Lux. Their price is from 3 to 5 million rubles. The close location of the forest and ponds attracts buyers of country houses and plots.

How to get there by car

Getting to the village of Kudinovo of the Noginsk district from Moscow by car is not at all difficult. To do this, go to the eastern part of the Moscow Ring Road and from there turn onto Nosovikhinskoye Shosse. Continue on this road for 22 km to the Elektrougli railway station. Then turn onto Kudinovskoe highway and go about 3 km to the village. The road without traffic jams from MKAD takes approximately 40-45 minutes.





How to get there by public transport

Communication with the capital is quite convenient. You can get from Moscow to Kudinovo in two ways: by rail and minibuses from the metro.

You can get from the Kursk station by electric train to the station Elektrougli. Then transfer to local buses 28, 29, or 31. They go towards Kudinovo. Travel time is approximately 1 hour 30 minutes.

A shuttle bus 387K runs from the Novogireevo metro to the village. You can get directly without a change. You need to get out at the Kudinovo stop. The same minibus also runs from the Novokosino metro station.

From the nearest town of Elektrougli to Kudinovo you can take buses 28, 29, 31. From Noginsk to the village there is a bus or minibus 31.
