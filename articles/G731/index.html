Abkhazia: hotels, inns, private hotels

Want to visit an amazing country full of natural treasures and contrasts? Abkhazia opens the doors for you. Hotels for every taste and budget will help to realize exactly the vacation you dreamed about. Five-star hotels with the highest level of service or small bungalows where you will be as close as possible to the natural environment ... The total cost of your vacation depends on the choice of hotel, so think ahead.

Numerous resorts

The inhabitants of this wonderful country are distinguished by friendliness and hospitality. Whatever hotel, boarding house, hotel, sanatorium you choose, you will everywhere feel the national flavor, warmth, care and respect. Private hotels in Abkhazia are especially welcoming. Visiting attractions does not depend on where you are staying. An awesome world of excursions prepared by Abkhazia awaits you. The hotels will provide a comfortable rest after a busy day spent exploring lakes and waterfalls, mountains and forests, grottoes and gorges, ancient palaces, monasteries and temples.

Resort town of Tsandrypsh (formerly Gandiadi)

"Abkhazian courtyard" is an ideal option for a quiet, family vacation. This is a private economy hotel with comfortable rooms and luxurious views. The rooms have the necessary furniture, as well as a bathtub, shower and toilet. Tourists are offered two two-story cottages; in total, up to seventy guests are welcome here. The staff is helpful and attentive. At 200 meters from the hotel there is a clean sand and pebble beach. Water activities will delight adults and children. The cost of rest - from 700 rubles per day per person (with meals).

If you are traveling with children, then Psou Holiday House is ideal for you. There is a playground and a special menu for the kids. The equipped beach is located just 30 meters from the hotel. Vacationers are accommodated in an eight-story building with standard double rooms. Each has a bathroom, furniture - new.

The vast territory of the hotel provides everything necessary for outdoor activities. Gym, tennis courts, volleyball, basketball, football grounds. For the young and energetic, discos are organized. Three meals a day include a variety of delicious dishes, among which everyone will be able to choose what he likes. The cost of rest is from 800 rubles.

National cuisine is what Abkhazia boasts. Hotels have restaurants in their territories that serve famous meat dishes and pastries.

One of the most popular holiday destinations. Developed infrastructure is ready to meet the requirements of the most demanding tourists. Often it is with Gagra that Abkhazia is associated. Alahadzi Village

A small village between Gagra and Pitsunda. Near the village, on the Black Sea, is located a complex of "Clean Water". Here you will find a more secluded and relaxing holiday, in the rhythm of which you are so well acquainted with the treasures that Abkhazia is fraught with. The mini-hotel is a few convenient and comfortable cottages, each of which is designed for 4-6 people. The rooms are spacious, which is very good if you have fidgety children. Each house has comfortable furniture, a set of dishes. The food is simple but tasty. Lots of snacks, pastries and fruits. The distance to the beach is about 100 meters. For children there are inflatable pools and slides with a trampoline. The cost of rest - from 900 rubles per day.

The spa town of Gaduat

This is the center of a relaxing holiday, here you rarely find noisy, youth companies. A large sandy beach and shady cypress alleys allow you to feel harmony with nature. You will forever remember your vacation in Abkhazia. Economy hotels provide an opportunity for budget spending in a sunny country. This applies to the boarding house "Chernomorets" (from 1000 rubles per day with three meals a day). The hotel area is closed and guarded. The rooms are clean, furnished. The food is simple, but varied and plentiful. The beach is a minute's walk, clean, but without deck chairs and umbrellas. The attitude of the staff is courteous and attentive. The lack of developed infrastructure is compensated by the opportunity to visit sightseeing tours and relax from the bustle of the city.





New Athos

A very picturesque resort located on the shore of a small bay at the foot of the mountains. The hills on which the resort is located are covered with lush greenery. There is a restaurant, bar, children's and sports grounds. The price includes breakfast. Cost - from 950 rubles.
