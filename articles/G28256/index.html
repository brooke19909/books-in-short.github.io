ZhZhZ for the military man. Accumulative-mortgage housing system for military personnel

The time when everyone could get housing from the state is over. Now the solution of the housing problem falls on the shoulders of those in need. Since 2005, a special system of the Target Housing Loan (LHL) for the military has been operating. The essence of this program will be discussed in this article.

Mortgage

To buy an apartment as a pledge through a bank, you need to make a down payment and pay a certain amount of money every month. Such a scheme may be suitable for representatives of typical professions with a stable income level, but not for people who have devoted their entire lives to public service. Especially for them, in 2004, the government developed a funded mortgage system (NIS).

The essence of the program is as follows. A NIS participant receives a monthly subsidy in his account, which he can use only to pay an advance on a mortgage. In 2013, its size was 18.5 thousand rubles. During the year, 222 thousand rubles could be accumulated. The size of the subsidy is the same for all employees, but annually it is indexed upwards. In 2014, this amount amounted to 233.1 thousand rubles. 36 months after participating in the program, you can submit a report on the issuance of a certificate (CL) for a soldier. This is the starting point in obtaining a mortgage. Although the money goes to the borrower's account, he cannot use it until a certain time. Therefore, formally, a person receives funds in loans. The program is aimed not only at providing housing for a certain category of citizens, but also at forming an army. Having received a loan from the state, a person cannot quit of his own free will. Otherwise, you will have to return all the funds to the budget.





Money accumulated in the borrower's account is multiplied by investing using reliable financial instruments. Funds can be received in cash only after dismissal from service and under certain conditions.

Further actions

After receiving the documents, it's time to start searching for the apartment. The certificate of ZHZ for the serviceman is the approval of the borrower by the bank, confirmation of its solvency. Apartments can be chosen in a finished house or a new building. An object on the primary market must be accredited by the Federal State Institution Rosvoenipoteka. There are not many of them. After the client decides on the choice, you can contact the bank to complete the contract.

To get a loan for military personnel, you need to fill out a questionnaire, an application for a loan and open an account, provide a package of documents and a report on the cost of the apartment. Papers are considered up to 10 days. After receiving approval, the employee must appear in the department to sign the contract. Mortgage terms

If a soldier becomes a member of the NIS, an accumulative account in his name is automatically opened. For graduates of educational institutions in 2005 and later, as well as persons who have undergone the training of junior officers after 01/01/2008, such a basis is to receive an officer rank. For warrant officers, warrant officers, who served three years, - the conclusion of the first contract. Volunteers to participate in the program must write a report to the commander. The date of its registration in the register will be considered the basis for inclusion in the NIS.

Each soldier is assigned a number on the basis of which the Federal State Institution "Rosvoenipoteka" opens a savings account. A notice of inclusion of a person in the register shall be sent to the unit.



Exit from the program

In case of dismissal from service, death, recognition of a person as missing, he is expelled from the NIS. The savings account is closed, and all funds, depending on the circumstances, are transferred to the military family or returned to the federal budget.

Three years after opening an account, the borrower is entitled to use the funds to purchase a home. After obtaining a loan for l / s, a subsidy will still be received. The accumulated amount at the time of dismissal from the army can be obtained in cash. Allowed to spend this loan to improve housing conditions or for other purposes. The same opportunity appears for people who have served for more than 20 years. A military man with ten years of experience can also receive money in cash if he was fired:

The accumulated funds under the ZHZ program can be used to make an advance and part of the monthly payment. The maximum loan amount is calculated on the basis of contributions that must be in the account of a soldier by the time he is 45 years old. How is a loan repaid for military personnel?

After 20 years of service, the money used within the framework of the NIS is transferred from the category of a special-purpose loan to a non-paid subsidy. If a person leaves before this period, he must return the funds with interest within 10 years. The contract ZHZZ provides an annuity scheme for debt repayment.

Insurance Nuances

The bank may resell the mortgage. When changing ownership, the borrower needs to amend the insurance contract. The soldier learns about this from an official letter from the bank. After receiving the notice, you must come to the insurance company and re-sign the contract within the agreed period.

The borrower can independently choose a credit institution for execution of the contract. The conditions are almost the same everywhere:

ZHZZ for a soldier - confirmation of the solvency of the client, the ability to buy an apartment on credit on favorable terms.
