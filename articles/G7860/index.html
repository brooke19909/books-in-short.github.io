Replacing the screen on the tablet. How to change yourself?

Today, tablets and phones from Samsung and ASUS are very popular. Now they are quite large manufacturers of computer equipment. The tablets of these companies are modern, functional and attractive in appearance. Unfortunately, devices from ASUS and Samsung are expensive, and various breakdowns entail serious repairs. If you adhere to standard operating rules, then tablets can serve their owners for a long time. As it turned out, these gadgets also have flaws and weaknesses. Now developed methods to eliminate the most common breakdowns.



Tablet repair. Screen Replacement

Often encountered such a breakdown of any device, such as a broken screen. This damage can easily get an ASUS device and a Samsung tablet. Screen replacement in this case is possible. Often, to fix the problem, contact the service centers, but you can try to fix the problem yourself. These devices have strong glass, which is even difficult to scratch. You can check this if you take the already broken screen and draw it with a knife. Another case is to sit on a gadget, then the glass breaks easily. Replacing the screen on an ASUS tablet is a waste of money, time and effort.

Touch Screen Replacement

In some models, you can change the touch screen separately from the display itself, it most often crashes or malfunctions. This replacement is difficult in that it is necessary to separate the display and the sensor, and this is very difficult to do, since they are tightly connected. Changing the glass on the tablet is much easier than on the phone, of course, this is due to the size of the device. Do not forget that before undertaking to replace the screen, you must have basic knowledge of computer repair. Otherwise, you can only do harm, which subsequently entails a big waste of money.



Before buying a screen, you need to inspect its brocade number. If you change and buy a touch screen yourself, then be sure to look at its number. In some devices, "Samsung" and ASUS - different screens. They may be similar in appearance and fit in size, but if they are interchanged, then your device will not work. Of course, there are variations of the touch screen, which will suit many devices, but this must first be learned from the manufacturers.

Resolving Touch Screen Issues

The touch screen of the required version is difficult to find. If you are going to change it to a screen of a similar model, you will need to flash the digitizer. This is an additional waste of money, time and effort.

If you have a device with built-in 3G, then the digitizer will be different from a regular board, since the SIM card slot is located inside.

If you changed the touch screen, but it does not work, contact the service center, as you did something wrong. In this case, a specialist will inspect your device and take the necessary actions. You will not be able to bring the device back to life, only a professional using his tablet with special programs will restore the touch screen.
