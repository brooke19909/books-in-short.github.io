Rest in Yalta in the winter: photos, reviews of tourists

Holidays in Crimea are very popular in the summer and winter. It is worth noting that on New Year's Eve all the hotels are already occupied by newly made tourists. So what attracts Yalta in the winter? Such a specific vacation has distinctive features that attract travelers. But before the planned trip, the main issue should be considered - is the weather in Yalta in winter.

History of Yalta

It is still impossible to establish when a settlement appeared in this area. Jalit is mentioned in the description of the Crimean peninsula, which was compiled by the Arab traveler Al-Idrisi in 1154. This date is considered the most reliable.

In ancient legends, Yalta was called the coast of salvation for the ancient Greek sailors. In 1783, when Yalta joined the structure of Russia, it was a tiny fishing town of 13 houses and a military redoubt, which at one time was built by Suvorov in 1771.

The first thing that they began to build on this earth is the bell tower of the church of John Chrysostom. After, in the autumn of 1873, it was consecrated, and the emperor issued a decree granting Yalta the status of a city. In the XIX century the city was significantly transformed. At this time, there were about a thousand houses and more than twenty thousand inhabitants.

What is the weather like on the peninsula during the winter season?

In this southern city, severe cold practically does not occur, and snow is a completely rare phenomenon.

Temperature in Yalta in winter:

  • In December it is rather cloudy and cool, such weather can be compared with March. During the day, the air temperature is about 5-8 degrees. Precipitation in the form of wet snow and rain often falls. This month, it is better to refrain from vacation, as it may not be lucky with the weather. The reviews of vacationers about Yalta in winter also indicate that unpleasant bad weather overtakes during this period: heavy rains occur and heavy dirt forms on the ground.
  • In the middle of winter, rainfall ends and thanks to sunny, clear weather the earth dries up. Daytime air temperature does not drop below 5 degrees. If you go to Yalta for a week in January, then probably the whole vacation will take place in sunny weather. It will certainly be cool, but nice due to solar heat.
  • February in Yalta is considered the coldest. Severe storms with strong winds rage almost the entire month. The thermometer will certainly not please with its indicators, demonstrating subzero temperature. However, in the second half of February, the weather may change in a positive direction: it will become a little warmer and sunny.




When planning to visit Crimea, it is important to remember that winds in winter are common here. Even at a plus temperature, there may be a feeling of cold.

The benefits of a holiday in Yalta in winter

The biggest plus of holidays in Yalta in the winter season is the absence of an influx of tourists, as happens in the summer. It is possible to purchase a ticket for an individual excursion for the same price as a group program. Secondly, the price tags for any services or goods are significantly underestimated. Therefore, a vacation in Yalta in the winter is considered to be an inexpensive pleasure.





Hotels with pool and food offer luxury rooms at a reduced price. They also arrange discounts, promotions and provide additional services. A similar picture is observed in nightlife, bars and cafes. Due to the fact that airfare fares are not in great demand in winter, it will be much easier and cheaper to buy them. Therefore, the date of dispatch can be thought out slowly.

Disadvantages of vacation in Yalta in winter

Photos of winter and summer views of Yalta are posted in the article. But how does one rest here in winter? Of course, this type of recreation has disadvantages. All negative reviews about Yalta in winter boil down to one point - strong winds. Because of this, traffic on the cable car is very often canceled . True, there is still a plus in this: you can climb a plateau with a taxi and go sledding and skiing.





Even if the tourist does not know how to ski or sled, he will certainly be taught this and will be equipped with all the necessary equipment: snowboard, skiing, boots.





A professional instructor comes to the aid of beginners for a fee. Also in winter navigation is closed and therefore all boat trips are canceled.

What to see in Yalta?

In the cold season, there is still something for tourists to do in the city on the Crimean peninsula. In winter, museums, palaces and exhibitions are open. You can ask to organize an excursion to the Massandra Palace and the winery of the same name, to the Livadia Palace, the Swallow's Nest, the crocodile aquarium on the Embankment, the palace of Alexander III and much more.





It is definitely worth climbing Ai-Petri (if it is windy - by transport, if not - by cable car). It should be noted that the plateau is cold, so when you arrive there, you need to dress warmly.

The main attraction of Yalta - Seaside Park

A very popular place on the peninsula is the Seaside Park named after Yuri Gagarin, which pleases with an abundance of trees and flowers that delight the eye until late winter. It is located in the southwestern part of the sunny city. Walking along it at the very beginning you can see a monument dedicated to the great Russian writer - Maxim Gorky. Walking a little further along the avenue, admiring the evergreen trees, it is worth stopping and looking at the color music fountain.





There is also an observation deck built in the center of which is equipped with a swimming pool. On both sides of the pool rotundas in the form of a semicircle are installed. How to dress

In the southern part of the sunny city, the weather is warmer than in other places. It is too wet. Therefore, even if the mark on the thermometer indicates positive temperature, cold and damp can be felt in the city. Travelers should bring along a membrane jacket, comfortable waterproof boots and warm sweaters.

Many tourists go to this city in the winter not only to relax and gain strength, but also to strengthen their health. Crimean air alone is worth what: the smell of pines and cypress, mineral salts, iodine and ozone is felt. In Seaside Park you can find a pump room with mineral water. Walking in the park at any time of the year is very useful.





In the boarding house "Seasons" also the prices for accommodation and medical procedures are significantly lower than in summer. It has various wellness services: complete body cleansing, treatment of the musculoskeletal system.
