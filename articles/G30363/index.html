Speakers Defender Hollywood 35: instruction and reviews

Defender is known as a provider of low-cost and functional audio devices. Among the most popular speaker models from this brand is the Defender Hollywood 35 5.1 device. What are its features? What do users write about their experience in operating these columns?





Device specifications

For starters, let's study the main characteristics of the Defender Hollywood 35 device. The device has:

  • total power - 45 W, front and rear speakers - 10 W, main channel - 5 W;
  • frequency range support from 200 to 20,000 Hz;
  • subwoofer power of 20 W;
  • subwoofer supporting frequencies from 50 to 200 Hz;
  • wooden case;
  • remote control included.

Experts attribute the columns in question to six-channel solutions of a budget class.

In terms of combining functionality and price, the Defender Hollywood 35 device can be attributed to the most competitive solutions on the market. These characteristics of the device are not so often found in offers of other brands that manufacture products in the budget segment.

Actually, what are the features of positioning the corresponding device on the market?

Column positioning in the market

Modern audio devices are sold in 3 main market segments - budget, medium, and Hi-End. Of course, within each of them there may be additional classifications. In addition, there are approaches according to which certain types of acoustics are models optimized for clubs, restaurants, stages, office, home use.





Speakers Defender Hollywood 35 BK legitimately attributed to the budget class, they are designed primarily for use in an apartment or small office. In the absence of alternatives, of course, they can also be adapted for placement in a catering institution - if its visitors are not too picky in assessing the quality of sound accompaniment. The speakers in question are certainly capable of reproducing high-quality sound, but it is not quite correct to compare their capabilities with the best mid-range models, as well as with Hi-End devices.

The market for low-cost audio equipment, according to many experts, is among the most promising segments. This is largely due to higher prices for equipment of more expensive categories. At the same time, users, while making decisions about saving, nevertheless expect to get a product when purchasing audio equipment that can satisfy their basic needs for good sound. That is, those manufacturers who can offer devices to the market lower in class, but comparable in quality with more advanced devices, have the right to count on an increase in market share.





Defender can be attributed to such suppliers. Very few brands are ready to offer the market six-channel equipment with a sufficiently large capacity, as well as support for a wide range of frequencies. According to experts, in the future a trend may form on the market, according to which the boundaries between many segments will be erased: budget models can be comparable, at least with middle-class devices, if not in terms of functionality, then at least in quality sound reproduction. In this sense, Defender claims to be a leader, now offering users a budget device that is comparable in quality and function to higher-end devices.

Let us now examine in more detail how the Defender product can withstand competitiveness. We will be interested, first of all, in such aspects as the functionality of the device, the convenience of controlling it, both in terms of standard means and in terms of using additional devices, the nuances of connecting the device to sound sources. Of course, studying user reviews will also be important to us.





Equipment

It will be useful to study in what configuration the device is delivered to the market. Inside the box in which the device is sold, the user will find:

  • subwoofer
  • set of satellites;
  • 3 signal cables, as well as 3 adapters;
  • remote control with batteries;
  • instructions;
  • warranty card.

The cables supplied in the set of speakers significantly increase the versatility of the device: they allow you to connect it to the main common user connectors, for example, those that are located on the PC sound card or on a DVD player.

Appearance

Speakers can be called compact enough, which facilitates their installation in an apartment or office. Device Defender Hollywood 35 is available in 2 color versions - black, as well as silver. You can choose the one that is optimal for the interior of a room.

The satellites that come with the kit are equipped with rubber feet that provide stability to the speakers when placed on smooth surfaces. The subwoofer looks quite massive. It is quite convenient on it - as the reviews characterizing the Defender Hollywood 35 device may indicate - the controls are located, as well as various indicators. Using appropriate components, the user can turn on the speakers, turn them off, set the optimal volume level, and also indicate the source of the audio signal.

On the rear panel of the subwoofer are the connectors to which the cables are connected - signal and satellite. Communication is carried out using RCA type connectors.

Let us now examine in more detail the key functional components of the Defender Hollywood 35 device. Among them is the speaker.

Speaker Components: Speaker

The corresponding hardware component operates on the basis of a band resonator. This solution allows, above all, to effectively play bass. From the point of view of the design of the speakers, the speaker is installed quite simply. Inside, the subwoofer is divided into 2 areas by a small partition - the considered hardware component is installed on it.

In addition to the speaker, Defender Hollywood 35 speakers are also equipped with a bass reflex, which is used to ensure the interaction of the internal areas of the subwoofer and the external space of the room. Electronic components. As for the electronic filling of the device - this is installed on a small board that receives power from a transformer.

The amplifier operates on 3 microcircuits that are mounted using DIP packages. In general, the build quality of electronic components Defender Hollywood 35 (user reviews can confirm this) is rated as high.

Remote Control

The next most important hardware component of the speakers is the control panel, which is also included by the manufacturer in the delivery kit of the device. According to users, this remote is characterized by compactness and excellent ergonomics. It works on 2 AA batteries.

It is noteworthy that more functions are available on the remote control than when using those speaker controls that are located on the subwoofer casing. So, for example, using the remote control, the speaker owner can not only adjust the volume of melody playback and select the input signal, but also adjust the sound through the channels. In addition, using the remote control, you can reset the device to those that are set by default.

Tests

What are the practical test results for Defender Hollywood 35 speakers? According to the results of experts, the device copes well with its work when compared with the capabilities of devices in the same budget segment. According to experts, a little lacking is the support of high frequencies, as is done in professional solutions. Sound reproduction is carried out with sufficient detail, with a high volume level.





The impression of listening to tunes can vary greatly with the different arrangement of satellites in the room. With this user Defender Hollywood 35 may be worth experimenting. But, of course, taking into account the operating recommendations from the manufacturer.

Speaker instruction manual: preparing the device for work

Above, we noted that the Defender Hollywood 35 BK speaker kit is included in the package. This document contains a number of noteworthy provisions. First of all, it regulates the preparation of relevant devices for work.

Satellites according to the corresponding document are classified into front and rear. The first is recommended to be placed in front of the user, and the distance between them should be about 2 meters. A subwoofer should be placed between the front satellites. In turn, the rear speakers should be located behind the user - at an angle of 30 to 60 degrees.





Connect Defender Hollywood 35 to a computer or other sound source so that there are no obstacles in front of the speakers that could distort the sound. In this case, you need to follow a number of recommendations according to the instructions. In order to connect the Defender Hollywood 35 to a six-component source, such as a DVD player, you need to use 6 RCA connectors, which are located on the rear panel of the subwoofer. In turn, if you are connecting to a stereo signal, the source of which can be, for example, a CD player, then you need to use 2 RCA connectors, which are located on the AUX panel, which is located on the subwoofer.

In order to make sure that the speakers are connected successfully to the computer, you can use special software from the manufacturer.

Instructions for the columns: the safety of operation of devices

A significant part of the instruction is devoted to the safety of the operation of the device. So, the manufacturer warns the user from placing the speakers in a damp room, from using the device in conditions of vibration, temperature, direct sunlight. It is not recommended to repair the Defender Hollywood 35 speakers yourself. The instruction instructs users to contact the seller or the specialized Defender service center in case of problems with the device.

It is recommended that the speakers be placed on stable surfaces. Do not place heavy objects on the subwoofer or satellites. It is advisable to position the subwoofer so that the distance between its rear panel and the wall is at least 10 cm.

It is recommended to set the sound volume on the Defender Hollywood 35 BK speakers to a minimum when the device is turned off. Before connecting the device to the sound sources, it is recommended to disconnect the speakers. If it is not planned to use the device from Defender for a long time, the device should also be disconnected from the mains.
