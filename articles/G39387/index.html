Hotel Eden Village Yadis Hammamet 4 * (Hammamet, Tunisia): photos and reviews of tourists

Modern tourists are always eager to see something new. If you are tired of the resorts of Turkey or Egypt, then you should pay attention to the small state of Tunisia, located in northern Africa. Inexpensive prices, quality service, coupled with a rich excursion program and therapeutic mud wraps, made this country one of the most popular destinations in 2016. Choosing a place to stay, travelers often stay at the Eden Village Yadis Hammamet 4 * hotel (Tunisia, Hammamet). How is it attractive for tourists?

Where is the complex located?

The hotel is located near Hammamet, which is famous for its high quality service. Excellent rest in Eden Village Yadis Hammamet 4 * will be possible for tourists who love a relaxing holiday, and youth companies. Thalassotherapy centers, nightclubs, restaurants and local bars are located here. The beach here is not very wide, but the sand is perfectly clean. It is very small and at the same time almost white, without pebbles and other impurities. Having settled here, it is worth visiting local attractions, namely the old Medina and the Spanish fort of the 16th century. Together with the children you can go to the amusement park or go to the botanical garden. More about room equipment

Apart from furniture and decor, all apartments of the Eden Village Yadis Hammamet 4 * hotel are equipped with equipment to make living in it more comfortable. The guests of the complex can always take advantage of the following amenities:

  • Private bathroom comes with a shower. There is a mirror, a hairdryer, toiletries (soap, shower gel), bathrobes and slippers, towels.

Entertainment at the hotel

Tourists staying at the Eden Village Yadis Hammamet Club 4 * hotel can have fun visiting the following places for leisure:

  • Private beach located 50 meters from the hotel. Guests enjoy free use of sun loungers, towels, sun umbrellas and air mattresses.
  • Gym. His visit is not included in the tour price and is paid separately. Aerobics and water gymnastics classes are held regularly.
  • Seasonal outdoor pool. Guests can also use the free sun loungers. There is a sunbathing area, but there are no water slides. There is also an indoor pool, which is open during the winter.
  • SPA-center. For an additional fee, it offers massage services, a solarium and a jacuzzi. Tourists can also visit the Turkish bath, sauna and steam room.
  • Water activities: diving and windsurfing.
  • Playgrounds for sports: volleyball, basketball, soccer, golf, squash, table tennis.
  • Billiard rooms.
  • Disco, day and evening shows.




Service and Facilities

For vacationers, the tourist complex Eden Village Yadis Hammamet 4 * tries to organize a quality vacation. For this, hotel guests can always use the following services:

  • Doctor's office. He provides his services for a fee. It mainly treats colds, poisoning and mild infections.
  • Currency exchange. Tourists at any time of the day can change any currency to the Bulgarian lev without a large commission.
  • Beauty salon, where guests can do makeup, manicures and pedicures, conduct wellness treatments for the face. A male and female hairdresser also works. All services are paid.
  • Dry cleaning, where for an additional fee the staff launder and iron the clothes of tourists.
  • TV room. Here you can watch satellite channels, as well as play computer games.
  • Shops and souvenir shop. It sells food, fruits, cosmetics, clothes, shoes and the press.
  • Secure car parking. Guests of the complex can leave their car here for free at any time. Paid car and bicycle rentals are also available.
  • Free internet in public areas: by the pool, on the beach and in the hotel lobby.




All about food: options, restaurants and bars

The food system in Eden Village Yadis Hammamet 4 * reviews are getting good. The hotel has been working on the all-inclusive system for many years. The price includes all meals, including small snacks. Guests can also enjoy without limitation any local drinks: wine, beer, mineral water, juices, alcoholic cocktails, espresso, American coffee, milk and tea. A free children's, diet or vegetarian menu is available upon request. All meals are served at the buffet on a strict schedule.

In addition, at any time, tourists can have a meal in the bars. Consider all the catering facilities that are represented in the hotel:

  • The main restaurant. It serves breakfast and lunch, as well as partial dinners. All meals are located at the buffet.
  • Restaurant The Brace. Located on an open terrace by the sea. It works from mid-June to the end of September, only in fine weather. It serves meat and fish, grilled, salads and fruits. Hotel visitors are offered one free entry to the restaurant.
  • Lobby bar. He offers tourists a snack with pizza, fresh pastries, ice cream, desserts or fruits. Tea, coffee, concentrated juices and mineral water are also served here.
  • Snack bar. Late breakfasts and afternoon snacks are served here. The menu includes pastries, pancakes and biscuits, as well as tea, American coffee, milk and juices.
  • Beach bar. Tourists can enjoy refreshing cocktails and grab a bite from a pizza or burger.




What hotel can offer children?

Being a family complex, the Eden Village Yadis Hammamet 4 * hotel is interested in making children relax here fun and comfortably. A cradle is provided free of charge for babies in the room, and parents can always use highchairs in the restaurant. During the holidays, you can leave the children in the mini club, where they will be busy with drawing lessons, applied classes and competitions. Also, hotel guests can always hire a qualified nanny who will look after the child while the parents are busy. Her services are paid separately.

From entertainment for children, the hotel has a small pool with a depth suitable for bathing babies. Rescue workers also work on the shore. There is a mini-club for children from four to twelve years old. There is also a children's animation in the complex. Disco for the smallest ones are regularly held. There is a children's playground for outdoor games, equipped with modern and safe swings and carousels.

Conclusion
