Pearl pattern. Knitting needles learn to knit a beautiful pattern

One of the most popular knit patterns among knitters is pearl. The structure of the canvas, designed by him, resembles small pebbles, which explains, in fact, the origin of its name. Many needlewomen recognize this pattern by other names - "rice", "1x1 putanka". In this article we will discuss how to knit a similar pattern, talk about which products can use this motif.



Learning to knit a pearl pattern with knitting needles (small pebbles)

For an example of this pattern, put an even number of loops on the knitting needles. We knit a small pearl pattern with knitting needles according to the description.

1 row. Remove the first edge loop. Next, knit like this: 1 front eyelet, 1 wrong eyelet. Repeat this sequence until the end of the row. Run the last loop in all rows. This helps to ensure that the edge of the product is flat.

2 row. Remove the loop edge . Next, knit the wrong side over the front, and the front one over the wrong side. Perform in this sequence to the end of the row.

Knit the 3rd row and all the odd ones as the 1st, and follow all the paired rows according to the description of the 2nd row.

The name is the same, the effect is different. Option number 2

From the following description, you will learn how to make a large pearl pattern with knitting needles. The canvas, designed by him, has a more pronounced structure, knitted "pebbles" are oblong. You can verify this yourself by looking at the photo.



We collect loops on the knitting needles and knit the first row according to the instructions. In the description, the symbol "asterisks" (*) is used. This means that the sequence of loops that is indicated between these icons should be repeated until the end of the row. So, we learn to knit a pearl pattern with knitting needles (large).

1 row: hem (remove), * 1 eyelet, 1 wrong *.

2 row: hem, * over the front loop, the front knits, over the wrong loop - the wrong *. While the picture looks like a 1x1 elastic. Next is the change.

3 row: edge, * over the front loop, the wrong side is performed, over the front loop *. The loops are shifted relative to the previous row.

4th row: hem, * over the front loop, the front knits, and over the wrong loop - the wrong *. In the next row, the pattern repeats from the 1st row.

Pearl (semi-English) gum: beautiful and original

This embodiment of the pattern is used to design cuffs on products. This gum looks interesting and elegant. How to knit a pearl pattern with knitting needles of this type? Learn the description.

To complete the sample, dial 10 loops.

1 row: hem, * 1 front, 1 wrong *, draw the last loop wrong.

2 row: hem, * purl the loop of the previous row without knitting, making a crochet over it, knit the next loop (front) *.

3 row: hem, front loops, perform facial, knitting them together with the crochets of the previous row. Remove the purl loops without knitting, while making crochets.

Knit all even rows according to the description of the 2nd row, and odd ones - like the 3rd row.
