What do lizards, rabbits and snails eat?

In the summer, brisk lizards can be seen in the garden, forest, garden. They quickly run among plants and stones or just bask in the sun. These animals have excellent "color" vision, however, many representatives of this reptile class are not able to make sounds.

Surely many lovers of exotic animals of this species will be interested in the question: what do lizards eat? If you want to have one of the representatives of the reptile class at home, you should learn in detail about the features of their behavior and nutrition. This will allow the animal to be kept in the conditions most suitable for it.

So what do lizards eat?

The main and favorite delicacy of these individuals is invertebrate animals. These include mollusks, worms, insects, spiders, millipedes, etc. Also, lizards are happy to eat various pests of the garden and garden: the bear, flies, beetles, caterpillars and butterflies. The benefit of reptiles is that they destroy carriers of various diseases. Their diet, depending on the habitat, consists of 35-90% of harmful insects and larvae. In the summer, finding food for reptiles is not difficult, you can dig up earthworms or catch a couple of grasshoppers.

What do lizards eat in winter?

Insects for feeding reptiles in winter can be bred independently, or can be purchased in a special store. For feeding lizards, crickets, tropical cockroaches, and flour worms are usually used. It is most convenient to give food to animals with tweezers. At the same time, while feeding the lizard of the flour worm, its head must be crushed with tweezers. This will protect the stomach of the reptile from the strong jaws of the insect, which can pretty much damage it. When feeding lizards with bugs, they should tear off the elytra.

What do rabbits eat?

Many pet lovers enjoy bunnies. And this is not surprising. After all, these charming fluffy pets bring a lot of joy and positive emotions, help create a cozy atmosphere in the house.

Their main food is hay, fresh grass and some quality dry food. Sometimes it is possible to give animals a small amount of fresh vegetables and solid foods that help grind teeth.

Decorative rabbits are strictly forbidden to eat starch and high-calorie foods, herbs that are unhealthy, and sweets.

Animals can use hay and water in unlimited quantities. In addition, rabbits can be given a variety of feeds and compound feeds, but no more than two tablespoons per day.

Animals are happy to eat vegetables with green leaves and herbs: salad, cabbage, broccoli, dill, mint, chicory, tomatoes, carrots, etc. In addition, daily it is necessary to give the rabbit different vegetables.

What do snails eat?

Snail breeders know that this unpretentious insect can be perfectly kept in a pond or aquarium.

These animals are very indiscriminate in food, so they eat fish food with vegetables wonderfully. They also feed on rotten or dead plants and soft vegetation. Any fish food is perfect for snails; they can be given soft vegetables and greens.

In addition, they can eat frozen food for fish and even dead fish.

In summer and spring, during the breeding season, snails should be fed a little more, and in winter - slightly limit the amount of food.
