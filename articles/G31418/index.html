Hotel Baohong Hotel (Hainan): overview, description, rooms and reviews

China is an amazing Asian country, which is literally teeming with various hotels with a very reasonable price and the highest level of service, comparable to the service in Europe. One of these hotel complexes can be safely called the Baohong Hotel Sanya, which will be discussed in the review.

Location





The distance to the ocean and the magnificent beaches of the area with azure water and white sand is easily overcome by 5 minutes of leisurely walks.
In addition, there are many of the largest business centers for building a business, which are visited by many influential people. Sanya is just 20 minutes away by car. The city center can be reached in 10 minutes. It takes an average of 20 minutes to reach the state-of-the-art local airport. Baohong Hotel 4 was built in 2005, during which time a great number of tourists from all over the country and neighboring countries visited its territory.

Sea and beach





The hotel is located in the most picturesque bay of Hainan - Dadonghai. The hotels located in this area occupy the second coastline and have a common beach along which the promenade and road extend.

On the state beach, the hotel has its own territory, equipped with sun loungers and umbrellas, which only guests of the complex can use.

The road to the beach takes only 5 minutes, just cross the road. You can use an electric car that runs every half hour, which takes you to the place in just 2 minutes.

The water in the sea is warm and clean. Sometimes algae can be washed ashore. The sea is not very deep, but the first meters you need to go on small shells, which is not very nice. Here it is quite possible to swim with young children.

Sunbeds on the beaches are almost back to back, so there is no way to stay in solitude.

Hainan is not only a beach holiday, so you can safely close your eyes to all the minor flaws. People go to the island in order to improve their health and get acquainted with the unique local culture. Beach and sea is a nice app.

Nutrition





Sanya Jinjiang Baohong Hotel offers its guests free breakfast. More precisely, they are included in the room rate. Dine and dine in the hotel restaurant or anywhere else. Outside the complex there are many cafes, restaurants and bars.

Buffet breakfast. The menu is quite varied, dishes are constantly replenished and every day updated with new ones. An assortment of fruits is always available.

Service





Rooms are cleaned several times a day. Every day, maids leave fresh newspapers in English and Chinese.

The staff, including the administration, speaks exclusively Chinese. But the hotel has a translator with knowledge of the Russian language. If you have any questions, you can solve everything through it.

Wellness





Many go to China with the goal of improving their health. Chinese medicine is recognized as one of the best in the world. You can go through various procedures in the numerous medical centers located near the Baohong Hotel 5 (Hainan). To save money, you can call your local city hospital and request treatment. The doctor will come directly to the hotel, put needles, massage and prescribe herbs. In medical centers, such procedures are several times more expensive.

Excursions

Sanya is rich in attractions. However, do not trust all the guides. Upon arrival, tourists are immediately lured to a sightseeing tour of the island, but it is better to refuse it. As a rule, they will not show you anything except factories where you spend a lot of money on completely unnecessary goods. You can explore the city yourself by choosing the desired route. The hotel can offer several sightseeing tours. They cost less than if ordered from a tour operator.

Vacationers greatly praise the park with the fancy name "The deer turned its head." This picturesque place is great for those who love beautiful photos. Trips to various factories, attendance at tea ceremonies and other similar events, as a rule, end with extortion of money. Such goods can be bought in stores several times cheaper.

Be sure to visit the island of monkeys and thermal springs. It offers vacationers 36 pools with radon waters. It is believed that thermal springs can heal any disease. Having been in China, do not miss this opportunity.

Entertainment

Unfortunately, the hotel does not have a pool. But you can visit the spa and beauty center.

In the evening, head to the night club. Similar institutions are famous for the island of Hainan. Baohong Hotel is located in the heart of city entertainment. Restaurants, bars, clubs - all within walking distance. Some establishments can be visited free of charge. Food and cocktails are relatively cheap. The restaurants serve huge portions, you can safely order one for two. By the way, the local food service is adequately adapted for European tourists.

General impressions

The hotel is ideal for active people. All services are provided at the highest level for a fairly low price. In the ranking of Sanya hotels, the Sanya Jinjiang Baohong Hotel complex occupies a leading position and is the most popular destination.

Among the shortcomings can be noted the lack of a private pool (guests have to use the services of neighboring hotels), a huge number of noisy Chinese and the lack of balconies in some rooms. An indisputable plus is the location of the hotel in the entertainment center of the city. Great beach is just 5 minutes walk. All kinds of health centers are located nearby, for the sake of which many go to Hainan. The island is built up very quickly. Every year there are new interesting places to visit, but the Baohong Hotel remains the unchanged leader among hotel complexes in its price category.
