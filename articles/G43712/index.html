Hotel Saigon Suoi Nhum Resort 4 * (Vietnam): description and photos

Want to discover Vietnam? Would you like to spend an unforgettable vacation on the seashore away from civilization? Do you plan to stay in a comfortable hotel? In this case, Saigon Suoi Nhum Resort 4 * (Phan Thiet) may be a good option for you. We will find out further what this hotel offers its guests.



Location

The four-star hotel in question is a few meters from Ham Tuan Nam Beach. In just 10 minutes, guests can walk to a local attraction - Ke Ga Lighthouse. Ocean Dunes Golf Club is a 20-minute drive away. In the vicinity of the hotel, according to tourists, there is no special infrastructure. For all the entertainment you need to go to Mui Ne or Phan Thiet. The hotel provides a free shuttle service twice a day. Also, you can always go to the place of interest to you by public transport (bus) or taxi. Many travelers, however, prefer to rent a motorbike. In this case, you will be able to freely move around all the surrounding areas. In addition, if you plan to travel a lot, renting a scooter will cost you much less than taxis and buses. As for the distance from the airport, the journey from it takes about eight hours. According to travelers, this fact is the only serious drawback of Saigon Suoi Nhum Resort 4 * (Phan Thiet). Although tourists who want to spend a relaxing vacation away from the noise of big cities, this fact is not particularly embarrassing.



Description

The hotel in question is medium in size. So, his housing stock is represented by 150 comfortable rooms located in the main building and several bungalows. The hotel has a rather large territory of its own. According to our compatriots having a rest here, she is very beautiful, green, clean and well-groomed. Here you can take a pleasant walk, admire the tropical vegetation and watch the bright fish that live in the picturesque pond.

The guests were satisfied with the two pools of the hotel. And one of them is filled with sea water. Near the pools there is a sun terrace with comfortable sun loungers. Some tourists liked to relax here even more than on the beach itself. At any time of the day, you can enjoy your favorite drinks at the poolside bar.

In addition to all of the above, Saigon Suoi Nhum Resort 4 * (Vietnam) has its own restaurant offering Asian and European cuisine, a 24-hour reception, luggage storage, a conference room, laundry service, a tour desk, a currency exchange office, a beauty salon, a spa center, billiard room, children's playground.

Our compatriots also pay attention that the hotel staff does not speak Russian. Moreover, not everyone understands English well. But communication difficulties, as a rule, do not arise.



Housing stock

As already mentioned, the hotel has 150 rooms in total. They all have a stylish design, are spacious, clean and comfortable. The apartments here are presented in the categories Standard, Deluxe and Suite. There are also several bungalows. Regardless of the type, each room has a TV with cable channels, comfortable furniture, telephone, air conditioning, refrigerator, tea set, a bathroom with shower and bath, a balcony or terrace. Apartments are cleaned every day. Also, several times a week, maids renew towels and bed linen and replenish personal hygiene supplies as they are spent.



Nutrition

The cost of accommodation in the hotel in question includes breakfast only. In general, according to many tourists, they are standard for most hotels in Vietnam. So, in the morning you can eat fried eggs or scrambled eggs, fried bacon, sausage, fruits, vegetables, pastries, as well as local dishes (soup fo, seafood and side dish). Guests have the opportunity to have lunch and dinner also in the hotel restaurant. The cook here, according to tourists, is not bad, and the prices are affordable. A number of travelers preferred eating in a cafe near the hotel. If you go to the city, you can eat in one of the cafes, which there are many. According to our compatriots, prices in almost all establishments are quite affordable, and they cook deliciously everywhere.



Prices, travelers opinion

As for the cost of living, for seven nights for two here you will have to pay from 21 thousand rubles for a standard room, from 24 thousand rubles for a deluxe room, from 27 thousand rubles for a deluxe room, located in a building near the beach, from 33 thousand rubles for a bungalow and from 49 thousand rubles for a suite. In general, according to tourists who have left 4 * reviews about Saigon Suoi Nhum Resort, hotel room prices are fully consistent with its category, level of service and comfort. As for the hotel rating based on guest ratings, it is 3.8 points out of a maximum of five. Judging by the comments left by our compatriots, this figure would be much higher if the hotel was located closer to large settlements. Otherwise, tourists do not make any special claims to the hotel.
