Hotel "Victoria Olympus": address, description, services, infrastructure, description of rooms, photos and reviews

Minsk is a very interesting city in Belarus. Life is constantly in full swing here: tourists come to visit local sights, residents of other countries and cities come to visit their relatives, and businessmen solve business issues with their business partners. All these people need good and short-term housing, where you can relax and take a break from the road.

The Victoria Olympus Hotel is very popular throughout the city. The hotel in Minsk, presented under this name, according to most tourists, is a very worthy place for a pleasant pastime both for nonresident guests and residents of the capital of Belarus. Let us consider in more detail the features of staying here, as well as some subtleties of room service and arrangement.

General information

Hotel "Victoria Olympus" is a place that is perfect for relaxation of any category of guests. In their reviews, the guests of the hotel in question talk about the fact that there are excellent conditions for business development, for staying with children or even in company with your loved one. Hotel Victoria Olympus is often chosen as a venue for weddings - there is a huge banquet room where first-class service is offered, as well as rooms for honeymooners where they can spend the night.

It should be noted that the hotel in question is one of the first in the whole republic that harmoniously combines in its walls excellent living conditions, as well as a huge Class A business center, distinguished by its multifunctionality.

The hotel at one time had guests of honor, including celebrities. During important sporting events, many teams prefer to stay in the rooms of the Victoria Olymp Hotel (Minsk).

The hotel offers tourists luxurious apartments, most of which offer stunning views of the Church of the Protection of the Holy Virgin.

Rating

The hotel in question has a rather high rating, assigned to it based on reviews left by guests. So, on the world-famous Booking portal, it has a rating of 8.8 out of 10, and on Tripadvisor - 4.5 out of 5. It should be noted that these indicators in themselves demonstrate the quality of service and comfortable stay of travelers at the hotel " Victoria Olympus, located in Belarus.

As for the ratings assigned on the basis of international standards, the hotel has a four-star status, however, many guests claim that its level is fully consistent with 5 *.

Location

The hotel "Victoria Olympus" is located in a very beautiful place of the Belarusian capital, which is located in the city center, but differs in its silence. The resting place is completely surrounded by parkland, which makes the view of the territory especially beautiful. The complex is located near the Church of the Intercession of the Blessed Virgin Mary, as well as from the Minsk Arena.

As for transport connections, the tourists who are in the hotel in question can easily reach any end of Minsk using the public transport services, the stop of which is located within walking distance from the hotel. The hotel is surrounded by several metro stations: Molodezhnaya, Kuntsevschina, Frunzenskaya, Sportivnaya and Pushkinskaya. As for the international airport, where many foreign guests arrive, it is an hour's drive from the Victoria Olympus Hotel, 45 km away. By prior arrangement of tourists, a comfortable passenger car provided as part of the transfer service can be picked up from it.

The surrounding hotel has a large number of shopping centers, entertainment venues, catering establishments and shops, which are visited by its guests. Of these, the Arena City, Korona Zamok and Ramonak shopping malls are very popular. If a tourist wants to have a tasty snack, many experienced travelers recommend visiting food establishments such as Underworld, Belvedere or Grand Courchevel restaurants, as well as Profitrol cafe.

Hotel "Victoria Olympus" is located in Minsk, at the address: Prospekt Pobediteley 103 (Central District of the city).

Attractions nearby

Hotel "Victoria Olympus" (Minsk) is surrounded by a large number of attractions that are interesting for tourists who come to appreciate the beauty of the Belarusian capital. Not far from here is Victory Square, as well as a botanical garden in which metropolitan residents like to stroll in the evenings. Fans of various types of creativity can visit places such as: the Palace of the Republic, the Belarusian State Museum of the History of the Great Patriotic War, the Y. Kupala Theater, the National Art Museum, and the State Circus. Fans of great art pay special attention to the Opera and Ballet Theater of the Republic of Belarus.

Fans of religious sites can visit local attractions such as the Cathedral of Our Lady, as well as the Cathedral of the Descent of the Holy Spirit.

Literally three kilometers from the hotel is Komsomolskoe Lake, to the surface of which travelers from different regions of the country very often come, as well as guests of Minsk.

Tourists who take walks in the vicinity of the hotel in question, as a rule, remain satisfied with what they saw. Here, according to them, the nature is very beautiful, despite the fact that Victoria Olympus is located in the central part of the city.

Standard Room

Apartments of a standard category, according to tourists, are equipped with everything that is necessary for a comfortable stay for a short time. There is furniture equipment designed for one or two guests. If necessary, an extra bed can be installed in apartments of this type.

The standard class rooms have Internet access, as well as an electronic safe and a landline telephone with which you can make international calls. In their free time, tourists who stay in this room can watch satellite channels broadcast on a large plasma screen.

The rooms of this group are equipped with an excellent sitting area, located near a large window and presented in the form of a pair of armchairs and a small coffee table. There is also a wardrobe made of natural wood - it is suitable for the careful storage of the guest's things that he brings with him.

In any room of the standard type, tourists can observe the working area, which is presented in the form of a desk and a comfortable armchair. It is here that many vacationers who make a business trip to Minsk can solve a number of their business issues.

Superior rooms

Superior rooms are also designed for one tourist or a couple. Often apartments of this type are chosen by newlyweds who prefer to spend their wedding night in the beautiful conditions of a luxury hotel.

The interior of the apartments of this category contains a large number of details constructed from dark natural wood, as well as pastel-colored fabrics.

Here, the attention of the guests is a large bed with a rectangular ottoman, as well as a pair of bedside tables. There is a wardrobe for guests' clothes. This room type also has seating areas and work areas.

Superior apartments also include rooms for disabled guests. They have furniture and plumbing installed according to international standards, taking into account all the features of the life of people with disabilities.

The considered group of apartments also includes studio rooms, which are presented on a small area, in the form of one room. Visually allocated space is divided into several zones: sleeping, working, and also for rest.

Suite and apartments
