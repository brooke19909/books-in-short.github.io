How much does a psychologist earn? Psychologist salary in Russia

The psychologist is considered a sought-after specialist. People have constant stresses, a high pace of life and difficult relationships in society, which leads to problems with the nervous system. But this leads to a deterioration in the general condition of the body. Professional help is important here. To understand the problems, as well as solve them, a specialist will help . How much a psychologist earns is described in the article.

Profession Features

A true professional can understand various situations. It will help to find and eliminate the cause of disagreements between people, to determine a solution, to help understand yourself. The help of such a specialist is needed in the team in order to establish relations between employees. Now many organizations have such a position.





The value of a psychologist is invaluable in forensics. The work of a specialist is complicated, but also interesting. The composition of the crime helps them to compile the image of the criminal, to determine what he is.

Demanded by the Ministry of Emergencies. It is especially difficult to work in this organization, since it is important not only to provide assistance to the victims, but also to identify their fears and help cope with them.

What do you need to get a profession?

It takes a desire. The reasons for becoming a psychologist are different for everyone. Some want to understand themselves, while others want to help others. For this you need to get an education. It is provided in various universities. Training usually lasts 5 years.





After graduation, a diploma is issued, after which you can get a job. It is possible to engage in private practice, but you must obtain a license to open the organization of psychological assistance. Naturally, those who want to become specialists are interested in how much a psychologist receives? Salary depends on many factors.

What is included in the salary?

To determine how much the psychologist earns, one should establish what the salary consists of. But you need to remember that a graduate without experience gets less than an experienced employee. In addition to length of service, salary depends on:

  1. Places of work. In Russia, a psychologist can work in various fields, as this specialist is required in educational institutions, private clinics, municipal hospitals and other government areas.
  2. Region of work. In large cities, incomes will be higher compared to small settlements. The highest salaries in Moscow and the region.

The most highly paid is considered a private psychologist. His work is evaluated by the hour. But income can be unstable.

Revenues in Moscow

How much does a psychologist earn in Moscow and the region? The average figure is 30,000 rubles. If you get a private clinic, then there is the possibility of earning from 500 rubles per hour. Experienced professionals with an excellent reputation in Moscow earn from 100,000 rubles. In government organizations, income usually does not exceed 40,000 rubles.

Typically, working in a private clinic requires experience in a public institution. During this period, it will turn out to find regular customers, to prove himself an excellent specialist. This will build a successful career.

Increase income

The average income of these specialists is not as high as we would like. Although this area is prestigious, many psychologists get quite a bit. How to increase your income? Now you can use the Internet for this. This is especially true for young professionals who have a small income.





Why is making money online easier? There are several reasons for this:

  1. There is anonymity on the Internet. Many people want the help of a professional, but they will never go to an appointment. There are chats for this, communication via Skype is possible. But in this case, making entries in the medical record and personal communication is excluded.
  2. You can provide paid advice on the forums. This will not only earn extra money, but also determine the direction.
  3. It is possible to create a blog in text or video format, which will be advertising services. It will turn out to gain customers and increase popularity.
  4. Another option for a side job is writing psychology articles that are in demand.

The psychologist can conduct trainings, seminars to increase the level of knowledge of people in various fields, address issues. To do this, you can hold events from the employer or create your own group. Experienced psychologists write books that allow you to deal with many problems. Such literature is considered to be in demand in society.

Thus, the psychologist has the opportunity to improve in order to receive a good income. And in what area to work depends on desire. This profession should be completely liked, because only then will succeed.
