Are there tours to the Ostankino tower?

Ostankino television tower is one of the main symbols of the capital of our country. Not everyone knows that this magnificent architectural structure has an observation deck. Anyone can visit it, you only need to find out when excursions to the Ostankino tower are held.

What does the excursion program include?



A visit to this object is possible only as part of a group. Due to the large number of people who want to visit the unique observation deck, it is recommended to pre-book excursions on the Ostankino official website or by phone. The entrance ticket price includes one hour of stay at the observation deck, as well as a visit to the broadcasting museum and a detailed story about the history of the structure. How to get to the seventh heaven?

Excursions to the Ostankino tower are carried out only after registration of all members of the tourist group. Be sure to bring identification documents with you. Try not to be late - registration of excursion participants begins in half an hour, after which you need to pay entrance fees. In order to avoid misunderstandings, study in advance the rules of visiting the facility.

The standard mode of operation is from 10:00 to 21:00, on holidays it is possible to change the schedule of excursions. If you want to save on an excursion program, learn that morning sessions on weekdays are the cheapest . Climb to the observation deck with children is possible if they are already 7 years old.
