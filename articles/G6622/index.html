Where to get a loan in Novosibirsk without too much fuss and information

Getting a loan has long become commonplace and familiar to most people. Banks are offering more and more new options for processing a consumer loan. This variety is sometimes difficult to understand. It is for this reason that it is important to determine exactly where to get a loan in Novosibirsk.





Types of lending

Lending is one of the most popular services in the financing market. She is in great demand all over the world. There are several main types of obtaining a loan:

  • Consumer credit - the issuance of cash to the population for a specified period and at a certain percentage. Also, this type of financing includes the sale of goods by installments or on credit in stores.
  • Credit cards.
  • Mortgage - the issuance of a large sum of money with an initial contribution or without it for the acquisition of real estate.
  • Car loan - issuing loans to purchase a car.
  • Microloans - receiving a small amount of money for a short period. As a rule, the repayment of this loan occurs at the end of the term and in full, taking into account interest.

It remains to decide what type of loan is needed, as well as to decide where it is better to take a loan in Novosibirsk.





Where to get a loan in Novosibirsk

Currently, a cash loan can be obtained directly by contacting the branch of any bank specializing in the issuance of consumer loans. To do this, it is enough to collect all the necessary certificates and documents, and the employees of the financial institution will fill out an application and send it for consideration to the security department. As a rule, making a decision does not take much time. In most banks, this takes no more than 30 minutes. In certain cases, when the borrower asks for a large amount and takes time to verify the documents, it can take up to three days to consider the application.

You can apply online. To do this, just go to the official website of the bank you like and fill out everything yourself. The decision in this case will come by SMS or a bank employee will contact the borrower by phone.

Documents required to obtain a loan

But we need to talk about where in Novosibirsk you can get a loan with a minimum package of documents.

All banks mentioned above are ready to provide their potential borrowers with credit on favorable terms and with a minimum package of documents. It includes:

  • Passport of the borrower.
  • SNILS (certificate of state pension insurance).
  • INN
  • Certificate of income 2NDFL or in the form of a bank.

However, these documents alone are not enough. At the time of application, the potential borrower should not have overdue payments on credit obligations in third-party banks. Having a good credit history is an important, but not the most important factor affecting a positive decision. If the borrower meets all the requirements and has a full package, he can choose any bank he likes and receive money without refusal. Where to get a loan in Novosibirsk, you already know.
