From Kirov to Nizhny Novgorod: determine the best way to get there

A modern person can have a huge number of hobbies and interests. Traveling is one of them. Many people like to learn new things and conquer inaccessible heights. And why, for example, do people so often move from Kirov to Nizhny Novgorod? Because it is one of the oldest and most interesting cities in Russia with unique historical and cultural monuments. In this city you can get acquainted with ancient architecture, take a look at the country's largest staircase, located near the Kremlin, take a cable car ride, see a lot of other attractions and make a lot of other discoveries for yourself. And for the trip to bring joy and positive emotions, you need to take care of many little things. For example, choose the right vehicle.





Criterias of choice

What is the best way to get from Kirov to Nizhny Novgorod? There may be several ways. Their choice will depend on several factors:

  • Health status. Older people, pregnant women, passengers with children, people with disabilities and those who feel bad during the journey should prefer the train. First of all, this is due to the fact that on the way there is the opportunity to change position several times, lie down and walk. Agree that it is very convenient.
  • If you want to make the trip as informative as possible, then your choice should be directed towards the car. During the journey, you can stop and discover new places. If you wish, you can have a bite to eat at a roadside cafe, stop for the night at a hotel, or just take a walk around the city.
  • The cost of the trip is another important indicator. The cheapest form of transport is the bus, but such a trip can be the most tiring. If you want to save time, then preference should be given to the aircraft. However, the cost of the ticket will be significantly higher than for other vehicles.


Having decided on which method of transportation is the most optimal, you can proceed to drawing up a route from Kirov to Nizhny Novgorod.

Method 1. By car

The distance between these two settlements is only 609 kilometers. It is easy to guess that you can safely get from Kirov to Nizhny Novgorod by car. The total travel time will be only 10 hours, provided that the driver moves at a speed of about 80 kilometers per hour and manages to avoid traffic jams and stops. Unfortunately, it is impossible to calculate the approximate cost of the trip, since the price characteristics of gasoline are different, and each type of transport consumes an individual amount of fuel.





On the way you will need to cross the following settlements: Kuznetsy (distance is 46 kilometers), Experimental Field (distance - 230 kilometers), Semenov (distance - 530 kilometers). There you can relax, take a walk and have a bite to eat. It is worth noting that the route between these two cities is not the most successful, there are many bumpy gaps on it. Along its perimeter there are many gas stations.

Method 2 - by train

Unfortunately, today there is no direct train or train on which you can overcome the distance from Kirov to Nizhny Novgorod. But this type of transport is one of the most convenient for travel.



Therefore, you will need to make a transfer at the following stations: Moscow, Novorossiysk or Novy Urengoy. Departure takes place at 21:00, 01:30 and 03:45. Approximate travel time is only 3 hours 55 minutes. The ticket price varies from 550-1200 rubles per person, depending on the category.

It is worth considering that in the summer season there is a rush of tourists in this direction, so you should book places in advance.

Method 3. By bus

The longest way to overcome the distance from Kirov to Nizhny Novgorod is by bus. Direct flights are available only in the summer. The main suppliers of these services are representatives of travel agencies. Approximate travel time is about 12 hours 30 minutes. There are a huge number of options with transfers in major cities, namely: in Moscow, St. Petersburg, Kaluga, Ufa, Samara, Ulyanovsk and Volgograd. It is worth noting that the minimum cost per passenger will be about 4150 rubles one way.

Method 4. By Air

For many travelers, the favorite form of transport is the plane. Unfortunately, due to the relatively short distance, there is currently no direct flight between these two points. This distance can be overcome only with a transfer. The total travel time will be 1 hour 30 minutes. The minimum ticket price per passenger is approximately 6370 rubles.

As you can see, there are several options for how to get from Kirov to Nizhny Novgorod. The choice should be made on the basis of 3 main factors: state of health, financial capabilities, preferences and the amount of free time.
