How to return insurance after paying a loan? Return insurance: tips, tricks

When customers receive a loan from the bank, they are offered to take out insurance. The service reduces the risk of non-refund. Moreover, it is valid for mortgages and consumer loans. When an insured event occurs, the insurance company transfers funds to the bank. These relations are governed by the contract, which spells out the rights and obligations of the parties.





But many customers repay loans ahead of schedule, which eliminates the need for insurance. Not everyone knows how to get back credit insurance. This procedure has some features that need to be considered by all customers of banks.

History

In 2009, the Presidium of the Supreme Arbitration Court approved a resolution by which bank commissions related to measures that violate consumer rights. For several years there has been a review of cases of the return of illegally paid money. Banks made the decision to cancel commissions, which were considered important income.

Over time, rates for lending money have increased. When the Federal Law ‚ÄúOn Consumer Loans‚Äù came out, a new problem arose - the imposition of insurance when applying for loans. This increases the commission, and customers do not know their rights. Because of concerns about the denial of a loan, people take out insurance. Even if the contract was concluded, you need to know how to return the insurance on the loan.

Who needs this?

There is still a need for insurance. Banks receive money back security under different conditions. When an insured event occurs, the insurance company transfers funds to the bank. If the amount exceeds the balance of the debt, the amount is paid to the debtor. With a lack of funds, the bank will withhold money from the client.





Typically, life insurance, health insurance. This service is provided to protect real estate from adverse factors. They are prescribed in the contracts. When an insured event occurs, the client must notify the insurance company so that compensation is provided. And after repaying the loan, insurance is returned according to special rules.

Types of Insurance Policies

The insurance provides for reimbursement of losses in the event of the death of a client or personal injury resulting in problems with the fulfillment of obligations. The service is the most common in the banking sector. Upon death or injury, the insurer pays the loan balance to the bank, and the bank is the beneficiary.





Another type of policy is property insurance. It is considered mandatory when applying for a mortgage or buying a car on credit. In case of loss, damage, damage to the acquired property, the insurer pays the loan amount. That is, by law, insurance of an apartment against fires, bays is considered mandatory. But the client decides whether to choose insurance in case of death and health disorders. In this situation, the law is on his side.

Insurance Features

By law, citizens are not required to take out insurance. It turns out that the bank cannot force the service to be compiled as mandatory, but the law says that loans are not issued without life insurance. This applies to obtaining a mortgage and car loan. If the document is drawn up, payments are transferred constantly, the question arises, how to return insurance for consumer loans?

The beneficiary in this case is the bank. But, as we already said, if there was a full payment of the debt, then the client can issue a return. The bank performs the recalculation and transfer of money. The insurer can receive only compensation. Its size is proportional to the duration of the document.

If there was an early repayment of the loan, is it possible to return insurance? Yes, in this case a refund is possible. But if the debt is repaid on time, then the return of the funds will not work.

The insurance procedure in each case is different. Everything is determined by the type of loan. It can be consumer and collateral. In the first case, life insurance is performed for the client, and in the second case, property.

The condition for the transfer of funds is a statement. You can return Sberbank loan insurance if the client has a disease in which it is impossible to sign documents. Mortgage

Insurance Refund Principles

The law on the return of insurance after repayment of the loan regulates such matters. There are 2 methods to solve this problem. The first is a pre-trial settlement. When the loan is repaid, the return of insurance funds occurs through the company, which was engaged in registration. Do not contact the bank. A financial institution is considered an intermediary. For customer acquisition, he is paid interest.

After repayment of the loan, the insurance is returned to the insurance company. The client needs to write a statement in duplicate, after which they are registered. If the company is far away, you can send a registered letter. The document should indicate the period during which an answer is expected. Along with this, you need to order an extract from the personal account, which indicates how the money was distributed.
