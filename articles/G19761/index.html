Spa hotels in the Leningrad region: rating, description, reviews

Every body needs rest. This is what allows you to stay afloat and steadfastly endure all the difficulties of everyday life. The modern world is so saturated that time for recovery needs to be allocated more and more often, and this does not always work. In order to get quality relaxation, there are special places that offer restorative procedures for the body. One of the most popular of them is spa hotels. This is ideal for those who need a vacation and who are tired of the walls of their home. There are spa hotels in many large and especially resort cities. Their services allow you to get the necessary boost of strength, enjoy a relaxed atmosphere and quality procedures.

Spa hotels of the Leningrad Region is an opportunity to take refuge from the pressure of the surrounding world and feel completely free from its shackles. Everyone needs such a vacation, but before visiting such a place, you need to make sure that its service is at a good level. Holidays in a spa hotel in the Leningrad Region need to be planned in advance, and it is important to familiarize yourself with all the options.

1. "Forest Rhapsody"

Forest Rhapsody is headed by spa hotels in the Leningrad Region, which are rated below. This resort town is located in one of the most environmentally friendly areas of the Leningrad region. Near the hotel there is Lake Big Simanskoe, along the picturesque coast of which vacationers can arrange evening walks. The number of rooms includes apartments in the cottage "Lesnoy", a suite in the houses "Rhapsody" and "Lake", as well as rooms in a standard class hotel, junior suite and suite.

The guest reviews of this place are exceptionally positive, because there are two gourmet restaurants in the territory where you can taste dishes of European and Mediterranean cuisine, a bath and a sauna, a swimming pool, playrooms for children, conference rooms, a fitness room, massage room, equipment rental for outdoor activities and much more. Forest Rhapsody provides quality service, a variety of services and pastime opportunities, which is why it occupies the first place in the list of spa hotels in the Leningrad Region.

2. Aquaclub and hotel VODA

The spa hotels of the Leningrad Region with a swimming pool necessarily include VODA, even its name speaks of it. This water and entertainment club is located in Sestroretsk. Within its walls, they truly appreciate the water element and offer vacationers comfort and quality relaxation, spending their leisure time in conjunction with water. In the main building there is the hotel itself, an incredibly large pool, saunas, a SPA center and even a water dance floor.

Thanks to modern technology, hospitality, the friendliness of the staff and the beneficial properties of water, the aqua club and the VODA hotel are the ideal place for a club, friendly or family holiday. Thanks to a bold design solution, the walls of the hotel are pleasing to the eye and cause sincere admiration. There are not very many rooms, but they are all carefully designed both from the design and from the practical side.

3. Country club "Scandinavia"

Country spa hotels in the Leningrad Region are a chance to turn away from the noise and speed of everyday life and touch nature. "Scandinavia" - a country club, which is one of the most popular resort places in the region. Luxury, high-quality service, the benefits of fresh air and natural surroundings, healing waters and amazing attention to guests - this is how this hotel appears to vacationers. Spa treatments include pastimes in the Finnish sauna, hammam, Russian wood-fired bathhouse, heated outdoor pool and jacuzzi.

The number of rooms is 76 rooms, among which are both standard and rooms with special luxury. According to vacationers, the Scandinavia country club is an unforgettable vacation in which everyone finds harmony and tranquility.
