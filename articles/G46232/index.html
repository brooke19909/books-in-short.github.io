Hotels in Ruskeale (Karelia)

The Ruskeala Mountain Park is a small but incredibly beautiful tourist complex located near the Finnish border, in the territory of Karelia. Tourists from all over the world come here to see the legendary marble canyon, fly above it tied by a cable, take a boat ride and visit the Ruskeala waterfalls. So, from which hotels in Ruskealy should you choose when traveling to a mountain park? Of course, the place where you plan to stay should have a convenient location, be clean and comfortable, the cost of living in a hotel also plays an important role. We picked up the 9 best hotels in Ruskeale, according to tourists who were fortunate enough to spend time in this corner of our country.

Hostel "Ruskeala"

In Ruskeale, among the hotels near the city center, the closest located hostel is Ruskeala. Moreover, the mountain park can be reached by car from the hostel in just 5 minutes. Free parking is available on the territory of Ruskeala to accommodate a car. Accommodation conditions are suitable for those who have a limited budget and are looking for a place to stay for one night, and those who want to rent a double room for a longer period. The hostel has everything you need for a short stay: microwave, washing machine, fridge, coffee machine, kettle and dishes.

On the territory of the accommodation facility there is a gazebo with benches and barbecue facilities for lovers of barbecue. The hostel is also suitable for those traveling with pets, in order to stay with a pet, you must inform the administrator in advance. The cost of a bed in a four-bed dormitory room will cost 900 rubles, and a double room will cost 2000 rubles per night. Hotel "Ruskeala" is located at: School street, house 22.

Camping "Forest Glade"

Among the hotels "Ruskealy" located near the city center, guests of Karelia especially fell in love with "Forest Glade". Camping is located at: st. Shkolnaya, 6, 5 minutes walk from the center. The campsite has 3 holiday homes for 2-3 people and a villa. On the territory there are also gazebos, hammocks, barbecue facilities and a campfire place. Facilities are located outside the houses. Marble quarry and waterfalls are just a 10-minute drive from Lesnaya Polyana Camping. 600 meters from the camping there is a lake where you can swim and sunbathe in the summer season. There is also a small grocery store nearby. The houses have a microwave, kettle, refrigerator, multicooker, heater, TV. During the cold season, the administration includes underfloor heating. One night in the "Forest Glade" will cost you 7,000 rubles. The beauty of nature, silence and proximity to the lake and forest will forever be remembered by anyone who arrives.

Guest House "King of the Hill"

One of the most popular Ruskeala hotels is the cozy guest house Tsar Gory, located in a picturesque place on Lisopilnaya Street, house 1a, on the river bank. Ruskeala Park of the same name is just a 10-minute walk away. The guest house offers a wide selection of rooms for families, as well as houses with a kitchen for noisy companies. On the territory of the hotel are located: braziers, tents, swings, gazebos, barbecue places, a children's corner. The area is popular for hiking. You can rent a bike, ATV or snowmobile from the property. Room prices start at 2200 rubles. Tsar Gory is an ideal choice among Ruskeala hotels near natural beauties for lovers of peace and fresh air.

Farm "Marble Mountain"

Another hotel "Ruskealy", which will definitely make you fall in love with the amazing nature of Karelia, is the "Marble Mountain" farm, densely surrounded by forest. Mramornaya Gora houses are located in a forest near ski slopes and Ruskeala Park on more than 3 hectares at Lesopilnaya Street, 7. The hotel is an ideal choice for fans of skiing and those who love outdoor recreation. Each house is designed for 4-5 guests, and it has everything you need for an emotional pastime, including a place for a bonfire. The territory also has a newly built bathhouse for lovers of steam. The cost of living in "Marble Mountain" - from 7000 rubles per night.

Holiday Village "Ruskeala"

One of the most popular Ruskeala hotels near the Marble Canyon in Karelia is the Ruskeala recreation center. The complex is located on the shores of Lake Hanki, not far from the Ruskeala waterfalls, and the Marble Canyon is located just 2 km from the recreation center. On the territory of the complex there are 2 hotel buildings, designed for 100 guests. Both buildings were recognized as historical monuments, which is why the administration cannot accommodate amenities in the rooms, so the bathrooms are shared and are located on the floor. On the territory of the recreation center there are: a tent camp, cafe, 2 baths and picnic places with barbecue. The cost of a bed in a common room for 8 people is 700 rubles, and the price for a night in a double room is 3000 rubles. The price of a tent for 4 people at a campsite is 500 rubles. For the placement of the animal will need to pay 200 rubles. At the hotel you can rent a boat, cheesecakes for skiing in the snow, skis and skates for rent. Base Address: 4 Hanki.

Guest houses "Fortuna"

1 km from the Ruskealy center, on Lake Hanki, is the territory of the Fortuna guest houses, which houses 3 four-bed houses (3,500 rubles per night) and 2 houses by the lake (5,000 rubles per night). At the disposal of guests of the complex in each house there is a flat-screen TV with cable TV, a computer, and Hi-Fi equipment. The kitchen has a stove, microwave, kettle and fridge. In addition, for the smallest on the territory there is a children's playground. Outdoor activities include barbecue facilities and a private beach area. Guests can go fishing nearby. Visitors to the hotel especially note the cleanliness and large size of the rooms, but the lack of Internet access is a bit confusing for tourists.

Mini-Hotel "Franz Elof Svennson"

Hotel Franz Elof Svennson is located 4 km from the center of Ruskealy, in the village of Sakhankoski, on the 27th km of the Sortavala highway. Near the hotel there is a private indoor beach, near which you can go fishing or canoeing. The guests of the hotel note the beautiful views from the windows of the hotel, the friendly and polite staff, as well as the good location between the canyon and the zoo. The price of a room in a room for 6 people is 700 rubles; the price of a single room with a lake view is 1200 rubles; budget double room will cost 2000 rubles; a triple room with 3 single beds will cost 3300 rubles.

Camping Soul Camp

There you can learn to survive in the wild, navigate the terrain, work in a team and just spend time actively. Soul Camp is a comfortable campsite equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. Camping guests note a space as close to nature as possible for outdoor activities with family and friends. The camp is completely autonomous and environmentally friendly, runs on solar energy and does not harm the environment. All tents are decorated in a pleasant indistyle, equipped with furnaces for heating, each of them accommodates 4 people. Near the tent there is a table, a fireplace, a barbecue and comfortable wooden chairs. Matches, candles, towels and bed linen are provided. Also, firewood at the base is free and unlimited. The area is popular for fishing and canoeing. The cost of one night is from 6000 rubles.

"Fox Mountain"

Guest house "Fox Mountain" is located in "Ruskeale" at the address: 86K-332 9. The tourist site began to work in April this year, so tourists especially note the quality service, cleanliness, comfort and coziness in the house. 200 meters from the guest house is the restaurant Ruskolka, where you can spend time both in the family circle and in a large company and taste delicious and hearty dishes.

All rooms have a flat-screen TV. A kettle and microwave are also featured. Some have a private terrace. Nearby the guest house, on the Tohmajoki River, you can go fishing or go hiking in the surroundings. The Ruskeala Mountain Park is just 100 meters away. On the territory of the "Fox Mountain" is allowed to stay with pets. The cost of one night is 3000 rubles.

As you can see, a large selection of hotels in Ruskeale, close to the main attractions, will satisfy the requirements of any traveler and will allow you to have an unforgettable time.
