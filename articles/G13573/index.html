Sandunes Beach Resort 4 * (Vietnam / Phan Thiet): hotel reviews

Now that Egypt and Turkey are practically excluded from organized tourism, many who want to relax on the warm sea choose Vietnam. One of the best hotels in this country is Sandunes Beach Resort 4, which has already been visited by many of our compatriots.

What do we know about Vietnam

The days when Vietnam was mentioned in connection with the long American war, napalm and exorbitant human sacrifices have long been a thing of the past.

The Indochina Peninsula, on which Vietnam is located, is washed by the South China Sea, and the closest neighbors are China, Laos and Cambodia. The form of government is a republic, and the Communist Party rules everything.

The Vietnamese are hardworking, welcoming and friendly. They treat everyone who speaks Russian with special warmth, recalling the Soviet Union with a kind word.





At all hotels - including Sandunes Beach Resort 4 - you can hear speech in Vietnamese, French, Chinese and Russian.

When is the best time to go to Vietnam

The high season lasts from April to November. Locals emit dry and wet seasons. The dry season is from December to April, at which time it rains very little. Wet - from May to November, during this period it often rains, usually at night. The rains are short, not more than an hour, but the time of their appearance is a secret of nature.

Resort Phan Thiet

Almost all of Vietnam is stretched on the coast of the sea, so the preferred form of relaxation here is beach. Phan Thiet is located in the south of the country, and between it and the village of Mui Ne there is a part of the coast on which all the best hotels are concentrated. From here, the capital of Ho Chi Minh City is only 200 km away.

The beaches of the resort are a constant topic of discussion. Some do not like the fact that the sand is very fine, palm trees are far from the shore, and the water is unclear from the waves, while it does not annoy others. All that distinguishes the weather of different months from each other is the amount of rain. It is always warm here, on the coldest night + 25 o C.

Hotel Features

Sandunes Beach Resort 4 (Phan Thiet) is located in a place known for its unique microclimate. Astronomers explored this paradise, here in 1995 the best conditions on the planet for observing a solar eclipse were created. After their observations, experts traveled around the world, and from them learned about pink dunes, a colorful port, eternal beautiful weather and quiet corners for relaxing under palm trees.





It is best to relax here for couples with children, since the main advantages of the resort are beaches, peace and the warm sea.

Those who are afraid of local sand fleas should better calm down: finding a real insect on the beach is not so easy.
