General Description and Historical Background

This hotel is located in one of the cleanest and quietest places in the village of Lazarevskoye. This beautiful red brick building stands on the site of a former fortress. In 1839, a coastal fortification was laid on the territory of a small settlement. The order was given by Admiral M.P. Lazarev, in whose honor the village was named.



The founded fortification became part of the Black Sea coastline and existed until 1854. After Russia was defeated in the Crimean War, it was almost completely destroyed, but the ruins can be seen today. It was on the site of this destroyed fortification that the construction of the Fort Arthur Hotel (Lazarevskoye) was started, which was completed in 2002 . It got its name thanks to the close proximity to the fortress. The three-star status of the hotel guarantees a comfortable stay with a large number of services. The peculiarity is that ‚ÄúFort Arthur‚Äù is a non-smoking hotel, which is ideal for families with children.

There is a cafe for guests at the Fort Arthur Hotel (Lazarevskoye). Meals are not included in the price, but you can have lunch or dinner at very affordable prices.
