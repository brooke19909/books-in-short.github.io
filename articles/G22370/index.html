Recreation center in Anapa "Olympic Village"

A trip to the sea is perceived by many exclusively as a beach holiday. However, you should not take the resort only as clean beaches and a warm climate. If you wish, you can usefully spend your vacation and learn a lot of interesting things about the place where you plan to spend your vacation.

Why you need to go to Anapa

Perhaps this is the best place to stay. The proximity to the Taman Peninsula and the foothills of the Greater Caucasus made the climate here very mild and warm. The region rich in historical sights will not leave indifferent any tourist. It is worth visiting the ancient "Russian Gate", which remained from the time of the Ottomans. It took more than 40 years (1788-1828) and six campaigns to conquer this territory from Turkey.

History lovers can visit the archaeological site, an open-air reserve. It is located next to the "Russian Gate".

From the Olympic village in Anapa you can go for an evening walk along Tersky Street, which stretches along the entire city. Children will appreciate the small red electric locomotive, which carries tourists through the picturesque area.

Where to stay in Anapa

The transformation of the city cannot be underestimated. If earlier reviews about it were not the best, now everything is different. Perhaps the presence of a cottage complex called the Olympic Village in Anapa played a role. Thanks to past world sports games, centers have appeared here for outdoor enthusiasts. Diving, rock climbing, fishing - this is only part of what the Olympic Village in Anapa offers. If possible, from here you can go on an excursion to Gelendzhik, Sochi. Holidays in Dzhemete

You can settle in comfort not only in Anapa, the Olympic Village in Dzhemete is ready to meet your guests as true Olympians. The complex consists of small but comfortable one-story cottages. Only 500 meters to the sea, pebble beach. And if you want to move, then from Dzhemete to Anapa to go not so far. This can be appreciated by tourists who move in their car. However, public transport runs regularly.

In Dzhemete there is everything for relaxation: restaurants, cafes, a market. You can take equipment and go camping. And for connoisseurs of real wine - old cellars. You can stay in the Olympic Village, and see Anapa as a tour.
