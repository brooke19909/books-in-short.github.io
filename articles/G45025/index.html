Openwork sleeveless crochet sleeve knits immediately as a whole. To do this, make the necessary measurements and a whole pattern of two halves of the front and back between them. A continuous pattern of pineapples will go to the armpit line, and then a mesh pattern. Then the sleeveless jacket is sewn in the shoulder lines and tied. Consider the pineapple pattern first.

First row

  • Gather a chain of loops equal to the volume of the waist.
  • Start the row with the lifting loops. In the same base, knit four columns with one crochet (you get half a pineapple).
  • Gather one loop and attach it with a connecting column in the sixth loop of the previous row (PR).
  • Then there are three arches. Pick up three loops. Connecting column enter the fourth loop of the PR.
  • Knit four loops and insert the hook into the 5th loop of the PR.
  • Then again comes the arch of three loops.
  • Pick up the air loop (VP). Skip five loops of PR and in the sixth loop you knit 9 crochet columns for pineapple. Then repeat everything.

Turn over the work (this is a double-sided crochet sleeveless jacket). Patterns begin and end with half a pineapple.

Second row

  • Pineapple fits in the same double crochet (CH), just add 1 VP between them. Get five columns.
  • Do 1 VP and connect it to the OL column between the arches of 3 and 4 loops. Then dial 2 CH, 1 loop, 2 CH (2 StN, 1VP, 2StN).
  • Then again do 2 StN, 1 VP, 2 StN in the connecting column of the previous row between the arches of 4 and 3 loops. Gather VP and proceed to knitting pineapple.

Third row

  • Pineapple begins with arches of three loops connected by a connecting column.
  • Collect 3 loops, go to the loop separating the first element (2 StN, 1 VP, 2 StN), and knit 2 CH, make a loop.
  • Knit another 2 CH, but insert the hook into the loop of the second motive (2 STN, 1 VP, 2 STN). Gather 3 VP and go to knitting pineapple.

In fact, the above crochet patterns are represented by knitting a pineapple and branches from it.



Fourth and fifth rows

  • Gather 3 VP and knit 2StN, 1 VP, 2 STN in the middle loop between 2 columns of PR.
  • Do 3 VP and knit pineapple.

Sixth row

  • Type 3 VP and go to the pineapple.

Seventh row

  • 2 stn, 1 VP, 2 stn in the middle of the first loop of the element (2 stn, 1 vp, 2 stn, 1 vp, 2 stn), 1 vp and knit 2 stn, 1 vp, 2 stn in the second loop of this element.
  • Next come 3 VP and pineapple.

This crochet sleeveless shirt for a teenage girl is knitted from square motifs. Each of them consists of ten rows. First take measurements and draw patterns. Next, tie a pattern of motive. Calculate how many whole squares are needed. If there are extra centimeters, then scatter them on the strapping of sleeveless halves of the front, bottom, neck, armhole.

Square motive pattern

  • Each row begins with three lifting loops (PP). Next is a double crochet (CH), 9 VP, 2 CH. Repeat thrice. All rows end with a connecting loop.
  • Close three loops in the ring.
  • 3 PP, 1 CH, 3 VP, 6 CH, 3 VP, 2 CH. Repeat thrice.
  • 3 PP, 1 CH, 4 VP, 1 CH, * 1 VP, 1 CH * - repeat 4 times, 4 VP. Repeat first 3 times.
  • With the help of connecting posts, move two loops to the left. 3 VP, 2 CH, 5 VP, connecting loop (SP), * 4 VP, SP * - repeat three times, 5 VP, 3 CH (knit on the air loops of the previous row), 3 VP. Repeat thrice.

Process completion

  • 8 VP, 3 CH, 5 VP, connecting loop, * 4 VP, SP * - repeat 2 times, 5 VP, 3 CH, 5 VP, CH (in the middle of the arch between 6 CH of the previous row). Repeat thrice.
  • 8 VP, SP (in the middle of the arch of the previous row), 5 VP, 3 CH, 5 VP, SP, * 4 VP, SP * - repeat 1 time, 5 VP, 3 CH, 5 loops, SP (in the middle of the arches of the previous row ), 5 VP, SP, 5 VP, 3 CH. Repeat three more times.
  • 8 VP, SP, 5 loops, SP, 5 VP, 3 CH, 5 VP, SP, 4V, CH, 5 VP, 3 CH, * 5 VP, SP * - repeat 1 more time, 5 VP, 3 CH. Repeat three times.
  • 8 VP, SP, * 5 VP, SP * - 1 more time, 5 VP, 3 CH, 5 VP, SP, 5 VP, 3 CH, 5 VP, SP, 5 VP, CH, * 5 VP, SP * - repeat 1 time, 5 VP, 3 CH. Repeat thrice.
  • 8 VP, SP, * 5 VP, SP * - 2 more times, 5 VP, 3 CH, 9 VP, 3 CH, * 5 VP, SP * - 4 more times, 5 VP, 3 CH. Repeat thrice.
