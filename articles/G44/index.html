The recreation center "Beach" in Kabardinka: the best vacation for everyone

Kabardinka resort is no less popular than other famous places for recreation. It is here that the season begins much earlier, and lasts longer than in other places. Beautiful nature and fresh air attract a large number of people. The recreation center "Coast" in Kabardinka attracts tourists not only from all over Russia, but also from other countries. After all, here you can relax perfectly, enjoying the sea.

General information

The recreation center "Beach" in Kabardinka invites guests and offers them excellent conditions for living. You can come here with children or a large company or alone. A separate bonus is the fact that they are allowed to take their pets to the base. Therefore, animals will not be bored at home without owners. Regarding nutrition, residents decide for themselves. They can visit the cafe in the village or cook on their own in the shared kitchen. There is wireless Internet on the territory, so you can always go online. A grill is also available for guests, which allows you to cook meat, fish, vegetables on charcoal.

There are several accommodation options available for guests. It can be an economical vacation in a room for four people. It is located in a bungalow, it has four beds and other necessary furniture. It also has a TV, air conditioning and a fridge. Facilities are located in the courtyard. When placing in a house, keep in mind that all amenities are also outside the house. It has two single beds, a sofa for two people, a table, a nightstand and a wardrobe. For added convenience, air conditioning, a fridge and a TV are also provided. To get to your own beach, you need to walk only 30 meters, and a little further there is a beach belonging to another boarding house.
