Operating Instructions: Operating Modes

The drive on the top of the camera contains the following camera control options:

  • Smart Auto - automatic scene recognition, in which the camera compares what is in front of the lens with the on-board database, and then immediately before recording the image compares this information with the distance to the object, white balance, contrast, dynamic range, lighting and color to choose the best storyline. In this case, the user can only turn on and off the flash.
  • Program - automatic exposure mode with limited manual settings (sensitivity, white balance, exposure compensation and flash).
  • ASM - allows you to manually set the exposure with aperture priority when the photographer selects the aperture, and the camera - the appropriate shutter speed, and shutter priority, in which the camera independently selects the desired aperture.
  • Fully manual mode, allowing the selection of all exposure parameters.
  • Smart Scene sets the scene that best fits the subject.
  • Best Face is a comprehensive face recognition mode.
  • Choice of effects - Low Light, HDR, Split Shot, etc.
  • Custom settings mode with the ability to set your own shooting settings.
  • Wi-Fi - allows you to share images.

Shooting efficiency

Turning on the camera takes ages 3.4 seconds, but then the camera‚Äôs performance is noticeably accelerated.

Automatic exposure in Smart Auto and Program modes is reliable and impressively fast. The Samsung WB350F smart camera is equipped with a fairly standard contrast AF system with central, multi and tracking AF. In this case, the scene is analyzed, the distance to the object is calculated, the nearest point is determined and the focus is fixed on it. The central AF option is perfect for portraits and traditional landscapes, as well as for street photography, since it does not require a face selection. All this takes no more than 0.1 s, and a series of 6 pictures is recorded at a speed of 7.1 fps.





Backlight

Pressing the flash button and puts it in the working position. It is attached to a metal folding mechanism that raises it almost 25 mm above the top panel. Since the light source is located to the left of the axis of the lens, the problem of red eyes should be significantly less.

Samsung WB350F's small multi-mode pop-up camera flash is praised by users for an acceptable range of lighting options, including automatic, automatic with red-eye reduction, fill-flash, slow sync, red-eye removal, and manual. It also allows you to produce backlighting with reflected light - just hold it at the required angle with the index finger of your left hand (while holding the other camera). This feature greatly enhances portraiture capabilities. According to user reviews, the flash reload time is 3-4 s.

Vibration compensation

A particularly important functional feature of the Samsung WB350F is called image stabilization, which provides a longer zoom, since it is almost impossible to hold the camera still at maximum magnification. Image blur is eliminated by quickly and accurately offsetting the vibration-compensating sensor. Image stabilization reduces shutter speed by 3 steps. It is also useful when shooting without a flash in dimly lit rooms or in situations where the presence of an additional light source would be too obvious.





Duration of work

The battery life of the lithium-ion battery SLB-10A with a capacity of 1030 mAh approximately corresponds to the average value for cameras of this type. The instructions for the Samsung WB350F indicate the number of 310 shots, which corresponds to 155 minutes of photo or 120 video recordings. The battery is charged inside the camera - an external charger is not included, but is offered as an optional accessory (along with a case, an A / V cable and a memory card with an adapter). The battery can be charged via a USB port from a computer or using the supplied AC cable.

The WB350F saves JPEG photos and high-definition video to MicroSD storage media.

When you turn on the camera, the lens extends from the housing, and when it is turned off, it retracts back. The built-in cover closes to protect the front lens. The zoom is smooth, fairly fast and relatively quiet, especially for such a telephoto lens. Video quality

Samsung WB350F Black captures HD video in MP4 format with a resolution of 1080p or 720p at a speed of 30 fps, and you can change the zoom while shooting a video.
