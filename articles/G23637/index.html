A bit about the location

This hotel is located in Side, in the immediate vicinity of its center. If you look at the hotel relative to the beaches, the distance to the nearest is 800 meters. This segment can be easily overcome in 15 minutes of uneasy walking. The building of a four-star hotel itself is very beautiful and causes only positive emotions when you see it. Beige and dark red colors were chosen as the primary colors for the building. This combination gives tourists a sense of comfort. In order to get to the hotel, it is necessary to overcome a fairly large distance. The fact is that Side does not have its own airport, so you can get to the Side West Park Hotel 4 * only through Antalya. Flights to Antalya are from almost every city in Russia, so there should not be any particular difficulty in flying to this city of Turkey. Upon arrival at the Turkish airport, be prepared for the fact that you have to choose between a bus and a taxi. The first option is more budget, but at the same time less fast and comfortable. A taxi can deliver you to the hotel, spending no more than an hour. The bus will take about an hour and a half. There is also the opportunity to take advantage of a special service that is provided by the hotel. It is called a transfer and lies in the fact that at the airport of Antalya a hotel car will be waiting for you, which can calmly take you to it.



Hotel facilities

The Side West Park Hotel 4 * hotel (photo above) is very large and has very comfortable rooms for its guests. They also have a certain list of services that they can safely use. On the bed you can find new linen, and in the bathroom a new clean towel and a working hair dryer. Food and drink

The hotel has its own restaurant, which provides its guests with a free breakfast, lunch and dinner in the form of a buffet. In other words, everyone can take for themselves the dish that he likes. If you want to taste alcoholic beverages, then on the territory of Side West Park Hotel 4 * there is a bar in which you can find all the popular alcohol, such as vodka, beer, wine and brandy.

Sports and recreation

The hotel is a four-star hotel, which means it must have a large complex for playing sports. In addition, the hotel has a large outdoor pool, which offers its guests the opportunity to lie down and sunbathe near it, as well as plunge into the refreshing water. The distance to the beach is only 800 meters, so if you wish, you can always go to the shores of the Mediterranean Sea and relax there.



Side West Park Hotel 4 *: tourist reviews

According to numerous reviews of people who rested in this particular hotel, we can conclude that it is decent and fully consistent with the price that it requires.
