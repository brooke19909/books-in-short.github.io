Detailed review of the Ginzzu R8 Dual

The manufacturer of the secure smartphone Ginzzu is part of Magic Corporation, based in China. The company is engaged in the manufacture of components for PCs, audio equipment and all kinds of computer accessories. Since 2009, the main focus has been the production of video recorders, security systems and rugged smartphones. Such mobile devices are budget, they can be purchased at a very reasonable price.

Of course, it cannot be said that the smartphone is equipped with all the latest technological innovations, a glossy body and a well-known logo on the panel, but its functionality is likely to appeal to fans of active sports, travelers and extreme sports.

Supply



The colorful packaging box shows an SUV that overcomes water and dirt. Near it is the mobile device itself, its name and the international standard IP 67, which indicates the presence of protection against water, exposure to high temperatures and damage during impacts.

In the package you can find a Ginzzu R8 Dual smartphone, a battery, a network adapter, a headset, a USB interface cable and a user manual. In addition, an unusual part is included in the kit, in appearance it is very similar to an army token, it is designed to work with the communicator in the winter.

Overview

In appearance, the Ginzzu R8 Dual is not much different from other protected devices. The case is made of plastic, which can withstand falling from a two-meter height and immersion in water. Along the entire side perimeter of the smartphone, manufacturers placed various plugs-gaskets. The back cover reliably holds on two screws that stand out strongly on the smooth surface of the case.

The device has bright orange inserts, with their help you can quickly find your smartphone in the mud or thick grass. The case consists entirely of matte plastic, such a coating is very practical, because it does not show scratches or dirt, and the Ginzzu R8 Dual communicator is comfortable to hold in your hand. The protective glass covers not only the screen of the mobile device, but also the lower function keys. On the one hand, it is more convenient to work on a smartphone, but on the other, there is absolutely no protection against accidental damage or scratching the display.





In general, the screen could not pleasantly impress, it does not have much resolution. Let's see how users rate the Ginzzu R8 Dual smartphone: reviews tell us that viewing angles are not the best, but the color rendering is good and the contrast is high, you can view files even in direct sunlight.

Control

The smartphone is controlled by four touch keys located at the bottom of the screen. At the top of the Ginzzu R8 Dual, we can see the power button of the device and the volume key. Activation is tight enough to avoid accidental clicks. The volume control key has not been slightly modified, it protrudes much beyond the body and can be damaged. The connector for the connecting cable is located deep in the casing of the mobile device; an elongated plug is included for connecting it.

Side view



Under the back cover of the Ginzzu R8 Dual communicator is a battery, slots for two SIM cards and an additional memory card up to 32 GB.

A distinctive feature is the status indicator of the smartphone, which can not be found in all models of protected devices. The device has a navigator and multimedia.

A 5 megapixel camera allows you to take pictures of acceptable quality outdoors. The 1800 mAh capacitive battery at maximum load can withstand up to seven hours of continuous operation, and in standby mode the device will last up to three days without recharging. These are pretty good features for a secure smartphone.
