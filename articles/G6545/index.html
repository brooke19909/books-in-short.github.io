Sunset Hotel Sharm (Egypt, Sharm El Sheikh): hotel description, reviews of tourists

Sunset Hotel Sharm is a small hotel in the resort of Sharm El Sheikh, which will delight you with its hospitality and quality service.

Types of guest rooms

Guests of the Sunset Hotel Sharm Resort can choose from various options for comfortable accommodation. The hotel offers:

  • 88 standard rooms, 62 of which overlook the pool, and the remaining 26 overlook the flowering green garden. This is a small one-room apartment, in which accommodation takes place on double or twin beds.
  • 2 rooms with family status due to its spacious area and convenient layout. These are two full bedrooms, each of which has its own bathroom. In one part of the room there is a double bed, and in the second - twin.
  • For those who are accustomed to relax with luxury - 2 elegant suites. This apartment can rightfully be proud of the described hotel. Photos of tourists testify to their sophisticated modern design. They represent a spacious bedroom, which, in addition to beds, is also equipped with upholstered furniture. The bathroom includes a spa bath.




The amenities provided in the apartment

Many modern amenities are provided at Sunset Hotel Sharm for the comfort of guests. It is worth highlighting the following:

  • a small furnished balcony with clothes dryer or a similar terrace, if we are talking about rooms located on the ground floor;
  • a small kitchen or kitchenette, where there is everything necessary for cooking and serving food;
  • a modern bathroom, which, depending on the class of apartments, will be equipped with a shower or bathtub (hairdryer, bath accessories and cosmetics are provided without fail);
  • a large wardrobe where you can place all your belongings;
  • a illuminated mirror in front of which there is a small dressing table for your cosmetics and accessories;
  • individual air conditioning system that allows you to independently adjust the air temperature in your apartment;
  • TV broadcasts a huge package of satellite channels, among which there are mostly music and entertainment (Russian-speaking also available);
  • the phone gives you the opportunity not only to call the reception, but also to make a call abroad;
  • in the minibar you will always be waiting for your favorite drinks;
  • the safety of valuables, documents and money can be entrusted to a safe with a combination lock;
  • due to the fact that the room is equipped with an iron with an ironing board, you can always tidy your things.

Resort Infrastructure

Hotel Sunset offers you the following infrastructure:

  • large outdoor pool with clear, clear water;
  • You can visit a large restaurant with international cuisine, as well as a bar with a huge selection of drinks;
  • large playground for children;
  • car parking, where you can leave a rented vehicle;
  • a shop with a huge selection of souvenirs made by local craftsmen;
  • tourist training center diving;
  • a special small shuttle that transfers guests to the beach;
  • a medical office, where, if necessary, call a professional doctor;
  • You can always return your soiled laundry to the laundry.
  • billiard club;
  • own sandy beach, equipped with all necessary amenities for relaxation.

Negative reviews

From the negative reviews about the hotel Sunset Sharm 3 * (Sharm El Sheikh), the following can be distinguished:

  • the beach is very far away (its distance from the hotel is about 3 km);
  • even if you arrive at the hotel hungry at night, no one will offer you dinner;
  • there are a lot of mosquitoes in the rooms, and cockroaches are sometimes seen;
  • large lizards crawl along the walls of the corridors;
  • the area near the pool is very small, and the water from the tank itself, by the way, smells very much bleach;
  • all day, as well as in the evenings, music plays very loudly by the pool;
  • the employees of the bar located on the beach are very defiant with tourists who bring water and drinks with them (most of all it affects women, since apparently they do not want to get involved with men);
  • the hotel has expensive and very weak Internet (for tourists, vacationing in Egypt, it is recommended to buy cards of local operators and use a mobile network);
  • since the beach area is very small, the sunbeds on it are located back to back;
  • massage is done in bad faith, despite the rather high cost.
