How to get to the market and where is it located?

By car, reaching the cherished goal is not at all difficult. Building materials are located on the Moscow Ring Road. Those who live in the southern district of Moscow will arrive here in a few minutes. Of course, it is important to consider traffic jams, as they significantly extend the travel time. If snowfall began in the capital, as always unexpectedly, then it is better not to take trips that day. It is also undesirable to visit the markets of the Kashira courtyard from 5 p.m. to 7 p.m., since at this time the traffic in this section may stall. The rest of the time, traveling here is much easier.





Motorists will be able to get to "Kashirsky Dvor-1", having approached the intersection of Kolomensky passage and Kashirsky highway. Kashirsky Dvor-2 spread its arms on Kashirskoye Shosse, Building 1. At the intersection of the Warsaw Highway and the Moscow Ring Road, Kashirsky Dvor-3 is located.

Those traveling on their own should first get to the Nagatinskaya station and take buses No. 164, 275, 298, 742. The same buses will be home from the Kashirskaya metro station, and No. 219 and 220 from the Kolomenskaya metro station.

The construction market "Kashirsky Dvor-1" is open from 9 a.m. to 7 p.m. If he was visited by a wholesale buyer, he can use the service of cargo delivery. You can send up to 15 thousand tons of goods to their destination.
