Hotel Club Selen Hotel Icmeler 3 *, Marmaris: description and reviews of tourists

Club Selen Hotel (ex. Selen 2 Hotel) 3 * (Turkey / Icmeler) in the reviews of tourists is often referred to as a very decent place to stay, located near the Mediterranean coast. There is always hot weather and conditions favorable for a great vacation in a tropical atmosphere. A huge number of trips to this hotel every year are sold to travelers by tour operators. The travelers themselves admit that they choose this place for their vacation because the price of the tour at Club Selen Hotel Icmeler 3 * (Selen Hotel) is pleasantly pleasing - the cost of a ticket to this place is about 55-65 thousand rubles, which is acceptable for many Russian inhabitants.

general information

Almost all travel agents recommend this place for a family vacation, justifying this by the fact that the hotel provides a considerable number of conditions for living with children from the age of 4 years.

Club Selen Hotel Icmeler, in accordance with international standards, has the status of "three stars", however, as tourists vacationing here report, for the range of opportunities provided, it deserves a much higher status.

Location

Club Selen Hotel Icmeler is located in a very picturesque place in the town of Icmeler, which belongs to the large resort region of Marmaris. It is not long to get to the center of the district from here - the settlements are separated by a short distance - 7 kilometers, overcoming of which by car will take only 7-10 minutes. Marmaris has a large number of entertainment venues, including cafes, restaurants and nightclubs. The coastline is 300 meters from the hotel, which can be overcome on foot in five minutes.

The nearest air terminal is in the city of Dalaman - a rather impressive distance of 100 km from the hotel to the airport. That is why, for the convenience of tourists, the hotel provides a transfer service in the standard package of each tour. In reviews of the Club Selen Hotel Icmeler 3 * it is often noted that it turns out to be a modern passenger car that is equipped with air conditioning.

Rooms

All rooms that are available in the hotel fund are located in two buildings. In total, their number totals 81. All available apartments are divided into two types: "Standard" and "Family", depending on the proposed living conditions.

Each room must have installed modern climatic equipment, which allows residents living in it to independently set the temperature regime, regardless of what climatic conditions are currently on the street.

The rooms have private bathrooms, each of them is combined with a bathroom. They have bathing areas in the form of a shower or bathtub. In addition, there is a sink. The bathroom has a furniture set, consisting of two curbstones - in which guests await towels and bathrobes, as well as a set of soap accessories.

Standard

Standard class apartments are designed to accommodate a couple of guests, which is why they are often chosen by those who came to rest with their loved one. In their reviews, guests often note that the design of the rooms is done in bright colors, which visually give lightness to the overall picture of the interior. They also note the excellent technical equipment of the rooms: there is a telephone, a safe, and a plasma TV on which satellite channels are shown, many of which are Russian-speaking, which is especially pleasing to tourists from the CIS countries.

As for furniture, Standard rooms are quite well-equipped. Each of them has a double bed with a high-quality orthopedic mattress, as well as hypoallergenic linen made of expensive material. The room has a large mirror and a dressing table, and it also has a working area in the form of a desk and a chair made of dark wood. For guests' things, a wardrobe, as well as small shelves are provided.



Family

In reviews of the Club Selen Hotel Icmeler 3 * hotel (Marmaris), very often guests who are on holiday with their entire family together with young children recommend booking "Family" rooms for themselves, which have all the conditions for staying for such groups. They are designed for four adult guests, for whom there are two beds: a single and a double bed, as well as a cot for a child, which can be installed on request - its installation is completely free. The beds are evenly distributed over the two available rooms.

As for the furniture equipment, the rest of it fully corresponds to what is available in the apartments of the Standard class.

Entertainment

Nutrition

Guests staying at the hotel can eat all-inclusive. A daily buffet is organized here for them, which offers dishes prepared in the best traditions of Turkish and Mediterranean cuisines. The food served here is undoubtedly to the taste of the guests, which is reflected in the reviews of travelers left during and after the vacation. The hotel has a large restaurant that you can visit at any time, as well as a bar that always offers original cocktail art, as well as snacks and desserts, recommended by many.



Services

Guests of the Club Selen Hotel Icmeler share in their comments the possibilities that the hotel provides to all travelers staying in it. One of the most popular among them is laundry and dry cleaning services. Car rental is also in great demand. Experienced tourists recommend renting them and going on an independent tour of local attractions, as well as visiting simply beautiful places. In case of using this opportunity, a free parking space is provided for cars at an open and guarded parking lot located on the hotel territory.

Aquazone

For vacationers, the hotel is equipped with a small, but pretty decent aquazone. It is represented by a harmonious combination of a sun terrace, on which there are several dozens of comfortable sun loungers. It also has large beach umbrellas - guests can use them to protect against the harmful effects of sunlight. If desired, vacationers can always visit the bar and order their favorite cocktails, most of which are original.

In the very center of the terrace is a small pool in which an automatic water purification system is installed, but the process of heating it throughout the entire existence of the hotel is due to exposure to sunlight. Near the large pool there is a small one - it is intended for children.



Beach

The hotel does not have its own site on the coastline, so its guests prefer to visit the public city beach, which is completely covered with sand and pebbles. Here, according to vacationers, you can stay with children - it is safe for them to stay here, since the beach is quite clean and the descent into the sea is gentle and gradual, which provides a high temperature for swimming for a long period.

Here, if you wish, you can rent quite comfortable sun loungers and umbrellas, towels and mattresses are not provided here, therefore, if necessary, you can take them from the hotel with you.

Prices

The Club Selen Hotel Icmeler (Turkey / Icmeler) has a fairly affordable pricing policy. The services provided here are inexpensive, and their payment is affordable for many ordinary Russian inhabitants. A visa, the presence of which is also necessary for entering Turkey, is issued separately, its cost is not included in the price of the tour.
