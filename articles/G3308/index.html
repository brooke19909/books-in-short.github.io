Experience is a great combination of comfort and low cost

Holidays in Sharm El Sheikh are not too focused on a quiet family vacation, it is more suitable for young people. This, in turn, explains the rather hectic nightlife of the resort.

Recently, Sharm el-Sheikh is considered quite a popular destination among Russians. And it is justified. Here you can not only relax comfortably, but also go diving, go on excursions and just have a good time. Nevertheless, not only young people are found among tourists, but also couples with children, as well as elderly people who come mainly from Europe and Russia.





Hotels

Holidays in Sharm El Sheikh for many are associated with inexpensive, but at the same time quite comfortable pastime. The resort has about two hundred hotels with different price levels. Their infrastructure is somewhat different from the European level of service. Therefore, to make the vacation a success, many prefer to stay in four- or five-star hotels located near the sea and having their own beach. They are guaranteed to provide comfortable service, quality food and many other services that are not in hotels with less "star rating". Among them, the five-star Experimental Kirosize Park Land, located in the Naami Bay area, is not the last one.

general information

This hotel was built in 2004. Its former name is Three Corners Kiroseiz Resort. "Experience" is a fashionable hotel that has two private beaches at once: coral and sand. In 2015, a major overhaul was carried out in it . The resort center of Sharm El Sheikh is three kilometers away. The airport is fifteen minutes away.





The hotel has a huge territory of three hundred thousand square meters, fully landscaped. Its infrastructure includes a currency exchange, several shops, a medical office, a hairdresser, four conference rooms, a 24-hour reception, a cinema, an Internet cafe, and a laundry.

Housing stock

"Experiences" is a hotel complex, the territory of which is divided into two zones - the first part, in which two-, three- and four-story buildings are located, as well as the second, where comfortable one-story bungalows and chalets are hidden among the trees. In total, seven hundred standard, deluxe, family and superior rooms are offered for living. There are also twenty-five apartments located on the first line from the sea. All are equipped with comfortable furniture, air conditioning, a plasma TV, balconies or terraces overlooking the water park.

Beach

Since "Experiences" is a hotel, part of which is quite remote from the sea, for the convenience of visitors from the main buildings, a free shuttle runs twice daily to the shore. For living umbrellas, sunbeds and towels on the beach are free with a bracelet.

For children

The hotel has two clubs: mini - Trixie Kids Club, as well as Trixie Cool, designed for teens up to twelve years. There is a playground and a room for classes, a shallow pool. Parents can get a cot for children and a high chair in the restaurant, use the services of a Russian-speaking nanny (for a fee).

Entertainment

The Experimental Quirosize is famous for its aquacomplex, which includes a swimming pool, a sauna with a steam room, a jacuzzi, as well as ozone baths and areas for massage and relaxation. On its territory there is a sports and health club Health Club Sheri Nile with modern fitness equipment, a fitness studio, which conducts aerobics classes.





There are also two outdoor pools with slides, a soccer field, tennis courts, darts, basketball and volleyball courts, as well as two own diving centers. There is a golf club five kilometers from the hotel.
