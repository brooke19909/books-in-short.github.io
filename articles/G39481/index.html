A bit about the resort

Amore is a Cyprus hotel located in the famous resort of Protaras. The city is known for its sandy beaches and beautiful rocky bays. Therefore, it is considered an ideal place for a relaxing and quiet family vacation. For this reason, many parents tend to go to Cyprus with their children to relax in order to improve their health.

Protaras has built many modern complexes, hotel-apartments for family visits. This resort can also be recommended to those who are looking for accommodation near Ayia Napa with its famous parties and entertainment, but at the same time away from the hustle and bustle.





Protaras is a vibrant town that stretches along the famous Fig Tree Bay. The tourism business has been actively developing here for the past three decades. Therefore, at present Protaras has become one of the main resorts in all of Cyprus.

The bay mentioned by us can rightfully boast the best beaches in the entire region. Here you can spend days on end splashing in the clear and calm waters, enjoying fruit cocktails and basking in the sun in the smallest sand. Supporters of secluded relaxation can relax on a rocky island, which a good swimmer can easily reach without a boat. In addition, tourists in Protaras cannot fail to please the promenade along the coast with a length of 15 kilometers, along which many shops, picturesque houses are built.

How to get to the resort?

Protaras has a convenient location in terms of traffic intersection. The resort can be reached quite quickly from Ayia Napa, which is only ten kilometers away. Even taxi services will not cost tourists so much. In addition, municipal buses and minibuses regularly run between cities.

Resort Climate

When planning a vacation, tourists are certainly interested in what the weather is like in Protaras. The resort itself is surrounded on all sides by beautiful cliffs and is located in the bay, so there are no winds. Even in January, the temperature never drops below +17 0 , and the water cools down only to +15 0 . In the summer, the air warms up to +27 0 , and the water - up to +23 0 . The season lasts from April to October. The temperature maximum can be observed in August and July (up to +40 0 ). But since the resort is located in the coastal area, the heat is not felt, it is mitigated by sea breezes. The weather in Protaras is incredibly comfortable for relaxing.





Thanks to its excellent location, the resort has an excellent climate and beautiful nature. Numerous bays with clear clear water attract thousands of tourists. In Protaras, the ebbs and flows are virtually invisible, as in the entire Mediterranean Sea. The water level has very slight fluctuations (30-40 centimeters). In the mornings, the beaches are a bit smaller, and in the evening a little deeper.

Local Attractions

Protaras does not have as many historical places and sights as other Cyprus resorts, but there are still interesting places here. For example, you can visit the plain of windmills, painted white, with which once the inhabitants watered their land.





You can also visit the Agios Elias Church, built on a high cliff. She has a great story. And after sunset, the building is transformed, dressing up in the lights of festive illumination. From a cliff, a stunning panoramic view of the whole city opens. The resort center has many local guides who can offer you a variety of excursions to nearby cities.
