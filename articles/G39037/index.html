Gift to locals

Of course, it is a sin not to use such generosity of nature, if not for medicinal purposes, then at least for rehabilitation. 70 km from the lake is one of the largest cities in Siberia, Irkutsk, with a population of more than 600,000 people. By the way, locals prefer to rest in the midst of the local beauties of nature. There are other rather large settlements in the Baikal region, whose inhabitants love their legendary lake. Naturally, civilized places of rest could not have arisen on its shores. Tents are tents, and the cultural pastime is also not without charms. Gradually, villages located directly on the shores began to turn into resorts not only of local importance.

Common Transformation History



Essential indicators of a fashionable sea resort - fine snow-white or gold sand, sea blue and snow-white liner - all this here, on the shores of Siberian Lake Baikal, has become a common landscape. For example, the village of Enkhaluk, whose recreation facilities are known far beyond Siberia, is famous for these indicators. Belonging to the Kabansky district of the Republic of Buryatia, the village is located 170 km from the capital Ulan-Ude and 430 km from Irkutsk.

The history of the transformation of the coastal village into a resort is trivial: two streets, with cows grazing nearby, are gradually built up with houses and turn into the first recreation center (in this case, the Buryatnefteprodukt camp site), which is gradually gaining fame. Hotels are being built, infrastructure is being created - in fact, a village is appearing, to the original name of which the word New Enkhaluk is added.

One of the pearls of Baikal



Of course, this paradise with snow-white beaches immediately became an elite village. But still, a large number of comfortable recreation centers make the trip here affordable and quite democratic in terms of pricing policy.
