Smartphone Fly 4413: specifications and reviews

The familiar appearance of a modern phone can no longer surprise a sophisticated user. Although sometimes a rather interesting set of functions is hidden under the nondescript and ordinary look of the device. Such a set has one of the representatives of Fly with an inconspicuous name 4413.

Appearance





In appearance, the Fly 4413 phone resembles a huge number of its Android counterparts. The standard edging made of plastic tries to disguise itself as metal, and the softly rounded sides for more convenient use do not distinguish the smartphone from the rest.

Navigation buttons are located at the bottom of the display, and a proximity sensor, lighting, a speaker and a two-megapixel camera are located on top.

Light weight, only 150 grams, and rounded edges allow the phone to comfortably lie in your hand when working or making calls. A protective glass covers the entire front of the device, keeping the display from damage and dust.

On top of the Fly 4413 device are USB and a standard headphone jack with a 3.5 mm jack.

On the back of the device is the main camera with 8 megapixels and a dual flash. Below, next to the company‚Äôs logo, is the smartphone speaker grid.

On the right is the power button, but on the left is the volume control.

The back panel of the smartphone is attached to a large number of grooves, which, in general, is a plus, but there is a chance of damage. Underneath the panel hides a slot for a flash drive, a removable battery and two slots for SIM cards.

Display





The Fly 4413 smartphone is equipped with a very bright 4.7-inch IPS-display with a pretty good screen resolution: 960 by 540. The display is protected by glass from external factors.

The phone‚Äôs sensor can handle five touches without problems, however, when working with the device, it is worth noting the low sensitivity of the display. The user will need to get used to pressing the sensor harder.

The indisputable advantage of Fly 4413 is the camera with the usual 8 megapixels for such devices. The camera resolution of 3840 by 2160 will also please photo enthusiasts. Filling

In the Fly 4413 phone, the specifications are also not inferior to many modern counterparts.

A pleasant surprise will be the MT6582M processor, which has four cores and allows the phone to cope with difficult tasks. The processor frequency in this model has as much as 1300 MHz.

One gigabyte of RAM looks a little faded against this background, but in principle this is quite enough. With light load, the smartphone will confidently perform its functions.

The phone is equipped with a Mali 400 Mp2 video accelerator, which processes content very soundly.

It spoils the overall impression of the Fly 4413 with its small amount of memory, only 4 gigabytes. However, this drawback can be corrected by the usual addition in the form of a flash drive. Memory expansion with microSD is possible up to 32 gigabytes.

Battery





The active use of all the advantages of the device significantly affects the state of the smartphone‚Äôs battery. A modern phone, equipped with a 1800 mAH battery, looks very mediocre. It is this amount of battery that the Fly 4413 has.

Under certain restrictions on the screen brightness and additional functions of the phone, the battery will provide approximately 6-7 hours of operation with constant use.

operating system

Sound

It is necessary to separately note the dynamics of the smartphone. The sound is very high quality and loud on both speakers. Problems with missed calls due to extraneous noise should not occur.

Additional functions

Many pleasant functions will help brighten up leisure time or facilitate the work of the device owners. To work with the Internet, the Fly 4413 smartphone has both regular mobile Internet and Wi-Fi function.

Small functions like radio, video, as well as audio players, a voice recorder and bluetooth are also present in this model.

Communication

Despite the fact that the Fly 4413 is a budget smartphone, it has two slots for SIM cards. Unfortunately, having only one module for communication, when making calls, the phone automatically sends a second SIM card to standby mode. The phone works with both GSM and 3G connectivity.

Equipment
