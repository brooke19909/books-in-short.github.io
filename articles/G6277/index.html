Where can I get a reliable phone in a metal case?

Phone in a metal case not so rare today. The ancestor of this series of devices is the Finnish company Nokia. She was the first to release a mobile phone in this version. Her successful experience has led many manufacturers to focus on this niche. One of the most common problems with older phones is the fragile plastic case. Over time, it worn out, deteriorated. In the fall could crack. All this prompted Finnish engineers to make this part of the communication device out of metal. This material

devoid of such shortcomings. The first phone in a metal case is the Nokia 6300, which has become a classic in its time.

Model Overview

Cell phones in this design can be found at three manufacturers: Nokia, Samsung and Fly. In addition to the 6300 model, the Finnish manufacturer offers 6303, 6700 and 515 more. Of these, the latter is of most interest. This is a brand new device that went on sale last year. His screen is 2.4 inches, and its resolution is 320 by 240 pixels. The memory capacity is 256 MB, if necessary, it can be expanded using a MicroSD memory card with a capacity of up to 32 GB. For video recording and photography, a front camera of 5 megapixels is provided. Supported as in normal

GSM networks and 3G. The battery capacity is enough for 3-4 days with a mediocre load. Nokia 515 is equipped with 2 slots for installing SIM-cards, which allows you to simultaneously work with two operators at once. Against the background of the Finnish device for communication, the Samsung C3322 metal phone in the metal case looks much weaker in technical specifications. First of all, it is a screen that is smaller. Its diagonal is 2.2 inches at the same resolution. Also
the camera is weaker - only 2 megapixels. The internal memory is also smaller - only 44 MB. But the battery is similar, and it will last for all the same 3-4 days with a mediocre load. Chinese manufacturers are trying to keep abreast of events everywhere, and the Fly B500 model is another confirmation of this. This is also a mobile phone in a metal case. In technical specifications, it is very similar to the Nokia 515. The only difference is in the amount of memory (only 44 MB) and battery capacity (its maximum is enough for 3 days, and even then with an interference fit). But at the same time, the software component, which the Finnish model has already tested with time and brought to perfection, raises complaints.





Purchase options

A phone in a metal case can be bought in three ways: in the market, in a store, and on the Internet. Best of all is the last option. Here the price is lower and the warranty is respected. Only the choice of an online store must be approached thoroughly. The other two options have a higher price, and therefore they are not entirely justified from an economic point of view.

Recommendations

The best cell phone in a metal case today is the Nokia 515. Excellent technical specifications and a reliable software component are its indisputable advantages. It is by these criteria that he has no analogues or competitors. Therefore, if you need just such a device, then this model has no alternative. This is the best device in this segment.
