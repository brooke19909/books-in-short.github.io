Bosch Laser Roulette: Benefits and Uses

Today, Bosch laser roulette is an indispensable tool for people involved in construction and decoration. The main purpose of the device is to measure distances, although this is not the only ability of the device.

Laser rangefinders are used both for indoor measurements and for working in open facilities.

Modern optoelectronic devices have an expanded range of functions, they allow you to calculate the volume and area of ‚Äã‚Äãpremises, perform measurements of inaccessible objects by the Pythagorean theorem, and exchange information with a computer. In addition, the Bosch laser roulette has a shockproof, moisture and dustproof housing, which allows it to be used in any conditions.

The principle of operation with the device is quite simple. The device must be placed on a flat surface and turned on. The device automatically adjusts and directs the generated red laser beam to the desired point. The mark is immediately displayed on the receiver, and the distance to the object is calculated and appears on the roulette display. The device sends pulses that reach the target and are reflected from it. After that, the built-in microprocessor calculates the distance based on the signal travel time.



Laser roulette has many advantages over conventional roulette. All measurements can be easily performed by one person. Using the apparatus, you can measure those objects whose parameters are almost impossible to determine with a conventional device due to obstacles.

The device works much faster and gives more accurate results. The generated laser beam serves as a visible reference point, which facilitates the work associated with the installation of windows, window sills, floor leveling and other actions.

Choosing a rangefinder, it is necessary, first of all, to determine the tasks that a Bosch laser roulette should perform. You should not overpay for additional, but unclaimed functions of the device.

For the production of simple finishing work in the apartment, it is enough to purchase a household class device. If the work is carried out in harsh conditions with the presence of complex objects, then you should consider buying a professional tool.



Modern models of rangefinders allow measurements to be taken at distances of up to 200 meters. If the work will be carried out in an apartment or on a small construction site, then the acquisition of a device with a range of 50 meters is enough. If you need to take measurements over long distances, you will need a Bosch laser roulette with the ability to mount on a tripod, since in this case it is difficult to do without it.

Almost all devices work with high accuracy, which will be enough to perform various tasks in both professional and domestic construction.

When choosing a device, you need to hold it in your hands. Bosch laser roulette should fit comfortably in the palm of your hand, not be too heavy and not slip out of your hand, for this its body is equipped with rubber nozzles.
