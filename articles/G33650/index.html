How to find out your retirement savings. How to find out about your SNILS pension savings

Pension accumulation is understood as means accumulated in favor of the insured, for which a part of the retirement pension and / or urgent payment is established. Any resident of Russia can regularly check the amount of deductions. Read about how to find out your retirement savings.

General information

Since 2015, working citizens have insurance pensions and savings. The first are divided into three types: old age, disability, in case of loss of the breadwinner. Retirement benefit payments are urgent, one-time or earmarked. Rights are formed on the basis of coefficients or points. Citizens may receive old-age benefits if:

  • reached 60 (men) and 55 years (women), certain categories may begin to receive benefits ahead of schedule;
  • their insurance experience is more than 15 years;
  • the minimum number of accumulated points is 30.

For each year of work, a citizen generates pension rights in the form of points. Their number depends on the option of pension provision. When forming only insurance payments to a citizen, 10 points are awarded. If funds are also directed to the formation of savings - 6.25.





Employers pay contributions at a rate of 22% of the payroll. At the request of a citizen, all this amount can be directed to the formation of an insurance pension. The second option is also possible: 6% of the tariff will be used to form the insurance, and 16% - the funded part of the pension. The choice is available only to citizens born after 1966. The accumulation of funds can be charged:

  • PF RF, choosing a management company (UK).
  • Non-state Pension Fund (NPF).




The selected organization will deal with the accounting of funds and their payment. But working citizens of the Russian Federation can at any time find out pension savings under SNILS. There are several ways to do this:

  • receive a notice in the mail;
  • make an extract from an individual account;
  • through the Internet;
  • at a branch of Sberbank.

In December 2014, the President of Russia signed a decree to extend the moratorium on pension savings. All 2015 deductions will be used to finance insurance pensions. What will happen to management companies next is unclear.

How to find out your retirement savings?

The Pension Fund distributes information on the account balance annually to all persons who have begun their seniority, until they retire. After receiving the letter, it is necessary to verify the correctness of the indicated data: F. I. O. and the SNILS personal account number. According to the document, you can track how faithfully the employer made deductions last year. The cumulative part of the contributions is displayed in the 3rd paragraph of the notification (for individual entrepreneurs - in the 2nd). The figure of insurance premiums is shown in the 15th paragraph. How to find out your retirement savings for your entire work experience? The total amount of deductions in two directions is displayed in paragraphs 4 and 16.





The notice also indicates:

  • the amount of funds transferred by the PF to the trust management of the company;
  • part of the contributions that were transferred under the Co-financing Program;
  • the amount of maternity capital, which is aimed at the formation of the funded part of the pension;
  • information about the selected management company and investment portfolio.

Such notifications are sent in the form of registered letters, which are handed to the client under his signature. If the letter did not arrive, you will have to personally contact the local PF branch for an account statement.

How to find out pension savings under SNILS?

This can be done via the Internet. To get started, you must register on the Gosuslug portal. This process may be delayed, as the authorities must identify the user. At the end of the procedure, you can go to the "My Account" using the SNILS number as a login. This method is the most convenient and safe, as it allows you to quickly generate an extract at any time of the day.

In the branch of Sberbank

You can find out the savings in the Pension Fund through the portal of a credit institution. But first you need to register in the information exchange system. To do this, you must contact the structural unit of Sberbank with a passport, SNILS and fill out the appropriate application. After registration in the database, the information will be available in the "Personal Account" of online banking. A similar application can be written at any credit institution in the country: Uralsib, Bank of Moscow, VTB, Gazprom. Here's how to find out the amount of retirement savings.





Alternative methods

There is another option, how to find out the amount of pension savings. If a citizen has chosen a non-state Pension Fund as the managing body for accrual and payment of savings, then this organization needs to know the account balance. The client can receive this information upon a personal visit and provision of SNILS. Some non-state pension funds provide a service such as a special service for a fee.

Increased security

Due to the increase in the number of cases of fraud, the government tightens control over transfers of funds between PFs. Complaints about the unauthorized transfer of money began to come from the Russians. A person draws up a loan for consumer goods, provides a copy of SNILS in a package of documents, and then, after a few months, receives a notification by mail about the transfer of funds to a non-state PF. According to preliminary data, the number of citizens deceived in this way exceeds 26 thousand people.





But even if you received a letter about the transfer of funds, you should not panic. The problem can be solved. But you have to run around a bit. It is necessary to write a statement to the non-state PF on termination of the contract. Better yet, complain to law enforcement. Then the NPF will be required to pass a test at the Central Bank for reliability.

In the meantime, the state is trying to change the rules of the re-registration procedure. If earlier it was enough to sign on the contract of the future insurer, now it is necessary to notify the PF in writing about the voluntary transfer of funds to another organization. Forms and samples of such applications are already being prepared.

Conclusion

The employer pays contributions to the PF for each employee at a rate of 22%. Since 2015, all this amount has been allocated to the formation of a seniority benefit. Citizens of Russia can independently track the volume of such deductions. How to find out where the pension savings? You can contact the fund for an extract, view the account balance on the website of the State Service or in the "Personal Account" of the bank.
