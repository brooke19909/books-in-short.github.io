Flat - what is it? Definition, characteristics and application in trading

To profit from transactions, speculators and investors use a wide variety of trading instruments. During training, special attention is paid to methods for predicting market quotes. This is a very important factor in trading. With the help of analytics, you can find out what market situation is happening at the moment and what can be expected in the future: trend movements, impulses or a consolidation zone. Forex Flat Definition

When the market is in the consolidation zone, a price range is created. Inside it, quotes periodically change: they drop, then rise, and in this way a flat is created. What it is, you can imagine if you build two lines, beyond which market prices do not go.

Types of flat:

  • lateral movement with constant levels;
  • directional change in price thresholds with smooth growth;
  • movements with replacements of support and resistance lines.

To understand the state of the flat, what it is, we can consider the simplest example with lateral movement.

The figure shows how market quotes move within the price range, but do not go beyond it. Periodically, the scope of the levels may narrow or expand. Such a movement always occurs only in a calm market, and there are no strong impulses in it. With a directed change in the price threshold, market quotes also act within the range, but at the same time they have a slope. And if we consider flat with the replacement of levels, then on the graphs you can clearly see how the resistance line changes to support and vice versa.

Features of trading in a calm market

Trading during the flat is characterized by constancy, which can be both long and short in time. When the market is in the consolidation zone, then all movements on it occur in a calm mode. During a flat, strong impulses or especially trends never occur. During this period, trading on the market is rather sluggish; deals are opened with small volumes of lots. Mostly small and less often medium-sized players, such as private traders, small banks and stock companies, take part in the auction.

This state of the financial market is very easy to determine on the chart yourself. To understand that flat is really a range of market quotes, it is enough to draw lines along the top and bottom of the candles that will determine the bounding box. Graphic constructions must be done in places of a large concentration of candles.

The features of trade in the financial market in the consolidation zone include its easy predictability. It will not be difficult to determine the direction of movement, as well as its purpose, since they are strictly limited by the range. All trading strategies developed for the flat are built according to the parameters of the forecast features for this market condition.

Flat advantages

The advantages of trading in the consolidation zone include easy forecasting of changes in financial market directions. If we look at historical charts, we can identify the following pattern: market quotes move within the range from the level of resistance to support and vice versa. They do not go beyond its borders and constantly repeat these movements until there is an impulse in the market, which will later develop into a trend movement.

Therefore, it is very important for all newcomers who are just starting a career as a trader to understand and determine what it is like a Forex flat. All trading strategies developed for trading in the consolidation zone correspond to these specific characteristics and only work when the market is calm and deals with small volumes are made on it.

A very common mistake of beginners during trading in the consolidation zone is the inability to determine when the flat feeders end and the impulse begins, which later develops into a trend movement.
