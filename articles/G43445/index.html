Backstage is what every photographer and videographer needs

Nowadays, it has become very fashionable to use the word backstage in the photo and video industry. But where did this concept come from, what does it mean and why is it increasingly common?

Meaning of the word backstage and its translation

The word backstage is borrowed from English. Backstage in translation means "backstage", "being behind the curtains", "backstage", "secret". In the Russian language understanding, backstage is essentially the same thing. This is what happens behind the scenes, before the performance or before the actual filming.





Backstage is all that the viewer will not see, that is, if we talk about a fashion show, he usually sees only the finished result, but how the models were prepared, how the stylist, make-up artist, costume designer worked with them, etc. He never finds out, although many are very interested. For such purposes, backstage was created. On television you can see programs called "Behind the Scenes" or "How was they filmed?" - this is all backstage.

In the photo, the backstage option is perfectly represented: we see how the models were prepared for display.

Backstage is a kind of entertainment for the audience.
