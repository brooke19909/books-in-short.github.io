Budget hotel near the village of Kemer: Sumela Garden 3 *

Among the Turkish "three rubles" there is one hotel located near the village of Kemer - "Sumela Garden". It is located in a very prestigious area called Beldibi. Despite such a small "star", this hotel stands right on the beach and has a very large territory. Next to her are many different shops and decent inexpensive cafes. The hotel is not very new, it consists only of old and new buildings. But the level of services and comfort in it is not inferior to hotels of a higher category. So, consider this interesting hotel.



Kemer, "Able Garden." general description

This is a small hotel. There are only sixty rooms with basic amenities. Eight more are two-room suites or rooms for families. Although the rooms are small, they are very clean and tidy. The view from the windows in the new building is to the sea, and from the old to very beautiful mountains. The hotel area is very green and beautiful, there is where to walk, including with a child - lawns, swings ... Nearby is a wild beach with picturesque cliffs. People who really came to rest calmly and relaxed will be completely satisfied. More active tourists prefer to dilute the sea and sun baths with excursions.
