Materials for the manufacture

For work you will need:

  • Beautiful glass or plastic transparent jar.
  • Scrapbooking paper: color with an unusual texture.
  • Decor: rhinestones, ribbons, twine, feathers and all that is enough for imagination.
  • Scissors.
  • Thermo gun for glue.

Gift container can be searched at home. For example, for this purpose, you can use a transparent plastic box for granulated tea or candy. If such a thing is not found in the house, go to a household goods store or a china shop. Beautiful paper and other decor can be purchased at the craft store. Now plug in the fantasy. The container can be painted with acrylics or special contours for glass. Stick rhinestones, feathers. As an option: wrap part of the can and lid with twine.

Next, cut the paper strips. Their length and width will depend on what size text you want to write. There may be wishes, words of love, compliments, dreams, memories - everything your soul wants. Roll paper stripes. Glue a ribbon or a thick thread to each scroll. Colorful ties look beautiful.

Memories of the sea

Let us dwell on the marine style in more detail. After all, it can be used as a decor. In addition, in such a jar it will be possible to attach a lot of shells and other gifts of Neptune that you brought from your vacation.

For decor you will need:

  • Bank.
  • White paint (suitable emulsion).
  • Acrylic putty.
  • Alcohol.
  • Two types of glue: PVA and Crystal.
  • Napkin with a pattern in a marine style.
  • Varnish for decorative work: transparent.
  • Printer paper (preferably color).
  • Brush (synthetic).
  • Marine decor.
  • Scissors.




Manipulations are performed in the following sequence:

  1. First of all, the bank needs to be degreased. For this, alcohol or acetone is suitable. You can also use an alcohol wipe.
  2. Take a napkin. Separate the top ball with a picture. We smear part of the container with PVA glue. We apply the top layer of the napkin with the pattern inward.
  3. We turn to creating the effect of the seabed. To do this, we select small pebbles, shells, sand. To create a flicker effect, you can mix it with white sparkles. Everything on our seabed needs to be fixed. To do this, fill all objects with transparent varnish.
  4. In the place where the napkin is, you need to prime the jar with white paint. Half of the container will remain transparent, the second part will remain white. Wait for it to dry.
  5. When the paint hardens, apply a thick layer of putty on it. Before it has dried, lay out the decor on it: pebbles, shells, rhinestones.
  6. Coarse large decor should be combined with a transparent part of the can. To create a smooth transition, we use sand, small and broken shells.
