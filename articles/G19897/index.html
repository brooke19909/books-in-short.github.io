Pitfalls you can meet when connecting

Before you connect the Internet to MTS, you need to determine the tariffs and pay attention to the "pitfalls" and cleverly veiled phrases in the contract drawn up by experienced lawyers of the company. For example: "traffic is unlimited, but with a limit." What is this suggestion talking about? Namely, that the service will have to be limited.

Or what is the phrase: "when using the package of services, the actual speed may differ from the stated". When signing documents, you must pay
attention to this disclaimer. Otherwise, there will be no one to make a claim, the decrease in speed corresponds to the condition of the concluded contract.

How to decide on the MTS package





Before you connect the Internet to MTS and purchase a package, it is best to call the company and talk with the operator. If the settlement is large, communication work in different parts of the city may be uneven. Only the operator will tell you exactly in which place the connection is better, and in which - worse.

Be sure to clarify what the daily quota is. Before you connect the Internet to MTS, you need to understand that it is not the number of packets that is limited, but the speed of their delivery.

For Moscow, MTS offers a BIT package. Monthly payment - 149 rubles, quota - 50 megabytes. For those who live in other regions of Russia, there is a Super-BIT package. Its traffic quota is 100 megabytes. This service costs 299 rubles a month.

What happens if the quota indicated in the contract is used up? No one disconnects the Internet, and you can continue to download packages in full. But at the end of the month such an invoice will be issued that it would be better if the service was interrupted. Software developers do not warn their users that they have exceeded their limit. Maybe programmers should consider installing a warning system? In some cases, the calculation of consumed packets is forgotten.

MTS unlimited Internet connection on the phone

After you have decided which package to purchase and signed the contract, you need to activate the service.

What is the advantage of unlimited internet?

Unlimited Internet has an advantage over the BIT service: its speed is not limited until the amount of downloaded information exceeds 1 megabyte. For those who use the BIT tariff package, the speed decreases after 5 megabytes per hour.

During the day, when using the Internet with a limited tariff, in the case of packet search, the speed decreases to only 16 kilobytes per hour.

If you exceed the limit, disable the service or change the tariff plan, after revising the terms of the contract, you will have to re-establish the GPRS connection, observing the same algorithm of actions.
