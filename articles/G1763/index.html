Hotel location

The five-star hotel complex "Akka Alinda Hotel" is located in the village of Kirish, seven kilometers from the popular resort of Kemer. This region attracts tens of thousands of tourists every year due to the warm sea, mild climate, magnificent landscapes and excellent beaches. The nearest airport is Antalya. The distance to it from the hotel is 65 kilometers. Thus, after arriving on the road to Akka Alinda, it will take you about an hour. The hotel is located on the first line and has its own sand and pebble beach. Distance to the sea is only 70 meters. The nearest bus stop is two hundred meters from the hotel. Guests can take short and exciting walks to both Kemer and Antalya. You can also visit the ruins of the ancient city of Phaselis.
