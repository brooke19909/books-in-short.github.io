IPhone - what is it? There's an answer!

This article is dedicated to the answer to this entertaining question.

First generation iphone

The presentation of the first Apple smartphone took place in the summer of 2007. The phone caused a real storm of emotions, because it was a revolutionary invention: Steve Jobs approached the matter in a very creative and original way. He was the first to introduce touchscreen, which made the iPhone popular. Almost everyone knows what a touchscreen is today, but still recall: this is a function of enlarging an image by moving the screen with two fingers. Talk about iPhone 5

Please do not be surprised why such a sharp transition was made from the first modification immediately to the fifth. The thing is that for the next four generations, iPhone developers have remained very conservative: the same 3.5-inch screen, only the 3G network was added from the new generation networks, and, of course, the classic look. But the next generation of the phone - iPhone 5 - appeared in Moscow shortly after it entered the world market, earning immediately the respect and trust of fans of new products in the world of technology. IPhone 5 has broken all the old stereotypes: a new 4-inch high-resolution screen, an incredibly powerful processor, a revolutionary camera and three speakers have appeared, which made the sound clearer and more voluminous! More specifically, this is a dual-core Apple A6 processor, an 8 megapixel camera with a five-element lens that allows you to take stunning pictures, replacing the camera on ordinary walks or even when traveling.
