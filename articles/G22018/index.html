Sea

Despite the fact that the Pickalbatros Sea World 4 * hotel is located on the second coastline, it has at its disposal a fairly large beach. According to our compatriots, walking to it can be reached in 10-15 minutes by an unhurried step. On the road you need to cross the roadway. As for the beach, it is sandy, clean. There are many sun loungers and parasols, a bar and restaurant. A little swim, you will find yourself on a rather large coral reef and you can admire its funny inhabitants. Therefore, when going on vacation, be sure to arm yourself with a snorkeling mask. Entertainment at the hotel

Hotel Pickalbatros Sea World Resort 4 * (Hurghada, Egypt) offers its guests a lot of leisure options.
