How to choose a digital video camera: a few recommendations

Everyone in life has a huge number of interesting events that I want to leave in my memory. The difficulty is that she is simply unable to capture all the memories. There are many solutions to this problem, but the camcorder is currently the most effective. Almost everyone has it. And those who do not have it, may be wondering how to choose a digital video camera? The decision should be approached as responsibly as possible.

Set expectations



Before buying such a device, you need to clearly determine the characteristics that are most important to you. The final answer, how to choose a digital video camera, is built solely on their basis. First of all, attention should be paid to the type of data carrier, recording format and frame resolution. Modern models work in a certain way, which depends on the recording format, namely the type of video compression. Speaking about how to choose a digital video camera, you should get acquainted with the types of compression of the video sequence. The first is the DV-view, which involves the individual reduction of each frame, while the compression ratio can be different even within the same fragment, the video stream is 25 Mb / s, the frame size is 576 * 720 pixels. Another format is called MPEG-2. It involves compression between frames, that is, only key fragments are reduced. For this species, very serious
hardware capabilities, and this format is inconvenient for editing. High resolution cameras support formats such as HDV and AVCHD. The first involves the use of amorphous conversion of the stream with high resolution without loss of quality and while maintaining a high flow rate. This type allows you to shoot with a resolution of 1980 * 1080 pixels. The second format assumes that the camera has significant resources for recording, storage and viewing.

If we talk about how to choose a good video camera, then it is worth noting the importance of the type of data carrier. Now these devices are capable of supporting 4 types of media: mini-DV-drives, hard drives, memory cards and cassettes. The first and last options are gradually losing relevance, as they have a low capacity, and their mechanism is unreliable. Cameras equipped with hard drives or drives in the form of memory cards do not have such problems, their resource is quite large, so they are ready to shoot many hours of video.



Dealing further with the question of how to choose a digital video camera, you need to pay attention to the optical stabilizer, shooting mode, zoom, and matrix, since these parameters can differ significantly from different manufacturers. It is difficult to say which characteristic will be better, it will be more correct to look at their combination. Speaking of manufacturers, we can say that the most popular are Philips and Sony.

So, if we talk about how to choose a digital video camera, then you need to know all its technical parameters so as not to be mistaken. This is the only way to ensure long-term use of the device.
