Specifications and description washing machine BEKO WKB 61021 PTMA

For those who do not want to spend a large amount on the purchase of household appliances, it is recommended to pay attention to the BEKO trademark. The products of this manufacturer are characterized by the optimal ratio of quality and cost. In the category of up to 15 thousand rubles, the buyer is invited to pay attention to the excellent washing machine BEKO WKB 61021 PTMA. The manufacturer has implemented everything necessary in it. Reviews about this model sound different, but still more positive. What exactly did the owners like and what moments were criticized, we will tell a little later. Now let's start with a description of the washing machine from Turkey.



Description

This model is presented in a classic style. The body is painted white. On the front panel there is a hatch door and control buttons. At the usual place is a box for detergents. Next to it is a rotary knob, with which you can select a specific washing program. No display. The notification of the included modes is carried out by means of LED indicators. Some owners were dissatisfied with the lack of a display, but during operation it became quite easy to get used to such a control panel.

BEKO WKB 61021 PTMA is a washing machine that has small dimensions. With standard widths and heights (85 √ó 60 cm), the depth is only 45 cm. Such dimensions make it possible to integrate the appliance into a kitchen set or to place it in a small bathroom.

Maximum load - 6 kg. Great for a family of 4-5 people. A hatch diameter of 30 cm is enough to load large items. The drum installed in the device is made of stainless steel. On its inner surface there are special ribs that help rotate the laundry. For the tank, the developers chose a high-quality material, which includes polymer alloys. Thanks to this decision, it turned out to reduce weight, but at the same time increase sound insulation. The tank turned out to be durable and reliable, which is not at all afraid of the effects of hot water and detergents.



Performance classes

Almost all buyers make high demands on household appliances according to the efficiency criterion. In order to be able to save energy, it is necessary to choose equipment of class not lower than A. In this regard, BEKO WKB 61021 PTMA will satisfy the requirements, since it consumes only 0.17 kWh / kg during operation. This indicator corresponds to class A +. With this model, owners can also save water. The consumption of this resource is 49 liters per wash cycle.

As for the main purpose, even at low temperatures, things with severe pollution can be washed perfectly. And this is not at all surprising, since the efficiency class is A.



Spin mode

Describing the characteristics of BEKO WKB 61021 PTMA, you can not keep silent about the spin. Moreover, the owners are very flattering about this criterion. Previously had to be limited to a maximum speed of 800 revolutions. This was not always enough for a high-quality spin, especially if it was about jeans clothes. In the described model, the manufacturer increased the speed of rotation of the drum to a rebound of 1000 revolutions. Thanks to this, things get almost dry at the exit.

Also in the reviews, the owners say that, if necessary, you can adjust the spin speed. This is extremely important when washing delicate fabrics that can easily be damaged.

Conclusion

The article examined not only the characteristics and capabilities of the washing machine, but also reviews on the BEKO WKB 61021 PTMA. This device is suitable for those people who do not have the means to purchase more expensive copies.
