The segment of the TV market that this device is aimed at

Today, television devices are divided as follows:

  • Entry-level devices with a resolution of 1366X768, low quality image output and a minimum set of technical specifications.

  • Mid-range televisions already have a resolution of 1920X1080, higher image quality and advanced technical specifications.

  • Premium television devices without fail display a picture in 4K format, have the maximum possible specifications of the technical plan.

According to the classification given earlier, Samsung UE40J6500AU TV belongs to mid-range solutions. Reviews highlight its excellent functionality and impeccable technical specifications.

Connection Methods

The Samsung UE40J6500AU TV supports the following connection methods:

  • One component port and a SCART connector are present in the device in question. Their main purpose is to connect external DVB-T / DVB-T2 receivers or currently outdated versions of satellite tuners.

  • 4 HDMI ports allow you to connect modern satellite receivers or mini-PCs. Also, such a port can be used to output a signal from a personal computer to a TV. In the latter case, the hero of this article will already act as a monitor.

  • 3 USB ports allow you to connect a flash drive to the device and view its contents. Also in this case, you can play various multimedia files from it.

  • 2 possible ways to connect to a computer network are implemented in this TV. One of them is wireless - WiFi, and the second allows switching using twisted pair cables - this is RJ-45.
