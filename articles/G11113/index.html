Making paper daisies for room decor

So that your apartment always resembles a fragrant summer garden, try to include unfading paper flowers in the interior of the room . Paper daisies will remind you of the hot summer, create a romantic mood in the coldest time of the year. Decorate curtains with flowers, decorate bouquets or make a beautiful panel on the wall.



Simple paper daisies

The first option for making simple wildflowers will not take you much time. For work it is enough to take a sheet of plain white paper, yellow cardboard, glue and scissors. First we make the core blanks. For this, we cut two identical circles from cardboard, with a diameter of 5-10 centimeters. Then proceed to the production of leaflets. To do this, white paper must be cut into equal bands. Their width should be equal to the width of the petal of the future daisy, for example two centimeters. The length of each strip will be twice the length of the petals, say 30 centimeters. The number of stripes will correspond to the number of your petals. 12 petals are enough. Now we form a bud, folding each leaf in half, without smoothing the paper. Glue them at the base, and then glue to one of the circles. After all the petals are in place, close the base of the petals with a second circle, fixing it with glue.





Complex paper daisies

To make crafts in this way, you will need ingenuity, special tools and patience. You need to find paper in white, orange, yellow and green, scissors, a toothpick, a ruler, an awl, a stationery knife and glue. First of all, we make blanks for the bud. To do this, cut out circles of white paper equal in size to the diameter of the chamomile. Next, we make in the circle the slots towards the center, without cutting the paper to the end. Next, carefully trim the edges of the paper with scissors, creating the shape of each petal. Then with the help of an awl, each petal is rounded, raising the edges. Try not to touch the paper in the center during these manipulations. To do this, hold the base with your thumb. Paper daisies will turn out more lush if you make two layers of petals by laying one circle on the other.

Next, proceed to the manufacture of a magnificent core. You can do it in two ways. The first option involves the use of the same blanks for the core as for the petals, only smaller in size, from yellow paper. Fold them in half and glue in a circle in the center. The second method involves the manufacture of a beautiful and neat core. For work, you need to take a strip of yellow paper, cut it into small fringe and wrap around a toothpick. Then remove, push the workpiece in the middle, giving it the shape of a cone. Now glue the core to the daisy, fluffing the fringe. A bouquet of daisies can be used as the design of the original panel on the wall or in the manufacture of postcards. If you want, fill in a box of flowers in a box, and then use them to decorate your apartment. If you want to make a volumetric composition, attach a wire to the base of the flower, wrap it with beautiful green paper and attach the leaves. Everything, a bouquet of paper is ready.
