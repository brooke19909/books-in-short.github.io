Hotel Blue Velvet Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Alanya, Mahmutlar): description, photos, reviews of tourists

Blue Velvet Hotel 3 * is a rural budget hotel near Alanya. It stands not on the shore, but to the sea from its residential buildings - two hundred meters. The hotel is located in the village of Mahmutlar, in its very center. It was built in 2001. But not so long ago the hotel went through redecoration, so the rooms look like new. How to get that near

From the airport to the hotel Blue Velvet Hotel 3 * (Turkey), even with a transfer you need to get about three hours. The fact is that the airport closest to Alanya is in Antalya.

If you are traveling on your own, then you should catch a regular bus. They go to Alanya quite often and are very comfortable. Tickets for them can be bought online and even choose a place. There is Wi-Fi in the lounge. And upon arrival in Alanya, you transfer to a minibus that will take you to the hotel in a matter of minutes.

But during the transfer, there is a guide on the buses that will tell you about all sorts of interesting places along the way. Near the hotel - literally two blocks away - there is a supermarket chain "Migros", where you can buy anything. In these stores, you can also very profitably change the currency. You pay in dollars, and change is returned to you in Turkish lira, and at the rate of the National Bank.

The hotel is located in the center, so literally at the exit from it there is a local walking area, cafes, attractions, and playgrounds.

Mahmutlar

This village, in the territory of which the Blue Velvet Hotel 3 * hotel is located (Turkey, Alanya), as if intended for a budget holiday. The local hotels are known for their low prices and at the same time good service. These are several streets running parallel to the sea.

The infrastructure of the village, although small, is well developed. And in Alanya you can leave from here by any minibus. The road is new and straight. But in the evening, after 23, you can get there either by taxi or by hotel transfer.

The village is surrounded by very picturesque rocky mountains covered with pine trees. And the color of the water here is amazing - it is completely emerald. Here you can take a walk in a beautiful shady park with benches and exercise machines along the sea.

Territory

Blue Velvet Hotel 3 * hotel consists of two six-story buildings with elevators. But it should be noted that the cabin is small. The elevator accommodates two people with things.

The reception is open around the clock. There are sofas and you can use Wai-Fay for free, by the way, at a very good speed. There is a small well-kept park.

The hotel has an outdoor pool, spa and hookah. A parking lot for tourists is located near the hotel. It is free for guests. On Saturdays, a bazaar arrives directly at the gates of the hotel, where you can buy fruits and vegetables.

Nutrition

Despite the fact that the Blue Velvet Hotel 3 * hotel has only three stars on its facade, tourists are fed here according to the "all inclusive" system. The hotel provides not only breakfast, lunch and dinner in the main restaurant, but also tea, coffee and pastries in the afternoon from four to five in the evening. Dinner ends at 9 p.m., and free drinks at the bar can be taken until half past nine.

They feed without any frills, but tasty. There are pasta, fish, chicken, salads, eggs and cheeses in different forms. Good fried mackerel, sprinkled with lemon juice and stuffed baked eggplant, cutlets such as kufte or kebab.

A wide selection of vegetables, both fresh and stewed. Breakfast includes delicious ham, beef sausages, cereal, and milk. Good soups that kids really like. From fruits there are oranges, watermelons, melons. True, there are very few varieties of desserts and sweets, which many tourists complain about. These are mainly puddings and cookies.

At the bar you can take beer, white and red wine, gin, vodka. There are vending machines with tea, coffee and powder juices. In the bar, everything is opened right at you and nothing is diluted.

Hotel Blue Velvet Hotel 3 *: service reviews

Guests praise the hotel's spa. It is not too expensive, but there are rooms for a varied massage, a sauna, a hammam, as well as a classic steam room. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel. You can play table tennis and darts.

For children, several times a week in the evenings mini-discos are organized, and for adults - entertainment programs and shows. The honeymoon room is decorated with flowers, and they also bring a bottle of wine to the room.

Both the owner of the hotel and all the staff are very friendly and attentive to customers, polite and welcoming. If something breaks or something is missing in the room - for example, the TV remote control - just tell the reception and everything will be done.

Almost all employees of Blue Velvet Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Alanya) speak Russian or at least understand it. If you suddenly forget something on the beach, then in the evening your staff will bring your things to the reception. Animators carry to discos in the city center. Where to go and what to see

What do you think that they choose 90 percent of Blue Velvet Hotel 3 * hotel guests as a tour object? Alanya! They perceive Mahmutlar as such a "sleeping area." But there are many interesting sights nearby.

Lovers of antiquity will surely like the ancient cities of Siedra and Laertes. For those who prefer natural beauty, experienced tourists are advised to go to the Dimchay River. There are amazing caves with salt lakes, stalactites and stalagmites. And here fish restaurants are open, where they cook the most delicious river trout in Turkey.

But after exploring the nearby attractions, you can, of course, go to Alanya on your own, ride a pirate yacht, explore the medieval docks and the ancient fortress, and also visit the famous Cleopatra beach with natural yellow sand.

Excursion in Pamukkale is quite popular from long trips. But keep in mind that you will have to travel 6 or more hours, and if you are in Alanya at the height of summer, this is not too pleasant. In Pamukkale itself you will spend no more than 3 hours. So keep this in mind if you decide on a trip. And of course, excursions are best purchased not in the hotel itself, but in street travel agencies. You will be provided with the same services, but much cheaper.
