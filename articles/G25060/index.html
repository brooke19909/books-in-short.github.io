Mass for modeling for children

Is your child 2 years old? Or maybe 3? Then it's time to start creating. And the mass for modeling will help to master this difficult matter. Children have a special connection between the work of the brain and fine motor skills of the fingers. The more it is developed, the faster and more clearly the child will speak. Mass for modeling has a lot of advantages over ordinary plasticine. First of all, this is material that even weak children's fingers can knead, and even if the baby sculpts another detail of a future creation, this one will not become too hard. Another plus, which will be appreciated mainly by parents and kindergarten teachers, is that it does not stick to clothes, a work surface on which they mold and to hands. The colors will not only pleasantly surprise you, but they will also give you the opportunity to gain the first experience in creating colors and their combinations, because the sculpting mass mixes perfectly to a uniform color for any quantity ratio. There is also a mass for sculpting, not hardening in air, but in the oven. However, this option is more suitable for adults - it is more rigid and it will not be possible for a child to make, for example, a gift to her mother.

Mass for modeling a clay hardens in 8 hours and is designed for babies older than 3 years. Believe me, sculpting from this material will captivate not only children, but also their parents. The only negative that can be found in such an ideal material is the inability to make too thin and tall figures, because ductility implies softness. But, if you make the parts separately, and after hardening assemble, wet the ends with water, then everything will work out.
