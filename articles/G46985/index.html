New buildings in Stavropol: a comfortable apartment for each client

Modern Stavropol is the cultural, industrial and business center of southern Russia. In recent years, a marked increase in the construction of large residential complexes and low-rise suburban villages has been noted in this city. Now, not only the indigenous people, but also visitors from other regions of the country wish to buy apartments in a new building (Stavropol).

Housing Market in Stavropol

Local developers offer their customers housing of any category. Payment can be a mortgage loan at the bank, or phased payments directly to the developer. How exactly to pay for the purchased property - the choice remains with the buyer.



For many, investments in new buildings (Stavropol) are an excellent investment option. Because the purchase of own housing in such a comfortable and large city can be carried out not only for direct residence in it, but also for subsequent rental.

Estimated cost of housing

In a city like Stavropol, new buildings from the developer are divided into three categories: elites, business and economy. In each of these categories, high-quality housing in a different price range is presented.

In the residential complex "White City", which is located on the street Dovatortsev, the cost of 1 square meter in a one-room apartment class economy starts from 30.5 rubles. Interestingly, in this complex, rental housing starts at 15 thousand rubles.



The new buildings of Stavropol, located in the city center, are ideal for those who wish to live within walking distance of developed infrastructure. For example, the Chocolate residential complex is ideally suited for this. The developers in this complex set the initial cost per square meter from 39 thousand rubles, and the rental of a two-room apartment starts from 25 thousand rubles.

Buyers will be more expensive elite class apartment. For example, the price per square meter in the Aleksandrovsky Park residential complex starts from 50 thousand rubles, and to rent a one-room apartment here, you need to pay 30 thousand rubles per month.

How to save money

How can you save money on buying new housing? To do this, you can purchase housing in residential complexes under construction. Such a purchase of real estate under construction, allows the buyer to save considerable money.



If you take a mortgage in the bank for the purchase of an apartment (new buildings in Stavropol is a good option for this purpose), then this is much more profitable compared to ordinary rental housing. After all, renting an apartment, a person gives his money every month and does not receive any real benefits that can be used as an asset in the future.

And taking a mortgage, a person, though in parts, but still buys housing, which will become his own in the near future. Also, do not forget about the constant increase in housing prices in the real estate market. This means that a person who takes a mortgage for housing under construction will pay the same rate: the possible price increases in the real estate market will not affect the price of the apartment.
