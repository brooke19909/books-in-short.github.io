Delfin Siesta Mar 4 * (Spain, Mallorca): hotel description, services, reviews

Mallorca is a beautiful island with an amazing sea coast, thanks to which thousands of tourists visit the island every year. The resort has long gained popularity among holidaymakers around the world, here are built good hotels that meet European standards. We want to talk about one of these complexes in our article.

A bit about the hotel ...

Delfin Siesta Mar 4 * is located in the town of Santa Ponsa, 130 meters from the coast and 200 meters from the famous beach of Cala de Santa Ponsa. The hotel was built in 1967 and has since been actively hosting tourists within its walls. In 2001, the complex was reconstructed, at present it is aimed at all categories of vacationers, including families with children.



The hotel is a five-story building with two elevators. In total, it has 113 apartments overlooking the garden or the sea. There are three rooms for people with disabilities in the arsenal of the hotel.

The hotel has a convenient location. The distance to the sea is small, which is very convenient. There is a public transport stop 150 meters from the property. From here you can get to the right place.

Number of rooms

In Delfin Siesta Mar 4 * her rooms are diverse. Guests are offered two categories of apartments:

  1. Standard double room - rooms for three guests.
  2. Family room. The apartments can comfortably accommodate four tourists.

All rooms are equipped with TV, telephone, satellite channels, safe, bathroom, hairdryer, mini fridge, air conditioning connected to the central system, terrace. The apartments have tiled floors. According to the schedule, cleaning is done daily. Towels are also changed every day. But bed linen is changed twice a week.



Terraces in all rooms are equipped with furniture, so they can not only dry clothes, but also have a good time with a cup of coffee. Wi-Fi in public areas is free of charge; in rooms, internet is available at an additional cost.

Dining at the hotel

Delfin Siesta Mar 4 * offers its guests a wide range of buffet dishes. Tourists can choose for themselves half board or All inclusive. The main restaurant is equipped with an open kitchen, the visitor offers the best dishes of international and local cuisines. The hotel has a bar where guests can enjoy ice cream and cocktails. The food in the restaurant is good and varied. Dishes for lunch and dinner are never repeated: several varieties of fish, pasta, potatoes, mashed soups, paella, meat, sliced, all kinds of snacks and salads. In the evenings, grill dishes are prepared. Vacationers are always offered a large selection of desserts and fruits. Every Friday, a gala dinner is organized in the restaurant; add to the main menu: chocolate fountain, blue cheese, jamon, shrimp, mussels. Guest service is fast and the whole restaurant staff is very polite and helpful.

Hotel infrastructure

Delfin Siesta Mar 4 * is located in a wonderful garden, where you can walk, enjoying the beauty of nature. The hotel has car and bicycle rental facilities, indoor and outdoor pools with an equipped relaxation area. In addition, there is a beauty and health center on site, offering guests a sauna, hammam, jacuzzi and massage treatments.

Beach

Delfin Siesta Mar 4 * (Mallorca) is located near a wide sandy beach. The azure sea has a gentle entrance, convenient for children. Nearby are shops and cafes.

Sports and Entertainment

Delfin Siesta Mar 4 * (Mallorca) offers its guests to visit the gym, play beach volleyball, visit the beauty and health center. The hotel always has a local guide that offers the most interesting excursion trips around the island. You can also rent a car at the hotel and travel around Majorca on your own. There is a bus stop near the hotel. By transport, you can reach the capital of the island in ten minutes.

A little bit about the resort ...

Santa Ponsa is a cozy quiet resort in Spain, located in the southwest of the island. Currently, the beaches of Mallorca are known throughout the world. Santa Ponsa attracts tourists with its unique sandy coast and the azure sea, as well as an active resort life. The city from the capital of the island can be reached by bus or taxi. Santa Ponsa has an excellent transport interchange, thanks to which you can quickly reach all the resorts of the island. Palma can be reached by high-speed bus number 102, which follows without stops. The 104th bus and the 109th bus go to Palma Nova and Magalufa, 110A to Calvi, and 110B runs between all resorts.



Hotel Delfin Siesta Mar 4 * (Mallorca) has a convenient location, near it there is a public transport stop, on which all of the above scheduled buses stop. Relaxing in a hotel, getting to any point on the island is not difficult.
