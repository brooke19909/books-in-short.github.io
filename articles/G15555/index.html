DIY crafts: coffee bean lamb, Christmas tree and hedgehog

Surely few people know that coffee beans can be used not only for making a drink, but also for making a variety of crafts. This article will present master classes on the manufacture of various crafts from coffee beans. For example, a lamb made from coffee beans or a sheep will be a wonderful gift for a loved one.

Coffee bean magnet lamb

To create a cute toy you will need:

  • coffee beans;
  • twine;
  • decorative elements.

Stages of creating coffee beans lamb:

  1. You need to start with the basics. From thick cardboard you need to cut out patterns: a head with ears and a torso. All these parts are well covered with paint. This is so that if the grains do not fit snugly against each other, no gaps can be seen.
  2. When the paint is completely dry, a layer of glue is applied and twine is taken. From the twine you need to lay out an oval for the future face. For the ears, you also need to lay out such two ovals.
  3. Hooves are also made of twine. To make them, you need to glue two grains together and fix on a thread.
  4. Ready hooves are attached to the body. So that the thread mount is not visible, coffee beans must be glued thickly and tightly. A coffee bean lamb or a lamb can be decorated in different ways.
  5. Now you need to stick the head to the body.
  6. Such sheep serve as a talisman, so you can attach a coin and a heart to them.

Lamb from coffee beans as a gift

Materials that are needed for crafts:

  • coffee beans;
  • twine;
  • paint;
  • cardboard;
  • plastic eyes.

Do it yourself lamb from coffee beans as follows:

  1. On paper you need to draw a sketch of the desired sheep.
  2. From this sketch, you need to cut out the head, and the resulting template is transferred to cardboard and cut out.
  3. Finished parts need to be well covered with a layer of brown paint to avoid gaps.
  4. The head is laid out in the same way from twine, as in the first method. Only in this option will plastic eyes be used. The coffee bean lamb can be decorated with any material.
  5. For one sheep, you need to make the legs of twine as in the first embodiment, and for the other legs are cut out of cardboard and covered with twine.
  6. The hooves are attached to the body in a known manner, and the whole body is covered with grains.
  7. The head sticks to the body.

Funny sheep from coffee beans will decorate any room.

Making a Christmas tree from coffee beans

Today it has become fashionable to make Christmas trees from a wide variety of materials - fabrics, feathers, papers and even coffee beans. A miniature coffee tree will fill the house with an amazing aroma. Its size can vary, but a small-sized Christmas tree looks more spectacular. You can do this craft with your children.





To dazzle Christmas trees from coffee beans, you will need to prepare:

  • cardboard cone;
  • coffee beans;
  • brown threads;
  • glue;
  • thick cardboard;
  • semolina;
  • clear varnish;
  • paint in a spray of gold color.

Only then can we start creating an unusual Christmas tree made of coffee.

Master class: Christmas tree made from grain coffee

A cardboard cone is well coated with glue and wrapped with brown threads. It is desirable that the turns are thick. Now you can glue coffee beans. To avoid gaps, there should be two layers of coffee beans. First, paste over from below, pressing the grains together tightly. The top should be open.

When the layer has dried, paste the next one. The second row should be glued as high quality as possible, covering all the gaps with grains.

When all two layers have dried thoroughly, you can begin to decorate the Christmas tree. To do this, you can use bows and beads. You can apply a wide variety of decorative elements, for example, feathers, rhinestones or sequins. It is best if the decor will be glued with transparent glue. So, Christmas trees made from coffee beans will look neater.

While the decor will dry, you need to create a trunk. To make it, you need to twist the pipe from cardboard so that it snugly fits into the cone. The trunk is painted with a golden hue and set aside to dry. With the help of semolina you can create a snow effect. Necessary places are smeared with nail polish and sprinkled with semolina.

Now go to the top. A little glue is dripping on top of the Christmas tree and a bow is glued. Further, at the bottom of the tree, cuts are made in a circle, which are expanded into petals and smeared with glue. With these cuts, the barrel must be inserted into the prepared base after pouring a little glue into it.

Thus, you can create Christmas trees from any materials, using coffee beans.

Spiny Animal Material

Hedgehog made from coffee beans with lemon, star anise and cinnamon will be a wonderful piece of furniture in any home that will be filled with the aroma of coffee and spices.





To create it, you need the following consumables:

  • plastic ball;
  • brown paint;
  • coffee beans;
  • Styrofoam;
  • cardboard;
  • spice decor;
  • black beads;
  • glue.

DIY coffee hedgehog

The plastic ball must be cut into two parts. A cone is cut out of the foam, which will be the muzzle of a hedgehog. One half of the ball is encircled on cardboard and cut out, and the other part of the ball must be painted brown.





The finished half of the ball is glued to the cardboard. Then the foam muzzle clings. With the help of twine, the muzzle must be wrapped. Further, the hedgehog from coffee beans is turned over and with the help of twine the bottom is glued over, starting from the center. After that, in the hemisphere you need to make two skeins of twine.

Starting from the center of the body, rows of coffee beans are glued, while they need to be tilted to the side. Further, grains are glued from the center to the muzzle. Eyes and nose are made from beads. On the back are attached: cinnamon stick, anise and lemon.





Such a hedgehog will not only serve as an interior item, but will also become an unusual gift for relatives and friends.

All crafts made from coffee beans (each master class is detailed) is very interesting, and it will take very little time to make them.
