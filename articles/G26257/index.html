Adler: hotel by the sea. Description of hotels and guest reviews

The pearl of the southern region of Russia is the city of Adler. A hotel by the sea, restaurants, the Olympic Park, attractions, entertainment, excursions and much more - this is what awaits vacationers who decide to spend their holidays in this city. It became especially popular after the 2014 Winter Olympics. The article highlights the Adler hotels by the sea. Indeed, this is what attracts most tourists: the opportunity to settle on the first beach line.

Adler: hotels near the sea

On the coast in Adler there are a lot of hotels. Their rank is very different, different and room categories, as well as services that are provided to vacationers. There are government agencies, there are private hotels. Mini-hotels in Adler by the sea are also not uncommon. There are tourists who specially settle in them, because they do not like to live in large noisy hotels. We invite you to get acquainted with some of the hotels that are the pride of the city.

Hotel Zara



The hotel is three hundred meters from the sea. It offers - an outdoor pool, free Wi-Fi throughout, a bar, a pebble beach. The price includes breakfast. Lunch and dinner are available at an additional cost. It is possible to rent a bicycle to explore the nearest attractions. Near the hotel there are a water park, a dolphinarium and an aquarium, as well as many restaurants. Judging by the reviews, the hotel has a quiet atmosphere, which is ideal for families with children. Also for families there is a convenient opportunity to get a free cot in the room and a baby seat for feeding in the hotel restaurant. Zara is a vivid example of a hotel in Adler on the seashore. Hotel rooms in the following categories:

  • Studio with a view of the coast;
  • Suite with a view of the coast;
  • deluxe suite with balcony;
  • standard room with a balcony;
  • standard without a balcony;
  • Disabled room.

Each room is equipped with air conditioning, safe, satellite TV. The bathroom has a hairdryer. Sheets are changed regularly. The interior of the rooms pleasantly pleases the eye with warm colors and cozy furniture. Well-trained staff will try to ensure that hotel guests do not need anything. Prices are quite reasonable and range from 1700 rubles. up to 3900 rub. per night depending on room category.

Hotel Dolphin



Dolphin is a hotel by the Black Sea. Adler is famous for hotels of this type, namely a spa resort. It is located on the first line of the beach, while the hotel to the center of Sochi is only half an hour away by shuttle bus. The hotel offers visitors a huge number of different rooms, it can accommodate up to a thousand people at a time. There are numbers of such categories:

  • standard for 1, 2 and 3 people;
  • Suite with special amenities.

All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, a refrigerator, and a TV in the room. Rooms with a balcony have beautiful sea views. At the hotel guests can play a casino and try their luck at the slot machines, visit the dolphinarium or dance at a disco in the disco club. You can also watch a movie in the summer cinema, and sometimes celebrity performances in the concert hall. Meals are included in the price, which means that you do not have to worry about cooking and buying products. You can just enjoy your vacation. Two hundred meters from the hotel there is a pebble beach where you can sunbathe and swim in the sea. There are many water activities there. The hotel will be a great place to relax for a family or for a young romantic couple.

Adler: hotel by the sea "Hunter"



Vacationers love this hotel for its convenient location: thirty meters to the sea, four kilometers to the center of Adler and two kilometers to the station. This building has four floors and 52 rooms of different categories:

  • double rooms "Standard";
  • Triple rooms "Standard";
  • double junior suite;
  • double suite.

All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, refrigerator. There is a refrigerator, TV, shower and toilet, hairdryer and all the necessary accessories for each of the guests. For an additional fee, it is possible to add one or two folding beds.

At the hotel, vacationers can enjoy the view of the garden with very rare plants from the subtropics. Right next to the "Hunter" is a water park and a park for kids with many attractions. In addition, there are many restaurants, night clubs, cinemas, clubs and bars nearby. On the coast, guests can enjoy water activities and rent a catamaran or scooter.

Meals are not included in the price, but for an additional fee the opportunity to eat like a buffet is provided. The hotel also has a laundry service, valuables and parking. There is a point for organizing excursions to different places famous for their sights. The Hunter is another great example of an Adler hotel by the sea.

"Neptune"



The location on the first line of the beach makes the hotel very attractive for tourists. In addition to the proximity of the sea (only two hundred meters from the building), it is very close to the center of Adler, a couple of kilometers to the station and only half an hour to the center of Sochi. The hotel's feature is a very quiet and cozy courtyard. The atmosphere of the hotel is saturated with home coziness and comfort, and the stylish interior of the rooms adds grace and elegance to the entire building. Neptune provides block-type numbers: two adjacent for 2 and 3 people. All are equipped with a refrigerator, each has a TV, shower and toilet. It is possible to place an additional clamshell.

The hotel has the opportunity to relax and have fun. It offers a disco, a beauty salon, a library. For families with children there is a wonderful playground, babysitting services are provided at an additional cost. For business people there is a seminar room.

Hotel visitors have a huge amount of entertainment to choose from:

  • visit to the water park;
  • water transport rental;
  • massage sessions;
  • a game of bowling or billiards;
  • water sports.

The price includes three meals a day. There is a luggage room for storing valuables and a safe. The rooms are serviced every day by well trained staff. Arriving here, you will understand that in a place like Adler, hotels near the sea, service, entertainment, everything is aimed at making your vacation unforgettable.

Hotel "Tower"



Arriving in Adler, a hotel by the sea is the first thing that vacationers are looking for. And they find, because there are a huge number of them. There are also small hotels, such as hostels. This category includes "Tower". Only seven rooms, but at the same time they are all equipped in accordance with all modern requirements. The hotel staff is small, but copes with all responsibilities. Many vacationers prefer such mini-hotels.

Forward



Another small hotel is Forward. Sixteen rooms are located in a small building, but are equipped with all technical facilities for the convenience of guests. There are standard rooms, and there are studio rooms with and without a kitchen. Reasonable prices and beautiful design of the rooms attract many tourists every year. For those who came to rest in Adler, the hotel by the sea will provide all possible tourist services.

Tourists reviews

People who visited the Black Sea in Adler brought with them exceptionally positive impressions. All Adler hotels are clean and have a high level of service. The pricing policy is designed for different customers and everyone can choose the option that suits them. Most of all, vacationers like the proximity of the sea. In addition, various entertainments amaze the imagination. No one here misses: neither adults nor children. Everyone has something to do and where to go. A huge number of excursions cause a storm of emotions. One thing is for sure: a vacation in Adler is a great idea.
