Vietnam, Nha Trang. Reviews for holidays in November

Resorts of this former socialist country are very fond of Russian tourists. There are a lot of them, and they usually go by big noisy companies. Domestic vacationers also like to leave detailed and detailed reports about their travels and experiences. One of the most popular resorts in the fraternal country of Vietnam, Nha Trang, is no exception. November (we will analyze the reviews of tourists about this month of rest below) is a time when agencies do not like to organize excursions there. After all, this is the season when Vietnam has wet winters and it often drizzles. But this is not a hindrance to rest!



Vietnam, Nha Trang. Atmosphere Reviews

Most tourists who relax in this country enthusiastically describe not only the picturesque bays of the resort, which on a foggy day look especially mystical, but also a surprisingly welcoming atmosphere. It is she who makes Nha Trang so attractive. Friendly, good-natured people who take pictures with you with pleasure, - this is how most tourists describe the locals.

Vietnam, Nha Trang. Reviews about the sea



Vacationers are usually satisfied with the coast, where they have to sunbathe and swim. They also note that both Vietnamese and visitors like to dine at the coastal cafes that set tables right on the beaches. It would seem that the heaps of garbage that remain after such gatherings will overshadow the life of morning tourists. But no! From early morning the sand is not just cleaned, but also invited by special machines, so everything is clean. In unfamiliar people, some waves are caused by big waves. But for most tourists, they did not provide obstacles in order to enjoy the sea baths. However, you need to be careful and always take sunblock with you even in November - the sun, when it comes out, is very scorching. Russian tourists here bathe at night - and even a typhoon does not bother them. It rains every other day, but not constantly. It is better to choose beaches away from the center - you need to go a little or drive, but they are just perfect there, as in the picture.

Vietnam. Nha Trang. Reviews about accommodation and excursions



Small hotels are popular here, but they are cozy and clean, with a family and warm atmosphere and friendly staff. Independent tourists prefer to stay in city hotels - without a pool, but with Wi-Fi and close to the beach. Excursions are advised to book not from domestic companies, of which there are no fewer than in Thailand, but from local residents. They say that it is cheaper and better, especially since many of the indigenous inhabitants of the resort know Russian. Very interesting excursions in Vietnam. Nha Trang (reviews of tourists about it recommend to go to the island of Winperl, Dalat, see waterfalls, and so on) is a cheap departure point to many points. Such travels acquaint with the culture and traditions of the people, landscapes, marine fauna.

Vietnam. Nha Trang. Reviews about shopping and cuisine

Here you can buy almost everything that is sold in more hyped Thailand, but much cheaper. Moreover, experienced tourists are advised to purchase souvenirs and things at night markets. It is not only cheaper and cheaper, but also more interesting - because here you can often eat and watch various shows. They definitely recommend buying pearls and clothes made of cotton like “bamboo”. From food - of course, seafood. Delicacies such as lobsters and lobsters that are unthinkable at prices in our latitudes are quite affordable in Nha Trang. Especially in November, when the season is not holiday, and there are not very many tourists. They cook with you, and the portions are so huge that they can hardly be eaten together.
