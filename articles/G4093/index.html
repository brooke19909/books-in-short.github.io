Park "Academic": reviews and photos

Moscow is the most populated city in Europe, a millionaire city. There are many park areas, as befits any metropolis. Some have been equipped since the Soviet Union, but there are completely new ones. The Moscow government promises to refine all parks by the end of 2018.

Many of them have already been put in order, some should be worked on. The mayor of the city says that several recreational areas were practically created from scratch. At the moment, there are more than 100 recreation areas in Moscow. Their number continues to grow, which is good news.





Park in place of garages

Where the bases of the Zhilishnik GBU used to be, now there is a new Akademichesky park. The main hallmark of this recreation area is the green hills, they occupy most of the territory. At first glance, it might seem that these are golf courses. As it turned out later, trees and bushes are planned to be planted here in late autumn.

Previously, on the site of the new recreation area there were garages, farm buildings and a cleaning equipment park. This, of course, did not create any comfort. Now there are many beautiful winding paths covered with tiles, there are playgrounds, arbors, benches.

We finished landscaping the park at the end of summer. Residents of nearby houses were incredibly happy with such a surprise, because it is much more beautiful than garages and untidy territory.

The new park in the Academic region of Moscow pleased local residents, because now the recreation area is a few steps from the house. Appreciated the park and lovers of running in the morning.

Fashion on the hills

The area on which the park is now located was very prominent. This feature was made the main highlight of the recreation area. The hills are covered with artificial rolled grass. This creates the effect of English lawns and makes the area very neat. Beautiful pedestrian paths meander between the hills. In the evening and at night they are highlighted, which creates an indescribable romantic atmosphere.

How to get there

To see the new recreational area with your own eyes, you need to get to the Akademicheskaya metro station. The park is located on D. Ulyanov Street, so you can move right along the sidewalk and turn right before refueling. You can immediately go towards school number 199. If you pass by a kindergarten, the Red Cross Museum, you will find yourself in a park.





Park pergolas and gazebos

The main attraction of the park can be considered the main square and a large semicircular pergola. Eleven smaller pergolas, arbors and benches are scattered throughout the park.

Akademichesky Park has become a lifesaver for adherents of active pastime. Here they equipped a sports and game zone. It includes a football field, horizontal bars, a basketball court and the latest treadmills. There are several sports fields, including for physiotherapy exercises. Special zones are also reserved for children. One of them is decorated with designer models of chemical elements. There is room for kids to jump and climb: on swings, slides and other attractions.

Ads by Darwin

If you go through the main entrance, you can see three monuments to dinosaurs. First, the thought comes in that it is a Jurassic theme park, but no. This is an advertising move. It is likely that many children will be interested in these creatures, and to learn more about them, you can visit the Darwin Museum, which is very close.
