Tri Trang Beach Resort 4 *, Phuket: hotel reviews

If you are used to staying in comfortable, yet budget hotels, and chose Phuket Island (Thailand) as your vacation destination , Tri Trang Beach Resort 4 * can be a very good option for living.



Location, general information, photo

Three Trang Beach Resort is located in Phuket, a ten-minute drive from Patong. You can get here by free bus, scooter or taxi. The nearest airport is 36 kilometers away. The hotel has its own sandy beach. Also two kilometers away is the famous Patong Beach.

This hotel has at its disposal 244 rooms of the categories Standard, Deluxe and Junior Suite. They are located in several buildings located at different levels. Many have great sea views. All rooms have a balcony, comfortable furniture, TV, small fridge, air conditioning, bathroom, electric kettle, hairdryer.





The hotel has two outdoor pools, a restaurant, a bar, a massage parlor, a gym, a tour desk and much more.

There were no complaints about the quality of the work of the maids by our compatriots. So, a thorough cleaning of the rooms is carried out every day. Check in, departure

According to travelers, accommodation of guests who have just arrived by their proper rooms at Tri Trang Beach Resort 4 * (Phuket) starts at 2 p.m. This is due to the fact that the maids need to clean and prepare the rooms just released by previous guests of the hotel.
