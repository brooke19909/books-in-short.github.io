Navigator without internet for Android. The best navigators

Technologies of the modern world are developing exponentially. If before people traveled by car and used maps for navigation, now this is not necessary. Convenient car navigators have been invented, making it possible to navigate perfectly to any terrain. Today it is convenient, practical and allows you to save a lot of your personal time.

If you need a free navigator on your tablet or smartphone, then this article will help you choose it. It is devoted to the description of navigation applications for Android. You can find out which ones are the best and why. Also, you will choose a convenient navigator without the Internet for Android.

Yandex Navigator

This is a full-fledged GPS navigator on Android. It allows you to quickly and efficiently lay a route to the desired address, route, any attraction (museum, monument), another city. It should be noted that the navigator from Yandex takes into account the overlap of streets and even traffic jams. This allows you to always be sure that the path will be offered the shortest and most convenient.

This navigator contains maps of the main highways of the planet, however, routing is available exclusively on the territory of the Russian Federation and Ukraine. Detailed maps of the settlements of the countries indicated above are drawn in detail, therefore, the application will be convenient for the population.



Does the navigator work without internet? Yandex may work offline. You will need to load the desired card into the memory of your smartphone or tablet. But the search for various objects (shops, monuments, schools, restaurants, hotels) and building a route on Android requires the transmission of network data. This means that you can only see the electronic map. Therefore, the navigator for the iPhone without the Internet, as well as the device for the "Android" will work limited.

Advantages of the application

  • Voice prompts (choice of male or female).
  • Navigator control using voice commands.
  • Ability to add traffic events.
  • Online indicator of traffic congestion.
  • You can search for the necessary roads and objects.
  • For MTS subscribers free unlimited traffic is provided.

Navitel

Navigation system in a compact application. Now Navitel is considered one of the best applications for Android. Many will ask: why? Because this navigator works without an internet connection.





When setting up, you need to download maps from the Internet to a computer, and then download them to your tablet or smartphone. The application works with the help of a GPS satellite; it quickly builds a route and searches for the necessary objects.

Additional services (require internet connection):

  • free map updates;
  • various services ("Traffic", "Friends", "Weather").

You need to remember that the application is free only the first 30 days, then you need to purchase a license. Navitel works well in most countries of the CIS and the European Union. The application also has the function of optimal route selection and can be connected to video cameras of the traffic police.

Osmand

OsmAnd - a navigator without the Internet for Android. If you need navigation precisely in offline mode, then this application will be convenient for you. It is necessary to download maps and it will be possible to easily lay a route. Voice prompts help navigate the terrain. It is possible to go back along the track.

Some points in management. The destination on the screen is indicated by a red flag. Orientation follows the red arrow, which indicates the desired direction. The ability to create and edit various objects is available (your data is transferred to the application servers). This means that you can independently indicate on the map the place where the monument or any restaurant or cafe is located.



An excellent navigator without the Internet for Android. But remember that the free version of the application allows you to download only ten cards.

Copilot

Another good navigator without internet for Android. It provides excellent detailed maps of the whole world, they are updated monthly. It is interesting that this navigator, when constructing a route, offers three of its options - the main one and two alternative ones. There is a convenient walking mode (for pedestrians) - absolutely all small buildings and any shops are indicated on the map. There is a function that allows you to publish information on your route and location on social networks (Twitter, Facebook). One of the distinguishing features is that you can make calls from your smartphone without leaving the navigator.





The application is completely free. But there is a full version of it (it is paid), which additionally provides voice prompts and gives access to maps in 3D mode.
