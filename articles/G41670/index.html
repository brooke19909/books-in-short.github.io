Where to go to Spain in the fall?

How fast the summer flew by! It’s a pity that it’s already impossible to bask in the sun and plunge into the cool river or sea water ... Besides, the majority is given a vacation only in September - where to go? To Spain, the answer is obvious. This sunny, bright, hospitable country will return you to warmth, because in September the summer is in full swing there.





In the central regions of the country, air temperature exceeds 30 ° C, and in the north +27 ° C. That is why in September those tourists who want to extend the summer season come here from all over the world.

By the end of the month it’s getting a little cooler, the amount of precipitation is increasing, it can storm at sea. But the weather is still very comfortable for relaxation. The cost of tours to Spain begins to decline, the price of services in hotels becomes lower, therefore, during this period, holidays will cost you much cheaper.

Many even experienced tourists can not imagine what happens in Spain in September. Where to go and where it is better to spend this time, we will try to figure it out.

In September, holidays in the Canary Islands and Catalonia are especially good. The famous seaside resort of Salou is most suitable for youth recreation. It is famous for its magnificent beaches, a huge number of amusement parks, the most popular of which is “Port Aventura”. The resort has a very lively nightlife. There are many restaurants, bars, cafes, discos and clubs. Local sightseeing tours will be organized for you.



Where to go to Spain in September for beach lovers? First of all, the resorts of the beautiful island of Tenerife. Here you can relax on any of the favorite beaches, even located on the territory of fashionable hotels. Payment is made only for services - rental of umbrellas, mattresses, deck chairs and for various water attractions.

Often people are interested in travel agencies, where to go to Spain in September. It all depends on what kind of vacation you prefer. Many will be interested in amazing city tours with visits to the historical and architectural sights of Spain. You definitely need to visit Barcelona and see the ancient buildings in Tarragona, see the ancient city of Girona, which preserves the monuments of various cultures.





Young parents are often interested in where to go to Spain with a small child. Almost any resort in this hospitable country is suitable for such a vacation. Everywhere for families with children created excellent conditions. But the best resort for families with children (according to our compatriots) is the Costa Dorado. There is a magnificent dolphinarium, water park, many attractions.

If you are still interested in the question of where to go on holiday in Spain, we can recommend you the world famous resorts - Costa Blanca, Costa de Sol, Costa Brava, Mallorca. Holidays in this country will leave an unforgettable experience. Many come to Spain to admire the colorful festivals and carnivals that they know how and love to host.
