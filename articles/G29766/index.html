Tomsk Hotels - Travel Tips

Hotels in Tomsk are cozy apartments where you can have a good time, invite guests or arrange a business meeting. The hotels in the city will always offer decent service and quality service. The choice of such establishments is quite large. The best hotels of Tomsk are described in this article.





History

Tomsk is a city with more than four hundred years of history. The spirit of noble antiquity reigns in it, there are many stone and wooden architectural monuments of the 17th-20th centuries. Over its long life, this city has managed to visit the military-strategic, commercial and cultural center of Western Siberia. Since the mid-18th century, Tomsk has been a traditional place of exile, which was visited by outstanding minds of its time. Currently, the city is the oldest in Siberia scientific, educational, innovative center. To visit Tomsk means to make an exciting adventure and chat with interesting people.





general review

The best hotels of Tomsk are located in the heart of the village. On Lenin Avenue - the longest street of the city - you can see many local attractions, administrative and business centers, so the traveler is recommended to settle in this area. There are several excellent and affordable hotels here - Sibir, Oktyabrskaya, Forum, Toyan and others. Comfortable living in the city is relatively inexpensive. And if you were promised comfortable rooms and good service, be sure you will be provided with them. In Tomsk live surprisingly hospitable and helpful people who are always ready to help. Attentive and polite attitude to the guests can be felt from the first minutes of your stay in the city.

"October"

The three-star hotel "Oktyabrskaya" (Tomsk) is located in the historical, cultural and business center of the city. From the windows of her apartments, facing west, offers stunning views of the river. Tom and the vast expanses of Western Siberia. From the top floors of the opposite side of the building, you can enjoy the magnificent panorama of the city, architectural monuments and temples. The largest shops, restaurants, museums and theaters are literally a few steps from the hotel.

There are 48 rooms in Oktyabrskaya. They can accommodate 62 people. The hotel has 15 luxury apartments, 19 single standard rooms and 14 two-room apartments, designed to accommodate two guests.

Attentive staff, a welcoming atmosphere, a cozy cafe, comfortable rooms with an elegant interior - all this you will find in the Oktyabrskaya Hotel. Living in the most expensive room (three-room suite) costs from 8,000 rubles. per day. For a stay in a double two-room room you will have to pay from 2000 rubles.





"Siberia"

Another very convenient place to stay is the Siberia Hotel. Tomsk impresses with its old buildings, and the aforementioned hotel is located in one of them. This hotel has a prime location. It is located next to the Glavpostamt transport stops, which are the city's interchange junctions. In Siberia, a high level of service is maintained, responsive staff works, an extensive range of services is offered.

The hotel has several suites: a single "By the fireplace" costs 6400 rubles. per day, simple single - 5800 rubles., Apartments "Wedding" and "Studio" will cost 4600 rubles. In addition, Sibir has standard apartments: single (3300 rubles / day) and double (3300 rubles / day).

In the hotel you can rent a conference room, visit the cafe-bar (working hours - from 7.30 to 23.00), use the services of the hairdresser "Image". If you want to relax properly, we advise you to go on vacation to the local sauna.

"Satellite"

In the area of ​​the city garden and the central stadium "Trud" is the hotel "Sputnik". Tomsk immediately disposes to itself magnificent green spaces. The mighty trees here seem like equal elements of the urban landscape, like ancient architectural buildings or wide motorways. Staying in the "Sputnik" will allow the traveler to fully enjoy the local beauties. The hotel is located in a very picturesque corner of the city.

If you wish to save money, we recommend that you stay in economy-class rooms of a sectional type (a bathroom and a bathroom for five rooms) for a relatively low price (from 1150 to 1750 rubles per day). If possible, allow yourself to stay in a luxurious two-room suite for 4200 rubles per day. The hotel offers special apartments for long stays or for guests with children, equipped with all necessary household appliances: stove, microwave, dishes, refrigerator and much more. Also in "Sputnik" there are standard double and single rooms for 3500 rubles per day.





Bonaparte

A great place to spend time is the city of Tomsk. Hotel Bonaparte is a world of comfort and sophistication. It is located in the business center of the city. All kinds of benefits are provided here: comfortable rooms, free Wi-Fi, delicious breakfasts, slippers and bathrobes, a modern air conditioning system, SPA center, etc.

Accommodation in Standard rooms is from 3,700 rubles. per day. Free Wi-Fi and buffet breakfast are provided. For staying in a Studio class room you need to pay from 4700 rubles. per day. It also features Wi-Fi and a complimentary breakfast. In addition, a bathrobe and slippers, drinking water, an iron and an ironing board are available on request.

Deluxe rooms feature contemporary design furniture in a classic style. Room space - 26 sq.m. It has a bedroom, hallway, bathroom. Stylish interiors, high ceilings, nice views from the windows make the Deluxe apartments an ideal place for relaxing, receiving guests and working. The cost of living in this room is from 5400 rubles. per day.

The guest can choose other apartments to his liking - "Royal", "Romantic", "Apart", "Business Suite", etc. The area of ​​the room is from 33 sq.m. The cost of living - from 6200 rubles. per day.

"Forum"

In walking distance from the main attractions of the city is a three-star hotel "Forum". Tomsk is a place where people take care of architecture. The hotel is located in one of the historic areas of the city. Large-scale reconstruction of the building was carried out in 2003. Part of the room stock was updated in 2013.

Guests are provided with seventy rooms of various price categories, which have everything you need. The cost of living in the hotel apartments is from 1250 rubles. The hotel provides a range of additional services: sauna, hairdresser, sauna. Free breakfast is available in the cozy cafe. Directly upon placement, we advise you to pay dinner and lunch. In the building there are two hotels in Tomsk. In addition to the "Forum" there is the premises of the hotel "Siberia", owned by another owner.





"Toyan"

One of the most unusual and interesting places to stay in the city is the Toyan Hotel. Tomsk is sometimes called the "Siberian Athens", and from the windows of this hotel you can see the most significant historical and architectural monuments of the city. Around "Toyana" life is always in full swing.

The hotel offers four classes of rooms: "standard", "standard +", "attic suite", "suite". The cost of hotel accommodation is from 4200 rubles. The rooms are furnished in the spirit of antiquity, the walls of the rooms are decorated with stone. The atmosphere of luxury, grandeur and respectability is set off by a noble combination of rich red and warm mustard flowers with dark muted tones. All apartments of the hotel are equipped with comfortable exclusive furniture: leather sofas, marble tables, wide double beds with orthopedic mattresses. Not all hotels in Tomsk boast an interior of a similar level. We advise you to choose a room in the Toyan Hotel. There you can feel like an honored guest in a beautiful Siberian city.





"Union"

Hotel Soyuz (Tomsk) meets all modern standards and requirements. There are conditions for a comfortable stay of guests from around the world. The hotel fund has forty-three comfortable rooms in several price categories: "apartment", "luxury", etc. There is also a special honeymoon room furnished in a romantic style.

The guests are provided with additional services: laundry, wireless Internet, cable TV, safes, hairdresser, and ticket office. There is a restaurant with excellent service and excellent cuisine, a conference room, a sauna with a pool and a warm parking for twenty cars. For regular visitors a flexible system of discounts. The cost of living in the hotel complex "Union" varies from 2800 to 5000 rubles.

Gogol

This is a new comfortable hotel in the very center of Tomsk. It has 24 rooms - from standard to two-room "suite" with a sauna and a jacuzzi. The apartments have a TV, minibar, telephone, safe, modern stylish furniture. The price includes breakfast, the menu - a lot of delicious dishes and drinks. The hotel is in close proximity to the city garden, the administration of Tomsk, the TSU building. Accommodation in a standard single room will cost 3600 rubles. per night, a single stay in the "suite" costs 8,000 rubles. per day.





Baston

Tomsk hotels are not only impressive establishments designed for dozens of guests. There are also small but very comfortable hotels. Very good reviews leave about the hotel "Baston", located near the railway station of the city. It has only six rooms, furnished in a classic style and decorated in bright colors. The apartments include a fridge, desk, bathroom with shower and hairdryer. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel. In Baston there is a shared kitchen where each guest can cook their own meals or try dishes from the hotel chef. The mini-hotel is located 25 kilometers from the Bogashevo airport, a five-minute walk from one of the most interesting sights of the city - the Bogoroditsa-Alekseevsky monastery. Prices for accommodation at this hotel are low.

Hotels in Tomsk offer travelers accommodation in rooms where the homely atmosphere is combined with a modern level of service and comfort. Each guest can choose a place to sleep and spend time, depending on preferences and opportunities. The city of Tomsk will certainly captivate you with beauty and hospitality!
