Sanatoriums and pensions Gurzuf: why you should choose them

The Crimean peninsula attracts a huge number of tourists from around the world on the shores of the Black Sea. In the hot summer months, the streets of Crimean cities are crowded with people. Sanatoriums and boarding houses of Gurzuf, a settlement within Big Yalta, have many years of experience in organizing treatment and recreation for guests from different countries.

Gurzuf

The history of this resort is very long. He survived many changes during the reign of various sovereigns, had many different names. After Crimea was first annexed to Russia, Gurzuf began to attract the Russian aristocracy. This resort place was very popular with Pushkin and Chaliapin. In honor of Fedor Chaliapin, they even named one of the mountains of Gurzuf, where he once arranged a concert. An ancient park adjoins the Chaliapin mountain. On its territory is the very famous children's sanatorium "Artek". In Soviet times, millions of pioneers have dreamed for years of getting to rest that begins immediately upon leaving the Moscow-Crimea train. Sanatoria, boarding houses of Gurzuf were a dream for their parents.





Treatment in Gurzuf

Gurzuf is located at the foot of the mountain amphitheater. You can find it thanks to Ayu-Dag Mountain (Bear Mountain), located on the eastern side of the resort. The territorial location of the village is such that from almost all sides it is surrounded by various bizarre mountains and slopes.

Gurzuf resorts and resorts, due to its unique location and climate, provide services for the treatment of various respiratory diseases. The effectiveness of treatment can be judged by publications in serious medical journals. World-renowned experts have repeatedly visited motels and resorts located in the village of Gurzuf. Reviews of these visits dispel doubts even among the most incredulous skeptics. Rest and quality of service are there at a high level, and you can not even talk about the effectiveness of treatment. Gurzuf resorts and pensions have maintained their well-deserved reputation as a global health resort for many years, providing medical services at the highest level.
