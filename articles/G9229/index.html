Health camp "Borok": reviews, prices

With the advent of summer vacation, parents often wonder about how to organize their schoolchildren's vacation. Moreover, so that it is useful for the body and overall development. A successful and fairly affordable option is a children's health camp. In it, children are offered a fascinating and varied leisure: various events, sports training, and clubs. One of these institutions is the Borok children's camp. By sending their child here, parents can be sure that their son or daughter will spend their holidays with benefit and pleasure.

General information about the camp

Children's health camp "Borok" is located in the Volozhinsky district of the Minsk region ", in the village of Borok. It dates back to 1968 and today is one of the largest and best such institutions in Belarus. The camp area covers an area of ‚Äã‚Äãabout ten hectares.



Rest here is organized in four shifts. The first of them opens in early June, and the last closes by the end of August. The total shift duration is eighteen days.

At the same time, the institution can take 560 children into its walls. The Borok camp operates only in the summer and invites children from six to eighteen years old to rest. The teaching staff includes student counselors with many years of similar activities, as well as experienced educators and medical workers.

Accommodation conditions and meals

In the camp, children live in cozy brick two-story buildings. Younger students are accommodated in rooms designed for four people. Separate buildings are provided for older children. Eight children can live in these rooms.

Accommodation conditions comply with all sanitary and hygiene standards. It has all the amenities, heating, cold and hot water. The camp area is fenced with a strong fence and is guarded by police. Therefore, parents do not have to worry about the safety of their children.

As for nutrition, it is four times a day. Camp "Borok" offers children only tasty, varied and dietary food. The menu always has fresh fruits and vegetables, as well as juices. As parents describe their impressions, they feed well and the children are always full and happy with the variety. There is also a buffet in the camp, where children can independently purchase fresh pastries, water, juice.





Wellness activities

Recreation camp "Borok" is located in a picturesque area, among amazing nature, near the Isloch river. Ecological walks are regularly conducted with children in the forest and on the pond, where you can sunbathe and enjoy the beautiful views.

Pupils of the camp undergo a daily physical examination, where the well-being and condition of the child are most carefully assessed. The vast territory of the camp allows you to accommodate two football fields, a volleyball and a basketball court. There is also table tennis and a modern gym. Every day large-scale sporting events are held in the camp, which has a beneficial effect on the well-being and mood of children.

In addition, a psychologist‚Äôs office is working in the camp - an experienced specialist will help children get used to the new team and resolve problem situations.





Leisure activities

Leisure in the camp is organized in such a way that children do not have time to get bored. For everyone, various interest groups are organized: modeling, painting on wood, drawing, a studio of soft toys and others.

The smallest visitors to the camp are offered game rooms in dormitories. For older children, there is a summer stage and a dance floor, where discos are held in the evenings.

Children are under constant supervision. Experienced educators and counselors can find a fascinating activity for everyone and pick up a key for any child.

However, the Borok camp is not only entertainment. There is an extensive library for children where you can find books for every taste. Shifts are organized thematically and various cultural events are regularly held. The camp has a large hall for watching movies and concerts.

Over almost fifty years of history, many interesting traditions have appeared in the camp. One of them is a fun fair of soft toys and crafts created by the hands of the guys themselves from a wide variety of materials. During the shift, children can earn local money. And at the fair, which has been held in the camp for seven years, you can buy these cute toys and souvenirs for them.





Cost of stay

The cost of staying in the camp depends on the age of the child. The younger the student, the cheaper the rest. So, for the smallest vacationers - from six to ten years - the cost of a shift will be 3,231,000 Belarusian rubles. For children under thirteen - 3,313,000. To send a child aged fourteen to eighteen years old, you will need to pay 3,366,000 Belarusian rubles.

This amount includes accommodation, meals, and all kinds of recreational and recreational activities. So you can safely send your child to the Borok camp, holiday prices here are quite affordable for the family budget.

How to get to the camp?

The camp is located forty kilometers from Minsk, so it will not be difficult to get to it from there. You can get there either by regular bus or by private car. In any case, the travel time will be no more than one and a half hours. A trip to picturesque places will leave only pleasant impressions.





Reviews about the camp "Borok"

Both schoolchildren who visited the camp and their parents share their impressions of the camp. Children and adolescents mainly write about the entertainment program, events, discos. The older generation is much more concerned about issues related to life and nutrition, as well as the attitude of camp staff towards vacationers.

It can be noted that in most cases reviews contain words of gratitude and appreciation. Especially emphasized are comfortable conditions, quality food and attention to children. It is also important that the schedule of the day in the camp is designed in such a way that the child almost never remains without classes.

In fairness, it is worth saying that there are also dissatisfied parents. But, speaking of the Borok camp, reviews of this nature relate mainly to staying in the institution of younger schoolchildren, who are not yet fully prepared for an independent holiday and are banally homesick. And older children really like everything. Therefore, many guys come here to rest for many years and come back with great pleasure.

Indeed, in the camp, the student learns independence and the ability to fully communicate with peers. And its safety and health will be taken care of by experienced and qualified teachers and medical workers.
