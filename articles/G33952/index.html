We are going to Reutov. The city's attractions

Reutov is a city in the Moscow region, which is located very close to the capital. Very often, tourists who want to visit Moscow stop here. However, Reutov himself is considered no less interesting and beautiful. The sights of the city attract many people.

Reutov's story

Initially, on the site of the modern city was the village of Reutovo. The first mention of it is associated with the scribe book of the 16th century. The origin of the name is shrouded in various legends. According to one of them, the name Reutov is due to the fact that many years ago the Vladimirsky tract passed through the city, along which exiles were sent to Siberia. It was here that their mourners supposedly stopped and mourned for their loved ones. The second version is based on the fact that Moldovan merchants who came from the Reut River once lived here. Some historians claim that the name is associated with a large number of howler bears that lived in these places. And finally, the most plausible version says that once there passed the signal line of defense of Moscow with bells, which were called reuts.



Reutov Attractions

The city is famous for many memorable places that have long been chosen by tourists who come to the Moscow region for excursions. The main attractions of Reutov we will consider in this article.



Temple of the Kazan Icon of the Mother of God

In the early 90s of the last century, an Orthodox community was formed in the city of Reutov. However, she did not have a special room, and services were held in the apartment of a residential building. The mayor of the city undertook the construction of the temple, and within three years, with the participation of the entire population, the Temple of the Kazan Icon of the Mother of God was erected. The main altar is dedicated to the icon, and the relics of the holy martyrs Alexander Smirnov and Fedor Remizov are stored in the walls of the temple.

Church of the Holy Trinity

July 20, 2008 city residents celebrated the patronal feast of the Temple of the Kazan Icon of the Mother of God . From here, the flock in procession went to the place of construction of the future Church of the Holy Trinity, where the first stone was laid and a cross was installed. The construction lasted four years, and in 2012 it was consecrated.

Chapel of St. George

In the center of the city is a memorial complex called "Reutovites who died for the Fatherland." On the territory of the complex is the Chapel of St. George, built in memory of those who died here in the name of the Motherland.

Start Stadium

The city stadium is deservedly the pride of the locals. Well-equipped football, volleyball and basketball fields, tennis courts, bicycle and jogging tracks, an excellent gym. Competitions of international level are often held at the stadium, and Reutov players can engage in their favorite sport under the guidance of experienced tutors.



Museum of Local History

The sights of Reutov are definitely worth seeing. In the late 90s, according to the decision of the City Council, a museum of local history was opened here. The main purpose of its discovery was to involve young people in the cultural life of the city. The museum has been around for almost 20 years. Over all these years, workers have collected a lot of antiquities, documents and photographs related to the history of the city. Currently, the museum is under reconstruction, however, some halls continue to work and take excursions.

Today, there are many paintings of different directions and eras. For local residents, personal exhibitions of artists from Moscow and the Moscow Region have long been familiar. Museum and Exhibition Center

Upon arrival in the city of Reutov, the sights of which are described in the article, you should definitely visit the museum and exhibition center. The road to discovering it was very long. On November 19, 1958, a local history society was founded in the city. Over the course of a long 30 years, his members collected documents, photographs, and memoirs of veterans. However, only in 1991 a room was allocated for the local history society. The opening of the exhibition halls took place three years later.

If you decide to visit the Moscow region, be sure to visit the city of Reutov. The sights of this place will leave pleasant memories.
