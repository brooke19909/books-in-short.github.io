Knitted boleros are an important element of the wardrobe

Recently, knitted boleros have gained particular popularity. These products can be of the most varied colors and sizes, but at the same time they always favorably emphasize the image of a girl or woman. They can be connected in various ways. Everything will depend on what technique you prefer.



Products made with a hook look especially beautiful. They are distinguished by filigree and originality. Most often they are made on the eve of the summer season or visiting the holiday. In the latter case, a very thin yarn with the addition of lurex is even purchased. Often, such knitted boleros can become the main decoration of a festive outfit. You can wear it with a simple straight dress. This will give the image a special sophistication and emphasize the style. Many brides, planning their celebration, order for themselves similar products that harmoniously combine with a wedding dress, warming and making the image complete.



If special requirements for clothing are not established, many women put on knitted boleros, going to work. Such a product can seriously compete with a business suit, especially if it is made with high quality, is ideally sized and has a long sleeve.

You can knit these products from a wide variety of yarn. If it is intended for cold time, then it is worth giving preference to threads thicker. They will allow you to create a voluminous warm product that will pleasantly warm your back on a cool evening. For the summer, of course, it is better to choose a thinner thread. Of course, in this case it will take longer to knit. But the resulting product will amaze everyone around you with its airiness. Especially if you have chosen a hook as a working tool. With its help, it will be possible to easily create an openwork model that will harmoniously combine with a summer wardrobe.



If we talk about styles, then knitted boleros can be different. If you are a beginner needlewoman, you can easily make a model consisting exclusively of sleeves. To do this, you will need to connect a rectangle, the length of which will be equal to the two desired lengths of the sleeve and the width of the back. The width should be greater than the girth of the hand in the widest part. Having connected such a rectangle in any convenient way, it will be necessary to sew it on both sides (to make sleeves). The non-crosslinked part can be tied in any convenient way.

Needleworkers with experience can pay attention to more complex models. In this case, knitting patterns of bolero will certainly help to cope with the task. Particularly elegant look models made with the formation of the armhole. They are perfect for a business suit and evening dress.

You can choose a suitable product by studying the relevant resources on the Web or magazines that traditionally publish not only an image, but a detailed description of it.

Now you know a lot about how to choose and create knitted boleros! Photos and diagrams will help you in this process! Good luck! Compliments and admiring glances are guaranteed!
