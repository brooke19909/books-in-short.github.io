How Cheap St. Petersburg Hotels Meet Their Requirements

A huge number of people come to the Northern capital every day, both on business and simply, to relax. And the first problem that worries the guest is the need to get settled, and as comfortable as possible, while spending a small amount of money. Many guests primarily pay attention to the goodwill at the meeting and the good attitude of the staff. Others pay attention to cleanliness of the room and additional inexpensive services. Today, competition among hotels is high, so many inexpensive hotels in St. Petersburg will please you with an atmosphere of carelessness and peace of mind, they will consider you not just a client, but a true friend.

Inexpensive hotels will appeal primarily to tourists, but are also popular among business people, businessmen who come to the capital as necessary. The question arises, how do budget hotels in St. Petersburg meet their requirements? Many hotels easily refute this misunderstanding with their quality, but at the same time inexpensive services. Imagine that having arrived in the city in the morning, you check into a hotel, if not a business class, but you have everything you need to stay, and besides, you have a huge choice of a good hotel. For the day you have important things to do in the city, so there is no need to overpay for services that you do not need. In each hotel you will find comfortable furniture and large tables where you can adequately receive guests. You do not even have to visit the store, as the hotel has everything you need, even your own kitchen.

It is wrong to believe that inexpensive hotels in St. Petersburg do not make any efforts to develop quality of service, which attract customers at a low price. Due to the high competition today in St. Petersburg, many hotels will show that comfort does not have to be paid much. Despite the fact that in inexpensive hotels you will not be brought breakfast to bed, you will not be given a room with billiards or bowling. But this is not the main thing, is it? In fact, many of the clients visiting the city choose places for temporary stay, primarily paying attention to its low price, as well as its location in the city. Many hotels of the northern capital are located in the most quiet and clean places of the city, where you are not afraid of noise and air polluted by cars. Often, hotels in St. Petersburg are located near metro stations. Nearby there are many shops and places of recreation, as well as attractions. For tourists, this is an invaluable advantage.

What could be better than spending the night in a cozy and comfortable hotel room? Many hotels in St. Petersburg will be a good place for you to relax, where you can gain strength after long walks in the capital. Upholstered furniture, a comfortable sofa and a good TV make you feel really comfortable. What else is needed?

Many customers who have left reviews about hotels in St. Petersburg will reveal to you all the advantages and qualities of such establishments. The chain of many hotels will pleasantly surprise you with their comfort and complement the impressions of a trip to the Northern capital. The hotel is a good choice, compared with a rented apartment, daily free housekeeping, the availability of inexpensive additional services make many people choose the first. In this case, you do not have to overpay a lot of money for the well-known names of advertised business class hotels. Inexpensive hotels in St. Petersburg is the best choice not only for temporary tourists, as they are also popular among residents of the capital who want to just relax. If you do not believe, you can see for yourself. Having visited at least once in such a hotel, you will save money and relax.

Let's look at a few examples of inexpensive hotels.

EuroHostelSpb Hotel

Just five minutes from Nevsky is EuroHostelSpb. This is a reasonably priced hotel. He is ready to accept 50 people. Of course, not every room is equipped with cosmetic sets, however, a shower and a telephone are in each. Also, if you want to cook something in person, there is a guest kitchen equipped with everything you need. Cost - from 800 r. This is subject to availability and beds. But, in general, the hotel is far from bad.

Hotel Azhur

Of course, this hotel is much better than the previous option, but the price is 300 rubles. more. All rooms are equipped with modern technology. In each you will find: safe, internet access, refrigerator, telephone, TV, hairdryer and bathroom. A considerable advantage of the hotel is its location with metro station Chernyshevskaya.

Hotel Elegy

The hotel rooms are very reminiscent of home decor. The hotel offers digital telephony services with international access. Very close to the hotel is the Hermitage, Yard Square, Summer Garden, Kazan Cathedral. This is very important for visitors. Just left the hotel and the architectural heritage of the city is already in front of you. The hotels will offer you laundry services, ticket booking, excursion services. The only drawback is its rather noisy location.

Hotel Sonata

This is a fairly inexpensive hotel, from 500r. per day. But each room has everything you need. Bathroom, shower, hair dryer, towels, TV. What else is needed? The hotel is located next to metro station Ploschad Vosstaniya. Nothing bad can be said about this hotel, except that the staff is a bit slow.
