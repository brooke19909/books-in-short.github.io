Samara recreation center "Polytechnic": everyone can afford a vacation

The Samara recreation center "Polytechnic" functions as a sanatorium and a children's recreation camp. Especially in the summer, the guesthouse is in high demand among tourists and residents of the region. An indisputable advantage is its location - 50 km from the city center, in the middle of a grove, surrounded by fir trees and picturesque vegetation. Nearby two rivers flow : the Volga and the Zhiguli, where you can swim in hot weather.





The recreation center "Polytechnic" offers tourists comfortable accommodation in two-story cottages and log houses. For example, five-seater houses are equipped with necessary furniture. The rooms are divided into sleeping and kitchen areas. All villas are well-equipped and equipped with kitchen sets, household appliances (microwave, refrigerator, kettle). There is also a glazed veranda overlooking the forest. Arriving as a large company, you can live in an eight-seat cottage or in a two-story log house with a large hall and five rooms.

"Polytechnic" (recreation center) has a fairly spacious territory, which houses a sauna for 6 people. Convenient parking for private cars is also built. Especially for business travelers, the Polytechnic recreation center offers technically equipped conference rooms for 100 people, banquet and VIP rooms. If necessary, professional staff will help in organizing various events, order a transfer, book tickets.





For active holidaymakers there are sports fields, a football field, a billiard room. Guests can go boating, fishing, cycling or exploring the area on foot. In winter, skis, sledges, skates are at your service. This is a great place for productive wellness activities.

The recreation center "Polytechnic" (Samara) will appeal to lovers of a calm and measured pastime. Large companies, couples with children and elderly people will like it here. Sports and playgrounds, developing centers, and creative rooms have been built for children. Funny animators entertain children with interesting programs, scenes in which children themselves can directly participate and receive prizes.

While the kids are under the supervision of experienced educators and are busy with games, parents can walk along forest paths, enjoy the virgin nature, admire the Volga and Zhiguli rivers. Those who want privacy, peace and relaxation will be able to get all this at this tourist base. Wildlife helps to recharge with positive energy, calm the nervous system, normalize blood pressure.





The recreation center "Polytechnic" is suitable for adventure lovers. Comfortable sightseeing buses will take tourists to the holy springs, the Zhigulevsky Reserve and other, equally interesting and memorable sights. Everyone will be able to find an acceptable option for recreation and entertainment.

This resort will give a lot of positive, pleasant memories and good mood - all this at an affordable cost. If you plan to spend time away from megacities, noisy freeways and large crowds, then you have a direct road to the Samara tourist center.
