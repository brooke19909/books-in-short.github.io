Do-it-yourself jumper: how to make an egg jumper

What material can I make a bouncer with?

The material, as a rule, is the simplest, what is usually found, as they say, "at hand":

  • stationery glue;
  • egg;
  • stationery erasers and paper.

Of course, the main materials are listed here. It does not take into account, for example, a stick for stirring glue, a brush, etc.

How to make a bouncer out of an egg

The easiest way is to use a chicken egg.

You will need:

  • 1 chicken egg;
  • 1 bottle of table vinegar (1 liter).

The jumper can be made on the basis of raw or boiled eggs. However, a boiled egg skipper is more resistant to shock, so it is recommended to use it.

Manufacturing Method:

  1. It is necessary to boil the egg and place it in a glass jar with a capacity of one liter.
  2. Fill the jar with vinegar (right down to the neck), close the lid and place in a cool dark place for three days.
  3. Rinse the egg in cool water. The shell on the egg will completely dissolve (vinegar will actively eat it). The egg should visually become a little transparent and very elastic, tight (resembling a rubber ball). In addition, it will significantly increase in size (compared with raw).

That's all. It turns out that the answer to the question: "How to make a jumper from eggs and vinegar?" simple enough.

Attention! A jumper from an egg (even from a boiled egg) is quite brittle, so you should not beat it too hard on the floor.

The oil does not have the property of protein compaction and will not be able to corrode the shell.
