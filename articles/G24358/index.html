Huawei Ascend P7 Smartphone: reviews, specs, and specifications

As part of this material, the Huawei Ascend P7 will be described . Reviews, specifications, specifications and other important information - all this will be discussed in detail in the article. Despite the fact that this device went on sale in May 2014, even now its capabilities are enough to solve a wide range of tasks.





Smartphone hardware

Powerful computing resources are at the heart of the Huawei Ascend P7. Owner reviews indicate that this gadget copes with the task of any level of complexity. Because it is equipped with a 910T quad-core processor from HiSilicon Kirin. Each core at maximum load is capable of operating at a frequency of 1.8 GHz and is built on the Cortex-A9 architecture. It is important to understand one essential point. The 4 cores of the A7 architecture will be weaker than the A9. In turn, a similar processor with exactly the same number of computing modules on the A15 will be more productive than the A9. Architecture "Cortex-A15" is still rare. Therefore, this processor boasts an excellent level of computing power. The CPU complements the graphics adapter model 450 MP from Mali. In terms of its performance, this solution belongs to the top segment. Such hardware resources are more than enough to solve any problem: from surfing websites to demanding 3D games. With all this, the smartphone can easily cope.

Memory subsystem

Superbly built memory subsystem of Huawei Ascend P7. Reviews from happy owners are proof of this. The RAM in this smartphone is 2 GB of the DDR3 standard. This is enough with the head for a comfortable and smooth operation of the device. The internal memory in it is 8 GB. Of these, about 6 GB is allocated to the needs of the user. They are enough to install many programs and place 5-6 films in MP3 quality. You can significantly increase the amount of memory by installing an external flash drive with a maximum size of 32 GB.





Ergonomics, body and usability

An interesting solution was found by designers when developing the Huawei Ascend P7 BLACK case. Owner reviews indicate this. The case of this monoblock with touch input is completely made of tempered glass "3rd generation Gorilla". And this applies both to the front of the gadget and the back. Only around the perimeter is it framed by a metal strip. To prevent dirt and fingerprints from appearing on the glass surface, a special 7-layer coating has been developed that masks them. The left edge of the smartphone - without controls. All buttons are displayed on the right side. There are volume swings and a button to turn off the device. The volume down key allows you to quickly take pictures. To do this, just press it twice in the locked state of the smartphone, and in less than 2 seconds you will see the result. In the upper part of the device, in addition to the previously specified 8 megapixel camera, there is a light sensor and a speaker. A headphone jack is connected to the top edge of the gadget. Under the 5-inch screen there are three touch buttons: "Menu", "Back" and "Home". A microphone and a MicroUSB connector are displayed on the lower edge of the smartphone. The device itself is currently available in two colors. In addition to the previously indicated black, there is also a white version. The Huawei Ascend P7 White smartphone will appeal more to the weak half of humanity, for which it is made.

Autonomy

This Chinese device has a 2500 milliampere / hour battery. Its resource with a minimum load is enough for 3 days. But with more intensive use of the smartphone - for 12 hours, and then you have to put the device on charge. The battery is built into the case, and if it becomes unusable, it will be problematic to replace it on its own. You will have to seek help from a specialized service center.





Software

An interesting set of software has a smartphone Huawei Ascend P7. Reviews highlight primarily the shell Emotion UI version 2.3 of its own design of a Chinese manufacturer and a special pop-up menu. Each of these components allows you to customize the gadget's interface according to the needs of the user. Due to this, you can significantly increase productivity and provide smartphone control with only one hand. Not every device with similar characteristics can achieve this.

Data exchange

The rich set of communication features of this smart phone. Among them are:

  • Support for all possible mobile networks today.
  • The Wi-Fi module, capable of operating at speeds of up to 150 Mbps.
  • Bluetooth 4th generation.
  • A navigation module that can work with both GLONASS and ZhPS.
  • For connection to a PC, the MicroUSB interface is provided. It is also used when charging the battery.
  • A separate connector allows you to easily connect an external speaker system to your smartphone.




Owner reviews

Perfectly balanced Huawei Ascend P7. Reviews from satisfied owners confirm this. It has everything: a powerful processor, a powerful graphics adapter, enough memory, an understandable and easy to use software shell. Its only drawback is the built-in battery, which can only be replaced with the help of a service center. But this is not so critical for the new device. Moreover, its cost is 440 dollars. Great smartphone at a modest price.
