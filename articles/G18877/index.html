How to make a snowman out of paper with your own hands?

Before the New Year holidays, handmade artists have the hottest time. The work is carried out with any materials, Christmas toys, decorative elements of the interior, door wreaths, greeting cards are made. Christmas trees and winter landscapes are depicted, Santa Claus and his granddaughter Snegurochka do not ignore the wizard and the snowmen beloved by their children.

They can be made not only from snow, but also from cotton wool and cardboard, paper plates and plastic cups, made up of quilling strips and glued by folding origami paper. They can be flat or voluminous. Stick on the base or stand in the middle of the room, hang on a rope on a Christmas tree instead of a toy.

In the article, we will consider how to make a snowman out of paper with your own hands, what options you can think of, what to have in addition for work. After reading the material, you will be able to repeat the samples seen in the photographs at home. Step-by-step explanations help you easily complete the task.

Stripe Option

Such a large snowman can be installed on cardboard, or can be used as a Christmas tree toy by hanging on a thread on a branch. To make each ball, you will need many thin, but dense strips of white paper. These should be segments of different lengths. If the bottom ball contains 15 cm, then the middle one is 10 cm, and for the snowman's head, take 6 cm.

So, how to make a snowman out of paper:

  • For fastening you will need a strong thread and a needle. One edge of the strip is pierced by a needle at a distance of 1 cm from the edge. All strips of the same length are successively threaded.
  • At the end, a strong knot is tied, but the thread is not cut, but stretches 10 cm up and a large knot is also tied.
  • Further the other edge of the strips is strung. The distance from the edge is the same - 1 cm.
  • After all parts are strung, a knot is finally tied.

The first ball is ready! The rest do the same. The balls can be fastened together with a glue gun or thick PVA.

How to make a snowman out of paper, you already understand. It remains only to decorate the product by attaching a hat of paper and tying a scarf from a satin ribbon. Hands can be made from yarn by slipping it between the strips.

Corrugated snowman

How to make a snowman out of paper in stages, you won‚Äôt have to tell for a long time. This work is affordable even for beginners:

  1. To create each circle, use several sheets of white paper A-4, depending on the size of the craft. For the lower part, you can take them from 6 to 8 pieces.
  2. Each sheet is bent with an accordion, the folds must be carefully smoothed, then they will keep their shape well.
  3. When all the blanks are completed, begin to collect the bottom ball of the snowman. Spread the last fold of each sheet with glue and attach one to the other.
  4. And the design is rounded off by connecting the first and eighth sheets.
  5. Then, pressing on the entire part, it is lowered to the base, smeared with glue. It is necessary to press down for fastening with the whole palm, press gently so as not to wrinkle the folds.

Christmas tree toy made of paper

Now we will tell you how to make a snowman out of paper so that it is light and can be hung on a Christmas tree branch:

  • A-4 sheet of white paper is folded in half and strips of 1 cm wide are drawn, but not completely (4-5 cm are not cut).
  • Then the part is expanded, and its edges are glued horizontally.
  • A thin strip of contrasting color is attached on top. This is the handle for which the craft can be hung on a branch.
  • You can decorate the craft with twig hands made of gray or brown paper and draw a face to the snowman.
