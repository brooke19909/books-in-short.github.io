Notebook Asus A6R: model review, photo

Laptops in the modern world almost occupy a leading position in sales and prevalence. It is worth paying attention to some budget models that have powerful features. One of these is the Asus A6R laptop . You can buy it in any specialized store at a price of about 30 thousand rubles. It is necessary to consider all its features in order to decide whether it is worth the money.



Characteristic

The diagonal of the monitor is 15.4 inches (1280x800), type WXGA. The screen has good color saturation. The viewing angles are simply amazing. However, if you slightly shift the screen to a vertical position, you will have to work hard to parse the image. Still, it is slightly distorted. From a battery with a capacity of 4400 mAh, the Asus A6R laptop runs for more than 2.5 hours and charges 1.5 hours, which is an acceptable result.

Notebook dimensions: 354x285x35 mm. The weight of the device reaches almost 3 kg. There are connectors for a microphone, headphones and flash cards. Drive - DVD-RW. The laptop supports Wi-Fi, Bluetooth (version V2.0 + EDR). There is a webcam at 0.35 Mr. The amount of memory is 2048 MB. Processor of the brand Intel, model Celeron M processor 360-380 (1.7 GHz). Operating System - Windows XP Professional. There are many special programs that allow you to freely communicate on the Internet.

Appearance

Ergonomics Asus A6R made at the highest level. It, as a "middleman" between the user and the software, is almost imperceptible. The keyboard has a pleasant appearance, it is not loud, so there will be no problems with it. The touchpad also suits almost all consumers. A nice bonus is the special buttons for opening a browser, mail, as well as a key for changing computer modes. All users claim that it is of high enough quality.

Equipment

Buying an Asus A6R laptop, you can find instructions, disks with some programs, a cable for the modem and a charger in the kit. Unfortunately, this is all that the manufacturer provides.
