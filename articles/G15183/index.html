The beaches of Krinitsa (Krasnodar Territory). Rest in the resort village

Summer has come, which means the vacation season has begun. Where to go to take a break from the bustle of the city, the noise of cars and stress? Of course, at sea! Fans of a measured and relaxing holiday should pay attention to the resort village Krinitsa of the Krasnodar Territory. There is everything for a relaxing vacation: forest and valley, river and mountains. Well and, of course, an excellent view of the sea surface! What is the history of the place? What holiday can this resort offer? Read on!





From a like-minded community to a beautiful resort

The history of the village is very unusual. Its name can be translated as "source" or "spring". In 1886, in a delightful place, on the shore of the Gelendzhik Bay, a community of intelligentsia appeared. Collected by her nobleman V.V. Eropkin. The idea that fascinated this aristocrat was populism. The first residents of Krinitsa coexisted together under the roof of a four-room house. Fourteen years later, the population increased to fifty. People were engaged not only in social transformation projects, but also in viticulture.

Even during the active actions of the Soviet government, the village with a poetic name continued to develop, while at the same time providing assistance to other similar-like settlements. The community, distinguished by its identity, operated for thirty whole years, right up to 1920.

Near the village was the Sheerman Artel, which also engaged in the cultivation of grapes. In the late twenties, the two communities merged, choosing the name Krinitsa for themselves.

Resort import substitution





Today Krinitsa of the Krasnodar Territory is not a comparable place to relax. The resort village is located in a picturesque river valley called Pshada, only 40 kilometers from Gelendzhik. Even from the farthest corner of the village, you can reach the sea in 20 minutes on foot. The way, by the way, will be very pleasant - fruit trees adorn the streets, fences are twined with vines.

Krinitsa will be enjoyed by couples, representatives of the older generation and children. An unforgettable vacation in the village will also be for scuba diving enthusiasts: the Black Sea here is characterized by impeccably clean water and allows you to see plants and animals. For this, scuba divers will need the simplest fins and mask.

Climatic conditions

The climate of Krinitsa can be attributed to the zone of dry and semi-dry subtropics. In summer it is slightly cooler here than in neighboring Gelendzhik. The reason for this is the cold water of the Pshada River. By the way, for the same reason, the sea in Krinitsa is slightly cooler than in other resort villages. But there are no cold winds here: Krinitsa is protected from them by Mount Shahan, 370 meters high.

Krinitsa beaches (Krasnodar Territory)





Finding the same quiet and beautiful place as Krinitsa is quite difficult. There are no noisy restaurants, night clubs. Nothing will disturb a sound sleep. Of course, the Cote d'Azur, clean pebble beach and untouched nature attract tourists. But in addition to this, Krinitsa can offer a lot of exciting entertainment. For example, in the village itself you can take a horse ride. On the main beach, vacationers are offered rental boats, scooters and catamarans. For tourists, showers, toilets, awnings and sunbeds are provided. Variety of relaxation will allow riding on bananas. By the way, a gentle staircase with 135 wide steps leads to the shore.

How to get there

Those who are going to Krynica for the first time are concerned about the question of how to get to the resort. There are several ways:

  1. The most convenient way to get to the village is from Gelendzhik. Bus services here begin at six in the morning and last until ten in the evening.
  2. Bus traffic to Krinitsa is well established from cities such as Novorossiysk and Goryachiy Klyuch. The distance between these settlements is about 100 kilometers.
  3. You can get to the resort from the capital of the Kuban - the city of Krasnodar. From morning till night, buses and fixed-route taxis run along this route.
  4. Tourists who prefer traveling by car need to go along the M4 DON highway. Road signs will help you navigate.
