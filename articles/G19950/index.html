Smartphone ASUS ZenFone Go ZB450KL 8Gb: owner reviews, specifications and features

ASUS is trying to keep up with the main players in the mobile device market and does not stop releasing new digital products. So the long-released Asus Zenfone Go ZB450KL 8GB smartphone received good ratings from users and gadget lovers. Despite the fact that its sales have been going on for a long time, it is still relevant.

Zenfone Go is a whole line of inexpensive, but at the same time productive devices for its price. In general, they are a reduced version of Zenfone 5. The main characteristics of the series are two SIM cards, a quad-core processor and good memory capacity.

Device description

Although the phone is designed for the average budget of the owner, its performance is quite stylish. The back side is rounded and pleasant to the touch, it seems like a metal case. Of the interesting features, it is worth highlighting the remote camera button, especially for selfies. This will please fans of taking pictures of themselves. Among the colors there is something to choose - silver, white, golden, yellow, blue and black. Productivity of the device and its device

‚ÄúUnder the hood‚Äù of the smartphone is the quad-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 410 MSM8916. This processor is an ARM chip designed for mid-range devices. By the way, he became the first 64-bit model from Qualcomm. The cores have a Cortex-A53 configuration. Display and its characteristics

It is interesting that in the official specifications the screen type is not indicated anywhere, however, judging by customer reviews of the Asus Zenfone Go ZB450KL 8GB, it is an IPS. Multiple touch supported, type - capacitive. Viewing angles are small, but budget explains a lot. The diagonal of the display is 4.5 inches with a resolution of 854 by 480 pixels. The total number of pixels per inch is 218. The screen is a standard of simplicity: in direct sunlight, the display will have to peer.





Multimedia device

As is customary on modern smartphones, Asus Zenfone Go ZB450KL 8GB has two cameras - rear and front. The first can take pictures with a resolution of 8 MP, and the second - at 2. The rear can be highlighted with an LED flash. To adjust the clarity there is an autofocus function. You can successfully use various barcode scanners.

Of the sensors on the device, there is an accelerometer that catches the position of the smartphone, a compass, probably so as not to get lost in the forest, a proximity sensor for a comfortable conversation and an ambient light sensor, which with this screen is very relevant.





The device can play MP3 and FM radio files, which is standard for many modern smartphones and is not particularly surprising.

Communication and communication components

The phone can work in all known protocols of cellular networks, including 4G. Which, in turn, has five ranges. Two slots for SIM cards differ in form factor - micro SIM and nano SIM. Their operating mode is alternate.

The built-in Wi-Fi module allows you to connect to a wireless network using the latest 802.11n standard. To communicate with devices there is Bluetooth 4.0 with backward compatibility support.

For navigation, GPS, Russian GLONASS or Chinese BeiDou are used. As an auxiliary satellite search engine, A-GPS is implemented.

Battery autonomy and capacity

Among the reviews on the Asus Zenfone Go ZB450KL 8GB there are many about the battery, which has 2070 mAh. Both positive and negative. Based on them, we can conclude that the battery holds an average of one day of use. For some, this is not enough, but someone is already used to such energy consumption. At the same time, the developers announced 16 hours of conversation and 13 in the mode of surfing websites over Wi-Fi.

Software and Interface

The smartphone has Android 6.0 installed. But this is not a feature of the phone. And the fact that inside is an exclusive ZenUI shell. It is optimized specifically for ASUS devices. It is an add-on for the interface. Improves the appearance and control of the device. Implemented gesture technology and special button combinations.

Features of the first use

Before use, it is worth giving some recommendations on the proper setup and use of the smartphone. Some of them are based on reviews of the Asus Zenfone Go ZB450KL 8GB, and some are taken from official manuals.





When installing SIM cards, it is important to remember that even though both slots support 4G, only one of them can work with the technology at the current time.

Zen UI has its own unique applications and features:

  • Power and acceleration. This mechanism closes unused processes and applications, thereby freeing up RAM and battery life. It is especially relevant after prolonged use of "heavy" applications.
  • Startup manager. It also optimizes processes and RAM by removing from the list of applications loaded at startup.
  • ASUS Mobile Manager. This is a set of tools for managing application settings and various characteristics, collected in one place. Setting permissions for programs, power saving, virus scanning, notification properties.
  • Blue filter. This tool gives the screen soft, calm tones that are optimal for reading.

As for installing firmware or obtaining root rights, this device is not provided for such actions. The fact is that its bootloader is locked, and it is currently impossible to remove this protection. Standard updates are installed normally or through recovery.





By the way, starting recovery mode is quite unusual. First you need to turn off the smartphone, then hold down the volume and power buttons. In the menu that appears, select Recovery. After the picture appears, you need to press the power button again, and then the volume up.

Also, when buying this smartphone, an additional 100 gigabytes of space for a period of 2 years was allocated to an account in the Google drive.
