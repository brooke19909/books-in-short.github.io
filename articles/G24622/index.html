RC "Namangan Bowling" in Lublin: address, reviews

The entertainment center "Namangan Bowling" in Lublin is a large complex that harmoniously combines a night club, restaurant, bowling, billiards and a beauty salon.





Restaurant "Golden Oasis"

Restaurant "Golden Oasis" is a rather large institution, consisting of two halls; the large one can accommodate up to 450 people, which allows organizing mass banquets here, and in the small one - about 100 people.

The restaurant's interior is made in a combination of blue and peach colors, and the furniture is decorated with white wraps. The hall has a large and beautifully decorated podium for newlyweds, the presence of which allows you to arrange magnificent weddings here.

Dishes of Japanese and European cuisine are offered to its guests by the restaurant in the Namangan Bowling (in Lublino). The menu in this institution is not one - in addition to the main one, there is also a separate list of dishes intended for banquets. There is also a children's menu, excellent wine and tea cards, as well as a large assortment of drinks at the bar.

Within the walls of the restaurant, guests can enjoy meals accompanied by live music that plays here daily.

The restaurant also provides targeted food delivery services. You can place an order by calling the phone number listed on the official website of the Namangan Bowling RC (in Lublino) in the "Food Delivery from Kemiroff" section, or by filling out the online form with the list of desired food there. Delivery is carried out only if the total amount of the order is at least 1,500 rubles. This service is completely free.

The restaurant welcomes guests every day from noon to three in the morning.





Night club "Sapphire"

The Sapphire night club every evening gathers Moscow youth on its dance floor, who love nightlife. The hot atmosphere constantly reigns in Sapphire, which is located in the entertainment center Namangan Bowling in Lublin. Photos taken during parties perfectly convey the mood of guests here.

The club has its own menu and an excellent bar menu with a good assortment of cocktails, as well as alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. Until the morning, guests of the place are lit on the dance floor to hot hits from local DJs.

The Sapphire club has an accumulative discount system in the form of registered club cards, which all interested guests of the establishment can purchase for settlement at the box office and at the entrance.





Beauty Salon "Natalie"

No less visited place in the entertainment center "Namangan Bowling" in Lublin is a beauty salon, which is called "Natalie".

It provides hairdressing services, and there is both a female and a male room. There is a good solarium. Of the range of services that the salon provides, the most popular are styling, haircuts, coloring, manicures, pedicures, nail extensions and other ways to care for them. It also provides the services of professional makeup artists and skin care specialists. Among the services provided by the beauty salon "Natalie", which is located in the RC "Namangan Bowling" (in Lublino), there is also an epilation.

The salon regularly holds promotions. So, new customers "Natalie" can use the services of masters with a 15% discount. A discount in the same amount is also provided to pensioners, but only if they use the services before 12 noon (the offer is valid on any day of the week).





Billiards

Fans of companionship can visit the billiards at the Namangan Bowling RC in Lublino. There is a large billiard room, made in green. At the disposal of guests this club offers 8 tables, which are designed to play Russian billiards.

Before visiting the billiard room at the Namangan Bowling in Lublino, you must pre-book a game table, which can be done by phone, which is indicated on the official website of the entertainment center. As for prices, renting one table on weekdays is much cheaper than on weekends or on holidays (on weekdays - 400 rubles / hour, and on weekends - 600 rubles / hour).

All those who love the game of billiards can also enjoy the convivial bowling game in Namangan (in Lublin).





Bowling

Bowling is the entertainment for which guests most often come to the Namangan entertainment center. Here, guests have at their disposal eight game tracks, one of which guests can rent for themselves and their company for a certain time. To do this, you just need to call the telephone number posted on the official Namangan Bowling website in Lublin. For the reservation it is enough to indicate the number of the desired track, the time of the visit, the period during which the guests plan to play, as well as the number of people.





As for prices, the cost of renting a track from Monday to Thursday is much lower than from Friday to Sunday or on weekends (from Friday to Sunday - 600 rubles / hour, on other days - 400 rubles / hour). But, despite the higher cost on weekends, as a rule, all the tracks are busy, as many Muscovites spend their free time at the Namangan Bowling (in Lublin).





Bowling Reviews

Guests of the entertainment center, as a rule, really like to go bowling. It is here, according to vacationers, that you can truly relax and immerse yourself in an exciting game in the company of your family or friends. In addition, according to vacationers, during a game of bowling you can not only relax, but also play sports, since a good game requires active movements, and this even helps to lose a few unnecessary pounds.

Guests who come to the Namangan RC to play bowling note that it is here that some of the best tracks in the city are located.

Also, visitors are pleased with the presence of a small cafe-bar, which is located directly on the bowling alley. Here, guests can replenish their lost energy with light snacks, enjoy a cup of tea, coffee or cool off with lemonade.

How to get there

Namangan Bowling in Lublin can be found near the Semashko bus station and the Bulbert fur Brand store. Also in the same building with the Namangan RC is the famous Burger Club fast food chain. The nearest metro station, which is located near this entertainment center, is called "Lublino".

Address "Namangan Bowling" (in Lublino): Moscow, st. Stavropol, 38/2.

The metro station closest to this point of the city is Lyublino, which is 576 meters from the indicated address. Therefore, getting to the entertainment complex is very convenient, using this particular form of public transport.

You can also get here by bus number 228 or 54 (exit at the stop "Hospital named after Semashko" or "Novorossiyskaya street"). You can also get here using passing minibuses.

All visitors to the entertainment center who plan to get to it by car can use the free parking lot located at the entrance to the complex.
