Characteristic

Lake Michurin has high banks, but not steep. In the northeastern part, sandy beaches prevail. Most often, these places are chosen by tourists for recreation. Most of the coast is plowed and covered with meadows. And in a distance of 10-20 m from the water, reeds grow, and sometimes these sections are up to 50 m long. Horsetail, bush grass and lobelia grow in the meadows.

Holidays on Lake Michurin

The coast of the lake is well developed. There are several recreation centers. For example, the hotel-garden "Michur Inn." For accommodation, rooms of different levels of comfort are offered. There are children's play areas on site. There is a restaurant, meals are provided. There is an indoor pool.

The recreation center "Michurinsky Dvorik" is located in the Priozersky district, located on the lake. There are 14 rooms for living with a bathroom. It features a tennis court, a sauna and an outdoor pool. There is parking.

The recreation center "Three Bears" offers tourists a comfortable cottage with a kitchen, bathroom and shower. There is a TV, microwave. There is a bathhouse at the base. Those who prefer to spend time actively are invited to visit the ski resort, which is 5 minutes from the Three Bears.

Fishing

Those wishing to relax in nature, having a picnic, have always been attracted to Lake Michurinsky. Fishing is also popular here. A lot of fish live in the reservoir, for example, pike, roach, perch, burbot. The latter is a rare "guest". The likelihood of catching a burbot increases only in October, and in the winter months it is necessary to choose revenge, where the depth will reach up to 8 m.

In the past, this pond was very popular due to the vendace that lives in it, which is also very large. It was believed that it was spread due to the possibility of good spawning. But today, catches have declined significantly.

Lake Michurin is popular in summer and spring. It is believed that this is the best time for fishing. Nibble is good mainly on the west and north coast. In winter, there are fewer catches, but perch comes across at a depth of 4-6 m.
