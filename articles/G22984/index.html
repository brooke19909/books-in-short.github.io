Rest in the Chelyabinsk region. What recreation center on Lake Akakul is worthy of attention?

So, it's time for the holidays. Almost every resident of Russia thinks where to spend the summer season. Resting abroad on the seas is, of course, the dream of many. However, the costs that will have to be incurred during the trip, not everyone can handle. But no need to be upset. There are excellent guest houses in which the prices and living conditions are quite reasonable.

For example, pay attention to Lake Akakul. The recreation centers Zvezdny, Bereg, Flattering Manor and other tourist houses are located on its shores and are surrounded by beautiful dense forests. Here you can relax from the hustle and bustle of the city, sunbathe on the beach and swim in the clear water. Various entertainment programs are organized on their territories, cafes, shops work, there are playgrounds and many other entertainments. Arriving tourists get a lot of pleasure. That is why we recommend paying attention to these recreation centers. In order to get to them, you will need a map of the Chelyabinsk region.

This guesthouse is a unique place for spending a summer vacation with the whole family. It is located just 80 km from Chelyabinsk. Here everyone can feel like a part of pristine nature. Pine trees grow in the area. Very close is Lake Akakul. This is what makes the air unusually clean. Choosing a holiday home "Star", you can not only fully relax, but also improve your health.

On the territory there are complexes and houses intended for living. Here you can combine relaxation with sports. To do this, there are equipped areas where football, basketball, and volleyball are played. Those who like to walk through the forest are offered special tours.
