Olympia is a Greek water park in Vityazevo

Vityazevo is one of the most popular resort villages of the Krasnodar Territory. Any resident of Russia knows this name, and only after hearing it does everyone imagine the sound of the sea surf and gentle sunbeams. What is especially nice, you can now relax in a calm resort village without giving up traditional resort entertainment. The water park in Vityazevo welcomes guests throughout the tourist season.

Feel yourself a hero of Greek myths!



Many amusement parks today are decorated in some special style. Not an exception and the water park in Vityazevo "Olympia", sustained in the traditions of Greek myths and architecture. At the entrance to the entertainment complex you can see a real antique temple with columns, and on its territory each of the attractions is dedicated to a particular legend or deity. Go to the kingdom of Hades, visit the possessions of Poseidon, find a way out of the labyrinth of the Minotaur and remember the myth of Prometheus. All these adventures are available in one territory, and a visit to such attractions for a long time will be remembered by your whole family!

Address and visit rules



The water entertainment complex is located at Vityazevo Village, Golden Sands Street, Building 4. You do not need to pre-register, you can come to the water park any day and buy entrance tickets immediately before visiting. At the entrance to the entertainment complex, security officers inspect the personal belongings of the guests: it is not allowed to bring objects that could harm others or the property of the center to the territory. Ticket price depends on the chosen day and time of bathing. One adult ticket costs about 700-1000 rubles. Discounts are provided for children, their size depends on the growth of a young visitor.

Reviews about the complex of water attractions



Tourists who visited Olimpia had opposite opinions about this center. Almost all visitors to the complex agree that the slides are really good, original and of high quality made. But many scold the temperature of the water. Moreover, some visitors to the center claim that even in the zone for the smallest it is very cold - and these are real reviews. Not everyone likes the water park in Vityazevo because of the level of service and the cost of entrance tickets. It is worth noting that on weekends and holidays the water entertainment center is really crowded, against which workers do not have time to quickly respond to requests and requests from all guests. This is a definite minus, but at the same time, an urgent problem of many water parks in the Krasnodar Territory.

As for the cost, it is, in principle, impossible to find a center in resort Russian cities with an equal number of attractions and a markedly lower ticket price. It is difficult to find children who are dissatisfied with a visit to Olympia. Young guests of the center usually like all the slides and pools, as well as the original thematic design. Perhaps their parents should rejoice more and pay less attention to minor trifles?
