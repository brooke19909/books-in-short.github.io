Chrysalis Boutique Hotel, Greece: overview, description and reviews of tourists

When planning a vacation, it is very important not to make a mistake with the choice of hotel. After all, even if only for a short while, it will become a place of rest and residence. Chrysalis Boutique Hotel is a hotel that can fully satisfy even the most demanding travelers with its level of comfort and service. The complex mentioned above is popular among people who want to relax in Hersonissos, and for this reason it is worth telling about it in detail.





General Hotel Information

To begin with, I would like to note that Chrysalis Boutique Hotel is a small and comfortable hotel with a fund of 22 rooms. It is designed for guests who value quality service and comfort, and are also interested in a peaceful and relaxing holiday.

The hotel we are considering is located in the village of Astypalea, 50 meters from the beach and only two kilometers from the center of Hersonissos. Location is good. Astypalea National Airport is only 4 km away. Kalymnos National Airport and Hippocrates Airport are also approximately the same distance from the hotel. As to one, and to another to go about 70 kilometers.

Although the hotel is located at a somewhat remote distance from the center of Hersonissos, which is the place of concentration of the main attractions, still there is where to walk and what to see. Literally 700 meters from the hotel, for example, there is the Church of the Blessed Virgin Mary and Guerini Castle.

What is in the hotel?

Chrysalis Boutique Hotel is located in a well-kept clean area surrounded by lush trees. Near the pool there is a spacious sun terrace with sun loungers and parasols.

High-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel and private parking is available. Everyone is provided with a free airport shuttle. If this service is necessary for guests, then they need to inform the property in advance, specifying information about their flight and time of arrival.

It is also worth noting that Chrysalis Boutique Hotel 4 * has its own library, tour desk, ticket office, luggage storage, laundry room (where they not only wash, but also iron + clean shoes), an office with fax and photocopying, rental cars. Babysitting / babysitting services can be provided. Food, drinks and newspapers are also delivered directly to the apartments of the guests.

What about entertainment? Outside the hotel, on the beach, you can go diving or fishing. Here are the most popular ways to diversify leisure.





Service

It is worth focusing on . At the Chrysalis Hotel (Greece, Crete, Hersonissos), the administration and staff do everything to make guests comfortable. There are both places for smoking and rooms for people who are not addicted to tobacco. All apartments are isolated. There are bridal suites and family rooms. Daily and high-quality cleaning, maids change linen and towels regularly.

It is also worth noting the attention of multilingual staff. The hotel has staff who speak Greek, English, Spanish, French and Italian.

Deluxe Apartment

Now you can pay attention to the consideration of rooms available in the fund Chrysalis Boutique Hotel 4 * (Greece, Crete). The most budgetary are deluxe apartments of 29 square meters. m with one large king size bed , individual climate system and minibar.

All rooms in this category have a balcony with sea views, a wide-screen TV, a safe, a work area, Wi-Fi, a small dressing room, an electric kettle and a coffee machine. There is also a bathroom with toilet, bath, shower and all necessary hygiene items . Bathrobes and slippers are also provided.

The cost of a daily stay in such a room for two people is about 6,000 rubles. This price includes 9 percent VAT, 0.5% city tax and breakfast.

Suites

Chrysalis Boutique Hotel also has apartments of this category. Their first difference from deluxe is the area, which is 41 square meters. m The second is the presence of a terrace, not a balcony. Even in suites, the space is divided into a living room and a sleeping area. In addition to a king-size double bed, there is a pull-out sofa inside. Capacity, respectively, is also large - in this room four people can live, not two.

What about the price? Suites are, of course, more expensive. About 8,500 rubles per day. But if you divide into four, it turns out not so much - about 2100 rubles. /day. Total, living in a room of the highest category is cheaper than in the notorious deluxe . Although the rest - in terms of amenities - there is nothing special about them.

By the way, the hotel has 6-bed suites divided into three zones. Their price is the same as for apartments designed for four.





Booking and hotel policies

A few words should also be said about this. Since the ratio of quality and price at Chrysalis Hotel 4 * is justified, the hotel is popular. Many tourists are interested in spending their holidays in this cozy and comfortable hotel. And since there are not many rooms in it, the reservation goes forward months. So if you also want to stay here, you need to reserve apartments long before the planned trip.

Check-in at Chrysalis Boutique Hotel 4 * (Greece) starts from 14:00 and lasts until 20:00. Departure is before noon. It is important to take this into account and select the flights most suitable for the indicated time.

The hotel allows children with any age. Children up to 2 years old are provided with a free cradle. At the same time, you do not need to pay for their stay at the hotel. Children from 2 to 12 years old must pay EUR 30 if they are accommodated in extra beds. The same, however, applies to adult guests. By the way, if guests know that they will need an extra bed or crib, they need to inform the property in advance by calling or writing to the property.





Payment

When making a remote reservation at Chrysalis Boutique Hotel 3 * you will need to provide your credit card details. The accommodation accepts "Visa" and " Master Card ". It is allowed to use only one's own card, as it will need to be presented upon check-in along with the passport - the data on both documents must match.

By the way, a security deposit is required upon arrival. Either cash or the same credit card is accepted. It is also important to remember that cash payments at the hotel cannot exceed the mark of 500 euros - this rule is established by state law.

Usually, if accommodation costs guests a larger amount, the problem is solved by means of an electronic transfer. In any case , you will have to contact the hotel directly for more information. Contact details can be seen on its official website or on the resource of a tour operator, through which usually many make reservations.

By the way, the price is also a significant plus. Two to three thousand rubles a day in high season per person - this is more than acceptable for Crete. And if you make an early reservation, then you can also save a good amount.

Who is this hotel suitable for?

Well, attention was paid to all the nuances regarding the Chrysalis Boutique Hotel in Greece - prices, reviews of tourists, a description of the hotel itself and the services provided in it. Finally, a few words about who this hotel will definitely like.

According to reviews, the Chrysalis Boutique Hotel should go to people who do not have the habit of focusing on a wide range of services and ways to diversify leisure. It must be remembered that this is a boutique hotel , as the name itself clearly indicates. A feature of all hotels belonging to this type is their unique stylistic design and a bias towards increased comfort of rooms.

Chrysalis Hotel is not a resort complex with five restaurants on site and its own casino, and this fact should be taken into account when going on vacation.

And here you should not go to people who want to have fun all day in entertainment establishments. All of them are concentrated in Hersonissos, which is located albeit at a small but still distance from the hotel.

But people who are looking for a cozy hotel for a relaxing, mostly beach holiday, will definitely like it here. This is confirmed by numerous reviews.
