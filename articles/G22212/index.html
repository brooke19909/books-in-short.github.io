Holidays in Koblevo: recreation center "Vinnitsa", "Chernomorets", "Oasis"

Koblevo resort is a recreation center of various levels of comfort and more than one hundred pensions located on the Black Sea. The length of the recreation area is more than seven kilometers. In Koblevo can simultaneously rest up to 20 thousand people. The main advantage of this resort area is the complete absence of industry - only salty sea breath, gentle southern sun, the sound of the surf and the enchanting aroma of the steppe forbs. All these conditions will ensure you a comfortable stay in Koblevo, the recreation center is ready to receive guests from May to September.





How to get to boarding houses and recreation centers

From Odessa you can get to Koblevo from the suburban bus station (next to Privoz), from here buses run every thirty minutes, the route of which runs along recreation centers and boarding houses. From Nikolaev (from the bus station) shuttle buses go to the resort area. In this unique resort you can find everything you need for noisy fun for young people or a relaxing holiday with children. It is believed that seven is a lucky number for the resort - its length along the coast is 7 kilometers, and in 7 visits to Koblevo will leave 7 ills and 7 diseases. In addition, they say, 7 years of life is added.

The recreation center "Vinnitsa"

The recreation center "Vinnitsa" in Koblevo is a modern vacation spot of the European level. The base is located on the Ukrainian part of the resort and includes a well-groomed, flowering area with an original landscape and cozy gazebos. The distance to the beach is about a hundred meters. The residential complex of five floors accepts vacationers since 2005 in 152 rooms. In the two-story dining complex "Different foods" there is a dining room for three hundred people.





The territory of the base and parking are fenced and guarded around the clock. Every day, who came to rest in Koblevo, the Vinnitsa recreation center offers a wide range of delicious dishes. Among the pines there is a playground for the youngest guests, which is a children's playground for games, and nearby there are benches on which parents or just resting can sit. There is also a restaurant and a summer bar. This place is ideal for families with children, as it is far from noisy areas. There is also a freshwater pool.

The infrastructure of the recreation center "Vinnitsa"

The main advantage and pride of the base is a functional indoor sports ground with professional flooring and good lighting. There is also a tribune for 120 people for those who want to watch the game. All the entertainment infrastructure is about 15 minutes walk where the Moldovan recreation centers in Koblevo are located. Here you can find discos, night clubs, restaurants, bars, an amusement park and many attractions for children. Three hundred meters from the base is a modern water park.





Beach "Vinnitsa"

About 100 meters from the building with numbers there is a sandy gentle beach. The shallow coast creates ideal conditions for children's swimming. There is a moderately shallow bottom, the water in the sea quickly warms up to a comfortable temperature. The beach is equipped with canopy canopies with a diameter of 30 meters each. Also, vacationers can rent a deck chair, pedalo, and so on. There is also a cafe-bar with ice cream and soft drinks on the beach.





Recreation centers in Koblevo: "Chernomorets"

"Chernomorets" is available for many families with children of any income level, since the prices here are relatively low. This place is located in a unique climatic zone: a combination of sea, coniferous and steppe air. This base is located in the Ukrainian part of Koblevo, in a picturesque pine forest. On the territory of the base there is one capital building with five floors and separate wooden houses. There is access to the sea. Pension Chernomorets is considered one of the most comfortable in Koblevo due to its very good location (30-100 meters from the Black Sea) and its own beach.

The recreation center "Oasis"

The recreation center "Oasis" in Koblevo is a real paradise in the Odessa region, on the Black Sea coast. The recreation center "Oasis" has everything necessary to spend a summer vacation alone or with children. The boarding house is located 150 meters from the sea, near the recreation center "Cote d'Azur". If you choose this place, you can count on an inexpensive, but at the same time comfortable vacation. Here you will definitely relax and gain strength for the whole year.





Oasis Base Infrastructure

The territory of this cozy base is completely surrounded by flowers and green spaces. The one-story building has comfortable rooms. There are also detached cottages and houses, a playground for children, convenient parking for cars. There is table tennis, billiards and a summer gazebo, where you can sit perfectly in the evening near a small pond. At will, vacationers can take barbecues and cook kebabs on them. The greenery in which the territory of the base is buried provides a pleasant cool atmosphere in any weather, which is very important in the summer heat. Amazing landscape design creates a wonderful atmosphere and sets out for a relaxing holiday in Koblevo. The recreation center "Oasis" on the Black Sea also accepts collective applications for arrivals of 35-40 people in several shifts.

Location of holidaymakers at the recreation center "Oasis"

For those who came to rest in Koblevo, the recreation center offers several possible accommodation options: suites, junior suites and modern wooden houses. Detached houses "standard" are designed for two, three and four people and are equipped with an open cozy veranda. Wooden buildings are located in the shade of a colorful green alley not far from each other, which is very convenient for couples with children. Each house has everything you need for a comfortable stay: beds, wardrobe, bedside table, refrigerator, TV and more. Facilities (shower and toilet) are located on the territory. For those who come to the Black Sea in their own car, there is a small parking lot near the terrace.

What is the popular resort of Koblevo

The resort is popular because it has everything that is needed for a good rest with your family. In addition, there are various entertainments that youth companies will surely appreciate. If we talk about the entertainment that the recreation center in Koblevo offers, the sea, the beaches are the main ones. Today there are three water parks, there are a large number of inflatable attractions, restaurants, cafes and nightclubs. In 2014, to the delight of vacationers, a dolphinarium was opened.





You definitely will not have to miss Koblevo. If you have the mood to travel or the bad weather will begin, then at your service are numerous excursion bureaus that will help you get acquainted with the unique sights of Odessa and Nikolaev regions. Koblevo is an ecologically clean area with sandy beaches and pine forests. The season for visitors to relax in this wonderful place opens in late May and lasts until the beginning of September. In season, the air temperature is 30-35 degrees, the water warms up to 23 degrees. In the resort area there are more than two hundred pensions and recreation centers of various comforts located at different distances from the sea. At the same time, the resort will be able to accept up to 20 thousand people.
