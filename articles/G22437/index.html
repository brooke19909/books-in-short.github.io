Great minds of Russia

During this period, a scientist lived and worked, who more broadly understood science and its imperfection - Kirill Butusov.

Astrophysicist, theoretical scientist put forward more than a dozen hypotheses about the structure of the solar system. On his account more than a hundred published scientific papers. After some time, most of the discoveries of the scientist were confirmed by astrophysicists of various countries.

Butusov Kirill Pavlovich was born in 1929, in the city of Leningrad. In 1954 he graduated from the Leningrad Polytechnic Institute. For seven years after graduation, he worked as a senior laboratory assistant and engineer at the Pulkovo Observatory. Since 1961 he taught radio engineering, physics and radio physics at universities of Leningrad.
In the period from 1988 to 1996 he worked in two research firms Alcor and Mitsar.

Later, for four years, Butusov worked as head of the department of computational mathematics and control at the Research Institute of Management of St. Petersburg University.

In 2011, he won the prize of the international public association RusFO (Russian Physical Society) for his services to science.
In the winter of 2012, an outstanding astrophysicist and philosopher died. Over the last ten years of his life, Kirill Butusov has become an honorary member of several scientific communities.

Gloria - an exact copy of the Earth

According to Butusov, the mysterious object is located behind the Sun and is an exact copy of the Earth.




The planet Gloria is constantly at the opposite point and rotates in the same orbit as the Earth. Due to the fact that the speed of bodies is the same, it is constantly behind the Sun (at the opposite point of the orbit).

How did the hypothesis arise that there is a "twin" of the Earth - the planet Gloria?

Thousands of years ago, in the annals of various peoples, information appears about the existence of a certain copy of the Earth. In the Middle Ages in 1666 and 1672, the Paris Observatory reported a discovered crescent-shaped object in the Venus region. At that time, astronomers suggested that it was a satellite of Venus. However, as it turned out later, this planet has no satellites.
The object appeared in the nineteenth century, but there is no reliable information and report on this event. The reason for the appearance of Gloria due to the Sun, experts consider the action of strong magnetic storms in the period of the seventeenth century.

Gloria - myth or reality?

Based on his calculations of the motion of Saturn, Jupiter, Venus and Mars, astrophysicist Kirill Butusov determined that there is another cosmic body in the solar system. The orbits of the planets under the influence of mutual gravity coincide with the existing position only in the presence of an "invisible" planet.

Observing the quirks of Venus, astronomers could not find out why the periodic acceleration of the body and deceleration occur. This object must be affected by a different, unaccounted gravitational force. The description of the second Sun can be traced in the mythology of almost all peoples. The Sumerian tablets mention the second Sun, which did not set beyond the horizon. Official astronomy refers the planet Nibiru to the field of pseudoscience.

However, recent studies by Japanese astrophysicists have confirmed Butusov's hypothesis about the presence of a massive body outside the Kuiper belt (begins beyond the orbit of Neptune). It says that our Sun, like most stars, has a pair system. According to his theory, our solar system had two luminaries, one of them was formed earlier and burned out completely.
