Seychelles, hotels: a description of the best

Seychelles offers a true paradise vacation. Traveling here can hardly be called cheap, so this destination is not very popular. Most often, newlyweds come here in search of an ideal place for a honeymoon. Consider the best Seychelles hotels, the services they offer, and the guest reviews about them.

What is attractive about vacation in the Seychelles?

They come to the islands in search of a calm and measured rest. Here you can admire the magnificent seascapes. Often, this resort is chosen for surfing and diving. The sea here is very clear, transparent, blue. Fans of sea fishing and leisurely walks on luxury yachts also choose the Seychelles. Hotels are located on the islands of Mahe, La Digue and Praslin. They feature the best infrastructure and the richest selection of entertainment. Island hotels are also popular, such as Frigate, Denise, St. Anne's Island. The high season is held here throughout the year, so you can come here in the fall, and in the winter, and in the spring.





There are many national parks on the islands, some of which, unfortunately, are closed to tourists. Seychelles Airport is located near Victoria, the local capital located on the island of Mahe. From here, you can fly to other large islands of the archipelago.

Raffles Seychelles 5 *

It is considered one of the best complexes that the Seychelles can offer. Hotels here most often consist of small villas, and Raffles Seychelles 5 * was no exception. It is located on the island of Praslin, the flight to which from the capital airport lasts only 15 minutes. Each villa has its own outdoor pool, air conditioning, a huge plasma TV with satellite channels, a coffee machine. The spacious balcony offers amazing views of the coast. The complex has a wellness center and a beauty salon, a kids club. Tourists can eat in six restaurants, one of which is located on the roof. It serves Mediterranean cuisine, the best alcoholic drinks, make a hookah.





Excellent reviews of guests receive the hotel Raffles Seychelles 5 *. The only drawback, in their opinion, is the overpriced vacation price. Much more in reviews of advantages. We list the main ones:

  • the hotel is located on a mountain with a beautiful view of the islands;
  • polite and well-trained staff, quickly fulfilling any request of guests;
  • Superior room interiors
  • the cleanest beach, the waiters regularly offer drinks, snacks and fruits;
  • restaurants have menus from many countries of the world, dishes are always fresh.

Chalets Cote Mer 3 *

But not only respectable holidays offer tourists Praslin and Seychelles. Hotels, reviews of which can be read in this article, offer relatively inexpensive accommodation. For example, the Chalets Cote Mer 3 * complex. The hotel is surrounded by a lush tropical garden, has its own access to the ocean, where you can go diving or sea fishing. The public beach can be reached in 10 minutes. The hotel rooms feature exquisite wooden four-poster beds, a fridge and air conditioning. The restaurant, located on site, has a cozy thatched roof. It serves fish and seafood dishes. All guests receive a free shuttle service to the hotel. Here you can also rent a car and travel around the island on your own.





Seychelles, whose hotels are described in the article, are rarely criticized by tourists. Even the three-star Chalets Cote Mer complex often receives positive reviews. We list its advantages:

  • daily and quality housekeeping;
  • good internet speed;
  • great view from the window.

The main shortcomings that the guests talk about are the following:

  • the beach is far from the hotel, and buses to it rarely go;
  • only eggs are served daily for breakfast;
  • a lot of insects, so you need to buy a tool in advance to protect against them.
