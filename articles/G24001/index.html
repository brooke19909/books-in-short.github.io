Thermal springs: Adygea invites

The article tells about the rest in Adygea. Talked about the benefits of thermal waters. The recreation centers and sanatoriums, where there are pools with thermal water, are listed. Briefly written about other types of recreation and attractions of Adygea.

The picturesque Republic of Adygea is located in the northern foothills of the Caucasus. It is one of the best resort areas thanks to the picturesque mountain and forest landscapes, healing air and unique thermal springs. The climate is comfortable and mild, it is not cold in winter and not hot in summer.

People come from all over the country to healing thermal springs. Adygea is also famous for ancient architecture. On its territory there are a large number of ancient and mysterious dolmens and barrows. It is not surprising that many tourists come here to not only relax, but also to improve their health.

The benefits of thermal springs

Since ancient times, local residents have used thermal springs for treatment, intuitively realizing that they have a great healing potential. And only the studies currently conducted have revealed the secret of Adyghe sources. The water in them is rich in useful trace elements that positively affect the human body. Therefore, people are so attracted by Adygea. Thermal springs - rest and benefit at the same time.





Bathing at the source helps to cope with diseases of the nervous, endocrine and cardiovascular systems. Water has a beneficial effect on the body with diseases of the digestive and respiratory organs. It also helps with colds. Bathing in thermal water helps to cure infertility and get rid of gynecological diseases. For people who want to relax and improve their health, the best option is Adygea. Thermal springs, recreation centers provide an excellent opportunity to combine relaxation and treatment.

The water in the springs does not fall below +20 year-round. Therefore, you can come here even in winter. Indeed, at this time of year there is no such influx of tourists, prices are reduced, and dense steam from sources against the backdrop of a snowy landscape will not leave anyone indifferent.

Recreation facilities

Almost all recreation centers and sanatoriums have thermal springs on their territory . Adygea provides a wide selection of hotels and resorts where vacationers can stay. Among the most popular, the following recreation centers can be distinguished.

  • "White Nights".
  • "Water Riviera".
  • "Cedar Forest".
  • "Aqua Thermo".
  • "Aquarius".

Recreation facilities differ in the number of pools, housing, infrastructure, comfort. It is safe to say that the main thing Adygea is famous for is thermal springs. Prices range from 2500 to 6000 rubles per day for two. The price depends on the class of room and the services that the pension will provide. But you need to understand that the price includes not only accommodation in the room, but also the use of the pool, most often meals (breakfast) and some procedures.

Base "White Nights" is located in the village of Tula. On its territory there are three pools with different temperatures. There are 15 Standard rooms and one Suite room for accommodation. The temperature of the local spring water reaches 85 degrees, but before it enters the pool, it is cooled. Water in this source helps to get rid of diseases of the endocrine system and increases immunity.





Kedrovy Bor is located in the village of Tsvetochny. On the territory there is one pool, the water in which is pre-treated. Purified water can be drunk, for this purpose there are drinking installations. The water temperature here reaches 37-38 degrees. The pools are equipped with modern equipment - hydraulic devices and artificial geysers. On the territory of the source there are 3 guest houses and gazebos, where you can fry kebabs and sunbathe. Local waters are indicated in the treatment of various diseases, the restoration of strength after operations and serious ailments is considered especially effective.

At the recreation center "Water Riviera" there are four pools with thermal water of different temperatures, it is worth saying that the water here has a pronounced taste of salt and the smell of oil. Separately, there is a children's pool. Guests are accommodated in houses and cottages with private pools. Also on the territory there are locker rooms and summer houses. Address: p. Tula.

The base "Aqua Thermo" is equipped with one pool (13 * 25), which has hydraulic devices. Guest houses are provided for living, there are also cafes and bars where you can drink and smoke a hookah in the evening. Address: p. Tula.

Base "Aquarius" has at its disposal 11 pools for adults and children. On its territory there are a hotel complex and cottages. The camp site is located in a very picturesque place - among the mountains, therefore, in addition to treatment, visiting this place, you can get just aesthetic pleasure. The water temperature in the pools here does not fall below 36 degrees. Near each house there is a barbecue, where you can cook kebabs from the meat of local pastoralists or fish that can be caught independently in local lakes.

Thermal water treatment

The water in the thermal springs at the outlet has a different temperature, which ranges from 20 to 100 degrees. Those sources that have a temperature above 60 degrees Celsius have to be cooled by diluting the mineral water with ordinary tap water. As a result, the water in the pool remains mineral, but the concentration of nutrients drops significantly. Therefore, such pools no longer have great healing properties. They nevertheless give a small healing effect, but they cannot be compared with concentrated thermal water. If you plan to combine rest with treatment, then for this purpose it is better to choose a sanatorium.





The sanatoriums provide various rehabilitation programs for both adults and children. In addition to treatment with mineral water baths, such procedures as physiotherapy, massage, inhalation are used. In the treatment of diseases such as bronchial asthma, bronchitis, hay fever, metabolic disorders, thermal springs help well .

Adygea has at its disposal the sanatoriums "Caucasus", "Lagonaki" and "Forest Tale". There are children's sanatoriums "Sun" and "Dewdrop".

Recreation and entertainment

Having plenty of rest in the healing pools, it will also be useful to enjoy an active holiday. Guests can enjoy a wide range of activities, such as canyoning, jeeping, mountain rafting, dangerous cave trips or snowmobiling. The list of entertainment is quite long, so everyone will find something to their liking.





Major Attractions

The main asset of Adygea is considered to be its unique and beautiful nature. Here you can take a look at Sahrai waterfalls, stroll along the Ammonite Valley and visit the Azish cave. A large number of waterfalls, gorges and caves leaves no one indifferent. Of course, it will not be possible to visit everything at once, since the list of Adygea sights is long.

After what has been said

Beautiful mountain landscapes, majestic waterfalls, dangerous gorges and caves attract people.

Tourists from all over the country come to visit the thermal springs. Adygea is rich in underground springs, which have healing properties.

Here you will find entertainment and lovers of outdoor activities, fishing and hiking.

Adygea is the land of untouched and incredibly beautiful nature, and only because of this you can visit it.
