How to weave a heart out of elastic bands: a detailed description

Interested in making jewelry from rainbow loom bands? Read how to weave a heart out of elastic bands, as well as a bracelet called "Heart of an Angel." These products look very cute and romantic. They will help you emphasize individuality and simply attract attention to yourself.





Heart Accessory Options

If you are thinking about how to weave a heart out of elastic bands, then you probably decided to make a romantic accessory for yourself or prepare someone a present for Valentine's Day. It is worth immediately identifying two varieties of products - those that are woven from volumetric hearts, and those where this element is built in the form of a pattern obtained by weaving gum in a special way.   • trinket;

  • hair band;
  • a ringlet;
  • earrings.

The second method weaves mainly bracelets, although the option is also suitable for creating a ring and a rim.

How to weave a bracelet made of elastic bands "Heart of an angel" (getting started)

This jewelry, consisting of a chain of openwork hearts, is not very difficult to make. The sequence of work is as follows:

  1. Put on both columns of the slingshot an elastic band of color No. 1, crossing it with a figure of eight.
  2. Put the second rubber band in color No. 2 on both horns without twisting, like all subsequent ones.
  3. Drop the first rubber band from the left column through the top to the middle.
  4. Move the top gum from right to left.
  5. Put another rubber band in color No. 2 on both horns without crossing.
  6. Remove the bottom loop from the right column through the top to the middle.
  7. Transfer the top loop from the left column to the right.
  8. Put a No. 1 elastic band on both horns.
  9. Drop from each horn the upper elastic band of color No. 2 loops to the middle.
  10. Put a rubber band of color No. 2 on both horns. From this moment, a sequence of steps will be taken that will be repeated in the future until the desired length of the bracelet is obtained.

Bracelet weave pattern

The sequence given below is steps 1 through 8 and is repeated:

  1. Drop the bottom loop from the left column to the middle, and then transfer the top from the right to the left.
  2. Put another rubber band in color No. 2 on both horns.
  3. Drop the bottom loop from the right column through the top to the middle, and transfer the top from the left to the right.
  4. Put on both horns an elastic band of color No. "1.
  5. Discard the upper loop of color No. 2 from the left column to the middle.
  6. Through the next loop of color No. 2, pull the hooked lower gum of color No. 1 and also discard it to the middle.
  7. Now you need to do the same with the right column. Discard the top loop to the middle, and pull the bottom through the previous gum and discard also to the middle.
  8. Put on both horns again a rubber band of color No. 2.
  9. Discard the lower gum of color No. 2 on the left horn to the middle, and transfer the upper gum of color No. 2 from right to left. You can assume that you know how to weave an angel heart bracelet from elastic bands. After all, this step is already a similar course of a repeating sequence of actions.

When you reach the required length, you need to fix the bracelet. Drop to the middle through the upper lower loops from both columns. Put on an elastic band of matching color on both horns. Remove the lower loops again to the middle, and transfer the remaining upper loop from the right column to the left. Stretch a pair of elastic bands from the left column on both and carefully put on the clip-on clasp. Fasten the second part of the clasp at the beginning of the bracelet.

The product is completed.

Useful bracelet weaving tips

The product you have turned bilateral. If you used a contrasting color scheme, then the sequence of hearts and the weaving of the middle part are clearly visible on the pattern. To master the principle of weaving and remember the sequence of steps, you can make the first bracelet from the same rubber bands. You will clearly understand what gum that forms - the heart or the middle, so you can even combine hearts of different colors in one bracelet, for example, two through two identical ones with a contrasting middle.

How to weave an angel heart without a slingshot

If you do not have the indicated device, you can completely do without it. If you have a machine, it is enough to use two columns from your design. If there is no this tool, but you still want to make a bracelet, you can try to make it right on your fingers. The middle and index on the left hand are used if you are working with the right. Another option is to make a slingshot of your own. You can take a plastic disposable fork and remove the middle two teeth or assemble the tool from felt-tip pens or any other rods or sticks.

How to weave a heart action figure out of rubber bands

The instructions below are for braiding only. It is quite convenient and fast. When choosing rubber bands for weaving, buy the smallest diameter and even, otherwise the figure may turn out to be sloppy. If you can‚Äôt weave a heart out of rubber bands on the machine, then just hook the version with a hook.





The sequence of work will be as follows:

  1. Put the first elastic on the hook and wrap around it an additional two times. It turns out three loops.
  2. Take the following two rubber bands and pull them into the rings on the hook, removing them from the tool. Put the other ends of the paired gum on the hook.
  3. The right pair of eyelets remains unused for now. Work with the left side. To make it, you need seven gum.
  4. Take the first and pass through the left pair on the hook, removing it from the tool.
  5. Put on the hook back the left loop from the first pair, and then the left end of the working gum.
  6. Take the next gum and pass it through the two extreme left loops, removing them from the hook, then repeat the sequence from the previous step.
  7. Follow the previous step with five more rubber bands. For each half of the heart, seven are used.
  8. Take the second hook and carefully transfer all the elastic bands onto it.
  9. Turn the hook over and weave the second half of the heart in the same way as the first of the seven elastic bands.
  10. Take one more elastic and throw on it the extreme left six loops. Put both ends of the elastic on the finger of your left hand.
  11. Of the next six gum, swap the left three with the right, that is, going to the right.
  12. Move the three elastic bands to the left onto the right loop located on the finger.
  13. Take one more gum and thread through the loops to the right of the hook.
  14. Fasten the four loops that have arisen to get a keychain.

Heart bracelet

Want to learn another way to weave a bracelet of rubber bands? A heart made according to the instructions in the previous section can be used as the main element. It is beautifully applied in one copy in the center of a woven thin bracelet.



Another option is a strip of repeating similar hearts. To do this, continue to weave the next heart on the remaining four loops according to the above instructions. Weave the desired number of hearts to the appropriate length of the bracelet, set the clip and you can try on.
