Beach, romance and green tourism

Of course, most people are going to go to sea, and this is not surprising. After all, what could be better than basking in the warm sand under the light sea breeze. For many, this is the best vacation. As for August, this month you can go to many countries, for example, Tunisia or Egypt, Turkey or Greece, as well as Bulgaria. The choice is very large. If you like the so-called green tourism and everything related to this, you can visit the eastern and western shores of Crimea. Here you can find not only a lot of dense greenery, but also a lot of interesting entertainments, such as diving or windsurfing. Active holidays can be alternated with a romantic one. At any resort there are cozy places where you can spend time with your loved one. In Crimea, these are quiet shallow bays, and in Greece - sandy beaches with emerald-clear water, in which you can see the most diverse exotic living creatures. Of course, you already know that rest on the sea in August is considered the most favorable. This is due to the fact that it is not so hot already, and the water is warmed up enough to relax with the children.



Where better to relax in August, or TOP places

Many tourists would like to see a visual list that can guide you when choosing a resort. Let's try to compose it. If you are a lover of beach holidays, we strongly recommend visiting Greece in August. Montenegro will also be pleased at this time. As for Turkey, you will not see the difference between August and July, it will be equally hot here. Tunisia is rightfully in fourth place, which in recent years has become very popular. In August, there is a "golden season" and there are a lot of vacationers. But it is worth paying your attention to the fact that prices this month are very, very high. If you are a fan of various excursions and festivals, then you can travel endlessly in Europe. These can be the most diverse countries, for example, Portugal or Italy, Greece or Spain. Holidays in Tunisia in August: advantages and disadvantages





This month the sea is incredibly warm. The temperature at night does not decrease much, this contributes to the fact that you can swim in the sea around the clock. August is the imperial month for a vacation in Tunisia. This month, nights last a very long time, so you can fully enjoy the full variety of entertainment. Here you will find not only a sandy beach with azure water, but also festivals that are held throughout the holiday season. You can enjoy the performance of artists in the style of jazz in the open air, and classical music is also very much appreciated. Therefore, if you are not only a lover of relaxing on the beach, but also respect classical music, then be sure to come to this heavenly place.

In the daytime, the temperature does not drop below 35 degrees Celsius, get ready for this. You will definitely get a beautiful tan, but if you are in the shade from 11 a.m. to 3 p.m., you will be able to avoid sunstroke. Of course, a vacation in Tunisia in August is famous for its nightlife. Restaurants, bars, strip shows and much more await you in this unforgettable place. Be sure to visit at least one sushi restaurant on the waterfront, where you will find not only a wide variety of dishes, but also very tasty food. As you can see, you will not have to get bored in Tunisia, so pay attention to this direction.

Sea vacation, or How to take everything from vacation

There is nothing worse than a wasted vacation. That is why it is better to fly south, where you can always relax well. However, here you will certainly encounter a problem that will be the difficulty of choice. You can go to Bulgaria, Montenegro, Egypt or Turkey, because all these places guarantee you a good rest. Be guided by your budget. If the amount of money is limited and you are looking for a budget trip, then without hesitation go to Bulgaria. It should be noted that sea vacations in August are the most expensive, so get ready for the fact that housing and food will be of high cost. At this time, almost all popular resorts raise prices by an average of 15% -20%, as an influx of tourists is expected. Turkey will be the most expensive this month, while Greece, Montenegro and Egypt are slightly cheaper. Although also a lot depends on how and how much you are used to relax. As for the Crimea, here, a vacation at sea in August will be lovely, but again the price bites. For the same money, you can fly to Greece for a week and still live in a 3 * hotel.



We fly on vacation to Greece

In recent years, this area has become extremely popular. This was influenced by several factors. Firstly, there are reasonable prices. As already noted above, they are almost similar to the Crimean ones, only the difference in the quality of rest is big. In Greece, the service is at a higher level, and the beaches are watched much better, even if they are free and do not belong to any of the hotels. The most popular islands are Rhodes, Corfu, etc. Corfu is very suitable for lovers of beautiful nature, since this island simply surprises with its landscape and is one of the most beautiful places on the Ionian Sea. If you still do not know where to fly to rest in August, then pay attention to the resort of Filaraki - a paradise with busy beaches, the prices here are exactly the same as in Rhodes. You will be pleasantly surprised not only by the sea, but also by the many entertainment programs, as well as sea activities that Greece will offer you. You already know where to relax in August in this beautiful country, so let's move on.

What to do if you really want to go to the sea, and money to spare? In this case, you need a clear cost control and savings. How to organize a family trip

It is rather difficult to decide where it is better to relax in August with your family. The fact is that there are many nuances. If you are going to rest on the sea with children, it is advisable that the coast is shallow, with well-warmed water and clean sand. This will not only give a lot of emotions to the child, but also protect him from abrasions, bumps and dangerous bathing. Of course, we must not forget that the attention of parents is the most important point. If you are looking for a good beach resort for your child, the sandy beaches of Bulgaria or Spain are recommended. Here you can not only enjoy the crystal clear sea waters, but also find a lot of entertainment, as well as attractions that are located on the coastal line.

For travelers, a tour of the castles of the Loire, which are located in France, is suitable. At the same time, you can see other attractions of this country. In the fears of Scandinavia, in particular, in the northern part, there is also something to see. Here and entertainment programs, and many informative and just interesting excursions. If parents cannot travel with their children, then the only right decision is to send their son or daughter to the camp.
