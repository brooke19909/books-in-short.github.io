Elite real estate of St. Petersburg: apartments near the metro "Petrogradskaya"



Petrogradsky district of St. Petersburg is one of the most popular for living. It is located on the Neva Islands. The area is characterized by picturesque landscapes and developed infrastructure. New buildings near the metro station "Petrogradskaya" offer a large list of luxury apartments. You can get acquainted with the projects on the website http://elita-spb.ru/buildings/search/metro_petrogradskaya . What are the benefits of living in the area? Why is elite housing so popular among Petersburgers?

Petrograd district and its features

Inexpensive apartments near the metro station "Petrogradskaya" - a huge rarity. Here are predominantly business-class housing estates. The houses have a small number of floors. The windows of the apartments overlook the river. Almost all new buildings are concentrated on the waterfront. It is this factor that affects the cost of housing. The prestige of the area is at a high level. Prices for real estate here are constantly increasing.

The popularity of these houses is explained by the presence of well-developed social and domestic infrastructure. In this area you can not only live with great comfort. In his line there are all conditions for successful work and training. There are approximately 60 kindergartens and other institutions of preschool education, 24 schools, as well as several medical schools, technical universities, etc. Moreover, there are large sports and entertainment complexes in the Petrograd district. These are "Jubilee", "Planet Fitness" and "Metrostroy".

Near new buildings near the metro station "Petrogradskaya" well-developed transport links. But since the area is located on seven islands, and the number of bridges is insufficient, there are often long traffic jams during peak hours. The traffic congestion negatively affects the environmental situation. Although in general the situation is quite acceptable.

The benefits of acquiring elite housing in St. Petersburg

Elite apartments in St. Petersburg are in demand not only among local residents, but also among visitors. As a rule, luxury housing is represented by modern complexes, which include several buildings. Such facilities are being built in prestigious areas of the city, including Petrograd.

The specifics of real estate forces construction companies to use exclusively innovative technologies, high-quality raw materials, which allows you to create strong, reliable, durable buildings with a modern look. The microclimate in elite buildings is characterized by a high level of comfort. In the cold season, the apartments are warm, and in the hot - cool. The front and courtyard areas are clean, ennobled and landscaped.

Developers care about their customers, therefore they equip apartments with high-quality door blocks and metal-plastic double-glazed windows. Particular attention is paid to utilities. The electrical wiring is laid at the highest level. The area near the elite houses is closed. Objects are guarded around the clock. Residents are offered modern multi-level parking.

Almost all new buildings belonging to the class of elite housing have the following advantages:

  • original design of complexes, houses and apartments;
  • attractive landscape with flower beds, recreation areas, sports and playgrounds;
  • beautiful panoramas from the windows;
  • developed infrastructure - shops, entertainment centers, public facilities;
  • the availability of modern engineering communications that contribute to the uninterrupted supply of life support resources;
  • convenient and practical layout, in which most of the useful area is saved;
  • the apartments are equipped with all necessary communications;
  • the presence of metro stations and bus stops nearby.

Today, not only wealthy people can afford elite housing. Favorable mortgage programs are a chance for working people who receive stable incomes to acquire comfortable real estate.

Today you can not only buy an apartment in a new building, but also rent it with a subsequent purchase. Many Petersburgers do just that.
