Abkhazia, Chernihivka: new life of the old village

If you are a lover of stunning beauties, a seeker of new impressions, and a warm and cozy atmosphere gives you special joy, your choice should fall in the Republic of Abkhazia. Chernihivka is a local attraction, gaining more and more popularity every year. And this is surprising, because until recently, few people knew about this place even among local residents.

How to get there

Chernihivka is conveniently located 30 km from the capital of the republic, the city of Sukhum. Head towards the village of Gulrypsh, after the traffic police post turn left - to the mountains. Before the trip, stock up on empty containers, because three kilometers from the turn you will meet the village of Markhaul, where there is a healing source of weakly mineralized water. It was in this village that Lavrenty Beria was born. 8 km from Markhaul there is a miracle, which is famous for Abkhazia - Chernihiv. Minibuses do not go to it, therefore, in the absence of personal transport, it is better to get with a guided tour.

Chernihivka, the photo of which you see in the article, is literally replete with tables arranged in the most unusual places: under a canopy of stones, near the water and at the top of the gorge. Left for lunch at one of these tables, you can see the beauty of the surrounding landscapes, so the meal will leave memories for life for every visitor.

Chernihivka, Abkhazia. A restaurant



It is in "Assir" that you can try dishes of national cuisine. The restaurant is so spectacular that its visitors are often lost, only stepping over the threshold. Therefore, contact the waiters - they will help with choosing a table. An order is recommended to be done right away, because Assir is always full, and you should not count on a quick dinner preparation. As a rule, you have to wait more than half an hour. While the order is being prepared, you will have enough time to take a walk and take a picture against the backdrop of local landscapes.

Local beauty

The first thing that attracts attention is an artificial waterfall. Many travelers note that here they feel the real power of mountain energy, get a charge of freshness and impressions that remain for life.





At the beginning of the gorge, water runs hundreds of meters, bypassing boulders and stones along the way, resulting in several beautiful waterfalls. Abkhazia, Chernihiv in particular, has a rich world of flora. Appreciate it by visiting these places! Go to the mountain platform, which is located a little further from the restaurant, because there you are provided with a truly thrill - not every waiter agrees to serve at such a height. All the mountain paths of Chernihivka are absolutely safe and equipped with handrails, so you can walk safely. A small mountain river rich in trout flows in the depths of the gorge. It can be ordered to the table and be present when fishing.

Instead of a conclusion

Abkhazia, Chernihivka - a place where you can not come without a camera or camera! Capture the Assir restaurant, a stunning gorge, an artificial waterfall, magical natural landscapes - these pictures will warm your soul for many years. Have a magical journey!
