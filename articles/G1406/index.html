DIY dolls

All girls play dolls. These toys are one of the first to appear in the children's room, teenagers, and sometimes even quite adult ladies, are in no hurry to part with them. In addition to the dolls themselves, on sale today you can find a variety of accessories for them, as well as furniture and entire houses. But not always in the store is exactly what you need. And so many doll lovers prefer to create some kind of objects for them with their own hands. What can be done at home without being a master? Anything: clothes, furniture and a variety of game and interior trifles - everyone can create such crafts for dolls.

Household stuff



It is much easier to beat a variety of everyday situations in a dollhouse when you have miniature food, dishes and other accessories at hand. Many of these items can be created with your own hands. The biggest problem usually occurs with filling the kitchen. A small amount of tableware is usually attached to purchased furniture sets; miniature sets can also be purchased separately. But what to fill plates and glasses with? Try making plasticine or polymer clay products and ready meals. Other masses for modeling, such as salt dough, are also suitable. From these materials it is not at all difficult to create dishes, vases, candlesticks and cups for the interior. Children will also like to create such crafts for dolls. With your own hands , if you want, you can do a lot - from an orange and a piece of cheese to a plate with fried eggs or a vase with a bouquet.

Dollhouse furniture



So, you already have clothes for dolls and various accessories for the game, which means that it is time to move on to the manufacture of more complex items. Doll furniture is something that is always lacking in the household of your daughter. And even if you purchase several ready-made sets, soon you will definitely need another small sofa or bedside table. For the manufacture of doll interior items, you can use anything you want: cardboard, foam, packaging from products and souvenirs made of plastic and paper. Doing such crafts for dolls, connect imagination and all your skills in various needlework techniques. Finished furniture can be painted and varnished, decorated with stickers or covered with cloth. Try decorating tables and cabinets using decoupage technique. If you have any wallpaper or self-adhesive film left from the repair , use these materials.

Matchbox and cardboard furniture

The easiest way to do paper crafts for dolls, but this material is quite fragile and short-lived. The situation is completely different with its main counterpart - cardboard. Cutting and bending cardboard sheets is not much more difficult, the finished furniture is quite durable and beautiful with high-quality design. Use any boxes or purchase sheets of cardboard in office supplies. Original functional furniture can be assembled from matchboxes. Best of these are chests of drawers (just glue the right number of boxes in a suitable configuration). Using only the inside of the match box, you can make shallow shelves. If desired, cardboard can be used to learn how to make fairly complex products. Attention: it is more convenient to cut corrugated cardboard with a clerical knife, and individual elements can be glued on PVA.

Dollhouse



If you have enough space and time to work, you can try to create a house for dolls. You need to start by preparing the basics. The easiest option is to use a cardboard box from household appliances or several small ones to decorate the doll house . The construction will be much stronger if you create it from plywood or chipboard. A great option for the lazy - use a ready-made old bedside table or bookcase. So, you have the basis of the doll house, if you wish, you can now install partitions (stick or nail - depending on the material chosen) and outline the location of the stairs. Next, go to the finishing work: paste the walls with wallpaper or decorative film, you can put real linoleum or oilcloth on the floor. If you wish, cut out the windows or make them using the appliqu√© technique using suitable pictures from magazines. Furnish the finished house with home-made furniture - and you can celebrate a housewarming party. Making crafts for dolls with your own hands is actually quite easy. The main thing is only to begin, after which new ideas will arise by themselves. This is a useful and interesting hobby for the whole family, which does not require significant material investments.
