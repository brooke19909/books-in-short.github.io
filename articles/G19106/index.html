Popular water parks in Makhachkala

The capital of Dagestan, Makhachkala, is located on the shores of the beautiful Caspian Sea.

Luxor

Rivers flow through the city and a man-made canal passes. Maybe that's why here is one of the best aquacenters in Makhachkala - Luxor.



It offers many attractions and activities. Four water slopes are dearly loved by the guests of the park. The longest descent is 10 meters high. From the descent along these slides is breathtaking. Water park "Luxor" in Makhachkala offers to swiftly sweep along the "Hydrotube", along spectacular turns and bends. Unforgettable emotions delivers a free fall descent.

Pool and other services

Fans of a relaxing holiday will appreciate the large pool with a sea wave. Its dimensions are impressive - 25x10 meters - and allow you to conduct swimming classes with the help of qualified instructors.



It is equipped so that it is comfortable for both adults and children. There is a geyser near one side of the pool. There are many who want to take a hydromassage course. Around the pool is always fun. It is surrounded by many cozy sun loungers.

The aquapark "Luxor" invites adults to visit a huge gym. On 300 m 2 there are a variety of fitness equipment, treadmills. Professional fitness trainers will always help you choose a recovery program.

The style of modern life is the beauty of mind and body. Specialists of the wellness center located in the water park are always ready to develop an individual rehabilitation program, including a SPA course. The best cosmetologists in Makhachkala will tidy up the skin of the face and body, and manicure and hairdresser specialists will complete the new look.

For babies

Water parks in Makhachkala are proud of the presence of children's water zones. They offer several shallow pools equipped with water slides, spray guns, fountains. Experienced lifeguards and instructors always look after the kids.

Where to eat?

After water procedures, you just need to visit the phytobar. On sale are always fresh, only squeezed, juices, coffee and snacks.



If you are hungry, it is better to visit Snack Master. It surprises guests of the water park with delicious sandwiches and mind-blowing nuggets.

The children's zone of the aquapark "Luxor" is proud of its development center - "Montessori". Here, in working with children, they use the Montessori technique , known throughout the world . It helps to study and reveal the individual characteristics of each child. Professional educators easily identify and develop the creative potential of each new baby. Here the kids are engaged in gymnastics.

Parties

Water parks in Makhachkala, including Luxor, are invited in the evenings to theme parties, to a world full of fun and favorite music - in the NewYork club. You can invite a girl to a romantic date here. And with friends to celebrate a birthday or just arrange a fashionable party. The organizers of club events are never repeated, so the entertainment program of the NewYork club is very successful.

Prices

Luxor is waiting for its guests every day from 10.00 a.m. to 10.00 p.m. Water park prices are within 500 rubles.



There is a subscription worth 2,400 rubles for 10 visits and 3,300 rubles for 15 visits. A bath visit per person up to 300 rubles.

Water park in Makhachkala "Aqualand": photos and description

The second most popular water park in Makhachkala is Aqualand. It is also rich in entertainment and attractions. Two pools (adult and children's) invite guests and residents of the city. Both are equipped with slopes of varying heights and steepness. Some of them are closed and have funnels.

The children's pool is equipped with fountains and a variety of splashes. The adult has a large geyser installation and waterfalls of various heights.

Water park in Makhachkala "Aqualand" surprises with its size and variety of services. Park guests love to attend a huge number of entertainment programs for children and adults. Evening programs attract with your favorite music tracks, karaoke, color music and amazing DJs.

The organizers of the water park created amazing conditions for visitors. These are cozy small changing rooms, clean showers and toilets.

other services

During school holidays, water parks organize summer camps for children. Educated and instructors conduct interesting educational activities, offer entertainment programs. And most importantly - bear full responsibility for the life and health of children.

Also, water parks in Makhachkala are always happy for young people. They offer to hold proms that are unforgettable. Modern neon lights turn the water park into a stunning disco room. Fun lasts until the morning!

Aqualand Waterpark once a week holds a youth-favorite Wet T-shirt competition. Any of the girls present can participate. She should be charming, active and relaxed. Victory is awarded by the applause of the audience.

Children's water park in Makhachkala, in addition to standard water entertainments, holds weekend contests with fun raffles and prizes.
