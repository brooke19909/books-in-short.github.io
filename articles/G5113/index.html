5 * Hotels: Sunrise Select Garden Beach Resort & Spa. Reviews, photos

The hotel complex 5 * Sunrise Select Garden Beach Resort & Spa is a great option for a family vacation, because there is an incredibly rich infrastructure, plenty of entertainment, as well as an absolute ban on smoking.





Resort Description

Resort Sunrise Select Garden Beach Resort & Spa 5 * (Hurghada) is a chic hotel complex that every tourist will like. In addition to the fact that the hotel has its own beach, there are also three swimming pools, one of which has a children's section.

Comfort is one of the main conditions for a quality and good rest. In this regard, the hotel complex rooms deserve special attention. Their elegant interior, as well as modern equipment give guests a sense of home comfort. One cannot but rejoice at the technical capabilities that are provided for in the guest apartments.

The hotel has several restaurants and bars responsible for good food. A rich entertainment and wellness program is also provided. Perhaps that is why this hotel has repeatedly received international awards from experts in the field of leisure and tourism.





Hotel location

Resort 5 * Sunrise Select Garden Beach Resort & Spa 5 * (Hurghada, Egypt) is located on the very shore of the Red Sea (first line). From here, the city center is 25 km away. To get to the international airport, you have to overcome a distance of 15 km.

Facilities at the hotel apartments

5 * Hotel Sunrise Select Garden Beach Resort & Spa in order to ensure maximum comfort for its guests provides for the following necessary amenities in the rooms:

  • depending on the number of storeys, your apartment will have access to a balcony or terrace with comfortable plastic or wicker furniture;
  • depending on the category of the room, the bathroom of your room will be equipped with a modern shower with hydromassage or a spacious Jacuzzi;
  • a hairdryer is provided in the bathroom;
  • air conditioning and temperature control are made thanks to an individual split system;
  • so that when leaving the room, you do not worry about the safety of your belongings, a safe with an electronic combination lock is provided, which only you can open;
  • for filling the minibar a separate fee is charged;
  • To view Russian and foreign satellite channels, a modern widescreen TV is provided;
  • Each room has an international and domestic telephone.




Hotel facilities

The 5 * Sunrise Select Garden Beach Resort & Spa Resort offers the following infrastructure:

  • a large conference room with the most modern equipment so that you can hold meetings, business negotiations or other events at the highest level;
  • there is a currency exchange office in the lobby, as well as an ATM for withdrawing cash from plastic cards;
  • There are several grocery stores, as well as souvenir shops and a jewelry salon;
  • there is a medical office, as well as the opportunity to call a professional doctor;
  • for washing and ironing your clothes, you can contact the laundry or dry cleaning service, which operates at the hotel;
  • there is an outdoor heated pool, which provides a children's section with water slides;
  • a few meters from the hotel there is a private beach with free sun loungers, umbrellas, mattresses and towels (note that there is no rescue service);
  • You can get a session of the latest cosmetic procedures in the spa;
  • the services of the Russian bath, steam room, sauna, Turkish hammam and jacuzzi are especially in great demand;
  • only the most skilled craftsmen work in the hotel's massage elephant.




Entertainment opportunities

The hotel and resort complex 5 * Sunrise Select Garden Beach Resort & Spa has prepared the following entertainment program for you:

  • free classes in the modern gym, which hospitably opens its doors to guests who are already 16 years old at the time of visiting the hotel;
  • on the beach you will find rental equipment for water sports and entertainment, as well as a center providing a full range of services related to diving;

For children

The most fastidious tourists are, of course, children. That is why Sunrise Select Garden Beach Resort & Spa 5 * pays so close attention to the organization of their leisure. So, for the kids in the hotel the following infrastructure has been created:

Negative reviews

  • cleaning the beach, as well as the general care of its territory practically does not receive attention;

  • local alcohol is of very low quality, and import prices are simply sky-high;
  • in the last few seasons absolutely any privileges and privileges for regular guests have been canceled;
  • housekeeping is very, very poor quality;
  • at the beach bar there is a constant lack of plastic cups for drinks;
  • huge queues always gather in the dining room due to the sluggishness of employees at the distribution, as well as the lack of seats.
