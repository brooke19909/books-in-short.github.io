Malta Language Camp for Kids and Teens: An Overview of the Best

In search of new experiences, and also wanting to improve or learn the language of world communication number one, we will go to a place of amazing beauty - the island of Malta! This is a great opportunity to combine relaxation with easy learning in a natural language environment.

Sunny island

Malta is a warm and sunny island, located somewhere between the shores of northern Africa and the southern points of the island of Sicily in the very center of the gentle Mediterranean Sea. Malta is called one of the islands of the Maltese archipelago, which gave its name to the whole state. The state, by our Russian standards, is very small, resembling rather some regional or regional center. An amazing country with a temperate tropical climate, fantastically beautiful landscapes, sandy and rocky beaches, wild and well-maintained (for every taste), rugged rocky shores and a turquoise blue clear sea. This island is the dream of any tourist, and annually fans of a quiet sea holiday from all over the world come here.

A bit of history

The Maltese archipelago is also interesting for its history leading to the distant past. Arriving here once, you should definitely go on the path of the past, discovering ancient monuments of architecture, visiting stone temples and sanctuaries, whose age exceeds the famous Egyptian pyramids. A land that knew more than one generation of colonialists, and absorbed parts of their national culture - Greeks and Spaniards, French and British, Arabs and Phoenicians, and even in some small period of history, Russians.

Second state language

This attractive territory for many, located at the intersection of the main sea routes from Europe to Asia and Africa, has long been an apple of discord. And only in our times, already in the 20th century, the state gains the long-awaited independence. Being a long time under the influence of the English-speaking culture, the Maltese logically made this language their second official language. Such is this land of myths and legends, fairy tales and medieval knights.

World English Learning Center

But Malta is interesting not only as a tourist attraction. It is here that you can not only have a great rest and get a lot of new impressions, but also spend time with purely practical benefits, namely, studying a foreign language. In the capital of Malta, as well as in other relatively large cities, for many years there are numerous language schools that accept students from all over the world. Learn here, of course, English - it is an unchanging leader in world communication.

In general, Malta is not only a center of world tourism, but also a world-famous center for learning English. In order to help people of all ages and nationalities better to master the first language of international communication, special summer language camps are organized here. Arriving at such a place, anyone can plunge into the laid-back atmosphere of communication with native speakers from the first days and not only overcome the language barrier, spend time with benefit and pleasure.

Language schools and camps

Depending on the availability of time and financial capabilities, everyone can choose for themselves a training program of various durations and different levels of complexity. It is also important to pre-determine the choice of school. And this, it should be noted, is a difficult question, since there are a lot of language schools in Malta .

Malta summer language camp is the best choice for any child

For children, probably, the best option for training will be a summer Maltese camp. Training at the summer camp allows you to combine immersion in an authentic language environment with a pleasant and useful sea vacation. All beach areas of Maltese cities are within walking distance of training centers. It should be noted that all international language camps for children and adolescents in Malta operate all year round. The training program includes language courses that are designed for periods of school holidays. Classes in a language camp in Malta take place in the morning, while in the afternoon you can completely devote to relaxation and exciting trips to the sunny islands and hospitable cities of the archipelago.

Overview of summer language camps

Before those who decide to embark on this linguistic journey, sooner or later the question will arise of choosing a place and a training program. Malta has many language camps for children. In this article we will try to introduce you to some of the most famous of them. The program of each camp is very diverse and includes, in addition to the curriculum itself, various activities: sports team games, beach volleyball, yoga, fitness, discussion and strategy games, master classes. In the evening, beach parties are organized for all teens on the beach. Among the best language camps in Malta, according to tourists, includes the Junior Camp. This is a place where traditional language training is accompanied by a large number of events - intellectual, sports and entertainment. This children's summer language camp is located in Malta in Sliema. The camp is designed for adolescents aged 11-17 years.

The language camp in Malta for teens Perfect English is open to all children from 10 to 17 years old, and it is located in Bugibba. The camp program, in addition to training, is rich in cultural events. Here, children get acquainted with the history of the Maltese archipelago, travel along the old streets of the modern capital, and also go on an excursion to the ancient capital of the state - Mdina. The program includes cruises, as well as visits to the cities with which the history of the Maltese knights is associated.

Malta Language Camp Lexica Malta Club for teens 12 to 17 years old is located in Gzira. To study in the camp, you must pass the entrance test. Classes are held at the base of the Chamber College training center, which offers various language programs - from individual lessons to business courses.

The international language camp in Malta in Sliema Bravo kid's has everything you need for a comfortable children's vacation. The program consists not only of training, but also of interesting excursions and island tours. The camp maintains constant contact with parents.

Children aged 7-16 years can study at the United English language camp. The program includes a variety of workshops and creative workshops, music games and sports. During the training, the guys watch and voice the cartoons in English themselves, and in the end they even make their own movie!

What could be more beautiful: having studied in bright and spacious classes under the guidance of experienced and friendly teachers, go on foot to the warm sea shore - where the bright turquoise wave slowly rolls onto the golden beach.
