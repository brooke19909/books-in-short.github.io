Equipment

Sale of the phone is carried out in a small box made of thick shockproof cardboard. It will protect the phone when delivered, for example, to your home or work. Options Philips W6500 is almost standard, in addition, it includes an additional removable panel. The kit includes:

Design and ergonomics

The appearance of the phone is quite modern and attractive, it immediately catches the eye. Without looking at the name, you might think that this is another phone from the Lumia series. However, coming closer, we immediately realize that we were mistaken.





The case is made of high-quality and practical plastic. The model is sold with two removable color panels - a bright yellow and more practical gray back cover. This allows you to reflect the mood of a person, as well as in a certain sense, his style.

Display

The Philips W6500 smartphone has an excellent 4.3-inch capacitive display. This screen has an average resolution, it is 540 by 960 pixels, and it turns out that for one inch the pixel density is 256 pixels. The capacitive sensor allows you to process up to 5 touches at a time.



The IPS technology used to create the screen makes the sensor incredibly sensitive, and the viewing angles and clarity of color reproduction are quite comfortable for outside viewers. The screen brightness has a good adjustment range, which helps to eliminate the lack of brightness when tilting the screen. By the way, tilt angles do not affect the color rendering level at all. Anti-glare protection helps preserve the image, leaving it clear enough on a clear, sunny day.

The display has the usual settings for the Android system. In addition to brightness, functions such as button illumination, backlight timeout, wallpaper change and auto-rotate screen are available.

operating system

The Philips Xenium W6500, which we are currently reviewing, runs on the Android operating system in version 4.2.2 of Jelly Bean. Camera

Two cameras are installed in the smartphone Philips Xenium W6500, reviews about them are quite twofold. The main camera of this phone has a resolution of 8 MP, which allows it to take excellent pictures in the afternoon, as well as shoot high-quality video with a resolution of 720p at a speed of 30 frames / sec. As for photographing and shooting at night, things are much worse here. The presence of an LED flash does not help much in the dark - photos and videos are slightly blurry and not very clear, although, on the other hand, what else can you expect from a phone? The main camera is equipped with automatic autofocus and a catcher for up to 10 people at a time. There is also the presence of macro photography - the minimum distance from the subject is 5 centimeters. The location of the main camera is very convenient - it is located in the upper back of the smartphone, clearly in the center.





In addition, the phone has a front camera with a resolution of 1.2 MP. It is great for shooting selfy and video calling, for example via Skype or Viber. The front camera is located unusually for smartphones in this category - on the left, and not on the right.

Multimedia



The Philips W6500, which we are reviewing, is equipped with standard video and audio players for Android 4.0. They are no different in new versions of the system: all the same design, ease of use, limited sound settings in the equalizer, etc.

In addition to players, the phone has an FM-receiver. It is distinguished by its power of receiving frequencies, with inserted headphones, the search for active radio waves is much better than that of its direct competitors. It works in the frequency range from 87.5 to 107.8 MHz.
