How to calculate the amount of yarn per product? Rules and Secrets

No one will argue with the statement that knitting is not only a form of creativity, but also a wonderful vacation. The craftswoman sits quietly, knits, as if by chance creating not only things that are useful, but also simply elegant and beautiful. But before starting this interesting work, the knitter should do a rather boring calculation. How to calculate the amount of yarn per product? Let's try to figure it out.

Why is math needed in this matter?

It is convenient to engage in knitting if the photo is taken from some source - a magazine or a book. Indeed, in this case, almost nothing needs to be calculated, since everything is already indicated in the description: the number of knitting needles or hook, the number of yarn, thread, and what size should result in a sample. But here lies a small pitfall. There are practically no ladies with standard sizes: someone has a chest higher, someone has a non-standard waist, someone has hands either long or short. Therefore, when sewing dresses, skirts and blouses, seamstresses have to adjust their patterns. Similarly, knitters in some cases need adjustments to yarn consumption when they work with knitting needles and crochet.

And this is if you do not mention the cases when the craftswomen knit something by eye, or "from the head." There is certainly no way to do without minimal preparation. How to calculate the amount of yarn per product? First things first.

What to say about bobbin yarn?

How to calculate the amount of bobbin yarn per product? Consumption is its advantage. On bobbins indicate numbers, for example, 20/4. This is the metric length of the thread: 20 is the number, and 4 is the number of threads of which, in fact, the yarn consists. When dividing the first by the second, the length of the thread in one gram of yarn is obtained. For example, if the designation is 30/2, then in one gram it is 15 m, i.e. 1,500 m per 100 g. Of such yarn, as a rule, they are knitted in three additions using 3,5 knitting needles.

Now you can tie the sample, wash it and see the result. The bobbin yarn has a special impregnation that leaves after washing, and the thread can reveal its beauty.
