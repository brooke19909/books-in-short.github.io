Let's talk about how to find protons, neutrons and electrons

Let's talk about how to find protons, neutrons and electrons. There are three types of elementary particles in an atom, each of which has its own elementary charge, mass.





Core structure

In order to understand how to find protons, neutrons, and electrons, we present the structural features of the nucleus. It is the main part of the atom. Inside the nucleus are protons and neutrons, called nucleons. Inside the nucleus, these particles can pass into each other.

For example, to find protons, neutrons, and electrons in a hydrogen atom, you need to know its serial number. If we take into account that it is this element that leads the periodic system, then its nucleus contains one proton.

The diameter of the atomic nucleus is ten thousandth of the total size of the atom. The bulk of the entire atom is concentrated in it. The mass of the nucleus exceeds thousands of times the sum of all the electrons in the atom.
