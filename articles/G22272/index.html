Hotel Marina Spa Hotel 5 * (China Sanya): photos and reviews of tourists from Russia

Even 10 years ago, tourists did not consider China as a resort country. More and more this state was associated with industry. Now the situation has changed a bit. A large number of tourists travel to this country to relax and visit interesting sights. Marina Spa Hotel 5 * receives guests from all over the world and Russian-speaking vacationers often choose this hotel for housing.

Where is?

Hainan Island is very popular among tourists. Dadonghai is a resort area that is developing and improving rapidly. Marina Spa Hotel 5 * is located near the city of Sanya, 12.7 km from the airport.





The hotel operates around the clock and check-in is done at any time. At the reception area there are always administrators and managers. Due to the expansion of infrastructure, many construction sites operate here.

Description of the complex

Marina Spa Hotel 5 * has 10 floors and is currently located in the built-up area of ​​Dadonghai. Therefore, the complex is remote from the center and the infrastructure near it is only developing.





The hotel occupies a fairly large territory, which is decorated in a traditional Chinese style with its inherent elements and symbols. Everywhere there are green spaces, and in the evening the lighting is turned on. In certain places where you can walk around the territory, decorative lanterns work.

The tourists are very surprised by the variety of figures that are placed around the complex in recreation areas. There are also classic Chinese-style gazebos where you can relax and enjoy views of the ocean and port.

Accommodations

Marina Spa Hotel 5 * offers its customers to choose one of the rooms they like:

  • Duplex Ocean View Suite with Two Double Beds;
  • double deluxe suite for two rooms with ocean view;
  • double one-bedroom suite with a balcony;
  • de suite for 2 people with two single or one double bed;
  • premium for 2 people with a view of the port;
  • two-story suite with one double bed and a sofa bed.

All rooms have air conditioning, electric kettle, minibar, safe, TV, bath or shower, toilet. The apartments also have work desks and chairs. Superior rooms have coffee tables and soft armchairs near them.

The bathroom includes a hairdryer, towels, bathrobes and slippers. All rooms also have mini hygiene kits, which are regularly updated as necessary.

Family rooms are non-smoking. For these purposes, special zones are allocated in the hotel. The complex also has non-smoking rooms. No pets allowed.

Dining at the hotel

Marina Spa Hotel 5 * (Hainan) provides guests with breakfast, which is included in the price. Meals are based on the "buffet". In the spacious hall, cooked dishes are displayed on racks in large containers or on dishes that vacationers can take in any quantity.

The menu always contains meat and fish snacks. The assortment also includes vegetables and fruits. Always at the racks there is an abundance of rice with all kinds of seasonings and additives. Standard breakfast lovers can take sausages and scrambled eggs. Also, oatmeal is always available.





A variety of desserts and pastries are always placed on racks. It serves a good range of hot and cold drinks. At other times, the complex has a restaurant and a grill bar with prices lower than in the city center. Here you can always dine with traditional Chinese dishes.

Entertainment

On the territory of Marina Spa Hotel 5 * in China (Hainan), a modern children’s playground with a special coating is equipped. There is also a club for children in the building. It has a pool with balls, trampolines and various toys, a zone for creative activities is allocated.

The hotel has a spa center. In it, guests of the complex can carry out cosmetic treatments for body care. Vacationers often book a therapeutic massage course here. As you know, better than Chinese experts in this field you will not find.





The center provides exotic skin care treatments. Big snails and even snakes take part in them. You can also make classic wraps with various components in this center.

Near the hotel there are interesting places worth visiting:

  • Park "Deer turned his head" ;
  • huge shopping center;
  • golf Club;
  • stadium;
  • amusement park.

Also in the city there are many various restaurants, bars and discos.

Beach and Pool

Marina Spa Hotel 5 * in Dadonghai (Hainan) has a very large pool on its territory. It has clear blue water that passes through special filters. Every morning the workers clean the bottom with special vacuum cleaners.

On the sides in some areas installed figures of women. They hold in their hands jugs from which water pours. In the evening, the pool has original lighting.





Around the area for relaxation and sunbathing. Sun loungers are installed here and at the request of guests on hot days, protective sunshades are provided. In this area there is a small bar where you can order any drinks and light snacks.

The hotel does not have its own beach. Guests are invited to visit the city's recreational facilities. In this regard, at certain hours a shuttle service is provided for these purposes. On city beaches, you can rent the necessary things for a comfortable stay and have a bite to eat in small cafes and restaurants.

Service in Marina Spa Hotel 5 * (China)

Hainan (Dadonghai) is visited by many tourists from Russia. Therefore, large hotels are trying to have guides in their staff who speak Russian. This hotel was no exception.

Room cleaning and towels are changed daily or at the request of tourists. At the reception, administrators work around the clock. They are always ready to solve everyday issues of customers or fulfill their request.

There is a parking lot near the hotel. At the reception you can arrange a rental car. Bike rental is also available here. Outdoor enthusiasts can go on excursions on their own.

At certain times, the complex organizes a transfer of guests to the city. Passengers drop off and pick up near the Gulf of Dadonghai. There are beaches and there are entertainment and shopping centers.

Laundry facilities and ironing services are available on site. Also, in rooms and beyond, tourists can use free internet. The reception has a luggage storage room.

The complex has a business center and conference room with all necessary equipment. After a business meeting, the restaurant can organize a buffet upon request.

Marina Spa Hotel 5 * in Hainan: reviews

On the Internet you can find a few reviews from tourists from Russia about the work of this complex. But those that are available are quite informative and useful to holidaymakers who choose a hotel to stay in Dadonghai.

Tourists note that the appearance and decoration inside the hotel is at a decent level. The reception has friendly staff that provides quick check-in and check-out.



Almost all Russian-speaking guests are satisfied with the work of a translator and guide. A young man always comes to the aid of vacationers if necessary. In the rooms of Marina Spa Hotel 5 * , according to visitors' reviews, it is always clean and all communications work.

Almost all guests are satisfied with the variety and taste of breakfast served in the hotel. The dishes are quite nutritious and enough for all the guests of the complex.

Of the negative aspects most often there are comments about the transfer to the beach. Guests complain that the bus deviates from the schedule and sometimes everyone does not have enough space in it. In this case, you have to spend extra money on a taxi.

Also, vacationers are not happy with the beach, which is located near the hotel. Near it is located a port and swimming next to sailing ships does not bring any pleasure. Also, because of the port in this coastal zone there is a lot of garbage.
