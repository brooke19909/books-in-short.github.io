Five Ways to Make a Doll Bed

Every girl has so many dolls. And each of them requires its own furniture. Well, do not sleep on the same toys on the couch? Now I want to tell you exactly how to make a bed for a doll.





Method 1

It is worth noting that making a sleeping puppet is not so difficult. You can just put on rags and put your pet there. But this is completely uninteresting! It‚Äôs better to try and make your doll a beautiful bed. To do this, you will need a corrugated cardboard (an excellent way out of the situation is packaging for large household appliances, it is made from this material) and some stationery. So how to make a bed for a doll? The first step is to cut cardboard of the right size. The length of the bed depends on the size of the chrysalis, but the width can be different. Five parts need to be cut out of cardboard: two for the backs, one for the mattress and two sidewalls so that the toy does not suddenly fall out. All this is glued with glue, on top you can glue it with newsprint (on the principle of papier mache), so the product will be stronger. Now it remains only to make the bed beautiful. To do this, you can glue it with pieces of wallpaper, a bright napkin and even a cloth - everything that comes to hand. The sleeping place for your favorite doll is ready!

Method 2

The next way to make a bed for a doll is to use small boards, you can plywood. It will be necessary to cut out three parts of the bed - for the mattress and two backs, then glue them together. The base is ready. Now everything needs to be painted over with paint, allowed to dry and beautifully decorated. You can also paint the bed with paints and then varnish. Throw a blanket and a couple of pillows upstairs - a beautiful bed for your favorite doll is ready!

Method 3

Another option is how to make a bed for a doll. To do this, you need foam rubber and various rags. The base will be made of a piece of foam, which you just need to wrap with a cloth so that it does not fall, to make such a mattress cover. The bed itself is ready. Now you need to make a little effort to decorate it. To do this, knit a beautiful plaid and a couple of pillows, you can make them from pieces of fabric. And to make the sleeping place look more attractive, put it under the wall and hang a rug at the head of the bed, it will seem so that the bed has a back.

Method 4

The next way to make a doll bed is to use empty matchboxes. To do this, they need to be glued together (in two or even three rows), and glued with colored tape on top. The bed for the doll is ready. Moreover, now the toy has a place where she can store her things (use boxes as drawers).

Method 5

You can also figure out how to make a four-poster bed for the doll. The base may be any of the above. The canopy itself can be built using wires stretched on four sides of the bed (they are easy to hide, for example, under the trimmings of tulle, which they can be wrapped around for decoration). It remains only to pull the fabric. This tulle, tulle, etc. The universality of this canopy is that it is easily attached to wood, cardboard and foam - any basis.
