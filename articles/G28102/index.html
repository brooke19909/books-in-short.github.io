Camp in Volgograd "Cape Verde": description, reviews

Fans of out-of-town recreation are offered their services by the Cape Verde camp in Volgograd. Among the shady trees on the Volga coast you will have a great vacation or weekend. And in cozy cottages, guests will find everything you need for a comfortable stay.

Description

The Cape Verde camp site (Volgograd) is located in the Tatyanka-3 microdistrict, in one of its most picturesque corners. Comfortable cottages with panoramic views of the Volga are waiting for guests at the landscaped well-groomed territory. The beach is just one minute walk from the health resort. The recreation center has its own restaurant, which serves European cuisine.

If bad weather prevents you from relaxing by the river, you can go for a swim in the indoor pool. By prior arrangement, you will be provided with a place in a free secure parking lot. It is also necessary to warn if you bring pets with you. This service is also free. There is a playground for children. You can use the equipment for playing badminton and table tennis, equipped with sports fields. Here you can not only swim in the Volga in the summer, but also have a wonderful time in the winter. The address of the Cape Verde camp site in Volgograd: Krasnoarmeysky district, st. Jack London, 1D.

Accommodation for vacationers

To accommodate guests at the recreation center , two options are offered:

  • Typical four-bed cottages with a terrace, two bedrooms, a living room, a kitchenette and a bathroom with shower. One bedroom has a double bed, the other two twin beds. The floors in the rooms are warm. Possible additional accommodation for two people.
  • Double cottage with a terrace, a bedroom, a living room, a kitchenette, a sauna, a bathroom with a shower. It is possible to accommodate two additional seats.

The price includes breakfast. For all vacationers at the Cape Verde camp in Volgograd there is free access to wireless Internet.

Amenities in cottages

Each house is equipped with the most comfortable amenities:

  • air conditioning;
  • flat screen tv;
  • DVD player
  • telephone
  • upholstered furniture;
  • a refrigerator;
  • a microwave;
  • an electric kettle;
  • necessary utensils;
  • free toiletries.

At the Cape Verde camp site in Volgograd (photo in the article), each house has its own terrace with a table, benches and barbecue.

Additional services

In order for your vacation to be varied and eventful, the base provides many additional services:

  • Wi-Fi is free throughout.
  • For picnic lovers, coal is provided for an additional fee (300 rubles).
  • To relax with the company, you can rent a gazebo (200 rubles per person).
  • In the year-round indoor pool, free time for vacationers is limited, the rest of the time is paid hourly (200 rubles per person). You can rent a pool for the company.
  • At the camp "Green Cape" (Volgograd) at your service comfortable banquet rooms. There are halls designed for 40 and 80 people.
  • Tourists can relax in the bath or sauna.
  • For fans of team games there is the opportunity to stretch out on the volleyball court.
  • Own beach recreation center is not equipped. The equipped municipal beach is just five minutes walk.
  • A conference hall, meeting room, and classroom are offered.
  • On the open area you will be helped to conduct a variety of events.
  • Meals are organized in a cafe.
  • There is a free car park located at the entrance to the territory.
  • You can ride a horse (paid service).
