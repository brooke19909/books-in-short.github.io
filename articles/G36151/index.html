Kuredu Island Resort 4 * (Maldives, Laviyani Atoll, Kuredu): room description, service, reviews

Maldives is a scattering of picturesque islets and atolls in the waters of the Indian Ocean. Every tourist dreams of being here. The resort is conducive to bliss and relaxation. Kilometers of secluded beaches, stunningly beautiful nature, upscale complexes - all these are the Maldives. In our article we want to talk about one of the hotels - Kuredu Island Resort 4 * (Maldives).

A bit about the hotel ...

Kuredu Island Resort 4 * (Maldives) is located on the amazingly picturesque island of Kuredu. The hotel offers its guests everything you need for an amazing vacation on the ocean. It is located in the northern part of Laviyani Atoll, you can get to it from Male airport by airplane in forty minutes. The atoll was originally opened in 1989 and surface villas were built on it. Tourists came here for diving. Later, more modern beach villas were added to the east of the island.

Diving

Kuredu Island Resort & Spa is of interest to diving enthusiasts. Kuredu is one of the best diving spots in the Maldives. There are a lot of interesting places: the outer spurs of the atoll, the underwater islands, and of course the rich underwater world.

About 50 dive sites are scattered throughout the Laviyani Atoll. Their number is regularly replenished with new places. Near the atoll, 15 species of sharks were seen. No less common are the rays that are regularly observed by divers. Experienced instructors will help not only experienced people, but also teach the basics of beginners. In addition, everyone can go snorkeling.

Kuredu Island stretches in the ocean from east to west, and it is more expanded in the eastern part, and in the west the land is greatly narrowed. The long south coast overlooks the Laviyani Atoll and has a gorgeous sandy beach. But the north side is facing the ocean. Here, beaches are rarer and protected by low walls.

Kuredu is one of ten Maldivian islands that offer golf courses. It may sound strange, but the Maldives is incredibly popular among Europeans because of golf. Many from year to year come back here not only to relax, but also to play golf.

June weather conditions

Since the street is already the beginning of summer, tourists will be interested in what is the weather like in the Maldives in June? At this time, the hulhang dominates the islands - this is the south-west wind, which brings precipitation. But do not be upset if you plan a vacation in June, since the likelihood of showers is high only at night. In the daytime, sunny and clear weather usually reigns. The weather in the Maldives in June is good for those people who can not stand the heat. A pleasant bonus for rest during this period will be more reasonable prices.

In general, on the islands in June, the temperature reaches +31 degrees, and sometimes it reaches + 34. At night, you should not wait for coolness, usually at this time the temperature is +26 degrees. Do not be afraid of the rainy season. The sun in June is more active than in winter, so a stay on the beach should be metered and certainly in the shade. And without a cream and a hat you just can not do here.

The probability of precipitation in June, although high, but the showers are not long and end quickly. The weather at the beginning of summer has one feature - it is humidity, which is inconvenient. However, it is well tolerated due to regular winds. Although residents from regions with a dry climate should still be careful.

Strong storms are possible in the first two weeks of the month, so swimming is not always possible. The water temperature reaches +28 degrees, but swimming in a storm is not recommended - it is very dangerous, especially in the reef area. Storms are temporary, so storms give way to clear weather.

However, the Laviyani Atoll is one of those places in the Maldives where good rest is possible in June, as it is predicted there is a high probability of showers of only 6 days. The water temperature in this case reaches +29 degrees, and the air - +31 degrees.

Tourists reviews

Continuing the review of the hotel, it is worth paying attention to the reviews of tourists. It is worth noting that travelers remain completely delighted with Kuredu and the Kuredu Island Resort 4 * service. Each guest is expected to fly to Male.
