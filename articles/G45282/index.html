Marjan Island Resort & Spa 5 *: photos and reviews

The resort Marjan Island Resort & Spa 5 * is an ideal solution for a family or romantic vacation.





Description of the hotel complex

The resort hotel Marjan Island Resort & Spa 5 * is a truly paradise, where the traveler can forget about all the worries and plunge into the atmosphere of a carefree vacation, relaxing comfort and vivid entertainment for every taste. A private sandy beach gives you the opportunity to splash around in the gentle waters of the Persian Gulf. And the pools will appeal to those who do not want to go to the beach.

The resort hotel Marjan Island Resort & Spa 5 * can be proud of its developed infrastructure, which has dozens of tourist and resort facilities. This spa, and restaurants, and gyms, and even its own huge water park, where everyone can frolic on extreme slides.

Speaking about living conditions at Marjan Island Resort & Spa 5 *, one cannot but mention luxury apartments that impress not only with exquisite design, but also with the highest level of comfort. This is achieved due to the fact that the equipment of rooms is thought out to the smallest detail and is aimed at meeting all the needs of guests.





Where is the hotel

Marjan Island Resort & Spa (Ras-Al-Khaimah) is located on the artificial island of the emirate of Ras Al-Khaimah on the shores of the Persian Gulf. It is approximately 45 minutes from Dubai. Very close to the hotel is the popular Al-Hamra shopping center, where you will spend unforgettable shopping. There is also a large modern water park.





Number of rooms

Marjan Island Resort & Spa (Managed By Accor) has the following apartments for guests arriving at the hotel:

  • Family apartments have a large area and are two bedrooms. It is especially convenient that each room has its own bathroom. Also in the room with a double bed, which is provided for parents, there is access to a small but very cozy balcony with plastic chairs and a clothes dryer.
  • Superior rooms are also two-room, but in addition to the bedroom they also have a living room. The apartments are quite comfortable, because the set of furniture and amenities in them is aimed at satisfying all the potential needs that guests may have.

Room Amenities

The main infrastructure of the UAE is hotels. Marjan Island Resort & Spa is quite popular among tourists. An important role in this was played by the fact that the hotel is characterized by an increased level of comfort. So, speaking of the rooms, one can note the presence of the following amenities in them:

  • a large plasma TV with a widescreen screen, broadcasting a huge package of entertainment and news channels, including Russian ones (in addition, the option to play video from laser discs and flash drives);
  • in the bathroom, in addition to the toilet, there is a shower or a bath, a washbasin, a large mirror with a lamp, a bathrobe and slippers, a branded cosmetic set, a hairdryer;
  • additional comfort is provided by carpet, which is laid on the floors of the room;
  • there is a special device that combines an electronic clock, an alarm clock and a radio station;
  • use of a reliable electronic safe is free;
  • there is no own air conditioning in the room, as the temperature is regulated by the general hotel ventilation system;
  • you can always control your body weight thanks to electronic floor scales;
  • to solve all pressing issues, without going down to the reception, use the phone (it is also connected to an international line);
  • the room has a minibar, but it is filled only for an additional fee;
  • there is free Wi-Fi internet;
  • tea and coffee set with electric water heater and service.




At the service of tourists

The hotel offers its guests the services of the following infrastructure facilities:

  • the hotel is responsible for food and gastronomic pleasures at restaurants where you can try both local dishes and international cuisine;
  • if necessary, you can call a paid doctor;
  • in the business center you can use computer equipment and cable Internet;
  • there are several bars where you can try many drinks, cocktails and smoke a hookah;
  • There is a large modern spa center, which has separate rooms for both women and men;
  • every day the hotel bus runs to the shopping center for free, but the transfer to Dubai is carried out at a certain rate;
  • there are several shops at the hotel where you will be offered not only products and souvenirs, but also clothes, jewelry, and even elite perfumes;
  • there is an opportunity to receive hairdressing services and do manicures;
  • a large outdoor pool with sunbeds and parasols standing around;
  • in case of bad weather there are indoor pools, which are divided into male and female departments (in different rooms);
  • for the holidays there is a banquet hall for 120 guests;
  • each of the three conference rooms can accommodate 50 people;
  • There is a bank office with a terminal for cashing out plastic cards, as well as with the possibility of currency exchange.




Positive reviews

Many tourists respond very well about the hotel Marjan Island Resort & Spa. The reviews reflect the whole range of positive moments that rest in this hotel complex gives:

  • a very beautiful area, which is constantly looked after by technical staff;
  • the beach is large and clean, strewn with white sand, with a lot of new comfortable sunbeds;
  • good condition of the rooms, as well as original and beautiful design;
  • good quality Internet - the signal is excellent in all areas of the hotel, on the territory and even on the beach;
  • excellent nutrition: a varied menu, the products are always fresh, a large assortment of dishes;
  • high-quality service, due to the professionalism of employees;
  • there is the possibility of choosing different food systems, so you do not need to adjust your plans to the dining room schedule;
  • windows of almost all rooms overlook the sea;
  • At the reception there are employees who are fluent in Russian.

Negative reviews

Despite the many positive moments of relaxation at Marjan Island Resort & Spa 5 *, the reviews also contain a number of negative comments:

  • there is practically no animation and any entertainment, which especially affects children's rest;
  • construction is underway near the hotel - this creates additional noise and very spoils the surrounding view;
  • the hotel area is not entirely landscaped, as it is still in a state of refinement.
