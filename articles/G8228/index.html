How to make beaded bracelets yourself

Perhaps such an accessory as a bracelet is the most universal of all jewelry created from beads. The rings are too tiny, and it is often difficult for beginners to cope with them. Necklaces and pendants require experience and at least minimal skills in beadwork. But the bracelets made of beads, with their own hands to weave which is not difficult at all, is just what beginners need to master various techniques.



Features weaving bracelets

In creating a bracelet, the main one is one of the main repeating motifs. In parallel weaving it can be a flower, a circle, a rhombus. The right color scheme enriches the product even with the most basic technique. Let's consider in more detail how to make bead bracelets.



The technique of "braiding"

The essence of the "braid" is to combine the 9 strands on which the beads are strung, to combine into one harmonious pattern and get a beautiful product. Before weaving a bracelet from beads, you need to stock up on a thin, but strong fishing line or a special wire; beads (for the first time it is desirable that they be the same size); according to the color combination map, select the desired gamut.



Process description

  1. Take 9 pieces of wire (about 40 cm, the length can be adjusted in the course of work, focusing on the size of the wrist).
  2. Fix the edges of all threads with one knot.
  3. It will be convenient to work with this workpiece further if you attach it to some base with a standard pin. For example, pin to a pillow or a special board for needlework. This technique is very practical in all cases, since bead bracelets can be made for a long time. It is convenient if during the break it can be left in a fixed form.
  4. For every three strands we string beads of the same color. For example: on the first three strands we put on green beads; then yellow; on the last - blue. In the end, do not forget to fix them with knots so that the product does not fall apart.
  5. The most important stage is weaving braids from the workpiece. The braid should be tight, the threads should be distributed directly and evenly. In the end, it remains to attach the fittings.

How to make bead bracelets using the parallel weaving technique

This is a very common weaving method. The first, from which they begin to master more complex options after a single thread. The process of weaving such jewelry is not so complicated, but it is very exciting.

Description of the main actions

Choose 3 colors for the item. For example, red, yellow and black. And also prepare a wire, fishing line or strong nylon thread.

So, weaving:

  1. In the front row - one black bead.
  2. In the second row - two red beads.
  3. In the third - three beads together: red at the edges, in the middle - yellow.
  4. In the fourth - again 2 red.
  5. In the fifth row - one bead - black.

Flower set

As a result, you should get one element in the form of a flower. Out of many such leitmotifs repeating one after another, a bracelet of the required length is created. Moreover, in this technique you can create and decoration on the neck. It will turn out a wonderful kit. Knowing how to make bead bracelets, go for it and decorate yourself with bright colors!
