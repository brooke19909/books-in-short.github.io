Huawei Ascend G6 Reviews. Huawei Ascend G6 Smartphone

Huawei is a well-known Chinese brand that produces low-cost, but high-quality electronics. A fairly popular model was the new product in 2014 - Huawei Ascend G6. Owner reviews indicate the balance of the smartphone, excellent quality / performance / price ratio, while it also has its drawbacks.





Front design

The Huawei Ascend G6 smartphone is an exact copy of Huawei's once-flagship smartphone - the Ascend P6 model. The gadget demonstrates a compact design philosophy thanks to the average diagonal of the screen, narrow frames around it and well-chosen colors. The Huawei Ascend G6 Black looks especially impressive: the case is not just black, but rather graphite.

Ahead, the G6 has a compact 4.5-inch display with a rather narrow frame around it (not nearly as inconspicuous as in the LG G2, but in the spirit of many analogues). Above the screen are:

  • 5MP front camera eye;
  • spoken speaker grid;
  • a set of sensors for automatic brightness and proximity adjustment;
  • LED notifier, notifying of missed events, removal and the need to install a charger.

Modifications

There are several versions that differ not in appearance, but in technical stuffing. Performance

The heart of the Huawei Ascend G6 is the 2-core Qualcomm Snapdragon. The processor model depends on the modification of the Huawei Ascend G6. Work autonomy

The Huawei Ascend G6 is equipped with a battery that is too small in capacity: 2000 mAh. In addition, it is not removable. According to reviews, when used as a phone (hour of calls, SMS, applications, the Internet, a few casual games), the device lasts about a day without recharging. AnTuTu Battery shows 330 points - a low indicator. With very active use, constant communication on social networks, surfing the Internet, SMS, numerous calls, you can count on 12-14 hours of work. The device charges quickly: only 3 hours.
