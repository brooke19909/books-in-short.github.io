5 * Hotels: Royal Beach Resort & Spa, UAE, Sharjah. Hotel description, reviews

Holidays in the United Arab Emirates is famous for its luxury. Oriental hospitality and traditions in this country are harmoniously combined with European business development, high technology and first-class service. The unique state seemed to have grown out of the sand of the desert under the scorching sun and literally in several decades reached prosperity. This is an oasis in the desert and a place of pilgrimage for many tourists.





If you have chosen this country for your future travel and leisure, then you should pay attention to luxury 5 * hotels. Royal Beach Resort & Spa, for example, is one of the most popular in the emirate of Sharjah. The luxury hotel is located on the shores of the Persian Gulf and offers its guests the highest level of service combined with a first-class beach holiday.

A bit about Sharjah itself

Emirate of Sharjah is the third largest member of the UAE, and is known as a tourist destination not so long ago. He is a close neighbor of Dubai, the center of which is only 15 minutes away, and therefore will be an excellent choice for those who want to spend a democratic vacation at a low price, but at the same time go on a day for shopping.

The Emirate of Sharjah is to some extent unique, because its shores are washed by two bays - Persian and Omani. Its capital has earned the title of the city of knowledge and culture of the Arab world. It is striking in its beauty and architecture, famous for a large number of educational institutions and museums, art centers, famous oriental markets. There are so many attractions in the city that the UNESCO organization in 1998 included it in the list of the greatest world capitals.

All tourists should remember that in the emirate of Sharjah, alcohol is completely prohibited, alcohol is not available not only in restaurants, but also in hotels and hotels for foreigners, moreover, even their import is prohibited. However, the proximity of neighboring Dubai allows you to add variety to your vacation, however, such a pleasure will not cost quite cheaply.

Hotel location





The luxurious and presentable 5 * Royal Beach Resort & Spa Hotel is located on the shores of the Persian Gulf, and from the windows of some rooms you can also see Oman. Own sandy beach is only 50 meters away, and this is a big plus, because just swimming in the sea anywhere in the emirate is prohibited. The city center, major attractions and large shopping centers are located very close, some can be reached on foot, while others go by free buses or taxis. Dubai and Sharjah International Airport is a 30-minute drive away.

Comfort and atmosphere

Without exception, all rooms are connected to a single air conditioning system, but with individual temperature controllers. In addition, guests can always use the services of international telephone communications, high-speed Internet, satellite TV with numerous, including Russian, channels, a safe, a hairdryer and a minibar. Room decoration combines the traditions of the East and European style. Golden red tones, carpet and glass chandeliers under a high multi-level ceiling in combination with Arabic patterns create a special atmosphere.

Hotel Territory

The area occupied by Royal Beach Resort & Spa 5 * (Sharjah), along with one of the best spa centers, is quite impressive. There are two swimming pools on it: outside, but under a roof with sun loungers, on a small platform under the sun, and indoor, inside the building, which operates throughout the year.





For independent travelers and those who arrived at the hotel in connection with work or a business trip, there is a private parking lot and car rental, taxi, porter and secretary services are provided.

What to see in Sharjah?





When tourists get tired of idle rest, the time begins to get acquainted with the cultural traditions and history of the country. Sharjah is located quite close to the Royal Beach Resort, and therefore it would be a terrible mistake not to go and see the old and unique Arab city, especially since it has something to pay attention to:

  • The Blue Bazaar is a spacious market built according to all Islamic canons and having more than six hundred shops selling goods for every taste: clothes, jewelry, modern electronics, but the carpets sold here are the most popular and famous.
  • A gold market with a huge selection of precious metal products, many of which are exclusive, and sellers are willing to bargain for their goods.
  • Al-Nur Mosque, built in 2005, is one of the prominent examples of Islamic architecture. She accepts tourists with oriental hospitality as part of sightseeing tours, but there are certain requirements for the appearance, and photo and video shooting is prohibited.
  • The Ferris Wheel "Eye of the Emirates" soars 60 meters above the ground and allows you to enjoy magnificent panoramic views. The cabins are glass and equipped with air conditioning, capacity - more than 300 people.
  • Aquarium - a huge underwater world, which offers visitors to observe the various marine life (there are more than 250 of them). There is also a cafeteria and museum on site.
  • Museums (maritime, Islamic culture, historical and cultural heritage, visual arts).
  • Musical fountains and much more.
