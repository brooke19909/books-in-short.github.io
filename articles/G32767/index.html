How to find out your debts over the Internet

Gathered abroad? Do you know that if there is a debt, the collection of which was made an official court decision, you may not be allowed out of our country?



To ensure that your plans are not frustrated due to debts on taxes and fines, it is best to check their availability and pay before the trip, just in case. And then the question arises: "How to find out your debts?". To solve this problem, you should have access to the Internet and the necessary documents at hand.

How to find out your tax debts?

Any resident of our country must transfer to the state a large number of different payments. These include income tax, property tax, land and transport tax . Of course, there are so many of them that you can‚Äôt follow everyone. Either the receipt did not come, or they forgot to seize it on the way to the bank. So she remained dusting on the shelf. And time is running out. The payment deadline ends. And for each overdue day, at least small, but still penalties and fines are charged. Therefore, in order not to bring everything to a critical moment, you must pay on time.



Nowadays, modern technology has long lost the need to queue for a tax inspector just to get some information, since you can find out your debts without leaving your home. The Internet will help in this.

To get information about unpaid taxes, you must go to the official website of the tax service. It should be remembered that in the section "Know your debt" you will need to give consent to the provision of personal information. Without confirmation, alas, you will not be able to find out about your unpaid taxes. Next, enter your personal data, enter the numbers with captcha and click "find". After a while, the screen will display all the information on your debt, if any, of course, is available.

If you wish, you can immediately pay for it. There are two ways: cash and bank transfer. To pay in cash, you will need to select the category of taxes for which you are going to pay off the debt , select the "cash settlement" method and click "form" to print the receipt for payment.

A non-cash method involves the payment of debt online using a credit card.

How to find out your debts on traffic police fines?



Every driver of the vehicle, probably, at least once in his life received a receipt to pay a fine, but for others, these receipts are a common thing. So they roll around unpaid. Then the receipts are lost, thrown out, but, ultimately, you have to pay. And what to do? This issue is resolved quite easily and quickly, since you can find out your debts by traffic police fines on the government services portal. On this site you will need to register. After entering all the data, go to the section "Electronic Services", go to the tab "Ministry of the Interior" and check for fines.

Also on this site for you is available and other, no less interesting, information regarding various public services.

That's it. Now you know how to find out your debts. You can pay for them and boldly pack on a long journey without worrying about anything.
