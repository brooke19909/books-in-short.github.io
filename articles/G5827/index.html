Hotel "Moscow" (Kaliningrad): reviews, photos, phone

If you like intelligent classics, then choose an institution such as the Moscow Hotel for accommodation. Kaliningrad has an interesting architecture, and this hotel successfully fits into the general ensemble.

Brief description of the hotel

One of the attractive accommodation options is the Moscow Hotel. Kaliningrad attracts not only tourists, but also business people who are very demanding about their place of residence. As for this institution, a rather wide range of services has been prepared for you, including car parking, wireless Internet, your own beauty salon and much more.

Comfortable rooms decorated in a classic style can boast the Moscow Hotel. Kaliningrad is saturated with European spirit, and the corresponding interior allows you to better feel this atmosphere. Despite the design in the old traditions, the furniture, as well as other amenities here are quite modern and comfortable.

It is worth noting the work of several catering facilities, which the Moscow Hotel has at its disposal. Kaliningrad is a large city, for acquaintance with which you need a lot of strength and energy, they can be obtained through good nutrition. Delicious, high-quality food will be available to you in the company restaurant, pizzeria, as well as in the popular cafeteria.





Hotel Location

Convenient location is characterized by the hotel "Moscow". G. Kaliningrad, Prospekt Mira, 19/21 - the address of this hotel. This is the business district of the city, which is located near the center. Leaving the hotel building, you almost immediately get to the zoo. There is also a public transport stop a few meters from the property. Within walking distance are many cultural attractions, as well as business and tourist infrastructure.

Positive reviews

Quite a lot of tourists choose for themselves such an accommodation option as the Moscow Hotel (Kaliningrad). Reviews about this institution contain the following positive information:

Negative reviews

Of the negative aspects of living in this hotel, it is worth highlighting the following:

  • guests are not provided with slippers, and therefore have to carry with them (well, or walk barefoot on the cold floor);
  • wheels without a lock are attached to the legs of the bed, and therefore, as soon as you try to lie down, it starts to ride;
  • Disposable shampoo bags are very difficult to open (no special incisions);
  • the room does not have air conditioning, and if you open the windows to ventilate, it becomes very noisy;
  • lack of an electric kettle;
  • for ordering elementary tea in your room you will have to pay almost like for a full dinner in a restaurant;
  • there is no hairdryer in the bathroom;
  • the toilet does not have a holder for toilet paper (it is simply placed on the tank);
  • no ramps for the disabled;
  • the waiters in the restaurant do not consider it necessary to bring change;
  • cold in the rooms.




General impression

It is worth noting that the Moscow Hotel is an ideal option for those who want to get to know Kaliningrad better. This establishment is characterized by an incredibly advantageous location. Literally a couple of steps away is a large zoo. You can also visit many cultural heritage sites. The building itself has a rich history. This option is often chosen by those people who arrived in Kaliningrad on a business trip, because the hotel is located in the so-called business district of the city.

Despite the fact that the main concept of this hotel is the atmosphere of antiquity and the classic interior, everything here is clean, new and fresh. The rooms are decorated in pleasant, soft colors, and therefore there is an atmosphere of special comfort. Buffet breakfast is included. Nevertheless, trying to provide a variety of dishes, the cooks forgot about elementary porridge. In addition, the cost of food is quite high. In general, tourists highly value the quality of services and the level of comfort in this hotel.
