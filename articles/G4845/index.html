Hungary. Photos of the most beautiful places in the country

One of the most interesting countries in the world can rightfully be considered Hungary. Photos of her unusually beautiful places, taken by the local population or guests, delight millions of people who have never even visited them. Of course, for lovers of fabulous photos, Hungary is exactly what you need. Pictures of ancient cities, wonderfully preserved architectural monuments, the picturesque Danube, the huge Lake Balaton and other unique places will for a long time become an unfading memory of a wonderful time.

Beautiful country

Today, Hungary is an economically developed modern state that has preserved centuries-old historical and cultural values. The territory of this country is inhabited by Hungarians, Serbs, Slovaks, Romanians, Gypsies, Croats, Poles, Germans, Ukrainians, Bulgarians, and Armenians. Naturally, each of the nations has contributed to the development of the state.



On the territory of Hungary , plains, mountains, and large reservoirs are surprisingly compactly located. In addition, the climate is quite warm in summers and mild winters. This is what irresistible Hungary entails. Photos of its attractions, unfortunately, do not always convey their true beauty. Seeing all this with my own eyes is, of course, much more interesting. Therefore, this state has been and remains popular among tourists from all over the world.

Thermal springs

Undoubtedly, Hungary is a very unusual and interesting country. The inexplicable combination of European orders and original original national customs and traditions in it does not cease to amaze. Rest here is the same multifaceted - this is walking along the old city streets, and enjoying the wonderful scenery of nature, and visiting interesting places. One of them is the thermal spas, allowing you to have a wonderful time and improve your health. Here it will cost much cheaper than in other European countries. And with this approach Hungary is even more attracted to itself. Photos taken while visiting the thermal resorts will complement the pleasant experience of vacationers.

To date, about sixty thousand such water sources have been discovered, of which more than a thousand - with a temperature of more than thirty degrees. The only cave healing bath in Miskolc-Tapolca in Europe is located on Hungarian territory. Also, Lake Heviz is still famous for its natural volcanic origin, the healing hot water of which reaches thirty-three degrees Celsius. Even in the capital, Budapest, there are about one hundred and thirty sources. In general, they provide up to seventy million liters of water per day, the temperature of which ranges from twenty-four to eighty degrees.



Of course, the basis of treatment at the resorts is the favorable characteristics of the waters, and many years of experience and knowledge of local experts, and traditional national hospitality.

Wellness Heviz

Lake Heviz (Hungary), the photo of which may not be known to everyone, is a large geothermal reservoir of natural origin. Formed as a result of the eruption of the volcano, this spring has two beating springs with hot and cold water. Swimming in the healing waters of the lake is a good help in the treatment of joints. The effect of such procedures is quite long. It is important for vacationers to know that with this method of treatment you need to move more. However, children under the age of fourteen are not recommended for swimming in the lake due to the estrogen hormones present in the water, which can negatively affect the children's body.

The symbol of Heviz is lotuses blooming all year round. This resort is most suitable for people who want privacy, walks in forest areas, enjoy the smells of flowering trees, as well as all who care about their youth and body beauty.

Freshwater Hungary

Lake Balaton (Hungary) - a photo of this reservoir will never fully replace its natural picturesqueness. Very often called the fresh Hungarian sea because of its impressive size, it falls in love with millions of people from all over the world. On its shores, you can endlessly relax from the bustle of the city, admire nature, fish or just swim in the clear water.



In the winter season, the surface of the lake freezes quite strongly and then expanse begins for lovers of boating sports. Although it was invented in Holland, it turned out to be an excellent find for Hungary.

There are also many historical sights to visit around Balaton. These include the twin towers of the Benedictine Abbey Tihany, the baroque palace of the Festetics family, the 13th-century Sigligeti fortress, the famous Badaczontomai wine-growing district, and the house-museum of the artist Jozsef Egri, as well as other amazing places.
