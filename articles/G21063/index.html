Cleopatra Island, Turkey - Resort of the Rulers of the World

It is difficult to find a country with the same rich landscapes and beautiful nature as Turkey. Here you can swim in the sea, sunbathe on the beach, climb the mountain, breathe fresh air in the forest. There is nothing surprising in the fact that the territory on which the state is located for many centuries belonged to different peoples, and bloody battles were fought for it. Today, the country hospitably welcomes millions of tourists who want to relax in its open spaces and visit unusual places, such as, for example, the island of Cleopatra.



Turkey at one time was a witness to many historical events. Here they rested, had serious negotiations, made political decisions by many rulers of the world. The legendary Roman commander Marc Anthony also liked this country. The man was in love with the Egyptian beauty Cleopatra, so he decided to give her a gift in the form of a beautiful island. It was impossible to choose the best place for secret dates. In the Gulf of Gokov of the Aegean Sea, near Marmaris, is the island of Cleopatra.

Turkey has many beautiful places, but it is special. Although only ruins are left of the past civilization, a still peaceful atmosphere reigns here. Previously, this place was the ancient city of Kedrai, in the center of which was the temple of Apollo. It was surrounded by thick ramparts, from which now only ruins remained. The best preserved remains of an amphitheater on the side of a mountain. Today, one of the long-awaited entertainment for tourists is an excursion to the island of Cleopatra. Turkey allows every beauty to feel like a queen.



The island is in a very picturesque place, everything is surrounded by greenery, but the most striking thing is the unusual white beach, sparkling in the sun and standing out against the sea. There is no such sand anywhere else in Turkey, so one legend is associated with it. If you believe the legends, when the Egyptian queen arrived in the city of Kedrai presented to her, she was outraged by the quality of the beaches, they seemed too rude to the crowned beauty. To please his beloved, Mark Anthony ordered to bring sand from Egypt to the island of Cleopatra. Turkey has only one place with such a pearl beach, so there is every reason to believe the veracity of the legend. Such sand is found only in North Africa, that is, in the homeland of the queen.

To swim in the azure transparent sea, sunbathe on a snow-white beach and wander among the ruins of the ancient city, tourists come to the island of Cleopatra. Turkey is closely connected with the Egyptian ruler, she often visited here, met with Mark Anthony. Her name is associated with a beach in Alanya, a bath in Pamukkale, and Side was her favorite place for secret meetings with the Roman commander. Of all the nearby resorts, tourists at least for one day, but come to the island of Cleopatra.



Although Turkey occupies an impressive territory on the map, it is almost impossible to get lost in it. You can go on an excursion with a guide, or you can rent a car and visit all the places of interest, get acquainted with the sights.
