Vinogradovsky district, Arkhangelsk region - forests and mystical stories

Arkhangelsk region is one of the largest territorial units of Russia and Europe. The total area is 589,913 square kilometers. The area in area even exceeds the territory of France and Spain. It was founded in 1937, with the administrative center in the city of Arkhangelsk.

The region has 21 districts, 7 cities directly subordinate to the regional authorities, 2 island territories.

Vinogradsky district

The administrative center of the district is Bereznik urban settlement. These territories are unique for their huge forests. The forest industry here is the basis of the entire economy, it accounts for 95% of the total volume of marketable products. According to rough estimates, the cutting area in the region occupies about 980 thousand cubic meters. And these are 40 organizations with different forms of ownership, which employ about 2 thousand people.





Administrative center

The Bereznik of the Vinogradovsky district of the Arkhangelsk region is also called the Dvina Bereznik. For the first time, the village was mentioned in 1676. Status - urban-type settlement - assigned only in 1962. 5,414 people live here.

Last year, the village celebrated its 340th anniversary. Celebrations were held on the territory of the Bereznik park, they were combined with the Semenov festivities. And at the stadium, which is located nearby, there were sports competitions. But traditionally in the park area local masters were located, who showed their skills and creativity in various fields, including potters, whose housewives were bought out without bargaining. The culmination of the holiday was a huge dessert, which consisted of 20 pies with berries.

Geographical and climatic features

Vinogradovsky district of the Arkhangelsk region belongs to territories close to the Far North. The area is located on the Severnaya Dvina River, in the place where Vaga and Vaenga flows into it. The lowest elevation in the area is 7 meters, and the highest is 235.

Weather in Vinogradovsky district of the Arkhangelsk region is characterized as moderate continental. Winters are long and cold, with an average temperature of minus 12 to minus 18 degrees. In the summer season, temperatures can range from +8 to +16 degrees.

Reserve

The territory of the conservation zone of the Vinogradovsky district of the Arkhangelsk region is famous, first of all, for Lake Clonowski. The pond has many keys and springs. A lot of fish are found in it, this is grayling, bream, pike, perch, burbot and roach.

A lot of animals live on the territory of the reserve: beaver, elk, brown hare, wolves and even bears. Swans, many species of wild ducks, hazel grouse, owl, black grouse and capercaillie live in forests and on the shores of the lake.

In the reserve of the Vinogradovsky district of the Arkhangelsk region conifers predominate, they make up about 64.8%. Of these, only 17.1% are young. The average age of pine trees is 108 years.





Lapazhinka

This natural monument was created in 1996. The total occupied area is 68.0 ha. The purpose of the foundation of the complex is the preservation of the natural environment and the study of natural phenomena.

In 1970, it was a tourist base, which was created by the artist N. Sozonov. In 1994, the Bereznikovskaya basic school with an ecological bias was located here. In 2010, the school was disbanded.





Mystic stories

The entire Arkhangelsk region is shrouded in mystical stories, because in the district there are dense forests and swamps. According to the old huntsman, this happened on the border of the Vinograd and Verkhnetoemsky region, where there is a huge swamp called the Great. There are many mushrooms and berries, but there are practically no people in this area. In these places, even the compass malfunctions, you seem to be going in the right direction, and you go a few kilometers from your native places. The huntsman walked only along the path, trying to get to the last hut in his territories, but the fog was thickening. And in an instant the fog disappeared, and around unfamiliar places, somewhere nearby a murmuring stream, located on the territory of another huntsman in the Verkhnetoemsky district. And my grandfather was surprised how it was possible in a few minutes to be 60 kilometers from his forestry.

Many people claim that they saw UFOs near the village of Vorontsy in the Vinogradsky district. Eyewitnesses say they saw unusual flying objects in 1999, 2014 and 2015. And some even personally met aliens!
