Owls Guest House, Dolzhanskaya: overview, features and reviews

Among the rest houses there is one, undoubtedly, worthy of attention - this is the guest house "Owls", which is located in the village of Dolzhanskaya, near the city of Yeysk. Guest House Owls has been operating since July 1, 2015. The owners of Dolzhansky “Owls” are good-natured family people with three children who love to travel. All the best that they find in other cities and countries is used in the organization of their hotel.



Location

Guest House "Owls" (Dolzhanskaya) is perfect for both family vacations and for relaxing with friends or a romantic weekend. To get to the place, it is recommended to use the navigator, driving in the coordinates: 46.646577, 37.807578. The location of the guest house will allow you to relax from the bustle of the city and enjoy nature. The beach is just 500 meters away. You can get to it in 7 minutes with a leisurely step. The beach, as a rule, is not crowded, and the sea will delight in the indescribable beauty of sunset. The Sea of ​​Azov is less salty than the Black Sea, which will serve as an important plus when resting with children.

However, you won’t have to give up the benefits of civilization either, in the village itself you can visit chain grocery stores, banks, post offices, etc., you can reach it in just 5 minutes by car.

What to visit?

Vacationers can also drive to Yeysk to visit many entertainment complexes, a water park, an amusement park, museums, and you can also swim to the uninhabited island of Seven Winds, where tourists watch rare species of birds.

Guest House "Owls" (Dolzhanskaya) is located in the immediate vicinity of Swan Lake, which is famous for its mud treating various joint and dermatological diseases.

5 km from the guest house is the place recommended for visiting tourists - the Spit Dolgaya. This nature reserve is one of a kind, and is also recognized as the best place in Russia for wakeboarding and kitesurfing.

Rooms

The rooms of the Owls house (Dolzhanskaya) are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. Each room offers beautiful views, is equipped with air conditioning and a fan, as well as a fridge, kettle, hairdryer and a modern digital TV.



Large double room has a private entrance, there are barbecue facilities.


The superior quadruple room has a comfortable outdoor dining area.



Also, the Hotel Owls (Dolzhanskaya) offers family rooms and suites. The value for money is perfect. Prices for accommodation here range from 1700 to 5700 rubles per day. In total, the guest house has 14 rooms, so you can be sure that your vacation will not be disturbed by numerous vacationers.

Services

For your comfort, the hotel has a shared lounge, and you can rent a bicycle. Tea and coffee vacationers can get for free and around the clock. The hotel has its own library and laundry facilities. Guest House "Owls" (Dolzhanskaya) is perfect for allergy sufferers, as all rooms are hypoallergenic.





There are special areas for smoking. Play areas for children are available both outdoors and indoors. Near the pool you can comfortably sit in a hammock or on a sunbed.

On site, all guests have the opportunity to use the services of free parking. Also for relaxation is a terrace, a beautiful garden, an outdoor pool, which is heated in the summer and autumn seasons, a bathhouse, a children's playground. It offers - free Wi-Fi.


Reviews

After reading the reviews about the guest house "Owls" (Dolzhanskaya), we can conclude that the rest of the guests fully satisfied. The atmosphere in the hotel can be called emotional. In search of information about Dolzhansky "Owls" reviews about the perfect vacation can be met constantly. The hospitable friendly staff cares about the comfort of the guests, the rooms are beautifully furnished, everything is clean and comfortable.



They love this place very much for families with children, because everything is provided for them here: from books with fairy tales to slides and a house with a kitchen. This means that children will be busy and parents will be able to enjoy swimming in the pool and devote time to their vacation. In addition, the hotel provides high chairs for younger guests.

Often young couples choose Dolzhansky “Owls” for a joint weekend, because the rest in this place can be called truly secluded. Unusually beautiful sunset, picturesque views and clean air create a romantic atmosphere.

Rules of stay

Relaxing in the guest house "Owls" (Dolzhanskaya) is comfortable with children of any age. Kids up to two years of age are accommodated for free. You can also populate one or two children under 6 years old for free. Older children, under 12 years, are accommodated in extra beds for 600 rubles per day. One room can accommodate one extra bed. Children over 12 years old are settled with payment of 1000 rubles per day.

Guest House "Owls" allows guests to relax with their pets, however, you must discuss this point in advance when booking a room in order to avoid misunderstandings. If you are planning a vacation with your pet, you should think over the nuances of his stay in advance, evaluate the possibility of his contact with other guests and their children, take care of his needs, needs and education. The amount of the surcharge for the stay of the animal must be clarified by phone when booking.

Owls House (Dolzhanskaya) allows its guests to invite unregistered guests to the room, but it is worthwhile to understand that the responsibility for the safety of furniture and other room conditions, as well as personal items, lies with them.

Hotel rules prohibit the use of any heating appliances brought with you in rooms. Instead, guests are invited to use the microwave and thermal sweat in the common room.

Check-in is from 14.00, check-out time is 12.00. If for the guest this time is inconvenient for arrival, the guest house is ready to provide a place to relax on the road, shower, tasty food, and then provide a room.

The Dolzhansky Owls themselves recommend visiting their hotel in the fall, because it is at this time of year that you can feel the maximum unity with nature and enjoy the beauty of the area, forgetting about the bustle of the city and its concrete walls.
