Cape Kadosh in Tuapse: description, photos and reviews

Resting is always good, but especially on the Black Sea. Many large and small beaches are scattered along the coast, many of which are fully equipped for recreation, others are less equipped, and there are also quite wild ones. One of these beaches is located on Cape Kadosh, in addition, you can see there unique objects and structures.

Good geographic location

Several million years ago, due to tectonic activity and mother nature, this beautiful and amazing cape Kadosh was formed between the village of Aga and the city of Tuapse. The territory occupied by the cape is approximately 300 hectares, and the coastal strip along the Black Sea extends for 5 kilometers. Nine streams flow through the cape, which then flow into the sea. The subtropical flora growing here is represented by different species of vines, shrubs, thirty species of trees. On the cape you can find very rare flowers that are protected and inscribed in the Red Book.

Where and how the name of the cape appeared is not known for certain; there are various legends and assumptions, but there is no exact answer. Archaeologists, conducting research on its territory, discovered three sites of primitive people. The age of these settlements is approximately 400 thousand years, the traces found and their stone tools are stored in the local museum of the city of Tuapse. Even in those ancient times, people were able to appreciate the indescribable beauty of this place.

Natural phenomena of the cape

This cape is almost not affected by civilization, its pristine nature attracts a large number of tourists and vacationers. Cape Kadosh in Tuapse is considered a natural phenomenon. If you look at it from the Black Sea, it will be impregnable steep rocky coast, overgrown with trees and shrubs. The lack of city bustle, shops and other benefits of civilization is a favorable factor for hiking, while you can admire the almost untouched nature, a diverse marine fauna.

Holidays on Cape Kadosh - an unforgettable adventure that will be remembered for a lifetime. This opinion is shared by many tourists and guests of the Krasnodar Territory in their reviews.

The cape itself is a monument created by nature itself. On its vast territory there is something to see, for example, the rock of Alexander Kiselyov. This name was given to the mountain education in honor of the Wanderer, who loved these places, often painted beautiful landscapes here. You can also visit the house-museum of the famous artist, which is located near this place. There are also legends of countless treasures hidden near the cliff, attracting adventure lovers. And this is not surprising, because the bizarre shape of the cliff has a sheer and very smooth wall. A feature of the rock is the vertical arrangement of sedimentary rocks, although horizontal is standard. The upper part is quite flat, there, among the trees, campers camp, there is a small beach below.

Wild but beautiful rocky beaches

Tired of the bustle of the city, tourists find solitude on the beach of Cape Kadosh in Tuapse. Despite the fact that this place is considered wild, there are some suitable coastal areas for recreation. They may not be as comfortable as city ones, but they have no competitors in terms of beauty. Another advantage of this place is the small number of vacationers. Due to the fact that the bottom is strewn with stones and boulders and has a steep slope, swimming here is not very convenient, but the surrounding landscapes cover all the inconvenience.

Many local nudist lovers find the beaches on Cape Kadosh ideal for this. So if you want to sunbathe in the sun in a secluded place, then keep in mind. In the vicinity of the wild beaches there is a campsite where you can stay "savage."

How to get there

Just four kilometers from the glorious city of Tuapse, is Cape Kadosh. If you wish, you can get to this place on foot, moving towards the village of Agoy, after an hour and a half leisurely walk, admiring the surroundings and breathing fresh sea air, you will reach the place. You can also reach it by car, moving along the central road towards the northwest of Tuapse. If you want to combine a land and sea trip, you can go on a sightseeing boat or boat and at the same time see the steep stone shores of this unique place from the sea.

Those who like to visit different tourist routes with guides in nearby settlements can book a tour. You will be guided to all interesting places, incredible stories will be told, you will also learn fantastic legends and various fables.

In the end, you can get to Kadosh by public transport. Drive to the SRZ stop (shipyard) located at 1 Frunze Street.

Shelter for vacationers

Near the Black Sea in the village of Agoy there is a small but very cozy hotel "Villa" Cape Kadosh ", from the picturesque cliff it is separated by some 200 meters. All that is needed for a good stay, the hotel has. Here you can also enjoy delicious cuisine, which includes homemade dishes, if you wish, you can cook yourself. You can eat on the cozy terrace, take a walk in the garden in the evening, while breathing in the fresh sea air. Guests can use free Wi-Fi, a bathroom (in each room), laundry services, order groceries in the room. For lovers of fishing, there is such an opportunity. If you arrived by your own car, it does not matter, there is free parking, in which the car will be supervised. According to vacationers, the windows of the rooms overlook the vast sea and beautiful mountains.
