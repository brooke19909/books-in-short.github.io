Interest on balance

Let's continue the debit card comparison. Some of them involve the calculation of interest on the cash balance.

This option allows you to get additional earnings in cases when there is a certain amount on the card balance at the end of the period indicated by the financial institution. According to the terms of user agreements, the bank accrues interest from the amount available on customer accounts. Such organizations use different accrual schemes. In the vast majority of examples, they are provided only in cases where, during a certain period of time, the holders spend the amount that was set by the bank in the form of a minimum.

For example, on the card balance by the end of the reporting period there are fifty thousand rubles, which are recognized as the balance on the card. The conditional financial institution will accrue the agreed interest for this amount if the user has spent thirty thousand over the past thirty-day period. If such a requirement is not met, then, accordingly, no interest is accrued.

When comparing debit cards, one more nuance must be taken into account.

Balance limit

A very common condition is the restriction on the amount of the balance for calculating interest. Banks set the maximum threshold at which accruals are made. Amounts over the set thresholds will not be taken into account in the calculations.

It is worth giving an example: the bank has set a maximum threshold for interest accruals in the form of one hundred thousand rubles, provided that during the billing period purchases were made on the card for at least half of the indicated amount. At the same time, when, for example, there are one hundred seventy thousand on the balance sheet, the surplus will not be taken into account. What does a debit card require?

The costs are usually as follows:
