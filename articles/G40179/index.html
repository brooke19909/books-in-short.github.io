How to sew a knight costume for a boy with your own hands?

Knights are associated with brave and brave warriors who are ready to help at any time. These brave guys are dressed in iron armor or chain mail, ride a horse and always have a sword at hand. So why not choose a suit of a knight for a boy, with his own hands to make which will not be difficult?





Costume Components

There are a lot of options for a knight's costume , so that everyone can choose something special for themselves. But the main details of the costume should be traced in any of the options. We suggest you familiarize yourself with this list:

  • helmet;
  • shield;
  • sword;
  • cloak or cape;
  • boots.

This list is not mandatory, but if possible we recommend that you follow it. Of course, in our time you can buy a ready-made full set of costume, but if you are not interested in such an easy way, then we will tell you how to sew a knight costume for a boy with your own hands. Now consider each part of the image separately.

Helmet

This knitwear was worn by all knights, so we recommend that you start making a costume with it. In the original, the helmet is made of iron and completely covers the face, but for our children's costume you can replace this option.

The hood cape can be sewn on your own at home. It can look, as shown in the figure:





As you can see, there is nothing complicated in tailoring, the main thing is to calculate with a cutout for the face, for this we recommend taking measurements from the child.

A good alternative to this cloak is a cardboard helmet crown. A helmet with vertical slots in the middle is cut out of ordinary cardboard, which is glued to the back or fixed with a stapler. The color of the cardboard is chosen to suit the color of the suit, or you can independently paint it in the desired shade.

If the image of a knight allows you to use a cape instead of a helmet, then you can craft it yourself. All from the same cardboard, the main rule is to measure the volume of the child‚Äôs head so that you do not have to redo it.

Shield

All sketches of the knight's costumes are depicted with a protective shield. You can make it from improvised materials that can be found in every home.





We offer to make a protective shield from ordinary brown cardboard, which is used for packaging goods. To do this, we need an even piece of cardboard, scissors or a clerical knife and paint.

We cut out the base of the shield from cardboard, note that its dimensions must be such that it is possible to completely cover the body. Next, we start painting our base. To do this, choose a neutral color or the main colors of the suit. You can depict a coat of arms on a shield.

Now we pass to the stage of creating a handle for the shield so that the child can easily carry it with him. To do this, on the reverse side we fix a strip of cardboard in a horizontal position. This can be done with glue or a furniture stapler.

Sword

Any self-respecting knight carries a sword with him, and is ready at any time to defend his honor. Therefore, the sword is the main attribute that must necessarily contain a knight costume for the boy. With our own hands it will not be difficult to make it and now we will tell you a few secrets.

The easiest option is to cut the sword out of cardboard in the same way as we cut the protective shield. The length of the sword is selected in accordance with the growth of the child, but should not be less than the length of the shield.

On a flat cardboard, draw a outline of the sword with a ruler and cut it out. After that, you can start decorating. This refers to painting a cardboard blank. Choose gray or light gray paint, and the handle in a contrasting shade.

For the convenience of the child, you can add a hip belt, on which you can hang the sword. After all, the suit for the child ‚ÄúKnight‚Äù has other attributes that must be worn in the hands, and tucking the sword in the belt, it will be easier for the baby to move.

clothing

The main part of the costume is, of course, outerwear, which can consist of several parts.

Pants choose a neutral black color that does not need to be additionally decorated.

But for the top, you can choose several options. If you pay attention to what the costume of the dark knight looks like, you can notice the cloak:





It is this option that is most often used for a knight costume. The cloak is easy to cut, because in shape it resembles a semicircle. It is only necessary to sew on the ties and overcast the edges of the cloak. If you pay attention to the sketches of the costumes that are presented in the article, you can note one feature. This is the presence of a coat of arms on outer clothing.

To do this, you must separately sew the coat of arms, and then fix it on a jacket or T-shirt. But you can simplify this process if you use a special paint for the fabric. On a light t-shirt with a brush, draw the desired image and let it dry.

As you can see, making a knight‚Äôs costume for a boy with his own hands is not difficult to do. The main thing is to show imagination and use your resources to the maximum. The clothing options described above can be changed and combined depending on your capabilities.

Footwear

Well, here we come to the final part of our article. How to sew a knight‚Äôs costume is now clear to everyone, it remains only to solve the issue of shoes.

Under the suit of a knight, you need to pick up dark high boots. But if you do not have such shoes, then you can replace it.

Choose any footwear with a low speed and in tone to it leggings or socks. This combination will visually highlight the shoes on the baby‚Äôs foot, creating the effect of high boots. And once again I would like to advise: do not be afraid of experiments and the implementation of new ideas.
