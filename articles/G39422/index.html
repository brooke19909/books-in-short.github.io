How to make a peacock costume with your own hands

For holidays and masquerades, girls of all ages like to dress up in costumes. Moreover, the images of the fair sex choose bright. For example, a peacock costume is very popular. How to do it, read below.

Dancer costume



To perform at a school evening or corporate party, girls often sew stunning stage dresses for themselves. The peacock costume turns out to be very effective and bright. How to create it?

The costume is based on a purple or lilac swimsuit. It can be both separate and continuous. You can pick up long gloves to match it. But the spectacular skirt will need to be sewn. In order for the dress to be easy to move, it must be free. Therefore, we cut the skirt from the turquoise fabric. To it we sew the peacock "tail". If you find a suitable fabric with a pattern of bright feathers in the store, consider yourself lucky. But if nothing like this fails, then the pattern will have to be applied to the material manually. To do this, you need paint on the fabric. Using the contour, we draw the outlines of the feathers, and then fill them with colored pigments. Sew the ‚Äútail‚Äù to the skirt, and the suit is ready. To make the image even more interesting, you can insert a peacock feather into the hairstyle .

Costume in kindergarten





Girls often try on the images of various birds. Therefore, the New Year's peacock costume is used in kindergartens annually. How to make it? It should be based on a swimsuit of blue or purple. If there is no such house, you can use a turtleneck or t-shirt. The top of these clothes should be made festive. We embroider with rhinestones or large beads the neck of a swimsuit, turtleneck or t-shirt.

Now we turn to the manufacture of the bottom. To sew a skirt, tulle of four colors is required. We cut the fabric into rectangles and string them onto an elastic band. Thus, it will be possible to create the first layer of the skirt. This will be the basis. According to the same scheme, it is necessary to make a petticoat, to which 10-15 cuts of long tulle should be attached. They will symbolize the tail. The peacock costume is ready. It remains to make a crown of feathers. Sew a cardboard triangle to a wide elastic band. We decorate the blank with rhinestones. And then 5-7 colored feathers should be glued to the back of the crown.

The image for the matinee



A peacock costume for a girl can be made in just a few hours. Its base will be blue or purple jacket. It is desirable that it be decorated with sequins. The second component of a peacock costume is a skirt. It should be made from tulle. Rectangles of several bright colors are strung on an elastic band. To make a colored skirt look like a peacock tail, feather outlines should be made from felt. We make blanks. They will need at least 20 pieces. Cut an oval from brown fabric, and circles from green, blue and blue. Now glue the parts in layers to each other, and then sew the blanks to the skirt.

Stylized peacock



The image of an elegant bird for a little princess can be made for a small one of tulle and feathers. As in other variations, a peacock costume for a girl will be created from a fluffy tutu skirt. We do it in the same way as in any of the previous paragraphs, but not only tulle, but also peacock feathers need to be sewn to the elastic. Where to get them? You can buy colored feathers in any store for creativity.

The skirt should be worn either on a swimsuit or on a bright body. The baby should feel comfortable in the suit. You can complement the image with face painting, which should decorate the face and hands. From shoes it is better to prefer bright shoes or rag ballerinas.
