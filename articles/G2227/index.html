Blue Bay Beach Hotel 4 * (Greece, Rhodes): hotel description, services, reviews

On the seashore is the luxurious Blue Bay Beach Hotel. This institution was opened in 1978. Thanks to the repairs carried out in 2011, the hotel has acquired a modern look and new equipment.





Location

Blue Bay Beach Hotel is 2 km from the popular resort town of Ialyssos. Rhodes, Ialyssos Beach, 85101 - The address where tourists can find this place. The hotel is very close to the beach. It is 10 km from the center of Rhodes, and 5 km from the international airport. There is a public transport stop just 50 meters from the hotel.

Accommodation

Comfortable accommodation is provided by the Blue Bay Beach Hotel. Rooms of various categories will be offered to tourists for accommodation. The following options will be available to you:

  • Standard Room is a one-room room, which is characterized by a compact area and laconic interior. Guests can stay in a large or a pair of twin beds. There is also a work area and a private furnished balcony. It is worth noting that guests can book connecting rooms of the Standard Room category.
  • Superior is an improved room category. The increased area, in addition to the bedroom, also made it possible to equip a cozy sitting area with upholstered furniture and a coffee table. There is a dressing table with a large mirror.
  • Family rooms accommodate 4 people (2 adults and 2 children). The room has a pair of one and a half beds (can be shifted), as well as a bunk bed for children. There is a cozy dining area and a large furnished balcony.
  • Suite is the best accommodation option at this hotel. The room consists of two rooms. The bedroom, decorated in warm colors, contributes to a good rest. The living room is bright and airy. There is upholstered furniture, which when unfolded is used to accommodate additional guests.

Negative reviews

Such a resort as the Blue Bay Beach Hotel (Ialyssos) is not without its drawbacks. 4 stars of this institution should not dazzle tourists with their brilliance. Tourists should be prepared for such negative moments:

  • in the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are practically no infrastructure facilities and entertainment facilities;
  • TVs in the rooms are quite old, due to which the image may be of poor quality;
  • a strong smell of bleach is felt in the pool;
  • on the terrace by the pool there are few enough sun loungers, and therefore they have to be occupied from the very morning (before breakfast);
  • standard rooms are too cramped to accommodate 4 people (not to mention extra beds);
  • old mattresses with protruding springs that cause serious discomfort during sleep;
  • in the dining room there is a constant lack of glasses;
  • the beach closest to the hotel is strewn with a lot of sharp pebbles;
  • for sunbeds for relaxing by the sea you need to pay 3 euros per day;
  • in the dining room you can often see dirty plates and glasses;
  • all animation in the hotel in English (including children);
  • if there are any problems in the room, employees are in no hurry to fix them, no matter how much you ask them about it;
  • special requests regarding the format of the beds (large or twin), as well as regarding the view from the window, are almost never taken into account;
  • in spite of the fact that a hairdryer is stated in the bathroom, this option is absent in some rooms (apparently the old one broke down, but they did not take care of the new one);
  • Guests are not provided with bottled drinking water.

If you want to relax with high quality and relatively inexpensive, you should pay attention to this institution. Despite the outdated interior and other shortcomings, it leaves a fairly pleasant impression.
