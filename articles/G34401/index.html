How to make a diary from Gravity Falls? Gravity Falls diary box: step-by-step instructions

Gravity Falls is an American animated series created by Alex Hirsch. It tells of the twins, Dipper and Mabel, who spend their holidays visiting Stan's cousin. He owns a Miracle Shack gift shop in the small town of Gravity Falls in Oregon. The guys will find out that something strange is happening in these places. In the first season, Dipper and Mabel find diary number 3. It contains various notes about mysterious creatures and supernatural phenomena occurring in Gravity Falls.





Diary No. 3, along with diaries No. 1 and No. 2, was written long before the twins arrived in Gravity Falls. Ford Pines, Uncle Stan's brother, wrote down a list of all the paranormal phenomena and strange creatures that inhabit the city.

If you want to learn how to make a diary from Gravity Falls, photos with step-by-step instructions will help you catch the general idea. The main thing is to show maximum imagination and a little patience for such an exciting activity.

DIY Supplies

To learn how to make a diary from Gravity Falls, you should prepare a few things. Here they are:

  • An empty box in the shape of a book.
  • Dark violet paint.
  • Black paint.
  • Masking tape.
  • Postcards and other paraphernalia from the animated series.

To keep your diary box empty, print or draw the pictures themselves with cartoon characters, locations, or anything else. You can also go to the official website of "Gravity Falls" and independently create a wide variety of postcards.
