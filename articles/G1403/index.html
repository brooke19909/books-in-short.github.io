Knitting for children with knitting needles: the most interesting (description with photo)

Small children, especially from 0 to 3 years old, knit clothes best with knitting needles. Knitted fabric, softer, more tender. The kid in such clothes will be comfortable and cozy. Experienced knitters will tell you this. Knitting for children with knitting needles is the most interesting activity for mothers, grandmothers, and older sisters. It has been tested for centuries. Tips for choosing

Knitting threads for children with knitting needles (the most interesting - there are a lot of them) would be nice to pick up in the store so that you can touch the yarn with your hands, hold it, twitch it, set fire to the tip of the thread with matches. Natural ones will burn without residue, and non-natural ones will melt. The finer the thread of yarn, the softer, more elastic the finished thing. You need to look at the composition of the yarn.

It is better to focus on the natural composition of the yarn, so that knitted clothes are breathable, and the child is as comfortable as possible in it. It is worth remembering that yarn from different manufacturers with the same composition can vary significantly by touch.

The main types of yarn

There is acrylic, woolen, half-woolen, cotton.

Acrylic yarn is one of the most common for children knitting. The most interesting and pleasant thing is that it is inexpensive, soft, durable. But acrylic yarn can stretch after washing, spools will form in the product. Again, acrylic is an unnatural component. When you need to choose acrylic yarn, you should pull the thread. If it stretches, then the product after washing can also stretch.





You can choose a mixed yarn - acrylic with wool, acrylic with a cotton additive. For warm winter models, it is better to pay attention to wool and half-woolen yarn. Now you can find soft merino yarn. She does not prick, pleasant to the touch. Cotton is suitable for summer.

On sale there is a specialized children's yarn. But preference should be given to a material with a natural composition. You can not completely trust the manufacturer. It is more worth trusting yourself, your experience and customer reviews, experienced knitters.
