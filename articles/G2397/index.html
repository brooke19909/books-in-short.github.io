How to install ICQ on the phone

ICQ (ICQ) for the mobile phone today is the most common application. And this popularity is easily explained. First of all, ICQ gives its owner a great opportunity to use the Internet messenger without a computer or laptop. Let's find out how to install ICQ on a Chinese phone or any other, to chat with friends, acquaintances and colleagues online.

Below is a detailed and very convenient step-by-step instruction.

Step 1. How to install ICQ on your phone: configure the Internet

In order to get mobile Internet settings on your phone, call your mobile operator or find out on its official website. As a rule, the operator will immediately send you Wap-Internet, MMS, GPRS-Internet settings in the message. Choose the latter and configure your mobile device for this particular work profile.
