Description

In the world, and especially in Russia, there are a lot of people who are big fans of soaring. That is why in almost every city in our country there are baths and bath complexes, which are ready to offer connoisseurs of bath procedures to experience all the advantages of hot steam. Nizhny Novgorod is no exception. For many Nizhny Novgorod residents, the Bagira sauna in Avtozavodsky District has become one of their favorite vacation spots, where you can take a good steam and relax. Here, customers will enjoy magnificent Finnish-style saunas, spacious pools with geysers, lights and waterfalls.

In total, the complex has five saunas with a capacity of two to eight people. Each room is made in its own individual style. For a romantic date, smaller and more comfortable rooms are suitable, and for relaxation as a friendly company, spacious halls with a large pool will become more suitable.





First hall

Provides for the placement of a company of eight people.

At the guests' disposal are a steam room with a broom, a shower room, a deep 12-meter heated pool, a geyser, a waterfall and a backlight. The pool is well suited for playing water volleyball. The room has a comfortable living room with a pool table, cable TV, karaoke, darts and the necessary furniture. There is also a separate room with a large bed.

The cost of room No. 1 in the Bagira sauna (Nizhny Novgorod):

  • From 9 a.m. to 4 p.m. - 450 rubles per hour.
  • From 4 pm to 9 am - 900 rubles an hour.




Second hall

Designed for six people. In the hall there is a steam room, a 3 by 4 meter pool with lighting, a geyser and a waterfall, a shower, a relaxation room.

The living room has billiards, darts, musical equipment with karaoke.

Accommodation price:

  • From 9.00 to 16.00 - 400 rubles / hour.
  • From 16.00 to 9.00 - 800 rubles / hour.

The third and fourth hall

Able to host a company of four. In the halls there is everything the same as in room 2, only billiards and a waterfall are missing.

Cost:

  • The first half of the day - 350 rubles.
  • Afternoon - 700 rubles.

Fifth hall

This number is for two people. The room has an infrared sauna, a shower, cable TV and music.

The price per hour of stay in the room is 250 and 500 rubles until 4 pm and until 9 am, respectively.
