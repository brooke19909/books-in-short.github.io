Hotel Dong Phuong 2 Hotel 3 *: description, reviews. Nha Trang beaches

Although Vietnam is not considered the most popular and desired tourist destination for residents of the Russian Federation, nevertheless, annually many of our compatriots choose the coast of this wonderful eastern country as a place to spend their holidays. In addition to a lazy beach holiday, tourists are waiting for exciting excursions, the possibility of cheap diving, beautiful nature and friendly locals. In addition, there are quality hotels with good service and low prices. For example, a three-star hotel Dong Phuong 2 Hotel 3 *.



The best resort of Vietnam

Nha Trang is the most popular of the sea cities of the country. It is also called the beach capital of Vietnam. The best hotel complexes are concentrated here, as well as quite comfortable budget hotels such as Dong Phuong 2 Hotel. Nha Trang is a noisy and fun resort of an almost European type (after all, Vietnam is a former French colony) with a superbly developed infrastructure. Those who prefer to spend their holidays in this city will surely like amazing landscapes: water of a gentle azure shade, snow-white sandy beaches, hilly emerald islands, like a necklace lined up along the bay.

Others are attracted by the vibrant nightlife full of entertainment, as well as restaurants with original cuisine. For lovers of sybaritic pleasures, there are upscale spa centers with many treatments. By the way, fans of excursions will also not be bored. Almost in the very center of the city are the ancient Cham towers, as well as the ruins of other historical buildings. Many of the city‚Äôs hotels, including even three-star ones, for example, Dong Phuong 2 Hotel 3 *, offer tourists interesting tourist routes for a fee.

Climatic conditions

In Nha Trang, the weather is comfortable all year round. Throughout the year, it is almost always warm and sunny, even in the rainy season, when powerful torrential rainfalls hit the city for only a couple of hours. Since during this period the sea begins to rage constantly, then most of the vacationers are windsurfers. Others are attracted by low prices, as well as the opportunity to watch the competitions of brave athletes - conquerors of waves, which are full of all the beaches of Nha Trang. Sometimes there is a typhoon on the coast. However, this is a rather rare occurrence.

How to get there?

If you want a budget holiday in Vietnam, then buy tours at the Dong Phuong 2 Hotel. In most cases, the package includes the cost of air travel. You will have to fly from Moscow or from one of the Russian cities, from where flights are made to major Vietnamese cities, for example, Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi, Hoi An, etc. Then you need to go to the resort by train or bus.

It is divided into twenty-eight communal areas, each of which, in turn, forms three sections: northern, southern and central. A favorite place for those who like to hang out is the last option. The southern part is more suitable for fans of a quiet holiday. Quality budget hotels such as Dong Phuong 2 Hotel are also located here. In the northern section are the sights of the city. There are almost no hotels.



Nha Trang beaches

This beautiful resort is located on the shore of one of the most beautiful bays in the world (29th place in the ranking). The beaches here are snow-white, well-groomed, with a rich infrastructure. They are equipped with all the necessary equipment. Their total length is 7 kilometers. On the first beach line there are not only extra-class hotels, but also modest budget hotels, which at one time were considered the best in the city, and today they have lost ground to new giant complexes equipped with the latest technology.

Hotel Dong Phuong 2 is considered one of the best among them. Here you will find the perfect value for money. By the way, rarely does a hotel have its own beach. All of them are almost municipal, which means that entry here is free. On the other hand, beach equipment is available for a fee.

"Dong Phong 2", 3 * hotel: location

This magnificent hotel is located in Lok Tho, near the city center on the first beach line. The road from the airport to it takes only 40 minutes. Near the hotel there is a water park "Phu Dong", as well as a diving club, large shopping centers, a market, as well as the old Chan Tower - one of the main attractions. Opposite the hotel is Phu Dong Park. Dong Phuong 2 Hotel is the starting point for most sightseeing routes, not only in Nha Trang, but throughout the country as a whole. By the way, at 6 kilometers is the mineral spring Thap Ba with hot water.



Number of rooms

The hotel has 120 comfortable rooms in the following categories: standard (22 sq. Meters, 2 single beds and one double), deluxe (32 sq. Meters, the number of beds is the same as in the previous apartments). There is also a "presidential suite." It occupies 34 square meters. meter. There is one double bed, a bathroom with a jacuzzi. All rooms are fully equipped with comfortable furniture. Each room has either a balcony or a terrace with outdoor furniture and with beautiful views of the sea.

For the complete comfort of guests, all rooms are equipped with audio and video equipment, satellite TV, minibar, air conditioning. Recent devices allow you not to feel the tropical heat. There is also wireless and also free internet, a bathroom with shower, hairdryer and all the necessary toiletries. There is no left-luggage office. However, if necessary, you can use a special cell at the reception. Housekeeping is daily.



Nutrition

The room rate, which is approximately 2000 rubles per night, includes only a buffet breakfast. It is served in a buffet. The restaurant, located on the ground floor of the hotel, offers European, Asian, including Vietnamese, cuisines. Dong Phuong 2 Hotel also has a lobby bar where you can enjoy a variety of hot and cold drinks.

Services

The hotel has a spacious (72 sq. Meters), equipped by modern standards conference room and a business center for various events. It is located on the 8th floor, in the same place as the pool with incredibly beautiful sea views. There is a solarium - a platform with sunbeds for sunbathing, and a pool bar. The hotel offers guests - parking, currency exchange, concierge service, tour desk, laundry and dry cleaning, bike rental, etc. Check-in at 14:00 local time, and check-out at 12 noon.



Entertainment

If you are staying in Nha Trang, then you definitely need to do the following:

  • Go to the gardens of Bao Dai and watch the confluence of the East with the West.
  • Visit the ancient tower of Po Nagar.
  • Take wellness treatments in the mud bath located in the suburbs of the resort.
  • Travel back to the pirate era by climbing onto the deck of an old ship, which also houses the oceanarium.
  • Take a ride in a gigantic amusement park on the cable car.
  • Cross to Hong Lao island and feed deer and monkeys.




Dong Phuong 2 Hotel: guest reviews and ratings

So you got acquainted with the location and internal structure of this three-star hotel. It is located in the best of the Vietnamese resorts - Nha Trang, on the first beach line. By the way, this is what, according to reviews of hotel guests, is its main advantage. The location is just perfect. And those tourists who are not indifferent to sea landscapes can almost admire either the daytime or the night sea almost around the clock.

As for the reviews regarding the service, unfortunately, they are not always positive. Guests complain about the kitchen. Perhaps, due to lack of habit, many dishes seem too spicy to them, and the smell of some Vietnamese delicacies, to put it mildly, discourages the appetite. There are also complaints about cleaning, which, although carried out regularly, but superficially. Nevertheless, this hotel has many advantages, including the availability of prices, thanks to which it is almost never empty.
