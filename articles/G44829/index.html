Save on lighting. Lamps LED "Philips"

Every year, utility tariffs, including for electricity, are growing all the time. But progress does not stand still and offers us solutions that save on payment for light.

A more advantageous alternative to the incandescent lamps that are familiar to us and the so-called "energy-saving" fluorescent are LED lamps.

Lamps LED "Philips"

Philips company is one of the most famous. For more than a century, it has been producing various electrical goods, including electric lighting systems. That is why Philips LED lamps are a reliable component of home lighting. Indeed, such products will work without replacement up to 15,000 hours!

The company offers various Philips LED lamps for:

  • shape (teardrop, slot, svezhevidnaya, spherical, spiral);
  • various degrees of light output;
  • brightness adjustment options;
  • color temperature (degree of cold / warmth of light);
  • base type (E14, E27, GU 5.3).

What are the benefits of semiconductor lamps?

Philips LED lamps for the home are one of the most optimal and affordable options. The main advantages can be called longevity and efficiency. Such lighting spends 90 percent less energy than obsolete incandescent lamps, and the life of LEDs is ten times longer than theirs. Of course, the cost of LED bulbs is higher than usual, but this difference is compensated during operation. After all, during the operation of an LED lamp, you can change a dozen incandescent lamps.



The Philips LED lamp housing consists of aluminum and plastic parts, so breaking this device is very difficult. During operation, very little heat is generated. Working in AC power networks, these devices do not make noise or flicker. On sale are lamps ranging from cold to warm type of lighting. It is possible to choose products with a specific focus of lighting and the ability to adjust brightness.

Are there any disadvantages?

In addition to the relatively high cost of the lamp, Philips LEDs also have some other disadvantages. One of them is not ideal light transmission. This is manifested in a certain change in the color of the illuminated objects. Sometimes this can cause slight visual discomfort. In addition, the combination of lighting with lamps of different types is undesirable.
