The best 5 star Cyprus hotels - reviews

Everyone wants to spend their holidays in Cyprus. The blue sea, the hot sun, the absence of all conceivable problems is the dream of each of us. You can travel to the fairy world without having a magic wand. You just need to board the plane and land on the islands of paradise. Resting without problems is not as easy as it seems. It is necessary to take care of a lot in advance. The most important item will be housing. To feel like an important person, choose 5 star Cyprus hotels.



Capital Coast Resort & Spa Hotel 5 *

Located in Paphos, next to the Lighthouse and Necropolis. Nearby are the Paphos Architectural Complex and the city port. The hotel provides exclusive rooms equipped with the latest technology. The hotel's spa features massage services, a jacuzzi, and a sauna. Pets are not allowed. Room rates include breakfast. The restaurant also serves lunch and dinner at an additional cost. Guests leave admiring hotel reviews. None of them regrets for a minute that he came to rest in Cyprus. Paphos, 5-star hotels from which they chose Capital Coast Resort & Spa 5 *, gave them unforgettable impressions and moments that you want to experience again and again.

Rooms

The best 5-star Cyprus hotels provide guests with excellent rooms that have everything (and even more) that they need for relaxation. Guests enjoy the fact that the rooms have a telephone, refrigerator, microwave, safe, household appliances, CD player, TV, iron, ironing board, hairdryer. According to hotel guests, it is very convenient that the rooms have small kitchens, dishes, cutlery, necessary kitchen utensils. You can cook your own food. The bathroom includes towels, bathrobes, slippers, toiletries. Room doors are locked using magnetic or electronic keys.

Infrastructure

It offers all the amenities that only 5-star Cyprus hotels can provide: an outdoor pool, bars, a restaurant, dry cleaning, laundry, currency exchange, ironing, car rental, fax, transfer. The hotel organizes excursions and wedding ceremonies. You can spend time with benefit on the tennis court, in the fitness center, sauna, pool.

Tourists leave a lot of positive reviews about the spa, bathhouse. Travelers love the way fishing and snorkeling are organized.

Sant Rafael Hotel 5 *

The hotel building is located on the sea coast in a quiet, beautiful park. It covers an area of ​​43,000 square meters. Excellent service is provided by well-trained staff. The guests here are treated very carefully. In their reviews, they write that they have not met a friendlier team. Around the hotel is a large park. Bird singing, clean air, beautiful landscapes will help to relax and tune in to rest. Guests like to go there before bedtime. 5 star Cyprus hotels provide their guests with comfortable rooms. It has a telephone, TV, hairdryer, electric kettle, minibar, air conditioning, radio, coffee and tea making facilities. The hotel provides towels, slippers, bathrobes, toiletries. In the reviews of the hotel they write that the bedding is very beautiful, it is often changed. The same can be said about towels and white robes. There is no need to think about how to spend time. To do this, there are outdoor and indoor pools, bars, a hairdresser, a restaurant, a shopping center, a business center, a conference room, and a sauna. Children really like the playground. In addition, babysitting services are available if necessary.



Amalthia Villas Hotel 5 *

The exclusive hotel complex includes 14 villas. They are located on the south coast of the island of Cyprus. 5-star hotels (only a small part of their splendor can display photos) offer guests a huge range of services. There are indoor and outdoor pools, a beautiful garden, a barbecue area, restaurants. There is a pool near each villa. The rooms have a safe, air conditioning, telephone, modern TV, bathroom, balcony. Bedding is changed several times a week and at the request of guests. Housekeeping is provided daily. Nearby is the Akamas Nature Reserve. Tourists say that this is a very beautiful place where it is very nice to walk and just spend time. The hotel has a sauna, jacuzzi, volleyball, darts, gym, tennis courts, a mini club. It helps a lot to families with children that there is a big playroom. For many, it became a salvation on cloudy days when you can not go to the beach.

Grecian Park Hotel 5 *

Hotels of Cyprus "5 stars", which are located on the golden beach of Protaras, provide their guests the opportunity to bask in the sun for as long as they like. Hotel Grecian Park 5 * is located close to the city center. Nearby is the National Park. The rooms have everything you need for a comfortable stay: air conditioning, radio, heating, minibar, safe, telephone, TV, balcony, bathroom. For the convenience of guests there are cafes, bars, a swimming pool, a jacuzzi, a children's playground, a disco, a nightclub, parking, a hairdresser, a restaurant, a shopping center, and a sauna.

Hotel guests really like the fact that you can rent a bicycle at the hotel, take advantage of babysitting services, play billiards, and basketball. Here you can ride a horse, do various sports, work out in the gym. In addition, entertainment shows, contests, and animation programs are held daily. Tourists describe all these moments in their reviews with enthusiasm.

Evalena Beach Hotel 5 *

The hotel’s luxurious apartments are located by the bay of Smokovnica, on a sandy beach in Protaras. The city center is about 200 meters away. Nearby are restaurants, taverns, shops, discos and banks - all the amenities that Cyprus offers. 5-star hotels (guest reviews confirm this) occupy the most advantageous places. Guests write in reviews that it’s much more interesting and fun to relax when there are many interesting places nearby. The hotel also does not cease to amaze. It regularly hosts theme parties with barbecue and folklore shows. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, TV, telephone, refrigerator. There is a small kitchen.
