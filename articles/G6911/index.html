The best place in the Republic of Adygea - Guzeripl village: recreation, recreation, services

Near the Caucasus Range is the village of Guzeripl. Holidays in this area are loved by many residents of the republic. It is located at an altitude of 670 meters above sea level. These beautiful places attract tourists. If you wish, you can go along the route to the Black Sea. Travel promises to be quite interesting and unforgettable. At the moment, the village is predicting the future of one of the most famous places in Adygea.

Description of the village

Guzeripl village (rest here is in high demand) is located in an ecologically clean area. Large areas planted with fir and oaks make this area not only picturesque, but also unique. Climatic features: summer temperature is +18 ¬∞ , while winter temperature is -5 ... -7 ¬∞ . Thanks to the microclimate in this area, the air has a healing effect.

If we consider the etymology of the name, then from Adyghe it is translated as "road". Among all other cities and towns, this is the most panoramic. From here you can see the maximum number of main natural attractions of the republic.

For tourists, a museum is located on the territory of the reserve. There are unearthly beauty representatives of flora and fauna. Not far from here you can find the largest dolmen located on the territory of Adygea.

Guzeripl, a vacation in which is as complete as possible due to a large list of services, is suitable for lovers of active pastime. Rafting competitions are constantly held here. As a rule, they take place at the beginning of the last spring month. All who have registered for participation carry out movement along the water artery by canoe, catamaran, raft or kayak. In parallel with these entertainments, a song festival is held.



Enektur

It is necessary to consider places where you can settle during a trip to Guzeripl. Recreation facilities are a budget option. One of these institutions can be called "Enektur." The complex is located in the forest, consists of three buildings. It accepts at the same time no more than 50 people. Guests can stay in suites, economy or standard rooms.

Vacationers at their disposal for a fee have the opportunity to go to the bathhouse, pool, play tennis (table / large), billiards. If desired, it is allowed to cook kebabs. All fixtures can be rented.

On the ground floor there is a hall, a banquet hall (maximum 60 people) is on the second. The conference room has a capacity of 50 seats. Nearby there is a bar, cafe.

The complex, which is located in coniferous forests, attracts everyone who came to Guzeripl. Rest will be remembered for walks in interesting places. They are organized by this database. If you wish, then for an additional fee you can go hiking to historical monuments, accompanied by guides.



Chalet Voyage

The complex is located in the reserve. In the guest house, the administration always welcomes every visitor with a smile. Coziness, harmony reign in the building, visitors feel real comfort. Cleanliness is given great attention. All rooms are cleaned.

The administration understands, she tries to do everything so that the guests are satisfied and return here again. Only 10 rooms, in which places for 25 people. Of the categories there is a standard and a suite. Each room has large beds with orthopedic mattresses.

Everyone wants to get into a great place to stay when they come to Guzeripl. The recreation centers of this village are warmly welcomed and remembered by the guests. Each Chalet Voyage building has sofas, TVs, and fireplaces. Equipped kitchen, which has all the necessary accessories. The complex has gazebos, a bathhouse and barbecue facilities. Parking is also provided.



"Eden"

"Eden" is located on the border of the reserve, which is located in the village of Guzeripl. Hotels, recreation centers - the most budget options for spending holidays. Even the most capricious visitors will be able to positively evaluate the quality of service and comfort in this complex.

On the territory of "Eden" are three cottages. Each room has been renovated, the interior is modern, the furniture is commendable. In addition to the bath, there is a pool and a dining room. On the border with the forest there are gazebos. Parking and cafes are available.
