Entertainment in Tolyatti: description, addresses

Entertainment in Togliatti is quite diverse. Residents and guests of the city can choose suitable for their preferences and financial opportunities. Especially popular in the city are amusement parks and all kinds of quests. Here you can have fun with friends or family.

"Fanny"

This amusement park in Togliatti has a rich history. It began its existence back in 1980. For all the time of work, more than once reconstruction was carried out here. Now it has a modern look and updated attractions and is located on the street. Frunze, 16a.

The territory has more than 50 different swings. There are attractions for the youngest visitors, as well as for adults, which cause the release of a large amount of adrenaline.

The park has a pneumatic shooting range, a race track and a variety of children's labyrinths. Here, the guys are sure to ride on their beloved carousel, "Daisy" and trains. Older children will not be able to get past the Roller Coaster.

In the warm season, a small open-air water park operates in the territory of the amusement park in Tolyatti. The attraction is very popular - in large inflatable balls, swimming around the pool.

Other entertainments

In the park "Fanny" not so long ago opened a contact zoo. It contains a variety of species of small animals that can be fed and cared for.

Comfortable benches in the shade are installed throughout. And also practically at every step there are trading shops where you can have a tasty snack. There are also stationary cafes with a varied menu in the park.

Outdoor enthusiasts can have fun at special venues designed for roller skating, skateboarding and biking. Throughout the territory, modern playgrounds are installed.

In winter, an amusement park in Togliatti also functions. A large skating rink is poured here and other entertainments are arranged.

Quests in Togliatti

This type of entertainment is becoming more popular every year. Such activity among visitors is connected with the hobbies of modern youth with computer games.

Quests in Togliatti provide an opportunity to feel like a member of one of them. The theme of the game can be very different - from a good adventure story to mysticism and horror.

Typically, quests come with a group of friends. Participants are given about an hour to solve the problem. During this time, visitors will have to solve quests, search for "keys" and get out of difficult situations.

The whole process is accompanied by beautiful animation, thematic decor and musical accompaniment.

The most popular quests in the city

Among residents and guests of Togliatti, favorites have already appeared in this form of entertainment. They come back here to beat their previous result.

"Uncle Tom's Cabin" offers to feel like a company of friends who have not found a way out of the dense forest. They got to the hut and decided to spend the night here. Young people cannot even imagine what lies ahead. This quest is located on the street. Homeland, 23.

"Toxicol. Secret Laboratory" offers to feel like rescuers around the world from dangerous weapons of mass destruction. The laboratory was attacked, and quest participants need to get out of it in the time given. They must bring with them new developments of dangerous weapons. The quest works in two modes - from 10 to 16 years and for adults. It is located on the street. Topolina, 4b.

"Hide and Seek" is entertainment in Togliatti for the whole family. Children from 5 years old can take part here. Participants find themselves in a dark city, on their way they meet various characters. Customers must either find a common language with them, or avoid meeting. What is not so simple.

The game is accompanied by various special effects, music and decor. Guests of the quest can choose their own mode - "funny" or "scary". The game is designed for 90 minutes. Up to 15 people can take part in it.

Children's entertainment centers

In the city on the basis of the shopping center there are many different complexes, where at any time of the year you can have fun with your whole family. On Lenin Boulevard, 1a, a recreation center is located in the recreation center Tolyatti.

Here, children under 10 years old are given the opportunity to visit the "Emerald City", where you can play in the soft maze, slide down the slides and jump on trampolines. Older children will be able to visit the bowling alley. And also in the center there are modern game consoles.
