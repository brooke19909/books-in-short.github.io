Hotel Islazul Oasis 2 (Cuba / Varadero): photos and reviews of tourists

Do you want an exotic and at the same time simple and homely cozy holiday on the island of Freedom? Then come to Cuba, to the Islazul Oasis 2 hotel. And do not be afraid of its low star rating. Here you will feel real pleasure. Firstly, the management of this hotel is carried out by the famous Islazul hotel business line. Secondly, it stands right on the seashore, and surrounded by its beautiful park and ocean of emerald tropical greenery.





How to get there

Travelers mostly arrive at the Islazul Oasis 2 (Cuba) from the Juan Gualberto Gomez International Airport. It is twenty kilometers from the hotel. As a rule, tourists use a shuttle service. And since the hotel stands at the entrance to the famous resort, the residents of the hotel, to which our article is devoted, get to the ocean first, and pick them up last.



Location

Islazul Oasis 2 is a two-hour drive from the capital of the island of Havana. But it is much closer to the international airport. Transfer from there in time is no more than half an hour. Its location, according to tourists, is more attractive than other hotels of this kind. First of all, it is remote from the tourist center of Varadero (actually standing on the road), which ensures its inhabitants peace and quiet. In addition, it is close to the Matanzas district, where you can inexpensively take a taxi and take a look at the life of ordinary Cubans. The center of Varadero can be reached on foot - not really in a hurry somewhere in half an hour. Or take a taxi in just five minutes. Some "fours" and "fives" are even further from the center, at the very end of the sand spit.

Sea and beach

The coast near the Islazul Oasis Hotel 2 is small. However, the beach is clean, with sunbeds, mushrooms and a bar. The sea is simply beyond praise. Sunset in the ocean is very smooth. But if you want white sand like in the picture, bars with Cuban music and parties, souvenir shops, then go to the center of Varadero, you will find all this only there. But on the local beach they make good cocktails, and you can immediately listen to music. And besides, there are no people on the beach, unlike Varadero, where there is simply nowhere for an apple to fall. True, there is no beer in the beach bar, but you can take it at the hotel in thermo jugs from the rooms and carry with you. A huge advantage of this beach is that there are no other hotels nearby, and thus, neither locals nor visitors come here. So you can safely leave things and swim. Tourists are advised to definitely visit the beaches and the Caribbean, especially with a mask. The underwater world is amazing. To the left of the beach there is a completely wild territory - it is very good for divers and underwater hunters. You can find lobsters, moray eels. On the territory of Islazul Oasis 2 (Cuba, Varadero) there is a good, clean pool with sun loungers, however, without a children's section. But with it there is a jacuzzi, which can accommodate six people.
