The remainder

Another very interesting trick is the inclusion in the table of the so-called balance at the end of the month. It is advisable to put it off for your needs. Let it be a nice bonus to 10% of your salary.

The balance at the end of the month is the difference between revenues and expenses. It is necessary to calculate the income and expenses of the family, and then subtract from the first paragraph of the second. And you will get how much money you have left. This technique helps very well when you need to learn how to accumulate funds. Perfectly visible difference at the end of each month.



Calculations

How to calculate the income and expenses of a family? To be honest, the formulas built into Excel are a great help here . They will quickly and accurately bring you the results, moreover, automatically. And then you will have to analyze the budget yourself.

It is very convenient, especially when you consider that Excel will play the role of both a notebook and a calculator.
