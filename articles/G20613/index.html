The infrastructure of the resort

Hotel Karmir Resort Spa 5 * (Kemer) was built in 2014. It occupies 18,000 m 2 . On a vast territory, there are three four-story buildings with 295 rooms, equipped with elevators, laundry, doctor's office, Internet cafe. Wi-Fi is free in the lobby. The hotel has a conference room with 400 seats.

On its territory, one indoor and two outdoor pools with three water slides, a tennis court. Guests enjoy the services of a beauty salon and spa. The huge spa complex offers visitors to relax in a Turkish bath or sauna, undergo therapeutic massage, take a hot tub and adjust the shape of the figure in the gym.





Accommodation in rooms

Spacious loggias adjoin the air-conditioned rooms at the Karmir Resort Spa 5 * hotel. The apartments are designed for a comfortable stay of single tourists, families and travelers with disabilities. Guests are accommodated in the compact, comfortable standard rooms of Standard Room. They are offered to stay in spacious one-room apartments Family Room. They arrange accommodation in two-room Junior Suites with a bedroom and a living room. King Suite is a one-room apartment with a single bed and a seating area. The rooms have Wi-Fi and wired internet.

The rooms in the Karmir Resort Spa 5 * hotel with laminated floors are equipped with a telephone, a free hairdryer, a safe, a minibar (the staff replenishes the bar with water, soft drinks and beer daily). Beach vacation

Hotel Karmir Resort Spa 5 * has its own beach. The length of the spit, strewn with sand mixed with small pebbles, is 150 meters. To the beach, 90 meters away from the hotel, tourists stroll along the beautiful promenade. The coast of the village of Goynuk, protected by the majestic mountain ranges of Taurus, is fragrant with pomegranate and orange orchards, palm and oleander groves.

On the incredibly clean beach that has earned the European Blue Flag for impeccable grooming, vacationers are provided with free mattresses, sun loungers and umbrellas. Towels are issued on deposit. In addition to sea bathing and sunbathing, tourists go in for water sports, fishing and ride pleasure yachts.
