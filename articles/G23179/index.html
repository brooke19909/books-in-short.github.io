Hotel 4 * Magawish Village Resort (Egypt, Hurghada): description, photos, reviews of tourists

The 4 * Magawish Village Resort has everything you need for an unforgettable vacation. No wonder tourists called this hotel a paradise on the Red Sea.

general information

Hotel 4 * Magawish Village Resort is one of the first resort complexes in Egypt, which was designed as a tourist village. Given that most travelers visit Hurghada precisely for the Red Sea, this hotel is simply an ideal place, because it has its own beach, which is 2 km long. The hotel is also famous for its picturesque views of the famous island of Magawish.

Particularly close attention at the 4 * Magawish Village Resort is given to all sorts of entertainment. They are so numerous and diverse that both lovers of imposing and leisurely pastime, and those who cannot imagine their life without exotic and extreme sports can find something suitable for themselves.

The hotel has a rich history and established traditions, because it was built back in 1979. But this should not scare away tourists, because more recently (in 2011) the hotel was completely renovated.





Where is the hotel located

One of the indisputable advantages of the 4 * Magawish Village Resort is its location. It is located in the very center of Hurghada and only 8 km from the airport. But the most important thing is that this resort village is located right on the shores of the Red Sea.





Number of rooms

The real pride of the Magawish Village Resort 4 * hotel (Hurghada) is its number of rooms. The hotel has 425 guest apartments, which are divided into 6 main groups.

It can be noted the restrained, but original interior of the rooms of the hotel Magawish Village Resort 4 *. Photos of tourists demonstrate comfort and cleanliness in the apartments.





Leisure opportunities

The Magawish Village Resort 4 * hotel (Hurghada) has a developed infrastructure, and also provides its guests with a huge number of opportunities for an unforgettable vacation and comfortable stay. So, the list of services provided by the hotel complex is as follows:

  • 24-hour reception and information service;
  • the possibility of ordering limousines for special occasions;
  • laundry and dry cleaning;
  • free wireless Internet (this is one of the few hotels in Egypt that does not charge an additional fee for using the global network);
  • rich animation program;
  • a supermarket, as well as several small shops and souvenir shops;
  • two large pools, one of which is equipped with the possibility of heating;
  • several tennis courts;
  • diving center services, which include equipment rental, training and instruction, as well as diving;
  • windsurfing equipment and training;
  • riding lessons with an instructor.




For the smallest tourists

Children are perhaps the most demanding guests of any hotel. Many parents take their kids on holiday to Egypt. Magawish Village Resort 4 * tries to make the stay of young tourists as comfortable as possible, providing them with the following services:

  • modern playground with swings, sandpit, slides and other entertainment;
  • kids club, where kids expect games, contests and other entertainment;
  • every evening after dinner, a disco;
  • children's tables in the restaurant;
  • play area on the beach;
  • two children's pools with slides.




restaurants and bars

No matter how beautiful and well-groomed the territory of the hotel may be, no matter how luxurious and comfortable the rooms may seem, the main place in the ranking of vacationers' priorities is food. Hotel Magawish Village Resort 4 * invites its guests to visit restaurants and bars where you can find food and drinks for every taste:

  • The Manta Restaurant is a spacious dining room, where three times a day a buffet awaits guests with a wide range of dishes from different cuisines of the world.
  • Restaurant "Dolphin" - specializes in the most popular dishes of different nations.
  • Seafood Restaurant - offers its guests to taste the various fish varieties, as well as many seafood delicacies.
  • Oriental cafe - cozy atmosphere, thematic design, hookah.
  • Bar "Dolphin" - offers guests drinks and snacks in the afternoon.
  • Disco Pub is an ideal place for lovers of music and strong drinks.
  • Pool and beach bars.




Bonuses and surprises

Since the competition among hotels located on the Red Sea coast is quite large, each of them seeks to win the hearts of tourists. For this purpose, a loyalty program or a bonus system works in almost every hotel. Hotel Magawish Village Resort 4 * (Egypt, Hurghada) offers its guests the following compliments:

  • newlyweds who indicated the purpose of the visit when booking a honeymoon, and can also confirm this information by presenting a marriage certificate, are automatically settled in a room with a sea view and receive a fruit basket as a gift ;
  • birthday people receive a birthday cake on their birthday, at the request of the guest a gift can be delivered to a room or restaurant;
  • tourists arriving at the hotel on their wedding anniversary also receive a cake (do not forget to present a certificate of marriage);
  • the hotel respects and appreciates its guests, so it tries to do everything to return here again and again - from the fifth visit you fall into the regular customers category and get the right to upgrade the room category, as well as a surprise in the form of a fruit basket.

Positive reviews

The format of the resort village is perhaps the most popular. That is why tourists are so fond of coming to the Magawish Village Resort 4 *. The reviews of guests who have been here before contain a lot of positive impressions and thanks to the resort complex:

  • convenient entry into the sea without stones, as well as a shallow depth, which is very important for tourists vacationing with children;
  • simplified registration and check-in procedure for regular guests;
  • good nutrition, always fresh and high-quality products, especially the diet will appeal to fans of diet dishes and a healthy lifestyle;
  • good children's animation, as well as an interesting entertainment program for adults;
  • friendly, polite and competent staff;
  • the spacious territory of the hotel with a large number of green spaces;
  • there is an opportunity to ride horses;
  • resettlement in a bungalow;
  • availability of toilet and shower on the beach;
  • a large number of sporting events (gymnastics, dancing, aerobics, games);
  • good surf school;
  • high-quality and regular cleaning;
  • the opportunity to dine on the beach;
  • quiet and calm atmosphere, remoteness of the hotel from noisy objects and crowded places;
  • guests who check into the hotel after dinner are given the opportunity to eat out of the way;
  • close to the airport;
  • a large assortment of desserts in the diet.




Negative reviews

Probably the most popular holiday destination among all Egyptian resorts is Hurghada. Hotel Magawish Village Resort 4 * is visited by many tourists, leaving reviews about it on specialized forums and other resources. Among all these comments, a number of negative points, complaints and complaints can be distinguished:

  • old furniture in the rooms;
  • sun loungers on the beach are not enough for all guests;
  • not very high-quality massage;
  • the menu contains too few fruits and fresh vegetables;
  • low speed of the Internet;
  • inflated prices for excursions to the hotel bureau;
  • there are not enough napkins in the restaurant;
  • the tour price includes only 100 Mb of traffic for the entire stay, which, of course, is not enough, a separate fee is charged for the further use of the service.

General impression

Despite the fact that the hotel does not look very fresh and requires, if not major, then at least cosmetic repairs, the general impression of it is quite pleasant. It should be understood that this is a municipal hotel complex of a budget price category, so it is unreasonable to expect from it the level of five-star resorts. It all depends on what goals you pursue, going to Egypt.

Hotel Magawish Village Resort 4 * will appeal to those who want to enjoy the sea and spend time in a quiet and relaxing atmosphere. If you are not spoiled by luxury apartments and delicacies, but just want to relax efficiently and inexpensively, then here you are.
