Hotel Seasing Boutique Hotel 3 * (Vietnam / Nha Trang): reviews and photos of tourists

If you are planning to visit Vietnam during your vacation, then you need to take care of the hotel in advance. In order to avoid any unpleasant situations, you need to listen to the opinions of former guests of a hotel. Today we look at Seasing Boutique Hotel 3 * (Nha Trang). What services does he offer? What can you expect while staying at this hotel? What is your impression of the former guests of this hotel? All this will be discussed later.





Introduction

One of the most visited cities in Vietnam is gradually becoming Nha Trang (it is the capital of Khanh Hoa province). Many consider him the pearl of the whole direction. Every year new resorts and hotels open here. Tourists are offered to spend their vacation away from the hustle and bustle, enjoy the sound of the sea outside the window, stunning sunrises and sunsets, quality service and fairly affordable prices. In 2013, according to Lonely Planet magazine, Nha Trang became "the most beautiful sea bay in the world." On its territory is the hotel Seasing Boutique Hotel, which will be discussed.

Description

The building of the Seasing Boutique Hotel (Vietnam) is a fifteen-story building. It is located within walking distance from the city center and the main tourist attractions, 200 meters from the sea, on the second coastline. From the balconies of the last floors you can see the whole city and its nearest outskirts. Near the tourist area is a large market. The hotel opened its doors to its first guests in 2014, since then the level of service has been continuously growing. Vacationers leave 90% of positive reviews on various Internet resources, give tips and tricks, and tell about their vacation in colors. Due to the constantly warm weather in Vietnam, the hotel is never empty.





Booking, check-in and check-out

You can book a room either through the website on which you need to leave a request, or by e-mail or by phone. As soon as the hotel administration notices the interest of a potential client, the representative will contact the applicant as soon as possible, answer all your questions, process the request and tell you about the conditions of settlement. Check-in is carried out daily at 14:00 local time, eviction - at 12:00. When booking more than 24 hours before check-in, a deposit is required (the exact amount will be indicated by the administrator). If canceled up to 48 hours before arrival, no fee will be charged. Tourists are advised not to make reservations, but to check the availability of seats by phone, and immediately rent a house on the day of arrival. According to them, there are always free rooms of all categories at Seasing Boutique Hotel, and there is no commission when paying on the spot, but you will have to leave a deposit. Accepted both cash and plastic cards. Reception is open 24 hours a day. It is possible to conduct VIP check-in or check-out according to the simplified and accelerated mode. Luggage storage and currency exchange services are also provided here.

Dining at the hotel

Seasing Boutique Hotel also receives positive reviews for good food. The guests note that the products are always fresh, the dishes are deliciously prepared, moreover, their variety is pleasing. Room price includes breakfast with morning delivery. Lunch and dinner are available at the restaurant on the ground floor. A team of professional chefs daily cooks dozens of dishes from Asian and European cuisine. If the guest pays for full meals, he can use the services of the buffet three times a day.

Additional services

All guests are offered a shuttle service from or to the airport. Free secure parking is available on site. Dry cleaning, laundry and clothing cleaning are available. Room service and housekeeping are provided annually. Tourists are greeted by a concierge, and all staff speak several foreign languages, including English, Chinese and Russian. Guests can use the high-speed wireless Internet around the clock anywhere in the hotel. Newlyweds can request that their booked room be decorated in honor of their wedding event. A compliment from the hotel in this case is a large platter of fresh fruit in their room. For those who visited the hotel on a working visit, the services of a conference room and meeting room are provided. They are located on the 14th floor. Anyone can use the services of a tour desk. Experienced guides provide a choice of several group trips ready for the main attractions of the city, help to create individual routes for tourists. The administration and staff of the Seasing Boutique Hotel, upon request, organize a transfer, order a taxi, and, if necessary, arrange escort (driver, security and translator).





Infrastructure and entertainment at the hotel

Seasing Boutique Hotel (Nha Trang) has a large swimming pool with sea views. There is a fitness room where you can take advantage of individual lessons with a personal trainer, and a spa center. There is a massage room. The SeaSing bar is open for visitors, where professional bartenders work every night and play loud music, the atmosphere of which has a lot of fun and new acquaintances, and a restaurant with a capacity of up to 150 people. There are separate areas for families, a library, as well as play areas for younger guests. There is everything necessary for practicing beach and water sports. There are several terraces and benches throughout. Bicycles can be rented.

Sea and beach

There is a separate access to the sea. At the private beach, sun loungers, parasols and towels are provided free of charge. Personal belongings are protected. Those who lived in this hotel note the attentive attitude of the staff towards the preservation of the property of guests during their bathing or walking along the coast. Some tourists noted that at the bottom in some places there are a lot of stones, so you need to be careful and watch your children as a parent.





Hotel Location

It is 40 km from Kamran Airport, and Nha Trang Airport is 32 km away. Located in the northern part of the city, in an area called Vinh Hai. All additional information can be clarified on the official website.

How to get to the hotel

First you need to be in Vietnam, in the city of Nha Trang. Every day flights from the capital of Russia are operated by several major air carriers. The flight time with at least one change will be from 12 to 19 hours (there are no direct flights), the ticket price is from 27,000 rubles. Further from the arrival airport to the hotel can be reached in three ways:

  • Alone on public transport (travel). This method is highly not recommended for foreign tourists who are in the country for the first time, do not know the language and do not navigate in Nha Trang.
  • Taxi.
  • Transfer from the hotel. The safest and budget option, the organization of which is worth worrying in advance.

It is important for guests to know a few points before deciding whether to use a mode of transport such as a taxi:

  1. Most taxi drivers in the country do not know English
  2. If there is no counter, it is better not to get into the car, but if there is no other option, then you need to agree on the cost of the trip in advance (with a language barrier, a leaf and a pen will come to the rescue)
  3. The cost will directly depend on the size of the machine (the larger the more expensive)
  4. Vinasun and some others have GPS navigation.




Nearby Attractions

Near the hotel there are both cultural and historical sites:

  • Alexandre Yersin Museum. Tourists who visited it are strongly advised to use the audio guide service (choosing their native language) when buying an entrance ticket, since all the inscriptions on tablets and exhibits are in French, without English dubbing. Hotel Seasing Boutique has a separate excursion to the museum, accompanied by a Russian-speaking guide.
  • Hon Chong. It is a stone garden. It is a visiting card of the tourist part of the city. It is recommended to visit it in sunny weather.
  • Nha Trang Cathedral. A modest but cozy temple. All visitors note the absence of religious pathos inside.
  • Hon Do Pagoda. Temple of the Sun. Construction was completed in the middle of the last century.
  • Xom Bong Bridge. The length of the bridge is about 400 meters.
  • The famous Vinh Hai Local Market, supermarkets.
  • Nha Trang Center Shopping Mall and ACE minimart. Nearby there are several inexpensive restaurants with national and European cuisine, including Hoa Nhan An Seafood Restaurant and Sibiryachka.
