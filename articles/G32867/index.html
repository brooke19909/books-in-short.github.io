Instructions for creating a volumetric star

Start making crafts with a choice of paper. For work, you can use either a regular sheet or bright multi-colored paper. A volume star made of paper with your own hands can be made of durable paper for scrapbooking, in this case the craft will come out quite dense, and it can be used as a decorative decoration for a room or a Christmas tree toy.



On the back of the colored paper you need to draw a circle whose diameter will correspond to the size of the star. A compass is suitable for these purposes, but if it is not, then you can use a saucer or other round object. After that, you need to draw a line from the edge to the center of the circle.

At the second stage, a transport ruler is useful. With its help, from a straight line you need to note an angle equal to 72 degrees, and draw a line. Thus, it is necessary to mark the entire circle - you get five lines.

The next step is to connect all the lines in the star. In the center of the part, you need to draw additional lines as illustrated in the picture. After that, the asterisk must be cut and bent along the main lines so that the drawing looks inward. If thick paper was used in the work, then folds should be worked on more carefully so that they are as pronounced as possible. For these purposes, you can use any blunt object (ruler, back of the knife, etc.).



A do-it-yourself 3D paper star is made quickly enough. In conclusion, you need to make a small loop so that a beautiful craft can be hung on a Christmas tree or elsewhere.
