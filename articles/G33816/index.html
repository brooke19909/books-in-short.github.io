Sochi, Arboretum park: how to get, reviews and photos

Are you going to visit Sochi? Let me congratulate you. This is an amazing region, warm and welcoming, huge and infinitely beautiful. All ideas about Russia as a cold country are scattered here. This is not surprising, because you will definitely visit one of the pearls of Sochi - the Arboretum park. Here eucalyptus flowers bloom and delicate tropical palm trees, flower beds are fragrant. The vast territory of the park allows you to spend the whole day here, without ceasing to be surprised at its charms. Today we want to tell the reader a little more about how to have a great time in Sochi. Arboretum Park is not at all a boring botanical garden, but the best place for a family holiday.



Hint for tourists

In fact, getting lost here is completely impossible. This is a tourist town where everything is set for vacationers. In general - this is Sochi. Arboretum Park is too famous a sight to have to search for. Take a taxi and in a few minutes you will be there.

However, many tourists decide to hit the road on their own in order to simultaneously appreciate the beauty of the city of Sochi. The Arboretum park, which can be reached in several ways, is located at Khostinsky district, Kurortny prospekt, 74. By purchasing a city map at any kiosk, you can easily find it.

Entrance to the Arboretum is through the Central Gate located in front of the Upper Park. You can get to them from the coast or the lower part on foot, but the road is not long and goes uphill. Therefore, it is best to use the funicular and start exploring from the upper part of the Arboretum, gradually going down.

By public transport you need to get to the Circus stop. Almost all routes go to it, so there will be no difficulties. Moreover, buses and minibuses pass here not only from Sochi, but also from neighboring resort areas. So, number 105 is an ideal option for those who want to visit the Arboretum (Sochi). How to get from the Olympic Park? You just need to get on the bus and get off at the indicated stop.

Mode of operation

Here you are expected every day, at any time of the year. The Arboretum park in Sochi is always beautiful, and no matter what the weather is outside. It is never sad here. The summer abundance of flowers replaces the autumn riot of colors. In winter, the most delicate plants are covered with special caps, but the territory does not lose its incredible beauty from this. And in the spring, he recommends that romantics visit the Arboretum park in Sochi.

Opening hours in the summer - from 8:00 to 21:00. If you want to consider every corner of the park, then come here for the whole day. The winter period begins on November 1 and lasts until March 1. At this time, visitors are expected from 9:00 to 17:00.



Cost

It depends on whether you will use the services of a guide. A ticket for one adult - 250 rubles. It allows you to walk around the entire territory. When buying a ticket, a park card is issued, which is very convenient. Children's cheaper, 120 rubles. Up to seven years old kids are free. The cable car for an adult - 200 rubles, for a child - 100. The park operates seven days a week.

Description

When going on an excursion, consider the air temperature in Sochi. The Arboretum park is very large, there are many shady alleys on its territory, however, before planning your trip, you need to get acquainted with the map. The fact is that it is divided into two halves. The plain is located closer to the sea, and the second on the side of the mountain. Both parts are connected by a tunnel, so from the main entrance you can go directly to the Lower Park. However, in this case, you will be forced to climb up the mountain slope in the very sunshine to explore the Upper Park. Therefore, we recommend using the funicular and starting the way down.



Air walk

The cable car starts at 9:00. Get ready for an excursion early so that you can go around the area until evening. In this case, you can take your time and completely calmly explore all the sights. By the way, the length of the airway is 908 meters, and the top view is simply magnificent. Although experienced travelers are advised to pay more attention to the park itself, so that later you do not have to wander.

The walk starts from the observation deck. It is from here that a stunning view of the surroundings, the sea and the mountains opens. A particularly spectacular sight will be in spring and summer, when you can see the whole variety of amazing plants, colors and shapes.

Upper park

It is best to start your walk from here. In the upper part there are interesting, stylized zones. Japanese garden with picturesque ponds, Chinese courtyard, grottoes and artificial waterfalls, caves, their inspection will take you a lot of time. If you want to admire the beauty of tropical palm trees, then welcome to the avenue of mayors. Do not miss the chance to explore the unique sequoia grove.



Animal world

If you come to rest with children, it will be interesting to take a walk to the farm of ostriches and peacocks. They can be fed, which causes a storm of delight in kids. Nearby is a corner where colorful parrots live, as well as a nursery of medicinal plants. The arboretum is a special place where visitors do not cease to be amazed at the surrounding beauties. It is especially pleasant to inhale a bouquet of aromas of plant exotic in the summer heat when shady alleys save from the sun no worse than an air conditioner.

central part

There is a beautiful legend that you can make wishes here and they will come true.

Lower part of the park

Going down here, tourists are already a little tired, and here is waiting for a long-awaited respite. The lower part of the park is smaller, and it is located on the plain, so the walk becomes easier. Here awaits guests the most beautiful and fragrant corner of the park - the rose garden. It collected about 70 varieties of domestic and foreign selection. Beautiful flowers delight the tourist throughout the season, from early spring to late autumn.

Very close is a natural pond, which is inhabited by the most beautiful birds. Here you can see black and white swans, storks, pelicans. Numerous sculptures adorn this part of the park. Near the southern gate is located one of the most interesting park objects, this is an aquarium, where you can get acquainted with the life of the bizarre inhabitants of the underwater world. These are moray eels and piranhas, stingrays and even sharks.



Reviews

If you like wildlife and hiking, then be sure to visit the Arboretum Park in Sochi. Reviews of tourists emphasize that this is a great opportunity to spend an eventful day. However, be sure to wear comfortable shoes, as the legs will be very heavy. Experienced tourists are advised to bring soft drinks. Of course, there are shops on the territory where you can buy everything you need, but the prices are quite high.

There are countless plants in the park. Near each of them there is a tablet with a name and a detailed description. If you do not use the services of a guide, then you may well find out all the information that is needed. Be sure to calculate your time in such a way as to stay here until the evening. For a small price, you can walk all day and enjoy the extraordinary greenery. It is especially good to spend some quiet minutes near the pond. Here all the problems disappear, and you just want to watch the unhurried life of its inhabitants.

The funicular deserves special attention. Judging by the reviews, many people visit the park solely because of the pleasure of taking a ride on the airway and seeing the surroundings from a bird's eye view. And then you can enjoy the view from the observation deck and take your time to go down the park territory.
