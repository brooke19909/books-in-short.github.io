Mar del Plata Argentina Attractions

The colorful country of Argentina annually attracts hundreds of thousands of tourists who want to get acquainted with local traditions and culture. Plunge into the hot summer want vacationers from all over the world.

Argentina on a world map

Throughout the country, the capital of which is located in Buenos Aires, takes eighth place. Located immediately in three climatic zones, it attracts tourists with its unique geographical position and various attractions that cause delight. Argentina on a world map is located in the southeastern part of South America and covers almost the entire continent. Due to the long stretch from north to south, the country's natural monuments are incredibly beautiful and are of particular interest to guests.



A resort built for local aristocrats

In the eastern part of the South American state, on the coast of the Atlantic Ocean, there is a colorful city, the name of which is translated from Spanish as "silver sea".

Five centuries ago, F. Drake explored the territory, wanting to find fur seal colonies. Then the Jesuit missionaries unsuccessfully tried to settle here, and only in the XIX century the most beautiful resort of Mar del Plata was founded by the owner of the meat processing factory. The small town in which the railway line from the capital of Argentina appeared has the status of a city. It develops due to wealthy visitors who have chosen a cozy corner at the beginning of the last century. Inspired by the most famous works of architecture, architects build the city under the impression of them. Luxurious villas are being built to house the rich of Argentina, and with the development of tourism, modern buildings appear.



Soon, Mar del Plata with numerous beaches turns into a popular resort, which becomes a favorite vacation spot with aristocrats.

Beach resort

Now a tourist center with a population of about 600 thousand inhabitants takes in the high season (from December to March) up to six million vacationers, enchanted by a picturesque place.

Of course, city beaches are always crowded. The promenade with night bars and entertainment venues occupies more than twenty kilometers. Almost all the beaches of the resort are municipal and arranged so that both lovers of noisy parties and lovers of a relaxing holiday will have a great time here.

The stone tower, which looks very unusual, also acts as an observation deck, where 50 people can be at the same time. Nature reserve

Tourists who want to watch amazing animals living in the wild go to the reserve, which received the status of a natural monument. Not far from the port there is a beach where about 800 sea lions live, freely feeling themselves. Visitors come to a unique corner to admire the pretty representatives of eared seals at close range.

Bridge-like house

In the small forest park of the resort of Mar del Plata, a local attraction is conveniently located, which is a residential building, which is made in the form of a bridge over a flowing stream. The authors of the building that appeared in the 40s of the last century wanted to demonstrate all the possibilities of modern architecture, and a unique structure can be seen not only from the side, but also from below. The unusual "house-bridge", in the design of which the stream perfectly fit in, eventually turned into a popular museum, and not a single tourist leaves without meeting an amazing mansion.



Port El Puerto

In 1916, the port of El Puerto was built, turning Mar del Plata (Argentina) into a huge fishing center. A walk along El Puerto is a fascinating journey, and tourists admire the harbor lifestyle, which has remained unchanged for a hundred years. You can look into the old port area, see a fishing shipyard, buy antique jewelry from sunken ships in souvenir shops. And for those who want to spend the night here, unusual hotels on the water open their doors, where instead of rooms, guests are provided with cabins.



Argentines themselves call Mar del Plata a "happy city", welcoming millions of travelers every year. The natural charm, mild climate, high level of service and interesting monuments cause an irresistible desire to visit here again to experience the special atmosphere of a fashionable resort.
