Antalya, hotel "Dolphin": description, service, reviews

Many tourists, considering options for vacation spots, turn their eyes towards Turkey. This country is famous for its high level of service and affordable prices. Moreover, here travelers are waiting for picturesque beaches, mountain landscapes, interesting historical sights and, of course, the incomparable mysterious atmosphere of the East.

Perhaps one of the most famous and popular Turkish resorts is Antalya. Hotel "Delphine" (Delphine Deluxe Palace), located here, has already managed to win the favor of tourists from all over the world. Here, youth companies, couples and business people feel comfortable.

Of course, before booking you should get as much information as possible. Where is the hotel located and how convenient is its location? What do former guests say about amenities and food? Should I bring a child with me here? The answers to these questions are sought by many travelers.

Location Information





To begin with, it is worth figuring out where the Dolphin Hotel is located. Turkey, Antalya, namely the picturesque resort village of Kurdu on the very shore of the sea - this is where you can spend a vacation that will become unforgettable.

This is a calm area, the territory of which is reserved for hotels and tourist buildings. In walking distance not only the beach, but also shops, shopping centers, cozy cafes and restaurants for every taste.

The lively center of Antalya with all its attractions and attractions is only 13 km away. You can overcome this distance both by taxi and by public transport. By the way, Antalya International Airport is only 10 km from the hotel complex. Of course, package tours already include the cost of a comfortable transfer, and the journey will take only a few minutes.

By the way, this is a relatively new hotel. "Dolphin" in Antalya was opened in 2005. Since then, hotels every year delight travelers with comfortable living conditions and good service.

How many rooms are in the hotel? Room Categories

The hotel complex Delphin Palace Deluxe 5 is considered to be quite large. At the disposal of tourists 557 rooms of the following categories:

  • 485 standard rooms - bright, spacious rooms with the necessary set of furniture and household appliances, which are designed for 2 people (+ 1 child);
  • 42 family rooms - consist of a separate bedroom with a large bed and a living room with folding sofas;
  • 24 suites - cozy double rooms with additional amenities and a unique interior design;
  • 6 rooms for disabled people - these rooms are ideally suited for guests with reduced mobility.

Of course, the hotel will help you choose the perfect room, depending on your needs and wishes.

Photo and description of living rooms





Many tourists like the vibrant and vibrant city of Antalya. The Dolphin Palace Hotel can offer strangers comfortable living conditions. All rooms (regardless of category) are large enough and bright, with parquet floors, a cozy design and new furniture. By the way, spacious balconies are also at your service. And panoramic windows allow you to enjoy views of the garden and the picturesque beach.

The rooms of the guests are waiting for: a cozy bed, a spacious wardrobe and a comfortable work desk, which will undoubtedly please business travelers. By the way, immediately upon arrival, a basket of fresh fruits is delivered to the room - a kind of compliment from the hotel.

Tourists are provided with clean towels (clean bring every day), soft bathrobes and slippers, as well as cosmetics and personal hygiene products. There is also a hair dryer (stationary).

Dining for tourists: what does the hotel offer?





Chic hotel complexes offering tourists entertainment and an all-inclusive scheme are what Antalya has become famous for. Hotel Dolphin is no exception. Paying for accommodation in one of the hotel rooms, you also get the right to a full three meals a day, as well as drinks and light snacks served in local bars.

By the way, guests do not complain about food. The spacious restaurant with a large outdoor terrace accommodates all guests, even at the maximum occupancy of the hotel. The menu is varied - guests are offered a huge selection of snacks, slices, salads, fresh fruits. Meat and fish dishes are served daily. One cannot but mention the amazing pastries and homemade ice cream, which are so happy for the little guests.

As for drinks, only products from a Turkish manufacturer can be obtained for free. The disco bar also serves some imported booze.

Conditions for a beach holiday





What in terms of beach holidays does the Dolphin Hotel offer? Turkey, Antalya in particular, is famous for its picturesque coast. The hotel territory goes directly to the Lara beach. This is a private plot, intended only for guests of the "Dolphin". As the guests say, the water here is clean, the entrance is convenient, and they take care of the beach and clean it.

Residents enjoy free sunbeds and umbrellas. At the hotel you can easily get a beach towel - it is issued free of charge, but on bail. On the beach, guests are served chilled drinks and light snacks. This is a really good place for complete relaxation.

Fun by the sea

Of course, when doing, tourists can always diversify their leisure time. You can rent a catamaran, a boat and other types of non-motorized transport off the coast for a reasonable price. Fans of more extreme relaxation have a good time here - tourists ride on boats, water ski, engage in sailing.

The beach has a large volleyball court. There is a diving school nearby. If you wish, you can hire a windsurfing instructor, although large waves rarely rise here.

Additional services for guests





What else can the Dolphin Hotel (Antalya) offer the tourist? The service in this place is really up to standard.

A laundry, dry cleaning and currency exchange are available on site. Luggage storage facilities are available. Guests use local Wi-Fi, and if necessary, you can usefully spend time in an Internet cafe.

In case of problems with well-being, you should ask the staff at the reception for help - they will definitely help to call a doctor. Medical services are paid separately (according to insurance conditions).

It offers guests ample parking with security and CCTV. By the way, there is a rental point nearby - tourists can rent a bicycle, motorcycle or car for a reasonable fee.

Business service

This hotel complex often becomes a haven for business people and even a venue for corporate vacations. It offers guests a business center where you can use a computer, scanner, printer, fax and other office equipment. There is internet throughout the hotel, and its speed is very good.

There are three conference rooms with a capacity of 40, 200 and 750 people. These rooms are used to organize seminars, meetings, as well as various banquets and even weddings.

How to brighten up leisure? Entertainment at the hotel





This hotel complex is designed to fully organize leisure guests. In the courtyard there is a huge pool with several sections of different depths. In the evening or in bad weather, you can relax by the indoor pool with a powerful heating system.

There is an opportunity to experience all the delights of a Turkish hammam. For an additional fee, guests are booked for a massage course (wellness, relaxing, healing). The hotel has its own wellness center, beauty salon, hairdresser.

Tourists have a good time playing basketball and volleyball. At your service are large tennis courts (rackets, balls and other equipment can be obtained free of charge, leaving a deposit). Guests enjoy a game of billiards, ping pong and even bowling. There is a gym (as evidenced by the reviews, there is plenty of equipment here).

In the evenings, animators organize fun outdoor shows and dances. Restless travelers spend nights at the local disco bar, where you can dance literally until the morning.

Conditions for families with a child

And an extra bed will be brought to the standard room on request. At the hotel you can take a stroller for a child, however, you need to leave a deposit.
