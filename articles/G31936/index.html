Hotels in Beldibi, Turkey: ranking, review of the best, photos and reviews of tourists

Beldibi is a small resort settlement in Turkey, located near Kemer. Many travelers going on vacation to Turkey do not see the differences between Beldibi and Kemer. But still, these are two completely different resorts that are 15 km away from each other. Beldibi has gathered all the advantages: the sea, unforgettable views of the mountains, the unique blue of the sky and, of course, hospitality.

Until the 1980s, no one had heard of this resort corner. It was just an inconspicuous village on the Taurus Mountains and the Mediterranean coast. Local residents were engaged in agriculture, mainly sheep, and citrus plants prevailed from plants. But in the 1980s, investors from Germany noticed the settlement and built small hotels in Beldibi. For several decades, this small village has become one of the most popular resorts in Turkey, which attracted tourists from all over the world. Beldibi beckons with its clean beaches, picturesque landscapes, as if created for photo shoots, historical places and developed outdoor activities (climbing, hiking, diving and rafting). Beldibi hotels in Turkey hospitably receive visitors all year round.

How to get there

Beldibi village is located 30 kilometers from Antalya, which is the nearest airport. He takes planes from different cities of Russia. Beldibi is easy to reach from this airport. They are connected by a highway, along which buses and minibuses go, in local terms - dolmush. So you can get to the center. The entire road takes 30-40 minutes if there is no traffic jam at the exit from Antalya. Hotel Map

Just a few parallel long streets from Mount Taurus to the seashore - that's the whole of Beldibi.

In Beldibi (Turkey) hotels are located everywhere. The best, four-star and five-star hotels, stand in a row at the edge of the sea on Ataturk Street. More modest options are on remote streets. Looking at the map of hotels, you will understand that there will be no problems with choosing a place. Officially, Beldibi is divided into three districts. Their names are extremely simple: the first, second and third. You can ask local residents about the boundaries of each of the territorial sectors. However, without going into details, we can say that the first Beldibi area is located on the coast, and the third Beldibi is farthest from the sea. Despite such a distribution of places - names of districts, the prices of hotel complexes in Beldibi-1 and Beldibi-3 are not very different. There are even competitions for the rooms of those hotels located in the third district, such as Club Salima (5 stars), because from the windows you can observe a magnificent view of the foot of the Taurus Mountains, and the beach is within easy reach.
