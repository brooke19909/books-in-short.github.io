Glaros Hotel 3 * (Alanya, Turkey): photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

If you, like many of our compatriots, want to have a great rest on the Turkish coast in Alanya, then the Glaros Hotel 3 * will be an excellent budget option for accommodation. We will talk about what tourists expect here, in this article.





Where is

This 3-star hotel is located just 500 meters from the center of the resort town of Alanya (Turkey). Thus, for its guests the entire infrastructure of a major tourist center will be, as they say, at hand. Just 150 meters from the hotel is the famous "Cleopatra Beach". The nearest air harbors are in Antalya (about two hours drive) and in Gazipasa (about half an hour drive). However, the last airport only accepts domestic flights. Therefore, if you plan to leave Russia directly to Alanya, then your path will go through the air harbor of the main resort city of Turkey - Antalya. So get ready for a two-hour drive to the hotel.





Alanya Glaros Hotel 3 *: description and photo

Glaros Hotel is a small, economical city-style hotel. It has a small territory on which there is a residential building, accommodating 52 rooms, as well as two swimming pools, a sun terrace and a bar. All rooms in the hotel are standard and simply decorated. Each is equipped with a private bathroom (hairdryer), cable TV, air conditioning, telephone, safe and balcony. Rooms are cleaned every day. A baby cot can be provided upon request.

As already mentioned, the hotel has two swimming pools: an adult and a children's. Near them there is a sun terrace with sun loungers and parasols. You can also play darts or table tennis here. Wi-Fi is free throughout the hotel.

Hotel guests can exchange currency, hand dry cleaning and laundry, book a tour or transfer to / from the airport. Car parking and car rental are also available on site.





Prices

As for the cost of accommodation, many tourists consider the Glaros Hotel 3 * (Turkey) a very budget option. So, according to our compatriots, at the height of summer you can get a ticket here for only 40 thousand rubles for two for 10 days.

Glaros Hotel 3 *: reviews of Russian travelers

Taking into account the fact that today more and more tourists are trying to independently choose hotels in the countries where they plan to spend their holidays, based on personal preferences and opinions of people who have already been here, and not be guided by the words of tour operators, we decided to save you a little time and introduce some of the comments of our compatriots who spent their summer holidays at Glaros Hotel (Alanya, Turkey). To get started, let's run a little ahead and say that most travelers were satisfied with their choice of place to stay. According to them, the price for accommodation here is fully consistent with the conditions and quality of service. They also note that this hotel is more designed for youth recreation. In general, many guests of Glaros Hotel 3 * had a great time here and are ready to return here again. But we learn about everything in detail.





Opinion of tourists about the location and territory of the hotel

According to our compatriots, the hotel in question has a very convenient location. So, it is located in the very center of Alanya. To "Cleopatra's beach" from here you can walk in just five minutes, and to discos and nightclubs - in 10-15 minutes. Also near the hotel there is all the necessary infrastructure: cafes, shops, supermarkets, pharmacies, etc. In addition, there are bus stops near Glaros Hotel where you can go to neighboring settlements. Nearby is an excellent water park, which is recommended to visit by almost all vacationers.

As for the territory of Glaros Hotel 3 * (Turkey), then, since this is a city-style hotel, its own area is very small. So, in addition to the residential building, there are two swimming pools and a sun terrace. Therefore, experienced travelers advise those people who prefer to spend almost their entire vacation at the hotel to find another place to stay.

Reviews about the number of rooms

According to tourists, both the building itself and the rooms of the hotel in question are decorated quite simply, without frills. However, here you will find everything you need. Hairdryer and air conditioning in the rooms are working properly. As for the plumbing, several guests complained about the fact that pipes leaked a little in the bathroom. However, this did not become a problem, since all the water went into the drain on the floor, and no puddles formed. Therefore, guests simply did not pay attention to this trifle. As for hot water, according to our compatriots, it is always there, without interruptions. Clean the rooms here every day. Towels, according to the guests of the Glaros Hotel 3 *, change if dirty ones are thrown to the floor. Also, experienced travelers recommend paying attention to the fact that single beds in the rooms cannot be moved when checking in. Therefore, if necessary, immediately ask for a room with a double bed.





Hotel comments

As for food, it, according to our compatriots, in this hotel is quite acceptable. So, according to guests, breakfasts here are always the same. For lunch and dinner, you can taste a variety of dishes from meat, fish, salads, fresh fruits. Please note that the meat is given in portions. If, for some reason, food doesn‚Äôt seem enough for you, you can always go to one of the restaurants, which have a lot of food near the hotel. As for desserts, they, according to tourists, are offered very few. However, many guests were only happy with this fact, since, in their opinion, it is already difficult to come home from vacation without a couple of extra pounds. Drinks in Glaros Hotel 3 * vacationers found quite high quality, especially beer and wine. Also, if you plan to skip breakfast, lunch or dinner (for example, go on an excursion), you can always ask to pack your food with you.

Opinion about the hotel staff

According to most vacationers, the Glaros Hotel has a very friendly, responsive and attentive staff. Administrators almost always try to check in guests immediately after arrival. If you arrive during breakfast or lunch, then first you will be asked to go eat, and then they will give you a room. By the way, guests are not wearing bracelets here, they remember everyone by sight. According to our compatriots, several of the staff speak Russian, so you can always explain if necessary.





Guest reviews of beach vacations and entertainment

According to tourists, "Cleopatra's beach" is only a five to seven minute walk from the Glaros Hotel 3 *. This beach is municipal, so you need to pay for the rental of umbrellas and sunbeds. But you can always sit on your towel or mat. The beach here is clean, it is regularly cleaned. The sand is small and pleasant, the entry into the water is gentle, without stones. Near the beach there is a playground. Also in the vicinity there are a lot of cafes and shops.

As for entertainment on site, two pools are equipped here: a deeper pool for adult guests and a shallow one for children. They are cleaned regularly, you can swim without fear. There is also a sun terrace and a ping pong table. Wi-Fi is good on site. There is no animation in Glaros Hotel, but many guests consider this a plus. But there are regular discos.
