Waterpark in Dnepropetrovsk: description, reviews

Residents of the metropolis are trying to leave away from the city in the summer heat. What if the vacation is still far away? You have to use the services of local entertainment venues. Many good reviews can be heard about the water park in Dnepropetrovsk. Compare this place with the sea coast, of course, is impossible. But here you can have a great time with your family and friends.

Basic information

In June 2016, the water park in Dnepropetrovsk first began its work. Photos of local residents filled the thematic forums and pages of social networks. Everyone wanted to understand what constitutes a new water amusement park. An interesting place was enjoyed by many, but the children were more delighted. And this is no coincidence. Kids club

Waterpark in Dnepropetrovsk offers the services of professional animators. This is a great solution for parents who do not know what to do with their child during the summer holidays. An interesting entertainment program is annually prepared by the park staff. Children are waiting for interesting games on the water, morning exercises, various master classes. Special classes for an additional fee are held for children who do not yet know how to swim.

In the kids club there is the opportunity to organize a birthday or other celebration. All information of interest can be found on the phone number of the administration.

Entertainment cost

What attracts a water park in Dnepropetrovsk? The price of an adult ticket is only 400 rubles. Children under 14 years old can go to the amusement park at a reduced cost (for 300 rubles). Toddlers up to three years old go to the territory for free.

Everyone has the opportunity to save by buying a certificate to visit the water park in Dnepropetrovsk. For 8 thousand rubles, you can purchase a subscription for 30 visits. However, dates are not indicated. The client has the opportunity to stretch the pleasure for the whole summer. The cost of a subscription for 20 visits is 6 thousand rubles.

Reviews about the water park in Dnepropetrovsk

About the new entertainment complex you can find mostly positive comments. The majority of vacationers are children over 10 years old and young people enjoying summer vacations. A lot of good reviews can be heard about adult water activities. Some rides are great for thrill-seekers. What is the Potty worth? This is a slide that you can pull off at a speed of 7 km per second. The attraction ends with a free fall into the water. Considering the reviews, the Volna slide is of considerable interest to the children, the height of which exceeds 10 m.

A lot of good reviews from parents can be heard about water activities for kids. You can have fun and safety in the amusement park. Many children like slides with a height of only 1.5 m, which descend immediately into the pool. The attraction is 9 m long. The guys are also interested in the Multislide complex, which consists of several slides. Near each attraction there are safety rules that parents should learn. There may be restrictions on age and weight. Children under 14 years old without adults are not allowed in the water park of Dnepropetrovsk.

The address and prices for basic entertainment can be clarified on the official site of the entertainment complex. The plus is that on the territory you can also have a snack, take a shower, soak up the warm sun on a comfortable sun lounger. Waterpark in Dnepropetrovsk is an entertainment complex of the European level. Therefore, if the vacation is not long, you should not despair. It also has a great time during the weekend.
