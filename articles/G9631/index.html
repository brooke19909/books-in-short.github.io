Akakul Lake

Only 80 km from Chelyabinsk and 7 km from the town of Kyshtym is the most popular pond for recreation in these parts. Akakul is a relatively small lake. Its maximum width reaches 3 km and a length of 4.8 km. The depth is also attractive for recreation and fishing: the minimum is 3 m, and the maximum (in the central and southern parts) reaches 9.6 m.
