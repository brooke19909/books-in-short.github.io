Hotel "Crystal" (Kemerovo): description, reviews, photos, address, phone, height

Who would have thought that just recently abandoned unfinished was the luxury hotel "Crystal". Kemerovo has acquired a chic hotel complex that welcomes guests from all over Russia, as well as from countries near and far abroad.

Brief description of the hotel

If you are looking for luxury apartments in the central part of the city, then the Crystal Hotel (Kemerovo) is just perfect for you. The building has a rather interesting history that will be interesting to guests. Despite the external simplicity of architecture, the hotel provides guests with quality service and fairly comfortable living conditions. Among other things, it is worth noting the advantageous location. As a result, this option becomes very attractive both for tourists and for people who come on a business trip.

The height of the Crystal Hotel in Kemerovo reaches 50 meters. The building has 17 floors. At the same time, the hotel itself is located from the 10th to the 17th. The rest of the space is a modern business center. The hotel has 118 rooms, which are distinguished by a cozy design and a large number of amenities to ensure maximum comfort. There is air conditioning, a TV and other necessary items of equipment. The bathroom provides you with a full range of bath accessories, as well as cosmetics for the shower.

Special attention deserves the restaurant, which is famous for the hotel "Crystal" (Kemerovo). He is loved not only by the hotel guests, but also by the townspeople. European cuisine is the main focus of this institution. In the evenings, live music is played here. This restaurant is also suitable for karaoke fans.
