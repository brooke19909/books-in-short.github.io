Holidays in Tunisia (Hammamet). Kheops Aqua Resort: photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Hammamet is considered a calm and conservative resort with a European level. According to tourists, it can be called one of the most respectable places in the region. And this is not surprising: wonderful beaches covered with fine sand, thalassotherapy centers, the most fascinating nightlife in the country - all this contributes to the thousands of travelers who prefer holidays in Tunisia to come here every year .

Hammamet



Prices for tours to this resort are available to many. A variety of entertainments, quiet measured rest, many bars, restaurants and night clubs, clear water, the possibility of combining holidays on the seashore with intensive wellness treatments - this combination attracts very different categories of vacationers to Hammamet. On the territory of this Tunisian resort, elderly tourists who come to improve their health, and young people, and couples with children, and romantically-minded lovers feel equally comfortable.

Hammamet is buried in greenery. According to local urban planning law, the height of buildings in the city should not be higher than cypress. That is why the resort is considered one of the most environmentally friendly in Tunisia. All conditions are created for children here.

Hotels



Hammamet, whose map of hotels is very busy, offers vacationers a choice of tours at any price level. Its hotels, many of which are built to a high European class, have not only developed infrastructure, but also high-quality service. Some of them are large SPA centers where, in addition to relaxation, tourists receive a range of wellness treatments. Typically, these hotels are built close to the sea and have four or five "stars".

Kheops aqua resort

Built on a vast territory, it belongs to the category of large. It has three hundred and twenty rooms, renovated in the style of action.

The hotel, which has a four-star category, is located in the suburb of Hammamet, in the town of Nabeul, which is considered a tourist area. There are many shops next to it, there is an excellent bazaar, two supermarkets, including the world-famous Karfur chain, whose prices are very low.
