"Amber" (Voronezh), residential complex: description, layout and reviews

Almost in the center of Russia is a large city - Voronezh. Just a little more than 500 kilometers, and you can be in the "cradle" of the Navy of the country. The population of Voronezh is already more than one million people. A lot of people move here for permanent residence from the northern regions of the state, which makes the city very attractive for developers. Many residential complexes are being built, and one of them is Yantarny (Voronezh). The residential complex is located in the city center.





Voronezh city

The city of military glory, the largest industrial, cultural, scientific center in the Central Federal District occupies the fifteenth place in the list of millionaire cities - all this can be said about Voronezh. He took an honorable second place in the ranking of green cities in Russia. All this makes it very attractive for living and attracts immigrants from the regions of the Far North and other cities. The Amber residential complex in Voronezh, among other new buildings, offers comfortable apartments for living.





Location of the complex

As already mentioned, the builder chose the most promising Kominternovsky district for the construction of Amber. Why promising? Because there are few industrial enterprises in it, close to the complex there are forest parks, squares, Figured and Triangular groves that give the main mass of oxygen. In addition, the district provides the most developed infrastructure of social facilities.

Transport accessibility

The place where the Yantarny residential complex (Voronezh) is located is 45th Infantry Division and Begovaya Streets. This is not far from the city‚Äôs major transport artery - Antonova-Ovseenko street, turning off which you can get into the complex. This arrangement guarantees good transport accessibility for future residents, allowing traffic jams to pass during rush hours. Not only with your own car you can quickly get home from the city center, but also by public transport: bus or taxi. The distance from the city center to the complex is a little over seven kilometers.





Description of the complex

"Amber" (Voronezh) - a residential complex, which consists of three residential buildings of varying floors from 15 to 18 floors. This is the construction of three positions and underground single-level parking separately. The houses are built of brick and aerated concrete block; the corporate style is modern facades made of ceramic bricks and the latest glazing systems.

Floor plans

Layouts of apartments will be different: from studios to three-room apartments. Therefore, everyone will find in the Yantarny residential complex (Voronezh), the prices of apartments in which are very attractive, housing to their taste and budget. As for the space, it will be from 22 to 90 square meters. The final deadline for the complex is scheduled for the last quarter of 2017.





All rooms in the apartments will be separate, which will allow residents to quietly retire and relax. The space of the apartments is ergonomic and is most useful. The developer provides only a rough finish, which involves the installation of an entrance metal door, a final floor screed, a complete intra-apartment wiring of electricity, high-quality plastering of the walls.

The cost of apartments

Amber (Voronezh) is a residential complex that will consist of 970 apartments. If we talk about a specific number, the project provides for 278 studios, 424 one-room, 216 two-room and 52 three-room apartments. Thus, it is seen that one-room apartments are traditionally more valued. And vice versa, large three-room options are practically not in demand.

As for the cost of residential premises, the studios are offered for sale for an amount starting from 31 thousand rubles per square meter, which is about a little more than 800 thousand rubles per studio. For a one-room one you will have to pay more than 1 million 200 thousand rubles, for a two-room one - about 2 million 300 thousand rubles, and a three-room one will make you fork out for 3 million 700 thousand rubles.

Infrastructure

Yantarny itself, a residential complex (Voronezh), is designed with developed internal infrastructure. It includes a one-level underground parking, a landscaped courtyard, children's playgrounds with swings, carousels, slides and a special coating. For adults, recreation areas and walking alleys are equipped.





As for the external infrastructure, here at the services of the residents of the complex are all the facilities that the city and specifically the Kominternovsky district can offer. For the young residents of the complex there is a gymnasium, a kindergarten, a school, and sports palaces. There are a lot of small shops and supermarkets of the federal chain in the district. There are bank branches and a forest park where you can take a walk.
