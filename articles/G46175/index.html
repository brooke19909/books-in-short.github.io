Route Kharkov - Dnepropetrovsk

Kharkov and Dnepropetrovsk are two magnificent cities located on the territory of Ukraine. Each of them has its main advantages, sights and interesting places. If you build a route on a map, you can see that the route between them is one straight line. This suggests that it is easy to overcome it on any personal or public vehicle.





How to get there by car

The distance from Kharkov to Dnepropetrovsk is only 218 kilometers. This means that it can easily be crossed on a personal vehicle. If you go by car without stops and traffic jams, with an average speed of 80 km / h, then the total travel time will be 2 hours, 44 minutes. Gas consumption will be approximately 24 liters.

There is only one option, how to get by car from one city to another:

  • You should leave Kharkov, heading along the highway to Krasnograd. Be sure to pay attention to the signs and after 108 kilometers you will find yourself in the appointed city.
  • The city of Pereshchepino is located on the outskirts of the Kharkov region, and about 41 kilometers of the route should still be laid to it.
  • The first city to meet already in the Dnepropetrovsk region - Novomoskovsk, will have to go another 46 kilometers to it. Here, the driver can breathe quietly - he was almost at the appointed place, only 23 kilometers were left to Dnipropetrovsk.

The road ahead is not close, but it is flat, wide and clean. Stay in the settlements that you will be passing by; there is probably something to see in them. On the highway there will also be several gas stations, roadside cafes and grocery stores.





We are going by train

The most convenient and comfortable option to get from Kharkov to Dnepropetrovsk is by train. Daily from the South Station (officially, "Kharkov Passenger") trains leave in this direction. Departure takes place at 20:01, and after 4 hours, 42 minutes the traveler will be at the appointed place.

In addition to the main train, there are two passing trains from Moscow and Kursk, they also follow every day. In the summer, if necessary, an additional route is introduced if there is a large flow of tourists.

The minimum ticket price is 183 hryvnias, which is approximately 431 Russian rubles. Discounts of up to 5 percent are provided for children. If the baby does not need extra bed, then he can travel with his parent absolutely free.

We go by taxi

Kharkov and Dnepropetrovsk are two popular cities among tourists coming to the country and local residents. Many decide to travel from city to city by taxi. This is not difficult - at the exit from the railway station, drivers often stand and offer their services to all those in need. But still, we recommend contacting an official organization, so the price for the route will be much lower.

By the way, in this country there are friendly people who, moreover, are fluent in Russian. So, you can get on the track, catch a passing car and ask the driver to give you a lift to the appointed place.

By bus

The daily route Kharkov - Dnepropetrovsk bus. In total, 12 flights a day are carried out: the first at 8:40, and the last at 23:15. Usually the roads in this direction are empty, there are practically no traffic jams. There are only a few stops along the way. Accordingly, this vehicle overcomes the distance quickly enough - in 5 hours.

Tickets can be purchased at the Kharkiv Central Bus Station, located at the Gagarina metro station. It is from here that the bus goes on the road. He arrives at the Central Station of Dnepropetrovsk, at the Vokzalnaya metro station.

This is very convenient, since a person who arrives immediately finds himself in the very center of the city and can safely reach any destination.





The approximate cost of a ticket per passenger is 141.52 hryvnias, which is 330 Russian rubles, 28 kopecks.

Flying on an airplane

The fastest way to overcome the distance Kharkov - Dnepropetrovsk - by plane.

The air distance between these two points is much smaller - only 196 kilometers. The total travel time is less than one hour. Unfortunately, there is no direct flight, so you will have to make one short change in Kiev.

It is worth noting that this service is very expensive compared to other vehicles - the price of one ticket is approximately equal to 8014 hryvnias, this will be about 18 705 rubles. Therefore, we recommend buying tickets in advance, approximately one month before the departure date, so they will cost several times cheaper.





There is a system of accumulative bonuses for regular customers of the airline, which subsequently can be very profitably used.

Kharkov and Dnepropetrovsk are two excellent cities that have vibrant architecture, cultural and historical values. If a person travels through a legendary country that was once part of the Soviet Union, then he certainly must visit these two wonderful sites. How can you overcome this distance? Fortunately, there are several good options.
