Olginka: Attractions and Entertainment. Olginka for adults and children

Olginka is an incredibly picturesque village, surrounded on all sides by mountains. This is a true paradise for those who prefer an active and full of impressions get-togethers at home gatherings. Arriving here, you should definitely visit all the sights and entertainment. Olginka is a small village, but there are enough interesting places here. Guests are offered walking tours to the ancient dolmens, river rafting and many more interesting things. Near the village there is a huge number of chic waterfalls with rocky shores, only some can be visited on foot, walking through the forest more often, and some will have to be reached by car.





With a properly planned time, a vacation in Olginka can become quite impressive. Attractions and entertainment here are for every taste and budget. Among tourists, educational excursions to the Goryachy Klyuch, Ashe Gorge and thirty-three more beautiful waterfalls are in great demand, the purity of the water and the picturesque landscape only comparable to Lake Baikal.

History tour

In ancient times, Olginka bore a completely different name - "Tume-kale", which means "fortress of Tum". With the onset of 1824, this wonderful village was renamed and named Olginka in honor of Princess Olga. As a result, everything ended with the construction on the Tu river of the Olga fortress, which is known to many tourists today.

The main asset of these places is the sea. Olginka is famous for its variety of unsurpassed places for swimming.

Hotel Beaches

For an additional fee, vacationers can use the pools, which are located in the hotel complexes. Such services are successfully offered by the Aquamarine hotel, the Agoy guest house, the Lermontovo hotel and other institutions. There is even such an option when the hotel has its own beach, a vacation on which costs a considerable amount of money. Despite this fact, this type of vacation is in unprecedented demand among couples with young children. It's all about the perfect cleanliness of the beaches themselves and the surrounding area. Also an obvious plus of such places for beach holidays is the availability of round-the-clock protection, thanks to which, vacationers can not be afraid to lose any of the things or, even worse, lose a child. Another significant advantage is that on the beaches at the hotels provides free use of sun loungers and other beach furniture.

Savage vacation

Lovers of wild beaches are most often located on the abandoned places of Olginka, located on the picturesque rocky shores. Often these corners of nature are remote from the central part of the village, but this in no way reduces the popularity of such places among tourists. Ways to relax, housing and food prices, the life of indigenous people and much more can be seen firsthand by visiting local attractions and entertainment. The Olga with open arms is ready to meet guests of different social levels and ages. Elderly people will probably choose a calm and measured relaxation on the territory of private beach areas, families with children will be happy with the beaches of shallow water. People who love nudism and relaxation in the style of Robinson Crusoe and Friday will also find refuge, in which there is not the slightest hint of the presence of civilization and comfort.

In addition to the beaches, there are many other tourist attractions that Tuapse (Olginka) offers. Sights, entertainment in these places are no less exciting than in more expensive resorts.

Water and entertainment complex "Orbit"



This magnificent entertainment complex is equipped with all kinds of water attractions, a swimming pool with a depth of 1.5 meters and a length of about 37 meters. Also, visitors are invited to a small outdoor swimming pool, which has a huge number of gutters and slides for swimming, kamikaze and anaconda attractions. To correct problems with the spine, visitors are offered a hydromassage with intensely flowing streams of warm water. There is even a jacuzzi where to be - a pleasure. For lovers of an active lifestyle, there are their sights and entertainment. Olginka is the favorite place of many who are fond of sports and is watching the figure.

On the territory of the Orbita water-entertainment complex there is a place for those who plan to come close to improving their own muscular frame. To do this, the complex has a set of simulators, a treadmill, an eleptic trainer and other attributes that a real athlete needs or who simply watches the figure and observes the regime of physical activity.

For fans of the game of golf, there is also entertainment, since the complex has a miniature golf club.

Waterpark "Summer"





Near the central beach of Olginka there is an open-air entertainment water complex - the water park "Summer". A variety of slides allows you to get great pleasure for both children and adults, and even the smallest visitors. Guests at the entrance are greeted by cartoon characters, from which children come in genuine delight.

The water park is not large in size, but this does not prevent it from being one of the most visited places in Olginka.

For the youngest visitors there is a swimming pool with animation. Older children can ride on various slides. You can feel the greatness of the raging sea in the wave pool. Adults can choose more extreme water rides.

For a bite to eat or quench your thirst, go to the bar and cafe located in the water park.

Tourists who prefer a diverse vacation at reasonable prices should definitely visit the village of Olginka. Sightseeing and entertainment (photos of all interesting places, unfortunately, could not be placed in this article) are numerous, any traveler will find something to his liking.
