Snood-hood with knitting needles: diagrams and description (photo)

A snood hood is a universal thing that can replace two things at once - a hat and a scarf. It is intended for girls who always want to look fashionable and do not want to spoil their hairstyles. Easy to take off and put on. It goes well with any clothing, except that which is made of bulk material or has its own hood. It costs in stores from 1.5 thousand rubles. Yarn for knitting

To make the snood hood soft and warm, you need to choose a good yarn for knitting it. It should be elastic and keep in shape. Therefore, it is better to pay attention to acrylic or woolen threads. You can, for example, take yarn of the following brands:

  1. Brilliant Vita. These strings are great for creating casual and holiday dresses. They allow you to create even the most fantastic patterns and realize bold ideas. Perfect for creating scarves, hats and snoods. They include 45% wool laster and 55% acrylic.
  2. Mohair Delicate Nako. This yarn, consisting of 40% mohair and 60% acrylic, is very beautiful and light, it is great for creating autumn and winter items. Economical in knitting. Any things created from it, very practical and soft, do not prick and do not cause allergies.
  3. Angora De Luxe YarnArt is a great yarn for creating light and at the same time warm things. It is very economical in knitting (about 2 skeins are enough for snood), it holds color well, does not prick and does not roll. Any products knitted from it, whether they are blouses, hats or snoods, retain their shape and size for a long time. Composition: 70% mohair and 30% quality acrylic.
  4. Cashmira Alize. Many women needlewomen like these threads for several reasons at once. So, they easily fit into beautiful patterns, suitable for knitting demi-season and winter things, including hoods and snoods, do not require special care. Products created from them turn out to be soft, fairly dense, non-short-walled and bright. They can be machine washed.

In addition, to create a snood-hood, you should use pure Angora or goat down. Products from them will turn out more elegant and airy, but not warm enough.

Size calculation

Another step that you need to do before you start reading the scheme and description of a knitted snood-hood with knitting needles is to calculate the size of the future product. Here it is worth considering that the height and thickness of a thing is usually indicated in the instructions and workshops. If you are only trying your hand at knitting, you should stick to them. Another female option

If you dream of creating a more modern version of a snood-hood with a photo, the diagrams and description located just below will help you with this. Just pay attention to the next moment: to get a snood, you need to knit the product from one end to the other with one piece. After completion of work, the scattered edges should be connected with a mattress seam.





To do this, you need to spread the product on the table with the ends to each other, carefully watching that nothing is mixed up. Then determine the first loops going beyond the edge. Pick up the handle of the front loop, pull the thread through it. The same thing to do from the other end. And so on until the product is firmly stitched. You can always learn more precisely how to perform a mattress stitch in knitting from the master classes of various needlewomen.

Competent care for snood-hood

After completing the knitting of the snood-hood with knitting needles, the schemes and description for beginner needlewomen can be put aside. Now it‚Äôs worth starting to prepare the product for wearing. To do this, wash it, dry it and iron it if desired. We‚Äôll tell you how to do it right.

For washing, you need to heat the water to a comfortable temperature, dilute a small amount of shower gel or liquid baby soap in it, stir well. Put a knitted thing in it and leave it for 0.5-1 hour to soak. After that, wrinkle it a little, squeeze it slightly (do not twist it!), Put it on a towel, roll it into a lump and move it to a dry clean sheet. Leave in a warm place (you can near the battery) to dry. Then iron through a moist gauze with a slightly heated iron (in no case hot!).
