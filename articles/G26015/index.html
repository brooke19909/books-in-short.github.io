Description

The Baltiyskaya Hotel in St. Petersburg is a small, cozy establishment with comfortable rooms (number of rooms: 54, capacity: 116 beds), and located in the historical part of the city. The hotel is known for the fact that in its walls guests are guaranteed to find comfort, peace, perfect cleanliness and order. Many guests note that the main advantage of the hotel is its remoteness from the noisy highway, the presence of attentive, friendly staff, quality service and quite reasonable prices. Hotel Creed: a combination of traditional Russian hospitality and modern service.

It offers a cozy cafe and a bar that is open around the clock. Guests come here to have dinner and spend a quiet pleasant evening watching TV. If desired, a cafe can arrange a banquet. The hotel provides a wide range of services for organizing celebrations, banquets, receptions, corporate parties, and provides on-site service. Each room at the Baltiyskaya Hotel provides for new comfortable furniture and modern technical equipment. For business meetings and business events, a conference room is provided.

The hotel was opened in 1990; major repairs were made in 2004. The hotel belongs to the economy class, recommended as the best option for accommodating business people and groups of tourists.
