Cheap hotels for a day in Moscow: list, description with photos, reviews

Moscow daily receives thousands of visitors. Some visit the capital on work issues, others want a cultural time. Almost everyone needs to find a place to sleep. At the same time, not everyone has good financial opportunities to rent a room in a prestigious hotel. The problem is easily solved. Cheap hotels for a day in Moscow offer their services. Below will be described the most popular options.

Mini-hotel "Kashirsky"

Those who want to spend the night comfortably and at the same time save money should pay attention to the hotel located on Kashirskoye Shosse (house 9), near the Kashirskaya metro station. For a daily stay you will have to pay at least 950 rubles. For such a price, guests stay in a standard quadruple room. The plus is that smoking is not allowed in the room. Therefore, guests here will feel quite comfortable.

The hotel also has a single room. You will have to pay extra for accommodation here. Apartments for couples are also available. For a room with a large double bed you will have to pay about 1,500 rubles. In this category the hotel has six rooms. There are also eight family rooms with two double beds. It is possible to install two extra beds.

An inexpensive hotel in Moscow for the night is an ideal solution for those who come to the capital on business. As a rule, such hotels have everything necessary for a non-long-term stay. Hotel Kashirsky offers free parking. There is free internet here. Each room has a refrigerator, TV, electric kettle.

About a mini-hotel, guests usually leave positive feedback. It is noted that it is always clean and comfortable. Some customers complain of the presence of an unpleasant odor of dampness.

Hotel Katyusha

The hotel is located on Kibalchich Street, house 9. In the immediate vicinity is the VDNKh metro station. The most popular are single and double rooms of category standard. If you consider cheap hotels in Moscow for a day, you can pay attention to this place. For the night you will have to pay about 2,000 rubles. Each room has comfortable single or double beds. This hotel is especially popular in the summer due to the availability of air conditioning. All rooms are stylized with Russian wooden motifs.

For family living, a two-room number (apartment) is suitable. Customers can use an iron and watch TV. Each room has free internet.

A lot of good reviews can be heard about the deluxe room of the Katyusha Hotel. These rooms also have a hairdryer, bathrobes, hygiene items.

"Semenovsky hospitable house"

This is a popular mini-hotel in Moscow. Inexpensive for a day everyone can stay here. However, pre-booking is recommended. Mini-Hotel Izmailovsky

This place is worth considering for those who are looking for a cheap hotel in Moscow. Nearby is the Izmaylovskaya metro station. Hence the name of the hotel. The exact address is Verkhnyaya Pervomayskaya street, house 6. For daily accommodation at the hotel you will have to pay about 900 rubles. The most popular are the rooms of the economy category. Plus, in each room there is a wardrobe, as well as a desk. This number is ideal for people coming to Moscow on a business trip.

Guests often leave positive feedback about a hotel. The disadvantages include poor sound insulation, poor cleaning of the bathroom in some rooms.

Halva Hotel

Many have to come to the capital for just one day. But in this case, they may need a quality rest. Visitors have the opportunity to rent a hotel for an hour in Moscow inexpensively. The Halva Hotel, located near the Cherkizovskaya metro station, is popular. The exact address is 11 Amurskaya Street, Building 2.

For an hour in a comfortable room you have to pay 300 rubles. This price includes the use of a comfortable double bed, a clean bathroom, and wireless Internet. If you need to stop for a day, you will have to pay 2,000 rubles.

Chocotel

This place is worth paying attention to those who are considering cheap hotels for a day in Moscow. There is also an option for accommodation for one hour. For a daily stay you will have to pay from 2000 rubles, for an hour - 550 rubles. The highlight of the hotel is the original design of the rooms. You can rent a room in Japanese or British style. A lot of good reviews can also be heard about the elegant French-style room. But a daily stay here will already cost more (from 4000 rubles).

Each room has a cozy double bed, TV, refrigerator, wireless Internet. For an additional fee, you can install another bed. Address: Perovskaya street, 66b, building 1.

Hostel "Three Penguins"

Hotels in Moscow are inexpensive for two in large numbers. But most often they are located far from the center. An exception is the Three Penguins Hostel. It is located next to Pushkin Square. The exact address is Pyatnitskaya St., 20, building 1. For a daily stay, one person will have to pay at least 1000 rubles. This amount includes the ability to use free parking, wireless Internet, a library and board games.

On the network you can find mostly positive reviews about the hostel. The pluses include comfort, convenient location, a good price-quality ratio. Cons are also present. Guests note that hot water periodically disappears in the hostel. Many also complain about poor-quality cleaning of bathrooms.

Near each bed there is a bedside table for storing personal belongings. For a surcharge, you can use the safe.

Double room of the second category is popular. For living in it will have to pay more than 1,500 rubles. This amount includes the ability to use wireless Internet, an electric kettle, and an iron.

For couples we offer a comfort room with a large double bed. In such a room there is also air conditioning and a refrigerator. In this category, the hotel has 10 rooms.

Reviews show that hotel guests are attracted by the polite staff and the cleanliness of the hotel. Cons include the lack of an opportunity to eat, poor sound insulation.

Hostel Olympia
