The key to coziness and comfort - interior decoration of a wooden house

So, you became the happy owner of the building, which you have long dreamed of. Now you have to take the next step and begin decorating, which will embody all your imagination.
If you start decorating a combined wooden house, then for its decoration you can use a combination of natural materials from wood, brick and wall paneling. Successful interior decoration of a wooden house will correspond to your taste and character, will delight the eye and soothe your emotional mood.
Currently, there is a huge selection of wood on the market and the interior decoration of a wooden house with exotic woods will give a fantastic look to the interior. At all times, wood has been highly valued for its natural qualities, which allows you to get a suitable set of architectural and technical style solutions for interior decoration.
Interiors using wood species have a positive effect on health. Natural wood perfectly regulates the microclimate of the house due to breathability. The interior decoration of a wooden house with wooden structures allows you to maintain a balance of humidity in the room. Therefore, many consumers want to build an environmentally friendly house of wood and create the necessary interior in it. The interior decoration of a wooden house will help you give the room the most fantastic interior and will cause a feeling of delight from the uniqueness of the pattern of valuable species of wood.
Each element of the house has its own individual character. You can emphasize the beauty of a wooden house using a large number of carved wooden furniture, the presence of fine carvings on frames and railings. Finishing welcomes the presence of handmade individual carved elements that will perfectly decorate your home. Very well combined with solid wood furniture and rich wooden shades. Wood aging technologies are used to decorate false beams.
Wooden log cabins also need a complex of finishing works, which include finishing ceilings, walls, doors, floors and windows. The interior decoration of the log house is strictly linked to the wiring and installation of sewage and water supply systems, electrical work. We have to consider the placement of technical elements.
The interior decoration of the log house includes a large segment of work on the processing of a log for a skeleton, polishing the walls and treating them with an antiseptic. flooring with laminate or carpet. Parquet with wooden skirting boards, various sills, fillets looks very good. Installation of decorative doors and windows, decoration of various details.
The lining has found its application in the interior decoration of log cabins. Of course, external decoration is also required. It is necessary to perform works on facing with finishing materials. Painting is used to protect the surface of walls from the effects of the external environment. They use artificial decorative stone and vinyl siding, natural stone panels and many other materials.
Attic decoration has its own characteristic features. The interior decoration of the attic begins with flooring, sheathing, the room is finished with the creation of the necessary layout. Often, the attic is sheathed on the rafters - the room becomes more spacious, but the cost of materials and costs increase. Mostly the attic is frame, respectively, and the partitions are made frame - shield. Mineral insulation for sound insulation is laid in the partitions. It is possible to finish the attic with a pine lining and a beautiful wooden window. A wooden staircase with balusters is used.

Ultimately, the choice of finishes will be left entirely to your imagination.
