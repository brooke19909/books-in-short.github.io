MegaFon: roaming abroad. Reviews, tariffs, how to connect

People often travel outside their country on a trip or in the interests of business. It is very important for such subscribers to keep their home phone number so that they can use their number as usual.





Buying a SIM card in another country is extremely unprofitable, since in most cases local communication is completely unnecessary, and calling home in Russia will cost a lot of money.

For its subscribers, the MegaFon mobile operator provides various services when traveling abroad for the convenience of communication. The cost of calls and the mandatory monthly fee in these cases depend on the country where the person travels.

Is the SIM card suitable?

First of all, it is necessary to find out in advance whether this SIM-card can be used in roaming outside of Russia. To do this, the subscriber must be connected to one of the basic services without a subscription fee, International Roaming. If it is not connected to the SIM card, the number will not work, even if it has all the necessary services. The fact is that this opportunity from MegaFon just provides the subscriber with such a function as registration in the guest network in the country where the person went.

The SIM card must be registered with a Russian citizen. If a person has a residence permit, no citizenship and temporary residence permit (foreign citizen), on the SIM card, in principle, there is no possibility of connecting this service with the MegaFon operator.





It is possible to activate roaming abroad only in the sales salon (store) with the presentation of the passport of the owner of the number. It is necessary to take care of this in advance, because without the owner of the number or in the contact center the service is not activated, and without connecting the SIM card will be inactive.

Roaming connection

For international roaming in MegaFon there are services for both talking and surfing the Internet. However, it is not recommended to enter the network, since the cost of 1 Mb, even with special discounts, is quite large. Subscription fee

MegaFon does not turn off tariffs in roaming, but leaves it the same, because without a tariff the SIM card is not active. However, if the client connected to the operator subject to a monthly subscription fee, then the money will be charged regardless of whether the tariff can be used or not. Also, the rest of the "home" services.

Perhaps, if the subscriber wants, they should be turned off for a while, and then re-activated. For the client, this can be a significant savings. For example, the total charge per day for two activated services is 12 rubles. A person travels abroad for 14 days. A total of 168 rubles will be written off for those services that he cannot even use. Reconnecting options will cost 30 rubles. Total, the subscriber will save 108 rubles.

Thus, MegaFon (and its partner in any country) does not support home options and tariffs in roaming. The company does not return money for those days when the subscriber could not use the services, even if these are options with Internet traffic that are completely unnecessary abroad.

Online Registration

Connecting MegaFon roaming will be useless if the subscriber has gone to one of the countries where there are no partner companies of the operator. A SIM card can be used only in 216 countries of the world, while there are a total of 251 states as of 2015.

For example, if a person went to Eritrea, which borders Sudan in East Africa, then there he will not be able to use the MegaFon SIM card.

Roaming abroad in the country where the client travels, necessarily provides for the presence of networks in which you can register. The presence of communication can be clarified in the contact center or in the MegaFon salon upon activation of the international roaming service.





At the entrance, the telephone itself must find one or more networks available for registration. After that, the user selects which one he will use the connection in. In roaming, it does not matter at all which network the subscriber will prefer, since the cost will be the same in any of them.
