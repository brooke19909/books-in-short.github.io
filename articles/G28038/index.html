Pension "Sun Stone", Crimea

Crimea is a wonderful unique place, which is very popular among vacationers. One of the best areas for recreation is the vicinity of Sudak. This place can offer tourists a lot of different hotels, guest houses and tourist centers. And quite popular is the boarding house for vacationers, "Sun Stone". What is this base? What services does it offer? These questions interest many travelers.

Where is the recreation center located





Pension "Sun Stone" is located on the Black Sea coast. Nearby, in the Kapsikhor Valley, is the picturesque village of Morskoye. The Sudak-Alushta highway runs nearby, so transport problems are unlikely to arise. And the distance to the famous large tourist city of Sudak is 16 kilometers.

The soft sound of the waves, endless vineyards, surrounded by mountains - this is what this land can offer its guest.

Description of the boarding house





The recreation center "Sun Stone" is a large tourist complex, which includes not only residential buildings, but also a park area. The boarding house is about 30 hectares, and most of it is occupied by a beautiful park. It is worth noting that on the territory of the base there are about five thousand exotic plants belonging to 124 different species. Moreover, on one side the guesthouse overlooks the sea, and on the other it is surrounded by forest and mountains.

“Sun Stone” is literally immersed in lush greenery, and the efforts of talented landscape designers have made it a real work of art.

What do base numbers look like?





There are several residential buildings at the base. Pension "Sun Stone" offers its guests check-in in one of 82 rooms. It offers apartments of the following categories:

  • One-room suite class with an area of ​​52 square meters. meter. This room has all the necessary furniture, including a comfortable double bed, a soft sofa, a table with chairs. There is also a necessary set of household appliances, including air conditioning with individual control system, a small refrigerator and a TV. A kettle and kitchenware are also included. In the bathroom you will find a large comfortable bathtub, where you can not only relax in the hot water, but also take a shower. There is also a toilet, washbasin and a large wall mirror.
  • There are also two-room junior suites with an area of ​​either 42 or 60 square meters. Large rooms are perfect for families. Of course, the apartments have the necessary set of furniture and a fully equipped bathroom.

It is worth noting that each room has access to a small private balcony with breathtaking views of the sea, mountains and the park’s park area. The rooms are always clean, as cleaning is done daily. Sheets are changed once a week.

Food scheme for vacationers





Of course, the boarding house "Sunny Stone" (Sea) offers its guests full three meals a day. All dishes are prepared here, in the dining room of the tourist base. Regardless of the degree of occupancy of the boarding house, in the dining room there is always enough space for all guests. The food here is varied, and the menu includes not only traditional dishes, but also some goodies of local cuisine, as well as special dietary food.

It is worth noting that you can eat in the village of Morskoye. "Sun Stone" is located next to the mass of catering establishments. You can easily find a cozy cafe or a luxurious restaurant to your liking.

Beach and water activities





A wonderful pebble beach is adjacent to the pension. Here you can always have a fun and pleasant time, basking in the rays of the warm sun or swimming in the azure clear waters of the Black Sea. Of course, there is the opportunity to engage in water sports, including some extreme forms of it. For example, tourists can go water-skiing or banana, take a boat trip, etc. By the way, the recreation center has its own boat for excursions along the nearby shores. But it is worth noting that these entertainments are not included in the cost of living on the territory of the Sun Stone Guesthouse, you have to pay for them separately.

Are there any conditions for living with children?

Most of the vacationers on the territory of the “Sunny Stone” boarding house are families with children. The rooms are large, and if necessary, you can deliver an extra bed for the child. As for nutrition, guests are also offered diet meals, which are quite suitable even for kids.

Children like to spend time in the boarding house. Especially for the youngest guests, a rather large playground was created at the base where you can play, meet other children and have fun. There is also a children's room, so parents always have the opportunity to leave their children under supervision for a while. And the child will surely enjoy the Cheburashka children's cafe, where the smallest visitors are offered a huge selection of sweets and delicious desserts.





Entertainment in the pension

In fact, the base "Sun Stone" can offer vacationers a lot of ways to have a good time. Those tourists who are accustomed to follow the figure and maintain physical fitness will like a gym furnished with modern equipment. There is also a sports ground where you can play futsal or basketball. These services are included in the room rate.

Of course, there is a paid service. For example, in the pension there is a large tennis court where fans of this sport can have a good time, and beginners can take a couple of lessons. For a small fee, you can use the guesthouse's car park, which is convenient for tourists arriving by their own transport. There is also a large cinema, where you can also spend time watching an interesting movie.

You will always have the opportunity to play billiards. The pension also has a modern sauna with a large pool. And very close to the recreation center there are shops, a bustling market, Internet cafes, as well as massage rooms, a beauty salon, lots of restaurants and cozy bars.

By the way, near the beach there is a beautiful promenade with an evening lighting system and comfortable benches. Here you can have a wonderful time strolling along the beach and enjoying the breathtaking scenery. A disco was also built nearby, where young people can have great fun with incendiary music.

Of course, this is far from all that “Sun Stone” can offer. Crimea is rich in sights, not to mention the breathtaking scenery and amazing nature. For holidaymakers regularly arrange excursions in the surroundings, including boat trips. For example, you will have the opportunity to see the Golden Gate of Crimea, as well as the New World and many other, no less interesting places.



Holiday Village "Sun Stone": reviews of tourists

It is immediately worth noting that almost all the reviews of vacationers are positive. By the way, most of the guesthouse's guests eventually become regular customers. Indeed, the beautiful nature, proximity to the beach and the clear sea make this place truly ideal for relaxation.

Tourists primarily note the convenience and comfort of rooms where it is really pleasant to spend time. Yes, and there is always an occupation here, ranging from swimming in the sea and ending with exciting excursions. They also praise the work of the dining room, where there is always enough freshly prepared and tasty food. The work of the maintenance staff is also up to the mark - all the employees of the boarding house are distinguished by exceptional courtesy and courtesy. Rest on the territory of the "Sun Stone" leaves an extremely positive impression, and the relatively low cost of living is also a considerable advantage.
