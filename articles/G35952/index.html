Biography

Marvin Himayer was born on October 28, 1951. He graduated from the school in 1968. A year later he went to serve in the US Air Force. March 17, 1971 he was sent to fight in Vietnam. There he served at the air base as a senior aviator. After 4 years, he was demobilized, and he returned to his homeland, in South Dakota. Marvin's acquaintances describe his character in different ways. Some speak of him as a good and friendly guy, while others consider him unreliable, too suspicious and dangerous.





Advocate of the legalization of gambling

Despite the fact that Himeyer did not live in the town of Granby for long, he managed to make both friends and enemies. His character was very controversial. Take, for example, the case when, as the owner of a silencer repair workshop, he once threatened his client to kill her husband if she did not pay him for the repair.

His friends said that if you have a friend, it is better not to find Marvin, but if he decides that you are an enemy, it is difficult to imagine a more dangerous opponent than him.

Conflict situation

The life story of Marvin Himeyer in the town of Granby at first did not portend anything tragic. So far, the cement plant, the walls of which was his workshop, has not decided to expand. The fact is that not far from Gramby was located the winter resort of Aspen. Millionaires like to come there, and each of them wants to have his own home there. The fashion for winter cottages provoked a construction boom, as a result of which the demand for cement increased.

In 2001, city authorities and a special commission approved the construction of a new cement plant by Mountain Park. After that, the owners of the enterprise began to buy nearby land. Their methods, to put it mildly, were not always legal.





The cement company paid an average of 50 thousand dollars for each of the sites, but Himeyer refused to sell his land at that price and asked for 270 thousand. When the buyers agreed, he raised the price to 500 thousand, and then to 1 million. Here are the owners decided to find a council on him.

Last straw

He wanted permission to install a sewer, but the land owner refused him.

Seeing such disrespectful attitude on the part of the official authorities, he buys a decommissioned dozer. Marvin Himeyer repaired the engine with his own hands and decided to pave his own road to his workshop, to bypass the factory territories. Preparation for war

As already mentioned, in the spring of 2004, Marvin's father died. His younger brother's wife later recalled that Himeyer behaved at the funeral a little strange, as if he came to say goodbye to all relatives. In addition, she thought that he did not want to leave at all.

Marvin began work on improving his bulldozer almost immediately as soon as he was refused permission to build a driveway to his workshop. He drove his tractor, which he planned to use to lay the road, into the garage and proceeded to modify it.

Modernization

First, Himeyer decided to strengthen the cabin with the engine. To do this, he installed home-made composite armor, which he made of steel sheets and cement poured between them. After that, he equipped several embrasures around the control center itself, and also installed one video camera in front and behind, and then displayed their image on the monitors in the cockpit.

Chimeyer Marvin John prepared very carefully, so he transferred small supplies of food and several bottles of water to the tractor cab. He did not forget about the cylinder with air, gas mask and weapons. He had a carbine, a rifle and a revolver.





As expected, he carried out the modernization of his bulldozer from three months to a year and a half. Surely Himeyer was struck by the fact that none of the casual visitors to the workshop was surprised or worried when he saw such a powerful armored car.

Before the war of Marvin Himeyer began, he specially recorded his message on several tapes. On them, he left an explanation of his act.

Granby City

Despite the fact that it was quite difficult for Marvin to drive such a bulky and clumsy vehicle, he managed to find and destroy his intended goals.
