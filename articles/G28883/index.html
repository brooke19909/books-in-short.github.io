Hotel Bamboo Beach Hotel & Spa 3 * (Phuket, Thailand): description and photos

Planning to visit Thailand? Did you choose Phuket as your vacation destination? Looking for a cheap hotel near the beach? Then Bamboo Beach Hotel & Spa 3 * can be a very suitable option for you. About what is offered to guests here, and we will talk further.



Location

Bambu Beach Hotel & Spa is located near the secluded Kalim Beach on the west coast of Phuket (Thailand). In its vicinity there are a lot of cafes, bars, shops, supermarkets, travel agencies, rental cars, massage parlors, as well as markets, shopping centers, pharmacies and other tourist infrastructure. Guests can walk to the popular entertainment street in 10-15 minutes on foot. The nearest airport is 45 kilometers away. So, upon arrival, you can reach the hotel in less than an hour. By the way, the hotel offers a shuttle service to the air harbor (surcharge).



General information, photo

Bamboo Beach Hotel & Spa 3 * (Phuket) first opened its doors to tourists in 2010. By the way, in some travel agencies it belongs to the category of not three-star, but four-star hotels. Its area is 2200 square meters. meters. She, according to travelers, is very well-groomed, there are a lot of greenery, palm trees, bright flowers.

The Bambu Beach Housing Fund is represented by 38 comfortable rooms located in two two-story buildings. The hotel has an outdoor pool with an adjacent sun terrace, equipped with sun loungers and parasols. Hotel guests can also visit the spa, which offers various beauty treatments and excellent massages. In addition, there is a pool table for guests. There is also a restaurant and a bar.

Rooms

As already mentioned, in total in Bamboo Beach Hotel & Spa 4 * there are 38 apartments. They are represented by the following categories:

  • Superior (area 30 sq. Meters, maximum occupancy - 2 people).
  • Deluxe (42 sq. M, double bed).
  • Executive (38 sq. M, double bed).
  • Family (53 sq. M, maximum occupancy - no more than 3 people).

Regardless of the type, each room is equipped with everything necessary for tourists. So, here you will find comfortable furniture, a TV with satellite channels, a filled minibar (you can use its contents for an additional fee, the price list is in the room), air conditioning, telephone, safe, bathroom, tea set, terrace or balcony. Free Wi-Fi is also available. A cot can be added to your room upon request. It is advisable to notify the hotel in advance of this need. According to our compatriots staying at this hotel, furniture and appliances are far from new. However, everything is in good working condition. If something fails, then be sure that after contacting the reception the problem will be fixed as soon as possible.

Cleaning service

Our compatriots, according to them, were quite satisfied with the work of the Bambu Beach maids. So, they regularly cleaned quite regularly, changed towels and bed linen, and also replenished supplies of personal hygiene in the bathroom. So the rooms were always clean and comfortable.

As for the rest of the staff at Bamboo Beach Hotel & Spa 3 * (Thailand, Phuket), then, as the guests note, they are all very friendly, hospitable, attentive and always happy to help. Please note that almost no one speaks Russian here. Explained, as a rule, comes in English.



Nutrition

The hotel has a restaurant. Here you can both have breakfast (included in the price) and eat during the day for an additional fee. By the way, hotel guests in the restaurant receive discounts. According to tourists, the food here is very tasty and of high quality, and the prices are sometimes lower than in a cafe outside Bambu Beach. So many of our compatriots enjoyed visiting the hotel restaurant not only during breakfast and enjoyed Thai dishes and excellently prepared seafood. The restaurant is open from 6:30 to 24:00. The hotel also has a bar where you can enjoy your favorite drink.

Beach vacation

As already mentioned, Bambu Beach Hotel is located on the beach. So, the distance to the sea is only a couple of hundred meters. Here is a quiet calm beach. However, according to tourists, it is poorly equipped, so most travelers prefer to visit other beaches. The closest is Karon Beach. It can be reached in just 15 minutes using the free shuttle service provided by the hotel. According to tourists, there is a well-developed infrastructure, many beach cafes and bars and a variety of entertainment for every taste. Experienced travelers are also advised to definitely visit the islands. You can get on them by ferry or boat. The beaches are magnificent and the water is very clear.
