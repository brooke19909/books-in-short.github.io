Sights of Astana: description, photo

The top ten largest countries in the world includes the Republic of Kazakhstan. An independent, distinctive and rapidly developing country with more than 120 different nationalities. The region is famous for its rich natural heritage, as well as water expanses and economic potential. The capital of Kazakhstan is Astana - a hospitable city with an amazing history.

More than 400 construction organizations took an active part in its beautification, which made it possible in a short time to create a modern metropolis with unique architectural objects in the Asian style. Sights of the city of Astana are multifaceted, interesting and unique.

You can inspect them in a few days, even without your own car: there are no problems with public transport. To make it easier for tourists to navigate, the article will describe the outstanding objects of the city, which it is recommended to get acquainted with. So, we present to you the main attractions of Astana.

Symbol of the Republic - Baiterek

The most popular tower with a height of more than 100 meters symbolizes the tree of life, prosperity and solid support. A huge ball of gold color weighing about three hundred tons was built on its top. The construction is a great asset of an independent state, it is depicted on various souvenirs, coins, postcards.

But do not assume that the other attractions of Astana are less attractive. From the top of the powerful monument, a bewitching panorama of the surroundings of sovereign Kazakhstan opens. Every day crowds of vacationers come here to touch the palm print of President Nazarbayev, take pictures and make a wish.



A mold is located inside a transparent ball, there are also several cafes, a gift shop with a wide range of national symbols. The tower has an aquarium and an informative gallery, presented by art exhibits of famous masters. Halls often host cultural and business events.

Mosque Nur-Astana

Sights of Astana are interesting in architectural and historical structures. The Muslim temple of Nur-Astana, which is famous for its size (the second largest after the Hazret of the Sultan), received special attention. A mosque was erected not so long ago, in 2005, according to the project of architect Hafiz. According to official figures, about seven billion dollars were spent on the construction.



The materials used were granite, glass, steel and concrete. The result was a modern eastern building, distinguished by grace and beauty. The gilded dome is crowned with 4 minarets. The interior adores luxury and size. At the same time, 5,000 men can be in the hall. For women, a spacious balcony is highlighted.

Palace of Peace and Accord

Guests of the city can see the sights of Astana (photos are given in the material), such as the Palace of Peace and Accord. This is a huge structure in the form of an Egyptian pyramid, proudly towering over the capital. It is also called the eighth wonder of the world. The palace was built in 2006 by a professional architect from Britain. The main purpose of its reconstruction is to hold business conferences, denominations and political meetings.



The author decorated the roof of the pyramid with 130 pigeons that embody the unity of the Kazakh people. Inside the palace you will see spacious conference rooms, a concert and opera stage and exhibition arenas. The largest building is considered the Cheops Atrium Hall, its area is 2000 m2. It organizes cultural and business events, exhibitions of famous artists from around the world. The hall is surrounded by a stunning Winter Garden with a rich flora.

Architectural object Schoolchildren's Palace

Arriving in Kazakhstan with your children, do not forget to visit the youth center, opened in 2012. The design of the palace is decorated in an oriental style. The whole structure is decorated with national ornaments. Inside there is a huge space consisting of gyms, tennis courts, a swimming pool.



The palace has a ring for martial arts, a planetarium, conference rooms, an observatory and much more that will interest young tourists. After inspection, you can climb to the roof of the building, flowering plants, flower beds are planted here and a panorama of the capital opens up. But this is not all the attractions of the city of Astana.

Khan Shatyr

A bright spot in the modern capital is the entertainment complex "Khan Shatyr", visible from anywhere in the city. The center is divided into several landscape zones. The top floor is an artificial beach, strewn with fine sand. The wave pool is filled with sea water. A lot of tropical vegetation has been planted here, conditions have been created for paradise relaxation.



In the presence of an observation deck, covered parking for cars. You can get to the desired floor by high-speed elevator. A dinopark works for children and adults - this is a corner with prehistoric animals (dinosaurs, mammoths). Every day guests are entertained by dancing, songs. Shopping galleries, fashion boutiques, souvenir shops, beauty salons, cafes, snack bars, business offices are highlighted. The center will have something to do with itself and what to see.

Triumph of Astana

The sights of Astana delight in its scale. A skyscraper has been erected in the heart of the capital, which since 2006 has been a symbol of the state. The height of the structure reaches 143 m. The building was designed according to innovative technologies, equipped with noiseless elevators, individual heating systems, and parking. This is a whole residential complex with 480 apartments.



A skating rink, a 25-meter pool, which is the pride of the capital, was built under the roof of the building. It offers visitors a network of retail stores, Finnish and Turkish baths, a medical clinic, a development center for children. In addition, the building has beauty and massage rooms, restaurants. For tourists, observation zones are open (open, closed). From the site you can see the circus, the center "Duman" and other objects.

The city of Astana welcomes nonresident tourists with hospitality and oriental hospitality. Sights set the architectural integrity of the Kazakh style. Traveling will leave an indelible mark on your memory.
