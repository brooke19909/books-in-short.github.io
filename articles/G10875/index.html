The surroundings of Sochi

The land close to the resort city is so rich in cultural and historical sights that since 1983 they decided to declare it a National Park, in the territory of which any kind of human activity is prohibited.

Special attention deserves the natural beauty of the region. About 40 rivers flow through the area, adding even more beauty and picturesque surroundings to Sochi. The adjacent resort area is dotted with canyons, gorges, caves and waterfalls, which are about 100 in height from 3 to 73 meters.

What to see in Sochi and the surrounding area? An excellent option is the territory of the Sochi National Park and the Caucasian Reserve, along which a fairly large number of ecological routes have been laid. Hiking trails introduce the natural attractions of the Sochi region.
