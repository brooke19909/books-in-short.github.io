Pomorie 3 * (Bulgaria / Sunny Beach) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

On a narrow peninsula, on the Black Sea coast, 14 kilometers north of Burgas, in the bay of Burgas Bay, there is a picturesque small resort town of Bulgaria - Pomorie. The bottom of the sea is shallow, the water in it is clear and clean. Sandy beach stretched over a distance of more than 4 kilometers. The fine sand of the coast is saturated with iron, giving it healing properties. The city is also rich in healing mud. Thanks to all these climatic conditions, the city is perfect for families with children, tourism and treatment.

The average temperature in Pomorie from May to September is 21 degrees. The water in the sea is warm, therefore it is suitable for swimming even in October, providing a chance to extend the tourist season. The strongest solar activity occurs in June and July. Precipitation, as a rule, is small in summer, but autumn is rainy.

Pomorie has a large number of guest houses, hotels, restaurants, cafes and shops. And food prices are relatively lower than at any other resorts in sunny Bulgaria. Thanks to mud therapy, the city is very popular among the population among all places for tourism in Bulgaria and Europe. Due to the large number of hotels, tourists have no problems with accommodation. Mostly popular among the population are three-star hotels, for example Paradise Pomorie 3 or Pomorie Bay 3. Hotel rooms

The rooms of the new 10-story Pomorie Beach building are equipped with air conditioning, a shower, a bathroom, a refrigerator, a telephone, satellite TV and a balcony with sea views.

There are no air conditioners in the rooms of the old building, but many vacationers claim that thanks to the sea breeze, you can do fine without them. The rooms also have a refrigerator, telephone, satellite TV, bathroom, shower and balcony.

Apartments in Pomorie, as well as ordinary rooms, are equipped with all necessary amenities. They consist of two rooms, a bedroom and a living room equipped with sliding upholstered furniture. All apartments have balconies with sea views.

Rooms also include a paid minibar.

As for room service, cleaning is done daily, towels are changed every three days. Visitors note the good quality of the maids.

Hotel Pomorie 3 offers all-inclusive or half-board options for those who wish to try meals at inexpensive local restaurants located nearby.

The breakfast menu usually includes scrambled eggs, fried bacon, vegetables, cheese, several types of sausages and ham, chocolate balls with milk. For dinner, two types of soup are served, potatoes of various cooking methods, pilaf, meat, a menu for vegetarians, dessert. Dinner includes the above dishes plus meat with a side dish. Guests note that they cook fresh dishes every day, although they are repeated, no one is left hungry. Alcoholic drinks are also available for lunch and dinner.

In the all-inclusive system, in addition to breakfast, lunch and dinner, both non-alcoholic and local alcoholic drinks are served in the lobby bar. Pancakes with various fillings, pizza, various sandwiches, puff rolls, coffee, tea and several varieties of ice cream are laid out in the hotel bar at the entrance to the pool.

Health and Wellness Center

In addition to relaxation, many visitors choose Hotel Pomorie 3 because of the wellness center. It successfully treats diseases of the musculoskeletal system, upper respiratory organs, central and peripheral nervous systems, skin diseases, as well as stressful conditions.

The center employs highly qualified doctors who conduct a daily examination from Monday to Friday and prescribe a treatment program.

Visitors of the center will be offered procedures such as: various types of massage, physiotherapy, electrophoresis, ultrasound, VHF, inhalation, magnetotherapy, barotherapy (pressotherapy), mud baths and body wraps.

As for the mud, the spa town is primarily known precisely because of its healing properties. Mud is extracted from Lake Liman, which is located near the city. It is formed at the bottom as a result of complex processes involving various microorganisms. It has a strong odor of hydrogen sulfide, it contains iron, estrogen and silicon.

And with another very popular product, many procedures are carried out in the wellness center - these are meadows. Meadows is a secondary product in the extraction of salt, it is a very salty liquid. It has a vasodilating, anti-inflammatory, muscle relaxant and antibiotic effect.

It is the presence of a wellness center that attracts many visitors, and they prefer small hotels, for example, the Olymp Hotel, Pomorie 3, despite its liveliness.

Sport

For sports enthusiasts, a fitness center, a gym are equipped, there is the opportunity to do both motor and non-motorized sports or play table tennis.

For children

Young guests will also have something to do. The hotel has a children's room, a playground and a children's pool.

Pools

Pomorie Relax 3 has two pools on site: an outdoor and an indoor. The huge outdoor pool is filled with sea water. Sun loungers and umbrellas by the pool are free.

Beach

The beach closest to the hotel is 100 meters away, has a rocky shore, so many visitors prefer located a little further with a sandy beach. Admission is free, but sunbeds and umbrellas will cost 6 euros or 12 leva (national currency).

Due to the presence of a large amount of iron in the sand, it has a dark color, which, in turn, provides a quick sun tan. The sea bottom with a slight slope is very convenient for swimming.

What to visit

For those who temporarily want to leave the Pomorie 3 hotel, Bulgaria has prepared many surprises. The town of Pomorie will offer a huge number of restaurants and cafes, reviews of tourists mention that the portions in them are very large, and the food is tasty and inexpensive.

As for excursions that can be enlightened at the Pomorie 3 hotel, reviews are advised to visit a 1-hour pirate boat excursion, to go on a wine tour, which is very popular among tourists who conduct tastings of famous Bulgarian wines. You can visit the Bulgarian village, go to the water park Nessebar or Action and get a lot of fun. The choice of excursions is very large, there is something to see.

Sunny Beach Bulgaria is a great place for a sailing trip, ships are leased. Or for fishing, diving, windsurfing or parasailing.


Price of rest

According to the price list of Pomorie 3 (Sunny Beach / Bulgaria) for 2015, a double room without a sea view and without air conditioning will cost visitors from 24 to 38 euros per day, depending on the month. Double room with sea view and air conditioning - from 29 to 46 euros. Single room without sea view and without air conditioning - from 21 to 35 euros, and with air conditioning and view - from 26 to 43 euros. Apartments - from 56 to 72 euros. Children from 3 to 11 years old are offered separate beds for 11 euros. For children under 2 years old they provide a free extra bed.

The amount includes overnight, buffet breakfast, use of seawater pools, sunbeds and umbrellas by the pool, as well as a resort fee. Supplement for half board - about 7 euros. Young children have a very good discount system. Prices at the hotel are much lower than in others.
