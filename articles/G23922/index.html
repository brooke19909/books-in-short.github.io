Club "Plekhanov" in Voronezh: photo report and reviews

Voronezh boasts a considerable number of local attractions. Tourists often visit the city because they want to see monuments, squares and other significant places with their own eyes. It is worth noting that Voronezh is recognized as one of the cleanest and most well-groomed cities. It is pleasant to walk here during the day, and in the evening the city begins to live its own life.

Fans of a pleasant stay and guests already know many worthy options. Among them, it is worth highlighting the Plekhanov club in Voronezh. Here visitors can not only relax comfortably, but also show their talent. Karaoke in the institution is in great demand, so sometimes you even have to wait your turn to sing your favorite song.





Information about the institution

Club "Plekhanov" in Voronezh consists of several floors. Each of them offers guests a pleasant and interesting pastime. For a comfortable stay in the halls there are soft sofas on which guests can relax or order food. On the ground floor can accommodate about 70 people. As for the second level, it is more spacious. 110 people can relax here.

The first level is a bar and a disco. Guests celebrate the chic sofas and the interior in the style of French palaces. Music on the dance floor is accompanied by a laser show. On the ground floor, guests are offered a very extensive menu, as well as a bar menu with a large selection of drinks.





Also, the Plekhanov karaoke club in Voronezh gathers lovers of singing on the second floor. Guests can relax on the sofas while listening to songs performed by other visitors while they wait in line. A selection of songs is provided from the song book, which allows you to choose your favorite hit.

Opening hours of karaoke on weekdays and Sunday - from 8 pm to 6 am. You can sing karaoke on Friday and Saturday from 8 pm to 8 am. Ladies can attend the club for free any day. As for the strong half of humanity, they can also come for free. Exceptions are weekends and holidays.

Menu

Plekhanov Club in Voronezh is ready to please guests not only with good music, but also with delicious food. Here you can taste Russian, Italian and European cuisine. Therefore, if a visitor is tired or hungry, then it is enough for him to call a waiter. Among the popular dishes are the following:

  • Cream soup.
  • Pasta with seafood.
  • Pasta with tomatoes.
  • Roast.
  • Rolls.
  • Risotto with mushrooms.
  • Grilled meat.
  • Spice ribs (pork).
  • Veal medallions with apples.
  • Rabbit in sour cream sauce.
  • Seafood.

These are some of the items that are on the menu of the institution. A glass of beer at the club can be purchased from 240 rubles. The bar card can also please guests. It takes not one, but four whole menu sheets. You can find a lot of cocktails, wines, spirits in it. Guests staying until morning can enjoy delicious breakfast. A check per person per evening is usually from 700 rubles.





How to get to the club

Many citizens, and even guests of the city, know about the location of the club. It can be reached not only by private car, but also by public transport, since many routes go to the destination. The institution is located at the intersection of two streets F. Engels and Plekhanovskaya. These names are familiar to many thanks to historical books.

The exact address of the Plekhanov club in Voronezh: Plekhanovskaya street, 14-E.

Club photo report

Every evening in the institution is always special. Upholstered furniture, warm colors, antique chandeliers and bookshelves allow guests to feel the atmosphere of luxurious and rustic life. Properly selected interior resembles an elite mansion, in which they hold the next cool party. The photo report of the Plekhanov club in Voronezh conveys how the public knows how to relax.

Themed parties are often held here, so visitors and DJs often wear costumes. Each photo perfectly conveys the atmosphere of celebration and fun, for which guests come here. The club has its own photographers, and here you can also notice the employees of the Geometry resource who make frequent reports from events. In photographs, you can often see famous and sought-after DJs in the city and country.





Visitors reviews

Guests of the club have already managed to become many citizens, as well as guests. Their opinion of the club can be found even on the Internet, backed up by photographs. Many reviews of the Plekhanov club in Voronezh are most often associated with the interior and chic furniture. It immediately impresses visitors, as it conjures up thoughts of palaces and bohemia. Separately, guests celebrate karaoke, which makes every evening much more interesting.

The relaxed atmosphere allows you to perfectly express your voice data. A separate part of the visitors writes that the light on the ground floor flickers too much with the music. Many visitors have a pretty atmosphere that prevails in the club. They often come to Plekhanov precisely for her.





Additional services

Club "Plekhanov" in Voronezh can be a great place for a photo shoot. Its design and surroundings are surprisingly suitable for this. Wedding photographs taken at the establishment look especially impressive. Many newlyweds choose this place in order to later recall their best days in the future, looking at the photos. Already many happy couples celebrated their triumph at the club, having previously held a photo shoot and even video shooting here.

The institution also has a hookah, the kitchen is open around the clock. The bar may be closed for a banquet. It offers free Wi-Fi.
