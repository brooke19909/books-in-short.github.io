How to make jeans frayed by yourself?

Jeans first hit the shelves of Soviet stores back in the 70s of the last century. And even then, among the stylish youth, it was decided to modify the classic blue trousers in improvised ways. Jeans were boiled, dyed, bleached and cut. More than half a century has passed, but nothing has changed. If you are interested in how to make jeans frayed and torn, then read our article.

What jeans are in fashion?



Deep blue pants are still trending. However, this model is more suitable for older people. But to the young, they may seem boring and unoriginal. In stores for young dudes, jeans of a very unusual appearance are more common. Some models look as if they had been worn for several years. Others are whitened in a special way, while others are torn.

Our moms and grandmothers are sometimes terrified by modern fashion trends. But young people know that it is worn and ripped jeans, which are difficult to determine their true age, and have been a real trend for several seasons. Moreover, such trousers can even be combined with a classic jacket or a strict shirt. The result is a very stylish look.

How to make jeans frayed?



Option number 1

If you want the illusion of faded paint and a very light scuff, then prepare the following:

  • small board, cardboard or plywood;
  • white;
  • brush or sponge;
  • small capacity;
  • water.

Mix the whiteness with water in equal proportions. Next, we put a board or cardboard inside the leg, so as not to spoil the back surface of the jeans. With a gentle movement of the sponge, apply the bleaching solution to those places that you want to lighten. We recommend that you make a vertical strip along the entire length of the leg, this will visually stretch the figure. Repeat the same steps for the second leg. Now you can rinse your jeans. With undiluted whiteness, draw a thin strip on your pants to get a smooth transition of color. After washing, your updated jeans will delight you with a stylish look.

Option number 2

If you are interested in how to make jeans worn with minimal time, then here is a way. Get the following tools:

  • pumice;
  • sandpaper with a fine abrasive surface;
  • board or cardboard.

Wet the jeans to make the fabric slightly damp. Now put the cardboard or board inside the leg. Pumice or emery paper work on the places where you want to get art scuff. We do not recommend rubbing the knees, as the fabric will tear quickly. Also, do not overdo it, because getting a hole is very simple. After the procedure, wash the jeans.



Option number 3

Perhaps this option will be the most difficult. However, as a result, you will get a very stylish thing, similar to those that are present on the windows of brand boutiques. So, how to make jeans frayed in this more laborious way?

You will need a thick tailor needle and a lot of patience. Gently prying, pull out the transverse threads with a needle. As a result, only longitudinal fibers should remain in the shabby place.
