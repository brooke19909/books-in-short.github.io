Hotel Red Castle Hotel 4 * Sharjah, UAE: reviews and photos of tourists

People who plan to spend a vacation on the picturesque seashore while enjoying eastern exotics at some point are sure to consider the United Arab Emirates as an option . This country ideally combines ancient history, a unique culture, the benefits of modern civilization and breathtaking landscapes.

Quite often, travelers stay at the Red Castle Hotel 4 * (Sharjah, United Arab Emirates). Reviews about this place are positive, because the hotel provides tourists with everything necessary for a truly eventful and pleasant vacation. So, what conditions should you expect? What does hotel accommodation really look like? The answers to these questions are worth exploring before booking a ticket.

Location Information





When searching for a hotel, every experienced tourist pays attention to his location. Where is the lucky traveler to book a room at the Red Castle Hotel Sharjah 4 *? UAE, Sharjah, one of the most beautiful and popular resorts - this is where many people prefer to spend their holidays.

It is worth saying right away that this is the second coastline. Nevertheless, getting to the beach is not difficult, and the windows of the top floor offer a wonderful view of the sea. From the international airports of Dubai and Sharjah can be reached in 20-30 minutes, which is also very convenient. In walking distance - huge shopping centers, shops, restaurants and cafes for every taste.

What does the hotel look like? Description of the territory, data on infrastructure





Red Castle Hotel 4 * is a large three-story building, built in oriental style. This is a fairly lively area. Nevertheless, guests can enjoy the silence, as in the evening it is not too noisy, and the living rooms have a good soundproofing system.

This is a city-style hotel, so there is no own courtyard as such. Nevertheless, terraces and lounges are available - they are located on the roof and on different floors of the building. By the way, the luxurious hall with high ceilings and luxurious decoration is of particular admiration. Here is located not only the reception desk, but also sofas, tables and other furniture necessary for relaxation. Parking is available near the building.

Rooms for tourists: number and categories

What in terms of accommodation does the Red Castle Hotel 5 * offer its guests? Sharjah, UAE are famous for their luxury hotels and this place is no exception. The resort has 147 rooms. Of course, there are rooms of different categories, so you will have plenty to choose from.

Most of the rooms (135) are standard rooms. The choice of tourists are offered rooms with two separate or one large bed. There are also 11 suites that stand out with more luxurious furnishings and the availability of additional equipment. It offers tourists and one royal suite, consisting of several rooms.

Hotel Red Castle Hotel 4 *: photo and description of rooms





In fact, the hotel offers decent living conditions, which is also confirmed by reviews. The rooms are spacious with large windows and luxurious furnishings. There is a desk, a large bed, a spacious wardrobe and, of course, a set of household appliances. Guests can use the TV (it is possible to watch some Russian-language channels), telephone, electric kettle, powerful air conditioning. All rooms are equipped with smoke detectors. Guests are offered free internet access. At your service: a safe, a minibar (can be used as a refrigerator), as well as a music system.

The bathroom is spacious, decorated with marble. The tired traveler will also be pleased with modern plumbing. By the way, some rooms have their own jacuzzi. Guests are provided with towels, soft bathrobes, slippers, soap, shampoos and other hygiene items. The quality of the cleaning does not cause complaints - the maids really keep the rooms clean.

What do tourists offer in terms of nutrition?

What can the guests of Red Castle Hotel 4 * (Sharjah, UAE) count on in terms of food? Reviews of former guests confirm that the food on site is delicious. It has a year-round spacious restaurant with luxurious furnishings. Here visitors are received three times a day, organizing good receptions. The menu in the restaurant is not bad: there are meat and seafood dishes, side dishes, delicious pastries, vegetable salads and fresh fruits.





A traditional Arabian restaurant is also worth a visit, where tourists are always ready to treat local dishes. There is a cozy bar with a large selection of drinks, as well as a coffee shop where you can taste incredibly delicious coffee and homemade cakes.

Beach: distance, amenities, entertainment

Rest on the seashore is just what every tourist counts on, deciding to stay at the Red Castle Hotel 4 *. UAE, Sharjah are famous for their luxurious beaches. One of them is located just a five-minute walk - you just need to cross the road.





The seashore is beautiful. Despite the fact that the beach is public and, therefore, crowded, it is very clean. The entrance to the water is convenient, and the soft sand under your feet feels nice. For a small fee, tourists rent sun loungers and umbrellas, and towels can be requested at the hotel.

Of course, travelers will not be left without entertainment. When doing, hotel guests can ride boats, boats and catamarans. Tourists also love windsurfing, scuba diving and other water sports.

Information on the availability of additional services

Having settled on the territory of such a luxurious hotel, tourists can certainly count on all the benefits of modern civilization. To begin with, it is worth saying that the hotel is often used for various business events. For business travelers, there is a small corner where you can use a computer, printer and other office equipment, receive or send a fax.





There are several conference rooms of various capacities - they can be used for meetings, seminars, negotiations and holidays. By the way, the hotel staff copes well with the organization - you only need to provide employees with information about the purpose of the event and the number of guests.

Throughout the territory, guests can connect to the Internet, and the speed is quite enough even for video calls. There is a pharmacy, a medical office, as well as a shop selling groceries, household items and souvenirs with the hotel logo. Near the reception you can always exchange money at a favorable rate. Guests have free access to an underground car park equipped with a modern security system.

How to organize leisure on site?

It is worth saying that tourists prefer to spend days relaxing on the beach, walking around the city and shopping in the largest shopping centers of the country. By the way, free buses run from the hotel to famous malls, which is very convenient.





Of course, you can relax on site. On the roof there is a large pool under the cover. Here, tourists swim, sunbathe, lying on comfortable sun loungers, and just relax, admiring the breathtaking view of the Persian Gulf. At your service - a fitness center, a small but fully equipped gym, as well as a spa, where visitors are offered a huge number of beauty and wellness treatments, including massage.

Do not exclude from the list of possible entertainment and excursions, because there are many sights of various kinds nearby. By the way, the hotel has a Russian-speaking guide, which is convenient for many tourists.

Is the hotel suitable for a child?

For parents about to relax with their children, the most important questions are about comfort. What to count on when staying at the Red Castle Hotel Sharjah 4 *? Reviews suggest that the hotel is suitable for a beach holiday. The rooms are clean and spacious enough to accommodate with a child, and if you wish, you can ask employees to bring an extra bed or even a cradle to your room (for a fee).

There is no special menu for kids, but among the variety of food you can always choose something healthy and tasty. The building has a spacious games room, and during the season small tourists are received at the kids club. By the way, in the city of entertainment there is plenty of entertainment for children of any age.

Hotel Red Castle Hotel 4 * (Sharjah, United Arab Emirates): reviews and recommendations for guests

Of course, getting acquainted with official information is extremely important. But it is always useful to find out what former guests think about the Red Castle Hotel Sharjah 4 * hotel complex. Reviews in 2017 are positive.

Tourists note that the building is really new - both relaxation rooms and living rooms are pleased with cleanliness and repair. Living here is convenient - there is the necessary furniture and appliances, and there are no interruptions to the Internet. Some guests complain about the uniformity of the menu, although no one leaves the hungry restaurant.

The advantage is the location of the hotel - from here you can easily get not only to the beach, but also to the central part of the city and all possible entertainment. Former guests recommend the Red Castle Hotel 4 * hotel complex (Sharjah, United Arab Emirates), reviews of which are so enthusiastic, are ideal for an unforgettable vacation by the sea.
