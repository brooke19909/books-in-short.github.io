Restaurant "Park of Culture" (Rostov): restaurant description

In each city, new establishments and food outlets regularly open that help modern people pamper their taste buds. Chefs in cafes try to make their dishes unique so that visitors are sure to remember them. "Culture Park" (Rostov) is also ready to surprise its guests with an excellent menu with good offers. The stylish design of the institution is liked by many visitors. It has a pleasant and light atmosphere that contributes to a comfortable stay.

general information

The institution has long been recognized as true gourmets and aesthetes. Here you can eat well and tasty. Each detail of the interior is selected with special attention, so in the aggregate everything looks very harmonious. The cafe belongs to the Ginza Project holding, which has more than a hundred establishments around the world.

For guests, several rooms are designed at once. Each room is furnished with quality furniture and decorated with decorative elements. The largest room is even equipped with a real fireplace. If desired, guests can choose a VIP-zone, it is located in the hall on the second floor. The wine library gives it a special charm. Visitors also like the winter garden and the open veranda, as here you can see various plants and flowers.

Restaurant "Culture Park" in Rostov has an excellent menu, which includes many dishes of Italian, Japanese and French cuisine. In addition to classics, chefs offer to try real delights. Institutional rolls are famous throughout the city.

Very popular seasonal menu. Guests can enjoy delicious dishes with mushrooms. In summer, they can order roast with chanterelles. And with the onset of autumn, try porcini mushrooms in a completely original dish. The average check per person is from 1500 rubles.

A separate bonus for guests is premium karaoke. A karaoke room called "Jelsomino Cafe" always attracts many visitors. Everything is made here under a real castle, you can see book racks, massive curtains. The room is great for parties and music events. Guests can try their vocal skills and listen to guest artists. Within the walls of the institution regularly perform groups from Russia and other countries.

Address

The institution "Park Kultury" (Rostov) is located at: per. Semashko, 51. It is not difficult to find a restaurant, as there is a large green area next to it. Many citizens well know the Gorky park. The nearest stop is called "Semashko Lane". The following transport goes to it:

  • Trolleybus number 1, 2, 22.
  • Buses No. 3, 3a, 7, 67, 70, 80, 88, 98.

Mode of operation

The restaurant is open every day. You can visit the "Culture Park" (Rostov) from 12.00 to 6.00 in the morning.

Additional features

The facility is regularly used for banquets and various celebrations. Guests can choose the most suitable room to rent it. Many newlyweds choose this particular restaurant for their important holiday. They like the good menu as well as the original design. "Culture Park" (Rostov) allows you to hold a chic event at any time of the year. For visitors in summer, a summer terrace is available, and in cold weather they can choose a winter garden. Celebration can be celebrated all night, as the doors close only at 6 in the morning.

The restaurant also welcomes small visitors with their parents. Children's chairs are specially prepared for them.

Guests can combine their free time with a pleasant walk. After all, the culture and recreation park in Rostov is located very close. In the open air, a good appetite always appears, so the townspeople often go to a restaurant. Guests of the city also do not forget to stroll through the park and stop by for a snack.

The institution is very popular with them. Nearby are also: an observatory, several museums and monuments.
