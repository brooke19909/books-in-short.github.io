Arksbank: reviews, history, products and rating

Arksbank is a fairly small credit institution registered in Moscow. Despite its modest size, it has its own network of territorial offices in Russia. The company's business is mainly aimed at attracting deposits from the population and issuing loans to corporate clients.

History

A credit institution was registered for the first time in 1992 and received the name "Bastion". It was originally created as a share-based bank. Already in 1993, the form of ownership of a closed joint-stock company changed.





In 1996, the owners of the bank again changed its form of ownership and slightly corrected the name, now it has received the name Joint-Stock Social Bank "Bastion". The bank worked under this brand until 2009, when the name changed again to Vkladbank. However, this name was used for only about a year and in 2010 it was replaced by its modern name. From that moment, a new round began in the development of the financial institution, and began to receive feedback from an ever-increasing number of customers.

Territory

From the moment of its foundation until the beginning of 2014, the head office of the credit institution was located in Voronezh, but then it was decided to re-register Arksbank in Moscow. From this moment, the credit institution from a regional has turned into a capital.

Despite its relatively small size, Arksbank operates not only in the capital, but also in cities such as Voronezh, Vologda, Belgorod, Chelyabinsk. Previously, the branch network was somewhat wider.





The credit institution does not have its own ATM networks and terminals. But since cards are not being issued, Arksbank does not receive bad reviews for this.

Performance and Rating

The relatively small Arksbank financial rating is also quite low and is in the third hundred Russian credit institutions. Services for corporate clients

The main direction of the credit institution is the servicing of legal entities and their lending. It is for this that Arksbank most often receives positive reviews. Corporate clients, in addition to maintaining accounts and loans, can also use services such as deposits, cell rental, bank guarantees at the bank.

Deposits for private clients

Actively attracts Arksbank deposits of individuals. In the activities of a credit institution, this is the main source of financing. Until recently, the interest offered by the bank was at a fairly high level, but with a change in the economic situation, it also lowered rates. "Arksbank" customer reviews, who decided to use the services of deposits, earned quite different, but most of them are still positive.





It is worth noting that the company does not accept deposits from individuals at its central office. Only legal entities are served there and currency exchange takes place. This is done to optimize the speed of work.

Profitable Deposit

Arksbank offers the ‚ÄúProfitable‚Äù deposit for customers who want to place from 100 thousand rubles or 10 thousand dollars or euros for 1 year. You can open it for 360 or 367 days. In this case, the rate in rubles will be in the first case 9%, and in the second - 10.5%. The interest on foreign currency deposits under the program also depends on the region of opening. In Moscow, having placed a deposit for 360 days in dollars or euros, you can get 4%, and for a period of 367 days - 4.5%. Branches located in other cities offer a rate of foreign currency deposits 1% lower.

It is allowed to replenish the deposit at any time, except for the last 30 days. Income is paid or capitalized every 3 months.

Deposit "Permanent"

For customers who want to receive a monthly payment, Arksbank offers the Permanent Deposit Program. These deposits are allowed to replenish, in addition, the client can independently choose the required period and currency of the account. The rate in rubles is the same for all offices of the bank and amounts to 1.8-10.5% depending on the chosen storage period. In foreign currency, rates will also depend on the region. The maximum rate - 4.25% - can be obtained at Moscow offices.





Deposit "Real"

For those who prefer to be able to partially withdraw funds, deposits under the Real program are suitable. They open for a period of 1 month to 1 year and allow partial withdrawal and replenishment of the account. The rate in rubles depends only on the placement period and can reach up to 9.8% per annum. In dollars, you can get a maximum of 4% when opening a deposit in Moscow offices.

Other services for individuals

Arksbank is ready to offer not only deposits to its customers - individuals. Consumer lending, safe deposit boxes, transfers and current accounts are also available to them.
