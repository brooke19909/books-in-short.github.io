Modern Troy - Dream Waterpark

Nature has given the world many amazing places where it is breathtaking from pristine beauty. One of these cities, attracting many tourists to rest, is the wonderful Belek resort in Turkey. Here, not only luxury hotels, wonderful beaches, exotic vegetation, but also ancient monuments meet vacationers.





There is a wonderful creation of human hands in Belek - an amazing entertainment complex "Troy". The water park is located on the territory of a chic modern hotel, where lovers of family holidays come with pleasure. The uniqueness of this structure is that it is made in the antique style, using a huge figure of the famous Trojan horse. This unusual building is an adornment of the famous resort.

“Troy” is a water park that amazes the imagination of tourists who came to rest with especially large water slides originating from the body of a giant horse. Access to the territory of this park is open daily. There is always a large influx of tourists who have the opportunity to get an adrenaline rush and rush down at high speed.

Thrill-seekers can be advised to visit the most extreme attractions that the Troy water park can offer. The price for visiting this unusual entertainment complex is $ 30, and you can zip around the hill you like an unlimited number of times. A lot of pleasure is provided to you!

Troy: water park for everyone

Indeed, even the appearance of this magnificent creation delights tourists, and adults and children will want to enjoy all kinds of entertainment that is offered in the water park. Vacationers do not need to climb one of the highest slides on their own. A powerful stream of water delivers them directly to a horizontal platform in front of the entrance to the hill, and the impression of such a climb is simply amazing.



For desperate fans of extreme sports, water slides are created in the water park, in which the gutters are at an angle of 40 degrees, which speeds up the descent as much as possible. Here you will not see the queue at the entrance, such a descent obeys only the most daring.

Around the park is an adventure river. Caves and an unusual attraction with a climbing mountain have been built here, from which vacationers throw the same powerful stream of water onto the hill. Right from the hill, daredevils enter the pool.

Troy is a water park in which surfing enthusiasts will also be interested. An artificial wave pool has been created here, where you can enjoy your favorite activity.

Not forgotten about the children here. For them, they created their own mini-water park directly on the ship, where there are small slides and a shallow pool, attractions and games, thoughtful and safe forms of entertainment. An important point remains the fact that absolutely all the fun on the water provides complete safety for young tourists. The fairytale picture is perfectly complemented by ancient armor scattered throughout the territory, evidence of the Trojan battle.



Each tourist makes a memory photo of a trip to the Troy water park in order to be able to show this unusual beauty to family and friends. After all, this wonderful park is still one of a kind. He leaves in the memory of tourists who visited on its territory a genuine delight and an indelible impression of everything he saw.

Belek Resort

In addition, this resort is famous for its beaches, luxurious hotel rooms, high level of service and amazing climate. So those who choose this particular place to stay will not regret their decision.
