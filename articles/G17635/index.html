Hotel Sovereign Beach Hotel 4 *: photos and reviews of tourists

Many people travel to Greece to have a good time and enjoy a beach holiday. She owns such a picturesque island in the Aegean Sea, as Kos. It is here that an excellent hotel complex called Sovereign Beach Hotel 4 * is located. It is located right on the sea coast, just two kilometers from the village of Kardamen and thirty from the airport. It is an ideal place for a romantic getaway. Therefore, it is worth telling about it in as much detail as possible.





Infrastructure

On the territory of Sovereign Beach Hotel 4 * there is absolutely everything that can make the stay of tourists comfortable and diverse. There is a large outdoor pool, a well-equipped gym, a sauna and a jacuzzi, a billiard room, a football field, two tennis courts ... A true paradise for lovers of an active lifestyle!

In addition, on the territory there is a small store where you can buy anything you want without going outside the hotel, and a conference room.

For the youngest guests there is a playground, a separate pool and a mini club. Adults, while their children have fun under the supervision of animators and nannies, can enjoy water polo, ping pong, beach volleyball or something else. As you could already understand, being in the Sovereign Beach Hotel 4 *, you certainly will not be bored.

Restaurants, bars and SPA

This hotel provides all-inclusive services. Delicious fresh breakfasts, lunches and dinners are served in the main restaurant. Greek and international cuisine is served there. The hotel also has a bar that is open until two in the night. They serve a wide variety of drinks - alcoholic and not only. The only caveat: in the case of this institution, the all-inclusive system works only until 23:00. Then - everything is paid.

The snack bar serves light snacks, ice cream, muffins, sandwiches and other gastronomic delights. And in unlimited quantities. And ice cream with drinks is also served at the poolside bar. True, it closes at 6 pm.

Sovereign Beach Hotel 4 * is also good because it has a magnificent SPA salon on its territory. Arriving at this place, you should definitely allocate time for wellness and beauty treatments. And in the SPA center you can buy a package of services. Better - for two. Joint stay in SPA will give an unforgettable experience. By the way, when you buy two procedures, the guest receives a third as a gift.





Service

In Sovereign Beach Hotel 4 * (Greece), excellent attitude towards guests. The staff is smiling and helpful, which cannot but rejoice. In addition, they provide a variety of services that can make guests stay more comfortable.

Free private parking and car rental are available. If a person does not plan to rent a car, then he can use the transfer service. It's free. You will only have to pay for a trip to / from the airport, since it is 35 kilometers from the hotel.

Individual check-in or check-out is also possible. The hotel has currency exchange, photocopying and fax facilities, and those who come to rest with children can take advantage of a babysitting service.

Room service and local fresh press are also available. Guests can be provided with VIP services or family rooms. Facilities for people with disabilities are also available, which is good news. As you can see, the hotel has it all. The only negative is paid Wi-Fi. It costs 10 euros per day here.





Economy Class Numbers

In the hotel Sovereign Beach Hotel 4 *, the photo of which is provided above, there are apartments of different categories. This is one of the main advantages of this hotel, as people of both average and substantial wealth can live here.

Take, for example, standard 2-bed rooms. Their area is 15 square meters. m. In a cozy, bright apartment, decorated in an attractive modern style, there are two large single beds, which look like one huge, king-size. Inside there is a small nice furnished balcony with garden views. In addition, there is a plasma TV with satellite channels, air conditioning, wardrobe, clothes rack, refrigerator with safe and, of course, a bathroom with shower and toilet. In addition, such trifles as an alarm clock, fresh towels, linen and free hygiene products cannot but rejoice.

Such a double room will cost only 65,000 rubles for 7 days. And this is with the all-inclusive service!





Larger apartments

Approximately the same conditions have 3-bed rooms. Their area is not much larger - about 17 square meters. m. Only, except for two single beds, inside there is also a folding sofa. The price of such a triple room is less than 80,000 rubles.

But family apartments (designed for 2 adults and 2 children) will be more spacious. Their area is 24 square meters. m. Inside there are three single beds and a sofa bed. In addition to the above amenities, there is also a bathtub (besides the shower), a wake-up service and ironing facilities. The price of apartments (also for 7 days) is 75 000 rubles.

Bungalow

This is the last category of rooms in Sovereign Beach Hotel 4 *. The reviews this hotel receives are exclusively positive. And all guests recommend booking a bungalow. If there is such an opportunity, of course, since usually they are not empty.

There are two types of bungalows - 26 and 32 square meters. m. Each has its own kitchenette, bathroom, two bedrooms (zoning is important) and other amenities that were mentioned earlier. But only the first option is designed for three people, and the second - for four. Prices, respectively, differ - 83 and 88 thousand rubles for 7 days. In general, the cost may vary - it all depends on the season in which a person decided to go on vacation. In the summer, of course, everything is a little more expensive.

In general, if you want to have a good rest at a very reasonable price, you should go to Sovereign Beach Hotel 4 *. Greece, the island of Kos is an amazing place. Arriving here once, you will want to return again. Especially in this hotel. So say many people who have been here! Pleasing is the good-natured and attentive staff, which, by the way, speaks four languages, among which is Russian. The rooms are cleaned carefully and regularly, replacing towels, hygiene products and linen with everything fresh. This is also a definite plus. The food is always tasty, fresh and varied. All in all, this is a really good hotel. And each inveterate traveler should verify this on their own.
