All Khimki restaurants

Khimki is a city in the Moscow region. It received an unusual name from the river on which it was built. About two hundred thousand people live here. The city has temples, museums, parks. Residents and many guests have places to go and what to see. Khimki restaurants are very popular. There are many for every taste.



Where they are? What do residents and visitors like to order? What are the most famous restaurants in Khimki? What is included in the menu? Can a family of several with modest income afford a trip to a restaurant? In the article you will find all the necessary information on this issue.

Khimki Restaurants

There are more than forty of them in the city. All restaurants in Khimki can be divided into several categories:

  • Quick Service: McDonald's, Burger King.
  • National cuisine: "Vietcafe", "Uzbek samsa and tortillas", etc.
  • Restaurants-clubs: Khimki, Jam.




Low cost restaurants

It is not necessary to have a large amount of money to eat and have a good time. There are restaurants in Khimki where prices are quite reasonable for people with average incomes. We offer the following facilities:

  • Restaurant "Legend" (Khimki) is located on Panfilova Street, house 12. Before entering the hall there is a huge aquarium with fish, from which it is very difficult to tear off children. A special program of games and entertainment will be offered for them. There are also two cafes, one in the open. Prices in cafes are much lower than restaurant prices. "Legend" is a good choice for family celebrations and children's parties.
  • Restaurant "Swan Lake". On weekdays, from 12:00 to 17:00, you can have breakfast or lunch for just 300 rubles. Delicious ice cream and desserts are sold here. In addition to the restaurant, there is also a cafe in which parents with children like to come. The Swan Lake complex is located on the banks of the Barashkinsky Pond. Address: Jubilee Avenue, 5a.

How to get to Khimki

The distance from Moscow is only 19 kilometers. It can be overcome in many ways:

  • By train or train, which runs almost every hour.
  • By bus. Travel time is a little over an hour.
  • By car, you will reach Khimki in half an hour.
