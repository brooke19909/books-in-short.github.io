Hotel Woraburi Phuket Resort Spa 4 *: overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

If you are accustomed to a comfortable stay and have chosen the Thai island of Phuket as your vacation destination, Woraburi Phuket Resort & Spa 4 * can be a very suitable option for you to stay.



General description, location, photo

Voraburi Phuket Resort & Spa is located across the road from the famous Karon Beach. The nearest international airport is 48 kilometers away.

The hotel consists of several four-story buildings, in which there are 205 comfortable rooms. There are apartments of the following categories: Superior, Deluxe, Junior Suite and Suite. Regardless of the type, all rooms have comfortable furniture, air conditioning, a TV with Russian-speaking channels, a safe, a minibar, a hairdryer, a bathroom, a balcony or a terrace.

On the small territory of the hotel there is a swimming pool and a sun terrace, a restaurant, a bar, parking and a spa.
