Nebug - a tourist paradise by the Black Sea

Located near Tuapse, Nebug becomes a place of attraction for tourists there. Attracts them a lot. For example, beautiful nature - 15 km of beaches abutting coniferous forests with valuable tree species. Even in the heat, the air is saturated with useful essential oils of pine needles, the smell of which is mixed with the aroma of the sea.





The sights of Nebug also include a group of dolmens in the valley of the Nebug River and a cascade of waterfalls, the most important of which, Blue, is located in a beautiful shallow gorge. It is called so because the water in the pond below seems completely blue due to the bottom covered with clay of the same color.

From the history of Nebug

It begins in 1864, when fifty Cossacks left the ship with their families and arranged a parking near the seashore. By the time the October Revolution arrived, the settlement had turned into a full-fledged village, which was part of the Black Sea province. Today, Tuapse district is included in it, Nebug is part of it and the administrative center of a rural settlement, in which up to 4 thousand people constantly live.





In fact, the history of these lands begins much earlier. Dolmens made of bronze and copper, found 13 km from Nebug, told historians about the Adyghe tribes who settled here in ancient times. To look at these historical monuments in the Nebug River valley, you need to join an excursion - it is difficult to get there on your own. For lovers of antiquity, dolmens are just a find - very impressive when you touch a thing done many thousands of years ago.

Services and prices

Children under 5 years old can get to the show for free. For large families and combatants, the administration makes discounts when showing the corresponding document. For everyone else in the Nebug Dolphinarium, the price is universal - 600 rubles. Performances are shown every day during the high season, at 11 and 17 hours. Starting in November, when the flow of tourists is noticeably reduced, daily impressions are canceled, and a new schedule is established, which must be specified immediately before the visit.





In addition to the main presentation, additional paid services are offered. You can swim with dolphins or take part in an art auction selling paintings painted by dolphinarium pets right at the time of the performance. For those who win the auction, there is another bonus - sailing in a boat, including with children. Dolphins are allowed to photograph during the performance of tricks, the only thing workers ask for so that the flash is turned off.
