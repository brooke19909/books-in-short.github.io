Queens Bay Hotel 3 * (Cyprus): hotel description and reviews of tourists

Paphos is one of the most famous resorts in Cyprus. Every year, many tourists come here from different parts of the world, attracted by a developed tourist infrastructure, the availability of hotels for every taste, as well as a mass of historical attractions. If you want to stay not in Paphos itself, but in its environs, in order to enjoy a relaxing holiday, but always be able to get to a variety of entertainment in a few minutes, then pay attention to the Queens Bay 3 * hotel.





Where is

This hotel is located three kilometers from Coral Bay. From the major tourist center of Cyprus, Paphos, it is only seven kilometers away. Queens Bay is located on a hill, from its windows you can admire the picturesque view of the azure waters of the sea bay and banana plantations. Literally a stone's throw from the hotel is the village of Kisonerga. There are restaurants, taverns, bars, various shops, supermarkets and souvenir shops. Paphos and other settlements can be reached by regularly plying buses or taxis. At 150 meters from Queens Bay Hotel 3 * there is a clean sandy beach.





How to get there

A great advantage for travelers planning a vacation in Paphos itself or its environs is the fact that the city has its own airport. Several Russian and European air carriers make regular flights here. Queens Bay Hotel is about 20 kilometers from Paphos Air Harbor. So after landing at the airport on the way to the hotel, it will take you no more than half an hour. It is very convenient, especially for those tourists who travel with children. You can get to the hotel from another airport in Cyprus, located in Larnaca. However, in this case, the road will take more than two hours, since this air harbor is 120 kilometers from Paphos. Therefore, if possible, it is better to choose a flight directly to Paphos air harbor.

Description and photos of the hotel "Queens Bay" (Paphos, Cyprus)

Queens Bay first opened its doors in the last century. However, in 2005 it underwent a massive renovation, so today it is a modern and well-decorated hotel, equipped with everything necessary for comfortable accommodation of tourists. Queens Bay 3 * Housing Facility (Cyprus) includes two hundred standard and family rooms. From the windows of most of the rooms, guests can enjoy the sea. The windows of the remaining rooms overlook a very picturesque banana plantation. The hotel has a restaurant, a bar, swimming pools, a sun terrace and a gym. This hotel is perfect for a relaxing family vacation.





Hotel Policies

Check-in of guests staying at Queens Bay 3 * (Cyprus) is carried out after two in the afternoon. If you arrive earlier, then if you have free rooms, they will try to accommodate you as soon as possible. If there are no free rooms, you will be offered to leave things in the luggage room and relax by the pool, enjoy a drink in the bar, have a snack in the restaurant or snack bar, or swim in the long-awaited sea. On the day of departure, release your number must be no later than 12:00. You can pay for the placement and use of additional services at the hotel both in cash and with Visa, Mastercard, Diners Club and American Express cards.





Special conditions

The Queens Bay 3 * hotel is always happy for guests with children of any age. Please note that you will not have to pay extra for placing a baby up to two years old in the additional arena provided. Extra beds may be provided for older children (no more than one for each room). The cost of this service should be clarified with the hotel. As for tourists traveling to Cyprus in the company of their pets, their accommodation is not allowed in this hotel.

Housing stock

As already mentioned, Queens Bay Hotel has 200 standard and family rooms. Each of them has its own bathroom with shower and hairdryer, air conditioning, TV with Russian channels, telephone, safe and balcony. Laminate flooring. Most of the rooms offer luxurious views of the bay. All rooms are cleaned on a daily basis. Sheets and towels are changed three times a week.





Nutrition

When booking a room at Queens Bay Hotel, you can choose the right catering system for you: breakfast only, half board or all inclusive. All main meals are held in the main restaurant of the hotel, where a buffet is served, which is presented both local and international dishes. Alternatively, you can have lunch or dinner at the restaurant by placing an order from the menu. The hotel also has a snack bar with a variety of snacks.

Beach vacation

Queens Bay Hotel is great for beach lovers . So, literally a hundred meters from it is a magnificent sandy beach. There are sun loungers, umbrellas and water sports equipment. You can use beach equipment for a fee. The indisputable advantage of the local beach is its gentle entry into the water, the absence of stones at the bottom, as well as weak waves. All of the above makes this place very convenient for families with children of all ages and those tourists who cannot swim.





Accommodation cost

The price of accommodation in the three-star hotel of Queens Bay Cyprus, according to tourists, is consistent with its category and the level of service provided. So, a seven-day accommodation in the midst of the holiday season in July and August will cost you from 37 thousand rubles for a standard double room, from 40 thousand rubles for a standard double room with sea view, from 44 thousand rubles for an improved double room and from 52 thousand rubles for a triple room. The indicated price includes breakfast. If you select a different power system, the price will change.

Queens Bay Hotel: traveler reviews from Russia

In order for travelers planning a trip to Cyprus to have a more complete picture of the hotel in question, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with the opinions of our compatriots who have already stayed here this year. Looking ahead, we note that the vast majority of tourists were very pleased with their choice of accommodation. They are sure that Queens Bay is an optimal combination of price and quality, and they are ready to recommend this hotel to their friends and acquaintances. But we will understand in more detail.





As for the Queens Bay Hotel 3 * rooms, our compatriots described them as quite spacious and pleasantly decorated. The furniture here is comfortable and in good condition. The technique is all new. Experienced travelers recommend, if possible, checking into a room with windows facing the sea, as the view will be magnificent. There were no complaints about cleaning the rooms, as well as changing linen and towels, since all these procedures were carried out regularly.

The hotel area, according to tourists, is small. It has everything you need (pool, sun terrace, gym, restaurant, bar), but there is no place for walking. Loved the guests own hotel beach. It is sandy and very clean. The entrance to the water is gentle. In addition, there are almost no waves, so this beach is perfect for tourists with children, as well as for those who can not swim.

As for food at Queens Bay Hotel, in general, our compatriots were satisfied with its level. So, despite the fact that some of the breakfasts seemed a little monotonous, lunches and dinners compensated for this drawback, as the guests were offered a very wide selection of meat and fish dishes, fresh vegetables and fruits, as well as desserts. Those who paid half-board at the hotel went to dinner at nearby restaurants, where, according to them, you can eat delicious food at an affordable price.

An extremely pleasant impression was left among tourists and the hotel staff. So, our compatriots were pleased with the fact that despite the fact that most of the guests consist of English and other Europeans, almost all hotel staff speak Russian. Thus, you will not have any communication problems here. In addition, the staff at Queens Bay Hotel is very pleasant, friendly and always ready to help.

As a conclusion, we say that, according to tourists from Russia, this hotel is perfect for family people with children, as well as older travelers who want to spend an excellent vacation in a peaceful place just a stone's throw from the sea. For young people who are accustomed to noisy fun in nightclubs, this hotel may seem boring.
