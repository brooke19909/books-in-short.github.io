The recreation center "Partizan" (Bryansk) is an unforgettable way to spend time!

The recreation center "Partizan" in Bryansk became famous not only in the region, but also far beyond its borders. For guests, excellent conditions have been created with comfortable accommodation, amazing food and memorable entertainment. The facilities in the park hotel are stylized as historical sights of the Great Patriotic War, talented landscape designers worked on landscaping.

Unique features

Arriving at the Partizan recreation center (Bryansk), tourists freeze with delight. Many have the impression that they were in a fairy tale. Just 10 km from Bryansk, next to the village of Belye Bereg, there is a wonderful suburban complex surrounded by a pine forest.





On a landscaped area, buried in shrubs and flower beds between the paths, there is an artificial lake, hotel and restaurant, tents, areas with a wood fireplace for leisure activities and much more.

You can have a great time at the Partizan recreation center (Bryansk) all year round. Beach holidays are offered from May to October.

Blue Bridge, Odessa Club, Watchtowers and other wonders of infrastructure

Guests of the Partizan recreation center (Bryansk) can stay in comfortable rooms of the residential complex. The restaurant serves delicious dishes based on unique recipes from a local chef. It serves a lot of dishes from noble species of fish, and the most delicious desserts can not help but try. There is a parking lot for car travelers, and VIP customers are met at the helipad.





Not far from the Partizan recreation center in Bryansk there is a famous glade where the valiant Schors with his brigade bravely fought against the Nazis. In memory of these events and places of glory of the region, a miniature copy of the Blue Bridge was built on the territory. You can climb the "Watchtowers" made of brick and wood, and explore the area with nice wells, flower beds, howitzers, summer arbors.

Odessa Club is a cozy beach area in the Partizan recreation center in Bryansk. Here you can sunbathe on the sun loungers while the children bathe in a special pool. The bar has a large assortment of light snacks, soft drinks, alcoholic (including elite) products. In the evening, numerous colorings, lights and illuminations are lit, an entertainment show program with a disco begins.

Services, their cost and other useful information

The most popular service, according to reviews of the Partizan recreation center in Bryansk, is fishing. The service costs 1,500 rubles. This includes a place on the lake, the necessary gear and catch of trout, grass carp, sterlet and other wonderful fish. Then tourists can proudly bring their booty to the restaurant and ask them to grill for their family or friends.

Prices at the recreation center "Partizan" (Bryansk) vary depending on the time of year and the number of guests. They need to be specified in advance on the website, booked and paid on the spot. Cancellation penalties will not be charged. The cost of living in a Standard room is an average of 2000 rubles / day.





On the base, they conduct field wedding registration, corporate and family events, have a wonderful rest with the whole family. By the way, there is an interesting free club "Young Partisan", in which young guests are taught useful skills and entertained by animators.
