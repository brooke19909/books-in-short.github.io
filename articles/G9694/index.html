Water park "Hippo" in Gelendzhik: description, prices, address

Gelendzhik is a very popular tourist city in the Krasnodar Territory. Today it has all the conditions for a comfortable and interesting stay. Vacationers can visit one of the three local water attraction centers. One of them is the Behemoth water park in Gelendzhik. What is the difference between this complex and others? What attractions does it have?

Description and photos of the aquacomplex

The Hippopotamus water park in Gelendzhik is located in the very center of the city, it covers an area of ‚Äã‚Äãonly 1.5 hectares. This is very small for water sports complexes of this category. However, thanks to a competent layout and modern solutions, such a small area really has everything that is necessary for a comfortable rest for lovers of water attractions.





The water park has three pools, a large children's water camp for the smallest, many slides for adults and adolescents. On the territory of the complex there is also a cafe and all the necessary infrastructure: changing rooms, toilets, first-aid post. Rescuers are on duty near each pool.

For adults and adolescents whose height is more than 140 cm, the Behemoth water park in Gelendzhik offers a ride on the following slides: Hydrotube, Braids, Multiform, Black Hole, Cyclone and Space Flight. In the children's town, young guests of the complex will find a large shallow pool with fountains and splashes, as well as as many as 5 safe slides.

Cost and visit rules

The Hippo water entertainment complex is open daily from 10:00 to 19:00. Tickets can be purchased all day or from 13:00 until closing time. The water park "Hippo" (Gelendzhik) has the following entry prices: a daily subscription for adults costs 900 rubles, and for children - 500 rubles. Tickets from 13:00: for adults - 800 rubles, and for children - 450 rubles. Children's tickets can be purchased for visitors under 10 years old. Attention: the majority of adult attractions are intended for use by guests of the water park, whose height is more than 140 cm.





When buying an adult ticket, each visitor receives an electronic bracelet. With it, you can pay for the order on the menu in a cafe located on the territory of the complex. The final calculation is made upon leaving the water park. This is very convenient: money and valuables can be left in a storage room. The purchase of an entrance ticket to the water park allows unlimited use of attractions, sunbeds and the entire infrastructure of the complex. Only purchases at food service outlets and gift shops are paid separately. The complex has free Wi-Fi.

Water park "Hippo" (Gelendzhik): address, how to get

The exact address where the Behemoth water entertainment complex is located: ul. Lunacharsky, d. 159. This is the very center of the resort city of Gelendzhik. Near the complex there is a public transport stop, which is called "Hippo Waterpark". Many city buses and minibuses stop here, these are the routes: 1, 3, 5, 7, 21, 40.





The water entertainment complex has its own free car parking. Do not be afraid not to find the Behemoth water park in Gelendzhik - its multi-colored high slides are visible from afar.
