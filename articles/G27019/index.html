Hotel Royal Tower Resort Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Kemer): reviews and photos

Since Turkey does not lose its position in popularity among our compatriots, in the article we want to talk about one of the hotels located between Kemer and Antalya.

A bit about the hotel ...

Royal Towers Resort Hotel Spa 4 * was built in 2011, its area is 11000 square meters. It is located 5.5 kilometers from Kemer, in the village of Kirish. The hotel is aimed at families with children. It consists of one five-story building, which has 138 rooms.



The complex is easy to reach, as it is fifty kilometers from the airport. Excellent location gives the opportunity to visit Antalya and Kemer.

Number of rooms

Royal Tower Resort Hotel 4 * has in its arsenal rooms of several categories:

  1. "Standard" - spacious and bright apartments are suitable for those who come alone or for couples with a child. All rooms are decorated in a contemporary style, and the bathrooms are equipped with everything you need (including toiletries).
  2. Family apartment. These apartments consist of a bedroom and a living room. They are very convenient for families and friendly companies traveling together.
  3. "Suite". The hotel has two luxurious "suites" with a terrace (area 60 sq. M.). The apartments are equipped with a jacuzzi, sun loungers and armchairs.

All rooms are equipped with central air conditioning, a minibar, a safe, a hairdryer, a TV, a telephone and free Wi-Fi. Apartments are cleaned and towels are changed daily. Bed linen is changed once every three days.

Dining at the hotel

Royal Towers Resort Hotel 4 * works, like many other Turkish hotels, according to the all inclusive system. Breakfast, dinner and lunch are served in the main buffet restaurant. The atmosphere of the establishment allows you to completely relax and enjoy pleasant moments on the outdoor terrace. The chefs of the complex prepare for their guests the best dishes of not only local, but also international cuisine.



In addition, the hotel has three a la carte restaurants and six bars, which offer a variety of drinks to tourists.

Sports and Entertainment

The Royal Tower Resort Hotel 4 * (Kemer) has its own Spa and Wellness Center. Within its walls you can relax and enjoy a relaxing massage, visit the sauna and Turkish bath. You can also work out in the fitness room.

Royal Tower Resort Hotel Spa 4 * offers its guests a visit, a tennis court, a gym, a volleyball court, water aerobics classes, a diving center, a game of billiards, indoor soccer, table tennis, mini-golf, and darts.

Complex infrastructure

Royal Tower Resort Hotel 4 * (photo is in the article) has outdoor and indoor pools, parking, car rental. In addition, the hotel has a fitness room, spa, hammam and sauna, hairdresser, beauty salon, laundry, dry cleaning, currency exchange, bars and restaurants. The hotel has a mini market, which sells the most necessary things for tourists. The front desk serves guests around the clock. A shuttle service is available at the hotel.

Children's holiday

The hotel administration takes care of its young guests, and therefore for the kids there is a children's menu, a mini club with animators and a playground. Babysitting services are available for a fee.

Hotel beach

Royal Tower Resort Hotel 4 *, the description of which we provide in the article, is located 150 meters from the coast. It has its own sand and pebble beach with sun loungers and umbrellas. There is also a bar on the beach where tourists can refresh themselves with cold drinks.

A little bit about the resort ...

Kemer resort is located in the coastal area between the mountains and the sea. It includes such villages as Kirish, Goynuk, Camyuva, Beldibi, Tekirova and, of course, the city of Kemer itself. Numerous hotels are located here from the north and south. Kemer is a particularly popular place among our compatriots who adore the all inclusive system. Therefore, the staff in this region speaks Russian. The main attraction of the city is Liman Street, where a large number of shops and shops are concentrated, starting from the simplest and cheapest, and ending with expensive ones. Discos, bars and restaurants are not only in the center of the resort, but also in small villages. For nightlife lovers, Kemer offers clubs: Budda, Infemo, Aura.



The nearest airport to the resort is in Antalya, about 50 kilometers away. Unfortunately, you cannot directly get to Kemer from it. First you should take a bus to the bus station, and then transfer to another bus. Nevertheless, it will not be difficult to get to the destination, since the transport connection between the villages is very well developed. Buses run until late in the evening.

Kemer has become so popular due to its wide pebble beaches. Some of the hotels organized for their guests a sandy coast of bulk sand, but pebbles are still in the sea at the bottom.

Entertainment and Attractions

If you consider yourself an active tourist destination and plan not only a beach holiday, but also a visit to interesting places, then you need to pay attention to Yoruk Park, which is a live ethnographic exposition dedicated to the history of the Turkish people. In fact, it is an open-air museum that can tell a lot about the life of Turkmen nomads. In the park you can see household items, dwellings, nomad utensils and their workshops, as well as get acquainted with the process of creating handicrafts, taste the traditional food of the tribes.



Do not forget about Antalya, if you relax in Royal Tower Resort Hotel 4 *. Tours (one-day) offered by local guides will help you get to know the main attractions of the city.

Not far from Kemer are the ruins of the Lycian port of Phaselis, which was founded in the seventh century BC by the colonialists from Rhodes. Along the main street of the city, the remains of bazaars, sea warehouses, public buildings, a theater, an agora, Roman baths have been preserved. Ancient ruins will be interesting to tourists.

At the foot of Olympos you can see the ruins of another ancient monument - the town of Olympos. Currently, it is a national park of the same name.





Kemer is the highest mountain in southern Turkey - Tahtali. Its height is 2365 meters above sea level. A stunning journey to it will help make the new modern cableway "Olympos", which ranks first in length in Europe and second in the world. The cable car is 4350 meters long. She receives guests daily. You can get to the cable car by shuttles plying from all the villages of the resort, including from Kirish. The climb to the top lasts ten minutes.

Royal Tower Resort Hotel 4 *: reviews

Having a conversation about the hotel, let's turn to the reviews of tourists who visited it. Their opinion will help us make a more correct impression of the Royal Tower Resort Hotel 4 *. Reviews (real) about this complex are contradictory. Some vacationers have unpleasant impressions regarding some points. Others, on the contrary, liked everything. We want to note that the hotel is four-star, and therefore cannot have luxury apartments and some special food.

In Royal Tower Resort Hotel 4 * Standard is the most popular category of rooms. As a rule, people come here looking for a budget vacation.





Tourists note that the hotel area is very small, there‚Äôs not much to go. The hotel rooms are quite good for economy class, with working plumbing and normal furniture. You can enjoy sea or mountain views from the apartment. The rooms on the ground floors do not have balconies, but open terraces with access to the pool. Maids clean rooms daily, some vacationers advise to tip (one dollar) to improve the quality of the process, but this is not necessary.

It is very convenient that in the hotel, as in all of Kemer, almost all the staff speak and understand Russian, so there is no language barrier. Reception workers move guests very quickly. The vast majority of vacationers are Iranians or the Turks themselves, so evening programs are more focused on them.

As for food, then, according to tourists, it is very tasty. Of course, some guests lack a variety of dishes, but in general, the food meets the four-star status of the hotel. Breakfast includes fritters, sausages, eggs, cereals, preserves, cheeses, sausages, toasts, vegetables and drinks. In general, a standard set for a continental breakfast.

For lunch and dinner on the table there is: lamb, potatoes, pastries, chicken, fish, rice, vegetables, pasta, broccoli, salads and much more. Indispensable guests of the buffet are delicious desserts and fruits. Alcohol in the restaurant and bar is free, but this only applies to locally produced drinks. Foreign brands are paid. A similar practice exists now in almost all Turkish complexes.

Tourists also note that the hotel's beach is a five-minute walk away. It is not big and there are not enough umbrellas with sunbeds for everyone, especially in the season when the hotel is crowded with vacationers. On the beach there is a mixture of sand and pebbles; entering the sea is very convenient and gentle. Pebbles at the bottom does not interfere at all. But the sea is incredibly clean and transparent. There are many attractions on the coast: scooters, parachutes, fishing and much more.





Guests note that the hotel always has representatives of the tour operator filling the hotel with vacationers. They offer numerous excursions. In addition, on the way to the shore, you will also certainly meet local guides offering their services. From whom to buy tours, of course, you decide. However, many tourists prefer to travel on their own. Moreover, the hotel is located in a very convenient location, and Kemer's traffic intersection makes it easy to get to anywhere. Experienced tourists are advised to definitely visit Antalya and go to the mountains by cable car. This will fill you with an unforgettable vacation experience. In addition, do not forget about Kemer, which is located very close to the village of Kirish, its sights should certainly be paid attention, breaking away from a beach holiday.

Instead of an afterword

Summing up the conversation about the Royal Tower Resort Hotel 4 *, I would like to note that the complex is an acceptable place for a budget holiday and fully meets the price-quality ratio. This means that it can be recommended for picky people who want to relax on the Antalya coast at an affordable price.
