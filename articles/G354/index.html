"Zodiac signs"

This alley is the result of the work of masters of Alexandria and Donetsk, Ivano-Frankivsk and Kiev. It was opened in the Forged Figures Park in Donetsk in 2005. In the same year, the first national blacksmith all-around championship was held in the city. Great masters competed in professionalism.

"Alley of fairy tales"

The year 2006 was especially productive when the Alley of Fairy Tales opened in the Forged Figures Park in Donetsk. It consists of twelve figures that depict fairy-tale characters beloved by children: Pinocchio and Lesovichka, the Head of Svyatogor and Kolobok, Mukha Tsokotuha and Thumbelina, Samodelkin and Pike, Pan Kotsky and the Rejuvenating Apple Tree.
