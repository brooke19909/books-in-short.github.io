How to replenish the "Corn" card: features, methods and recommendations

Bank card "Corn" from the company "Euroset" appeared on the market for a long time, but still enjoys great popularity. The reasons for the demand are the improvement of the available range of opportunities and the opening for customers of new profitable features and offers. For example, relatively recently cash back was introduced from purchases from the world-famous trading platform AliExpress, Spormaster and many other partners. In addition, now you can replenish the "Corn" card (without and with a card) for free, without additional commissions. At the moment, the holders of this payment instrument have several ways, using which they can easily replenish their money account.

You can use the "Corn" plastic card not only in the territory of the Russian Federation, but also when going on vacation abroad. Currency conversion occurs at the rate set by the Central Bank, so paying for purchases is simple and quick.

Tip for holders

There are several ways to replenish the account of the Corn payment card today. In most cases, money is credited to the balance instantly (if the operator has no problems), but the amount of the fee may vary significantly. Specialists recommend that holders of such payment instruments find an organization that is ready to accept payments to the balance sheet without additional fees. Moreover, such methods exist today.

If the operation requires urgency, then a bank transfer is not recommended for the following reasons:

  1. The payment deadline may be delayed up to 5 banking days.
  2. The probability of "mechanically" making a mistake when filling out the details is high.

Otherwise, each user can choose the most convenient way for him, how to replenish the "Corn" card.

Internet bank

Recently, among the holders of payment instruments from the Euroset company, it has become popular to carry out all organizational operations with settings and topping up an account through the Personal Account. You can access it through the Internet bank on the official website of the program. In it, you can attach any bank card to the account and carry out quick payment transactions. For example, this is the fastest and best way to replenish the Corn card from Sberbank card.

It is important to note that the presence or absence of a commission will depend on the amount of the transaction.

So, a transfer without additional commission will be carried out if the replenishment amount is more than 3,000 rubles. If you pay less than 3,000 rubles, the size of the commission will be 30 rubles. For Visa cardholders without a Visa Direct service, a commission fee of 1%, but not less than 30 rubles.

Outlets

As an option, where to replenish the "Corn" card, you can use any large shopping center, there is sure to find a partner store through which you can replenish your account. For example, the official Kari fashion shoe stores or the offices of Euroset itself, which are available in most large shopping centers, will help in solving the problem. This is the best option, how to replenish the "Corn" card without a commission. As a rule, money is instantly credited to the account.

There is no additional charge for balance replenishment. All that is required is to present the passport and card number for replenishment to the store employee. It is possible to transfer to a card using cash or through the terminal, paying by bank transfer.

It is worth taking into account that amounts over 150,000 are not recommended to be credited to the account at a time, as this may lead to a delay in cash receipts. In order not to experience any feelings and worries in the future, it is recommended to divide a large amount into parts and transfer it to the account every half hour.

Partner Bank Visit

Since the official Euroset offices are not always within walking distance, you can use credit institutions that are partners of the Golden Crown - Loan Repayment system or the terminals of MKB Bank as an alternative. As a rule, replenishment of the account through the offices of such credit organizations is carried out without commissions or with a minimum percentage. You can familiarize yourself with the full list of financial institutions through which you can replenish the card, and the amount of fees charged on the official website of the Corn program.

A money transfer through a bank is a very relevant way to replenish a Corn card. But it is worth remembering that the operation may take up to 5 business days. To speed up the transfer, it is recommended to take note of a few tips from banking experts:

  1. All data must fully coincide with those specified in the contract.
  2. It is not necessary to indicate your own data in the recipient's column, since the organization that accepts the payment will act in this person.
  3. In the appointment it is necessary to write down the card number and the full name of the holder.

Do not forget that this operation is performed by companies specializing in Golden Crown money transfers. Replenishment can be done not only your card, but also the plastic of another person.

ATMs and terminals

You can replenish a card through an electronic payment system or terminals with the well-known QIWI. They also allow you to make a similar operation with Sberbank. To conduct a transaction through the terminal, the cardholder must follow the following instructions:

  1. Go to the terminal menu in the translation section.
  2. Indicate card details.
  3. Indicate the amount of transfer.
  4. Deposit money.
  5. Confirm the operation.

Through MoneySend

Another way to replenish the "Corn" card. This is the system that banks such as Gazprom and Alfa Bank use. To correctly fill out an application for replenishment, it is enough to indicate the following data:

  • sender card number;
  • expiration dates;
  • a three-digit CVV code located on the reverse side;
  • number of the card to which the transfer will be made.

It is worth noting that on this resource they often launch promotions and advantageous offers that will help save on commission fees or get a nice bonus for using services. Enumerations can be made many times, there are no restrictions in this case.

Collateral deposits

Another way to replenish the "Corn" card is to get a loan. Holders can apply for a loan and receive a transfer to this payment instrument through online lending, MFIs or a service connected through the Euroset company.

Conclusion

There are enough options, how and where to replenish the "Corn" card. The holder has a choice, and most importantly, you can transfer money to your account instantly and without commission. Judging by the reviews, more and more users are switching to cashless payments via the Internet bank or the corresponding application for a smartphone.
