Moscow Credit Bank: professional review





Moscow Credit Bank, a review of which can be left on the company's main website, is currently one of the largest financial institutions in the Russian Federation. This bank entered the country's financial market almost immediately after the Soviet Union ceased to exist in the distant 1992, and since then it has been constantly expanding its sphere of influence. At the moment, this organization is successfully operating and occupies a significant position in any sector of the financial market of the Russian Federation. Despite the fact that it is represented only in the Moscow region and Moscow, its branch network is quite extensive. Today it has over 70 different customer service and sales points. Moscow Credit Bank, branches of which can be found in any corner of the capital of our homeland, provides a full range of financial services to both corporate clients and private depositors and borrowers.

Basic Services

At the moment, the financial institution focuses on lending and servicing individuals. The most popular among residents of the capital are mortgage, consumer and car loans. In addition, Moscow Credit Bank, a review of which can be found both on specialized websites and on the main page of the company, is actively engaged in the implementation of its own credit cards. At present, more than 600 thousand residents of the capital have become holders of credit cards to this financial structure.



In working with corporate clients, the bank focuses on servicing various foreign economic contracts, collection services, and international trade financing. To carry out leasing operations, Moscow Credit Bank, a review of which can be obtained from any financier of the capital, has a subsidiary company, MKB-Leasing. Today in the database of this credit organization there are more than 280 thousand private customers and about 25 thousand corporate.

In addition to lending services , the bank provides safe deposit boxes, payments and money transfers to other countries, opening and maintaining current accounts, as well as various investment units.



Since 2004, Moscow Credit Bank, a review of which can be left on any banking site, has been a member of the state deposit insurance program, which implies the return of deposits to depositors if the financial institution is declared bankrupt. This not only confirms the extraordinary strength of the bank's position in the financial market, but also gives its customers a certain confidence in the future. Within the framework of this program, any client of the bank can count on receiving his contribution as compensation from the state. However, the maximum amount of the insured deposit is limited to seven hundred thousand rubles.

Today, Moscow Credit Bank, whose branch addresses can be found by contact phone, provides the highest level of service, and its reliability allows us to consider partnership with it for many years without any risk.
