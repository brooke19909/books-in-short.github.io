On the history of the origin of backgammon

This game is one of the oldest in the world, which appeared in the east. It is known that in the tomb of the Egyptian pharaoh Tutankhamun was found a likeness of this fun. According to many historians, an interesting and entertaining entertainment - backgammon - is older than chess. Their modern varieties have a fundamental resemblance to their distant ancestors. The board is divided into four equal and identical parts, symbolizing the four seasons.

Twelve cells for chips on one side are twelve months of the year. There are 24 cells for chips in total - the number of hours in a day. The location of the points on the bones is in strict order, their sum on opposite sides is 7: 1-6; 2-5; 3-4. There are 15 pieces of chips of one color, the same number of other, dice - 2 pieces.

Game types

There are two types of backgammon - short and long. In Russia, long ones were widely used, and it will be discussed about them in the article. The short game has many nuances and differences, determined primarily by geographical and national characteristics.
