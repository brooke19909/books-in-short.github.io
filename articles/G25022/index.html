The best laser levels: rating, reviews and comparison of models

Laser level - a device for creating perfectly straight lines in several planes. The equipment is widely used in industry, construction and architecture. The device can also be used in domestic conditions.

Principle of operation

Levels form a projection of the laser beam on the surface. The guidelines for the various activities are the points and lines obtained.

Laser marking is used for:

  • Tile laying.
  • Drywall installation.
  • Wallpapering.
  • Stucco lighthouses.
  • Carrying out work on electrification.
  • Furniture manufacturing.

Laser devices are divided into five categories. When choosing the levels are guided by 8-10 factors.

How and which laser level to choose?

When choosing a laser level, they rely on several parameters:

  • Cost. For household use, an inexpensive device without additional functions is suitable. Models with wide functionality are selected for continuous work and are more expensive.
  • Duration of work. For many hours of trouble-free operation, only electronic devices are capable. Pendulum counterparts function for 20 minutes and require recharging.
  • Type of level. The most accurate measurements are guaranteed by point equipment. Linear instruments are used to create a perfectly smooth surface. Rotational levels project vertical, horizontal and inclined planes.
  • Projection range. It is impossible to work on construction sites of a large area with a level of minimum range.
  • The number of projections. For domestic use, a level forming two intersecting projections is sufficient. At large work sites and workshops, a model with a large number of projections is required.
  • Measurement error. Powerful and functional models have an error rate of 0.3-0.4 mm / m. The error of household appliances is greater.
  • Working temperature range. A considerable advantage is the ability to use the level at a negative temperature in an open area.
  • Self-leveling. The best option is a limit angle of 5 degrees, the ability to adjust the compensator for a short period of time. Qualitative models of laser levels are equipped with a function to turn off the compensator.
  • Type of mount. Stable level support is required when operating in an open area. An important parameter when choosing a level is a wall element for installation and tripod mount.
  • Housing protection. It is indicated in the marking. The standard IP54 protection allows you to work with the device in a dusty area and in the rain.
  • Equipment. The standard set includes a bag, flat targets, special glasses, a battery and a charger.
  • Design. It does not affect the quality of work, but the usability of the device depends on it.

Laser levels of any manufacturer are promoted on the market as one of the best, which greatly complicates the choice of a particular brand of equipment. A wide range of levels is presented on the market, however several brands are preferred:

  • ADA. A well-known company specializing in the production of laser equipment: rangefinders, levels, pyrometers and other devices. The products are of excellent quality.
  • DeWalt. Supplies equipment and tools of the highest class. Brand laser levels are distinguished by quality and reliability controlled by engineers.
  • Bosch An ad-free German company renowned for perfect product quality. Laser levels manufactured under the Bosch brand have high quality, reliability and accuracy of measurements. Devices are checked by engineers at each stage of production.
