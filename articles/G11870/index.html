How to make a caps from a newspaper easily and quickly

Very often in our life there are situations when you need to get out on the spot with the help of improvised materials. Imagine a situation when, having gone to a picnic or a summer cottage in the summer, you forgot to bring a cap. And the sun's rays find you even under the trees. Or you started a renovation at home, and in order to prevent dust from falling on your head or drops of paint or glue to fall, you will need a hat. In all such cases, you can get out of the situation with a regular newspaper. Today we will tell you how to make a caps from a newspaper.


To make such a hat is not difficult even for a student. You will need a regular newspaper for this. In principle, any paper is suitable for these purposes: both wallpaper, and the old wall calendar, and even pages from the magazine. The difference will be only in size. So, do-it-yourself caps from a newspaper!

You need to get a regular rectangular newspaper. If necessary, cut it with scissors, giving the desired shape. The first thing you should do is fold the newspaper in half.




Now bend the corners of the newspaper so that you get a triangle.
At the bottom you should have a strip of paper. How to make a cap from a newspaper can be seen in the figures that are presented in the article. The next step is to wrap the free bottom of the newspaper up. Do it first on one side of your future cap. Fold well with your finger to fix the paper. Turn the resulting product over and bend the free edge strip, as you did with the first side.

You have free corners that stick out a bit and spoil the look of the product. What do you need to do with them? They can be bent or glued with glue or tape. And if a stapler suddenly is at hand, he can also help you with this. How you fix the corners, your fantasy will tell you.

Spread your cap. In order for her to sit well, bend her in half by pulling on the corners that you secured. You will get a triangle. Now you can try on the resulting cap. Well, you already know how to make a caps from a newspaper!

We told you about the simplest method of folding a paper headpiece. There are several options for how to make a caps from a newspaper, but they are already a little more complicated and require some sleight of hand. Practice first on simple things and boldly move on to complex ones. From the newspaper you can make a cap even with a visor! Folk art knows no boundaries.


In addition to the fact that such a cap will perform a practical function, it can also be used for the game. You can do such paper crafts with your children. This process develops imaginative thinking and motor skills. A newspaper pilot can be a whole adventure story. If you glue a couple of feathers to it and paint it with bright gouache, your baby will be able to imagine himself an Indian. And the evening with the kids will be very fun, with a laugh.
