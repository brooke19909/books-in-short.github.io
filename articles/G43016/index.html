Can I get a loan with a copy of my passport, TIN and SNILS?

Loans and borrowings - this is what many citizens have very much needed lately. True, this process is accompanied by the collection of a fairly large list of various papers. Some people ask: is it possible to take a loan with a copy of a passport or with photocopies of other documents? And if there is such an opportunity, then how and where to bring the idea to life? All this will be discussed further. In fact, this issue plays an important role for many borrowers.



What do you need

To begin with, you will have to find out exactly what documents, in principle, are needed to get a loan. After all, only then does (or does not) make sense to make copies. Paperwork with this process is more than enough. So if you are thinking about whether it is possible to take a loan from a copy of your passport, first find out what list of securities you need to present to the bank to apply for services.

There is nothing difficult to remember here. First of all, you must present any identification document. This is usually a passport of a citizen of the Russian Federation. If he is not there, it does not matter, but many banks refuse to issue a loan. Secondly, you need a written application for a loan, as well as a certificate of your income (2-NDFL, work book, 3-NDFL, billing statements, service agreements). Thirdly, you will be asked for the TIN, SNILS and military ID (for men under 27 years old). Additionally, a certificate of marriage / divorce, the birth of children, a marriage contract (if any) are required. As soon as you collect a full package of documents, contact them at the bank for a loan.

Certification

But the solution to our question does not end there. It's no secret that any copy can be notarized. And then she will have at least some power. Very often this is done when they cannot or do not want to present the original documents when applying to one or another authority.
