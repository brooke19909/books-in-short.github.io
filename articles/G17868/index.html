How to make beautiful bracelets from ribbons with your own hands?

What jewelry is not made of: bead, leather, thread, wire. Sometimes quite unexpected accessories are used, for example, nuts. You can also make bracelets from ribbons - with your own hands you can weave very bright and original little things. Two narrow satin ribbons will be required . They must be at least one meter long, the same width and contrasting colors. For example, red with white, and yellow or orange will successfully combine with black.

The strength of homemade bracelets from ribbons even to beginner craftswomen, because it is very easy. Before weaving, the ends of the ribbons must be tied together in a knot, leaving the tips at fifteen centimeters. They come in handy for the clasp.





After that, make two loops on each of the tapes. Their size should not exceed 10-15 centimeters. Then put them on top of each other, bend the black one, as if to create a knot. As a result, she wraps around the bottom ribbon and returns. Insert a yellow loop into it and tighten. After that, change the tape. Now thread the yellow loop into black. So, alternating colors, continue weaving until the decoration is obtained the desired length. Particular attention should be paid to the first puff. It is the most difficult, as it requires effort. Further action will be easy. At the end of the work, when the bracelets made of ribbons are almost ready, do it yourself with the hands in the last loop to skip the tail (it should be a different color) and draw it to the end. After that, pull both strips in different directions. Get a knot. So the original square bauble will come out.





Do it yourself with bracelets to make bracelets from ribbons. Do not tighten too tight so that a clear and even pattern develops, and the strips fit equally. As a basis for weaving using some smooth surface. It is recommended at the beginning of work to fix the end of the loop on it with a nail, pins. So you won‚Äôt lose the tape. To control the degree of tension of satin stripes, it is enough to insert a strong harsh thread in the center of the future product.





There are many options, for example, you can make a round bracelet from ribbons or surround. It weaves from four satin stripes. It is also made quite easily. After the ribbons are tied together, one of them, for example, white, must be bent from top to bottom to make a loop. Shift it across the other, red. The third strip, beige, bends so that it overlaps the previous one. Skip the fourth tape into the loop that formed first, and tighten. This must be done carefully. Get a square. The ribbons must not be allowed to crumple, the shape of the bracelet will suffer from this. To make the volumetric decoration look more impressive, it can be slightly twisted around its axis during the creation process. To do this, it is enough to slightly shift the weaving to the side, without stretching it in length. So weave to the required size.

In this way, not only bracelets from ribbons are obtained, you can make jewelry with your own hands, as well as various pleasant little things for interior decoration.
