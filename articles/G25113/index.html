Moscow Industrial Bank: rating of banks, services, president of the bank

The banking sector has been actively developing in recent decades. Among the population, lending and other financial services are popular. However, not all existing lending institutions are equally successful. Only the most famous and sought after companies are included in the rating of banks. Industrial Bank is one of such organizations. Let's find out more about him.

Description

Moscow Industrial Bank is known as a major player in the financial services market in Russia. The history of its existence began in 1990, that is, back in Soviet times.

Six years after its founding, the company was transformed into an open joint stock company. For the current period, it owns a developed network of regional representative offices and maintains branches in thirty subjects.

Potential clients

The Moscow Industrial Bank is pleased to see legal and physical persons as its own clients. For the current period, it serves about seventy thousand enterprises and more than one and a half million individuals.

Industrial bank in a ranking of banks occupies a high position. Often information about its products is published in specialized publications. Moreover, it is trusted by customers. And according to the Central Bank, it is included in the list of thirty largest banks in Russia, gaining increasing importance and popularity in the financial services market.

The basis of the client base is large companies engaged in industry, as well as services and trade. As borrowers, a credit organization is contacted by companies that deal with the economy, as well as real estate, construction, leasing services, trade and agriculture.

Bank President

This position in the Moscow Industrial Bank is taken by Abubakar Arsamakov. The total share of him and family members in the authorized capital is 12.42%.

Arsamakov Abubakar was born in 1956. He studied in civil engineering, as well as in financial universities. Since 1982, he began his own career in financial organizations. He was an employee of such corporations as Stroybank, Gosbank, Agroprombank, Promstroybank. In all of the above organizations, Abubakar Arsamakov worked during the Soviet era.

Since 1990, the current president has been actively involved in the fate of the Moscow Industrial Bank. Under his leadership, this company has gained fame and popularity.

Relatively recently received the title of Honored Economist of the Russian Federation in accordance with the decree of the President of the Russian Federation.

Structure

Moscow Industrial Bank has a difficult ownership structure, information about which is not fully disseminated. It is authentically known that the largest block of shares belongs to the president of the company, as well as members of his family. The shares of the remaining shareholders do not exceed ten percent in the authorized capital.

The head office is in Moscow. However, Moscow Industrial Bank boasts a well-developed branch network, which has more than two hundred offices located in several dozen cities. For example, an additional office is located at: st. Pyatnitskaya, d. 76.

The Moscow Industrial Bank, whose addresses are published on the official website, has more than six thousand employees. This is an impressive figure. The number of ATMs available to a financial company has more than two thousand devices. Moreover, most of them are located in the metropolitan area. However, industrial bank ATMs are also present in other regions. This allows the financial institution to increase the number of its own customers.

Services

The Moscow Industrial Bank, as mentioned above, provides services to both individuals and legal entities. As you understand, these categories of customers have different requests. The company responds to them and provides related services. Let's talk about them in more detail.

Individuals can receive the following services by contacting the Moscow Industrial Bank:

  • Making a bank card.
  • Apply for a mortgage or consumer loan, and in case of a positive decision to get a loan.
  • Those who seek to preserve their own funds can open deposits in an industrial bank on favorable terms.
  • Make transfers and pay for services.
  • Rent bank safes.
  • Purchase insurance products.

There is a brokerage service for those who not only spend, but also invest their own money.

In addition to the opportunities listed above, customers contacting the Moscow Industrial Bank can receive services remotely. To do this, it will be enough to go through a simple registration and gain access to your personal account.

Moscow Industrial Bank also provides services for legal entities:

  • Settlement and cash services.
  • Credit programs that take into account the industry specifics of customers' business.
  • A public deposit account that is intended for the placement of funds that a notary accepts as a deposit.
  • Maintenance of contracts that involve financial settlements between the parties to the parties.
  • Leasing services for legal entities, including individual entrepreneurs.
  • Acquiring services for companies that accept cashless payments for their own customers.
  • Placement of funds.
