Tips: how to tie bows beautifully?

Often we have to decorate some things or make decor elements with our own hands. What could be easier than making a bow for this? For example, it can be the final element of some postcards made by you.



In order to decorate gift wrapping, you need to know how to tie bows beautifully. In this simple matter, there are many options. Bows are classic, strict, tiered, two-tone, etc.

In order to tie them, you need a ribbon, it can be a beautiful satin basma or a rigid special fabric, which is convenient to give any form. Depending on what type of bow you choose, you may need glue and scissors. The main thing is to do everything carefully so that the tape does not wrinkle, and you get neat details of the decor.

How to tie classic bows beautifully? In order to make such a bow more even, you can use a fork. But at the same time the bow will be small. Take the fork and your ribbon, fold it in half. Throw a loop on the cloves of the fork. Pull it well. Thread the bottom of the tape over the top, between the second and third cloves. Now flip the right edge of the basma over the loop in the same place. Turn the plug over. You will see two ends of the ribbon that you need to tie to a knot. Correct them and remove the bow from the fork, cut the excess. To ensure that the edges of the basma do not unravel, swipe a lit match over them. Such small bows can, for example, be glued to baby hair clips, and your

daughter will be delighted.

How to tie beautifully tiered bows? To do this, you will need ribbons of different widths, it also looks beautiful if they are at the same time and multi-colored. It is necessary to take three segments and cut them so that the wide basma is more authentic, and the narrow - the shortest. Next, you need to form the ends of the tape, cutting out the triangles at their edges. Now lay the three basmas on top of each other, take another one in order to tie them in the middle. If you need to fasten such a bow on a gift box, the ribbon that you will combine, you need to take a long one so that it is enough to wrap the box. It looks beautiful when the middle is of a different color, and around the edges, for example, she has sewn beads.

How to tie a beautiful bow of a strict look? You need to take the tape and fold it so that a joint is formed in the middle of the bottom side. It must be fixed with glue. Next, take basma of a different color and width. We will close the ugly joint with it, wrap the ribbon in the middle around the bow and also fix it with glue. You can take another smaller width and do the last action again.



How to tie terry bows beautifully? You will need a tough special fabric. This is necessary to make it more convenient to shape the bow. Fold the ribbon so that you get a circle. Its diameter will be the size of the bow. Now, diagonally cut the corners of the tape, and rotate it so that they are in the middle. Tie the incision site with a thin basma or thread. After that, take out the inner loops and stack them nicely. That's all!

Now you know how to tie bows beautifully, and your gifts will be the most elegant.
