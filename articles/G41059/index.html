Nikolaevka, "Southern" boarding house - rest in Crimea for the whole family

On the Crimean peninsula, not far from Simferopol, is the village of Nikolaevka. The convenient location has transformed it from an ordinary seaside village, of which there are many on the coast, into a popular and developing resort.



Location and climate

The village of Nikolaevka is located on the western Black Sea coast, in the Kalamitsky Gulf. The modern resort is located in the coastal-steppe zone with a mild climate, which has a beneficial effect on the human body.

The day breeze, which does not meet forest or mountain barriers, brings the sea coolness and freshness to the steppe, and the night breeze is filled with aromas of flowers and steppe grasses. Neighborhood with the steppe provides lower humidity than in other resorts of the peninsula. Many tourists note that rest in Nikolaevka is remembered by the fact that it is very easy to breathe here even in the most intense heat.



Another indisputable advantage of this village is the absence of seaports and industrial enterprises in the district. In summer, the weather is dry and hot (+ 27 ° C). Surprisingly, unlike the southern coast of the peninsula, Nikolaevka does not have a velvet season. The sea in the second half of September is no longer too comfortable for swimming, although the water warmed up during the summer gives off heat for a long time. Thanks to this, the winter here is snowless and very mild. In January, the temperature does not drop below + 2.5 ° C.

Rest in Nikolaevka

Without a doubt, a beach holiday is the main one in this resort village. The beach here stretches for eight kilometers. The shore is covered with sand and colorful pebbles. The sea is shallow with a smooth approach. It is very convenient for families with small children. The beach strip continues beyond the territory of Nikolaevka. In the north-west there is a wild beach, on which there is practically no infrastructure. South of the pier, on the other side of the village, are the beaches of boarding houses. And if you walk along the coast for another 300 meters, you can find yourself on a nudist beach.



Entertainment

Despite its relatively small size, in terms of the number of entertainments, this village is not inferior to the larger and more popular resorts. In Nikolaevka there is an excellent Luna Park with various attractions for both adults and children, many restaurants, cafes and bars. Fans of nightlife will not be bored here. They will always be welcomed at the popular nightclubs “Babylon”, “Sky” and “Baikonur”.

And for younger guests in the village built wonderful playgrounds, trampolines, slides. Thanks to such a set of entertainment, weekend tours have been actively sold by many agencies to the village.

Fans of outdoor activities will also not be disappointed with the trip. The village has a very decent football field, basketball and volleyball courts, tennis courts. And, of course, there is a full range of standard beach activities: jet skiing, pills, bananas, parachuting, boat trips and sea fishing. And if these entertainments do not seem enough to you, then you can go to the “Banana Republic” - one of the largest water parks in Crimea, located 30 km from the village.

Boarding houses

The hotels and pensions located in Nikolaevka are distinguished, first of all, by large well-groomed territories and developed infrastructure. As a rule, in their territories there are saunas and pools, libraries and concert halls, sports grounds, tennis courts, etc. The following pensions are located in the village:

  • "Radiant".
  • "Power Engineer".
  • Helios.
  • "Solar".
  • "Paradise."
  • "Emerald".
  • "Southern".

In addition, there are comfortable hotels and recreation centers. The latter most often serve weekend tours. Youth companies that have come for a couple of days to relax from the bustle of the city and stuffiness like to stay here.





Nikolaevka: "South" (boarding house)

It is no exaggeration to say that this is the most popular guesthouse in the village. It is located in a picturesque park with an area of ​​more than five hectares and is considered one of the best in the south-west of Crimea. The park was designed by specialists from the Nikitsky Botanical Garden. I must say that they worked hard for fame - exquisitely designed flower beds, year-round flowering perennial and annual plants - all this pleases the eye.

In 2009, a holiday was held in the village of Nikolaevka. Yuzhny (boarding house) celebrated its thirtieth anniversary. Over the years, it has been repeatedly recognized as the best place for a family vacation.



Accommodation

Guests are offered to settle in a six-story building, in which rooms of the following categories are located:

Standard

2-3-bed rooms with two or three single beds with bedside tables. In double rooms, an extra bed (folding bed) is allowed. All rooms have a bathroom, shower. The rooms are equipped with a TV and fridge.

Superior Standard

Double rooms with a double bed (no extra beds). The rooms have televisions, refrigerators, balconies with sea views, air conditioning. Vacationers are provided with an electric kettle and a hairdryer.

Suite

2-room room with a bedroom and a living room. Equipped with a TV, air conditioning, fridge, kettle and a set of dishes. Balcony with pool or sea view.



Studio Suite

One spacious room with a kitchen area. Air-conditioned room with a TV, fridge, kettle, dishes. The room has a double bed, wardrobe. Balcony with pool view. The bathroom has a bathtub, boiler, hairdryer.

Suite - VIP

Spacious room with an area of ​​54 m 2 . Consists of a living room and bedroom. The living room has modern furniture, a coffee table, a TV. The bedroom has a double bed with bedside tables, a wardrobe. The balcony offers stunning views of the sea and the park. The room has air conditioning, individual safe.





Beach

An excellent beach vacation offers Nikolaevka. “Yuzhny” is a boarding house with a sandy and pebble beach 120 meters long with a shallow bottom. Free of charge are provided with sun loungers, umbrellas, the use of fresh water showers, a dressing room, and a volleyball court.

In addition, there are paid services on the beach:

  • water attractions;
  • rental of beach equipment.

Distance to the beach from the building is 150 meters. Shady park paths lead to it, surrounded by fragrant lavender and cypress trees. In the morning, animators work on the beach - they play with children, and adults are invited to play volleyball.

Nutrition

The boarding house offers three meals a day in the dining room according to the buffet system. A special menu has been developed for children. The range of dishes is varied and balanced. Guests are offered tasty and healthy food, lots of vegetables and fruits, a variety of pastries. Vacationers are served by waiters in two cozy rooms.



The boarding house offers a system of custom menus, which should be equivalent in quality, assortment and cost. In addition, guests can visit the café-bars of the Yuzhny boarding house:

  • "Water Area" - a pool bar.
  • "Cottage" - a cafe on the waterfront.
  • "Lavender" - a phyto-bar.

Reviews

Many of our compatriots have already visited the Yuzhny boarding house (Nikolaevka). Reviews say that many come here more than once, which indicates the excellent quality of the services received.

First of all, tourists are fascinated by their natural beauties Nikolaevka. "Southern" - a guest house, according to guests, ideal for relaxing with your family. Travelers note that the rooms are very bright, comfortable, equipped with everything necessary for relaxation. The boarding house staff deserves kind and warm words for their high professionalism, attention and individual approach to each guest.
