Kurgan is not the most interesting city in Russia, it is worth visiting in transit on the way to Kazakhstan or just to replenish your collection of seen Russian regional centers.

Bus ride

Buses from Yekaterinburg to Kurgan leave from the northern bus station from 5 a.m. to 10 p.m. and at 2 a.m. Their arrival point is the Kurgan bus station, which is located next to the train station. In this sense, Kurgan is similar to Petropavlovsk and Yekaterinburg and differs, for example, from Tyumen.

A ticket from Yekaterinburg to Kurgan will cost from 700 rubles, therefore, the fare is about 2 rubles per kilometer. The trip will take 6.5-7.5 hours. On the way, the bus makes several short stops: in Kargopol, Kataysk, Dolmatovo, Shadrinsk.

In the opposite direction, from Kurgan to Yekaterinburg, buses run from 7 am to 11 pm.

Car trip

The Yekaterinburg-Kurgan route can be reached in about 5 hours by car - it depends on the congestion of the route and the weather. You need to exit on E-22 to Kosulino and there take the R-354 towards Kamensk-Uralsky. After this city, the route leads to the Kurgan region. You need to go to Kurgan through Kataysk, Dolmatovo and Kargopol.

A more exotic option is also possible. From Yekaterinburg you need to get to Tyumen on the highway E-22. On the bypass of Tyumen will be a pointer to the mound. This is where you need to turn off, then move to your destination through the district center Isetskoye. Thus, a 530-km route will be obtained, but along the way you can call in Tyumen or, for example, at the Upper Bor resort near it.

Why go to Kurgan?

The city is not very interesting. His museum fund is inferior to all neighbors (Yekaterinburg, Chelyabinsk, Tyumen), except Petropavlovsk, which is located in Kazakhstan.

It is worth visiting the typical museums: local history and art, as well as the Museum of the Decembrists and Aviation.

In the Central Park in summer you can ride the train for the children's railway.

An ostrich farm with a contact zoo is located just south of Kurgan.

From this little-known regional center you can go to the village of Chastozoozerye, which is interesting not only for its lakes, but also for the magnificent cathedral and monuments to Yesenin and Russia.
