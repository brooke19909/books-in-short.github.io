Hotel Nha Trang Palace Hotel 4 * (Vietnam / Nha Trang): photos and reviews of tourists

If you have chosen the Nha Trang resort in Vietnam and are looking for a comfortable hotel near the sea, then the Nha Trang Palace Hotel 4 * can be an excellent option for you.





Location

This hotel is located one kilometer from the center of the popular Vietnamese resort, close to the modern Nha Trang Center shopping center. The distance to your own beach is 200 meters (you need to cross the road along the way). The nearest airport is Kamran, 37 kilometers from the hotel. The journey from the air harbor takes about 45 minutes.





Nha Trang, Vietnam, Nha Trang Palace Hotel 4 *: general information, photo

Nha-Trang Palace was built in 2014. It is a modern 15-story building. The hotel does not have its own territory. The housing stock includes 168 comfortable apartments of the following categories: suite, club suite, deluxe, superior. Some of the rooms have magnificent sea views. All apartments have the necessary furniture, air conditioning, TV, telephone, safe, minibar, bathroom, electric kettle, hairdryer. Guests are provided with personal hygiene products, tea and coffee making facilities, bottled drinking water, as well as bathrobes and slippers. Towels are cleaned and changed every day. All apartments have wireless internet access. You can use it for free.

Meals at the hotel are carried out according to the "BB" system, which includes breakfast only. However, for an additional fee, guests can have lunch and dinner at Nha-Trang Palace Restaurant. Here you will be offered dishes of both local and European cuisine.

With regard to infrastructure, the hotel has: a conference room, parking, an outdoor pool, a bar, a fitness center, a beauty salon, a tour desk, a car rental service, laundry, dry cleaning and much more. The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day.

Housing stock

Almost all guests were satisfied with the apartments of the Nha-Trang Palace Hotel. So, according to them, since the hotel is new, the rooms here are freshly renovated. The fact is that they are not electronic here, but are locked with a small key, which can be easily lost. However, if this happens, then you should not worry about the safety of things in the safe. At the reception you will be given a new key, but for the loss of the old you will have to pay a fine.

Also, not all guests were satisfied with the balconies in the apartments. According to them, in almost all rooms they are quite small. There is nowhere to put furniture on them. You can only go out and enjoy the surroundings. By the way, our compatriots advise, if possible, to settle on the upper floors. Cleaning

Our compatriots did not express any complaints about the work of the maids of the Nha Trang Palace Hotel 4 *, the reviews we are reviewing. So, according to them, housekeeping is carried out every day and is quite thorough. Maids regularly change towels, linen and replenish sets of personal hygiene products (shaving accessories, toothbrushes and pastes, shower gel, soap, shampoo, cotton buds, toilet paper, etc.). In addition, tea and coffee bags and bottled drinking water will be brought to your room every day. If you are not in the room, the maids will shut the curtains and prepare your beds. If you are in the apartment, then you will be offered to drink tea or coffee and leave drinking water.

Check in, departure

Many travelers staying at Nha-Trang Palace really liked the fact that in the vast majority of cases, newly arrived guests are resettled immediately, without condemning them to wait for the checkout time. So even if you arrive at the hotel early in the morning, you can literally get the keys to your room within 10-15 minutes. This will allow you not to lose precious minutes of vacation.

As for leaving the hotel, the occupied apartments should be vacated before noon. Please note that the vacated room is checked by the hotel staff. And if they find a shortage of towels or broken dishes, then you will be asked to pay for the damage. Many guests were surprised by this fact, since, according to them, in many cases some items of dishes already had chips. Therefore, experienced tourists recommend inspecting all items in the room immediately upon check-in, and in case of any defects immediately inform the receptionist about this.





Location

Nutrition

The cost of living in this hotel includes breakfast only. They are held in a restaurant. According to tourists, breakfast here is very diverse. So, every day in the morning you can have a great meal and get a boost of energy. In the breakfast menu you will find bacon, cheeses, delicious pancakes, pancakes, scrambled eggs, omelette, cereals, Pho soup, several types of fresh fruits, vegetables, yogurt, juices, tea and coffee.

Guests can also have lunch and dinner at the Nha-Trang Palace Restaurant. According to our compatriots, they cook well here. But many travelers chose to eat in different cafes and restaurants around the hotel and in the center of Nha Trang. This will allow you to try various dishes. Cook, according to tourists, is good almost everywhere, and prices are affordable.





Sea

A big plus, according to many tourists, is the fact that the Nha Trang Palace Hotel 4 * has its own beach. True, as guests note, there are not so many sunbeds and umbrellas here. Therefore, if you want to comfortably sit on a deck chair, it makes sense to come ashore early. But even if you do not find a free sunbed, you can always sit right on the sand, laying a towel. A beach worker will try to find a sunny umbrella for you.

As already noted, the beach from the hotel can be reached in just five minutes. It is only necessary to cross the road. The beach is sandy. The sea is clean, sometimes there are waves. Experienced travelers warn everyone who plans to go on holiday to Nha Trang that the sun is very active here. Therefore, it is recommended to use sunscreens, preferring products with a high level of protection.
