The recreation center "Utulik": rest on Lake Baikal is available to everyone!

Holidays on Lake Baikal are a worthy alternative to trips to the warm seas. The incredible beauty of the surroundings of the great lake will not leave anyone indifferent. What is especially nice, today the tourist infrastructure of the region is quite developed. On the shore of Lake Baikal, every vacationer will find a suitable accommodation option and a lot of entertainment to their own taste. For example, today the guests are hosted by the Utulik recreation center year-round.

general information



In the distant 1961, at the foot of Hamar-Daban, a small tent town was laid out. Pioneer tourists were attracted by the picturesque valley, located just 2 kilometers from Lake Baikal. Very soon, the Angara campground was organized here. In 1993, the place that tourists liked was given the name: "Recreation center" Utulik "." The territory is being landscaped, capital residential buildings and related buildings are being built. Today, the ownership of the recreation center is just over 8 hectares, vacationers are offered accommodation with meals and a lot of related services.

Accommodation and infrastructure



The recreation center "Utulik" works year-round. In the warm season, 500 guests can simultaneously accommodate here, and in winter - 200 guests. In buildings designed for year-round use, there are single and double rooms of various categories. In the warm season, guests are offered cozy guest houses with a capacity of 4 people and modern cottages designed to accommodate several families. All rooms delight guests with a modern renovation and a cozy atmosphere. Vacationers are offered three meals a day in the dining room (Russian cuisine), you can also visit the bar and organize a celebration - there is a banquet hall. On the territory of the recreation center there are sports and playgrounds, guests are offered the organization of excursions, trips and rental of various sports equipment. The recreation center "Utulik" can also boast its own Russian bathhouse with a font on the river bank, and in the evenings tourists are offered to listen to live music or even visit a disco.

All the possibilities for relaxation - in one place!



Everyone can have a good rest and get a lot of new impressions at this recreation center. Every year, lovers of outdoor activities and extreme sports come here. There is a place in Utulik for those who prefer to enjoy solitude and silence. Fans of noisy companies who fled from the big city will not be bored either. In this area there are many accommodation options for vacationers, for example, the famous recreation center "Baikal" in Utulik. But still, many tourists admit that it is enough to rent a room at Utulik at least once, and you will definitely want to return here. This recreation center not only offers decent conditions at affordable prices, but also attracts guests with its special atmosphere. Good news for young parents: there are no restrictions on placement with children. On the basis of Utulik all conditions for a family vacation with kids of all ages are created.

The recreation center "Utulik": photos and reviews of tourists



The shore of Lake Baikal is a place with a unique ecosystem and microclimate. Staying here has a beneficial effect on health, and the picturesque natural views help to relax. The recreation center "Utulik" offers comfortable living conditions for everyone. The territory of the complex pleases with an abundance of greenery, vacationers can choose between detached cottages and rooms in common buildings. During your stay at this recreation center you can completely forget about domestic problems and responsibilities. Three meals a day are organized for guests, you can order housekeeping and take advantage of other household services. There are no problems with leisure activities. The recreation center "Utulik" reviews has positive reviews from vacationers of various ages. Here, pensioners are happy to spend time, young and active try themselves in various extreme sports. From the residential buildings of the sanatorium to the sandy beach no more than ten minutes walk on foot. You can also have a good rest at the recreation center in the winter. At this time of the year, trails are laid, pistes for downhill skiing and tubing are arranged, and the New Year holidays are celebrated magnificently and merrily.
