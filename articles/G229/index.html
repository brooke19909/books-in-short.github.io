Pension "Tavrida" (Alushta): photos and reviews

Crimea is a paradise where you can have a wonderful rest at any time of the year. Dozens of boarding houses, rest houses, sanatoriums work here. The boarding house Tavrida enjoys well-deserved popularity. Alushta has long been considered the best resort on the peninsula. It was here that the last Russian tsar met his future wife, and the famous Botkin considered the Alushta climate to be much better than the Yalta. Clean and always fresh air of the resort gives health, and Tavrida employees provide a homely, cozy and friendly atmosphere to all their guests.

Location

Thousands of people from different parts of the Earth like Crimea, Alushta. Pension "Tavrida" is located in the central district of the city on Lenin Street, house 22. From Simferopol it is 45 km, from Bakhchisaray - 77, from Feodosia - 137. The coast of the Black Sea and the nearest city beach are about 400 meters from the pension, from Station - 1 km.





The location in the center of Alushta is very convenient, as there are many shops, cafes, discos, restaurants, as well as a public transport stop, a market, a pharmacy, a post office, an amusement park, an aqua park and several bank branches. They get to the boarding house from Simferopol by taxi, minibus or trolleybus No. 51. You can walk from the bus station to Tavrida. The road takes only 10 minutes.

Description

Pension "Tavrida" (Alushta) began to function in 1957. According to the architecture of those years, the buildings were built with high ceilings in the rooms and very large windows. In 2001, they underwent a complete renovation, updated the rooms, equipped them with modern electrical equipment, preserving the overall architectural style. The territory of the pension is small, but very beautiful. Its only three-story building (plus basement) is surrounded by centuries-old firs, junipers and pines, giving all vacationers pleasant and at the same time healing coniferous aromas.





Among the trees there are cozy gazebos with plastic furniture, a children's swing, as well as a barbecue, where guests of the boarding house fry kebabs. There are even hammocks for children and adults. There is a reception desk in the lobby where you can rent a cell for storing valuables, order a taxi or transfer, and take things to the left-luggage office. Wi-Fi works well in the lobby and in the park gazebos. There is no elevator in the boarding house. A wide staircase leads to the upper floors. Parking, as such, is also not available, there is only a small patch, where you can park for a short time.

Nutrition

Tours to the boarding house "Tavrida" (Alushta) are different. You can order only accommodation without meals, a tour with breakfast or with full board on an all-inclusive basis. Meals are provided in the cafe. From May to October, a self-service buffet system is in operation, and from October to May, a custom menu system is in place. If you wish, having bought a tour, you can add any type of food upon arrival at the hotel. Full board for one person costs from 400 rubles per day.





It should be noted that the menu in Tavrid is pretty good. Preparing cereals, hot dishes, meat, chicken, fish, vegetables, pasta, rice, pastries. In addition, you can always eat in the cafes and restaurants of the city.

Winters are mild with an average air temperature of up to 2 degrees. Frosts are rare. In summer, Alushta is not as hot as in other places on the coast. Leisure

A holiday saturated with impressions is given by the Tavrida boarding house (Alushta). Reviews of tourists note that on its territory among firs and junipers you can just sit quietly with a book or laptop, breathing in the magical aromas, or you can have fun with your friends by organizing a barbecue. A successful location makes it possible to visit all the sights of Alushta and the surrounding area without unnecessary trouble, admire the rivers Ulu-Uzen, Demerdzhi, the mountains of Babugan, Chatyrdag, Chamny-Burun. In addition, the boarding house organizes special tours (historical, wine), celebrates the New Year, Christmas, it is possible to book rooms for the weekend or during school holidays.

Reviews

For everyone who can do without the luxury of five-star hotels, the Tavrida boarding house (Alushta) is suitable. Reviews of tourists who have been here in different years are somewhat different. The human factor, which is the professionalism of employees, is largely to blame. In general, the following advantages are noted:

  • location (all objects of urban infrastructure are close);
  • beautiful territory;
  • democratic prices of tours;
  • convenient travel;
  • good rooms; suites and junior suites;
  • free accommodation for children under 10 years old.

Most recurring cons:

  • old furniture and plumbing in some rooms;
  • not tasty food (but it's a matter of taste);
  • the hostility of individual employees;
  • too crowded beach;
  • hot water in the rooms on schedule.
