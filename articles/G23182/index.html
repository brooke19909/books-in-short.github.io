Rest in Primorsko-Akhtarsk: reviews of tourists

The former military fortress - Primorsko-Akhtarsk - has now become a water resort. Every summer people come here, longing for the warm sun and sea. It has everything that travelers craving for relaxation in the lap of nature - wild sand spits, picturesque bays, reserved lands and mud volcanoes can crave. There is a shallow sea, a great coast for surfers, a mild climate, comfortable housing, not too many people and enough budget prices. Reviews about the rest in Primorsko-Akhtarsk served as the basis for this article.

Where to swim

According to reviews about holidays in Primorsko-Akhtarsk, the resort is very suitable for families with children. There are good wide beaches, clean, sandy. The sea is very shallow, so young children are just paradise. And the water is very warm. But keep in mind that on the wild beaches often come across sharp shells. Therefore, so that your child does not cut himself, listen to the advice of experienced vacationers - go to sunbathe on the Central Beach. He is cleaned of shells, and he is comfortable. There are sun loungers and umbrellas (from 20 rubles per hour), as well as scooters and catamarans for rent.

Windsurfers love the central beach. There is even a special center for training beginners. Travelers who love outdoor activities wrote in reviews of Primorsko-Akhtarsk that there is a school for managing yachts and other small sailing ships. Well, if you are real tourists and you are attracted by wild, reserved places, then welcome to the Achuevskaya and Yasenskaya spits. There is nothing at all except sand, water, birds, rare animals and plants. But the sea is deep, you can swim and dive. ‚ÄúSavages‚Äù with tents are often located here.



Where to live

If you come to this resort, then you are unlikely to meet with a frequent problem of the Russian south. Here is a large offer of housing, including budget. This is the private sector, and guest houses, and mini-hotels, and pensions with recreation centers. Only about a hundred options. The beaches are so wide that many guest houses have not only excellent living conditions, but also very picturesque sea views. Therefore, tourists often illustrate reviews about holidays in Primorsko-Akhtarsk with photos taken from balconies and loggias. Many offers of budget housing - from 300 rubles per room. Tourists most often rate the local service as good.

The Marisol Hotel, the Venice Guesthouse and the Lotos Recreation Center deserved the best reviews. Primorsko-Akhtarsk, or rather, its central part, is located three kilometers from this boarding house. Therefore, it is located in the steppe, but thanks to irrigation gardening, its territory is surrounded by greenery. On the territory of the base there are summer houses, a hotel-type building with European-quality repair. The beach at the Lotus has its own, three meals a day, comprehensive.
