Company and production

The company was established in 2003 in the Czech Republic. Production facilities are located in 9 countries. The product range includes:

  • domestic and commercial air conditioners;
  • multizone systems and heat pumps;
  • chillers, fan coil units, remote heat exchange units;
  • precision systems, rooftops and condensing units;
  • ventilation equipment;
  • fan heaters.



Air conditioners "Lessar" are installed both in apartments, cottages and multi-storey residential buildings, as well as in industrial facilities, in large retail and office premises and enterprises.

Service

Any equipment needs maintenance and service. For split systems of any manufacturers it is necessary to carry out preventive work and cleaning in a timely manner and in full.





This will help to avoid damage to the device and reduce the likelihood of a negative effect of the system on a person.

Maintenance of the air conditioner must be carried out according to the schedule established by the manufacturer. It is advisable for split-systems for home use to conduct it twice a year. The scope of work should include the following operations:

  • system diagnostics for a sufficient amount of refrigerant;
  • cleaning and disinfection of filters, heat exchangers, fans;
  • check the drainage system;
  • antibacterial treatment of filters and other surfaces.

We recommend that you regularly clean and maintain the "Lessar" air conditioner; instructions describing the characteristics and functions are provided in Russian. Using it simplifies troubleshooting and problems in the system.
