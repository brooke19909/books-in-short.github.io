Taxes on TIN are now under the force of everyone

Due to the large number of different payment receipts, people cannot figure out exactly what and where to pay. The Federal Tax Service has foreseen this fact and provided an opportunity for all individuals and legal entities to see their unpaid TIN taxes. All information is provided online, you just need to go to a special site and enter your personal TIN. This is a very convenient service that simplifies the search for your own tax debt, eliminates the need to defend queues and upholster the thresholds of tax inspections. The main thing is to have access to the Internet on hand.



In order to find out taxes on the taxpayer identification number, you need to go to the section "Personal Account of the Taxpayer" on the website of the Federal Tax Service and enter information about yourself:

- Full Name;

- individual taxpayer number;

- region of residence.

After your personal account is created, you can get all the information on taxes. If an individual has debts, then the amount payable will be displayed in a special window. Moreover, the program allows you to generate a payment order, which is a receipt with which you must contact the bank to make a payment. In this way, you can find out the tax on the TIN and immediately generate a payment document. The program is very easy to use, so even the most inexperienced PC user can easily cope with this task.



The service allows you to generate property, transport and land unpaid taxes under the TIN, as well as taxes relating to individual entrepreneurship. By the way, you need to be prepared to detect errors in the report, so you always need to double-check everything. Such a tax payment system is more accessible for taxpayers; it provides ease of use, thereby increasing the effectiveness of the fight against tax evasion.

Knowing your taxes under the TIN is very important, as significant delays lead to various troubles: a penalty is charged daily, accounts are blocked, travel abroad is prohibited. Nobody needs such problems, so they can be avoided if you pay on time.

Once all the TIN taxes are recognized, you can pay, including penalties and fines. Payment is possible by bank transfer and cash. In the first case, the system offers to pay using bank accounts and payment terminals. True, not all banks are indicated, and Sberbank and Promsvyazbank are among the most popular.



It is better to make a slightly larger amount, since the date of the report may not coincide with the day of your request. All paid receipts should be stored and not discarded (at least) for three years. After 10 days after payment, you should check the debt information again. In any case, when disputes arise, all the information in the form of paid receipts will be in your hands, which is indisputable evidence of a lack of debt.
