Ritmix RMD 1055 Tablet Review

The South Korean manufacturer in 2013 released a new model at that time, which was called Ritmix RMD 1055. The company added quite noticeable features that are striking, first of all, in the eyes of those people who often use the equipment from this developer. The tablet works on the Android operating system version 4.1. The screen is used in good quality, the battery is powerful, as for a budget category device, it is also capable of working with 3G networks. It is worth noting that in addition to all this, an excellent processor is installed.

Equipment

The tablet, which is discussed in the article, is sold in an interesting box. On it you can find a list of what is in the kit. Moreover, on its surfaces are printed all the basic characteristics of the device.

What is included in the package? In addition to the tablet, the user will find in the box a charger, a cable for connecting to a computer, an instruction manual and a case. The latter is black in color, made in the form of a book, it is made of leather. It should be noted that this model, unlike the previous ones, has a special cover in its kit, and not a velvet bag. The only drawback of this device is that it does not have a hole for the camera. Therefore, if there is a desire to photograph something, you will have to remove the Ritmix RMD 1055 tablet from it.



Design description

The tablet got a black color, and there are no buttons on its outside. All that can be seen on the front panel is the inscription Ritmix. It is located on top, and a little to the right of it is easy to find the front camera. She is located just below the corner.

Often there may be problems with the camera. However, users are not advised to solve them on their own. Do not wonder how to disassemble the Ritmix RMD 1055 tablet , it is better to take it to a service center. Otherwise, you can create even bigger problems.

The rear panel has a dark gray color, on the left there is a slot for a SIM card, a port for an external drive, as well as charging and headphones. The last connector is standard. If you look closely, you can notice the special mechanical buttons that are responsible for adjusting the volume, as well as the on / off key of the device. The speaker is located at the bottom of the rear panel.



Specifications

The tablet has a diagonal of 9.7 inches. The aspect ratio is as comfortable as possible, so using this device will be quite convenient. The screen received an IPS matrix, and the number of pixels per inch is 142 ppi. According to consumers, such characteristics are enough to perform basic actions: watch movies, photos, work and so on. However, it should be noted that pixels become visible on solid images.

The tablet has pretty good performance. It runs on two cores, and the processor frequency is 1.6 GHz. RAM received a size of 1 GB. Therefore, it will not work to perform serious tasks on the tablet, but watching movies, typing, or playing slightly capacious games will be quite convenient and comfortable. Hanging can be noticed only if the tablet starts to overheat. That is, you can‚Äôt, for example, simultaneously watch a movie and charge the device. However, there is a special program for the Ritmix RMD 1055 tablet, which makes it possible to control the processor load.

The 7800 mAh battery impresses many, so this nuance can be noted as a positive characteristic. Built-in memory is 8 GB. If this space is not enough, then the internal storage can be supplemented with a flash card. The gadget works stably only with external drives up to 32 GB in size.



Communication and multimedia

The Ritmix RMD 1055 tablet is equipped with a Wi-Fi and 3G module. The latter works quite well and stably, no failures are observed. Some consumers say that Wi-Fi can sometimes stop functioning, and it‚Äôs better not to go far from the signal source.

Unfortunately, many users complain about the sound quality through the built-in speaker. However, if you do not take this nuance into account, then in general it can be said that it (even with the fact that it is the only one) is quite sonorous and clear. However, in order to enjoy listening to music, it is better not to set it to maximum volume and not to listen to heavy rock compositions. The sound in the headphones is good and there are no complaints about it. The Ritmix RMD 1055 tablet received two cameras: the front and the main. The first has a resolution of 3 megapixels, and the second - only 2 megapixels. Accordingly, it cannot be said that this device allows you to take high-quality photos. However, in case of emergency, the built-in matrix will always come to the rescue. Moreover, the rear camera and front camera can be safely used for talking on Skype.



Total

In general, it will be useful to say that the Ritmix RMD 1055 tablet is good, it fully justifies its cost. In appearance, the device is beautiful and pretty pretty. The functionality is at a good level, since all the necessary options for the free use of the tablet are present here. The cost of 9 thousand rubles. will allow you to purchase a great productive device that runs on Android and supports all 3G networks. This is not to say that the device has any serious disadvantages, since at its cost it is quite decent and even more or less high-quality. That is why this gadget is in good demand, and buyers leave mostly only positive reviews.
