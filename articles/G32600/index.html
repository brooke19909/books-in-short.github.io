How to make a spaceship out of paper quickly and easily

Having succumbed to the charm of science fiction, the beauty of space, scientific research and unusual stories, almost every one of us in a certain period of our life dreamed of becoming someday a real astronaut or explorer of the cosmic depths. I wanted to fly around the Earth, land on other planets, explore unknown worlds, just travel in an endless void. However, children do not just dream, they embody desires in games. So, how to make a spaceship out of paper?





Options

If you want to create a spaceship, you can resort to two options. One of them involves the possession of the ancient art of folding outlandish paper figures that came from mysterious Japan. Origami ships are elegant, beautiful, easy to fold. A spaceship made of paper can be made in another way: by drawing a layout, carefully cutting it out and folding it using glue or adhesive tape. Which option to choose is everyone's business.

Origami ship: getting started

For creativity you will need a real trifle: a rectangular sheet of paper. It can be color or white, it all depends on the intent of the creator. However, it is desirable that the width of the sheet is less than seven times as long. Work begins with the addition to the center of one corner of the paper, then the second (on the other hand). Following is the classic bomb, that is, the sheets open and fold again, but already to the opposite side of the taken leaf. The next step is folding the leaf from the folded to the center edges of the sheet. In a similar way, a simple paper airplane is created.





Origami: continued work

Now you need to fold the opposite ends of the sheet, that is, connect the two curved triangles. The figure collapses twice, the protrusions themselves are laid out from the folded base of the ship. Everything, the spaceship is ready. It can be left white or painted with colored pencils, crayons or felt-tip pens.

Spacecraft from the layout

How to make a spaceship out of paper, if you want to create something more ambitious, grandiose and complex? For this, there is a second option: the creation of ships using a breadboard model and improvised office supplies. Spaceships are very different: separate, on coasters, on the airfield or base, in front of a hangar for equipment. The resulting masterpiece can be used in games or even presented to friends.





Materials

So how to make a spaceship out of a layout? First you need to pick up the necessary tools. This, of course, is cardboard, colored paper, pencils, a ruler, scissors and stationery glue. It is also advisable to get thin tape.





How to make a boat: getting started

First, a sheet of cardboard is picked up, the height of the starship is determined. If it should look like a classic rocket, then everything is quite simple. A rectangle is cut out of the cardboard, which must be rolled up. The latter is fastened with glue. So the spaceship is ready. On another sheet of cardboard, draw the tail and wings, then carefully cut them.

Process completion

When figuring out how to make a spaceship out of paper, you need to remember that all patterns should be smooth, clear, and lines neat. So, in the lower part of the future spacecraft body, three slots erupt into which cut fins are inserted. Everything, you can proceed to the nose of the rocket. For this, an even circle with a diameter larger than the spacecraft body is cut out. It should be folded with a cone and glue the edges with glue or tape. The resulting nose is put on the body and attached with adhesive or all the same adhesive tape. Now that the base is ready, you can cut the decorations out of colored paper: stripes, decorations, portholes. They gently adhere to the spaceship.
