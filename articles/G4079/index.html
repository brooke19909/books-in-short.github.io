Description

Non-state pension fund Gazprom operates in the Russian financial market, engaged in activities to ensure pension savings, including early pension savings of citizens. The Gazprom organization also operates in the field of compulsory insurance of the pension fund.

Offices of the non-governmental pension fund Gazprom are opened in large cities throughout Russia, their total number has exceeded 60 units. This organization boasts incredible reliability, according to information provided by leading national rating agencies.

Since January 2015, the organization has been included in the register of non-state pension savings funds at number one. Gazprom is also a member of the self-regulatory organization NAPF. Fund employees have all the accumulated skills and abilities, and the company has vast experience in the financial sector.

NPF Gazprom for pensioners and working citizens





If a citizen who has reached retirement age has a previously concluded contract with an NPF, then he has the right to contact any nearest office of the company to make pension payments.

An attractive system is in place for working citizens, providing the funded part of pensions. By concluding an agreement with a company, an individual can effectively manage the cumulative part of his retirement pension by transferring it to the management of a reliable company.

People who paid taxes to the state every month, after concluding an agreement with a commercial pension fund, can take part in a special co-financing program, as well as generate additional payments when they reach retirement age. It is only necessary to personally participate in the NGO program.

In order to conclude an agreement with a Gazprom entity for an individual in order to manage their pension savings, a person needs only to have a passport and a certificate of state pension insurance. At the office of the NPF company, the client will be helped to fill out all the necessary documents, as well as provide detailed advice.
