What is scrabble

Scrabble is a board logic game designed for a small company from two to four people. The main goal is to compose words from the letters of the alphabet, for each of which the player receives a certain number of points.

The name of the game is literally translated from English as "rummaging, scratching, scribble."

There are many varieties of the legendary game. The most famous analogue of the Scrabble game in Russia is Scrabble. There is also "Balda" - a greatly simplified version of the game that does not require a special set.

How to play Scrabble

As a rule, the following components are present in a game set: a playing field, a bag with letters, and letter stands. It will also be useful to arm yourself with paper and a pen for scoring.

First of all, it is necessary to determine the order of progress. To do this, shuffle the letters in the bag and pull out one for each player. The player who receives the letter closest to the beginning of the alphabet makes the first move. The sequence of moves of the remaining players is determined in a similar way.

At the beginning of the game, each player takes 7 letter tokens. The player to complete the move first must lay out the word from the available letters, passing it through the middle of the board.

The essence of the game is to build the most ‚Äúexpensive‚Äù words. There are two ways to build words: from top to bottom or from left to right. When creating words, it is desirable to activate as many colored cells as possible.

On the playing field there are cells painted in one of four colors: blue, pink, blue or red.

Blue Points earned in one letter are doubled.
Pink Points earned per word are doubled.
Blue Points earned in a single letter triple.
Red Points earned per word triple.

Bonuses from several colored fields add up. Upon receipt of several bonuses, they are counted in the order of reading the word.

The game ends in two cases: when there are no letters left in the bag for distribution or when all the players "pass" twice in a row.

Determination of the winner is carried out by counting the points scored by each of the players. The participant with the most points is declared the winner.
