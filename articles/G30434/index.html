Pension "Anchor", Koktebel: photos and reviews

Pension "Anchor" is located in the resort village of Koktebel, which is located just 20 kilometers from Feodosia. Today, Crimea, including Koktebel, is developing fruitfully, attracting more and more vacationers from all over Russia and the near and sometimes far abroad. The thing is that this area has gorgeous prospects for ensuring a comfortable stay for many citizens.




Crimean nature is striking in its diversity and picturesque views. Only at sunset in the Crimea can you watch forever.

Koktebel Attractions



In Koktebel, vacationers on each beach are invited to fly a hang glider and feel like a bird. Diving courses along with experienced instructors are also in great demand.

Here you can even go for a walk through the clouds in a balloon. This type of leisure enjoys special popularity among couples and newlyweds, since there is nothing more romantic than a declaration of love made during take-off to heaven.

Pension "Anchor" (Koktebel) - this is exactly what you need for a better vacation, which will be remembered for a lifetime. If you need the help of medical workers, there is a huge team of qualified medical personnel.

The ecological situation on the territory of Koktebel is most favorable for treatment with natural resources, namely mud from local lakes, healing baths with healing water and clean mountain air, which literally pierces every cell in the body.

The resort is supplied with therapeutic mud at the expense of lakes under the names Ajigol and Chokrak. Rest and Wellness



The medical profile of the institution involves the treatment of the central nervous system, as well as the respiratory system. The base of the medical procedures of the boarding house includes physical therapy, various physiological procedures (electrophoresis, etc.) and an inhalation.

Of the water procedures, the guests are offered Sharko shower, hydromassage, contrast shower, coniferous and pearl baths.

Residence



Anchor (boarding house 2 *, Koktebel) has rooms occupying a five-story building. Here are apartments of various classifications, equipped with all necessary furniture and appliances for relaxation. Each room has a balcony for observing nature from above, there is a refrigerator, TV, shower, toilet with all necessary accessories. Amenities are complemented by modern air conditioning with heater support function.

The double rooms of the boarding house have 2 single or 1 double bed, bedside tables, coffee and writing tables, refrigerators and banquets, armchairs, a built-in wardrobe for storing outer clothing. All bathrooms in the rooms are combined, each has a mirror, shower, sink and hairdryer for drying hair after a shower. There is a set of towels.

Entertainment



From the entertainment on site - a magnificent pebble beach, equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. There are sun loungers and sun loungers, a volleyball, basketball and other active games ground, and a water transport rental center.

At the guests' disposal are a tennis court, a table for playing billiards, and equipment for power sports.

Those who come to the beach by car have nothing to worry about, because during the entertainment the car will be closely monitored by the guards, which, moreover, is absolutely free.

Communication with the outside world in the boarding house is carried out using pay phones, which are paid through special cards. Pension "Anchor" (Koktebel) has a bar with an abundance of various wines and drinks, as well as a library for those who like to while away time, re-reading long-forgotten but still favorite works of world classical literature.

In Koktebel there is a diving training school, quite famous in tourist circles, in which many professional "explorers of the deep sea" studied. Surfing here is also a very affordable form of water leisure. Boards can be rented.

Excursions

A multifaceted and exciting vacation offers its guests Crimea (Koktebel).

The new, recently (in 2007) open water park in Koktebel will delight with a variety of all kinds of slides and water attractions, which are made in the style of ancient pirates and their structures. The slides under the names Black Cuttlefish and Shore Brotherhood are especially impressive.

Among other things, the boarding house "Anchor" (Koktebel) offers guests the most interesting excursion trips to Yalta, Alushta, Feodosiya, Sudak and other nearby cities. A very pleasant pastime will be a boat trip, during which you can even sunbathe, enjoying the gentle sunshine.
