Country club "Yuna-Life": water park, fitness center, spa, hotel, reviews and prices

Yuna Life Holiday House is located near Moscow (on Dmitrovskoye Shosse). Its visitors relax after the tiresome city bustle. Here you can enjoy the fresh air and enjoy the beautiful scenery. At the same time, it is not at all necessary to abandon the familiar comfortable conditions. It offers guests modern rooms located in a business-class hotel, restaurants in which the chef offers gourmet dishes, as well as festive show programs.

Location

The opening of the Yuna Life holiday home took place in 2009. It is located eight kilometers from the Moscow Ring Road if you drive along Dmitrovskoye Shosse. Nearby is the Sheremetyevo-2 Airport. From it to the country club is only five minutes away by car. A truly wonderful place on the bank of the Klyazma reservoir was chosen for the holiday home.

Benefits

Yuna Life Holiday House has just recently opened. In this regard, he, unlike many similar institutions near Moscow, has a modern organization of space and leisure. Guests of Yuna Life have the opportunity to combine active and fashionable relaxation. It offers visitors a spa complex and swimming pools, a fitness club and a water park, tennis courts and a restaurant, a platform for indoor soccer and much more.



With its comfort and modernity, the number of rooms of the establishment is also pleasing. Visitors are offered apartments, town houses, as well as double rooms. Any of the visitors can choose an option acceptable for themselves.

Another advantage of a holiday home is its compact size. This allows you to quickly get to the right place, located on the territory of "Yuna Life", in order to get pleasure from the provided rest. All that is needed is nearby.



This magnificent holiday home can be booked in advance. To do this, just visit the official website of the company and leave your application. Yuna Life Country Club offers prices to potential visitors only direct. The site contains additional information regarding the holiday home (location map, address, contact numbers, room rates and photos). It also posted reviews of those who have already visited this wonderful place.

Territory

Yuna Life is an entire architectural complex. It is made in a modern style inherent in Western Europe. On the territory of the complex there are three residential buildings, which are interconnected by an aquazone. This is a real highlight of Yuna Life. The water park offers wonderful water rides for guests of any age.

Number of rooms

Hotel Yuna Life offers spacious two-story townhouses to accommodate guests. They are in the first building. Their area is from ninety to one hundred and fifteen square meters. They are designed to accommodate from two to six people.



In the second building are one-story apartments. Their area is from eighty-six to one hundred and thirteen square meters. At the same time, from two to eight guests can settle here.

The third building includes double rooms ranging in size from twenty to forty square meters. At the same time, each of them is unique in its interior and design, while at the same time meeting the standards that are provided for the luxury category.
All rooms have satellite TV, air conditioning, and wireless Internet (Wi-Fi). It is worth noting that all residential premises are designed in such a way as to satisfy the highest requirements of customers. The number of rooms is a symbiosis of the amenities and advantages of a spa stay with the warmth and comfort of home space. Any of the living quarters corresponds to a pleasant expensive interior of a decent country house. This is the entrance hall, and the furnished living room, and the kitchen room with various built-in appliances, and romantic bright bedrooms, which contain elements of oriental decor, and a large bathroom, which is equipped with a shower room with a jet massage or a jacuzzi. All this creates an atmosphere of comfort, harmony and an incomparable aesthetic picture.

From the rooms of the first and second buildings there are separate exits to the territory of the holiday home. The landscape is made in the English classical style. Here you can admire the Alpine slides and neatly trimmed lawns, a stone bridge that is thrown over a natural brook, as well as take advantage of the sunbathing areas, summer terraces, and sit in the open cozy lobby bar.

Aquazones

They are available in every building of the holiday home. Moreover, their round-the-clock visits for guests of Yuna Life are absolutely free. The aquazones have two indoor pools ( one of them can be accessed directly from the rooms of the first building), a bath and sports complex (with a Turkish bath and a Finnish sauna), a jacuzzi and a water park. Guests at the holiday home can take part in traditional bathing ceremonies held by high-class professionals.

Sports complex

For those who prefer a healthy and active lifestyle, the country holiday house offers classes in the fitness center. This is a large sports and fitness complex, which includes a gym, a five-a-side football field, a table tennis court, outdoor tennis courts and much more. Guests of the country club have a unique opportunity to hold not only individual, but also collective gaming and sports events. Toddlers attend a children's tennis academy year-round.
