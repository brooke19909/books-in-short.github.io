How to make ripped shorts from jeans? Second life of things

What to do if the old, but the most favorite jeans are not only out of fashion, but also frayed at the bottom? It is already impossible to wear them, and the hand does not rise to throw it away. After all, they fit so well ... Only one thing remains: to make shorts from old jeans! The idea, of course, is good, but how to implement it? Everything is much simpler than it seems at first glance.

How to make ripped shorts from jeans: which jeans are suitable



To get stylish and fashionable shorts from old, but still dearly beloved jeans , not so much is needed. Wash your trousers first. If possible, try to remove all stains and old dirt. Even if you think that this place will still be below the cut line. After the jeans have dried, you can start creating! Most importantly, they sit well on the hips and waist. In this case, the shorts will perfectly emphasize the figure.

They can always be covered with applique, embroidery, rhinestones or just make a hole in this place, imitating ripped jeans. Depending on what style the shorts will be, you can either hem them or just leave a cut edge. If you plan small holes, then the edge can be hemmed. If the product is in a more relaxed style, then there is no point in hemming. As a result of simple manipulations, you managed to trim the old jeans. A new life for them will now begin in the form of shorts. This will allow you to stay with your favorite thing much longer, even in a new look!
