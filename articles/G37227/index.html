How to transfer money from MegaFon to MegaFon: popular ways

Everyone has heard that MegaFon has a service that allows you to transfer money from one number to another. But many may not know how to do this. But this function can be very useful, especially when you or your friend has run out of funds on your balance, and you need to urgently call. And in this article we will figure out how to transfer money from MegaFon to MegaFon. Three main methods that the directly presented operator provides will be analyzed. In addition, we will tell you how to transfer money from MTS to MegaFon.

Transfer using a USSD request

The first in line will be considered the Mobile Transfer service. It is carried out using a USSD request, which is quite convenient. Let's look at how to transfer money from MegaFon to MegaFon using it.





As mentioned above, you will need to send a USSD request. It looks like this: * 133 * amount _ which_ will be sent * recipient_number #.

After that you need to press the call key. To make it more clear, let's look at an example. Suppose you decide to transfer to the number 89264358955 the amount of 500 rubles. To do this, dial * 133 * 500 * 89264358955 # and press call.

After you do this, you will receive an SMS with confirmation. It will also include instructions on what to do next. When you fulfill all the conditions, the transfer will be completed.

This was the first way to transfer money from MegaFon to MegaFon, but far from the last. Two more are in line, so we move on immediately.

Translation via SMS

Now let's talk about how to transfer money from Megafon, not only to MegaFon, but also the numbers of other operators. This will help us a service from a company called "Money Transfer". This operation is carried out using an SMS request. This method may seem much easier to many.





To get started, start creating an SMS message. In the text box, enter the number to which you send the money, and then, after a space, the amount of sending. All this, therefore, must be sent to the number 8900. In order to be more clear, it is worth giving an example. The following should appear in the text field: "9264358955 500". Note that data is indicated with a space.

Translation through the MegaFon website

There is another answer to the question of how to transfer money from MegaFon to MegaFon. The service is provided directly through the site and is the simplest of all presented. Therefore, if you have at hand a computer connected to the Internet, it is recommended to use it.

Go to money.megafon.ru/. On the main page, select "To another phone." You will see the desired page. A form will be presented there. Everything is pretty simple. First enter the amount you want to send, then the recipient‚Äôs number, and then your number.

Now you need to confirm that you are not a robot and click on "Translate". You will be taken to a page where the data you entered will be indicated. If everything is accurate, then click "Translate". A message with instructions for confirmation should now come to your number. Following it, follow all the steps and the money will be sent to the user.

Transfer from MTS to MegaFon

Now let's figure out how to transfer money from MTS to MegaFon. This is done very simply through the MTS website. This method is very similar to the third presented in this article. So let's get started.





You need to log in to https: // pay.mts.ru. Now follow the instructions:

  1. In the left menu you need to select "Mobile Phone".
  2. Now on the operator selection page, select "MegaFon".
  3. You need to fill out the form. Indicate the recipient's number, transfer amount and do not forget to check the item "From the MTS mobile phone account".
  4. Click "Next."
  5. Log in to your account.
  6. Confirm the operation started.

After that, you can check your account, the amount equal to that indicated by you during the transfer, plus the commission should be withdrawn from it.
