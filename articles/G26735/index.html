"Burj Al Arab" - a 27-story building in the form of a sail with a height of 381 m (above the Eiffel Tower). Each room in it is two-story. The smallest is 170 m2 and the largest is 780 m2. There is no general reception in the hotel, here the "Check-in" takes place in the rooms. On the "jumper" of the sail, at an altitude of two hundred meters (18th floor) is the restaurant "Al Muntaha". The elevator lifts guests at a speed of 7 m / s. This most expensive hotel in Dubai has its own beach. Here guests are provided with various services for free. For example, wipe glasses or put a cucumber mask on your face . Due to the fact that the hotel belongs to the Jumeirah network, tourists are allowed to visit all the restaurants and beaches included in it.

Hotel "Atlantis"

The rest that Dubai offers is not comparable with anything. Hotels compete with each other in terms of service, quality and quantity of services provided. The rating of hotels in Dubai changes quite often. However, there are hotels that are out of competition and are in the first places of the list, and the service in them is always at the highest level. One of these is Atlantis. The building is located on its own beach. From the windows of the rooms there is a wonderful view of the waters of the Persian Gulf or the city.
