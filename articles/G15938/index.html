Details on how to call the police from your mobile

Often situations happen in life when calling the police is a matter of vital importance. Let's ask any person if he knows how to do this? We will say with full confidence that units will respond positively.



It used to be simple, since there were landline telephones, and they could call the police by dialing 02. Now the matter is complicated by the fact that people use mostly more sophisticated equipment, and not everyone knows how to call the police from a mobile phone. Let's try to figure out how to do it right. If you simply dial the unified police number 02, as on a landline phone, then nothing will work. Called phones must contain at least 3 digits in the cellular communicator. What to do, how to call the police from a mobile?

Special combinations

There is a call option from a single international and Russian numbers. Option 911 is known to all. This is an international rescue call number. On it you can call the police from a mobile phone. The operator will listen to you and redirect the call to the police services or give you the police number of your region. Phone number 112 has recently also become known to almost all residents of our country. This is a combination of calling a single rescue service in Russia. You can call this number from any mobile operator, and even when the phone does not have a SIM card.

Save everything in the phone book



In order to quickly attract an outfit of special services in an emergency and not to wonder how to call the police from a mobile phone, be sure to write down all the numbers of the police station in the directory. The fact is that under stress, the human body can react inappropriately. You can get confused, completely disconnect from the real situation and not immediately remember the phone or even forget it. But you will clearly remember that in the contacts you have the right number.

Regional Police Numbers

Gradually, we are approaching the final answer to the question of how to call the police from a mobile phone. The numbers of the uniform rescue services are good because they will always answer you. But there are also pitfalls. As you know, there are always a lot of calls to the numbers of these services. Operators have to redirect incoming calls and clarify information, and this takes time. Another nuance is that you may not immediately get to the right employee, which in some cases is undesirable, because help is needed immediately. In order to protect yourself, you must make sure in advance that in an unforeseen situation you don‚Äôt rush about how to call the police from your mobile phone. Find out the phone numbers in your area in advance and enter them in your contacts. By doing so, you will feel more confident and reliable.



Numbers of special services of mobile operators

You should be aware that the number of call guards of different operators is somewhat different. And in order to know how to call the police from your mobile, you need to contact your service provider. You should also find out the short phone numbers on the website of the same operators.

Take the main mobile operators: MTS, Megafon, Tele2 and Beeline. If you use the services of the first three, then the representatives of the law can be heard on the short number 020. And in order to call the police from Beeline mobile, dial 002.



For smartphone owners, there is another unique opportunity to call law enforcement. Install a special application on your phone. In this case, the owner will have an advantage over other users of cell phones. It will not only allow you to quickly contact law enforcement agencies, but also write a statement to the police and find your local precinct. If you find yourself in an unfamiliar village, the application will be a godsend.

We hope that we have fully described how to use a cell phone when calling the police. It is worth adding that the numbers can be allocated to a special group or made available for speed dialing. The technical capabilities of mobile phones allow this to be done without problems. Then it will be possible to call the police very quickly, with two clicks of buttons. It remains only to wish that you do not happen such situations when you need to urgently call the guards.
