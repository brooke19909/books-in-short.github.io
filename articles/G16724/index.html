Trout farm in Kislovodsk: outdoor activities and delicious cuisine

The beautiful Caucasian city of Kislovodsk is a real treasure of the region. Sometimes it is called a pearl, because it is hidden between the hills, as in the shells. The mild climate attracts tourists throughout the year. In Kislovodsk health resorts you can relax, improve your health with local mineral water and enjoy beautiful views of the mountains. Travel agencies, in addition to traditional attractions, offer to visit a trout farm in Kislovodsk, founded at the beginning of the 20th century.
