DIY Tips for making a Star Wars stormtrooper costume

Every fan is required to have a white Stormtrooper helmet from the Star Wars saga. The interest in the universe created by George Lucas is so great that people are ready to spend a huge amount of money and time to create costumes exactly like the heroes of films. The characters and costumes of the story are so thought out that it is not easy to do this.

Fans know that there are more than eight kinds of helmets. Costumes of attack aircraft of different types of troops also differ from each other. But if the fan made a firm decision, then nothing is impossible.

Create a design layout for a Star Wars stormtrooper costume

The first step is to choose the costume that needs to be done. Having decided, find as many high-quality images of characters in a suit as possible in order to consider the outfit and equipment from different angles in the smallest details. For example, you can take a first-order attack aircraft, a photo of which is given below.





It is necessary to carefully examine the suit, select the details and elements that can be replaced with ready-made things, for example, look for similar shoes among old shoes and simply paint them white, pockets and bandoleers can be replaced with plastic boxes, and thin black will perfectly play the role of an elastic underwear turtleneck and knit pants.

It is known that in the armor of the stormtrooper from the first Star Wars movie there were 18 details. Consider the level of detail of the costume: instead of 18 parts, you can make 10, but the character‚Äôs costume will still be recognizable. The following image shows the main parts of the armor of a stormtrooper costume.





In free access there are patterns of costumes and helmets of stormtroopers. Finding patterns to fit your size will not be so easy. You can cut the suit on the finished patterns of plain paper, glue and arrange a fitting. The selection and editing of finished drawings can take a lot of time and effort, sometimes it is easier to do from scratch than to edit an already finished work. Attention in the finished patterns worthy of the drawings of the helmet - they can be safely taken to work.

Choosing material for a stormtrooper costume

The choice of material determines all further work on the costume. The helmet can be made from plastic bottles, papier-m√¢ch√© and cardboard. When choosing the first option, you will need to look for a bottle that is similar in shape and size, for papier-m√¢ch√©, you need to think about what to make the basis of and whether there will be enough art skills available for the master, and if you choose cardboard, you will also need putty or epoxy to strengthen the product.

Regardless of the material chosen, many paintwork materials will be required:

  • base for paint;
  • white paint in a spray can;
  • black paint in a spray can;
  • glue;
  • black electrical tape;
  • felt-tip pen;
  • a sharp knife for cutting thick cardboard;

How to make a stormtrooper costume from paper

A stormtrooper costume will require a lot of thick cardboard. According to the layout of the outfit, draw the details of the suit on cardboard. Further, the blanks are cut with a special sharp knife, the necessary bends are made and they are tried on for the future attack aircraft. If necessary, the details are shortened, the fastener system is thought out, depending on the individual dimensions of the model.

Parts made of non-thick cardboard are easier to bend - there are no bends left on them and they look better. The downside of such armor will be their fragility. Putty can help in this situation. It is necessary to spread the putty and apply a thin layer on the inside of the armor.





When the parts of the costume are adjusted and processed, apply a base on them, and after drying, cover with white paint from a spray can. It is better to apply black parts with a felt-tip pen over white paint.

Costume detail

The most crucial moment in creating a do-it-yourself stormtrooper costume is detailing. Even if inaccuracies and flaws were made at the stage of creating the armor blanks, adding small details to the existing base will help to fix the suit and make it easily recognizable.

It is worth spending time in the toy store and trying to find the most similar models of plastic pistols, boxes and other small and at first glance unimportant details. If you do not find elements similar in shape and size, you can use polymer clay to make jewelry. It is necessary to sculpt on top of the armor, paint on top. Plastic weapons should also be decorated in branded black and white colors.

We master the stormtrooper helmet

As previously stated, for a helmet it is better to take ready-made patterns that need to be cut from plain cardboard. Helmet parts have a large number of small folds. All of them are neatly cut and glued. The photo below shows an already glued helmet.





To hide the joints, you can use electrical tape and glue the helmet. A thin layer of plaster must be applied on both sides. Then the helmet is painted and applied parts. The costume is ready!
