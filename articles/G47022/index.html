Hotels in Sochi welcome their guests

Sochi is probably the name of the city many people know, because it is a magnificent resort with warm, sea, clean beaches, beautiful nature and local attractions. There are a lot of hotels, hotels, boarding houses and sanatoriums in the city, which will gladly accept tourists of the resort. Among a wide variety of hotels you can choose inexpensive, with sea views, with a private pool.

Not all vacationers can afford accommodation in an expensive hotel, so a number of comfortable and inexpensive hotels are suitable for them. Mini-Hotel Marianna is located in the center of Sochi, in the immediate vicinity of major shopping centers, near the sea and railway station. The rooms in the hotel are cozy, furnished with modern furniture, each of them has air conditioning, Internet access. On the ground floor, a cafe is open for visitors, where you can eat, drink a cup of coffee with delicious, fresh pastries. Pleasantly surprised by the proximity of the hotel to the sea: it is no more than 300 meters.

Two-star hotel "Victor" is a four-story building. Which is located in the very center of the city, not far from the circus. The hotel has excellent service, good rooms equipped with a split system,   there is a TV, necessary household appliances, you can eat in the dining room, where the cooks will surprise you with delicious, home-cooked dishes. From the windows of the hotel offers beautiful views of the park area, the coastline of the sea, which can be reached in just 15 minutes. If you need to book a room with one of the hotels in Sochi, you can rent here .

Very close to the sea, just 150 meters away, is the VIVA guest house, it is a three-story building with balconies, the original layout of the rooms. The building rises in the very center of Sochi, has comfortable rooms with air conditioning, soft beds, a sofa, TV, refrigerator and everything you need for a good rest.

There are many hotels in the city with their own pools, of course living in them is a bit more expensive, but comfort and service are worth the cost. A large and comfortable seawater pool is at the Sea Star Hotel. This hotel is located in the center of Sochi, in a quiet, environmentally friendly area. Remoteness from busy highways makes rest in it more complete. Vacationers settle in comfortable rooms, furnished with modern, upholstered furniture. Each room has a shower, soft towels are provided, linen is changed and rooms are cleaned every day. The water in the pool is filtered and often changes, swimming in such water will bring pleasure, help relieve accumulated fatigue, but if you decided to swim in the sea, then it is only 700 meters with a quick step.
