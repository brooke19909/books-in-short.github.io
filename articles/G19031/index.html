Hilton Hotel, Kaluga: photos and descriptions, addresses, services, reviews

The Hilton Business Hotel in Kaluga provides excellent accommodation conditions for both business and leisure travelers. The location on one of the central streets provides convenient access to cultural and business facilities. The fact that the institution belongs to a well-known network is a kind of guarantee of high quality service.

Location

The address of the Hilton Hotel in Kaluga is 74/3 Saltykova-Shchedrina Street. It is about 10 minutes walk from the historic city center. Nearby there are several public transport stops, from where you can quickly get to anywhere in the city. The train station is 5 km away and the airport is 8 km away.

The closest to the hotel are such cultural and infrastructural objects:

  • Victory statue - 2 km;
  • Central Park of Culture and Rest - 2 km;
  • Regional Drama Theater - 3 km;
  • Regional Philharmonic Society - 3 km;
  • Museum of Local Lore - 3 km;
  • Tsiolkovsky House-Museum - 4 km;
  • Museum of the History of Cosmonautics - 4 km.

What to see in Kaluga?

If you are planning to visit Kaluga, plan your cultural program in advance. Here are the attractions that are most popular:

  • The Museum of the History of Cosmonautics is the largest museum dedicated to space exploration in Russia and even in the world. The museum was founded in 1967 with the direct participation of Yuri Gagarin and academician Korolev.
  • Kirov Street is the central street of the city, on which the main attractions, administrative institutions and business objects are concentrated. There are many beautiful old buildings.
  • Regional Museum of Local Lore - the building is an architectural monument dated to the 17th century. This picturesque mansion is well preserved until today almost unchanged.
  • Museum of Fine Arts - was founded in 1917. The institution is located in the building of the estate of the XIX century, built in the Empire style. The basis of the exposition is a private collection of the doctor and philanthropist Vasiliev.
  • The Tsiolkovsky Museum is the house in which the space explorer Tsiolkovsky lived and worked for 29 years. A year after the death of the scientist, his memorial museum was founded here.
  • The House of Masters is a club museum located in a wooden house of the 19th century. In 1990, the building was restored and the museum was opened. Today, there are presented handicrafts and samples of decorative and applied art.
  • Chambers of merchants Korobovyh is one of the main attractions of the city, a striking example of stylized architecture of the XVII century.
  • The Cathedral of the Trinity Life-Giving - the building of 1819, which is the second version of the temple. The first was built in the XVII century from wood.
  • The Church of Cosmas and Damian is an elegant five-domed church, which was erected in 1794 according to the project of one of the students of the famous Rastrelli. The walls are decorated with mosaics depicting the acts of St. Nicholas the Wonderworker and St. George the Victorious.
  • Victory Square is a memorial complex consisting of an obelisk, a fountain, a granite wall and the Eternal Flame. In 2014, all facilities were restored.
  • The park named after K.E. Tsiolkovsky is the place where the scientist himself once liked to walk. The park was laid back in the XVIII century with the planting of a linden alley.
