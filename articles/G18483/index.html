Repino: attractions, museums

What is interesting can be seen in "Penates"

The most important attraction in Repino is the Penates Cottage Museum. Russian artist Ilya Repin lived in this building with his wife. For thirty years, a creative family lived in this cozy and beautifully equipped house with various outbuildings.

Unfortunately, today tourists are not able to see the original design and interior, because the building was completely burned down during the war. Nevertheless, caring architects exactly restored this Repino landmark in order to preserve the memory for many years.



On the territory of the site is a beautiful house with a cozy park area, gazebos and a pond. The interior of each room is amazing and original. Even the gates leading to the courtyard are distinguished by a special architectural design.

The story of a creative family is attractive and intriguing. Once a week, an open day was held here. On this day, the house could be visited by anyone who longs for communication with famous people. The artist helped guests feel at home. They themselves could go into any room, enjoy the treats that the owners of the allotments must have prepared for dear guests. In a unique way, the dining room was equipped on which the inscription was visible: "Open the doors and go in." In the center of the room was a table, the middle of which was spinning non-stop, so that each guest could be treated to the desired dish.



In summer, the museum-cottage is open from 10.30 in the morning until 18.00, and in winter from 10.30 to 16.00. Entrance to the territory costs 200 rubles.

House of Writers in Komarovo

On the street Lieutenants House 31 there is another very curious attraction: the House of Writers. The building was built back in 1956. The building was inhabited by Soviet writers who could indulge in a muse in this building calmly and without excessive noise and create their works, which were later read by people of the entire USSR.

Locals said that from all the windows of this building the sounds of typewriters were heard daily. Usually this lasted until lunch, and at that moment even passers-by died down so as not to disturb peace and not distract writers from the creative process.

The rooms of the writers' house were tiny; they were arranged poorly. Nevertheless, the creators were comfortable working in such conditions, because one person relied on a separate room.

You can go inside the house if you first agree with the administrators of the Writer's House. This will allow you to fully plunge into the atmosphere of the institution.

Chukovsky cottage

Therefore, in this place it smells of history, the spirit of the muse is felt. Currently, tourists are only given the opportunity to see the place where the cottage was, since in 1986 the building and everything that was on the territory adjacent to the building burned down. To this day, local authorities are trying to achieve the restoration of the former dacha of the famous poet.

Central Park in Repino

Description of Repin's attraction, such as the central park, is quite interesting. It is large enough, it was originally built on a wasteland. In one of the parts of the park there is a canal that flows into the lovers' pond. Famous writers, poets and composers liked to walk around this park.



The wide streets shine with greenery, along the entire perimeter of the park there are benches on which you can sit while enjoying nature. It occupies a fairly large area, so it is worth going there all day to take a leisurely stroll through the most beautiful surroundings.

Attractions in Komarovo

All the sights of the Leningrad region in Repino and Komarovo are difficult to list. A popular and frequently visited place in this area is the Komarov necropolis. There is a tourist attraction on Ozernaya street. It is here that the oldest cemetery is located, where you can visit the graves of famous prose writers, poets, composers, scientists. The grave of Akhmatova is the Repino attraction, which is most visited in the Komarov necropolis.



At the burial site of Akhmatova, a monument is installed, which is visible from almost anywhere in the cemetery. Throughout the necropolis, the burial of the poetess is the most noticeable, large.
