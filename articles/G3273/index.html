Makryammos Bungalows Hotel 4 * (Greece / Thassos): photos, prices and reviews

Thassos, part of Greece, deservedly bears the title of the greenest island of the entire Aegean Sea. So, olive trees, pines, spruces, cedars, lindens and numerous shrubs grow here. In addition, this island is famous for its luxurious white beaches and excellent climate. Holidays here are preferred by people who appreciate the magnificence of nature, tranquility and the purest azure sea. If you decide to spend an unforgettable vacation on the island of Thassos (Greece), Makryammos Bungalows Hotel 4 * will be a great option for a place to stay. We suggest today to get to know this hotel more closely, and also to find out what impressions our compatriots have about their holidays here.





Thasos island, Makryammos Bungalows Hotel 4 *: photos and description

This hotel consists of picturesque bungalows located in a pine forest on its own territory of the hotel complex near the coast of the Aegean Sea. It was built in the last century, the last large-scale reconstruction was carried out here in 2006. Today the hotel complex offers everyone to stay in one of 206 comfortable rooms of the following categories: standard (single and double), superior and suites. All of them are quite spacious and equipped with everything necessary (air conditioning, bathroom, TV, refrigerator, etc.). Also on site there are swimming pools for adults and children, a restaurant, a bar.

Prices

As for the cost of accommodation at the Makryammos Bungalows Hotel 4 *, it fully corresponds to both its category and living conditions, as well as the level of service. So, a day of living here will cost you in the amount of 120 euros. For early bookings, this price can be significantly lower.





Makryammos Bungalows Hotel 4 * (Thassos, Limenas): reviews of vacationers from the Russian Federation

Since today the vast majority of travelers, when planning a vacation, focuses not only on the official description of a particular hotel and the advice of tour operators, but also on the impressions of people who have already stayed here, we decided to ease your task a bit and give generalized comments from our compatriots about their pastime in Makriamos Bungalos. Looking ahead a bit, it is no exaggeration to say that, according to many tourists, this hotel is one of the best on the island of Thassos. Therefore, most of the travelers from Russia were very pleased with the choice made. And they absolutely do not regret about the money spent on vacation. But we will understand everything in detail.

According to the guests, a cot can be provided here on request without any problems. If necessary, you can also use an iron and an electric kettle. To do this, you should contact the reception. Tourists who have already visited the Makryammos Bungalows Hotel 4 *, reviews of which we are considering, advise travelers arriving here to bring slippers and (optionally) a bathrobe, since these items are not provided to guests in the rooms.

Dining experience

As for meals at Makryammos Bungalows Hotel 4 * (Greece), reviews from tourists from Russia are mostly positive. So, according to them, breakfasts are quite diverse. Many were pleased that, in addition to the standard set of several types of sausages, cheeses, bread, granola, rolls, cereals, etc., every day the cooks fried eggs, scrambled eggs, bacon, pancakes. The guests also liked the choice of dishes offered for dinner. The menu always had superbly prepared first and second courses, as well as several salads, a lot of sweets and fresh fruit. In addition, several times a week there are evenings of barbecue, national Greek and Italian cuisine and fish dishes. Drinks are available for a fee.

As for lunch, you can eat for a fee at the hotel restaurant or go to one of the Limenas taverns. You can be sure that almost everywhere they cook great.





Did tourists like the hotel staff?

Concerning the staff of Makryammos Bungalows Hotel 4 * (Thassos, Limenas), our compatriots also had a positive opinion. So, according to the guests, the staff here, as, indeed, in most hotels in Greece, are very friendly and attentive. It employs highly erudite people who are always happy to answer any question and help in solving any problem. In addition, a big plus is the fact that the staff at the reception, like most of the rest of the staff, speak Russian. So you will not have any communication problems here.

Impressions of vacationers about entertainment at the hotel

As for other entertainments, there are practically none of them at the Makriamos Bungalos hotel. So, for example, you will not find noisy animation here all day and evening.
