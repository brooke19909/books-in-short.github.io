Where is Evpatoria, how to get to the city, what places to visit

Evpatoria is a small town on the north-west coast of Crimea. Its area is 65.4 km 2 , and its length is only 22 km. Nevertheless, it is one of the largest resorts in this part of the peninsula.

Where is Evpatoria

Evpatoria is 65 kilometers from Simferopol, you can get to it by train, train, bus or car. Buses are easy to find - they stand across the road from the station, depart every 20 minutes and go to the Evpatoria bus station, which is also located near the railway. After 21.00 it is best to order a taxi, since minibuses no longer run. The city is located in the steppe of the Crimean peninsula on the shores of the Gulf of Kalamitsky, in this area there are no mountains and hills. Height above sea level - about 10 meters. In the place where Evpatoria is located, the best sandy beaches of Crimea.



City climate

It has a unique climate: coastal-steppe, moderately dry, warm, without sharp changes in temperature and pressure. The air, filled with the aroma of steppe grasses, is constantly changing, thanks to the monsoons and good purging of the flat terrain. On three sides, the city of Yevpatoriya is washed by reservoirs: the Black Sea from the south, Lake Sasyk-Sivash from the east and Lake Moinaki with a series of estuaries - from the west. There are more sunny days in this town than in other resorts of the peninsula. The sea is clean, warms up quickly, the difference in depth is smooth. The climate in the place where Yevpatoria is located is compared with the best resorts in Italy and France.

Moinaki Lake

Walking along the narrow streets of the Old Town, you can catch one of the famous Yevpatoriya trams and get to the healing lake Moinaki. Recently, due to human intervention, the ecosystem of this formerly famous mud lake has been disrupted. The place where Evpatoria is located has always been famous for the healing properties of its brine and mud. But, starting from the nineties, they began to gradually disappear, as a result of which Evpatoria could lose the glory of a balneological resort. The mud baths, for which it was once impossible to get into treatment, were neglected for a long time. In 2015, it was decided to restore the dilapidated building of the recreation complex and the adjacent territory.
