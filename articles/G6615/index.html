LCD "Orange": prices, layouts, reviews

When deciding on the acquisition of real estate, many today pay attention to new buildings. Modern layouts, high-quality communications, convenient location of the house - all this is available to everyone. Excellent apartments are offered by the Arsenal construction company in the Orange residential complex.

Developer Overview

The construction company Arsenal has been operating in the market for more than 10 years. During this time, many buyers have already managed to move into their apartments. A house was commissioned in Vologda at the address: Karl Marx Street, 103. More than 200 apartments were sold.



A new multifunctional residential complex is being built on the same street today . LCD "Orange" (Vologda) will be commissioned in 2018. Today, investors and buyers can observe the progress of construction. The construction company managed to gain customer confidence thanks to the interesting architecture of the buildings and convenient layouts. On the forums you can find many good reviews from those people who have already managed to call in their new homes.

LCD "Orange" in Vologda

How does a developer attract buyers? First of all, this is the quality of the material from which the houses are made. People pay attention primarily to reliability and safety, because in the new apartment will have to spend more than one decade. Walls and partitions are built of high-quality brick with good heat and sound insulation. In winter, such buildings are warm and cool in summer.

LCD "Orange" - a nine-story high-rise buildings. Buyers note that in each entrance there are two elevators (passenger and freight). In houses that have already been commissioned, a house territory has been equipped. There is a parking, sports and playground.

A lot of good reviews can be heard about the developed infrastructure. Five minutes from the residential complex is a public transport stop, although Karl Marx Street itself is almost the center of the city. Nearby is a beautiful green park, many cafes, restaurants and shopping malls.

Not only the Arsenal construction company offers good apartments in the Orange residential complex. Reviews show that complexes with the same name are in other cities.

Sochi

Along Pasichna Street (house 57), a nine-story high-rise building was commissioned. LCD "Orange" in Sochi is a building that meets all European quality standards. Customer reviews confirm this. The houses were erected on a monolithic foundation, the walls - from the block. Such material perfectly retains heat. Buyers are also pleased with high-quality sound insulation. The most popular are two-room apartments (from 60 sq. M.). Price - from 75 thousand rubles per meter.



Pleased with well-developed infrastructure. There is a public transport stop near the house. Nearby are shopping centers, cafes, restaurants, pharmacies, grocery stores. A preschool educational institution operates 10 minutes from the building.

The house area is also well-maintained. There is a playground for children, and on the ground floor there is a parking lot.

St. Petersburg

In the northern capital there is also an LCD "Orange". St. Petersburg is a city that is developing dynamically. More and more tall buildings appear not only on the outskirts, but also in the center. A lot of good reviews can be heard about the house, located near the metro station "Parnas" in the Vyborg district. The building has already been commissioned, many buyers managed to move into their apartments.



The residential complex is represented by three monolithic buildings. In total, 900 apartments are offered (300 in each house). People who have already managed to buy real estate here leave at least positive reviews. Pleased with the layout, as well as a beautifully landscaped house area. There are playgrounds and guest parking.

Buyers are offered one-room studio apartments, "kopecks" and "three rubles". Prices - from 70 thousand rubles per square meter.

Odessa

An excellent apartment can be purchased in the LCD "Orange" in Odessa. The house is located in a seaside area, in a place with well-developed infrastructure. Only 15 minutes away, according to reviews, is the historical center of Odessa.



The new building has already been commissioned. Everyone can call into the apartment immediately after concluding a deal with the developer. Three types of apartments are offered: one-room (from 40 sq. M.), Two-room (from 62 sq. M.), Three-room (from 89 sq. M.). The price of a "square" is from 50 thousand rubles.

Reviews show that the house is really thought out to the smallest detail. The partitions between the apartments are made of thick aerated concrete blocks, providing high-quality sound insulation. Each staircase has a high-speed silent elevator. The apartments have false ceilings, painted walls.

If you believe the reviews, all the described residential complexes are perfect for life. However, before making a choice, it is worth weighing all the pros and cons. To blindly trust the opinions expressed on the Internet is not worth it.
