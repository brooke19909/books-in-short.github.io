Actually, vacation here can hardly be called an idle pastime. As for the climate, it is tropical trade wind in this part of the Caribbean islands. In January, the thermometer rises to an average of twenty degrees, and in summer to +35. The weather in Cuba in October, for example, has its own vagaries: the fact is that at this time the rainy season is ending, and every day it is getting better. Moreover, the sun at this time is not as aggressive as in the summer or in September.

The main reason that many are forced to refuse to visit Cuba is the high cost of trips due to the long transatlantic flight. Therefore, most tourists buy tours in October, when air carriers make generous discounts. In turn, the weather in Cuba in October is quite suitable for beach bliss.
