Rear panel

Display

The matrix of the screen is made using IPS technology. This means that the screen has good viewing angles. In the sun, the display looks good, there is a good margin of brightness, designed just for such situations, so as not to burn out the picture and fonts in natural light. Otherwise, it should be noted that due to the use of IPS technology, the image will be smoothed, and the load on the eyes of the owner will be reduced to a minimum value. That, by the way, allows you to read comfortably even in a dark environment without serious damage to eyesight.

The diagonal of the screen in this case is 4.7 inches, the resolution is 960 x 540 pixels. With color rendering, everything is fine, the display displays up to sixteen million different colors and their shades. The touch screen is a capacitive type.
