Beldibi Santana Hotel 3 - An Excellent Vacation in Turkey

Description: Beldibi Santana Hotel 3 is an excellent three-star hotel located in the village of Beldibi 1, 26 kilometers from the central district of Antalya.

It occupies the second beach line. It is 11 kilometers from the city of Kemer, and 41 kilometers from Antalya Airport.

The hotel itself includes two three-story buildings, built in 2005.

Numbers: Beldibi Santana Hotel 3 consists of 88 rooms. All are spacious, decorated in soft colors.



The room has a TV, there are two Russian channels, a telephone, air conditioning, a minibar (empty). For work, a desktop is installed. The bathroom is tiled (floor and walls), there is a shower. Each has a balcony. This room can accommodate one extra bed. Pets are not allowed.

Nutrition: Beldibi Santana Hotel 3 features a main hotel restaurant with an open-air terrace . The restaurant seats up to 90 people. During meals, all Turkish-made drinks are served free of charge. In addition to the restaurant, there are two more bars, the opening hours of which are from 9.30 to 23.00. One is located near the main restaurant, and the second is by the pool. Light snacks are offered to vacationers throughout the day. In addition, here you can buy any local drinks, ice cream, freshly squeezed juices.

Beach: Beldibi Santana Hotel 3 has its own pebble beach, located 250 meters from the hotel. Umbrellas, beach towels and sun loungers are provided free of charge. Guests at the beach can enjoy various water activities, including a boat trip.

Additional Information: For the convenience of holidaymakers, on-site parking is available. There is a superb outdoor pool, sunbeds are provided free of charge. For those who decide to combine work and leisure, there is a conference room, the Internet in all public places. There is a separate room for luggage storage, the administrator has a safe (for a fee) for storing valuables of guests during their holidays. There is a small cinema. Among other entertainment there is animation.





For sports enthusiasts, the Beldibi Santana Hotel offers free table tennis and board games. You can play billiards at an extra charge. Among the paid services are doctor services, laundry, concierge, currency exchange. Those who care about their beauty while relaxing are advised to visit the fitness center, Turkish hammam, sauna, massage therapist.





For very young holidaymakers, babysitting services are provided.

Reviews: One of the places where you can relax is Beldibi Santana Hotel 3. Reviews show that those who rested here are very happy with their bright and spacious rooms. The service is fully consistent with the classiness of the hotel. The rooms and grounds are kept clean and tidy.

The hotel staff is polite and friendly. Among the attendants there are employees who speak a little Russian. The lobby constantly organizes sightseeing trips to nearby attractions. The most popular is a sightseeing trip to Antalya.

There is a gift shop where you can buy magnificent and unusual gifts for your friends and relatives.
