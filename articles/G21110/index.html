Hotel El Mouradi Hammamet Resort 5 * (Tunisia / Hammamet): traveler reviews

Our article will be interesting to those who plan to spend a vacation in Tunisia. We want to talk about the hotel El Mouradi Hammamet Resort 5 *, located on the first line of the famous Hammamet resort.

About hotel

Hotel El Mouradi Hammamet Resort 5 * is located in the resort part of Yasmine. From it to the airport about seventy kilometers (Tunisia - Carthage). Complex address: Hammamet, Tunisia, 8050, Zone Touristique Yasmine Hammamet. Hotel guests will not be difficult to get to the center of Yasmine, just walk some three hundred meters. Therefore, tourists will have a great opportunity to visit the most interesting sights, including the famous pirate bay.

El Mouradi Hammamet Resort 5 * hotel can be a good option for a budget relaxing family vacation with kids and people aged. You should not expect five-star chic from the complex, but it is quite suitable for a vacation on the sea coast.



Hotel El Mouradi Hammamet Resort 5 * is a five-story building on the coast. It was built in 2000, and the last repair was made in 2014. The complex has its own sandy beach, which separates the promenade from the hotel.
