If you want to go to Egypt, but you are limited in means, then this is not a reason to refuse a trip to the Red Sea. The choice of the resort falls on Sharm el-Sheikh for a reason - this is a more respectable and interesting resort area in terms of entertainment than Hurghada.





Sharm El Sheikh

Along with premium hotels in Sharm el Sheikh there are cozy and hidden from the wind economical hotels, almost each of them has its own coral reef. Hotels are accessible for young people, and it is she who makes up a considerable part of the contingent visiting discos, hookah bars, night clubs. Sharm el Sheikh also has several casinos.





Ras Mohammed

Also note the Ras Mohammed National Park, which is Egyptian pride. You should definitely come here to enjoy the unique representatives of the African flora and fauna (gazelles and foxes). In the southern part of the park, it is proposed to go diving and see corals, which are more than two billion years old. In addition, the park has a bay with water that has healing properties and has an even more saturated salinity composition than the water of the Dead Sea.

Holidays from Fortune





It will be very good to save if you use the Fortune system, which is rapidly gaining popularity in our country.

A variety of tours Fortuna Sharm El Sheikh 4 * provides travelers the opportunity to choose a cheap tour, based on the resort and the category of star hotel. The only thing that a tourist will not know until his arrival in Egypt is the name of the hotel.

The tourist offer of Fortuna Sharm El Sheikh 4 * does not mean choosing a specific hotel, but one of those that is included in this system. According to the rules of "Fortune", the client chooses the resort, type of accommodation, duration of the tour and dates of the trip, as well as the category of the hotel by the number of stars. And only upon arrival, the tourist will know which hotel he will be accommodated in.

The tour operator provides a guarantee of the settlement of a tourist in a hotel of a particular star rating.

The advantage of this system is mainly in cost. These tours are cheaper than usual by 20%, so they are available to almost everyone.

Benefit for the tourist





The offer of hotels is formed according to the following principle. A few days before the start of the tour, those rooms that have not been booked are offered at bargain prices. They populate those who bought Fortuna. The benefit is that with regular sales of rooms, the prices for them differ, and according to the Fortune system, the cost for these hotels will be the same. Consequently, the client always wins, as the price of a tour according to the Fortuna system is lower than the cost of the most economical option for regular accommodation.

Hotel reviews Gardenia Plaza Hotels & Resorts 4 *
