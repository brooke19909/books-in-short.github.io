Hotel Patong Lodge Hotel (Thailand / Phuket): photos and reviews of tourists

Tourism in Phuket has become popular relatively recently - only about five decades ago. But this time was enough for travelers from all over the world to fall in love with this amazing tropical island. The Thai government had to make a lot of efforts to create such an infrastructure that could satisfy tourists with a wide variety of needs. The island has many beaches and bays, covered with white sand. They are washed by the turquoise Andaman Sea.



Phuket Holidays

This Thai island is not only very beautiful, but also unusual. Phuket is definitely worth a visit, especially for those who want to diversify their vacation. If tourists do not like the noisy Bangkok or Pattaya, then it is better to immediately go to this island. The fact that Phuket is an exceptional place is also evidenced by the numerous reviews left by those who have already been here.

The island, which is called the pearl of the Andaman coast, is the largest in Thailand. It is connected to the mainland by a dam. Nearly eighty percent of Phuket‚Äôs territory is rainforests, which, combined with waterfalls, coconut plantations, luxurious beaches and the azure sea, have made the island an ideal holiday destination. Phuket has several famous beaches. Among them is the famous Laguna Beach, where wealthy people who come from all over the world spend their time, and where there are five-star luxury hotels of many hotel chains.

Another beach - Surin - retained the atmosphere of a traditional Thai village. In recent years, it has become popular with tourists. The pristine beauty of Kamala Beach is great for solitude and relaxation. It houses cozy three- and four-star hotels, in which there is everything you need to relax. The Phuket FantaSea amusement park, famous throughout Thailand, is located on this stretch of coast .



Patong

Judging by the reviews, it is the most crowded beach in Phuket. There are hotels for every taste and budget, including guesthouses, a large selection of restaurants and bars, massage parlors, discos, etc. The island's nightlife is right here - on Bangla Road. That is why Patong is even nicknamed "Little Pattaya." Those who are looking for peace and solitude to relax in a corner untouched by civilization will not like it here. And although the prices for services or entertainment in Patong are an order of magnitude higher than in the rest of Phuket, nevertheless, huge armies of tourists come here.

The choice of hotels here is huge: these are real five-star ‚Äúpalaces‚Äù and small apartments for budget vacationers. Many Russians choose solid 3-star hotels for their stay, for example, Patong Lodge Hotel.

Description

It is located in the northern part of Patong Island, on a green, picturesque hill. The rooms offer magnificent views of Kalim - another beach in Phuket. Hotel Patong Lodge Hotel 3 * is perfect for those tourists who want to find themselves in the lap of tropical nature, while not too far from civilization. The bustling Bangla Road begins just a short drive away. There are a lot of entertainment venues in which life literally boils.

Patong Lodge Hotel is built on the first line, almost on the seashore. You can drive to the center of Phuket in half an hour, and the international airport is thirty kilometers away, which is a very big plus for those who come with young children. Sufficiently long flights and a long shuttle exhaust the children, but those who choose Patong Lodge Hotel 3 * will be in the hotel‚Äôs booked room in forty minutes.



Infrastructure

Being a three-star hotel, however, it offers its customers a fairly wide range of services. It has an elevator and a conference room. Those who wish can take their clothes to the laundry room and use dry cleaning. The Patong Lodge Hotel offers free parking, a tour desk and a rental car, including a bicycle.

The registration desk never closes. Here you can immediately draw up documents and get room keys, regardless of arrival time. In addition, you can fax or print documents at the counter. For many, such a service as a safe cell is very relevant.

Patong Lodge Hotel was built in 1989, and in 2014 there was a major overhaul. The territory is completely landscaped. According to the rules, pets are not allowed in the hotel, including guide dogs. Check-in time is set for fourteen hours, and check-out is at noon. At the reception, an employee will help you call a taxi or arrange a transfer if necessary.

Patong Lodge Hotel has several compliments, including for newlyweds or regular customers. Upon arrival, tourists receive a cocktail, two bottles of a carbonated drink and a garland of orchids.



Housing stock

Patong Lodge Hotel 3 * (Phuket, Patong) is located on a hilly scenic area in the midst of palm groves. It has one hundred and thirty rooms of the following categories: standard, Superior, as well as Deluxe and Suites. All are renovated in Thai national style and feature comfortable dark wood furniture. All rooms have balconies separated from the rooms by large floor-to-ceiling panoramic windows. Sitting here, you can admire the surrounding nature and enjoy the tropical garden.

They also include satellite TV, a free safe and minibar, direct dial telephone and air conditioning.

The high-quality texture of wooden furniture is in perfect harmony with the soft drapery of the dense curtains, creating coziness. Dark curtains contribute to a good day's rest for young children who fall asleep perfectly under the singing of birds.

Bathrooms combined. They have showers, a hairdryer and all the necessary toiletries.

Room service is available around the clock. There are also apartments for smokers in a separate wing.

Nutrition

Patong Lodge Hotel (Phuket) offers only one concept - a "buffet with breakfast." In addition to the main restaurant, tourists can also enjoy the Ocean restaurant, which offers amazing views of the tropical jungle surrounding the hotel from its terrace. Here guests can enjoy delicious Thai or international dishes, and in the evenings you can listen to live music.



There are also two bars on site - Coco and Terrace, which serve refreshments as well as light snacks throughout the day. If desired, you can order food in the room. This service is provided around the clock.

Beach

Patong Lodge Hotel 3 * (Phuket) has its own fenced and guarded swimming area. From the main building to the beach - a hundred meters. Towels for the sea are provided free of charge at the reception. Tourists do not need to pay extra for renting umbrellas and sunbeds.

Judging by the reviews, the coast of the Andaman Sea, including Patong Beach, is a place ideal for bathing young children. The coating is sandy, and the entrance to the water is gentle, which is convenient for the safety of the kids.

For children

The infrastructure of this hotel for small children includes a shallow pool and a children's room. Upon request, parents can get a folding bed in their room, there is also a babysitting service on request. In the restaurant, children are given high chairs for easy feeding.

Additional Information

There is no animation at this hotel. If there are waves at sea, you can swim in the pool located on the territory. Loungers are placed around. You can play billiards at the extra cost of renting tables.



Fans of active pastime will enjoy visiting a cabaret, as well as attending fights of Thai boxers. In addition, in the office at the reception you can rent a vehicle, including a motorbike or a bicycle, to independently see all the sights. Here you can buy excursion tours for a two-day sea excursion to the island of Phi Phi Don, on the Siam Niramit show , in Khao Lak, etc. There are a lot of entertainments on the beach, primarily water. Rent diving equipment, many with experienced instructors do scuba diving, ride a bicycle or a catamaran, etc. Many tourists celebrate their birthday at the hotel, receiving a compliment from the administration of Patong Lodge Hotel 3 *.

Reviews

Many of our compatriots believe that Patong Lounge is very good for its three-star category. He, without claiming to a higher level, is fully consistent with the established.

Check-in is fast enough: without waiting for the time stated in the rules, the receptionist, if there is a free number, immediately issues keys and helps to convey things. The staff speaks not only Thai, but also English, therefore, as a rule, Russians do not have communication problems.



A lot of good words have been said about the rooms that Patong Lodge Hotel 3 * offers for living. The reviews of tourists indicate that they are well repaired, the plumbing is working properly, there is a free safe, a TV with a USB input.

As for nutrition, it is rated by our compatriots for the highest score. True, it seemed to some that breakfasts were not very varied, although most tourists who were satisfied with the menu called them full dinners.

There are several negative reviews regarding the location of the hotel. The guests did not like the lack of supermarkets nearby and cheap restaurants.
