Crochet a fashion snood scarf

In winter, I want warmth, coziness. We offer to crochet a snood with your own hands. It belongs to one of the types of scarves. Snood can be worn as a large collar or as a hood. This trendy accessory will look original on you.

Snood scarves: crochet

First you need to decide on the material of the product. Which is better to choose?

Take this issue seriously, as the scarf comes in contact with delicate skin and can cause irritation. Therefore, the yarn should be short. We advise you to choose a boucle, mohair, and if you want to knit a product for spring as well, then choose viscose, cotton or acrylic. It is worth deciding on the color scheme. Choose according to your color typeface.

Choosing a knitting pattern

This is an important stage of work. A scarf connected by a volumetric or openwork pattern will look beautiful. There are all kinds of crochet patterns for scarf snood, you just have to choose. After all, even a simple drawing will look luxurious. We present you a choice of several schemes.

Crocheting a Snood Scarf: Workshop

For work you will need:



- yarn (two hundred grams);

- hook number 4;

- a needle;

- zeal.

Working process

We will crochet ‚Äúsnood‚Äù guided by the following patterns:

Scheme 1. A chain of eight air loops is dialed, close the row with connecting columns . The result was a circle. In the subsequent rows (there should be five of them) we make a climb of two air loops. We continue to knit like that. We pass two double crochet in two loops of the previous row. We collect four more air and three double crochet. When the row is closed, we make an ascent to go to the next row. Continue to knit the small squares in this way.

This scheme is suitable for beginner knitters.

Scheme 2. It will be interesting for experienced needlewomen. Six air loops are gathered, at the end make a connecting column and close the row. Thus, you have a circle. In the second row, dial twelve double crochet, alternating with air loops. The third - three double crochet, knit them in each loop of the second row. Remember to add two air loops each time. Next is a complex pattern, be careful! In the fourth, we make a climb of three air loops, closing with a half-column, and we knit them in the nearest knot of the previous row, then we pick up four more. Repeat the previous action. And we close the row with three loops and half-columns. In the fifth row, three air units must be used for lifting. Alternate four times: three double crochet and simple loops. Knitting is coming to an end. In the final row, alternate three air loops with three columns and loops five times.

Similarly, tie the desired number of squares according to the first or second pattern. Once done, proceed to assemble the product. We sew the finished squares of the desired length in one strip. Lock her in the ring. Tie the edges of the snood scarf with a crocheted crochet, a simple double crochet. In the kit, you can knit a hat and mittens according to this technique.

Crochet knitted snood is ready! With such a scarf, you will always have a great mood and amazing look. Good luck in your creative endeavors!
