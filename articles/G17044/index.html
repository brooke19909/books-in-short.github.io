Platja Park 4 * (Spain / Costa Brava): photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Costa Brava is a famous resort located on the coast of Catalonia. Translated from Spanish, the name of this place means "rocky, wild coast." I must say, the name is fully justified. Coniferous forests, wild cliffs, mysterious caves, picturesque bays - all this is the Costa Brava. There is a huge number of hotels and hotels, and one of the most popular options for tourists is Platja Park 4 *.





Resort infrastructure

Before considering individual hotel complexes, a few words should be said regarding the resort infrastructure. Costa Brava is not only a relaxing holiday on the shores of the azure sea. It is also the richest architectural monuments, a lot of historical attractions, various theaters, museums. The infrastructure of outdoor activities and entertainment is also quite well developed, which is the main reason why youth companies come here. Water sports, boat trips on yachts, mountain bikes, water parks, night clubs, bars, restaurants - these places will definitely appeal to people who cannot sit still. In general, as you can see, the Costa Brava is a vacation for everyone.

Platja Park 4 * is a fully equipped resort hotel with all amenities, which is located in a favorable quiet area just a few steps from the coast of the clear sea.
