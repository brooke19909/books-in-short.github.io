Exchange rate policy

Until 1974, the RMB was traded, as a rule, with a reference to the pound sterling, but then quotes began to be applied to the US dollar. The Chinese authorities began to be pressured to liberalize their monetary policies, since, according to representatives of other countries, due to the low renminbi exchange rate, China gained an advantage due to purchasing power.
