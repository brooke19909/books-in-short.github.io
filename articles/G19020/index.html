Akti Beach Village Resort 4 * (Cyprus / Paphos): tourist reviews, prices and photos

Cyprus is one of the three largest Mediterranean islands. It is located near the eastern shores of the Mediterranean Sea, in close proximity to the Turkish and Syrian coast. This island is called the birthplace of the goddess of love Aphrodite, in addition, many legends and mythical stories are associated with it. The island has unique architectural monuments that are several thousand years old. Thanks to these attractions, as well as its wonderful climate and nature, Cyprus is one of the most popular tourist destinations not only in Europe but throughout the world.

General characteristics of the service in Paphos

You can fly to this wonderful resort by direct flight from many cities of Russia. After all, one of the two air gates of Cyprus is located in Paphos (the second - in Larnaca). There are regular flights from Moscow and St. Petersburg, and from other major cities of the Russian Federation to Paphos you can get on charter flights, which are operated only in the high season (from April to October). Consequently, those tourists who have purchased tickets to one of the hotels in Paphos or Larnaca, for example, in Akti Beach Village Resort, will be able to comfortably stay in their rooms a few minutes after arrival.



Paphos, due to the general level of service, as well as the improvement of infrastructure, is considered the most elite resort on the island. Aktibeach Village Resort 4 * (Paphos, Crete)

On the seafront promenade of Pafos resort, 11 kilometers from the international airport, there are beautiful landscaped gardens that are famous for their exotic plants, unique trees and flowers. Here you can find bushes of fragrant roses and jasmines, citrus trees, olives and figs, pine and spruce, etc. In the middle of this beautiful park is the hotel "Aktibich Village Resort 4 *". The city center is only 4 km away. It is a family hotel in type. It is located on the seashore, just above the coastline. Almost all the windows of the hotel look at the turquoise surface of the sea, the rest - at the beautiful landscape of the garden.



The infrastructure of the hotel Akti Beach Village Resort

The hotel has one indoor and two outdoor pools, around which spread out palm trees. There are sun loungers on the terrace next to the pool, there is also a special area for sunbathing. Inside the building there is a gym, fitness club, Karidais spa center with a Turkish hammam, a Finnish sauna, a jacuzzi with a hydromassage, as well as a massage room. Outside there is a tennis court, playground for other games, table tennis, billiards. Three kilometers from the hotel are golf courses. On its territory there is a mini market and several souvenir shops, and free wireless Internet is available.

Beach

The path to the pebble beach of the hotel is laid among trees and shrubs. In Cyprus, you rarely see sandy beaches, so for the safety of your feet it is better to buy special rubber shoes in advance. The hotel's private beach has sun loungers and umbrellas, although most tourists prefer to sunbathe on the green terrace, designed specifically for sunbathing. Here, on the beach, tourists can be offered to do yoga (for free), diving (for a fee), as well as other entertainments.

Services and living conditions

According to the charter established in the hotel, check-in time is considered to be 14.00, and departure - 12.00. Rooms are cleaned daily. The hotel offers car and bicycle rental, dry cleaning and laundry services, and tourists can also use ironing facilities (iron and ironing board). For a fee, they are provided with a safe for valuables.

Sightseeing Tours

There is a travel desk in the lobby. Here tourists are offered various excursions to the numerous attractions of both Paphos and the entire island. Many of them have long been included in the World Heritage List of the international organization UNESCO. The main attractions of these places that are worth visiting are Petra tou Romiou, where, according to legend, the goddess Aphrodite, Villa of the god of winemaking Dionysus, the cave of Saint Solomonia, the monastery of Saint Neophytos, the Royal Tombs, etc. in Paphos were born also there are the most fascinating museums of Cyprus, namely the Archaeological, Ethnographic, Byzantine, etc. The city also has Akamas National Park - a beautiful place for walking and family vacations. It is located on the peninsula of the same name, whose deserted beaches fascinate with its pristine beauty. These secluded places are especially popular with newlyweds who have decided to spend their honeymoon on the island of Aphrodite.
