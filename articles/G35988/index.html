Hedef Kleopatra Golden Sun Hotel: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Hedef Kleopatra Golden Sun Hotel is a three-star hotel located 1 km from Alanya on the Mediterranean coast in Turkey. Every year the hotel receives thousands of guests from all over Europe. The Turks themselves like to relax here.

A bit about Alanya

Since the Hedef Kleopatra Golden Sun Hotel is located near the city of Alanya, we will begin our review with a brief description of this settlement.

Alanya is located in the province of Antalya. Although only 100,000 people live in the city, it is considered a major seaport in Turkey and, of course, one of the country's main resorts. Vacationers from all over Europe and the countries of the former CIS come there in season, so the number of people increases several times.

The town is located on the coast of the picturesque Mediterranean Sea, only 138 km from Antalya. Tourists usually fly by plane to Gazipasa Airport, which is only 40 km from the Hedef Kleopatra Golden Sun Hotel, so they do not have to travel long. You can book a transfer at the hotel or take the shuttle bus (dolmushi). The bus station in Alanya is just 200 meters from the hotel.

The invasion of tourists in this town began after the discovery by the world community of the healing properties of the air of the famous Damlatash Cave. This event happened back in 1960. Many came to receive medical treatment and check the health benefits of such a find.

When Turkey was actively engaged in the development of the Riviera, then, of course, they touched on innovation and the coastal city. Since 1990, construction began on hotels, guest houses, private hotels, apartments to accommodate a large number of tourists. After all, before the discovery of tourism in this area, the main income was agriculture, mainly growing fruit for sale.

Alanya Attractions

Tourists who come to relax at the Hedef Kleopatra Golden Sun Hotel can not only use the services of numerous bars, restaurants and various catering establishments. Alanya itself is a beautiful old city with fortified walls, with the famous Red Tower of Kyzyl Kule, a shipyard, monuments of Ataturk and Ala ad-Din Kay-Kubad. It is interesting to visit the archaeological museum and the Alarahan caravanserai. Kemal Ataturk's house-museum is also located on the territory, not to mention the most beautiful old mosques of the city and its central square.





There is a list of natural attractions for lovers of beautiful places. We will name some of them: the healing cave of Damlatas, Cleopatra beach, the mountain river Dim Chai, the most beautiful canyon Sapadere.

Rooms Hedef Kleopatra Golden Sun Hotel 3 *

According to the reviews of numerous tourists, the hotel rooms are not pleased with either the cleanliness or quality of the furniture. But first things first. All hotel rooms are standard, designed for two or three visitors. However, upon request, they can add a single bed to the room at an additional cost.

Each room has a toilet with a toilet and bathtub. There is a hairdryer, but judging by the reviews, it is not always in working condition. They give towels, which are rarely changed, at the request of tourists. Now in Turkey in all hotels there is a program to preserve the country's ecology. It was decided in hotels not to wash once again without special need, so as not to clog the sea and rivers with waste detergents and detergents. Apparently, this hotel has the same rule, so use the towel carefully during the week, as you will get another towel only at the end of the seven-day period.





Each room has air conditioning, but, according to some, is rather weak. There is also a TV with satellite channels for every taste. The room is 25 square meters. meters. The key to her goes to the electronic safe. The room has a telephone and a minibar. There are many pieces of furniture where you can put things: bedside tables, a TV stand, a wall resembling a kitchen, a cupboard against the wall.

Each room has a small balcony on which there is a plastic chair and ropes for drying things hang. The view from them is very different, depending on the location of the case. Some overlook the city, others overlook the mountains nearby, some overlook the expanse of the sea or the pool. If you are going to the resort with children, then be prepared for the fact that the loud music of institutions and the noisy fun of tourists end only at the beginning of the night. The rest of the evening you will have to listen to this cacophony or participate in it, which will be difficult to do with a small child.
