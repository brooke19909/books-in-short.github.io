What do HTC Desire EYE smartphones think? User reviews

Why did HTC start developing such an unusual device? And then, that, like all mobile device developers, it is chasing the current trends. Want a bigger screen? Please get an even larger diagonal instead of the 5-inch screen. Want more power to run apps? A multi-core processor and a powerful graphics accelerator will cope with any game. Love yourself to photograph more than others, here you have a good front camera, which is in no way inferior to the main module. It is this camera that the HTC Desire EYE mobile device is equipped with. But what do users think of him?

Smartphone Appearance

To begin with, the smartphone goes on sale in two colors. And both options look good. The first is a combination of red and white, the second is a dark blue back panel with blue accents at the edges. The material used to create the case, as always, is of good quality. This is polycarbonate, thanks to which the smartphone is unlikely to ever slip out of hand.



The first thing that attracts attention is the huge front camera and a double flash on the left side of it. This is a new feature from HTC, a gift for those who like to take selfies. Next to it are two sensors - lighting and proximity. Two speakers made in the form of black stripes above and below the display also look unusual.

On the back cover in the upper left corner is the main camera module with a double flash, next to them is a microphone and the HTC logo in the middle. The left side is decorated with two slots - for a SIM card and a memory card. The right side was occupied by the volume and power keys, and just below the camera button. At the bottom there is an entrance under the charger, and on the opposite is a headphone jack. By the way, the smartphone is positioned as waterproof and is able to swim under water for half an hour.



In principle, many reviews left about the HTC Desire EYE phone note the design of the device and the arrangement of elements for the better. Although there are complaints about the speakers, which are often clogged, or the wide frames around the display. And there are individual reviews specific to HTC Desire EYE Red, which note a very dirty white back panel.

Display

The front of the smartphone is decorated with 5.2-inch diagonal SuperLCD 3 FullHD technology. It has large viewing angles, a good margin of brightness (the image is perfectly visible in bright sunlight), and protection against damage in the form of GG3 glass is also provided.



Apparently, the display is actually high-quality, as indicated by the reviews left on the HTC Desire EYE phone. True, there is information about the appearance of a yellow spot on the screen after some time. But this is an isolated case. Most likely, factory marriage.

Cameras

Already by the combination of the three letters ‚ÄúEYE‚Äù in the name of the smartphone, you can understand what its main feature is. The device has two completely identical cameras. The main module has a resolution of 13 megapixels and supports many modes for shooting. But this is not about him at all.

The main guest of the review is the front camera, which has the same characteristics as the main one. And therefore, it has the same dimensions. The interesting thing is that cameras can shoot at the same time, and then combine the pictures into one frame. Moreover, this mode is also active for video recording. There is an image retouching function, which is designed to improve the quality of pictures. And to control the camera, you can use both voice commands and a timer.



But what do the users who bought the HTC Desire EYE smartphone say? They leave mixed reviews. Basically, of course, everyone is happy with the cameras, at least the front. But there are those to whom the main module seems weaker than the declared resolution (13 megapixels), and those who noticed that the cameras are a little yellow.

Performance

The device runs on Android OS 4.4.4, a quad-core Snapdragon 801 processor, Andreno GPU and 2 GB of RAM. 16 GB is allocated for data storage, but there is support for a 128 GB memory card. As for applications, all of them, even the most demanding ones, should work smoothly. Fortunately, the hardware allows.



At the time of the release of the HTC Desire EYE smartphone, performance reviews were positive. And now its filling is considered serious enough to run various applications.

Offline work

The smartphone is supported by a non-removable 2400 mAh battery. In theory, this should be enough for a day. There are also two energy-saving modes. When you turn on the usual screen brightness decreases, data transfer and vibration are turned off. And an improved energy-saving mode, in addition to the already mentioned, lowers the processor frequency, disables sensors and some sensors. True, in this case only some applications will be available: phone, calculator, messages, calendar and mail.

Looking through the reviews left about the HTC Desire EYE, it is easy to see that the battery is not the best part of it. It can be said that more than half of all claims users attribute to a too weak battery. But in defense of the device it is worth saying that many modern smartphones suffer from this.
