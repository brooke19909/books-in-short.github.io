We choose the best hotels Pattaya 3 stars

Among the travelers who gathered in Thailand, the most popular hotels are 3 stars Pattaya, which are a comfortable and economical type of vacation. Danish people choose the proximity of entertainment venues.

3 star hotels in Pattaya offer affordable accommodation, while providing guests with all the necessary amenities. There are a lot of establishments in this category, so in order to choose your own option, you can refer to the information provided by tour operators or to the reviews left by travelers who have already visited the hotel.

In most cases, hotels offer guests only breakfast. Lunch and dinner will have, most likely, in the nearest cafes or restaurants. However, this will not be a big problem, because Thailand has low prices for almost everything.

Many Pattaya 3 star hotels are located near the coastline, so it is convenient to get to the beach from here. Almost all rooms in institutions of this category are air-conditioned. Guests are offered a variety of services, including for business people.





It should be noted Pattaya hotels with their own beach, as such institutions are most convenient for family or secluded relaxation.

The hotel complex "Camelot" is considered one of the best in the southern region of the city. An interesting design of the building in which the hotel is located, looks like a medieval castle. At the disposal of travelers are several heated pools, a chic garden and cozy rooms that can accommodate from 1 to 5 people.

Popular among tourists is the Jomtien Garden Hotel. Its advantage is its good location - not far from Phuket Airport. For entertainment, the establishment offers Thai massage, tennis, billiards and much more. There are restaurants on site that offer local specialties and international dishes.





All hotels in Pattaya are distinguished by a friendly, smiling staff who are happy to help guests with what they can do. In some institutions, Russian-speaking employees work.

Hotel Aiyara Grand enjoys popularity among Russians. It was built several years ago, but has already been recognized as one of the best in Pattaya in its category. Less than a kilometer away is the municipal beach, where tourists can find something to their liking (lounging in the sun or taking a catamaran, depending on the preferences of vacationers).

3 star hotels in Pattaya are not just a budget vacation option abroad. These establishments are distinguished by high-quality service, despite reasonable prices.
