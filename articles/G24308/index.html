Hotel More - Kemer, Turkey: reviews. More Hotel 3 *

Hotel More (Kemer) is located on the Mediterranean coast, just a 2-minute walk to the beach. However, the main attraction of this area is the magnificent nature of the Taurus Mountains, with beautiful ecological trails and canyons.

A few words about Kemer

Kemer is a small town within the Turkish Riviera with a population of only 20,000 people. Since Kemer is a resort and port city, it is natural that in the season the number of residents tripled. For a comfortable holiday for tourists in the town, many hotels, pensions and hostels have been built.





It is located 42 km from Antalya, so vacationers from the airport are not so far away. The weather in the main season is clear and dry, in summer the heat can reach 40 degrees. Winters are mild. So you can come to rest not only in the summer, but all year round.

Drinks in bars

During the day, you can take refreshments in two bars. They work from 9.00 to 21.00. There are 4 types of sparkling water and the same number of types of fresh freshes for the choice of vacationers. The water in the cooler is good, purified.

Alcoholic drinks can be taken as early as 11.00. The price list has wines: white, red. They are dry and not very strong, diluted. Of the strong drinks there is vodka, but also, according to tourists, not strong. There is no beer in the bars at all.

During the day, you can have a snack in the snack bar with sandwiches, chips, small snacks.

Additional hotel services

Hotel Sea (Kemer) offers laundry and dry cleaning services, as well as ironing services. The fee is separate. On the ground floor in the main building, you can leave valuables in a safe, but at an additional cost.

For children, there is a small pool and a playground on site.





On the sandy and pebble beach, sun loungers and umbrellas are provided free of charge, but according to vacationers, you should arrive at the beach early, as at 9:00 am everything is already taken, you cannot find free ones.

For lovers of outdoor activities, the hotel has only darts and a table tennis table. If necessary, you can rent a car. Since the airport is nearby, hotel transfer services are not provided.

Entertainment

In addition to relaxing on the beach, the place is famous for its mountain routes. Groups with the necessary equipment and experienced guides are organized in the beautiful mountain canyon Goynuk, the cave of Beldibi, on the Lycian ecological trail. The bay in Camyuva, Takhtaly mountain, the "burning" Chimera mountain will also impress. It is interesting to climb to the top of Tahtali Mountain on the Olympos Teleferik cable car.





Lovers of ancient architecture can admire Ataturk Boulevard in Kemer, the central square with a clock tower, watch dancing fountains and a monument to Ataturk, go to the Huzur Cami Mosque on Dertyol Boulevard. And a trip to Antalya will also entertain.
