Kopanskoe - a lake for mental relaxation

130 km from St. Petersburg is an extraordinarily beautiful pond called Lake Kopanskoe. Everyone who is tired of the bustle of the city comes here to relax. In these places there are cozy houses, as well as places for tents.

Description of the lake

Kopanskoe is a lake in the Leningrad region that belongs to Kingisepp district. The reservoir stretched from north to south for almost seven kilometers, becoming wider, now narrower. The average width is approximately 1.4 km. The lake is deep enough, in some places it reaches 24 meters. But this is at the deepest points, since closer to the coast the distance to the bottom is 4 m. In places where a person‚Äôs foot reaches, the bottom is sandy, but silt starts a little further.

The lake is picturesque because it is surrounded by neat hills on which pines, aspen, oaks and birches grow. The Crane Spit stretches from the western side; it is distinguished by white sand and tall pines. The rest of the shores also have sandy beaches. Such a landscape can not but like, so many people choose Kopanskoe for relaxation.



The lake is connected to the Gulf of Finland by the flowing Peypei River. It greatly affects the number and species composition of fish that lives in Kopansky. Also from the Suma river to Kopansky there is a channel.

Rest on the lake

The northern side of the reservoir placed on its shore the sanatorium-sanatorium "Lake Kopanskoe", and there is also a recreation center "Blue" and "Kopan". But you can stop not only at the base, but in your own tent: every summer, lovers of wild relaxation choose Kopan Lake.

The recreation center "Kopan", in addition to cozy houses and cottages, has a campsite on its territory. This is where many lay out their tent. But there are tourists who do not have their own camping equipment, so the base has rental equipment: sleeping bags, tents and other things.

Silent hunt

But on the lake you can relax in another way. If you are on the southern shore of a reservoir, head west toward the third sand pit. There is an unshaded tall spruce forest, as well as birch undergrowths. It is in these places that you can successfully pick up mushrooms. How to get there by car and what road do you need to go? Take the Tallinn Highway first. Having reached the village. Runners, turn right at Koporye. Curious story

An unusual story is associated with this lake. At one time it was considered a secret object. At the dawn of the 20th century, torpedoes were tested in this place. After that, here on the lake they were installed on warships. By the 30s, a military settlement had formed here, various tests were carried out. But in the war during the retreat, this place was cleared, and all structures were demolished. Since that period, silence remains on the lake.
