Broadway Club in Rybatsky: modern retro-style parties

The Retrodiskoteka company invites citizens of St. Petersburg and tourists to visit a nightly rest facility called Broadway. This club is located in the Nevsky district, not far from the Rybatskoe metro station. The guests of the establishment are offered a great opportunity to have fun in the company and listen to their favorite songs of past years.

Club Description

After dark, life in the city does not stop. On the contrary, late evening is the most suitable time for night lovers. Broadway, a club in the Fisherman's district, invites visitors to experience the atmosphere of fun, music and dance.

It has everything you need for an active, interesting and comfortable pastime. Some believe that the club is a place of relaxation, designed exclusively for young people. However, this is not at all true. "Broadway" - an institution that is suitable for recreation for people of absolutely any age. Here you can listen to musical compositions of past years, dance from the heart and feel young and energetic. The songs sound pretty loud, but unobtrusive. The sound is always of high quality recording thanks to specially selected equipment. Many visitors come here to celebrate a birthday or just sit in the company of friends. The institution has all the possibilities for recreation: karaoke, bar, halls for celebrations.

Broadway, a club in the Fishing District, is located at Skachkov Lane, Building 5.

The institution is open on Fridays and Saturdays from eight in the evening until six in the morning.

Festive events

The club offers its customers a great opportunity to organize a banquet in honor of any special occasion. For this purpose, the institution has a hall in which one hundred and fifty visitors can comfortably sit and relax.

Here you can listen to your favorite songs of past years, dance, taste interesting and tasty drinks at the bar and order Japanese, European or traditional Russian dishes. Karaoke lovers can enjoy their favorite hobby. Due to the presence in the institution of a specially equipped room with musical equipment, visitors to the Broadway Club take a unique opportunity to feel like real pop stars.

If we talk about prices, the cost of an entrance ticket will cost guests 250 rubles. Hall rental for a festive event - from 2000 to 6000 rubles.

Many people like the bright and stylish interior of the premises of the Broadway club in Rybatsky, photos of the institution are presented in the sections of the article.

Assortment of dishes

The menu of the establishment is characterized by moderate prices for food and drinks. The average order value is about a thousand rubles. As for the range of dishes, it is approximately as follows:

  1. Cold and hot snacks (meat, fish, vegetables).
  2. Salads.
  3. Snacks for beer (croutons, chips).
  4. Hot dishes of chicken, potatoes.
  5. Sushi rolls.
  6. Hot meat dishes (steaks, barbecue).
  7. Dessert.
  8. Juices, mineral water.
  9. Alcoholic drinks.
  10. Tea coffee.

Visitors who order alcohol before ten in the evening receive a 50% discount. Those who hold a festive event at the Broadway Club in Rybatskiy are allowed to bring drinks with them.

Opinions of visitors about the institution

Broadway, a club in the Fisherman's district, is an institution about which there are both positive and negative reviews. Some customers are satisfied with the organization. They claim that it is interesting to relax and spend time here, the quality of food and drinks is quite good, and the prices are reasonable.
