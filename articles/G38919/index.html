Control

Monopods can be without control and with control. The second option is more modern and practical. There are 1 or more keys on the handle. If there is one button, press it to search for Bluetooth, establish a connection, press for a photo or video. If the smartphone is connected using the headphone jack, then the same steps are performed, but without BT search.

Additional buttons include zoom and focus. The third option is monopods with a remote control. Management will be more convenient. Some smartphones work by voice commands. If it is missing, you need to install the Play Market or the App Store.

Additional accessories include a nozzle holder, fastening to a waist belt, a cover. They may vary depending on the model. There are other types of monopods in demand among buyers. Some of them are used not only for smartphones, but also for cameras.

Which is better to choose?

There are many devices. When choosing, you need to consider some criteria. A wired monopod with an AUX cable and a button on the handle is a non-volatile solution. It does not need to be charged and charge controlled.

Wireless with a built-in button and a battery will be a convenient solution, but due to the discharge of the battery, the device may stop working at the wrong time. The device with a wireless remote control is similar to the previous version, but the disadvantages include hand occupancy and the likely loss of the remote control.

The appropriate type is selected, you need to take into account the reliability and versatility of the mount. It must be firmly fixed. It is desirable that there are rubberized inserts. The telescopic tube extends easily. It does not bend, and in the connection areas they do not scroll under the mass of the smartphone. And the monopod head should rotate freely.

It is necessary to determine the dimensions of this device. Indeed, portability is important for some, while functionality is important for others, and any size can be. Given the basic selection criteria, it will turn out to purchase a suitable device that will allow you to take high-quality selfies.
