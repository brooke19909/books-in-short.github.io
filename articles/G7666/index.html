KP "Pavlovskiye Dachi" - healthy and comfortable housing!

The construction of the cottage village, called the "Pavlovsk Dachas", is carried out by the Maximum Life Development Group of Companies. It is located in a cozy and beautiful place located near the Slavyanka River. Large forests located near the city of Pavlovsk surround the built-up territory. From the village under construction from cottages to the highway is only 4 km, with which an asphalt road will connect the residential area .

Description of the future village

Pavlovskiye Dachi, a cottage village under construction in the Leningrad Region, is distinguished by its unusual, picturesque nature and ecology. The mixed forest adjacent to it, with an abundance of mushrooms and berries, creates excellent, and most importantly, safe conditions for life and good rest. In addition, the abundance of architectural monuments, beautiful places and parks allows you to successfully organize a cultural recreation program.





Currently, the cost of land in the Pavlovskiye Dachi microdistrict is low, so anyone can build a house here. An undoubted advantage for people is that there are no strict requirements for the construction and appearance of the house.

Comparison with other villages

Against the background of nearby cottage villages under construction (these include Pokrovsky Gorki, Pavlovsky Posad, Pokrovskoye and others), Pavlovskiye Dachi is considered an attractive offer, but this is only at the very beginning of construction and without communications. Over time, the price of land in the village will rise and reach an average level - in this case, the middle class of Russians will no longer be able to afford it.

The Maximum Life Development Group of Companies is a fairly new company for the market located in St. Petersburg, however, it has already successfully built and commissioned a project of the Tsvetochnoye DNP located in the Lomonosov District. Land sales in Tsvetochnoye are already closed, all the promised communications have been completed in the village, and construction of houses is being actively promoted today.

What is the social sphere and institutions of Pavlovsk Dachas?

For permanent residence, the optimal social sphere is important anywhere. And despite the fact that the village of Kommunar is closest to it, the social sphere is much better in a more remote residential area, namely in Pushkin and Pavlovsk.

According to reviews, it will be quite realistic for parents living in the village of Pavlovskiye Dachi to identify their children in kindergarten and school, even despite registration in the region.





In addition, in these cities there are various shopping centers, a post office, various types of shops, Sberbank, a hospital and other institutions necessary for a normal and full life.

No less important is the fact that on the western side of the area under the name "Pavlovsk dachas" there will be the city of Yuzhny. If such a project comes to life (which will require a lot of time and effort), then this will lead to both certain advantages and disadvantages. The pluses include the accessibility of all institutions, as well as the possibility of unhindered access to the highway. The disadvantages include the fact that a large part of the Starogatchinsky forest will be cut down.

Currently, it is possible to get to the future village through Pavlovsk and Pushkin, or while driving along the Kiev highway, followed by a turn to Verevo.
