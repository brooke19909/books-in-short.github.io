How to check the tariff plan on MTS yourself?

The principles of acquisition and use of SIM cards from different mobile operators practically do not differ from each other. So it is with MTS: if you want to use communication services, you should purchase the most suitable tariff plan. Most customers who are satisfied with the terms of the contract do not even think about the possibility of changing them. However, sometimes a need arises and the question arises of how to check the tariff plan for MTS. Being one of the largest mobile communication operators in our country, the Mobile Telecommunication Systems company offers its users a lot of options for clarifying the necessary information on their account, activating additional packages, changing the set of available services, etc. This article will help answer the question of how to check the tariff plan on MTS.





Options for viewing room information

  1. Via mobile phone: by entering some combination of numbers and symbols.
  2. Through a technical support specialist.
  3. Via the Internet (through the official website of a mobile operator or application that can be installed on a phone or tablet).

Let us consider in more detail how to get the interesting information about the account by each of the above methods.





Which team to check the MTS tariff plan via mobile phone

This method of obtaining information is the most convenient, since it does not require additional conditions (an Internet connection, the ability to make a call, etc.). By entering USSD-requests, you can not only clarify information about the tariff plan, but also change it, disable or connect any options. Similar opportunities are present in other mobile operators. To find out your MTS tariff plan, you must enter the command * 111 * 59 #. Information will be displayed on the screen of the mobile device.

How to check the tariff plan for MTS through user support

Another way to check information on a number that does not imply an Internet connection is to call the client department of the company. It is noteworthy that it is completely free for network subscribers. By contacting the consultation service, you can not only use the functions of the automatic informant and find out all the necessary data, but also talk with the dispatcher who will help you choose a new tariff plan with more acceptable conditions, if necessary. To connect, dial 0890 and press the call button. A significant drawback of this method of informing is the long waiting time, since sometimes the queue can be more than 10 minutes.

Using the Internet to check your plan

For advanced Internet users, a whole arsenal of tools for managing their own account is available. To use it, you must visit the official website of the mobile operator, register on it, following the prompts of the system. This option is convenient for viewing data from a computer or laptop. To access the account management tools, you should use the free mobile application, which can be downloaded through the store for the relevant devices. Access to your personal account, as in the case of the website, is carried out by phone number. However, after the initial data entry, it is possible to save it, which means that you do not have to log in each time by opening the application on a mobile device.
