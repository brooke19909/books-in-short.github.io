Alhambra Thalasso 5 * (Tunisia, Hammamet, Yasmin): description and reviews

Perhaps every tourist who occasionally prefers to spend his vacation abroad at least once thought about visiting Tunisia. This country has become famous all over the world for its beautiful beaches, luxury hotels and affordable prices. Vacation here is an opportunity to immerse yourself in exotics, without giving up the usual benefits of civilization.

One of the most popular Tunisian resorts is considered to be Hammamet. It is here that vacationers stop, dreaming of spending a few days on one of the most beautiful coasts of the country. Of course, there are plenty of hotels and pensions here. But experienced travelers often recommend the Alhambra Thalasso Hotel. According to reviews, the hotel has everything to provide guests with comfort and a bright, full of impressions leisure.

Location Details





It's no secret that when choosing a place to stay, it is important to pay attention to such a factor as location. Indeed, during the vacation you want to enjoy all the benefits of civilization. So where to look for the Alhambra Thalasso 5 * hotel complex?

Hammamet, namely the picturesque resort corner of Yasmin - many people prefer to stay here. By the way, this is a rather lively resort, the popularity of which is growing every year. More and more hotels are constantly being built here, and dozens of colorful restaurants and small cafes await travelers. By the way, the beach is just a few minutes away.

The lively and incredibly colorful city center is only 7 km away - you can get there by bus or taxi. Most travelers land at Monastir Airport, which is approximately 65 km from the Alhambra Thalasso hotel complex. Tours offered by agencies already include a comfortable shuttle service. Even in the case of independent travel, the road to the hotel does not take much time.

Hotel Alhambra Thalasso: infrastructure description

According to reviews, the hotel looks very original. The architect who designed the buildings skillfully mixed traditional Moorish and Andalusian styles, and today travelers can feel as if in a real oriental tale.

The hotel consists of several interconnected multi-storey buildings, between which cozy courtyards are equipped. Here you can relax in silence, enjoying the fresh air and bright colors of exotic flowers. By the way, the territory is quite large - there are pools, terraces, and sports fields.

Number and category of rooms

Alhambra Thalasso 5 * - a medium-sized hotel, which consists of 184 rooms. Naturally, guests can choose a room of suitable sizes and categories. Most of the rooms are quite spacious standards, which have either two single beds or one king size bed. There are several small but cozy single rooms.

It also offers two-room rooms. One of the rooms is reserved for a bedroom with a comfortable large bed, and the second is a living room with modern furnishings and folding sofas (if necessary, they can replace extra beds).

Hotel Alhambra Thalasso: room description and photos





As former guests say, living conditions are not bad. The rooms are bright, there is enough space. Of course, the rooms are lined with necessary furniture for work and leisure. The beds are large and the mattresses are comfortable.

The life of a modern tourist is difficult to imagine without a variety of household appliances. The hotel provides guests with the need for convenience. It features a powerful air conditioning system that is easy to control with the remote control. On TV you can watch some music and Russian-language channels.

In the rooms, tourists can connect to the Internet, which is very convenient. There is also a safe with an electronic lock and a telephone. Room service is round the clock. If desired, tourists can order the alarm service (someone will wake you up in the morning), as well as arrange for the delivery of the press.

Guests will appreciate the presence of a bathroom with new equipment. You can count not only on hot water, but also on clean towels (their maids bring it every day), shampoos, soap and other personal hygiene products (replenish their supplies as you use them). Of course, the rooms maintain a certain level of cleanliness - every day the maids carry out wet cleaning, bed linen is changed several times a week.

Should I rely on food? Hotel Cafes and Restaurants





Undoubtedly, nutrition is an important element of a truly good vacation. After all, a trip to another country and immersion in a different culture cannot be imagined without acquaintance with the cuisine.

What does the Alhambra Thalasso hotel complex offer? Meals are chosen by guests, guided by their own needs. You can pre-pay breakfast only or visit the main Alcazar restaurant three times a day. Generous buffets are served here for visitors. As former guests say, the menu here is varied, the food is fresh. Light breakfasts with plenty of fruit will appeal to even the most discerning traveler. And during dinner, you can count on an abundance of meat and fish dishes. And of course, do not forget about the delicious oriental delicacies.

By the way, Hotel Alhambra Thalasso has several other interesting places on the territory of the hotel. For example, for an additional fee, you can visit the Granada restaurant, where you can fully enjoy the delights of Italian cuisine. But the Sevilla restaurant is always ready to offer visitors delicious dishes prepared on the grill.

Tourists respond well to the colorful Moorish cafe Al Andalous, where you can always have a good time. It is recommended to relax with a glass of strong drink in the lobby bar Cortoba.

Of course, this is a lively area, so dozens of cafes and restaurants are within walking distance. However, as evidenced by the reviews and ratings of the hotel, guests prefer to eat on its territory.

Where to look for a beach? Facilities for vacationers





As already mentioned, the modern hotel complex Alhambra Thalasso 5 * is located just a few meters from the main building. A five-minute walk along a wide avenue lined with flowers is unlikely to be a burden to anyone. By the way, this section of the coast belongs to the hotel, so only its guests rest here.

According to tourists, the sea is warm, clean and calm. The beach is cleaned several times a day, so it's nice to relax here. Guests enjoy free large and clean towels, comfortable sun loungers and umbrellas. For an additional fee, you can rent a private tent for the whole day. A bar is open daily, where visitors are always ready to offer drinks from the refrigerator, delicious cocktails, fruit slices and even light snacks.

Of course, fans of more active pastime will also find something to do without problems. For tourists on the shore there is a volleyball court with good coverage. For a small fee, you can rent catamarans and other non-motorized modes of transport. Vacationers get a lot of pleasure from boat trips, parasailing classes, water skiing.

Two kilometers from the Alhambra Thalasso is a large sports center with a diving school. Here travelers can take a quick training course, having received the necessary documents. Tourists who already hold certificates here rent the equipment necessary for immersion, as well as pay for water excursions to the most beautiful places on the coast. By the way, the sea world here is bright and diverse - it is worth seeing with your own eyes.

Description of additional service





What should a tourist expect at the Alhambra Thalasso? The service here is really high level. It is unlikely that anything will overshadow your vacation.

For starters, it's worth talking about amenities. Laundry and dry cleaning services are available on site. For a small fee, clothes can be ironed and shoes can be cleaned. Guests can use the services of an exchanger - the exchange rate is quite normal. By the way, there is an ATM in the main lobby, and both cash and bank cards are accepted for payment. If desired, hotel guests can get a separate safe deposit box in the 24-hour video surveillance area, as well as leave unnecessary luggage in the left-luggage office.

There is a medical office at the hotel - in case of health problems, a doctor will be called to you. And one of the premises of the main building was rebuilt into a pharmacy - here you can buy everything you need for treatment and recovery. There are shops for you that sell various household trifles, as well as branded souvenirs with hotel markings.

Part of the yard is arranged for parking, which is guarded round the clock. By the way, near the parking lot there is a rental car - here tourists rent cars, bicycles, mopeds.

The hotel also has a good business center. For business travelers, there is a small meeting room, as well as a conference room with the necessary equipment (the premises are used for holding not only business, but also holiday events). The hotel can use fax, computer and office equipment. And of course, throughout the territory there is free access to the Internet.

How do travelers spend time? Leisure at the hotel





Alhambra Thalasso is designed and equipped to fully organize the leisure of travelers. Cozy courtyards have terraces for sunbathing, as well as swimming pools (one of them is filled with sea, salt water). In the evenings or in bad weather, tourists have fun by the indoor pool, which is equipped with a modern heating system.

Fans of outdoor activities will not be upset. There are several tennis courts on the territory where experienced players have a good time, while beginners learn the basics of sports from professionals.

You should definitely visit the local wellness center. In the spa, visitors are offered a huge number of anti-aging and wellness treatments - these are massages (including therapeutic ones), all kinds of peels and body wraps. A popular hotel is the availability of its own thalassotherapy center. The territory has a jacuzzi with a powerful hydromassage system, as well as a bathhouse, a sauna and an oriental hammam, which has already become legendary.

Nearby is a beauty salon in which experienced stylists, makeup artists and hairdressers are ready to receive guests. There is also a fitness center with a training room - here you will find modern equipment, as well as professional instructors who are always ready to give advice or make an effective training schedule. By the way, animators regularly conduct various group classes, in particular morning exercises, yoga, dance, aerobics. You can also try archery.

Just a kilometer from the hotel there is a huge golf club with large fields and a training center. Both experienced athletes and beginners have a good time here.

Throughout the day, guests are entertained by animators - they organize volleyball tournaments on the beach, water polo competitions in the pool, various competitions and other events. In the evenings, guests will enjoy vivid performances and live music.

For a small fee, guests can visit the video game room. There are also several tables for billiards. There is also a luxury casino in which many travelers gather in the evenings.

Of course, do not forget about excursions, because Hammamet and its surroundings are rich in historical, cultural and architectural attractions.

Vacation with a child: is the hotel suitable?

Parents planning a vacation with their child are interested in the presence of certain conditions, because the children are more demanding on the level of comfort. Reviews indicate that the hotel complex Hotel Alhambra Thalasso is suitable for family holidays - in the courtyard of the hotel you can often see children playing at different ages.

As already mentioned, on the territory there are two-room rooms that are simply ideal for families with children. On the other hand, an extra bed can even be delivered to a standard room. With nutrition, problems are also unlikely to arise, because a lot of simple but nutritious dishes that are suitable for a child of any age are served at the table.

When it comes to entertainment, here the Alhambra Thalasso has something to offer. One of the courtyards for babies has a special pool. By the way, the kids have a good time on the beach too, splashing around on the shallows and building sand castles. During the high season there is a club where good qualified teachers work with the guys. They also look after the children during the day, organize various games and sports events.

Near the mini-club there is a spacious playground with swings, sandboxes and other attractions for children of all ages. For an additional fee, parents can use the services of an experienced, professional nanny.
