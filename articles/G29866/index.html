Geographical position

In the very center of Asia extends a large mountain range - Altai. On the border between Kazakhstan, Mongolia and China is Gorny Altai - the pearl of untouched nature. As the name implies, the region is dominated by medium-mountainous terrain. The Katun River is one of the main arteries of the region. It flows from the highest passes to the confluence with Biya, where it forms the Ob River. The height of the river in the resort is approximately 1000 meters above sea level. Gorny Altai (‚ÄúFinnish Dacha‚Äù) is a favorite vacation spot for residents of nearby regions.





Features of rest in the Altai Mountains

In recent years, new bases and hotels have been actively built here to accommodate tourists. Since the weather in the Altai Mountains is determined by the climate, there is a pronounced seasonality. The high season lasts from May to the end of September and from December to February. At this time, lovers of outdoor activities come here. In summer, people who want to be in nature, as well as lovers of hiking and extreme entertainment: rafting, bungee jumping, mountain climbing, are selected in the Altai Mountains. And in winter, winter sports lovers and companies gather here seeking a secluded holiday. The bases for which Altai Mountains is famous, including Finnish Dacha, offer a whole range of entertainment and activities that, together with fresh air, make the vacation so wonderful. The specifics of relaxation in the Katun valley is that here you can see many rare plants, waterfalls, caves. For walks, special training and special equipment are not required. Although there are also difficult routes for more prepared tourists.





Climate and weather

Mountain Altai is located in the zone of temperate continental climate. However, the weather is significantly affected by the topography of the place. In general, the region is characterized by warm summers and cold winters. In the Katun valley, summer begins in May, in July the temperature can rise to 35 degrees Celsius, on average, the air warms up in the summer to 23 degrees. Winter begins in November and ends in March, the thermometer during this period can drop to -30 degrees, but average temperatures are around 12 degrees. The weather in the Altai Mountains is subject to great fluctuations, but in general it is milder than in the lowlands.





Altai nature

Tourists travel to Altai to see the untouched, beautiful nature. In the area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe Katun River, nature is not inferior to Alpine in its characteristics. Mountains of medium height, pine, fir and cedar forests, a stormy river, and all this is almost in its original form. Air is one of the main treasures of Altai. The most valuable phytoncides of coniferous plants, which have a very healing effect on people, are dissolved in it. Each recreation center on the banks of the Katun River offers different walking routes in the surrounding area. Middle Altai is suitable for hiking and simple ascents to the peaks. In the Katun valley, you can see many beautiful places: waterfalls, lakes, mountain peaks, caves. And also consider rare representatives of the mountain flora and, possibly, the fauna.

Number of rooms

The Finnish Dacha base offers accommodation in comfortable houses of various levels. In total there are ten two-story houses of different capacities. Each has a guest area with a fireplace, which can accommodate a large company. Cottages "Divny", "Emerald" and "Mountain" suggest accommodation all year round and are rented by numbers. Each house can accommodate up to 28 people. Number-type cottages allow you to rent part of the house or the whole, depending on the number of vacationers. All rooms have private facilities and large balconies. For a large company, it is proposed to rent a separate VIP class cottage, 300 sq.m. There are three bedrooms, a large dining room, an outdoor terrace, a bathhouse, a private pool with a children's section. 7 two-story cottages of different designs measuring 120 square meters can accommodate up to 10 people. The houses have their own territory with gazebos and barbecue facilities. For families and small companies, one-story houses with all the necessary amenities are suitable. The cedar house can accommodate up to 4 people, an arbor and a barbecue area adjoin it. All houses are located on a landscaped, guarded territory, guests can use their own plot near the house and a public area.





Nutrition

There is a restaurant for guests of the Finnish Dacha base, where you can always get high-quality food. The main principle of the chef: a maximum of local, environmentally friendly products. The benefit of neighboring farms in sufficient quantities can supply meat, dairy products and vegetables. A buffet breakfast is served here each morning, with set lunches and dinners, as well as a large menu for ordering meals. The restaurant area can accommodate about 100 people, for banquets there are special rooms for 20 people with a fireplace and karaoke. Guests can enjoy music and dance programs on weekends in the evenings.

Those who wish can well organize their own meals. Many houses are equipped with kitchen utensils, a barbecue area adjoins each house, where you can cook on fire or in a cauldron.





Entertainment

At the Finnish Dacha base, there is no question of what to do. For outdoor enthusiasts, excursions to the most beautiful places of Mountain Altai are offered: Manzherok, Devil's Finger Mountains , Sinyukha, Maiden Tears Waterfalls, Kamyshlinsky, the famous Tavdinsky Caves, Teletskoye Lake, this is not a complete list of attractions that can be seen. For lovers of extreme relaxation, katun rafting, hunting and fishing, ATVs in the taiga are offered. Those who want to take care of their health are given the opportunity to play billiards, table tennis, swim in one of the 3 pools, rent a bicycle and take a walk along the mountain trails. The base also offers massage services, there is a cedar barrel and baths.

For children there are playgrounds with animators, sports corners, children's pools, attractions. If there still seems to be little entertainment, you can drive to the Manzherok zone, where there is a beach area, several clubs and restaurants, and mass entertainment events are held on weekends.
