Club "Caviar" (Kostroma) - the best place for lovers of nightlife

The nightclub "Caviar" (Kostroma) has long received the status of an elite institution among lovers of the city's nightlife and residents of nearby areas. Many want to get here and often go specifically to the city just to visit the "Caviar".

The opening of the club "Caviar" (Kostroma) occurred relatively recently - in September 2016. The event was attended by famous stars of Russian show business with their concert program (in particular, the Beasts and the Brothers Grimm groups).

This place is located in a two-story entertainment center of the same name, in which, in addition to the club, there is also a restaurant, karaoke and a concept club designed for ceremonial banquets.

Fun, celebration, parties, prestige and luxury - all this combines the club "Caviar" (Kostroma). Address: Kostroma, Second Volzhskaya, 4c - this is in the Factory District of the city.





Interior

The interior of the institution is simply amazing with its chic and luxury! Twilight reigns in the halls. Especially pleasing is the dance floor, which is truly just huge. Above it there are two more tiers with VIP-boxes for visitors. The club has a lot of tables that are designed both for large companies and for two people - they are mainly booked by couples. In total, the club "Caviar" (Kostroma) offers 235 soft seats and about 700 on the dance floor.

Stars and hosts of events entertain guests from a large stage, which is equipped with the latest technology, equipped with the best sound and lighting equipment. Near the stage there is a large professional remote for DJs, which is made in the form of a crane. Around the dance floor are tables in the form of barrels, which hints at the predominance of marine themes in the interior of the institution.

Entertainment

Club "Caviar" (Kostroma) is constantly visited with concert programs by the stars of Russian show business and famous DJs. Sometimes the institution hosts Comedy Club concerts, which gather connoisseurs of good humor. All concerts and events are held on the main stage. The administration often organizes events with contests and prizes for guests.





Kitchen and bar

Club "Caviar" (Kostroma) can offer its guests a bar with a wide variety of drinks, both alcoholic and non-alcoholic. Bartenders are always ready to offer several branded cocktails or create a new "work of bartender art" individually by order of the visitor.

Guests can choose only the most delicious and unusual snacks for drinks. Various snacks from fruits and vegetables, meat and fish slices are especially popular in guests' orders. You can treat yourself to a dish prepared on the grill - a delicious example is the charcoal salmon with tartar sauce. It is worth noting that the products used to prepare the dishes are fresh, as the club administration cooperates exclusively with reliable and trusted suppliers.

The service is top-notch: the waiters are kind and helpful, attentive to visitors, orders are delivered quickly, despite the high attendance.





Additional Information

The night club "Caviar" (Kostroma) has its own entry rules. In particular, a dress code has been set, there is also face control - only adults are allowed. The staff of the institution has an impressive amount of security, since the safety of guests here comes first.

The institution accepts both cash and credit cards. The price category of the club's services is quite high, but this is the case when the price justifies the quality.

For club visitors who come in their cars, there is a guarded parking lot. The building itself has an open access point to the Internet, which allows guests to always stay, as they say, in touch on social networks.

Club "Caviar" (Kostroma) is open exclusively on Fridays and Saturdays, from 8 pm to 5 am.





This place is ideal for special occasions: for example, within its walls you can celebrate a birthday in the noisy and cheerful company of your friends - the holiday will remain unforgettable.
