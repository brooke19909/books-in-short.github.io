Estella Hotel Apts 3 * (Cyprus, Limassol): rating, reviews, photos

An unforgettable Cyprus holiday will delight travelers with the cozy resort hotel Estella Hotel Apts 3 *. Here, absolute comfort and quality service are organically combined with budget rates for accommodation.





Location

Just a six-minute walk from the well-maintained public beach is the resort Estella Hotel Apts 3 *. Cyprus, Limassol, Germasogeia, 4532 - the address where travelers can find this institution. The distance from the hotel to the main infrastructure facilities is as follows:

  • pharmacy - 0.1 km;
  • mini-market - 0.1 km;
  • supermarket - 0.3 km;
  • fish restaurant - 0.3 km;
  • night club - 0.5 km.

Accommodation

Travelers who appreciate comfort should opt for Estella Hotel Apts 3 *. Here are the options available for placement:

  • Studio is a spacious room with a private kitchen area. The guests are accommodated in two single beds, which can be installed together or separately. Opposite is a dressing table with a mirror, which, thanks to additional lighting, can be used as a writing. Near the window there is a small folding sofa for relaxing and accommodating an additional guest.
  • The superior studio also has twin beds and a desk. In the recreation area, instead of a sofa, there is a coffee table with a couple of comfortable soft chairs. The main difference from the previous category is that the kitchen is allocated in a separate room.
  • The family room is functional and large. It has two bedrooms. Each of them has its own combined bathroom, as well as access to the balcony. One of the rooms is equipped with a kitchenette, as well as a dining area. One and a half beds and a folding sofa are provided for guests.
  • Suite is a luxuriously decorated, recently renovated room that combines several functional areas within one space. The bedroom is represented by a large orthopedic bed, a pair of nightstands and a dressing table. In the living area there are 2 soft sofas (which can also be used as extra beds), an armchair and a coffee table. There is a workplace. There is also a kitchen area with household appliances, kitchenware and a dining table.

  • TV practically does not work;
  • Too small kitchen area, which is inconvenient to cook;
  • the rooms lack a microwave;
  • there are many cracks on the ceiling and walls;

The main advantage of this hotel is its convenient location, it will certainly please active tourists who like to spend time on the beach and on excursions.
