Jamo - speaker system: models, features

Danish company Jamo is one of the oldest European manufacturers of audio equipment. In the history of the manufacturer, there were several turning points, each of which raised the quality of its products to a new level. Today, the company conducts its activities, mostly based on its own developments, which allows it to form a unique sound style. Engineers are developing in different directions, increasing the technological capabilities of Jamo products. The modern speaker system is endowed with wireless modules, optimized interfaces and, in principle, new communication capabilities. All this in different forms is also present in the equipment of the Danish manufacturer.





Jamo Product Features

The company combines two seemingly opposite styles in its creations. So, frankly experimental models are regularly traced in its assortment. Among the most striking examples can be identified unpacked 6-way Jamo speakers. The R 909 speaker system demonstrated how optimized the high-end technology can be. However, this development has not received further development.

Nevertheless, the main and most sensitive features of the models of this company are determined by a completely different - conservative style. Following him and the best traditions of the production of audio systems, the creators endow the speakers with clear, dynamic and open sound. Music lovers characterize Jamo speakers as natural and realistic. The detail and comprehensive study of the composition is provided by many modern companies, perfecting the technical component of the performance of the speakers. But in this case, there is a lively sound, and this can only be achieved by a skilled audio engineer.

Model Studio 180





The model is not a business card of the company, but many music lovers loved it for its balanced characteristics and affordable price tag. The complex with a sensitivity of 92 dB produces 170 W and covers a frequency range within 50 Hz-20 kHz. Obviously, such indicators attract many, given that the model is sold on the market for 20-25 thousand rubles. What explains such an advantageous offer? Many note the frankly low quality of the materials of the body and finish. Therefore, immediately after the purchase, it is recommended that the Jamo Studio 180 speaker system be improved in terms of insulation. This procedure is carried out by lightly smearing plasticine, and from the inside, experts also advise gluing the cases with a Gerlen sound insulator. For the depth of playback of the lower spectrum, it is desirable to increase the rigidity of the structure, physically strengthening it with spacers.

Model C 95





Representative of the younger segment of 2-way floor systems. Although the company specializes in 3-way massive speakers, successful models also appear in the middle and junior classes. In this case, the decoration and the internal filling deserve attention. As for the first, the developers used metal inserts and as a whole realized the design in a monolithic style, which is also characteristic of Jamo products. The C 95 speaker system in terms of internal design has many differences, which are the new twitter with an increased gimbal and a special high-frequency link configuration.

An innovative TDR distortion minimization system was used, due to which the sound became rich and clear. As for the filling materials, the diffusers are made from hybrid composites, and the speakers use titanium and aluminum.

Model S506 HCS3

One of the most famous systems of the company released in recent years. The interest in this kit is due to the combination of high power and small size, which ensures ease of use without loss in basic performance. However, to extract the most profitable sound, you still have to carefully choose the installation location of the components. For this system, in particular, placement in the corners is contraindicated, as this will lead to minimization in the depth of the bass.





The front design is represented by a 2-way component, which is complemented by one tweeter and a pair of mid- and low-frequency speakers. Of particular note is the modified Jamo S506 HCS3 Wenge speaker system, which implemented an interesting feature in terms of balance in volume and lower spectrum reproduction. Even at low volume, low frequencies are not clogged with the upper register, giving a clear and detailed disclosure of the sound "picture". As for the middle and high frequencies as such, they are initially reproduced without problems, naturally reflecting the tiniest nuances of the part.

Conclusion

Each manufacturer of audio equipment over time develops its own tools to achieve a high quality product. Balancing the characteristics of the internal filling, optimized finishing materials and extensive customization options are the key points that Jamo draws attention to. The acoustic system of this brand may not be so well known to a large audience of lovers, but professionals are well acquainted with its merits. In addition, among the leaders of the segment, the company is gradually gaining weight, popularizing high-tech solutions. For users of entry-level speakers, these products may be of interest and affordable cost. At least against the background of competing systems.
