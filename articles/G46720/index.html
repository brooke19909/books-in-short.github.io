We make bright and beautiful Christmas bouquets of sweets with our own hands.

It is always nice to give original and beautiful gifts made by yourself. And it is doubly pleasant to surprise your loved ones and friends with a gorgeous bouquet of roses, tulips or crocuses with a sweet surprise inside. It is not difficult to make such a present; anyone, even a novice florist, can cope with such a task.

In this article we will tell you how to make New Year's bouquets of sweets with your own hands, as well as reveal some secrets of making sweets. Such bright, tasty and beautiful crafts will surprise and delight anyone.

We make creative Christmas bouquets of sweets with our own hands

Candy compositions are made out in two versions: in the form of a standard bouquet and in the form of a basket. Crafts made using a floristic basket look more chic and expensive, and in addition, you can put more sweets in it. To make such a presentation - a bouquet of tea roses - you will need the following materials and tools:

  • basket for flowers;
  • material "mesh" for the design of the basket;
  • sweets in foil;
  • corrugated paper (cream, red);
  • watercolor paints;
  • thin tape;
  • scissors;
  • spool of thread;
  • knitting needle;
  • skewers for barbecue or tree branches;
  • wooden toothpicks.

In the meantime, we begin to prepare the hearts of roses. We take the candy, slightly expand the wrapper and strengthen the toothpick in sweets, the main thing is that the filling is non-liquid. New Year's bouquets from candies. With our own hands we will make a stylish composition from Rafaello

We bring to your attention another wonderful New Year's composition - a bouquet of Rafaello sweets. It is done quite simply. For work you will need sweets, foil, wire, organza, thin tape, corrugated paper.

Bouquet of sweets: manufacturing instructions

Each candy is wrapped in foil and attached to the wire. Then small squares (20 * 20 cm) are cut from the organza, folded in half and wrapped around each Rafaello. It is convenient to fix the organza with a thin ribbon. The wire is decorated with foil. After that, all the flowers are collected in a bouquet, which is made out of corrugated paper.
