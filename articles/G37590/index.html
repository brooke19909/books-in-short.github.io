Hotel "Amaks", Perm: location, description of rooms, hotel infrastructure, photo

Hotel "Amaks" in Perm is a link in the well-known hotel chain. Belonging to a well-known brand is one of the guarantees of quality service. Here you will find comfortable and cozy rooms, as well as a host of additional services for work and good rest.

Location

The convenient location in the central part of the city attracts travelers to the Amaks Hotel in Perm. The address of the institution is Monastyrskaya street, 43.

The main attractions of Perm are within walking distance from this place - Opera and Ballet Theater, Drama Theater, Museum of Local Lore, the house of the merchant Gribushin and many others. Directly opposite the hotel is the Transfiguration Cathedral.
