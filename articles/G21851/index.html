Mukarnas Hotel, Turkey: description of rooms, service, reviews. Mukarnas Resort & Spa 5 *

Choosing the direction of your trip is not always simple and easy. If you go on a trip yourself, it is enough to choose what the soul of the person going on vacation asks for. When planning a family vacation, it is necessary to take into account the interests of all family members: someone prefers bathing and sunbathing in the cleanest waters of the Mediterranean, for some it is important to have a variety of day and night activities, and someone is a supporter of fascinating excursions to the sights of the newly explored area.

The city of Alanya is one of the most popular tourist centers of Turkish lands, which is preferred by residents of different countries. It is located in the intermountain valley on velvet sands. The mild climate, clean air and beautiful landscapes delight the soul of every vacationer, regardless of age. Five-star hotel Mukarnas Resort & Spa - creates all the necessary conditions for complete relaxation and enjoy the days spent in Turkey.





general characteristics

Hotel Mukarnas Resort & Spa 5 * (Turkey, Alanya, Okurcalar) was erected in 2007 and for the entire period of its operation attracted a large number of tourists, giving an unforgettable pastime at the resort. Antalya International Airport is 90 km away. Hotel "Mukarnas" 5 * (Turkey) is located amid an abundance of green vegetation represented by palm trees and shrubs. Pets are strictly prohibited. Car parking spaces are provided free of charge.





Number of rooms

The total number of rooms in the hotel "Mukarnas Spa Resort" (Turkey) is 425, four of which are designed for people with disabilities. There are 50 non-smoking apartments. All chambers are divided into categories:

  • Standard
  • Family
  • Junior
  • Luxury.

All rooms are decorated in a classic, simple style. Color reference - shades of beige in combination with brown. The floors are carpeted or laminate. Unique floor lamps, an original shape of mirrors and spaciousness provide a comfortable stay. All chambers have balconies, most with direct or side views of the Mediterranean Sea. Meeting sunrises and seeing off sunsets at a glass table on wicker chairs is to the liking of every hotel guest. Panoramic windows offer a beautiful view of the endless blue distances and the green park.

Room cleaning at the Mukarnas Hotel (Turkey) is carried out daily, it is carried out by tidy, conscientious maids, linen is changed twice a week. Wi-Fi - in each room, use is included in the price. Cots are available upon request for free.

Mukarnas Hotel: room description

The room furniture is presented as a standard set: beds, bedside tables, armchairs, table and wardrobe. Equipment includes a direct dial telephone, satellite TV, hairdryer. The operation of the central air conditioner regulates the temperature regime depending on weather conditions. An electronic safe is available to everyone, provides peace of mind to vacationers and a lack of concern for cash or important documents.

Important is the availability of an electric kettle and a set of dishes for tea drinking. Tea set and coffee of different varieties, along with sugar are offered daily during your stay. The minibar includes carbonated and soft drinks, water and juices. A distinctive feature is its daily replenishment.

The bathroom is finished with high-quality ceramic tiles, equipped with a shower or bathtub. The presence of all the necessary toiletries and hygiene products allows you to relax without thinking about anything. Disposable slippers and bathrobes are provided to make your stay in your home.

Family rooms at the Mukarnas Hotel (Turkey) are of a larger scale, which is 40-50 square meters, and the presence of two rooms connected by an adjacent door. Some have bunk beds.

Swimming as a form of active pastime

The swimming complex is represented by four pools: 3 open and one indoor. The main one has a depth of 1.45 m, surrounded by green tropical plants and green palm trees, showers, sun loungers with shadow canopies and umbrellas. Soft and comfortable mattresses contribute to a relaxing mood while enjoying sunbathing, giving a unique tan of dark colors. The existing wooden bridge, which is forbidden to dive, conditionally splits the pool into two parts.

The closed swimming area includes a small pool for children, a large one and a jacuzzi, which act as a water massager. Through panoramic windows, the light of the rays of the sun penetrates here and attracts people to the street. That is why the number of holidaymakers here is usually small.

The small water park, which has four fascinating slopes, delights not only teenagers, but also the older generation of travelers.

Beach

The beach area is one of the most important on the trip, here vacationers spend the lion's share of vacation time. Hotel "Mukarnas" the beach has sand and gravel, the entrance to the water is gentle. The distance to it from the hotel is 100 meters. An amazing highlight is the pontoon - an artificial territory that protrudes into the sea. Like the coastal zone, it is dotted with sun loungers, umbrellas and awnings. There is a special place for diving - a favorite pastime of all generations.

The beachfront bar, an integral part of the All Inclusive concept, offers visitors refreshments, alcoholic cocktails, fresh juices and a range of fast food snacks. Everyone will be able to find a snack to their liking, after prolonged splashing in the azure waters of the Mediterranean.

For children, a children's playground with a slide and a swing is built. Towels are issued free of charge, in the amount of one piece per day. A number of water attractions are represented by banana skiing, skiing, parasailing and other fun activities.

You can compete in a team game thanks to the volleyball court. This game allows you to get to know new people of different nationalities, expand your horizons, while not forgetting about caring for the body.

The beach is equipped with changing rooms and a shower. At the entrance there is the opportunity to rent a cozy gazebo, where a romantic time spent in company with a loved one will be remembered forever.

Responsible rescuers monitor the order and safety of vacationers 24 hours a day.
