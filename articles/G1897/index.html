Crafts with cats: interesting ideas from improvised materials

Cats are one of the most amazing animals. They live next to people for more than five millennia. They combine many conflicting qualities - sensitivity, kindness, pride, independence, etc. Cats have become very popular animals in folklore, literature. In addition, children simply adore them. That is why cat crafts are very popular. Let's consider different options for creating crafts from improvised materials.

Chenille wire cats

Chenille wire is a very pleasant and soft material that children like crazy. Make a cat out of it for a child is not difficult.

Take for a cotton ball of different diameters. The smaller will be used for the head, the larger for the torso. Make a small hole and apply some silicone glue from the gun to it. Next, we take a brown or gray chenille wire, insert it into the hole and begin to carefully wrap around the ball. Similarly, wrap the second ball with wire.

Then with glue we connect the torso and head of the cat. We twist paws, ears and a muzzle from a white wire. Now we connect all the parts with glue. It remains to make a tail from the wire with a white tip and glue the eyes. Antennae can be made of ordinary wire. Nice little kitten is ready.

Chenille wire is a very convenient material from which you can create a variety of designs. Imagine with your child and make a whole family of cats!

Felting cat

Another great option is to make a felting cat using the felting technique. Such a figure will turn out to be very cute and realistic.

We take two foam balls. They can be purchased in the store for creativity. You can buy balls in the shape of an egg. With a smaller ball we cut a little, this will be the head of the cat. We insert a toothpick inside the larger of the balls, prick a small ball and glue them with glue.

Now we take special needles and wool for felting. We put the material on the foam with the needles . You can take any colors. White cats with black spots look beautiful. We twist the tail, ears and paws in the palms and glue it. Eyes can be made from cardboard or purchased "running" eyes for crafts. The antennae are made of thread or wire.

Sponge cat

Even two-three-year-old kids can do this cat work with the help of their parents. It is ideal for playing with a small child, because it is soft, but at the same time elastic. If you make eyes from a button, the child will be able to play with the cat in the bathroom.
