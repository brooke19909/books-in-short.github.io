Hotel Panorama Village & Hotel 3 * (Greece, Crete): description, services, reviews

When it comes to beach vacations in Europe, many tourists choose the island part of Greece as their vacation destination. The most famous and popular among travelers is the island of Crete, which perfectly combines the picturesque nature, clean beaches, interesting history and, of course, sincere hospitality and first-class service. Often, tourists choose a hotel complex Panorama Village Hotel 3 * for a stop.

Of course, before booking it is worth getting as much information as possible about the chosen place. For example, it is useful to know where the hotel is located, what is the distance to the airport, city and beach. Do they offer decent living conditions here? Will there be any problems with nutrition? What is the opinion of former hotel guests?

Location and additional information about the hotel





Hotel Panorama Village is located in a small but very colorful fishing village called Agia Pelagia. By the way, it was erected on a hillside, and therefore from almost anywhere there is a breathtaking panorama of the bay and the sea. Just 18 km away is the lively island capital of Heraklion. All planes fly here, so the road to the hotel does not take much time: just a few minutes in a comfortable transfer - and you can enjoy the comfort of the hotel.

The hotel is a complex of several three-story buildings. There is also a fairly spacious area that resembles a beautiful, blooming garden - you can admire the bright colors of nature, relax in the shade of cypress trees, relax and sunbathe on the terraces. By the way, the hotel was opened in 1984 - it has been hosting tourists from all over the world for decades. In 2007, a complete reconstruction was carried out here.

Room conditions: photos and additional information





The hotel complex is considered quite large - on its territory there are 160 rooms. These are standard rooms designed for 2 or 3 people (a rollaway bed can be delivered if necessary). At your disposal will be your own balcony or a small veranda, where it is so nice to relax.

The furniture in the rooms, although not new, is comfortable and beautiful. Hotel guests can count on working household appliances. Air conditioning will help to cope with high temperatures. There is a small refrigerator where you can store purchased food and drinks. For a separate but small fee, tourists use a safe. Naturally, the room has a TV (you can watch satellite channels), a radio and a telephone.

The bathroom is small, but the plumbing is new, and there are no problems with the water supply. You can take a shower at any convenient time. Guests are provided with personal hygiene products, as well as clean towels (they change almost every day) and a hair dryer (not too powerful, but enough for drying). The maids at the hotel are honest and the rooms are always clean and fresh.

Do guests offer food?





Of course, when planning a vacation, you should worry about nutrition, because this is an important part of relaxation. Hotel Panorama Village Hotel 3 * offers tourists a very convenient all-inclusive scheme. Of course, if you wish, you can refuse to eat at the hotel, but the reviews of the guests indicate that the food here is incredibly tasty.

Breakfasts, dinners and lunches are held in the hall of the hotel restaurant - there are a sufficient number of tables, chairs and utensils, and the room itself is decorated in a traditional Greek style. Guests are offered snacks, salads, fresh fruits. There are always several meat and fish dishes to choose from. Quite often, the restaurant serves seafood. Add to that the side dishes, delicious freshly baked pastries and desserts - this is how the food on site looks like.

There is a bar with a terrace by the pool - here you can get a chilled drink and light snacks. Bartenders are also happy to make original cocktails for guests.
