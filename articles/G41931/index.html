Sanatorium "Sosnovy" in Izhevsk is a diversified health resort in Udmurtia

The multidisciplinary health resorts of Udmurtia include the Sosnovy sanatorium in Izhevsk, located in a forest-park zone. A year-round health resort specializing in preventive health improvement programs accepts adults and parents with children. Each patient who came to the "Pine" on a package tour receives an individual complex of wellness treatments.

Infrastructure

All the sanatoriums of Izhevsk, including Sosnovy, are provided with the necessary amenities for living and undergoing medical procedures. This is a residential building with a capacity of 200 beds, which looks like a good hotel with different comfort rooms and, accordingly, is equipped with modern technology in more comfortable rooms. The suites include a fridge and TV. In the evening, you can relax on the balcony, taking a session of natural herbal medicine before bedtime. Balconies are in every room, regardless of the level of comfort.





There is a swimming pool, saunas, a winter garden with exotic plants and a cinema in the forested area. Specialization and equipment of the sanatorium

The sanatorium specializes in more than 50 types of treatment and prevention, thanks to being equipped with the most modern equipment. Among them: diseases of the gastrointestinal tract, female and male genital areas, skin diseases, endocrine, nervous and respiratory systems, as well as the musculoskeletal system.





In order to carry out rehabilitation and treatment, there is a treatment department in Izhevsk sanatorium "Sosnovy". It has a hydropathic center, heat treatment and electrotherapy rooms, treatment rooms for healing showers and underwater massage. There is a mud bath, massage rooms, herbal medicine, phototherapy.

Salt cave - the pride of the sanatorium

For treatment of the respiratory tract, lungs and general health improvement, the sanatorium has a halochamber and a unique installation that lowers the oxygen content in the air, almost like in the mountains at an altitude, called "Mountain Air". Pulmonologists from St. Petersburg and specialists from the Sosnovy sanatorium in Izhevsk were the first in Udmurtia to equip a halochamber or, as it is called, a salt cave, revetting the walls of the hall with salt slabs. Thanks to several courses of procedures in the salt cave, children who were registered in the dispensary with respiratory diseases recovered. And this magical salt cave works very simply. In a room with a humidity of 60% and a temperature of 20-23 ¬∞ C, finely dispersed salt is sprayed. Its particles, falling on the bronchi, during several courses bring patients to normal health.





Hydropathic and mud baths

All sanatoriums in Izhevsk, and Sosnovy, are no exception; they use imported peat mud from Chernushki-2 deposits in the Zavyalovsky district and blue clay from Sharkansky district in mud therapy. For thermal treatment of the musculoskeletal system, a mineral of petroleum origin - ozokerite is used. Undergoing treatment, the patient's body receives biologically active substances that penetrate the skin, contribute to increased blood circulation, improve tissue nutrition, showing a painkiller effect.

In the hydropathic center of Izhevsk sanatorium "Sosnovy", patients have the opportunity to take various baths, depending on the appointment of the doctor. Typically, baths such as pearl, salt-coniferous and with sea salt are prescribed to patients for the overall healing of the body and eliminate fatigue. Patients with arthritis and arthrosis are usually prescribed turpentine baths. Doctors recommend iodine-bromide baths for patients with diseases of the nervous and endocrine systems.
