We choose Lake Kazan for relaxation

There is an amazing city in Tatarstan where the thousand-year-old Muslim oriental culture is in perfect harmony with modern western skyscrapers. It is no less exciting to study its nature, especially the lakes of Kazan, most of which are located directly in the city.

This is exactly what the capital of Tatarstan looks like - a hospitable hostess of a beautiful and majestic region.

City ponds

Even his legends are associated with water, as it has been since ancient times. For example, the name of the city itself is based on the legend that in search of a place for its construction people turned to the sage and wizard for help. What was their surprise and disappointment when they heard that the city would be where a boiler with water would boil without fire.

For a miracle to happen, they made it of gold and began to check, maybe where the earth warmed up enough to boil water. And so it happened - in the place where the Bulak-river flowed into the Kazanka, the boiler began to boil without flame. Here the city of Kazan arose, named after that very event.



And it so happened that it is here that numerous lakes are located, which for modern residents of the capital have become their favorite places to relax:

Blue Lakes

A whole chain of ponds stretches, framed by emerald greenery, just 20 km from the city. Lakes with water of unusual blue color seem to flow into one another and resemble the low of precious sapphires dropped by someone here a long time ago.

The blue lakes of Kazan are of karst origin and formed on the site of craters resulting from subsidence of soil hundreds of years ago. The earth has failed along with trees, the remains of which can be seen today.



Most of the lakes are shallow, but everyone has very clear and transparent water, which diving enthusiasts often use. Fishermen do not exist here, since there is no fish at all - cold and salty water does not allow it to live or breed here.

Underground sources that feed the Blue Lakes with their waters give them a sulfate composition, which makes them medicinal, so you can often meet people bathing here, although the water temperature of +8 degrees requires some hardening.

Divers of the Blue Lakes have a custom to install a decorated Christmas tree at the bottom of the deepest of them, which is clearly visible from the shore due to clean and clear water.

Deep Lake

How big is the capital of Tatarstan can be judged by the size of the reservoirs located on its territory. Deep Lake (Kazan), a recreation center on its shore belong to the Kirovsky district of the city. This is a surprisingly beautiful wild corner of nature inside city life.

Surrounded by coniferous forest, the reservoir is located behind the village of Zalesny. Its depth is 26 m, and only on one side is the coast adapted for swimming and diving, for which special bridges are equipped here. Very clean sand and a water temperature of +23 degrees make it a favorite place for a family summer vacation, when work "does not let" people out of the city.



Very close to the coast, in the forest, there are cozy cottages of the recreation center "Deep Lake". Their fund is designed for 1-, 2- and 3-bed rooms with all amenities. The houses are equipped with heating, so here you can celebrate the New Year or spend the winter holidays. Luxury cottages have a fireplace, air conditioning and a toilet in each room.

Emerald lake

This place can hardly be called a lake, since its origin is completely artificial. In place of the sand pit, which was excavated by excavators from year to year, a pond was formed that filled groundwater. Be that as it may, Lake Emerald (Kazan) fully lives up to its name, and its waters are really unusually beautiful in color.

It is good that the reservoir passed into private ownership, and here they strictly monitor cleanliness, are fined for rubbish thrown in the wrong place (there are bins everywhere), and they monitor the state of the sand. Water also meets all sanitary standards, and on the shore for the convenience of vacationers there are changing rooms and "mushrooms" from the sun. For the kids, the "paddling pool" is fenced for swimming, as the bottom here abruptly breaks, which can be dangerous for those who can not swim.
