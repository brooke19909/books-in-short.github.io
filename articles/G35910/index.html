Cyprus, Protaras: traveler reviews

White sandy beaches, favorable resort conditions, the azure sea, picturesque landscapes, rich cultural and historical heritage attracts tourists to its networks. Protaras receives only positive reviews from travelers, because it is one of the best resorts on the island. It is located a few kilometers from Ayia Napa, the tourism business in the city began to develop in the 80s of the last century. Today it is a successful and popular resort in Europe and not only a resort with its name and face.



Unique sandy beaches and convenient descent into the water are different from other maritime countries of Cyprus. Protaras (the water temperature here does not fall below + 15 ° C even in winter, and reaches + 25 ° C in summer) is ideal for families with small children. The length of the coastline is large enough, which allows you to choose a beach at your discretion. For example, Skoutari Beach is suitable for snorkelling, and Fig Tree Bay offers a wide selection of water activities. Most hotels, private villas, apartments are located near the coast. It is very convenient for tourists who prefer a beach holiday.

A huge number of attractions remarkable Cyprus. Protaras receives only good and warm reviews from travelers, but they mainly relate to high-class service, beautiful nature, although there are interesting places here too. Many will want to visit the Ocean Aquarium, in which about 1000 species of representatives of the marine element live. Separately kept enclosures with penguins and crocodiles. Not only adults, but also children will like it here.



Spectacular performances are famous for Cyprus. Protaras (a map of attractions will help to visit all interesting places) amazes guests with a delightful show of “Dancing Fountains” that dance to the music in color rays. Many will like a walk to Cape Cavo Greco, which is notable for its bizarre shape of the coastline and sea caves. Particularly successful photographs are taken in this place, the newlyweds come to take pictures, sitting on a bench by the tree, and also kiss on the "Bridge of Sinners" in order to be in love forever.

Unique architectural sights of interest Cyprus. Protaras, reviews of which cause a desire to visit this amazing place, does not have many interesting structures, but still all visitors should visit the church of St. Ilias. This building is unique in that it is located on a high cliff. To climb to it, you need to go up the stairs, which offers a wonderful view of the whole city. A “wish tree” was planted near the church, on which a ribbon should be tied so that the cherished dream comes true.



The vibrant nightlife of many young people is attracted to Cyprus. Protaras receives only positive feedback from outdoor enthusiasts, because there is a wide selection of entertainment venues, many restaurants, bars, discos, nightclubs. Holidays at this resort will be remembered for a long time, each traveler will bring home a lot of positive emotions and beautiful photos.
