The best shopping centers in Bangkok

Bangkok is one of the most visited cities in Thailand. It is in it that not only the main attractions of the country are concentrated, but also huge shopping centers offering visitors a wide range of goods. Why is Thai shopping so popular among Russian tourists? First of all, these are the lowest prices throughout Thailand, which will please especially residents of provincial cities. All Thai products are famous for their excellent workmanship, in addition, shopping in Thailand is always an appropriate bargain.

The best shopping centers in Bangkok are entire multi-storey buildings, divided into specific types of goods. Large city shops number in the hundreds, so any tourist can easily find one of them. In most cases, each separate floor of Bangkok's shopping centers is dedicated to a particular type of product: children's clothing, shoe stores, or sports and fitness products.

All shopping centers in Bangkok are usually concentrated in the tourist areas of the city, usually within walking distance from the subway. In this article, we will focus on the most interesting shopping sites, which should definitely be seen by every visitor to this beautiful exotic country.

On the territory of the huge mall, the best famous brands such as Gucci, Prada, Louis Vuitton and many others will be available for your attention. It is worth noting that if you went to the Siam shopping center in search of budget goods, then this decision is rather reckless, since the price category for most of the products is slightly overestimated. But it is in this shopping center in Bangkok that monthly large sales are held.

Terminal 21

Terminal 21 Shopping Center is the youngest Thai venue. This is truly an amazing place, each floor of which represents the culture of a particular country. The name of the shopping center came from the fact that outwardly it resembles an airport building. A visitor can easily travel to different world capitals simply by changing floor by floor. Tax free

A pleasant tax refund procedure awaits every tourist who has arrived in Thailand and made a purchase for a certain amount of money. The Tax Free system in this exotic country has a slightly different name - VAT Refund and guarantees a 7% return on the total amount for which the purchase was made.

What are the rules for VAT Refund?

Thai VAT Refund system is a special yellow A4 form with an attached original receipt from the store. In addition, Thailand has a number of tax rules:

  • Firstly, the amount of one purchase must be more than 2,000 baht in order to receive a special check attached to the form.
  • Secondly, the total amount of all purchases must exceed the mark of 5000 baht.

Before shopping, you should make sure that the store operates according to the VAT Refund system, as evidenced by the specialized blue and white logo shown in the photo below.

In order to fully use the services of VAT Refund, you must:

  1. Be a tourist.
  2. Not be on the crew list of one of the airlines.
  3. Fly out of the country through one of the international airports.
  4. The purchase period should not exceed 60 days.

Conclusion

Many major shopping centers in Bangkok practice a generous system of discounts, as well as periodically conduct large sales, where prices for most of the products presented drop immediately several times. Thailand is a country of contrasts, full of surprises! Have a nice shopping!
