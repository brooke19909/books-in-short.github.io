Olmecs and Aztecs

Long before mankind became aware of what continent Mexico is on, the highly developed Olmec civilization inhabited the New World. Today, they are mentioned by volumetric stone heads created by them. The invention of hieroglyphs and a calendar belongs to this very people.



Mexico is the name of one of the northern wandering tribes. In the end, the name of this tribe formed the basis for the name of the whole country. Its leader Xolotl became the founder of the Aztec tribe. And even later, the national symbol of the state will be the distinctive sign of the Aztecs - an eagle that sits on a cactus.
