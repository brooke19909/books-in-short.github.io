Guests can go boating, catamarans or a boat trip along the Volga, go fishing or go mushroom and berry picking into the forest. There is a pier on the river bank. You can rent sports equipment. Children will enjoy talking to animals in the zoo. The following accommodation options are offered:

  • four-bedded room in a house with two single and one double bed - from 1500 rubles per day per person;
  • double room with two single beds - from 1500 rubles per day per person.

Each room is equipped with a shower, TV and toilet, the price includes three meals a day. The recreation center is located at: Yuryevets district of the Ivanovo region, the village of Yakimikha.
