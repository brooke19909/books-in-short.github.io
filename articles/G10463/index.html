Where is it better to spend a vacation? Summer Holidays: Yevpatoriya, recreation center, services, photo

With the onset of the summer season, many begin to think where to go to relax. As a rule, residents of large cities prefer to spend holidays on the sea. In this case, the Crimean peninsula, Yevpatoriya, will become an ideal option. Recreation facilities in this city are located right on the coast. Thanks to the excellent conditions and reasonable prices, they always have a lot of visitors.

This article will be useful for those who want to visit Evpatoria for the first time. In order to help you choose a suitable recreation center, we will describe the most popular ones.

Acquaintance with the area

So, you have decided to come to the wonderful city - Yevpatoriya on the peninsula. Rest here will be simply unforgettable. There are many hotels, resorts and recreation centers for tourists. Let's face it, the proposed housing for different tastes and means. Those who would like to have a good rest and not spend a lot of money at the same time are the ideal holiday base. They offer, of course, not fashionable rooms, but there are both a suite and a junior suite. If the requests are more modest, then you can limit yourself to a double or single room. As a rule, depending on the cost, a certain list of services is offered.

Recreation center "Arina", Yevpatoriya

On the territory of this recreation center there is one four-story building in which tourists are accommodated. At their service there is a parking for up to 50 places (additional charge). The guests in the rooms are waiting for excellent conditions: shower, hot water, refrigerator, air conditioning. The kitchen is in a separate building, it is equipped with modern appliances, there is also a microwave. On the territory far from the residential building there is a mangal zone. Here you can cook your own barbecue and use the barbecue. For children there is an equipped playground. Parents may not worry about them, as the territory of the base is protected. Therefore, nothing can spoil the parents rest.

Evpatoria, the recreation center "Arina", are popular among vacationers, primarily due to affordable prices. There is also a beautiful beach, close to the market, post office. There is a cafe in the base. For adults, there are tennis courts, billiards tables and an area for active sports.



Holiday Village "Seaside"

All guests who came to rest, Evpatoria (recreation center "Seaside") will be warmly welcomed and give pleasure. Here, crystal clear air, sea breeze, pleasant sun, clean beach, warm water. A new three-story building, designed for 12 rooms, has been built on the territory of the recreation center. Each of them has a balcony where you can have a tea party or just spend time reading a book. Hot water in the rooms is constant, there is a shower, new furniture, TV and fridge. For youth, a disco with an entertainment program is arranged in the evenings.



The recreation center "Dolphin 93"

In the city of Yevpatoriya on Simferopolskaya street, there is a recreation center called "Dolphin 93". It is very close to the sea. Vacationers can hear the sound of the surf right from their rooms. There are three buildings on the territory. Among them there are both single and double-decker. It is worth noting that the parking zone is located directly behind the buildings, you do not need to pay extra for the car. The rooms have a kitchen with all necessary appliances, hot water, shower, air conditioning and TV. Maid service is available upon request. Sheets and towels are changed after 5 days. The territory is cleaned and guarded daily around the clock. That is why nothing can overshadow the rest. Evpatoria, recreation center "Dolphin 93" offer discounts to their regular customers.
