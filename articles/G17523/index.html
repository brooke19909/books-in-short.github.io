Hotel Titanic Palace 5 (Egypt / Hurghada)

Description: Spend your vacation on the Red Sea at the Titanic Palace 5 hotel, founded in 2010, just 10 km from the airport of Hurghada, a few steps from the beach. This is a real paradise for couples with children, since a huge water park with water slides and pools is built on a spacious and clean area. For guests, all conditions are created for an unforgettable, safe, relaxing and comfortable stay.



The adjacent territory is striking in its landscape splendor, here every bush is a work of art: beautiful green spaces pleasantly delight the eye and harmoniously fit into the general interior of the hotel. And in the middle of this green oasis are one-story bungalows and the main building, which houses the registration department and rooms.

Being in the complex of Titanic Palace 5 Hurghada, you will constantly feel the friendly attitude of the staff and the indescribable atmosphere of the holiday. A huge selection of gourmet restaurants, varied leisure time and interesting animated performances will turn your vacation into a fairy tale that will remain in your memory for a lifetime.

Rooms: Pension Titanic Palace 5 has 593 rooms: Standard (one-room - no children allowed), Suite, Family (2 bedrooms), Bungalow Room (35 m2 with access to an outdoor pool - accommodation only for adults without children) .



Each room, regardless of its category, is beautifully decorated, equipped with air conditioning and a spacious terrace with amazing views of the courtyard or the blue sea. Guests are free to use the provided amenities, such as: bathroom, plasma TV (satellite TV), long-distance telephone line, safe, hairdryer.

There is Internet access and a bar with drinks (filled for an extra charge). You do not have to think about cleanliness and order, employees who will clean your room and make the bed will think about it for you daily.

Meals (all inclusive): Comprehensive and wholesome meals are offered to guests at the Titanic Palace 5. Hotel reviews by tourists are enthusiastic. The tables are always full of fresh fruits, vegetables, dairy products and meat. The diet includes juices, water, snacks and a variety of desserts.

During the day, guests can visit restaurants (a total of 5 establishments - three of which are paid) and 6 bars located both on the territory of the complex and on the beach. In the evening, there is a disco bar where you can have fun and enjoy elite drinks.

Beach: A sandy, clean and well-kept stretch of coast is owned by Titanic Palace 5. It has beach equipment and water sports.

Additional information: The guests will enjoy the following entertainments: a billiard room, table tennis, slot machines, air hockey, bowling, playgrounds for outdoor games and a number of other sports. Business people will be able to conduct the necessary negotiations in comfortable and technically equipped conference rooms.



You can improve your health by visiting the SPA center, where experienced and highly qualified employees will carry out a range of cosmetic and wellness treatments. At your disposal will be a gym, disco, beauty salon. If you wish, for an additional cost you will have a relaxing massage.

Younger guests will be delighted with fun animations, water slides and a games room. For children there is a cinema and a development club. A separate menu, high chairs and a cot are provided for babies.

Digest: An excellent youth tourist center Titanic Palace 5, according to numerous reviews of vacationers, deserves the highest ratings and the best praise. At the height of service, quality food, multifaceted leisure and tastefully decorated rooms. The parents of the kids spoke well about the children's service.

The hotel is always clean and comfortable - the staff is always friendly and attentive to the requests of citizens. The undoubted pride is the Aquacenter, where you can splash, swim and somersault all day. An unobtrusive and interesting animation and entertainment program. A disco is open for young people until the early hours of the morning. If you want your vacation to be eventful, fun and memorable - feel free to go to Hurghada.
