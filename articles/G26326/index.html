The Fair House Beach Resort & Hotel (Koh Samui, Thailand): photos and descriptions, hotel infrastructure, services, reviews

On the tropical island of Koh Samui, among coconut groves on Chaweng Noi Beach, Fair House Beach Resort Hotel is an ideal place to relax. Koh Samui or Koh Samui is the third largest and second most famous island, after Phuket, located in the waters of the Gulf of Thailand. It is separated from the capital by 500 kilometers, but there are no direct flights to the resort.

How to get to the resort island

Fortunately, there are proven ways to get to the island and get to the Fair House Beach Resort Hotel. They differ in speed and cost, so everyone can find a convenient option.

The fastest option is Bangkok Airways flights, which deliver tourists to the island in bus mode. These flights are frequent, up to 12 times a day, and last only an hour and ten minutes. From the plane to the hotel, tourists are transported by fun trams or combi jeeps. Taxi services are also available. The downside is that the method is very expensive.

It will be less costly to reach the island with AirAsia. Airplanes fly from the small Don Muang Airport, where you can get by taxi, and fly to the cities adjacent to the island, from there you need to take a ferry to Samui. This method is half the price, and the situation can be further eased by ordering combined tickets for flights and ferries.

You can also get to the Fair House Beach Resort Hotel and by train, thus lowering costs, especially since trains also differ in terms of comfort. The first class is a coupe with air conditioning for two, the second without air conditioning, but, according to tourists, the most convenient and pleasant for the price. In the third grade there are only soft chairs. The trip takes 8 hours, it is better to take night flights in order to arrive at the ferry in the morning.

The most inconvenient but economical way to transfer from Bangkok to the island is by bus, which takes you to the first ferry in 11 hours. The ship will sail to the Cote d'Azur in an hour and a half.

This corner of the planet was created in order to relax, enjoy clean air, free from urban smog. The island is surrounded by numerous beaches, and even at the height of the season, you can find a secluded beach for unity with nature.

The clear waters of the Gulf of Thailand and the favorable climate attract tourists from all over the world.

Location

In addition to the pristine nature in the heart of Koh Samui, contrasting with the developed infrastructure on the coast, the chic Fair House Beach Resort Hotel 3 will make a comfortable stay.

It is located isolated from the bustle of civilization. Surrounded by strange plants and pleasant sounds of nature, the Fair House Beach Resort Hotel immediately captures the love of its guests. The picturesque view of the hotel is in perfect harmony with the general style of the island, and its excellent condition and beauty make it possible to enjoy your vacation and take pictures at unusual locations.

Fair House Beach Resort provides a wide range of services that make your stay more comfortable and enjoyable.

Hotel pools

The hotel has two swimming pools. The first is a beach pool with an outdoor jacuzzi. Fresh water in it is warmed by the rays of the sun, and the filter constantly keeps it clean. Along the perimeter of the pool there is an extensive sun terrace with sun loungers, on which guests have the opportunity to soak up the sun and get an even golden tan. The second pool is located on a hilltop with breathtaking views of the sea and the rocky waterfall. Visitors note the beauty and originality of this location.

Thai massage

The highlight of Thailand's relaxation program is Thai massage. The hotel provides professional Thai massage at the highest level. Sala Tai is a unique place, near the coast, where you can enjoy high-quality massage of the face, neck, shoulders and feet. The service is available daily from 9 am to 6 pm, and if desired, it can be obtained even in the room.

Private beach

The hotel has a private beach, with white sand, a gentle descent to the sea, comfortable sun loungers and large umbrellas. The beach is located on Chaweng Noi, where only hotel guests rest, so there is no crowd of tourists, which is an undoubted priority.

On the beach, guests sunbathe, swim, ride a banana or take diving lessons. In addition, motor and non-motorized sports are available on the shore. This makes the rest entertaining for all age categories.

Additional services and reviews of tourists

Guests at the hotel have the opportunity to use the services of laundry, a doctor on call, currency exchange, car and moped rental. There is also free parking on site.

In their reviews of the Fair House Beach Resort Hotel, guests note that it is convenient to move around the territory on a moped or tuk-tuk, as objects such as the sea, reception and bungalows can be distant from each other. In addition, the territory is vast, it is surrounded by greenery, birds chirp and frogs everywhere, lizards run and squirrels jump, and I want to look at all this constantly. Tourists note the high quality of service, the cordiality of the staff, excellent food.

Samui is a small island, but there is something to see. Near the hotel there is one very spectacular location where you can replenish your collection of photos. This is a large Buddha statue, decorated with all kinds of colors, as well as several temples. Among them there is even an Orthodox church. The hotel can book an excursion to Ang Thong National Park, where tourists ride on quad bikes and elephants.
