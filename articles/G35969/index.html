Hotel "Russia" (Nalchik) is one of the largest in the whole Kabardino-Balkarian Republic. It is located in the very center of the famous resort. The proximity of cultural and business facilities is an additional convenience that the Rossiya Hotel provides its guests. The city of Nalchik is always happy to welcome guests.





Nalchik

It is a large Russian city, the capital of the Republic of Kabardino-Balkaria, located at the foot of the Caucasus Mountains (400 = 600 m above sea level).

This is a large balneological and mountain-climatic resort. The recreation area covers approximately 1/4 of its territory. In Nalchik there are up to 40 sanatoriums, boarding houses, rest houses and camp sites. In addition to mud and mineral springs, guests are offered an amusement park, artificial lakes (4), a park, a cable car, a hippodrome and a zoo. The presence of picturesque mountains around the city determines the interest of vacationers in mountaineering and horseback riding. The routes to V. Balkaria, to the Blue Lakes, to thermal springs, to the Valley of narzans, to the Chegem Gorge are especially popular. Nearby is Mount Elbrus (the highest peak in Europe) with the Elbrusier National Park.

Reviews

Residents call the hotel "Russia" greetings from the Soviet past, which they define as dull, modest, almost ascetic. Guests note that repairs are made only in the lobby on the 1st floor. It is usually shown on advertising pictures. The hotel rooms are different, according to the reviewers. On the floors there is a shower room with a sign: "keys on duty", designed for those living in standard rooms. Guests note that there are so-called "improved" standard rooms with a bathroom, which do not have both water and a hairdryer with toiletries.

The cost of such a home is about 3000 rubles. A hairdryer, which you can ask the duty officer on the floor (50 rubles for half an hour), is not included in the bill, a check is not issued. Suites (for 4000 rubles), according to residents, also do not differ in special comfort. Separate residents value breakfast. The complex breakfast includes: sausage, buckwheat, bananas, a piece of bread, some butter and cheese, tea, which are issued after presentation of the coupon - a piece of postcard received by guests upon arrival. If you happen to forget the coupon, you will have to go back and present. Otherwise, pay in cash.
