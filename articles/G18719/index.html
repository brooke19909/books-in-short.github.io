General Rules to Know

After reading this article, you can familiarize yourself with what Podushkin offers you. The hotel, the photos of the rooms of which are presented here in a large assortment, is actually very peculiar, vibrant and interesting. However, before booking a room, it is better to familiarize yourself with the rules that the administration makes. First of all, do not forget to book rooms in advance, because there are not as many as there are people who want to have a good time. By the way, reservations are made by phone. Services are provided only to adults, while the number of guests in economy rooms is no more than two, in the rest you can adjust this at your own discretion. Regardless of the number of guests, payment remains fixed. It is necessary to confirm the reservation three hours before check-in, now you will need to provide your passport or driver‚Äôs license. Immediately upon arrival, the guest pays for all the time that he plans to stay. At the end of it, another 15 minutes are given for the fees, after which the administrator will ask you to pay or leave the room. However, if guests decide to leave the room earlier, payment for unspent time will not be refunded. Do not forget about the important point: check-in is considered complete when the guest who issued the room leaves the hotel, even if other people stayed in the room. In this case, one of the remaining must re-arrange it for himself.
