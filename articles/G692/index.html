Egypt, "Hilton Taba Resort and Nelson Village 5 *": reviews, photos

Many tourists choose Egypt as their vacation destination. Hilton Taba Resort is a luxurious hotel complex that offers its guests a full range of services, decent living conditions and excellent service. Naturally, people planning their holidays are interested in more information. Where is this place located? What level of service can you expect? Is the hotel suitable for a child? How do tourists respond about it? The answers to these questions will be interesting and useful to many readers.

Hotel Location Information





For many people who choose Egypt as their vacation destination, the Hilton Taba Resort has become a permanent stopover. In fact, the hotel has a lot of advantages, including its location.

A brief description of the infrastructure of the complex

The hotel complex "Hilton Resort Taba" 5 * is a luxury resort with a large territory, in the center of which there is a ten-story building. Part of the area is reserved for swimming pools, relaxation terraces and various sports grounds. Here you will find exactly cropped lawns, bright flower beds, small gardens of tall palm trees and other exotic plants.

By the way, the hotel is part of the world famous Hilton Hotels & Resorts chain. It was built in 2000, and reconstruction and cosmetic repairs are carried out regularly - the hotel fully complies with all international requirements and standards.

How many rooms are there in the hotel?

The hotel complex "Hilton Taba Resort" can be considered relatively large, because on its territory there are 400 rooms of different categories:

  • Guest Rooms (there are single and double, as well as spacious family rooms);
  • Deluxe Rooms - rooms with comfortable furnishings, a high level of comfort and a spacious balcony with sea views;
  • Deluxe Room Plus - these rooms, among other things, have a beautifully designed seating area, as well as a balcony with a view of the flowering garden or the sea;
  • Suites are spacious rooms that have a separate bedroom and a living room with an internal door.

Depending on your needs and wishes, administrators will certainly help you choose the most comfortable option.

Hotel complex "Hilton Taba Resort" (Egypt): photo and description of rooms





Naturally, for many tourists, the issue of the availability of certain amenities in the rooms is important. All rooms at the Hilton Taba Resort & Nelson Village are spacious and comfortably furnished, including beds, bedside tables, sofas and even large desks.

A set of household appliances is also present. On TV, guests can watch satellite channels, and use the air conditioner to create the most comfortable conditions in the room. You can also use the phone, although international and long distance calls are not included in the price. There is a safe for storing documents, as well as a minibar with a cooling system, although filling it with drinks is also paid separately. Wireless internet access is also paid.

The luxurious marble bathroom deserves special mention, where you can relax in the aromatic bathtub or quickly refresh yourself in the shower. There is a toilet, a washbasin, a large wall mirror, and a bidet. Guests are entitled to a set of clean towels, as well as hygiene products, which are replenished as they are used. Rooms are cleaned, as well as towels are changed every day, and bed linen is changed upon request.

Food scheme for vacationers





Like almost all hotels in Egypt, Hilton Taba Resort offers all-inclusive accommodation - guests are provided with full meals, as well as light snacks and drinks throughout the day. Egyptian-made alcohol is free - you have to pay extra for everything else.

For breakfast, lunch and dinner, tourists are invited to the Palm Court main restaurant. Here, each visitor will be able to choose dishes to their liking. They mainly offer food of the usual international cuisine, although themed evenings are held almost every evening, devoted to the traditional dishes of a particular country. There is a diet menu, as well as a large selection of dishes for vegetarians.

In the restaurant Marhaba you can taste the culinary masterpieces of the East and Egyptian cuisine in particular. Pirates Bar offers light snacks and freshly squeezed juices. You can have a good time at Nelson Village Pub and Rainbow Lounge Bar. Until two in the morning there is a disco bar where you can enjoy drinks and dance to beautiful music.

Where is the beach?





It's no secret that the beautiful sea and clean beaches - this is exactly what tourists are waiting for, choosing to relax in Egypt. The Hilton Taba Resort hotel is located almost on the seashore - the hotel territory opens to it.

The entrance to the sea is quite convenient, but since the bottom is covered with pebbles and coral reefs are located nearby, you should take care of the availability of special shoes. The sea is mostly calm and warm. There are plenty of sun loungers and umbrellas on the shore - they are intended exclusively for hotel guests, as the beach is private and therefore closed to outsiders. Nearby there is a small bar where you can get drinks and even light snacks.

Water and beach activities for travelers





Every year, lovers of fun water activities go to Egypt. Hilton Taba Resort is a hotel that offers a huge range of various entertainment services. In particular, on the beach you can play volleyball, rent a canoe, ride a catamaran, canoe or water ski. It is also possible to rent a boat and go fishing, which can also be an exciting experience. You can also try sailing and parasailing or just go snorkelling while watching the vibrant colors of the underwater world.

The hotel has a windsurfing school where you can rent a suit, choose a board, or take a short course. By the way, there is a diving center, where, again, you can rent equipment or ask for help from professionals - they will tell you about the basic rules and safety measures for diving and even swim with you.

Are there any conditions for children?

Since many tourists prefer to travel with their children, an important issue is the availability of certain amenities. For example, an extra bed will be delivered to your room, and in the restaurant you can ask for a high highchair. By the way, there is a good children's menu.





In terms of entertainment, the hotel also stands out. There is a special pool for children on site, in which children can have fun and get to know other children. There is also a well-equipped playground with swings and slides. Most of the guys, as a rule, spending days in the territory of the local mini-club, where not only animators, but also teachers work with them. Children learn art, learn dances, prepare theatrical performances and participate in team games. If necessary, you can leave the baby for several hours with a nanny.

Additional services

Of course, there are some other services at the hotel that will save you from small household problems. There is a covered parking. A currency exchange is constantly working, as well as an ATM, which greatly facilitates the situation with money. And you can rent a safe from the administrator, or use the storage camera.

The hotel has a small conference room, as well as a ballroom for 550 people. These rooms are used both for seminars and business meetings, and for organizing various holidays.

Leisure and entertainment for tourists

If you want an active, eventful rest, then this hotel is perfect for you. In the courtyard there is a complex of small pools, around which sun loungers and parasols are placed. Here you can swim, relax, sunbathe and enjoy cocktails from the bartenders.





The hotel also has tennis courts, basketball and volleyball courts. You can also play table tennis and billiards. There is a jacuzzi, steam room and hairdresser. A local attraction is the casino, which is always crowded. And you can take a few Arabic lessons or do oriental dances. During the day, guests are entertained by animators, and in the evenings, vivid performances are organized on site. In the evening, you can visit the disco.

Naturally, this is not all that the Hilton Taba Resort complex can offer. Egypt is a country that is simply designed for excursions. The hotel has a tour desk where you can buy an interesting tour for yourself. Guests have the opportunity to explore the entire Sinai Peninsula and visit Israel, which can be considered an undoubted advantage.

Egypt, Hilton Taba Resort: what do travelers say?

Of course, the opinion of tourists who have already managed to visit one place or another, sometimes carries much more information. What do people say about the Hilton Taba Resort? Reviews are mostly positive. Guests say that the area here is really clean, compact and well-equipped. The rooms are also spacious, although not all have a balcony.

The hotel offers really good food - a varied menu, every day replenished with interesting new dishes from the chef. By the way, other bars and restaurants on site also provide delicious food. And of course, a feature of the hotel is the presence of a legal casino, which is very popular among guests and just visitors.

An undoubted advantage is the proximity to the beach, which is within easy reach. The hotel has several shops, which is also convenient. Moreover, from here you can easily get to Israel on a tour.

As for the service, the staff here are polite, decent and helpful. Animators are funny guys who don't let you get bored. And almost all the staff speaks at least a little Russian, so there will be no problems with communication. The only drawbacks are the lack of noisy nightclubs and parties, as the area is calm and more suitable for family vacations.
