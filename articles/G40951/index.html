Kazakhstan. Climate and weather. Features of the climate of Kazakhstan

Kazakhstan is located in the central part of Eurasia. It is located simultaneously in Asia and in Europe. By its length, the country takes the second place among the CIS territories and the ninth in the world. Kazakhstan borders on several countries at once: Russia (in the north and west), Kyrgyzstan, Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan (in the south) and China (in the east).

The state is divided into 14 regions and 2 large centers. The largest cities are Astana and Almaty. Of particular importance is the city of Baikonur, which was rented by the Russian Federation until 2050.

General characteristics of the climate

Quite often the question is asked about the climate in Kazakhstan. It has very clear features of a sharply continental zone. In January, the temperature ranges from -19 to +1 degrees, depending on the location. In July, the average indicators are +17 ... +31 about . The summer period is accompanied by very dry weather; in some areas, temperatures can reach +50 degrees. Winter is practically non-snowy and quite cold - the maximum figure that has been recorded is -58 o .

Climate-forming factors and climate features of Kazakhstan allow us to grow more than 6 thousand plant species here. In this area, aquatic representatives of the flora are also able to exist, but there are fewer of them. Sivers apple tree is growing on the territory of the state, which is recognized as the ancestor of modern species of this fruit. It is mainly located only in Kazakhstan, which means that it is the local climate that is suitable for full-fledged life.

Seasons

Due to the fact that Kazakhstan, whose climate is continental, is characterized by hot summers and cold winters, it is easy to guess that dryness is the second name for the weather in this region. The state is famous for its long and large-scale sandstorms, which mainly occur in the north. The sky is rarely cloudy, both in the cold season and in the warm season.

Winter can be easily experienced due to the minimum rainfall. In the east, strong winds most often blow - the only minus of this period. The climate features of Kazakhstan are in the almost complete absence of snow, although the temperature can "please" too low marks. They can be explained by cold air masses. If snow falls, it does not differ in huge snowdrifts.

If the south winds came from Iran, it means that you can meet spring. As a rule, they are delayed only in the southern part of Kazakhstan. In May and June, moving air flows are rare. In July, a pernicious dry wind is characteristic, destroying huge stocks of rural products.

Autumn is quite short, because already in November snow can fall, and the temperature drops to -10 o .



Climate-forming factors and climate features of Kazakhstan

The climate in Kazakhstan depends on several factors:

  • Atmospheric circulation. Oceanic air masses move too quickly to the territory of the state (in just 3 days).
  • Solar radiation. It is so large that it exceeds 2 thousand hours. Moreover, this figure is much higher than that shown by Moscow, being with Kazakhstan on the same latitude. This is due to reduced cloud cover.

Other VMs, other than those coming from the ocean, also affect the climate. Wet, moderate masses bring thaw in spring and cold in winter. And clouds can cause precipitation. Arctic VMs contribute to severe deterioration of climatic conditions. And the tropical masses resume the regime of sultry summer without a drop of rain.
