Rating of embedded washing machines by quality and reliability

Built-in appliances have long ceased to be some kind of wonder. Now in any kitchen you can find washing machines hidden in the cabinets, dishwashers and other equipment. But let's talk specifically about washing machines. They are ideal for the role of embedded technology. And it is precisely them that are usually produced in this form. But before we move on to the rating of built-in washing machines, we need to consider how they differ from conventional washing machines. And the differences are actually a lot. It's just that not everyone knows about them.

The main differences between the built-in washing machines

With ordinary washers, everything is clear. Many people know about them. But embedded machines must have a number of features that are absolutely necessary for them to function properly. In general, it is precisely such differences that characterize the type of washing machines. Now we will consider those options that a priori should be present in the embedded technique of such a plan.

  • Leakage protection. This option is required for the built-in washing machine. If an ordinary washing machine can do without it, since it is usually installed in the bathroom, the built-in one will not do. Since the kitchen, furniture, etc. will be flooded. Almost all equipment of this type has protection. Even partial.
  • Curtains for doors. Any built-in machine must have curtains on its body for installing cabinet doors. As a rule, curtains are installed on both sides. Just then the user removes the excess. Some cars do without curtains. They have a slightly different design, and doors are installed differently.
  • Vibration Reduction Option. When the centrifuge is working, any machine begins to vibrate violently. But in the case of built-in appliances, this can be dangerous, as furniture can fall apart. Therefore, this technique is equipped with a vibration reduction function. Not to say that every machine was equipped with it, but such a chip is available.
  • Only horizontal loading. This is clear. Do not place top-loading machines in kitchen furniture. In this case, the porthole of the machine should not be very convex, since it will interfere with the doors of the cabinet closing.

All of the above - the main features of built-in washing machines with horizontal loading. If these devices do not meet these requirements, then it is highly likely that the equipment will quickly become worthless or ruin the repair in the apartment. So it‚Äôs definitely not possible to build an ordinary car in the kitchen.

How to choose the built-in machine?

In general, this is not so easy. And if at hand there is a specialist in this field that you fully trust, then it is better to use his services. And if not, then you need to consider the following features, which in no case should be overlooked.

  • Dimensions of the machine. If you buy a car that is larger or smaller than your kitchen cabinet, then there may be problems with its placement. In general, you first need to buy a car and only then make a stand under it. But pure attention should be paid to the depth. It will not be good if the machine sticks out and spoils the view.
  • The presence of leakage protection. This has already been said. If the machine is not sufficiently protected, then the likelihood of a very unpleasant situation in the future is high. It is better to be safe and get a really high-quality product than to spend money on a new car and on repairs (including neighbors).
  • Noise and vibration level. Built-in appliances should work quietly. Otherwise, being in the same room with her will be problematic. A strong vibration can damage furniture and everything else. Therefore, you need to pay special attention to these features. To avoid various unpleasant situations and discomfort.

All of the above will help you choose the right washing machine for installation. However, you must not forget about reliability. And now we will present the rating of embedded washing machines in terms of quality and reliability.
