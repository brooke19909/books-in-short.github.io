Ibiza - island of romance and delightful sunsets

Ibiza is an island of delightful sunsets, picturesque beaches, love and romance. This wonderful land in the Mediterranean Sea is spread out and is a real paradise for DJs, parties for world celebrities. Many hotels, restaurants, bars, discos and an amazing Mediterranean climate make it the most popular youth resort.

Climatic features

Ibiza is an island with excellent weather conditions and many sunny days a year. In these places, warm and short winters and pleasant, not sweltering hot summers. Throughout the island there is a temperate Mediterranean climate.

Ibiza is an island where the beach season lasts from May to October, exactly at this time the largest influx of tourists. The island is no less charming in late spring and early autumn, during this period you can come to enjoy the unique atmosphere of tranquility and natural beauty.

Ibiza Resorts

A diverse nature, all kinds of attractions and a high service service make it possible to spend an excellent vacation on the island. Here everyone can find a suitable place to relax, quiet, secluded beaches or noisy parties and walks until dawn.

Ibiza is an island with the cleanest beaches that stretch along the entire coastline, unique natural landscapes and a lot of entertainment for every taste. The capital is the city of Ibiza, it is one of the oldest cities in Europe, it is of great historical and architectural value. It is here that the world-famous Necropolis of Puig das Mulins and the amazing castle of Castell are located. One of the most popular resorts of the island is Playa den Bossa, it is located very close to the central metropolitan airport and is famous for the longest beaches on the island. Tourists can find a lot of entertainment in the outstanding beach clubs Bora-Bora and Space. The resort of Santa Eulalia is characterized by picturesqueness and tranquility, excellent conditions for relaxing with children are created here. As well as vacationers, there are many shops and shopping centers that will please with a wide range and fairly low prices. Ibiza is an island with charming pine forests, it is in such green spaces that the city of Es Cana is buried. In addition, tourists will enjoy the magnificent harbors of the city with colorful fishing boats. Es Cana is a favorite place for a hippie party. Throughout the territory there are beautiful resort cities, which the island of Ibiza is famous for and popular. Photos taken in these exotic places will long remind you of an amazing vacation. The small cozy village of San Miguel is located on the north coast, couples come here to enjoy privacy, silence and romantic landscapes.

Another charming resort in Ibiza can be called the city of Portinach, which is famous for its calm atmosphere and magnificent landscapes of pristine nature. Incredibly beautiful and picturesque Balearic Islands, Ibiza delight with its beaches and rocky peaks, blue bays and charming pine forests.
