Loan to pensioners at VTB 24: conditions, interest

Despite the fact that older people are extremely neat and accurate borrowers, banks are in no hurry to give out loans to pensioners. VTB 24, for example, does not have special programs for pensioners, but it is possible to take a loan under one of the standard programs. A pensioner should not expect a large amount, the maximum loan amount is likely to be 100 thousand rubles. Such restrictions are primarily associated with low monthly income.





Loan conditions

A loan for retirees at VTB 24 can be obtained only in national currency, that is, Russian rubles. The maximum installment plan will be 5 years, and the annual interest rate will vary from 15 to 27%. If the future client receives a minimum pension, this cannot be the reason for the refusal to receive a loan at VTB 24. Installment conditions will nevertheless differ depending on the income of the citizen.

Borrower Requirements

A loan to pensioners at VTB Bank is available with a proven monthly income of 20 thousand rubles, if the case concerns a resident of a region of the Russian Federation. Citizens living in the Moscow region will need to show a monthly income of at least 30 thousand rubles.

An integral requirement is a positive credit history. By the way, the lack of credit history is not a negative factor for banks. Rather, it is neutral information about the creditworthiness of the client. Definitely, only a negative credit history significantly reduces the borrower's creditworthiness.

To get a loan at VTB 24, the terms of the contract require mandatory life and health insurance of the borrower.

How to lower the interest rate

Citizens who are classified as non-working pensioners can get a loan at a lower interest rate if they attract guarantors or make collateral. Guarantors can be any individuals with a permanent place of registration who work and can prove the level of income with a certificate in the form of 2-personal income tax in the amount of at least 20 thousand rubles per month after tax.





Real estate, for example, an apartment owned by a borrower, can act as security. So, getting a loan for pensioners at VTB 24 will be much easier, the amount can be increased, and the interest rate reduced.

Package of documents for obtaining a loan

To apply for a loan will have to collect a minimum set of documents:

  • loan application form;
  • pensioner's ID;
  • Russian passport with a permanent place of registration.

For non-working pensioners who have additional income, you can submit a certificate in the form of 3-personal income tax, and for workers - 2-personal income tax or in the form of an employer. Retirees who continue to work can also make a copy of the work book certified by the employer.





It does not hurt to take a certificate of the amount of the assigned pension or make an extract on the movement of funds on a bank card that is tied to a pension account. Typically, such a certificate is required if the amount of the desired loan is higher than 500 thousand rubles. It should be noted that the certificate should reflect the amount of income for the past six months.

Online application

To apply for a loan to pensioners, VTB Bank offers to visit the nearest bank branch or fill out an application online. On the site you can fill out a simple form that contains the following data:

  • complete F. I. O .;
  • Contact details;
  • desired loan amount;
  • loan terms.

Additionally, the future borrower will be asked to enter passport data, indicate the address of registration and, if available, information about the workplace. So, a working pensioner must indicate the form of employment: full-time work, fixed-term contract, private practice or individual entrepreneurship. Refinancing

If you have one or more loans, the bank may offer a refinancing service. With the help of such a program, you can pay off debt at other banks. In simple terms, if there is a loan in Bank A, then you can draw up a refinancing program in Bank B and completely repay the loan in the first bank. Each client has different reasons for processing refinancing. Some borrowers, for example, combine several loans from different banks into one.

By the way, re-issuing a loan under the refinancing program will help to improve the credit history, since the client, in fact, fully repays the initial loan. Also for this purpose, you can take several small loans and, observing all the requirements, repay them on time.





At VTB 24 Bank, a refinancing service can be issued at 13.5-17% per annum. The minimum interest rate is available upon execution of a refinancing agreement in the amount of 600 thousand rubles. The maximum refund period is 5 years.

It does not matter what is executed: refinancing at VTB 24 or a loan - conditions are provided to pensioners on an equal footing with other borrowers.

Mortgage credit lending

There are many reasons why mortgage lending for pensioners in the Russian Federation is practically unavailable. A relatively low income of an elderly person cannot cover high mortgage payments, even if one considers that a pensioner has additional sources of income.





Obviously, the health of older people is not perfect and when issuing a long-term loan such as a mortgage, banks are not insured against the illness or death of the borrower.

You should not consider an employed pensioner as a potential mortgage borrower, as the bank understands the high risk of losing a job by such a client.

Conclusion

Age is not a reason for refusing a loan. Banks often issue loans to pensioners. A non-working pensioner can get a loan in the same way as a continuing one. VTB 24 offers several cash loans, without dividing customers by age. Although there are no preferential conditions for pensioners, the situation can be improved by attracting guarantors or making collateral for a loan. VTB gives a loan to pensioners for a period of six months to five years, and the amount varies from 50 thousand rubles to several million.
