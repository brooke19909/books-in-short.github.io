Comfortable hotels in Kabardinka

In the Krasnodar Territory, in the resort town of Gelendzhik, there is a picturesque village called Kabardinka. By climatic conditions, it resembles the sunny Crimea, there are no fogs, the sun shines year-round, and the sea - like fresh milk. Due to the abundance of mountainous hills and forests, the village is lightly windy and warm.





The holiday season is open until winter. Hotels in Kabardinka are conveniently located in relation to the coast and infrastructure. Among the huge abundance of housing, you can find both an economical option and an average level at an optimal cost. Each hotel has its own flowering garden.

On fertile lands are broken vineyards and beautiful trees with shrubs. The unique nature and favorable climate have a healing effect on humans, which is why rest and hotels in Kabardinka are so "crazy" popular.

All year round the weather in the village is comfortable. In summer, the temperature reaches +40, in winter it does not fall below +7. The sultry heat is easily tolerated here, thanks to the sea breezes and humidity. It should be noted that the beaches in the resort area are very clean, well-maintained, and the descent into the sea is comfortable and safe.





The water level in the sea rises rather slowly, which is why Kabardinka is a great place to relax with children. But not only is the village famous for its warm sea and clean mountain air, it also has many medical sanatoriums and health centers.

Today Kabardinka is a resort area on the Black Sea, where you can cure almost any disease, including in children. Everything else in the resort offer affordable prices for treatment and accommodation. Most tourists recommend medical resorts and hotels in Kabardinka.





One of the largest and most prestigious hotels is Nadezhda. This is an elite SPA complex located 90 km from Anapa Airport and 20 km from the station. The medical center treats diseases of the nervous system, respiratory organs, and blood circulation. Also gynecological, digestive, endocrine, genitourinary, general therapeutic diseases. The medical profile includes pediatrics. For guests organized quality food and fun entertainment. At 100 m, the coastline is rubbed.

For more economical pastime there are private hotels. Kabardinka is not only a beach place, but also a recreational resort. The air here is saturated with plant flavonoids and iodine, therefore it is favorable for people suffering from bronchopulmonary diseases.





Pleasant walks along the pine forest and juniper groves will relieve colds. The atmosphere of calm, silence attracts Kabardinka. Hotels (prices, more precisely) are available for any wallet. For lovers of ecotourism - diverse natural resources with relict pines. For an additional fee you can catch a fish - the catch will be royal.

And accustomed to comfortable conditions, we offer to visit the private hotel Veronika. This is a modern guest house with a variety of entertainment and spacious rooms. For discerning tourists, suites have their own kitchen area. The hotel has a swimming pool, steam room, gym.

Optionally, you can choose two or three meals a day. The cost of living is moderate, and the service is at a high level. All hotels in Kabardinka offer a wide range of leisure activities and are located near the sea. A measured, relaxing or active and memorable vacation is guaranteed.
