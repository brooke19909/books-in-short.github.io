Plumeria Resort Pattaya 4 (Thailand / Pattaya): photos and reviews of tourists

If you want to have a great rest in a quiet place close to all the tourist infrastructure of Pattaya (Thailand), then the four-star Apart-hotel ‚ÄúPlumeria Resort‚Äù can be a good option for your stay.



Location, general description, photo

Plumeria Resort Pattaya is located north of the center of Pattaya. The famous Wong Amat Beach is 250 meters away. On the way to the shore, you will need to cross the roadway. Pattaya's nightlife center, Walking Street, is five kilometers from the hotel. You can get here by taxi or tuk-tuk. Bangkok Airport is 123 kilometers away.

This hotel was built in 2006. Its housing is represented by 106 studios and villas. All apartments have their own fully equipped kitchen, air conditioning, bathroom, cable TV, safe, refrigerator, furnished terrace or balcony. Wi-Fi is available for an extra charge.

Plumeria Resort has a restaurant and bar. The room rate includes breakfast only. However, for an additional fee, guests can have lunch or dinner in the hotel restaurant, tasting excellently prepared dishes of both national and international cuisines. The bar is located in the lobby of the hotel and offers everyone a wide selection of drinks and snacks.

Housing stock

As evidenced by the comments left by our compatriots, the rooms offered by the Plumeria Resort Pattaya (Pattaya, Thailand) almost all vacationers were very pleased with. So, according to the guests, the apartments here are spacious, bright, furnished in a traditional style. Furniture and appliances in the rooms are not new, but everything is in good, working condition. Tourists also especially note the large built-in wardrobe, in which you can fold not only things, but also suitcases with bags, and a safe that you can use for free. There is also a minibar in the room, in which the maid reports two or three bottles of drinking water every day during cleaning. As for the furniture, then in the apartments, according to our compatriots, more than enough. So, in addition to comfortable large beds, there are bedside tables, a chest of drawers, a pair of armchairs, a desk, a mirror. For an additional fee of 500 baht (if necessary), one more berth will be installed in your room. Pleased tourists and the presence of balconies in the apartment. They have a pair of comfortable chairs and a table. So here you can enjoy sitting while admiring the hotel's garden. Some travelers were pleasantly surprised by the availability of kitchens in the rooms. Moreover, they are equipped with everything necessary. So, here there is a stove, microwave, refrigerator, sink, range hood, cabinets and dishes, including pans, pots, plates, cups, etc.

As for the bathrooms in the rooms of the Plumeria Resort Pattaya, the reviews we are reviewing, tourists describe them only on the positive side. So, according to them, they have not only a shower cabin, as in most other four-star hotels, but also a separate bath. The room itself is quite spacious.

The only minus of the apartments of this hotel, some guests considered too noisy air conditioning. However, according to them, one can quickly get used to this or use earplugs before bedtime.



Cleaning, maintenance

As for this paragraph, on the whole, there were no particular complaints from the guests on this side. So, guests say that wet cleaning of the rooms was carried out every day. Bed sheets and towels were changed twice a week. The maids regularly replenished supplies of personal hygiene products (soap, toilet paper, shower gel, shampoo).

Due to the fact that both furniture and appliances in the apartments of Plumeria Resort are not new, sometimes something can fail. However, worrying about this, according to travelers who have been here, is not worth it. So, the problem should be immediately reported to the administrator at the reception. He will send you a wizard who will fix the malfunction as soon as possible.

Population

According to our compatriots, in most cases they just try to place 4 * guests who have just arrived at the Plumeria Resort Pattaya hotel in their rooms as soon as possible. In addition, administrators always take into account the wishes of tourists. For example, if you come with a big family or company, then you can ask to move you into neighboring rooms or apartments, interconnected by an internal door. If even at the moment this is not possible, you will be offered to relocate in a few days, when suitable rooms become available.

As for the day of departure, as a rule, the hotel administration does not require guests to leave the room before noon, as is the case in many other hotels. This is very convenient for those tourists whose departure is planned only in the evening.

Nutrition

The price of accommodation in Plumeria Resort Pattaya 4 * (Thailand, Pattaya) includes breakfast only. Most tourists were satisfied with the catering facilities. So, according to them, for breakfast you will be offered to choose a menu of your choice according to your taste. Basically, they cook eggs in various variations - boiled, omelettes with different fillings, scrambled eggs, etc. In addition, fresh vegetables and fruits, salad, jams, juices, pastries, tea and coffee are freely available in the restaurant. According to many travelers, such breakfasts were quite enough. During the day, guests can eat outside the hotel. In addition, since the rooms have kitchens, you can buy products in the market or in the supermarket and cook your own meals.



Sea

As our compatriots note, you can walk from the hotel to the beach in just five minutes with an unhurried step. Sunbeds and umbrellas here have to be rented for an additional fee. But you can always sit on a brought mat or towel directly on the sand. No one will force you to rent a deck chair. By the way, beach towels are issued at the hotel. You can exchange them every three days at the reception.

According to tourists, the sea here is quite clean. The beach is sandy, and its cleanliness is constantly monitored. As for entering the water, in some places at the bottom there are stones, but for the most part you can enter the sea without problems. So it‚Äôs quite possible to do without special shoes.



Entertainment

In general, according to tourists, this hotel is suitable for a relaxing pastime. So, here no one will offer you noisy entertainment. Guests of Plumeria Resort Pattaya 4 * can have a great time swimming in one of the two pools or sitting on a comfortable lounger on the sun terrace. Deck chairs here, according to our compatriots, in abundance. So at any time of the day you can comfortably sit on a sunbed. As for the pools, there are two of them in the hotel.
