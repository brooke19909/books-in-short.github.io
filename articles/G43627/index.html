Singing and dancing fountains in Dubai

The most vivid and unforgettable experience is given to tourists who visited the United Arab Emirates and saw the magnificent performance that the fountains in Dubai show. You can admire this grandiose show near the Dubai Mall supermarket, located opposite the tallest building in the world - Burj Khalifa. No one will remain indifferent to the singing and dancing fountains.



Musical show

The show starts at 6 pm and repeats every half hour until 23.00 on weekdays and until 23.30 on holidays. One performance lasts 3-5 minutes. Each show has its own highlight. They differ from each other in musical accompaniment, dynamics and character of the performance.

The specialists who create the show constantly come up with new compositions for dancing water. Dubai fountains are mostly white. This is done so that the audience is not distracted by the beautiful colors, but admire the game of water jets.

Unique attraction





The singing and dancing fountains in Dubai are among the highest and grandest in the world. They are impressive in size and have amazing sound and visual special effects. The fluctuation of jets of water, lighting and music create a bewitching and unforgettable sight.



The jet hits a height of 150 meters, which corresponds to the height of a fifty-story building, and draws a variety of shapes. At one time, the fountain lifts 83 thousand liters of water into the air. The singing fountains in Dubai are highlighted with 50 colored spotlights and 6 thousand light sources. Dance of water and play of light



The musical fountains in Dubai are a real extravaganza of light, sound and water. Water rises from the bottom, making a noise and the roar of a waterfall.
