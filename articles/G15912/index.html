Orient Palace 5 Hotel, Sousse, Tunisia. Booking, Prices, Photos.

Description: Hotel Orient Palace (Tunisia) stands on the seashore, two kilometers from the city of Sousse and 20 kilometers from the airport. Nearby is the port and pier with luxury yachts.



The hotel, built in 1989, occupies a four-story building and a small area with exotic vegetation and pools. The last repair was completed in 2011.

Orient Palace 5 is suitable for any tourists: youth companies, families, senior travelers, business people.

The small hotel has a relaxed, relaxed atmosphere. The hotel is distinguished by a modest simple atmosphere and a fairly low price for the 5-star category.

Rooms: Orient Palace 5 guests can choose a smoking or non-smoking room from 375 apartments of several categories: standard, family, junior suite, ambassador suite and presidential suite. Most of the rooms have sea views.

The rooms include linen change three times a week, daily cleaning, 24-hour room service.



The apartments have a spacious terrace, safe, TV, radio, air conditioning, cable TV, minibar. The bathroom has a shower or bath, sink, hairdryer, mirror, a set of accessories for washing.

Beach: Orient Palace 5 is located right on the sea line, on its own sandy beach. You can use umbrellas, mattresses, sunbeds, towels for free.

Meals: The hotel has several restaurants with European and Oriental cuisine. The poolside bar offers refreshments. Grill and barbecue are served outdoors. Vacationers are invited to the pizzeria and Moorish cafes.

The hotel provides all-inclusive and half-board services. The main restaurant with international and national dishes offers a self-service system. Once a week, guests have the opportunity to make an order in the Italian a la carte restaurant for free .



Information for vacationers: The Orient Palace Hotel took care of a carefree and comfortable stay for customers. On the territory there is a store, laundry, left-luggage office, beauty salon, first-aid post. Guests can rent a car, exchange currency, and book a photo shoot.

Entertainment and wellness services are especially widely represented. The hotel has an outdoor and indoor pool, tennis courts, pool tables, bike rental, a small golf course, a children's pool, a mini club, and a children's playground. For guests organized discos and evenings with live music.

A rich sports program is offered: beach volleyball, water games, aerobics, archery, minigolf, scuba diving, water skiing.

For a fee you can visit the gym, sauna, jacuzzi, massage room, Turkish bath.

Orient Palace 5 provides business services to business customers. They are provided with a conference room with equipment for 500 people and office equipment.

Reviews: Tourists note the convenience of rooms, including a lounge, a spacious room, a large terrace, a separate bathroom. The rooms are kept in perfect cleanliness, all equipment is in good condition, they show one Russian channel on TV.

The cleanliness of the sea and the beach is amazing, canopies and sunbeds are installed everywhere, which, at the request of tourists, will be moved to any place.

They offer a lot of delicious and varied food. There is a traditional European food and a wide selection of seafood dishes: fried fish, octopus, shellfish. As usual - lots of fresh, delicious pastries and desserts.

Particularly pleased with the low price with a decent standard of accommodation and service.
