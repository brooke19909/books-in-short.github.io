Manual sewing machine for the home - which one to choose?

First of all, decide for what purposes you need a manual sewing machine at home. How often will you use it? If from time to time, as a hobby, then you can find a used model.

Browse through local newspapers, browse online bulletin boards, and call repair and service technicians.

Hand-held mini-sewing machine hem all types of fabrics, used for minor repairs. Consider her as an option.

Want to get to the master level? Then it‚Äôs worth buying a new electromechanical machine with guarantees from dealers in stores. The price range in this case starts from 100 Euro and higher.

The most expensive are specialized models. Basically, they are bought for ateliers, workshops, for mass production over a long period of time.

The following brands are in the ranking of the best manufacturers: Singer, Baby Lock, Janome, Bernina, Elna, Sears-Kenmore, Pfaff, Husquavarna.

Pay attention to the type of shuttle, power, adjustment, number of options, and for what types of fabrics the machine is intended. Think about spare parts. It is important to quickly and easily find the necessary parts and replace them in the event of a breakdown. The quality of the materials of which the manual sewing machine itself is made is also important. Think about whether all the stitches and functions are useful to you. Their number also affects the cost.

On a note



The machine with a horizontal shuttle has an easy threading, more options, a low percentage of wear of parts, a quiet sound during operation, a clear, even line.

For patchwork, patchwork it is better to give preference to a machine with automatic thread trimming.

For sewing with thin, light, sliding fabrics, the upper conveyor is convenient.

Today, microprocessor-controlled electronic sewing machines are becoming popular. They provide many operations: a simple press of a button - and the settings you set instantly switch. Tips for choosing a needle, seam, line length, information about the causes of the malfunction are displayed on the LCD. The thread tension itself is also adjustable. The machine embroiders ornaments, alphabet, numbers, performs a movement back and forth and in different directions. Explore a puncture stabilizer model that takes the thickest fabrics.



The dream of an experienced master is a computer-controlled sewing and embroidery machine: she knows how to sew everything. Some advantages when working with it: powerful functionality, a huge selection of patterns for embroidery. Price starts from 800 Euro.

Remember! The ability to sew beautifully, easily, with enthusiasm depends on a quality machine. Use these recommendations, tips from this article, read the instructions and descriptions of your favorite models. And make the final purchase decision when you try it in practice. Together with the manager, make the main types of seams, learn to correctly switch all functions, do not hesitate to ask questions. With proper use and proper care, your manual sewing machine will last much longer.
