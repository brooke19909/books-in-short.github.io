Do-it-yourself Tangram: a fun and useful activity

Tangram is a Chinese puzzle that was created more than 4000 years ago. The simple manufacture of tangram makes this puzzle even more popular. The puzzle consists of 7 parts: 3 triangles (2 large, 1 medium), square and parallelogram, which as a whole form one large square. If you add up all 7 fragments of tangram, you can get a wide variety of figures, the outlines of which resemble various household items, structures, animals, and even people.

Puzzle Benefits

But above all, tangram is a fascinating tool for the development of the logical thinking of a person, especially a child. A do-it-yourself tangram is one of the best pastimes of parents with their children. The puzzle will help the child acquire the skills of analyzing images, highlighting geometric shapes, breaking an object into parts, and composing parts of the whole.

Instruction manual

The process of creating tangram is simple. The cost of this puzzle is low. Do-it-yourself tangram is often made of white or color cardboard. For example, you can use cardboard from an office folder, boxes from under any household appliances, colored cardboard for students. A more practical and durable version can be made of plastic. On any of the proposed surfaces, it is necessary to apply a template and cut. Cutting out the elements according to the template, you get a ready tangram with which you can start playing. Together with the child, you can choose from a variety of samples an outline that is interesting to you and put together a figure that should consist of all seven parts of the tangram that do not overlap each other. By the same principle, you can invent your own figures. For the smallest, they draw patterns on paper and assemble the elements like a mosaic.
