Coffee maker Scarlett SC-037: description, specifications, customer reviews

How do you imagine your perfect morning? Where does it start? Surely many of you will answer: "Of course, with a cup of coffee." But in the morning we are always in a hurry somewhere, and there is very little time left for coffee, how to prepare your favorite drink quickly, and - what is also important - delicious? A coffee maker comes to the rescue. To determine the choice of a new kitchen assistant, be sure to study the characteristics and functions of a particular appliance. In the article, we consider the advantages of the Scarlett SC-037 coffee maker, and also analyze customer reviews.

Description

What do you think is important for a good coffee machine? Appearance, functionality, build quality or cooking speed? Focusing on your preferences, you can consider many models of coffee makers of different price categories. Why is the Scarlett SC-037 the best option for the money? Let's get it right. The presented model has an affordable price, compact dimensions, stylish design and even the function of making cappuccino. Ease of use will conquer any coffee lover. A huge advantage is the ability to prepare a drink for several people. But some consider this a minus, because you will need to pour water into the flask every day.

Pros and cons

It is worth mentioning separately the cooking speed, Scarlett SC-037 is a very quick assistant, you can get distracted for a while, for example, spend a morning toilet, and start enjoying aromatic coffee or cappuccino. Since the price of the device is low, you should not expect too much from it. The Scarlett SC-037 coffee maker does not have a water level indicator, but do not take this as an obvious minus, because the manufacturer assures of comfortable home use of the device, it will not work for serving the flow of people.

Specifications

The compact, convenient and easy-to-use coffee machine Scarlett SC-037 is capable of creating a vapor pressure of 4.3 bar, which is quite enough for home use. The power of the device is 800 watts. The size of the flask for water is not too large, but you can cook about 3-4 cups of your favorite aromatic drink. The operating mode is adjustable in two positions: coffee and cappuccino. The model is very easy to operate, does not require special care. The coffee maker has a special drip tray. Any housewife will appreciate the new kitchen assistant, not only for its functionality, but also for its modern design.

Reviews

Scarlett SC-037 is the perfect coffee machine, and it won the hearts of many buyers with its price, design and the ability to make cappuccino. Most of the reviews are extremely positive, every second consumer praises the coffee maker for the speed of preparation of drinks and the infinitely long operation of the filter. One of the most frequent negative reviews is that the water flask is too small.

Many customers claim that after a while using this coffee maker, the taste of coffee worsens and the brewing function is intermittent. It turns out that bolted connections quickly weaken, and water trickles through the joints, and pressure does not form. You just need to tighten the bolts, perhaps during transportation the assembly of the coffee maker was slightly damaged, and the fasteners were loosened.

For continuous operation of the cappuccino function, you need to monitor the cleanliness of the nozzle, which is located at the end of the tube. When milk is heated, it quickly clogs. But, as with any device, you need to monitor it and repair it in time to enjoy the taste of your favorite drink longer.
