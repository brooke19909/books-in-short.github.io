Learn to crochet a potholder - an original decoration for a kitchen interior

It is impossible to do without kitchen gloves in the kitchen.

This thing necessary in the household can also play the role of a decorative element and decorate the interior of the house. Learn how to crochet a tack with the most basic knowledge in this needlework. All you need is to be careful and be able to knit only chains of air loops and crochets. You can create a series of such tacks using different colors of yarn, the remains of which have already accumulated in the house over the years. In addition, your children and other household members will benefit greatly by helping you in this matter. For example, children can do part of the work by tying all tacks with threads of a single shade. Thanks to such cooperation, they will learn to work in a team, master the skills of artistic creativity, develop taste and a sense of harmony, and gain knowledge in needlework. But, most importantly, without which they can not do in life - they will learn to bring the matter to the end. Take time and spend leisure time with benefit - make crocheted potholders with children.

Schemes for creating an unusual decoration for the kitchen

You will need yarn in three colors (leftover threads are fine). Shades should combine well with each other and be in harmony with the interior of the kitchen. It is better to crochet a tack 3 mm thick. Now be careful and understand the principle of knitting from photographs.

  • Start knitting a tack: crochet a ring made of a thread of the first color. As shown in the illustration, you need to complete 18 single crochet.
  • To the resulting ring should be added 9 elements with 23 air loops in each. Tie in two rows of single crochet stitches. Pay attention to the fact that at the top of each ‚Äúpetal‚Äù you need to change the direction of knitting, so that later you get an imitation of a fold (a kind of ‚ÄúMobius loop‚Äù).
  • Continue with a second-color thread and also knit two rows of single crochet stitches.
  • Switch to yarn of the third color. After two rows of similar knitting, fasten the thread.
  • Spread the petals of your work and cover with a damp cloth to fix the position of all the elements. After the fabric has dried, continue to knit the tack.
  • Crochet around the perimeter with a thread of the first color, single crochet. Make two rows. Do not forget to create the basis for the loop at the top of one of the petals (16 air loops, which should also be tied with simple columns).
  • The decoration for the kitchen is ready.

Crocheted tack for beginner needlewomen is the best workout. Instead of just making patterns based on various patterns, the craftswoman does useful things for her family. It brings much more joy from his work.
