Dakotaraptor

The research team found the skeleton of a dinosaur, which was named dacotaraptor. The remains date from 66 million years ago, that is, the late Cretaceous period. These predators are known as small, fast and agile dinosaurs. They had stiff tails and sharp claws that helped in the hunt. Their growth ranged within 5 meters, the dakotaraptor was among the largest and most dangerous predators. There is no evidence of hunting in packs, the question remains open, and debate continues on it.

Studies have shown that some members of the family had feathers that at least partially covered the trunk. The plumage of large species remains a topic of discussion. Dakotaraptors do not have the so-called pen pen on the surface of the forearm. These elements indicate the place where feathers are attached to the bones of modern birds.

Paleontologists suggest that the presence of plumage took place, but it did not serve to fly, these species lost this ability during evolution. Prior to this finding, it was believed that the tyrannosaurus is the most formidable predator, however, the young Rex dacotaraptor may well compete and be a formidable adversary.

Dinosaur raptor: a description of the appearance

It was about 2 meters high, 6 meters long. Its skeletal structure resembles a modern turkey or chicken. The bones were hollow but strong. The head was rectangular in shape with powerful jaws with razor-sharp teeth. The utaraptor had long and relatively thin upper limbs ending with three fingers with claws, of which the middle was the longest. The long tail was used as a tool to maintain balance. The legs were short and robust, with four toes on each foot.

The first toe was never used anywhere, the second had a retractable claw that could grow up to 24 cm in length and was covered with a layer of keratin for protection. The third and fourth fingers were used for balance. Perhaps the dinosaur had binocular vision, like an eagle. His hearing was also excellent. Scientists have recently discovered that predators can hear low-frequency sounds better than herbivores. They are able to smell prey per kilometer. Feathers covered arms, legs, tail, and the rest of the body was under dense fluff.

Raptor Weapon

The largest raptor is a dinosaur, which, in addition to size, had many extremely dangerous weapons. His first weapon was the brain. Utahraptor was a true master strategist. His second weapon is the claws that he used to grab and tear his prey, as well as get the best piece of meat. The third combat weapon was his second finger with a deadly claw, which he used to stab his victim, falling into the jugular vein or spine, causing paralysis or instant death.

The sharp teeth in his jaw were weapons that were used to tear and eat prey. The decisive role in the hunting process was played by the tail, used for balance during the pursuit of prey. The predator's menu was not complicated, as a rule, they hunted for everything that was available to them. Due to the fact that their habitats were characterized by an arid climate, animals had to drink a large amount of fresh water.
