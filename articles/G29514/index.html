Lords Hotel Sharjah 4 * (Emirates, Sharjah): photos, prices and reviews

If you are faced with a choice where to go on vacation on your next vacation, then the best option would be the United Arab Emirates. This country is capable of not only giving each guest unforgettable days. She is the owner of the most modern infrastructure. It is here that the world's best beaches and hotels are located, among which - Lords Hotel Sharjah 4 *. In addition, there is the most favorable climate for recreation.



Holidays in the UAE

The UAE annually attracts thousands of tourists from all over the world. The main reason for this popularity is, above all, the sun shining throughout the year. Equally important is the fact that there is excellent service, the latest technological ideas, chic shopping complexes are embodied. Artificial Palm Islands are considered an illustration of achievements. No less impressive is the Parus Hotel. The man-made achievements of the United Arab Emirates can be complemented by magnificent sandy beaches. UAE also attracts shopping enthusiasts. The most visited resorts are Fujairah, Abu Dhabi, Dubai and Sharjah. UAE also attracts history buffs. Each of the seven emirates has something to see. The architectural structures are so magnificent and unique that it is impossible to unambiguously determine which emirate to visit in the first place. For lovers of extreme sensations, sightseeing trips to the vast expanses of the desert, to volcanic lakes and unusually beautiful oases are provided.



Gorgeous Sharjah

The resort city, famous for its abundance of unique museums, theaters, galleries, which rightfully bears the title of "cultural capital of the UAE" - Sharjah. Rest here will allow you to get a lot of vivid and unforgettable impressions, see a lot of interesting and new. Sharjah is the only emirate located on the border of the Gulf of Oman and the Persian Gulf and borders on such a popular resort as Dubai. It has many attractions, including the commercial markets of Al Manar and Sharjah, built in the Islamic style. And so that not only the residents of the city, but also its guests have the opportunity to hide from the scorching sun, there are many parks, the most popular of which is considered to be Al-Jazeera, where many different attractions are collected - both for adults and for children. For lovers of intellectual entertainment in Sharjah, many museums are open. Attracts vacationers and the Al-Buheira embankment. General description of the hotel

Among all the hotels that are located in the city of Sharjah, Lords Hotel Sharjah 4 * is very popular. This magnificent hotel is the best suited for an economical and at the same time comfortable stay with the whole family, as well as for business visits to the city. A private sandy beach is 100 meters away.



The hotel opened in 2008. A five-story building is provided for accommodation. All of its rooms have a unique and elegant design. All rooms have modern and comfortable furniture.

It should be noted that the hotel got its current name in January 2012, and before that it was called Lords Beach.

Location

Hotel Lords (UAE, Sharjah) - the map clearly shows this - is located not on the coast of the bay, but directly in the city, on the second coastline. It is 14.9 km from Dubai International Airport. Sahara Center and Center Al Tawoon Mall are nearby. Free transfers are provided to the city's shopping and business centers. Nearby are many attractions worthy of attention, as well as a saltwater aquarium and numerous attractions.

Number of rooms

To your vacationers Lords Hotel Sharjah 4 * provides 100 rooms, among which almost everything is the Standard Room, with the exception of two Family Rooms. The maximum number of people in a room is three. One extra bed is possible. The windows of all apartments have access to one or another part of the city, but not to the coastal strip. Standard rooms have the appropriate technical equipment. Everywhere there is a TV set with satellite TV, among the broadcasted channels there must be one Russian-speaking one. Each room has its own air conditioning. A telephone is connected to each room, which allows you to order any additional service directly from your room. For an additional payment it will be possible to connect the Internet. To store valuables and documents, a safe is installed in the room (for a fee).



Standard rooms are equipped with a bathroom with a toilet and a washbasin. The hotel provides the opportunity to use terry bathrobes, slippers and bath towels. There is a necessary set of toiletries, and there is a hairdryer for drying hair.

There is a minibar, which is filled after advance payment. In addition, the room has a refrigerator and a set for making coffee or tea, as well as the necessary utensils. This allows you to prepare a light breakfast without leaving your room, making tea or coffee.

All rooms are tastefully furnished. The rooms have modern and comfortable furniture.



Nutrition

Unlike other world-famous four-star hotel complexes, the UAE hotels (Sharjah in particular), in addition to European dishes, offer treats of Arabic national cuisine. No exception and Lords Hotel. The following types of food are provided here : BB, HB, FB. Breakfast and lunch are served buffet style in the hotel restaurant. Dinner is ordered according to the menu. Upon reservation, breakfast can be served directly in the room.



Restaurants and bars at the hotel

Lords Hotel (Sharjah, UAE) has a spacious restaurant called Al Saraya. It is filled with a pleasant romantic atmosphere, has a beautiful national interior. The menu includes dishes of not only European, but also national cuisine. There is an elegant Harbor Lounge bar in the lobby, serving meat and seafood dishes prepared in a variety of ways. Hot and cold Arabian drinks can be sampled at Side Walk Coffe. Here you can have a snack with cold snacks.

Beach

The hotel does not have its own beach. But guests of Lords Beach Hotel Sharjah 4 * can easily take advantage of the city. Next door is an excellent sandy beach of the same name. It is considered one of the best in the city. In addition, it has an excellent location. It is easily accessible from both Sharjah Airport and Dubai Airport. It is superbly equipped and has a gentle entrance to the sea. On the outskirts of the city is the equally popular Khor Fakkan beach. Along the Coral Beach is also Al Cornish, which has a sand cover. Along the coast stretches a series of slender palm trees. It is always clean and there are never strong waves. And if you want to get to the beach, where you can ride a jet ski and water ski, then you should go to Al Khan Lagoon - it is located between Sharjah and Dubai. This place is especially popular with fans of active entertainment. It is impossible not to note the lagoon of Hamriyah, which is protected and extends from Ras al-Khaimu to the free territory of Hamriyah. Windsurfing enthusiasts gather here.



Issue price

The cost of the tour with accommodation in this hotel (for seven days with the type of food "breakfast") is $ 1102. If you buy a ticket for a similar time for two adults, but, in addition to breakfast, include dinner in it, the price will increase by $ 200. If an individual transfer is included in a similar tour, its cost will be from $ 2386.

Territory, infrastructure and services

On the territory of Lords Hotel Sharjah 4 * a small pool with temperature control is open. Parking is available. There is a gift shop where you can buy a small gift that reminds you of a wonderful vacation. For those who come for business purposes, there is a conference room with a capacity of up to 70 people. There is a business center, currency exchange. In public areas, extra charge - Wi-Fi. In addition, dry cleaning and laundry services, shoe shine, ironing.



A special room is reserved for luggage storage. Valuables and documents can be left in the safe installed at the reception. If necessary, you can use the services of a professional doctor. At the reception you can always buy fresh press, as well as sign up for a particular tour, the organization is done daily.

To maintain the beauty for guests, there is a fitness center. For an additional fee, you can take a steam bath in the sauna or visit the Turkish hammam, and then order a massage. An unforgettable experience guaranteed!

Sports and Entertainment

What else can Arab hotels please guests? The Emirates (Sharjah in particular) does not have as many hotel complexes as Dubai, but all of them offer their guests some kind of entertainment. Lords Hotel is no exception. Guests can play darts, table tennis. For an additional payment, guests can enjoy video games and billiards. For those who seek to keep themselves in shape even while relaxing, a gym is open.

Services for children

The management of Lords Beach Hotel Sharjah 4 * made sure that young guests here were also interested and comfortable. They have a children's pool and a mini club. The restaurant provides a children's menu during meals, while very young guests are invited to use highchairs. This room can accommodate a baby cot upon prior request. If adults want to enjoy a fully magnificent holiday, they can take advantage of babysitting services.

How to get to the hotel

You can get to the hotel from Sharjah Airport by hiring a taxi, the cost of which will be from 40 dirhams. You can also get to the hotel from the Emirate of Dubai, a minibus from which leaves every 30 minutes, the fare is 5-10 dirhams (40-80 rubles).

Guest reviews

And what do tourists say about their stay at the hotel we are considering? Firstly, all vacationers admire the beauty of the UAE. Sharjah (photos are presented in this article) cannot fully convey all the charm of this corner of the globe. If there is such an opportunity, be sure to visit the emirate, and you will be able to see for yourself the veracity of the reviews of those travelers who were lucky enough to relax here.



Many positive comments relate to the location of the hotel. Those who do not want to go to urban equipped beach areas can visit the wild free beach located near the hotel. It is also sandy, with a gentle entrance to the sea. Despite the fact that there is no entertainment and the corresponding equipment on its territory, you can perfectly sunbathe and swim here.

Many vacationers in their reviews note excellent Arabic cuisine. A plus is the presence of a diet menu. The attendants, who have knowledge of the Russian language, are also not neglected, which facilitates communication during the holidays. No complaints about the rooms. All are decorated in a contemporary style. The designer skillfully tried to make each room special. All rooms have superb furnishings.

Not without claims. So, not everyone likes the lack of their own beach, and the nearest recreation area is completely not equipped. Therefore, you have to sunbathe, sitting on a beach mat or bath towel. There are vacationers who complain of poor soundproofing in the rooms, which makes it difficult to fully relax in the evening, when all the guests gather in their rooms and discuss the day spent. Nevertheless, there are much more positive comments.
