Hotel Jardin Hotel Pratumnak 3 * (Thailand / Pattaya): photos and reviews of tourists

Jardin Hotel Pratumnak 3 * Resort is a great place for unpretentious travelers who want to get to know Thailand better and have a comfortable rest.





Short hotel description

Resort Jardin Hotel Pratumnak 3 * is located in the south of Pattaya, just a few steps from the popular public beach. So that you can be in touch with the whole world, you are absolutely free to use the wireless Internet. In addition, I would like to note the comfort in the rooms, which is achieved not only due to the pleasant and neat interior, but also due to the availability of a large number of amenities. All apartments have a balcony.

Jardin Hotel Pratumnak 3 *, taking care of the comfort of its guests, provides for a wide range of services, as well as infrastructure. At any time, reception staff will help you solve problems or answer questions you may have. You can also use the parking lot, make a relaxing massage or go on an exciting tour with a professional guide. Massage will be a pleasant and logical conclusion to an active tourist day.

In order for travelers to have enough strength and energy for new discoveries, they need good nutrition. At the Jardin Hotel Pratumnak 3 *, the restaurant is responsible for this. All tourists are recommended to at least once try exotic local dishes that you will not find anywhere else. If you are not inclined to culinary experiments, then you will be kindly provided with a European menu with your usual dishes.





Where is the hotel located

Resort Jardin Hotel Pratumnak 3 * (Pattaya) is located in the southern part of the city, about 50 meters from a large and well-kept public beach. In the vicinity of the hotel there are many shops, cafes and other outlets. To get to the center of Pattaya and walk along its busy streets, you will have to overcome about 3 km, and to admire the pier, you need to travel about 2 km. About an hour and a half takes the road to Bangkok. The same amount you need to get to the airport.

Hotel guest rooms

Hotel Jardin Hotel Pratumnak 3 * (Pattaya) is quite miniature, because it has only 18 rooms. Nevertheless, even such a small number of apartments has a certain gradation in the categories:

  • Most of the hotel has standard rooms, designed to accommodate two guests. The room area is quite large - almost 36 square meters. m. Such a space allows not only to place beds for the main guests, but also provide extra beds.
  • Family apartments are twice as large as standard. We can say that they are two full rooms, interconnected by an interior door. To ensure additional comfort, the apartment has two bathrooms, which greatly simplifies the accommodation of large families or companies.
  • The most expensive rooms in this hotel are deluxe. They are quite small, but at the same time they are distinguished by luxurious decoration and a wide range of amenities. So, the bathroom is equipped with a bathtub, and the space of the room itself is conditionally divided into a bedroom and a living area. There is also a small corner for the kitchen, where you can cook dinner for yourself and your loved ones.




Room Amenities

The Jardin Hotel Pratumnak 3 * provides a full list of amenities in the apartments that guests need for a comfortable stay in them. The following list can be distinguished:

  • in each of the rooms there is a TV, which is connected to a package of cable channels (there are also Russian-language programs);
  • a refrigerator is provided in the apartments so that you can store your products in it (this item is important enough if you do not want to spend money on food in cafes and restaurants);
  • there is access to your own small balcony, which provides drying for bath towels and linen, as well as plastic chairs, so you can sit in the fresh air;
  • if you pay an additional fee at registration, the minibar in your room will be filled daily;
  • the bathroom includes a hairdryer, cosmetics, and a set of towels.




Positive reviews

Many tourists, arriving in the resort of Pattaya, choose the resort hotel Jardin Hotel Pratumnak 3 * for themselves. Traveler reviews about this institution will help you make an objective picture, so that it is easier for you to make a choice. So, speaking about the positive aspects of relaxation in this hotel, it is worth paying attention to the following points:

  • it is pleasant that the hotel strives for continuous improvement - the rooms are constantly trying to update something and, due to their capabilities, make them more comfortable;
  • the restaurant has very tasty and high-quality food, which is in many ways superior to what the nearby catering establishments offer;
  • the hotel is always quiet and calm, so it is pleasant to spend evenings on a cozy terrace, admiring the night city landscapes;
  • good location of the hotel - in a fairly quiet area near the beach and a large market where you can buy groceries cheaply;
  • the hotel has a very good massage parlor, the masters of which own a variety of both traditional and exotic techniques.
