Learn how to make fragrant coffee toys with your own hands.

Coffee is a drink that is not just loved, but worshiped by most of humanity. Its aroma and unique taste make you wake up in the morning, cheer you up and energize you for the whole day. Coffee drink lovers have learned to use this product not only in cooking, but also in the craft industry. "How?" - you ask. They began to make coffee toys. How to make a coffee toy?

The process of making a coffee toy is very simple and interesting. Engaged in this type of needlework, you will get a lot of pleasure and a charge of positive emotions. And with coffee, it cannot be otherwise. So, let's get started.

We prepare the following materials and tools:

  • piece of cotton fabric;
  • a needle;
  • threads
  • filler (synthetic winterizer or folofiber);
  • acrylic paints;
  • PVA glue;
  • scissors;
  • a brush;
  • paper for making patterns;
  • sponge;
  • decorative ornaments (lace, ribbons, beads, sequins).

To make coffee toys fragrant, for their manufacture, of course, you will need your favorite type of coffee. As additional flavorings, you can use cinnamon or vanilla.



Making coffee souvenirs: step-by-step instructions

  1. Draw a full-size toy pattern on paper and cut it out. Choose simple figures in the execution (heart, flower, bear, horseshoe), the implementation of which will not require the manufacture of additional small parts.
  2. Transfer the pattern to the fabric, circle the contour. Fold the fabric in half and cut two identical parts along the marked line.
  3. Manually or on a sewing machine, sew both parts of the product together, leaving a small hole.
  4. Turn the workpiece and fill it with filler. Sew the hole.
  5. Dissolve coffee in warm boiled water. Add the desired flavor to it.
  6. Apply coffee liquid with a sponge to the toy. You can also sprinkle vanilla on top of the souvenir.
  7. Dry the product in a warm room for 12 hours. In order to avoid the appearance of stains, it is recommended to hang the toy.
  8. Dressing. Coffee toys can be decorated with acrylic painting, embroidery, and beads. From the tape, make a loop, for which a coffee souvenir can be hung in the place chosen for it.

So fast and easy you can make coffee toys with your own hands.
