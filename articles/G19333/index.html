Hotel Rose Hotel 3: beach holidays in Kemer

Hotel description: The relatively small but cozy hotel Rose Hotel 3 is located on one of the most colorful resort cities in Turkey called Kemer. This is a wonderful place very close to the Mediterranean coast.





Here guests are offered comfort and tranquility, as well as a lot of ways to have fun and have fun. It is thanks to the comfort and hospitable atmosphere that tourists come here every year from all over the world.

Rose Hotel Kemer was built not so long ago - the doors for tourists opened here in 2001. And already in 2011 there was a thorough reconstruction, after which the hotel became even more attractive. Description of rooms: As already mentioned, Rose Hotel 3 is not too big - 80 standard rooms work on its territory. All rooms are simply furnished. However, the interior is pleasant and comfortable. By the way, almost every room comes with a spacious balcony with a dining table and chairs - here you can enjoy the scenery and fresh sea air.

There is a private bathroom where guests can take a shower and relax in the hot tub. There is also a wash basin, towels and quality cosmetics.

Naturally, the rooms are equipped with all necessary equipment, including powerful air conditioning, TV, safe and telephone.

The rooms are cleaned daily, so the cleanliness is unlikely to have to complain. Linen on the bed is changed once a week or at the request of the tenant.

Meals : Rose Hotel 3 operates on an all-inclusive basis - guests are provided with full-fledged meals, consisting of traditional Turkish and international dishes. The main restaurant, where meals are served, is huge and has a pleasant, cozy atmosphere. There is an outdoor area for 180 people, as well as a spacious, marbled hall for the simultaneous reception of 250 guests. There are bars in the lobby and pool area with a huge selection of drinks and exotic cocktails.

Beach : Well-kept, pebble and sandy beach is located just 100 meters from the hotel territory. Here guests can experience all the charms of a beach holiday, from swimming and sunbathing to diving and motorized sports. There is simply no time left to be bored.





Additional services : Hotel Rose Hotel 3 provides tourists with everything necessary. To begin with, it is worth noting that a medical center, parking and car rental are constantly working here. There is also a hairdresser, spa and shop where you can buy souvenirs and essentials.

As already mentioned, Rose Hotel 3 has several indoor and outdoor pools where you can always socialize and have fun. There is a sauna, a bathhouse and massage rooms. Hotel guests can always play billiards or pass the time for the best board games.

Reviews: Hotel Rose Hotel 3 collects guest reviews mostly positive. In fact, this hotel is in great demand and has a good reputation among vacationers who prefer comfort and coziness. Clients pay attention to food - on the tables there is always a huge selection of delicious dishes, as well as fresh fruits. They note comfortable rooms and excellent service. This hotel can really give an unforgettable vacation.
