Family holidays - outdoor activities: an overview of parks and recreation centers, interesting directions for excursions, reviews

Few can boast that they like to relax actively. Most people prefer to lie idle on the beach or by the pool. However, in the modern world, outdoor activities are becoming increasingly popular. Family holidays in this case involve a lot of positive emotions from playing sports.

Today, there are network family parks for outdoor activities in which the whole family can relax. Classes here will find both kids with mothers, and teens with adults. A big plus of such places is that in one closed territory they contain a lot of entertainment. You can spend the whole day in such a park.

Joki joya

Joki Joya is one of the largest family vacation centers in the country. You can spend active time in the park both on weekends and on weekdays. The entertainment center has its branches in all major cities, and their addresses are presented on the official website. The Joki Joya Family Activity Center offers slides, mazes, bungees and dry pools to its guests. There is a zone for the smallest kids with bright entertainment.

Rope webs, cable cars - this is what awaits you in the center. For those who love hockey and football there are specially equipped fields. Therefore, you can gather large companies or come to play with families. Foam pit and trampoline complex like a lot of kids. They jump with great pleasure and have fun in them. For older children and adults there is an interesting attraction "Skalodrom". It is located next to the foam pit and the trampoline arena.

But air guns are not a hobby for children. Dad loves this attraction so much that in this section the children simply watch. A multi-level game labyrinth allows you to develop thinking and ingenuity. Children and adults can try to solve problems together, getting out of the maze. For a change, the complex has wooden and underground labyrinths. Slots "Air Hockey" and "Basketball" captivate children and adults of all ages. This type of entertainment may be suitable for a more passive rest after active games. You can relax and rejuvenate with benefit. Kids really like the Farm ride. This is an imitation of a courtyard in the village.

Children can find various animals here, as well as run on a green lawn. Nearby there is a "Volcano" and "Children's Pyramid." Joki Joya Park can organize children's parties and celebrations. Many children and parents will have a great and profitable time. This birthday will be remembered by all for a long time. You can order a separate room decorated with balloons or hand-painted. In order to capture these moments, you can call a professional photographer.

On your holiday, experts can come who will give aqua makeup to children and adults. Animators and a special show program will charge the whole company with positive emotions and good mood.

Prices at Joki Joya are reasonable. All over 18 years old admission is free. Children on weekdays can walk in the park for 500 rubles, and on weekends for 950 rubles. A ticket for 1 hour costs 300 rubles. For a holiday, groups of 8-11 people can rest for 500 rubles per hour per child, with the number of children from 12 to 15 people, the cost will remain the same. Groups must be at least 8 people.

Hlop-top

This is a large network of parks for outdoor activities. The uniqueness of Hlop-Top lies in the fact that all parks are different and made in an individual style. The park itself is a city or castle in which children can run, have fun, overcome mazes and climb rope ladders. Here you can celebrate birthdays or celebrate holidays. Animators and a show program will help to make any holiday unforgettable. Children's menu in the cafeteria - healthy and tasty food. Dishes in a children's style.

Gorilla park

This park is suitable for those who want to get a lot of emotions and feel a surge of adrenaline. Most rides are very fun and extreme. Both boys and girls really like downhill and laser-tang. Tarzanka and a game labyrinth will help to unite all family members and have fun.

Racing against time is a complex with slot machines, stylized as the interior of the future. Nearby is a large rope course, after which you feel like a real athlete. GorillaPark Family Fun Park offers ongoing discounts and promotions. For example, from the 15th day of the month you can get a discount on entering the park. Also, at some time there are discounts on air hockey games, and every Tuesday a discount on the ticket accompanying 50%.

Skiing

Skiing is not only good for health, but also very exciting. The whole family can usefully spend time. Ski resorts are currently popular.

A big plus is that the ski resorts are located outside the city. Fresh air away from city dust is a healthy vacation for the whole family. For the youngest athletes, it is imperative to take an instructor who will monitor safety during the descent. Turning to a travel company, you can plan your active family vacation (tours to ski resorts).

You can relax a couple of days, but you can stay for a longer time. There are different slopes on the slopes for adults and children, beginners and professionals. Equipment and necessary equipment will also be found for the whole family. After such an active holiday, you can visit the spa facilities. Restaurants or swimming pools, which are necessarily in the parks.

Swimming

Active family vacations with children can be advantageously spent in the water park. It will be fun for both adults and children. Slides and fun rides will energize and energize. You can relax and gain strength in the jacuzzi or the large pool. And the whole family will be able to eat in a cafe or restaurant, which is in every water park.

Bike

Do not forget about such popular transport as a bicycle. A walk in the park or in the country on two-wheeled vehicles can be pleasant and exciting. Of course, for the smallest - on a tricycle. Today, in order to safely transport children on a bicycle, you need to purchase a special chair with straps and a helmet for yourself and the child. Choose specially designated places for skiing - bike paths.

Sport games

All children like it when their parents play soccer, volleyball or basketball with them. Such an active family vacation unites children and adults very much. Harmony and understanding begin to reign in families. In the parks you will definitely find sites and the necessary equipment for such games. One has only to assemble a friendly team, and forward to adventure.
