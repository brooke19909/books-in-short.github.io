Hotel Villa Petrova 3 * (Budva, Montenegro): overview, description and reviews of tourists

Montenegrin Budva is located in the central part of the Adriatic coast of the country. This ancient town was built about 2.5 thousand years ago. Today, Budva is one of the most popular resorts among tourists around the world. Good reviews about this southern village appear not only due to convenient beaches and the warm sea. Many tourists praise him for the opportunity to rent inexpensive and at the same time quite comfortable accommodation. For example, pretty comfortable rooms are rented in Budva at the Hotel Villa Petrova 3 *.

How to get to the hotel

This comfortable three-star complex is located in one of the quietest areas of Budva. The distance to the beach from the Hotel Villa Petrova 3 * (Budva, Montenegro) is only about 400 meters. Opposite the hotel is a beautiful park. In order to get to this complex from the street, tourists need to go around the large Kangaroo complex.





The nearest airport to Budva is in Tivat. The distance between these two towns is only 10 km. You can overcome it by taxi or by public transport. The nearest bus stop is just 700 meters away. Some tourists go from the airport to the hotel on foot. But still, more often than not, vacationers who have booked rooms in Villa Petrova 3 * are delivered to their destination by the tour operator who sold the ticket.
