Expocenter (Moscow): Exhibition Schedule

Today, the most famous and visited venue for various exhibitions is Expocenter. Moscow offers its guests the best, most modern and comfortable venue for events. Today we want to briefly introduce you to the services provided by the exhibition center, as well as the schedule for the near future.



general description

In fact, Expocenter (Moscow) is far from a new platform. Not so long ago, this center celebrated its half-century anniversary. This is a considerable period for which a huge number of exhibitions of various scales have passed here. Despite the themes and social significance, they all have one thing in common: they were carried out at the highest professional and technical level. This is the initial task of the exhibition site, which Expocenter is doing very well. Moscow annually gathers many tourists, among which many businessmen. All of them, except for a pleasant pastime, have a goal - to visit large exhibitions, find new suppliers or wholesale buyers, advertise their goods or services. This exhibition center perfectly copes with these tasks.



What is a

Even the exterior of the building attracts business guests with its concise, but at the same time very presentable appearance. This is an ensemble of architectural and engineering structures that meet the most modern requirements. The development of this center has been going on systematically for the past 50 years. Today, it is Moscow that can offer the best conditions for holding any large-scale event. Expocentre Fairgrounds has nine pavilions with the most modern equipment for conferences and seminars. Here, at the same time, dozens of meetings of various kinds can take place. That is why the whole business elite wants to come here, such meetings provide an opportunity to acquire new connections.



Where is Expocenter located (Moscow)

And this is also an important factor. Do not worry about how your guests will get and settle down, only one for whom the success of the event does not matter. Especially if the meeting venue is the capital of Moscow. Expocentre Fairgrounds is located in the very center of the city, next to the business district. That is, within walking distance from the main highways of the city, hotels and the metro. Visitors and business partners can easily, and most importantly - quickly, get to the place and plan all day.



Guests Location

The metropolitan infrastructure will also take care of this. Especially pleasing is the fact that there is a huge number of hotels and hostels in the immediate vicinity, where you can rent an hour, relax from the road and put yourself in order. We will name only some of them that Moscow is especially famous for. The Expocenter is located almost right next to the Crown Plaza Hotel, Novotel, Na Krasnaya Presnya Hotel, WTC Moscow Apartments, Empire City, MosApart Apartments and others. As you can see, there is a choice, both in terms of cost and the number of services provided, the scope here is quite wide, you can choose for every taste and budget. The exhibition center has three entrances. These are North, South and West. Given the scale of the building, this is very important. Thus, it can be reached from almost any direction, so choose yourself. It is very important that guests are convenient to come by metro. In walking distance of the station "Exhibition" and "Business Center".



Exhibitions: Spring 2016

In fact, the activities here do not stop throughout the year. But in the spring, the most anticipated exhibitions are most often held, for which they are prepared especially carefully. Therefore, at this time, the Expocenter is especially popular among tourists. The exhibition (Moscow) "World of Skin" will be held within these walls in the very near future, from March 22 to 25. This is the 45th International Festival of Shoes and Leather Products. Subjects - finished products: shoes, leather goods and accessories, as well as finished fur products and shoe care products.

The 12th International Forum on Medical Information Technologies will be held on the same day. The subject of the exhibition will be computer systems for research and diagnostics, as well as various software: electronic case histories and outpatient cards and much more.



Travels

The very next day, March 23, you are invited by the twenty-third International Exhibition of the Tourism Industry. The topics are the National Tourism Office, the Tourism Information Center and the tourism industry association. That is, you can get all the information about where and how you can relax. And the choice is great, it is a beach holiday, sea and river cruises, wellness holidays, shopping and educational tours, as well as much more.

On the same day, an exhibition-fair "Street of Masters" is waiting for you. Bright and extraordinary exhibition is sure to please you. Here you can find interesting handmade souvenirs and jewelry, wooden toys, Russian sweets and handmade cosmetics.

Realtor activities

Already on March 24, an international exhibition dedicated to foreign real estate meets its guests. It is attended by agencies and dealer companies that operate in various countries of Western and Eastern Europe, as well as Central and East Asia. You can get full information about buying or selling, renting or renting houses and apartments, apartments.

Schedule for April

And again, welcome to the Expocenter (Moscow)! The schedule of exhibitions for April pleases with great variability. Here everyone will find what he likes. On April 12, the concept of Russian automobile dealers begins to work. Thus, the organizers set as their tasks the provision of a single platform for all participants in the automotive business to discuss current issues and exchange strategic information, to get acquainted with the products and services of car dealers suppliers.

Electronics Week

On April 13, the Russian Week of Electronics and Automation 2016 begins. This is always a very popular exhibition, as it offers guests a huge selection of electronic components and modules that are important for a wide range of industries. Subjects - various semiconductor devices, electromechanical components and connection technologies, software and hardware for the development of integrated circuits, electronic modules and much more.

On the same days, the exhibition center hosts a specialized exhibition of instruments and means for measuring and testing control, which will present a huge amount of various equipment that can be useful to you as part of your own production and business.

On April 16, the Country Life Festival begins to work. Here, a huge amount of various garden equipment for summer holidays will be presented. This barbecue and grills, barbecue and gazebos, tools and appliances for cooking in the fresh air. Within the framework of this exhibition, you can also find options for the supply of collapsible gazebos and garden furniture so that the coming summer is pleasant. As you can see, a huge number of business opportunities are presented to you by Moscow. The Expocenter, whose photo you can see, is a launching pad for a start-up business and real opportunities for an existing one.
