Hotel Amfora Hotel 3 * (Bulgaria / Sunny Beach): photos and reviews of tourists

Cozy and comfortable atmosphere for living and a good rest will be presented to its guests by Amfora Hotel . This three-star hotel is one of the few on the coast that offers tourists the all-inclusive concept.





About hotel

Amfora Hotel has been welcoming guests since 1971. Thanks to the restorations that were carried out in 2007 and in 2011, the establishment has a modern look and meets the highest standards. Amfora Hotel is a 20-minute walk from Sunny Beach and just 10 minutes from Nessebar. It is worth noting that the hotel is adjacent to the famous dunes, which are under the protection of UNESCO. The distance from the international airport of Burgas is 30 km.





Description of rooms

Cozy and comfortable will be your stay at Amfora Hotel. Here, tourists are available such options for accommodation:

  • Double standard room is characterized by compact size, but at the same time cozy atmosphere. The central place in the room is occupied by a large bed (or two separate). A makeup table and a workplace are also provided. There is a private furnished balcony.
  • For family accommodation, two-room rooms are provided. 2 bedrooms are separated by a sliding door. Each room has a TV. In addition to beds, upholstered furniture for relaxation is provided. It is also worth noting the presence of a balcony.
  • For travelers who are accustomed to absolute comfort, apartments are provided that resemble a full-fledged apartment. There is a separate bedroom, living room and kitchen. The hallway has a large mirror. Sun loungers are available on the private terrace.




Facilities

Everything that a modern tourist needs in order to feel comfortable is in the rooms of the Amfora Hotel. Bulgaria will show you its hospitality, which is manifested in the following set of amenities:

  • combined bathroom, which, depending on the category of room, is equipped with a bath or shower;
  • built-in hairdryer (except for standard rooms);
  • air conditioning system;
  • satellite television;
  • landline telephone (domestic and international);
  • wireless internet signal;
  • private furnished balcony or terrace;

When booking a room, the following points should also be considered:

  • the price is for the whole room, and not for a specific number of guests;
  • all taxes are included in the price;
  • the price includes food on the system "All inclusive", which includes breakfast, lunch, dinner, drink, snack;
  • kids up to 2 years old stay in rooms for free;
  • if children under 12 years are accommodated in existing beds, no charge will be charged for their stay;
  • an extra bed for children under 12 years old will cost 50% of the cost of living, and for an adult - 80%.

Negative reviews

Not without flaws in the hotel Amfora Hotel 3 *. Bulgaria, Sunny Beach is a fairly budget resort, and therefore it is not surprising that there are a number of negative points. From the reviews you can find out about the following disadvantages of this hotel:

  • folding sofas and armchairs located in the rooms are terribly hard, and therefore it is inconvenient for additional guests to sleep on them;
  • in the immediate vicinity of the hotel there are practically no entertainment facilities;
  • old washed bath towels and bedding;
  • hotel bars open late enough - at 10 in the morning;
  • the staff is not too friendly with the guests, and also not inclined to spiritual conversations;
  • most of the hotel staff pretend not to understand a word in Russian, although this is not so;
  • even if the hotel has available rooms, before 14:00 they do not check in (only for an additional fee);
  • bartenders at the hotel do not hesitate to require a tip;
  • in standard rooms there is no hair dryer;
  • there is no clothes dryer on the balcony, and therefore you have to bring along or buy rope and clothespins;
  • the fish in the restaurant is not always tasty;
  • all rooms are terribly smoky;
  • dirty carpet;
  • some of the rooms are not renovated, as no one warns guests in advance;
  • the restaurant serves poor-quality cheese with a rubber texture;
  • low-quality drinks in bars (this applies to both alcohol, juice and soda);
  • despite the fact that sun loungers and umbrellas on the beach are paid, they need to be booked early in the morning (before breakfast), otherwise there will be no empty seats;
  • the animation is rather mediocre;
  • low speed wireless internet;
  • cleaning is mainly done in the afternoon, which is not very convenient;
  • very mediocre cleaning quality;
  • You can get shampoo and other hygiene items only through lengthy bickering with employees;
  • paid wireless Internet in rooms;
  • rarely change bath towels and bedding;
  • high prices for accommodation, if we compare them with the quality of the services provided;
  • use of sunbeds and umbrellas on the beach is paid;
  • In the rooms, which overlook the sea, the noise from the beach disco is heard at night.

Conclusion

"Amphora" is a pretty decent "troika" in the popular resort of Sunny Beach. This place is distinguished from other hotels of the same category by the All Inclusive system and quite decent food. When choosing a room, it is worth paying attention to the fact that not all of them have been repaired. It is also worth paying attention to insufficient quality cleaning and sluggish animation. Given all the above facts, we can say that the prices for accommodation are overstated.
