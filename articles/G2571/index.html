Want to know how to watch videos on iPad?

Today, for many iPad has already become a familiar thing, however, not everyone knows the exact information about which way is most convenient to watch video on the iPad. Moreover, we say frankly that for some (not particularly advanced) users, solving such a problem is a whole problem. This article will help all people interested in solving this issue!





You can view any video through the iPad in one of two available ways: online or by pre-downloading it. Each of these methods has its advantages and disadvantages. In any case, to understand how to watch video on the iPad, you must have minimal knowledge about the features of the programs and applications developed specifically for Apple products. They are sold (in some cases, distributed free of charge) in the App Store.





Using such applications from the App Store allows you to watch videos online on many popular sites, rather than torment yourself with unnecessary thoughts about how to watch videos on an iPad. So, for example, the YouTube application of the same name operates on the YouTube site. A worthy alternative is the Youtube Client software module. Both products are the most convenient for working with this site.

Using the McTube application allows you to not only watch, but also download videos from the site directly to your iPad. In order to understand how to upload video to the iPad, it is enough to carefully read the instructions for this program. Do not forget, however, that in addition to this application, there are many more utilities that allow you to upload video from the Internet to the iPad. True, they are not specifically designed to work with the YouTube site.





Also, online video using the iPad can be viewed from the pages of the social network VKontakte, from the sites of the most popular network cinemas and television channels. For example, ORT or NTV can be watched perfectly using the iPad (through special applications that exist in paid and free versions).

If you do not want to spend money on the purchase of special applications for watching online video on the iPad, then the issue of how to watch video on the iPad remains relevant for you. In this case, you can advise downloading movies and videos from the Internet in the form of archives, and then unpack them and view them using the player. However, it should be noted that the standard video player on the iPad does not support all formats. Therefore, this option will be the answer to the question of how to watch video on the iPad, only in 6-8 out of 10 cases. Therefore, we recommend that you also use special programs designed to convert video files to iPad. One such program is the freemake video converter utility. With its help, you can quite easily convert any video format to Mp4, and then move the file to the gadget. As a rule, there are no difficulties with converting files. If you encounter difficulties, you just need to re-read the instructions for the program!

In principle, there is nothing complicated! The main thing is to strictly follow the recommendations of the manufacturers. Have a nice view!
