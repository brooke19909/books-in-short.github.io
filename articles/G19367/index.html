Auris plaza hotel al barsha 5 - a place where you feel like a king

Description: There are places in which all conditions are created so that guests remain consistently satisfied. Auris Plaza Hotel Al Barsha 5 is just such a place.

In one of the most expensive areas - Dubai Al-Bashra - there is a luxurious modern hotel complex that captivates its guests with unusual rooms, exquisite decoration and excellent staff that can make your stay memorable. This hotel complex is called - Auris Plaza Hotel Al Barsha 5.





The city center is 1.5 km from the hotel. All communications are located next to the hotel complex: metro, buses and train station.

Rooms: The fund of the complex has 337 rooms of various comforts. Their design is made in a luxurious Arabic style.

Each of them has air conditioning, heating, satellite TV, a minibar, a relaxation area, functional furniture and Internet access.

The bathroom comes with branded cosmetics and bath amenities.





Beach: You have to walk only 200 meters to get to the beautiful sandy beach of Auris Plaza Hotel Al Barsha 5, which offers special services for guests. In addition, if necessary, massages can be performed right on the beach. In the bar located on the beach of the complex, you can order exclusive exotic drinks, selected according to the individual taste of the client.

Meal Plan: A buffet breakfast is included each morning, which is included in the room rate. At the request of visitors to the Auris Plaza Hotel, full board can be arranged in the Didon Restaurant. The chef will surprise you with his ability to cook both traditionally Arabic dishes and international specialties. The hotel's snack bar serves exotic snacks and cold drinks around the clock.

Additional Information: For all comers, Auris Plaza Hotel Al Barsha offers round-the-clock access to the pool, spa, massage parlor and fitness center. In addition, each guest can use the services of car parking personnel in a private car park, order newspapers and lunch in the room, use dry cleaning services, order a transfer from / to the airport and the local area, use a safe and use the concierge service.





If necessary, leave the children in the care of the nanny, always qualified personnel with experience in the field of work with children.

Auris Plaza Hotel Al Barsha 5 has special rooms suitable for allergy sufferers, non-smokers and people with disabilities, as well as soundproofed rooms. You can order them both in advance and upon arrival at the hotel complex.

The hotel offers hookah lovers all kinds of aromatic additives and flavoring agents, which can be tasted in the lounge.

For conferences and celebrations, you should use several conference rooms with different capacities. There is a luxurious banquet hall for unforgettable celebrations.

The famous Mall of the Emirates with many boutiques of famous brands, perfumery and jewelry halls, restaurants and Arabian cafes is a kilometer away. Equally popular is the exotic entertainment center Dubai Mall.

World famous palm islands are 8 kilometers from the hotel. There are many excellent golf courses near the city. And all lovers of luxury holidays should visit Dubai, Kizana Dubai, water park. These places are unlikely to leave their visitors indifferent.

Reviews: The hotel has a personal approach to each guest, any problem is instantly resolved. Meals at Didon restaurant justify the high prices, as the chef is a real magician who can realize any culinary fantasy. Luxurious rooms, convenient location and amazing spa treatments make your stay unforgettable. At this hotel, you can rely on the staff, as children under the supervision of a nanny are always satisfied.
