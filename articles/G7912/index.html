Siolim is a typical provincial town in North Goa, surrounded by tropical greenery. It is quiet and calm, located some distance from the beaches. Hotel location

A cozy three-story hotel is located in Siolim, 55 km from the international airport. The building is made in the traditional colonial style for these places and is surrounded by a well-kept garden. The Portuguese architecture is felt in the architecture of the building. Arched windows have shutters and bars, the walls are decorated with decorative brickwork. Nearby are cafes, a market and some of the attractions of the town. Address: 403517, Porta Waddo, Marna, Siolim, Bardez, Goa.





Hotel La Casa Siolim 3 (North Goa, Siolim) is located at a fairly remote distance from the beach - the third coastline. The Chapora River flows within walking distance. Therefore, renting a car, motorcycle or scooter is relevant. This will allow you to move independently and not depend on travel companies and routes. A great way to explore the country and region.

Number of rooms





The capacity of the hotel is low, but this is more likely a plus than a minus. In total, twenty-five rooms of three levels of comfort are available.

  • 7 standard rooms;
  • 12 deluxe rooms;
  • 6 family rooms.

Regardless of which accommodation option you choose in La Casa Siolim 3 *, the basic package includes air conditioning (which is extremely important in a tropical hot climate), a ceiling fan, a refrigerator and two bottles of water (daily), a safe, TV with satellite channels.

The hotel bathroom includes a toilet, sink, shower, towels (daily replacement). The hotel gives information that pets are allowed. For an additional fee - receiving room service, a minibar, as well as wireless Internet. The hotel does not have a lift and facilities for people with disabilities.

Hotel infrastructure





The hotel, as already mentioned, belongs to the category of "bed and breakfast", so it is logical that it does not offer its guests an abundance of a variety of services, including entertainment. Hotel Cancellation Policy

If you have finally planned your trip to India and chose La Casa Siolim 3 * in Siolim as your place of residence, be sure to read the rules of accommodation. We recommend that you always leave room for maneuver and choose the conditions under which cancellation of a room reservation will be free at least 5-6 days before check-in. In this hotel, the rules are such that pre-paid cash will be returned to you in 100% only if the refusal occurred at least seven days before the expected date of arrival. Refunds will only be made at 50% if you change your mind between the week and 48 hours before arrival. Well, at the last moment (within two days) nothing can be canceled, the hotel reserves the right to the deposited cash amount.

What audience is the hotel focused on?

The budget hotel La Casa Siolim 3 * (India) is primarily aimed at tourists with limited financial resources. These are those people who do not sit still, and they only need housing in order to spend the night. Do not count on first-class service and entertainment here.

The hotel is suitable for independent travelers, couples, groups of friends, to a lesser extent families with children, especially younger children. You should take into account the remoteness of housing from the beaches and calculate your strength and your companions. The national composition of vacationers is quite mixed, quite a lot of Indians from neighboring regions.

Beaches near Siolim





It is important for tourists to know that the maximum distance from the town of Siolim, and therefore from the Hotel La Casa Siolim 3 * to all the beaches of North Goa, is fifteen kilometers. The route is easy to overcome, especially if there is a car, scooter or even a simple bicycle at the disposal. The closest to the urban settlement are the beaches of Morjim (4 km), Anjuna (6 km), Tivim (14 km). Great places to relax, everyone will find exactly what they want for themselves - peace and solitude or bright and noisy parties.

Morjim Beach is a three-kilometer sandy coast. It is full of restaurants with light awnings made of palm branches, it burns with its bright sun and pleases with the warm sea, and at night it plunges into the atmosphere of absolutely stunning parties, indescribable in energy. Morjim is perfect for outdoor enthusiasts. Kite surfing and windsurfing are popular on its territory.

Anjuna is a small village with its own beach. It is perfect for daytime relaxation and noisy evenings in the company of friends. It seems that the atmosphere of the holiday reigns here twenty-four hours a day and three hundred sixty-five days a year. On Wednesdays, a large market is organized on the beach, the assortment of which is beyond description. Tourists will find here everything from local crafts to delicious and fresh food.

Hotel La Casa Siolim 3 *: location reviews

As already mentioned, the hotel is one of the most budget accommodation options in North Goa. He cannot boast of proximity to the beaches. La Casa Siolim 3 service reviews





The hotel received mixed reviews from the guests. It should be said in advance that the assessment of the relationship between the administration and staff is often extremely subjective, therefore it is worth treating them as an opinion, and not as a 100% true axiom.

Hotel guests for the most part speak of an average level of quality of service. First of all, complaints are addressed to personnel who are not always ready to understand and explain, and besides, not everyone speaks English. Secondly, the quality of cleaning the rooms is called into question, since it is not carried out regularly. Thirdly, according to some tourists, the restaurant turned out to be a mid-level cafe. After paying half board or full board on the spot, it turned out that such a service could not be provided at all. However, in this case, the fault of the travel agency is in the face, as the hotel does not hide the information that only breakfast is possible. In addition, guests did not like the fact that wireless internet was available only in the lobby, and was not available in the rooms.





But the hotel guests speak very favorably of the owners as a very friendly and pleasant married couple.

What to see in Siolim?

The village is rather modest in size and level of development of tourist infrastructure, so locals often call it a village. However, the proximity of the river and the sea makes it almost the center of fishing in the region. For the traveler, there is everything you need: cafes, ATMs, hotels, a market and souvenir shops. Every year Siolim attracts an increasing number of tourists, so there is hope that the service will gradually develop and improve.





Despite its size, the city has three interesting sights. Two of them are historical and architectural in nature - this is the Church of St. Anthony and the Temple of Sater. The third is rather entertaining and gastronomic. Every day, an impromptu market opens on the descent from the Chapora River Bridge. He works with a break, first for two hours in the morning (from 8:00), and then from five in the evening until midnight. A large market is replete with freshly caught fish and seafood. For a very low price, right before your eyes, you will not only be cleaned, but also prepared.
