Krasnodar hotels: addresses, descriptions, reviews

The city of Krasnodar since its foundation at the end of the XVIII century is the resort center of Russia. Today it is a large metropolis full of all kinds of attractions. Krasnodar has a developed infrastructure for recreation and entertainment, which includes modern hotel complexes, small hotels, cafes, restaurants and night clubs. Truly huge opportunities open up for tourists to choose the place of residence that is most suitable in terms of cost and comfort. The most famous hotels in Krasnodar will be presented in this article.

Hotel Intourist

The hotel is located in the central part of the city, on 109 Krasnaya Street. The airport is just a twenty-minute drive away, and the train station can be reached on foot in five minutes. Nearby are the Academic Drama Theater and Exhibition Center. The guests of the city who come with children will also be interested in the nearby local zoo. Tourists are accommodated in comfortable rooms equipped with everything necessary for living (household appliances, furniture). Each room has a private bathroom. A bathrobe and slippers are provided.

Meals are provided in any of the five hotel restaurants, from the windows of which a panorama of the city opens. In restaurants, visitors are offered a variety of dishes of Russian and European cuisine. The hotel also has a lobby bar, which always has a huge selection of all kinds of drinks. For an additional fee, food, at the request of the tourist, can be served directly in the room.

Grand Hotel Uyut

Hotel Uyut (Krasnodar) positions itself as a place where guests are offered an increased level of comfort. Like most hotels in the city, it is located in its central part, near local attractions (Shukhov Tower, a singing fountain) at the address: st. Vorovsky, 118.

Residence

The rooms are made up of standard class apartments. The highest level of comfort is provided in the presidential suite. In total there are two hundred rooms of various classes. All of them are provided with air conditioning, modern appliances. There are private rooms for non-smokers and people with disabilities. Each one has a private bathroom and a balcony.

Services

Guests can access the Internet at any time. Vacationers rise to the upper floors using high-speed elevators. Secure parking is also available. Ironing and laundry services are available for guests, a tour desk is available, whose staff will help you book theater tickets and decide on an excursion program.

The 24-hour front desk always provides fresh newspaper and magazine issues. Hotel guests can relax on the terrace.
Meals are provided at the restaurant. You can also eat delicious food in the hotel cafe. At the request of guests for an additional fee, food will be served in the room.
Hotel "Comfort" (Krasnodar) provides great opportunities for all kinds of major events. A spacious conference room is used for this. For city guests who have arrived for business purposes, office equipment is provided.

You can get to the hotel using the shuttle service. At the same time, guests are welcomed at the airport or at the railway station. In principle, almost all Krasnodar hotels provide such a service. The reviews of people who have visited the hotel indicate the high quality of the services provided. The staff is friendly and professional.

Mini-Hotel Kuban-Vostok

In addition to large hotel suites, small cozy mini-hotels also operate in the city, which include the Kuban Hotel (Krasnodar). It is located in the city center on Zheleznodorozhnaya street, 11/2. The bus and train stations are just a short walk away.

The hotel offers guests check-in rooms of various categories (standard, superior rooms, economy rooms and executive suites). All apartments are provided with air conditioning, which creates comfortable conditions for guests at any time of the year. Throughout the hotel for guests in the open access free internet. Accommodation in a mini-hotel is most suitable for people who value the quality of service at a very reasonable cost.

Hotel "Platan" (Krasnodar)

The hotel is assigned the category of "four stars." It is also located in the central part of the city, in the immediate vicinity of the aquarium, city theater, entertainment centers and other attractions. Address: st. Guard, 41.
The room stock consists of eighty-two modern, well-equipped rooms. They are all decorated in a contemporary style and feature beautiful furniture. Each room has a bathroom with all necessary amenities.

Meals are provided in the restaurant. Here you can order and hold a gala event or a romantic dinner. Breakfast is served in the room. Also for various events, there is a wonderful, modern conference room that seats up to forty people.

The list of services provided for an additional fee includes: ironing and laundry of guests, car parking. A bicycle rental service is also available. Settlement of new guests is carried out from one o'clock in the afternoon until late in the evening. Front desk staff is available 24 hours a day.
By the quality of service, level of comfort, the hotel is fully consistent with its status and can be recommended for tourists who value the originality of room design, convenience and comfort.

Hotel Marton

Among the inexpensive vacation spots, the Marton Hotel stands out. It provides guests with the widest opportunities for a comfortable stay. For example, connoisseurs of a cozy atmosphere can check into a chic deluxe room. Also offered are relatively inexpensive, but no less comfortable, economy class rooms. The Marton Hotel (Krasnodar) is located in the city center, on 91 Severnaya Street, close to local attractions and entertainment centers.

Services and entertainment

The hotel has a wonderful indoor pool, there is a good library. Everyone can have a great time at the bar, as well as playing billiards. There is also a sauna. It should be noted the high level of professionalism of employees performing their work efficiently and unnoticed by guests. Those employees whose duties include direct contact with guests are friendly and always ready to give the necessary advice.
For an additional fee, the hotel provides room service, car rental and office equipment. The institution is an excellent example of the optimal ratio of the cost of services and the quality of service for guests and can be recommended for settling guests who want to get maximum comfort at a relatively low cost.

Krasnodar - the center of tourism

An efficiently functioning leisure industry has been created in the city. All kinds of hotels in the city center of Krasnodar offer tourists accommodation in rooms of various categories. Each person, in accordance with their financial capabilities and ideas about comfort, has the opportunity to choose the most suitable option for themselves to live. Almost all Krasnodar hotels are concentrated in the central part of the city, close to local attractions. Most hotels offer approximately the same level of comfort and service. Pricing is also similar. Therefore, choosing one or another hotel, you must be guided by personal preferences regarding the design of rooms. In any case, Krasnodar hotels are the best choice for accommodation.

In the event that the financial issue is not of fundamental importance, you can give preference to the most luxurious and prestigious hotel TROY.
