How to find out traffic on MTS: 6 easy ways. Special cases

Many tariff plans of the "red operator" suggest the availability of prepaid high-speed traffic packages provided on a monthly fee basis. After this volume has been exhausted, the Internet will be available at low speed. To restore the latter, you need to buy a new package for a fee. Therefore, it is important to be aware of how to find out traffic on MTS in order to calculate it for a month. We will present you some convenient and easy ways to obtain such information.

Method number 1: USSD request

This is the simplest answer to the question ‚Äúhow to find out traffic on MTS.‚Äù Your actions are simple:

  1. Dial the command * 107 #, then the call button.
  2. In the list that appears, select "Internet" - send the number 1, then clicking on the call button or "Send".
  3. After a while, you will receive an SMS message with information about the remaining traffic. That's all!

For the Smart line, another request is valid: * 100 * 1 #. Its sending is absolutely free - you will also receive an SMS with information.

If you have exhausted the volume of the main package of Internet traffic and purchased an additional one for a fee, then another request will help you find out its balances: * 111 * 217 #. A message with the necessary information will also be returned.





Method number 2: sending commands

How to see traffic on MTS yet? Imagine a simple universal command: * 217 #. It is relevant for the following options and tariffs:

  • SuperBIT.
  • "BIT".
  • "MiniBIT".
  • "Internet Maxi".
  • "Internet VIP".
  • "Internet Mini".
  • "MTS-Tablet".
  • "MTS-Tablet Mini".
  • additional packages of Internet traffic.

But as for the tariffs of the Smart line, then in this respect this command is useless - only the request described in the previous subheading is relevant.

After you send a request to the operator, a message will be sent to your smartphone with information about the selected tariff plan, as well as about the remaining megabytes-gigabytes of traffic.





Method number 3: send a message

We continue to talk about how to find out traffic on MTS. The following method is also quite common - it is sending SMS. The instruction is also simple:

  1. Submit a "?" (question mark without quotes) to the number 5340.
  2. You will receive a message in response with information about the remaining amount of traffic.

Method number 4: official application

How to find out traffic on MTS literally in a few tapas on the screen? Download the official application of this mobile operator onto your smartphone or tablet (it is available in "App Store", "Play Market"). Register or log in to it. After that, it will be enough for you to open the program to find out the remaining traffic on MTS - immediately its volume will be displayed on the main page.

Let us draw your attention to the fact that the ‚Äúred operator‚Äù is actively promoting its mobile application today, so USSD requests will soon become irrelevant - the rest of the traffic can be found mainly through this software.





Method number 5: personal account

Go to the official website of the "red operator" in the browser. Go to the ‚ÄúMy MTS‚Äù section. Log in: enter your phone number and request a password via SMS - the incoming code will need to be entered in the appropriate field.

Go to the "Internet" tab - there will be information about the remaining traffic, connected options.

Method number 6: user support service

And another simple method is to dial the number 0890 from the MTS telephone. The call is absolutely free. An employee of the contact center will advise you about the rest of the traffic, as well as suggest other interesting information.

Special cases

Let's look at special cases showing how to see traffic on MTS.

Tablet . The following programs can be used for these devices:

  • * 100 * 1 # and the call button. The team is suitable for tariffs of the Smart line.
