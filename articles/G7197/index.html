Difficulty choosing

Which connection is better: MTS, Megafon or Beeline? In fact, this topic is of interest to many. And to understand it is very problematic. After all, each company has its pros and cons.

MTS

Each company is now in demand package rates. They imply a monthly fee, but the price includes Internet, mobile communications and even some additional services. For example, MTS has a Smart package. Depending on its type, you can get:

  • Smart mini - 200 minutes of conversation with MTS subscribers in Russia and with other operators in the home region per month, as many free SMS messages, 2 GB of Internet;
  • Smart - 4 GB of Internet traffic, 400 minutes of calls, the same amount of free messages;
  • "Smart Unlimited" - unlimited Internet in Russia, 200 minutes of calls throughout the country, as many free messages;
  • Smart + - Internet traffic in 6 GB, 900 minutes of free calls and SMS messages.

The main advantage of these offers is that within the home region you can call MTS numbers for free. Moreover, as indicated by the operator, the talk time is not limited. Many indicate that such packages are suitable for active people, whose majority of friends use the services of MTS.

There is also a profitable tariff "MTS Super Zero". It allows you to talk for free with MTS subscribers within your home network for 20 minutes a day when replenishing your account with 100 rubles (in some regions this amount varies). So which connection is better: MTS, Megafon or Beeline?

Connection quality

If you discard personal preferences and leave "naked calculation", you should pay attention to the quality of the services provided. And at their cost. These two indicators can seriously affect the final decision.



In Russia, many subscribers indicate that MTS has the greatest stability. This operator is famous not only for the quality of services, but also for their cost. In second place is Beeline. It also works quite stably, but network failures and malfunctions are observed more often. With a heavy load on the network, you can use the services of an operator, but at a very low speed.

In last place is Megaphone. This operator, although it is considered large, but it still does not differ in stability. Of all the proposed companies, it works the worst.
