Hotel Club Thapsus Aqua 4 *, Tunisia, Mahdia: photos, reviews

Cozy and pretty Mahdia is one of the smallest authentic resorts in Tunisia. It is located on the east coast, between the island of Djerba and Hammamet. Mahdia gained fame for its sparsely populated beaches covered with clean white sand. This resort is most suitable for lovers of a secluded relaxing holiday, as well as for spending quiet leisure time with family. Mahdia cannot boast of a particularly developed tourist infrastructure; there are almost no hotels directly in the city limits. The tourist area here is distinguished by the fact that almost all hotels, with the exception of the central one, are located outside the city, on the first coastline.





Hotel Club Thapsus Aqua 4 * (Tunisia, Mahdia) reviews characterize quite ambiguously. Vacationers note both the many advantages and disadvantages of the service provided by this hotel.

Acquaintance

Hotel Club Thapsus Aqua 4 *, the photo of which is presented in the article, is 2 km from the center of Mahdia. The institution is located in a four-story building facing the sea, and has a large landscaped area. The opening date at the Club Thapsus Aqua 4 * hotel is 1994. According to reviews, the hotel is distinguished by the presence of excellent animation programs, including children's ones, as well as many entertainment and active games.

Infrastructure

Hotel Club Thapsus Aqua 4 * (Tunisia, Mahdia) provides guests with the opportunity to use a private beach, two outdoor pools (one of them is open all year), a children's playground, a sun terrace, a restaurant and free private parking. Club Thapsus Aqua 4 * guests can rent a car. Reception is open 24 hours a day. There is also a hairdresser, lounge and gift shop on site. In addition, at the Club Thapsus Aqua 4 * hotel, vacationers can use the conference hall with 400 seats, a lobby, a safe (for a fee).

For entertainment and leisure.

Club Thapsus Aqua 4 * (Tunisia) offers a rich assortment of opportunities for an exciting and wholesome vacation. For lovers of an active lifestyle, you can get a charge of vivacity in the pools:

What do the guests say?

Opportunities for entertainment provided at the hotel are evaluated by vacationers in different ways. Many argue that no one will be bored at the hotel: the animators are entertaining both children and adults. For kids, even mini-discos are held. In addition to animation, adults are also offered many other interesting events: sports, discos, theme parties. Some guests, on the contrary, call the animation one of the minuses of the hotel and claim that in the evening there is absolutely nowhere to go. In their opinion, the local amusements are boring, and the music coming from evening and afternoon performances only disturbs peace and interferes with relaxation. The playground, which is advertised as active, has been under repair for a long time, which is a cause of disappointment for some sports enthusiasts.

Beach

The hotel is located 100 meters from the sea. Guests have a wonderful opportunity to relax on the sandy beach (hotel property). Free umbrellas are provided here. Sun beds and mattresses can be used for a fee. There is a bar. According to reviews, Club Thapsus Aqua 4 * on the beach has a very convenient entrance to the sea.

Guest opinion

Hotel guests call the sea truly fabulous, chic, the water in it is warm, bright blue. The beach is also described as "very good", with clean white sand, shells. True, the loungers here are almost all broken, to occupy the lounger, you have to wake up at five in the morning. Hotel guests note the presence of garbage on the beach, but the responsibility lies with tourists leaving empty glasses, bottles, etc. The bar starts operating at ten in the morning, and a rich assortment of food and drinks is offered. At night, the beach closes.
