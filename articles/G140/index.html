Base of active recreation "Serebryansky stone": photos and reviews of tourists

Traveling in Russia is becoming increasingly popular. Due to its vast territory, tourists have a huge selection of destinations. One of the most popular places to visit is the Urals. Mountains majestically rising above the plain, lush greenery of flowers and trees, clear water murmuring in the rivers - these are the components of relaxation in the Urals.

For those who want to spend time with maximum benefit, a lot of guest houses and recreation centers were built in the Urals. One of them is the Serebryansky Kamen recreation center, where all the necessary conditions for a comfortable vacation are created.





Description

On the territory of the recreation center there are residential buildings, a cafe and a dining room, as well as several gazebos and places for self-cooking barbecue.

As for the number of rooms, visitors are offered a choice between living in a summer house, an insulated house, a traditional Nenets plague, as well as in their own tent.

Rest at the Serebryansky Kamen Hotel (Sverdlovsk Region) is possible at any time of the year, because the camp site has both light and warm houses, and excursions to interesting places in the Urals are organized all year round.





Services

The camp site workers offer many different services for organizing leisure of visitors and their comfortable stay in the hotel:

  • Nutrition. The exact cost is negotiated by agreement and depends on the number of meals, type of food.
  • Transfer. The service is provided for a fee, the driver takes two thousand rubles from the group. Moving is carried out in large new cars. The boot space is roomy.
  • Equipment for outdoor activities in winter. It includes skiing (400 rubles per day), snowshoes (the price is the same), as well as a sleeping bag (200 rubles per day).
  • Necessary things for lighting a fire and cooking barbecue. In that case, if you want to cook a barbecue and sit with friends around the campfire, you can do it in the Serebryansky Kamen camp site. Firewood, coal, barbecue are provided by the base for an additional cost. Grill, places for lighting bonfires and catering (gazebo) are free.
  • The bathhouse. The room accommodates up to 5 people, and the cost of its use depends on the time of year. Payment is done hourly. The average cost is 1000 rubles.
  • Organization of children's leisure. In their free time, children will be able to relax in a special playground located on the territory of the Serebryansky Kamen camp site. The rope town covers a large area and contains many interesting slides and obstacles.




Leisure

Inside the hotel there are offers for trips to interesting places of the Urals. Excursions are organized when recruiting a group of a certain number of people. Among the proposed options are visiting some lakes, hiking with a guide, as well as jeeping.

  • Hiking takes place on proven routes that lead to the most amazing and colorful places with rich and lush nature. Routes include walks through the forest, along mountain rivers, as well as through the mountains themselves.
  • The Serebryansky Kamen base offers guests one of the most unusual excursions - jeeping. The trip is carried out in hostel cars, along with the group a guide rides, who talks about the amazing landscape of the Northern Urals. The presence of children on the tour is welcome. In addition, during the tour, the group can stop and swim in the mountain rivers. The duration of the trip is not fixed and in most cases takes a whole day. The cost is negotiated on the spot.
  • A visit to Lake Spaysky. The distance between the settlement and the lake is 15 km, the hike is carried out along small forest paths. Serebryansky Kamen (recreation center) offers the group about to visit the lake an experienced guide who will show the right path and tell about interesting facts about the point.

In addition to these routes, the recreation center offers a lot of interesting options - a large selection of tourists is provided.





Advantages

  • Beautiful nature. The Northern Urals is a place with amazing nature. The richness of greenery, the abundance of mountain rivers and deep transparent lakes, lush vegetation will inspire every tourist!
  • Inexpensive accommodation. Prices for accommodation in the Serebryansky Kamen camp site are low, an overnight stay will cost you no more than 5 thousand rubles in a superior room.
  • An interesting list of excursions. The leisure plan is really huge: tourists are offered the opportunity to choose not only the route and final destination, but also the type of trip: hiking, jogging, jeep riding, skiing or snowmobiling.
  • Good ecology. The cleanliness and freshness of the air in the Northern Urals deserves special attention. Due to the remoteness of the base from large cities, tourists can enjoy the freshness of the mountain air and the cleanliness of the territory.
  • Convenient location. Despite the remoteness of the Serebryansky Kamen base from large cities or villages, it is very easy to reach the final point. The nearby town of Karpinsk is 44 km from the camp site.

disadvantages

  • Expensive additional services. The cost of the necessary services is quite high - this extends even to the organization of showers and meals, which is a huge minus of the camp site.
  • Bad connection. You will not be able to call relatives and friends while in the Serebryansky Kamen base. The camp site offers the ability to make calls in a specially designated place, but the network does not catch the whole territory. The same goes for Wi-Fi.




Climate and ecology

The climate of the Northern Urals is sharply continental. This type of climate is very convenient for tourists: the territory has long hot summers, a small amount of rainfall, as well as long frosty winters. The seasons change very quickly, and autumn and spring are present here very briefly. In the event that you want to organize a vacation in the warm season, the best time to travel will be the end of spring - the beginning of autumn. Some tourists prefer winter holidays. In this case, a good time to travel is December - January. In February, the base becomes very cold, frequent snowstorms and snowstorms occur.

The ecology of the northern Urals deserves admiration: the air here is absolutely clean, and the territory is not covered with a lot of garbage.

Base Rules

On the territory of the camp "Serebryansky stone" there are some rules:

  • When booking and renting rooms or houses, an identity document is required.
  • Payment for accommodation should be made immediately after checking in the room.
  • Children resting on the rope camp must be accompanied by their parents. You cannot leave them unattended.
  • All visitors should not litter and must monitor the cleanliness of the room in which they live.
  • Pets are allowed, subject to certain restrictions. Large breeds of dogs should be muzzled and walk strictly on a leash. All waste products must be cleaned by the host.
  • Each guest should be careful about the environment.




How to get there

One of the most conveniently located recreation centers is Serebryansky Kamen. How to get to the appointed place?

Due to its convenient location, every tourist will be able to get to the base on a regular regular bus that runs along the Karpinsk-Kytlym route. The flight number is 105. It is worth remembering that the bus leaves on this route not every day, namely on Mon, Wed, Fri, Sun Departure time from Karpinsk is 8.00 and 16.00, and the return route is 10.00 and 18.00. The duration of a trip from one point to another is 2 hours. The schedule is valid in local time.

The road from the nearby town of Karpinsk to the camp site takes a little time. A tourist should get to the village of Kytlym, and turn left after it after 100 meters.

Cost

Accommodation at a camp site is quite cheap. The minimum cost per night is 300 rubles per person, the maximum is 5000.

Reviews

Judging by the reviews left between 2014 and 2016, the Serebryansky Kamen camp site, a photo of which many tourists admire, is one of the best places to stay. Despite its shortcomings (expensive services, excursions), rest in a camp site brings only the best impressions. This is not surprising, because high-quality, good housing, amazing, colorful nature, delight anyone.

In addition, discounts and special offers are available on the camp site, which fully compensates for dissatisfaction with paid services. So, employees give a discount to those who are birthday and those tourists who book rooms for a period of more than 2 days. With an increase in the length of stay, the cost gradually decreases. In addition, the birthday presents a gift: an hour of bathing in a traditional Russian hut.
