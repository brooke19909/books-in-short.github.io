Newborn skin features and yarn

Babies have very delicate skin, and hard knitting can rub it. The product should be soft to the touch, so knitted things for babies have a more delicate texture. However, in the first weeks of life you should not wear any woolen blouses, dresses, overalls, shirts on your naked body.

Also pay attention to the type of yarn. It is better to buy expensive special children's yarn than cheap skeins of hand or in the market. The fact is that poor-quality material can cause redness of the skin, itching and even become an allergen. Therefore, you need to knit things for a newborn from natural yarn.



Cotton allows baby skin to breathe, does not cause allergies. But some things from it become tough, especially if you knit tight. In this case, choose a blended yarn made of cotton and acrylic, which creates airiness and softness. Woolen threads, choose only for children, soft (for example, from merino wool). However, in many children and adults, wool causes itching and redness, so choose mixed skeins, for example, the same acrylic or microfiber.

How to work with knitting magazines?

When creating children's things, also pay attention to the density of knitting and connection. The hard weave not only rubs the skin, but also increases the weight of the product. No child will be comfortable in such clothes. Try or loosen the thread when knitting, or choose a different pattern, or take the knitting needles a size larger.

You need to knit things for the newborn without seams, especially for clothes that will fit to the body through one vest or socks. That is, it is better to stitch the products outward. The seams can be decorated with a neat stitch, and the baby will be comfortable in such clothes. Or connect the parts with a hook, needle with a secret stitch when the product looks solid.



Model in the magazine, choose the size that matches the growth of the baby. Buy the material specified in the instructions. If you purchase other yarn or knitting needles, then calculate the loops based on your density. It is best to make a full-length pattern on paper and apply the product to the piece about the time of knitting.
