Layout of a townhouse: features and details of home improvement

Today, real estate, in particular, townhouses in Moscow, are presented on the Internet. Developers actively advertise their creations and offer to purchase housing at a bargain price. But how to choose and not make a mistake with the purchase? This article will familiarize you with the features of acquiring a townhouse in the suburbs.

The best option for real estate investment - townhouse

Townhouse projects are considered the ideal type of housing. Real estate is a priority in choosing, not only because of the pleasant price, but also due to the favorable ecological state, because town houses are located, as a rule, on the outskirts of the city.





Note that noisy neighbors will no longer bother you in the townhouse, and no one will refuse a pleasant bonus - a small plot of land where it is possible to realize all your garden fantasies.

Real estate situation analysis

On any of the Internet portals it is not difficult to find an offer for the sale of real estate outside the city in the vicinity of Moscow - a townhouse.

The current economic situation worries the client even more, because no one wants to overpay. Therefore, in search of a better offer, a potential buyer of real estate in Moscow or Moscow region tries to find an option that combines the best:

  • quality;
  • price;
  • geographic location;
  • transport accessibility;
  • the infrastructure.

Those who have already managed to acquire a townhouse outside the city, note that this option is very popular, and some have even managed to resell real estate by investing in another.

Layout details of townhouses

When designing modern townhouses, planning is made with emphasis on mobility and functionality. The area of ‚Äã‚Äãthe house, compared with a city apartment, allows you to realize the most daring design projects and fantasies.

Important! For the price you have to pay for a 2- or 3-room apartment, in the suburbs you can actually buy a more spacious townhouse, the layout of which is not as modest as in a high-rise building.





Modern housing is designed to be not only beautiful, but also functional. No matter how original the project, in the first place it is worth paying attention to the level of comfort, coziness and simplicity.

The buyer, first of all, is looking for convenience, and designers should not forget about it. Therefore, the layout of townhouses put forward special requirements that make living in the house as comfortable as possible, and the use of living space is 100% rational.





For example, in a two-story house, the first floor is reserved for non-residential premises: a hall, a living room, a kitchen-dining room, a garage; the second - for residential: bedrooms and an office.

The attic is intended for use as a space for relaxation. Here you can equip a home theater or game room, additionally using the free zone. Sometimes the attic space is used to equip another bedroom - for guests.

Features of the layout of the townhouse allows you to harmoniously divide the house into zones, which helps to minimize noise and privacy.

Arrangement of stairs in a townhouse

In the house you can equip a staircase in any of the rooms. In any case, it will be convenient. Using a spiral staircase will save usable space, and the free space under the classical corner can be used as a pantry, which is also a good option. It all depends on the style. Emphasizing the interior, the stairs are even placed in the center of the room, focusing on it.

The layout of the bathroom and its place in the house

An important point is the availability of a bathroom on each floor of the house. In this case, the bathroom is placed so that you can enter it directly from the corridor, rather than entering through the room.

The bathroom is preferably located on the floor with bedrooms, downstairs is a guest sanitary unit with a toilet and sink.





As you noticed, the layout of the townhouse from the builder is an important element that contributes to the formation of healthy interest among consumers. The buyer first of all appreciates the harmonious placement of rooms and the convenience of their location for themselves.

Note that the townhouse is a profitable option for real estate, presented on the modern market in the light of high-quality and profitable housing, built according to European standardized technology.
