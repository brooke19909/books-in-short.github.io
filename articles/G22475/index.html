Where to swim

Kabanari Bay 3 Hotel is located near a beautiful beach. It is municipal, like the entire coast of the island, and, of course, equipped. There are sunbeds, umbrellas. Near the large boulders on the shore you can swim with a mask and watch the fish. The sea is warm, gentle, amazingly blue and very salty.
