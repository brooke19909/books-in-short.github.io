About the hotel

The hotel was opened in the fall of 1995 and became the first four-star hotel in the region. Judging by the numerous reviews of its guests, this place can be safely called the visiting card of the capital of Siberia. The institution offers guests a full range of services for quite reasonable money. It is worth noting that the complex was created for people who value coziness and comfort in everything.

Hotel location

Kvoliti (Tyumen) has a good location in the heart of the city. A few minutes walk from the hotel is a picturesque promenade, government building, offices of oil companies. In the immediate vicinity are local attractions, shopping and entertainment centers, historical buildings. You can get from the hotel to the airport in just five minutes, to the train station in a couple of minutes.

Number of rooms

The institution offers accommodation in 230 comfortable rooms, of which 76 are single, 114 are double, 25 are suites, 18 are studios and a presidential suite. Each room has air conditioning, TV, refrigerator, workspace, coffee and tea making facilities, free Internet access. The bathroom has a set of cosmetics, hairdryer.
