Hotel "Mirage" (Kazan): reviews, photos, address, phone

Many Russians and foreigners come to Kazan to admire its unique historical and architectural monuments, to walk along the wonderful streets and squares. Businessmen also come here, because it is a large industrial and business center of Russia. All the guests of the city are pleased to provide their services with the wonderful five-star Mirage Hotel. Kazan is famous for its hospitality. The hotel workers sacredly honor this good tradition, trying to do everything so that their guests can spend their holidays at the highest level.

Location

The Mirage Hotel (Kazan) is located almost in the heart of the city, a few steps from the famous Kremlin, which guests can clearly see from the windows of the rooms. The location of the hotel is also very convenient in relation to the railway station, which is only 10 minutes on foot, and transport can take about 3-4 minutes. When approaching the city, from the train cars you can see the hotel and easily navigate in which direction to move to it. Public transport stops are also a few steps away, and the metro station is a 5-minute walk away. Nearby is the entertainment and shopping center "Pyramid", which often hosts concerts by popular artists.



How to get there

You can walk to the Mirage Hotel (Kazan) from the station if there is no heavy load. It takes only 10 minutes to do this. From the bus station to the hotel, approximately 20 minutes on foot. By taxi it takes only a couple of minutes and will cost about 100 rubles. By public transport, traveling to the Mirage Hotel is also very short. On buses No. 37.47 and 45, as well as on trolleybus No. 4, you need to get off at the Sports Palace stop, which is about 200 meters from the hotel. If you take buses No. 6, 15, 37, 75, 79, 89, 98 or trolleybus No. 10, you need to get off at the Central Stadium stop, it is 750 meters from the hotel to it.





From the airport, which is located 30 km from Kazan, there are several access options. The easiest and most expensive is a taxi. The journey takes about 50 minutes. You can also use public transport. It is most convenient to catch an electric train and get to the Kazan-Passenger station, which will take no more than 20 minutes. Next you need to follow on foot or by bus. Bus number 197 runs from the airport to the city, but if you use it, you will have to change trains either by metro or other city buses.

Description

The Mirage Hotel (Kazan) received its first guests in 2005, and the last major reconstruction was carried out here in 2009. Since it is an urban-type hotel, it has practically no own territory. All infrastructure is concentrated in the building, but in front of the main entrance there is a ramp and a spacious parking area. The main entrance is decorated with a small lawn and green spaces. The design of the hotel building is ultra-modern, includes several geometric shapes made of glass and concrete. The hall is very spacious and bright, inimitably stylishly decorated. Everything shines and sparkles here, but the huge panoramic windows are especially impressive, from which everything that is located around is clearly visible.

Infrastructure

The Mirage Hotel (Kazan) provides services at a European level. Reception is open around the clock. The staff is very friendly, able to quickly resolve all issues and at the same time create a good mood among the guests. Here you can order a transfer, translator, secretary, use a fax and printer, call a taxi, book air and train tickets, rent a car, use the services of guides (a walk in Kazan), and hand over laundry and ironing personal belongings.





For business events, the Mirage Hotel (Kazan) has conference rooms (4 units) and a business center. Each hall is individually designed and equipped with the latest equipment, appliances, furniture. The capacity of the halls is from 20 to 180 people.

Leisure

The Mirage Hotel (Kazan) has several leisure facilities - a swimming pool, a fitness center, a SPA salon, a massage room, a beauty salon and a sauna. The use of all these pleasures is included in the room rate. The pool is the largest in Kazan among such hydraulic structures in hotels. Its area is almost 200 square meters, and its depth is up to 1.8 meters. Sun loungers are installed near the pool, water in it is not chlorinated. In the SPA-salon there is a sauna, steam bath, turbosolarium. The beauty salon has a beauty parlor and hairdresser.





The Mirage Hotel (Kazan) fitness center is equipped with modern fitness equipment. This place is perfect for lovers of outdoor activities, as well as for those who are used to always keep their body in shape. Among the simulators are treadmills, exercise bikes, steppers and many others.
