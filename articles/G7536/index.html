On the beach

It is worth considering that entry to the beach is paid. The cost is 100 rubles. At the same time, the time spent on the beach does not affect the price. Immediately there is a strip of birch forest. It is here that many tourists set up their tents. And beyond the forest - Golden Sands. The fraternal sea meets everyone with warm water and a shallow shore.

The sand on the beach is shallow and yellow. The bottom of the reservoir, like the shore, is shallow. The main advantage of this place is that the water warms up well and quickly. But lovers of diving this place will not like it. Deep places begin far from the coast.

There is no garbage here, as everyone who drives to the beach is given special bags. After they can be put on the road. Filled bags are collected by special machines and transported.
