Hotel Golden Sea Pattaya 3 * (Thailand, Pattaya): description, reviews

When choosing a place for the next vacation, many tourists turn their eyes to Thailand. It is an incredibly beautiful country with centuries-old culture, breathtaking nature and delicious cuisine. One of the most popular resort cities is Pattaya - this is where the bulk of travelers who want to relax on a beautiful beach flock here. Of course, a huge number of hotels work here, so the tourist always has a choice. Quite popular is the Golden Sea Pattaya 3 * hotel.

Naturally, when planning a trip, you need to pay attention to many factors, in particular, the location of the hotel, the conditions of food and accommodation, the availability of amenities and entertainment.

Location Features





Choosing a hotel, travelers by no means least pay attention to the location. Hotel Golden Sea Pattaya 3 * is located in the central part of the resort city of Pattaya. Nearby are shops, cafes, massage parlors, as well as a large shopping center.

The distance to the public beach is about 600 m - a convenient road leads to the shore, so walking is unlikely to be a burden. The nearest international airport is in Bangkok, about 150 km away. By the way, the hotel provides its guests with a shuttle service.

What does the hotel area look like?

Hotel Golden Sea Pattaya 3 * is located in a quiet tourist area. It consists of one large, multi-storey building, built in an interesting Thai style. The hotel territory is small (the area is about 8 thousand square meters) and is completely fenced. Security guards are constantly working here, so there is no need to worry about security.

The courtyard will delight you with lush greenery and original Thai decorations. There is a pool and a comfortable terrace for relaxing. Travelers enjoy spending time on site. By the way, the hotel was opened in 2008, and already in 2015 there was a thorough restoration.

Hotel Golden Sea Pattaya 3 * (Pattaya, Thailand): room information





The hotel complex offers its customers check-in in one of 200 rooms. Of course, there are rooms of different capacities and categories. For the most part these are standard rooms, and there are both double and more spacious, family rooms. The hotel also has deluxe and suite rooms with more spacious balconies, additional upholstered furniture and a kitchenette, which is very convenient, especially for parents with children who need separate meals.

According to reviews, the rooms are spacious with large windows. Depending on the location of the room, you can enjoy views of the surroundings or the pool. Of course, guests can count on the necessary furniture and appliances. There is a powerful air conditioning system, as well as a safe, telephone and TV (for the most part they show local cable channels). The kitchenette (if any) is equipped with the necessary appliances, there are dishes, in some rooms even a dining table. It includes a small fridge, an electric kettle and a set for making hot drinks. The minibar is filled with drinking water and is replenished daily during cleaning.

The tourist will be pleased with a clean bathroom with functional fixtures. Here you can take a shower. There is a dressing mirror, clean towels (including those that you can take with you to the beach), as well as shampoo, soap, a shower cap and other hygiene items.

Dining on site: what do guests offer?





Tourists paying for their stay at Golden Sea Pattaya 3 can count on daily breakfast. They are held in the form of a buffet at the local restaurant. Travelers say that the menus here are quite diverse - there are light salads, snacks of international and Thai cuisine, fresh pastries, desserts and, of course, aromatic tea.

On the territory of the same restaurant you can have a meal and throughout the day, however, they will cook for you by order and food is paid separately. You can have a good time in the bar with a stylish atmosphere and a relaxed atmosphere - here visitors are offered drinks and delicious, light snacks.

Beach: amenities and entertainment on the beach





Of course, when arriving in Thailand, tourists expect to rest by the sea. Hotel Golden Sea Pattaya 3 is located on the third coastline - the distance to the coast is about 600 m. A direct road leads to it, and the walk does not take much time. Since the beach is public, you need to understand - rent of sun loungers and umbrellas is paid separately each time. On the shore there is a bar where you can eat, and there is enough entertainment.

For example, vacationers can rent a catamaran or boat, ride a boat along the coast, go sailing or get a dose of adrenaline on water skiing. There is also a diving school, where beginners can get a certificate after a short course of training. You can also hire a windsurfing instructor. And on the beach there is a spacious volleyball court where tourists are constantly having fun.

Additional service: what to count on?





The hotel tried to create the conditions in order to make the rest of the guests easy and enjoyable. For example, here you can easily exchange currency. At the reception there is an opportunity to rent an individual safe, and there is also a left-luggage office, which is very convenient. As with most hotels, laundry and dry cleaning services are available.

There is a small, compact parking with a modern security system - you can use it for free. By the way, you can rent a car without problems in the city. Internet is available at the hotel, but its access is paid separately.

Pattaya, hotel Golden Sea Pattaya 3 *: leisure of guests





Immediately it is worth saying that in most cases, tourists come to Pattaya, wanting to have a good time on the beach and have fun in the city. But you can have a good rest at the hotel. As already mentioned, in the courtyard there is a large swimming pool with clean fresh water, where you can freshen up and even play water polo with a small team. In addition, comfortable sun loungers and wide-brimmed sun umbrellas are located on the terrace.

Golf courses are located not so far from the hotel, which will certainly please fans of this sport. In addition, there is the opportunity to go cycling or have fun fishing. Do not forget about the interesting excursions that are offered almost everywhere in the city. And there are also many massage parlors near the hotel, because it is impossible to leave the country without trying out all the wonders of Thai massage.

What do travelers say?

Sometimes a short conversation with people who have already managed to visit a particular hotel helps to make up the big picture. So what can you hear from tourists about the Golden Sea Pattaya 3 * hotel? The reviews are mostly positive. The territory here is small, but well arranged, with beautiful decorations. The rooms are clean and the cleaners really come every day, and not only clean, but change towels, replenish stocks of soap and shampoos.

The location is also beneficial - you can walk to the beach, market and shopping center. Breakfast is delicious, and the menu is quite varied. Nevertheless, tourists should understand that they are not going to a luxury hotel complex with an all-inclusive system. Golden Sea Pattaya Hotel 3 * is perfect for an overnight stay, but entertainment will have to be sought outside its walls. On the other hand, the cost of living here is very affordable, especially considering the level of service.
