Hotel Saritas Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya): description and reviews

Among the many cozy resorts in Turkey, travelers prefer the small cozy town of Alanya, which is located on the Mediterranean coast. Hot summers, mild winters, little rainfall, fresh air and many historical sights from year to year attract here curious tourists. Amazing sand and pebble beaches, which are considered one of the best on the Turkish coast, are located here. Crystal clear water, secluded bays and a gentle slope into the water led to a favorable vacation for lovers of romance, as well as the possibility of spending a vacation with the whole family. The 4-star Hotel Saritas, located on the golden sands of the Mediterranean, will bring unprecedented comfort, high-quality service and an unforgettable pastime for representatives of all age groups on a long-awaited trip.





General information

The hotel is represented by three seven-story buildings, commissioned on May 1, 1995. To get to the necessary floor, vacationers are helped by the elevators available in each building. The last repair was carried out in 2015. The total area is 6,000 square meters, equipped with a swimming pool, children's entertainment, souvenir shops and food stalls.

The airport is 110 km away. On the territory there are a lot of greenery, exotic palm trees and bright flowers. The paths are paved with stone, many benches and urns. The shady area allows you to enjoy the Turkish scents of nature in the hot summer days.

The complex consists of 220 residential rooms. Pets are strictly prohibited. Non-smoking rooms and for people with disabilities are available. The staff speaks four languages: Turkish, Russian, German, English.





Residence

The hotel has 220 rooms equipped with 550 beds. Among them there are categories:

  • Standard
  • Family

The interior is dominated by bright colors. Designers have given an unprecedented comfort to the apartments due to the successful combination of colors with decorating elements. The floors are covered with high-quality ceramic tiles. The saturation of the lamps makes the room bright even in the dark. Each room has a balcony, equipped with necessary furniture for pleasant quiet evenings and sun fare. The furniture is represented by a modern high-quality set, made mainly of wood: beds, a table, chairs, a wardrobe and bedside tables. The standard room is 16 square meters, while the family rooms are represented by two rooms connected by a door and can accommodate a maximum of 5 people.

Apartments are cleaned daily, linen is changed twice a week.





Supply system

The meals are provided according to the all-inclusive concept three times a day (breakfast, lunch, dinner) according to the buffet system. Regarding food, tourists staying at Saritas Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya) leave extremely positive reviews. Hearty and nutritious dishes of vegetables and meat, delicious desserts and snacks, cereals, seafood and much more are included in the daily serving for guests. Meals are held according to the established schedule. The main restaurant has a capacity of 400 people, it is decorated with multi-level ceilings, designer curtains and many spotlights, which makes the stay in its walls a feeling of home warmth and coziness.

The pool bar serves refreshing alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, light snacks and pastries. Taste aromatic teas and natural coffee available in the daytime.

Ice cream, freshly squeezed juices, imported alcoholic beverages are not included in the price and are paid extra.

The S bar, which welcomes guests in the evening, allows you to enjoy the wonderful melodies of a colorful nation and sit in a quiet cozy atmosphere, forgetting about all the problems.





Aquazone

Staying at the Saritas Hotel 4 * hotel (Turkey), each guest has the opportunity to enjoy swimming in the cleanest pool, which is cleaned every night. Its area is 286 square meters. It is surrounded by numerous sun loungers with mattresses. A large area for sunbathing allows a long time to be in the open air, getting a unique Turkish tan. For those who prefer shady corners - the location under the umbrellas will appeal to you.

Children's leisure and entertainment

About Saritas Hotel 4 * (Turkey) reviews of a positive plan are left by grateful parents. Their kids really have something to do here. Nearby there is a pool for children, equipped with an amazing twisted descent with an open gutter. Riding on a water slide enthralls children of all ages, and splashing and diving in the clear water of the pool helps to spend the endless energy of the kids and prepare for a healthy night's sleep.

The convenient location of the aquazone elements made it possible for children and their parents to rest close to each other. Adults will be able to enjoy their stay at the resort, and children will have plenty of fun in the fresh air under the supervision of adults.

Babysitting service is available for a fee on request. A qualified teacher will take the time of the baby with exciting games and educational activities.

The restaurants have high chairs. Cots are available on request.





Beach area

The location of this hotel in the first coastline determined the closest distance to the sea - 50 meters. As soon as you cross the roadway, using the underpass, guests immediately find themselves on velvet sands, combined with fine pebbles, Alanya, Saritas Hotel's private beach. Sun beds with mattresses and umbrellas in this part of the resort are provided for a fee. No beach towels.

The presence of a bar made it possible to take soft drinks near the noisy waters of the Mediterranean Sea, however this institution is not included in the all-inclusive program and is paid extra.

For lovers of active entertainment on the beach there is a wide list of possible pastimes: banana and water skiing, catamarans and canoes. The presence of a volleyball court allows you to stretch your body and compete for the championship.





Ways of hanging out

Having enjoyed a lot of swimming in the clear waters of the Mediterranean, most vacationers want to spend their free time in an exciting way. To do this, every evening for the guests of the resort complex are artists of the animation team. The number of talented animators is three, among them there is a Russian-speaking representative. Colorful shows, quizzes and competitions, dances and fun will be filled every evening within this institution.

For lovers of physical exertion and exercise, a rich and professionally equipped gym works. Entrance to it is paid, use of the inventory is free. Experienced instructors will help maintain your body in good shape, and possibly remove a couple of extra pounds, by developing an individual training program.

Board games, such as chess, backgammon and tennis, do not require additional charges. But billiards and spa treatments are not included in the price. Sauna, hammam, various massage techniques performed by qualified employees of the SPA-salon will give a feeling of relaxation and enjoyment to each of its visitors.
