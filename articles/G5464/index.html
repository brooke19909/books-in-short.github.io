Uzhhorod hotels - from an economy option to an all-inclusive

Planning a vacation in Transcarpathia? What is worth knowing about Uzhgorod before the trip and where to settle with the greatest comfort? Hotels in Uzhgorod, as well as pensions and hotel complexes in its vicinity, offer customers not only European service and quality, but also the original Carpathian flavor.

The Pearl of Transcarpathia

Uzhhorod is a regional center in Western Ukraine and partly a cozy tourist city in the mountains. Uzhhorod is located near the Hungarian border and is famous for its unique flavor. Here you can admire the ancient Uzhgorod castle, the Greek Catholic Holy Cross Cathedral, Transcarpathian old dwellings and churches, as well as enjoy the wonderful mountain scenery and traditional dishes. Uzhgorod hotels provide a wide range of services and entertainment, and helpful staff will do everything to make the client feel comfortable.





The hostel is ideal for students and large groups

Hostel Misto is located near the historical center and the park. Guests have access to a shared kitchen, bathroom, lounge with comfortable sofas and free Wi-Fi. A gift shop, parking and airport shuttle are also available at an additional cost. The administration works 24 hours a day and also speaks Russian. Rooms are available for 6, 5, 3 and 2 people.

Price: from 96 UAH.

Another budget option is the Egan hostel. Here you can not only spend the night, but also have a tasty meal and drink coffee in the local bar. Guest rooms are equipped with LCD TVs and air conditioning. Free luggage storage, Wi-Fi and parking are provided.

Price: from 99 UAH.

The hostel is a very budget option, which nonetheless allows you to plunge into the life of the city with your head, meet new people and experience unforgettable adventures. In addition, if you are looking for hotels in Uzhgorod in the city center, the location of the hostels will please you.

In such hotels in Uzhgorod, you can have fun and have a good rest at an adequate price.
