Chelyabinsk hotels: rating, photo. The best hotels of Chelyabinsk

The Ural city of Chelyabinsk is worth a visit to everyone. It is known as a significant industrial center of Russia, while it is also a cultural center. In this place are located: regional art gallery, museum of local lore, museum of applied art.



The city also has a zoo, a circus, a hippodrome with 10 horse clubs. In Chelyabinsk there are 2 concert halls and 6 theaters where you can find a performance to your taste. In this city, the Flower Festival is held every year, as well as many concerts, parades, and sports events. In all 8 recreation parks you can relax and have fun. You should also visit the institutions of modern culture. You can easily get here by train or plane.

In this article we will consider the rating of hotels in Chelyabinsk.
