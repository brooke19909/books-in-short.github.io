How to make do-it-yourself wolverine claws from paper

If you want to know how to make wolverine claws out of paper , then by reading this article you will learn how to do this. After all, to make it easier than ever. Follow a series of sketchy steps, which are listed below, and the craft is ready. We will make it with our own hands, based on the origami technique. So be patient and material.

How to make Wolverine Claws from paper

To create a product with one hand you will need:

- white paper (five sheets);



- your skillful pens.

Paper Origami: Wolverine Claws

Get ready, we're getting started. We will learn how to make wolverine claws out of paper . We take a sheet. Tip: you should not use thick paper, best office paper. You can make claws of white paper. But how will they look if you select a color! Amazingly bright! But the choice is yours.

So, bend the upper left edge diagonally. We connect it to the right. It should turn out at the top an acute angle. We repeat this action now on the other hand. From the upper right edge of the sheet we fold diagonally. It turned out a scheme that will facilitate us in the future assembly of the product. Next, bend the upper end to the middle of the sheet. We should get a house-shaped figure.

We bend the lower left edge. Then the right one too. The result is a rhombus. Fold it in half. Now we are shaping our blank. We bend the right edge at an angle of thirty degrees. Fold it in a circle again. We bend the left corner towards the right. Then we wrap the edge of the workpiece and insert it into the inner pocket. We fix it. The result is such a module.



Be sure to make sure that the claws of the wolverine from the paper are smooth and nothing is puffed. Fingers give the product a three-dimensional appearance. Next we make a pocket. We will insert a finger into this recess. Now you already understand how to make wolverine claws out of paper and can independently make the other four blades in this way.

So, all the details are ready. We put it on our fingers and go to the carnival show to scare people. You can wrap the product with food foil so that the nails seem metallic. As you already understood, making such an accessory is quite simple and will not be difficult. Such claws can be made together with children. They can then play with them, portraying a lion, tiger or any other predator. However, the child himself will figure out what to do with this craft. But this way of creating claws cannot give strength to the product.

Other material options





You can take thick cardboard and cut ten elongated triangles. Make fasteners. To do this, take the ice cream stick. Make slots. To know at what distance to do them, first measure the thickness of your fingers. Connect the slots in the wand and in the claws. Secure with glue.

Other options are made of wood and metal. But not everyone has such an opportunity.
And therefore it is better to choose paper or cardboard.
