Hostel in Krasnogorsk, Moscow region

It will be easy to choose a hostel in Krasnogorsk, Moscow Region, for relaxation. The proposed options should be considered, since they are the most inexpensive, but of high quality. The rooms of the hostels described are clean and comfortable. There is all the necessary furniture and accessories for personal hygiene.

"Helena"

The minimum cost per day is 3500 rubles. Hostel "Elena" in Krasnogorsk is quite popular. Next to it is the metro station. Guests enjoy free internet. It works throughout the hostel. In addition, there is a shuttle service. The front desk is open all the time.

The rooms have furniture and comfortable beds. They are decorated in dark colors. Near the hostel there is a grocery store, a cafe. In the latter, you can eat cheaply.

Hostel Krasnogorsk "Elena" is located near the ski resort, so it is suitable for people who prefer to actively spend time outdoors. The hotel offers a special place where you can store your skis.

In the reviews, guests put more emphasis on the cleanliness of the rooms. The service is also top notch.

"Happy people"

The cost of a room in this hostel is 900 rubles and above. The hostel is located on Krasnogorsky Boulevard. This location is as convenient as possible. The hotel itself is cozy, inexpensive and comfortable. There is internet. Can be accommodated with pets. The hostel has 27 rooms. They are bright and have a modern style. The berth is comfortable. There are appliances and furniture that are necessary for a successful stay. There is a shared bathroom. The front desk is open all the time. The staff is always ready to help or resolve any issue. In the shared kitchen you can cook yourself a meal. If you do not want to, then this hostel in Krasnogorsk is located near bars, cafes and restaurants. Products can also be purchased at supermarkets, which are also nearby.

The hotel sells ski passes. In addition, a library is open. You can play bowling, tennis, board games. There are bicycle rentals. The administrator can organize horseback riding lessons as well as hiking. There is a playground for children. The staff speaks not only Russian, but English and French.

In the reviews, guests note that the hotel employees are polite and helpful. The rooms are spacious, bright. The beds are soft. Rest in this hostel is as pleasant as possible.

Myakinino

Beautiful hostel in Krasnogorsk, the minimum cost of a room in which is 500 rubles. This hotel has a wonderful setting. Guests arriving by car can leave them in the free parking lot. For guests there is Internet access. The rooms are both general (male and female), and individual. The rooms have heating and air conditioning, which allows you to keep the air temperature in optimal condition. The dormitory rooms on the beds have curtains that allow you to close your eyes. In addition, they practically do not let in sunlight. There is a shared kitchen where you can prepare your own dinner. You can purchase products in a store located nearby.

There is a shared lounge with a TV. Non-smoking rooms and families have private rooms. The rooms are cleaned every day.

In the reviews, guests note that the hostel reigns in a homely atmosphere. The beds are comfortable, the rooms are warm, the design in the kitchen is interesting. The administrator is a friendly person who will offer tea or coffee, as well as answer any questions.

Star myakinino

This hostel in Krasnogorsk is inexpensive - the cost per day is from 540 rubles. Next to it is a sports complex and an aquarium. Guests are offered an extensive list of services. For children on the TV tuned channels with cartoons. There is a parking where you can leave your car. All rooms are comfortable. They are made in warm colors. There is internet. The rooms have beds, wardrobes and other furniture. The hostel has a kitchen. It serves a delicious breakfast. If desired, you can go to cafes or restaurants.

There is an ATM on site. The rooms are isolated, noises from neighbors are not heard. It is possible to book a transfer from or to the airport.

The reviews write that the hostel is in a beautiful and quiet place. Nearby there are all the main attractions of the city. Value for money at an optimal level. The rooms are clean and large. Breakfast is served in the morning.

Crocus

Hostel Crocus in Krasnogorsk is inexpensive. The price per day starts from 600 rubles. The city center is 3 km from this hotel. There are 30 rooms, which are divided into six categories. Equipped shared kitchen. Guests can rent ski equipment. In addition, ski school passes are sold. The hotel also organizes hiking and board games. Nearby there is a museum and a church. Free parking is available. Shuttle services are available at an extra charge. The airport is 17 km from the hotel, and the train station is 14 km.

The rooms have heating. You can order food delivery to your room or buy ready-made packed lunches. At the request of the hotel can make a special diet menu. Free evening entertainment is provided. For children there is a playground on the street, a play area in the hotel. A children's buffet is open, as well as channels with cartoons in the hostel's TVs.

In the reviews, many write that the hostel is in good repair, friendly workers. The beds are comfortable, the sheets and towels are clean. Settlement is fast. The property offers tea or coffee at all times.

The kubz

The cost of living in this hostel is 800 rubles per day and above. Free internet, storage space for luggage and personal belongings is provided. Rooms are cleaned every day. The common living room has a TV. You can watch it with a big company. No smoking on site. In the kitchen you can warm up your own food; or you can go to the cafe.

The rooms have air conditioning and heating. There is a playground on the street. Family rooms are available. If you wish, you can use the laundry service, but at an additional cost.

The reviews write that the hostel is not noisy, comfortable to sleep. The price is optimal.

Summary

The article describes the most famous hostels of the city. They are comfortable to relax at any time of the year. The rooms are warm and comfortable. Most of them are located in a quiet place.
