Hotel Villa Mihajlovic (Budva, Montenegro): description, reviews

Do you want to spend your vacation on the beach of the Adriatic Sea, enjoy the quiet bays, delicious food and attend fun parties? Then Budva is what you need, because this city is considered the most beautiful and popular resort in Montenegro. The climate in Budva is Mediterranean, with warm summers and mild winters. The city has more than 100 various hotels, pensions and apartments.





About the city of Budva

Budva is one of the best seaside resorts, around which are located more than 35 clean and beautiful beaches. The city of Budva on a map of Montenegro has a special place, because it is here that the country's mass tourism is concentrated. It is from the beaches of the resort that there is access to the clear sea, and not to the bays, as in other resort places. The city has a lot of restaurants, cafes and bars where you can taste delicious European and traditional food. On average, a complex lunch will cost 5-9 euros per person, ice cream (1 ball) - 1-2 euros, bottle water 1.5 l - up to 0.7 euros. In Montenegro there is practically no industrial production, so here you can taste not only delicious, but also environmentally friendly fruits.





The city has a large number of attractions, so you will not be bored at the hotel. Budva district on the map of Montenegro is located in the central part of the Adriatic coast, where you can meet a combination of such picturesque landscapes as mountains and the sea. In Budva and near the city you can observe beautiful architecture and amazing sights, including:

  • ancient churches;
  • the most beautiful bay of Europe - the Bay of Kotor;
  • Dzhurdzhevich's bridge ;
  • Durmitor landscape park;
  • canyon of full-flowing Tara;
  • Moraca River Canyon;
  • Budva Old Town;
  • island of St. Nicholas and others.

In the city of Budva there are a large number of beauty salons, sports fields and tennis courts, health centers and massage parlors, thanks to which you can improve your health. This is where you can find nightclubs, discos and even casinos. The resort provides an opportunity for both a relaxing and rich, fun and active holiday. Quite often, various theatrical performances and music concerts take place in the city.





About the hotel

Villa Mihajlovic is located on the second line of the coast of Budva, about 700 m from the Old Town. The hotel welcomes guests from all over the world. The villa is well located in relation to pharmacies, restaurants, shops, markets, public transport and the promenade. From the windows of Villa Mihajlovic you can watch the quite busy street Jadra≈Ñski Put or nearby apartments, buried in verdure against the backdrop of formidable and at the same time mysterious mountain peaks. 250 meters from the hotel there is a municipal sand and pebble beach where you can find sun loungers, umbrellas and other services for an additional fee.

Villa Mihajlovic (Montenegro) is a quiet and comfortable 3-star hotel, which is perfect for convenient and comfortable accommodation. The rooms have all the conditions that allow you to relax and cook your favorite meal.





Hotel rooms

The hotel is designed in a modern style, where you can find wooden carved furniture and checkered rugs. The flooring is tiled. Hotel Villa Mmihajlovic has only 7 rooms, namely:

  • 4 double rooms;
  • 3 triple (it is possible to place an extra bed).

Regardless of the type of room, all rooms have French beds, air conditioning, cable TV, a small refrigerator, minibar, dishes, a set of cups and a kettle for hot drinks, a bathroom with a shower, Wi-Fi access. Double rooms have access to a private terrace with a table and chairs, where you can enjoy nature at any time. The bathroom has a separate bathroom, shower area, necessary towels, a hairdryer and shower cosmetics.

Hotel facilities

In Villa Mihajlovic 3 * (Montenegro, Budva) meals are not provided. However, due to the presence of a kitchen in many rooms, you can cook yourself or warm up ready-made food. Near the hotel there are a large number of restaurants and cafes serving European and traditional cuisine. In the market at a lower price, tourists have the opportunity to purchase vegetables and fruits. In Budva you can taste delicious seafood (octopuses, sea fish, shrimp, shellfish, etc.), prepared in a variety of ways.

Russian-speaking staff works at Villa Mihajlovic, so that you can easily clarify the necessary route or solve difficulties. Rooms are cleaned every day, and bed linen is replaced every 3, 5, 7, and 10th day, depending on the change of staff. Towels are changed every second day. Room rates also include laundry services. The hotel staff provides a high level of service.

About the sea

Villa Mihajlovic is a 7-minute walk from the nearest beach. Relaxing in Budva, you can enjoy the clear azure blue sea. The waters of the Adriatic Sea are saturated with healthy salts and minerals, which is why they are considered very healthy. For greater convenience, special showers are equipped on the beaches where you can rinse with fresh water. The cost of sun loungers on various beaches ranges from 10 to 20 euros per day. The water temperature is quite comfortable and in the summer reaches +27 degrees, and only occasionally cool currents appear. The Adriatic Sea in Montenegro is considered the cleanest in Europe. From water activities you can try:

  • diving (explore wrecks and the beauty of the underwater world);
  • boat and boat riding.
