Technology of Excellence: Karcher Steam Generator



Karcher is not just a brand, it is a household name. Saying "Karcher", all mean only one thing - high-level equipment for washing and cleaning.

Company goals

The line of products presented by the corporation may not be very large, but it deserves respect. They do not exchange for the release of well-selling technical devices, but they improve and work exclusively on what they consider their own business - on high-tech equipment for cleaning surfaces. So what is famous for the products of the German concern? Ease of use, build quality, ease of use, of course, with its positive yellow color. The Karcher technique is a bright ray of sunshine that can clean any dirt. For this, there are already mini-washers, vacuum cleaners, steam generators and much more, and the lineup consists of both compact technology and powerful units for cleaning large areas.





Steam generator

At this point in time, the steam generator is considered the most popular product of the company. Karcher, or rather the company's engineers, brought to life several variations on this subject. The first, of course, is a conventional device for cleaning rooms using hot steam. The essence of the development is that in a confined space, water boils, and steam at the command of your hand enters through the hose exactly to the place where you need to remove dirt. The best-selling instance of this group is the Karcher SC 1020 steam generator. It does not have many nozzles that can remove dirt in the places you need, but it saves money for those who do not need extra parts. Even with a standard set of elements, you will wash the floor, walls and furniture, clean the stove.

Ironing system

The second way that engineers propose using a steam generator is with a Karcher iron. The fantastic abilities of the steam supplied under pressure will smooth out any things. German foresight prompted engineers to attach the iron to the main unit. As a result, using the same device, simply changing the nozzles, you can clean the surfaces and iron clothes. Moreover, an ironing board connecting the functions of an iron was added to the attached devices. Roughly speaking, ironing takes place from two sides: both from the side of the iron and from the side of the board. The ironing system - the Karcher SI 2125 steam generator perfectly copes with this task. Please note that not all nozzles can be attached to such steam generators.



Steam cleaner

The third most unusual use of steam is a vacuum cleaner. The imagination of enterprising Germans here was able to find application to this device. And in apartments and offices, units are moving proudly that can cleanse pollution with hot steam at a time, collect the moisture left after cleaning, and vacuum it. Compact wiper

Well, the last thing to say is the wiper. A small compact device will help to clean mirrors and windows without chemicals and prolonged rubbing. Model SC 952 will perfectly cope with this task. This Karcher steam generator, the price of which is about 3,000 rubles, is considered one of the best-selling, as it perfectly combines the minimum set of functions necessary for cleaning a small apartment.
