The magnificent combination of the calm warm Black Sea and the centuries-old forest that descends to the beach along the mountain rapids is what attracts the village of Nebug. Holidays, reviews of which you will be pleased, will be perfect for any category of tourists, from lovers of peace and quiet to those who prefer outdoor activities or extreme water sports. The main reason why the inhabitants of the country visited this year the village of Nebug is vacation 2013. An important advantage of this resort village is its excellent beaches, which stretch along the coast for 15 kilometers. They mainly consist of well-rolled pebbles and a small amount of sand. The coastal area along the beach line is cut with ravines, valleys and beams. The most popular among the guests of the village is the Central Beach, which is located between the Sea Club Hotel and the Yamal Beach. Its length is 500 meters and its width reaches 50 meters. The coastal bottom smoothly goes into the depth, so many families with children prefer it. In addition to these advantages, its main advantage is its proximity to the center of the village, which is located just a 10-minute walk.



But this is not the only beach, which is famous for the village of Nebug. Holidays, reviews of which confirm all its advantages, can be spent on a private beach, where there are significantly fewer visitors. Guests of the village give the most preference to the closed beach of OK Prometey. It is well equipped. Shaded canopies were installed, a sports field was equipped . There are a number of water activities, and a boat is provided for a walk. Equally attractive is the closed beach of the Yamal Lightning complex, which is 200 meters long and 20 meters wide. It is ideal for families with children, as there is a well-equipped playground. In addition, there is a cafe, there is a barbecue, sauna, rental point.

But excellent beaches are not the only possible vacation on the Black Sea. Nebug offers its guests to visit their water park "Dolphin", which was opened in 1997. This is the greenest water park in all of Russia, and in its beauty and variety of plants, it can compete with the Arboretum in Sochi. The first thing that catches your eye in this beautiful place is the European service. There is a free car park, luggage storage, excellent restaurants. Its unique feature is that it works at night. Here begins a real extravaganza of light and water, which is created using the illumination of trees and laser lighting of the surrounding mountains.
