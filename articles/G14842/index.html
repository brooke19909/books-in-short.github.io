Nha Trang: excursions to one of the best Vietnamese resorts

One of the most famous resorts in Vietnam is Nha Trang. Many tourists are interested in excursions to this city, because there is really something to see in these places. The resort is located in the south of the country, it is notable for its developed infrastructure, picturesque landscapes, numerous entertainment facilities, opportunities for diving. Nha Trang has a very beautiful promenade along which it is pleasant to stroll in the evening, there are also many shops and restaurants, the numerous islands, the clear sea and snow-white beaches amaze with fabulous beauty.



A nice feature of the resort is the fact that almost all hotels are located in close proximity to the beach. Tourists only need to cross the road, and they will immediately find themselves near the sea. Nha Trang has many interesting sights. City tours are most popular among holidaymakers. Tourists will visit the Institute of Oceanology, where 23 species of marine fauna live in 23 aquariums, and also look at the Long Sean Buddhist Pagoda. Not a single sightseeing tour is complete without visiting an art gallery, a trip to the Hindu towers of Ponagar, and Hong Tieng Park.



For lovers of beach holidays and water activities, Nha Trang is also provided for attractions. Excursions to the islands of the bay will not leave anyone indifferent. At Hong Moon Island you can go diving and snorkeling, as well as paragliding, jet skiing. Here, tourists will be offered a seafood lunch, the tour usually ends with a visit to the aquarium. On the island of Hong Thi you can see deer and ostriches, relax on the beach, enjoy the waterfall. There are a lot of monkeys living on the island of Hon Lan, for fans of these restless animals an exciting circus performance was prepared by Nha Trang.

Excursions to the crocodile farm and Yangbai waterfall are always popular among tourists, because only here you can see the real life of ethnic minorities, look at the picturesque landscapes of green valleys and impenetrable jungle. You can swim in the waterfall if you wish, and then look at the performance organized by the locals and visit the crocodile farm, where the hosts will feed you delicious dishes of meat of ostriches and crocodiles, which Vietnam is famous for.



Nha Trang tours and prices are very affordable. Depending on the popularity of the place visited and the duration of the trip, the cost of participation of one person can be from 85 to 175 dollars, but traveling in groups of 3-4 people is much more profitable - from 40 to 85 dollars per trip. Excursions in Nha Trang are not only informative, but also healthy. Reviews of tourists about thermal springs and a river cruise are only positive. Here you can look at ordinary Vietnamese villages, wander among the coconut thickets. And thermal springs and mud baths will have an antibacterial and restorative effect on the whole body. A trip to Nha Trang will leave only the best memories. Having been here once, you will want to return again and again.
