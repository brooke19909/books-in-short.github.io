Hotel Victoria, Tuapse: overview, description and reviews of tourists

Tuapse is a cozy resort town with a very well-developed tourist infrastructure. As a vacation destination, it is annually chosen by thousands of Russians. Of course, one of the indispensable conditions for a good holiday is comfortable housing. There are many good hotels in Tuapse. For example, very often vacationers book rooms at the Victoria Hotel.

Hotel Location Features

This hotel is located in Tuapse on the first coastline. The distance from the sea to the hotel is only about 100 m. Walking distance, thus, the beach can be reached in just 4-5 minutes. Many tourists believe that this object has the following address - Hotel Victoria, Tuapse, Gagarina 37. This is not entirely correct. On the official website of the hotel it is indicated that it is located on the street. Gagarina, 38. The city center can be reached from the hotel in just 5 minutes.





In the immediate vicinity of the hotel is the mouth of the Tuapse River. The seaport of the city, the bus station and the railway station are located approximately at the same distance from the hotel (800-900 m). 30 km from the hotel is the popular village of Lazarevskoye (Sochi).

You can get to the hotel from any of the stations by public transport or taxi. Also, many tourists book a transfer at the hotel. This option, of course, is the most convenient.

general description

The Victoria Hotel (Tuapse) is a modern two-story building with an attic and a spacious terrace on the ground floor. The hotel reception is open 24 hours a day. Residents can check into the reserved rooms at 12 noon. You need to leave the hotel at the end of the stay at 10 hours. The hotel rooms are non-smoking. You can book accommodation by phone +7 (988) 5004554 or on the official website of the hotel. In the latter case, not only the necessary category of the room and the number of places, but also wishes should be entered in the form. You can use electronic money or a credit card to pay for your reservation.





Hotel Rooms

Hotel Victoria (Tuapse) offers a total of six categories of comfortable rooms to the guests of the city. If desired, here you can remove:

  • four-seater "Standard" of the increased comfort;

  • Prestige Room

  • Vostochny Suite;

  • Paris Suite

  • regular triple "Standard".

Offers tourists the hotel "Victoria" (Tuapse, Gagarina, 38) including rooms for business travelers or sports groups with several individual beds.





In addition to wardrobes, beds and nightstands, the hotel rooms are equipped with:

  • coffee tables with chairs;

  • soft corners;

  • split systems;

  • Modern flat-screen TVs

  • refrigerators.

In each room, among other things, there are bedside rugs. A shower and a bathroom in the Victoria Hotel are in all rooms without exception. Of course, for the availability of such facilities, the Victoria Hotel (Tuapse) also received excellent reviews from tourists.

Opinion of residents about the number of rooms

Reviews about the rooms in this hotel among tourists are also good. Most vacationers believe that the rooms at the Victoria Hotel are clean and comfortable. Maids in the apartments are cleaned often. Bed linen is also changed at sufficient intervals in this hotel. Household appliances in the hotel rarely breaks. If problems nevertheless arise, their staff tries to solve as quickly as possible.

Very comfortable consider vacationers and beds in this hotel. The mattresses here are soft and new. The same goes for blankets and pillows.

Hotel "Victoria" (Tuapse), thus - a place to stay quite comfortable. Dissatisfaction with its guests express mainly only for the presence of cockroaches in the rooms. Of course, there are not too many of these insects in the hotel (most of the guests do not even notice them), but they still exist. Of course, in this regard, the hotel owners should take certain measures.

Infrastructure

The Victoria Hotel has earned good reviews from tourists, including for the availability of various additional services. For example, on-site guests can use WiFi for free. Moreover, according to many former residents of the hotel, the Internet speed in the building is quite acceptable.





Tourists can have fun at the hotel by playing darts. There is no large pool in the courtyard of the Victoria Hotel. This, of course, is not too convenient. But the hotel has a bath with a steam room and a small pool. Also in the courtyard of the hotel there is a barbecue.

Works on the territory of the Victoria Hotel and its own tour desk. If necessary, its staff can help guests with planning trips to interesting places in Tuapse and its environs.

Parking at the hotel has its own. Due to its presence, many upon arrival home write on the Internet a good review about the Victoria Hotel in Tuapse. Guests can leave a car in the hotel parking lot for free.

Reviews of tourists about the infrastructure and service

Of course, the Victoria Hotel (Tuapse) offers not so many additional services to its guests. But the set that is available, many tourists consider it quite sufficient. After all, the cost of living in this hotel is lower than in many other Tuapse hotels.

In particular, good reviews of guests on the network are available about the steam room of this guest house. She has a very large area, and it was built from environmentally friendly, emitting useful ether compounds, lindens when heated.

The attitude of the staff towards their guests at this hotel is also widely praised. Absolutely all hotel staff behave politely and courteously with guests. If necessary, the tourist can be relocated, for example, to another, more convenient room.





Dining at the hotel

In addition, the hotel has a restaurant "Victoria". The food reviews for this hotel on the web are also mostly good. The hotel's restaurant serves Caucasian, Russian and European dishes. Breakfast, tourists, if desired, can be ordered directly to the room. The choice of dishes in the hotel restaurant, judging by the reviews of former guests on the Internet, is very good. At the same time, the cooks of the hotel are really good. There are no complaints from tourists regarding the amount of food served for breakfast, lunch or dinner.

Hotel beach

In order to get to the seashore, guests need to walk, as already mentioned, about 100 meters. For this, including the deserving of a good hotel "Victoria" (Tuapse) reviews from tourists. Directly opposite this hotel is the public main city beach. Swimming in this place is quite convenient. The coastline here is covered with pebbles and small stones, and the descent into the sea is gentle. Judging by the reviews of tourists, there are usually not too many people on the city beach of Tuapse. The sea itself in this place is quite clean.





The infrastructure on the city beach of Tuapse is very good. For the convenience of vacationers, beautiful dressing rooms are installed here. Sunbeds and umbrellas can be rented at some places on the beach. Their cost, like everywhere else on the Black Sea, is not too high (about 100 rubles).

Of the inconveniences of this beach, many guests note only the presence of a rather large number of smokers. For the rest, many tourists respond positively to this place on the seashore.

City infrastructure near the hotel

Of course, tourists who choose the Victoria 2 * hotel (Tuapse) to stay, have the opportunity to use all the amenities offered by the city to vacationers. On the way from the hotel to the beach, for example, there are many stalls where vacationers can purchase the necessary bathing accessories, T-shirts, sunglasses, souvenirs, etc. All kinds of excursions are sold on the promenade.

There are many cafes and restaurants near the hotel. If you wish, you can not have too expensive lunch or dinner.





What you should know about

The hotel with the name Victoria in Tuapse is not the only one. Therefore, when collecting information about this hotel, if you want to book a room, you should be more attentive. For example, there is a hotel with the same name in the village, located next to Tuapse - Lermontovo. Hotel "Victoria", hosting tourists in this village, offers a much larger number of services than considered by us in the article. For example, tourists can leave their child with a nanny here, use beach equipment for rent, etc. Of course, the cost of living at this Victoria hotel is more expensive.
