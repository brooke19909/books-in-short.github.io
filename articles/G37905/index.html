Start

Before you start playing the Imaginarium, the rules of which are described here, you need to prepare a game deck. To do this, you need to determine the number of cards. After counting the right number of pictures, postpone the extra ones. For four players (minimum number) 96 cards are needed, for five 75 cards are needed, for six - 82, and for seven players (maximum number) - 98. It is not logical, then these are the rules! Now everyone should choose a chip and cards of the same color, necessary for voting. There are only seven sets in the game, and if, for example, five players play, then extra chips and cards need to be removed.



First move

The board game "Imaginarium" (the rules are presented here) does not imply any rivalry, but still it is necessary to choose a leader who will make up the first associations. The authors of the game themselves offer this method: take voting cards and select one at random, turn it over and check with other players. The leader is the one with the largest figure in the picture. But this is not necessary, and you can choose the player making the first move at your discretion.

Now the leader should choose one of his pictures, make an association on it and put it face down on the table. Additional tasks

Some fields on the map are marked with special icons, and the presenter, who fell on such a cell, must take into account some restrictions. If the chip hit the cloud with the number 4, then the association should consist of 4 words. Once on the field with the image of the TV, the player must come up with an explanation related to the film, series, cartoon and so on. For a field with an interesting Abibas logo, you need to come up with an association associated with a brand - it can be a slogan or an excerpt from advertising and so on. The final

You can play the Imaginarium indefinitely. The rules of the game assume the finale as soon as the cards in the deck run out. In this case, the winner will be the one who moved forward along the field as much as possible. But if you wish, you can always shuffle the deck and continue the adventure. And if one of the elephants reaches the last cloud, then you can send it to the next round - it all depends on the desire of the players.
