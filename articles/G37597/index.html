Marriott Grand Hotel (Moscow): description, address, reviews

More and more tourists are eager to visit Moscow, the capital of the Russian Federation. Numerous theaters, museums, the magnificent architectural ensemble of the Red Square - here, the city guests will find many interesting and informative. Of course, I would like to stay closer to the center of the metropolis. Refined and comfortable conditions are offered to you by the Marriott Grand Hotel, at the intersection of Tverskaya and Pushkinskaya streets.

Description

The hotel has a five star category. From here, in just five minutes, you will reach the Tverskaya metro station, from where you quickly get to Red Square. Within walking distance from the Marriott Grand Hotel are the theaters of Satire and Lenkom, a concert hall named after P.I. Tchaikovsky.

Also in the immediate vicinity are administrative and cultural institutions, monuments of architecture and art. Here you can rent a car, use the guarded parking lot. Upon request, guests leave their car in a paid guarded underground garage. A cash machine is installed on site, boutiques, a souvenir shop, a newsstand, and a currency exchange are working. Wireless internet access is available for free. It features a pool and wellness facilities. There is an elevator. Facilities for disabled guests are provided. Two restaurants of the hotel present modern European cuisine. The Marriott Grand Hotel in Moscow is located at 26/1 Tverskaya Street.

Accommodation

For accommodation, guests are offered several categories of rooms:

  • Deluxe with a large double or twin beds - 10 800 rubles per day.
  • Superior with two double beds - from 12,000 rubles per day.
  • Deluxe "Executive" with a large double or two double beds - from 13,200 rubles per day.
  • Studio Suite with two double beds - 28 800 rubles per day.

At the request of the guests, the price of breakfast can be included in the price of 2000 rubles. All rooms at the Marriott Grand Hotel Moscow are equipped with air conditioning, TV, fridge, telephone, safe, ironing facilities, desk, free toiletries. Rooms with high ceilings, soundproofed windows.
