general information

This hotel is perfect for a budget holiday for those who on vacation do not forget about their physical condition. This category of tourists can usefully spend time in a small Oriole Hotel Spa 2 *.

This two-star spa hotel is located in the center of Nha Trang, on the second line from the sea, five minutes from the beach. There are many attractions around, including the famous ancient towers of Po Nagar, which are only a kilometer away. Another advantage of the hotel is the proximity of the city airport, which is only forty minutes away by car. You can walk to the center of Nha Trang in just ten minutes at a leisurely pace. Tram Huong Tower, a market, a cathedral and a sailing club are also nearby.

The hotel was built in 2009, but in 2014 it was overhauled. He has no territory.

Hotel Policies

The following rules apply in Oriole Hotel Spa 2 *: check-in at two in the afternoon, check-out at noon. There is no need to pay for children under the age of eleven if they are accommodated in a room with adults without an extra bed. However, the child is not entitled to a free breakfast. Pets are not allowed.

The hotel does not provide a crib or cot, as well as high chairs in the restaurant upon request. Internet in Oriole Hotel Spa 2 * is free both in public areas and in rooms.



Beach

From the hotel the beach is three hundred meters distant. You can reach it in just three minutes.
