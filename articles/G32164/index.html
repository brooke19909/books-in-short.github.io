Cheget is a ski resort. Cheget height, resort description, vacationers reviews

Mount Cheget is located in the Republic of Kabardino-Balkaria and is included in the mountain system of the Caucasus. It is one of the peaks of the Elbrus region, and the height of Cheget is almost 3650 meters. Let's find out a little about the resort and the slopes of this amazing mountain.

Cheget - ski resort

Snow covers Mount Elbrus four months of the year: from December to March. However, some routes can be used from the end of November until May. At other times of the year, tourists are attracted to this wonderful place by stunning beauty of nature and, of course, life-giving mountain air. Travel agencies offer a variety of travel dates: from a weekend to three weeks. Especially popular are the New Year and Christmas holidays. When purchasing a ticket, be sure to check with the tour operator what is included in its price. Usually this includes: delivery from the airport, hotel accommodation, two or three meals a day, and a mountain lift subscription. Many tour operators include tours of Elbrus, as well as sightseeing tours. Cheget is a ski resort that attracts tourists with its location: just a few meters from it there is a cable car, along which you can reach the ski slopes.

Skiing

Cheget is the most popular place for those who can not imagine life without skiing, because this is the concentration of the best special roads that are not inferior in quality to European ones. It is not for nothing that the Cheget tracks are known to skiers as one of the most difficult in the classification. By the number of fifteen, elevation changes from 2100 to 3050 meters. In total, there are four lifts and three lines. And although the tracks are not equipped with snow cannons and are completely dependent on the weather, they seem to take care of themselves. This is evidenced by the hillocks formed on steep sections, which settle overnight, providing conditions for a descent. On the diagrams you can see the signs of the tracks: from easy to difficult. However, skiers argue that these signs are very arbitrary, and an easy trail can be difficult. The mountain is equipped with a viewing platform with a cafe at an altitude of 2719 meters.

What Cheget offers to vacationers

As already noted, the slopes of this ski resort are quite steep and complex. That is why it has become a tradition to hold the annual Russian Freeride Championship here. A large number of hillocks for the pier is not an obstacle for skiers. But for snowboarders, they turn into a problem. However, there are also equipped jumps for them on Mount Cheget. The ski resort offers its vacationers a new type of entertainment called Heli-ski. It involves a descent from a hill from which no one has yet rolled down. Those who wish are delivered there by helicopter. By the way, in European countries this type of entertainment is prohibited, as it is considered dangerous to life and health. But the European tourists themselves are attracted by Mount Cheget, the Elbrus region attracts them with a fabulous welcome, optimal prices and, of course, high-quality tracks.

sights

What, besides the tracks, can surprise Cheget vacationers? The ski resort usually offers sightseeing tours. Tourists should definitely visit the Hood Mountain - from its top a magnificent view of the Elbrus region opens, from which it is simply breathtaking. Do not leave indifferent skiers and springs of Jila-Su. Here, two powerful springs of mineral water come out, and nearby are many more small ones. Sources have different composition of mineral water, which means that the healing effect of it is not the same everywhere.
