The most interesting cities in China

The People's Republic of China is unusually interesting and diverse. This country combines the wonders of technological progress and the splendor of nature. Healing medicine of China is known all over the world, Chinese cuisine is loved by people in almost all countries, and the whole world uses products manufactured at local factories.



The most famous cities of China attract many tourists with their size, dynamism and exoticism. The megacities in China are really huge. Often the population exceeds tens of millions of people. The huge anthill cities consist of tall skyscrapers, and their area is so large that they are visible from space.

Describing the cities of China, the list of which is huge, it is best to start with Beijing. The capital of the country is not the most ancient city, but according to the style and dynamism of life, it can be called one of the most interesting for tourists to visit. The landscape and architecture of the city were greatly changed by the Olympics, the preparation of which was carried out here in accordance with the usual scale. Here you can find both ultra-modern skyscrapers, as symbols of a developed and more advanced civilization, as well as ancient pagodas made in the traditional Chinese style. The presence of buildings that reflect the history, culture and spirituality of the development of the nation suggests that even with the rhythm of modern life, local residents are drawn to their roots.



The main attractions of the main city of China include the Gugun Palace Complex, the Temple of Heaven and the famous Chinese Wall. A walk along it should be an obligatory point of the excursion program. In addition, in the capital there are a large number of Buddhist temples, which anyone will be interested in seeing.

Continuing to describe the cities of China, the list of which is huge, let us dwell on Hong Kong. It is a dedicated administrative district, the territory of which occupies the peninsula. The metropolis is washed by the seas on three sides, the climate in it is subtropical, quite hot.

When visiting this city of China, not a single tourist misses a point such as a visit to the largest Buddha statue. The huge monument, 34 meters high, is amazing in its size. Behind the statue is one of the best and most popular beaches where you can relax from the bustle of the city.

In addition, in Hong Kong there are many interesting places. Original souvenirs and purchases can be made on Hollywood Road, there is a maximum concentration of antique shops and souvenir shops, as well as shops with clothes, interior items and decorations. Nearby is the harbor, in which thousands of people live in their fishing boats. This spectacle is very striking and makes you think about a lot. In Hong Kong, you can also visit the local Avenue of Stars, the Aquarium, Disneyland and the unique in beauty show "Symphony of Lights."



The unique eastern country is fraught with many interesting things. A map of China with cities, upon first inspection, makes it clear that a single visit here is unlikely to be enough.
