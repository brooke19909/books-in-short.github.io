External data and equipment

Smartphone Asus, that of the first generation, that of the second, despite the increase in performance, characteristics and other parameters, it still remains a "heavyweight" in the physical plane. The 5-inch screen, as some reviews write, occupies only 70% of the front side. At the same time, the body, made in the form of a boat, does not reduce the phone when viewed from the side. In the widest part, the thickness reaches 11 mm. And the most serious minus that can push away the buyer - the Asus smartphone does not have a removable battery. And this drawback of the first generation is not fixed in the second. It has a removable back cover, slots for microsymbol (reduced SIM) and memory cards, but there is no possibility to replace the battery. Also the minuses include the weight of the device. The complete set of the device is completely in Asus style - there are even headphones in the box with the phone.
