Karaoke club "Territory" in Khimki: photos and reviews

Club "Territory" in Khimki is an institution that is visited by dozens of guests every day who prefer fun and delicious food. Often on social networks there are a lot of reviews left by visitors to the "Territory", which say that there is a wonderful atmosphere of warmth, comfort and eternal friendship.

general information

This institution was opened in 2007. Since then, a huge number of guests have gathered in its walls every evening, who value not only the internal atmosphere, but also local food prepared by a team of professional chefs with great culinary experience.

Club "Territory" (Khimki) refers to a considerable network of institutions of the same name, which are located throughout Moscow. Each of them is very popular in its area, since the guests, during the first visit to the institution, are satisfied with its general concept, due to which they often remain regular customers.



Location

Many guests who visit the Territory club in Khimki note that it is located in a place with excellent transport links to other areas of the capital. Near the entrance to the institution there is a public transport stop, where guests of the institution often come on buses and trolleybuses.

Club "Territory" address: Khimki, Rodionova street, 2, 2nd floor.



Interior

Inside, everything is decorated in a fashionable style. The overall picture of the interior is represented by an original combination of black, gray and white colors, which create an atmosphere of ease. Here on smooth walls you can see white inscriptions and small images. On one of them you can observe the original murals with the image of famous American stars from the 50-60s.

Guests, sitting on comfortable sofas and armchairs with beige cloaks, behind square wooden tables, can observe a beautiful view of the city street, which opens from large windows to the entire wall.



As a decor, several compact shelves are installed in the hall, on which are placed books and indoor plants, the greenery of which visually makes the interior of the hall more fresh. There is also a large LCD TV, which often broadcasts the most interesting events from the world of show business, fashion and sports.

Kitchen

The menu of the Territory restaurant-club (Khimki) presents a large selection of dishes prepared in the best traditions of European cuisine. There is also a considerable amount of Japanese rolls and sushi. Guests visiting the restaurant are offered a good assortment of snacks, including cold ones (assorted, Parisian appetizer, Greek eggplant rolls, Vodka herring, Tataki cucumber noodles, shrimp chips), and hot (suluguni bars, squid rings, chicken wings, beer shrimps, mini-barbecue "Thai"). In some salads, only light dishes are presented (New York, Greek, Favorite Territory, Dang Ing, Bin Tang, Caesar with chicken or tiger shrimps).

As for hot dishes, among them there are several options for meat steaks, which are very popular with visitors, as well as fish, grilled and steamed. In addition, the institution offers a small assortment of pasta prepared according to traditional Italian recipes ("Bolognese", "Carbonara", with seafood).

Fans of sweet food at Territory Club (Khimki) can be pleased with the presence of a large number of desserts (Muara, Tiramisu, Absolute, Three Chocolates, raspberry Cheesecake, Truffle cake, fruit platter, apple strudel )



Bar

The bar menu of the restaurant is represented by a large selection of alcoholic drinks and cocktails, which are prepared by experienced and talented bartenders. If desired, guests can spend time at the contact bar, which is installed in a large room, and drink real works of art. They can offer a large selection of wine and champagne, as well as vodka, whiskey, cognac, tequila, rum, brandy, sambuca and several options for aperitifs, which are also popular.



If we talk about soft drinks, then guests prefer to order local milkshakes, home-made lemonades, freshly squeezed juice, fruit drinks, as well as sparkling water produced by world-famous companies. Visitors also have the opportunity to choose tea and coffee to their liking - there are also a lot of hot drinks on the menu.



Mode of operation

The institution welcomes guests on any day of the week from noon to five in the morning (on Fridays and Saturdays the club closes an hour later). If desired, guests can reserve a table for themselves on a specific day of the week and time. The administration recommends this to those who wish to visit the institution on the evening of Saturday or Friday, when the institution hosts the most noisy parties, more massive than on other days. Photo reports from the Territory club (Khimki) made during such events can convey only part of all the fun that reigns in the bar at such moments.
