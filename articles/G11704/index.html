Hotel Wela 4 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria): photos, reviews

Our tourists discovered Bulgaria during the times of the Soviet Union. Young people came here to all kinds of congresses; here, children rested in international pioneer camps. Now tourism in Bulgaria is even more developed, dozens of new hotels have been built, ideal conditions for recreation of all categories of citizens have been created. The comfortable hotel Wela 4 * enjoys well-deserved popularity. Sunny Beach - the resort where it is built, is known worldwide for its golden sands, wonderful sea and well-developed infrastructure. Let's take a virtual walk around the hotel and see how it fits into the general atmosphere of carelessness and celebration prevailing on the sunny Bulgarian coast.

Location

In a wonderful place, Hotel Wela 4 * was built. Bulgaria (Sunny Beach - its oldest and most famous resort in the world) is located in the southeast of Europe and is washed by the waters of the Black Sea. In the eastern part of the country, in the region of Burgas, near the town of Nessebar, the resort Sunny Beach is located. By central street, it is unofficially divided into the northern part, more comfortable and beautiful, and the southern, from a tourist point of view, not very interesting.

Wela 4 * is located in the northern part, which is very convenient for a good rest. Indeed, it is in the north of the resort that the best bars, nightclubs, cafes, shops, a water park and other entertainment facilities are concentrated. The nearest city to the hotel and the resort, to which you can even walk for 40 minutes if you wish, is Nessebar. You can also get to it by regular buses, which run in season with a frequency of less than 10 minutes. Hotel guests can reach the southern part of the resort by a sightseeing summer tram.

How to get there

It‚Äôs not difficult to get to Wela * from Russia and any other European country. Sunny Beach is located just 40 km from Burgas, the main city in the region. Here is the second largest airport in Bulgaria with the same name airport. There are flights from major Russian cities here in season. Flight time from Moscow is less than 3 hours (depending on the airline). The most convenient way to get from the airport to the hotel is by taxi. It will cost from 30 euros. I would like to warn that taxi drivers in Sunny Beach in Bulgaria are slightly higher than even in the capital, therefore, when ordering a taxi, it is better to use the services of official companies, rather than private owners. Another convenient way to get there is a pre-ordered transfer. It is also paid, but the price is fixed. The only drawback is that the transfer along the way can bring tourists to other hotels, so the road instead of 10 minutes stretches for half an hour. The third option is to travel by regular bus, the ticket for which costs only 1 lev, but it will only take you to the nearest stop from the hotel, and then you have to get there on your own.



Description of the Territory

From a distance it attracts the views of Wela 4 * (Sunny Beach). The photo shows a stylish five-story building made of glass and concrete structures made in sunny yellow and beige colors. The hotel area is not very large, but there was a place to harmoniously place a pool, sports and playgrounds, a cafe and a parking lot. All infrastructure objects are decorated with dozens of flower beds, many deciduous trees, figuredly trimmed Christmas trees and pines. Alleys are laid everywhere, benches are arranged, and thematic sectors are separated from each other by evergreen hedges. Guests who do not want to leave the territory can relax here with great comfort.

Infrastructure

The most diverse, taking into account different tastes, pastime is able to offer guests Wela 4 *. Sunny Beach was created for a carefree vacation and an unforgettable experience, and the hotel is a miniature resort. It was built in 2012, that is, everything here is new and perfectly functioning. On the territory there are many facilities for leisure activities, and approximately in the center stands the only high-rise building - a sleeping building. The reception is located on its ground floor, in a bright, always creating a positive mood lobby. It works around the clock. The staff speak Russian. Here, in the lobby there is a shop and a beauty salon, a lobby bar, a billiard room, a fitness club, laundry and ironing. At the reception, tourists can exchange currency, check in their luggage, leave valuables in a safe, call a taxi, order excursions and tickets, call a doctor, order a nanny for their baby.



Rooms

Only 250 rooms of different categories and capacities are offered for accommodation by Wela 4 * (Sunny Beach). Reviews about them are only favorable, but when booking you need to consider where the windows go. So, for those guests who plan to sleep peacefully at night, it is advisable to take rooms from which the view is opposite to the pool side. But in the rooms with windows overlooking the pool, music and fun will be heard almost until dawn. You can also hope for peace and silence in the rooms located at the ends of the building. The bathroom, which is also spacious, is decorated with light tile, has a bathtub, washbasin and toilet. All bathing and washing facilities are provided.

Nutrition

The hotel Wela 4 * (Sunny Beach, Bulgaria) feeds its guests tasty, hearty and plentiful . Reviews about the organization of serving dishes and the menu are very good. The hotel infrastructure has a large central restaurant with a compartment inside the building and an open terrace. Type of food - buffet. For breakfast, tables are served with multiple slices of vegetables, sausages, cheeses, there are trays with cooked vegetables, side dishes, fruits, sweets, dairy products. There are vending machines in the lobby where you can take juices and various drinks, as well as coffee and tea, as much as you like.

For lunch, guests are offered two or three kinds of soups, side dishes, familiar to everyone and exclusive salads, dishes from the Bulgarian cuisine, desserts.

For dinner, the menu is about the same, but without soups, but often there is a barbecue.

In addition, in Wela 4 *, guests can have pizza, sandwiches during the day, and ice cream is offered for an afternoon snack.

The hotel has three bars. One in the lobby, the second near the pool, and the third in the hall of the night club, where discos are held.



Leisure for adults

Each guest can find for themselves something interesting in Wela 4 *. Sunny Beach provides an opportunity to have a bright and fun time, but there is always something to do on the hotel territory. Its highlight is the pool with a rather large mirror of the water surface, filled with mineral water. There are always plenty of comfortable sun loungers and umbrellas around. With active and not very tourists, groovy animators spend morning exercises, aerobics, funny contests, comic games in the morning.

For those who are friends with sports, the hotel has football, basketball and volleyball courts, a tennis court, a place for playing darts and squash, and even a huge chessboard with chess almost the size of a man was built.

The hotel infrastructure has a good spa with massage rooms, a bath, a sauna, and fitness.

The guests are not bored by the perky team of animators arranging professional concerts, shows, and interchangeable scenes.



Service for children

In Wela 4 * (Sunny Beach), parents with children are comfortable and pleasant. For children, there is a separate pool on the territory, a children's playground decorated with an evergreen hedge. A mini club with toys, books, paints, albums is equipped in the building. There are even walkers for the smallest. Parents can request cots in rooms. The restaurant offers dishes suitable for babies.

Sea

Beaches are the main attraction of Sunny Beach resort. Wela 4 * separates from the coast 250 meters, if you go straight through the territory of the private casino "Platinum", and 350 meters, if you move around the path. The beach is divided into two sectors - paid, where you need to leave 8 leva for 2 sunbeds and an umbrella, and free, where you can freely sit on your own litter. The paid sector has clean sand, fine, skin-friendly. Entering the water here, it is gently sloping, as on the Sea of ‚Äã‚ÄãAzov, and you need to go about 100 meters to a depth. Algae are rarely washed ashore, jellyfish are almost never found. Entertainment on the beach is classic - banana, tablet, jet ski, parasailing. All this is for the money. Near the beach, there are dozens of outlets selling beach accessories, souvenirs, food, drinks, clothes, shoes.



Additional Information

For different categories of vacationers, the Wela 4 * hotel is excellently suited. Bulgaria, Sunny Beach (its most famous Black Sea resort) are constantly improving their tourism business. Today in Sunny Beach there is a lot of entertainment for large and small - a water park, to which hotel guests can walk, an amusement park, many shops and nightclubs. And for those tourists who want to get deeper acquainted with the history and traditions of Bulgaria, there is the opportunity to make excursions to neighboring Nessebar. On the territory of Wela 4 * it is also noisy and fun, but guests looking for silence are provided with rooms away from the objects of evening and night entertainment.

Check-in is carried out mainly after 14-00, who arrived earlier, you have to wait in the wings.

Prices for tours depend on the season, type of room and tour operator.

For children under 2 years of age, payment is not taken.

Pets are not allowed to settle.
