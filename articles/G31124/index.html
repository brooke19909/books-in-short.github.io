The choice of a debit card begins with an analysis of banks and their possible offers. Debit card costs



To issue a debit card, some banks may charge a monthly fee, or it may be a certain percentage for the issue. Depending on the validity period of the card, its functions and capabilities, the number of discounts offered and the category of customers, a price is set by a particular bank. For example, to issue a regular Sberbank card, you will need an amount in the region of 300 rubles, but for a card of the same Alfa-Bank (Cosmopolitan), the price for the issue starts from 600 rubles and above.

Obligatory payments

After it becomes clear what the debit card is for, what it is and what types it happens, you need to understand the necessary payments that the bank may charge, namely:

- annual payment for finished services;

However, these sanctions do not apply to all debit cards. Some financial institutions only charge a fee for the issue, and then the client can manage their funds as they like, without paying extra money.
