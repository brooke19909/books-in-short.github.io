The semiconductor heart of a smartphone

A very powerful processor solution is used in Highscreen Hercules. Reviews add to this that this smartphone without any problems copes with almost any task, including the most difficult one. There is nothing surprising. The gadget uses the MT6592 CPU developed by Mediatek. It immediately includes 8 computing modules. On the one hand, they are based on the A7 architecture. Its distinctive feature is a high degree of energy efficiency. But, on the other hand, the frequency of each module can reach a maximum load of 2 GHz. So it turns out that in this processor energy efficiency and performance are harmoniously combined. Therefore, even the most demanding software today on this device will go without the slightest hitch (including the most recent three-dimensional toys).
