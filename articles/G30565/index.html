Hotel Fiesta Beach 4 * (Tunisia, Djerba): photo and reviews of tourists

Tunisia is considered the most suitable replacement for Egypt, whose popularity has declined markedly over the past few years. You can come here all year long. And tourists are attracted by soft sandy beaches, a high level of service and quality entertainment. Travelers who are looking for the best combination of price and quality often stay at the Fiesta Beach 4 * hotel (Tunisia, Djerba). But is it worth choosing it as a place to relax?

Location and basic hotel information

The tourist complex was built in 1984 right on the coast of the island of Djerba. On its territory is the main building and several bungalows, which are designed to accommodate several families. All buildings are made in the Arabic style. In total, the complex has 370 rooms and only 20 of them are located in the main hotel building. Most of the tourists come here from France, Germany and Russia, so the complex has several employees who are fluent in Russian. Check in at the hotel Fiesta Beach 4 * (Tunisia) starts at noon. It allows children of all ages to stay, but pets will not work. Their appearance at the hotel is strictly prohibited.





The hotel is on the first beach line. A private beach is about 100 meters away. The nearest town, the city of Midoun, is 5 km away from the complex. You can get to it by taxi, which takes you to the resort center in just 10 minutes. There tourists can find many restaurants, bars, shops and souvenir shops. Djerba Island International Airport is 25 km away.

Housing and room facilities

Most of the rooms of the Fiesta Beach Club 4 * hotel (Tunisia) are located in small cozy bungalows built in oriental style. The complex has 370 rooms. All of them are standard apartments designed for two people and a child. Their area is about 16 square meters. There are non-smoking rooms in the hotel. Three rooms are fully adapted for guests with reduced mobility. Rooms feature small covered balconies or terraces. They overlook the garden or pool.





Also, all apartments are equipped with the following amenities to ensure a comfortable stay at the hotel:

  • Central air conditioning. It is mainly used in the summer to reduce the temperature in the rooms.
  • An electronic safe for storing valuables, documents and money. Guests of the hotel Fiesta Beach 4 * (Tunisia) can use it for free.
  • A TV with satellite channels. Among them there are Russian channels.
  • Direct telephone. International calls are only available at an additional cost.
  • Mini bar. It is provided free of charge. Replenished daily. Only mineral water and Coca-Cola are included in the standard filling.
  • Bathroom. There is a large mirror, a wash basin, a hairdryer and a set of cosmetic accessories (shampoo, shower gel, liquid soap).
  • Room service. A thorough cleaning of all rooms is carried out daily. Towels and bedding are regularly changed.

Service and Facilities

An extensive network of infrastructure facilities has been developed for the guests of the complex, which significantly improves the quality of the proposed vacation. Tourists who visited Fiesta Beach Djerba 4 * (Tunisia, Djerba) can use the following services:

  • Internet is available for free in the hotel lobby and by the pool.
  • Room for computer games and Internet access. His visit is free for guests.
  • Private guarded parking. Paid car rental is available.
  • Several shops. Tourists can buy alcohol, tobacco, newspapers, snacks and light snacks, as well as beachwear, shoes and souvenirs.
  • Beauty and hairdresser. All services are provided at an additional cost.
  • Laundry room, where guests can wash their clothes.
  • Currency exchange point.
  • Doctor's office. Each reception is paid separately.
  • Amphitheater, which hosts entertainment events.
  • Four conference rooms that are equipped with all necessary computer equipment. Tourists can also use fax, scanner and photocopying services.




Entertainment in the complex

In addition to competent service, the Fiesta Beach 4 * hotel (Tunisia) offers its guests a lot of entertainment that will appeal to lovers of outdoor activities and passive tourists. We list the main leisure options offered by the complex:

  • Disco in the evenings. Drinks are served here for a fee, but the visit itself is available to all tourists.
  • An entertainment program that includes sporting events, as well as animations that tell about the culture and history of Tunisia.
  • 1200 sq. M freshwater outdoor pool m. Sun loungers and umbrellas are offered for free. Beach towels can only be obtained after making a deposit. There are water slides.
  • Indoor heated pool filled with thermal water.
  • A wellness center that offers treatments for the face and body. Tourists can visit balneology sessions, a Turkish bath, massage courses, a sauna, a steam room or a jacuzzi.
  • Water entertainment center on the beach. Here you can ride on a banana, water skiing, catamaran, as well as try parasailing and windsurfing.
  • Playgrounds for sports: mini-golf, indoor soccer, volleyball, tennis, soccer and archery.
  • Halls for playing darts and billiards.
  • Gym. There are also regular classes in water aerobics in the pool.




Food system, bars and restaurants

Travelers staying at the Fiesta Beach 4 * hotel (Tunisia, Djerba) leave positive reviews. Perhaps most of all they praise the power system. The all-inclusive concept is offered to the guests of the complex, the price of which includes all meals, as well as any drinks of local production. The system runs from 9:00 to midnight. If you want to have a bite to eat at night, you will have to pay extra for food. A children's and diet menu is provided free of charge.

All guests can eat at the following catering facilities:

  • The main restaurant. All meals are held here. International and local dishes are served. For tourists, an extensive buffet is served.
  • Italian restaurant. Tourists can try pizza, pastas, and grilled dishes. For hotel guests there is one free entry to each restaurant for the entire time of rest.
  • Mediterranean restaurant. It only works in the summer.
  • Central bar.
  • Pool Bar. It serves chilled drinks, cocktails, ice cream, fruits.
  • Beach bar. It works only in fine weather.
  • Disco bar. Open until 1:00, discos are regularly held here.
  • Moorish cafe. Here vacationers can have a bite to order fresh pastries, desserts, tea or coffee.




Conditions for children

Being a family complex, the Fiesta Beach 4 * hotel (Tunisia) has prepared a variety of entertainment options for children. There are two shallow pools, one of which is equipped with water slides for guests over four years old. In the morning and in the afternoon there is a mini-club offering an entertainment program for children from five years old. There is also a playground equipped with safety slides and swings.

On request, babies are provided with a cot for babies in their rooms, while restaurants always have chairs for feeding. Also, tourists, if desired, will be able to hire a nanny. Her services are paid. Their average cost is about 5 euros per hour.





Positive feedback from guests

The opinions of tourists are an important indicator of the quality of the hotel. The Fiesta Beach 4 * complex (Tunisia) is not an exception, and good reviews leave it. Travelers loved him for the following advantages:

  • Clean well-kept hotel area. Around there is a lot of greenery, and the air is filled with aromas of flowers that grow everywhere.
  • For lunches and dinners, a large variety of dishes are served, which will especially appeal to lovers of oriental cuisine.
  • Unobtrusive animation.
  • Very clean white sand, which is nice to walk barefoot.
  • Sunset at sea is very smooth, so it is perfect for swimming children.
  • Upon arrival, the hotel staff tries to populate tourists very quickly.
  • Very nice rooms. If deficiencies are found, the administrator will immediately offer to move into other apartments.




Negative guest reviews

It is unlikely that there will be a tourist complex that would satisfy all the tourists who rested in it. Negative opinions were not spared and the hotel Fiesta Beach 4 * (Tunisia). Reviews in 2016 note the following disadvantages:

  • Camels are walking on the beach, from which there is an unpleasant odor. Sometimes their feces are found, which hotel staff tries to quickly clean.
  • Among the staff there are rude and arrogant employees.
  • Tourists are not advised to leave the hotel alone because it may be unsafe.
  • No alcohol and snacks at the beach bar. We have to constantly return to the pool.
  • The hotel has many flies.

Conclusion

The tourist complex Fiesta Beach 4 * (Tunisia) has minor flaws, so it can be recommended as a place to relax. It is suitable for both active travelers and those who like to spend time lying on the beach. Good location in a quiet place makes it a good option for families with children and the elderly. The proximity to the center will be convenient for young people who prefer to spend time in bars and nightclubs.
