The recreation center "Dawn" Anapa: general characteristics and reviews

The period from May to October is the time during which most seek to get a vacation. And it is at sea resorts that it is possible to combine several areas of relaxation at once: a change of scenery, entertainment, excursions and the ability to improve health due to the healing power of salt water and fresh air. Therefore, with the expectation of a large number of vacationers, the infrastructure of cities and villages along the Black Sea coast is constantly expanding and updating. And one of the places worth visiting is the recreation center "Dawn" in Anapa. This wellness center has for many years become a great vacation spot for visitors.

From the history of Anapa

The first historical references to the city of Anapa date back to the 15th century. During this period, a Turkish fortress stood on the site of the modern city, which due to its position was considered impregnable. Over the course of several centuries, Russian troops made a number of attempts to capture Anapa. And during all this time the fortress changed hands several times. Anapa then acquired the status of a city, then lost it again. Several times the city was destroyed to the ground, and then rebuilt. The last time this happened during the Great Patriotic War.

The rich history has turned the area in the Anapa region into a source of valuable historical finds. Many of them now constitute the exposition of the Museum of Local History.

Since 1929, the Soviet government decided to turn Anapa into a children's resort. And although work on the implementation of this project was suspended due to the war, since 1947, the full restoration of the city began. During these years, the long-standing problem of local residents related to the low quality of drinking water was resolved. To do this, from the Kuban River to Anapa, a water supply was made. And since that time, 153 sanatorium-resort establishments have appeared on the territory of the city and nearby villages, so up to 300,000 people can rest on this territory at the same time. One of the most popular in Anapa is the Zarya recreation center in Dzhemete. There are no empty seats during the holiday season.



Features of the resorts of Anapa

It was no accident to turn Anapa into a children's resort. This city has several features:

  • there are sandy beaches that are not found everywhere on the Black Sea coast;
  • the city is located in a shallow bay, so here you can teach children to swim without fear that they will suddenly find themselves at too great a depth;
  • Anapa is one of the sunniest cities on the coast - out of 200 resort days there are only 48 without sun;
  • close to the city there are sources with mineral, sulfide and hydrogen sulfide waters;
  • Thanks to the developed infrastructure, Anapa is easily accessible by bus, train, plane and car.

The benefits of a holiday base

Every year, the recreation center "Dawn" in Anapa attracts hundreds of tourists. This is facilitated by the following features of the institution:

  • the resort complex is 2.5 hectares of protected territory;
  • hotel rooms are two-story buildings made of glued beams, which implies relaxation with a minimum number of neighbors;
  • The main advantage of the base is its proximity to the sea, which is 80 meters from it;
  • voucher to the base includes access to the beach owned by the institution, vacationers on which is much less than the city;
  • proximity to urban infrastructure, the ability to book excursions available in Anapa.

All this makes the placement and stay at sea as comfortable as possible.



Description of the recreation center "Zarya" Anapa

Most rooms are designed for two people. But if necessary, you can live in three of them. If the room is booked by a family with a child under three years old, the baby lives free of charge, subject to timely notification of the administration. Economy-class housing does not imply amenities inside buildings. On the territory there are common male and female showers, toilets.

On the territory of the complex of the recreation center "Zarya" in Anapa there is a kitchen where you can cook the products purchased in the city. And for those who plan to devote themselves completely to rest, a restaurant, a bistro cafe and a bar are open.

For those who have arrived to rest in their own car, there is a guarded parking lot. Those who are worried about their valuables can rent a cell in a safe for their safety. If necessary, you can get emergency medical care at a special point on the territory of the base. And everyone who combines outdoor recreation with the virtual world can access the Internet through Wi-fi.
