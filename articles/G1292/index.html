How to do tricks with cards?

Want to surprise your friends? Master the tricks with cards for beginners. Thanks to these skills, you can attract attention in the company. Let's look at the popular card tricks.

"Spit out the money!"

Ask one person to choose any card and remember it. Put the card back in the deck. After shuffling, take out and show the cards in turn until a certain point, after which invite your friends to play for $ 1. If the next card is the one you have chosen, then you win, otherwise you lose. Flip the card. Take the win!





Secret

  • Shuffle the deck (take half a deck without jokers).
  • Open the deck with a fan and let choose a card.
  • Shuffle the deck again, divide it into two halves, and let the card put on the bottom pile. For no one to see, look at the bottom card of the top stack. Connect the halves.
  • Turn over and show the cards in turn, to the one that you imperceptibly spied.
  • Offer to play for $ 1.
  • Flip the last card they made. Take the winnings.

Aces

In this trick, you need to ask the person to divide the deck into four parts, then it will need to be shuffled, and in the end he will see that there is an ace on top of each part.

Secret

The person you ask to participate will think that this is an accident, but in fact he puts the aces upstairs. All the participant needs is to listen to you.

  • Put the aces up. Do not show their focus participant.
  • Ask him to divide the deck into four parts. Watch out for the part where the aces are.
  • Next, tell the participant to take one of the piles (where there are no aces), remove three cards from the top and put them down in piles, then let them put on the card from the top of the pile to the other three piles.
  • Same thing with two other piles without aces.
  • And last - repeat this with the part where the aces are. That's all.

These card tricks will surely delight others! If you like these tricks, then read the article further. You will learn some more interesting tricks with cards.

"Swap"

  • For this trick, you will need a duplicate card (for example, seven tambourines). In advance, lay out the deck of cards so that the bottom is seven tambourines, then any other (for example, six crosses) and after it again seven tambourines.
  • Show the audience that you are mixing the deck; do not touch the bottom three cards. At the end, put the bottom seven up. The penultimate card is now the seven tambourine. Ask to remember their last card, holding the deck in your left hand so that you can move the bottom card back.
  • Put the deck in your hand in a horizontal position and pretend to pull out the bottom card, but actually push it back and pull out the penultimate one. Lay it face down on the table and ask someone to cover it with your hand.
  • Next, transfer the top seven tambourine down the deck and mix it without touching the two lower cards.
  • Then let the audience remember the bottom card, which is already the seven tambourines, and pretend that you are pulling it out, but actually pulling out the six crosses. Put it face down on the table. Another viewer must also cover her with his hand.
  • The viewer now thinks that the first card is six crosses, and the second is seven tambourines. Next, tell everyone that, swap these two cards without touching them. For the view, make some movement for the focus. Ask the two viewers to let go of the cards, look at them and show everyone.
  • Since their attention was drawn to these cards, then you can easily put the seven tambourines in your palm gently and hide it in your pocket. In order for the audience not to suspect anything, you can let them inspect the deck.

Conclusion

Now you know some tricks with cards for beginners. Having trained, you will learn how to do them yourself. If everything works out for you, then you can start learning other card tricks. We wish you good luck and success!
