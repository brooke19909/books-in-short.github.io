Beauty lives here. How to make DIY jewelry

Recently, handmade, or, in Russian speaking, needlework, has been particularly popular. And this is not surprising, because such products are distinguished by their unique originality and special charm.



Today we will talk about how to make jewelry with our own hands. After all, they distinguish you favorably in the crowd, no one has them. Only you have them! And every woman has a natural desire for individuality.

With the development of some skills in jewelry, you can create real masterpieces, similar to what you see here in the photo. But this is by no means by each of you. We need special tools, materials, and most importantly - the great desire and talent of the designer.

If you do not possess either one or the other, then I will tell you how to make jewelry with your own hands without any difficulties in literally half an hour.

So, we begin a small master class. Topic: how to make jewelry with your own hands.

To make these beautiful long earrings, we need a little multi-colored beads or small beads, a few detachable metal rings, a pair of earrings (the so-called earrings fasteners), thin pliers (thin-nose pliers).

If you do not have metal rings, take a regular wire, wind it on any rod of suitable thickness with a dense spiral, and then cut it into separate turns. Get rings. For each such ring you need to put on a bead from two open sides and pinch it (see the diagram).
Insert the next open link into it, fasten the beads on it and clamp it again. Thus, we will make a chain decorated with beads of the desired length. We attach a schwenzie to it, and the first earring is ready. We do the second in the same way.

If not one, but three chain links decorated with beads are inserted into every second ring, then we get the earrings a little more voluminous. By changing the colors of the beads, you can create different versions of earrings for your colorful summer outfits.



As you just saw, making jewelry with your own hands is not so difficult. And if you master the art of weaving from wire, you can create absolutely fantastic things with just the graceful rotation of the pliers. These are such special round tongs.
