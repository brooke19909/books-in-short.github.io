Marina Phuket 4 * (Thailand, Phuket): hotel description, photos and reviews of tourists

Thailand is a popular resort country among domestic vacationers who are attracted by its hot climate, combined with the low cost of trips. For an acceptable amount, tourists can stay in a good hotel, fully equipped with everything necessary for a first-class holiday on the sea. If you are planning a vacation in Phuket, pay attention to the inexpensive hotel Marina Phuket Resort 4 *, which is located near the popular Patong Beach. It is about this resort complex that will be discussed in this article.

Location Details

The location of this hotel is ideal for tourists looking for a place to relax in Phuket. The complex is located in a quiet area, entirely planted with tropical vegetation. It is noteworthy that The Marina Phuket Patong 4 * is built on a high hill 200 meters from the coast, so it offers amazing views of the sea. But there are some drawbacks: the road to the public beach will take about 10-15 minutes. At the same time, returning to the hotel, tourists will have to climb the hill. The distance from the beach is compensated by developed infrastructure. Near the hotel there are many restaurants, cafes, shops and souvenir shops. In addition, nearby is a large entertainment complex "Dinosaur Park", which is a favorite vacation spot for tourists with young children.

This is not to say that the hotel is very far from the airport. An international terminal accepting flights from Russia is located directly on the island of Phuket. The distance to it is about 50 km. You can overcome it in 30-50 minutes. Tourists can take a taxi to the hotel or book a paid shuttle in advance.

Important information

This hotel will be a great place for a relaxing stay. The fact is that the Karon Beach Marina Phuket 4 * complex is built on the territory of a tropical garden. Its area is about 68,000 m 2 . The management of the complex carefully takes care of the local flora. On site you can even see rare tropical plants. It was opened to guests in 1985, but do not think that this is an old hotel complex. The last repair in it was carried out in 2016, when not only the number of rooms was updated, but also the public premises and the external facades of the buildings.

The hotel is a complex of detached bungalows, decorated in a tropical style. It features an outdoor pool and a sun terrace. For recreation, it is often chosen by tourists with young children, as the hotel allows guests of any age to stay. Catering

The price of accommodation at the Marina Phuket 4 * hotel includes only morning meals. If desired, tourists can also pay extra for dinners, but drinks will not be included in the price. There are also 3 restaurants on site. They specialize in cooking Thai specialties and delicacies. Here, tourists can also try exotic seafood. You can enjoy a drink at the bar, which is located in the hotel lobby. Light snacks, ice cream and fresh fruit are also served there.

Service and Facilities

On the vast territory of the Marina Phuket 4 * hotel, a developed network of infrastructure facilities has been created that allows you to make your vacation here as comfortable as possible even for the most demanding tourists. So, at the entrance to the complex there is a free car parking. Guests can leave their car here at any time without prior reservation.

In the main building of the hotel there is a reception desk, where an English-speaking administrator is working around the clock. He will not only help guests with solving their problems, but will also exchange currency, arrange car or scooter rental, and call a doctor in the room upon request. If desired, guests can take their clothes to the laundry for a fee.

Entertainment at the hotel

Marina Phuket 4 * complex is aimed at tourists who are looking for a place for a relaxing holiday on the sea, so they don‚Äôt offer a large entertainment option. Most of the time guests spend on the city beach, located 200 meters from the complex. It is completely sandy and equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay: sunbeds, umbrellas, mattresses. But rent is charged. There is a water entertainment center on the beach where you can pay for diving lessons.

If you do not want to spend time and energy on the road to the sea, you can comfortably relax by the outdoor pool filled with fresh water. Next to it, as expected, there is a spacious sun terrace with free sun loungers and parasols. For a fee, guests can enjoy a Thai massage parlor and a minigolf course.

Accommodation with small children

Located in a quiet area, Marina Phuket 4 * complex will be an ideal place for families. Because of the green area, tourists with small children often choose it. They are given substantial discounts on accommodation and offers a certain range of amenities.

So, a baby crib can be delivered to the room upon request before the arrival of guests. In restaurants, there are always free high chairs that are convenient to use for feeding babies. If necessary, a nanny can be called to the room, but her services are provided for a fee. To ensure that children on site are not bored, a separate shallow pool with fresh water is equipped for them.

Positive feedback about Marina Phuket 4 *

Of course, one cannot say that this complex has an ideal reputation among tourists, but they often leave rave reviews about their stay here. They liked the level of service, which, in their opinion, is fully consistent with the cost of living. It is noteworthy that many tourists come here not for the first time, preferring this hotel to more expensive complexes.

In their reviews, tourists indicate the following advantages of this accommodation option:

  • Large pool with clear water. It is located on the roof of the main building, so it offers amazing views of the panorama of the city and the sea.
  • Friendly and sociable staff who always do their job with quality.
  • Large and comfortable beds in the rooms.
  • A huge landscaped area, which is regularly cleaned by employees. Trails are equipped everywhere to make it convenient for tourists to walk.

Negative feedback from tourists

And although this hotel, as a rule, is praised by tourists in reviews, they also often point out service deficiencies in them. It is worth noting that usually they are not significant and do not spoil the impression of rest. But you still need to know about them, so we list the main disadvantages of this hotel:

  • Some bungalows are located next to the highway, which makes it very noisy even at night.
  • Sometimes rusty water runs from a tap.
  • Slow internet speed. The connection is constantly breaking.
  • Poor housekeeping. You can see stains on the furniture, and dust and hair on the floor. In addition, linen and towels are changed extremely rarely.
  • Monotonous and tasteless breakfasts that do not match their value.

Thus, the complex Marina Phuket Resort Karon 4 * will be an excellent option for tourists who prefer a relaxing holiday at sea. If you like tropical gardens, then be sure to come to this hotel. At the same time, travelers seeking active entertainment may feel bored in this place.
