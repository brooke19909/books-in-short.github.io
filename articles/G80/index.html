Tourist base "Angarskaya Gorka"

Among the tall pines in the forest, near Irkutsk, there is a wonderful corner for relaxation. Wooden comfortable houses, fresh coniferous air, mouth-watering kitchen - all this creates a cozy atmosphere. It‚Äôs good to stroll around the recreation center , breathe clean air, enjoy the silence.

The tourist recreation center "Angarskaya Gorka" is located at: Irkutsk region, Angarsk district, 12 kilometers of the Savvateevsky tract.

The base is located in a pine forest. Guests can settle in cozy wooden houses, fabulous terraces and cottages for large companies.

Accommodations

Economy rooms are located in one-story building number 3. One-room rooms can accommodate up to 4 people. The cost of living varies from the conditions provided. In the cheapest rooms are: beds, desk, wardrobe. The rooms above have a TV and fridge. The building is heated and ready to receive guests all year round.

The hotel has three one-story cottages with luxury rooms:

  • "Blue";
  • "Red";
  • "Green".

One room with a bathroom is equipped with furniture, a refrigerator and a TV. Cottages are heated and receive guests of the recreation center year-round.

Nutrition

The cafe on the territory of the tourist base includes a bar and two banquet rooms.

The bar serves European cuisine with a varied menu. Three-course set meals can be ordered.

In a small banquet room for 30 people, the administration of the base offers to celebrate a celebration, wedding, anniversary or corporate event.

The large banquet room seats up to 80 people and is divided into two rooms. At the request of the client, they can be combined into one.

Leisure activities

On the territory of the Angarskaya Gorka camp site there is a large bathhouse with a cedar steam room. The bath has all the conditions for a comfortable stay: a large open veranda, a swimming pool, a TV, a refrigerator, a wardrobe and changing cabins.

For lovers of active games in the fresh air, football, basketball and volleyball games are offered on equipped platforms. Tables for table tennis, barbecue area, gazebos for evening gatherings.

Woodland with lowlands and heights is ideal for playing paintball. The territory is illuminated around the perimeter, there is a shooting gallery and equipment for children.

On the equipped playground, you can relax with your child: ride on a large circular carousel, designed for both children and adults. In the winter season, an ice slide and an ice rink are arranged at the base.

Communication services and transfer

On the territory of the tourist base "Angarskaya Gorka" there is access to the Internet, mobile communication is working. On the basis of cash and bank transfer, credit cards are accepted.

For an additional fee, a transfer is provided from the city airport, from the Irkutsk railway station.

For guests of the base there are constant discounts on accommodation, use of the bathhouse and rental of sports equipment.

Angarskaya Gorka is an ideal place to relax from the bustle of the city, where you can spend a great weekend.
