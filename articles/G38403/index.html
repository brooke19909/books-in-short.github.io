Katkova Shchel, private sector: description, popular guest houses

A good opportunity to have a budget vacation near Sochi is a trip to the Katkova Slit. The private sector of this resort village we will try to describe below. Here you will find the sea, picturesque cliffs, mountain rivers - in a word, all that the Caucasus is famous for. And housing will offer you different things - from the most budgetary to luxurious. Come and see for yourself. It is quiet, calm, family-like. Well, if you miss nightclubs, you can drop in Sochi in the evening, since this is not so far away. We will also describe here as an example several guest houses of the resort.





Katkova Village Slit

This resort is located some ten kilometers from Lazarevsky. The climate here is subtropical, warm, which attracts hundreds of tourists to these places. The season lasts a long time - from May to October. If you come here, the climatic conditions here will be the same as in Sochi, but for the money you will undoubtedly win. The village got its name from the name of the Russian farmer Mikhail Katkov, on whose territory mulberry trees grew. Those who want a relaxing holiday, wide beaches, beautiful landscapes and mountain air come here. And for families with children - especially welcome to Katkova Slit! The private sector is cheap, quality service, the hosts are friendly. Often you can find luxury conditions. Nearby are the same ecologically sound resorts of Volkonka and Chemitokvadzhe.





General characteristics of housing

The private sector in Katkova Slit is represented by mini-hotels, guest houses, as well as houses, turnkey rooms and entire apartments with the owners. Since the village actually consists of two intersecting large streets - Kurgannaya and Magistralnaya, then all housing is mainly concentrated there or in neighboring lanes. Food, as a rule, vacationers cook themselves - in shared kitchens or in separate kitchens, which are located directly in the rooms. Very often, owners have cafes or dining rooms where they feed their guests for a fee. Many houses are in the garden or lined with palm trees. Despite the fact that the rooms are located in large buildings or bungalows, the furniture and showers are new, almost everywhere there are suspended ceilings, plasma TVs, there is a laminate on the floor. But there are also very modest economy class rooms. Such housing, depending on the price and degree of comfort, is chosen by both wealthy people and those who can only afford a budget vacation.

What tourists say about vacation

Most of the reviews about the Katkovaya Slit are positively characterized by the private sector. Owners often meet guests right at the train car and provide them with a free shuttle to their accommodation. The yard area is usually very green, often beautifully decorated. A lot of place to relax, there are gazebos. Almost everywhere there are playgrounds. Some guest houses are located right on the banks of a mountain river, where children can have fun during the hot season. If tourists ate in cafes owned by homeowners, then the feedback on the quality of the food is good. The kitchen is homemade, the products are fresh, and the children, who are usually picky, die everything with appetite. The owners are unobtrusive, do not stand above the soul, and the requests of vacationers fulfill with a smile.
