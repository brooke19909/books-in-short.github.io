Hotel "Fereniki", Crete - a guarantee of upscale relaxation!

Just a few steps from the vast expanses of the Mediterranean Sea, the central building of the Fereniki Hotel is located - an ideal place for everyone to forget about the city bustle, problems and worries. Like the Fereniki Hotel itself, Crete is generally famous for its gardens, spas, playgrounds, restaurants, pools, bars, and therefore is always ready to offer vacationers an upscale and truly unforgettable vacation!

Hotel



According to the guests, the modern hotel complex "Fereniki" (Crete) is the best in terms of quality of accommodation throughout Georgioupolis. The hotel includes six different buildings. Each of them is well-equipped and boasts stunning views. All of them function independently, have a unique style and their own name - this is an amazing feature of the Fereniki Hotel, Crete. Reviews of tourists are often full of comments that the hotel individually suits the requirements of each guest - this is a big plus!

SPA

A real oasis will help you get away from the fast rhythm of life and heat for a while, where the East meets the West, and modernity meets the classics. Qualified staff offers tourists to take advantage of simple, but quite complete personal hygiene programs. Such procedures will satisfy the needs of the tired body and mind of every vacationer!

Nutrition



Breakfast is served in the hotel restaurant, from where, as from anywhere in the Fereniki Hotel, Crete appears in all its glory. For lunch and dinner, tourists can choose any buffet. The chef of the restaurant offers outstanding dishes, tempting appetite and satisfying every taste. Near the pool there is a delightful bar where you can quench your thirst with drinks and cocktails, sunbathe and relax with quiet music at sunset.

Georgioupolis

The Georgioupolis region has the longest beach in North Crete, which extends from the Gulf of Almiros, known for its unsurpassed natural beauty, to the Kavros region on the border of the Rethymnon province. Nature lovers will love the nearby wildlife sanctuaries, where you can see wild ducks and turtles, as well as see the incomparable Lake Kurna. Georgioupolis is in close proximity to airports, ferries, hospitals and major cities such as Chania and Rethymnon. Near the Fereniki Hotel (Crete), several archaeological sites and attractions have been preserved, such as the ruins of the Knossos Palace and Samaria Gorge. This is an ideal place for a relaxing holiday, and for walks, excursions.





Rooms

All apartments have well-equipped bathrooms. In addition to standard rooms, the hotel offers studios for groups of 1-3 people. A large company or family can be accommodated in a one-room apartment with a spacious open-plan living room. Each room has two single beds, two sofa beds and a TV, a double bed in the bedroom. Each room has a balcony or veranda with impressive views of the mountains or the sea, an adjustable air conditioning unit, a refrigerator, satellite TV, a telephone for internal and external calls, a large wardrobe, a dressing table, a hairdryer and a safe, although you will have to pay extra for it separately. Greece, Crete, "Fereniki" ... What could be better ?! Have a magical journey!
