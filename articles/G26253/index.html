Hotel Gorki Panorama 4 * (Russia / Krasnaya Polyana): traveler reviews

For many residents of Russia, Sochi is a magnificent resort on the Black Sea coast. Here, anyone can enjoy plenty of fun, enjoy the wonderful nature of these places, improve their health in the hospitable local sanatoriums and get warm under the warm rays of the sun.

However, for several years now, not only sea lovers have come to Sochi, but also fans of snow and skiing sports. After all, every lover of winter entertainment can easily please the modern ski resort in Sochi - Krasnaya Polyana (Sochi, Krasnaya Polyana), already well known far beyond the borders of our country.





Gorki Panorama is a 4 * hotel, which, along with other hotels of this place, gladly accepts all travelers who decide to conquer the peaks of the Caucasus Mountains.

Sochi is not only a beach holiday

For many years, the Russian resort of Sochi has been associated in people with a warm sea and long hours under the hot sun. In summer, people come here just for this. After all, after a busy year, everyone wants to relax and have enough fun, try traditionally southern dishes and recharge with solar energy for the next year.

However, after the Winter Olympic Games were successfully organized and held here, they also talked about Sochi as a winter resort that can compete well with many foreign representatives of this tourist destination.

One of the interesting resorts on the territory of Krasnaya Polyana is Gorki Gorod, which is pleasant to visit not only in winter but also in summer. The resort is divided into two parts: the lower and upper cities, between which you can move by car or using a separate cableway.

How to relax in Krasnaya Polyana

In Krasnaya Polyana, outdoor activities are most welcome. In winter, this is primarily skiing and snowboarding. After all, it is precisely for this that visitors from other countries come here. In the warmer months, you can go hiking or car rides in the mountains and enjoy the beautiful scenery.

Beautiful hotel "Gorki Panorama"

In the resort "Gorki Gorod" there are many hotels of different categories. For example, at the top of this place is the Gorki Panorama 4 * hotel, from the rooms of which tourists can admire the mountain peaks covered in snow-white enthusiastically.

The beige five-story hotel building was built recently, in February 2014. Gorki Panorama - 4 * hotel, which operates throughout the year. Therefore, in winter, those who enjoy skiing have a rest in it , and in summer you can go on exciting mountain hikes and go to the coast from here.

Gorki Panorama is a 4 * hotel (Krasnaya Polyana), which has its own spa center with an indoor pool, a restaurant and a bar with a rich selection of food and drinks and a large selection of various conference rooms. It is also very convenient to get to the ski lifts from here, which bring vacationers to the top of the ski slopes. Go to them from the hotel will be only some 100 meters.

Thanks to the excellent service and services offered, the star category of this hotel is fully justified. Those who appreciate the good equipment of rooms, the close location of the lifts, as well as beautiful views of the Caucasus Mountains, should definitely choose Gorki Panorama (4 * hotel) for their holidays.
