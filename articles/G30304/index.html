Maxim Poletaev: biography, photos and interesting facts

At the moment, Maxim Vladimirovich Poletaev is acting as Advisor to the President. He is also the Chairman of the Management Board of Sberbank PJSC. The article will consider the formation of his career.

Education

Maxim Vladimirovich Poletaev was born on April 6, 1971 in the city of Yaroslavl, 250 kilometers northeast of Moscow.

He was educated at Yaroslavl State University in the field of accounting and business. He graduated in 1993.

Career. Early stages

After that, he worked as an assistant at the same university in the department of economics and organization of production. The directions of scientific research in this period were:

  1. Modern methodological and theoretical problems of improving accounting and auditing in Russian enterprises.
  2. Features of the functioning of the financial system in a transitional Russian economy.
  3. Problems of improving accounting, auditing and analysis.

In 1994-1995 Poletaev went on an internship to the Sberkasy of Germany (Sparkasse, Kassel). The system of savings banks in Germany includes 710 institutions in 12 regional associations, 13 land banks, 13 land building savings banks. During the internship, he studied the system and features of the banking sector in Germany.

Career progression

An important role in the life of Maxim Poletaev was played by knowledge of foreign languages, in particular English and German. Thanks to ownership of the latter, the future chairman of the board of Sberbank was able in 1994-1995. get an internship at Sparkasse Savings Banks (Kassel, Germany).

In 2002, Maxim Vladimirovich Poletaev went for an increase, namely, he began to manage the Baikal Bank.

Such experience promoted Poletaev to the position of vice president of Sberbank in 2009. Almost at the same time, he was appointed director of the department responsible for corporate development of the bank. In the same year, in October, he was appointed the chairman of the Moscow Bank.
