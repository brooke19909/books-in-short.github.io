How to sew a skirt on the floor - the main trend of the season

The fashion of recent years is based on the motto ‚ÄúThe simpler, the better!‚Äù. Therefore, models that are as simple as possible and without "frills" do not leave the catwalks. Dresses of direct cut, light blouses without tucks, skirts of different lengths, simply assembled with elastic ... Here is the last element of the wardrobe, in connection with the onset of heat, just pay special attention. Indeed, in how to sew a skirt on the floor, in fact, there is nothing complicated.



A lightweight fabric, such as chiffon, knitwear or fine silk, is best suited for a new thing. How much will it take for a fashionable skirt? It all depends on the length and your height. The desired length of the skirt must be multiplied by two and added to the obtained number of centimeters twenty: by the belt, hemming of the bottom and possible errors. The width of the fabric also varies: too thin material can be folded in half, or even three. It all depends on the degree of splendor of the future skirt, which means, again, on your desire. But the standard width of the fabric is usually one and a half meters.

In addition to the fabric itself, you will need an elastic band (its length depends on the circumference of the waist), scissors, a sewing machine and threads in tone.

Before you sew a skirt on the floor, we will open it. He is extremely simple. To begin with, we determine with a belt. If it is one-piece, then skip this step, and if we want to sew the belt separately, then we need to cut the strip from a piece of fabric. Its length is equal to the circumference of the hips, and the width - depending on the desired width of the belt. Do not forget only that the strip will need to be folded in half. So, we sew it into the ring, put the elastic inside, sew along the entire length. If the skirt is sewn from elastic knitwear, you can do without elastic.



The belt is ready, now let's deal with the skirt itself. I hope you have already decided on her appearance by scrolling through fashion magazines, where a wide variety of different models of skirts are presented . The photos must have also been saved to check with the original as you sew. So, on the purchased piece of fabric you need to make only one seam - along the length of the future skirt. And if you took the fabric for two lengths, then before you cut the skirt, you need to cut it in half and make two similar seams on each half. We will put them into one another, which will give the skirt extra volume and splendor, and also hide the excess if the fabric is too transparent.



The upper section of the skirt needs to be adjusted to the circumference of the hips. This can be done by picking up the fabric on a thread or laying folds on it. This will not affect the general technology of how to sew a skirt to the floor, but the products will look a little different. After that, we sew a belt to the main part. It will remain to process the bottom: double needle or hem. Before doing this, it is better to measure the skirt, and it is with that shoe with which you are going to wear it later. By the way, some types of fabrics do not require treatment at all, which is just for beginners.

Here is the skirt and ready. In it you can go shopping, for a walk with your child, for a romantic date or even for a solemn event. You can be proud of yourself, because now you are not only dressed in the latest fashion, but also know the answer to the question of how to sew a skirt on the floor with your own hands.
