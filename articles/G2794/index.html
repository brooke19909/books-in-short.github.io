Mangrove Hotel Ras Al Kha 4 (United Arab Emirates / Ras Al Khaimah): overview, description, characteristics and reviews of tourists

Many tourists choose the UAE as their vacation destination. This is a magical country that offers its guests a mild climate, picturesque beaches, a warm sea and, of course, a huge number of hotels of different sizes and categories. One of such paradise places for spending leisure is the hotel Mangrove Hotel Ras Al Kha 4 *. This hotel complex offers its guests excellent conditions, and at a relatively low price. Of course, when going on a trip, tourists are first of all looking for additional information about a particular place. So is it worth going to this hotel?

Location Description

Of course, the first question of the tourist is exactly where the Mangrove Hotel is located. Ras Al Khaimah (Ras al-Khaimah, Ras al-Khaimah) - a picturesque resort village on the Persian Gulf. There are many hotels, shopping centers, markets, attractions, in short, everything for an interesting and enjoyable vacation. By the way, in five minutes you can walk to the large shopping and entertainment center Al Safir. Another is located 2.5 km from the hotel building. About the same distance is the famous Tower Links Golf Club. Five minutes by taxi you can get to the city center.





The nearest airport is about 20 km away. The international airport is about 80 km away. You should not be upset about such a long distance, as travel agencies provide customers with a transfer to the hotel and back.

Hotel Mangrove Hotel 4 * (Ras Al Khaimah, United Arab Emirates): description of the territory

This hotel complex is a large multi-story building, built in an interesting Art Nouveau style. The territory of the Mangrove Hotel Ras Al Kha 4 *, although small, but beautifully furnished, decorated with flowers and greenery. By the way, elevators are constantly working in the building, so you should not worry, even if your room is located on the top floor.

By the way, this hotel is part of the world famous hotel chain called the Bin Majid Group. It was built recently, namely in 2012. Naturally, an elegant design and the presence of all modern amenities await you inside.

What do the numbers look like? Photo and description

The Mangrove Hotel Ras Al Kha 4 * hotel is quite large - on its territory there are 186 rooms of the following categories:

  • 90 One-bedroom suit.
  • 60 Executive rooms.
  • 30 Standard rooms.
  • 6 presidential suites.




Regardless of the categories, all rooms are bright, clean, with large windows and modern renovation. The furnishings are pretty simple but very elegant. Some apartments have a private balcony (this information should be specified when booking the hotel). In any case, the windows offer breathtaking views of the surroundings and even the sea, especially when it comes to rooms on the upper floors. In the rooms, of course, there is a sufficient amount of comfortable and practical furniture, as well as a set of household appliances, without which it is simply impossible to imagine a comfortable stay for a modern traveler.

Of course, the rooms are equipped with a modern air conditioning system with an automatic control system. There is also a TV, so you can pass the free evening watching your favorite satellite channels. There is a telephone and a small safe. The minibar is filled with drinking water, soda and juices - other drinks in the room are brought only at the request of the client for an additional fee. Internet access is also available in the rooms, but it is an hourly service.

In the bathroom you will find a spacious shower, wall mirror, sink, hair dryer, as well as a set of hygiene products. Cold and hot water is supplied constantly. Towels are changed every day during room cleaning.

Nutrition: what should the guests expect?

Since the issue of food is important for many tourists, it is worth considering what the hotel has to offer. When paying for the room, you yourself can choose the power plan, whether it is only breakfast or all-inclusive. Of course, experienced tourists recommend paying at least two meals a day. The main meals are held in the Rainbow Restaurant, which, by the way, is open around the clock. For the most part, visitors are offered international cuisine. The food is always fresh and is served in sufficient quantities. Here you can get drinks, including juices, tea and delicious coffee.





For a small fee, you can also dine at Chinese Corner Restaurant, which serves authentic Chinese cuisine. In the local coffee shop you can also enjoy the aroma of oriental coffee, enjoy delicious desserts and even order a hookah. Of course, you can eat outside the Mangrove Hotel anytime. Ras-Al-Khaimah has many restaurants, cafes and pizzerias where you can have a good time.

Are there conditions for water and beach holidays?

What quality of beach vacation can a hotel guest expect? Almost any review of the Mangrove Hotel contains information that the coast here is incredibly beautiful. It is worth noting that the hotel does not have its own beach, but guests can relax on a site belonging to the neighboring Bin Majid Beach Hotel. By the way, a shuttle regularly runs to the shore, which takes tourists to the beach and back - you can also take a taxi in 5-10 minutes.





The sea here is clean and warm. There is a quiet backwater where children bathe. On the shore you can relax on comfortable sun loungers, and you do not need to pay extra for them. For a small price, you can grab a bite to eat at the beach bar. The underwater world is beautiful, so many travelers prefer to snorkel. Here, as in any other tourist destination, you can rent a boat or catamaran, go water skiing, boat, banana, in a word, do many types of water sports.

Does the hotel offer additional services?

The hotel has created the conditions for a truly comfortable stay. To begin with, it is worth saying that the territory of the Mangrove Hotel Ras Al Kha 4 * hotel is constantly guarded. The staff will help you call a taxi or even buy plane tickets. Laundry facilities and luggage storage are available. Here you can rent a car. Internet access is available throughout the hotel, but this service is charged separately. There are several professional nannies who will stay with the child for several hours.





The hotel has a fairly well-equipped business center with a spacious conference room. There is a fax, printer, computers, equipment for audio and video conferencing. If necessary, here, on the spot, you can hire a secretary.

Leisure for guests: is it possible to have fun here?

How do hotel guests spend their time? Immediately it is worth saying that on one of the floors of the hotel there is a large freshwater pool where you can relax, swim and refresh yourself. Tourists will also enjoy the wellness center, where you can steam up in the sauna, relax in the jacuzzi, go for a few massage sessions or sign up for cosmetic, wellness treatments.





Nearby is a large golf center where fans of this sport can have a great time. The hotel itself has a gym with modern equipment for guests who want to always keep fit. If you wish, you can always ask for help at a local tour desk, where you will be advised of the most interesting tours in the surrounding area, and throughout the country. By the way, experienced travelers recommend visiting Dubai at least once - the capital of the tourist world, where you can have fun and do some shopping.

What kind of vacation is the hotel suitable for?

Hotel Mangrove Hotel 4 * is primarily intended for a relaxing beach holiday. As you can see, you can have fun at the hotel, but most tourists spend here only in the evenings, relaxing on the coast during the day and exploring the surroundings. Children can be bored here, although families with a child often stay at the hotel. Quite often, a hotel becomes a venue for corporate vacations, as the presence of a business center allows this. By the way, employees independently organize meetings, seminars and other events.

Hotel complex Mangrove 4 * (Ras Al Khaimah, United Arab Emirates): reviews

Having familiarized with the impressions of tourists, you can get a huge amount of valuable information about a particular hotel. So what do they say about the Mangrove Hotel Ras Al Khaimah? Ras Al Khaimah, United Arab Emirates - a great place for a pleasant, relaxing holiday on the beach. And, of course, first of all, travelers note the incredibly beautiful and always warm sea. The hotel is modern, and the infrastructure is thought out to the smallest detail. The rooms are spacious and clean enough, and a beautiful view from the window will delight any tourist. The food is also at a fairly good level. Some guests note the uniformity of the menu, but nevertheless you do not have to starve.





By cons include the minimum amount of entertainment for children, but still this is a city-style hotel. On the other hand, the hotel has a huge advantage, as it offers relatively low prices for accommodation, which cannot but please travelers who are looking for a cheap but good vacation.
