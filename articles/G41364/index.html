Hotel Happy Life Village Dahab (Egypt / Dahab): reviews

If you plan to have a great rest on the Red Sea and are looking for an affordable and comfortable hotel on the first coastline, then Happy Life Village Dahab may be a good option.



Description, photo

Happy Life Village Hotel Dahab 4 * is located 10 kilometers from the city of Dahab. Sharm El Sheikh and the airport are 100 kilometers away. The hotel complex was opened in 2001. The housing stock of the hotel consists of 144 rooms, which are located in several two-story buildings. All apartments have a TV, telephone, air conditioning, bathroom, hairdryer, balcony or terrace, refrigerator. Internet can be used at the reception. The hotel has a restaurant, several bars, a gym, four swimming pools, a sun terrace, and sports grounds. Meals are organized on an all-inclusive or half-board basis. According to tourists, this hotel is perfect for a quiet measured vacation on the shores of the beautiful Red Sea.

Especially if most of the vacation is planned to be held at the hotel. Therefore, modern travelers try to take care of the hotel ahead of time. Housing stock

Since, according to tourists, Happy Life Village Dahab is far from a new hotel, modern design and new rooms cannot be found here. However, all apartments are quite spacious, clean, bright. They have good furniture, all appliances are in working condition. If some kind of breakdown happens, then after contacting the reception it will be eliminated as soon as possible. As our compatriots note, despite the fact that the maids regularly replenish the supplies of shower gel and shampoo in the bathroom, it is advisable to take them from home. The fact is that for many tourists they seemed to be not of high enough quality.

As for cleaning, the guests were generally satisfied with its quality. Guests also note that maids regularly make funny towels on the beds.

As for the location of Happy Life Village Dahab 4 *, here the opinions of travelers are somewhat divided. So, some guests did not like the fact that the hotel is located at a fairly large distance both from Sharm El Sheikh and the airport, and from the nearest city of Dahab. However, most tourists argue that this fact should be considered at the planning stage of the trip. So, if you want to walk around the neighborhood every day and visit bars, restaurants, shops, bazaars, etc., then it makes sense to choose a hotel in Sharm El Sheikh or another resort city. Beach vacation

Since the Happy Life Village Dahab 3 * hotel (by the way, in Germany, for example, it is regarded as a "three" and not a "four") is located on the shore, its guests will be able to fully enjoy the sun and sea. So, you can reach the hotel‚Äôs own beach in a maximum of five minutes from anywhere in the hotel. There is a bar and there are many sun loungers and parasols. Mattresses are also provided. The beach worker will bring them to your deck chair. By the way, as some tourists say, if you want to sit on a deck chair closer to the pontoon, then it makes sense to occupy it early in the morning. However, free sunbeds in other places you can find at any time of the day.

The beach itself is sandy, clean. You can enter the water both from the shore and from the pontoon. But in the first case, you will have to go to the depth for quite some time. The bottom is rocky, corals are found. Therefore, it is not recommended to enter the water without special shoes. From the pontoon, you can go down immediately to the depths. Near the coast is a fairly large coral reef with a variety of interesting inhabitants. Therefore, be sure to "arm yourself" with a mask for snorkeling and optional flippers.





Entertainment

Since Happy Life Village Dahab 4 * is mainly designed for a relaxed, calm and leisurely vacation, there is no special entertainment program here. So, on the territory of the hotel there are several sports grounds where you can play tennis, volleyball. In addition, during the day, animators offer everyone to do aqua aerobics, gymnastics, water polo. Once a week, the hotel complex hosts a show of visiting artists.

Our compatriots in their reviews note that they were satisfied with the hotel pools. There are several of them, in one there are water slides. According to the guests, the water in the pools is very warm and clean. Next to them are equipped with sun terraces, there are sun loungers, there are also bars where you can both have a snack during the day with junk food and treat yourself to your favorite drink.

No special knowledge is required for this, but some of our compatriots expressed some dissatisfaction with this matter.
