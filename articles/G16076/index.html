Nizhny Novgorod Region - Tourist Mecca of Russia

Nizhny Novgorod region is one of the most attractive for tourists and vacationers. Its capital is Nizhny Novgorod, whose camp sites are a favorite place of residence for lovers of rural recreation and river tourism. The natural wealth of Nizhny Novgorod is unique.





A large number of unique monuments of architecture, as well as cultural and historical attractions are concentrated here.

A lot of sanatoriums, camp sites, boarding houses and hotels have been built on the territory of the region, all conditions have been created for travelers and vacationers to have interesting and meaningful leisure.

About country rest in Nizhny Novgorod

A variety of specifics, taste and age ranges, trends, pricing policy distinguishes tourist Nizhny Novgorod. The camp sites and recreation centers subordinate to him are located mainly on the banks of the Gorky reservoir. The northern regions (Linda and Uzola), the south (Arzamas highway), and also the environs of Dzerzhinsk are rich in recreation areas.

Categories

Nizhny Novgorod suburban camp sites are divided into the following categories, differing in price and availability of recreational and health services:

  • Budgetary. Rest houses that were rebuilt from former children's camps, and departmental pensions. They provide comfortable living conditions and an interesting range of services: skate and ski rental in winter, a sauna or a bath, and hiking or cycling in the summer.
  • Special Designed and built specifically for a rural holiday. More functional, equipped with developed infrastructure and service.
  • Base for outdoor activities. Gaining more and more popularity. They provide extreme and active tourism services: paintball, kiting, skiing, etc.
  • Sanatoriums. Every year, they expand the range of services for rehabilitation and treatment, increase competitiveness with popular foreign sanatoriums.
  • Country hotels and clubs. They have a developed infrastructure, the highest level of service, provide a wide range of services. They are a worthy alternative to vacation abroad.
  • Children's camps. Presented in the area in great abundance. For the relaxation of children, summer and winter shifts are provided.
  • Tourist clubs. Their number is growing every year. Active tourism is particularly popular: hiking, trekking, etc.

Those wishing to spend a few pleasant days outside the city should study vacation offers on specialized sites and make their choice.

Fishing and hunting base "Vasilsurskaya Sloboda"

One of the favorite places for hunters and fishermen who come here from all over the Russian Federation. Located near the village of Vasilsursk, at the mouth of the Sura River, on the right bank of the Volga.





Base "Vasilsurskaya Sloboda" is a three-deck floating hotel, designed for 45 people. It consists of 2-3-bed comfortable rooms equipped with everything necessary for leisure activities:
