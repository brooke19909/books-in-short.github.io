Travel to Sharm El Sheikh: hotel rating

If you are planning to travel to Sharm El Sheikh, the hotel rating will help you with a choice of accommodation during your vacation. Of course, the list of recommended hotels is a very useful thing, but you should take into account your desires and preferences and do not forget about financial opportunities. Even with the most economical budget, you can find the best option, because Egypt offers hotels for every taste and budget.





Everyone who goes on vacation to Egypt knows about Naama Bay. It is there that first-class and luxurious five-star hotels are located. Since this is the first coastline of the city of Sharm El Sheikh, the rating of hotels of this category is always quite high, and the prices here correspond to a luxury vacation. If you like a more economical vacation, then you can easily choose one of the hotels located across the road. Even by choosing 3 stars, you, in principle, will not lose anything (the service will still be excellent), and you will be able to visit the territory of almost any five-star hotel absolutely free of charge, work out in the fitness center or dine in the restaurant.
