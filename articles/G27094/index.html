Safe Search - Filter unwanted material

SafeSearch is a useful option in the modern world. Mobile applications and computer browsers can completely exclude questionable content from the search. Everything that is marked 18+ and is only allowed to be viewed by adults is hidden automatically. However, not everyone likes this concern for our psychological and, in some cases, mental health. It so happens that it is very necessary to find exactly the material that is completely excluded from the search by the security settings.





Taking care of children

This function, first of all, owes its appearance to large companies. The constant lawsuits for moral damages, accusations of inciting ethnic hatred, anti-Semitism, racism and even homophobia - all this bothered the major Internet service providers. Safe search is the simplest solution, thanks to which all such problems for companies have disappeared by themselves.

First of all, this mode protects children from viewing prohibited content. This includes not only explicit material, but also scenes with violence and, in some cases, even with political propaganda. Such materials are not just hidden, they are completely removed from the search. As long as SafeSearch is active, nothing prevents children from receiving only reliable information.





There are many variations of this feature. SafeSearch is integrated into the programs themselves. Starting with social networks and ending with large browsers. It is different everywhere and has different thresholds of rigor. Some applications do not even miss scenes with the advent of weapons, while others, on the contrary, hide only material of a frank nature. At the same time, they do not hide the material containing scenes of violence.

Taking care of adults

Creating algorithms that would be impossible to disable is a very bad idea. SafeSearch is designed primarily to protect children from questionable information. However, it is an unpleasant obstacle for adults. The program is not able to understand the age of a person by his search query. However, it is happy to demonstrate the mechanism of its deactivation at the very first request ‚ÄúHow to disable safe search?‚Äù Completely disabling such protection is not always necessary. Some programs allow you to change the degree of filtering. In other words, the user can determine for himself what material to watch. The famous Google search engine and browsers from the same company allow you to determine the degree of filtering. User can choose strict or moderate filtering. It is important to understand that a considerable amount of material is still filtered and disappears.





For those who are not completely satisfied with filtering, it can be completely removed. In this case, the entire volume of search results at the user's request will be demonstrated.

Safe Search - The Difference in Details

Each major social network and every browser that respects itself has a filtering function for material . Moreover, exactly the same option exists in the mobile versions of popular applications. Even those created by third-party developers. However, she always looks and works differently. How to remove safe search completely? A single answer to this question simply does not exist. It all depends on where exactly the deactivation of content filtering is required. In the Russian social network "VKontakte" just go to the search settings and uncheck the box. In the Google search engine and browsers from this company, you can also go to the settings and choose one of three options:

  • moderate filtration;
  • strict filtering;
  • completely disable filtering.

Advanced applications

The computer version of the VKontakte social network no longer has the ability to turn off the filter. This can be circumvented, but you will have to rewrite some of the code yourself. Not everyone is ready for this.
