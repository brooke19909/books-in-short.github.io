Gelendzhik, boarding house "Builder". Gelendzhik, rest, pensions

On the Black Sea coast at the foot of the Markotkh mountain range at a distance of 35 km from Novorossiysk is the city of Gelendzhik. Pension "Builder" is one of the most popular places among holidaymakers from Russia and many other neighboring countries. Here you can and have a great break from the daily bustle, and improve your health.

General information about the boarding house "Builder"

The boarding house is located in a cozy area, which is literally buried in flowers and various greens, including shrubs and conifers. 100 meters from it is its own luxurious pebble beach. Responsive staff is always ready to help. By the way, not every holiday home in Gelendzhik can boast of such a high level of service.





Pension "Builder" is located in a building that was built in 1981. In 2003, its partial reconstruction was carried out, and in 2005 - redecoration. Nearby are the amusement park Admiral Vrungel, the water park Golden Bay and the Utrish Dolphinarium. Thanks to this, the guests of the Builder can spend their free time from procedures, meals and relaxing on the beach in the most interesting place for them. Especially visiting the parks and the dolphinarium is liked by the youngest travelers who require a constant change of activity for an interesting pastime.

How to get there

Krasnodar Airport is 180 km from Gelendzhik, Anapa Air Harbor is 100 km and Novorossiysk Railway Station is 35 km. Accordingly, the traveler can purchase air or train tickets, having decided to spend his vacation in the resort of Gelendzhik. Rest boarding houses located on the territory of this city can offer both passive and active, and health-improving.





Regardless of which type of transport you choose for your trip, you must first arrive in Gelendzhik, and only then look for the Stroitel hotel. So, from the airport of Krasnodar to the resort's bus station, buses regularly leave, the situation with transport from Anapa and Novorossiysk is similar. To get to the Builder after arriving in Gelendzhik, you need to wait for bus number 1 and get to the stop "Sanatorium" Blue Wave ".

Accommodation and meals

Tourists who have come to rest in Gelendzhik, the Stroitel boarding house offers to stay in one of the 188 rooms located in the main and medical buildings, one- and two-story cottages. It is worth noting that the windows of 84 rooms offer stunning views of the sea.

There are three types of rooms in the main building: double rooms in the block (2 + 2 people), standard double rooms and double two-room suites. Guests who choose the first type of room are offered a toilet, shower and refrigerator for two rooms, a TV and a fan are in each room. Double standards are equipped with a private bathroom, there is also a TV and a fan. Two-room suites have a separate toilet and shower. In addition, they are equipped with an electric kettle and air conditioning, a TV. All rooms have access to a private balcony.

In the medical building there are standards that are equipped with two beds, shower, toilet, air conditioning, refrigerator and TV. The cottages have triple two-room rooms and standard double rooms. As for the equipment, it is the same as in the rooms of the medical building.





Between June and September, the Builder, like many other Gelendzhik pensions, offers buffet meals. It is included in the price. During the rest of the year, meals can only be ordered at an additional cost.

Improvement and treatment in the "Builder"

Pension "Builder" has its own, although not very large, medical base. People who have problems with the musculoskeletal system, cardiovascular system, respiratory system, and also suffering from ENT diseases can be treated here.

Specialists of the boarding house apply the following methods of treatment and rehabilitation: massage, hydrotherapy, balneotherapy, physiotherapy, inhalation, mud therapy. Holidaymakers, who decided to improve their health during the period of rest in the city of Gelendzhik, the Stroitel boarding house provides the above procedures every day, except Sunday, although you must make an appointment in advance. The hotel also collaborates with the Blue Wave Sanatorium, which is located in the immediate vicinity, and some services related to diagnostics and treatment are provided there.





Opportunities for fun

For all those who decide to spend their holidays in the city of Gelendzhik, the hotels located on the territory of this resort offer a wide variety of opportunities for a pleasant and eventful pastime. However, unlike the "Builder", not every hotel can boast its own beach. But most people come to Gelendzhik just to enjoy swimming in the sea and a long pastime under the gentle sun. If you are one of them, then the Stroitel boarding house will be the best choice, because its territory smoothly passes into the beach.

In addition, the recreation center has a library, a children's room, a sauna, a sports ground, and a disco. The hotel organizes numerous excursions to nearby attractions, as well as boat trips on boats.
