Ruself stabilizers: operation reviews and an overview of the best models

The stabilizers "Rouself" is a fairly high-quality equipment, as evidenced by numerous positive reviews. This device perfectly helps in cases where the owners of the house urgently need a device that will equalize the input voltage. It is worth adding that it is appropriate to speak here about its alignment, and not about an increase, for example, since with an input indicator too high, the stabilizer will reduce it.

The main selection parameter

There are several parameters by which the Rouself stabilizer is selected, however there is one main - this is the type of network.

In order to go to the store to purchase such a device, you must first measure the voltage in the network using a multimeter. If the readings are significantly reduced - from 90 to 140 V, for example, in such cases it is recommended to purchase a stabilizer that is able to equalize the voltage, starting from 90 V. If, on the contrary, a strong voltage jump occurs quite often in the network, a single-phase stabilizer is preferred . Such devices have a fairly high input indicator - up to 270 V.

Additional selection options

There are several other requirements that must be observed when buying a device such as the voltage regulator "Rouself".

Firstly, this is the total power. The device must have a greater power indicator than all consumers in its network that are turned on at the same time. Knowing this numerical value, you can easily purchase the right unit at an affordable cost.

Secondly, like most other equipment, stabilizers have a coefficient of performance, it is also an efficiency. In this case, the higher the efficiency, the less electricity the device itself will consume during operation at the maximum possible load. The cost of such a stabilizer "Rouself" will be slightly higher, but in the future it will pay for itself.

Thirdly, it is important to pay attention to such an item as the life of the device, subject to all the rules for its use. It should be long enough.

The last item on the list, but not in importance, is the accuracy of stabilization of the device. Experts do not advise purchasing an aggregate whose operating error is more than 8%. Most budget options have an error just in the range of 5 to 8%. However, if there is any refrigeration, pumping, or other exact and expensive equipment in the home network, it is better to spend a little more money, but purchase a Ruself voltage regulator, the error of which is no more than 5%.

General description of models

There are several common features for all models of this manufacturer. All of them have a built-in digital display, which is located on the front panel. It is designed to be able to check the performance of the device at the moment and at any time. In addition, there is an indicator available to help determine both the input and output voltage values.

It should be noted that to purchase a voltage stabilizer "Rouself 1200" or any other model is necessary only high-quality and original assembly. This is very important, since only in this case is it possible to guarantee that the ventilation openings on the case will cope with maintaining the optimal temperature regime of the device for a long period of its operation.

Bypass Mode and Benefits

Bypass mode is an optional feature that is also available on almost all models. It is intended so that the device can redirect the input signal bypassing the functional equipment at the time when a network malfunction occurs. In other words, this is an auto power off function that protects the device from damage.

As for the advantages of Ruself units, it is worth paying attention to the following properties:

  • the shape of the output voltage will completely repeat the shape of the input signal;
  • high coefficient of performance;
  • a sufficiently high level of protection against too high or, conversely, too low input voltage indicators;
  • the range of regulation is wide enough;
  • there is the ability to configure voltage parameters;
  • The price-performance ratio of these devices is very acceptable.
