Should a child be sent to the Mayak camp in Yevpatoriya?

Each parent with worries thinks about where to send his child for the summer holidays, so that it would be fun and his beloved child would be under a reliable wing. Often, for children, mothers and fathers choose the Mayak camp, which is located in the Crimea in Yevpatoriya.



What is attractive about the Mayak children's camp

Parents want to know the maximum information about the institution to which they plan to send their child. The Mayak camp deserves attention for the following reasons:

  • First of all, it is worth noting that only professional teachers work among the camp staff. Therefore, you can be sure that the son or daughter will be in good hands when they arrive at the institution.
  • Of course, it is important that the Mayak camp in Yevpatoriya is located in close proximity to the coast. Accordingly, the kids are taken to the sea so that they receive sea and sun baths.
  • In the Mayak Children's Health Camp, in addition to excellent relaxation, the child will also receive the necessary treatment from specialized specialists. This children's institution specializes in the treatment of skin diseases, cardiovascular system, endocrinology problems. They also help restore the musculoskeletal system, ENT organs, and the gastrointestinal tract. In order to restore the health of children in the camp, various events are held, such as: aromatic therapy, mud baths, inhalations with different contents, massages, healing mineral waters.
  • If parents are worried and afraid to send their child to camp alone, they can be accommodated in the immediate vicinity with a precious child and enjoy at least a rest by the sea. The territory of the complex includes not only the Mayak children's health camp, there are also buildings designed for families. That is, a son or daughter can relax, accompanied by professional teachers, doctors and animators, and mothers and fathers can relax separately, but close to their child.

Reasons to send a child to the Mayak camp

Naturally, mothers and fathers should clearly understand why they should bet on this particular children's camp. In fact, there are a lot of reasons why you should pay attention to this particular children's health camp, and they are as follows:

  • The child will find many new acquaintances and make friends with children from different cities and even countries.
  • Intricate and ideological animators and organizers always know how to amuse children. Sports competitions, stage performances, competitions and singing around the campfire are just some of the options for children's leisure. Every day in the Mayak camp, children are pleased with more and more new activities that are not only interesting, but also useful for the full development of the younger generation.
  • The child will become more independent, since without parents he will have to make decisions and feel more mature.
  • A son or daughter will undergo a course of medical procedures that will give health for the next year.
  • Boys and girls become more confident after staying at the Mayak camp. After all, professional psychologists and educators will surely make sure that everyone is equal in strength, capabilities and skills.
  • A good balanced diet at Mayak Camp will improve your gastrointestinal tract.
  • Daily trips to the sea will saturate the child's body with iodine.
  • Pleasant and comfortable rooms where children are accommodated; campers are properly equipped and will help boys and girls to feel comfortable and at home.

These are just some of the reasons why you should send children to rest and improve their health in the Mayak pioneer camp for children. Each parent will find a lot of advantages that will direct the idea to make a choice in favor of this particular institution for summer holidays.

Nutrition

Resting in the Mayak, the child will never walk hungry. A proper diet includes 5 meals a day. Food for children is prepared varied, exclusively from food tested for suitability.

Healing procedures

Everyone should visit the Mayak camp, regardless of whether they have health problems or not. If they are not there, then the child will simply gain strength by breathing the sea air and taking sun baths. If there are any chronic diseases, then it is possible to count on the following services:

  • Procedures for the treatment and maintenance of ENT organs.
  • Massages.
  • Physiotherapy, where they will do the various procedures necessary to maintain overall health.
  • Sports and gymnasiums, which are located on the territory of the "Mayak", can also rightly be considered medical. After all, after playing sports, a person's normal organs and back become normal.

On an individual basis, for an additional payment, you can stipulate the possibility of providing the child with other medical services.

Housing

Children are housed in buildings under the name "Cornflower" and "Carnation." In these buildings, children will live 4-6 people in the ward. The rooms have beds and a washbasin. On the floor there is a toilet and shower. For a pleasant leisure in the lobby there is a TV, a loggia.
