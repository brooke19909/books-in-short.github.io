How to sew a top with your own hands in half an hour?

A familiar situation: you want a new thing, but you don‚Äôt have the right size, the colors are not the same as what is hanging in the store - it does not fit at all, the fabrics are of poor quality. There is only one solution: to sit down at a sewing machine. Many women are interested in how to sew a top with their own hands, and how difficult it is. It turns out that even a novice craftswoman will cope with this task. After all, we sew the top, as a rule, according to the simplest principles and often even without a pattern.





Of course, it all depends on which model we choose. Beginners are advised to choose knitted materials for the top. It only at first seems that working with stretching tissues is complicated. In fact, using a suitable stitch, foot and thread in a sewing machine, you can do everything in no more than an hour. And if you have an overlock and at least basic knowledge of how to use it, the question "how to sew a top with your own hands" is resolved for you. It is enough to take a piece of fabric equal in length to the product (plus two to three centimeters on each side for allowances). Its width should provide a soft fit to the body. If the fabric is knitted, you can just sew the side seam and get a ring. Elastic materials do not even require the use of rubber bands or straps, and they will still keep on the figure. Although you can use them if you wish. From above it will be possible to make a drawstring into which to insert a knotted ribbon or braid. The easiest option for the product to hold - an elastic band.





Knowing how to sew a top with your own hands, in half an hour you will provide yourself with a new thing by the summer. A strapless top can also be worn in the cold season - for example, under a jacket. This is the simplest model. If you try and look for instructions in fashion magazines on how to sew a top with your own hands (for example, in "Burda Fashionable" or "Diana"), you can make more complex styles. The basic rule is this: if the material is not knitted, a fastener will be required. Often such models are sewn with a lock (hidden zipper) in the side seam. Not every woman likes this decision, especially considering how often the clasps break. In the event that the fabric is inelastic, tucks will also be required in the bust area, which will create volume. Straps can be either home-made (from braid, from the same fabric, woven), and purchased. Nevertheless, for beginner needlewomen who do not know how to sew a top with their own hands, we strongly recommend knitwear. A few simple manipulations - and you will get a completely original product. Then it all depends on the imagination. A bandeau-style top can be decorated both from above and from below - for example, sewn (or glued) rhinestones, lace, ribbons, weaving.

Having processed a thin strip of knitwear with an overlock, you will get a beautiful frill that can be used as a trim or make elegant straps out of it.

Of course, every woman has her own preferences in choosing materials. However, we recommend that you primarily use fabrics that are high in cotton or rayon. Firstly, they are comfortable to wear: they do not electrify, do not slip, and do not stick to other clothes. Secondly, they are extremely hygroscopic and hygienic. Due to its ability to absorb moisture, cotton knitwear is an indispensable material for summer. The festive top can be made of silk and velvet, but you should always remember about your comfort, then at any party in the July heat you can feel at ease.
