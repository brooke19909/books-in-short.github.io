Park-hotel "Festival", Ryazan: photos and reviews

Park Hotel Festival (Ryazan) is a relatively young holiday home, modern and comfortable. There are all the necessary conditions for an excellent pastime at any time of the year, but it is especially good to relax here in the summer, surrounded by forests and ponds. Let's talk more about the Park-Hotel-Club Festival, which is becoming more and more popular every year.





Location

This magnificent place is located in the Ryazan region, in the Klepikovsky district, namely in the village of Chulis, which is 7 km north of the village of Oskino. In the immediate vicinity of the park-hotel - Meshchersky National Park. Thus, the complex is located in one of the ecologically clean and picturesque places, which undoubtedly makes it even more attractive for vacationers. The distance to the recreation center from Ryazan is 90 km, from Moscow - 170 km.

Description

Park Hotel Festival (Ryazan) is a suburban restaurant and hotel complex located near the Meshchera nature reserve, surrounded by beautiful nature and away from noisy cities and busy highways. Ryazan region is famous for its ecology and beautiful nature. Nearby are forests, lakes and rivers. Here you can relax in the lap of nature, away from the bustle of the city and regain your strength. The park hotel is suitable for couples with children, as well as a youth company.





Apartments

In the holiday house there are cottages and rooms for every taste and financial capabilities. Three Austrian buildings, apartment rooms in the Bylina building, the Vikings hotel, VIP cottages De Lux and Russian Manor, houses (one-story cottages) Rainbow, two-story cottages Lesnaya Skazka. In addition, the Provence Hotel opened in 2014, in which visitors are also expected to have comfortable and fully equipped rooms. Park Hotel Festival (Ryazan), the photo of one of the buildings of which is presented below, looks very cozy and surrounded by incredibly beautiful nature.





Infrastructure

Here are cottages of different price categories and comfort levels, sports grounds, hotel buildings, an aquacomplex with two pools, SPA and baths, a kids club, a banquet ground. Also at the guests' disposal are the European cuisine restaurant Heart of Paris, Ukrainian mustard tavern, Sphere summer catering, an open-air stage for performances, the Summer Veranda Russian restaurant, Pickles Mountain cellar, football and basketball fields, tennis court, billiards, table tennis, bike and roller skate rental, kids club, conference room for 100 people and more.

In the Park-Hotel Festival, the warmth of Russian customs and traditions, Scandinavian rigor, grace of Alpine houses and French charm harmoniously combine. People's traditions are honored here, while not forgetting modern entertainment, among which are billiards, a mini-zoo, a water park, karaoke, table tennis, and rental of skateboards and snowmobiles. Waterpark is the biggest pride of a recreation center in Spas-Klepiky. At the entrance is a huge aquarium in which you will see the most beautiful representatives of the deep sea. The cost of visiting the water park, by the way, is quite affordable.





Park-Hotel Festival (Ryazan): reviews

The overwhelming majority of reviews left by visitors characterize the recreation center in the Ryazan region exclusively on the positive side, there are very few negative points. This truly indicates a high level of service in this institution and the quality of the services provided.

Speaking separately about the Vikings building, on the outside it looks like a rock with a cave, inside everything is decorated in accordance with the northern surroundings, and there is also heavy wooden furniture. Most of the visitors who lived here liked the interior. Each hotel building is decorated in its own style. For example, "Rainbow" is a cottage for 6 people. All rooms have air conditioning and secure parking. The recreation center has a large territory, which allows you to place many objects on it.

The main restaurant Heart of Paris is a three-story building. It combines the unusual atmosphere of France and the taste of Russian dishes. It is decorated in accordance with the Parisian style. According to visitors, the design of the institution is beyond praise, and the restaurant is famous for its good cuisine. Although it is noted by some that the range of dishes is not very rich, but for children the choice is quite small. In addition, the price tag is equal to Moscow. Breakfast is free, but lunch and dinner are at your own expense.

The choice of catering establishments here is quite wide: there are several restaurants and cafes in which you can choose what you like most. The water park and baths are especially pleasing to the guests: both in Russian, and in Turkish and Finnish style, which are located on the shore of the pond, as well as a well-groomed territory with thoughtful infrastructure.

Despite the fact that the holiday home has Wi-Fi, in some parts of the complex it can be a problem. It is noted that, if you wish, you can visit Ryazan in order to see the sights of the city: for this purpose, you can order transport here.

Park-Hotel is a beautiful, luxurious building, claiming to be the elite, which is nice to be in, the rooms are furnished with chic furniture. In each of them, all conditions are organized for visitors: there are necessary hygiene products, individual sets of SPA cosmetics, soft terry bathrobes, surprises for children. Of the shortcomings - poor soundproofing. The staff is attentive and polite.

Some point out that not much entertainment is provided for children. The children's pool is well equipped: the water is warm, there are slides, balls and life vests. The buildings and cottages are built from environmentally friendly materials, the complex has a convenient layout, the interiors are thought out to the smallest detail by design masters and delight with their organicity and great style. A varied, healthy diet is provided. The price includes a buffet. On weekdays, it does not differ in the abundance of options. As some say, the menu lacks diet foods.

Visitors are pleased with the large territory of the complex, a mini-zoo, a pool with slides, billiards, sports grounds, and they are also attracted by the interesting architectural concept of the institution. You can rent bicycles, which are very good for walking, besides the tracks are smooth, and it‚Äôs a pleasure to ride them. Well-groomed and clean territory creates the feeling that you find yourself in a fairy tale. Adults and children also like to visit the zoo, which is inhabited by forest deers, funny little horses, raccoons, beautiful swans, camels, peacocks. The hotel has a farm where you can see various animals, including goats, sheep, cows, pigs, geese and ducks. There are two ponds: one of them is for swimming, the other is for fishing. Near them there are gazebos with barbecue facilities.

Often in the holiday home various promotions are offered. Some visitors mark the spa program separately: it features very high-quality treatments. Of the minuses of the park-hotel - mostly high prices for services and a poor access road, however, the latter does not apply to the institution itself.





For whom is a vacation in a Ryazan park hotel suitable?

This place is intended not only for relaxation, but also for business meetings. Park Hotel offers a conference venue with all the necessary equipment. In order to hold large corporate holidays and parties, you can use the three-story restaurant complex "Heart of Paris", which seats up to 350 people. Also, the institution often hosts various celebrations, weddings, holidays and events. Park Hotel Festival (Ryazan) is perfect for those who decide to spend a romantic vacation, as well as go on a trip to the countryside with children and a big noisy company. Comfortable rooms with a wide range of accommodation prices, as well as modern infrastructure, are offered to vacationers.

Park-Hotel Festival (Ryazan): how to get there?

From Ryazan you can get on your car along the highway P123 Spas-Klepiki - Ryazan to the intersection with Egorievsky highway. After that you need to move along the P105 highway (in the direction of the city of Tuma) until you turn to the village of Oskino (the next left turn). From Moscow: along Yegoryevsky highway, intersection with P123, not reaching Spas-Klepikov. Then there will be a left turn, then continue along P105 (towards Tuma) until turning on the village of Oskino. Look carefully at the signs that lead to the Park-Hotel Festival (Ryazan). Address of institution: Ryazan region, Klepikovsky district, the village of Chulis.





Jobs and contact phone

Mobile communication on the territory of the park-hotel is supported only by Megafon and MTS operators. Those who wish to celebrate their birthday will receive a 10% discount on accommodation. At the request of guests, the staff will organize an entertainment program for you with a DJ and a presenter. For advice on living at a recreation center, call: 8 (499) 216-74-25.

For those who wish to become an employee of this institution, job offers the Park Hotel Festival (Ryazan). The vacancies open here are a waiter, sous-chef, head of the sales department, instructor-rescuer of the water park, and a cosmetologist.

Conclusion

This is a unique hotel in its own way, in which different styles, good old traditions, which are attributed to the past, and the best trends and developments of the present, are fantastically combined. Here you can retire to nature, immerse yourself in relaxation and find yourself entertainment to your liking. Most of the reviews, as we see it, are positive: visitors note a beautifully arranged territory, a fairly extensive infrastructure, rooms for every taste, delicious food, high-level service, although the vacation here is not cheap, but it's worth it. This place is ideal for lovers of forest nature and a peaceful atmosphere, in addition, you can arrange an excellent photo shoot here.
