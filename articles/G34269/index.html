The best radar detector with GPS and DVR: reviews

Today, many manufacturers of gadgets offer combined devices, including video recorders and navigators. Such double hybrids have become quite common in the modern market. However, there are more advanced options. In addition to navigators and DVRs, they also include radar detectors. But such a combination of devices is still quite rare. The fact is that the task of manufacturers in this case is very complicated.





Indeed, it is very difficult for leading companies to produce such a device of compact size and optimal price. That is why triple models are produced by only a few companies and have a limited lineup. In this regard, choosing the best radar detector with GPS and a DVR is not an easy task.

Hybrid selection

What is he, the best GPS radar detector and DVR? It is difficult to choose a suitable model due to the fact that:

  • it is necessary to focus on the characteristics of all built-in devices at once, some of which may be close to ideal, while others, on the contrary, differ sharply from it;
  • hybrid gadgets combine many functions due to the loss of quality of work of individual devices.

Given these problems, it is worthwhile to understand that the best radar detector with GPS and a DVR cannot be ideal in all respects. The most picky drivers are advised to buy three devices separately.

Key selection criteria

By what parameters should you choose the best radar detector with GPS and a DVR? You will need to pay attention to the navigation systems of devices. Among them, there are those that are by far the best. Their list includes such systems as Navitel and Autosputnik, TomTom and Garmin, PocketGPS Pro and IGO, as well as Navteo.

A good radar detector with a DVR and GPS should have enough RAM. It should be chosen at the level of 64 MB. But this volume can be large. With an increase in the level of RAM, the gadget is approaching the ideal.





Also, a good radar detector with a DVR and GPS will record normally if it has a two- or three-megapixel camera. However, hybrid devices, as a rule, are equipped with simpler cameras - single-pixel ones, with which you can conduct a general recording of the terrain. As for the various details, they will not be visible on the video of your device due to its low resolution.

The best radar detector with GPS and a DVR should have a display, the optimal value of which is 5 inches. Only in this case, the device will give the opportunity to view the video without affecting the view of the road. Moreover, a higher resolution display will bring the gadget closer to ideal.

Let's move on to the characteristics of the navigator. For this device, an important role is played by the time of its hot, as well as warm and cold start. The first of them is carried out at startup 2-3 seconds after turning off the device. A warm start time means that the device can start after a few minutes. And the third indicator indicates the possibility of the gadget working after a long shutdown (30 minutes or more). The best radar detector with GPS and a DVR, these gaps are minimal.

There is another characteristic by which drivers can select a three-component device. These are the frequency ranges over which the radar detector operates. A quality 3 in 1 gadget perceives them in a wide range. However, it is worth remembering that according to this characteristic, even the best radar detector with GPS and a DVR will be inferior to a specialized device.

Despite its small distribution in the market, hybrid gadgets are included in a certain popularity rating. Each item in this list includes a device that has one or more outstanding capabilities that differ from other analogues. Such a rating will help the driver to navigate when choosing a model.

Conclusion

Anyone who decides to purchase a convenient and practical hybrid should keep in mind that the market for these gadgets is not yet developed. In this regard, before buying, it is important to carefully consider the model you like and evaluate all its advantages and the existing negative characteristics.

It will be necessary to study the existing line of models on the official websites of the manufacturers of these devices. This will not succumb to the tricks of scammers. For example, the Arena PRO 9000 model advertised by some sellers is nothing more than a myth, through which money is drawn from car enthusiasts who prefer new products. Under the guise of a supposedly advanced gadget, they are selling a fake, the real value of which is much lower than the requested amount. Going to the official website of the company, you can see that such a model has not yet been released.





In general, according to motorists, combined devices in terms of quality characteristics are significantly inferior to individual DVRs, radars and navigators. However, buyers have already managed to appreciate the compactness of the device, and do not mind saving on the price. With a preliminary study of the positive and negative sides of each of the models and the conscious choice of a gadget, it will become an indispensable tool for every car enthusiast and a very worthy purchase.
