Hotel Topkapi 3 * (Mahdia, Tunisia): overview, description and reviews of tourists

All those tourists and vacationers who have planned their trip for the cool season, in fact, to decide on a resort is not so difficult. Holidays at sea at present are not luxury, but affordable reality, even if we are talking about early spring or late autumn. Tunisia is an excellent destination for those who want to be in a paradise with an amazing climate, a chic warm sea and meet the planned budget.





If you carefully and demandingly approach the choice of a resort, want to be in a truly heavenly place, we advise you to pay attention to Mahdia. This is a small port town with white-stone houses in the traditional Moorish style - a real oasis in the middle of the desert. Picturesque narrow streets, boulevards - an ideal place for a relaxing family vacation. You will hardly find hotels in the city, mainly they are located north of the center. Most of them are located on the first coastline of the Mediterranean Sea, there are luxury 5 * hotels and more modest options. We offer to evaluate the Topkapi 3 * hotel (Mahdia), and to understand how this option is suitable for potential tourists. Reviews of real vacationers, as well as information from the largest tourist portals will allow an objective review.

About hotel

Topkapi 3 * (Mahdia) - a beach type hotel located on the first coastline. This is a great option for those tourists who want to spend their days relaxing on the Mediterranean coast and enjoy the beauty of the local landscapes. The hotel enjoys a favorable geographical position, in the immediate vicinity are all infrastructure facilities.





Hotel Topkapi 3 * (Tunisia, Mahdia) occupies a four-story building and has a small but well-maintained green area, which makes breathing here very easy. This is a small budget option with a minimum of additional services and good service. This is a great option for those who are limited in funds, but aimed at a great beach holiday. Surely you are interested in Topkapi Beach Mahdia 3 * reviews and prices. Tunisia (Mahdia) is an excellent resort where people with different income levels can relax. If we talk about the cost of living in this hotel, it is enough budget. On average, a double room with one bed and breakfast will cost 3,000 - 3,500 rubles. Depending on the season and duration of the holiday, the cost can vary both up and down.

Rooms

The hotel fund Topkapi Beach 3 * (Tunisia, Mahdia) is represented by 102 rooms. All of them are equipped with the most necessary for relaxation: a set of furniture, appliances, electronics, a private bathroom.

So, the first thing that impresses the guests is a separate sanitary unit, that is, a bathroom and toilet in Topkapi 3 * (Mahdia). Reviews of tourists confirm that they have not seen this in many luxury hotels. This is a huge advantage that everyone will surely appreciate.





Of the technology in the rooms there is air conditioning, providing a pleasant coolness and freshness, a TV with satellite channels and a mini fridge. Each room has an excellent spacious balcony with stunning views of the resort. The furnishings of the rooms are rather modest, of furniture there are only beds, small bedside tables and a table with a mirror. At the same time, it is quite cozy and comfortable. Speaking about the work of technology, tourists note its serviceability. Of course, some encountered minor breakdowns, but all of them were eliminated upon the first call to the hotel staff.

Service

The wait staff is quite polite and pleasant, but not too quick. In principle, for a 3 * hotel, this is normal. The rooms are cleaned daily, bed linen and towels - as they become dirty. The cleaning is superficial, but overall the rooms are kept clean and fresh.

Entertainment on site

Topkapi Beach Hotel 3 * in Mahdia (Tunisia) has a small territory, but there is enough entertainment here: a huge outdoor and indoor heated pools, as well as a pool for the youngest tourists.

On the territory there is a small souvenir shop, as well as several shops where all tourists and vacationers can purchase everything they need. Reviews emphasize that prices here are among the most affordable in the entire resort: you should not even try to find cheaper.





Hotel Topkapi 3 * (Mahdia) is recommended for a relaxing family vacation, but there is an excellent disco with a capacity of up to 100 people. In the evenings, incendiary musical compositions sound here, all guests, even children, have fun. All lovers of active and outdoor activities will be able to play tennis, beach volleyball. An excellent spa complex with a bath, sauna and a range of excellent beauty treatments is available for all vacationers for a fee.

Many vacationers emphasize the high quality of the animation: neither adults nor children will be bored here. Among the vacationers, various sports are held, in the evening there are show programs for them involving magicians, dancers and singers.

Nutrition

Hotel Topkapi 3 * (Mahdia) offers all vacationers meals in their own restaurant. This, perhaps, is the main drawback that almost all vacationers refer to. Breakfast here is scarce: even in season you will not see fresh fruits on the table, they really are not enough. Lunches and dinners are more varied, but you should definitely not expect anything from them: potatoes, pasta, baked vegetables, chicken or fish, a little baking. Of course, no one will remain hungry, but you will definitely not get much pleasure from eating here.





If you consider yourself a true gourmet, want to get acquainted with the national cuisine of Tunisia, we recommend that you refuse to eat at the hotel and eat at local cafes and restaurants.

Beach

The hotel is on the first coastline, has its own sandy beach, sunbeds and umbrellas are free for all vacationers. The beach is wide, clean, the sea is simply amazing. Sun loungers and umbrellas, even in high season, enough for everyone. It is necessary to be prepared for the fact that beach towels are not given out at the hotel - they will have to be taken with you. A rental point for sports and water activities is open on the beach: tourists often rent parachutes, ride bananas and dive.





The beach is ideal for families with small children: warm fine sand, gentle entry into the water, a small depth of sea water in the coastal zone. That is why so many families spend their holidays here.

To summarize

In other words, the Topkapi 3 * hotel (Mahdia) is an excellent option for a relaxing family vacation. The resort is located in a picturesque place of Tunisia, surrounded by greenery, gives the opportunity to spend several days on the chic coast of the Mediterranean Sea. This is a modest budget option that many will surely enjoy. Of course, it is not without a number of shortcomings, but as a whole corresponds to its description.
