Hotel Jiraporn Hill Resort 3 *. Phuket: overview, description and reviews of tourists

What else to attract tourists from Phuket (Patong), if not the emerald sea, sheer cliffs, wonderful snow-white beaches, waterfalls, Buddhist temples and coral reefs?

Introduction

The largest island in Thailand is a great place to relax in body and soul. While cold and frost prevail in our country, this is an excellent vacation time on the island. Here everyone will be satisfied.



Phuket can offer a variety of discos and clubs, bars, shops, restaurants for every taste, many entertainment and shopping centers. If you like outdoor activities, there is a wide range of entertainment: yachts, windsurfing, canoeing, parachutes, water skiing, snorkeling.

A bit about the island

It is never too late to enjoy the warm sun and clean sand while sipping a soft drink. In addition, the island is attractive for tourists with its cultural and historical sights, the rich water world of the Andaman Sea and top-level golf courses.

When choosing housing, you will come across many options. It offers both small bungalows near the beach, which is suitable for lovers of unity with wildlife, and amazing world-class hotels with their own golf course and large swimming pool.

For a budget holiday, the Jiraporn Hill Resort 3 * hotel is perfect. Here you can have a good time!



There are many hotels of different levels in Phuket, a three-star hotel Jiraporn Hill Resort Patong 3 * in Patong Beach will be an inexpensive and high-quality option. This hotel many recommend visiting.

Characteristic

Jiraporn Hill Resort 3 * (Phuket) is a small, but cozy, luxurious hotel, which is located near Patong Beach. The hotel is hospitable, the service is top-notch, the staff is kind and friendly.

The convenient location of the hotel provides an opportunity to quickly reach the white beach to enjoy your vacation. The transfer system offers to organize a free daily shuttle to Patong Beach, as well as, by prior arrangement, to the airport.

Patong Beach is perfect for young people who like to walk, because the beach is full of entertainment. For lovers of unity with nature, the Kamal coast is suitable, where you can relax from the noise and bustle. Karon Beach is an excellent option for active holidaymakers, where you can not only attend parties, but also learn to surf.



If you do not want to walk to the beach on foot, then for you Jiraporn Hill Resort Patong 3 * offers motorcycle or car rental services.

Positive reviews

Most tourists, after a holiday, feel like sharing their impressions of the Jiraporn Hill Resort 3 * hotel. Reviews on the official website of the hotel or other popular sites will be useful for those wishing to go to Phuket.

So, leisure for tourists and accommodation here is quite budget. If you want to relax economically, then this hotel will be the best option for you. In case of a family trip, it is better to book a standard room, as it is larger, has a huge balcony, a kitchenette and many wardrobes.

disadvantages

The hotel has a problem with towels, they are old and washed, and foot towels with cuts and holes, but they are clean and change every day. In addition, far to the beach. You have to rent a bike or use the shuttle service to Patong Beach. This is perhaps the main disadvantage of the hotel. But for such a service and low price it can be forgiven.



The weak point of the hotel is the ground floor. The room on the ground floor is unpleasant due to humidity and dampness, as well as due to insects in the room. The room itself is normal, decorated in white, with ceramic tiles on the floor, but the furniture is a bit old.
