Overview of popular Toshiba air conditioners

Toshiba offers customers a wide range of air conditioners that are suitable for various premises: administrative, retail, residential and so on. It is unlikely that you can find a person who has not heard of this company offering high-quality devices. They are made on the basis of modern technology. Often, it is the Japanese who are the first to introduce new features into their technology.

When buying, you need to understand that regardless of the quality of the purchased device, the long-term operation is also determined by the installation and consumption of the air conditioner, therefore it is better to install the devices with the help of professional masters.

Toshiba air conditioners are preferred by many. They are excellent in appearance and in their characteristics. Devices are able to decorate any room and fit into the atmosphere.

Inverter devices SKVP-ND series

The leader in sales is the SKVP-ND. An air conditioner purifies the air in a room no worse than any air purifier. How did the manufacturer achieve this? An active two-stage plasma filter was built in, designed in accordance with the standards by which JEN air purifiers are created. The technology allows you to achieve maximum results to remove various odors and dust. At the same time, the Toshiba air conditioner, the instruction to which is included, works quickly.

In the Russian Federation, as well as in a number of other countries, a special adaptation model is being received, designed for the climate. Unlike other devices, it has a fan with a speed controller, a compressor crankcase and an external unit sump. The last two parts have an important feature - they are equipped with heating. The technology allows the device to work in cooling mode to -10 degrees and heating to -15 degrees. Low temperatures do not slow down the operation of the device.

In addition, an ozone-friendly refrigerant is installed. Assembly is carried out in Japan, so the manufacturer guarantees quality.

Inverter devices of the SKVR series (wall type)

If you plan to buy a Toshiba air conditioner with an air purification system, pay attention to the SKVR device - it is considered an alternative.

According to the characteristics, it differs little from the above option and meets all the stated requirements. The difference is only in the place of assembly - it is carried out in Thailand. The air conditioner works with ozone-friendly refrigerant and twin-rotor compressors. It costs less, and therefore is in demand.

Wall conditioners NKHD series

If the consumer does not need Toshiba inverter air conditioners, you can look at HKHD with a standard compressor. The device is considered one of the leaders in sales. This is due to the low price, good and fast functionality, quality assembly.

As described above, this series is produced in Thai factories. Equipped with air ionizer and plasma filter. Meets all standards, like other devices of this company.

Air conditioners have the function of air purification and heating, for which a filtration system is used. Due to the fact that the series includes a wide range of products, many use them in rooms whose area does not exceed 65 square meters. m. Due to its small size, the air conditioner guarantees excellent air purification.
