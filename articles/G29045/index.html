Amra (sanatorium in Abkhazia): accommodation, prices and reviews

Abkhazia is often called the "land of dreams" or the sunniest and most hospitable state in the world. Not all tourists know that in the Abkhaz resorts you can not only have a good rest, but also improve your health. Health resorts offering a variety of treatments were erected here during the Soviet era. Many of them still function today, for example, Amra, a sanatorium located in a picturesque place and offering a whole range of modern medical services.

General information about the medical institution

Sanatorium "Amra" was built in 1952, as a health resort, designed to relax and treat senior government officials. The health-improving complex is located in the historical center of Gagra (about 22 kilometers from Adler). The sanatorium has its own comfortable beach, and around its buildings there is a unique natural park. Within walking distance from the complex there are a lot of historical attractions and modern tourist sites.





Amra is a year-round resort, which is especially pleasant; today, in order to relax here, you don‚Äôt need to have any special status, you just need to get a ticket or rent a suitable room for your vacation period without receiving treatment.

Treatment profiles

The sanatorium specializes in the specialized treatment of diseases of the cardiovascular system, musculoskeletal system, and respiratory organs. Wellness programs are also offered to vacationers, including those aimed at improving the psychological state and comprehensive relaxation. At the same time, the complex can accommodate 240 guests.





"Amra" is a sanatorium in which all the conditions for family vacations and accommodation of elderly vacationers are created. Treatment programs include various types of therapy. This is a variety of baths and showers, inhalation, massage. During their stay in the sanatorium, each guest can undergo an examination and get advice from local specialists of various profiles.

Accommodation options and prices

The health-improving complex has two residential buildings, consisting of rooms of various capacities and comfort class. These are luxury and junior suites with European-quality repair and sea views, as well as the standard accommodation option, which is more affordable. All rooms are modernly renovated, have private bathrooms, balconies, comfortable furniture and necessary appliances.

How much does it cost to rest in this sanatorium? A double standard room will cost from 2500 rubles, and a suite - from 3000 rubles. Prices are without meals and additional services at the beginning of the active tourist season.





Amra is a sanatorium hosting guests with a minimum number of documents. In order to spend your vacation in it, you need to purchase and arrange in accordance with all the rules a ticket and insurance for the period of rest. Children up to a year stay in the complex for free.

Sanatorium "Amra" (Abkhazia): infrastructure and related services

The health-improving center has its own private sand and pebble beach. From residential buildings to the sea - about 50 meters. There are umbrellas and sunbeds on the beach, for an additional fee you can ride a boat or catamaran.

On the territory of the sanatorium there are sports grounds, as well as an indoor gym, sauna. The complex also boasts its own library and shared lounges.

For guests of the complex there is a cafe and a bar, meals on vouchers are offered in the dining room ("buffet"), discos are held in the evenings.

Excursions to the sights of Abkhazia can be ordered, also without leaving the resort, at the local tourist office.
