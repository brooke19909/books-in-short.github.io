Best hotels in Stary Oskol: addresses and reviews

Stary Oskol is an administrative center, as well as a city of military glory, which is located on the banks of the Oskol River and its tributaries. The city is rich in natural resources and attractions, which makes it attractive for tourists. And there are many opportunities for entrepreneurs. For whatever purpose you arrive in Stary Oskol, the hotels of the city will provide you with a warm welcome, comfortable accommodation and quality service.
