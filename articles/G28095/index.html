How to connect and how to disconnect a credit of trust on Megafon: instruction

To remain without communication at the most inopportune moment - this situation happens to each of us. This is unpleasant, so we try to protect ourselves from this. Cellular operators offer various methods of insurance: credit of trust, promised payment, transfers between numbers. In the article, we will figure out how to connect and how to turn off a credit of trust at Megafon, and consider limits.

What is a credit of trust?

If you have been a subscriber of the operator for a long time, often use communication, connect additional services, then the mobile operator offers you preferential terms. Disconnection does not occur immediately as soon as your balance runs out of money, but only if the negative balance reaches a certain mark. For Megafon subscribers the range is from 75 rubles to 600. The size of the limit depends on how often and how much you use the services. The calculation of the allowable balance occurs every month.





Terms of use

There is no monthly fee for a credit of trust, connection is free. Only individuals can use the service, while the tariff plan can be any. The subscriber has every right, if he no longer needs a Megafon credit of trust , to disable the service. It is also free.

The size of the credit limit is calculated based on the terms of service in the network and average monthly charges (three consecutive months are taken into account). If you have become a subscriber recently (from 3 to 10 months), and the size of the average monthly replenishment of the account is in the range from 150 to 499 rubles, then you can count on a credit limit of 75 rubles. If the replenishment amount is in the range from 500 to 999 rubles, then the limit is 100 rubles, if from 1000 to 1499 rubles - 150 rubles, if more than 1500 rubles, then 200 rubles of the limit.





You are a subscriber from 11 to 15 months, in which case you can count on the following limits:

  • replenishment from 150 to 499 rubles - 75 rubles,
  • from 500 to 999 - 150 rubles,
  • from 1000 to 1499 - 200 rubles,
  • over 1500 - 300 rubles.

If you have been using Megafon for more than 16 months, the trust loan is as follows:

  • from 150 to 499 rubles replenishment - 100 rubles,
  • from 500 to 999 - 200 rubles,
  • from 1000 to 1499 - 300 rubles,
  • over 1500 - 600 rubles.

How to connect a credit of trust?

There are several ways to go:

  • contact service offices for help, in company salons or to authorized Megafon dealers;
  • dial * 105 * 83 # on the phone and press the call button;
  • send SMS to number 5138 with the text "1";
  • get into the voice menu system by calling 050083 and follow the system prompts;
  • call the subscription service at 0500;
  • use your personal account ("Service Guide").

Before giving consent to the connection of a service, be sure to familiarize yourself with the rules for its provision, which are posted on the official website, as well as with instructions on how to disable a credit of trust on Megafon.
