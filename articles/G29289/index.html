List of cafes on Novokuznetskaya

In the Central Administrative District of the capital of Russia there are a considerable number of institutions that are visited daily by residents and guests of the city. In each of them, something special attracts the visitor.

So, what cafes and restaurants on Novokuznetskaya are especially popular? Here is a list of some of them.

Mitzva bar

An excellent cafe near the Novokuznetskaya metro station, where a huge number of visitors gather daily, the influx of which increases in the evening. This bar is located at the very beginning of Pyatnitskaya Street, in the building at number 3/4.

Inside, everything is done in a classic style, using a huge amount of wood details. As for the cuisine, guests can enjoy deliciously prepared dishes of Israeli cuisine. The menu at the Mitzva Bar (cafe on Novokuznetskaya) was developed by professional Israeli chef Mark Tov, who is the chef of this institution.

As for the cost of dishes, it is relatively low - the average bill for one guest here is about 1,500 rubles, which is not enough for the center of Moscow.



Burger heroes

One of the most favorite places of Muscovites is a burger-cafe near the metro station "Novokuznetskaya". The exact address of the institution: Bolshaya Ordynka street , 19, building 1.

Guests who come here can enjoy deliciously prepared burgers, which are collected in a colorful and fairly extensive menu. Here, especially tasty, according to vacationers, are burgers such as Black Mamba (with cherries and bacon), Popeye the Sailor (with balsamic and spinach), as well as the signature Baron Dupont (with cream cheese and dor -Blue). The institution belongs to the famous Moscow art figure, whose imagination was applied in the process of creating the original interior of the institution.

The cost of food is not high - on average, the bill per person costs less than 700 rubles.

"Wai Mei!"

The cafe on Novokuznetskaya, which offers all visitors to taste delicious and at the same time simple traditional dishes of Georgian cuisine, is regularly visited by those who want to get acquainted with the culinary culture of this country. There is an institution on Pyatnitskaya street, in building 8. You can visit it any day from 10 am to 11 pm. The peculiarity of the institution is that its menu has absolutely no alcohol.

Inside, everything is very simple: tables for guests and an open kitchen, where chefs prepare the ordered dishes. A special style is emphasized by the walls decorated with masonry and windows without curtains, from which a view of the crowded Moscow streets opens.

The average bill in this cafe on Novokuznetskaya is about 500 rubles per visitor. This pricing policy, combined with deliciously cooked Muscovites, is very attractive.



Bq

BQ - cafe on Novokuznetskaya, where guests can be surprised by excellent food prepared in traditional Italian style. In the proposed menu you can find several classic works of culinary art, characteristic of Japanese and European cuisines. However, the emphasis by the cooks of this institution is on meat - it is cooked in all kinds. Of course, the most popular are the magnificent steaks prepared by talented cooks over an open fire.

Inside the bar-cafe on Novokuznetskaya everything was done with taste, in gray-beige-chocolate tones, in some places diluted with lush greenery of large indoor plants.

Guests can sit on soft sofas and armchairs at massive tables made of natural wood and watch the streets of the capital, enjoying delicious food.

The average bill in a cafe BQ is about 1,500 rubles per person.



"Nikolai"

A simple institution that Muscovites loved because of the deliciously cooked food by the chefs. Its menu offers vacationers excellent pastries, among which the attention of the guests was won by branded pies: meat and sweet, puff and butter - for every taste and budget.

Everything is done very simply inside: there are many objects made of light wood in the interior, guests can sit on benches and stools at square tables. The attraction of the establishment is a large bar counter, where you can comfortably sit down and order a variety of drinks.

Guests note low prices for items from the menu. On average, one visitor leaves here about 700-1000 rubles.

"The juice"

A fine cafe on Novokuznetskaya next to the metro is Sok. It has gained incredible popularity by the fact that within its walls you can taste delicious dishes of vegetarian cuisine. Recently, "Juice" is in the TOP of the most visited establishments of this type.

The halls are made in a restrained French style, the interior is juicy, bright, conducive to a pleasant stay. Tables are arranged rather densely to each other, which visitors do not always like. In the summer, guests can sit on a small summer terrace, which can significantly increase the number of visitors. Tables in the institution cannot be booked in advance - such a service is not provided.

As for the prices, they are very pleasant - the bill, as a rule, does not exceed 1500 rubles. In addition, everyone on weekdays can take the opportunity to dine at a 20% discount (the offer is valid at certain hours - from 12:00 to 16:00).



"House-cafe"

It is here that you can be offered several options of homemade dumplings or juicy cutlets that will appeal to even the most demanding gourmets.

Villa della pasta

An excellent cafe on Novokuznetskaya, which is always crowded with guests in the evening. And this is not surprising, because the atmosphere of real Italy reigns in its walls! Guests can enjoy the excellent work of the chefs, as well as the magnificent interior, each detail of which breathes grace and grace.

Inside, everything is made in the style of an Italian village: there are many elements of wood, brick and brushwood, which are harmoniously combined with wicker chairs, lacquered tables for guests and a huge white fireplace that warms visitors in the winter season. In summer, a summer terrace opens in the cafe, where everyone can relax in the fresh air.

As for the cuisine, it is represented mainly by dishes prepared in the Italian style. Signature steak, pizza, pasta and risotto won great popularity among the guests.

The average bill at Villa Della Pasta is about 1,500 rubles, which Muscovites classify as quite reasonable prices.



Meatless

Muscovites themselves call this stunning cafe near the Novokuznetskaya metro station a real meat kingdom, as its menu contains a wide variety of dishes, the main ingredient of which is meat. Grilled dishes and signature juicy steaks have long won the hearts of guests. High-quality beer is in great demand, which is ideal for hearty food presented on the menu. As visitors noted in their reviews, here they prepare magnificent, simply divine pastries.

The interior of the cafe is made in a restrained brutal style. Exquisite wood and leather finishes prevail. Some walls are decorated with brickwork, along one of them there is a massive bar counter. Upon entering the institution, a vaulted ceiling immediately catches your eye, which visually makes the ceilings higher and the room spacious.

The average bill for one guest here is about 1500-2000 rubles.

"Gigue Dryg"

A magnificent cafe on Pyatnitskaya Street, where guests at the entrance are met by the famous literary character - Cheshire Cat. The cafe is located at this address: st. Pyatnitskaya, 62.

The interior of this establishment is simple and restrained, made in a harmonious combination of black, gray and dark brown colors. Guests who come here can very comfortably sit on chairs with soft lining, at the black square tables.

As for the cuisine, the institution is famous for its excellent food prepared in a European style. It has an amazing cocktail list. Some customers who come here cannot resist the temptation to sit at the huge bar, where there is a very large assortment of drinks.

Face control and dress code do not work in this place. There are also wonderful and very attractive prices - one guest, on average, pays about 700 rubles for lunch, which is very cheap by Moscow standards.



"Essay"

Cafe on Novokuznetskaya called Essays will never be missed by true jazz fans - soft music in this style plays constantly here. This gives the atmosphere that reigns here, romanticism and peace. Often in the walls of the "Essay" you can meet famous personalities.

Guests who come to the cafe "Essays" enjoy the interior designed by the designer with great taste. Everything is done in beige color, which perfectly contrasts with red textiles and brick walls. Along one of them is a small stage equipped with modern sound and lighting equipment - young music groups often organize performances here.

As for the kitchen, it is no less sophisticated than the refined interior of the cafe. It is dominated by dishes traditional for European cuisine, in which there are many original snacks and wonderful salads. Separately, it is worth mentioning the rich selection of soft drinks, alcoholic and non-alcoholic cocktails. The menu is small, but the positions presented in it are quite enough to satisfy the taste needs of any gourmet. The average bill per client here is about 1000 rubles.



Belfast

And, finally, another visited institution in Moscow is a real Belfast pub, in which everything is designed in the traditional Irish style. Here every evening gather true connoisseurs of beer and excellent snacks to the foam.

The interior is made in the Irish style. Of furniture - massive tables of large sizes and tall benches on which visitors can sit. Irish flags and small pictures in frames, traditional for the culture of this country, hang on the walls.

The menu boasts a variety of beer snacks made from meat and fish, and grilled dishes. There are a small number of classic salads, burgers, hot and cold branded sausages. The bar card presents a decent selection of light and strong alcohol for every taste.

As for prices, they are quite low: the average bill varies around 1000-1500 rubles per person, which is quite affordable for Moscow.
