Ski resorts in Kazakhstan: photos and reviews

Ski resorts are very popular . Kazakhstan is one of those countries that accept a huge number of tourists who want to ski, sled or use other similar equipment. The article describes the best resorts in the region.

Medeo

"Medeo" is a unique skating rink among the snowy mountains. It is filled with the purest water, polished to a mirror shine and is open to visitors from late autumn to early spring. An unforgettable atmosphere is created by constantly sounding music and magical lighting in the evening. There are all amenities: car parking, benches around the rink, rental of skating rinks and skis, cozy cafes where you can order delicious hot chocolate. In addition, the nearest city is some 15 kilometers from Medeo. Kazakhstan is known for its mountain air, mild climate, a huge number of evergreens, blue sky and beautiful picturesque landscapes. What else is needed for outdoor activities?

According to people, Medeo is a great place to have a good time. All tourists are struck by landscapes that open from the highest point. The whole city, located nearby, from this place is visible at a glance. Numerous reviews of vacationers claim that the service is top-notch.



Chimbulak

Chimbulak is a magnificent place just 25 kilometers from Almaty, the capital of Kazakhstan. It is always warm and sunny. The deep snow layer lies from late autumn to spring. The route can be chosen at your discretion and level of training. There are trails for beginners, as well as long paths for experienced skiers, extreme slopes for snowboarders. All this impresses tourists who visited Kazakhstan.

Chimbulak is a base where all the necessary equipment is provided. You can rent it or buy it at a sports store. Professional instructors will always come to the rescue and answer all questions. A comfortable hotel, restaurants, entertainment, a town for children will also be a pleasant bonus at the resort.

Many vacationers come here not only to ride, but also to visit extensive excursions. The lifts work here, moving on which, you can see stunning landscapes. This is what many visitors highlighted. Judging by the reviews, none of the tourists received negative emotions.



Ak-Bulak

Ak-Bulak is a chic resort an hour's drive from Almaty. Here you can go snowboarding or skiing. Trails are divided by degree of difficulty. The majestic mountains, completely strewn with snow, are breathtaking. All ski resorts (Kazakhstan) are famous for their wonderful atmosphere. The air is simply oversaturated with oxygen, and the feeling of euphoria in this place does not leave even for a minute. Snowboards and skis can be rented. Qualified instructors will never refuse help and will certainly conduct training before the descent. This is an important point, according to visitors.

Rest in the summer here will also be wonderful. At this time, there is a rental of mountain bikes, ATVs. Due to the fact that there are many picturesque glades in the district where you can have a picnic, as well as ride a horse along mountain trails, the time spent in these places will be remembered for a long time. Vacationers like beautiful nature, quite convenient is the fact that there is a number of "civilization".



Edelweiss

Edelweiss is a ski resort in Ridder, namely in the village of Ulba. It opens its doors from the beginning of winter to mid-spring, however, like many other ski resorts. Kazakhstan is a state in which there are really a lot of them.

The resort has 5 tracks for snowboarding or skiing, as well as a separate track for children. Accommodation is provided in hotel rooms. There is a parking for cars, a bathhouse. All equipment can be rented.

Experienced instructors conduct training workshops for children, there is a special ski school. You can warm up and have a bite in cafes or restaurants. Beautiful landscapes, frosty air and bright sunshine will be a great addition to a great vacation. Feedback from visitors suggests that you can come here even with children, because all the conditions provided are as comfortable as possible.



Tekesu

"Tekesu" is a ski resort near the town of Tekeli. There are two long tracks here: one for beginners, and the other for confident skiers and snowboarders. For a comfortable stay there is a magnificent hotel where you can also have a tasty meal. The described ski resorts have good service. Kazakhstan in this area amazes with the magnificence of the snowy mountains, which simply fascinate with its majesty, and the forests stretch to the horizon.

Here you can ride not only on skis or snowboards, but also on sleds or snowmobiles. Instructors will tell and teach all the necessary skills, and in cases of unforeseen circumstances, specialized medical care will be provided. Tourists talk about affordability and complete comfort. The location of the resort is quite good, everywhere amazing landscapes.
