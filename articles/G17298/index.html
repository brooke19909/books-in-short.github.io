Dominican Republic hotels: overview and reviews

The Dominican Republic is the pearl of the tropical island of Haiti. Picturesque nature, white beaches, blue lagoons attract tourists to the Dominican Republic all year round. The combination of affordable prices and good quality service in local hotels is a pleasant advantage of a resort country.

Dominican Republic - tropical paradise

The peculiarity of the Dominican climate is that at temperatures above +30 Àö almost all year round the heat is easily tolerated. Due to the constant light breeze, even air conditioning in hotel rooms is sometimes not used.

In this tropical republic, the traveler immediately feels the unique atmosphere of the holiday and general relaxation. Locals are friendly and always welcome guests. Here no one is in a hurry, everything is subject to the measured rhythm of life of a tropical resort. Tourists will find white beaches, gentle waves, pleasant sea coolness and local holidays and carnivals, which are held in the Dominican Republic constantly.

Guests will also enjoy local hotels with their variety and prices.

Features of hotels Dominican Republic

All hotels in the Republic of Dominican Republic are classified and have stars. Depending on financial capabilities, you can choose a luxurious or affordable hotel. It is important that in the Dominican Republic hotels correspond to their status and meet the expectations of tourists. There are hotels for families with children, noisy youth club hotels. Some are more suitable for active tourists, others - for those who want to spend a vacation on the beach. But all the hotels of the Dominican Republic share several characteristics:

  • all hotels are located close to the beach and ocean;
  • low buildings;
  • almost all hotels operate on an all-inclusive basis;
  • the hotel staff is friendly and friendly, but practically does not speak Russian;
  • almost every hotel is surrounded by picturesque territory with lush tropical vegetation.

A nice feature is also that the cuisine at the resorts is adapted to the European taste, there is no usual for tropical islands problems with specific or too spicy food.

The best hotels of the country

In the concept of "best", each person puts their own meaning. For some, impeccable service is important, for others - an incendiary entertainment program, for others - a restaurant menu. The best hotels in the Dominican Republic can have either five or three stars.

For example, the three-star Talanqura Beach Resort is located in a true paradise. Here tourists will find a calm measured rest on an all-inclusive basis. At the same time, vacationers can use all the services on site for free. The territory itself is so large that guests have the opportunity, if they wish, to spend time in solitude on an immaculate beach or near one of the pools.

Bavaro Princess is a great place for families with children. The hotel has its own nature reserve with amazing plants and animals. On the territory of the ecological park you can easily find flamingos or penguins.

Casa Bonita is one of the best hotels in the world and offers guests an elite vacation. It is located in a secluded, very picturesque place. The room fund is equipped with the latest hotel fashion. All the elements of a luxurious stay are present here: a restaurant with fine cuisine, chic living conditions, golf and horse riding as entertainment, a high level of service.

List all the hotels in the Dominican Republic that are worthy of attention, it is impossible. The main thing is that every person here will find exactly what he needs.

Punta Cana Resort Hotels

One of the most popular resorts in the Dominican Republic is Punta Cana. The largest number of five-star hotels are located here, and new ones are built every year. In Punta Cana in the Dominican Republic, hotels are located in large private areas with lush tropical greenery and convenient infrastructure.

This resort is very suitable both for elite recreation and for youth, as Punta Cana is full of vibrant resort life and a lot of entertainment for every taste.

Tourists reviews

Reviews of Dominican Republic hotels are usually positive. Most often, tourists note a high level of service for relatively little money. The hotel rooms are equipped with everything necessary, the cleaning is done at the proper level, the service is unobtrusive and of high quality. Tourists respond well to the quality and variety of food and drinks. The only thing is advised to wash your hands thoroughly before eating and drink only bottled water.

Admiration for everyone, without exception, is caused by the territories adjacent to the hotels. Exotic tropical plants, small waterfalls and large pools, terraces and cozy secluded corners: here tourists have where to restore peace of mind and have fun. Each hotel also offers guests a lot of entertainment and a variety of show programs. Holidays in the Dominican Republic can be fun and not boring, without ever leaving the hotel.

Each person dreamed of relaxing on a tropical paradise island at least once. And if you are going to make your dream come true, Dominican Republic hotels are waiting for you.
