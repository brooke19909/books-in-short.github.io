Best kebab electric vertical: reviews

Electric kebab is a versatile unit where you can cook meat and vegetables, and without adding oil. The most convenient option - electric vertical barbecue. What are the advantages of these units and which brand to give preference to?

Design Features

Vertical units suggest the location of the skewers in a horizontal position. Such skewers are cylindrical. The heater in them is located in the center, and skewers hang around it - there can be up to 10 pieces. TEN is well protected by a strong cap for which creation heat-resistant glass is used . A special container below serves as a container for juice and fat draining from meat.





Modern electric vertical shish kebab is good in that it has a thoughtful design. Due to it, dishes are cooked at a constant temperature, so they are well-fried, preserving all the beneficial properties. When choosing a model, you need to proceed from a number of parameters:

  1. Power. The smallest kebabs have a small power - up to 700 watts, but such models are enough for a leisurely preparation of a small portion of kebab. If you have more serious requests, then you need to choose a unit of greater power - from 1000 watts. Such models are able to fry meat in just half an hour.
  2. Material. Most of the products are made of aluminum or stainless steel: the former are lightweight, but do not have resistance to external influences. The latter are more reliable, but also weigh more.
