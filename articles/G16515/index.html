Selection of tools and yarn

The variety of patterns, patterns and textures of yarn gives rise to the most amazing forms of hats, and the main role is played by a pattern that is correctly selected for a particular type of yarn. Therefore, we will talk about materials and tools before starting to consider patterns for caps with knitting needles, schemes and a description of which will be offered. Getting started, it is necessary to remember that the preliminary stage is the selection of knitting needles corresponding to the thickness and texture of the thread from which the future product will be made. For example, thin knitting needles (up to 2 mm in diameter) are designed to work with thread, the meter of which in a hundred-gram skein exceeds 600-800 meters. Note that knitting with a thin thread is aerobatics in the execution of hats of various styles.

The most fertile material for knitting hats is yarn of medium thickness. The selection criterion is the number of meters in a 100 gram yarn skein - 250-300 meters. The optimal size of the knitting needles for a similar thickness of yarn is No. 2.5 - 3. The correct ratio of the thickness of the thread and the number of knitting needles will give a more even fabric and help to avoid unnecessarily loose or too tight loops when working.

Today, fashion models of hats made of very thick yarn have come into fashion . They are knitted on knitting needles No. 8-12 with a facial or garter stitch. It is not customary to use various patterns on such knitted fabrics, since, due to the volume and thickness, any pattern on them will look like an excessive pile of loops.

Yarn

The variety of fibers makes it possible to choose exactly that yarn, the implementation of the model from which it seems to the master the most convenient or suitable. Of course, elite brands of natural wool are the best, albeit expensive, material for making a knitted pattern. But today, hats made of wool blend, modern mixed fibers, cotton or linen look no worse.

Knitting patterns for hats: simple and effective

For beginners needlewomen, masters recommend simple patterns based on shawl, front or back smoothness, as well as alternating loops, various versions of which create spectacular geometry from rhombuses, triangles or squares, as, for example, on the diagrams presented in the article.

Shawl knitting is the easiest knitting option. All rows are knitted with facial loops both from the face and from the inside of the knitted fabric. Hosiery is characterized by a smooth surface on the front side of the work and the wrong side - from the inside. Depending on the model conceived, the face can be any side of the resulting canvas.
