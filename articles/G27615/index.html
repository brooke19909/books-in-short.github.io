City of Polotsk (Belarus): hotels. Hotel "Polotsk": address, description, photo

One of the most ancient cities of the Republic of Belarus - the picturesque Polotsk - was first mentioned in chronicles dating back to 862. The city is located in the Vitebsk region, where the small river Polota flows into the Western Dvina. Polotsk is often called an open-air museum. And this is completely justified: on a small area (41 sq. Km) there are many ancient buildings. This Lutheran Church, and the famous St. Sophia Cathedral, and the house of Peter.





Thousands of guests annually visit this picturesque city - on official business, visiting relatives and friends or to explore city sights. Surely tourists are interested in the question: “Where to stay? Is there a hotel, a guest house, a hotel in this small, by modern standards, city? ” Polotsk offers all its guests comfortable accommodation at very reasonable prices. In this article, we will present you only a few options.

“Dvina” (F. Skoriny, 13)

This is one of the oldest hotels in the city. The majestic building, decorated with a colonnade, was inaugurated in late December 1954. Hotel "Dvina" today is part of the hotel complex "Slavic". A magnificent view of the picturesque coast of the Western Dvina opens from the windows of the hotel rooms.

Most recently, major repairs were carried out here, and today Dvina has 111 rooms of different levels of comfort. All of them are equipped with everything necessary for rest and work. The hotel complex has a restaurant where experienced Belarusian chefs will offer you delicious dishes of national cuisine.



Number of rooms

Hotel "Dvina" offers rooms of several categories:

  • single standards;
  • double standards;
  • higher category single and double;
  • Suite (3-room). Room rate includes breakfast.

The cost of living is from 1900 to 2900 rubles per day. The complex has paid parking, dry cleaning, laundry. Guests can eat at the Slavyansky restaurant, Spasskoye cafe, and the cafeteria.

Hotel "Dvina" (Polotsk): reviews

Most guests note that the hotel is well located in the city center. The rooms are comfortable and cozy, the staff is friendly and the prices are quite affordable.

Hotel Polotsk (6th Guards Army, 47)

This hotel is very popular in the city. Hotel Polotsk is located in a calm and quiet area, in a hostel building. It is very small, but it is very cozy and comfortable. Here you will be offered to choose one of five numbers.

Hotel "Polotsk" is located in a multi-purpose building, in which in addition to the hotel there are:

  • beauty saloon;
  • barbershop;
  • buffet;
  • gym.

Guests are offered rooms:

  • standard (single);
  • Suite for two guests.

All of them are furnished with comfortable soft and cabinet furniture, there are TVs, air conditioners. The standard rooms have showers, and the deluxe rooms have a bathroom with the necessary toiletries.



Hotel "Polotsk" is famous for its very affordable prices. Accommodation here will cost you from 1,500 to 2,300 rubles per day (depending on the room category).

“Sail” (Nizhne-Pokrovskaya, 50a)

This hotel belongs to the training base of regional and national rowing teams. She received her first guests in 2012.



Guests are offered here to accommodate thirty-nine single one-room rooms, one one-room single superior, nine one-room double rooms and one single two-room room. All are furnished with the necessary modern furniture. The rooms have televisions, refrigerators, showers.

The Parus Hotel (Polotsk), reviews of which are overwhelmingly positive, attracts guests with a convenient location close to the main attractions of the city, comfort and cleanliness in modernly decorated rooms and quite affordable cost of living (from 1600 to 2100 rubles).
