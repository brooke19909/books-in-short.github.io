Capital of colombia

Colombia is considered a country of fabulous wealth. It combines a spicy mix of Andean, Caribbean and Amazonian culture. Colombia is a unique country rich in resorts, bewitching with its picturesque nature, unusual sea coasts, unique views of the Amazon, and also famous for a large number of various attractions. All this makes it one of the best areas for tourism in South America.



Colombia is directly related to the city of Bogota. It is he who is the largest in the country and the most visited by tourists and travelers. In addition, Bogota is the capital of Colombia.

Colombia is located in the northwest of South America. It is the only South American country that has access to the Atlantic Ocean (across the Caribbean Sea) and the Pacific. This is the reason for the presence in the country of a large number of unusually beautiful beaches.

With regard to climatic conditions, the inhabitants of Europe need to get used to the terrain. Due to the location of many attractions and facilities in the mountain regions at high altitudes, acclimatization at lower altitudes is necessary. In addition, due to the country's hot climate, measures should be taken against solar radiation and solar radiation.



Colombia secured the name "country of princes and beggars." The reason for this is the contrasts in social life. Wealth and poverty here are intertwined. Due to this circumstance, the situation in the country with security is also not entirely simple. This also applies to natural hazards and urban. Colombia is considered a country that stands out among others, as the country with the highest abduction rate in the world . Therefore, when traveling in Colombia, be careful. In addition, drugs and narcotic drugs are common in the country. Avoid gifts from unfamiliar residents of this country. Do not forget to also check the currency that you are given change to, or for which you exchange your money, because a certain amount of fake dollars is turning around in the country. As for natural hazards, one can name the presence of many sharks off the coast of the country, as well as a large number of various marine poisonous creatures.



Colombia is one of the most expensive countries in the world. The capital of Colombia is located on the banks of the beautiful Rio San Francisco River. Prices here are much higher than in neighboring countries. Colombia is also considered a country of gold and silver, as it occupies a leading place in Latin America in the extraction of these natural resources. In addition, it is the world's largest exporter of emeralds. So, while relaxing in Colombia, do not forget to bring products from these precious metals as a souvenir.

The capital of Colombia - Bogotá - combines both ancient colonial and modern architecture. The city is full of contrasts: wealth and poverty merge with each other, on the narrow, tangled streets, winding along the mountain slopes, you can meet both cool cars and mules, luxury houses here intersect with miserable shacks. The capital of Colombia is also famous for its sights, museums and churches, its cultural life. All this mixture of opposites makes this unusual city one of the charming, but at the same time aggressive cities of this country.

Holidays in Colombia bewitch all tourists and travelers, and you will remember for a lifetime.
