Where to go to rest in the sea in winter - interesting ideas and recommendations

Winter holidays attract a large number of tourists. The vastness of Russia is unlimited, so it can be organized on the territory of its homeland. However, some people prefer to spend holidays in other, hotter and exotic countries, exploring the sights and culture of various peoples. Where can I go to relax in the winter? What features do these or other resorts have? This is our article.

Central Europe

In winter, the central part of Europe is a great place to visit with a tourist visit. Where to go to have a rest in the winter abroad cheap? A great option could be Finland, Sweden and Austria. It is here that at this time of year the New Year atmosphere reigns, pleasing to the eyes of both adults and children.

In Germany and Austria, New Year's fairs and sales are held in winter. On them you can not expensively buy great things and souvenirs for relatives and friends. In addition, here you can have fun on seasonal attractions, enjoy national treats and take part in competitions. The most fun cities in the world are Munich, Cologne and Vienna. However, as experienced tourists note, a large number of travelers come here, due to which a noisy crowd of people forms, which not everyone likes.

In Rovaniemi in Finland you can meet the real Santa Claus. That is why a trip to such a "magical land" will appeal to children.

Warm countries

When cold weather rages in Russia, many of its inhabitants dream of the onset of heat. However, some prefer not to wait for warm days, but to take matters into their own hands. Such lovers of summer go to the southern countries, to the tropics, to the famous resort islands. Where to go to have a rest in the winter? Where will it be warm and cozy even in January and February? The most popular destinations are Egypt, UAE, India, Thailand and Kenya. It was at this time in the above states that air temperatures were still high. In recent years, holidays in Madagascar and Tanzania have become popular.

Where can I go to relax in the winter at sea? A great option can be the Mediterranean countries. The refreshing sea breeze here pleases tourists at any time of the year, and the prices for trips in the winter season are pleasantly surprising. However, it should be remembered that in countries such as Greece, Italy, Cyprus in the winter is pretty cool, it can even snow. Therefore, there is no question of beach vacations. But at this time, prices in hotels and restaurants are quite acceptable, and in stores often arrange all kinds of promotions.

Exotic islands

Where to go to rest in the winter at sea? Tropical islands can become an excellent option, many of which are washed not only by sea, but also by transparent oceanic waters of blue color. Many fans of exotic prefer this type of vacation, however, as they notice, not everyone can afford it, since the cost of a ticket to such places is usually about 1500-2000 dollars for two.

The most popular destinations where many travelers seek to relax in the winter are Seychelles, Maldives and Canaries. In addition to a wonderful paradise vacation, every traveler is offered a wide selection of excursions and all kinds of entertainment. In addition, the islands offer top-class service, which always pleases fans of quality relaxation.

Why do tourists choose these islands as places to go to sea in winter for relaxation? Everything is simple. It is during the cool period in the climatic zones where they are located that the real paradise atmosphere reigns, and the temperature is kept at around 35 degrees, so that the water warms up to the state of fresh milk.

Where to go to relax in the winter at sea inexpensively

Winter vacations are not always expensive. Many travel agencies can offer quite budget options for spending time, moreover, in real paradises. An example of this is an inexpensive vacation in the famous Maldives. As some experienced travelers note, if you do not pay attention to hotels located on individual islands, you can please yourself with an exotic atmosphere and first-class service at a very reasonable price. Many of them recommend visiting Maafushi - a place in the Maldives, where a huge number of guest houses are located. It is in them that most tourists prefer to rent housing at an affordable price - about $ 45 per day. As for food, in local restaurants it costs, on average, about 3-5 dollars per person per day.

An inexpensive vacation can be organized in Cambodia (Sihanoukville), where the daily cost of a decent room is about $ 5, as well as in Gran Canaria (Spain), where you can enjoy the golden sands and a pleasant tropical atmosphere at a price of $ 18 per person per night .

Where to go to relax with a child in winter

Often, people who love to travel and have already had children, the question arises of where you can relax with them in the cold season. Of course, there are many options.

When choosing a place where you can go on holiday abroad in the winter, having a child, you should pay attention to whether the hotels in this region provide accommodation for minors. As a rule, these are available in most hotels in the UAE, as well as on the island of Sri Lanka and Thailand, which offer excellent accommodation for tourists of different ages.

For the youngest travelers in the above countries there is a wide selection of entertainment. An example of this is the Dubai Mall and Kidzania in the UAE, as well as a huge number of interesting natural gardens, water parks, amusement parks, as well as other entertainment corners and venues that will not be against visiting not only kids, but also their parents.

Preferring holidays in European countries (in the case of planning a family vacation), you can pay attention to such famous cities as Levi and Romaniemi (Finland), Livigno (Italy), Poiana Brasov (Romania), Bansko (Bulgaria), Jasna and Bohinj ( Slovenia).

Ski resorts

Where to go in winter to relax abroad? When choosing a permit for the winter, you can consider the ski resorts, which are both in Russia and abroad. Many tourists prefer to spend their holidays in Bohini (Slovenia), Luosto (Finland), Ramkalni (Latvia), as well as in Shahdag (Azerbaijan). All these resorts are famous for having beautiful slopes. You can ride there on sleds, snowboards and, of course, skiing. A considerable number of tourists say that Bohinj, Ramkalni and Shahdag are the most correct answers to the frequent tourist question, where to go for a cheap winter vacation. The daily cost of living in local hotels ranges from 13 to 40 dollars. Of the above, the most expensive ski resort is Luosto. Here for a day you have to pay from 170 dollars. However, being in this village, you can get a lot of positive emotions from visiting local attractions. One of the most striking among them is the Amethyst Mine and the Northern Lights Chapel.

Over a long period, the Polish Zakopane remains a very popular ski resort, where people from different regions of the world come. Many travelers are attracted not only by the original and safe slopes, but also by the unique culture of the local population. The price of housing in Zakopane is quite small - from $ 15 per day for one room. Tourists are very fond of visiting local attractions, among which the most popular are Dunajec Castle, the village of Chocholow, and the impeccably beautiful Sea Eye Lake.

Unique journey on the Golden Ring

Where to go to have a rest in the winter? You can turn your attention to the popular tourist route along the Golden Ring of Russia. Choosing just this type of vacation, you can learn a lot about your native country, as well as get acquainted with a huge number of the most interesting and unusual sights, since the tourist's path runs through the ancient settlements of the vast Motherland. As practice shows, it will take about a week to see all the attractions included in the tourist route. However, if you wish, you can visit only a separate ancient city. This opportunity also attracts many.

As for the cost of the trip as part of a trip from a travel agency, it is about 18-20 thousand rubles for two. Subject to the purchase of a one-day tour to a certain ancient city, it can cost up to 5000 rubles.

Many experienced travelers recommend starting their trip from Moscow - in this case, the total cost of the trip will be much cheaper, and the tour will become more interesting.

Another vacation in Russia

In addition to a tour of the cities included in the Golden Ring, travelers can choose a tour to other equally interesting places. An example of this is a trip to the Caucasus, where mighty mountains and original professional descents will surprise even experienced skiers. A great option for fans of this type of vacation will be the famous resort of Krasnaya Polyana, located in Sochi.

Fans of extreme recreation in the winter tend to get to Altai, the Kamchatka Peninsula or Lake Baikal, which is one of the main attractions of Russia. No less beautiful nature reigns on Lake Seliger, renting a house near which can cost about 12,000 rubles for 3-4 days.

Where to go to have a rest in Russia in the winter? You can arrange an inexpensive vacation for yourself and your family in Kaliningrad. Fans of unique architecture often go to this village, which is famous for its huge number of attractions. In winter, they, decorated with hats of snow, look more original. The cost of an excursion tour to this ancient city is about 15-17 thousand rubles. Duration is about 3-6 days, depending on the tour package. During this time, you can explore various unique objects and get acquainted with the culture of the local population. Having been here, travelers are delighted.

Veliky Ustyug is a region that is becoming especially visited on the eve of the New Year holidays and Christmas. The secret of his fame lies in the fact that Santa Claus lives here. Most tourists come here with their children in order to introduce them to a living fairy-tale character. Prices for accommodation at the hotel of Veliky Ustyug in the midst of the spa season reach 2000 rubles per day, which is acceptable for many Russians.

Crimea

Where to go for a cheap winter vacation? In Russia there are a considerable number of interesting places and cities that attract tourists with their culture, attractions and nature. Recently, one of the most popular places is the Crimea.

On this peninsula there are a large number of cities, each of which has its own culture and customs. Basically, tourists prefer to visit winter Yalta. A weekly tour for two in this city costs about 30,000 rubles with the condition of living in a hotel with a high level of service and food. If desired, travelers can include tours and any other entertainment in the price.

Fans of mountaineering can go in for winter climbing Mount Ai-Petri, which is located in the Crimea. Many tourists love visiting the Nikitsky Botanical Garden, as well as the hero city of Sevastopol, where there are a huge number of sights of the war years.

Experienced travelers recommend not contacting travel agencies with a request to organize a vacation in Crimea, to do it yourself, competently using Internet resources that offer affordable housing in the private sector.

Visa-free countries

Where to go to relax in the winter without a visa? There are also a lot of answers to this question, moreover, among them there will be both neighboring countries and very distant states.

An example of an ideal place for a winter holiday can be Abkhazia, South Ossetia, Georgia, as well as the Republic of Belarus. Russian citizens have the right to enter these states without a visa. To cross the border, only an internal passport is required.

Where to go to relax in the winter, without a visa, but with a passport? Many countries are happy to welcome Russians on their territory. Examples include Barbados, Bahrain, the United Arab Emirates (if you have a biometric passport), Venezuela, Vietnam, Dominican Republic, Egypt, India, Jordan, Iran, Cambodia, Kenya, Cyprus, Macedonia, Lebanon, Moldova, Nepal, Panama, Paraguay, Serbia, Syria, Chile, South Korea and South Africa. Israel is very popular among visa-free countries, the border of which can be crossed by a passport with an insurance policy.

Among the states where Russians can go on vacation in the winter without a visa, there is also a large list of islands, including the most popular exotic ones: Maldives, Cuba, Sri Lanka, Seychelles, Philippines, Northern Mariana, Hainan, Madagascar and some others.

As a rule, to cross the borders of these states, you need a passport, a certain amount of funds for accommodation (for each country a separate amount), as well as a return ticket.
