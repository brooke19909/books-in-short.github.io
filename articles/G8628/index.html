Hotel "Nostalgie" (Ussuriysk): address, description of rooms, infrastructure, photos and reviews

Ussuriysk is a small quiet town located in the Primorsky Territory. Every tourist who goes on a trip to the Far East will definitely spend several days here to get acquainted with its magnificent nature and interesting places.

Overnight options

There are a lot of places where to stay for the night in the city of Ussuriysk. The most popular at the moment are the hotel complexes "Ussuriysk" (2100 rubles / day), "Oasis" (2500 / day), "Imperial" (2000 rubles), "Eden" (2 thousand 800 rubles per room).

Choosing for yourself a more budgetary, but no less comfortable option for accommodation, we advise you to pay attention to the hotels Vesta (1100 rubles / day), Albatros (1400 rubles) and Nostalgie (1600 rubles per room). The last accommodation we will consider in this article in more detail.

Nostalgia

Hotel Nostalgie in Ussuriysk is a small budget hotel located in the heart of the city, which is ideal for unassuming travelers who want to stay in comfort and coziness for an adequate price. In this review, we will try to provide more detailed information about this property. Below you can find out his exact location, services, description of rooms, as well as see his real photos.

Hotel coordinates

The exact address of the Nostalgi Hotel is Ussuriysk, Komsomolskaya Street, 42.

The hotel, according to guests, has a very good location in the city center. In walking distance there is a bus stop, shops, a huge supermarket, several cafes. A ten minute walk is Lenin Square.

The distance from the airport of Vladivostok to the hotel is 65 kilometers, from the railway station - three kilometers.

Contacts

If you plan to rent a room in this organization, then you should contact its manager. The phone number of the Nostalgie Hotel in Ussuriysk can be found on its official website on the Internet. It also contains more detailed information about the work and services of the complex and an online booking service is available.

Description of the hotel complex

The Nostalgie Hotel in Ussuriysk is relatively new. She began to receive guests only four years ago, but in such a short time she managed to get a lot of positive feedback. The hotel is a modern three-story building. It offers guests - many rooms of various categories at very competitive prices. Also in the complex there is a bar that is open at any time, a beauty salon and an excellent sauna.

Number of rooms

The rooms in the complex are very clean, bright, have a simple interior. Each room is equipped with a TV and air conditioning. There is a dining area with a fridge and electric kettle. The bathroom comes with free toiletries and slippers.

In total, there are seventy-five rooms at the guests' disposal, among which are standard single, double, triple and quadruple rooms, as well as junior suites, deluxe rooms, suites and an improved room for one person.

The hotel allows people of all ages. But it is worth noting that extra cots are not available in the room.

Nutrition

A restaurant and a 24-hour bar are on site. The restaurant "Nostalgie" (Ussuriysk) serves European cuisine, the bar has an extensive assortment of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. The hotel offers a continental breakfast, which is included in the room rate. Breakfast includes porridge, eggs, scrambled eggs, sandwiches with sausage.

Accommodation cost

Many guests of the city have repeatedly talked about favorable prices for hotel accommodation, the description of which is presented in this review.

The hotel accepts both cash and bank transfer payments.

Interesting places nearby

It is best to start a walk around the city from the historical center - "New Arbat", as the locals call it. It is located just two kilometers from the Nostalgie Hotel. Here you can see several buildings of the XIX century with beautiful arches and bas-reliefs and a magnificent cathedral, which was built in the traditional Russian style in 1907.

Connoisseurs of cultural events will be interested in visiting the House of Artists, the theater of the Eastern Military District, and the Komissarzhevskaya Theater.

The Museum of History and Local Lore, the building of which is considered a monument of architecture, features more than 1,500 exhibits introducing the city guests to the traditions of the peoples living in Primorye.

The Museum of Nature contains stuffed birds and animals, herbaria, fragments of significant ecosystems of the Far East.

For tourists with children, a trip to the Sapsan Zoo will be interesting, where you can see a bear, wolf, deer, leopard and other animals.

The landscape and historical park "Emerald Valley" is the center of Primorye tourism, also a very attractive place for travelers. Here you can visit a wooden fortress, attractions of ancient Russia.

A wonderful place to relax is the lotus lake.

Another attraction of the city is Komarova Nature Reserve. About 850 species of plants grow here. If you are lucky, you can meet local residents: deer, chipmunks, and roe deer.

In the city park you can see the symbol of Ussuriysk - a huge turtle made of stone. The monument was created in the seventeenth century.

What tourists say about the location

Judging by the reviews of vacationers, the hotel "Nostalgie" is a pretty good budget hotel in the city. Many guests note its excellent location, spacious rooms, friendly, polite and kind staff.

The hotel rooms have everything you need for a comfortable stay: simple but comfortable furniture, balcony, air conditioning, toilet, fireplace. The rooms are clean and tidy.

Departure from the rooms is carried out exactly one day after arrival, which is very profitable and rarely found in such institutions.

The room rate includes breakfast. Many guests note that the dishes are without frills, simple, homemade, but very tasty.

Another big plus is the availability of free Wi-Fi with a normal network signal, as well as guarded car parking under the windows of the complex.

The guests did not find significant disadvantages of the hotel. The hotel is quite consistent with its price and is suitable for undemanding tourists, which is very important to consider before booking. The hotel positions itself as a two-star and is fully consistent with this.

From comments: according to some guests, the rooms lack a hair dryer for drying hair. Guests also note poor soundproofing of the walls.

Hotel rating is quite high. Tourists give him eight points out of ten.

Conclusion

Summing up, we can say that the hotel is simple, without frills, but the ratio of quality and price is very good.
