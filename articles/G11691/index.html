The best beaches of Nha Trang (Vietnam). Nha Trang Hotels with Private Beach

The hottest destination for tourists planning a vacation in the Socialist Republic of Vietnam is Nha Trang. The beaches of this resort will be the topic of our article. But first, we briefly describe Nha Trang itself. What is special about it and how does it stand out from other coastal cities of Vietnam? The fact is that the season in this part of the country lasts all year round. In winter, when the region of Hanoi is cool and damp, and in the summer, when the rainy season begins in southern Vietnam, beautiful weather reigns in Nha Trang. And everything at this resort is aimed at obtaining a quality beach holiday. But here you can not only sunbathe and swim. Nha Trang is both thermal springs and healing mud. So a beach holiday can be combined with treatment. And whoever is in good health, can have plenty of fun in the many clubs and discos in Nha Trang. Romantics will be delighted with the local bay with beautiful islands, and gourmets can enjoy grilled lobsters right on the beach. The city has many budget hostels and hotels, and the front page is occupied by luxury hotels.



City Beach

Before starting our review of the best coasts of the resort, let's discuss when it is best to go to Nha Trang. It has already been mentioned that the weather in this part of Vietnam pleases tourists all year round. But the South China Sea can "put them a pig." From the beginning of November and somewhere until mid-February, a storm season is observed off the coast of Nha Trang. In principle, they may not be, but strong currents make swimming dangerous for inexperienced swimmers. But, in principle, you can jump on the waves off the coast. What are urban beaches? Nha Trang stretches along the sea for seven kilometers, from north to south. And all this edging, in fact, is one sandy strip. This is not to say that this is one beach. Although there is no clear border between the northern and southern parts, they are different in terms of cleanliness, comfort, and well-developed infrastructure. The water is clean everywhere, and the coast is cleaned as far as possible. But in the northern part, right next to the beach, there is a busy Tran Fu street, always loaded with motorbikes. And in the south, a spacious pedestrian zone with a park and sports fields separates the road from the sea. In the northern part, the beach infrastructure is not developed. All water activities, as well as places equipped with umbrellas and sunbeds (rent - 50 thousand dong), are concentrated in the south.

Hong Heo (Jungle Beach)

This is a real paradise. Here you can feel like Adam and Eve in Eden. Close to the sandy shore, the jungle approaches, mountains rise from which a twelve-meter waterfall falls. In his bowl you can swim. A strip of sand is broken by mountain ledges into small beaches hidden from prying eyes. Nha Trang is far away - sixty kilometers on the other side of the Heo Peninsula. Swimming at Hong Heo is absolutely safe. The ocean wave breaks away from the coast, and the shallow water is quiet. The picturesque nature of Jungle Beach attracts newlyweds and romantics here. Monkey Island

Nevertheless, the best beaches of Nha Trang are located not on the mainland, but on the islands. There are a lot of them in the local bay - from small to very tiny. A visit to Monkey Island does not promise a relaxing beach holiday. Rather, it will be a pleasure tour. After all, the name of the island is consistent with its main inhabitants. There are a lot of monkeys here. They are semi-tamed, which means that they will beg for food from you, and especially impudent individuals and extort it. It is better not to take small children to the island - the monkeys perceive as a competitor anyone who is shorter than their height, and can offend such a "stranger." But even without children, you need to look at both. The inhabitants of the island love to rummage around in bags, grab cameras and carry them to the crowns of palm trees. But, despite this, tourists like to sail to the local beaches. Nha Trang is located nearby, and there is the necessary infrastructure (sunbeds, umbrellas, jet skis, kayaks, a good cafe, etc.). Descent into the sea is very gentle. To the depth you have to go about fifty meters. There are no hotels on Monkey Island, so you need to leave it before sunset.

Water slides, which are breathtaking even for the most courageous, diverse rides, the largest aquarium in Vietnam - all this fit on the largest island in Nha Trang. At the water you will find no less choice of entertainment and just the perfect tourist infrastructure. Despite the abundance of people, the island has the cleanest sandy beaches of Nha Trang. Tiny islets

If you are interested in not only a relaxing beach holiday, but also exciting snorkeling, head to the Kau Da dock area. Regular ferries to some islands run from there. In order to get to the very tiny land areas, surrounded by coral reefs and turquoise shallow water, you will need to rent a boat. Of course, you will need to take lunch with you, and you should not count on special amenities, but it is there, on the small islands, that the truly best beaches of Nha Trang are located. Reviews recommend visiting Hong Tam, Hong Mot, Moon and Mieu. These islands are famous for their safe coral reefs, calm sea with no strong currents and clean beaches. In addition, they can be easily and quickly reached from Nha Trang.



Front Line Hotels with Private Beach

This resort has special rules. The entire coastal strip is in municipal ownership. This means that all beaches are open to the public and free of charge. However, this rule has exceptions. Almost all the luxury five-star hotels on Nha Trang beach have their own fenced off sites from the world. An example is Evason Ana Mandara Nya Chang Resort, one of the most prestigious hotels not only in this resort, but throughout Vietnam. To the south of the city is Six Senses Ninh Van Bay, a five-star hotel complex consisting of villas. Other hotels with their own beach include Winperl Resort, Hong Tam and An Lam Ninh Vinh Bay. But hotels with a rank lower than their beach do not have. The only exception is the Quartet of Diamond Bay Resort.
