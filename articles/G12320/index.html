Hotel Tri Trang Beach Resort





The hotel is located 16 kilometers from the central part of the island and approximately 34 kilometers from the airport. Tri Trang Beach Resort 3 was opened in 2005, and reconstruction was carried out in 2011. The total area owned by the hotel exceeds 11 square kilometers. The location of the hotel is very, very favorable, as it stands on a mountain, and below is the promenade, on which every vacationer can find entertainment to taste.

Tri Trang Beach Resort 3 welcomes both foreigners and tourists from Russia. The complex has several tall buildings at its disposal, the most cumbersome of which is building No. 5. Its terrace offers magnificent views of the raging sea. Also, the road to Patong is very clearly visible from this building.

The hotel has its own private beach with pure white sand. The beach area is equipped with several changing rooms, a rescue service, rental of leisure equipment and kiosks with many exotic goodies. Also, this hotel has its own pool with clean, warm water and a hydromassage function.
Each hotel room has a wide bed, a refrigerator, a TV, internet, a bathroom with all amenities. Also, in each room for vacationers there is a terrace or balcony and a climate control system.

Guest reviews

According to vacationers' reviews, the second building is considered the best hotel building, and the fourth building is considered the worst. Especially good guests speak about the services of local masseurs. Although the cost of massage at the hotel is much higher than the price of that offered on the beach, nevertheless, the hotel makes it much better. Massage at the hotel costs 450 baht, and 300 at the beach. Directly on the seashore is a restaurant that is famous for its fresh seafood dishes. Vacationers note the advantageous location of the hotel. Another very attractive advantage is its own beach recreation area. The place is very quiet and clean, you can surf the Internet for free. In terms of food, Phuket hotels practice a buffet system and free breakfast and lunch. This suits many vacationers, however, there are those who do not like this food and those who would like to have shops and catering facilities near the hotel. Also, there is not enough cheaper transport. A free shuttle to Patong is often crowded, and there are simply no additional transportation options.

Among the minuses of the hotel, vacationers also highlight the fact that the elevators that deliver people to the beach work very poorly, and there are a lot of flights of stairs in the hotel.
Very close attention should be paid to the height of the waves, which reaches its maximum in the summer. Speaking about Tri-Trang beach, it should be noted that the height of the waves is minimal here, therefore, rest with children in this place is completely safe. It is characteristic that when the waves are completely absent on the other beaches, they are present on the Tri-Trang, albeit insignificant. 3 star hotels in Phuket (1 line) are represented by another decent hotel.

Hotel Rattana Beach Hotel




The hotel is characterized by unsurpassed quality of service and comfort. Everything in the hotel is designed in the traditional architectural style of Thailand, which has always fascinated and attracted tourists from different countries. The level of training of hotel staff is considered very high, which makes staying there incredibly comfortable. The hotel has wireless Internet, all infrastructure, a swimming pool and even there is a water park nearby. All rooms are equipped with the latest technology. There is a shower, separate bathroom, TV and fridge, all bath amenities.

Phuket International Airport is a 45-minute drive from Rattana Beach Hotel 3. The hotel has 45 comfortable Standard rooms (1, 2 and 3 beds), equipped with a TV, air conditioning, refrigerator, bathroom with shower or bath.
The hotel services include:

  • Continental breakfast.
  • Restaurants and cafes at the hotel.
  • Free places to relax on sun loungers.
  • 24-hour front desk.
  • Left-luggage offices.
  • Children's room.
  • Car rental.
  • Exchange office.
  • Dry cleaning and laundry.
  • Carrying out excursion trips.
  • The help of the secretary.
  • Sauna and Turkish hammam.
  • Spa treatments.
  • Gym with a variety of fitness equipment.
  • Jacuzzi with hydromassage.
  • Diving courses.
  • There is a stylish bar at the hotel with an abundance of a variety of cocktails and light snacks of fruits or vegetables.

Vacationers about the hotel

Tourists note the excellent service and friendly attitude of the staff of the Rattana Beach Hotel 3. The rooms are selected as soon as possible and meet all the wishes of the guests. The beautiful view from the windows will not leave anyone indifferent. Very good location of the hotel only pleases. It is necessary to pay for communication via the Internet, but this does not spoil the impression.

Nearby there is a souvenir shop, various agencies, shops, beauty salons and parking lots. Food prices are low. As for the hotel rooms, all bed linen and dishes are clean, the seating area includes tables and chairs. Towels are changed every day. There is no problem cleaning the rooms, the staff is honest and not capable of theft.

Hotel Horizon Karon Beach Resort & Spa 3 *





The project of the hotel complex was designed specifically to serve entire families. Horizon Karon Beach Resort 3 was opened in 2005. It is located next to the bays of Karon and Patong. The distance to the latter is only 7 kilometers. Phuket Airport is located 70 km from this place.

Accommodation

The list of rooms at the hotel includes 90 different options, of which 65 have sea views and 25 have beautiful gardens with a variety of trees. To hotel guests:

  • Restaurant of sea delicacies.
  • Grill bar with various dishes cooked on charcoal (chicken, fish, meat, vegetables).
  • Internet cafe with free network connection.
  • A minibar is located next to the pool and offers cocktails and light snacks, open from 11 p.m. to 9 p.m.
  • Conference rooms for up to 200 people at a time.
  • Beauty and SPA Center.
  • Gym.
  • Club for children of any age with experienced teachers.
  • Common beach.

What do tourists say?

Guest reviews are mostly positive. There are some disadvantages, but they are insignificant.

Equipment for recreation in the beach area is provided exclusively for a fee. Each room has a bathroom with shower and all amenities, TV with antenna, air conditioning, safe for valuables, telephone. Also, there is equipment for ironing things (iron and a special board). There is a thermos and everything you need for making tea or coffee. Also, there is a rather wide balcony with a bright door through which a lot of sunlight enters the room. Room service at this hotel is open 24 hours a day, seven days a week.

Sunset beach resort





Hotel Sunset Beach Resort 3 deserves extremely positive reviews. It does not differ in large size, but captivates with a cozy atmosphere and friendly staff. The hotel itself is located in the northern zone of Patong, on its outskirts. There is a beach across the road from it. The hotel buildings (there are two of them) are perpendicular to each other and to the seashore. The hotel complex has its own salon of SPA-services, a gym for fitness, a gym section, a swimming pool with a jacuzzi function, a sauna and a children's room. There are also 2 restaurants and 3 bars with light snacks and drinks. As for the Internet connection service, this is only possible for a fee at a special rate. Some rooms in the hotel are equipped with everything you need for the kitchen, including utensils, appliances and furniture. The option is appropriate for those who like to combine outdoor activities with comfort.

Separately, it is necessary to highlight some more hotels in Phuket 3 stars (1 line).

Inn Patong Beach 3 * is famous for its affordable prices, good location and developed infrastructure. The rooms have scenic views from their balconies. And in the pool bar you can enjoy delicious cocktails.





The Beach Boutique House 3 *

The hotel is located in a picturesque bay. The street on which the hotel is located stretches along the entire southern coast of the island. Near The Beach Boutique House 3 (Phuket) is one of the best beaches on the island. The hotel offers accommodation in 39 comfortable rooms of three types (standard, deluxe and family).





According to guest reviews, this is an ideal place to relax with children. In a small area there is a swimming pool with a children's area. A hotel employee takes water daily for analysis.

The food here is excellent, but you need to get used to Thai cuisine. Tourists are advised to choose those establishments in which locals eat. Since they cook the same everywhere, and in restaurants for foreigners, prices are higher.

You can also come across many positive reviews about Thavorn Beach Bungalow 3, located in western Phuket.





The name of the hotel itself indicates that vacationers live in separate bungalows and villas. The hotel has excellent infrastructure with a swimming pool, tennis courts, volleyball and football courts. Especially captivating is that everything is provided for relaxing with children. Babysitting services are available upon request . The hotel has a kids club, and the local restaurant even has a children's menu.

By choosing any of the above hotels, the tourist will receive maximum comfort and care for very little money.
