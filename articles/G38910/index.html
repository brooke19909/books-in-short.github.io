London

The title "The most beautiful city in Europe" claims, first of all, the capital of Great Britain. In the list of visits, it is recommended to put it in first place. And this is no coincidence. First of all, London can safely compete with American New York for the honorary title of the world capital. In addition, the bulk of Russians are familiar with the English language. London will be a good choice for adapting to the style and culture of Europe.



The main disadvantage of the capital of Great Britain is that to any tourist the city may seem the most expensive in the world. However, there are many ways to cheaply live in London. Savings in finances are facilitated by the fact that visiting all the famous museums of the capital is absolutely free. The same applies to many walking tours. Westminster Abbey, Buckingham Palace and, of course, the famous Big Ben - this is not a complete list of attractions that it makes sense to visit.

Venice

The most beautiful cities in Europe attract tourists either in any particular season, or by any of their attractions.





However, this does not apply to Venice. This city is beautiful all and always. Its main problem is only numerous tourists. Even in winter, the streets of Venice are crowded with people, and in the summer a real crowd begins here. At the same time, there is a shortage of budget places to stay.

Amsterdam

One of the most beautiful European cities is considered the capital of Holland. Amsterdam attracts tourists with its medieval architecture. On the ancient streets of the city you can admire the beautifully preserved ancient buildings built in the seventeenth century. This was the period of the greatest prosperity of Holland. In addition, Amsterdam, unlike many cities in the world, boasts its many channels. By their number, the capital of Holland is second only to Venice.

The people of Amsterdam are passionate lovers of cycling. Often, this particular vehicle replaces cars. The streets filled with cyclists are an attractive landscape for many tourists. Amsterdam is also famous for its Red Light District.

Copenhagen

Not all tourists choose excursions to the capital of Denmark. They are frightened off by the high cost. However, an alternative can be an independent trip.





In this beautiful European city, you should definitely admire Rosenborg Castle. Excursions in its halls are free of charge. There is an old castle opposite the beautiful Botanical Garden, a visit to which also will not leave indifferent any of the tourists. The most beautiful sights of Copenhagen include canals, museums and interesting restaurants with a special charming design.

Ghent

Many tourists, as a rule, flock to large cities. However, they forget that there are other equally interesting places. The most beautiful small cities in Europe are also worthy of the attention of those who travel around the Old World. One of the most beautiful settlements in Belgium is Ghent. This is a small picturesque town, which is especially beautiful at night. In addition to magnificent landscapes, Ghent has an interesting centuries-old history. On its territory there are many museums of design and art.

Galway

The most beautiful places in Europe are not necessarily noisy and crowded capitals. A mass of pleasant impressions will be left by the medieval Galway, located in Ireland. On the territory of this town there are many interesting festivals that are real spectacular shows. Attractive for tourists and the surroundings of this place.

Utrecht

This small town is hardly widely known to tourists. However, his visit will certainly leave a lasting impression in the soul of any traveler. The town is famous for its many canals, on the banks of which there is a whole string of shops, bars and cafes. The surroundings of Utrecht are also interesting to visit. For their inspection, it is most convenient to rent a bicycle. Clean air and amazing landscapes will bring true pleasure.

Bordeaux

A beautiful French town, which often travelers simply do not mark on the map of their route. However, Bordeaux is included in the list of settlements that make up UNESCO's cultural heritage.





The city is famous for its incredible beauty of neoclassical and classical architecture. This area is especially beautiful in the evening, when the sunset colors the sky with delightful shades.
