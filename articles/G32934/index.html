Phones up to 15 thousand with a good camera: an overview

Perhaps the most popular goods are phones up to 15 thousand with a good camera. This price category is the most optimal for those who do not pursue fashion and prestige, but dream of a high-quality and powerful smartphone. Smart technology manufacturers have something to offer their customers.

Smartphone Acer Liquid Z630

Acer is better known for producing quality computer hardware. Fans of the Taiwanese manufacturer should be pleased with the Liquid Z630 smartphone. The gadget is large enough, due to the impressive size of the screen (5.5 inches), which occupies almost the entire front panel. 16 GB of internal, of course, is not the most solid indicator, but the possibility of using a flash card will solve the problem. But the 4000 mAh battery looks very attractive. When operating in moderate mode, you can count on 2-3 days of operation without charging.

Of particular interest are the cameras. Both the front and the main have 8 megapixels. Of course, for the main optics this is a very mediocre indicator. But if you need phones up to 15 thousand with a good selfie camera, you will like this option. Fans of the brand will be pleased with a large number of branded applications that come out of the box.

The main complaints are related to design. The device looks a little bulky. The speaker has an unusual round shape that cannot be called stylish. The lower frame is almost completely occupied by the company logo, although it would be more reasonable to place control keys on it.





Smartphone LG G4s H736

LG G4s H736 is a pretty good phone up to 15 thousand. This device has 2 main advantages - an interesting "space" design and a powerful processor for as many as 8 cores. A high-quality screen shows a juicy picture. The main camera gives good quality photos. Nevertheless, it does not reach other devices in its price category.

If you plan to purchase this unit, you should be aware of its shortcomings:

  • the amount of RAM is only 1.5 GB;
  • the internal volume is sorely lacking, and the device supports flash cards of no more than 32 GB, which will not be enough for an advanced user who likes to take photos, listen to music and watch videos;
  • a 2300 mAh battery cannot provide long battery life without being connected to a charger;
  • When installing updates to the operating system, the phone starts to noticeably "slow down".

Smartphone ASUS ZenFone 3 Max

Considering phones up to 15 thousand with a good camera, you can not get past ASUS ZenFone 3 Max. Another Taiwanese manufacturer of computer technology is confidently conquering the smartphone market. The device is running the 6th version of the Android OS and is equipped with a large 5.2-inch screen. With these parameters, the battery capacity of 4130 mAh promises decent autonomy.

The main 13-megapixel camera makes pretty good shots. The color reproduction is good, the results are decent even in low light. But you should not expect miracles from the 5-megapixel front camera. An additional advantage will be the presence of a fingerprint scanner.

As for the design, it is concise and sophisticated. But it is worth noting the irrational use of the front panel. Instead of placing the control buttons on the bottom, they were brought to the screen. But it can not but rejoice at the location of the volume control key on the side, unlike many smartphones of this manufacturer, in which this element is located on the rear panel.
