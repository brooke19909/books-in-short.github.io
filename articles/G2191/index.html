Hotel Arina Sand Resort 4 * (Crete, Greece): tourist reviews and photos

Resort Arina Sand Resort 4 * was built in 1979. His last restoration was carried out only 5 years ago. So the hotel looks new and modern. Over the course of all this long time, the hotel receives tens of thousands of guests every year. It is popular for many reasons. And which ones should be described in more detail.





Location

There is a wonderful resort village on the island of Crete, known as Kokkini Hani. It stretched along the sea for 15 kilometers east of Heraklion. Which, incidentally, can be reached by bus number 1, which leaves from the stop (it is located next to the hotel) every 15 minutes starting from six in the morning and up to one in the morning. By the way, in the same place, in Heraklion, is the nearest international airport. And you can take a taxi, but it will cost about 20-25 euros.

All the most famous sights also need to go. But they are located at a distance of 8-10 kilometers from the hotel. These include the Knossos Palace, the Heraklion City Garden, the port, the Municipal Art Gallery, the Museum of the History of Crete, the Kules Sea Fortress, the Venetian walls and many other interesting places.

The most important thing is that directly Arina Sand Resort 4 * hotel is located among picturesque tropical vegetation, at arm's length from the sea. This is the main highlight of the hotel.

Entertainment

Being in Arina Sand Resort 4 *, you can brighten up your leisure time in different ways. The hotel has an outdoor pool with sun loungers, umbrellas and towels for each guest. On the beach, you can do many other things besides swimming. For example, ride a "banana", rent a scooter, jet ski.

Fans of body and soul entertainment can go to the fitness center or massage. The hotel also has darts, a games room, a billiard room, a tennis court and everything you need to play volleyball.

In the evenings, various animated shows are held on site. By the way, there is also a separate playground for children whom parents took to rest with them, as well as a mini club for them. So no one will be bored.





Services

In Arina Sands Resort 4 * (Greece, Crete) there are a large number of various services. Naturally, the hotel has free Wi-Fi and private parking (a place is provided to guests upon arrival, no need to book in advance).

There is a left-luggage office, a photocopy and fax machine, car rental, currency exchange and laundry facilities. In addition, there is a large conference room and a 24-hour reception, which you can contact to resolve any issues.

Guests can also order room service and babysitting services. Calling a specialist for working with children usually helps those who want to be alone, but they have no one to leave the child with.

All descriptions state that the staff speaks German, Greek, English and Italian. But as people who have been here assure, workers still speak Russian quite well. Such a nice nuance cannot but rejoice. Just recently, the influx of tourists from Russia has greatly increased, so many began to study the Russian language.

Accommodation options

And now you can tell in more detail about the apartments in which guests of Arina Sand Resort 4 * (Crete) can stay.

You can start the description with 2-local standards. Their area is 28 square meters. Also in the room there is a bathroom with toilet, bathtub and hairdryer. There is also a comfortable sofa bed.

Also, much depends on the tour operator and on whether the hotel provides discounts. And their hotel offers quite often. Sometimes you can pay for a room 70 percent less than the basic cost! About booking and accommodation

Please be aware that at Arina Sand Resort 4 * (Kokkini), check-in starts at 15:00. And the departure lasts until noon. The hotel serves its guests on an all-inclusive basis, but on the day when the guests leave back home, they are not provided with food and drinks.

The hotel can accommodate children of any age. But extra beds and cribs are not provided. Guests can only sleep in places that are already in the rooms.

By the way, at the time of booking it will be necessary to indicate more details of a personal credit card, which then will have to be taken with you on a trip. She acts as a kind of guarantee of the arrival of guests for the hotel. They accept only "Visa" and "Master Card" there.

By the way, since the hotel is popular, it is better to reserve apartments in advance. And it is better if you have the opportunity to go on vacation in the fall or spring. At this time, the water temperature does not fall below 18 degrees. And the norm is 20-21. So you can not be afraid to freeze. In the evening, of course, it is cool, but in the afternoon more than comfortable.
