What is a personal account, why is it needed?

A personal account means a certain sequence of digits that is unique for each subscriber and is his personal ID for the operator. By naming a personal account number, you can get information of interest from technical support specialists or in the Rostelecom salon, deposit money to pay for services, etc. A personal account number is assigned at the time of concluding a contract for communication services. Usually, when connecting, the specialist performing the work draws the attention of the user to this identifier, by which he will subsequently pay for the services. It is indicated in the documentation that is issued to the client in the company envelope.
