The first water park in Tver

More recently, positive news was published: a modern water park in Tver may soon appear. While the construction of the object has been suspended, but I want to believe that a universal recreation center will nevertheless appear in a small town.





About city

Tver is considered one of the most ancient cities in Russia. All ancient sights are preserved in it. This place is also one of the most environmentally friendly: it is built on the banks of the Volga River, has a huge number of green areas and a minimal level of gas contamination.

However, entertainment in Tver is not as much as we would like. On Saturday night, you can visit the local cinema, a small restaurant or club. You can also play a quest or hide and seek in the dark. As for the children, there are few recreational facilities for them. As a rule, these are game rooms, amusement parks and street playgrounds.

Do you need a water park in Tver? Sure, yes! It will bring children and adults completely new emotions, allow you to have a good time and enjoy your vacation as much as possible!





Planning a future facility

It is still unknown what the future water park of Tver will look like. Photos of the entertainment center for the whole family have not yet appeared on any news site.

The Tver administration claims that it will be located on the territory of the Yuzhny microdistrict. It will be equipped with the most modern equipment, several slides and water attractions. There are two main pools - a children's and an adult, and a children's play area with musical fountains will also be built.

Approximately one billion rubles will be required to create a new facility. It will be a private organization funded by Western and Russian branches. It is already known that the cost of one ticket will be from 180 to 500 rubles. The estimated payback period of the entertainment center. It will be from 5 to 6 years. Residents were already excited by the news that the first water park in Tver would soon appear. His address, however, is not yet known to anyone. We can only say for sure that the complex will be located in the center of the Yuzhny district.

What now?

At the end of 2015, the construction of the facility began. Almost two years have passed, but so far it has not been completed. Why is this process stopped? No one can give an exact answer to this question. The city administration claims that they temporarily do not have the financial ability to continue construction due to the currency problem and the crisis in the country, but very soon a new water park in Tver will nevertheless appear.

Travel agencies offer an alternative for local residents: travel to such entertainment venues is organized. Among them, the closest is located in the city of Moscow, the distance to it is just over a hundred kilometers.





Will there be a water park in Tver? No one can give an exact answer to this question. However, I want to believe that this will happen in the coming years. Young children and adults will have the opportunity to have a wonderful rest in the territory of their hometown.
