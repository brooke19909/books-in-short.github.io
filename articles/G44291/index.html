Tariff "Violet" ("Tele2"): description, conditions, features

Tele2 company regularly pleases its customers with new tariff plans and advantageous offers on the use of communication services. Some of the previously available tariffs undergo changes, becoming more accessible and interesting for subscribers. The number of changed included the tariff "Purple" ("Tele2"). Its conditions were substantially adjusted, in particular, this affected the monthly fee. In this article, we will talk about what opportunities are currently proposed for this TP, and about its features in different regions of the country.





Tariff "Violet" ("Tele2"): description of the previous option

If we go back a while and look at what TP Violet was like before, we will see several differences (they may be absent depending on the region):

  • the presence of a subscription fee of three hundred and fifty rubles ;
  • included call volume (300 minutes to all numbers in the home area: landline, mobile);
  • a package of text messages (100 pcs daily).

At the same time, after exhausting the provided limits, call billing amounted to 90 kopecks per minute of connection (billing was per minute, regardless of the direction of the call).

This tariff plan was the best suited for subscribers who do not want to constantly replenish their balance and are not used to setting restrictions on the use of communication services.





Tariff "Violet" ("Tele2"): current conditions

Currently, the conditions of the tariff plan under consideration have changed significantly. Firstly, the subscription fee was canceled. Money is written off from the cellular number account only after performing paid actions (sending text or multimedia messages, calls, establishing an Internet connection, etc.) Thus, you can save on cellular communications by controlling costs. Secondly, for subscribers of the Tele2 operator (tariff "Violet", Voronezh), per-second tariffication of calls is set (only for calls to numbers of your region). Which means that when connecting in 15 seconds you will not have to pay as much as 60. At the same time, when calling to long-distance numbers and to other countries, per-minute billing is saved. Thirdly, there are no options implying a package of minutes / messages. Each action is charged in accordance with the terms of TP.
