About Hainan Resort

Hainan is a tropical that belongs to China. It is also the province of the same name. The resort island is located on the coastline of 1,500 km. Its central part is covered with dense forests, there are also plantations of mangoes, coffee, pineapples, coconuts and bananas.

The climate of the island is truly unique, due to which the swimming season lasts all year. In addition, the resort area is very clean. Local authorities take care of this. According to reviews, a vacation on Hainan has several advantages. Among them is the ease of obtaining a visa. Climatic conditions

According to tourists, vacation in China is always beautiful due to the amazing climate. The average annual temperature of the region is +24 degrees, and the sea - +26 degrees. Clear and sunny weather at the resort is observed more than 300 days a year. The island stretched from south to north for 308 kilometers. Probably for this reason, the climatic conditions of the northern and southern parts differ from each other. Sanya has a tropical climate on the south coast of the resort, centered on Sanya. Throughout the year there is no choking heat and cold. Cold air masses do not penetrate here because of the mountains. The open sea is always warm.

In the north of the island the climate is subtropical, therefore it is rather hot in summer and cooler in winter (temperature indicators never drop below +18 degrees). And in February and January fogs are observed. According to reviews, a vacation in Sanya is always successful. The southern region of the island is the best place to stay. The unique climate makes the resort very favorable.

Dadonghai

Dadonghai is one of the bays of the island. Her beach was very fond of our compatriots. Its length is not so large and is only four kilometers. The beach is located between the mountains, and therefore in winter the sea is warmer here than in other bays.

But the sand on the beach, although light, but not crumbly. You can no longer ride a bicycle on it. Sunset at sea is sandy and gentle. Sometimes near the shore there are holes washed out by water, but there are not so many of them. The sea in the bay area is very clean, transparent and has a beautiful blue tint. Periodically, waves are thrown on the coast of shells and small pebbles.

The beach is equipped with sunbeds and umbrellas (free of charge), lifeguards are always on duty at the main entrance.
