Hotel Jurmala Spa & Conference Center 4 * (Latvia, Jurmala): description and customer reviews

Jurmala is the best Baltic resort. Numerous hotels are located on the coast of the Gulf of Riga , among which is the Hotel Jurmala Spa. The modern resort complex is located within walking distance of the coast. The hotel offers not only accommodation in comfortable rooms, but also the opportunity to visit the spa complex.

A bit about the hotel ...

Hotel Jurmala Spa is located just 25 kilometers from Riga in the picturesque town of Jurmala, whose population is 60 thousand people. The city has always been the most famous and popular resort in Latvia.

Hotel Jurmala Spa is located on the boardwalk - Jomas Street, which is the main pedestrian area. It focuses on shopping and entertainment. The hotel was built in 2005 on the coast of the Gulf of Riga. Since then, the complex has successfully hosted resort guests. Hotel address Hotel Jurmala Spa: LV-2015, J≈´rmala, Jomas iela 47/49.

Hotel Bars

There is an Aqua bar in the wellness center for visitors. After the renovation, the Seaside Bar opened on the eleventh floor. Visitors can enjoy views of the Gulf of Riga and Jurmala. The menu of the bar contains not only drinks, but also sweets, snacks, desserts and delicious cocktails.

SPA-center

Jurmala Hotel Spa 4 * has an extensive infrastructure. The main feature of the institution is the presence of its own large spa and wellness center.

For visitors, the staff offers relaxing massages, cosmetic, water treatments, hairdresser services, pedicures, manicures, sports classes. The complex has three swimming pools, five types of saunas, a gym. In total, the spa has more than 200 varieties of procedures, among which you can choose something for yourself.

Beauty Lounge Beauty Center will allow you to get out of the daily bustle into the atmosphere of beauty. Body and face care procedures will help restore beauty with the help of well-known brands. Sea mud and algae, golden peeling, relaxing baths - this is not the whole range of procedures for restoring spiritual and physical harmony.

The Wellness Oasis Recreation and Sports Center will allow you to relax and get a boost of vigor and energy. It offers visitors five pools, each of which has a different temperature regime, a jacuzzi, solariums, saunas, a water bar. The gym is equipped with the latest cardiovascular equipment and power equipment. Individual training is possible under the supervision of a personal trainer.

Bath complex

If you wish, you can visit different types of baths in the spa:

  1. Salt (48 degrees) sauna, which has a beneficial effect on the skin.
  2. Finnish - this is a classic hot sauna (80 degrees) gives excellent muscle relaxation. Thanks to high temperatures, harmful substances are eliminated from the body. A visit to the sauna stimulates anti-stress mechanisms, strengthening immunity.
  3. Sanarium (60 degrees) - a bath with a pleasant herbal aroma, which is useful for the respiratory system.
  4. A Roman or steam bath (100% humidity) helps to normalize the condition of the skin.

There are Hotel Jurmala Spa and Conference Center (conference center), which includes seven modern rooms of different sizes and capacities. In total, they can accommodate up to 700 people. On the eleventh floor is the largest of them, it can accommodate 350 people. The panoramic windows of the room offer stunning views of the Gulf of Riga and the city. The conference center is equipped for events of different levels: from a regular banquet to international congresses. The qualified staff of the institution will help in organizing and conducting the program of any complexity.

In addition to business events, in the Hotel Jurmala Spa & Conference Center 4 * you can hold a wedding celebration or a festive banquet. The administration of the institution offers all kinds of programs and gifts from the hotel.

Tourists reviews

Continuing the review of the Jurmala Spa & Conference Center, I would like to draw attention to the reviews of tourists who managed to visit it. Many people call the complex the best in Jurmala. Is it really?

Reviews about Hotel Jurmala Spa are quite controversial. Visitors to the complex note the presence of shortcomings in the institution, which should not be in a four-star hotel.

The hotel is represented by a variety of apartments. Standards are most in demand. They are well equipped and have an attractive design. Especially good are the panoramic windows offering stunning views of Jurmala. And yet the rooms are not perfect. Many tourists note poor cleaning. Floors can be dirty throughout your stay. The contents of the minibar are replenished regularly, as well as supplies of detergents in the bathroom. Guests who visited the hotel in the fall note that the rooms are not heated in September-October, which causes discomfort, since it is already quite cold at this time. There is no carpet in the apartment. The floors are covered with durable but cold material. One of the drawbacks of the number of rooms is the lack of bathrobes and slippers in the standards. Higher category rooms include bathrobes, but they are very worn. According to tourists, such textiles do not correspond to the four-star status of the hotel.

Hotel Jurmala Spa & Conference Center has a restaurant and several bars. The rate includes a standard breakfast buffet. Visitors are satisfied with the quality and variety of the morning meal. Continental breakfast leaves a positive impression.

The restaurant is open for lunch and dinner. Regarding the quality of the dishes, many complaints arise. Many tourists note not the best taste of food. But the restaurant service pleases all guests.

The restaurant serves not only hotel guests, but also private corporate parties or banquets. During such periods, guests are invited to dine at the bar. The range of his dishes is a bit more modest, but you can eat.

The bar on the top floor has a beautiful design, and its panoramic windows offer stunning views of Jurmala, it is especially impressive in the evenings.

Since the complex is located on the central promenade of the city, in the immediate vicinity are numerous cafes and restaurants where you can have a good time. Tourists recommend using the services of the nearest establishments, as they offer a very diverse menu at reasonable prices. In addition, a visit to various restaurants will allow you to fully appreciate all the advantages and taste qualities of local dishes. Each institution in Jurmala has its own unique charm and charm. Even if the weather is bad outside the windows, you should go for a walk, which is completed in one of the cafes. Awesome experience guaranteed. Not for nothing that all the guests of Jurmala respond with particular warmth to local restaurants.

Spa reviews

According to reviews, Hotel Jurmala Spa has a large spa complex, which occupies two floors. It is he who enjoys the greatest popularity. Most of the guests come to the hotel for a weekend to stroll in Jurmala in the evening and soak up the spa. The standard package for the hotel includes access to the spa until 18.00. Guests can choose any two procedures for free visits, the rest must be paid separately.

The spa facilities can be used regardless of hotel stay. Many tourists or residents visit the complex by paying a subscription.

Many positive reviews are left by visitors about the hotel baths. Such a diversity of their species is difficult to find elsewhere. However, the spa complex has several disadvantages. Visitors note the fact that it is far from new, therefore it needs repair. In the spa you can see rusty taps and handrails. Pool water is very chlorinated. If you do not tolerate chlorine or are prone to allergic reactions, then it is better to refrain from visiting the reservoirs of the complex.

Guests are advised to use the spa on weekdays only. The complex serves not only hotel guests and guests, but also groups. Therefore, at the weekend there are an incredible amount of people in the spa, there are lines not only for the procedures, but also for the reception. Such congestion and a large crowd of people does not contribute to relaxation. The best time to visit the complex is on weekdays until 18.00.

General impression of the hotel

Hotel "Jurmala" is the most famous institution of the resort. Its popularity is largely due to its convenient location on the main street. The hotel building is a short walk from the beach. If you travel to Jurmala in the summer, it is difficult to find a closer location to the sea.

Among the guests of the establishment there are many regular customers who regularly spend weekends there at any time of the year, delighting themselves with spa treatments and walks around the city. Yes, and locals visit the baths and pools of the complex.

The guests of the establishment highly appreciate the work of the staff, but nevertheless small flaws often happen. In general, the hotel can be recommended for relaxing in the resort, despite the shortcomings. They are not so significant as to spoil the overall positive impression.
