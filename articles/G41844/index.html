Location Kemer, Beldibi

Hotel "Selchukhan" (Turkey) is surrounded by the Tor Mountains. It is located fifteen kilometers from the center of the village of Kemer. To get to it, you need to fly to Antalya, and then either use the transfer, or take a bus or dolmush to Beldibi. From the airport, this area is forty kilometers away. From the bus station of the city of Antalya - twenty. Historically, this territory has long been called Lycia. The village itself, as already mentioned, was built exclusively for tourists, halfway from Antalya to Kemer. It houses mainly expensive fashionable hotels, as well as shops and entertainment centers. Minibus number 28 goes from here to Kemer. You can take a walk in Beldibi through beautiful parks that stretch towards Antalya.



Territory and infrastructure

Hotel "Selchukhan" (Turkey) stands in a green garden in the midst of orange trees. In addition, other citrus fruits, avocados grow here. It consists of old and new buildings, each of them has an elevator. The hotel has a leather and fur store, gym, spa and night club. True, the latter is not on the territory of the hotel, but across the road. When you come here, a special officer will guide you through the back streets and tell you where you are. Near the hotel there is a cafe with beautiful cheap freshly squeezed juices and a lot of shops.

Number of rooms

Hotel "Selchukhan" (Turkey) has two hundred and five rooms. Tourists call the stay in them very comfortable. Sixteen of them are intended for families, but they are one-room. The interior is stylish, concise. Each room has air conditioning, which can be used not only for cooling the air, but also for heating. The TV has at least one Russian channel. Bathrooms are almost perfect. The room catches "wi-fi." They clean well, but if you want a change of bed, drop the sheets and duvet cover on the floor - that's how it works. Sea and pool vacations

"Selchukhan" is a hotel (Turkey), which has its own beach. To get on it, you need to cross the road. All the way to the sea is about seven minutes in the park. The coast is large-pebble, so slippers will not interfere. The beach is not bad, equipped, with cabins, sunbeds and mattresses, towels and umbrellas. Everyone has enough space and amenities. A plastic pier was built for better entry into the water. If you go down to the sea along it, you will immediately go to the depths. Drinks at the beach bar - both strong and cocktails - are available in unlimited quantities. There are even refined liquors such as Baileys.

The water is very clean and warm, even at the end of the season. On the left, eight hundred meters from the beach, there is still a wild coast. There are small pebbles and beautiful rocks with pine trees, but there is no shadow at all. The hotel has two more pools - children's and with slides. You can spend time there in case of stormy weather. But in any case, tourists praise the slides very much. The older children are completely delighted.



County Trips

Of course, tourists mainly visit this hotel for a beach holiday. But there are those who specifically choose this area, which is famous for Mediterranean Turkey - Beldibi. Hotel "Selchukhan" is approximately the same distance from Kemer and Antalya, and you can easily get there by bus to take a walk on your own. Go to one place and another a little more than a quarter of an hour. Bus number six, which leaves from the Migros supermarket, will take you directly to the center of Antalya. There you can explore the old fortress, walk through the local cafes and make purchases at the Turkish bazaar. You can visit the beautiful ruins of the ancient city of Phaselis on your own. There are connecting routes in Kemer. Well, if you want longer excursions, then it is better to purchase them on the streets of the village.
