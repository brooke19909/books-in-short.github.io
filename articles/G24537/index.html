Along its borders is a mountain range - the Small Caucasus. Naturally, such a relief forms the corresponding climate - alpine, continental, and only in the south - subtropical. Armenia, Tsaghkadzor

Tsaghkadzor is not only one of the most beautiful cities in Armenia, but also one of the best resorts. Crystal clear air and picturesque nature attract many visitors who come to these places at any time of the year.
But still it enjoys greater popularity as a place of winter recreation. The softness of this pore allows you to enjoy all the delights of skiing, the season of which begins in mid-November and ends in mid-April. Here on a cable car from a height of 2819 m you can admire the Hrazdan Gorge, relax with a cup of Armenian tea in a cozy cafe and just admire nature, breathe fresh air.

For the comfort of holidaymakers, the resort has a lot of hotels, pensions, rest houses, as well as a sports base, swimming pool, hot springs, which Armenia is famous for. Tsaghkadzor is a diversified resort. Many establishments offer such activities as tennis, billiards, sauna. And in the local restaurants and cafes, Armenian cuisine will not leave anyone indifferent. Hotels are very comfortable, with reasonable pricing: from 2000 rubles. in two-star with three meals a day up to 9600 rubles. and higher in a five-star.



Arzni

Arzni resort is located high (1250 m) above the sea surface. The place of his birth was the amazingly beautiful Hrazdan River with its picturesque gorge. The climatic conditions at the resort are very mild: moderately cold winters, cool summers and warm autumn. These places are famous for healing mineral waters, which are rich in carbon dioxide and contain mineral elements.

In Arzni everyone has the opportunity to undergo a full course of rehabilitation. Mineral and coniferous baths are offered, which are famous for almost all the resorts of Armenia. Vanadzor

Vanadzor is the third city after the capital and Gyumri. It is located in the foothills of the Pambak and Bazum ranges. It is divided by three rivers: Tanzut, Pambak and Vanadzor. Climatic conditions are very favorable. Warm winters and cool summers create the perfect weather for relaxation.

Vanadzor received the status of a health resort city due to its favorable location and mineral waters. He is also famous for the school of painting and the art gallery. Everyone can relax in these places, regardless of age. After all, this is not only recovery, but also all kinds of excursions, the opportunity to discover something new. Accommodation in a two-star hotel will cost about 1400-1500 rubles.



Dilijan

Talking about the resorts of Armenia, you can not remain silent about Dilijan. This is a city that has found its cozy place in the forest Agstev valley. It was in this place that the summer residence with the hunting grounds of the royal Arshakid dynasty was built.
Dilijan is considered a resort town. A favorable climate, mountains, fresh air and mineral waters help to overcome many diseases. There are sanatoriums "Mountain Armenia", "Dilijan", as well as a nursery for the treatment of tuberculosis. It offers year-round recreation centers and pensions. Prices for accommodation in boarding houses are not very high, the room costs about 2000 rubles., Breakfast is included.
