Green Theater: Sochi, Riviera Park

One of the most recognizable parks in Russia is the Sochi Riviera Park. Here is the famous "Green Theater". Sochi is visited by famous Russian and foreign musicians, performers, comedians and so on. The theater hosts their performances, creative meetings and concerts.

Park "Riviera"

The park was founded in 1989 by V.A. Khludov. In Soviet times, the residents and tourists of Sochi rested and walked here, making dates and meetings. Currently, the park is a historical and cultural attraction.





For guests in the park "Riviera" there is plenty of entertainment. The following facilities are located on the territory:

  • various attractions;
  • sports town;
  • exhibition halls;
  • library;
  • dolphinarium;
  • aquarium
  • night club and cafe;
  • kartodrom;
  • Green Theater (Sochi);
  • alley of stars.

The park has rich vegetation, among which there are exotic trees and flowers. Visitors can admire modern sculptures or relax in the shade of beautiful fountains.

Open air theater

The Green Theater (Sochi) has been operating since 1959. It holds about one and a half thousand people. Performances and screenings of films are held in the open air, so the theater only works in the warm season.





It is located among centuries-old trees, the foliage of which in hot weather serves as a shelter for spectators from sunlight. People come to the Green Theater (Sochi) to enjoy the healing air, which incorporates the aromas of coniferous trees and the sea.

Every summer, famous foreign and Russian pop singers, musicians, colloquial artists, comedians and actors come to the park. It organizes creative meetings and evenings, festivals, and concerts. The venue and stage of the Green Theater are equipped with the most modern equipment. Recently, rock musicians, chanson performers and satirists have become frequent guests here.

The theater always has a warm and cozy atmosphere. There are numerous walkways and paths near the venue. After a concert or a creative evening, you can take a walk, breathe the sea air and enjoy the warm southern evening.

Near the theater there is an alley of artists, where you can buy your favorite painting from a local artist or order a self-portrait. There is also the opportunity to stroll along the avenue of stars, in the planting of trees of which the stars of domestic show business took an active part.

How to get there

The Green Theater is located (Riviera Park, Sochi) on Yegorova Street 1. You can get here by shuttle bus, taxi or your own transport. The park covers a large area, and you can drive up to it from different sides.
