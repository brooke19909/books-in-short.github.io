LCD "Monolith": description, reviews

New buildings in Nizhny Novgorod do not cease to please with their diversity. Modern developers offer excellent projects located in developed areas of the city with spacious apartments and landscaped grounds. Residential complex "Monolith" is a bright representative of complexes that meet all the requirements of modern residents. As part of this material, we will give him an objective assessment, identify strengths and weaknesses, and also consider options for acquiring apartments in the complex.





about the project

Residential complex "Monolith" (Nizhny Novgorod) is represented by the largest developer of the city - LLC "Monolith". The project provides for the construction of an 8-section building on 17 floors with built-in public facilities, as well as its own infrastructure.

Location

LCD "Monolith" is located in the Nizhny Novgorod district of the city, at the intersection of new streets. The project threatens to become one of the best, as evidenced by the reviews of the first buyers. On the east side, new buildings border a slope to the banks of the Volga, on the west side - on the green zone of the shopping and entertainment complex, on the north - on the wasteland, which will be built up in the near future, and on the south - with a tunnel across the river. The place is picturesque, spared from the accumulation of harmful industrial production, due to which it attracts the attention of many Nizhny Novgorod residents. A picturesque view of the Volga and amazing ecology are perhaps the main advantages of the complex.





Transport accessibility

Near the house there is a public transport stop. In the Nizhny Novgorod region, there are enough public transport routes, so residents of the new building can get to anywhere in the city.

Infrastructure

New buildings in Nizhny Novgorod, appearing in new areas of the city, cause a number of concerns. Many believe that the first residents of such houses will be uncomfortable. But, destroying the prevailing stereotype, I would like to note that the project provides for the construction of its own multi-tiered parking, due to which it will be possible to save the yard from the accumulation of cars. In walking distance from the complex is the shopping and entertainment center "Lenta", where you can make all the necessary purchases. The construction of educational institutions is not provided for on the territory of the Monolit residential complex, but their absence is compensated by a kindergarten and a school located just a few minutes away - apartment buyers say that they brought their children there without any problems.





Accomplishment of the adjacent territory is represented by sports and playgrounds, recreation areas and walking paths. The improvement of the embankment of the Volga River is planned, which will become a favorite place for walks of the residents of the complex.

Apartments, layouts

Apartments in Monolith residential complex are for rent with rough finish, like most apartments in other new buildings of the city. The houses are being built using monolithic technology, which provides them with reliability, strength, and durability. The first two phases of the project have already been put into operation, the sections are populated. What does the Monolith LCD offer? The reviews of the first residents of the new building confirm that the house turned out to be warm, all work was carried out efficiently, there is electrical wiring in the apartments, double-glazed windows are installed. Moreover, it was pleased with excellent sound insulation.





There are apartments for every taste to choose from: from small compact studios to four-room apartments of an improved layout with an additional bathroom. The developer focused on the comfort of each resident, offering rational solutions. Do not want to live in a standard apartment? Your attention will probably be attracted to open-plan options.

Reviews

The first residents of the commissioned buildings are not shy to leave feedback. Many of them emphasize proximity to infrastructure, peace and quiet. There is enough greenery around: a lot of plantings, flower beds and flowerbeds are broken, the air is clean and fresh. Excellent transport interchange pleased car owners: now you can get to anywhere in the city within half an hour.





Of course, no matter how good the complex may be, there are some comments that may affect the choice of potential buyers of real estate. Residents of the new building are unhappy with the point development of the area, the proximity of other complexes, which deprives them of their original spaciousness and comfort. Most of the negative reviews cover the unfair work of utilities, dirt on roads and in yards. And, of course, residents would like to see more playgrounds and sports fields, the presence of sports and recreation center.
