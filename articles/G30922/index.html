Marmara Hotel Apartments 4 * (United Arab Emirates, Dubai): hotel description, room types, service, reviews

Dubai is the most famous resort in the UAE. The Emirate has a very developed infrastructure with a large number of hotels of different levels. In our article we want to talk about one of the four-star complexes of the city called Marmara Hotel Apartments.

A bit about the hotel ...

Marmara Hotel Apartments is an apartment hotel located just a short walk from the Zayed Sheikh Highway and the Mall Emirates Shopping Center. Also quite nearby is the Sky Dubai indoor ski complex. And the most famous mall in Dubai is located ten kilometers from the hotel. The hotel was built in 2011, therefore it belongs to the category of young establishments. It is great not only for tourist trips, but also for business. The hotel is located just thirteen kilometers from the airport.

Entertainment and Attractions

As we already mentioned, the hotel is very convenient in terms of location, since next to it there are shopping centers, shops and all kinds of entertainment. Marmara Hotel Apartments is located next to the metro station, from which you can reach interesting places of the resort. In addition, you can use the services of local guides and go on an exciting tour. Resting at the resort, it is worthwhile to take time to explore its beauties, breaking away from a beach stay.

It is impossible to relax in Dubai and not to see the famous Burj Khalifa, which is the tallest building in the world. Its height is 828 meters. The shape of the building resembles a stalagmite. At an altitude of 555 meters, there is the highest observation deck, which you can climb to view the city from a height.



Dubai has a large number of mosques. Among them, the most interesting for tourists is the Jumeirah Mosque. This is the largest religious building in all the emirates. By the way, this mosque is one of the few that tourists who do not profess the Muslim religion can visit.

Any tourist will certainly be interested in the local water park, executed on the same scale as all buildings in Dubai. Wild Wadi Water Park is equipped with attractions that will give amazing emotions. The water park is considered one of the best in the Middle East.

If you are interested in the history of the country, then you should go to Fort Al Fahidi. On its territory you can see the traditional reed houses of the local population. The fort is equipped with serious dungeons, equipped during its construction. In addition, his exposition has many interesting exhibits dedicated to the prehistoric, medieval and modern era.
