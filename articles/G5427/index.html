Mitsis Alila Resort & Spa 5 * Deluxe (Greece, Faliraki): description, photos and reviews of tourists

The hotel complex Mitsis Alila Resort & Spa 5 * Deluxe is designed for a beach holiday. It is located near the sea. This is a hotel with the real five-star service. The hotel is new, opened in 2013. It is part of the largest chain of Greek hotels, Mitssis Alila. This complex positions itself as intended for a romantic pastime or for couples. Therefore, most of the services in it are suitable for comfortable relaxation of adults in luxury style. Resort Faliraki

A huge tourist center with resorts and mini-hotels for every taste and budget - this is what this former fishing village is today. Accommodation here is comfortable, the food is very tasty and well-prepared. In addition, Faliraki has the best beaches on the island. The sea and the sun are more than enough here. No wonder Mitsis Alila Resort & Spa 5 * Deluxe (Faliraki) is located in this place. And if vacationers get tired of having too quiet pastimes, then local night clubs and other similar establishments that open after ten in the evening are at their service. Ermu Street, where all this is located, has been renamed to Bar Street. There are also two beautiful monasteries - St. Amos and Elijah the Prophet, as well as the best water park in Rhodes.

What is nearby

Mitsis Alila Resort & Spa 5 * Deluxe Hotel is located directly on the seashore. Administratively, it belongs to the city of Faliraki, which is four kilometers from the complex. But practically it is located in the village of Kallithea. Near the hotel - a very interesting place. It is called the "springs of Kallithea." These are the most beautiful baths on the island, fully restored. They impress with their architecture, gardens and wild beaches. Weddings are often held there, and the place itself is very picturesque. For short trips, the hotel is beautifully located. Near the bus stop, you can easily go to Faliraki.

Territory

Hotel Mitsis Alila Resort & Spa 5 * Deluxe, the photo of which illustrates this article, is located in one eight-story building. For the convenience of tourists, the hotel has four elevators. Accommodation for tourists

Hotel Mitsis Alila Resort & Spa 5 * Deluxe is quite large. It has three hundred and fourteen rooms. Of these, 136 are standard and superior rooms. There are still 117 family rooms, 23 - junior suites. Vacationers can live in bungalows - 6 standard or 48 family. There are also two deluxe rooms. Everywhere there are balconies (on the first floors - terraces). Each room has a safe, tea and coffee making facilities. All rooms for guests are decorated in an original way, with a design approach.

Hotel Mitsis Alila Resort & Spa 5 * Deluxe. Description of the beach and water activities

The hotel has its own coast. It is covered with small pebbles, mixed with sand. On the beach there are umbrellas and sunbeds, the use of which is included in the price of the permit. Its territory is quite large, and it is almost never crowded. Volleyball and water polo are played on the beach. But special towels for sunbathing are issued only for a deposit of twenty euros. A water sports center is also equipped here. The entry into the water is good, the sea itself is very clean. The bottom is visible even at a depth of six to eight meters.
