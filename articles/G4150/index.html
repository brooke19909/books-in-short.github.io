Alfa-Finance: reviews, services

Many Russian banks offer their clients, along with deposits, additional tools for generating income. Some of them may be based on investing in real assets of businesses. Among them is the Alfa Finance program. What is its specificity? What profitability from participation in it can be expected?





Alfa Finance Overview

Alfa Finance is an investment program from Alfa Bank, initiated in 2010 and still open to the participation of depositors. It is more correct to write its name together - AlfaFinance, but in Russia a separate spelling has been established. What are the specifics of the respective initiative?

Judging by public information, through the Alfa Finance program, a bank with a consonant name attracts private funds to invest in the assets of real companies. This is probably their fundamental difference from mutual funds, where financial instruments can be, in particular, commodity and financial exchanges. Profitability from participation in the corresponding program could potentially be greater than when placing funds on bank deposits.

The wide geography of the presence of the Alfa Finance program can be noted: Voronezh, Yaroslavl, Moscow and other large cities have branches of the corresponding bank. There, investors can draw up their investment contracts.





Program benefits

The advantages of using a financial instrument such as Alfa Finance, the bank that initiated it, sees the following:

- the program guarantees the safety of depositors' funds, even if their total amount exceeds 1.4 million rubles (the state limit for the size of repayable deposits in the event of a bankruptcy of a credit institution);

- a private investor transfers resources to experienced financial professionals, but at the same time has the opportunity to participate in the selection of the optimal income generating tool;

- profits can reach about 25-30% per annum and even more.

The main element of the Alfa Finance program is an investment strategy. At the moment, the bank that has developed the appropriate financial instrument offers the client a choice of 7 such. Consider their specifics.

Novo Nordisk is known as the world's largest insulin producer. The Celgene brand produces drugs for the treatment of cancer and immune system problems. Gilead Corporation develops anti-HIV drugs. - LUKOIL, one of the most famous brands in the automotive fuel industry.

Despite the current crisis in the Russian economy, these corporations remain stable. Investments in their assets, thus, can be characterized by good profitability. Many investors note that now, while the shares of leading Russian companies are relatively cheap, you can invest in them to get an excellent increase in capital upon the fact that the general stock market of the Russian Federation enters the path of sustainable growth.

Precious metals, experts say, are protected from inflation and the effects of political instability. Analysts describe the corresponding strategy proposed by Alfa-Bank as one of the most balanced in terms of profitability and stability of assets that are the objects of investment.

Program ratings and reviews

How promising can investments be under the Alfa Finance program? Opinions on this subject are expressed by both participants and financial analysts. Assessments of citizens and representatives of the expert environment can be divided into those that:

- reflect the assessment of the prospects of investments;

- concern the quality of Alfa-Bank‚Äôs service in terms of communication with program customers;

- associated with the assessment, in fact, of the concept proposed by the financial institution, with the determination of the degree of trust in it.





As for the prospects of investments, citizens characterize the program as quite attractive. Many note that they considered participation in it not as the main method of generating income, but along with, for example, the placement of funds in bank deposits, since the profitability of the corresponding investments is comparable to that characterizing traditional deposits. Thus, it is completely impractical to take a loan to participate in the Alfa Finance program - its rates will be higher than the probable return on investments.

The quality of service of the relevant banking institution is evaluated by its customers quite positively. The hot line "Alfa Finance" is functioning, as citizens believe, correctly. It doesn‚Äôt matter in which city the brand is present. In fact, the geography of service under the program coincides with the one where the bank that initiated the Alfa Finance program is operating. In Taganrog, Voronezh, Yaroslavl - in those cities where the brand is present, you can become an investor. The quality of service is quite at the level, regardless of the actual geography of the bank. If the city in which the citizen invests as part of Alfa Finance is Voronezh, then there will be no problems getting the necessary information regarding this program, as well as when the investor is in Taganrog or Yaroslavl. Feedback difficulties are also not typical for communications between Alfa-Bank and customers. If a person wants to ask a question about the program that Alfa Finance Bank has established, the contacts of the nearest offices of the institution are always available online.

Reliability as the most important criterion

As for the evaluation of the concept proposed by Alfa-Bank, it seems quite promising to investors. Investments in the assets of real corporations or in precious metals are considered by many analysts as much more reliable ways of earning than, say, currency trading. Stability of income generation may predispose the stability, which is characterized by the bank that initiated the Alfa-Finance program. According to experts, the license was revoked from him, it just cannot be so, due to the fact that his financial stability indicators are among the most optimal in the Russian banking market. Alfa-Bank is one of the largest Russian credit organizations.
