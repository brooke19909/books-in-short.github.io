Operational leasing - why is it profitable?

This type of operations in the field of finance as leasing is well known to businessmen and owners of their own businesses. This is a great alternative to lending, which is suitable for both large enterprises and small businesses. There are many varieties and types of leasing, but there are two key ones: operational or financial.



Leasing in general is such an activity, as a result of which a company acquires certain property in order to transfer it for rent to another consumer. Depending on whether the client at the end of the contract will be able to redeem it, and different types of leasing are allocated .

Operational leasing - the specifics of this type of service

More than 90% of all leasing transactions are related to financial leasing. Companies in this way acquire new equipment, machinery, cars or commercial real estate, seriously reducing tax pressure and reducing assets. This is a way to get away from classical lending schemes, more profitable and convenient.

Operational leasing is a type of lease in which property is transferred for a certain period to the client, but upon termination of the contract is returned to its owner. This option can not be compared with lending, because the consumer never becomes the owner of the property.



Financial and operational leasing are two components of the same type of activity, but they differ significantly in principle of work, and are designed to satisfy different needs. If in the first case this is a way to extend the acquisition of a car or equipment in time for the benefit of yourself, then in the second it is rather an opportunity to use the best new products in each field of technology, without planning to buy them.

Operational leasing costs customers more, because the risk level for the tenant in this case is higher. On the other hand, the consumer does not need to pay full depreciation of property for a short period, he pays only some of it. This option justifies itself well when using expensive property for a short time.
