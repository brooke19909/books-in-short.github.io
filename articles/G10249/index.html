How to make plasticine ice cream? We create with children

Before you start sculpting from expensive materials, you should practice on clay. If you want to engage in creativity with a child, then it is better to buy clay for children's creativity. Firstly, it is inexpensive, and secondly, it is available in different colors. In this article we will tell you how to make plasticine ice cream.





Making a simple treat

You should start with simple models. Before making ice cream from plasticine, wash your hands so that dust particles do not spoil the finished work. Also wipe the table. Take three pieces of plasticine of different colors, put them together and roll them into a ball. Then cut the bottom of the stack as shown in the photo. Then take a toothpick or special tool with a small ball at the end and make uneven edges from the bottom. How to make plasticine ice cream ? Take a piece of waffle-colored plasticine and mold the bottom. Stick sweetness to the horn. With a stack, draw a net pattern on the horn. Plasticine ice cream is ready!





How to make a waffle mold?

How to make plasticine ice cream to make it realistic? To do this, you need to mold not only the delicacy itself, but also the waffle cone. It can be done in several ways (the article describes how to make plasticine ice cream). You can roll out a thin layer of plasticine and draw a mesh pattern on the stack. Then a circle is cut out of this layer and ice cream is curled.

The second method is a bit more complicated and more expensive to manufacture. It consists in the fact that you buy polymer clay of any color, roll it into a thin layer and also make a mesh pattern with a knife. Then the resulting mold must be baked. After baking, let it cool. Now you can use the mold! Just print on plasticine.

Now you know how to make plasticine ice cream, which is very similar to the present. In addition, it is rare, but in some stores you can see ready-made molds for making wafers.

Plasticine spiral ice cream

In general, before you mold food from plasticine (including ice cream), carefully study the photographs of real food. So you can notice shortcomings in your work in time to have time to fix them. Roll up a long brown sausage first. Then carefully twist it into a spiral. From a light brown mass, mold a horn. Stack draw a pattern.





How to mold plasticine food, ice cream in the first place? Stick the sausage to the horn. Then, stick small, colorful circles on the ice cream itself as a decoration. Now you know how to make food from plasticine with your own hands simply and quickly. As soon as you learn how to make beautiful horns from the mass for children, you can safely start sculpting from polymer clay. After baking, plastic products become hard, and most importantly, flexible. They will not break during a fall and will not be remembered during rough handling. And they can also be varnished. In addition, from such a product you can make jewelry in the form of a bracelet or earrings. The above says how to make plasticine ice cream from two different types.

How to make popsicles?

There is another interesting type of product. Now you will learn how to make fruit ice cream from plasticine. Take a few pieces of mass to sculpt different colors. Put them together and smooth the edges. Colors can be very different - from blue to yellow. Then stick a piece of light brown plasticine to the ice cream. This is a wooden stick. Below will be described another way how to make ice cream from plasticine. Similarly, chocolate coated popsicles can be made.





To do this, first connect the mass for sculpting different colors. Then roll out a large cake of dark brown plasticine and stick ice cream around it. Further actions depend on exactly what you want to make your dessert. You can only half cover it with chocolate. And you can cover the whole, and then cut off part of the ice cream with a knife or a stack so that its colored layers are visible. You can also make small decorations in the form of small sticks or balls.

How to make your product more durable?

If you want ice cream to serve you for a long time, then mold it from polymer clay. As we wrote above, this material after baking becomes very durable. In addition, a good model can be pasted with pieces of paper in several layers, then cut and pull out all the clay. Then you need to glue two halves and again glue the product with torn pieces of paper. Such ice cream can be decorated and played with in a dollhouse. Now you know how to make plasticine ice cream with your own hands.
