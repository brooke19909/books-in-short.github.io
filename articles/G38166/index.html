Hotel Now Garden Punta Cana 5 * (Dominican Republic / Punta Cana): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Dominican Republic is a real tropical paradise for people who love nature, beach vacations and sea activities. This is a country where eternal summer reigns and the sun always shines. It is not surprising that in recent years more and more people come here. There are many good hotels here, living in which you can make your stay even better. And one of those is Now Garden Punta Cana 5 *.





general information

This is a new hotel complex, which was opened in December last, 2015. It is located in close proximity to its own sandy beach in the resort, known as Punta Cana. This, incidentally, is a unique place. Indeed, it is here that the beaches are covered not with ordinary sand, but with coral, snow-white color. And in this city is the airport, which is the largest on all the Caribbean islands. But this is not the only interesting fact. It is also the first private international airport in the world.

It is worth noting that this hotel shares the territory with a neighboring hotel complex known as Now Larimar Punta Cana 5 *. Dominican Republic (Punta Cana) is a popular resort, and these places are rightfully one of the best here. What is important, guests of one hotel can use the infrastructure of another.

This resort also has several interesting sights. For example, the Dolphin Island park (500 meters), a little further - Palma Real Shopping Village, a couple of hundred meters later - Cocotal country club. A 20-minute drive takes you to the unique freshwater lagoons and La Cana Golf Club.





About Services

Now Garden Punta Cana 5 * has a well-developed infrastructure. Here, guests can take advantage of a wide variety of services, from free Wi-Fi and a private car park to room service and newspapers.

In the lobby there is a 24-hour reception, which provides accelerated check-in and check-out, currency exchange and ATM, tour desk and luggage storage, as well as individual lockers. Concierge services are also available.

There is also a laundry room with dry cleaning, where guests can also iron things and compress trousers. There is also a business center, conference room, shared lounge, several shops on site, car rental, a beauty salon with a hairdresser. Now Garden Punta Cana 5 * even has apartments designed for guests with disabilities. Everything in them, from the bedroom to the bath and shower, is adapted to their specific requirements.

By the way, this hotel has Russian-speaking staff, which for the Dominican Republic is quite unusual, but a pleasant fact. More employees speak German, English, Spanish and French.





Leisure

Now Garden Punta Cana 5 * is a hotel that is perfect for both leisure and entertainment. There is a casino, an outdoor pool, a sun terrace with sun loungers and umbrellas, where in the evening they organize various shows, a night club, two outdoor tennis courts.

There is also equipment necessary for various water sports. For example, snorkelling, canoeing, diving, windsurfing and fishing.

However, in addition to the above, the hotel also has a bowling alley, karaoke and a gym with fitness equipment. Horse riding and golf courses are also organized here, which are located near the hotel.

By the way, while adults are having fun, their children, if they have one, can play in a mini-club, where professional educators monitor them.

For body and soul

Now Garden Punta Cana 5 * (Punta Cana), like any other hotel of this level, has a professional SPA center that provides a fairly diverse list of services. There is a Turkish bath, sauna, jacuzzi, several massage rooms. Medical and cosmetic procedures, such as aroma and hydrotherapy, are especially popular. Swedish and sports massages are also made here . They help to relax as much as possible and restore the body. And here they do stone therapy and deep muscle massage.

And, of course, while staying here, you must definitely take time onsen. So in Japan are called hot thermal springs, which are available in many SPA-hotels.

About nutrition

Now Garden Punta Cana 5 * hotel is also good because it serves guests here on an all-inclusive basis. The hotel complex has four restaurants and several bars.

A restaurant called Windows serves breakfast and lunch. International dishes are served there. If you want to try Italian delights, then you should go to Caper. But it only works in the evening. Like Trattoria, which cooks amazingly delicious pizza. And Barefoot Grill can be visited for lunch. The barbecue and grill are great there. By the way, if you want to enjoy gourmet cuisine without leaving your room, just call the telephone number in each room at the reception and make an order.

It is also worth knowing that hotel guests are allowed to visit the restaurants and bars of the neighboring Now Larimar Garden Punta Cana 5 *. There is an excellent Mercure restaurant serving French cuisine. There is also the Bluewater Grill Restaurant, Carnival Cafe and Castaways. So the variety of guests in any case provided. Here you can try anything - this is assured by people who have been to one of the hotels. They even serve such delicacies as lobsters, lobsters, French champagne, mussels, squids, sea bream, piglet milk, blue cheese (even camembert), meat, poultry and much more. From alcohol and drinks - rum, whiskey, gin, wine, beer, tequila, martini, juices, fresh, teas, coffee, etc. In general, each person will find something to their taste.





Apartments

Now Garden Punta Cana (Punta Cana, Dominican Republic) has a total of 180 rooms. And all are deluxe. Spacious, bright apartments decorated in bright, eye-catching colors. The interior is well placed for relaxation. Furniture, plumbing, decor - everything is new and beautiful, because the hotel was opened only a year ago.

The rooms are designed for two guests. Inside there is one very large 2 double bed. Although, if necessary, guests can provide apartments with two standard, separate.

The apartments have access to a private furnished balcony or terrace, a plasma TV, radio, telephone, two safes (one of which is for a laptop), air conditioning and ironing facilities. But that is not all. Deluxe rooms also include a fan, tumble dryer, walk-in closet, trempel, electric kettle and even a coffee machine with a minibar. Naturally, the apartments also have a bathroom. Bathrobes, slippers, a hairdryer, towels - all this is also provided for each guest. In general, the rooms have absolutely everything that should be. Even a separate whirlpool is available.

Price policy

The first thing to say is that this hotel does not have fixed prices for accommodation. In the Dominican Republic it all depends on the season. And from when the person decided to book a room. Also, the presence or absence of discounts depends on this. If, for example, guests decided to book an apartment 1-2 weeks before the trip and, say, in October-November, then for them the price will be only 60 thousand rubles. This is really very modest - 30 thousand rubles. per person, considering the all-inclusive service.

But if in advance, a few months before the trip, "fix" a room in mid-spring, for example, then you have to pay three times more. Therefore, the best option is to monitor the dynamics of prices and wait for the moment when they will be minimal.

By the way, if guests have a child and they want to take it with them on vacation, then you need to know - you will have to pay extra for $ 67 per night additionally. And he will have to sleep on that bed, which is in the room, since they do not provide extra ones. Here, a child under the age of 12 is considered a child. Anyone older is registered as an adult.





What do the guests say?

And now you can talk about what they leave about staying in Now Garden Punta Cana 5 * reviews. People who stayed here assure: the place is wonderful. Probably one of the best in Punta Cana in terms of combining price and quality. A small but cozy and well-groomed green area, planted with palm trees and lawns - the first thing that catches your eye upon arrival. A significant part of it is occupied by the pool - deep, but always clean. The staff here is following this. And the rooms are cleaned every day, changing linen and towels.

By the way, the people here are very pleasant and polite. Conscientiously carry out their work and in every possible way try to please the guests. One has only to ask for something - they will do it right away.

Larimar

A few words should be said about this hotel, with which the described complex shares the territory. Now Larimar Garden Punta Cana 5 * gets positive reviews too. This hotel is bigger and richer. There are several times more apartments here - about 540. There are tropical deluxe rooms, rooms with club privileges and those that are designed to accommodate several people (more spacious). Speaking of prices, Now Larimar is, of course, a little more expensive. Therefore, many, analyzing the cost and benefits, stop in the next, more budget. After all, the infrastructure is common. Except for the Preferred Club. Only VIP guests of the Larimar Hotel are allowed to enter - those who have paid for this privileged service.





In the end

A few words should be said as a conclusion. If you carefully study the reviews, you can come to one conclusion: definitely worth a trip here. Staying on vacation in one of the hotels, you can get double pleasure. There is a beach, a gentle sea, all kinds of entertainment, a casino, a SPA center and gourmet restaurants. A polite staff makes the rest even more enjoyable. In this place everyone will receive a positive experience and will definitely want to come back here again. Numerous reviews are a direct confirmation of this.
