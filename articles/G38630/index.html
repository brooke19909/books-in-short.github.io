Marmaris Sea Center Hotel 3 * (ex.Sun Maris Central) (Turkey / Marmaris): photos and reviews of tourists from Russia

The Turkish coast still attracts our compatriots with a variety of hotels, among which there are not only fashionable establishments, but also quite budget ones. In general, tourists have a fairly large selection of places to spend a vacation. In our article we want to consider a three-star hotel Marmaris Sea Center Hotel 3 *.

A bit about the hotel

Marmaris Sea Center Hotel 3 * is located in the center of Marmaris. The distance from the airport in the city of Dalaman is about one hundred kilometers. The hotel was built in 1998 and has since been very successfully working in the tourist field. The last major renovation of the complex was carried out in 2010. Description of Marmaris Sea Center Hotel 3 *

The hotel is quite modest in size and belongs to the category of small establishments. The room fund of Marmaris Sea Center Hotel 3 * consists of only 55 apartments. All hotel rooms are presented in one category - these are standards. Not so long ago, the complex underwent repair work, so the inside of the institution has a fairly modern look. All apartments are connected to the central air conditioning system, due to which the rooms maintain the optimum temperature. Bathrooms are equipped not only with slippers and bathrobes, but also with bath accessories and detergents.



All apartments are furnished with standard furniture, which is enough for a comfortable stay. Each room has a large double bed, two bedside tables, a wardrobe. If you have a lot of luggage with you, then it is more reasonable to place it in a storage room.

The apartments of Marmaris Sea Center Hotel 3 * have large flat-screen TVs with satellite channels, among which there are Russians. But there are no safes and minibars in the rooms.

Service for children

Marmaris Sea Center Hotel 3 * is more designed for youth recreation. But here you can fully accommodate with children.



Of course, the infrastructure is not aimed at babies, but everything in the rooms can be installed on request cots. The hotel's restaurant offers high chairs for young guests. In the main pool for babies there is a separate bathing area. If necessary, it is possible to order babysitting services for the child.

Catering

Marmaris Sea Center Hotel 3 * (Turkey), like other resort hotels, offers all-inclusive meals. The main restaurant serves buffet meals. Among the variety of European and Turkish dishes, guests of the establishment can choose something suitable for themselves. The hotel also has several bars, the menu of which is presented not only with drinks, but also with snacks and sweets.

Hotel Service

Marmaris Sea Center Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Marmaris) offers its guests to use standard services, including currency exchange and the ability to use the Internet. If necessary, receptionists can call a doctor.



However, for his services will have to pay separately. Unfortunately, the hotel does not have a car rental point, which is very inconvenient, according to tourists.

Beach vacation

The hotel is located near the coast. According to reviews of Marmaris Sea Center Hotel 3 *, the nearest beach is 100 meters from the hotel. The coast is across the road from the complex, which can be overcome through an underpass. In this region, beaches have sand and pebble cover. A bit about the resort

Marmaris has the status of the best resort in the country. In general, the town can be called the most European in all of Turkey. More recently, only the British and Dutch rested here. But everything is changing, and now at the resort you can meet many Russian tourists. Numerous guests are attracted by the terrific climate of the area. Even during the high season there is no exhausting heat. And the sea in the closed bay is calm, because the islands cover the bay. There are practically no waves. Smooth entry into the sea is very convenient.



In general, Marmaris is very famous among young people, since the fame of a dance resort has entrenched in him. Marmaris consists of four villages - Armutalan, Sarigerme, Turunch and Icmeler. Each of them has its own characteristics and advantages.

The beaches of the resort are not a very wide strip, which is separated from the hotels and restaurants by the roadway. The entire coast has a sand cover and a gentle slope. The city beach of the municipality is always crowded with people. And its purity leaves much to be desired. However, there are other beaches in the resort, and some of them are even marked with the Blue Flag.

Marmaris is incredibly popular for diving. In its vicinity there are more than 50 centers offering dives to ancient ruins. Noteworthy and underwater animals. One of the most famous places for divers is the Basa Cave.

Nutrition Reviews

According to reviews, Marmaris Sea Center Hotel 3 * (Marmaris) operates on an all-inclusive basis. The main restaurant offers a fairly varied buffet with tasty and fresh dishes. For example, for breakfast guests are served eggs, jam, honey, cereal, granola, cookies and other standard dishes. But for lunch there are many salads of fresh vegetables, soups, meat, side dishes, sweets, stewed vegetables. For dinner, the assortment of dishes is about the same. Of fruit, the restaurant usually serves melons and watermelons.



As for drinks, wine, tea, coffee, beer are available to guests without any restrictions, thanks to the all-inclusive concept. Many tourists praise food very much. Of course, the assortment of food is not as great as, for example, in five-star establishments, but everything is prepared very tasty. And nobody will remain hungry here.

General impression of the hotel

Numerous enthusiastic reviews from tourists are the best recommendation for the establishment. Guests are noted by friendly staff who are attentive to all vacationers. Of course, only one person speaks Russian at the reception. But this does not stop our tourists from relaxing at all. There is practically no language barrier; one can always explain.

The hotel grounds are incredibly small. Or rather, it simply does not exist. Behind the building there is only a small pool with a small seating area and several sun loungers. The hotel is a standard city institution. And the whole complex has an incredibly convenient location. Located in the center of Marmaris, it gives all guests the opportunity at any time to walk through the local streets and walk to the market and the pedestrian street with bars and restaurants.

Despite the fact that the hotel is located on the second line, the distance from it to the beach does not exceed hundreds of meters. As mentioned, only the carriageway separates the hotel from the coastline. A special underpass allows you to safely cross the road. Of course, the nearest municipal beach is quite dirty and crowded, so not all guests want to relax on it.

Every day at nine in the morning a bus leaves the hotel, which takes tourists free of charge to Nirvana Beach. In the evening he returns. Everyone can visit the beautiful coast. The road to the beach takes only about ten minutes. Tourists will definitely recommend visiting Nirvana Beach. Firstly, this is a very beautiful place, and secondly, there are much fewer people, and the coast and water are incredibly clean. Stunningly beautiful photographs can be taken on Nirvana Beach. Even if you do not want to go to this beach every day, then at least once you need to visit it.



For those who do not want to get up early for the sake of transfer, the salvation will be dolmushi, where you can easily get to Nirvana Beach.

Of course, there is no animation and playgrounds, but with such a close location of the sea hardly something else is needed. In general, tourists from Russia highly recommend this institution from the best side. Therefore, when planning a vacation in Marmaris, you can pay attention to it.
