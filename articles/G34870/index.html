Invites the Tymoshkov shopping and entertainment center in Bryansk

Bryansk is located on the Desna River, it is the regional center of the Russian Federation with a population of more than four thousand people. The first references to Bryansk are found in epic annals with the name "Debryansk". In the XIII century, the Bryansk principality was formed, which was part of the Principality of Lithuania. The city was in the center of interests of many states and was repeatedly subjected to attacks and sieges. Only under Peter I was Bryansk significantly strengthened, a shipyard and an artillery arsenal were built for campaigns against Turkey. Currently, the city has not lost its industrial potential, more than twenty factories and factories of various directions work, and the trading sector is represented by modern shops and shopping and entertainment centers.

SEC "Timoshkov" (Bryansk, st. Krasnoarmeyskaya, 71-75)

The mall is located in the very center of the city, which allows you to get to it from any area by public transport. Also for personal transport there is a parking lot for one hundred and fifty places and a two-level underground parking for 250 cars, which avoids problems with street parking in the city center. The seven-story building housed more than 150 stores of various profiles. The Tymoshkov shopping and entertainment center in Bryansk offers all home goods, furniture of Russian and foreign manufacturers, any household appliances and various services. Branded clothing and footwear stores provide a huge selection for every taste and budget. In stores you can pick up a gift, buy souvenirs and accessories, please ladies with jewelry, cosmetics and perfumes.

Entertainment

A variety of pastimes can be provided by the Timoshkov shopping and entertainment center in Bryansk. The cinema has eight halls with different capacities from 90 to 170 people. All halls are equipped with the latest technology and sound in 3D format, they have a unique design and very comfortable seats. The cinema has a VIP lounge for 32 people, where you can watch not only films, but also private shootings for a large family or company. For youth, the Veles nightclub operates in the mall. Here, the night city comes to life, massive parties and theme parties are held to the sounds of the most modern music. The Mowgli Family Leisure Center takes care of the little ones. In the play areas, the kids will have fun under the supervision of qualified animators. There are slot machines and a children's cafeteria with a varied selection of sweets and drinks.

Sports and outdoor activities

The Tymoshkov shopping center in Bryansk offers the opportunity for outdoor activities. Twelve lane bowling is equipped according to international standards, with a good rental base and a cafe where you can have a tasty snack, choose drinks and cocktails. The Billiard Club chain of billiard clubs provides the most diverse selection of tables for fans of this interesting game. Sports enthusiasts can visit the fitness club, where under the guidance of experienced instructors they will train simulators, Pilates equipment and other fitness techniques.

It is always interesting and good time to spend time in the Tymoshkov shopping center in Bryansk. Opening hours daily from 9.00 to 21.00.
