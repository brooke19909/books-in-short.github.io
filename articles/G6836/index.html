Cleopatra Apts: relaxing in hot Spain

Once the town of Tenerife was a quiet and peaceful place by the sea, and now has become a real garden of paradise for lovers of a comfortable stay. Spain is famous for its climate, hospitality, culture, history and, of course, attractions. If you decide to relax in Spain, then you should stay at Cleopatra Apts.



Hotel location

It is located in Spain, in the lovely resort town of Tenerife, on the shores of the warm, gentle ocean. Hotel Cleopatra Apts is one of the best places to relax with family, friends, or your loved one. There is an airport near the hotel and you won‚Äôt have to wait long for arrival after a hard flight. Cleopatra Palace is one of the five famous Mare Nostrum Resort hotels, which means that it has no equal in terms of comfort, service and cuisine. The complex forms the letter "P", and inside there is a swimming pool with marble columns, a fountain, a jacuzzi and statues. This place is a real paradise for vacationers.

Description of rooms

The hotel has a few rooms - only 199. Each room is impeccable: beautiful views, comfortable furniture, beautiful design ... The apartments are furnished with bathrobes, telephones, satellite TV, terrace or balcony, air conditioning, hairdryer, and mini bar and safe. The rooms are cleaned daily, so the apartments are in perfect condition. Cleopatra Apts has several room types:

- Double rooms "Standard";
- suites with a balcony;
- suites with private pool, terrace and oceanfront view;
- Suite Suite with a terrace, a swimming pool and stunning views of the ocean;
- Royal apartments with bedrooms, a jacuzzi, a pool, a terrace and ocean views.

Cleopatra apts infrastructure

The hotel is equipped with chic rooms for business and social gatherings, dinners and many other events. Here you can even celebrate your wedding! Themed rooms will create an incredible atmosphere! For example, the Salon Fontenoy Hall is decorated in a classic 18th-century style, with candelabra and wall tapestries. Here you can spend your evening at home with your friends or colleagues. The hotel also has Salon Berri and Grand Salon, which create an atmosphere of lightness, luxury and wealth!

Entertainment, sports, leisure and service

Cleopatra Palace Tenerife offers the following services: parking, 24-hour reception, safe, elevator, business center services, security, doctors. As for entertainment and sports, tourists will enjoy the presence of four different pools, as well as a nudist rooftop pool, jacuzzi, spa, hairdresser, aerobics and sports hall, sauna and fitness center. You can also play mini-golf, billiards, tennis and squash at the hotel. Thus, you will diversify your free time from excursions and spend it interestingly. And little vacationers will be able to play at the playground at their leisure, as well as swim in the pool. In addition, the animators will take care of the complete safety of your kids.

Nutrition

Cleopatra Apts provides half-board meals or only a buffet breakfast to choose from for its guests. The complex also has 12 restaurants and several bars where you can taste delicious dishes and have fun. Cleopatra Apts is a place that is worthy of admiration and love of tourists, because it is not in vain that it is so popular!
