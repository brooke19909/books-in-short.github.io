general characteristics

The hotel complex Armas Kaplan Paradise Hotel 5 * (Tekirova) was commissioned in 1997, the last repair was carried out in 2015. The total area is 33,000 square meters. Hotel Armas Kaplan Paradise Hotel 5 * (Kemer) consists of two five-story buildings equipped with elevators, which makes it easy to overcome the path with heavy suitcases from the reception to your room.
