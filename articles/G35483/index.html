Hotel Life Green Hill Hotel Hv1. Turkey, Antalya

Description: Life Green Hill Hotel Hv1, a beach hotel complex, built in 1995, is conveniently located on a hilly hill of the resort of Antalya in the middle of beautiful nature, 8 km from Alanya Airport. The hotel occupies about 150 thousand m 2 of land: on this rather vast area there are 18 two- and three-story villas equipped with everything necessary for a quality and relaxed vacation on the coast.



The boarding house Life Green Hill Hotel Hv1 is famous for its rich infrastructure - you do not have to go outside the hotel to have fun and relax. All this you will find in a beautiful area with its own zoo and flowering garden, which grows decorative palm trees, amazingly beautiful shrubs and flowers. This is a mini-town with stone stairs, narrow paths, an amusement park and a vineyard.

At the hotel, every detail is thought out to the smallest detail and created to give warmth to its guests. And so that the rest was even brighter, more interesting and tastier, enchanting shows are organized daily on the territory of the house , which undoubtedly will appeal to both young tourists and adults.

Rooms (331): Upon arrival, guests will find magnificent rooms with a private balcony. From the windows of half of the rooms you can enjoy a view of the flowering garden and the Mediterranean Sea. For the most demanding customers there are luxury apartments with two rooms.



Room equipment: air conditioning, direct dial telephone, satellite TV (Russian channels are broadcast), bathroom with shower, paid safe, bar with a wide range of drinks. Cots are available for children (for a fee). Cleaning is done daily, and a bed change is made several times in seven days.

Meals (all-inclusive): Breakfast at the Green Hill Bay Hotel Hv1 is served from 7 a.m. as a buffet. Guests eat three times a day, and all day you can enjoy free hot dishes, salads, hamburgers, pastries and drinks of different strengths in the snack bar.



There are several restaurants in the building, one with an open terrace and views of the blue sea. In these establishments visitors are treated to dishes of national and European cuisines. The menu includes elite varieties of alcoholic beverages and a large assortment of natural juices. For youth, the Terrace Bar is open around the clock. There are also three more bars on the territory where you can try real Turkish coffee and delicious pastries.

Beach (own): The beach can be reached by funicular or go down the stairs yourself. The coast is sandy, wide, with a gentle descent into the water and breakwaters. Tourists can take beach amenities for free and enjoy their vacation at least all day. Tasty cakes are made on the shore and refreshing juices are offered.



Additional information: Equipped playgrounds, a development club, a small zoo, a safe outdoor pool and entertaining animations await young guests at Life Green Hill Hotel Hv1. Parents' comments about the children's program are simply enthusiastic. And in the evenings, guys can watch cartoons and dance.

For adults, several swimming pools have been built on the territory around which there are comfortable sunbeds and sun umbrellas. In one of the pools there are water slides of different heights that will appeal to active people. Guests can also visit the gym, massage room, Turkish bath and sauna.



From sports entertainment: table tennis, aerobics, billiards, darts, motor and non-motorized types of water activities on the beach. Free internet is available in the lobby. The building has many souvenir and grocery stores, there is a left-luggage office, dry cleaning, an exchange office and a tour desk.

Digest: Excellent guesthouse Life Green Hill Hotel Hv1, offering a lot of entertainment, a storm of positive emotions and chic living conditions. The admiration for many was wonderful and fun animation, the friendliness of the staff, the cleanliness in the whole building, a varied and high-quality menu. Parents with kids praised children's entertainment.

Pleased with a large well-groomed area, dotted with colorful plants and tall spreading trees. The beach also left only positive memories for vacationers. Thanks to the breakwaters, the sea was always calm. The hotel administration is trying every year to increase the level of services for the benefit of guests.
