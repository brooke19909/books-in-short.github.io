Club Novostar Dar Khayam (Tunisia / Hammamet): photos and reviews of tourists

Tunisia is an amazing distinctive country in North Africa on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea. Millions of travelers travel to Tunisia, attracted by the possibilities of a varied holiday. This corner of the earth absolutely confirms all the archaic ideas about the classical resort: kilometers of fantastic flat beaches with white and golden fine sand, picturesque landscapes of immense Sahara, warm dry climate, rich cultural heritage with a touch of French colonial traditions, many unique sights, thalassotherapy centers, spicy cuisine, a unique combination of oriental flavor of the Gallic culture.

Tunisia is very difficult to call a dynamic or fast-growing resort, it is generally difficult to suspect it in some growth! But he does not become less attractive from this and annually stably receives his impressive portion of international tourists.

To travel to Tunisia, a Russian traveler does not need to go through the infernal visa pre-registration procedure. Hotel Club Novostar Dar Khayam 3 * (Tunisia, Hammamet)

The hotel has an almost perfect location. It is located right on the shores of the fabulous Mediterranean Sea with access to one of the best beaches in the Hammamet area. Club Novostar Dar Khayam is located 6 km from the city. The historic Yasmine Hammamet is 14.5 km away and the shuttle to Enfida Airport is 30 minutes (60 km).

The hotel was built back in 1975, a little later than the city of Carthage. But still, for a hotel this is too old an age. Everything was pretty dull until in 2009 the hotel joined the Novostar Hotels chain, and then underwent a massive renovation in 2010. Before the hotel became part of the Novostar chain, it was called Dar Khayam 3 *.

Standard check-in time is 14:00, check-out is 12:00. Tourists in their reviews have repeatedly noted that if the hotel has ready-made rooms, then guests are populated without problems, they are not forced to wait for the official time. For the summer period, Russian-speaking personnel work here, including Russian children's animators

Hotel Territory

This large complex has a spacious, well-groomed area. There are only seven pools here. One of them is a whole sports and entertainment water park with five slides, which delight both large and small tourists who have arrived at the hotel (Tunisia).

Club Novostar Dar Khayam 3 * has a common infrastructure with another hotel in the chain called Club Novostar Omar Khayam. Guests can freely spend time in the open spaces of the neighboring complex.

There are also small children's pools with fountains and small safe slides. They can be visited from 10am to 6pm. Sun beds and umbrellas by the pool are free, towels are issued on the security of 20 TND, mattresses are paid.





Also on the territory of the Club Novostar Dar Khayam 3 * complex there are:

  • platforms for playing beach soccer, archery;
  • mini-golf courses (12 km);
  • tennis courts;
  • gift shop and shopping center;
  • currency exchange point;
  • SPA complex and beauty salon, sauna, hammam and fitness center with a gym;
  • an amphitheater and a large platform for evening animation;
  • conference rooms.

There is free Wi-Fi in the lobby and at the reception, and wireless internet in the rooms must be paid separately.

The hotel has a main restaurant (Swedish line) and two a la carte establishments. There is also a children's menu. If the hotel has more than 20 children resting, then the main restaurant serves a separate Swedish line with baby food. A la carte restaurants have free admission 2 times a week. Advance booking required. One restaurant prepares classic Italian dishes, while the other offers spicy masterpieces of national Tunisian cuisine.
