Pathology of the musculoskeletal system

This group can include:

  • arthritis, polyarthritis of non-tuberculosis origin;
  • residual manifestations of rheumatoid arthritis caused by heart failure (up to grade I);
  • rheumatoid polyarthritis to the II degree;
  • deforming arthrosis;
  • osteochondrosis;
  • consequences of joint damage;
  • diseases of the spine of non-tuberculosis origin;
  • ankylosing spondylitis (spondylosis, spinal osteochondrosis, ankylosing spondylitis);
  • diseases of muscles, bones, tendons;
  • delayed fracture consolidation;
  • bone calluses (in the absence of a false joint);
  • osteitis, periostitis, bursitis, tenosynovitis, periarthrosis, contracture;
  • non-hematogenous osteomyelitis (in the event that surgical intervention is not required).




Nervous system

When using physiotherapy, the following pathologies of this group can be effectively adjusted:

  • Diseases of the nervous system (peripheral) - polyarthritis, radiculitis, raliculoneuritis, sciatica, lumbalgia, lumbar ischialgia, neuritis, polyneuritis, facial neuritis, ternary nerve, lesions of the nerves of the extremities, neurofibromioma (in the absence of exacerbation).
  • Diseases of the nervous system (autonomic) - polyneuritis, solarium, sympathoganglionitis (after the end of the acute phase).
  • Nerve damage - injuries of the plexus, roots, nerve trunks (in the absence of surgical indications).
  • Diseases and consequences of injuries caused to the central nervous system are residual manifestations of encephalitis, arachnoiditis or encephalomyelitis (only after the end of the acute phase, but not earlier than three months after the disease).
  • The consequences of open or closed brain injuries - in the first case, treatment in a sanatorium is possible only after 5-6 months. from the moment of damage, in the second - after 4 months.
  • Myelitis - 4 months after the end of the acute phase.
  • Residual manifestations of poliomyelitis (acute) - after 3-6 months. after the end of the acute phase.
  • Cerebral atherosclerosis (regularly occurring circulatory disorders in the brain of mild or moderate degree) - after 3-4 months. from the last attack.
  • Pathologies of the nervous system of a functional nature - neurasthenia, as well as its hypo- and hypersthenic forms; neurosis, neurosis-like and asthenic conditions, irritable weakness syndrome.
  • Vegetative-vascular dysfunction caused by trauma, infection or endocrine disorders.
  • Migraine in any form of manifestation.
  • Cerebral palsy.

Genitourinary System

The complex of wellness procedures offered by the sanatorium "Khilovo" (Pskov region) also has a beneficial effect on the restoration of the human body after suffering from urological diseases of any form and etiology, including chronic prostatitis.

Rehabilitation after occupational diseases and injuries

  • The residual manifestations of injuries of the nervous system (both peripheral and central) are the consequences of a closed or open TBI. Physiotherapy procedures in the sanatorium are possible after 3-4 months. from receiving a closed injury and after 5-6 months. after receiving open head injury or patchwork craniotomy. In this case, there should be no significant violations in the motor sphere that do not allow the patient to move independently, for example, paralysis. Also contraindicated are increased intracranial and blood pressure, episodes of epileptic seizures, hypothalamic crises and mental disorders.
  • Residual manifestations of injuries in the spine and spinal cord. Treatment in a sanatorium is possible 4-6 months after an injury or neurosurgical operation. The patient should not have deep cuts, purulent-septic complications, vascular and neurotrophic disorders, disorders of the motor system and the impaired functioning of the pelvic organs.
  • Residual manifestations of injuries of plexuses, roots and nerve trunks of limbs (pain syndromes, vascular disorders). Direction to the sanatorium "Hilovo" is possible no earlier than 1.5-2 months from the time of surgery or injury. The patient should have positive dynamics in the process of restoration of functions. A prerequisite for staying in a sanatorium is also the availability of the ability to independently service and move around (with sensitive and motor impairments).

Wellness treatments

The idea to relax and at the same time improve your health at all times was very popular. Exactly this type of vacation is offered by the sanatorium "Khilovo". The Pskov region is rich in healing natural resources, which competent scientists and doctors did not fail to take advantage of. The embodiment of their labors became new techniques aimed at the natural healing of the human body. For the prevention, treatment and restoration of the health of its guests in the arsenal of the resort there are a number of various wellness programs and procedures. Of particular note are:

  • Balneotherapy - in other words, external treatment with mineral water. A beneficial effect on the human body is achieved due to the rich chemical composition, temperature, and also the hydrostatic pressure exerted by it . Healing baths or showers with healing water are very useful for disorders of the cardiovascular or musculoskeletal systems, diseases of the internal organs, skin or nervous system.




  • Hydrogen sulfide baths - thanks to the procedure, microcirculation in the body improves. Blood flow in the liver, kidneys, muscles, and the brain increases. This leads to a decrease in platelet aggregation and blood viscosity, a decrease in swelling of perivascular tissue. The principle of action of hydrogen sulfide baths is to increase the number of reduced and reduced oxidized proteins, which leads to a decrease in the biosynthesis of cholesterol and fatty acids, and normalization of the exchange of mucopolysaccharides. This procedure has an effective effect in the treatment of coronary heart disease, cerebral ischemia, peripheral vascular disease and some other disorders of the cardiovascular system.

And this is not the whole list of diseases that will help to correct the natural resources of "Hilovo" (sanatorium).

How to get there

There are several options. You can arrive at the Khilovo sanatorium from the city of St. Petersburg by a long-distance bus. Departure is at 8:15 from the bus station. His address is Naberezhnaya Obvodnogo Canal, 36. Arrival in the sanatorium "Khilovo" - at 13:30. Return flight time: departure from the health resort at 15:30, arrival in St. Petersburg at 20:45. In addition, for the convenience of visitors, every Monday they are met by a health resort transport. The site of the sanatorium "Hilovo" contains the exact bus schedule. If necessary, you can arrange a meeting on other days and times according to a pre-filled application. To do this, you can call the "Hilovo" (sanatorium).
