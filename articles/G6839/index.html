Kindergarten is like fabulous scenery from Disneyland.

State farm named after Lenin, the children's park "Lukomorye" - a combination of these words has long been a standard in organizing children's leisure.

"At the Seaside ..."

Children's Park "Lukomorye" (state farm named after Lenin) invariably arouses admiration among children and parents at any time of the year.

Thirty-three heroes, as if inviting to proceed to a fairy tale, are majestically and strictly met at the gate, on which the dragon is depicted.
As usual in any fairy tale, at the beginning of an unexplored path lies a stone with the lines carved on it: "You will go to the right ..., to the left ...". With each further step, travelers understand that history comes to life and is addictive, beckons to see what will happen next.

Here is a fabulous tree. Around it is a golden chain wrapped. A fairy-tale cat, a scientist walks on it, looks at each visitor with curious huge eyes. The mermaid is comfortably located on the branches - obviously he is not in a hurry anywhere, I am glad to every guest.

A hut on chicken legs. The roof was overgrown with fly agaric, the ravens sat in a friendly company and looked down on passers-by. Just do not live in the hut of the Baba Yaga. Windows and doors are in place. Moreover, you can enter the hut and slide down an excellent hill.

The fountain surrounded by sculptural frogs, the sparkling coolness of the beating jets is real grace on a sultry day.

Interactive towers are waiting for guests, where mechanical dolls live: songs are sung, fairy tales are told - just press the button located next to each window.

Lukomorye Park (state farm named after Lenin) prepared a real adventure for young guests - a suspended cable town stretching between the trees. Not even a town - a huge multilevel city with rope ladders, tunnels.

For beginners and very young daredevils, the height above the ground is small: so that the kids are not afraid, and their parents did not catch their hearts from worries about their child from excitement.

The child will master, making his way along the simple structures, and can already move to structures located at a higher height, with a different level of difficulty.

Parents can also remember their childhood and swing on a swing, while the kids have fun on the rides, ride on a carousel with a horse.

The fairytale castle, through which the airways of the cable town lie, is striking. Stairs, secret passages, the real royal throne ... From the doors and windows a variety of slides invite to slide down: straight and twisted, gentle and steep.

A fabulous journey takes a lot of energy, especially for young adventure lovers. The creators of the park took care of visitors to the children's center.

At the entrance there is a pavilion where you can buy ice cream and water, enjoy fresh pastries.

Lukomorye Park (state farm named after Lenin) is also a wonderful log cafe with the unusual name Grandfather Au. Here you can relax, and in winter, warm up and dry children's clothes that are wet from the endless rolling down from the icy slides.

The cafe has wooden furniture, cozy at home. Visitors are met by waiters in Russian folk images. The dishes are delicious, the menu is varied:
