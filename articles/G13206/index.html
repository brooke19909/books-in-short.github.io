Mykonos island (Greece) awaits guests

Are you looking for an exclusive, prestigious and fashionable vacation? Then we advise you to go to the island of Mykonos (Greece). He, as they say, does not require advertising. The richest, most fashionable and brilliant party people in the world prefer to go here. Why? Yes, because here you can not only relax one hundred percent, but also see an unprecedented combination of natural beauties and marvelous landscapes "cultivated" by man. With all this, rest is not expensive - hotels are aimed at tourists with the most varied incomes. So, before us is the mysterious and legendary Mykonos.



Greece has long become attractive to Russians and the entire world community. They come here on vacation and on vacation. But getting to Mykonos means catching luck by the tail. This is confirmed by many of those who were lucky enough to be here. Indeed, from the first time, this amazing island cannot even be described in its entirety. Stunningly beautiful beaches and some unearthly blue and white streets, as if descended from the paintings of the Impressionists. Fun around the clock and the whole year. Shopping that makes offers impossible to refuse. All this is Mykonos! Greece itself as if invites its guests to the celebration of life, but on this island, entertainment is a whole art. In a word, you will not find such an atmosphere in almost any place.

There are only a little more than six thousand inhabitants, but they are all distinguished by a joyful, welcoming and good-natured character.

Although the island has a rocky topography, the capital - the city of Chora - is located on flat ground. Mykonos (Greece) used to be very dependent on maritime trade. Therefore, all the main metropolitan attractions are concentrated near the harbor. Here is the temple of Our Lady of Paraportiani, a church from the period of the Byzantine Empire. And from here you can take a ferry to the equally legendary Greek island of Delos - the birthplace of the twin gods Artemis and Apollo.



What else is so interesting in Mykonos? Greece, photographs of the sights of which appear on calendars and wallpapers, often appears before us in the form of dazzling white houses, stairs descending to the coast, or windmills towering above the "Martian" landscape. So, it is possible that these pictures were taken here.

The island even boasts its own "Venice" - the Alefkandra district, where boats enter the cellars of houses. If you are interested in history, you can visit the archaeological museum. Pelicans walk freely on the coast and even allow themselves to be photographed. By the way, Mykonos (Greece) is a place of universal tolerance and cosmopolitanism. Muslims, nudists, representatives of sexual minorities, wrapped to eyebrows, traditional married couples - all here get along peacefully and without conflict. Everyone finds his own corner in this paradise!
