Sunny Beach Costa del Sol

At the southernmost tip of the Iberian Peninsula is the famous Spanish resort of Costa del Sol. The very geographical name of this place is capable of saying a lot. Translated from Spanish, it means "sunny beach". And the name perfectly characterizes this stretch of the coast of Andalusia. It is really sunny here, and the onset of winter can be seen only by looking at the calendar. Air temperature rarely drops below +16 ¬∞ C even in January. Costa del Sol is an off-season resort. The sun shines here 320 days a year. This, although not the only one, but one of the main reasons for its popularity.



Costa del Sol, Spain. Hotels, Attractions and Infrastructure

This stretch of the Mediterranean coast stretches from Malaga to Gibraltar. Here everything is turned towards the sea, and there is completely nothing missing that could impede a comfortable rest and a pleasant pastime. In this part of Andalusia there is no manufacturing industry and facilities that have a negative impact on the environment. The basis of the economy in Malaga is the reception of tourists from all countries and continents. For many kilometers stretched along the coast of the picturesque beaches with clean sand. And a little higher, on the slopes of coastal hills covered with lush subtropical vegetation, stretches an equally endless string of hotels, hotels, campsites, bars, restaurants, night clubs and other entertainment venues.





The tourist infrastructure in the Costa del Sol has been built for a long time and meets the highest international standards in this area. The resort coast of Andalusia is one of the most prestigious in the whole Mediterranean, and the land here is very expensive. This circumstance has been appreciated by many rich and influential people in this world. Real estate owned by them is scattered along the entire coast to Gibraltar itself. Representatives of artistic bohemia and show business are not deprived of their attention on this coast.

In addition to a luxurious beach holiday, ancient Andalusia offers its guests the opportunity to join their unique culture, whose monuments, objects and artifacts are abundant along the entire coast of the Costa del Sol and not far from it.
