Let us consider in more detail how knitting a patent gum fits. Following each step, it is easy to obtain a blank for the execution of any product.





Execution technique

This pattern is considered classic and is one of the most popular ornaments.

So, how does patent gum fit with knitting needles? The scheme for obtaining this pattern is not required. A detailed description of the work will be sufficient.

  • 1 row. Pick up an odd number of loops on the knitting needles.
  • 2 row. Alternate facial and wrong. The first loop in the row is removed, and the last is knitted to get a neat edge.
  • 3 row. Knit the front in the usual way. The wrong loop is removed along with the thread thrown over it.
  • 4 row and subsequent. Repeat the previous step. In this case, the front is tied from the filament of the previous row with the double crochet.

Following the instructions, it is worth paying attention to the fact that patent gum with knitting needles does not fit too tightly. The tension of the thread should be the same and uniform throughout the fabric. The ornament looks especially beautiful and embossed with a sufficient thickness of yarn. Scarves are often knitted with this pattern. A description of one of the models is given later in this article.

How to tie a scarf with patent rubber?

A very original and elegant product is obtained if you use several working shades when working. A scarf with a width of 17-18 cm and a length of 95-100 cm will require approximately 200 g of wool blend yarn with an average thickness of the thread. When alternating colors, take them in an equal volume. To start, dial 33 loops plus 1 edge on both sides. Perform the first row with a simple rubber band, alternating between the front and the wrong. From the beginning of the edge, remove the hem without knitting. At the end of the row, perform it on the wrong side. Then proceed as described above. It is better to alternate yarn according to colors through several rows, not stretching the threads along the fabric, but breaking off. At the end of the work, the nodules must be hidden with a hook between the threads. When the scarf reaches the desired length, knit the last row, as the first - without crochets. Then crochet close all the loops. Decorate the item with long tassels or pompons. In addition to this simplest way to knit a scarf, patent gum is also used in a circle. With its help you can get an unusual hat-snood.





How to make a pattern in a circle?

This technology of ornamentation is somewhat different from obtaining a direct canvas, when knitting which all rows, starting from the third, are the same. Patent gum, made with knitting needles in a circle, is obtained by alternating two work options. Having connected the typed loops in a circle, take the border between them. Then, as usual, start with a simple rubber band. In the second row, crochet over the wrong loops. In the third round, the usual course of work is changing. Knit the wrong crochet loops on the wrong side, and remove the front loops without crocheting together with the thread. In the fourth row, everything is done the other way around. That is, removed facial are performed facial, and wrong are removed with a crochet. The finished web is obtained, like a regular patent gum. In this way, for example, a five-spoke sock cuff is knitted. Having examined the technology for obtaining the ornament, we proceed to the manufacture of the headgear-pipe.
