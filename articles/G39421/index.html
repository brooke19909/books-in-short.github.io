Volgograd, minibuses: timetable. Routes of minibuses of Volgograd

In a city like Volgograd, minibuses are an important component of public transport. The largest center of the southern Volga region cannot develop without road infrastructure. To ensure the freedom of movement of residents and guests of the city, an extensive network of city transport has been created, where fixed-route taxis play an important role.





Transport issue

The metropolis, where officially more than 1 018 000 people live, has become a place of attraction for tourists, students, migrants, workers from other regions. According to experts, on certain days in the city there are up to one and a half million people. Utility vehicles - buses, trams, trains and trolleybuses - are not always able to guarantee the transportation of all comers, especially during peak hours. In addition, on secondary roads it is inexpedient to let “big” equipment. In this regard, the load on public transport in Volgograd is significant. Minibuses mainly represented by private companies save the situation.

In addition to city routes, this type of transport carries passengers throughout the region. Mainly in satellite settlements and large regional centers.

Minibuses "Volgograd - Volzhsky"

The city of Volzhsky (327,000 people) is a large industrial center. Between him and Volgograd - 21 km in a straight line and 29 km along the highway. Two satellite cities are united by a developed public transport network. Including shuttle vans. For example, one of the most popular is route 246: the Volgograd-Volzhsky minibus runs from Tulak Street (the regional center) to the wholesale base (Volzhsky).

Minibuses "Volgograd - Kamyshin"

Kamyshin is the third most populated city in the Volgograd region. The distance to the regional center is 170 km. In the city of Volgograd minibuses call in 3-3.5 hours. The permanent route is organized by LLC “Noi-T” in comfortable minibuses. Departure is carried out from the central bus station Kamyshin.

  • Volgograd-Kamyshin: every hour from 7:00 to 19:00.

Minibuses "Volgograd - Mikhailovka"

The district center Mikhailovka is another popular intercity destination within the region. There are more than three dozen enterprises in the city, and the settlement is also a popular destination for a relaxing holiday. From Volgograd to the city of Mikhailovka minibuses depart from the bridge between the railway and the bus station. Departure from Mikhailovka is carried out from the city bus station. Distance - 180 km, estimated travel time - 3-3.5 hours.

Bus Schedule

The city department has approved a specific work schedule for each route. In a city like Volgograd, minibuses begin to work in the early morning. In time to get to work, study will not be a problem. Most routes operate from 6:00.





At peak hours, the movement of fixed-route taxis is debugged very well. Most serviceable machines work on the lines. However, in the evening, the number of minibuses decreases markedly, time intervals of movement increase. This is due to a decrease in passenger traffic. Some routes finish work by 19: 00-20: 00. After 22:00 only a few routes work (No. 24a, 28, 30a, 39, 51k, 53, 54a, 57k, 59, 84, 99).

Thousands of minibuses daily help residents of the millionth Volgograd and the region to get to their destination. Without their help, public transport would not be easy to serve passengers. This type of transport is evolving, the fleet is being updated, routes are being optimized, drivers learn courtesy.
