Jumpsuit. Cutting master class

To sew a cat costume for a boy with your own hands, you will need a fleecy fabric in two colors - the main color and a light spot on the stomach.

The front closure is best made with a zipper. If it is decided to make it on buttons or buttons, you should consider this moment when cutting out the details.

  1. Before making a cat costume for a boy with his own hands, the patterns should be transferred to paper. This is most conveniently done using graph paper used by draftsmen. Then all the details are cut out.
  2. Matter of the primary color is folded along in half, face inward.
  3. On the material lay out the details of the pattern marked ‚Äú2 children.‚Äù, Because after cutting it will turn out just a couple of such details. Therefore, drawings of the back, front and sleeves are taken.
  4. The patterns on the fabric must be laid out so that the direction of the shared, indicated on the part, coincides with the direction of the shared thread on the piece of fabric.
  5. All parts are carefully circled with a special disappearing felt-tip pen for fabric. You can use dry soap or chalk. A pencil is taken at the worst end, as it leaves a mark, which is then difficult to remove. When contouring parts, allowances for seams should be provided.
  6. Double details are cut with scissors. The fabric is laid inside out.
  7. Cut out the tail of a cat.
  8. From a lighter tissue, a spot is spread on the stomach.




Sewing a jumpsuit

First you should sweep (bait) the details of the back and front.
