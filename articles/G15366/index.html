Hotel Royal Palms Beach 5 * (Kalurata, Sri Lanka): description, photos and reviews of tourists

Sri Lanka is suitable for tourists who are tired of the bustle of big cities. This island paradise offers a truly relaxing vacation. Travelers will find sandy beaches, modern hotels, a lot of ancient attractions and quiet leisure options here. Connoisseurs of luxury and first-class service often choose Royal Palms Beach Hotel 5 *, which is located in the resort area of Kalutara .

Holidays on the island

Sri Lanka is considered a unique holiday destination that attracts travelers who are tired of the monotonous resorts of Turkey, Egypt and other popular tourist countries. You can relax here in winter and summer. The temperature usually does not drop below 25 degrees throughout the year. The only exceptions are the mountainous areas of the island. Sri Lanka will be an ideal option for the New Year holidays. At this time, there is dry and sunny weather without rains and strong winds.

The tourist complex Royal Palms Beach 5 * is located in the south-west of the island. It is part of the famous resort of Kalutara , which is considered a local center for water entertainment. Tourists will appreciate the wide variety of sea sports. Here you can go diving, windsurfing, spearfishing and sailing. The only drawback of the resort is the high cost of living. Budget tourists are unlikely to be able to find a hotel at low prices. The flight from Moscow here will take about 10 hours with transfers, which is also unlikely to please travelers.

Where is the complex located?

Hotel Royal Palms Beach Hotel 5 * (Sri Lanka) has a favorable location. It is considered part of the resort closest to the capital - this is the city of Kalutara . You can get here from Colombo Airport, which is approximately 84 km from the hotel. Depending on traffic congestion, the journey takes about 2-3 hours by bus. For guests for a fee, a round-trip transfer can be arranged. Colombo is 52 km away. You can get to it by taxi.





The hotel building is 60 meters from the public sandy beach. You can reach it in just 2 minutes. Near the complex there are a large number of shops, restaurants and cafes that are aimed at Russian travelers. You can get around the resort by taxi. Small buses, which are called tuk-tuks , are also popular here . The central part of Kalutara is only 2 km away.

Local cuisine and food concepts.

The cuisine of Sri Lanka is famous for its spices, fragrant tea of ‚Äã‚Äãall kinds, as well as rare tropical fruits. Local restaurants serve a huge amount of fish and seafood dishes. Meat, on the contrary, is extremely rare here. Cows, as in India, are considered sacred animals on the island. Therefore, most often they cook pork, which is fried with a lot of seasonings and curry sauce . Fried rice is the most popular side dish. It is prepared here according to a special recipe, frying in coconut milk. Of the desserts, you should definitely try the local pies with fruits, spices and nuts.





Many guests like to eat outside the hotel, because the food in local restaurants is very cheap, and the portions are huge. Therefore, the Royal Palms Beach 5 * complex offers guests a variety of food concepts. For example, you can only pay for breakfast. Or take half board, which additionally includes lunch or dinner. For travelers who prefer to spend time on the beach, it is advisable to pay a full board or an all-inclusive system. The price includes all meals, as well as local drinks, desserts and ice cream. She works every day until 1:00. All drinks, including alcoholic drinks, are served in glasses.

Approximate cost of living

Complex Royal Palms Beach Hotel 5 * ( Kalutara ) can hardly be called a budget hotel. Apart from the cost of the flight and visa, accommodation here will cost tourists about 9,000 rubles per night in a Deluxe room type. The price also does not include meals. If you take a ticket with daily breakfasts, then the price will increase to 11,000 rubles. When buying tickets in the high season, be prepared to pay 15,000 rubles or more.
