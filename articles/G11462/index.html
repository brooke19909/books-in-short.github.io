Hotel Quisisana 3 * (Rimini, Marina Centro): description, services, reviews

Every year a huge number of tourists come to Italy. Some are attracted by sights, others by shopping, others by the sea. The best option for living in this country is inexpensive, but comfortable, as they say, nice little family hotels, where you can always find decent service and genuine hospitality. One of them is Hotel Quisisana 3 *, located in the amazingly beautiful town of Rimini in northwestern Italy, right on the Adriatic coast. It attracts tourists with gorgeous beaches, beautiful old streets, which are so nice to walk in any weather, fascinating service and a unique Italian atmosphere.

How to get there

Even those who first arrived in Italy, it is not difficult to get to Quisisana 3 *. Rimini lies no more than 8 km from the airport, where, from April to mid-October, charters from many Russian cities arrive. In the remaining months of the year, you need to fly to Rimini with transfers in Rome or in some European cities. From the airport to the hotel, you can get a taxi for 30 euros or at a negotiated transfer price from the travel agency where the ticket was purchased. Thrifty citizens can get on a city bus for just 1.3 euros, which will drop them off at stop number 15. From there, to the entrance to the hotel - no more than a dozen meters.



Description

Photos of Quisisana 3 * hotel show a slender five-story building located on a typical Italian street. Other similar houses adjoin it, creating a classic flavor of a small seaside town in the northern part of the country. The hotel area is a small space around this building and an open terrace with tables in front of it, carefully decorated with flowers in pots and flowerpots. In the immediate vicinity of the entrance is a compact parking lot, which, unfortunately, is not enough space for cars of all guests who want to leave them here. If even a place was found, car keys should be left at the concierge.

Infrastructure

In the brochures you can see photos of the Quisisana 3 * hotel, showing a beautiful hall. The owners of the hotel furnished it with comfortable modern sofas, decorated them with fresh flowers, but left objects of antique decor, such as mirrors, shelves, chests of drawers, lamps, paintings.

All this gives the hall the desired coziness and creates a soulful home atmosphere. At the reception, unfortunately, they do not speak Russian, but they understand a little English. Here tourists can take a Wi-Fi code for free in order to use it in their rooms, leave luggage and valuables for storage, call a taxi, arrange an airport transfer, use a fax or photocopy machine. Immediately, in the lobby there is an elevator, a restaurant and a bar.

Rooms

Quisisana 3 * (Rimini) has 52 rooms of the category "standard". Their design complies with the principles of minimalism - everything is just the most necessary. Furniture, bedspreads, walls and curtains are made in pastel beige colors. The rooms are very compact and designed to accommodate one, two or three unpretentious guests. Furnishings - beds, wardrobe, table, chairs, air-conditioner for a fee, mirror, TV (no Russian channels), fridge (for a fee), good wireless internet, hairdryer, balcony.

The hygiene rooms in all rooms are also very compact. They are equipped with a shower (without a booth and without a tray), toilet, bidet, washbasin. Toilet paper and shower facilities are provided. Tourists, as a lack of rooms, point out that the toilet is too noisy and that after taking a shower there is always a lot of water left on the floor.

Sea

Very close to the shores of the Adriatic Sea is Hotel Quisisana 3 *. Italy, Rimini - one of the most popular resorts, has always been famous for its excellent beaches. For some tourists, it is somewhat unusual that the coastline in Rimini, stretching over 15 km, is divided into numbered sections (from 1 to 100), but in practice it is quite convenient. So, Quisisana 3 * belongs to plot number 52. This means that anyone can relax there, but for hotel guests, upon presentation of a card, there are discounts. The beach is paid. Money in the amount of 15 euros must be paid every day in a small office located at the very entrance to the beach. For guests of Quisisana 3 * this amount is less by 5 euros, but places at the water's edge are more expensive.
