Chalet "Wave": description and reviews

Volna (camp site) is a modern leisure complex that meets high standards and is located 20 km from Kremenchug in an ecologically clean area. Here, vacationers are offered all kinds of entertainment and comfortable rooms for living.

Where is

The Volna camp site is located in the Kirovograd region, in the city of Svetlovodsk. The complex itself is located in the railway station "Revovka-2" on the street. Factory, 4l. The institution has a separate closed well-groomed territory.





Volna Chalet accepts guests daily by appointment. The territory of the complex is guarded around the clock. There is a large parking lot for guests' personal vehicles. Phone camp site "Wave" can be found on the official website of the organization.

Accommodations

The guests of the complex are offered a choice of several types of rooms of different comfort classes:

  • economy;
  • standard;
  • Junior Suite
  • Suite

For a fee, an extra person can be accommodated in the room.





All economy rooms are located in a separate building. It has comfortable beds and bedside tables. The bathroom is on the floor. Children under 7 years old stay in these rooms free of charge without a separate bed for sleeping.

Junior Suite has a large double bed with a sofa bed. This room has air conditioning and a fridge. The bathroom is equipped with a shower. It contains a mini set of hygiene products. The room has a large plasma TV with satellite antenna. Guests can use the free internet.

Standard rooms have a large double bed or two single beds. The room has a bathroom with shower. The room has air conditioning and a fridge. There is an ironing board on the floor.

The suite has a modern renovation and comfortable furniture. The room is equipped with all necessary items, like a junior suite. In this category of rooms there are small saunas. These rooms are designed for relaxation of small companies and families with children.

  • fishing;
  • rental of gazebos;
  • beach;
  • walk on the water;
  • bicycles for rent.

Additional services

A quality vacation means not only comfortable living, but also a fun pastime. Chalet "Wave" (photo in the article) offers visitors to use additional services:

  • rent a gazebo with barbecue for 8 people .;
  • rental of fishing equipment;
  • fishing on the equipped platform;

  • sports equipment rental;
  • conference room rental.

On the territory of the complex, guests can relax without accommodation during the day. To do this, they must rent a gazebo. In this case, they can park their vehicles and use the available communications at the hotel.

In the forest outside you can pick mushrooms and berries in season. Evening walks through the pine forest are also popular. The aroma of needles and clean air will not leave anyone indifferent.

Holidays

"Wave" (hostel) at the highest level organizes any celebrations. Here you can spend a birthday or a wedding. Guests can also use the conference room and plan the organization of important negotiations in this place.

Often corporate meetings are held at the camp site. Chefs in the restaurant offer to try dishes of European and Ukrainian cuisine. All items from the menu are prepared with fresh products. There is a large selection of freshly squeezed juices and delicious desserts.





When ordering a conference room, customers can use the necessary equipment. There is a modern multimedia system and high-quality devices to provide the event with sound.

The employees of the complex provide professional service and envelop customers and their guests with unobtrusive attention and care. Participants of the celebration at the end of the celebration can use the services of the hotel and rent rooms for relaxation.
