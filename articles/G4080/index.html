Steam Mop

Every woman dreams that it takes less time to clean the house. But I want the cleaning to be not only quick, but also of high quality, to a shine. Some time ago, an interesting device appeared on sale, which, according to the developers, will help to clean up the house many times faster: clean various surfaces, disinfect them and even get rid of unpleasant odors. All this combines a steam mop, it can help women to clean the house without much hassle. But is it really so? Is advertising worth believing? Let's try to figure it out.



Steam Mop: How It Works

The device is based on steam. Water is poured into a special tank, and then heated to a certain temperature. Further steam enters through openings, the amount of which depends on which nozzle will be selected. A steam mop can be used to clean various surfaces - linoleum, parquet, marble, carpets, etc. True, you should not use such a device to process surfaces that steam can damage. For example, wax floors.

The nozzle for the floor has a practical size, a microfiber pad is put on it. Thanks to it, the surface remains almost dry after cleaning. But the possibilities of the steam mop do not end there. If you detach the top from the base, you will have a sufficiently powerful steam sterilizer in your hands. With it, you can get rid of dirt and bacteria almost anywhere in your home: on windows, stove, curtains, children's toys and so on.

Steam mop: advantages and disadvantages



Let's look at the advantages of the device, as well as note the disadvantages that also occur.

Pros:

  • Cleans, removes dirt from various surfaces.
  • Disinfects, fights bacteria.
  • It does not require the use of various chemicals.
  • Cleaning is many times faster.
  • Cleans dirt even in hard to reach places.
  • Allows you to adjust the temperature of the steam.
  • Included are several nozzles for comfortable cleaning.

Minuses:



If you believe all of the above, then the device is a real find for the home. But is a steam mop so effective? The reviews of those who have already managed to use it are mixed. Many say that it does not fight dirt as effectively as we would like. It cannot cope with severe pollution, and the weight of the device is very large to make it convenient to use. And yet not all reviews are negative. A lot of those who were satisfied with their purchase and now are happy to clean up their home.

Summing up, we can say that the steam mop is quite an interesting and functional device. At least, according to the declared characteristics. Is it worth buying? Probably everyone will find the answer to this question for himself. After all, there are many people, as well as opinions and tastes. Therefore, everyone will relate to the work of a steam mop differently.
