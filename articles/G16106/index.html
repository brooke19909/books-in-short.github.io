Hotel Orfeus Queen Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Side, Colakli): photos and reviews of tourists

Orfeus Queen Hotel & Spa 4 * is a hotel recommended for recreation by many tour operators from around the world. Vacation packages in it must be purchased in advance, long before the start of the holiday season. Such popularity is primarily due to inexpensive vacation prices: a tour here, including all insurance and travel expenses, costs two tourists an average of about 50,000 rubles. For such a price, a weekly stay in the capital's conditions with excellent service is provided.

general information

Orfeus Queen Hotel 4 * - the hotel is modern. It was built in 2015. Since then, repair work has not yet been carried out in its walls, so that tourists have the opportunity to admire its pristine beauty. In their reviews, they often mention that the hotel building has excellent finishes, using the best solutions in the interior, as well as using modern technical equipment.

Location

Orfeus Queen Hotel 4 * is located in Side - one of the most famous resort regions of Turkey. Often a traveler chooses this particular place to rest, guided by knowledge of the favorable climatic conditions prevailing here. Vacationers talk about the fact that throughout the resort period there is quite hot weather, perfectly softened by the air masses coming from the sea.

9 kilometers from this place is the center of Side, where a huge number of places for entertainment are concentrated, and each tourist can choose an occupation to his taste. The seashore is located at a distance of just over half a kilometer from the Orfeus Queen Hotel 4 *, which is not always viewed from the positive side by travelers. However, this fact, nevertheless, does not make the hotel less popular, as every day a free bus delivers to the beach vacationers.



Rooms

The number of rooms Orfeus Queen Hotel 4 * (Colakli) pleases many travelers with an excellent choice of apartments. In total, there are 132 of them and they are presented in three categories: standard, family and promotional.

Each room has a bathroom, which is equipped with modern fixtures. There is not only a bathtub and a toilet, but also a washbasin. In addition, a small box is reserved for the bath kit provided by the hotel. Each bathroom is equipped with a hairdryer.

Many reviews of Orfeus Queen Hotel 4 * say that the right decision by the developers of the project was to install HVAC equipment in each room. According to them, this allows you to create the most favorable temperature conditions for rest and maintain them at a certain level throughout the rest.

Standard

Standard category apartments provide accommodation in their small area (25 m2) of three guests. There are two beds for them: one of them is single, and the second is double. This room is decorated in a combination of delicate mint and white colors, which makes the overall picture of the interior light. There is also a small number of details made of light wood, in particular, this applies to the wardrobe for tourists and bedside tables.

On the technical side, the room is equipped with a TV, minibar, Internet access, and, if desired, guests can rent a safe.



Family

These rooms are two-room apartments, which are also designed to accommodate a couple of guests. A standard furniture set is installed here, as in one-room standards. However, here it is complemented by a comfortable working area, represented by a desk and a chair made of light wood. Staying in these rooms, guests can use the minibar for free, access to Wi-Fi, and watch satellite channels on TV.

The apartments have a balcony with garden and sea views.

Promo

Promo rooms at Orfeus Queen Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Side) are for two. They are often chosen by couples who plan to spend a romantic trip. It has an excellent double bed with expensive linens and a dense orthopedic mattress, as well as a pair of nightstands for a small number of accessories and clothes. On the wall of the room is equipped with a plasma TV, which broadcasts satellite channels, among which, according to tourists from the CIS countries, there are several Russian speakers.

Rooms of this class are equipped with a French balcony, where you can admire the brightest and most memorable sunsets and sunrises.

Nutrition

A huge number of comments left by tourists is devoted not only to the peculiarities of living in rooms, they also speak about the intricacies of food that is provided in the Orfeus Queen Hotel 4 *. Travelers often mention that the hotel serves Turkish and Mediterranean dishes, served in a large buffet restaurant. They share their impressions from the workshop of the cooks, as well as from the high level of service from the waiters.

In addition to the restaurant, there are four bars in which you can surprise with skillfully prepared cocktails and light snacks for different types of drinks. A lot of attention from vacationers is given to desserts presented on the menu.



Entertainment

Many vacationers admit that most of the time spent on vacation, they were in the aquatic zone, which is located in an open area, near the hotel. It is quite large in size, and is a large sunny terrace with a pool in the middle.

Fans of relaxation and spa treatments have the opportunity to visit the massage room, where for a small fee they can immerse any tourist in a state of complete relaxation. If you want to relax for free, but with benefit, guests of the Orfeus Queen Hotel 4 * often go to the thermal zone, represented by a hammam and sauna.



Travelers who prefer to maintain their health at a high level, even while on vacation, can visit the gym and outdoor sports grounds. It is completely free. On a paid basis, you can play billiards: the cost of such pleasure is about $ 7 per hour.



In the evening, vacationers take part in discos, as well as show programs that are conducted with the participation of an animation team. If desired, hotel guests can gather in a group and book a guided tour of the attractions of Side and Cholakli. According to the reviews of tourists, the cost of such trips is small. Guided tours are conducted by knowledgeable and sociable guides, among whom there are those who speak Russian. According to tourists from Russia, this is a positive point.
