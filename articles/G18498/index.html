What are the features of the PIXI 3 device line? What do users say about their experience in operating the device?

Features of the PIXI 3 3.5-inch Phone

The Alcatel One Touch PIXI 3 phone in a version with a 3.5-inch screen has a length of 112.2 mm, a width of 62 mm, and a thickness of 11.9 mm. The display of the device is capable of functioning at a resolution of 320 by 480 pixels. The screen is made using TFT technology. Belongs to the category of capacitive, can recognize user gestures. The smartphone is controlled by Android Kitkat OS in version 4.4. The device has a 2-core processor, operating at a frequency of 1 GHz. The device supports basic communication standards, including WiFi, Bluetooth. As well as WiFi Direct technology. Internet connection is possible through a 3G channel. The device can be connected to a computer using a USB 2.0 cable.





The Alcatel One Touch PIXI 3 smartphone in this modification is equipped with 2 cameras. The main one has a resolution of 2 megapixels, the front - less, 1.3 megapixels. Using a smartphone, you can record video in 480p resolution at 15 frames per second. The amount of RAM installed in the device is 512 MB. The flash memory resource is 4 GB, there is support for additional microSD modules - up to 32 GB.

Smartphone Alcatel One Touch PIXI 3 in the considered version for a number of world markets is equipped with a battery capacity of 1150 mAh, for the European one - a battery with a resource of 1300 mAh. The indicator corresponding to 1150 mAh can provide stand-alone operation in standby mode for about 381 hours - when the phone is in 2G mode, 286 hours - when the device is activated with an active 3G channel.

Alcatel One Touch PIXI 3 with 4-inch screen

So, we are studying the following modification of the Alcatel One Touch PIXI 3 device - 4-inch. What are its features?





First of all, it is worth noting that the Alcatel One Touch PIXI 3 4-inch is available in 2 versions - 3G and 4G. The dimensions are slightly larger than the previous version of the smartphone. The device is 121.6 mm long, 64.4 mm wide, and 11.6 mm thick. These indicators correspond to the 3G modification of the device. In the 4G version of the device, the dimensions are slightly different: the length of the corresponding phone model is 121.3 mm, the width is 64.2 mm, the thickness is the same, 11.6 mm. But the difference, if you do not take a closer look, you can obviously not notice at all.

The device under consideration, officially referred to as Alcatel One Touch PIXI 3 4013D, like the previous modification of the smartphone from Alcatel, is equipped with a capacitive screen that supports gesture control. The display size is 4 inches, the supported resolution is 800 by 480 pixels. The operating system of the device is the same as that installed in the previous model - Android KitKat OS in version 4.4. In a modification that supports the 3G standard, a 2-core processor is installed. In the 4G version of the device, the chip has 4 cores. Both versions of the smartphone support Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, connecting to a computer via microUSB 2.0 cable.

The phone has 2 cameras - the main one with a resolution of 5 megapixels and frontal at 2 megapixels. Both indicators, therefore, are superior to those of the device in the previous modification. Using the phone in this version, you can shoot good quality video with a resolution of 720p and a decent recording speed of 30 frames per second. At the same time, the 4G-modification of the smartphone can play video in 1080p resolution. The amount of RAM installed in the device is 512 MB, as in the previous version of the phone. The flash memory resource is 4 GB, there is support for external microSD modules within 32 GB.

Depending on the version of the phone produced for a particular market, the battery capacity can be 1300 or 1400 mAh for the 3G version. The battery capacity of the 4G-modification of the device is 1650 mAh. In the first case, the battery will last about 433 hours if the smartphone is used to work in 2G channels, or 325 hours if the device is used on a 3G network. The charging time of a 1300 mAh battery is about 3.3 hours.

What else can be noted among the features of this modification of the Alcatel One Touch PIXI 3 phone? The characteristics of the device in terms of the processor and RAM allow you to run many modern applications and games on it. Some of them can be downloaded using QR codes - their reading is supported by the device and noted by mobile gadget lovers as a very useful feature. The device has a built-in gyroscope, support for FM radio. The user has the ability to control the screen size using gestures of 1 finger.
