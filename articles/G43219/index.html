Malye Berezhki Village in Solnechnogorsk District, Moscow Region: Attractions

Moscow is the most beautiful city in the country; a lot of attractions for every taste are concentrated in it. But, if you are tired of the endless hustle and bustle, look for the opportunity to spend at least a few days in separation from the metropolis, we suggest you go to the village of Berezhki Malye in the Solnechnogorsk district of the Moscow region. At first glance, this is an unremarkable village, but only at first glance. As part of this material, we will talk about those attractions, interesting places that are still worth visiting and seeing with your own eyes. First things first.

Berezhka Manor

The village of Malye Berezhki boasts a rich history, and it became so unattractive and forgotten not so long ago. Now practically no one lives here, there are no vacationers at all. But the streets of the village keep a long history. And here is the first proof - the Vyazemsky estate of the same name. True, only the old park has survived to our days, and that one is on the verge of extinction. Many of those who had the opportunity to visit here do not even suspect that they were on the territory of the once chic estate.





Just a few years ago, one could still find the remains of a foundation reminiscent of a manor, and even that one was dismantled. The construction of the estate dates back to the 19th century, but in historical sources there is no complete and reliable information about it, as well as exactly how it looked.

Beach

The Istra Reservoir is a great place for a summer vacation. But we do not want to pay attention to well-known boarding houses and recreation centers, but a wild beach, which can become a haven for lovers of isolated relaxation. Small Berezhki on the Istra Reservoir is an excellent option. If you have been looking for a quiet, calm and rid of vacationers place for a long time, where you can not only sunbathe, but also swim in warm water, go to the village beach. It can be safely called one of the main attractions. Around it is absolutely wild untouched nature, the view from the riverbank is stunning. There is a complete feeling that they were somewhere in a completely remote place. You will never think that this is the Moscow region.





A great option for a large company with tents and provisions. A quiet and deserted place with a sandy beach and a gentle entry into the water. No entertainment and infrastructure, but this is precisely the main advantage of the place. Be sure to take a walk to the beach if you find yourself in Small Berezhki. By the way, this place has long been chosen by nudists for whom it is so important to be away from condemnation.

Diva Cottage

One of the main attractions of the village is the famous cottage of Alla Pugacheva. Small Berezhki (Solnechnogorsk district) due to its originality, isolation, silence and tranquility, again, picturesque places many years ago were chosen for the construction of a whole suburban estate star stars. Probably, it was then that so much attention was riveted to the small village.





Only 100 meters from the beach mentioned above, and you are near the cottage of Alla Borisovna. If you look at the Small Berezhki (Moscow region) from above, you can see that the cottage is very reminiscent of a noble estate from the works of Turgenev. Over 50 hectares of land, a luxury house, a garden. What else is needed for happiness? When Alla Borisovna moved to the chic Maxim Galkin mansion, her cottage was empty for a long time. But now it is the residence of the grandson of Nikita Presnyakov. It was here that he moved with his wife. To visit Small Berezhki and not to walk along this mansion is a huge crime. Of course, you are unlikely to be able to see something: the mansion is closed to the eyes of the press and onlookers by a tall fence, which in fact does not reduce interest in it.

Nature

Nature is a special value of the village. To find something like this in the suburbs is almost impossible, because huge hectares of forest belt have long been cut down. Small Berezhki are located on the Istra reservoir, the views are amazing. That is why artists and photographers come here so often - away from city noise, you can really immerse yourself in creativity.





The forests here are rich in mushrooms and berries, nature is so virgin that there is a feeling of a dead place, and it is the silence and tranquility inherent in it that modern residents of the metropolis lack so much. Walking through the forest, breathing in the clean fresh air, admiring the beauties of the Istra Reservoir - this is why curious tourists and travelers come here.

How to get there

If you look at the map, the ancient Malye Berezhki is located in the Solnechnogorsk district of the Moscow region, only 50 km from the capital. If you decide to go by car, you should move along Pyatnitsky highway. If there are no traffic jams in just 40 minutes, you will be able to get to the place. But what about all those who do not have their own car? Are they simply not destined to see all the beauty that we have described?





So, the easiest and fastest way is to get to the village by train. According to the schedule, an electric train leaves from the Leningradsky railway station to the Kryukovo stop. Then you should transfer to bus number 23, which will take you to the village of Malye Berezhki.

Another option is to get to the place from the Voykovskaya metro station. From there, bus number 440 regularly leaves, it goes to Solnechnogorsk. Then you should transfer to bus number 27, which will take you to the village. This method is longer, but also has a right to exist.

To summarize

There are so many amazing and really beautiful places in the Moscow Region that it is impossible to tell about all at once. Most of them were discovered by curious tourists and travelers who love to discover new, unknown, to study the history of their native land. If you are tired of all the famous places of interest, you want to visit a place that few know about, feel free to go to the village of Malye Berezhki. Perhaps you will find another attraction that will be your little discovery. And those places that we mentioned in the framework of this material definitely deserve attention.
