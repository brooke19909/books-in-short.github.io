DIY Owl Pillow: Pattern and Pattern

Do you like to decorate your interior? Want to create some fashion accessory? Owl pillow continues to be at the peak of popularity. Let this symbol of wisdom settle on your couch. Make it easy. There are options that even a beginner can handle. They are represented first. Choose any pattern and create beauty for your bedroom or living room.





The simplest owl pillow

This option is made from a regular rectangle or square, that is, you don‚Äôt have to sew a complex three-dimensional shape and turn it from the wrong side to the front side. Even schoolgirls cope with the manufacture of the square when they do the tack.

Without much difficulty, you will get a great owl pillow. With your own hands, the pattern is very simple. The scheme of work is as follows:

  1. Cut from the fabric two rectangles (squares) the size of the future product, taking into account allowances for the seams. One part can be made, but with a fold, if the material allows. In this case, you do not need to sew one edge, since there will be a fold.
  2. Cut decorative elements from shreds of different colors. You will need eyes, beak, wings. All this is easily done without any patterns. Although, if you are not confident in yourself, make paper templates first, and then circle them on the fabric.
  3. When all parts are cut out, attach them with pins or basting thread in the right places on the front of the pillow.
  4. Stitch the parts in a zigzag seam so that the edge does not crumble during operation. You can use a fabric that does not "spill over".
  5. Turn the product inside out. Make side seams. Remember to leave the packing hole open.
  6. Turn the product to the front side. Through the non-sewn portion, fill the ‚Äúpouch‚Äù with soft material. Use sintepon, foam rubber, shreds, special fillers.
  7. Sew the hole manually by pre-fastening the parts with pins.

Rectangular option with applique

Use the following simple idea. You will have a second, but different do-it-yourself owl pillow. The pattern used is the same as in the previous section.

Two rectangular parts are used: for the front part and for turnover. The difference is that here the owl is depicted completely on the surface of the pillow. You can make a whole story composition from several different fun characters. Do as you find it easier. The principle of operation is fully consistent with the above.





Owl pillow with owls

In order to make such an accessory, you will need more strength, patience, skills and materials.

An owl pillow (a pattern for it) is made of paper, a sheet of newspaper, graph paper or any other that is available. Also take the fabric that is at hand. From ordinary chintz or calico can be very funny colorful birds. So, first make a big owl. The sequence of actions will be as follows:

  1. Make a pattern for the half of the pillow detail.
  2. Take the fabric for the base and fold it in half.
  3. Along the fold line, fasten with pins the part made in the first step so that this fold is the center of the future pillow.
  4. Circle the pattern and cut out the part.
  5. In the same way, the back of the pillow is made.
  6. Cut out eyes, wings, beak.
  7. Attach details to the front side of the pillow.
  8. Connect the two halves from the inside and sew along the contour almost closing the seam. Leave a small hole sufficient to unscrew the product and fill it with a padding polyester.
  9. Turn out, stuff.
  10. Manually sew the hole. It is better to do it where it will not be visible, for example, at the bottom of the pillow.
  11. You make little owls according to a similar pattern.
    They can be the same as a large owl pillow (the pattern is proportionally reduced in size) or slightly different in shape (be more elongated or, conversely, made in the form of balls, which are easy to form through the use of a large amount of packing).

Your owl family is ready.

Pillow with pockets

Want to be more original? Use this idea!

A do-it-yourself owl pillow can be sewn with pockets (one or more). TV remotes are usually put there, but they can quite fit and a few owls. The work is performed in the same way as in the previous section. The difference is that at the stage of attaching the decor to the front side of the pillow you need to sew a pocket rectangle, after processing the edges (it is better to bend the upper one). You can divide it into two by making a vertical seam in the middle.

Baby Pillow

To decorate the kid‚Äôs room and prepare interesting entertainment for him, make a bright accessory for him.

The pillow toy owl will definitely please the child. The pattern can be used any of the above. The meaning of the work is to unfasten the wings. They are easy to make with Velcro. In addition, between the layers of fabric from which they will be made, lay rustling material. Buttons that will come unfastened are suitable as eyes. For decorative and educational purposes, sew in a zipper or eyelets with drawstring.

Such a pillow-toy uses fabrics of different textures, and most importantly, bright colors. If you make a few more owls, you can fill them with different fillers, so that the child feels it. In one cushion, put the synthetic winterizer, in the second small balls, in the third pebbles. Such an educational game will be useful and enjoyable for the baby.

Crochet

Such an owl pillow also looks very cute and beautiful. The easiest way is to make an accessory based on a circle.

The sequence of work will be as follows:

  1. Make two large round parts for the front and back of the pillow.
  2. Also do the required number of circles for the eyes.
  3. Ears are performed according to the scheme: pick up the required number of air loops, a connecting column, a lifting column , knit one row with columns with a crochet or without. Reduce the loops in opposite sides of the circle in the same places. This will form the shape of a volumetric triangle.
  4. Perform wings in any way.
  5. Combine all the elements into one. You can do this with a needle or crochet. What is more convenient for you.
  6. The shape of the round cushion can be knitted whole without stitching the parts. This is done as follows: first a circle is knitted, then a reduction is made and several rows are made with the same number of loops (this size corresponds to the thickness of the pillow), and then a second circle is created - also by reducing the number of loops. The pillow will be stuffed through the center of the back side, after which the circle is tied, and the thread is fixed.

Knitted cute accessory will decorate your room and will attract the attention of guests.

You saw that an owl pillow with your own hands is done quite simply. If you have to test your strengths for the first time, choose the simplest option. The needlewomen who can sew can use any idea they like.
