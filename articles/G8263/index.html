Hotel Augustus 3 * (Costa Dorada): description and reviews

If you are planning a summer vacation, then you will definitely enjoy Spain. Here you can choose from a large number of budget and decent hotels located in small towns right on the coast. Since the beginning of May, interest in trips to Europe has significantly increased among our compatriots. Most tourists are actively looking for reviews on the most popular and affordable hotels, so as not to be mistaken and choose the most suitable. Today we will tell you about the wonderful Augustus 3 * hotel complex (Costa Dorada). You will learn about all the nuances of staying at this hotel and you will be able to decide how suitable it is for your relaxation.





Hotel Description

Most Spanish resorts were built up very chaotically, so hotels are not always located in convenient places for vacationers. But the Costa Dorada is a completely different region of Spain, here all hotel complexes were created in strict accordance with the architectural plan. They are slender along the coast, making it easier for tourists to access the sea.

This fact partly ensures the popularity of the Augustus 3 * hotel in Costa Dorada. Of course, it does not belong to the new hotel complexes, but it is quite a worthy option for families with children who prefer a beach holiday to everyone else. The hotel was built in 1971, in recent years it has significantly worn out and dilapidated, so in 2016 a large-scale reconstruction was carried out, reviving it to life. However, tourists note that the rooms on the ground floor have not undergone very significant changes, so you should not settle in them.

Hotel Augustus 3 * (Spain, Costa Dorada) is a charming four-story building, painted in pale blue. It looks great against the backdrop of greenery and white clouds, creating the feeling of a fairytale castle. The large hull has two wings and an elevator, making it easy to climb to the top floor.

The hotel complex has three hundred and twenty-four rooms of several categories. For families with several children, there are large two-room rooms, allowing absolutely everyone to stay comfortably and enjoy their vacation.

Augustus 3 * (Costa Dorada) is a network hotel. It is part of the large Augustus Hotels chain, known throughout the Mediterranean. Nearby is Cesar Augustus 3 * (Costa Daurada), its territory is available to all guests resting in the hotel complex we are describing. Many of our compatriots visited him to swim in the pool or take pictures.





Location

The hotel is located in a quiet resort town of Salou, two kilometers from its center. It belongs to the hotel complexes of the first coastline and has its own access to the beautiful sandy beach. Five kilometers from Augustus 3 * (Costa Dorada) - Cambrils. This is another small city by the sea, which is very easy to reach by public transport. It does not differ in a lot of entertainment, but can diversify the measured resort life of vacationers.

Reus Airport, where most international flights arrive, is only twelve kilometers from the hotel. Therefore, the road will take no more than half an hour in the morning and evening hours. During the day, traffic jams are possible, but they resolve quickly enough.

Infrastructure

European hotels are not distinguished by an abundance of infrastructure and a vast territory, the Augustus 3 * hotel complex in Costa Daurada does not stand out from the general trend. It has a fairly compact area, which fit two pools, sunbeds, umbrellas and a small playground. Just immediately after the pool, the beach begins, so most of the time guests spend there.

The hotel has one restaurant and bar, where you can order not only cocktails, but also a variety of snacks. They are served at any time of the day and have an unusual exquisite taste.

Around the large pool is a sun terrace and sunbeds with umbrellas. Their number is enough to ensure that even with full occupation, there is no hype about empty seats.

If you travel in Spain by car, then you will appreciate the ample parking. But keep in mind that you will have to pay extra for a place on it. It is not included in the price.

Reception, like in many other hotels, is open around the clock. Despite the fact that the tourist catalogs indicate the absence of Russian-speaking staff, in fact, it is available. Therefore, you can easily solve all your problems in your native language. Luggage storage and an exchange office are located at the front desk. Thanks to this, you can not worry about the safety of your belongings and feel calm all the time allotted for rest. If you have a large amount of money or other valuables with you, then you can leave them in the safe. However, it is paid by a separate bill.

For those who wish to go on an excursion, the hotel has a small office that will be happy to find the right tour for you. The cost of such a service will not exceed the average price for excursions in Spain.

Business travelers will appreciate the small but convenient business center where you can send a fax or make copies of documents. Please note that these services are not included in the room rate.

In Augustus Hotel 3 * (Costa Daurada) there is a small solarium. It is intended for tanning on cloudy autumn days, but also works in the summer. Hotel guests can use it for free.

Of particular interest to all potential guests of the hotel complex is Internet access. In the rooms it is wired, and you will need to pay extra for it. But on the territory of the hotel there is Wi-Fi, the signal catches perfectly in restaurants and at the reception.





Infrastructure for children

The hotel administration also thought about its youngest guests. For them, several infrastructure facilities have been built that will not let the fidgets get bored.

Very small children can freely frolic in a separate pool. It has a small size and depth. In addition, the children's pool is located next to the adult, so the kids will always be in sight.

For especially active children, a small sports ground has been built where you can run and frolic without harm to health. All designs are installed taking into account their active use, and their reliability is checked daily by employees of the hotel complex.

In the building of the hotel is a compact but comfortable games room. There, evenings with children are occupied by animators and mini-discos are held.

Entertainment for adult hotel guests

If you do not like to leave the cozy territory of the hotel in the evenings, then you will have to look for entertainment here. And this is not at all difficult, as former guests of the hotel complex say.

The hotel has a pool table, so every evening you can arrange tournaments and competitions for the title of the most experienced player. Every day, after dark, various events are held on the ground floor with the participation of animators and guest artists. Most tourists respond warmly to these shows.

Adults can also take part in darts tournaments or rent a bicycle and arrange for themselves a truly unforgettable adventure. In addition, views along the coast favor such walks.





Hotel Features

If you plan to travel with your pet, then keep in mind that the Augustus Hotel 3 * (Spain, Cambrils) in Costa Dorada does not allow such accommodation in its rooms.

All areas of the hotel, except for special areas, are non-smoking. Violators of this rule will face a very impressive fine. Those who go on a trip with a large company and book more than five rooms can expect a significant discount when processing documents.

Regular guests can receive a compliment from the hotel, presented a discount of fifteen percent.

Number of rooms

Many guests of the hotel compare his rooms with the neighboring Cesar Augustus 3 * (Spain, Costa Dorada). We can say that they are largely identical, even the furniture in the rooms belongs to the same collection. And this is not surprising, because these hotel complexes belong to one large chain.

Most of the rooms are refurbished and are in perfect cleanliness. Guests can choose from the following room categories:

  • Double Room.
  • Twin Room.
  • Triple Room.
  • Quadruple Room.

All rooms are well equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay.





Description of rooms

Each room has a terrace or balcony. They are equipped with light chairs and tables, there is also a clothes dryer, where it is very convenient to hang clothes after sea bathing.

The bathrooms have personal hygiene products, which are replenished daily by maids, and a hairdryer.

They are equipped with air conditioning and heating, satellite TV and a minibar. Family rooms are additionally equipped with refrigerators, which is very convenient for children.

Many rooms have direct or side sea views. The maximum possible accommodation in one room is five people (two adults and three children).

Accommodation with children

Please note that there is no capacity for extra beds in the rooms, therefore, when traveling with children, it is necessary to book rooms with the required number of beds in advance.

The hotel rules stipulate that two children under sixteen years of age using existing beds stay at the hotel for free.





Check In Rules

Paperwork and check-in is carried out from two in the afternoon until half past eleven. If you go to Augustus 3 * to Spain (Costa Dorada, Cambrils), you can be sure that you will be quickly settled even outside the specified period of time. Usually the hotel staff is very quick and friendly.

Departure is carried out until twelve in the afternoon.

Nutrition

The hotel allows its guests to book a room with different food systems:

  • breakfast;
  • half board;
  • full board.

Breakfast in the restaurant is quite varied, they are served on a buffet. But dinner is held in a more solemn atmosphere and at the entrance you must wait for the waiter to guide you to the table. Sometimes the waiting time takes up to half an hour.





Reviews of our compatriots about the hotel complex

The hotel is unequivocally rated by Russians for a solid C grade. It fully meets the expectations of tourists, however, almost every guest can highlight the pros and cons of this hotel complex.

The pluses of most guests include the following points:

  • excellent sound insulation in the rooms;
  • good animation (in Spanish);
  • proximity to the beach;
  • convenient location close to shops, cafes and bus stops;
  • the opportunity to get an early breakfast by appointment;
  • clean and tidy area;
  • Friendly and helpful staff.

Among the minuses are most often noted:

  • poor housekeeping;
  • shabby furniture;
  • tasteless food in lunch boxes;
  • drinks during breakfast and dinner are available at an additional cost.

Often in reviews this hotel is compared with Cesar Augustus 3 * (Costa Dorada, Cambrils). From the comments you can understand that both hotel complexes are on the same level and their main difference is the location to the sea.

Summarizing all of the above, it is safe to say that Augustus 3 * is an excellent choice for those who want an inexpensive and relaxing holiday on the Spanish coast.
