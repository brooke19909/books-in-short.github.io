"White River" - a residential complex in Ufa: description, reviews

In the harsh climate of the Ural Mountains is the largest economic, cultural and scientific center of the state - the city of Ufa. Its population has been steadily increasing over the past eight years and has already exceeded one million people. In this regard, the city is considered promising for developers. This is evidenced by the numerous residential buildings built. Among them is the residential complex "White River" in Ufa.





A bit about the city

Ufa is a dynamically developing huge metropolis, occupying 31st place in terms of population in Europe. It concentrates about two hundred industrial enterprises of the oil refining, chemical, and engineering industries. Due to this, Ufa in 2013 took 7th place in the ranking of the largest industrial centers in Russia. But not only enterprises form the life of Ufa. The city also has many entertainment, educational, cultural, sports and recreational facilities.

Location and transport accessibility

LCD "White River" is being built at the address: Kuznetsovsky Zaton Street, 20. This is not the center of the city, but rather its outskirts, the Kirovsky District. But the distance to the center of Ufa is about 6 kilometers, which can be easily overcome both by personal and public transport. Next to the Belaya Reka residential complex in Ufa, there is a large transport artery - Pugacheva Street, where there are many bus and taxi stops. Travel time to the center takes several minutes.





Description of the residential complex

Residential complex "White River" in Ufa is a single residential building, consisting of three sections of different floors: 25, 19 and 16 floors. It is being erected using a monolithic-frame technology of ceramic brick filled with red brick. In addition, the walls are insulated with environmentally friendly mineral slabs, which are covered by a modern high-tech facade from external damaging effects.

Improvement in apartments involves radiation distribution of heating with the installation of individual heat consumption meters. The developer also provided for increased thermal insulation of the facades, thanks to the use of the building materials described above. Installing energy-efficient double-glazed windows will also help minimize heat loss. Complex developer

Builds LCD "White River" in Ufa "Gosstroy." This is a group of companies, which is a high-tech multidisciplinary construction complex. The holding staff is more than three hundred people. Thanks to its impeccable activities, the company firmly takes third place among the largest developers of the Republic of Bashkortostan and is among the top 200 developers of the country.





In total, the company has built more than ten objects of both residential and commercial real estate. The total building area was approximately 48 thousand square meters. To purchase real estate from this developer is quite simple and affordable, as the company cooperates with ten leading banks of the country, implementing various mortgage programs. So any interested buyer can choose the most suitable and profitable lending program.

Infrastructure: internal and external

Since the Belaya Reka residential complex in Ufa is not even in the center, but not so far from it, its residents will not experience a lack of social infrastructure facilities. Nearby are kindergartens, secondary schools, shops and supermarkets. There are also bank branches, gas stations, a church and a sports complex.

The White River complex bears such a name partly due to its location on the sleeve of the White River. The internal infrastructure of the project of the developer includes the arrangement of a small promenade with recreation areas for children and adults. It is also planned to improve the territory with a landscape park. A great advantage will be the view from each apartment to the river.
