The recreation center "Teremki" (Novosibirsk): characteristics and reviews

In a surprisingly beautiful place, on the shores of the Ob Sea, the recreation center "Teremki" is located. Novosibirsk is an hour and a half away from it, so it is easy to get here both by car and by bus. In the latter case, you need to get to the village of Krasny Yar. Picturesque views of nature, remoteness from big cities and roads, silence and clean air - here there are all conditions for a wonderful holiday in winter and summer. Tourists will enjoy comfortable rooms, homely food in the restaurant, friendly service and plenty of entertainment. Active young people and families with children will be delighted with the rest.

The recreation center "Teremki" in the Karakan forest

Novosibirsk is the center of the region, which is characterized by surprisingly picturesque places. South of the city on the banks of the Ob reservoir is a huge national park - Karakansky pine forest. About 100 thousand hectares of pristine nature with clean air and an abundance of a wide variety of vegetation are suitable for outdoor recreation.





On the border of the Karakan pine forest there is a recreation center "Teremki". Novosibirsk is located not far from it, so the city residents like to relax here. People come here to breathe clean air, walk along forest paths, enjoy peace and tranquility. The year-round recreation center "Teremki" provides guests with a comfortable stay. This is an opportunity to sunbathe on the beach and swim in the Ob Sea, go fishing or go boating. On the territory of the base there are sports fields, green lawns and even barbecue or barbecue areas. In winter, you can ski or skate here.

Accommodation conditions at the camp site

The recreation center "Teremki" (Novosibirsk) provides vacationers with comfortable double and quadruple rooms. Each of them has a separate entrance and a set of necessary furniture for relaxing, cooking and eating. There are separate houses for 3-4 people without amenities. And the rooms in one-story and two-story buildings are equipped with a toilet with shower. Each room also has a fridge, TV, dining table and even dishes. Only 17 buildings of various degrees of capacity are ready to receive guests all year round.





The cost of living at the camp site is from 1200 rubles and above. It all depends on the comfort of the room. The cheapest is accommodation in houses without amenities, which are located on the territory of the base. For an extra fee, you can order maid service, rent sports equipment, board games or fishing equipment, visit the sauna or rent a barbecue area.

Services and leisure

The recreation center "Teremki" (Novosibirsk) has its own promenade with a well-equipped beach. Everyone can rent a boat or sunbathe, swim or go fishing. The large territory of the base is landscaped and well equipped. Occupation to your taste here will find vacationers of any age. What is on the basis of services and entertainment?

  • Free parking.
  • Cozy sandy beach.
  • Boat trips.
  • Hire catamarans and boats.
  • Mangal area.
  • Sauna.
  • Tennis court.
  • Sports grounds.
  • Green lawns for yoga.




Young guests will also not be bored, as there is a large playground with an autodrome for cars and bicycles, an indoor game pavilion, an inflatable trampoline, and various swings. All children are delighted with the mini zoo where you can feed animals - deer and rabbits. Therefore, many families believe that the best vacation spot is Teremki (recreation center, Novosibirsk).

Guest reviews

All who came here note that the picturesque nature and clean air help to relax and take a break from the gray everyday life and city bustle. People like that the camp site is landscaped, comfortable rooms are provided, and the service is top-notch. All the conditions for a comfortable stay and relaxation are provided here. Everyone can find something to their liking here. Corporate parties at the base are also great, they help to unite the team. Many note the high level of service and the friendliness of the staff. Conveniently, there is a store on the territory where you can buy not only products, but also the necessary little things. Therefore, many say that the best option for relaxation is Teremki (recreation center).





Novosibirsk is one of the largest cities in Russia. And its inhabitants need to at least occasionally relax in nature, breathe clean air. The best place for this is the recreation center "Teremki".
