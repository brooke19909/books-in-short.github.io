Hotel "Paradise", Adler: reviews and photos of tourists

One of the inexpensive leisure options is the Paradise Resort Hotel. Adler is not just a beautiful city, but also a unique natural area. Having settled in this hotel, you can quickly reach not only the sea, but also the river.





Brief description of the hotel

If you want to have a good rest on the seashore, then choose the Paradise Hotel for yourself. Adler is a fairly popular tourist destination, and this hotel has already managed to establish itself well. This is a relatively new institution that has been operating since 2006, and in 2008, overhaul and modernization was carried out. Now it is a small cozy hotel, which has a well-groomed green area, and also provides you with direct access to the city beach.

It is worth noting the comfortable and cozy atmosphere of the rooms that the Paradise Hotel offers its guests. Adler will be at your fingertips when you go to the balcony of your apartment. Despite the fact that here you will not find original design solutions (the room decor is very simple), you will have access to all the necessary amenities that will provide you with a good rest.

As for food, you can get it in the cafe that the Paradise Hotel has. Adler has many catering establishments, but this is in no way inferior to them. During the summer, breakfast is included in the price. An √† la carte menu is available throughout the day with the option of ordering international dishes.





Resort Location

Many tourists choose the surroundings of Sochi for their summer holidays. Hotel "Paradise" (Adler) is located in a picturesque park area in close proximity to the sea. Also nearby are many cafes, restaurants, entertainment centers and a large water park. The train station is approximately 5 kilometers from this hotel. And if you want to visit Sochi, then the road will not take you more than 10 minutes.





Resort Hotel Rooms

One of the most beneficial accommodation options in Adler is the Paradise Hotel. Here are the following room categories:

  • Standard room is a small bedroom, designed to accommodate two guests. You can choose between a large or a pair of single beds.
  • Superior standard room is slightly larger in size than the previous category. Also, among other things, a sofa is provided here, which when unfolded acts as an extra bed.
  • Superior rooms differ not only in a significant area, but also in the number of amenities. It is both a bedroom and a living room. A nice addition to this ensemble is its own balcony.
  • Deluxe rooms consist of two rooms. One of them is a bedroom, and the second is a living room. It is worth noting that, taking into account the extra beds, it can accommodate 4 guests. Therefore, this option is often chosen for family vacations.




Amenities in guest rooms

Your vacation in Adler promises to be unforgettable and comfortable . The Paradise Hotel has taken care of the following amenities in the apartments:

  • a large bed or a pair of single beds with comfortable orthopedic mattresses;
  • a huge wardrobe with a mirror, where all your things can easily fit;
  • a TV that is connected to a satellite dish (you can watch about a hundred entertainment and news channels);
  • due to the presence of air conditioning, you will not be bothered by the summer heat;
  • a refrigerator is provided to ensure the safety of your products;
  • the bathroom can have a shower or bathtub (depending on the room category), as well as a hairdryer, a set of towels and disposable cosmetics;
  • all rooms have a wireless internet signal (use of this service is included in the price).




Services for guests of the resort hotel

Hotel "Paradise 3 *" (Adler) offers its guests to use the following list of services:

  • at the reception there is a safe in which each of the guests can rent a cell for storing their valuables;
  • in summer, breakfast is included in the price of the room (in other seasons you can order this option for an additional fee);
  • free wireless internet is available throughout the hotel;
  • the central place in the courtyard of the hotel is occupied by a freshwater pool;
  • if necessary, you can be provided with temporary use of iron and ironing board;
  • if you are afraid to oversleep, departure, excursion, or breakfast, then ask the hotel staff to wake you at the appointed time;
  • during check-in you can get full reference information about the city;
  • the hotel has its own small library, where each guest will find literature to his liking;
  • You can order a transfer to any point you need (note that the tariff for transportation doubles at night);
  • visit to the sauna;
  • paid parking;
  • the ability to deliver dishes from the restaurant to the room;
  • organization of sightseeing tours.




Positive feedback on the Paradise Hotel in Adler

In order not to make a mistake in choosing a hotel, always rely on the opinions of other travelers. So, the Paradise Hotel (Adler) is quite popular . Reviews about this place contain the following positive comments:

  • the hotel is very well located - the beach is very close by, as well as a huge number of entertainment centers and catering establishments;
  • the windows of the rooms offer a beautiful mountain landscape;
  • on the balcony there are armchairs, as well as drying for linen; on each floor in the lobby there is a kettle, iron and ironing board for general use;
  • very good breakfast menu;
  • the staff is very attentive and does its job well;
  • reasonable prices for set meals;
  • workers change bed linen upon request;
  • there are a lot of sockets in the room so that you can turn on not only household appliances, but also charge mobile devices.




Negative reviews

Travelers highlight a number of negative aspects that characterize the hotel "Paradise" (Adler). Reviews of tourists say the following:

  • the interior is very old-fashioned and inharmonious;
  • the rooms are cleaned quite rarely;
  • the territory of the hotel is closed, and therefore, if you leave the city late in the evening, then it is not a fact that you can get back;
  • nearby is a shopping center, which constantly plays loud music (especially this interferes in the evening);
  • strong audibility between numbers;
  • old mattresses with protruding springs;
  • There are no shelves for cosmetics in the bathroom.

General impression

It is worth noting that the Paradise Hotel will be an ideal vacation option for those who want to enjoy the sea and enjoy nature, but do not pay close attention to the interior of their room and the hotel as a whole. Everything here is quite ordinary and not very new. It can be seen that when designing the rooms, the main task was precisely saving, rather than creating a cozy atmosphere. Nevertheless, everything that may be needed for a full stay in the room is available. Also pleases the presence of a balcony with furniture.

Special attention deserves food. Breakfast is prepared very tasty and homely. Also, a comprehensive lunch will cost you quite inexpensively. But you will not be able to have dinner in the dining room (it closes in the evening), and therefore you will have to look for an alternative.

If you like peace and quiet relaxation, then this hotel is most likely not suitable for you. Not only is the audibility between the rooms very strong, but also music from neighboring shopping and entertainment centers is constantly heard. Another source of noise is taxis, which in large numbers accumulate under the windows of the hotel. If this fact does not bother you, then the Paradise Hotel will be an excellent budget option on the Black Sea.
