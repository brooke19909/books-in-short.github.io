How to sew zippers in pants correctly

Many people involved in sewing are interested in the question: how to sew a snake in trousers? Particularly interesting coverage of this topic will be for lovers of products made of inelastic materials. The zipper is beneficial in functional terms, it looks beautiful and neat.



For many of us, going to the studio is far from cheap. You can save enough money if you learn how to sew zippers in pants and learn some other skills. To do this, you do not have to be a professional. Often, beginners cope with such work very quickly. The detailed instructions below provide a step-by-step explanation of how to sew zippers into pants. To do the job correctly and accurately, you must use the following tools:

  • sewing machine;
  • a snake;
  • a ruler;
  • tailor pins and chalk.

List of necessary materials for work:

  • classic pants;
  • fabric for a bounce of a rectangular shape.

Before you begin, you should familiarize yourself with the special terminology. The slope is a strip of fabric designed to protect against getting into the interdental spaces of the lower garment or parts of the body. A fabric is called a cover, which serves to cover the zipper from the outside (it is located differently on men's and women's trousers).

Stage 1. Getting Started

The first step is to prepare the slope. To do this, take a small piece of fabric and draw a rectangle on it using a tailor's chalk. The length of each side will depend on the magnitude of the clearance. The width of the rectangle is calculated by the formula: the width of the gap * 2. The height of the rectangle must be exactly the same as the length of the gap.

Important! The corners of the drawn rectangle must be rounded. Gently cut. Then fold the resulting rectangular piece of fabric along the length so that the wrong side is inside. It is necessary to protect the edges of the cut tissue from shedding. To do this, make a mark. Basting is a special processing of the material (securing a section of fabric with threads). On a simple sewing machine, this is done with a zigzag stitch.

Stage 2. Paving

Take the classic pants face up. Using a tailor's chalk, mark the line where the middle seam will go. If you want to sew a zipper on the trousers for a woman, then draw a parallel midline on the left side. Keep in mind that the parallel line should extend one centimeter closer to the edge of the surveillance. Fold the fabric inward on the wrong side and process it (overlay with a zigzag seam).



Stage 3. We fix the snake

At the end of the third stage, a small allowance was formed. The snake needs to be placed under it so that its teeth are visible, and secure it with tailor's pins.



Stage 4

Place a slope under the snake. To do this correctly, you need to compare the outer edges of the bounce with a zipper until they completely coincide. Match the waistline with the top edge of the bend. Sew with a zigzag seam. Stitch (stitching is the process of joining two or more parts of different sizes).

Stage 5

Fold the trousers so that the midlines coincide. The right side of the tape should be sewn to the right side. Please note that this applies to trousers for women. In men, everything is done the other way around.

Step 6. Edging

From the wrong side, process the edges of the gap, to do this, use a single line. Mark the line on the front side. It will serve as a guide for the finishing line.





Stage 7. Completion

Align the finishing line. For greater durability, place a small zigzag seam below.
