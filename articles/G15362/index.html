Hotel Four Seasons 5 * (Limassol, Cyprus): description, services, reviews

Cyprus is a place where many people come to relax and enjoy the local nature. There are a huge number of hotels and guest houses. But one of the best is Four Seasons 5 * (Limassol). Cyprus annually receives tens of thousands of tourists, and many choose this particular hotel as a temporary residence. So empty numbers here are rare. So, it is worth telling about all the advantages of this hotel.





Location

One of the main criteria that potential tourists are guided by when choosing a hotel is their location. The vast majority of people go to Cyprus to devote their entire vacation time to beach vacations. And the ideal option therefore will be Four Seasons 5 * (Limassol, Cyprus). This complex is not so close to the sea - it is located on a private sandy beach, which is awarded the Blue Flag Award. And it is very prestigious. Since this prize is awarded by the European Community for safety and cleanliness. By the way, this exclusive and independent complex is owned by Muskita Hotels Ltd.

True, it is far from the airport. From it to the hotel by car about 50 minutes. But there is a definite plus. Because taking off and landing planes will not interfere with silence and solitude. But from the center of Limassol, the complex is only 8 kilometers away.

It should also be noted that in 2009 Four Seasons Limassol 5 * became the first hotel in all of Cyprus to be accredited by Investors in People.

Restaurants

This hotel complex has several luxurious and exclusive establishments where you can enjoy excellent cuisine.

The first is Vivaldi. Italian restaurant with excellent cuisine and a private lounge. In it, guests can reserve a table for 6-16 people. Interestingly, it was recognized as the best hotel restaurant in Cyprus in 2009 and 2010.

The next establishment is Mavrommatis. This gastronomic restaurant was opened with the assistance of the famous Parisian institution of the same name. Here is a special kitchen. It offers classic Greek cuisine cooked in the French style. This is also the best hotel restaurant. It was recognized as such in 2008.

There is also an outdoor restaurant called Mavrommatis & Vivaldi Al Fresco. It is located on the terrace with stunning views of the sea and the pool. This institution works from May to October. Interestingly, this restaurant offers a separate menu for people who love seafood and fish.

If you want to try ethnic cuisine, then you should definitely visit Seasons Oriental. Caf√© Tropical is worth a visit to people who value comfort and atmosphere most of all. This restaurant is surrounded by tropics and waterfalls. It is quiet and calm, which cannot but rejoice. This establishment serves a buffet breakfast, as well as a variety of lunches and dinners. A children's and vegetarian menu is also available.





Bars

Four Seasons Hotel has many top-class establishments where you can enjoy a variety of delights. In addition to several luxurious restaurants, there are also numerous bars. The main establishment is Vista Bar & Terrace. The best cocktail bar of the complex with a lively, but at the same time intimate atmosphere. Many Four Seasons Hotel guests love to spend time here, because it is here where live music plays in the evenings.

Splash Bar is located by the main pool. This establishment is open all day. There you can try not only soft drinks, but also ice cream and light snacks. The Garden Pool Bar, which is located in the garden, serves the same thing, only people over 18 years old are allowed to go there.

Beach Cafe Bar is located close to the beach. There is also an area with tables. And the last of the institutions is called Garden Court & Bar. It is located on the ground floor of the Atrium. Open from 16:00 daily. There you can comfortably accommodate and order something from a wide range. Also in this bar there are 3 LCD TVs, sockets for laptops and chairs. This place is ideal not only for relaxation, but also for those people who like to work at their computer in a comfortable environment.

Other establishments

In addition to all of the above, Four Seasons Hotel 5 * (Cyprus) has a place called Colors Cafe. This is a cafe, and a bar, and a pastry shop. It is simple, but nonetheless stylish. Therefore, many here like it.

It is also worth noting that the fact that music shows are regularly held near the pool and lagoon. Visitors are expected not only a welcome drink, but also a buffet with fantastic fireworks at the end of the event.

And, of course, while on vacation in Four Seasons Limassol 5 *, you should definitely visit Shiseido SPA. This complex is one of six Shiseido Spas worldwide. And, accordingly, the only one in Cyprus and throughout the Eastern Mediterranean. Many tourists come here just to visit the spa. This center is equipped with luxurious rooms where people can treat themselves to various anti-aging and wellness treatments. Also there is a zone for relaxation and rest. Everything in this SPA center is designed in accordance with the well-known Shiseido Qi philosophy. Interestingly, thalassotherapy procedures are still available here.

Entertainment

Four Seasons 5 * tours sell out very quickly. This hotel is popular and you can see why. After all, there is everything that can come in handy for a positive pastime and comfortable rest.

There are several pools. Two of them are open. Only one is ordinary and the other with a jacuzzi. The third, respectively, is closed, with the function of heating the water. There is a free sauna, steam room, jacuzzi, two floodlit tennis courts. In addition, there is a billiard room, slot machines, table tennis, gym, fitness center, aerobics room. And from March to November you can do some kind of water sport. Needless to say, even aquafit is here. So everyone will find entertainment to their liking.

But the most important entertainment for many is the beach. He is just perfect here. Largely because this beach is sandy and is intended exclusively for visiting by guests. There are sun loungers, umbrellas and towels - all for free.

Many people come to Cyprus as families. Some even take their children with them. In fact, the complex has everything that only the smallest guests need. Children's pool with a water slide, a kindergarten with teachers (kids from three to eight years old are accepted there), a club with animators (for children from 7 to 13 years old). There are many other services. For example, an entertainment center, high chairs in restaurants, a nanny (on request), baby bathrobes.
