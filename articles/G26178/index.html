How to get to the Luzhniki sports complex? Metro is the most convenient way

The largest Russian stadium, of course, is the Luzhniki sports complex. On a vast territory of 153 hectares, there is a whole city with a large and small sports arenas, a sports palace, a swimming pool, football fields, tennis courts, athletics complexes, a hotel, cafes, restaurants, a health care center, etc.

All these objects form a harmonious architectural and park ensemble. The construction of such a grandiose project was completed in record time in 1956. Since then, the sports complex has become an arena for the most prestigious sports and cultural events.
