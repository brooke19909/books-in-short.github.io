Twin Towers, September 11 tragedy

The terrible tragedy that occurred on September 11, 2001, claimed the lives of a huge number of people. 2973 people were killed, and this, you see, is a significant figure.

The terrorist attack was preceded by the capture of four aircraft bound for California and the eastern United States. The aircraft tanks were full, so we can say that they turned into guided missiles.

What happened on this terrible day? On September 11, the twin towers collapsed.





At 8:45, one of the aircraft crashed into the North Tower - the Boeing 767. 92 people were on board (11 crew members, 5 terrorists and 76 passengers). The plane crashed between the 93rd and 99th floors. The fuel that burned in the tank rushed a pillar of fire down the elevator shaft, killing even those people who were in the lobby. At 10:29 a burning building collapsed, burying a huge number of people with it. The number of the aircraft crashing into the twin towers is AA11.

At 9:03 a plane also crashed into the South Tower, it was the second Boeing 767. The blow fell between the 77th and 81st floors. There were 65 people on board (5 terrorists, 9 crew members and 54 passengers). At 9:59 local time, the burning building collapsed. The aircraft number is UA175.

There were two more planes. One of them struck at the Pentagon, it happened at 9:40. Killed 184 people. And the latter fell in a Pennsylvania forest, near Pittsburgh.
