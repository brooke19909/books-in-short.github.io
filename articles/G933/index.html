Sports and rifle complex and recreation center "Fox hole" in the suburbs: address, reviews

Most often, the speed of life in a metropolis does not allow you to get out into the countryside. After a hard working week, which does not always go smoothly, I want to lie down on the sofa, grab a sandwich in my hand and just relax. However, the change of scenery, according to psychologists, is a much more positive factor that allows you to forget about the problems. And a great place where you can relax your soul and body, is considered a recreation center "Fox hole".

Base location

Only forty kilometers from Moscow is the sports and shooting complex "Fox hole". This is not yet another residence of Santa Claus, as the inventors of pathos advertising love to imagine suburban vacations. This is a playground where you can really spend time with your family. The road to the base, excluding traffic jams, will take about two hours. During this time, children will not have time to get tired. At the weekend, it will take longer to travel. You need to keep this in mind when calculating the route. You can get in several ways: along Dmitrovsky, Yaroslavsky or Ostashkovsky highway. First you need to come to Dmitrovsky district. The complex is located in the village of Ignatovo (north direction from Moscow). By the way, not far from it is the Ikshinskoe reservoir. For lovers of fishing, this is another reason to choose a vacation in the countryside.

Housing stock

"Fox hole" in the suburbs has a unique number of rooms. New guests of the Boomerang Hotel and Fox Inn are always welcome on site. The arrangement of rooms is indicated in such a way that guests can choose the most convenient accommodation option.

Hotel complexes allow guests to fully plunge into the atmosphere of suburban life. The hotels in the Fox hole in Ignatovo are designed as cottages, and each of them has an individual exit to the street. It offers - rooms of various categories: luxury, junior suite, comfort, studio. If your family is large, friendly and noisy, the best choice is a separate wooden cottage "standard" or "comfort".

Shooting complex

The visiting card of the recreation center "Fox hole" is a shooting complex. You can choose both shooting at the shooting range, and at targets. The place is designed not only for professionals. Beginners or just amateurs will also find a lot of interesting things for themselves.

Complexes for shooting occupy a vast territory of four thousand square meters. For fans of shooting outdoors, there are outdoor areas with all the necessary equipment. Those who are distracted by foreign street objects and feel better in a special room can choose indoor areas.

You can shoot at the dash from several types of weapons. At your service pistols, sports rifles, airguns. Hunters can test rifled weapons here. You can choose any distance from 100 to 300 meters. There are imitation targets such as hares, ducks, pheasants or pigeons.

If you don‚Äôt know how to shoot, but always wanted to try or take a couple of lessons, a group of experienced instructors will come to your aid in the Fox Hole in the Moscow Region. Do not worry. If you have never held a weapon in your hands, you will only take your first steps in shooting with the special Scatt shooting simulator.

An interesting fact is that a youthful shooting section operates on the territory of the club. Accept children from 12 years. Graduates of the school repeatedly became winners in various shooting competitions. The complex holds annual competitions in various types of shooting, not only at the local level, but also at the European level.

For regular customers organized a pleasant system of discounts and various privileges. For example, club members can now shoot on Wednesday and Thursday.

The price list in the Fox hole is practically no different from the prices in similar Moscow establishments. On average, renting a shooting place for an hour will cost a thousand rubles a week, and two thousand on weekends.

Summer fun

On the territory of the Fox Hole in the Moscow Region there is something to keep yourself busy in the summer, if you are not fond of shooting. Near the complex is located the picturesque lake Iksha. Fishing enthusiasts will appreciate fishing on its shores. Lovers of outdoor activities will enjoy water skiing on scooters and water skiing. Lack of equipment is not a problem: it can always be rented on site. Children also easily find something to do. The beaches are equipped with everything you need to have fun with your friends. Water slides, table and tennis, a swimming pool will not leave indifferent even the smallest visitors.

What to do in winter

"Fox hole" in the suburbs works all year round. And winter is always the busiest time. During this period, hunters prepare for the new season, training and improving their skills.

If you decide to relax outside the city in winter and come with your family, the management of the complex will offer ski trips for short distances to enjoy the beauty of the winter forest, as well as the ice rink. Winter fishing, snowmobiling and sleigh rides are organized. After an active day in nature, you can relax in the spa or Russian bath. It is important here to warn staff in advance about the desire to rent a room.

Additional services

The complex in the Moscow region "Fox hole" on the Dmitrov highway is one of the best. The base is equipped with the latest technology. The latest throwing and small arms are equipped with computer support. An arms workshop operates in the territory, in which experienced specialists work. They carry out not only the maintenance of weapons, but also make complex repairs, if necessary. In addition, Fox Hole is always happy to provide weapons and ammunition storage services.

Reviews from the guests of the Fox hole are only positive. Beautiful ladies appreciate the spa area, where from the wide range of services you can choose exactly what suits you. Families with children report that they can leave their children without fear under the supervision of attentive animators who will amuse the children and give parents several hours of personal time.

As an additional service, the center runs a cynological club, where you can leave your pet with a quiet soul. In the evening, you can spend a beautiful dinner in the restaurant, play bowling, billiards, test your abilities in karaoke. In the "Fox hole" in the suburbs you can conduct various business events, corporate parties and banquets. There are magnificent banquet rooms for this.
