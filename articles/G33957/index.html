Briefly about the hotel

This complex was built exactly 35 years ago. Despite this age, everything inside looks modern and new. Which is not surprising. After all, a complete reconstruction was carried out 7 years ago, and cosmetic repairs are carried out regularly.

This hotel is ideal for people who want to both get acquainted with the local culture and enjoy a beach holiday. After all, Dubai is located on the Arabian Peninsula, washed by the Persian Gulf. And Hotel Ja Jebel Ali Beach Hotel is located at all on its own private beach, the length of which is about one kilometer. So there is enough space for everyone.

And the airport is pretty close - about 40 minutes from the hotel. Also nearby is the golf resort and SPA Jebel Ali. Other interesting sights to drive. They are within 10-20 kilometers of the hotel.

Transfer

I would also like to dwell on this service. Hotel Ja Jebel Ali Beach Hotel provides its guests with a free shuttle service to the shopping centers of the capital. But that is not all. They are also welcomed at the airport - right after the guests receive their luggage, they should start looking for representatives of the hotel with a sign that says its name.

If the transfer is ordered not from the Dubai airport, but from the one located in Abu Dhabi, then tourists need to go to the sixth exit from the lobby and look for a driver there. Because there are no hotel representatives at Abu Dhabi International Airport. A transfer from Sharjah Airport is also possible .

They take guests to the hotel not for free. The price depends on which airport they arrive at. The minimum amount is 200 dirhams (3,400 rubles), and the maximum is 475 (about 8,070 rubles).
