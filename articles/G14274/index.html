Profitable profession in Russia: review, payment level, tips and reviews

Ideal work should bring moral satisfaction. But even for the most interesting business a person undertakes, first of all, for reasons of material gain. Therefore, it is important to know in which particular industries you can count on a decent level of wages. What are the most profitable and prestigious professions in Russia?

Top manager

One of the most profitable professions in Russia and in the world is a top manager. Top management in large companies and commercial banks can receive up to $ 2 million per year. But do not think that they receive money for "beautiful eyes." In addition to specialized education, a top manager must have all kinds of international certificates, know several foreign languages, and most importantly, have a solid track record in the industry (in particular, in senior positions).

If you intend to become a top manager in the future, keep in mind that you will not belong to yourself. You will have to regularly attend negotiations, conclude contracts, plan, analyze, and solve some current issues. You will have a minimum of time for personal life, hobbies and just relaxation.

Internal Communications Manager

An internal communications manager is a fairly new, but very profitable profession in Russia. The average salary varies from 100 to 250 thousand rubles. Such specialists are vital in large enterprises. The manager establishes communication between managers and subordinates, develops and maintains a corporate style, and also creates a comfortable working environment.

The work is very difficult, but terribly interesting. Since the manager has to work with staff, he must perfectly master the legislative base, audit standards and other information on this issue. Studying foreign experience is also welcome.

Accountant

For many decades, the accountant continues to be one of the most profitable professions in Russia. But the level of wages depends on both the degree of professionalism and the place of work (or rather, on the financial capabilities of the company). At large enterprises, a good specialist can receive about 350 thousand rubles. per month.

Over the past few years, the requirements for accountants have significantly tightened. To qualify for a position in a prestigious company, you need to have a higher economic education, know the legislation in the field of taxes and fees, be able to keep records perfectly, and so on. Well, of course, there should be a decent length of service.

IT specialist

Given the relentless growth in the level of computerization, it is easy to assume that one of the most profitable professions in Russia is an IT specialist. There are a lot of computer scientists in the country, who on average earn about 60 thousand rubles. per month. But the salaries of true professionals are calculated in much larger numbers.

To become a good IT specialist, it‚Äôs important not just to be proficient in programming languages. It is also necessary to choose one or several priority sectors and deeply study the specifics of their work. A good specialist may not be attached to a specific enterprise, but work in a freelance format.

Auditor

Answering the question about which professions in Russia are profitable, one cannot ignore the profession of an auditor. The average salary of such a specialist is 35-40 thousand rubles. But real professionals (which are few in the country) can earn up to 200 thousand rubles. per month. The final figure depends on the prestige of the audit company, as well as on the volume of documents that a specialist has to work with.

Some large companies prefer to have their own full-time auditor. It should not only conduct regular audits, but also constantly streamline and improve the workflow. This helps identify bottlenecks and minimize financial loss.

Collector

Interestingly, one of the most profitable professions for men in Russia is a collector. The economic crisis has made adjustments to the solvency of the population. People began to borrow more, but there are often problems with the return of funds. In this regard, banks resort to the services of collection firms. The salary of such a specialist directly depends on the amount of debts returned. The most initiative and professional specialists earn up to 400 thousand rubles. per month.

Beauty master

Some of the most profitable professions for girls in Russia are related to the beauty industry. Manicure, hair removal, cosmetology, tattooing, visage - all this and much more brings about 150 thousand rubles to popular masters. per month. The beauty master does not have a fixed salary. Earnings depend on the level of professionalism (from which the cost of services varies greatly) and the workload of a specialist. And most importantly - you can grow to the scale of your own business, which will significantly increase profits.

To become a beauty master, you need creative talent and a great desire. You should start by taking courses on your chosen profile. At first, earnings will be small, but as your client base grows and knowledge and skills improve, your well-being will grow. Keep in mind that the beauty industry does not differ in constancy; something new always appears in it. Therefore, you will have to invest in vocational training.

Brand manager

Another of the most profitable professions in Russia for girls is a brand manager. Of course, men are also open to the industry, but who else but a woman can find subtle leverage that allows you to control the minds of the buyer? The job is to create the face of the product so that it is recognizable. So that out of hundreds of items, for example, shampoos, the buyer prefers yours. Depending on the prestige of the brand with which the manager will collaborate, as well as on the level of his creativity, wages can amount to hundreds of thousands of rubles per month.

Personal driver

In the list of the most profitable professions for men in Russia, the profession of a personal driver occupies a worthy place. Working for a businessman or a politician, you can earn about 150-200 thousand rubles. per month, drive a premium car and wear an expensive suit. It would seem perfect work. But it is not as simple as it seems at first glance.

To be a personal driver of an influential person, it‚Äôs not enough just to drive well. You need to have a presentable appearance, and also be prepared for the fact that you have to adapt to the boss's schedule. In fact, you will not belong to yourself. In addition, you will become an involuntary owner of some confidential information, because of which you will be the object of persecution of competitors and journalists. In some cases, good physical preparation is required so that, if necessary, the driver can fulfill the function of a bodyguard.

Chef

Of the highly profitable professions in Russia, the chef can be noted. Among wealthy people there are many gourmets who are not averse to refreshment in an expensive restaurant. Chefs of such establishments can receive 200-300 thousand rubles a month or more. Of course, profile education is important, but the most important thing is talent and creativity. To become a chef of an elite restaurant, you need to have your own flavor. Well, of course, you have to start with a simple cook. A huge advantage will be the availability of work experience abroad.

Civil Aviation Pilot

Pilot is a romantic and at the same time very difficult profession. Air transportation is associated with a huge responsibility for the life and health of passengers, as well as with great risk. Naturally, in order to motivate a person to go into this profession, the salary must be appropriate. For example, Aeroflot pilots can be cited. With a flight of 90 hours or more, the salary is about 350 thousand rubles. per month.

Dream Job Search Tips

  • Get higher education in the field you are interested in. Defending a dissertation will be an added advantage.
  • Constantly replenish knowledge. Be sure to attend courses, seminars and trainings to keep abreast of the latest trends.

  • Do not strive to achieve everything at once. To get a high-paying position, you have to start from the bottom. But this is an invaluable experience.
  • Respond to vacancies. Even if you don‚Äôt fit in any parameters, feel free to submit your resume. Perhaps you will have a chance to prove yourself in an interview.

What to expect in the future?

Young people who dream of career heights and material well-being should think ahead. It is important not only to understand what are the most profitable professions in Russia now, but also what the labor market will look like in five to ten years. Judging by the reviews of analysts, young people should pay attention to such professions:

  • Market Analyst. With a tendency to increase competition, as well as expanding the range of products and services, this profession will not lose its relevance in the near future.
  • Business consultant. More and more people are eager to start their own business. They need to be taught all the intricacies, modern techniques and pitfalls of this difficult task.
  • Lawyer. This profession is relevant at all times, because the level of legal literacy of the population leaves much to be desired. People will always need someone who can advise and represent their legitimate interests.
  • Logistician. Transportation costs account for a significant share in the cost of production. It is important that someone can minimize this figure.
  • HR specialist. Regardless of how market conditions change, enterprises will always need high-quality personnel.
  • Dentist. This is a profession for all time. It is the dentists who are considered the most wealthy among physicians.
  • Scientist. Physicists, biologists, mathematicians, chemists - it is on such people that technological progress rests.
  • Show Business. Actors, singers, television presenters and other blue screen stars are considered one of the most wealthy people in the country. Naturally, achieving success in this area is something like Russian roulette. Talent alone is not enough.
