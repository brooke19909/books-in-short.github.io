The recreation center "Oka" (Ryazan region): description and reviews

The recreation center "Oka" (Ryazan region) has a convenient location, because it is only 35 km from the city of Ryazan. Around the campsite are picturesque places, and it offers a beautiful view of the river of the same name. And so that the rest was also comfortable, all conditions were created on the basis of Oka.

Accommodation





The recreation center "Oka" (Ryazan region) is a whole complex that consists of a hotel building and several cozy houses that stand separately on the territory.

A total of 14 hotel rooms are available, 12 of which are double rooms (the rest are single and triple). In total, 28 people can stay at the hotel. The double rooms have one and a half and double beds, bedside tables, a wardrobe, a table and chairs. They also have a small fridge and TV. Each has a separate bathroom with a shower.

In summer houses there is also everything necessary for comfortable living and an internal telephone is provided for communication with the administrator. In total, they are designed for 12 people.

All year round you can stay in the hotel rooms. The houses are intended only for summer holidays. The price depends on the time of year, but at the end of 2016 - the beginning of 2017, the minimum cost of living per day was 1,500 rubles.

Service and entertainment





The recreation center "Oka" in Ivanchino (Ryazan region) has everything you need for a comfortable rest a small company and holding public events. In the camp site you can:

  • in winter skiing, snowboarding, sledding and tubing, there is even a ski lift;
  • have picnics at any time of the year in the surrounding area;
  • play badminton, tennis or volleyball on specially equipped venues;
  • play paintball;
  • children can play in the area equipped for them with swings, horizontal bars and toys;
  • grill kebabs on the grill;
  • take a bath in the bathhouse;
  • go fishing;
  • relax on the sandy beach;
  • enjoy loneliness in numerous cozy corners on the territory of the base;
  • to visit the neighboring villages of Konstantinovo and Poshchupovo with a guided tour.

All equipment necessary for outdoor activities can be rented at the recreation center. For those who have arrived on their own vehicles, parking is provided. Wi-Fi internet is available throughout.

Nutrition





The price includes breakfast. In addition, a cozy cafe and a restaurant with banquet rooms are open on the camp site. For self-cooking, a separate small kitchen with all necessary kitchen equipment is provided.

Reviews





Despite the advantages of the Oka recreation center (Ryazan Oblast), the reviews of vacationers over the past few years about it are mostly unflattering. Among the unpleasant discoveries, guests note the following:

  • On the territory of the base you can not only rent winter equipment for outdoor activities. There are instructors, snowboarders. It would seem that this is a plus, but in fact - a minus. The lesson registration scheme works poorly, and the instructors themselves are not able to organize classes.
  • Throughout the Wi-Fi catches poorly, even in the rooms are not always there.
  • The menu in the cafe is not a great choice, the quality of the dishes is not up to par.
  • Most rooms require redecorating.
  • The rooms are a lot of dust and dirt, clean poorly. They can settle in non-tidy rooms.
  • Attendants are not always welcoming. You can face outright rudeness.
  • Since the recreation center is located on the banks of the Oka River and such types of recreation as fishing and relaxing on the beach are available, a welcoming atmosphere was expected. In fact, it turned out that the path leading to the coast is a clay path, which is washed away from the rains. The descent is very steep. There is no lighting, as are landmarks. After dusk, you can easily get lost.

Despite such significant comments, there are those who believe that the Oka recreation center (Ryazan Oblast) is a good place to relax in nature at a budget price. And all thanks to the special location and the surrounding nature. Also, a large number of guests were satisfied with a wood-fired sauna with a large pool, the presence of covered gazebos with barbecue. Many were impressed by the winter activities, especially skiing and tubing, as well as dog sledding.

It is hoped that if there is an external gloss, the hostel will raise the level of service to what is declared, and will become a truly amazing vacation spot without any complaints.

Location





The address where the recreation center "Oka" is located: Ivanchino village, Ryazan region. It is seven kilometers east of the village of Konstantinovo - the birthplace of Sergei Yesenin. To get there, you need to turn off the Novoryazanovsky highway towards the village. There are signs on the way.
