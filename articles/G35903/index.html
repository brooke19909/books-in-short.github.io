Active investor. How you can earn fast and a lot

Currently, income can be obtained in different ways. There are, for example, traditional earning options, such as finding a job or starting your own business. Or you can arrange for passive income. This alternative earning channel is called investing.

Who is an active investor? I must say that this is primarily a person who is constantly in search of new opportunities for himself to invest money to make subsequent profits. Moreover, his goal is not ephemeral profit in the very distant future, which his investments will be able to give, but earnings here and now.

An investor is a subject of investment activity, investing their own, borrowed or borrowed money for profit. He can be a natural person, legal entity, including a foreign one, less often - a state authority, an international organization. Capital is most often invested in objects of entrepreneurial activity - companies, startups, hedge funds, real estate, works of art, securities, precious metals and so on.

Basic concepts

Terms of investments, as a rule, range from a couple of minutes to several weeks. Against this background, it is not important whether the company is good or bad, whose shares are acquired. Active investors predict the movement of value over a certain time interval (it doesn‚Äôt matter which side up or down), and only make a deal based on this forecast. If successful, they make a profit, sometimes very significant. It can reach twenty percent a week.

Active investors are also called traders. Active investors or traders are constantly in front of the computer, watching quotes, studying statistics and important financial news. In fact, this is their job. The more profitable transactions they make, the higher income they have. Some individuals trade on several platforms at once, and taking into account the time difference, it turns out that they track quotes twelve hours a day. But this, of course, is an extreme case. Typically, traders trade eight to nine hours a day.

Active investing

From the point of view of active investors, it is necessary to look for entry points to markets that have significant growth potential in short periods of time. Of course, it is impossible to catch all the good points. But even if you take only twenty percent of all possible transactions, for the year comes the profit in the region of four hundred percent.

What should be chosen: active or passive type of investment?

In that case, if you pay attention to large business, it is clear that financial corporations with banks and management companies are not actively involved in investing. Any of their investments are aimed at long terms. Their business is constantly evolving and expanding regularly. Next, we find out what are the differences between the process of active and passive investment.

Active and passive investor: the difference

First of all, the difference lies in time costs. And they are not in favor of active investors, who are forced to constantly look for opportunities to make profitable investments. Their profitability directly depends on this. There are no deals, which means there is no income. Passive investors are freed completely from this. Money works for them, and not vice versa.

The next difference is the safety of funds. The factor of maximum portfolio diversification enables passive investors to sleep peacefully. When investing at long intervals, the risk of getting to minus is almost zero. For traders, everything is exactly the opposite. They risk their investments in every transaction. And in the event that the principles of money management are not used, then the danger of losing the lion's share of active investment is commonplace.

The main advantage of passive investors is constant profit. Let it be small, but stable. This is what active investors cannot earn, who earn thirty percent of profit in one period, and who subsequently don‚Äôt get a penny or even go minus. And they can do serious losses for several months in a row.

Thus, on the one hand, the process of active investment provides investors with a lot of different opportunities for successful investment. Using a short-term trend, you can earn good money. In this regard, theoretically active traders should receive greater profitability compared to passive counterparts. Next, let's talk about where to invest in order to earn.
