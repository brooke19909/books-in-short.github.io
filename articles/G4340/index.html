Hotel Aladdin 4 (Hurghada / Egypt)

The hotel was built in 1995 and is renovated annually. The pension has a rather large area, on which cozy one-story villas and the main two-story building are concentrated.



Near the hotel there is a paid water park "Titanic" with water slides and pools, nearby are shopping and entertainment centers, a bazaar and excellent restaurants. The services of neighboring hotels are at the complete disposal of tourists: Aladdin-Beach Resort, Jasmin Village and Ali-Baba Palace. At Aladdin 4 you will find a multifaceted form of leisure, oriented to a completely different category of guests.

This is one of the few four-star hotels in Hurghada that offers all-inclusive holidays. Stunning conditions for beach, youth, calm and active holidays are created for tourists in this paradise. Due to the wide range of services, the guesthouse can be recommended to both young and old people. Couples with children will be delighted with the children's entertainment program.

Types of rooms: 420 rooms with spacious terraces are in the Aladdin 4 hotel. Reviews from guests are mostly positive. Many praised the nice design of the rooms, others talked about cleanliness, while others liked the furniture. For especially demanding customers, there are chic two-bedroom luxury apartments.



Every day, the maids do wet cleaning, and replacing the bed - according to the schedule or at the request of the guests. Standard rooms are equipped with a bathroom with a set of toiletries, split-system, satellite TV, a bar with drinks (filling for a fee), a safe, a compact refrigerator. You can always contact the administration by phone, which is installed in each room.

Meals: At the Aladdin 4 you will find a full three meals a day. International and local dishes are served on the tables. If you prefer Italian cuisine, then visit the Oasis restaurant, where real paella is prepared. You can enjoy delicious cocktails in the hotel bars, of which there are as many as five.

Beach (own): Just 100 m from the hotel there is a clean well-kept beach. On the coast there is a water entertainment center and a Genie's grill bar. Every day, guests can do water aerobics with a professional trainer right at sea. At free disposal: sun loungers, awnings, towels and air mattresses.



Additional information: An outdoor pool, a small zoo, a good playground and a development club have been built for little fidgets. High chairs and a cot are provided for children. Your children will be delighted with the animation program and entertainment.

Adults can not only enjoy the rays of the Egyptian sun, but also spend time cheerfully and actively, playing tennis or billiards. In the evening, interesting performances, contests and a disco are organized on the territory - no one will be bored here.

Those who have arrived on business matters can use the conference hall for 100 people and free high-speed Internet at any time. To maintain your health, there is a gym and massage room. For entertainment: Jacuzzi, outdoor pool, minigolf, squash, basketball.

Digest: High-quality service, serenity, vivid emotions - all this you will find in the Aladdin 4 tourist center. Hurghada is a tourism center where you can fully relax on the seashore, gain vitality and inspiration. This hotel has everything you need for an unforgettable, fun and interesting pastime.

This is one of the most visited, prestigious and comfortable hotels in Egypt, where tourists come from all over the world. The main contingent is the Germans. A lot of couples with young children, newlyweds and adults. All customers were satisfied with the food, comfortable rooms, friendly attitude and, of course, the clean coast.
