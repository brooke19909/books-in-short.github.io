Kabardinka, "Pochtovik" (boarding house): address, description of rooms, recreation, sea, reviews

One of the most popular resort places in Russia is the village of Kabardinka. "Pochtovik" is a boarding house located in its very center. It is not too expensive and at the same time a tourist complex that is well-known among tourists. This is a union resort. It works in season.

The earliest month of holidaymakers' arrival is June, and the late holidaymakers leave in October. The boarding house is loved by both large companies of cheerful youth, and parents with children. Moreover, kids are accepted here from the age of two. You can come here in an organized way, with a ticket from a travel agency. It is also possible to book a room on your own by calling directly to the boarding house. You only need to make an advance payment.





Kabardinka

"Pochtovik" is a boarding house located fifteen kilometers from the famous resort of Gelendzhik. The village is located on the shore of the Tsemess Bay of the Black Sea. It is surrounded on almost all sides by mountains. Located in a green valley, the resort is protected from the winds by the Marhotsky, Doobsky and Tuaphat ranges. Therefore, the climate is mild, allowing you to grow fruits, grapes. It also promotes relaxation at sea. The village is very beautiful. Tourism infrastructure is developing here more and more. Every year, Kabardinka changes beyond recognition. In addition to a beach holiday, various festivals, concerts and other interesting events are at your disposal.

The climate here is healing, there are sources of mineral waters, and the air is useful for asthmatics. This is one of the driest resorts in the Caucasus. The number of sunny days is more than in Sochi. If you are a supporter of educational tourism, and not just a “seal” pastime, it is better to rent a bike and ride around the area. You can also rent a scooter and a stroller for the baby.





How to get there

To arrive in the village of Kabardinka (Pochtovik is a pension, which is located in it), you must first arrive by train to Novorossiysk, fly to Anapa or Krasnodar. Regular buses run from the airport or train station to Gelendzhik. And from there minibuses will deliver you to Kabardinka. From Novorossiysk to the pension you can also get by bus or taxi.

But the most popular route for vacationers is still “Kabardinka-Gelendzhik”. A bus travels the distance between the two resorts in twenty minutes. You can use both intercity and local flights. Of the latter, buses No. 32, 101 run well. 646. But in order to drive almost to the boarding house itself, it is best to take the thirty-first minibus.





Territory: what is nearby?

The boarding house has its own park, several sports fields. It covers an area of ​​more than four hectares. There is a concert hall and a dance floor. The territory is closed, strangers are not allowed there. You can park the car - guarded parking lot. Everything is very clean, well maintained, comfortable and pleasing to the eye. Vacationers like the numerous flower gardens and rose gardens, as well as the fountain. There is where to walk with the children, relax in the shade on a hot day.

Near the pension there is an amusement park, a carousel. There are many cafes and sightseeing stalls nearby. You get to the beautiful promenade of the village by going out the gate of the boarding house. Almost the entire infrastructure of the resort is located within walking distance. From the sea you have to go a little uphill, but the climb is smooth. It passes through a shady park.





Rooms

Now let's see how holidaymakers settle in this resort in the village of Kabardinka. “Pochtovik” is a quite large boarding house. It can accommodate five hundred people. Several buildings have rooms of various categories. First of all, these are standard rooms designed for one, two or three guests. They are located in two-story cottages. There is a bathroom with shower, air conditioning, TV, balcony. In the bathroom there is a hairdryer, clothes dryer.

The cheaper guest rooms have partial facilities on the floor. The rooms are two-room "junior suites", but the content there is almost the same as in the "standards". True, they are in the new four-story buildings. There is good quality furniture and well-functioning plumbing. In the pension you can also live in two or triple wooden houses. Hot water on schedule - two hours in the afternoon and the same in the evening.





Services

Having fun, sunbathing and improving your health - that’s why the majority of vacationers travel to the village of Kabardinka. Pension "Pochtovik" meets all these criteria. Vacationers can visit a variety of treatment rooms, massage and physiotherapy, as well as try oxygen cocktails in a special bar. The boarding house specializes in the prevention of pulmonary and respiratory diseases, as well as arthritis and joint diseases.

Those who wish to relax and soak in the warmth visit the sauna. Sports enthusiasts can play volleyball and burn extra calories through classes in the gym. A library also works here, and in the evenings, fascinating films are shown in the concert hall. A playground is equipped for younger guests. There is a pool with slides designed for tots. There is a children's play center with a teacher. On the territory there are barbecue grills for barbecue and barbecue. You can book boat trips with the administration, including on a yacht.

Nutrition

This boarding house has a dining room that feeds guests on the "custom" menu. Meals consist of breakfast, lunch and dinner. You can buy a ticket in such a way as to eat in local cafes. However, tourists are advised to take rooms with meals. They feed here for slaughter and very high quality. Food is liked even by capricious children. Well, if you want something with alcohol, then all the cafes located on the embankment of the village of Kabardinka are at your service.





Sea

Anyone who came to sunbathe and swim will not be disappointed. The boarding house is only a few tens of meters from the beach, covered with the smallest, sand-like pebbles. Such a coast brought glory to the village of Kabardinka. The sea here is warm, the water is clean and clear. There is enough space on the shore for everyone, although people are a bit much in season. Equipped beach, it is cleaned several times a day.

There is also a cafe where you can buy kvass, water, ice cream, snacks. Since the guesthouse is located in the center of the resort to get to the beach, you don’t even have to cross the road. But if you come with children, keep in mind that the depth begins immediately at the entrance to the water. In addition, the resort has a good swimming pool with sun loungers, where you can also sunbathe.





Kabardinka, boarding house "Pochtovik": reviews

Tourists vacationing at this resort claim that this is one of the best places in Kabardinka. They recommend that others stop here, not in the private sector. The pension is suitable for a quiet holiday with your family, as well as in order to calm and relax the nerves shaken over the year. Vacationers like proximity to the sea.

Pension "Pochtovik" (Kabardinka) prices are very reasonable - from six hundred rubles per place in a wooden house without amenities to two thousand three hundred per person in a "junior suite". The staff is polite, pleasant, friendly. It is possible to book a variety of excursions: a trip to Gelendzhik, a dolphinarium, walks in the vicinity. The rooms, especially in the new buildings, are in excellent condition. Getting to the boarding house is not too problematic, given the distance Kabardinka-Gelendzhik. Bus or taxi - it's up to you. It all depends on what means you have.
