Hotel Beau Rivage Boutique Resort 4 *, Jordan: reviews, description

Choosing a place for an entertaining trip, which can be combined with swimming in the deep sea and getting a dark tan, many people stop in an amazing country - Jordan. It combines developed infrastructure with vast desert expanses, many attractions with a bewitching underwater world, comfortable living with clean ecology.

One of the secluded hotel complexes, which is suitable for lovers of a relatively relaxing holiday, where you can forget about the problems of city life and enjoy a stay at the resort, is the Beau Rivage Boutique Resort, located on the coast of the Aqaba Bay of the Red Sea. Here you can not only relax, find peace, retire and learn the charms of untouched nature, but also get a lot of positive emotions, take beautiful photos of picturesque landscapes that will take their rightful place in the family album.





Climatic conditions

This is one of the most stable Arab countries, which leads to a constant influx of tourists to these parts. The country's location was determined by its inherent subtropical climate. Year-round high water temperature makes it possible to spend holidays here at any time of the year. Summer nights are incredibly warm and calm. Winter night temperature does not drop below ten degrees.

general characteristics

Arab splendor, national generosity, warm hospitality and excellent living conditions - all this is offered to its guests by the Beau Rivage Boutique Resort 4 * hotel (Jordan). Reviews about this complex are found mainly in a positive manner. It is located five hundred meters from the sea, has a compact, cozy territory, each centimeter of which is covered with green spaces.

The four-story main building accommodates a reception, apartments for residents, a restaurant, bars, laundry and other hotel infrastructure. Once inside, holidaymakers freeze from what they saw: the highest ceilings in the lobby, marble floors, chic chandeliers, lots of spotlights and giant windows - all this creates the feeling of being in a royal castle.

Check-in with pets is not allowed. The hotel has non-smoking and disabled rooms, equipped with everything necessary for their comfortable stay. Free internet is available throughout the hotel. Check-in is carried out in accordance with international standards at 14:00. Leave the apartment before noon.

The distance to the airport is 28 km, transfer by arrival and by wire is included in the price of the ticket. The nearest spa center, Aqaba, is 15 km away.





Number of rooms

The hotel has 105 rooms, among which there are the following categories:

  1. Standard Room.
  2. Superior Room.
  3. Mountain View.

From the windows of all apartments of the Beau Rivage Boutique Resort 4 * hotel (Jordan), the photo of which you have the opportunity to see in the article, there are beautiful views of the sea or mountain hills. The interior is presented in a classic style, and it is predominantly dominated by all shades of white combined with brown. Designers worked wonderfully, creating a restrained and comfortable atmosphere. The floors are covered with laminate. Among the features of the decoration is the perfectly selected color of the curtains to match the shade of the tree from which the furniture is made. The most beautiful creations of local artists decorated the walls. Most of the rooms of the Beau Rivage Boutique Resort 4 * hotel in Jordan (reviews confirm this) have access to the balcony or terrace, which are equipped with a table with chairs. Here, drink a cup of aromatic coffee or tea without leaving the chambers.





Housing arrangement

The furniture is presented by a spacious wardrobe, a table with armchairs, a luggage stand and soft beds. Room equipment includes an individual air conditioning system, which allows maintaining a favorable microclimate, an LCD TV, a telephone and an electric kettle. Iron and ironing board are available upon request.

The minibar is replenished daily with drinking water and tea and coffee sets, which include stickers of a variety of coffee, tea and sugar.

In order not to worry about important documents and money, you can use the safe located in the closet.

The bathroom is equipped with all modern equipment, has a bathroom or shower, personal hygiene products and a hairdryer.

Towels are cleaned and cleaned daily by tidy maids. However, due to the lack of a large abundance of staff to clean up the room may come after dinner. Bed linen is changed three times a week. In reviews of Beau Rivage Boutique Resort 4 * (Jordan), there were no serious complaints about cleanliness and order.





Supply system

Guests of Beau Rivage Boutique Resort 4 * reviews on food quality leave mostly mostly positive. In accordance with the All-Inclusive concept, three meals a day are organized according to the buffet system - breakfast, lunch and dinner. According to this buffet way of serving dishes, everyone has the right to choose what he likes and fill his plate on his own. This takes place at Le Chateaux's main restaurant, according to a pre-set schedule.

The interior of the main restaurant has marble trim, the hall is well lit, the furniture in coral shades is soft and comfortable. Eating can be done in the fresh air, for this there is a sufficient number of places near the kitchen by the pool.

Le Brasserie is a cafe plus a bar where you can enjoy a light snack, enjoy sweet desserts or quench your thirst with local alcoholic drinks and freshly squeezed juices.

Near the pool is the Le Cockiage bar, where you can enjoy cocktail masterpieces by local bartenders.





Beach

Hotel Beau Rivage Boutique Resort 4 * (Jordan, Aqaba) is located on the second coastline. To get to the sea, you need to overcome an unfavorable alley. There is a pier on the beach, you can dive from it to get acquainted with the underwater world of the Red Sea, the entrance to which, by the way, is gentle and sandy.

In order to sunbathe comfortably, guests can use the Berenice beach for free, which is equipped with numerous sun loungers with soft mattresses and umbrellas for those who prefer to stay in the shade. Towels are also free. You can get there by bus, which regularly leaves from the threshold of the hotel complex.

Among the activities that allow diversifying beach activities, one can single out a game of volleyball. Team cohesion and competitive spirit will appear for everyone who enters the team of vacationers. Water rides are presented by slides, riding a banana. The opportunity to go out into the open sea and go fishing will inspire lovers of fishing tackle and a rich catch.

Snorkelling or diving are integral components of a vacation program for almost every tourist. A unique opportunity to get acquainted with coral reefs, the rich world of the inhabitants of the water abyss and the relief of the underwater world is available to every traveler - both an experienced driver and a beginner.





Aquazone

Early morning exercises or evening swims can be done in the outdoor pools of the hotel complex. Their maximum depth reaches one and a half meters. They are surrounded by comfortable sunbeds and umbrellas. There is a shower nearby.

Professional instructors organize entertaining sports classes near this zone, including morning invigorating exercises and aqua aerobics classes. Good mood, improved physical fitness and a surge of new strength are achieved thanks to light physical activity and uncomplicated exercises. These classes are interesting not only for the female half of the population, men are also happy to demonstrate the ease of execution of any movement.





Leisure activities

The evening animation at the hotel is very modest, and sometimes completely absent. You can play darts or boccia at any time of the day or night, these services are included in the price. Which, in general, is not popular with all tourists.

However, more preference is given to tourists to get acquainted with local attractions. This is due to the rich history and the presence of unique architectural monuments that allow you to personally touch the history and secrets of ancient times. Among the places of interest to visit are the fortress and port of Aqaba, the Wadi Rum desert, the dolphin reef, archaeological museums, the sunken ship and a number of other wonderful places. Excursions can be ordered both on-site and immediately include vouchers in the travel package.

Paid services

Everyone who arrives on their own vehicles is provided with a free parking space. Laundry and dry cleaning services are paid extra. Due to the lack of entertainment for children, it is possible to use the babysitting service. A qualified teacher will occupy the kid with entertaining games, creative tasks and devote all his time to him. While parents can safely go about their business.

Cots are not available in the room, only cots are provided for arranging an extra bed. Reception is open 24 hours a day. Doctor services are available at an additional cost upon request.

Beau Rivage Boutique Resort 4 * (Aqaba): guest reviews

Tourists who visited the walls of the Beau Rivage Boutique Resort hotel do not skimp on positive reviews. The latest furniture, restrained design, striking with its gloss and elegance, appeal to every vacationer. The lack of a varied animation program is replaced by spa residents with shopping and excursions to local beauties and attractions. Uninterruptedly working Internet, according to guests, is also one of the undoubted advantages of the hotel, because it allows you to contact relatives and relatives at any time of the day. Almost every second tourist who visited this hotel, in his reviews indicates that he will certainly recommend the hotel to his friends and acquaintances, and he himself does not mind returning here again.

Instead of an afterword

In general, analyzing reviews of Beau Rivage Boutique Resort 4 * (Jordan), we can conclude that the hotel is definitely worth considering as an option for accommodation during your stay in the country. Here, the administration and all hotel staff will make every effort to ensure that your vacation is not spoiled by minor troubles, and you yourself feel really comfortable.
