Regina Aqua Beach Park Resort 4 * (Hurghada, Egypt): reviews, photos, hotel description

The Regina Aqua Park Beach Resort ex Royal Regina Resort 4 * hotel (Egypt, Hurghada), built in 1996 and twice renovated - in 2009 and 2014, is invariably popular with both Russian-speaking and Western European tourists.

The reason is the location of the hotel complex: on the one hand, on the first line of wonderful beaches, and on the other, near the famous shopping and restaurant Sheraton Road. From the airport to the hotel, the distance is only 7 km. The price of the tour with accommodation in a double room at the current time is $ 382 per person.

Description of the territory of the hotel complex

The hotel territory is quite spacious (38 thousand square meters) and remarkably well-groomed. Numerous neat pedestrian terraces and paths are laid along it, between lawns and flower beds. It immediately catches the eye of guests of Regina Aqua Park Beach Resort 4 *. Description of the hotel in the reviews of tourists notes thoughtful landscaping of its territory.

Especially comfortable are the recreation areas around the pools. Sun loungers and umbrellas are placed here to protect from the sun. Lots of greenery. The hotel area is carefully cleaned and maintained by the efforts of staff in perfect condition.

Landscape designers planted and decorated a classic Mediterranean-style garden. Between trees and shrubs, invisible during the day, garlands are hung artificially. But when it gets dark on the street, they are turned on together with lights and spotlights, and this creates a very original evening landscape that you want to admire.

The hotel hall, the reception area, which the newcomers are the first to see, looks presentable: marble flooring, double ceiling heights, marble columns.

Catering

The food of the guests of the Regina Aqua Beach Park Resort 4 * hotel is provided by the main restaurant, three restaurants by appointment and three bars. At the same time, we note that the menu of a four-star hotel is quite diverse. The main dishes are prepared from chicken, turkey, beef, fish. Enough salads. There are a lot of pastries and sweets. Of the fruits, most orange and dates. Melons are rarer, and watermelons are even rarer. However, in the main menu there are no delights designed for the taste of gourmets than it happens in five-star hotels.

In general, according to tourists, the food is hearty, varied, but not wonderful.

Hotel beach

The distance from the buildings of the Regina Aqua Beach Park Resort 4 * hotel to the beach is approximately 100 meters. For the most part (except for the extremities) it is sandy and regularly cleaned. Moreover, the textured sand: white and coarse. There is a pontoon for convenient entry into the sea. It is spacious, comfortable, with sun loungers and umbrellas in sufficient quantity for relaxing beach people. Reviews of tourists about beach vacations are extremely positive.



From noon a buffet is working here by the sea. Bathing people have the opportunity to try refreshments, snack pastries, sandwiches, pizza both within the all-inclusive system and for an additional fee. Also, for an hour and a half in the afternoon, buffet workers cook meat on the grill and treat them to vacationers.

Animators are actively working here, organizing the game of beach volleyball.

At the Regina Aqua Park Beach Resort ex Royal Regina Resort 4 * hotel, there is a diving school, and beginners who want to get acquainted with the beauties of the reefs, which are located on both sides of the beach, take several basic lessons.

Pools

The pools of the hotel complex are located just in the middle - between the sea and the buildings. Large - for swimming, swimming and games (water aerobics, water polo) for adults, and the smaller - for children. In the adult pool, 5 water slides of various degrees of difficulty are equipped.

No wonder the word "water park" is contained in the name of the hotel complex Regina Aqua Park Beach Resort 4 *. Description of the delight of children from games on them under the supervision of parents and animators in itself can take pages. What could be more beautiful than the world of childhood? When they function (and this is a couple of hours before lunch and a couple after), the pool area becomes the epicenter of joy and fun. This process is skillfully and unobtrusively led by animators.



However, lovers of a relaxing holiday here are also not deprived. Artificial ponds have created a complete comfortable relaxation area with sun loungers, parasols. In addition, royal palm trees provide additional protection against direct sunlight.

Children's animators organize a play holiday for small spa visitors (4-12 years old) as part of a mini-club. There is a playground near the children's pool. In addition, the restaurant is equipped with special places for them, and the chefs prepare food adapted to the tastes of small holidaymakers as part of a special menu.

The buffet in the pool area is good in that, in addition to soft drinks and pastries, you can enjoy ice cream, a glass of beer, and local wine as part of the all-inclusive system. The recreation area by the pool is so good that some of the guests of the Regina Aqua Park Beach Resort ex Royal Regina Resort 4 * do not reach the sea. Their feedback on the recreation area by the pools, which allows you to relax in the style of the lounge, is positive. However, they also express regret that the hotel does not have a pool with heated water. And he would not be in the cold season, when the water temperature for natural reasons is about 18 degrees Celsius.

Hurghada Resort Infrastructure

After walking a few minutes, the hotel guests find themselves in the city center: on Sheraton Street, stretching from the historic center of the old city to the seafront. That is, you do not need to spend money on transport to get to the shops (the huge Hurghada Marina Boulevard hypermarket, the textile Karkush, Levis jeans, the Pharaoh leather, dozens of original souvenir shops), restaurants (McDonald's, Pizza Hut) , KFS), entertainment complex (Senzo Mall).



Having traveled a bit to the old city, tourists find themselves in a huge oriental bazaar, open from 13-00 to 23-00.

Excursions

They can be booked directly at the reception. Tours are also offered by agents from the tour desk. Signing up for a mini-trip around the Land of the Pharaohs is easy and simple. However, the price for it will be one and a half to two times higher than in urban travel agencies with a similar level of service. Many tourists save money this way.

In addition, it is advisable not to focus on long excursions (the valley of the pyramids of Giza, Luxor) in January-February during a 50-day seasonal wind. At this time, sandstorms are not uncommon. But the rest of the year, of course, these places should be visited at least once. The National Archaeological Museum in Cairo is magnificent.

Traditionally, outdoor enthusiasts buy sightseeing tours to the southern and northern islands, rich in wonderful exotic beaches and waterfalls.

A separate bonus of staying in Egypt is the possibility of a two-day excursion to Jerusalem.

About theft in Egypt

However, the sites of travel agencies also contain recommendations to take into account the theft factor in the hotel in question. There are few such reviews: only two. But they should be taken into account. In both cases, cash was simply stored in a suitcase.

In one case, the laptop disappeared. However, the vast majority of vacationers in their reviews argue that they did not encounter such a problem. It may well be that they were written by representatives of competing hotels. Who to believe? In any case, it is recommended to be careful and keep money and valuables in the safes of Regina Aqua Park Beach Resort 4 * reviews.

Theft at the hotel is sporadic. However, when heading to markets and shops, ladies should not take handbags with them. By the way, all Egyptians on the streets do not carry this accessory familiar to our women. In the modern Land of the Pharaohs, theft of handbags is thriving just on the street in broad daylight among women. Half the trouble, when a runaway man grabs a handbag. However, there are cases when a thief-motorcyclist drags a woman behind her bike who could not get free from the handle of her purse ...

Conclusion

Summing up the description of the Regina Aqua Park Beach Resort 4 * hotel, we note that tours here are invariably popular.

The economy class hotel complex has a wonderful beach, its own water park. The manager of any five-star hotel can dream of his animation team. Here, under the supervision of the employees of the mini-club and in their company, the children feel carefree and joyful.

Each guest of the hotel will find for themselves in Hurghada their own leisure option: beach vacations, diving, shopping, excursions. All this is available.
