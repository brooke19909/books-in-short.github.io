History of the state on Palace Square

The square itself is a landmark for a reason. In January of the distant 1905, a peaceful demonstration of the workers of the city was shot at by the tsarist authorities. After such sad events, the Palace Embankment was renamed the Embankment on January 9th.

In the heart of the Palace Square stands the monumental Alexander Column of unusual pink granite. Its height is 47 and a half meters. At the top of the column is a figure of an angel with a cross.
