Presenter - what is it and what is it used for

Presenter - what is it? Presenter is a remote control for switching presentation slides.

What is a presenter for?

It is a well-known fact that most people perceive information best of all visually. Therefore, the use of presentations is a simple and effective way to convey important information to a large audience. Presentations are used at lectures, at various seminars, at meetings, in a word, wherever there are speakers and listeners.





In order for the presentation to be successful, and the public‚Äôs attention not to be scattered during the presentation, it is important to switch slides on time and promptly. It is not very convenient to do this with the mouse and computer - you have to be distracted from the audience, concentrate on the switching process, try to remember what I said earlier.

In order to conduct presentations it was easy and convenient, the scientists developed a special remote control for presentations - a presenter. One of the most popular models today is the Logitech r400 presenter.

Appearance of the device

So, how the presenter works, what it is and what this device is for, we learned. Now let's talk about how he looks. In electronics stores, the presentation presenter is packed in a transparent blister, through which it can be seen that it is equipped with a red laser pointer, its range is 15 meters, the device does not require installing drivers when used with modern versions of Windows.





On the back of the box there is information about the capabilities of the device and the scope of delivery. The package includes: the presenter itself, a wireless receiver, 2 standard AAA batteries, a case, the documentation that says how the presenter works, what it is, what it is for, how to fix malfunctions, if any.

The manufacturer provides a 3-year warranty and full technical support. The case is made of soft mesh material. The device itself is like a TV remote. It is ergonomically shaped. Before first use, remove the white protective strip at the installation location of the presenter batteries. The wireless receiver looks no different from a regular USB flash drive.

Specifications

  • The device model is Logitech Wireless Presenter R400.
  • Black color.
  • Presenter weight - 57 grams, wireless receiver - 6 grams.
  • Class 2 red laser, wavelength from 640 to 660 nm
  • Power - 2 AAA batteries.
  • Wireless Technology - 2.4 GHz.

Advantages and disadvantages of the device

To begin with, let us say that the Logitech r400 presenter is convenient to hold in your hand. Due to the fact that all the buttons on it are of different sizes and shapes, it‚Äôs convenient to use it even by touch. When you turn on the device in the computer, the presenter is perceived by the operating system as a keyboard.

To open presentations, it is recommended to use Microsoft Office PowerPoint, and not OpenOffice Impress, because in OpenOffice the screen off button does not work correctly. The slide switch speed depends on the power of your computer.





The presenter's laser pointer works well in rooms without lighting and at a distance of 5 meters. At a distance of 10 meters from the slides and in bright light, the laser indicates objects that are noticeably worse. The battery life of the device is quite large. When using the pointer, the battery charge should last for 20 hours of continuous operation, without using the pointer - up to 1000 hours.

If the batteries are nearly empty, the range of the laser pointer decreases markedly.

The Logitech r400 Presenter is easy to use, comfortable and ergonomically designed.

Now that you know everything about the presenter (what it is and how it will make your life easier), you can purchase it and use it in your work. He will become an indispensable assistant for you if, by the nature of your activity, you are often forced to speak to an audience. The remote control of presentations will make the speaker‚Äôs work easier and more enjoyable, and the audience will be able to fully appreciate the presentation.
