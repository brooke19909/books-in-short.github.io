Fashion trends. Boho sundress: pattern

Boho style has not such an extensive history. It was born already in the 21st century, but despite its young age, it has already managed to win its place on the fashionable Olympus. A distinctive feature of the boho style is the absence of any restrictions. It is suitable for those people who value freedom and love comfort. Things in this style are pretty simple. Therefore, it is not necessary to replenish the wardrobe from the stores. You can create your own boho style things. All that is required is a little free time and a sewing machine. In this article, we will consider how to sew a boho sundress with a pattern.

Boho sundress

This thing is very feminine. Therefore, if you want to emphasize your natural forms, then a boho style sundress will be a great solution.

You can pick it up for any figure. The main thing is to know the features of your body and be able to highlight the advantages by properly masking the flaws.

Usually a sundress is made maxi or knee-length. An interesting is the combination of several types of fabrics. The boho sundress pattern is very simple, and therefore it is easy to sew it yourself at home.

They complement the product with ornaments in the form of lace, embroidery with ethnic motifs, fringe.

We choose a fabric for a sundress

You can make the product from a variety of materials. If this is a summer option, then choose the appropriate fabrics: linen, silk, chiffon. For other seasons you can make a velvet dress, jeans.

Which color and pattern is better?

According to the color scheme, the boho style sundress can be any. The most popular colors that are used to create it are classic: white, gray, beige, as well as all kinds of shades of green, brown, blue.

The same variety in the choice of pattern for patterns boho dress. Folklore and ethnic motifs are the most common. The popular cell and fabrics with a small flower pattern are not inferior to them.

Sundress boho. Pattern

Consider how to sew a boho sundress. To do this, we choose a model traditional for this direction. Even a novice tailor will be able to tailor such a sundress. The model looks as follows. This is a sundress with wide straps and a one-piece bodice.

Measurements that are necessary to build a pattern:

  • Chest width. The measure is removed over the sternum.
  • The width of the back. The measure is taken at the protruding points behind.
  • Shoulder height. It is defined as follows: from the base of the neck to the armpit.
  • Length of the product. You remove the mercury according to your personal preference.

Stages of pattern construction

  1. We put a point T. In the upper corner, put off the measurements of the width of the chest and back, plus an increase in free fit (eight centimeters) and denote it with a point T 1 .

  2. On the segment TT 1 it is necessary to postpone the segment T 2 T 3 . It is equal to the width of the chest. In order to put these points, we divide the segment TT 1 into two parts. And from the middle we put aside on both sides 1/2 of the measure of the width of the chest.
  3. Now, down from point T, draw a line equal to the height of the shoulder and set point K. We do the same with points T 1 and K 1 .
  4. It remains only to postpone from the points T and, accordingly, T 1 down the measure of the length of the product. We put the points H and H 1 .

The pattern is almost ready. It remains only to make the straps. They are small strips of fabric with a length equal to a double measure of shoulder height plus five centimeters. The width of the straps is ten centimeters.

Basting sequence

First, we are engaged in a seam, which will be located on the back side. After we process the shoulder edges and top. Then we sew the straps. And we complete the pattern of a sundress in the style of boho by processing the bottom.

The product is ready. You can try it on. But with what to wear such a thing, read below. You can also connect your imagination and sew a sundress in the style of boho with your own hands with a pattern slightly modified.

What to wear

Boho style involves layering. Therefore, do not be afraid of the abundance of things and accessories in one image. As for the sundress, you can wear it with leggings, leggings and even shortened models of trousers. If we talk about the addition from above, you can safely wear a self-made product with sleeveless jackets, vests in the style of punk and cowboy. An interesting look with a knitted cardigan. Do not be afraid to combine things that are incompatible at first glance in the boho style. For example, for a light sundress, you can choose a large coarse cardigan, denim jacket or vest.

Attention to accessories

For a boho style dress, you should not choose gold or silver jewelry. The best option would be jewelry that refers to ethnic motifs. You can complement the image with such accessories as scarves and scarves.

Experiment - and create your own fashionable boho dress with your own hands according to the pattern proposed above!
