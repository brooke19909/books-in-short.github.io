Extreme entertainment in Volgograd

Fans of extreme relaxation can go play paintball. It is also possible to jump with a parachute. The price also includes a three-hour ground training. The jump takes place from a height of one kilometer. Before this, you will definitely need a health certificate and make sure that your weight does not exceed 95 kilograms. The minimum weight is 45 kilograms. If you are parachuting for the first time and it is difficult for you to overcome your fear, then you can commit it with an instructor. In this case, you do not have to undergo training. Plus, the price will include not only a jump, but video and photography.
