What is he, the most beautiful city in Germany?

Germany is not only one of Europe's leading economies, it has also excelled in tourism. Travelers are very attracted by the attractions located here, nature and entertainment. Often, visitors with difficulty can decide which city in Germany to go first. By the way, the country has a well-developed railway system. It is difficult to determine which is the most beautiful city ​​in Germany, but you can visit several of them that make your heart tremble at once.

Berlin

Berlin is the honorary capital of the Federal Republic. It is home to many attractions, including those that carry an important historical heritage. Night clubs, libraries, galleries, cafes, restaurants and much more await tourists in this glorious city.

Berlin, claiming the title of "The most beautiful city in Germany", is divided into 96 districts and 12 districts. The village was founded long before the formation of the main borders of Germany - in 1237, despite the fact that the first mention of it dates back to 1170. The area is 892 km 2 . The population reaches more than 3 million as of 2013. Near the center of Berlin is a beach on the lake, which is one of the most visited places. The forest is in the same demand, quite a lot of rare animals live in it.



Frankfurt

A distinctive feature of the city called Frankfurt (Germany) is that it stands on the River Main. In the economic sphere, the locality showed itself on the good side and became a financial center. Moreover, the city is a union of the beautiful old-style Roemerberg - the current "heart" of Frankfurt - and modern types of skyscrapers. As a rule, tourists first visit monuments and museums, and after a busy day, they drink and eat local sausage.

Frankfurt is much older than Berlin. And maybe he is not the most beautiful city in Germany, but has a cool atmosphere and unforgettable charm. The first mention of the settlement appeared back in 794. The area is relatively small - only 249 km 2 . The main attraction of the city is the Cathedral of St. Bartholomew. It was founded in the XIV century and to this day retains its popularity. St. Paul's Church is also in demand. It does not have such a rich historical background as the first building, but carries a great historical heritage, being a symbol. After all, it was in this room that the residence of many influential people, including the parliament as a whole, was located.



Dusseldorf

Dusseldorf has long been the most beautiful city in Germany for many travelers. The village has unique historical information. The old part of the city was almost completely destroyed during the last war in this country. However, it was actively restored. And what can be seen here is the result of the efforts of the builders of that time. The architectural structures of this area for several decades. To this day, the state allocates a sufficient amount of money for the restoration. True, the only Neander church was not restored.

The first mention of Dusseldorf was recorded in 1135. Received its title of city in 1288. The area of ​​the settlement is 217 km 2 . The number of citizens is constantly growing. And almost reached the mark of 600 thousand. The Old Town is the center of tourism in Dusseldorf. This is where most of the historical heritage is located.



Dresden

The capital of Saxony - the city of Dresden - is located near the border with the Czech Republic. The old city, called Altstadt, is the most visited by tourists. It does not have a large area, so it is really possible to get around on foot in just one day. The first mention of the city was recorded in 1206. The area is 330 km 2 . The population reaches 530 thousand people.

For a long time, Dresden has been an important cultural and tourist center. For a long time, the settlement was considered one of the most important in these areas, according to UNESCO, but after the construction of a modern bridge over the Elbe from the list was removed. Almost 63% of the total area of ​​the city is forest. A paradise for young people will be one of the districts of Dresden - Neustadt. It is on its territory that the maximum number of night clubs, cafes and other entertainment venues is located.



Essen

Germany is the state in which Essen is located. This city is the leader in the number of museums. As a rule, tourists with pleasure go around everything and share their positive impressions with their friends. The Krupp family mansion is also very popular among travelers. Often, every visitor comes to this place. The city itself is located on the territory of the world's largest agglomeration.



Essen Square is 211 km 2 . Its population is 567 thousand people. Essen (Germany) is in the +1 time zone, switching to +2 in the summer period.
