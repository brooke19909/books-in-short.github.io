FPV quadrocopters: description, specifications and reviews of the best models

Unmanned aerial vehicles, or quadrocopters, now use not only movie makers and paparazzi. Basic models have become available to the average citizen due to strong competition and market congestion. Cheap quadrocopters can be bought for less than $ 100, and a gadget with a good camera and review can be found in about a couple of hundred, while really high-quality and more complex models have exceeded the mark of $ 1000.





The complex, which means expensive design allows you to configure and reprogram almost all the basic functions to your needs, turning the drone into a truly autonomous device that is able to independently make decisions and follow a certain nature of behavior in a given situation.

Most fans of this kind of gadget prefers models equipped with the FPV (First Person View) function, that is, the ability to see everything that happens in the first person. Moreover, the signal receiver can be anything: FPV-glasses, smartphone, laptop or desktop computer - it all depends on the specific model of the drone.

Below will be presented small reviews of quadrocopters, which differed in front of the general public and experts in quality, autonomous operating time and functionality, and somewhere simplicity and balance of characteristics. The selection includes devices for different tasks and different budgets, so you can choose something for your needs without problems.

Air Hogs RC Helix X4 Stunt

Give someone a big quadrocopter with all kinds of gadgets and autonomous flight for the whole day, but someone, on the contrary, is interested in small but nimble models that can fly inside buildings or in small courtyards. One of the distinguishing features of this kind of drones is the hull materials, which are usually made of rubber (for greater safety of the pilot and others).





Small spool, yes expensive ($ 90) - this is exactly about the Helix X series. The design of such a plan gives a significant advantage to novice pilots in view of the rubberized body and the ability to fly in tight spaces. Judging by the reviews of the owners of the model, many fans made their first flights with Helix.

This FPV quadrocopter is one of the best options for professional flights. If you like to improve the design of your drone specifically for your needs, such as reducing weight or reprogramming for walking your dog, then this model is your option.





The device is easily synchronized with a computer to install additional software, and the programs used are open source, so you can redo everything and everything.
