Smartphone BQ-5059 Strike Power: specifications, description, reviews

In the domestic market of mobile devices, not only smartphones of American, Korean, Chinese companies, but also Russian ones are represented. One such brand is BRIGHT & QUICK (BQ). The price of Strike Power BQ-5059 and other devices of the line varies within $ 100, so we can say that the company specializes in the production of budget devices.

In the spring of 2017, the company introduced three smartphones at once. Outwardly, it is almost impossible to distinguish them. Only, each has a corresponding inscription on the back cover: Strike 2, Strike P Easy and Strike P. They differ in each other and in technical characteristics. The last two models are equipped with capacious batteries, as evidenced by the prefix Power in the name. But, as for the rest of the "filling", it corresponds to the initial level.

The hero of this article was only one smartphone company BQ. The price of Strike Power BQ-5059 at official points of sale reaches 6 500 rubles. In order to answer this question, let's study the technical characteristics of the gadget.

Studying the characteristics of the BQ-5059 Strike Power, you need to consider the features of the display. In this model, the developers installed a 5-inch screen. It is based on the IPS OGS matrix. Two-touch multi-touch option implemented. The picture is displayed in HD quality.

Especially users highlighted the MiraVision function, thanks to which you can make changes to the display settings, changing the saturation, tint temperature, contrast, sharpness.
