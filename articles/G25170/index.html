Trip out of town

The city of Luga, Leningrad Region, reveals a unique opportunity for us to plunge into the world of country recreation. A very beautiful and picturesque landscape will appear before your eyes: pine and deciduous forests, blue lakes, winding rivers. The warm air and the abundance of coniferous forests create a unique, inimitable and healing climate.



It is here that Rubin hospitably opens its doors for you - a recreation center located 140 kilometers south of St. Petersburg in the small village of Shalovo.
