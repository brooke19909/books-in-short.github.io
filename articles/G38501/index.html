How to get from Perm to Nizhny Novgorod?

Every day people move from Perm to Nizhny Novgorod. Why are they doing this? Perhaps they are forced to move because of work, want to visit a distant relative or chat with friends. The most popular reason is travel. Indeed, in these distant cities there are so many interesting places that I want to admire. It is worth telling more about each of them.

Several reasons to visit Nizhny Novgorod

Nizhny Novgorod is a place that is visited annually by a huge number of tourists. They choose this city for travel for several main reasons:

  • Here you can take a cable car ride. The view from it is amazing. For local residents, this is an ordinary public vehicle, and it is of great interest to tourists.
  • This amazing place has a pedestrian street Bolshaya Pokrovskaya. People here like to walk with children, because it is safe, beautiful and very cozy.
  • In Nizhny Novgorod, several unusual monuments have been installed, with which you definitely need to make a memorable photo.
  • It is recommended to visit museums. This city has a lot of historically interesting places. Cultural workers love to talk about them.
  • The Nizhny Novgorod Kremlin deserves special attention .

Amazing architecture, extensive leisure, picturesque landscapes - all this is an occasion to visit this excellent place.



Several reasons to visit Perm

Perm is a city in the Urals, very cozy and beautiful. A huge number of tourists come here every year. Several reasons motivate them to this action:

  • In the center of the city there is a sculpture of a bear. It is believed that if he rubbed his nose, then for a long time in life there will be luck.
  • There is an unusual cafe, which is located directly on the tram, you can replenish your energy reserves, while looking at the beauties of the great city.
  • For tourists, the entire path is divided into two parts with a red and green line. Each of them corresponds to a specific topic. Each attraction has a stand with useful information, it will be interesting to get acquainted with each guest.
  • All visitors take a photo with a frame with ears, it is a real symbol of this city.
  • It is definitely worth visiting any cafe in this city in order to taste a real Ural soup, they prepare it here using special technology.




Why move from Perm to Nizhny Novgorod and vice versa? There are many reasons. In order for the trip to be pleasant and comfortable, and most importantly, safe, you need to choose the right vehicle.

The most interesting way

The distance from Nizhny Novgorod to Perm allows you to make a move on your own vehicle. This option is the most interesting, since during the road the driver can get acquainted with a large number of interesting places, in addition, you can plan the route yourself.





The total length of the entire route is 980 kilometers, it will have to spend about 14 hours. In order to get from one city to another, you need to do the following:

  • It is necessary to leave Perm in the direction of Krasnokamsk, the path to the first destination is 43 kilometers.
  • After 150 kilometers, the city of Klenovka will appear on the way, it is the last settlement in the Perm region.
  • The next destination is the Udmurt Republic, to which you need to go about 366 kilometers.
  • Next, it is worth crossing three more large points: Chuvashia, the Republic of Tatarstan and Cheboksary - from 639 to 744 km of the way.
  • After 813 kilometers there will be the first settlement in the Nizhny Novgorod region - Nikolskoye, from it there will be very little left to the designated destination.

Following the highway from Perm to Nizhny Novgorod, you will notice several gas stations with affordable prices, the road will be pleasant.

The most comfortable option

A train follows daily from Perm to Nizhny Novgorod, which goes to St. Petersburg and Moscow, and leaves from Chelyabinsk or Novy Urengoy. The total time spent on the journey will be 14 hours 34 minutes. At the long-distance Perm station, the train is at 4:58, and already at 21:32 it arrives at the Moscow station of Nizhny Novgorod.



The most tedious option

There is no direct bus flight from Perm to Nizhny Novgorod. If you wish, you can choose the option with transfers yourself. But at the same time, the total travel time will be more than a day.



The fastest option

The fastest way to get from Perm to Nizhny Novgorod is by plane. Every day, Avia Management Group CJSC operates a direct flight, the journey time on it is only 2 hours 10 minutes. In addition, you will have to spend time to Bolshoi Savin (Perm), where the airport is located.



There are several vehicles that allow you to overcome the path from one city to another, you should choose the most optimal option.
