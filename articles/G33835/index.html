Best Chinese smartphones of 2014

Oneplus one

In 2014, OnePlus launched new Chinese smartphones. The ranking of the best gadgets led model One. This is the first smartphone company that runs on the Android operating system. The users liked the new product, so the phones instantly parted from store shelves.
