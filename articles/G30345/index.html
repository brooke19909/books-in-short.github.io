How to make a horse with your own hands from threads: a description of the process

  2. We prepare the threads. We measure the length of the wire from the head to the end of the body, we multiply this figure by two.   3. The design of the legs. Through the wire frame, where the parts of the front legs are attached, throw a bunch of threads and wind these blanks to each leg with the same yarn. Do it in a spiral. Design the rear legs in the same way.

  5. Tail making. Throw the yarn blank for this part of the product through the frame from below - where the hind legs are attached, and fasten the threads (on the back). Fluff the ponytail.

What can a horse be made of? From fabric and coffee!

Surely you are surprised by such a combination of materials to fulfill the symbol of 2014. The next workshop will tell you how to make a fragrant coffee horse. For work, you need such materials:

How to make a horse with your own hands from textiles? First of all, make a pattern from paper and transfer it to a double folded fabric. Cut two identical textile pieces. Sew the product, leaving a small hole. Through it, fill the workpiece with filler (synthetic winterizer, holofiber). Sew a hole.
