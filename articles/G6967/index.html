Guzeripl: Hotels for Tourists

The mountain village of Guzeripl, located about eighty kilometers from Maykop on the left bank of the Belaya River, borders the Caucasian Nature Reserve. The nearest train station is in Kamennomostsky. Bus service is available with Maykop.





Around the village are surrounded by forests and mountains. In the twentieth century, it was the center of tourism with the first walking route that had access to the Black Sea. Every year lovers of rafting and bard songs gather in Maykop district. They are going to the festival in Guzeripl, which takes place in parallel with international competitions on Belaya.

Alluring Adventures

Adygea is a fabulous country of high mountains, stormy waterfalls and untouched nature. And all this can be seen by visiting the Maykop district. For this beauty, tourists come to the village of Guzeripl. They go to the mountains, float along rivers, look at the remains of historical and archeological monuments that have reached us through millennia. Many people come from abroad to relax, enjoy the clean air and visit dolmens. Tourists are offered a variety of routes: both walking and horseback. You can take part in rafting on a mountain river or try yourself in mountain climbing. For a comfortable stay in the village of Guzeripl, hotels are located near the points of departure of routes.

Beautiful landscape

A great place to stop while relaxing is the Enektur Hotel. The building is located on the banks of the Belaya River and fits perfectly into the surrounding landscape. Comfortable rooms are designed for people of any income. There are cafes and a restaurant.





For visitors who have come to relax in the lap of nature, there is a fireplace room, a bathhouse and a sauna, and gazebos on the river bank. Those who want to saturate the weekend with adventures will help in organizing rafting and climbing, excursions and horseback riding. The hotel has a rental of sports equipment.

Carefree vacation

In the village of Guzeripl, hotels are constantly waiting for their guests. Abago is a young but promising hotel, which is located a hundred meters from the river and a five-minute walk from the center of the village. It works year-round and has everything necessary for a good rest of travelers. The well-appointed rooms are equipped with a TV and Wi-Fi. Breakfast is included in all room rates. For vacationers with children discounts are provided, there is a games room, a playground and a pool. Hotel "Abago" has three restaurants and cafes, the menu in which you can choose your own.





Lovers of outdoor activities are assisted in organizing excursions and hiking, horseback riding and jipping. Hotel "Abago" organizes for its guests quests, rafting and climbing with experienced instructors on the tracks of any complexity. There is a rental center for sports equipment. Despite its relatively young age, this place has won the love of thousands of vacationers. On New Year's holidays in the village of Guzeripl hotels and hotels offer tourists special holiday programs.

Busy weekend

Another great place to relax, located next to the river, is the Belaya Reka Hotel. Built in 2014, it is designed for the relaxation of urban residents who are accustomed to a certain level of comfort. Each room has a TV and cable TV, safe and refrigerator, wireless internet. Room rates include a complimentary breakfast. The price per room depends on the level of comfort and the presence of a balcony, but does not exceed the size of a democratic one. The cafe-bar serves a choice of dishes or a set lunch for three hundred rubles.





For guests there is a hammam, a sauna, a large indoor pool with a geyser, a gun and a counter current. On the river bank are gazebos in which you can stay alone with nature. The hotel provides sports equipment rental services and organizes hiking and horse riding tours in the surrounding area. You can go rafting under the guidance of experienced instructors. Nearby are monuments of architecture and history.

sights

Before a trip to Guzeripl, hotels should be studied in advance and the place of rest that you like most should be chosen. Locals will be happy to tell the story of their homeland. Take you on a tour of the area.

One of the attractions is Partisan Glade. During the Civil and World War II, partisan bases were located in this place. Nearby is a lake rich in fish, and a tourist shelter where you can spend the night. Arriving for a vacation in Guzeripl, you can not take the time to visit a nature reserve located on the other side of the river. From the bridge to it no more than three hundred meters.





There is a dolmen on the territory, the appearance of which dates back to the first or second century BC. The exposition of the museum is very interesting. His workers are people passionate about their work. They will tell you about all the historical and archaeological finds.

Leisure

The village has guest houses where you can live, having plunged into the traditions of the peoples of the Caucasus. Around there are many camp sites that fans of outdoor activities choose. Special routes have been developed for them, along which even an inexperienced tourist will pass. During all pedestrian crossings, an overnight is provided in comfortable houses and good nutrition. Horse trails are also designed to suit riders.
