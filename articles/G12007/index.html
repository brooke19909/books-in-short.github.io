Shopping and entertainment center "Galaxy", Krasnodar

TRC "Galaxy" (Krasnodar) is a modern shopping and entertainment venue, which houses over 250 shopping facilities and regularly hosts leisure events, festivals, fairs, as well as charity events and various event programs. The one-story building of the complex was designed by the French architectural company Coplan, which developed it in full accordance with modern world standards. The grand opening of the Galaxy Shopping Center (Krasnodar) took place in September 2010.

Location

The building of the complex, located in the Karasun district, is regularly visited by residents of nearby areas of the city. Near the complex there are public transport stops (bus, tram, trolleybus and minibus), creating maximum comfort for residents of all of Krasnodar.





Enviable accessibility is provided by a comfortable parking lot for 1750 parking spaces, which are already used to by regular customers of the Galaxy Shopping Center (Krasnodar). How to get there A ten-minute distance from the city center allows you to easily reach the complex from almost any area: by trams (No. 8, 9, 10, 20), trolleybuses (No. 13, 14), buses (No. 1, 2, 10, 15, 18 , 51, 90) and almost all minibuses.
