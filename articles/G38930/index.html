Hotel Stari Hrast Villas (Budva / Montenegro)

The three-star Stari Hrast Villas complex, founded in 1990, will give its visitors a lot of unforgettable emotions and good mood. The hotel is located in the center of the picturesque and vibrant resort of Montenegro called Becici. It is here that the entire tourism industry is concentrated, and golden beaches stretch over 1800 m.

Description

The hotel is a small villa of three floors, in which rooms of category A and B are located. This cozy cottage has a compact house area with a beautiful Mediterranean park and a comfortable patio. Villa Stari Hrast Villas is fully landscaped and updated: the last renovation was carried out in 2012.

Near the hotel there is a very rich infrastructure, which includes numerous retail shops, nightclubs, restaurants, cafeterias, bars and concert venues. There is also a bus stop nearby , providing easy access to the spa town of Budva. You can recommend the complex to large companies, adult couples and elderly tourists.

Number of rooms

Guests are offered accommodation in comfortable and fully equipped rooms (a total of 20). Each room, regardless of category and pricing, is cleaned daily. Some rooms have cozy terraces overlooking the flowering garden.





All rooms are equipped with a private bathroom and toilet. In category A there is a shower stall. For your comfort, you will find air conditioning, a color TV and a compact refrigerator.

Nutrition

For customers of Villa Stari Hrast Cat B, there are several options for meals: three meals a day (FB), two meals a day (HB), breakfast only (BB) and without food. All guests can use the on-site restaurant with international dishes, cafes and bars.

Beach

You can sunbathe and swim in the warm sea water by visiting the free municipal beach, which is a 5-minute walk from the Stari Hrast Villas. The coast is sandy and pebble, clean, equipped with sunbeds and awnings (for a fee). On the shore, guests can rent a catamaran, ride a banana, canoe, water slide or boat. Fans of something "hotter" can go windsurfing or diving.

Additional Information

For the convenience of tourists, a car park is equipped. The service also includes a 24-hour reception, a conference room and the Internet. Visitors can take excursion programs or go on their own to inspect and get acquainted with the cultural and historical heritage of the country.
