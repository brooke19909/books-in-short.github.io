Hotel Ocean Palms 4 * (India, Goa): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Many tourists, choosing a place for a vacation, turn their eyes towards India. This is an amazing country with a completely unique culture and mentality, an interesting history and, of course, picturesque nature. Here, every traveler can count not only on entertainment, but also on modern comfort, quality service and sincere hospitality of local residents.

If you are going to relax on the Indian coast, you probably thought about staying on Goa. This is an incredibly picturesque island with a developed tourist infrastructure, miles of white beaches and hotels for every taste and budget. And one of the popular hotel complexes is Ocean Palms 4 *.

Of course, when planning a trip, an experienced tourist pays attention to all possible trifles. Important, for example, are not only the living conditions and appearance of the rooms, but also the location of the hotel, the distance from it to the sea and the vibrant city centers. It is worthwhile to find out in advance about the proposed food, and also ask how travelers respond about your chosen hotel. So what really is an Ocean Palms 4 * hotel? Is it possible to come here with the whole family and have a good time?

Where to look for a hotel? Location Features





As you know, when choosing a hotel, tourists are by no means the last to pay attention to its location. So where to look for the hotel Ocean Palms 4 * (India). North Goa is an incredibly beautiful place just a few meters from the world famous Calangute Beach. It is here, in a quiet area, that you can spend your vacation.

In truth, many guests note that the location is really profitable. In two minutes you can walk to the large shopping center "Calangute", which will undoubtedly please fans of shopping and entertainment. Two kilometers away is the famous Kaliz art gallery, and 4 km is the Aguada lighthouse, which is definitely worth a visit if only for colorful landscapes. Panaji, the capital and resort of Goa, is just 12 km away. The nearest major train station is 16 km away. But the airport is a little further, about 45 km, although the guests are provided with a convenient transfer, so there is no need to worry much about the road.

This is a medium sized hotel. It consists of two four-story buildings, built in a simple but elegant style. By the way, the buildings were recently renovated, so today guests are pleased with the novelty and pleasant interior.

There is a private territory, which is closed to outsiders - guests here relax in peace and quiet. According to reviews, everything here pleases the eye with greenery and bright colors of exotic colors. There is a swimming pool, as well as relaxation terraces and sports grounds. What do hotel rooms look like? Photos and additional information





The hotel complex Ocean Palms 4 * is not too big - on its territory there are 50 rooms. Here you will find small but cozy one-room rooms, as well as two-room apartments with spacious balconies.

All rooms are furnished with quality furniture and modern appliances. It includes air conditioning and a fan, a telephone and a small safe. You can skip the evening watching TV (it is connected to a satellite dish). There is also a DVD player - you can choose what exactly and when to watch. Ironing board and iron are delivered to the room upon request. On the table you will find a coffee machine, an electric kettle and a set for making tea and coffee. During cleaning, drinking water and some other drinks are delivered to the room.

The bathroom is small but fully equipped - there is a toilet, shower, washbasin, large mirror, as well as a hair dryer and some hygiene items.

Can I rely on food at the hotel?





If you are going on a trip, it is worthwhile to ask questions about nutrition, because without this it is difficult to imagine a really high-quality vacation. Guests at Ocean Palms Hotel 4 * are offered daily breakfasts, which are held in the form of a buffet in the hall of the hotel restaurant. According to reviews, it serves quite tasty dishes, including scrambled eggs, toast, delicious fresh pastries, light snacks, exotic fruits and some traditional Indian dishes. There is also a bar where you can order a glass of strong drink or exotic cocktails.

The rest of the day, travelers eat outside the hotel. It should be said right away that there is no shortage of catering establishments here. Restaurants and cafes are located nearby, you can even eat on the beach, enjoying delicious dishes and sea views. The prices, as well as the menu, are different here, so every tourist will surely find something to his own liking.

Where is the beach located? Facilities by the sea





Of course, deciding to spend a vacation in Goa, tourists rely on beach activities. Hotel Ocean Palms 4 * is located just a 15-minute walk from the large beautiful Calangute Beach. This is a popular place where sometimes several hundred tourists gather, so you have fun and entertainment.

As the reviews say, the coast here is clean, covered with soft white sand, as well as the seabed. The entrance to the sea is convenient, there is also a wooden pontoon. This is a public beach on which several necks are located. As a rule, buying a drink in a cafe or giving away a symbolic amount, tourists get sun loungers and umbrellas, which, of course, is very convenient. A rescue team is constantly working on the shore.

Water recreation: how are tourists having fun?

Sunbathing and swimming in the gentle sea waters are always a pleasure. But what do travelers who are accustomed to a more active holiday do? In fact, no one is bored here. The beach has a spacious volleyball court. Nearby is a water sports center where you can rent, for example, a catamaran, canoe or boat.

For a small fee you can parachute or go water skiing, banana. Tourists love to take boat trips and snorkel, considering the features of the local underwater world. Of course, those who wish can go sailing, try diving or windsurfing - on the shore it is easy not only to rent the necessary equipment, but also to take a short informative training course.

Is it possible to count on additional conditions?





Of course, tourists who choose the hotel complex Ocean Palms 4 * as a stopping place can count on some amenities. Immediately it is worth saying that almost throughout the territory there is access to the Internet with good speed. It offers guests a business center where you can use a computer and some office equipment, send a fax or letter. Leisure of guests: how to have fun at the hotel?





In most cases, tourists spend their free time on the beach or exploring the sights of the surroundings. On the other hand, you can relax at the hotel. A swimming pool with clean and warm water is equipped on its territory. And around it, on the terrace, there are sun loungers and umbrellas. Here, guests take sunbaths, chat and just have fun.

You should definitely visit the SPA salon, where for a small price they offer a lot of wellness and relaxation treatments, including a hot Jacuzzi and several types of professional massage. A gym with modern, functional equipment is open for guests. In your free time you can visit the TV lounge with a large collection of diverse films. Tourists spend good evenings playing table tennis. The hotel also has a tour desk where you can help you choose an interesting trip around the island and tell you how to get to the brightest sights of the surroundings.

Is it possible to count on conditions for relaxing with a child?

For parents planning a vacation with their child, comfort issues come to the fore. Immediately it is worth saying that couples with children often stay at the Ocean Palms 4 * hotel. In the room you can request an extra rollaway bed, and for breakfast you can always pick up something for the guys.

In addition, a small shallow section in the communal pool is equipped for them. Kids have a great time in the games room, where there are lots of toys, attractions and even special slot machines. There is a playground with swings in the fresh air, where the guys play and play sports. For a small fee, you can hire a professional nanny to look after your child with pleasure.
