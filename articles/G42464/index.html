Hotel "East", Tyumen: photos and reviews of tourists

In the city of Tyumen there are several upscale hotels that meet the international standard. They stop numerous guests of the city. Today, the largest of them is the Vostok Hotel. Tyumen, being simultaneously the administrative center of the region of the same name, annually receives several thousand guests. And this is not surprising. After all, the city is the largest industrial center of the region. Therefore, business people come here from all over our country and from abroad. Many of them choose the Vostok Hotel (Tyumen) for their stay.



Description

This hotel is considered the largest in the city. She is assigned a three-star category. In 1964, local newspapers published an article stating that for a long time it was necessary to expand the hotel fund of the city. They announced that the Vostok Hotel (Tyumen) would soon appear. The address at which it was decided to build his building is Republic Street, 159. This is the central part of the city. The hotel was commissioned in 1973. In 2009, the Vostok Hotel (Tyumen) was certified as an object of the tourism industry. He was assigned the category of "three stars."

How to get there

The hotel is located on one of the central streets. Those who independently get to it can choose a taxi and public transport. You can come from Roshchino airport by shuttle bus No. 141. Having got off at the Central Market stop, you should go to another route. Buses No. 11, 14 will take you to Pamyati Square, located directly next to the hotel. Travel time is about fifty minutes. From the railway station to the hotel there are buses number 13, 25, 27.
