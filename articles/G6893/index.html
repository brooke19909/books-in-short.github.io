Aurora Oriental Resort, Sharm el Sheikh: review, description, features and reviews

In all months, except January and February (due to winds), the Aurora Oriental Resort (Sharm el Sheikh) 5 * hotel is in demand among vacationers as a quiet family place.

The hotel complex, average in volume of its number of rooms, guarantees a high-quality five-star rest at a classic level at economical prices. On average, a weekly tour with accommodation in a standard room will cost travelers $ 550 per person.

Location

It is built on the coast of the quiet and shallow Nabq Bay, 18 kilometers from the heart of Sharm El Sheikh, the bustling and vibrant Naama Bay. The modern bright three-story residential buildings of the hotel complex with large loggias overlook a chic elongated pool with a smooth outline.

A pedestrian bridge leads directly through the pool, through which you can go to the main hotel building with a reception area.



From the windows of the hotel complex, if you look into the distance, you also have a sea view of Tiran Island, located 20 km from the coast.

The journey from the airport to the hotel takes only a quarter to a third of an hour. The hotel, built in 2001, has been modernized, as well as equipped with modern infrastructure and service. According to guest reviews, he fully justifies his five-star status. Due to the quality of its service, it is very famous in this region and is popular with tourists.

In the advertisements of numerous travel agencies you can see his address: Aurora Oriental Resort, Sharm el Sheikh, Nabq Bay. This bay is shallow, there is a calm and quiet resort place. Families with children usually come here. For lovers of easy swimming, this place is perfect. For connoisseurs of snorkeling, each time before diving, you will have to spend 15 minutes on the path along the pontoon.

However, you should not go here in the winter months: when the hot wind from the desert often intensifies to sand storms.

Hotel Territory

The territory was designed and landscaped by masters of landscape design. Well-groomed lawns, palm trees, specific deciduous trees and bushes trimmed by topiary craftsmen are combined with hotel pools. The pedestrian terraces and paths are well-groomed and neat. All this really turns the territory of the hotel Aurora Oriental Resort (Sharm el Sheikh) into a cozy and comfortable corner for a spa holiday.

The local atmosphere itself contributes to the special resort mood of the guests and their relaxation. Those who come here not for the first time have long looked for their favorite places: for some it is a place by the pool, for others a restaurant by appointment, for others a beach.

Hotel rooms

The hotel complex is located in the main three-story residential building of a housing estate of comfortable coastal villas. The rooms at Aurora Oriental Resort (Sharm el Sheikh) are bright and airy. They are furnished with high-quality classic-style wooden furniture in accordance with a separate design project. Therefore, the atmosphere in them is harmonious and soothing. Panoramic windows provide good lighting and picturesque sea landscape.



The rooms are equipped with necessary appliances: air conditioning, TV, hairdryer, telephone, kettle. The spacious bathrooms are equipped with a "double": shower and bath.

Respected here and Internet lovers. The Aurora Oriental Resort (Sharm el Sheikh) has fast enough paid Wi-Fi.

The rooms have a balcony with a table and a pair of chairs.

Rooms are cleaned and linen and towels are replaced daily and without tip. Leaving the same $ 1, in gratitude from the staff you can see additionally made figures of swans, fish, elephants, etc.

Beach

The hotel beach is vast and stretches along the coast for almost half a kilometer (450 meters). Beach chairs, sun umbrellas, mattresses, the beach is fully provided. Hotel guests use all this equipment for free.

Entrance to the water - from the pontoons. The bottom relief is very gentle (this is a feature of the Red Sea) and beachmen will have to walk 200 meters to a depth acceptable for swimming. But, having plunged from the pontoon at this depth, bathers find themselves right next to picturesque corals inhabited by colorful fish.



Enthusiastic bathers bathe even during the day when the air temperature warms up to 45 degrees Celsius. They simply put on white sun-shirts and dive with a mask and snorkel.

By the way, in the summer in terms of swimming and diving, Nabq Bay is more comfortable than Naama Bay. The water temperature in the hot summer months is only 28 against 35 degrees Celsius. This circumstance means for the spa guests a precious coolness in the water, while the air warms up to a hot state. Still, the climate in Egypt is tropical desert.
