Tretyakov Gallery

Speaking about the main attractions of Russia, it is impossible to ignore this museum, because the best masterpieces of Russian painting are collected here. In the Tretyakov Gallery you can see the canvases of such great painters as Repin, Bryullov, Levitan, Vasnetsov, Vrubel, Savrasov, Kiprensky, Ivanov, Kuindzhi, Vereshchagin, Polenov, Tropinin, Fedotov, Shishkin and others. The Tretyakov Gallery and the most extensive collection of unique Russian icons are famous . The world-famous icon of Andrei Rublev "Trinity" is part of the permanent exhibition of this museum.





Hermitage

St. Petersburg ... This pearl in the marvelous necklace of Russian cities can boast of a huge number of attractions, but since we are strictly limited to small volumes of the article, we will only talk about the Hermitage. The majestic building is located on the banks of the Neva, on one side it adjoins the Palace Embankment, on the other - the Palace Square with a tall (47.5 m) Alexandrian column in the middle.





Until 1917, the Hermitage was the residence of Nicholas II - the tsar whom Russia lost during the revolution. Today he is called the martyr king, and the Hermitage is considered one of the largest museums in the world. To visit all its expositions, you need to go 20 km. The collection of this cultural center has about 3 million exhibits. Here you can see with your own eyes the beautiful paintings by Leonardo da Vinci, Titian, Raphael, Rembrandt, Giotto, Vermeer, Rubens, Goya, Cezanne, Van Gogh, Renoir, Gauguin and many other brilliant artists.

Journey on the Ring

Yaroslavl, Suzdal, Kostroma, Vladimir, Ivanovo, Ples, Pereslavl-Zalessky, Rostov the Great (the full list consists of 20 items) - this is the Golden Ring of Russia. The cities we named have an ancient historical past. We list briefly their most famous sights.

Yaroslavl An ancient and majestic city, standing on the high bank of the beautiful Volga. It was founded in 1010 by Prince Yaroslav the Wise. Here tourists are offered to visit:

  • The Museum-Reserve is historical and architectural. It is located in the vast territories of the Yaroslavl Transfiguration Monastery.
  • The architectural ensemble in Korovniki (Vladimir Church, the Church of John Chrysostom, the bell tower - all this is real historical Russia).
  • Church of St. John the Baptist.
  • Art Museum with a huge collection of ancient Russian icons.

Suzdal. Mentioned in chronicles since 1024. The entire territory of this small town is dotted with beautiful churches and architectural ensembles. Those who are interested in the ancient Kremlin of Russia should definitely visit the Suzdal Kremlin. Behind its walls is the famous Christmas Cathedral with blue domes decorated with gold stars. In Suzdal, tourists are shown:

  • Museum of Wooden Architecture and Peasant Life.
  • Pokrovsky monastery.
  • Euthymius Monastery.
  • Church of Boris and Gleb.
  • St. Nicholas and the Assumption Church.




What you can see in the old Volga city of Kostroma:

North of Russia

Excursions in Russia lie in the territories of its northern lands. These are amazing ancient lands, to which the Tatar-Mongol yoke did not reach, which ruined many cities of central Russia. Numerous unique historical monuments, ancient rites and traditions, ancient legends and epics have been preserved in these places. Arkhangelsk, Vologda, Kargopol, Veliky Ustyug, Kirillov, Petrozavodsk, Solvychegodsk - all this is northern Russia. Today, tourist routes in these places offer a visit to the Kizhi Museum-Reserve. It is located on the islands of Lake Onega in Karelia. Its main attraction is the wooden Transfiguration Church. It is adorned with 22 chapters. Today, Kizhi is a state reserve. This complex is protected by UNESCO.
