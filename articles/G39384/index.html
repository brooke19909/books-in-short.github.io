How to make a bow from a satin ribbon

In order to decorate a blouse or a children's blouse, bag or hairpin, gift wrapping or just arrange a flower bouquet, use bows. Of course, they can be purchased ready-made or you can make a bow out of a satin ribbon or other material yourself. To do this, you just need to be patient and train for about fifteen to twenty minutes. Well, of course, prepare supplies. And then turn on the fantasy and start creating!

Where can I use finished products



Find use for such an element of decor is quite simple. You can decorate gift wraps or decorate the box by simply pasting it with fabric or beautiful paper and placing a luxurious satin ribbon bow on the lid. Choosing a harmonious combination of shades, you will get a real masterpiece that can amaze even sophisticated guests. Having made several of these bows, you can draw a decorative curtain on the window or please your daughter by gluing them with a heat gun to ordinary invisibles or hairpins. You will get completely unique hairpins that all the girls in the class or kindergarten will envy.

How to create a masterpiece

In order to make a bow from a satin ribbon, it is necessary to prepare

the material from which we will create our creation. It will take several meters of a satin ribbon of different widths and shades, optionally organza or silk, threads, needles, a heat gun, as well as various beads, hairpins, beads, figures from damaged decorative rubber bands for hair and everything that can be used as decoration. You may also need double-sided tape - in case you plan to make a bow from a satin ribbon, the photo of which we offer.

First, measure the desired length of the tape. To do this, you need to remember one pattern that will help you quickly create copies of the ideal size. If you plan to make a bow with a size of 5 cm, then the ribbon should be 25 cm long. Therefore, we respect the proportion

finished product and consumables - 1: 5. Then we begin to form the bow itself from a satin ribbon. To create a beautiful and stylish instance, you need a smooth and puff-free tape. Form the two loops so that they are the same size. Then, fixing the center of the bow with a thin ribbon, tightly drag the place where the loops intersect. For this, use a tape of a contrasting shade or in perfect harmony with the basic tone of the workpiece. After the center was wrapped a couple of times with a thin ribbon, you can fix it either using a heat gun or using small stitches of thread. The ends of the bow are carefully cut diagonally and very carefully melted over a candle or lighter.

Decorate bow

How to make an exclusive option? Very simple. To do this, we make bows from satin ribbons and decorate them to your liking. Rhinestones and beads, beads or decorative small flowers or figures ... It all depends on why we need this element of decor. And from my own imagination, of course!
