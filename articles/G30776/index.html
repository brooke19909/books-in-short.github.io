Staraya Russa (resort). Rest and treatment in Staraya Russa

Staraya Russa is a resort in the Novgorod region, a place where you want to return again and again. The recreation area is located in a picturesque corner of Russia, but beautiful nature is not its main advantage. Staraya Russa is a resort that is famous for its mineral waters and healing mud. Every year, hundreds of Russians and visitors come here to improve their health, gain strength and energy. It is worth saying that the conditions for a vacation in Staraya Russa are no worse than in the world-famous resorts Bad Ischl, Abano Terme and Uryazh-les-Bains. So, are you still sure that a full vacation is possible only abroad? We will dispel your doubts!





History and Modernity

Staraya Russa is a resort located in the same city. The ancient Russian settlement on this place arose in the middle of the XII century. For a long time, the town was the most important salt-producing center of the Russian Empire, but over time it lost its industrial significance due to the low salt concentration in the springs.

Since the reign of Catherine the Great, Staraya Russa began to attract the attention of many doctors, including the discoverer of Caucasian healing waters - Dr. Haas F.P. The efforts of the researchers were not in vain, because already in 1828 the construction of the resort began in the city.

Nikolai Dobrolubov, Eugene Leuchtenberg, Vera Komissarzhevskaya, Konstantin Stanislavsky, Maxim Gorky, Fedor Dostoevsky - this is an incomplete list of famous people who felt the power of the healing waters of the Novgorod region.

Today Staraya Russa is a resort that attracts the attention of tourists from all over Russia. There are all conditions for a healthy stay. The resort has many sources of mineral water and healing mud, a network of hotels and restaurants, a spa center and a gym.





Medical base

The medicinal properties of mineral waters were known in ancient Greece. The Romans, following their predecessors, began to build baths at the sources. And in the Renaissance, mineral spas became centers of social life. Today, treatment with water and healing mud is booming. And one of the best mineral resorts in Russia is considered Staraya Russa.

The main wealth of this wellness area is the unique healing waters of different levels of mineralization and healing mud. Old Russian sulphide-silt mud has no analogues in the whole world. They have unique healing properties: they stimulate blood circulation, improve metabolism at the cellular level, restore tissue respiration and accelerate regeneration processes.

The Muravyov Fountain is another amazing object, thanks to which the resort of Staraya Russa is famous (a photo of an unusual building is presented below). It is the most powerful self-flowing mineral spring in Europe. Its unique composition of water has anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, regenerating and analgesic effects.





If you suffer from diseases of the circulatory, digestive, nervous systems or musculoskeletal system - go to the resort of Staraya Russa! The reviews of people who visited this amazing place confirm that Old Russian mud and mineral waters are a unique cure for all ailments.

Therapeutic techniques

Sulphide-silt mud and mineral waters have a number of useful properties. They have anti-inflammatory and soothing effects, normalize blood circulation and metabolism, increase immunity and slow the progression of even the most serious chronic diseases.

Treatment with old Russian waters and mud is indicated for people suffering from problems with the gastrointestinal tract, respiratory, nervous, circulatory or endocrine systems, dermatological and gynecological ailments.

The treatment and rehabilitation center in Staraya Russa has the latest equipment, which allows the use of the most effective treatment methods.

The following treatments are available at the resort:

  • mineral and aromatic baths;
  • healing souls;
  • mud applications ;
  • therapeutic massages;
  • halotherapy;
  • inhalation;
  • physiotherapy (UHF, SMV, UHF, phototherapy, electrophoresis, laser therapy, etc.);
  • thermal pool.




Recreation and entertainment

If you want to spend your vacation with benefit - go to the resort of Staraya Russa! The prices for services here will undoubtedly please you, and the excellent conditions and friendly staff will make your vacation unforgettable.

A vacation in Staraya Russa will be remembered by a mass of positive emotions, because its employees are sure that a good mood is the key to a quick recovery. The resort has its own beach on the shore of Salt Lake, a gym, a spa center. In addition, there are several hotels, restaurants, bowling, billiards and other entertainment complexes.





3 reasons to go on vacation and treatment in Staraya Russa

  1. Unique healing waters and healing mud, as well as one of the most powerful self-draining mineral fountain in the world.
  2. Qualified doctors and attentive staff.
  3. A varied and rich holiday that will appeal to both adults and children.
