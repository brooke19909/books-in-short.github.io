Palma 3 * (Montenegro / Tivat) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

One of the most popular resort towns in Montenegro is Tivat. Thousands of tourists from around the world come here every year. A significant percentage of vacationers is occupied by our compatriots. Hotels in Tivat (Montenegro) offer travelers accommodation for every taste. So, there are magnificent luxury hotels and very budget apartments. Today we decided to introduce you to the three-star Palma 3 * hotel. We offer to find out together what is offered to tourists here. We will also find out what impressions the Russians made about their stay in this hotel and in Tivat as a whole.





Location

The three-star Palma Hotel has a very good location. So, the city center can be reached on foot in just five minutes. The hotel itself is located almost on the embankment, so there are a lot of restaurants, bars and cafes nearby. As for the beach, like the vast majority of Montenegro hotels, Palma 3 * does not have it. Basically, in this country the beaches are municipal. The nearest to the hotel is only a couple of tens of meters. So you have to, leaving the hotel, just cross the promenade, and you are already on the beach.

As for the city of Tivat itself, it is small, very green and ideal for a quiet measured rest. It is very easy to find Tivat on the map of Montenegro, since one of the two airports in this country is located here. As a rule, most tourists whose ultimate goal is the coast of a given state choose this air harbor as the place of arrival. Most of all, of course, in this case, those who plan to stay at the Tivat hotel will be lucky. So, the distance from the airport to the city center is only 2.5 kilometers. So after landing in the air harbor, the path to the hotel takes only a few minutes.

Travelers staying at the ‚ÄúPalm‚Äù or another Tivat hotel and planning to travel around the country will also appreciate the city‚Äôs good location. So, it is located almost in the very center of the coast, therefore it is convenient to get from here both to the southernmost settlement of Montenegro - Ulcinj, and to Herceg Novi, located at the opposite end of the coast near the Croatian border. The center of tourist life throughout Montenegro - Budva - is located 17 kilometers from Tivat. And the distance to the ancient and interesting city of Kotor is only 8 kilometers.





Three-star hotel "Palma" (Montenegro, Tivat): photos, description

This hotel is considered one of the best in the city. Besides the fact that it offers guests excellent service and accommodation in comfortable rooms, it boasts a good location near the beach in a quiet place, within walking distance of all Tivat's attractions. ‚ÄúPalma‚Äù is perfect for family tourists who come to rest with children, as well as for older people. It will be comfortable here for young people. In total, this hotel has 122 comfortable rooms (suites, standard and apartments). Some of the rooms have a magnificent view of the Adriatic Sea. The rooms have everything you need for a comfortable stay. By the way, unlike many hotels on the Montenegrin coast, Hotel Palma 3 * operates not only in the summer, but throughout the year. Therefore, if you decide to make a pleasant trip to this amazing country in the fall, winter or spring, the Palma Hotel will open its doors to you with pleasure.

Hotel Policies

As in the vast majority of hotels around the world, the "Palm" also set the time of check-in and check-out of vacationers. So, check-in of the staying guests in the rooms of the category reserved by them is carried out from two in the afternoon to eight in the evening. However, this does not mean that if you arrive earlier or later than the indicated time, you will be refused accommodation. On the contrary, the hotel staff will do their best to populate you immediately upon arrival. But keep in mind that if you arrive at the hotel early in the midst of the tourist season (July and August), there may not be any rooms available. In this case, you will have to wait until the previous guests vacate the room, and the maids prepare it for occupation. At this time, you can leave things in the luggage compartment and have a bite to eat in the restaurant, take a walk in the surroundings or go straight to the beach. As for the date of departure, the occupied room should be vacated before noon. When you are discharged from the Palma Hotel (Tivat, Montenegro), you should also pay for the entire stay (if you have not previously settled with a travel agency), as well as for using additional services.

You can pay both in cash and with plastic cards of such systems as "Visa", "Mastercard", "Maestro" and "American Express". In addition, please note that in case of self-reservation of a room in this hotel by credit card, the prepayment amount may be deducted from you. At the same time, when you check out of the hotel you will only have to pay the remaining amount.





Accommodation for guests with children

Since the three-star Palma Hotel (Tivat, Montenegro) also positions itself as an institution for family vacations, guests with children of various age groups are always welcome here. Moreover, if you place a baby up to two years old in your room on existing berths or in the additionally provided arena, then you will not have to pay for it. If your child is from 2 to 12 years old, an extra bed will be provided. Such accommodation will cost 60% of the cost per day per person. Children over 12 years old or adults on an extra bed will have to pay 90% of the room rate per day for one guest. Please note that only one extra or cot can be accommodated in each room. In addition, the hotel should be informed in advance about the need for this service and wait for confirmation from it.

Pets welcome

This item will be very important for those travelers who do not want to leave their four-legged pet at home during their holidays in Montenegro. So, unfortunately, the rules of the Palma 3 * hotel prohibit pets on its territory. Therefore, you will either have to find another hotel from the Pets Friend category.





Number of rooms

As mentioned above, the three-star Palma Hotel, located in Tivat (Montenegro), has 122 rooms. The room fund is represented by 74 standard rooms (area 17 square meters, side view of the mountains and the sea, the presence of one double or two single beds, the absence of a balcony, maximum occupancy - two people); 40 suites (area 17-25 square meters, double or triple occupancy, direct or side sea view); 8 apartments (34 square meters, two rooms, direct or side sea view, maximum occupancy - four people). All rooms have necessary furniture and a private bathroom with a shower. The deluxe rooms also have a minibar, air conditioning, TV and a balcony. There are no safes in the rooms, but you can leave valuables for storage in the safe at the reception. All rooms are regularly cleaned and bed linen and towels are changed.

Nutrition

The room rate at the Palma 3 * hotel (Tivat, Montenegro) includes two meals a day (breakfast and dinner). Breakfast is continental, while international and national dishes are served for dinner. In addition, you can always have a meal during the day in the hotel restaurant, ordering an √† la carte dish. The hotel has a bar with a wide selection of both soft and alcoholic drinks.





Sea and beach

As already mentioned, the Palma 3 * hotel (Montenegro) does not have its own beach. The nearest municipal beach is just a couple of tens of meters from the "Palma". To get to the sea from the hotel, you only need to go through the promenade. The beach here is a concrete slab and a small strip of pebbles. By the way, according to tourists, the local beach is one of the best in Tivat. For an additional fee, you can rent sun loungers and parasols here.

Infrastructure

Since Palma 3 * is a city-style hotel, it practically does not have its own territory. Therefore, most of the entertainment and opportunities for outdoor activities is located outside the hotel. In the hotel itself, guests have the opportunity to dine in the restaurant, enjoy a drink in the bar or spend time with health benefits and figures in the gym.

In addition, the ‚ÄúPalm‚Äù has a conference room for up to 50 people, and a meeting room for up to 25 people. Both of these rooms are equipped with everything necessary for various business events.

Hotel guests have free Wi-Fi access in public areas. In addition, the hotel Palma 3 * (Montenegro) has its own parking.





Cost of living

As for the price of accommodation in the hotel in question, it is consistent with the three-star level. So, a seven-day stay here at the height of the holiday season (July-August) in a standard room for one person will cost from 24 500 rubles, for two - from 32 000 rubles, and in a suite designed for a maximum accommodation of four guests - from 49 000 rubles. In low season, the cost will be much lower.
