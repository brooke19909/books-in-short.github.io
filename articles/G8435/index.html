Skating rink in the Tauride Garden - relax with pleasure

Many modern ice rinks allow visitors to have a good time at any time of the year. Thanks to this, residents of St. Petersburg and its guests can go to the rink in the Tauride Garden, which has long been considered one of the best. For visitors, there are all the necessary conditions for relaxation, as well as excellent service. In addition, the object is located in a picturesque and unique place where you can always walk with great pleasure.

general information

The skating rink in the Tauride Garden attracts people of all ages. Here come the company of friends, families and couples in love. On ice you can meet not only teenagers, but also older people. In the arena, you can engage in not just amateur skating, but also professional.

Where is the ice rink

The place to stay is very popular in the city. Almost every citizen can tell where the skating rink is located. However, knowing the exact address, you can find it yourself. The skating rink in the Tauride Garden is located on Potemkinskaya Street, Building 4, Building A. The Tauride Garden can become a reference point for the search.

There are several ways to get here. Right next to the building is a stop called "Tchaikovsky Street". It can be reached by buses under numbers 46 or 136. A minibus number K-76 also runs. If desired, visitors can walk to the rink from the Chernyshevskaya metro station.

Rink prices in the Tauride Garden

On weekdays, tickets for adult guests cost from 350 to 500 rubles, depending on time. For children under 14 they will cost from 250 to 400 rubles. Ticket price is indicated for one hour. On weekend tickets for adults start at 400 rubles and from 350 rubles for children. The skating rink is open on weekdays from 9am to 11pm, and on weekends and holidays from 12.00 to 23.00. The skating rink regularly runs at night. They start at ten in the evening and last until 5 in the morning. A ticket for them costs from 500 rubles.

Additional features

For visitors created the most comfortable conditions. There is a wardrobe, locker rooms with lockers, showers. For the safety of guests, a doctor works. You can relax and have a snack right in the cafe. There is also a gym with good equipment on site. Separate places are intended for visitors on the second floor, so from there you can watch how others ride. Mass skating takes place to the music, which makes them more spectacular and positive. An ice rink in the Tauride Garden invites parents to bring their children to sports clubs. Here you can learn not only hockey, but also figure skating.
