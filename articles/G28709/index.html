Where is the hotel and how to get to it

This hotel is located by the sea in Kanoni - the most ancient part of the island. Three kilometers from the hotel there is a very popular attraction among tourists around the world - the Mon Repos Palace. Corfu city center from the hotel by public transport can be reached in just 5-10 minutes. The island's airport is approximately 4 km from the hotel. Tourists can reach the place by transfer bus or taxi.





The distance to the beach from the hotel is about 300 m. Hotel guests can reach the seashore in about 15-20 minutes.

Mouse island

This interesting attraction, which is one of the symbols of Corfu, is located right next to the hotel. Some tourists sail to the Mouse Island of Pontikonisi directly from the hotel's beach. This place attracts tourists primarily for its incredibly picturesque landscapes. There is even one very interesting legend about the appearance of Mouse Island. According to legend, once Pontikonisi was a ship of the Odyssey itself. But the angry Poseidon once took and simply turned it into stone.

The only building on Pontikonisi is the Pantokrator Monastery. The island was named Myshin for its tiny size and also because the winding path leading to the monastery really resembles the tail of this little rodent.





Other attractions available

Of course, guests of the Hellinis Hotel 3 * (Corfu, Kanoni), in addition to the Mouse Island, can visit Corfu and many other interesting places. For example, such sights as the Mon Repos Palace, Paleo Frurio, the Angelokastro fortress are very popular.
