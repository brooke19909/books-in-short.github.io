Murmansk region, Polar Dawns: acquaintance with the city

In Russia there are many unique and picturesque lands. One of these is the Murmansk region. Polar Dawns is an amazing city with an attractive name. It is located in this region. The settlement Polar Dawns was erected in 1968. The main goal was the placement of builders and workers involved in the construction of the future energy heritage of the city - the Kola Nuclear Power Plant.



a brief description of

The Russian Federation is a fairly large state. It has many regions and districts. And it is in the North-West that the Murmansk region enters. Polar Dawns is a small town located on the Niva River. Also on its territory there is a beautiful Pinozero. The total territorial area was 3.6 square meters. km And the population does not exceed 15,000 people. An interesting fact is that polar nights almost never occur here. And the shortest day is only once a year, in December.

City development

After the large-scale project - NPP - was completed, the city itself began to come to life. The founder is considered to be the chief architect of the station A. Andrushechko. When the nuclear power plant started operating, the whole Murmansk region began to develop rapidly. Polar Dawns in 1974 was awarded the status of a working village. And already in 1991, it was officially named the city. His first mayor was A. A. Chistova.

Polar Dawns can rightfully be called the energy capital of the Murmansk region and the entire Kola region. In addition to the large-sized Kola NPP, which provides more than two thousand jobs, the population of the city works at the Niva Hydroelectric Power Station, in electric installation companies and boiler houses.



Transport

The city has an excellently developed transport system. Numerous buses, minibuses and taxis, as well as working vehicles, which are engaged in the distribution of power engineers, builders, workers in the food and machinery industries. In addition to its own city routes, there is a long-distance connection. These routes cover almost the entire Murmansk region.

Polar Dawns - a city where there is a railway station. High-speed electric trains pass through it in different directions. Near the city limits there are two airports, the nearest one is Khibiny.
