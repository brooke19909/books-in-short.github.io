Rest in Gelendzhik.

Gelendzhik in 2011 recognized as the most popular Black Sea resort of the Krasnodar Territory. It is worth noting that most likely this was due to the unique location of this city relative to the sea. All resorts of Gelendzhik are located on the banks of the picturesque and vibrant Gelendzhik Bay, a vacation on the shore of which is perfect for both adults and children. For family and youth recreation, there are a huge number of trips to this city. Last minute trips to Gelendzhik are also chosen by people who have not visited quality sanatoriums and boarding houses for a long time. But besides this, Gelendzhik can boast of its rather high-quality hotels, which are well-equipped for a variety of leisure activities.

The beginning of the summer season and the arrival of new tourists in Gelendzhik is usually celebrated with the help of a colorful carnival, which is subsequently accompanied by a solemn procession of all visitors. At this time, the city hosts a huge number of famous performances and concerts. In addition, it is likely that having issued last-minute tours to Gelendzhik just before the arrival of the main tourists, you will have a chance to see a lot of sports competitions. For example, every year a European sailing regatta is organized in the sea, and in the mountains there are often competitions in climbing the Markhot ridge.

Also, a distinctive feature of these resorts is that the rest in Gelendzhik will be everywhere accompanied by playing music and smiling people. All this gives a good mood and a pleasant atmosphere of a constant holiday. It is from the beginning of the summer arrival of tourists in the resort of Gelendzhik that all possible recreational activities are opened, and the sanatoriums and boarding houses begin to work in this special reinforced mode at this time.

However, in addition to purely everyday joys, in this city you enjoy nature and climate. Especially mild Mediterranean climate, which is much drier than in Sochi, as well as the warm sea and surprisingly gentle sun, will also give you many pleasant minutes. For a more convenient and simple acquisition of tanning, everything is equipped here for the device on a sandy beach. The incredibly clean and healing air in the resorts of Gelendzhik allows everyone to literally be reborn. There are also unique natural factors, such as iodine mineral waters and silt mud, which attract people here not only for treatment, but also for undergoing relaxing procedures necessary for everyone. The tourist life in the resorts of this city seems bright and unforgettable.

Especially for people with poor health, natural pensions have been created here, which are equipped with all the latest medical equipment, and the staff consists mainly of qualified and time-tested doctors. So, rest in Gelendzhik will help you both have fun and improve your health.

Gelendzhik is a modern resort city with developed infrastructure for any type of active and passive recreation. Of the main attractions, mineral springs and a chairlift are noted here , which then ends in a beautiful park for extreme entertainment called Admiral Vrungel. The most popular holiday in Gelendzhik enjoys outdoor enthusiasts, as there is every opportunity for jipping, horseback riding and boat trips. It is especially pleasant to have a rest in Gelendzhik because at its resorts the prices for the services and entertainment provided are quite acceptable.
