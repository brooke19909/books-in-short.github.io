How to make do-it-yourself board games: ideas, instructions, and photos

Board games are a great way to spend time with the whole family. You can make such entertainment easily and quickly from improvised materials. A handmade game made of wood will become an original and memorable gift.

How to come up with an interesting game

In fact, the question of how to make a board game with your own hands should not be difficult. In order to invent it, you do not need to reinvent the wheel. Already existing entertainment is enough to pass the time with family and friends. You need to take some classic game as a basis and try to repeat it. The uniqueness of the finished product will give manual execution and time invested in the thing, the work of the master.

The main thing is to determine the age of the players and their number. If the fun is intended exclusively for children, then it is worthwhile to fulfill it in the most vivid and beautiful form. The content should be tailored to the individual interests of the child, then the baby will be happy to spend time with entertainment.

What can I make a game of





Materials for making entertainment are very different. You can do children's board games with your own hands, both from paper and cardboard, and from felt or wood. It turns out to be very interesting when materials different in texture and color are combined.

Good fun for children can come from a metal candy box. The bottom of the package must be glued with paper pictures, and as animals, objects and buildings, you can take felt figures with sewn-in magnets. Such toys will be kept on the field, and the box itself is easy to store and take with you on trips or for a walk with the child.





The photo shows a good example of how to make a board game with your own hands - a puzzle. You need to take popsicle sticks and stick a picture on them, wait for the glue to dry. Then gently cut the pattern with a paper knife. On the back of the sticks, you can stick pieces of Velcro fasteners, then wooden puzzles will be conveniently mounted on a felt substrate.

Board games for children

There are some easy ways to make a paper board game for children. They will only need paper or cardboard, pencils, felt-tip pens, a ruler - a stencil, scissors and glue.

A very interesting entertainment for a child can be dominoes. The principle of entertainment is known and understandable to everyone, so questions should not arise about how to make a board game with your own hands. If you use self-adhesive multi-colored paper, then the chips can be made very quickly. On children's cards it is better to draw multi-colored geometric shapes, so you can teach a child during the game. For those who are preparing to go to first grade, you can make dominoes with letters and numbers.





Try to repeat the famous and simple game "Ladders". To do this, simply draw a paper on the squares, number them. Then, each decorate and draw the stairs in random order. The role of the chips can be played by small toys made of chocolate eggs, figures from a popular designer or just cute trinkets.
