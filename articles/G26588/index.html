Hoa Lam Hotel 2 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang, Nha Trang): hotel description, services, reviews

Vietnam today has become quite an attractive destination for Russian tourists. Compared with other Southeast Asian countries, it has a more developed tourism infrastructure. For visitors, Vietnam is a real rest. Here, tourists can combine beach activities with all water activities and educational excursions, from natural, historical and even folklore.



Great stay

Vietnam is attractive for Russians by cheapness. In the local cafes and restaurants you can eat deliciously and inexpensively, besides, the cost of many goods from well-known world brands that have opened factories in this Asian country is much lower than anywhere else. So excellent shopping is provided for tourists.

For some time now, our compatriots have chosen one of the most popular Vietnamese resorts - Nha Trang, or, as the locals call it here, Nha Trang. It has long been included in the list of most visited cities in the world. Nha Trang (or Nha Trang) is equally suitable for lovers of outdoor activities, including sightseeing, and for those who prefer a measured rest. Including for travelers with young children. That is why the hotel business in Nha Trang is at a fairly high level, developing so far.

There are a lot of entertainments in the city: bars, restaurants, cafes, discos, there are excellent massage parlors and, of course, a huge selection of hotels, starting with one- and ending with five-star apartments. Among them, the last place is occupied by the Hoa Lam Hotel. The 2 stars this hotel is awarded fully correspond to the level of services that it offers.



Description

The hotel is three hundred meters from the famous Nha Trang Beach. Tour operators, he is positioned as a relatively inexpensive and convenient for a good rest, combined with excursions. Nearby are the largest sailing center and club in the country.

Hotel Hoa Lam Hotel 2 * was built in 2010. The last major renovation in it was carried out in 2015. Each year, furniture in the rooms, sanitary ware are updated, minor repairs are carried out.

The hotel is located at 14B Biet Thu Nha Trang. The distance to the international airport is forty kilometers.

Infrastructure and Services

The hotel has almost no territory, and this is not surprising, since it is urban. There is an elevator in its eight-story building. In the lobby there is a small shop, a left-luggage office, an exchange office, a depository safe. The Hoa Lam Hotel 2 * has a parking lot, for which you have to pay separately two and a half dollars per day, laundry, bike rental and a tour desk.

The reception is open around the clock. Concierge service is available. Here you can order a transfer to the airport for an additional fee, call a taxi.

Guests can enjoy freshly made juices from local exotic fruits on the open veranda located in front of the hotel.

Housing stock

Hoa Lam Hotel 2 * is considered small. In its only building there are only twenty four standard and deluxe rooms. The rooms have two single beds or one double. Depending on the category, the area is respectively: for the standard - twenty, for deluxe - twenty-eight square meters.



They also include direct-dial telephones, flat-screen cable TVs, seating areas with armchairs and a coffee table, dressing table and wardrobe, luggage rack, fridge and air conditioning. For using the minibar should be paid extra.

All rooms have balconies with city views. Bathrooms combined. They have a shower and a hairdryer. Deluxe rooms also include bathrobes and slippers.

Marble tiles on the floor. The furniture is designed in the same style, the windows - dark curtains. Dryers are installed on the balconies.

Bed linen is changed every three days, cleaning is carried out daily. Maids for small tips will delight tourists with their skill in making different animals from towels.

Nutrition

The hotel provides only paid service "breakfast in the room." Residents can eat in one of the nearby cafes or restaurants. It should be noted that the prices here are quite low, in addition, judging by the reviews, there are tasty and inexpensive meat and seafood. For example, for two hundred dongs in a nearby small cafe called Local, the three of you can have a hearty meal. In the small restaurant "Veranda" you can taste snake and crocodile meat inexpensively.



In addition, in the lobby there is a small shop where there is always fresh fruit and sweets. If desired, you can also order freshly squeezed juice from local exotic fruits.

Beach

Distance to the coast - three hundred meters from Hoa Lam Hotel 2 *. Vietnam is famous for its sandy beaches. Nha Trang is no exception. The road from the hotel passes along the street, where there are many cafes and restaurants, as well as shops. Nha Trang beach is municipal, only small areas on the coast are owned by large fashionable hotels.

The entrance to the water is comfortable, gentle. Beach cover - white fine sand. For umbrellas, sunbeds and trestle beds you need to pay separately.

Tourists need to consider that from mid-November to the end of February, the most unfavorable season for swimming begins here. Entry into the water is not allowed due to the presence of high waves.

What to see

In the city of Nha Trang there is a mud bath and two mineral springs. Those who wish can rent bicycles in the hotel lobby and explore all local attractions on their own. Convenient location is one of the advantages of Hoa Lam Hotel 2 *. Nha Trang is a city where there are a lot of attractions that must be seen by those who come to this resort. These are the towers of the Ponagar Cham, built at the end of the thirteenth century by the ruler of Jaya Simkhavarman, and the Longshon Pagoda, behind which on the top of the hill there is a huge Buddha sitting on a lotus flower.



Those who wish can go to the largest in Nha Trang and, accordingly, the most visited by tourists Cho Dam market. Of course, it cannot be called cheap, since the prices here are designed specifically for visitors, but on this market you can buy almost everything, after bargaining and lowering the price.

If twenty minutes to go north from the city center, then you can walk to Cape Hong Chong or Stone Garden. Representing a huge heap of boulders of an unusual shape, this place has an unknown origin and is an amazing creation of mother nature.

Entertainment

In the hotel Hoa Lam Hotel 2 * they are not due to lack of territory. But within walking distance are Nha Trang Beach and Nha Trang Night Market. Those who love diving can use the services of diving centers, which are oriented, among others, to beginners. The main dive sites are around the island of Moon. Here you can see not only stunning beautiful small fish and jellyfish, but also moray eels, armored and even barracudas. Under the water, the island of Moon has more than four hundred species of coral - soft and hard, which form real gardens.

A twenty-minute drive from Hoa Lam Hotel 2 * is the cable car, which runs directly above the sea, on which you can reach the huge water park "Winterland". There are many water activities, including slides. Many tourists specially come to Nha Trang to visit the "Winterland", which also has an aquarium and a large number of attractions.

Reviews

An inexpensive hotel that is suitable for those who are not going to spend the whole day in a room with a TV remote control ... That's how many of our compatriots speak of Hoa Lam Hotel 2 *. Reviews of Russians in some issues vary, but one thing is common to all: a great price for the services provided.

Of course, lovers of luxury and pathos will not like it here, but you need to understand that the affordable cost of living cannot correspond to the amenities that are available in local fashionable hotels.

In all reviews, Russians warmly respond to the hotel owner and staff. Always smiling and friendly, they readily solve all problems.

And although the furniture in the rooms is not new, but they have everything you need for a comfortable pastime.

Some did not like the small areas of standard rooms, as well as the lack of services designed for small children. However, it is rather the fault of tour operators who do not warn their customers that the hotel is not suitable for families. The lack of territory is also perceived differently by our compatriots. Most Russians spent very little time at Hoa Lam Hotel 2 *, especially since tourists have to eat in a restaurant or cafe. Most vacationers are either on the beach or visiting local attractions, which are abundant in Nha Trang.



As for food, the hotel provides it only as a paid service "breakfast in the room." But this is practically not upsetting for any of the Russians, since there are many places around where you can eat cheaply, having got acquainted with the dishes of Vietnamese and Asian cuisine.

The beach did not cause any complaints, and although it is three hundred meters away, the road passes by local shops and cafes, and therefore does not create inconvenience.

Having summarized the reviews, we can say that most Russians have remained quite a given hotel, moreover, they recommend choosing Hoa Lam Hotel 2 * in Nha Trang - convenient both in terms of price and location.
