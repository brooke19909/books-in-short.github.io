DIY cocoon hanging chair: workshop

Each of us seeks to equip the interior of the room in such a way that it was not only cozy, but also comfortable. This is quite logical, because after hard working days I want to relax in a comfortable environment so that nothing bothers. Such a favorite place can be a cocoon chair.

Previously, such designs were used only in suburban areas, but today it has become fashionable to hang them in urban dwellings. And why not, if space allows?

A hanging cocoon chair is not cheap, so it is not available to every ordinary resident. How to be Everything is obvious: you can make a chair with your own hands, because it is not as difficult as it might seem at first glance.





What is a cocoon hanging chair for?

Such a chair is simply necessary for those who like to swing. Comfortably sitting on the pillows, with a book in hand, slowly swaying, you can immerse yourself in a world of peace.

Thanks to the design in the chair you can retire, hide from others and relax calmly. This is especially important for those who value their personal space.

The cocoon chair will become an indispensable element for those who prefer the boho style in the interior.

Types of Hanging Armchairs

  • Armchair with a rigid frame.

The frame is usually made from rattan and vines, in some cases from acrylic and plastic. Cushions and small mattresses are used as seats.

  • Chair hammock.

The design of such a chair is similar to hammocks, the only difference is in size and fixtures.

  • Cocoon chair.

A feature of the model is that 3/4 seat is closed. Basically, the walls are made woven in the style of macrame.





  • A drop chair.

The model is similar to a suspended house, especially suitable for installation in a children's room.

  • Armchair on the counter.

The main feature in the mount - due to the wide stable legs there is no need to mount it to the ceiling, the design is placed on the floor. The advantage of such a chair is that it can be placed anywhere in the house.

Features of creating a hanging chair

Today, there are more and more craftsmen who managed to make a hanging cocoon chair with their own hands, a master class in the manufacture of such a construction tells in detail about this process.





A distinctive feature of the chair is its similarity to a spider cocoon. Such an element will decorate absolutely any interior and become a favorite vacation spot. In its structure it is an unfinished ball braided with ropes.

The simplest creation technology is as follows:

  • First of all, a frame is created. As a rule, for this, 2-3 hoops of the same size are used, which are interconnected so that a ball is obtained. To keep the ball better in shape, several more half rings are set inside.
  • To make it comfortable to sit in the chair, you need to put the seat there. It can be a pillow, a plate, or you can just weave it.
  • In order to be able to sit quietly in the ball and not fall through, it is necessary to fit it with a net. There are also several options: you can buy a ready-made net, you can weave yourself, or you can just fit a chair with a rope.
  • The chair must be hung from the ceiling. This will not be a problem if you initially provide hooks or brackets on the product, through which you can attach the chair. After all, the main thing in a hanging chair is the ability to swing from side to side. In some cases, a spring is used so that it can swing in addition up and down.

How to make a macrame chair

Under the power of everyone to make a cocoon chair with their own hands, a master class of the process itself, the main thing is to have it at hand.





You will need:

  • two metal hoops with a diameter of 90 and 110 cm, section from 35 mm;
  • polyester cord with a diameter of 4.5-5 mm;
  • hook for knitting No. 8-9;
  • slings - 12 m;
  • 2 wooden rods 60-80 cm;
  • tape measure, scissors.

The bottom of the chair can be crocheted or weaved using the macrame technique.

First of all, it is necessary to wrap the hoop with a cord, tying every tenth knot.

Starting from the center, a tight knit circle of air loops and single crochets is crocheted. After 6-7 laps, you can proceed to the manufacture of a thick, viscous seat, as well as a back that can be made into a net.

The knitted product in the finished form should not reach the edges of the circle by 10 cm. Knitting will require about 120-160 m of cord.

To fix the seat on the hoop is from the side of tight knitting, at this time it is necessary to evenly stretch the napkin along the diameter of the hoop.





Making a Seat Outboard Chair

The macrame seat is much more economical, you will need the following parts of the cord:

  • 8 pieces of 6 m;
  • 4 to 5;
  • 4 to 4.5;
  • 2 to 4.

Now you need to place the workpiece on the hoop. 8 threads are fastened in pairs in the center at a distance of 6 cm from each other. All others are fixed with the same distance on the sides.

To fix the cable, it is necessary to make a knot on each thread. We knit with the location of the knot at a distance of 6 cm, periodically we make a stretch.

In order for the cocoon chair to be with a fringe, it is necessary to lengthen each thread by a meter and do not cut the end.

How to make a frame and backrest

To make a cocoon hanging chair with your own hands, you need to take care of a solid frame. Wooden rods should be wrapped with a cord and inserted into the back, they should be fixed before knitting the back.

The hoops on the opposite side must be secured with a cord. The frame fits in the same way as the seat - a network of nodes.

The next step is the location of the slings on the seat and the suspension of the chair.





We make a hanging hammock chair

To make a frameless cocoon chair with your own hands, you will need to acquire the following materials:

  • two meters of dense fabric;
  • wooden stick;
  • a drill;
  • carabiners for attaching the rope;
  • climbing rope;
  • sewing accessories.

So, we fold the fabric in half, count from the upper corner of 18 cm, mark the triangle on the fabric to the lower corner, cut it off.

A fabric is pulled into the threaded ropes on both sides, and the ends of the cable are inserted into other holes of the stick and secured with a knot.

To fix the chair on the ceiling, a hook and two carbines are installed on it, the chair rope is threaded to the bottom.

It remains to put a pillow on the seat and you can relax.

Hanging chair with cover

In order to make your own cocoon chair with a cover, you will need:

  • 2 m thick tissue;
  • metal hoop with a diameter of 90 cm;
  • lightning 1 m;
  • carbine;
  • 10 m of rope;
  • sewing tools.

Fold the fabric in half and cut out a circle so that it is 25 cm larger than the hoop.

Sew a zipper.

At the edges of the product, cut 4 holes, grind.

We put the hoop in the case, insert the rope into the holes and attach it to the carabiner.

Choosing a cocoon chair is a matter of taste

The manufacturing options described above are not unique and unique. You can supplement existing technologies with your own ideas or even come up with something new. The main thing is to treat this process with imagination and desire.





Especially often you can find hanging chairs made of twigs, wooden slats.

Instead of hoops for the frame, you can use anything that comes to your head or comes under your arm. As a weaving, you can also choose absolutely any options, whether it be ordinary ropes, fabric or a fishing net.

Buy a cocoon chair or do it yourself - it's up to you anyway. The variety of materials on the market allows you to create hanging chairs from everything that only your heart desires. Recently, more and more attention has been paid to products made from natural materials, they do not harm the environment and owners and looks aesthetically pleasing. So, for example, a rattan cocoon chair is a great option.

Previously, such structures were installed, as a rule, in country houses and on personal plots, but in modern times they have become frequent guests in modern apartment designs.

Having made a hanging cocoon chair with your own hands, you can create a master class after this event yourself. Then a lot of people who did not dare to make such a seemingly complex element of decor would follow your example.
