Do you need a beautiful and comfortable apron for your kitchen? With your own hands we will sew it!

There are never too many kitchen aprons. Every woman agrees with this statement. The vocation of the apron is to help the housewife protect her body and clothes from pollution during cooking. Therefore, this kitchen accessory should be sewn so as to cover the torso as much as possible. Not all industrial aprons meet this requirement. But it does not matter. Each hostess can sew an apron for the kitchen with her own hands. How to do it right, we will describe in this article.



Preparatory stage

To independently sew an apron for the kitchen, you need preliminary preparation, which includes the selection of materials and tools. For work you will need:

  • fabric (cotton, linen, thin raincoat fabric);
  • wide braid;
  • threads
  • centimeter;
  • pins
  • a piece of chalk;
  • sewing machine;
  • scissors.

Take measurements and cut out the pattern

Before you start to cut and sew an apron for the kitchen with your own hands, you must correctly take measurements and perform a pattern. To do this, go through the following steps.

  1. Measure the width of the hips, determine the length of the product you want from the top to the waist line and from the last to the bottom.
  2. In accordance with the obtained dimensions, a rectangular pattern of the apron for the kitchen is performed: thigh volume + 5 cm per hem, total length of the product + 10 cm (5 cm each on the top and bottom for the hem).
  3. Fold a rectangular piece of fabric in half in length and determine for yourself where the top of the product will be and where the bottom will be.
  4. Select the line of the belt. To do this, draw a perpendicular straight line from the side edge to the top and measure the length of the product to the waist along the fold. Add another 5 cm, mark with a pencil. Divide the length of the top edge in half and also mark. Connect these two points together. Pin the fabric along the length of the line.
  5. Cut the fabric along the fold lines and unfold the product. The cut off parts can be used for sewing pockets.

Tailoring

For those who are just mastering the sewing technology, we recommend that you first make all the connections manually with stitches. Put a braid on your shoulders, align its ends so that they lie on your chest. Attach the pattern of the apron to the body and attach the braid to the right places with pins. Then everything is simple: sew a border around the edges of the product. Its long ends will serve as ties.





Do the pockets on your own apron for your kitchen. To do this, from the pieces of fabric, make a pattern of one or two parts of your desired shape and size, bend the edges and sew on the product. Now sew all the seams on a typewriter. You can decorate the product with embroidery, applique from fabric.

We sew an apron for the kitchen with our own hands: all the pros

Hand-made things are always original and exclusive. Handmade aprons are no exception and have a lot of advantages. Firstly, you will sew such a kitchen accessory exactly in size in your desired color scheme. You can also adjust the length of the product yourself. Secondly, it will be much cheaper to sew an apron on your own than to buy in the market or in the store. Moreover, for its manufacture you can use old things (dresses, robes, shirts). Well, and thirdly, needlework always brings reassurance, satisfaction and positive emotions, and this is so important for your health and well-being. Create with pleasure, good results for you!
