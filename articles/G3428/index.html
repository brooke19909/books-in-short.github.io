Hotel Kemalhan Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Alanya): description and reviews of tourists

Alanya is a lively and beautifully developed region in Turkey, which every year comes to visit more and more tourists. All of them get for themselves permits in travel agencies, which provide for accommodation in hotels of different levels. Often, operators offer holidays in one of the most decent hotels in the region - Kemalhan Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Alanya).



general information

This place offers a decent vacation in a large modern building, which is located within walking distance from the sea. Kemalhan Beach Hotel 4 * (ex. Merlin Beach Hotel) was built in 2009, in accordance with the project of a team of talented architects. During the entire existence of the hotel, small renovations have already been held twice in the building and on the territory, during which the technical equipment of the rooms was improved, as well as the service system was improved.

Today, professionals in the tourism business recommend this place for a family vacation, as well as for trips with your loved one.



Where is?

Kemalhan Beach Hotel is located in Turkey, in one of the country's largest resort regions - Alanya, literally four kilometers from its center. Not far from Kemalhan Beach is a small resort town of Kestel, where a considerable number of places of interest for tourists are concentrated - they usually come here for a cultural vacation.

Kemalhan Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Alanya) is not on the first coastline, but at a distance of 70 meters from it. The hotel and the sea are separated by a highway, which can always be passed through the underpass.

As for transport hubs, in the vicinity of this resting place there are two large airports that are international. One of them is located in Alanya (30 km), and the second is in Antalya (140 km).

Rooms

In total, the hotel has 99 comfortable, modern rooms. All of them are defined in one category - standard - and offer high-quality furniture, installed in a minimum quantity, for convenient accommodation of tourists.

Each room has a private bathroom, furnished with modern fixtures, the set of which has a shower, a bathroom and a washbasin. There are also several bathrobes and bath towels. If desired, guests can use the proposed set of soap supplies.

Since one of the last renovations, individual air conditioners have appeared in the hotel's apartments, which allow guests to feel as comfortable as possible in any weather - with their help the temperature is regulated.

Each room has a small area of ‚Äã‚Äã20 square meters. m - and is equipped with a personal balcony.



Room furniture

Each room is equipped with modern furniture, which can accommodate two guests. It is created from quality materials, among which the advantage is clearly given to wood.

Here, tourists can stay in two single beds or one double. All of them are quite soft and tall, have orthopedic mattresses and covered with linen from natural fabrics. Between the sleeping places (if there are two beds) there is a cabinet where vacationers can store a small amount of personal belongings. If desired, they can use the working area, which is represented by a desk and a chair.





A TV is located opposite the beds. According to tourists, it broadcasts several Russian-language channels as part of a cable broadcasting system. Also here, for a fee, the opportunity to use a minibar and a safe is provided - the rental fee is calculated once a week and amounts to about 600 rubles.



Entertainment

In their reviews of Kemalhan Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Alanya), tourists often say that when they stay at the hotel they do not have to be sad. All this is due to the availability of entertainment opportunities on its territory. One of them is daily evening discos in which absolutely everything can take part, and absolutely free of charge. They are carried out in the open air, within the aquazone. During their conduct, animators show three of their programs for the entire hotel. In the daytime, guests can participate in competitions that also take place near the pool and at outdoor sports grounds.

Fans of a more relaxing holiday prefer to play billiards in the well-equipped room.

Nutrition

Fans of delicious food often leave in their comments an opinion on the possibility of food at the Kemalhan Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Alanya). The best place to get a rating for this service group is because it provides guests with delicious food prepared in the best traditions of Turkish and European cuisine. Travelers pay special attention to those dishes that are prepared with seafood - many recommend recommending them for sure, talking about the fact that they have extraordinary taste.

The main restaurant here serves a daily buffet, which guests can visit three times a day. As part of the All Inclusive concept, you can enjoy original, locally made drinks.

In addition to the restaurant, Kemalhan Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Alanya) has three bars. They offer a great selection of salads and desserts. In addition, they can surprise you with original cocktails, as well as alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. The list includes coffee and tea.



Aquazone

One of the favorite places for tourists in this place to relax is the aquazone, which offers not only active, but also relaxing vacations. Its territory is represented by a large sunny terrace, located in the open. Here, under umbrellas, on fairly comfortable sunbeds, vacationers can sit down and drink a glass of cocktail after water procedures.

In its very center is a swimming pool that is filled with purified fresh water. It warms up under the influence of sunlight, which is hardly possible in the winter.



Beach

During the holiday season, many travelers try to spend more time in the coastline area. Here the hotel has its own beach, the area of ‚Äã‚Äãwhich is completely covered with small pebbles and sand. This place is perfectly landscaped for guests of different ages. Many parents note that it is quite safe to be here with children.

For adults, there are several dozens of sunbeds on the beach, as well as umbrellas that protect sun lovers from burns. Vacationers often note that, heading here, it is advisable to take a towel from the body, since there are no points of issue on the coastline.

Spa

The spa is often mentioned in various guest reviews. They talk about the fact that only masters of their craft work in this place. This is especially true for massage therapists, able to immerse the client of any gender and age in a state of complete relaxation.

The Kemalhan Beach Hotel 4 * ("Kemalhan Beach Hotel") also has a beautiful thermal zone located in the spa center. It consists of a sauna and hammam, built according to the best Turkish traditions. Here, each guest has the opportunity to relax as hot as possible. Many travelers recommend staying here, without fail to use the services of parmesisters - with their help the process of soaring can be made more enjoyable.

All services provided in the spa center of Kemalhan Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Alanya) are paid, they are charged hourly.

Sport

Lovers of an active lifestyle also find something to do while staying at this hotel. Here, guests are offered active water sports. A separate stream of comments from travelers is paid to the aquaclub, which is located on the beach of the Kemalhan Beach 4 * hotel (Turkey / Alanya). The prices for its services, as vacationers note, are quite reasonable. For a separate payment, equipment for sports can be provided, as well as the services of instructors who can teach surfing, water skiing or diving.

If you wish to go sightseeing, guests can rent bicycles. They are very convenient to explore the surroundings of Alanya.

Prices

Kemalhan Beach Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Kestel) offers a pretty decent vacation at affordable prices. Perhaps this is why the place is so popular among travelers from around the world. The average cost of a weekly tour for two here is about 30-35 thousand rubles. Travelers are especially pleased that the standard package for such a price without fail includes all transportation costs, including transfer. The package provides the concept of "All inclusive", which also includes food.

After the rest, tourists often share their impressions gained during their stay at the hotel. They tell not only about the excellent service, but also about the interior in the rooms, about the attractions located in the district, they recommend visiting some places. Such comments are often supported by vivid photographs that were taken during the rest.
