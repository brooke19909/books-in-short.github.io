Communicator - what is it? How does it differ from a smartphone

Today you will not surprise anyone with ultra-modern mobile devices. In the consumer market, the range of gadgets is so large that sometimes you just get lost when choosing a phone, smartphone or the same communicator. But in fact, these are all devices that have completely different functions. So, let's figure it out: phone, smartphone and communicator - what is it?

Telephone

The most ordinary phone can not boast, unlike the above brothers, the presence of multifunctionality. Its main purpose is calls, MMS, SMS, Internet access. On mobile phones, as a rule, their own individual operating system is installed, which is not visible to the user.



In a regular mobile device, you cannot perform several actions at the same time, for example, write SMS and listen to music.

Smartphone

If you translate the word smartphone from English literally, it means ‚Äúsmart phone‚Äù. On these gadgets installed their own, more functional OSes. Such a system allows you to quickly and seamlessly work on the Internet. Smartphones are phones that are closest to PC functionality. In such devices, applications are installed that allow you to make presentations, work with documents, read electronic books and so on. And you can open multiple applications at the same time. Naturally, the question immediately arises: what is the communicator?





Communicator

This device is a real pocket laptop. Only this mini-computer has built-in mobile phone functions that allow you to make calls, write SMS and so on. On communicators, a more serious operating system is installed: Windows Mobile (smartphones are running Android OS, with the exception of Apple's smartphone, it has its own proprietary OS - iOS).

Quite often, in the technical specifications, the abbreviation QWERTY is noteworthy when choosing a device such as a communicator. What it is? This is the name of the keyboard of a mini-PC, the panel of which extends from the side. Currently, all handheld computers are also equipped with a touch screen.

For devices such as communicators, prices will be an order of magnitude higher than for the same smartphones (for example, the cost of HTC models varies from 7,500 rubles to 19,000 rubles). But again, it all depends on the manufacturer. I would like to make a small digression to pay attention to a series of low-cost mobile devices. Developers do not stop at manufacturing only smartphones, the proposed models have tablets and laptops, you can also find a telephone communicator.





Summary

If you compare all three devices discussed above, you can say without a shadow of a doubt that the device will be more powerful and functional than the same smartphone. For some reason, it is generally accepted that such gadgets are more suitable for business people with an active lifestyle. But this is not entirely true, communicators will be useful both to students and those who have to spend a lot of time on the Internet while on the road. This compact gadget will be able to save space in your bag, in addition, it is quite relevant today. After reading this article, you can now probably answer the question: "Communicator - what is it?" And at the same time share useful information with friends and acquaintances.
