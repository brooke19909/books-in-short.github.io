Hotel Kalia Beach Hotel Gouves 3 *, (Greece / Crete): description, services, reviews

People who are going to spend a vacation on the seashore somewhere in Europe often turn their eyes to Greece. After all, this country managed to become famous not only for its great history, but also for its picturesque beaches and good service. In most cases, tourists prefer the island part of the country, namely Crete. Of course, hundreds of hotels take visitors to the island. And one of the good places is considered to be the Kalia Beach Hotel Gouves.

Of course, first you need to find out as much as possible about the chosen place. Does the hotel really provide good living conditions and what impressions do tourists have after a vacation?

Kalia Beach Hotel Gouves (Greece): location and infrastructure





As you know, when choosing a hotel you need to pay attention to its location. A small hotel complex Kalia Beach Hotel Gouves 3 * is located in a quiet area, in the vicinity of the resort village of Gouves. This is a small village, which for the most part consists of boarding houses, hotels, shops and cafes. The distance to the beach is about 120 meters, which is convenient. By the way, the airport is about 15 km away - the road to the hotel takes only 15-20 minutes by taxi or comfortable transfer.

The hotel itself consists of a main two-story building (here, of course, there is a reception, restaurant, places for relaxation and some of the rooms), as well as a complex of compact cottages and small bungalows. The hotel area is well maintained, clean and beautiful. There are terraces, pools and sports grounds. The guests respond positively about this place and say that spending time in the yard is really nice.

What do the numbers look like? Information about rooms and photos





Hotel Kalia Beach Hotel Gouves 3 * is small and compact. Nevertheless, there are 75 rooms here - these are mainly standard double rooms (an extra bed can be delivered here on request), although there are several more spacious deluxe rooms. The atmosphere is straightforward, but guests can use comfortable furniture and relax on a soft bed. It features a private balcony (on the ground floor there is a veranda with access to the hotel).

You can count on some other amenities, in particular your own bathroom (shower, toilet, sink), as well as household appliances. Guests can use the small but powerful air conditioning. There is also a radio and telephone. Refrigerators are not available in all rooms, but at the request of guests they are necessarily delivered. The same applies to TV, as most tourists return to the hotel only for a night's rest.

Travelers who have already managed to visit this hotel say that the rooms are very clean. Maids clean the rooms and change towels every day. Bed linen is changed every 3-4 days.

Dining for hotel guests: what to count on?





The Kalia Beach Hotel Gouves hotel complex offers its guests an all-inclusive service - good food. On the other hand, you have the right to refuse three meals a day and eat in any cafe you like (there are enough of them in the vicinity) without tying yourself to the hotel.

In any case, you are always welcome at the local restaurant. And the menu, as the reviews say, is not bad. Breakfast includes pancakes, toast, jam, cereals, salads and fresh fruit. The choice of dishes for lunch and dinner is more diverse - visitors are served different types of meat and fish, fresh seafood and incredibly delicious desserts (from time to time there is also homemade ice cream). In the hotel bar, residents are offered different wines, delicious Cretan beer, as well as different cocktails.

Beach: distance, amenities and entertainment





As already mentioned, Kalia Beach Hotel Gouves is located near the beach. The distance is about 120 m, a road leads to the shore. This is a public coastal strip, so renting a sun lounger and an umbrella (if necessary) is paid separately. The beach here is very beautiful, and the entrance to the sea is convenient.

Of course, tourists here can spend time more actively, boating, water skiing and boats. There is a diving center where equipment is rented and beginners are trained. The coast is also suitable for windsurfing and sailing.

Hotel Kalia Beach Hotel Gouves 3 * (Crete): leisure and additional services for tourists





Can a tourist living in this hotel count on additional services? Of course, yes. The hotel has its own covered parking (garage), as well as a point where you can rent a car or moped. There is a small shop where you can buy interesting souvenirs and company gifts from the hotel. And in a small shopping center they sell food and household items.

In the courtyard of the hotel there is a swimming pool with a specially equipped and separate children's section. There are umbrellas and comfortable sun loungers around. By the way, there is also a restaurant terrace where you can eat in the fresh air.

As for leisure, guests have a good time playing table tennis. There are tennis courts where you can even take a short training course. There is a video room and billiards tables. There is a playground with swings for little tourists. In the evenings, live music is often played here, during the high season there are various performances for guests.

Excursions are one of the most popular attractions. After all, Crete is one of the most interesting islands, where a unique culture arose in due time. You can visit the ancient temples and even the ruins of the ancient labyrinth.

How do travelers respond about the hotel?

Experienced tourists know that when choosing a hotel you need to pay attention not only to official information, but also to the opinions of travelers. What does the Kalia Beach Hotel Gouves say? The reviews are for the most part really positive. Tourists believe that this is a good hotel, which is perfect for a relaxing holiday in peace and comfort.

The rooms are large, bright and very clean. Furniture is not new everywhere, but serviceable and comfortable, and household appliances work like clockwork. There is always water in the bathroom. Decent food. The menu may not always be varied, but the dishes are incredibly tasty and fresh.

The hotel has other advantages, in particular the proximity to the beautiful beach, as well as low prices for accommodation. This place is suitable for tourists who want to relax economically without losing quality of service.
