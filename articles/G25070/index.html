Features of the resort area

The main criterion according to which travelers choose Kemer among other resorts in Turkey is its proximity to the international airport, a large-pebble beach, a clean and transparent Mediterranean Sea even near the coast (the water does not become muddy from bathing, as on sandy beaches), mountainous terrain with numerous pine trees that grow here almost on the rocks. The "Stone Belt" extends throughout the Antalya coast.

Beldibi area: description, location, hotel base

The international airport is just 15 km away. This criterion is a priority for tourists who do not want to bore themselves with an additional long transfer after the flight. The village is located west of Antalya and is divided into the first, second and third districts.





Over the past few years, investment injections in Beldibi have contributed to the creation of more comfortable hotels with developed infrastructure, private beaches, entertainment venues and shopping centers. The main infrastructure is located along the main street of Ataturk, which stretches parallel to the coast at a distance of about 7 km. Hotels in Beldibi (Kemer) - various categories: from economical and simple 1- and 2-star hotels to fashionable 5 * Rixos complexes. Prices depend on the number of stars, quality of service, services, food and rooms, distance from the beach.

In the village itself, tourists can go to numerous shops with souvenirs, leather goods, oriental sweets. The restaurants and cafes offer amazing national dishes, which are worth drinking local pomegranate wine. The main entertainment venues are located in the hotels of Beldibi and Kemer.

Natural conditions

The village is located along the slopes of the Taurus Mountains, which are surrounded by greenery. Beautiful tropical trees and shrubs, fragrant exotic flowers and, of course, pine trees grow here. Mountain ionized air in combination with the sea and aromas of needles create all conditions not only for a good rest, but also for the healing of the body.





Beldibi beaches are considered one of the best in Turkey. Transparent and warm water, gentle entry into the sea attract tourists here. Among vacationers there are many families with children, youth.

What time is better to relax in Beldibi: weather at the resort

It is believed that this is a year-round resort. You can breathe in the healing sea air even in winter, and many hotels have heated salt-water pools. The weather in Beldibi allows you to swim from April to early November - this is the beach season. The Mediterranean Sea during this period warms up to +26 ... +27 , and the air temperature varies in the range +25 ... +40 . The hottest months are July and August, this should be taken into account for people who can‚Äôt tolerate the heat .

The cold period lasts from December to March: there may be rains or night frosts (extremely rare). The most favorable time for a beach holiday is the second half of September.
