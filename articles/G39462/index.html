Can everyone do massage?

There are contraindications to the Yamaguchi foot massager. They are exactly the same as with other types of massage. Before buying and using it is necessary to consult a doctor.

Categorically, the impact of the device is contraindicated in people who have the following problems:

  • inflammatory processes;
  • vascular disease;
  • damage to the skin;
  • tumors of various origins;
  • diseases of the joints and ligaments.
