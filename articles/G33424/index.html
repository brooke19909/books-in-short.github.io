Infrastructure

"Snow Pad" is part of an already created and equipped microdistrict. All its residents will have access to the necessary infrastructure. Kindergartens, schools, a clinic, a shopping and entertainment center have already been built in the district, cafes and bank branches have been opened. The first floors of the houses will be reserved for commercial facilities in the service sector: residents of the micro-district will have their own hairdresser, fitness center, atelier and much more.





The project provides for the improvement of the local area: here there will be modern playgrounds and sports grounds, areas for walking and recreation. Residents of the complex will not have to leave its territory to enjoy the delights of summer and winter outdoor recreation.

Car owners will appreciate the availability of ample parking with a sufficient number of parking spaces. The complex is under round-the-clock security, there is a video surveillance system that guarantees the necessary safety to car owners.
