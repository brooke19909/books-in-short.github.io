Louis Princess Beach 4 * (Cyprus / Larnaca) - photos, prices and reviews

It's no secret that in summer most people go on a trip. After all, I really want to take a break from the hard workdays. And Cyprus provides the tourist with a lot of ways to have a fun and profitable vacation.

Today, many travelers as a temporary haven choose the Princess Beach 4 hotel complex, located in a secluded corner of the island, surrounded by incredibly beautiful flowering gardens. So what awaits the traveler here? These questions are asked by many tourists.

Hotel Location





A clear sea, a pleasant climate and amazing beaches - this is what Cyprus can offer the traveler . Hotel Princess Beach 4 is located in one of the most picturesque corners of the island, near the sea, in the popular resort area called Larnaca. The distance to the city center is only 7 kilometers - you can get there by taxi or bus. An international airport is also located nearby, 10 km away. Near the hotel complex there are lots of restaurants, shopping centers and shops.

The hotel area is small, but clean, well-groomed, decorated with palm trees, decorative flowers, curly bridges and other masterpieces of landscape design. In the center there is a large five-story building, from which two side wings depart - there are bungalows here.

It is worth noting that the last time a thorough repair was carried out in 2008. Therefore, do not worry about the availability of modern technology - the atmosphere inside the hotel fully complies with all international standards.

What rooms does the hotel offer?

The hotel complex Princess Beach 4 is considered relatively small - on its territory there are 138 rooms. For the most part, these are standard rooms designed for 2 adults at a time. Nevertheless, there are also large family rooms located in small two-story bungalows - you can stay here with a big company.





Potential customers should be aware that the rooms here are really clean and spacious. Necessary furniture available. Each room has a small balcony or access to a spacious terrace - from here you can admire a side view of the sea or watch life by the pool.

Of course, in the rooms, guests will find all the necessary household appliances, without which a truly comfortable rest is impossible. Between May and October, the rooms are equipped with central air conditioning, and in the colder months they include a heating system. On TV, you can watch your favorite channels (there are also the most popular Russian ones). Guests can also use the telephone and radio. For a small fee, they will open a safe where it is convenient to store documents and valuable trifles. There is also a minibar, but drinks must be paid separately.

The bathroom, of course, is equipped with new, modern fixtures. Here guests can relax in the bath and take a shower - hot and cold water is supplied without interruption. There is a washbasin and a wall mirror. Guests can also use a hairdryer to dry their hair. And, of course, each client has a set of clean towels.

Also, you should know that the rooms are cleaned every day. Linen on the bed is changed very often. If necessary, you can order room service, but this service is paid separately.

Beach and water activities on the coast





Guests at Princess Beach 4 can use the municipal beach. It is located just fifty meters from the hotel building. The beach here is clean, well-groomed, covered with sand and pebbles (it is better to worry about appropriate shoes in advance). Despite the fact that the beach strip is narrow (its width is only 20 meters), comfortable sun loungers and deck chairs are available in sufficient quantities. Hotel guests can use them at no extra charge. Beach towels are issued by the pool, but on bail, which, of course, is then returned.

The entrance to the sea is convenient, and the aground stretches for several meters. Therefore, closer to the shore, the water is always warm, which is perfect for bathing children.

Of course, lovers of more active recreation will also find something to their liking. Here you will have the opportunity to ride a boat, catamaran, boat, water ski, etc. But it is worth noting that water activities are not included in the price - you have to pay for them separately.

Does the hotel offer additional amenities?

Of course, on the territory of Larnaca Princess Beach 4 they tried to create all the conditions for a truly comfortable stay for guests. Travelers using their own or rented transport can leave it in a small but carefully guarded parking lot.

Laundry and dry cleaning services are also available. Clothing care services are not included in the price, but the prices are not too high. You will also have the opportunity to visit the local gift shop, where you will be offered a lot of interesting and original souvenirs. The hotel has a wireless Internet connection, but this service also belongs to the category of paid.

And the hotel is often used for corporate and business leisure - there is a well-equipped business center, as well as a conference hall with a capacity of 100 people.

Entertainment and leisure of tourists





Of course, the hotel guests are waiting for fun entertainment. Near the residential building there is a large freshwater pool. Here, basking in the sun on a comfortable sunbed, you can not only get an even tan, but also chat with other guests.

For travelers who want to always keep fit, a spacious gym with new equipment has been created. There are also sports fields for playing futsal, basketball and volleyball. You can have a great time on the tennis court. And for clients regularly arranged group classes in yoga and aerobics.

Also, guests have the opportunity to use the sauna and jacuzzi. There are several massage rooms where for a small fee professionals will offer you their services.

There is always the opportunity to have fun with board games, including tennis, billiards, darts, etc. And in the evenings, a team of animators works with the guests. As already mentioned, the hotel regularly hosts theme nights, and the show program on such days corresponds to the dishes offered. Guests also have the opportunity to visit a wine tasting and enjoy the exquisite taste of local drinks. In addition, guests of the hotel are entertained with dance numbers, performances by local singing groups and other exciting performances.

Of course, this is not all that Princess Beach 4 can offer you. Larnaca is an excellent place that is suitable for both shopping and sightseeing. Here you will be helped to choose suitable tours and get acquainted with the incredibly interesting and mysterious history of the island.
