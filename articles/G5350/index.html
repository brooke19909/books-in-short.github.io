Koh Chang Island, Thailand: hotel reviews and experience

Koh Chang Island is part of Thailand and has long been chosen by travelers who value not only clean beaches, rich nature and delicious local food, but also a high level of service and comfort for the entire stay. Do you know about this place? If not, we offer an article with reviews about Koh Chang in Thailand.

general information

Koh Chang Island (Thailand) is a part of the famous Mu-Koh Chang Marine Park and is the third largest after Phuket and Samui. Only about six thousand people live on the island and all of them are engaged in the tourism business. Every day, hundreds of tourists arrive on the island, and helpful, friendly workers do everything possible to make them remember their holidays for a long time, leaving extremely pleasant memories. Of the attractions, Koh Chang boasts only the magnificent Klong Plu Waterfall, as well as the Orthodox Church named after Sergius of Radonezh. The main goal on the island is to enjoy the sun, the beach and ripe fruit for six months to come. Choosing a hotel will help reviews about Koh Chang, Thailand, those who have already been there.

"Paradise Resort and Spa"

This hotel is considered one of the best on the island of Koh Chang in Thailand, and reviews with photos confirm this. A happy owner of four stars, he daily lives up to his level. All hotel rooms are equipped with the latest technology and offer their guests a full range of necessary services in everyday life.

The room has air conditioning, which is a prerequisite in such a humid hot climate as Thai. In the relaxation area, you can get refreshing drinks in the private minibar and sit comfortably on the sofa in front of the plasma TV. You can start the morning with sports water treatments in the pool or with a contrast shower in the room, which has all the necessary bath accessories. Also, tourists can enjoy a wide range of dishes in the restaurant and sightseeing routes.

Hotel reviews

Reviews with photos about Chang in Thailand in general, and about the hotel in particular, are extremely positive. Of course, some will say that this is not surprising, because the cost of a room per night starts from four thousand rubles. However, hotel guests believe that the cost is equal to quality. So, one of the hotel guests, vacationing with his family, praises only everything: the location, the kitchen, the structure of the hotel and the surrounding landscapes. Quiet, cozy, family place. However, the guest warns, it is important to be careful and look under your feet, as snakes can crawl out onto the clean paths in the sun. In this case, it is necessary to inform the hotel staff who are engaged in catching snakes.

"Marina Sands Resort"

The popular network has its representative office in Thailand. One of the best hotels offers its guests a full range of travel services, ranging from transfers from anywhere, whether it be the airport, bus or train station, to exotic dishes in the restaurant, prepared according to an individual recipe.

The location of the hotel is quite interesting. Nearby is the royal marina and amazingly beautiful coral reef, as well as five pristine beaches.

The hotel rooms are designed so that guests do not need anything. In the bathrooms you can find all the necessary bathing accessories, including bathrobes, slippers and towels, in the recreation area there is a plasma TV with a full package of satellite channels. The evening can be spent in the spa or pool, and then eat in the restaurant or bar. All food and drinks at this hotel can be ordered to your room just like at any other hotel on the island of Chang in Thailand. Reviews of tourists, photos and video greetings confirm the level of service.

Opinion of tourists about "Marina Sands Resort"

Those who prefer to change locations and go somewhere outside their home during their holidays know how pleasant it is to book a hotel and find there quality equal to the price. Positive, and, most importantly, real reviews from tourists about Koh Chang in Thailand are not uncommon. On the contrary, people who rested in these places speak of excellent, clear service.

Guests note comfortable living conditions: large beds, including single beds, daily general cleaning of rooms, as well as a clean beach and pool. The staff immediately solves the issues that arise, so guests have nothing to worry about, in addition, the hotel offers a choice of French, Italian, Thai, Japanese or German dishes. In terms of food, reviews of Koh Chang in Thailand extol the local cuisine, the one that the chefs know to the smallest detail. Guests praise the silence and heavenly views from the rooms.

"Paradise Bungalos"

It is impossible not to mention such an unusual hotel complex as the "Paradise Bungalow" on the island of Chang, Thailand. The reviews of tourists who spent unforgettable vacation weeks there dispel recent doubts about such an unusual place of settlement - Thai bungalows.

They are equipped with the latest technology. If you want to plunge into a hectic international life for several hours, you will find a plasma TV with a full package of satellite channels. There is free wireless Internet to scroll through your page on social networks on time. With particular delight, the guests of the hotel complex recall the pristine beaches within five minutes walk, magnificent lush plants, as well as the close proximity to elephants, which gives the vacation a unique shade. Nearby, as noted by tourists, many conveniently located shops with souvenirs, food and fresh fruit.

Alchemi

For travelers traveling alone and not burdened with luggage, this hotel will be the best vacation option on Koh Chang in Thailand. The reviews say that for a small fee the staff provides quality services: the room has air conditioning, which will save you from stuffiness at any time of the day, free wireless Internet. In the morning, guests can enjoy breakfast. A great advantage of the hotel, judging by the reviews, is the availability of non-smoking rooms.

There is a restaurant on site, the chefs will prepare dishes of local, French, Japanese or Italian cuisine, there is a bar with a wide selection of alcoholic and soft drinks. If you prefer to move around the island by transport and rent a motorcycle or car, this is not a problem either: free parking is available on site for guests. All guests note the cleanliness and comfort of a small hotel, which, however, has everything you need for a great holiday.

"Ci San Bungalow and Resort"

One of the favorite places to visit was and remains the island of Chang in Thailand. We provide reviews with photos in the article not only to show the beauty of the island, but also to explain to future travelers all the possibilities of excellent accommodation. One such option is the Sea San Bungalow and Resort. For those who are looking for the best value for money - this is an ideal option. Quiet, cozy place, surrounded by clean beaches and a magnificent park, even the most picky tourists like.

Each room has a plasma TV with a full package of satellite channels in case you do not miss a single episode of any show. On the terrace with magnificent views of the mountains and gardens you can relax in the evening, drink tea and listen to the birds singing and the sound of the ocean. To keep in touch with loved ones, there is free Wi-Fi. Tourists who have already visited this hotel note the cleanliness, cozy, homely atmosphere and helpful, competent staff.

Grand Three

This hotel is in the economy class, which does not prevent the staff from providing quality services to their guests. Each room has its own small terrace and a bathroom with everything you need. Each room also includes a plasma TV, fridge and air conditioning. Guests who spent their holidays there unanimously say that the place is ideal for families. Silence, rich nature, the proximity of the beach will fill all vacation days with carefree fun.

Tourists also note a useful room service. This is especially to the liking of those who spent all day on their feet exploring the island and returning to their room, want to have a quiet dinner, sitting on the couch by the TV. By the way, tourists note that this hotel provides free still water.

You can rent a bike or car from the hotel to explore all corners of the wonderful island in less time. You can spend your free time playing billiards or swimming in the pool.

Blue Lake Resort

Here where it is possible to touch the nature as much as possible on vacation, so it is in a small hotel "Blue Lake Resort". After all, the bungalows are built of quality wood, which makes staying in the rooms a real adventure. Each bungalow has Wi-Fi, satellite TV, a hot shower and a fridge. Guests can spend time on a private, equipped beach, order food in the room, enjoy all types of massage and spa treatments that the hotel offers its guests.

A feature of this hotel, guests call the opportunity to stay here with their wards, animals that you decide to bring with you. Many guests chose this hotel because of this feature, for which he receives many thanks. Some guests who combine leisure and work emphasize the convenience of location: Bangkok is only three hours from the hotel, which can be overcome even with a car rented from the hotel.

"Seabriz Kochang"

Nearby there are inexpensive restaurants with delicious local food, shops, and a twenty-minute clean beach. A feature of the hotel is its interior decoration. All furniture is made of hard wood, which gives the interiors a solid look.

Guests who have visited the hotel have an extremely positive experience, which they share in their reviews. For example, visitors note the excellent work of the staff, who meets and helps to accommodate as soon as you get out of the car. The administrator draws up new arrivals in a few minutes, and the staff delivers luggage immediately to the room.

Tourists also note the daily general cleaning and change of bed linen, the presence in the room of dishes and products for a pleasant evening tea party on the veranda overlooking the garden and the ocean that stretches far in the distance.
