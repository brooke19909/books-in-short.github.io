The great advantage of acquiring apartments in ZILART is the space of nature created here. Education Centre

Negative

I must admit that there are enough negative reviews about ZILART. Some of the new settlers emphasize the poor quality of work by builders, which affects comfortable living. For example, in many basements of already commissioned houses there is water, most likely, the proximity of the river or problems with drainage systems or communications affects. However, the developer does not react to this problem, no measures are taken to eliminate this scourge. And because of it, it is constantly damp in the apartments on the first floors of residential buildings, mold and fungus are already forming on the walls and floor. But to bring them out is almost impossible.

Buyers lose confidence in the developer when they find out that their home is connecting to communications on a temporary basis.

In terms of design, some customers are unsatisfied with architectural solutions. The facades seem too gray and gloomy. In addition, it is very far to go from the metro station - about thirty minutes. But transport accessibility in our time is one of the key factors when choosing an apartment in a particular area. The ability to quickly, quickly and without traffic jams to get anywhere in Moscow is very much appreciated. In this regard, this complex is significantly inferior to many housing complexes in the Russian capital. You have to get to the metro with transfers, which takes too much time.

According to many potential clients of the developer, the existing infrastructure is poor. In the immediate vicinity there is one large shopping center, but there is no more entertainment, nowhere to go, especially with a child. In the future, of course, they promise to build a dozen and a half kindergartens and developed infrastructure, but many already live in this complex, practically cut off from civilization. This is the trouble of most domestic developers when they first build and sell houses, only after that they take up the implementation of improvement projects around.

Also, interest holders are not satisfied with the quality of work that the developer provides. Flaws on the facades are urgently trying to cover up, but there are suspicions that in a couple of years this will result in serious problems for those who purchase apartments in these houses. In addition, the delivery of houses is constantly postponed, disrupting buyers all plans to start repairs and a possible relocation.

At the same time, the apartments are just a lot of money, but the work is moving very slowly, it seems that the company simply does not have enough resources to master such a large and large-scale project.
