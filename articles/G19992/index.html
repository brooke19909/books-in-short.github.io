PGS World Palace 5. An ideal place for tourists with children, couples and business people

Description: PGS World Palace 5 is located in the quiet village of Kirish, in the suburbs of the vibrant Turkish resort of Kemer. Antalya, located 60 km from the hotel, is transported every half hour.



The hotel, located between the sea and the pine-covered Toros ridges, offers a relaxing beach holiday amidst the dazzlingly beautiful nature of the eastern Mediterranean. PGS World Palace 5 is an ideal place for tourists with children, couples and business people. Built in the mid-90s, the hotel was last reconstructed in 2011.

Rooms: The large hotel has at its disposal 814 rooms accommodating up to 2,000 guests.

PGS World Palace 5 rooms are standard rooms, duplex family apartments and terrace suites. All of them are decorated in national traditions in combination with the latest technological achievements. The rooms have cozy balconies, parquet floors, a reliable safe and a stocked minibar. The rooms offer pleasant views: sea, fragrant garden or blue expanse of pools.



Luxurious suites with terraces, which serve as a solarium or a place to relax and eat, are designed for travelers with high requirements. You can enter the balcony from the bedroom or from the living room, which has a seating area with sofas and a work area with a desk.

Excellent facilities are provided at the hotel for families with children. Duplex rooms with a capacity of 5 people have a bedroom and a bath on each floor.

Eight rooms are equipped with special facilities for the disabled.

Meals: In the PGS World Palace 5, the Ultra All Inclusive service is practiced, which provides six meals a day, including, in addition to traditional meals, late dinner, early breakfast and soups.

Two buffet restaurants offer meals indoors or on the terrace. There are dishes of world cuisine, a variety of drinks, fruits and flour products.



A rich menu is presented in a la carte restaurants. These are exquisite wines and treats in a romantic Italian establishment. The exceptional taste of fresh seafood in the fish restaurant. Regional cuisine and live national music in Turkish. Unrivaled Chinese, Japanese and Thai dishes at the sushi bar.

For desserts and pastries, PGS World Palace guests are invited to the lobby pastry shop. In the Gozleme house you can try the traditional Turkish tortilla with filling, which is served with chilled Ayran. The pool bar serves drinks during the day and is the venue for evening music shows. Beer and mineral water are always abundant in the beach bar. The tea house serves Turkish coffee and tea during the day. The spa bar will delight you with fresh fruit drinks and mineral water. A disco bar will help quench your thirst during nightly fun. The coffee shop, with its original architecture and magnificent views, serves the best types of tea and coffee.



Beach: Guests are invited to relax on the sandy and pebble beaches of the Mediterranean, where towels and sunbeds are provided for free use.

Additional Information: Relaxing at PGS World Palace means endless entertainment and a carefree pastime.

From morning to evening, animators work at the hotel, involving guests in games, contests and competitions on water and land. In the evening, music shows and discos begin.

Little guests spend no less interesting time, for whom informative classes and outdoor games are held.

It is difficult to imagine a large resort complex without a SPA center with healthy procedures.

Five function rooms are designed to hold any kind of events, whether it is a business meeting, presentation, corporate banquet or family holiday.
