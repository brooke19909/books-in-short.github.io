Holiday Village "Yubileinaya" (Crimea): reviews, location, conditions and prices

How to choose from the many offers on the tourism market that is right for you? Let's try to figure it out. In recent years, the Crimean coast has become a kind of magnet for Russians. Older people want to remember their vacation in Soviet times, and young people are attracted by new and unknown. Judging by the reviews, the "Jubilee" recreation center in the Crimea is a great place to spend unforgettable summer days on the Black Sea coast.

Convenient location

The western coast of Crimea has especially clean beaches. The water in the Black Sea is more transparent, since the shores here are mostly pebble or sand and pebble. This criterion is noted as positive in the reviews about the Yubileynaya recreation center (Crimea, Nikolaevka). The village is very quiet, clean and calm.

At 40-50 m from the sea lies a beautiful street. The embankment, on it the complex is located, surrounded by a beautiful park with tropical trees. In the reviews about the recreation center "Yubileinaya" in the Crimea, they write that the road to your own sand and pebble beach takes no more than 5 minutes. In a short distance there is an amusement park with attractions that children love so much. The village center, where there is a market and shops, is a 15-minute walk. Buses to Bakhchisarai, Simferopol, Yalta and other resort towns regularly run from the village. The fare is about 50-300 rubles. one way (depending on distance).

Prices for accommodation and meals

The hostess of the complex is very welcoming and hospitable to her guests. Can be accommodated in wooden houses for 2, 3 or 4 places. They have beds, chairs, cabinets, wardrobes, electricity. Hot and cold showers, sinks and toilets are located on site. In the reviews about the recreation center "Yubileinaya" (Crimea), it is noted that the atmosphere of the rooms is rather modest, but cozy. Some compare this place with a country cottage, where everything is so cute and neat.

Three meals a day are offered to guests: the hostess herself prepares delicious dishes that are original in the quality of home-made ingredients. On the recreation center "Yubileinaya" in the Crimea (according to reviews) you can choose dishes for children: cereals, soufflé, light soups. Often served meat, pastries, vegetables and fruits. You can eat outdoors on the veranda, enjoying the beauty of the surrounding nature, sea views and fresh coastal air.

The price in the high season for an adult (along with complex nutrition) is 1400 rubles. Children under 6 years old can stay at the base for free (without providing a berth and meals). A child from 6 to 12 years old can get a 10% discount.

On the seashore there is an excellent bar where they cook delicious kebabs, serve alcoholic drinks, juices, snacks. In the center of the village you can go to a restaurant, cafe or snack bar.

Negative moments (according to reviews of the Yubileinaya recreation center in Crimea)

Most of the complaints were caused by the interior decoration of the rooms. Vacationers complained about the unsightly and old wallpaper, broken furniture. In addition, the rooms were poorly ventilated, there was a heavy and musty smell that spoiled the rest and mood.

Another negative characteristic of b / o was pricing. Many argue that the cost of uncomfortable houses is really overstated. Prices at the recreation center "Yubileinaya" in Crimea are much higher than in similar tourist complexes in this region. They also complain about the lack of a play area or playground for children. Single reviews mentioned rude treatment of guests by the hostess of the complex. It is noted that over time, communication was established and the vacation went well.

Positive reviews

The main plus is its convenient location. This consists in the close location of the beach (first coastline), an amusement park, market, shops, transport accessibility to other resort towns. Vacationers appreciated a clean, neat and comfortable area where you can safely spend your holidays in silence.

Most of all the pleasant words in the reviews relate to the culinary abilities of the hostess b / o. They note that they have not tried such delicious and satisfying cooking for a long time. All dishes are very fresh, cooked in excellent sanitary conditions.

The main positive aspect that can be summarized is that places in the complex must be booked in advance. High demand can not appear on the second-hand, if the living conditions there are poor quality.
