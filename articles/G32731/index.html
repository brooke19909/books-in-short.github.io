Hotel Tanka Village (Sardinia, Italy): description, services, reviews

If you dream of an elite vacation, then you need to buy tours to Sardinia. This beautiful Italian island is located in the Mediterranean Sea, 200 kilometers west of the shores of the Apennine Peninsula, and the island of Corsica, owned by France and known around the world as the birthplace of Napoleon, is only 12 km from it. By the way, tours to Sardinia are mostly mixed, they combine a beach holiday with an interesting excursion program. The resort area is interspersed with archaeological and nature reserves. For a beach holiday, the southern and southeastern shores are especially suitable. It is here that beautiful hotel complexes are located, on the signs of which 5 or 4 stars flaunt. By the way, 4-star hotels in terms of conditions and services are almost nothing inferior to five-star ones. For example, the Tanka Village Hotel (Sardinia), famous throughout the island, provides tourists with the highest level of service. At the same time, its main advantage is its convenient location and incredible beauty of the beach. That is why many tourists, choosing tours to Sardinia, prefer this comfortable hotel.



Sardinia

Many thousands of tourists come to Sardinia annually, and among them there are many fans of windsurfing, swimming, hiking, rock climbing and diving. The most intense month for the island is August. During this period, it is difficult to find at least one unbooked place in hotels or guesthouses of the coast, while in the corners of the island remote from the beach, no less interesting life is boiling, and the price for accommodation in guest houses is very low. Sardinia is autonomous, although officially owned by Italy. However, the locals, the Sardis, do not like to be called Italians, but consider themselves to be the chosen people belonging to the ancient Nuragic civilization, which is for many centuries ahead of the Etruscans - the ancestors of modern Italians. This is evidenced by stone monuments belonging to 1500 BC. e. The capital of the island is Cagliari - an ancient and very beautiful city with picturesque architecture.



How to get to Sardinia?

If you bought a tour to the popular hotel in the south of Sardinia, Tanka Village, then from Moscow to the island you will have to get either via Rome by Aeroflot or Alitalia airplanes, and then transfer to domestic flight to Cagliari, or by a direct charter flight, which happens only in high season. There is also a ferry service that connects Sardinia with nearby ports. As for the road to the Hotel Tanka Village (Sardinia), when you purchase a full package from a tour operator, you will be met at the airport and comfortably delivered to the hotel entrance.

Resorts in Sardinia

The best resorts of the island are located on the south coast. It is here that the aforementioned four-star Hotel Tanka Village (Italy / Sardinia) is located. In this part of the island, the beaches are fine sandy, and the snow-white hotel buildings are buried in the greenery of pine and palm groves. Here you can see many beautiful bungalows on the seashore, which can become a wonderful temporary nest for honeymooners. Sardinia is also attractive for diving enthusiasts. Near the coast there are many wrecks, and a journey through the depths of the sea turns into a real adventure. The best resorts of Sardinia are also Santa Margherita di Pula, Villasimius, Costa Smeralda and others.



Climate of Sardinia

As with most of the Mediterranean islands, the climate in Sardinia is subtropical. The holiday season here lasts quite a long time: from May to mid-November, that is, most of the year. The air temperature warms up as much as possible in August and reaches 34 degrees. Until mid-November it is about 22, but in the daytime the water is quite warm, and the most daring do not refuse to swim in the sea. The most pleasant weather happens in September. This is the real velvet season. In addition, at this time there are few vacationers with children, and this is due to the beginning of the school year.

Rooms

Hotel Tanka Village (Sardinia) has 903 places to accommodate, as already noted, these are mainly bungalows and apartments.   • Domus They are located in three 3-storey buildings, which are located 400 meters from the sea. Among them there are rooms of category Standard (45) and Junior Suite (84).
  • Domus standards - one-room rooms located on the third floor and have a wide terrace overlooking the garden. It can accommodate up to four people. And the rooms of the Domus Junior Suite are two-room and are located either on the 1st or on the 2nd floors. They consist of a bedroom and a living room. There are two bathrooms, as well as a terrace or patio (for those rooms that are located on the 1st floor).
  • Dimora Superior. These are two-room rooms, which are located 300 meters from the beach in 2-story buildings with an elevator. Next to them is a mini club. Dimora Superior consists of a bedroom and a living room, two bathrooms. It can accommodate a maximum of 5 people, but ideally 2 adults and 2 children.
  • The bungalows, which also come in five types: Casbah Standard, Monte Superior, Casbah Superior, Dimora Standard, Monte Garden. All of them are comfortable and are located either on the first beach line or next to the pool. They are also either two-room or one-room.

Tanka Village (Sardinia): guest reviews and ratings

Most vacationer stories about staying at the Tanka Village Hotel are positive. Tourists are also not happy with the fact that there is only one large restaurant on such a large territory, and also that on the beach, even for hotel guests, sun loungers and umbrellas are paid.
