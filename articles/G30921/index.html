Digital camera Sony Cyber-shot DSC-W810: description, shooting modes, reviews

Sony's compact line of cameras is regularly updated with new members of the family, in which previous qualities are improved and the latest technologies are embodied. Manufacturers of models of this class rarely decide on radical changes that allow owners to get higher quality pictures. Typically, the development of such devices occurs gradually and involves only cosmetic changes. In turn, the Sony Cyber-shot DSC-W810 camera showed a different approach to the evolution of the segment. The developers provided the camera with a more advanced matrix and introduced a rich set of various options and effects.

General information about the camera





The new generation of the popular line in all versions received a 20.1-megapixel CCD-matrix, thanks to which the quality of images was significantly improved. Perhaps this is the main advantage that distinguishes Sony digital cameras in the updated series. In addition, the cameras have the ability to record HD video, supplemented by the option of instant playback.

Also, the model has acquired an expanded list of adjustments and settings, among which there are many automatic functions. For example, using Beauty technology, a user can eliminate defects directly in a digital image after a photo shoot. The camera is also suitable for creative people who love all kinds of experiments. In addition to image effects, the Sony Cyber-shot DSC-W810 also has the ability to create panoramic shots. Previously, only professional devices had this ability, but now photographs with a 360-degree overview have become available to ordinary users.

Specifications





According to the main operational indicators, the device has some advantages over competitors. First of all, the Sony Cyber-shot DSC-W810 stands out with a good resolution for its price segment, but there are other advantages, which are confirmed by the technical characteristics:

  • The focal length of the lens is in the range from 26 to 130 mm.
  • Zoom features - up to 5x zoom.
  • The number of pixels is 20.1 million.
  • Resolution - 5152x3864.
  • The photosensitivity of the device is from 100 to 3200 ISO.
  • The diagonal of the LCD screen is 2.7 inches.
  • Built-in memory - 27 Mb.
  • Power - due to the built-in battery with a capacity of 630 mAh.
  • The video format is AVI.
  • Additional features - orientation sensor.
  • Dimensions of the device - 9.7 x 5.6 x 2.1 cm.
  • Mass - 111 gr.

The camera stands out not only for its functionality, but also for its appearance. A good technical filling is organically combined with compact dimensions. It is worth noting the stylistic advantages. In addition to the basic version, the Sony Cyber-shot DSC-W810 Black is also available in silver and pink.

Shooting modes





The series has previously pleased users with a rich enough arsenal of modes for shooting. In particular, the camera offers a wide range of options for working with white balance , as well as tools for operating in automatic format. The Sony Cyber-shot DSC-W810 also has a new modern mode called Party. It is recommended to use it in low light conditions - for example, in the evening or at night. Activating this option automatically adjusts the operating parameters of the camera to provide an improved flash. For this purpose, optimized ISO sensitivity settings are used, and color brightness and exposure are adjusted. As a result, the user can count on creating clear and vivid pictures even at night.

Positive camera reviews

The model demonstrates a rare example when the expansion of functionality in a budget device is welcomed by a fairly wide mass of users. The owners of the device note the benefits of focusing, the ability to shoot in panorama format, optimal photosensitivity and long battery life. True, with regard to the quality of the pictures, the reviews are quite restrained. Even despite the well-matched matrix, Sony digital cameras of this series lose in color reproduction, but show good detail. Good user reviews also apply to video recordings. Improving the HD format in the new members of the family has benefited not only the "picture", but also the sound. As a universal device for amateur photo and video sessions, this model is a good solution.





Negative reviews

With all the desire of Japanese developers to create a technologically breakthrough product in the budget segment, they failed to achieve a serious improvement in the quality of photographic materials. But if the disappointing characteristics of the images can be forgiven for the frankly low cost of the camera, then ergonomic problems have disappointed many owners of the Sony Cyber-shot DSC-W810. Reviews, in particular, note an inconvenient menu, although the controls are very compact and affordable. There are also complaints about the processor, which takes a long time to process the pictures taken and, with problems, goes into different modes. An inconvenient interface, coupled with the slowness of the technical stuffing - these are rare shortcomings of compact digital devices, but in this case, such problems are observed. Compensates them, again, the affordable price, stylish design and still good photo quality for its level.

Conclusion





Large manufacturers are cautiously approaching production in the budget segment. Few manage to combine high quality, ease of use and at the same time not to raise the price tag. The Japanese company is known as a reliable manufacturer of expensive, but high-quality and modern electronics. The Sony Cyber-shot DSC-W810, for obvious reasons, could not fully comply with these characteristics.
