Sanatorium Ordzhonikidze (Sochi). Sanatorium named after Ordzhonikidze: photos and reviews of tourists

Sanatorium Ordzhonikidze (Sochi) opens its doors to everyone who wants to spend a wonderful vacation by the sea, and at the same time improve their health. During their stay in the health resort, tourists have the opportunity to enjoy walks along the alleys of the local park, relax on benches, swim in the sea, live in comfortable rooms and undergo treatment in one of the best medical diagnostic centers in Sochi.

A bit of history ...

1934 is the year when it was planned to have a sanatorium in Sochi. Sergo Ordzhonikidze, who was then the People's Commissar of Heavy Industry, personally chose a place for a future recreation center. The planning and construction of the sanatorium was entrusted to the chief architect Ivan Kuznetsov, who organized a team of technicians and architects necessary to implement such a grandiose project. Jung S.A. was appointed to the post of construction manager, and Zaretsky K.S. became the chief engineer of the project Design took very little time, in 1935 the construction of the first buildings had already begun. At the end of 1937, the sanatorium received the first vacationers. A little later, a fountain was erected on the territory of the recreation center.

The Great Patriotic War led to the cessation of the sanatorium. However, in those years he not only stood idle, but served as a hospital, as he could provide the wounded with all the conditions for good treatment and rest. Soon after the war, the activities of the sanatorium were resumed. The construction of a funicular in 1952, which connected the Ordzhonikidze sanatorium (Sochi) with the city beach, also became a pleasant bonus for vacationers.

Accommodation in Ordzhonikidze

Due to the fact that the sanatorium Ordzhonikidze (Sochi) consists of 9 buildings, vacationers have the opportunity to choose the option that suits their wishes for their accommodation, regarding the equipment of the room, its area and cost. At the same time, the health resort can accommodate 640 people. The rooms are one-room and two-room, designed to accommodate 1, 2 or 3 people.





All rooms have their own bathroom with shower, washbasin and toilet. The rooms are equipped with air conditioning, comfortable beds, TV, beach towels, radio and all necessary furniture. Bed linen in standard rooms is changed 1 time for 5 days.

Tourists living in two-room "junior suites" and "suites" have at their disposal a coffee and tea service and an electric kettle. Bed linens in these rooms are changed every 3 days. The suites also have a bathtub and bidet. As for the size of the rooms, it depends on the number of rooms, the estimated number of persons accommodated in them, and varies from 10 to 37 square meters.

Diagnosis and treatment in a sanatorium

As mentioned above, in addition to the fact that this recreation center is perfect for spending summer vacations involving swimming in the sea and relaxing on the beach, people suffering from a number of diseases tend to spend time here, as the resort can offer excellent conditions for the diagnosis and treatment of their body . So, Ordzhonikidze is chosen by people suffering from diseases such as gynecological, immuno-allergic, musculoskeletal, skin and cardiovascular systems.

In addition to the excellent climate and fresh air, which positively affect human health, the Ordzhonikidze sanatorium (Sochi) also has a clinical diagnostic laboratory, functional and immuno-allergic diagnostics, which is the key to successful treatment. Among the most common procedures that are used to treat patients, it is worth noting various therapeutic baths (including dry carbon dioxide), massages, inhalation therapy, mud therapy, mechanotherapy and apparatus physiotherapy.

Additional services in the sanatorium

In addition to the main sanatorium, Ordzhonikidze (Sochi) offers a number of additional services. So, vacationers can visit the sauna, equipped with two steam rooms. The recreation center offers a gym in which experienced trainers work, capable of drawing up an individual training program for them, which will be aimed at body shaping, training the cardiovascular system, burning fat or fighting other problems.

There is a conference room on the territory of the sanatorium for up to 250 people, ticket offices where you can buy tickets for railway and air transport, a photo services pavilion, a tour desk, a library, tennis courts, and also playgrounds.

Prices and reviews of tourists

The sanatorium Ordzhonikidze offers prices for everyone who wants to spend their holidays in Sochi in the range from 2 400 to 5 000 rubles, depending on the category of room, season, and the presence or absence of treatment. The indicated price includes accommodation in the selected room, meals, use of the sports complex, swimming pool and gym.
