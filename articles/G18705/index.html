Arkhipo-Osipovka: reviews of tourists about an unforgettable vacation

On the Black Sea coast, at the mouth of the Teshebs and Vulan rivers , the resort village Arkhipo-Osipovka is located. It is far from the bustle of bustling megacities. The village is surrounded by a semicircular mountain range, which is densely covered with forest. Arkhipo-Osipovka (reviews confirm this) has a very beautiful nature, healing mountain-forest and sea air, the southern sun and the gentle warm sea, which has a beneficial effect on the health of every vacationer. In addition, the village has an excellent location. It is only an hour away from Gelendzhik.



Rest: the main advantages

This village receives its guests at any time, as there are sanatoriums that operate all year round. Those who have already rested in such a village as Arkhipo-Osipovka, leave mostly positive reviews. It's no secret that there are practically no strong winds due to the mountains located around the resort. Plus, the temperature in winter often reaches 20 degrees.

Beaches

There is another plus that plays an important role during the holidays in the resort village of Arkhipo-Osipovka - beaches. Reviews of numerous tourists note that here is an excellent beach, where small pebbles alternate with white sand. The entrance to the sea is comfortable, the bottom is sandy, which is great for those who plan to spend their holidays with children. The beach is superbly equipped. Locker rooms are installed here, there are fountains with fresh and clean water.



Nutrition

Many tourists in their comments pay much attention to nutrition in the resort village of Arkhipo-Osipovka. Guest reviews describe and recommend a number of places where you can eat tasty and inexpensively. Among them are the Horizon cafe, the Glutton dining room, the Dolphin diner and the Hummingbird cafe.

Leisure activities and excursions

Not only the excellent climate and magnificent beaches is famous for Arkhipo-Osipovka. Reviews of tourists quite a lot describe all kinds of hikes and activities during the holidays. For nature lovers, there are many picturesque places in the vicinity of the village, including the Sosnovaya Slit tract, where a large grove of Crimean pine deserves special attention . Fans of long crossings are recommended to participate in a three-day trip to Podkolzin Yar. The length of this route is 35 kilometers. During the campaign, the group enters the Blue Grotto, Close Gorge and stalactite caves. Anyone who wants to go along the channels of mountain rivers will visit the Bzhidsky and Teshebsky waterfalls. Fans of hoary antiquity will be able to see dolmens located on the ridge of the Gypsy crevice and in the vicinity of the village of Pshada.
In addition, in the vicinity of the village there are many places for fishing, and in the forests there are berries and mushrooms. From Arkhipo-Osipovka, a number of excursions are made to the sights of nearby cities. And nightlife lovers are waiting for clubs and discos.

Accommodation in Arkhipo-Osipovka

In terms of housing, this village practically does not differ from many resort settlements located on the Black Sea coast. Sanatoriums, hotels, hotels, pensions - all this Arkhipo-Osipovka offers for its vacationers. The private sector (reviews confirm this) is most popular when placing vacationers. The cost of housing is different. It depends on the distance from the sea, on comfort and on the month in which the rental is made. In any case, you can always find an option that matches your financial capabilities and satisfies as many wishes as possible. Recently, mini-hotels and private guest houses have been in great demand. Such accommodation in an economy class room costs from 250 rubles, while renting a double room in a hotel costs from 1600 rubles.
