May Day Park in Ufa: a brief overview

Pervomaisky Park in Ufa is a place where residents of a densely populated city gather in the evenings and on weekends. As the citizens themselves note, here you can arrange a romantic date with your loved one, take a walk with your child or chat with friends, often people come here alone in order not only to breathe fresh air, but also to plunge into their thoughts.





general information

The park was founded in 1991 and from the very beginning of its existence bore a different name, in honor of the area in which it is located - Kalininsky. Only in 1995 did the situation change and the object received the name that currently exists. Since 1996, this urban facility has municipal status, and 10 years later, it was documented as a municipal unitary enterprise.





In the park, as residents of the city, it is very comfortable to relax, as there is beautiful nature. Its visitors are attracted by beautiful landscapes, which are revealed in a special way at different times of the year, as well as a lake located almost in the central part of the territory allotted for the city park. Often near him bright photo shoots are held, newlyweds come here.

Currently, residents of Ufa and the city administration are predicting the rapid development of the park as a single object. In the near future, it is planned to develop entertainment infrastructure on its territory, as well as the construction of new facilities that will be of interest to visitors.

Where is

Pervomaisky Park in Ufa is located in one of the most passable places in the city. It can be reached quite easily by public transport, as on all sides the resting place is surrounded by public transport stops "DK UMPO" and "Administration of the Kalinin district." They can be reached by trolley buses No. 8, 9, 15, buses No. 35k, 59e, 74, 110, 165, 15, 69, as well as minibuses No. 71, 71a, 74, 165, 165t, 210, 217, 221k. In addition, many guests arrive in the park in their own cars, which can be left in the parking lot located near the entrance to the resting place.

The park is located in the Kalinin district of Ufa, at the intersection of two major city streets: Ordzhonikidze and Mashinostroiteley. On the one hand, it borders on such an important urban object as the Kalinin District Administration. Also not far from it is a boarding school, registry office and a large store "Polushka", in which many residents of the surrounding neighborhoods make their purchases.





Entertainment programs

On days that are considered city and state holidays, entertainment events and festivities are held on the territory of Pervomaisky Park in Ufa. In order to make events more vivid, a large stage has been erected here, which is equipped with modern sound and video equipment during concerts. During concerts in the park guest stars perform, as well as local talented groups.

On an ongoing basis, such events are held in Pervomaisky Park (Ufa) as Shrovetide and the ceremony of burning effigies, New Year and Christmas festivities, the celebration of City and Republic Day, as well as a festival of ditties, which both old and young people like to attend.





Winter holidays

In the cold season, when snow falls in the city, residents of this village and surrounding towns go to Pervomaisky Park (Ufa) in order to take part in the snowy fun. For residents and guests of the city, a big snow slope is organized here every year, on which everyone can ride. There is an equipment rental point, as well as a group of instructors who, if desired, can teach both adults and children the art of skiing.

On frosty days, a large ice rink operates in the lower part of the park, which representatives of different age categories also like to visit. In social networks, as well as on thematic sites, you can find a considerable number of reviews left by guests of this place, which tells about the atmosphere prevailing on the ice rink and the snowy slope. Often bright reviews reflecting the mood of those present are attached to such reviews.





Vacation with children

As in any other similar place in the city, in Pervomaisky Park of Culture and Rest you can spend time with your family, taking small children with you. In addition to quiet and peaceful walks along the green alleys, here you can arrange a real holiday for children with ride on attractions. On a regular basis, a large train is installed on the territory of the place for recreation, on which kids can ride through the green territory of the park with a breeze. In addition, there is a Veterok merry-go-round, several labyrinths are equipped, in the warm season there are inflatable slides and large playgrounds with sand, where children can play and meet new friends.

In the summer, in Pervomaisky Park (Ufa), carousels are brought in from neighboring regions. The cost of riding them is relatively small, and the emotions received by children and adults in moments of entertainment are quite bright.





Catering establishments

Between entertainment and hiking, guests of the park in the Kalininsky district of Ufa can visit catering establishments, which offer a wide selection of dishes prepared in the traditions of Russian and European cuisines. The number of such places increases sharply in the warm season. As for the winter period, on a regular basis in the park there are two cafes that offer pancakes, hot and cold drinks, as well as salads, snacks and meat cooked over an open fire.
