LCD "Winter-Summer" in St. Petersburg: review, description and reviews

LCD "Winter-Summer" - an author's project of comfort class housing with an exceptional location. If you have been dreaming of your own apartment in St. Petersburg for a long time, prefer comfort, this project will definitely attract your attention.

According to the design documentation, LCD "Winter-Summer" (St. Petersburg) is represented by 5 houses on the 21-22 floors. The architectural solution is based on the theme of the seasons. The developer decided to reflect it in the color scheme.

On the one hand, the quarter is washed by the picturesque river Bolshaya Okhta, which is why residents of houses whose facades are facing it will receive not only comfortable housing, but also an amazing view.

Location

For the construction of the residential complex "Winter-Summer" (St. Petersburg) , the Krasnogvardeisky district of the Northern capital was chosen. The complex is being built just a 5-minute walk from the Ladozhskaya metro station. Transport accessibility

LCD "Winter-Summer" boasts excellent transport accessibility. Within walking distance from the buildings are the railway station and the Ladozhskaya metro station, from where you can reach any point in the Northern capital. At 800 meters from the complex - direct access to Kosygin Avenue and the Ring Road. The traffic is rather dense, but the residents of the complex note that there are practically no large and long traffic congestions.





Infrastructure

The complex creates the most convenient social and shopping and entertainment infrastructure. A school for 450 students and a kindergarten for 190 children will appear in the courtyard. All the necessary commercial establishments will occupy the first non-residential floors of new buildings: a store, pharmacy, bakery, shopping and entertainment centers.

The developer paid great attention to the safety of residents, fencing the territory of the complex and equipping it with a 24-hour video surveillance system, additional cameras are installed in children's playgrounds, parking lots.





Ecology

The complex is located near the northern border of St. Petersburg, so its residents can easily get out into the countryside. The embankment along the river will turn into a comfortable walking zone, the territory of the complex will be ennobled: a real garden will be laid out on it, which will favorably affect the ecological situation. Only half an hour from the complex are the three largest tourist areas with lakes, a forest belt and clean air.

Price policy

Of course, those who plan to purchase an apartment in the Zima-Summer residential complex are worried by prices no less than other components. The developer offers to purchase an installment plan before the completion of the object (December 2017) or to issue a mortgage loan on favorable terms.

Expert opinion
