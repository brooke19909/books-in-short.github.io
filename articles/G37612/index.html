Croatia - resorts. Croatia is a vacation. Croatia Resorts by the Sea

Convenient location is one of the undoubted advantages that Croatia has. The resorts of this country attract crowds of tourists every year. The coastal nature of the Adriatic Sea is considered truly unique. Holidays in picturesque places near the sea are very popular. Clear water, pebble beaches, mountains and forests leave no one indifferent. The local air is useful for those who have diseases of the lungs and other respiratory organs.



General information

The balneological resorts of Croatia, the description of which will be given below, are known far beyond the borders of the country. Being on them, you can successfully restore health, maintain immunity and gain new vitality. Resorts here have a developed infrastructure, which, undoubtedly, is another advantage of the country. The capital of the state is the city of Zagreb. There are also several ski resorts in Croatia. They are not very large, but comfortable.

Country features

The lack of noisy entertainment is one of the main distinguishing features that Croatia has. Resorts are designed for a calm, quiet family pastime away from megacities. Meals are not provided at any time of the day or night. Several thousand kilometers of coast, studded with pine needles, coves and cliffs - this is what Croatia boasts of. Relaxation on a clean and well-kept coastline surrounded by beautiful nature - what could be better?

Natural features

A high level of ecology is another advantage that Croatia has. Holidays on the coast for a long time remain in the memory of tourists. Many of them will certainly come back here again and again. Croatia resorts with sandy beaches are quite common. Pleasantly surprised by the cleanliness of the coastal zone. The territory is not dotted with umbrellas and other paraphernalia. Resorts in the country have approximately twenty mineral springs. There is an amazing field where unique healing oil is extracted from. For those who want to completely merge with the surrounding nature, nudist beaches function. They are very popular. Some come to Croatia precisely because of them.

Croatia map with resorts

The whole country is divided into three tourist zones. This, in particular, South and Central Dalmatia, the Istrian peninsula. Entrance to the shore is free, all beaches are municipal. You can not use sunbeds and umbrellas for free. Their rent costs about 2 euros. However, certain hotels provide their guests with related products completely free of charge. Some resorts in Istria (Croatia) are artificial. They have a concrete platform. The rest are small pebble lagoons or plateaus.

Central Dalmatia is characterized by pebble beaches. They are considered the best on the entire coast. The local pines grow very close to the surf line. For this reason, there is no need for umbrellas. South Dalmatia has the most diverse beaches. There are pebbles, and concrete, and rocky. Sandy beaches are located on islands that are located in areas of Dubrovnik. Istria is one of the main centers of concentration of lovers of nudist tourism.



Selection of the optimal route

You can use the services of Aeroflot. Flight Moscow - Zagreb departs several times a week. Charters are also organized. During the tourist season, such flights can be reached in Dubrovnik, Split and Pula. They are performed once a week. During a certain period in the last paragraph flights are made much more often. In Croatia, domestic flights are widespread. They also deliver passengers to all of the above points. Krk Island can be reached on a charter flight in summer. He leaves once a week. From Belarus and Ukraine it is also convenient to get to Croatia. Charter flights depart from capitals. They deliver passengers to the main resort centers of the country. Residents of the CIS countries to visit Croatia must obtain a visa. Russians are no exception.

Custom Features

There are no restrictions on the import of foreign currency. When crossing the border, the presentation of valuable technical and professional equipment is necessary. In order to bring animals into the country, you need to have a special veterinary certificate with you. Plants can be transported across the border only in its special places where phytosanitary control is carried out. This also applies to seeds, organic fertilizers. There is a permit for duty-free import of tobacco, cigars and cigarettes. However, there are certain limitations. They also apply to perfumes, colognes, teas, coffee and light spirits. There are restrictions on the export of certain items that are valuable from a historical and cultural point of view. To do this, you must have the appropriate permissions. They can only be given by the state administration for the protection of the country's natural and cultural heritage. A permit for the export of ammunition, weapons, narcotic substances and hides of wild animals is also required.



Car rental

In Croatia, car rental is widespread. Rent a car in this country can only be over 20 years old. It is also necessary to have driving experience. He must be at least three years old. It is required to have a driver's license with you. You must provide a document proving the identity of the person. As a rule, a passport is enough. Best paid by credit card. If it is absent, it is necessary to leave a deposit, its size can be up to three hundred euros.

Car rental is available at many places, such as airports. Many rental companies operate here. The minimum cost of one day rental is 50 euros. When concluding a contract with a rental company, you should pay attention to all the details. The most important thing is to find out exactly what services are included in the rental price.

Scooter rental is available on the entire coast. The amount of the deposit is from 100 euros. You should know something about the peculiarities of the price lists of rental companies: the prices there are indicated without tax. As a rule, its amount is at least 20% of the entire price. The country has a right-hand traffic, local drivers are quite polite. The roads are in excellent condition, for many they seem unusually narrow, so you should be careful. In Croatia, it is customary to strictly observe traffic signs. Local pedestrians are accustomed to the respect of car drivers. This situation is observed throughout Europe, especially for cyclists - they are very respected and they are all carefully circled.

The Mediterranean climate prevails on the coast. Summer is very dry, winters are wet and warm. The sultry time is easily tolerated, several key factors contribute to this: the abundance of greenery, the sea breeze and dry air. The swimming season is quite long. Before going on vacation, you should familiarize yourself with the weather conditions in Croatia in the near future. Choose the optimal tourist area will help the map. Precautionary measures

In the coastal waters of Croatia, sea urchins are very common. They like to hide in secluded places. For protection, it is best to wear special slippers. They can be bought almost everywhere in the tourist area. There are very few sandy beaches in Croatia. On the coast, predominantly small pebbles. However, for sand lovers there are a couple of suitable places. Basically, such zones are located on the islands.

Features of some settlements

Split is located in the center of the coast, between the mountains of Dubrovnik and Zadar. Thus, Dalmatia is divided into two parts. Split is the second largest city in Croatia. It is located on the Marjan Peninsula. The airport is 25 kilometers away. The city has many pebble beaches. The historical center of Split is a very important place, it is recorded in the UNESCO World Heritage List.

Archipelagos of islands surround the city from the sea, they include Vis, Bishevo, Hvar, Drvenik, Solta, Brac. Dubrovnik is a great place for those tourists who are interested in cultural attractions. By its beauty, the city is comparable only with Amsterdam and Venice, it occupies a worthy place in the top three of these picturesque places. These cities are European Renaissance monuments. Guests of Croatia should definitely visit this paradise, which makes a lasting impression on every visitor. Dubrovnik has a rich history. It is a major port of the Adriatic Sea. The city plays an important role in the economy of the whole country. Its extremely attractive appearance annually attracts huge crowds of tourists. Amazing views of the city currently have no analogues. There are many modern buildings made of glass. Their neighbors are buildings erected in the XV-XVIII centuries. At the same time, the ancient city square is viewed from the windows of the most fashionable boutiques. Such intriguing contrasts are considered the highlight of this unique place.

Hvar is the sunniest and longest island in the Adriatic. Ultraviolet rays illuminate the local land almost all year round. This is one of the most beautiful holiday destinations in the whole country, which is due to a great many factors. For example, the presence of small medieval cities gives this place a special atmosphere, and numerous bays complement the overall picture. The smell of lavender, which is felt everywhere, no one leaves without attention. The abundance of subtropical vegetation is also attractive for connoisseurs of natural beauty. Mild climatic conditions make the rest as comfortable and memorable as possible. Here, as in the capital of the country, life does not freeze for a second.
