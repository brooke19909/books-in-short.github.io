Detailing an MTS account in a few clicks

Suspiciously a lot of money is spent on mobile services? This happens if you do not follow the tariff update, because new services and tariff plans are introduced that allow you to select communication services and modify the existing payment system for specific needs, which helps to save money.



Nevertheless, before you do anything, you need to find out where in a particular case funds are taken off from your mobile phone - this can be helped by bill details. MTS, in particular, offers almost instant report generation from a personal account on its official website.

It is extremely easy to do this: you need to go to your personal account on the MTS website. Details of the account can be found in the menu of the Internet assistant in the "account" and the "cost control" sub-items. Then you can choose a detailed breakdown of expenses for the current month, for previous months, as well as get details of conversations. The first 2 points will give a consolidated report on all calls and SMS for the month, but the last point will provide a detailed list of calls, SMS and additional services for the selected period, indicating the tariff, time of the call and the total cost of the action. It is very convenient to work with data when the details of the MTS account are presented in the form of an Excel table. This helps to work with samples and sort data in various ways.



An analysis of reports of this kind will help to understand what exactly the lion's share of the funds is spent on from the phone account and optimize expenses by connecting additional services or changing tariffs. There is also an electronic tariff calculator on the site, which, based on needs, will help you choose the right tariff plan and connect it.

Detailing an account on MTS will also help you see how many numbers and in which
regions make calls, as well as the amount of Internet traffic spent while on the Internet. In addition, the site has a service that allows you to find out the cost of messages to short numbers, which will help protect yourself from scammers and not go into a deep minus. The advantage of such detailed detailing is that it indicates the specific numbers to which calls were made. However, this is also a danger - such important information should be safely hidden from prying eyes.



The Internet assistant also allows you to disconnect and connect additional services, set ringtones to the beep, and also use the Favorite Number service, which makes it possible to reduce the cost of calls to the most frequently dialed number or several numbers.

Of course, a detailed account of MTS can be obtained at any office of the company, but why go somewhere if you can get a detailed report without leaving your home or even using a smartphone? In addition, in this case, it is necessary that the person contacted by the SIM card is contacted at the office, and this is not always the case. That is why it is much more convenient to request details through your personal account.

It is also convenient that the details of the MTS account can be formed in the form of graphs, charts and tables - as you wish! And all this for only five minutes!
