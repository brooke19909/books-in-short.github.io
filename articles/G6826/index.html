"Tsypina Gora" - modern entertainment for outdoor enthusiasts

"Tsypina Gora" is a cultural architectural and natural reserve located in the west of the Vologda Oblast. This is the peak of the mountain range in the Russian North National Reserve.

To the north-west of the Vologda region there is a modern ski complex called "Tsypina Gora". The recreation center is located at a total distance of 116 km from the regional center - the city of Kirillov. You can reach your destination by heading along the Volgograd - Kirillov highway.





Experience in the recreation center in the Vologda region for over 10 years. Since 2003, the complex has been working on the organization of leisure and skiing for skiers and snowboarders. The ski slopes are located on the southern slope of the mountain and are suitable for skiing at any level. This is ideal for beginners: the descent is long and gentle. It is in such circumstances that it is best to learn to ski or snowboard.

Relief features

The main route was created on the basis of the southern slope of the natural relief. Partially the slope was poured. Correction of the relief allowed to form on it several ski slopes of different descent complexity.

Track Features

All slopes for skiers are located on the southern slope of the Tsypina Gora complex. The elevation difference on the ridge in some places reaches 75 m. The length of one of the longest routes is almost 500 meters. A special training slope for beginners in skiing and snowboarding was artificially formed on the territory of the Tsypina Gora recreation center.





A special training zone is located on the territory of the ski complex, which was put into operation since 2008 and successfully operates. Here you can take a training course from an experienced instructor and after two hours stand confidently on your feet.

Additional services

Vacation is designed to relax. And where is this to be done, if not on Tsypina Hill. To get a full vacation, the administration offers its customers a full range of services:

  • organization of skiing (300 rubles / hour) and equipment rental: ski suit and set of skiers: from 500 rubles / day; tubing: from 100 p./h .;
  • help of a ski instructor: 500 rubles for every 3 hours of instruction;
  • organization of skiing on your own ski equipment / transport (snowmobile): from 600 p. / day;
  • organization of evening skating for individual groups (from 15 people) at a cost of 500 rubles per person;
  • Climbing the slope on a ski lift: from 500 p. / Day;
  • catering for vacationers;
  • Possibility of accommodation in: hotel - 3 000 rubles; Guest house - 20 000 rubles (rent);
  • organization of leisure activities for wives and children accompanying skiers.

How to get to the recreation center

Heading towards Medvezhyegorsk from Vologda along the highway of the same name, at 110 kilometers turn towards Kirillov. Moving along the road, follow the sign for Belousovo and turn right onto the road that leads to the Tsypina Gora ski resort.





From Cherepovets, leaving Kirillov, following the Vologda - Medvezhyegorsk highway, already 12 km from the regional center, follow the Belousovo sign and turn left to the Tsypina Gora Nature Reserve.

The ski complex is designed for a one-time reception of up to several hundred people per day. The popularity of the resort has reached its climax: in the season up to several hundred people ride on a slope and feel comfortable thanks to the high throughput of the slopes.

If you are looking for a place where you can actively relax with your whole family, visit the Tsypina Gora ski resort. There is a lesson for both an adult and a child. Trails of varying difficulty, a highly developed infrastructure, recreational facilities for children and adults - all that is needed to organize a comfortable stay, combined with entertainment and quality service.
