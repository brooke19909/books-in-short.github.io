Hotel Pension Fox 3 *, Prague, Czech Republic: overview, features and reviews

Prague is a city in the Czech Republic that is unique in nature. It has a huge number of historical monuments, as well as beautiful architectural objects that attract the eyes of tourists from different regions of the world. It is here that tourists from different parts of the world often go to rest in order to enjoy the unusual beauty of the city and visit all the most interesting places that are located in it.

Hotels in Prague are incredibly popular among tourists. A huge number of trips every year are purchased by travelers at various travel agencies. One of the most popular hotels is Pension Fox 3 *, which, despite its category, offers quite good living conditions for a decent payment.

general information

As stated in the official description of this vacation spot, Pension Fox 3 * is a hotel that is perfect for families. It is a small guesthouse with silence and a heavenly atmosphere.

This hotel was built in 1980 and, since its foundation, has been delighting tourists with a wonderful atmosphere and excellent service. As tourists themselves note in their reviews of Pension Fox 3 *, its building has a rather modern look, due to the regular repair and restoration work. During one of them, which was made in 2013, the hotel completely replaced the ventilation system and installed modern air conditioners in each apartment, with which tourists can now adjust the temperature conditions in the apartment to the desired level, regardless of the weather in it's time outside.

Location

The hotel is located near the central part of Prague, literally within a 15-minute walk. Not far from it is the metro station "Garden of Eden", which allows tourists here to easily get to any part of the city quite quickly. Pension Fox 3 * (Prague) is located within walking distance of entertainment places - it is next to it that there is a large entertainment center where guests can find a club, restaurant, karaoke and several other interesting places. If desired, guests have the opportunity to visit the hypermarket located next door to the guesthouse.

In their reviews of Pension Fox 3 * (Prague), tourists say that the hotel is located in a fairly quiet area, where there is practically no noise. In their opinion, this is favorable for families with small children, which is often taken into account by tourists who want to spend their holidays with the whole family.



Rooms

Pension Fox 3 * has a very small number of rooms - it consists of only 14 apartments, which are decorated very beautifully and comfortably. For the decor of each of them, modern furniture was used. Smoking is strictly forbidden in them - for this purpose the hotel has a separate playground.

All apartments are divided into two categories: standard and comfort. Each of them has an individual bathroom with a full list of sanitary ware, which is necessary to maintain hygiene. In addition, in this place for recreation, each guest at the entrance to the room receives a set of bath accessories.

Standard

Standard class apartments are divided into two types, depending on how many people can be accommodated in the room. There are 11 double rooms and one single. Each apartment has a modern bed with an orthopedic mattress and light bedding. All guests can choose a room with single beds or with one double. On a free basis, a small safe is installed in the rooms, as well as a minibar, which is filled with cool water at the entrance of guests. If desired, guests can watch TV, which is in the room, it is connected to a satellite broadcasting system.



Comfort

Comfort class apartments are perfect for tourists who want to relax with the whole family, as they are designed for three guests, for whom an appropriate number of beds is provided. If necessary, a cot can be accommodated in the room, for which payment must be made.

The room has a place to store guests' clothes in the form of a large closet, divided into shelves, as well as a pair of bedside tables. If necessary, vacationers can use the phone connected to the international line, as well as a safe for storing small valuables. The apartments have a plasma screen, watching programs on which is the favorite pastime of many guests of the boarding house, many of them talk about the fact that Russian-language channels are also broadcast here, which is convenient for tourists from the CIS countries.

The main feature of Pension Fox 3 *, which pleases tourists, is that here, for an additional fee, you can stay with pets, which can not be done in many other hotels.



Nutrition

Many tourists who visited Pension Fox 3 * (Prague) talk about the fact that food is produced at the highest level. Every day in the large dining room, which is located in the hotel building, a table is set where all residents are invited. In their reviews, they talk about the fact that for lunch, breakfast and dinner they only serve wholesome food prepared by talented chefs. Much attention in the diet is paid to dairy products, meat products, sweets, as well as fresh vegetables and fruits. Guests who come to the dining room can independently choose the right food for themselves from the entire presented list. The cost of visiting this place is included in the price set for accommodation in Pension Fox 3 * (Czech Republic / Prague).

Many tourists in their reviews talk about the fact that not far from the hotel there are several excellent restaurants serving excellent dishes. Often they share their impressions from visiting such places. Among them, they often distinguish the SOCHI restaurant, where they can surprise you with dishes of Ukrainian and Georgian cuisines, as well as DIANA, where you can taste excellent meat dishes that are cooked in a hunting style, mainly over an open fire.



Massage

Hotel Pension Fox 3 * offers its guests a number of services, among which massage performed by real professionals is very popular. To provide this type of service, the hotel has a well-equipped office, the interior of which allows any guest to relax. All procedures that are carried out here are performed on a paid basis. Guests note their particular effectiveness, especially with regard to techniques aimed at treating diseases of the back and spine. According to experts, all procedures here are carried out according to German methods.

Service

The hotel Pension Fox 3 * (Prague) for the convenience of guests offers a wide range of services and additional features. Among them, rental of various objects is in great demand. Here you can rent a small refrigerator, hairdryer, and an ironing board with iron. If necessary, guests can be provided with an additional breakfast for a small surcharge. If necessary, the room can be cleaned, which is also paid by guests.





Booking Policy

Advance booking of a room is the main condition for entering this hotel in Prague. In Pension Fox 3 * (Prague), you can order the desired apartment for certain dates on the official website of this place for relaxation or by calling the administrator, whose number is also listed in this source. Despite these possibilities, most tourists prefer to buy a ticket to this place from tour operators - in this case, the reservation for the room is set automatically.

Prices

The cost of a tourist package to the Pension Fox 3 * hotel varies depending on which city of Russia it was purchased in and which way the tourist will need to go to the destination. So, for residents of Moscow, its average cost is about 45,000 rubles, which is based on two guests who will stay at the hotel for a week. This price includes transportation costs in advance. Travel agents offer travelers different travel options: on the plane, on the bus and on the train - depending on this, the price of the tour as a whole changes dramatically.

Many agents recommend that tourists buy a tour to include a trip to local attractions. The traveler will do the right thing if he takes advantage of this offer, since in Prague there really is something to see.



Reviews

A huge number of hotel guests after a vacation in it leave a lot of reviews that can be found on thematic sites or on personal pages of travelers on social networks. To many comments, vacationers attach colorful photographs taken during the holidays.

In their reviews, tourists often talk about the fact that the hotel provides pretty good service. This is especially true of the administration, which includes responsive and polite people who are always ready to help guests in resolving issues related to accommodation. They also talk about the excellent work of the room service.

As for nutrition, vacationers note a serious approach on the part of the administration to this issue. Guests are treated only to the most nutritious and healthy dishes, which are prepared in the best European traditions.



The situation in the rooms also causes a lot of pleasant moments, which is reflected in the comments left by travelers about this place. On the positive side, tourists are considering the fact that there is free access to the Internet in all apartments, as well as the presence of Russian-language television channels. Also, many guests respond positively to permission to stay here with pets.
