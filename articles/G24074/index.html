How to make a vase out of paper. How to make a vase from corrugated paper

Why do you need a paper vase, you ask a question. The answer is quite simple - this craft can be a great decoration for the interior of your home, office or just a wonderful gift. In this article you will find information on how to make a vase out of paper. Today there are a huge number of techniques for creating crafts from this material. You will meet them by reading the article.



Workshop number 1. Vase - the best gift for Valentine's Day

Any glass container can be turned into a beautiful and original vase, which can be presented to a loved one. In this master class we will use the technique - decoupage. To understand how to make a vase out of paper, you will need:

  • glass vessel;
  • corrugated paper (red, white and pink) ;
  • brush;
  • sponge;
  • scissors;
  • adhesive for decoupage.

Cut out the squares from the white corrugated paper. Apply glue to a glass jar. Glue the squares so that there are no gaps. Now cut out the hearts of red paper. Make five and glue it. Position symmetrically on the vase. Apply another layer of glue on top. Do not be afraid - after drying, it becomes colorless. Use a sponge to remove excess glue. The finished product can also be varnished. Here's a vase made of corrugated paper.

Master class number 2. How to make a vase from paper tubes

To make such a product, you will need:

  • Office or corrugated paper - you can use newspaper, magazine or notebook sheets. The most important thing is to choose one thing, do not mix several types of paper.
  • PVA glue.
  • Spoke.
  • Whatman.
  • Lacquer.

Take a piece of paper. Cut into long strips ten centimeters wide. Use a knitting needle or a cocktail tube to wind the tubes. To prevent the paper from unwinding, fix its edges with glue. Do not make too thin tubes, otherwise the product will not look good. The height of the crafts depends on the length of the wound tubes. It is necessary to craft 50 pieces. When all the tubes are wound, make a base for the vase. Whatman is best. Do not choose thin paper, otherwise the vase will not last you long. Twist whatman paper into the cylinder of the necessary diameter. Glue the tubes. Now attach two strips on the top and bottom. They are made from the same tubes, but they must be flat. Smooth it with an iron, wrap it around your finger and press firmly with your thumb. Glue these strips. Next, trim the top of the vase obliquely. To do this exactly, use an elastic band and two pins. Cut along the intended path. Now paint the product. You can use stain, varnish or gouache. When the vase is dry, start making the bottom. From the cardboard cut a circle equal to the diameter of the vase. Glue it and apply varnish too. You can insert a bottle inside the cylinder. The vase is ready!



The creation of such crafts can be entrusted to the child. There is nothing complicated in the assembly.

Origami technique . Paper vases

Origami modular is the creation of crafts from the same fragments connected together. This needlework appeared in China.

We bring to your attention several original ideas - how to make a vase out of paper using the origami technique. It is best to start with simple products, for example, with a vase for sweets, and then move on to more voluminous compositions. Before starting the craft, you need to fold the triangular modules according to the following scheme:

  1. Take sheet A4, divide into 8 equal rectangles. Cut out the resulting shapes. Take one of the rectangular pieces and fold in half.
  2. Bend and bend the workpiece vertically, drawing a midline.
  3. Bend the corners in the middle.
  4. Turn the workpiece over and lift the edges up.
  5. Bend the corners of the figure by bending them through a triangular element and tuck inward.
  6. Bend the workpiece in half.

The result is a module that has two pockets and two corners.

Master class number 3: a vase for sweets

The craft is assembled from triangular modules. All must be the same size. To work, you need the following number of modules: 80 white and 140 yellow. You will also need scissors and glue. Making such a craft is simple. The main thing is to know the assembly diagram. It can be found in any specialized magazine.

So, for the first row, take twenty white modules, and for the second - twenty yellow. Connect a chain of parts of two rows into a ring.

In the third, connect twenty yellow modules. Turn the resulting ring to the other side.

In the fourth row, insert evenly thirty yellow modules.

In the seventh, put on thirty whites.

In the eighth row, insert the same number of parts, only back to front.

Ninth make of forty yellow details. Evenly add ten modules in a circle.

Thirty yellow fragments are required to make the bottom of the vase. Insert them into each other. Connect in a ring. Glue the bottom to the main workpiece. You can also make pens at will in the same way.

  • Third: one light green (KS), two blue (KS), one light green (KS), one yellow (DS). Repeat this alternation of modules four more times.
  • Fourth: two yellow (DS), one light green color (KS), one blue (KS), one light green (KS). Repeat four times. Turn out a ring.
  • Fifth: one yellow (SDS), two light green colors (SPL), one yellow (SDS). Alternate four more times. Repeat this action in subsequent rows.
  • Sixth: two pink (SDS), one yellow (SDS), one light green color (SPL), one yellow (SDS).
  • Seventh: one white (SDS), one pink (SDS), two yellow (SDS), one pink (SDS).
  • Eighth: two pink (SDS) and three yellow (SDS).
  • Ninth: one pink (SDS) and two yellow (SDS).
  • Tenth: one pink (SPF) and one yellow (SPF).
  • Eleventh to sixteenth: one pink (SPF), one yellow (SPF).

During assembly, bend the craft outward, thereby giving the vase a shape.
