Description

The Natural Resort 3 * Hotel (Phuket, Patong, Thailand) is a modern-class hotel that will allow you to enjoy an unforgettable vacation in full. The Natural Resort is a three star hotel built in Phuket Town. The hotel has about 165 rooms, which include bathtub, television, air conditioning. It is possible to order an extra bed.

Hotel The Natural Resort 3 * (Phuket), reviews of which are mostly positive, is located just minutes from Patong Beach, which is one of the best beaches in Thailand. Vacation spots

There are many places of entertainment nearby. Bangla Road is within a 2-minute walk.
