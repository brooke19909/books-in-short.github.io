Smartphone Nokia X2 Dual Sim: reviews and features

A year ago, it was believed that it was impossible to combine Microsoft services and the Android operating system, but at the moment we can already see the second generation of smartphones based on the Nokia X Platform. A distinctive feature of such mobile devices was the lack of functionality of Google and Play services. The manufacturer‚Äôs graphical shell was added to the Android operating system. Unlike Windows Phone, the presented smartphone still has the ability to download a huge number of Android applications, their range compares favorably with other similar models. The Nokia X2 Dual Sim communicator, the review of which we offer you, has a more powerful hardware base and an updated operating system compared to the first smartphones in this line.

Delivery Overview





In a conversation about Nokia X2 Dual Sim, user reviews will provide us with an invaluable service, as they will help to achieve objectivity. The packing box is made of high-quality and dense cardboard, you can be sure of the safety of its contents. It contains the image of the smartphone, its name, the manufacturer‚Äôs logo and information about the mobile device and the location of the company. The box is quite presentable and bright, able to immediately attract attention. The information sheet contains the name of the smartphone, its configuration, basic technical specifications and possible color options (for example, Nokia x2 Dual Sim Green). The packing box extends to the side, after which we have access to the communicator and all related accessories. Complete with a smartphone is a network adapter, stereo headset, warranty card and user manual. By the way, an important point about the Nokia X2 Dual Sim: reviews by many users call it a little strange the lack of a USB cable and memory card, which will have to be purchased separately.

Structure



Like all Nokia mobile devices, the side walls are soldered with a back cover and form a kind of rectangular box into which the device fits and snaps into place for its greater strength, durability, guaranteed absence of gaps and creaks. The Nokia X2 Dual Sim smartphone is quite easy to remove from the box if you need to change SIM, battery or memory card. The design of the mobile device is very similar to the advertised Lumia, it is very recognizable and familiar to almost all users of communicators of this manufacturer. Distinctive features of the smartphone are bright colors and a minimalistic rectangular shape. The manufacturer offers the model in several versions - light green, black or bright orange. It can be noted that the smartphone has become even more angular along the side faces, and on the back cover the roundness has completely disappeared. Compared to other glossy mobile devices, Nokia X2 looks more advantageous, which is facilitated by a two-layer bright color coating. Conventional plastic on top is supplemented with a layer of transparent polycarbonate. Such a solution reliably protects the smartphone from the appearance of scratches characteristic of a glossy case, and also gives the appearance of the device a beautiful depth effect. Instructions for removing the back cover are available in the user manual. You should not have any difficulties with this process, you can even do without improvised means. Under the back cover is a removable battery, slots for a memory card and two SIM cards.

Nokia X2 Dual Sim: display reviews



The screen has a protective and oleophobic layer, this allows you to minimize fingerprints during use. Also, the device has an anti-reflective coating so that light sources do not distract from working on a smartphone. The side panels and glass are separated by a special plastic thin insert, now it does not protrude from the edges of the case and when placing the smartphone face down on a hard surface of the smartphone, you need to be very careful so as not to damage the display. On the top panel there is a speaker, a connector for a USB cable and a stereo headset. On the right side there is a volume key and a button to turn on and lock the mobile device. The smartphone was assembled very efficiently, when pressed on the screen, color stains do not appear, and when rotated, nothing bends, and the geometry of the device remains unchanged.

Volume



Nokia X2 Dual Sim - Android-smartphone, in which only one multimedia speaker is built-in, when the mobile device is located on the front side, the sound can be muffled. The device is quite loud, but the negative side is the complete absence of low frequencies. The speaker is mainly intended for listening to music, games and watching movies.

Shooting

The front camera of the mobile device has a resolution of 0.3 megapixels, and the main - 5 megapixels. Available are aperture optics, a sensor, digital four-time zoom, auto focus and LED flash. You can get good pictures both in the daytime and at night. When shooting in a dark room or at night, be sure to turn on the flash. Video quality is not impressive, poor results are preserved even when setting the settings to the maximum and when increasing or decreasing the screen resolution.

Conclusion



To create its new product, Nokia has fully worked on past mistakes, improved the operating system, increased the productivity of the smartphone and slightly changed the controls, while retaining the bright youthful design of the mobile device. For its price, the model turned out to be quite successful, there were no complaints about the rigidity of the structure, the quality of its assembly, color rendering, the operation of the navigator, wireless interfaces, and the launch speed of applications for all the time. It should be said that the Nokia X2 Dual Sim reviews are currently mostly positive.
