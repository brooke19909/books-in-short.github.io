The magical country of Egypt ... Safaga - a unique resort on the Red Sea

So, you decided to visit one of the oldest states on the planet - Egypt. Safaga on the map of the country is located near the Red Sea and is a small resort-type city. Safaga is located 50 kilometers from Hurghada. Not far from the city is the most important trading port of the country, passenger ships cruise here. They connect Egypt with Saudi Arabia and Jordan.



Medical tourism

If you want to improve your health, then go to Egypt. Safaga, in turn, has many balneological sources. Even the sand on the beaches of Safaga has healing properties. It effectively treats psoriasis, other skin ailments and diseases of the musculoskeletal system.

Active tourism

Safaga provides outdoor enthusiasts with ample opportunities. Here you can go windsurfing and kitesurfing. By the way, it was here in 1993 that the World Championship in these sports was held. Many come to these places to go diving on the coral islands.

Excursions

Due to its favorable geographical location, many interesting excursions can be booked from Safaga. Just one day will take a trip to the heart of Egypt - Luxor. Previously, this was the capital of the ancient state of Thebes. The city has preserved magnificent temples, tombs and a necropolis. This tour will provide an opportunity to touch the culture and history of the great state of Egypt. Safaga gives you the opportunity to visit the capital of the country of the pharaohs. An excursion to Cairo will take tourists two days. Here you will see the famous pyramids, the Sphinx and the Egyptian Museum. Perhaps you want to get into an oriental tale, then the Palace of the Thousand and One Nights is waiting for you! This miracle is located on the outskirts of Hurghada. You can get acquainted with the beauties of the underwater world in two ways. The first is, of course, diving, and the second is a submarine tour.

Egypt: Shams Safaga 4 *

The four-star Shams Hotel (Safaga) is one of the best in the area. It is located on the first line from the beach, just 200 meters. You can book rooms in a three-story building or in a bungalow. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, terraces or balconies, a fridge, a TV and a bathroom. The hotel has 2 swimming pools, one of which is for children, the other for adults. Various entertainment programs and discos are held daily. If you want to take diving courses, then choose Shams (Safaga, Egypt). Prices range from 2000 rubles per person. Beginners can take scuba diving courses, rent appropriate equipment and book an excursion to coral reefs. Tourists relax in the hotel on an all-inclusive system. There are 3 restaurants, 4 bars and cafes, a conference room, shops, a currency exchange, and a laundry service for you. A playground is open for children, and experienced animators will brighten up the relaxation of any child. On a clean beach you can take umbrellas, beach towels, mattresses and sunbeds absolutely free.

Conclusion

The magical country of Egypt ... Safaga is a unique opportunity to combine your vacation with active sports, make excursions to historical places, and also improve your health.
