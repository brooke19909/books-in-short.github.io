Design

In terms of appearance, the smartphone differs little from most similar devices. The case is almost rectangular, since the upper and lower ends have small curves. Speaking of rounding. This also applies to the back cover of the device, which makes the phone seem more compact.

At the expense of the material, there is no illusion - this is ordinary matte plastic soft touch. But it is good enough, as it makes invisible scratches and other minor damage to the case. Screen Features

How does the Lenovo A 2010 smartphone function? Customer reviews suggest that the device has high contrast, but medium color rendering. Dark is transmitted darker, and light is lighter. Moreover, the settings are rather modest, but they relate mainly to brightness. The sensor works well, it responds quickly to touch, but only supports two clicks at a time. Software

The device runs on Android, version 5.1 Lollipop, which provides the device with excellent performance. Applications, browser, multimedia files - everything opens quickly and works smoothly. By the way, the interface of this model differs little from the previous devices of the company.

Although there are still some innovations, for example, an updated notification panel and application manager. Of the pre-installed programs, there is only the most necessary.

Multimedia features

To listen to music, the most common media player is installed. There is also an FM tuner and voice recorder with the ability to enable recording during a call. The video player is characterized in that it can trim the video. And there is also a zoom-in function that allows you to select a specific area on the screen, and then zoom it in. And although both players cannot boast a large set of settings, there are no complaints about their work. This opinion is formed by most people who bought a Lenovo A 2010 smartphone. Communication opportunities

Great news: the smartphone can work in 2G, 3G and 4G networks. And he does it pretty cleverly. Where such confidence? From users who leave gadget A 2010 Lenovo reviews. Many of them note 4G connection for the better.

Battery life and memory

Another important point is the autonomy of the device. Battery capacity - 2050 mAh. Conclusion
