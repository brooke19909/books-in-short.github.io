Making origami: a vase of flowers

Origami is an interesting technology for creating beautiful crafts, available to both children and adults. All you need is paper and your patience. Today we will create the simplest and most original product using origami. A vase that can be made in a few minutes can be used to decorate a finished glass vessel with water. Thus, even fresh flowers will stand in the original craft. You can find other destinations for the product. For example, your origami vase will be a convenient tool for storing buttons, staples, or will be used as a stand for pencils.





Instruction for work

1. Take a sheet of double-sided square paper. Mark the fold line by bending the sheet diagonally. Then straighten it. Do the same with the second diagonal.

2. Now you need to outline a horizontal fold, folding the sheet in half. Spread it again. Fold the paper into the rhombus along the resulting lines. To make the vase-origami neat, iron the resulting figure well.

3. Bend the right and left corners of the rhombus to the center. Turn the workpiece over and repeat the procedure with the remaining corners.

4. Now the upper left corner must be bent to the center, bent and lowered to the middle. We also do the rest of the corners, directing them alternately inward.

5. To make the origami vase a stable bottom, carefully bend the bottom corner of the structure. If at some stage you made a mistake, then don‚Äôt worry, just expand the model and follow the folds to see which step of the instruction you skipped. It is important to accurately bend, otherwise the product will not work. If you stacked everything carefully, the origami vase should be ready.





Use cases

1. This craft is done very quickly and its creation does not take much time. But it can come in handy for room decor with dry flowers. You can also make beautiful flowers using the origami technique : roses, lilies or tulips. And then the craft will be a wonderful gift for colleagues and acquaintances.

2. Try to create a real work of art by decorating the product with rhinestones, sparkles, or painting with gouache. Fix the spangles by spraying ordinary varnish on it. Such an article will fit perfectly into the interior of your apartment, or will become a beautiful decoration on your desktop.

3. Make the model out of orange paper . Flip the design over, draw a funny face, and your origami vase will become a decoration for the holiday Halloween.

4. Design a craft from large paper, place a vessel with water in it. Such a vase can be used to store fresh flowers.





Making an original vase (origami) out of paper will be easy even for a child who is sure to join your creative process. If your baby is still small, let him paint the paper, and make an unusual and multifunctional origami vase out of it yourself. Thus, the craft will be both a subject of entertainment and a great gift for your family or friends. Just a little patience and perseverance is enough!
