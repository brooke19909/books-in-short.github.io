Cheap hotels in Chelyabinsk: overview, description of rooms, prices

To stay in an unfamiliar city, it is not necessary to spend large sums of money on expensive hotels. Travel and leisure options are available at hostels and small family-run hotels. Cheap hotels in Chelyabinsk can be found both in the city center and near the railway station. In order not to make a mistake with the choice and spend a comfortable time, it is worth paying attention to the description and reviews of those who have already managed to appreciate all the pros and cons of hotels.

Hotel "Visit"

If you want to stay in the city center, we advise you to pay attention to the cheap hotel in Chelyabinsk, which is located at ul. Elkina, 76. They received their first tourists in 2003 and has 50 rooms for a comfortable stay.

For one bed in a room designed for 3 residents, you will have to pay only 560 rubles. In addition, the hotel offers delicious and affordable breakfasts right in the hotel building. Their price is 200 rubles.

Throughout the hotel there is free Internet, air-conditioned rooms, cable TV and comfortable beds. The hotel has a banquet hall, cafe, hairdresser, photocopy and fax services. Near the hotel there is a parking lot where visitors can leave their car under 24-hour security.

Guests note in their reviews the well-coordinated work of the staff, the courtesy of administrators and quick check-in. Negative feedback concerns mainly the old elevator and not always clean rooms.

Hotel Dream House

Another hostel in Chelyabinsk, which is located in the center, belongs to the budget category. It is located on the street. Timiryazev, d. 37. The cost of living in a 6-bed room per person is 390 rubles. Meals at the hotel are not provided even for a fee. But it offers travelers a kitchen equipped with all necessary equipment, where you can cook simple dishes yourself and save on food in a cafe or restaurant.

Staff can provide a hairdryer upon request. Shuttle services can be arranged for a fee and slippers, bathrobes and hygiene products can be provided. Throughout the hostel there is access to the Internet, such a service is included in the price. There is also the opportunity to visit the Turkish sauna on site.

Reviews about this cheap hotel in Chelyabinsk are mostly positive. Guests note the order in the rooms and corridors, comfortable modern furniture and clean linens.

Greenhostel

On the street Lovina in house 13 is another hotel in Chelyabinsk, the price of accommodation in which pleases those who are looking for an economical option. The cost of living for one person is 390 rubles. For the money, a comfortable bed is provided in an 8-bed room. This hotel is located just 2 km from the city center and 3 km from the railway station.

For the convenience of guests equipped kitchen, there is the opportunity to use a hairdryer, iron, ironing board. Bathrooms are on the floor, hot water is available around the clock.

Of the pluses, tourists note the convenient location of the hostel in Chelyabinsk, the friendly staff and the cleanliness of the rooms. Of the minuses - the inability to pay by credit card, the hotel only accepts cash.

Nice Hostel

If you plan to stay in a hotel in Chelyabinsk near the train station, then Nice Hostel will be an excellent option. It is located on the street. Svobody, 108a, which is 300 meters from the railway station.

For 780 rubles you will be provided with a bed in an 8-bed room, which is equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. Judging by the reviews of the guests, the furniture here is new, the bedding is fresh, and the room is perfectly clean. Free internet and parking are available.

The hostel has a kitchen with kitchenware and appliances, a washing machine and lockers for personal belongings of tourists. Literally within walking distance are grocery stores, supermarkets, cafes and eateries.

The only negative that guests noted in their reviews was the lack of an elevator, so you will have to go up to the rooms on the 5th and 6th floors.

Hostel "Lovebirds"

At 700 meters from the train station and 2 km from the city center is a cheap hotel in Chelyabinsk with the unusual name "Lovebirds". It is located on the street. Ovchinnikov, d. 17a and provides tourists with a bed in a 14-bed room for 370 rubles. Despite the fact that the room is designed for a large number of residents, it is rarely 100% full. As a rule, 5-6 people live in one room at a time. The size of this room is quite large - 40 square meters. meters.

The hostel has a 24-hour reception desk, where, according to guests, upscale and polite staff works. Each tourist can use the Internet for free, a safe for storing expensive items and a shared bathroom with modern fixtures.

As an advantage of this cheap hotel in Chelyabinsk, guests note the availability of separate non-smoking rooms and a ban on smoking in all common areas. Among the shortcomings, they say that it is not possible to pay with a plastic card.
