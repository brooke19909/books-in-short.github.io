Hotel "White Nights" (St. Petersburg): service, apartments and prices

One of the most visited cities in Russia by tourists is St. Petersburg. Visitors are attracted to this metropolis a lot, but the main thing is, of course, its cultural heritage. In any case, for whatever tourist arrives in St. Petersburg, he will need a temporary place of residence. And a good option would be the White Nights Hotel. St. Petersburg is a large metropolis, therefore the position of the hotel is of considerable importance for tourists. This complex is located in the Nevsky district, which borders Central, as well as Frunze, Kolpinsky and Krasnogvardeisky, each of which is rich in attractions.





general information

So, the White Nights Hotel (St. Petersburg) is located 3 kilometers from the metro station Dybenko Street. Also from the hotel you can pretty quickly get to Pulkovo Airport. Since it is 20 kilometers from here. A Moscow Railway Station is 14 km away.

The hotel has free Wi-Fi and private parking. There is also a sauna, restaurant, gift shop and tour desk. The reception is open around the clock, in addition, guests are available expedited or individual registration of their arrival / departure. Also, guests can order room service. And, what is nice, the White Nights Hotel (Peter) allows pets. And no additional fees are required.

Other options

Many solo travelers also love to come to St. Petersburg. The White Nights Hotel offers separate apartments for them. Under the terms they are the same as the 2-seater "Standards", only 7 days of stay in them cost 13,300 rubles. But the price includes the free cancellation service.

In general, this hotel can be an excellent budget option for temporary residence in the popular cultural center of Russia. You will only have to pay for your stay in advance of your arrival. Money is transferred by bank transfer. To clarify the details, the administration will contact a potential guest after he sends a reservation request.
