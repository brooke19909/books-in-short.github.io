The recreation center "Golden Sands" (Kirillovka)

The urban village of Kirillovka is one of the most popular resorts on the Azvian Sea. Affordable prices and a warm sea annually attract a huge number of vacationers. It offers tourists more than 300 pensions and dispensaries. The recreation center "Golden Sands" is one of the most famous recreational centers of the resort.

Infrastructure of Kirillovka



The village itself is located between two estuaries - Molochny and Utlyuk, which allows, in addition to a beach holiday, to develop spa treatment. These estuaries are known for their healing silt mud. The resort is under active development. In addition to continuous improvement in the number of rooms, the infrastructure is developing. Recently, a modern water park and dolphinarium have been built.

The beaches of Kirillovka



The main beaches of the resort are located on two braids - Fedotova and Peresyp. They are ideal for families with children. All beaches are sandy, with the presence of a small shell. For a while you have to get used to walking on the shell water for a while. Some see this as a positive effect in the form of free foot massage. One of the entertainments of swimmers is to "swim to the spit." It is on almost every beach and is located at an average distance of about 20 meters from the coast.

All the beaches of the urban village of Kirillovka are municipal, and the entrance to them is free and free. However, there are additional services in the form of paid sunbeds and umbrellas, water activities, beach bar complexes and cafes. Entrepreneurial locals from morning to night offer directly on the beach to buy Azov dried and smoked fish, corn, cold beer, take pictures with exotic animals, as well as other "basic" beach products.

How to get to Kirillovka?

The easiest way to come to the resort is with your own car. The distance from Kiev is approximately 700 km. If you use public transport, it is best to get with a change in Zaporozhye or Melitopol, where in the summer minibuses run every 30 minutes in summer. Travel time from Zaporozhye - 2 hours, from Melitopol - 1 hour. Base "Golden Sands" (Kirillovka) is located near the bus station. Therefore, it can be reached on foot or use the services of private drivers, of which there are plenty at the bus station. Some fixed-route taxis transport vacationers to recreation centers.
