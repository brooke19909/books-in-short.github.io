How to sell an apartment without intermediaries: step by step instructions. How to sell apartments so as not to be deceived

As soon as the owner of the apartment is thinking about selling, there is no doubt that you should contact a realtor. The specialist will do everything in the highest rank, find the right buyers and help arrange all the documents. But the commission will also have to pay a considerable amount. Many realtors charge 1% of the transaction value. It is easy to calculate how much money the seller loses. But you just have to study some of the nuances, and housing will be able to be sold absolutely safely without intermediaries. So how to sell an apartment?

We carry out an assessment of real estate

Before placing an advertisement for the sale of an apartment, first of all, it is necessary to determine its cost. This moment is quite responsible. If you put too low a price, the deal will not work out profitable. But overpriced housing can be on sale for years. Before you sell apartments, it is worth exploring the real estate market in a particular region. To do this, for example, you can look through the pages of the corresponding portals and forums for several days and look for similar ads. Initially, you can set a price that will slightly exceed the market. If the demand for real estate is low, the amount indicated at the beginning will have to be slightly reduced.



There are a number of factors that affect the final cost per square meter. First of all, this is the location of the house. If the living space is located in a not very prestigious area where there is no good transport interchange, it will not be possible to sell it at a high price. The price can also be affected by the age of the house, its number of storeys, and the availability of auxiliary communications. Selling an apartment that was built less than 3 years ago will be much faster and more profitable. After all, this is almost a new building. The view from the window also matters.

The level of solvency of the population in a particular area also indirectly affects the value of real estate. It will not be too expensive to sell housing if people live on minimum wages. Even those who pre-set aside funds for the acquisition of real estate, try to consider cheaper options.

How to sell an apartment fast and expensive?

To change the level of solvency of the population or to move housing to another area will not work, because it is beyond your strength. But here you can still slightly increase the cost of housing. Everything must be done so that the apartment visually looks more expensive. Most people make the final decision only after examining a potential purchase. If the apartment looks perfect, you can sell it for good money.



Simple cosmetic repairs can significantly increase the chances of an early sale of real estate. One has only to glue the wallpaper, paint the ceilings and tidy the balcony. It will be possible to sell a room apartment at the price of a piece if you make a good renovation. It is recommended to entrust this work to a true professional. Living space with designer renovation always costs much more. Experts felt that after simple finishing work, the value of real estate increases by 20-30%.

We are preparing an apartment for sale

Before placing an advertisement for the sale, it is worth bringing the apartment in proper form. After all, potential buyers can come to the "bridegroom" at any time. How to sell an apartment if it is not cleaned? People who want to make a deal will not have the best impression.

First of all, it is necessary to eliminate visible shortcomings. These are cracks that appeared as a result of subsidence of the house, peeling paint on the batteries and windowsill, as well as rusted pipes. Broken plumbing and smudges in the bathroom will clearly push potential buyers away. In addition, if the apartment has not been repaired for a long time, there will be an unpleasant odor. It is worth getting rid of it first.





The clutter of the premises should also be eliminated. Almost all apartments have different little things that are difficult to get rid of. These are souvenirs brought from abroad, packages with unclaimed items, old soft toys, etc. All this is a memory for the owner of the living space. But for a potential buyer, these "values" seem like trash. Those who are determined to sell the apartment without intermediaries should take out all unnecessary to the garage. In the room should be a minimum of things. You need to leave only furniture and basic necessities.

Some pieces of furniture will also have to be disposed of. First of all, this is done in order to visually expand the space. In addition, a potential buyer will probably want to arrange the furniture in his own way. It is necessary to create everything so that another participant in the transaction could show his imagination. Well, if at the time of sale will be able to move to another place altogether. Why? Everything is simple. The sale term of an empty apartment is reduced several times.

We compose an ad correctly

Everyone knows that advertising is the engine of commerce. Selling real estate is no exception. And the advertisement is an advertisement. It is necessary to place information about the sale in various newspapers and on electronic resources. At the same time, it is worth a little money to make the ad stand out among many others. To sell an apartment without intermediaries will quickly allow high-quality text. You can come up with a bright headline that will attract potential buyers.

The body of the ad should be as informative as possible. A potential buyer wants answers to all his questions. Those who seek to sell an apartment in Moscow should not only describe the area, but also indicate its advantage. If there is a metro station within a five-minute walk from the house, you should definitely remember this. All information must be entered compactly, in small sentences. Too long and tedious text will not interest the buyer.

The announcement, of course, must be composed correctly. If necessary, you can use the services of the corrector. The specialist will not only correct spelling, speech or punctuation errors (if any), but also tell you how to make sentences correctly.

How else to advertise a property?

If you need to sell an apartment in Moscow or another city urgently, you need to use every opportunity. You can post information about the sale on television or radio. There are also completely free advertising methods. The announcement can be hung on the porch door or car window. Anyone who needs an apartment in a specific area will definitely read the information on the sale.





So, the ads are compiled, designed and posted / placed in the newspaper, the Internet, on the radio, etc. What next? As soon as information about the sale has become available to potential buyers, you need to be prepared for the fact that there will be a huge number of calls. Well, if in the ad you specified what time it is better to call. It might be worth selling an apartment on vacation. This time can be fully devoted to the transaction. Try to pay maximum attention to each client. The larger the number of people with whom you communicate, the higher the likelihood that housing will be sold in the near future.

How and when to organize a viewing?

Inspecting the apartment is an important step. Only after the client sees the property in person, he will decide on the transaction. Is it worth it to sell an apartment if it is not put in order? We have already raised this question. Recall again: the lack of repair and proper cleaning will not play in favor of the seller.

It should be prepared for the fact that, in addition to potential buyers, realtors will want to visit the apartment. On the phone, they are most likely not to be presented as such. But as soon as they cross the threshold of the house, they will immediately begin to offer their services. You can sell a new apartment without the help of intermediaries. But if the housing does not have a presentable appearance, and you need to make a deal faster, it makes sense to take the help of specialists.

What documents are needed?

If the buyer is ready to complete the purchase and sale transaction, the time has come for paperwork. It is necessary to put in order the passport of the owner of the apartment and the technical passport of the room, in which the main parameters will be indicated. It is also impossible to sell an apartment without the consent of the family members registered in it. The only exceptions are children under 18 years of age. In addition, such documents will be needed: a certificate of the state of the personal account, an extract from the house book, a copy of the financial and personal account, a certificate from the tax office.

The seller and the buyer at the stage of preparing the documentation draw up a preliminary contract. At the same time, a security deposit is paid for the apartment. This will guarantee the good faith of the buyer. Is it worth it now to sell an apartment without collateral - it's hard to decide. Making all the necessary paperwork is a costly procedure. However, most certificates are valid for no more than a month. If the buyer at the last moment refuses the transaction, the money will be wasted. The deposit is not returned to the buyer.

How to sell an apartment if it belongs to several owners?

It is possible to sell a share of the apartment. Moreover, the deal has a huge number of nuances. It can not be compared even with the sale of a dorm room or in a communal apartment. The problem is that determining the boundaries of a share is quite difficult. The co-owner of the premises can occupy the whole room. But this does not mean that he is its rightful owner.

You can sell a share in the apartment only if the housing is privatized. In this case, the entire room will have a common personal account. According to the legislation of the Russian Federation, an apartment that is owned by several people can be sold only with the consent of all of them. The right to acquire a share is primarily acquired by the co-owner.

The wife has the opportunity to sell her husband an apartment or her share. A deal can be made with an unauthorized buyer only by agreement with the immediate family registered in this housing. A notification is sent to the owners of the property, which indicates information about the upcoming sale. If within a month another owner does not make a decision on the acquisition of the sold part, it can be offered to unauthorized persons.

Not everyone knows whether it is possible to sell a share of an apartment if minor children live in it. It is difficult to answer this question. Each situation is individual. A decision can be made in a judicial proceeding if the owners cannot agree on the details.





How to draw up a contract of sale?

Before selling an apartment, it is necessary to clarify the correctness of registration of basic documents. When a buyer is found, this will help to conclude a contract of sale much faster. This is the main legal document that describes the nuances of the transaction.

The main data of the contract include the value of the property, a description of the subject of the transaction. In addition, it indicates the passport data of the parties, as well as lists the documents that are necessary for the sale of real estate. If the buyer previously made a deposit, this information is also displayed in the contract. The main document for the sale of real estate describes the obligations of the parties, their mutual agreements, and also indicates the date of the transaction.

The contract of sale is the main document by which money can be transferred. If cashless payments are made, this must be indicated. Information is also entered on the financial institution through which the transaction is executed, and the account number.
