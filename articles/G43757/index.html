Hotel in Kaliningrad: which one to choose? Photos, tips and reviews

The city until 1255 bore the name Twangste, Koenigsberg - until 1946 and only after the death of the Soviet party and statesman M.I. Kalinin did it become known as Kaliningrad. The merger of Russian and German cultures makes this city absolutely unique, and completely unlike any other. Guests and tourists are impressed by the architecture, which fits hotels, monuments of different conflicting periods and eras. It remains in memory for a long time, and is carefully guarded by local residents and administrative bodies.



The modern city is an important transport and logistics, automotive and trade center, attracting foreign investors. Luxurious hotels of Kaliningrad (inexpensive and luxury) are open for them. The region has developed scientific and educational and research areas, therefore, cheap hotels, cheap apartments and hostels for students work.

Kaliningrad, Hotel Skipper

This hotel offers its services to people who want to relax in comfort, but at the same time inexpensively. The hotel has 18 rooms that are equipped in a marine style. They have everything they need for a comfortable life. Thanks to the small number of rooms, all customers are provided with a friendly and attentive attitude of the staff. This hotel in Kaliningrad is located in the center. The rooms offer stunning views of the Cathedral and the sea, which makes it even more popular.



Reading reviews of the Skipper Hotel, you can understand that it is ideal for students and anyone who expects to spend a minimum of money on housing. But for people "with claims" such an institution is definitely not suitable.

Hotel Mayak

Mayak Hotel (Kaliningrad) is a branch of Sofrino Hotel Complex. It is located near the city center with all its attractions and historical monuments.



Cozy double and single rooms of economy class at a low price are offered by the Mayak Hotel. Kaliningrad now receives a huge number of guests year-round, and this hotel is a great option for accommodation, individual relaxation or for small tourist groups.

Judging by the reviews of this hotel, we can say that it is ideal for tourists who are interested in the sights of the city and who do not plan to spend any large amounts on housing.

Hotel "Tourist", Kaliningrad

"Tourist" is a hotel and restaurant complex located on one of the main streets of the city leading to the business center and airport.

Guests are offered a choice of Standard, Apartment and Suite rooms.

Hotel "Tourist" (Kaliningrad) provides guests with: a modern banquet room and a conference room, free car parking, a steakhouse and a night club.



Reading reviews of this hotel, you can understand that it is ideal for businessmen who have come here for work, as well as for small corporate groups. According to people staying here, the hotel has a good level of service and adequate cost of living.

Hotel Tchaikovsky

This 4-star hotel is within walking distance of the city center.

The cozy restaurant offers its guests to enjoy a rich selection of European dishes. Free internet access is available here. If you arrived in the city by your own car, then parking is at your disposal.

The staff of this Kaliningrad hotel, the price of accommodation in which is not so high - about 2500 rubles per day, will be happy to arrange a transfer from the airport for you. The 24-hour reception staff will advise you on nearby attractions, and you can also order a taxi if necessary. A laundry service is available here. Smoking is not allowed in the rooms.

This hotel is mostly positive reviews - high level of service, excellent cuisine, lots of additional features. Of the negative opinions, one can only find that you cannot smoke in hotels. But they are left only by adherents of this bad habit.

Hotel "Blues"

This hotel in Kaliningrad is located 10 minutes from the city center. The Amber Museum and the city zoo are located next to the hotel. The airport is half an hour away.



The restaurant offers a large selection of traditional European and Russian hot dishes and snacks. You can enjoy a glass of your favorite drink, as well as chat in the cozy neighboring bar.

Looking through the reviews about this institution, you can find out that this is a very cozy place with excellent cuisine and a large restaurant, where locals love to celebrate weddings and anniversaries.

Hotel HELIOPARK Kaiserhof

This hotel in Kaliningrad is located on the coast of Pregol, in the city center. Near the hotel there is the Baltic Expo Exhibition Center and the Cathedral.

The hotel serves a delicious, complimentary breakfast in the morning. The hotel's sunny restaurant serves international and local dishes. In the lobby bar you will find a wide selection of different drinks, in addition, in this place you can read any book taken in the hotel library.

Evaluating reviews about this hotel, you understand that this place is intended for people who value high quality of life, excellent cuisine and good service. But the cost of living here corresponds to all this.

Hotel "Triumph Palace"

The four-star hotel in Kaliningrad Triumph Palace is located in the business heart of the city. Nearby is the South Bus Station. From here you can reach the Holy Family Church of Kirche in 5 minutes on foot. The Oceans Museum is 15 minutes from here.

Reviews about this place say that this place will be appreciated by businessmen, as well as people seeking a relaxing holiday with a high level of service.

Hotel "Dona"

Hotel "Dona" is located in the city center, near the Rosgarten Gate, as well as the Kaliningrad Museum of History and Art. The hotel has comfortable rooms, a cafe on site, and free parking. If you look at reviews about this institution, you can find out that a well-trained, hospitable staff is working here, ready to receive guests around the clock.

Hotel Moscow

Moscow is a three-star hotel located in the business part of Kaliningrad. The zoo is located directly opposite it, and the city center can be reached in fifteen minutes. The train station is 4.5 kilometers away.



Reviews of the Moscow Hotel show that this establishment has a very convenient location, good cuisine in a restaurant located on its territory, as well as an adequate price level.
