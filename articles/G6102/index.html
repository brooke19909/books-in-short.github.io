How to stick fabric to fabric



As a rule, in this way they either decorate the finished product (applications, flowers) or strengthen the upper fabric. The second option is used mainly when sewing jackets, coats, jackets. In such products there are some details that, without additional sizing, do not hold the shape well enough. These include collar, collar, cuffs, etc. It is also sometimes necessary to glue the bottom of the trousers or skirts in order to more accurately design the edge of the product. In any case, adhesive fabrics are most often used, and if you need to glue the application, you can use a thermal gun. The use of PVA glue is advisable only in the manufacture of panels of fabric on the wall. Considering that this glue is water-soluble, it is not worth using it to fix the usual application on a T-shirt or children's jeans. Otherwise, you risk after washing to get separately the jewelry from the washing machine and the product separately.

How to glue doublerin, sickle or other adhesive material

To strengthen an excessively soft fabric, use adhesive woven or non-woven

materials. A layer of glue is applied on them, which does not stick until it is heat-treated with a hot iron. How to stick fabric to fabric? With a warm but not hot iron, iron the fabric quickly so that all folds are smoothed out. Then carefully lay out the parts that need to be strengthened on an adhesive basis. Make sure that the adhesive layer is facing the fabric. Then use a hot iron to smooth the parts thoroughly. It is not recommended to use steaming, since in this case the adhesive base may lose its properties. If wrinkles are formed or small air bubbles remain, you can additionally iron the fabric. In the event that this does not help, you should steam the adhesive material and repeat the whole procedure again, but with a different piece. Parts of the product requiring sizing must be laid out on the adhesive material. Cutting to the moment of heat treatment is not recommended, since in the process you can move the part outside the adhesive. In this case, you have to redo the work.
