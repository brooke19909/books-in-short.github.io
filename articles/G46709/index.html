What is attractive camp "Island of childhood" in Tyumen

Camp "Island of childhood" in Tyumen is a great solution for parents who want their child to have a great summer vacation. Of course, some prefer to send the child to the cottage or to the village for the whole summer, while others decide that the son or daughter will be at home. Nevertheless, it was in the children's camp "Island of Childhood" in Tyumen that boys and girls break away from the heart, enjoy a rich and interesting vacation to the fullest. Moms and dads should consider the issue of acquiring a ticket to a fun summer for their beloved child.





Information about the children's camp "Island of childhood" in Tyumen

Parents should find out what conditions and opportunities in this camp:

  • The Island of Childhood is located on the territory of 14 hectares. A large area indicates that the camp has enough space for entertainment, active games, festivals and other fun and exciting events.
  • Vacationers settle in rooms for 4-6 people. The rooms have a large closet where children can fold their clothes, and each has their own personal bedside table in which you can put personal hygiene items.
  • There is a toilet and shower on each floor. In the lobby, children can watch TV, drink water from the cooler.
  • In the camp "Island of childhood" in Tyumen, complex meals 5 times a day. They feed children with proven, high-quality products, and also fill the diet with fruits, vegetables and sweets.
  • A child in the camp "Island of childhood" will be able to engage in favorite sports, take part in competitions and concerts, as well as attend clubs where you can learn something new and interesting.
  • The tent camp on the island of Childhood makes the leisure of boys and girls even more interesting. Here they will be able to gain the skills of a tourist wild holiday.
  • And for children there is a playground, funny carousels, trampolines, pools with plastic balls.




On the territory of the "Island of childhood" there is an activity for boys and girls with different interests, preferences and hobbies.
