How to choose a refrigerator for a car

Hot summer, trips out of town - how many pleasant memories are associated with such a vacation! But to make your picnic really comfortable, you should buy such a useful thing as a refrigerator for a car. It is compact, allows you to preserve the freshness of products and will be simply indispensable in travel.





The main difference between the device and a thermal bag is that it is able to maintain a low temperature for a longer time. You can not worry about the safety of food and drinks during the long journey, if you have a container in the car in which the refrigeration unit is built-in.

By types of devices, it is possible to divide into three groups: a compressor refrigerator for a car, thermoelectric and adsorption.

The first view is similar in design to a similar household appliance. It uses a one-piece compressor. A distinctive feature is the economical consumption of electricity. Such an aggregate for a long time keeps products frozen. Low temperature is created very quickly, and the dimensions of the device can be selected for a wide variety. If it is connected to the network of the machine, then when the voltage drops , the device automatically turns off. Very convenient refrigerator for a car from the cigarette lighter. Such a device can be built into the interior of the car. There is an opportunity to pick up freestanding models.





The second type has a lot of positive characteristics. It is equipped with an aggregate consisting of semiconductor modules. His work uses the Peltier effect. The device is silent, safe to operate, has a compact size. Very reliable and durable. Its uniqueness lies in the fact that such a refrigerator for a car is capable of not only cooling products, but also heating them. In this case, the temperature range can vary from -27 ¬∞ C to +65 ¬∞ C. All models of such devices are stable in vibration and are not susceptible to mechanical damage. Even after turning off the power, they maintain the temperature offline for six hours.





The adsorption structure device has a large volume. But at the same time, it has significant disadvantages: it does not tolerate shaking. On the car in which such a device is located, you need to drive very carefully.

A portable refrigerator for a car can be connected to a household electrical outlet or to your car. Therefore, it is very convenient, it can be successfully used in travel, in a hotel, in a hospital, as well as in a tourist camp. The device is even suitable for storing medicines.

Devices for cooling products are produced by companies such as Mystery, BOMANN, WAECO and other manufacturers. As an example, get acquainted with the model "ATLANTIC". Its volume is 24 liters, it has an adapter for connecting to a 220V power supply. Its thermoelectric case is made with polyurethane filling. It works in heat and cold.

Undoubtedly, a very useful thing is a refrigerator for a car. The price of the device can vary from 3 thousand rubles. and higher, but you can always pick up a model for any wallet.
