Want to know how many kilometers are from Moscow to Orenburg?

Those who love to travel will surely enjoy the city of Orenburg, because you can find classes for every taste in it: there are many monuments and museums in the city, there are theaters and the famous embankment. Well, the craftswomen are ready to please you with the best downy Orenburg shawls - the main highlight of this city.





The easiest way to go to Orenburg is from Moscow, which is why we will consider this option of travel. Note that in the same ways you can get in the opposite direction. You can move from one city to another by train or plane, as well as by car or bus.

How many km from Moscow to Orenburg

To travel, you need to know the distance between cities. Those who are going to travel in the near future are most concerned about how many kilometers are from Moscow to Orenburg. In fact, it all depends on how exactly you plan to move. So, the direct distance is 1227 km, and 1454 km will have to be covered by car on the roads.

A train

Traveling by rail is the most traditional option, although you will have to spend about 18 hours on the way. Despite how many kilometers are from Moscow to Orenburg, you can choose options from reserved seats to luxury. Depending on the class, the price will also change. So, a reserved seat will cost from 2,300 rubles, a coupe will cost 4-7 thousand rubles, and the cost of a Lux class ticket can reach 12,300 rubles.

By the way, you can not worry about the quality of cars, because the reviews about them are exceptionally good.

The car

If you like traveling by car, you probably wondered how many kilometers from Moscow to Orenburg are by car. The answer is simple - 1454 km. This seems a lot to some, but those who are used to traveling can easily cover all this distance in one day. The road will go mainly along the M5 highway, which is well known to residents of the middle lane.

From large cities en route from Moscow to Orenburg will be Togliatti and Samara, where you can also stay if you wish and opportunity.

The approximate price for gasoline for a car is 3000 rubles.





Airplane

Despite how many kilometers are from Moscow to Orenburg, the plane will quickly take you from one point to another. So, the flight itself will take a little less than 2 hours, but do not forget that you need to add time to pass the control.

Airplanes between cities do not fly regularly, only several times a week. However, their price pleasantly surprises, because they will cost about like a train ticket. So, the cost starts from 4,500 rubles, and flights are made by both domestic and foreign airlines.
