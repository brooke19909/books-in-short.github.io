Cottage Village "Malakhovskoe Lake": reviews

Moscow is a huge metropolis, one of the top ten in the world by the number of inhabitants. It is a city rolled up in concrete and asphalt, which is sometimes difficult to breathe.





Cottage villages

Many residents seek salvation in their suburban life. More and more people choose housing outside the city, in the fresh air, preferring to work in the capital and live in the fresh air. This can be seen in the increased demand for cottages in the villages. Developers specifically select areas near the Moscow Ring Road so that future residents have the opportunity not to change their thoroughly familiar lifestyle and work, but to get to it unhindered. Moreover, almost all citizens currently have their own transport. In many cottage villages, single-family homes are being built, in many with cottages for several families. One of them is the cottage village "Malakhovskoe Lake".





Location

About fourteen kilometers from the main artery of the megalopolis - Moscow Ring Road - is the cottage village "Malakhovskoye Ozero". How to get to it, you can easily find out by opening the map. The village is located in the Lyubertsy district. You can get to it by your own transport along one of the two highways: Ryazansky with a transfer to Zhukovskoye or Yegoryevsky. In addition, to avoid intermittent traffic jams on the highway, the village can be reached by rail, that is, by train. She goes to the station "Malakhovka", from where you can walk a half kilometer on foot to the village itself. The trip home takes no more than half an hour.





You can also take various buses and fixed-route taxis, which begin their movement from the Vykhino metro station and will comfortably deliver directly to the village.

A bit of history

Already since ancient times in Malakhovka it was customary among the Moscow elite to rest from the works of the righteous. This has been a unique holiday village since 1885. And at present, the developer has chosen this place for the construction of a low-rise residential complex for a reason.

Why unique? Because it was located in a pine-birch massif that has been preserved to this day among peat mud, ponds, near the lake of the same name with a huge amount of fish. Equipped with beaches for recreation and truly healing air - this is what makes the Malakhovskoye Lake cottage community so prestigious for living. The authors of the project managed to realize in it all their ambitions for the construction of buildings of European level, the organization of a comfortable space.





Description

The project provides for the construction of 199 houses of different floors, designed in a single modern architectural style. In 2010, the developer received a building permit, after which the project was put into operation. Until 2014, the facilities were commissioned in stages. And in 2016, almost all the buildings were already settled and happy new settlers celebrated their so successful acquisitions.

In addition to apartments, the developer also offers for sale luxury mansions up to two hundred square meters in area. There were also four-room apartments and two-level apartments with their own land. The developer does not finish the work, so as not to take away from the future owners their plans and ideas about future housing.

Infrastructure

Lyubertsy district of the Moscow region is the place where the new cottage village "Malakhovskoye Lake" is located. Indeed, a few meters from this settlement lies Malakhovskoye Lake. Actually, it was the lake that gave it its name. As already mentioned, the developer very successfully chose the place on which the cottage village "Malakhovskoye Lake" was built. P. Malakhovka has its own developed infrastructure, which automatically becomes available for residents of the new village. These are schools for children, including music, and kindergartens, and a clinic.





The cottage village "Malakhovskoe Lake", photos testify to this, is located next to the Malakhovsky Park, which has a 19th-century summer garden. It is good to take a leisurely walk in the evening after a working day or relax with the whole family.

In addition to the infrastructure of neighboring Malakhovka, the cottage village "Malakhovskoe Lake" is also rich in its own infrastructure. At present, a restaurant and a shopping center are already pleasing guests. Car enthusiasts can use the services of service stations and car washes. The developer still plans to build a kindergarten.

Village Benefits

As already mentioned, the builder chose the ideal location for his village. Thanks to him, the cottage village "Malakhovskoye Lake" has only the most positive reviews. The biggest plus is the small distance from the metropolis and excellent transport links, which allows you to easily get to work or on business.

Malakhovskoye Lake is cleaned and suitable not only for relaxing on the shore, but also for swimming. By the way, she loves spending her leisure time in this place. On the coast you can play various games, ride rides, sit in sushi bars and cafes. And in the forests surrounding the village there are mushrooms and wild berries in large quantities. Needless to say, rivers flowing nearby! They are full of fish, which cannot but please lovers of a relaxing fishing holiday.

Thus, those who chose this particular cottage village for their permanent residence will not be disappointed in their decision. The excellent ecological situation, the presence of a lake suitable for swimming, a forest that fills the lungs of residents with oxygen - all this speaks in favor of the right choice of owners of houses and apartments in the village of Malakhovskoye Ozero. And given that there is no difficulty with traveling to work in the capital, then this project simply does not have visible minuses.

Even without traveling to the metropolis, you can find everything you need for life directly in the village or in neighboring Malakhovka. The developer fulfilled all his promises and turned the village into a true paradise for lovers of fresh air and a comfortable life.
