Going on a trip: how many km are from Moscow to Saratov

From the capital you can go to the old Volga city of Saratov, not only for business, but also for the weekend, vacation or vacation. Beautiful river views, delicious traditional cuisine, interesting sights - all this will make your stay in Saratov memorable and enjoyable. The distance from Moscow to Saratov is small, so weekend tours are very popular. However, you can go to the city on the Volga by car or train, as well as take a plane.





We determine the distance: how many kilometers are from Moscow to Saratov

If you could travel directly! How quickly then people found themselves in the right places, how much time would have been saved then!

Asking the question of how many kilometers are from Moscow to Saratov, using the map you can get the answer: directly only 727 km.

However, roads and other roads are rarely laid in a straight line.

To get from the capital to Saratov, you have to pass the Moscow, Ryazan, Tambov and Saratov regions.

On the road by car

Departing to Saratov by car, you have to drive about 12 hours and overcome, depending on the chosen route, from 860 to 930 km.

Option 1

Leave the capital on the M-4. A toll road with a length of 100 km leads in the right direction, but due to its high speed, the distance is overcome quickly. The permissible speed for the first hundred kilometers is 130 km / h, which means that the motorist can safely move at a speed of 149 km / h.

The fare on paid sites depends on a number of indicators:

  • car loading capacity;
  • time of movement - night or day;
  • payment method - cash or T-pass.

The minimum payment will be 42 rubles, the highest - 100 rubles.

What does a motorist get for his money? High-quality pavement on a road with several lanes, lighting, a lot of gas stations. However, you can save money and take advantage of the free section of the Kashirskoye highway. Choosing this option, how many kilometers from Moscow to Saratov will have to travel? You need to drive 930 km, the journey will take about 10 hours 45 minutes.

After Kashir, turn left onto R-22 and go through Tambov, Borisoglebsk, Kalininsk to Saratov.

Option 2

Drive from Moscow along the Novoryazanskoye shosse onto the M-5. Through Bronnitsy, Kolomna, Lukhovitsy, Ryazan, Shatsk, Nizhny Lomov you need to get to Penza. Going around the large city on the right, you should get onto the R-158 highway, which will lead to Saratov.

How many km from Moscow to Saratov by car in this case? In kilometers, it turns out less, only 860, but the time is longer - about 12 hours. It should be borne in mind that there are only 4 lanes on the road, the traffic is dense.

Option 3

Leave Moscow on R-158 and move along this highway. Such a journey will increase travel time by 1 hour.

Moving along any automobile route, you will have to spend about 80 liters of gasoline, this will cost 2000-2400 rubles.

Air travel is the fastest way

You can fly from Moscow to Saratov from Domodedovo and Sheremetyevo. Carriers are 2 companies: Aeroflot and Saratov Airlines. How many kilometers does Moscow travel from Saratov to airplane? Only 728 km, spending on it 1 hour 13 minutes.





The first flight departs at 09:40, the last - at 20 hours. There are still flights at 15:00, 17:25 and 18:50.

Air travel is the fastest way to be in another city. Therefore, most passengers are willing to pay a fairly high price for tickets from 5,285 to 10,873 rubles. The cost of the flight and the conditions of baggage allowance depend on the carrier company. Please note that there are seasonal and calendar discounts on airline tickets.

To Saratov from the capital by bus

You can leave from Moscow to Saratov by bus daily from the South Gate bus station. On the road, the bus is a little over 14 hours, driving the distance from Moscow to Saratov in 840 km. Arriving transport to the city bus station in Saratov, Moskovskaya street, 170.

The first flight leaves at 15:00, then the flights follow with a short time interval: at 15:45, 16:45, 17:00, 18:00, 18:30, 18:45, etc. The last flight leaves at 23 h

Bus tickets will cost from 1259 to 1356 rubles.





Coupe or reserved seat
