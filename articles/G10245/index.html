This war of mine

The simulator of survival in a military conflict from the company 11 bit studios. Only the player has to see the other side of the coin: they have to manage not a detachment of brave warriors who destroy the enemy left and right, but a group of civilians who have taken refuge from the horrors of war in the nearest dilapidated building.

The project was included in the list of the best paid games on Android almost immediately after the release. Its cost is approximately 800 rubles without discounts. There is no paid content or advertising; upon the release of updates, it is enough to simply reinstall the application.

The plot is based on real events (the war in Serbia), the player will have to get food, ammunition and materials for the repair of the shelter - to prevent any member of the group from dying.

The mobile application has a number of scenarios to choose from. Two or three people are given at the start. During the game, other survivors may come to ask for asylum, so the number of members of the squad is compensated.
