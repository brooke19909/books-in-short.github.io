Mineralnye Vody bus station and its schedule

The city of Mineralnye Vody is located in the south of the Stavropol Territory and is a convenient transportation hub. In Mineralnye Vody, the car and the railway station, as well as the airport, are located not far from each other. With the help of bus stations you can get to different cities of the European part of the country and the North Caucasus.

Features of the suburban bus station

If you arrive in the city by train, it is quite easy to get to the Mineralnye Vody suburban bus station. You need to go a little south of the railway station to the Moscow Industrial Bank and near it turn to the west, on Gagarin Street. The bus station is located in house number 98. Minibuses number 2, 3, 6, 8, 14 go to it from different parts of the city. Grocery stores, a tire service, a public toilet, a drugstore and even a monument to the 1938 howitzer are located near the building. The airport is about 5 kilometers away, take a taxi for 10 minutes.

Buses from here go mainly to nearby cities, for example, Essentuki, Lermontov, Pyatigorsk, Zheleznovodsk. Bus number 108 goes to the civilian village west of the city. Flight number 119 goes to Kumagorsk. Flight 128 follows south to Goryachevodsk and makes a stop not far from the waterfall and the end point of Pyatigorsk trams. Essentuki Bus Station can be reached by 133 buses.

Features of the new bus station

The second bus station is more modern, located in the southern part of the city near the Ruslan market on Sovetskaya Street, which is part of the E-117 highway. International flights

From the Mineralnye Vody bus station you can buy tickets for international flights. At 11:55 a bus leaves for Sukhumi (21 hours on the way).

The southern directions are Tbilisi and Yerevan. Even a local flight with departure at 7 in the morning is organized to the capital of Georgia. Passing buses from Moscow, Petersburg, Stavropol go to Yerevan.
