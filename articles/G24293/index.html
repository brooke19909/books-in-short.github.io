Prestige Hotel (Vityazevo): description, features and reviews of tourists

In one of the best and most comfortable Black Sea resorts, the village of Vityazevo, the new modern Prestige Hotel has hospitably opened its doors. Vityazevo has a well-developed infrastructure, so your stay here will be comfortable and cozy.





Opened in 2014, the mini-hotel is located on the third line from the coast.

Description

The Prestige Hotel (Vityazevo) is a three-story fenced building, which is under 24-hour security. It is built in a classic style. There is a ground floor, which also has rooms (55 in total). The beach in the hotel area is shallow, so it is ideal for families with children. The swimming area, although public, is not crowded. In addition, the beach has a wide sandy strip, where adults love to arrange volleyball battles, and children in the sand are just expanse for games.





The distance to the seashore is 700 meters. It's not far, about 10 minutes on foot. On site you can sunbathe by the pool. And for those who do not like to be in the sun for a long time, there is a closed terrace where you can order refreshments. Children can spend their time on the playground, and for those who like to actively move, the hotel has tables for playing table tennis.

The famous Byzantium theme park is a 15-minute walk from the hotel. Optionally, guests can visit the water park and the dolphinarium. For them, grocery markets and souvenir shops work all day. The mini-hotel has its own free parking for cars. Check-in time is 14:00, eviction is 12:00.

To make your stay as comfortable as possible, the mini-hotel Prestige (Vityazevo) offers its guests a sufficient number of services:

sights
