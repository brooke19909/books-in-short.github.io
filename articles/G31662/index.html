The recreation center "Forest Fairy Tale", Yekaterinburg - the territory of comfortable rest

30 km from the city of Yekaterinburg in the village of Baltym, the recreation center "Forest Fairy" is waiting for its guests. It is located on the shores of the unique Baltim Lake. The recreation center is proud of the high level of service and developed infrastructure. It includes: several cafes offering different cuisines, a Green-Bar with a cozy hall for parties, a barbecue room that always offers tasty and fresh barbecue, a large banquet room, gazebos for company relaxation, areas for self-cooking barbecue, a rental point for sports and other equipment , recreation areas, several baths and horseback riding.





Guests are provided with comfortable accommodation and maximum entertainment, both summer and winter. The entire territory of the complex is treated against mosquitoes and ticks.

Residence

The recreation center "Forest Fairy Tale" (Yekaterinburg) provides a large selection of apartments. It offers alpine houses and comfortable cottages. There are several types of them:

  1. Big hut: designed for a company of ten people.
  2. Medium hut: here can accommodate up to eight people.
  3. Small hut: designed for four vacationers.

In any home, at the request of the guests, an additional place can be installed.





All cottages have modern furniture and household appliances. Be sure to have a kitchen area and a toilet with shower. Near the houses and cottages there are equipped places, sheltered by a gazebo, for cooking barbecue and eating.

The recreation center "Forest Fairy Tale" (Yekaterinburg) offers comfortable double alpine houses, hidden from prying eyes. They have all the necessary amenities, including a bathroom and kitchen.

Nutrition

The food system offered by the recreation center is diverse.

Guests can cook their own meals in the kitchen, which is in every hut. It is equipped with everything necessary for cooking and eating.

If there is no desire to cook, you can choose the kitchen of any of the cafes located in the recreation center.

"Forest Fairy Tale" (recreation center, Yekaterinburg, Baltim) offers simple meals in the cafe "Nautilus". It serves Caucasian and Russian cuisines and offers a huge selection of soft drinks. According to vacationers, it is much more comfortable and tastier in a cafe with real Hungarian cuisine. They are prepared only in cast-iron dishes and served exquisitely.





In the barbecue "Forest Fairy Tale" they cook amazing meat. Reviews almost a carbon copy: "It is impossible to break away!".

There are in the base of Green-Bar. It has a huge selection of alcohol, cocktails (including alcoholic drinks), fruits, etc. This bar is a great place for organizing banquets. It accommodates up to forty guests.

Reservation

When booking, you need to make 100% prepayment for rented housing or a gazebo. In case of cancellation of the rental or inability to arrive, the prepayment is not refundable.

Check in to the base for cars of any capacity is paid. To arrange a gazebo for rent, you need to inform the state number of the car and its brand. If the company arrived by several cars, one of them will be allowed free entry to the internal parking (the rule is valid for renting gazebos).

"Forest Fairy Tale" (recreation center, Yekaterinburg), how to get there

How to get to the place of rest, administrators tell when booking a place to live or gazebos. If necessary, a paid transfer to the "Forest Tale" is organized. If in general, then first vacationers need to get to the village of Verkhnyaya Pyshma. Then take a taxi to the base.

Base address: Yekaterinburg city, Starotagilsky tract, 23 km, Lake Baltym.
