Hotel Shell Beach Hotel & Spa 4 * (Hammamet, Tunisia): overview, description, rooms and reviews of tourists

The Shell Beach Hotel & Spa 4 * resort is very popular among guests visiting Tunisia. Here, traditional Arabian luxury is organically combined with European standards of service.





Hotel location

Less than 500 meters from the luxurious beach is the Shell Beach Hotel & Spa 4 *. Tunisia, Hammamet, Yasmin district, 8050 - the address of this resort. It is only 10 km from the historic city center, where the bulk of the attractions are concentrated. Carthage International Airport is 65 km away from this hotel.

If you decide to relax in Shell Beach Hotel & Spa 4 *, be sure to visit the following tourist sites:

  • medina Yasmin (1 km);
  • Hammamet seaport (1 km);
  • Museum of the History of Religion (1 km);
  • Villa of Sebastian George (6 km);
  • Neapolitan Museum (20 km).

Number of rooms

This hotel has 132 guest rooms. You can choose one of the following accommodation options:

  • Family rooms are suitable for relaxing with your family or friends. Two rooms have large and twin beds. To accommodate an additional guest is a folding soft sofa. The furnished balcony offers pool views.
  • Standard rooms can be designed for single, double and triple occupancy. Guests can take one and a half beds. Also in the rooms are armchairs and coffee tables.
  • Suite is the best accommodation option in this hotel. The room is a spacious studio, which combines the functions of a bedroom and a living room. It is worth noting the presence of a small kitchen area. There is also access to a balcony equipped with garden furniture.




Room features

Everything you need for a good rest and comfortable stay is in the rooms of the Shell Beach Hotel & Spa 4 *. Hammamet will provide you with a warm welcome. In your apartment you can use the following options:

  • Private furnished balcony with views of the pool or the picturesque surroundings (not in all rooms);
  • combined bathroom equipped with a bath or shower, as well as an additional hairdryer;
  • individually controlled air conditioning system;
  • TV connected to a satellite dish (channel package includes one Russian-language program);
  • landline telephone, which will provide you with a direct connection with the reception at any time of the day;
  • a reliable safe with a combination lock will ensure the safety of your valuables, documents and money;
  • a small refrigerator, the use of which is possible at an additional cost.

Entertainment

For the rest to be bright and full, guests need entertainment. In the Shell Beach Hotel & Spa 4 * hotel, you can organize your leisure as follows:

  • an outdoor pool where you can swim at any time of the year thanks to the presence of heating;
  • terrace with sunbeds and parasols;

Services

All the services necessary for a quality stay will be provided to you at the Shell Beach Hotel & Spa 4 *. Tunisia, Hammamet - a welcoming resort that will delight you with the following service:

  • room service;
  • the possibility of forming an individual diet menu;
  • copying documents and using fax (the service is available at the reception);

Supply system

Guests of the Shell Beach Hotel & Spa 4 * (Tunisia) can eat half-board. This concept implies a full breakfast, including drinks, as well as a hearty dinner.

Also, guests have the opportunity to eat on an all-inclusive basis. This service implies the following features:

  • breakfast, lunch and dinner in buffet style (including drinks);
  • from half past nine in the morning until 23:00, local alcohol is available for guests, as well as soft drinks in the lobby bar;
  • from 10:00 to 18:00, the poolside bar serves cocktails, snacks and ice cream;
  • From 16:00 to midnight, there is a Moorish cafe where you can drink traditional Arabic tea and smoke a hookah.




Additional Information

It is worth getting acquainted with additional information about the hotel, going on vacation to Tunisia Lodge Shell Beach Hotel & Spa 4 *. It is worth paying attention to the following data:

  • Registration and check-in of the guests is carried out in the period from 14:00 to midnight;

General impression

The most different opinions can be heard about the Shell Beach Hotel & Spa 4 * hotel (Tunisia). Reviews focus on the fact that the decor of the hotel is quite old. This is also exacerbated by the fact that employees make little effort to care for her. True, a large amount of greenery brightens up the situation, which creates a life-giving shadow. Therefore, this hotel is chosen by active tourists who spend little time on the territory and in the room.

A fundamentally different attitude towards local and foreign tourists is one of the key problems of the Shell Beach Hotel & Spa 4 * hotel (Hammamet). Reviews indicate that quite strict orders are established for travelers. But local tourists behave noisily, leave trash everywhere, and no one makes any comments about this. This is another reason why the hotel is not suitable for lovers of spending time in the room or by the pool.
