Water parks in Poland: list, photos and reviews

Poland is a country with a temperate climate. In summer, the air temperature is +16, closer to the southern part +19 degrees, and in winter - from -1 to -4.5 ¬∞ .

Precipitation falls in the maximum amount in the mountainous regions - up to 1800 mm, and in the plains - up to 500 mm.

Therefore, winters in Poland are quite snowy, and summers are cool and humid. And in view of such climatic features, lovers of resort vacations during the holiday season can hardly afford to relax in natural conditions. Fortunately, many modern complexes have been built in Poland: water parks, hotel aquazones, indoor pools.





On their territory, both adults and children can have a great time.

Water park "Reda" (Poland)

The small and picturesque city of Reda is located between the cities of Gdansk and Sopot. A feature of this town is a water park of the same name.

This is a magnificent complex of medium size. There are several places for swimming: a large pool, a river (along which you can swim on air mattresses), as well as a children's pool with a ship and slides.





The river overflows around the entire water park. You can also go to it from a high hill.

And in the pool with artificial waves only guests with a height of more than 1.4 meters are allowed to be!

The main ‚Äúfigures‚Äù of the aquacomplex are sharks, which are located in a special aquarium-pool behind glass. They can be seen rolling down the hill. The administration guarantees 100% safety for vacationers from these rather big fish.

There is also a jacuzzi bathtub located in the vent of an impromptu volcano.

In general, the atmosphere in the water park is warm, as is the temperature. And if it seems cold, then there is a sauna. However, children are not allowed into it.

Sopot (Poland)

The water park is the most popular in the country, but it is slightly smaller in size than Reda. But one of the largest.





In general, Sopot is a famous resort on the Baltic coast. Clear water and golden sand, a muddy bottom do not leave anyone indifferent. But rainy weather does not allow to fully enjoy water bathing for lovers of sunbathing and aqua procedures.

Sopot is located near Gdansk, as well as the city of Gdynia - in the Trieste.

In the water park, guests will find as many as three indoor pools, one of which is for children, and the rest are sports. But that is not all. In the open air there are two more huge pools, one of which is open all year.

There is a pool with artificial geysers, water flows and an island. There are also two slides in the water park, whose height is 12 m: the first is 110 m long, and the second, steeper, is 70 m.

The water temperature is 34 degrees.

For adults there is a sauna, steam room, solarium, aquamassage, massage room, jacuzzi, as well as a bar and billiards.

Water parks in hotels

In recent years, it has become popular both in Poland and in other countries to open the so-called water parks at hotels. These are whole water complexes - with slides, recreation areas and cafes.

Such a striking representative of the new water park in Poland are:

  1. Hotel "Golimbieshki" in Miko≈Çajki.
  2. Hotel Verde Monatana in Kudowa-Zdroj.

"Green Island"

This new water park is located in the hotel complex Verde Monatana. This is a whole paradise for lovers of water treatments. The visitors are provided with a pool with geysers, a sports and children's pool, water rides, a slide of a total length of 40 m, as well as saunas, a shower, a laconia.

Opening hours: daily from 8.00 to 22.00.

Rest in Mikolajki

There is also a very popular water park "Miko≈Çajki" (Poland). According to reviews, both adults and children adore it, not only residents of this country, but also tourists from all over the world.

The aquacomplex is located within the Golymbieshki hotel. There are pools:

  • the first is 26 m long and 13 m wide, 1.7-2.6 m deep;
  • the second - 21 m long, 11 m wide, 1.7 m deep, with geysers, and the waves that they emit are so truthful that they seem to be real, not artificial;
  • the third - with hydromassage;
  • the fourth is for children.

There are a variety of slides. There is also a sports outdoor pool 25 m long, 16 wide and 2 m deep. It is intended for professional swimming.





Jacuzzi in the water park of several types: calcium and mud. Such baths are quite healthy.

On the territory of the water park there are special places - terraces, designed for relaxation, sunbathing and a simple contemplation of local beauties.

Very good cuisine in the restaurant: varied and hearty, good bar with cocktails and other drinks. And what is most pleasant and important, friendly staff. As for the prices, they are quite acceptable. The price of the ticket to the water park also includes a towel, which must be returned at the exit.

In addition to water procedures, there are bowling, billiards, a golf club for vacationers, and a trampoline and slot machines for children. By the way, for vacationers at the hotel entrance to the water park is free. Also in Miko≈Çajki (near the hotel) there is a boat station and an equestrian club. This allows you to diversify the rest. At the boat station you can rent kayaks, a catamaran, a motor boat or oars, a yacht, a kayak.

And in the equestrian club you can walk around the beautiful stable and even ride horses. The instructor will help with all the necessary nuances to those who do this for the first time. Kids are offered pony rides. There is also an excursion ride on a horse through the area.
