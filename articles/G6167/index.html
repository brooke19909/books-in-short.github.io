Varshava Hotel 2 * (Golden Sands, Bulgaria): hotel description, reviews

Sunny Bulgaria has long been famous for its resorts and attracted tourists with its mild climate, warm sea, delicious national cuisine and the friendly attitude of polite hosts, traditionally friendly to tourists from Russia. A wide selection of excursion offers diversifies the rest, and the budget cost of services allows the country to remain one of the main directions of Russian mass tourism. A tour to the Varshava Hotel is a great solution for a pleasant and affordable vacation on the golden Black Sea coast.

Location: modern resort in a natural environment





The complex is located on the territory of the main tourist brand of the coast - Golden Sands National Natural Park. Varshava Hotel is conveniently located in the very center of the tourist "heart" of Bulgaria. The city of Golden Sands was built in 1956. Architects and builders created a resort paradise intended to become a tourist mecca of the Bulgarian Black Sea coast. It was originally planned to create 60 hotels for receiving guests, but now this figure has doubled.

In the 90s, privatization was carried out, thanks to which significant investments began to come into the tourist infrastructure of the resort. The quality of services provided by vacationers has significantly improved and rose to a high level of European traditions. Without exaggeration, the Golden Sands resort can be called a tourist metropolis, in the hotels of which (among them the Warsaw Hotel) at the same time up to 30 thousand people can relax. The warm and mild climate allows the tourist season to last six months - from May to October. The ability to plan your vacation in such a wide time range is an additional incentive for tourists from different countries to visit this place.

15 kilometers from the resort area is Varna - the third largest city in Bulgaria, a cultural, financial and commercial center and the largest port. The ancient Black Sea ancient settlement is famous for its museums and exhibitions. The History Museum features the world's oldest gold items made 600 years ago. Museums of archeology and Renaissance, naval and nature science expositions - visiting Varna has become a tradition for vacationers on the golden beaches of the resort area. Tourists often describe the impressions of visiting Varna in the most enthusiastic terms. A special place among the reviews is occupied by the zoo and the dolphinarium located in it - a popular place of entertainment for many guests of Varna.

Hotel and rooms: on the basis of reasonable sufficiency





Among a significant number of offers, it is difficult to immediately choose a hotel suitable for all parameters. Varshava Hotel is the most suitable place where you can conveniently sit and have fun spending the time intended for relaxation. A beautiful eleven-story building, equipped with 3 elevators, is located in the green part of the resort area. The main building was erected in 1960, but was subsequently renovated and brought to the modern standards of tourist facilities. The second building of the complex took the first vacationers in 2008 and provides services at the 3 * level. Between the buildings is equipped with an internal passage. A distance of 350 meters, which separates the main entrance to the hotel from the sea, can be taken by road train or walk along a picturesque park alley.

The complex has 126 double and 14 single rooms. All have balconies with sea or resort views. In a standard set of beautification rooms (area 12m 2 ) are equipped with:

- sleeping places and places for storage of things;

- bath or shower;

- cable or satellite television;

- fan;

- refrigerator;

- telephone;

- a cosmetic set for the bathroom.

Air-conditioned rooms also have sea views. The complex has Internet and Wi-Fi, a conference room, a medical office, massage, fitness, free parking for customers' cars and a shop. For an additional payment, Varshava Hotel 2 * provides laundry services and safe rental. Guest reviews focus on another hotel service - tourists who have stayed in the hotel for more than 6 days are provided with free transport to the airport upon departure.

Recreation and entertainment: within walking distance





The hotel offers vacationers a standard set of entertainment, similar to those operated by all budget-friendly hotels in Bulgaria. 2 stars (according to the hotel "ranking table") determine the necessary minimum provided to guests of the resort. The rectangular pool with a children's "paddling pool" and the recreation area equipped with plank beds and umbrellas. For guests of the hotel there is a restaurant and an Internet cafe. Vacationers can use the gym and table tennis. The billiards room works on a paid basis.

The restaurant, lobby bar and pool bar serve refreshing drinks and a wide range of cocktails. The menu includes dishes of European and national Bulgarian cuisine. The organizers of the holidays do not forget about the little guests - a section of healthy baby food is presented for vacationers with children.

In addition to the shallow pool, children are given at their disposal a playground equipped with game attractions. At the request of parents, during the absence of adults, a professional nanny provided by the hotel can be with children. Accessibility and a good level of service make family vacations in Bulgaria (prices for which successfully compete with other destinations) one of the main trends in the tourist flow.

Beach: Golden Necklace of the Black Sea





The splendor of the Golden Sands beach area is worth staying separately. Clean air, a coastal line in a green frame of a pine forest and parkland, overflowing horizon and a high bottomless sky - this is how a sunny beach meets visitors. Golden Sands (Bulgaria considers them a precious pearl of its coast), according to one legend, got its splendor because of the gold that was once buried here by ruthless pirates robbing sailors. Outraged by their cruelty, the nature crushed gold bars into sand and scattered it over the sea coast. Apparently these were significant treasures - the one-hundred-meter gold coastline of the resort stretched for 3.5 km.

Flowing fine sand, a gentle beach, warm and clear sea ... Bulgarian beaches belong to the state and there is free access to water anywhere on the coastline. Some coastal areas are rented by businessmen who set up sun loungers, umbrellas, bamboo and reed pavilions on the shore - a small fee is required for use. Near paid beaches there is a place for free relaxation, where you can sunbathe and swim without unnecessary financial costs. In general, the democratic nature of rest can be called a feature that everyone who comes to Golden Sands feels. Reviews of tourists indicate that it is possible to have a good rest and have fun on the coast without any special monetary expenses.

Excursions: a frozen chronicle of the world





Holidays in Bulgaria are impossible without touching the history and culture of the Balkan state. Each step along the ancient land, remembering the Thracians and the Odrisian kingdom, the empire of Alexander the Great and the chariot race of Ancient Rome, arouses curiosity and the desire to touch the millennial roots of the hospitable Slavic people. The easiest way to quickly get to know the country is to book an excursion or rent a car yourself, or with the help of the staff of Varshava Hotel 2. Bulgaria (Varna is the nearest city) is a small country in which interesting places can be found in any of the nearby cities.

Varna is the sea capital of Bulgaria. The distance is 15 km. Archaeologists have established that in the VI century BC. e. Odessos, a Greek colony, was located at this place. Nowadays - a large modern tourist, cultural and historical center. What to see:

1) Church of the Assumption of the Virgin - a postcard symbol of the city. The current temple, considered the most beautiful religious building in Bulgaria, was built at the end of the XIX century. Located at the intersection of Botev and Barnenchik Boulevards.

2) Sea Temple - has Russian roots. It was built on money donated by the merchant Nikolai Paraskeva. Once in a storm, he prayed to St. Nicholas about salvation and promised in case of deliverance from dangers to build a temple in his honor. The merchant fulfilled the promise and donated 50 thousand rubles for the construction. The temple is located on the street of Prince Boris I.

3) The archaeological site of the late II century - Roman baths.

4) Seaside park with dolphinariums and museums located in it.

5) Aladzha is a rock monastery founded around the 12th century.

6) The residence of the Bulgarian kings - travelers from Varshava Hotel 2 * will certainly pass this place. Located on the road from Varna to Golden Sands.

Varna gave shelter to many Russian officers who did not accept the 1917 revolution and emigrated to Europe. For 7 years (1949-1956) the city bore the name of Stalin, in the year of whose death the Black Sea froze.

Souvenirs: from vacation as a keepsake





Each trip remains not only in memory, but also takes place on shelves and coffee tables in the form of souvenirs and memorable trinkets brought from the next vacation. Should not fall out of this trend and rest in Bulgaria. The prices of local products are flexible and a varied assortment is offered for the pleasure of tourists.

  1. Rose oil. The famous Valley of Roses, in which flowers are grown for three centuries. Beware of fakes. Each bottle of real rose oil is sealed and has a certificate. And besides this, cosmetic creams and soap are made with rose oil. Another original, purely Bulgarian souvenir is soap with dried rose petals.
  2. Bulgarian wines. The falsification of wines in the country is prosecuted by law, so the quality of drinks is kept at a high level. In addition, local winemakers have not forgotten about the famous roses and make liquor from their petals.
  3. And again the "pink" theme. Jam from rose petals - surprisingly delicate and delicate. You will not find such a delicacy in any other country in the world.
  4. Cooker masks. Cookers are the patrons of fertility, driving away evil spirits. In their honor, an annual festival is held in Bulgaria. Masks are wooden, leather, fur ...
  5. National embroidery of Chevychev. Filigree Bulgarian patterns and very delicate work.
  6. Copper Products. Can be purchased on any street artisans. Often, a souvenir is made by the master right in front of the buyer.
  7. Ceramics. Products of national pottery for all occasions - cups, plates, jugs, wineglasses. At the request of the client, commemorative inscriptions are applied to the ceramics. Many vacationers book the inscription: "Golden Sands, Bulgaria." Product prices depend on the size of the product and place of purchase.
