Riviera beach (Odessa) - comfort and coziness by the Black Sea

At station number 12 of the Big Fountain is a modern beach-entertainment complex - "Riviera". Today it is already a legendary place among residents of Odessa and guests of the city. From other beaches of the coast, it compares favorably with the number of services provided and comfort. The complex is also proud of the staircase that meets vacationers. Judging by the reviews, she is more beautiful than the famous Potemkinskaya.

Guest facilities

The complex begins its work after the tenth of May, and ends after September 15th. By the end of the season, the water warms up to 26-28 degrees.

Riviera Beach (Odessa) has its own pool. It is positioned as a child. You can take a shower before and after swimming in the sea. The best restaurant of the city

Odessa complex "Riviera" is not only a beach holiday. There is a restaurant with the same name, offering the best dishes of European and national cuisine, as well as a night club.





Many guests who visited the beach "Riviera" (Odessa), reviews write enthusiastic. Tourists praise salads, especially Norwegian. Bodybuilder girls love spinach soup. Organized leisure

Both the restaurant and the club are very popular. Riviera Beach (Odessa) is often booked for large events: corporate parties, weddings, anniversaries, etc. Most of the summer holidays are organized on the covered terrace, which is located in the sea, a few meters from the coast.





The nightclub of the beach entertainment complex often hosts pop stars and plays live music.
