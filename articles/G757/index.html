We make flowers from corrugated paper (candy flowers)

Such an original and very beautiful gift, like flowers made of corrugated paper (candy flowers), can be presented for almost any celebration. They can be placed in a basket, or you can simply arrange with an elegant bouquet.

In any case, the gift will be very unusual and memorable.

Poppy

To make the craft, you will need corrugated floral paper (black, red, green), 1.2 mm thick wire, glue, artificial leaves and chocolates (depending on the number of buds).

Manufacture

Corrugated paper flowers (candy flowers) are made quite simply. First, cut blanks from red paper - rectangles of 1.5 divisions of the corrugation. And from black - the same pieces, but only a little larger. From red blanks cut and form neat petals. At the same time, stretch the upper part quite strongly, giving the part volume.

Thin "noodles" to the middle, cut the black rectangles. Twist the flagella with reinforcement to form stamens. Prepare the candy - squeeze the ponytails so that you get a ball. Wrap the sweet in a black blank (with flagella). Bend the wire and insert it into the paper roll with candy. Wrap the corrugation around the wire. Firmly fix the craft with a thread. So, let's start picking flowers from corrugated paper (candy flowers). Put a little glue on the tip of the petal and fix it on the stem. Lock the rest of the workpieces in the same way. In total, each flower should have four petals. Tap the base of the bud with green corrugation. Do not forget to attach a small leaf to the stem. Corrugated paper flowers (plain) are ready. From them you can collect a beautiful bouquet, decorating it with a decorative ribbon.

Large Corrugated Paper Flower

To make a giant rose, you need a roll of duct tape (green), glue, a pencil and several patterns (according to the size of the craft): 3 drops, 8 hearts, 1 core, 3 leaves. Corrugated paper (double-sided) is also required.

Manufacture

From colored paper, carefully cut six petal-drops and 5-6 hearts. Set the workpieces aside.

From green paper cut out three leaves, as well as a long rectangle - this will be a cup for the bud. Wrap the wire with colored tape. And then cut into three pieces. Take the petals and with both hands begin to gently stretch the corrugation from the center to the edges. This will give our blank bulk form. Turn the petal over and slightly twist the top of its edges with a pencil. Next, let's deal with the inside of the rose. Take the droplet-shaped blanks. Attach one of them with adhesive tape from one end to the wire. In the same way, fix all the other petals (drops and hearts). Using adhesive tape, gently combine the leaves and stems in pairs. Wrap a cup of green paper around the base of the bud. Tape the junction with tape. Connect the stems with the petals and continue to wrap the flower to the end.
