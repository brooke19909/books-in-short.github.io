Hikkaduwa (Sri Lanka) - a luxury resort and a paradise for divers

Hospitable Hikkaduwa attracts tourists with its picturesque landscapes, interesting entertainment programs and unique local flavor. Sri Lanka has long gained fame as a popular, quiet and peaceful resort, so people are happy to come here to relax. Travelers who come from large, noisy and dusty cities, it seems that they are in heaven or a good fairy tale. In Hikkaduwa, everything contributes to relaxation: the gentle and warm ocean, golden beaches, green palm trees, delicious and refined cuisine.



The town is located 100 km from Colombo and is considered one of the most comfortable on the whole island. A large selection of cheap accommodation, different levels of hotels and even two high-class hotels located directly on the ocean offers visitors to Hikkaduwa. Sri Lanka hospitably welcomes guests who come from different parts of the world. In restaurants and cafes, they will be happy to feed both local and European dishes close to European cuisine. Seafood is in great demand on the island. In the restaurants of hotels and hotels, chefs prepare delicious squid, lobster, fish. You can buy seafood on the market yourself, and in a cafe for a small fee, order their preparation. In this case, the process is allowed to observe.

Sri Lanka is ideal for snorkelling and diving. Hikkaduwa (photo of coral gardens cannot fail to attract attention) offers a huge selection of dive centers dealing with both experienced divers and beginners. Everyone can see with their own eyes the fabulous underwater world. In addition to reefs, divers can explore wrecks off the coast, including the British ship Earl of Shaftsbury. It is located at a depth of 12-21 m, those who wish can even get inside.

Swimming with huge turtles is another attraction that Hikkaduwa can offer tourists. Sri Lanka is home to many interesting animals and birds. The turtles that live in the resort are not at all afraid of people, they are happy to eat kelp directly from their hands, let themselves be stroked, and sometimes ride on themselves. You can go to the lake, where a huge number of birds live. Local landscapes amaze with their beauty, here you can be alone with nature, forget about all the problems and concerns.



Not only the golden beaches and warm ocean waters are rich in Sri Lanka. Hikkaduwa also receives positive feedback from tourists thanks to the huge number of shops where everything that you want is sold. Vacationers can purchase unique handmade items, jewelry, batik and much more.
