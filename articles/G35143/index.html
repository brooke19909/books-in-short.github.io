Hotel "Bogorodsk" Adler: description, rooms, beach and reviews of tourists

The southernmost resort in Russia is Adler - the younger brother of Sochi and the closest neighbor of Krasnaya Polyana. From a technical point of view, they have long been a single entity, but in terms of tourism it is still worth distinguishing them. Moreover, in sunny Adler there is something to see, where to go and what to do.

Adler Tourism





According to popular tourist portals and traveler reviews, one of the best accommodation options in the city is the Bogorodsk Hotel, which opened in 2007.

Adler is 17 km long along the Black Sea coast and the nearest beach to the hotel is only a few tens of meters. However, rest here is not only the sea. The hotel operates throughout the year, hosting guests who come with the aim of sightseeing, to conduct wellness treatments, skiing and just relax.

Adler was originally developed and positioned as a resort city, the treatment in which is based on special silt mud and Matsesta water enriched with hydrogen sulfide. The 2014 Olympics served as a new impetus for the modernization of the city, thanks to which high-tech world-class sports facilities appeared here and the infrastructure was updated.

Hotel Location





The first thing worth emphasizing is the area in which the Bogorodsk Hotel (Adler) is located. Nizhneimeretinskaya Bay (Olympic Park) is located between two rivers - Mzymta and Psou. This is a completely new, ecologically clean area, 5 km from the border with Abkhazia.

The building here is almost 100% built from scratch, with the lion's share of accommodation facilities located on the beach (first coastline). From the doors of the Bogorodsk Hotel to the coast literally a few dozen steps - 20-30 meters. His address: Russia, Adler, st. Nizhneimeretinskaya, d. 155.

The five-story hotel building has a modern and original design. Its appearance resembles a ship with numerous cabins. The interior design is represented by a successful and harmonious blend of classic style and modern comfort. Ornamental plants, bright colors, cheerful shades of the hall and patio remind you that you are on vacation.

Number of rooms





The three-star hotel Bogorodsk (Adler, Russia) has 58 comfortable rooms of various categories:

  • 33 standard double rooms with two single beds (some of the rooms with a balcony);
  • 11 studio rooms with a mini-living room, a balcony and a large bed;
  • 4 de lux rooms (2 rooms) with two single beds, a living room, upholstered furniture and a balcony;
  • 10 lux rooms (2 rooms) with a balcony, upholstered furniture in the living room and a large French bed.

All rooms are under the control of the internal security service, video surveillance is carried out in the corridors, they are equipped with a centralized electronic lock, a security and fire alarm system.

Adler: Hotel Bogorodsk - accommodation prices

The hotel offers its guests accommodation rates above average. This is due primarily to the location on the first coastline, the proximity of the beach and, of course, the recently rebuilt Olympic town with developed infrastructure and entertainment.

Accommodation in a standard double room will cost from 1,500 to 3,800 rubles., Studios - 2,000-5,300 rubles., DE LUXE and LUXE - 2,500-5,800 rubles. overnight. The administration provides regular customers discounts.

When planning a trip and early booking, keep in mind that the hotel asks for a 30% prepayment of the cost of living, the rest of the amount is paid on the spot.

Specific prices for accommodation should be clarified by the administration, they may vary, depending on the season and congestion.

Hotel Features





The three-star Bogorodsk Hotel (Adler) operates according to the standard European system. Check-in and check-out take place from two in the afternoon until noon, respectively. With a preliminary request, the time may be changed.

Keep in mind that the price of accommodation always includes parking services, wireless Internet on the territory and in rooms, and rental of sun loungers.

If you are going on vacation with children, then the issue of accommodation will depend on their age. A small child under three years of age who does not occupy additional space can stay at the hotel for free. Children under 12 years of age and older on an extra bed in the room are offered a discount of 70% and 50%, respectively.

Additional services

Meals are included in the price. For an extra charge, you can order dinner and breakfast. Numerous reviews about the Bogorodsk Hotel in Adler in the list of its main advantages and advantages include a food system with delicious dishes of Caucasian and European cuisine. The restaurant is located on the ground floor of the hotel.

Additionally paid round-the-clock room service, transfer to and from the airport, laundry and safe boxing, car rental, ordering tickets (railway and air), as well as sightseeing services.

Beach near the hotel





As already mentioned, the Bogorodsk Hotel (Adler) has a very good location on the first coastline. Within walking distance is a spacious pebble beach belonging to the Olympic Park, built in 2014. It stretched along the entire Nizhneimeretinskaya embankment for 6 km: from the border with Abkhazia and almost to the seaport.

According to reviews, the infrastructure here is well developed. On the beach there are equipped changing cabins, sun umbrellas and sunbeds (for an extra charge), shower cabins. In addition, you can relax and have a bite to eat in cafes and restaurants, and at the rental points or shops you can buy all the necessary accessories (towel, rubber rings, masks, etc.). Hotel Bogorodsk (Adler): reviews

When choosing an accommodation option at a resort, you should always pay attention to the reviews of travelers who have stayed at a hotel, hostel or hotel before you. Awareness in this matter always helps to navigate on the spot and avoid minor troubles.

According to the popular tourist portal, the Bogorodsk Hotel, located in Adler, has a rating of 7.8 out of ten possible. The total result is derived from the assessment of individual items.





The most satisfied hotel guests are its location. A beautiful and well-maintained beach within a couple of minutes of walking, the proximity of the Olympic town and many infrastructure at the same time as silence and beauty were appreciated by more than 90% of vacationers. Many of them noted the magnificent panoramic view from the roof of the building. The second important factor is the wireless Internet access that the hotel has, in addition, it functions perfectly.

Around the same score - eight points - earned hotel staff and cleanliness of the rooms. The employees, according to the guests, do their job well, are always ready to help, the service is unobtrusive. The rooms are cleaned with high quality.

Of the minuses - in some of them there is old furniture, which does not quite match what was stated in numerous official photos. The elevator of the hotel earned a negative rating. It is quite original, the cabin is made entirely of glass, so a picturesque view of the sea opens before your eyes. However, cases were recorded when he did not work for several days, which creates certain difficulties in a five-story building.

Guests praise the breakfast at the hotel. Not everyone is happy with the range of dishes, but they are absolutely satisfied with their quality. According to the guests, the portions in the restaurant are very large, the food is hearty and tasty. Of the minuses - a big load on the dining room, where in the mornings it is crowded, so sometimes you have to wait until a free table appears.

If you are planning a vacation on the Black Sea coast in Adler, then pay attention to the Bogorodsk Hotel 3 * (Russia). Sochi with the newly-built Olympic town will be right at your side (a 15-minute walk). At any time of the year, everyone will find entertainment to their taste: from hiking in the park and measured beach relaxation to extreme skiing.
