How to make do-it-yourself hair clips from satin ribbon

Unusual hair clips for girls are not so cheap, and unique handmade products can be very expensive. But why pay extra money to someone if you can create hair clips from a satin ribbon with your own hands? Moreover, this process will not take much time and will not require any tremendous costs. All that is needed to create a beautiful hair accessory is the most common hairpins and elastic bands, ribbons of different widths and sewing accessories.



Hairpin "Curly"

So, the first version of the hairpin made of satin ribbon with your own hands - "curls". She will need round wooden dowels, strips of contrasting colors half a centimeter wide, as well as paper clips or wooden clothespins and a base for attaching to hair. The manufacture of hairpins from satin ribbons of such a pattern begins with the fact that the entire core is wrapped in ribbon and secured on both sides. After the required number of curls is wound, the workpieces are laid out on a baking sheet and baked in the oven at a temperature of 180 degrees for about 7-10 minutes. After this time, the tapes on the dowels need to cool, then they are removed from the woods and the ends are trimmed. For reliability, they can be fused a little, so that in the process of wearing the ribbons do not tear apart. It remains to fold the curls, sew in the middle and attach to the elastic or hairpin.



Hairpin "Bow"

The second variant of a satin ribbon hairpin (do it even easier with your own hands) is a bow. The main thing here is to choose a beautiful ribbon, it can be polka dots or with hearts. Ribbons with stripes or with the favorite of all the girls, Kitty, will also look beautiful. There are two options for making such a bow. If the basis of this jewelry is elastic, then the bow can be tied directly to it and fixed so that it holds firmly in shape. If it is attached to the invisibility, then it is done separately: a piece of tape (about 10 cm) is closed in a circle, folded with a seam in the middle and tied with another tape, creating a beautiful knot. For a volumetric double bow, two or three closed segments are made, one slightly larger than the other, stacked on top of each other and a beautiful knot tied in the middle, after which the workpiece can only be attached to the hairpin.



Hairpin "ruffles"

The third option is ruffles. To make such a hairpin from a satin ribbon with your own hands, wide invisibility is taken as the base, ruffles are collected from its length from a ribbon 2.5 cm wide, making seams in the middle of the ribbon with a needle. The ruffles are collected tight, the edges of the tapes are melted, after which the workpiece is glued or sewn to the base.

If you assemble the tape from one edge, you can form a beautiful flower, sew several beads or spangles in the middle and attach it to the base. Such a flower can also decorate the headbands.


Hairpins made of satin ribbons are easy to make, the main thing is to turn on imagination and stock up on the necessary details. A huge amount of materials can be combined with ribbons - these are beads, and fur, and nylon, all kinds of rhinestones and pendants, sequins and even felt. Plus, such jewelry is not only in their low cost, the main advantage of these accessories is the uniqueness and the ability to create a thing that will match the color and style of clothing as much as possible, which is important enough for a harmonious look.
