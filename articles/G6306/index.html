We knit plaits "Zigzag" crochet

Crocheted blanket will be an excellent warm accessory in your interior. In addition to the warming function, it plays the role of decorating the room, adding coziness and joy. An important and crucial moment when knitting is the correct selection of shades of yarn.

In the art of crocheting a plaid zigzag, there are some nuances and features. Let's consider the main schemes.

Openwork plaid

A beautiful bedspread made of threads of various colors is knitted according to the following schematic description. The pattern is based on an openwork zigzag with the addition of shells.

Knitting this plaid is very fast due to the fact that shells are formed using columns with four crochets.

For the manufacture of a delicate and cozy little thing is best to use acrylic yarn of medium thickness.

At the beginning and end of the work, a neat tight edge is formed - this is obtained due to knitting of three rows (first and last) without crochets.

If there are threads from knitting, then you can attach a fringe or brush along the edge.

Plaid "Zigzag" for beginner needlewomen

A warm and beautiful knitted thing adds coziness and comfort to any room. Each novice craftswoman who knows how to knit air loops and double crochets can knit a wonderful Zigzag blanket with a crochet from the remains of yarn or by acquiring several skeins of the same yarn of different colors.

The knitting pattern of the bedspreads is absolutely uncomplicated. The work begins with a set of chains of air loops. Do not forget that due to zigzags the size of the plaid is reduced by one third in width. To calculate the required number of loops, tie a sample.

Before starting the process, lay out all skeins of yarn on the countertop, deciding in which order the colored stripes will be arranged.

Knit the fabric according to the attached scheme, changing the color of the threads every 2 or 4 rows. Having reached the required size, tie around the perimeter. To straighten the wavy edge, make double crochet, and in the recesses - double crochet.

Crochet a zigzag plaid for a newborn

A responsible and serious task is to create a blanket for the baby - warm, gentle, soft and, of course, beautiful! Yarn must be selected carefully, given its composition, thickness, hypoallergenicity.

The plaid linked to the discharge from the maternity hospital can later be used as a walking blanket, a bedspread, etc.

So, we start crocheting a children's blanket. As a basis, we take a zigzag pattern, which uses 5 shades of acrylic thread - white, lilac, pink, lime and blue.

Knitting goes according to the pattern, which starts with the loops in front of the rapport, then the rapport itself, and to the end to the desired width, finishing knitting with loops for the rapport. The first rows are knitted only once, in the fourth row, and then they repeat the third and fourth - until the desired length of the plaid.
