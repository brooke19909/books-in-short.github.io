Hostel Ananas

Hostel Pineapple (Prague) is located in the lower district of Wenceslas Square. A walk to the Old Town Square with chimes takes some 5 minutes. There is free internet.





Judging by the reviews, guests use a shared bathroom, while a hairdryer can be taken at the front desk. The rooms are equipped with free lockers. Most rooms have air conditioning.

Facilities include a shared kitchen, luggage storage and a shared lounge. Guests can use the safe at the front desk for free. A variety of snacks are sold around the counter. Shuttle services are available at an additional cost.

Hostel Florenc

The next hostel (Prague) is also interesting. Florence is located near the National Museum. Pets are allowed at this place. It features free Wi-Fi, a terrace and barbecue facilities. The reception is open 24 hours a day.





Based on reviews, guests can play darts and table tennis. Not far from here are the Wenceslas and Old Town squares.

Hostel Chili

Considering the hostels in Prague in the city center, it is worth highlighting Chili, which is located in a historic charming five-story building right by the Vltava River. It offers a shared kitchen, bright rooms, free Wi-Fi and a seating area. Smoking is not allowed in the hostel.

Guests have access to a private or shared bathroom. There is a 24-hour reception desk. Lockers are available for an extra charge. Here guests can mingle and relax in the shared lounge.





Judging by the reviews, restaurants, clubs and shops are located nearby. You can walk to Prague Castle and Charles Bridge in 10 minutes . A tram stop is 150 meters away. A metro station is 300 meters away.

Hostel Rosemary

We continue to consider hostels in Prague, reviews of which many are interested in today. Rosemary features modern rooms. It is located near the famous Jewish synagogue, near the railway station. There is a games room and baby care is provided.

The functional rooms here have central heating and wooden floors. Some rooms have shared bathrooms. All others have their own bathrooms, in addition, separate kitchens with a cozy dining area.

Here, judging by the reviews, it offers guests a well-equipped shared kitchen where you can cook your favorite food. Internet is available in public areas, including a shared lounge with a library and satellite TV. Tea and coffee are offered free of charge here all day.

The hostel has an internet corner with a printer and 2 computers that can be used for free. Laundry facilities are available at night.

Hostel Advantage

This hostel in Prague is located near the National Museum. It offers rooms with bed linen. There is a restaurant with a large beer garden serving international and Czech cuisine.





Judging by the reviews, guests can use the living room with computers, a library and a TV. Free internet is also available. The hostel is included in the hostel international network. It is located near the metro station. Vysehrad Fortress is located at a distance of 1 metro station.

Artharmony Hostel

The Prague Artharmony Hostel is located 10 minutes from Wenceslas Square, near the Vltava River and the metro station. Guests enjoy free internet.

All rooms of the hostel are individually decorated and decorated with various natural materials, including wood, stone and bamboo, as evidenced by numerous reviews. It features a 24-hour front desk and a TV lounge.

Hostel Dakura

This hostel in Prague is very conveniently located for exploring the city. It is located near the metro station and Wenceslas Square. Judging by the reviews, guests are provided with breakfast, shuttle service and luggage storage. The hostel occupies the former municipal bathhouse, which was built in 1922.





The spacious and bright rooms feature wooden floors, a shared bathroom with toiletries and a work desk. An alarm clock service is also provided.

Judging by the reviews, guests can relax in the cafe, and bars and restaurants serving classic Czech drinks and dishes are nearby. There is a vending machine for snacks and drinks on site. In addition, guests can use the shared kitchen, which is fully equipped.

You can find out what to do and what to see in Prague at the local tour desk . Laundry facilities are also available. It is worth noting that Prague Castle is 1.4 km from here, and the airport can be reached in 20 minutes by car.

Hostel U Bubenicku

U Bubenicku is a Prague hostel with free internet and a Czech restaurant. It is located in the historic building of the city, 3 minutes from the embankment of the Vltava River and the National Theater. This hostel is connected by excellent transport links with other areas of the city.





Judging by the reviews, the hostel offers dormitory rooms that are decorated in different colors, with individual lockers, wooden floors, and a shared bathroom. There is no elevator and you can access the rooms exclusively by stairs.

Judging by the reviews, the hostel serves a continental breakfast in the morning, which includes jam, bread, cheese, ham, butter, as well as tea and coffee. The restaurant serves a variety of regional dishes. Packed lunches are available on request.

The Cathedral of St. Cyril and Methodius and the Dancing House are 300 meters away. It offers a free bicycle storage room and a free Prague map. The nearest tram stop is a 2-minute walk away and a metro station is about 5 minutes from here.

Plus Prague (hostel, Czech Republic, Prague)

This hostel is located in a quiet residential area, not far from the train station, as well as the metro station Nadrazh Holesovice. It offers rooms with a private bathroom, a sauna, an indoor pool, and free Wi-Fi. The Ortenovo namesti tram stop provides direct access to the Old Town.





You will reach the Vystavishte Exhibition Center and the Tesla Arena and Incheba Arena stadiums in 10 minutes by tram, while in the Mala Strana and Charles Bridge districts - in just 20 minutes. After a busy day, guests can rejuvenate in a recreation area with free internet terminals and a large-screen TV, in addition, in the sauna or indoor pool, open all year.

The family-run restaurant serves a buffet breakfast, as well as international and Czech dishes. Judging by the reviews, the guests are provided with secure parking. You can always contact the front desk for help. By the way, employees speak different languages.
