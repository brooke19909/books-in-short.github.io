Hotel Despo Hotel 3 * (Crete, Greece): overview, description, rooms and reviews

Everyone who plans to go on vacation to a particular resort, primarily cares about the location. How not to make a mistake so that it is both comfortable and inexpensive? To do this, it is worth studying the reviews of those people who have already visited a particular hotel. So, today we will talk about Despo Hotel 3 * (Greece, Crete, Heraklion).

general information

Hotel Hotel Despo 3 * in Crete is a great option for those who want to not only explore Greece, but also have a good rest on the Mediterranean coast and at the same time save money. The hotel buildings are located in the small town of Gouves. The complex, although it is an economic option, but works on an all-inclusive system. Heraklion is 18 km away, and the city of Hersonissos is also nearby. The hotel is small, has 2- and 3-story detached buildings, in which there are only 74 rooms.





Hotel Territory

The territory, according to the guests, is more like a large garden, in which the hotel buildings are located. A lot of greenery, unusual trees and shrubs, well-groomed flower beds - all this not only makes the air clean, but also decorates the surrounding landscape. The owners lit a real vineyard around the buildings.

Also on the territory of the Despo Hotel 3 * hotel is a large swimming pool, an indoor pool, a separate large sun terrace, restaurants and 2 outdoor bars. There is a children's playground and a separate pool for babies. In addition, the windows of the rooms offer incredible views of the mountains and the vast expanses of the Mediterranean Sea.





Hotel beach

Hotel Despo Hotel 3 * (Crete) is located on the 3rd line and is separated from the sea by a narrow roadway. A beach with fine and white sand, large stones are rarely found here. Sunset at sea is smooth, gentle, sand is also at the bottom. Due to the pleasant climate at sea, there are rarely storms and big waves.

The beach itself is 600 m from the hotel, you can get to it in 10-15 minutes by leisurely step. In addition, a scheduled bus runs regularly, which can also deliver to the sea. The beach has everything you need to relax: sunbeds, deckchairs, umbrellas - all for a fee for vacationers.





Sport

The owners did not forget about lovers of outdoor activities and sports. For them, on the territory of the hotel there are free tennis tables, there is an equipped gym and separate rooms for yoga and aerobics.

For an extra fee, guests can enjoy jet skiing; those who love speed can enjoy water skiing and catamarans. There is also the opportunity to go diving; for an additional fee, the staff will prepare scuba diving, but the equipment needs to be rented. Administrators organize boat trips, diving courses or fishing on the high seas. Surfers and windsurfers will also not be left without equipment.

Rooms

The vast room complex of the Despo Hotel 3 * hotel (Crete, Greece) provides for its guests more than 70 rooms. Including there are suites, family rooms with separate rooms for children and single and double "standards". Check-out time and check-in - 14:00, check-out is until 12:00.

The rooms are equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay. The rooms are modernly renovated, the air conditioning system is functioning. Wi-Fi is also available throughout the hotel, but only for a fee - 2 euros per hour.





Nutrition

Meals are provided on an all-inclusive basis. Throughout the day, the hotel has a main restaurant, where you can not only have a hearty breakfast, but also have a good lunch and refreshment in the evening. The menu is varied, in addition, it is updated daily, cooks are prepared from high-quality products. Traditional Greek cuisine as well as familiar European dishes are provided. But for more original delights you will have to go to the nearest cafe or bar.

La Carte Restaurant serves a selection of Mediterranean dishes. A snack bar is also open 24 hours a day, where you can enjoy light snacks, drink wine and relax. The lobby bar can offer guests aromatic coffee, tea or fresh pastries.

For guests, meat dishes are prepared, as well as treats from poultry and fish, cold and hot snacks, soups and broths, salads and fries. Special attention should be paid to flour products, pastries, local desserts and ice cream. A lot of drinks: juices and fruit drinks, strong and soft drinks. It is possible to order dishes directly to your room. There is also a separate menu for children.





Hotel Services

Despo Hotel Gouves 3 * hotel does everything possible to make the rest of its guests enjoyable. The staff is working around the clock, the maids and administrators are friendly and smiling. There will be no problems with the language barrier, employees communicate freely in English, German, Greek and French. You can reach the front desk directly from your room.

Free guarded parking is available on site, and there is no need to reserve a place for a car in advance.

The hotel has luggage storage facilities, you can use car rental, a safe, dry cleaning or exchange currency directly in the lobby. For business people, it is possible to use the conference room for meetings, which is equipped with modern equipment, there is also a fax and photocopy machine.

The hotel has a sauna, hairdresser, there is a medical office, a small beauty salon - all this for extra money. Shuttle services are also available for a fee.





Entertainment and leisure

Half a kilometer from the Despo Hotel 3 * hotel is a more lively area, where there are many shops, kiosks, it is here that souvenirs and crafts are purchased. In specialized pavilions, the hosts offer to try and buy quality wine at affordable prices.

But you can taste traditional Greek cuisine in numerous restaurants and cafes. It serves classic dishes of meat and fish, serves first-class snacks and, of course, strong drinks. In the bars and discos every evening they conduct entertainment programs, contests, play live music.

Near the hotel there is a golf club, the AquaWorld Aquarium, various museums (Museum of the Battle of Crete, Heraklion Museum) and others. There is also the nearby Kules fortress, the Venetian walls, the Knossos palace - it will not take you 30 minutes to get to all these attractions by bus.





Little guests

The hotel staff is always happy for couples with children. It is worth noting that the stay in a room of a child under 2 years old is free, in addition, a cot is provided. Children under 6 years old also have free rest at the hotel. A child from 6 to 12 years old costs 10 euros per night in a separate bed.

As for entertainment for young guests, they have a separate pool with low slides, a games area and a playground. There is also a children's corner with various games. There are all kinds of swings, carousels, you can play with balls. For a fee, you can use the services of a nanny: while the parents are resting or have gone on business, the kids will be supervised.

Tourists were satisfied with the number: although it is small, but comfortable, it suits a modest family. In addition, the friendly staff tried to please everything, smiled and responded to any requests. The beach and the sea are gorgeous, there are many entertainments for every taste and budget.
