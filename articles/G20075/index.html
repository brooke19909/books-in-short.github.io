Waterpark in Kirilovka: description, services, attractions and reviews

The water park in Kirilovka is the place that will give unforgettable minutes of joy, fun and laughter. It is there that every member of your family will find entertainment of their own interest. Especially for children it will be an unforgettable vacation. Further from the article we learn a lot of interesting things about this water park.

The water park in Kirilovka will provide you with a wide variety of attractions, where most of them are for children.

The main element in his work is water. Thanks to advanced technologies, it is extracted from deep wells, then it goes through many stages of thorough mechanical cleaning, filtration and complete disinfection of harmful bacteria and fungi. Therefore, the chlorine content is completely absent in it, and its quality meets all international standards.

Comfortable conditions for guests

The administration also took care of the comfort of its visitors by providing individual lockers for personal belongings and locker rooms located throughout the park. There is a first aid station, a pharmacy, a photo center and a souvenir shop on the territory. For drivers who come to rest, car parking is equipped.

In addition, at your choice there will be three cafes, a pizzeria and a restaurant with a large selection of varied food.





What are we famous for?

The water park in Kirilovka has 34 attractions, 16 of which are for adults, and the rest are for young visitors.

One of the advantages is the pool, which is located in its central part and covers a huge area of ‚Äã‚Äãmore than 1700 square meters. Around him are sun loungers with umbrellas, a cocktail bar - all this makes the rest for visitors pleasant and comfortable. In this area, you have a beautiful view of all the slides.

For lovers of a more relaxing holiday, the water park in Kirilovka offers to visit the famous attraction called "Lazy River", whose length is about 340 meters, and it passes through the entire recreation area. This is the place where you can quietly and measuredly swim in one of the water facilities, relax and have fun. For more convenient moving guests through it placed six bridges.

In the territory of the water park visitors are given a choice - to sit in the sun to sunbathe, or to hide from them under the awnings.

In addition to all of the above advantages, I would like to add that daily in the central pool water aerobics is conducted under the guidance of a professional fitness trainer. Musical accompaniment of classes, light physical activity, the sun, water splashes - all this will bring a lot of pleasure, good mood and physical fitness.

The organizers also worried about nightlife lovers. A spacious night club opens for them. A large dance venue, an outdoor pool, bright lighting effects, all kinds of entertaining shows, exciting contests, famous DJs and, of course, foam parties.





Rest for children

There is a special area for younger guests. The organizers made sure that for young visitors the rest was a trip to the country of fairy tales. There is a swimming pool with a six-meter pirate ship, on which a variety of slides are located. Here, children bathe and play under the supervision of qualified animators, so their lives are in danger, which allows parents to relax.

Welcome to us

To get to this kingdom of the water world is not difficult if you decide to spend your vacation in Kirilovka near the water park and settled in the village itself or at one of the recreation centers on Fedotova Spit, for this you will need to walk or take a minibus.

And you can also rent comfortable rooms in a hotel complex located directly on the territory of the park, which will save time and travel costs.

Visit the water park in Kirilovka, ticket prices will delight you. It is also worth noting that the cost depends on the length of time that you plan to spend. For children with height no more than 1 m, admission is free.

The cost of the water park in Kirilovka, with a minimum stay of 2.5 hours, for an adult is 220 hryvnias, for a child - 200 hryvnias (about 500-560 rubles).

Very often, people appreciate the variety of cafes and restaurants with delicious food.
