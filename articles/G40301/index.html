Pension "Gelendzhik Bay": description, services, reviews

A ten-minute walk from the main promenade of the city, near the pine park with a picturesque alley of Lenkoran acacias, there is the Gelendzhik Bay boarding house. You can familiarize yourself with the photo of the holiday home, as well as with the description of the number of rooms and infrastructure below.

Infrastructure

Pension "Gelendzhik Bay" in Gelendzhik was built in 1975. From 2006 to 2010, a comprehensive reconstruction was carried out, during which the number of rooms and other rooms of the hotel were updated. The modern boarding house is a five-story building and a separate wing with a dining room.

Three meals a day in the rest house are carried out according to the "buffet" system.

On the first floor of the building is located:

  • boarding house administration;
  • conference hall;
  • safe and left-luggage office;
  • Cafe;
  • bar;
  • library;
  • gym;
  • sports equipment rental;
  • table tennis;
  • children's playroom;
  • laundry and ironing room.

The territory of the boarding house is quite large, landscaped. On it is a children's playground, as well as benches in the shade of trees.

Vacationers who come in private cars can use the free secure parking.

The beach is a common city, sand and gravel. It is located five hundred meters from the gates of the "Gelendzhik Bay."

Number of rooms

The room fund of the Gelendzhik Bay boarding house is represented by 84 apartments of various categories located in a five-story building.

Guests can stay at:

  • double or triple standard;
  • double or triple superior standard;
  • double two-room.

The rooms are equipped with single or double beds, bedside tables, wardrobes. There is a refrigerator, TV, split system. The bathroom is equipped with a shower.

The cost of living is from 2620 rubles per day.

Guest reviews

For many years now, Gelendzhik Bay has been hosting guests from all over the country. Many tourists come to rest here not the first time, noting the excellent service and affordable prices.

The main key points due to which the pension earned a positive reputation:

  • The rooms are spacious, bright and clean.
  • Cleaning takes place daily.
  • Wi-Fi works throughout the boarding house.
  • On each floor there is a cooler with drinking water.
  • The double rooms have a tea set and an electric kettle.
  • Bath amenities are updated as needed.
  • Sheets are changed every five days.
  • Three towels are provided for each person, regardless of room category. Replace with clean ones every three days.
  • Hot water is supplied uninterrupted.
  • The location of the pension is convenient. The beach is a five-minute walk, a picturesque park is right behind the gate, the central market is also within walking distance. Nearby is a public transport stop, shops, restaurants.
  • Rental equipment for children (highchair, sports equipment and so on) is free.
  • Access to the gym is included in the price.
  • The dishes in the dining room are always fresh, the menu is varied. Constantly fresh fruits, juices, compotes, meat, fish, soft pastries.
  • All rooms have magnificent views of the bay or mountains.

Among the minuses of the pension, vacationers note only the lack of an elevator and combined balconies.

Location

Pension "Gelendzhik Bay" is located at: Gelendzhik city, Pervomaiskaya street, 39.

By public transport to the holiday home can be reached in the following ways:

  • from the bus station: minibus No. 6a to the stop "Gelendzhik Bay";
  • from the airport: by bus number 5 to the stop "Market".

Also, any guest can order a transfer to a boarding house from Anapa, Gelendzhik or Krasnodar Airport and from Novorossiysk Train Station.
