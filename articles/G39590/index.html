Hotel Palumbo Kendwa 4 * (Zanzibar): photos and reviews of tourists

Want to spend your vacation in a real tropical paradise? Then go straight to Kendwa Beach. Palumbo Kendwa 4 * is a comfortable boutique hotel located right on its shore. Its windows offer amazing views of such picturesque northwestern islets such as Popo, Daloni and Tumbatu. It is an ideal place for relaxation. To make sure of this, it is worth listing the main advantages of the hotel.





Beach

This is one of the key advantages of the Palumbo Kendwa 4 * resort complex. Kendwa Beach, on which it is located, is called the beach of millionaires. He is the most expensive in all of Zanzibar. And definitely the best!

There are practically no tides, no algae, and the azure color of perfectly clear water looks so bright that it becomes difficult to believe in its naturalness.

The beach is constantly cleaned, so there is no debris on it from the ocean or from human life. Snow-white sand is fine, soft and loose - you want to sunbathe right on it, and not on a deck chair or towel.

And yet - Kendwa Beach is one of the few that offers stunning views of the amazingly beautiful sunsets.

Nutrition

As in any worthy resort complex, Palumbo Kendwa 4 * (Zanzibar) has a restaurant serving breakfast and dinner. Lunches are organized on the beach.

Mostly nutritional reviews are positive. European breakfasts, there is plenty to choose from - vegetables, fruits, pastries, pancakes with jam, freshly squeezed juices, ground coffee, cheese and meat slices and much more.

For lunch and dinner, there is more variety. They serve fish in various forms, very tasty and tender octopuses, grilled vegetables, lobsters, salads, snacks. The chef is Italian, so you can always find something original and gourmet on the tables. By the way, there are no specific dishes (in terms of spiciness and spiciness). If you want, you can season the food yourself - there is a separate table with spices, salt, pepper and other additives.
