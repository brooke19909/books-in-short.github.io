Hilton Long Beach Resort Hotel - 4 Star Complex in Hurghada

The Egyptian resort of Hurghada is famous for its long coastline with sandy beaches and coral reefs. Here is a real paradise for divers and surf lovers. Tourists from all over the world come to Egypt to enjoy underwater wonders. The infrastructure of various entertainments, both water and "land", is also developed. From Hurghada you can make excursions in any direction, camel riding in the desert is popular.

Resort Hurghada is represented by many hotels of various levels, which are located on the coast with a chain of 40 kilometers. Active development of the resort area is already taking place on the 2nd and 3rd lines from the sea, therefore, in coastal hotels you can relax peacefully, the noise of working equipment will not be heard here. One of the most respectable and solid hotels in Hurghada - Hilton Long Beach Resort Hotel , perfect for families.

This hotel complex is one of a series of the world-wide developed hotel chain Hilton. In Hurghada, there are 2 more five-star complexes : the Hilton Resort Hotel Hurghada and the Hilton Hurghada Plaza Hotel, and in the 4-star category - the Hilton Villa Resort Hotel.

Hilton Long Beach Hotel consists of several buildings: the main 3-storey building, a family building of 3 floors and a complex of 2-storey villas. To accommodate guests are offered 912 rooms with various levels of comfort and spaciousness. In general, the hotel combines a reasonable ratio of high level and reasonable price, while maintaining the priority of a comfortable stay for couples with children and tourists who want to retire from the hustle and bustle.
