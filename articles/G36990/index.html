Hotel Elphistone Resort 4 * (Marsa Alam): reviews, rating, photo

Today, many people who want to spend time in Egypt, choose a large hotel complex called Elphistone Resort 4 *. Marsa Alam is a wonderful combination of warm climate, picturesque coral reefs and interesting excursions. This is where the luxury hotel is located

Description of location and territory





Elphistone Resort 4 * (Marsa Alam) is a luxury hotel located in the picturesque south of Egypt, almost on the very shore of the Red Sea. This coastal area is famous for its large living coral reefs and rich underwater world. Marsalam city center is about 25 km away. The nearest airport is located in the same city, about 37 km away. But the Hurghada Airport is much further - 280 km. Therefore, tourists arriving in this city should prepare for a long trip. The picturesque coast of the Nile and the legendary historic complex of Luxor are approximately 230 km away.

Elphistone Resort Marsa Alam 4 * occupies a large territory, which is decorated with beautiful flower beds, tall palm trees, curly shrubs and neat lawns. The hotel consists of the main building and cozy three-story bungalows built in an interesting Nubian style. There are pools, terraces for relaxation, sports grounds, in short, everything for a pleasant, relaxing pastime. By the way, most travelers spend almost all the time on the beach and hotel grounds, rarely leaving its borders.

Hotel Elphistone Resort Marsa Alam 4 *: photo and description of rooms





Of course, living conditions interest travelers first of all. What do the rooms at Elphistone Resort Marsa Alam look like? Reviews, photos indicate that the rooms here are spacious, comfortable, furnished with modern furniture. It offers views of the sea and the surrounding area. By the way, there are one, two, triple standard rooms and luxury rooms. In total, there are 270 of them.

In the room you will find all the necessary household appliances. In particular, you can watch TV, cool the room with air conditioning, use a safe, telephone and radio. A bathroom is also available. It has a toilet, shower, mirrors, wash basin, as well as all the necessary toiletries.

What kind of food is offered to residents?





Many tourists ask what kind of food the hotel can offer. Like most hotels in Egypt, Elphistone Resort Marsa Alam 4 * operates on an all inclusive scheme. Red Sea Hotel's main restaurant hosts receptions three times a day. Guest reviews indicate that the choice is really large (by the way, meat and fish dishes, fresh fruit and pastries are always enough). It serves Italian, international and Egyptian cuisine.

In addition, the hotel has other facilities where you can have a bite to eat or get a delicious oriental cocktail. For example, tourists like to visit the local √† la carte restaurant Fish Market, which serves seafood and several types of fish. On the beach is the Pizza Corner pizzeria, where you can enjoy the taste of real Italian pizza. You can have a good time in the stylish Omar el Sherif bar, where guests are served aromatic oriental coffee, tea, liquors and a variety of delicious cocktails (both non-alcoholic and alcoholic). The Sun Shine Bar, located right by the pool, also offers light snacks, ice creams and drinks.

Where is the beach located? Water activities





As already mentioned, the Elphistone Resort Marsa Alam 4 * hotel is built almost on the seashore and owns a large private section of the beach. The coast here is really beautiful, near the shore there are picturesque coral reefs, so travelers are advised to use special shoes. Sunset at sea is cleared, there is a shallow area where children can have fun. A long pier has been built for holidaymakers, which leads directly to the coral.

Naturally, guests can count on sun loungers, mattresses, umbrellas and clean beach towels. Near the shore there is a pizzeria where you can have lunch, and on the beach there is a bar serving chilled drinks.

This place will appeal to diving enthusiasts, since the underwater flora and fauna here are incredibly diverse and beautiful. You can even enjoy the view of coral reefs and see the bright fish while swimming with a mask. In addition, the hotel has a diving club, where experienced divers can rent all the equipment, and beginners can take a short training course before the first dive.

In addition, you can rent a boat, a catamaran, go boating, water skiing, go sailing, try parasailing, in a word, get a lot of vivid impressions.

Children's rest on the hotel territory: are there any conditions?

Naturally, many tourists today travel with their children. Should I take a child with me to the Elphistone Resort 4 * hotel? Marsa Alam is a great place to have fun. The hotel itself offers a sufficient number of services for a comfortable stay, including folding beds, special children's dishes and chairs in restaurants.

For children, there is a swimming pool and a spacious playground with swings, slides and sandboxes. And the guys like to spend time in a mini club, where they can play, have fun with new friends, engage in drawing, dancing and sports. And in the evenings, animators organize a mini-disco.

Additional service





What can the hotel complex Elphistone Resort 4 * offer? Marsa Alam is a place famous for genuine hospitality and high quality service. You can be sure that your vacation will not be overshadowed by domestic problems. The hotel has ATMs, here you can exchange currency. The laundry is constantly working. In case of health problems you can count on the help of a qualified doctor. Here, at the hotel, you can purchase the necessary medicines, as a pharmacy is open around the clock.

There is also a fairly large shopping arcade selling household items, souvenirs, clothes, jewelry and much more. For a small fee, you can use the services of an Internet cafe. The hotel also has free parking. Here you can rent a car (if you have the necessary documents) and even hire a driver.

How are tourists having fun? Conditions for interesting leisure





There are no shopping centers or entertainment complexes near the hotel. On the other hand, a lot of ways to have fun can be found on the hotel grounds. In the courtyard there are three large pools, and around, on the terrace, there are sun loungers and umbrellas. Various sporting events and competitions are regularly held here.

Those who wish can play volleyball or football, as the hotel has sports fields. It offers guests and two tennis courts. You can also have fun playing billiards or table tennis. Travelers recommend visiting the spa, where they offer a huge number of various wellness and relaxing treatments. It features a sauna, an outdoor jacuzzi, a bathhouse and massage rooms. There is a gym with modern equipment. You can book a ride on horses or camels. The hotel also offers desert tours, quad biking and more.

The animation team entertains guests throughout the day. In the evenings, the amphitheater organizes various shows and shows. There is also a disco bar where you can dance and just have a good time in the company of friends.
