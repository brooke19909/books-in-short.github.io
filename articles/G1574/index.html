The simplest jacket

There are many ways and patterns to knit an openwork jacket. Schemes and description of models will tell you in detail how to cope with such work, and you will surely get a beautiful and high-quality result. One of the simplest models is a shortened jacket with lace straps. For this model, you will need yarn in which a 50-gram skein contains 115 meters of thread, number 4 knitting needles, a button or an air clasp. The size of the finished jacket is 44.

So, we knit a short jacket, back:

  • For the back you need to dial 112 loops. Then knit 10 cm with the front surface, adding 1 loop at the edges.
  • Make another such increase at a height of 4 cm = 116 stitches.
  • After 10 cm, at a height from the set edge of 24 cm, it is necessary to close for the armholes of the sleeves in each second row 1 time 5 loops, 2 times 2 loops, 4 times 1 loop. On the knitting needles will remain 90 loops.
  • When the height of the armhole reaches 21 cm, remove the middle 22 loops from the pin, and finish off both shoulders separately as follows: on the edge of the neck, cut 2 times 2 loops and 1 time 1 loop, knit evenly to the end of the back.
  • At the same time, the shoulder should be knitted: on the side of the armhole in each 2nd row, close 3 times 7 loops and 1 time 8 loops of the shoulder. Knit the second shoulder similarly.

For the shelf:

  • Dial 43 loops, knit, making increases, as on the back, only one edge.
  • At a height of 21 cm from the bottom, begin to knit the neck as follows: reduce 1 loop in each 4 rows 7 times, then in each 6 row 8 times.
  • I knit the sleeve out the same way as on the back, starting from a height of 24 cm from the edge of the product.
  • Tie the shoulder line as follows: at the height of the armhole of 21 cm, in each 2 row close 8 loops twice. The second shelf fits in the same way.

Sleeves:

  • We start with a set of 60 loops. Knit with front stitch, adding 1 loop on each side in each 5 row, until 86 are on the knitting needles.
  • With a sleeve length of 21 cm, begin to knit the okat: on both sides, in each 2nd row, close 1 time 5 loops, 2 times - 2 loops, 18 times - 1 loop, 1 time - 2 loops and 1 time - 3 loops.
  • After 15 cm of okat of the sleeve, the remaining loops must be closed. Both sleeves are knitted equally.

To assemble, lay out all parts on a flat surface, moisten slightly and allow to dry. Then all sew them together. To decorate on the left and right shelves, you need to tie an openwork strip by typing loops on the knitting needles along the edge of the product. You can knit, for example, with the pattern suggested in the photo.





You can, if you wish, crochet a lace border, and if knitting is still being mastered and complex patterns are a problem, you can buy a suitable lace lace 6 centimeters wide at the store and sew it with threads matching the color. Sew a button and buttonhole or air clasp at the level of the beginning of the neck.

Knitting needles

Knitwear can serve for a very long time, be a decoration of the wardrobe, if properly looked after. Care comes down to washing, drying, ironing and storage. Wash knitted items in warm water with gentle detergents, best with your hands. If machine washable, then things should be put in a special bag.

Dry such clothes in a straightened form, gently squeezing in a clean towel or sheet. Openwork jacket can be ironed with steam, holding the iron on weight, or with the help of a steamer, from the wrong side. It should be stored by carefully folding it and placing it in a bag with a sachet of moths. Hang it on the shoulders is not worth it, as the canvas will stretch and deteriorate. So a knitted openwork jacket with knitting needles, the patterns and description of which are chosen to taste and executed with care and zeal, will bring a lot of pleasure at any time of the year in any setting.
