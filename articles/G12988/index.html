Additional features

The location of the sports facility is well known to those people who visit the nearby Sports Palace. It is convenient for many athletes to combine training, as several significant centers are located on one large territory. Yantar Stadium is adjacent to the complex, which provides a host of services. Citizens come here with their children, as sports sections are also open for them. For the younger generation, as many as 4 schools are intended. In them you can learn figure skating, synchronized swimming, hockey and futsal. Education is led by well-known coaches and champions of the country.

In the sports palace near the stadium there is a large ice arena. It is also used for concerts and performances. On ice regularly held competitions in hockey and figure skating.
