The best sights of Sochi

Sochi is experiencing a second youth. Although he received the unofficial title of one of the main resort centers of our country more than half a century ago, the popularity of this health resort increased sharply after the 2014 Winter Olympics. In preparation for this grand sporting event, many objects were built in the city, which today have become recognized sights of Sochi. Photos with the names of these modern architectural masterpieces, as well as cultural, historical and natural monuments located in and around the city, can be seen on many tourist avenues that advertise our country abroad. This article will talk about some of them.

Historical monuments

The ancient sights of Sochi are few. However, among them there are many interesting objects. It:

  • Church of the Assumption of the Blessed Virgin Mary. The church was built in 1904 on the site of an old wooden one. In the Soviet period, the rector of the temple was shot, and he himself fell into desolation. Only in the early 2000s, the restoration of the shrine began. Today, the church, located in the Adler district at ul. Afipskaya, 2, appears before the Orthodox believers in all its glory. Especially noteworthy is its interior decoration, made with the money of the Greek community of the city.
  • Observation tower on Mount Akhun. This monument of federal significance was built in the mid 30s of the last century. The initiative came from Joseph Stalin himself, who ordered the restoration of the Circassian observation tower, which had collapsed from time to time. Since no one remembered what she looked like, the architect S. Vorobyev developed a project for the construction in the Ottoman style. Immediately after that, in just 101 days, a powerful 30-meter tower was erected, which became one of Sochi's most famous historical architectural sights. From its observation deck, one of the best views of the resort city and its surroundings opens. The height of the mountain is 663 m above sea level. Climbing the observation tower is recommended in clear weather. Due to the high altitude at which the point of view is located, on cloudy days, tourists may literally be above the clouds and not see the magnificent landscape for which this tour was started.
  • Volkonsky dolmen. In the vicinity of Sochi there are many monuments of the Bronze and Iron Ages. Among them, the Volkonsky dolmen is especially famous. It is the only structure of this type in the world, completely carved in a sandstone rock. The monument to the most ancient civilization that existed in these parts 5-6 millennia ago is located near the village of Volkonka, not far from the highway connecting Sochi with Novorossiysk.

The most famous attractions of Sochi of this type are:

  • Adler Dolphinarium, located at ul. Lenin, 219.
  • Zoo complex "Laura" (Krasnaya Polyana). In its spacious enclosures you can see lynxes, rare sika deer, miniature roe deer, jackals, wolves and foxes. There, tourists can watch wild boars, alpine goats, chamois, as well as Kuban and Dagestan tours, bison, vultures, waterfowl, badgers, raccoon dogs, raccoons and marten.
  • Adler Aquarium. Tourists who visited this attraction in Sochi, as a rule, leave the most enthusiastic reviews about the underwater tunnel, a walk through which allows you to see fish and marine animals in all their glory and at close range.
  • Adler Monkey Nursery (village of Vesyoloye, 177 Mira St.). According to Darwin, these cute animals are our closest relatives. Perhaps that is why the photos of Sochi's attractions, where they live in spacious enclosures, are of great interest to tourists. In total, 3,500 primates live in the nursery. Historical museums

Such attractions of the city of Sochi are little advertised. Nevertheless, having visited the historical museums of the city, you can learn a lot of interesting things. For example, tourists who want to know more about the past resort, should go to the street. Vorovsky, d. 54/11. There is the Museum of the History of Sochi. Its halls feature several interesting exhibits. Particularly noteworthy are exhibits related to the flora and fauna of the Krasnodar Territory, the culture of the peoples inhabiting these places, space exploration and the feat of the region's inhabitants during the Second World War.

Visits deserve the Adler Museum of History. There you can get information about the archaeological sites of the region, the events that took place on its territory from the beginning of the 19th century until the October Revolution, as well as during the Civil and Great Patriotic Wars.

Museums associated with famous personalities

Among the attractions of Sochi, whose photos are most often placed on advertising posters, a special place is occupied by Stalin's cottage (address: Khosta, Kurortny pr., 120). Today it is turned into a museum, where all the interiors are preserved as they were in the days when the leader of the peoples spent their holidays here. Of particular interest to tourists is the wax figure of Joseph Vissarionovich, sitting in the office of the Generalissimo.

Sochi Park

Many tourists are interested in children's attractions and entertainment in Sochi. In the city and its environs, everything has been done so that little vacationers have the best memories of visiting the resort.

The most interesting amusement park on the Black Sea coast of the Krasnodar Territory is Sochi Park. Many people compare this huge entertainment venue with Disneyland. However, the Sochi residents believe that their park is unique, and they are largely right. After all, there, children and adults will meet with the heroes of Russian folk tales, as well as with characters from Soviet and Russian cartoons.

Iceberg Palace

This famous sports facility was built in Sochi for the 2014 Winter Olympics. It can accommodate 12,000 people at a time. In addition to the main one, there are ice rinks in the ice palace for training skaters and for conducting short track competitions.

Initially, it was assumed that after the Olympics the palace will be dismantled and moved to another Russian region. However, these plans were not implemented, and today Iceberg continues to be one of the main Olympic attractions in Sochi.

Rosa Khutor

This modern ski resort is located on the slopes of the Aibga Range. On Rosa Khutor you can not only go skiing or trekking, but also visit the cultural and ethnographic center My Russia. There you can get acquainted with the history and customs of the peoples inhabiting the Russian Federation and the features of 11 regions of our country.

The Berendeevo Kingdom

A visit to this landmark of Sochi both in winter and in summer will please people of any age.

Park "Berendeevo Kingdom" is located in the valley of the Kuapse River. There you can admire a cascade of 7 waterfalls. The tallest of them, the Berendey Beard, reaches a height of 27 meters. You can also see stone dolmens in the nature park and taste delicious Caucasian dishes right in the forest cafe.

Akhshtyrskaya cave

This natural attraction is located on the highway leading to Krasnaya Polyana, on a cliff near the Mzymta River. Scientists have discovered that the Akhshtyrskaya cave was formed 350,000 years ago. In it, at different periods of the existence of our planet, Neanderthals and Cro-Magnons lived. The latter were engaged in the manufacture of pottery in it, and later even erected partitions that helped protect themselves from drafts.

Inside the cave, lighting was provided and there are stairs with a railing. Guided tours are organized.

The easiest way to get to the Akhshtyrskaya cave is from Adler, by buses with numbers 131 or 135, following to the stop "Trout farm".

Park "Southern cultures"

This corner of nature is located on the outskirts of Adler. There you can see trees and plants from Japan, China, Africa, as well as from both American continents. There you can stroll along the avenue of the tulip tree or along the bamboo grove, and then relax in the shadow of the Himalayan cedars. The decoration of the park is a magnificent rose garden where you can see the rarest varieties of the Queen of Flowers.

Circus

The resort capital of Russia has all the conditions for organizing a cultural holiday for adults and children. For example, back in the Soviet period, the Sochi circus was more popular . Artists from the best circus groups of our country and neighboring countries regularly perform there, presenting dizzying show programs, including with the participation of trained animals. Over the years, viewers could see stars such as Yuri Durov, clown Pencil, Yuri Kuklachev in his arena . Variety artists Lyudmila Gurchenko, Joseph Kobzon, Lyudmila Zykina and others also often performed in the Sochi circus.
