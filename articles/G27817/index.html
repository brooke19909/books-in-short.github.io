Why and how to make a saddle in Minecraft

Today, Minecraft is considered one of the most popular games. In accordance with the plot, each player meets pigs in it, which can be saddled. Therefore, many game lovers are wondering how to make a saddle in Minecraft. Is it even possible to create it? This will be discussed in this article.

Minecraft : how to make a saddle?





Of course, most players use pigs as a food source. But these are far from all possible options. For example, with them you can ride on ice and have fun. True, for this you first need to saddle the animal. So how to make a saddle in Minecraft?

It is immediately worth noting that the saddle is a rather rare element. Of course, some things can be created in Minecraft, but this does not apply to saddles. Such a device can only be obtained in two ways:

  • A saddle can be found in one of the treasure chests. Many people know about this method.
  • In addition, this thing can be purchased. Look for her should be a butcher living in the village. Naturally, here you will buy it, so you will need resources. In most cases, approximately seven emeralds will have to be paid for a saddle. By the way, if someone else does not know, then emeralds can be mined using a diamond or iron pickaxe in the territories of a mountain biome.

It is worth recalling once again that a saddle is an extremely rare thing. And the fact that you have found a butcher does not mean at all that the opportunity to purchase this device will necessarily turn up. Yes, some players are lucky the first time. But in most cases, you will have to be patient and not stop trying to discover this miracle thing.

Saddle - what is it for?





We examined the issue of how to make a saddle in Minecraft, or rather, we learned that this element can only be found. Therefore, you should familiarize yourself with the properties of this thing, as well as the advantages that it provides the player.

If you became the owner of such a rare item, now you need to throw it on a pig. To do this, take the saddle in the active hand, point the cursor to the pig you selected and click on the right mouse button. By the way, by clicking on the same right button you can sit on it.

So what is a saddle for? To begin with, it is worth noting that if you ride a saddled pig on ice, then the speed of movement will be much higher. Until recently, animals with saddles moved erratically and uncontrollably. But after a number of innovations, the player got the opportunity to control the pig. To do this, you need a fishing rod with a carrot. The meaning of its use is simple - the animal will move in the direction in which you will direct the bait.





By the way, pigs can climb obstacles, but only those whose height does not exceed the height of one block. In addition, there is another, quite popular and funny trick. If while riding an animal you can throw eggs or snowballs at it, then the pig is able to fly into the air. And some more useful properties about using a saddle:

  • You can saddle a young piglet - in this case you will have to wait until it grows up, but the saddle will not go anywhere.
  • Previously, the saddle disappeared after the animal "died". Now, after the death of the pig, it simply falls out - you can easily take it and use it again.

Now you know how to make a saddle in Minecraft, as well as its properties and some interesting tricks. Have a nice game!
