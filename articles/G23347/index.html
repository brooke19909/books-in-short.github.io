Grodno hotels: overview, description, rating

The city of Grodno is quite popular among tourists due to the fact that for some time it bore the title of the cultural capital of Belarus. It is located on the banks of the Neman. Nearby is the border with Poland and Lithuania. This explains the even greater interest in the city. Consider what hotels are in Grodno.

Which hotels do tourists recommend?

Tourists have compiled their rating of those hotels that deserve praise. We will consider them in this article.

  1. Hotel "Neman".
  2. Hotel "Cronon".
  3. Hotel "Slavia".
  4. Apartments "Slavia".
  5. Hotel "Semashko".
  6. Hotel "Prigodichi".

Hotel "Neman"

Cost for one day: about 3500 rubles.

In the room stock of the Nemana Hotel in Grodno there are 77 chic accommodation options. The complex is located near the river. The rooms have all the necessary equipment. The decor is done in calm colors. The bathroom has a bath, shower, toilet. There are both cheap and expensive rooms. Therefore, any tourist will be able to choose apartments that are suitable for him. There is a bar on the premises. Here you can order dishes of national cuisine. If there is a need for a diet menu, then the hotel is ready to provide it. A conference room is open for important events. The hotel is quite well located. Thanks to this, you can quickly reach many attractions of Grodno.

The hotel has its own parking. There is internet. Air conditioning, safe, heating. The sauna is open. The reception is open 24 hours a day. There is an elevator. There are places for smoking. If desired, you can order a transfer service. Up to three days maximum register foreigners and stateless persons. You can order a reservation of tickets, tables in the restaurant through the hotel. The first-aid kit is fully equipped. A cot is provided for children. You can rent a car. The hotel arranges excursions. The staff speaks English and Russian.

Hotel Cronon

Cost per day: about 10 thousand rubles.

This building is completely surrounded by greenery. Park Hotel Kronon is distinguished by the fact that having a modern design, it creates a sense of presence in the last century. All rooms are decorated in English style. The colors used are calm. Due to the installed equipment, even the most demanding client will be satisfied. Everyone likes restaurants. They are in a quiet area, the dishes are delicious. There is a terrace. Music plays in the room. In addition to the fact that this hotel in Grodno is located in a park area, it is also near the river. As a rule, those who are not interested in sights and shopping come here. All tourists who live here prefer secluded silence.

Internet is free. There is parking. Equipped terrace, there is a garden. There is a safe, air conditioning, heating. You can ski. Bring breakfast to the room. If you wish, you can ask for packed lunches with you. There is a bar. The restaurant has a diet menu. Open library, conference room. A babysitting service and a menu are provided for children. Guests are offered hiking, cycling, fishing. This Belarus hotel has a sauna and pool. Non-smoking rooms, honeymoon are available. Bike rental is free. The staff speaks a large number of languages: English, Belarusian, German, Polish, Russian.

Hotel "Slavia"

Cost for one day: about 2500 rubles.

Hotel Slavia is located in a quiet part of the city of Grodno. The complex has rooms that are equipped with modern appliances and comfortable furniture. There is a restaurant on the ground floor. Here you can eat European and national dishes. Near the hotel are attractions.

There is heating, air conditioning, safe. Parking is available. You can come with pets. Breakfast, groceries, lunches, drinks are delivered to the room. There is a bar. Internet is free. There are currency exchange offices near this Belarus hotel. Tour desk open. There is a separate suite for honeymooners. Laundry or dry cleaning services are available. Rooms are cleaned every day. There is a special area for smoking. If necessary, shoes can be returned for cleaning. There are special cameras for storing things. If you need to wake up on time in the morning, you can ask the administration to wake up. The hotel is able to serve conferences, meetings, organize excursions, provide carriers, call a taxi. You can also book theater tickets and any events through the complex. The staff speaks English, Belarusian, German and Russian.

Apartments "Slavia"

Cost for one day: about 3100 rubles.

Another Slavia hotel is located in the city center. Here, not only quality guests are served, but also provide free parking, Internet. The apartments are decorated in a classic style. There is a TV connected to cable TV. The bathroom has a shower, hairdryer. Near the hotel there is a cafe where you can try European cuisine. There is also a bar. At affordable prices, you can order hot and alcoholic drinks. The hotel is a kilometer from the train station. In 10 minutes on foot you can reach the bus station.

Hotel "Semashko"

Cost for one day: about 2700 rubles.

Hotel "Semashko" in Grodno is located near the city center. The number of rooms consists of 41 rooms. Each has a unique design, as well as the necessary equipment for relaxation. Guests are allowed to use the pool located on site. The restaurant serves a buffet. It is located on the ground floor. Dishes are completely different. Hotel "Semashko" in Grodno has a good location. From it you can easily get to any attraction. The staff speaks English, German, Polish, Portuguese, Belarusian and Russian.

Hotel "Prigodichi"

Cost for one day: about 1200 rubles.

Nice hotel in Grodno. On its territory there are houses and apartments. When checking in, guests can choose the number that they like best. It consists of a bathroom and a bedroom. There is a separate building with a kitchen, a bathroom and a guest area. Accordingly, there is a shared kitchen for meals. It has all the necessary household appliances, as well as kitchen utensils. The hotel is in a quiet area. To get to the sights or the city center, you will have to order a taxi or you can use public transport. Nearby is a forested area.

Pets are allowed at this hotel in Grodno. A heating system has been created. Ski equipment hire is available. The garden is equipped and there are barbecue facilities. Parking is available. The rooms are non-smoking and for families separately. You can play table tennis, go hiking, horse riding and fishing. The staff speaks Polish and Russian.
