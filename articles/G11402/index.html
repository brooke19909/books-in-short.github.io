How to find out your Kyivstar number? All ways

Many subscribers of mobile operators do not know their numbers, so it is important for them to understand how to find out the number of their Kyivstar number . Indeed, when filling out any questionnaire (on the Internet, in government agencies, etc.), you always need to specify a mobile number so that the client can then call back.

Method number 1. Phone menu

There are two proven ways to find out your Kyivstar number . Each of them is quite simple. The SIM card has an internal memory, where the mobile is saved. How to find out the Kyivstar phone number? We'll have to rummage around a bit on the phone menu. If the subscriber, for example, has a Nokia-202 model (half-touch telephone), then go to the "Menu" section. Here you need to select the "Contacts" option. By going to this section, the user will see the option "My numbers". If the subscriber of the Kyivstar operator goes as the main one for the subscriber (this phone model supports 2 SIM cards), then he will be able to see his own combination of numbers. How to find out the Kyivstar phone number if the card is not the main one? You need to go to the menu section "SIM card manager", where you can fix the settings by making the Kyivstar card the main one. For phones and smartphones on the Android system, the method is about the same. In the menu, the subscriber selects the "SIM card tools" option, then you need to find the "My numbers" section.





Method number 2. Operator

How can I find out my Kyivstar number? The operator himself provides such an opportunity. The operation is somewhat similar to the process of determining the cash balance in the account. Dial on your phone *, then 161 # and press the call key (on most models it is shown as a green phone). The request is processed for several seconds, and then the response with the number in the international format is displayed on the screen. The operator always takes care of his subscribers, so getting information about his number is easy.
