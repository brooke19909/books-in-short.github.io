Rest in Yalta in September: the velvet season in Crimea

Yalta, as you know, is considered a well-known and respectable resort of Crimea. Its main feature is its amazing climatic conditions. The resort is beautiful at any time, and especially in the summer. But, if your vacation did not fall in the summertime, then you should not be upset. Holidays in Yalta in September are also possible, because the peninsula is known not only for beaches, but also for stunning sights.

Yalta in September

You can relax in the domestic sea resort only in the summer - this is a traditional stereotype that does not apply to Crimea. Holidays in Yalta in September are no less beautiful than in the summer, just it has its own characteristics. At this time, there is no longer summer heat, so you can take the time to the stunning beauty of the resort and see all that is not available in the summer due to the intense heat.

There are fewer tourists in Yalta in early autumn, there are no longer large crowds, as many leave the resort due to the start of the school year. The region is plunging into a state of some peace. At the same time, there has been a slight decrease in housing prices and fruits. Therefore, a vacation in Yalta in September has its advantages. Since the city is located among mountains, on the banks of a picturesque bay, the weather conditions in early autumn are quite acceptable for a good vacation. Developed infrastructure, beautiful landscapes and famous architectural monuments will make your vacation unforgettable.



Yalta is located on the same latitude with such a famous city as Italian Ravenna, which indicates the similarity of climatic conditions. The number of sunny days in the city is the same as in Nice or San Remo.

Big Yalta

Yalta includes such villages as Alushta, Alupka, Nikita, Gurzuf and many others. On the coast, the climate is close to Mediterranean. And if you climb the mountains, then the air becomes much drier. Many connoisseurs of the resort believe that autumn is the best time to relax in Yalta. Especially this period is good for people who have health problems.

The Velvet season

Yalta became a popular holiday destination after members of the royal family erected summer residences in Oreanda and Livadia on the coast. Since then, the city has become fashionable among the Russian aristocracy, and its air has been declared incredibly healing. Weather in September

Rest in Yalta (Crimea) in September is also facilitated by weather conditions. In early autumn, the temperature regime is much more comfortable than in the summer. But at the same time, there is still a lot of sun on the coast, especially in the first half of the month. The first two weeks the weather is usually not inferior to summer. And by the end of September, the temperature will gradually drop to autumn levels.



In early autumn, you can still sunbathe on the beaches. The temperature is quite comfortable, but there is no extreme heat. In the first half of the month the air temperature is + 26-28 degrees. Further indicators are reduced to the level of + 20-25 degrees. By the end of September, the number of hours of sunshine and the length of daylight are gradually reduced, and winds are also increasing. Nights are pleased with the long-awaited cool, so you can sleep comfortably with an open window without air conditioning. Going to Yalta in early autumn, it is worth taking a few warm things with you so that you can throw in case of need. As a rule, there is no rain, except in the second half of the month.

Transport Yalta

Based on the reviews of tourists about holidays in Yalta in September, we can say that at this time it is very comfortable to travel and see the sights, and for this it is worth knowing what kind of transport is available. In Yalta there are trolley buses, fixed-route taxis, buses and cruise ships. City transport begins to work from five to six in the morning, and ends at 23.00 or 24.00. Taxis can be called at any time of the day. But motor ships (voyages) transport tourists from May to October, so in September navigation is still in full swing.

There are 53 bus routes and 4 trolleybus routes in Yalta. In addition, the city has a pretty decent selection of taxi services, as a rule, all cars are equipped with taximeters.



Boats and motor ships are another type of transport. They can make a fascinating journey along the big Yalta, to Nikita, Alushta, Gurzuf, Simeiz, Swallow's Nest, Sevastopol and Balaklava. Reviews of tourists about holidays in Yalta in September give reason to recommend a sea trip as a must-see resort destination.

Holidays in Yalta in September: what to do and where to go on an excursion?

The beginning of autumn is a great time for active trips. If you are a lover of sightseeing, then it makes sense for you to go on holiday to Yalta in September, the reviews of vacationers are a direct confirmation of this. It is no secret that the resort is famous for its many palaces and other interesting places that are definitely worth a look. However, in the summer, even with all the desire, it is difficult to devote time to numerous trips that are very exhausting in the heat. September is quite another thing, when it is still very warm, but there is no intense heat, this is the most wonderful time when you can get acquainted with local beauties.

In Yalta and around it are the main symbols of the Crimea - From the Massandra Palace to the Swallow's Nest. Culturally, the resort is not inferior to many European cities. Numerous historical sights are so beautiful that they are worth seeing with your own eyes. Due to the fact that since the nineteenth century representatives of the royal family came to rest on the peninsula, the whole aristocracy also rushed after them. The resort has gained unprecedented popularity, so quite a lot of beautiful palaces and estates appeared here quite quickly. Of course, not all of them have survived to our time. And those that survived cause genuine interest and admiration.

swallow's Nest

In the center of Yalta, you can see the famous palace of Emir of Bukhara, made in the Moorish style. Currently, it is part of the Yalta sanatorium. The main symbol of the South Bank - the Swallow's Nest - cannot be ignored. A small castle on the edge of the cliff is made in a medieval style. The history of the place of this unusual place is rooted in ancient times.



However, the present appearance of the castle acquired only in the early thirties of the last century. Once a one-story building was an ordinary summer residence, and only by order of Baron Shtengel the Swallow's Nest began to look the way we used to see it. At one time, the castle was in disrepair due to the effects of the earthquake. Subsequently, the corresponding work was carried out and since then everyone can enjoy its beauty and make amazing photos on its background.

Interesting places of the resort

If you plan to spend your vacation on the South Coast, then you should consider your vacation: where can I go to Yalta in September? The resort is beautiful and interesting at any time of the year.
