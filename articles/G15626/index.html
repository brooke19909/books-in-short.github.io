Villa Borghese in Rome: description, photos and reviews

Villa Borghese in Rome is an amazing landscape park, which is located north of the center of the Italian capital. In the 17th century Cardinal Camillo Borghese built a picturesque palazzo, which became a favorite place for walks of the Romans and visitors.

Villa Borghese in Rome is the third largest public park in the Italian capital. The parks of Villa Ada and Villa Doria-Pamphili are ahead of the attraction in terms of territory. The area occupied by him is 80 ha.
