Hotel Poppa Palace 3 *, Phuket: photos, reviews

Poppa Palace 3 * in Phuket is one of the most beautiful hotels in Thailand. It attracts the attention of travelers from different parts of the world with its unusual interiors in apartments and the mass of opportunities offered in the service sector. There is a considerable amount of entertainment that will appeal to representatives of different age categories.



general information

Hotel Poppa Palace 3 * (Phuket) was built in 2004. Due to the short time of its existence, the building of the building still has its original appearance and looks modern. Renovation works are periodically carried out inside it, during which the interior decoration of the apartments and the building itself is improved, as well as its technical equipment is improved.

To date, one large building has been built in the area reserved for the Poppa Palace 3 * hotel (Thailand / Phuket), in which the entire number of rooms of this vacation spot is concentrated. Also on the site there is an outdoor pool with a sun terrace, car parking and several places for recreation and entertainment of tourists.

Poppa Palace is ideal for families with children, a romantic trip, as well as for a business trip, as the building has a large conference room and the apartments have great workplaces.

Location

Poppa Palace 3 * is located in Phuket - an island in Thailand, which is known as the largest resort region of this country. A climate favorable for exotic holidays reigns here. Experienced travelers recommend going to rest here in the autumn and winter, it was at this time that sunny weather and a rather high temperature reigned on the island.

The hotel is located within walking distance from the coastline on the famous Patong Beach. From Poppa Palace 3 * (Thailand / Phuket), you can easily get to the bus stop through which regular buses pass, they can be used to go to any part of the island. Many tourists are thus looking for entertainment places, which are many in Phuket.

Despite the fact that the hotel is located almost on the coastline, tourists often go to other beaches that are nearby. Among them, Karon, Kamalu and Kathu are most often visited. It is only 16 km from central Phuket, which can be easily reached by taxi or car rental.

The nearest air traffic hub is located 45 kilometers from Poppa Palace 3 * (Phuket), and the central bus station is 16 km away.



Rooms

The hotel has a small number of apartments - 105. All of them are located in one large building, which extends up to five floors. They are divided into several groups: standard, deluxe, studio, family and junior suite.

Everything here is perfectly arranged from a technical point of view. Modern climatic equipment is located throughout the building, and while staying in the rooms, tourists can independently adjust the desired temperature regime regardless of weather conditions on the street.

In Poppa Palace 3 *, each apartment has a private bathroom with shower and toilet. There is also a washbasin and a place to store bath accessories provided to each guest.

Standard

The standard apartments offer guests a comfortable environment, located on 24 sq.m. Tourists note that the interior of the apartment is decorated in a traditional Thai style, which is expressed in a combination of bright yellow and brown colors, as well as the use of a large amount of wood in individual details.

The furniture equipment installed here is designed for a couple of guests, for whom there is one large bed with an orthopedic mattress and hypoallergenic bedding. Opposite it, on a long surface, there is a TV that broadcasts cable channels, among them there are a couple of Russian-speakers, and the rest of it is arranged for a working area. Near the entrance to the room there is a large wardrobe made of walnut wood, where tourists can store their clothes.

Vacationers staying here can use the minibar, safe for a small amount of valuables, and also a telephone for free, calls for which are paid separately. In addition, the apartment has a kit designed for making hot drinks.

Studio

In their reviews of Poppa Palace 3 * (Phuket), tourists often say that the studio rooms are very bright, they are decorated in a beautiful and original Thai style. They can accommodate three tourists at the same time. Two beds are designed for them: double and single. If you wish, you can install an extra bed here, but an extra charge of 800 baht is required for it.

As for the technical equipment, this class of apartments also has a safe, a telephone and a minibar, the content of which is updated every day. If desired, vacationers can use the tea and coffee set.

Deluxe

The windows of the deluxe rooms offer stunning views of the gardens and the sea, which is ensured by their placement on the second and fifth floors. Each of them has an individual balcony, where you can relax, enjoying the view of the picturesque surroundings.

A total of 25 square meters. m can accommodate three guests at a time, for which there are two beds: single and double. Each of them adjoins a small stand for small accessories. Along one of the walls there is a large wooden wardrobe designed for things.

Junior Suite

All travelers who wish to relax in extra comfort are offered accommodation in junior suites, which provide all the conditions for accommodating four guests. Their large area (45 sq. M.) Is divided into two rooms, in one of which there is a bedroom with two king-size beds, and in the second there is a living room, where in addition to a soft furniture set for relaxing there is a coffee table and a working area. Opposite a large folding sofa is a suspended plasma TV connected to a satellite broadcasting system.

The bedroom has access to the balcony, from where guests can enjoy the beauties of the surrounding nature, drinking hot drinks prepared with the help of an electrical substation installed in the living room.

Nutrition

The Poppa Palace 3 * hotel (Phuket) has a large restaurant serving national cuisine, as well as those prepared in a traditional European style. Absolutely all guests can visit this place. Here, a daily buffet is served for tourists, which serves original salads, snacks, desserts and hot dishes. Travelers really like meat cooked on an open fire, according to them, it is unusually juicy and has excellent taste.

If desired, vacationers can request here a separate diet or children's menu. Lists of meals are provided free of charge, and the dishes contained in them are prepared quite quickly.



Conditions for children

In a large number of reviews left by tourists, you can find comments that Poppa Palace 3 * (Thailand / Phuket) has all the conditions that are necessary for a comfortable stay with children.

For children, the hotel provides a completely free crib type playpen. The administration of the place of rest recommends to notify in advance of the need to install it, as the number of beds of this type is limited.

If desired, parents can send their child to relax in the water zone located on the open part of the territory of the hotel Poppa Palace 3 * (Phuket). In the photo of this resting place, you can often watch a separate pool, which is equipped with everything necessary for the safe swimming of the kids. Its bottom is covered with anti-slip material, and the water in the niche changes every day, due to which it is constantly in a clean state.

The catering facility for young guests provides an additional menu from which you can always order bright and very tasty dishes.

Entertainment

In their reviews of Hotel Poppa Palace 3 * (Thailand / Phuket), many travelers say that you can not get bored while staying on its territory. There is a wide range of entertainment options designed for vacationers with different preferences.

Popular among guests is the water club, which is located on the coastline. It has the opportunity to go diving, water skiing or even learn the skill of surfing. If desired, vacationers can use the paid services of an instructor who will help to study the features of the development of a particular type of water sport.



Many vacationers prefer to go on excursion tours in the vicinity of Phuket. Within their framework, you can explore interesting sights and historical sites, explore the outlandish nature of Thailand and even get to know the locals. Travelers recommend purchasing such tours directly in Thailand, so their cost will be much lower than what is declared in the travel agencies of Russia.

According to vacationers, the average cost of a ticket to the Poppa Palace hotel costs about 140,000 rubles. This price includes a weekly stay for two in standard apartments, as well as the necessary transportation costs on both sides.
