Hotel Donau 3 * (Rimini, Italy): photos and reviews of tourists

The Adriatic Riviera in Italy is a favorite place of rest and shopping for those lucky people who have a Schengen visa in their passport. For one trip to Rimini, you can take a rich excursion program to historical and cultural attractions, purchase a beautiful bronze tan, swim in the warm sea, enjoy the delicious delights of local cuisine and return home with a full suitcase of branded items. But where to stay on the Adriatic Riviera? In this article, the Hotel Donau 3 * is presented to your attention. Rimini is a very ancient city. It was founded in the third century BC. The modern city has overgrown with resort beach areas, stretching for fifteen kilometers along the sea. And one of them is Marina Centro. The first word in the name indicates that this area is seaside. And the second is that it is the modern center of the city of Rimini. Historic sites are located inland. And Marina Centro is the center of modern Rimini, with its shops, restaurants, beautiful promenades and rampant Italian fun.



Where is the hotel Donau 3 * located

Italy, Rimini, Marina Centro, Via Alfieri, 12 - this is the address of the hotel. It is located on a quiet street, but within walking distance from the vibrant evening life of the resort. At seventy meters the Adriatic Sea is noisy. The choice of hotel guests is offered two beaches nearby - No. 51 and 52. Getting to the hotel from the station or airport is not a problem. Near the Donau 3 * hotel (Rimini) there is a bus stop on the city route 15. Tourists say that it is more profitable to purchase tickets at print booths or at points of sale of tobacco products. There they cost euros, while the bus driver has two. The ticket is valid for ninety minutes and with it you can transfer to different routes, including trams. Tourists are also advised to purchase a pack of ten coupons. So you pay only eight euros. The historical sights of the old city can be reached in twenty minutes. Hotel Donau is surrounded by the benefits of civilization. In walking distance there is a supermarket, cafe "Amerigo", a tour desk "Samarkand". Federico Fellini Airport is seven kilometers from the hotel.



Territory

Donau 3 * (Rimini) photos are presented as a typical city hotel. She has no territory as such. The hotel occupies a four-story building with an elevator. Most recently, major repairs were made. The building pleases the eye with a fresh finish. Almost all rooms, except three or four, have small balconies. A characteristic feature of this hotel is that a flag of the country from which the tourist occupying the room came from is hung over each balcony. By this sign - flying colorful banners - the hotel can be easily recognized. Moreover, the sign at the hotel itself is small, inconspicuous. The entire ground floor is occupied by a reception and a restaurant with lounge. At the entrance there is a terrace with tables. This hotel is loved by Russian tourists who come to Emilia-Romagna for shopping.

Rooms

Hotel Donau 3 * in Marina Centro is very small. It has only twenty one numbers. So the atmosphere in the hotel reigns just family. The hostess speaks Russian, one of the girls at the reception too. The tourists described the rooms as small but comfortable. There is only one category of guest rooms at the Donau Marina Centro 3 * hotel - Standard. Rimini is often visited by two. After all, this is a city glorified by Dante, who told the descendants the story of the tragic love of Paolo and Francesca. Most of the hotel rooms are designed for two guests, and they have one wide bed. But there are rooms for three people and even four. But due to the small area, children are accommodated in bunk beds in these rooms. What else is in the bedrooms? Tourists report on a TV with satellite channels (one of them is Russian), on air conditioning, heating in the winter, a telephone and a safe. Bathrooms feature showers and bidets. They cleaned the rooms every day, towels are changed every other day, bed linen - once a week. The rooms can connect to wireless Internet for an additional fee.



How to feed at the Donau Hotel

Hotel Donau 3 * (Rimini) offers guests to choose their own diet. Breakfast is included in the price. They are served in buffet mode. Breakfast runs from eight to ten in the morning. Guests are offered hot drinks from a coffee machine, croissants, sausage and cheese slices, milk, granola, pastries. But there are other nutrition options: HB Plus and FB +. This is an improved half and full board. At these two meals, vegetables and snacks are displayed on a common table (buffet format), and waiters bring other dishes on a fixed menu. The chefs at this hotel specialize in Italian delights. The hotel also has a bar with a small outdoor terrace. Budget tourists were content only with breakfast at the hotel. There are a lot of opportunities to have lunch and dinner at Marina Centro. Tourists recommend cafe "Amerigo". Conad Supermarket, two blocks from the hotel, offers ready-made meals and snacks.

In the lobby of the Donau 3 * hotel (Rimini) there is a cozy common living room with a large TV and library. The building has four floors with an elevator. What to see in Rimini

To the old town to go leisurely step only half an hour from the hotel Donau 3 *. Rimini reviews are advised to inspect, starting with the Arc de Triomphe of Augustus. From it is close to other attractions inherited by the city from the ancient era. This is the Tiberius Bridge, which was built in 21 AD and which still regularly performs its function (and not only for pedestrians, but also for cars), the Roman amphitheater and the "House of the Surgeon".
