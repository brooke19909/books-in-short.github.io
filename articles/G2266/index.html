Club Magic Life Jacaranda Imperial 5 * (Turkey, Side): photos and reviews of tourists

Turkey is a country that has already managed to win the love of tourists from all over the world. This is quite natural, because here travelers can relax on the shores of the Mediterranean Sea, plunge into an interesting oriental culture and enjoy high-class service.

One of the famous resort places is the city of Side. Of course, travelers can choose from dozens or even hundreds of hotels. And the Club Magic Life Jacaranda Imperial 5 hotel complex is considered a quite popular holiday destination.

Of course, before booking a room, it is better to learn more about the selected hotel. For example, how close is it to the beach and is it suitable for families? What do the rooms look like? Are there restaurants and cafes on site? What do tourists think about this hotel?

Hotel Club Magic Life Jacaranda Imperial 5 (Turkey): location and description





Of course, first of all, you need to ask about the location. Club Magic Life Hotel Jacaranda Imperial 5 is located near the seashore. Side city center is 10 km away, and there is a public transport stop nearby, so you can easily get around the area. Antalya Airport is approximately 55 km away (the hotel provides a shuttle service).

This is a new hotel complex - it was opened in 2014. The hotel consists of a large multi-story main building, built in a modern style, as well as 29 small bungalows. The hotel territory is spacious - its area is 13 hectares. It is very beautiful here, because everywhere there are lawns, flowerbeds, terraces, various decorations, and the pool, like a river, crosses almost the entire yard (some bungalows go directly to the pool).

Hotel Club Magic Life Jacaranda Imperial 5: photo and description of rooms





The hotel is quite large and consists of 774 rooms. There are both standard double rooms and more spacious family apartments. Some of the rooms are located in bungalows located directly above the pool, here guests have their own spacious terrace with sunbeds and direct access to the pool.

Of course, you can count on the availability of comfortable furniture and household appliances. Air conditioning, TV, telephone, radio and small safe will make your stay here more comfortable. By the way, guests are entitled to a minibar, which is completely filled immediately upon arrival.

The bathroom is equipped with high-quality fixtures - there is a spacious shower, toilet mirror, wash basin and, of course, clean towels and hygiene products. Cleaning is carried out every day, and with high quality - the rooms are always clean and comfortable.

Dining for hotel guests





What can a tourist use the services of Club Magic Life Jacaranda Imperial 5? Side is a large resort city, almost all hotels here operate on an all-inclusive basis. Paying for accommodation, you also get the right to breakfast, lunch and dinner, which are held in the spacious premises of the main restaurant. Guests confirm that the food is very good. There is always a large selection of dishes, the food is fresh, tasty, prepared from quality products.

In addition, during the day you can get a drink in any of the seven local bars, including strong drinks (although the rule applies only to locally produced goods). Sandwiches and other light snacks are also served here.

There are also several a la carte restaurants on site. They work like in a regular institution - you order from the menu and pay for the order separately. Travelers can enjoy the delicacies of Turkish, Asian and Italian cuisine. There is also a fish restaurant where you can count on a large selection of fish and seafood dishes.

Recreation and entertainment on the beach

Almost all tourists planning a vacation here rely on a beach holiday. What can Club Magic Life Jacaranda Imperial 5 offer? Turkey, Side in particular, is famous for its picturesque coasts. The beach is located just 500 meters from the hotel territory - it can be reached both on foot and using a convenient free transfer.

Of course, you can count on the presence of sun loungers and umbrellas. At the hotel you can get a beach towel. According to reviews, the coast here is very clean, the entrance to the sea is convenient, and the depth increases gradually.

As for lovers of more active recreation, here you can find almost all the possible entertainments - boating, catamarans, water skiing and boats. A lot of positive emotions will give parasailig. Travelers can go windsurfing, scuba diving, sailing, beach volleyball and some other sports.

Can I count on an additional service?





Hotel Club Magic Life Jacaranda Imperial 5 is designed for an easy, carefree vacation. Here, all organizational issues and domestic problems are quickly resolved. The hotel offers dry cleaning and laundry services. There is a currency exchange, a safe at the reception desk, and a luggage storage room. Ample parking is available. Here you can rent a bicycle, car and some other types of transport.

Almost everywhere in the territory there is free access to the Internet. For guests equipped with a business center and a spacious conference room. Employees will undertake the organization of a business meeting, seminar or other event. Also, the hotel can call you a taxi or arrange a transfer.

Leisure and entertainment: what to do on site?





There are plenty of ways to have fun and have a good time at the hotel. On the territory there is a large pool that forms something like a stream - you can swim both in the central part and in any other, more secluded place. Around there are several terraces with sun loungers and umbrellas, where you can have a good time.

At your service a water park with water slides of different sizes - this is entertainment for both children and adults. The pool often hosts various sports activities.

Fans of sports recreation will not be disappointed - there are several tennis courts, as well as basketball courts and a real football field. At leisure, you can look into the SPA center, where they offer a lot of procedures, as well as several types of massage. The hotel has a beauty salon and hairdresser.





At leisure, guests spend time at the cinema or playing a game of billiards. In the evenings, various shows and performances are organized in the spacious amphitheater, and later guests are invited to dance.

Holidays with the whole family: conditions for a vacation with a child

The Club Magic Life Jacaranda Imperial 5 hotel complex is designed primarily for family vacations. All conditions have been created here so that not only adult tourists, but also their children have a rest. Of course, you can count on amenities such as an extra bed and high chair. The buffet has a special children's menu. And children under the age of 12 can live with their parents for free.

In terms of entertainment, the hotel also has a lot to offer. There is a special children's pool, as well as a games room and a spacious playground with swings and sports equipment. The kids club is constantly working, where the kids have fun together with the animators. And there is also a junior club where teenagers are invited - here they can have fun and make friends from all over the world. By the way, a team of animators works with the guys during the day, and in the evenings mini-discos are organized.

How do tourists respond?





Is everything really so good here? What do travelers say about Club Magic Life Jacaranda Imperial 5? Reviews are only positive. This is a really good five-star hotel, and the cost of living on its territory is relatively low.

A huge territory, cleanliness and rich design, comfortable rooms and modern appliances - all this can not but rejoice tourists. Cooks pamper guests with delicious dishes every day, and the animators cope with their task, creating an easy summer mood among the guests. You can play sports, swim in the sea, join the new interesting culture and make new friends. By the way, the employees here understand Russian. Reviews say that all employees are very polite and really work in good faith.

Tourists recommend the Club Magic Life Jacaranda Imperial 5 hotel to people who like a light, calm, but at the same time fun holiday on the seashore.
