The best hotels in Sevastopol in the city center

Sevastopol is perhaps the most recognizable city in Crimea, although it is not the capital of the republic. Hero city, historical and cultural place, port, a favorite resort of Russians and residents of neighboring countries. Of course, many today prefer fashionable overseas resorts, forgetting that it is so close. Nevertheless, true connoisseurs of the picturesque nature of Crimea and its affordable relaxation have remained.

If you have chosen this city for your vacation, the best hotels in Sevastopol in the center will be an excellent option. But there is some problem - before the annexation of Crimea to Russia, the tourism industry was in a state of neglect, for more than 20 years no funds were invested in it. That is why it is quite difficult to find a decent option for living here, corresponding to the very picky taste of modern tourists. But we still try to do it. Reviews of real vacationers will be a good information base.

"Olympus"

The best hotels in Sevastopol, located in the very center of the city - the opportunity to organize an unforgettable vacation. One of the very popular among modern tourists is the hotel "Olympus". The name in this case speaks for itself.





Tourists looking for the most comfortable living conditions, ready to pay a comparable price for this, will surely appreciate this option. A small but upscale hotel located in the very center of the city, but at the same time removed from the hustle and bustle of endless noise, which is one of its main advantages. All 15 rooms at the hotel are extra comfortable. In addition to modern furniture, the room has a TV, air conditioning, safe, hairdryer. All vacationers will have access to a parking lot, a swimming pool with sun loungers and parasols. Everyone who wants to make a trip around the city and the Crimea as a whole will be able to sign up for an excursion. The price includes breakfast.

"Art Bay"

Mini-hotel "Art-Bay" (Sevastopol) is another fairly popular option. Affordable prices combined with excellent location and first-class service make it so popular among modern tourists. The beach is just a stone's throw away, and the largest attractions of the city are not far away. Do not know where to stay in Sevastopol? Pay attention to this option. By the way, its owners surprisingly managed to save the guests from noise, giving them peace of mind during their holidays, which, in principle, is unusual for the resorts of Crimea.





The choice of vacationers are offered single and double rooms. In addition to furniture, each of them has a TV, a refrigerator and air conditioning - not so much, but quite enough for a comfortable stay. Free high speed internet is available for hotel guests. The windows offer stunning views of the mountains and the sea, already breathtaking. Meals are not provided, but just a few steps from the hotel there are enough cafes and small restaurants where you can eat deliciously for a small amount. This is a great hotel in Sevastopol, in the center. We strongly recommend that you pay attention to it.

Hostel "Kanga"

No money for luxury apartments in the best hotel in the city center? Pay attention to the Kanga hostel. It is located in the center, on the embankment of Sevastopol, and is literally buried in the sights. The hostel will be a great option for young people who are not confused by the somewhat cramped conditions and the noise coming from the street.





The rooms are quite bright and comfortable, albeit not so spacious. This is a decent and affordable alternative to hotels in Sevastopol in the city center. Each vacationer will get a comfortable bed, a set of bed linen, a set of towels. Friendly and polite staff will always help you choose the best leisure program. Food is not provided, but within walking distance there are enough catering establishments.

"Old city"

In every city, especially a resort, there are iconic places. So the guest house "Old Town" is a visiting card of Sevastopol. All those who have spent at least one vacation here are returning again and again. It is located on a quiet street in the old city, therefore fully lives up to its name. To the beach - just 10-15 minutes in a calm step. Comfortable rooms for 2-4 people are equipped with everything necessary. In the courtyard is a small pool with sunbeds and houses - a complete alternative to the sea during a storm. And of course, all tourists will have access to wireless high-speed Internet, and absolutely free of charge.





Agidel

A very small hotel in Sevastopol can offer a comfortable stay, in the center is Agidel. The mini-hotel has only 3 rooms, but each of them is distinguished by increased comfort. Each room has its own small kitchen with a set of household appliances and necessary utensils. Hot water is supplied continuously without any interruptions. In addition, each room has a refrigerator, air conditioning, a mini-safe, and guests have uninterrupted internet access.

"Sevastopol"

And finally, the namesake of the resort town. Considering the hotels of Sevastopol, it is simply impossible not to mention this option. This is the best hotel in the city, located on one of its main streets - Nakhimov Avenue. This is a huge hotel with a variety of rooms of various price categories, everyone will find the best option for themselves. Of course, the cost of living in such a hotel can hardly be called democratic, but for comfort and prestige you must pay accordingly.





Each room has air conditioning, refrigerator, safe, minibar, telephone, satellite TV, high-speed Internet. In addition, there is a private parking lot, a restaurant where you can not only have breakfast, but also have dinner in a pleasant company, your own spa complex and conference room. And importantly, the hotel is suitable for people with disabilities, all the necessary conditions have been created for this.

To summarize

Sevastopol is the largest resort in Crimea, a city that is visited annually by hundreds of thousands of tourists. It would seem that problems with accommodation should not arise here. But a hotel in Sevastopol in the city center is a luxury for many. As part of this material, we have selected the best options that can satisfy even the most demanding vacationer. And, no less important, our review includes both luxury and expensive hotels, as well as more budget hotels, which are also located in the city center. We hope you find this review useful when planning your vacation.
