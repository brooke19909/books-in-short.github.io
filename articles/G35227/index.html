Villa "Park Chair", Crimea: reviews and photos

Crimea is a unique paradise on earth, as if specially created for rest and recreation. On the southern coast of the peninsula are concentrated the most popular and chic resorts. There is everything for relaxation: the purest turquoise sea, pebble beaches, a healing microclimate, tropical vegetation and, of course, picturesque mountains that, like a wall, protect the resort from winds and bad weather.

Tourists are ready to take tourists all year round hotels, rest houses, sanatoriums and health resorts, with different living conditions: from budget private mini-hotels to luxury hotels. Among these many places of recreation can be distinguished snow-white villa "Park Chair" (Crimea), which is located twenty kilometers from the beautiful Yalta, in cozy Gaspra.

Accommodation for vacationers

The elite building of the Marat sanatorium (Gaspra, Crimea) is Park Chair, a villa with twenty-four comfortable rooms at its disposal. For VIP-persons there are two Premier Suites.

The rooms are from the second to the sixth floor. They are furnished with expensive modern furniture. All rooms are with a design layout, with large loggias. The views from the hotel are stunning: the sea, Ai-Petri Mountain and the picturesque hotel park.





All apartments of the villa "Park Chair" (Crimea) meet the requirements of four stars. They have everything you need for a comfortable stay: sets of towels, a hairdryer, exclusive furniture, luxury plumbing, a mini-safe and so on. The furnishings in the rooms are conducive to peace and excellent relaxation.

Junior suites can accommodate two people, deluxe rooms are quadruple.

Villa infrastructure

"Park Chair" - 4 * villa (Crimea), which is located separately, in a beautiful park, and is fenced off from other recreation areas.

The hotel administration is located at the entrance, at the bottom there is also a huge swimming pool with all the modern amenities and two steam rooms.

There is a Wi-Fi zone in every corner of the villa.

Surrounding area

As mentioned above, the villa is located on the territory of the sanatorium "Marat" and in fact is one of its buildings, so guests have at their disposal the entire infrastructure of the resort complex, the territory of which is more than seven hectares.

An open-air pool is the highlight of the hotel. Around the pool is a large relaxation area with sun loungers, umbrellas and awnings.





On the territory of the complex there is a Sirena restaurant, a grill cafe, several lobby bars, a cinema, conference rooms, a playground and a medical center.

Distance to the sea, beach

The villa is located five hundred meters from the sea, on a hill. The beach can be reached by going down the alley. The journey will take from ten to fifteen minutes.

The resort complex has a cableway that also leads to the sea and takes vacationers to the beach in a few minutes.





The beach at the hotel is private, pebble. All vacationers at Park Chair villas receive a VIP beach card, which gives the right to use all beach equipment: sun loungers, umbrellas, awnings, showers, changing rooms. For a fee, vacationers can stay in cozy bungalows with mattresses and curtains.

Villa "Park Chair" (Crimea): reviews

Reviews of vacationers about staying in this elite resort hotel are almost all positive. Tourists are quite satisfied with the rest spent here and consider it worthy, like the popular resorts of Turkey and Egypt, and you can trace the fact that every year the reviews are getting better, that is, the hotel is improving and developing.

Guests note the professionalism of the staff, its presentability, hospitality and courtesy.

All guests are satisfied with the rooms. The reviews say about the successful layout of the villa, about cleanliness, decent furniture and household appliances. High-quality plumbing is also not satisfactory.

Some tourists say that there is some disorganization of the administrative staff during check-in, especially at night: there are no carriers of suitcases and nocturnal meals.

Nutrition, in principle, is not satisfactory. The food is tasty and quite varied, but there is no such abundance, as, for example, in hotels in Turkey.

Regarding the beach, vacationers make comments. There is some neglect and mismatch with photos from the official site. Many sun loungers are after repair and must be replaced long ago. Mattresses are not available on sun loungers, while in all popular resorts this is the norm.

There are no complaints about the sea itself, the sea is clean, the slope is gentle. There are some negative reviews about off-season cold water, but this does not apply to reviews about the villa.

The outdoor pool pleases both children and adults. Vacationers respond positively about it, water slides and hydromassages do not let you get bored.

In general, Park-Chair (villa 4 *, Crimea) receives positive reviews and satisfies vacationers. However, the administration has much to strive for. Still, when compared with the popular resorts of other countries, the local people are somewhat behind, but the conditions for development, of course, exist.

Tourists leave negative reviews about the hot water supply, which is not available at night. By VIP standards, this is unacceptable, water should be around the clock.

Animation at the hotel is, but relatively weak, as tourists say. The children's club of creativity and the magnificent children's organizer is celebrated and praised.

The location of the hotel is flawless. Very comfortable. Proximity to all Crimean sights, convenient location, park area and the views that opened did not leave indifferent tourists.

Reviews of vacationers at Park Chair villa allow us to conclude that in order to have a dignified and comfortable rest, it is not necessary to fly to distant lands. Our close and affordable Crimea can offer a vacation no worse, and maybe even better, because it is dearer.
