Toroneos Hotel - a great vacation on the Greek coast

The 2-star Toroneos Hotel is built on the coast of Sithonia Peninsula in Greece. At a distance of half a kilometer from it is the village of Nikiti - a place with a fairly developed resort business.

There are many bars and traditional taverns. Nearby are archaeological sites, windmills. Another resort village - Neos Marmaras, is twenty kilometers away.

The architecture of Toroneos Hotel is Macedonian. Seven two-story cottages are literally buried in a mixed orange and olive garden. The territory is thought out to the smallest detail. Around - blooming bougainvilleas and roses. The hotel was built in one of the beautiful places on the peninsula, on the first sea line. Its infrastructure includes a laundry room, a TV room, a small shop.

The hotel has its own parking, the use of which is free. The 24-hour reception has a depository safe, here you can also call a taxi. A hundred meters away is a bus stop. Thessaloniki Airport is eighty kilometers away.

Rooms

The Toroneos Hotel housing stock consists of 60 standard and ground floor rooms with a balcony or terrace, with a maximum of three people. The simplicity of their style combined with comfort makes it possible to relax. Residents can use the refrigerator, watch TV. In the combined bathrooms - showers, everything necessary for hygiene, as well as a hair dryer. Ground Floor rooms are located a few steps below ground level. The hotel does not have facilities for disabled guests, and check-in with animals must be agreed in advance. All rooms are air-conditioned and have ceramic tiles on the floor.

Nutrition



The Toroneos Hotel organizes an all-inclusive meal service, served as a buffet in the restaurant. It includes free drinks, juices, ice cream, coffee, brandy, and local beer. Toasts and pizza are not provided as free for this food concept. There are also two bars: by the pool and on the beach. Upon request, you can get food in the room. This service is available for a fee.

Beach

Toroneos Hotel has its own pebble-sand beach, which can be reached in three minutes. Everything you need - umbrellas, sunbeds, towels - is free of charge. The entrance to the sea is comfortable, sandy.

Additional Information

Toroneos Hotel 2 * offers children a small playground, on request an extra folding bed. Adults can swim in the pool, play tennis, ride water bikes. At the reception you can book a tour of interesting places. There is also a bicycle rental point for those who prefer to explore the surroundings on their own. Nikiti has excellent taverns where evenings are held in a traditional Greek spirit.

Guest reviews



Those who prefer a relaxing and economical vacation should definitely visit the Toroneos Hotel 2, Halkidiki, Sithonia. This is one of the best places to relax on the Greek coast. Those who stayed at this small hotel have the opportunity to enjoy a measured rest among the greenery on the shores of the cleanest sea. The staff at Toroneos Hotel is always smiling and helpful. The rooms are clean and regularly cleaned. The only drawback for some is the monotony of nutrition.
