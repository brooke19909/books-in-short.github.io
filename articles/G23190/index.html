How to choose a vacuum cleaner without bags for collecting dust "Samsung": reviews, photos

The first models of bagless vacuum cleaners significantly lost to traditional units in productivity and ease of cleaning. With the help of such a device, it was also impossible to perform maintenance of most floor materials, including wood. Nevertheless, manufacturers did not abandon the concept of wet cleaning and in modern design such models are practically devoid of the shortcomings of the first generation. A vivid confirmation of this is the Samsung bag-free vacuum cleaner for dust collection, which organically combines both high power and a wide range of options. The Korean company carefully approaches the development of the technical and operational base, and pays special attention to the design with the construction of models, which increases ergonomics.





Features of Samsung bagless vacuum cleaners

Such devices are able to provide work in two main cleaning modes, that is, perform wet and dry cleaning. Thanks to the washing format of operation, the housewife will effectively and quickly cope with carpet and hard floors, and traditional suction will help get rid of garbage in other places. In this case, to switch between modes, you will not have to replace the functional elements that are supplied with the Samsung vacuum cleaner without a dust bag. The photo below shows a container that is used for both operating modes.





But the most important advantage of this type of model is the aquafilter. It is this element that ensures the efficient handling of garbage after collection. The filter creates a vortex flow in the container, which helps to preserve dirt and dust in the water. Along with this, the removal of harmful particles along with air flows is ensured. For final cleaning, HEPA filter elements are used, which do not allow allergens, dust mites and all kinds of bacteria to spread around the house during the vacuuming process.

Main technical parameters

Vacuum cleaners should be evaluated on a wide range of criteria, which can be divided into two categories. First of all, these are operational capabilities - for example, power and suction power. The second group of parameters for selection involves design features. As for productivity, this is one of the strengths that the Samsung vacuum cleaner has without a dust bag. Reviews note that even entry-level models with a power of 1200 W are able to cope with high volumes of work efficiently. Also in the lineup are units whose power potential reaches 2000-2200 watts. These are models that are close to professional in their capabilities, but design and increased ergonomics still give them vacuum cleaners for domestic use. Now you can go to the design features. Just the absence of the bag led to the compactness and lightness of such a technique. A container with water came in place of the traditional dust collector, but it is much easier to handle.
