Mini-hotel "Black River" (St. Petersburg) for a budget holiday

St. Petersburg is one of the main tourist cities in the world and the northern capital of Russia. Travelers of different age categories and nationalities are eager to come here to see first hand the white nights, watch how bridges are drawn, and get acquainted with the rich historical and cultural heritage of the country. In the city, every street remembers the imperial times, ancient monuments seem to come to life and entail.

Being in a city on the Neva River, as if plunging into the past, you feel like part of that era. Numerous nightclubs, restaurants and bars help return to a modern lifestyle. Many years have passed since the founding of the city, during this period the region has been ennobled, settled down with high-rise buildings, shopping centers, the metro and, of course, hotel complexes.

Arriving in the city, travelers seek to see more attractions without spending money on expensive rooms. Especially for sightseeing, a three-star hotel-hostel "Black River" (St. Petersburg) was built. It is about her that will be discussed in the publication.

Geographic location





The budget hotel is ideal for people with a small income who come to St. Petersburg for new experiences and vivid emotions. "Black River" - the metro located within walking distance from the complex of the same name. A 10-15 minute drive is the Moscow railway station and Pulkovo Airport.

Thanks to a successful transport fork and close proximity to central objects, business travelers, students eager for new discoveries and couples who come for romance flock to the northern capital. Infrastructure near the hotel is replete with shopping galleries, green park areas, fashion boutiques, youth entertainment venues.

Accommodation options





Mini-hotel "Black River" (St. Petersburg) is a small building consisting of four floors. The first two are concentrated 23 numbers. The rooms are small, 15-20 m 2 , decorated in a simple style. The rooms have a city view. For budget holidays, economy-class rooms are designed for 2-4 people, with amenities per unit.

The hostel also has standard rooms with a dedicated bathroom. There are studio type rooms with a living room, bedroom and kitchen areas. For personal use, guests are provided with a microwave, household utensils and kitchenware. On request, a baby cot is delivered.





It should be noted that all room categories are connected to high-speed Internet. The rooms have beds, bedside tables, wardrobe and LCD TV. Disposable slippers and shaving accessories are provided. As for cleaning, it is carried out according to a certain schedule.

Given that the hotel "Black River" (St. Petersburg) is focused on budget tourists, you will not have to expect an abundance of services. However, the hotel provides parking - paid. Laundry facilities, 24-hour room service and a TV room are at guests' disposal.

What to see in the city?

Mini-hotel "Black River" (St. Petersburg) conducts sightseeing tours for its guests. Experienced guides will introduce you to the history of the city, tell you in detail about all the significant objects. You will certainly be delivered to the Peter and Paul Fortress, the Kunstkamera and the Hermitage. Travelers will be offered a boat trip along the Griboedov Canal, the Moika River and the Neva. A lot of vivid impressions and pleasure are provided to you.





And a week is not enough to visit all the significant places in the northern capital. There are about 250 historical buildings in the city alone, not to mention museums, drawbridges, pedestals and parks. The suburb of St. Petersburg is also replete with cultural attractions that will interest tourists.

Digest

Mini-hotel "Black River" (St. Petersburg) in some cases deservedly receives positive reviews from visitors. A wonderful hostel in the center of a beautiful city with a rich history. For little money you are provided with a bedroom with the necessary furniture and a TV. At the cost of savings you can visit the sights.

At the same time, the quality of service at the hotel is not lost. The hostel staff is friendly, courteous and attentive to all its guests. The clear benefits include a 24-hour front desk, free internet and a convenient location. In general, this is a good hotel for a reasonable price. Only you have the choice of overnight stay.
