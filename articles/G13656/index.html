Hotel "Grand Avenue" in Yekaterinburg: description and reviews

The Grand Avenue Hotel in Yekaterinburg is considered one of the most convenient and comfortable. This three-star complex is located in the very center of the city, so it can be a great place to stay for guests.

general information

The Grand Avenue Hotel in Yekaterinburg is one of the first business-class establishments. The hotel was opened in 2006 and since then has become one of the most popular places among city guests. It is located at 40 Lenin Avenue. The Grand Avenue Hotel (Yekaterinburg) is advantageous in terms of its location. Being in the very center, it makes it possible to quickly get to key objects of the city. In general, the institution is focused on business guests. Next to it are large shopping, business and exhibition centers, the Administration of the city and the region, the best cinemas and theaters of Yekaterinburg.

How to get there

Any guest of the city is interested in the question of how to get to the Grand Avenue Hotel in Yekaterinburg. It is worth noting that with this point you will not have problems. There is a public transport stop near the train station. From here you can get to the hotel in about half an hour without any changes. To do this, you can use the trolleybus number 9, bus number 1 or minibus number 057. You need to get off at the stop "Lenin Avenue". The Grand Avenue Hotel (Yekaterinburg, address provided in the article) is just a few minutes away. By car from the station can be reached in ten minutes.
