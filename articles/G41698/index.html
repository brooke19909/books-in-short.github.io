Hotel Napasol Boutique Hotel (Ayia Napa, Cyprus) - overview, description and reviews of tourists

Cyprus is a resort island, whose hotels are ready to receive tourists within its walls all year round. One of them is the Napasol Boutique Hotel in Ayia Napa. It is often advised to choose as a place of rest for many tour operators, noting that the prices are low, and the service and level of accommodation are quite decent.

Location

Napasol Boutique Hotel is located near the Nissi Beach city beach, which is well placed for a good holiday. There are other hotels next door to this vacation spot that also host tourists throughout the holiday season.

Ayia Napa is a couple of hours drive from the international airport where tourists arrive. A comfortable bus takes them from here and takes them to the hotel.

Vacationers at Napasol Boutique Hotel often prefer to visit not only the beach, but also interesting places that are in the area. So, experienced tourists recommend going to nightclubs and restaurants in the evening, which are nearby - here, according to the guests, you can have a great time. Almost within walking distance from this place for rest there are several attractions of the city, including the Thalassa Museum. If you wish, you can use public transport and go with children or in the company of a loved one to the city water park.

general information

Napasol Boutique Hotel was built recently - in 2016. That is why its buildings have a new, modern look that guests like. It belongs to the category of mini-hotels, it is located on a small territory and presents to the guests a small number of rooms.





Tourists in their reviews say that both inside and out, it is very stylish and beautiful, it is nice to be here and spend your vacation.

Rooms

All tourists who come to this place of rest are offered a tiny selection of apartments - there are only 19 of them. All are presented in the rooms so that guests can feel comfortable and cozy here.

All apartments have only modern furniture, which is made of quality materials. Each room has an individual air conditioning, which allows you to adjust the temperature conditions to optimal, allowing guests to enjoy pleasant coolness on hot days.

The rooms also have private bathrooms. They have one bathroom, shower cabins, as well as a sink. In addition to all this, a hairdryer and a set of bath accessories are provided. Each guest at the time of entry receives a set consisting of a large terry towel, bathrobe, and slippers.

Room Interior

In reviews of Napasol Boutique Hotel 3 * you can often meet the opinion of guests that the hotel rooms are very beautiful, their interior is stylish and original. Its colors are dominated by white and blue shades, which give a certain lightness to the overall picture. Each room here has huge windows all over the wall, covered with light white textiles - they offer stunning views of the endless sea spaces, and some of the city neighborhoods can be observed.





As for the furniture, the central place in any apartment is occupied by a large double bed made of wood and covered with a blue blanket. The rooms, which are designed to accommodate four people, have two beds of this type. Also, all rooms have a pair of soft blue armchairs and a small coffee table made of very durable glass. Guests can flip through magazines, as well as drink tea or coffee made using the tea substation, which is also in the room. All rooms have small white wardrobe cabinets, as well as small bedside tables near which beds can be folded up.

There is a large plasma TV on the wall on which you can watch your favorite satellite broadcasts, since a large number of Russian-language channels are broadcast on it. For an additional fee, rooms of any category can be equipped with a safe, minibar or telephone.

Almost every room has a private balcony, on which there is a small table and a pair of neat white chairs. From here, tourists can observe the surrounding nature.

Living conditions for children

Most of the guests who come to rest here note in their comments that the Napasol Boutique Hotel 3 * (Cyprus, Agia-Napa) is not suitable for families with children. In their opinion, it is best to come here with your loved one - the atmosphere is filled with romance.





However, despite this, the hotel provides some facilities for babies. In particular, they include the presence of a playground where you can have fun and actively spend your free time. As for food, the local restaurant has special seating areas that are comfortable for this category of guests, as well as a special menu, the dishes of which attract attention with their bright colors and mouth-watering names.

Parents note that in this place there is no possibility of renting a crib and strollers, which is very inconvenient.

Nutrition

For guests of the Napasol Boutique Hotel in its main building there is a small restaurant, the interior of which is made as spectacularly as in the whole building. Here at any time you can taste the perfectly prepared dishes of European and Mediterranean cuisines. In particular, guests recommend getting here to pay attention to dishes prepared with seafood. According to them, they have special taste qualities that are not found in any institution. Many of them focus on the fact that upon request, a guest can be provided with a diet menu that contains only the lowest-calorie dishes.





The restaurant, if necessary, provides room service, delivering food directly to the apartment.

The hotel also has a bar where you can not only spend time at a table with a cup of tea or coffee, but also try snacks, salads, as well as a number of original desserts. In particular, tourists recommend trying ice cream, which is prepared here according to a company recipe.

Hotel Services

In reviews of Napasol Boutique Hotel, it is often noted that the hotel administration provides a wide range of services for guests, with which you can significantly improve the conditions of your vacation.

First of all, the services provided by dry cleaning and laundry are very popular - they are paid, and their cost directly depends on how many things need to be washed. Often, guests rent cars here, where they then go to explore the surroundings of the hotel and local attractions. If the tourist has a car, a parking space in an open, but guarded parking lot is provided completely free of charge for the entire stay of the guest at the hotel.





Throughout the Napasol Boutique Hotel, including its rooms, tourists can enjoy free Wi-Fi access. It should be noted that due to this they have the opportunity to stay in touch with their friends and relatives around the clock.

Conference hall

If necessary, the conference room, located on the ground floor of its building, can be rented to tourists who are on a business trip. In terms of its parameters, it is ideal for holding various business events and meetings with business partners. A small area houses furniture for up to 40 people: comfortable upholstered chairs and tables. A small set of stationery is also provided.

As for the technical equipment of the hall, it has a projector, laptops, copier, printer, fax, as well as sound equipment and video equipment, which will certainly make any presentation even more vivid.

Aquazone

For tourists who like to sunbathe or perform water procedures, the hotel has a small aquazone, represented by a pool with clean water. It is cleaned daily, and the filling is heated by the action of sunlight, as this process is not automated. The pool has a coating that prevents slipping, its sides glow at night.





Around the water lies a sun terrace, where guests can relax and sunbathe. For a comfortable stay, guests have several sun loungers and beach umbrellas. If desired, guests can rent large towels from here.

Beach

The coastline is located near the Napasol Boutique Hotel 3 * (Cyprus) and is public, that is, tourists from other hotels, as well as residents of the city, can relax on the local beach. In order to get to this place, guests need to overcome a short distance - 100 meters.

On the beach, everything is arranged for a good vacation. Its territory is covered with fine and very soft sand, and the water during the holiday season is warmed up to a comfortable temperature - about 23-26 degrees.

If you wish, staying here, tourists can rent comfortable sun loungers, umbrellas, as well as towels. It also rents air mattresses for swimming. Along the coastline you can find a lot of entertainment designed for both extreme people and fans of a quieter, relaxing holiday.

Prices

Napasol Boutique Hotel (Ayia Napa) attracts many tourists with its nice accommodation prices. If we talk about the exact cost of a ticket to this hotel, then it will depend on the departure point and the season chosen for the rest. So, in the midst of the spa season for Muscovites, a trip to Napasol Boutique Hotel 3 * (Cyprus, Ayia Napa) costs an average of about 75,000 rubles. Most tour operators include flights, weekly stays, and travel from the airport to their destination at this price.

Tourists reviews

Guests of Napasol Boutique Hotel (Cyprus / Ayia Napa) often share their impressions gained during their holidays on social networks or on the websites of travel agencies. As a rule, in their comments they give an assessment of the living conditions and level of service.

Most tourists note the decent work of hotel administrators who are always trying to help tourists solve their problems and issues related to accommodation. They work around the clock and are located at the front desk. Some guests from Russia consider that the majority of employees speak Russian as a pleasant moment.

A lot of attention is paid to the rooms, or rather their interior. Undoubtedly, the interior decoration pleases the guests. As they tell, staying in the room, you can truly relax and enjoy every minute of relaxation.





Guests very often pay attention to the opportunity to visit places that are in the area. Many of them in their reviews recommend visiting certain places and share their features. Most of the young travelers are advised to go on vacation to the nightclubs of the city, as the hotel itself lacks evening entertainment and animation does not work.

If you evaluate in general, then guests are ready to recommend this hotel for relaxation to their friends and relatives.
