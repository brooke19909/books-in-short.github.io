"Azure" is the best water park in Belgorod!

Belgorod residents and visitors can spend their weekends on the Cote d'Azur. You do not need to go abroad for this. It is enough to travel a few kilometers outside the city, to the village of Nizhny Olshanets. Fans of extreme sports and active pastime will appreciate the slides and pools of the Lazurny water park.

Water rides, large pools, sun loungers and entertainment programs will appeal to everyone. The space is not only equipped for adults, but also includes a children's area. About the water park "Azure"

The water park in Belgorod appeared only in 2017. The best engineers from different countries worked on his project. All the latest developments and technologies were taken into account, so this is a modern space for recreation.

The park is located directly in the open air, but, regardless of the weather, the water temperature is constantly +30 degrees. On the territory of 3 thousand square meters. m are located:

  • 3 pools;
  • 3 slides for adults and 3 mini-slides for children;
  • area with sun loungers and umbrellas;
  • area with gazebos;
  • entertainment area.

Each visitor will find a pool to his taste. Anyone who learns to swim, can soak in the water of a small pool. Well, for those who like to dive and swim at great depths, the complex has two more large reservoirs.

In the Belgorod water park provides a recreation area. If you ride a hill or swim tired, you can lie on a sun lounger and take sun baths. For large companies and families there are special gazebos where you can chat and eat.

Often at the entertainment venue animators hold various events and contests. This type of leisure activity is especially interesting for children. During the performance, the child is not only in the fresh air near the pool, but also has fun. In the evenings, dances and concerts await visitors.

Water Park Slides

Aqua complex "Azure" includes three slides, different in level:

  • Multislide is an open slide with slopes. Perfect for those who are afraid of heights or just starting to ride a roller coaster. Entrance is possible with growth from 1.5 meters.
  • "Pipe" is a closed slide. Climbing the tower, a man sits down and rolls at great speed down the pipe. At the exit, he enters the pool. Entrance is also possible with growth from 1.5 meters.
  • The Wave is the most extreme slide. You can go down it only on special air mattresses or circles. Entrance is allowed with growth from 120 cm.

Don't worry about security. All slides were thoroughly examined. In addition, a rescue instructor is assigned to each attraction, which controls the process of descent of the swimmer. Any weather in the Belgorod water park - grace. It‚Äôs so cool here!

How to get to the water park

The address of the water park "Lazurny": Belgorod, Belgorod region, s. Nizhny Olshanets, st. Razumenskaya, 15. Do not be afraid that the water park is located outside the city. From the center to the "Lazurnoe" road takes about 15 minutes.

Also accessible by public transport: buses 103 and 232.

Ticket Prices at Lazurnoye

The water park in Belgorod provides two fares for buying a ticket. The first is a half-day ticket (5 hours): 300 rubles for children and 400 rubles for an adult. The second is a full day. 600 rubles for children, 800 for adults.

In the morning, the ticket price will be lower. If the stay is paid in the afternoon, the amount will be from 400 rubles for a children's subscription and from 600 for an adult.

Ticket price depends on the person‚Äôs height. A free ticket is issued if the child is less than 1 meter tall, as well as for children under 5 years old.

A child ticket is paid for people from 1 to 1.5 meters tall. People above 1.5 meters pay an adult ticket. The additional payment includes the rental of gazebos and towels.

The schedule of the water park "Lazurny" from 10:00 to 21:00. The morning ticket is bought from 10:00 to 13:00, afternoon from 13:00 to 19:00. Sale ends at 20:00.
