Hotel Washington Resort Hotel & Spa 5 * (Turkey / Side): photos and reviews of tourists

Spend a wonderful weekend, an unforgettable vacation or summer vacation with the whole family is possible in sunny, hospitable and colorful Turkey, choosing a resort that meets the taste preferences of each participant of the trip. Wanting to abstract from the hustle and bustle of city life and reunite with nature, many stop at places like Side or Manavgat. Surprisingly picturesque landscapes, fresh air and developed infrastructure led to the annual influx of a large number of tourists into these parts.

general information

The five-star Washington Resort Hotel & Spa 5 * hotel is located among coniferous forests in the lake district. Those who dream of new experiences, vivid photographs and a pleasant stay at affordable prices come here. The advantages of this resort are endless: many attractions, excellent opportunities for active pastime, mild Mediterranean climate and the absence of hot heat.

Accommodation

The hotel complex Washington Resort Hotel & Spa includes 305 rooms. They are represented by the following categories:

  • Family Room;
  • Sea View;
  • Land View;
  • Large Room.

The interior of the Washington Resort Hotel & Spa 5 * (Turkey, Manavgat) is made in a modern style. Designers have worked wonderfully to create a creative design of rooms. The experimental combination of white with bright colors pleases the eye of every vacationer. Some chambers are decorated with wall paintings by local painters. The rich colors of bedding and bedspreads are in harmony with the curtains.

The floors are covered with laminate or carpet. Numerous sconces and floor lamps complement the interior of the space. There is access to a balcony equipped with plastic furniture for a wonderful outdoor recreation. Seeing the sunset or meeting the dawn will be doubly pleasant when you see the starry sky or the azure waters of the Mediterranean.

Daily cleaning and towel changes are carried out by tidy and conscientious maids, mainly in the morning. Room Service is available around the clock and requires an additional charge.





Home improvement

Wooden furniture is made of high-quality and environmentally friendly material, includes: beds (single, double or bunk), a folding armchair, a desk, a spacious wardrobe, bedside tables. The central air conditioning system provides favorable temperature conditions depending on weather conditions outside the window.

A multi-channel TV with Russian-language programs makes it possible to watch your favorite TV show or current news at any time. A large mirror helps to impose beauty for fashionistas and fashionistas who every second monitor their appearance. The presence of a minibar pleases all residents, and its fullness includes drinking and mineral water, soft drinks and juices. Further updates are possible at an additional cost. The presence of an electric kettle allows you to brew coffee and tea without leaving the living area.

An electronic safe is designed to save important papers, documents or cash, which ensures a carefree vacation. To communicate with family and friends, you can use the phone for an additional fee. Wi-Fi and free internet access are not available.

Bathroom

The bathroom is distinguished by its spaciousness and expensive design. Equipped with bath or shower, hairdryer and mirror. Marble countertop under the sink adds gloss to the design. There are all necessary personal hygiene products and cosmetics. The number of towels complies with the regulatory standards of five-star hotels. To take a bath in such conditions, as guests note, is a pleasure.





Satisfying hunger

The hotel complex Washington Resort Hotel & Spa 5 * (Side, Turkey) receives positive reviews for the organization of catering. He receives guests under the program "All Inclusive", which is valid from 10:00 to 00:00. The form of serving food is a buffet, which is filled with a variety of vegetable salads, cereals, meat and fish dishes, desserts, fruits and many other delights for satisfying the hunger that has appeared. Five meals a day are held in the main restaurant, which includes an indoor dining room, an outdoor terrace and a botanical area. Capacity is designed for the simultaneous reception of 700 people. Dishes are mainly international.

Designer ceilings with original lighting, comfortable furniture and marble floors add a high price to the interior of this establishment. High chairs and a children's menu are provided for young children.

Bars and Restaurants

In addition to the main restaurant, you can eat in the additional two A la Carte, which work on a custom-made menu. Their focus is Ottoman and Italian cuisine. Reservation of the table and payment are required. They can be visited only in the evening. It is necessary to remember the dress code: it is impossible to have dinner in swimming suits and pareo.

All dishes are prepared by leading chefs and international cooks who do their job with pleasure.

During the day, you can grab a bite to eat at the snack bar and quench your thirst at the beach bar, poolside, and lobby. All drinks offered are included in the price of the permit; only freshly squeezed juice and imported alcohol are paid extra.

In the cozy patisserie you can taste delicious Turkish coffee along with original desserts. Younger guests prefer ice cream served at certain hours.

Beach

Hotel Washington Resort Hotel & Spa 5 * (Side, Manavgat) is located on the first coastline, it is possible to get to the sea in only a few minutes. A well-kept beach area with many sun loungers with soft mattresses, umbrellas, wooden walkways - staying here takes most of the time for travelers. The beach is sand and pebble, has a gentle slope into the water. The sea is crystal clear, the bottom with underwater inhabitants is visible. For a detailed acquaintance, you can snorkel or use the diving service on the high seas. Fascinating deep flora and fauna has not left anyone indifferent.

There is an equipped volleyball field on the coast. New acquaintances and pleasant communication can be found by playing a team game. For children - a playground with a variety of slides, ladders, swings and carousels.

Water entertainment is represented by riding a banana, water scooters, canoeing, ring. Essential infrastructure facilities are showers, changing rooms and toilets. Towels are provided free of charge.

What to do for kids

Washington Resort Hotel & Spa 5 * reviews of a negative plan are extremely rare. Basically, they are all positive. At times even grateful parents leave enthusiastic responses. They argue that the leisure of little guests is thought out to the smallest detail, because if the baby is satisfied, the parents are happy. The daytime Froggy Kids Club is open to guests aged 4 to 12 years. Here, under the supervision of a qualified teacher, the children will pass a series of master classes, perform developmental tasks, and make interesting crafts. Drawing, constructor games, picking up puzzles and much more is available to every kid. A cheerful team of animators will paint the day with bright colors and unforgettable emotions.

The giant trampoline complex with slides impresses every kid, it is almost impossible to pick up a child from here, as many tourists vacationing with families say. Futsal, sports and competitions will increase immunity and improve the health of children.

Babysitting services are paid extra.





Recreation and Sports

The desire to keep the body in good shape is realized through the fitness center. A well-equipped gym with lots of exercise equipment is hung with mirrors. Qualified instructors will make up the volume of individual loads for everyone. They also conduct aerobics and fitness classes.

Darts, table tennis and chess games are available at any time of the day. Basketball field, tennis courts, billiards - everyone will find an occupation pleasing to the soul. Payment for lighting and equipment is carried out upon. Hammam, sauna, jacuzzi will relax the body of every visitor.

Spa services include a number of anti-aging and therapeutic procedures to restore the body and improve appearance. Massage will plunge into the world of pleasure, because professional massage therapists know their job.

The evening animation program in the amphitheater will present amazing performances to guests in the form of fiery dances, humorous scenes and interesting contests. The evening ends with a disco.

Additional services provided at the hotel

For those arriving by car or those who have rented a vehicle, free parking spaces are provided. Payments can be made by credit card, there is a currency exchange. Laundry and ironing services are paid extra.

Three conference rooms for meetings and other public events are equipped with advanced technologies, equipped with a projection screen, wired and wireless microphones, a projector, and comfortable furniture.

On site there is a hairdresser, beauty salon, grocery stores and souvenir shops.

Summing up all the above, we can conclude that the Washington Resort Hotel & Spa 5 * hotel (Turkey, Side) is definitely worthy of the attention of those who decided to visit this beautiful city.
