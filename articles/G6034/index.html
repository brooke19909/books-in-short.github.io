Hotel Aparthotel Four Elements Suites 4 * (Spain / Costa Dorada): reviews and photos

Spain is a beautiful country with a variety of climatic conditions, heterogeneous vegetation and flora, as well as with a lot of attractions. And also a very popular place among tourists. Especially loved by travelers is a resort like Salou. It is rich in hotels and hotel complexes. And I would like to mention Aparthotel Four Elements Suites 4 * - a hotel located in La Pineda, one of the villages of Salou.

Service

Aparthotel Four Elements Suites 4 * has everything you need to provide customers with a comfortable environment during their stay. The reception is open 24/7, there is a left-luggage office, a taxi call service, and a tour desk. There is also a laundry with dry cleaning and a currency exchange office (with a favorable rate). If guests wish to travel by car, they can be offered car and bicycle rental services.

The tour desk will advise you on any questions regarding the resort. Visitors can recommend places that are really worth visiting, tell a few secrets regarding economy and travel. The staff is multilingual, there are even Russian-speaking employees, so there will be no problems with contact. The guests here are treated warmly and politely, but if you want to attract local people even stronger, you can learn some basic phrases in Spanish, thus demonstrating your respect for Spanish culture.





Entertainment

Aparthotel Four Elements Suites 4 * is a hotel complex designed for a comfortable stay of tourists during their holidays. But in our time, the hotel is also perceived as a place for entertainment.

The hotel has a small but comfortable movie theater where you can spend your time watching a movie. There is also a games room, two outdoor swimming pools, a nearby golf course, and a billiard room. Lovers of an active lifestyle will love the playground.

And in the evenings, guests are entertained by animation staff, so that guests can enjoy vibrant and spectacular shows.

By the way, Four Elements Suites Aparthotel 4 * (Spain) also has its own Wellness Center with massage rooms and a gym. Here guests will be able to spend time on health and beauty treatments, relaxing with both soul and body. The center employs highly qualified specialists with a medical education, so the program will be selected individually for the person.





For children

If guests with a small child intend to come to Aparthotel Four Elements Suites 4 * (Costa Dorada), then they can have no doubt that they made the right choice. The hotel has a children's mini-club in which the kids are supervised by educators.

In the play area, children can make new friends and have fun with them. There are board and electronic games, cartoons, a hall for creativity. Animators are working in the mini-club, making the kids' leisure even more diverse. These are dance events, gymnastics, competitions, mini-tournaments, a disco and much more.

By the way, a children's pool is also here. It is shallow, so even the smallest can swim in it. And at the same time they will be in full view of adults, since the pool is located in such a way that parents can monitor their children from anywhere.





About nutrition

Aparthotel Four Elements Suites 4 *, like any other self-respecting institution, has a restaurant and bar where guests can satisfy their hunger and thirst.

Many visitors in their reviews note precisely the aspect of nutrition. They say in the comments: the food in the hotel is delicious. European breakfasts here: there are eggs in various forms, fried sausages and sausages, bacon, sausages and ham, flakes, oatmeal. Fresh bread is served, from which you can make crispy toast, supplemented with jam, pasta, butter, cheese. A wide variety of pastries: there are cheesecakes, donuts, rolls, croissants. And of course, fruits are diverse, from nectarines and apples to plums and pears.

For lunch and dinner - a lot of meat. And in a variety of variations. There is duck, chicken, lamb, seafood, pork. Side dishes are also in variety: potatoes, stewed and fresh vegetables. A mass of cakes for dessert and baking. If a person ordered "all inclusive", then you do not need to pay for drinks separately, as is the case with a guesthouse, for example. They serve traditional sangria, homemade wine, juices, coffee, lemonades and much more. In general, it will not work to stay hungry here.





About apartments

Aparthotel Four Elements Suites 4 * (La Pineda) has 166 rooms. The most budgetary are the standards. The space is divided by an interior door into a living room and a bedroom. There is also a balcony. The living room has a pull-out sofa and a huge plasma TV with cable channels. There is a dining area, free Wi-Fi and even a kitchen with a fridge, microwave, hob, oven and coffee maker.

Also, the apartments are equipped with air conditioning with climate control, safe, telephone. There is a bathroom with shower, toilet and bath.

In suites, the conditions are almost identical. Only spaces are larger and berths, as they are designed for a larger number of guests. Prices need to be determined individually, but on average apartments cost about 155 euros per day. The cost may vary depending on the season.

Important Nuances

Check-in at the hotel starts at 15:00, and guests must leave the apartment before 11:00. But if guests arrived earlier, then they can be issued immediately, provided that the room they ordered is free and cleaned.

Upon arrival, a deposit in the form of cash or a credit card is required, the data of which the person indicated when booking. You will also need to present an identity card with a photo (passport).

It is worth knowing that in this hotel cash payments by law can not exceed the amount of 2500 euros. If a person is issued a larger bill for accommodation, then he will need to contact the administration for information. By the way, a city tax of one euro per person is still additionally paid.
