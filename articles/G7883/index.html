How to choose a Sberbank card?

The choice of a debit card should be made based on your needs, financial capabilities and lifestyle. In this case, you can fully take advantage of all the advantages of this tool and get the maximum benefit for yourself.

Despite the fact that the types of Sberbank cards are completely different, their payment systems are mainly Visa and MasterCard, which are accepted for payment all over the world. These payment systems differ in that "Visa" is more common in the USA, Latin America, Australia and Canada, and "MasterCard" - in European countries and Africa.

Another difference is that the Visa payment system charges a commission of 0.65% of the total payment for currency conversion operations. As for MasterCard, it does not charge a commission at all.

Instant Issue Cards





If you need a card urgently, and you do not have the opportunity to wait a couple of weeks, then you can get a card immediately when contacting a bank branch. This requires a banking service agreement. In addition, Sberbank offers types of plastic cards that do not have annual service. And also you do not need to pay anything for the card itself. Sberbank Youth Debit Cards





The types of these cards were specially developed taking into account all the needs of young people under 25 years old. Their advantages are as follows:

  • The types of such cards are relatively inexpensive.
