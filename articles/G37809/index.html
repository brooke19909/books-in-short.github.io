A bit about the resort

As we already mentioned, the SunConnect Djerba Aqua Resort 4 * hotel (Tunisia, Djerba) is located on the famous island of Djerba, which is the southernmost point of Tunisia. Of course, the island cannot be called a center of entertainment, such as, for example, Sousse, but nevertheless there are some interesting places here that are worth seeing in between beach vacations.

The island has become so popular because of its endless sandy beaches and wonderful climate. Washed in warm Mediterranean waters, it is suitable for relaxation almost all year round. The temperature of air and water on the island is always a couple of degrees higher than on the continent. In addition, Djerba is not lifeless sand, but a completely green island with numerous olive groves growing here due to the huge number of underground sources.





Who and when first discovered the island, is exactly unknown. However, since ancient times, the views of many conquerors were riveted to him. In the capital of the island of Houmt Souk, there is a port, which is considered one of the largest in the whole Mediterranean, and it occupies the first place in the whole country for industrial fishing. On the streets of the city there are many cafes and restaurants that offer visitors the most delicious and fresh seafood dishes.

The island has many beautiful lagoons in which you can see flamingos living here in natural conditions.
