Night clubs in Rostov-on-Don. The best night club (Rostov-on-Don)

After a hard day, I just want to relax somewhere and throw out my energy. To do this, you can just visit a night club. Rostov-on-Don has a lot of such institutions.

All clubs can be divided into the following categories:

  • standard;
  • karaoke clubs;
  • clubs "open air";
  • unusual clubs;
  • strip clubs.




The best places of entertainment Rostov-on-Don

The best nightclubs in Rostov-on-Don are always filled with longing to relax. Residents of the city should pay attention to the following institutions:

  1. "Cuba Libre" is one of the most popular holiday destinations of Rostovites. He has been working for the second decade and continues to delight his visitors with the theme of "Che Guevara." This place is appreciated for the fact that it never changes its traditions and continues to keep the brand. Havana's style makes the Cuba Libre club different from its competitors.
  2. "Crystal" - a network night club. Rostov-on-Don and other cities are endowed with this institution for entertainment. A feature of this place are themed parties that take place here every weekend. There are no barriers - you can relax with your soul and body.
  3. "Honey" is a successful project, which is located in the city center and is equipped with a black and pink interior. The presence of VIP rooms only adds points to the rating of this institution. And one more thing: it works all week and is distinguished by its diverse program.
  4. Bavaria is a club that is designed for both youth and clubbers of mature age. The music here is very diverse: from the hits of the nineties to the most popular and new.
  5. "Tesla" is a vacation spot that works in various directions. It is a leader in the quality of parties and the number of visitors.
  6. "Paradox" - an institution that is worth a visit to music lovers in completely different directions (that is, music lovers). There is both rap and pop music, as well as deep house. The parties are accompanied by charming go-go dancers.




Top karaoke clubs in Rostov-on-Don

Rostov-on-Don nightclubs, the list of which is almost endless, offer a service such as karaoke. It is intended for those who want to not only become a star on the dance floor, but also to demonstrate their vocal abilities. Popular places with karaoke include:

  1. "A-Major" - the most pathos karaoke club, which is known for its glamorous audience and high prices. But despite this, it is worth visiting at least once.
  2. Persona Grata is the second most popular night club. Rostov-on-Don is famous for karaoke, and this institution is equipped with everything necessary to have fun. The playlist here has nearly one hundred thousand songs. In addition to singing, the club hosts various theme parties.
  3. "Cliffs" is known for its unusual interior and professional acoustics. Karaoke lovers claim that singing here is a pleasure.
  4. Sing & Smog hosts the loudest and most colorful parties. The dance floor seats one hundred and fifty people, and the banquet hall seats seventy guests. There is also a summer platform from which you can watch performances by artists, participate in competitions and other show programs.
  5. Enigma is a stylish place for those who want to recharge with drive, positive and distract from boring everyday life. The night club will bring you joy and fun into the atmosphere.
  6. FaSol is equipped with special equipment that will make singing even better. Do not ignore the large dance floor and delicious cocktails.
  7. Addison is a place for people who are twenty-one years old. There is strict face control, which is only a plus for visitors to the institution.

Of course, these are not all the clubs in Rostov-on-Don, but only the most top-rated and most famous.
