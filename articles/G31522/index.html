Resort Rio 5 * (India, Goa): description, hotel reviews

If you plan to relax in Goa (India) and want to stay in a comfortable hotel with excellent service, then as an option for your stay we suggest you consider the five-star Resort Rio.



Location, general description, photo

Resort Rio 5 * is located on the north coast of Goa. The nearest Baga beach is three kilometers away. You can get here absolutely for free using the shuttle provided by the hotel. Dabolim International Airport is 47 kilometers away.

Resort Rio consists of a five-story building and several two-story villas. In total, the hotel has 239 rooms of standard and deluxe categories. All are decorated in a modern Indian style and equipped with the necessary furniture and appliances.

The hotel has a large swimming pool with a children's section, a sun terrace, spa, gym and sports grounds. The hotel has several restaurants (buffet and √† la carte) and two bars.

Housing stock

The rooms of the Resort Rio 5 * hotel (Goa, India), our compatriots, judging by their reviews, were quite satisfied. Moreover, this applies to both guests staying in apartments of the standard category, and those who prefer a more expensive accommodation. According to tourists, all rooms are quite spacious, very nicely decorated, with comfortable high-quality furniture and properly functioning appliances. If something fails, then report it to the reception, and the problem will be solved as soon as possible by the hotel staff. In their reviews, travelers mention the quality bath amenities provided by the hotel as pluses. Also, the guests were very pleased with the fact that in all rooms, as well as in the rest of the Resort Rio, there is free Wi-Fi. Moreover, the connection speed is good enough everywhere. So you can always be in touch with your friends and relatives and share with them your impressions about the rest and photos.



Cleaning

Our compatriots practically had no complaints about the work of the hotel maids. According to them, the cleaning was carried out every day and was carried out quite qualitatively. Towels were also updated daily, stocks of drinks in the minibar, tea and coffee making facilities and personal hygiene items in the bathroom were replenished. In addition, maids constantly add bottled drinking water at the rate of half a liter per person. Bed linen was changed several times a week. If you find that for some reason the maid did not clean the floor or forgot to put something for you, tell her about it at the meeting or inform the reception.



Service

The guests of the Resort Rio 5 * hotel (India, Goa), according to them, were pleasantly surprised by the attention to them from the staff. So, the guests note that all the staff here are always friendly, helpful, smiling. The hotel staff will always open the door for you, will be happy to answer any question and fulfill any request. However, please note that almost no one speaks Russian here, with the exception of one of the administrators at the reception. Therefore, you need to recall a little the English curriculum.

A pleasant addition to the relaxation of some guests of the Resort Rio was the fact of receiving compliments from the hotel. So, those guests can count on them for the duration of their stay at the hotel their birthday, wedding anniversary, as well as those tourists who plan to spend their honeymoon here. Special attention is also given to regular customers of the hotel. As for the compliments themselves, they usually include a delicious cake, a basket of fruit and a bottle of good wine or champagne.

Check in, departure

According to our compatriots, the Resort Rio 5 * hotel in most cases adheres to the checkout time. Therefore, if you arrive at the hotel in the morning, then be prepared for the fact that you will receive the keys to your room only after two in the afternoon. However, this does not mean that you have to spend several hours in the lobby near the reception. So, you can leave your things in the luggage room (then the employees of the Resort Rio will transfer them to your apartment) and begin to relax, going to the pool, to a restaurant or directly to the beach.

As for the day of departure, the occupied room should be vacated before noon. If you plan to leave the hotel only in the evening, then there is the opportunity to extend your stay for several hours at an additional cost. However, it is advisable to notify the administrator at the reception in advance of such an intention so that there are no misunderstandings with the settlement of new guests.



Location

Despite the fact that the Resort Rio 5 * hotel (Goa, India) is located at some distance from the coast, most tourists found its location very successful. So, according to them, the hotel is in a quiet and peaceful place, away from the busy roadway. In the vicinity of Resort Rio there are a lot of shops, cafes, excursion bureaus, car rental points, etc. You can take a taxi or tuk-tuk to any place from here.



Nutrition

This hotel offers guests a choice of several options for which meals will be provided. So, guests can only pay for breakfast, half board or the all-inclusive concept. However, experienced travelers advise all tourists going to Goa to stay on the option that involves only breakfast. Indeed, in this case, you will not be attached to the hotel, but you can safely explore the sights of an amazing country, visit any beaches, try new dishes in various cafes and restaurants, etc.

As for the quality of food at Resort Rio, on the whole it satisfied our compatriots. True, some of the guests who, for an additional fee, had lunch and dinner several times at the hotel, claim that the prices here are, in their opinion, too high. Therefore, they whenever possible preferred to eat outside the hotel, especially since there are a lot of cafes in its vicinity.
