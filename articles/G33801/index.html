How to make a crocodile costume

Simple suit



The easiest way to collect your son for a matinee is to make him a paper outfit. A crocodile costume can consist of only one part, namely a headdress, which will be both a mask and a tail. To produce such an attribute as follows. Cut out two long strips of green paper. One of them needs to be looped. This will be a mount around the head. It is possible to impart rigidity to the structure due to an additional rib. It must be glued across the rim. Now you should make a crocodile face. Cut a rectangle out of paper and round it on one side. The second edge should be attached to the headgear. Now glue nostrils of black paper and eyes onto the face. It remains to cut the teeth-triangles. They need to be glued perpendicular to the rectangle. The next step is the manufacture of the tail. Cut a wide rectangle of green paper. Now the edges need to be zigzag. Be sure to make sure that the triangles are symmetrically located relative to the right and left sides. Finish the tail with one triangle located in the middle. We attach the blank to the headdress. The crocodile costume is ready. To add expressiveness to the look, you can add green pants and a turtleneck in the same color.

Egyptian crocodile



Such an image will be original and will definitely help your child stand out from the crowd. The crocodile costume will consist of a mask and a tail. All this can be done from cardboard and brown paper. We find a box that fits the size, cut a hole under the head below. Now you need to make a mouth. We cut a hole in the lateral parts, which should resemble a waveform. From the white cardboard we cut out the teeth-triangles and fasten them into a ready-made mouth. From yellow paper you need to make ovals. Glue them on the top edge of the box and draw a pupil inside the eye. It remains to cut the corner of the box, which remained at the back of the head. The resulting hole, of course, must be sealed. We turn to the manufacture of the tail. We form a cone from paper and stuff it with newspapers or padding polyester. It is best to dress a child in all white, in extreme cases - in beige. We attach the tail to the pants, we also need a mask on the head.

Sophisticated costume



Such an outfit can be built for an important event. Crocodile costume will be made of felt. To do this, cut a long rectangle from green material. Its front side must be rounded. We retreat from this edge of 60 cm and cut a hole under the head. Sew a rectangle around the edges. The main decor will be located on the back. It should immediately form a tail from the bottom edge of the rectangle. To do this, put the blank on the child, fold a cone from the lower edges. In this position, the felt needs to be fixed. If desired, the tail can be stuffed with synthetic winterizer. Throughout the back, from the throat to the formed cone, two ribbons need to be sewn. How to arrange them, you can see the picture attached above. On the sides of the ribbons you need to sew on triangles of light green color. They should also be continued on the tail. But the rectangle that the ribbons form should be filled with randomly cut waves. You need to decorate the stomach with the same decor. A green cap, to which eyes made of two green balls are sewn, can complement the suit. You can also make paws from felt, which with rubber bands should be fixed on the arms and legs of the child.

Crocodile Costume Genes



Such an outfit may be required by the animator for a children's party. It is very easy to assemble a crocodile costume with your own hands. You need to find green pants and a jacket to match them. It will be interesting to look if the lapels of the collar and trouser legs are decorated with squares or circles. A white shirt and a yellow tie should be worn under the jacket. Hands should be worn in green gloves. The most difficult thing is to make a crocodile mask of Gena. You can make it from polyurethane foam or a piece of foam. We make a rectangular blank. Now you should give it the shape of a crocodile face. This is most conveniently done with the help of a clerical knife. The finished mask should be painted with gouache, and if possible, then acrylic using an airbrush. Be sure to complete the image by wearing a brown hat on the head of the crocodile.
