Zarinsk hotels: description and reviews

Zarinsk is a city in the Altai Territory on the Chumysh River. The settlement is relatively young, it was founded in 1979 by the merger of the village of Sorokin and the village of Zarinsky. Zarinsk is an industrial city where chemical products are actively produced and exported. There are also some interesting sights here. If you plan to visit Zarinsk, the hotels of the city will provide you comfort and coziness.

What to see in Zarinsk

Zarinsk is a small industrial town. Nevertheless, there is something to see in it.

Here are the main attractions:

  • Inter-settlement museum of local lore and museum of tourism. Archeological finds, objects of peasant life, documents, works of local artists, etc. are exhibited here.
  • Memorial of Glory. The memorial complex dedicated to the locals who died during the Second World War.
  • Ascension Church. Cathedral in a picturesque area, built in 1990.
  • Cave temple complex. It was dug by monks in the 19th century. The total length of the corridors is 250 meters.
  • Salair Ridge. An arched mountain range (up to 500 meters in height) that separates the Chumysh and Tom rivers.
