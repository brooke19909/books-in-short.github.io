Cesars Temple De Luxe Hotel 5 *, Belek: reviews and photos of tourists

If you prefer a comfortable stay and the resort Belek (Turkey) was chosen as the place for spending your vacation, Cesars Temple De Luxe Hotel 5 * may be a suitable option for living.



Location

The five-star Cesars Temple De Luxe hotel is located in a picturesque place surrounded by pine forests on the very seashore, five kilometers from the center of the spa town of Belek. Antalya, the tourist capital of Turkey, is 45 kilometers away. The nearest airport is 35 kilometers away. However, as a rule, travelers take about one and a half hours to travel from the air harbor to the hotel. Indeed, on the way, in most cases, the bus brings tourists to other hotels.

Turkey, Belek, Cesars Temple De Luxe Hotel: general information, photo

This hotel was built in 1995. Since then, renovation has been carried out several times here. The hotel complex occupies a very impressive territory of 200 thousand square meters. It houses the main four-story building, forty two- and three-story villas, as well as several restaurants, bars, sports grounds, a kids club, swimming pools and much more. This hotel is perfect for families with children, as well as for spending time with young people and older tourists.



Rooms

The housing stock of the hotel is represented by 599 apartments of the following categories: standard, family, royal suite and economy. There are also six rooms, specially equipped for the comfortable accommodation of people with disabilities. When booking accommodation, you can choose the view from the window - the garden, pool or sea. Regardless of the category, all rooms have a pleasant design and are equipped with the necessary items for a comfortable stay. So, here there is comfortable furniture, a TV, a telephone, a minibar, air conditioning, a bathroom, a hairdryer, a balcony or terrace. Caesars Temple provides 24-hour room service.

Housing stock

According to our compatriots, the Cesars Temple De Luxe Hotel 5 * hotel, reviews of which we are considering, was planned and built very high quality and thoughtfully. Therefore, rooms of all categories here are cozy, comfortable. Even standard apartments with a small area are equipped with comfortable large beds and modern appliances. Almost none of the guests complained about breakdowns. But even if there was some kind of malfunction, it was enough to contact the reception, and the problem was solved as soon as possible. Check in
