Kokshetau hotels: description and reviews

Kokshetau is a city in the Akmola region of Kazakhstan. The village is located on the shore of Lake Kopa and is surrounded by the picturesque foothills of the Kokshetau Upland. The city is famous for its interesting history and rich cultural life. If you plan to visit Kokshetau, the hotels of the village will provide you with a warm welcome.

Hotel "Dostyk"

One of the most popular accommodation options in Kokshetau is the Dostyk Hotel. The institution is located at: Abaya street, 69, which is just 650 meters from the picturesque Lake Kopa and a 13-minute walk from the central part of the city. Nearby is a park, shopping complex, cinema, drama theater and university.

  • single standard - from 2369 rubles.

By choosing this hotel for accommodation, you can take advantage of the following advantages:

  • sauna;

  • room service;
  • conference hall;
  • banqueting hall;

Reviews about the hotel "Dostyk"

You can hear a number of positive comments about this hotel in Kokshetau:

  • good location in the city center near the main infrastructure facilities;
  • friendly and helpful staff;
  • optimal ratio of price and quality of services provided;
  • a good breakfast is included in the price;
  • on each floor there is a cooler with drinking water;
  • in the cold season, rooms are perfectly heated;
  • excellent cuisine in the restaurant.

And also some notes:

  • if the windows overlook the area, it will be quite noisy;
  • insufficiently thorough cleaning;
  • clogged drain and the smell of sewage in the bathroom;
  • it is cold in the corner rooms; an additional heater is required;
  • outdated interior rooms;
  • old TVs with poor picture and sound quality.
