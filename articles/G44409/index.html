Decide which is better - Nikon or Canon

Choosing a camera is a very complicated process, and when it comes to deciding which is better - Nikon or Canon, then there are even more problems. Both manufacturers produce solid and reliable devices that can provide not only amazing quality of images, but also similar capabilities in many respects. It is worth saying that there are a huge number of similarities between the two brands, which is why for many there is not even a question of which is better, Nikon or Canon, since they do not see the difference. In skilled hands, each of them can get excellent results, but at the same time they still have certain differences. They will influence your final choice.



If you compare Nikon and Canon, then you should first consider some points that relate to each of the manufacturers. First, consider the merits of representatives of the first brand. It is customary to highlight the excellent quality of images that can be obtained in low light conditions. Representatives of the second brand in this regard lose quite significantly. Most Nikon cameras have a much larger number of autofocus points than canon counterparts. Quite often, due to the lack of autofocus points, the photographer is forced not only to change the points, but also the composition as a whole, since it cannot capture the object you like. Canon is superior to Nikon in terms of flash control. There is an opinion that the Nikon technique is more user-friendly, that is, it has a lot of amenities, as well as a number of very pleasant things.



When deciding which is better - Nikon or Canon, it is worth considering the advantages of the second manufacturer. Here it is customary to note the possibility of shooting high-quality video. The latest Nikon models also have such an opportunity, but the required level of quality is still beyond their reach. It has long been the case that cameras from the second manufacturer are much more affordable. If you consider a Nikon or Canon camera with similar capabilities, the second will be 8-10% cheaper.



For many photographers, such an indicator as the number of megapixels is important, since with its large value it will be much more convenient to work with the image in the future. Usually Canon in this plan is ahead of Nikon. It should be noted the availability of lenses and cameras produced under each brand. All modern Canon cameras are equipped with a built-in motor, and Nikon usually do not have it or the user is required to slightly overpay for the presence of such an option. Experienced users note the fact that for Canon cameras it is much easier to purchase special adapters that allow you to connect the lens from Soviet technology. Experienced photographers often have a similar need, because their lenses have been stored for a long time.
