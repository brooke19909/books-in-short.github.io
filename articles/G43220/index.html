How to weave vegetables and fruits from rubber bands: a detailed description of weaving on a slingshot

If you decide to learn such a hobby, stock up on materials and patience. You need to start with a simple one. For weaving you will need:

  • a slingshot with horns or a frame with a hook;
  • multi-colored elastic bands;
  • fasteners.


With the help of a plastic slingshot, work is simpler and faster. However, a more advanced and difficult tool is sold - a frame with a hook. It produces more complex options.

Materials can easily be purchased at a store selling office supplies or at specialized points for sewing and needlework.

If you decide to use the easier option, a slingshot with horns will already be attached there. There are also 2-way with 4 horns and completely different in size.

Weaving techniques

When buying a particular kit, you can choose a color that you like more than others. If you want to learn weaving from gum, fruits and vegetables are best made from red, purple, green, orange. They will suit you better than others. If these are simple figures, then you can use beige, black, white and so on. In addition, on sale you can find striped and neon colors.

Weaving can be done using the following techniques:

  • "fish tail";
  • "caterpillar;
  • "iris";
  • Dragon Scales
  • "French braid";
  • spider.

For example, the first method, called the "fish tail", allows you to make a massive tourniquet. It can be performed from 50 gum. In order to make work in the technique of "caterpillar", you need a machine with 4 columns. For the "iris" you will need a fork, and for the technique of "dragon scales" - a hook and a machine with 1 row of posts. For a "spider" is enough machine with 3 columns. So you can weave not only bracelets, but also fruits and vegetables from gum on a slingshot.
