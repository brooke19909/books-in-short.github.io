Hotel Arinara Bangtao Beach Resort 4 * (Thailand, Phuket): photos and reviews of tourists

For those who decided to go with their whole family on vacation to Thailand, we want to talk about the Arinara Bangtao Beach Resort 4 * hotel. The complex, located next to the beautiful beach of fine white sand, will not leave anyone indifferent.

Arinara Bangtao Beach Resort 4 *: hotel description

The hotel is located on the west coast of the famous Phuket. The family complex offers its guests everything you need for a perfect rest: from stylish apartments to delicious food. The hospitable staff tries to do everything to make tourists feel at home.



Arinara Bangtao Beach Resort 4 * hotel is a half-hour drive from Phuket. The administration of the complex offers a large selection of rooms for every taste, so that all guests can choose a suitable option. All apartments are decorated in the style of Southeast Asia and have direct access to the pool with sea views.

Number of rooms

Arinara Bangtao Beach Resort 4 * hotel offers a wide choice of apartments. The complex itself is a series of modern small buildings surrounded by a pool, overlooking the gardens and the sea. In the interior design of the rooms, the designers adhered to a minimalist style, the furniture was made in the best Asian traditions.

All apartments are air-conditioned, have a balcony, minibar, TV, wireless Internet, safe, coffee and tea sets, toiletries, hairdryer and much more.

The hotel has the following room types:

  1. Deluxe - luxury apartments with two separate double beds. This room is good for a large family. This room has a private terrace and a mini fridge.
  2. Deluxe Pool View allows you to enjoy a wonderful view from the second floor. The apartments are divided into two zones, which makes it possible for all family members to have their own space. The room is equipped with two large beds.
  3. Pool Access. These apartments have their own access to the pool and are located on the ground floor. The interior is made in the traditional style of Asia. The room is divided into two zones, each of which has its own double bed.
  4. Premium Studio Pool View. The apartment is located on the third floor and has a balcony overlooking the pool and hotel grounds. The room has a seating area.
  5. Premium Studio.
  6. Junior Suite is a spacious and elegant junior suite with a living room (there is a sofa bed) and a bedroom.
  7. Arinara Suite is a one-room suite with beautiful sea views. This room has a separate bedroom, a living room with a kitchenette and a dressing room. The apartments are equipped with bath and shower. This room is perfect for a romantic getaway.

Hotel infrastructure

Arinara Bangtao Beach Resort 4 * boasts the best pool in Phuket, around which there is a relaxation area. There is also a water slide.

The hotel has two bars, a laundry room, safes, three restaurants, a billiard room, and a fitness room.

Dining at the hotel

Arinara Bangtao Beach Resort 4 * operates on an all-inclusive basis. But if desired, tourists can only take breakfast.

Meals include European and Chinese dishes. For breakfast, for example, omelettes, fried eggs, sausages, bacon, fried noodles are served. Waffles and pancakes are baked daily, and there are a lot of fruits on the tables. The cocktails are made somehow in their own way, obviously not adding alcohol, but still it turns out delicious.



Food is always enough, it is fresh and tasty. Those who are going to Phuket for the first time should not even worry about food, having heard the stories that they serve only beetles and worms. This is utter nonsense. In all restaurants and hotels the food is normal and does not differ from the standard set of any resort.

Not far from Arinara Bangtao Beach Resort 4 * (Phuket) there are local restaurants where you can have a tasty and inexpensive dinner and lunch. Here you can find dishes for every taste. There are even Russian establishments where you can order kebabs and okroshka.

The main restaurant of the Arinara Bangtao Beach Resort 4 * hotel (Phuket) offers many wonderful dishes. In the evenings, live music is played here, theme nights are organized for guests.

Rim Hama (Restaurant) serves Thai specialties with many spices. This establishment works from lunch until late at night, but the most romantic atmosphere is observed in the evening, when special lighting is turned on.

The Drift Beach Bar is in the open. Sitting on the sofa, you can enjoy the ocean. Here, at any time you will be treated to delicious cocktails.

"Brazier" - a restaurant that prepares for visitors a lot of seafood, steaks and other variations of the barbecue. This place is well suited not only for a romantic evening, but also for a friendly party.

The poolside Dip pool Bar serves snacks and cocktails.

Entertainment

In the Arinara Bangtao Beach Resort 4 * hotel (photos are given in the article), animators offer vacationers to do aerobics, gymnastics, water polo. The amphitheater organizes shows and discos in the evening.



In addition, tourists can play darts, table tennis, billiards, beach volleyball, futsal, as well as visit the sauna, Turkish hammam and gym. All of the above services are included in the price. For a fee, vacationers can use the services of a car rental, tennis court, hairdresser, visit the SPA center, take diving courses.

Hotel beach

Hotel Arinara Bangtao Beach Resort 4 * (photo gallery is given in the article) does not have its own beach. Right next to the complex is a city beach with beautiful fine sand. Bang Tao Beach is one of the longest beaches in Phuket, its length is more than seven kilometers. It is on its territory that there are hotels located on the first line. There is no road between the Arinara Bangtao Beach Resort 4 * hotel (Thailand, Phuket) and the coast, which is very convenient. From the complex you immediately get to the beach. On the shore there is a lot of space and spacious, few people. Sun beds and umbrellas are paid (200 baht).

The bathing approach is gentle and comfortable, the water is warm, but often muddy and with algae. The beach is pretty clean, but algae are rarely cleaned. However, this is not the hotel's fault, since the coast is municipal.

Nearby there is the beach of a neighboring hotel, there are practically no people on it in the daytime, so those who seek solitude can safely take advantage of it.



Hotel Arinara Bangtao Beach Resort 4 * boasts a pool with various exotic fish, which children love to feed. Such entertainment does not bother them.

Travel Tips

Those who chose Phuket and Arinara Bangtao Beach Resort 4 * (Thailand) as their vacation destination should definitely think about what sights are worth seeing. Phuket is a rather large island, there are many interesting places and attractions. These are natural beauties - mountains, waterfalls, bays, wild jungles and nature reserves - and cultural, historical monuments - museums, temples. Any tourist will be able to find something interesting for himself.

But how to get to the sights? There are only two options: you need to rent a transport, call a taxi or buy an excursion. You should not rely on public transport, since it is poorly developed on the island. You will simply lose a lot of time and will not see anything. It‚Äôs quite convenient to purchase sightseeing tours, which are distributed by many agencies, and go on a trip as part of a group. In addition, you need to understand that many attractions are not located on the island itself, but on the mainland or the nearest islands. It is easier to get to such places by an excursion group.

Phuket Attractions

The Talang Museum (national) will tell tourists about the history of Thailand and its modern realities. Six rooms are dedicated to various periods in the life of the country.

No less interesting is the museum of sea shells, which contains an incredible number of all kinds of shells, more than twenty thousand.

The statue of the Big Buddha at an altitude of more than 370 meters is especially popular among tourists. This attraction is still under construction, but there are always a lot of people around it. The height of the statue is forty-five meters, it is perfectly visible from the southern beaches. At the moment, this is the most popular and visited local attraction, which is certainly worth a visit. Near the statue there is a temple, an observation deck, which offers a beautiful landscape of the island. The size of the statue itself and breathtaking views are very impressive.

Chalong is the temple complex, the most important on the whole island. It is a place of pilgrimage for Buddhists. Here is a large shrine - the bone of the Buddha. It is located in the sarcophagus and is available for viewing by people. Once a year, a huge fair is held in the temple.



Near the beach of Kata Noi there is an observation deck Karon. It offers a unique view of three large beaches: Kata Noi, Kata and Karon. Numerous guests come here to meet the sunset, because it is here that the sun hides beyond the horizon.

Khao Phra Thaeo National Park is a nature reserve that preserves the wild jungle. Here in natural wild conditions live gibbons, wild boars, monkeys, bears, more than a hundred species of birds. The park has the largest waterfall on the island.

It is believed that while relaxing on the island, you definitely need to visit the Similian Islands. They are located off the coast of Thailand, in the Andaman Sea. All nine islands are included in the national park of the country. They are called a paradise. Experts say that here are the best places for diving and the best beaches.

Younger tourists will love the Phuket Zoo. He is really amazing, only tickets to him are expensive. Here you can see crocodiles, butterflies, birds. A special delight in people is the park of beautiful orchids and an aquarium. In the zoo you can not only admire wild animals, but also watch shows with their participation. During such programs, animals do amazing things. All this is worth a look.

In the aquarium of Phuket, you can see hundreds of inhabitants of the deep sea. And the butterfly garden will simply amaze you and your children with an incredible amount of insects. The park is recognized as one of the best in the world. More than forty species of butterflies and many bugs, grasshoppers, bees, dragonflies, flies live here.

But the botanical garden is a new place that was opened in 2011. Here, under the open sky, many tropical plants and animals are collected. There is also an orchid park, theme gardens, palm grove, orchards, cacti, exotic butterflies.



There are several bird parks on the island, which are home to more than a thousand species of birds brought from different continents.

The famous Phuket Fantasy Park is one of the most important entertainment complexes. It is definitely a must visit. This is a small Thai city in which its own life flows, has its own culture and history. Bright events are held here daily. We can say that this is a family attraction filled with a variety of games, shops, carousels with Thai motifs.

The vast territory is divided into three parts. The first zone hosts half-hour shows for adults and children. In the second there is a restaurant. And in the third part is the "Palace of the Elephants." The park is somewhat reminiscent of Disneyland, only it is made on the basis of Thai culture.

We have listed only some of the attractions that are in Phuket, in fact there are many more. For one trip, do not review everyone.
