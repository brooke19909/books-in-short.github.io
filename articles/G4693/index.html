Jo An Beach Hotel 4 * (Greece / Crete / Rethymnon): reviews

Holidays in Greece are a combination of a warm and clean sea with a rich excursion program, because this country is primarily famous for its vast cultural heritage. Crete is traditionally considered the birthplace of Zeus. It is usually chosen by tourists who want to plunge into the history of Greece, but at the same time have a budget vacation at sea. Many hotels have been built along the coast of the island. One of the popular options for accommodation is a small but cozy complex Jo An Beach Hotel 4 * in Crete.

basic information

This hotel is usually chosen by tourists who want to stay near the sea. Jo An Beach Hotel Apart 4 * appeared in 1983, but the buildings are undergoing regular cosmetic repairs. True, counting on a new interior in the rooms is still not worth it, since their last renovation was carried out in 2008.

The room stock of the complex consists of 90 rooms. They cannot be accommodated with pets. Facilities for people with disabilities are also not provided.

Location Details

According to tourists, the main advantage of the complex Jo An Beach Hotel 4 * is its favorable location. Often in Greece, budget hotels are built away from the sea, but this hotel is located directly on its own sand and pebble stretch of the coast. From the residential building to the beach you can walk in just 2-3 minutes. At the same time, there is no road between the hotel and the coast, which was positively appreciated by tourists with young children.

The complex is located in the quiet village of Adele, so it is suitable for travelers who prefer to relax and relax. However, if you wish, you can quickly get to large settlements. For example, the city of Rethymnon is only 7 km away. It is easier and cheaper to get to it by bus. For the convenience of tourists, a public transport stop is located just 50 meters from the hotel.

The nearest airport is Heraklion, the capital of Crete. It is approximately 72 km from the hotel. The entire trip to the property after arrival will take about two hours. If desired, guests of the complex can pre-pay a round-trip transfer.

Number of rooms Jo-An Beach Hotel 4 * in Greece

In their reviews, tourists indicate that the hotel will not be able to surprise with luxurious and expensive room interiors. All living rooms here are simply but tastefully decorated. Moreover, they are equipped with a host of additional amenities so that tourists feel comfortable throughout their stay. In total, the hotel Jo An Beach Hotel 4 * (Adele, Rethymno) offers 90 rooms. Most of them are standard, such rooms are designed to accommodate two adults. Large families can stay in special family apartments. The maximum number of people living in them is 6 adults. The hotel also has two-level apartments, consisting of two separate bedrooms and a living room.

Each room has its own private bathroom equipped with a shower. Free of charge, each guest is provided with a bathrobe and slippers, as well as a set of towels and hygiene items. In the bathroom, tourists can use a hairdryer and a large cosmetic mirror.

Living rooms are also equipped with everything necessary. In them, tourists can find beds, comfortable sofas, televisions, desks, wardrobes and dining sets. A clothes dryer is located on the open balcony. The room must have air conditioning, refrigerator and telephone. You can also ask for free utensils for making hot drinks. For a fee, a safety deposit box is provided.

Rooms are cleaned daily. Sheets and towels are usually changed at least twice a week.

Catering

The cost of accommodation in the hotel Jo An Beach Hotel 4 *, as a rule, includes food. Most often, tourists prefer to pay for the All Inclusive system. It includes three meals a day, as well as unlimited snacks, soft drinks and spirits, including homemade wine, beer, brandy.

All meals are served in the main restaurant. Guests can also enjoy a meal and a coffee in the lobby bar. Refreshing drinks are served at the outdoor pool bar. If you are going on a long trip, you can ask the administrator to collect your lunch box. But you need to do this 1-2 days before departure.

Infrastructure Details

Hotel Jo An Beach Hotel 4 * does not provide guests with a wide selection of services. Car parking is located on its territory, where tourists can leave their car for free. The reception is open 24 hours a day. It will help tourists exchange currency, rent a car, take their things to the laundry room or connect wireless Internet. Almost all additional services at the hotel are provided only for a fee. Upon request, tourists can ask the administrator for an iron and ironing board in the room.

Beach and sea

A significant advantage of the hotel Jo An Beach Hotel 4 * is its own beach. The sand here is mixed with pebbles, so you can‚Äôt walk barefoot on it, otherwise you can cut yourself. Therefore, it is worth buying special beach shoes in advance. The coast is fully equipped for a comfortable stay, sun loungers and parasols are installed here, but their rent is available for a fee. Tourists can also use showers and toilets. The entrance to the sea cannot be called smooth, at the bottom lies a natural stone slab, so children should swim in special shoes under the close supervision of their parents.

Tourists can relax near the outdoor pool, located on site. It is filled with fresh water. Next to it is a sun terrace with sun loungers.

Other entertainment options

And although the complex is focused exclusively on beach vacations, there are other entertainments here. Guests at the hotel can play billiards or volleyball in the sand. A water entertainment center is 100 meters away. There tourists can rent a non-motorized or motor boat, water skiing or a catamaran. All center services are not included in the price and are paid separately.

But the animation team in the hotel is not even in the summer season. The mini-club for small children does not work here, so tourists often have to plan their leisure on their own.

Conditions for accommodating small children

Due to its convenient location and low price, this hotel is often chosen by tourists with children. When buying a ticket, parents can expect discounts if their child is under 12 years old. For infants, a cradle is provided free of charge in the room. But their number in the hotel is limited, so you need to warn staff in advance that you would like to receive it. The restaurant is also free to take a highchair. You can call a nanny in the room, but her services will be paid separately.

There are few entertainments for children at the hotel. A shallow compartment in the outdoor pool is allocated for them. It is filled with fresh water and does not heat up. The hotel has a playground on the synthetic surface. Children can play it for free.

Reviews about Jo-An Beach Hotel 4 *

This hotel has a good reputation because tourists often respond positively about it. Although they note in their reviews that he also has shortcomings, unfortunately.

Most of all, tourists liked the location of the hotel. It is located in a lively, but fairly quiet area, where at night you can sleep peacefully with the window open. Near the hotel there is a beach that can be reached quickly. Conveniently, there is a bus setting nearby. Tourists appreciated the local quality of service. In the reviews, they indicate that the hotel staff speaks good English and always try to please the guests, quickly fulfilling their requests. The food in the restaurant is varied. Be sure to serve meat and fish dishes, and on the table there are always seasonal vegetables and fruits. In addition, several supermarkets are opened near the hotel, so you can buy the necessary products yourself if you wish. The beach at the hotel is clean and not crowded with other vacationers. Even at rush hour you can always find yourself a free sun lounger.

But there are also disadvantages. In their reviews, tourists indicate that the hotel Jo An Beach Hotel 4 * in Rethymnon has a very small territory, so sometimes it is too crowded on the street. For all equipment, employees upon check-in require a deposit, even for the remote control from the TV. Hot water in the bathroom is intermittent, and towels do not always change on time. In addition, the rooms themselves have long been redecorating.
