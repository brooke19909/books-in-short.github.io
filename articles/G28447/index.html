Where to eat in Yaroslavl is tasty and inexpensive? Photos and reviews

Yaroslavl is one of the ancient cities of Russia. Its history goes back over 1000 years. Here is a huge number of monuments of architecture and art. Moreover, the city center is included in the list of cultural monuments, which are under the special protection of UNESCO.



Every year, tens of thousands of tourists come here. But sooner or later, each of them raises the question: where to eat in Yaroslavl? Focusing on the feedback of residents and visitors, we will tell you about the most famous, inexpensive establishments of the city. Where they are? Where can I go in the city to have fun?

Where to eat in Yaroslavl

In such a big city, there will be no problems with this issue. Here is a huge number of cafes, restaurants, canteens, eateries and other places where you can eat. If you are looking for where to eat in Yaroslavl, use the following tips.

  • In cafe-pizzerias, prices are low, while the assortment is the most diverse, in addition, you can order delivery of ready-made meals to your home.
  • A large number of dining rooms will help solve the problem of where to eat cheaply in Yaroslavl. Here you will be fed very quickly and economically.

City Restaurants

Among the large number, there are those in which prices are quite reasonable, and the quality of service is very high.

Things to do in Yaroslavl

From a wide selection of options we offer you the following:

  1. Interactive museum for children and adults "Aleshino Compound". Russian heroes will tell you about the traditions of the distant past, help make a protective amulet and give a cheerful mood.
  2. Yaroslavl Zoo is considered not only one of the best in Russia, but also in Europe. For animals created conditions as close as possible to their environment. Children will be delighted with the opportunity to stroke some animals: goats, hens and others. There is a contact zoo.
  3. Visit the planetarium and you will have a unique opportunity to see the beauty of the starry sky. The sensations are indescribable, as if you are moving through outer space. This is due to the special technologies used in the planetarium.
  4. Yaroslavl Chamber Theater will bring great pleasure to fans of theatrical art. Tickets must be bought in advance, as city residents love to come here.
