T-shirt remodeling is an easy way to get a stylish new thing.

Women are great craftswomen and inventors. They are very fond of showing off in new clothes and every time they are upset if they do not find the opportunity to purchase a new wardrobe item. However, among the fair sex, there is another category of lovely ladies - restless needlewomen. They are from old things capable of reviving fashionable and interesting new things.

Simple art

Remaking T-shirts is an art that more and more lovely women and girls are gradually mastering. Even teenage girls know how to create something stylish, interesting from a boring knitted little thing. T-shirt is a material convenient in operation and encouraging creation and inspiration.

Worn T-shirts and T-shirts are designed to turn into household rags, with which they wash windows, doors, furniture and utensils, but with a competent approach, an old, boring thing can sparkle with new colors, and delight its mistress with an updated look.

Practical and stylish

With the redesign of T-shirts, women save the budget and get the necessary and unique clothing models, practical gizmos. Of the previously used T-shirts and t-shirts, needlewomen create the following masterpieces:

  • new styles of t-shirts;
  • original and elegant blouses, sundresses;
  • pillowcases
  • cover;
  • bedside rugs.

New models

Remaking from an old T-shirt will help the young girl get a new item in which she can flaunt another season. There are some very simple ways to update a boring thing, and more complicated options.

The following methods are simple alterations of t-shirts:

  1. At a t-shirt sleeves and a neck are cut off. In the middle of the face sheet, an even incision is made to the level of the chest and is tied to a knot. Summer light thing is ready.
  2. A deep neckline is made. Cropped edges are bent by 1.5 cm and hemmed. Elastic band stretches through the hemmed "tunnel". An elegant T-shirt with bare shoulders will appeal to a romantic person.
  3. A coquette girl will like this option: cut a deep semicircle on her back. Stitch a wide ribbon to the neck of the shirt, which is tied at the back. The edges of the ribbon, falling to the bare back, make the image summer and unusual.
  4. In another embodiment, the back of the T-shirt is cut in even horizontal lines. Cheeky, easy, beautiful.
  5. This alteration of the T-shirt allows you to create a nice blouse, in which you are not ashamed to go for a walk, or have a cup of coffee in a nearby coffee shop. A large triangle is cut out on the back of the shirt, from the bottom to the neck. Instead, a lace triangle is sewn. A lace bow will decorate the neck. You can replace the triangle with a neat big heart.

Without scissors and sewing

Alteration of T-shirts with their own hands is possible without cutting. An old product can be changed by other means:

  1. Make an inscription on the fabric. To do this, you will need fabric paint, a T-shirt, a tight bag and a stationery knife. First, we make the inscription on the bag, cut out the letters and put the template on the T-shirt. We apply paint on top, according to the rules specified in the instructions. Hold until completely dry and remove the bag. A new phrase or word is ripening on a T-shirt.
  2. A half-painted item looks stylish and unusual. It is necessary to take a white or very light plain t-shirt, dilute the paint in a container. Dip part of the product in a bowl, hold for as long as indicated on the package, dry. For long-term use do not wash in hot water.

New bed

Remaking T-shirts is a practical skill that will allow you to create stylish pillowcases and a duvet cover. Sewing them is very easy.

The same squares are cut out of unnecessary t-shirts and stitched together. It is better to give preference to those areas of clothing that have inscriptions or drawings. The result will be bright and unusual.
