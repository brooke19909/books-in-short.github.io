Where to go from Moscow for the weekend: an overview of interesting places and recommendations

Currently, metropolitan life is really bubbling, and local residents often have the desire to radically change the situation. Where can I go in Moscow? Where can I organize a good rest? Next, we consider the best options for interesting places both within the capital itself and in the suburbs.

Senezh Lake

Where can I go on vacation in Moscow? According to most residents of the capital, the ideal option for relaxation is Lake Senezh - a large and very clean reservoir, surrounded by beautiful nature. This place is a favorite haven of metropolitan fishermen, because it is here that you can catch real eel, the length of which reaches 1 meter, or large crucians.

Senezhsky Lake is a reservoir with crystal clear water, the total area of ‚Äã‚Äãwhich is 8.5 km 2 . As for its depth, in some places it reaches 6 meters.

Senezh Lake is the place that is most actively visited from April to December - in the rest of the year there is complete calm. In the warmer months, the lake is especially popular, so if you want to visit it, you should pre-book a place for yourself. This is done by phone number of the administration.

Fans of outdoor activities can take the opportunity to ride on catamarans, boats, as well as windsurfing and diving in the water. An excellent clean beach is equipped on the shore of the lake, on which there is everything that is necessary for a relaxing holiday.

Senezhsky Lake is located in the northern part of the Moscow Ring Road, at the entrance to Solnechnogorsk. In order to catch fish in the lake, you need to get a special permit from the administration, the cost of which is 1,500 rubles. If you want to rent a room in a hotel located on the shore, you will have to pay 3,000 rubles per day.

Pilgrim porto

There is a desire to get a walk outside the capital, but do not know where you can go from Moscow? In the Solnechnogorsk district of the Moscow Region there is the famous Serednyakovo estate, and on its territory there is a unique object - Piligrim Porto - a park created specifically for filming films of various formats.

In the comments left by Muscovites, it is quite often noted that the site in question is that place, a stay in which, of course, will appeal to many children.

It is worth noting that on the site under consideration you can find objects whose style is characteristic of different eras. Among these are the scenery of this seaport, taverns, cemeteries and even prisons.

The facility under consideration is located 30 km from the capitals, in the village of Firsanovka. Moreover, you can easily get here by public transport. The cost of one visit to Piligrim Porto ranges from 50 to 150 rubles, depending on the age of the guests.

Park-Museum "Ethnomir"

Considering those places where you can go with your child in Moscow, you should definitely pay attention to another site, the territory of which is a haven for exhibits showing the features of different peoples of the world.

The Ethnomir complex occupies a large territory - a little over 80 hectares. It is divided into several park zones, each of which reflects the identity of a particular people. In particular, it is here that you can familiarize yourself in detail with the features of the life of Indians, Japanese, Australians, Greeks, Italians, etc.

On the sites of the park in question it is often possible to become a participant in various master classes. Guests of the complex note the well-developed infrastructure on its territory, as well as the presence of a large number of catering establishments and hotels, where you can stay for a daily rest.

The park-museum in question is located in the direction of the Minsk and Kiev Highways, just over 100 km from the capital. The cost of visiting the sites is 600 rubles, and for representatives of privileged categories - 450 rubles.

Quarry in Lytkarino

Where in Moscow can I go for a beach holiday? A great place for a good time by the water is the quarry located in the Lytkarino town near Moscow.

Around the pond are clean sandy beaches surrounded by pine trees. Moreover, visitors to this reservoir are pleased with the well-developed infrastructure in this place. In particular, for guests of a natural object, places for playing volleyball, as well as zones for lighting bonfires with barbecue facilities, are equipped at separate sites.

Deauville

Where in Moscow you can go for a walk in winter or summer? At any time of the year, Muscovites are interested in visiting the Deauville cottage town. This place is famous for the fact that on its territory there are a large number of incredibly beautiful houses, the architecture of which is made in the European style of the period of the 19th century.

In addition to unique and very beautiful buildings on the site, in Deauville you can observe a well-developed infrastructure, represented by the presence of a large number of cafes, sports grounds, restaurants, spa centers and even a large beach.

It is worth noting that most Muscovites prefer to go to Deauville for just one day, since renting a cottage, as a rule, seriously affects the family budget of the average Russian.

Yaroslavl

Where can I go from Moscow in winter? In Yaroslavl, according to residents of the capital, during the cold season you can observe unique landscapes that will leave a pleasant impression in your memory for a long time.

Having been in Yaroslavl, you should definitely visit the main attractions of the city, the most popular include:

  • Elias Temple;
  • Arrow Park;
  • House of Muses;
  • museum of music;
  • Volga embankment;
  • Transfiguration Cathedral.

It is worth noting that the Ilyinsky temple is a landmark whose architectural style interests tourists from the capital. If you want to visit this object, it is worth remembering that it is closed in the cold season, therefore, if you want to explore the unique interior of the temple, it is best to go to Yaroslavl in the summer.

While in Yaroslavl in the winter, you definitely need to visit Sovetskaya Square, on which, on the eve of the New Year's holidays, a tall and very beautiful tree is set up annually, and festivities in the folk style are held near it, in which everyone can take part.

It is worth noting that all Muscovites staying in Yaroslavl will definitely not be bored, because more than 800 interesting places are represented within this city.

Peredelkino

When considering those places where you can go for a walk in Moscow, you should definitely mention the unusual district of the capital - Peredelkino, which is located in the south-west direction on the Moscow Ring Road, at a distance of 5 km from the central part of the capital.

Peredelkino is a small writer's town, on the territory of which there are a large number of museums opened in honor of various figures of Russian art (Chukovsky, Pasternak, Okudzhava). Many Muscovites call this place the embodied history of literature of the Soviet period.

Peredelkino is a settlement that is famous for its unique nature, as well as clean air. Here you can also admire the beauty of churches and numerous temples, as well as the building of the residence of the Patriarch.

Where can I travel by train from Moscow? As practice shows, many residents of the capital prefer to get to Peredelkino on this particular type of transport.

Krasnogorsk

At present, Krasnogorsk is recognized as the greenest town in the Moscow Region. It is located only 22 km from the capital, so you can get to it both by personal car and by public transport.

Practice shows that Krasnogorsk is a settlement that becomes especially popular in the spring and summer. At any time of the year, guests of the town can please themselves with skiing at the Snezh.kom site. Moreover, here you can visit the popular Znamenskoye Gubailovo estate.

Husky Land

In the village of Bogdanikh there is a unique ethnocultural complex - "Husky Land", on the territory of which a detailed and rather fascinating acquaintance with the peculiarities of everyday life of the peoples of the north is offered.

Arriving at Husky Land, you can become a spectator of a unique theatrical performance, in which you can look at an interesting ritual of a shaman. Guests of this place are also offered the opportunity to shoot from a real bow.

On the territory of the Husky Land ethnocultural complex there is an interesting camp where real Evenks, Nanai, and Chukchi live. In addition, each visitor to the region in question is greeted by animators dressed in national costumes of the peoples of the North.

If desired, visitors to the ‚ÄúHusky Land" can ride in a team with sled dogs, as well as take pictures with them.

The park in question is located in the Moscow region, in the Leninsky district, at the address: 18 Bogdanikha village, Krylatskaya street.

Bird Park "Sparrows"

Where can I go today in Moscow? In 2005, in the Moscow Region, on the territory of the Zhukovsky District of the Kaluga Region, a unique Sparrow Park was opened, visiting which you can admire the unique species of birds, as well as just relax in beautiful nature.

Park "Sparrows" is a place in which there are several sites for active and passive recreation, barbecues are installed and there is a place for playing paintball. Visitors to such a park pay special attention to the fact that there seems to be a possible ride on a horse, from which both children and adults will be delighted.

Entrance to the Sparrow Park is paid - for one ticket for an adult you will have to pay 400 rubles. Preschool children have the right to visit the park area for free.

Prioksko-Terrasny Reserve

Both children and adults will definitely be interested in visiting a unique natural site located in the suburbs of the capital - in the Serpukhov district. The uniqueness of the Prioksko-Terrasny Reserve is that more than 1000 species of animals constantly live on its territory.

Where can I go on a weekend from Moscow? Most of the recommendations left by the residents of the capital indicate that the Prioksko-Terrasny Nature Reserve is the ideal place for a family vacation. A wonderful atmosphere reigns here, as well as the cleanest air, provided by the presence of a huge number of coniferous trees, and in some areas you can notice the plantings of ornamental shrubs and flowering plants.

The reserve in question is located in the town of Danki, in the suburbs. The cost of one visit to the park for adults is 250 to 400 rubles, depending on which site there is a desire to visit.

Interesting places in the capital

It is worth noting that to visit interesting places it is not necessary to travel outside the Russian capital. Where can I go for a walk in Moscow? It is worth noting that in the territory of this metropolis there is a large selection of interesting places, which include:

  • Museum "Moscow Lights" (Armenian Lane, 3-5, building 1);
  • Museum "House on the Embankment", inside which the atmosphere of the 1930s was recreated;
  • Museum of Musical Culture Glinka
  • restaurant "In the dark ?!" for fans visiting unusual places;
  • Savvinsky Compound, located in the arch of the house number 6 on Tverskaya;
  • Pharmaceutical garden (Prospect Mira, 26, building 1), which is considered the oldest botanical garden in all of Russia.

Where can I go at night in Moscow? A great place for an unforgettable vacation in the dark will be the observation deck "Moscow City", from which you can view the panorama of the city from a bird's eye view (58th floor). Of course, it can be visited during the day, but at night the capital is covered with a lot of lights, which makes the view of it more vivid, beautiful and unforgettable.
