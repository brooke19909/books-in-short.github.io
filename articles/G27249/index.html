Morgan Stanley: forecasts, analytics, rating, reviews and addresses

Morgan Stanley is the world's largest bank. It was founded by Henry Morgan in 1939, and in 2008 it was renamed a commercial bank. The main areas of his activity are operations with corporate securities, asset management, consumer lending (through the Discovery Card division). Offices and subsidiaries are represented in 42 countries of the world, including in the Russian Federation.

Background

In 1932, the Glass-Stigll Act entered into force in the United States, which prohibits banks from investing. As a result, JP Morgan & Co. I had to reorganize and create an institution engaged exclusively in investment activities. So, on September 16, 1935, Morgan Stanley Bank appeared in the USA.





Achievements

Over the years, the organization has developed not only investment activities, but also other areas:

  • In 1964, MS created a model for analyzing the financial markets.
  • In 1967, the bank opened its first branch in Paris. In the same year, a redemption transaction for Brooks Harvey & Co. took place. Inc., which allowed the financial institution to enter the real estate market.
  • Since 1971, the bank began work in the field of exchange trading and, after 15 years, placed its own shares in the primary market.
  • In 1997, a merger with Dean Witter Discover, which was engaged in the issuance of bank cards and the provision of brokerage services. Subsequently, the credit institution used these payment instruments to provide consumer loans. But in December 2006, Morgan Stanley Bank announced the launch of the Discovery Card in a separate company.

2008 financial crisis

Morgan Stanley is one of the affected institutions from the 2008 crisis . Investments in hedge funds for 2 weeks brought $ 128.1 billion in losses. Then, a Fed loan of $ 107.3 billion helped the bank avoid bankruptcy. In September of the same year, the Japanese bank Mitsubishi UF J Finance bought back a 21% stake in Morgan Stanley.





September 22, 2008, it was announced that MS would become a traditional banking corporation regulated by the Fed. In January 2009, Morgan Stanley, an American bank, joined Citigroup in organizing MSSB, a company that manages the capital of large customers. A large half of the shares (51%), as well as an option with the right to repurchase the remaining share belong to MS.

Morgan Stanley Bank (Moscow)

Since 1994, Morgan Stanley has been operating in the domestic market in such areas as investment consulting, underwriting (placements of shares in Lukoil, Gazprom, AFK, Pyaterochka, Rosneft, Evraz, etc.) , development, provision of mortgages. In 2006, an American bank even participated in the organization of the City Mortgage Bank, but after a year and a half put up its assets for sale.





The Russian LLC Morgan Stanley Bank was registered in mid-2005. More than half of the assets (68%) belong to non-residents. The organization is aimed at working with legal entities. The Bank does not accept deposits of individuals, but shows high turnover on correspondent accounts, which, in turn, is reflected in high rates of business activity.

Areas of work

Morgan Stanley Bank operates through divisions and branches around the world, providing customers with a number of financial services, in particular:

  • Corporate rights management: increasing capitalization (publication of shares, underwriter), advising (on mergers and acquisitions, restructuring, project financing), exchange activities, risk management, investment activities.
  • Providing brokerage services and investment advice to private investors.
  • Asset management of institutional and private individuals, operations with fixed income securities.
