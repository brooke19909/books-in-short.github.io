Preparing for the carnival. We make angel wings with our own hands (three ways)

There is a costume party coming up , and you think, with whom to dress up? We offer you to play the role of an angel. Reincarnate in this image is not difficult. The costume is also quite simple to complete. You will surely find a white robe among the things of your wardrobe, but we will teach you how to make angel wings with your own hands . This article presents three workshops on how to make this attribute. Study them, choose the one you like best and start the creative process.



We make white angel wings from improvised means. Preparation stage

This model is made of the most affordable materials listed:

  • A3 format cardboard (2 sheets);
  • White paper;
  • white wipes;
  • wide elastic (about 2 meters);
  • pen.

In addition, PVA glue, a thermogun, scissors, a pencil, sewing accessories, a stapler are needed for work.

Instructions for the manufacture of angel wings for a fancy dress from napkins and feathers

On paper, draw a sketch of the wing of the size you need. Cut the template, transfer it to the cardboard. Perform two such parts symmetrical to each other. Cover them on both sides with white paper. Next, try on the product to the back and outline the places where the elastic bands will be attached. Keep in mind that the distance between them should be equal to the width of the back. Sew them to the cardboard blanks or pin them with a stapler. Seal fasteners with white paper patches. Now proceed to the design of the product. Glue the wings of an angel with a thermogun along the edges with white long feathers. Decorate the remaining space with napkins. How to do it? Apply PVA glue to the entire surface of the wing. Crumple a napkin and place it on a cardboard part. Thus, design the entire product. Crumpled napkins are very similar visually to fluff. Angel wings , made in this way, look very believable. Leave the craft to dry at room temperature. Press it down at the edges (where the feathers are attached) with hard objects. Performing this action, make sure not to take napkins. After the attribute of the carnival costume dries, you can add it with decorative elements: rhinestones, beads, painting with acrylic paints.

Flap white cloth

An angel‚Äôs costume, namely its main part - wings, can be made very quickly from thin textile. For their manufacture you will need such materials: white fabric (cotton, linen, silk), thread and needle, stapler, cardboard, scissors.

Want to know how to make such an attribute of a fancy dress? Then study the description.





Perform the template and the cardboard base as described in the first version of the product. Next, tighten the blanks with a white cloth, secure it with a stapler. Tear another piece of textile into long strips with a width of 3-5 centimeters. Gather them with stitches on the thread along one edge and pull it slightly, forming a frill. Pin the resulting lush piece onto the wing horizontally with a stapler. Thus, shape the entire front side of the product. Angel wings, made in this way with their own hands, turn out to be voluminous and very beautiful. Torn edges of the fabric are associated with feather and down and look very natural on such an accessory.
