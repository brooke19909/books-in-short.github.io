GPS speedometers: description, specifications

GPS speedometer is a measuring device that instantly determines the speed of movement. According to the measurement method, centrifugal, chronometric, vibrational, induction, electromagnetic and satellite speedometers are distinguished.

What is it?

GPS speedometer works by satellite positioning system . The device determines the speed electronically as the distance traveled, divided by the travel time. It takes a signal of speed of movement, but with a small error. By the type of indicator, they are analog, arrow, tape, drum and digital. The digital speedometer indicator is a liquid crystal display that displays the speed in numbers.



The main problem of such a device is rapidly changing speed indicators or long delays in readings. All this leads to the fact that the speed data at a certain point in time, for example, during braking and acceleration, are indicated incorrectly. The most popular are analog speedometers. Digital are used in a few models.

The digital GPS speedometer has its pros and cons. On the one hand, this is a modern device with an attractive design and small size, on the other - the presence of errors, constant jumps in numbers knock the driver and distract from the road with inaccurate indicators. Despite the classification, absolutely all speedometers have an error of readings. Manufacturers of these technical devices create devices in such a way that their performance tends to increase against the actual speed of movement. It is measured as a percentage.

What is it needed for?

GPS speedometer for Android is a convenient program with which you can measure the speed of movement. In order to use it, you need to download the application on your smartphone, start and find the active connection. This speedometer is ideal for bicycles and cars. It shows the total distance, current speed, has a counter. A significant drawback of the program is a large error. The navigation device has the same capabilities, but is installed on the front panel of the car.



Specifications

The speedometer on Android without GPS performs the same function as a regular device, but without using an active connection. For example, a GPS navigator with an econometric EcoDrive Speedometer is a convenient device. It is characterized by maximum accuracy, informs the driver about all the characteristics of the movement. The indicator of economy of movement is based on the analysis of acceleration, speed, elevation. Thanks to him, the driver in real time is able to adjust the style of movement and reduce fuel consumption.

On the digital instrument, indicators are shown on the display. This information allows the driver to control the time, distance traveled, altitude, average and maximum speeds. In order to switch from one information to another, you just need to touch the screen. All navigation speedometers are compact, lightweight, have an attractive ultramodern design.



Such speedometers are suitable for all types of transport: land, water and air. In addition, electronic devices with a GPS system are used for hiking and sports. The difference between Android programs and physical devices is in the margin of error and price. Speedometers are expensive, unlike applications downloaded to a smartphone.
