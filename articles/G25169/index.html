Astoria Hotel (Vladivostok) - general description

This spacious and bright four-star hotel was built only two years ago, and therefore is equipped with the latest technology. "Astoria" is a complex of seven floors, where there is everything necessary for a pleasant stay.

Who should come here? First of all, to people in whose life comfort is not the last place, and they are not ready to give it up even on business trips or on vacation. Also true gourmets will not be disappointed by tasting the breakfasts offered by the Astoria Hotel (Vladivostok). Location

Hotel "Astoria" (Vladivostok) is located near the administrative and historical centers of the city, in close proximity to it there are museums - geological-mineralogical and military-historical, a fashion theater called "Pygmalion", an aquarium, universities, the Golden Horn Bridge , funicular, art gallery, Church of the Intercession of the Blessed Virgin Mary, as well as a sculpture of Murom‚Äôs patron saints of the family - Peter and Fevronia.

In addition, near the hotel you can find a large number of shops and shopping centers, cinemas, cafes and restaurants, a sports complex - in short, everything you need for an active life.

Hotel "Astoria" (Vladivostok), the photo of which you see below, is located at a distance of about three kilometers from the railway and six from the bus station, forty-five kilometers from the airport. Therefore, by car, you can get to the stations in no more than twenty minutes, and to the airport in a maximum of an hour.





Planning to arrive without a car? Not a problem. Within walking distance from the hotel there is a bus stop called "Tool Factory", from where you can get to any end of the city.

Hotel rooms

Hotel "Astoria" (Vladivostok) offers one hundred and thirteen clean and comfortable rooms. They are divided into three categories: "standard" single, "standard" double with two single or one double bed and double "suite".





What is the difference between these numbers and what is their similarity? Each of them has everything for a comfortable stay: air conditioning, LCD TV with satellite channels, refrigerator (minibar), safe, upholstered furniture, kettle, coffee and tea, bottled water for drinking, telephone. There is a bathroom in all rooms, in it you will find a hairdryer, bathrobe, slippers, shower gel, shampoo and a private bathroom. The bathroom in the hotel is combined.





Single rooms from double rooms differ only in size - the first ones are slightly smaller. The "luxury" differs significantly from them - there are two rooms: a bedroom and a living room with upholstered furniture, there are dishes and even a bar counter. The majestic whirlpool and shower stall awaits its visitors in the bathroom. The remaining amenities in the rooms are identical.





Each room is tastefully furnished, the interior is made in the same style, most recently renovated. The pleasant colors of the rooms will make you feel at home here. One can rightfully say that the Astoria Hotel (Vladivostok) in Russia is one of the most sophisticated and stylish.

To stay in touch even on vacation or to communicate with colleagues on a business trip, you can use the wireless Internet - it is available in every room here, and it‚Äôs completely free and at a decent speed.

Nutrition

Hotel "Astoria" (Vladivostok city) offers its visitors a truly chic restaurant called "Twinkle" with delicious Asian, European and Russian cuisines. A continental breakfast is included in the price of the vouchers , and it certainly will not disappoint you: such a variety of dishes, moreover, of high quality and not cheap, can hardly be found in hotels of this category. This fact is noted by many visitors who have been here. Products used for cooking are environmentally friendly and are brought to the hotel exclusively from farms.

If you want to really relax and unwind, the hotel can offer you the service "Breakfast in bed" or order food in the room. But note that a certain amount may be charged for these services.

There is also a bar where you can enjoy drinks or light snacks.

The restaurant's interior is made in a modern loft style and is rightfully recognized as one of the best modern institutions in Vladivostok, because it personifies the oriental spirit of this city as well as possible.





For business travel

Hotel Astoria (Vladivostok) includes a conference room that can comfortably accommodate fifty people. There is everything for a successful seminar, conference or training. It is also possible to use photocopy, fax, use wireless Internet.

If there is a need to conduct business negotiations or a meeting, the hotel also has the conditions for these events - a special room is equipped. And you can mark the successful completion of negotiations at the banquet hall.

For children

Accommodation with children under the age of ten is free, but only if the child is not provided with a separate bed and he lives in a room with a parent or guardian. Special beds are not available for young visitors.

Breakfast for children under ten years of age is also included in the price. For families with children over ten years of age who plan to use an extra bed to accommodate a child, you must pay a thousand rubles at the reception for providing a berth. Breakfast for children in this case is also payable.

For foreign citizens

If you live in another country and you need a visa to enter Russia, the Astoria Hotel (Vladivostok) will provide you with all the necessary documents that are needed to prove where you are staying.

To receive documents, you need to book a room and contact the hotel reception by fax, phone or e-mail. Please note that a certain amount may be charged for these services even if you subsequently decide to cancel your room reservation. All obligations and arrangements are made only between the hotel and you.

Terms of Accommodation

Check-in at the hotel is carried out according to generally accepted European rules - from 14:00, check-out - from 12:00. If you need a later check-out or early check-in, this should be reported in advance at the hotel reception. In both cases, additional service charges may apply.

Upon arrival at the hotel, you must present an identification document with a photograph. A deposit in cash and with a credit card will be charged from the guests, which is returned upon departure (provided that the room is preserved in its original form).

Payment for accommodation can be made both on the official website of the hotel, and directly on the respondent. We accept Visa and Master Card, as well as cash.

Accommodation with small animals is possible, but for this it is necessary to arrange with the hotel in advance by writing a letter in response to the reservation confirmation, as there are restrictions.

All rooms are non-smoking; smoking is prohibited on site.

It is possible to place three people in double rooms on an extra bed. The surcharge will be one thousand rubles.
