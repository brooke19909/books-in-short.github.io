Luxury Bahia PrincIpe Bouganville (Dominica / La Romana): tourists photos and reviews

Luxury Bahia Principe Bouganville 5 * is one of the best hotels in the Dominican Republic, which every year is visited by a huge number of tourists from around the world. Both the management and the vacationers themselves often notice that in this place there is everything necessary for a pleasant pastime. Attention travelers are presented with comfortable rooms that are equipped with the latest technology, as well as a huge SPA center where guests can undergo a number of cosmetic procedures. On the large territory of Luxury Bahia Principe Bouganville 5 * there are various kinds of entertainment that tourists of all age categories like.



general information

The hotel was built in 2013 and belongs to the Don Pablo Collection (DPC) leisure group. Initially, accommodation was available only to adults, and the place was called Luxury Bahia Principe Bouganville (adults only). In May 2017, the situation changed radically - family holidays with children were allowed here. However, despite this, many tourists in their reviews posted on the Internet often notice that such a hotel is more suitable for a romantic getaway for two.

Location

Guests of Luxury Bahia Principe Bouganville in their reviews of the hotel tell us that this place to stay is located in a very favorable area, where there is a wonderful climate and unusual nature. Directly from its territory there is access to a clean and picturesque beach, the line of which stretches for 2.5 kilometers.

In the southern part of the Dominican Republic, where the hotel is located, there is always sunny weather, which allows tourists, while resting, to enjoy the high temperature in the daytime. Many travelers in their comments note that there is almost no heat, since the air is perfectly softened by the influence of moist masses coming from the coast of the Caribbean Sea.

Luxury Bahia Principe Bouganville 5 * hotel is located on the territory of a large resort region in the Dominican Republic - La Romana, in which a large number of guests from Russia and the CIS countries rest. The place where it is located is quite secluded - the nearest settlement is located 12 km from here. It is in it that a large number of places for entertainment are concentrated that vacationers like to visit.

The main transportation hub used by all tourists who come to relax in Luxury Bahia Principe Bouganville 5 * is the airport in La Romana, which is located at a distance of 45 km (an hour by car).



Rooms

This hotel belongs to the category of large, as its number of rooms is quite large - it consists of 414 apartments, which are located in luxurious two-story villas, each of which has its own terrace - from it you can observe a magnificent view of the vast expanses of the Caribbean Sea.

All apartments are modernly renovated and have excellent technical equipment. Here, according to travelers, you can relax and spend time in a wonderful atmosphere of comfort and luxury. In reviews of Luxury Bahia Principe Bouganville, it is often said that the interior design of the rooms sets up for an excellent holiday. The interior of the rooms is made in light pastel shades, which make it more fresh and spacious in appearance.

The rooms have private bathrooms with sound and modern fixtures. As a place for swimming, there is a bathtub with a jacuzzi and a shower. In addition, there is a bathroom and sink. In free-standing white cabinets, a set of bath accessories is provided, as well as a bathrobe, slippers, a towel and a hairdryer designed for drying hair.

Technical equipment

As for the technical equipment of Luxury Bahia Principe Bouganville (Don Pablo Collection), it is very good. It features a large plasma TV with satellite channels. Tourists from the CIS countries note on the positive side the presence of Russian-speaking among them. On a free basis, you can use the electronic safe with which the safety of small valuables is ensured. There is also a minibar in the rooms, the assortment of which is replenished every day with new chilled non-alcoholic drinks. The apartments have a telephone, through which calls to the reception are free, and those made via the international communication channel are charged according to a separate price list.

Guests can always prepare their favorite hot drink using a special substation. Many vacationers recommend drinking them on the outdoor terrace or on the balcony, which offers stunning views of the surroundings.

Services

Luxury Bahia Principe Bouganville offers a huge range of services that make hotel guests' lives more comfortable. One of them is the possibility of restaurant service in rooms, which provides for the completion of the order and its delivery to the apartments at any time of the day. Also, at the entrance to this place for recreation, tourists receive a welcome cocktail.

Travelers pay special attention to the quality of work of maids. According to them, the service personnel of this class copes with the tasks set perfectly, leaving completely clean apartments after each visit.

Each hotel guest has the right to use the services of a butler, who is also an excellent consultant. It is with him or with the administrators at the reception that tourists can get answers to all questions of interest regarding accommodation, services or the location of interesting objects.

Throughout the hotel, tourists have the right to use free Internet access. With the help of this, every vacationer can keep in touch with his family and friends, even while being at a considerable distance. Also, thanks to this opportunity, vacationers share their impressions gained during the rest with their subscribers and friends on social networks.

As for the additional features, there are several places in the Luxury Bahia Principe Bouganville (only adult) where they provide additional services. These include a beauty salon and a hairdresser, a conference room, a travel agency. In addition, hotel guests can always use the paid services of a doctor, whose office is in a separate building. If desired, tourists have the opportunity to rent a car and go on it on a short trip around the neighborhood.



Nutrition

Much attention is paid by tourists to food, which is organized for guests of the Luxury Bahia Principe Bouganville (DPC). There are six restaurants on site, among which the most popular are those serving Italian and Mediterranean cuisine. A daily buffet is served at the main establishment for travelers on an all-inclusive basis. On a free basis, the buffet also offers a large assortment of local drinks, including tea and coffee, as well as several types of alcohol.

If desired, vacationers can always look into any of the seven bars that are located on site. They offer a large selection of soft drinks and colorful cocktails. In addition, in the menu you can choose snacks, mouth-watering and very original desserts, as well as exotic fruits.



Sport

Fans of an active lifestyle, while staying at the hotel, can visit the large gym, in which active sports are possible. In the open area there are several courts for playing tennis.

Luxury Bahia Principe Bouganville has water sports facilities, including windsurfing and diving. If you wish, you can master this type of hobby from scratch by paying for the services of an instructor. The hotel offers the opportunity for an hour of free water sports or a game of tennis.

Some guests prefer to rent bicycles, which, according to vacationers, are very convenient to go to the outskirts of La Romana, exploring the unique attractions of this region. Renting this type of vehicle is charged hourly.

Beach

The description of the Luxury Bahia Principe Bouganville hotel states that it is located on the first coastline and has its own beach, which is 2.5 kilometers long. This is truly a large territory, distinguished by its beauty. In addition, tourists note in their reviews that along the coastline is always clean, here you can comfortably sit on the huge sun loungers under umbrellas. If desired, vacationers can visit the beach bar.

All tourists in this recreation area can use public toilets and locker rooms, which are evenly installed throughout its territory. The coastal part is guarded around the clock, and also a group of rescuers are on duty on it.



Prices

Tourists who stay in such a hotel very often in their reviews mention the cost of a ticket to it. So, the average price of a week's rest is about 240,000 rubles. It includes renting a double room in the villa, all transportation costs, as well as a transfer from the airport to which you will be arriving. If desired, travelers can include any sightseeing tours in it, but experienced tourists recommend choosing destinations on the spot, in a bureau that operates directly in the hotel building - in the Dominican Republic, the cost of excursions is much cheaper than if they are paid through operators.

Many tourists consider such a cost of tours to the La Romana hotel to be quite acceptable, given the whole range of possibilities that are provided to vacationers during their stay in such a place.
