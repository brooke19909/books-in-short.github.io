About how to transfer money from Beeline to MTS and much more

The global trend in the financial sector today is such that cash is increasingly being squeezed out of circulation. Whether we like it or not, virtual payments are practiced every day more and more and therefore you should get acquainted with how this is done in practice.

Mobile payments

Situations when it is necessary to transfer a certain amount of money from the account of one mobile operator to another often arise. Let's try to master the technology of mobile payments on the example of one specific transfer. Let us consider in detail how, as a user of the Beeline mobile operator, transfer money to MTS. The implementation of such an operation is not significant. And if you remember the correct sequence of actions, then in the future you can use this method of mobile payment and balance replenishment on an ongoing basis. So, how to transfer money from Beeline to MTS? There are two ways to do this.



Use the internet

This is by far the easiest way to make such a transfer. But it is optimal only with a computer and Internet access. In order to transfer money from Beeline to MTS, we simply follow the correct procedure. Through any search engine we find the Beeline mobile communication site and open this page. We go to the section of the site "Mobile Payment" and from all the options that have opened before us, we select the necessary one. Just fill out the form, enter the phone number of the recipient - any telecom operator, in our case, MTS. And in another column we denote the amount we intend to transfer. Click "Pay." Next, a new page will open on which you should log in. In other words, enter your phone number and confirmation code, which will be immediately sent to this number via SMS. After entering the confirmation code from SMS, it remains only to click once to complete the operation. As you can see, there is nothing complicated in answering the question of how to transfer money from Beeline to MTS. Especially when there are no restrictions with the Internet. But the second option is a little more complicated than the first.



About how to transfer money from Beeline to MTS without using a computer

In this case, contacts with a mobile operator are not made through his website on the Internet, but via SMS messages. In this situation, they have to dial and send twice. First of all, we send to the number 7878 from the Beeline phone an SMS message of the following form: "MTS (phone number) (transfer amount)". After this, you should wait a bit until a response message arrives that will contain a confirmation code. After sending this code back, the money transfer operation can be considered completed. The amount will go where you sent it. In order not to be mistaken, the MTS operator number should be indicated without the initial eight. The text of the SMS message should be typed carefully, not forgetting the spaces.
