Ways to replenish the Megafon balance with a credit card

Today we will try to find out how you can replenish the Megafon balance with a bank card. This is not the most difficult task, but it is important for modern subscribers to know some features of operations. Otherwise, to cope with the task does not work.

Replenishment Methods

How can I replenish the Megafon balance with a bank card? There is no single answer to this question.



Modern subscribers are offered a very wide range of ways to recharge SIM-cards using bank plastic. For instance:

  • through the Internet portal of the mobile operator;
  • using the service "Auto payment";
  • via SMS request;
  • via the USSD command;
  • Using ATMs or some payment terminals;
  • through online banking;
  • using a special voice menu;
  • through mobile banking.

We will talk about all these ways of replenishing a Megafon SIM card using bank plastic below. Most of them do not provide for the payment of a commission.

The minimum payment is 10 rubles, the maximum per week is 10,000 rubles. More than 5 thousand cannot be transferred at a time.



Help site

To replenish Megafon‚Äôs balance with a credit card without commission, you can go to the mobile operator‚Äôs website. In just a few minutes, a citizen will cope with the task.

The algorithm for replenishing the Megafon balance with a bank card will look like this:

  1. Open megafon.ru in a browser.
  2. Go to the "Payment" section.
  3. Click on the line "Top up balance".
  4. Put a mark near the item "Bank card".
  5. Indicate the number of the recipient of the transfer.
  6. Prescribe the amount to replenish the SIM card.
  7. Click on the "Continue" button.
  8. Indicate the details of the plastic. For convenience, users are offered a simple graphical menu.
  9. Click on the "Pay" button.

All that remains now is to confirm the transaction. No commission is charged for this appointment. Money comes to the recipient very quickly - just a few minutes after processing the request.

Map binding

To replenish MegaFon‚Äôs balance via the Internet with a credit card, some prefer to activate the Auto Payment service. It is easily customizable on the official page of the mobile operator. The option allows you to transfer money to specified phone numbers under certain conditions.

As a rule, the first step to using auto payments is to link bank plastic to a SIM card. It is done like this:

  1. Go to the site of the operator "Megaphone".
  2. Go to "Payment" - "Set Auto Payment".
  3. Indicate the phone that will be tied to bank plastic.
  4. Confirm procedure.
  5. Enter the details of the data from the bank card.
  6. Click on the "Snap" button.

It is done. Now we can think further on how to replenish the Megafon balance with a bank card. After binding the plastic to the SIM card, the subscriber is offered a more extensive list of options.

SMS commands

For example, some people prefer to use fast self-service commands. Let's say SMS requests.



A subscriber with plastic pre-tied to a number can replenish the Megafon balance with a bank card by creating a simple command that will be sent by SMS.

It is necessary to act like this:

  1. Open SMS on the phone.
  2. Write the amount of payment in the text of the letter.
  3. Send a message to the number 5117.

After these actions, the subscriber will be transferred funds from bank plastic, pre-tied to a Megafon phone. Commission, as in the previous case, is not charged.

Important: the transfer amount must be written in rubles. Otherwise, the operation will not go through the processing stage.

USSD commands

But that is not all. To replenish Megafon‚Äôs balance with a credit card in the Volga region, you can use USSD-requests. A similar option is valid in other regions of the Russian Federation. The main condition for using the opportunity is the presence of pre-attached bank plastic to the SIM card.

To transfer funds you need:

  1. Put the phone in dialing mode.
  2. Type * 117 * amount #.
  3. Press the "Call Subscriber" button.

In a few seconds, the USSD request will be processed. Accordingly, the money will be debited from the bank card. No commission is charged for such transactions.



Voice menu

Some subscribers prefer to avoid the above-mentioned new-fangled options. Therefore, they can be advised to use the voice menu "Megaphone". It will help to recharge a mobile phone account.

Just call 0500910 and wait for an answer. Further, following the instructions of the answering machine, complete the procedure.

Set up auto payment

Megafon subscribers can permanently get rid of problems with a lack of funds on the SIM card. To do this, just configure the option called "Auto Payment". With the binding of plastic to the number we have already met. But this is only half the battle.

Next, you have to set up auto payment. To do this, you need:

  1. Put the phone in dialing mode.
  2. Dial a command like * 117 * 03 * top-up amount * minimum_ on the SIM card "#.
  3. Click on the "Call" button.

That's all. Now, from the linked card, automatic replenishment of the SIM card account will occur. An operation occurs every time when the balance of the mobile device drops to the value "minimum_on_SIM-card".

ATM or terminal

It is proposed to replenish the Megafon balance with a bank card through payment terminals or ATMs. Consider the process using the example of Sberbank.

In order to transfer money from a card to a phone, you must:

  1. Insert the plastic into the ATM and enter the PIN.
  2. Click on the button "Payments in my region".
  3. Click on the button "Mobile Communications".
  4. Indicate operator. In our case, this is Megaphone.
  5. Enter the phone number you want to recharge.
  6. Prescribe payment amount.
  7. Confirm transaction.

In a minute, the money will be transferred from bank plastic to the specified SIM card.

Internet banking

To replenish the Megafon balance with a Sberbank bank card, you can use Internet banking. Here the Sberbank Online service comes to the rescue.



The algorithm of actions will be as follows:

  1. Register on the Sberbank Online portal.
  2. Log in to the service with your username and password.
  3. Go to the "Payments and Transfers" block.
  4. Click on "Mobile Communications".
  5. Indicate the network operator.
  6. Enter the phone number of the recipient.
  7. Indicate the amount of transfer.
  8. Choose bank plastic from which you want to write off money. Actual if the client has several cards.
  9. Click on "Pay."

It remains only to confirm the operation and wait. Money will be credited to the indicated account within a few minutes. There is no commission for the request.
