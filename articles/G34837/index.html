Insula Resort, Turkey: holidaymakers reviews, prices, photos and hotel description

This spa hotel used to be called the Royal Vikingen Resort, and under this name it is most often found in search engines. Chic suburban "five" "Insula Resort" (Turkey) is located not very far from Alanya, twelve kilometers, and very close to the village of Konakli. The hotel stands right on the seashore. From here it is easy to get to Alanya, Manavgat and Side, as well as other iconic places. If you have enough time for excursions, you can choose: book them from guides or visit attractions on your own.





How to get to this hotel and what is nearby

First you need to fly to Turkey, best of all - to the Antalya airport. There are two options here - to use the hotel transfer (this service is available on most tours) or to get on your own by bus if you are not traveling with a group. The stop is right outside the hotel. By the way, on the same bus or on a shuttle bus-dolmush you can get to Alanya. Prior to this amazing beauty and sights of the city, only a quarter hour drive, and buses run literally every five minutes. This once again underlines the good location of the Insula Resort. Original Turkey - if you want to get to know it - is located directly opposite the hotel. Welcome to Konaklƒ± village: there you can buy everything your heart desires, from decent textiles to fur coats. In addition, there is a lunapark for children, beautiful squares with fountains, night clubs, cafes. The village center is only ten minutes walk. To return to the hotel without getting lost, you should focus on the clock tower. It is located just opposite the entrance.





Hotel Territory

The hotel was built in 1990, but in 2004 it was completely renovated. It is a large six-story building. There are three more villas. Its territory is small, but cozy and comfortable. All rooms and facilities have a very compact location. There is an extremely well-groomed garden where something blooms all the time. It is maintained in almost perfect cleanliness, sheared bushes, lawns. There are many places where you can take beautiful photographs as a keepsake. If you are missing something, then an entire arcade of various shops and shops is at your service right on the territory of Insula Resort and SPA. Here you can buy souvenirs, various little things for the beach, sweets, cigarettes.





Where guests are accommodated

Insula Resort is a fairly large hotel. It has almost three hundred rooms. These are standard and family rooms. The latter are larger in area (25 square meters). There are also rooms for people with disabilities - there are two of them. The guest rooms have everything that a person needs for a comfortable stay - a bathroom, comfortable furniture, television (four Russian channels), radio, telephone, air conditioning. Everything is working properly, and in the event of minor household problems, you can always contact the staff who will solve them immediately. Cleaning is daily. Almost every room has a balcony. There is a dryer for clothes, towels and bathing suits, as well as a table and chairs (plastic) so that you can enjoy a drink while admiring the picturesque landscape. 44 rooms have a sea view. The rooms have safes and minibars, which are replenished every day with drinking water. Wireless internet is working.

Services of the Insula Resort Hotel

Turkey is a country where all tourists are trying to make their holidays comfortable. We will begin the list of hotel services primarily with basic services. There is a playground and a club for children under twelve years of age. The TV room is designed for large companies. In order to read something light on the beach, there is a library. To the delight of outdoor enthusiasts, you can play beach soccer, volleyball. Water sports - for a fee. But this is not all the features of the Insula Resort Hotel. Turkey has a positive reputation as a country where hotels have a lot of animation, entertainment, shows and discos. All this can not be counted in this hotel, and these services are of excellent quality. Games and entertainment begin at ten in the morning, and everyone can find something to their liking. A lot of activities on the water. Well, of course, one cannot fail to mention the Salus per Aquam spa, the procedures of which (sauna, hammam, several steam rooms, a barrel, a pool, a relaxation room where you can drink tea, including pomegranate, as well as a fitness center ) are free for guests. But for the massage you have to fork out a little. However, it is beyond praise - there is a general, and therapeutic, and lymphatic drainage. Various types of hydrotherapy, stone therapy (hot stone massage) are offered . In a word, you should not regret money for health.





Beach

The sea is not far from the Insula Resort Hotel. Turkey has a predominantly pebble coast, and therefore the beach is appropriate. But he has his own at the hotel, and various accessories for sea recreation, such as sun loungers, sunbeds, sun umbrellas, are provided for free. Special towels are also provided. In addition, the beach in the sunbathing zone is almost sandy, and pebbles are only at the entrance. Although the descent into the sea is good, gentle, it is recommended to have special shoes. On the territory of the "Insula Resort SPA" there are three swimming pools (children's, outdoor and indoor), as well as a water park. The latter is admired by the guests, especially the three slides and the jacuzzi.





Prices

Hotel "Insula Resort" (Turkey) belongs to the budget "five". Price per room per day starts at forty dollars. An average tour in July-August for a week will cost one and a half to two thousand, depending on the company. Additional costs - safe in the room - two dollars a day. A typical massage program costs about forty conventional units. You can get to Alanya for about two dollars. On the territory of the hotel there is a service for packing luggage before departure - this will cost you five conventional units.
