Relax at the Sultan Gardens Resort. Sultan Gardens Resort 5 *: location, description, number of rooms.

Arriving on holiday in Egypt, the lion's share of tourists from all over the world is trying to quickly escape from everyday life and plunge into the warm waters of the Red Sea. Therefore, many are trying to choose a hotel located as close to the beach as possible, and ideally also close to the airport so that the road is not too long. In Sharm el-Sheikh, this is definitely Sharks Bay. The area is located only 5 km from the place of arrival, however, this is not the main advantage of the shark bay. Here, vacationers will find a real coral paradise with a very rich underwater world, which fully allows you to enjoy diving and plenty of snorkeling.





If this is exactly what you were dreaming about, then be sure to pay attention to the luxurious five-star Sultan Gardens Resort. In it you will find the perfect combination of quality, service, price and a great location.

Sultan Gardens Resort Hotel: location

As already mentioned, a comfortable resort complex is located in the Sharks Bay area, just 6 thousand meters from the airport and 7 thousand meters from the night market and Naama Bay. It occupies the first coastline and has a private enclosed sandy beach directly opposite the picturesque island of Tiran. Next to the hotel is an 18-hole golf course, an international conference room, and on the contrary, an exotic botanical garden. Guests will certainly appreciate the fact that a shuttle bus runs daily to Naama Bay (2 trips per day are free).

Hotel Restaurants

The hotel has an All Inclusive catering system. As a pleasant bonus, they offer exclusive services to regular customers and newlyweds, and a cake for birthday parties. The hotel administration is firmly convinced that a holiday is inconceivable without a good table, therefore, it offers its guests high service and first-class cuisine. Guests of Sultan Garden Resort 5 * (Sharm el Sheikh) You will be pleasantly surprised by the variety of gourmet dishes offered by six restaurants on site.

The main one is Linah, which is ready to offer everyone goodies to taste, including 16 types of hot dishes and a wide variety of desserts. In it, you can visit a themed evening dedicated to Chinese, Indian, Russian, Mexican, Italian, Thai or Egyptian nights. This place is the jewel of Sultan Gardens Resort. Sultan Gardens Resort 5 *, in addition, offers to enjoy Italian cuisine at Casa Mia, the freshest seafood in the Fish Market, spicy Indian food at the Spice House.





Bars

The hotel also has seven bars. Among them is a sports venue on the roof, which offers, in good company with delicious drinks and light snacks, to watch football matches, other important events of this area on a wide screen or play billiards. Two bars are located right on the beach, and a couple of similar objects are located near the pools.

Entertainment and sports

For comfortable relaxation and vivid impressions on the territory of the five pools. "Severny 38" - heated, intended only for adult guests of Sultan Gardens Resort. Sultan Gardens Resort 5 * equipped it with separate changing cabins, a spacious outdoor jacuzzi and a bar with drinks, including alcoholic ones. It has direct access to 24 luxurious Premium rooms.

In addition, guests can get acquainted with the Turkish bath, visit spa and fitness centers, use the golf course, tennis court, go diving, beach sports, water activities.

Analyzing reviews of the Sultan Gardens Resort 5 * hotel, we can definitely say that it is in good standing with tourists not only from Russia, but also from other countries. Add to this the excellent location and proximity of the Red Sea - and get the perfect vacation option.
