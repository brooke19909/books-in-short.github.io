"New"

Hotel "New" is located at the entrance to the city, near the highway Moscow-Minsk. Modern interchanges and convenient location allow guests to easily get anywhere in Smolensk.

The hotel complex has a large secure parking. In the absence of a personal car, the problem of remoteness from the historical center is solved with the help of a relatively cheap taxi, and a bus stop is 200 meters away .

Hotel "New" (Gubenko St., 26) offers guests five categories of rooms:

  • standard DBL (3400 rubles);
  • standard double TWIN (3500 rubles);
  • Standard Superior Superior (3700 rubles);
  • Junior Suite Junior Suite (4200 rubles);
  • two-room Suite Suite (4600 rubles).

All rooms are decorated in a classic style, and the price includes: breakfast, Wi-Fi and secure parking.

Additional services:

  • laundry;
  • organization of excursions;
  • transfer;
  • accommodation with pets;
  • luggage storage.

Traveler Reviews

According to guests, the Novy hotel is 100% justified by its name - the rooms are clean and comfortable, well-groomed area. The location of the hotel is offset by developed infrastructure. In the hotel complex you will find a restaurant, currency exchange and even a gift shop.

Of the minuses, guests note the lack of an elevator. In addition, the restaurant with a beautiful interior is not entirely happy - the waiters are in no hurry to serve, the breakfast buffet is modest, and the menu does not even contain half of the declared dishes.

Premier

The Premier Hotel is another complex on the outskirts of Smolensk (64 Krupskaya St.). Major sights are a twenty-minute walk away. There is a shopping and entertainment center across the road, so at any time guests can go to the cinema, a supermarket or go shopping.



The rooms are available for booking:

  1. Standard - from 2500 rubles.
  2. Standard Plus - from 2900 rubles.
  3. Deluxe - from 3500 rubles.

The rooms have air conditioning, a safe and a telephone. A hairdryer, kettle and kitchenware are available upon request, as well as an ironing board and iron.

At extra charge:

  • laundry;
  • driver services;
  • transfer;
  • flower delivery.

For business meetings, negotiations and presentations, the Premier Hotel offers rental of special equipment and a conference room for 100 seats.

There is a restaurant on the territory of the hotel complex, providing full-fledged meals for guests. Own wine list, a wide range of dishes and first-class service are also available during celebrations or coffee breaks.

Three stars

Guests rate the Premier complex with three stars. The modern rooms are in good condition, the staff is attentive to requests, and the hearty breakfast in the restaurant leaves the most pleasant experience.

As for the minuses, most guests note a dull view from the window of the industrial zone and a small parking lot, which may not be enough space for everyone.
