How to make a fish out of rubber bands: methods, schemes and reviews. How to weave a fish on a gum loom

Many needlewomen know how to weave gum bracelets, while few have created a fish. Such a figure can serve as a keychain, talisman or just an unusual decoration. In this master class we will try to make a fish on a slingshot. This is pretty easy to do. For 1 figure you need 38 rubber bands.

How to weave a fish on a slingshot

Before weaving a fish from gum on a slingshot, you need to prepare 37 yellow and 1 light green gum (choose any color you like).

The process of making fish begins with a ponytail:

  1. Take a slingshot and wind on the right side 1 yellow elastic 3 times.
  2. Then throw 2 rubber bands on both parts of the slingshot and fold the lower ones to the center.
  3. Again, throw 2 pieces and fold the loops from the right and left columns. Transfer the rubber bands from the left to the column on the right side.
  4. Now pull the elastic on the column on the left side, scrolling it in 3 turns.
  5. Pull a pair of rubber bands over two columns, but fold the loops only from the column on the left side.
  6. Pull on a pair of elastic bands again and discard one pair from each column. On the next pair - discard all rubber bands from the posts.




We swam the tail of the fish. How to weave a fish figure out of elastic bands further? Now pull 4 pairs of rubber bands in sequence:

  1. Take 2 yellow, throw on a slingshot and remove both rows under them. Repeat 3 times.
  2. Drag the rubber bands from the column on the left side to the right.
  3. Bring the hook into the loop where the fins connect, throw 2 rubber bands, pull through it. Pull the edges onto the hook and throw these rubber bands onto the column on the left side.
  4. Throw 2 more pairs of rubber bands.
  5. On the first two elastic bands, discard all the loops from the column on the left side. On the second pair - only the top 2.

Making the fish eye

Now you need a gum of light green color for the fish‚Äôs eye:

  1. Throw the elastic on the column on the right side and scroll 4 times. Pull on top 2 yellow elastic bands on both posts.
  2. Discard all light green gum, and then 2 gum from a column.
  3. Throw the yellow rubber bands on the column on the right side.
  4. Bring the end of the hook into the same loop on the tail, throw a pair of rubber bands, stretch them and throw on a column on the left side.
  5. Throw a pair of elastic bands on both columns and fold all the loops from the left.
  6. On the second pair, drop 2 loops, repeat with another pair of rubber bands.
  7. Place 1 rubber band on the machine and discard the rest on it.
  8. Tighten the eyelet. Then you can hide it.

The fish is ready! Use it to decorate a bracelet or as a key ring.





Gum Goldfish

Want to know how to make a fish that looks like gold out of rubber bands? It is believed that such a figure is a powerful talisman that can bring good luck to the owner. Let's make it to give good luck to ourselves and our loved ones.

Before weaving a fish out of gum, you need to prepare 8 beige gum, 16 red and 1 black. This is an option how to weave a fish from gum on a fork. You will also need a hook to work. Getting started:

  1. At the first stage, take 1 red rubber band and throw it on a column on the right side, scrolling in 3 turns.
  2. Then throw 3 beige elastic bands and fold the bottom red loops.
  3. Throw 2 red rubber bands, discard beige. Throw a pair of red again and fold the 2 lower ones.
  4. Now throw the rubber bands from the column on the left side to the right and hook in a loop of three red rubber bands located above the beige ones. Throw it on the column on the left side.




Making a fish eye

  1. Take the black rubber band, throw it on the column on the right side, scrolling in 4 turns.
  2. Throw a pair of red rubber bands, discard the rubber bands from the column on the left side, black ones from the column on the left side, and another 1 pair of red ones from the right column.
  3. Throw 1 pair of red elastic bands and discard all the elastic bands on them from the posts.

We continue to weave the body of a goldfish

Throw the rubber bands from the column on the left side to the right. Then we perform the following steps:

  1. Take a pair of rubber bands, consisting of one beige and red. Throw on the column on the left side, scrolling in 2 turns.
  2. Throw the same pair without twisting, discard the rubber bands only from the column on the left side.
  3. Transfer the elastic bands from the left to the column on the right side. Take again a pair of rubber bands in beige and red, throw on a column on the left side, scrolling in 2 turns.
  4. Throw the next pair on both columns, fold the loops from the column on the left side.
  5. Throw the pair from the column on the left side to the right.
  6. Throw a pair of rubber bands over the column on the left side again, scrolling in 2 turns. Throw another pair and discard the rubber bands from the column on the left side.
  7. Now throw 1 red rubber band and discard all the loops from the posts below.
  8. Throw the remaining rubber band onto the hook, removing from the slingshot, and tighten the knot, pulling 1 loop through the other.
  9. Throw a loop on the tail.




This golden fish weaves so easily.

How to weave a fish out of elastic bands on the Monster Tail machine

There is another option for creating figures from gum. You can weave a keychain in the form of a fish on the Monster Tail machine. For work you will need: a machine, 54 rubber bands of the main color (for example, take the yellow ones) and 2 black rubber bands for the eyes. If you want to make fins, additionally prepare 4 gum blue. This figure weaves very quickly.

We start with the tail:

  1. Take the machine, put the yellow rubber band on the column on the right side of the bottom row and twist it in 3 turns.
  2. Throw the second rubber band on the second column nearby, also turning 3 times.
  3. Next, we will take 2 gum. Throw a pair of elastic bands on the right columns in the upper and lower row.
  4. Throw 2 elastic bands on two columns in the middle.
  5. Take the hook and discard the rubber bands twisted in 3 turns.
  6. Lower the elastic bands lower, throw one more time in a pair on the two right and two central columns.
  7. Discard the bottom rubber band.
  8. To make a narrowing on the tail, throw the rubber bands from the right column in the middle on both sides. Use your fingers to hold the product so that it does not slip off the machine.




Gum fish body

We continue the master class on how to weave a fish out of rubber bands on the Monster Tail machine.

We begin to create the body of the figure:

  1. Throw 2 rubber bands on top, discard the lower layers. There should be 2 loops on both sides. Lower the gum as low as possible.
  2. Throw 6 elastic bands in pairs, making sure that they do not twist and sit on the posts exactly, one above the other.
  3. We grab the 2 lowest rubber bands and drop them from the posts.
  4. Throw 2 top gum onto the column on the left side, 2 from the next layer - to the right. Turn the machine over and repeat all the steps on the other side. Each column should have 2 layers of rubber bands.
  5. Take 1 yellow rubber band and throw it horizontally on 3 columns of the bottom row, pull and rotate the figure eight. Throw back on these 3 columns. This gum will connect all the loops, then there will be no holes on the body of the fish.

Fins for fish

If you want to make fish fins, you can take a pair of rubber bands of a different color and throw them on two columns in the center. Throw 2 yellow rubber bands on the extreme posts.

  1. Using the hook, first discard the horizontal rubber twisted in two turns, and then all the rubber bands of the lower layer.
  2. Throw the elastic back horizontally onto the 3 columns of the front row, but do not turn it over.
  3. We continue to make fins, throwing a pair of blue elastic bands in the center.
  4. On the extreme columns we again place 2 yellow elastic bands. Discard the horizontal eraser first, then all the loops of the bottom layer. Repeat twice, placing the yellow rubber bands in the center in the second stage.




Making the fish eye

For the eye, take 2 black elastic bands: throw the first one on the central column of the bottom row, twisting it 3 times. We do the same on the opposite column.

We continue to weave a fish:

  1. Throw 1 yellow rubber on 3 columns of the bottom row. Then - 2 elastic bands for all columns in pairs, as they did before.
  2. Discard the horizontal eraser first, then all the yellow ones below. Black is not touching.
  3. Now you need to throw off the black rubber bands. Grab 2 loops and remove them. Throw the elastic again horizontally on 3 columns, twisting it with an eight. Throw one elastic band on each pair of columns.
  4. Discard the horizontal rubber band, then all the rubber bands of the lower layer.
  5. It remains to connect all the rubber bands, throwing all the loops from the extreme columns to the central ones. Start with the right column in the front row, then - drag the elastic on the opposite column to the center. Repeat on the other side.
  6. Throw the rubber band on the two central columns, discard all the rubber bands from the bottom layer.
  7. It remains to make a nodule by throwing the rubber band from the column of the top row down, and discard the bottom.
  8. Remove the gum from the machine with a hook. Sip it, tightening the loop.

The fish is ready! Now you know several ways how to weave a fish from gum on a machine tool or slingshot. Creating this figure is very simple, because you can quickly make a lot of key rings or pendants and present them to friends.
