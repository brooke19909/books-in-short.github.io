How to make a phone call over the Internet?

Modern information technologies provide a lot of opportunities. People can communicate, send photos and various files to each other, being on different continents, without making special efforts for this. To make calls, now it is not at all necessary to have a mobile phone or funds in its account. It is enough to purchase any device that allows you to access the Internet. Not all users are aware of this feature. In this article we will talk in detail about whether it is possible to make a phone call via the Internet and what is needed for this.





Benefits of Internet Calls

Using the global network for calls has several advantages:

  • Simplicity. It is not necessary to have a remarkable mind and to have any specialized knowledge.
  • Accessibility to the World Wide Web. A microphone, headphones or a headset are all the tools a user will need to make a phone call over the Internet.
  • Profitability. A number of services and specialized programs make it possible to chat with friends and family on surprisingly favorable terms (and in some cases even for free) even when calling to long-distance and international numbers.




Calls from computer to computer

To call via a computer with users who are also at this moment sitting in front of the monitor, it is enough to install any messenger. You can even make a video call if you have a webcam. You only need to install the desired program or application. For example, Skype, "Agent Mail.ru", ICQ.

In most cases, such calls are free and do not imply restrictions on the time of communication. In this case, it does not matter where your interlocutor is located - on the other side of the city or even in another country. The main thing is that he has Skype installed. But to call a phone number via the Internet is somewhat more difficult.





Services for calls to mobile numbers via the Internet

On the network you can find many different services and software that can be successfully used for communication. And through some you can even call your home phone via the Internet. Consider the most popular programs that provide similar features:

How to call via the Internet to a mobile phone in other ways?

A number of services have also been developed for large organizations and firms that need to regularly interact with partners or customers. Their work is done through the SIP protocol. The use of IP telephony is also widespread. Such communication options provide interaction with mobile and home numbers, with subscribers within their network.
