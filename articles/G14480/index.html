Hotel reviews The Three Corners Sunny Beach Resort 4 *

Egypt is a great place for a wonderful pastime, because here the warm sea and picturesque coral reefs are harmoniously combined with exotic culture and ancient history. One of the most popular resort cities in the country is Hurghada, which is famous for its beautiful beaches and luxury hotels. It is here that the hotel complex The Three Corners Sunny Beach Resort is located. So what conditions can tourists expect? Is the hotel suitable for families? How do guests respond to this place? Answers to these questions will be useful to many readers.

Where is the hotel situated?





The Three Corners Sunny Beach Resort is a large hotel complex located just off the coast of the Red Sea. This is a wonderful place for a pleasant beach holiday. By the way, the airport (international) is located only seven kilometers away - a hotel or travel agency will provide you with a transfer, so after 20 minutes you can enjoy the picturesque territory of the hotel and the sincere hospitality of the staff. The center of Hurghada is about 3 kilometers away. You can cover this distance on foot or call a taxi. Here you will find shops, colorful markets, large entertainment and shopping centers, as well as many interesting sights.

Hotel The Three Corners Sunny Beach Resort 4 * (Hurghada): room description





This hotel complex can be considered relatively large, as there are 301 rooms. Basically, this is a single and double standard apartments, although there are several large, family, which are designed for simultaneous accommodation of 4-6 people.

All rooms are quite spacious, well decorated and bright. The room has a small balcony with a set of furniture - here you can relax, enjoying the sea air, the beauty of the Red Sea or a view of the exotic garden.

Each room has a small but cozy seating area. Naturally, there is also the necessary equipment, including a TV (connected to a satellite dish), air conditioning, telephone and a small safe. There is also a minibar with a cooling system, but its filling is paid separately.

The spacious bathroom has a bath or shower. There is a toilet, a large cosmetic mirror, a set of towels and hygiene products. The rooms are cleaned every day, so you are unlikely to have to complain about cleanliness.

Catering for hotel guests





For many tourists, an important point is food. What can the Three Corners Sunny Beach Resort offer? This hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis familiar to Egypt. Three times a day, guests are invited to the main buffet restaurant. The choice of dishes here is quite large, so every tourist will be able to make a suitable menu for himself. Each evening, themed evenings are organized here, including dinner, which serves Italian, French, Mediterranean and Oriental dishes.

You should definitely visit the specialized restaurant Chez Pascal, where you can taste interesting dishes of Belgian cuisine, as well as various steaks, fish and seafood. The hotel has an Italian √† la carte restaurant called Dolce Vita. It is located on the beach, so you can enjoy delicious Italian dishes to the sound of sea waves. By the way, you can get here only by prior registration.

There is a bar on the beach, by the pool, as well as in the lobby. Here you can get drinks at any time, including some alcoholic drinks, as well as snacks and desserts.

Beach holidays for tourists





The Three Corners Sunny Beach Resort hotel complex has its own beach area, which is located in close proximity to the residential building: the sea can be easily reached in a few minutes. The beach here is small, but clean and well equipped. The entrance to the sea is quite convenient, although, given the proximity of the coral reef, tourists are advised to use special protective shoes. The entrance to the sea is from a long pontoon. There are several small areas where children can swim, and adult tourists, as a rule, prefer depth.

There are plenty of sun umbrellas on the beach, as well as comfortable sun loungers with mattresses. Here you can get a beach towel. There is also a bar and restaurant on the beach.

For lovers of more active pastime, there is a volleyball court. Naturally, for a reasonable price, you can ride a boat, water ski, a boat and do other water sports. Coral reefs are the most striking attraction of Egyptian beaches, so diving is a favorite activity of tourists. The hotel has a scuba diving school where you can take a training course, rent the necessary equipment, buy a tour and spend your first real dive with professional divers.

Are there any conditions for living with children?





Since many people prefer to travel as a family, an important issue is the availability of certain amenities. According to tourists, Three Corners Sunny Beach Resort is well-suited for tourists with children. To begin with, it is worth saying that if necessary, a bed for a child will be delivered to your room quickly. The restaurant will certainly offer a highchair for the smallest guests. And of course, from the abundance of dishes in the daily menu, you can choose something suitable and useful for your child.

The hotel is not far behind in terms of children's entertainment. In the courtyard there is a special shallow pool where the water is always warm. Here your baby will be able to dabble and have fun. There is also a well-equipped playground with swings, slides, orphanages and sandboxes. And of course, the guys are waiting every day at the kids club, where experienced animators and educators pay attention to them. Your child will have the opportunity to do art, dance, take part in fun team games and productions.

Additional services for guests

Of course, the territory of The Three Corners Sunny Beach Resort has everything you need in order to provide the tourist with a pleasant and comfortable pastime. Laundry and currency exchange are constantly available here. Also, if necessary, you can use the services of a doctor, as the hotel has a medical office. And for tourists, a shopping arcade has been arranged. Here you can buy leather and jewelry, souvenirs and other interesting things.

Leisure and entertainment





Of course, hotel guests will not be bored. In addition to the beaches, you will have the opportunity to have fun and relax in the hotel itself. The courtyard is reserved for three large pools, two of which are heated in cold weather. There are several small water slides that are used by both children and adults.

In addition, guests can participate in darts competitions, play mini-golf, billiards and table tennis. Every day on the territory of the hotel various group classes are held, including water aerobics, yoga, oriental dance classes, etc. In the evenings, hotel guests are invited to wonderful shows and interesting performances, which are arranged by cheerful and lively animators.

Naturally, this is not all that The Three Corners Sunny Beach Resort has to offer. Hurghada is a wonderful place for lovers of educational and sightseeing holidays. Local attractions can be advised even on site. Here you can pick up and purchase interesting tours that will make your vacation truly unforgettable.

What type of vacation is the hotel suitable for?

The Three Corners Sunny Beach Resort Hotel is primarily intended for families. Here, in fact, very often families with children stay. On the other hand, youth companies will also not be bored, because the city center with all its discos and nightclubs can be reached in just a few minutes. The hotel is also suitable for fans of excursions, as in the city you can buy a lot of different tours to help explore the surroundings. And in the resort area with a huge number of markets and shopping centers it will be comfortable and fun for shopping lovers.
