Hotel "Viking Castle" in the Crimea: description and reviews of tourists

If you want to enjoy the pristine Black Sea coast, the Viking Castle Hotel located on the western coast of Crimea invites you . Crimea , namely Cape Tarkhankut, where the hotel is located, is famous not only for the clear sea, but also for the pristine beauty of nature. Here you can not only relax, but also explore the picturesque surroundings. Such a vacation will leave an unforgettable experience.

Description

"Viking Castle" (Crimea) is located near the small village of Znamenskoye of the Black Sea region, between Evpatoria and the Black Sea. The hotel is located right at the base of the picturesque Spit Belyaus, which means "white mustache". The braid got its name from the color of sand, which is really almost white here. The hotel is a 3-storey building and an attic. The dormitory building of the resort and entertainment complex "Viking" in architecture resembles a medieval castle. The territory has full infrastructure for a good rest. The hotel has its own beach area 120 meters wide with clean natural sand, with a gentle entry into the sea. The beach is equipped with exotic umbrellas. The beach also has a bar serving cool cocktails. For those who want to move around, there are playgrounds for beach volleyball and beach soccer. You can go diving and underwater fishing. Children can have fun on swings, trampolines and water slides. The hotel has its own restaurant. The restaurant is unusual - it is a Viking ship. In its upper part is equipped with an observation deck from which you can admire the magnificent panorama of the sea. There is also a barbecue patio. The hotel has its own cinema. For those arriving by car, a parking space is provided in a guarded parking lot. The hotel has 58 rooms. The distance to Yevpatoriya is 60 kilometers, to the Black Sea - 16 kilometers. A shuttle bus runs from Evpatoria to Znamenka.

Room Amenities

Hotel "Viking Castle" (Crimea) in all rooms provides guests with the following amenities:

  • a double bed;
  • satellite television;
  • refrigerator;
  • air conditioning;
  • free wireless internet;
  • a bathroom with round-the-clock availability of cold and hot water.

All rooms (except the attic) have a folding sofa. Rooms are cleaned daily. The hotel provides smoking areas. Facilities for disabled guests are available. Pets allowed. The rooms have the possibility of an extra bed. The hotel "Castle Viking" (Crimea) prices for similar services are set as follows:

  • additional bed with meals for a child from three to seven years - 600 rubles per day;
  • extra bed with meals from seven years - 1200 rubles per day.
  • Children under three years are free of charge.

An extra bed is a sofa, a cot or a cot.





Catering

Meals at the Viking Castle Hotel (Crimea) are organized taking into account the most demanding tastes:

  • The hotel restaurant has a capacity of 80 seats, has an open terrace with sea views. The interior is decorated in the style of a Viking drakkara. Meals are organized as a buffet with a variety of delicious home-style dishes.
  • For those who want to eat on the beach, near the beach, there is a convenient platform with gazebos and barbecue facilities. Here, vacationers will be offered a varied menu and a large selection of soft drinks.
  • There is also a billiard bar, which offers a wide range of alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks.
  • If you want to spend a romantic dinner by candlelight, then a table will be laid for you in the gazebo located on the beach.
  • For children, the hotel offers a special children's menu.

"Viking Castle" (Crimea): positive reviews

Of course, rest in such a beautiful place will not leave anyone indifferent. This is also evidenced by the numerous reviews of hotel guests:

  • All visitors to the hotel really like the unusual impressive structure of the hotel, which looks very nice against the backdrop of the steppe landscape.
  • The hotel has many balconies and stairs, each of which offers beautiful views.
  • The sea and the beach are very clean, well-groomed, no algae.
  • There are many seagulls on the shore that are very close to them.
  • The hotel has excellent, very tasty and varied food.
  • Especially guests note that in the morning they always served fruit, and in the afternoon - white or red wine.
  • The child of one of the guests during his vacation at Viking had a birthday, so during breakfast the restaurant staff presented him a cake and a cocktail.
  • In a separate building is a cinema with a large screen. During the day, cartoons were shown there for children, and movies for adults in the evening.
  • Very quiet and peaceful place for those who want to relax from the bustle of the city.
  • There are tourists who, in addition to relaxing on the beach and swimming in the sea, really enjoyed walking on the steppe and breathing the steppe air. For walking on the steppe roads, the hotel has bicycles that are provided free of charge.




Hotel reviews are negative

Unfortunately, the Viking Castle Hotel (Crimea) has negative reviews from some guests:

  • Little greenery on site.
  • In the evenings there are a lot of mosquitoes.
  • In the immediate vicinity of the hotel there is no entertainment.
  • Very expensive accommodation.
  • Many vacationers complain that the rooms have old furniture, some beds have worn mattresses, so as not to feel the springs, we had to put two blankets on them.
  • Also, hotel guests believe that it is time to carry out repairs.
  • There are reviews that the rooms were cleaned no more than once every three days and that the hotel lacked technical staff.
  • Guests believe that the menu could be diversified with seafood dishes.
  • Given that the hotel is located separately, tourists consider the lack of a sanitary unit on its territory to be a drawback.

Conclusion

"Viking Castle" is not only a relaxing holiday by the clear azure sea and the boundless Crimean steppe. Here you can see the amazing ruins of the Greco-Scythian settlement dating back to the 4th century BC, get acquainted with the unknown way of life of the ancient peoples. You can go fishing on Lake Donuzlav. The hotel and its surroundings are very popular venue for photo shoots. Hotel "Viking Castle" is located at the address: Crimea, the Black Sea region, the village of Znamenskoye, the tract Belyaus. This is a very beautiful and attractive place worth visiting.
