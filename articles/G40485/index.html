Larissa Park Hotel 3 * (Turkey, Kemer) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

Those who want to relax on the beach, in a fabulously beautiful resort, must go to Kemer. This colorful corner of Turkey is located less than forty kilometers from Antalya. Tourists will appreciate the warm waters of the bay, the majestic Taurus Mountains and the friendliness of the locals. Homer also mentioned Kemer in his Iliad.

Holidays in Beldibi

The resort is very compact. It has only a few parallel long streets. Such a layout of Beldibi is explained by the fact that on the one hand it is washed by the sea, and on the other it is limited by the slopes of the Taurus.

The resort, like Kemer, boasts a developed infrastructure designed for a comfortable rest of tourists, modern restaurants and bars and a huge number of inexpensive shops.

Hotels in Beldibi are built along the coast, along Ataturk Caddesi Street. Modern hotel complexes with high quality service will make your vacation unforgettable. There are hotels for every taste and prosperity, ranging from five-star hotels with a full range of services corresponding to such a high category, to economy-class establishments. The latter, with their infrastructure, despite fairly affordable prices, are in no way inferior to similar European hotels. But they win by the ratio of "stardom" and cost. Larissa park

The hotel was built in Beldibi, fifteen kilometers from the center of Kemer. It is always full of tourists. The coast on which the hotel is located is known for its magnificent nature. After all, the mountain air here is literally saturated with the healing aroma of pine forests. The Mediterranean Sea, on the shore of which the hotel was built, has always been especially popular among tourists. General information, prices

Larissa Park Hotel was built in 2004. The owners carried out the last major renovation only three years ago. The plumbing has been completely replaced, the rooms are furnished with new furniture, the territory and the building are landscaped.

The hotel is part of the Larissa Hotels hotel chain, which is quite widely represented on the Turkish coast.



The four-story building is built in a classic style. The prices for hotel accommodation are low. For seven days of rest in a room of the "standard" category (with departure from Moscow airport), Russians must pay about twenty-eight thousand rubles. Sometimes this amount changes - it all depends on the occupancy of the hotel and airlines operating charter flights from the capital.

In addition to this hotel, on the coast we are considering is Larissa Park Akman (Kemer). He has a higher category - four stars, and, accordingly, higher prices for accommodation. For example, for the same seven nights you need to pay about thirty-five thousand rubles. Larissa Akman Park 4 * is one of eight hotels included in the hotel chain mentioned above and located on the Mediterranean coast of the Turkish Riviera.

Description

The hotel area is more than seven thousand square meters. It is landscaped and fully landscaped. In the evenings, vacationers can stroll along the shady alleys of the park, admire the surrounding sea and mountain landscapes, breathing in the aroma of pine and orange trees.

At Larissa Park Hotel there is free parking, which can park not only cars, but also large tourist shuttles.

The distance from the hotel to the airport is thirty-five kilometers. The shuttle bus covers this distance in a maximum of forty minutes, which is very convenient for those who come to this hotel with small children.





Infrastructure

In the Larissa Park Hotel 3 * building on the ground floor there is a lobby, a currency exchange, dry cleaning, a medical room. There is also a left-luggage office, it is possible to use a depository safe.

At the 24-hour reception you can order a taxi, arrange a transfer, as well as use a fax and photocopy machine. The hotel offers a tour desk office where you can buy tours to many of the region's attractions. If desired, tourists receive a Russian-speaking guide.

Upon arrival, vacationers are settled very quickly. Bracelets are given to them in just half an hour. The hotel practices a free change of non-liked rooms, as evidenced by the numerous reviews of those who stayed in this institution. Sometimes the influx of guests is so great that the staff does not have time to cope with the flow of settlers. If the booked room has not yet been released, customers are provided with another, including more expensive.

How to pay? There are 4 types of credit cards accepted at Larissa Park Hotel, including Visa and Master. Porter services are also available.

Bathrooms

In the combined bathrooms , new plumbing is installed: a comfortable shower, washbasin and, of course, a toilet. All rooms include hair dryers. Every day, all necessary personal hygiene items are updated for free. The bathrooms have non-slip ceramic tiles on the floor.

Service

In all rooms of Larissa Park Hotel 3 * (Kemer) you can get wireless Internet, for the hour of using which you need to pay three euros. An extra bed, bedding, and an iron and ironing board are available on request.

When placing a child under the age of twelve in a room, a folding bed is issued free of charge, and in other cases it is necessary to pay for it in the amount of half the cost of an adult.

The hotel is pet friendly. There is no need to agree in advance with the administration or pay extra.



The staff performs daily housekeeping, doing it neatly and unobtrusively when guests are in the restaurant. There is also a wake-up service. There are no rooms for non-smokers and people with disabilities.

Nutrition

Those who are attracted by the Turkish Riviera, the Larissa Park Hotel is perfect. Food is organized according to the concept of "all inclusive." Buffets with a variety of delicious food are served in the restaurant, which always has a cozy atmosphere that is comfortable for eating. The all-inclusive system operates from eight in the morning until eleven at night. The menu always has fries and chicken, fresh fruits and, of course, vegetables, a lot of salads and delicious pastries. For small children, cooks can prepare diet soup on request.





The restaurant's working hours are given to guests upon check-in: breakfast is offered until ten in the morning, lunch is served from twelve to two, and dinner tables are served from six to nine in the evening. Children from four to six hours at the pool are given free ice cream. During the day you can drink instant coffee with cakes.

There is also a poolside bar where you can get free drinks from local producers and snacks. Within a ten-minute walk of the hotel there are many inexpensive cafes and hookah cafes serving real Turkish coffee and matchless baklava.

Beach

As already noted, the Larissa Park Hotel (Turkey) is built on the second coastline. From it to the beach only four hundred meters. The road leading to the sea is very convenient. Just five minutes walk - and vacationers find themselves on a pebble beach, where everything is offered for comfortable sunbathing and swimming. Umbrellas, trestle beds and sun loungers for hotel guests are issued free of charge. There is also a shower and toilet.

Entering the water requires special shoes. There is no pier as such, but with sandbags there is a path that is especially convenient for children.

Entertainment

An outdoor freshwater pool is on site. Around it are set free sunbeds and umbrellas. There is also a bar. On the beach, vacationers have a wide range of entertainment. Those who lead an active life even on vacation are invited to visit the sauna or hammam.
