Adler, Resort Town - features, interesting facts and reviews

The resort town in Adler is a microdistrict located in a beautiful place surrounded by mountains, forests and the sea. Here are concentrated living rooms with different levels of comfort. And the number of cafes, restaurants and all kinds of stalls is difficult to count.

This neighborhood is named after a huge complex. It consists of several high-rise buildings and extensive infrastructure.

Why is it worth relaxing here?

Adler is a 20-minute drive from Sochi, so going there for excursions and taking a walk around the city is not difficult. Public transport runs well enough, and there should be no problems with travel.

But the prices in the resort town of Adler are several times lower than in Sochi. This applies to accommodation, food and entertainment. In the named small village there is also something to see and where to go for fun.

The railway station, airport and other transport interchanges are in close proximity from this place. The central street of the city passes through the district, along which public transport in any direction moves.

Beach

To get to the sea, you will need to go through the underpass under the railway track. The pebble beach in the resort town of Adler is fully equipped. It has comfortable sun loungers that you can rent for an hourly fee.

On the shore there are a variety of attractions and attractions: water slides, trampolines. Banana and cheesecake rides, jet ski and catamaran rental are organized, there are diving centers.

The beaches are equipped with changing rooms and dry closets. It is cleaned daily, so you can safely let children play in the sand.

Entertainment

It is in this area that almost all places are concentrated for an interesting and fun pastime. Therefore, in the spa town of Adler there are always many tourists. Here you can‚Äôt walk in silence and without bustle.

In the named area is the Dolphinarium "Water Area". Here, up to 4-5 performances per day are held during the season. The average show lasts 50 minutes. It involves dolphins, white whales, fur seals.

At the same time, up to 1000 spectators can attend the performance. Visitors, at will and for a fee, take pictures with animals and buy paintings painted by them at auction.

Water park "Amphibius" in the spa town of Adler is located in the open. In total there are 11 slides of different levels and heights. The most extreme is considered Kamikaze.

There are several pools on the territory. Each has sun loungers where you can sunbathe and relax. There are several cafes in the water park. They mainly sell fast food dishes.

The oceanarium in the resort town of Adler is quite large. The total volume in aquariums is 5 million tons of water. In total there are 29 exhibition halls with 4000 representatives of underwater inhabitants of the seas and oceans. A transparent tunnel was built here, walking along which you can see beautiful bright fish and even sharks.

Hotel "Resort town" in Adler

This private hotel is 150 meters from the sea. Her exact address: Adler, Sochi, Enlightenment Street, 158/1. The hotel area is completely fenced. She is guarded around the clock. Access to vacationers is made only by magnetic key.

Near the complex there are several playgrounds, entertainment centers, an aquarium, a dolphinarium and an amusement park.

Within walking distance there is a market where you can buy fresh fruits and souvenirs.

This hotel is great for families. For an extra bed a day they charge only 100 rubles. The kitchen is fully equipped on site, where you can fully cook food.

Number of rooms and meals

The hotel has rooms with different levels of comfort:

  1. Standard. It is designed to accommodate up to four people. Two - in the main place (2-bedroom or 2 single beds), one - in the armchair-beds, and it is possible to provide a folding bed. The room has a plasma TV, fridge, toilet and bathroom.
  2. Superior comfort. The same number of holidaymakers can live here as in the standard one. There is a balcony with furniture, all the necessary equipment, own bathroom.
  3. Mini - a small room to accommodate 2 people (perhaps the accommodation of another guest). Appliances and toilet in the room.

Bed linen is changed every 5 days. If necessary, maids can remodel the bed upon request ahead of schedule.

The average prices for the summer in the "Resort Town" of Adler per room range from 1000 to 2500 rubles. per day. Meals are available for a fee. Guests can make use of the dining room.

Here you can order two or three meals a day. On average, for one adult a complete diet per day will cost 750 rubles. If desired and necessary, vacationers can use the summer kitchen for free. It has the necessary equipment and utensils.

Especially often parents who come with small children cook here. Because the baby may still have a specific menu and its own nutritional characteristics.

Vacation Reviews

Many tourists leave comments about vacations spent in the resort town of Adler. In their opinion, there are always a lot of people and very noisy. And also on the beach after 10:00 it is almost impossible to find a free place.

Vacationers like the developed infrastructure in the area. All the necessary facilities are in close proximity to the hotels. Tourists note that the prices in the private sector of the Resort town in Adler for food and accommodation are several times lower than in the center of Sochi.

You can find negative reviews about the spread of various rotovirus infections in the summer. But this problem is now encountered in almost all resorts both in Russia and abroad.

Guests of Adler are satisfied that all entertainment complexes are located in almost this area. Therefore, every day you can visit a new interesting place and not waste time traveling by public transport.

There are double opinions about food in the cafe. Tourists are still advised to choose places where prices are at an average or high level. So you can count on the good quality of the dishes and their freshness.
