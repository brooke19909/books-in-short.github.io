The technique of lazy patterns

If you find it difficult to create a scarf with complex manipulations and multi-colored yarns, we recommend that you familiarize yourself with knitted patterns with knitting needles. They are easy to learn and will not cause difficulties.

In order for the technique to succeed, adhere to the set of unspoken rules:

  • Check the number of loops after removal or tying to notice a defect in time.
  • Properly combine the color palette so as not to waste time in vain.
  • Try not to tighten the hinges too much, but make sure that they do not hang, as otherwise the product will not meet your expectations.

So, it is advisable to apply the above methods of knitting not only with the help of a strict summary of patterns. You can also come up with your own. In search of inspiration and future creations, a summary of original ideas will help you.

Rib scarf

People tend to buy more material so that they do not accidentally encounter a lack of color in the store. If you want to get rid of the remnants of yarn, then take the chance to create a unique product.

Experiment with color, as you are given the opportunity to make each strip or use a pattern of several different colors, widths and lengths. To use heavier yarn, it is recommended to use large knitting needles.

Knitted patterns with descriptions are selected for your taste, since the scarf itself implies a huge space for creative thinking. The end result should have an odd number of stitches and a shift interrupting one of the loops. All this is necessary to create a texture that will not constrict the pattern.

Even if a specific project uses the rest of the material for a scarf, the same type of offset stitches can really be used as the creation of blankets, pillowcases or other convenient items. Decorative ornaments - knitted with patterns - are also suitable for a budget gift option, if you use a rib.

A larger pair of knitting needles will provide a looser pattern, while a smaller pair will provide a tight stitch. For easier finishing, do not cut the thread. Instead, each time you get to the right edge, twist them together so as not to accidentally transfer. To change the width of the strips, add a new color, just weave the ends of the yarn.
