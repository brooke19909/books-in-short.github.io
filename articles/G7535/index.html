Performance first

Reviews for the Asus Zenpad 10 Z300C tablet begin with a description of the powerful Intel Atom processor. The crystal has 4 cores and uses a 64-bit platform. The amount of RAM, as practice shows, cannot be called decent - 2 gigabytes for such an expensive device are a minimum. But for multimedia and office applications this is enough. And for fans of demanding toys, it is better to look at tablets with a large amount of memory and a more efficient processor.

Users have questions about the graphics accelerator. The manufacturer acted dishonestly with customers by providing a gadget with an LTE modem with a PowerVR G6430 video card (like on the Iphone 5S), while in other versions a weak Mali 450 MP4 video adapter is installed. On the other hand, the user is still not destined to play productive games anyway, so there is no point in overpaying for a powerful accelerator.

Technical Games

Apparently, the manufacturer promotes only one modification on the market, and all other products are needed only to create competition in budget niches. We are talking about the tablet Asus Zenpad 10 Z300CL. Reviews of the negative nature of potential buyers can be understood, just compare the technical characteristics of all devices.





The main camera modification Z300CL has a 5-megapixel sensor with software interpolation up to 8 megapixels. For other products, the camera has a resolution of 2 megapixels. The same goes for the front camera (0.3 megapixel versus 2 megapixel sensor).

The manufacturer also played with the amount of internal memory, equipping an expensive modification with a 64-gigabyte solid-state drive, while younger products have exactly half the amount. It all boils down to the fact that the older modification is a more sought-after purchase in the domestic market (only the price can stop - 25,000 rubles).
