DIY tank cap

The hat is a fashionable and popular hat. Masters from all over the world have learned to not only sew hats, but also knit and knit.

Knitted hats are a great solution for those who do not like the assortment offered in stores. Having put a little effort into mastering the crochet technique and having sorted out several patterns, anyone even a beginner knitter can knit a hat on her own.

Tankman Helmet

A model that is in demand among needlewomen is a tank cap. There are several ways to knit such a headdress - in a circle with knitting elements with convex columns, as well as sewing parts of the decor.





Beginning of work

Knitting hats, like any product, consists of several successive stages:

Stage 1: selection of materials. If the hat is knitted for a child, then it must be borne in mind that the threads should be hypoallergenic, not prickly, have an even soft structure. The thread twisted, with the addition of angora or wool, is not suitable for knitting specifically for this model - the fluffy structure of the thread will not allow the hat elements to look organic and natural. Cotton threads or threads with the addition of wool or acrylic (not fluffy) are well suited for knitting. The hook should be taken one size smaller than recommended by the manufacturer if the cap should be stiff and tight. If the density of knitting is low, this should also be taken into account when choosing a tool - a too large number of the hook will make the hat soft and it will not keep its shape.

2nd stage: measuring the size of the head. To measure the circumference of the head, it is necessary to lay a centimeter around the head so that the tape runs in the middle of the forehead around the head above the ears and along the back of the head. Another convenient way is to fold the hat in which the child is comfortable, cut in half and measure with a centimeter the semicircle of the head along the bottom of the hat (elastic band or cuff).

3rd stage: calculation of the density of knitting. To calculate the density of knitting, you must first decide on the pattern. If several patterns in the header are used, then the design sample is knitted for each pattern. For measurement, a 10 by 10 cm sample is knitted. The resulting piece of cloth is washed and dried, and then steamed. After this, the ruler measures the number of loops, which is included in 1 cm of the canvas.

4th stage: first row set: if the cap is knitted from bottom to top, then to calculate the number of air loops of the first row, a calculation is performed where the head circumference in cm is multiplied by the number of loops in 1 cm. For example, to cover the head in 48 cm and at a density knitting 2 loops in 1 cm you need to dial 96 loops. If knitting is on top, then you need to calculate the diameter of the bottom of the cap. To do this, measure the height of the head with a centimeter from the top of the head to the middle of the forehead and multiply by 2. You can measure this parameter on the cap - the measurement is taken in the part where the cap is narrowing.

Knitting the main part

The tanker's hat is crocheted in a circle.



Knitting begins with a chain of 5 air loops, which is closed with a connecting loop into a ring. Then, 12 single crochet stitches are knitted inside the ring: three lifting stitches (replace the double crochet) and 11 stitches. The row is closed by a connecting loop that connects to the third lift air loop.

Further knitting is carried out according to the scheme. In each row it is necessary to make several increases. To make it convenient to knit, many needlewomen count the loops or use a marker - a contrasting thread or a special bracket that marks the place where the increase is made.

Amigurumi helmet

You can knit a hat in the amigurumi technique - then you do not need to knit lifting loops - knitting moves in a spiral. However, the pattern may be shifted, therefore, in this design, only double crochet or single crochet columns are used.

When the diameter of the bottom of the cap reaches the required parameter, the additions cease to be done. In a circle they knit a cap of a tankman so many rows so that the height of the cap becomes equal to the height of the head from the crown to the middle of the forehead.

It is most convenient at this stage to do a fitting on the model - the shape of the head and forehead height are different for everyone. So that the cap fits perfectly, a fitting is useful.

Hat design

Having tied the height of the cap, proceed to the design. In the front of the cap, where the forehead is located, knit pegs. To do this, they attach to the cap and knit a row of such a length that it is enough to cover the forehead (about 15 cm). Knitting goes in swivel rows, at the beginning of each row the necessary number of lifting loops is made (2, 3, and so on, depending on which pattern the pegs are knitted). Height - about 5 cm. At the end, it is necessary to tie the element "hand step".

In place of the ears, it is necessary to repeat the process - attach a loop, tie 10 columns and knit 10 rows in rotary rows. The length should be such that the strip covers the ears.

Additional items

The additional elements that make up the parts that make the tank cap similar to the real one include ear pads and compensating harnesses.





For overlays in the same pattern as the bottom of the cap, knit a small circle, then, knitting 5-7 rows of a circle, knit 4-5 rows without additions. After even rows are connected, knitting can be closed or continued by knitting in the reverse order of decrease. If you make reductions, then the element will turn out to be larger and denser. If you leave the hollow billet, then to give it a shape during sewing, you can carefully put the synthetic winterizer inside. It will act as a filler, allowing you to keep in shape and will additionally warm your ears.

Harnesses are knitted from rows of double crochets. You can also connect them with convex columns.
