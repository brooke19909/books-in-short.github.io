Hotel Citrus Parc Hotel 4 * (Thailand, Pattaya): overview, description and reviews

In Southeast Asia is a state known to all as Thailand. It is washed by the Gulf of Thailand and the Andaman Sea. It is this fact that has turned Thailand into a popular exotic resort. There are many excellent hotel complexes where you can not only live, but also have fun. One of those is the Citrus Parc Hotel 4 *. This is what it is worth talking about.





Location

Citrus Parc Hotel 4 * is located in the southeastern part of Thailand. And to be more precise, in Pattaya. It is about 165 kilometers from Bangkok. It has an ideal climate for relaxation. Even in December, the thermometer does not drop below 22 ° C.

So, the hotel is located in Banglamund - a suburban area of ​​Pattaya, located in its southern part. From here, you can drive to the famous Walking Street, which is the epicenter of the resort's nightlife, in 10 minutes. A paradise for lovers of nightlife and related entertainment.

But the most important thing is that you can walk to the sea in 10 minutes. Or rather, come down. There is a slope, but it is small. However, people usually do not get back in 10, but in 15 minutes. However, the hotel is still located in a good location. Attractions such as Pattaya Park Tower, Water World Park, Dongtan Beach are just a stone's throw away.





Services

At Citrus Parc Hotel 4 * care about the comfort of guests. That is why they tried to organize all the services that the guests of the resort may need. It has everything - from parking nearby and ending with free Wi-Fi.

In the lobby, the reception manager greets guests. It is worth contacting if you need the services of a nanny, laundry, dry cleaning, or if you want to ask something. The staff speaks not only Thai, but also English. And here, in the lobby, there is a left-luggage office and a tour desk.

The hotel has a good conference room with a meeting room, as well as a business center with a small office that has fax and photocopying services.

In addition to the above, at Citrus Parc Hotel 4 * for guests organize transfers, delivery of food, drinks, newspapers, and provide VIP services. Upon individual request, guests can be provided with a honeymoon suite or a family room. By the way, smoking in public places in the complex is prohibited, but for people who love cigarettes, there are separate places.

Leisure activities

In Citrus Parc Hotel Pattaya 4 *, you can brighten up your leisure time in different ways. Here, as in any other worthy hotel, there is an outdoor pool open all year round, a sun terrace and a barbecue. For lovers of more active relaxation, karaoke and a gym equipped with modern fitness equipment are organized. Anyway, everything is absolutely new at the hotel, because it was opened about two years ago.

There is also a golf course nearby. There is also a SPA center offering guests a wide selection of various services. Here you can try everything - from a restorative deep muscle massage to an entire spa program that lasts several hours and includes several treatments. In general, guests are usually introduced to the full list upon arrival.





Bars and Restaurants

On the territory of Citrus Parc Hotel 4 * (Pattaya) there are several excellent establishments in which guests are offered a wide selection of delicious dishes and quality drinks. The restaurant, known as Palate, prepares fragrant dishes not only from international but also from Asian and Western cuisines. And in two bars (in the cocktail and the one by the pool) they offer desserts, juices, alcohol and various drinks.

As tourists who were here say, staying hungry at Citrus Parc Hotel 4 * (Thailand) will not work. The food here is good, everyone will find something to their taste. Even breakfasts are varied - several dishes are changed every day. Serve a special sour-spicy soup with shrimp and seafood, which is called Tom Yam, pasta carbonara, curry with chicken. And this is just a small list of dishes offered for breakfast. Also, a cook prepares a Thai noodle soup for each guest - as soon as he asks. And, of course, there are European breakfasts here - scrambled eggs, omelets, pancakes, grilled sausages, fresh vegetables, pastries, coffee, teas, etc.

2-seater deluxe

Many people are interested in prices before starting to plan their trip to such a wonderful place like Pattaya. Citrus Parc Hotel Pattaya 4 * is an ideal place for a budget holiday. There are really modest prices. So, for example, a week of living in a stylish and modern deluxe will cost two guests 25 t. (with breakfast). And that is the standard price. But there are still discounts. However, in any case, it will be inexpensive.

The suites are decorated in warm blue tones and are 24 square meters. m. Inside there is a huge and comfortable 2 double bed. There is also a minibar, an electric kettle, a widescreen “plasma”, a cozy corner and a bathroom with a shower and a hairdryer. There is no balcony, but the windows offer a pretty attractive view of the garden and pool.

The room has cable and satellite TV, there are two safes (one of which is designed for a laptop), air conditioning, ironing facilities, dressing room, alarm clock, refrigerator. The bathroom even has a bidet. In general, everything you need. And also linen, towels and accessories for making drinks (coffee, tea, sugar, cream, glasses, spoons, etc.).





Other options

Also in Citrus Parc Hotel Pattaya 4 * (Pattaya, Thailand) there are such apartments as Grand Deluxe. In principle, the amenities and conditions here are the same as the previously mentioned room. “Grand” is perhaps a few square meters more spacious. For this, guests will need to overpay 5 t.

But many decide simply to book the Citrus Suite. This category in Citrus Parc Hotel 4 * gets extremely positive reviews . There are several reasons for this. Firstly, the area is 31 square meters. m. Explicitly more than in the previously mentioned apartments. Secondly, windows. They begin here from the floor and end at the ceiling. The windows here are incredibly tall and wide - all over the wall. This provides a great view. But you should not worry about privacy, because these suites boast the presence of automatic double curtains. In general, this room is more luxurious and refined. In the photo provided above, you can see it.

And the price of a weekly stay for two people will be approximately 35-40 tons. (also with breakfast). It is also worth knowing that in apartments of this category, even one child is allowed to stay for free, aged less than four years. However, "Citruses" in 95% of cases book couples in love or newlyweds.





Connecting rooms

Such apartments are also in Citrus Parc Hotel Pattaya 4 *. The reviews left by other guests inspire many to travel to this place. But many decide to come in company. Well, the adjoining apartments are designed to accommodate four adult guests (two children are allowed for free). Their area is 48 square meters. m. This large room is divided by an internal door into two separate bedrooms. The first has a large 2 double bed. And in the second - two standard, separate. Each room has a TV and all the amenities mentioned earlier, including the living area. And, importantly, the adjoining apartments have two separate bathrooms.

For such a large company, staying in this room will cost 45-50 tons. (with breakfast). This is the most profitable option. After all, if you calculate, in a week only 11-12 tons will go per person.

Important information

What else do you need to know about Citrus Parc Hotel Pattaya 4 *? Citrus Park Pattaya is a 4-star hotel that enjoys considerable popularity. That is why, if you want to stay here, you need to use early booking. To settle in the number that pleased, and not in the one that remained free.

When applying, you will need your personal credit card details. You can’t use someone else’s, because you will have to provide yours when you check in, along with an identity card. The hotel accepts several options. These are “Visa”, “Master Card”, “American Express”, “JCB” and “Union Pay”. You can pay in cash upon arrival. But the administration can block a certain amount on the card - as a guarantee of the arrival of guests.





About the rules of residence

Check-in starts at 14:00, and departure lasts until noon. If guests know that they will arrive later than 18:00, then the hotel should be notified about this by contacting the property directly. And upon arrival you will need to make a deposit, the amount of which is 2000 TNV. This is about 3,550 rubles at the current rate.

As mentioned earlier, children under four years old can stay at the hotel for free. And for babies two years old (and younger) the same rule applies. Only they will be provided with a cot (free). But for all the elders have to pay extra. The price is fixed - 1000 TNV. And it doesn’t matter whether it is a child of five years of age or an adult guest who came for company. But the apartments will have an extra bed. By the way, if you need it, you must inform the administration in advance so that they can prepare it.

What do guests advise?

The guests who were here assure that if you want a bright, carefree, but budget holiday in Thailand, then you should definitely go here. The best hotel with such a good location and conditions can not be found. Directly opposite the hotel building is a 24-hour mini market. And the other, with a more varied choice - in a minute walk. In front of the hotel there is an excellent currency exchange office, where the best ruble exchange rate in relation to Thai Baht in all of Pattaya operates.

Want some original food? You can safely go to the sea - there is a large market where tourists will find a lot of trays of fast food. But you shouldn’t pay attention to the name - there, for a little money, fish, kebabs and seafood are perfectly fried, right at the client's place. And on the market they sell a variety of fruits. In general, from the hotel you can quickly get to almost anywhere.





Reviews

Many people like this hotel. Smiling and good-natured staff, low prices, stylish and comfortable apartments. Some, however, do not like the fact that they are not equipped with balconies. But mosquitoes and insects do not fly in - this is a plus.

Daily cleaning, as well as a change of towels. Linen is covered with a new one on demand, and normally - every other day. Every morning after cleaning, replenished supplies of bottled water, coffee, tea, sugar and milk.

The hotel is small but well maintained, with clean green areas and a cozy pool, the ideal condition of which is always monitored.

In general, to summarize, we can confidently say that this hotel is perfect for couples, companies and honeymooners. Perhaps, in terms of value for money, the complex is the best in the south of Pattaya. If you wish, you can go here on vacation and see for yourself.
