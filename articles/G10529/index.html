Hotel Park Krestovsky, St. Petersburg: prices, photos and reviews of tourists

For a comfortable stay and convenient accommodation, most visitors to St. Petersburg book hotels in advance. The basic requirements that tourists make at the same time are quite standard: the proximity of the location of the place of residence to transport hubs or places of events, the maximum quality and quantity of services at a reasonable price, nice, clean and beautiful rooms.

All of these criteria are fully met by the recently rebuilt Hotel Park Krestovsky Hotel. It is located on the island of the same name near the center of St. Petersburg, in a forested area, right on the Gulf of Finland.

Hotel and its infrastructure

The Hotel Park Krestovsky Hotel was built relatively recently - in 2009, it is positioned as a three-star hotel. The building has the appearance of a closed pentagon with four towers and two circular extensions on the outside and in the courtyard. The hotel is painted in white and orange tones.

The hotel has 5 floors, with 292 rooms. At the same time, it can accommodate at least 350 guests. It offers rooms with park and car parking views.

A distinctive feature of the hotel is its unique location. "Hotel Park Krestovsky" is located in an ecologically clean area, surrounded by a green park and mirror ponds. At the same time, the city center can be reached in just 20 minutes in any way possible: by metro, taxi or own car.

Hotel Park Krestovsky: reviews of tourists

On the positive side, the hotel guests noted primarily their location in a quiet green area and the ability to quickly reach most of St. Petersburg sights by public transport. The second plus is the large area even of standard rooms. Many also mention the friendly and executive staff and the large free parking.

Negative reviews deserved monotonous breakfasts, a poor internet signal in some places of the hotel and the high cost of food in both restaurants. It is not recommended to check into the hotel during city holiday or sporting events - room prices are skyrocketing, and waiters during the influx of tourists do not have time to cope with their duties. Also, upon arrival, you should immediately examine the location of the smoking area, so that later you will not be surprised at the bill for the corresponding fine - here it is equal to the cost of the room in which you decided to raise.
