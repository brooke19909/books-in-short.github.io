Where to relax in the winter at sea? Several destinations for a perfect vacation

If you have a vacation in the cold season, but still want to relax on the beach and swim, you have to think about where to fly to the sea in winter. There can be a lot of excellent destinations, it all depends on personal preferences and the budget of the trip. So, where to relax in the winter at sea?



Holidays in Egypt

The country, located on the Red Sea coast, has been loved by tourists for a long time. Egypt is one of the countries where you can relax in the winter at sea inexpensively, especially in comparison with exotic islands. The average temperature of the winter months is about twenty-five degrees, and the sea is slightly colder - its temperature is about twenty-two degrees. The most popular resorts for winter holidays are Hurghada and Sharm El Sheikh. The advantages of these places are in a good ratio of price and quality, as well as the picturesque Red Sea. For example, not far from Sharm el-Sheikh there is Dahab Bay, which has coral reefs and many bright fish. Divers from all over the world go for dives here. In your free time, you can go on an excursion, because little can be compared with the Egyptian pyramids.



Thailand beaches

Another place where it is really great to relax at sea in winter is Thailand. Here you can find relaxation for every taste - from ecotourism to diving. And all this against the backdrop of a beautiful exotic nature. On average, the air temperature in the winter months is about thirty degrees, the water is never colder than twenty-five. The most popular destinations in this country are Phuket and Pattaya. However, almost the entire coast of Thailand is an excellent beach, so if you wish, you can choose a less crowded place. Good-natured locals, a high level of service and a variety of fresh vegetables and fruits at low prices will help to spend even a budget vacation.

Holidays in the Maldives

Maldives - a real treasure of the Indian Ocean. This is the place where relaxing in the winter at sea is a real pleasure. The temperature here is about twenty-eight degrees Celsius; the water in the sea is also incredibly warm.

Such a vacation cannot be called democratic in cost, but the impressions and pleasure received over the days spent here are definitely worth the money spent. On the ocean coast there are many popular complexes with the highest level of service, many of them have not only private beaches, but also SPA programs.

Amazing Canary Islands

Canaries are one of those destinations where you can enjoy the sea in winter and those who don't like the heat too much. Here, a soft, comfortable temperature, provided by the cold current and the influence of the trade winds. There are volcanoes on the islands, many places for interesting excursions, a developed entertainment industry and, of course, a beautiful ocean. The water temperature does not drop below twenty degrees, so you can swim even in the coldest months of the year.
