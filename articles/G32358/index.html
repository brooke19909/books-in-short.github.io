Holidays on the peninsula of Crimea. Kazantip - the best place for family holidays

If you do not know where to spend the summer most vividly and colorfully, then you should pay attention to such an old and familiar resort as Crimea. Kazantip is one of the capes in the Crimea. It can be safely called one of the most interesting and modern resort places on the entire peninsula. If you are tired of the usual cities, such as Yalta or Theodosius, and you want to visit a place that is not much different from the most popular resorts in Western Europe, then you should definitely visit Kazantip. And what to see and what to do, if you travel with children, we will tell further.





Cape Kazantip

Kazantip is well known to young people as one of the most popular club resorts in the CIS countries. Moreover, Kazantip is called the domestic version of Ibiza - no less famous youth resort in Europe. Nevertheless, although for most people the cape is associated with noisy discos and parties, where there are plenty of young people, on the Crimean peninsula (Kazantip), relaxing with children (reviews confirm this) is also possible. Tourists who have visited these places say that the peninsula is perfect not only to have an unforgettable time with peers, involving yourself in a series of endless parties, but also to have a wonderful time with a child, admiring the unique natural monuments of this protected area.
