How much is 5 rubles 1998? Varieties of coins and their prices

Those who start collecting coins should be aware that their value is not always limited to face value. Such samples come across whose price can pleasantly please the owner. For example, no one can immediately say how much it costs 5 rubles 1998. To do this, carefully consider the coin.

First feature

All banknotes that are or were previously in circulation on the territory of Russia are made at two well-known mints. One of them is located in the capital of our country - Moscow, and the other - in St. Petersburg. Despite the fact that the appearance of each coin is predetermined and strictly approved, samples are used in everyday life that have some differences compared to other products. It is these specimens that become the subject of lust for numismatists. But even an experienced specialist will not be able to immediately determine, for example, how much is 5 rubles 1998. To do this, he will first need to find out where a particular product was released. As you know, in the work of each enterprise, minor failures sometimes occur. Finished products in this case will not be considered defective. But it will remain individual characteristics that will make it unique.
