Hotel "Azimut" (Astrakhan): description, address, photo, reviews

Hotel "Azimut" is one of the best places for guests in Astrakhan, which is ideal for a pleasant pastime for different groups of guests of the city. Let us further consider the main features of this place, as well as some reviews of its guests.

General information

Hotel "Azimut" (Astrakhan) was built in 2016. Today it is one of the most modern and comfortable places in the city, where, according to tourists, you can stay for any purpose: with a working visit, as part of a tourist trip or while on a romantic trip with your loved one.

In their reviews, guests of the hotel in question often note that, while staying in its rooms, you can admire stunning views of the surroundings, the river surface of the Volga, as well as the alleys of city parks located within walking distance from the Azimut hotel.

The hotel has a rather high rating. So, based on the reviews of guests, this place to stay and rest is rated at 9 points out of a possible 10 (according to the specialized website "Bucking"), which is a fairly high indicator of the level of service and comfort.

Location

The Azimut Hotel (pictured) is located in Astrakhan, in the central part of this interesting city for tourists. Within walking distance from it are city attractions, as well as a lot of places where you can have a great time in the company of a loved one or family.

Not far from the location of the hotel in question is the Volga embankment, which, according to vacationers, is particularly picturesque. Here you can see the city berths, where you can constantly see unique ships that attract the attention of tourists from other cities.

The considered vacation spot is within walking distance from transport links, from which you can easily reach anywhere in the city. Moreover, just a fifteen minute drive from it is the international airport "Narimanovo", which can be reached by taxi or by ordering a transfer service at the hotel.

Near the Azimut Hotel there are a lot of entertainment venues, among which the most popular among tourists are restaurants and cafes (Rosemary, Izba, Almond, Cream Cafe).

The hotel "Azimut" is located at: Astrakhan, Kremlin street, 4.

Attractions nearby

As already mentioned above, the hotel in question is located in the historical district of the city, in which a huge number of attractions are concentrated that will become interesting to tourists. These include the monument to Peter the Great, the Astrakhan Kremlin, as well as the Assumption Cathedral, unique in its architecture. On the city promenade you can watch a unique sculpture of a shoe-polisher, as well as a monument erected in honor of the family. Near the hotel there is an urban planetarium, which can be visited by all the family members.

Rooms

The reviews about the Azimut hotel (Astrakhan) very often say that it is here that you can relax surrounded by comfort and coziness. Guests describe both the hotel itself and the attitude of the staff. The descriptions of the rooms of the Azimut Hotel (Astrakhan) made by tourists often indicate the local interior, which is presented in the apartments, as well as the quality of the furniture installed.

In total, the room stock of the hotel in question consists of 242 apartments presented in different categories of comfort: standard, smart, superior and luxury. Regardless of the category, any hotel room is equipped with a furniture set, necessary for sleeping and relaxing, as well as all the necessary equipment for living. Moreover, in each room of the Azimut Hotel (Astrakhan) there is a bathroom, fully equipped with furniture and sanitary ware, as well as bath accessories, necessary for daily maintenance of personal hygiene.

A modern climate system has been installed throughout the building of the place for recreation, which allows maintaining the temperature in the rooms at the proper level regardless of the time of year.

Standard

Checking in the standard rooms of the Azimut hotel class (Astrakhan), according to tourists, is an excellent outlet for those who want to save on accommodation, but spend time in a comfortable environment. The cost of a daily stay in a room of this class is 1980 rubles.

The rooms of the class in question are divided into two groups: single and double. Moreover, the Azimut Hotel (Astrakhan) has several standard apartments, divided into two rooms. They can accommodate two guests at a time, while in a regular room - only one.

The furniture of standard rooms, according to vacationers, is rather modest, but, nevertheless, you can stay here in comfort. Here, even in a single room, there is not only a sleeping place, presented in the form of a bed, but also a working corner. The reviews of such apartments say that this particular type is perfect for an overnight stay during a short trip.

Smart

Smart type rooms are designed to accommodate a couple of guests. As practice shows, it is this type of apartment that is in greatest demand, since it offers a pleasant stay in comfortable conditions provided for a relatively small fee - from 3,100 rubles per day.

The rooms of this type of Azimut hotel are equipped with two excellent beds, which are represented by one double bed or two single beds. In addition to the bed, the room has a clothes hanger, a wardrobe, a pair of bedside tables, a large mirror, a desk and a chair. Here on the wall there is a large plasma TV connected to a satellite broadcasting system.

Guests who are engaged in business often choose smart-type rooms, as they offer free access to the Internet, which can be used at any time of the day.

Superior

Superior rooms are well equipped with furniture and appliances. All of them are designed to accommodate a couple of guests for whom the bedroom has a king-size double bed. Near it there are bedside tables, and there is also a wardrobe. The superior room has a desk and a comfortable armchair for easy work.

As for the technical equipment of the apartments of the Superior type, there is a large plasma TV, which is installed in the second room - the living room. The plasma screen is connected to a satellite broadcasting system. There is also a landline telephone, hairdryer, fridge, minibar, ironing facilities, as well as Wi-Fi.

The cost of living in a superior room is 3600 rubles per day. If desired, the client has the right to include breakfast in the amount - in this case, the price increases to 3900.

Suite

The apartments, presented in white, in which a stylish classic design has been created, attract the attention of many people who want to move into the Azimut Hotel (Astrakhan). In reviews of rooms, people very often say that luxury apartments are designed for those who value not only comfort but also cosiness. According to tourists, the rooms of this type are very well equipped with both furniture and plumbing.

The suites have a large double bed, covered with expensive linen, which is changed every day. Moreover, there is furniture necessary to ensure the proper safety of the guests' clothing, as well as a safe and several lamps that fit perfectly into the overall picture of the interior. The second room of the apartment is equipped with a corner for relaxing and watching your favorite TV shows, as well as a workplace. The room also has a cooler with purified water and a minibar.

In reviews of these apartments, it is very often noted that living in them is relatively inexpensive - 4000 rubles per day.

Service

As stated in most reviews of the Azimut hotel in Astrakhan, the services and facilities in this place are provided at a fairly decent level, despite the fact that the hotel has an official 3 * status. Here, on a permanent round-the-clock basis, there is a reception desk, behind which you can always find an administrator in order to get advice on issues of interest.

The hotel provides daily housekeeping, while the high-end apartments are replaced with a minibar. Hotel guests tell in their comments that each room is soundproofed, which is undoubtedly considered a big plus.

If necessary, everyone can order a transfer service from the place of arrival to the point of accommodation and back, as well as excursions. A bureau for organizing such events is located on the ground floor of the hotel.

For the convenience of guests in the building of the hotel "Azimut" is equipped with an elevator, which everyone can use.

Infrastructure

Hotel "Azimut" has a fairly well-developed infrastructure. Without leaving the building, guests have the opportunity to visit a beauty salon or to hand over their things to dry cleaning, as well as to the laundry.

The hotel has a visa support department, in which, if you encounter problems with entry-exit documentation, you can get expert help.

In reviews of former guests, it is often said that Azimut can be a great place for temporary residence of city guests making a business trip. The thing is that here is a well-equipped conference room, which often hosts presentations, meetings with business partners, as well as other similar events. Moreover, on the ground floor of the building there is a business center where you can use office equipment, as well as access to Wi-Fi. All the services provided by the business center, hotel guests can use for free.

Recreation and Sports

Hotel "Azimut" in the city of Astrakhan offers excellent conditions for those who prefer not to sit still even while relaxing. For such a group of guests, an excellent massage area is provided here, as well as a fitness center equipped with a minimum set of simulators necessary to maintain good health. There are billiards and table tennis halls on the ground floor of the Azimut building, and there is also a library with a wide selection of literature.

Everyone can visit the fishing spots equipped on the banks of the Volga River at the site assigned to the hotel. You can take advantage of this opportunity on a paid basis by renting all the necessary equipment.

Nutrition

Hotel guests have the opportunity to eat without leaving the walls of its building, as it has an excellent restaurant and a lobby bar. Tourists in their reviews say that they offer an excellent menu, the content of which contains a wide selection of dishes of Russian, Italian and European cuisine. Every day a buffet is served for hotel guests, which can be visited by all those who have breakfast included in the payment for their stay.

Speaking about the features of the Azimut restaurant, tourists often recommend trying local dishes made from fish. Many of them are made according to unique recipes.

As for the lobby bar, the majority of Azimut guests are convinced that this establishment is the place ideal for drinking hot drinks, as well as eating delicious desserts.
