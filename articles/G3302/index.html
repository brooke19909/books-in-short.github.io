Simple and beautiful booties crocheted and knitted for a boy

Knitted booties for a boy will be a great alternative to expensive shoes, until the baby begins to actively walk. They will help warm the crumbs legs. The appearance of the product is more attractive than conventional socks. You can choose any manufacturing scheme that suits your parameters - the variety of options is huge. Crocheted booties easily.

The choice of yarn for the manufacture of booties

First of all, you need to choose the right yarn. It should be soft and pleasant to the touch, so as not to irritate the skin of the crumbs. Here are some of the threads you can make booties for the baby:

  • Children's acrylic of various thicknesses. This type of yarn is very soft and delicate.
  • Plush. It is soft and pleasant to the touch. Suitable for making booties designed for walking.
  • Often used decorative yarn: grass, boucle, thread with lurex.
  • Cotton is an ideal option for making the summer version of shoes.

Knitted booties for a boy should be decorated in the appropriate color scheme. The standard options are blue, cyan, white, green, purple. You can combine and combine several colors within the same product.

Design options for booties for a boy

Knitted booties can have a different design that determines the functionality and principle of use of the product:

  • Shoes-socks - relevant for the smallest. They do not squeeze the baby's leg and are very comfortable to put on.
  • Shoes-slippers - suitable for children who begin to walk. The product may have ties or other fastener options.
  • Booties-boots - should be high to cover almost the entire part of the leg to the knee. The product is suitable for use in the cold season.

Booties can have a different shape: flat or three-dimensional.

The option of knitting booties with knitting needles for beginners

The easiest option for making are marshmallow booties. You will need 25 g of yarn in two colors. This color is used to improve the contrast of the product. Need a pair of knitting needles and thread with a needle.

Knitted booties for boys schemes and description have all sorts. But the following are most optimal:

  1. Dial 30-35 loops. The amount will determine the height of the booties.
  2. Knit 80 rows of garter stitch - all facial.
  3. In the 81st row, you need to close half the loops from the original set. Now the front part of the boot will be formed.
  4. Introduce a thread of a different color, knit 4 rows alternately with purl and facial loops. It should be stocking.
  5. Then return the first thread and knit 4 rows with facial loops.
  6. Alternately changing the balls, you need to knit 6 strips of each color. The latter is not knitted to the end, but only 3 rows are formed. Next, cut the thread, leaving 40 centimeters of the tail.
  7. Thread the needle and staple the product. To do this, gradually stretch the needle and thread through the loops, removing them from the knitting needle. Thus, the workpiece is stitched in a circle. The lower part must also be sewn.
  8. Next, we form the booties sock. To do this, string on the needle with thread loops from each strip and tighten.

Lock the threads and trim the leftovers.

Finishing finished booties

In addition to color design, you can think of an additional decor. There are many options for finishing the product. Knitted booties for a boy can be decorated as follows:

  • Pick up a wide satin ribbon of the same color as the extra yarn used to form the toe strips. Fold the ribbon in a bow and sew in the center of the sock, where it was pulled together.
  • Weave a small pendant from beads and beads. Beads can be of different shapes, sizes and colors. After manufacturing, the element is fixed in any part of the toe.
  • You can knit jewelry from threads. For example, make a miniature bunch of grapes with leaves and fix the decoration on any part of the booties.
  • The actual option is embroidery with the help of threads, yarn, ribbons, beads. It is enough to choose a drawing suitable for the boy.

You can combine several decoration options. A bow from a ribbon on which a pendant made of beads is placed will look interesting.

Knitted booties for a boy with knitting needles can be made in accordance with more complex patterns - just understand the description.

The original version of crocheting booties for the baby

It is even easier to make booties with a hook. You need to take the threads (two colors can be), the hook of the corresponding number. Crocheted booties for a boy with a description can be made in accordance with such a scheme:

  • You need to start from the sole. To do this, you should adhere to a simple scheme for its manufacture.
  • Tie the sides of the booties. The first row of the transition is knitted so that the hook captures only the back thread of the loop. When using two colors, all work is so far done from one type of yarn.
  • Then a toe is formed (it is possible from a thread of a different color). Knit the first row with half crochets connecting in one loop. Each next row knit in the same way. As a result, the number of loops is gradually reduced.
  • After making the toe, the rest of the booties are circularly knitted. Do not short knitting in the center of the toe!

Tighten the top of the booties. Sew a satin ribbon bow under the tackle, a few beads down. Thus, the booties will visually look like a tailcoat.
