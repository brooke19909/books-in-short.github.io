Golden 5 Paradise Resort 5 * (Hurghada): description, photos and reviews of tourists

The Golden 5 City hotel complex, managed by Princess Egypt Hotels in Hurghada, has a vast territory. It combines several comfortable hotels. In the central part of the resort complex is the beautiful Golden 5 Paradise Resort (For Families Couples Only).

Its guests use the services included in the all-inclusive package for the entire Golden 5 City complex, which consists of seven hotels of various categories. It is not forbidden to use services requiring additional payment. This means that vacationers have full access to the infrastructure of neighboring hotels (by and large, they cannot get into their room stock only).





Guests, once in the territory of neighboring hotels, relax in bars, enjoy food in restaurants. They relax by the pools, bask in the sun loungers on the multi-kilometer beach, have fun in the water park. They are warmly welcome at the diving center and health center. Tourists with great pleasure use the services in the centers of water sports and horse riding. They are allowed to play on tennis courts, football and golf fields, and billiard rooms.

Those who are bored with entertainment in the hotel complex are sent to the resort town of Hurghada. Golden 5 Paradise Resort, a hotel with excellent service, becomes for them only a place where you can have a tasty meal, put yourself in order and have a good night's sleep, tired of outdoor activities.

Hotel Location

The comfortable Golden 5 Paradise Resort 5 is adjacent to the beachfront. It is surrounded by a dancing fountain, the main pool and lush greenery of vegetation, forming a magnificent park. The hotel is adjacent to the dancing fountains, a water park and a mosque, the Maritime Museum and the aquarium.

Next to it lies a street with shops, bars and restaurants crowding along it. A major shopping and entertainment center is a five-minute drive away. The central part of Hurghada is 18 kilometers from the hotel, and the international airport is 10 kilometers away.

Hotel Features

Golden 5 Paradise Resort is an all-inclusive hotel. Payment for apartments includes the cost of food and drinks offered by bars and restaurants. In addition, guests have the right to use certain types of recreation and entertainment programs for free. In addition, guests pay special orders for food and drinks and services that are not included in the all-inclusive package.
