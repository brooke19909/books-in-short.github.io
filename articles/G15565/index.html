Features Nokia 1100

And even today, this little girl with a black and white screen evokes wild love and respect among her many fans. Read on - and we will talk in detail about its characteristics, pluses and minuses.

Battery

The battery in the Nokia 1100 is pretty weak by today's standards, only 850 mA. But the phone consumes power sparingly, and you can get 16 days (400 hours) of standby time or 4.5 hours of conversation. But, strangely enough, buyers not only confirm these statements of the manufacturer, but also give even larger numbers. In addition, the battery is notable for its enviable durability - many users say that their device has been actively used for more than 5 years and still the battery holds a charge.

Scandalous model

With all its advantages, this model is a representative of the budget class. But on sales sites for the Nokia 1100 RH, they ask for several hundred, and sometimes thousands of dollars.

Why is there such a stir? About 7 years ago, news began to appear on the Internet that the software of models manufactured at a factory in Bochum, Germany (as indicated by the RH index) has errors that allow hackers to conduct illegal manipulations with it. According to one source, the location of such phones can not be tracked. For others, they can be reprogrammed and intercepted bank transaction codes in order to transfer money from bank accounts in an unauthorized manner.

To summarize

Today, Nokia, or, more correctly, Microsoft, is again selling affordable phones, the main function of which is calls. But in terms of quality and durability, they still cannot be compared with models such as the Nokia 1100 or 3310. Today, it is unprofitable for the manufacturer to produce a phone that can last more than 10 years. And therefore, despite the fact that the 1100 model has long been discontinued, it is still in active demand in the secondary market.
