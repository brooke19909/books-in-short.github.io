How effective is Samsung ultrabook?

Compact, hardy, and now trendy, ultrabooks are increasingly attracting the attention of buyers. Ultrabooks stand out not only for their reduced size, weight, extended battery life, but are also often used to emphasize the exclusivity of its owner. In addition, the latest ultrabook models have quite a lot of computing power.





Ultrabooks are developed by all the leading manufacturers of computer equipment. Samsung is also not far behind global trends. The Korean company produces several lines of laptop computers, specialized for various needs and different audiences. To make it easier for customers to make a choice, Samsung has divided its ultrabooks into three categories.

Ultrabook Samsung 5-series, like others, has excellent mobility. He copes with everyday tasks. A good example in this series is the Samsung 530U3C ultrabook. A discrete AMD Radeon graphics card is enough to watch high-definition video and simple games. The beech has a 14 "matte display with a resolution of 1366 x 768. A 500 GB hard drive should be enough for a collection of photos, music and films. For" notorious collectors "there is a built-in optical drive.





Ultrabook Samsung 7-Series is characterized by increased performance of a new generation 4-core processor. The presence of built-in and discrete graphics cards allows you to switch between economical "office" and resource-intensive game modes. 15.6 "HD display with 1600x900 ppi allows you to see the smallest details of the image.

Ultrabook Samsung Series 9 - the thinnest and most compact. The third generation of Intel Core i7 processors delivers excellent performance with reduced power consumption. 4th Generation Intel integrated graphics also allow you to perform everyday tasks and save battery power. The compact 13.3 '' HD display (1600 x 900 ppi) has glare protection and is twice as bright as conventional displays, so you can see a clear picture even in bright sunlight. In addition, these ultrabooks have an automatic brightness adjustment and a backlit keyboard for comfortable work in low light.





Samsung ultrabooks of different series combine unique technologies that make the Korean company a leader in this segment of computer equipment. The increased durability of the Samsung Ultrabook case is achieved using solid aluminum billets. The presence of an additional SSD-drive allows you to boot and "get out of sleep" in record time - up to 9.1 and 1.4 seconds, respectively. Monitors with anti-reflective coating and SuperBright function allow you to see a vivid picture even on the street on a sunny day. Thanks to the MaxScreen technology, the ultrabook screen increased by 1 inch, while the dimensions of the ultrabook itself remained record small.

Stylish and ergonomic, modern and elegant - this is how consumers describe the Samsung ultrabook. The price and performance of this little miracle of technology will satisfy every taste. And lovers of personality can order exclusive models of the Limited Edition line.

Among all the products in this category, Samsung ultrabook is one of the first places and is in great demand.
