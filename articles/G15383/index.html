Hotel Marti Myra Hotel Kemer 5 * Turkey: reviews

The last 2-3 decades, Turkey has become the most favorite tourist destination for Russians and citizens of other former republics of the USSR. Moreover, not only Antalya is in demand, but also other nearby resorts, on which deluxe hotel complexes have been built in the past few years, for example, Marti Myra Hotel Kemer 5 * in the elite village of Tekirova. It is no coincidence that wealthy people come here to rest, preferring comfort and silence.



Kemer - the pearl of the Mediterranean

Not all villages of this resort are expensive, like Tekirova, where there is just a giant five-star Marti Myra Hotel. Its history goes back almost 30 years. The village of Tekirova

As already noted, one of the best villages in Kemer is Tekirova. Marti Myra Hotel 5 * is located in it, it occupies a vast coastal zone. It has a great climate, not very humid, not asphyxiating and not very hot. All in moderation. The swimming season lasts from May to October, although even at the beginning of winter the temperature of the water does not drop below 19 degrees, so that daredevils can swim in the sea, while in other parts of the northern coast of the Mediterranean Sea this definitely will not work.

Anyone who has already managed to visit various sections of the Turkish coast will immediately note that Tekirova is a special resort, it is really elite, and therefore attracts wealthier tourists. Describing it, many use the word paradise. It is located at the farthest distance from Antalya, which means fuss. The local landscapes are beautiful : the azure sea adjoins a strip of light sand, then there are hotels, followed by pine forests and orange gardens, which fill the whole district with their aroma.



How to get there

If you are going to Marti Myra Hotel (Kemer), then know that you have to fly to Antalya. But it is very close, only 70 kilometers from Tekirova. Since the hotel is five-star, it provides all its guests with a shuttle service - very comfortable cars. Therefore, on the way to the hotel, tourists can relax and enjoy the beautiful scenery. By the way, in Turkey there are no bad roads.

Room Description and Service

Each guest of this hotel at the entrance to his room cannot hide his admiration from the picture he saw and the feeling that he is a welcome guest here. All rooms are furnished with comfortable, high-quality and beautiful furniture and decorated with beautiful textiles. There is a balcony or terrace. Superior Deluxe rooms have an electric kettle and a full tea / coffee set. All rooms have a free safe and minibar, flat-screen TVs, satellite channels, and direct dial telephones (for a fee). Marti Myra Hotel: traveler reviews

This hotel is a great example of five star accommodation. Getting on its territory, it seems that you are in a paradise.
