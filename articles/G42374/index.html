The best hotels of Nadym

Nadym is a Russian city that is the center of the district of the same name in the Yamalo-Nenets District. Despite the rather harsh climate, guests often come to the city. Someone wants to admire the Nadym river, the Azat Safa mosque or the church of St. Nicholas the Wonderworker, someone is working here. Where does a visitor stop? What hotels of Nadym can be called good?

Hotel "Lake"

In the center of Nadym, right on the shores of Amber Lake, is a cozy hotel "Lake". The indigenous people of this northern city believe that it is here that you can find the most beautiful panoramic views. Guests can please themselves with wonderful nature, silence, beautiful streets and a park located very close by.



There are cafes and restaurants nearby. Breakfast is served at the hotel, but lunch and dinner must be sought on your own. Almost all Nadym hotels operate on this principle. If you want to cook your own food, then Ozernaya will provide you with dishes and appliances.

Guests are offered rooms of varying degrees of comfort - from economy class to luxury. It is possible to accommodate large families. Each room has free Wi-Fi and a TV.

Convenient and the location of the hotel. From the airport to the destination can be reached in 20 minutes. By public transport or taxi from the "Lake" you can quickly get to anywhere in Nadym.

Hotels of Nadym: "Hope"

Very close to the airport (just one kilometer) is a comfortable hotel "Nadezhda". It can even be reached on foot.

On the ground floor of the building there is a cafe serving delicious homemade food, and a comfortable bar.

The rooms have everything you need for relaxation and living: comfortable beds and upholstered furniture, TV, shower, refrigerator, Internet access.

On-site parking is available, which guests can use for free. Polite staff on duty at the reception around the clock will always answer questions from guests and help to cope with any problems.

The advantage of this place is a pet permit, free ironing service and a hairdryer. Smoking is strictly prohibited on site.

Hotel "Agat" and "Iceberg"

Agat welcomes guests in the suburb of Nadym, the village of Lesnoy. The distance to the city center is only 3 km. Guests are offered rooms of various price categories. Private parking and cafes offer their services.

If you find yourself bored at the Agat Hotel and don‚Äôt know what to do, then head to the nearby Yamaloff Restaurant or to the Veterans Monument.

The Iceberg Hotel (Nadym) is located on the shores of Amber Lake. There are rooms of various configurations, up to the presidential (there are four in the hotel). The infrastructure of the hotel is impressive. It offers guests: a conference room, a gym, a pool, a beauty salon, a bar and a restaurant.

Three-star hotel is located a kilometer from the city center. Nearby are the cathedral mosque and the museum of history and archeology of the city.

Apartments "Finnish complex" in Nadym

Finnish Complex Apartments is 13 km from the airport and 23 km from the train station. It offers free Wi-Fi, car parking, and a kitchen with a fridge, kettle, microwave and stove. Towels in the bathroom and bed linen are included in the price of the apartment.

Children under 12 years stay free of charge for extra beds.

This is not to say that the hotels of Nadym are numerous. But they are all quite comfortable, modern, clean and offer a great vacation. Here you are always welcomed at any time of the day!
