Spa capsule: relaxation, healing, rejuvenation

SPA is a rest and restoration of vitality. And spa treatments are a special relation to oneself, one's appearance, health, and state of mind. Such procedures are vital for residents of large cities who live in stress, experience constant headaches, experience depression and know what insomnia is. In such conditions, rest, even for a short time, is especially necessary.



Spa capsule is one of the latest inventions in the field of general rejuvenation of the body. This equipment is a device whose dimensions vary depending on the set of procedures for which it is intended. This device is complex, designed for a specific set of procedures that are aimed at restoring the body, as well as relieving stress, chronic fatigue, physical and emotional stress.

The spa capsule, whose reviews are mostly positive, combines water, oxygen, infrared and other treatments. It is this complexity that has won such a love of clients.

Oxygen procedures here are represented by oxythermia - the effect (at elevated temperature) of oxygen on the surface layers of the skin. Since oxygen is an excellent antioxidant, its action is able to withstand the aging processes of the body. Its regular exposure helps the skin to be in good shape. Thanks to this procedure, the entire body is rejuvenated.



The spa capsule is also designed for water procedures - various massages, the types of which depend on the model of equipment. Complex massage acts on the whole body, enhancing blood circulation, due to which the cells of the organs receive more oxygen, which leads to increased immune and regenerative functions of the body. In the capsule, natural oils, salts, gels and other additives are used to enhance the effect.

The spa capsule (some models) has the function of infrared treatments. Such radiation is many times more useful than ordinary heat exposure, because it penetrates not only the surface cells of the skin, but also into the tissues, activating many centers of the body.



Procedures in the capsule help detoxify the body, restore biorhythms, cleanse and improve the skin, reduce weight, relieve muscle pain and fatigue. The spa capsule works in accordance with the classical methods of therapy in combination with innovative technologies.

The size of the capsules is correlated with the size of the solarium: about 2 meters in length and 1 meter in height and width.
