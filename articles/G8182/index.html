How to make a box out of cardboard the easiest?

Often, our compatriots have a question about how to make a box out of cardboard in the most simple way. This may be due to the desire to elegantly wrap a gift to his wife for the new year or birthday. And also, for example, they can be used to sort children's toys and put them separately for a boy and a girl. In general, situations that prompted a person to think about making such a fake. The technology of how to make a box out of cardboard is not so complicated, it is affordable for everyone. In the process of creation, you need a standard set of materials and tools that everyone has available.





Getting ready

Before starting work, it is better to collect materials and tools that may be needed in the manufacturing process in one place. This will significantly reduce the time spent on creating such crafts. So, we need:

  • cardboard;
  • triangle ruler;
  • glue;
  • a simple pencil;
  • paper for the design of the finished product.

Markup

Before you make a box out of cardboard, you need to draw markings with a pencil and a ruler-triangle. First, divide the workpiece into 2 rectangular parts. One of them should be bigger. The box itself will be made of it. But the second, smaller one, is the lid. We start with the first one.

We make two lines diagonally and determine the center of the base. On one of them we lay the angle of the triangle ruler and select the necessary lengths of the sides. Draw them with a simple pencil. Similarly, we get the remaining two sides. Then you need to draw the side rectangles of the package. To do this, continue each of the edges of the base with a pencil line. The length of all these lines is the same - this will be the height of our box. Next, the ends of the resulting markings are connected parallel to the base. You should get a cross made up of rectangles. Now it is necessary to supplement its two sides located at the edges with trapezes for applying glue. To do this, draw lines from two sides parallel to the side ribs. Each of them at the edges is limited by slices at an angle of 45 degrees. There should be 4 such trapeziums in total. Marking is also applied on the second blank for the lid. Only its base should be 2-3 mm larger, otherwise it will not fit on the lower part. Also its sides should be smaller.

Cut, fasten, embellish

How to make a box of cardboard, you almost know. Now it remains to cut both blanks carefully along the outer lines. Next, we make a bend along the remaining lines and fix the side faces with glue. We pause for it to dry. Now the question of how to make a box out of cardboard will not bother you anymore. But it must be decorated. And here it all depends entirely on the flight of your imagination, which in this situation is best not to limit. You can draw with a marker, you can use colored pencils, you can paint. But, as experience shows, in such a situation a set of colored paper is best. Under each outer part of the box we make a cut out of it and glue it on top of the cardboard. All this is done in one color. And then you can cut out hearts, flowers and anything from sheets of a different color, and stick them on top of the base. This can be done and the inscription if necessary.





Conclusion

So can be made not only gift boxes made of cardboard, but any other. At the same time, it can be made exactly the way you need it. Well, and in accordance with your preferences to decorate it. The flight of imagination is not limited to this - we take it boldly and do it!
