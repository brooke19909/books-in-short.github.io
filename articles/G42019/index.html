SJ4000 action camera review: specifications and reviews

In this article, we will consider the technical properties of the SJ4000 action camera, which has become popular due to its unique parameters and capabilities. It has a waterproof container, allows you to shoot Full HD at an angle of 170 degrees. It has a built-in 1.5-inch screen, in the configuration there are many different mounts that can be installed on various planes and surfaces.

Along with this camera, its modification is on sale - the SJCAM SJ4000 Wi-Fi action camera, which adds the ability to control the device via the Internet. The technical characteristics of these two cameras are the same with a few exceptions, so we will consider them at the same time, emphasizing the small details of their differences. In fact, these two modifications differ only in the availability of Internet access via a Wi-Fi point.

Key parameters and hardware data

The SJ4000 Full HD action camera has a 1.5-inch display, the lens of the device is wide-angle, at 170 degrees with 4x magnification. Inside the device is a Novatek NT96650 processor. Supports menus in 10 common languages ‚Äã‚Äãof the world, including Russian.

Records and plays video in resolutions 640x480, 848x480, 1080x420, 1920x1080. The video file format is MOV.

It can carry out a single frame, a photo with a delay, recognize facial expressions. It has a USB port. The SJ4000 Wi-Fi action camera, like Full HD, is charged from a 5V adapter, the battery capacity is 900 max. The device supports microSD cards. The weight of the device with a battery is only 60 grams.





Exterior design

The SJ4000 action camera review begins with the look. The size of the device is slightly larger than the box of matches, the thickness is two times larger. When assembling, high quality plastic is used. The camera fits nicely and comfortably in the hand, does not slip, fingerprints do not remain on it. The models of the device are available in various shades, but the most popular color of the device is black, which gives the device rigor and presentability.

The camera has four buttons: in front - turn on, go through the menu; at the top - start and stop shooting, on the side there are two keys that are responsible for the playback volume, image enlargement and acceleration (deceleration) of video scrolling.

SJCAM SJ4000 action camera: equipment

The device kit includes various mounting mechanisms made of durable and reliable plastic. These parts can be combined with each other, getting the necessary fastening. How to connect them is described in the instructions.

Also included is a steel cable for insurance that can withstand much more weight than the weight of the camera. In the package you can find double-sided tape for mounting the device on a fixed surface. However, it can peel off in the sun, so you should always be safe with a cable.





Airtight chamber

SJ4000 action cameras are equipped with a special aquablock, durable and reliable. It snaps firmly so that water cannot get inside. In addition, in a waterproof container there are chambers filled with air. This is done so that the camera could not drown if it falls off the mount during underwater shooting.

An additional back cover with openings on the sides goes to the aquablock. It is designed to enhance microphone performance in wet or dirty conditions when the camera is not used underwater.

Sound

The SJ4000 action camera, reviews of most users emphasize this, has a good sound: from almost five meters you can hear almost every word. Although some people are not satisfied with the sound quality. This, most likely, depends on environmental noise and build quality of a particular model. The recording here is accompanied by a superimposed music track, without the sounds of nature and ambient noise.

Photo and video

The photo quality of the SJ4000 action camera is low, it is better to entrust this function to ordinary cameras. The video is much better. Can record in Full HD 30 frames per minute. One second of shooting in this format takes about 2 megabytes of memory.

Well-developed function of night shooting. The image quality is surprisingly good. Naturally, nothing can be removed without complete illumination in complete darkness, but if it is still present, then the evening landscapes and scenes turn out to be of good quality.





Wifi function

The Sjcam SJ4000 WiFi action camera connects to the popular Google Play and Apple Store applications, but when connected to the Windows operating system, the device is not detected. A special USB cable is needed here. This is one of the most significant disadvantages of the device.

The camera can be controlled from a mobile device based on "Android" or IOS, the main thing is that the device is in the zone of Internet coverage.

To access the network, the SJ4000 WiFi action camera uses any access point it can find. No need to additionally purchase special cables to connect the device. For example, just go to any cafe where there is Wi-Fi, as the camera itself will connect to the network.





Size of saved files

At the maximum resolution (1080p) of image quality, the SJ4000 action camera reserves about 300 megabytes of memory for a 3-minute clip. 32 gigabyte microSD cards last an average of 5.5 hours of shooting.

If you reduce the resolution to 720p, then a 10-hour video can be placed on the same card. At the same time, the shooting quality will not be much worse than with high resolution.

Instrument Features

The SJ4000 action camera, in addition to the ability to record cyclically for 3.5 or 10 minutes, has the function of backscrolling video and shooting 60-120 frames per minute.
Often a camera is installed instead of a car recorder, as it has a loop recording function.





Device price

The camera described by us is popular among users. It can be found in almost every specialized store in all CIS countries. A device with a standard set of equipment costs an average of $ 80. The same Chinese camera with additional accessories (some mounts that are not in the usual kit or another battery) will already be priced at $ 100-120.

This model is so famous, convenient and affordable that it began to be falsified even in China itself. Therefore, in order not to buy a fake, you should purchase a camera from trusted sellers. Also on the latest models of the device they install a special logo with the inscription "SJCAM".





Conclusion

This device pleases its owners with high quality workmanship and low price. Of course, there will be people who find some kind of defect or who are unhappy with the functionality. But we should not forget that the product is fully consistent with the parameter "price - quality". Whoever wants to get a professional camera can pay hundreds of dollars for it. And this camera for its $ 100 has the following advantages:
- Full HD video recording in 1080p resolution.
- Waterproof aquablock that can be used at a depth of 25-30 meters.
- 1.5-inch LCD display that allows you to view the captured video files;
- There is a function to delete photos and videos without connecting to a PC.
- Replaceable battery.
- As an option, it is used instead of the DVR, thanks to the loop recording function.
- Has the ability to turn on at the set time and has a motion sensor.
- Projects date and time on a still image and video.
- The function to scroll back the captured video.
- There is HDMI - output to the TV, for widescreen viewing of the shot clip.
- It can be used as a webcam when connected to a computer.
- The Wi-Fi option provides connectivity with mobile devices and applications.

Thus, it can be seen that the device is fully responsive to its price. Reviews of most users of this device emphasize its great features at a low cost.

This matchbox-sized gadget is not only an entertainment device, but also a very useful thing. Sometimes you need to shoot something without attracting attention, secretly and unexpectedly capture a scene, then the camera we are describing will be an indispensable assistant. True, this is a video camera, the photos on it do not have the high quality that high-quality digital cameras are used to pleasing our eyes. But in some cases, in the absence of a camera at hand, you can use the described camera. But nevertheless, its main advantage is the quality of the shot video, both under water and in various natural and climatic conditions, with the possibility of installation in almost any place.
