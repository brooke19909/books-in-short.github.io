Sights of Moscow region: Pirogovskoe reservoir

Where to go on the weekend, close to nature, and not far from the city? Picturesque shores, amazing expanses of water, proximity to the capital, the beach and fishing places attract vacationers who are tired of the noisy metropolis.



On its banks, the Pirogovskoye reservoir has several recreation centers, the most popular of which is the Bay of Joy. People from all over the Moscow Region come here, because it has everything that is needed for an excellent vacation. Cozy gazebos, beach soccer or volleyball, excellent cuisine and plenty of water with many entertainments. Getting here is pretty easy: a bus runs from Medvedkovo metro station.

Pirogovskoe reservoir, a map of which abounds in good places for family vacations and fishing, invites you to a weekend. Having overcome only twelve kilometers from the Moscow Ring Road, you can fully breathe clean air and swim in the clear water, take sunbaths on the beach or have a picnic.



Pirogovskoye Reservoir is a reservoir with a length of approximately seven kilometers and an average width of one kilometer. Near the village of Chivirevo, in catchy areas, catchy places are located that attract fishermen. At this point (closer to Pirogovo), the reservoir is connected to the Klyazma reservoir. The complex bottom relief caused both peaceful and predatory fish to be found in the water column. You can catch it on almost all fishing gear. With a fishing rod you can hunt for perch, roach, scavenger, bream and other fish. However, as on any lake near Moscow, the nibble here is not always stable. Fishermen, of course, have their secrets that help them not to be left without prey any day. They carefully choose the bait, feed, that is, collect the fish to the point of fishing. It should be noted that the Pirogovskoye reservoir is considered one of the best places in the Moscow region for fishing perch. This predator lives in abundance, and very worthy specimens are found. In addition, you can catch zander, pike and burbot on a spinning rod from spring to late autumn.

There is another attraction that Pirogovskoye reservoir is proud of - the beach, or rather, the beaches, because on the banks of the reservoir there are both private paid furnished sunbathing areas, as well as public and wild ones. Here, vacationers themselves choose what they like best: perfectly clean fine sand with a golden tint or rocky shore, where you can stay in friendly company. The reservoir has a wide variety of water attractions and entertainment, sports grounds, rental points for boats, a yacht club. And there are drinking fountains, showers, toilets, cafes, cozy cottages for spending the night.





Camping will allow you to escape from the grayness of everyday life, recharge your batteries for work, fill yourself with optimism and cheerfulness, vivid impressions. After all, nothing calms a person as much as communicating with mother nature face to face.
