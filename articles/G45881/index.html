Blouse patterns: the simplest wardrobe solutions for all occasions

For a long time, wardrobe items remain the best ‚Äúweapon‚Äù of women, capable of winning men and defeating competition among rivals. This is an integral beauty tool along with cosmetics, manicure and hairdo, almost a part of the lady herself, who completes her image and makes it attractive and beautiful.





Of course, dresses and skirts will always occupy a leading position, but the combined options with trousers, suits or shorts should also not be overlooked. Patterns of blouses, so abundant in their diversity, make it possible for all women to create their own unique image to please the eye and emphasize the figure. This element of clothing does not leave the annual Olympus of fashion, and this year he returned also with gifts from the distant 50s - ruffles, ribbons, assemblies and basques. Therefore, the patterns of this year's blouses are complicated: the results of sewing on their basis are multilayered and abound in catchy decor. Ruffles can be on collars, sleeves and cuffs, and on the bodice. And light fabrics, flowing and flying, complete the airiness of the images. This is a cambric, chiffon and silk, and their translucent texture is also advantageously used to add frankness and sexuality to the image.



For curvy

Of particular difficulty has always been the pattern of blouses for full, because they do not fit tight-fitting options that emphasize the waist. Therefore, they are most suitable for such a figure, hiding all the flaws and at the same time not at all reducing femininity and sexuality, patterns of loose-fitting blouses with a neckline and one-piece sleeve. Of the fabrics, it is better to choose natural ones that allow the skin to breathe. It can be linen, cotton, silk. Another option will be lace inserts in the neck and shoulders, this will add to the image of elegance, femininity and grace. The risky choice may be the pattern of a sleeveless blouse, because you should not leave your hands open as a complete one.





Perhaps the most successful will be flying chiffon blouse models, decorated with ruffles in the neckline and cuff assemblies. The length of such blouses should reach the protruding points of the buttocks or be higher by a maximum of 5 cm. When combining this option with a pencil skirt and high-heeled shoes, the effect is unbelievable, because in this way femininity and lightness will be present, and curvaceous forms will be presented in in the best light.

For slim ladies

Patterns of blouses for slender women, of course, are much more diverse: simple shirt cut, and in the Roman style, and decorated with wave-like basques, which this year the famous couturiers Dolce & Gabbana presented to the public. Also decorative ribbons on a turn-down collar or stand have returned to fashion, which can be both freely released and tied in elegant bows. Sleeves-lanterns and vertical decoration with ruffles along the fastener on buttons are also relevant this season. This will allow you to combine blouses with jackets, and basques visually lengthen your legs, add sexuality, emphasize the hips.
