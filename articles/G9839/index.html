Heat and steam: Surgut saunas

In the cold climate of Surgut (Khanty-Mansiysk Autonomous Okrug), the absence of a warm sea and beach is compensated by the variety of saunas in which you can warm up your body and soul.

Surgut saunas vary in:

  • price;
  • types of steam room;
  • services provided;
  • the presence of a kitchen;
  • working hours;
  • service.

A visit to the steam room improves the condition of the body by regulating metabolic processes and improving blood circulation. Regular visits to saunas and baths help residents of Surgut resist viruses and strengthen their immune systems. A visit to the steam room has a beneficial effect on the mood, because here you can relax and unwind.





Residents of Surgut know a lot about steam and heat.

Surgut saunas in different areas of the city

Saunas are located in all areas of the city, so it’s easy to find the right one nearby and go there to relax:

  1. In the central area: “Berloga”, “Orion”, “Laurel”, “Torii”, “Charm”, “Catherine’s yard”, “Pearl of Siberia”, “Territory of the first”, “Tsar’s cloister”, “Knyazhevo”, “Berth” ".
  2. In the Northern residential area: “Bear Corner”, “Pyramid”, “Tunnel”.
  3. In the Northern industrial region: “Cutie”, “Eden”, “Coquette”, “Vavetta”.
  4. In the Northeast residential area, the most populated, there are most of Surgut's saunas: “Lux”, “Courage”, “Golden Three”, “Bellatrix”, “Palermo”, “Rubin”, “Trio”.
  5. In the Eastern residential area: Barvikha, Ideal, Friday, Orchid.

Price variety

Choosing the only one from the variety of Surgut saunas that will give real pleasure and relaxation, it is worth paying attention to the price range.

There are saunas in Surgut, which offer an hour-long visit at a price of 280 r. up to 6300 p.:

  • Nefteyugansk-Service - the most affordable rates, from 282 rubles per hour, accommodates up to 40 people;
  • 500-800 rubles per hour - “Barvikha”, “Livadia”, Stella ”;
  • 800-1300 p. / Hour - such prices are offered by the saunas “Borodino”, “Kit”, “Trio”, “At the Farm”;
  • up to 1600 p. - “Aura”, “Russian Bathhouses on Lingonberry”, “Noble Nest”, “Friday”.

Regularly saunas hold promotions, offering discounts on the cost of visiting:

  • “Tariff for two” campaigns;
  • for the birthday person;
  • summer promotions.

Surgut steam room

Choosing a sauna, many fans of this type of relaxation have their own preferences, choosing a steam room. Surgut saunas offer such varieties of steam rooms:

  • Finnish - low humidity and air temperature up to 110 0 ;
  • Russian, including wood, with the same indicators of humidity and air temperature;
  • in black - smoke from burned firewood enters the steam room;
  • hammam - high humidity at temperatures up to +40 ;
  • Japanese (ofuro) - is a barrel of hot water;
  • infrared - heating is carried out by the influence of infrared rays.

When choosing an institution, you need to determine exactly what type of steam room is preferable for you. In Surgut saunas, there can be from one to several varieties of steam rooms.

In expensive saunas, bath attendants serve visitors by adding or decreasing the amount of steam, as well as conducting massage sessions with brooms.

Many people choose those saunas that have a font or pool. Cold invigorating water is most often poured into a small pool; you can swim in large ones. The water temperature in them is at least +23 o .

Another great relaxing product is the jacuzzi.

Additional services

Attracting customers, Surgut's saunas offer various additional services that make your stay even more enjoyable.

Many establishments have cafes that prepare dishes of European or Russian cuisine for visitors, serve draft beer (Tsarskaya Kupel, Bellatrix, etc.).





Some saunas have a barbecue, where you can cook your own barbecue.

Exotica sauna attracts customers by offering free taxi delivery if the steam room order is more than 3 hours.

There are baths specially for women ("Amazonia") and men ("Lord"), where you can relax both alone and in a large company of friends and girlfriends.

What additional types of services are offered in Surgut saunas:

  • first of all, beloved by many billiards;
  • karaoke;
  • air hockey, table tennis;
  • hookah;
  • Wi-Fi
  • parking;
  • salt cave.

Almost all saunas offer guests comfortable lounges, where you can safely sit at a table or lie on sofas, listen to music or watch TV.

Many institutions practice the provision of health-improving services, such as simple and therapeutic massage, spa therapy, cosmetology procedures, aromatherapy.

When negotiating your visit with the administrator, you can arrange for guests to be shown a strip show or an erotic massage.
