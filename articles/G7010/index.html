How to create flowers from beads: a master class for beginners

Create your own unfading and beautiful flowers is easy. They will become

worthy decoration of your house and will complement the interior in an original way. Next, an instruction is presented for your attention, which allows you to visually see how flowers are made from beads (master class).

You will need:

  • beads of different colors to create flowers and leaves (in this case, blue, white, green and yellow);
  • copper wire, with a thickness of not more than 25 mm in diameter. It is sold in a sewing store;
  • wire cutters;
  • threads of green color.

Flowers from beads. Workshop for needlewomen

  • To create violet flowers, bite off a piece of wire with a length of 30 cm with a pair of pliers. The first row is as follows: you need to string 7 blue beads on a wire and twist it so that a ring is obtained. We remove one end of the working wire to the side and do not use this petal in weaving. The second row is performed according to the principle of the first, we take so many beads to surround the created ring. We make three rows of blue beads, and the fourth (edging) do white. The petal is ready. There are six such petals. Moreover, the paired petal is made from the free end of the wire left at the beginning of the work.
  • To create a violet stamen, bite off 10 cm of wire, fold it in half and string yellow beads in free order.
  • Put the petals in a flower. Insert the stamen into the center and twist the wire into the stem of the product. We make 9 such pieces. So the bead flowers are ready. A master class in creating violets will help even a beginner to beadwork.
  • To create violet leaves, you need to prepare a wire 60 cm long, fold it in half and string green beads, supposedly 15 pieces, and twist the wire into a loop. The technique of weaving a leaf differs from weaving a flower in that here we use both ends of the wire. It is necessary to string the beads on both sides, fold the two ends together and twist several times. So continue until the leaf is completely formed. We make 11-12 pieces of leaves.
  • We wrap the stalks of leaves and flowers with green threads so that they look like natural ones.
  • Product assembly: master class. Bead flowers are placed in the middle. We surround the resulting bouquet with leaves. We twist the resulting barrel with wire for strength.
  • We place the violet in a pot; for stability, you can overlay it with decorative stones.
  • Bead flowers are ready, the master class is over!


A huge number of interesting products can be made using beads! A more sophisticated weaving technique will allow you to create different trees and flowers from
beads. You can find master classes and weaving patterns on the Internet. It will be easy for you to master them, especially if you have perseverance and attentiveness. Create your own unique and original souvenirs. Give them to your relatives and friends, because only a gift made independently will be able to transfer the warmth of your hands to a dear person. I wish you creative success!
