Republic of Abkhazia. Pension "Sunny". Reviews



Abkhazia is an independent republic and one of the most picturesque resorts located on the Black Sea. Surrounded by the Caucasus Mountains, this island of freedom and pure nature does not seem to be affected by humanity. Almost all year round the sun shines here. Residents of this corner do not know what a polluted atmosphere is. Eucalyptus groves and sea air - this is what Abkhazia is famous for. Pension Solnechny, reviews of which you will see below, is located a few kilometers from the city of Gagra, in a village called the Cold River. Living in this holiday home, you can enjoy the beautiful scenery of the Gagra Range and taste the national cuisine.

Abkhazia Pension "Sunny". Reviews and Description

One of the most popular holiday destinations for couples and youth companies near Gagra is the Solniy boarding house. Here you will find entertainment for yourself and your loved ones, thanks to which you will not be bored all days of vacation. The guesthouse is located on a large territory and is surrounded by a pine forest. This area will appeal to those who come to Abkhazia in order to enjoy nature and clean air.



The hospitality of the Republic of Abkhazia will welcome you. Pension "Solnechny", reviews of which are extremely positive for holidaymakers, personifies the charm of this resort. There is a sand and pebble beach near the hotel houses. Water in the Black Sea on the Abkhaz coast is always clean. There are practically no algae and jellyfish. In the boarding house there is a complex for a full spa vacation, which includes baths, saunas and a massage room. Lovers of outdoor activities will love tennis courts and a sports field. In the guesthouse, guests are offered a buffet. There is also a 24-hour bar. For those who are not satisfied with the standard food "to choose", there is a cafe with high quality service. In the evenings, the guesthouse hosts discos, competitions, karaoke parties and much more.



What else is Abkhazia famous for? Pension Solnechny, customer reviews which say that there is no better place to stay near Gagra, provides guests with a shuttle service to the cities and sights of the republic. Each vacationer is simply obliged to visit New Athos, to see the famous cave with his own eyes and to visit one of the oldest monasteries. Also, you will not regret if you go on an excursion to the waterfalls and Lake Ritsa. Picturesque and beautiful in the summer of Abkhazia.
