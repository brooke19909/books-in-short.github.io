Hotel Bariscan Hotel Alanya 3 *: description and reviews of tourists

Turkish tourists appreciate Turkish Alanya for its mild Mediterranean climate, unique nature and rich historical past. Of course, the resort infrastructure in this city, popular with tourists from all over the world, is developed simply superbly. Local hotels offer guests of Turkey a very good service. This applies to the three-star hotel Bariscan Hotel 3 *.

This comfortable hotel is located in the suburbs of Alanya - about 9 km from its center - in the village of Mahmutlar. Housing stock

In total, 64 rooms are equipped at Bariscan Hotel for vacationers. Most of them belong to the popular inexpensive standard class. The hotel also has several family rooms. All rented rooms at Bariscan have:

  • air conditioners and safes;

  • TVs and telephones;

  • hairdryers in showers.

The floors in the hotel rooms are covered with hygienic ceramic tiles in a pleasant color. Provided at this hotel and Wi-Fi. However, guests, unfortunately, need to pay separately for the opportunity to use the Internet. Wifi costs $ 2 a day in the complex. Paid in Bariscan rooms and safes.

Guest reviews on rooms rented at the hotel

The rooms in this hotel, according to vacationers, are clean and comfortable. No frills in the rooms of Bariscan Hotel Alanya 3 * (Turkey) are not offered to tourists. But there is everything you need for a relaxing holiday. In addition to beds, tourists are provided with a large wardrobe, bedside tables and a sofa.

Some of the hotel rooms in the bathrooms do not have watering cans, but bathtubs. Bariscan Hotel also praises guests for this. As for the work of household appliances in this recreation center on the Network, there are both good reviews and not very good ones. In some rooms of the hotel, air conditioners, for example, function perfectly; in others, unfortunately, as vacationers note, they cannot cope with their task.





The maids in the apartments of this complex are not particularly cleaned. In this regard, reviews on the Bariscan Hotel on the web are pretty cool. Nevertheless, the hotel rooms usually do not look too dirty. The hotel earned serious complaints from tourists mainly for the fact that its workers too rarely change towels in the showers. Even throwing this accessory on the floor here is in most cases absolutely useless.

In addition to comfortable furniture, many guests include very beautiful views that open from the windows. Of most Bariscan rooms, vacationers can enjoy the sea.

What is the infrastructure of the hotel

Actually, on the territory of this hotel itself, its guests are provided with the following services:

  • Wi-Fi - for an additional fee;

  • outdoor swimming pool;

  • currency exchange point;

  • playground for table tennis.

Tenants of the complex can rent cars rented in Turkey in the free parking lot belonging to him. Pre-booking for a place in this parking lot is not necessary for vacationers who have rented hotel rooms. For children, the hotel has a playground.





Infrastructure Reviews

Living at the Bariscan Hotel (Alanya), according to tourists, is quite convenient. The pool of this hotel, for example, has earned good reviews from vacationers. Swim in it, according to tourists, is quite nice. The water in the hotel pool is usually warm. At the same time, it does not smell like bleach. Tourists include the pluses of the hotel's pool, including the fact that a water slide is equipped here. Of course, vacationers with children consider this particularly convenient.

The elevators in this hotel, judging by the reviews of tourists, are working properly. Travelers living in a hotel on the upper floors do not experience any inconvenience.

Many tourists do not recommend changing money at the exchange office of the complex. Experienced vacationers recommend doing this in the city. Not far from the hotel, near the market, there is a point offering exchange at a better rate.

Tourists consider some of the disadvantages of this complex is not a particularly large number of amenities for children. In addition to the slide and the playground, for them, unfortunately, nothing is provided for here. Even children's animation in this hotel is never carried out.

The staff at Bariscan Hotel are polite to the hotel guests. However, judging by the reviews of tourists, in this regard, the majority of three-star complexes Bariscan inferior. The staff at this hotel treats tourists staying here more neutral than friendly. Some vacationers also note the fact that the staff of this hotel is somewhat "lazy" and perform their duties.

How to feed at the hotel

Most of the guests praise the Bariscan's dining room. The food here is quite tasty and varied. And guests can take large portions in the hotel restaurant. The only thing some vacationers do not like is that too few meat dishes are served in the hotel's dining room. Most of the guests here are fed chicken. Sometimes tourists are also offered fish. But with all this, residents of the complex can take 2-3 salads, greens, about 4 types of cheeses, pasta, potatoes, rice in the hotel restaurant. Also quite often sausages and delicious, according to vacationers, stewed vegetables are served in the dining room.

Strong alcohol in the bar of the Bariscan Hotel 3 * (Mahmutlar), unfortunately, is usually diluted. But beer at the hotel guests, judging by the reviews, is offered quite good.





An interesting feature of this complex is that the dining room here is not indoors, but right on the street - under a canopy. Eating in the fresh air, according to tourists, of course, is much more pleasant. In the evenings, there are also fun discos for vacationers.

Bariscan Hotel 3 *: beach reviews

The sea near the hotel, according to many tourists, is simply excellent. But local beaches, including the one owned by Bariscan, have some of their own characteristics. The entrance to the water here, unfortunately, is somewhat difficult. Quite sharp stones can interfere with swimming at the beach. Especially careful vacationers should be during excitement. Scratching rocks on the hotel beach is actually very easy.

To make your holiday in Turkey more pleasant, tourists who have chosen the Bariscan hotel should definitely buy special bathing slippers for themselves and their children. This will make relaxing on the local beach much more comfortable.

The water in the sea opposite the hotel, judging by the reviews, is very transparent. Therefore, experienced tourists recommend that beginners also need to buy masks. Thus, although there are not so many animals in the sea in this place, it will be possible to diversify your vacation quite well.

For small children in local shops, including special bathing socks. Their dense lower part protects the child from hot pebbles on the beach and from sharp stones in the water. Experienced travelers also definitely recommend to beginners to buy such an accessory.





Hotel beach infrastructure

Its loungers and umbrellas on the beach of the Bariscan Hotel (Turkey) guests can use for free. In this regard, about the hotel on the Internet there are only good reviews. There are enough loungers on the beach of the complex to all guests.

Unfortunately, no entertainment is offered to tourists on the beach of the hotel, including volleyball. But hotel guests can visit the bar directly on the seashore.

City infrastructure near the hotel

Of course, tourists who choose the Bariscan Hotel Alanya 3 * for their stay can explore all sorts of interesting places in Alanya and its environs. For example, many hotel guests go to have fun at the local large water park Water Planet. You can spend time here very much fun. However, in order to get on some attraction in this park, as tourists note in their reviews, you usually have to stand in long lines.

Those vacationers who do not like to waste their time in this way would be better off to go to another, also quite large water park located in the very center of Alanya. The dimensions of this entertainment water complex are not as large as Water Planet, but its visit is cheaper. Riding a roller coaster here, among other things, is possible without queues.





Of course, guests of the Bariscan Hotel 3 * (Turkey) and all kinds of local cafes can visit . The food here, as many tourists note, is very good. At the same time, dishes at city catering establishments in Alanya are offered at not too high cost.
