Best places in Samara: TRC "Aurora"

Samara, as a good hostess, is getting ready to receive guests: the streets are being repaired, the facades are painted, the readiness of all entertainment complexes is checked. The 2018 FIFA World Cup awaits a large number of guests. Everyone needs to be accommodated in hotels, fed, given the opportunity to relax, have a good evening. All major shopping and entertainment complexes of the city announce their readiness. Among them, the Aurora shopping mall (Samara) plays a large role.

Center shops

Nearly 100 world-famous and regional trade brands are represented on the territory of the Aurora Mall. The trade center is the representative of one of the largest chains in Russia, the Lenta hypermarket. The format of trade in goods of local producers with low prices attracts many residents of Samara. Organizations specializing in high-quality goods of local manufacturers have placed their shelves next to Lenta. World brands of sports nutrition are well represented. For more than 20 years, Flex-Sport has been operating in the Samara Region . The quality of the products he sells is well known.

In Samara, stores of the Aurora Mall shopping center are represented by such major brands as Sportmaster, LEGO, L'Etoile, Ralf Ringer, M.Video, DNS and many others. For the smallest buyers, there are Detsky Mir, Imaginarium, Daughters and Sons, Pelican, ELC Early Learning Center, Kidsroom, and Shaluns.

Pet lovers and aquarium owners will find their goods.

Restaurants

Naturally, what a mall without McDonald's, KFC and BurgerKing. The city has a well-developed network of less well-known eateries in the capital. So, Samara shopping and entertainment complex "Aurora" is famous for its chain of restaurants "Eastern Bazaar", the Tatar fast-food chain "Tyubetei". Perhaps the proximity to Kazan affects it, but the city chefs are very good at preparing national dishes. Not without Georgian hospitality with the cafe-hinkalny "Hops Suneli."

Lovers of solitude can sit in cozy coffee shops and have a cup of coffee. Fans of foamy are friendly opened by Pub 47. In a city famous for its beer, it is difficult to compete, but this pub successfully demonstrates the quality of its drinks and the opportunity to watch a sports match in a noisy company. Football fans do not pass him by.

Kinomax

For moviegoers, one of the leaders of the Kinomax movie theater chain opened its doors. There are 6 cinema halls in the Aurora shopping center in Samara, while 797 spectators can watch movies at the same time. For VIP-guests there is a separate comfortable room for 47 people. At your service there is a conference bar, a games area. Near restaurants, cafes. Online ticketing is available. Without leaving your home, you can pre-order tickets and calmly enjoy your favorite movie.

All the halls of the cinema of the Samara shopping and entertainment complex Aurora are equipped with the latest technology. For display in 3D, innovative RealD 3D equipment and a high-quality DolbiDigital sound system are installed. Large widescreen screens, comfortable ergonomic chairs - everything is provided for the convenience of the audience.
