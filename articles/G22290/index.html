Hotel Ivana Palace 4 * (Bulgaria / Sunny Beach): tourist reviews

If you want to go on a budget vacation to Bulgaria, then you can safely choose the Ivana Palace 4 * hotel as a temporary residence. Perhaps, in terms of combining price and quality, this is one of the best options in the eastern part of the country. And the advantages of the hotel can be described in more detail.





general information

So, Ivana Palace 4 * is located in Sunny Beach. This is the largest and most famous resort in all of Bulgaria. Its characteristic feature is a sandy beach stretching 10 kilometers into the distance.

From the hotel you can walk to the sea in a couple of minutes. Nearby is a bus stop, so if you want to go somewhere you will not need to spend money on a taxi. And there are many attractions nearby. Although, many of them can be reached on foot. A nightclub called Orange Disco is 300 meters away. A kilometer away is the notorious Chaika Beach. A little further - the Royal Beach Mall, which can be considered a paradise for shopaholics. At a distance of 1.3 kilometers there is a water park, and a 5-minute drive away is the old Nessebar, which is a UNESCO-protected attraction.

The nearest airport to the hotel is in Burgas. It is about 35 kilometers away. Not that far. In general, the location at Ivana Palace 4 * is favorable - it is difficult to disagree with this.

Ivana Palace 4 * is a modern hotel, so it has everything a tourist needs. Free Wi-Fi is available throughout the complex; there is a camera in the lobby for storing things and a currency exchange office. There is also a laundry room where, in addition to washing, ironing clothes, private parking, a small business center and a modern conference room with multimedia equipment, projectors, improved sound insulation and high-speed Internet.

By the way, a few words should be said about the staff. He is multilingual here. The hotel staff speaks Bulgarian, German, English, French and, what pleases most of all, Russian.





Fun and relaxation

In Ivana Palace 4 * (Bulgaria), leisure can be brightened up in different ways. Of course, the vast majority of guests spend their time on the beach. The hotel also has an excellent swimming pool and terrace, where guests like to sunbathe.

Many come here with children. And you can not worry about their pastime. After all, the complex has a separate playground for the smallest, a shallow pool and a mini club, in which animators and tutors watch the children.

The hotel also has a health club with a jacuzzi, sauna and fitness center. By the way, specialists working there make excellent reflex and relaxing massages. In general, you won‚Äôt have to be bored - everyone will find what to do with himself when he is at the hotel.

Important information

Hotel Ivana Palace 4 * begins to register its guests from 13:00. They should leave before noon. But many are interested in the question of what to do in case of early arrival. The answer is wait. But this is rarely done, as guests are usually populated immediately, because there are a lot of rooms in the hotel. In addition, the complex itself is resettled only at the very peak of the season.

You can stay with children at the hotel, but you do not need to pay only for those who are not 2 years old. All who are older are considered adult guests occupying a separate full-fledged place. By the way, if the baby needs a crib, then this nuance should be indicated in special wishes. And when making a reservation, you will need credit card details. The hotel accepts Visa, MasterCard and JCB.





Tourists on vacation

People who visited Ivana Palace 4 * (Sunny Beach) are happy to share their impressions. The vast majority of holidaymakers make the choice in favor of this particular hotel because of its declared "star rating", other good reviews and more than reasonable prices.

Especially good here in the last weeks of the season - at the end of September. It is advisable to pay half board, as many cafes, bars and restaurants are closed. After all, tourists are becoming less and less every day at the resort. And this is for lovers of a relaxing holiday, because in September in Sunny Beach a peaceful atmosphere sets in. And the hotel is not relocated.

Many people note the excellent knowledge of the Russian language by the staff. And besides, employees speak German and English with dignity, as people who have the opportunity to assess their knowledge of these skills say. By the way, not only local channels are broadcast on TV. You can find Russian and German. However, it is unlikely that someone, while on vacation, actively watches television.

The rooms are simple, good, but with a rather weak soundproofing. If even at the height of the season you want to be in silence, you need to ask for apartments in the depths of the corridor. The rooms there are really no noise.





General impressions

In general, about the rest in Ivana Palace 4 * reviews leave mostly positive. The apartments are cleaned daily and honestly. The maids are very tactful and polite - they always tidy up the rooms when guests relax on the beach or have breakfast. All the little things, like a safe and a refrigerator in the apartments, are free, and not like in many other hotels. In the rooms themselves, everything is thought out to the smallest detail - there is even a socket for an electric shaver. And on the balcony there is a table with chairs, an ashtray and even a clothes dryer.

And the pool is not only large, but also clean. His condition is constantly monitored here, and the smell of bleach is not at all felt. The depth, of course, is not very large - about one and a half meters. But the descent to it is convenient. In general, to freshen up - that's it.

Tourists are advised to take beach towels with them on vacation, as they are not issued at the hotel. There are sun loungers near the sea, but paid. If you do not want to splurge, you can throw a towel - the beach is clean, so it's nice to lie on the sand. There are jellyfish and algae in the sea, but in August. This should not be surprising. After all, the sea is Black, and this is its characteristic feature.

In general, if you want to relax on a budget in a good place, you can go here. Many people were satisfied and even decided to return to Ivana Palace.
