The resort town of Chernomorets, Bulgaria: description and reviews of tourists

This resort town is located on the Black Sea. Despite its small size, it is cozy and comfortable and ideal for a relaxing family vacation. About two and a half thousand people live here permanently.

The city of Chernomorets (Bulgaria) is located in the Burgas region, thirty kilometers from the international airport. Next door (8 km) are the resorts Dunes and Sozopol.





History

Compared to other resorts of the country Chernomorets (Bulgaria) has a not so ancient history. The first settlements on these lands appeared in 1728. Then it was a very small fishing village of St. Nicholas. In honor of the patron saint of sailors and fishermen, a magnificent Orthodox church was built in the city.

City Chernomorets (Bulgaria): description

The picturesque and very green town of Chernomorets is a great place for lovers of relaxation on the less crowded coast. There are five beaches that are separated by beautiful rocky shores. A kilometer from the city is the picturesque Vromos Bay, which was chosen by lovers of underwater fishing. And two kilometers from Chernomorets there is a beautiful beach, one of the best in the country - camping Gradina, which was previously called the Royal Beach.





Chernomorets (Bulgaria), the photo of which you can see in this article, is mainly built up with one-story buildings. It is very cozy and green - in every courtyard there are a lot of flowers and shrubs. Today this resort is still developing. On shady streets there are a lot of cozy restaurants and cafes, from the windows of which panoramas of the city and the sea open.

Chernomorets is a great place for recreation for lovers of fishing, diving, surfing, riding on jets and scooters, boat trips on yachts. And children will certainly be struck by an inflatable water castle.

Chernomorets is famous far beyond its borders for its numerous flower beds and green spaces. This small town has several parks and squares. There are no huge and noisy hotels, as well as a large concentration of holidaymakers, which is typical for larger resort cities of the country.

What to see?

Many vacationers traveling to this sunny country do not know what to see in Chornomorets (Bulgaria). First of all, it is magnificent nature, small cozy bays on the coast, beautiful parks and shady squares. But the main attraction of the city is an amazing temple, consecrated in honor of St. Nicholas.





It is not at all like other similar structures. His yard has been turned into a real paradise. Each centimeter is planted with amazing plants. Orchids and lotuses, marsh irises and lemon trees, paradise apple and kiwi are just some of the inhabitants of this marvelous garden.

The temple was erected, as they say, by the whole world, and its construction was completed in 1858. To worship the shrine of the temple - the holy relics of Nicholas - pilgrims came here not only from all over Bulgaria, but also from abroad. In 1994, a terrible fire destroyed almost all the interior decoration, but many considered it a great miracle that the fire did not touch the wooden cross and the icon of St. Nicholas. For voluntary donations of citizens and residents of other regions of the country, the temple was restored in a very short time.

But in 2014, the world community was shocked by the terrible sacrilege. The temple was hacked and the relics of St. Spyridon and St. Nicholas were stolen from it. In order to somehow calm the feelings of believers, the Italian city of Bari sent an ark from the world of St. Nicholas the Wonderworker. The Moscow Patriarchate of the Russian Orthodox Church also helped. She donated to the cloister the relics of eleven saints revered and respected in our country.

Relaxation

There are not many tourists from Russia in Chernomorets. Bulgarians and residents of other countries of Eastern Europe like to spend their holidays here. This is due to the fact that in the described town tourists are organizing leisure activities on their own - they rent housing, plan leisure activities. Nevertheless, Chernomorsk (Bulgaria) can provide all guests with a decent and varied vacation. There are two amusement parks, many restaurants and cafes that offer national dishes, water rides on the beach, you can go fishing in the sea or take a walk in the local botanical garden.

Most hotels offer a variety of excursions, including to neighboring Turkey. Chernomorets (Bulgaria), reviews about the rest in which is always positive, has several beaches. They are located in small sea bays and are distinguished by cleanliness and grooming. It is very comfortable to relax with young children here: the entrance to the sea is smooth, the depth is increasing gradually.

In southern Bulgaria, the summer is early, so the holiday season begins already on the twentieth of May and ends in the middle, and sometimes at the end of October. The best time to relax is June and September.

Hotels

In Chernomorets there are no very large hotels and hotels. Some vacationers consider this a disadvantage of the resort, while others consider it a virtue. In addition, all the hotels offered for accommodation in Chernomorets (Bulgaria) are distinguished by comfort and a high level of service.

Kosko 4 *

This wonderful family-run hotel is located in the city center. It has a summer garden with a terrace and an outdoor pool. The central city beach is a five-minute walk from the hotel, and the bus station is a hundred meters from the hotel. The coastal resort town of Sozopol with its famous beaches is just eight kilometers from the hotel.





Guest rooms are equipped with LCD cable TVs, air conditioning and work areas. Wi-Fi (free) is available throughout. A buffet breakfast is served daily. The lobby bar features a daily press. Shops, bars and restaurants are located nearby.



Malibu 4 *

Another family-run hotel is a ten minute walk from the beach. Here, for guests, a terrace (on the roof) has been created, on which there are necessary barbecue facilities. This is a mini-hotel in which there are only three comfortable and cozy rooms. All are equipped with 32-inch flat-screen TVs. Each room has a private bathroom with a hydromassage shower.





St. Nicholas Complex 5 *

This wonderful complex is located on the seafront, between Chernomorets (3 km) and Sozopol (8 km). It has fifty rooms of different levels of comfort. Some have kitchenettes, washing machines and LCD TVs.





Vacationers can visit the physiotherapy rooms, a swimming pool located in a beautiful landscaped garden, a bar. The restaurant of the complex offers a varied menu, which includes dishes of national and European cuisines. The pool and terrace serve drinks and snacks. Guests can use the sauna with a hot tub and work out in the fitness room (surcharge). A 24-hour front desk can arrange car rental.

Children's camp

Chernomorets (Bulgaria) became known to tourists in 2013, when an international children's and youth camp was opened here. "VIP Chernomorets" - a children's camp in Bulgaria - the best place to relax for children of different ages. Young guests from different countries feel at home here. Great mild climate, not too tiring flight, excellent cuisine and a unique opportunity to meet new friends every year attract groups of children and youth, including from our country.

The children's camp "Chernomorets" (Bulgaria) is located on the seashore, seven kilometers from the museum city of Sozopol and twenty-five kilometers from Burgas. Children between the ages of seven and seventeen are accommodated in the modern Murite Beach complex. It is located on the first line from the sea, in a quiet location, within walking distance from the resort center.





The camp "Chernomorets" (Bulgaria) has its own guarded beach, which is located in front of the complex. There are one hundred umbrellas for vacationers. From 8:00 to 18:00 rescuers and security staff are on duty at the beach. The camp hosts beach volleyball and soccer competitions. Children can visit the cafe, which offers sweets, fruits and soft drinks. The road to the beach is reduced if you go to him on a water slide.





Apartments have been prepared to accommodate young guests. Three to four people live in the room.
