Weaving baubles for beginners. Options from different materials

Each person should have some special occupation that will help to spend their free time usefully and fill their life with something interesting and unusual. Such a hobby can be, for example, making baubles, which have recently become increasingly popular. They are also called friendship bracelets. Such a product can even become a real work of art. However, first, of course, you need to master the weaving of baubles for beginners.



A bit of history

The tradition of wearing woven bracelets came to us from a single tribe of North American Indians. It was believed that the person who received this thing as a gift should definitely wear it until the bracelet breaks. This tradition was borrowed by hippies who, having exchanged baubles, considered themselves called brothers. Now these products are widely used. They are not only made with their own hands, but also bought. Weaving baubles for beginners is also available. But it should be remembered that such bracelets were constantly worn because the one who weaved them, according to popular beliefs, put part of his soul, warmth, friendship or even love into the jewelry. As long as the bauble is whole, she can guard her owner. Now such bracelets can also be a symbol of mood, musical addictions, and simply a stylish decoration.



What do the colors symbolize

Initially, baubles could tell a lot about the person wearing them, his qualities and social status. However, due to the wide distribution of such bracelets, now they can have different meanings in different companies and groups. So, the white color here usually symbolizes freedom, sophistication, the road and so on. And red can mean love, joy, blood, fire, energy; blue - peace, sky, calm, friendliness, cleanliness. Of particular importance are combinations of different colors. Red and white indicate eternal love, red with green - love of nature, blue with green - the track.

How to make baubles



For the manufacture of bracelets simple materials are used: threads or beads. Of the threads, floss is most often used, since they are more durable and durable. You can weave a bauble also from modern materials, for example, from wires.

Usually, all types of weaving baubles are divided into two groups: direct or oblique (mosaic). They are not too different in complexity. You can easily find simple lessons that describe weaving baubles for beginners, and more complex patterns. Experiment and learn something new! There are also patterns for weaving baubles with names. Such gizmos are very popular among teenagers. For recognition of their feelings, any other scheme of baubles from a mouline thread is suitable . All of them are quite interesting and diverse, they will help to create a unique and stylish gift for a friend or girlfriend, beloved or just a pleasant acquaintance.

Most often, in order to start weaving, it is not necessary to have so many: floss threads of different colors, scotch tape, scissors, desire and inspiration.

Weaving baubles for beginners is a pleasant and exciting experience. With a little training, it will be possible to make inexpensive gifts for various holidays, not only cute, but also with a certain meaning embedded in them, which will make them truly unique.
