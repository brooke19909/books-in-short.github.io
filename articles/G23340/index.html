How to crochet booties: for beginners to help

How to crochet booties? For beginner knitters, this presents certain difficulties. Looking at beautiful, fishnet socks, you want to make the same for your sister, daughter, niece or just for a newborn baby friends. But where to start? What threads to choose and how to knit? We will try to answer all the questions!

Crocheted booties . For beginners tips. Choose yarn



If this is your first product for babies, then most likely you will definitely have a question about which threads are best to use ... You need to decide what time of year and where the baby will wear them. If these are openwork and lightweight home socks, in which the child crawls or lies in the crib, then cotton yarn is enough or synthetic thread can be used. All of the above applies to the summer period, when there is a great risk of overheating the baby. In the cooler season, it is worth using woolen or half-woolen threads. In this case, crochet booties for beginners will be much faster and easier to knit. Indeed, on warm things there is no sense in knitting an openwork pattern or frills. Warm booties should be really warm.

How to knit booties

Some needlewomen use patterns. Crocheting booties becomes easy and enjoyable. And others, on the contrary, knowing the dimensions of the finished product, knit what is called "by eye". It is difficult to say which of the manufacturing methods is more correct. If the booties are warm, then you can do without a scheme. But if it is planned to manufacture an openwork version, then, most likely, a clear description of the work can not do. More experienced craftswomen can simply draw the planned pattern, and then knit it according to the outline. Also, for them, it is usually enough to have only a clear photo or a pair of amazing booties seen somewhere. Given that the product itself is uncomplicated, connecting it is not difficult. There are several ways to do this. Consider one of them.



First, determine how large the planned booties will be. Then we knit the sole with a single crochet. It is not necessary to make an anatomical shape. For the baby, a simple oval sole is enough. The main thing is that it fits the size. Then carefully follow several rows of double crochets to form the height of the future booties. Half the booties have already been crocheted. For beginner craftswomen - quite a tangible achievement. Continue on. After making several rows, close the edge. After that, you can either knit the top, starting it with the toe of the booties, or knit separately and fasten with threads and a needle. After you managed to close the sock, you can start forming the final part of the booties. Tie it so that it is possible to thread a satin ribbon or tie a chain of air loops and use it to fix it.

How to decorate booties for a newborn? Crochet tie ears



Having selected and knitted a product, it is necessary to decorate it. Someone does this with beads or ribbons. However, you can go the other way. Tie up funny ears and, having sewn them to the finished booties, having drawn a muzzle, you will get a real bunny! In the same way, you can make the face of a kitten or a fox. It all depends on your imagination!
