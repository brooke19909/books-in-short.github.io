Hotel Caves Beach Resort 5 *: reviews and description

In this article we will consider the hotel Caves Beach Resort 5 *. Reviews of tourists will help to make a brief overview of the rooms and the hotel, the services that are provided to guests here.

Where is the hotel situated?

There is an institution in Egypt. The nearest major city, located 10 kilometers north of the Caves Beach Resort 5 * hotel, is Hurghada, and the airport is about 15 kilometers away.

Short hotel description

The hotel was opened in January 2014, at the moment two buildings of the Caves Beach Resort 5 * are already operating. Reviews of tourists warn that construction work is still ongoing here, delivering some inconvenience to vacationers. The hotel is made in an unusual and unique stylization - in the form of mountain caves, its area is 40,000 square meters. m





Rooms

All apartments are made to match the external design - in the form of caves and grottoes. Stylish rooms, decorated with high-quality stone and wood, amaze guests of the Caves Beach Resort 5 *. Guest reviews note the unusual interior design. Guests complain about the lack of lighting and poor polishing of furniture, which is often injured by the fingers.

The hotel has 360 rooms: 100 standard (area - 40 sq. M, accommodation - up to 4 people), 120 junior suites with sea view (area - 40 sq. M, King bed, accommodation - 2 + 2), 80 suites with view at sea (area - 50 sq. m, accommodation - 2 + 2), 50 royal rooms (area - 60 sq. m, two King beds, accommodation - up to 4 people), 10 rooms for guests with disabilities.

Dining at the hotel

The hotel operates on an all-inclusive basis. The hotel complex has several restaurants and bars.





The main restaurant is Flintstone, which is located on the ground floor of the main building. It is made in a very original style: in the form of a large cave, along the walls of which there is a large aquarium populated by representatives of marine fauna. There is also a terrace overlooking the pool, while the Flintstone restaurant can accommodate up to 600 guests of the Caves beach resort 5 * hotel.

Reviews of tourists note delicious cuisine, a variety of dishes, their interesting and beautiful presentation. There is always fish (fried, grilled and steam), in addition, there are dishes of beef, lamb, chicken, duck and quail. Garnish is served with rice, potatoes (boiled, baked and french fries), spaghetti and vegetable dishes (stews, grilled and steamed). Salads in the generally accepted concept at the Caves Beach Resort 5 * hotel (Hurghada) are not accepted, but instead they offer a more original solution: there are cutting boards and knives on the table, lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, peppers, cabbage and other vegetables are served in vases. whole, nearby are various spices, herbs and sauces - everyone can make their own salad to their taste and be sure that it will be really fresh. For dessert, a large selection of pastries and fruits is offered.





In the afternoon, you can dine at the Dino Restaurant on the beach.

You can have a snack, drink milk, alcoholic or non-alcoholic cocktails, juice, tea or coffee with canapes or cakes in one of the bars: on the beach, near the pool or in the lobby bar. Alcoholic drinks are only local, but judging by the reviews, high-quality alcohol, its choice is large.

Hotel pools, beach and sea

The hotel has three pools, one of which has water heated in the cold season. There are sun loungers and umbrellas with tables near them, bartenders, upon request, make cocktails (especially praise mojito), serve chilled juices, canned drinks and beer.





Hotel Caves Beach Resort 5 * (Hurghada) has its own beach with a length of about one kilometer. There are sun loungers and umbrellas on the beach, a bar and a restaurant (only at lunch time), a shower (not always open).

The sea is shallow, to a depth where you can swim, go about 300 meters. To enter the water you need special shoes, which can be purchased in a souvenir shop on the hotel territory, otherwise you can injure your legs on dead corals at the bottom.

Those who wish to go for a swim can go to the pier of a neighboring hotel: here, several wooden entrances are equipped with a wooden pier. There are no living corals (they were flooded with concrete during construction), flora and fauna do not indulge in diversity.





Tourist note

In order to stay at the Caves Beach Resort 5 * hotel (Egypt), you should know about several points:

- they are not allowed here with pets, regardless of their size and the presence of veterinary passports;

- children under 16 years of age are prohibited, an exception is made only in rare cases for adolescents 14-16 years old;

- the territory is poorly adapted for people with disabilities, few ramps, transitions are arranged in the form of "humpbacked" bridges.

A new, original and unusual hotel can be a good alternative to the usual comfortable hotels. A delicious food and excellent service fully compensate for some of the inconvenience associated with ongoing construction work.
