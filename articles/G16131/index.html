Be Live Experience Turquesa 4 * (Cuba, Varadero): hotel description, services, reviews

Cuba is an island where there is a huge variety of hotel complexes and hotels that meet the modern standards of the world tourism business. One of them is the hotel in Varadero Be Live Experience Turquesa.

Short description



An unusually comfortable and cozy hotel is located on the seafront, the construction of buildings dates back to 1998, and the last global reconstruction was carried out in 2010. The complex is huge in area, it includes about 36 buildings.

For all services at the hotel it is customary to pay with Visa and Master cards. As for animals, pets are not allowed in the hotel. The hotel is located at: Las Morlas km 12.5. 42200, Tainos (Varadero) in Cuba. The distance from Varadero Airport to the complex will be 36 km, and the distance to the center is 8 km.

Accommodation





The room fund of the Be Live Experience Turquesa 4 * hotel (Cuba) is 268 rooms, each of which has a set of everything you need for a comfortable stay. There is a bathroom with a shower or bathtub, towels and all the necessary hygiene items. There is Internet access, there are comfortable beds, wardrobes, tables for applying makeup, chairs and other furniture. Each room is cleaned daily and bed linen and towels are changed every week. If desired, you can order food in the room directly from the hotel restaurant.

The bar and lobby are open from 11 a.m. to 12 a.m. The standard room accommodates a maximum of 3 people; children's beds are available on request.

In the superior room, in addition to the listed amenities, there is a hydromassage system in the bathroom, which is turned on upon a request made in advance. Superior room with a terrace for families with small children implies the presence of a small but completely sanctified area for guests with tea and coffee accessories, tea and delicious ground coffee.

Nutrition

As for delicacies, there are 2 restaurants of Cuban and international cuisine on the territory of the hotel, which can be visited for free twice a week. Service in these establishments is carried out according to the buffet system.





There is a bar on the beach at the hotel where you can get refreshing drinks. In order to receive a full list of services, you need to show the manager a special card in advance, by which a personal table and a waiter will be reserved for the client. From 10 pm to 6 am, vacationers can enjoy light snacks and salads.

Additional services

For couples preparing for marriage, the hotel provides toastmaster services and other wedding bonuses. For lovers of outdoor activities it is possible to rent a bicycle, and the owners of private cars can use the free guarded parking lot. For renting a motorcycle a small fee is charged. The hotel has a souvenir shop where you can buy cute memorable gifts for relatives. In the snack bar, you can buy a variety of snacks on the road or just for fun. Access to the worldwide network in the restaurants is provided for a separate monetary reward. If there is a special need, you can call a highly qualified doctor who can help resolve any health problems. Hotel pool towels are provided free of charge.

Umbrellas, deckchairs, sunbeds for sunbathing are also in free use. The Varadero Beach at the hotel is very clean, showered with fine golden sand, the entrance to the water is convenient, which allows even small children to swim there. Arriving at this hotel once, you will not remain indifferent.

About accommodation





A bus takes 20 minutes from the airport to the complex. The path is accompanied by guide stories about the local characteristics of the country. No matter how much you arrive, you can only check in after 16:00. From 12.00 to 16.00 the rooms are prepared for check-in. You can leave your luggage and go for a walk.

It is better to book rooms on the second floor. The spacious rooms are equipped with huge beds, a table with chairs, dressing table, TV, air conditioning. There is a balcony with plastic furniture. The bathroom has a washbasin, mirror, toilet, bathtub, hairdryer and bidet. In the hallway there is a large wardrobe and a safe.

At the reception you can immediately change money at a good rate. The hotel itself consists of separate two-story houses.
