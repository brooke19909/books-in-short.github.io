Laskovy beach in the village of Solnechnoe: how to get, map, photo

Where can I relax in the summer in Russia? I immediately recall the Black Sea, Anapa, the banks of the beautiful Volga. But what about the Gulf of Finland and the Tender Beach? If you will spend the summer in the vicinity of St. Petersburg, then be sure to visit this hospitable coast.

The history of the village of Solnechnoe

It exists for a long time, but if earlier it bore the Finnish name Ollila, then in the post-war time it was renamed to Solar. It is located on the magnificent coast of the Gulf of Finland. Even in Soviet times, the beauty of these places was very much appreciated; having a small house here was considered a real luxury. Due to this, the village grew rapidly, and the number of people willing to lie on the shore of the bay increased. Later this place began to be called "Tender" beach. He fully justifies his name. When distinguished guests visited the Soviet Union, they were often brought here. In particular, the famous Charles de Gaulle was very fond of spending time on this beach. Many prominent party figures also did not mind warming themselves here under the warm sun.

Surrounding nature

The northern region pleases with lush vegetation. There is no exotic here, in the village, immediately behind it and near the beach the usual trees grow: spruce, pine, alder, birch. Everything is familiar and dear to the heart of a Russian person. The abundance of coniferous trees creates a special microclimate. The forest is close to the beach, if you wish, after sunbathing, you can hide in its luxurious shade. It is especially beautiful here in spring and autumn, and this is due to the great merit of rose hips, of which there are a huge number. Its flowers and fiery red berries are a real decoration of the area.





Coast

There are a lot of pebbles and stones that children love to throw into the water. But basically the beach is covered with sand. In the summer, many tend to get out of the city in the village of Solnechnoe. "Affectionate" is a beach located just 30 kilometers from St. Petersburg, that is, you can reach the resting place in less than 30 minutes. It is considered the best on the coast. Here the soft sandy bottom is shallow and the water manages to warm up well. The southern part of the beach is especially loved by tourists, there are no sharp stones and the depth is sufficient to dive.

If you are still considering how to spend the weekend, come to Laskovy beach in Solnechny. Photos will surprise friends with their beauty for a long time. By the way, despite the well-maintained condition, the beach is municipal, and admission to it is free, as well as the use of sunbeds and umbrellas. You will have to pay extra only for attractions.





Best time to visit the beach

Since the Gulf of Finland is quite shallow, the season opens here very early. However, in June there are still not many people on the coast, the hype begins in early July. On the advice of experienced tourists, it is better to get on the road in the morning, so that you can catch up on Laskovy (St. Petersburg beach) earlier than others. How to get there - everyone chooses. You can go by car, train, bus - all the same, the road flies very quickly. Arriving after eleven, you risk not finding a free place to relax. The pandemonium here is normal, but it continues until the end of the season, sometimes longer.

The beach is usually very clean. Sometimes there are cigarette butts buried in the sand, but this is the exception rather than the rule. This is not surprising, taking into account the enormous number of ballot boxes installed on its territory. It does not take time to find where to throw the trash.

Sporting events

How to get there

If this is your first time going to the "Affectionate" (beach), the map will be the best assistant. According to it, you can navigate in the direction, calculate the distance and estimate the necessary amount of fuel. In general, the route is not difficult, Primorsky highway is beautifully lit and equipped with the latest signs.





It is not necessary to use personal transport. Every 30 minutes, an electric train leaves from the Finland Station in the direction of the Solnechnoye village. Upon reaching the desired station, it remains only to cross the platform and move towards the embankment. The way from the stop to the beach will take about 5 minutes. Municipal bus No. 411 departs from the Chernaya Rechka metro station, and a K-400 minibus stops here. These modes of transport are slower and take about 40 minutes. Also, a K-305 minibus departs from the Staraya Derevnya metro station, and a K-600 from Parnas. Another route - "Prospect of Enlightenment - Solnechnoe", here you need a taxi K-680. Another option is good for cycling fans. On a personal or rental bike, you can easily overcome this distance.
