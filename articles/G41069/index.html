Recreation center "Lukomorye", Penza: description, attractions and reviews

The most amazing place to relax soul and body in the Penza region is the recreation center "Lukomorye". This is not just a complex for entertainment and family weekends. This is an equestrian tourist club. It is located at the edge of a picturesque forest on the stunningly beautiful coast of the Sur reservoir. The main specialization of the club is equestrian sports. But there are many others.



Caring for guests

A feature of the base is horse riding training. For those who wish, several scenic routes have been developed for horseback riding in the vicinity.

The recreation center "Lukomorye" (Penza) has prepared for vacationers a lot of shady arbors, areas for cooking barbecue and glades for relaxation.

Check-in time is 13:00, check-out is 12:00. Children under two years old have a rest at the base for free (without providing a berth).

Rooms must be booked and prepaid. It is not refundable if the reservation is canceled less than a day before entry.

Free guarded parking is available.

Lukomorye awaits vacationers all year round. In summer, relaxing on the beach is wonderful; in winter, skiing and sleigh rides or ice skating are exciting. In the offseason fishing is exciting ...



Number of rooms

Vacationers are accommodated in the Golden Mane Guesthouse and wooden cottages. All apartments have the necessary conditions for a comfortable and pleasant stay.

The apartments, which the recreation center "Lukomorye", Penza offers (prices depend on the number of guests), are divided into "luxury", "standard" and houses near the economy class.

Each of them has two rooms (bedroom and living room), a kitchen and a bathroom. It also has a balcony and a veranda with a seating area and a table. The room is equipped with modern kitchen appliances and a TV with satellite antenna.

Additional services

Meals are not included in the estimated cost of the permit. Each room has a kitchen area. It is equipped with modern appliances and cooking sets. There is also a table with chairs and cutlery. The hotel complex has a shared kitchen.

There is a cafe with Uzbek cuisine at the base. The menu is quite varied, the bar has many drinks, including strong ones.

The recreation center "Lukomorye" (Penza) has a large detached banquet hall and helps in organizing various celebrations.

Also at the disposal of guests there are two Russian baths (heated by firewood), a small contact zoo and fishing spots are organized. Outside the recreation complex there are several picturesque lakes. They catch grass carp, crucian carp, carp, silver carp and other types of fish.



Communication with horses

The main entertainment offered by the recreation center "Lukomorye" (Penza) is horseback riding. An experienced instructor teaches the basics of riding for both adults and children. Several interesting routes have been developed around the area. There are individual hiking trips, and there are in the group. Of the summer options, the most impressive trip is to the Surskoye Reservoir, and of the winter ones, skiing in a fabulous forest in triples with bells.
