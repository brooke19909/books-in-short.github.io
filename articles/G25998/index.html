Hostels in Riga: list, description and cost

Hostels in Riga, despite the low prices for accommodation, are quite comfortable. Therefore, tourists often choose one of them for a temporary stop. Thus, they manage to save finances for other entertainment and food.

Hostels of Riga often have a homely atmosphere; guests feel comfortable and calm here. Almost every one of them has the opportunity to cook food.

Maestro

This small hotel is located along Stabu iela 81-10. You can walk here from the Old Town in seven minutes, and the nearest airport is no more than 10 km. Each room has a plasma TV, wardrobes, desk.

Hostel guests in Riga have free access to shared toilets and showers. The hotel has a kitchen. It has all the necessary equipment and a large set of dishes. In the kitchen, guests are offered free coffee.

At the choice of tourists, the hostel offers 4 rooms, which can accommodate from 1 to 3 people. The rooms have single beds or one large. Guests can use the luggage storage service at the hotel. The approximate cost of it is 2 euros per hour.

Children under 6 years old with parents can stay for free. The average cost of living per day is 1500 rubles.

Hotel Sabina (Riga)

The hotel is located in the Bolderai district. Taxi is approximately 10 minutes from the city center. The hotel is in the same building as the police station.

The following categories of rooms are at guests' choice:

  • single;
  • double and triple;
  • Suite

All rooms have wooden furniture. There are wardrobes, desks, bedside tables and a TV. Each room has its own toilet and bathroom.

The average cost of living per person per day is approximately 22 euros. The hotel provides additional services:

  • Fitness Centre;
  • pool;
  • sauna;
  • beauty shop ;
  • restaurant and bar.

The hotel does not serve breakfast. But not far from it there is a small grocery store and a cozy cafe with affordable prices.

Hostel Turiba

The hotel is located in New Riga at the address: Graudu iela 68. To get to the center you will have to overcome the train by a distance of about 10 minutes.

The hotel is located in a quiet area within the university. The hostel has a dining room where meals can be organized for large groups of guests.

Tourists can stay in rooms for 4 people. There are either bunk or single wooden beds. The rooms have wardrobes, desks.

Each floor has shared toilets and showers. The hotel has a living room with a large TV and soft sofas, as well as a computer room with free internet.

Tourists can use the laundry service for an extra charge, and they can also take on an iron and ironing board. There is a shared kitchen with all necessary equipment and utensils.

Free parking is available near the hotel. The reception is open 24 hours a day. The average cost for a bed in a room is approximately 110 rubles.
