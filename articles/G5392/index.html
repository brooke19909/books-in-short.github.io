Hotel Ajman Palace 5 * (Ajman): description, reviews, photos

All travelers who want to take a break from the frantic pace of modern life, from the annoying noise of megalopolises and spend unforgettable time under the gentle sun on the Persian Gulf, should stay in the hotels of the smallest emirate of all the UAE - Ajman. Today, here, as in the days of pearl mining and growing dates, silence and tranquility reign. No noisy parties and vibrant nightlife. Their absence is explained by the relative youth of this resort.

In the emirate of Ajman, you can relax on the sea coast and enjoy the comfort. Hotels gladly opened their doors to guests, offering them service at a fairly high level. On the territory of the majority of hotel complexes there are magnificent pools, health centers, restaurants, cafes, bars and other establishments intended for an interesting pastime. Some hotels even have their own beach.

Tourists who prefer a VIP vacation should choose five-star hotels. Here the quality of service is fully consistent with the level of declared stardom.

Recently gaining popularity among vacationers Ajman Palace 5 * (UAE, Ajman). Ajman Palace is a fairly new hotel where guests can appreciate the Arabian architecture and true oriental hospitality.

About hotel

The seven-story building of The Ajman Palace Hotel 5 *, built in 2012, with its appearance and interior resembles a palace in the best traditions of oriental culture. There are towers, patterned rooms, in the lobby there is a ceiling in the form of a dome and a column with light.





Ajman Palace 5 * (Ajman) is recommended for couples to relax with both children and without them.

A feature of the hotel is that alcoholic drinks are not offered to vacationers here. You also need to be prepared for the fact that upon checking in they will ask to make a deposit.

Location

The Ajman Palace 5 * is located right on the shore of the Persian Gulf. The distance to Sharjah International Airport is eleven kilometers, to Dubai International Airport - twice as much. The hotel has its own small territory and a private beach, the length of which is one hundred and fifty meters.

Number of rooms

The hotel is ready to offer its guests rooms of various categories: from one-room standards to suites and apartments. All rooms of Ajman Palace 5 * (Ajman) have a private bathroom, equipped with a washbasin, shower / bath, toilet, some have a bidet. Also in all rooms are available:

  • comfortable beds and furniture;
  • air conditioning system;
  • TV with satellite channels;
  • refrigerator;
  • mirror;
  • safe;
  • tea set ;
  • hair dryer;
  • iron;
  • slippers and bathrobe.




Many rooms have a balcony with sea views. The hotel also has 3 equipped rooms for people with reduced mobility, 17 Connect rooms and 169 non-smoking rooms.

Bed linen and room cleaning are daily and free of charge. Room service is available 24 hours a day for an extra charge.

Nutrition

When buying a tour to the hotel Ajman Palace 5 * (Ajman), guests can independently choose the type of food: breakfast, half board or full board. Vacationers are served on a buffet in the main restaurant.





The hotel has three restaurants, bars and a pastry shop, ready to please their guests with culinary delights of different countries:

  • main restaurant "Ankor Bistro" (offers international and Mediterranean cuisine);
  • Restaurant "Arabian Gourmet" (Middle Eastern cuisine);
  • "Dragon's Lair" (Japanese and Thai cuisine);
  • H2O Lounge Bar (the pool bar offers vacationers up to one hundred kinds of mineral water);
  • Nojoom Lounge ("Shisha Lounge" on the roof located outdoors);
  • Juice and Nutrition Bar (bar at the fitness room);
  • lobby bar "Ankor cafe";
  • pastry shop (pastries, sweets, cakes to order).

Beach

The Ajman Palace 5 * hotel (Ajman) has its own beach covered with sand of white color. It is located directly in front of the hotel. Everything necessary for a comfortable beach holiday (large towels, sun umbrellas, comfortable sun loungers and mattresses) is provided to guests free of charge.





Hotel cleanliness is carefully monitored by hotel staff. Professional rescuers and medical personnel are always nearby.

Also here, as throughout the hotel, the Wi-Fi service from the hotel works great.

Additional services

Booking the hotel The Ajman Palace 5 *, tourists receive a number of additional amenities:

  • outdoor pool and wellness facilities;
  • gym and spa;
  • Sauna and Turkish bath;
  • services of an experienced massage therapist;
  • beauty saloon;
  • doctor's services;
  • laundry and dry cleaning;
  • rent a car, moped or bicycle;
  • parking;
  • free shuttle to shopping centers;
  • currency exchange operations;
  • ordering excursions;
  • business center and conference room services.

The hotel has a shop and two kiosks with souvenirs and newspapers. For celebrations, there is a banquet hall.





The Ajman Palace 5 * also offers its services for organizing weddings and implements special programs for newlyweds.

To pay for additional services, cash and credit cards are accepted.

The hotel staff speaks many foreign languages, including Russian.

Entertainment and sports

Water sports and outdoor activities at the hotel include water aerobics and windsurfing. You can also rent a small boat for boat trips.

From land sports there are darts, basketball, badminton, volleyball, soccer, tennis and a jogging terrace.

Free of charge, all vacationers can visit the fitness center, equipped with modern equipment, a warm infinity pool and an outdoor jacuzzi. For an additional fee, you can take swimming lessons from professionals or use the services of a personal trainer in the gym.

For children

The hotel Ajman Palace 5 * (Ajman) has all the conditions for recreation not only for adults, but also for kids. There is a children's pool, which is carefully protected by a canopy from bright sunlight, and a playground, which will be fun for children of all ages. There is a mini-club where cognitive lessons and entertaining classes are held for children, joint walks along the coast are made, master classes in drawing and painting dishes are organized, and animated films are shown.





Babysitting services are available at an extra charge.

A baby cot can be provided in each room upon request. In restaurants, vacationers always have high chairs and menu items suitable for children.

Guest reviews

Everything that you can see and read in the tour leaflets of tour operators about The Ajman Palace 5 *, reviews of experienced travelers really confirm. Tourists who visited this hotel are very pleased to share their impressions of the trip on the Internet.

They liked the beautiful and comfortable rooms, new comfortable furniture, large and spacious bathrooms. Almost all vacationers were satisfied with the cleaning in the room and the frequency of changing linen and towels. True, some tourists caution that despite the five stars, the floor in the room is tiled, and you should be careful after wet cleaning, as you can easily slip.

Special thanks are given to guests by the cooks and restaurant staff. Vacationers were pleased with a wide range of delicious dishes for breakfast, lunch or dinner, speed and quality of service. However, some guests say that they would like to see more fruit on their tables.

The beach and the sea also cause a lot of rave reviews. I like everything: clean sand, a small number of vacationers, comfortable sun loungers, a warm shallow sea. But, choosing this hotel for relaxation, it should be noted that the sun begins to illuminate the beach from eleven in the morning, previously the entire coastline is in the shadow of the hotel building.





There is also another negative point that you need to know in advance and take into account when checking into a hotel: in some rooms, noise from the air conditioning system located on the roof of the hotel interferes with sleep at night. At the same time, even closed windows do not save. If possible, staff always meets and changes the number at the request of the client.

Despite some of the shortcomings identified, most vacationers still believe that the hotel deserves its five stars. It is here that unsurpassed Arabian luxury is organically combined with modern amenities, and this, in turn, makes unforgettable guests stay at Ajaman resort.

Ajman Palace 5 * (Ajman) is a hotel complex, which they willingly return to and recommend to their friends and acquaintances as an excellent hotel at a very affordable cost.
