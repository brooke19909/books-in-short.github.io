DIY gift certificates: how to bring joy to a loved one

DIY gift certificates are not difficult to make. This is an original gift that will be remembered for a long time. It will be pleasant for a young man or husband to receive an interesting surprise with attractive subtext from his soulmate. The "filling" of the certificate can be any. In this article we will tell you how to budget and in an original way you can congratulate your loved one on a holiday or just to please him with an unexpected present.

Why come up with when you can buy a ready-made gift?

Creative girls make a gift certificate with their own hands, with whom they are not bored. Indeed, very often gifts are selected based on the degree of practicality and need for life. A woman should be a mystery, and original gifts are part of her image.

Certificate of Wish

Surely every girl knows what her beloved is dreaming of. For this reason, it will be easy for any female to come up with and beautifully verbally design several wishes. Do-it-yourself gift certificates are made from the heart. If they are aimed at fulfilling a dream, they will become a welcome embodiment of the perfect gift. By implementing this idea, you can be as sure as possible of the success of the event. Such a gesture will not go unnoticed.





Points system

How to make a gift certificate with your own hands? A loved one will definitely like a non-standard approach, so you need to include imagination. You can give a certificate sheet for the fulfillment of a single desire to choose a partner. But to create intrigue, it is recommended to use points that can be used to evaluate each action that was conceived in advance. For instance:

  • erotic massage - 300 points;
  • breakfast in bed - 500 points;
  • hugs - 1000 points;
  • night of love and affection - 2000 points;
  • striptease - 500 points;
  • forgiveness of resentment - 5000 points;
  • joint photo shoot - 1000 points;
  • an evening with friends without a second half - 1000 points;
  • relaxing bath - 500 points.




To get feedback, you can leave one blank field so that the donor can enter the item on his own as a pleasant bonus. Such an opportunity will only increase the value of the presentation.

Gift Instruction

The cardboard certificate enclosed in a beautiful envelope looks the most advantageous. DIY gift certificate for men is very simple to do. To implement the plan you will need:

  • decorative cardboard with a romantic pattern;
  • ribbons, flowers, paints, color gel, felt-tip pens, sparkles and other attributes for decoration;
  • the envelope;
  • sets of paper for scrapbooking.

You can make a DIY gift certificate on one sheet of cardboard or fold it in half in the form of a postcard. Product size depends on personal desire. There are no evaluation criteria; it can be absolutely anything. Next, you need to write or print the desired text on the selected cardboard format. Then perform the application at will, using various decorative tools. The envelope is recommended to be made of kraft paper, decorated with beads, rhinestones and ribbons.

Book of wishes

You must have creative thinking skills to make gift certificates with your own hands. Creating a book requires more time and perseverance than a certificate. But the result will have a larger reaction from the second half.





The appearance of such a gift is much more impressive, and the content is more interesting. Of course, it is easier to order the execution of such complex work in the printing house, but independent work will be more valuable.

Necessary materials

This option for creating a gift involves the presence of many pictures and photographs. The search for the necessary material will take some amount of personal time. For full readiness, you will need:

  • cardboard sheets, which will become full pages of the book, so the quality of the material should be excellent;
  • printed joint photos or just pictures with a romantic connotation;
  • a wish list;
  • decorative elements (laces, ribbons, buttons, beads, rhinestones, corrugated paper, scrap paper);
  • hole punch and beautiful braid or tape.

Instruction manual

First of all, you need to choose a beautiful and dense sheet of cardboard, which will be the title page. On it you can write "Book of desires for a loved one." The design of each sheet depends on personal imagination, so it is important to simply trust your feelings and create.





On each sheet you need to stick a photo or picture, signing a wish. You can make a book by the type of coupons - if you want to use it, you just need to tear out the selected sheet. From the edge of each sheet, leave 2 free centimeters. At the end, this part should be punctured with a hole punch and stretching ribbons, connecting the pages of the book together.

DIY Gift Vouchers - The Best Gift

Gifts from the heart are always the most valuable. A distinctive feature of an envelope or a book with wishes is that the couple can execute them together. The girl prescribes upcoming events on paper that would be pleasant to both partners. This practice strengthens the relationship in a couple and helps to diversify gray everyday life. This is much more interesting than a material thing and will save perfectly as an idea during a financial crisis. Also, such a gift without a reason perfectly refreshes communication.
