What is the largest country in the world? Russia is the biggest country in the world. If we talk about the extreme points of the territory of the Russian Federation, they are located as follows:

- The western extreme point is located on the Baltic Spit, located near Kaliningrad. This spit is divided by the border between Poland and the Russian Federation. The southern part belongs to Poland, and the northern - to Russia. This is the extreme point in the West;

- Cape Chelyuskin - the northernmost continental point of Russia and Eurasia, located on the Taimyr Peninsula. Named in honor of the navigator of the northern expedition S.I. Chelyuskin, who first discovered this cape in 1742 and plotted it on a map;





- Mount Bazardyuzyu - the southern extreme point, located actually 3 kilometers from one of the peaks of the main ridge of the Caucasus Mountains, 4466 m high, on the border of the Russian Federation and Azerbaijan;

- Cape Dezhnev - the extreme point of the eastern continental part of Eurasia and Russia. It is located on the shores of the Bering Strait, on the Chukotka Peninsula. The cape was named in 1879 in honor of the Russian navigator and traveler Semyon Ivanovich Dezhnev, who first rounded this cape in 1648;

- the most extreme point in the East - located a few kilometers from the Bering Strait, where several Diomede islands are located. One of them is the island of Ratmanov, owned by the Russian Federation. In good weather, the island of Kruzenshtern, belonging to the United States, located 4 km to the east, can be seen from it. Ratmanova Island is considered the extreme point of the East.
