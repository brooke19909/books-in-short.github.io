Tourists planning to visit Egypt should pay attention to the rather cozy and comfortable hotel Sultan Gardens Resort 5 * (Sharm El Sheikh). According to many travelers, the conditions here allow you to have a great time relaxing, enjoying all the delights of a tropical climate.

Where is the hotel located?

Sharks Bay is a very beautiful and quiet place. It is here, not far from the Red Sea, that the Sultan Gardens Resort 5 * hotel (Sharm El Sheikh) is located. It is located not far from the magnificent Old Town, in which there are quite a lot of interesting places. It is only 5 km away. No less unique place, namely Naama Bay, is 7 km from the hotel.



The road from the airport to the hotel does not take much time, since the distance between these objects is only 5 km. Tourists can get to the place of residence by bus or choose more comfortable travel conditions by ordering a transfer.

Why is the hotel noteworthy?

Many hotels can boast of any advantage, whether it is a beautiful area, a magnificent beach, spacious rooms or more. In Sultan Gardens Resort 5 * (Sharm El Sheikh) everything leaves only a pleasant impression. The Arabian architecture of all the premises of the complex captivates with its beauty at first sight. But, having become acquainted with the hotel, you can see elements of modern design that fit very harmoniously into the overall look.



The level of service will also delight guests. In the ranking of hotels in Egypt, precisely because of the quality of service, it occupies one of the leading positions of Sultan Gardens Resort 5 * (Sharm El Sheikh). "Sheraton" (hotels of one of the most prestigious chains) do not surpass this hotel complex in anything. And this is noted by many tourists.

The well-groomed gardens of the Sultan Gardens Resort 5 * hotel (Sharm El Sheikh) look amazing. The lush greenery surrounding the buildings gives the impression that you are in a truly heavenly place. A variety of flowers captivate with their beauty, and perennial palm trees give a special charm to this amazing place.

The interior of the hotel is no less attractive. In the spacious hall, where the emphasis is precisely on the oriental style, you can comfortably sit on the soft sofas. This allows you to speed up the waiting time for check-in, although they try to provide guests with rooms as soon as possible.

What about the numbers?

Despite the fact that tourists travel to Egypt to spend time on the beaches and the sea, living conditions are very important. In the Sultan Gardens Resort 5 * hotel (reviews of tourists confirm this), guests are waiting for well-equipped rooms of various types:

  • 320 Standard Room, designed for two people;
  • 32 Family Room, which accommodates a maximum of 4 people;
  • 151 Junior Suite, which can comfortably accommodate 4 guests;
  • 4 Villa Sea Side - luxury three-bedroom apartment;
  • 53 bungalows having 2 floors.

Regardless of the category of rooms, vacationers will have everything you need for a comfortable stay. You can contact the hotel by phone in the room. International calls can be made for a fee. You can also shorten the time while watching TV. The room is also equipped with a minibar, air conditioning. Vacationers can use an electronic safe to store valuables.



In each room you can admire the surroundings from the balcony or terrace, equipped with high chairs and a table, at which it is quite pleasant to sit with a cup of tea or coffee. In the bathroom, depending on the category of rooms, there is a bath or shower. All plumbing works properly, and guests do not have to deal with faulty taps. The hotel staff always monitors the availability of toiletries and regularly updates them. Each bathroom has a hairdryer.

The hotel rooms are wet-cleaned daily. After the cleaners, the rooms are always very clean. The linen in the rooms of the Sultan Gardens Resort 5 * hotel also changes daily.

Accommodation systems

Guests can stay at the hotel according to two systems:

  • All Inclusive
  • Ultra All Inclusive.

They are provided with privileged breakfasts, lunches and dinners. Breakfast can be served free of charge directly to the rooms. Food quality

The guests will be very pleased with the variety of dishes provided by the main restaurant of the Sultan Gardens Resort 5 * hotel (Sharm El Sheikh). A photo of the hall will allow you to see that you can eat here in fairly comfortable conditions. The room is well ventilated thanks to an excellent air conditioning system. The menu includes vegetarian and diet dishes.



It is possible to visit the restaurants A la Carte:

  • Sea Market Food offers its visitors excellent seafood dishes.
  • Fleur De Lys serves American cuisine.
  • Casa Mia awaits fans of Italian cuisine.

You can have a good meal in the bars located near the pool or on the beach.

Infrastructure

Like all five-star hotels, Sultan Gardens Resort 5 * (Sharm El Sheikh) offers many services that can be used for free or at a certain cost. The hotel has many shops where you can buy a wide variety of goods. There is also a hairdresser in which experienced specialists will put your hairstyle in order. You can improve your appearance in the spa center, where for a certain price you have the opportunity to visit the sauna, jacuzzi, hammam or massage room.



If necessary, business people can use the conference room, where you can conduct negotiations or various kinds of meetings. You can supplement your knowledge in the hotel library, which contains very interesting books.

Laundry or dry cleaning services are available at an additional cost. They can also iron clothes well. Medical assistance is provided only for a certain amount. The doctor is present at the hotel around the clock and will come on call at any time.

Entertainment at the hotel

A varied and exciting pastime is provided to all hotel guests. Here you can swim in 3 pools filled with fresh water. You can sunbathe on the sun loungers, which hotel guests can use for free.

Vacationers are also provided with various opportunities to spend their time actively. Volleyball and mini-golf are equipped on site. There is also a tennis court. You can use the services of an instructor who will teach you how to play tennis. From board games, you can give preference to billiards or tennis.



The hotel has a diving center, which will teach you the intricacies of diving and even conduct test diving with a scuba diving in the pool. The hotel can also engage in other equally exciting water sports.

For lovers of night walks, a disco bar offers their services. Here you can not only dance, but also watch various show programs and performances. A group of animators and local creative groups entertain the audience.

Sultan Gardens Resort 5 * (Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt): a description of a beach holiday

The hotel has its own sandy beach, where guests are provided with free sunbeds and umbrellas. The entrance to the sea is quite convenient. Near the coast it is small enough, which is very pleasing to the kids who are ready to splash in warm water for at least a whole day. Adults can get to places convenient for swimming through a specially constructed pontoon.



Near the beach there is a coral reef. Near it it is very interesting to swim, since the underwater world is extremely rich in various inhabitants. You can admire a variety of tropical fish.

When going to the beach, do not forget about special shoes, since during swimming you can injure your legs with corals.

To whom rest in hotel is suitable?

In the Sultan Gardens Resort 5 * hotel , the photo of which can be seen in the article, all conditions are created so that guests of any age can spend time useful and exciting. Here it will be comfortable for families with small children, because the kids at the hotel pay a lot of attention. Young people can also find entertainment for themselves. During the day they can go in for sports, attend various excursions, and in the evening have fun at a disco. The elderly are waiting for the silence of the gardens and a calm, measured pastime.
