The recreation center "Arrow", Azov. Description, reviews

Do you like to relax by the river, go fishing, listen to birdsong in the morning? Then you are invited to the recreation center "Strelka" located on the right bank of the Don. Azov is just an hour away from the health resort. Spacious Don steppes, gently sloping river banks, home coziness and the most comfortable living conditions are ideal for those who like a measured rest away from the hustle and bustle of the city.

Description

The recreation center "Strelka" (Azov) is located on an island in the lower reaches of the Don River. The territory is surrounded by greenery, there are many fragrant flowers everywhere, wooden houses smell of Karelian pine. In the summer, it will be fresh and cool in any heat.

On the clean sandy shore there are gazebos, barbecue facilities, and a bathing area. There are sports grounds for those who want to compete in volleyball or football, tables for playing table tennis, and fitness equipment. Here you can go fishing and, in the evenings, relax in the bath complex, which includes two steam rooms, pools and lounges.

The restaurant serves European and Don cuisine. In the summer cafe on the open veranda, you can simultaneously enjoy the art of the local chefs and admire the magnificent scenery.

Parking for car owners is free. There is also everything you need for fans of the game of paintball. The recreation center "Strelka" is located at: Azov city, Rostov region, Right bank of the Don river, 1.

  • Cottages with standard double and triple rooms, with wooden beds, bedside tables and chairs. The rooms are also equipped with a mini fridge, TV, split system, necessary furniture, a bathroom.
