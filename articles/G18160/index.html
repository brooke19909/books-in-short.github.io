Alexia Premier City Hotel 4 * (Greece, Rhodes): hotel description, service, food, reviews

If you want to get to know Greece better, head to the island of Rhodes. Comfortable accommodation and quality service will provide travelers with the Alexia Premier City Hotel.





a brief description of

From May to November, the resort hotel "Alexia" accepts travelers from all over the world. The hotel was opened in 1968, and therefore you can safely talk about its rich history and traditions of service. In 2014, a complete reconstruction of the institution was carried out, so that it acquired a modern look.





Location

This hotel is located on the first beach line. The distance from the sea is about 30 meters. This is the western part of the island of Rhodes, which is famous for its beautiful nature and picturesque beaches. The main street of the city is a 5-minute walk from here. The international airport is 13 km away.

This hotel is ideal for active tourists who are interested in natural and cultural attractions.
