Catering establishments

Tasty and high-quality food, as well as refreshing drinks are provided to guests by the restaurants and bars of Luna Blanca Resort & SPA 5 *. Luna Blanca Resort has the following catering facilities:





  • Turquoise is the main restaurant where the best dishes of Turkish and international cuisines will be presented to your attention. You can eat in the luxuriously decorated room or sit on the outdoor terrace. Dishes are served in buffet style.
  • Anchor - Mediterranean restaurant. Its design is designed in bright colors with spectacular lighting. The atmosphere is complemented by elegant furniture. The menu features fish and seafood dishes.
  • Mezzaluna is the perfect place for a romantic dinner or friendly get-togethers. The calm atmosphere and alluring aromas of Italian spices make this restaurant one of the most beloved among the guests. The main menu items are pizza and pasta.
  • At Sadabat Restaurant, guests can enjoy Ottoman culinary traditions. The themed palace interiors and authentic music complement the atmosphere.
  • In the afternoon, a cozy patisserie with cute plush armchairs and coffee tables opens its doors for guests. There are not only popular desserts, but also traditional Turkish sweets.
  • The 24-hour lobby bar has a relaxed and friendly atmosphere. It serves aromatic coffee, hot tea, alcohol, as well as light snacks and pastries.
  • Arena - a cozy pool bar. Here you can not only enjoy refreshing drinks, but also a hearty meal. This bar is the epicenter of entertainment.
  • Beach lovers can visit the Caretta Bar, which serves cold beer and sweet soda. You can also try Turkish cakes and kebabs, which on the shores of the azure sea seem even more delicious.

Helpful information

As in any other resort institution, in Luna Blanca Resort & SPA 5 * there are some rules governing accommodation. Guests of this establishment should know the following:

  • in all restaurants and bars drinks are served exclusively in glasses (sale in bottles is prohibited);
  • waiters and bartenders have the right to refuse to serve alcohol to a guest who, in their opinion, is intoxicated;
  • visiting themed restaurants is possible only by appointment;
  • depending on weather conditions, the schedule of work of public catering establishments, as well as the time of animation events, can be changed;
  • smoking is strictly prohibited in all areas of the hotel.
