How are human activities and needs related? Need, activity, motive of activity

A lot has been said about how activities and needs are related. There are whole scientific works devoted to this topic. She is really very interesting and diverse. It is difficult to discuss it all, because the essence concerns the most unknown creature in the world - man. But here are the basic concepts I would like to note attention.





General Provisions

How are human activities and needs related? Before answering this question, it is worth deciding on concepts. Activity is a certain type of human activity that is aimed at achieving one or another result. Motive pushes on its implementation. He is a kind of motivator - what makes a person act. Need, in turn, is characterized by many scientists as a need or deficit of something that is necessary for a person. We have a lot of them. They are divided into categories. There are spiritual, material, social, psychological, biological needs. And they are all natural, as their satisfaction fully contributes to the harmonious attitude of a person and his full existence in society.





Intellectual component

You can tell a lot about how activities and needs are related. And first of all you need to turn to the mind. More specifically, to the intellectual activity of man. It involves the receipt of information, its processing and assimilation. Man is a dubious creature. And from any information received it is quite capable of extracting benefits for itself. It is also human nature to learn. Many go to universities, get education. They are engaged in self-development: they read, they study languages. What for? Because we have a need for self-affirmation. It is important for a person to feel himself valuable, useful, necessary, possessing certain skills (maybe even unique to some extent). This is the motive for intellectual activity.

Materiality

Speaking about how activities and needs are connected, one cannot but touch upon such a topic as money. Everyone needs them. Because only money gives us the opportunity to buy housing, clean water, food, clothes, a bed for a healthy sleep. And the need for all of the above is our biological needs. Free of what we need, we can only get air. For everything else you have to pay.

And we get money only for work and labor. This is a reward for the benefit brought to the state, society or organization. By the way, work is also a source of satisfying certain social needs. Everyone sometimes wants to feel in the spotlight, to receive praise, approval, respect and a share of admiration. Well, all this can be obtained if you try and overfulfill the plan, for example. Or achieve other success at work. Perhaps they will even write out such a trifle as a bonus to a soul and purse that is so pleasant.

Continuity

As it was already possible to understand, all the previously listed concepts are directly related to each other. Need, activity, motive of activity is an inextricable triangle in which one comes from the other.

It is important to note that everything carries a focused character. Whether it is the needs of economic activity or creative. In any case, human activity is aimed at achieving a particular goal. It is a fact. Because aimless activity is not activity. But just a pastime.
