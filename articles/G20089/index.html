DIY crafts: how to make lynx claws out of paper

Fantasy manifests itself in different ways and ideas. If you are looking for an answer to the question of how to make lynx claws out of paper, then you probably decided to surprise others with something original.





Origami style

Origami is a special paper folding technique. To some, many origami-style crafts seem difficult to make, but they aren‚Äôt. How to quickly make paper claws? The scheme of crafts is given below.

For production, you need one sheet in A4 format. First you need to make a crease in which a square sheet is obtained from a rectangular sheet. The lower corners are slightly bent. Bend in half and again. Next, we carry out the folding of the left half of the strip. Wrap this piece on the left. We hide the protruding corner in the hole.





In order for the finger to freely enter the hole, it must be made more voluminous. It remains to make another 9 claws on the remaining fingers.

Lynx paper claws: phased implementation

How to make lynx claws out of paper for a girl? Simply and easily. It is important to follow the description below.

It is advisable to prepare a sheet of A4 paper: arrange it vertically.

  1. The right corner must be wrapped so that a rectangle is obtained.
  2. The bottom corner is bent up to get a figure with five corners or a pentagon.
  3. The opposite side is bent.
  4. Further, the part is folded diagonally, resulting in a triangle.
  5. The figure rotates to the left and doubles the left edge.
  6. The right side must wrap the left.
  7. In the same direction, another bend is carried out.
  8. In order for the claw to keep its shape, you need to bend one corner inward.
  9. It is advisable to straighten the finger hole a little for ease of entry.

So we answered the question of how to make paper claws for the girl. As you can see, this is not too difficult.





Naturally, this is not the only information on how to make paper claws of a lynx or other predatory animal. There are other ways.

Complicated Option

How to make lynx claws out of cardboard? As well as paper, although it should be noted that it can be quite difficult (cardboard is difficult to bend and give the product the desired shape). Although you can try if you wish. But there are plenty of ways to make paper claws. We described the simplest options above, and now imagine a complicated one. It is necessary to stock up on the following tools:

  • scissors;
  • with a simple pencil;
  • black gloves;
  • glue gun;
  • spray can with black or gray paint;
  • sheets of thick paper.



The previous two diagrams show small claws. And how to make lynx claws out of larger paper?

  1. We fold the paper sheet into a triangle, and on it we draw the edges of another triangle and cut it out. The resulting figure is applied to thick paper and circled. Cut along the contour and turn it into a cone.
  2. On the cone, draw two lines along the diagonal, which then need to be cut. It turns out three identical parts that need to be inserted at an angle into each other. Parts stick together.
  3. If we are talking about cat's claws, then they are ready. They can be put on fingers. The size of the claw lends itself to correction; it is enough to slightly trim the ends of the parts. By lengthening, longer claws can be obtained.
  4. Colored claws look very original. In this case, you can paint the crafts. Acrylic paint from a spray can do. Cute cat claws are ready!

Good advice

We presented several options for how to make lynx claws out of paper. Before starting production, you need to familiarize yourself with the following useful tips:

  1. You can make claws from plain white paper or cardboard, and colored sheets are suitable for the original craft.
  2. It is important to follow the correct sequence in the manufacturing process of the claws. Instructions are given so that the craft retains mobility and naturalness.
  3. The length of the claws can be adjusted depending on the desire: one can be worn on the other, but in this case it is necessary to glue them together for convenience and shape retention. To remove the length, it is necessary to trim all parts of the claws.
  4. In order to keep the crafts as long as possible, it is advisable to fasten the parts with glue. Do not abuse it, from too many claws will not look natural.
