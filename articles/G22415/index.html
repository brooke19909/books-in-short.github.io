SPA hotels in St. Petersburg: review, addresses, reviews

St. Petersburg is one of the most popular cities in Russia. The northern capital provides a lot of opportunities for tourism and business. But in a busy city there should always be a place to relax. The best SPA hotels in St. Petersburg will help to relax and restore strength for new achievements .

Hotel "Voyage"

Not far from Pulkovo Airport and the ExpoForum exhibition complex is the modern Voyage Hotel. Address - Pulkovo highway, 107. The institution was opened in 2004, and in 2016 a large-scale reconstruction was carried out. For a comfortable stay guests have the following features:

  • 98 comfortable rooms at a price of 1800 rubles per day;
  • three rooms specially equipped for disabled guests;
  • food in two luxurious restaurants;
  • free wireless internet;
  • the possibility of placing pets (from 5000 rubles + 1000 rubles deposit);
  • two conference rooms with modern office equipment;
  • mini-office with modern office equipment;
  • organization of weddings;
  • transfer organization.

Reviews

You can hear such positive comments about the Voyage Hotel:

  • spacious rooms with a pleasant modern interior;
  • good soundproofing of rooms;
  • on each floor there are coolers with drinking water and ironing rooms;
  • excellent hearty breakfasts are included in the price;
  • At early check-out guests are provided with packed breakfasts;
  • convenient location relative to the airport;
  • spacious bathroom;
  • wide, soft and very comfortable beds;
  • upon arrival, they settle without problems before 14:00, without requiring additional payment;
  • very attentive, understanding and qualified administrators.

And such negative ones:

  • during the heating season the rooms are very hot and stuffy;
  • insufficiently thorough cleaning of the rooms;
  • guests with pets are accommodated in ugly and groomed rooms on the ground floor;
  • remoteness from the city center.

Hotel "Lotus"

A quiet and relaxing holiday will provide guests of the SPA Hotel "Lotus" in St. Petersburg. The facility is located at 15A Novolitovskaya Street, which is a 10-minute drive from the Lesnaya metro station. For a comfortable stay, guests are provided with the following options:

  • accommodation in a mini-hotel (from 2500 rubles per day);
  • SPA programs for relaxation, rehabilitation and external transformation (from 2800 rubles);
  • purchase of gift certificates for visiting the spa area;
  • sauna for up to 15 people with an eight-meter pool;
  • cosmetic procedures for the face and body;
  • massage room;
  • organization of corporate events;
  • vertical tanning bed ;
  • beauty saloon;
  • professional photo shoots.

About this spa hotel in St. Petersburg you can hear such positive reviews:

Hotel "Mirage"

The Mirage SPA Hotel in St. Petersburg is 29 km from Pulkovo Airport. The address of the establishment is 13. Roadside Alley. The following facilities are available for recreation and accommodation:
