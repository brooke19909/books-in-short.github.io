"Multi-cook" - what is it? Multicook function in a multicooker

Needless to say, today the multicooker has won the hearts of more than one housewife and firmly occupied the niche owed to her in the consumer market. Which is not surprising, because its small bowl "fits" almost the entire kitchen, because it can replace many of the usual appliances of household appliances. This is so important for those who suffer from a shortage of space, and we have a lot of such housewives, because most apartments, as you know, very rarely please their owners with large sizes.

Who needs a slow cooker?

By the way, the opinion that the multicooker is simply vital for those who cook a lot and really skillfully is erroneous. A huge number of housewives - excellent cooks - perfectly do without any newfangled appliances. A miracle pan is more likely to come in handy for those who are always in a hurry somewhere, constantly busy at work, or simply lazy not only to spend a lot of time at the stove, but also to understand the intricacies of cooking. But for them, this device is a truly valuable asset. But deciding to buy a slow cooker, you need to at least approximately understand what this unit is. For all multicookers are different and have certain functions, some of which, by the way, are sometimes not needed at all. And most of the questions are caused by the Multi-Cook mode in a multi-cooker. Is he needed or not? This almost Hamletian question is being asked by many consumers. Therefore, now we will try to give an answer to it.

So, "Multipovar" - what is it and what is it "eaten" with?



Determine the concept

I must say that for some this definition is somewhat strangely associated with the miracle pan itself. So, many people think, having heard the word "multi-cooker", that this is some completely different household appliance that has nothing to do with a multicooker. Moreover, sometimes the sellers themselves sin this, not really understanding the meaning of the concept, which is what misleads the consumer. This is not a separate device.

Therefore, it is necessary to clearly know about the "Multipovar" function, that this is a special program in a multi-cooker, created by and large for professionals, but, oddly enough, can significantly facilitate the preparation of food for beginners and those who would not like to spend a lot on this process time.

Each step allows you to change the temperature by five degrees.
