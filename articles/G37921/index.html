How to decorate a postcard with your own hands: original ideas

A beautiful card is an invariable attribute of any holiday. After all, with the help of it you can tell a person about your feelings for him and congratulate you with triumph with all your heart. In the world there is a huge number of various cards for every taste and any subject. But the best of them is considered to be handmade. To make a presentable congratulatory craft, you only need creative thinking and the right materials. And how to decorate a card correctly so that it has all the necessary parameters, our article will tell.

Spring drops

Speaking of cards for such a bright holiday as March 8, we can say that they should all look sunny, as they symbolize the arrival of spring. Therefore, each product dedicated to this holiday must fill with light, warmth and tenderness of colors. In such cards, it is best to use a combination of light green, pink and yellow tones, because they inspire and saturate with the energy of solar heat after a long cold winter. An example of what homemade postcards from March 8 can be will be the photos presented below and a master class on making crafts. To do this, you will need such materials: colorful cardboard with an ornament, pearl beads of different colors, double-sided colored paper, glue and scissors.





Working process:

  1. A sheet of colorful color cardboard should be folded in half and form the desired size of the future postcard. If necessary, cut off the edges on it, making them slightly rounded.
  2. Draw an oval with openwork edges on a sheet of pink paper and cut out all the necessary details with a special knife. The result should be a figure with a solid middle and lace edges. The oval should be glued in the center of the postcard in a horizontal position.
  3. From white and pink paper, it is necessary to cut seven small flowers with carved edges (preferably of different shapes). In order for all the flowers to be beautiful, they must first be drawn and then cut.
  4. From green and light green colored paper you need to cut a lot of leaves of different shapes and sizes. The lighter the edges of the finished leaves will be, the more beautiful the postcard will look.
  5. Next, proceed to the manufacture of parts for a large flower. It will consist of several parts. To do this, draw 15 petals of elongated shape on a sheet of white paper and cut them out. Then you need to bend each petal in half (vertically) and press the edges with your fingers. After that, it is necessary to cut 10 petals from pink paper, but of a different shape (as indicated in the photo). It is not necessary to bend the pink details.
  6. When all the details for decorating the postcard are ready, you need to glue them. In the lower right corner of the card, you need to glue the white petals in the shape of a flower. Details should be placed close to each other, with sharp folds down. Next, you need to start forming the middle of the flower by gluing pink petals so that they have a cup shape. In the center of the resulting flower you need with glue to attach a lot of small white beads.
  7. To the left and right of the large flower, it is necessary to glue pink flowers with the middle of pearl beads.
  8. In the upper left corner, you need to fix 3 white flowers with pearl centers.
  9. Now it remains to decorate the craft with carved leaves, and it is almost ready for use.

Similar cards "From March 8!" can be made in absolutely any color and have other shapes of flower petals. And in the center of the lace oval you can write a beautiful wish.

Beloved mommy!

For the closest person in the world, for mom, I always want to make the best and most unique surprise. Therefore, we will consider how to decorate a card for mom, which is suitable for any occasion. For its manufacture, the following materials will be required: double-sided cardboard in violet color, a sheet of white paper, satin ribbon (lilac).





You will also need: 2 sheets of white paper, rhinestones, butterfly prints, glue, scissors and any paint.

Progress:

  1. Bend a sheet of cardboard in half.
  2. On a sheet of white paper, border the curly edges with scissors.
  3. Next, on the same sheet, make prints of butterflies of different colors.
  4. From a satin ribbon cut a piece the size of the width of a white openwork sheet.
  5. Then you need to glue a sheet of white paper with curly edges in the middle of the cardboard. At the same time, you need to stick on this sheet a piece of cut tape, bending its edges under the paper. The tape should be located at a height of 3-4 centimeters from the bottom of the white sheet.
  6. From another piece of tape, make a bow with beautiful edges and fix it on the glued tape.
  7. On another sheet of white paper, leave several prints of butterflies in different colors. Then they need to be cut, bent in half and leveled. The resulting butterflies must be glued to the card, applying glue to the sharp bends of the parts.
  8. The final step in decorating the product is gluing a rhinestone on the openwork edge of white paper.

If for the manufacture of such crafts there are no necessary seals, then butterflies can be drawn independently. They will look no worse than printed ones.

To the delight of lovers

Bright holiday Valentine's Day has millions of fans across the globe. Therefore, lovers are trying to give each other the most beautiful cards and valentines. And how to decorate a home-made card in the style of lovers, we will consider below.





As can be seen from the photo of the craft, it is not at all difficult to make it. For this you will need: a sheet of cardboard folded in half, hearts made of paper of different colors, a bow from a satin ribbon, white dense threads, a sheet of gray cardboard. To do all the work in the following order:

  1. Stick a rectangle made of gray cardboard onto a sheet of cardboard folded in half.
  2. Stick hearts with threads in the form of balloons on a rectangle.
  3. Glue a satin ribbon bow at the bottom of the threads gathered in a bundle .

The card is completely ready, but if you wish, you can paste many multi-colored hearts of various sizes inside it.
