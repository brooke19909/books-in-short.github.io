Hotels in Timashevsk: addresses, phone numbers, rooms, reviews and ratings

Timashevsk is a city of the Krasnodar Territory with extensive infrastructure and rich history. It is located at the intersection of railways. A cardboard factory operates in the city, construction materials are produced and the famous Kuban candy factory is located here.

The article tells about the most famous hotels in Timashevsk. Cost of living, services and conditions, as well as customer reviews.

Timashevsk: Hotels, motels, guest houses

This city in the Kuban is very often visited by tourists who are sent to the Crimea or to the sea. Here you can find housing both in the center and on the periphery, closer to the highway. For a long stay, you can rent a guest house or move into one of the houses of the hotel complex.

Hotel "Tourist"

The hotel is located on Turgenev street, house 18. Hotels and hotels in Timashevsk are mostly located in the city center. The cost of living in this hotel is from 3,500 rubles. In the rating, according to reviews of tourists, it is in the first place.

The hotel is made in an expensive luxurious style. A two-story house with columns and a veranda attracts many tourists. On the inside there is a beautiful garden, on the other hand, the windows overlook the city.

At the entrance to the hotel it seems that you get into the most expensive hotel in New York. Tiled floors, printed wallpaper, glossy ceilings and all this is highlighted by the bright light of the lamps. Chic, brilliance and high cost - these are the main directions in the interior of the hotel. Leather sofas and unusual plants fit perfectly into the interior.

The rooms also continue the theme of luxury. All apartments have huge paintings on the walls. Here you can admire the sea views and floral motifs. The bathroom has a shower or bathtub. A hairdryer, bathrobes and toiletries are also available.

Guests praise this hotel well. It is conveniently located relative to the track. Chic repair and courteous staff. Breakfast is a pleasant surprise (seven options to choose from). The hotel does not have its own cafe or restaurant. Guests believe that this is the only negative. Everything is clean, convenient and comfortable. Timashevsk hotels offer quality and affordable service to their guests.

Hotel "Theta"

This is a place where tourists can stay for the night, located on Internatsionalnaya Street, 11. This is the city center. However, for those who are first time in Timashevsk, finding a hotel is difficult. The thing is that it is located above the building of the shopping center, and the entrance to the hotel is separate, from the end of the building. In the dark, hard to navigate. It is in second place, according to tourists.

The cost of living is from 1,500 rubles per day. You can book a room either on the website or by phone. On the ground floor there is a wonderful cafe serving delicious and hearty breakfasts. Tea and coffee at the hotel are free.

The interior of the rooms is classic. There are rooms in blue, beige and peach tones. All apartments have their own bathroom, TV, fridge, air conditioning and a set of dishes.

Guests like this hotel. In their reviews, they note that more than once they stayed in it on the way to the Crimea. Hotels in Timashevsk in the Krasnodar Territory are famous for their hospitality. At the first visit, it is difficult to find this cozy place, but the polite and friendly administrator answers all questions and tells you how to get there, even at night.

Hotel "Central"

The hotel opens its doors to the following address: 54 Krasnaya Street. The cost of living is from 2 500 rubles. There are apartments of different levels of comfort. Hotels in Timashevsk (Krasnodar Territory) allow you to make reservations both by phone and on the site, without prepayment.

The interior of the rooms is a bit like a rustic style or French rural motifs. Many floral prints on both wallpaper and bedspreads. The rooms have a standard set of furniture, a bathroom, a TV and a refrigerator.

Near the hotel there is a park, the arch of lovers and a museum. You can also visit the shopping center or other attractions of the city. Not all hotels in Timashevsk boast a similar location.

Guests have a good opinion about the hotel. Most often, visitors stop at it. Convenient location and comfortable conditions contribute to this. In their reviews, guests say that the interior seems ridiculous at first, but gradually you get used to it. The rooms are clean and comfortable. Guests believe that the hotel may be in third place in the ranking of the best holiday destinations in Timashevsk.

Guest house "Horizon"

A small hotel is located at 20. Smolenskaya Street. The affordable cost of living (from 800 rubles per day) attracts many tourists. Guests rate this mini-hotel at 8 points and put it in fifth place in the ranking.

Actually, this is an ordinary private house, which is converted into a mini-hotel. Spacious shared kitchen with a TV, fridge, kettle, microwave and utensils. Also, the bathroom and toilet are nearby and are shared by all guests. The hotel has three rooms of different capacities (from 2 to 4 people). Each room has its own TV. Internet is available throughout the house. There is parking for cars living.

Vacationers like this place. In their reviews, they say that for a small fee you can live in comfortable conditions. Hotels in Timashevsk are much more expensive than guest houses. Such apartments are convenient to rent a large family or friendly company. You can prepare meals and have family dinners in the kitchen.

Hotel complex "Swedish Village"

In this place you can stay for the night, as well as stay for a long stay. The complex is located at: Sadovod microdistrict, street 70 years of October, house 6A. The hotel is 3 kilometers from the city center. The cost of living is from 3,000 rubles. There are rooms of different levels of comfort. The complex is in sixth place in the rating by guest reviews.

Visitors can stay in one of 20 rooms in the main building or in family villas. There are also houses for several companies.

The houses are made of solid wood. The interior is dominated by wood. There are beautiful carpets on the floor. Upholstered furniture in harmony with curtains and textiles. In the morning, a continental breakfast is served. Guests can use the sauna or outdoor pool (free of charge). Throughout the Internet, there is parking for cars.

Guests enjoy visiting this hotel. In the reviews, they say that they like to take a steam bath in the sauna and refresh themselves in the pool. One minus - there are no signs to find the hotel. Regardless of how many people stay at the hotel, a buffet breakfast is served in the morning. A good selection of dishes, so you can have a hearty meal.

Conclusion
