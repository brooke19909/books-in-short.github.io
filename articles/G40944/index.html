Hotel Dolphin (Croatia) - a charming vacation spot

Turquoise sea waters, the gentle sun, aromas of a pine forest - all this awaits tourists in Croatia. Nature in these parts is striking in its beauty: there are numerous lakes, green forests and rocky shores, clean air and enchanting landscapes. All this attracts many tourists on vacation. In addition, Croatia has a well-developed infrastructure and hotel service. Hotel "Dolphin" (Croatia) is located near the tourist center and the cozy town of Porec.

Location

The hotel is located in a cozy place, surrounded by a majestic pine park. Only sixty kilometers separate the Hotel Dolphin (Croatia) from the international airport of Pula. The nearest center, and this is the town of Porec, is only five kilometers away. The beach area is 50 meters from the hotel, which is very convenient and comfortable for tourists. Near the "Dolphin" are a supermarket and a variety of restaurants and cafes.



Building features

Hotel Dolphin (Croatia) is a six-story building with 793 rooms. This is a standard apartment for one, two and three people. The rooms have a telephone, comfortable bathtubs or showers, some have access to a balcony and terraces. The hotel has made excellent modern repairs and has an outdoor pool. Hotel Dolphin (Croatia) is a great place for a budget holiday.

Beach area

Very close, a few dozen meters from the hotel, is a cozy beach. It mainly consists of pebbles, partially covered with a concrete platform. For comfort, use sun loungers, the price of renting them with umbrellas is only three euros. This beach has a certificate of quality, which speaks of the best and most environmentally friendly beach area. Calmness and harmony distinguish the hotel "Dolphin" (Croatia). Photos of this excellent corner for rest are bewitching and call to return again.



Supply system

At certain times, breakfast, lunch and dinner are held, all meals are organized according to the "buffet" system. An interesting and attractive moment will be themed dinners, which take place once a week. Vegetarians can enjoy a special menu.

Services and entertainment

A great place for a relaxing family vacation will be Croatia (Hotel Dolphin). Prices in this complex in high season per room range from 80 to 100 dollars per day. One of the main advantages of this hotel complex is a huge outdoor pool with sea water, a comfortable sun terrace with sun loungers and umbrellas (its area is more than 300 square meters). Also on site are a sauna and a solarium, a hairdresser.

In the hotel you can use the safe, internet zone, relax in the TV room. Lovers of outdoor activities Hotel "Dolphin" (Croatia) offers windsurfing and diving, football or basketball. There are also well-equipped playgrounds for mini-golf, volleyball, table and tennis. Those wishing to enjoy the beauties of the coast can rent catamarans or try their hand at water skiing and canoeing. And at the end of the vacation, be sure to purchase a memorabilia in a souvenir shop on site.
