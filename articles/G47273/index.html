Headphones Sony Extra Bass: review, advantages and disadvantages

For those who value expressive and deep bass in musical compositions, Sony Extra Bass headphones have been created. The accessory is developed on the basis of technologies that reproduce low-frequency resonating sounds, which provides excellent bass sound in musical compositions of any direction.

High-quality low-frequency sounds can be reproduced thanks to the Advanced Vibe Structure technology used in Sony headphones. It is based on a specially created acoustic volume inside the earphone that naturally amplifies low frequencies. Thanks to this development, the company managed to create a lineup of Sony Extra Bass headphones, which are characterized by high quality, dynamics and purity of reproduced low frequencies and bass.

For a complete immersion in music, comfortable headphones with high-quality and excellent sound are required. The new generation of Sony headphones was developed taking into account these foundations, which were laid down in the accessory design concept: the MDR-XB generation is lighter, thinner and more compact, created using the highest quality materials. Models of this series can be used both for leisure and for traveling: Extra Bass headphones provide the necessary level of comfort, quality and sound style even during long listening to music.





Vibrant low-frequency resonance

Expressive and deep bass, coupled with high-quality and clear sound - the most accurate description of Sony Extra Bass headphones. The MDR-XB series was created for music lovers and lovers of bright bass, the rhythm of which sets the rhythm of the whole day.

At the heart of Sony Extra Bass headphones is the technology for reproducing low-frequency resonating sounds in musical compositions of any genres, which guarantees excellent and clear bass sound.

Dynamic and high-quality sound

The vibrant sound of the MDR-XB line of headphones is provided by Advanced Vibe Structure technology. Sony's innovative development makes it possible to reliably, dynamically and powerfully reproduce low-frequency sounds in any musical composition.

The official review of Sony Extra Bass headphones from the manufacturer indicates that this technology is a specially created acoustic volume inside the headphone that naturally amplifies low frequencies. Thanks to this innovation, the company managed to create a unique line of headphones, which are characterized by the quality, dynamics and purity of low frequencies, regardless of the genre of the music being listened to.





Unique and unrivaled design

Full immersion in music is impossible not only without high-quality and excellent sound, but also without a convenient, ergonomic and comfortable design. Headphones Sony Extra Bass were originally created and adapted to the modern lifestyle, which was reflected in the appearance of the gadget.

The new MDR-XB line of headphones is thinner, lighter and more compact; they are made from high quality materials. They can be used both when relaxing at home, and while traveling: the gadget provides a high level of comfort, sound quality and style.

Model Sony MDR-XB950BT

A versatile and high-quality headphone model that works with and without wires. To listen to music on the go, the Bass Boost wireless mode is most suitable: it is universal for listening to any tracks. The disabled Bass Boost function is compensated by an amplifier, which is used for wired connection and listening to uncompressed music files. For those who prefer club sound, this model of headphones is ideal - it allows you to listen to music for 20 hours on a battery charge in wireless mode.

Headphones have been available on the Russian market since September 2014. The average price in the official store of the manufacturer is 11 thousand rubles.

Headphones MDR-XB450BV

The case of the model is completely plastic, but the original radial pattern on the cups and the difference in textures give the impression that metal was used to create the headphones. Despite this, many users say that I would like to add real metal to the design.

The wire bends easily and does not get tangled during operation. The design and the curved shape of the plug allow you to connect headphones to any gadget. A rather stiff headband often causes excessive pressure on the head while listening to music, so it is recommended to try on a model before buying. However, the headphones are adjusted very quickly and easily, which allows you to adjust them to individual preferences.

In the instructions for Sony Extra Bass headphones, their features are noted: inside one of the cups there is a compartment for two AAA batteries that power the gadget in vibration mode. It is activated by pressing a special key on another bowl, next to which is the vibration intensity regulator. Both elements are easy to grope.

A rather original solution with batteries is very convenient, but it has its drawback: the batteries discharge very quickly. Those who prefer to constantly use the vibration mode will have to carry extra batteries with them all the time.

The intensity of vibration and bass is adjustable depending on the listening content. It is desirable to reduce the effect for heavy genres with low frequencies, for pop music, on the contrary, increase it. However, if desired, the model can be turned into ordinary headphones, completely disabling vibration mode.

Headphones have a great bass effect, comparable to that in the sound of powerful car speakers. Such features, however, are not for everybody: the fastidious music lover can find flaws in the rather high-quality sound of this model.

For the cost of these headphones, you can purchase a more professional analogue from a professional point of view, however, vibration modes and emphasis at low frequencies allow familiar compositions to sound in a new way, immersed in the bright world of music.
