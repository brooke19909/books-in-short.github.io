Nokia 3210 - a phone from the past: description, features and advantages

A convenient Nokia mobile phone, which was born in the summer of 1999 in Finland. The Nokia 3210 model has established itself in the market of the first cell phones and almost immediately became quite popular. A very nice design, good enough quality, the ability to make calls and send messages literally anywhere brought the phone widespread. Belongs to the budget class.



Phone presentation

The gadget consists of a monoblock, dual-band. Equipped with a monochrome screen with a resolution of 84 by 48 pixels. Its size, in comparison with modern devices, is simply scanty, but at that time it was also a curiosity. There are enough buttons, they are massive and easy to use. Akkumulators Nokia 3210 (type Ni-Mh) offers a capacity of 1250 mAh, which allows you to talk on the phone without interruption for up to five hours. In standby mode, the battery lasts about 5-10 days.

Nokia 3210 is the first mobile phone with a built-in antenna. Despite this, the reception is quite good. The standard channel is GSM 900/1800, there is a built-in modem.

Since this is one of the first models of Nokia phones, there is no question of any Bluetooth, Wi-Fi and camera. Nokia 3210 has only one slot for a standard size SIM card.
