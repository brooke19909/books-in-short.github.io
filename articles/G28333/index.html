Who is the developer? New buildings from the developer

In the modern world, shared construction is widespread . This concept implies a special form of investment activity. At the same time, the developer (this is often a construction organization) is engaged in raising funds from both individuals and legal entities. In world practice, it is accepted that participants in this form of construction are called "equity holders".





Operating principle

According to the terms of the contract, the developer, having received permission to put the property into operation, must transfer all the documents to the participants in shared construction. The role of the latter, as mentioned earlier, can be both legal entities and individuals who have timely concluded an agreement to participate in this type of construction of residential and public buildings. Thus, from the context of this article it can be revealed that the meaning of the word "builder" implies any legal entity that has ownership or lease rights to a particular land plot and is engaged in the search and subsequent attraction of funds from the so-called "equity holders" .





Important Details

Currently, more and more often you can see ads on billboards containing similar phrases: "apartments, offices, new buildings from the builder." What is meant by these words? Let's take a closer look. As mentioned earlier, the developer is a legal entity that undertakes to carry out all measures for the implementation of the investment project. However, all of the above does not allow an accurate definition of the concept under consideration from a legal point of view. Thus, the following wording was adopted by the current legislation: the developer is an authorized person who has the right to manage capital investments for a specific period of time. However, in the event that the organization in question acts as a customer, it is given the name "customer-developer".





Field of activity

A legal entity endowed with such status is often engaged in the search and conclusion of contracts with contractors, suppliers and other organizations that may be necessary for the successful completion of construction work. In addition, it must be remembered that the developer himself is, in a sense, a contractor. Only the general. Consequently, he himself can not only oversee the implementation of construction works, but also engage in them. As a rule, this can be the development of a design project, installation of an alarm system, installation of internal communications, etc. In addition, since the customer-builder for the most part is represented by fairly large organizations that have existed for quite some time, then, accordingly, he has considerable experience in the field of the work under consideration. So, he knows all kinds of aspects and nuances that may arise at each stage of construction. Therefore, such an organization can predict in advance possible options for the development of events and, thus, avoid the most unfavorable of them.
