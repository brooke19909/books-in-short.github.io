How to find a person in Viber by phone number

Viber, aka “Viber”, aka “Vaiba” - the most popular cross-platform messenger. To this day, it is one of the favorite applications of millions of users around the world . Viber has earned its popularity not only due to free messages, calls and lack of advertising. Convenience and simplicity - these are its main trump cards . “We wanted to do something that was super easy to use. What your grandfather could use without your help, ”said one of the creators of the project, Marco Talmon, to the popular blog about the Internet and startups The Next Web .



Viber is intuitive and simple in everything. From authorization to sending a picture that a user can create on the screen of his gadget. Viber was created for communication, and very often users ask themselves how to find a person in Viber. The messenger interface is very convenient. Finding the right contact in the app is easy.

Contacts in Viber, or How to find a person in Viber

Make it easy. The messenger syncs with your contact list automatically. After installing and activating the application, the user sees in the “Contacts” section all the data in his notebook. You can also notice that some contacts have a Viber icon, and some do not.

Contacts with the icon already use the application, and you can immediately chat. If it is not in front of the contact, then the user has not yet installed the messenger, and you can send him an invitation. For the convenience of the user, the application developers moved all contacts using the messenger to a separate Viber tab to improve the visibility and usability of the application.

Ways to Find a Person in Viber

Finding a person in Viber by phone number is the easiest. If you know him, then you can easily find the user in Viber. To do this, go to the “Add Contact” menu, enter the number and click “Continue”. If the contact is registered in Viber, you will see its profile. Just add a contact and start chatting. If the contact is not registered in Viber, the program will offer to give him a name and send an invitation. Also, Viber supports an interesting function - adding a contact using a QR code. You can ask a friend to go to the “QR Code” menu and open the “My QR Code” field. Scan it - and add a new contact! This is one example of how to find a person in Viber.



Miracles of synchronization

All contacts that you add to Viber are synchronized with the list of contacts on your device. If you notice that this did not happen, check the version of the messenger update and the speed of your Internet connection. If necessary, restart the application and reboot the device. This usually solves the problem.



It will not be possible to find the person “Weiber” by first name, last name. Viber is not a social network and positions itself as an application without being tied to an account. Just log in through your phone number and communicate without boundaries, authorization and passwords! Viber is convenient and functional. Now you know how to find a person in Viber . Adding friends, as you see, is easy. Keep in touch!
