Hotels in Krasnaya Polyana, Sochi: overview, description, selection

Krasnaya Polyana is a stunning ski resort in Russia, located in the Sochi region. The Olympic capital rightfully bears the title of a resort of international level, because it was here that in 2014 the Winter Olympic Games were held. Only 40 kilometers from the village there is an airport, and the proximity to the Black Sea makes this place even more attractive. In our article we want to talk about hotels in Krasnaya Polyana (Sochi).

The complex is located on several levels in the center of the resort, in the foothills of the Main Caucasian Range. An incredibly large hotel can simultaneously accommodate up to 10 thousand tourists. At his disposal - more than three thousand rooms in eleven buildings. Apart-hotel "Gorki Gorod" welcomes guests at any time of the year, as the all-season resort is always interesting for tourists.

"Dynasty"

Apart-Hotel Dynasty (Krasnaya Polyana) was designed by the famous architect Krasovsky, who gave him the style of an alpine chalet. Large panoramic windows of the complex allow you to admire the beauty of the mountains at any time of the year. The houses are made of natural materials and equipped with autonomous boiler rooms for heating. All apartments in the complex are equipped with kitchens with all the necessary attributes. Modern technology will make your stay at the hotel comfortable. Inside each apartment, guests can find everything they need: from ironing supplies to cutlery. The institution's apartments are equipped with balconies with stunning views of the mountains, centenary fir.

The hotel is represented by improved studios, suites, family suites, apartments. The complex has a restaurant, a bathhouse and other infrastructure facilities that make the stay at the resort enjoyable.

"Hold"

Hotel Oplot is a beautiful complex in Krasnaya Polyana located at 9a Kalinovaya Street. The hotel is located on a hill surrounded by picturesque mountain ranges. This is one of the most popular places in the resort, as evidenced by the many positive reviews of tourists. Scenic views from the rooms make your stay even more enjoyable. The mountain ranges overgrown with evergreens will not leave anyone indifferent. Extensive balconies allow you to enjoy natural beauties and breathe in the purest air.

Oplot is one of those hotels in Krasnaya Polyana (Sochi) that offers its guests quality service and a well-developed infrastructure. It can comfortably rest couples and large friendly companies.

The hotel complex offers accommodation in rooms of three categories: standards, studios and apartments. They are all good in their own way, have balconies and huge windows. The rooms are equipped with comfortable furniture, satellite TV and toiletries. The hotel provides a variety of leisure services. Friendly and competent staff tries to help tourists in all matters. For guests with cars, free parking is provided. The complex has a real Russian bathhouse. And next to the hotel there are cafes, a market, restaurants and shops.

Bridge Mountain

No less attractive for guests of Krasnaya Polyana resort is the Bridge Mountain Hotel, which is the highest-rise complex. The institution is chosen by supporters of active and family vacations at any time of the year. The hotel infrastructure will make it possible to spend time interestingly for all categories of tourists. It is located in the center of Krasnaya Polyana. Near the hotel there are ski lifts at the Gorki Gorod, Gazprom and Rosa Khutor resorts. In the summer, guests have the opportunity to live in the mountains, and swim - on the coast of the Olympic Park, which is located 40 minutes from the hotel.

The room fund of the institution is represented by economy standards, standards, junior suites and apartments. To services of tourists - a bath spa complex of a modern level. Professional staff will help you spend your time in a peaceful atmosphere.

The hotel has a cafe-bar "Purple Moose" and a bar with karaoke "Perepoy". For business events, a conference room is provided.

"Alpine courtyard"

In the center of the village of Krasnaya Polyana is the Alpine Yard Hotel, at an altitude of 550 m above sea level. The two-story complex is decorated in the form of a chalet, which creates an atmosphere of complete harmony with the entire landscape of the mountain village.

The room fund of the Alpine Courtyard (Krasnaya Polyana) is represented by double, triple rooms, two-room apartments and two-level rooms. All are equipped with refrigerators, televisions, air conditioning, new furniture and bathrooms. Each room has an open balcony and offers stunning mountain views.

The hotel also offers bike rental, a steam room and a tour desk. A caring staff offers excellent service.

Hotel Peak

Resorts should also pay attention to another hotel Krasnaya Polyana in Sochi - "Peak". It is located at the foot of the Caucasus Range (540 m above sea level). The four-star complex offers accommodation in comfortable rooms and villas. The hotel was opened in 2010, after reconstruction. The proximity of the institution with the mountains allows guests to offer unique mountain activities and hiking.

The hotel has a health and beauty center. The hotel is a good place for corporate events. It is convenient to get to the Caucasus Biosphere Reserve from it. In summer, guests can relax on the Black Sea coast, located just 30 minutes from the complex.

The hotel has a good infrastructure. It features a gym, spa, massage rooms and a Finnish sauna. "Peak" is a hotel in Krasnaya Polyana (Sochi) with pools. Several conference rooms of the hotel allow holding seminars and other business events in it.

A five-minute drive from the hotel there are snow trails, which allows guests to take advantage of all the amenities of a mountain resort. The complex has an Italian restaurant and a restaurant "Crystal Hall", as well as a lobby bar.

Hotel Alm

Among the resort hotels there are a lot of establishments offering an extensive infrastructure that allows you to provide a decent vacation to guests. Many tourists prefer hotels with outdoor and indoor pools, where you can swim without spending time traveling to the sea. Indoor ponds are relevant at any time of the year.

One of the Krasnaya Polyana hotels with a swimming pool is Alm, located 32 kilometers from the Khostinsky Fortress and an 18-minute walk from the resort center. The room fund of the institution consists of comfortable apartments with terraces. In the morning, a continental breakfast is served at the restaurant. A seasonal outdoor pool allows guests to swim and enjoy their time by the water.

Rixos Hotel

A great option for people choosing a hotel with a swimming pool can be the Rixos Hotel. The spa and indoor pond combine skiing with pleasant treatments. The hotel complex is located in the village of Esto-Sadok, a hundred meters from the Gornaya Karusel ski lift. The hotel was deservedly recognized as the best in Russia in 2016-2017.

Its rooms are equipped with all amenities and necessary equipment. All apartments overlook the beautiful mountains. Most of them have balconies, and the suites have a living room. The hotel has its own night club and restaurant.

It features a modern spa with sauna, Turkish hammam, hydromassage, indoor pool and fitness center. Hotel guests have the opportunity to attend massages, beauty and wellness treatments. In addition to all other infrastructure, the hotel has a kids club.

Rosa Springs

Rosa Springs Hotel is located in the Rosa Khutor resort area. The complex has its own spa and pool. All rooms are equipped with air conditioning, TVs, tea / coffee sets, furniture and bathrooms. Some apartments have a living room.

A buffet is served daily. The hotel has not only a restaurant, but also a phytobar offering healthy drinks.
