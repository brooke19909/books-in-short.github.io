Hotel The Park Hotel 3 * (Pattaya, Thailand): photos and reviews of tourists

Each of us has the right to a well-deserved rest. Sometimes we get so tired of the hustle and bustle that we want to go to a heavenly place located on the coast for at least a week. This place is just Thailand. The most popular resort here is Pattaya - the center of resort life along the coast. It is here that you can not only get a beautiful even tan, enjoy the warm rays of the sun and swim in the cool sea, but also get to know Asian culture, local attractions and entertainment more closely. Pattaya offers great opportunities for both beach and sightseeing vacations, for which tourists from different parts of the globe managed to fall in love with it.

But where to stay here? Tour operators offer hotels of various price categories. Which one to choose? We want to bring to the attention of all those tourists and travelers who are just planning a trip to Thailand, The Park Hotel 3 * (Pattaya), which can become a good option for living. Reviews of real vacationers will help to make an objective assessment of this hotel. Let's get to know them better.





About the specifics of hotels in Pattaya

Pattaya is the main tourist center of Thailand. In season, there is an incredible number of vacationers from various corners of the globe. Nevertheless, there are no problems with accommodation due to the huge number of hotels and hotels of various categories. All beaches of the coast are municipal, which is why it is foolish to expect that you will find an option on the coast here, at least you have to cross the road. In Pattaya, the coastline, that is, the beach, is separated from the city by the roadway.

There are practically no all-inclusive hotels, most often breakfast is included in the price. These are small subtleties that allow you to objectively look at local hotels.





About The Park Hotel

So, Nova Park Hotel 3 * (Pattaya) is an inexpensive hotel located just 200 meters from the beach, literally across the road. The hotel boasts proximity to the central part of the resort, due to which vacationers will always be aware of all the events, will be able to take an active part in the cultural and nightlife of the city.

This is one of the most famous and popular hotels in Pattaya. The staff of The Park Hotel 3 * (Pattaya) is extremely polite and hospitable, attentive enough to every vacationer. Tourists note that from the moment of arrival to the issuance of keys no more than 10-15 minutes pass. At the hotel you can find enough signage in Russian, which only confirms the focus on Russian tourists and vacationers.





The hotel is represented by several buildings, one of which is also a tower building, which received the title of the tallest building in Southeast Asia. The main building is represented by 270 rooms, and the tower building - 460 comfortable rooms. The hotel was awarded 3 stars, but many of those tourists who spent their holidays here find it amazing.

Rooms

If you pay great attention in choosing a hotel to rooms, their decor and level of comfort, you definitely should like The Park Hotel 3 * (Pattaya). Most reviews are similar to each other: no matter what type of room you choose for yourself, it will definitely have a comfortable bathroom, toilet, satellite TV, wireless Internet, as well as its own balcony and minibar. The vast majority of rooms at The Park Hotel 3 * (Pattaya) are facing the coast, so it offers a beautiful view of local beauty - you can enjoy the sound of the surf in the morning, meet the sunrises and see off the sunsets.





Many vacationers are interested in the availability of a safe in the room, where without any fears and fears one could leave valuables, documents and money. Unfortunately, the rooms do not have a safe, but all tourists can use the safe at the reception for free.

The rooms in the hotel are bright and spacious, in many respects the effect is ensured by panoramic windows and panoramic sliding doors leading to the balcony. In the bathroom, in addition to towels for the face, legs and shower, tourists are offered cosmetic products. Unfortunately, there is no hairdryer in the bathroom, and if necessary it can be ordered at the reception. I want to say a few words about the minibar: it is paid. Any drink you take from there will have to be paid.

There is a tile on the floor, there are no rugs, even in the bedside zone. Tourists should be extremely cautious, including with equipment: when the phone hits the tile, it shatters. If you like warmth and homeliness, it is better to bring slippers with you. Speaking about the shortcomings, I want to mention the sockets - there are only three of them in the room. Given the huge arsenal of equipment in the baggage of every tourist, three outlets may simply not be enough - be sure to bring an extension cord with you.





The room does not have a wardrobe - only hangers and shelves. Basically, there is enough space to arrange all the things that you take with you on a trip.

Service

When checking into The Park Hotel 3 * hotel (Pattaya), tourists do not have to pay a deposit, the password for wireless Internet will be issued along with the keys at the reception. The kind staff will bring your things to the room, which will delight you after a tiring and long journey.

Rooms are cleaned daily, towels and bed linen are changed upon request. Every day, 0.5 bottles of water are allocated for each tourist. The hotel staff is friendly and polite, will try to answer all your questions, will help in choosing an excursion program.

Hotel Territory

In fact, all the entertainment that is so necessary for tourists and vacationers in the resort, Pattaya can offer in sufficient variety. Pattaya The Park Hotel 3 * offers all vacationers to visit one of the two chic outdoor pools, a sauna, a spa complex, where you can enjoy massage and a number of cosmetic procedures. All sports vacationers will be able to start their morning in the equipped gym. As already noted, The Park Hotel 3 * (Thailand, Pattaya) is located in the very center of the resort, so there are many shops, souvenir shops, and beauty salons. There is even a diving school - a great opportunity to gain the necessary skills for all those who plan to go diving.





Be sure to visit the water park, the entrance to its territory costs 100 baht. This is an amazing water attraction, from which not only children, but also adults will be delighted.

Nutrition

The hotel has several restaurants where all holidaymakers can enjoy national Thai or European cuisine. Be sure to visit the most famous spinning restaurant in the tower, which offers stunning views of local beauty.

The price of the hotel includes breakfast in the restaurant, located on the same floor as the reception. Time for breakfast is from 6-00 to 10-00, if tourists go on an early excursion, they can always order breakfast with them, for this it is necessary to notify the administration the day before. Breakfast is varied enough, you definitely will not stay hungry. Tourists complain about the lack of dairy products, fresh pastries, but this is already a matter of taste. Most often, breakfast is given scrambled eggs or scrambled eggs, sausages, cold cuts of meat and cheese, rice, vegetables, fresh fruits, aromatic coffee and tea.

Beach and sea

The beach is located through the harbor from the hotel, it is municipal, but at the same time quite clean. And all this is Pattaya. Pattaya Nova Park Hotel 3 * really can only boast of the benefits of its location. Quite a lot of vacationers gather on the beach in season. The sea is clean, but it is difficult to call it transparent here. If you want to enjoy the crystal clear sea water and run along the snow-white sand, go to the city pier, get a ferry ticket for just 60 baht, you will be delivered to the island closest to the resort - Koh Lan.





Entertainment

The hotel is located in the center of the resort, so all entertainment is available here. Near the hotel there are many shops, bars, restaurants, souvenir shops. The beach is equipped with water sports and sports equipment rental. The kind staff of the hotel will help in choosing an excursion program, prompt contacts of the best travel agencies of the coast.

To summarize

In general, The Park Hotel 3 * (Pattaya) is a good hotel, despite being one of the oldest in the resort. The staff is friendly, trying to satisfy all the wishes of vacationers. The hotel is fully and fully consistent with its value: spacious rooms equipped with everything necessary for relaxation, first-class service and proximity to the cultural and historical sights of the city. If you do not know where to stay and spend your vacation, this is a great option. We wish you a pleasant stay and an unforgettable experience.
