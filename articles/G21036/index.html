Hotel Claridge Hotel Dubai 3 *, United Arab Emirates: photos, reviews

The UAE is a state that is often visited by tourists from Russia for recreation. It is no secret that the best conditions for living are offered here, as well as excellent service. Hotels located in this country are very popular among travelers. One of them is Claridge Hotel Dubai 3 *, located in the city of Dubai.

Many tourists in their reviews of Claridge Hotel Dubai often notice that it is perfect for a vacation with friends, a romantic trip or for a stay with the aim of solving business issues, since such a vacation spot is located near the quarter with business centers.

Location

Claridge Hotel Dubai is located in the UAE, in one of the most famous resort cities - Dubai. The hotel is located in the famous district of the city - Deira, in which large markets are concentrated, where it is possible to purchase the most interesting goods. Often, tourists go here for souvenirs for relatives and friends.

Not far from the location of the hotel there is a business quarter, which can be easily reached using taxi or regular buses passing through the bus stop, which is 300 meters from Claridge Hotel Dubai 3 *.

The nearest air traffic point, where, as a rule, foreign tourists arrive, is also nearby - literally 7 kilometers away. Travelers note that this location is quite convenient.





Rooms

The hotel offers accommodation in 98 comfortable apartments, which are concentrated in a large four-story building. Each room has a private balcony - vacationers rate it as having positive comments. From the balconies you have a beautiful view of the surroundings of the metropolis, and from some you can explore the beautiful expanses of the sea.

During one of the latest renovations in the rooms of the hotel, a quality repair was made that fully complies with European standards. Many travelers note the excellent technical equipment of the apartments, as well as the beautiful interior, which offers a relaxing holiday.

Most of the rooms in this vacation spot belong to the category of standard, there are also several suites. Each of them is equipped with a modern bathroom, where there is everything that is necessary to maintain hygiene: shower, sink and bathroom. If desired, guests can use the bath accessories provided by the hotel, which are located in the cabinets installed here.



Standard

Standard class apartments at Claridge Hotel Dubai 3 * (Dubai / Deira) are represented by 88. Each room is located on a small area - 26 square meters. m, on which there is one room - a bedroom, designed for three guests.

For guests in this apartment has three beds, which are represented by single and double beds. Near them are equipped with cabinets in which it is possible to store clothes and a small amount of personal items. The room has a TV connected to the cable broadcasting system - according to travelers, it broadcasts several Russian-language channels, which is pretty good.

On a paid basis, vacationers can use the safe and minibar, the filling of which is updated every day with fresh soft drinks. Hotel guests can use the Internet and stay in touch with their families and friends.



Suite

Suite rooms offer vacationers superior comfort. They are located on the top floor of the building, thanks to which tourists living in apartments of this level can enjoy a beautiful view of the urban surroundings.

The suites are divided into two rooms, one of which is a bedroom, and the second is a living room. The furniture equipment available here is designed for two guests, in connection with which rooms of this type are often chosen by couples for a romantic trip.

The bedroom has a large double bed with a high mattress and expensive hypoallergenic bedding. On either side of it there are two small pedestals, one of which has a landline telephone. In the living room, the centerpiece is a large furniture set, consisting of a soft sofa with plush upholstery and a pair of armchairs. Between them there is a small coffee table made of shockproof glass, and on the contrary there is a plasma TV. In addition, the living room is equipped with a workspace, which consists of a desk and a chair.



Guests of these apartments often celebrate their excellent technical equipment. According to tourists, a wonderful climate equipment has been installed here, with which guests can independently adjust the desired temperature in the rooms. Also, vacationers can use the safe and minibar.

Services

Guests staying at the hotel can take advantage of a number of opportunities that are available here. In particular, one of the most popular among them is a transfer. Such a service is provided absolutely free of charge and is carried out in a comfortable minibus from the hotel to the beach or shopping center and back.

Also, vacationers can take advantage of car rental - this opportunity is paid, rent is charged hourly. Vacationers note that with this machine you can go to explore interesting sights, which are many in the district, as well as visit entertainment places - the largest number of them are concentrated in the central part of Dubai. For a rented car, the hotel provides a free parking space on its territory.

On the ground floor of the building there is a small shop selling souvenirs. Next to it there is an Internet club where every tourist can spend time on computer equipment for a fee. The hotel also provides dry cleaning and laundry services.



For business

Guests who are in the Claridge Hotel Dubai 3 * (United Arab Emirates / Dubai / Deira) on a business trip, often take the opportunity to rent a conference room, which is in the hotel building. Its area is well equipped for various events related to doing business. Here, modern sound and lighting equipment is installed, which allows you to make any event more vivid.

As seats for guests in the hall there are comfortable chairs and lacquered tables made of dark wood.

Nutrition

Fans of delicious food do not remain indifferent to the dishes served at Claridge Hotel Dubai. There is a luxurious restaurant where a team of professional chefs can please even the most demanding gourmets with perfectly prepared Indian dishes. Here every day a buffet is organized, where guests staying on an all-inclusive basis are invited. Guests note that the food served here has not only amazing taste, but also a great aroma, obtained through the proper use of spices. The restaurant menu has a considerable number of specialties.



The hotel also has a coffee bar. It is recommended to visit by many vacationers, telling in their reviews that the menu of this institution has a huge number of varieties of tea and coffee. In addition, they can offer a wide selection of cocktails and drinks, among which there are many alcoholic ones. If desired, vacationers can enjoy local desserts and snacks.

Sports and spa

Lovers of an active lifestyle often visit the gym, which is located in the building of the hotel. There are several units that allow guests to maintain their body in great shape. Visiting this place is completely free.

Prices

Many travelers in their comments left after a vacation at Claridge Hotel Dubai share the cost of a tour to this hotel. Most of them consider it quite acceptable, given the conditions that are provided on its territory. The cost of a ticket to this place is about 45,000 rubles. Purchased at this price, the tour includes a six-day stay at the Claridge Hotel Dubai for two in a standard room. In addition, it includes the all-inclusive concept, which implies the possibility of food in a luxurious restaurant, a visit to the excellent spa area and gym.

If desired, vacationers can pre-book a tour of the interesting places that are located in the city of Dubai and its environs, but many travelers do not recommend this to Russian travel agencies.
