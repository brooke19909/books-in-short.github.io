How to choose a navigator for a car: some useful tips

With the advent of GPS navigators, many drivers breathed a sigh of relief. Now you can easily get anywhere in the city or country, because a smart device will always tell you the right direction, let you know when to turn, and what kind of interference can wait on the road. But in order not to be disappointed in your purchase, you should know how to choose a navigator for the car. Now you will find out what you should pay attention to.

Navigation system



You should start with it, because this is the main thing, without which the navigator is no longer a navigator. Each navigation system works with a specific brand of GPS. Those. To understand how to choose a car navigator, you need to determine the system, thereby narrowing the search for models. Currently, four navigation systems are common in our country:

  • Garmin - is considered a leader among all navigation systems, is very popular.
  • Navitel - provides the most matched maps, more than once made attempts to provide full voice control of the device.
  • iGO is a budget navigation system, but the quality of the maps is quite decent.
  • Autosatellite - a domestic navigation system, works mainly with Hyundai and GlobalSat. The advantage of Autosatellite is that it allows you to track traffic jams in real time.

Display



To know how to choose a navigator for the car, you must definitely decide on the size of the display. It can be both 3.5 inches, and all 7. Manufacturers are not tired of improving their new products, making the screen as large as possible. Small displays are the cheapest, the picture quality is not up to par. Therefore, the larger the screen, the better. Especially in the event that with the help of the navigator you are going to not only get directions, but also watch videos, surf the Internet (there are also such models).

Memory size

It should be enough to load maps. Therefore, if there is not enough internal, it should be possible to install an additional memory card.

Mounting reliability

In search of an answer to the question of how to choose a GPS navigator, one should also consider the mounting mechanism. It must be reliable and not fly, for example, in the event of a collision with a bump or pit.

Additional features



Currently on sale you can find a wide variety of navigators. They differ not only in cost, but also in their functionality. Think about what you need, because choosing a navigator for a car means getting a device that will fully satisfy your needs. The presence of Bluetooth allows you to update maps via satellite and control the device without stopping from driving, and Wi-Fi to connect to the Internet and use the navigator as a PC. You can choose a device on which it will not be difficult to watch movies and listen to music. Again, the more versatile the filling, the higher the cost.

Now you know how to choose a navigator for the car. More precisely, we got acquainted with the most important thing, which we should definitely pay attention to. You should also take an interest in the opinions of other people about a particular model, about its advantages and disadvantages.
