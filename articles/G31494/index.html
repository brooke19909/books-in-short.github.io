Holidays in Vietnam: a few reasons for this country

So, if you are embarrassed by a trip to Asia or you do not consider yourself a category of people who are easily ready to experiment with their own vacation, then read our article and perhaps you will start planning a trip to Vietnam in the near future.

Hotels in Vietnam provide a very high level of service even with a small number of stars. That is why in the country you can relax quite cheaply and safely.

The beaches of Vietnam can not be described - they are simply incomparable! Almost all travelers who have at least once rested in this Asian country know this. The main beaches of Vietnam are clean and stretch for many kilometers. Almost all of them are state owned and are well equipped. Absolutely anyone will like a beach holiday here, because in many resorts of the country there is a gentle entrance to the water, and the sandy bottom is ideal for kids.

The excursion program in the country is quite diverse. Tourists can provide trips to historical places, as well as a number of recreational excursions that will please extreme people.

Nha Trang: the best resort of Vietnam

Nha Trang is considered the tourist capital of the country, Russians love to relax here and praise this amazing place in every way. Of course, this resort can please the inhabitants of our country yearned for sea and sun. Three beaches of Nha Trang are considered one of the best in the country, and the bay is successfully covered by islands and is ideal for diving. Moreover, it is in this resort that the best diving schools are located on the entire coast.

In addition to beach activities, Nha Trang is ready to please holidaymakers with an entire island, which houses the huge entertainment center Winperl. You can get here by cable car, stretching directly from the port. Many tourists consider it a bright attraction of the resort.

If you decide to go on vacation to Nha Trang, then choose the hotel. Best for a family with kids, Lucky Star Hotel 2 * (Nha Trang) is suitable. Reviews of tourists about this hotel complex allow you to confidently recommend it to other travelers. So that you can make your own opinion about this hotel, try to talk about it in more detail.

Hotel infrastructure

For a two-star hotel, Lucky Star Beach Hotel 2 * copes quite well with its duties. Of course, he cannot please the vacationers with a large number of restaurants and bars, but the quality of the services provided can completely cover this disadvantage.

From all that our compatriots usually expect, Lucky Star Hotel 2 * (Vietnam) can offer guests one restaurant, reception, elevator and a large parking lot. Hotel Accommodation Features

Lucky Star Hotel 2 * (Nha Trang) settles guests after two in the afternoon. In some cases, when the number is free, administrators can make concessions to you and settle you much earlier than the deadline. Please note that pets are not allowed in the room rules. If this fact is concealed, the guest will have to pay a fine and vacate the occupied room.

Check-out is carried out before twelve o'clock in the afternoon, by this time the hotel guests must pay all bills and remove their belongings from the room. Please note that tourists can only pay in cash. No one will accept bank cards at Lucky Star Beach Hotel 2 *. Therefore, when checking into the room, have a small amount of cash on hand.

Many of our compatriots who rested at Lucky Star Hotel 2 * always leave positive feedback on the work of the reception. Hotel guests note that the administrators are always friendly, ready to answer any question and even understand a little Russian. Moreover, this is quite a rarity among the Vietnamese, they are usually extremely difficult given the Russian language.

Service in the hotel complex

We already mentioned that the hotel staff at any time of the day or night in the highest class serves guests staying at the Lucky Star Hotel 2 *, Nha Trang. Reviews of tourists about their pastime in the hotel allow us to conclude that the hotel is able in the near future to get at least one star more.

In the rooms of the hotel complex you can always use free Wi-Fi and order dishes from the restaurant at any time. If you need to exchange currency or rent a car for several days, you can always do this without leaving the hotel. You can also just buy excursions in Nha Trang - a small bureau operates in the hotel complex, the staff of which, taking into account all the voiced wishes, will select several trips that can diversify your vacation.

Dirty clothes can be sent to the laundry, but this service is paid separately at the hotel rate. The maids will return you clean and ironed clothes (of course, for extra money). Some tourists who travel to Asian countries prefer to take their basic things to the left luggage room. At Lucky Star Beach Hotel 2 *, it is present, for a small fee your things will be stored securely throughout the entire stay in the room.

Quite often, Russians vacationing at the hotel used the services of a ticket office. Here you can buy tickets for a train, plane or for any concert. All this can be done without even leaving the cozy reception.

Fun for kids

Unfortunately, children at the hotel will be bored. There is no games room for them, and babysitting services are not even provided. For children under twelve years of age, sharing a room in the same bed with parents will be free. An extra bed is for children under sixteen. Its cost and necessity is negotiated at the stage of booking a room.

Despite the lack of playgrounds, families with children often stay at this hotel. In addition to the beautiful green area, there is a water park nearby, where you can spend the whole day. You can get to him in just fifteen minutes.

Hotel Rooms

The hotel consists of thirty-four rooms, each of which is in good condition, which is another significant argument in favor of choosing the Lucky Star Hotel 2 *. Photos of tourists posted on the Internet confirm the fact that the hotel rooms have new furniture, appliances and plumbing. In general, they create a rather favorable impression.

The rooms are divided into four categories:

  • double standard, where on the territory of twenty-five square meters can accommodate two people;
  • Superior double room has two beds and can accommodate two people;
  • deluxe room has a sea view;
  • the family room is the largest and can accommodate four people in two rooms.

Each room is well equipped, it has everything you need:

  • air conditioning;
  • mini bar;
  • bath accessories;
  • bathrobes and slippers;
  • bath set;
  • plasma with satellite connection;
  • safe.

Agree that for a hotel with two stars this is a rather extensive list. And all this is new and works properly. Rooms are cleaned daily, but according to Russian vacationers "in an Asian manner" - a bit superficially.

In some cases, tourists were annoyed by wet towels and the obsessive smell of damp, but even the hotel guests themselves attributed this trouble to rainy weather outside. Indeed, in the rainy season in Vietnam it can be very damp for several days in a row. Hotel guests also note that maids can skip one day and do the cleaning, so at the reception you must indicate if you need a maid in your room today.

Hotel beach

Lucky Star Beach Hotel 2 * does not have its own beach. Hotel guests will need to go to the municipal beach, where a certain fee is set for sunbeds and umbrellas. At the entrance to the beach, ticket offices are usually set up or special people are standing who keep records of free sunbeds.

At the reception, vacationers can get beach towels and even a thermos, where it is convenient to make tea to take it with you. Many tourists consider the best beach, which is located near the hotel Lucky Star Hotel 2 * (Vietnam). Reviews about him leave quite often, mostly they are positive. The sea within the beach is clean, and the sand is cleaned daily, so vacationers do not even see the garbage that is brought in by the waves.

Some tourists in their comments clarify that the sea near the hotel is extremely turbulent. But this fact is most likely due to the fact that from December to February frequent storms come to Nha Trang. In another season, the sea is extremely calm and offers swimming.
