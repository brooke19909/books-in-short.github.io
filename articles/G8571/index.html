Aegean Park (Marmaris): hotel description, service, reviews

Aegean Park (Marmaris) is a famous hotel in Turkey, which is loved by many tourists. It is located in one of the most famous resorts of this country, in a picturesque port, right on the Mediterranean coast.

Resort in Marmaris

Aegean Park (Marmaris) is well known to tourists from around the world, because every year this resort receives about two million vacationers. Among them are many of our compatriots.





Marmaris itself is located along the coast of the bay, on all sides the port city is surrounded by mountains. It is comfortable to relax here, because storms never reach the coast. The entrance to the bay protects the island, which takes the brunt. Arriving in Marmaris, you can get acquainted with the history of Greece. The ancient island of Rhodes is regularly served by ferry.

What to see in Marmaris?

Tours to Marmaris are in stable demand among almost all categories of tourists who choose Turkey for holidays. It is an ancient city, although the exact date of its foundation is unknown. The first mention of it in official documents dates back to the 11th century BC. Then he was known as Fiskos. In the X century BC, the city came under the protectorate of Rhodes. Later, the Persians settled in it. In 334 BC, Marmaris was conquered by Alexander of Macedon, after which he returned to Rhodes.

In the second century BC, the Romans began to rule the city, soon finally joining it in the Roman Empire. In 395, Marmaris is part of the Byzantine Empire. In the 13th century, it was captured by the Ottoman Turks, and later it was annexed to the Ottoman Empire.



In the XVI century, Sultan Suleiman the Magnificent orders here to build a fortress to protect the city from external enemies and invaders forever. At that time, the settlement gets its current name, which literally means "brilliant", "shining." In 1957, a strong earthquake struck the city. As a result, Marmaris was destroyed almost to the ground. It had to be restored from scratch. The city received a powerful impetus for economic development in the 80s, when it became one of the tourist centers of Turkey.

Among the attractions that especially attract tourists who have taken tours to Marmaris, it is worth noting the ancient castle of Marmaris Fort, which is located in the old part of the city. Now a museum is open there. In the eastern part of the resort is the so-called Street of bars. This is a popular walking area with a lot of restaurants, cafes and other entertainment venues. Nearby on a small hill is the largest water park of Marmaris, which is visited almost daily by hundreds of tourists.

Weather in Marmaris

This Turkish resort has a predominantly Mediterranean climate. It is coldest in January. The thermometer column does not rise above 7 and a half degrees. It rains, sometimes even wet snow. The absolute temperature minimum in these places is minus four degrees.



In summer, the weather is hot and dry. The entire holiday season is about plus 30 degrees. The temperature maximum, which was officially recorded by weather forecasters, was 52 degrees above zero.

Hotel room

Of course, it is best to make your impression of Aegean Park Hotel 3 *, the hotel infrastructure according to the reviews of tourists who have already been there. Most of the rooms are decent, spacious and with good views. Mostly tiles lie on the floor, everywhere it is well cleaned and light. TV, hairdryer, air conditioning and shower are working properly. If you have any difficulties, helpful staff is ready to help. True, as a rule, your assistants will count on a generous tip. Among the minuses, tourists are often called irregular cleaning of rooms. For example, linens are rarely changed. Daily room service comes down to taking out the trash, cleaning the bed and replacing soap and toilet paper.
