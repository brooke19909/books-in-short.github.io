Family holidays in the Crimea with food: tips and reviews

Crimea is a magnificent peninsula washed by the waters of two seas: Azov and Black. This is one of the most unique places designed by nature for various types of recreation, including beach. This, of course, contributes to the location and climatic conditions. Lovers of beautiful landscapes, magnificent landscapes, etc.



Culinary tours

For gourmets, Crimea is also an ideal place, because here you can taste many interesting, tasty and piquant dishes. Tourists who arrived at the resort, want to definitely try them, but sometimes do not have time. Therefore, an ideal option is a vacation in Crimea with meals organized in a hotel, guest house, recreation center or hotel. Be sure that the menu will definitely include dishes of the national cuisine of the aboriginal people of the peninsula - Crimean Tatars. In addition, real chebureks, lagmans, bashbarmaks, etc. can be tasted in any restaurant. From the reviews of vacationers it becomes clear that the best option is a pre-paid two meals a day. What is the attractiveness of Crimea?

Firstly, Crimea captivates its guests with its unique nature, natural and historical sights. Secondly, it attracts with a healing climate, healing and wellness opportunities, such as mineral waters, mud, and, of course, an excellent level of service, which is improved every year.

Since some resorts of the peninsula are considered balneological centers, people with various health problems often come to Crimea, many of them are kids. In a word, a vacation with a child in the Crimea with meals is an ideal option for those families who come here both for treatment and for the prevention of diseases.

At the same time, it is not necessary to stay in a sanatorium at all. Today, rest in the Crimea with a pool and meals can be obtained not only in star hotels and resorts, but also in guest houses, recreation centers, in the private sector, etc. All the people of the peninsula are working to provide guests with maximum comfort, regardless what is the thickness of their wallet.



Rest in Crimea

Many consider this miraculous peninsula as a place intended exclusively for a beach holiday. However, some resorts are not on the coast, but this does not prevent them from being a great place for spending vacations, summer vacations, etc. It was in these areas that in Soviet times, recreation centers, boarding houses, motels, youth and children's camps were built. Of course, the establishments planned in those days were not equipped with the best way. In many of them, the rooms had practically no amenities, toilets, showers, etc., however, rest in the Crimea in a boarding house with meals was always at the highest level.

Modern leisure facilities

Today, everything is a little different: most tourist camps, hotels, and boarding houses have been reconstructed and adapted to generally accepted standards, and at the same time, guest houses operate in almost all Crimean resorts - private comfortable estates providing tourists with the highest level of relaxation. And if a few years ago, only housing was provided to tourists in them, today vacation in the Crimea in the private sector with meals is also a common occurrence.

Sometimes guests of several guesthouses eat in one private dining room, while, like in a hotel, they can choose different types of food: only breakfast, half board, full board. Of course, most often tourists choose the second position and eat in the common dining room only in the mornings and in the afternoon, and in the evenings they go to try something tasty in numerous cafes and restaurants. Nevertheless, this option will provide a typical economy vacation in Crimea. There will be no problems with food.

After all, Crimea, by its climatic conditions, by the beauty of nature, by a large number of natural and historical sights, amazing canyons, waterfalls and stormy rivers, is simply an ideal place for fans of educational, beach, extreme and culinary tours.
