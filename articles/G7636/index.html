New World, Crimea (2015): reviews of tourists

If you do not like the huge number of vacationers in large resorts, we recommend you pay attention to the village of New World (Crimea). The sea here is just as warm and clean, the sun is as bright as in other resorts of the Black Sea coast. But it is a very quiet and picturesque place. The resort is located seven kilometers from Sudak.



Today we will tell you about the vacation and the impressions of those people who have already visited the New World last season. Crimea (2015), reviews confirm this, hospitably met all tourists who want to relax comfortably in its resorts.

The location of the village

This resort is located in Green Bay. Two more bays - Blue and Blue (with the famous Royal Beach) - are washed by a village in the south-west. They are separated by a winding, like a huge prehistoric lizard, Cape Kapchik, through which the Through Grotto stretches. Its length is more than 77 meters. The village is famous for its magnificent sand and pebble beaches.

Many tourists who visited here for the first time were struck by the New World (Crimea 2015). Guest reviews indicate that the nature of these places is fascinating. The resort is surrounded by picturesque mountain peaks that reliably protect it from the winds.



In the west of the village stands Koba-Kaya - a pointed mountain. Its name translates as "Cave Rock". A little to the west is Mount Syndyk and the quaint cape Karaul-Oba. In the northwest you can see the majestic mountain Sykhtlar. In the northeast, the Sokol rock mass hangs over the village.

Climate

The village is protected by mountains from piercing winds. The climate here is smooth and mild. Snow in these places is extremely rare, but spring comes much earlier than in neighboring Sudak. The average air temperature in July is +26 ¬∞ C, in January it reaches +2 ¬∞ C.

sights

The main attraction of the village is its unique nature. The reserve located here was created in 1974 to preserve unique vegetation. Relic junipers (tree-like) and pines grow here.



At this point, the Golitsyn trail. It begins at Green Bay and leads to a huge grotto. The length of this trail is about five and a half kilometers. It passes near the most beautiful corners of Crimea: a botanical reserve, Chaliapin's grotto, Green, Blue and Blue bays, the Tsar's beach, juniper grove.

Wine Museum

A winery in the village was founded by Prince Golitsyn in 1878. On the eve of the centenary of the enterprise, the Museum of the Plant History was opened here. It is located in the house of L. Golitsyn.

In addition to several rooms that have preserved the interior items that belonged to the prince, tourists can visit the basement of this house. Here were stored bottles with the best drinks that Nicholas II himself had tried in his time. In the tasting room you will be offered to try wine by candlelight.

Relaxation

This small village is attractive, first of all, with a measured and unhurried rhythm of life. This is especially important for people who are tired of the hustle and bustle of a modern city. A small village in Crimea, which has been known since antiquity, fully lives up to its old name - Paradise. It translates as "paradise."





The beauty of the landscapes and the amazing aromas of juniper groves amazed people who were vacationing in the village of New World, Crimea (2015). Reviews of tourists say that this place was created by nature itself for relaxation and relaxation. If you want to be alone, you can sunbathe and swim in the bay or on a wild beach. Many people think that vacationing in the New World (Crimea) is a great idea. There are a number of reasons for this:

  • the possibility of living in a private sector or hotel;
  • quite affordable prices;
  • variety of outdoor activities;
  • fascinating excursions.

Types of rest

Comparing the New World with other resorts of the peninsula, it should be noted that there is no such variety of entertainment, but this does not mean that tourists will be bored here. Holidays in the New World (Crimea) will surely appeal to those who love hiking. A variety of routes along juniper groves have been developed for them. No less exciting is the inspection of local bays with steep mountain slopes.





History buffs can visit the monument to the creator of this resort - Lev Golitsyn, as well as Emperor Nicholas II. In addition, you can see the ruins of an ancient monastery and the ruins of the Bosporus Fortress. Tourists who prefer outdoor activities can go diving.

In memory of a trip to this paradise, vacationers can bring home handmade souvenirs - they are sold on the promenade and in souvenir shops.

Sea and beaches

According to legend, which the inhabitants of the village like to tell, Emperor Nicholas II rested in these places, but today the Royal Beach is closed to visitors. However, do not despair - in the New World there are many stunningly beautiful beaches.

The main beach of the village is located in Green Bay. Everyone can visit it, since admission is free. There is always perfectly clear water, there are no waves and wind, since it is protected by mountains on both sides. The beach is landscaped, there are several cafes and small shops.



In the Blue Bay is a pirate beach. In the XII-XIII centuries, pirates and smugglers lived here. The beach is rather narrow, covered with medium-sized boulders, and at the bottom there are stones covered with algae. Popular films such as Three + Two, Amphibian Man were shot here.

There are wild beaches in the village . One of them is Monastic. Camping lovers gather here. You can get here by boat.

Are there any sanatoriums in the village?

It is difficult to call the New World a health resort. Nevertheless, vacationers can count on some health benefits. This primarily concerns the problems of the respiratory tract, which are beneficially influenced by clean air with an admixture of essential oils of juniper and pine trees.

New World (Crimea): private sector

This type of accommodation is preferred by many Russians. This is explained quite simply. In the small village of New World (Crimea), the private sector is represented quite widely - from small apartments and rooms to spacious cottages equipped with everything necessary. Prices in this case are significantly lower than in hotels.

Hotels

Despite the small size of the resort, there are hotels in the village. Novyi Svet (Crimea) can offer you quite a variety of accommodation. These are the hotels Juniper Grove and Veselaya Scorpena, the Prince Golitsyn Hotel, the Elena boarding house. You can stay in a mini-hotel "Champagne" or in the hotel "New World". These are not very large hotels. The New World (Crimea) is good because there are no crowds of tourists. Judging by the reviews of people vacationing here last season, the most popular hotel is the New World.





Here can rest up to seventy people. For accommodation, forty rooms of various categories are offered. They offer breathtaking views of the mountains or the sea. Hotel "New World" is perfect for a romantic honeymoon. In addition, here you can organize corporate events that participants will remember for a long time.
