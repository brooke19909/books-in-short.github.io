Ai-Serez Hotel, Sudak: photos and reviews of tourists

Hotel Ai-Serez is located in a picturesque cozy corner of Sudak, at the address: st. Hadji Gerai, 10, on Mount Firaine. The building is made in an interesting architectural style in the form of a monolithic building made of glass and concrete on 4 floors. Hotel decoration is done in warm gold color. The hotel windows offer beautiful views of the Crimean mountains and the sea. You can also admire the opening landscapes of the Genoese fortress.
