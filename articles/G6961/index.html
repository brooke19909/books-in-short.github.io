The most popular stopping place for tourists who come to enjoy the beauties of the Altai Mountains. If you are looking for a recreation center on the shore of Lake Aya, then this is one of the most optimal options.

There is definitely enough space for everyone: the size of the area occupied by the base is more than 12 hectares. Well-groomed territory, breathtaking mountain views and quality service will help you escape from the bustle of the city in a quiet corner of harmony.





At many recreation centers of the Altai Mountains on Lake Aya, the pools are equipped with the most modern technologies.
