Crochet openwork hat for girls - an indispensable thing in the summer!

In the summer, quite often there are hot and very sunny days, during which you should not leave your house without a hat. This can negatively affect the health status of any person, and even more so a small child. Parents who let their daughter out in the heat without a headgear act very recklessly. In such weather, an openwork hat crocheted for a girl is an irreplaceable thing! It will not be hot in it, at the same time it will be able to protect the head of the child from the scorching sun. In addition, by choosing the right model, you can turn your little daughter into a real beauty.



However, before starting work, it is worthwhile to purchase suitable threads. Crochet openwork hat for girls, as a rule, is made of thin cotton yarn. Most often, work in this case starts from above. To do this, a chain of five to seven air loops is knitted and locked in a ring. Further, the work can be performed in a circle. As a result, a finished openwork hat, crocheted for a girl, will not have seams. This is an undeniable advantage of such products. After all, a seam can very often cause a child‚Äôs discomfort. In addition, it can hardly be called a decoration for a children's headdress. Especially when it comes to such beautiful products as an openwork hat.



The choice of pattern in the manufacture of hats for children can not be called a simple task. Everything will depend on personal preferences and the style of the headgear. It may turn out that the width of the report will be such that for a particular model it will not work. The best option for making a headdress is a crocheted hat, a diagram of which is printed in an authoritative magazine. In this case, the recommended number of loops for a specific size can be no doubt. In addition, such publications quite often issue various special issues containing models designed exclusively for children. This will allow, having knitted a beautiful panama for her daughter, additionally make a beautiful openwork sundress. This set will be her favorite clothes in the hot season.



If you do not know which model should be preferred, then pay attention to the caps of the daughter's girlfriends. Many little girls with pleasure wear the same panama hat as their neighbor. It should be noted that if you decide to crochet various hats, schemes will certainly help in this. Their description always clearly reflects the sequence of work. Therefore, even a beginner needlewoman will cope with the creation of a panama. In the scheme, it will be possible to find the required number of loops that need to be dialed in order to make the product of the required size. Before work, it is advisable to tie the sample to make sure that with the recommended thickness of the thread and the number of the hook it will be possible to ensure the required density. Otherwise, a crocheted fishnet hat for a girl will turn out to be smaller or, conversely, larger than necessary.
