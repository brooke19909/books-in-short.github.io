Sea View Patong Hotel 4 * (Thailand, Phuket): photos and reviews

If you plan to relax on the island of Phuket and are looking for a comfortable hotel on the beach in a quiet area with well-developed infrastructure, then Sea View Patong Hotel 4 * may be a suitable option for you.



Location Description

This hotel is a couple of hundred meters from Patong Beach. Patong city center is about two kilometers away. Bangla Road and Djangla Ceylon Shopping Center can be reached by tuk-tuk or on foot. The area in which the hotel is located is quite calm, but there is a well-developed infrastructure with cafes, restaurants, markets, shops, etc. The distance to Phuket Airport is 45 kilometers.

Sea View Patong was opened in 1993. Since then, renovation work has been carried out here several times. Sea View Patong Hotel 4 * (Phuket): traveler reviews

Experienced travelers know how important it is not to make a mistake when choosing a hotel for a vacation abroad. After all, the hotel becomes a kind of second home for the tourist, and each of us wants to return in the evening to a cozy and clean room, where there is everything necessary for a comfortable stay. Therefore, many people planning to go on vacation, not sparing time and energy, are studying the reviews of those who have already visited this or that place. After all, tourists in their comments share their own experiences and talk about the advantages and disadvantages of a particular hotel, which they themselves had to face. Today we want to invite you to find out what opinions the travelers have about the Sea View Patong Hotel 4 * located on Phuket Island. Reviews vacationers from Russia and other CIS countries, it must be said, the vast majority are positive. A confirmation of this is the rating of the hotel. It is based on guest ratings and is 4.5 out of the maximum five points possible.



Number of rooms

The apartments provided to them in this hotel, our compatriots were mostly satisfied. So, according to them, the rooms at Sea View Patong are quite spacious, stylishly decorated, bright and very comfortable. The furniture here is not new, but very high quality, in good condition. The technique works without interruption. Many tourists note that the rooms have everything that you may need during your vacation - from a tea set and umbrellas to a beach bag with towels and slippers. Also, guests recall that in the bathroom shampoo, shower gel and hair balm are in large enough containers that are convenient to use. Other personal care products are also available - ear sticks, toothbrushes, toothpaste, hand cream, toilet paper, etc.

As for the minuses, some guests complained about the fact that at Sea View Patong Hotel 4 * (Phuket, Thailand), wireless Internet in rooms is available only at an additional cost.



Location, Territory

Many tourists admitted that in many respects they chose the Sea View Patong Hotel precisely because of its location. So, first of all, travelers were attracted by the fact that the hotel is located just across the road from the beach. A big plus for many guests was the fact that the hotel is in a quiet place, where nothing will interfere with your vacation. At the same time, there is a developed infrastructure. So, within walking distance for hotel guests there are many shops, supermarkets, souvenir shops, travel agencies, massage parlors, etc. The nearest market can be reached on foot in a quarter of an hour. The center of Patong - Bangla Road - is also a walk away. On the way you will take no more than half an hour.

As for the hotel territory, tourists describe it as small, but very green and beautiful. It is very nice to be here.



Check in

According to the rules of the hotel, the settlement of new guests is carried out after two in the afternoon. However, many tourists who arrived at the hotel in the morning, according to them, were accommodated much earlier. So you are unlikely to have to wait long to check in, even if you arrive at Sea View Patong early in the morning. Also note that you will be asked to make a deposit upon arrival. Its amount is about $ 100. Upon discharge from the hotel, the deposit will be returned to you. By the way, with regard to departure, the room should be vacated before 11 a.m.
