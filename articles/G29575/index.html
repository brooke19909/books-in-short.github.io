Ribbon Costume





Suit "Chung-Chang" with your own hands can be made from ribbons. What is needed for this?

  • Wide ribbons of three different colors.
  • Satin colored fabric.
  • Elastic.
  • Scissors.
  • Candle or clear varnish.
  • Tape measure.
  • Sewing machine.
  • Thread, needle.

Working process:

  1. Measure your baby‚Äôs waist.
  2. Cut a piece of elastic at the right size.
  3. Cut the satin ribbon of the same length, leaving space for the seams. The width of the strip should be two centimeters larger than the elastic.
  4. Cut the ribbons for the skirt of the size you need (leave a centimeter at the seams). Flush the edges with a candle or paint over with transparent varnish.
  5. Fold the satin fabric in half in width. Fold each edge five millimeters and sew. This is the future belt.
  6. Start sewing ribbons inside the belt, alternating different colors. Insert the tape inwards by a centimeter and make literally one seam with threads. This is necessary to fix it in place. When finished, cover with a second layer of belt and sew on a sewing machine.
  7. Insert the elastic and sew the edges.
  8. Do the same with sleeves.

For such a bright suit, the bottom black clothes may not work. So look for brown tights and a turtleneck.

Trash bag suit





You can make a Chung-Chang suit with your own hands even out of garbage bags. You will need:

  • Garbage bags in green.
  • Artificial flowers.
  • White wide elastic.
  • White long lace or clothesline.
  • Threads.
  • White cloth or cups.
  • Scissors.

What do we have to do:

  1. Measure the place where the skirt will be worn and cut the desired length from the elastic.
  2. Sew the edges of the elastic and place it on the back of the chair.
  3. Cut strips of 0.5-1 centimeter wide from the garbage bag.
  4. Sew the bands onto the elastic. Sew flowers on top.
  5. To create the top of your costume, use cups or circles cut out of white fabric. They need to sew flowers.
  6. Sew to the top on both sides of the rope longer than necessary. Subsequently, she will have to be tied behind her back. Sew another one so that it passes through the neck. Looks like the top of a bathing suit.
