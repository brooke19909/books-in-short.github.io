The st Regis Moscow Nikolskaya: description, services

Most tourists, when visiting various countries of the world, wish to enjoy the most worthy living conditions. Moscow was no exception, where the St. Petersburg luxury hotel is located in the very center of the city Regis Moscow Nikolskaya.

About hotel

Hotel The St. Regis Moscow Nikolskaya is just a few steps from the famous Red Square, the pompous Bolshoi Theater and St. Basil's Cathedral.

It combines a rich cultural and architectural heritage combined with modern design. Regis Moscow Nikolskaya allows guests of the capital to enjoy unhindered access to the most famous historical sights, as well as exclusive shops of the vibrant center of the capital of Russia.

Services

Guests at the St. Regis Moscow Nikolskaya offers a wide range of options. For example, try classic Italian cuisine and unforgettable dinners at their signature A Tavola restaurant.

The Iridium spa and indoor pool offer a relaxing stay and a cozy atmosphere, while views of the Kremlin and the sparkling streets of the city open from a panoramic platform at the top of the hotel.

In total, the hotel has 210 rooms, of which 44 are suites. The design of each room uses exquisite furniture, which is combined with the spacious size of each room. Guests can enjoy marble bathrooms with Remede amenities, as well as a large Italian courtyard with a large number of rooms.

The hotel concierge service can fulfill any request, from the morning wake-up call to breakfast in the room, the provision of food and drinks in the apartment for a fee, as well as dry cleaning and ironing services. Transfers, including to and from the airport, are provided separately and at an additional cost.

Throughout The St. Regis Moscow Nikolskaya Hotel has unlimited Wi-Fi. In addition, a 24-hour front desk with expedited check-out and check-in, as well as staff speaking three languages: Russian, German and English, are always at guests' disposal.
