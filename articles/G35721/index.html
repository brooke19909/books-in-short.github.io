Aqua Bella Beach Hotels 4, Turkey: overview, description, features and reviews

The history of the resort Aqua Bella Beach Hotels 4 * dates back to 1996. But this should not frighten tourists, because in 2006 the hotel underwent a complete reconstruction and overhaul.





Description of the resort complex

Hotel Aqua Bella Beach Hotels 4 * consists of one building, which has a height of 5 floors, as well as a small two-level block located in the garden. It is located very close to the beach. In addition, for swimming, tourists can use two outdoor pools, as well as one that is located indoors (works in bad weather). Also in the courtyard of the hotel there is a wonderful garden, in the shade of which you can hide from the Turkish heat.

The kitchen is one of the most important moments of relaxation. The resort and hotel complex Aqua Bella Beach Hotels 4 * will give you an unforgettable experience of national Turkish dishes, which will be prepared for you by the best local chefs. Also at your service there are many bars that are ready to provide you with delicious high-quality drinks, and also allow you to refresh yourself with a variety of snacks.

Particular attention in the Aqua Bella Beach Hotels 4 * is given to entertainment. Most of all here they like dancing. This hotel complex hosts international festivals, as well as incendiary concerts. For guests regularly held dance master classes in the most popular choreographic areas.





Hotel location

The resort and hotel complex Aqua Bella Beach Hotels 4 * (Kemer) is located in the small tourist town of Beldibi. This is the very coast of the Mediterranean Sea, and from the hotel building to the beach no more than 10 meters. If you want to walk around the center of the crowded Kemer, the path to it is about 12 km. The distance from the hotel to Antalya International Airport is 35 km.





Number of rooms

  • 117 standard rooms are located in the main building. Despite the miniature area (16 sq.m), they are able to accommodate two people, as well as 2 additional guests in folding beds. Each room has a balcony.
  • 7 apartments, which are located in the garden building, are very similar to standard rooms. No more than 2 people can accommodate in them. All rooms have a glazed French balcony overlooking the sea coast or an outdoor pool.

What is provided in the rooms

Hotel Aqua Bella Beach Hotels 4 * can offer you the following amenities that are available in each of the rooms:

  • a balcony, a loggia or a panoramic window so that you can enjoy the beautiful scenery of the resort town of Beldibi at any time;
  • a bathroom with a toilet, a shower and a washbasin (for greater convenience, a large mirror, an electric hairdryer, a cosmetic set for bathing and all necessary bath accessories are provided here);
  • split system that will allow you to change the air temperature in your apartment according to your needs (it works both for cooling and heating);
  • for a fee, the hotel staff will fill a minibar according to your taste preferences for drinks;
  • the room has an electronic safe, but using it requires a daily payment);

Positive reviews

Tourists have long been fond of tourists by the resort hotel Aqua Bella Beach Hotels 4 *. Reviews about him show that in a relatively small area there are all the necessary facilities for a bright, comfortable and unforgettable vacation. This is confirmed by numerous positive comments on specialized tourism resources:

  • a large private beach, on which there is always enough space, regardless of the number of vacationers;
  • good work of massage therapists (qualified professionals who are fluent in many techniques);
  • a quiet and peaceful atmosphere not only in the hotel and on its territory, but also throughout the town;
  • very convenient hotel accommodation relative to the sea coast;
  • a very beautiful mountain landscape opens from the windows of some rooms;
  • good food with a diverse assortment of dishes and drinks.




Negative reviews

Among the negative comments regarding this hotel, it is especially worth highlighting the following:

  • constant problems with hot water in the rooms (especially for the upper floors);
  • dirty dishes in the hotel dining room, as well as an untidy uniform among the waiters and workers at the distribution;
  • at the entrance to the hotel there are several large garbage cans that emit an unpleasant odor;
  • the complete lack of animation for both adults and children;
  • very old furniture and plumbing in the rooms, and the whole hotel is in need of repair;
  • at the entrance to the sea there are many small fish that bite very painfully;
  • construction is being carried out in the immediate vicinity of the hotel, which not only spoils the view from the window (of those rooms that overlook the mountains), but also creates a lot of noise and garbage;
  • there are practically no fruits on the buffet menu (the only thing you can sometimes count on is not very high-quality watermelons);
  • Hotel staff and the administration practically do not respond to requests, comments and complaints of guests.
