Yarn "Alize" - creative, warm, soft

Now knitted things are in fashion , they become especially popular in the cold season. However, when buying knitted clothes in stores, people overpay a lot, because such things cost, as a rule, quite expensive, and their quality is often very poor. It is for these reasons that many have found a solution to this issue and are independently knitting clothes. The price is much less, the quality is much more, and at the same time there will be a reason for needlework. In the modern 21st century, the variety of knitting materials is very large. Thread now: choose - I do not want. However, do not forget that they can be completely substandard. At present, one of the most sought after and popular is yarn from Alize. It is produced in Turkey, therefore it is considered one of the few highest quality. Also , Alize yarn is divided into several types. These types include:



1. Alize DANTELA

These are Turkish-made threads that are designed for hand knitting. They are openwork ribbon yarn, which is sold, usually in skeins. Such threads are ideal, for example, for knitting a scarf, mittens or mittens for children and adults. The composition of this yarn includes: 84% acrylic, and 16% polyamide.

2. Alize DANTELA VUL.

This yarn is new. It is an openwork, half-woolen, which is just perfect for knitting warmer, winter scarves. One skein of such yarn, as a rule, is enough for one scarf or for several mittens. But, of course, it all depends on the size of the future product.



3. Alize Cashmere

This is Turkish yarn designed for hand knitting. Threads are purely woolen of excellent quality. The fiber of this yarn is usually medium in size, smooth and slightly fluffy. It consists of: 100% wool, the weight and volume of the skein is 100 grams and 300 meters.

4. Alize Mohair Classic

Yarn "Alize Moher Classic" is a high quality thread that is knitted manually. Also, it is certainly suitable for knitting a sweater, blouse or suit for a child. Its composition contains 70% mohair and 30% acrylic. Material consumption depends on size.

5. Alize ASTRAGAN

This type of yarn can only be knitted manually. Most often it is used for the manufacture of various clothes, plaids, all kinds of toys and the like. Alize yarn (astargan) is a voluminous, warm and soft thread that consists of mohair and wool. It is usually sold in large skeins. Also, such yarn has a karakul effect due to its curls and rings on the basis.



6. Alize ANGORA SPECIAL

Usually used for hand knitting. Often this Alize yarn is sold in large oval skeins. Usually it is plain and "fabulous" color. This yarn consists of 40% acrylic and 60% mohair.

7. Alize DECOFUR

Yarn "Alize" is intended only for hand knitting. It resembles weed due to its long pile. Sold in skeins. Its composition includes 100% polyester. The weight and volume of one skein is 100 grams and 110 meters.

There are also other types of these threads. For example, yarn "Alize Diva" or Alize SOFTY. All types of yarn differ only in the best quality. You can buy "Alize" yarn both in specialized stores for sewing and knitting, and in online hypermarkets.
