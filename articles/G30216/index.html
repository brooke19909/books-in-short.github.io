Gun making

Before you start making origami out of paper, you must have everything you need for this type of activity. Before construction, you will need certain parts and materials, for example:

  • thick paper sheets;
  • glue, which should be exclusively heated;
  • rubber;
  • scotch tape, marker and scissors for paper processing.

It is worth noting that for the shotgun made of paper the color of the material itself does not matter at all, you can choose it to your taste.





Manufacturing scheme

So, how to make a shotgun out of paper? By this principle, you can make any paper weapon to your taste. Only the dimensions of the parts and their location when gluing the product itself will differ.

After you have prepared everything you need, we proceed to the manufacturing itself. First of all, we use paper and fold it into a tube, at the same time we start this process from the corner of the paper and move further exclusively along the diagonal. It is necessary to make the paper sheets take the form of a cylinder, and inside this figure there is little space. This structure will be the foundation, other types of parts will come from it. To learn how to make a shotgun out of paper, you need to be extremely careful and accurate.

Around our foundation, it is necessary to twist the second part, almost the same tube, only a few more. You just need to wrap paper around the first cylinder. As a result, a large-sized tube should be obtained that will simulate the one that shoots.

Then it is important to fasten these tubes. You can do this using tape. It is necessary to qualitatively glue the structure so that it cannot unwind. If the edges are not perfectly smooth, then you should trim them to achieve the desired result.





Next stage

Next, a paper gun diagram tells us the following actions. Using our so-called template, you need to make the same tube. But we need to cut it into certain parts. From this cylinder, a handle should be obtained, as well as a barrel and a trigger: 5.15 and 8 centimeters in length, respectively.





Parts collection

The next step, according to the paper gun scheme, is to assemble all the parts that are separate. In this case, the adhesive that you prepared in advance will help you. Places for connecting parts can be determined by yourself, because in this case they should be exactly the same as on standard combat pistols.

So, we make paper origami next. You need to twist the tube again, but in this case, the space inside it is not necessary to leave. It would be great if this tube had a different color. In addition, its diameter should be smaller, since it must be included in an existing cylinder. After creating the tube, you need to ensure that it can move smoothly inside the large cylinder.





Using all the same paper and rubber bands, you should make a trigger. From paper you need to make a narrow strip, but at the same time a long one. Next, you need to twist this product and cut off the edge, not forgetting to fasten the edges with tape, so that the paper does not unwind. Having bent the tube at the end, it is necessary to insert into the bend a short cut edge with an elastic band. This edge will cling to the back of the trigger during erection.

Next, tape you need to glue the rear edge of your gun. This is necessary so that the gum could not slip out. Thus, you will be able to make the trigger, which is necessary to create a paper gun. The process of this mechanism is quite simple and banal. Pulling the trigger pushes the bullet at a sufficiently high speed.

Conclusion

After reading our article and learning how to make a shotgun out of paper, you can make it yourself. A responsible and intriguing moment will be checking and testing your device. This process is best done on the street, because at home you can break something (as it turned out, paper crafts are not as harmless as ordinary paper flowers). Your task is to load paper weapons and pull the trigger to let the bullet fly out. If this worked out, now you can be proud of your success in a new business.

As for the production of bullets, they are best made from semi-finished paper. To insert a bullet in its immediate place, you need to slightly press the hook so that the elastic stretches a few centimeters. Then you can load your paper weapon by putting a bullet. In this case, you can shoot again and enjoy your own product.
