Choose a recreation center. Khabarovsk - a great place for a summer vacation

In early May, all people are in anticipation of a summer vacation. Of course, many prefer to go to foreign resorts. But such a luxury is not affordable for everyone. For those who want to relax inexpensively, it is worth knowing that Russia also has decent places. As a rule, these are recreation centers.

Khabarovsk is a city that was built on the right bank of the great Russian Amur River. This water artery is an important resource, not only in industrial and transport terms, but also in tourism. Every year the city welcomes a large number of guests. They can see the sights and enjoy plenty of beach activities. There are numerous recreation centers on the coast.

Khabarovsk is located in a favorable climate zone. Summer is hot enough, but humid. The average temperatures of this season are + 21 ... + 25 ¬∞ . Most precipitation falls in August (about 155 mm), and the warmest month is July. This information will be useful to those planning a vacation in Khabarovsk.

So, let's look at popular recreation centers.

Khabarovsk, "Dolphin" (recreation center)

Dolphin is within walking distance of the city center. On the territory of the recreation center there is a park area with numerous green spaces. Here for visitors are built arbors designed for a maximum of 30 people. The beach is equipped, there are changing cabins, a dry closet is provided. For those who want to hide from the rays of the summer sun, gazebos are offered. By the way, they can arrange banquets, as the structure can accommodate up to 50 people. Barbecue corners can be rented with or without a house. There is a cafe on site where you can grab a bite to eat and chilled drinks. However, such services are offered by almost all recreation centers (Khabarovsk). Dolphin is ideal for those who like to spend time actively. It has a paintball range. This game is popular. Also here is:

  • banquet room;
  • Russian sauna;
  • hotel;
  • parking.




The recreation center "Zaimka"

Quite popular in these parts is Zaimka (recreation center). Khabarovsk is located just 20 km from it. This location allows you to get as far away from the bustle of the city.

Infrastructure:

  • sauna / bath;
  • pool;
  • spa treatments;
  • billiards;
  • bar / cafe;
  • gym;
  • hotel.

It is possible to rent ATVs, bicycles, go-karts and other sports equipment. Volleyball and basketball courts are open to visitors. For lovers of other sports, there is a tennis court, and you can also play mini-golf. The beach is within walking distance. For your stay comfortable rooms are provided with everything you need. On the territory there are beautiful parks where you can retire and enjoy a relaxing holiday.



The recreation center "Arizona" (Khabarovsk)

The recreation center is 25 minutes from the city center. On the territory there are many gazebos with a capacity of 6 to 50 people. The beach is near the pond. There are also swimming pools. For living you can rent a house, and for a banquet - a gazebo. All vacationers are provided with barbecue facilities. There is a bathhouse. This recreation center has gained popularity thanks to the Gibbon Flight attraction. It is a stretched rope, which is fixed at a sufficient height. The person is fastened with safety belts, and he makes a jump. What is unique is that it happens all over the water. It often hosts a battle of dancers in a large gazebo and holds discos every evening.



Holiday Village "Village"

The tourist complex "Village" is popular among vacationers. It is not like the usual recreation facilities. Khabarovsk is 76 km away. This complex is suitable for both history connoisseurs and ordinary tourists. A museum of indigenous peoples is open here. Presented are national homes, costumes and household items. You can visit the museum yourself or use the services of a guide. Vacationers can also enjoy the beautiful forest and clear river. Holiday houses are designed for visitors who are willing to stay for a while.



Royal

For those who are used to relaxing in comfort, it is proposed to stay in the Royal hotel complex. It is located on the main street of the city. The following services are provided for guests:

  • bar;
  • Cafe;
  • Finnish saunas
  • pools;
  • rooms for every taste and budget.

The saunas have fonts, relaxation areas and karaoke. Free internet throughout. Laundry facilities are available. There is parking and a car wash. You can eat in the express cafe or in the restaurant. There is a billiard room where amateur tournaments are often held.

You can choose recreation centers for a long time (Khabarovsk is well developed in terms of tourism), but it is worth understanding that each place is beautiful and unique in its own way. The city is very interesting. Come over. You will not regret!
