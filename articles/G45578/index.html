Knitting bags with needles. Smart clutch

To knit, you need a dense yarn with nylon or silk thread of medium thickness. It is better to choose yarn that is well twisted and stiff so that the finished product keeps its shape. The finished canvas is folded according to the pattern. To keep the handbag in shape, it is recommended to make the inner part of the same dimensions of thin artificial leather. The lining is put inside the clutch and the two parts are joined together, tied around the edge with a "crustacean step" (single crochet, made from left to right) from the front of the product. Hook holes are made in the leather lining. The finished clutch can be decorated with knitted flowers, embroidered with beads or beads.
