Michelia Hotel Nha Trang 4 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang): description and reviews

Vietnam is an interesting and exotic country that has long gained popularity among our compatriots as a place to stay. For this reason, we want to talk about one of the good hotels called Michelia Hotel Nha Trang 4 *.

A bit about the hotel ...

Michelia Hotel Nha Trang 4 * was built at the beginning of the twentieth century and was called at that time "La Frigate". After many years of work, it underwent several renovations and in 1972 it became one of the most comfortable and beautiful hotels in Nha Trang. The hotel guests were representatives of the French government, businessmen and foreign investors. Michelia Hotel Nha Trang 4 * was made in terms of European-style architecture, and now the complex also provides services at a European level. It is located in the heart of Nha Trang (Nha Trang), very close to the public beach and a five-minute drive to the station. Therefore, tourists who come here to relax, get there is not difficult.

Number of rooms

In addition to a convenient location, Michelia Hotel Nha Trang 4 *, which is a multi-storey building, has 199 rooms. Each apartment has the following amenities: bathrobes, air conditioning, desk, TV. The hotel has a wide selection of rooms of various categories:



  1. Presidential Suite (its area is 115 square meters). The spacious apartments overlooking the sea coast have a separate living room with kitchen area, a bedroom with a king size bed. In addition to other amenities, the room has a kettle, safe, DVD player.
  2. Superior (area 25 square meters). This room type offers pool or city views. The apartments are equipped with two single beds, a bathroom. They can accommodate no more than two guests.
  3. Superior Premier (area 32 square meters). Guestrooms have river and city views. The apartments have two single or one large bed, a balcony with city views, a bathroom. They can accommodate no more than three guests. These rooms can be booked as connecting rooms.
  4. Deluxe (area 32 square meters). These rooms have sea and city views. They are equipped with two single or one large bed. They can comfortably accommodate two people, in addition, there is one extra bed.

All rooms have Wi-Fi, there are cosmetics, a safe, a minibar, and a fresh press.

Dining at the hotel

Michelia Nha Trang Hotel 4 * (Vietnam) has on its territory a restaurant serving Asian cuisine, as well as a restaurant with European cuisine, a la carte restaurant, banquet hall and coffee shop.



The hotel serves a buffet. Here is a very high quality food. The restaurant serves a large number of very varied dishes, directly at the visitors prepare a seafood barbecue. Local soups and hot dishes are very tasty. Delicious fruits deserve special praise.

Hotel infrastructure

Michelia Hotel Nha Trang 4 * (Vietnam, Nha Trang) has a good infrastructure. On the ground floor of the building is a 25-meter pool, the depth of which ranges from one to one and a half meters. A currency exchange, gift shop, laundry and car rental are also available on site. For all kinds of business events, the hotel has a conference room.

Sports and Entertainment

Michelia Hotel Nha Trang 4 * (photo is given in the article) offers its guests to visit a fitness center, spa (massage, sauna, jacuzzi), a disco, as well as tennis, fishing, diving. A babysitter can be booked at the hotel for children.

Hotel beach

The hotel is not far from the beach, it is separated from the coast by a road. You can reach it on foot in no more than five minutes at a leisurely pace. On the shore there are umbrellas, sunbeds. Beach towels are provided each time you visit the sea.

About the resort ...

What are the benefits of Michelia NhaTrang Hotel 4 *? Nha Trang is a resort town, or rather, the country's beach capital. The hotel is very conveniently located and is located near the center, a ten minute walk. It is also very convenient that the beach is very close.

The town itself is very cheerful and noisy. Thousands of tourists come here every year to relax. Some appreciate the resort for its white beaches and the azure sea, for the beauty of green hills and tropical islands in the bay. And others like the nightlife of the town. More relaxed holidaymakers are delighted with the spa treatments and mud baths. Ancient Cham towers located in the city center are no less interesting to tourists.

Nha Trang (Nha Trang) - is located 1300 kilometers from Hanoi (the capital of Vietnam). The population of the city is small, only 200 thousand people. Locals are engaged in fishing, fish processing, and, of course, serving tourists.

Holiday Benefits

Why do vacationers come to Michelia NhaTrang Hotel 4 * (Vietnam)? Nha Trang is famous not only for its sea coast, but also for very healing mud and springs. Therefore, while relaxing in a hotel, you can not only soak up the beaches, but also experience the beneficial effects of local mud. Good comprehensive treatment, air with the aroma of eucalyptus - all this has a positive effect on people with weak immunity and with respiratory diseases.



An interesting fact is that the sea in the city is very warm, its temperature is always at least +26 degrees. And only in February and January the thermometer drops to +24. In summer, there is heat (+ 33-34 degrees), in winter, very rarely, the temperature can drop to +15 degrees. Attractions and entertainment

What is good Michelia NhaTrang Hotel 4 *? Reviews of tourists indicate a good location of the complex. The center is nearby, which means you can walk to local attractions. In addition, the proximity of the sea pleases. In general, diving is very developed in the city. The best time for this sport is from the beginning of March to August. The famous seven-kilometer beach of Nha Trang is considered the best and most beautiful in the south of Vietnam. Large coconut palms protect from the strong sun not only walking tourists, but also bathers in the sea. The city's bay is protected from the sea by islands and mountains, which protects the population from storms and winds. The sea water here is incredibly clear and clean, and the water inlet is quite sharp, after two or three meters the depth becomes more than human growth. Therefore, with the children you need to be especially careful.



Among the main attractions that are worth seeing are the Dien Khan Citadel, built by the Chin Dynasty in the seventeenth century, Longshon Pagoda (a monastir inhabited by monks), French Gothic style Nha Trang Cathedral, Bao Dao villas (the highest ranks of the country inhabited them), Tyama Towers built between the seventh and twelfth century.
