Zante Royal Resort & Water Park 4 * (Greece, Zakynthos): hotel description, reviews

Greek islands have always attracted crowds of tourists. Mostly people come here who want to not only relax by the sea, soak up the beach, take sun baths, swim in the purest azure water, but also get in touch with history and see the fruits of the ancient people's creativity. Both mainland and island Greece are suitable for this. However, in recent years, the islands have become particularly popular tourist destinations. Tourists are attracted by small but excellent hotels built in the Greek style. These include the Zante Royal Resort 4 * (Greece, Zakynthos Island).



Island of art

Zakynthos is located in the Ionian Sea, in its southern part. It stands out with a green spot on the turquoise surface of sea water. Due to its geographical location, i.e. proximity to the Peloponnese peninsula, tourists who have stayed here, for example, at the Zante Royal Resort Hotel, can also visit many of the islands of the Greek archipelago, as well as some historical areas of mainland Greece, such as ancient Olympia and even Athens. - the capital of the country.

The island is interesting both in terms of the history of art and architecture, as well as a unique nature reserve. It is known as the habitat of huge turtles. In addition, Zakynthos is considered to be a source of inspiration for many creative natures, as well as the best love corner, a paradise for lovers. What are the Blue Caves, which are located in the southern part of the island! That is why tours to the Zante Royal Resort And Water Park hotel are so in demand among newlyweds who go here to celebrate their honeymoon. By the way, if you wish, you can organize a romantic wedding here in Greek traditions. Believe me, it will be very interesting, exciting and will be a memory for the rest of your life.



Cultural heritage

The island is home to the Greek national poet Dionysus Solomos, as well as Xenopoulos. By the way, being on the island as part of your tour to the Zante Royal Resort hotel, you will immediately notice the influence of the Venetian and Florentine cultures, which in combination with Greek traditions gives every corner, every piece of land a special flavor. In literature, one can often see how Zakynthos is compared with these Italian cities and is even called Florence of the East. The islanders are especially surprised by their musicality, it seems that it is in their blood. There is evidence that the ancient Zakynthrians worshiped the god of music - Apollo.

Zakynthos today continues to be considered one of the most important cultural centers of modern Greece. Every day various musical - song and dance - festivals are held here. Many of the participants who came to the island as part of these events stay at the Zante Royal Resort And Water Park 4 * hotel. And all the guests of the hotel can not only have a wonderful time under the gentle Greek sun, enjoying the pure waters of the Ionian Sea, but also get in touch with the true traditions of these arts, becoming a spectator and, if desired, a participant.


Zante Royal Resort 4 *: tradition

There are 44 villages on the island. They all try to maintain a traditional lifestyle. And this means that the locals do not follow the tourists' suggestions, but rather, tourism develops taking into account the interests of local residents. For example, they impede the construction of giant hotel complexes, which by their appearance can bring disharmony into the pristine beauty of the island. There are practically no hotels representative of the global hotel brands, although the level of service even in the smallest hotel facilities can be estimated at the full five. The aforementioned Zante Royal Resort And Water Park Hotel is one of the largest on the island, since there is a water amusement park on its territory, but it is also built in accordance with the general concept for the entire island. The hotel buildings are located in the form of terraces, gradually descending to the water. Their exterior is also made in the Greek style, which incredibly harmoniously fits into the picturesque picture of the island. On the beach along the water's edge in a semicircle are sun loungers and umbrellas the color of the Greek flag.

Vasilikos

This small village is located 16 kilometers southwest of the island's capital. A distinctive feature of this village is a lush park and garden area, which encircles the golden beaches, framed from another groan by the turquoise surface of the sea. You can get to Vasilikos along a road that runs along the coast. And already on the way you can enjoy the magnificent sea views that amaze with their beauty and sometimes even seem incredible, fantastic. The village is perfect for those who like a quiet and relaxing holiday away from the hustle and bustle. Vasilikos hotels, including Zante Royal Resort 4 (Zakynthos), are chosen, as already noted, by newlyweds who are attracted by the atmosphere of romance prevailing on the island, couples with children, business people who need relaxation in the truest sense of the word, well and persons on well-deserved rest.

By the way, diving fans also come here - travelers through the depths of the sea. Pure clear waters, an amazing underwater world are simply created for practicing this sport. If you are going to try yourself for the first time, experienced instructors from the indigenous inhabitants of the island will instruct you and help you make the first dive in your life.

The island has a National Marine Reserve, and many of the beaches, including the village of Vasilikos, are under its patronage. The thing is that loggerhead turtles live here in coastal areas. This is an endangered species of animals, and the country's authorities are doing everything possible to preserve them. This is another reason to come to the Zante Royal Resort 4 * hotel with children. Be sure that for your kids it will be the most interesting vacation. From morning to night, the hotel will be occupied by animators for your children and provide them with wonderful leisure. In the evening, you can take them to the beach, where they can watch the life of turtles in natural conditions.
