Samsung Phone Lineup: Features and Description

Samsung is one of the leading manufacturers of mobile phones and tablets today. The range of Samsung phones contains devices of various classes - from budget devices with a minimum of functions to top-end expensive models that have absorbed the latest engineering achievements. In this article, you will learn all about these phones.

Flagship

For many years, the flagship of this company is the Galaxy S series, which is currently produced in the seventh generation. It is also possible to purchase previous Galaxy models in mobile phone stores, however they are no longer manufactured.

Today, the Samsung lineup is headed by the Galaxy S7. In the device, the company used all the best practices and added support for all the functions that are at their disposal. The device is still controlled by Android. The heart of the smartphone is an 8-core processor, a 4GB RAM module and a 32 or 64GB hard drive, depending on the version. If this is not enough, you can always install a memory card with a capacity of up to 200 GB.





The smartphone display is worthy of separate praise. The large screen is 5.1 inches and with 2K resolution will surprise even the most sophisticated user. The display has the best viewing angles and color reproduction among all competitors. The only worthy opponent today is the Apple iPhone 6 and all its modifications.

Galaxy S7 has a version of Note. The gadget has an enlarged screen - 5.7 inches. Otherwise, the characteristics remained the same. The device is designed for those who seemed small display of the main model.

Middle price segment

Following the lineup of Samsung phones, the Galaxy J line continues. In terms of technical equipment, this series is slightly weaker than the flagship, but at a much cheaper price. The J7 is equipped with a 5.5-inch display with Full-HD resolution. Here is the same processor with 8 cores, but the frequencies are cut in it. The device is controlled by the Android operating system version 5.1. The amount of RAM is 3GB. For storage there is a 16 GB hard drive and an additional slot for memory cards with a capacity of up to 128 GB.





Budget smartphones

The lineup of Samsung phones continues the more budget device - Galaxy J1. For modest money, you get a pretty productive device. It has a processor with 4 cores, 1 GB of RAM and 8 GB for data storage with support for memory cards up to 128 GB. The resolution of the screen is a little frustrating - just 800 by 480 pixels. However, with a small diagonal of 4 inches, the image looks good. The picture is distorted only at strong slopes, it practically does not fade in the sun.
