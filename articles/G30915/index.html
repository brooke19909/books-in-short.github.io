Novotel Sharm El Sheikh Beach 5 * (Egypt / Sharm El Sheikh): photos and reviews of tourists

A lot of holidaymakers from all over the world annually choose Sharm el Sheikh, the famous Egyptian resort city of European significance, as their place of rest. Reviews about the rest in his hotels, located on the coast of the Riviera of the Red Sea, flood all the tourist portals.

A wide choice of leisure tours for the city guests is provided by 314 hotels (statistics taken from the annual rating of Egypt hotels - 2015). Most of them are five-star, however, there are four- and three-star.



The Egyptian resort business is good in that you can have a good rest here for a fairly affordable price.

Pricing principles are known. Roughly speaking, in most cases, the standard price of the tour is structured: it consists of 70% of the payment for hotel services, 30% of the funds paid by you is the price of the flight. Accordingly, speaking the language of an accountant, air travel is presented as an overhead cost, and the longer your stay in the Land of the Pharaohs, the more average each day (taking into account the reduction in the share of these invoices), cheaper.

In addition, the purchase of "hot tours" promises significant price benefits.

In this review article, while talking about the Novotel hotel, we will also pay attention to hotel complexes of other travel companies of different price categories.

Five star hotel in the heart of Sharm El Sheikh

The Novotel Sharm el Sheikh Beach 5 * hotel is a desired place for many tourists. Italians love him the most. So if you have the opportunity to purchase a tour to stay in it, and even in the period from June to September, do not refuse! On the facade of this medium-sized hotel complex with a room fund of 197 rooms, five stars deservedly flaunts. It really meets the highest evaluation criteria: based on the quantity and quality of the services provided, judging by the comfort in the client rooms, as well as the sophistication and aesthetics of the interiors and landscape designs (by the way, all the photos in this article illustrate it exclusively).

It offers visitors - two pools, three restaurants, two cafes, a bar. As part of the all inclusive, guests use the services of a hammam, jacuzzi, gym. They are also provided with a range of paid services: massage, beauty services, diving schools, windsurfing schools.

Hotel "Dessole" (former - "Pyramid"). Mini city

However, there are among holidaymakers and those who adore everything global, textured. Such people, accordingly, will want to buy a tour not just to a five-star hotel, but to a large five-star. Hotel "Kahramana". Ideal for youth

Well, what about the holidaymakers who are not financially pulling at a five-star hotel? For example, youth. After all, I want to hang out in the world-famous Naama Bay clubs (Little Buddha, Planet Hollywood, Hardrock Cafe) and the budget is running out.

However, there is a solution, since the Sharm el-Sheikh resort business is famous for its rating hotels in all price categories. Among the four-star hotel complexes also have their flagships. Hotel "Luna". Quality budget vacation

However, for those who want to save money there is an even more budget option - the three-star hotel Luna Sharm el Sheikh. Positive in his choice is a good location. Close to the Old City, the entertainment complex "A Thousand and One Nights", Naama Bay, a water park.

The hotel is small. Its two-story buildings form a ring in the middle of which is a swimming pool. It is better to relax here from June to September, since water heating in the pool is not provided. In cold weather, he is simply idle. The hotel area is compact, but landscaped very intricate and aesthetically pleasing.

We recommend to settle on the second floor (there are balconies).

The hotel rooms are quite comfortable, but the bathroom is a bonus: functional, convenient, spacious. Cleaning is daily, thorough.

The food from the hotel restaurant is hearty, tasty. Main dishes - from beef, chicken, turkey, liver, fish. Regularly - fresh cucumber salads and tomato. Fruits: watermelons, dates and melons.

Of course, the assortment is not as wide as in the five-star ones, but even the guests from Western Europe were completely satisfied with it.

The hotel does not have its own beach. Its guests use the services of the city beach, which is called Farsha. The fare to it is 2 Egyptian pounds.
