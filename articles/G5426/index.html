How to see the rest of the MTS traffic?

You can view the rest of MTS traffic in several ways at once. This article and the recommendations contained therein will be relevant for those subscribers who use the Smart series tariff plan (with prepaid services) on their SIM card.



The question of how to see the rest of the MTS traffic is very popular among mobile Internet users. By connecting, for example, the Smart Mini tariff, under the terms of which one gigabyte of traffic is provided monthly, the subscriber will need to regularly monitor how many megabytes have been spent, how much is left. This will help to avoid cases of writing off an additional fee from the SIM card balance for connecting new volumes of traffic and not to be left without the Internet at the right time. There are several options for obtaining relevant information. Let's consider them in more detail.

All ways

  • Application for mobile gadgets (tablets, smartphones) .
  • Personal account accessed through the official resource of the operator.
  • USSD request service .

How to check the balance of Internet traffic on MTS via the Internet?

It will be convenient for subscribers who have wired Internet access to a personal account on the operator‚Äôs website. Once registered, the client will be able to regularly view the information of interest to him about his account, to carry out any operations. Here you will see full information about Smart tariff plans: traffic balance, minutes and messages. At the first visit to your personal account, before you see the rest of the MTS traffic, you need to go through the registration procedure, following the prompts posted on the main page. In the future, to access data by number, you will need to specify the password assigned by the subscriber independently.





Customers who actively use the Internet from mobile devices will be interested to know that there is a special application developed by MTS to perform the same operations, but on gadgets such as a smartphone and tablet. Downloading this program is free (you can do this by going to the market of the operating system). The functionality of the mobile application is not much different from that presented in your personal account. When you first open the program on the device, you will also need to go through authorization. In the future, access to data by number will be done using a PIN code assigned by the user personally .

How to check the rest of the traffic on MTS: instruction (USSD service)

Short service commands (via asterisks and gratings) are very popular, because with their help you can quickly and without an internet connection get information about the balance, balances on TP, room costs, etc. Many users still prefer to use this particular service for receiving information about the status of your account.





To see the rest of MTS traffic on the display of a mobile device, you need to dial the following combination: * 111 * 217 #. In response to the SIM card with which the request was received, a notification will be sent that displays the balance of the packages included in the tariff plan.

other methods

You can also find out the rest of MTS traffic on Smart tariffs through the employees of the operator‚Äôs call center. By calling 0890, you can consult on any question of interest regarding your account. Please note that calls to the support service are not charged, provided that the call to a single number is made from the SIM card of the current operator. When calling a specialist, you should clarify the information about the owner of the room .





You can also contact customer support by calling toll-free 8-800-555 -0890 (all calls to this number are free, including from landlines and SIM cards of other telecom operators). Please note that by calling 0890, you can use the services of an automatic voice system - by switching between menu items you can also get information about your TP, balance included in the service tariff, clarify which options are activated on the SIM card, listen to other information.
