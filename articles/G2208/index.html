Limerick Holiday City in Ireland

A small country, which is famous for its clean air, picturesque landscapes, majestic castles, is not very popular among tourists from Russia. Few agencies offer trips to the Emerald Isle, and private tours to Ireland are quite expensive. However, those who want to discover new corners can be advised to organize a trip themselves, because it is easy to do, and getting an Irish visa is much easier than an English one.

The city founded by the Vikings

The tiny Limerick in Ireland is a great place to spend a vacation. The ancient town, attracting tourists with its mystery, is the administrative center of the county of the same name, located in the south-west of the country. The first people appeared here about 3 thousand years ago, but only in the 9th century the Vikings came to this land, forming a colony in a picturesque place where the small river Shannon originates.

From a small settlement grew a modern city, the third largest.

Weather and climate

The territory of the Irish pearl is dominated by a temperate maritime climate. In winter, the air temperature does not rise above 10 ¬∞ C during the day, and often exceeds 20 ¬∞ C in summer. Tourists who are going on vacation should take into account that the main feature of the climate is humid weather, and throughout the year there is a huge amount of rainfall in the form of snow and rains. A comfortable time to visit Limerick in Ireland is a sunny summer.

Medieval quarter

The historic city center is formed by three districts, and the most ancient of them is English. It is here that the most popular tourist sites are located. Guests can walk around the medieval quarter of Limerick on foot. The city in Ireland, consisting of ancient stone buildings, strikes with a special atmosphere, and thanks to its architectural monuments it is so easy to immerse yourself in past eras.

What to see in the city?

The main attractions of Limerick are the ancient architectural structures that reached the descendants in excellent condition. King John's Castle is located in the heart of the medieval quarter. This is an impressive building with powerful walls, narrow loopholes and three round towers. Now there are exhibits telling about the important role in the history of the city of a defensive fortress.

The Cathedral of the Holy Virgin Mary, combining the Romanesque and Gothic styles, appeared in the XII century. In the oldest city building, built of local limestone, which is famous for its bluish tint, services are now being held. A spire of religious attraction is the highest in the country.

No less interesting for tourists is the Hunt Museum (Hunt), famous for its extensive collection of artifacts from Greece, Rome, Egypt and Ireland. The pride of the cultural institution is stone and wooden statues, ceramic and crystal products made during the Middle Ages.

Picturesque village

Adair is considered the most beautiful village in Ireland - an object of cultural and historical heritage, where each stone remembers the glorious past of the region. The settlement, as if descended from the pages of fairy tales, is an amazing sight. Ancient houses, the roofs of which are covered with straw, picturesque gardens, a magnificent castle, gloomy monasteries, cozy cafes do not leave anyone indifferent.

The village, located on the Meig River, 16 kilometers from Limerick (Ireland), owes its unusual appearance to Count Dunraven, who independently developed the design of streets and cottages. Pastoral landscapes evoke a romantic mood, and it is no coincidence that many tourists come here to celebrate the wedding.

What else is the city famous for?

Throughout the world, the city is famous for its limericks - humorous five-verses that were created by local poets over the years.

Numerous green promenades are wonderful places created for hiking. Clean air and a peaceful atmosphere contribute to a relaxing stay.

There are an incredible amount of souvenir shops, fashion boutiques, and antique shops selling excellent quality goods.

Limerick in Ireland is a true holiday city. All year round, various festivals and festivals take place here, where you can get acquainted with national culture. City pubs serving local beer lure travelers from around the world.

Tours to Ireland are popular with tourists from around the world due to the huge number of attractions and stunning landscapes. To get acquainted with the ancient Limerick, you should definitely book an excursion program, and then walk along the narrow streets on your own.
