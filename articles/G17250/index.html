Skating rink "Tauride Garden": where to go on holidays

On New Year's holidays, people often make up a program to spend interesting days off. The winter season is great for active sports, including ice skating and skiing. It is not necessary to be a professional athlete to ride on ice on your own pleasure. The Tavrichesky Sad skating rink allows you to take up your favorite hobby not only in the cold season, but also at any other time of the year. But in winter, the place is especially popular, as many people come here to ride with family or friends.

general information

The indoor ice rink in the garden is quite popular. There is good ice, and you can also ride in the same sweater in cold weather. Every hour the rink is poured, so sliding on it is very comfortable. For visitors include musical accompaniment, so each session is very interesting. Good ice is suitable for both beginners and professionals. Athletes regularly train on it.

The Tauride Garden skating rink is open every day. You can arrive from 9 a.m. to 10 p.m. But nightly sessions also take place regularly. Information is available in the complex itself when you can ride late in the day. At these sessions, guests will find not only good music, but also lighting effects. Usually they start at 22.30 and last until 5 in the morning. Entrance to the rink on a normal day costs 450 rubles. For night skating the price is 500 rubles. On weekends and holidays, the ticket price is 600 rubles. For pupils and students there is a system of discounts.

Mass skating ("Tauride Garden") at the weekend starts at 9.30 in the morning and lasts until 10 in the evening, on Saturday from 9 in the morning to 20.45. Skate rental is available at the property. There are both for figure skating, and for hockey. A large dimensional grid allows you to choose the right option for yourself. On weekdays, skate rental will cost 200 rubles. On holidays, the price for them remains the same, but at night they will be more expensive: 400 rubles. If necessary, guests can contact specialists for sharpening skates.

Address

You can find the Tauride Garden ice rink in St. Petersburg at the following address: Potemkinskaya Street, building 4-A. The place is quite popular, so many citizens will be able to tell where it is. Landmarks can be the Tauride Garden, as well as the Neva River, which is located quite close. The skating rink is located near Voskresenskaya embankment.

You can get to it by metro. The Chernyshevskaya, Ploshchad Vosstaniya or Mayakovskaya stations are suitable. It is also accessible to come by train, if you leave at the station "St. Petersburg". As close as possible to the rink is a bus stop called "Tchaikovsky Street". Buses 46 and 136, as well as the K-76 minibus, stop at it.

Additional features

For the convenience of visitors created the most convenient conditions. Things are available to leave in the wardrobe and personal lockers. The complex also has showers, toilets. To assist at the rink there is a doctor. If guests are hungry, then they can always go to a cafe or restaurant. They are open right in the complex. At food outlets you can taste delicious pastries, hot drinks, Italian and European dishes.

Many visitors come not only to the Tauride Garden ice rink, but also to the fitness center. It has various simulators that allow you to maintain a healthy lifestyle.
