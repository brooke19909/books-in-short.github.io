Limpopo - water park in Tashkent: description

Not far from the Tien Shan, in the middle of a flowering oasis, the city of Tashkent is spread. This is a huge metropolis with numerous streets, squares, fountains and parks, combining the modern elegance inherent in European capitals and a special oriental flavor. Many tourists come here to relax, sightseeing, architectural monuments.

The city is located in the continental climate, so it is quite hot and arid summers. In July, during the whole day and night, the temperature can stay around 40 degrees Celsius with a plus sign.



When the season of such sultry heat is in full swing, many citizens are eager to find themselves near the water. Where exactly you can relax and swim in the summer heat is a question that many are asked. The answer to it may be a trip to the water park. Several such parks have been built in Tashkent, so the inhabitants of this city always have the opportunity to enjoy water procedures. The only thing left to do is to choose an institution.

The water park in Tashkent, the photo of which is presented in this article, is a wonderful place for a family vacation. Description of the water park

There are several diverse water parks in Tashkent that differ in size and number of slides. ‚ÄúLimpopo‚Äù began its work six years ago and is currently considered the average area in the city. On its territory there are four outdoor swimming pools with several water slides. The largest pool has a depth of 1.4 meters and is equipped with hydromassage. There are plenty of sun loungers around the pools where you can relax and sunbathe. Also at Limpopo there is a cafeteria, a playground for children, locker rooms, cozy booths.

Among the children's attractions there are inflatable scooters and zorb, which you can ride for an additional fee (50 rubles).

Location

Waterpark "Limpopo" in Tashkent is located in the Mirabad district, on Talimarjan street 135 (the territory of the Furkat park). Contact details of the organization and detailed information can be found on the official website. The water park is open daily from 10 am to 10 pm. Box office hours are until 9 p.m.

Customer opinions about the complex

Despite the appearance of a new water park in Tashkent, reviews about Limpopo continue to be positive. Many citizens consider it the most suitable institution of this kind in the city for a relaxing holiday with children. It is here that there are as many as five slides for the youngest customers, riding on which brings great pleasure to the kids. Older children prefer to swim in the adult pool. They are delighted with children's scooters and balloons, in which you can perfectly frolic.



As for the water in the pools, according to visitors, it is very clean and warm. Swimming is nice. The territory of the water park is clean and well-groomed, which is also an important factor when relaxing with children. For those who have enjoyed the swimming, there are comfortable sunbeds for relaxation on the territory of Limpopo, there is enough space for everyone.

He who is hungry goes to a cozy cafe of the complex, where you can have a tasty snack, taste refreshing drinks or enjoy aromatic tea with cake. In front of the cafe there is a small playground where the guys play while their order is being prepared.

A big plus of the water park is its small workload. There are practically no queues here, all people relax measuredly, no one is in a hurry. The only thing that guests are advised to take into account is the complex - in addition to paying the admission ticket, those who wish to swim will have to leave a deposit in the locker room in order to get the key to the locker. Visitors also note that valuables should not be left unattended, because in case of their loss, the organization is not responsible.
