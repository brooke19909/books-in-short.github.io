Hotel Simantro Beach Hotel 4 * (Greece / Halkidiki): overview, description, rooms and reviews

The building of the hotel complex Simantro Beach Hotel stands out from all other hotels. It looks very attractive. Largely due to its unusual architecture. Indeed, the image clearly shows elements of the Byzantine style. However, the appearance is not the only feature of this hotel.





About the hotel

Simantro Beach Hotel is built in the same style as the famous monasteries of Mount Athos. In the process of creating the complex, stone and wood were used, which further distinguishes the hotel from other hotels. By the way, it was built in 1992. Full restoration was carried out in 2000.

The hotel is located in the northwestern part of the peninsula, known as Kassandra. It occupies most of the territory of Halkidiki. Kassandra stretches 50 kilometers along. According to legends, it was this peninsula that was considered the land of giants.

From the hotel to the beach you can walk in just 1-2 minutes. But this is not the only nuance of its location. The complex is located 65 kilometers from the international airport. It takes a little more than an hour to get to the city called Thessaloniki from here (it is 75 km away).

By the way, the hotel itself is a 15 different buildings. The complex occupies a large territory, so you will not be able to miss it.





Service and Facilities

Simantro Beach Hotel has everything that guests may require. There is free wireless Internet, a private car park, an ATM with currency exchange (right in the building), as well as a left-luggage office and a tour desk. By the way, guests are also offered lockers and individual check-in (by agreement).

If people come to rest with their children, then they can call a nanny. The hotel also has childcare professionals. And also laundry, ironing and dry cleaning. The hotel even has an office where you can receive / send faxes, a business center and a large spacious conference room.

In addition to the above, on the territory there are shops, car rental, as well as the organization of the delivery of drinks and food to the apartments. Guests should be aware that they have the right to ask for soundproofed or family rooms.

As for the staff, well-trained professionals work here. Hotel staff speak four languages. In addition to Greek and English, also German and Russian. The latter is especially liked by our compatriots who want to relax here.

Leisure

While relaxing at the Simantro Beach Hotel, there are many ways to have fun. The most popular method here is, of course, the sea. It is here in close proximity. As a matter of fact, many people come here for the sake of the beach, tanning and sea baths.

However, on site you can have fun. There are swimming pools (two open and one indoor, heated), a sun terrace, and a barbecue. Lovers of physical activity will like a sports hall, which has a large number of different simulators.

And there is also a SPA center in which you can devote time to wellness and beauty treatments. Massage, pedicure and manicure, aroma and hydrotherapy, sauna - all this is available in the local spa owned by the hotel.

More guests can go diving, tennis and various water sports. Even the children that their parents took with them on a trip will not be bored here. Two separate small pools and a playground are intended for them.





Bars and Restaurants

Many people, before opting for a particular hotel, are interested in how they are fed. In Simantro Beach Hotel 4 * there are three restaurants and the same number of bars. Naos is an institution decorated in an unusual style of the monastery refectory. There you can taste dishes of both national and international cuisine. By the way, a vegetarian menu is also offered. This restaurant is open every day, starting at 7 a.m. Closes after nine in the evening, after dinner.

Also in Simantro Beach Hotel 4 * there is a restaurant called Troulos. People can taste the treats there from 20:30 until midnight. And there is also the option to visit The Olympos Beach Taverna. This restaurant serves traditional Greek dishes, as well as fish and light snacks. It works from noon until 18:00.

Fans of soft drinks, ice cream, snacks and coffee will love the Maistrali Beach Bar. It is open every day, and you can find it on the beach. By the way, by the pool, there is also a bar. And in the lobby of the main building there is a Macedonian establishment with a huge selection of alcohol and more. It is open until half past one in the night.

According to people staying at Club Hotel Simantro Beach, the food in all the places listed is very tasty and of high quality. There is plenty of it: meat, fish, poultry, pastries, ice cream, and also vegetables and various fruits (nectarines, peaches, oranges, dates, watermelons, melons, apples, pears and much more). Good mashed soups are also served. Usually vegetable and tomato. And breakfasts are also varied - you can taste yogurts, scrambled eggs, sausages, cookies with muffins, sausages, cheese and much more.

Even a children's menu is available. And for infants, it is also diverse - 10 types of all kinds of mashed potatoes. In general, no one will remain hungry, deciding to settle in this hotel.
