Something from the story

Kemer in the era of antiquity bore the name Idrios (under the rule of Byzantium), and the Lycians owned it. When the Ottoman Empire came to power, the village began to be called Eskikey, which from Turkish means "Old Village". In those days, the inhabitants of the settlement often suffered from large mudflows from the mountains. After such disasters, everything around turned into a swamp, which began to cause strong outbreaks of malaria.

In 1910, the construction of a stone wall was begun, which was supposed to protect against such natural disasters. The constructed wall is similar to a belt, which in Turkish sounds like "Kemer". And so the name of this settlement arose.

Kemer until 1970 was a rather seedy fishing village due to the lack of good roads connecting with the rest of Turkey, from which this settlement was practically cut off at that time. After the construction of new highways, everything changed radically. The village began to develop rapidly. Soon, beautiful hotels grew between the sea and the mountains, and Kemer gradually turned into a city with a growing tourism business, which is a city-forming.



And what a sea in Kemer, and what a nature! This resort is not quite typical for Turkey, as vegetation abounds here, and all this is enclosed by magnificent mountains. Many Russians say that the landscapes are a bit like the Caucasus (Black Sea coast).

Today, most of the local population is engaged in the hotel business.

Geographical position

Kemer is not very large (the population is only 22,500 people), surrounded by greenery, a resort town located 40 kilometers south of Antalya. It is located on the coast of a wonderful bay.

What sea is Kemer washing? Turkey has many wonderful resorts on the shores of three seas. And this amazingly beautiful resort town is washed by the azure clear waters of the Mediterranean Sea. Kemer is considered the "heart" of the Antalya coast.

The resort area of ​​Kemer is divided into separate areas: Goynuk, Beldibi, Camyuva, Kirish, Tekirova and Kemer.

About climate and water

What sea in Turkey in Kemer is known to many who have been here. It is much cleaner than in other resorts. And the climate here is special.

Kemer is located in the Mediterranean climate zone. At the same time, despite the geographical proximity to Antalya, Kemer has a special, as noted above, microclimate. For example, the monsoon character is not so pronounced here - in winter there is less rainfall than in Antalya, and rainfall in summer is a rare occurrence. Therefore, Kemer has a drier climate than Antalya.

Summer is hot, sunny - the temperature in the shade sometimes exceeds 40 ° C. Clear weather in Kemer lasts most of the year. But winter is cooler in these places. Often there are frosts, sometimes snow falls.



What is the sea in Kemer, what is the water in it? The big advantage of the resort is that the sea here is always clean and very transparent, it is not muddy. Water in the winter has a temperature of 17-19 ° C, and in the summer - about 27 ° C.

According to the latest data, the warmest water in the Kemer Sea happens in July, August and September. In April, its average temperature is 17.9 ° Celsius, while the maximum and minimum values ​​reached 18.9 ° and 17.4 °, respectively.
