Alushta, boarding house "Sea". Crimea, Alushta: boarding houses. Alushta, pensions: reviews, prices, photos

The southern coast of Crimea is one of the most visited resort places on the peninsula. Transparent, clear sea, sandy beaches, beautiful landscapes, healing mud and natural springs - all this attracts tourists. One of the most popular cities in Crimea is considered Alushta. Pension "Sea", along with other resort centers, for many years accepts vacationers from different countries. It is located in a quiet and cozy corner, it allows you to relax, take a break from the hustle and bustle, enjoy the clean and fresh air and, of course, improve the body.



Location of the boarding house "Sea"

What is this resort center good for? The proximity to Simferopol Airport (less than an hour's drive), high-level service, a wide variety of entertainment programs, and most importantly - a good location in the lap of nature - all this makes vacationers a desire to come to the boarding house "Sea". Alushta (Crimea) used to be called the Russian Riviera, in the 19th century it was the most prestigious resort in Europe. Today, the city is striking in its cleanliness, a huge selection of entertainment programs, and wellness facilities offered by hotels.

Pension "Sea" is located in the Professor's Corner, 11 original buildings are hidden in the shade of exotic trees of the relic park. All buildings have different architecture, but at the same time look harmoniously against the backdrop of a mountain landscape. It is never too stuffy here, and all thanks to the "Gate of the Winds." Through these passes air flows of enormous power.

Alushta is always glad to married couples with children, youth, elderly people. Pension "Sea" is located away from noisy places, so people come here in search of peace and quiet.
