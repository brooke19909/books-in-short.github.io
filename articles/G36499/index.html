Magic Royal Kenz Thalasso & Spa 4 * (Tunisia / Sousse): photos and reviews of tourists

Numerous destinations are open for the modern tourist, which is why you can enjoy the sun, sea and gorgeous views all year round. All lovers and true connoisseurs of beach vacations who want to relax, enjoy the peace and tranquility, and certainly not carry on tiresome excursions, quite often choose Tunisia. And all this is largely due to the chic service and affordable cost of rest. A comfortable climate, a clean Mediterranean Sea with a rich underwater world create amazing conditions for relaxing on the coast.





When choosing a resort, we suggest paying attention to Sousse. Many vacationers managed to fall in love with him for the developed infrastructure and proximity to the coast. If you had the opportunity to relax in other towns and villages of the country, you will definitely feel the difference, because most of them are stretched along the coastline. Sousse is a completely different story: everything is so conveniently located that the tourist can only relax and tune in to a calm and relaxed vacation. All vacationers are impressed by the city's public beach: the widest coastline with clean fine sand - what else do beach lovers need? The hotels here are excellent, with first-class service and developed infrastructure. Most of them offer a full range of travel services, so vacationers do not even have to leave the hotel. Magic Royal Kenz Thalasso & Spa 4 * is a hotel that is quite often found in offers from tour operators. Based on reviews from real customers, we offer you to evaluate it from all sides.

About hotel

Magic Royal Kenz Thalasso & Spa 4 * (Tunisia) is a beachfront hotel located on the first coastline, just 10 km from the center of Sousse. This is a large enough hotel with a huge surrounding area, lots of entertainment and a great beach. Judging by the numerous reviews of tourists, the hotel fully justifies its 4 * category and managed to earn a good reputation in the largest tourist portals.





Population

Even after a short flight, I want to get the room keys as soon as possible and begin to relax. So, most tourists say that they settle in a hotel even earlier than the agreed date. Vacationers who arrive in the early morning receive a room in 5-7 minutes. Agree, such a courtesy of the staff simply can not go unnoticed.

Rooms

Hotel Magic Royal Kenz Thalasso & Spa 4 * 470 spacious rooms with a gorgeous view of the coast. Each room has a balcony, in superior rooms it is replaced by a luxurious terrace where you can spend your evenings with a glass of aromatic wine. In a separate bathroom, all conditions for a comfortable reception of water procedures are created: high-quality plumbing, a set of bath towels for each vacationer, a hairdryer, bath accessories. When pre-booking the services of the thalasso center, tourists will receive bath towels and slippers. The air conditioning system is responsible for the comfort and coolness of the rooms. Of the technical equipment, it should be noted a TV with satellite channels, as well as a telephone for communication with hotel staff. Use of the safe is at extra cost. Filling the minibar is paid, this must be taken into account during your stay so that when you evict from your room you will not encounter an unpleasant surprise.





There is not much furniture in the room, but it is enough for a comfortable stay: wide beds, bedside tables, a chair, a table, a wardrobe in the hallway, a small sofa with a table and plastic furniture on the balcony. All furniture is modern, stylish. In general, the rooms are quite bright. And let the tourists who come to the sea spend only a minimum of their time in the room, I want to be in the most comfortable atmosphere.

Room service

Rooms at the Magic Royal Kenz Thalasso & Spa 4 * Hotel (Port) are cleaned daily, and towels are also changed every day. Cleaning is done during the absence of tourists in the room, so nothing will disturb their comfort. Garbage is thrown out, the floor is washed, plumbing is cleaned, towels and bed linen are changed - upon returning to the room, vacationers will notice pleasant freshness and cleanliness.

Nutrition

Magic Royal Kenz Thalasso & Spa 4 * hotel offers all-inclusive meals. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served in the main restaurant of the hotel. For a bite to eat, check out the poolside bar. Alcoholic and soft drinks can be consumed around the clock in unlimited quantities.





What kind of food does the Magic Royal Kenz Thalasso & Spa 4 * hotel offer to all vacationers? Reviews of tourists note that the tables literally burst with an abundance of dishes: fish, meat, chicken, fresh vegetables and fruits, sweets, fragrant pastries, drinks to choose from - this is only a small part of what awaits every tourist in the hotel restaurant. The table is constantly replenished, so there is no need, barely waking up, to run for breakfast: you certainly will not stay hungry. Some note a lack of seafood, but if you look at the photo you will see that shrimp, squid, even mussels are served for dinner. If you suddenly want to try something special, you can always visit one of the local restaurants and enjoy the culinary masterpieces of national and Mediterranean cuisine.

Entertainment on site

If you choose the hotel Magic Royal Kenz Hotel Thalasso & Spa 4 *, you can have a great time on its territory. There are two outdoor pools and one indoor pool with sea water and heating, however, their use is not included in the price - you will have to pay extra. Tourists note that in the season two outdoor pools are enough: they are really big, equipped with convenient slopes, there are several slides. The indoor pool is located in the thalasso center, most often used by tourists who come to Tunisia in the winter, when the water in the sea becomes cooler.

The Magic Royal Kenz Thalasso & Spa 4 * hotel has good animation for adults and children: you definitely won‚Äôt be bored. Various sports are held between vacationers, fiery music is played in the evenings, everyone can attend water aerobics classes, play beach volleyball, tennis or badminton. Access to the fitness center and rental of water activities are available at an additional cost.





For an additional fee, everyone can visit the thalasso zone, where a peaceful atmosphere reigns. A relaxing and invigorating massage, scrubs and body wraps can all be found here. Prices are more than reasonable, you can definitely not even try to find an alternative in the city.

Service

Vacationers are greeted by the friendly and polite staff of the Magic Royal Kenz Thalasso & Spa 4 * Hotel (Tunisia), ready to answer all your questions and solve problems 24 hours a day. For an additional fee at the reception you can use the safe for storing documents and valuables. An access point to the wireless Internet is located only in the lobby, you can use it for free. As the tourists themselves assure, the speed is quite enough to find the necessary information and communicate on social networks.





If suddenly tourists feel sad, you can always contact the reception and get acquainted with the excursion program of the resort. Quite often, vacationers go on a luxury yacht to the open sea for fishing, diving, and admire the beauties of the resort from the ship. You can go on an extreme and exciting journey on ATVs. The excursion program is not impressive with its diversity, but harmoniously fits into a calm and measured beach holiday

Beach

Hotel Magic Royal Kenz Thalasso & Spa 4 * is located on the first coastline, respectively, just a stone's throw from the sea. The beach is not bad, clean, with fine sand and a gentle entry into the sea. The Mediterranean Sea here is beyond praise. If you have long wanted to go diving, spearfishing, this place is simply created for you. Many of those tourists who first come to Tunisia are ready to spend all their free time on the coast: such beautiful landscapes open before their eyes.





The disadvantage of the hotel is the minimum number of sunbeds and umbrellas on the beach: a real fight is being waged for them. In order to take a free place, vacationers have to get up early in the morning and go to the beach Magic Royal Kenz Thalasso & Spa 4 *. Photos of tourists only confirm this. In season, the beach is not only crowded, but also quite dirty.

To summarize

Hotel Magic Royal Kenz Thalasso & Spa 4 * is a good option for a vacation in Tunisia. An excellent landscaped area, developed infrastructure, high-quality and varied food, comfortable rooms and impeccable service are what attract many vacationers. Of course, I would like for the price to include more free services, but for the price of the room that the hotel offers, you can forgive him this drawback.

We advise you to take a closer look at this option if you are looking forward to an excellent holiday on the Mediterranean coast.
