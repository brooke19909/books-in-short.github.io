Veko Refrigerators: customer reviews. Veko refrigerators: how to choose

Veko brand refrigerators are manufactured by one well-known company, Arkelik, which is located in Turkey. This company produces a wide variety of electrical appliances, starting in 1955. Previously, she was part of Kok Holding.

Its founder is considered to be engineer Wehby Koch. Initially, he was engaged exclusively in building materials. Further it was planned to produce various pastes and canned food under the Veko brand. However, this idea was not destined to come true. Soon at one of the factories, Wehby Koch began to manufacture light bulbs. In Turkey, they were in great demand, and the business of the company went up.





The first Veko refrigerators

The world first saw Veko refrigerators in 1959. The Arkelik company produced them in parallel with washing machines. Since 1977, Veko refrigerators in Turkey have been in great demand. In the same year, the company's management decided to build a separate plant in order to make the best refrigerator in the country. For this purpose, a new enterprise "Ardem" was founded.

The company's specialists constantly studied the market and produced more advanced refrigerators. The real popularity of "Veko" deserved only by 1990. At that time, refrigerators of this brand were used in many countries of Europe, as well as America.





Advantages of Veko refrigerators

Most refrigerators of the Veko trademark are famous for their convenience. In this case, freezers are usually located in the upper part. The dimensions of many models are quite compact, which undoubtedly pleases many. Management as a whole is pleasant and does not cause difficulties.

As the refrigerant in the refrigerator, the familiar type P600a is mainly used by everyone. There is a special antibacterial coating inside the freezer . The Nou Frost dry defrost system is installed in almost all models. Inside the refrigerator there is a sufficient number of shelves and drawers for storing food. Additionally, various egg holders may be included.





What are the downsides?

Of the shortcomings , the noise level can be noted first . For most refrigerators, this figure is at the level of 50 dB. Compared to other manufacturers, this is too much. As a result, being near a running compressor for a long time is not very comfortable. Repairing the Veko refrigerator costs a lot; this is another drawback.

Among other things, there are problems with the doors. As a rule, their fastening is not very strong. As a result, they often break down in many, they have to be repaired. An additional inconvenience lies in the fact that the doors can not be outweighed. As a result, if the furniture in the kitchen does not allow opening the Veko refrigerator (manufacturer Arkelik) to the right, then it will have to be moved to another place.

Summary

Summing up, we can say that Veko refrigerators are quite competitive. There are certain disadvantages associated with excessive noise, but they are not critical. Among the advantages can be identified the power of freezers, as well as the management of refrigerators.

Finally, the company's cost policy is rather soft and Veko refrigerators have different prices (from 17 to 30 thousand rubles), which always allows you to choose the right model for yourself.
