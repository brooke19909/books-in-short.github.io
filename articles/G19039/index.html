Description

On the fenced and guarded territory of the Pelagiada sports and recreation complex, you will find small log houses made in the same architectural style. In two reservoirs intended for swimming, pure spring water. There is a private sandy beach, equipped with showers, changing rooms, toilets. Shadow zones have been created where you hide from the hot summer sun. Near a small pond, specially designed for young tourists, after swimming, you can play in the playground. For adults, there is a volleyball court, as well as billiards. For those wishing to relax, the sauna is open.

In the evenings, the area is illuminated. Car owners are provided with a parking space. Pelagiada is the venue for the Open AIR youth music festival. Barbecue lovers can use barbecue grills and sit in company equipped gazebos. It has its own cafe, and in the banquet hall and on the summer terrace you can celebrate a family celebration or hold a corporate party. The recreation center "Pelagiada" in the Stavropol Territory is located at the address: Pelagiada village, Sofievskaya street, 1A.

Accommodation for vacationers

Guests are offered two options for accommodation.

  • At the recreation center "Pelagiada" (photo below), you will find all city amenities in separate eco-friendly wooden buildings. Each room has a bathroom, air conditioning, TV and fridge. Near the house is a gazebo with barbecue. For accommodation in such a house guests will pay from 3000 to 5000 rubles per day, depending on the conditions provided.
  • For guests there is also an economic offer - accommodation in trailers. Each has air conditioning and a fridge. The cost of living in such trailers is from 2000 rubles per day.

One house can accommodate 4-6 people at a time. Meals are not included in the price. Holidays at the Pelagiada (a base for tourists) for children under 12 years of age are provided free of charge. Rest on the "Pelagiad", a base for tourists. Reviews

Tourists leave both positive and, unfortunately, many negative reviews about their stay in the Pelagiada Sports and Recreation Complex.

  • From the positive, guests like the clean, well-groomed and green area around beautiful nature.
  • There is a large equipped sandy beach.
  • It is also good that there are many gazebos and tables in the shade of the trees.
  • As for the reservoirs, they, according to guests, are not cleaned, and they have a lot of garbage.
  • Food at the base is not organized.
  • In log houses, everything is located very closely, it is difficult to turn around.
  • Some vacationers had a TV hanging on the side of the bed, so there was no way to watch it.
  • From sports entertainment only volleyball net on the beach.
  • Also, many people think that the prices for staying here are very high.
