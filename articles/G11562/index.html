Curious story

Where did these smooth, sharp, small and large stones come from on the beaches of Sochi? And these mountain rivers and rivulets have been grind the Caucasus Mountains for centuries and carried various debris, boulders, tiles to the Black Sea. The sea continued to grind and grind them. The mountains of the Caucasus are 70 million years old. They grew and formed during the eruption of lava. After all, the local mountains are volcanoes that are already extinct. Pumice is a frozen magma, filled with air, and therefore light. There are tuffs and basalts that simply froze at the exit of the vent. They are heavier than pumice, but, of course, lighter than granite. By the way, it can also be found on the beaches.
