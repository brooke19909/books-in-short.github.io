Marina Holiday Club 4 * (Bulgaria, Pomorie): hotel infrastructure, room description, service, reviews

Bulgaria is perfect for a budget holiday on the Black Sea, so today it is so popular with Russian tourists. Along its coast there are many resorts that have their own focus and features. One of them was the city of Pomorie , famous for its healing mud. Therefore, people come here not only in search of a relaxing beach holiday, but also in order to restore their health. The resort has many hotels of different price categories. If you prefer high-quality service at reasonable prices, then pay attention to the Marina Holiday Club 4 * apartment complex in Bulgaria. We will tell in more detail in our article about its location, rooms, service and reviews of tourists who have rested here earlier.

Holidays in Bulgaria

Like any other tourist country, Bulgaria has its own characteristics that you need to know before you buy a ticket to one of its resorts. It has hotels of various categories, most of which are still considered budget. Once they had sanatoriums for the ruling Soviet elite, but after a regime change, they underwent a thorough renovation and were adapted to the needs of modern tourists. The high season begins here in mid-May and ends in mid-September. In addition, Pomorie is considered the sunniest resort in the country, as the climate here is more similar to the Mediterranean. The weather in Bulgaria in June is good, the temperature varies from 20 to 25 degrees, while the water warms up steadily to 23 degrees. However, July is considered the most favorable month for swimming here, when the temperature is kept at around 30 degrees.

Residents of Russia and most CIS countries for holidays in Bulgaria will need medical insurance and a visa, and they can be issued at the embassy. Tourists prefer to get to the country by air, but you can do this by ship, your own car or train. There are 4 international airports in Bulgaria, which are located in Sofia , Varna, Burgas and Plovdiv. There is a direct train from Moscow to the capital in high season, and you will have to spend about 52 hours on the way. At the same time, he stops for a long time in other large cities of Europe, for example, in Belgrade and Warsaw.

Hotel location

Location is one of the decisive factors that tourists rely on when choosing a hotel. Marina Holiday Club 4 * complex in Bulgaria is built on the outskirts of the resort of Pomorie , so even at night it is quite quiet. At the same time, the hotel is slightly removed from the central part of the city. To get to the historical district, tourists need to overcome 2 km. The nearest public beach is 200 meters away. Near the hotel there is a large water park, as well as a salt museum and a lake. There is a bus station 3 km from the complex, from where you can leave for other regions of Bulgaria. The closest to Pomorie is Burgas , which is 12 km away. There, tourists can visit large shopping centers, an opera house, a football stadium and a marina.

The international airport is 6 km away. From it to the resort can be reached by bus, taxi or own car. In addition, a tour bus with air conditioning to the hotel and vice versa can be included in the ticket for an additional fee.

Hotel Marina Holiday Club 4 *: room description

As mentioned above, the rooms of this hotel resemble studio apartments , that is, they have everything you need not only for an overnight stay, but also for a full stay. For example, guests can cook their own meals. The rooms are modern renovated, which is not distinguished by luxury. The walls are painted in pastel colors, and a laminate is laid on the floor. Hotel staff provides daily cleaning in all living rooms. They change linen and bath towels about 2 times a week, however, they can do this more often on request. All apartments have a private outdoor balcony, which includes a small dining area and a clothes dryer. The hotel windows face the courtyard where the pool is located, or the city buildings.





We list the main apartments that the housing stock of this hotel has:

  • Two Bedroom Apartment - a two-room suite consisting of a separate bedroom, a living room and a bathroom. One of the rooms has no windows. Glass doors are installed between the rooms. Room size is 55 square meters. m. Here 5-6 guests can live.

Entertainment

Negative hotel reviews

However, a rare hotel boasts an impeccable reputation. The guests of this hotel in their reviews also note many shortcomings. But they are not too significant and will not greatly spoil your impression of rest. We list the main shortcomings that residents of Marina Holiday Club 4 * noted:
