Location Description





Travelers are primarily looking for information about the location of the hotel complex. Anonymous Beach Hotel 3 * is located in the famous resort called Ayia Napa. The city center with all its attractions is just 1.5 km away. The nearest airport is approximately 48 km away.

The hotel itself consists of one multi-storey building, as well as a small courtyard. The entire territory is surrounded by a lush tropical garden, the paths of which are always pleasant to walk. The famous Sandy Beach is just 100 meters away.

Hotel Anonymous Beach Hotel 3 *: photos and a brief description of the rooms





Of course, for a pleasant stay, living conditions are important, because in the evening you want to return to a cozy clean room. The hotel complex offers travelers decent living conditions, as evidenced by reviews of guests.

The hotel has 90 rooms. For the most part, these are standard double rooms, but there are also connecting rooms that are suitable for tourists vacationing with a large family or company. The furniture in the rooms is comfortable, there is access to a terrace or balcony. Depending on the location of the room, the windows have views of the sea, pool or tropical garden.

You can count on a set of necessary equipment. In particular, an air conditioning system is an indispensable assistant in the summer heat. While relaxing, you can watch satellite channels on TV. There is a telephone and a small safe, and a minibar is stocked upon request. By the way, room service is available around the clock.

There is a small bathroom with a shower, toilet, washbasin and mirror. Towels are changed every day. Rooms are also cleaned daily, and bed linen is changed two or three times a week.

Meals for guests





Without a doubt, good nutrition is very important for a really good vacation. What can Anonymous Beach Hotel offer? Accommodation on site includes daily breakfast in the main restaurant. In addition, guests can pay for dinner. By the way, two meals a day is the most popular scheme among travelers. The restaurant menu is quite extensive, and the food is always tasty. Moreover, almost every day themed nights are organized for tourists on the cuisine and customs of a country.

The hotel also has a tavern with an outdoor terrace serving Cypriot and Greek dishes. There is a bar by the pool, which mainly offers chilled drinks and cocktails, as well as light snacks and desserts.

Where to look for a beach? Water leisure tourists





What in terms of a beach holiday can Anonymous Beach Hotel offer? Ayia Napa is a resort famous for its picturesque coasts and mild climate. The nearest Sandy Beach is located just 100 meters from the hotel - you can get to it in a few minutes. This is a public beach, and therefore you will need to pay for the rental of a deck chair, umbrella, beach towels .

The coast here is clean, covered with soft sand, and there is a quiet backwater near the coast where children like to swim. Naturally, here travelers can ride boats, boats, water skiing, engage in sailing and scuba diving, in a word, you can count on a full range of entertainment.

Additional services for guests

Despite the fact that the Anonymous Beach Hotel does not belong to the group of luxury five-star resort complexes, it still offers some additional services. For example, here you can easily rent a car or other form of transport. Guests enjoy a free parking area.

In addition, you will have the opportunity to rent a safe from the administrator, which will provide your documents and things with the maximum degree of security. In case of health problems, travelers can rely on the help of a good doctor (his services are paid separately). There is also a small mini market where you can purchase products and some household items.

Entertainment and leisure of tourists





Most tourists come to this city in search of beach fun. Nevertheless, you can have a good time at the Anonymous Beach Hotel. In the courtyard there is a small but clean pool, around which are comfortable sun beds with mattresses and umbrellas. Here, guests can get a clean towel and a chilled drink. Tourists like to spend time by the pool, because here you can swim, refresh yourself, sunbathe and just enjoy chatting with other travelers.

You can also use the sauna, although this is a paid service. In addition, the hotel has a TV lounge with a large collection of a variety of films and cartoons. Tourists who want to always keep fit can visit the gym with modern equipment.

In the evening, themed parties are organized for guests, in particular Cypriot, Italian, Greek. You can enjoy delicious dishes, music and the atmosphere of a country. Do not forget about sightseeing holidays. At the local tour desk you can get a map and information on the most interesting places in the surroundings, as well as purchase a group tour with a guide who will gladly reveal the secrets of the island to you. And do not forget that you will live almost in the city center, where there is a lot of entertainment.

Rest with a child





Many tourists prefer to spend holidays with their children, because they also need healing sea air and fun entertainment. What can the Anonymous Beach Hotel offer? Reviews say that children of different ages feel quite comfortable at the hotel.

If necessary, parents can ask to put a comfortable rollaway bed in their room. As for food, the local restaurant and tavern has a fairly large selection of dishes that are suitable for the child.

In the courtyard there is a small shallow pool designed for bathing children. The guys have good fun and on a spacious playground with swings, a sandbox, etc. The hotel provides professional babysitting services, however, this is paid separately.
