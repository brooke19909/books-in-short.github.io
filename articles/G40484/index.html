Werner von Braun: biography, family, photos and interesting facts

It seemed that as soon as the Apollo 11 broke away from the launch platform, the world entered a new era of space exploration. Among the people who watched the launch from the launch room 30 years ago was Dr. Werner von Braun - the creator of the Saturn rocket, on which astronauts were supposed to go to the moon. He promised that this space journey would open new frontiers for man. From the surface of the earth, ships will set off to plow the universe, benefiting science and all of humanity. Von Braun became the new Columbus for America.

Werner von Braun and his dreams of space

Werner von Braun, whose biography is revealed in the article, dreamed about space from childhood. He lived to make his dream a reality. He believed that flying into space is a necessary step in the evolution of mankind, and fate will help him take this step. However, the new dawn of science has become for some people a painful memory of years of exhausting labor, almost as slaves.

Werner von Braun, whose photo you see, was a very intelligent person. He wanted to build a rocket at all costs. He believed that the fate of mankind is the conquest of Cosmos, and was ready to pay for it. The biography of Werner von Braun became an endless crusade. He was ready for anything, just to make a breakthrough into space. The spacecraft he created to fly to the moon based on a ballistic missile was a new step in evolution. While Brown himself took a step from the Nazi to a NASA employee.

Werner von Braun family

Interest in the stars arose at Werner in Berlin in the 20s. He was born into an aristocratic German family. For many centuries their land belonged to the east in Germany. As minister, the head of the family moved to his Berlin residence. Werner was the second of his three sons. Great attention was paid to raising children in the family. Werner's interest was shared by thousands of his fellow citizens. Many believed that a sufficiently large rocket would lift anything. During World War I, a rocket was used as a weapon. Now, embraced by another utopian idea, people believed that it would help them open the doors to a new peaceful era. The work of rocketry enthusiasts inspired von Braun and his brother to do their own experiments. They built a small rocket launcher of fireworks. She crashed into the basement window of a grocery store, and her father said that this was the end of the space epic for the brothers. Werner did not stop it.

Cooperation with the Nazis

Hitler was impressed with Brown's success, but he was not satisfied with the speed of work. In the official photo, Werner von Braun barely found the strength to smile. The meeting was not very good. Work in a concentration camp

The Werner von Braun rocket was built at a secret underground factory in the Harz Mountains. For work on the rocket, a concentration camp was created. At first it was necessary to expand the underground tunnel. For 5 months, 8 thousand people practically did not see the daylight, digging this tunnel. They were extremely brutally abused by SS guards who monitored their work. In the works from overwork thousands of people died. Many were killed by the guards.

Von Braun frequented the tunnel. Recently discovered documents confirm that he was present at meetings when the use of slave labor was discussed. At one of these meetings, it was decided to replace the deceased prisoners with two thousand French prisoners. In addition, von Braun often visited the Buchenwald concentration camp, which was located nearby.
