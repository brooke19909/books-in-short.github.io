How to find out the budget classification code? Tax classification codes for taxes

The problem of how to find out the budget classification code is faced by almost every taxpayer when the deadlines for paying taxes come. Nobody can avoid it: neither the accountant of the organization responsible for the relevant transfers to the tax, nor ordinary citizens who own housing, land, a car or a simple boat motor.





How to find out the budget classification code?

All codes of the budget classification, in the colloquial terms of the KBK, - both in terms of expenses and incomes - are approved by the Ministry of Finance with its order. It seems that the simplest and most reliable way would be to find this order either in the library or on the Internet, take the necessary information from there, pay taxes and live in peace. But here's the problem: the budget classification code - CSC - consists of as many as twenty digits, and the Ministry of Finance has a tiny peculiarity - annually in December to make small, but changes to the classification approved by it.





Improve, so to speak. And this improvement itself turns out to be that if a potential taxpayer finds the wrong version of the KBK and pays taxes on it, then the Federal Tax Service will simply not see this payment and will number the bona fide taxpayer in debtors, quietly charging penalties. Of course, the problem can be solved by visiting the inspection and writing a statement. But, firstly, this is an additional cost of time and nerves, and secondly, it will be desirable to present the primary document of payment. It is not difficult for organizations to raise the primary market, after all, they keep accounting records, but for an ordinary person, everything can be much more complicated. Do all keep billing documents for three years? Of course not. KBK for citizens

Since the tax authorities formulate and send out special notifications to simplify the procedure for paying the basic taxes (land, transport, property) that the legislation has imposed on ordinary citizens, usually the question of how to find out the budget classification code does not arise. Personal income tax is usually levied , paid and reported by the organization where the person is officially employed. The task to find out the required BSC arises when a citizen, for example, has purchased a house, paid for treatment or training, because there is a natural desire to receive a tax deduction provided by law. To do this, you need to fill out and submit to your inspection a declaration in the form of 3 personal income tax. The budget classification code in it is a very important attribute.





Tax prefers to take it purely in electronic form to simplify the processing procedure. The best way out when compiling it is to go back to the tax website, find the appropriate program and download it to your PC. Filling will not present any difficulty: it will be enough to choose the right positions from the drop-down context menus. If you do not have your own computer, then you can go to the inspection directly to fill out the papers: they usually provide equipped workplaces where you can fill out your declaration in a live queue.

Conclusion

Budget classification codes of both expenses and income are very important details in most payment documents. Ordinary people do not need to know them by heart, but it is very important to have the skills to find them.
