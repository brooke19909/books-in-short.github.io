Hotel "Astoria" in Saratov: description, location, reviews

If you are looking for an option of comfortable and affordable accommodation in the city of Saratov, the Astoria Hotel will be an excellent accommodation both for a day and for a longer period. The review below contains all the basic information about the operation of this complex. Here you can find addresses, descriptions of services, room categories, cost of living, as well as reviews of guests who have stayed in hotels of this network.

Description

Hotels "Astoria" in Saratov is a whole complex of hotels, which includes hotels called "Volga", "Saratov" and "Olympia". The complex was established in 1990 and is considered the largest organization providing hotel services in the Saratov region. In any hotel complex customers are waiting for quality service, comfort and reasonable prices.





Location

All hotels of the complex are located in the center of Saratov:

  • The Astoria Hotel (renamed the Volga Hotel in 1956) is located at Kirova Ave., Building 34.
  • The Saratov Hotel is located at 2 Sadovaya Street, building No. 9.
  • Hotel "Olympia" is located at: Chernyshevsky street, 61.

Detailed information on the work, services and prices of each hotel can be found on the official website of the complex or at the hotel administrators.

When booking rooms from the site, customers receive a five percent discount.

Services

The complex offers more than two hundred rooms of various categories. To accommodate business people who come to the city at conferences, seminars or sports events, inexpensive rooms of the Economy category will be convenient. Pop stars, celebrities and VIPs are perfect for luxury rooms, as well as Apartment and Studio.





Each hotel of the Astoria complex (Saratov) has unlimited Internet access, cable TV, free parking, transfer, laundry, and organization of excursion programs. The price includes breakfast. Accepted both cash and card payments.

"Olympia"

This is a nine-story hotel located in one of the central areas of the city of Saratov. An ideal place to accommodate guests who wish to be in the very center of the cultural, business and sports life of the city. Tourists are attracted by the developed infrastructure of the hotel, a panoramic view of Mother Volga, the proximity of major attractions.

Among the services provided by the hotel, there is free Wi-Fi, parking, laundry, television, transfer, a cozy restaurant, a yoga center and a medical and cosmetic center.

The hotel offers guests the rooms of the categories "Apartment", "Suite", "Studio", "Junior Suite", single and double standards and one room "Economy". Prices range from 800 to 4,200 rubles per day.





Hotel reviews

First of all, guests like the location of Astoria hotels, all of them are located in the center of Saratov. From any hotel you can easily reach all points of the city both by private car and by public transport.

Judging by the reviews, the hotel complex provides quality services and decent service. Guests note the good work of the staff, the warm welcome, the stable operation of the Internet, quality cleaning, comfortable furniture, clean linens.

According to the guests, the Saratov and Olympia hotels have excellent breakfasts. From the windows of "Olympia" offers a magnificent view of the Volga. Hotel "Saratov" is located a few meters from the park. The Volga, formerly Astoria, is located in the heart of the Saratov Arbat.

Guests do not like poor lighting, insufficient number of outlets in rooms, lack of a kettle in the rooms. Some guests of the Volga Hotel were disappointed with the breakfast.

Conclusion

Summing up, we can say that the Astoria hotel complex (Saratov) receives positive reviews, which means they will make their stay in the city comfortable and enjoyable. The calm atmosphere and quality service will allow guests to relax after a busy day.
