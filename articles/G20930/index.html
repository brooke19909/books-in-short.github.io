Can I use the tablet while charging the battery?

Everyone who has become the proud owner of a tablet sooner or later raises the question of how to properly use the gadget and extend its life.

Very often they ask whether it is possible to use the tablet while charging. Some believe that this will negatively affect the battery of the device, and it will fail more quickly. Is this true or myth?

How to use the battery

  3. At least sometimes let the tablet completely discharge before turning it off, then charge it in the off mode.

How to charge a tablet

The device can be charged both through the network and through a computer by connecting a USB cable. The charge process through the adapter takes several hours, the device connects to a 220 W outlet.

It takes at least half a day to charge the tablet via USB from a PC, and the computer cannot be turned off. The power at the USB port is insufficient. But if you have nowhere to rush, then you can safely use this method.

Experts advise charging the battery after it is completely discharged, especially with a newly-purchased device. The brand new ‚ÄúiPad‚Äù must be charged from a complete shutdown at least three times.

But can I use the tablet while charging the battery? If the gadget has a lithium-ion battery, then it is not recommended to overload it, but it is advisable to charge it in the off mode. A device with a built-in lead-acid battery can safely be used during charging.

Regardless of whether the use of the device while charging is detrimental to the battery or not, the tablet will heat up more, because it is subjected to excessive load. Therefore, the question of whether it is possible to use the tablet while charging from the network, the answer is rather positive than negative, but still do not get involved in games and watching a movie, it is better to wait until the device is fully ready for use.

It happens that the adapter that came with the tablet broke. Then there is a need to buy a new one. How to avoid excessive heating of the battery, if you need to use the tablet when it is charging

Those who just recently became the owner of the tablet often doubt whether it is possible to use the tablet while charging, because the battery can overheat, and then the gadget will fail. Fortunately, if you follow simple rules, then using the tablet while it is connected to a power source will be absolutely safe for the device. Do not open several programs at once. Launched applications slow down the gadget, especially during the charging process. Experts recommend abstaining from games for a while until the tablet is fully charged. Bulky graphic toys require maximum power, which leads to overheating of the battery. When the gadget is charging, do not load it by downloading various information from the network. These actions will also slow down the processor, and the tablet will not only heat up, but will also freeze at the time of recharging. In other cases, use the gadget without restrictions. And to make the tablet charge faster, you can reduce the screen brightness in the settings.

Powerbank tablet charge

Many are interested in the question of whether it is possible to use the tablet while charging from an external battery. This accessory is used to replenish the battery capacity when it is not possible to charge the gadget from the outlet. The capacity of the external battery varies between 7000-10000 mAh. Today, almost everyone has a Power Bank. The external battery is easy to use, all that is needed is a connecting cord. The charge of this unit is enough to fully replenish the energy of the tablet.

Using the gadget while charging from Power Bank is allowed without restrictions. Less current is supplied from the external battery than when charging from the mains, therefore, the gadget will not heat up and hang, but the charge time will increase slightly. To make the tablet charge faster, decrease the brightness of the display and turn off programs that are not in use.

If you completely turn off the gadget, it will make up energy even faster. Now you should have no doubt about whether you can use the tablet when charging the battery from Power Bank.

Some believe that you should not charge the tablet until it is cut down. The ideal option is to periodically recharge the tablet, rather than allowing it to turn off.
