How to knit booties "Dogs" using knitting needles: a detailed description

Today, all that a small child needs is commercially available. But only a thing that is done with your own hands can give love, warmth and care. Every baby needs soft and warming shoes. The first slippers in the life of the baby are booties. Dogs embroidered on these socks look original, elegant and uplifting. The booties fit easily, and since the legs are still small, the work will be quick. This article describes several methods for knitting booties with a puppy's face.





The basic principles of knitting

For those who knit booties for the first time, it is important to follow the technique and the procedure for performing work. In this case, the shoes will correspond to the size of the legs and get elegant and warm. Often, when knitting booties "Doggies" are used five stocking knitting needles with the third number. There are options for creating soft slippers using two knitting needles. In any case, for knitting a small thing, the tool must be thin.

The seams on the baby's toes should be neat and thin. The main parameter is the size, which will allow the baby to move his fingers.

Yarn Requirements

The yarn should warm the leg, as well as the weather and season. In winter, it is wise to use woolen threads, in the demi-season period, booties from mixed yarn of wool and acrylic will be appropriate, in summer you can knit cotton socks.

As a rule, needlewomen use colored, juicy and bright colors of threads to create children's clothing. So the colors of the ‚ÄúDoggie‚Äù booties can be varied. Combination of several shades is allowed.

The accent that distinguishes the puppy can be individual details, for example, nose, eyes, ears. The muzzle can be embroidered with threads or use buttons, beads that will need to be sewn firmly to the booties.





Master class: booties ‚ÄúDoggies‚Äù (base)

Work on the creation of socks begins with knitting the soles. Our example will be described with a set of 35 loops (child‚Äôs foot size - 8 cm). To find out the exact number of loops, you need to measure the length of the baby‚Äôs foot, excluding the fingers, determine the density of knitting (that is, find out how many loops fit in your size) and double them. Knit the first two rows with facial loops. Next, knit all the even rows according to the following pattern: remove the hem and make the front loop, then form a crochet and knit to the middle two loops. Before and after you need to do one double crochet. Then knit the other half with the front ones, also form a crochet in front of the last two loops, knit the front and edge loops. Odd rows knit in the front. Perform work until 51 loops form on the spoke.

Next, divide (visually) the part into three unequal parts using markers. On the sides should be placed on 20 loops, and the middle part will contain 11 loops. The extreme parts must be knit in the front method, and the middle with an elastic band 1 x 1. Knit in this way 6 rows. The beginning of the toe of the booties-dog is formed. Further, in odd rows, the extreme loops of the middle and sides should be knitted together until 31 loops remain on the spoke. Sew another 6 cm with elastic and close the loops. Fold the product in half and sew the open lower and back parts together. Unscrew the top to form a cuff.





Create a face

In this embodiment, the nose and ears for knitted booties ‚ÄúDoggies‚Äù will be created from pompons, i.e. these will be separate elements. To do this, you can use a fork or index and middle fingers. Wrap yarn around your fingers or fork. The more you wrap the thread around the auxiliary tool, the more fluffy the pompon will be. Then tie the entire structure with an additional piece of thread and tighten the strong knot. The workpiece can be removed and cut the loops on the sides. Fluff the strings to form a round fluffy ball. Use scissors to trim the ends of the threads that are knocked out in even rows. Make two more donuts. Sew a nose on the booty toe, attach ears to the sides of the shoe. Two identical buttons located on the lapel of the sock can serve as eyes. Knitted booties "Dogs" are ready. Following the description, create a shoe for the second leg.

Puppy head

This option involves tying the head of the dog as a separate element. Take a yarn matching your booties color and draw 4 loops with thin knitting needles. Even rows should be knitted in the wrong way. In the first front row, double the number of loops. That is, knit one more loop from each. In the third row, repeat the increase. 16 loops formed on the spoke. Knit the product unchanged to the 9th row, in which you need to reduce the number by knitting every two loops together. In row 11, repeat the reduction in the number of loops. Cut the thread, pull the remaining tip through the last 4 loops. Sew open the edges of the product, fill the workpiece with a synthetic winterizer and tighten the thread to cover the hole. To highlight the muzzle, use a needle to pull the thread through all the loops of the 6th row and not pull it very tight. Lock the thread tip and hide.





For ears, put 4 loops on the knitting needles and knit several rows with a scarf. In the last row, cut the loops in half and sew to the head of the dog. In the same way, make the second eye. Embroider the nose and eyes on the muzzle with a dark thread. Attach your head to the front of the sock.
