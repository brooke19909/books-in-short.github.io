Anapa, "Grand Hotel Valentina": photos and reviews of tourists

If you love the sea and the sun, then Anapa will be the ideal place for your summer vacation. "Grand Hotel Valentina" will provide you with comfortable living conditions, as well as a full range of necessary services.

Brief description of the hotel

Thousands of tourists annually host Anapa. "Grand Hotel Valentina" is one of the most favorite places to relax, because this is an institution with a good reputation and high standards of service. Here you can take advantage of the spa, visit the gym, and also take a steam bath in the sauna and much more.

Anapa will spread out in front of you from the windows of your room. "Grand Hotel Valentina" offers its guests luxurious apartments decorated in a simple classic style. Everything here is aimed at creating a cozy atmosphere for guests. This and high-quality furniture, and modern appliances, and a spacious bathroom, and many other amenities.

As for nutrition, the variety of cuisines is famous for establishments of such a city as Anapa. The Grand Hotel Valentina has an elegant restaurant that serves a deluxe breakfast every morning. During the day, you can order dishes not only Russian, but also Mediterranean and Oriental cuisines. If you want to enjoy your favorite drinks, then visit the lobby bar, where relaxing music is always played.





Anapa, "Grand Hotel Valentina": address

The hotel is located on Terskaya Street 103. It is very close to the city beach, as well as the central promenade. The journey to the international airport will take you about 20 minutes. Thus, Anapa, "Grand Hotel Valentina" is characterized by a rather convenient location. Hotel staff will tell you how to get there if you call them upon arrival in the city (+7 (86133) 3-98-28). The landmark for you will be the city administration building, which is located within walking distance from the hotel. Also very close to the water park, archaeological museum and many other tourist infrastructure.

Hotel Rooms

The dream of many people is a summer vacation in Anapa, in the "Grand Hotel Valentina". You can stay in apartments of the following categories:

  • Standard room can be equipped with two single beds or one large. There is also a small seating area, represented by soft chairs and a coffee table. You will also have access to your own balcony, from which you can enjoy city or sea views.
  • The apartments of the studio category are characterized by a vast area, within which there is a bedroom, a work area, and even a small corner for relaxation. These apartments overlook the central part of the city. The view will be especially beautiful in the evening, when the illumination of numerous entertainment venues is turned on.
  • Apartments "diplomat" is an example of style and comfort. In the cozy bedroom you can fully relax after an eventful day. As for the living room, here, in addition to upholstered furniture, there is also a workplace. A separate room is allocated for the dining room.
  • Premium rooms are an example of rational design thought, combining practicality and elegance. 50 sqm apartment m. consist of three rooms - a bedroom, a dining room and a living room. Special attention should be paid to the presence of your own furnished balcony, from which you can enjoy the bewitching sea views.
  • Presidential Suite can satisfy the most demanding guests. The style of the room is designed in the European spirit and immerses guests in the atmosphere of the Middle Ages. This is a great option for a family vacation, because there are as many as 4 rooms - a living room, a dining room, a bedroom and a nursery. The feature of the room can be considered huge panoramic windows that offer a bewitching view of the sea.
