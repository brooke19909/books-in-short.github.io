Holiday Village, Bryansk. Sosnovy Bor: reviews, prices, photos

City dwellers increasingly travel outside the city to rest. Someone with tents, someone more like a separate house, and some choose a camp site. The main thing is that the rest is well organized and brings health benefits.

Do you know what special air pine has? Bryansk attracts tourists with the opportunity to breathe in the aroma of needles. Clean forest air will help relieve stress after a working week and will charge you with energy for a long time.





35 kilometers from the city of Bryansk, in the village. Domashovo, st. Sadovaya, 18, is the recreation center "Sosnovy Bor". From the name you can immediately understand where exactly the complex is located. Sosnovy Bor is a great place to recover from hard work. The air is saturated with coniferous odors (volatile), essential oils, which has a beneficial effect on human health.

The base is waiting for you all year round!

The recreation center (Bryansk) "Sosnovy Bor" is located outside the city, which allowed to preserve the pristine nature of this place. On the territory there is a lake with a sandy beach. In summer, it is pleasant to swim in it, sunbathe, and in winter have fun, riding downhill on a sled.

When the snow lies, the pine forest is incredibly mesmerizing. Bryansk leaves tourists with an unforgettable experience - you can go skiing or just take a walk along the snowy trails.





Places for rest

You can go to the cafe to meet friends. It has three rooms and a varied menu. You can sit in solitude, and if you wish, order a banquet. If you want to change the situation, go to the gazebo. It is designed for a feast in all weather conditions.

People who have already visited this complex say that you can take aromatic baths at any time and relax - the SPA complex is always at your service. There you will be treated with the use of oils, you can relax, inhale the smell of exotic flowers and feel a little happier.

Using Wi-Fi, you can plunge into the world of information and communication. In the conference rooms you can hold a business meeting with business partners - this opportunity provides recreation. Bryansk, whose pine forest is striking in its beauty, will be remembered for a long time.

Residence

You can pre-book a house by phone. To get to the base, however, you will have to do it yourself, but you cannot get lost - just leave the city and get to the village of Domashovo. On the street Sadovaya, 18, you are waiting for fifteen well-maintained houses with a modern and elegant interior. Living in them is very comfortable.



They can be two-, three-, four-room. Everywhere there is autonomous heating. Each house has a spacious veranda, shower, telephone, TV, bathroom, refrigerator. Around well-groomed lawns. Pine forest begins almost immediately at the threshold.

Types of rest

On the sandy beach of the recreation center (Bryansk, Sosnovy Bor) you can play beach volleyball - special nets are installed on the territory. Those who wish can try their hand at basketball. Having rented a bicycle, you can ride not only in the recreation center (Bryansk) "Sosnovy Bor", but also explore the nearby forest. Having departed a little from the complex, you will have the opportunity to collect mushrooms.

To diversify the evening, you can visit the sauna at any time of the year. There are four of them: Finnish, salt, Turkish, herbal. You can enjoy the aromas of coniferous forest, flowering meadow, oak barrels. After spa treatments, it is especially pleasant to swim in the pool, there is a jacuzzi.

If a sedentary lifestyle is a burden to you, you can play on the football field. You can dispel boredom by playing darts. The recreation center "Sosnovy Bor" in Bryansk offers a ride on an ATV or bicycle. For those who like privacy, the complex offers gazebos.





Bar

Upholstered furniture and a cozy atmosphere will help you relax and try the culinary delights of cooks. The recreation center "Sosnovy Bor" is able to satisfy the tastes of all visitors thanks to a varied diet. The menu will allow you to organize a feast for friends and business partners. The bar has satellite TV and karaoke.

Prices

The cost of living in the houses depends on the number of rooms and guests, which the recreation center is ready to accept. Bryansk, pine forest, clean air, comfortable atmosphere - all the conditions make it possible for you to relax at affordable prices.

  • Renting a house will cost from 3,000 to 4,400 rubles.
  • Breakfast in the cafe costs 100 rubles, lunch and dinner - 200 rubles each.
  • The cost of billiards is 200 rubles per hour.
  • Three types of simulators cost 200 rubles per hour.
  • Brazier rental is 150 rubles per hour. Firewood, skewers, grill are given to it.
  • A pool, billiards and a sauna for six people will cost 1200 rubles. For each subsequent - another 150 rubles.
  • You can rent a bicycle, ice skates, an ATV, ice-boats, and children's cars. The price of these items will be indicated by the administrator.
  • For 600 rubles per hour, you can rent a conference room.




Guest reviews

People who have already rested in this complex assure that this is an ideal place for celebrating graduations or student meetings. It is possible to install a summer tent, however, you will have to use mosquito repellents. They can be purchased from the administration.

Another advantage is the recreation center "Sosnovy Bor" in Bryansk. You will not be ashamed to show the photo of your party to your friends and acquaintances - the territory of the base has been ennobled, grass is cut everywhere, and beautiful pine trees. On the lake there is a convenient sandy beach.

Guests assure that this complex is suitable for celebrating anniversaries. The level of service is high, delicious dishes, the proper organization of the event - the administration combined all the components of a great evening. Air conditioners work inside the tent, there are no mosquitoes.

The complex is happy to host families with children. All conditions are created on the basis. Clean and comfortable rooms, each with a kettle, TV, comfortable beds (mattresses), in the bathroom - shower. The food is very tasty, portions are large, the area is safe - it is easy to walk with a stroller. The pool does not smell like bleach.

Bicycles, balls, scooters can be rented at the base. Vacationers note that the staff is always polite and friendly - this is an important "bonus".





To summarize

Here you can comfortably relax from the bustle of the city, forget about all the troubles and problems for a while. The rustle of needles calms and gives peace. SPA center, swimming pool and aroma procedures will help to relax and restore health.

Clean air can be enjoyed with friends or family. For children there is also entertainment, so they will like the recreation center. Bryansk, whose pine forest gives a charge of vivacity, will leave them a good impression. Plunging into the atmosphere of pristine nature is the best way to relax and gain strength for new achievements.
