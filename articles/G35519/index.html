One room "standard"

Inexpensive and very comfortable room, designed for one or a maximum of two tourists. Room size is about 20 m 2 . The room has a bed and bedside table, wardrobe and TV, as well as a minibar. For business trips, an ergonomic work area is provided. A minibar is available upon request. The bathroom is combined with the bath. The interior is quite simple but very cozy, so even businessmen often prefer these rooms. The cost of living for two people without meals is 2100 rubles, and a full board for two costs 5000 rubles.



Two-room "comfort"

This is one of the most popular double rooms that the Chistye Prudy hotel (Sochi) can offer. The room consists of a bedroom and living room. In the bedroom you will find two twin beds, which can easily be turned into one double bed. In addition, the room is equipped with bedside tables and floor lamps, a wardrobe and a TV. The second room is not equipped, it is designed to accommodate additional space on request, for example, a nursery. The cost of rest without food is 2700 rubles. Two-room "luxury"

Tourist trips

The Chistye Prudy complex (Sochi) is located in one of the most beautiful corners of Russia. Here at any time of the year there is something to see and what to do, so if you like movement, and there is a craving for adventure, then you will definitely like it here. On the first day, we recommend a walk through the magnificent park near the hotel, as well as admire the stunning lakes. When it's boring - choose any route and go on a trip.

Local mineral springs are famous throughout the country, so do not be too lazy to visit the Chvizhepsinsky narzan spring, which is located in a village called the Bear Corner. Here everyone can taste the healing water with a unique composition. Having taken care of your health, you can go look at the largest and most beautiful Akhshtyr cave, which is located in the picturesque Dzikhrinsky gorge. Stunning surroundings, including the Dry and Wet Canyons of the Psaho Mountain River, are a topic for a separate route. Here you will find rivers and waterfalls, huge cliffs overgrown with forests, as well as karst caves. Traveling through these gorges, you can see a lot of interesting things. This is a very small part of what excursions the Chistye Prudy hotel (Sochi) offers you. The reviews confirm that if you are an active and inquisitive person, then you will not have a single free minute, because there is something to look at. You can visit the art Glass studio and watch the work of glass blowers and be sure to bring a small souvenir with you home.
