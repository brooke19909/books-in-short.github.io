Iberostar Mehari 4 * (Tunisia, Djerba): hotel description, services, reviews

Tunisia is interesting to many people today. One with its sights, the other with the sea. In any case, tens of thousands of tourists come here every year. Everyone chooses a hotel to their taste, and many stay at Iberostar Mehari 4 *. I would like to tell about this hotel.





general information

Iberostar Mehari 4 * is located on the island of Djerba, located in the Bay of Gabes, in close proximity to the beach. The village is called Tagermess. It is relatively close to the large city of Houmt Souk. It takes about 20 minutes to get to the airport. By the way, in front of the hotel there is a bus stop, so tourists do not need to spend money on a taxi to get to any place.

Attractions are not very many, but still they are available. In three minutes you can reach the crocodile farm, the Djerba Explorer park and the Lall Hadriah Ethnographic Museum. And next to the hotel there are supermarkets, shops and souvenir stalls. In general, the tourist infrastructure here is well developed, which plays into the hands of many tourists.

About Services

In Iberostar Mehari 4 * there is everything that should be in any good hotel. Free high-speed Wi-Fi, private parking, car rental, gift shop, currency exchange, as well as a 24/7 reception and a beauty salon with hairdresser. It also provides childcare services. On the territory there are still several shops, laundry with dry cleaning, doctor's office, ATM, computer Internet corner. The conference room Iberostar Mehari 4 * also has. There are three of them, to be more precise. The largest accommodates 150 people. All rooms are equipped with modern technology and are fully adapted for conferences, banquets, receptions, etc.

The staff here is multilingual. The people working at the hotel speak not only Arabic, but also English, German and French. As tourists who were here say, the staff does not speak Russian, but it tries.





Entertainment

The hotel has not only those services that will help make the guest's stay comfortable. There is also everything you need to diversify your leisure.

There are several pools (one of which is indoor), an excellent gym with exercise equipment, and conditions for playing basketball and volleyball. There is bicycle rental, a golf club, a nightclub and a jacuzzi. Every day, guests are entertained by animators, and in the evening they hold grandiose performances. Of particular interest are folklore shows with dancing and musical performances.

The complex has all the conditions for aerobics, table tennis, darts, archery and water polo. But that is not all. There are also two tennis courts with hard surface and five with rubberized. And nearby the hotel is a huge golf course with 27 holes, a water sports center and a horse riding club.

Nutrition

In Iberostar Mehari 4 * (Tunisia), there are several excellent establishments in which guests can satisfy their hunger. One themed restaurant. There, guests are offered excellent Tunisian cuisine, prepared according to traditional proven recipes. The restaurant is open from 19:00 to 22:00.





The second institution is open daily. Guests are served buffet style. And they can enjoy refreshments not only Tunisian, but also international cuisine. For breakfast, the restaurant is open from 7:00 to 10:00, for lunch from 12:30 to 14:30, and for dinner from 18:30 to 21:30.

Also on the territory of Iberostar Mehari 4 * (Djerba) there are several bars. The Melita lobby opens at 10:00 and serves its customers until midnight. The beach bar works in exactly the same mode. But the institution near the pool closes at 18:00.

In general, as the guests here said, the quality of food and drinks is decent. Even the coffee beans from the machine are delicious.
