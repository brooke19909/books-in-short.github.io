Ostapovsky passage: history, transport, prospects

Ostapovsky passage is a passage located on a site belonging to the South-Eastern administrative district of the capital of the Russian Federation. To be more precise, it is located in areas such as Tekstilshchiki and Nizhny Novgorod.

History: General Information

Ostapovsky passage got its name thanks to the Ostapovsky highway, which was formed by an official decree in 1920. Thirty years later, most of the latter became part of today's Volgograd Avenue. At the same time, the government of such a city as Moscow made Ostapovsky passage an integral part of the Ostapovsky highway. Moreover, according to archival documents, the name of this highway was given by the name of one of the workers of the Dynamo electric machine-building plant. According to current information, this man was killed by one of the officers in February 1917 during a demonstration on the corner of Vorontsovskaya and Taganskaya streets.

Location

The Ostapovsky passage starts from Volgogradsky Prospekt, and then goes in the direction of the southeast. After several kilometers, it passes directly above the site of one of the ring highways of the capital - the Third Transport Ring. The journey near the railroad tracks of the Kursk direction of the Moscow Railway ends.

Road junction





As for public transport, which can be found on this highway, it is primarily the metro, buses and trolleybuses. The latter are represented by numbers 27 and 38. If we talk about buses, the most common during peak hours is the bus at number 74. It goes along the route: "Metro station" Taganskaya "- Saratovskaya street." At the intersection of Volgogradsky passage and Ostapovsky passage, you can also find buses under numbers 74 and 161. On weekdays, public transport number 99 and number 633 go. On weekends, an additional bus number 99 runs. As for the metro, Ostapovsky passage is a highway on which the Tekstilshchiki and Volgogradsky Prospekt metro stations are located. The latter is located about eight hundred meters northwest of the intersection with the street of the same name. The first is located two and a half kilometers to the southeast. Countdown must also be made from the intersection with Volgogradsky Prospekt.

Key Development Prospects





Relatively recently, at a Government meeting, the project for the construction of such an engineering structure as a flyover at Ostapovsky passage was officially approved. It will pass through the railway belonging to the Kursk direction of the Moscow Railway (Russia). Moscow (Ostapovsky passage is one of the busiest) will thus be able to significantly relieve the stretch of road between Lublin Street and Volgogradsky Prospekt. The competent implementation of this project will make it possible to significantly improve the movement of cars throughout the South-Eastern District of the capital of the Russian Federation. At the same time, it should be noted that a separate place in the project was given to comments on the preservation of green spaces and the installation of special soundproof windows in residential buildings located on this stretch of road. In general, the construction of this flyover on Ostapovsky passage is part of a large-scale reconstruction project of the entire Volgograd Avenue. All necessary changes are planned for the period from 2013 to 2015. During this time, a tunnel will be laid under Lublin Street and a bridge structure will be erected at the intersection of Volzhsky Boulevard and Volgogradsky Prospekt.
