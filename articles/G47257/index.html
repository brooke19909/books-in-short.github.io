Safety regulations

Before using the Hyer HW60-BP12758 washing machine, you should:

  • make sure that the transport bolts are removed;
  • connect the unit to a separate outlet with grounding;
  • carefully inspect the plug and cord for damage;
  • disconnect the power cord only by the plug;
  • install the washing machine away from heat appliances, protect from direct sunlight;
  • watch that the power cord does not bend, does not fall under appliances and furniture;
  • carefully inspect all hoses and fittings for leaks.

During operation, the machine does not need to be touched with wet hands. Children and persons with disabilities should not be allowed to play with the packaging material. The machine is not installed in places of high humidity and in the area where water can leak. For example, it is strongly discouraged to install the appliance under the sink. If a leak occurs, you need to disconnect the device from the network and wait for the natural drying of the device. The unit is not installed on soft surfaces, carpets, near walls and furniture.

During the workflow, the following is prohibited:

  • touch the door of the car; it may be excessively hot;
  • put various objects on the machine;
  • to wash products from foam rubber and rubber;
  • during washing, load the powder into the washing hole;
  • open the hatch door if the tank is half full with water;
  • to cover the household appliance with a plastic bag, so the machine will not be able to dry completely, which can cause corrosion.

If the washing machine is used carefully, in accordance with the instructions for use, it will last a long time and without breakdowns.

Before first use

HW60-BP12758 washing machines are equipped with a bottom cover that must be installed when installing the appliance. To install it, the unit is laid to one side and all legs are wrapped. Install the cover. Screw all the legs through the cover. Raise the household appliance and put it in place.

Before operating the washing machine, the transportation bolts are dismantled. They fix the tank from the inside while moving the device. To remove the bolts they need to be unscrewed. Get plastic stabilizers and rubber seals (they are inside the machine). Close the formed holes with plugs.

Before the first wash, adjust the position of the legs. Align the device with alcohol level. To do this, the foot nut is loosened with a wrench. By turning the legs adjust the height of the device. As soon as the leg is in the desired position, the support nut is tightened.

Before the first use, hoses are connected to the machine. The filler is connected on one side to a special filling valve, and on the other - to a cold water tap. The drain hose is fixed with a special bracket supplied. It will help prevent water from escaping. This hose is attached to a clip located on the back of the washing machine. It must be fixed in such a way that the distance from the floor is 80-100 cm.

Haier washing machine HW60 12758: instructions for use

This appliance is intended for domestic use only. It is washed in several stages:

  1. The machine is connected to the electrical network.
  2. Open the tap with water and make sure that there are no leaks.
  3. Sort and prepare linen.
  4. Things are loaded into the car, and the hatch door is tightly closed until it clicks.
  5. Detergents are loaded into a special container.
  6. Choose a washing program.
  7. After washing, disconnect the appliance from the power supply and stop the water supply.
  8. The hatch door is opened and laundry is taken out.

The washing machine (Haier HW60 1211N, HW60-BP12758 or any other model) should start without any laundry during the first wash. This will remove residual oil and stagnant water from the appliance that may remain after factory testing.

Washing machine care

Caring for this washing machine, according to users, is usually straightforward. After each wash, turn off the water supply and disconnect the appliance from the network. The appliance door is left open so that all moisture dries. If the unit is not used for a long time, unscrew the drain hose and remove all water from it.

Each time after washing, clean the detergent tray. To do this, it is removed from the machine and rinsed under running water. Wipe dry and set back.

The machine body and its parts are cleaned with a soft cloth using liquid soap. For care it is forbidden to use aggressive chemicals.

Once a month, strictly according to the instructions, the filter, filling valve and filling filter are cleaned.
