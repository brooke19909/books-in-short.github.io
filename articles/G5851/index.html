Chiraz Club 3 * (Tunisia / Monastir) - photos, prices and reviews of tourists

One of the most popular resorts in both Tunisia and throughout Africa is Monastir. In the past, in its place was a Roman settlement. However, hundreds of years later, it became one of the most attractive places for tourists on the whole continent. People from different countries come here in search of an inexpensive vacation on the magnificent beaches, combined with the opportunity to visit a variety of attractions. In addition, Monastir has a wide hotel base and well-developed tourist infrastructure. So everyone here will find accommodation and entertainment to their taste and budget. If you want to spend a vacation in Tunisia and are looking for an inexpensive, but comfortable hotel on the beach, then pay attention to the Chiraz Club 3 * (Monastir). Today we offer you to get to know this hotel more closely, and also to find out what impressions of our compatriots stayed in it.





Where is

If you have a map in front of you, Monastir (Tunisia) you can easily find on the coast, 150 kilometers from the capital of this state. As for the Chiraz Club hotel, it is located between the cities of Monastir and Sousse. The distance to the first of them is 17, and to the second - 13 kilometers. The resort area in which the hotel is located is called Skanes. Not far from the hotel there are several interesting sights of Tunisia: the Muslim city of Sidi Bou Said, the ancient Medina, as well as Safari Park. A big plus of the Chiraz Club 3 * hotel is its location directly on the seashore. So an excellent fine sandy beach with a gentle entry into the water is literally a hundred meters here. It belongs to the hotel, so the use of sun loungers and parasols is free.



How to get there

Most tourists from Russia arrive in Monastir through the local air harbor named after Habib Bourguiba. It is located just seven kilometers from the Chiraz Club Hotel. Since the opening of the international airport in Enfida, the number of flights to the local air harbor has decreased significantly. However, many charters from Russia still fly here. If you plan to get to Tunisia through other airports - Enfide or the capital Tunis-Carthage - then from there you can get to Monastir either by intercity bus or train, or again by air. So, the flight will take you about one hour. Experienced tourists are sure to recommend on approaching the resort to pay attention to the opening landscape. You will see colorful ponds serving to evaporate salt, and in winter you can also see picturesque flocks of pink flamingos.

Posting Rules

As in many hotels in different countries of the world, in accordance with the rules of the hotel, registration of arriving guests is carried out here starting from two in the afternoon. But in the event that you arrive at the Chiraz Club earlier, the administrators will do everything possible to populate you as soon as possible. If there are no free rooms, you will be offered to leave your luggage in the storage room and go to the restaurant, take a swim in the pool, visit the beach or take a walk around the territory. As soon as your room is ready to move in, you can enter it. As for the date of departure, you should vacate the occupied room in Chiraz Club 3 * before noon. At the same time, you will be asked to hand over the keys and pay for the entire stay and use of additional services. You can pay both cash and plastic cards "Mastercard" or "Visa". If you have previously paid for the hotel accommodation to your tour operator, then you will only have to pay for additional services, if any.

As for children, the Chiraz Club hotel allows guests with children of any age to stay. Moreover, if you place a baby up to two years old in your room on existing sleeping places, then nothing will be required to pay extra. For living in your room an older child or an adult on an extra bed will have to pay 50% of the cost of accommodation per person per night. A maximum of one extra bed is allowed in each room. If you need such a service, please contact the hotel in advance and notify it.

As for tourists traveling with pets, the rules of the Chiraz Club 3 * (Monastir) do not allow their accommodation on the territory of the hotel. Therefore, you must either leave your pet at home during your vacation in Tunisia, or look for another hotel, which is more loyal to guests with four-legged pets.





Number of rooms

As mentioned above, the Chiraz Club Hotel (Tunisia) has 334 comfortable rooms. Some rooms offer beautiful sea views. Other rooms overlook the garden and grounds. The housing stock here is represented by standard single, double and triple rooms with the possibility of installing additional beds. Each hotel room is equipped with a private bathroom with bath or shower and toiletries, air conditioning, TV, telephone, mini fridge. There is a balcony. Rooms are cleaned, and bed linen and towels are changed regularly.

Nutrition

Meals in the hotel in question are organized on an all-inclusive basis. This means that throughout the day, guests of the Chiraz Club 3 * have the opportunity to enjoy a variety of dishes (both national and international cuisine), snacks, desserts, as well as local drinks.





Sea and beach

The three-star Chiraz Club Hotel is located on the very shore of the sea, literally a hundred meters from its own sandy beach. There are sun loungers and parasols, as well as equipment for entertainment on the water.

Entertainment

For those who want to combine relaxation on the sun terrace with a bath in the salt water, the hotel has a large outdoor pool with sea water. In addition, the hotel has an indoor pool, as well as a pool designed for the smallest guests.

Lovers of outdoor activities will also find something to do here. So, at their services on site there is a golf course, tennis courts, ping-pong tables, football, basketball and volleyball courts. Also, aerobics classes are held here and there is the opportunity to play billiards, darts or giant chess.

If you want to take care of your beauty and health, then be sure to visit the local spa. It has a sauna, hammam, jacuzzi, massage room, as well as a beauty salon.

Throughout the day, a team of animators works on site. Its representatives organize various contests and entertainment events. Moreover, their attention will be covered by both children and adults. Infrastructure

Hotel Chiraz Club 3 * has all the necessary infrastructure for a comfortable stay and accommodation for tourists. So, the reception is open around the clock. Turning to the reception, you can leave valuables in storage in a safe, call a taxi, exchange currency. In addition, the hotel has a hairdresser, shop, laundry, dry cleaning, car rental and tour desk.

Cost of living

We suggest that you familiarize yourself with the price of accommodation in the rooms of a three-star hotel in Tunis under the name Chiraz Club. So, a seven-day accommodation (per person) in this hotel will cost you an average of 9.9 thousand rubles (for a single room), from 11.9 thousand rubles (for a double room) and from 13 thousand rubles (for a triple room) ) The cost of rooms with sea views will be slightly higher. These amounts include all-inclusive meals, VAT and city tax.
