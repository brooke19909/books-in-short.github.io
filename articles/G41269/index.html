Holidays in the Philippines - Idyll for Romantic Natures

about the country

The Philippines is a state located on the many (more than seven thousand) Pacific Islands in Southeast Asia. The Philippine Islands are part of the Malay Archipelago.

In the south, the Philippines are washed by the Sulawesi Sea, in the north by the Bashi Strait, in the east by the Philippine Sea, in the west by the South China Sea. There are many mountains, most of which are of volcanic origin. About half of the territory is covered with rainforests with palm trees, bamboo and orchids. The animal kingdom of the country is very diverse. And in coastal waters there are a huge number of fish and shellfish that can form pearls.

The climate on the islands is tropical, turning in the equatorial in the south. The average temperature on the coast is about 24-28 ¬∞ C, in the mountainous areas a little cooler. Almost all the islands have systems of picturesque caves, many of which are still not explored and leave tourists with the opportunity to become pioneers. Holidays in the Philippines are ideal for lovers of warmth, beach idyll and adventure.



Tours to the Philippines

Holidays in the Philippines is constantly in demand by tourists. Travelers are called here white sand, vibrant nature and amazing local culture. Every year, a huge number of visitors rest on the cores.

Last-minute tours to the Philippines are especially in demand in the season. After all, there are such shopping opportunities that I would like to save on a flight in order to enjoy the opening wonders of shops and bazaars.

Here you need to go to those who love nightlife. Many islands offer round-the-clock discos, clubs, and eternal music at any time of the year. All this you can see and enjoy first-hand, gaining last-minute packages to the Philippines.

Of course, the most popular is a beach holiday. After all, it is here that the best beaches in the world. 30 thousand kilometers of coastline are covered with white sand. The water temperature does not drop below 25 degrees.

Holidays in the Philippines can be combined with a stay not only in the areas of tourist paradise, but also visit completely uninhabited islands.

The islands have a unique opportunity to spend time incredibly active, joining the hiking in the mountainous areas. The terrain is suitable for real mountaineering. You can hike to Mount Natib, Peak de Loro and Peak Taal. The highest point is the peak of Apo. Climb to the top with reliable escort.

On the islands you can swim underwater. Without this, a vacation in the Philippines would not be complete. Equipment from the simplest to the professional can be rented at the hotel. Here diving is regulated professionally. When ordering a trip under water, you can be sure that the centers offering them are licensed to do so.

The most interesting places are in the areas of Anilao and Batangas. It is also worth visiting the islands of Malajimbombank and Marikaban. Under water, you will see coral reefs, the richest world of fish, turtles, barakkud, starfish and even real shrines and cemeteries of ships.

Local shops and markets are able to stun a tourist with a richness of bright colors, aromas of national cuisine and the sounds of trade. You can buy almost everything here: fabrics, cane furniture, clothes, cutlery and much more.

The most favorable period for trips to the Philippines is the time from December to the end of April. The conditions for recreation here are ideal, since the country lives off tourism. Holidays in the Philippines are absolutely comfortable, laid-back and varied.

At the main resorts you will be met by fantastic hotels with the highest level of service, chic restaurants, bars and a variety of entertainment clubs. If you prefer a quiet vacation, you can choose the islets of Mactan, Badian or Manila.

In the resorts for tourists, opportunities for relaxation in SPA salons are opened. Good fun is watching dolphins and whales, helicopter tours and many more options offered by hotel tour operators.
