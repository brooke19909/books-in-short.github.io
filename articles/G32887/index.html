Porto Cervo Resort (Sardinia, Italy): overview, features, attractions and reviews

Porto Cervo is one of the most interesting cities in the north of the Italian island of Sardinia. This resort is not for backpackers and budget travelers. Here, celebrities and people are richer. It was created, as if in a fairy tale, at the request and direction of a real prince. This resort has become a household name for luxury vacation for VIP-persons. Many of them built palaces and villas here. Although travelers with average incomes, his visit can also be affordable. Photos of this resort adorn the glossy covers of glamorous magazines as a symbol of "star relaxation." Now we will find out why.

Location and how to get

So, Sardinia ... Porto Cervo is located in the north-east of the island. This area is traditionally known as Gallura. It is part of the Olbia-Tempio region. Porto Cervo resort is located on the beaches of the Costa Smeralda (Emerald Coast). If you rented a car at the airport of Olbia - the closest to the resort - then you need to move along the SS 125 highway. It is better to get off it following the sign for Costa Smeralda or the city of Artsacena. After 8 kilometers, you turn right onto the local highway number 73. It leads to another resort town, Porto Rotondo. And you will have to go to our resort on highway 59. Resort History

Porto Cervo (Sardinia) is a fairly young resort. He made journalists around the world talk about himself when, in the 60s of the 20th century, Prince Karim Aga Khan the Fourth acquired these lands. He lived in Switzerland, was related to the prophet Mohammed himself and was the imam of the Shiite Ismaili community. He really liked the picturesque landscapes around, although he traveled around the globe. He decided to build an elite resort on this place, which would fit into the local nature. For this, the prince, who did not know the money bill, hired the best architects of his time. Among them was Luigi Vietti. It was these architects who designed and built the current city of dreams. The prince personally ensured that neither the ecology nor the traditional style of the island's buildings were disturbed by the creation of a new resort. It was built around the port, located in a deep natural bay, in the shape of a deer ("worm"), hence the name. Since that time, the crowned persons of all countries, as well as presidents, celebrities, millionaires, movie and show business stars - in a word, all people with money rushed here.

Resort Porto Cervo

First of all, it is famous for its magnificent harbor. It can be seen already when you approach the city. It usually jokes about seven hundred ships, and the resort's yacht club is one of the most exclusive in all of Italy. Here the most expensive yachts belonging to different celebrities are moored. And when you think about what to see in Porto Cervo (Sardinia), first of all take a walk along the promenade and admire the snow-white ships. Surround the harbor of rare beauty hills. And the city itself is a great example of modern Italian architecture. Not only Luigi Vietti, but also Jacques Cuell and Michele Busiri Vichi put their souls into it. The result is an amazing mixture of traditions and new trends. Since then, other resorts in Sardinia have begun to practice this type of architectural style. In the most beautiful palace of the resort was the residence of the prince himself. And from the central square, Piazzetta, the most fantastic view opens up on the Costa Smeralda. It is located on a hill in relation to the port, and the audience often gather here in the evenings. The square and narrow streets with bright balconies are the heart of the city. And villas, hotels and night clubs are located on the coast.





Porto Cervo Sardinia Attractions

The pearl of the resort, of course, is the dazzlingly white, strange sloping church of Stella Maris (Star of the Seas). It was designed by architect Michele Busiri Vichi, who tried to embody the style of the famous Catalan Gaudi on Sardinian material. The local golf club is a creation of the genius of modern design Robert Trent Jones, who turned the fields for the game into a work of panoramic art. If you want to explore the surroundings, be sure to visit Cape Capo Ferro, where the lighthouse is located. It is a rock of 46 meters high, cliffing steeply into the sea. You can still go to the town of Santa Teresa Gallura itself, the administrative district of which, in fact, is the resort of Porto Cervo (Sardinia). Here you will find beautiful bays, Capa Testa and Marmorata, as well as cozy coastal restaurants. And you can go on a yacht to the neighboring Maddalena archipelago. For its natural attractions, it is declared a National Park. This ridge of islands is located in the Strait of Bonifaccio, between Corsica and Sardinia. On one of them - Caprere - Garibaldi lived and died, and now there is his house-museum. From it to the island of La Maddalena stretches a six-meter dam. The archipelago is famous for the rare seabirds nesting there. And the island of Santo Stefano has a reputation for being the most beautiful in the Mediterranean because of its pink beach. Well, for comparison, look at the neighboring resort villages - Bahia Sardinia and Puerto Cannigione.

Beaches

Since, as we have already said, Porto Cervo is a fashionable resort in the north of Sardinia, rich people come here to breathe in the sea air and take sun baths. The coast of the Costa Smeralda itself is good enough for a vacation of this kind. But we will tell you about the best beaches of the resort. The first of these, Cala Granu, is located near the harbor of the city. It is a bay with white sand, a gentle entrance and a shallow bottom, protected from all winds. The beach is literally a five-minute walk from the resort center, so families with children love it. It's not far to go here, and swimming is completely safe. In general, full relaxation.

The second most popular beach, Pevero, is three kilometers from the city. The depth of the sea there is also shallow, but it was chosen by celebrities, as well as paparazzi who are looking for a convenient moment to spy on the personal lives of the stars.

La Chelvia Beach is also good, but it is covered with coarse sand. Since it is surrounded by very beautiful cliffs of pink granite, nature lovers and divers come here. But no matter which beach you choose to relax, everywhere you will see a unique combination of wild pristine beauty and glamorous gloss.
