Rethymno Village Hotel 3 * (Greece / Crete / Platanias): description and reviews

Greece is a fairly popular destination among Russian tourists. Throughout the year, tens of thousands of tourists come here to enjoy the local flavor and amazing nature.

Most travelers choose Crete as their destination. There are many hotels that deserve attention, but now we will focus on only one of them, and this is Rethymno Village Hotel 3 *.

Location

This hotel complex is located in Rethymnon, considered one of the most beautiful cities on the island. Only 100 meters separates it from the long sandy beach of Platanes, stretching over 13 kilometers.

This is a quiet, secluded place - the nearest airport (Suda) is 40 kilometers away, while another, located in Heraklion, is about 58 km away.

However, attractions are still there. Be sure to take a walk to the Venetian port, the archaeological museum, the center of Byzantine art, the fortress of Fortezza, as well as the city park.

Nutrition

Every traveler should know that there is a restaurant in the hotel complex. The institution works according to the following schedule:

  • 7: 30-9: 30: breakfast.
  • 12: 30-14: 00: lunch.
  • 19: 00-21: 00: dinner.

Service at the restaurant Rethymno Village Hotel is carried out according to the buffet system. In addition to the main meals, snacks are organized in the bar. You can come there from 11:00 to 12:00, as well as from 14:30 to 15:30. And in the late afternoon, from 16:30 to 17:30, a tea party is held.

In the period from 12:30 to 21:00 everyone can enjoy an ice cream in the bar.

You should be aware that in Rethymno Village Hotel 3 * from 10:00 to 23:00 served strong and soft drinks. All guests can try homemade wine, draft beer, sprite, cola, lemonade, soda, tea, filtered coffee. In the evening, from 19:00 to 23:00, guests are offered brandy, crayfish and ouzo. After this time, drinks are also available, but at an additional cost.

By the way, for guests arriving late in the evening, the hotel provides cold snacks delivered directly to your room.
