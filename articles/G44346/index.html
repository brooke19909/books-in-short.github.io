The resort village of Arkhipovka-Osipovka: rest

Not far from the famous Russian resort of Gelendzhik, a rather popular resort village Arkhipovka-Osipovka has spread. The Black Sea, rocky mountains and calm atmosphere are increasingly attracting tourists from various regions of the country to this place. Previously, this village was known as a place of fortification for military purposes. And now tourists are invited to improve their health and relax at the resort Arkhipovka-Osipovka. Rest in this place always brings both pleasure and new strength.



History of the formation of the resort

Once upon a time in the place of a modern resort there were ancient settlements. But so far only structures from slabs that were built during the Bronze Age have survived.





The famous event, in connection with which the village was named, had a tragic end. An ordinary soldier Arkhip Osipov blew up a warehouse with military supplies with him in order to preserve the fortification erected in 1837. Thanks to this feat, the enemy was defeated.





In order to honor the memory of this hero, a white cross was erected at the site of the warehouse. Previously, this memorial place could be seen from the sea, but now, given the rapid growth of hotel buildings, this cross is distinguishable only from certain angles of the tributaries. This historical place can be reached by two stairs, on the side of which there are guns from the time of the Caucasian war. But also in order for this event to remain in the memory of both residents of the village and tourists, a reconstruction of the fortification was carried out.

Holiday at the resort

Arkhipovka-Osipovka resort is a vacation for couples as well as for tourists who travel with a cheerful company, and in general even one traveler will not be bored here. People with low incomes can spend their holidays here, as well as those who can afford a luxurious and relaxing holiday in expensive hotels, where there is also a swimming pool, an elite bar, gyms, etc. Usually people with an average income seek housing in the private sector . As many tourists and travel agencies advise, so that the rest is pleasant and does not cause problems, housing must be taken care of in advance. Indeed, in the resort Arkhipovka-Osipovka, the private sector is very often loaded.

Travel planning should begin with a reservation. Indeed, the popular village of Arkhipovka-Osipovka offers relaxation for every taste, and in the high season it will be difficult to find suitable housing. Therefore, you need to take care of this before the trip. Find hotels, addresses in the private sector, and if you want a change, find out where the wild beaches are.

Rest in hotels and the private sector

Many hotels are located in very attractive places where everyone can rent a room. Most of the ethical hotel complexes are located on the seashore. But, despite this, many owners of the complexes offer to use the pool. It is very convenient, for example, in the morning when you want to refresh yourself.

Internet and parking, cable TV can be found not only in every hotel, but also in the private sector. Of course, the service in hotels is much higher, so not everyone can afford to rent a room in them. But the private sector has already begun to raise its level, and many self-sufficient individuals prefer to rent rooms in such places, since there you can plunge into the home atmosphere.

Climate at the resort

Very warm climate at a resort such as Arkhipovka-Osipovka. The weather here corresponds to the usual Mediterranean climate. Mostly sunny weather prevails, so the holiday season begins much earlier than in other areas. The average summer temperature exceeds thirty degrees. You can start swimming in the sea from the last month of spring, and the closing of the season most often occurs in October. The water in the sea is very clean - this is due to the fact that two rivers flow into the bay, due to this there is water exchange.





This resort is a great place for families. Vivid impressions and good emotions after such a vacation will remain for a long time. But it is worth remembering that it is necessary to prepare for a trip to any resort, including a trip to such a village as Arkhipovka-Osipovka. A card that must be bought after arriving at the resort will tell you about all the attractions and help you find the place where the room is reserved.
