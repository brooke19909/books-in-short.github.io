Samui Koh Samui Resort (Thailand, Samui): description with photo, reviews of tourists

For several years, the island of Samui is considered one of the most popular places for exotic holidays among Russian tourists. There are many hotels offering a decent vacation for everyone, one of which is Samui Koh Samui Resort. Let us consider in more detail the main features of living in this place, as well as some reviews of vacationers. Also, we will take a brief look at several other hotels of the same chain that are located on Koh Samui - Manathai, Mercure, Renaissanse, as well as Malibu.

Total information

Samui Koh Hotel Samui Resort is a great place to relax, which attracts tourists with the opportunity of a budget holiday in decent conditions, surrounded by the exotic atmosphere of Koh Samui.

This hotel was built in 2005. Currently, its buildings have a modern look, which is ensured by regular reconstruction work.

The main principle of the hotel is to provide the most comfortable conditions for the people living in it. This place to relax is often chosen by couples in love who want to retire and enjoy the detachment from worldly fuss.

Speaking about the rating of the place in question, it should be noted that the Koh Samui Resort hotel, according to guests' reviews, has a rating of 7.5 out of 10 available, which is a pretty decent indicator. In accordance with international standards of service, the hotel has a status of 3 *.

Location

The hotel in question is located in the popular resort - Koh Samui (Thailand). This place is famous for its amazing conditions for recreation, as well as the fact that in its district there are a lot of attractions interesting for tourists.

The hotel is within walking distance from the sea, on the first coastline, which, according to tourists, is its big plus. 10 km from it is a place of concentration of interesting entertainments - the city center can be easily reached by a car rented at the hotel or by taxi.

Rooms

The room fund of Samui Koh Samui Resort is small - it consists of 20 apartments. According to vacationers, each room presented in the hotel fund is decorated in an original style using modern and very comfortable furniture.

All rooms presented in the hotel fund are divided into several groups, depending on the degree of comfort: Superior, Deluxe, Superior Hot Deal and Deluxe Hot Deal. Each of them has not only furniture, but also the necessary technical equipment needed for a comfortable stay (air conditioning, TV, safe, minibar, telephone). The decoration of the rooms has a large amount of textile, which gives some airiness to the overall picture of the interior. Hotel guests have the opportunity to use Wi-Fi access. For a fee, guests can order room service (every day from 10:00 to 22:30).

As for the furniture equipment, it is designed to accommodate a pair of adults and one child. Each room has a large king-size bed, which in some apartments is replaced by a pair of single beds. In addition, the room has a sitting corner (sofa and coffee table) and a place designed to store guests' clothes (wardrobe). There are small cupboards next to the bed in the apartment. Deluxe, Superior Hot Deal and Deluxe Hot Deal apartments have workstations in the form of a desk and an armchair.

The rooms are equipped with private bathrooms, which have modern plumbing, as well as bath accessories, bathrobes, slippers and towels, the number of which is calculated on the number of guests.

All types of rooms have a private outdoor terrace, where vacationers prefer to drink morning coffee while admiring the surrounding beauties.

Conditions for children

Often, guests of Samui Koh Samui Resort (pictured) become tourists who rest with the whole family, including with children. In order to ensure the maximum level of comfort for this type of guests, the hotel offers them free cots in the apartments. Moreover, at any time, parents can use the paid nanny services, which are billed on an hourly basis.

SPA and sport

In reviews of the hotel in question, one can often find comments addressed to the services provided at the local spa center. Here, according to vacationers, wonderful massage therapists work, capable of doing several types of massage. Moreover, in this complex you can visit cosmetic procedures, which will also deliver a lot of positive impressions. The spa also has a jacuzzi. All services provided in this complex are paid.

Even while on vacation, sports fans have the opportunity to maintain their figure in the norm. For this purpose, a small gym has been created here, which everyone can visit on a paid basis.

Nutrition

In the comments left about the Samui Koh Samui Resort, it is often mentioned that excellent food is offered here, designed for fans of different directions in the kitchen. A small restaurant, Kohsamuiresort, operates on a regular basis within the hotel. The menu of this establishment consists of dishes of Asian, European and Oriental cuisine, which will satisfy even the most demanding gourmets. As part of the accommodation program, hotel guests have the opportunity to attend the buffet, served here from 07:00 to 10:00 for free.

In the comments left by tourists living in the hotel in question, there are sometimes reviews regarding another catering establishment - the Mimosa restaurant, which is located next to Koh Samui. Here, they said, unusual seafood dishes are served, which you should definitely try if you come to Samui.

Additional features

Hotel Samui Koh Samui Resort provides its guests with the most comfortable living conditions. Some services are provided here and there are additional features that everyone can use if necessary.

The building of the main building of the hotel in question has an excellent conference room, designed for business meetings, presentations and other business events. Moreover, ceremonies and celebrations are often held here.

If you wish, vacationers can rent a car at the hotel - using this vehicle you can go to the surroundings to explore the nearest attractions. By the way, on the territory of the hotel there is free parking intended for the transport of guests.

The building of the hotel in question has a small beauty salon, dry cleaning and laundry services, as well as an Internet corner where guests can use Wi-Fi and computer equipment for various purposes.

The hotel accepts both cash and some international credit cards. On the ground floor of its building there is a currency exchange, as well as an ATM.

Prices

Many Russian tourists are interested in how much it costs to relax in the hotel in question. So, practice shows that the average cost of a tour to this place is about 65,000 rubles. As a rule, it includes a weekly stay for two in standard apartments, meals, as well as a round-trip flight. If desired, tourists can include a transfer in the tour price - this service is provided in a comfortable car equipped with air conditioning.

Manathai

When considering a list of hotels in the Koh Samui chain, you should definitely pay attention to the Manathai Resort Koh Samui - a place that guarantees an excellent pastime. The average cost of a tour to the specified hotel is about 150,000 rubles, but, according to most tourists, this price is quite justified.

The reviews about Manathai Resort Koh Samui 4 * say that this hotel has a rather favorable location. It is surrounded by the most interesting sights of the region, on the first coastline. Samui Airport is a 20-minute drive from the hotel in question, which is also very good - having arrived on the island, tourists do not get tired during a long trip.

The Manathai Resort Koh Samui has three modern buildings, in which the number of rooms consists of 133 apartments. All of them offer comfortable conditions for a pleasant pastime.

The hotel Manathai Resort Koh Samui 4 * has a well-developed infrastructure: there are shops, ATMs, a currency exchange, a tour desk, dry cleaning and even a medical center. Hotel guests can eat at the local restaurant, and on hot days visit the open-air aquazone. If desired, guests of the place to relax can visit the gym.

Manathai Resort Hotel is ideal for families, as its territory has all the conditions necessary for a safe and fun pastime for children. Upon entering the hotel room with a child, a special bed is provided. If necessary, parents have the opportunity to leave their baby with a nanny or in a kids club - such services are provided for a fee.

Mercure

Hotel Mercure Koh Samui Beach & Resort offers its guests a quite decent vacation at the 4 * level. When planning your trip to this vacation spot, you should be prepared for a tour price of 95-100 thousand rubles (for two).

The reviews of Mercure Koh Samui Beach & Resort say that this place is ideal for beach lovers, because it is located on the coastline. Moreover, in the territory of the considered vacation spot there is a lot of entertainment that will appeal to both children and adults.

The Mercure Koh Samui has an excellent aquazone, which is especially important on hot days, there is a large restaurant and several bars. The hotel has a massage room.

On the ground floor of Mercure Koh Samui there is a large tour desk where you can book a tour of local attractions - such an activity will undoubtedly be very exciting for every tourist.

Renaissanse

Renaissance Koh Samui Resort is a place that is great for organizing youth and family vacations. On its rather large territory there are two modern, well-equipped buildings, as well as several villas. The hotel is located on the seafront, which allows its guests not to spend much time moving to the beach.

Guests at Renaissance Koh Samui are provided with all the facilities needed to organize a decent stay. Its buildings have a well-developed infrastructure, and in the open area there is a large aquazone, several playgrounds, as well as restaurants and bars in which vacationers take food.

The hotel in question has its own SPA center, which offers massage services, thermal relaxation, as well as beauty treatments - all of them are paid.

The average cost of a weekly tour in Renaissance Koh Samui for two is about 120,000 rubles.

Malibu

While briefly reviewing the features of Samui Koh Resort hotels, you should definitely pay attention to such a vacation spot as Malibu Koh Samui Resort & Beach Club. The reviews about this place to stay constantly mention that it is here that you can get as comfortable as possible and spend your time noisy and fun. This hotel is often chosen for youth recreation, filled with parties and constant fun.

The hotel in question is located opposite the Central Festival shopping center, on the seashore. It has a private beach, which is open exclusively for vacationers.

The hotel offers its guests comfortable apartments equipped with modern appliances and new, very comfortable furniture. Each room has a minibar, safe, air conditioning and a large plasma TV.
