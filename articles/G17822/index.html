Several ways to get from Bryansk to St. Petersburg

Russia is one of the most beautiful countries in the world. You can talk about all its sights endlessly. Many people have no desire to go to foreign countries, as they know that they can have a good rest in their homeland. For example, among travelers there is a fairly popular route: Bryansk - St. Petersburg. There are several vehicles that can be reached from one point to another.





What should be considered when choosing a vehicle?

Choosing a way to overcome the distance along the route Bryansk - St. Petersburg, it makes sense to make a choice based on the following factors:

  • Well-being of the person. If he is cradled on the road, his back may hurt or dizziness may begin, then it is better to choose a train. If necessary, you can lie down in a sleeping car and have a good rest, which is noticeably invigorating.
  • Cost. The cheapest form of transport is the bus. Basically, students use this way of transportation to save money.




  • Limited time. If you need to quickly be in another city, then the best option would be a plane.

If there is a personal car, then it will become the ideal vehicle for the traveler. The driver in it will feel confident and comfortable. In addition, he can independently plan his trip.

By car

The distance from Bryansk to St. Petersburg is 996 kilometers. If you wish, you can overcome it on your own vehicle. If you move without stops, the road will take about 12-13 hours. You won‚Äôt be able to calculate the exact cost of the trip in this way, since each car has an individual fuel consumption, and a different price is set for certain gasoline in each city. But still, if you go with the five of you, then this figure will be significantly lower than when moving in the same composition by public transport.





From Bryansk you should go to the highway E95. It is intercity, so the federal services constantly monitor its condition. It is difficult to detect any irregularities on it. Another advantage is that while traveling to the final destination, at each fork you can see signs. This will not let you go astray.





There are many cafes, gas stations and grocery stores along the way. On the way, it is recommended to call in the city of Pskov. In it you can relax, sit in a cozy cafe, enjoy the sights and even spend the night in one of the hotels.

By train

Every day from the Bryansk-Oryol station departs the train Bryansk - St. Petersburg at number 140M. He follows through the city of Moscow. It will take more than 14 hours to spend the entire road, that is, this vehicle leaves the departure point at 14:22, and at the destination it is only at 5.25.





Due to the frequency of the flight, problems with tickets almost never happen. They can be bought at the box office immediately before departure. The minimum cost per passenger is a little over 2000 rubles.

By bus

Of course, it is more convenient to travel from Bryansk to St. Petersburg by car, but not all people own their own transport. In this case, you can overcome this distance by bus. Unfortunately, there is no direct transit from one city to another. You will have to get to the capital of Russia, and there you need to make a transfer to another route. Such flights are carried out five times a day only in the morning, from 3 to 7 hours.

Total travel time is 19 hours. If there are traffic jams on the roads, this figure may increase markedly. The total cost of tickets will be approximately equal to 1700 rubles.

By plane





There is a direct flight from Bryansk to St. Petersburg. Thanks to this, you can be in another city after a minimum period of time - from 1 hour 50 minutes to 2 hours 30 minutes. The only drawback is that the flight is operated only three times a week: on Tuesday, Thursday and Saturday. In the summer, the departure time is 9:25. Please note that the schedule varies depending on the season. On any other day, you can reach the appointed place by air with a transfer at the airport of Pskov or Moscow. In this case, the total travel time will be longer. The minimum ticket price is 5,000 rubles. It is recommended to buy tickets in advance, a few days before the departure of the aircraft, you can do this at the airport ticket office.

The cultural capital of Russia is an amazing place, the beauty of which can be talked about endlessly. It has a huge number of attractions. The entertainment industry is also rich. Is it worth explaining for what purpose more than 100 thousand passengers leave from Bryansk to St. Petersburg annually? There may be several optimal options for such a trip. Before you go on a trip, you should weigh the positive and negative sides of each vehicle and, given these features, choose the most suitable option.
