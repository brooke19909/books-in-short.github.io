Gromovo (Crimea): features of rest

Crimea is a quite popular resort among Russians. A comfortable climate, warm gentle sea, endless sandy beaches, affordable prices are what really attract tourists. But in recent years, not large resort towns with a large concentration of holidaymakers have been especially popular, but small villages where you can fully enjoy a calm and measured vacation. The village of Gromovo in the Crimea can just be attributed to such. In addition, few people know about its existence and whereabouts. That is why, within the framework of this material, we will evaluate the village in various aspects and determine how suitable it is for recreation. The advice and recommendations of experienced tourists will only help in this.





About the village

So, Gromovo (Crimea, Black Sea coast) is a small village in the western part of the Tarkhankut Peninsula. It is located 17 km from the administrative center - the urban-type village of Black Sea, and just a few kilometers from the Belyaus Spit - the coast, which is truly admired by everyone.

The village of Gromovo is small, consists of only 8 streets. Its main attraction can be called vineyards and peach gardens. Be sure to visit them if you choose this place for your vacation.

Climate

Crimea is famous for its mild and comfortable climate for relaxation. The air is fresh, its temperature is favorable not only for sunbathing and swimming, but also for exploring the many attractions of the coast. And what is the weather like in Gromovo in the Crimea? Perhaps to the west the weather conditions are changing somewhat?





The weather here is excellent: rains are very rare, and the air temperature is kept at around 30-35 degrees throughout the season. You can relax here from May to September. Fresh sea air, clean and uninhabited beaches with fine sand, an abundance of grapes and other gifts of nature make this place a real paradise for tourists and vacationers.

Beaches

The village of Gromovo in the Crimea is located 1.75 km from the Black Sea coast, so you have to get to the beach by private car or on foot. But we strongly recommend going to one of the amazing and picturesque places of the region - the neighboring village of Belyaus. It is famous for its crystal clear sea water and fine white sand. This feature allows many to compare the resort even with the Maldives. It is enough to see photos of vacationers, as doubts will disappear by themselves. Surely many tourists who vacation in the Crimea every year do not know about the existence of this amazing place, which allows him to remain deserted and secluded until now.





This place seems to have been created for romantics, families come here with tents in order to fully enjoy their unity with nature. You do not have to rush to the beach in the early morning to take a free place. This is simply out of the question here.

Where to stay

The village has several small private hotels with rooms ranging from economy class to luxurious suites. If you wish, you can stay in the private sector: rent a room or half of the house with a separate entrance. Prices are affordable - from 400 rubles per night.

And those tourists who are not embarrassed by the proximity to nature and spending the night on the street can set up a real campground and enjoy all the delights of a "wild" vacation.

Leisure

What does the village of Gromovo in Crimea offer to modern tourists and vacationers? Holidays here are preferred by those who are really tired of the hustle, bustle, crowdedness of popular resort towns. Quite often here you can see tents with tourists who prefer a "wild" vacation.





If you think that the place is suitable exclusively for a beach holiday, then you are deeply mistaken. Archeology lovers will be able to go to the neighboring village of Belyaus, where archaeological excavations of the ancient Scythian settlement have been ongoing for many years. Interested in the Soviet past? Be sure to bring diving equipment with you and dive to a depth of 12 meters, where you can see a unique underwater attraction - the only "Avenue of Communist Leaders" in the world.

Western Crimea is a paradise for fishing lovers. During trolling on the sea, you will feel the real excitement of the fisherman, about which so many professionals say, and enjoy your catch. Just remember to capture the result on camera.

Of course, this place also attracts romantics, lovers of sitting by the fire, enjoying the sound of a guitar, the rustling of trees and the noise of the surf.

In a word, here everyone will find for themselves what he was looking for so long.





Treatment

Gromovo (Crimea) is a great opportunity not only to relax, enjoy the peace and quiet, but also to take care of your health. Many pensions and resorts offer anti-aging and therapeutic procedures based on local mud. The result will definitely live up to your expectations.

To summarize

The village of Gromovo (Black Sea region, Crimea) is one of the few coastal resorts that managed to maintain its originality and history. Only a few tourists know about this place, so the rest here will be calm, quiet and measured.

We strongly recommend that you plan to visit him when traveling to Crimea. Everyone should see the beauty of this place and, if possible, spend here at least a few days.
