Hotel "Mahdia", Tunisia. Mahdia Palace Thalasso 5 *: description, reviews

Mahdia is a relatively young resort in Tunisia, which has already managed to gain popularity among lovers of a quiet holiday. In addition to a pleasant pastime on the beach, tourists can visit the numerous thalassotherapy centers. They offer wellness treatments for face and body, bathing in healthy sea water and mud wraps. Staying here, travelers often choose the Mahdia Palace hotel. But is the price consistent with the quality of service?

Hotel location

This resort is famous for having the best hotels in Tunisia. Mahdia with 5-star complexes with the best health centers, high quality services, sparsely populated and white beaches abounds. This place is for lovers of a secluded and quiet holiday. All hotels here are located on the first beach line. To get to the central part of the city, tourists will have to overcome a distance of 3 km. You can do this on a special train that runs from the coast to the center, or by ordering a taxi.





Hotel "Mahdia" (Tunisia) was built right on the beach, so guests do not have to spend a lot of time on the road to it. Private sea is located 100 meters from the main entrance to the hotel. It takes less than 5 minutes to get there. You can arrive at the complex from the nearest airport of Monastir (55 km). For tourists a paid shuttle is available in both directions. Tunisia's main attraction , the Coliseum in Al Jem, is 35 km from the hotel.

Number of rooms

For tourists coming to rest in Tunisia, the hotel "Thalassa Mahdia" has prepared 336 rooms. Some of them are designed for guests with reduced mobility. There are also connecting rooms connected by a door in the complex. Large families or friendly youth companies can live in them. All apartments have a private balcony, some of which have amazing sea views. On the ground floor, the rooms are equipped with terraces surrounded by palm trees and shrubs. All rooms are carefully cleaned daily. Towels in the bathroom are changed every day.

The design of rooms in Mahdia Palace Thalasso 5 * skillfully combines classic and Arabic styles. The luxury and novelty of design will be appreciated even by the most fastidious tourists. The apartments are complemented by coffee tables, chairs, bedside tables. Elegant lights hang on the walls. The room is decorated with blackout curtains and paintings painted in a traditional oriental style. The last repair here was completed in 2010.

Room Amenities

Room equipment is a significant factor when evaluating hotels. The rating of hotels depends on it. Mahdia (Tunisia) cares about its reputation, therefore, each room here is equipped with the highest class. Tourists can evaluate the level of the hotel using the following equipment:

  • Separate bathroom. The bathroom is equipped with modern fixtures. There is a washbasin, a shaving or makeup mirror, toiletries, and a hairdryer. Free slippers and bathrobes are provided.
  • Central air conditioning with which a comfortable temperature is maintained. Moreover, tourists can regulate it at will.
  • Mini bar. The Mahdia Palace Hotel (Tunisia) provides it for free, but you will have to pay extra for it.
  • TV and radio. Each room has satellite channels, among which there are several Russian-speaking.
  • Telephone. Used to contact hotel staff or for private calls, which are paid separately.
  • A plastic card that replaces traditional room keys. With it, you can quickly open and close the front door.
  • Safe box for storing valuables. Using it is included in the price.

Tourists who have settled in the hotel "Mahdia" (Tunisia), can count on a high level of service. The following infrastructure facilities work for a comfortable stay of guests in the complex:

Vacation with children

Hotel Mahdia (Tunisia) is located in a quiet location, which is perfect for families with children. For babies, a cradle is available on request. All restaurants are equipped with high chairs for easy feeding. All children under two years stay free of charge. Children between the ages of two and twelve receive a discount on accommodation.

There are many activities for children. For children over five years of age there is a mini-club where they can engage in drawing, reading books, participate in educational games and competitions. There is also a private children's pool on site. There is an open air playground.

Travelers who visited the hotel "Mahdia Palace Talasso 5 *" (Tunisia) leave positive reviews . They note the following advantages of the complex:
