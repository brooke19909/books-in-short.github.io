Training

In fact, it is extremely easy to manufacture. It is better to buy its small details, that is, a bright shirt, hat, boots and a colored bandana, ready-made in the store, but we will sew the skirt and vest ourselves. To do this, we need fabric: white with a black spotted pattern (preferably soft, not loose and with small pile) and trimmed red. To make Christmas costumes for girls with our own hands turned out just right, we measure the distance between the shoulders of the child, the volume of the collar, chest and waist, as well as the length of the vest. Now we fold the spotted fabric in half, in the middle we draw a vertical line. It should be equal to the length of the future product + 2-3 cm. And already from the last points we draw lines to the intersection with the lower edge of the fabric.
