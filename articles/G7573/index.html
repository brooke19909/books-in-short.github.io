"Mitino World": customer reviews

Reviews about the "Mitino World" are of interest to numerous potential new settlers who are looking for a new apartment. This is a whole family quarter located in the Russian capital in a new microdistrict, within walking distance from the Pyatnitskoye Shosse metro station. In this article we will tell you what conditions buyers can count on when buying apartments in this place, whether the developer is reliable and whether you can trust him with your money.

The construction company notes that this is practically the first residential complex in Moscow to be created in the format of a family quarter. This is its main feature. Everything around is as if specially created for the comfortable life of a large and friendly family. Social infrastructure is thought out to the smallest detail, which allows you to create a special atmosphere for relaxation, sports and creativity.

The first stage includes twelve buildings of various number of storeys. Of these, a network of panel buildings (from 11 to 25 floors) and five monolithic (24 floors each). For landscaping in the yards will equip sports and playgrounds, recreation areas. Walking paths will appear in the park along the river and residential buildings, with a total length of about six kilometers, which will lead an active lifestyle.

Architecture

In reviews of the residential complex Mir Mir Mitino, many admit that they are attracted to original architectural solutions. The family quarter is being built with semi-closed courtyards. A certain contribution to aesthetics is made by a single style of color solutions. From the side of the street, the facades are made in natural and harmonious colors, and in the courtyards - in bright or warm colors, depending on the specific thematic area to which this or that house belongs.

Particular attention is paid to the formation of a barrier-free environment. Between the entrance to the entrance and the level of the sidewalk, a minimum height difference is provided everywhere. Glass doors let in daylight, and the lobby will always be cozy due to the high ceilings.

All high-rise buildings belong to modern panel house-building systems. They differ from each other in a variety of planning and architectural solutions, a respectable appearance, as well as increased durability, strength and energy efficiency. Three-layer exterior panels are able to provide thermal insulation that is comparable to the effect of a brick wall. In the apartments themselves, except for loggias or balconies, a place for the installation of air conditioning is necessarily provided.

Beautification

In customer reviews of the "Mitino World", many note that they are satisfied with the accomplishment carried out here. The territory of the entire residential complex looks modern and attractive.

Complex landscaping was carried out, the work of competent landscape designers is visible, small architectural forms are installed. In the yards, playing, children's and sports grounds, recreation areas are without fail.

Infrastructure

Nowadays, when purchasing an apartment in a new building, the infrastructure of the entire microdistrict plays an important role. The reviews on the family quarter "Mir Mitino" emphasize that it is located in the North-Western District of Moscow.

One of the key factors in choosing a home is its transport accessibility. In this regard, this residential complex is most conveniently located for both motorists and pedestrians.

Within walking distance from the houses there is a Pyatnitskoye Shosse metro station, as well as several highways. Including the Moscow Ring Road, Volokolamsk and Pyatnitskoye Highways. So the residents of this family quarter may not be worried because they will regularly be late somewhere and not be in time. You can comfortably and quickly get from here to the very center of the city in about 30-40 minutes.

In general, in terms of its infrastructure richness, this microdistrict is significantly superior to many others that are in the Russian capital. On its territory there are five clinics at once (including children's, adults and dental), nine schools, including with in-depth study of foreign languages, and as many as 37 kindergartens.

There are places for leisurely fun. Nearby is the cultural center "Mitino", the capital's musical theater "Monoton". For fans of an active lifestyle, there are several fitness centers, as well as a children's pool called the Pearl.

Families with children will find it interesting to attend early development courses for children. You can make all kinds of purchases and just have fun in the shopping centers "Rook", "Mitino", "Mandarin", "Ark", "Tuk-Tuk".

The real natural pearl of these places is the Mitino landscape park, in which most residents of the microdistrict love to spend time with pleasure.

How to get there

The family quarter is located on the territory of the Mitino intracity municipality, near the village of Rozhdestveno. The sales office, in which you can be given any detailed advice on the residential quarter, as well as show how finished and under construction apartments look, is open from 9:00 to 21:00.

You can get here by car or public transport. If you are coming by car, then you need to move along the Leningradskoye Shosse from the city center. Take the Volokolamsk highway, at the intersection with the Moscow Ring Road, turn onto Pyatnitskoe, moving to the metro station of the same name. Before it, turn right onto Muravskaya Street.

Negative

It is worth recognizing that you can find a lot of negative reviews about the residential complex Mir Mitino in Moscow. The main complaints are related to the fact that these are prefabricated houses. Despite the fact that the builder assures that only the most modern technologies are used, the majority are still confident that any panel is much worse than a monolith. Currently, such options are used only in low-quality houses, mainly economy class.

In addition, there is a high degree of development, which also causes a lot of negative reviews about the residential complex "Mir Mitino". Potential residents fear that there will not be enough underground parking at all, given that now there is a car in almost every family, or even not one. The number of underground parking lots that are planned in the quarter is much less than the planned construction of apartments.

In addition, the houses themselves are being built in the lowlands, where it will be damp all the time, over time, all the rainwater will begin to drain. Knowing that this will be a significant disadvantage for local residents, the developer is still actively building, which provokes dissatisfied reviews about the "Mitino World".

Negativity appears due to the fact that the developer is constantly postponing the deadlines for the delivery of houses. This indicates either that he does not have enough resources to complete such a large-scale project, or that serious violations are detected during acceptance, because of which it is not possible to complete the commissioning of houses.

In reviews of apartments in Mir Mitino, those who faced similar problems claim that they intend to seek payment of a forfeit, including through a court. Regularly, the deadline for houses is delayed for an impressive period, up to one year. As a result, plans for repair and settlement collapse before our eyes.
