Dessole Saadia Resort 3 *. Hotel Dessole Saadia Resort 3 *: photos, prices and reviews of tourists from Russia

Tunisia is a multifaceted exotic country where every tourist can find an interesting activity for himself. The main thing is to devote at least a little time to getting to know her. The main goal of most travelers traveling to Tunisia is a beach holiday. However, it is worth noting that here you can not only swim in the warm sea, bask in the luxurious beaches and enjoy the high level of service offered by the Dessole Saadia Resort 3 * hotel. Tunisia has on its territory many noteworthy natural and man-made attractions, to which excursions are often organized from the hotel.





Hotel location

Not far from the sea, at a distance of 11 km from the center of Monastir, is the Dessole Saadia Resort 3 * hotel. The airport is 3 km away, and Sousse is 13 km away. Vacationers who choose this hotel for their stay can reach these cities using small ground-based electric trains that stop at a station located 0.5 km from it.

Nearby there is a riding school and a golf club, visiting which you can greatly diversify your stay in Tunisia.

General information

The hotel is presented in the form of three three-story buildings that were built in 1995. They are located in a large green area, in the middle of which there is a pool. The hotel, although located on the second coastline, has its own sandy beach. It takes no more than 5 minutes to go to it, and in summer there is no need to do it at all, since Dessole Saadia Resort 3 * provides free horse-drawn carts that transport all vacationers to the sea.

Among the paid services provided by the hotel, it is worth noting the use of the gym, Turkish bath, sauna, procedures in the SPA center, calling a doctor, laundry and ironing services, scooter rental, canoeing, windsurfing and sailing.

Reviews of tourists from Russia

During the existence of Dessole Saadia Resort 3 * (Tunisia), tourists from Russia rested there more than once. And even if not all, then many of them shared their impressions of the hotel, its staff and Tunisia in general. Thanks to this, today it is already possible to get the most vivid idea about this hotel and the country before the trip. Although it is necessary to make an adjustment to the fact that all opinions are subjective.

Perhaps, it is worth starting from what reviews Russian tourists leave about the territory and hotel rooms. Everyone notes the abundance of greenery, a cozy exterior and interior of the hotel. The rooms, as stated, is cleaned daily, so it is always clean. However, according to some vacationers, sometimes in the bathroom you can see cockroaches and ants, which is very unpleasant.

As for food, tourists claim that it is tasty, although monotonous. There are seafood, cereals, meat, fruits. But the majority of the people were not satisfied with the drinks in the bar, as they consist mainly of ice. Not the best impression was on vacationers about the beach: often dirty water, lots of algae and jellyfish. But the animation at the hotel is satisfied, without exception. It is argued that animators throughout the day and evening constantly think out what to do with both adults and children.
