Quickly and simply sew a suit of nesting dolls for girls

Simple, fast and inexpensive - the costume of a nesting doll for a girl also belongs to the category of such costumes. There are several options for its manufacture. We will start the material with the most affordable.



Method one

We stock up on the following materials:

  • bright summer dress (light fabric should be used for sewing it);
  • multi-colored ribbons;
  • bright scarf;
  • thin jacket with wide sleeves;
  • thread and needle.

An important point: these things should not look too messy. You need to select them so that they fit together. The prepared ribbons need to be sewn to the sundress, and the matryoshka costume for the girl is ready.

Second way

This method involves not just collecting the product from several parts, but sewing it.

The following materials for work, if we make a doll costume for a girl with our own hands, it is better to prepare in advance:

  • White blouse. It is preferable that she was with flared sleeves. If there is no suitable one, then an ordinary white T-shirt can be taken as a replacement. Then lantern sleeves are sewn onto it.
  • Colorful scarf (can be replaced with a scarf).
  • Soft wire.
  • Fabric for a sundress (suitable as plain and in a saturated large pattern).
  • Threads with a needle.
  • Do not do without bright beads.
  • If a plain fabric is chosen, then the sundress can be decorated with ribbons or bright applications.

Next, we will consider in stages how to sew a suit of nesting dolls for a girl.

  1. To begin with, we determine the length of the sundress, it should be equal to the length from the armpits to the floor. Leave fabric for sewing strapless and yoke.
  2. From the wrong side of the fabric, in the waist area, you need to sew a fabric strip. It is called beyka.
  3. We connect the fabric from the sides, giving it the form of a sundress. Having made the assembly in the upper part, we attach the yoke and straps to the product.
  4. After making the gateway, we sew a sundress on the bottom. Insert the prepared piece of soft wire into it. Its length should be equal to the volume of the baby‚Äôs chest.
  5. In the place where the tie was previously sewn, you also need to stretch a piece of wire. Moreover, its length should be twice as long as the length of the piece that is stretched in the lower part of the sundress.
  6. It is quite possible to replace the wire with tulle - a rigid grid. And make a lower skirt or sew to the bottom of the product as an overlay. This is done with the aim that the bottom of the sundress was magnificent.
  7. Next, you need to get a handkerchief. Having folded it in half, we sew a double line at the bend point. Next, we pass the wire between the threads. After tying the knot, the ends of the wire are tucked into it. In this case, the scarf will be held tightly on the child's head.




We decorate a sundress

Matryoshka costume for the girl is almost ready. All that remains is to decorate it. If monophonic material is taken for sewing, then peas sewn on it, applique, sequins or bugles will look good. Here is a simple and quick way to decorate a sundress with peas. To cut them, you need crepe satin and a glue web. After the circles are superimposed on the fabric (glue bottom, fabric top) and heated with an iron.

New Year's costume for dolls for the girl turned out to be glorious. The baby will look charming in him.
