Heritance Ahungalla 5 * (Sri Lanka): reviews

Amazing Sri Lanka - the island of the Indian Ocean, the cradle of Ceylon Buddhism, where no one is in a hurry, and life flows smoothly and smoothly. Friendly, but not annoying people, stunning beauty of nature, wild animals that peacefully coexist with people and endless golden beaches leave a deep, unforgettable and vivid impression of a visit to this country.

Hotel Description

Hotel Heritance Ahungalla 5 * is a luxurious beachfront hotel complex located in Ahungalla, 76 km from the capital Colombo and 115 km from the international airport (three hours drive from it). The hotel is a three-story building. The hotel was opened in 1993 and renovated in 2006. The area occupied by the complex is 6 hectares.





Rooms

All 152 rooms of Heritance Ahungalla 5 * hotel (Sri Lanka), the interior of which is stylish and tasteful, have ocean views, are equipped with air conditioning, minibars (paid service), tea / coffee maker and satellite TV. If the guest needs something, he can call the reception from his room and ask his own question. At any time of the day, either a maid or one of the hotel staff will come up and help with solving the problem. Room cleaning at Heritance Ahungalla 5 * hotel (Sri Lanka) is daily. Those who choose the rooms of the Suite and Luxury categories will be able to find a soft bathrobe and slippers in the rooms and, having put them on, feel completely at home! These rooms are also equipped with CD / DVD players, so music lovers will be pleasantly surprised by such a compliment from the hotel.





All guests of Heritance Ahungalla 5 * can use free Wi-Fi connection in the entire hotel complex. Upon request, guests are offered an ironing board and an iron to look their best on vacation. And you can use the laundry on site, where for a moderate fee your clothes will be like new. All rooms of Heritance Ahungalla 5 * are equipped with safes, and bathrooms have a hairdryer. Each hotel room is designed to accommodate a maximum of four guests.

Hotel facilities

The guests of Heritance Ahungalla 5 * (Deluxe-rooms) can feel all the delights of luxury-relaxation in this hotel. A luxurious golf course, a well-equipped fitness center and a tennis court coated with state-of-the-art materials allow you to enjoy your sporting activities to the full.

Heritance Ahungalla 5 * (Sri Lanka): reviews

Regular guests of the hotel who regularly visit it noted that the level of service has not improved, although progress should be made every year in any area.





Nevertheless, the age of the hotel did not affect the beauty of the territory and the interior. Delicious food and a large selection of dishes were noted. Many guests were satisfied with the work of the animators and the excellent high-quality procedures of the spa. It seemed to some guests that the hotel rooms were in need of repair, and the staff were eager for tips.

But overall, the guests described the staff as attentive and friendly. One guest did not have internet, but this problem was fixed within an hour.

Now about the location. The hotel is far from the center, and the lack of an ATM in the hotel is a clear minus. Nearby there are no attractions and large shops and restaurants. But the sea is right behind the doorstep, but, according to the guests, there was often a shortage of sun loungers and umbrellas, they should be occupied from early morning, this is no longer possible to do. Perhaps the hotel management should replenish supplies for sea beach holidays.

It takes about five hours to travel from the capital to the hotel, which is tiring, especially considering the traffic jams that are rather narrow in Colombo.

The staff welcomes new arrivals with fresh tropical fruits. Many guests noted a quick check-in.





The chef was very praised, who delighted the guests with the skillful preparation of dishes from pork, lamb, chicken, as well as all kinds of side dishes. The pastries were also delicious. You just have to be careful, some foods are unusual for us - Asians often have too much spices, so those who suffer from gastritis and high acidity of the stomach should be on the alert.

Tourists are advised to bargain when buying absolutely any thing both on the hotel territory and beyond.

If you want to buy oils and creams of Ayurveda, it makes sense to visit a city pharmacy, they sell real drugs, not fakes.

Some guests were confused by lizards, newts and other animals on the territory, but any Asian country is exotic, and you should be prepared not only to meet chipmunks and lizards, but also mosquitoes, so stock up on repellents.





As for the surroundings of Heritance Ahungalla 5 *, the reviews of many tourists contained an enthusiastic impression of visiting a small fishing village a little further from the hotel. It turns out that it is absolutely safe for foreigners to be in it, since its inhabitants are very good-natured and friendly, and not at all savages, as some might think. The nature of the island shocked many with its pristine beauty.

Those guests who decided on an independent study of island life and rode the river on a rented boat highly recommend this mini-voyage as a real deep acquaintance with Sri Lanka, its outlandish jungle, flowers, animals and bright unearthly colors.

Summing up, it can be noted that Russian tourists leaving reviews about the hotel were very pleased with the small number of compatriots. In general, the conclusion suggests itself: the Heritans hotel is ideal for those who like peace, appreciate privacy and want to take a break in relations with Russian reality in order to return to their homeland completely updated and truly rested.
