Sharjah, Coral Beach: Holidays in the UAE

For those who have chosen to relax in the UAE (Sharjah), Coral Beach Resort is one of the excellent 4-star hotels for accommodation. It is located on the shores of the Persian Gulf. It is 30 kilometers from the nearest airport in Dubai. It is only 20 minutes away. The central part of Sharjah is only 5 kilometers away. There is a regular bus service from the hotel. This is a rather old hotel, founded back in 1975. It is a complex of buildings, among which one main in six floors and 4 villas, separated by a wide road. The territory is decorated with greenery, which allows you to hide from the hot rays of the sun in the daytime. The hotel is an ideal choice for an economical family vacation. In addition, among the staff most of them speak Russian.



Number of rooms

"Coral Beach Resort" provides for its vacationers, as already mentioned, 156 rooms, as well as 4 villas located across the road. The rooms are decorated in bright colors, air conditioning. There is a small bathroom, satellite TV (there is a Russian channel), a telephone. In addition, there is a hairdryer, a radio, a minibar (filled for an extra charge), a small refrigerator. The room has internet, which connects at an additional cost. There are no balconies. There are conditions for the disabled.



Nutrition

Like most hotels of this category, located in Sharjah, Coral Beach Resort offers its guests the following meals: breakfast or half board, which includes breakfast and dinner. The latter can be replaced by lunch. The hotel has 5 restaurants, 2 of which offer European cuisine (Al Shail, Al Hamur), 1 - Italian cuisine, 1 - fish menu. The pool has a bar serving refreshing drinks and snacks.



Beach

Those hotels located on the first coastline in Sharjah (Coral Beach Resort is no exception) have their own sandy beach. It is pretty well maintained and clean. Beach equipment is provided free of charge. There is a playground where you can play volleyball, soccer, badminton. You can participate in various water activities.

Additional Information

Those who at least once chose the Coral Beach Hotel (Sharjah) for their holidays , leave mostly positive reviews . The main advantage for most is the presence of Russian-speaking staff. There is good food and cleanliness of the hotel. For vacationers built 2 swimming pools, one of which is filled with sea water. Set 2 small slides. For those who maintain their beauty while relaxing, there is a beauty salon and a fitness center.

During your stay in Sharjah (Coral Beach), you can enjoy various sports activities. For guests there are built 2 tennis courts, there is a volleyball court, a gym. For an additional fee, you can play billiards, go diving, visit the sauna and hammam. There is a well-equipped conference room that seats up to 600 people. If necessary, you can rent a car. Free parking is available on site.

A number of services are provided for guests, among which there are paid ones. There are all conditions for relaxing with children.
