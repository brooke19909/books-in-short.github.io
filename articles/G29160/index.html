The involvement of satellite technology by man in everyday life is carried out more and more actively. So, the popularity of GPS beacons is growing - devices that allow you to determine and transmit the geographic coordinates of objects in motion at the required address. In particular, cars. Device Overview

The Avtofon-Mayak device, reviews of which there are numerous Russian users, is a device with a function of geographical positioning by GPS coordinates, involving the use of GSM technologies of a similar purpose. This device is optimized for placement in the car and tracking its current location.





How can I use the "Autophone"?

You can use the device in various ways. Firstly, with the help of "Autofon" you can quickly track the movement of a stolen car. Secondly, by activating "Autophone", the reviews of many owners of this device confirm the corresponding possibility, it is convenient to fix the coordinates of a vehicle during the trip - for example, a friends car that moves along a different route.
