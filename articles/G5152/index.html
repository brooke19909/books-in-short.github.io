Princess Kate Middleton: biography





Kate Middleton, whose biography is a true modern fairy tale, was born in a very ordinary family. She studied at school and institute, was the most ordinary British. Until the cherished meeting, about which all the girls dream, probably.

Kate Middleton: a biography of childhood

The future Princess of the British Crown was born in 1982 in the small town of Reading, which is located in the county of Berkshire. This is where the biography of Kate Middleton originates. Her family was not poor, but not rich. Both parents worked in aviation, his father was the captain of the aircraft, and his mother worked as a stewardess on British airlines. Two more children were later born in their family: a younger sister and brother Kate.





When the girl was only five years old, her parents took care of family income and opened their own small business. A girl from an early age tried to help them in business. The family business was built on the implementation of various souvenirs and jewelry that were sent in packages to the buyer. Apparently, one of Kate‚Äôs parents had an entrepreneurial vein, because very soon the company gained sufficient fame and popularity throughout the UK. Various souvenirs were bought up at an unprecedented speed, and successful businessmen barely had time to count the profits. Very soon, the family moved to a luxurious mansion, which became their new residence. The future princess herself studied very diligently in high school and dreamed of building a career in art criticism.

Kate Middleton: a biography of a young age

After leaving school, the girl successfully entered Marlborough College, in Berkshire. Here she mastered the educational program and passed exams in chemistry, art history and biology. In addition, Kate successfully played tennis and athletics here. In 2000, the girl received a diploma, after which she decided to travel a little. The next few months she spends in the USA, Italy, Chile. Interestingly, even during her wanderings, the future princess continues her studies. So, in Florence, she enters the regional branch of the Institute of Berlin. In Chile, takes part in a charity program.

A year later, the girl returns to Britain and enters the University of St. Andrews. And here is a turning point in the life of Kate Middleton. Her biography acquires completely new colors: she meets Prince William. In 2003, a relationship was established between them.

Princess Kate Middleton: biography

However, the prince's attention was not at all as simple as it seemed from the very beginning. Kate has long been under the gun of journalists. The burden of publicity reflected heavily on her. This state of affairs almost led to the final termination of relations in 2007. However, after this unpleasant episode, the Prince did everything to protect his beloved from unwanted attention. In 2010, all the British media announced the news of the engagement of the couple. And in April 2011, a solemn wedding ceremony took place. At the end of 2012, the media began to actively hint at the princess's pregnancy. Indeed, in July 2013, the new heir to the British crown, Prince George of Cambridge , was born .
