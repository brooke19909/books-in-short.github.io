Zeus Turunc Hotel 4 * (Turkey / Marmaris / Turunge): hotel description, photos and reviews

The Turkish resort of Marmaris, spread on the shores of the Aegean Sea, is located in a bay protected from all sides by mountain ranges. It is surrounded by national parks in which unique pine groves are protected by the state. The access to the sea is blocked by the island, so there are almost always no waves.



general information

Marmaris is considered Turkish Nice, a city of celebration and fun. There is a vibrant nightlife and inexhaustible opportunities for outdoor activities and entertainment. Seven kilometers from Marmaris is one of the largest yacht clubs in Turkey. Many divers come to the resort, for whom here is a real expanse.

But not only outdoor enthusiasts will like Marmaris. Here come those who prefer exclusively beach vacations. The resort has a very developed tourist infrastructure with numerous hotels designed for any client. Here you can stay not only in luxury apartments, but also in budget hotels, of which there are many in Marmaris. But the most popular accommodation option is four-star hotels, which are located on one of the resort's beaches, for example, on Turunc. It is located next to the forest. The coast of Turunc is completely indented with small bays, fenced with cliffs. It is here that the Zeus Turunc Hotel 4 * is located.



Description

Located two hundred meters from the center of Turunc, this four-star hotel in recent years has become popular with Russians. Zeus Turunc Hotel 4 * (Marmaris), according to tour operators, is an ideal option for relaxing with your family or with a company. The hotel is only an hour away from the airport, so visitors, admiring the road along the sea, do not even notice how they get to their destination. The last time Zeus Turunc Hotel 4 * was completely updated in 2014. It has a small but very green area. The hotel building is surrounded by mountains, on the slopes of which pine trees and citrus crops grow.

Location Zeus Turunc Hotel 4 * is quite successful. Nearby there are all the necessary facilities - shops, cafes. Nearby is a water park with a dolphinarium. In ten minutes you can walk to the center of small Turunc.

Beach

The distance to the sea from the residential building of the hotel is about thirty meters. The bathing area is sandy, although there is a small strip of pebbles. Rubber bathing slippers are welcome. There is no bar on the beach. Free only sun loungers. For sun umbrellas and towels you need to pay extra. The beach infrastructure includes showers and a toilet.



Services for children

Judging by the reviews, it is very comfortable for young tourists to relax in the Zeus Turunc Hotel 4 * (Marmaris). But since the hotel is small, there is no playground for children. Upon request, parents can get a cot for one child. Chairs in the restaurant are in the public domain. For children in the main pool there is a shallow compartment with slides.

Additional Information

On the territory of Zeus Turunc Hotel 4 * (Turkey, Marmaris) there is a gym. Guests can also visit the spa, beauty salon, play billiards and darts. There are many types of water activities on the beach. There you can rent snorkeling equipment and go diving under the supervision of an instructor. Seven kilometers from the hotel there is a yacht club - the largest in Turkey.

The hotel does not accept tourists with pets. Smoking in public places is not allowed. Internet is free of charge. The hotel has a swimming pool with slides. Around him are sun loungers and umbrellas.



Zeus Turunc Hotel 4 *: reviews

I must say that most Russians liked this hotel. In their reviews, they note its good location: around the mountain, clean air and many trees.
