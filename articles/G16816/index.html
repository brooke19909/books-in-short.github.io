Hotel Turtles Beach Resort 3 * (Goa, India): description and photos

If you want to relax in a quiet place on Goa near the beach and are looking for a comfortable new hotel, then as an option for accommodation, be sure to consider the three-star Turtle Beach Resort (Morjim).



Location

The hotel in question is located on the north bank of the estuary called Chapora, seven hundred meters from Morjim Beach. Dabolim Airport is 55 kilometers from the hotel. So upon arrival, your journey to Turtles Beach Resort will take about an hour. The city center is 12 kilometers away. In the vicinity of the hotel there are shops, cafes, car and bike rental offices, tour desks and much more. According to travelers, it is most convenient to move around Goa by renting a scooter. So you can visit any attractions and beaches, while saving on taxis and tuk-tuk.



General information, photo

Turtles Beach Resort is a new hotel, built in 2015. The housing stock is represented by 84 rooms located in one building equipped with an elevator. The hotel's own territory is small, but there is a swimming pool and a sun terrace.

Rooms at Turtles Beach Resort are represented in Standard, Boutique, Superior and Suite categories. They have comfortable furniture, a large plasma TV, air conditioning, a bathroom with shower. Please note that standard apartments do not have a refrigerator by default. It can be provided for an additional fee of about 200 rupees per day. Also in the rooms there is no hairdryer and safe, but they can be taken for free at the reception. But throughout the hotel there is access to Wi-Fi.



Nutrition

Turtles Beach Resort rates include breakfast only. They take place in the hotel restaurant and consist of scrambled eggs or scrambled eggs, Indian dishes, toast with jam, tea, coffee and juice. During the day, guests can also eat at the hotel (for an additional fee) or visit the cafe, which are many both in the vicinity of Turtles Beach and on the beaches of Goa.
