Fujairah: attractions, interesting places, leisure

This is one of the most popular resorts in the United Arab Emirates. This is the youngest emirate with the capital of the same name. Fujairah's sights are secluded recreational areas, picturesque nature, as well as unique cultural and architectural monuments.

General information

The only emirate among all is strikingly different from other regions of the country in many respects. So, the nature of Fujairah is a separate phenomenon. Unlike other emirates, green valleys and endless golden sand beaches stretch here. There is no oil production, which only positively affects the attractiveness of the resort from a tourist point of view.

This is the only emirate washed by the waters of the Arabian Sea (all the rest are surrounded by the waters of the Persian Gulf). There is no place for skyscrapers and business centers. But agriculture is well developed. The emirate lives on the ongoing tourist flow. By the way, the inhabitants of the UAE themselves with great pleasure prefer rest on Fujairah to other popular resorts. There are all conditions for a secluded holiday: well-developed infrastructure, lush greenery, endless beaches, as well as unique sights of Fujairah.

Tourism in the emirate is developing well. This is particularly facilitated by the unique sights of Fujairah, of which there are a great many. It is worthwhile to dwell on some of them.

What to see?

Tourists should definitely visit Suk al-Jumaa (it is sometimes called the "Friday market", although it works every day). This is a vivid example of a typically oriental bazaar with a distinctive Arabic flavor. Here you can buy fabrics and spices, exotic goods and unusually beautiful souvenirs, as well as handicrafts and, of course, oriental carpets. Tourists should be reminded that moderate bargaining is a familiar phenomenon, so you can safely ask the merchant to reduce the price.

Fort El Hale, built at the end of the 18th century, perfectly retained its original appearance. The construction initially performed defensive functions. Almost until the middle of the 20th century, the fort was the residence of Sheikh Fujairah. Currently, it is a museum that surprises with unusual exhibitions and expositions.

Al-Bidiya Mosque is another striking landmark of Fujairah, built in 1446 from brick and clay. A distinctive feature is the absence of a minaret and a dome (only 4 instead of the usual 7 or 12). The interior is not luxurious. However, this mosque is a unique architectural monument in Fujairah.

UAE attract various categories of tourists. One of the most striking events are bullfights. This is a peculiar local tradition, the popularization of which was facilitated by the Portuguese.

Tour program for tourists

Travelers who decide to visit Fujairah in the UAE should definitely visit the following places:

  • Etisalat Tower, which is one of the tallest buildings in the city. It is located in the central part of the emirate. You can recognize this building by the sphere that is installed on the top floor of the building.
  • Snoopy Island is a beautiful place created by nature. It is located near Fujairah. It has everything divers need.
  • Al-Vuraya Nature Reserve is a unique natural attraction of the emirate. On its territory unique representatives of flora and fauna live. The reserve is considered the first national park in the UAE. You can get on your own by car or with a group of sightseers.
  • Mineral springs "Ain al-Gamur", formed as a result of the eruption of the volcano, are a favorite vacation spot and treatment not only for tourists but also for local residents.

Shark island is no less popular among divers. Crystal clear water and the diversity of the animal world contribute to attracting tourist flow. A suitable place for diving is also the cemetery of flooded cars - in addition to breathtaking landscapes, you can also find unique representatives of the sea kingdom.

  • An excursion to Musandam, which is part of Oman, is very popular. This is another place known among divers. Here you can meet unique representatives of the animal and plant world, picturesque underwater views, rocks and bays. The cost of the tour is about 300 dirhams.

Nature and weather

Almost the entire territory of the emirate is covered by mountains with the exception of the coastal strip. Fujairah is located on the coast of the Persian Gulf at a time when all other emirates are located on the shores of the Gulf of Oman. It is because of this that the climate of Fujairah is unusually mild, which, in turn, contributes to the development of agriculture. The cultivation of crops in the open field is an unusual occurrence for other emirates.

Winter is the rainy season, which cannot boast of torrential downpours. So, for the entire period, about 80 mm of precipitation falls (for example: in the Dominican Republic, only 400 mm fall in autumn). Wealthy residents of the Emirates often buy property in Fujairah only because the local climate favorably affects their general well-being.

Holiday at the resort

Here is everything that even the most spoiled and demanding tourist needs. Fashionable hotels and more economical options for accommodating tourists, great opportunities for outdoor activities and unique sights, picturesque natural landscapes and favorable weather in Fujairah, exquisite cuisine and oriental flavor - all this will undoubtedly leave the most vivid impressions about the rest.
