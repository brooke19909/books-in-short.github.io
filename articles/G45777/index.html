Meet the "Lighthouse". Water park in Sochi for family holidays

Mayak is the oldest water park in Sochi. He opened his doors for the first visitors seventeen years ago. It took only a few months for the center of water entertainment to win the recognition of guests. The complex became known far beyond the Krasnodar Territory. His possessions occupied the picturesque coast of the Black Sea. The area he occupies exceeds twenty hectares.

How to get there

Mayak is not the only water park in Sochi, but it is only located in the heart of the city. A few minutes walk from it rises the Festival Concert Hall. There is also access to the beach and to numerous coastal eateries. In the immediate vicinity of the box office of the water center, public transport stops are concentrated. The complex stretches along the seaside promenade. Next to it shuttle buses and buses run to the Stereo movie theater and the Moscow hotel.





The water park in Sochi is served by a group of professional lifeguards. On its territory there are trading enterprises and cafes. At the peak of the holiday season, a restaurant offers a special diet menu. It offers visitors comfortable cabanas, fully equipped shower rooms, rental equipment.
