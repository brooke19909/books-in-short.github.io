Hotel Nissi Beach 4 * ("Nissi Beach"). Hotels in Ayia Napa (Cyprus)

Among young people, the Cypriot beach Nissi Beach has recently been extremely popular . Hotels located near the sea are in great demand. Every year young people come here from all over Europe who want to not only relax on the sea, but also have fun.

Nissi Beach

Hotels located along the sea are most convenient for relaxation. They are suitable not only for couples with children, people aged, but also for young people who strive to always be aware of what is happening on the local beach. The fact is that in the summer, Nissi Beach turns into a party place. Summer is a time of foamy discos and DJs.

It is for this reason that the hotels on the Nissi Beach are so popular among those who already have 16, but have not yet turned 50. What could be better than living by the sea and participating in all the holidays being in the center of events.

Hotel Nissi Beach

A great place for a summer vacation can be Cyprus. Hotel "Nissi Beach" 4 * is always very popular among tourists. This is due to its advantageous location.

Not every hotel can boast that it is located on the Mediterranean coast. Nissi Beach Complex (Cyprus) has its own tropical garden, which occupies 100,000 square meters.

First of all, the hotel will appeal to those vacationers who intend to spend their holidays at sea, and not by the pool, the beach is within easy reach. Even the smallest tourists will be able to walk on their own.



The Nissi Beach Hotel (Ayia Napa, Cyprus) has excellent infrastructure. On its territory there is everything necessary for a good good rest. And to the very center of the town is only a couple of kilometers. Near the complex there are many cafes, shops, bars and restaurants.

Number of rooms

The Nissi Beach Hotel (Ayia Napa, Cyprus) has a decent number of rooms, which is represented by 270 apartments:

  1. The Garden View Rooms. The complex has 118 such comfortable rooms with a gorgeous view of the beautiful tropical park. The apartments are located next to the main building. Each room has a bedroom, a guest area, a balcony and a bathroom.
  2. The Sea View Rooms. The hotel has 104 rooms with amazing views of Nissi Bay. Each of them is made in calm soft beige and blue-blue tones. The room has a bedroom, a small living area, a balcony and a bathroom equipped with not only a shower, but also a bath.
  3. The Beach Bungalows. The hotel features 18 bungalows with private gardens. Some of them are located near the sea. The bungalow has a cozy living room, bathroom, its own private courtyard.
  4. Suite Beach Bungalows. The complex has 30 such luxury apartments with private gardens. They are located right on the beach. The rooms have a bedroom (with a double bed) and a living area, as well as a private terrace with sea views and a bathroom.

All apartments are designed in a Mediterranean style. Guests will enjoy bright interiors and comfortable furniture. The rooms of all categories are equipped with air conditioning, heating, hairdryers, safe, satellite TV, telephone, radio, minibar.

The hotel has a 24-hour service. Rooms are cleaned daily. Also, every day, stocks of the minibar are replenished and detergents in the bathroom are updated.

Nissi Beach Infrastructure

The Nissi Beach complex (Cyprus) has a conference hall for all kinds of events. Experienced hotel staff will help organize any business meeting or other event. There are three rooms of different capacities. The largest of them can accommodate up to 500 people, and the smallest - up to 30. The hotel practices half board. The restaurant serves nutritious and varied dishes.

On the territory there is a children's room, a table for organizing excursions, an exchange office, a beauty salon, laundry, kiosks, shops, car rental and parking are available.



While relaxing in the hotel, tourists can visit the sports and health complex. There are gyms and gyms, a solarium, a sauna and a steam room. Since Nissi Beach is a year-round resort, vacationers will appreciate the indoor pool, whose services can be used from November to April, when the sea is no longer relevant. You can also visit body treatments. During the warm season, an outdoor pool operates; it will appeal to those who do not seek to take sea baths on the shore.

Also on the territory there are: a tennis court, mini-football and mini-golf courts, a basketball court. Water aerobics classes are held every day in the outdoor pool.

In general, everyone will be able to find an interesting occupation and not be bored, not to mention the sea and the beach, which are the main goal of visitors.

Vassos nissi plage

Near the Nissi Beach there are several more complexes, which can also be considered as an option for vacationers.



Vassos Nissi Plage 4 * (a shortened version often used by our compatriots - "Vassos Nissi Beach") is a hotel that offers its guests indoor and outdoor pools, a sauna and a gym. There is a water park 200 meters from the complex, and the beach is just a two-minute walk.

Weather in Ayia Napa during the summer season favors sea vacations, but the resort has long earned the title of year-round.

Located on the coast, Vassos Nissi Hotel can be a great place to relax. For this, the complex has everything you need. Hotel rooms have balconies with stunning sea views. All apartments are equipped with minibars, flat-screen TVs and air conditioning. Bathrooms are equipped with all necessary cosmetics and a hairdryer.

The hotel has its own highlight. A full English breakfast is served here each morning . And in the evening there is a bar and restaurant for tourists. In addition, the complex organizes entertainment programs that will not let you get bored.

For those tourists who cannot imagine their life without sports, there is a tennis court and a fitness room on site. Professionals will offer you massage services while animators take care of your children.

In general, the hotel is new and meets all the requirements of its category. According to youth reviews, the rest here is very good, especially pleased with the close proximity to the most party-like Nissi Beach beach.

Rooms at the Vassos Nissi Hotel

The hotel is represented by 126 standards and suites. The budget room can accommodate up to three people. It has a TV, air conditioning, safe, hairdryer, minibar. Internet services are additionally paid. There are also apartments for guests with disabilities.

Deluxe rooms are quite spacious (82 square meters). They have a bedroom and a living room, as well as two bathrooms, a TV, internet, coffee and tea sets.

Hotel Restaurants and Bars

The main restaurant of the hotel - Yamm Lounge organizes buffet meals for guests. But the institution Famous Beach offers national dishes, there is also a specialized children's menu. The lobby bar and pool bar throughout the day delight you with cool cocktails and good desserts.



The hotel complex has an outdoor pool with a relaxation area equipped with free umbrellas and sunbeds. This type of vacation is suitable for those who are indifferent to the sea beauties and beach parties, preferring not to leave the hotel and enjoy the artificial pond. The hotel also has a baby pool.

For an institution of this level, Vassos Nissi has a good infrastructure: a conference room, car rental, exchange office, shops, dry cleaning, laundry, doctor, parking.

Adams beach hotel

Adams Beach is another hotel on the Mediterranean coast, which is located near the Nissi Beach. From the balconies of the rooms a beautiful seascape opens up. Guests at the complex have access to several pools, a spa and a tennis court.



The hotel boasts not only a wonderful location, but also nice rooms with wooden furniture. All are decorated in warm pastel colors. The apartments are equipped with air conditioning and satellite TV. All rooms have balconies, some of which offer wonderful views of the Mediterranean Sea.

Hotel facilities

Guests at Adams Beach Hotel can enjoy the Olympic spa, gym and massage. The complex has a souvenir shop and a supermarket. There are several restaurants and bars. All of them offer guests the most delicious dishes of different cuisines, from the Mediterranean to the eastern. Dionyssos Tavern serves a buffet for tourists, while Vala serves the best of fresh fish.

Instead of an afterword

For those planning a vacation on the Cyprus Nissi Beach, hotels on the beach will be the best option. The largest congestion of people on the coast is observed in the season (from June to August), at this time there is the most active partying, which is more interesting for young people. Couples may like to relax in a quieter time, for example, in May or September. During these months you can enjoy the beauty of the beach and the silence, broken only by the sound of the surf. Although the sea itself is beautiful at any time of the year.
