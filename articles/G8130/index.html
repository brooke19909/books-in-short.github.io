Hotel "Alara Kum" (Turkey / Alanya): description, guest fund, meals





The Alara Kum complex (Turkey) is an ideal resort place for spending summer vacations with your family. It is located in the forest-park zone of the village of Inzhekum, only 3 km from the village of Avsallar and 23 km from the center of Alanya. Nearby is the motorway, nightclubs and shopping galleries. The convenient location of the hotel (in a picturesque bay, protected from the winds by a tropical park) allows tourists to fully enjoy the tranquility and silence.

The hotel is famous for its well-groomed golden sand beach - on the coast, your children can safely swim in shallow water and frolic on the beach. The Alara Kum Guesthouse (Turkey) has a friendly atmosphere, the staff and the administration do everything possible to ensure that your vacation is of high quality and comfortable. For the convenience of customers, a small market and a number of other establishments are open in a small local area .

Fans of sightseeing can always get a ticket to any tour directly in the complex or rent a car and go on their own to get acquainted with the majestic objects of the resort, visiting ancient structures, temples and ancient villages. Directly at the hotel you will be offered fun animation programs, sports entertainment and wellness treatments.





Number of rooms

The tourist center "Alara Kum 5" (Turkey) offers accommodation in comfortable and equipped with all necessary rooms, which are located in two buildings: 5-storey and 6-storey buildings. All 197 rooms are equipped with personal air conditioning, satellite TV (there are Russian channels) and a balcony with wicker furniture.

From their windows, guests can enjoy the outdoor pool, forest or the Mediterranean Sea. In addition, in all rooms there is a bathroom with shower, toilet and hygiene products. Tourists can use a direct telephone line to contact the administrator. There is a safe, hairdryer and a bar with drinks (use is paid separately). Cots are provided for children. Wet cleaning is provided daily , and every other day, bed linen is changed.





Nutrition

While staying at the Alara Kum Hotel (Turkey), guests eat all-inclusive. Fresh vegetables, several varieties of cheese, a variety of fruits and other dishes are always present on the tables. There are 6 bars in the hotel with a wide selection of soft and soft drinks, exotic cocktails, snacks and desserts. There is a restaurant with an outdoor terrace. An interesting menu at the restaurant will delight you with delicious seafood, meat and fish dishes.

Beach (own)

Although the hotel is located on the second line of the beach, the road will not take you more than 5 minutes. You can get to the sandy coast through an underpass. The emerald sea, surprisingly clean, warm, with convenient and safe descent, is a godsend for parents with young children.





Every day, the shore is cleaned from large stones and debris. All beach amenities (mattresses, sunbeds, awnings) are free of charge for customers. For active and energetic tourists there is a water entertainment center: parachute, surfing, canoeing, skiing and motorcycle.
