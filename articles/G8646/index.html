Boro-Boro Island - Paradise in Paradise

Once on the island of Boro-Boro, you understand that this may be paradise. There is no concept of time in this place, therefore enjoyment of life goes on endlessly.

The place where the island of Boro-Boro is located is located in the Pacific Ocean. It is included in French Polynesia near Tahiti. This is one of the most expensive vacation spots today. Island hotels with swimming pools, white sand beaches, sunset on the water - all this not only offers a relaxing holiday, but also creates a feeling of peace and solitude, complemented by light bursts of the ocean.

Fans of stories and legends love to visit Mount Pachia, where they are told various biblical stories, including how the God of War descends from the slopes.



No less exciting are underwater excursions: the only coral park on the planet is located here, and those who love extreme sports can feed barracuda, stingrays or sharks.

Curious tourists find it interesting not only to visit the sights that the island of Boro-Boro is rich in, but also to get acquainted with the aborigines and traditions of this paradise in paradise.

The resort is not only gourmet restaurants. It is stylized as a Polynesian village. There are no night clubs and discos. Tourists are entertained by local residents: they weave panamas, paint pareos, and prepare national dishes.

In 1942, the Americans built a military base here, and after the war, everyone who served here went home. Their stories about this amazing place, photos of the island of Boro-Boro contributed to the fact that tourists gradually began to come here. And since then their flow has not stopped.

Romantics and newlyweds come here from all over the world to see how Mount Otenamu merges with the sky above the crystal clear water of the lagoon against the background of lush hibiscus flowers encircling the lagoon like a necklace.
