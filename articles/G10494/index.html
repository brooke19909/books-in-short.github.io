Hotel Sentido Letoonia Golf Resort 5 * (Turkey, Belek): overview, description and reviews of tourists

We can say with confidence that Turkey is considered the most favorite country among Russian tourists. Thousands of compatriots rest here every year. This country has many advantages. It provides a quality beach vacation at low prices. In addition, tourists can visit the sights that remained after two great empires: Byzantine and Ottoman. And of course, inexpensive shopping. You can buy clothes, shoes and souvenirs here very cheaply. When choosing a hotel in the resorts of Turkey, it is worth paying attention to the Sentido Letoonia Golf Resort 5 *, which has won many positive reviews.

Basic hotel information

The hotel was built in 1997. It is located on a vast territory (75,000 ha), surrounded by a forested area. Sentido Letoonia Golf Resort 5 * (Turkey, Belek) is a tourist complex that includes 22 residential buildings: the main seven-story building, an additional four-story building and many small bungalows. The last redecoration of all hotel rooms was completed in 2015. The complex is designed for families who love a moving lifestyle. There are all conditions for living with small children. Children under 12 years old are given discounts. And check-in with animals is strictly prohibited.





The hotel is located 45 km from the central part of the resort of Antalya. The nearest airport is 35 km away. The road to it will take on average about an hour. The tourist village of Belek is 8 km away. The hotel itself is located on the first line, 100 meters from a private beach. Check-in starts no earlier than 14:00 local time. You need to leave the hotel before noon. Part of the staff is fluent in Russian, which will greatly simplify communication if any problems arise.

Number of rooms

For travelers who come here on vacation, the Sentido Letoonia Golf Resort 5 * hotel (Belek) has prepared 450 comfortable rooms. Among them there are standard double rooms, and spacious apartments, and separate bungalows, which can simultaneously accommodate 6 people at once. The complex has a large number of rooms equipped for guests with reduced mobility. Also, each apartment is equipped with a host of amenities that allow you to make your vacation even better. We list the main ones:

  • Flat screen tv. The rooms have satellite TV with the ability to view Russian-language channels.
  • Electric kettle and tea and coffee making facilities.
  • Safe for storing things, money and documents. It is provided free of charge.
  • Mini bar. A daily supplement of soft drinks and beer is included in the price at Sentido Letoonia Golf Resort 5 *.
  • Telephone.
  • Central air conditioning for rooms located in the main building and individual for bungalows.
  • Slippers, bathrobe, towels, hairdryer and toiletries in the bathroom.




Daily housekeeping is also available. Tourists can also order free room service from 00:00 to 7:00. The rest of the time the service is paid. Bed linen is changed 3 times a week.

Hotel infrastructure

Being a five-star hotel, the Sentido Letoonia Golf Resort 5 * complex offers its guests first-class quality of service. A developed network of infrastructure facilities provides guests with the following amenities:

  • Internet cafe. No admission fee. There is also free Wi-Fi in all public areas.
  • A business center with 5 conference rooms. The largest of them accommodates 900 people.
  • Free car parking.
  • Spa center offering wellness services for face and body. Barber, massage room, Turkish bath, Jacuzzi and sauna.
  • Doctor's office.
  • A huge number of outlets: souvenir and jewelry shops, clothing stores, bags, silver.
  • Fitness Centre.




Entertainment at the hotel

A huge selection of diverse leisure options has been prepared for its guests by the Sentido Letoonia Golf Resort 5 * hotel. Tourists can have fun by visiting the following sites:

  • Six pools: four outdoor and one indoor. There is also a swimming pool for sports swimming (semi-Olympic). Two water slides. Umbrellas, sun loungers and beach towels are provided free of charge.
  • Water sports: windsurfing, water aerobics, boat and non-motorized boat rides, fishing and diving.
  • Daytime animation. Evening and night discos with live music. There is a disco bar. Entertainment shows for adults and children.
  • Slot machines (non-gambling).
  • Fields for mini-golf and indoor soccer.
  • Playgrounds for sports (darts, giant chess, bocce, golf, volleyball, classic and table tennis, archery, soccer, basketball).
  • Billiard room.
  • Paid bike rental.




Food system, restaurants and bars

Like many other hotels in Turkey, Sentido Letoonia Golf Resort Hotel 5 * operates on an all-inclusive system. The price includes all meals, including brunch, afternoon tea and other snacks. Free alcohol, cocktails, cold drinks, ice cream and freshly squeezed juices are provided for breakfast. Special children's, dietary and vegetarian menus are available upon request. The hotel has many restaurants and bars:

  • The main restaurant serves buffet meals. All guests of the complex can eat here for free.
  • Restaurant "Riverside". Specializes in seafood.
  • Restaurant Kebabci. It serves Turkish cuisine.
  • Restaurant Chopstix. Asian smithy, including sushi, rolls and seafood.
  • Bar "Atrium".
  • Beach bar.
  • Pool Bar.
  • Turkish coffee shop.
  • Vitamin bar. You can order infusions and decoctions of medicinal herbs.
  • Garden Bar.
  • Bar "Mediterranean Sea".




Tourists reviews

Many hotels can offer the resort of Belek (Belek). Sentido Letoonia Golf Resort 5 * stands out among them with a huge number of positive reviews from tourists. They like the beautiful and clean area around the hotel, spacious rooms with fresh renovation, a varied and tasty menu. Children love local animators and enjoy going to the mini club in the morning. According to the guests, the hotel has a very friendly staff who, when solving problems, always goes towards the customers.

Guests also note the shortcomings of the hotel. For example, poor wifi in public places, poor soundproofing in rooms and too noisy neighbors. Loud music plays almost until morning, the staff insistently offers to book an excursion or visit any store.

Thus, the hotel can be recommended for family and outdoor activities. For a small price, it offers a lot of services and entertainment, among which each visitor will find something of their own. Friendly staff and cheerful animators will help you spend an unforgettable vacation.
