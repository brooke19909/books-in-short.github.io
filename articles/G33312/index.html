Lenovo TAB A10: specifications and reviews

Lenovo Tab 2 A10-70L 16Gb LTE is a mobile digital device (tablet), which went on sale in 2015. The novelty immediately attracted the attention of most buyers. The manufacturer used innovative developments. The tablet is based on the famous MediaTek processor. High performance is provided by two gigabytes of RAM. And the 10.1 ∫ screen guarantees the user a comfortable pastime. Such characteristics, combined with a price of 18 thousand rubles, can intrigue any buyer.



Design

If you look at customer reviews, then the Lenovo Tab 2 A10-70L in design will pull only a four. The reason for this is the typical design. The case is plastic. The developers used a matte finish, thanks to which a touch on the tablet gives an unforgettable pleasure. However, you can not keep silent about the soiled. Soft-touch coverage is already known to customers, and in their reviews they often point out such a drawback as the accumulation of fingerprints.

The gadget has dimensions of 24.7 √ó 17.1 √ó 0.89 cm. The side edges are slightly rounded, which allowed developers to visually reduce the thickness of the tablet. The front panel is designed in a typical pattern. It has a large screen, a camera lens and a light sensor. But the light indicator in the Lenovo Tab A10-70L is not provided. The frame around the display is black or white. The camera lens is displayed on the rear panel. There is also a speaker. Its hole occupies the entire upper part. Also on the lid, the manufacturer placed a company logo. Mechanical buttons (power key and volume control), which are used to control the tablet, are located on the side on the left side. There is also a microUSB connector. The developers brought the microphone to the bottom, and the port for connecting the headset to the top.

Given that the Lenovo Tab A10 works with networks of mobile operators, the designers have provided slots for a micro SIM card and a removable drive. They are covered by a stub.

According to users, the build quality is pretty good. However, they noticed one feature - when you press the back cover, a slight deflection is felt. Most likely, this is due to the presence of free space between the body and the internal "stuffing".



Display Feature and Reviews

The Lenovo Tab A10-70L tablet has many advantages. One of them is the screen. It is quite large - 10.1 inches. The display technology used in the gadget is currently the highest quality. This is an IPS matrix. She is already familiar to users. Wide viewing angles, high color reproduction, real shades, optimal brightness margin - all this is guaranteed by the developers of Lenovo Tab A10-70L.

In the category of tablets up to 20 thousand rubles, screens with a resolution of 1920 √ó 1200 px are practically unrealistic. Famous brands use such characteristics in models whose prices are an order of magnitude higher. Unfortunately, the pixel density is not high enough - only 224 ppi. But users do not consider this a significant drawback.

For protection, the developers used glass. The oleophobic coating allows you to almost completely get rid of the accumulation of fingerprints on the screen.

Hardware "stuffing"

Lenovo Tab 2 A10-70L 16Gb LTE runs on a 4-core processor from MediaTek. Computing modules operate on the principle of 2 + 2. With an increase in load, the clock frequency reaches 1300 MHz. Users have no complaints about the MT8732 chipset model. The tablet showed excellent results. He performs all tasks quickly: applications do not freeze, the browser does not slow down. The graphics processor is Mali-T760. The graphics accelerator allows you to efficiently display animated and other images.



Memory

Lenovo Tab 2 A10-70L shows high performance thanks to two gigabytes of RAM. They are quite enough for excellent performance, and the user can hardly experience any restrictions. The only thing that can cause problems is with modern toys. During their launch, subsidence in the FPS is observed.

The storage of native memory is 16 GB. It is designed to install downloaded applications, pictures and other files. In order for users to fully enjoy the capabilities of the gadget, the developers have provided support for external drives. The device will read information from flash drives with a capacity of not more than 64 GB.





Battery and battery life

The Lenovo Tab A10-70L is a non-removable battery. It is made using a lithium-ion composition. Battery capacity is 7000 milliamps per hour. This resource is enough for 12 hours of video viewing. This figure will correspond to reality if you turn off the mobile signal.

Many users have left a large number of praise for autonomy on the net. During the tests, the tablet showed excellent results. Depending on the intensity of the load, the battery life is enough for 4-5 days.

Multimedia features

Studying the characteristics of Lenovo Tab A10-70L, you can not keep silent about the capabilities of cameras. The rear works on the basis of an 8-megapixel module. For the front-end manufacturer chose a 5 megapixel matrix. Such equipment is fully consistent with modern requirements.

The advantages of the device can also be safely attributed to stereo speakers. The sound is pretty loud. Music tracks play cleanly.



Conclusion

Summing up the review, we can safely say that this tablet model for 2015 had no competitors. Similar characteristics were found only in the flagship models of world-famous manufacturers such as Samsung. However, their products were much more expensive. Therefore, the Lenovo tablet stood out clearly in the assortment range. The optimal combination of price and functionality has made the gadget a sales leader for a fairly long period.
