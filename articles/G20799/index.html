Tula region: attractions and interesting places

Tula region is located in the center of the East European Plain. It was formed in 1937. Of course, the Battle of Kulikovo, which laid the foundation for the formation of the sights of the modern Tula region, is of crucial importance for the region. Kulikovo Field, the Tula Kremlin are preserved historical monuments that allow tourists to feel the atmosphere of that time. Everyone has heard that the Tula region attractions have interesting and significant.



It is unlikely that you will be able to get acquainted with all the monuments of architecture and art for a short tour. But every tourist is obliged to try the famous gingerbread and tea from the samovar. The sights of Tula and the Tula region can be listed for a very long time. But the leading places in terms of tourist attendance are occupied by Kulikovo Field, the Tula Kremlin and the only exotarium in Russia. One of the tasks that the Tula region sets itself is to restore the sights and give them their original appearance. Kulikovo Field is an iconic museum-reserve, which every Russian should visit. For more than 10 years, work has been ongoing on its restoration and reconstruction. Specialists are trying to restore the landscape of the historical battlefield as accurately as possible, to return the forests and fields.
