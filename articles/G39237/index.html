Real estate agencies in Moscow: rating, reviews

The housing problem and rental housing are problems familiar to Muscovites and guests of the city at any time of the year or day, but now they are especially acute. It is important to find professionals to help in such a crucial matter. Especially for those who find it difficult to choose, a rating of Moscow real estate agencies was compiled - the best and most trusted companies. Well-trained specialists will fulfill all the wishes and select the option of the transaction that suits you.





1. Evans

In the first place is Evans. Evans is a Moscow real estate agency with rating and contacts provided below. This is one of the main companies offering a full range of services. The company provides brokerage services in Moscow and around the world. The company was created by American entrepreneurs back in 1991. They were pioneers, and their task was to fulfill the expectations of customers in the market. Based on Western standards, the company professionally promoted its real estate services in Moscow.

In 1995, Evans got its current name, and since August 1997 the company has been controlled by Russian partners. Now Evans is recognized as one of the leading full-service real estate concern providing brokerage services in Moscow and around the world. Evans is a member of the American Chamber of Commerce and the Association of European Businesses in Russia. Despite many years of existence, workers here are mostly young and ambitious. They work in the field of residential real estate, provide assistance in renting, buying and selling in the center and suburbs of Moscow. Evans privileges working with corporate and individual clients, creating contacts with both tenants, buyers, sellers, and lessors. The main focus of the company is the premium market segment, and 80% of customers are members of the expat community in Moscow. All certificates can be obtained at the central office or on the official website of the company.





2. Four Squares

In second place is Four Square. The ratings and reviews of the Moscow real estate agency are impressive. This is a progressive Western company specializing in real estate, which has been operating in Russia since 2005. Four Squares offers experience in leasing, relocation and commercial property management. As management notes: “We are proud of our high-quality standards of service, an active management policy and a team of trained professionals and bilingual real estate professionals.”

3. Astoria Realty

Astoria Realty is another leading but now British real estate company in Moscow. Astoria management consists of two people. This is a British businessman living and working in Moscow, as well as a Russian businessman with extensive experience in real estate. The concern offers a wide selection of elite housing for its customers. The consultants have many years of experience in helping expatriates find a new home. Astoria Realty consultants report that foreign clients are looking for elegant rental properties or even acquire premises for business. Representatives are also pragmatic in their negotiations in situations of sale and purchase - this is often said by the company's clients. The company's specialists advise emigrants on a wide range of services and provide qualified and effective assistance to meet all the needs of their customers. Astoria Realty is the third largest company in the ranking of Moscow real estate agencies.





4. Beatrix Real Estate

Beatrix is ​​another company included in the top 6 best in the ranking of real estate agencies in Moscow. Over the past 20 years, the company has survived a global crisis and managed real estate transactions for several thousand satisfied customers. The primary advantage is a good reputation and trust of people. Founded in the early 1990s and led by Marianna Svechinskaya, from the first day Beatrix was based on the most stringent principles of managing Western business. The company was formed, created and developed along with the Russian real estate market. During the tumultuous post-perestroika Russia, Beatrix served the first immigrants who moved to Moscow. The support of the first corporate clients - the newly formed independent media and the bold Dutch emigrant community (shortly afterwards the diplomatic corps) - provided indispensable business support and a solid launch pad. So the name Royal Dutch came to be, and the mark of the highest quality was awarded. Now the company is engaged in the sale of luxury real estate and has on its account several thousand regular customers. People say that they are satisfied with the services of the company. Beatrix Real Estate is proud to be one of the pioneers in the country's market.



5. Blackwood

Concern, the fifth in the rating list of real estate agencies in Moscow, was established in 1991. It is one of the oldest and most experienced real estate companies in the Russian market. The English name was chosen due to the fact that foreign groups are actively promoting themselves in the Moscow market. Today, the main clients are foreign citizens who have arrived in Russia on a work visa. Thanks to a respectable list of emigrants, the company entered the market and became a serious player at the level of international standards. In 1993, PriceWaterhouseCoopers became Blackwood's first corporate customer. At the same time, the company began working with a number of foreign embassies, diplomatic missions and large Western companies.





6. At Home Moscow

At Home Moscow is at number six in the ranking of Moscow real estate agencies. It provides its services in Moscow in the residential real estate market, offers assistance in organizing rental, purchase and sale of housing in the center and suburbs. The Home Network Real Estate Agency has been working in this area for many years and has managed to establish excellent working relations not only with potential tenants, but also with lessors, other property companies and local authorities. Customers note that with this company you can always be sure that employees will be able to organize the best offer for customers. The company works mainly with the search for elite real estate for the community of working foreigners in Moscow, although they have cheaper offers.

These 6 companies in the rating of Moscow real estate agencies provide quality service and, according to reviews of former customers, offer their unique approaches to customers and full support right up to the entrance to the purchased housing. For those who are panicky afraid of contacting such companies, this rating will help in choosing the best team. They will make finding a property an easy and enjoyable process.
