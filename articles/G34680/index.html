Mysterious Montenegro: rest, reviews from tourists

Montenegro - a small European state located in the southeast of Europe - in recent years has become very popular among Russians. The amazing nature of the country, graceful mountain peaks, transparent Skadar Lake and warm sandy beaches attract tourists who dream of a fabulous vacation. How might mysterious Montenegro be of interest to travelers? Vacations, reviews of experienced travelers and prices for tours - you will learn about all this in order from this article.





sights

This Balkan country cannot boast of its size, but due to its rich history it will surprise with a huge variety of natural and architectural monuments .

Budva city

Budva is considered one of the oldest cities on the Montenegrin coast. The fortress walls of the Old Town have been preserved here, in the center of which there are several ancient temples and an archaeological museum. What is it - old Montenegro? Rest (guest reviews confirm this) in Budva - these are beautiful winding streets and charming fishing restaurants.

City of Cetinje

The ancient capital of Montenegro is located in a picturesque valley surrounded by mountains. This city, having survived many palace intrigues, is legendary that is inhabited by ghosts. Tourists who visit this place will be fascinated by chic castles, churches and monasteries. Here are the most famous museums of the country, for which Montenegro is famous. The rest, reviews of which are filled with mysterious stories about local brave souls, will be remembered by city streets with cozy cafes and a carpet of snowdrops blooming in the spring near the Tsetinsky monastery.

City Bar

This is a major port city in the country. The most famous architectural building is the palace of King Nikola. Here is an old chapel and chic greenhouses. On the territory of the Bar there are many beautiful temples that have been preserved by ancient Montenegro. Rest, reviews of which will be the most positive, will help you better know the culture and history of this country.





Entertainment

Montenegro will be interesting not only for those who like to soak up the beach. This cozy corner of the earth is also sought by tourists who prefer extreme sports.

Diving

The underwater reefs of the Adriatic Sea are densely populated by corals, which makes Montenegro attractive for divers. Scuba diving enthusiasts can enjoy old wrecks or explore limestone caves.





Ecotourism

In recent years, holidays in the private sector in Montenegro have become popular. For example, eco-villages work here, offering tourists a vacation in the lap of nature. Guests are offered safari tours, hiking trails, rafting.

Skiing

Fans of winter holidays also seek to get into this country. From December to April, ski resorts open here. For example, in Zabljak there are 12 ski slopes designed for athletes of various levels of training.

Conclusion

Montenegro offers all this and much more to its guests. Holidays, prices in 2013 (September-December) of the year for which they remain very attractive for Russians, here correspond to European quality. For example, for 8 days of amazing relaxation you will pay from 22 thousand rubles, including air travel and a great opportunity to get acquainted with the culture of this mysterious country.
