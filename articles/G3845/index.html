Hotel Mistral Mare Hotel 4 *: reviews and photos of tourists

In Greece there is a wonderful resort, known as Istron. Here in 1990, the Mistral Mare Hotel 4 * was built. It is not new, but in 2008 the building went through the restoration process. The hotel looks beautiful - two buildings, towering on a hill above the sea, can be seen from afar. And what is his interior! Once inside, everyone will experience aesthetic delight. It is impossible to describe in words, so you should take a look at the photo below, which conveys a riot of colors, national color and sophistication of design. However, the hotel has many other features, in addition to its interior.





Location

Mistral Mare Hotel 4 * is located in Istron, or rather, in the very small village of Kalo Chorio. It refers to Lacition. This is a nom in Greece, located in the east of Crete. It is worth knowing this, as for lovers of attractions. At 10 kilometers from the village is Agios Nikolaos, the capital of Noma. And the airport is 75 km away. Far away, but there is a plus - nobody will get in the way of taking off and landing planes.

But, of course, the main advantage of the hotel is that it is in close proximity to the sea. Literally 300 meters away is the famous Golden Beach. And from the windows of the apartments of the hotel offers a wonderful view of the Mirabelon Bay. In general, a picturesque place. Especially popular among lovers of walks, beautiful views and young people. Some do not like it here - but only because they have to go down to the beach, and many tourists want the sea to be at arm's length.

What is in the hotel?

Mistral Mare Hotel 4 * has all sorts of amenities and offers various services. Everything, if only the guests were comfortable. It has an outdoor sun terrace and swimming pool, a billiard room, table tennis, and a fitness center with exercise equipment. There is also a massage room and a sauna.

There is free private parking, a 24-hour reception, luggage storage and a small business center. The hotel also offers currency exchange, laundry service, car rental, and a fax and photocopying service. By the way, even guests can use the services of a concierge. And, if you need to purchase something, then you do not have to leave the hotel - there is a mini market here.

The hotel also has polite staff. What is most interesting is that people who are employees of the hotel speak three different languages. Among which are English, Greek and Russian.





Nutrition

In Mistral Mare Hotel 4 *, as in any other good hotel, there is a restaurant and a bar. Breakfast, lunch and dinner are served there. As the people who have been here say, the food in the establishments is not bad. Breakfasts, as elsewhere, are standard - a variety of pastries, jams and preserves, eggs (boiled, fried, omelettes), cheese and sausage slices, granola and cereal with milk, and much more.

Lunches with dinners are more varied. Moreover, they are not repeated. They serve seafood, lamb, poultry, pork, beef, squid, sausages, ribs. And this is just a small list. They offer guests turkey, cuttlefish, barbecue and much more. There are also soups and salads. By the way, vegetables are served separately. They are all sliced, so that a person can make a salad for himself by typing a little bit of everything. Serve a lot of different fruits. There are oranges, grapes, nectarines, watermelons and melons. Alcohol is also good, especially local beer and wine. And here they serve delicious ice cream.

Booking and accommodation

The hotel is very popular. Therefore, it is advisable to reserve the apartment at least a couple of months before the planned arrival. Having been puzzled by resolving the issue of booking earlier, you can get the opportunity to occupy the best room. And about preferences regarding the look and other nuances it is better to indicate in the line of additional wishes.

The hotel can accommodate with children. If the child is not two years old, then for him the administration will provide a small crib.

Check-in at the hotel starts at 14:00, but if guests arrived earlier, and the room booked by them is free, then they will check in immediately.
