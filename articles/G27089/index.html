How to repay a loan ahead of schedule in Sberbank: description of the procedure and recommendations

Not every organization acting as a creditor provides for early repayment of a loan. Sberbank customers are more fortunate: it allows you to pay off all or part of the debt if you wish. To carry out the operation, the borrower will need to visit the service office and contact a specialist in lending to the population. After filling in the papers, the contract is terminated or its components are changed (payment schedule). In order not to cause difficulties in the calculation, you should know how to repay a loan ahead of schedule in Sberbank.

Peek into a loan agreement

The agreement with the bank drawn up during its execution will help determine the actions for early repayment of the loan. The contract will indicate the method used to calculate the monthly payment: annuity or differentiated. The first method is characterized by the same recommended contributions to repay the loan. It is easy to recognize it yourself: if you need to pay a fixed amount each month, then the loan is annuity.





Differentiated payments are inconsistent. Every month they change, gradually decreasing. For example, at the beginning of the loan period, the client pays the bank 600 rubles, and at the end - 400 rubles. Moreover, interest will also gradually decrease, but the amount of the main debt is written off in equal shares.

About differential and annuity payments

To understand how to repay a loan ahead of schedule in Sberbank, you need to understand the essence of the formation of monthly installments. For example, returning borrowed funds to the bank in an annuity way, the client subscribes to the following conditions:

  • every month it is necessary to pay the same amount during the entire loan term;
  • the payment consists of a portion of the principal debt and interest monthly accrued on the entire loan amount;
  • most of them are paid in the first half of the contract.

As a result, the amount of borrowed funds is repaid only in final payments. Therefore, "repaying" a loan is more profitable in the first half of the contract, when the bank has not yet received most of the interest from the client.

With a differentiated method of loan repayment, monthly installments vary in amount. They are the amount of the principal debt per 1 payment period, and interest. The latter are not calculated on the entire loan amount, but on its balance. This method of repaying a loan is more beneficial for the borrower - the overpayment is much less.

General terms

How can I repay a loan ahead of schedule at Sberbank? There are a number of requirements that will have to be met in order to make a calculation. The Bank does not make requirements for the minimum size of the contribution, does not prohibit both partial and full repayment. In addition, the borrower does not have to pay a penalty for early repayment.

It is necessary to inform Sberbank 30 days before the planned repayment time.





In case of early repayment of the debt, the client has the right to regain part of the insurance for the period that will not fall within the period of use. Of course, this procedure is longer than repayment of a loan, but feasible if desired. The process also begins with the submission of an application, the form of which can be asked at the bank.

Action algorithm

In order not to wonder how to early repay a loan at Sberbank, you need to know what remains to be done for this. The general rules for the successful transfer of funds and recalculation (closing) of the loan amount are reduced to the following points:

  • write an application for early repayment and take it to the bank branch 30 days before the planned payment of funds;
  • make sure that the employee stamps the application for its acceptance for consideration;
  • pay the set amount in any convenient way;
  • make sure the balance changes;
  • request a new payment schedule (in case of partial repayment) or documents confirming full repayment of the loan.

It is worth noting that the monthly installment scheme is rarely changed, since Sberbank most often draws up loans with annuity payments. With their partial repayment, the validity of the loan agreement changes, while the amount of installments remains the same.
