Location

The southernmost island in terms of places where tourist services in Greece are developed is about. Crete. How to get to the hotel

There are two ways to get to Crete in Central Hersonissos Hotel 3 *. If you purchased a tour at any Russian travel agency, most likely, they will organize an air flight to Heraklion, the capital of Crete. Hersonissos is located 20 km east of it, but the hotel will need to be driven through the whole city, so the distance increases to 25 km. At the airport, organized tourists, as a rule, are waiting for a modern transfer, which will take them to their place along a perfectly smooth asphalt road. The journey will take no more than 20 minutes, but waiting for your suitcases at the airport can take up to an hour. If you travel through beautiful Greece on your own, and if you follow Crete from Athens, it is better to get there by ferry. This trip in itself will be an unforgettable adventure, plus, a ticket for a ship costs half as much as a plane. Ferries arrive at the port of Heraklion or at the port of Agios Nicholas, a resort located 40 km from Hersonissos. You can get to the city by regular bus or taxi.

Sea

Despite the rich past of Greece and its beauty, many tourists just for the sake of the sea go to rest in this country. About the beach near Central Hersonissos Hotel 3 * reviews of our compatriots are mixed. They call the advantages of its location from the hotel just 200 meters, the cleanest sea, where at a depth of even 10 meters you can calmly view the bottom and its inhabitants, excellent weather, which means gentle sun, gentle wind, low waves. The disadvantages of the beach for vacationers are as follows:

- it is municipal (like almost everything in Greece), so sun loungers and umbrellas are paid here;

- poor entry into the sea (slippery stones underfoot);

- small in area (made by paid sunbeds, it is very difficult to find a place to spread your free towel);

- cool water even in June.

Regarding the last point, it should be noted that Hersonissos is located on the northern tip of the island and not in the bay, where the water could be warmed up well, but simply on the seashore with running water. For this reason, it only gets warm by July.
