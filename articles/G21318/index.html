Private hotels, Dzhubga. Hotel by the sea - reviews and photos

Fans of travel and those who wish to have a good rest or spend a vacation with health benefits are ideally suited by the resorts of Tuapse district of Krasnodar Territory. No wonder vacationers compare it with paradise: mild climate, fertile land, hospitable locals, clean air and gentle waves attract many tourists from all over Russia.

Like at home



You can relax on the Black Sea coast in a pleasant environment with good service in the village of Dzhubga. The hotel and guest houses of the resort village, which will be discussed below, are comfortable and cozy. According to tourists, the level of service is so high that it can easily compete with European hotels. They celebrate the incredible beauty of a welcoming region. The village of Dzhubga itself is located on the Black Sea, at the mouth of the river of the same name, right in the forest among centuries-old trees. A wide variety of conifers makes the resort air healing. Convenient is the location of the village itself - between Gelendzhik and Tuapse. It is enough to turn by car from the M4 highway, as you are already there. If you get by air, then the airports of Gelendzhik and Krasnodar are at your service. Tuapse train station is only 55 km from the destination.

Hotel number 1

Many buses from all over the south head to the village of Dzhubga. The hotel of the same name is the center of the resort. It rises majestically in the very center of the village, attracting the attention of tourists and local residents. All the entertainment, attractions are located in the immediate vicinity, including the water park famous throughout the coast and numerous night bars and restaurants. Those who stayed in the hotel "Dzhubga" are simply obliged to visit the "water" entertainment, because for them discounts are provided. The staff is famous for its competence, courtesy and professionalism. Guests enjoy this pleasant surprise.

Resort infrastructure



Local residents take the opportunity to make money on the tourism business, and their enterprise helps to improve this industry. Thanks to them, private hotels are developing more and more. Dzhubga is growing, and in the village you can find a haven for a while on very financially favorable terms. According to the assurances of tourists, every vacationer can easily find accommodation here to their liking.

A new bus station has been built in the village, a modern water park has been opened, the territory is being improved. The resort village of Dzhubga is developing and becoming more beautiful every year. The hotel with the same name meets all the modern requirements and aspirations of the guests and sets the pace of development of its brothers.

Location



Since the village is located on the Black Sea, it has a wide sand and pebble beach with a length of about 900 meters. It is very convenient for young children and for adults. Numerous vacationers note a large number of water rides and a variety of entertainment on the shore. Boat trips with fascinating stories about the local attractions of the village of Dzhubga are offered. The hotel can also be located near the Black Sea, and this makes the vacation romantic. Numerous cafes and restaurants are scattered along the promenade, in which live music invitingly sounds in the evening. Vacationers say that night rest in the Black Sea resort is not inferior to daytime.

The melody of your holiday

The coast is dotted with sanatoriums and guest houses. Just ten minutes walk from the sea is the hotel "Melody". Dzhubga offers a large selection of hotels on the coast, but this place has a high level of service. Tourists note that there is an opportunity to have a great rest and at the same time improve their health. The building consists of three buildings, in which the rooms differ in comfort and, accordingly, their price.



Spend your free time in accordance with your desires. There is table tennis and billiards, a pool with warm water and a hot sauna, shops and cafes. For organized recreation for young tourists there is a playground, which is equipped with equipment for games and entertainment.

A very beautiful landscape opens from the windows of each Dzhubga hotel. By the sea there are more than a dozen guest houses, sanatoriums, among which tourists will find apartments to their liking. But each of them has its own zest. A distinctive feature of the Hotel "Melody" is that with each new arrival a show-presentation is organized and a disco is held.

The hotel buildings are located in the center of the village, so the bus station, water park and market are nearby, and its remoteness from the highway makes it possible to enjoy peace and quiet. The mountain serpentine is equipped for quad biking, which offers a panoramic view of the Black Sea and local mountain slopes.

The hotel offers organized excursions. These are "Eagle Rock", "Dry Beam", as well as routes to waterfalls and dolmens. Fans of extreme sports enjoy the opportunity to participate in a jeep tour on the slopes of a mountain serpentine. All this will help to relax and recharge with energy, which is enough until the next vacation.

Pier by the sea

There is another place that has earned fame among visiting holidaymakers. Just fifty meters from the Black Sea on the river bank is the hotel "Prichal". Dzhubga is well located, which allows you to profitably develop the hotel business. The hotel building has three floors with its magnificent courtyard, in the territory of which there is a pool for children and water slides for them. Tourists say that here the level of service is slightly inferior to the previous place, but the rest does not get worse.

The rooms are spacious and comfortable, each equipped with a comfortable balcony with a magnificent view of the courtyard. This is very convenient for parents with children who can watch their children without leaving their room. There is also a paid parking for personal vehicles, which is guarded around the clock. Directly opposite the hotel building there is a beautiful dining room with home cooking, in which professional chefs prepare dishes of different nations. Here they take orders for complex dinners, which, if desired, will be brought to the room. Sand and pebble beach is located 50 meters from the hotel and is equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay by the sea. Hotel "Prichal" is recommended for a complete and relaxing holiday for couples with children.

Heavenly place



Despite the growing popularity of foreign resorts, local beauties continue to attract thousands of tourists during the holiday season. From May to October, the life of the coast literally boils. The fun does not stop for a minute, everywhere the contented faces of vacationers and loud laughter. It is worth coming to the Krasnodar Territory at least for a week. This is enough to love the sea, the sun, the beach.

The atmosphere of "sea recreation" is charged with aromas of freshness and magnificent landscapes of mountains and waterfalls. The possibility of outdoor activities adds positive emotions to traditional entertainment. Holidays on the Black Sea coast in the hotels of Tuapse district of the Krasnodar Territory will be unforgettable.
